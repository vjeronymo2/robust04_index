BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova] [Text] Moscow March 30 TASS--An explosion which occurred in the Osenny Boulevard in the vicinity of a new residence of the Russian president ``does not have a political colouring,'' ITAR-TASS was told at the Russian president's press service. ``This is an ordinary crime,'' the press service said, relying on a conclusion made by competent interior officials. At 12:30 p.m. Wednesday, an explosive device went off in a Zhiguli car parked in the yard of a house located in the Osenny Boulevard, 12, block 2. The explosion killed a driver of the ``Oton'' trade company who lived there, and ``a criminal boss'' was injured. The criminal was hospitalized. Two more girls, aged 8 and 10, were injured. The explosion was presumably targeted at the ``criminal element'' interior officials said. The incident occurred at a distance of one kilometer from a new city residence of the Russian president. Neither Boris Yeltsin, nor his family are living in the new house yet.
