940308 FT 08 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Paribas French Investment Trust PARIBAS FRENCH Investment Trust showed net assets of 146.85p at the December 31 year end, against 124.57p six months earlier and 118.34p 12 months ago. Net revenue increased to Pounds 257,000 (Pounds 179,643), giving earnings of 0.86p (0.6p) per share. The dividend is held at 1p.
