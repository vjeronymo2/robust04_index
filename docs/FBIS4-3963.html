BFN [Text] Beijing, April 25 (XINHUA) -- Canadian Governor-General Ramon John Hnatyshyn arrived here at noon today for a state visit to China. The arrival marked the first visit to China by the head of state of Canada in the past seven years since 1987. Greeting the governor-general at the airport were Sun Yongfu, chairman of the Chinese Government reception committee and vice- minister of railways, and Vice-Foreign Minister Jiang Enzhu and other senior diplomats and officials.
