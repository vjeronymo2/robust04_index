July 10, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: HUNTINGTON BEACH;

DRIVER DIES IN CRASH; PASSENGER IS UNHURT

A 23-year-old sailor was killed early Sunday when he lost control of the car he was driving on Pacific Coast Highway and crashed into a fence.

Richard A. Frost of Long Beach was killed about 2:20 a.m. when the car struck the wood-frame, chain-link fence near Warner Avenue.
