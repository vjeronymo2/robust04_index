930721 FT 21 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Interest costs restrain Central Motor Auctions Higher interest charges offset cost cutting and improved operating efficiencies at Central Motor Auctions in the half year to April 30, and profits before tax declined from Pounds 432,000 to Pounds 347,000. Auction proceeds fell from Pounds 152.8m to Pounds 144.2m but profits at the operating level expanded by 49 per cent, from Pounds 359,000 to Pounds 536,000. The previous period included Pounds 185,000 from disposal of fixed assets. Earnings per share worked through at 1.81p (2.7p) and the interim dividend is maintained at 1p.
