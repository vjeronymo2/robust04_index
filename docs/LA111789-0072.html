November 17, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Lin Schedules Shareholders Meeting: Lin Broadcasting has scheduled a special shareholders meeting on Jan. 12 for a vote on the company's proposal to merge its cellular telephone operations with those of Atlanta-based BellSouth Corp. New York-based Lin proposed the merger and a restructuring of the company that would include a spinoff of its seven commercial television stations, in an effort to defeat a hostile bid from McCaw Cellular Communications of Kirkland, Wash.
