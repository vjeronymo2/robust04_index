March 2, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

GARDEN GROVE;

CHILDREN'S PARK TO BE DEDICATED ON FRIDAY

The city will dedicate a 27,000-square-foot children's "garden park" at 3 p.m. Friday in ceremonies at the site, 12631 Keel Ave. in the Buena-Clinton neighborhood.

The park was developed at a cost of $190,500 in funds provided by the city and state, a city spokesman said.

Mayor W.E. (Walt) Donovan and other city and state officials will speak.
