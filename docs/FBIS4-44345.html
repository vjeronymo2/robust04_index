BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] A giant merger in the press world -- the dailies MILLIYET and HURRIYET became partners. The Dogan Group which owns MILLIYET joined the Hurriyet Holding Company by buying 50 percent of its shares. The news, heard only this afternoon, created a great commotion in the press world. In statements they issued this evening, the two dailies confirmed the partnership. They said that this merger will enable the two groups to make a major technological step. They said that the existing installations of the two groups inside Turkey will be joined, and new printing installations set up in certain places. They also pointed out that the partnership will enable them to extend a speedier and more modern newspaper service to their readers both technically and from the viewpoint of content.
