BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 28 -- Party General Secretary Do Muoi received here this afternoon Lao Foreign Minister Somsavat Lengsavat who is on a visit to Vietnam. The Lao chief diplomat conveyed regards from president of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee Khamtai Siphandon to Mr. Do Muoi. He also expressed his satisfaction at the development of the special friendship, fraternal solidarity and all-sided cooperation between the two parties, states and peoples. He affirmed that Laos always respected and strived for the constant consolidation and further promotion of those traditional relations. Speaking to his guest, General Secretary Do Muoi welcomed the Lao foreign minister's visit and the results of the talks between the two foreign ministers. He said that the two sides should exchange views and experiences with each other, strengthen the effective and all-sides cooperation and promote the special solidarity between the two countries.
