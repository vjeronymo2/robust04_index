BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 22 -- Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet arrived in Kuala Lumpur today to attend the forum of leaders of the Pacific Basin Economic Cooperation Council (PBEC) at the invitation of Malaysian Prime Minister Mahathir Mohamad and Chairman of the PBEC Mr. T. Bahdia. Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet was welcomed at the airport by Prime Minister Mahathir Mohamad, Foreign Minister Abdullar Ahmad Badawi of Malaysia and chairman of the PBEC T. Bahdia and others.
