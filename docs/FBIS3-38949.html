Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] "It is a first, significant step forward in sorting out the affair." This is how Foreign Minister Andreatta commented on the Bosnia ultimatum decided by the NATO Council. Andreatta, who is attending the Geneva conference of countries bordering the former Yugoslavia, added: "There are still some unanswered questions." He cited problems concerning the international administration of Sarajevo, disarmament, and the coexistence of different ethnic groups in single districts of Sarajevo.
