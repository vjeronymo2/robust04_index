Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Report by Cha Man-sun from Vienna] [Text] North Korea notified the U.S. side that it will allow the International Atomic Energy Agency's [IAEA] inspection team to enter North Korea from 1 March. At the U.S.-North Korea contact held on 22 February in New York, North Korea notified the U.S. side that it will allow the IAEA's inspection team to enter North Korea so that it can start inspections from 1 March. Councillor Yun Ho-chin who is participating in the IAEA Board of Governor's meeting as the North Korean representative, said that because the United States put forth the practical implementation of the exchange of special envoys of the South-North as a condition, no agreement has been reached regarding this kind of schedule. He added that the entry of the inspection team can be delayed depending on the attitude of the United States.
