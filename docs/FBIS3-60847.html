Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Viktor Zhilyakov] [Text] Krasnodar, 24 Dec -- The helicopter carrying the terrorists and the hostages seized in Rostov-on-Don has just lifted off from a Krasnodar military aerodrome. It circled Krasnodar several times and took a course towards Mineralnyye Vody. One hopes that the schoolchildren who were taken hostage are safe and sound. The bandits have still not been identified.
