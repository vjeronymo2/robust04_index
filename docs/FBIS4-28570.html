BFN [Excerpt] Eight persons -- five elected representatives from Kachin State and three NLD [National League for Democracy] organizers -- who absconded to the (Pagyaung) region in Kachin State and formed the NLD Liberated Area-North, surrendered at Myitkyina on 5 June through the KIO [Kachin Independence Organization], Kachin Special Region-2. They are U N. Nang Gam, elected representative from Machambaw Township constituency; U Yau Si, elected representative from Putao Township constituency; U Marpan Sin elected representative from Hkawbude Township constituency; U Kya Mya elected representative from Mohnyin Township constituency-2; U Kyaw Maung elected representative from Mohnyin Township constituency-1; U Min Swe and U Ba Win, NLD organizers from Kyunhla Township; and U Thein Aung, NLD organizer from Indaw Subtownship, Mogaung Township.
