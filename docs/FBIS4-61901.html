BFN [By unidentified ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow May 10 TASS -- There are no Georgian guerrilla groups in the Abkhazian Gali region, says a statement of plenipotentiary representative of the Georgian state head on settlement in Abkhazia Aleksandr Kavsadze received by ITAR-TASS today from the press center of the Georgian Embassy in Russia. The statement refutes Abkhazian claims that 250 armed Georgians allegedly crossed the Georgian-Abkhazian confrontation line along the Inguri River to terrorize the local Abkhazian population. Kavsadze "personally controls the regional situation and can say for sure there are no Georgian guerrilla groups or other units on territory of the Gali region. Such Abkhazian reports aim to prepare the public for a new wave of deportation of the remaining Georgian population from the Gali region."
