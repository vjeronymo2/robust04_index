920317 FT 17 MAR 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There were 19,586 packages for the day, reports the Tea Brokers Association. There was strong and widespread demand. Bright liquoring East Africans sold at firm rates while good mediums were a strong feature and 3 to 5p dearer, while other descriptions showed little change. Offshore; there was good general demand with brighter liquoring teas firm to dearer, but plainer Central Africans tending easier. The highest price realised this week was 169p for a Rwanda pd. Quotations: landed quality 150p, non landed, good medium 112p, medium 92p, low medium 80p.
