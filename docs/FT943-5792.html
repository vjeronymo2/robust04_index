940902 FT 02 SEP 94 / UN chief tries to calm population row Mr Boutros Boutros Ghali, United Nations secretary general, last night sought to calm a bitter debate on abortion, contraception and extra-marital sex which threatens storms at the Cairo population conference, Michael Littlejohns reports from the UN in New York. He said before leaving for Egypt that the controversy, which has been led by the Vatican and Islamic fundamentalists, was 'encouraging'.
