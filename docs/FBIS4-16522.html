BFN [Unattributed report under the "From A to Z" rubric] [Text] The Lithuanian Radio and Television Company board has requested that the Lithuanian Government appropriate money to air "Ostankino" television and "Mayak" radio programs. If the "top chiefs" agree, the programs will be aired daily starting 1800. According to Gediminus Ilgunas, the company's chief, this will cost 800,000 lits or around $200,000.
