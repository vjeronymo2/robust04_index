BFN [Report by the Information Service of 107th Knightly Motorized Brigade from Gradacac] [Text] A fierce artillery attack on Gradacac has been underway since early morning. At 0400 the aggressor began an all-out attack. So far today, more than 30 of the largest-caliber howitzer shells from Skugric have struck at the town center. At the same time, tanks in Turic [words indistinct] and Bucje are attacking the town and the surrounding area, while 120-mm mortars are literally showering the industrial zone and the access roads. Bearing in mind that the defense lines, especially in the region of Novalic, Sibovac, and Mionica are being attacked from 82-mm mortars together with intensified fire from antiaircraft machine guns, antiaircraft guns, and infantry arms, this morning has been one of the most dramatic of late. While Serbian politicians are supposedly championing peace [words indistinct] the fascist policy are savagely attacking the civilians of this town. At the time of this dispatch, Gradacac is veiled in smoke caused by grenades of the largest calibers. For the time being we do not have any reports on possible casualties of this attack, which is still underway.
