920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Cumminseed, very firm due to disappointing crop in Turkey. Indian cuminseed offered at usd 3600 per mt, cif Europe. Recent drop of US dollar makes nutmegs and mace more attractive in local currencies. Indonesian cassia firmer because of short covering for July and August shipment. Pimento, Mexico slowly offering Aug/Sep shipment usd 1850, later positions discounted. Ginger, cochin firmer due to rumours about smaller new crop in Dec/Jan and Jan/Feb, quoting usd 1200 cif. Spot discounted usd 1150. Chillies, due to political problems some West African countries are not able to export small red chillies. Quoted at usd 3200/mt.
