BFN [Text] Talking to the leader of the CSCE Moldovan mission Richard Samuel in Kishinev [Chisinau], Moldovan President Mircea Snegur pointed to the need for continued CSCE activities in his republic. According to Snegur, Kishinev [Chisinau] and Tiraspol based dialog on the CSCE Moldovan mission's advice for stability in Transdniestria. The multi-party elections of 27 March, said Snegur, and the victory, in the elections, of a centrist-oriented party, made the solution of the Transdniestrian problem possible. Snegur and Richard Samuel met in the runup to next week's session of the CSCE's standing council which is to concentrate on the extension of the Moldovan mission's mandate.
