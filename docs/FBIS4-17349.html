BFN [Text] The National Council of the National Salvation Front [NSF] adopted a resolution on Sunday which says that Boris Yeltsin's resignation is the compulsory condition for signing the Civic Accord agreement. The National Council members also expressed protest against plans to hold Russian-American manoeuvres in the Orenburg Region (the Urals) and urged all patriots to join this protest. Ilya Konstantinov was elected chairman of the NSF's Executive Committee. Four more co-chairmen were elected in addition to the 17 already working: Sazhiy Umalatova, Valeriy Skurlatov, Mikhail Titov and Valeriy Smirnov.
