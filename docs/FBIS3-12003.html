Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Remarks by President Alberto Fujimori in response to an unidentified reporter's question, place and date not given -- recorded] [Text] [Reporter] What is your opinion, Mr. President, of [Colombian] President Cesar Gaviria's election as OAS secretary general? [Fujimori] I want to reiterate here the opinion I have always held of President Gaviria. I believe that his tenure as secretary general will give the OAS the dynamism it needs in this process of modernization the whole world is undergoing. I think that in his capacity as future secretary general, he will lay great emphasis on regional integration, on economic and commercial development. [end recording]
