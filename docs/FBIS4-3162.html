BFN [Text] A delegation of Moscow Radio International is visiting China from 5 to 12 April. A protocol on cooperation between Moscow Radio International and China Radio International was signed in Beijing on 11 April. The document consists of 10 articles. One of them envisages that each of the two sides will ensure, at its own expense, the transmission of its programs via a satellite channel to an earth receiving station in the capital city of the other country. The signing ceremony was attended by Wang Feng, Chinese deputy minister of Radio, Film, and Television; (Chiang Renhua), director of China Radio International; (Xin Yimin), deputy director, and other responsible officials. Our guests also visited China's largest city of Shanghai where the potential for multifaceted cooperation between Shanghai People's Radio and Moscow Radio International were discussed. The Russian delegation visited the construction site of the Pearl of the East television tower, the highest in Asia. The delegation will leave Beijing for Moscow on 12 April.
