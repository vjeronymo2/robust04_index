940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / Household spending rises 1.9% The average household's weekly expenditure rose by less than Pounds 5 per week between 1992 and 1993, said the Central Statistical Office yesterday. The 1993 weekly average was Pounds 276.70, a 1.9 per cent increase on 1992's Pounds 271.80. Gross weekly household income was Pounds 353 in 1993, up from Pounds 342.90.
