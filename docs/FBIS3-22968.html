Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, Feb 14 (MENA)--The supreme State Security Prosecution has ordered the detention for 15 days of members of extremist groups who are being interrogated. The 12 were arrested and were found in possession of leaflets propagating ideas of Jama'ah al-Islamiyah (Islamic Groups). The leaflets also objected to interrogations being carried out with the Arabic weekly "AL-SHA'B," mouthpiece of the Labour Party. Investigations revealed that the group were planning to provide hideouts for fugitive terrorist elements. They were also found in possession of arms which they intended to use in terrorist operations.
