Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "The Russian's Debts"] [Text] Wherever Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, the right-wing winner of the Russian elections, appears, he is bound to leave everybody confused. During his stay in Austria, the Russian went to Graz to have his skin and eyes examined at the local hospital. After the treatment he casually disappeared -- without paying -- it has now been reported. (Initially the doctors kept quiet -- out of politeness toward their "prominent" guest?) Now the director of the hospital wants to send a bill for the amount outstanding to Moscow.
