BFN [Text] On 25 June, Paraguayan Foreign Minister Dr. Luis Maria Ramirez Boettner traveled to New York (United States) where he will participate in a meeting sponsored by Ecosoc [Economic and Social Council], a UN economic and social organization. The meetings will begin on 27 June and end on 29 June. Paraguay joined Ecosoc last year and this is the first time a Paraguayan foreign minister will participate in these meetings. The Paraguayan foreign minister will travel from New York to London to join a presidential entourage that will begin an official visit to the UK that will end on 7 July [date as published].

Bolivian and Uruguayan Foreign Ministers Depart
Bolivian Foreign Minister Antonio Aranibar Quiroga and Uruguayan Foreign Minister Sergio Abreu Bonilla returned to their respective countries on 25 June after participating in meetings in Asuncion to relaunch Urupabol [Permanent Joint Commission of Uruguay, Paraguay, and Bolivia], a political organization that was dissolved in 1982 because it was described as being unnecessary.
