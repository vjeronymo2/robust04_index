August 14, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL ROUNDUP: ENCINO, WOODLAND HILLS ACCOUNTING FIRMS MERGE

Two accounting firms -- Getzoff Accountancy Corp. in Encino and the Woodland Hills firm of Barry W. Krowne -- merged to form Getzoff & Krowne.

Terms were not disclosed.

The combined firm has more than 900 clients and will be based in Encino.

Getzoff Accountancy was formed in 1973 by Stephen Getzoff. The firm moved to Encino last year from Beverly Hills. Krowne started his firm in 1977.
