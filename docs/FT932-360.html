930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Acsis calls for share suspension Dealings in shares of Acsis, the healthcare and interior contracting group, were suspended at 1 1/4 p yesterday at the directors' request pending publication of refinancing and restructuring proposals and of its preliminary results for the 1992 year. It was expected that the proposals, which have been agreed with the group's bankers, and the results, would be published 'no later' than July 30 with the annual report and accounts being published on or before August 6.
