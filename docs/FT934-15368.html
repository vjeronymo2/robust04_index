931012 FT 12 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Provision pushes up loss at CountyGlen CountyGlen, the Dublin-based property company, saw losses before tax increase from IPounds 264,000 to IPounds 489,000 (Pounds 466,000) over the 12 months to April 30. The outcome was struck after a provision against debtors of IPounds 235,000. Directors said the company was 'vigorously pursuing the recovery of the full amounts due'. Losses per share amounted to 13.5p (6.1p).
