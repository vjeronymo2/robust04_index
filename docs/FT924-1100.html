921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / Finance & The Family: Small companies perk up - At a Glance Small company shares seem to be ending the year on a high note. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) rose 1.9 per cent from 1126.07 to 1147.06 over the seven days to December 17; the County index increased 1.8 per cent from 868.43 to 884.2 during the same period.
