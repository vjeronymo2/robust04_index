941231 FT 31 DEC 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico dragged lower Mexican stocks closed down 1.47 per cent dragged lower by investor profit-taking after Thursday's steep rise. The IPC index closed off 35.40 points to 2,375.66. Traders said investors were partly disappointed that the address to the nation by President Ernesto Zedillo failed to deliver details about the country's revised economic plan for 1995. Following Thursday's 3.14 per cent advance, the appointment of Mr Guillermo Ortiz Martinez as Mr Jaime Serra Puche's successor as finance minister did little to encourage markets. Venezuela Caracas stocks ended the last day of trading in 1994 slightly stronger for the sixth successive session. The Merinvest index rose 1.09 per cent to 132.82, Benchmark utility Electricidad de Caracas rose one bolivar to 243 on 131,000 shares. Brazil was closed for a holiday.
