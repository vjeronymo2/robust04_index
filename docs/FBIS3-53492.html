Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A protocol on cooperation between the Great Jamahiriyah and Ukraine in the economic, commercial and cultural spheres as well as in the spheres of oil, transportation and installations was signed in Tripoli at noon today. The protocol was signed for the Libyan side by the brother assistant secretary general for international affairs of the General People's Committee for International Liaison and International Cooperation, and for the Ukrainian side by Mikhayl Pavlovych, head of the first directorate of the Ukrainian Foreign Ministry.
