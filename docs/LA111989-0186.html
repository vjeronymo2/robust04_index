November 19, 1989, Sunday, South Bay Edition

PEPPERDINE WOMEN SPIKERS SWEEP LOYOLA

Pepperdine completed a sweep of the West Coast Conference women's volleyball title Saturday with a 15-9, 15-8, 15-12 victory over Loyola Marymount in Gersten Pavilion. Pepperdine finished conference play at 14-0 (20-8 overall). Loyola fell to 7-7 and 8-18.

Tami Seidenberg led Pepperdine's attack with 13 kills and nine digs. Freshman Raquel Glenn topped Loyola with 11 kills and seven digs.

The Lions complete the season Tuesday with a nonconference match at UC Santa Barbara.
