920709 FT 09 JUL 92 / International Company News: C. Itoh seeks group listings TRADING HOUSE C. Itoh wants to list two group companies on the second section of the Tokyo Stock Exchange, with the aim of revitalising the group and seeking capital gains, Reuter reports from Tokyo. The two are chemicals maker C. I. Kasei and distributor C. Itoh Warehouse and Transport. C. Itoh said it hopes to list two or three group companies a year.
