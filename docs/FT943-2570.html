940919 FT 19 SEP 94 / Rudeness 'common' in jobs market Rudeness and discourtesy towards job applicants is widespread, the Industrial Society says in a report published today. Ms Julie Amber, the society's recruitment specialist, said she had found a surprising number of personnel managers who treated job applicants with complete disregard and rudeness. She found that many never replied to applicants who failed to be shortlisted and that treating people discourteously at interview was commonplace.
