Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] Ichiro Ozawa, general secretary of Shinseito [Japan Renewal Party], is reportedly preparing to visit the United States to break an impasse in the Japan-U.S. relations. Asked by a group of reporters about Mr. Ozawa's visit to Washington at the prime minister's official residence on 10 March, Prime Minister Hosokawa said: "I have not heard of it at all." Asked by a reporter if he has ever discussed the issue with Mr. Ozawa, the prime minister said: "I have not discussed it with him. When I inquired of each ministry and agency about his reported visit to Washington, all of them said they did not know about his visit to the United States."
