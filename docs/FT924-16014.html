921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / International Company News: Armco warns of bigger loss ARMCO, the US steelmaker, yesterday warned its third-quarter loss would be larger than expected, reflecting softness in the aerospace, automotive and some electrical markets, writes Karen Zagor in New York. It said its deficit would be slightly more than the Dollars 15m, or 17 cents, second-quarter loss, excluding one-time items.
