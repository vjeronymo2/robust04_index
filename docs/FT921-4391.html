920310 FT 10 MAR 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea A strong demand prevailed reports the Tea Brokers Association. Bright and good medium Africans ruled fully firm to dearer while the remainder were irregular but little changed overall. There were very few withdrawals. Ceylons showed a dearer tendency. Offshore fairly good demand but at slightly lower levels for most descriptions. The highest price realised this week was 171p for a rwanda pf. 1. Quotations quality 155p, nom good medium 110p medium 92p low medium 80p.
