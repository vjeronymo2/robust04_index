920730 FT 30 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Hickson in Pounds 1m flooring disposal HICKSON Flooring Distri-butors, a subsidiary of Hickson International, the chemicals group, has sold the Leeds branch of its floor coverings distribution business to Alvin Morris, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Mercado Holdings. The consideration is expected to amount to Pounds 1.03m cash. An initial Pounds 900,000 has been paid. The disposal continues Hickson's strategy of divesting its floor coverings division and concentrating on its core chemical businesses.
