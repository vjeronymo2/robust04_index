940509 FT 09 MAY 94 / UK Company News: P&P purchase for up to Pounds 6.6m P&P, the computer hardware supplier and services company, is buying Computers for Business (Scotland) for up to Pounds 6.6m. The company also reported an encouraging start to the present year to November 30 1994. Profitable contributions continued to be made by most areas of the business and earnings were showing significant progress. An initial Pounds 3.74m for 94.3 per cent of CFB will be satisfied by the issue of 4.5m shares, valued at Pounds 3.11m, Pounds 520,724 in loan notes and Pounds 108,597 cash. P&P has an option to buy the outstanding shares for an initial Pounds 260,000 cash. A further Pounds 1m payment is variable depending on net assets at completion and there is an additional profit-related payment to a maximum of Pounds 1.6m. The additional payments will be satisfied by a mixture of shares, loan notes and cash. CFB reported 1993 pre-tax profits of Pounds 530,157 (Pounds 606,000) on turnover of Pounds 15m (Pounds 12.1m).
