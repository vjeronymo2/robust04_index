941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / World News in Brief: European Monetary System Rankings on the EMS grid scarcely changed last week and there was little movement on the European crosses in spite of turmoil on world financial markets. With the German elections looming, the D-Mark weakened, bringing it down below the Irish punt. The Dutch guilder slipped back slightly and the French franc strengthened fractionally.
