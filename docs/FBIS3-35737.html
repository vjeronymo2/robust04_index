Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 27/1/1994 -- During yesterday's session, Moldovan Parliamentary Presidium ratified the Moldovan-Ukrainian intergovernmental agreement on negotiating the state credit granted to Ukraine by Moldova in 1992. The Parliamentary Press Service informed BASA that the debts between the two states for 1993 were not calculated yet and the credit examined by the Presidium supposes the attestation of a sum of $28,400,000 dollars indebted by Ukraine to Moldova for Moldovan foodstuffs and industrial products. The credit will be reimbursed chiefly by supplying energy agents from Ukraine. The Presidium also examined the questions concerning the granting of privileges of not paying excises to "Malovata" farm, Dubasari; payment by Moldova of the fee as a member of European Radio and TV Union; approval of expenditures of National Bank and the newspaper NEZAVISIMAYA MOLDOVA for 1994. Also, the Presidium discussed the participation of Parliament's leadership in elections. It was decided that Presidium members will participate in elections without being dismissed, but they will not use the state technical means.
