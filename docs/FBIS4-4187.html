BFN [Text] Antananarivo, 22 Apr (XINHUA) -- President Albert Zafy of Madagascar received Zhu Chengcai, outgoing Chinese ambassador to Madagascar, over here today. He greatly praised the friendly and cooperative ties between Madagascar and China, and expressed the hope to see such ties continue developing. Zafy pointed out: Madagascar and China are both developing nations. Therefore, they share the same views on many international issues, particularly those concerning the quest for development. He thanked China for providing aid to Madagascar. He also spoke highly of the hardworking and disciplined Chinese who reside in Madagascar and contribute to the country's economic development. Before the meeting, Malagasy Prime Minister Ravony and An-de-li-ya-man-gu [1344 1795 6849 0068 2581 6328], president of the Malagasy Popular National Assembly, met Zhu Chengcai on separate occasions, and exchanged opinions with him on ways to further consolidate bilateral ties.
