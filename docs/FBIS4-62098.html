BFN [Viktor Artemenko report from Smolensk: "Smolensk `Electric Bulb' in Belarus Homes"] [Text] It is a pleasant and rare occasion considering the days we live in -- a five-year treaty of cooperation between the Smolensk AES [Atomic Electric Power Station) and the Belorussian [Belarus] Energy Ministry has been signed. The document envisions mutually advantageous terms of technical and material assistance, and the provision of scientific and technical information. This event was preceded by another, no less important event -- a 750-kilovolt power transmission line originating at the "Belorusskaya" substation at the Smolensk AES was put in operation. The 750-kilovolt power transmission line was constructed jointly by builders and operation personnel of Smolensk's Desnogorsk and of Belorussia. A large group of builders and operation personnel of the Smolensk AES were awarded certificates of honor of Belorussia's Supreme Soviet and the Ministry of Power Engineering for this successful work.
