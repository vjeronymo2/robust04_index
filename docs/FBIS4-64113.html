BFN [IPRINDA press agency report under rubric "According to Reports From Correspondents and Information Agencies": "Statement by the Political Council"] [Text] On 17 May, the Georgian Social-Democratic Party (GSDP) Political Council disseminated an appeal to the republic's population in which an analysis is given of the complex sociopolitical situation. Proceeding from this, the GSDP Political Council believes: The head of the Georgian state should resign and, at the same time, a national salvation committee that will be able to implement laws should be formed. Otherwise, considers the Political Council, it will be impossible to speak about the territorial integrity of Georgia, its independence, and the physical salvation of the Georgian nation.
