940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / Camdessus meets Algerian leader Mr Michel Camdessus, managing director of the International Monetary Fund, yesterday met Algerian President Liamine Zeroual to discuss an economic restructuring programme, writes Our Foreign staff. Agreement is conditional on Algeria accepting major economic reforms and devaluing the dinar. It is expected to lead to a Paris Club rescheduling of that part of Algeria's foreign debt which is guaranteed by foreign governments - about Dollars 13bn (Pounds 8.9bn) out of a total figure of Dollars 26bn.
