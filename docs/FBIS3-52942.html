Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Riyadh, 8 Jan -- His Royal Highness Prince Sultan Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, the second deputy prime minister, minister of defense and aviation, and inspector general, received in his palace in Riyadh this evening French Defense Minister Francois Leotard and the delegation accompanying him. His Royal Highness Prince Sultan Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz held a meeting with Mr. Francois Leotard, which was attended on the Saudi side by His Royal Highness Prince Sa'ud al-Faysal Al Sa'ud, the minister of foreign affairs; Mr. Ibrahim al-Anqari, special adviser to his majesty, the custodian of the two holy mosques; Muhammad Aba-al-Khayl, the finance and national economy minister; and Health Minister Faysal al-Hujaylan. The meeting was attended on the French side by Foreign Minister Alain Juppe, the French ambassador to the Kingdom, Mr. Jean Bressot [name and title as received], and the delegation accompanying the French defense minister.
