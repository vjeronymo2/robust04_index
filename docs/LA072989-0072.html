July 29, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

CHINA HONORS MORE SOLDIERS

Chinese newspapers said Friday that 12 more soldiers have been named "Defenders of the Republic" for their role in crushing pro-democracy demonstrations in June, bringing to 22 the number awarded the title.

An order signed by top leader Deng Xiaoping praised the 12 for bravery in breaching protesters' barricades, saving stricken comrades and other acts during the army assault on Tian An Men Square, the People's Daily said.
