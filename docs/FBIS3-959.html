Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] President 'Umar al-Bashir of Sudan arrived in Nairobi at noon today and was received by his host, President Daniel arap Moi, at the Jomo Kenyatta International Airport. President al-Bashir is here to attend the Intergovernmental Authority on Drought and Development, IGADD, summit to launch the peace talks on Sudan. IGADD's Sudanese peace initiative is chaired by President Moi and aims at restoring peace in the war ravaged southern Sudan. [passage omitted]
