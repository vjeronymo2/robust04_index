940512 FT 12 MAY 94 / International Company News: Conrad Black paid CDollars 2.4m at Hollinger Hollinger, the Canadian newspaper holding company, said its chairman Mr Conrad Black received CDollars 2.4m (USDollars 1.7m) in remuneration in 1993. It added that his wife, journalist Barbara Amiel, was being nominated to the board. Mr Black has a 45.4 per cent controlling interest in Hollinger. He received a CDollars 1.56m base salary, plus CDollars 282,000 bonus and CDollars 581,000 in other compensation.
