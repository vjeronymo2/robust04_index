Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanaa, Jan 14 (AFP) -- A Briton and a Canadian held for 10 days by a Yemeni tribe east of here were freed on Friday [14 January], a spokesman for the U.S. company Hunt Oil which employed them said. The two men, along with two Yemenis, who had been held in al-Aymaniyah in the Ma'rib region some 100 kilometres (62 miles) east of Sanaa, arrived in the capital "in good health" on Friday, the spokesman said.
