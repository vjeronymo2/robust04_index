940203 FT 03 FEB 94 / Romania pact Romania's ruling Party of Social Democracy said yesterday it had agreed on a coalition with the hardline Romanian National Unity party to include other extreme nationalist and former Communist parties, writes Virginia Marsh from Bucharest. The two parties said they aimed to include the Socialist Labour party, self-proclaimed heir to the Romanian Communist party, and the anti-Semitic, xenophobic Greater Romania party. They hope to conclude talks by the end of the month.
