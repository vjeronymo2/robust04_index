April 19, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: SAN DIMAS;

MCHENRY MAYOR PRO TEM

Sandy McHenry, reelected to the City Council April 10, has been elected mayor pro tem by his colleagues on the council.

He replaces Maria Tortorelli, who did not run for reelection this year.

Mayor Terry Dipple, who ran unopposed in the April 10 election, will continue as mayor for two more years.
