May 22, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Cargo Services Sold: T. G. Shown Associates Inc., a private company in Laguna Hills, said it agreed to sell Northwest Airlines' cargo services in seven states. Under the general sales agency agreement, T. G. Shown will sell Northwest's air freight and small-package service in cities where Northwest does not have its own dedicated sales representatives. The states covered by the agreement are Michigan, Wisconsin, Indiana, Ohio, Alabama, Kentucky and Tennessee.
