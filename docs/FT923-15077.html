920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / Parliament and Politics: Tory media man to quit MR Shaun Woodward, the Conservative party's director of communications, said yesterday that he was to stand down. He said he had 'received offers' from City firms and academic institutions but wanted to consider his options before making a decision. During the general election campaign he faced criticism over the Tory effort, but yesterday won praise from Mr John Major and Sir Norman Fowler, Tory party chairman.
