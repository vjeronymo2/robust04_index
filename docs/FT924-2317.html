921214 FT 14 DEC 92 / Dushanbe battles rage Gun battles raged in suburbs of the Tajik capital yesterday with Islamic guerrillas stepping up hit-and-run attacks two days after the arrival of a new government in Dushanbe, Reuter reports from Dushanbe. The guerrillas set ablaze fuel storage tanks and fought with ex-communist gunmen in the city's southern Islamic stronghold of Avul where a senior Russian army officer said six people were killed in clashes which knocked out a thermal station providing much of the heating of the city.
