940818 FT 18 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Armitage Brothers increases 22% Pre-tax profits rose 22 per cent at Armitage Brothers, the pet products manufacturer, in the year to May 29. Turnover was up 3 per cent to Pounds 22.7m and generated profits of Pounds 1.07m, compared with Pounds 875,000. Earnings per share rose from 14.6p to 17.9p. A recommended final dividend of 3.7p makes a total of 6.4p (6.2p) for the year.
