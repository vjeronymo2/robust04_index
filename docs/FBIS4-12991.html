BFN [Excerpt] Qiryat Shemona, Israel, April 15 (AFP)--The army on Friday [15 April] urged Israelis living near the border with Lebanon to take shelter for fear of more rocket attacks. Soldiers in vehicles with loudspeakers drove through northern communities urging residents to go down to underground shelters at 1:00 p.m. (1000 GMT), an AFP correspondent said here. [passage omitted]
