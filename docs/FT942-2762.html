940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Robert Lowe to raise Pounds 3m via placing Robert H Lowe, the clothing, printing and packaging company, is raising about Pounds 3m net in a placing and open offer. It is proposed to use the funds to cut borrowings and provide additional working capital. There is a firm placing of 3.25m new ordinary shares with Strand Associates, an investment company, and of 3.25m with the chairman's family trust, both at 8p per share to raise Pounds 520,000. Further, there is to be a placing and open offer of three new ordinary for every 40 held and 21 new ordinary for every four convertible held to raise a further Pounds 2.7m before expenses. There will be a consolidation and sub-division of the existing ordinary shares with conversion of the existing convertible preference shares into ordinaries on a seven-for-one basis. At November 5 last, shareholders' funds were Pounds 402,000 and net borrowings came out at Pounds 4.4m.
