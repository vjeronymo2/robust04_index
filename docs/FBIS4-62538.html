BFN [Text] An agreement between the Latvian and Romanian Governments, which aims at the introduction of most favored nation status in trade between the two countries, was approved at a session of the Foreign Affairs and Security Committee of the Cabinet of Ministers today. According to the government information agency, several agreements with subjects of the Russian Federation were also approved at the session: on trade and economic cooperation between the Latvian and Karelian governments, and between Latvia and the Mari El Republic, as well as an agreement between the Latvian Ministry of Economics and the Russian Ministry of Machine Building [as heard] on cooperation in the machine building sector. The composition of a government commission to monitor the implementation of the accord on the Russian troop withdrawal and the package of agreements was approved at the committee's session. In addition, the committee decided to set up a working group for drafting concepts of civil defense and a center for dealing with emergencies.
