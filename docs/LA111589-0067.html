November 15, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Weighty Matters for Oprah: It's been exactly one year since Oprah Winfrey revealed to the world the secret of her 67-pound weight loss. But has she kept it off? Winfrey is poised to answer that weighty question today and will also talk about the difficult aspect of dieting on her syndicated TV show, airing at 3 p.m. on Channels 7 and 42. ALEENE MacMINN
