BFN [Text] At the 41st Bulgarian Socialist Party [BSP] Congress, which opened today, party leader Zhan Videnov read a report on the party's political line. Sasha Karakaksheva, chairperson of the BSP All-Party Control Commission, reported on this commission's activity. Nora Ananieva, chairperson of the BSP parliamentary faction, reported on the faction's activity. A news conference took place after the end of the first session. Zhan Videnov told the news conference that the Socialists expect to receive more than 40 percent of the votes at the next elections, but they have no intention of usurping power. The BSP categorically denies that it was the perpetrator of criminal rule, Videnov said. Nora Ananieva said that the BSP parliamentary faction will implement the congress decisions, but it will act in a flexible manner. A debate on Zhan Videnov's report on the party's political line has opened.
