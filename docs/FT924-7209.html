921118 FT 18 NOV 92 / UK Company News: BTR raises Dollars 13m from US sale BTR, the industrial conglomerate, has sold US Supply, a Missouri-based distributor of plumbing products, for Dollars 13.2m (Pounds 8.7m) cash. The buyer was Ferguson Enterprises, the largest wholesale plumbing distributor in the US, and a subsidiary of Wolseley, the UK building distribution company. BTR acquired US Supply as part of Thomas Tilling in 1983 and put it into the Summers distribution group in 1987.
