Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (March 1) TURAN--"Recently the pressure on the democracy and freedom of speech in Azerbaijan intensified", says distributed today appeal of Baku's newspaper "AZADLYG." The document reports that newspaper "AZADLYG" has been openly subjecting to persecution for the last time. During the past days the police of Baku's Sabail region has been repeatedly searching the building of the editing house of the newspaper. The policemen armed with machine guns encircled the building. The representatives of the police and the authorities gave no details of their activities to the journalists. The police seized a room in the editing house of the newspaper by force. Today's issue of "AZADLYG" is banned by the political censorship. This morning the employees of located there organizations "AZADLYG", and TURAN NEWS AGENCY were not allowed to enter the building. In this connection "AZADLYG" newspaper addressed an appeal to the world community and Baku-based foreign Embassies. It says that basic democratic freedoms are brutally violated in Azerbaijan. "We hope that World Democratic Institutions will express their attitude towards present situation in Azerbaijan", stresses this document. It should be stressed that independent "AZADLYG" newspaper has the biggest circulation in Azerbaijan.
