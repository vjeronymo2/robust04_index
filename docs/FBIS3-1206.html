Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] New Prime Minister Roch Marc Christian Kabore yesterday granted an exclusive interview to Radio Burkina. The current prime minister, appointed by presidential decree, is not new to the political scene. He is 37 years old and has held successive posts as minister of transport and communications from 1989 to 1990, minister of state from 1990 to 1992, minister of state in charge of finance and planning from 1992 to 1993, and then minister in charge of relations with institutions.
