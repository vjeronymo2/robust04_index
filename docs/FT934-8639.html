931115 FT 15 NOV 93 / Arafat urged to curb militants Ministers in Prime Minister Yitzhak Rabin's Israeli government yesterday called on PLO leader Yassir Arafat to do more to control Palestinian militants who are attacking Israeli targets in the occupied territories, David Horovitz reports from Jerusalem. Mr Arafat has condemned the killing last month of a Jewish settler, Haim Mizrahi, allegedly by five activists from his own Fatah faction of the PLO. There were also reports last night that Mr Arafat and Mr Rabin are to hold a second summit in Cairo next month.
