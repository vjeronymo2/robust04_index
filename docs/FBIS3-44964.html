Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 5 Jan (XINHUA) -- The CHINA JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS PAPER [Zhongguo Chuzhongsheng Bao 0022 0948 0443 0022 3932 1032] today published a message which extends its heartfelt gratitude to all concerned teachers and friends. The message said: In the past five years since its founding, the CHINA JUNIOR HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS PAPER, as a young seedling, has enjoyed support and cultivation from various sectors. So many people who cherish the youth have extended their love: Proletarian revolutionaries of the older generation, including Comrade Deng Xiaoping, who inscribed our paper's title; Comrade Kang Keqing, who personally came to the press office to encourage us to produce a good paper for the children; and Bing Xin and Xia Yan, who are more than 90 years old and who inscribed for the paper.... We should diligentily carry out this sacred mission and wholeheartedly serve in this undertaking for young people.
