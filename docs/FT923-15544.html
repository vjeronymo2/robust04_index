920701 FT 01 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Dwyer incurs loss of Pounds 731,000 Dwyer, the property investment company, incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 731,000 for the six months ended March 31, against a Pounds 644,000 profit last time. Losses per share increased to 5.67p (0.34p) and the interim dividend is omitted - 1.5p was paid previously.
