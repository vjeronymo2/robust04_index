940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / UK Company News: SWP advances 31% SWP Group, the USM-quoted maker of specialist components for the construction industry, increased pre-tax profits by 31 per cent from Pounds 170,000 to Pounds 222,000 in the year to June 30. Sales from continuing operations jumped to Pounds 8.78m (Pounds 1.08m). But raw materials and consumables took Pounds 4.43m (Pounds 488,000) and staff costs surged to Pounds 2.46m (Pounds 509,000). Interest charges fell to Pounds 136,000 (Pounds 144,000). Earnings per share came to 0.6p (0.5p).
