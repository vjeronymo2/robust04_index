930406 FT 06 APR 93 / London Stock Exchange: Budget Changes The Financial Times will implement with effect from April 5 the Budget reduction in the income tax rate applied to dividends to 20 per cent from 25 per cent. This will affect gross dividend yields shown for individual companies in the London Share Service, and those calculated for the FT-SE Actuaries UK Share Indices and the FT-Actuaries World Indices.
