CSO [Summary] In 1993 the cotton area in Liaoning Province was 530,000 mu, and the average per mu yield was 55 kilograms, an increase of 30.7 kilograms over 1992. As of 22 February national cotton procurement totaled 45 million dan, a decrease of 22,350,000 dan from the same period in 1993. As of 22 February Liaoning had procured 200,000 dan, a decrease of 225,000 dan from the same period in 1993.
