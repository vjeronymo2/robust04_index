BFN [Text] At the last experts meeting about the Pact of Stability in Athens, Slovakia succeeded in pushing through its demands. This has created conditions to enable the Pact of Stability to become a suitable platform for the implementation of the foreign policy interests of Slovakia. This was said by Prime Minister Jozef Moravcik in an interview given to the Press Agency of the Slovak Republic in connection with the preparation of his official visit to France, where he is to discuss, among other things, this initiative. Prime Minister Moravcik envisages that, at this stage, the drafting of the documents for the Pact of Stability is acceptable to Slovakia. In this connection, he stressed that the pact unequivocally respects and promotes the principle of the inviolability and immutability of borders.
