940421 FT 21 APR 94 / Buthelezi banks on poll miracle Chief Mangosuthu Buthelezi, leader of the mainly Zulu Inkatha Freedom party, yesterday told supporters that it would be a miracle if his party did well in next week's South African elections, given its late entry to the race. The chief, pictured alongside his party's flag at the election rally, was launching his campaign in Ulundi, capital of the KwaZulu 'homeland'. Report, Page 30
