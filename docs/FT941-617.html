940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Bournemouth Water Bournemouth Water, which supplies water to the Bournemouth and Dorset area, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 4.75m to Pounds 4.84m in 1993. Turnover advanced more strongly, from Pounds 12.1m to Pounds 12.7m. Earnings per share fell from 469p to 285p basic and from 320p fully diluted. A proposed final dividend of 91p makes 120p (86p) for the year. The company's parent is Biwater.
