BFN [Report by Yusuf Shakir: "Russian Ambassador to Cairo: We Have Stopped Arms Shipments to Yemen; Our Recognition of South Depends on Brahimi's Report"] [Text] Cairo -- Russian Ambassador to Cairo Vladimir Polyakov has denied that Russia has provided any military aid to the parties to the Yemeni conflict. He revealed that Moscow has stopped sending arms shipments to the two warring parties. He stressed that his country's recognition or nonrecognition of the South Yemen state will be determined in the light of the report UN envoy Lakhdar Brahimi is to submit to UN Secretary General Dr. Butrus-Ghali. The Russian ambassador said following his meeting yesterday with Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid that he had informed the League that Moscow has instructed him to coordinate positions with the Arab League vis-a-vis the Yemeni crisis and to closely monitor what is happening in Yemen. He pointed out that Russia supports an immediate cease-fire and the initiation of a direct dialogue among the conflicting parties. He added that this is in line with the Arab League's position. He revealed that the stage ahead will witness a Russian move on the current situation in Yemen through the results Brahimi is going to reveal in his report on the crisis. However, he did not specify the nature of the move.
