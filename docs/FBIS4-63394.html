BFN [Text] President Boris Yeltsin has no reason to distrust or punish his Interior Minister Viktor Yerin, presidential spokesman Vyacheslav Kostikov said in a statement issued in Moscow on Friday. This follows a proposal from a number of parliamentary factions that the minister be dismissed following the recent assassination of Russian deputy Andrey Aizderdzis. The president has examined materials related to the case and thinks the ministry's actions to be prompt and impeccable, the statement says. It also quotes Yeltsin as saying there has been certain progress in the ministry's campaign against crime and corruption. The spokesman said the question of Yerin's dismissal was not on the agenda.
