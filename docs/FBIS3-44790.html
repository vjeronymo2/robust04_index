Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] In the course of seizing the opportunity to develop their economies, different localities in Guangxi have paid attention to commodity sales. Commodity sales in various localities are thriving amid stability. In society, commodity sales have seen a comparatively large increase. According to statistics, from January to November, Guangxi registered an increase of 20.8 percent over last year in total commodity sales. Five cities registered commodity sales increases of more than 23 percent. The total retail sale volumes of Fangchenggang, Beihai, and Nanning increased by 48 percent, 34.9 percent, and 33 percent respectively. There were varying increases in commodity sales in the eight prefectures of the region, with Guilin, Wuzhou, and Yulin registering growth rates higher than Guangxi's average.
