Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev, 9 Mar -- Yuriy Karasyk, Ukraine's minister of agriculture and food, has reported that a result of the visit by a Ukrainian Government delegation to the United States was, in particular, the signing of agreements on Ukrainian-U.S. cooperation in the agrarian sector, [agreements] on a commission of experts, and on opening credit lines. An adviser on agrarian issues will come to Ukraine from the United States in April within the framework of these agreements. Twenty million dollars of concessionary credit will be handed over to Ukraine to purchase soya and so will 40 million dollars of commercial credit (at an annual interest rate of five percent) for other agrarian sector needs.
