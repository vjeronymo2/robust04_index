Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Council of Ministers held its weekly ordinary session today under Prime Minister Ahmad Husayn Khudayyir. Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz attended the meeting. In its session today, the council reviewed the efforts of the Agriculture Ministry and the committees entrusted with carrying out the grand agricultural campaign for the 1993-94 winter season to fulfill the requirements of the campaign. The council decided to follow up in detail the implementation of the campaign and its results at the beginning of every weekly meeting of the Council of Ministers. The council discussed other topics on its agenda, and approved an increase in the capital of the Industrial Bank from 75 million dinars to 150 million to encourage investment by the private industrial sector and in response to the requests presented to the bank. The council also discussed organizational matters, and adopted the required decisions and recommendations.
