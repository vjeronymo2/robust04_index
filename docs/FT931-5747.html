930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Overheads reduce Stat-Plus INCREASED overhead costs at its Leeds and Leicester sales and distribution centres were behind a 25 per cent fall at Stat-Plus, the office and legal stationery retailer, in 1992. On sales little changed at Pounds 11.5m, the pre-tax figure fell from Pounds 4.81m to Pounds 3.61m. Earnings per share were 11.3p (15p). An increased final dividend of 4.44p is recommended, for a total of 8p (7.125p).
