BFN [Text] The Zairian involvement in the Angolan conflict continues. In Malanje Province Zairian citizens are fighting alongside the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA]. Our correspondent Francisco Kuringana has further details. A total of 48 civilians who fled from areas controlled by the rebels today presented themselves to the government forces stationed in Quemba area, 14 km east of Malanje. These civilians said Zairian citizens not only fight alongside UNITA forces but also carry out trade activities in Caculama District and accord logistical assistance to Jonas Savimbi's men.
