Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] The press service of the Defense Ministry has reported on the mechanism for the subsequent replacement of the Ukrainian peacekeeping battalion in former Yugoslavia. The personnel of the 420-strong unit is to be replaced during this and the following months. The battalion will be reinforced by another unit that will be entrusted by the command of blue helmets deployed in Bosnia, with controlling the vicinity of the city of Srebrenitsa. All of the Ukrainian draftees are volunteers and serve in the Odessa Military District. Currently, they are completing a special language course.
