BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Husamettin Cindoruk, Turkish Grand National Assembly [TGNA] speaker, has left for Bulgaria as the official guest of the Bulgarian National Assembly Speaker Georgi Yordanov [name and title as received]. In a statement he made prior to his departure at Istanbul's Ataturk Airport, Cindoruk stated the Turkish-Bulgarian friendship has gained momentum in recent years. Explaining that important changes regarding the democratization process are taking place in Bulgaria, Cindoruk stressed that Turkey gives great importance to the economic and political development of the region. Cindoruk and the parliamentary delegation accompanying him will be returning to the country on 6 April.
