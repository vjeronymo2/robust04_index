Language: Slovene Article Type:BFN [Text] According to the data provided by the Bank of Slovenia, our country's foreign exchange reserves amounted to $1.472 billion by the end of November last year. In November they increased by $13 million. In the same month, our country's foreign debt amounted to $1.801 billion. Until the end of last year, we paid out 333 [million] out of 1.680 billion of the long-term debt. This year, our country's long-term debt should be reduced by $481 million more.
