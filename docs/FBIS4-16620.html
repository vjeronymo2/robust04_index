BFN [Text] Today, a meeting was held in Almaty of representatives of Supreme Kenges deputies from all the regions of the Republic. At the request of the parliamentarians, President Nazarbayev took part and spoke to the deputies. He congratulated the people's deputies on the high confidence in them and wished them every success in their work. The head of state shared his opinion on the nature of the work and activity expected from the Supreme Kenges of the new convocation. He expressed his hope that from the very beginning the first ever democratically elected professional parliament will work constructively in the interest of the state and for the whole Kazakh nation. The president stated his readiness to work in a close, concerted, and efficient way with the Supreme Kenges in order to lift our society out of the crisis as quickly as possible and to pave the way toward a civilized future. Nursultan Nazarbayev believes that the Kazakhstani parliamentarians have every possibility to formulate their own specific style of law-making, which will take into account the particular features of our Republic, in particular its multiethnic population. He also answered deputies' questions. At issue were proposals regarding an initiative deputy group on how to begin the first session of the Supreme Kenges in an organized way. The proposals were approved in general. On the same day, at the House of parliament, Central Election Commission Chairman Karatay Turysov awarded the deputies their newly designed badges. The session will open tomorrow at 1000.
