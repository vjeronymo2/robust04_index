930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Real Time Control down to Pounds 492,000 Real Time Control, the USM-quoted computer systems group, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 492,000 for the year to end-March, against Pounds 1.19m. Turnover dipped from Pounds 6.84m to Pounds 6.11m. Earnings came out at 4.9p (11.1p); the dividend is maintained at 3p.
