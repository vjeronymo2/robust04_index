940114 FT 14 JAN 94 / Crash victims' sons win Pounds 80,000 The two youngest sons of a couple who died in the M1 Kegworth air crash five years ago were awarded Pounds 80,000 agreed damages against British Midland Airways in the High Court yesterday. Mr Brian Chilvers and his wife Anne were returning to Northern Ireland from London when the Boeing 737 crashed, killing 47 people. They left five children, the youngest two of whom were still dependent on them.
