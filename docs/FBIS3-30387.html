Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] At the Council of Ministers on 2 February, a meeting took place between Prime Minister Lyuben Berov and the parliamentary faction of the New Alliance for Democracy [NSD]. The meeting took place at the invitation of Mr. Berov. According to NSD Deputy Chairman Stefan Bozhilov, the draft budget, the proposed no-confidence vote, and certain general social and political issues were discussed at the meeting. The NSD adheres to its decision not to support the no-confidence vote proposed by the Union of Democratic Forces.
