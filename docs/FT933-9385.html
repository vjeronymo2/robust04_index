930810 FT 10 AUG 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa BULLION price recovery hopes and the weak financial rand took gold shares higher, the sector index rising 52 to 1,866, and industrials were lifted in aggregate by the news that the conglomerate, Barlow Rand, would announce its unbundling strategy after hours. Industrials rose 20 to 4,580 and the overall index by 31 to 4,041.
