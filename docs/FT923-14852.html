920706 FT 06 JUL 92 / Sikorsky bids for Dutch deal SIKORSKY Aircraft, the helicopter maker which is a subsidiary of US defence group United Technologies, has bid for a potentially large contract to build sea-air rescue helicopters for the Dutch government, Patrick Harverson writes from New York. The Dutch Defence Ministry confirmed on Friday that Sikorsky had presented a bid for three helicopters, the initial phase of a Dollars 1.75bn (Pounds 916m) programme to replace all 86 military helicopters flown by Dutch forces.
