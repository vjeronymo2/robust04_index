EFFECTIVE DATE: June 17, 1994. 21 CFR Parts 510 and 522 Animal Drugs, Feeds, and Related Products; Change of Sponsor AGENCY: Food and Drug Administration, HHS. ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is amending the animal drug regulations to reflect a change of sponsor for a new animal drug application (NADA) from Boehringer Ingelheim Animal Health, Inc., to Phoenix Scientific, Inc. EFFECTIVE DATE: June 17, 1994. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Benjamin A. Puyot, Center for Veterinary Medicine (HFV&hyph;130), Food and Drug Administration, 7500 Standish Pl., Rockville, MD 20855, 301&hyph;594&hyph;1646. SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: Boehringer Ingelheim Animal Health, Inc., 2621 North Belth Hwy., St. Joseph, MO 64506&hyph;2002, has informed FDA that it has transferred ownership of, and all rights and interests in NADA 99&hyph;169 for Oxytocin Injection to Phoenix Scientific, Inc., 3915 South 48th St. Terrace, P.O. Box 6457, St. Joseph, MO, 64506&hyph;0457. Accordingly, the agency is amending the regulations in 21 CFR 510.600(c)(1) and (c)(2) and in 21 CFR 522.1680(b) to reflect the change of sponsor.
