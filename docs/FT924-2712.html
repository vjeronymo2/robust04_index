921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / Defence switch found to be slow THE MINISTRY of Defence has made slow progress in replacing service personnel with less costly civilian staff, the National Audit Office reports today. The percentage of civilians employed in the three services was the same in 1991 as in 1988, the public-expenditure watchdog says. Ministry of Defence: Developments in Control and Use of Manpower. HMSO. Pounds 5.85.
