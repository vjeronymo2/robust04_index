Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Libyan authorities have denied reports from the Cairo representative of the opposition Libyan National Salvation Front claiming that several people were shot and wounded on 17 March in Bani Walid, 150 km south of Tripoli, after they attacked public buildings. According to this opposition leader, the demonstrations broke out after Libyan television broadcast false confessions extracted under torture from army officers implicated in a military rebellion in Bani Walid last October. According to the director of Libya's foreign information service, the claims of this opposition leader are hardly credible, given his collaboration with foreign information services, in particular the CIA.
