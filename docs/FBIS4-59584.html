BFN [Text] At his place of residence in New York this morning, Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz held another two sessions as part of his talks with representatives of UN Security Council members. He first met with David Hannay of the United Kingdom and later with Djibouti's Roble Olhay. Mr. 'Aziz is scheduled to confer later today with Russia's Yuliy Vorontsov and Special Commission Chairman Rolf Ekeus.
