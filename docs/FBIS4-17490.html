BFN [Text] Russian President Boris Yeltsin has sent a letter to Alija Izetbegovic, president of the presidency of Bosnia-Herzegovina. Yeltsin writes: It is with great concern and disquiet that we have followed the developments surrounding Gorazde and Bosnia-Herzegovina as a whole. We strongly condemn any activity which results in the killing of the civilian population, Yeltsin writes in his letter to Izetbegovic. Russia has been taking all possible steps to end the bloodshed as soon as possible, to restrain the deadly currents of the war, and to restore a situation that would help in finding a political solution. To this end I sent the Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, to Belgrade. The activity of our special envoy Vitaliy Churkin in Sarajevo and in Pale was aimed at resolving the crisis. We insist on the Bosnian Serbs complying fully with the UN conditions regarding Gorazde, President Yeltsin says in his letter to Alija Izetbegovic.
