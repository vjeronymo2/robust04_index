921223 FT 23 DEC 92 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton and Jute COTTON Spot and shipment sales in Liverpool for the week ended 18 December amounted to 200 tonnes against 228 tonnes in the previous week. Subdued offtake did not bring many operations. Support was forthcoming in certain specialist styles notably in the Central Asian and Mali range.
