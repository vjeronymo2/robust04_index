Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Georgian Prime Minister Otar Patsatsia arrived in Yerevan today. During a meeting at the Armenian Government offices, the Armenian Prime Minister Grant Bagratyan and Otar Patsatsia discussed economic cooperation between the two countries, including the gas supply, protection of the gas pipeline, train transportation, and other matters of mutual interest. The Armenian Deputy Prime Minister Vigen Chitechyan, and State Minister Gagik Shahbazyan also took part in the meeting. The Georgian prime minister left Yerevan today.
