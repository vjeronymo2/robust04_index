BFN [Text] Tbilisi, June 22 (SNARK) -- "If we manage to achieve a considerable progress in cause of Abkhazian conflict's settlement, a process of general stabilization in Caucasian region will begin", declared Eduard Shevardnadze in his interview to SNARK. Answering the question on the ways of the Karabakh problem's settlement, the Head of Georgian State mentioned that the Abkhazian conflict's settlement will be a great spur for solving all the conflicts in Transcaucasia with the participation of Russia. "The form of the Abkhazian conflict's settlement is unique and it will be a good example in searching the ways of solving the Karabakh conflict", said the Head of Georgian State.
