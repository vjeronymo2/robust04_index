December 14, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Integrated Resources Inc.: The financial services firm announced a second-quarter loss of $1.04 billion due to failing businesses and cheap asset sales in a bid to avoid bankruptcy. In the same period a year ago, the New York firm earned $12.8 million. Revenue in the current period totaled $70.6 million, down 38% from last year. The company said the loss exceeded a previously anticipated $600-million decline mainly because of substantially lower than expected proceeds from the sale of its core businesses to Los Angeles-based Broad Inc.
