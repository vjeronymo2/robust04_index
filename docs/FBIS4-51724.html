BFN [Currency in Singapore dollars] [Text] Singapore registered strong growth in external trade last month. The volume grew more than 13 percent on a three-month moving average basis. The value of the total trade jumped about 11 percent to 24 billion dollars. Import volume also rose 11 percent to over 12 billion dollars. Non-oil exports saw a 17 percent rise in volume as shipments to both the U.S. and Japan recorded rises. Deliveries to the U.S. were up 13.5 percent and a surge of 24.7 percent to Japan. This growth was largely in integrated circuits, computers, and computer peripherals. But non-oil exports to the EC dipped 2.9 percent.
