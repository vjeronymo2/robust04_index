BFN [Manki Ponomarev report: "Bill Clinton: Russia Is a Great Power"] [Text] U.S. President Bill Clinton, in Europe to celebrate the 50th anniversary of the landing of allied troops in Normandy, is being subjected to a concentrated assault by representatives of the mass media. They are taking the opportunity to interview the head of the White House, to ascertain his views on particular topical issues, and to make them known to the public. In one such interview which Clinton granted to French television, he specially dwelt on relations with Russia and declared that he was "very satisfied" with their development. In this connection the President emphasized that Russia "really is a great power." At the upcoming G-7 meeting in Italy, he pointed out, Russia will take part at a "higher level than ever" and will participate fully in the discussion of political questions. Touching on the problem of the Russian Federation Armed Forces' participation in settling conflicts in the former Soviet republics, Bill Clinton pointed out that "Russia can play a leading role in resolving certain problems in that region." But on condition, he remarked, that "the UN and other multilateral forces participate" in this process. "Thus it is not a question of restoring the empire. Great power -- yes; empire -- no," the head of the White House concluded.
