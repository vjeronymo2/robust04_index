930818 FT 18 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Stonehill expands to Pounds 264,000 PROFITS before tax of Stonehill Holdings expanded from Pounds 49,000 to Pounds 264,000 for the year ended March 31. The results reflected the acquisition of Cathay International United Investments. With the acquisition the group's activities and operations are focused predominantly on the People's Republic of China. There are significant opportunities to expand in China through acquisitions and joint ventures, and by developing CIU's existing operations at the Landmark Hotel in Shenzhen. In the UK the group continued to make progress at the Stonehill Business Park and with the management contract relating to the Montpelier Business Park in Ashford. The furniture division was closed at the end of the year. Turnover from continuing operations was Pounds 3.95m (Pounds 4.56m) with operating profit at Pounds 1.87m (Pounds 1.83m). Net interest charges dropped to Pounds 1.6m (Pounds 1.79m). Earnings per share came to 0.027p (losses 0.042p). It is planned to reorganise the capital. Preference dividends are in arrears totalling Pounds 252,000.
