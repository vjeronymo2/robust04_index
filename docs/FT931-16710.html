930105 FT 05 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Barclays sells stake in Israeli retail bank Barclays has sold its stake in an Israeli joint-venture retail bank for an undisclosed sum. The clearing bank said it sold its holding in Barclays Discount Bank - which it set up with Israel Discount Bank in 1971 - to CP Holdings, a private company controlled by Mr Bernard Schreier, a UK-based businessman. It said the move was part of its strategy of concentrating resources on core businesses.
