BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS] [Text] Dushanbe 13 April, TASS -- A conflict caused by an incident in the Darvas direction of the Tajik-Afghan border on 12 April, when a detachment of Pamir militants blocked the passage for a Russian motorized convoy, was settled at 1530 local time Wednesday as a result of talks, ITAR-TASS learned from Russian military sources. This provocation was aimed at seizing ammunition carried by convoy trucks. Russian border guards managed to block both initiators of this action and militants of another group who came to their rescue. As a result of a dialogue which continued for several hours the road was cleared. According to Russian servicemen, this incident was brought to the notice of the authorities of the Gorno-Badakhshan autonomous region in the hope that they will give an adequate response.
