BFN [Text] On 7 May, at the Vietnam Scientific-Technological Associations Union Office in Hanoi, Comrade Nong Duc Manh, member of the party Central Committee Political Bureau and chairman of the National Assembly held a working session with representatives and members of various scientific-technological associations. After hearing scientists, representing more than 300,000 members of the union, report on the activities and achievements of the union during the past period as well as its measures aimed at creating favorable conditions for scientific-technological associations to carry out their tasks effectively, Comrade Nong Duc Manh commended the union members for their efforts and high sense of responsibility. He highly appraised the role and position of the intelligentsia representing various scientific-technological associations in Vietnam. He said that these people have engaged in research and diversified tasks which have positively contributed to the renovation process. He expressed the hope that various scientific-technological associations would draw on experiences and conduct more creative research to improve scientific projects and apply scientific-technological innovations to the socioeconomic development program, thereby contributing to building the law-governed state of the people, by the people, and for the people. He also called on these associations to positively participate in the struggle against hunger and poverty, thus contributing to implementing the objectives of making our people prosperous, our country powerful, and our society just and civilized.
