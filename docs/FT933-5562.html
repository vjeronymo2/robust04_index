930902 FT 02 SEP 93 / International Company News: Lauritzen trade fall LAURITZEN, the shipping and shipbuilding group, made a loss of DKr73m for the six months ended June, compared with a profit in the same period last year of DKr18m. Turnover slipped to DKr6.79bn from DKr6.85bn. Order book at the Danish shipyards and industrial companies, however, increased to DKr4.3bn from DKr3.4bn. The group warned that its results for the full year were likely to be lower than in 1992.
