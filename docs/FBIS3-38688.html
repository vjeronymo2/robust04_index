Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Telekom, ZDF, Kirch, and Bertelsmann Establish Pay Television Station"] [Text] Frankfurt/Main -- With the entertainment concern Bertelsmann AG, the Munich film trader Leo Kirch, the Multimedia Market, and ZDF, German Telekom wants to get into the growing pay television business. This was reported by Telekom Executive Board member Dieter Gallist. Telekom thinks that 19 million households in Germany are now "qualified for pay television" -- but only 5 percent are subscribers to pay television. The share is already 17 percent in France and 55 percent in England, it was stated.
