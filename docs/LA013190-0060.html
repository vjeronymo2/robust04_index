January 31, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL BASKETBALL

In other girls' games:

Thousand Oaks 56, Royal 42 -- Sophomore Michelle Palmisano broke the school career scoring mark with a game-high 27 points.

BOYS

Loyola 104, Alemany 69 -- Ryan Jamison scored a game-high 27 points to lead Loyola in a Del Rel League game at Alemany.

Ray Awad had 19 points and Tim Fontenette had 15 for Alemany (8-13, 1-5).

Chaminade 79, St. Genevieve 58 -- Senior guard Colin Havert recorded a triple-double in a San Fernando Valley League game at Chaminade.

Havert scored 24 points, added 10 rebounds, 10 assists and made four of five three-point attempts for Chaminade (10-9, 4-3).

Antelope Valley 68, Saugus 60 -- Bobby Paiz scored a career-high 19 points and teammate Broch Chase added 19 to lead Antelope Valley (18-4, 6-1) in a Golden League game at home. Greg Yumbar scored 24 for Saugus.

Montclair Prep 81, Village Christian 45 -- Montclair Prep clinched the Alpha League title with an easy win at home.

Jackie Shemtov scored a game-high 23 points and teammate Jason Gerber notched a triple-double with 10 points, 10 rebounds and 10 assists for Montclair Prep (17-3, 10-0). Robert Thomas scored 19 points for the winners.

Marshall Fundamental 60, L.A. Baptist 50 -- L.A. Baptist lost a key Alpha League game at home.

Marshall Fundamental (5-4 in league) received a game-high 21 points from Curtea King and 13 more from teammate James Shane to hurt the playoff chances of L.A. Baptist (7-13, 4-6).

The Knights were led by Larry Masters with 11 points off the bench.

Highland Hall 97, Oak Hill 77 -- Highland Hall set a school record for points in a game in a Westside League contest at Oak Hill.

Three Highland Hall players topped the 20-point plateau, led by Robert Ramirez (27 points and 12 assists) and Greg Anastas (27 and 15 rebounds). Craig Ernst had 22 points and 15 rebounds for the Hawks (17-2, 11-0), who outscored Oak Hill, 62-47, in the second half.

Faith Baptist 71, Campbell Hall 53 -- Darren Wyre scored 22 points to lead Faith Baptist to its second consecutive Delphic League victory.

Einstein Academy 49, West Valley Christian 45 -- Forward Ari Vinocor scored a game-high 14 points and center Alex Chernyausky chipped in 11 and 10 rebounds as the Rockets (4-10, 4-6) won their fourth out of their last five Westside League games.

Schurr 62, Burbank 56 -- Alfred Alvarado scored a game-high 22 points to lead Schurr (6-12, 2-5) to a Foothill League win at Burbank.

Mojave 78, Paraclete 48 -- Mike Thompson scored a game-high 26 points to lead Mojave (5-2 in league) to a Desert-Inyo League win at home.
