930803 FT 03 AUG 93 / Tanzania aligns currency Tanzania harmonised its exchange rates yesterday bringing the central bank's 'official' local currency rates into line with market-determined rates offered by commercial banks, Reuter reports from Dar es Salaam. A Bank of Tanzania statement said the official rate, previously used for a range of government imports, western aid and items such as oil, would conform with market rates used by the private sector since last year.
