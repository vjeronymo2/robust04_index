April 24, 1989, Monday, Valley P.M. Final

LOCAL;

BODY FOUND AT SUN VALLEY OFF-RAMP

An unidentified person was found shot to death on a freeway off-ramp in Sun Valley today, Los Angeles police said.

Few details were available. Police said the body was found on the Lankershim Boulevard off-ramp from the southbound Golden State Freeway.

The body was discovered about 10 a.m., and the freeway ramp was expected to remain closed until at least 12:30 p.m., California Highway Patrol dispatcher Susan Simmons said.
