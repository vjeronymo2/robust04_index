930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / Gunmen kill ex-minister A former minister was shot dead as he left his home in suburban Algiers yesterday, AP reports. The official Algerian news agency APS said Mr Djilali Liabes, 45, was shot at his home in Ben Omar in south-east Algiers by three gunmen who fled. Ben Omar is a stronghold of Moslem fundamentalists, who have faced a government crackdown for more than a year.
