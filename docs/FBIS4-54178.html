BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 26 (KCNA) -- His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk of Cambodia arrived here today by special plane to preside over a roundtable for peace and national reconciliation of Cambodia slated in Pyongyang. Arriving to attend the roundtable meeting were also H.E. Prince Chea Sim, acting head of state and president of the National Assembly, H.R.H. Prince Norodom Ranariddh, first prime minister, H.E. Prince Hun Sen, second prime minister, and the suite from the Cambodian Government side, and H.E. Khieu Samphan, president of the the Democratic Cambodia side, and the suite. They were met at the airport by Yi Chong-ok, vice president, Kim Yong-nam, vice premier and minister of foreign affairs, and officials concerned. Officials of the Royal Embassy of Cambodia in Pyongyang and Qiao Zonghuai, Chinese ambassador to Korea, were present at the airport.
