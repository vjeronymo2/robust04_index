BFN [Text] The cabinet has authorized an additional 4,000 Palestinians from the territories to work inside Israel in tourism, industry, and agriculture. The cabinet ministers were told that the right-wing Knesset members [MK's] would be evacuated from the ancient synagogue in Jericho. Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin noted that a precedent for such action had already been set. Our political correspondent Shlomo Raz reports that the cabinet will convene on Wednesday [11 May] to ratify the agreement with the PLO. The agreement will also be brought before the Knesset for approval. The cabinet also discussed the decision to promote Efi Fein to the rank of brigadier general after Minister Yosi Sarid raised the matter.
