September 6, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: ANAHEIM;

3 SUSPECTS IN PARKING LOT ROBBERIES ARRESTED

Anaheim police have arrested three suspects in connection with armed robberies in parking lots along Beach Boulevard.

Police arrested Sidney DuBois, 24, a transient; William Marvin Bailey, 21, of Los Angeles; and Audraca Coleman, 31, of Anaheim. The three are suspected of robbing two men and a woman at about 9:20 p.m. Monday at an auto dealership at 16742 Beach Blvd. The suspects pulled up in a red vehicle, pointed two guns -- possibly a handgun and automatic weapon -- and demanded their money, said Huntington Beach Police Lt. Ed McErlain.

About 10 minutes later, two men and a woman in the parking lot of a restaurant at 16160 Beach Blvd. reported a similar robbery.

About an hour after the second holdup, a Huntington Beach police officer returning home from work spotted a red vehicle in Buena Park with three people matching the descriptions of the robbers. The officer called for local police, and Fullerton, Anaheim and Buena Park officers chased the suspects until they were caught at the intersection of Northgate Lane and Citron Street in Anaheim.

All three are being held in Huntington Beach Jail.
