BFN [Text] A Lithuanian bureau for contacts with NATO was established at NATO headquarters today. The press attache of the Lithuanian Ministry of National Defense announced that National Defense Minister Linas Linkevicius was taking part today in a seminar of the Northern and Baltic states. The seminar is entitled Russia. Also attending today's seminar are the defense ministers of Finland, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, Estonia, and Latvia. Among the key issues under discussion: security of the Baltic region, assistance by the Northern countries to the Baltic states, relations with Russia, the defense system of the Baltic states, and cooperation in forming the Baltic peacekeeping battalion.
