Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Committee in Action"] [Text] The Federation Council Committee for Security and Defense Questions has begun work. Yesterday there was a meeting with representatives of the Defense Ministry, and a special group was formed for dealings with the Federation Council Committee. Your ITAR-TASS correspondent was told this by Petr Shirshov, chairman of the Committee for Security and Defense Questions. In its work the committee intends to "cooperate with the corresponding committees of the State Duma and also to expand contacts with the corresponding committees of the CIS countries." Petr Premyak, deputy chairman of the Committee for Security and Defense Questions, believes that the committee especially needs to cooperate with the corresponding committees in the Ukrainian parliament and in the parliaments of the Baltic states. In Petr Shirshov's opinion, the committee will be guided in its activity by the Russian Federation Constitution, will also monitor the execution of the laws, and where necessary will prepare proposals and submit them in the form of legislative initiatives.
