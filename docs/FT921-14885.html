920114 FT 14 JAN 92 / Observer: Switchback A coda to last week's cautionary tale of Douglas Hawkins, the one-time James Capel electronics analyst who has spent the past four years 'rationalising' Office & Electronic Machines. After a buffeting by the recession and other woes of the real world, he is hastening back to the familiar fold - slotting in as electronics and telecommunications analyst at Smith New Court. He concedes that his spell on the outside may make him 'more sympathetic' to the kind of management foibles analysts generally gleefully swoop upon.
