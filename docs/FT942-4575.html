940609 FT 09 JUN 94 / Pizza Hut plans to expand by 20% Pizza Hut, the restaurant and fast-food chain, said yesterday it intended to open more than 40 restaurants and 60 delivery units throughout the country over the next two years - a 20 per cent increase - creating up to 2,000 jobs. It is seeking town-centre sites, free-standing units in out-of-town retail parks and delivery units in high streets and other locations. The number of pizza restaurants is rising by about 12 per cent a year with delivery outlets - the fastest-growing sector - increasing by about 30 per cent a year.
