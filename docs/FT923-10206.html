920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / Property sale MEPC, the UK's second-largest property company, has sold an office building in Croydon, south London, to Rotch Property Group, a private company, for about Pounds 45m. The 442,500 sq ft building is let to the Department of the Environment. MEPC said it had sold the building, called Apollo and Lunar House, because of its limited prospects for rental growth.
