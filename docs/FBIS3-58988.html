Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Instructions for the Construction of Bombs"] [Text] In Salzburg, computer discs with instructions on how to make bombs have been discovered. The discs also contained circuit diagrams for the construction of letter bombs. The confiscated computer discs were passed on to the officials in the Interior Ministry investigating the letter-bomb attacks. They also offer a recipe for the production of nitroglycerin.
