January 25, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

PREP BASKETBALL ROUNDUP;

RATHBUN AND WOODBRIDGE TOP TRABUCO HILLS, 50-46

Leslie Rathbun scored 5 of her 20 points and played tough defense in the fourth quarter as Woodbridge High School scored a 50-46 victory over Trabuco Hills on Tuesday in a Pacific Coast League girls' basketball game at Trabuco Hills.

Woodbridge (15-5 overall, 5-0 in league play) trailed Trabuco Hills, 34-30, after three quarters. The Mustangs (11-6, 4-1) got their biggest lead of the game, 38-31, 22 seconds into the fourth quarter. But the Warriors cut the lead to 40-39 in the next 3 minutes.

After Marta Bickert's field goal and DeDe Sampson's free throw extended Trabuco Hills' lead to 43-39 with 4 minutes left, Kris Willis scored on an inside drive and free throw to cut the lead to 43-42.

With 2:57 left, Rathbun, who had stolen the ball from Sampson, hit an outside bank shot to give the Warriors a 44-43 lead.

Woodbridge built its lead to 50-43 and did not allow another Trabuco Hills point until Bickert hit a 3-point shot with 13 seconds left.

Sampson, who turned the ball over three times in the fourth quarter under Rathbun's pressure, finished the game with 23 points.
