Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beirut, Feb 3 (AFP)--Arab-Israeli bilateral peace negotiations will resume February 15 in Washington at the delegates' level, Lebanese Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz said Thursday [3 February]. The 12th session of the bilateral talks which began on January 24 ended Wednesday in Washington. The negotiations were held at secret locations at the heads of delegations level between Israelis, Lebanese, Syrians, Jordanians and Palestinians.
