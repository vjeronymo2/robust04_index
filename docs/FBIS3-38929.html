Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Culture Minister Fikri Saglar has said that the time has come for the world to renounce its double standard toward Bosnia-Herzegovina and take action. Enest Drakovic, Bosnian minister of education, science, culture and sports, concluded his contacts in Turkey and returned to his country this evening. He was seen off at Esenboga Airport by Saglar, who criticized the fact that the Western world has remained indifferent to the massacre of thousands of people in Bosnia-Herzegovina and called on the world to take immediate action in Bosnia-Herzegovina and lift the arms embargo. Drakovic said that the agreements reached during his contacts in Turkey constitute sound foundations for future cooperation between the two countries.
