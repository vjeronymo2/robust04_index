920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Relyon declines to Pounds 1.5m Profits of Relyon Group, bedding and furniture maker, fell from Pounds 1.75m to Pounds 1.5m pre-tax for the opening half of 1992. Turnover declined by Pounds 1m to Pounds 21m. The interim dividend is maintained at 1.75p from earnings of 4.77p (5.53p). Pre-tax profits for the 1990-91 year fell to Pounds 2.67m (Pounds 3.24m).
