920923 FT 23 SEP 92 / US warned over Taiwan jets China's foreign minister warned yesterday that the US decision to sell 150 F-16 fighter jets to Taiwan put into doubt the trustworthiness of the US, Reuter reports from New York. Qian Qichen, the foreign minister, told the Foreign Policy Association in New York that the decision, coupled with threats to slap Dollars 3.9bn (Pounds 2.2bn) in tariffs on Chinese goods, meant the two countries were in danger of entering a new era of confrontation and poor relations.
