The WIR survey broke down the sources of injuries into employees hit by trees; injured in slips or falls; while using chain saws; and while operating equipment or motor vehicles (Tables 7&hyph;10). As Table 7 indicates, almost one-half of those employees injured by trees were hit by falling wood.
