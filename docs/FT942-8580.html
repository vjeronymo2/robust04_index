940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / London Stock Exchange: Reckitt upset Shares in household products group Reckitt & Colman nosedived after the company's chairman gave a grim message to shareholders at the annual meeting. Sir Michael Colman said the year had 'started slowly' and that trading was turning out to be as difficult as in 1993. Reckitt, trading 3 higher at 677p immediately before the meeting, dropped sharply as the news filtered into the market, eventually closing a net 40 down at 634p. Turnover in the shares reached 3.2m, the second highest single day's business this year.
