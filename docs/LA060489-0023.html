June 4, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

RADIO REALITY

Mourning the death of KEDG, Carolina P. Briones lashes out at "myopic capitalistic greed" (Letters, May 21). I have a suggestion: Let her take out a license, buy a building, fill it with a transmitter and other electronic equipment, buy the records she likes, hire a staff and go on the air.

I'll look forward to her next letter, in which she explains exactly how she accomplished her first year of successful programming.

MERRILL E. SARTY

Los Angeles
