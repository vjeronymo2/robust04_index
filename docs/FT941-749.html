940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / Colleges' NVQ awards probed Allegations that colleges are indiscriminately awarding competence-based National Vocational Qualifications (NVQs) in order to secure funding are to be investigated by the National Council for Voluntary Qualifications. Mr John Hiller, chief executive of the NCVQ, said: 'I would urge that any evidence of malpractice is forwarded to the council without delay. Ultimately it is in no one's interest to award qualifications not merited by the student.'
