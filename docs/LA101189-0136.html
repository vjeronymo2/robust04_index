October 11, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

The annual Venice Art Walk, a walking tour through studios of artists living in Venice, is soliciting submissions from all Southern California artists for the image to appear on its 1990 T-shirt. The image must incorporate the words "Venice Family Clinic Art Walk '90." Only one entry per artist will be accepted. Submissions should include artist's name, address and phone number on the back of the work and be accompanied by a self-addressed, stamped envelope. No slides, transparencies or photographs accepted. Entries must be sent to the Venice Family Clinic, 604 Rose Ave., Venice, Calif., 90291, and be received by Dec. 6. Art Walk '90 is scheduled for May 20. Information: (213) 392-8630.
