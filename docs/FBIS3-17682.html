Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] According to the press service of the Nagorno-Karabakh army, the Monday attempt of Azeri forces to break through Karabakh defenses on the eastern section of the front (Agdam region) proved a complete failure. At dawn Azeri armored forces backed by combat planes and helicopters launched an offensive along the entire eastern frontline. "Thanks to skillful and efficient actions by Karabakh forces all the Azeri attacks were countered by 9 a.m. the enemy suffered great losses, especially in combat vehicles", the press service reported. A spokesman for the press service claimed over 10 armored vehicles were destroyed, among them six T-72 tanks and three armored infantry vehicles. Losses in personnel on the Azeri side exceed 150 persons. On the Karabakh side losses were minimal: one man was killed and 14 were wounded. In the afternoon the Azeri forces were thrown back to their initial positions. Hostilities in that section stopped. Noteworthy changes were not reported from other sections on Monday.
