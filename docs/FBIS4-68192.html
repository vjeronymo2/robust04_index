BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Eight members of an illegal leftist terrorist organization have been captured in Istanbul and Mugla while they were planning an armed attack. According to a statement issued by the Istanbul Security Directorate, three automatic rifles, three revolvers, four hand grenades, large amounts of dynamite, fuses, chemicals and equipment used in the manufacturing of explosives, and organizational documents were found in searches of the militants' houses.
