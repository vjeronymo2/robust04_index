920814 FT 14 AUG 92 / World News in Brief: QE2 probe Captain Robin Woodall, 58, master of the liner QE2, said he thought his ship was in deep enough water when it hit the seabed. 'I couldn't believe that we could have touched bottom', he told an inquiry in Boston into how the QE2 grounded after leaving port off the island of Martha's Vineyard, Massachusetts.
