941017 FT 17 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Net asset value rises at Value and Income Fully diluted net asset value per share of Value and Income Trust was 106.6p at September 30, up from 101.1p a year earlier. Net revenue for the half year was up 28 per cent, from Pounds 664,000 to Pounds 852,000. Earnings per share rose to 1.98p (1.56p) and the interim dividend is lifted from 2p to 2.1p. The directors intend to recommend a final of 2.1p.
