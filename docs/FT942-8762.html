940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / Call to change car numbering format A move to change the car registration plate format, with its annual prefix letter, is being launched by the Society of Motor Manufacturers and Traders. The current system, which will see the introduction of M-prefixed plates on August 1, continues to create problems for motor traders and car makers because of the huge sales bulge it causes in August, Mr Peter Ward, the society's new president who is chairman and chief executive of Rolls-Royce Motor Cars, said yesterday. August accounts for nearly a quarter of the year's total sales, but Mr Ward said the month had a depressing effect on sales as early as May.
