BFN [Text] Colonel Mustafa al-Karrubi, member of the Libyan leadership council, was received today by President Hashemi-Rafsanjani, to whom he presented a written message from the Libyan leader, Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi. Conveying Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi's warm greetings to our country's president, Mr. Karrubi said the aim of his trip to Iran is to find ways of deepening fraternal relations between Iran and Libya and to activate the joint commission in order to increase the volume of economic and trade exchanges between the two countries. He reported on the political and economic situation in Libya and said: The political and economic sanctions imposed on Libya by some Western countries are unjust and violate international law. For his part, Hashemi-Rafsanjani expressed gratitude for the Libyan leader's friendly message. He said relations and cooperation between the two countries is at a good level and emphasized the need to expand them. Referring to the political and economic pressure of world arrogance against independence-seeking countries, the president said the path of independence and refusal to surrender to the bullying which dominates the international order is filled with difficulties but leads to desirable results. Hashemi-Rafsanjani also emphasized the need for a peaceful and just solution, through international organizations, to the existing differences between Libya and some Western countries.
