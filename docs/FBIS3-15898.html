Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Azeri troops are not giving up attempts to break through the defences of the Karabakh army in different parts of the Armenian-Azeri conflict zone. This was reported to Interfax by the press service of the defence army of Nagorno-Karabakh. According to the press service, at 21:00 on Wednesday Azeri formations opened mass rocket and artillery fire at Karabakh positions in the Martuni Region. On Thursday morning intensive shelling began in the south-west of the conflict zone. The press service also reported the concentration of Azeri troops in Fizuli and Martuni directions. The command of the Karabakh army is fighting back. Meanwhile, the Azeri Defence Ministry reports Armenian attacks in Fizuli region of Azerbaijan in the direction of the strategic railway station Goradiz as well as shellings of communities in Ter- Ter and Agdam regions.
