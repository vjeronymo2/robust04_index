920423 FT 23 APR 92 / UK Company News: Copymore rises 10% to Pounds 1.02m Shares in Copymore, the USM-quoted office automation equipment distributor, yesterday rose 15p to 55p following a 10 per cent rise in 1991 profits to Pounds 1.02m pre-tax. Turnover slipped to Pounds 28.4m (Pounds 29.5m) due to the closure of two non-mainstream businesses. Interest charges were cut to Pounds 511,000 (Pounds 761,000) and earnings emerged at 5.9p (4.9p) per share. Year-end gearing was reduced from 196 per cent to 95 per cent. Earlier this month shareholders received a second interim dividend of 1.7p in lieu of a final, making a 2.7p (2.5p) total.
