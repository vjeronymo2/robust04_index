Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Albert Maloverian] [Text] Tallinn March 18 TASS -- The Estonian Foreign Ministry welcomes the agreement on terms, date and procedure of Russia's troop withdrawal from Latvia, the Estonian Foreign Ministry said in a statement for the press on Friday. A draft agreement between Russia and Estonia was prepared for the 17th round of bilateral negotiations, "but Russia was not ready to sign it," the statement said. According to the Estonian Foreign Ministry, the Russian troop pull-out should not be linked to any additional terms. Estonia expresses the hope that "Russia will be ready to initial the agreement at the next round of talks, due to be held in Moscow from April 5 to 6.
