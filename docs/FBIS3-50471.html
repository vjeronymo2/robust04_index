Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Tuxtla Gutierrez (Mexico), 2 Jan (EFE) -- News sources have confirmed to EFE that the Mexican Army is fighting Zapata National Liberation Army [EZLN] rebels in Ocosingo, Chiapas State. According to the sources, the rebels occupied the towns of Oxchuc, Huixtan, and Guadalupe Tepeyac along the Guatemalan border today. According to reports published by EL TIEMPO newspaper of San Cristobal de las Casas, Chiapas State, fighting began in Ocosingo at about 1500 local time (2100 GMT). There are no reports of dead or wounded. The Army has intermittently bombarded the town of Ocosingo and there are reports of at least seven separate clashes between Army troops and rebel forces resulting in 14 dead and more than 20 wounded.
