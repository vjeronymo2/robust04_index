921126 FT 26 NOV 92 / Observer: Market talk For a brief moment yesterday, the entire French financial system was under siege as 30 protesting French farmers stormed the Paris bourse. Once inside, they set off fire-crackers in protest at attempts to cut French farm subsidies and reach a Gatt deal. 'Nobody knows if they were buying or selling,' said one options dealer. The consensus was that they were probably bulls.
