920226 FT 26 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Lloyd Bank's New Zealand arm ahead National Bank of New Zealand, part of the Lloyds Bank group, reported a 22 per cent rise in net profit to NZDollars 82m (Pounds 25m) for 1991, despite higher provisions for doubtful debts, which rose from NZDollars 27m to NZDollars 38m. The results represented a 14.2 per cent return on shareholders' funds, compared with 12.8 per cent last year. Mr John Anderson, chief executive, again denied rumours that the bank was for sale.
