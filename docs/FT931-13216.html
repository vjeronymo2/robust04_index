930125 FT 25 JAN 93 / Observer: Colourful brief Andrew Arden, the 44-year-old Queen's Counsel who may be called on to carry out an inquiry into the 'unprecedented corruption' alleged at Lambeth Council, is no stranger to the doings of London's loony left. As a part-time thriller writer who has a taste for watching Hill Street Blues, drinking Southern Comfort and smoking Camel Cigarettes, Arden loves nothing better than investigating a good scandal. In the late 1980s he reported on an alleged freemasonry racket in Hackney. His weighty tome opened with the words: 'In the London Borough of Hackney, the buck is in orbit.' In Lambeth, it seems, it is in outer space.
