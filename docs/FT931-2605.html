930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / International Company News: Ahold increases payout to match rise in profits AHOLD, the Dutch-based food retailer, is to lift its dividend by 10.6 per cent, matching the increase reported yesterday in 1992 full-year results, writes Ronald van de Krol. The company also said it would conduct a one-for-10 rights issue later this year. Ahold posted net profit of Fl 305.0m (Dollars 163m) in 1992 from Fl 275.8m the year before, on sales up 7.3 per cent at Fl 22.3bn. The dual-currency dividend is to be raised to Fl 1.15 and Dollars 0.45 a share from Fl 1.05 and Dollars 0.40 last year. Ahold said net profit would have been Fl 8.1m higher but for the 6 per cent decline of the dollar against the guilder.
