Language: Tajik Article Type:BFN [Text] A ceremony of signing the agreement between Tajikistan and Russia on the drafting of citizens [of Tajikistan] for military service in the ranks of the Russian Border Troops took place today. On behalf of the Russian Federation, Colonel General Andrey Nikolayev, the commander in chief of the Russian Border Troops and on behalf of Tajikistan, Mahmadsaid Ubaydulloyev, deputy chairman of the Council of Ministers of the republic, signed the agreement.
