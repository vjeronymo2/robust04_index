920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / Arts and sports groups win grants MORE than 170 arts and sports organisations will share Pounds 17.5m distributed by the Foundation for Sport and the Arts yesterday. The foundation was set up last year to help sports clubs and arts companies and was part of an effort by football pools companies to head off a national lottery. Yesterday's awards bring the money distributed to more than Pounds 50m, close to the foundation's annual budget of Pounds 60m. The grants ranged from Pounds 200 to Pounds 500,000.
