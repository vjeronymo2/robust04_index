BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Solntsev] [Text] Moscow June 23 TASS -- A delegation from the Russian Liberal Democratic Party led by Vladimir Zhirinovskiy is going to visit North Korea at the beginning of October at the invitation of North Korean President Kim Il-song, ITAR-TASS learned from sources close to the LDP leadership. The visit will promote the development of contacts which were established during the visit to Pyongyang by LDP Deputy Chairman Aleksandr Vengerovskiy in April, 1994 on the occasion of Kim Il-song's 82d birthday, the same source said. In an interview given to ITAR-TASS during the visit Vengerovskiy stressed the intention "to maintain an active position as regards North Korea." LDP sources said that during Zhirinovskiy's forthcoming visit an agreement on cooperation will possibly be signed. "We do not reject proposals to develop contacts," the same source said.
