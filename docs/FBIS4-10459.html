BFN [Text] According to a survey by the Sao Paulo State Federation of Industries, 82 percent of industrial companies already have converted their prices into Real Value Units, URV's. The survey was delivered today to Finance Ministry special adviser Milton Dallari. The survey also shows that nearly 40 percent of commercial stores already have set their prices in URV's. Dallari, who today met with nearly 200 businessmen in Sao Paulo, said the government will institute the real only when all prices have been converted into URV's. Commenting on the conversion of utility rates, Dallari said that only the conversion of electricity and telephone rates will require an implementation decree, which will not be issued until June. Other utility rates will be converted into URV's by May.
