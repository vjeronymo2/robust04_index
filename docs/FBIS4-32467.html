BFN [Text] Tirana, June 23 (ATA) -- Albanian Parliament approved in its yesterday's seance the intergovernmental agreement between the Republic of Albania and the Republic of Austria, between the Republic of Albania and the UN High Commissariat for Refugees, the agreement with the International Federation of the Red Cross and Red Crescent. The agreement with Republic of Austria, which comprises economic exchanges, defines that for facilitating such a relations between the two countries, the two sides will treat each-other according to the principle of the most favourite nation in regard to customs and other taxes and facilitating the relations between the enterprises and organizations and associations called "enterprises." This cooperation will mainly be promoted in agriculture, forestry economy, chemical industry, light industry, energy resources, construction, health and environment protection, tourism, exchange of information, taking in consideration the legislation in power in the two countries which have signed this agreement. The other agreement between the Albanian Government and the UN High Commissariat for Refugees (UNHCR) defines the rights and the obligation of the two sides. The agreement has 17 articles and intends to set the basic conditions for cooperation between the UNHCR and the Albanian Government for carrying out the international protection and functions of the humanitarian aid in favour of refugees. During this seance, the parliament ratified the agreement between the Albanian Government and the International Federation of the Red Cross and Red Crescent. The agreement creates a real opportunity that a delegation of this international federation exercise its normal activity and function in conformity to the existed laws of the Republic of Albania.
