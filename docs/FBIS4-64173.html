BFN [Text] Tallinn, Estonia, 30 May -- The Ukrainian Embassy in Estonia has received a note from the Estonian Ministry of Foreign Affairs. The document says that in accordance with the treaty on friendship and cooperation between Ukraine and Estonia, which reaffirms the two countries' territorial integrity and inviolability of their borders, Estonia will be guided by these principles in its policy toward Crimea and considers establishment of any official relations between Tallinn and Simferopol impossible.
