Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A pro-Vatican magazine has quoted a Vatican diplomat as saying that there is a possibility that the bombing of the Sayyidat al-Najat Church [on 27 February] was perpetrated to protest the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Vatican and Israel. The Vatican diplomat revealed that the Holy See received signals in December that an attack against a Christian target in the Middle East was inevitable. He added: We feared attacks against Christian targets in the Middle East. He noted that the earliest signs of such a possibility emerged around Christmas, when the agreement between the Vatican and the State of Israel was signed.
