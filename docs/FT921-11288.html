920201 FT 01 FEB 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Pepper market has picked up, reports Man-producten. Black pepper firmer on fewer offers from Brazil at higher prices and limited material offered by other origins. Spot traded at USDollars 1,275 to Dollars 1,300 a tonne, Jan/Feb shipment at Dollars 1,175 cif. White pepper, on the contrary, tended weaker on failing demand, ample supplies in origin and increased selling pressure. Spot about Dollars 1,350 and Jan/Feb shipment at Dollars 1,275 cif.
