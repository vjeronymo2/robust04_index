Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Fatah-the Uprising today claimed responsibility for the infiltration of the Lebanese-Israeli border by three fedayeen who were killed in a confrontation with Israeli forces near al-Majidiyah. In a statement released today, Fatah-the Uprising said the fedayeen group raided the Dan settlement at dawn today using machine guns and rockets and clashed with the settlement guards for two hours, killing or wounding several Israelis. Citing security sources, REUTER reported that the three fedayeen crossed the border and that the Israelis discovered them near the village of al-Majidiyah on the Lebanese-Israeli border.
