BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 25/5/1994 -- Ion Batcu, chairman of the Coordinating Council of the recently registered Peasant Christian Democratic Party of Moldova [PTCDM], declared today to BASAPRESS that "the current government is the most incompetent Moldova has ever had, being incapable of pulling the country out of its deep crisis." He specified that the PTCDM will lead the political movement of the peasant parties in Bessarabia. "We think the problems of the village are to be solved first. If the government does not do this, the crisis will never be overcome," said Batcu, mentioning that his party will be in opposition to the current governing forces. Tomorrow at noon, the PTCDM will hold a press conference, in the hall of the Journalists' Union (House of the Press).
