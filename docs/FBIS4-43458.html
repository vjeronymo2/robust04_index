BFN [Text] Ankara (AA) -- Twelve deputies from the Democracy Party [DEP] have resigned from their party. DEP acting leader Remzi Kartal sent a fax to the speaker of the Turkish Grand National Assembly [TGNA] informing him that the following deputies resigned from DEP: Hatip Dicle (Diyarbakir), Leyla Zana (Diyarbakir), Ahmet Turk (Mardin), Sirri Sakik (Mus), Orhan Dogan (Sirnak), Sedat Yurtdas (Diyarbakir), Selim Sadak (Sirnak), Ali Yigit (Mardin), Nizamettin Toguc (Batman), Mahmut Kilinc (Adiyaman), Naif Gunes (Siirt), Zubeyir Aydar (Siirt). After the resignations, Remzi Kartal remains the only DEP deputy in the TGNA.
