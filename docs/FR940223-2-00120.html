NUCLEAR REGULATORY COMMISSION Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes: Meeting Notice AGENCY: Nuclear Regulatory Commission. ACTION: Notice of meeting.

SUMMARY: The meeting of the Advisory Committee on the Medical Uses of Isotopes (ACMUI) scheduled for February 24, 1994, has been canceled. The notice for this meeting appeared in the Federal Register on February 9, 1994 (59 FR 6061). The next meeting of the ACMUI will be held on May 19 and 20, 1994. Information regarding this meeting will be noticed no less than 15 days before the meeting. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION, CONTACT: Sally L.
