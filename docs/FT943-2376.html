940920 FT 20 SEP 94 / World Trade News: Mitsubishi Heavy, Ube Industries win Tabuk Cement contract Saudi Arabia's Tabuk Cement has awarded a contract worth Dollars 257m for a cement and associated power and desalination plant to Mitsubishi Heavy Industries and Ube Industries of Japan, Reuter reports from Dubai. The 1.1m tonne-a-year plant is to be built in the coastal town of Duba in north-western Saudi Arabia.
