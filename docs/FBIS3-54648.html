Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Radio Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] The main issue for Kazakhstan in the military sphere is close military cooperation with the Russian Federation, according to an address by Kazakhstani Defense Minister Sagadat Nurmagambetov to the military department collegium. We quote from KAZAKHSTAN SARBAZY -- WARRIOR OF KAZAKHSTAN. Realistic conditions for such cooperation do exist, the minister stressed. The legal basis for this is the document on friendship and cooperation between Kazakhstan and Russia.
