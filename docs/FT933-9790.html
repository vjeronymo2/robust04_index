930807 FT 07 AUG 93 / International Company News: Sharp rise at Berliner Bank BERLINER Bank, Germany's tenth-largest private commercial bank, announced a sharp increase in operating profits and net trading income for the first six months of the year compared with the same period last year, writes Judy Dempsey in Berlin. Group partial operating profits rose to DM285.7m (Dollars 166.1m), against DM188.4m, while parent partial operating profits increased to DM244.5m from DM172.7m. Net trading income rose by DM6m to DM24.7m. Risk provisions, which totalled DM140.1m during the 1992 first half, fell 41 per cent to DM100m for the same period this year.
