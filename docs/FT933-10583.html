930803 FT 03 AUG 93 / International Company News: Amsterdam to delist shares in Daf SHARES in Daf, the Dutch truckmaker which collapsed in early 1993, are to be delisted from the Amsterdam stock exchange on September 1, with official trading to end on August 31, the bourse said yesterday, writes Ronald van de Krol in Amsterdam. The shares are now quoted at Fl 0.45, compared with their flotation price of Fl 47 in mid-1989. Shareholders include the Dutch state, the Flemish regional government of Belgium, and several institutional shareholders.
