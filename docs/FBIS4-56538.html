CSO [Editorial Report] Managua LA PRENSA in Spanish of 15 April 1994 on page 16 reports that the Netherlands has forgiven $2 million of Nicaragua's $15-million debt. The Netherlands forgave more than half of Nicaragua's $38.5 million between 1991 and 1993. The article reports that the Netherlands will contribute $1.3 million to the environment and energy sector in support of a geothermal project which will reduce Nicaragua's dependence on oil. The two countries are negotiationg a $20-million geothermal project to be financed by the Netherlands, according to the article.
