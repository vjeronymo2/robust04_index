Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Estonian Foreign Ministry has welcomed the treaty initialled at the last round of Russian-Latvian talks on the withdrawal of Russian Armed Forces from Latvia, which stipulates that the troops must be withdrawn by 31 August this year. The Estonian Foreign Ministry also expresses the hope that on the basis of the proposal made by the Russian prime minister to withdraw troops from Estonia by 31 August, and on the attitude of the international community that no additional conditions may be attached to the withdrawal, Russia will be ready to initial the treaty on the withdrawal of the troops at the next, the 18th round of talks, which will take place in Moscow on 5, 6 April.
