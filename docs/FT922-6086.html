920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / Danish poll shows swing to EC treaty A late swing in favour of the Maastricht Treaty on European Union appears to be taking place among Danish voters who go to the polls on June 2 to vote in a binding referendum, writes Hilary Barnes in Copenhagen. The latest Gallup poll suggested a majority of 41 per cent were in favour of the treaty, with 39 per cent against.
