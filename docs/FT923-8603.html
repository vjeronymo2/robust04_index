920811 FT 11 AUG 92 / Sri Lankans stone ministers Thousands of mourners hurled stones and abuse at Sri Lankan government ministers at the funeral yesterday of a popular general killed in a weekend landmine explosion, Reuter reports from Colombo. At least 15 people, including two ministers and some soldiers, were injured. Police in riot gear fired shots and tear-gas into the air to disperse the crowd of more than 100,000 surrounding top military men and politicians at the Colombo funeral. Denzil Kobbekaduwa, posthumously promoted to lieutenant-general from major-general, was killed with nine other people in the blast in northern Jaffna on Saturday.
