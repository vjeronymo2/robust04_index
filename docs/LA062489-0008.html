June 24, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ZSA ZSA'S ENCOUNTER WITH POLICE

In defense of our very fine police department I must tell you that the officer did not (as Gabor said) "make her sit in the middle of the street."

He did what any good officer would do after being hit by a citizen: He handcuffed her wrists behind her back and sat her down on the curb while he conducted his investigation. I saw this with my own eyes.

SHELLY KORNHANDLER

Beverly Hills
