May 4, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

BUST OR BOOM? Debunking a federal report that said Orange County has a dangerous glut of office space, a new state study says the area is still economically healthy (D1). . . . But the state expresses concern about "high-asset growth" -- that is loans made -- among some state-chartered banks. . . . And some Orange County banks are among the state's most aggressive lenders, possibly putting themselves at risk if the local economy falters. Jeffrey A. Perlman and Dennis McLellan
