July 25, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Pacific Scientific Co.: The Newport Beach-based manufacturer of industrial and aerospace products posted second-quarter earnings of $1.8 million, up from $859,000 in the year-earlier quarter.

Revenue rose 12% to $46.9 million from $41.9 million. For the six months, earnings totaled $2.7 million, up from $1.7 million a year earlier. Revenue increased 9%, to $91.7 million from $84.3 million.
