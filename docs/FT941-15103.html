940117 FT 17 JAN 94 / International Company News: Bombardier to raise CDollars 210m Bombardier, the aerospace group, is raising CDollars 210m (USDollars 157.8m), to bolster working capital, with a public offer of 10m class B common shares at CDollars 21.875 a share. The underwriting group is led by Scotia McLeod, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. The new equity issue will have a 6 per cent dilution factor, analysts said.
