930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Roxspur shows Pounds 343,000 loss Roxspur, the USM-quoted playground equipment manufacturer, formerly Levercrest, incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 343,000 in the six months to November 30. The outcome, struck after remedial costs of Pounds 195,000 and reorganisation charges of Pounds 94,000, compared with a profit of Pounds 1,000 last time. Interest took Pounds 59,000, against Pounds 51,000. Turnover declined 20 per cent, from Pounds 2.45m to Pounds 1.95m. Losses per share were 6.7p (earnings of 0.02p).
