930108 FT 08 JAN 93 / Letter: No colour blind mice here Sir, May I add one more item to '20 things you didn't know . . .' (January 4)? The field mice seeking comfort in the winter rummage in my recipe drawer in the country and devour cuttings from The Times but eschew the pink pages of the FT. Long live the pink 'un] Elizabeth Cortazzi, 16 Hamilton Close, London NW8 8QY
