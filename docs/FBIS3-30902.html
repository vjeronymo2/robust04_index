Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague (DDP/ADN) -- Defense Minister Volker Ruehe sees "certain possibilities" that Slovakia could accede to NATO by the end of the century. Ruehe said at the conclusion of a two-day visit to Bratislava (Pressburg) that this is, however, dependent on international developments. The German delegation has gained the impression that Slovakia is preparing for membership of western structures in all areas. In Bratislava, Ruehe met President Kovac, Prime Minister Meciar, Assembly Speaker Gasparovic, Foreign Minister Moravcik, and his counterpart Andrejcak. An agreement on cooperation in the military field was signed during the visit. Ruehe said that he sees the preconditions for close, positive cooperation between Germany and Slovakia.
