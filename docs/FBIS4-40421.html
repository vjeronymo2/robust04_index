BFN [Text] Moscow 15 June TASS -- The Tajik Defence Ministry commanded today an operation aimed at the liquidation of the opposition armed groups near the settlement of Garm, where Deputy Minister Ramazan Radjabov was killed early in the morning on Wednesday. According to the ministry, Radjabov and his escort were moving along a village road in two vehicles when they were suddenly found themselves under automatic fire from an ambush laid by a group of Islamic opposition. The deputy minister, Captain S. Khokhlov and four soldiers from Radjabov's escort were killed on the spot, one was injured and is now in bad condition, the source told TASS.
