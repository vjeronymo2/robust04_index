BFN [Text] We have just received a report on the meeting of the KDNP [Christian Democratic People's Party] executive committee. Levente Suekoesd reports: [Suekoesd] Spokeswoman Krisztina Szilagyi told our program that at the meeting of the KDNP executive committee today a decision was made to exclude the possibility of coalition with the MSZP [Hungarian Socialist Party]. The spokeswoman also said that at the meeting of the national executive to be held on 25 June, the leadership would resign; it will operate as an acting body until the new leadership is elected.
