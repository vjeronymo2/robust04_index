Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Dushanbe January 17 TIA-KHOVAR-TASS -- At least ten armed transgressors were killed in a clash on the Tajik-Afghan border this past Sunday when an armed anti-government group of more than twenty militants attempted to cross the border from Afghanistan into Tajikistan. An Uzbek battalion of the CIS collective peacekeeping force helped the Russian borderguards repel the transgressors who retreated to Afghanistan. None of the CIS and Russian servicemen was hurt.
