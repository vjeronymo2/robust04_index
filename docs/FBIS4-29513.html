BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 18 -- Local authorities of Bolikhamsai province (Laos) and Nghe An province (central Vietnam) have signed recently minutes on cooperation in economy, culture and social affairs. The agreement was made during a visit by a delegation of Nghe An province led by its people's committee President Pham Xuan Tuy to Bolikhamsy. The two sides also agreed to open one more check-point in Gnot Nam area which has common border line with Nghe An. Earlier, a delegation of the archive department of the Prime Minister's Office led by its Deputy Head Duong Van Kham, paid a week-long working visit to Laos, ending on June 16.
