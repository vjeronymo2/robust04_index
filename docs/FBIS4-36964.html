BFN [Text] Brigadier General Yom-Tov Sami', head of the Israeli-Palestinian liaison committee, says that the joint IDF [Israel Defense Forces] and Palestinian police headquarters have proven themselves a great success. Sami' said the security situation in the autonomous areas is satisfactory, and voiced optimism concerning the Gaza-Jericho agreement. He added, however, that unless the economic situation in the Gaza Strip improves, the security situation could deteriorate. Our correspondent Geva' Qra'-'Oz reports that Brig. Gen. Sami' divulged that in the framework of their preparations for Yasir 'Arafat's arrival in the Gaza Strip, the Palestinians have started preparing a helicopter landing pad in Gaza, despite the fact that they are not authorized to do so.
