Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday, provincial party committee Secretary Liu Fangren spoke at a family planning forum emphatically calling for strengthening leadership, carrying out family planning in a down-to-earth manner, and comprehensively improving the province's family planning quality. The family planning forum was held under the auspices of the provincial party committee and government and attended by prefectural, autonomous prefectural, and city leaders as well as comrades from the provincial family planning office . At the forum, provincial Governor Chen Shineng relayed the spirit of General Secretary Jiang Zemin's instructions on family planning work. Zhang Yuqin, provincial vice governor and family planning commission director, gave an account of the province's family planning work and made family planning arrangements for 1994. In his speech, provincial party committee Secretary Liu Fangren stated: Comrade Jiang Zemin has warned us against being overly optimistic about family planning achievements and urged us to fully understand the arduousness and protractedness of family planning work, speak the truth,; and stop covering up facts and deceiving oneself and others in this regard. Liu Fangren noted: Comrade Jiang Zemin's instructions fully tally with the realities in Guizhou. We should strive to seek truth from facts, boost morale, and conduct family planning in a down-to-earth manner. Party and government organs at all levels in Guizhou should implement to the letter Comrade Jiang Zemin's instructions on family planning work, strengthen leadership over family planning work, urge all departments concerned to support family planning work, and make more contributions to Guizhou's family planning work. Also attending the forum were Long Zhiyi, Wang Siqi, Li Wanlu, Liu Yulin, Miao Chunting, and some other leading comrades.
