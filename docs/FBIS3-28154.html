Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 2 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The Presidium of the Mongolian Democratic Union made a statement, addressing its members and supporters. In the statement it is noted that abuse of power and bribery are widely spreading in the country among a state and government high officials, reaching the level capable to cause a threat to the national security. In this connection the Mongolian Democratic Union announces about its intention to liven up its political activity.
