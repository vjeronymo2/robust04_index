BFN [Text] On the basis of a memorandum of understanding signed today between Construction Jihad Minister Gholam Reza Foruzesh and Irelands' Defense and Marine Minister David Andrews, the two countries decided to cooperate in fishing industries, including caviar, prawn, and canned fish trade and the export of the said products to Ireland and other countries. Granting study bursaries to Iranian students studying fishing industries, marine wild life, and maritime installations, as well as the decision to hold training workshops in Iran and Ireland were among the key aspects of the agreement reached between the two ministers.
