BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina trusts in the success of the April conference, which will bring together representatives of the Cuban Government and a portion of the Cuban community abroad. Robaina announced that so far, the majority of invitations for The Nation and the Emigre Community Conference have been accepted. Robaina reiterated that extremists (la histeria) are not invited to this meeting. Robaina added that the meetings will focus on immigration, the parliamentary system, family reunification, and travel procedures, but that the Cuban organizers are open to including other subjects. [Radio Rebelde adds later in the cast that at Robaina's invitation and in his company, members of the diplomatic corps are visiting the Isle of Youth Municipality]
