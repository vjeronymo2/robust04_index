941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / Commodities and Agriculture: Coffee futures lose steam - Market report London Commodity Exchange COFFEE futures lost steam and closed lower in thin volume following arbitrage selling and conflicting reports about the Brazilian weather situation, dealers said. COCOA prices also slipped as dealers awaited news regarding the size of the Ivory Coast's crop. At the London Metal Exchange COPPER prices staged an impressive rally on short-covering and fresh buying in the afternoon to break back above Dollars 2,480 a tonne for three months delivery. The ALUMINIUM market sucked in speculative buying as the three months price pushed back above Dollars 1,620 a tonne. Compiled from Reuters
