BFN [Text] The government has restored the certificate of registration of the United Democratic Party [UDP], which was disbanded after its president, Enock Kavindele, resigned. According to a letter signed by [name indistinct] and sent to [name indistinct] and company, the law firm which pursued the restoration of UDP, UDP has been advised to collect a certificate of registration. UDP Interim Party President Mr. Kapenda Okwuni released the letter to ZNBC [Zambia National Broadcasting Corporation] today. And Mr. Okwuni said now that the controversy over the existence of the party is over, the ward counselors in all councils where UDP has seats should continue to represent their electorate at council meetings.
