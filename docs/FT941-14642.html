940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / International Company News: US airline plans Dollars 400m issue Northwest Airlines, the fourth-largest US carrier, is to return to the US stock market by the end of March through a public offering of Dollars 400m of new shares, representing 25 per cent of the enlarged equity, writes Richard Tomkins. The airline left the market in 1989 when it underwent a Dollars 3.65bn leveraged buy-out. It narrowly escaped bankruptcy last year by persuading employees to accept an equity stake in return for labour concessions.
