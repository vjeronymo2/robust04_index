BFN [Text] The Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali has chaired a cabinet meeting on a number of issues. Dr. al-Majali briefed cabinet ministers on the visit to Amman by PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat and his talks with his majesty the king. He also gave a report on the visit of Japanese Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa, who has pledged his country's support to the kingdom in various aspects. During the meeting, Minister of State for Foreign Affairs, Talal al-Hasan, presented a report on the signing of the PLO-Israel agreement on implementing self-rule in Gaza and Jericho. Mr. al-Hasan represented Jordan in the signing ceremony in Cairo on Wednesday. Trade and Industry Minister Dr. Rima Khalaf submitted a report on a Jordanian-French cooperation seminar held in Paris. She asserted France's desire to develop economic and investment cooperation in the public and private sectors. The cabinet also listened to a report by Minister of Education Khalid al-'Umari on the meetings of UNESCO's executive council held in Paris. He said the council has underlined educational programs for all and the importance of democracy and international tolerance. Cabinet ministers discussed other issues on the agenda and took appropriate measures.
