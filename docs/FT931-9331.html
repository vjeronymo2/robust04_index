930215 FT 15 FEB 93 / Satellite TV 'in 2m homes' THE NUMBER of UK homes with satellite dishes was 2.015m by the end of 1992 according to the German-based research company GfK, whose figures exclude Northern Ireland, the Channel Islands and the Isle of Man. Its assessment is lower than the latest Barb figures (from February 1), showing 2.323m installed dishes throughout the UK.
