Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sergey Bulantsev] [Text] New Delhi January 3 TASS -- President of Uzbekistan Islam Karimov arrived in India on a three-day official visit. The esteemed guest was received by President of India Shankar Dayal Sharma in the presidential palace decorated with the national flags of the two countries. During his talks in the Indian capital, Islam Karimov will meet with Prime Minister P.V. Narasimha Rao and other leaders of the Republic and also address a meeting of representaives of India's business circle. As already reported, during the course of the visit the Uzbek president will sign six agreements which will define the further direction of Indo-Uzbek cooperation, above all, in the sphere of trade and economy.
