940813 FT 13 AUG 94 / World News in Brief: Sally Gunnell wins 400-metres hurdles Sally Gunnell of the UK celebrates winning the 400-metres hurdles final at the European athletics championships in Helsinki in 53.33 secs, the fastest time so far this year. She already holds the Olympic, world and Commonwealth titles for the event. Britain's Colin Jackson won the 110- metres hurdles in 13.08 secs, outside his world record of 12.91 secs.
