Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Husni Mubarak received a telephone call from U.S. President Bill Clinton last night during which they discussed developments in the Middle East region, the talks Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin held during his visit to the United States, and the efforts being made with the various parties concerned to maintain the peace process. President Mubarak highlighted the importance of preserving the lawful rights of the Palestinians in the occupied territories by enforcing measures that provide for their safety and protection.
