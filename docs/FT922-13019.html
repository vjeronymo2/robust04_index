920414 FT 14 APR 92 / Mandela confirms split from wife Nelson Mandela, leader of the African National Congress, confirmed yesterday that he is to separate from his wife, Winnie, after 33 years of marriage. Mr Mandela, pictured above with former ANC president Oliver Tambo, told a Johannesburg press conference that his love for Mrs Mandela remained 'undiminished'. Page 6
