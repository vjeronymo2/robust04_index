930607 FT 07 JUN 93 / French Defence minister Leotard in Sarajevo French Defence Minister Francois Leotard is met at Sarajevo yesterday by UN commander General Phillipe Morillon. On a visit to French troops, he said that France could provide no more United Nations forces for Bosnia. He understood the Bosnian government's anxiety about UN plans to set up safe areas for Moslems but said France had done enough for the UN already.
