931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / International Company News: Scotts to buy WR Grace subsidiary SCOTTS, the US garden products group, is to acquire Grace-Sierra Horticultural Products for more than Dollars 100m from a subsidiary of WR Grace, the specialty chemical group, and other investors, Reuter reports. Scotts said that after the deal, Grace-Sierra's business and operations would be combined with those of Scotts to form the world's largest turf and horticultural products company, with combined 1993 sales of nearly Dollars 600m. Grace-Sierra manufactures and markets speciality fertilisers for nursery, golf course, greenhouse and consumer markets. The company said that the acquisition would add significantly to sales growth and earnings potential through marketing efficiencies and cost reductions. WR Grace added that the deal included repayment of Grace-Sierra's indebtedness. The company added that the acquisition was expected to be financed through a combination of long-term sub-ordinated debt and bank borrowings.
