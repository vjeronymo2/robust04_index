BFN [Text] Yerevan ("Azg" daily -- Noyan Tapan), 03/31/94 -- "Many people emigrate from Armenia non-legally, making their registration extremely difficult", the RA [Republic of Armenia] State Minister Rafael Bagoyan declared during a parliament session on March 29. According to the Minister, some 300,000 people emigrated from Armenia in 1991-93. Mr. Bagoyan reported, a migration service was created at the Ministry of Social Security, a similar structure is planned to be created at the refugees department.
