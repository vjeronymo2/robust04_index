930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / International Company News: Colgate increases stake in Indian arm COLGATE Palmolive is paying Dollars 25m to lift its shareholding in its Indian business to 51 per cent from 40 per cent, writes RC Murthy in Bombay. The US group will purchase 11.93m shares of Colgate Palmolive India. The Indian company plans a scrip issue and will also make a one-for-10 rights issue at Rs10 a share. Colgate is the leader of Indian toothpaste market with 60 per cent share, but lags Hindustan Lever and Procter and Gamble in personal products and fabric care items.
