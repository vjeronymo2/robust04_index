940124 FT 24 JAN 94 / Top staff beat RPI The latest Sedgwick Noble Lowndes index of executive pay shows that pay for senior managers has risen much faster in the past year than for most employees and well beyond the 1.5 per cent ceiling demanded by the government last year for public-sector workers. The rise in the retail prices index is still below 2 per cent and the government is seeking to freeze the public-sector pay bill. ---------------------------------------------------------------------
