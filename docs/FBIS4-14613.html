BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Maynayev] [Text] Bishkek 1 April, ITAR-TASS -- Kirgizstan [Kyrgyzstan] is viewing with understanding Kazakhstan President Nursultan Nazarbayev's idea concerning the creation of a Eurasian union. The press service of Kyrgyz President Askar Akayev has announced that he supports the initiative of Kazakhstan's leader and thinks it promising. "I understand that it can become a subject for a serious discussion by the heads of state of the Commonwealth member-countries at their summit on 14-16 April. I think that the idea and the proposal which specify the main principles and institutions which will underlie the possible formation of the Eurasian union will serve to further the deepening of comprehensive integration of those Commonwealth member-countries which wish to join the process of shaping a new inter-state formation," Askar Akayev said.
