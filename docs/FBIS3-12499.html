Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah has said there is great optimism over the Jordanian-Omani mediation efforts. In press statements published today, he said what has been published abroad about the Yemeni crisis contains a great deal of exaggeration and overstatement. He stressed that the Yemeni people and leadership are committed to the implementation of the accord document, which was signed in Amman by all Yemeni political forces under the auspices of His Majesty King Husayn on 10 Ramadan.
