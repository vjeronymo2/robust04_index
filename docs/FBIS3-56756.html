Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Talalayev] [Text] Moscow January 19 TASS -- A regular meeting of the Russian Security Council was held in the Kremlin here today under the chairmanship of President Boris Yeltsin. The agenda of the meeting, the first this year, included only one item: "On the programme for developing and producing perspective types of armaments and military hardware". Reports on this subject were delivered by Andrey Kokoshin, first deputy defence minister of the Russian Federation, Viktor Glukhikh, chairman of the Russian State Committee for Defence Sectors of Industry, and Viktor Mikhaylov, Russian minister for atomic energy. The meeting was attended by Viktor Chernomyrdin, chairman of the Russian Government, Oleg Lobov, secretary of the Security Council, and other council members. Taking part in the meeting also were Oleg Soskovets, first vice-premier, and Yuriy Baturin, presidential assistant for national security, who was appointed to this post early this January. The latter two, are not members of the Security Council. Absent from the meeting were such members of the Security Council as Defence Minister Pavel Grachev, Interior Minister Viktor Yerin, and also Yegor Gaydar. As to Vice-Premier Boris Fedorov, he came to the Kremlin in the morning, but unexpectedly left it before the meeting began. A statement on the results of the meeting is expected to be released by the presidential press service later in the day.
