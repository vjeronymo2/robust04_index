November 8, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

FileNet Corp.: The Costa Mesa manufacturer of image-processing systems said it lost $506,000 for the third quarter, contrasted with a net loss of $1.8 million for the corresponding 1989 quarter.

Revenue rose 20%, to $22.3 million from $18.6 million. Nine-month net income totaled $2.9 million, contrasted with $205,000 for the first three quarters of 1989. Revenue was up 30%, to $73.3 million from $56.5 million.
