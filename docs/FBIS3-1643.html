Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Haikou, February 26 (CNS)--Shareholding system enterprises have become an important force in Hainan's economy with 125 limited companies being approved since 1991, raising capital of RMB [Renminbi] 20.56 billion. Some of these enterprises are set up based on state-owned enterprises with others created from non-state-run ones or formed by putting technology, capital and land into capital stock or by merging, joint-running or contracting. These shareholding enterprises are involved in tourism, industry, transportation and finance and have certain notable characteristics. First, they make more reasonable the industrial structure in Hainan with 23 such companies engaging in tourism, 17 in industrial development, 16 in the development of whole pieces of land, seven in comprehensive agricultural development and five in infrastructural construction, with all achieving greater profits. Second, these companies are an important contributor of funds for infrastructural construction in the province with some 60 percent of fund for the construction of the Sanya International Airport, the Eastern Expressway and electric power stations coming from these companies, resulting in relieving the shortage of funds and speeding up infrastructural construction there. Third, these companies have also become important sources of the local financial income with five listed companies alone handing in taxes of RMB 60 million last year. The reason for the rapid development of these kinds of companies in Hainan is the market economy in the province and the greater autonomy of its enterprises.
