June 10, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

SOMETHING IS MISSING, AND IT'S MORE THAN JUST LASORDA'S BELLY

Any Dodger fan would admit that a scan of the team's statistics in the morning paper is enough to ruin a good cup of coffee.

Let's face it, last year it was Kirk Gibson. This year it's Eddie Murray. The Dodgers are not a one-man team, they are a team that has simply forgotten they are still this year's world champions.

RAUL MARTINEZ

Northridge
