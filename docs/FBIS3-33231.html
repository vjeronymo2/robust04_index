Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Lima, 18 Feb (AFP) -- A military spokesperson announced today that the Supreme Council of Military Justice (CSJM) began trying the 11 soldiers charged with the disappearance and deaths of a professor and nine students of La Cantuta University. The trial is being held behind closed doors and strict security measures are being observed. CSJM Secretary Major Alvaro Castro told the press that the CSJM ordered the transfer of the prisoners to the Simon Bolivar Garrison where the trial will be conducted. This was done last night. The press will be denied access to the trial and only civilian lawyers will be allowed entry.
