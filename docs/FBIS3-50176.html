Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, leader of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia, has postponed for a few days the date of his arrival in Poland. Janusz Bryczkowski, who invited Zhirinovskiy to Poland, told AFP that his guest will arrive in Poland not on Thursday but on Sunday [30 January] because of a visit to Belgrade, which had not been planned earlier. Bryczkowski said in a telephone conversation last night that Zhirinovskiy expressed his concern about the lack of guarantees from the Polish authorities concerning his security during the visit to our country.
