Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 25/2/1994 -- The biggest heroin haul since 1970 was captured at the Boros customs station, Bihor County, a frontier crossing between Romania and Hungary. It is about 22 kg of Mexican heroin, 84-88 percent refined, found in a Citroen with Dutch registration number LT 79 LH, belonging to tourist Jovic Goran, born in Yugoslavia,now having double French and Yugoslav citizenship, who intended to leave Romania. The "commodity," in 500 gram parcels, was hidden in a specially arranged place in the car.
