August 12, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

THE WEEK AHEAD

TEEN ARRAIGNMENT -- Two Northridge college students accused of killing a Pacoima pastor over a car insurance dispute are scheduled to be arraigned Monday in San Fernando Superior Court.

SHOPPER'S LANE -- The Santa Clarita City Council will consider on Tuesday a developer's request that the city pay $12.8 million for road improvements needed to support the area's first regional mall.
