BFN [Text] By decree of the Azerbaijani president, Seyavush Mamed ogly Velimamedov has been relieved of his post of head of Agdamskiy Rayon Executive Authority. Elman Ferman ogly Allahverdiyev has been appointed head of the Terterskiy [Mir-Bashirskiy] Rayon Executive Authority. Serdar Nedzhit ogly Hamidov was relieved from this post.
