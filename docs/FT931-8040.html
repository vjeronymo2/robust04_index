930220 FT 20 FEB 93 / Trafalgar House closes Clyde yard TRAFALGAR House has closed the Scott Lithgow construction yard at Port Glasgow on the lower Clyde because of a lack of orders in the offshore fabrication industry. The former shipyard, which last April employed 900 people, has had almost no work since November. The yard is for sale as a going concern and employs 24 people.
