930201 FT 01 FEB 93 / International Company News: Frost 2-for-1 scrip Shares in Frost Group rose 26p to 570p on Friday when it announced a 2-for-1 scrip issue to improve marketability. Frost runs the largest independent chain of petrol filling stations in the UK. Mr James Frost, chairman, said the price of the shares was comparatively high in relation to many UK listed companies.
