July 5, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: PASADENA;

REWARDS FOR SAVING WATER

Water users who reduce their consumption by 15% or more this summer will be rewarded with prizes of drought-tolerant seeds and plants and ultra-low volume toilets.

The rewards are part of the Water Savers Awards Program to be conducted this month through September by the city's Water and Power Department.

In addition, a group of water customers will be selected every two weeks, and their water usage compared with the same billing period in 1989. The two customers saving the most water during each two-week period will receive free toilets.

Information on the water-saver program and on how to conserve can be obtained by calling (818) 584-WATER.
