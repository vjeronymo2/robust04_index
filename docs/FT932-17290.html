930401 FT 01 APR 93 / UK Company News: Boxmore Intl Boxmore International, the USM-quoted packaging and printing group, announced pre-tax profits up 18 per cent to Pounds 3.62m in 1992, against Pounds 3.06m. Turnover rose 44 per cent to Pounds 32.2m (Pounds 22.3m) and the result was after an exceptional Pounds 289,000 charge (Pounds 46,000 credit). A proposed final dividend of 2.53p makes a total of 3.68p (3.475p adjusted), payable from earnings per share of 13.1p (11.9p).
