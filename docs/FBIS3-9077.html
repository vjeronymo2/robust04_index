Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tuzla, Bosnia-Herzegovina, March (AFP) -- An armoured vehicle of the United Nations Nordic battalion was destroyed Friday by shell and heavy machine gun fire near Gradacac in northern Bosnia, UNPROFOR spokeman Anders Person announced. Person told AFP that the nine soldiers in the vehicle were safe, adding: "We don't know who shot at the vehicle." Twenty heavy tanks were sent to the scene and NATO planes flew overhead as a "power demonstration," Person said.
