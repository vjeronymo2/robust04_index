930810 FT 10 AUG 93 / International Company News: Earnings slide at gold producer CAMBIOR, the international gold producer, saw second-quarter revenues climb 26 per cent to CDollars 66m (USDollars 51.50m) but net profit slip, mainly because of higher costs. Net profit was 8 cents a share against 16 cents a year earlier, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal.
