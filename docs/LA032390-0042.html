March 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: BABYWATCH

Live With Frank and Kathie Lee: Frank and Kathie Lee Gifford are the proud parents of Cody Newton Gifford, who made his debut on Thursday, weighing 8 pounds, 15 ounces.

Both mother and baby are doing fine. The couple chose the name Cody while watching a Cleveland Browns game. Cody Risien, offensive tackle for the Browns, coincidentally retired on Thursday at age 32. Another coincidence: It was his birthday. BETH KLEID
