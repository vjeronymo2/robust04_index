Language: English Article Type:BFN [By Georgian News Agency correspondent for TASS] [Text] Tbilisi March 29 TASS -- The Georgian Defence Ministry's press service refuted today an Abkhazian report claiming Abkhazians have occupied the whole of Abkhazian Svanetia. According to information of the press service, position fighting goes on in the Kodor Ravine near the Verkhnyaya Lata settlement. The enemy has put Georgian positions under artillery and mortar fire and uses heavy materiel. Some ten combat choppers have brought more personnel and materiel to the area, but the sides' positions have not been considerably changed within the past 24 hours. The volunteers who have five killed and four wounded are repulsing the enemy who has lost up to 60 militants, reports the press center.
