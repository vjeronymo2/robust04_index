Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] By order of Viktor Chernomyrdin, the Economics Minister Aleksandr Shokhin, as a member of the government's presidium, is temporarily in charge of the ministry for cooperation with the CIS states, Shokhin himself told journalists on Monday. According to Shokhin, "Vladimir Mashitz will be responsible for the economic affairs in the ministry." As for Konstantin Zatulin, a member of the PRES [Party of Russian Unity and Accord] faction in the State Duma, of which Shokhin is a member) [no open parentheses as received], he is the head of the Duma's Committee for CIS Affairs and Ties with Compatriots. Shokhin has no information about the nomination of Zatulin as the minister for cooperation with the CIS states. As it was reported earlier, Shokhin was also appointed the Economics Minister. Shokhin hopes that the Economics Ministry will have "real authorities, although this does not mean the revival of GOSPLAN (the State Planning Committee)." Shokhin also expressed hope for the real interaction with the Finance Ministry reminding that in the past the two ministries had different approaches to the resolution of economic problems and public discussions took place which the new economics minister hopes to avoid in the future.
