BFN [Excerpt] A responsible source at the Ministry of Defense of the Democratic Republic of Yemen has said that the forces of the ruling military regime in Sanaa continued today their desperate attacking attempts on all the battlefronts. They launched random rocket and artillery attacks on residential quarters and peaceful villages in expression of the regime's hysterical state and inability to achieve any progress on all the battlefronts. In a statement to the ADEN NEWS AGENCY, the source pointed out that the Armed Forces of the Democratic Republic of Yemen faced with legendary courage these desperate attempted offensives. They inflicted crushing blows on the invading forces resulting in heavy losses. The source pointed out that eight hostile tanks, three armored cars and two Katyusha rocket vehicles were destroyed. The northern troops also suffered hundreds of dead and wounded. [passage omitted]
