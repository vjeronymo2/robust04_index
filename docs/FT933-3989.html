930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Sumit net asset value rises to 103p Sumit, the development and venture capital investment trust, reported fully diluted net asset value per share of 103p at June 30 compared with 97p a year earlier. The rise reflected an improvement in the performance of a number of the investments. With gross revenue advancing to Pounds 582,000 (Pounds 370,000) for the first half of 1993 net profits were up from Pounds 22,000 to Pounds 243,000. Earnings per share were 3.4p (0.3p) and an interim dividend of 0.7p (nil) is being paid. The company said it was confident the improvement seen in the first six months would continue in the next 12 to 18 months.
