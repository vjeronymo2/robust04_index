May 18, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

TRAFFIC TOLL

There was a 6.8% increase in fatal accidents in Orange County in 1989 from the previous year, while statewide the number of fatal accidents decreased by half of one percent. The good news for Orange County in the California Highway Patrol's annual figures is that the number of fatal and injury accidents involving drivers who had been drinking decreased 12.9% and 11.1% respectively.
