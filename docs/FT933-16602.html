930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Gresham Telecom Gresham Telecomputing, the USM-quoted software design group, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 284,000 to Pounds 419,000 in the half year to April 30. After eliminating the discontinued hardware broking operation turnover was virtually unchanged at Pounds 3.06m. Earnings per share were 0.86p (0.57p).
