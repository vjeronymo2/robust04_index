930703 FT 03 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Chartwell deficit grows to Pounds 445,000 Strong pricing pressures and lower sales resulted in increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 445,000 at the ChartwellGroup, USM-quoted maker of carpet tiles and cubicles for the contract market, in the year ended March 31. Losses last time were Pounds 39,000. Turnover was reduced to Pounds 5.62m (Pounds 9.51m). There were exceptional reorganisation and relocation costs of Pounds 80,000. Losses per share were 5p (0.4p earnings) and there is no dividend (0.4p). The directors believed the company would return to profitability during 1993-94.
