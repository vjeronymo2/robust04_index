Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] The first and largest group of the Greek expeditionary contingent in Somalia was repatriated yesterday afternoon. The Greek soldiers returned with their "daughter," a young Somali girl whom they adopted during their stay in the distant country, and her Greek grandmother. The political leadership of the Ministry of National Defense welcomed the 70 Greek soldiers and officers, the 13-year-old Maria Andreou and her grandmother Anna Vourexaki during an official ceremony yesterday afternoon at the Elevsis military airport. The repatriation of the remaining 40 men will take place in the middle of next month after arranging for the return of the hardware which was sent to Somalia.
