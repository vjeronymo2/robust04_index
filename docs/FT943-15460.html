940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / W Europe car sales up 11.8% West European new car sales rose by an estimated 11.8 per cent in June compared with the same month last year, providing fresh evidence that the region's car market is firmly on course for recovery. Provisional statistics from the European Automobile Manufacturers' Association show that the June upturn helped lift sales for the first half of the year to 6.09m, an increase of 6.6 per cent on the same period of 1993.
