941027 FT 27 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Venturi Inv Trust Venturi Investment Trust's net assets stood at Pounds 9m at the end of the six months to September 30, a fall of 2.5 per cent, compared with a 1.9 per cent decline in the FT-SE 100 Index. The undiluted net asset value per ordinary geared share was 4.2 per cent down at 27.47p. Earnings per income share were 1.62p (1.84p). An interim dividend of 1.66p (1.56p).
