BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 11 (KCNA) -- The book "21st Century and Leader" is now being widely read by people in Seoul and Kyonggi Province, South Korea, according to radio Voice of National Salvation from Seoul. The book deals with the greatness and leadership feats of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. A professor Kim of Seoul National University said General Kim Chong-il is the leader of the 21st century. The earth moves around the dear leader Kim Chong-il who is the conning-tower of the era of independence and the world is advancing along the road indicated by him, the professor added. Choe Sun-nam, a company clerk residing in Songdong district, Seoul, said he could hardly repress the national pride of holding General Kim Chong-il in esteem as the leader of the 21st century. National concord, the future of the reunified country and world peace lie in holding in high esteem the dear leader Kim Chong-il who is possessed of such leadership ability as universal gravitation with which he unites all the people into one with his magnanimous politics of love for and trust in the people, he said. A writer Yi residing in Uijongbu city, Kyonggi Province, had this to say: The dear leader Kim Chong-il is not only a great master of independent politics who has brilliantly carried into practice the anthropocentric chuche idea fathered by President Kim Il-song in the land of the northern half of Korea and built a paradise of the people, but also a peerless great man who is possessed of literary and military accomplishments, versatile talents and profound knowledge and popular virtues. He wished the dear leader Kim Chong-il good health and a long life for the prospering 21st century.
