Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over still of regional map] [Text] One terrorist has been killed in operations carried out by the security forces in the Kocadagi region of Diyarbakir's Kulp District. A total of 57 persons abetting the terrorist organization and trying to get organized in the state of emergency region have been captured. Two terrorists and two abettors have been captured in Malatya. According to a statement issued by the Malatya Governor's Office, three Kalashnikovs, three hand grenades, and organizational documents have been seized.
