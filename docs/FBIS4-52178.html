BFN [Text] His Majesty Norodom Sihanouk Varaman, king of Cambodia, has said that the Khmer Rouge should surrender to the Royal Government of Cambodia [RGC] the regions they control, dissolve all their armed forces, and join the RGC and the Khmer Royal Armed Forces through an amendment of the Constitution. This is a solution. Another is the reorganization of elections. His Majesty stated this in his 7 May clarification letter. The king added that the two solutions are just his own suggestions and that he has not asked the government or the National Assembly to adopt or implement them.
