920903 FT 03 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Cakebread Robey losses deepen Cakebread Robey, the Middlesex-based builders merchant, has announced a drop of 8.8 per cent in turnover to Pounds 11.39m for the first half of 1992, and an increased pre-tax loss of Pounds 667,000. The loss for the whole of 1991 was Pounds 1.65m following a first half deficit of Pounds 191,000. The loss was struck after redundancy costs of Pounds 56,000. There is again no dividend. Recession caused merchanting sales to fall by a further 7.9 per cent and bad debts at Pounds 178,000 for the half year are already well above the previous year's total. Manufacturing turnover fell by 14 per cent.
