BFN [Text] The first meeting of the Northern League's federal political secretariat ended shortly after midnight. During the meeting, matters pertaining to the organization and work of League leaders in national institutions were discussed. The League confirmed its full support for the government. Institutional Reforms Minister Francesco Speroni also explained the meaning of the words laborism and independence. Laborism is understood as "nonconservatism" and independence as "a faction of constructive criticism" within the government, which "propels the League into goverment."
