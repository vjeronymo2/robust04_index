920903 FT 03 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Morgan Crucible Pounds 10m purchases Morgan Crucible has completed five acquisitions costing a total Pounds 10.3m. The companies are expected to achieve a combined annual operating profit of more than Pounds 2.5m. The companies acquired are the Lodge ceramics division of Smiths Industries, a majority interest in Refractories and Mineral Investments BV, the clean room wipes business and related assets of Coventry Manufacturing of Los Angeles, Epirez of Australia, a manufacturer of speciality chemicals, and the cutting fluid business and related assets of Tapmatic Corporation of California.
