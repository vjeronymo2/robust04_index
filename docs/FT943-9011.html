940813 FT 13 AUG 94 / Finance and the Family: C & G's new share-out scheme - At a glance Cheltenham & Gloucester Building Society has put forward a new scheme for sharing out the Pounds 1.8bn cash bid from Lloyds Bank. This would increase payments to members to up to Pounds 13,500 for each qualifying account. The meeting at which members will vote is now planned for March 1995. (Details page V).
