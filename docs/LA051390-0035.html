May 13, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ONO AND THE MEDIA

Re "Yoko Ono, a Decade After the 'Horrible Thing,' " May 6:

Interviewer Glenn Plaskin wasted little time with polite patter and dug into the subjects that we're all dying to know about: Yoko's abortions, finances, sexual conduct and drug history.

Amid all this he asks, "No matter what Yoko does, she's frequently the victim of bad press. Any idea why?"

More amazing still is that Yoko somehow felt obliged to answer these inquiries. Being famous must be a real treat.

ALEXANDER D. VAIL

Avalon
