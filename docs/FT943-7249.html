940824 FT 24 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Capital Industries dampens speculation Directors of Capital Industries, the financial services and packaging materials group, yesterday moved to dampen speculation of a possible bid for the company by stating that neither it nor Rutland Trust, its main shareholder, had received any approach nor been in any talks which might lead to an offer. The statement followed a sharp rise in the company's share price from 174p to 184p on Monday. Yesterday, the shares eased 1p to 183p.
