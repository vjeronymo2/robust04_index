941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / International Company News: Marsh & McLennan ahead Marsh & McLennan reported third-quarter revenue of Dollars 827m, up 8 per cent from Dollars 766m for the same period of 1993, agencies report. The world's largest insurance broking organisation announced a management shake-up, with Mr David Holbrook elected chairman, Mr John Sinnott president and chief executive, and Mr Timothy Mahoney vice-chairman. Net income increased 10 per cent to Dollars 83m, compared with Dollars 76m last year. Earnings per share grew to Dollars 1.14, an increase of 10 per cent from Dollars 1.04 a year ago. For the nine months ended September 30, revenue increased 8 per cent to Dollars 2.6bn from Dollars 2.4bn last year. Net income totalled Dollars 299m, an increase of 11 per cent from Dollars 270m a year ago.
