930519 FT 19 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Narborough hit by exchange differences Narborough Plantations' pre-tax tax profits fell from Pounds 176,000 to Pounds 102,000 in the six months to December 31. The culprit was the exchange rate; the difference on exchange in the first six months was a loss of Pounds 26,000 against a profit of Pounds 82,000. An interim dividend of 0.5p (same) has been declared; pre-tax earnings were 0.76p (1.32p) per share.
