Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] News from the south indicates that the Israeli occupation forces continue to fire scores of illumination flares over Kafr Tibnit, Zawtar al-Sharqiyah, and Zawtar al-Gharbiyah Villages and the al-Litani River basin. Earlier the occupation forces fired scores of shells in the direction of valleys and hills adjacent to the al-Litani River basin. Reasons for the shelling were unknown.
