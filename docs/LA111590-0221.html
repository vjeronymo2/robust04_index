November 15, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

TROY HIGH TO HOLD AN ALUMNI NIGHT IN BASKETBALL

The Troy High School boys' basketball team will hold an alumni night beginning at 6:30 p.m. Wednesday at Troy.

The first event will be a "legends" game featuring alumni from odd years against those from even years. That will be followed at 7:30 by a game matching alumni of the past six years against the varsity.
