910502 FT 02 MAY 91 / (CORRECTED) Letter: Ignoring reality Correction (published 3rd May 1991) appended to this article. Sir, According to the London Business School economists (April 29), business confidence should have returned to its pre-Second World War level by now. Their statistical techniques indicate that UK firms remain unduly pessimistic Is this a case of 'if reality does not agree with my model, too bad for reality'? Stephan Schattmann, 65c Wigmore Street, W1 Correction (published 3rd May 1991). Mr Stephan Schattmann's letter yesterday referred to the Gulf war. The reference to the Second World War was an editing error.
