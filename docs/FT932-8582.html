930518 FT 18 MAY 93 / International Company News: Profits rise at Bank of Ayudhya Bank of Ayudhya, Thailand's fifth-largest commercial bank, said net profits rose 15 per cent in the first quarter of 1993 to Bt647.71m, reports AP-DJ. The result is roughly in line with analysts' forecasts that called for Bt2,615m in the full year.
