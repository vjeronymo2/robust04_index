Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The British minister of state for foreign and commonwealth affairs and minister for overseas development, Baroness Lynda Chalker, arrived in the country today for a two-day official visit to Malawi. During the visit, Baroness Chalker will hold discussions with His Excellency The President Ngwazi [paramount] Dr. Kamuzu Banda as well as political leaders from both the ruling and opposition parties. She will also meet members of the National Consultative Committee, among other (?leaders). Speaking to newsmen on arrival in Lilongwe, Baroness Chalker welcomed changes that are taking place in Malawi [word indistinct]. She said it was important that the progress that has been achieved so far should be maintained. Baroness Chalker noted that her government has already contributed $300,000 through the United Nations to assist in Malawi's forthcoming general election. She added that she will be discussing further [words indistinct] with the Malawi Government.
