931126 FT 26 NOV 93 / Technology: Data networks keep ahead of the pack - Worth Watching The market for data networks in Europe will be worth Ecu4.8bn (Pounds 3.65bn) by 1997, almost double the market in 1992, according to the latest forecasts from consultants Ovum. According to Vans Markets Europe, the traditional telephone companies will take a decreasing share of the market for these value-added networks, which include packet switched, X25 and managed data networks. France and Germany are the two biggest markets, followed by Spain and the UK. Ovum: UK, 071 255 2670.
