BFN [From the "Union Radio News" newscast] [Text] Members of Congress have expressed surprise at the suspension of guarantees. The Democratic Action Party, National Convergence, and MAS [Movement Toward Socialism] have announced that they support the measures. MAS Deputy [Gustavo] Marquez said the country was in need of forceful decisions. Copei [Social Christian Party], however, has asked the government to explain many issues that remain obscure. According to Copei President Hilarion Cardozo, the government's incoherence, the inconsistency of the measures, and the extemporaneous manner in which they were disclosed have intensified the crisis.
