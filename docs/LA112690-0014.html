November 26, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

A Swinging Series: A new series of three Saturday-night concerts beginning Feb. 9 has been unveiled by the Los Angeles Philharmonic to reach a younger audience. Each evening begins with a reception and private viewing at the Museum of Contemporary Art, followed by the concert and dancing (Latin/European, rock and big band) in the Dorothy Chandler Pavilion. Entitled "Philharmonic Style," the subscription costs $29.50-$39.50 for each evening. CLAUDIA PUIG
