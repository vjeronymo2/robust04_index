930422 FT 22 APR 93 / UK Company News: S Jerome cuts losses to Pounds 246,000 S JEROME & SONS (Holdings), the West Yorkshire-based textile group, cut pre-tax losses from Pounds 1.15m to Pounds 246,000 for the 1992 year. Turnover totalled Pounds 36.2m (Pounds 37.1m) of which textiles accounted for Pounds 21.4m, an increase of 7.5 per cent. The results included nine months of CMR, the electronics division which was sold in October. The sale helped reduce year-end borrowings to Pounds 2.86m (Pounds 6.38m). Losses per share were trimmed to 3.7p (10.5p). The dividend for the year is omitted - last year 1p was paid.
