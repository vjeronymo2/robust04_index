Language: English Article Type:CSO [Excerpt] The Drug Enforcement Commission (DEC) has arrested 22 Zambians and seven foreigners for trafficking in narcotic drugs with a total street value of K438.5m between November 1 and December 24. Of the 29 people arrested, 20 had been successfully prosecuted and convicted, six were awaiting final completion of their cases and only one was discharged. Among foreigners who had drugs ranging from cannabis to heroin were two Guineans, one Namibian, one South African, one Tanzanian, one Zimbabwean and a Malian. A statement released by the commission in Lusaka yesterday shows the drugs seized by officers were mandrax worth K174,954,600, heroin valued at K185,062,500, cannabis--K79,700 and cannabis seeds, worth K7,810,000.
