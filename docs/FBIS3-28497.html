Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 6 (KCNA) -- The foreign minister of the Republic of Cuba, Roberto Robaina Gonzalez, in a solidarity message to his Korean counterpart Kim Yong-nam, reaffirmed the invariable support and active solidarity of Cuba for the arduous struggle of the Korean people for socialist construction and the independent and peaceful reunification of the country, the supreme desire of the nation. "The Cuban people will always express solidarity with the Korean people in their efforts to fulfil the noble tasks put forward by President Kim Il-song in his New Year address," stated the message.
