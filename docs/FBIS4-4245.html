BFN [Text] Beijing, April 29 (XINHUA) -- The People's Insurance Company of China has decided to raise the indemnity for air trip accident insurance as of May 1 this year, today's "PEOPLE'S DAILY" quoted an official of the company as saying. The indemnity for each ticket with a premium worth 10 yuan will rise to 100,000 yuan (about 11,200 U.S. dollars) from 60,000 yuan, the official said.
