BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 19 -- A delegation of the Control Commission of the Communist Party of Vietnam Central Committee led by Do Quang Thang, Politburo member, secretary of the party Central Committee and head of the commission, paid a working visit to China from May 7-17. During the visit, made at the invitations of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC), the delegation held talks with a delegation of the Central Commission for Discipline Inspection of the CPC Central Committee led by its secretary Mr. Wei Jianxing who is also Politburo member and secretary of the CPC Central Committee. The two sides informed each other of their activities, exchanged experiences and discussed measures to promote their cooperation. The delegation was also received by Mr. Hu Jintao, permanent Politburo member of the CPC Central Committee, and visited a number of economic and cultural establishments and historical sites in Beijing, Xian, Shanghai, Guangzhou and the special economic zone of Shenzhen.
