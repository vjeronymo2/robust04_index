July 9, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MAYOR BRADLEY REVAMPS DWP

In reply to your June 23 article: "Moratorium Imposed on DWP Tours":

I commend Bradley in his attempt to make DWP an honest and responsible agency.

However, I must warn the mayor to keep a close eye on DWP officials, as they are very adept at skirting agreements, moratoriums, laws, etc., After all, they've had nearly 100 years of practice here in the Owens Valley.

Also, Mayor Bradley, if you think about it, its initials give us clues to its true character. In your case, "DWP" stands for "Damn Well Please!" as the agency has done just that for years. However, in the Owens Valley the initials stand for "Damn Water Pirates."

MELINDA SALMONDS

Olancha
