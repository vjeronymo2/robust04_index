BFN [GIA report under rubric "According to Reports From Correspondents and Information Agencies": "Session of the Adzharian Supreme Council Presidium"] [Text] A regular session of the Adzharian Supreme Council Presidium has been held. The personal request submitted by Dzhemal Nakashidze, chairman of the Adzharian Autonomous Republic Council of Ministers, to be released from his post has been granted. The proposals made by the heads of ministries and departments in the autonomous republic have been taken into account and it has been decided that the chairman of the Supreme Council will temporarily also fulfill the duties of chairman of the republic's Council of Ministers. By a decree of the autonomous republic's Supreme Council Presidium, Aleksandr Pagava has been appointed first deputy chairman of the Adzharian Autonomous Republic Council of Ministers and has been released from his post as a minister acting as chief coordinator of branch ministries and departments.
