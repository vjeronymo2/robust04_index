Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A responsible source at the Ministry of Interior has stated that at 1255 today, Wednesday, 26 January 1994, an explosion took place at Cinema Revoli, located in Saqf al-Sayl, Quraysh Street, when a film was being shown. The explosion took place in the projector room, which is situated in the theater's second floor. The explosion wounded seven people, one seriously. There was also some material damage to the theater. The wounded were admitted to a hospital. The competent security agencies are investigating the incident to determine its causes.
