BFN [Text] Rasht, Gilan Prov., May 13, IRNA -- President Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani said here today that the capacity of the discovered gas deposits of the country would be enough for 250 years. Noting that gas is considered as the best fuel for the country, the president said piped gas had been supplied to 240 cities and towns throughout the country, and that all other places should indeed be provided with piped gas. Elsewhere the president said Iran is considered as a steel exporter today, and predicted that steel production would reach five million tons in the current calendar year. Iran is treading the path of self-reliance in many areas including aluminium, copper, and petrochemical industries, he added.
