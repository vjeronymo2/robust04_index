920512 FT 12 MAY 92 / UK Company News: New management for BAA hotels BAA, the airport company, has brought its involvement in hotel management to a close by handing the operations of its two remaining airport hotels, at Heathrow and Stansted, to Hilton. The Heathrow Sterling hotel will become a Hilton International while the Stansted Harlequin will be a Hilton National. The mangement will be undertaken on 50 year leases.
