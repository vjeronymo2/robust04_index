BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 28 -- Kim Il-song, secretary general of the Workers's Party and president of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK) has received the visiting delegation of the Communist Party of Vietnam led by Hong Ha, secretary of the CPVCC [Communist Party of Vietnam Central Committee]. President Kim Il-song warmly welcomed the Vietnamese party delegation's visit as a manifestation of the strengthening of the relations between the two parties, and peoples. He asked Secretary Hong Ha to convey his best regards to party General Secretary Do Muoi, President Le Duc Anh and other Vietnamese party leaders. He also expressed his pleasure at the fine development of the relationship between the two parties and peoples, and his wish for new step of development in the DPRK-Vietnam relations. For his part, Mr. Hong Ha conveyed best regards of party General Secretary Do Muoi and President Le Duc Anh to President Kim Il-song, Kim Chong-il and other Korean party leaders. He affirmed that Vietnamese party's policy is to enhance the traditional friendly relations of the two peoples, and for peace cooperation and development in the region and in the world as a whole.
