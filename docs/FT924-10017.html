921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / Binns Sunderland store to close BINNS department store in Sunderland is to close on January 30 with the loss of 140 jobs, its parent company, House of Fraser, announced yesterday. The company blamed the recession, high local unemployment, poor economic prospects and the loss of trade to the House of Fraser store at the MetroCentre, Gateshead. It hopes to redeploy some staff to its other five stores in north-east England.
