910418 FT 18 APR 91 / UK Company News: Castle Mill Intl back in the black Castle Mill International, with interests in gifts, character products and clothing, swung from losses of Pounds 88,555 to profits of Pounds 313,861 pre-tax for 1990. Turnover advanced from Pounds 15.7m to Pounds 23.14m. The dividend for the year is lifted to 1.15p (1p) from earnings of 0.8p (losses 0.88p). Extraordinary income of Pounds 150,423 (provisions Pounds 334,850) reflected profit on disposals.
