Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Independent Electoral Commission [IEC] has begun an investigation into reports of people under the age of 18 years being in possession of temporary voter cards. The IEC said the irregularities had allegedly occurred in Natal. Two Scotland Yard officers are assisting in the investigation. The IEC said steps had been taken, in cooperation with the Department of Home Affairs, to tighten procedures for obtaining temporary voter cards. It said that possible strategies were being investigated to filter out underage holders of temporary voter cards before the elections.
