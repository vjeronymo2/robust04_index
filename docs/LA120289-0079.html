December 2, 1989, Saturday, Southland Edition

THE MALTA SUMMIT;

TODAY'S SCHEDULE

* 9:45 a.m. -- President Bush arrives aboard Soviet cruiser Slava; participates in arrival ceremony with Soviet President Mikhail S. Gorbachev.

* 10:05 a.m. -- Bush meets with Gorbachev aboard ship, and remains for lunch.

* 2:35 p.m. -- Bush returns to Belknap, private time.

* 4:30 p.m. -- Gorbachev and Bush meet again aboard Slava.

* 7:55 p.m. -- Gorbachev arrives aboard U.S. cruiser Belknap for dinner hosted by Bush.

Times are Malta time
