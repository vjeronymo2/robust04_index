920529 FT 29 MAY 92 / Intermission The Royal Opera House, Covent Garden, is to close for three years from 1997 for Pounds 250m of refurbishment backstage and front-of-house. An appeal is to be launched for Pounds 90m, with the rest of the cost coming from a shops-and-offices project. The Royal Opera House said: 'The auditorium is listed and we can't completely change it . . . but there are things that can be improved, like air conditioning'
