930410 FT 10 APR 93 / World Commodities Prices Fruit and vegetables French Golden Delicious are a very good buy this week at 25-35p a lb (25-45p) reports the FFVIB. Other good buys include Spania strawberries at 50-80p (60-80p) per 8oz punnet and Jaffa Shamouti oranges at 10-25p each (10-25p). Amongst the vegetables English leeks continue to be good quality at 50-60p a lb (55-70p) and English winter grown Spring greens are excellent at 35-40p (45-50p). Green, red and yellow peppers are between Pounds 1.00-2.50 a lb depending on the colour (Pounds 1.00-2.50). A variety of lettuce is available this week, flat/round at 25-35p each (25-30p) and Iceberg at 65-85p each (50-90p).
