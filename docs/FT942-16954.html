940407 FT 07 APR 94 / UK Company News: Epwin expands in Canada Epwin Group, a maker of PVC-u windows and doors, is acquiring 50 per cent of Berlinex Polymers, based in Edmonton, Canada, for CDollars 3.5m (Pounds 1.7m) cash. Berlinex, which makes its own range of PVC-u window extrusions, reported profits before interest and tax of CDollars 819,000 on turnover of CDollars 4.3m in 1993. Net assets at December 31 were CDollars 1.67m. Epwin will fund the acquisition from its own resources and bank facilities.
