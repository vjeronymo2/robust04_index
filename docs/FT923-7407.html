920818 FT 18 AUG 92 / Hekmatyar 'ruled out' of government AFGHAN President Burhanuddin Rabbani yesterday ruled out any return to the coalition Islamic government for a renegade mujahideen chief whose forces have pounded Kabul with rockets and artillery for more than a week, Reuter reports from Kabul. He said Mr Gulbuddin Hekmatyar, who leads the fundamentalist Hezb-e-Islami faction, had given up any right to membership in the 10-member ruling Leadership Council when he launched a his rocket bombardment of Kabul 10 days ago. Mr Hekmatyar renewed a ceasefire offer on condition the Uzbek militia that fought for the former Soviet-backed communist government withdrew from the capital within a week, the Hezb-run Message of Freedom radio said.
