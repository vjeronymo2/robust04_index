BFN [Text] David Levi, the chairman of the Jordan Rift Valley Regional Council, says that according to an updated map he has seen, the boundaries of the Jericho autonomous enclave will be larger than what was recently agreed upon with the PLO. The town's boundaries have been expanded to include an area of several square kilometers to the south. The perimeters of the village of al-'Awja' have also been expanded almost up to the border fence. Levi told our correspondent Gil Litman that construction work on the Jericho bypass road has not yet begun.
