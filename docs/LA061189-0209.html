June 11, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

PASSENGER KILLED WHEN TRUCK FLIPS

A 24-year-old man was killed early Saturday when the truck he was riding in overturned on the on-ramp from Camino Capistrano to northbound Interstate 5 in San Juan Capistrano, according to the California Highway Patrol.

Andrew Sanchez of San Juan Capistrano was crushed to death when the truck flipped over and fell into a ditch about 2:45 a.m., CHP officials said.

The pickup's driver, identified by the CHP as Raul Macias of San Juan Capistrano, was arrested on suspicion of felony drunk driving.
