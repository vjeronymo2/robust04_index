BFN [Text] Khartoum, 21 Apr (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak has made a telephone call to Sudanese President 'Umar Hasan al-Bashir, during which he reiterated Egypt's keenness to safeguard Sudan's unity, assuring him that it will do its utmost in this regard. The official AL-SUDAN AL-HADITH newspaper said today that Syria is continuing its efforts to mediate between Egypt and Sudan and convene meetings between officials of the two countries to narrow the differences in views between them on the Hala'ib dispute.
