Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 17 KYODO -- The farm ministry Thursday [17 March] announced a plan to import an additional 400,000 tons of rice to meet the rice shortage in the preharvest season. This brings the total amount of rice set for emergency import to 2.1 million tons. The additional 400,000 tons will consist of medium- and short- grain rice produced in Australia, the United States and China, officials of the Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries ministry said.
