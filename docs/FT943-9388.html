940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Chamberlain oversubscribed The intermediaries offer from Chamberlain Phipps, the shoe components and footwear manufacturer, was subscribed 1.8 times. Of the placing and offer of 22.9m shares at 165p apiece, 15.2m were placed with investors. Of the balance, applications were received for 14m. Dealings are expected to start on August 15.
