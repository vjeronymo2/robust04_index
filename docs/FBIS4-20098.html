BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- Federal Interior Minister Manfred Kanther and his Croatian counterpart, Ivan Jarnjak, signed an agreement in Bonn today which makes provisions for, among other things, the repatriation of former civil war refugees from Croatia. The Interior Ministry announced in Bonn today that the federal states can now begin repatriation on 1 May. This applies to refugees who came to Germany before 22 May 1992. To handle the devastation caused by the war, repatriation will take place in stages. Moreover, refugees from the areas of Croatia occupied, disputed, or devastated by the Serbs will not be returned until a second phase in the first half year of 1995.
