BFN [Text] Finance Minister Dr. Muhammad al-Razzaz has stated that 21.446 billion pounds will be earmarked for servicing the internal and external debts in the new budget, which will begin on 1 July. Dr. al-Razzaz pointed out that the treasury bills make up most of the internal debts owed by the government. He stressed the importance of the treasury bills in streamlining the budget deficit and absorbing the surplus in the country, whether from individuals or banks, to reduce inflation. The minister also stressed the importance of the treasury bills in stopping the drop in interest rates on savings in Egyptian pounds and in maintaining the rates of exchange. Dr. al-Razzaz added that the state is spending 1.5 billion pounds to adjust the financial structure of the Egyptian railway and 220 millions to extend electricity to the countryside.
