920808 FT 08 AUG 92 / The Barcelona Olympics: Two Britons in javelin final Britain's Steve Backley (left) banished the memory of last year's world championship nightmare to qualify for today's javelin final. The 23-year-old European champion, who failed in the qualifier in Tokyo 12 months ago, unleashed an 80.76m effort with his second attempt to go through automatically. Mick Hill joined Backley in the final with a throw of 79.66m. Former world-record holder Jan Zelezny of Czechoslovakia led the 12 qualifiers with a throw of 83.96m.
