920813 FT 13 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Brandon Hire in red and passes final Brandon Hire, the USM-quoted tool, catering and furniture hire group, blamed the recession as it lapsed into the red in the 12 months to April 30 and passed its final dividend. Despite sizeable cost reductions, the pre-tax deficit amounted to Pounds 291,000 compared with profits of Pounds 54,000 in the previous year. Turnover fell some 18 per cent to Pounds 3.98m. Interest charges declined to Pounds 230,000 (Pounds 259,000). Gearing was reduced from 45 per cent to 35 per cent. Losses per share emerged at 1.72p (earnings of 0.87p). The interim dividend of 0.1p stands for the year against the previous single distribution of 3.33p.
