BFN [Text] The Federal Government has declared illegal the proposed national industrial action planned for Thursday [9 June] by some categories of employees of NEPA [National Electric Power Authority]. The minister of state for power and steel, Elder Wole Oyelese, told a Radio Nigeria Correspondent in Lagos that the proposed action was uncalled for. He explained that the employees were only out to create unnecessary problems for government. He reminded them that government will not hesitate to take drastic action against anyone who attempts to frustrate efforts aimed at installing peace and harmony in the country. The minister stated that everything has been done to forestall any disruption of electricity supply by unscrupulous elements.
