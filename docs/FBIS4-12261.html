BFN [Text] It has just been reported that a short while ago, a terrorist opened fire at several persons standing at the Ashdod Junction. Two people were wounded. One of the bystanders shot and killed the terrorist. Police forces and emergency medical teams rushed to the scene, and the wounded are currently receiving treatment. This was reported by our correspondents Yehudit Yehezqeli and Aviv Bushinsky. [Tel Aviv IDF Radio in Hebrew at 0616 GMT on 7 April adds: "Initial reports received by the defense establishment indicate that four people were apparently slightly wounded and are being treated at the scene" and that "additional terrorists may have been involved in the incident."]
