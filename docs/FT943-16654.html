940704 FT 04 JUL 94 / World News in Brief: Sampras victorious: Pete Sampras of the US beat Croatia's Goran Ivanisevic 7 - 6, 7 - 6, 6 - 0 to retain his Wimbledon singles title. Over the Wimbledon fortnight, more than 1,000 people were turned away with invalid tickets bought as part of corporate hospitality packages.
