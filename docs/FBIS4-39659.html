BFN [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk stated that following the withdrawal of nuclear weapons, the Ukrainian army will be equipped with high-precision weapons, which are not weapons of mass destruction. He told the cadets of the Zhitomir military school that Ukraine, Russia, the U.S. and other countries were working on this type of weapon. The acquisition of high-precision weapons is provided for in the Ukrainian military doctrine, approved by the Ukrainian parliament in November 1993.
