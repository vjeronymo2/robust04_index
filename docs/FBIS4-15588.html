BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Schegoleva] [Text] Sochi April 10 TASS -- The first meeting of the commission for voluntary return of refugees and displaced persons to Abkhazia ended late on Saturday night in Sochi. The meeting was held in accordance with the four-side agreement signed in Moscow on April 4. A statement adopted at the meeting stated the discussion of a program of the commission work, including the activities of experts on specific issues. The sides agreed that a mission of the U.N. High Commission for Refugees [UNHCR] will come on a working visit to the Abkhazian Gali region and areas of temporary accommodation of displaced persons "in order to prepare the arrangements on the return of refugees and displaced persons". The visit will be held before the second meeting of the commission scheduled for April 26. The sides confirmed their readiness to abide by principles and provisions of the Moscow agreement on voluntary return of refugees and displaced persons, proceeding from the fact that the return of refugees will be related to the deployment of peace-making forces. At the same time, the sides believes that preparations for the return of refugees and displaced persons must begin immediately. The statement was signed by head of the Georgian State committee for Refugees Valeriy Vashakidze, first deputy chairman of the Abkhazian Council of Ministers Leonid Lakerbaya, head of the Russian Federal Migration Service Nikolay Chulkov and Anton Vervey on behalf of the office of the U.N. High Commission for Refugees.
