BFN [Unattributed report: "Sympathies With 'Russia's Choice'"] [Text] There has been a meeting in the Kremlin between Russian President Boris Yeltsin and representatives of the "Russia's Choice" faction. They discussed collaboration and cooperation between democratic organizations. The president noted that he is close to the political views of the "Russia's Choice" bloc and that he would work in close contact with it. At the same time, Boris Yeltsin stressed that he will be seeking points of contact with other Russian political forces, since he is the president of all Russians, and will be meeting with representatives of other parties and blocs. Regarding the situation in the State Duma, Boris Yeltsin noted that, despite the broad range of political views in the Russian parliament, he is confident that he will be able as president to cooperate and reach common ground with the people's representatives. He noted with satisfaction that he has a good working relationship with State Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin.
