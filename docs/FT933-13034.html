930721 FT 21 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Quadramatic shares at 4% premium SHARES in Quadramatic, the coin-handling and optical group, ended their first day's trading yesterday at 128p, a 4 per cent premium to the flotation price of 123p. The placing and intermediates offer valued the Oldham-based company at Pounds 36.4m. It raised about Pounds 20m to pay off debt, and 25 per cent of the shares on offer went to retail investors.
