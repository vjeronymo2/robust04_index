November 22, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

General Automation Inc.: The Anaheim-based computer company reported a loss of $460,000 for the first quarter of its fiscal 1991. Net income of $54,000 was posted for the corresponding quarter last year, including extraordinary income of $233,000. Sales for the quarter ended Sept. 30 rose 37%, to $12.3 million from the year-earlier $9 million.
