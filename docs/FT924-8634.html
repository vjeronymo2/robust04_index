921110 FT 10 NOV 92 / UK Company News: AAH says it has 83% of CMRG AAH Holdings, the pharmaceuticals wholesaler, announced that as at 3pm on November 6 it had received valid acceptances for 22,068,806 shares - 83 per cent - in respect of its offer for CMRG. As acceptances exceeded more than 80 per cent of the share capital of CMRG, the offer has been declared unconditional and will remain open until further notice. The partial cash alternative however will close at 3p on Friday November 20. The Secretary of State for Trade and Industry has decided not to refer the transaction to the Monopolies and Mergers Commission.
