BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] [Defense Minister] Pavel Grachev left Moscow for Rostov-on-Don. The Russian defense minister has begun a three-day tour of the North Caucasus. Among other things, he will visit subunits of the Russian peacekeeping forces deployed in the zone of the Georgian-Abkhaz conflict. He will also find out how military units withdrawn to the North Caucasus region from foreign countries, including the Baltic republics and Germany, are settling down.
