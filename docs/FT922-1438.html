920623 FT 23 JUN 92 / Egyptian lawyer sentenced A Cairo court sentenced lawyer Rashad Nabih to three years' imprisonment yesterday for writing a bad cheque for EPounds 1.54bn (Pounds 250m) in connection with a massive Islamic banking scandal, Reuter reports from Cairo. The cheque was meant to repay some 187,000 depositors in the collapsed al-Rayan Islamic investment company. Judge Gamal el-Kholi said Nabih could pay EPounds 1m as an alternative to prison.
