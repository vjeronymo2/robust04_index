Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Zhang Huanli (1728 3562 0448): "Zhu Rongji Meets Japanese Chinese"] [Text] Tokyo, 26 Feb (XINHUA)--This morning, Vice Premier Zhu Rongji, who was visiting Japan, met with representatives of Chinese residents in Japan and Japanese citizens of Chinese origin at the Chinese Embassy in Japan. At the same time, he also called on the staff of the Chinese Embassy in Japan and other Chinese institutions in Japan. Zhu Rongji encouraged them to make further contributions to the development of Sino-Japanese good-neighborly and friendly relations and mutually beneficial cooperation. Today, Vice Premier Zhu Rongji also attended the luncheon held by Kisanro Ikeura, chairman of the Organization for Promoting Japanese-Chinese Investment. Zhu Rongji expressed his gratitude to the friends in the Organization for Promoting Japanese-Chinese Investment for promoting cooperation between the two countries and hoped that the Japanese business community would join the Chinese side in continuously developing mutually beneficial economic, trade, scientific, and technological cooperation. He Chunlin, deputy secretary general of the State Council, and other major members of Zhu's entourage also attended the luncheon.
