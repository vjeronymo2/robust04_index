BFN [Text] The president's office has released details of a letter sent to Silvio Berlusconi yesterday, in which President Oscar Luigi Scalfaro draws the prime minister's attention to certain problems "where a lack of a solution" could "cause problems for the Italian Republic both inside and outside its borders." Scalfaro writes that it is about giving assurances: 1. that there will be complete faithfulness to the alliances of our foreign policy; 2. that the person in charge of the Interior Ministry will not take up stances which are contrary to the principles of national unity; and 3. that the principles of social solidarity will be respected.
