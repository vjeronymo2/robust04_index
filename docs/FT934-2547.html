931214 FT 14 DEC 93 / Andorra set for coalition Andorra, the tiny principality on the Franco-Spanish border, emerged yesterday from its first constitution-based election after more than 700 years of feudalism on course for a coalition government, Reuter reports from Andorra la Vella. The centre-right Agrupament Nacional Democratic party of the outgoing head of government, Mr Oscar Ribas Reig, won the largest share of the vote. He expressed confidence in being able to pursue reform with a coalition government.
