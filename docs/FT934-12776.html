931025 FT 25 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Gartmore Shared Equity GARTMORE SHARED Equity Trust has received applications under its offer for subscription for 29.2m geared ordinary income shares at 100p each and for 18.8m zero dividend preference shares at 103p each, totalling Pounds 48.6m. As applications for the geared ordinary income shares exceeded the 26m available, applications up to 6,000 will be met in full and applicants above that will receive 43.5 per cent. Applications for the zero dividend preference shares will be met in full.
