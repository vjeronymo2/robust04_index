BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valentin Pavlov] [Text] Barnaul May 10 TASS -- The Novosibirsk water reservoire is threatened with arsenic and ammonia poisoning. A 30-cm-thick layer of foam, containing a high concentratiion of arsenic and ammonia has passed the Altay and is flowing now towards Novosibirsk. The poisoned stream is reported to be in the vicinity of the village of Shelabolikh on the border of the Novosibirsk region at the moment. Until now no poisoning cases have been reported, though there is a great danger of the contaminated water entering into the city water pipeline network.
