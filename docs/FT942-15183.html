940416 FT 16 APR 94 / UK Company News: Scroggs leaves with Pounds 770,000 Mr Cedric Scroggs, the former chief executive of Fisons who was ousted from the pharmaceuticals group last year, received an ex-gratia payment of Pounds 417,000 in respect of damages for breach of contract. Including salary he received a total of Pounds 731,837 plus pension contributions of Pounds 37,657. Fisons' annual report also discloses that it made an ex-gratia payment of Pounds 340,000 to Mr Bob Lankester who resigned from the group after his job as head of the scientific equipment division was removed in a reorganisation.
