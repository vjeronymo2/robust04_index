May 6, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

PROS AND CONS OF 'DR. GRIMES' BITTER PILL'

Regarding the very long article on Dr. Grimes, what a sad commentary on our civilization that such a talented and accomplished doctor is using his medical knowledge to extinguish life rather than save it.

It was especially disturbing to read that the doctor and his wife couldn't stand to see his first hospitalized pediatric patient die, but that it doesn't bother them to see a life snuffed out with the use of the RU-486 pill or other means.

EMMA P. COLLINS, Glendale
