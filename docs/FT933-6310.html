930828 FT 28 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Chillington talks on Anglo-Eastern stake The directors of Chillington Corporation, tools, trading and plantations group, stated that they are in discussions with a number of parties which may or may not lead to an offer being made for either its whole interest or a substantial part of its 49.2 per cent stake in Anglo-Eastern Plantations. Anglo-Eastern directors said it was possible that any such sale may lead to an offer being made for the whole of Anglo. Anglo's shares rose 11p to 59p while those of Chillington put on 3p to 35p.
