BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 2 (KCNA) -- Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] Hwang Chang-yop today met and had a friendly conversation with a delegation of the Coordinating Council of the Federation of Peace and Friendship Organisation of Mongolia and the Mongolia-Korea Friendship Association led by General Secretary of the Federation D. Bilegt Onna visit to Korea. D. Bilegt said that he was deeply moved to see achievements made by the Korean people under the wise guidance of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. He laid stress on expanding and developing Mongolia-Korea friendship.
