941213 FT 13 DEC 94 / International Company News: US life insurer pays Dollars 300m for fund manager Lincoln National, the US life insurance and financial services group based in Fort Wayne, Indiana, said it was paying around Dollars 300m for Delaware Management Holdings, which has Dollars 25bn under management. It will also take on Dollars 209m of the company's debt. The deal is another sign of the consolidation under way in the US investment management industry. With Delaware's Dollars 16bn of institutional assets and Dollars 9bn of mutual funds, the enlarged Lincoln will have Dollars 60bn under management in all. Delaware also manages Dollars 2.2bn of international bond and equity assets in London.
