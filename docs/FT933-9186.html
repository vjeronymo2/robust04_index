930811 FT 11 AUG 93 / Hospital waiting lists top 1 million THE NUMBER of people on hospital waiting lists has topped a million for the first time. Provisional Department of Health statistics published yesterday show 22,789 people joined waiting lists in the three months to the end of June, when the total reached 1,017,763. Over that period hospital waiting lists of people waiting more than a year for treatment grew by 15.7 per cent to 65,465. But Dr Brian Mawhinney, health minister, said the figures showed the number of patients waiting more than a year for treatment was down by 17,000 compared with the same period last year.
