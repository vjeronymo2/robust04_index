BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Igor Pshenichnikov] [Text] Oslo, 29 Apr -- Ways of settling the Bosnian crisis were discussed here today during a meeting between Vitaliy Churkin, the Russian president's special envoy and deputy foreign minister, and Norwegian Foreign Minister Bjorn Tore Godal. Vitaliy Churkin, who arrived in Oslo on Thursday evening, will also have talks here today with Thorvald Stoltenberg and Lord Owen, the co-chairmen of the coordinating committee of the international conference on the former Yugoslavia.
