940704 FT 04 JUL 94 / Letters to the Editor: View fell flat Sir, The interesting article by Colin Amery about the Pugin Exhibition (Architecture, June 20) included some debatable interpretation. Observations ranging from the alleged dependence of William Morris to allegedly ill-informed modern designers may not be supported by fact. Also, your correspondent's comments about flat designs and flat pack furniture might suggest sympathy with the flat earth movement] Janet Jones, Partners - Interiors, 10 Canonbury Place, London N1 2NQ
