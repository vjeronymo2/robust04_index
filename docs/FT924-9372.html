921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / International Company News: GE considers Polish fund GENERAL Electric is considering managing one of the 20 investment funds that Poland is soon to establish under its ambitious Mass Privatisation Scheme. According to Mr Alistair Stewart, a vice-president, the US group would be working on mass privatisation through Kidder and Peabody, one of GE's Financial Services subsidiaries and Onex, a Canadian holding company.
