Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Damascus, March 27 (XINHUA) -- The Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad today received a message from Kim Il-song, president of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK). The Syrian television quoted a spokesman of the Syrian presidency as saying that Choe Ki-chong, chairman of the Education Commission, conveyed the message to President al-Asad during their meeting this afternoon. However, the Syrian spokesman did not reveal anything about the content of the message. He only said that message dealt with the situation in the Far East and the bilateral relations between the two countries. Diplomatic sources here said that the visit of Kim Il-song's special envoy came at the time when the U.N. Security Council began discussing the file of nuclear activity of DPRK.
