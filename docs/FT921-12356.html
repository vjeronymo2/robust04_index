920127 FT 27 JAN 92 / Observer: Lost thimble Times are tough for the elite band of Paris fashion houses that opened the new season's haute couture collections yesterday. The couturiers are not the only ones under financial pressure. The 'Golden Thimble', the coveted prize traditionally given to the best collection of the season, will not be awarded this time. The Thimble is looking for a new backer. It, like the couturiers' customers, has run out of money.
