BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 27 May -- Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin today issued a decree on changes to the Council for Personnel Policy under the Russian president. According to the document, Nikolay Yegorov is replacing Sergey Shakhray on the Council. This follows the president's edict of 16 May relating to the minister for affairs of nationalities and regional policy, the president's press service said.
