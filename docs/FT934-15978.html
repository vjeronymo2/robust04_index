931008 FT 08 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Jones Group For the half year to June 30, Jones Group, the Irish manufacturing, shipping and oil distribution company, turned in pre-tax profits of IPounds 1.18m (Pounds 1.12m). This compared with losses of IPounds 5.29m which included a IPounds 6.5m loss on disposal of subsidiaries. The results were in accordance with FRS 3 and revealed turnover of IPounds 31.7m. Last time this figure amounted to IPounds 50.6m including IPounds 16.2m from discontinued operations. Earnings per share were 7.8p (44.4p losses) while the interim dividend is maintained at 4p.
