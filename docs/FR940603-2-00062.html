EFFECTIVE DATE: July 5, 1994. [Docket No. 94N&hyph;0194] Apothecon, et al.; Withdrawal of Approval of Six Abbreviated Antibiotic Applications AGENCY: Food and Drug Administration, HHS. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is withdrawing approval of six abbreviated antibiotic applications (AADA's). The holders of the AADA's notified the agency in writing that the drug products were no longer marketed and requested that the approval of the applications be withdrawn. EFFECTIVE DATE: July 5, 1994. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Lola E. Batson, Center for Drug Evaluation and Research (HFD&hyph;360), Food and Drug Administration, 7500 Standish Pl., Rockville, MD 20855, 301&hyph;594&hyph;1038.
