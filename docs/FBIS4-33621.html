BFN [Excerpt] EP [Peruvian Army] General Jaime Salinas Sedo, retired, was absolved yesterday by the Military Court Supreme Council on charges of slandering the Armed Forces. The Military Court, nevertheless, sentenced him to 60 days imprisonment for slandering his superior, Army Commander Nicolas Hermoza Rios. Military Court Prosecutor Colonel Raul Talledo Valdiviezo appealed the sentence after it was announced. He had requested a four year prison term and a fine of 100,000 soles. [passage omitted]
