May 2, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

ALL ABOARD: Anaheim's Snow House Moving Co. -- as its name indicates -- usually moves houses and usually rolls them through the streets. . . . But next week the company will move a 5,000-square-foot office building by water. . . . Because the one-story, modular structure, now being used by the L.A. Harbor Department in San Pedro, is too wide to move through the streets, it will be dollied to wharfside and hoisted onto a barge for a 1.5-mile trip to another waterfront site. Barbie Ludovise and Lanie Jones
