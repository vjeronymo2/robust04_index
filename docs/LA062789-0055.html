June 27, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Actress Diana Rigg will be the host of the PBS series "Mystery!" next season, taking over from Vincent Price who has hosted the program since 1981. Rigg, who played Emma Peel on the British spy series "The Avengers," also will star in a "Mystery!" presentation next year titled "Mother Love." ALEENE MacMINN
