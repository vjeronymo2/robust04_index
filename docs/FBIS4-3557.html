BFN [Text] The official talks between President Husni Mubarak and Chinese President Jiang Zemin began at the guest palace in Beijing today. The talks cover a number of bilateral issues and ways to bolster cooperation between the two countries as well as a number of important Arab and international issues. After their talks, the two presidents will attend a ceremony for the signing of two agreements between Egypt and China. The first agreement deals with legal cooperation, including the extradition of criminals, implementation of sentences, and cooperation in the collection of evidence and investigation. The second agreement deals with the protection and encouragement of investment in the two countries. The Chinese president will hold a formal dinner banquet at the guest palace this evening in honor of President Mubarak and the delegation accompanying him.
