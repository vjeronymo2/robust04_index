February 16, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

BLEDSOE MERIT SCHOLAR FINALIST

Tempestt Bledsoe, the 16-year-old actress who plays Vanessa Huxtable on "The Cosby Show," has become a finalist for a National Merit Scholarship, her publicist said.

The nationwide academic scholarship is awarded on the basis of students' academic record and scores on the PSAT, a scholastic aptitude examination, according to a statement issued Thursday by publicist David Brokaw.

Bledsoe has studied at the Professional Children's School in New York City. She is graduating from high school with straight A's, Brokaw said.
