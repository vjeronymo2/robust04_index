941010 FT 10 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Try director stands down Try Group, the construction and housebuilding group, said on Friday that Mr Terry Wood, managing director of the Try Build building subsidiary and a main board director, had resigned with immediate effect. No reason was given for the move, although the company later elaborated that Mr Wood was 'retiring of his own vocation'. Mr Wood, 54, joined Try in 1989 from R Mansell, the building group. He was appointed to the main Try board in 1992.
