Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Abu-Dhabi, 13 Jan (MENA)--AL-ITTIHAD today cites Arab diplomatic sources in London as saying that the latest confessions by 'Adnan Yasin, a Palestinian official accused of spying for Israel, reveal that he foiled a Palestinian attempt to assassinate former Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Shamir two years ago. The sources said Yasin told the Mosad about an Israeli man recruited by the PLO agencies to assassinate Shamir after arranging a secret visit to Tunis for the Israeli, who was arrested immediately after he returned to Tel Aviv.
