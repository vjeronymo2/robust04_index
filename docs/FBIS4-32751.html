BFN [Text] Budapest, 28 June (MTI) -- At the founding session of Parliament this afternoon, Prime Minister Peter Boross formally announced the expiration of his cabinet's mandate. The erstwhile cabinet will continue on for a short time as a caretaker government with limited powers. After this procedure, President Arpad Goncz commissioned Hungarian Socialist Party Chairman Gyula Horn to form a new government. Horn formally accepted the commission, and announced that he would present the program of the new government to Parliament within a short time.
