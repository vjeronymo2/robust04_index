941223 FT 23 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Flying Flowers Flying Flowers, the Jersey-based flowers-by-post concern, has forecast an increase in 1994 profits from Pounds 1.1m to more than Pounds 1.75m - an increase of at least 59 per cent. The company, which saw its shares edge ahead 1p to 81p yesterday, said that Christmas trading had now been completed and was 'well in excess of 1993'. This had contributed to an 'excellent year' for the business. The full year results will be announced in mid-February.
