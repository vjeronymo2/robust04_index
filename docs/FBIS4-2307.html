BFN [Text] Beijing, 7 Apr (XINHUA) -- Invited by President Karimov of the Republic of Uzbekistan, President Niyazov of Turkmenistan, President Akayev of the Republic of Kyrgyzstan, President Nazarbayev of the Republic of Kazakhstan, and Premier Puntsagiyn Jasray of the Government of the State of Mongolia, Premier Li Peng will pay official visits to the abovementioned five nations from 18 to 30 April. The visits were announced by Wu Jianmin, spokesman of the Chinese Foreign Ministry, at a press conference here today.
