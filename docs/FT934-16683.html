931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Securicor Pounds 4m acquisition Securicor Omega Express, part of the Securicor Group, has acquired Scottish Express, the freight transporter, from BAA for Pounds 4m. The alliance between Securicor Omega, the overnight small parcels carrier and Express, will enable both companies to strengthen their services, Securicor said.
