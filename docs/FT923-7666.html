920815 FT 15 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Graseby disposes of circuit board arm Graseby has sold the business of Graseby Flexitech, maker of flexible printed circuit boards and assemblies on the Isle of Bute, to Flexible Technology for Pounds 650,000 cash. In 1991 Flexitech incurred a loss of Pounds 526,000 on sales of Pounds 2.66m.
