Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Aleksander Kwasniewski, leader of the Democratic Left Alliance [SLD] has given an assurance that the existence of the government coalition is not threatened at present. In a statement for the Signals of the Day, the early morning slot of the Polish Radio first program, Aleksander Kwasniewski said that there was no possibility at present to replace the coalition of the PSL [Polish Peasant Party] and the SLD with any other political alliance. Kwasniewski said that his talks yesterday with Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak focused, among other things, on making the government activities more effective. Talks on the issue are to continue today, and, according to Aleksander Kwasniewski, they are going in the right direction. In Kwasniewski's view, Minister of Finance Marek Borowski will certainly not resign.
