July 12, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

COUNTY MUSEUM SCREENING;

'THE LEMON DROP KID' IN COUNTY MUSEUM SCREENING

"The Lemon Drop Kid" with Bob Hope and Marilyn Maxwell will screen today at the County Museum of Art as part of this month's Wednesday matinee film program.

Also scheduled at the Leo S. Bing Theater -- "The Fuller Brush Girl," with Lucille Ball and Eddie Albert, next Wednesday and "The Road to Bali" with Bing Crosby and Bob Hope on July 26.
