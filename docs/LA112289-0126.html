November 22, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: SANTA ANA;

DAY-CARE CENTER ADDING 30 SPOTS

A day-care center run by the Assistance League of Santa Ana has been expanded from 70 to 100 children, according to the center.

The center, at 1002 W. 2nd St., is open from 6:30 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. It accepts children from 2 to 5 years old and provides breakfast and snacks. Tuition is on a sliding scale up to $80 per week. The center accepts children who are not toilet-trained.
