COMMISSION OF FINE ARTS Notice of Meeting The Commission of Fine Arts' meeting scheduled for 16 June 1994 has been cancelled. The next meeting is scheduled for 28 July 1994 at 10 a.m. in the Commission's offices in the Pension Building, suite 312, Judiciary Square, 441 F Street NW, Washington, DC 20001 to discuss various projects affecting the appearance of Washington, DC, including buildings, memorials, parks, etc.; also matters of design referred by other agencies of the government. Inquiries regarding the agenda and requests to submit written or oral statements should be addressed to Charles H. Atherton, Secretary, Commission of Fine Arts, at the above address or call the above number. Dated in Washington, DC 24 May 1994. Charles H. Atherton, Secretary.
