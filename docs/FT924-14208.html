921010 FT 10 OCT 92 / International Company News: Nordic Investment Bank - Correction PROFITS at the Nordic Investment Bank rose 26 per cent to SDR32.0m (Dollars 48m) in the first eight months of the year. This was incorrectly reported in yesterday's Financial Times. The latest figure compares with an SDR25.4m profit in the comparable 1991 period. The Helsinki-based bank, which is owned by all five Nordic countries, charged SDR1m in the latest eight months in respect of anticipated loan losses.
