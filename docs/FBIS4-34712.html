BFN [From the "Union Radio News" newscast] [Text] Riots in Carabobo State left one person dead, hundreds injured, three police agents wounded, two trucks burned, and 500 people arrested. [Begin reporter Xiomara Gallardo recording] The riots started at the end of a march held as part of a day of civic demonstrations, which took place today to protest price speculation and increases in public services rates. The National Guard and the Carabobo State Police intervened in the riots and militarized the city's main streets. Police Chief Osvaldo Barreto said the situation is already under control. [end recording] Meanwhile, there was a new wave of protests at the Central University of Venezuela this afternoon. [Begin reporter Florisol Suarez recording] Masked university students again clashed with the Metropolitan Police after a truck belonging to the Caracas Electrical Company was burned. There was even a shootout between police agents and masked students; no one was injured. The Rinoceronte, an armored vehicle, arrived at the scene of the shootout. [end recording]
