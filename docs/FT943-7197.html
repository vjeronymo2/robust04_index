940824 FT 24 AUG 94 / World News in Brief: Benetton racing team signs deal with Renault Benetton Formula, the leading grand prix racing team this year, is ending its eight-year partnership with Ford, its engine supplier, in favour of forming a new alliance with Renault. The French carmaker announced yesterday that it had reached agreement to supply Formula One engines to the Benetton team for three years starting in 1995.
