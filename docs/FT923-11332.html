920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Drayton Far Eastern assets drop The net asset value of Drayton Far Eastern Trust was 90.7p at June 30, a decline of 6 per cent on the 96.3p of a year earlier. Net revenue for the six months fell from Pounds 607,000 to Pounds 525,000 for earnings of 0.454p (0.525p) per share. The interim dividend is held at 0.125p.
