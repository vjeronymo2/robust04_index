Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] Field Marshal Sir Richard Vincent, chairman of the NATO Military Committee, arrived in Riga today. At a meeting with Dainis Turlais, commander of the Latvian defense forces, and other government representatives, Vincent discussed Latvia's participation in the NATO Partnership for Peace program and the course of the establishment of a Baltic peacekeeping battalion. The Latvian representatives familiarized the guest with the structure of the Latvian defense forces and problems of legislation related to the defense forces. This is the first visit to Latvia by such a high-ranking NATO official.
