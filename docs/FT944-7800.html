941123 FT 23 NOV 94 / Letters to the Editor (1): Well paid, well equipped and subject to market forces Sir, British Gas boss Cedric Brown must be understandably flattered that his shareholders deem him worthy of nearly Pounds 500,000 a year ('Air of mystery surrounds utility pay packages', November 22). One wonders how they would have valued him had he an understanding of the importance of public relations. Gerry Hinde, 2 Ward Road, Southsea, Hants PO4 9PA
