930202 FT 02 FEB 93 / World Stock Markets: Argentina BUENOS AIRES ran into profit-taking following Friday's surge on government measures to boost liquidity, cut banking costs and reduce interest rates. The weighted Merval index of 19 most traded shares fell 18.20, or 4.25 per cent, to 410.04, after Friday's 9.3 per cent gain and a fall of about 10 per cent in the previous fortnight.
