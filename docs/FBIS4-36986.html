BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received Lebanese President Ilyas al-Hirawi at al-Rawdah Palace at 1130 today. Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam attended the meeting, which was followed by a closed meeting between the two presidents. Later, Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi, and Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' joined the meeting. Talks dealt with several issues pertaining to developments in the region and the Arab arena. They also reviewed the peace process and the stage it has reached. The two sides stressed the importance of continuing cooperation and coordination and working to achieve a just and comprehensive peace in accordance with the agreed upon bases of the peace process and the resolutions of the international legitimacy. They also discussed matters of interest to Syria and Lebanon. The meetings were interrupted by a working luncheon banquet attended by 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam, Mahmud al-Zu'bi, and Faruq al- Shar', along with the two presidents.
