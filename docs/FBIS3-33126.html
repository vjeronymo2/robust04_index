Article Type:CSO [Editorial Report] Managua LA PRENSA in Spanish of 1 February 1994 on page 4 reports that El Rama farmers are seeking investors for ginger and sweet potato. The Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock (MAG) is negotiating a $50,000 investment from the Italian Government to support the cultivation and export of the two products, according to the article. Patrick Bolanos, president of the Nontraditional Products Association, says that Greek investors are planning to purchase ginger and sweet potato at the port of La Esperanza on the Atlantic Coast. The article reports that over 1,600 manzanas of ginger, sweet potato, yuca, and other nontraditional products will be planted this year with international financial assistance.
