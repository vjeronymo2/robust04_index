920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / Survey of Software at Work (13): CHP Consulting - Supplier Profile CHP Consulting is a UK company formed in May 1990 by three leasing computing specialists from Andersen Consulting. Financial details: turnover: Pounds 410,000 in first year, 1990-91;60 per cent growth expected for 1991-92. Pre-tax profit: Pounds 255,000 in 1990-91. Staff: ten, including the three founder directors, plus two part-time support staff. Services: computing consultancy and bespoke software development in the leasing and asset finance industry, mainly on IBM AS/400 and System 38 computers.
