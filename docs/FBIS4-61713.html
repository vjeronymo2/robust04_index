BFN [Editorial Report] Moscow Mayak Radio Network in Russian at 1315 GMT on 7 May carries a report on observer Viktor Levin asking Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev how the Germans are fulfilling their obligations under the agreement for the withdrawal of the Western Group of Forces from Germany. Grachev replies: "The German side is fulfilling its obligations with regard to providing back-up for the withdrawal of our troops very well. As well as the 7.8 billion DM [German marks], we have also received from the German side a further 55 million DM to provide facilities for the troops being withdrawn. There are no obstacles to the sea or rail transport routes. "We are also fulfilling our obligations. For instance, we have already withdrawn all our munitions from the territory of Germany."
