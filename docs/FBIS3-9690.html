Language: Slovene Article Type:BFN [Text] The head of the club of deputies of the Liberal Democracy Party [LDS], Jozef Skoljc, says that the proposal to impeach minister Jansa was not a proposal to alter the political balance of powers: [Begin Skoljc recording] In a way, at least in my opinion, it is very problematic if the cooperation in the coalition is made conditional on a certain minister being untouchable. This is a new position in these talks and neither the Christian Democrats nor the Social Democrats have ever championed this position so far. It is also rather nonpolitical to say that the Social Democrats equal Janez Jansa. The Social Democrats were given an opportunity to take part in the government. Now, whether they will decide for cooperation or not is another matter. However, the fact is that whenever somebody has been replaced so far, parties did not leave the government coalition because of it. Woe to those parties that are prisoners of the ambitions of their presidents! [end recording]
