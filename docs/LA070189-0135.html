July 1, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

NEWSMAKERS

Dennis Bradshaw, Michael Hajeck and Gail Itow have joined MicroNet Technology as vice presidents. Bradshaw has been appointed vice president and marketing director of the company, an Irvine-based data storage products manufacturer. Earlier, Bradshaw held positions at CMS Enhancements, AST Research and MicroSci. Hajeck was named vice president and sales director. Previously, he was with CMS Enhancements where he was involved in sales of data storage products. Itow, MicroNet's new controller and vice president of finance, joins the company from Arthur Young & Co.
