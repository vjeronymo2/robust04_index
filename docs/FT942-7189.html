940526 FT 26 MAY 94 / International Company News: Record results for Magna Magna International, Canada's biggest independent car parts maker, posted record sales and earnings for the third quarter ended April 30, reflecting strong north American car sales, improved margins, lower finance costs and greater efficiency. The European plants are breaking even and are expected to pick up with the economy. Third-quarter net profit was CDollars 72.4m or Dollars 1.18 a share, up 63 per cent from a year earlier, on sales of CDollars 1bn, up 36 per cent. The quarterly dividend is being raised to 26 cents a share from 20 cents. Nine-month net profit rose 68 per cent to CDollars 169.2m or CDollars 2.82 a share on sales up 29 per cent to CDollars 2.5bn. Capital outlay was CDollars 135m in the third quarter, including buying Austria's Zipperle and 11 per cent of Atoma in North America from Mitsubishi. Zipperle has sales of about CDollars 150m. Magna sells parts and assemblies to car and light truck makers.
