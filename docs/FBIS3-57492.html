Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] The situation in the areas of confrontation in the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] remains tense. The NKR Military Information Service has revealed that Azerbaijan's military forces renewed their attacks against the central areas in Mardakertskiy Rayon yesterday morning. But the NKR defense units obstructed the attacks and inflicted many casualties on the enemy. According to reports received by 1030 yesterday, one Azerbaijani armored vehicle was hit. Meanwhile, combat operations also took place in the northeast of the front line. Azerbaijan's military forces tried to penetrate the inner areas of Mardakertskiy and Kelbadzharskiy Rayons. The NKR forces stopped them. The clashes elsewhere on the front line took the form of positional fighting. Azerbaijan's aircraft tried to violate NKR airspace yesterday. The NKR freedom fighters hit six enemy armored vehicles in the clashes in Mardakertskiy Rayon last night and destroyed two of them. The enemy units left the bodies of their dead colleagues and fled the battlefield. The NKR Defense Headquarters has revealed that frequent clashes have recently been taking place between the Azerbaijani military forces and the Azerbaijani People's Front's armed groups.
