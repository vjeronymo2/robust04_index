920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Mezzanine Capital net assets up 3.7% Mezzanine Capital & Income Trust 2001, which specialises in acquisitions in the US, increased the net asset value of its income shares by 3.7 per cent to 110.1p over the year to March 31. A final dividend of 7.25p is proposed, to give an unchanged total of 14.5p.
