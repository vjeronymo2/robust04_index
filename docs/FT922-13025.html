920414 FT 14 APR 92 / Observer: Heads you win The outcome of the general election may not have done much for the reputation of the professional pollsters, but one of them, the high-profile Robert Worcester of Mori, can at least look forward to a consolation prize. He says he stands to win several hundred pounds on private bets - including a a bottle of champers from BP's Bob Horton - that the Tories would win a 22-seat majority. Maybe he should stick to his hunches next time . . .
