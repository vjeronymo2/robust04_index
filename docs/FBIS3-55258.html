Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Central Election Commission has announced the date of elections to the Federal Assembly in Russia's regions where they were not held or were boycotted. A representative of the Commission told INTERFAX that on March 13 elections would be held in Tatarstan (the Volga region). On the same day elections to the State Duma will be held in some of this republic's one-mandate electoral districts, including the Naberezhniye Chelny, Almetyevsk, Nizhnekamsk, and Privolzhsk districts. Pending repeat elections, candidates will be advanced and an election campaign will be held. On March 26, elections to the Federation Council will take place in the Chelyabinsk two-mandate electoral district, and on March 6 in the Yamalo-Nenets electoral district. The Central Election Commission reports that the elections will be organized by the local election commissions consisting of the same members and held in the same polling stations on the basis of the adjusted lists of electors.
