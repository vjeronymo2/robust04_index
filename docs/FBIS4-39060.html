BFN [Irina Vladykina report under the "Ornithology" rubric: "Zhirinovskiy's "Falcons" Build Their Nest in the Urals. Liberal Democratic Party of Russia's Universities in Yekaterinburg"] [Text] A reception was arranged in Yekaterinburg the other day for the opening of an LDPR [Liberal Democratic Party of Russia] headquarters in the city. According to LDPR Urals coordinator Vyacheslav Senko, the local organization numbers around 500 Zhirinovskiyites up and down the Urals. At the moment they are busy opening their own university and setting up a scientific research laboratory to study anomalous phenomena. True enough, under Mr. Zhirinovskiy's instructions, the party's membership and the presence of money in the party till cannot be divulged. But journalists have managed to find out that "himself" is planning a visit to Yekaterinburg in June. After power ends up in the hands of Vladimir Volfovich [Zhirinovskiy], they were told at the Yekaterinburg LDPR headquarters, 30 seats in the State Duma would be made available to the Urals organization. In the meantime, the local organization of the Zhirinovskiy party sets itself the task of organizing a massive admission of new members into its ranks, and to start its own "URALS" newspaper.
