940128 FT 28 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Shell buys rest of Moroccan unit Royal Dutch/Shell is buying the remaining 50 per cent of shares in Societe Shell du Maroc from the Moroccan state-owned Societe Nationale des Produits Petroliers. No financial details were given and Shell declined to comment further on the deal. Shell Maroc, created in 1974, has 25 per cent of the Moroccan oil sector.
