April 16, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

SIGNS COME DOWN

Residents of Santa Ana and other users of Santa Ana streets: Have you noticed how "clean" our city is? The day after the April 4 election, the majority of campaign signs began coming down.

I think it is just great and a sincere "Thank you" is due to those responsible for removing these eyesores. Hopefully all future candidates will take note and remove their campaign signs immediately after Election Day.

LUELLA TUCKER

Santa Ana
