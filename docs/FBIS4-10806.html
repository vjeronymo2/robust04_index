BFN [Text] Jorge Madrazo Cuellar, president of the National Human Rights Commission (CNDH), and Guatemalan Human Rights Ombudsman Jorge Mario Garcia Laguardia yesterday signed a cooperation agreement to conduct research, exchange information, and publish material which supports the work of institutions devoted to the defense, promotion, and respect of human rights in Mexico and Guatemala. The CNDH reported specific actions to be conducted by the respective ombudsmen include courses on human rights, cooperation, exchange of information and experiences with similar institutions in other countries, and research and attention to Mexican and Guatemalan immigrants' problems regarding human rights.
