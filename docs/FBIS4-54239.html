BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 26 -- A delegation of the General Political Department of the Lao People's Army (LPA) led by Sr. Lt. Gen Siphon Phalikhan, member of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party (LPRP) Central Committee, vice minister of national defense and chairman of the LPA General Political Department, left here this morning, concluding its visit to Vietnam. While here, the Lao Army delegation was received by President Le Duc Anh. It had working sessions with officers from the General Political Department of the Vietnam People's Army. The delegation also exchanged experience with some Army units in political works.
