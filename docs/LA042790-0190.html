April 27, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

TESTS RUN ON GAME SHOW HOST

Game show host Bert Convy, 50, was in fair condition and underwent tests Thursday to determine why he collapsed during a hospital visit with his mother.

"They are still running some tests to try to figure out why he passed out," said Ron Wise, spokesman at Cedars-Sinai Medical Center. "His condition is listed as fair and he's stable." On Sunday, the "Win, Lose or Draw" host was visiting his mother, Monica, who has been hospitalized for a stroke, and he passed out, Wise said. The nature of the tests wasn't disclosed.
