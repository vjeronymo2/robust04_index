BFN [Text] Tehran, June 10, IRNA -- Bosnian Minister of Foreign Affairs Irfan Ljubljankic in a speech at the Friday prayers here today thanked Iranian Muslim people for their aid to his people. "Iranian relief aid stregthened this feeling in the Bosnian people that they are not alone in their legitimate defence against aggressors," he said. Up to now, he said, over 200,000 Bosnian Muslims have been killed and that the only reason for the whole aggression and massacre of innocent people is that the Bosnian people were Muslim. He termed the defence against the Serbian aggression as "the natural right of the Bosnian people" and called for the lifting of the arms embargo on his country. Ljubljankic who arrived here today for talks with the Iranian authorities on the current Bosnian peace negotiations, said that the Bosnian Government would continue its efforts to gain the United Nations consent to the dispatch of the Islamic for [passage indistinct].
