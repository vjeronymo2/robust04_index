March 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

COMMERCE BANK LEASE VALUED AT $10 MILLION

The Bank of California has leased a 47,021-square-foot office building at 5100 S. Eastern Ave. in Commerce Business Park in City of Commerce, a master-planned development of Trammell Crow Co.

Merritt Real Estate Group, a Los Angeles-based firm, negotiated the $10-million lease.
