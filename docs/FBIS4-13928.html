BFN [Text] A NATIONAL NEWS AGENCY correspondent in al-Nabatiyah reports that the Iqlim al-Tuffah heights came under concentrated artillery fire from the occupation forces' positions at about 1930 today. The shelling, which was accompanied by heavy machine-gun fire, was directed mainly at Mlita, al-Luwayzah, and Jabal Safi. At 2000 the forests of al-Litani were raked by bursts of machine-gun fire from positions held by the Agent Lahd militia [South Lebanese Army] in 'Alman and al-Shumariyah. The occupation forces this afternoon shelled Qabrikha and Tulin with mortars and tank guns, causing heavy material damage. The Qabrikha-Majdal Silm road also came under sniper fire from the occupation forces' positions in Tallusi. Meanwhile, the Amal Movement has announced that one of its groups attacked an enemy mechanized patrol on the al-Qantarah-al-Tayyibah road at 1745 today, using rockets and automatic weapons. An enemy M-113 armored personnel carrier was destroyed and all its occupants were killed or wounded. At the same time, another group attacked an infantry patrol on the same road, skirmishing with it for 15 minutes and killing or wounding some of its members. A third group directed concentrated 82-mm mortar fire against al-Qantarah.
