941102 FT 02 NOV 94 / Construction work increases Confidence is creeping back into Britain's construction industry with orders and inquiries from customers steadily improving, a survey by the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors shows. The survey of more than 200 firms of quantity surveyors showed that workloads had risen by 1.5 per cent during the three months to the end of September compared with the previous three months.
