Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Asyut, 12 Mar (MENA)--Anba Saverios, archbishop of al-Muharraq Monastery in al-Qusiyah, has stated that the shooting at the monastery yesterday was an isolated incident intended to grab media headlines, strike at national unity, and divide the nation's two communities. In an exclusive statement to MENA, the archbishop said the incident will have no effect on the relations between Muslims and Christians but will make them more determined to adhere to national unity. On the circumstances of the incident, he said that at approximately 1800 yesterday, a citizen accompanied by his relatives arrived in a car to visit the monastery. The head of the monastery apologized to them because they could not enter the monastery during the period of fasting. He added that while the car's passengers were standing outside the monastery, unknown people opened fire at the car and its passengers. He added that 27 empty cartridges fired from an automatic weapon were found at the scene of the incident.
