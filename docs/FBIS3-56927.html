Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Aleksandr Lukashenko, head of the Belarussian parliamentary ad hoc commission on corruption in top echelons of power, called on the Supreme Soviet at its meeting behind closed doors in Minsk today to hold a secret vote of confidence in Speaker Stanislav Shushkevich, Prime Minister Vyacheslav Kebich, Prosecutor General Vasiliy Sholodonov, Interior Minister Vladimir Yegorov, and KGB Chairman Eduard Shirkovskiy. Lukashenko believes that these men are to a greater or lesser degree to blame for allowing Lithuanian authorities to kidnap and take to Vilnius Lithuanian communist leaders Mikolas Burokevicius and Juozas Jermalavicius in Minsk on 15 January.
