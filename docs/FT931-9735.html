930212 FT 12 FEB 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Cape seedless grapes at Pounds 1.50-1.60 a lb (Pounds 1.60-1.80) and seeded varieties at Pounds 1.75-1.95 a lb (Pounds 2.00-2.50) are plentiful this week reports FFVIB. Lemons at 15-25p each (15-25p) and Naval oranges at 10-30p each (10-30p), depending on size, remain superb fruit buys. Button mushrooms at 60-80p a 1/2 lb (60-80p) and breakfast mushrooms at 60-70p a 1/2 lb are this week's best veg buys. Another good buy is Savoy cabbage at 25-35p a lb (25-35p). Tomatoes at 50-65p a lb (60-70p), Round lettuce at 25-30p each (25-30p) and celery at 50-65p a head (50-65p) round off this week's best buys.
