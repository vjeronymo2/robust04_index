920925 FT 25 SEP 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables French William pears at 39-45p a lb (35-45p) are superb this week, along with English Worcester apples at 29-35p a lb (25-35p). Outspan oranges at 10-25p each (10-25p) and New Zealand kiwifruit at 16-20p each (16-20p) remain good fruit buys. Onions are abundant at 15-25p a lb (15-25p) and red salad onions are 35-45p a lb (35-50p). Green vegtables continue to be stable with cauliflower at 45-55p each (35-55p) and Savoy cabbage at 20-25p each (20-25p). Chinese leaves at 50-60p a lb (50-65p) are the best salad buy. Tomatoes at 45-55p a lb (40-45p) and Round lettuce at 20-25p each (20-25p) conclude the weeks best buys.
