Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The training year was begun at a large tank unit in Camaguey with a military review presided over by Julian Rizo, a Politburo member and president of the Camaguey Province Defense Council. Rizo earlier viewed an exhibit of the tank unit's means for defensive combat, communications, and production. The latter included the troops' food self-sufficiency supply capabilities, based on their own agricultural-livestock self-management. At the ceremony, Colonel Jose Pina Perez discussed the work to be undertaken, with a view to maintaining the unit's high combat readiness. The unit has been a National Vanguard unit for (?three) consecutive years, and is a great FAR [Revolutionary Armed Forces] (?forward) unit. Julian Rizo acknowledged the progress made by the unit's officers, noncommissioned officers, and enlisted men in achieving prestige within the Eastern Army, as well as, in this new year, the perfection of dexterity and command of the material.
