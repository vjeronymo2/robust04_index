December 16, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Coach Sam Wyche of the Cincinnati Bengals was fined by the NFL for closing his locker room to reporters after a 24-17 loss to Seattle last Sunday.

Commissioner Paul Tagliabue, in a letter to Wyche, cited Wyche's violation of league policy and his commenting that he wouldn't rule out closing the locker room in the future.

The league did not announce the amount of the fine.
