930823 FT 23 AUG 93 / Construction Contracts: Roadbuilding BARDON ROADSTONE, a subsidiary of Bardon Group, has won a range of new contracts for blacktop supply, surfacing work and roadstone aggregates in England, valued in total at over Pounds 10m. Bardon Roadstone will supply about 450,000 tonnes of coated stone and 300,000 tonnes of roadstone to these contracts. They include the A46 Leicester western bypass for the Department of Transport, on which the main contractor is a joint venture between AMEC and Alfred McAlpine, together with two drystone supply orders in northern England. The works will commence this month and continue well into 1994.
