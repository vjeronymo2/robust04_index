BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko and Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev, 26 June (TASS) -- "If I am elected president of Ukraine, I will insist on parliament immediately considering the treaty on the non-proliferation of nuclear weapons," Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk said in an interview with TASS on Sunday [26 June]. Kravchuk reported that he had already submitted his proposals on this issue to parliament. "Joining the treaty, although with certain reservations, has been the only correct step taken by Ukraine in this direction," the president stressed. He is convinced that "the world and the Ukrainian people itself will estimate this step at its true worth." Kravchuk expressed confidence that the newly-elected parliament would resume considering this document.
