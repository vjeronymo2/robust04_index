August 24, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

FOR SALE? Every day the federal government's Resolution Trust Corp. hangs onto Irvine-based Lincoln S&L, it loses money.

In the first 3 months of this year, it lost $133 million. Now that a federal court has cleared the way for government to dispose of Lincoln (D1), what is the likelihood of finding a buyer to take over the thrift? "About nil," says Brea-based S&L analyst Gerry Findley. "There is more prospect of them dismantling it and selling off pieces." The feds won't speculate. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
