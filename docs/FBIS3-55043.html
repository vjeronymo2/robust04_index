Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 4 Jan (IRAR-TASS)--Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin has signed decrees releasing Sergey Yushenkov, Mikhail Mityukov, Mikhail Poltoranin, and Yekaterina Lakhova from their duties in connection with their election as deputies of the State Duma of the Federal Assembly. The Russian Federation president's press service has reported this. By a decree of the Russian President, Valeriy Butov, the president's representative in Maritime Kray, has also been released from his post.
