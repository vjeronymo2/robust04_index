BFN [Text] His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad received Tian Zengpei, PRC vice foreign minister, and the delegation accompanying him at the al-Rawdah palace at 1300 today. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' and PRC Ambassador in Damascus Li Qingyu. Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' received PRC vice foreign Minister Tian Zengpei this morning. The meeting was attended by Ghassan 'Allush, assistant foreign minister; (Yoran Quangli) deputy director of the West Asian and North African Affairs department at the PRC Foreign Ministry; and Chinese Ambassador in Damascus Li Qingyu. The discussion dealt with the international situation, the developments in the region, and the latest developments in the peace process. They also discussed ways to enhance friendship and cooperation between Syria and the PRC. A session of talks has been held between the Syrian side under Ghassan 'Allush, assistant foreign minister, and the PRC side under Tian Zengpei, PRC vice foreign minister.
