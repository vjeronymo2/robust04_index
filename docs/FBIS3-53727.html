Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Yemeni-Saudi joint experts committee on borders held the second meeting of its seventh session in Sanaa this evening. The seventh session began yesterday evening under brother Ja'far Sa'id Ba-Salih, deputy director of the Presidential Council Office and head of our country's side, and Dr. Matlab al-Nafisah, head of the experts branch at the Saudi Council of Ministers, who heads the fraternal Saudi side on the committee. The committee resumed discussion of the viewpoints and proposals submitted by the two sides. The committee began its seventh session at 1900 yesterday evening, Monday. The Yemeni News Agency, SABA', has learned that a cordial and fraternal atmosphere permeates this session, reflecting the solid fraternal relations between the two people.
