Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] At noon today, President Glavkos Kliridhis briefed Serge Abou, the European Union [EU] observer on the Cyprus issue, on the proximity talks on the confidence-building measures [CBM's]. In a statement before the meeting, Abou mentioned that last night he had an interesting discussion with Gustave Feissel, the UN secretary general's alternate special representative in Cyprus, adding that he will meet him again before he leaves. Abou said his talks in Cyprus are mostly informative.
