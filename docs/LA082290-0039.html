August 22, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

The Huntington Beach Art Center will receive about $30,000 from the recent 4-day equestrian fund-raising event.

That brings to about $125,000 the amount raised toward a $750,000 goal needed to begin refurbishing of an 11,000-square-foot facility to house the proposed community arts center, slated to open in 1991. A free, informal open house and tours will be held at the site Thursday, Friday and Saturday. Staff members will discuss program plans and answer questions. Hours are Thursday, 4 to 7 p.m.; Friday, 4 to 8 p.m.; and Saturday, 11 a.m. to 3 p.m. The facility is at 538 Main St., Huntington Beach. Information: (714) 536-5258.
