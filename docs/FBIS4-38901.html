BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 3 Jun -- A Russian soldier from the peacekeeping forces was killed on Wednesday [1 June] evening in the town of Kurgan-Tyube (Khatlon oblast), an ITAR-TASS correspondent was told today by military sources in Dushanbe. Private Pavel Mikheyev, a professional soldier from the 201st Russian motorized infantry division, was shot by persons unknown with an automatic weapon while on his way home. He is the fifth victim among the Russian servicemen in Tajikistan this week.
