930923 FT 23 SEP 93 / Treasury lobbied over YT budget THE Department of Employment is lobbying the Treasury to keep the Pounds 1.7bn annual Youth Training budget from being transferred to the education department after objections from employers and their representative bodies. The education department wants to divert the bulk of the budget for YT - the scheme now administered by the employer-led Training and Enterprise Councils -to help fund its plans to expand college-based training.
