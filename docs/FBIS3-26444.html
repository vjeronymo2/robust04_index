Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN ["Special dispatch": "Zhu Rongji Inspects Chongqing"] [Text] Chongqing, 7 Feb (TA KUNG PAO)--Zhu Rongji, member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and vice premier of the State Council, is inspecting work in Chongqing. According to informed sources, yesterday, Zhu Rongji went to an enterprise that has suffered losses and held an informal discussion meeting there. Yesterday evening, Zhu Rongji said at a Spring Festival gathering in Chongqing City that last year Chongqing did remarkable work and he wished Chongqing even greater victory in the new year. Xie Shijie, secretary of Sichuan Provincial CPC Committee, and Xiao Yang, Sichuan governor, are accompanying Zhu Rongji on his inspection trip.
