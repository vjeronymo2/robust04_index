BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] The creation of a special defense region, the reduction of and provisions for the troops withdrawn here were the main issues discussed during a two-day visit to Kaliningrad oblast by a delegation from the Federation Council, headed by Petr Shershov, chairman of the security and defense committee. During meetings with the leaders of Russia's enclave oblast, with the command of the Baltic Fleet, and the 11th Army of the Russian border troops, the delegation discussed the possibility of Russia joining the international body regulating air traffic, which would considerably simplify the coordination with neighboring countries of military and civilian flights along designated passages. At the end of the visit the deputies' commission assisted at navy military training exercises.
