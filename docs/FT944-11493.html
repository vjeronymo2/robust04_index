941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / Finance and the Family: Directors' transactions The sale of stock by Geoffrey Brown, chief executive and major shareholder at CentreGold, an electronic games publisher and distributor, came soon after its final results. Broker Smith New Court took company executives to visit its institutional contacts, a move that generated considerable interest in CentreGold. But the resulting demand for stock could not be met in the market; thus, Brown's sale. Bluebird Toys featured a couple of weeks ago when chairman Torquil Norman sold 1.2m shares. Although he undertook not to sell any more, one of the executive directors, David Laxton, has now disposed of almost half his stake. The buying in Wickes is significant, if only because it is unusual to see quite so many directors buying all together. The share price has languished since announcement of final results in August.
