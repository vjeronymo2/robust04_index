BFN [Text] Damascus, 25 May (MENA)--Former Yemeni President 'Ali Nasir Muhammad has praised the efforts exerted by Arab leaders, primarily President Husni Mubarak, King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, and Shaykh Zayid Bin-sultan, to contain the Yemeni crisis. Speaking to a MENA correspondent in Damascus tonight, the former Yemeni president said that he and several Yemeni figures are holding consultations to stop things deteriorating in Yemen, achieve a cease-fire, reverse the south Yemeni decision to secede, and start negotiations. The new initiative, he added, also aims to lift the siege on several Yemeni cities, preserve unity, and end the bloodshed.
