BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 26 Jun (WAKH) -- Shaykh Hamdan Bin-Zayid Al Nuhayyan, minister of state for foreign affairs in the United Arab Emirates [UAE], today conferred with Dr. Yasin Sa'id Nu'man, member of the Yemeni Socialist Party's Political Bureau. Mr. Nu'man told the United Arab Emirates News Agency that the session addressed developments in Yemen and explored ways to implement UN Security Council cease-fire Resolution 924 and the possibility of a return to dialogue. Nu'man said he appreciated the UAE's attitude toward developments in Yemen. With reference to the efforts being made to end hostilities, Nu'man said this should be the target of political efforts. He called on the international community to work toward that end.
