October 23, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BAY AREA QUAKE;

BAY AREA EARTHQUAKE FATALITIES ARE LISTED

As of 4 p.m. Sunday, the following had been listed by coroners' offices in Bay Area counties as killed in the earthquake. Names were released after next of kin were notified. Thirty-eight bodies were recovered in Alameda County, 11 in San Francisco; five each in Santa Clara and Santa Cruz counties and 1 in Monterey County.
