940315 FT 15 MAR 94 / International Company News: Australian engineering group up 11% Australian National Industries, the heavy engineering business which owns Britain's Aurora group, yesterday reported an 11 per cent increase in first-half profits to ADollars 29.8m (USDollars 21.2m) after tax and extraordinary items during the six months to end-December. Sales rose from ADollars 610m to ADollars 767.3m, and operating profits for the first half stood at ADollars 46m, compared with ADollars 41.8m last time. The company predicted further profit improvements as an upturn in business in its distribution and service divisions extends to other group interests.
