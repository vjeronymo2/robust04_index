August 14, 1990, Tuesday, Southland Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

PAKISTAN;

KASHMIR BORDER BATTLE REPORTED

Pakistan said its armed forces repulsed an attack by Indian troops along the cease-fire line that divides the disputed territory of Kashmir.

India denied the claim. Pakistan's defense ministry said the attack on a remote observation post in the Himalayas occurred over the weekend, after talks ended between the two countries to ease border tensions. Officials said Indian troops used heavy artillery, anti-tank guns and mortars in an attempt to capture an outpost in the Kel sector of Pakistan-controlled Kashmir.
