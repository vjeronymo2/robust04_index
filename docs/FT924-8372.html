921111 FT 11 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Merchant Retail slides to Pounds 467,000 MERCHANT RETAIL Group returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 467,000 for the half year to September 26 on turnover of Pounds 88.8m. Although the figures compared with Pounds 1.22m and Pounds 79m respectively for the same period of the previous year directors said 'robust trading' was experienced at its discount supermarkets and department stores. Trading profits of the Normans supermarkets fell to Pounds 1.36m (Pounds 2m) but at that level were considerably ahead of those for the second half of the past year. A slight decline in trading profits at the Joplings department stores to Pounds 736,000 (Pounds 818,000) reflected tighter margins and an increase in sales on interest-free credit. The interim dividend is cut to 0.35p (1.1p), in line with the company's objective of maintaining the full year pay-out - the final dividend was omitted last time. Earnings on the enlarged capital emerged at 0.44p (0.97p).
