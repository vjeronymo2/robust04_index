931202 FT 02 DEC 93 / World Trade News: Chinese licence for Gallup The Gallup Organisation, the world's largest survey research group, announced yesterday that it had become the first western company to receive a licence to operate a market research company in China, Patrick Harverson writes from in New York. Gallup China, a joint venture between Gallup, based in New Jersey, and Carrie Enterprises, a state-owned but US-managed import-export company, will conduct market research surveys on behalf of US, European and Asian clients, including Chinese companies.
