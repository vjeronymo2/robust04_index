940708 FT 08 JUL 94 / S Korea's economy grows 8% South Korea's economy grew an estimated 8 per cent in real terms in the first six months of 1994, and full-year growth will nearly match that rate, Deputy Premier Chung Jai-suk said yesterday, Reuter reports from Seoul. The 1994 estimated growth of 7.5-8 per cent compares with a 5.6 per cent GNP rise last year. The government will now give priority to stabilising prices.
