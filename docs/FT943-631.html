940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Klearfold climbs Klearfold, the Pennsylvania-based packaging company with a London listing, reported pre-tax profits for the first half of 1994 ahead from Dollars 1.1m to Dollars 1.6m (Pounds 1.01m). Turnover was Dollars 27.9m (Dollars 21.4m). Earnings per share were 6.2 cents (4.2 cents) and an interim dividend of 1 cent is being paid this time.
