Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, February 10 (ATA) -- From the humanitarian aid delivered recently in Kosova, 174 tons of food were given to the Serbians while 64 tons to the Albanians, RILINDJA of Kosova writes on February 9. RILINDJA comments that this proportion is three to one in favour of the Serbians when the Albanian population makes up 90 percent of the whole population in Kosova. At the end of the last year, the surgery clinics received a financial aid at a level of 48 thousand USA dollars and an aid in medicaments of 7 thousand DM [German marks], but the Albanian patients mostly buy their medicaments and other necessary materials for the surgeries.
