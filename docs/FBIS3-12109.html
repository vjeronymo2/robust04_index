Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Commander in Chief Fidel Castro, president of the Councils of State and Ministers, is present at the plenum of the expanded national committee of the Cuban Workers Federation [CTC] being held in Havana. The meeting concludes the first phase of the workers parliaments process, which has been carried out successfully in all provinces. The plenum of the expanded national committee of the CTC is analyzing workers' suggestions regarding economic efficiency and the restructuring of internal finances. The whole process of the workers parliaments represents a milestone in the political life of Cuban workers and demonstrates the profoundly socialistic nature of our working class.
