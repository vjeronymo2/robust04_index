Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Official Yemeni sources said this evening that a joint Jordanian-Omani military mission will arrive in Yemen at any moment to help merge the northern and southern units in the united Yemeni Army. This joint move comes amid the continued clashes between northern and southern army units that have erupted since the beginning of this week. According to southern sources, more than 20 military personnel have been killed in these clashes.
