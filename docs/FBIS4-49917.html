BFN [By reporter He Maoji (0149 2021 4921)] [Excerpts] Kunming, 9 May (XINHUA) -- [passage omitted] The "Monument of the Red Army Crossing the River," whose title was inscribed by Comrade Chen Yun and which stands on the bank of the Jinsha Jiang at the Jiaoping ferry crossing in Luquan County, Yunnan Province, has been completed. Today, over 1,000 people of all nationalities from the two provinces of Yunnan and Sichuan gathered at a ceremony to unveil the monument. [passage omitted] The monument was built along the riverside. Its height is 18.85 meter [m] and width 24 m, and it is shaped like a pyramid. On the top-most part of the monument stand the bronze statues of Red Armymen baring the upper parts of their bodies and holding up oars. Comrades Liu Huaqing, Zhang Zhen, Xiao Ke, and Yang Chengwu wrote inscriptions for the monument.
