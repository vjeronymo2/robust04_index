BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 3 (KCNA) -- The National Committee of the Quebec Communist Party of Canada recently adopted a resolution supporting the Pyongyang declaration "Let us defend and advance the cause of socialism." The resolution denounced the U.S. imperialists and the South Korean authorities' provocative acts of constantly threatening peace on the Korean peninsula. Condemning the large-scale campaign against the DPRK over its "nuclear issue" as a move to destabilize the socialist Korea, the resolution expressed solidarity with the Korean people in their struggle for defence of socialism.
