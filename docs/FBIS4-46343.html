BFN [From the "Hookup" program] [Excerpt] A civil passenger airplane of Communist China's Southern Airlines was hijacked to Taiwan last night [6 June]. After processing by our airport police for three hours and nine minutes, the plane with 138 passengers and crew on board was allowed to leave Chiang Kai-shek [CKS] Airport and to return to the mainland via a third region. Hijacker (Chou Wen-chiang) was turned over to the Taoyuan District Procuratorate for investigation. [passage omitted]
