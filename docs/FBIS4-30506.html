BFN [Text] Japan has decided to offer Vietnam official credits to help finance the construction of a new international airport in Hanoi. The decision follows Vietnam's request for assistance made to the Japanese Government mission which visited Hanoi earlier this month to discuss details of a package of official loans. Tokyo plans to grant loans for multi-billion-dollar projects as one of [words indistinct] in developing countries to be financed by official credits in the next fiscal year, beginning next April. The project of this new airport will be built south of the present airport, about 20 km north of Hanoi. This airport is expected to be completed around the year 2000.
