Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 0900 GMT on 20 January reports that "the Lebanese border unit of the Galilee District Police last night seized 10 kg of heroin believed to be worth over $1 million. Our correspondent in the north Yo'av Aviv reports that the drugs were seized from an Israeli drug dealer who had smuggled it from Lebanon in the Metulla region." Tel Aviv DAVAR in Hebrew on 23 January carries a report by Shmu'el Tagar on page 4 which states "last Friday night 10 kg of marijuana were seized from an apartment in one of Elat's new neighborhoods." The report notes that the Elat police have detained three persons suspected of smuggling the drugs from the Sinai for distribution in Elat and the rest of Israel, adding that "this morning the three will be brought before an Elat judge for extending their remand." Jerusalem THE JERUSALEM POST in English on 27 January in a report by Raine Marcus and ITIM on page 14 reports that "a man and woman suspected of dealing in marijuana in Tel Aviv's Sheinkin area were remanded for a week by Tel Aviv Magistrates Court yesterday." The report notes that "detectives found two kilograms of marijuana in a pillowcase and an additional four grams elsewhere in the apartment, but both suspects deny any connection to the drugs." THE JERUSALEM POST adds that "after a period of drought Tel Aviv is now flooded with marijuana, according to a senior narcotics squad officer. In the last two months alone detectives in the greater Tel Aviv area have seized 30 kilograms of marijuana, apparently grown in Sinai, said the officer." Later on in the report, the paper writes: "In Petah Tiqva yesterday, police acting on a tip off found 12 kilograms of marijuana in an abandoned building. Nobody has come forward to claim the drugs and police are looking for the owner." "In Haifa yesterday, Egged member Aharon Zalmanovitz, 35, of Nahariyya, was remanded until February 6 by the Haifa Magistrates Court on suspicion of smuggling and dealing heroin. Police found 1.3 kilograms of heroin in Zalmanovitz's apartment."
