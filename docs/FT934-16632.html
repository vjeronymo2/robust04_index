931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Landed demand was very strong throughout, reports the Tea Broker's Association. North Indian teas met keen competition with coloury mediums often 4 to 8p dearer and selected quality types again a good feature. East Africans were very well supported with brighter liquoring sorts 10 to 15p higher, sometimes more, mediums 5 to 10p up. Ceylons sold readily, advancing 10 to 15p. Offshore continuing strong enquiry with prices 5 to 15p up. Quotations quality 200p/kg, good medium 135p/kg, medium 125p/kg, low medium 100p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 255p/kp for an assam pd.
