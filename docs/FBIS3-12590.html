Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The District Labor Court in Tel Aviv has ordered the Bezeq Company employees to return to normal work in another four hours and until 1300 on Sunday. The court will then reconvene to consider the company management's demand to issue restraint orders against the employees. Our correspondent Meron Tzur reports that the judge warned the employees that if they do not obey the order, they risk being held in contempt of court.
