September 25, 1989, Monday, San Diego County Edition

BURGLARY SUSPECT TAKEN AT COUNTY COMPLEX

San Diego police surrounded county office buildings at Ruffin Road and Clairemont Mesa Boulevard Sunday after an automatic burglar alarm sounded at 7:47 a.m.

After four hours, a police dog was sent in to the county complex and found an intruder, identified by police as Damon B. Miller.

Miller suffered a minor wound from a dog bite.

He was arrested and jailed on suspicion of 30 counts of commercial burglary. Police said no further details were available Sunday.
