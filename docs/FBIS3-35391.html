Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Security, living conditions, and the issue of displaced persons were discussed yesterday at the cabinet's regular session and during a meeting between Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri and Minister Walid Junblatt. [passage omitted] An official Lebanese source said the cabinet approved Defense Minister Muhsin Dallul's request to purchase 175 M-113 U.S. personnel carriers for 60 billion Lebanese pounds. The source added that the used vehicles will be shipped from the U.S. base in Wiesbaden, Germany. [Beirut Voice of Lebanon in Arabic at 0615 GMT on 24 February adds: "The cabinet was informed that a Fatah splinter group led by Munir al-Maqdah was behind the firing of a Grad rocket at Israel."]
