940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / World News in Brief: First woman general in German history Colonel Verena von Weymarn of the German Air Force, will on April 1 become the first female general in German history. She has been appointed by defence minister Volker Ruhe. Meanwhile it was announced that Russia is to take part in large-scale naval manoeuvres with Germany, Britain, the Netherlands, Norway, Russia and the US off the Norwegian coast from March 21.
