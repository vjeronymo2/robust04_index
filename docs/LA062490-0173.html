June 24, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CALIFORNIA IN BRIEF;

SANTA CLARA;

GIANTS SHOULD PAY RENT, OFFICIAL SAYS

The San Francisco Giants will have to pay rent to the city of Santa Clara if they build a stadium on city land, City Manager Jennifer Sparacino said.

The National League team wants to use 120 acres of municipal property -- rent free -- to build a 45,000-seat stadium. Sparacino has proposed a $5.4-million annual rent. Sparacino gave the Santa Clara City Council a list of 13 conditions, including one that the team and stadium develop and pay about $60 million in costs to prepare the stadium site.
