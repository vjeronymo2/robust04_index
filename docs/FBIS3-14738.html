Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] The Azerbaijan Ministry of Foreign Affairs Press Service has announced that Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov has arrived in Germany on an official visit. Gasanov was invited to visit that country by Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel. In a statement to Radio Liberty, the German ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary in Azerbaijan said that Gasanov and Kinkel will hold talks on the mutual cooperation between Germany and Azerbaijan in various fields. They will also discuss the situation in the region, how to end the dispute between Azerbaijan and Armenia, and [words indistinct]. The ambassador said that during their talks with Gasanov, the German officials will maintain the view that the dispute between Azerbaijan and Armenia should be resolved within the CSCE framework. He also stressed that the two foreign ministers are expected to sign an agreement on the safeguarding of Germany's interests in Azerbaijan and Azerbaijan's interests in Germany. In general, the prospects of mutual cooperation between Germany and Azerbaijan in various fields have inspired hope for the future.
