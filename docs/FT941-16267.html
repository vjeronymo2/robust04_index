940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Barrow Hepburn at Pounds 7.3m Barrow Hepburn, a wholly-owned subsidiary of BTP, the specialist chemical company, returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 7.32m for the half year ended September 30. That compared with Pounds 4.52m previously and was struck from a turnover of Pounds 90.56m (Pounds 64.6m). Acquisitions added Pounds 10m to turnover and Pounds 1.53m to operating profits. Earnings amounted to 11.88p (7.37p). The results were included in BTP's results for the half year to September 30.
