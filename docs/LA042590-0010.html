April 25, 1990, Wednesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: OXNARD;

PROGRAM TO PLANT TREES GETS DONATIONS

A new tree-planting program in Oxnard has received 12 donations so far, including $1,000 from GE Plastics, for the purchase and planting of trees in the city's 34 neighborhood parks.

The goal of the Oxnard Parks and Recreation Commission is to receive donations for 500 trees by June.

"People have a choice of indicating which park they want their tree to be planted in . . . and what kind of tree," said Michele Izay, acting recreation superintendent. Organizations and individuals may choose to plant palm trees or one of several types of flowering trees, other deciduous trees and evergreens.

Donors may also choose one of three sizes of trees as well, at a cost of $15, $40 or $150. KIM KASH
