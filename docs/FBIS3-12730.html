Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres says the Labor Party is seeking to expand the coalition to increase the government's endurance capacity and its ability to conduct the peace negotiations. Minister Peres says that before his party can continue the negotiations with opposition parties to bring them into the government, it must discuss the matter first with its partners. Labor Party leaders will meet this afternoon with Meretz ministers and will ask them to remove their opposition to Tzomet's inclusion in the coalition.
