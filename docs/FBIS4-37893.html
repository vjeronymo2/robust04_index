The mojahedin were able by the conquest of Chman-e Hozori, Kasa Burj hill, and security posts around Bala Hesar and finally of the Bala Hesar fort, yesterday afternoon, the Golboddin-Dostam militia suffered a heavy defeat [sentence as heard]. Bala Hesar fort was the Golboddin-Dostam militia's very strong position. From the heights of this fort, militia fired several times into the residential areas of Kabul, foreign embassies, and installations of public utility. As a result of the complete conquest of the Bala Hesar fort, militia have suffered heavy losses. Corpses of the Dostam-Golboddin militia litter the fort's surroundings. The forces of mojahedin and the Islamic army were able to inflict losses on the militia as a result of heavy weapons fire. In the course of the attack much damage was inflicted on the militia. The precise number will be announced later. According to a report by Bakhtar Information Agency correspondents, mojahedin of the Harakat-e Eslami organisation have captured large areas, including Darolaman Palace, Doghabad, Pilawari, and a section of Khairabad hills, in the west of Kabul, from the Golboddin militia. And in the direction of Bala Hesar's southwest, the Islamic army forces captured the strategic hill of Kasa Burj and many fortified positions of the Golboddin-Dostam militia in Maiwand Wat and in Shor Bazar. The received reports say that in the course of those heavy battles dozens of the militia were either killed or captured. The captured people include two prominent militia generals who joined the mojahedin.
