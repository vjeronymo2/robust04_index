April 18, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE: L.A. COUNTY

Kurt Olson has joined the advertising agency Hill, Holliday, Connors, Cosmopulos/Los Angeles as senior vice president and media director. Olson comes to the agency from Mojo/U.S.A., the San Francisco agency that had previously operated as Allen & Doward, where he had been senior vice president and media director-U.S.
