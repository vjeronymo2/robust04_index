Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The discussions of the PLO Executive Committee have resumed today at the PLO headquarters in Tunis in anticipation of a firm resolution from the UN Security Council following the massacre at the Ibrahimi mosque in Hebron. Within this context the PLO leaders are expecting the UN Security Council to issue a resolution that will guarantee the effective protection of the Palestinians in the occupied territories before the final answer is announced to President Bill Clinton's request that the negotiations should be resumed in Washington.
