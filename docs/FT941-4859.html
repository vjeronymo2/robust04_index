940309 FT 09 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Clyde buys oil field stake for Pounds 12.7m Clyde Petroleum, the exploration and production company, has agreed to buy Agip UK's 17.88 per cent interest in UKCS Block 16/27a (south) in the Andrew Field for Pounds 12.7m. The consideration will be funded from Clyde's own resources and includes a reimbursement of Pounds 9m for Agip's past costs, which will qualify for tax relief. Clyde's Pounds 30m share of development costs will be funded by a fully non-recourse financing underwritten by Chase Manhattan Bank.
