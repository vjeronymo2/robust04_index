BFN [Dahaf Institute Poll Conducted by Dr. Mina Tzemah] [Text] The following question was put to a representative sample of Likud members: Which of the following people do you consider best suited to be the Likud's candidate for prime minister in the next elections (answers given in percentage points)? Binyamin Netanyahu--48; Binyamin Begin--17; Ari'el Sharon--14; David Levi--9; Dan Meridor--8; none of the above--3; no reply--1. The poll was held on Tuesday and Wednesday among a representative sample of 503 Likud members. The maximum margin of error is 4 percent.
