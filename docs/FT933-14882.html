930712 FT 12 JUL 93 / Observer: Bargain issue Somewhat misunderstanding the recent reports that people wanting to adopt children may be charged Pounds 1,500 a time, a reader has called offering his five sons for instant sale. Although they're a snip at the official going-rate, he says, anyone preferring a bulk deal could have them as a job lot for Pounds 7,000. What's more, the youngest is only 27.
