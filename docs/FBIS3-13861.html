Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Tunis, 21 Mar (MENA)--Dennis Ross, U.S. coordinator on the peace process in the Middle East, this evening met with the Israeli delegation currently visiting Tunis to discuss ways of resuming the negotiations between the PLO and Israel and implement the Declaration of Principles agreement. Ross briefed the Israeli delegation on the outcome of his talks with Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat today on the possibility of resuming the talks as quickly as possible. The Israeli delegation briefed Ross on the results of its meeting with the Palestinian party yesterday evening, the Palestinian stance on the Israeli proposals to resume these negotiations, and the conditions the PLO is making for a return to the negotiating table. The Israeli delegation, which includes Uri Savir, foreign ministry director general, and General Amnon Shahaq, deputy chief of staff and head of the Israeli delegation to the Taba talks, arrived in Tunis yesterday.
