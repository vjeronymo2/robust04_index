BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 25 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received solidarity messages from general secretary of the Central Committee of the All-Union Bolshevik Communist Party Nina Andreyeva, secretary general of the National Direction Committee of the Sudanese Union-African Democratic Rally in Mali Mamadou El Bechir Gologo and president of the Socialist Progressive Party of Lebanon Walid Jumblatt supporting the struggle of the Korean people to defend the dignity of the nation and sovereignty of the country. The messages say the resumption of the "Team Spirit" joint military exercises and the deployment of Patriot missiles in South Korea pose a nuclear threat to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The messages urge the United States to give up at once its schemings to stifle Korea and resume dialogue with the DPRK without any condition. They express firm support and solidarity for the resolute stand of the Korean people to safeguard the sovereignty and security of the country.
