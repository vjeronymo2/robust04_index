921007 FT 07 OCT 92 / Storms kill nine in Italy The death toll in a wave of storms battering Italy rose to nine yesterday as police reported seven more people had been killed in the past 24 hours, Reuter reports from Rome. Four people died in road accidents as cloudbursts created atrocious conditions. Two others were swept into the sea and a priest was drowned when he slipped into a swollen canal in the northern city of Padova. Throughout the country police reported chaotic traffic conditions as landslides and floods played havoc with the road network.
