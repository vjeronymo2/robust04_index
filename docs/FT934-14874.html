931014 FT 14 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Reflex placing funds purchase Reflex Group, the Dublin-based computer services and software concern, is to acquire 75 per cent of CPMS Holdings, the Maidenhead-based software house, for Pounds 325,000. The acquisition will be financed by the placing of 845,036 shares - representing up to 5 per cent of Reflex's equity - at 63 1/2 p per share. The existing senior management of CPMS will remain with the company and will retain the minority 25 per cent holding. The total investment, including costs and working capital, is IPounds 500,000 (Pounds 475,000).
