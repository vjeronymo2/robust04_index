BFN [Text] Budapest, 25 May (MTI) -- The Hungarian Foreign Ministry on Wednesday [25 May] asked MTI to publish the following statement: "The Foreign Ministry of the Republic of Hungary expresses concern over the Crimean situation, which carries the danger of aggravating tension and thus may have an impact on the stability of not only Ukraine but even its neighbors and friends. "The Foreign Ministry welcomes and supports the efforts made by the sides concerned, primarily Ukraine, to settle the debate in a peaceful way, through negotiations. "Hungary hopes that all steps made in the next days will be based on respect for the European scale of values and international law."
