Language: Slovene Article Type:BFN [Text] The Foreign Ministry has, in the meantime, reported that a group of men, supporters of Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, last night gravely violated public order in Bled. The Foreign Ministry informed Yuri Stepanovich Girenko, charge d'affaires of the Russian Embassy in Ljubljana, about the event that had disturbed some of the Slovene public and advised that Vladimir Zhirinovskiy and his men leave the territory of the Republic of Slovenia as soon as possible.
