BFN [Unattributed report: "Cairo Studying Iranian Proposal To Form Committee To Develop Relations"] [Text] Cairo, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- Cairo is currently studying a proposal submitted by Tehran to form a bilateral committee at experts level to hold regular meetings in both capitals within continued mechanisms to improve relations between the two countries. Well-informed sources in Cairo have told AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT that Egypt and Iran have agreed to prepare a view of how to develop and improve bilateral relations to avert the eruption of any new disputes between them. The sources said that Egypt told Iran of the necessity to stop its support for extremist Egyptian groups and not to shelter fugitive elements, especially after Pakistan has expelled these elements from its territory and in view of their intention to reside in Iran, according to information available to Egyptian security services. The sources pointed out that Iran has expressed willingness to establish ties of security cooperation between the two countries in various security fields, including combating terrorism.
