January 1, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE STATE

A missing teen-ager who authorities had stopped looking for over the protest of his parents was in fair condition after he was found wandering and disoriented in the Cleveland National Forest. Andrew Campbell, 15, of rural Guatay had been missing six days when he was found Friday in an oak grove by a father and son who had gone for a drive in the San Diego County backcountry. Campbell was suffering from minor frostbite.
