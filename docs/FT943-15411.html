940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / UK Company News: AB Engineering Despite increased losses from its catering equipment side, Associated British Engineering raised pre-tax profits by 56 per cent, from Pounds 216,000 to Pounds 338,000, for the year to March 31. Turnover climbed 27 per cent to Pounds 52.7m. The result was struck after pension fund contributions of Pounds 200,000 (Pounds 48,000), reflecting the end of the pension holiday. Earnings per share were 0.04p (0.01p) and an unchanged single dividend of 0.05p is declared. The company said that diesel engineering and the Middle East activities had continued to make profitable progress.
