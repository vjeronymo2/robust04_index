920403 FT 03 APR 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables New season pears from Cape are now plentiful, reports the FFVIB. There is a wide variety in the shops including Cape William Bon Chretian at 45-65p a lb. Other good buys include English outdoor rhubarb at 30-35p a lb. Cape and Chilean white seedless grapes at 90p-Pounds 1.00 a lb and bananas at 48-60p a lb. Also a wide variety of apples are available including BC Red Delicious at 55-65p alb. Home grown Spring cabbage is superb this week at 25-35p a lb, along with Spania broccoli at 50-55p 8oz pre-pack. English cucumbers are abundant at 50-70p each along with English and Dutch tomatoes at 80-90p a lb.
