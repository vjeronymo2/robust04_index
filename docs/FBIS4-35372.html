BFN [Text] In Amman, Ibrahim Ghawshah, official spokesman for the Hamas Movement, has stated that the Movement intends to establish a political party in the occupied territories that comprises all the opposition forces in the territories. Ghawshah explained that the party will deal with political tasks that would be agreed upon and that will be in line with the changes and current facts on the ground in the Palestinian arena, particularly following the implementation of autonomy in Gaza and Jericho. Ghawshah added that this issue is being discussed within Hamas, which holds consultations with its allies within the Alliance of Palestinian Forces, or the 10 Palestinian opposition factions which have their headquarters in Damascus.
