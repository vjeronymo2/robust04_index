March 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

'DECLARATION' CAUSES OUTPOURING OF DEEPLY DIVIDED OPINIONS

I am appalled at what your paper thinks is news. The Times once again has shown its views as being non-traditional, liberal, immoral and biased.

The article contains the story of a woman who was supposedly "born again," and after feeling sexual desires for another woman, rejects her faith and follows after her own lusts by giving into the lie that homosexuality is an acceptable alternative lifestyle.

STEVEN J. NOSKO

Costa Mesa
