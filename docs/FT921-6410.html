920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / International Company News: Shearson in Dollars 300m lawsuit THE Official Creditors Committee of bankrupt First Capital Holdings said it had filed a Dollars 300m lawsuit on First Capital's behalf against Shearson Lehman Brothers, the US securities house, and others alleging breaches of fiduciary duty, gross mismanagement and manipulation, Reuter reports from Los Angeles. Shearson, a unit of American Express, is First Capital's controlling shareholder. The suit also charges Shearson with liability for the fraudulent transfer of hundreds of millions of dollars of cash and assets. Also named in the suit are Mr Robert Weingarten, former chief executive of First Capital, Mr Peter Cohen, former chief executive of Shearson, and others. Shearson Lehman said it believed that the lawsuit was 'without merit'.
