BFN [Text] The following appointments have been made in the high command of the People's National Army by Liamine Zeroual, head of state and minister of national defense, according to a statement released by the presidency today, Thursday: - Major General Ahmed Gaid Salah -- commander of Land Forces; - Brigadier General Mohamed Benslimane -- commander of the Air Force; - Brigadier General Said Bey -- commander of the 1st Military District; - Brigadier General Mohamed Bekkouche -- commander of the 2d Military District; - Brigadier General Hocine Benhadid -- commander of the 3d Military District; - Brigadier General Fodil Saidi -- commander of the 4th Military District; - Brigadier General Rabah Boughaba -- commander of the 5th Military District. The statement adds that the Major Generals Khelifa Rahim, Mohamed Mokhtar Boutammine, Ahmed Djenouhat, and Abdelhamid Djaouadi have all been appointed to other duties.
