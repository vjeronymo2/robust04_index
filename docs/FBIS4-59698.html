BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] In a telephone call to Qol Yisra'el's Arabic-language Department, the Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] 'Izz-al-Din al-Qassam terrorist squad claimed responsibility for this morning's attack near Hebron. The callers gave details about the way the attack was perpetrated. Army forces, headed by Major General Sha'ul Mufaz, IDF [Israel Defense Forces] commander in Judaea and Samaria, were rushed to the scene of the attack. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the vehicle in which the terrorists were traveling was found totally burned a short time ago in Hebron. Deputy Defense Minister Mordekhay Gur said that extremists in the Palestinian camp want to torpedo the peace process but will have no choice but to enter into negotiations with us. Gur sent condolences to the families of the dead and wished the wounded woman a speedy recovery. The deputy minister is touring Israeli settlements in the Jericho area today. He passed through Jericho but did not stop there. Reactions in the Knesset to the attack: The National Religious Party said this is merely the opening shot in a joint offensive by the PLO and the other terrorist organizations. Tzomet said it is additional proof that the actions of the Palestinians will force us into a bloody war. Our correspondent Hayim Hecht reports that Moledet said today's deaths are more on the list of Yitzhaq Rabin's war casualties.
