December 14, 1989, Thursday, Southland Edition

NO BONUS, STRIKE HALTS VENEZUELA'S MAIN PORT

Workers have started a strike at Venezuela's main port of La Guaira, 16 miles north of the capital of Caracas, a union spokesman said Wednesday.

"We have paralyzed activity until we get our end-of-year bonus and vacation pay," said Leonardo Marcano of the Workers Trade Union, which represents the 2,853 workers who began their strike Tuesday. Negotiations on the pay demands were proceeding, a spokeswoman for the port authority said.
