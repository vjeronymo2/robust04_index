BFN [Text] Yesterday Radio Moldova, quoting sources in Tiraspol, informed its listeners about the presence in Moscow of General Aleksandr Lebed, the commander of the 14th Army of the Russian Federation. General Lebed had held meetings with President Boris Yeltsin and Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev. The same sources said that during the meeting, they approached issues related to the future situation of the 14th Army, and especially, the partial transportation to Russia of its military patrimony and the turning of the 14th Army into a Russian military base on the territory of the Republic of Moldova, BASAPRESS news agency informs. According to the same news agency, the Tiraspol command of the 14th Army was attributed the status of military police. At the same time the number of the personnel of the former command was increased by placing under its subordination the battalion of military police set up by former employees of the Tiraspol [Dniester Republic] juridical bodies. Its military command was taken out of the jurisdiction the command of the 14th Army and transferred to that of Russia's Interior Ministry.
