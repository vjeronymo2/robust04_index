940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / London Stock Exchange: Trafalgar hit Turnover in conglomerate Trafalgar House rose to 17.9m, making it the day's most heavily traded stock, and the shares fell 9 to 86p, after analysts rushed to downgrade full year profit expectations following the group's interim figures. Profits of Pounds 13.4m fell below market expectations and the group also cut the dividend. The list of brokers downgrading included BZW which reduced its full year forecast by Pounds 31m to Pounds 54m. Mr Mark Cusack at the securities house said: 'Investors that gave the company the benefit of the doubt have been badly let down. The potential is still there but recovery now looks another year out.'
