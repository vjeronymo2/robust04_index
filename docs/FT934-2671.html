931213 FT 13 DEC 93 / Youth Training shows poor results A quarter of young people leaving the government's Youth Training scheme become unemployed, while only a third gain qualifications, says a report published yesterday by the independent Unemployment Unit and the charity Youthaid. As many as 52 per cent of all young people on the scheme leave early. The highest unemployment levels of YT leavers are London - 29 per cent - and northern England - 30 per cent.
