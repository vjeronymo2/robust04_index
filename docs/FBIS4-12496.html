BFN [Text] Tehran, 9 April, IRNA -- The Iranian Embassy in Paris has denied allegations that Iran ever provided any arms to the Islamic Salvation Front (FIS) of Algeria. In a statement issued on Friday, the Iranian embassy denied claims raised by the French daily `LE FIGARO' on Iran's possible involvement in arms supply to FIS. According to official reports released here an Israeli-made assault rifle has been found in the house of an Algerian national in Paris which was searched by the French police. In the past, certain segments of western mass media have tried to justify the West's support for the military-installed government in Algeria by accusing foreign countries of involvement in the Islamic movement of the Algerian people.
