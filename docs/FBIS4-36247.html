BFN [Text] Lakhdar Ibrahimi, the UN envoy to Yemen, arrived in Cairo today from Geneva after meeting with UN Secretary General Dr. Butrus Butrus-Ghali, who is currently in Geneva to discuss the Yemeni problem. Ibrahimi will also discuss the events in Yemen with Egyptian officials. He will travel to Yemen afterward, in an attempt to convince the two sides to the conflict to adhere to the Security Council resolution issued last week that called for an immediate halt to the fighting in Yemen.
