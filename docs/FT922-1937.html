920619 FT 19 JUN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Top quality English strawberries remain plentiful this week with prices at 90p-Pounds 1.20 (90p-Pounds 1.20) a lb reports the FFVIB. New Zealand kiwifruit at 20-25p each (20-25p) is a good fruit buy, along with Spania, French, and Italian peaches at 15-20p (20-30p) each. Peas are this week's best vegetable buy priced at 30-50p a lb (45-55p). Jersey Royals and English potatoes at 10-15p a lb (15-20p) continue to be excellent value. English tomatoes are superb this week at 40-65p a lb (45-60p), along with celery at 50-60p a lb (50-60p) and English Spring onions at 40-45p a bunch (35-45p).
