Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Dezful (Khuzestan) Jan. 3, IRNA -- Two Ba'thist spies and a government official of that country were executed by opposition forces in Iraq last week. An Iraqi opposition source said here Monday that the two secret agents of the Baghdad regime were captured by the people in Umm al-Shuyukh region of Qurnah near al-Basrah. The executed government official, he said, was in charge of the political bureau of the ruling Ba'th Party of Iraq in al-Basrah.
