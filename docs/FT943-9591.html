940810 FT 10 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Edinburgh Oil in the red Edinburgh Oil & Gas, the USM-quoted oil and gas exploration and development company which is one of the UK's largest onshore acreage holders, incurred pre and post-tax losses of Pounds 60,000 for the six months to June 30. Last time, there were profits of Pounds 103,000. Turnover declined from Pounds 1.17m to Pounds 886,000. The result reflected the impact of low oil prices, while production was also slightly lower. A return to profit was expected in the second half. Losses per share were 0.22p (0.6p earnings).
