Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Dec 30 (AA) -- Azerbaijani Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov said on Thursday [30 December] that no one could spoil friendship ties between Turkish Republics. Gasanov departed Turkey after completing his official contacts. Foreign Ministry officials commenting on the visit said Turkey and Azerbaijan decided that Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic's border could not be changed by force. It went on saying that the two countries decided to set up a study group to prepare a new protocol about a joint border between these two countries. Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin said Turkey will extend Azerbaijan $10 million credit within the framework of a protocol which was earlier signed between the foreign ministries of Turkey and Azerbaijan. The credit will be used in the construction of diplomatic buildings that are to be set up in Baku. President Suleyman Demirel invited Azeri President Geydar Aliyev to Turkey and Gasanov said the visit will probably take place in Jan. Gasanov said a summit meeting of Turkish Republics which will be held between Jan 21 and 22 in Baku will show the other countries that no one will be able to spoil our friendship ties.
