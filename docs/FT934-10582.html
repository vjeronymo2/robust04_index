931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / International Company News: Texaco sued by Ecuadoreans TEXACO, the US energy group, is being sued for Dollars 1bn over alleged pollution from oil production operations it ran in the Amazon Basin in Ecuador, writes Richard Waters. The action, filed in the federal court in Manhattan, has been brought on behalf of Ecuadorean indians who claim that waste from the operations polluted their environment. Texaco denied the allegations, saying that it had operated in the country in accordance with 'sound internationally accepted oil industry practices' and the laws and regulations of Ecuador.
