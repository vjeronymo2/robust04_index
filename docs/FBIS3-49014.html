Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The forint will be devalued by 1 percent as of 3 January. The decision was made by the Hungarian National Bank [MNB] within its scope of authority. The MNB gave the strengthening of the U.S. dollar on international foreign exchange markets as the reason for the decision. A communique points out that the measure is in line with the MNB's official exchange rate policy.
