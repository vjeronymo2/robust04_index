BFN [Text] The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song received a reply message from Russian Federation President Boris Nikolayevich Yeltsin for sending him a congratulatory message on Russia's national holiday. The reply message follows: Pyongyang. Your Excellency DPRK President Kim Il-song: Your Excellency respected Kim Il-song, I express thanks to you for sending congratulations and magnificent wishes on the occasion of Russia's national holiday, a day in which the declaration on the Russian Federation's national sovereignty was adopted. [Signed] Yeltsin [Dated] 16 June 1994, Moscow, Kremlin
