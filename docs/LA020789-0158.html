February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County P.M. Final

LOCAL;

MISSING SANTA ANA POLICE DOG FOUND

A missing Santa Ana police dog was found this morning about two miles from its handler's home.

CHP Officer Janice Campbell found Vasko, a 5-year-old German shepherd, about 7 a.m. in the ice plant along the northbound Garden Grove Freeway at the Valley View Street off-ramp in Westminster. The dog was unharmed.

Vasko escaped early Monday when strong winds blew open a wooden gate at the Westminster home of his handler, Officer Mike Fleet.

"He's just ecstatic, to say the least," Santa Ana Police spokeswoman Maureen Thomas said of Fleet's reaction.

Fleet's family dog, a 6-month-old female Malamute puppy named Nakita, ran away with Vasko and has not been found.
