BFN [By correspondent Yi Tong-sik from Beijing] [Text] Mrs. Mutsuko Miki, wife of former Japanese Prime Minister Miki, said that North Korean President Kim Il-song hopes that a North-South summit will be held on 15 August, the national liberation anniversary. Mrs. Miki revealed today that during a luncheon for her on 19 June, North Korean President Kim Il-song said he wants to hold a North-South summit on 15 August and wants the South side to come to the North. She noted that Kim Il-song also hoped for the achievement of North-South reunification and for the establishment of diplomatic relations with Japan at an early date, and that he expressed satisfaction with increased exchanges with Japan. Mrs. Miki visited Pyongyang together with her family for about a week starting last Tuesday at the invitation of Kim Il-song.
