Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 2 (XINHUA) -- General Zhang Wannian, chief of general staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA), met with and hosted a banquet for a foreign affairs delegation from the Ministry of the People's Armed Forces of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK) here this evening. The eight-member delegation arrived here yesterday. The guests are also scheduled to visit the cities of Hangzhou, Shanghai and Xian.
