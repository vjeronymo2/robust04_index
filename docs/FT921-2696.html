920318 FT 18 MAR 92 / EC's budget faces delay EC leaders are unlikely to finalise spending plans for the next five years when they meet in Lisbon in June, the president of the European Parliament said yesterday, Reuter reports from Lisbon. Mr Egon Klepsch said the parliament would achieve a unified position on the so-called Delors Two package of budget reforms for 1993 to 1997. 'But I am convinced it will be difficult for the council in June to have the same result,' he said.
