Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Aleksander Meksi has sent a telegram to His Excellency Andreas Papandreou, prime minister of the Republic of Greece, on the occasion of Greece's taking over the EC presidency. I have the pleasure to send you my most cordial greetings and best wishes for the success of this important mission, the telegram says. Mr. Meksi expresses his conviction and wishes that this lofty mission will serve development, welfare, and peace in the region and beyond. Prime Minister Meksi expresses his conviction that this new mission of Greece will help in developing and expanding further the friendly relations and cooperation between our two countries and in boosting general development and Albania's integration into Europe.
