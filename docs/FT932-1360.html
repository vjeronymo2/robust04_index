930624 FT 24 JUN 93 / Picasso's 'Le Repos' Picasso's 'Le Repos', a portrait of his mistress Marie Therese Walter, sold for Pounds 1.5m at Christie's in London last night. It was part of the collection of the late Illa Kodicek, which raised Pounds 3.3m. The money goes to the National Association of Boys Clubs
