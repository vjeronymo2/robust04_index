July 17, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

ACCIDENT KILLS 5 ISRAELI SOLDIERS

A stray artillery shell accidentally exploded today near a group of soldiers on a training exercise, killing five and wounding 10, the Israeli army command said.

Three of the wounded suffered serious injuries, the military said.

The accident occurred this morning while a reserve unit was conducting an exercise involving the capture of a position at a training base in southern Israel, it said. The military command released no further details.

Army officials who spoke on condition of anonymity said the accident was at a military base near Tzeelim in the southern Negev desert, 15 miles from the Egyptian border.
