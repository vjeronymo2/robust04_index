Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 1 (XINHUA) -- Visiting Secretary-General Enrique Correa Rios [spelling of name as received] of the Chilean Government, reaffirmed that his country had carried out and will adhere to the one-China policy, recognizing the government of the People's Republic of China as China's sole legal government. He made the remarks when meeting with Luo Gan, secretary-general of the Chinese State Council, here this evening. Luo appreciated the stance of the Chilean Government. Luo also admired the achievements that the Chilean people, under the leadership of President Patricio Aylwin Azocar, have made in safeguarding their national independence, developing their national economy and realizing social stability. He expressed his hope that the Chilean people will make greater progress under the leadership of President Eduardo Frei Ruiz-Tagle [spelling of name as received], who is expected to take office soon. The two officials briefed each other on the political and economic situation of their respective countries and exchanged views on international issues. They agreed that relations between China and Chile have broad prospects. After the meeting, Luo gave a dinner in honor of his Chilean counterpart. Earlier today, Liu Zhongde, Chinese minister of culture, and Li Shuzheng, head of the International Liaison Department of the Chinese Communist Party's Central Committee, met with Correa on separate occasions. Correa and his party arrived here yesterday evening as guests of the Chinese Government.
