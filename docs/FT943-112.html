940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / International Company News: Murdoch in home shopping venture Mr Rupert Murdoch's News Corporation has taken its first step into home shopping through a 50-50 joint venture with Warnaco, a US manufacturer of up-market underwear. Warnaco products will be marketed in Asia through Mr Murdoch's Star television network. Mr Murdoch said the venture would start by transmitting advertisements and short programmes in Mandarin and Hindi. 'We have a dozen channels in Asia at present, and a lot of unsold advertising time,' he said. 'We'll experiment with five-minute shows, two-minute shows and one-minute commercials.' The venture is also the first foray into home shopping by Warnaco, a fast-growing manufacturer of men's and women's underwear, shirts, sweaters and ties under such brand names as Ralph Lauren, Calvin Klein and Fruit of the Loom. Sales last year were Dollars 625m. Mr Murdoch and Mrs Linda Wachner, head of Warnaco, said they were looking at further joint ventures in Europe, Mexico and Latin America. Mrs Wachner said: 'We need to look at how successful we are in Asia before going further.'
