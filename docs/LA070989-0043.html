July 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE HISTORY

A correction of a minor error in "Batman: The Gamble," June 18:

Batman did not have his own radio show through the '40s, as the article said. Frank Buxton and Bill Owens write in their excellent reference book, "The Big Broadcast" that "Batman and Robin occasionally appeared as guests on 'Superman.' "

DAVID FOLKMAN

West Hills
