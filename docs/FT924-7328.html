921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Oglesby & Butler falls to IPounds 74,000 Oglesby & Butler Group, the Irish-based electrical and electronic power tool company, reported profits of IPounds 74,000 (Pounds 81,100) before tax for the six months to September 30. The decline from last time's IPounds 164,000 came on turnover of IPounds 1.51m (IPounds 1.82m). Earnings per share worked through at 0.61p, down from 1.33p last time.
