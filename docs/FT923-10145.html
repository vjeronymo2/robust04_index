920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / UK Company News: John I Jacobs falls Pounds 0.41m into the red John I Jacobs, the ship owner and broker, fell Pounds 408,000 into the red in the six months to June 30. There were profits of Pounds 270,000 previously. Turnover dropped to Pounds 1.13m (Pounds 2.06m) and the pre-tax result was after a provision of Pounds 300,000 (Pounds 127,000) against the group's interest in PLT, formerly Property Lending Bank. An exceptional credit of Pounds 50,000 (Pounds 75,000) represented the release of part of a provision for broking department development. Losses per share were 1.72p (0.57p earnings) but the interim dividend is maintained at 0.5p.
