Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The outskirts of Buyut al-Sayyid, al-Mansuri, and Majdal Zun came under artillery barrage form Israeli enemy positions in al- Bayyadah at dawn today. Israeli helicopters flew over these areas and raked nearby valleys and woods. At dawn today, Israeli helicopters flew over Zibqin, Kafra, Yatar, and Haris and fired flares. Israeli gunboats opened machine-gun fire at the area extending from al-Hamra' to al-Qulaylah plain and al-Mansuri today.
