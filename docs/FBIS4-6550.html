BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April [dateline as received] -- Foreign Minister of Ukraine Anatoliy Zlenko and his wife arrived in Hanoi this afternoon beginning their official visit to Vietnam as guest of Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam. They are accompanied by many senior economic officials and businessmen of Ukraine. Prominent among those present at the welcome ceremony were Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam and his wife and Deputy Foreign Minister Nguyen Dy Nien. In the evening, Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam and his wife gave a banquet in honour of the Ukrainian foreign minister and his entourage.
