Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Under the chairmanship of leader President Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], the PLO Executive Committee met yesterday, Sunday, in the Tunisian capital. The Palestinian News Agency, WAFA, reports that the PLO Executive Committee discussed the latest developments in the peace process and the obstacles placed by Israel on the path of implementing the Palestinian-Israeli Declaration of Principles agreement.
