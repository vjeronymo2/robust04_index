Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, March 2 (TURAN) -- This morning police armed with machine guns continued to control the building of the headquarter of the PFA [Popular Front of Azerbaijan]. Employees of the PFA have still not been permitted to enter the building. The order was given by Mr. Allahyarov, the Chief of Baku's Sabail District department of police. Yesterday, the police searched the building for the entire day. According to the Deputy Chairman of the PFA, Arif Aliyev, all doors and most furniture were damaged by the police. Moreover, all telephones and computers were stolen. Nakhichevan's sources told TURAN that yesterday the police suggested that the employees of the Nakhichevani branch of the PFA should leave the building. The same sources report that a special regiment of police was sent to the Ordubadskiy Region in Nakhichevan. As is known, presently the leader of the PFA, Abulfaz Elchibey, lives near there. All those facts cause further tension in Nakhichevan. Meanwhile, the opposition is still subjected to persecution in Baku. This morning, the policemen removed the employees of Nasimi District branch of the PFA from their building. At the same time, official mass media waged a smear campaign against the opposition. "The enemies of the Azerbaijani nation must be punished", reports Azeri television.
