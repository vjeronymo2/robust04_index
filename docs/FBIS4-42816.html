BFN [Text] Larnaca, June 6 (CNA)--General Nassar Zahir, Egyptian Assistant Minister of Interior, arrived here Monday [6 June] morning for a five-day visit. Zahir, who is accompanied by Brigadier Majdi Sharif, will sign tomorrow an agreement on security matters with Cypriot Minister of Justice and Public Order Alekos Evangelou. The agreement will focus in particular on cooperation in combating organised crime, drug trafficking and international terrorism. It also provides for the exchange of information on security matters, on scientific issues, on aliens and forged documents. The agreement allows for exchange training programmes between the two countries. The Egyptian official will have a meeting with Dinos Mikhalidhis, minister of interior and police chief, Andreas Potamaris, and other senior members of the Cyprus police force to discuss the implementation of the agreement. Zahir leaves on Friday.
