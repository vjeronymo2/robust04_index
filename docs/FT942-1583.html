940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / International Company News: Mycom expands in lottery group Mycom, the Malaysian gaming and property group, plans to acquire a 51 per cent stake in Richland Worldwide which has interest in a Papua New Guinea gaming company, Reuter reports from Kuala Lumpur. Mycom will buy the Virgin Islands-incorporated Richland stake for USDollars 5m in cash. Richland owns a 70 per cent interest in The Lotto, a company which operates a lottery business in Papua New Guinea.
