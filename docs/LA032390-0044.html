March 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

'Bird' Award Winners: American tenor saxophonist Stan Getz, 63, and Belgian guitarist Philip Catherine, 48, won the "Bird" jazz prize Wednesday, awarded annually to an American and a non-American at the North Sea Jazz Festival in The Hague, Netherlands.

They will be presented in July. Philadelphia-born Getz gained a wide reputation in the 1960s for his ballads and lively bossa novas. The award is named after legend Charlie (Bird) Parker. BETH KLEID
