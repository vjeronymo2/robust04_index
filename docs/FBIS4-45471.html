Involved in Cocaine Smuggling AU1406135594 Vienna DIE PRESSE in German 14 Jun 94 p 10 AU1406135594 Vienna DIE PRESSE German BFN ["(a.z."-initialed report: "NATO Officer as Wire-Puller in Cocaine Scene")] [Excerpt] [Passage omitted] Dragan B. has been exposed as a cocaine dealer. He repeatedly smuggled cocaine from Belgium and Colombia to Austria. During his last coup he was caught in Dusseldorf by the German police, which had been alerted by the Austrians. He had four kg of cocaine on him with a street value of 7.5 million schillings. One kilogram of cocaine from former deliveries was found with the total of eight persons of the inner circle of the gang, among which it had been distributed. In Belgium, the Vienna police has now established the identity of a high-ranking Belgian NATO officer who had supplied the cocaine. The Belgian authorities, however, have so far ignored the Austrian investigations: Not only has the arrest warrant not been executed, and the suspect, who has been incriminated by several witnesses, has not only not been extradited, the Belgians have not even arrested the man. "This man is somehow untouchable," sources from the Vienna police said. Eight persons have been arrested, while complaints against another 25 have been filed. [passage omitted]
