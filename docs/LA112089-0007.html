November 20, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

TEACHING EVOLUTION

(State Supt. of Public Instruction) Bill Honig is to be commended for his change of position regarding the teaching of evolution. I also agree that we should not withhold scientific facts from our children, but let's be sure we teach all sides of the issue -- those things that are proven facts along with those that remain theories. To say that evolution in total is a fact is just not a true statement.

HERBERT H. LEWIS

Ventura
