920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / International Company News: Playmates in Chinese venture PLAYMATES Holdings, the Hong Kong toy company which markets and distributes the Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtle, plans to produce and sell the toys in China, writes Simon Davies. Playmates has taken a 42 per cent stake in a joint venture with Pacific Concord, which will manufacture, distribute and retail Playmates toys over the border. The new company will be called Concord Playmates Holdings.
