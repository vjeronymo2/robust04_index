BFN [Boris Lysenko report: "Pavel Grachev Proposes Holding Reichstag Session"] [Text] Berlin -- Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev has voiced a desire to include a session in the Reichstag building as part of the ceremonies to mark the Russian troops withdrawal from Germany. We can assume that the discussion of this proposal from Pavel Grachev will not be easy. Such a request was made last year by Colonel General Matvey Burlakov, commander in chief of the Western Group of Forces, but nobody took it seriously at the time.
