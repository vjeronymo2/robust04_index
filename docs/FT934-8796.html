931113 FT 13 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Pittencrieff in reconstruction talks Pittencrieff, the oil and gas concern, is in talks with its subsidiary, Pittencrieff Communications, which may lead to a reconstruction. The board said that it had for some time been examining various ways of securing maximum value for its shareholders, and would notify shareholders as soon as possible in the event of any further developments. The shares closed 4p up at 439p yesterday.
