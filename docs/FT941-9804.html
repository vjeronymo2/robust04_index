940212 FT 12 FEB 94 / UK Company News: BTR Nylex expands wine bottle capacity BTR Nylex, the Australian offshoot of the UK-based conglomerate, is to invest ADollars 90m (Pounds 44m) in expanding its glass container manufacturing facilities in Adelaide to meet requirements of the Australian wine industry. Once complete, in early 1995, the facility will be able to produce a further 160m wine bottles a year.
