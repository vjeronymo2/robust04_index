BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 11 (KCNA) -- Yang Hyong-sop, chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly, returned home today after attending the inaugural ceremony of the South African president as a special envoy of the great leader President Kim Il-song. A delegation of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea led by him paid visits to Zambia, Cameroon and the Congo.
