BFN [Text] A regular Supreme Council session continued its work today in Almaty. The Supreme Council examined and adopted a preliminary schedule for examining issues by plenary sessions over the coming two months. It introduced changes into composition of certain committees and the Control Chamber, confirmed a regulation on paying allowances to wages of deputies, committee staff, the Control Chamber, Secretariat, and the Supreme Council's administration, depending on their length of service. Marat Kalmatayev, the chairman of the Committee for Law, Order, and Legal Reforms, spoke about the amendments to the law on administrative violations. The law was adopted as a whole. The Supreme Council confirmed Nazhamdin Eskaliyev, the ambassador of Kazakhstan to Uzbekistan, nonresidential ambassador to Tajikistan. Mukhametkasym (Shakenov) was confirmed as the Supreme Council's head of apparatus.
