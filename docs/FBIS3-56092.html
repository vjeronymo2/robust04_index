Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Speaker of the Federation Council Vladimir Shumeyko, elected to the post Thursday, has told the deputies that he intends to do his best for the Federation Council as the supreme body of state power. By Shumeyko's proposal, it was decided to make nominations for the post of the deputy speaker on Friday morning. Before that he intends to hold consultations on candidates with different deputy factions. In line with the earlier approved regulations, the speaker of the Federation Council will have three deputies. Shumeyko said that all deputies, despite their posts, will have equal rights. One member of Shumeyko's team has described as a convincing victory Shumeyko's election to the post of the speaker. No one can reproach Shumeyko for victory due to pressure groups. Ninety-eight votes were cast by convincing supporters of Vladimir Shumeyko, he said.
