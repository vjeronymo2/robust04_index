Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 22 Feb (MENA)--The Cairo newspaper AL-AKHBAR has reported that Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin will meet in Cairo within 15 days or after the 'Id al-Fitr holiday to sign the final agreement to transfer civilian authority. In its issue for tomorrow, Wednesday, the newspaper said that Dr. Nabil Sha'th, adviser to Palestinian president, will hold a news conference tomorrow at noon to discuss the results of the Cairo round of negotiations.
