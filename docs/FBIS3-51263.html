Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Finance Minister Rudolf Hommes was uninjured in a bomb attack staged by individuals in an attempt to assassinate him. By chance, General Octavio Vargas Silva's car was passing by and his driver immediately went to the aid of the finance minister and drove him away. The minister has not yet spoken to reporters, but we are told that he has just telephoned President Cesar Gaviria to inform him of the incidents that occurred this morning at 0800. [Santa Fe de Bogota Emisoras Caracol Network at 1419 GMT on 17 January adds that the finance minister was traveling eastbound in a gray BMW with license plate AH-4772. A bomb exploded as the vehicle was about to cross the bridge on 5th Avenue. The left part of the car was severely damaged. According to reports received by Caracol, the explosive device that was planted beside a utility pole contained between five and six kg of dynamite. The Santa Fe de Bogota Metropolitan Police and Administrative Department of Security experts are currently evaluating the situation and are trying to determine what type of bomb it was.]
