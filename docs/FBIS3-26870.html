Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Provincial party, government, and military leaders solemnly gathered at a Lunar New Year tea party in Chengdu's Jinliu Hotel this morning. Zhu Rongji, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau Standing Committee and vice premier of the State Council, attended the tea party and extended festival greetings to the Sichuan people. The tea party was attended by more than 430 people, including such provincial party, government, and military leaders as Xie Shijie [secretary of the provincial party committee], Xiao Yang [governor], Zhang Zhijian; Yang Xizong [chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee], Nie Ronggui [chairman of the provincial committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC)], and people of all walks of life, as well as representatives of all quarters concerned. [passage omitted] Secretary Xie Shijie presided over and delivered a speech at the tea party, saying: Today, we have gathered here happily to observe the Lunar New Year Festival with Comrade Zhu Rongji, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau Standing Committee and vice premier of the State Council. Over the past year, guided by a series of important speeches made by Comrade Deng Xiaoping during his South China tour, as well as the spirit of the 14th CPC National Congress, Sichuan Province has further expanded opening up, deepened reform, and scored gratifying achievements in economic construction and other undertakings. Today, in observing the Lunar New Year Festival, we hope to see more progress and prosperity in Sichuan in the new year 1994. Today's tea party is being attended by comrades in charge of the provincial party committee, people's congress, people's government, CPPCC committee, and discipline inspection commission; by comrades in charge of the Chengdu Military Region, Chengdu Military Region Air Force Unit, Sichuan Provincial Military District, and Sichuan Provincial Armed Police Corps; by comrades in charge of the Sichuan Provincial Higher People's Court and People's Procuratorate; by persons in charge of the provincial committees of various democratic parties and people's organizations; by comrades in charge of the Chengdu-based national news organs; by retired veteran leaders and representatives of retired veteran cadres; by representatives of advanced models of the Chengdu-based People's Liberation Army [PLA] units and armed police units; by representatives of model workers on all fronts and representatives of ethnic minorities, including the Thai Nationality, the Sang Nationality, and the Miao Nationality; and by representatives of religious circles. Governor Xiao Yang also delivered a speech at the tea party, saying: Comrades and friends, today provincial party, government, and military leaders, as well as representatives of the people of all walks of life in Sichuan, have gathered happily at this tea party to jointly observe the traditional Lunar New Year Festival. We are especially happy that Vice Premier Zhu Rongji and some state ministry and commission leaders also are present at this tea party and have joined the Sichuan people in celebrating the Lunar New Year Festival. This has demonstrated that the CPC Central Committee and the State Council have been greatly concerned with Sichuan. On behalf of the Sichuan Provincial CPC Committee and the Sichuan Provincial People's Government, I would like to take this opportunity to extend warmest New Year greetings to Vice Premier Zhu Rongji and to all the state ministry and commission leaders here present, and to wish them a happy Lunar New Year, good health, and good work. I also would like to extend Lunar New Year greetings to the Sichuan people and the Sichuan-based PLA units. In his speech, Governor Xiao Yang recalled some major steps taken and some important achievements scored by the people of all nationalities in Sichuan under the guidance of Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics and of the party's basic line over the past year, saying that the Sichuan people have successfully seized each and every excellent opportunity to deepen reform and expand opening up, pushed forward economic and social growth in an expeditious manner; and successfully built a socialist market economic structure. After expounding on a series of major tasks and goals set for the year 1994, Governor Xiao Yang concluded his speech by saying: The new year 1994 is one full of opportunities, challenges, and hopes. Let us unite more closely and join the people of all nationalities in Sichuan in carrying out down-to-earth work, bravely making explorations and forging ahead, making more progress, and striving for a brighter future under the leadership of the CPC Central Committee with Comrade Jiang Zemin as the core! Lieutenant General Zhang Zhijian, political commissar of the Chengdu Military Region, also spoke at the tea party, extending Lunar New Year greetings to the party and government leaders of Sichuan Province and the city of Chengdu, to people of all nationalities who have been diligently working on various fronts in Sichuan, to the families of glorious revolutionary martyrs, and to both soldiers and demobilized soldiers who have been transferred to civilian work. Comrade Zhu Rongji, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau Standing Committee and vice premier of the State Council, delivered a speech at the tea party. He said: Comrades, Happy Lunar New Year! I am very pleased today to attend the 1994 Sichuan Provincial Lunar New Year Tea Party. I would like to take this opportunity to extend -- on behalf of the CPC Central Committee, the State Council, and the leaders of State Council departments who have come with me to Sichuan -- festival greetings to the Sichuan Provincial CPC Committee and People's Government; to the leaders of all party, government, military, and mass organizations who are present at the tea party; to veteran comrades who have made enormous contributions in different historical periods; and to all PLA officers and soldiers, as well as the Sichuan people! I wish all of you happy Lunar New Year! I also wish all of you good health, good work, and happy family! Vice Premier Zhu Rongji went on: In 1993, guided by Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics, Sichuan forged ahead energetically and scored phenomenal achievements on all fronts. Last year, Sichuan not only witnessed fast-paced national economic growth, but also handled properly the relations between reform, development, and stability to a certain extent, thus conforming with both the work principles formulated by the CPC Central Committee and the overall national interests. The CPC Central Committee has called for seizing each and every excellent opportunity to deepen reform, expand opening up, push ahead with development, and maintain stability. Sichuan's enormous achievements have been fully affirmed by all the State Council department leaders who have come with me to Sichuan this time. After going back to Beijing, we will study conscientiously the demands you have raised this time, and will take practical steps to help you resolve problems. I believe that led by the CPC Central Committee with Comrade Jiang Zemin as the core and by the Sichuan Provincial CPC Committee and People's Government as well, the Sichuan people surely will be able to score greater achievements in the year 1994! I wish all the comrades present at this tea party good health! Thank you! A number of excellent entertainment performances were staged at the Lunar New Year tea party.
