910511 FT 11 MAY 91 / UK Company News: All-round growth for Whitbread Inv Net asset value per share at Whitbread Investment Company had risen to 654.26p by the end of March. That compared with 568.1p six months earlier, and with 583.6p at the end of March 1990. In the year ended March 31 1991, earnings per share rose from 11.69p to 14.05p, and the dividend is raised from 11.6p to 13.9p, the final being 9.95p.
