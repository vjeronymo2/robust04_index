BFN [Text] Tirana, June 22 (ATA) -- In the meeting with the French parliamentary delegation led by Jean Francois Denion [name as received], on June 21, the Albanian president, Sali Berisha, appreciated the support of France and French personalities in international institutions for the development of the democratic processes in Albania. President Berisha made a panorama of the achievements and institutional, economic, and legislative reforms. He said that there are many possibilities for cooperation in different between the two countries. Mr. Denion expressed his support for the consolidation of relations in fields of mutual interest such as tourism and trade focusing on the possible coming of French investors in Albania.
