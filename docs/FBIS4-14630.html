BFN [From 4 April HOMMIKULEHT, p 3; "News Bulletin" No. 63/94] [Text] An agreement on legal aid and legal relations between the three Baltic states came into force on Sunday, April 3. The agreement guarantees the Baltic states citizens the right to appeal to the courts, prosecutors, notaries and other legal institutions of the three member countries. The agreement also concerns legal entities. The agreement was concluded on November 11, 1992 in Tallinn.
