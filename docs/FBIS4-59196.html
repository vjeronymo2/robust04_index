BFN [Text] Terrorists fired antiaircraft missiles at Israel Air Force helicopters operating in south Lebanon yesterday and this morning. The missiles missed their targets. Our correspondent Yo'av Aviv reports this is the first time that the terrorists have fired missiles at IDF helicopters. Sources in south Lebanon reported that this morning there were fire exchanges between the terrorists and the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] and SLA [South Lebanese Army] in the al-Qantarah and Bint Jubayl areas.
