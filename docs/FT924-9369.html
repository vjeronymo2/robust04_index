921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / UK Company News: WIC net asset value falls WHITBREAD Investment Company reported an 11 per cent increase in net revenue, from Pounds 5.62m to Pounds 6.25m, for the six months ended September 30. Net asset value at the end of the period was 657.1p against 689.4p a year earlier. All the companies in which stakes are held increased their dividends to lift gross income from Pounds 9.18m to Pounds 10m. Earnings per share were 9.9p (8.91p) and the interim dividend is raised from 4.2p to 4.6p. WIC, in which Whitbread, the brewer, has a 49.5 per cent stake, raised Pounds 79.8m from selling shares in Marston and Morland, the regional brewers, and in Boddington and Devenish, the pub operators, to comply with the government's beer orders. Most of the proceeds have been reinvested in brewery and drinks stocks.
