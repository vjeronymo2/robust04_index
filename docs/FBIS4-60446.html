BFN [Text] Baghdad, 21 May (INA)--President Saddam Husayn has received congratulators on the occasion of the blessed 'Id al-Adha, may it bring welfare and blessings for his excellency, great Iraq, and the Arab and Muslim nation. Present to extend congratulations to his excellency the president on this happy occasion were 'Izzat Ibrahim, vice chairman of the Revolution Command Council [RCC]; Taha Yasin Ramadan, vice president; 'Abd-al-Majid al-Rafi'i, Badr-al-Din Muddaththir, and Ilyas Farah, members of the National Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party; Taha Muhyi-al-Din Ma'ruf, vice president; fighter Staff General 'Ali Hasan al-Majid, RCC member and defense minister; Muhammad Hamzah al-Zubaydi and Mizban Khadr Hadi, RCC members; members of the Iraq Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party; Ahmad Husayn Khudayyir, prime minister; Hatim Hamdan al-'Azzawi, chief of the Presidential Office; ministers; those who hold the rank of minister; and fighter members of the General Command of the Armed Forces. President Saddam Husayn thanked the well-wishers, wishing everyone, and through them the children of our great Iraqi people and the glorious Arab nation, continued glory and a prosperous future, God willing.
