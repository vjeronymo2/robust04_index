Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Under brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council and Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] Central Committee secretary general, the YSP Political Bureau held a periodic meeting in Aden from 6-7 January 1994. The Political Bureau reviewed the overall developments on the Yemeni political scene and the progress of the national efforts being exerted to contain the current political crisis and find objective solutions which will enhance the march of unity and democracy in the country. The Political Bureau reviewed the results of the current national dialogue among various parties and political forces and the steps that have been taken to resolve the current crisis. Greatly appreciating the spirit and results of this dialogue, the Political Bureau stressed the need to continue the dialogue with a greater vitality so it can achieve its exceptional objectives and tasks.
