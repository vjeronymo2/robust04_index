BFN [Text] Mae Hong Son -- Karenni rebel President Saw Maw Reh died of heart failure Monday at the Thai-Burmese border village of Ban Huai Surin in Khun Yuam District here, a Karenni rebel officer said yesterday. He is survived by his wife and six children. The Christian burial ceremony was held at Na On Village inside Burma near his headquarters on Wednesday. Saw Maw Reh, 74, the president of the ethnic Karenni government since 1992 until his death, formerly served in the British army until 1948. He later joined the ethnic Karenni rebel group to fight for self-rule from Burma. Saw Maw Reh began suffering heart problems last year. He died on his return from the provincial town where he had received medical treatment, said the officer.
