October 28, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

GROSSMONT AT LAST BEATS GOLDEN WEST IN VOLLEYBALL

Grossmont College defeated Golden West in a women's volleyball match Saturday after failing against the Rustlers (12-1) three times this season.

Grossmont won, 11-15, 15-7, 15-10, 10-15, 15-9 in a nonconference match at Grossmont and improved to 9-4. Brenda Irby had 18 kills for Golden West.
