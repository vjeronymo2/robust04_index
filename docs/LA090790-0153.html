September 7, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

SPOTLIGHT / COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE: FOCUS: LAKE FOREST BUSINESS CENTER

Lake Forest Business Center, at the interchange of the San Diego and Santa Ana freeways, consists of 28 one- and two-story buildings. The majority owner of the business park is Burnham USA Equities, which is selling the individual leased buildings to the investment community. The project, built in 1980, is 89% leased.
