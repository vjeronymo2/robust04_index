Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 18 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The spring session of the State Great Hural (SGH), which started its work three days ago, is sitting without participation of the representatives of the opposition. Six members of the Parliament, representing the coalition of the Mongolian National Democratic Party and the Mongolian Social Democratic Party, have stopped participating in work of the spring session of the State Great Hural. On Wednesday [16 March] they announced that they would not participate in the session till the Parliament discusses the initiatives of the president of Mongolia, which he has put forward in his speech at the opening of the SGH session, and adopts a corresponding decision on them. The State Great Hural Standing Committee on Hurals and Administration has considered as expedient to submit for consideration to the present session of the Parliament the president's proposal on elaboration of the draft law on elections to the State Great Hural, on making amendments and additions in the Law on State Great Hural in a new fashion. But the Parliament has not accepted the president's draft resolution concerning ensuring the guarantees of work on propaganda of activity of the State Great Hural through the state mass media.
