Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Krivoshey] [Text] Moscow January 17 TASS -- Russia and Estonia have agreed to postpone their talks which were due to resume outside Moscow on January 18 until a later date. A Russian Foreign Ministry official said that the talks had been postponed because of the illness of the head of the Russian delegation. The talks are expected to resume in a week's time.
