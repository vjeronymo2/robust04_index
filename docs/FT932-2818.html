930617 FT 17 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: 'Fresh start' Tories promoted THE FIRST of the Tory class of '92 to have signed the Euro-sceptic Commons motion last year calling for a 'fresh start' in European policy began their government careers yesterday on the lowest rung of the ladder, as unpaid ministerial aides, Alison Smith writes. Dr Liam Fox, MP for Woodspring, becomes parliamentary private secretary to Mr Michael Howard, home secretary, and Mr Nigel Evans, MP for Ribble Valley, becomes PPS to Mr David Hunt, employment secretary. Four others who became MPs in 1992 were among eight PPS appointments. Mrs Angela Browning goes to the employment department; Mrs Angela Knight goes to trade and industry; Mr Michael Trend joins the environment department; and Mr Rod Richards joins the Foreign Office team.
