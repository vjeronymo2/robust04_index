940615 FT 15 JUN 94 / SIB plans fresh complaints procedures New procedures for handling complaints by investors are to be introduced by the Securities and Investments Board, Mr Anthony Nelson, economic secretary to the Treasury, announced last night, Ivor Owen writes. He told the Commons the appointment of a complaints commissioner was one of the proposals under discussion. The arrangements being discussed would aim at providing improved monitoring of the individual self-regulating organisations answerable to SIB. SIB proposed to assign individual complaints officers to the self-regulating organisations to ensure continuity and clarity in the handling of complaints.
