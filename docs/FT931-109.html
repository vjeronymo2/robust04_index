930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Clifford Foods Clifford Foods, the subject of a Pounds 50m recommended offer from Unigate, returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 3.77m for 1992, a 29 per cent downturn on the previous year's Pounds 5.3m. In connection with the takeover a second interim dividend of 7.1p is declared in lieu of a final, making a same-again 11.5p total. Turnover expanded from Pounds 140.6m to Pounds 143.2m. Earnings emerged at 15.32p (21.99p).
