940706 FT 06 JUL 94 / International Company News: Hoechst to set up drugs joint venture in China Hoechst, the German chemicals group, is to set up a pharmaceuticals joint venture with the North China Pharmaceuticals Corporation, Reuter reports from Frankfurt. Hoechst said it would own 50 per cent of the venture which would have total capital of DM38m (Dollars 23.3m). NCPC is one of China's largest pharmaceuticals producers. The venture will involve distribution and sale of medicines. Hoechst said NCPC would market selected Hoechst products until the joint venture's projected drug production facilities come on line at the beginning of 1996.
