BFN [Text] Manuel Marin, vice president of the European commission for humanitarian aid, stressed in Havana the political willingness of the European Union to help Cuba, not only in the humanitarian field, but also in the economic and technical fields as well. According to an AFP dispatch, Marin met with President Fidel Castro and praised the opening made by the Cuban Government, yet, Marin specified, the European Union is waiting to see the economic reforms that will, no doubt, result from the National Assembly of the People's Government special session.
