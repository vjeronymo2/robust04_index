BFN [Text] The Foreign Ministry's press service has reported to Estonian Radio that Foreign Minister Juri Luik had a meeting today with ambassadors of the European Union [EU], at their request. The ambassadors voiced concern over some practical aspects of the implementation of the law on foreigners. At the same time, it was emphasized once again the the EU accepts the Estonian law on foreigners and its legal logic. The ambassadors who attended the meeting noted that the EU's position on troop withdrawal remains the same, which means that the Russian troops must leave Estonia unconditionally by 31 August. The EU has also notified the Russian Federation of this position. Juri Luik provided a progress report about the registration of aliens as well as about reorganization of the citizenship and immigration department.
