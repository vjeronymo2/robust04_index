September 16, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

MILLIKAN WATER POLO TOURNAMENT;

CAPISTRANO VALLEY WINS WATER POLO TITLE

Tournament most valuable player Kevin Eggert scored six goals to lead Capistrano Valley to a 13-7 victory over Miraleste in the championship match of the Millikan Tournament at Belmont Palza pool.

Eggert scored seven goals in a 14-7 semifinal victory over Dos Pueblos. He scored 27 goals in the five tournament matches for Capistrano Valley (6-0).

Capistrano Valley's Dan Hancock, who scored four goals in each of the semifinal and final games and had 21 in tournament, was also named to the all-tournament team as was goalie Nick Kittredge and Stod Buckley.

In other tournament games:

Long Beach Wilson 14, Edison 13 -- Brian Melstrom scored six goals for Edison but the Chargers (3-2) lost in the tournament's fifth-place match.
