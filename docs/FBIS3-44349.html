Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] New Delhi, December 30 (XINHUA) -- The strengthening of relations with China and the European union was regarded as successes of India's foreign policy during 1993. Speaking to reporters here today, External Affairs Minister Dinesh Singh also said that the foreign secretarial-level talks with Pakistan from January 1-3 next year would pave the way for political dialog between the foreign ministers and prime ministers of the two countries. He said that problems with Pakistan could be settled only bilaterally without foreign intervention.
