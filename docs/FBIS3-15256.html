Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Yuriy Sizov, Vladimir Taranov] [Text] Moscow March 3 TASS -- "Political, civil and national stability is the ground on which Uzbekistan is building its future," Uzbek President Islam Karimov said today at a meeting with representatives of the Russian political and business circles, held at the Uzbek embassy in Moscow. He stressed the importance of the documents, signed during his visit to Russia, and made special mention of President Yeltsin's address to the State Duma. For the first time ever "CIS member states were given an answer concerning the priorities of Russia's domestic and foreign policy. Russia is a great power. We believe in Russia, in its great future. Our belief is based on the historic roots, without which our today's friendship and cooperation would be impossible," the Uzbek president said. "Russia should be a guarantor of peace and stability in Central Asia. There will always be two poles in the world, and we in Uzbekistan are keeping closer to Russia's pole," he stressed.
