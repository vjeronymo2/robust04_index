940827 FT 27 AUG 94 / UK Company News: New London reduces losses to Dollars 3.25m New London, the oil industry services company, reported sharply reduced pre-tax losses of Dollars 3.25m (Pounds 2m) for the year to March 31, against Dollars 23.7m. The result was achieved on turnover halved at Dollars 71.9m (Dollars 135.9m), which included Dollars 46.6m from discontinued activities. Pre-interest losses were Dollars 2.96m (Dollars 20.2m) including Dollars 1.6m from discontinued activities. Net interest charges fell from Dollars 3.55m to Dollars 293,000. Losses per share were 3.3 cents (23.2 cents). Mr Paul Kesterton, chairman, said the year was affected by the disposal of International Drilling Fluids to Schlumberger which was completed on September 1. The result included a five months' contribution from IDF and was struck after a loss on disposals of Dollars 12.2m offset by a prior year provision of Dollars 12.3m. The total consideration for IDF was Dollars 11.4m plus the acceptance of Dollars 30m of bank debt. During the period New London also paid Dollars 10m and 12m shares to Electra Private Equity Partners to repay debt and deferred interest.
