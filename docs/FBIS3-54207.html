Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa returned to Cairo this afternoon following a brief working visit to Israel. Musa said he would submit a full report to President Husni Mubarak on his talks with Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin and Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres. He added that Rabin entrusted him with a message to President Mubarak in reply to one from the president.
