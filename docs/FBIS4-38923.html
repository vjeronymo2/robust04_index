CSO [AZERTADZH report: "Azerbaijani-Iranian Relations Are Strengthening"] [Text] The continued expansion of relations between Azerbaijan and the Islamic Republic of Iran is of great significance for both states, and we support the development of this cooperation. This was stated by Geydar Aliyev, president of the Azerbaijan Republic, on 25 May as he received a delegation of the neighboring country headed by Gholam Reza Aqazadeh, minister of petroleum of the Islamic Republic of Iran. The minister was accompanied on the trip by Ali Abdulalizade, governor of Northern Azerbaijan, and other officials. Gholam Reza Aqazadeh conveyed to Geydar Aliyev greetings from Hashemi-Rafsanjani, president of the Islamic Republic of Iran. The head of the Azerbaijani state asked that his respect and esteem and best wishes be conveyed to President Hashemi-Rafsanjani. Geydar Aliyev observed that a new date for his visit to the Islamic Republic of Iran, which would take place shortly, had already been determined. The signing by the two states of a number of important documents is scheduled. There was in the course of the meeting a comprehensive exchange of opinions on a consolidation of economic relations of the two countries and the commissioning of latent possibilities of their mutual relations and a discussion of topical questions of cooperation in the petroleum sphere. Fuad Guliyev, first deputy prime minister of the republic, State Counselor Vafa Gulizade, Ali Asgar Nekhavandian, ambassador of the Islamic Republic of Iran in Azerbaijan, and other officials took part in the meeting. The same day the delegation headed by Gholam Reza Aqazadeh, minister of petroleum of the Islamic Republic of Iran, was received by Rasul Guliyev, chairman of the Supreme Council of the republic. Prospects of an expansion of the two states' economic cooperation were discussed in the course of the conversation. Ali Asgar Nahavandian, ambassador of the Islamic Republic of Iran in Azerbaijan, was present at the meeting.
