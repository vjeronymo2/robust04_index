BFN [Text] Israeli warplanes attacked the Iqlim al-Tuffah region in the south at 1125 today, targeting Mlita and al-Luwayzah with two successive raids. Israeli warplanes continue to fly over the area. Israeli warplanes fired four air-to-surface missiles on Mlita Hill at 1110. The Lebanese Army opened fire on the planes. The Israeli air raids were accompanied and preceded by intensive air activity over al-Nabatiyah and Iqlim al-Tuffah. At 1105 the warplanes fired two rockets on Jabal Safi in Iqlim al-Tuffah. The air raids have caused an atmosphere of fear and panic among the citizens, who have remained in their homes. There are no reports of casualties yet. Only material damage has been done.
