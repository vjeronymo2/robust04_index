920727 FT 27 JUL 92 / Local elections in Albania Albanians went to the polls yesterday in the first local elections since the anti-communist Democrats swept to power in national elections in March, Reuter reports from Tirana. The mood in the Tirana was quiet despite a heated pre-election campaign in which the key forces were the Democratic party, which is the biggest anti-communist force, and the Socialist party, the renamed communists.
