BFN [Text] An official visit by PRC Prime Minister Li Peng to Kyrgyzstan ends today [25 April]. Li Peng has met with President Akayev, Supreme Council Chairman Medetkan Sherimkulov, and Prime Minister Dzhumagulov. A package of official documents was signed, among them an agreement on trade and economic cooperation, an accord on the further development of cultural links, and an agreement granting a loan of 50-million yuan to Kyrgyzstan. Today Prime Ministe Li Peng will visit the Chinese Embassy and in the afternoon he will fly out to the capital of Kazakhstan.
