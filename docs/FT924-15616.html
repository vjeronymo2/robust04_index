921003 FT 03 OCT 92 / Markets: At a Glance - Cut-price offer from Fleming Discount bonanza time continues, this time with an offer from Fleming Investment Trust Management, the investment trust manager. It is waiving its 1 per cent initial charge for private investors into its Overseas Investment Trust; the waiver is for lump sum payments through the Fleming Share Plan. Fleming is also offering to swap UK quoted shares for shares in the Overseas Investment Trust free of charge. Both offers end on November 31. Further details on 071-920-0539.
