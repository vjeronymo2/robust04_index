BFN [Text] A number of the members of the parliamentary bloc representing the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] have issued a statement in which they reiterated their strong and firm adherence to Yemeni unity as the fate of our people, stressing that no individual, group, or political force can encroach on this unity. They condemned the sedition ignited by the rebellious gang of apostasy in the YSP leadership among the sons of the one people. The members of the YSP parliamentary bloc also denounced the secession declaration, affirming its political illegitimacy. They considered this declaration a crime and high treason against our Yemeni people. The YSP parliamentary bloc reiterated their adherence to constitutional legitimacy based on the results of the parliamentary elections held on 27 April 1993.
