BFN [Text] Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, former [Yemeni] prime minister and envoy of 'Ali Salim al-Bid, secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, arrived this evening in Damascus. He is accompanied by 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Dali, member of the [Socialist] Party Political Bureau. He was received at Damascus International Airport by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar'. Mr. al-'Attas made a statement to a Syrian Arab News Agency, SANA, correspondent in which he said: I am pleased to visit fraternal Syria. I look forward to meeting with President Hafiz al-Asad and the brother officials to exchange views on conditions in Yemen. He added: We have great confidence in the efforts Syria is exerting to end the fighting and preserve Yemen's unity and what can be saved of the ties of brotherhood that were hit and greatly affected by the war.
