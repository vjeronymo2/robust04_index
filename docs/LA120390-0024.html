December 3, 1990, Monday, Southland Edition

CALIFORNIA IN BRIEF;

BERKELEY;

5 HELD, $8 MILLION IN COCAINE SEIZED

Police seized 17 kilograms of cocaine with an estimated street value of $8 million and arrested five men in a drug bust in the marina area, authorities said. The five men were held for investigation of possession of cocaine for sale. Those arrested were identified as Pablo Diaz, 44; Alvaro Tolosa, 27; Dario Isaza-Acosta, 58; Fernando Tolosa, 25, and Jaime Sanchez, 35.
