930707 FT 07 JUL 93 / International Company News: Tuntex Petrochemicals Tuntex Petrochemicals yesterday confirmed plans to build Taiwan's seventh naphtha cracker with a projected total investment of NDollars 87bn (USDollars 3.3bn), writes Dennis Engbarth in Taipei. The facility will have an annual throughput capacity of 10m tonnes, or 22,500 barrels a day. A spokesman estimated that the complex's annual production could reach a value of NTDollars 91bn. The proposed site would be close to Formosa Plastics' cracker in a coastal industrial zone near Taihsi in Yunlin county. The project would be the largest single investment by the Tuntex Group and officials said that Tuntex would seek to gain the participation of domestic and foreign investors in the project.
