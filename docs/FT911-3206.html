910425 FT 25 APR 91 / UK Company News: Oliver Resources losses reduced A significant reduction in the exceptional write-off of deferred exploration costs enabled Oliver Resources, the Dublin-based oil and gas exploration company, to cut pre-tax losses from IPounds 1.93m to IPounds 780,338, or Pounds 509,000 sterling, in the year to October 31 1990. Operating losses of this USM-quoted company came to IPounds 345,561 (IPounds 426,442), before exceptional charges of IPounds 393,836 (IPounds 1.43m) and interest received of IPounds 264,413 (IPounds 117,267). Losses per share were 1.2p (4.35p).
