931111 FT 11 NOV 93 / UK Company News: FUIT offer oversubscribed The intermediaries offer for subscription of up to 7.5m ordinary shares in Finsbury Underwriting Investment Trust at 100p per share was subscribed just over three times with some 22.62m shares being applied for. Applications have been scaled down with intermediaries being allocated about 33.16 per cent of the number applied for. In addition to the intermediaries offer, 22.5m ordinary shares have been placed firm.
