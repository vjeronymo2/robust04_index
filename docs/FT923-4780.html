920904 FT 04 SEP 92 / People: Appointments David Powell, formerly group legal director of Midland, is appointed director of group legal services at GUINNESS. He succeeds Derek Councell, recently moved to Prudential as legal services director. ***** Brian Walsh, finance director of GKN and a non-executive director of Westland, is appointed to the board of COOKSON GROUP in a non-executive capacity. ***** Rod Martin, md of JAMES LATHAM's Midland and Western company, has been appointed to the main board. ***** Peter Wild is to become general manager of CWS non-food marketing and distribution group on the retirement in December of Ken Stephenson. ***** Anthony Cooper, finance director of HENRY BOOT, has taken on on the additional role of company secretary on the retirement of Peter Hawley. ***** Cornelia Varney has joined P-E INTERNATIONAL in the new position of resources director. She comes from CSC-Index.
