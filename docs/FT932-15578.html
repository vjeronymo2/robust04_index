930413 FT 13 APR 93 / Eastern Europeans reciprocate on EC livestock ban THE CZECH Republic yesterday joined Poland and Hungary in banning imports and transit of livestock, meat and dairy products from the European Community, agencies report from Prague. This follows the EC's ban last week on imports of live animals, meat, milk and dairy products from eastern Europe. The EC said it was imposing the ban until May 10 to help stop the spread of foot and mouth disease.
