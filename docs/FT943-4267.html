940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Gartmore Value Inv Gartmore Value Investments announced a net asset value per share of 30.9p at the end of the quarter to July 31, down from 35.9p last year. Net revenue for the period was Pounds 436,000 (Pounds 240,000). Earnings per share were 0.88p (0.48p) and the first interim dividend is unchanged at 0.9525p
