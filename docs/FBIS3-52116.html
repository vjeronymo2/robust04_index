Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Security forces today announced that seven nationals were killed between Thursday and Friday in Blida, Biskra, Relizane, and Chlef. The same source also announced that two secondary schools, five middle schools, and two primary schools were set on fire between Thursday and Friday in the provinces of Blida, Oum El Bouaghi, Chlef, and Ain Defla.
