BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin last night held consultations with a number of cabinet ministers on Palestinian plans regarding the establishment of institutions in Jerusalem and creating facts on the ground. Qol Yisra'el's political affairs reporter says the ministers were told that the Palestinians are seeking, among other measures, to establish an economic authority and a university. They also want to have Orient House -- the unofficial PLO headquarters -- granted special status and they want to deploy their own security operatives in the area.
