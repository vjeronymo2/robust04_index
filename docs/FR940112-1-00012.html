DATES: Comments must be received on or before February 14, 1994. 14 CFR Part 71 [Airspace Docket No. 93&hyph;ANM&hyph;39] Proposed Amendment of Class E Airspace; Lakeview, OR AGENCY: Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), DOT. ACTION: Notice of proposed rulemaking.

SUMMARY: This notice proposes to amend the Lakeview, Oregon Class E airspace. This action is necessary to accommodate a new instrument approach procedure at the Lake County Airport. The airspace would be depicted on aeronautical charts for pilot reference DATES: Comments must be received on or before February 14, 1994.
