940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Goodhead For the half year to November 30, Goodhead Group, the printer and publisher, swung from losses of Pounds 3.71m to profits of Pounds 303,000 pre-tax. The figure took in a Pounds 36,000 profit (loss Pounds 3.45m) on the disposal of discontinued activities and a loss of Pounds 43,000 (Pounds 309,000) on sales of fixed assets of continuing operations. It also included profits of Pounds 137,000 from two companies since disposed of. Turnover on continuing activities totalled Pounds 13.4m (Pounds 12.4m). Basic earnings per share emerged at 0.4p (losses 23.2p).
