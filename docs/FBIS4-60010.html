BFN [Excerpt] The President, His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, has received a message from His Excellency 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Yemeni Presidential Council, dealing with the latest developments in Yemen. The message was conveyed to the president by Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah who met with the president at the presidential office today. President Zayid again called for an end to the internecine fighting and for preserving Yemen and the Yemeni people's achievements. He appealed to the Yemeni people to stop the bloodshed and seek the language of reason and dialogue to serve Yemen and its people. [passage omitted]
