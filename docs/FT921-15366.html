920110 FT 10 JAN 92 / Labour strategy on council tax LABOUR PEERS are to attempt to make the council tax, which the government plans to introduce as a replacement for the poll tax in April 1993, more closely related to ability to pay. Lord McIntosh of Haringey, chief Labour spokesman on local government in the Lords, warned ministers last night to expect 'many, many amendments' to the Local Government Finance Bill.
