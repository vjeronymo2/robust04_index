BFN ["Pool" item] [Text] Doboj, April 23 (TANJUG) -- Moslem forces mounted strong attacks on Serbs in the broader area of Doboj in northern Bosnia, while inter-Moslem fighting continued in western Bosnia. As reported by military sources of Serbs in Bosnia, the Moslem forces were most active on the front near Teslic, southwest of Doboj, where, coupled with strong artillery support, they executed two strong attacks which were repelled. The town of Teslic, too, itself came under mortar fire. There was fighting also northeast of Doboj, where the Moslem forces used all weapons at their disposal in the attacks on Serb positions. The Serb side was forced to respond, after which the attacks ceased. Inter-Moslem fighting in western Bosnia, as reported by the Moslem-controlled Sarajevo Radio, continued along all the separation lines in the Cazin and Velika Kladusa municipalities. There was heavy fighting with the use of artillery and infantry since the early morning hours between the forces loyal to Moslem radical leader Alija Izetbegovic and the formations of the autonomous province of western Bosnia, which was last year formed the followers of peaceful solution to the crisis in Bosnia.
