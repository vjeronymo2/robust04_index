940503 FT 03 MAY 94 / Gas de-regulation plans expected The government is expected to publish next Monday the much delayed proposals for de-regulation of the gas market from 1996. The proposals are politically sensitive because many industry analysts believe they will lead to price rises for some consumers. The document was initially expected several months ago following government decisions in December on de-regulation. Last week Ms Clare Spottiswoode, director-general of the industry's regulatory body Ofgas, who has helped to write the document, threatened to publish a version of it if the government delayed publication for much longer.
