BFN [Report by unidentified ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Dushanbe, 21 Jun -- The press center of Tajikistan's Defense Ministry reports that in the course of troop operations between 7 and 17 June in Garmskiy, Tajikabadskiy, and Jirgitalskiy Rayons, a stronghold of Tajik opposition fighters, who were receiving military assistance from Afghan mojaheddin instructors, was crushed in the territory of the destroyed villages of Chevi Mangaryk. The operations were conducted jointly by units of the Defense, Interior, and Security Ministries of Tajikistan. In the course of just one battle, 12 opposition fighters were killed and seven wounded, other casualty figures are being clarified. German and British-made radio communication equipment, as well as a grenade launcher, grenades, and firearm ammunition were seized. Fire drill notebooks in Tajik and Pashto and other documents belonging to the fighters were found. Law-enforcement agencies detained several members of armed groups. Four servicemen of the Defense Ministry of Tajikistan were killed in the fighting and nine were wounded. Measures were taken during the operation to ensure the maximum safety of civilians from nearby villages. The tasks set before the troops have been carried out. Units of the Armed Forces of Tajikistan have now returned to their sites of regular deployment. However, despite all the measures that are being taken by the authorities and the forces to stabilize the situation in the Garm group of rayons, the situation there remains tense.
