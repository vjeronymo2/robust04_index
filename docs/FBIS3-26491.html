Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN ["Decree of the State Council of the People's Republic of China"] [Text] Beijing, 2 Feb (XINHUA) -- Decree No. 143 of the State Council of the People's Republic of China. "The State Council's Regulations on Collecting Agricultural Tax From Incomes of Special Agricultural Products" are hereby promulgated and become effective beginning 30 January 1994. [Signed] Premier Li Peng [Dated] 30 January 1994
