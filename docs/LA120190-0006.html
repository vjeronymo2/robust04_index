December 1, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MILKEN'S SENTENCE

Ivan Boesky was given a prison sentence of three years. Milken must "go behind bars" for a maximum of 10 years.

The Rev. Jim Bakker was sentenced to 45 years. Ah, good old jurisprudence.

I wonder whose crimes did the most damage to the citizenry?

ELDON BURGESS

Montrose
