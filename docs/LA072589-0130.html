July 25, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

MAN, 92, KILLED AS CAR OVERTURNS

A 92-year-old Tustin man was killed Monday night when his car left the roadway and flipped over, authorities said.

Harry G. Ericson was found dead at the scene of the one-car accident in the 20100 block of Santiago Canyon Road in Orange, according to the Orange County coroner's office. The cause of the 6:55 p.m. crash is under investigation.

The victim's wife, Rosemary Ericson, a passenger in the car, was taken to Chapman General Hospital in good condition and was later released in the care of family members, hospital officials said.
