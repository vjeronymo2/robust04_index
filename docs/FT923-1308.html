920924 FT 24 SEP 92 / Tec to subsidise company wage bills SOUTH Derbyshire Training and Enterprise Council is to offer small manufacturing companies a wage subsidy to help them retain employees, Paul Cheeseright writes. The scheme, announced yesterday, is believed to be the first time any of the 82 Tecs - which administer government training schemes in England and Wales -has offered to help companies to meet their wage bills. Costs of the South Derbyshire scheme will be met from the Tec's reserves.
