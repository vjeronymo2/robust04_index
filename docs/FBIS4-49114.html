BFN [Text] Beijing, 3 May (XINHUA) -- Starting today, compatriots in Taiwan and Overseas Chinese abroad will have a new source to promptly know the situation of the securities market and joint stock enterprises on the mainland -- the "Mainland Securities" special broadcast. The "Mainland Securities" program is sponsored jointly by the China Securities Paper and the Central People's Broadcasting Station Department of Broadcasting for Taiwan. The program will be beamed to Taiwan, Hong Kong, and Southeast Asia in the Amoy dialect. It was learned that the "Mainland Securities" program includes "Weekly News Roundup," "Market Observation," "This Week's Commentary," "Comments by Specialists," "the Trend of Corporations," "Introduction of Enterprises," and other subjects. It will be broadcast by Programs One and Two of the Central People's Broadcasting Station every Tuesday. The duration of the program is 10 minutes and will be broadcast 10 times a day.
