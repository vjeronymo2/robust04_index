940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / TUC slams government over Swan Hunter The TUC congress yesterday adopted an emergency resolution condemning lack of government support for Swan Hunter, David Goodhart writes. Mr Barry Davis of the AEEU engineering and electrical union said the government had lied to the people of Tyneside and to CMN, the potential buyer. 'We were all given hope, but now we know that they had decided in January this year to close the yard.'
