930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / Yeltsin urged to reinstate Gaidar President Yeltsin is being urged by reformists to restore to his government Mr Yegor Gaidar, a leading architect of Russia's market reforms who was sacrificed as prime minister last December to appease the conservative parliament. It was reported yesterday that the prime minister, Mr Viktor Chernomyrdin, would soon meet Mr Gaidar to discuss his return to government.
