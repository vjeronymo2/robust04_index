921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / Observer: Sugar test For a man who has just signed up Alan Sugar, the establishment's non-executive headhunter Colin St Johnston is surprisingly reticent about his plans for his new client. Although St Johnston's Pro-Ned outfit despises the old boy network which fills corporate boardrooms, it still prefers to work behind the scenes. It has helped troubled companies like Wace and Brent Walker find new non-execs, but finding someone prepared to stand up to Alan Sugar will be an acid test of its effectiveness. If St Johnston comes up trumps, he should be able to increase his standard Pounds 12,000 finder's fee.
