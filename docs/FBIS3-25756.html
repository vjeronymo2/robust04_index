Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] We have just heard that King Zwelithini and Chief Buthelezi held an unscheduled four-hour meeting with Foreign Affairs Minister Pik Botha in Pretoria last night. Mr. Botha has confirmed that the meeting focussed on the deadlocked constitutional talks but did not give further details. Mr. Botha said he believed that if the problems surrounding the Zulu monarchy could be resolved there was good chance that the other problems could be overcome.
