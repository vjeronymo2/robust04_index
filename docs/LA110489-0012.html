November 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

JUDGE SENTENCES ZSA ZSA GABOR TO 3 DAYS IN JAIL FOR SLAPPING A COP

Judge Charles Rubin imposed penalties on Zsa Zsa Gabor that were fair and just. He made it clear celebrities are not above the law. That arrogance, intimidations and outrageous behavior cannot be tolerated.

TOM DEMPSEY

Playa del Rey
