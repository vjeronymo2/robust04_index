Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Last year's inflation rate in Uzbekistan reached 1,000 percent because of excessive money supply and the shortage of consumer goods, an Uzbekistan senior banking official told INTERFAX. Minimal wages, pensions, and grants increased sevenfold in 1993 and their volume by 350-fold compared with 1990, and average wages rose 320-fold, according to Uzbekistan's State Committee for Statistics.
