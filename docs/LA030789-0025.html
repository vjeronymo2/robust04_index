March 7, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

CAL STATE NORTHRIDGE WOMEN TO OPEN PLAY IN DIVISION II WEST REGIONAL ON FRIDAY

Cal State Northridge will play Florida Atlantic University in the opening round of the four-team NCAA Division II West Regional women's basketball tournament Friday at Cal Poly Pomona.

Northridge (21-8), which finished second in the California Collegiate Athletic Assn. and is ranked 14th in the nation, will play at 6 p.m. in Kellogg Gym. Third-ranked Pomona, which defeated Northridge, 75-62, on Saturday to win its fourth consecutive CCAA postseason tournament title, meets Cal State Hayward (13-14), the winner of the Northern California Athletic Conference tournament, at 8 p.m.

The winners will meet in the final Saturday at 7:30 p.m.

"I'm happy," Northridge Coach Leslie Milke said of the pairings. "But I'm a little surprised they brought that team (Florida Atlantic, 21-7) in to this regional.

"I haven't seen them play this year, but from the conversations I've had with other people, they sound like they're going to be good."
