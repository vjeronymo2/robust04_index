January 28, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

MAUREEN O'CONNOR

Please don't use your valuable and limited space to cover such peripheral topics.

Los Angeles also continues to suffer the same civic ills set forth: homelessness, choked roads, aging library and sewer systems, undermanned police force, drug- and gang-related troubles, trash-disposal crisis and multiple airports, each crowded and dangerous. (None, in all honesty, as much as San Diego's.)

The "Maureen" article was up to your usual good standards; please direct that same attention and skill to topics closer to home.

MICHAEL YOUNGER, Toluca Lake
