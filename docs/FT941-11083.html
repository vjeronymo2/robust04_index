940207 FT 07 FEB 94 / World News in Brief: Berlusconi promises an economic miracle Media magnate Silvio Berlusconi staked his claim to the leadership of Italy before supporters gathered in Rome for the first convention of his centrist movement, Forza Italia. His message was simple: Italy's fortunes would revive within five years with him running the country. Italians would enjoy a 'new miracle' if his party won the March elections.
