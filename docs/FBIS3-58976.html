Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Israel's President Ezer Weizman, on an official visit to Turkey, has issued a call to Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad to make a courageous decision about meeting Israeli leaders face to face for peace. At a dinner in honor in Ankara, Weizman also sent condolences to al-Asad on the death of his son. [Begin Weizman recording, broadcast in progress] ....to send my condolences to President al-Asad for the loss of his son. I hope that he will continue to lead his country on the road to peace. I believe that President al-Asad, when he says Syria made a strategic decision for peace, I believe in his sincerity and integrity. He is a man of honor, but he has to understand that in peace, like in war, one has to recognize the mentality of one's counterpart. It seems to me that in Geneva, he failed to understand the expectations of public opinion in Israel. I do hope that in the near future he will make a bold decision and meet Israelis, meet the prime minister, meet the foreign minister, talk to them, understand them, and know the spirit of the country. [end recording]
