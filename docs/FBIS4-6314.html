BFN [Text] Foreign Minister U Ohn Gyaw hosted a dinner in honor of Mr. Prasong Sunsiri, visiting Thai foreign minister and chairman of the ASEAN Standing Committee, and his delegation at 1900 yesterday at the People's Park Restaurant. The dinner was attended by Deputy Foreign Minister U Nyunt Swe and invited guests. Also present at the dinner with Thai Foreign Minister Mr. Passong Sunsiri and his delegation were Mr. Wirasak Futrakun, Thai ambassador to Myanmar [Burma].
