BFN [Unattributed article: "NATO: Italy on Russia in Partnership for Peace"] [Text] (ANSA) Rome, June 23 -- As host to the upcoming G-7 summit in Naples in July, Italy welcomes Russia's entry into the NATO Partnership for Peace program, applauding Russia's growing involvement in international cooperation and the government's efforts at internal reform, according to a statement by the foreign ministry on Thursday [23 June]. The entry of Russia into the Partnership for Peace, the foreign ministry said, "constitutes an important contribution towards the establishment of a European security system based on cooperation and dialogue in a united Europe."
