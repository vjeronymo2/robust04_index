930908 FT 08 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Cantors hit by recession A FALL in pre-tax profits, from Pounds 2.1m to Pounds 1.67m, was announced by Cantors, the retail furnishing group, for the year to April 24. The result was achieved on turnover of Pounds 60.3m (Pounds 63.3m) which reflected the continuing recession. There was some slight improvement in current trading and early results from the new units in retail parks were encouraging. Earnings per share were 6.66p (9.02p). The final is held at 3p for a maintained 4p total.
