941029 FT 29 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Laser-Scan Laser-Scan Holdings, the USM-quoted computer group, suffered a pre-tax loss of Pounds 730,000 in the six months to June 30 on reduced sales and after capitalising the Pounds 304,000 product development costs of its Gothic technology. Profits were Pounds 45,000 last time. Delays in order placement in uncertain economic conditions resulted in a 48 per cent fall in sales to Pounds 3.02m (Pounds 5.78m). Continuing activities carried an operating loss of Pounds 350,000 (Pounds 42,000 profit) and discontinued activities lost Pounds 380,000 (Pounds 21,000 loss). Losses per share were 4p (0.3p earnings).
