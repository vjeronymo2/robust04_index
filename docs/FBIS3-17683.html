Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] The Defense Ministry press service reported that Armenia's Armed Forces are continuing to shell the settlements in northwestern regions of Azerbaijan. The settlements in Tauzskiy Rayon has been coming under fierce rocket and artillery fire from Choratan military base in Berd region of Armenia since this morning. Armenia's occupationist army launched an attack on Agdamskiy front on 27 March. The brave fighters of the Azerbaijani Army managed to halt the enemy's offensive and then launched a counterattack and liberated Shirvanly and Karakashly in Agdamskiy Rayon. At present, the battles are continuing in the direction of Geoytepe 10 km north of Agdam. One enemy tank and a considerable number of troops were destroyed. Our positions in Fizulinskiy Rayon also came under fire yesterday. Our fighters took countermeasures and silenced enemy's firing positions.
