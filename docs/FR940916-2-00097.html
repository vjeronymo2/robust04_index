Unless the answer consents to this Order, and answer shall, in writing and under oath or affirmation, specifically admit or deny each allegation or charge made in this Order and shall set forth the matters of fact and law or which Dr. Kusmik or other person adversely affected relies and the reasons as to why the Order should not have been issued.
