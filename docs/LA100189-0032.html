October 1, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

SATURDAY NIGHT SECRET

Regarding Lawrence Christon's article "Live from New York" (Sept. 17), I am shocked that he neglected to mention one of the most extraordinarily accurate satirists of the last 15 years: Christopher Guest. Not only was he arguably the main component of the National Lampoon Radio Hour, but for two seasons he astonished us with his comedic flexibility, musical prowess and character subtlety.

TONY FORKUSH

Hollywood
