941118 FT 18 NOV 94 / Libyan exchange rate move Libya has set a multiple exchange rate for the dinar one week after a devaluation, a senior official at Libya's Central Bank in Tripoli said yesterday. The official rate will remain calculated on the basis of one SDR for 0.525 Libyan dinar, while a new foreign exchange agency will sell the US dollar for 1.019 dinars, Mr Nouri Barioul, the official in charge of foreign exchange in the bank, told Reuters. He said the new rate would apply to private sector imports.
