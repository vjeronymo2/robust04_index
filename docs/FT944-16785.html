941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / The Conservatives in Bournemouth: Hunt promises dynamic economy Mr David Hunt, minister responsible for the Citizen's Charter, claimed that a dynamic market economy could flourish only with the Conservatives in office, Ivor Owen writes. He said this had to be clearly demonstrated, and individuals and families reassured that they could feel secure under the present government. Mr Hunt admitted the dangers of a Labour government were 'real enough', but insisted the Tory party's current poor showing in the opinion polls could be remedied. He said: 'Opinion polls have taken us to the edge of the abyss before, but the people never have.'
