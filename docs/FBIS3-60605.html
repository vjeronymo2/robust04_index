Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Ukrainian Academy of Sciences' institute of sociology has carried out a survey among the population. Thus, 45 percent of people questioned are in favor of Ukraine retaining the status of a nuclear state and only a third want its nuclear disarmament. The popularity of politicians was also unexpected. Former Prime Minister Leonid Kuchma is most popular. President Kravchuk is in second place, with Rukh Chairman Vyacheslav Chornovil third and Supreme Soviet Chairman Ivan Plyushch fourth. However, most consider Leonid Kravchuk nevertheless to be the top contender for the new election of president.
