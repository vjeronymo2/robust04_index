BFN [Text] Belgrade, 3 May (TANJUG) -- Croatian Defense Council [HVO] forces and regular Croatian Army units have resumed their provocations by firing from infantry weapons at Muslim army positions in the Mostar area, the Muslim Radio Sarajevo said today. According to the information service of the Fourth Corps of the Muslim army, since yesterday Croatian troops have been using mortar and antitank missiles for attacks on the Mostar battlefield. According to the Muslim radio, this is yet another indication that the extreme part of the HVO has still not given up its expansionist ambitions and its ultimate goal of dividing Mostar.
