BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS special correspondents Vadim Byrkin and Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan 16 May TASS -- The Azerbaijani Army shelled the positions of the Nagorno-Karabakh defence forces last night in the Mardakert and Agdam districts, the Karabakh Army press service said on Monday [16 May]. The Karabakh Army did not retaliate, observing the cease-fire agreement. The press centre also said that the Azerbaijani Army is moving its troops along the northern border of the disputed enclave.
