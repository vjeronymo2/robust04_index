920305 FT 05 MAR 92 / Hong Kong cuts transaction duty HONG Kong announced that it would lower stamp duty on share transactions to 0.4 per cent from 0.5 per cent, Reuter reports from Hong Kong. The cut, effective April 1, continues a trend started last year, when the rate was cut from 0.6 per cent. It would keep Hong Kong in line with international trends, said Mr Hamish Macleod, the colony's financial secretary.
