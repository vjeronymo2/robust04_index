BFN [By reporter Zhao Jian (6392 0256): "Chen Ziying Says All Hong Kong Residents' Legitimate Rights and Interests Should Be Protected"] [Text] Beijing, 19 May (ZHONGGUO XINWEN SHE)--Chen Ziying, deputy director of the State Council Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office, said here today: All Hong Kong residents' legitimate rights and interests should be protected. Chen Ziying made the above remarks when meeting with a delegation of British overseas Chinese community leaders led by Chiu Tien, chairman of the London Chinese Community Neighborhood Association. Their ancestors came from Hong Kong's New Territories, and Chiu Tien and his party are very much concerned about Hong Kong's smooth transition. Responding to the guests' worries, Chen Ziying said: Hong Kong's stability and prosperity is the most important concern during the transition period. After 1997, we will handle things in strict accordance with the Hong Kong Basic Law. He stressed: The principles of "one country, two systems" and "Hong Kong people ruling Hong Kong" will never be changed. Chiu Tien and his party visited China at the invitation of the National People's Congress Overseas Chinese Affairs Committee. Some other noted figures in the overseas Chinese community in Britain also came, including Chen Chin-yu, Shan Sheng, Wen Liang-sheng, Huang Jung-fu, and Teng Han-sheng. Tomorrow, they will go to Sichuan to get an understanding of the investment environment there.
