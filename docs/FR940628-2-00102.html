Persons wishing to present statements or obtain information should contact the RTCA Secretariat, 1140 Connecticut Avenue NW., Suite 1020, Washington, DC 20036; (202) 833&hyph;9339. Any member of the public may present a written statement to the committee at any time. Issued in Washington, DC, on June 21, 1994. David W. Ford, Designated Officer.
