December 4, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

Best Seats in the House: For the first time ever, seats on the stage of the Dorothy Chandler Pavilion will be filled by audience members. Because of overwhelming demand, 100 stage seats will be made available for Wednesday's joint recital by violinist Isaac Stern, cellist Yo-Yo Ma, violist Jaime Laredo and pianist Emmanuel Ax. The strategically located seats will go for $50 apiece. CLAUDIA PUIG
