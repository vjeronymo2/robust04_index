Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, Mar. 11 (A.A.) -- Sixteen members of the outlawed fundamentalist Hezbollah organization were caught in eastern Van on Friday. The State of Emergency Region Governor's Office told the Anatolian News Agency that 10 rifles and a large quantity of ammunition were found in houses belonging to Hezbollah members. The members are believed to be responsible for various crimes including throwing fire bombs and setting several buildings on fire.
