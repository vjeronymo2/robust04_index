BFN [Text] The Yemeni News Agency, SABA', has learned that Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council member, has instructed Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar, the acting prime minister, to quickly move a convoy carrying food supplies and medicines to Aden Governorate to be distributed free of charge to citizens in the governorate in order to meet the urgent needs of citizens in all parts of the governorate. The instructions come on the heels of the orders by the brother Presidential Council chairman and supreme commander of the Armed Forces to our Armed Forces, the forces of unity and legitimacy, to abide by the cease-fire resolution and halt the military confrontation against the secessionist rebels in compliance with the UN Security Council resolution on the situation in our country and to give a fresh chance to the secessionist rebels to rejoin the ranks of unity and legitimacy.
