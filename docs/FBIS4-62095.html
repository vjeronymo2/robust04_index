BFN [Report from ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA and ITAR-TASS column under the "Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow" rubric: "Command and Staff Exercises"] [Text] Command and staff exercises for [rehearsing] command and control of manpower and resources of the Russian Ministry for Civil Defense, Emergencies, and Natural Disasters in eliminating the consequences of natural disasters began on the territory of the North Caucasus yesterday. Civil defense troops and the ministry's central air-mobile detachment and search and rescue subunits are taking part in them.
