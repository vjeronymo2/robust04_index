931222 FT 22 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Batleys declines to Pounds 2.9m Pre-tax profits of Batleys, the private cash and carry wholesale chain, declined from Pounds 3.1m to Pounds 2.85m in the 26 weeks to October 30. Turnover was down by Pounds 7m to Pounds 232m. Earnings came out at 12.59p (13.71p).
