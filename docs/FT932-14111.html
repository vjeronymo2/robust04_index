930420 FT 20 APR 93 / Czech industry slows Industrial output in the Czech Republic fell 8 per cent in the first two months of 1993, compared with the same period last year, according to Mr Karel Dyba, economics minister, Reuter reports from Prague. Government statistics showed construction dropped nearly 11 per cent, while exports - excluding those to Slovakia - fell by more than 23 per cent. The government expects GDP to grow by 1-3 per cent this year.
