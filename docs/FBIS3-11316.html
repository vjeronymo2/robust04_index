Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The government appointed Augusto Garcia as the new mines and energy deputy minister. Garcia had been in the post of secretary general of the Mines and Energy Ministry. Garcia, an attorney from Barranquilla, has held the positions of secretary general of the Northern Financial Corporation, regional manager of La Andina Insurance Company, and director of the Business Register of the Barranquilla Chamber of Commerce. Garcia will replace Federico Rengifo Velez, who recently submitted his resignation.
