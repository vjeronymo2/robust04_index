Proposal: Operating Budget, Supporting Schedules, and Board Resolution. Office: Public and Indian Housing. Description of the Need for the Information and Its Proposed Use: This schedule will ensure that Public House Authorities (PHAs) follow sound financial practices and that Federal funds are used for eligible expenditures. PHAs will use the forms as a financial summary and analysis of immediate and long-term operating programs and plans to provide control over operations and achieve objectives.
