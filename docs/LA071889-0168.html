July 18, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

WILLIAMS TO DROP CHARGES IN DIVORCE

Washington Redskins quarterback Doug Williams has agreed to drop a forgery and theft complaint against his estranged wife, an attorney for Lisa Williams said today. As part of a divorce agreement, Lisa Williams will also have custody of the couple's child, said attorney Lewis Unglesby.

The settlement is to be finalized after being signed by the judge, Unglesby said.
