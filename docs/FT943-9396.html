940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / UK Company News: F&C Enterprise Foreign & Colonial Enterprise Trust reported a net asset value of 79.9p per share as at June 30. That compared with values of 72.9p at December 31 and 66.3p at end-June 1993. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 841,000 (Pounds 374,000) for earnings of 0.89p (0.4p) per share.
