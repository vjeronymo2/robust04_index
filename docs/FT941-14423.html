940120 FT 20 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Property sale falls at Coutts Consulting Coutts Consulting Group, which provides training and consultancy services to banks and other financial institutions and operates residential training centres, said the completion of the sale of its vacant Berkshire property, Winkfield Place, for Pounds 1.3m, had not taken place because of a default by the purchaser. Accordingly, the deposit of Pounds 65,000 had been forfeited and the property was being remarketed. A number of approaches had been received, it said.
