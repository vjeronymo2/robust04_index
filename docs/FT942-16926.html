940407 FT 07 APR 94 / International Company News: Artika Optima proposes public issue Artika Optima, one of Indonesia's largest plywood manufacturers, said it planned to go public in June by offering 66m shares, or 30 per cent of its enlarged share capital, Reuter reports from Jakarta. The offering is expected to raise Rp400bn to Rp500bn (Dollars 12.7bn-Dollars 15.9bn), making it one of the bourse's largest capitalised stocks. About 30 per cent of the proceeds would be used to acquire Maluku Dinamika Semen, an affiliated cement company.
