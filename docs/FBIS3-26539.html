Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 9 (XINHUA) -- Premier Li Peng today called on Peng Zhen and Wan Li, both former chairmen of the National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, extending spring festival greetings to them. Li, who has just returned to Beijing from an inspection tour, presented flower baskets to the two retired leaders, and wished them good health and happy new year. Li, also a Standing Committee member of the Communist Party's Politburo, briefed Peng and Wan on the current domestic situation and a series of policies and measures taken by the party Central Committee and the State Council to develop the economy and push ahead with reform and opening. He also solicited their opinions and proposals on different aspects of work. Peng and Wan voiced satisfaction with the work of the party Central Committee and the State Council as well as happiness with the economic development, reform and opening. Also, they expressed full support to the principle governing work in 1994 formulated by the party Central Committee, that is, to seize the current opportunity for development, deepen reforms, open China wider to the outside world, promote economic growth and maintain stability. They also expressed the hope that the party Central Committee and the State Council will continue to lead the Chinese people of various nationalities in working hard to score still greater achievements in the new year.
