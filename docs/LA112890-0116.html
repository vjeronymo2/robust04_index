November 28, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

TONIGHT: The Menorah Theatre, a new troupe formed by the Jewish Community Center of Central Orange County, will present "Angel for the Prosecution" by company artistic director Jon Garon at the Jewish Community Center, 12181 Buaro St., Garden Grove.

Curtain tonight: 8 o'clock. Tickets: $12.50. Through Dec. 18. Information: (714) 534-8011. Jerry Hicks and Jeffrey A. Perlman
