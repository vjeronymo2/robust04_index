BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow April 7 TASS -- Spokesman of the Russian president said on Thursday that Russia plans to build no military bases in Latvia. "The issue of deploying any Russian bases on the territory of Latvia was not raised and does not exist. The radar station in Skrunda cannot be viewed as a military base. All issues connected with the withdrawal of Russian troops from Latvia have been solved in the Russian-Latvian agreement which has been initialled", Kostikov said in a statement. On Wednesday a document received by TASS from the presidential administration said President Boris Yeltsin had okayed the proposal of the Defence Ministry to have nearly 30 military bases in CIS countries and Latvia. Kostikov said "explanations about a technical mistake, as a result of which Latvia was mentioned in the wrong context, will be provided by diplomatic channels".
