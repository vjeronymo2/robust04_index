August 2, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Reliance Group Holdings Inc.: The New York-based insurance, investment and development company reported second-quarter income of $164.7 million, contrasted with a $5.9-million loss for the year-ago period. Revenue was up 26% to $1.15 billion, boosted largely by sale of the company's General Casualty subsidiary. Profit gains were registered by its title and mortgage insurance units, but soft pricing and a loss of $9 million from fire damage in the recent Santa Barbara-area disaster were blamed for lower profit in property and casualty insurance.
