940324 FT 24 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Exeter Preferred Amended results for the period February 1 1993 to January 31 1994 show net profit Pounds 159,000 (Pounds 219,000), after tax of Pounds 73,000 (nil). The tax charge had erroneously been excluded in the earlier announcement. Earnings per share are 0.68p (0.94p) and revised dividend 0.68p (0.939p).
