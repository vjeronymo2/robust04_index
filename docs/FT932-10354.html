930508 FT 08 MAY 93 / World News in Brief: General Motors General Motors of the US, the world's biggest vehicle maker, is to set up a Dollars 100m (Pounds 64.90m) utility vehicle plant beginning in 1994. GM, acting through its Brazilian subsidiary, will work with CIADEA, a local car company, which will hold an undisclosed minority stake in the venture. GM quit the country 15 years ago because of political unrest and economic decline. Its return is seen as an indication of increasing international confidence in Argentina's economic reforms.
