BFN [Text] Tirana, May 6 (ATA) -- According to an annual report of the International Corporation of Free Trade Unions of Kosova, published recently, Serbian authorities have dismissed from their jobs 147,000 Albanians who are replaced by Serbians and Montenegrins. Besides, there are 125 other Albanian workers who are also dismissed by the Serbian administration of the Product Enterprise in Skenderaj, which takes the third place for its capacity in Kosova.
