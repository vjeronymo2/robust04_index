930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Era Pre-tax profits at Era Group, the specialist retailer, plunged from Pounds 2.16m to Pounds 970,000 in 1992. A rise in rents and services of Pounds 950,000 contributed to a Pounds 1.7m increase in operating expenses. Turnover was Pounds 73.3m (Pounds 71.1m) and earnings per share 0.96p (2.18p).
