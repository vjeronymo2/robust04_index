BFN [Text] Wellington, April 14 (XINHUA) -- Western Samoa said it is grateful to China for its assistance and has much to learn from China in the economic, social and cultural areas, said a report from Apia received here today. This was stated by Western Samoa's head of state Tanumafili II Malietoa when receiving credentials from new Chinese Ambassador Wang Nongsheng on Wednesday [13 April]. The head of state noted that Western Samoa and China have been enjoying very friendly relations since they established diplomatic relations 18 years ago. Although China is not well-off at the moment, it has extended huge and effective assistance to Western Samoa, he said. Wang Nongsheng arrived in Apia on April 10.
