941119 FT 19 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Cambridge Water Cambridge Water lifted pre-tax profits by 54 per cent from Pounds 1.77m to Pounds 2.72m for the six months to September 30, on marginally reduced turnover of Pounds 7.47m, against Pounds 7.63m. Earnings per share climbed from 394p to 500p and the interim dividend is lifted to 125p (65p), partly to reduce disparity. The company said income and expenditure had been in line with forecasts and budgets. New connections were being made at about the same rate as last year, which was well below the long term average.
