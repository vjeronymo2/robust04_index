920309 FT 09 MAR 92 / UK News (Employment): FT survey of Tecs AN opinion survey of Training and Enterprise Council directors is being carried out by the Financial Times. Early response to the survey has been strong, with nearly 300 Tec directors already having responded. The closing date is today, but after requests from Tec directors we have made arrangements to be able to include returns made by March 13. The survey aims to establish Tec directors' views on the effectiveness of the councils, their structure, purpose and funding.
