BFN [Text] At 1115 today, President Hafiz al-Asad received Lebanese President Ilyas al-Hirawi. The meeting was attended by Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam. It was followed by a closed meeting between the two presidents. Talks dealt with regional and international issues, inter-Arab relations, and issues of common concern. The two presidents focused on issues related to all tracks of the peace process. They stressed the importance of continuing consultations, cooperation, and coordination between the two friendly countries. The two presidents also reviewed the development of bilateral relations and expressed their satisfaction with the steps accomplished within the framework of the Treaty of Brotherhood, Cooperation, and Coordination in all fields.
