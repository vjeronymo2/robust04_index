BFN [Text] Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa returned to Cairo from Brussels this evening after chairing meetings of the Egyptian-European Cooperation Council. In his arrival statement, he said the council meeting discussed means to enhance cooperation between the two sides and that an agreement was reached to start a new phase of Egyptian-European (?partnership) in a new framework by taking the new situation into consideration. Concerning the situation in Yemen, he said the military conflict was a tragic action that would achieve no good and that it would not resolve the dispute. He added that efforts to handle this situation must continue, and there must be an immediate cease-fire, disengagement of forces, and a dialogue to deal with this situation in accordance to the Yemeni leaders' views. He noted that Egypt is monitoring the situation carefully.
