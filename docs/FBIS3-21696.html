Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, February 24 (SNARK)--Young people of Racha region in the north of Georgia are on the edge of survival--this is the opinion of Soso Makhadiany, the deputy of the Georgian parliament. The reason is that during last four months Racha, the relatively well-being settlement, transmuted into the center of drug business and drug addiction. He said to SNARK that the drugs are trafficked in Georgia from Georgia. According to Makhadiany the only way to stop this evil is to block the border, that passed through Oni city. The police doesn't control the situation, and even more, the authorities of the region are involved in the drug business.
