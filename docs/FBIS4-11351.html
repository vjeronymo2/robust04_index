CSO [Editorial Report] Managua BARRICADA in Spanish of 9 April 1994 on page 8 reports that Edmundo Castillo has been expelled from the National Conservative Party, PNC, for one year because he does not follow the party line. The article explains that Castillo, who is renowned for his oratory skills in the National Assembly, has a reputation for being "very independent." Castillo explained that he was expelled because he supported constitutional reforms and because he criticized the party for practicing nepotism and for only representing a single class of society.
