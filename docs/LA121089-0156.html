December 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Johnny Rodgers, the 1972 Heisman Trophy winner, and another man allegedly approached Andre Ware's mother last weekend about representing the Heisman Trophy-winning quarterback from the University of Houston.

Houston Athletic Director Rudy Davalos said he immediately reported the incident to the university and the state of Texas.

A Texas law passed in 1987 bars agents from signing players with college eligibility. Ware is a junior.
