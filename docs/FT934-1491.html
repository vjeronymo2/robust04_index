931218 FT 18 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Welsh Industrial net assets up 46% Welsh Industrial Investment Trust reported a 46 per cent rise in net asset value per share to 195.52p as at October 5, against 134.19p a year earlier. After-tax revenue for the six months dropped from Pounds 38,544 to Pounds 30,367 and earnings per share were 2.25p (2.86p).
