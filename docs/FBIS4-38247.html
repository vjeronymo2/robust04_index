BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 29 Jun (MENA) -- [passage omitted] A military source in Aden reports that grinding battles are now raging between northern and southern forces on the outskirts of Aden. In a statement faxed to MENA from Aden today, the source said the northern forces are trying to advance toward Aden using all types of weapons, including aircraft. He added that the northern forces launched air raids on several vital economic installations.
