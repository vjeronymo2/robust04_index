940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / International Company News: Insurer advances Cathay Life Insurance, Taiwan's biggest insurance company, saw profits nearly double in the first month on the back of stock investments and real estate sales, writes Laura Tyson in Taipei. Net profits for the year to June 30 were NTDollars 6.75bn (USDollars 255m), up 90 per cent from NTDollars 3.55bn a year earlier. Revenues during the same period climbed 19 per cent to NTDollars 94.32bn.
