Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Boris Fedorov has said that he will join a State Duma faction of liberal and reformist character called the "Union of December 12." Radio Liberty reports that he understood that he might remain in the shadow of Gaydar without the support of people who hold the same views. Irina Khakamada, the leader of this union, believes that Fedorov is a man who has a clear program.
