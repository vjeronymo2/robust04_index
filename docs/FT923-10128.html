920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / International Company News: General Dynamics to cut 5,800 jobs GENERAL Dynamics, the US defence contractor, plans to cut around 5,800 jobs at its Fort Worth, Texas, division by the end of 1994, writes our Financial Staff. The company said yesterday the cuts were a response to falling defence expenditures and a drop in the production rates of its F-16 fighter aircraft. The Fort Worth division currently employs around 20,000 people.
