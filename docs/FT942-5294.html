940606 FT 06 JUN 94 / Iraqis sentenced in Kuwait A Kuwaiti court on Saturday sentenced five Iraqis and one Kuwaiti to death for plotting to kill former US President George Bush when he visited the emirate in 1993, Reuter reports from Kuwait. Another five Iraqis and two Kuwaitis were sentenced to prison terms ranging from six months to 12 years for offences including possession of explosives, liquor smuggling and illegal entry. One Kuwaiti was acquitted. The death sentences are subject to review by another court and by the ruler of Kuwait, the emir.
