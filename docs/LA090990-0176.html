September 9, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

* Henry J. Todd has been named vice president and general manager of the product support division at McDonnell Douglas Space Systems Co. in Huntington Beach.

He was previously vice president and general manager of the firm's quality systems division. Bonnie W. Soodik was named the new general manager of the quality systems division. Soodik joined McDonnell Douglas Automation Co. in 1977, then moved to Douglas Aircraft Co. in 1987 as program manager for information resource management.
