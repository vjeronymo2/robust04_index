August 12, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BLACK PLAYERS FELT UNWELCOME IN JAPAN

About those Ram players who were complaining because they got turned away by nearly every nightspot they tried to enter in Tokyo: What are these guys doing going to nightclubs anyway during training season?

These yo-yos should be concerned about getting into top physical condition, not boozing it up in some Tokyo gin mills. Bring back George Allen.

LANNY R. MIDDINGS

San Ramon
