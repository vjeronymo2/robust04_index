940812 FT 12 AUG 94 / International Company News: Sabena hopeful Sabena, the Belgian national carrier, reported first-half earnings were BFr450m (Dollars 13.9m) higher than expected, fuelling hopes that the airline might break even in 1994, Reuter reports from Brussels. 'For the first semester we received BFr450m more than we had expected,' said Mr Yves Panneels, a company spokesman. Airline sources had been expecting a full-year loss of about BFr2bn.
