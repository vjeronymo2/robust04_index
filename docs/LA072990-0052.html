July 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

POP EYE

AND FINALLY: KLOS-FM's top-rated Mark & Brian team will be on vacation until Aug. 8, but the rock station has lined up an intriguing array of morning-deejay replacements. Guest jocks include Steve Garvey, Jim Belushi, David Hasselhoff and Laker stars Michael Cooper & Byron Scott. . . . And Monday is "Where Is Jimmy Hoffa Day" at Rhino Records in Westwood. The shopper with the best guess as to Hoffa's whereabouts wins a free Rhino T-shirt.
