Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "January Inflation 8 or 20 Percent?"] [Text] The independent Macedonian television A1 cites unofficial sources on differences between the Macedonian Government and the State Statistics Institute, emerged towards the end of January, i.e., with the start of the realization of the stabilization program for 1994. The disputable issue is the January inflation rate. According to the Government plans, it should have been 8 percent, but the Statistics Institute announced a 22 percent inflation in January.
