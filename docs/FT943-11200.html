940801 FT 01 AUG 94 / Business Travel: Gatwick alert About 40 aircraft were diverted from Gatwick airport yesterday after an aircraft tyre burst during take-off. The incident - on one of the busiest travel weekends of the year - put the airport runway out of action for more than an hour while the Ljubljana-bound Adria Airways DC9 was towed away. None of the 100 crew and passengers was hurt. About 267,000 people passed through the airport over the weekend.
