930508 FT 08 MAY 93 / World News in Brief: London stocks Early deals saw the FT-SE 100 Index down 14 points but the UK equity market closed with relative optimism, up 7.4 at 2,793.7. Share prices brushed off the initially bearish effects of the government's poor showing in local elections and turned higher as they moved into the new account. Market report, Page 15
