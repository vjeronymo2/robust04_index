BFN [Text] Shanghai, April 1 (XINHUA) -- A passenger plane of Lufthansa German airlines made a maiden flight from Frankfurt to Shanghai Thursday [31 March], marking the official opening of the route. This is Lufthansa's first direct route between Europe and this Chinese metropolis. The Boeing 747-400 plane will fly from Shanghai to Frankfurt every Monday and Thursday. Lufthansa has also increased its flights to Beijing to six times a week.
