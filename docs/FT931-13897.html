930121 FT 21 JAN 93 / Parliament and Politics: PM emphasises domestic agenda MR JOHN MAJOR last night sought to restore momentum to his domestic political agenda by emphasising the priorities of increased industrial investment, improved vocational education and higher standards in public services. In the first of a series of party political broadcasts focusing on domestic policy issues, the prime minister signalled also new measures to combat juvenile crime, with plans shortly for legislation allowing tougher sentencing of juvenile offenders.
