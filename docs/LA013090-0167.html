January 30, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

MADER WINS SUPER GIANT SLALOM

Guenther Mader of Austria won a World Cup super giant slalom today, beating Norway's Ole Christian Furuseth by .45 seconds.

Mader was timed in 1 minute, 14.79 seconds to Furuseth's 1:15.24. Norway's Atle Skardaal, winner of the 50th anniversary Hahnenkamm downhill at Kitzbuhel, Austria, 10 days ago, was third in 1:15.26.

It was the second victory of the season for Mader, who has won five times since his first win in 1986.
