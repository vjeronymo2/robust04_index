Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Stuttgart (DDP/ADN) -- After the German White-Collar Union (DAG), the Public Service and Transport Workers' Union (OeTV) has now also called token strikes in all areas of public service. Following four hours of talks by the wage negotiation committee, OeTV Chairwoman Monika Wulf-Mathies said this afternoon in Stuttgart that the token strikes are to take place on 28 February and 1 March in eastern and western Germany. In this way the union hopes to make the employers more open to negotiation at the fourth round on 9 and 10 March. There are to be token strikes at various sites throughout Germany.
