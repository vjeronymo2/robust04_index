February 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

'LETTER' TO EX-PRESIDENT PRAISED FOR ITS HONESTY

Sam Hall Kaplan's column (Jan. 22) on ex-President Reagan was a breath of fresh air, truth, honesty, etc. after the stifling accolade given to this, our worst President in the history of our republic.

Thank God that the infamous Ron and Nancy dog and pony show has closed its run. He can no longer veto sound legislation that fails to fit into his shallow, inhumane ideology.

I hope that you will continue to write on the subject of Reagan's illusions. The American people are being cruelly deceived by this amiable dunce.

JOHN UTVICH

San Marino
