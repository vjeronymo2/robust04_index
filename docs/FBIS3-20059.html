Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Luebeck (DPA) -- Last night, unknown perpetrators carried out an arson attack on the synagogue in Luebeck. Since the fire was soon discovered and the fire-brigade was called, only minor damage was caused. The room where the divine services are held was not damaged. Winfried Tabarelli, senior director of the Criminal Investigation Department for the South of Schleswig-Holstein, said at the scene that there had been no indications of an attack. It is impossible to guard every possible target of an attack around the clock. Nevertheless, the attack has to be rated as attempted murder, since six families live in the house, he said.
