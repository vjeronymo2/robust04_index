941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / Glass winner London-based architectural practice Gollifer Associates was yesterday named the winner of a Europe-wide competition mounted by the Tyne and Wear Development Corporation for a Pounds 4.5m National Glass Centre to be built on Sunderland's riverside. The building, both a business centre and a tourist attraction, will have a large glass roof on which the public will be allowed to walk.
