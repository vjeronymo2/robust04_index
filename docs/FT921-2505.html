920319 FT 19 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Expanding SME rises to Pounds 1.6m Sanderson Murray & Elder (Holdings), the expanding motor distributor, returned profits of Pounds 1.55m pre-tax from a turnover of Pounds 72.4m for the year to end-December. The results compared with Pounds 836,000 and Pounds 22.6m respectively for the 18 months to December 31 1990. Earnings rose to 44.1p (32.3p) and a proposed final dividend of 7p makes a 10p (same) total. The company's 50p shares, which rose 11p to 535p yesterday, are to be sub-divided into five 10p units.
