930407 FT 07 APR 93 / Computer plant for Cumbernauld ELONEX, the personal computer producer, is to have its main components manufactured at a plant to be set up in Cumbernauld near Glasgow by Cordata, an associated company. Cordata plans to invest Pounds 5.5m in a new 40,000 sq ft plant, which is expected to employ 175 people. Mimtec, the electronics manufacturer which is a subsidiary of Murray International, a privately owned Scottish company, confirmed yesterday that it is to set up a Pounds 20m plant at Gourock, Renfrewshire, which will carry out subcontract work for International Business Machines. The plant will employ 400 people.
