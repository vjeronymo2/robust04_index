940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / International Company News: Philips multimedia move Philips Consumer Electronics, a division of the Dutch electronics group, Zenith Electronics, the US television and electronics manufacturer and Compression Labs, a specialist in video conferencing technology, are combining to develop and market technology for interactive television services. They will jointly develop and market TV set-top-boxes for both digital and analogue networks. These devices will enable cable TV and telecoms network operators to deliver a wide range of programming options, such as video-on-demand, video games, home shopping and other video services with two-way interactive communications. The companies plan to combine digital and analogue television standards to create set-top-boxes capable of receiving today's analogue signals.
