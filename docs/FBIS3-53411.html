Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' last night received Japanese Deputy Foreign Minister Hiroshi Fukuda. Raslan 'Allush, deputy foreign minister; Walid Rajab, director of the Asia Department; (Kanaka), director of the Middle East Department at the Japanese Foreign Ministry; and Minoru Kubota, Japanese ambassador in Damascus, attended the meeting. Discussions dealt with the developments of the peace process and bilateral relations. Hiroshi Fukuda stressed Japan's interest in the expected Geneva summit between Presidents Hafiz al-Asad and Bill Clinton. He hoped that this summit will be successful and help achieve a just, comprehensive peace in the region.
