Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The scheduled third trial of the medium-range surface-to-surface missile, Agni, has been postponed. The test-firing of the country's most sophisticated missile was to be carried out today at the interim test range at Chandipur in Orissa. Our correspondent, quoting official sources in Balasore, reports that the trial was postponed at the last stage of the countdown this morning because of some technical snag. The exact date of the launching will be notified later.
