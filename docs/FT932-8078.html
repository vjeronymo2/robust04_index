930520 FT 20 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Fulcrum Investment asset value 48.11p The net asset value per capital share of Fulcrum Investment Trust stood at 48.11p at April 30 against 12.65p six months earlier. Net revenue for the 18-month period amounted to Pounds 1.11m for earnings of 13.49p per income share. In the previous 12 months net revenue was Pounds 291,368 for earnings of 9.08p. A 2.92p fourth interim dividend in lieu of a final is declared, making 13.07p (9.2p) for the period.
