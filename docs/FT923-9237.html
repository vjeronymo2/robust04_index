920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / Observer: Heidi Hi As might be expected, Zurich's media gnomes are digging feverishly into all aspects of the mysterious case of Ms Alexandra Gantenbein, the 44-year-old member of the 'I am' religious sect who married the 72-year-old chairman of the Lindt & Sprungli chocolate empire. According to one newspaper report yesterday, Frau Gantenbein was a waitress until she was 36 and her real name is Heidi.
