BFN [Text] According to reports received by Radio Sweden, the 20 or so Swedes still remaining in Yemen will be evacuated from Sanaa later today with the help of German planes to Djibouti on the other side of the Red Sea. Steen Howue-Christinsen, the Swedish ambassador to Saudi Arabia, is currently in Sanaa to coordinate the evacuation of the Westerners remaining in Yemen. He now also confirms that missiles hit the town or fell close to the town of Sanaa last night.
