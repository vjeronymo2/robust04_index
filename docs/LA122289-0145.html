December 22, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

DESTEFANO AND HANLON PACE ORANGE COAST

Scott DeStefano had 24 points and Chip Hanlon had 23 to lead Orange Coast College to an 86-68 victory over host Grossmont.

Alan Schlines had 18 points for OCC, which improved its record to 11-2. OCC controlled throughout and held a 37-25 halftime advantage.
