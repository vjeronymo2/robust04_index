Language: Tajik Article Type:BFN [Text] His eminence Haji Akbar Turajonzoda, first deputy leader of the Islamic Rebirth Party of Tajikistan, has once again stated that the Russian troops stationed in Tajikistan together with UN forces, under the supervision of Islamic countries and the European Community, can play the role of peacekeeping and take part in disarming the warring side. Haji Akbar said: We too understand the geopolitical interests of Russia and do not want to deprive Russia of them [words indistinct]. Haji Akbar once again reiterated that the Islamic Rebirth Party of Tajikistan will first hold talks with Russia. Talks with Russia will determine the course of our future negotiations with the authorities in Tajikistan.
