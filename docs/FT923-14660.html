920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Daejan dips to Pounds 16.2m DAEJAN HOLDINGS, the property investment group, reported a marginal decline in pre-tax profits, from Pounds 16.4m to Pounds 16.2m, for the year to end-March. Gross income grew by Pounds 1.57m to Pounds 24.3m and comprised: rent and other charges receivable, after outgoings, totalling Pounds 15.3m (Pounds 14.2m); surplus on sales of properties amounting to Pounds 8.82m (Pounds 8.49m); and other income of Pounds 200,000 (Pounds 68,000). The tax charge fell slightly to Pounds 5.75m (Pounds 6.2m) and minorities were reduced to Pounds 12,000 (Pounds 35,000). The company said its portfolio had been professionally valued as at March 31 and the resultant Pounds 25.9m fall in the value of its investment properties had been incorporated into the 1991 accounts. The net asset value per share based on the balance sheet figures at that date was Pounds 11.44. A valuation of the group's trading properties indicated an excess over book value of Pounds 59.2m. Had this figure been incorporated into the accounts the net asset value per share would have been Pounds 15.07, the company added. A final dividend of 13p (16p) is proposed making a 25p (24p) total.
