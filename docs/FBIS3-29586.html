Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] According to the agreement reached between the Myanmar [Burma] Oil and Gas Enterprise and Texaco Exploration Myanmar and Corporation, drilling operations at Ye Mahn Aung-1 -- a test well in oil exploration block M-13 offshore from Tenasserim -- will begin on 14 February. It is estimated that drilling operations will last for about 45 days. A mariners' notice has been issued warning fishing trawlers to refrain from entering the vicinity of Ye Mahn Aung-1: North latitude 13 degrees, 17 minutes, and 41.70 seconds; and East longitude 96 degrees, 58 minutes, and 31.40 seconds.
