930219 FT 19 FEB 93 / Pounds 21m project at London Zoo LONDON Zoo yesterday announced a Pounds 21m investment programme designed to turn the 36-acre Regent's Park site into a world-class animal conservation and breeding centre. Dr Jo Gipps, zoo director, said Pounds 2.5m had already been raised. Two Londoners have pledged Pounds 250,000 cash towards development and Pounds 1m towards a children's zoo and education centre.
