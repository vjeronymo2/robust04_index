Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riga, Mar 02, BNS -- No heavily beaten up Russian soldiers were taken to Riga war hospital in February or March this year, Igors Golins, Russian West-East army unit's press center head, told BNS denying the agency ITAR-TASS information published Tuesday [1 March] in newspaper PANORAMA LATVIJI. ITAR-TASS declares that the North-West army unit leadership announced about the case of beaten up Russian soldiers to Latvian Foreign Ministry but Golins denies it. Ivars Embrekts, Latvian Foreign Ministry press secretary also affirmed that the ministry has received no announcements from North-West army unit's leadership. North-West army unit's medical center chief informed Golin that no journalists have spoken neither with the leadership of medical center nor soldiers undergoing treatment at Riga war hospital. Golin said he has no idea where the wrong information came from and noted that the leadership of North-West army unit will officially deny it in the near future.
