April 18, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

STRICTLY BUSINESS: Perish the thought that Orange County developer Barry G. Hon bought all those golf courses because he's a golf fanatic (D1). . . . The $967 million that he'll pay for 19 golf courses and other real estate was purely a business investment, he says. . . . "I don't play enough golf to have a handicap. I set the record for losing golf balls wherever I play. I can hit any pond of water anywhere in eyesight." Steve Emmons and David Reyes
