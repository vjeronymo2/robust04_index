940309 FT 09 MAR 94 / International Company News: SNC-Lavalin strong SNC-Lavalin, the Canadian-based engineering and construction management group, posted a 74 per cent gain in 1993 net profit, to CDollars 16.3m (USDollars 11.9m), on revenues 6.5 per cent higher, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. Mr Guy Saint-Pierre, president, said international volume rose.
