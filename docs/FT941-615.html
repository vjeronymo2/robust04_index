940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / UK Company News: GR (Holdings) Losses at GR (Holdings), the leisure and fitness, property and sheepskin processing concern, rose from Pounds 124,753 to Pounds 216,882 over the half year ended December 31. The deficit took account of a provision against costs of joint interests amounting to Pounds 121,782 (nil) and a loss on investments of Pounds 98,518 (nil). Directors said the second half loss was likely to be slightly in excess of the Pounds 333,000 recorded for the same period of the previous year. Turnover of continuing activities totalled Pounds 2.1m (Pounds 2.14m). The interim dividend is held at 0.4p. Losses per share emerged at 2.3p (1.3p).
