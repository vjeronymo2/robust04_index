BFN [Text] Sanaa, 12 May (INA) -- Information Minister Hasan Ahmad al-Lawzi today said Yemeni forces have taken the strategic town of al-Dali', more than 100 km north of Aden. In a press statement, al-Lawzi said the fall of al-Dali' means the end of one of the important military arsenals and zones the secessionists had relied on.
