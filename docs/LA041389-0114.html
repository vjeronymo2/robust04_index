April 13, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

An Oklahoma football player told a judge he witnessed the sexual assault of an Oklahoma City women by teammates Nigel Clay, Bernard Hall and Glen Bell in an athletic dormitory Jan. 21.

Jimmy Fennell testified he was in the bedroom of Clay's dorm the night the woman said she was attacked and that all three had sex with her. The three players are accused of first-degree rape in the alleged assault.
