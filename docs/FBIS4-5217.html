BFN [Text] Seoul, April 8 (YONHAP) -- President Kim Yong-sam had a telephone conversation with outgoing Japanese Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa Friday afternoon, in which Kim consoled the resigning premier. The telephone talks lasted about 15 minutes beginning 6 P.M. It was called by Hosokawa. The Japanese prime minister explained to President Kim about the background that led him to decide to resign as premier and thanked the president for the cooperation Kim showed to him. President Kim said there would be no change in his friendship with and trust in Prime Minister Hosokawa, which he said had been cemented through mutual visits and summit talks.
