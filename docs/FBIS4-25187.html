BFN [Text] The Rizhao-Wulian-Linyi optical fiber communications cable formally opened a few days ago, thus thoroughly eliminating the strained situation on circuits serving between Rizhao and Linyi and allowing Linyi, Yinan, Yishui, Juxian, Wulian, and Rizhao city's Donggang District to embark on a path of quickening its long- distance communications service.
