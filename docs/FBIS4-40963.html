BFN [Report by INTERFAX: "Unprofitable Mines Will Be Closed"] [Text] This year Russia will earmark 305 billion rubles for closing unprofitable coal mines. For the first time the Russian state budget envisages a separate category for expenditure on such objectives. In the near future, according to "Rosugol" Company Deputy Chairman N. Garkavenko, there are plans to complete work on closing down five unprofitable pits, including pits in the Pechora coal field. Another six pits will be shut down before the end of 1995.
