January 23, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Meredith L. Donoho has been named general manager of Kitchell CEM's Southern California regional office in Costa Mesa. He has been with the Phoenix-based construction management firm since 1984. Donoho has been the company's manager on the Orange County Intake Release Center, on the East Mesa Detention Center and on the California Medical Facility-South in Vacaville.
