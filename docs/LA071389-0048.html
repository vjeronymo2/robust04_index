July 13, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: OLIVIER

"Wuthering Heights," one of Laurence Olivier's most memorable romantic films, will open Friday at the Samuel Goldwyn Pavilion Cinemas as a tribute to the acclaimed actor who died Tuesday at the age of 82. The 1939 film, which also stars Merle Oberon, David Niven and Geraldine Fitzgerald, is booked for a one-week engagement. ALEENE MacMINN
