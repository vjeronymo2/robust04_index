SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: Background Under the Fair Housing Act (42 U.S.C. 3600&hyph;3619), the Department is authorized to investigate complaints alleging discrimination in housing. Section 810(f) of the Fair Housing Act requires the Department to refer complaints to agencies that have ``substantially equivalent'' fair housing standards, as determined and certified by the Department. The certification standards are codified at 24 CFR part 115.
