Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] At 1430 today Israeli military planes carried out a raid on the headquarters of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine-General Command [PFLP-GC] in the al-Na'imah hills. The Lebanese Army's ground resistance confronted the planes. The raid left one man dead and another wounded. A Lebanese Army source told the NATIONAL NEWS AGENCY that two Israeli planes flew over the al-Na'imah hills and fired a missile at the position. The source said that the Lebanese Army confronted the raiding planes with antiaircraft fire. [passage omitted] A PFLP-GC spokesman told AFP that a PFLP-GC member was killed while on the way to the hospital and another was seriously injured in the raid. The spokesman pointed out that the raid targeted a clinic run by the PFLP-GC.
