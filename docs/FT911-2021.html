910502 FT 02 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Drayton Asia Trust Drayton Asia Trust reported that net asset value per share at March 31 stood at 90p, down from 98p at the same time last year. The figure, however, represented a 38 per cent improvement on the September 30 year-end figure of 65p. Net revenue at this interim stage fell from Pounds 993,000 to Pounds 339,000. Earnings per 10p share came to 0.337p (0.988p).
