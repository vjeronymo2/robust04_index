Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 20 (KCNA) -- The opposition Democratic Party of South Korea in a statement released by its spokesman Saturday urged the U.S. Government to drop the projected deployment of Patriot missiles in South Korea, which might provoke the North, a radio report from Seoul said. The statement also demanded that the South Korean authorities pursue a self-determined diplomacy for the relaxation of the tensions on the Korean peninsula, said the radio.
