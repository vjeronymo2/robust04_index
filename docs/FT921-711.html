920327 FT 27 MAR 92 / Boycott warning Libyan leader Muammer Gadaffi, angered by the UN sanctions move, says he will boycott all hostile countries and keep his business for friendly ones, Reuter reports from Paris. 'No more markets. I will not buy anything from a country aggressive to us. Not the smallest trifle,' he told the French Figaro Magazine in an interview published today. Col Gadaffi stopped short of saying western companies would be expelled.
