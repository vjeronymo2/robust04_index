930302 FT 02 MAR 93 / Confidence gap emerges in north A MARKED difference in confidence about recovery has emerged between professional property advisers and companies with head offices in northern England, a survey by Coopers & Lybrand, the accountancy firm, shows. Nearly 70 per cent of 154 firms or businesses surveyed expect an upturn in northern property markets in the second half of this year. But while a quarter of professionals in surveying, engineering and construction think recovery is already under way, only 14 per cent of occupiers agree.
