Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Text] Indonesia has again reiterated its proposal that the arms embargo against Bosnia be lifted because of the unbalanced arms strength. This was stated by Minister/State Secretary Murdiono to newsmen at Merdeka Palace an hour ago, after Bosnian President Alija Izetbegovic paid a courtesy visit on President Suharto. According to Minister Murdiono, Indonesia is making a continuous effort to support the Bosnian people's struggle. Bosnian President Alija Izetbegovic, who flew to Jakarta this afternoon of his own desire, is making the working visit to Indonesia after visiting Malaysia and Brunei Darussalam.
