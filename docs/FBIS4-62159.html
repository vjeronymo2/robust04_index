BFN [Ivan Gavrilenko report: "One `Desert Storm' Will Suffice"] [Text] The Orenburg Oblast Administration building has been picketed. The participants in this protest action demanded the cancellation of Russian-U.S. exercises in the Totsk training area and the rejection of cooperation with servicemen from the U.S. Third Infantry Division under the command of General Holder, which participated in operation "Desert Storm" in Iraq. Earlier, local representatives of 14 public movements and parties, including the Orenburg organization of the Agrarian Party, protested the joint exercises in Totsk. The protest against the "peacemaking exercises" is beginning to strike a chord in other oblasts and republics in the Urals and Volga region.
