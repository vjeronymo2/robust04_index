920125 FT 25 JAN 92 / Nadir seeks court order MR ASIL NADIR, former Polly Peck International chairman, is to seek a High Court order halting the Pounds 378m claim against him by the company's administrators. The court was told yesterday that the application for suspension of the administrators' civil action would be on the ground that it was based on allegations similar to those on which Mr Nadir faces criminal charges and that he had now been adjudicated bankrupt.
