June 12, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS: DEVELOPMENT FIRM NAMES NEW PRESIDENT

Albert Marshall has been appointed president of Newport Pacific Development Corp., Newport Beach.

He will assume the day-to-day management duties, succeeding Magdy Hanna, chairman of the company. Marshall, former executive vice president, joined the company in 1987 as an in-house architect. He previously owned a Houston-based architecture, construction and development firm.
