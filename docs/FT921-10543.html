920205 FT 05 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Updown net asset value at 477.25p Updown Investment Company had a net asset value of 477.25p per share at February 3 compared with 442.5p a year earlier and 493.57p per share at the interim stage on June 30 1991. Net revenue advanced from a restated Pounds 531,000 to Pounds 618,000 after aggregate tax of Pounds 214,000 (Pounds 204,000). Earnings per share rose from 13.27p to 15.45p. The directors are proposing to pay an increased dividend of 12p (11p) for the year.
