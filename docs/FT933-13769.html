930717 FT 17 JUL 93 / Clerk accused of Pounds 6.6m theft A FORMER clerk with the London offices of Salomon Brothers has been charged with the theft of Pounds 6.6m from the US investment bank. Mr Alexander Darlow was charged with stealing the money on or before May 28. He was bailed to appear before London's Horseferry Road magistrates on August 12.
