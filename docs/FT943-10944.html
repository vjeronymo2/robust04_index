940802 FT 02 AUG 94 / International Company News: Iveco Iveco, the truck division of Fiat, has made a bid for Nasr Automotive Manufacturing Company (Nasco), the Egyptian state-owned vehicle maker, a Nasco senior executive said, Reuter reports from Cairo. Mr Abdel Wahhab el-Habbak, chairman of the state company which owns Nasco, said it was the only bid he had received.
