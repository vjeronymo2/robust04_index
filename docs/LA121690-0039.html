December 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

THE NEW LEAR

So Norman Lear has decided to do his own brand of evangelism ("Divining the New Lear" by Judith Michaelson, Dec. 2), sort of a cosmic mix of comedy and God.

We're all waiting to see whose "god" Lear will choose to ridicule. I think we Bible-believing Christians have a good idea which one it will be.

DEBRA PEEPLEZ

Inglewood
