930707 FT 07 JUL 93 / More jobs to go at Aerospatiale THE French aircraftmaker Aerospatiale plans to cut 1,450-1,500 jobs next year, after reducing its workforce by 1,145 this year, a source in the company told Reuter in Paris yesterday. Blame was placed on 'the major weakness of new orders in the aircraft division'. Aerospatiale currently employs 45,000 people.
