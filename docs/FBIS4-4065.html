BFN ["Special dispatch": "Foreign Firms Allowed To Engage in Wholesale Business and Set Up Foreign Trade Companies in Shanghai"] [Text] Shanghai, 7 Apr (LIEN HO PAO)--Shanghai recently decided to allow foreign firms to engage in wholesale business and to set up foreign trade companies. The adoption of this policy will not only mark a major reform step in the opening of the mainland's tertiary industry, but will also greatly promote development in the Chang Jiang Valley. Reportedly, the details of the scheme for implementing the decision are being formulated intensively. In the course of expanding the scope of using foreign funds, apart from opening wholesale business, foreign trade, and retail sales of producer goods to foreign companies, Shanghai will also allow mainland retailers to launch joint ventures and carry out cooperation with foreign chain stores.
