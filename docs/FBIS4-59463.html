BFN [Text] The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] has evacuated all its installations in the city of Rafah in the Gaza Strip, but the transfer of powers in the place has still not taken place. This is reported by our military affairs correspondent Karmela Menashe. Commander Yitzhaq Aharonovich, chief of the Border Police, visited the Gaza Strip today to supervise the evacuation of his forces from the area. Aharonovich said the Border Police is currently preparing to undertake the task of ensuring the safe passage of Israelis in the autonomy areas. It is also preparing for the joint patrols with the Palestinians, slated to start in the next few days.
