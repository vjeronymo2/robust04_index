April 29, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ARCHBISHOP MAHONY'S LETTER AND ABORTION ISSUE

Pro-life demonstrators should be honored that the lawyers for the American Civil Liberties Union are so enthusiastically persecuting them ("$5,000 Fines Urged in Abortion Protest," Metro, April 25).

If the ACLU, known for embracing criminals' rights and defending Nazi marchers, is against pro-life activities, the pro-life people must be doing something right.

CAROLINE MIRANDA

North Hollywood
