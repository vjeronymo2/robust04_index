BFN [Article by "(dz)": "There Were More Than 300 Cases of Malfunction at Dukovany in 1993"] [Text] Dukovany--At the Dukovany nuclear plant, in the vicinity of which environmentalists commemorated the eighth anniversary of the Chernobyl disaster, there were 315 cases of malfunction and deviations from the standard. Three of them were described as failures due to flaws in security measures. The report that the State Office for Nuclear Safety drafted for the government states that the emergency signaling systems were activated 12 times in 1993. Despite this, the State Office for Nuclear Safety considers the operation of the Dukovany nuclear plant to be safe and reliable.
