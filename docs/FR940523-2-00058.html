EFFECTIVE DATE: June 22, 1994. [Docket No. 94N&hyph;0181] Fujisawa USA, Inc., et al.; Withdrawal of Approval of 22 Abbreviated New Drug Applications AGENCY: Food and Drug Administration, HHS. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) is withdrawing approval of 22 abbreviated new drug applications (ANDA's). The holders of the ANDA's notified the agency in writing that the drug products were no longer marketed and requested that the approval of the applications be withdrawn.
