Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 11 (KCNA) -- A spokesman for the South Korean opposition Democratic Party reportedly said in a commentary March 8 that the Democratic Party would push ahead to the last with the drive for the abolition of the "National Security Law" (NSL), complaining that the Kim Yong-sam group was opposed to the abrogation of the "NSL", an anti-reunification fascist law. The position of the party strongly calling for the abolition of the "NSL" was manifested earlier at a press conference called by the chairman of the party on March 5.
