Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 18 (XINHUA) -- Li Tieying, Chinese State Councillor and minister in charge of the State Commission for Restructuring the Economy, met with a Romanian Government delegation here this morning. The two sides had a cordial talk on topics of common interest. The delegation, headed by Mircea Cosea, Romanian minister of state and president of the Council for Co-ordination, Strategy and Economic Reforms, are here on an 8-day visit at the invitation of the commission.
