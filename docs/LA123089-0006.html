December 30, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

CAL STATE TRUSTEE CREDENTIALS, CHANCELLOR'S SALARY INCREASE

Your article on Marianthi Lansdale outraged me. Is she saying it's okay to lie? What a terrible example she represents for the young people of this country. To make matters worse, Chancellor Reynolds appears to condone such behavior. What is happening to the values of our society?

KATHERINE O'KLEIN

Whittier
