940315 FT 15 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Berisford makes Pounds 7m property sale Berisford International, the food, financial services and property group, through Berisford Property Investments, has sold the Castlehill industrial park in Stockport, Gtr Manchester, for Pounds 7.63m, or Pounds 7m net of voids and expenses. The property is a fully-let industrial investment of 150,000 sq ft.
