940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Cantab Cantab Pharmaceuticals, Cambridge-based biopharmaceuticals company reports a virtually unchanged net loss of Pounds 783,000 for the first quarter ended March 31 1994, compared with Pounds 778,000. The company said that the result was within budget. For the whole of 1993 there was a loss of Pounds 2.88m The result was struck after research and development expenses of Pounds 976,000 (Pounds 817,000) and net interest of Pounds 241,000 (Pounds 89,000). Loss per share was 0.08p (0.13p).
