920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Dan-Air denies link with Virgin MR DAVID James, chairman of Davies & Newman Holdings, which owns the Dan-Air airline, has denied that the group is in merger talks with Virgin Atlantic, the airline headed by Mr Richard Branson. Mr James said yesterday: 'The only discussions which have taken place with Richard Branson have been intermittent dialogues over the past two years considering various marketing initiatives. There are no dialogues in hand beyond such minimal levels and certainly nothing which would justify talk of a merger.'
