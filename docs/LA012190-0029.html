** "HANGIN' TOUGH LIVE" NEW KIDS ON THE BLOCK CMV ENTERPRISES VIDEOCASSETTE ($19.98).

If you're not a girl in your low teens, this 50-minute tape probably won't interest you.

But those in that target group will find plenty to l-o-v-e in this no-frills package. This white teen vocal group is so into piercing falsetto leads and so heavily influenced by such '60s and '70s R&B vocal groups as the Stylistics that it's like watching five Bobby Brown wannabes. Sorely missing: interviews to provide insights into this pop phenomenon. DENNIS HUNT
