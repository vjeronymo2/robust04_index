BFN [Text] Vrnjacka Banja, May 27 (TANJUG) -- One person was killed and nine wounded when a hand granade exploded in a bus full of passengers travelling from Krusevac to Vrnjacka Banja in the central part of the Yugoslav Republic of Serbia on Thursday night [26 May]. The person suspected of having thrown the granade into the bus was arrested after the incident. Investigation is under way.
