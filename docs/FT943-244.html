940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / Lloyd's sends out warning letters Lloyd's yesterday sent out warning letters to 4,500 Names in the first stage of its campaign to recover some of the estimated Pounds 1.3bn outstanding from the lossmaking investors. The letters are from Lloyd's debt recovery department, set up two weeks ago to reinforce the insurance market's recovery efforts. Names unable to pay are being asked to contact Lloyd's hardship fund before the end of the year when it is to be abolished.
