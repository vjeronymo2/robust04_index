930318 FT 18 MAR 93 / Files on land deals sent to DPP NEW files concerned with the Operation Cheetah probe into allegations of corrupt land deals have been sent to the director of public prosecutions. The disclosure yesterday follows the end of the trail in which Mr Derek Hatton, former Labour deputy leader of Liverpool city council, was cleared of charges of conspiring to defraud the council over two car park sites in the city.
