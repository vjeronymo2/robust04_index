Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Edwin Mallon Avalos, the Radio Fides correspondent in Cochabamba] [Text] During a news conference, the Permanent Assembly for Human Rights [APDH], along with various groups such as peasant organizations and the Departmental Workers Federation, reported a U.S. Embassy onslaught against those who dare to report DEA abuses in our national territory. This remark was made by Dr. Victor Gutierrez, the APDH president in Cochabamba.
