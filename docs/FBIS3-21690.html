Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Hamburg (DPA) -- Police and customs impounded 17 kg of opium in a garden in Norderstedt near Hamburg. The garden plot belongs to a 54-year-old Iranian, in whose apartment a small amount of this drug had been found previously, police reported on Friday [25 February]. One kilo of opium was hidden in the garden house, another 16 kg and 15,000 marks were found by the police buried in the ground. The police were unable to give a black-market value for the drugs.
