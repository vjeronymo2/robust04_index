941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Seton buys Orovite Seton Healthcare Group is acquiring SmithKline Beecham's Orovite, the multi-vitamin supplement. It is paying Pounds 3.78m cash for the trade mark and goodwill. In 1993, the brand made a gross profit of Pounds 810,000 on sales of Pounds 1.23m.
