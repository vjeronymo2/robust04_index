940223 FT 23 FEB 94 / Observer: Slave Labour In trying to wrongfoot the competition, Merseyside's Conservative MEPs have fallen flat on their faces as they limber up for the Euro-elections. 'Labour's Euro-Socialist manifesto commitment to a 35-hour day (sic), 4-day week would either cost British industry Pounds 20bn a year - or force British workers to accept a 20 per cent pay cut,' they calculate. Sounds more like a recipe for dispatching Britain's economic ills. If only Labour could deliver.
