910507 FT 07 MAY 91 / Appointments: Berisford US posts BERISFORD INTERNATIONAL has appointed Mr Barry O'Connor as president of its US holding company, Berisford Inc. Mr Richard G. Kirschner has been appointed chief financial officer. The present chief executive officer, Mr John CW Mitchell, completes his contract term on June 30 and will resign from the board. The board of Berisford Inc will comprise Mr Larry Frenkel (corporate counsel), Mr Sholem Greenbaum, and Mr George Weisz (non-executive). Mr Weisz is a senior partner with New York law firm Cleary Gottlieb Steen & Hamilton. ***** Sir George Turnbull has been appointed a non-executive director of WESTLAND GROUP. He is chairman of Inchcape, a non-executive director of Kleinwort Benson, and chairman of the Korea Europe Investment Fund. ***** Because of increasing commitments in Southern Ireland Mr Timothy Barry is stepping down as deputy chairman of CHELSEA BUILDING SOCIETY, but will remain a director. He is succeeded by Mr Maurice Hart, a director. ***** Lord Trefgarne has been appointed a non-executive director of AWD, Dunstable. He was minister of state for defence procurement. ***** HARDING GROUP has appointed Mr William Clarkson as a non-executive director. He is a director of WSJ (Contracts), and The Kenmore Group. ***** Mr Masamichi Tamada, managing director and chief executive of MITSUBISHI FINANCE INTERNATIONAL, London, returns to Japan shortly to become general manager, corporate banking division 2 of the Mitsubishi Bank. He is succeeded by Mr Juntaro Fujii, currently chief manager, capital markets division, in the Tokyo head office. ***** GIROBANK has appointed Mr Richard Banks (pictured) as senior general manager, personal banking. He was general manager, corporate banking north. He will responsible for managing the accounts of 2m personal customers and 700,000 Visa cardholders. Girobank is a subsidiary of the Alliance & Leicester Building Society. ***** BUTTE MINING has appointed Mr Peter Burnett as a non-executive director. He is a director of Charter Consolidated, Anglo American Corporation of South America, Latin American Investment Trust, and Waverley Mining Finance. ***** Mr Kenneth Cherrett has been appointed chairman of the CREDIT INDUSTRY FRAUD AVOIDANCE SYSTEM. He is also director of partnership sourcing at the Confederation of British Industry, to which he is on secondment from the TSB Group. ***** LLOYD THOMPSON GROUP has promoted Mr John Birkmire and Mr Richard Corfield to the board. Mr George Stuart-Clarke has been appointed a non-executive director. He is a director and joint head of corporate finance at Lloyds Merchant Bank, and previously was managing director of Arlington Securities. ***** Mr Ron Dexter has been appointed managing director of A1 PACKAGINGS group, taking over from Mr James Harding who founded the business in 1935. Mr Dexter joined the group 15 years ago and was managing director of A1 Packagings (Southern).
