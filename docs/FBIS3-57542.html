Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Controversial reports are coming from Baku and Stepanakert about the fighting in the zone of the Armenian-Azeri conflict. A spokesman for the Azeri Defence Ministry has said that units of the Azeri army yesterday held successful operations in the Kelbadzhar area of the republic. As a result, the spokesman said, the Kelbadzhar group of the Armenian army was forced to retreat to the Armenian-Azeri border, while Armenian units fighting in the Mardakert area of Karabakh weer being cut off from supply bases in Armenia. Meanwhile, a spokesman for the Karabakh defense army claims that there are no position changes in the Kelbadzhar area. The spokesman said that up to 70 Azeri servicemen were killed during hostilities in the area. Among the Karabakh casualties are four persons killed and 11 wounded. The spokesman said that on Wednesday morning Azeri troops resumed the offensive in the south-east and southern sections of the front.
