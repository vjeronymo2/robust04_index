Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Iraq has informed the United Nations that U.S. aircraft violated its airspace from 10 to 20 December for surveillance purposes and provocation. A protest note sent to UN Secretary General Dr. Butrus-Ghali said the aircraft mounted 745 sorties in south and north Iraq, flying at various altitudes and speeds. An American reconnaissance plane, TR-1, violated the Iraqi airspace in the southern parts of Iraq and then headed for Saudi Arabia.
