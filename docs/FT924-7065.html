921118 FT 18 NOV 92 / International Company News: Audi names head Mr Franz-Josef Kortuem has been nominated as chairman of Audi, part of the Volkswagen motor group, Reuter reports. Mr Kortuem has been responsible for Audi's marketing since May this year. He replaces Mr Ferdinand Piech who succeeds the retiring Mr Carl Hahn as management board chairman of VW.
