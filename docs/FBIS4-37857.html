BFN [Excerpt] It was announced in Washington today that the United States has decided to grant the Palestinian representation in Washington the status of an official foreign mission. A Palestinian source said that Washington informed the PLO of its decision during a meeting held at the U.S. State Department between the PLO representative in Washington, Dr. Hasan 'Abd al-Rahman and (Eric Muswell), head of the foreign representation department at the State Department.
