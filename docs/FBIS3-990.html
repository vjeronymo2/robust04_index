Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pretoria March 16 SAPA -- Former Conservative Party [CP] MP [member of parliament] for Ermelo, Moolman Mentz, has joined Gen Constand Viljoen's Freedom Front, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reported on Wednesday [16 March]. Mr Mentz said he had accepted a request to make himself available as a candidate for the Freedon Front in the April election. The Freedom Front was meeting in Pretoria on Wednesday morning to finalise its candidate list for the election before the 4.30PM deadline.
