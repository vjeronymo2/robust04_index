941210 FT 10 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Microvitec buys Microvitec, the USM-quoted software, networking and display systems group, has acquired Justfree, trading as Layer One, a specialist networking company, for up to Pounds 2m in cash and shares. It has also bought Perex, a supplier of touch screen technology, for Pounds 2.43m in cash, shares and a vendor placing.
