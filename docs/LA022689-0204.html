February 26, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

UC IRVINE BEATS SOUTH CAROLINA TO REACH INDOOR TENNIS FINAL

Mark Kaplan and Richard Lubner of UC Irvine defeated Steve Longley and Richard Lynch of the University of South Carolina, 6-7, 7-6, 6-3, to give the Anteaters a 5-4 victory in the semifinals of the National Team Indoor tennis tournament Saturday at Louisville, Ky.

The victory by Kaplan and Lubner, the Anteaters' No. 2 doubles team, proved to be the deciding point in the match, breaking a 4-4 tie. With the victory, seventh-ranked Irvine (5-4) advances to today's final against fourth-ranked California, a 5-2 victor winner Kentucky.

In singles, Trevor Kronemann defeated Longley 6-2, 6-4; Lubner defeated Lou Gloria, 6-3, 4-6, 7-5, and Shige Kanroji defeated Dave Herman, 7-5, 6-3, to help Irvine.
