920307 FT 07 MAR 92 / Finance & the Family: Smaller companies drift Small company shares drifted slightly downwards in the week before the Budget. The County Small Companies index fell 0.1 per cent to 956.7 in the week to March 5 while the Hoare Govett Small Companies Index (capital gains version) dropped 0.04 per cent to 1207.27 over the same period.
