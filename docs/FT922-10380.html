920501 FT 01 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Bletchley Motor rises to Pounds 753,000 Bletchley Motor Group increased pre-tax profit by 21 per cent, from Pounds 623,000 to Pounds 753,000, in 1991. This USM-quoted Vauxhall, Ford and Rover dealer, limited its fall in new vehicle sales volume to 8 per cent, increased sales in the second hand market, and had another good year in contract hire. Sales and servicing accounted for Pounds 238,000 of the profit and contract hire almost doubled its contribution to Pounds 485,000. Overall turnover rose to Pounds 60.2m (Pounds 55.9m). Growth in turnover had already been seen and the group was ahead of budget in the first quarter of 1992. Earnings per share were 15.8p (12.9p) and the final dividend is 4.1p for a total of 8.2p (7.85p).
