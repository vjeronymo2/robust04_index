October 2, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

L.A. GROWTH AND WATER SUPPLY

I enjoyed reading your article about Edwin Neumann and his fight with the city over wasteful water practices.

I have long respected the saving of water since having to haul drinking water as a kid. If people want to live in a desert then they might think of a desert landscape. Good article!

J.D. WHEAT

San Bernardino
