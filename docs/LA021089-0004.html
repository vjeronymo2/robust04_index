February 10, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"How loud must we cry? How many of our children must die before our city leaders will say enough is enough and put an end to this suffering?"

-- Pat Moore, one of about 100 people who marched through the streets of Compton to demand an end to gang violence, such as the shooting that took the life of 2-year-old Phillip Fisher last month.
