940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Merchants Trust Net asset value at the Merchants Trust improved from 256.9p to 259.4p over the 12 months to July 31, however it was lower than the 302.9p at the January 31 year end. Net revenue for the six months to the end of July was Pounds 6.47m (Pounds 6.34m) for earnings per share of 6.32p (6.2p). A second interim dividend of 2.85p is being paid to make 5.7p (5.3p) to date.
