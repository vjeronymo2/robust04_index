941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / Oil tanker 'had loaded in Iraq' An oil tanker captain whose vessel was intercepted in the Gulf on suspicion of violating UN sanctions said yesterday he had loaded diesel fuel in Iraq for export in violation of the ban. Captain Izzat Abdulhadi Khalifa, master of the Al Mahrousa, stated he had loaded 3,162 tonnes of diesel oil in the Iraqi port of Zubayr between October 16 and 19, then sailed south. The Saudi-owned, Honduran-flagged tanker was brought to Kuwait under escort after being stopped by a US warship in international waters on Saturday.
