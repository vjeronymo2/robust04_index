BFN [Text] Defense Minister Julian Garcia Vargas has announced that he wants to achieve a gradual reduction of the Spanish blue helmet contingent in Bosnia. In addition, Garcia Vargas said the soldiers are to be replaced by aid workers and Spanish businessmen who will help to rebuild Mostar. Julian Garcia Vargas. [Begin Garcia Vargas recording] In our case, if the pacification of Mostar goes ahead as planned, it is probable that in the next few months I will think about a reduction, mainly because the blue helmets will be replaced by civilian experts, aid workers, and businessmen [end recording]
