910430 FT 30 APR 91 / UK Company News: Usborne hit by exceptionals Profits of Usborne, the agriculture, property and motor products group, fell from Pounds 2.43m to Pounds 1.63m pre-tax over 1990. Turnover expanded from Pounds 136.21m to Pounds 210.26m. Profits were struck after taking account of a swing from exceptional credits of Pounds 879,000 to debits of Pounds 172,000. Tax took Pounds 531,000 (Pounds 626,000) and extraordinary items Pounds 648,000 (Pounds 873,000). Earnings amounted to 1.68p (2.77p) per share and an unchanged final dividend of 1p makes a same-again 1.5p.
