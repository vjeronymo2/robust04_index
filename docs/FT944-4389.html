941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / UK Company News: New Zealand Invest New Zealand Investment Trust had a net asset value of 191.72p per share at October 31, against 214.63p a year earlier. Net revenue, after tax of Pounds 126,235 (Pounds 122,523), amounted to Pounds 261,269 (Pounds 174,106) for earnings per share of 2.61p (1.74p). A final foreign income dividend of 1.5p (0.6p) is proposed, for a total of 2.5p (2.1p).
