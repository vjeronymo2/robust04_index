930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Landed good general demand,report the Tea Brokers' Association. Better liquoring East African teas remained fully firm. Mediums were well supported but prices tended easier. Plainer descriptions met less enquiry at easier rates. Offshore fair demand with Ceylons a good feature. Brightest teas were dearer: others barely steady. Quotations quality 150p nom, good medium 135p, medium 100p,low medium 82p. The highest price realised this week was 150p for a Rwanda pf. 1.
