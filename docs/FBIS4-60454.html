BFN [Text] Khartoum, 25 May (MENA) -- The Sudanese Foreign Ministry summoned Egyptian Ambassador to Sudan Dr. Hasan Jad-al-Haqq late last night to inform him of its protest over the two statements the Egyptian Foreign Ministry issued in reaction to the Sudanese authority's closure of some Egyptian establishments and homes in Sudan last Friday. The Sudanese ministry claimed that these measures were within the context of enforcing the new Sudanese law on rents.
