921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / UK Company News In Brief HOLLAS: Hollas has reached an agreement with Kleene-e-ze to joint venture its Hawkshead Sportswear subsidiary. The offshoot, involved in the mail order and retailing of outdoor clothing, country wear and footwear, incurred a loss of Pounds 923,820 in the 12 months to March 31; net assets at that date were Pounds 740,029. ***** BETA GLOBAL Emerging: Results of placing and open offer which closed on October 30, showed there were applications for 69,920 C shares of the 3m available under the open offer. The balance would not be allotted. The 10.8m C shares subject to the placing would be allotted in full. ***** CAIRN ENERGY: in its open offer to shareholders a total of 8.87m shares were taken up, 36.5 per cent of total available. ***** DOEFLEX has acquired the remaining 40 per cent of its subsidiary, Horizon Industries NV, for an initial consideration of 202,014 Doeflex ordinary and a further consideration of up to 304,518 shares. ***** ELECTRONIC DATA Processing, the computer software and services company, is buying Via Systems and Open of Colorado for a total of Dollars 1.21m (Pounds 740,000). Via incurred a pre-tax loss of Dollars 11,000 on turnover of Dollars 1.48m in the year to January 31. Pre-tax losses at Open in 1991 were Dollars 52,000 on turnover of Dollars 154,000. ***** GKN: Australian arm has sold its portable buildings activities - GKN Modular Buildings - to Hardie Bondor, a subsidiary of James Hardie Industries, for ADollars 4m (Pounds 1.8m). ***** GREENALLS GROUP has, through its Premier House catering division, acquired The South Marston Country Club, Swindon, and The Crossroads, Northampton, for a total Pounds 2.3m cash.
