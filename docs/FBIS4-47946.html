BFN [Text] The police have carried out dawn operations to seize illegal arms, ammunition and explosives at the premises of Radio Pretoria and at Fort Wonderboom in Pretoria. Warrants making the action possible were issued after the police received information that there were illegal arms and explosives on the premises. A police spokesman said that weapons had been found, but he declined to give further details. A news conference is to be held on the matter later today.
