BFN [Text] The Broadcasting Council met today for the first time. Rein Veidemann, deputy chairman of parliament's cultural commission, was elected its chairman, and Toivo Tasa, editor-in-chief of the VIKERKAAR magazine, was elected his deputy. One of the council's primary tasks is to appoint directors general for Estonian Radio and Estonian Television, within the next six months, to introduce a procedure for broadcasting advertisements and election campaigns and to define the conditions for the use of transmitters. The council must also endorse the constitutions for both Estonian Radio and Estonian Television.
