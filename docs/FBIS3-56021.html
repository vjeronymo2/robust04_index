Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Aleksandr Krylovich and Vladimir Taranov] [Text] Moscow, 12 Jan -- It is of fundamental importance that all the specific perpetrators of the unlawful, provocative, and unfriendly acts committed on 10 January by armed Latvian militia against the Russian ambassador and two Russian generals in Riga receive due punishment, Grigoriy Karasin, the director of the Russian Federation Foreign Affairs Ministry Information and Press Department, said today at a briefing at the Russian Federation Foreign Affairs Ministry Press Center He stressed that it was also essential for the Latvian authorities to take every measure necessary to prevent such highly dangerous incidents occurring in future. Grigoriy Karasin said that Moscow was awaiting a detailed official response by the Latvian side to Russia's actions.
