941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Daewoo Motor - Contracts and ventures South Korea's Daewoo Motor will export about 20,000 passenger cars to Romania's Automobile Criova next year in preparation for the completion of a joint-venture factory in Romania. Daewoo Motor, the nation's third largest car maker, will export its Lemans, Cielo and Espero to the Romanian company Automobile Criova.
