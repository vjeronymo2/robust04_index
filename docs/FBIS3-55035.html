Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Radio Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] Russian troops are to be finally withdrawn from Germany by 31 August 1994, said Colonel General Matvey Burlakov, commander in chief of the Western Group of Forces, at a meeting in Wuensdorf with Manfred Stolpe, president of Brandenburg land. During the meeting both sides agreed on joint efforts for the withdrawal of Russian troops from Brandenburg, including from Wuensdorf, where the headquarters of the Western Group of Forces is situated.
