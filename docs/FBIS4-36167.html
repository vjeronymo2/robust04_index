BFN [Excerpts] The House of Representatives met this morning in a session under Shaykh 'Abdallah Bin-Husayn al-Ahmar, the House speaker. [passage omitted] The House set up a committee comprised of the following members: Ahmad 'Abdallah al-Hajri, Hasan Muhammad al-Matari, 'Abd-al-Rahman al-Akwa', 'Abbas 'Ali Mu'ayyad, 'Abdallah 'Abdu Mahdi, Muhammad 'Abdallah (?al-Kilsi), Sultan Hizam al-'Atwani, Yahya Mansur Abu-Usba', 'Abdu Muhammad al-Janadi, and Muhammad Yahya Hammud al-Sharafi to discuss developments in our country and submit an urgent report to the House on the measures that should be taken by the parliament. The people's deputies remarked that delegations should be set up with representatives from the parliament, the government, and some popular institutions and political organizations to leave for fraternal and friendly countries and to meet with Arab and world organizations to explain the events in the Yemeni arena. The House will resume its meetings tomorrow, Wednesday, God willing.
