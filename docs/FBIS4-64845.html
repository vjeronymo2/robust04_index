BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- A second group of German citizens was evacuated from the Yemeni capital of Sanaa on Sunday [8 May] with the help of the Bundeswehr. According to the Defense Ministry in Bonn, an aircraft with 107 passengers on board landed in Djibouti, East Africa, in the afternoon, another aircraft also carrying 107 passengers is on its way. A third flight that was originally announced for Sunday will presumably not take place before Monday. According to a Foreign Ministry spokesperson, 280 Germans who want to leave Yemen appeared at the German Embassy in Sanaa throughout the day. Another special Lufthansa plane is expected in Frankfurt from Djibouti on Monday afternoon. It will have approximately 200 passengers on board.
