Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Apology by Renata Wrobel: "January Results of Public Opinion Research Institute for RZECZPOSPOLITA; Correction: Coalition Loses Support"] [Text] In my discussion yesterday of the results of an opinion poll conducted at the request of our daily by the Sopot Public Opinion Research Institute [PBS], I made an error. I mixed the table of November survey results with the table of results from research conducted in January, as a result of which most of the conclusions I drew are wrong. In today's paper we will present the correct data. I would like to apologize to the readers and PBS for misleading them.
