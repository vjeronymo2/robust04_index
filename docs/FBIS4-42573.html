BFN [Text] Government Spokesman Evangelos Venizelos described as positive the statement by Stephen Oxman, U.S. assistant secretary of state for European affairs, that human rights in Albania are somewhat violated lately. Venizelos justified his description of Oxman's statement by saying that the latest statement improves on the one by State Department Spokesman Michael McCurry. McCurry had said the United States has no evidence regarding the violation of human rights in Albania. Replying to a question, Venizelos said the Greek Government believes there is a climate of tension and insecurity which insults the human rights of the Greeks of Albania.
