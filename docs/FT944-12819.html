941031 FT 31 OCT 94 / Business Travel: Bombay chaos Overcrowding, breakdowns and a shortage of funds have turned rail commuting in and around Bombay into a daily nightmare. The 2,000 trains on Bombay's suburban network trundle nearly 5m people to work each day. Frustration has turned to violence five times in the past four months, with commuters wrecking property. The authorities plan to use longer trains and phase out level-crossings. But raising funds will be difficult. 'We may have to consider approaching the European markets, through the finance ministry, for cheaper funds,' said an official.
