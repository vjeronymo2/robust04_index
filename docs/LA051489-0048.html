May 14, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

SENIOR YEARS

Thanks for your articles and separate section ("The Special Years, 50 and Beyond: The Time of Your Life," May 4) that highlighted the 50-plus age group -- I'm one of them.

I really feel it is a neglected group of people, and the marketing people, if no one else, are missing a lot by not noticing us or mentioning our existence. I also felt your statistics about growing older were heartening and less negative and frightening than many I've seen. We can all use that good information.

MRS. D. BARKER

Escondido
