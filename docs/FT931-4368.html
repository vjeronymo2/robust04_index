930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / Observer: In the soup Tokyo airport police uncovered an illegal shell operation when they arrested 35-year-old Chopradit Pichest arriving from Bangkok. Besides having 121 eminently saleable rare turtles pouched in a special smuggler's vest, he had 14 more in a whisky bottle and a further 40 in a flight-bag. All were alive.
