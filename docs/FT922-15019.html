920403 FT 03 APR 92 / UK Company News: Brooks Service slumps to Pounds 0.6m The recession, a substantial reorganisation, and heavy expenditure on training and quality improvement hit Brooks Service Group in the 12 months to December 28. This textile and retail services combine saw sales fall to Pounds 23.8m (Pounds 25.4m), operating profit more than halve to Pounds 1.11m (Pounds 2.47m), and pre-tax profit slump from Pounds 1.51m to Pounds 645,000. Earnings per share were 3.6p (8.7p) and the final dividend is cut to 2.75p (3.91p) for a total of 4.05p (5.75p). The profit included Pounds 24,000 exceptional credits (Pounds 330,000 charges), being recompense of Pounds 175,000 for relocating a retail shop, less rationalisation costs in rental services, including redundancies, of Pounds 151,000.
