August 9, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY: CONSUMER NEWS

Air Rifles Recalled: Crosman Products Inc. is recalling nearly 53,000 of its Model 3100 air rifles, which shoot pellets and are used for target practice, because they may have a malfunctioning trigger mechanism, the Consumer Product Safety Commission said. No reports of accidental discharge have been reported. Owners should discontinue use and call (800) 836-3100 for instructions on returning the rifles.
