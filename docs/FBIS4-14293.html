BFN [Text] Cairo, Al Tayr [April] 28, JAMAHIRIYAH NEWS AGENCY -- Hasan al-Alfi the minister of interior in Egypt has announced that the next few hours shall witness the arrest of a group of Muslim brothers elements whom he described as terrorists. Al-Alfi confirmed that the elements group which was recently exposed has for its sole purpose the destruction of Egypt and undermining the reforms in every way. He indicated that large amounts of foreign currencies had been seized with the elements confirming their state of being agents for others. Al-Alfi also said that the elements of Muslim brothers are a group of mercenary terrorists undermining the Islam and distorting its image.
