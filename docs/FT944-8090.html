941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / Commons committee votes Benn off The Commons committee on privileges, which is investigating allegations of sleaze against Tory MPs, last night voted to remove Mr Tony Benn, the Labour MP for Chesterfield, from its membership, James Blitz writes. The decision came after Mr Benn decided to release full details of the last meeting of the committee to the press, even though its deliberations should have been private. The committee's decision will be debated on the floor of the Commons.
