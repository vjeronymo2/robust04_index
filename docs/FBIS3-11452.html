Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Buenos Aires,21 Mar (NA) -- Members of the Socialist Workers Movement (MST) and the Workers Party (PO) are waiting at the entrance of the Sheraton Hotel to "repudiate the presence in the country" of U.S. Vice President Albert Gore. Gore arrived in Argentina last night and is now attending the opening ceremony of the World Telecommunications Conference at the Libertador Hall of the Sheraton Hotel in Buenos Aires.
