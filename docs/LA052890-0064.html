May 28, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

IN COURT

A summary of Southern California-related business litigation developments during the past week.

'Dick Tracy' Extra Sues Walt Disney: An actress on Wednesday sued the Walt Disney Co., actor Warren Beatty and his Mulholland Productions Inc., claiming that she was injured on the "Dick Tracy" set when she was asked to "violently throw herself to the ground repeatedly." The Los Angeles Superior Court suit says Victoria Taft was working as an extra when she was asked to perform "footfalls," which are usually done by trained stunt artists. (Filed May 23, 1990. Case No. EC000266)
