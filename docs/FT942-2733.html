940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / International Company News: Ahold group report 14.7% profits rise Ahold, the Dutch-based international food retailing group, reported a 14.7 per cent increase in first-quarter net profit, slightly below the 15.4 per cent rise in the fourth quarter but higher than the 12.5 per cent gain posted in 1993 as a whole. Net profit rose to Fl 110.3m (Dollars 59.6m) from Fl 96.2m in the first quarter of 1993, on sales up 7.8 per cent at Fl 8.6bn. Operating results in the Netherlands rose by 3.2 per cent to Fl 90.4m. Operating profit in the US was up nearly 5 per cent at Dollars 50.5m.
