Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 12 (XINHUA) -- A ceremony to mark the 69th anniversary of Sun Yat-sen's death was held in Beijing today. Sun was the great leader of the revolution of 1911, the Chinese bourgeois democratic revolution, that overthrew the Qing dynasty. Among those who were present at the ceremony at Zhongshan Park were vice-chairmen of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress Ni Zhifu, Sun Qimeng, Lei Jieqiong, Cheng Xiyuan, Lu Jiaxi and Wu Jieping, and vice-chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Ye Xuanping, Zhao Puchu and Sun Fuling. The CPPCC National Committee, the Central Committee of the Kuomintang Revolutionary Committee, the United Front Department of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party and Beijing Municipal Government presented flower baskets in front of Sun's portrait.
