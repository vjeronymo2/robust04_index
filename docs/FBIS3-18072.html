Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- In shooting down four Serb combat aircraft over Bosnia-Herzegovina, the United Nations has demonstrated its resolve not to let the budding peace process be destroyed, according to Social Democratic Party [SPD] leader Hans- Ulrich Klose. Klose said in Bonn today that the SPD welcomed and supported the no-fly zone imposed by the UN. The SPD hopes that consistent compliance with UN resolutions will be taken seriously by all Bosnian parties, thus improving the possibility of political solutions, Klose said.
