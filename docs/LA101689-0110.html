October 16, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

ON THE SIDELINES;

DOLPHINS' JOHN OFFERDAHL SIGNS

Dolphins linebacker John Offerdahl signed a one-year contract and reported to camp today, ending a 2 1/2-month holdout that caused him to miss the team's first six games.

Terms of the deal were not immediately disclosed, but a source close to negotiations said the contract will permit Offerdahl to become a free agent after this season.

Offerdahl, a Pro Bowl selection in each of his three years in the NFL, had been in the option year of a contract that was to pay him $201,500 this year. The Dolphins offered him a four-year, $2.6-million deal in July, but when training camp began, team management refused to negotiate until Offerdahl reported.
