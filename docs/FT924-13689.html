921014 FT 14 OCT 92 / International Company News: Amdahl to shed some 900 jobs AMDAHL has become the latest US computer-maker to cut jobs. The California-based mainframe computer company is to reduce its workforce by about 9 per cent, or some 900 people, next month. It said the cuts were 'in response to a recent decline in demand for the company's mainframe computers'. Last month Amdahl said it expected a loss for the third quarter. 'We are witnessing a curtailment of capital expenditures and a deferral of customer buying decisions because of the difficult economic times,' said Mr Joseph Zemke, president and chief executive.
