Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 24 (KCNA) -- Railway workers in the DPRK keep effecting innovations. The general bureaux of railways carried hundreds of thousands more tons of freight than their plan in recent one month. The transport turnover of the general bureau of Western railways was 207,400 tons more than the assignment in the period. The general bureau of Eastern railways and the Kaechon general bureau of railways hauled freight 20 to 30 percent more than the rated traction tonnage by scrupulously organizing and commanding transport and reducing the stoppage time of wagons. Haeju Chongnyon, Unpit, Paechon and many other railway stations have hit their first-quarter assignments ahead of schedule.
