Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Last night, Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev held a telephone conversation with the U.S. secretary of state during which the two men discussed the problem of a Bosnian settlement. Galina Sidorova, adviser to the Russian foreign minister, said that air strikes against any of the warring sides in Bosnia-Herzegovina would be justified if they prompted these sides to accept a compromise. However, she feels that in present circumstances, such strikes would only lead to a further brutalization of the conflict.
