December 31, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

A FINANCIAL PLANNER CAN BE A GOLD MINE

I feel that the article about financial planners ("Financial Planner, Broker Offer Different Services," Dec. 3) in your special section deprecates financial planners by showing a negative and distrustful attitude without giving any mention of the many worthwhile benefits of utilizing such a professional.

As the owner of a retail showroom, I have found the complexity of coordinating my attorney, accountant, investments, tax planning, personal health insurance and group health benefits an almost impossible task without the guidance of my financial planner.

My financial planner implements the various financial products he recommends and sells insurance policies. My time is much more effective while utilizing a financial planner.

Good financial planners should be well paid; they are literally worth their weight in gold.

NICHOLAS PAUL HERTNECK

Laguna Beach
