930422 FT 22 APR 93 / Clwyd renews Granada attack LABOUR'S shadow National Heritage secretary Mrs Anne Clwyd has renewed her attack on Granada Television for its performance since regaining its ITV licence. The Independent Television Commission rejected a complaint from Mrs Clwyd that Granada was in breach of its licence. The Labour spokeswoman has asked Sir George Russell, chairman of the ITC, to look again at the issue.
