BFN [Text] Warsaw, June 24 -- The government won the vote in the Sejm on Friday on acceptance of its information on the "Strategy for Poland" by 244 votes for acceptance, 87 for rejection, 13 abstentions and 116 not voting. The "strategy" is the government's economic programme for the years 1994-97. Deputies from the government coalition parties, the Democratic Left Alliance (SLD) and the Polish Peasant Party (PSL) voted for acceptance as did two Union of Labour (UP), one Non-Party Reform Bloc (BBWR) and one independent deputy. No deputy from the Freedom Union (UW), the Confederation for an Independent Poland (KPN), the German minority or the Polish Socialist Party voted for accepting the information.
