June 18, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

B OF A'S BIG COMEBACK

Regarding the June 4 story, "Back from the Brink at B of A":

What a superb testimonial to talent, skill, experience and training of personnel at Wells Fargo. Without the direction provided by the former Wells Fargo cadre, Bank of America might well have drowned in the quicksand of ignominy.

W. D. MURPHY

Canoga Park
