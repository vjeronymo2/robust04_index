940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / World Trade News: S Africans hail visit by Gatt chief The National Economic Forum, South Africa's tripartite body made up of government, business and labour, yesterday said the brief visit to the country by Mr Peter Sutherland, director general of Gatt, had consolidated the country's strengthened ties to the world trade body, writes Matthew Curtin in Johannesburg. It said the visit had set the country on the path of active participation in Gatt's planned successor, the World Trade Organisation. Mr Sutherland's visit included meetings with President F W de Klerk and Mr Trevor Manuel, head of the ANC's economics department.
