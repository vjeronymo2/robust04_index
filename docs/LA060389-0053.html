June 3, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

CHUNK OF CONCRETE HITS CAR, KILLS N.Y. MOTORIST

A chunk of concrete fell from the underside of a city expressway, killing a motorist when it crashed into the roof of his car, police said. Benjamin P. Bernstein, 49, died Friday about three hours after the 10-by-5-foot chunk of concrete fell from FDR Drive.
