BFN [Text] Ljupco Georgievski, leader of the Internal Macedonian Revolutionary Organization [VMRO]-Democratic Party for Macedonian National Unity, who is visiting Bulgaria, had a meeting with Filip Dimitrov and Stefan Savov, leaders of the National Coordinating Council and the parliamentary faction of the Union of Democratic Forces [SDS], respectively. Ljupco Georgievski expressed satisfaction with and gratitude for the consistent position of the SDS on asserting the independence of Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] in the international arena. The common position of the participants in the meeting, against the background of the recent Greek actions regarding Macedonia, was that the balance in the Balkans must be preserved by all means.
