Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Negotiations Between Ukraine and Azerbaijan"] [Text] On the eve of the New Year, governmental delegations of Ukraine and Azerbaijan held negotiations in Kiev. They discussed an extensive circle of problems concerning the development and intensification of the interaction between the two countries in the economic, financial, and other spheres. An agreement was signed on trade and economic cooperation for 1994 and an accord was reached on holding negotiations at the level of presidents and parliamentarians at the beginning of 1994 for the purpose of further development of interstate relations. The Ukrainian delegation was headed by Vice Prime Minister V. Shmarov and the Azerbaijani one by L. Abbasov, first deputy prime minister.
