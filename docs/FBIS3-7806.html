Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Article by Dj.B. and M.P.: "The Richest Oil Field in Serbia"] [Text] Turija, 25 Feb -- Today's launching of regular production in the first phase of the "Turija North 1" oil field in the vicinity of Srbobran marks the 45th anniversary of the Serbian oil company "Naftagas." The "Turija North 1" oil field is the largest of its kind discovered in Serbia over the past three decades. According to "Naftagas" manager Srboljub Stankovic, Turija and the eastern part of Srbobran literally stand on oil. The subterranean field, situated between two points at a depth of 2,000 meters is coated in a layer of oil 47 meters thick. Some $60 million has so far been invested in the "Turija North 1" oil field. This year, another $32 million is needed for the second phase of construction, while the third phase -- the construction of transportation systems and oil and gas pipelines calls for another $11 million. Around 86,000 metric tons of oil were produced in this area last year. This year, the production of 120,000 tons of oil is planned. Vladimir Zivanovic, republican minister for energy, was among the many guests who attended today's celebrations.
