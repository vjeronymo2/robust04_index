BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova] [Text] Moscow, 20 Apr -- "A balance between the executive and representative powers is taking shape." This was stated by Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin at a meeting with the heads of administrations of Russian krays and oblasts, when he was summing up the results of elections to local bodies of power that had been held in a number of regions. According to Boris Yeltsin, "the elections were normal, though not everywhere was their quality good." In June there will be elections in the rest of the regions. "We must continue work to truly provide a real division of power," stressed Boris Yeltsin.
