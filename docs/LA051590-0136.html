May 15, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

CHAPMAN GAINS 2 BASKETBALL PLAYERS

Two community college players agreed Monday to attend Chapman College on basketball scholarships in the fall.

The players -- Zlatko Josic, a 6-foot-5 forward who averaged 17 points and nine rebounds a game in two seasons at El Camino College, and Al Jackson, who averaged 12 points and 4.5 assists in two seasons as a point guard for West Valley College in Saratoga, Calif. -- each transferred to four-year schools last season.

Josic, who had limited playing time at Clemson last season, will have one year of eligibility remaining. Jackson, who transferred to Cal State Northridge but was academically ineligible, will have two years of eligibility remaining.
