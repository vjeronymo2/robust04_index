BFN [Text] Leader of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia (LDPR) Vladimir Zhirinovskiy sharply criticized the decision of the State Duma to transfer to the mandate commission the appeal of acting Prosecutor General Aleksey Ilyushenko asking for the consent of the Duma for bringing Zhirinovskiy to justice on warmongering and inciting ethnic hatred charges. Zhirinovskiy stated that Ilyushenko himself was "nobody and is sitting in his office only because the president wants it." Ilyushenko motivated his appeal by the results of the preliminary investigations due to the publication of Zhirinovskiy's book "The Last Push for the South." Ilyushenko referred to provisions in the Criminal Code against warmongering and inciting ethnic hatred.
