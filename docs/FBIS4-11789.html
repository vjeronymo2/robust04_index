BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres this evening spoke with the foreign ministers of Italy and Norway about the international presence in which their countries will participate. Peres explained that the presence is not military, but rather civilian and economic in nature. Our political correspondent Udi Segal reports the two ministers expressed their countries' willingness to participate in the international observers force. The foreign minister will summon the Norwegian, Danish, and Italian ambassadors in the next few days in order to discuss the details.
