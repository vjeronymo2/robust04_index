920708 FT 08 JUL 92 / World News in Brief: Note of protest struck over Queen The design of a new plastic-coated Australian Dollars 5 banknote has stirred opposition in Australia. The new note, held above left by David Wallace of a Sydney coin exchange, replaces the design featuring Caroline Chisholm (below), a 19th century Australian social worker. Gordon Andrews, designer of the original note, said the new design could 'look a little bit silly' in a few years if Australia becomes a republic.
