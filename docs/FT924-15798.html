921002 FT 02 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Board changes at CST Jupiter Tyndall has proposed the appointment of three new directors for CST Emerging Asia Trust, which it manages and in which it holds a 29.9 per cent shareholding. They are Mr John Craig, deputy chairman of Jupiter, Mr Michael Heathcoat Amory, Jupiter's managing director and Mr James Roe, a director of Jupiter European Investment Trust. If the new directors are appointed, Jupiter intends to discuss proposals with the new board to cut the discount of the trust's market capitalisation to its net asset value.
