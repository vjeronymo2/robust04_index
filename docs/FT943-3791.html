940912 FT 12 SEP 94 / Company News (This Week): United Biscuits - Other companies The UK biscuits and snacks manufacturer is likely to report on Thursday interim pre-tax profits of about Pounds 75m (Dollars 116.25), compared with Pounds 70.8m a year earlier before a Pounds 62.7m gain on disposals. An increase in the dividend is unlikely. Keebler, its US subsidiary, will show some progress.
