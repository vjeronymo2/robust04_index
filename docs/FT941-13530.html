940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Christies disposes of leasehold interest Christies International has completed the disposal of its leasehold interest in Sussex Mansions, 63-85 Old Brompton Road, South Kensington, London. The property has 40 furnished flats on short lettings and 10 flats on long underleases with protected tenancies. Consideration is Pounds 9.85m cash.
