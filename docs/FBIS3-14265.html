Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanaa, 24 Mar (INA)--A Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] source has denied that it has decided to close its offices in the northern governorates and to withdraw its personnel and return them to southern governorates. In statements published today, the source described as unfounded fabrication reports published by the London-based Saudi paper AL- HAYAH on this subject. The source stressed the YSP's unity orientations and adherence to the Yemen's unity.
