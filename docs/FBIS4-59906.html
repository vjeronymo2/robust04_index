BFN [Text] President of the Republic Liamine Zeroual has issued a legal decree based on the recommendation of the justice minister stipulating the pardon of prisoners who were involved in cases not related to crimes of terrorism, sabotage, and state security. The decree stipulates the cancellation of the prison sentences for those who were sentenced to three months' imprisonment, which allows the release of about 1,000 prisners. It also stipulates the partial reduction of prison sentences ranging between 4 and 7 months.
