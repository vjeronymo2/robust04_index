February 23, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

* Transamerica Life Cos., Los Angeles, has named Holly Kolinski vice president-product and market development, Transamerica Reinsurance. Kolinski previously was vice president-product development and international sales at Citibank.

Oscar (Ozzie) Scofield has been named vice president-sales, Transamerica Reinsurance, and chairman of Osborn Laboratories, an insurance testing services lab. Scofield previously was president and chief executive at General Reassurance Corp., Stamford, Conn.
