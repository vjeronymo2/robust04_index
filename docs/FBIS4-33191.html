BFN [From the "Early Morning" newscast] [Text] Diego Fernandez de Cevallos, National Action Party [PAN] presidential candidate, has reiterated to the media what he has been saying all along: he will not participate in the debate among all the presidential candidates. On 28 and 29 May, he said the following: [Begin Fernandez recording] I believe that if there is time for multiple debates, it makes no sense for all of us to meet on the same day and at the same time, especially when the objective of the debate has already been expressed. I repeat, I think there is enough time; it makes no sense for us to meet at the same time and place. [end recording]
