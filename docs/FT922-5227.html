920601 FT 01 JUN 92 / International Company News: Coca-Cola to invest Dollars 1bn in E Europe COCA-Cola said it plans to invest nearly Dollars 1bn with its bottling affiliates to expand business in central and eastern Europe until the end of 1995, with over Dollars 200m targeted for Poland, Reuter reports. The announcement by the world's largest soft drinks company came as it opened a Dollars 30m plant in Gdynia, Poland. Coca-Cola's biggest investment is targeted for eastern Germany, where it previously announced plans to spend Dollars 450m over several years.
