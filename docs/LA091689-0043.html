September 16, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

St. Bernard 21, St. Francis 3 -- St. Bernard (1-1) capitalized on four turnovers and cruised to an easy victory at St. Francis. Two plays after Jon Acuna fumbled deep in St. Francis territory with a minute to play in the first quarter, Terry Sullivan ran four yards for the first of his two touchdowns.

Trailing, 7-0, in the third quarter, St. Francis (0-2) scored its only points on a 33-yard field goal by Kevin Hurley. St. Francis was limited to 77 yards passing and 33 yards on the ground. TODD JACOBSON
