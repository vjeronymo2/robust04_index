Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Dec (MENA)--Four police sentinels from the al-Qusiyah district force in the Asyut Governorate were killed today when unidentified men opened fire at them. In reporting this, a security official in the Interior Ministry said that citizen Na'im Bakhit Hanna, who happened to be on the scene, was also hit by the indiscriminate firing and succumbed to his wounds. The source said that the criminals fired at the four sentinels as they were going to work at the police post in Bani Qurrah and seized their weapons. The commanders of the Asyut Security Directorate, the Public Security, the State Security intelligence, and experts of the criminal laboratory went to the scene of the incident to investigate. Security forces are searching the area to arrest the criminals.
