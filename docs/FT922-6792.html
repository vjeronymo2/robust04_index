920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 9m orders awarded to John Sisk JOHN SISK & SON, the UK-based construction subsidiary of the Sisk Group, has won contracts totalling Pounds 9m in April. The largest of the batch is a meat processing plant in Buckingham for Hireglobal worth about Pounds 4.6m. A further contract has been secured for work at the South Bank studios of LWT.
