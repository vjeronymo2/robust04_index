BFN [Text] The Ukrainian cabinet of ministers has decided on resuming work at one of the enterprises in the town of Pripet, which is located inside the 30 km exclusion zone around the Chernobyl nuclear power station. This decision was made in connection with the need to provide for the functioning of Ukraine's nuclear power industry and meet its requirements for spares and nonstandard equipment. Work at Pripet's industrial enterprises was halted on 26 April 1986 -- the day of the disaster at Chernobyl nuclear power station.
