March 29, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

BAD REVIEWS: Three Orange County congressmen -- Reps. Dannemeyer, Dornan and Rohrabacher -- were the subject Wednesday of the nationally syndicated Washington column written by Jack Anderson and Dale Van Atta. . . . The three "are beginning to make nuisances of themselves," the writers said. . . . "Their specialty is moral outrage" and "their pushy style robs them of the credibility it takes to wield power." And while they're popular at home, "the trio often flops in Washington." Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
