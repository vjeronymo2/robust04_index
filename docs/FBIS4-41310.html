BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Igor Borisenko, Pavel Vanichkin, and and Oleg Velichko] [Text] Washington, 23 June -- Today's meeting at the White House with the U.S. President Bill Clinton was "very substantive," Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin told a news conference. He said that during their conversation they touched on matters connected with the upcoming meeting in Naples in July of the heads of the leading industrially advanced countries, which will be attended by Russian President Boris Yeltsin.
