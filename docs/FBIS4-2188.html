BFN [Excerpt] The digital communications network which provides various modernized communications means has recently been completed and made available in the province. This is an indication that the comprehensive communications capacity of the province's postal and telecommunications departments has joined the world's high ranks. The digital communications network is a new communications method combining computers with modern communications technology. The digital communications network now available in the province is a network with diverse functions and group exchanges and is of a high world level. With this equipment, the province is able to conduct digital communications with all cities at or above the city and prefectural levels in the province, with 30 capital cities, most cities at or above the city and prefectural levels, and some cities at the county level in the country, and with 23 countries and regions in the world, including the United States, Japan, France, and Germany.
