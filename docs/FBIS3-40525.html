Article Type:CSO [Text]Objectives Based on revised mid-term plans for the defense capacity provisions (1991-95), efforts will be made to complete one stage of technical research and development. To contribute to improving support at the qualitative level of defense capacity, in addition to seriously examining remarkable advances in the latest technology, the promotion of cooperative relationships between Japan and the United States in research and development will be considered and the technical R&D position will be refined.
