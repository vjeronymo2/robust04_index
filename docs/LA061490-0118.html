June 14, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Dick Tracy Everywhere: If you can't get into the movie, you can watch classic "Dick Tracy" films on TV.

The Family Channel will air a Dick Tracy fest tonight and Friday at 7 p.m. to coincide with Friday's release of the new film "Dick Tracy." The festival features four Dick Tracy classics from the 1940s beginning with "Dick Tracy," followed by "Dick Tracy Versus Cueball," both starring Morgan Conway. On Friday, "Dick Tracy's Dilemma" and "Dick Tracy Meets Gruesome" with Ralph Byrd will finish the festival. BETH KLEID
