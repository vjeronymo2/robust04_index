941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / Biffen to retire from Commons Mr John Biffen, a long-serving former cabinet minister, said yesterday that he would leave the Commons at the next election. He has been a Shropshire MP since 1961. Mr Biffen, 64, became Treasury chief secretary after the Tory victory in the 1979 election. He was made trade secretary in 1981 and leader of the Commons in 1982. He was dismissed from the government after the 1987 election. Observer, Page 15
