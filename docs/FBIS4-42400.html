BFN [Unattributed article: "Clinton at Quirinale Palace"] [Excerpt] (ANSA) Rome, June 2 -- United States President Bill Clinton and his wife, Hillary, went to the presidential Quirinale Palace at mid-morning for talks with Italian head of State Oscar Luigi Scalfaro. Clinton was met on his arrival by Scalfaro, accompanied by his daughter Marianna, and after the traditional playing of respective national anthems and a review of the guards retired in the Quirinale for their talks. The American president began his day in Rome with a jog in the central Villa Borghese park, located close to the embassy residence of Villa Taverna, along with the Washington ambassador to Rome, Reginald Bartholomew.
