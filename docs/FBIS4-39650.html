BFN [Text] Copenhagen, Denmark, 8 Jun -- Ukrainian Ambassador to Denmark Konstantin Masik and Danish Defense Minister Hans Haekkerup met to address questions of security and stability in Europe and cooperation between Ukraine and Denmark within the framework of the "Partnership for Peace" program. Mr. Masik informed the defense minister of the situation in Crimea and the work of the Ukrainian and Russian delegations to settle the issue of the Black Sea Fleet. Mssrs Haekkerup and Masik exchanged views on the results of the work at the latest NATO session and the possibilities of that organization's expansion.
