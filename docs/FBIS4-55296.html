BFN [Text] Warsaw, 12 May (PAP)--At 0900 today the strike continued in only seven hard coal mines, but production is likely to be resumed today. A total of 19 mines were on strike before 0600; production was halted in 13 others and reduced in another six. Miners were still occupying the State Hard Coal Agency building, said its deputy chairman, Henryk Chroszcz. Strikes continue in Piast and Brzeszcze mines (Nadwislanska hard-coal company) as well as in Jastrzebie, Pniowek, Moszczenica, Borynia, and Zofiowka mines (Jastrzebie hard-coal company).
