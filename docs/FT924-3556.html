921207 FT 07 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Crossroads Oil Pounds 107,000 in loss For the six months ending September 30 the USM-quoted Crossroads Oil Group incurred an unchanged operating loss of Pounds 80,112. The after-tax loss emerged at Pounds 106,903. That compared with profits of Pounds 267,368 which included exceptional revenue of Pounds 377,142. Losses per share worked through at 0.25p (earnings 0.75p).
