September 23, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

RAIDER MOTTO: HAVE BALL, WILL TRAVEL

An article (Sept. 14) on the Raiders' possible move to Sacramento stated that a Sacramento audience booed suggestions that the city's money might be better spent on housing for the homeless, helping drug addicts and building libraries.

Seems as though Sacramento citizens, like so many others in many U.S. cities, consider only financial gain and never the human aspects.

HENRY N. EHRLICH, Los Angeles
