Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Armenian president has stressed the importance of his country's relations with the Islamic Republic of Iran and has called for the broadening of bilateral relations and cooperation. According to IRNA, a verbal message from President Hashemi- Rafsanjani was conveyed by the director general of the CIS desk at our Foreign Ministry during his meeting with Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan yesterday. In the course of this meeting, regional developments and the Islamic Republic of Iran's efforts for the establishment of peace in the Nagorno-Karabakh region were assessed. The director general of the CIS desk at our Foreign Ministry also met the Armenian vice president and the foreign minister and delivered a written message from Dr. Velayati to the latter. During the meeting, the Armenian foreign minister stressed that the Islamic Republic of Iran can play an important role in resolving the Nagorno-Karabakh problem. He called for our country's cooperation in containing this crisis.
