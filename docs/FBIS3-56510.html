Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, 14 Jan (ARMENPRES) -- The Armenian Foreign Minister Vahan Papazyan yesterday received Morteza Bank, head of the Eastern Europe and CIS Department at the Iranian Foreign Ministry, who is on a visit to Yerevan. They discussed matters pertaining to the development of political, economic, and cultural cooperation between Armenia and Iran. Mr. Bank presented a message from the Iranian Foreign Minister `Ali Akbar Velayati in which Armenia's efforts for the establishment of peace in the Karabakh conflict zone was particularly highly assessed. The Iranian diplomat also conveyed a verbal message from Iranian President Hashemi-Rafsanjani to Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan, who joined the meeting later on.
