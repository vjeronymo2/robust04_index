930122 FT 22 JAN 93 / Observer: Prophetable? As the ultimate in cutting out the middle-man, Israel's Bezeq phone company is offering a direct line to God via facsimile. Just call Jerusalem 972-2-612-222 and your fax will be placed in the crevices of Judaism's holy of holies, the Wailing Wall. The company profits solely from the faxing. Onward delivery is free of charge.
