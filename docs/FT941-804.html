940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / Bosnians meet over peace pact Bosnia's Moslem-dominated parliament yesterday met to ratify an agreement to form a federation with the Croats in a move Mr Haris Silajdzic, the Bosnian foreign minister, said was a 'defeat for Serbian aggression'. The meeting coincided with Serb charges that the Bosnian army had attacked Serb-held towns in northern Bosnia.
