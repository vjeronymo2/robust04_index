940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / Humberside fights abolition plan Humberside county council is applying for judicial review of the Local Government Commission proposal that it should be abolished. Avon, Cleveland, Derbyshire and Somerset have all already challenged similar plans to replace them with all-purpose unitary authorities, although the Association of District Councils has criticised this as a delaying tactic and a waste of council taxpayers' money.
