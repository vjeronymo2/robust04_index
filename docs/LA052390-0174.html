May 23, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

PRINCE OUT OF NEWLYWEDS' SUITE

Prince is used to getting royal treatment, but one Dutch couple decided to put a stop to the divine rights of rock stars.

A Dutch court has ruled in favor of Marcel Soek and Merith Gijtenbeek, who sued the posh Hotel des Indes for allegedly giving their VIP suite reservation to the singer.

The court ordered the hotel to pay a $13,369 penalty unless it gives the Imperial Suite to the couple for their June 2 wedding night, the couple and the hotel management said today.

The couple booked the suite in March, but "the hotel told us after several weeks that the room was going to be given instead to an American pop star along with 10 other royal suites," Soek said.

"They didn't tell us who the star was, but the lawyer slipped the other day in court and said it was Prince," Soek said.

Hotel Des Indes General Manager Bernard Felix said the hotel has not decided whether to appeal the ruling.
