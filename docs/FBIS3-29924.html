Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 24 (KCNA) -- Chinese Ambassador to Korea Qiao Zonghuai arranged a party Wednesday evening at his embassy on the occasion of the 35th anniversary of the conclusion of the intergovernmental agreements on cultural cooperation and air services between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the People's Republic of China. Present on invitation were chairman of the Committee for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries Chong Chun-ki, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Cho Kyu-il, Vice-Minister of Culture and Art Kim Chong-ho, Director of the Civil Aviation General Administration Kim Yo-ung and other officials concerned. On hand were officials of the Chinese Embassy. Speeches were made there.
