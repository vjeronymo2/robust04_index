April 21, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

UC IRVINE SIGNS 6-10 KANSAS PROSPECT

Dan Augulis, a 6-foot-10, 200-pound senior at Shawnee Mission South High School in Leawood, Kan., signed a letter of intent Friday to play basketball at UC Irvine.

Augulis averaged 10 points and six rebounds last season and his team went 24-0 and won the Kansas 6-A state title.
