920801 FT 01 AUG 92 / Olympic News In Brief: Fresh air for runners Barcelona's Olympic traffic regulations are a breath of fresh air for marathon runners. Pollution in the city centre could fall by 15 per cent now that cars have been banned from the most central streets to stop snarl-ups, according to a study being conducted by a Barcelona university in collaboration with German and US scientists. Competitors, already battling with sweltering heat and high humidity, earlier complained the sea was filthy and swimming with debris.
