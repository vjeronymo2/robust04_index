BFN [Text] Long An has invested tens of billion of dong to build a medium-voltage power line linked with the national electric network. The local people have contributed 50 percent of the construction cost. At present, 130 of the 180 villages and wards of the province use electricity supplied by the national grid. All 32 villages of Duc Hoa and Chau Thanh districts have been provided with electricity for home use and production. Long An is striving to supply electricity to all villages in the province in 1995 using its own budget, money collected from electric fees, and funds contributed by the local people.
