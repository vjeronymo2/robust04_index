November 8, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Fujitsu to Unveil Smallest Pocket Phone: The world's smallest pocket cellular phone -- a 10.2-ounce package slightly larger than a box of deluxe cigarettes -- is expected to be unveiled today in Los Angeles by Fujitsu America Inc. Although it is only slightly smaller than Mitsubishi and Motorola models, its aggressive $1,195 price is sure to bring new competition to the pocket phone market, already the fastest growing segment of the cellular equipment industry.
