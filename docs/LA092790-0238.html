September 27, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

STATE;

BAKERSFIELD TICKET HITS JACKPOT

One lucky ticket had all six winning numbers in Wednesday night's "Lotto 6-53" game and claimed the $14.8-million jackpot, lottery officials said.

The jackpot-winning ticket was purchased in Bakersfield, they said.

There were six tickets with five numbers plus the bonus number. They each won $108,506 and divide a prize pool of $651,036. A total of 206 tickets had five of six numbers, each winning $3,160. They shared a prize pool of $651,034.
