930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / International Capital Markets: Hong Kong Futures seat for Morgan Stanley MORGAN Stanley Asia is to take a seat on the Hong Kong Futures Exchange (HKFE) and has also been accepted in principle as a clearing member of the exchange, AP-DJ reports from Hong Kong. The group said it would be able to begin its own execution and clearing facilities in Hong Kong within the next few weeks. Morgan Stanley recently acquired a second seat on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange. According to Mr John Wadsworth, the securities group's managing director, the move reflected the firm's rising commitment to Hong Kong and the region.
