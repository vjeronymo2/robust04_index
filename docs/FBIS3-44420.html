Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The Second Plenary Session of the Eighth Qinghai Provincial CPC Committee was opened in Xining yesterday. As the first important meeting held by the provincial party committee since the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee, the eighth provincial party committee second plenary session shoulders heavy responsibilities and is to accomplish the following three major tasks: 1. To further study and implement to the letter the spirit of the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee, as well as heighten understanding and unify ideology in this connection. 2. To sum up the province's work this year and plan and arrange for the province's work in 1994, heighten understanding of both importance and significance of the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee, and strive to unify ideology among all comrades in accordance with the spirit of the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee decision. 3. To emancipate the mind, seek truth from facts, brace up, open up new vistas, forge ahead, and make unreserved efforts to mobilize, organize, and guide broad masses of cadres and people of all nationalities in Qinghai to attain each and every grand goal laid down at the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee. On the evening of 21 December, the eighth provincial party committee second plenary session held a preparatory meeting at which provincial party committee Secretary Yin Kesheng delivered an important in which he urged all comrades attending the meeting to concentrate efforts on conscientiously studying and discussing the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee decision, further heighten understanding and improve comprehension of the issue of building a socialist market economic structure, more clearly view and define new situations and new tasks facing Qinghai's reform and economic development in 1994, and strive to seek a correct understanding of both the current situation and reform process. Also attending the plenary session preparatory meeting were Tian Chengping, Cai Zhulin, and Sang Jiejia, provincial party committee deputy secretaries, as well as Chen Yanqin, Wang Hanmin, and Yao Xiangcheng, provincial party committee standing committee members.
