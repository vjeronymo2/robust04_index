930821 FT 21 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Abtrust Scotland offloads Seaforth Abtrust Scotland Investment Company, will realise Pounds 1.7m from the sale of Seaforth Maritime group to Brown & Root, part of Halliburton Company, in a deal worth about Pounds 8m. The profit to Abtrust will be about Pounds 1.6m on its original investment of Pounds 100,000 and represents an overall internal rate of return of 51 per cent. The disposal will add about 1.3p to Seaforth's net asset value.
