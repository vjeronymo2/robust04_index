July 5, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

Correction Appended

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: MONTEREY PARK;

OFFICIALS' SALARIES CUT

The City Council last week cut the annual salaries of the assistant city manager and the administrative assistant to the city manager by $24,356 and $11,400 respectively.

The two positions are vacant.

During the 1990-91 fiscal year, the assistant city manager will earn $64,596, and the administrative assistant will earn $34,200. Meanwhile, City Manager Mark Lewis' salary will increase from $87,820 to $93,708.
