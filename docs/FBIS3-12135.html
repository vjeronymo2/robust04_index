Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Al-Zaqaziq, 26 Feb (MENA)--Awqaf Minister Dr. Muhammad 'Ali Mahjub has condemned the massacre that occurred during the dawn prayers yesterday in the al-Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron, West Bank. He called for placing the Jerusalem and the al-Aqsa Mosque issues on the list of urgent and essential issues that require a speedy solution. He appealed to the Islamic world, which is passing through one of its worst stages, particularly in Afghanistan and Somalia, to close ranks and stand up to preserve its standing and prestige. This statement was made at a popular rally held in the village of al-Sama'inah in Faqus District in al-Sharqiyah Governorate last evening. He noted that the goal of his tours of Egyptian governorates is to explain the facts to the people and to enlighten them about the dangers facing Egypt and threatening its security and stability. He noted that Egypt's stability ensures the stability of the Arab world, as Egypt is the safeguard of security and peace of the Islamic world.
