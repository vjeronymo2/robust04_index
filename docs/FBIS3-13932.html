Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The newspaper AL-SAFIR has reported that the security forces arrested a fugitive suspect involved in blowing up the Sayyidat al-Najat Church in al-Zuq. This brings the number of detainees who have confessed to participating in the crime to five, including a monk. A search is under way for six fugitive suspects, including the official in charge of the operation, who escaped to the occupied border zone. AL-SAFIR adds that the detainees confessed to receiving their orders from two officers in the Mosad, the Israeli intelligence service. Investigations continue to determine how much the organizations to which the detainees belong know about the operation. According to AL-SAFIR, the official in charge of the operation is Pierre 'Ayn, a demolition expert who worked as an aide to a former Lebanese Forces official and who escaped to the occupied border zone along with two of his assistants.
