Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] AKHIR KHABAR has learned that the security forces apprehended 30 members of Fatah-the Revolutionary Council [led by Abu Nidal] in Amman on Wednesday [16 February], and that 'Abbas al-Qaysi and Fathi Abu-Salimah were among those apprehended. The wife of one of those apprehended informed AKHIR KHABAR that the security forces had asked 'Abbas al-Qaysi, a Amman-based Fatah-Revolutionary Council leader and former prisoner, to report to them. Meanwhile, they searched his house and detained him, along with others, most of whom were veteran prisoners who spent many long years in jail. They had been freed in early 1990.
