BFN [Text] Belgrade, May 10 (TANJUG) -- Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, president of the Liberal-Democratic Party of Russia, who was in Belgrade since Sunday, left the Yugoslav capital on Tuesday morning. The Serbian National Renewal (SNO), a non-parliamentary party which orgnaized the visit, said that the Russian delegation had left for Moscow, via Budapest, to attend a session of the Russian parliament's lower house (Duma) on Wednseday. On Monday Zhirinovskiy addressed a rally marking Victory Day in the centre of Belgrade.
