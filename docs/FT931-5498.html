930305 FT 05 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Ferry Pickering falls to Pounds 1.93m Ferry Pickering, the publishing, printing and packaging group, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.53m for the 16 months to December 31, compared with a restated Pounds 2.26m for the previous 12 months. Turnover for the 16 months was Pounds 38.9m against Pounds 29.1m, and earnings per share came out at 9.77p (12.68p). A final dividend of 2p is recommended for a total of 7.5p (5.5p). The figures were produced in accordance with accounting standard FRS 3.
