BFN [Text] Tripoli, Al Maa' [May] 25, JAMAHIRIYAH NEWS AGENCY-- The international editor of the Jamahiriyah News Agency commented on the lecture given by Anthony Lake, the national security adviser to the American president, to the Institute of Middle Eastern Policy on the 17th of Al Maa'[May] which reaffirmed the American policy of domination in the Arab region as well as the basis for this policy which revolves around attempts to dominate the resources of the region while guaranteeing the security of the Israeli occupiers who are a tool for American imperialist policy in the region. The editor said that the comments by the advisor to the American president demonstrated the mad frenzy of the imperialists resulting from their military domination of the world. The editor said that the ideas which came out in this lecture truly expresses the new wave of colonialism, occupation and invasion which has prevailed. He said this showed that there was a dreary future on the horizon which threatens peace, security and stability and will abort the peaceful process of development of all nations.
