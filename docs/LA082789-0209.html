August 27, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ON THE COVER

Cable television strikes up "A Conversation With Dinah" this week.

Dinah Shore, who has hosted talk shows on network TV and in syndication, returns for a new round of celebrity chats Monday at 5 p.m. on the Nashville Network.

In the debut of her half-hour series, Shore talks with Nancy Reagan. It's the first interview the former First Lady has given since leaving the White House. Other guests this week are Betty White (Tuesday); John Forsythe (Wednesday); Angie Dickinson (Thursday); Rosemary Clooney (Friday) and Jack Lemmon (Saturday at 7 p.m.).

Starting next week, the series will be shown Tuesdays and Thursdays at 5 p.m. and Saturdays at 7 p.m.
