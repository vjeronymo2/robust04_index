920629 FT 29 JUN 92 / World News in Brief: White sheet protest Thousands of people on the Italian island of Sardinia hung bedsheets from windows to protest against the kidnap five months ago of eight-year-old Farouk Kassam. The kidnappers recently sent Farouk's family part of one of the boy's ears and demanded L7bn (Pounds 3.2m) ransom.
