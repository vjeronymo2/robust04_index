930422 FT 22 APR 93 / International Company News: Deficit for Polaroid POLAROID, the US photography group, suffered a first-quarter net loss of Dollars 122.8m, or Dollars 2.63 a share, including an after-tax charge of Dollars 125.7m for accounting changes, writes Karen Zagor. A year earlier, it earned Dollars 6.2m, or 13 cents. Sales rose to Dollars 468.4m in the latest quarter from Dollars 236m. Estimated net income, excluding special items, was Dollars 2.9m, or 6 cents, in the 1993 quarter. Polaroid expects start-up costs for new product to affect its operating results this year.
