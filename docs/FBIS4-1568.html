BFN [Text] Beijing, March 30 (XINHUA) -- Chinese President Jiang Zemin encouraged major Hong Kong-based newspapers and magazines to contribute even more to the prosperity and peaceful transition of the territory. The president made the remark here today during a meeting with the visiting chief executives of the Hong Kong "WEN WEI PO," "TA KUNG PAO," "HONG KONG COMMERCIAL DAILY," "NEW EVENING POST," "ZIJIN MAGAZINE" and "ECONOMIC INFORMATION AND AGENCY." Jiang hoped that the newspapers and magazines would further improve, love the motherland including Hong Kong, protect the interests of Hong Kong residents and promote the peaceful transition of the territory in line with the basic principle of "one country and two systems." The chief executives arrived here yesterday at the invitation of the Information Office of the State Council, the Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office of the State Council and the Hong Kong branch of the XINHUA NEWS AGENCY.
