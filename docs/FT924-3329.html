921208 FT 08 DEC 92 / Coutts to open Leeds branch COUTTS, THE NatWest subsidiary bank that claims to service only a niche market of 'high net worth' individuals, is to open a branch in Leeds. There are two other English branches outside London - in Bath and Bristol - and Leeds was chosen because Coutts already has about 500 customers in the Yorkshire and Humberside region.
