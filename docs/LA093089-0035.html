September 30, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

CSUN SET TO OPEN CCAA SOCCER SEASON

The Cal State Northridge soccer team will open California Collegiate Athletic Assn. play at 7:30 tonight against Cal Poly San Luis Obispo at North Campus Stadium.

The Matadors (3-2-2), ranked 11th in the NCAA Division II, will try to rebound from consecutive losses to the University of San Diego and Nevada Las Vegas in last week's UNLV tournament.

Cal Poly San Luis Obispo (4-0-3) is ranked eighth in Division II.
