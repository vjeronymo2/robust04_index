920805 FT 05 AUG 92 / Ex Rolls chief dies SIR Denning Pearson, chairman of Rolls-Royce when it collapsed in 1971, has died at the age of 83. James Denning Pearson joined Rolls-Royce in 1932. In 1949 he became a director of the aero engine division and in 1957 he was made chief executive of the group.
