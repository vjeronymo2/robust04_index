930525 FT 25 MAY 93 / Israeli soldier shoot dead two Palestinian youths Israeli soldiers shot dead two Palestinian youths in the occupied territories yesterday Reuter reports from the West Bank. A Palestinian said soldiers in the West Bank town of Nablus shot dead one youth after stones were thrown at an Israeli bus. The army confirmed the death. Palestinians said soldiers also shot dead an activist for the Islamic Jihad group in the Gaza Strip town of Rafah, seriously wounded another activist and arrested a third.
