BFN [Text] President Joaquim Chissano, who is visiting Cabo Delgado Province, may be addressing a mass rally in the capital of Palma District, 400 km from the city of Pemba. Yesterday the head of state attended a banquet with a group of women, members of the Mozambique Liberation Front in Pemba. In a message, the group says that the Mozambique National Resistance and other unarmed opposition parties have already recognized defeat in the forthcoming October elections by calling for a government of national unity without defining valid criteria. On the occasion, Joaquim Chissano said the women should educate the citizens to vote for the party and candidate offering them prestige and representation. The head of state is scheduled to return to Pemba tomorrow afternoon after addressing a rally in the capital of Nangade District.
