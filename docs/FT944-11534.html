941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / UK Company News: TLG oversubscribed TLG, the holding company for Thorn Lighting Group, whose shares, priced at 115p, will start trading next Thursday, said its public offer was 3.2 times subscribed. Applications for up to and including 600 shares will be allocated in full; between 700 and 3,000 shares will receive 600; 4,000 and above will receive 20 per cent, to a maximum 100,000 ordinary shares.
