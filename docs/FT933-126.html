930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Lincat Lincat Group, the USM-quoted commercial catering equipment company, lifted pre-tax profits by 72 per cent, from Pounds 810,000 to Pounds 1.39m, over the 12 months to June 30. Mr Martin Craddock, chairman, said the increase, which came on turnover ahead 21 per cent to Pounds 14.1m (Pounds 11.7m), reflected progress across the four group companies. A proposed final dividend of 4p lifts the total to 6p (5.1p), covered 2.3 times by earnings of 14p (8.2p) per share.
