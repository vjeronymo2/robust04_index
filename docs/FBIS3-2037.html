Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Comrade Zhang Wannian, chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army [PLA], met with a delegation of the Foreign Affairs Bureau of DPRK Ministry of People's Armed Forces led by Major General Kim Hak-sam, director of the Foreign Affairs Bureau, and arranged a banquet for them in Beijing on 2 March. During the meeting, the two parties talked in a warm atmosphere about friendship between the PRC and the DPRK, and between their respective armed forces. They also exchanged views on the exchange [naewang] of PRC and DPRK armed forces in 1994.
