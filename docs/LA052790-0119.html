May 27, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SCOREKEEPERS NEEDED

Recreation Department officials are looking for scorekeepers for baseball and softball games.

Games will be played Mondays through Fridays from 5 to 10 p.m. and Saturdays from 9:30 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. at Artesia Park. Scorekeepers will be paid $4.50 to $5.50 per game depending on game time. Interested people should call the Artesia Recreation Department at 860-3361.
