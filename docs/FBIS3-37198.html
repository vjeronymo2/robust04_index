Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Ivan Ivanov and Gennadiy Talalayev] [Text] Moscow February 15 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin and British Prime Minister John Major signed a joint statement on reciprocal non-targeting of strategic missiles in the Kremlin here today. Asked by journalists after the signing ceremony, Yeltsin said: "Some people are trying to solve the problems of Bosnia without Russia. We shall not allow this to happen. Russia will take an active part in the negotiations in order to end the war in Yugoslavia through negotiations."
