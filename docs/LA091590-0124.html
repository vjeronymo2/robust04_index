September 15, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: ANAHEIM;

'20S REVUE PLANNED FOR SENIOR CENTER

The city's Senior Center will sizzle this weekend with two performances of "Roaring '20s -- Come Alive," a revue of some of the most popular songs and dances from the flapper era.

Anaheim Senior Center groups such as the Dapper Tappers and the Memory Melodears Chorus will give their rendition of the greatest hits of the 1920s. Other local groups also will participate. The shows, sponsored by the city, will be at 2 p.m. Saturday and Sunday at the Anaheim High School Cook Auditorium, 811 W. Lincoln Ave. Admission is $3.
