940211 FT 11 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Kleinwort High net asset value improves Kleinwort High Income Trust had a net asset value per share of 117p at end-December compared with 84.5p a year earlier and 93.7p at June 30 year-end. The value per zero dividend preference share was 130.4p, 116.9p and 123.5p respectively. Net revenue for the half year improved from Pounds 1.01m to Pounds 1.15m for earnings of 3.84p (3.37p) per share. A second quarterly dividend of 1.875p makes an unchanged 3.75p so far.
