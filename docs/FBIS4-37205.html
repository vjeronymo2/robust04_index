BFN [Text] Tunis, 17 Jun (SPA) -- Hamid al-Ghabid, secretary general of the Organization of the Islamic Conference [ICO], today said that the meeting of the Organization's Ministerial Council would be held next September or December in Tunis. In a newspaper interview published in Tunis, he said that consultations are under way between the Organization and Tunisia to determine the time of the meeting. He pointed out that the meeting would concentrate on discussing the situation of the city of Jerusalem within the framework of the peace process, which the ICO supports, in addition to the situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina.
