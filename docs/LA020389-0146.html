February 3, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

SPOTLIGHT / COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE: A WEEKLY LOOK AT OFFICE AND INDUSTRIAL REAL ESTATE IN THE COUNTY.;

FOCUS: TISHMAN EXECUTIVE TOWERS

The first of the complex's twin towers was completed early last year and construction on the second is expected to begin at the end of 1989. The complex has a health club for tenants and an underground tunnel connecting the towers to the parking garage. Marie Callender's A La Carte restaurant is scheduled to open next week on the ground level.

FACTS AND FIGURES

Address: 1100 Town and Country Road, Orange

Primary Contractor: Tishman Construction Corp.
