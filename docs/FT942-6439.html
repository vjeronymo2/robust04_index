940530 FT 30 MAY 94 / Correction: Astra and Takeda settlement Under the terms of the settlement between Astra, the Swedish pharmaceutical group, and the Japanese group, Takeda, Astra will receive unspecified consideration for Takeda's future sales of products containing lansoprazole and not the generic version of Losec (Astra's best-selling anti-ulcer drug) as stated in a May 27 story headed 'Astra-Takeda patent row settled'.
