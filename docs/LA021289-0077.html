February 12, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

FORMER KAISER HEADQUARTERS TO BE OFFICE BUILDING

Plans are being made to convert Kaiser Permanente's former headquarters at 4747 Sunset Blvd. into an $18-million outpatient office building.

Kaneko Ford Design will design interiors, which are expected to be under construction in July with completion within a year.

Kaiser moved its headquarters to Pasadena in 1985.
