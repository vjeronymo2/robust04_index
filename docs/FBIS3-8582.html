Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bratislava, March 11 (CTK) -- After the dismissal of Vladimir Meciar's cabinet Roman Kovac is likely to become new Slovak premier, chairman of the opposition Christian Democratic Movement (KDH) Jan Carnogursky told CTK today. He said the view was not held only by him, as Kovac's candidacy resulted from preliminary agreements of the opposition parties and groups in the case of the no-confidence vote in Meciar's government. Until recently, Roman Kovac held the post of deputy premier. He was, however, dismissed by Meciar as he stood up against him.
