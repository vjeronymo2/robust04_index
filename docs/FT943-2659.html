940917 FT 17 SEP 94 / Books: Fifty years of Norman Parkinson New York, New York - exuberance and optimism captured in 1955 from 'Parkinson: Photographs 1935-1990' (Conran Octopus Pounds 40). Nearly 190 black and white pictures trace the 50-year career of Norman Parkinson, one of Britain's leading fashion and portrait photographers whose reputation carried throughout Europe and across the Atlantic. His subjects ranged from Noel Coward and Queen Elizabeth II to Seamus Heaney to Ivana Trump.
