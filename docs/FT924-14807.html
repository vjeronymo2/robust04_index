921008 FT 08 OCT 92 / Fashion runs for currency cover: Retailing RETAILERS who are supplied from outside the UK face serious problems from currency fluctuations, particularly in the fashion sector. The cautious will have taken steps to protect themselves by taking out currency cover. 'We are not currency speculators . . . but a lot of people will have taken a certain amount of cover,' says one high-street retailer. Next, the fashion retailing group, has tried to be even more cautious by progressively scaling back on its overseas sourcing in the past two years. Mr David Jones, Next's chief executive, says the currency fluctuation had produced 'an added impetus' to source more from within the UK. 'We were sourcing almost 40 per cent from the UK,' he continued. 'But it's now about 50 per cent the UK and 50 per cent abroad, of which European countries - Italy, Portugal and Spain - are becoming more important.'
