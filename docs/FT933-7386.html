930821 FT 21 AUG 93 / Finance and the Family: B & W launches equity bond - At a glance Bristol & West has launched a guaranteed equity bond with a fixed rate element. Investors can split their holding between a one year investment account paying 6 per cent net, and a guaranteed equity bond, which rises in line with the FT-SE 100 Index. A minimum of 10 per cent and a maximum of 50 per cent can be placed into the investment account. Guaranteed equity bonds: page III
