941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Martin Currie Eur Martin Currie European Investment Trust had a net asset value of 117.9p per share at end-October against 117.7p a year earlier and 127.8p at the April 30 year-end. Net revenue for the six months jumped to Pounds 228,000 (Pounds 99,000) for earnings per share of 0.99p (0.43p). The trust does not pay interim dividends.
