BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 15 (KCNA) -- A decision of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK], the Central Military Commission of the WPK, the National Defence Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Central People's Committee of the DPRK on awarding the rank of vice marshal of the Korean People's Army to Comrade Kim Ik-hyon has been adopted. The decision dated April 15 says that it has been decided to award the rank of vice marshal of the Korean People's Army to Comrade Kim Ik-hyon who fought devotedly with arms in his hands from the first days of the Korean revolution for the motherland and the people in the anti-Japanese revolutionary war to defeat the Japanese imperialists and in the great fatherland liberation war for routing the U.S. imperialist aggressors, holding the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song in high esteem as the centre of unity, and has devoted his whole life to strengthening the people's Armed Forces, defending Comrade Kim Il-song and the party with arms.
