920411 FT 11 APR 92 / Brel wins Pounds 140m order BRITISH RAIL confirmed yesterday that a Pounds 140m order for 47 four-car Networker trains for its Network SouthEast division would go to Brel, Britain's biggest trainmaker, thereby helping to safeguard 1,500 jobs in York. The order had long been halted by lack of funds, but the government announced during the election campaign that it was allowing BR to go ahead with the order.
