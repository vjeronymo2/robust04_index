Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Belgrade, 22 Feb (TANJUG) -- Radomet Kuzet, the republic of Serbian Krajina [RSK] justice minister, and Krste Zarkovic, the deputy internal affairs minister of the Srpska republic, inspected the bridge on the Sava River between Bosanska Gradiska and Stara Gradiska. According to the RSK news agency, ISKRA, they determined that the situation on the bridge has returned to normal and that the RSK internal affairs ministry was in control of the border between Bosnian and Croatian Serbs.
