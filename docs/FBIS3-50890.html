Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Mexico City, 8 Jan (AFP) -- An alleged spokesman of the Zapatista National Liberation Army [EZLN] told AFP over the telephone that the car bomb planted at a Mexico City shopping center early Saturday morning is "the first retaliation against Carlos Salinas de Gortari" for covering up for "his cousin, Patrocinio Gonzalez, the genocidal murderer of Indians." Gonzalez is currently the government secretary, "This is an anonymous call, write this down quickly," a man with a faltering voice said before reading the message. Early this morning a car bomb exploded up in an underground parking lot at the Plaza Universidad Shopping Center in southern Mexico City. There was extensive damage to some 20 shops, but no one was injured. The man who phoned AFP gave no evidence that he was in fact a member of the EZLN.
