September 12, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

DIGEST LCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: HOLLYWOOD FREEWAY OFF-RAMPS TO BE CLOSED

The Roscoe Boulevard off-ramps on the Hollywood Freeway in Sun Valley will be closed for 18 days starting today, the state Department of Transportation announced.

Caltrans said the closures are needed for work on a nearby storm drain.

Detour signs have been posted by the contractor, Caltrans said.
