Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev -- "I have no conclusive information about what was achieved by [Deputy Prime Minister] Shmarov and [Deputy Foreign Minister] Tarasyuk in Washington," said Oleksandr Lavrynovych, the deputy chairman of the People's Movement of Ukraine [Rukh], interviewed by a UNIAN correspondent on 11 January. "All this, however, is reminiscent of the corresponding period in the history of the Ukrainian People's Republic. If Ukraine really gives up its nuclear weapons by being promised some mythical compensation, and not just gives them up, but removes its weapons to Russian territory, it will be a step that will be conducive to the liquidation of Ukraine's statehood."
