920723 FT 23 JUL 92 / Unemployment to rise in EC Unemployment in the European Community will rise from an average 9.5 per cent this year to 9.7 per cent next year, the Commission forecast in its annual employment report yesterday, writes David Buchan in Brussels. Ms Vasso Papandreou, the EC employment commissioner, warned that the targets for economic convergence and fiscal austerity, set down in the Maastricht treaty, 'should not be achieved at the expense of unemployment which is already too high'.
