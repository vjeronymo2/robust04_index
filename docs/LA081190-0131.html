August 11, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: SANTA ANA;

ANTIQUE CAR PARADE FEATURES 200 AUTOS

Automobiles from decades gone by will weave their way through Santa Ana today during the 15th annual Antique Car Classic Parade.

Sponsored by the city and South Coast Village, the event will feature about 200 vintage cars from the 1920s to the 1970s.

The cars will compete for best of show and for prizes in 20 other categories.

The event begins at 10 a.m., with judging at Centennial Park at the corner of Edinger Avenue and Fairview Road. At noon, a parade of cars, along with a police escort, will proceed south on Fairview to Sunflower Avenue east, then to South Coast Village, at Sunflower and Plaza Drive.

Jeff Ervine, disk jockey at KWIZ in Santa Ana, will be the grand marshal. Several members of the City Council are expected to make appearances.

The cars will be on free public view in the parking lot of South Coast Village from 12:30 to 4 p.m.
