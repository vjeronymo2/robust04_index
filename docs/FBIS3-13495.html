Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Islamic militants have issued a veiled warning today that revenge for the Hebron massacre might come in Brooklyn, New York. Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] said in a leaflet distributed in the Gaza Strip that the gunman who attacked a van of orthodox Jews in New York last week was inspired by Islamic teachings. One person was killed and three others wounded in that attack by a 28-year old Lebanese-born taxi driver.
