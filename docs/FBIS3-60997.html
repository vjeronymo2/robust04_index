Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanliurfa, Dec 7 (AA) -- Security forces killed six terrorists, including two Syrian citizens in a clash in southeastern Turkey today. AA learned that security forces on an operation early today in the Zeytindagi region of Suruc township, came across a group of terrorists. A clash erupted between the security forces and the terrorists who responded with fire to the calls of surrender. Of the six terrorists killed, three were women. Two hide-outs were found in the same operation. The terrorist organization is responsible for the deaths of more than 10,000 people in the region since 1984, when it launched a bloody campaign against the Turkish state and people. The two Syrian terrorists are named Mezit Muhammet Ismail and Binevs (nickname--Dilan). Officials said that woman terrorist Dilan was a bloody militant and was being searched for for a long time.
