920904 FT 04 SEP 92 / Mexico to privatise ports THE MEXICAN government has announced that it is to privatise the country's ports, in an attempt to boost their productivity, writes Damian Fraser from Mexico City. It will auction concessions to manage nine ports, including those at Acapulco, Progreso, Lazaro Cardenas and Manzanillo. Puertos Mexicanos, the state ports authority, is to be disbanded.
