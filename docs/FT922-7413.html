920519 FT 19 MAY 92 / Letter: A tax on fossil fuels Sir, For all the scoffing at the idea of a tax on fossil fuels, is there not a case for the UK to take unilateral action? Some Pounds 30bn switched from employers' National Insurance contributions to fuel is surely in line with the Conservatives' philosophy on indirect taxation and it might even reverse the trend in redundancies. Alan F Greene, 32 Glen Road, Fleet, Hants GU13 9RQ
