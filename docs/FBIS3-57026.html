Language: English Article Type:BFN [By GIA correspondent for TASS] [Text] Moscow January 19 TASS -- Georgian leader Eduard Shevardnadze met on Wednesday with Israel's official governmental delegation. The delegation included officials of the Israeli Foreign Ministry and some economic agencies. The talks concentrated on prospects for Georgian-Israeli relations. Participants in the meeting also discussed the proposed visit by Shevardnadze to Israel, during which the two sides are to sign a package of basic agreements to set up a legal framework for ties between Georgia and Israel.
