931127 FT 27 NOV 93 / Holiday company fined Pounds 3,500 Falcon Holidays, a subsidiary of Owners Abroad, the holiday company, was yesterday fined Pounds 3,500 for making bogus promises in a brochure. The company admitted three breaches of the Trades Descriptions Act at Lewes Magistrates' Court in East Sussex. The court heard that a brochure had made inaccurate claims about the quality of the beach and leisure facilities at Cala Canutells, in Menorca.
