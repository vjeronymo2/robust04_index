Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Reynold Georges, leader of the Alliance for Liberation and the Advancement of Haiti [ALAH], states his stand. In a statement to Signal FM, he wants the Haitian Government to seize the fuel tanker so the fuel can be distributed throughout the entire territory. Let us listen to Senator Reynold Georges: [Begin recording, in Creole] We say that Haiti is not a country under trusteeship, but an independent country. Everything in Haitian territory belongs to the Haitian Government and people. We ask the Haitian state to seize this fuel as property of the Haitian state as soon as it arrives in the country and to distribute it in accordance with Haitian law governing the matter. [end recording]
