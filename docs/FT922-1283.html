920623 FT 23 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Heavitree Brewery down at Pounds 177,000 Heavitree Brewery, the pub operator, turned in a reduced pre-tax profit for the six months ended April 30, down from Pounds 290,000 to Pounds 177,000 despite turnover ahead at Pounds 3.06m against Pounds 2.83m last time. Earnings per share came out at 1.2p (1.1p) and the interim is unchanged at 0.6p.
