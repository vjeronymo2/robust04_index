July 5, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: WEST HOLLYWOOD;

$31.7-MILLION BUDGET OKD

The City Council gave final approval to a $31.7-million operating budget for the fiscal year that began July 1.

The budget includes about $650,000 in cuts that were made to make room for an expected $1.5-million drop in state funding next year. Hardest hit by the city's budget ax was the Public Works Department, which was eliminated to save more than $100,000. The City Council plans to trim an additional $750,000 from the 1991-92 budget.
