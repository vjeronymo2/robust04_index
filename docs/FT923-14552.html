920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / BAT chief to lead probe into police SIR Patrick Sheehy, chairman of BAT Industries, is to head the review of police pay and responsibilities set up by Mr Kenneth Clarke, the home secretary. The terms of reference of the inquiry are 'to examine the rank structure, remuneration and conditions of service of the police service'. Mr Clarke has asked for the report by May next year. Other members of the inquiry include Mr John Bullock, joint senior partner of Coopers & Lybrand, the accountancy firm; Mr Eric Caines, National Health Service personnel director, and Sir Paul Fox, former managing director of BBC Network Television.
