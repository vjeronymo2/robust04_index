August 18, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Paul Solon, a lawyer from Tiburon, Calif., is the leader at the midpoint of the Race Across America, a non-stop bicycle race from Costa Mesa to New York.

Expected to reach the Missouri-Illinois border today, Solon has averaged 16.3 m.p.h. in covering 1,568 miles. Michael Secrest is second, 60 miles behind Solon, and Bob Breedlove is third.
