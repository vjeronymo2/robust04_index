Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 8 (XINHUA) -- Some Chinese leaders have visited former party and state leaders in Beijing and Shanghai, extending Spring Festival greetings to them. The leaders, from the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC), the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress (NPC), the State Council and the national committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), were entrusted to make the visits by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China. Among them were Tian Jiyun, vice-chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, Qian Qichen, vice-premier, Wen Jiabao, alternate member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee, and Ye Xuanping, vice-chairman of the CPPCC national committee.
