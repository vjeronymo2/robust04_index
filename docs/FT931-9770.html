930212 FT 12 FEB 93 / International Company News: Union Bancaire Prive improves UNION Bancaire Prive, one of Switzerland's largest private banks, said net profit in 1992 rose 16 per cent to SFr108m (Dollars 73.9m), writes Ian Rodger in Zurich. The bank said assets grew 20 per cent to SFr10.8bn and shareholders' equity was over SFr757m at year end.
