940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / Privatisation sale stopped in Israel The latest stage in Israel's privatisation programme, the sale of Shikun U'Pituach construction company, has been cancelled by its underwriters because of fears the issue was priced too high to attract buyers in the depressed Tel Aviv Stock Exchange, writes David Horovitz in Jerusalem. Tomorrow's planned sale would have been the first entirely state-owned company to be sold off and was expected to raise about Shk1bn (Pounds 226m).
