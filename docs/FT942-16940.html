940407 FT 07 APR 94 / International Company News: Colgate in Czech venture Colgate-Palmolive's Czech subsidiary has signed a joint-venture agreement with Czech Spojene Kartacovny to produce toothbrushes, Reuter reports from Prague. The deal calls for up to 400,000 toothbrushes to be made monthly at the plant in the southern Bohemian town of Pelhrimov, under the US company's label. The companies said Czech demand was running at 12m-15m toothbrushes yearly. The Czech plant plans to export 80 per cent of its output.
