931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / UK Company News: James Latham back in black with Pounds 0.6m James Latham, the timber importer and building materials merchant, swung back into the black in the six months to end-September with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 598,000. At the March year end there was a deficit of Pounds 2.2m, principally arising from a Pounds 1.89m exceptional charge, although at the interim stage of that year the company reported a profit of Pounds 54,000. Turnover for the latest six months amounted to Pounds 35.1m, against Pounds 21m - including Pounds 1.39m from discontinued operations - last time. The interest charge fell to Pounds 497,000 (Pounds 616,000) and tax took Pounds 160,000 (nil). The interim dividend is maintained at 1.5p from earnings of 7.91p (0.29p).
