September 30, 1989, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

QUAYLE TO SPEAK IN MISSION VALLEY

Vice President Dan Quayle will be the keynote speaker Oct. 17 at Big Brothers of San Diego County's annual fund-raising dinner in Mission Valley.

"We anticipate that he will bring congratulations from President and Mrs. Bush, the current honorary co-chairs of Big Brothers/Big Sisters of America," a San Diego public relations spokesman said.

Nearly 500 people are expected to attend the organization's largest event held this year at the Town & Country Hotel.

After Quayle's address, Big Brothers will present its Man of the Year Award to Charles R. Cono, partner and owner of Hanken Cono Assad & Company, Inc., a San Diego real estate firm.

Big Brothers of San Diego and its affiliated Sisters program have been in the business, for 28 years, of matching children of single-parent families with adults who volunteer their time and emotional support.

In San Diego, about 60,000 children live with only one parent, and that number is increasing by 30% each year, said Joseph De Cristofaro, executive director of Big Brothers of San Diego.
