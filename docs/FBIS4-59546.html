BFN [Text] The higher committee charged with preparing for the festivals marking the fourth anniversary of the Yemeni Republic met this morning under brother Deputy Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Wahhab al- Anisi. During this meeting, the conferees discussed the timetable for the celebrations of the Republic's national day, the fourth anniversary of unity and the establishment of the Yemen Republic. Despite the difficult circumstances brought about by the gang of apostasy and secession in the Socialist Party, the committee decided to hold festivals to mark this dear anniversary in all governorates in the country; the anniversary of the day on which our people's dream and great goal -- the restoration of unity and the establishment of the Yemeni Republic -- was achieved on 22 May 1990.
