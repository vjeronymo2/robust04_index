930923 FT 23 SEP 93 / Directors' pay growth slows A QUARTER of all board-level directors received earnings increases including bonuses of 14.1 per cent or more in the year to July, a survey of top pay by Monks Partnership, the pay consultancy, has found. However the overall rate of increase in fixed pay for board directors fell to 5 per cent for main-board directors and 6 per cent for subsidiary-board directors. Last year the increases were 7.7 per cent for main-board directors and 7.9 per cent for subsidiary directors. The median increase for parent-company senior managers was 4.1 per cent and for those in a subsidiary it was 4.7 per cent.
