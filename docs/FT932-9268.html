930514 FT 14 MAY 93 / Observer: Paws for thought The White House works in mysterious ways. Hence, when tiny Bryn Parry Studios in Downton, Wiltshire, received an urgent overseas order earlier this week, they sensed the handiwork of Socks, America's First Cat. Socks's owner had been let down by a US supplier and the English studio was asked to stage a last minute rescue by supplying 22 picture frames for a presidential presentation. Although the SOS arrived after the close of business on Monday, the frames were winging their way to Washington by Tuesday afternoon. The only mystery is why the White House insisted on having plain designs instead of the frames surrounded by cartoons of cats, for which the company is noted.
