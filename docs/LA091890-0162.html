September 18, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

LOCAL;

TRANSIENT, 44, SLAIN IN TUJUNGA

A 44-year-old man was fatally stabbed for an unknown reason shortly after walking out of a friend's home in Tujunga, Los Angeles police said today.

The victim, a transient, was stabbed at 9:30 p.m. in front of the house in the 10300 block of Fernglen Avenue, Detective Al Ferrand said.

His name was not released pending notification of his family. No arrests have been made.

"He had apparently just walked out to the front of the house and shortly thereafter came back in with a stab wound," Ferrand said.

The victim collapsed inside the house and died. Ferrand said detectives do not know the motive for the slaying nor do they have any witnesses who say they saw the attack.
