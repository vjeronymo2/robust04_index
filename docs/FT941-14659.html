940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Worthington Worthington Group, the trimmings and buttons maker, is acquiring Henderson Holmes & Reiss for Pounds 1.73m, to be satisfied as to Pounds 1.58m in cash with the balance in shares valued at 51p each. To finance the cash element Worthington is proposing a 1-for-3 rights issue of up to 6.32m ordinary shares at 51p to raise about Pounds 2.9m. Henderson Holmes makes waistbands and tapings. For the year to March 1993 it reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 482,000 on turnover of Pounds 7.9m.
