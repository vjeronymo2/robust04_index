931214 FT 14 DEC 93 / International Company News: Iberdrola holds midway payout Iberdrola, Spain's biggest private sector utility, will pay a gross interim dividend of Pta31.25 per share for 1993, unchanged from the previous year, AP-DJ reports from Madrid. The final dividend for 1993 will be decided at a general shareholders meeting next spring, the company said. In 1992, Iberdrola paid out a final dividend of Pta30 per share.
