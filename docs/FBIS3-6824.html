Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] Recently, the 4th Military Region party committee held a mid-term party congress in the city of Vinh in Nghe An Province with 108 delegates representing thousands of party members in the military region. Senior Lieutenant General Le Kha Phieu, member of the Political Bureau, secretary of the Central Committee, and director of the Political General Department of the Vietnam People's Army, attended the congress on behalf of the Military Central Committee. The report of the 4th Military Region party committee carefully reviewed work performance during the last two years. The committee has sufficiently improved its national defense performance in the region, contributing to the political stabilization and socioeconomic development and national protection. The congress also mentioned duties in the near future and pledged to accomplish all requirements set forth by the all-army party congress. Senior Lieut. Gen. Le Kha Phieu hailed achievements by the party committees and forces in the 4th Military Region. He also outlined key directions for the congress to study and discuss.
