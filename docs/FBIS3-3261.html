Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By Gao Deshu (7559 1795 3412), reporter, and Wang Zhigang (3769 1807 0474), correspondent of the Tongxian County reporters' station: "Tongzhou Television Station Begins Broadcasting"] [Text] The Beijing Tongzhou Television Station officially began broadcasting at 1825 on 6 February in order to provide the people in urban and rural areas of Tongxian County to have a harmonious and happy Spring Festival. The Beijing Tongzhou Television Station is Beijing's fifth county-level (district-level) television station approved by the Ministry of Radio, Film, and Television. Its construction, which took one year, has provided people the luck to watch more television programs.
