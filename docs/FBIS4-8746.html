BFN [Text] Zagreb, April 16 (AFP) -- NATO air strikes were underway around the eastern Bosnian Moslem enclave of Gorazde on Saturday, sources close to the UN Protection Force here said. The source, who requested anonymity, said at 6:30 p.m. (1830 GMT) British and U.S. planes were striking at Bosnian Serb forces attacking the enclave, over which a NATO jet was downed earlier in the afternoon.
