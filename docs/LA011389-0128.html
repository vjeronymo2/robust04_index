January 13, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

GARDEN GROVE;

STORM DRAIN PROJECT TO AFFECT TRAFFIC FLOW

The construction of a storm drain project on Chapman Avenue in Garden Grove is scheduled to begin in mid-January. The project is designed to relieve problems with standing water and flooding on Chapman from Gilbert Street through East Euclid Street.

Work in these areas is expected to continue until July. Traffic flow and parking in the construction area will be restricted during the work. Efforts will be made to provide access to businesses and residents along the route.

For more information, call Mel Araki in Garden Grove engineering services at (714) 741-5181.
