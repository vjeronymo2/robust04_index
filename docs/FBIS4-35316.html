BFN [Text] An attack of the Golboddin militia was aborted in Logar Province. Quoting a security source of the country, a BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY correspondent reported: The infamous militia of Golboddin launched an attack against the positions of the Islamic Army at 0400 in Mandokhel in the center of Logar Province. The militias were forced to retreat as a result of the resistance of the mojahedin and soldiers of Brigade 800, Jehadi Division 36. The report says that two militia were killed and eight others were injured, and the enemy's one set of BM-1 rocket launchers was destroyed.
