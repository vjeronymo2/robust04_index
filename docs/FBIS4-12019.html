BFN [Text] Baghdad, 4 Apr (INA)--A parliamentary meeting was held today between the National Assembly's Arab and International Relations Committee and the visiting Jordanian parliamentary delegation. The conferees discussed means of cooperation and coordination at Arab and international parliament meetings with the objective of doubling efforts to lift the blockade imposed on the Iraqi people. They also discussed bilateral parliamentary relations and means of promoting them. Dr. Safa' Salih, chairman of the Arab and International Relations Committee, led the Iraqi side, and 'Abd Musa al-Nahar, deputy speaker of the Jordanian House of Representatives, led the Jordanian side.
