Language: French Article Type:BFN [Communique issued by the Action Committee for Renewal in Lome on 15 March] [Text] The head of state, General Gnassingbe Eyadema, today received a delegation of the Action Committee for Renewal [CAR] led by CAR Chairman Yaovi Agboyibor, in line with the consultations he has initiated following the proclamation of the results of the legislative elections by the Supreme Court. The discussions, which lasted from 0855 to 0935, focused exclusively on general issues, notably the return of concord, social peace, and security in the country to enable the rapid resumption of economic activities. The modalities for forming a new government were not tackled at the meeting but the CAR leader said when the time comes the two opposition parties, who won the absolute majority of seats at the National Assembly, will hold discussions with the head of state as a united front.
