930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / The Coal White Paper: Closures and reprieves Ten pits where production has already ceased will not reopen: Betws and Taff Merthyr (S Wales), Cotgrave and Silverhill (Notts), Grimethorpe, Houghton Main and Markham Main (Yorks), Parkside (Lancs), Trentham (Staffs), Vane Tempest (North East) Two other pits will also close due to 'impending exhaustion': Bolsover and Sharleston. Six collieries to be mothballed: Easington and Westoe (North East), Rossington (Yorks), Bevercotes and Clipstone (Notts), Shirebrook (Midlands), Maltby colliery near Rotherham will cease production but development work will continue. Twelve pits to be reprieved Wearmouth (North East), Bentley, Frickley, Hatfield, Kiveton, Prince of Wales (all Yorks), Markham (Derby), Rufford, Bilsthorpe, Calverton (Notts), Point of Ayr (N Wales) Silverdale (Staffs).
