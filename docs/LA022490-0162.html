February 24, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

* James R. Morrell has joined American Suzuki Motor Corp.'s automotive division in Brea as Los Angeles regional sales manager.

He is responsible for overseeing all sales operations, advertising and dealer development activities in the 14-state region. Morrell had previously been Los Angeles regional manager for Peugeot Motors of America from 1986 through 1989. Earlier positions include national sales manager and regional manager for Isuzu Truck of America in Los Angeles and Western regional manager for American Isuzu Motors.
