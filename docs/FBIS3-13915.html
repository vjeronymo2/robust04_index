Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Informed sources have said that Jordan has received an indirect Kuwaiti offer on bilateral relations. The sources added that the offer calls for restoring Kuwaiti-Jordanian relations if Amman announces its agreement on the Iraqi-Kuwaiti border demarcation. The sources said that Jordan has not responded to the offer which was conveyed unofficially by a party described as a friend of both countries.
