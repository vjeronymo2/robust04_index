Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] The government, through National Defense Minister Martin-Fidele Magnaga, has just made an important decision. The curfew decreed on 9 December 1993 has been lifted as of today. The state of alert is now just an unpleasant memory. Therefore, Gabonese can resume their evening entertainment. Minister Magnaga announced this to our reporter Florentin Agouendo this morning in the following excerpt: [Begin Magnaga recording] Trusting to our people's wisdom and maturity and with the president of the Republic's consent, the government has decided to lift the state of alert and all related measures, including the curfew. [end recording]
