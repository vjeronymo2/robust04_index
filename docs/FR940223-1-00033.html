On or before the 16th day after the end of the month, each handler shall pay to the market administrator the amount, if any, by which the amount as specified in paragraph (a) of this section exceeds the amount specified in paragraph (b) of this section: (a) The total obligation of the handler for such month as determined pursuant to §1135.60.
