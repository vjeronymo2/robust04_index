921029 FT 29 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Blystad assets below value of bid BLYSTAD Group, the offshore oil services company, yesterday reported interim pre-tax losses of Pounds 4.62m, compared with a profit of Pounds 579,000. The loss reduces Blystad's net assets to Pounds 7.2m, below the value of the unconditional bid from Abbot Holdings, Mr Alistair Locke's private group. The deficit for the six months to June 30 included a Pounds 2.6m write-down in the value of a semi-submersible drilling rig which is laid up in Singapore, and an Pounds 878,000 provision covering the loss in value of a quoted investment. Abbot said last week it had received acceptances of its preference share offer from holders representing more than 75 per cent of the shares.
