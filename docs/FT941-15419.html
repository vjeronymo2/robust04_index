940115 FT 15 JAN 94 / Taiwan says it may postpone talks with Beijing Taiwan, stung by two diplomatic defeats at the hands of China this week, said yesterday it may pospone high-level talks with Beijing scheduled for the end of this month. France and China released a joint statement in which Paris agreed to ban future sales of French arms to Taiwan. The southern African state of Lesotho switched diplomatic recognition to China from Taiwan leaving Taipei recognised by only 28 foreign governments.
