910502 FT 02 MAY 91 / Unita deal confirmed President Jose Eduardo dos Santos of Angola confirmed yesterday that an agreement had been reached with Unita rebels on a ceasefire in the country's 14-year civil war, Michael Holman, Africa Editor, writes. Mr dos Santos said in Luanda, the Angolan capital, that 'we have reached a political understanding. This does not mean the war will end immediately, but. . . steps will be taken so that we may yet achieve a cessation of hostilities from this month.'
