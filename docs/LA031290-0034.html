March 12, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Totally Cosmic Talk: If you manage to stay awake past Arsenio, you could have a cosmic experience. "Beyond the Line," a New-Age talk show, makes its U.S. debut in Los Angeles today on KCOP Channel 13 at 1 a.m. In Oprah/Donahue style, the one-hour Canadian show will address reincarnation, attitudinal healing and other psychic subjects. Hosted by Canadian talk-show host Laurier LaPierre, the show airs through Friday for a one-week test. BETH KLEID
