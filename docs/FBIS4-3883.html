BFN [Text] Beijing, April 25 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier and Foreign Minister Qian Qichen met here this afternoon with Bakr M. Rasul, director-general of the Arab Labor Organization. Rasoul visited China at the invitation of Chinese Ministry of Labor with the purpose of getting better understanding of China's professional training and labor administration. He said he will also explore ways for cooperation between Arabian countries and China in the fields of labor.
