Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Major General Dani Yatom has acceded to the request of Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin and Chief of Staff Ehud Baraq and accepted a temporary appointment as acting commander of the Central Command. Maj. Gen. Yatom will resume his duties as the prime minister's military secretary as soon as another officer is permanently appointed to head the Central Command. Meanwhile, Colonel 'Amos Gil'ad will replace Maj. Gen. Yatom at the prime minister's bureau. Our Army affairs correspondent Karmela Menashe notes that Maj. Gen. Yatom served as commander of the Central Command until approximately 10 months ago.
