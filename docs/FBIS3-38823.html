Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] "We must use all political and military means to ease the situation in Bosnia before the spring offensives," Foreign Minister Beniamino Andreatta told a EU [European Union] summit of foreign affairs ministers in Brussels. Andreatta said that he agreed with the proposal made by French Foreign Minister Alain Juppe to give the Serbs an ultimatum to leave Sarajevo and to create a security perimeter to keep the Serb artillery far away from the city's suburbs.
