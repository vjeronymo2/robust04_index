BFN [Text] As part of Arab endeavors to contain the crisis in Yemen, today [7 May] Algeria reiterated its preparedness to contribute all it can to the settlement of the Yemeni crisis, calling on both sides of the conflict to stop the fighting. An authorized source at the Foreign Ministry said today that Algeria is following the events in Yemen with concern and calls on both parties to the conflict to begin a dialogue. The source added that because of its feeling of responsibility as a member state and as current president of the Arab Maghreb Union, Algeria has recently made a number of political moves in an effort to reconcile the viewpoints of the two parties in conflict.
