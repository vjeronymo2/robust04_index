Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] On 30 March, the Liberal Democratic Party [LDP] established an "emergency project team on Northeast Asian affairs," which is chaired by Policy Research Council Chairman Ryutaro Hashimoto, in a bid to deal with the international situation concerning possible sanctions against the DPRK (North Korea). From now on, the team plans to meet once a week to study from an independent standpoint what form Japan's crisis control should take. The project team intends to present emergency proposals to the government if the situation becomes more tense.
