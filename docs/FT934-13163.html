931022 FT 22 OCT 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables There are plenty of tasty English apples to choose from this week, English Cox's orange pippins are 20-50p a lb and English Bramley cooking apples are 25-50p a lb, and English Russets are 40-50p a lb. Other good fruit buys include New Zealand Kiwifruits at 15-20p each and English, Dutch and Belgian Comice pears at 30-60p a lb. English, French and Spania carrots are the best vegetable buy this week at 12-25p a lb. English and Dutch leeks at 35-70p a lb and English and Dutch onions at 15-25p a lb are also good buys. Spania and Canary Island tomatoes are this week's best salad buy, widely available for 35-70p a lb.
