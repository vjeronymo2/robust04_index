Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, December 25 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The newspaper "ARDIYN ERH" informs its readers about work of the session of work of the State Great Hural, the supreme legislative organ of the country. The session has finished discussion of the issue about immigration of citizens. As the newspaper notes, all members of the parliament paid particular attention to the issues of protection of human rights. The newspaper publishes commentaries concerning reflection of protection of human rights in the country's Constitution. This issue is connected with labour relations between collectives. The supplement to the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party central newspaper "UNEN," "BODLYN SOLBITSOL," [name of supplement as received] publishes an article concerning Russification of the Mongolian language. Numerous examples of wrong use of terms are given in the article. The author points out to importance of preserving the Mongolian language in purity.
