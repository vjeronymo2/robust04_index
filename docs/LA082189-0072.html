August 21, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

LEACH TAKES TITLE IN DOUBLES EVENT

Jon Leach of Laguna Beach and Jimmy Panagopoulos of Aurora, Ill., won the boys' doubles title at the United States Tennis Assn. International Grass Court Tennis Championship Sunday in Philadelphia.

Leach and Panagopoulos defeated Sean Steinour of Vineland, N.J., and Lars Beck of Villanova, 7-6 (7-5), 6-4.
