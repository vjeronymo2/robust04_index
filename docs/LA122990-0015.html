December 29, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

FAN LOOKS FOR HELP ON WHOM TO RESPECT

When you go down the list of great names in baseball's Hall of Fame, it's easy to identify the likes of Ted Williams with the Boston Red Sox, DiMaggio and Mantle as Yankees, Koufax and Drysdale and Jackie Robinson as Dodgers, Musial with the Cardinals, Feller with Cleveland and Bench with Cincinnati, to name only a few. But with the multimillion-dollar game of musical free agency, it will be tough not to associate future Hall of Famers with the Bank of America.

BILL SULLY

Santa Monica
