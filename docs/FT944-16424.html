941013 FT 13 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Ryan Hotels rises 53% Ryan Hotels, the Dublin-based hotel operator, lifted pre-tax profits 53 per cent from IPounds 526,000 to IPounds 804,000 (Pounds 795,000) in the six months to July 28. Turnover improved to IPounds 12.2m (IPounds 11.5m) generating flat trading profits of IPounds 2m. Earnings per share worked through at 0.97p (0.55p) and the interim dividend is held at 0.5p.
