Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 24 Feb -- A delegation of the Socialist Party of Serbia [SPS] led by Mihailo Markovic, a member of its executive group, began its first ever visit to Russia today, the SPS information department has reported. During their stay in Russia the SPS delegation is expected to meet representatives from all of Russia's major political parties, members of the Russian government, and both houses of the Russian parliament, the SPS notes in its statement.
