Language: Finnish Article Type:BFN [Text] All the votes have now been counted. About 3.2 million were cast. Martti Ahtisaari received 53.9 percent -- about 1.7 million -- and Elisabeth Rehn received 46.1 percent, about 1.4 million. The difference between the candidates is 7.8 percent; in terms of numbers of votes it is about 250,000. The turnout was 82.3 percent.
