Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague Jan 1 (CTK) -- As of today, the citizens of the Czech and Slovak Republics living in the other country will not pay administrative fees for issuing permanent residence permits. The relevant note was handed to Vilem Holan, director of the Czech Foreign Ministry Slovak Department by counselor-envoy of the Slovak Embassy in Prague, Jozef Stank, on Thursday [30 December]. The note reciprocates a note from the Czech Foreign Ministry of December 22. The cancellation of the fees was initiated at the meeting between the Czech and Slovak presidents, Vaclav Havel and Michal Kovac, in Lany, Central Bohemia, on November 17.
