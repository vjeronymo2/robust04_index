920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / Warrant issued for De Lorean THE RUC Fraud Squad was yesterday granted a warrant for the arrest of Mr John De Lorean, the American whose Belfast car company crashed 10 years ago, after receiving Pounds 70m in government money. Last week Mr Fred Bushell, former Lotus Car Company chief, admitted he conspired with Mr De Lorean and others to defraud De Lorean Motor Company of nearly Pounds 10m. The US statute of limitations means Mr De Lorean cannot be brought back against his will.
