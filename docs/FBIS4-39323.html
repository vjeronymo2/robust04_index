BFN [Report by a TIA "KHOVAR" correspondent for TASS] [Text] Dushanbe 7 June TASS -- Tajikistan is creating its own security ministry frontier guards. A decree to this effect has been signed by Tajik President Emomali Rakhmonov. The frontier troops are being created on the base of the state border guard department, military formations of the republic's Ministry of Security and by transferring military personnel from the armed forces and interior troops. The Tajik Ministry of Security is instructed to work out within a three-month period and present its proposals regarding the general structure and strength, places of dislocation, a plan of building and developing the frontier troops. May 28 will be marked every year as the Frontier Guard Day in Tajikistan.
