BFN [Text] Boris Yeltsin has formed a national committee of the Russian Federation to prepare for paricipation in the world summit in the interests of social development. The Russian president's instruction notes that the national committee has been set up in connection with the UN General Assembly resolution which envisages the holding of a world meeting in Copenhagen in March 1995. Deputy Prime Minister Yuriy Yarov has been given the post of chairman of the committee. This was reported by the press service of the Russian Federation president.
