Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Zagreb, Feb 16 (AFP) -- An UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] fact-finding mission has been unable to determine the origin of mortar fire that killed 68 people in a Sarajevo market earlier this month, an official of the United Nations Protection Force in Yugoslavia announced Wednesday. The mortar shell which also wounded 200 other people on February 5 may have been fired by either side, Serb or Moslem, Canadian Colonel Michel Gauthier, who heads the investigation committee, told the press.
