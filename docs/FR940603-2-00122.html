SUMMARY: In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1980 (title 44, U.S. Code, chapter 35), this notice announces a request for clearance of a new information collection. Form RI 94&hyph;7, Death Benefit Payment Rollover Election Form (FERS), provides FERS surviving spouses and surviving former spouses with the means to elect payment of FERS rollover-eligible benefits directly or to an IRA. There are estimated to be 700 respondents for RI 94&hyph;7. It takes approximately 60 minutes to complete RI 94&hyph;7. The combined annual burden is 700 hours.
