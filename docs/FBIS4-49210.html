BFN [Text] Beijing, May 3 (XINHUA) -- The departments directly under the Chinese People's Navy held an evening today to mark the 45th anniversary of the founding of the People's Navy. Liu Huaqing, member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC) and vice-chairman of the Central Military Commission, attended the evening. Also present were Chi Haotian, minister of defence; Yu Yongbo, director of the General Political Department of the People's Liberation Army (PLA); and Fu Quanyou, director of the General Logistics Department of PLA. Before the gathering began, Vice-Chairman Liu met with Liu Zhiyan, a Navy soldier who was seriously injured in rescuing six children. He praised Liu Zhiyan's bravery and encouraged him to continue to serve the people. Entertainment at the event included singing and dancing which depicted the development of the Chinese People's Navy under the leadership of the Chinese Communist Party and the three generations of leaders, Mao Zedong, Deng Xiaoping and Jiang Zemin.
