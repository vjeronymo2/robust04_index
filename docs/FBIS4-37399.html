BFN [Text] A group of the heroes of the popular resistance in the Abyan Governorate laid an ambush for the aggressive invaders along the road from (Am'ayn) to al-Urqub. They succeeded in destroying a military bus and killing all the soldiers in it. This daring operation, carried out by our noble heroes from the sons of the dauntless Abyan Governorate, came in the wake of the spread of the popular resistance in all the areas of the Abyan Governorate and in every area under the invaders' occupation.
