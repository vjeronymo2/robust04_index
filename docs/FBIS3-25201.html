Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] British Foreign Secretary Douglas Hurd has canceled a four-day trip to South Africa and Swaziland because of events in Bosnia. This has been announced in London by British officials, who said that Mr. Hurd had not yet rearranged his trip to southern Africa. However, they said it would not take place in the immediate future. Mr. Hurd was to have left today for the visit that included meetings with State President F.W. de Klerk and ANC [African National Congress] leader Mr. Nelson Mandela.
