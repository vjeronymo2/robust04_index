BFN [Telephone report by Correspondent Homero Lopez Ortiz from Monterrey -- live] [Text] At 0900 this morning a strong explosion destroyed a branch of Banorte, located on Manuel L. Barragan and Tlalpan Avenues in the neighborhood of Valle de Anaguas in San Nicolas Uzgarte, a metropolitan municipality of Monterrey. So far, it has not been possible to determine the cause of the explosion. What was confirmed is that the building was totally destroyed after its personnel and some clients had already entered. The strong explosion also damaged some homes adjacent to the building. Some vehicles were also totally destroyed. Only license plates were left of some vehicles, which were left in pieces. Red Cross units of San Nicolas Uzgarte Municipality, the Red Cross, the Green Cross, firefighters, and State Governor Socrates Rizo Garcia are at the scene of the tragedy. Also, Attorney General Benito Morales is there. State officials are trying to rescue the victims of the tragedy which occurred at 0900 this morning in the Banorte branch, located on Manuel L. Barragan and Tlalpan Avenues in the neighborhood of Valle de Anaguas in San Nicolas Uzgarte in Nuevo Leon. So far it has been impossible to determine the number of victims. We know that there were some clients and personnel inside.
