930617 FT 17 JUN 93 / Commodities and Agriculture: Jute exports rise Jute exports, which account for 28 per cent of Bangladesh's annual export income, rose in 1992-93 as world market prices fell by about 14 per cent, agriculture officials said, reports Reuter from Dhaka. The country expects to earn Dollars 430m from exports of 1.51m bales of raw jute and 594,000 tonnes of jute goods in 1992-93. In 1991-92 it earned Dollars 387.63m from exports of 1.4m bales of raw jute and 496,000 tonnes of goods.
