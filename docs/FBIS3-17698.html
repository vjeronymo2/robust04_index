Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Head of state Eduard Shevardnadze yesterday held a meeting with heads of the Georgian Cabinet of Ministers. They discussed the delayed repayment of debt to Turkmenistan for supplying Georgia with gas. The sum is in excess of $150 million. Other issues of an economic and industrial nature were discussed during the session, and, in particular, ways were sought for strengthening the Georgian coupon and prospects were discussed for restoring the republic's former economic links and the revival of the old branches of its industrial complex. In his address to the session, Eduard Shevardnadze especially stressed the necessity for bringing wages in line with prices in the country and assigning the government to set up a working group that will in one week work out a program for economic transformation during the present stage.
