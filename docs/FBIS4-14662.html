BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Shanayev] [Text] Vladikavkaz April 2 TASS -- An official meeting between the presidents of North Ossetia and Ingushetia, Akhsarbek Galazov and Ruslan Aushev, began in Nalchik at 11.00 on Saturday. It has become known from sources close to the North Ossetian president that the situation in the state-of-emergency area is under discussion. Special attention will be devoted to the enactment of the Russian president's decree on "a procedure for the return of refugees and forced migrants to the places of their permanent residence on the territories of North Ossetian and Ingush Republics." The Ossetian side has prepared a package of important documents connected with the implementation of the Russian president's decree for the dialogue in Nalchik. The basic principle inherent in the document is striving to maintain peace and inter-ethnic accord in the North Caucasus.
