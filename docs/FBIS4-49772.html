BFN [Text] Beijing, May 10 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier and Foreign Minister Qian Qichen met with former U.S. Secretary of State Dr. Henry Kissinger and his party here this evening. The two sides exchanged views on regional and global situation and Sino-U.S. bilateral relations. After the meeting, Qian hosted a dinner in honor of Kissinger and his party. Chinese Vice-Foreign Minister Liu Huaqiu was present on both occasions. Dr. Kissinger and his party arrived here this afternoon to participate in an international symposium on the strategy of China's foreign trade and economic cooperation at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation.
