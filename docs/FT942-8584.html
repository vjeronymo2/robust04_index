940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto closed slightly higher in moderate trading, extending its advance to four straight sessions. The TSE-300 index added 8.9 at 4,278.0 and rises led declines by 422 to 346. Volume was 66.5m shares. Eight of the 14 stock groups rose. The golds, banks, industrial products and mining sectors were firmer, while energy stocks recorded the day's biggest decline, a fall of nearly 1 per cent.
