940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / MPs' Swans plea is rejected Mr Roger Freeman, defence procurement minister, yesterday rejected requests from Tyneside Labour MPs Mr Steve Byers and Mr Nick Brown for design work to be allocated by the government to the design team of Swan Hunter, the Tyneside shipbuilder which is facing closure. Mr Freeman said Swans had to win work by competitive tender. Special arrangements could not be made.
