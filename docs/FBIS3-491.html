Language: Somali Article Type:BFN [Text] A spokesman for the Somali National Alliance [SNA] has said that Pakistani troops threw out wounded Somalis who had been admitted to their hospitals. The SNA spokesman described the move as the worst violation of human rights, adding that the Pakistani troops had previously committed such a violation. The spokesman added that some of the patients who had been ejected had died. He appealed to Somalis to take patients to Somali hospitals.
