Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- The withdrawal of the German UN contingent in Somalia will begin in early February via a combined land, air, and sea transport operation, German Defense Minister Volker Ruehe told the Bundestag Defense Committee in Bonn today. Personnel as well as sensitive equipment unsuitable for maritime shipment will be transported back to Germany by air. Seven ships are envisaged to bring back the bulk of the materiel. During the withdrawal, the troops will make use of a stopover point halfway between Mogadishu and the German base at Beledweyne, as well as organize air and sea transshipment in the Somali capital. The last ship is due to leave Mogadishu on 19 March. [passage omitted]
