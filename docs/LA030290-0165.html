March 2, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Loreen Olsen has been named executive vice president and chief operating officer for Irvine City Savings and Loan Assn. Olsen, who joined the S&L in 1989, had previously been senior vice president of finance and operations. In addition, Vicki Harris, formerly branch manager of the main office, was appointed senior vice president, savings/marketing; and Delores Steinwand was promoted to senior vice president, finance/accounting from her previous position of vice president/controller.
