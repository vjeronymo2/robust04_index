920907 FT 07 SEP 92 / International Company News: Sales ahead 9% so far at Menzies SHAREHOLDERS at the annual meeting of John Menzies, the wholesale and retail distributor, were told that sales in the first quarter had increased by 9 per cent over the same period last year although consumer demand in general remained at a low level. The company's cash position was healthy and the cost structure was under tight control. Prospects for the full year depended as always on the Christmas period. The recent referral of the newspaper wholesaling industry to the Monopolies and Mergers Commission would take up much management time, the meeting was told.
