BFN [Text] The president, Dr. Shankar Dayal Sharma, has left New Delhi today on an eight-day state visit to Bulgaria and Romania. A high-level delegation, including the home minister, Mr. S.B. Chavan, and the civil aviation minister, Mr. Ghulam Nabi Azad, and ministers of state, Mr. Arvind Netam and Mr. Salman Kurshid, are accompanying the president. Our correspondent accompanying the president reports that the visit will explore the possibility of strengthening cooperation between India and the two East European countries. The president was seen off at the airport by the vice president, Mr. K.R. Narayanan; the prime minister, Mr. P.V. Narasimha Rao; his cabinet colleagues; the Uttar Pradesh governor, Mr. Motilal Vora; and the three service chiefs. In Bulgaria, six bilateral agreements are to be signed including one on avoidance of double taxation. The highlight of the president's visit to Romania will be adoption of a basic political document by the two countries. The president will also address a joint session of the Romanian senate and the chamber of deputies. The University of Bucharest is to confer an honorary doctorate on Dr. Shankar Dayal Sharma.
