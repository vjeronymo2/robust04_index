940919 FT 19 SEP 94 / Company News (This Week): Bertelsmann - Other companies One of the world's largest media groups, and still privately owned, presents its annual results tomorrow, having forecast that turnover will rise by 6 per cent to over DM18bn (Dollars 11.4bn). The Gutersloh-based group seems to have put the Vox debacle behind it and has been focusing on a new pay-tv venture.
