Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Edict No. 2284 of the Russian Federation President dated 24 December 1993 and signed by Russian Federation President B.N. Yeltsin: "On the State Program for the Privatization of State and Municipal Enterprises in the Russian Federation"] [Text] For the purposes of deepening economic reforms and guaranteeing the stability of the legal bases of the unified market for facilities subject to privatization in the Russian Federation, and in line with Paragraphs 2 and 3 of the Russian Federation president's Edict No. 1598 of 7 October 1993 "On Legal Regulation During the Period of Stage-By-Stage Constitutional Reform in the Russian Federation," I decree: 1. That the State Program for the Privatization of State and Municipal Enterprises in the Russian Federation presented by the Russian Federation Council of Ministers-Government be ratified and that it be brought into force as of 1 January 1994. 2. That it be incumbent upon the Russian Federation Council of Ministers-Government: to draw up proposals for the introduction of the appropriate amendments and additions to existing Russian Federation tax legislation by 25 January 1994; to prepare and publish by 25 January 1994 a Register of Enterprises Under Flotation in order to ensure the right of Russian citizens to make use of their privatization checks; to bring normative acts promulgated on previous occasions into line with the State Program for the Privatization of State and Municipal Enterprises in the Russian Federation by 1 February 1994; to prepare by 1 March 1994 normative acts guaranteeing the fulfillment of the State Program for the Privatization of State and Municipal Enterprises in the Russian Federation; to draw up draft Russian Federation Laws "On the Privatization of State and Municipal Educational Establishments" and "On the Privatization of Health Institutions and Pharmacy Establishments and Enterprises" by 1 March 1994; to elaborate by 1 March 1994 a draft Russian Federation Law on transforming state enterprises into treasury-funded [kazennyye] plants (factories, institutes, and other organizations) financed directly from budget funds; to elaborate and ratify by 1 March 1994 a Statute on the Procedure for the Use of Civil Defense Facilities and Property by Privatized Enterprises, Establishments, and Organizations. 3. That it be incumbent upon the Russian Federation State Committee for the Management of State Property together with the Russian Federation State Committee for Questions of Architecture and Construction and the Russian Federation Ministry of Culture to elaborate by 1 March 1994 a Standard Protection Contract to be concluded on the privatization of vacant premises located in buildings which constitute historical and cultural monuments. 4. That it be established that the procedures set up under the Russian Federation president's Edict No. 1662 of 19 October 1993 "On Improving Payment Transactions in the Economy and Increasing Liability for Failure To Make Them on Schedule" extend to enterprises at which the share of state ownership comprised less than 25 percent at the time that the Russian Federation president's Edict No. 1662 of 19 October 1993 was issued. 5. That it be incumbent upon the Commission for Securities and Stock Exchanges under the Russian Federation president to approve within two months a single form of certificate of entitlement to complete transactions involving securities and the procedure for issuing it. 6. That it be incumbent upon the Russian Federation State Committee for the Management of State Property and territorial property management committees to carry out in the period leading up to 1 July 1994 the necessary work to compile a list of property belonging to nonstate organizations (trade unions, the former DOSAAF [Voluntary Society for the Promotion of the Army, Aviation, and Navy], consumer cooperatives) founded at the expense of state budget funds. 7. That the following decisions by the Russian Federation Supreme Soviet aimed at curtailing the process of privatization in the Russian Federation be deemed null and void: Russian Federation Supreme Soviet Presidium Decree No. 2626-1 of 30 March 1992 "On the Rightful Succession of the Russian Federal Property Fund in Respect of Part of the Property of Former USSR State Structures." Paragraph 1, sub-paragraph "a" of Russian Federation Supreme Soviet Decree No. 5475-1 of 21 July 1993 "On Introducing Amendments and Additions to Russian Federation Supreme Soviet Decree No. 3020-1 of 27 December 1991 'On Separating State Property in the Russian Federation into Federal Property, the State Property of Republics Within the Russian Federation, Krays, Oblasts, the Autonomous Oblast, Autonomous Okrugs, the Cities of Moscow and St. Petersburg, and Municipal Property." 8. That it be incumbent upon organs of state power and administration of the republics within the Russian Federation, the autonomous oblast, autonomous okrugs, krays, oblasts, the cities of Moscow and St. Petersburg, and the relevant property management committees to bring republic and local privatization programs into line with the State Program for the Privatization of State and Municipal Enterprises in the Russian Federation within two months from the day of its publication. 9. That it be incumbent upon the Russian Federation State Committee for Statistics together with the Russian Federation State Committee for the Management of State Property within a three-month period to work out the procedure and format for the presentation of statistical accounts on privatization by enterprises. 10. That the State Program for the Privatization of State and Municipal Enterprises in the Russian Federation be published. 11. That this edict be submitted for examination by the Russian Federation Federal Assembly. 12. That this edict enter into force from the moment of its signing. [Signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin [Dated] Moscow, The Kremlin, 24 December 1993 No. 2284
