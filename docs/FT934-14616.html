931015 FT 15 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Scantronic buys distributor Scantronic Holdings, the electronic data communications group, has acquired Alarmexpress Holdings for Pounds 370,000 cash and a deferred consideration to a maximum Pounds 650,000. Alarmexpress is a distributor of security and related products, including those manufactured by Scantronic. It made profits of Pounds 57,000 before tax for the 12 months to October 31, with an extraordinary charge of Pounds 1.4m relating to misappropriations by a subsidiary company director which left the company with a deficit on net assets of Pounds 1.1m.
