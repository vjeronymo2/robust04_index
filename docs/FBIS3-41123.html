Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Today"--BTA lead] [Excerpt] Sofia, January 26 (BTA) -- The Svilengrad District Court sentenced Erfan Erfi of Macedonia to three years and eight months imprisonment for attempting to smuggle 10.230 kg of unrefined heroin in an Opel Manta on September 4, 1993. Jaroslaw Nedzulka, a Polish national caught in an attempt to carry 10.430 kg of heroin illegally across the border in an Audi, received a six year prison sentence. Andrzej Dromond, who travelled together with Nedzulka, was acquitted. The drug traffickers will also pay fines, and their cars are confiscated.
