940228 FT 28 FEB 94 / MP calls for GP funds review A National Audit Office investigation into the allocation of money to fundholding general practitioners has been demanded by Mr Alan Milburn, Labour MP for Darlington. Mr Milburn said yesterday that audited figures he had obtained from the government showed that in 1991-92, fundholders returned to the Department of Health only Pounds 2.5m of their Pounds 17m budget surpluses.
