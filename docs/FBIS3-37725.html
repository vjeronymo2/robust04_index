Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow February 23 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin said on Wednesday he is convinced that the national armed forces will remain loyal to the constitution and pledged to ensure social benefits for the military. "I am convinced that the personnel of the Russian Armed Forces will remain loyal to the constitution, the military oath and duty, cherish the military rank and army comradeship", Yeltsin said in a message of greetings to the armed forces and war veterans on the occasion of the day of motherland defenders marked on February 23. "The armed forces are a core of the Russian statehood, guarantor of peace, stability and democratic gains. They should be powerful, mobile and equipped with the most efficient armaments. The state will do everything to increase the prestige of military profession and make servicemen and members of their families feel better protected in the social sphere," the president added. "The day of motherland defenders is a special holiday. It is marked by the entire people, who pay tribute and show respect and gratitude to Russian servicemen. "Russian servicemen were the first to counteract forces, which tried to destroy our state with fire and sword and enslave our people. They saved Russia in the most difficult, mortally dangerous moments of war. "You, the servicemen of the armed forces, are worthy heirs and successors of military traditions. At a time of trial you show restraint and firmness and honestly fulfill the professional and civic duty. "I wish you, dear servicemen, your dear and near, veterans and civilian personnel of the armed forces, health, happiness and prosperity. Glory, honor and gratitude to you for your hard work and success to you in life and military service!" says the message.
