BFN [Text] In a letter addressed to the Nonaligned Movement conference convening in Cairo, brother Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, has said that the statements made by Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin on Jerusalem cast doubt on Israel's willingness to comply with the self-rule accords signed with the PLO. Brother President Abu-'Ammar said: The prime minister of the Israeli entity made statements in which he said Jerusalem is the united capital of Israel forever. Abu-'Ammar added that these statements are a violation of the agreement signed in Washington on 13 September, particularly with regard to Jerusalem. He said that these statements call into question the willingness of Israel and its leaders to comply with signed agreements. He noted that the Declaration of Principles on the self-rule stipulates that the final status of Jerusalem will be discussed during the last phase of talks together with the issues of settlements and refugees.
