940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / International Company News: Asahi posts 17% pre-tax increase Asahi Breweries, the second largest brewer in Japan, posted a 17.3 per cent increase in non-consolidated pre-tax profits to Y9.98bn (Dollars 100m) in the first half to end-June, on sales 7.7 per cent higher at Y375.8bn, writes Emiko Terazono in Tokyo. The company said cost-cutting efforts and strong sales of beer and soft drinks helped boost profits, which at the after-tax level advanced 26.9 per cent to Y3.2bn. The interim dividend is being maintained at Y4 a share.
