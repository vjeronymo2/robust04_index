BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondent] [Text] Wuensdorf, 24 May -- Russian Federation Defense Minister Pavel Grachev today left the the western group of forces for Brussels where he will acquaint the NATO leadership with the basic tenets of Russia's military doctrine and his position on the "Partnership for Peace" program. The ITAR-TASS correspondent has learned from relaible sources that the Russian minister will not, as previously planned, fly to Moscow after his official visit to Belgium -- which is to take place on 26 27 May -- but will return instead to the western group of forces. He intends to take part in a ceremony seeing-off the air force of the western group of forces.
