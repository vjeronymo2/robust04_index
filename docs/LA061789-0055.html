June 17, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

'NAPOLEON' SCREENINGS

I was somewhat disappointed in your May 30 Morning Report item on Frances Coppola and the film "Napoleon." (A feature of this film is my musical score, which I conduct.) "Napoleon" is not being brought "out of exile," as your article says, but has been going strong in major cities as recently as six months ago -- in Hanover, Valencia, Madrid, etc.

CARMINE COPPOLA

Woodland Hills
