931129 FT 29 NOV 93 / Ankara PM in reshuffle Turkish Prime Minister Tansu Ciller yesterday reshuffled her cabinet, changing five ministers of her own True Path party, Reuter reports from Ankara. Mrs Ciller, who took office in June, made no changes among ministers of her coalition government's junior partner, the Social Democrat Populist party, the Anatolian news agency reported. All the True Path ministers had resigned to give Mrs Ciller a free hand to reshape her cabinet.
