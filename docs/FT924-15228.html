921006 FT 06 OCT 92 / Rail work for Rothschild BRITISH RAIL has appointed NM Rothschild, the merchant bank, to help it draw up ways of charging train operators for use of its railway tracks after privatisation. The government's adviser on track charging is the consultancy arm of Coopers & Lybrand, the accountancy firm. The Department of Transport is also being advised on privatisation by Samuel Montagu, the merchant bank, and accountants KPMG Peat Marwick.
