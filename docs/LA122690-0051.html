December 26, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: MISCELLANY

. . . Marcus O'Sullivan, winner of the Wanamaker Mile in five of the last six Millrose Games, will run in the event in the 1991 meet, Feb. 1 at Madison Square Garden. Another victory would make O'Sullivan the third most prolific winner in the 66-year history of the race, behind Eamonn Coghlan and Glenn Cunningham.
