Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Egyptian Foreign Ministry has decided to recall its ambassador from Israel for consultations following the Israeli massacre. Reports say that Egypt, the Palestinians, and the Israelis are holding exchanges aiming to find an acceptable response to the massacre. Today, a large number of the Israeli diplomatic mission's personnel left Cairo as part of the security precautions adopted by the Israelis in Egypt for fear of reprisals to the massacre.
