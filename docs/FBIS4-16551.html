BFN [Text] Yasushi Akashi and Vitaliy Churkin, special envoys of the UN secretary general and the Russian president, held talks with the leader of the Bosnian Serbs Radovan Karadzic in the Sarajevo suburb of Pale today. This was the first meeting between Akashi and Karadzic since NATO planes bombed Serb positions near Gorazde a week ago. An ITAR-TASS correspondent has learned at the Zagreb headquarters of peacekeeping forces that at the Pale meeting they discussed conditions for a cease-fire under which Bosnian Serbs could suspend their offensive on Gorazde.
