940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Alexander Russell Alexander Russell, the Glasgow-based quarrying, concrete and coal company, reported pre-tax profits more than doubled from Pounds 632,000 to Pounds 1.45m for the six months to June 30. The advance was achieved on turnover up from Pounds 17.6m to Pounds 18.9m and after a reduced interest charge of Pounds 458,000 (Pounds 761,000). Earnings came out at 3.34p (0.75p) and the interim dividend is doubled to 1p.
