920407 FT 07 APR 92 / Small Accountancy firms far more pessimistic SMALL ACCOUNTANCY firms are more pessimistic about their prospects than they were four months ago, a survey from the London Society of Chartered Accountants shows. According to Mr Philip Hollins, a partner with accountants Hacker Young, who co-ordinated the survey, a third of the 250 respondents were less optimistic for the next four months than they were for the previous four. New audit regulation mechanisms and other professional regulations were the most important factors.
