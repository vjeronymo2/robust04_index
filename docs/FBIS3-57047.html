Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report in the "Provincial Chronicle" column] [Text] Kazan -- As of 1 February polling stations will be open for repeat elections to the Russian Federation Federal Assembly scheduled for 13 March. According to officials at the Tatarstan Central Election Commission, no changes are expected in the stations' operating procedures. The coordinating council of the republic's sociopolitical organizations incorporating more than 30 groups issued a statement containing an appeal to renounce the repeat elections. The council believes that no elections "must be held until a treaty has been signed between the Russian Federation and the Tatarstan Republic."
