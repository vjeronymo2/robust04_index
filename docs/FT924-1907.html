921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / BP to shed another 9,000 jobs British Petroleum is to shed a further 9,000 jobs worldwide by 1995 as part of a cost-cutting drive. This will take its expected job losses to 20,500, or 20 per cent of the workforce. The company had previously announced plans to cut 11,500 jobs by early 1993. Report, Page 23
