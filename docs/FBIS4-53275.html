BFN [Text] Senior General Than Shwe, chairman of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] of the Union of Myanmar [Burma] and commander in chief of the Defense Services, received the visiting delegation from the People's Republic of China led by Yunnan Province Governor He Zhiqiang at the Ministry of Defense's Dagon House at 1000 today. Also present were General Maung Aye, vice chairman of SLORC and deputy commander in chief of the Defense Services; Lieutenant General Khin Nyunt, secretary-1 of SLORC; Lt. Gen. Tin U, secretary-2 of SLORC; Brigadier General Abel, minister of national planning and economic development; U Ohn Gyaw, minister of foreign affairs; Thura U Aung Htet, director general of the Protocol Department; and Liang Feng, PRC ambassador to Burma.
