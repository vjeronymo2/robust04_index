Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] At the invitation of U Ohn Gyaw, minister of foreign affairs of the Union of Myanmar [Burma], a seven-member Cuban delegation led by Roberto Robaina Gonzalez, foreign minister of the Republic of Cuba, arrived this evening on a Thai International flight for a visit to Myanmar. The Cuban foreign minister and delegation were welcomed at the airport by Foreign Minister U Ohn Gyaw, Deputy Foreign Minister U Nyunt Swe, and high-ranking officials from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Foreign Affairs Minister U Ohn Gyaw received the visiting Cuban foreign minister at Karaweik Hall at 1800 this evening. They held a meeting during which they exchanged views on matters of bilateral interests in a friendly and cordial manner. At the meeting, the Myanmar side was represented by Foreign Affairs Minister U Ohn Gyaw, Deputy Foreign Affairs Minister U Nyunt Swe, and high-ranking officials, while the Cuban side was represented by the visiting Cuban foreign minister and delegation. At 1930, Foreign Affairs Minister U Ohn Gyaw hosted a dinner for the esteemed guests.
