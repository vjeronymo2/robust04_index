920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Dixons sees mild recovery Mr Stanley Kalms, chairman of Dixons, yesterday confirmed anecdotal evidence of a mild recovery in UK consumer spending by telling shareholders that the electrical retailing group had shown a 'more positive trend' in sales since early July. However, he said there had been no significant changes in the group's other businesses, which span property development in Europe and electrical retailing in the US.
