921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There were 14,095 packages for the day, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Landed prices for the smaller quantity of Assams on offer were often 15 to 20p dearer particularly for better liquoring sorts. East African teas also met sustained support and advanced by 5 to 15p with brightest descriptions and good mediums featuring very strongly. Offshore improved demand at 10 to 20p dearer rates. The highest price realised this week was 311p for a burundi pf. 1 Quotations: quality 225p, good medium 180p, medium 160p, low medium 115p.
