BFN [Text] Beijing, May 9 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing met with Salim Ibrahim, vice-president of the U.S.-based Du Pont company, and his party here this afternoon. Ibrahim, who is also chairman of the World Textile Association, arrived here may 7 as guest of the China National Textile Council. Later, Vice-Premier Li met and had a friendly conversation with Nguyen Trong Vinh, former Vietnamese ambassador to China, and his group. The Vietnamese guests arrived here May 7 at the invitation of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs (CPIFA). President Liu Shuqing of the CPIFA today also met with them and hosted a dinner in their honor.
