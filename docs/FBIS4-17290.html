BFN [POSTFACTUM report: "One-Shot Action To Cut All Taxes Possible in Fall"] [Text] Aleksandr Shokhin has advised that a one-shot action is possible in Russia in fall 1994 cutting back all taxes 10 to 20 percent which, he thinks, would considerably liven up production. He pointed out, however, that this is possible only if the money not to be received into the budget is made up for from the proposed reserve standby credit ($4 billion) that may be received as soon as in the fall.
