BFN [Report by C. Govender] [Text] Durban April 26 SAPA -- Special arrangements have been made for the issuing of temporary voters cards (TVCs) during the three-day election period, KwaZulu government spokesman T C Memela announced on Tuesday. He said TVC's would be attainable at all KwaZulu polling booths. KwaZulu citizenship certificates could also be used as identity documents, said Mr Memela. Persons in possession of such a certificate need not apply for a voter card, he added.
