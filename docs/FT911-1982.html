910502 FT 02 MAY 91 / Appointments: Chairman of Iveco Ford Truck Mr Giancarlo Boschetti has been appointed chairman of IVECO FORD TRUCK, Watford, and Mr Alan B. Ford to the new post of managing director. Mr Felice Cantarocco retires as chairman and chief executive, but will remain on the board. He was recently elected vice president of the Society of Motor Manufacturers and Traders. ***** Mr Bob Murray has been appointed a non-executive director of FKI. He is chairman of The Sovereign Capital Corporation. ***** GRIFFITHS & ARMOUR, insurance brokers, has appointed as partners Mr Andrew Johnstone, Mr Nicholas Brace, Mr Kevin Driscoll and Mr Derek Murray. Mr Brian Griffiths and Mr Alan Kilgour retire as partners on May 31 but remain as consultants. ***** Mr Ian Leigh has been appointed group finance director of COLORVISION, Speke. He was group financial controller. Mr Colin Lewis, non-executive finance director, has resigned. Mr Cliff Proctor has retired from the group board to concentrate on his role as part of the senior management team. Mr Bernard Michaelson, a founding shareholder and director, becomes a non-executive director. ***** Mr Frank Carter has been appointed sales director of REFUGE ASSURANCE, main operating company of Refuge Group, Wilmslow. He was director and general manager of the group's personal loan subsidiary, RLJ Finance. ***** Mr Steve Appleyard has been appointed chief executive of LEGRAND ELECTRIC, Dunstable. He was managing director of MTE. Mr Appleyard replaces Ms Dominique Montion who is moving to a French manufacturing subsidiary in the Legrand Group. ***** ISOTRON, Swindon, has appointed Dr John Woolston as sales and marketing director. He was marketing and technical manager. Mr Terry Summers, sales director, retires at the end of June. ***** ALPHA WIRE, Feltham, has appointed Mr Bob Williams as managing director. He joins from Alpha Wire Corporation's headquarters in New Jersey, US. ***** Mr John Oakley, chairman of GROSVENOR DEVELOPMENT CAPITAL, is retiring in September, when he will be succeeded by Mr David Beattie, managing director. Mr Peter Gale, of County NatWest Investment Management, and Mr Tim Amies, a former partner of stockbrokers Laurie Milbank, have joined the board. On his retirement from County NatWest Investment Management Mr Richard Cutler has resigned as a director of Grosvenor Development Capital. ***** ALLIED DUNBAR ASSET MANAGEMENT has appointed Mr Howard Maguire as head of UK equities from May 1. He joins from Hill Samuel Investment Management where he held a similar post. ***** MICRO AUTOMATION has appointed Dr Paul Williams as executive chairman. He was managing director of TA Group. ***** Mr Stephen Liddle has been appointed a director of GEOFFREY OSBORNE HOMES. He was building manager. ***** ROLLS-ROYCE MOTOR CARS, a Vickers company, has appointed Mr Alastair Clark as a non-executive director. He is head of financial markets and institutions division at the Bank of England. ***** Vickers marine engineering division has appointed Mr Craig Hooson as commercial director, based in Newcastle upon Tyne. He was senior manager in the business appraisal department of Vickers. ***** Mr Simon Hall and Mr Nigel Renton have been appointed joint managing directors of Fraser Bloodstock, and Mr David Gibbons becomes managing director of Fraser Agricultural Risks, both companies in the FRASER GROUP. ***** SMITH BARNEY, HARRIS UPHAM & CO INC, New York, has appointed Mr Charles W. Santoro as a managing director, and head of cross border investment banking. He will be operating out of London and New York. ***** Sir Hugh Bidwell has been appointed non-executive chairman of RIGGS AP BANK, succeeding Sir James Cleminson who is retiring but remains a non-executive director. Sir Hugh is chairman of British Invisibles, vice chairman of Allied-Lyons Eastern, and a director of Argyll Group. ***** Mr Alan Curtis (pictured), chairman of Group Lotus, is to join Formula One's TEAM LOTUS as a non-executive director.
