941215 FT 15 DEC 94 / Increased use of Thames urged More use should be made of the River Thames for tourists, commuters and commercial users, the River Thames Working Group, chaired by transport minister Mr Steven Norris, said yesterday. It called for better promotion and co-ordination of tourist passenger services and other leisure amenities on the river, a review of the location of piers and further promotion of opportunities to develop bulk cargoes on the river through the freight facilities grant scheme.
