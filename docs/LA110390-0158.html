November 3, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

SPOTLIGHT / COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE: FOCUS: TRIANGLE SQUARE

Triangle Square, a $60-million retail and entertainment complex, is being built as part of Costa Mesa's redevelopment program. The Mediterranean-style building will feature restaurants, shops, an eight-screen cinema and a 40,000-square-foot supermarket.

Also included will be a half-acre Town Square, an open-air courtyard where entertainment and community events will be held.

FACTS AND FIGURES

Address: Harbor Boulevard, Newport Boulevard and 19th Street, Costa Mesa

Owner/Developer: Joint venture of Triangle Square Associates & Capital and Counties USA Inc.

Architect: Altoon & Porter, Los Angeles

Contractor: Keller Construction, El Monte

Financing: Dai-Ichi Kangyo Bank, Los Angeles and Citicorp Real Estate, Costa Mesa

Scheduled opening: Spring, 1992

Total acreage: 4.5

Leasable space in square feet: 185,000

Parking capacity: 1,160

Source: The Bower Agency Inc.
