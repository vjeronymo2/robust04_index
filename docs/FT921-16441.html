920103 FT 03 JAN 92 / Albanian leader's heart attack Albania's President Ramiz Alia has suffered a heart attack but his condition is now good, Albanian radio reported yesterday, Reuter writes. The radio, monitored by the British Broadcasting Corporation in London, quoted a health ministry statement as saying that Mr Alia, 66, suffered the attack early on Wednesday. He became president in 1985 on the death of Stalinist leader Enver Hoxha, who had ruled for over 40 years.
