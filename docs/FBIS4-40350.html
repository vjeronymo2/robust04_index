BFN [Text] In the evening of June 14 Abkhazia cut back by 80 percent the supply to Georgia of electricity produced at the Inguri power station. Vice Premier of the Abkhaz government, Vladimir Mikanba, told Interfax that this was in retaliation at the acts of sabotage carried out by Georgian forces in the Gali region of Abkhazia. Electricity cables were destroyed leading to a loss of supply to a number of regions, including the capital Sukhumi.
