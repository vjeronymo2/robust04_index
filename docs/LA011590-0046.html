January 15, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

POLL POOPERS: One problem with polls is that they depend on honest answers. . . . A post-election survey on Measure M -- last fall's unsuccessful transportation tax -- found that 24% of the people who claimed to have voted actually didn't. . . . "People notoriously over-represent their voting behavior," said pollster Mark Baldassare, who did not work on this survey. "People are embarrassed about the fact that they don't vote." Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
