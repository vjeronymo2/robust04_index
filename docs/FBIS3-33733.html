Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 31, IRNA -- Secretary General of Lebanon's Hizballah, Sa'id Hasan Nasrallah, today briefed Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati on the latest developments in his country and in the region. Nasrallah, here at the head of a delegation to take part in the ahl-ul-bait conference, referred to the Zionist regime's recent aggressions on villages in southern Lebanon, the Zionists new plots to stir national unity and violate Lebanon's sovereignty. He underlined his determination to resist against the occupying forces and defuse their plots. Velayati in return underscored unity between Islamic and national forces against Zionist plots and called for their vigilance and resoluteness. He also said that he was ready to support the rightful and just defence of the Lebanese Muslims against the "inhumane" aggressions of the Zionist regime to free the occupied lands and restore territorial integrity of Lebanon.
