Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id and Her Excellency Pakistani Prime Minister Mrs. Benazir Bhutto held a session of official talks at the al-Hisn Palace in Salalah today. Talks dealt with relations between the two friendly states and ways to promote them in various fields in a way that would serve the interests of both the Omani and Pakistani peoples. The two sides also exchanged views on several international and regional issues of common concern. [passage omitted]
