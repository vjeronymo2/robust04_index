November 8, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

MARINA HIGH'S SORTINO PLANS TO ATTEND USD

Melisa Sortino, a guard for the Marina High School girls' basketball team, has made an oral commitment to attend the University of San Diego next year, her mother, Diane, said Tuesday.

Sortino, a 5-foot-7 senior, averaged 20 points a game for the Vikings last season.
