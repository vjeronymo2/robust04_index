Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Pirot, 24 Mar (TANJUG) -- The goal is the destruction of all things Slav and of orthodoxy, and that is a big war against Russia. You are our first line of defense, Aleksandr Vengerovskiy, vice president of the Russian Liberal Democratic Party, said at the Pirot municipality headquarters this evening. Vengerovskiy is heading a group of Duma deputies visiting Yugoslavia. Vengerovskiy said that the Duma deputies arrived in Yugoslavia with the intention of learning about the situation in this country so that the Russian parliament could help with the abolition of sanctions against Yugoslavia. The Russian Duma deputies, who are in Yugoslavia at the invitation of the Serbian National Renewal [Srpska Narodna Obnova], will visit several places in Serbia and Montenegro.
