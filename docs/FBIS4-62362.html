BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan, 14 May -- The ways of the settlement of the Karabakh conflict were the subject of the talks held in Yerevan today between Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan and other members of the Armenian leadership, on the one hand, and Jan Eliasson, chairman of the CSCE Minsk Group on Nagorno-Karabakh, his deputy Mathias Mossberg, and Vladimir Kazimirov, representative of the Russian president and head of the Russian mediatory mission on the other. The CSCE and Russian representatives arrived in Yerevan from Baku on Friday [13 May] evening. It is not yet known what specifically was discussed at the talks. Armenian official services only confirmed the fact of the meeting. In the afternoon the joint mission of the CSCE and Russia went to Nagorno-Karabakh where it will hold talks with the leadership of the Republic.
