920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Multitrust net asset value at 35.64p Net asset value at Multitrust was 35.64p per share at the December 31 interim stage, up from 33.56p a year before but down from 41.92p at the June 30 year-end. At the pre-tax level the trust achieved Pounds 83,944 in the six months, against losses of Pounds 21,014. After tax of Pounds 21,134 (Pounds 1,876) earnings emerged at 1.45p per share (losses 0.53p) and a maiden interim dividend of 1.5p is declared. The board intends to pay a further 1p at the year-end.
