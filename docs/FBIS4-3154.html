BFN [From the "News About China" program] [Text] Visiting Chinese State Councilor Song Jian has said in New York that he hopes Sino-U.S. cooperation in science and technology will be further expanded and that the present cooperation between the two countries is a model for political and business relations of the future. He made the remark at a reception held by China's consul general in New York. The reception was also attended by more than 100 leaders from science and business circles in New York.
