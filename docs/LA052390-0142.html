May 23, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

4-A BASEBALL;

ROUNDUP

Covina 9, Brea-Olinda 4 -- Ryan Ott went three for four and drove in a run for visiting Brea-Olinda but the Wildcats gave up three unearned runs in the second inning and Covina led 4-0.

Marc Kaiser (4-3) allowed six runs and five hits to take the loss as the Wildcats finished the season 17-9-1. Covina is 19-7.
