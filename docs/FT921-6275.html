920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Standby credit for Aran Energy Aran Energy, the Dublin-based oil and gas exploration and production group, has completed a Dollars 197.5m (Pounds 113m) revolving credit and standby facility which will be used to develop the Alba North and Gryphon fields in the North Sea. The 10-year facility was arranged by the Bank of Scotland and follows Aran's 1990 rights issue which raised IPounds 37.4m.
