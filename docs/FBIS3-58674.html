Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Jan 18 (AA) -- The United States Secretary of State Warren Christopher on Tuesday [18 January] sent a message to Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin saying that the outlawed separatist terrorist organization should be hold in a mechanism that will be set up between the U.S. and Syrian foreign ministers. [sentence as received] Christopher speaking on a meeting between the U.S. President Bill Clinton and Syrian Head of State Hafiz al-Asad in Geneva said that Clinton had warned al-Asad on the support Syria is giving to terrorist organization. The U.S. President had qualified the outlawed organization as the number one terror organization adding that his country and Syria had important differences on the issue.
