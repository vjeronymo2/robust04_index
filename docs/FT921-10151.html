920207 FT 07 FEB 92 / London house prices down 15% THE VALUE of London's housing stock is Pounds 27bn down since the end of 1988 according to figures from the Association of London Authorities. Average house prices fell 15% from Pounds 104,900 in the final quarter of 1988 to Pounds 89,200 at the end of last year. The association's figures are based on average prices paid by Halifax building society customers. The biggest fall was in Tower Hamlets, which includes Docklands, where average house prices fell from Pounds 141,600 to Pounds 89,200.
