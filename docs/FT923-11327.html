920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Simon Engineering Pounds 0.5m purchase Simon Engineering, the engineering, environmental and industrial services group, has acquired the oilfield production chemical sales and services business in the Uintah basin district of Utah from Jetco Chemicals, a subsidiary of Procter & Gamble, for Dollars 1m (Pounds 520,000). The purchase was carried out through Simon's Unichem International subsidiary.
