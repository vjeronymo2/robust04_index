January 22, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEW BOOKS: AROUND HOME;

SUSAN DUCKWORTH'S KNITTING

SUSAN DUCKWORTH (Ballantine Books; 1988)

IT MUST BE England's chilly climate that inspires so many handsomely designed sweaters. Duckworth uses all-over patterns (flowers, peacocks, ducks), lots of colors -- and tiny needles. But the book is more than a series of instructions; each chapter is introduced with a very pretty arrangement, and in the back of the book is a colorful spread of gloves. ($25) Judith Sims
