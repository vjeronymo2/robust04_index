Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 28 (YONHAP -- South Korea and China concluded a bilateral cultural agreement and a double taxation prevention agreement here on Monday during President Kim Yong-sam's current official visit to China. The signing took place at the Great Hall of People immediately after the summit between President Kim and Chinese President Jiang Zemin. The cultural agreement was signed by Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu and Chinese Cultural Minister Liu Zhongde and the double taxation prevention agreement by Minister Han and Chinese Finance Minister Liu Zhongli. Both agreements are to take effect 30 days after the two countries notified each other of the completion of their respective parliamentary ratification and other necessary domestic procedures.
