Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A security source announced this evening that the Lebanese and Syrian security agencies in the north arrested a network involved in explosions in Tripoli and its suburbs. Two of the network members, Marwan 'Ali Mir'i from al-Qalamun and Jihad Kamal Khalil from Tripoli, admitted that they carried out the following operations: Blowing up a shop in al-Bahsas on 31 October, blowing up a shop in al-Tall in al-Kurah Square on 10 December, blowing up a shop on al-Jummayzat Street in Tripoli on 14 December, blowing up (?the Windiz) shop in al-Mina on 23 December, and blowing up a shop in al-Zahriyah in Tripoli on 28 December. The authorities concerned are pursuing the investigations to uncover all those involved.
