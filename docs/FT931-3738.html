930313 FT 13 MAR 93 / Pounds 3.9bn help urged for low-paid A Pounds 3.9bn package of support for the low-paid financed by modest tax increases on high-income earners will be proposed today by the Low Pay Unit in a submission to the chancellor. The proposals include raising tax allowances and age-related personal allowances by Pounds 350, changes in national insurance contributions and a Pounds 1-a-week increase in child benefit.
