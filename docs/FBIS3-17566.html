Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shchegoleva] [Text] Sochi March 26 TASS -- Over the past 24 hours of the Georgian-Abkhazian armed standoff, the Abkhazian troops liberated the village of Lata in the Kodorskoye Gorge on the territory of Abkhazia and moved on further in the mountainous region. They have taken over three armoured vehicles, several artillery and anti-aircraft guns, a well-informed source in the Abkhazian Government told ITAR-TASS on Saturday. "The village of Lata and the Kodorskoye Gorge are in the very heart of Abkhazia. We are fighting on Abkhazian territory and our troops are liberating Abkhazian villages," Abkhazian Deputy Prime Minister Vladimir Mikamba said.
