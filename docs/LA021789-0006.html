February 17, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

HOMELESS

All the news is overwhelming about the homeless and homeless children. What's happening to all that money -- the millions of dollars that is confiscated from drug raids? There's enough money there to build shelters and keep the homeless fed. Put those millions to good use. Now is the time. It's a disgrace for any city to have these families sleeping in the streets.

JACK DIAMOND

Los Angeles
