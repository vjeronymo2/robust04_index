July 28, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

PLACING RESPONSIBILITY IN THE JUDAS PRIEST SHOOTING CASE

Nowhere have I seen any indication that the grief-stricken (or is that "greed-stricken"?) parents have been held accountable for leaving two disturbed, drug-addicted boys alone with a quantity of alcohol and dope . . . and a loaded "12-gauge sawed-off shotgun." Isn't possession of a sawed-off shotgun a felony in Nevada?

CHRISTIAN LEOPOLD SHEA

Hollywood
