920523 FT 23 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Interest boost for Lyons Irish A sharp increase in net interest received helped Lyons Irish Holdings, the Dublin-based tea blender, raise pre-tax profits by 23 per cent in the year to March 7. On lower turnover of IPounds 25.1m (IPounds 27.7m) profits rose from IPounds 6.4m to IPounds 7.87m (Pounds 7.2m). Net interest income rose to IPounds 2.66m (IPounds 2.05m). Earnings per share were 48.9p (39p) and a final dividend of 11.5p is recommended for a total payment of 17.2p (14.9p). A one-for-one scrip issue is also proposed.
