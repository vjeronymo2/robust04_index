Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- On Thursday [24 February], the Federal Cabinet decided to establish a Technology Council to make proposals for the purposeful promotion of important fields of research and technology. At the same time, it is expected to contribute to the more rapid transfer of research results into products and technological processes. Focal areas are biotechnology and genetic engineering, new materials, as well as energy, environmental, and transportation technology.
