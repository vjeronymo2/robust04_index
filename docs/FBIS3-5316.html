Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 4. -- Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam held talks here this morning with his Mongolian counterpart T.S.Gombosuren. The two ministers exchanged views on international and regional issues of common concern and disussed measures to promote the economic, commercial cooperation as well as cooperations in other fields agreed upon during the Vietnam--Mongolia high-level talks. The talks took place in an cordial atmosphere and reached fruitful results.
