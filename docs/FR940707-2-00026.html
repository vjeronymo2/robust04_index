DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE Agency Form Under Review by the Office of Management and Budget DOC has submitted to the Office of Management and Budget for clearance the following proposal for collection of information under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act (44 U.S.C. Chapter 35). Agency: Bureau of Export Administration (BXA). Title: Defense Diversification Pilot Survey. Agency Form Number: None assigned. OMB Approval Number: None. Type of Request: New collection. Burden: 4,000 hours. Number of Respondents: 2,000. Avg Hours Per Response: 2 hours. Needs and Uses: Commerce is conducting an assessment of defense subcontractors in order to determine the firms' long&hyph;term strength and viability, as well as to find out what government resources would be useful to them in diversifying their operations.
