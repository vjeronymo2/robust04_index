930828 FT 28 AUG 93 / International Company News: Astra International 50% up at halfway ASTRA International, the Indonesian automotive company, yesterday announced a 50 per cent increase in first-half net profits to Rp63.89bn (Dollars 30.4m), signalling a recovery after a traumatic 1992, writes William Keeling from Jakarta. Net sales increased 19.5 per cent Rp2,521bn, despite a 1 per cent drop in car sales to 47,371 over the year. Brokers attribute the rise in sales to improved figures from the United Tractor's heavy equipment subsidiary.
