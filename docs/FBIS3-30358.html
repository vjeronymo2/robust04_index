Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] A two-hour warning strike, which is to take place on 2 February, has been announced for the entire Silesian DOKP [Regional State Railways Directorate] region by the Regional Solidarity Section of Railway Employees. The strike will start at 0700 [0600 GMT]. The traffic of passenger and freight trains will be halted. The other trade union confederations of the region are also to join in the strike. The trade union members are demanding payment of the arrears of 850,000 zlotys [Z] due to them for last year and a pay raise of Z800,000 for each employee from 1 January. The trade unions have announced that, unless the Polish State Railways [PKP] General Directorate embarks on negotiations, the trade unions will make preparations for an all-out strike.
