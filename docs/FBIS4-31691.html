BFN [Text] Croatian President Franjo Tudjman today received U.S. Ambassador Peter Galbraith in the presidential palace. Tudjman informed him about the activities of the Croatian authorities in implementing the Washington agreement, and about his imminent visit to Sarajevo. He particularly stressed that, despite certain rumors in the public, the establishing of the Croat-Muslim federation is closely linked with the setting up of a conferederation with Croatia. Ambassador Galbraith said that the U.S. leadership holds the same stances on that issues. They also discussed the improved bilateral relations between the two states and specific steps that need to be taken in that sphere. They spoke especially about investments by U.S. firms in the Croatian economy.
