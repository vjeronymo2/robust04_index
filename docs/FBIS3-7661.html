Language: Tagalog Article Type:BFN [Text] The Philippines has lodged a diplomatic protest with Malaysia in connection with the arrest by Malaysian authorities of some 1,000 Filipinos in Kuala Lumpur last Sunday. This was revealed by Alberto Encomienda, Philippine ambassador to Malaysia. According to Encomienda, the arrested Filipinos are working in Kuala Lumpur. They were arrested and reportedly imprisoned by Malaysian authorities after attending a church service. The Filipino workers were arrested when they allegedly failed to present documentary evidence showing that their stay in Malaysia is legal.
