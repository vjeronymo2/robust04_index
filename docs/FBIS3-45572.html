Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 20 (XINHUA) -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey V. Kozyrev will visit China later this month, Wu Jianmin, a spokesman of the Foreign Ministry, confirmed in a weekly press conference this afternoon. "But the specific date is yet to be set through consultation by Chinese and Russian sides, and we will release information specifically covering his visit," Wu said. China and Russia are enjoying very good relations with each other, and the relations between the two countries in all areas have been steadily growing, Wu said. During the Russian foreign minister's visit in China, the two sides will exchange views on bilateral ties as well as international and regional issues, Wu said.
