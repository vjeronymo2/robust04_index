BFN [Text] Complete strike was observed in occupied Kashmir today to protest against official hindrances in the way of people's contact with the visiting Islamic countries' ambassadors in the valley. All shops, business establishments, educational institutions, and private and public offices remained closed throughout the valley. Transport was completely off the road. A spokesman of the All Parties Hurriyat Conference accused the governor of not allowing the public access to the foreign envoys. Meanwhile, another six people have been martyred and many injured in military crackdowns and clashes. Scores of youths were arrested during siege and search operations in different parts of the valley.
