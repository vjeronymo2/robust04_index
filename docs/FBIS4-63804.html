BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Yezhov] [Text] Moscow May 24 TASS -- After a recess on the second day of Russian-Ukrainian talks, during which Russian Premier Viktor Chernomyrdin went to meet President Boris Yeltsin for their regular Tuesday get-together, negotiations resumed with the participation of the Crimean vice-premier, Yevgeniy Saburov. Today's agenda consists of items relating to the Black Sea Fleet and the Crimea. The Russian and Ukrainian delegations headed by Viktor Chernomyrdin and Yukhym Zvyahilskyy are now meeting in a narrow format.
