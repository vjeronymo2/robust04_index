930619 FT 19 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Bridgend falls Pounds 3m into red Bridgend Group, which has interests in distribution and leisure, incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 3.08m for the 1992 year on turnover of Pounds 15.75m. The result was after a property revaluation charge of Pounds 3.39m. In the previous year profits were Pounds 900,000 on turnover of Pounds 17.5m. There was a turnround from interest received of Pounds 22,000 to a charge of Pounds 179,000. There was also an extraordinary charge of Pounds 525,000 (Pounds 669,000) relating to discontinuing the property division. The retained loss was Pounds 3.82m (Pounds 313,000). Losses per share came out at 11.5p (earnings 2.4p). A final dividend of 0.2p is proposed for a total of 0.4p (1.2p).
