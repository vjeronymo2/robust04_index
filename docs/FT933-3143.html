930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / UK Company News: London Forfaiting lifts profits by 25% LONDON FORFAITING, the specialist trade finance house, reported a 25 per cent increase in pre-tax profits in the half year to the end of June as its international network continued to expand. Profits rose from Pounds 8.4m to Pounds 10.5m. More than 80 per cent of its revenue is generated overseas. Forfaiting is the provision of trade finance through fixed rate loans, which are sold at a discount to banks and international companies. Net income before administrative expenses rose to Pounds 15.8m (Pounds 12.5m). The company increased its presence in Hong Kong, New York, Milan and Prague. It recently opened a Paris office. Bank interest receivable and income from certificates of deposits fell to Pounds 1.2m (Pounds 1.9m). Earnings per share increased 25 per cent to 8.03p. The interim dividend rises to 3.2p (2.9p).
