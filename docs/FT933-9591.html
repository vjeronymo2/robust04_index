930809 FT 09 AUG 93 / Observer: Law maker Contrary to expectations, the recent death of Professor Northcote Parkinson, best remembered for his pronouncements of the blindingly obvious, has not curtailed the supply of new Parkinson's laws. Yesterday, Lord Parkinson, the former Tory party chairman, produced one of his own on Anthony Clare's radio programme, In the Psychiatrist's Chair: 'In politics people like to give you what they think you deserve and deny you what they think you want.'
