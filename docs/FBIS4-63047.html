BFN [Unattributed report: "The Military Will Be Put on a Starvation Diet"] [Text] As MOSKOVSKIY KOMSOMOLETS has learned, Viktor Chernomyrdin has sent to the State Duma a draft federal law: On Changes, Suspension, and Revocation of Some Legislative Enactments Bearing on the Military Laws Package. In a cover letter, the prime minister notes that owing to the existing financial and economic situation, a shortage of funds and a budget deficit, it is impossible to preserve privileges for the military. It will take 90 trillion rubles [R] to implement all the measures envisioned earlier and therefore it is essential to reduce food ration compensations from R60,000 to the minimum monthly wage level (R14,620) and fully to abolish credits for housing construction programs for the military.
