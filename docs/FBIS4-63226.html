BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yuriy Grachev] [Text] Vladivostok May 20 -- A Chinese naval squadron left Vladivostok on Friday, heading home, upon completing the programme for a four-day friendly visit to Russia's far east. The farewell ceremony was attended by Vice-Admiral Igor Khmelnov, acting commander of Russia's Pacific Fleet, Cong Guimin, military attache of the Chinese embassy in the Russian Federation, and by other officials. As a token of gratitude for Russian hospitality, the guests planted a tree of friendship on the shore of the Golden Horn Bay.
