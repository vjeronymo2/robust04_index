Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Feb. 3, IRNA -- Heshmatollah Mujeddedi here today called on the Islamic Republic of Iran to send relief aid to Afghan refugees in southern deserts of Afghanistan. Mujeddedi, who is here at the head of a delegation from Afghanistan's Supreme Islamic revolution Coordination Council, in a meeting with Deputy Foreign Minister for Asia-Pacific, Ala'ddin Borujerdi, expressed regrets over the ongoing clashes in Afghanistan. He also thanked Iran for its efforts to end clashes in Afghanistan and urged the Iranian officials to continue their efforts to put an end to the hostilities. Borujerdi said that as before Tehran would do all it can to provide the Afghan refugees with their requirements. He expressed concern over the present situation in Afghanistan and said Iran would spare no efforts to help Afghanistan solve its problems.
