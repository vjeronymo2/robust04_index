940822 FT 22 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): OMV - Other companies The Austrian integrated oil and chemicals group returned to profit in the first quarter after two years of losses, and is expected to reveal modest figures in the black for the second quarter tomorrow. Investors will be looking for confirmation that the group's aggressive cost-cutting and economic recovery will combine to produce a strong jump in cyclical earnings jump next year, even in the still loss-making chemicals and plastics businesses.
