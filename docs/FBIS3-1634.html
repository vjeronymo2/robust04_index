Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Reporter Guo Lihua (6753 4409 5478)] [Text] Shanghai, 27 Feb (XINHUA) -- Shanghai's Educational Television Station, the fourth television station in Shanghai, began transmission today. The three other television stations in Shanghai are: the Shanghai Television Station, the Oriental Television Station, and the Shanghai Cable Television Station. This specialized television station is sponsored by the Shanghai Municipal People's Government Education and Public Health Office. Its programming will focus on education. It will serve the reform, opening up, and social development of Shanghai with interesting, rich, and colorful educational, scientific, and artistic programs. For the time being, the Shanghai Educational Station will air a total 15 hours of programs a day. Among the programs are: academic education, nonacademic education, old-age education, forum on educational reform, teachers training, educational information, culture, science, technology, public health, sports, law and morality, children's world, and colorful forum.
