940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Samuel Heath Samuel Heath, the hardware and giftware maker, reported pre-tax profits ahead from Pounds 95,000 to Pounds 136,000 in the six months to September 30. Turnover was static at Pounds 3.59m (Pounds 3.55m) with increased exports making up for lower domestic sales. Earnings per share were 3p (2.1p). The interim dividend is held at 1.5p.
