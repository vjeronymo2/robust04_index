931123 FT 23 NOV 93 / UK Company News: F&C Eurotrust lifts asset value Foreign & Colonial Eurotrust saw its net asset value per share improve from 161.5p to 210.4p over the 12 months to September 30. The 30 per cent rise, however, lagged the FT-Actuaries Europe Index which rose 33 per cent over the same period. Available revenue amounted to Pounds 751,000 (Pounds 865,000), reducing earnings per share from 1.47p to 1.26p. The single distribution is maintained at 1.23p.
