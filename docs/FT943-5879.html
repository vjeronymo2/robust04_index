940901 FT 01 SEP 94 / International Company News: Bakrie profits rise to Rp42bn Bakrie & Brothers, an Indonesian holding company with interests in steel pipe production, telecommunica tions and plantations, said its unaudited first-half net income rose to Rp41.99bn (Dollars 1.93m) from Rp3.76bn a year earlier, writes Manuela Saragosa in Jakarta. The company's profits were boosted by a one-time gain of about Rp38bn from the sale of 30 per cent of its Bakrie Electronics unit to PTT Telecom of the Netherlands. Sales climbed to Rp273bn from Rp99.6bn in the year ago period.
