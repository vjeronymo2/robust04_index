BFN [Text] Russia will deliver the first of the seven cryogenic engines it is to supply to India in 1996. This was disclosed by the Russian first deputy foreign minister, Mr. Anatoliy Adamishin, in an interview to UNI TV during his visit to New Delhi recently. Mr. Adamishin, however, said under the terms of the recent agreement the technology for the cryogenic engines will not be transferred to India. He said India and Russia are likely to sign a comprehensive agreement on cooperation in the field of space technology during the forthcoming visit of the Indian prime minister to Russia. In reply to a question, Mr. Adamishin said Russia has been supplying spares for India's defense equipment. He said the two countries are discussing a proposal to set up joint ventures to manufacture military spares.
