930819 FT 19 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Ovoca Resources Ovoca Resources, the Dublin-based exploration group, incurred losses of IPounds 7,823 (Pounds 7,281) before and after tax over the 12 months to December 31. The outcome compared with a deficit of IPounds 519,479, of which IPounds 500,000 represented exploration expenditure written off. The group, shares of which are traded on Dublin's Exploration Securities Market and under Stock Exchange Rule 535 (2), is principally involved in processing for gold but also seeks base metals and minerals in Ireland, Brazil and Ghana. Losses per share worked through at 0.06p (4p).
