August 20, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH SURELOCK: THE SILICON SLEUTH

What should have been one of SURELOCK's best days ever fizzled after the eighth race when Tight Spot was disqualified from first to 10th, ruining a $40 exacta.

However, the computer still showed a $47 profit for the day.

The computer's bet today is a $10 exacta box, Majestic Moment-Decidedly Ragtime-Boldly Excellent, in the eighth.
