BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondents Lyudmila Aleksandrova, Nikita Yermakov, Yuriy Sizov, Vladimir Shekhovtsev] [Text] Barcelona April 13 TASS -- "Russian President Boris Yeltsin said everything about prospects for Russia's joining the Partnership for Peace programme at his press conference in Madrid yesterday," Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev told ITAR-TASS here today. "I cannot add anything to what he said," Kozyrev added. On Tuesday, Boris Yeltsin said Russia is not yet linking the situation in Bosnia to membership in NATO's Partnership for Peace scheme. "But Russia is also in no hurry to sign that programme," the Russian president said on Tuesday. Asked if he is planning a trip to the former Yugoslavia, Andrey Kozyrev replied "not in the next day or two".
