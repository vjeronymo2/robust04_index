Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- Helmut Schaefer, the minister of state at the Foreign Ministry, has expressed clear criticism of arms exports from Islamic countries to Afghanistan. He explicitly mentioned Saudi Arabia, Iran, and Pakistan. Everything has to be done to halt the flow of weapons. Schaefer expressed concern about the civil war that has flared up again in Afghanistan. The situation has led to floods of panic-stricken refugees moving to Pakistan. The minister of state estimated the number of refugees at more than 250,000. He called upon the Bundestag to take up the question of the Afghanistan conflict.
