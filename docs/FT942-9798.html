940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / UK Company News: TJ Hughes shows rise 9% to Pounds 1.6m TJ Hughes, the discount retailer based in the north-west of England, increased pre-tax profits by 9 per cent in the year to January 29 from Pounds 1.47m to Pounds 1.6m. Sales were up 8 per cent, from Pounds 40.2m in the previous 53 weeks to Pounds 43.5m. The final dividend is raised to 1.75p, making a total of 2.5p (2.3p), payable from earnings per share of 5.45p (5.2p). again final of 1.8p.
