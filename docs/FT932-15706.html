930410 FT 10 APR 93 / International Company News: Laidlaw hampered by bad weather LAIDLAW, the international waste management group, says bad weather and the recession dampened its second-quarter results. Net profit was USDollars 28.1m or 10 cents a share in the three months ended February 28, against Dollars 28.2m, or 11 cents, a year earlier. Revenues were Dollars 469m, up 1.4 a cent. There were more shares outstanding in the latest quarter. First-half profit rose to Dollars 77m, or 28 cents a share, up 12 per cent from Dollars 68.7m, or 27 cents, a year earlier. Revenues were Dollars 982m against Dollars 984m. ADT, the security and vehicle auction company, and Attwoods, the waste management group, provided Dollars 11m in the latest quarter, against Dollars 8.9m a year earlier.
