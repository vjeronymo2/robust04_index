October 11, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Hold the organ music: Friday the 13th has apparently exacted its curse on Valentina Vitale and Anthony Nunzio, the title characters of "Tony n' Tina's Wedding." The producers of the environmental theater piece that recreates an Italian-American wedding celebration have canceled this Friday's press performance. The reason: anticipated noise interference from a Rip magazine party-concert (featuring Faster Pussycat, Wolfsbane, Phantom Blue, Love Hate, Johnny Krash, Mimes) that will be going on in a room directly above at the Park Plaza Hotel where the mock wedding is staged. ALEENE MacMINN
