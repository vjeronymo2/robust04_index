920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / International Company News: Loss provisions hit Baltica BALTICA HOLDING, which controls the Baltica insurance and banking group, has downgraded its December forecast of profits of DKr850m (Dollars 133m) for 1991 to between DKr200m and DKr300m, writes Hilary Barnes in Copenhagen. This follows large loss provisions by the banking side and losses on credit and guarantee insurance business.
