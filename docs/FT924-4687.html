921201 FT 01 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Vistec improves to Pounds 1.31m VISTEC GROUP, a supplier of computer systems, software and services, sustained its progress in the half year to October 31 despite continued deterioration in the economy. The period saw sales rise 31 per cent and pre-tax profit 29 per cent to Pounds 18.7m (Pounds 14.3m) and Pounds 1.31m (Pounds 1.01m) respectively. Earnings rose to 0.73p (0.56p) and the interim dividend is lifted to 0.125p (0.1p).
