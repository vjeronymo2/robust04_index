CSO [Summary] In the first quarter of 1994 the Changchun No 1 Motor Vehicle Plant sold more than 41,000 vehicles, a 9.4 percent increase over the same period in 1993, and sales income exceeded 4.24 billion yuan, a 37.7 percent increase. Diesel trucks were the most popular selling item. In the first quarter, the plant produced more than 12,000 diesel trucks, a 63.6 percent increase over the same period in 1993. In the first quarter the plant produced 2,700 spare parts, fulfilling 45 percent of the annual plan.
