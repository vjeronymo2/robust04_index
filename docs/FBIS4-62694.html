BFN [Text] Eduard Shevardnadze, head of state and chairman of parliament, today met (Ivanciu Nikolae Zulianu) deputy chairman of the Parliamentary Assembly of the Economic Council of the Black Sea Basin Countries and chairman of the International Commission of the Romanian Parliament. The head of state assured the guest that Georgia, no less than other countries, is interested in cooperation with the Black Sea basin countries. The view was expressed that the above-mentioned community [as heard] will be a guarantor for the economic security of the council's member-states. An agreement was reached at the meeting about an exchange of official visits by the head of the Georgian state and the president of Romania.
