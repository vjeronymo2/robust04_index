Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The foreign secretary, Mr. J.N. Dixit, has returned to New Delhi from Islamabad after talks with his Pakistani counterpart Mr. Shaharyar Khan. He is likely to brief the prime minister today. At the end of the two days of foreign secretary-level talks, Mr. Dixit indicated that New Delhi would make fresh proposals regarding the Siachen Glacier and Sir Creek to normalize relations between the two countries. He said despite the fundamental differences over Kashmir, both sides agreed to find ways of resolving the problem. A joint statement issued at the conclusion of the seventh round of talks stressed the need to continue the dialogue with a view to addressing all outstanding issues.
