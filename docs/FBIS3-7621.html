Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Congolese Prime Minister Yhombi-Opango received a DPRK Foreign Ministry delegation led by Vice Minister Pak Myong-ku on 25 March. During the meeting, the prime minister said that the world's people highly respect the great leader His Excellency President Kim Il-song. He pointed out that the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula must be resolved through the method of dialogue and negotiations. He emphasized that he hopes that Korea's reunification will be realized without intervention by outside forces, independently, and through the method of the confederal system, as elucidated by the great leader His Excellency President Kim Il-song. He said the Congolese people's feelings of fraternal friendship toward the Korean people will never change. The Congolese secretary of state in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs charged with cooperation, other functionaries concerned, and the DPRK ambassador to Congo were on hand.
