January 11, 1990, Thursday, Valley Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Soccer: The Agoura High girls' coach was incorrectly identified in Sunday's edition.

Agoura's coach is Don Hagenburger, who replaced Dave Godwin this season.

Godwin, who led the Chargers to four consecutive Southern Section championships in his five years as coach, was granted a one-year leave of absence to pursue educational goals. His schedule, however, allowed him to return this season as an assistant to Hagenburger.

Godwin will return as coach next season, according to John Crow, the school's athletic director.
