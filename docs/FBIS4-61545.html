BFN [Text] Yerevan, 7 MAY (SNARK) -- Though Nagorno-Karabakh has not been recognized de jure as a party involved in conflict, however de facto this has been recorded during the Bishkek summit, told the journalists in Yerevan today the Chairman of Armenian Parliament Babken Ararktsyan. By his words, the participants of this meeting came to a general opinion that there wouldn't be any progress in peaceful resolution of problem if Karabakh is not recognized as a conflicting party. Giving an answer to the SNARK question, why did Azerbaijan refuse to sign the Bishkek protocol, Ararktsyan observed: "I think Azerbaijan has not yet developed a distinct political course, and not only with respect to Karabakh." At the same time, the Speaker thought unlikely the new escalation of military conflict in the nearest time.
