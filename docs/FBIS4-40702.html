BFN [Text] Kiev -- The press service of Ukraine's Ministry of Defense has reported that on 16 June, Army General Vitaliy Radetskyy, Ukraine's minister of defense, visited Elsworth Airbase (in South Dakota) during his official visit to the United States. He viewed the missile launch silo of a strategic ballistic missile, which had been prepared for elimination and destruction in the presence of the head of the Ukrainian military department. Vitaliy Radetskyy evaluated this step as a manifestation of confidence in and respect for Ukraine, and also as a continuation of mutually beneficial relations between the military departments of both countries. On 17 June, Vitaliy Radetskyy met employees of the RAND Corporation in Los Angeles. During the conversation he stressed that as a guarantee of its national security Ukraine has its own armed forces and also its defense industry, which should provide the military with armaments and fighting equipment.
