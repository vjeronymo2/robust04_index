Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] We heard a short while ago that the leadership of the Rally for Culture and Democracy [RCD] announced that the party will not take part in the national conference [scheduled to take place on 25 January]. However, the RCD has pointed out that the reason behind this decision was that the adopted platform on the transitional period to be discussed by the national conference contains a retraction from the one proposed by the Higher State Council.
