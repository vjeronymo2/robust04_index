President's Committee on the National Medal of Science; Meeting In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92&hyph;463, as amended), the National Science Foundation announces the following meeting: Date and Time: Thursday, November 10, 1994; 8:00 a.m.&hyph;3:00 p.m. Place: Room 320, National Science Foundation 4201 Wilson Blvd, Arlington, VA.
