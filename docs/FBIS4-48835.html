BFN [Text] The president of the Republic, Pascal Lissouba, returned to Brazzaville shortly before 1500 at the end of an African and Asian visit that lasted 17 days. This is the longest foreign visit by President Lissouba since he assumed power on 31 August 1992. President Lissouba visited South Africa, Kenya, Thailand, the PRC, and Ethiopia where he attended activities marking the 31st OAU anniversary.
