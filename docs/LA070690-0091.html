July 6, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

EASTERN EUROPE FILMS IN GAY & LESBIAN FEST

More than 100 films from 15 nations will screen during the Los Angeles International Gay & Lesbian Film and Video Festival today through July 12 at the Directors Guild of America, 7920 Sunset Blvd.

Four films about gay men and women in Eastern Europe are included:

* East Germany's "Coming Out," Friday at 7:30 and 9:30 p.m.

* "Another Way," a feature-length film from Hungary, about two women journalists during the 1956 uprising, Saturday at 7:30 p.m.

* "Sex 121 and the Gulag," about the spread of AIDS in the Soviet Union, and "Polskiseks," a documentary about Poland's Solidarity movement and the Catholic Church working together to ban abortion, Monday at 7 p.m. Both short films are from Britain.
