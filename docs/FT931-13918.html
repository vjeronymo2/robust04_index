930121 FT 21 JAN 93 / GPs fear drugs range limits UP to 80 per cent of UK general practitioners believe government plans to limit further the range of drugs they can prescribe will lead to a deterioration in care for National Health Service patients, a survey suggests. The poll of 200 GPs for the Association of the British Pharmaceutical Industry by Milpro, a division of MAI, also suggested two-thirds are opposed to the proposals. The government plans to stop paying for most drugs within 10 therapeutic categories.
