Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, March 10 (SNARK)--The Republic of Nagorno-Karabakh comes out for the immediate cease of fire and the beginning of constructive negotiations on peaceful settlement of the conflict, the effective guarantees of non-resumption of hostilities provided, told SNARK today the temporary Chairman of the Supreme Soviet of Nagorno-Karabakh Karen Baburyan. However the latest political moves of Azerbaijani leaders testify, in his words, that official Baku still relies on force methods of solving the problem, which is equally bad for the Armenian and Azerbaijani people. This, continued the Head of Karabakh Parliament, can be proved by such facts, as torpedoing of Russian and CSCE peaceful initiatives, intensive buying-in of arms, using of foreign mercenaries and military instructors. Such a policy of Baku, stressed Baburyan, does not in the least serve the cause of peace in the region. Moreover, it can, in his opinion, lead to serious destabilization of the situation in Azerbaijan itself.
