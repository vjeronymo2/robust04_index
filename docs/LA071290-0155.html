July 12, 1990, Thursday, Ventura County Edition

INTRODUCING BABIES TO WORLD OF WATER

For those interested in having their babies learn to swim, here's a summary of what's available around the county this summer:

Marjean's Swimland, Santa Paula. Marjean will teach any age -- from babies through grandparents. Swimland guarantees that your child will swim. Ten one-hour lessons. $135. 647-6374.

Pierpont Racquet Club, Ventura. Baby Bubblers class. Two weeks, six lessons, half-hour classes. After six lessons, your child will be mobile in the water. No guarantees. $36. 648-5161.

Oak Ridge Racquet Club, Simi Valley. Parent and child. Ten 25-minute lessons. $41. 522-5454.

The Ventura YMCA offers a "Skipper Class" for infants 6 months or older. It's an introduction to the water. Main objective is a smooth introduction to the water. 25-minute classes. $28. 647-2131.

The city of Ventura offers Mom, Dad & Me classes for ages 6 months to 2 years. Two weeks. $20-$25. 654-8922.

The Santa Barbara, Montecito and Thousand Oaks YMCA's are among other area YMCA's offering instruction. Some start as early as 3 months.

Pleasant Valley Recreation and Park District offers programs for all ages. Ten 50-minute classes, $20. 482-1996.

Daland Swim School, Thousand Oaks. One year and up. Eight 20-minute lessons. $72. 495-5210.

Conejo Recreation and Park District offers parent and child classes for ages 6 months to 4 years. Two weeks. $21. 495-4674.

Rancho Simi Recreation and Park District offers Parent and Me lessons. Eight 25-minute classes. $40. 584-4400.

Cal Lutheran University offers parent and child classes for children from 6 months of age. Ten half-hour classes. Two weeks. $48. 493-3395.

Easter Seal in Ventura offers infant classes in a 95 degree pool, but not until mid-summer because of the illness of infant instructor Dawna Rhodes. 647-2131.

The American Red Cross has just published a book called "Infants and Preschool Aquatic Program Parents Guide." A resource book offering tips, information and safety hints. Available from the Ventura Red Cross chapter for $1. 643-9928.
