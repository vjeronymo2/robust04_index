BFN [Text] Russia will not supply Georgian customers with natural gas in 1994. This is what knowledgeable sources in the Russian Gazprom organization told the (OIL INFORMATION AGENCY). According to the Russian gas distribution plan approved for this year, Gazprom intends to have all surplus raw material resources sent to Ukraine to be stored in underground gas storage depots to ensure uninterrupted gas supplies to western European countries. Georgia currently receives natural gas from Turkmenistan.
