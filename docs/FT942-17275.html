940406 FT 06 APR 94 / Bus employees in Pounds 27m buyout The northern half of Greater Manchester Buses has been sold to its employees in a Pounds 27m deal. The southern half of the business was bought by its management and employees last week for Pounds 25m. Nearly all 2,600 employees of GM Buses North joined in raising Pounds 2.5m. The Manchester office of Murray Johnstone, the Glasgow-based finance house, and Banc Boston Capital took Pounds 13m of equity.
