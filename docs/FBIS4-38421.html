BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Ivanova] [Text] Moscow May 27 TASS -- The Russian State Duma has proved to be adherent to the former Supreme Soviet's traditions, Chairman of the All-Russian TV and Radio Company Oleg Poptsov told ITAR-TASS here today, commenting on the Duma decision to form its own TV and radio service. In his opinion, the Duma "claims on certain information exclusiveness", which may provoke a chain reaction on the part of other power structures, including the juridical one. "Then we will have a splendid TV and the radio," said Poptsov. Lamenting there were no deputies to show wisdom and understanding, Poptsov stressed "the main feature of democracy is openness, modesty and availability of power bodies, their ability of self-estimation, and not claims on privileges".
