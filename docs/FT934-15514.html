931012 FT 12 OCT 93 / Norwegian opposition to EC membership rises NORWEGIAN opposition to EC membership has risen to a record 60 per cent, up from 58 per cent registered just days ahead of last month's general election which propelled the staunchly anti-EC Centre party to main oppostion status, writes Karen Fossli in Oslo. The poll, published yesterday in the daily Dagbladet, saw those in favour of membership slip to 28 per cent from 31 per cent last month. Opposition to membership within the ranks of the governing Labour party rose to 36 per cent (28 per cent).
