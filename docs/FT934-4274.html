931204 FT 04 DEC 93 / UK Company News: AAH expands pharmacy side AAH, the diversified distribution company, has acquired Foster & Plumpton, an operator of 29 retail pharmacy outlets in Yorkshire and Humberside, for an initial Pounds 12.5m. Consideration will be met via Pounds 7.3m in ordinary shares with the balance in loan notes. An additional sum not exceeding Pounds 500,000 is dependent on valuation of the net assets being acquired. For the 12 months to September 30 1992 F&P returned profits before tax of Pounds 212,000. Net assets at that date totalled Pounds 838,000. AAH estimated that F&P would contribute profits (before financing costs) of not less than Pounds 1.5m for the year to March 31 1995 as a result of reduced overheads and operational synergies.
