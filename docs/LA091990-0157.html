September 19, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

GRAHAM WINS MATCH IN PARIS

Debbie Graham of Fountain Valley beat West Germany's Sabine Hack, 6-3, 3-6, 6-4, today to advance to the third round of the Paris Women's Open.

Graham, 19, who reached the semifinals of the Virginia Slims tournament in San Diego, will face fourth-seeded Julie Halard of France in a third-round match on Friday. Halard eliminated Angeliti Kanellopoulou of Greece, 4-6, 6-3, 6-3.
