940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / Economic Watch: Domestic economic confidence improves French household confidence about the outlook for the economy continued to improve, the national statistics institute said. 'Continuing the trend seen since the beginning of the year, the (seasonally-adjusted) opinion of households on the general economic situation and on their personal situation improved in July.' However, its July monthly survey showed expectations about future living standards remained unchanged and that more people than in June expected unemployment to rise.
