940611 FT 11 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Reduced Pounds 549,000 loss for Bridgend Reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 549,000 were announced by Bridgend Group, the wholesale distribution and leisure concern, for the year to December 31. Losses in 1992 amounted to Pounds 3.61m. Turnover slipped to Pounds 15.6m (Pounds 15.8m) including Pounds 64,000 (Pounds 25,000) from discontinued operations. Losses per share amounted to 2.2p (13.4p); however, a final dividend of 0.1p is proposed for a 0.2p (0.4p) total.
