BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Olga Solodova] [Text] Moscow, 30 Mar -- The office of the Russian procurator general has instituted criminal proceedings in connection with the publishing of libellous material in OBSHCHAYA GAZETA with regard to a coup d' etat allegedly being planned in Russia. The ITAR-TASS correspondent was told at the information and public relations center of the procurator general's office that the case requires thorough investigation and that the individuals who will be called to account for this "sensational" report cannot be named.
