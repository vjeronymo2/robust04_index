May 3, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

CLEAN CAMPAIGN: UCI student elections officials expect all the campaign posters to be gone by Saturday afternoon, 48 hours after the end of voting. . . . Why? Because a candidate who doesn't comply could be disqualified, even after he's won.

And no one will know the winners until Monday. . . . But is it legal? No, says attorney Dan Stormer, who successfully sued the university in 1985 to reform student election regulations. "It's ludicrous. It's election by intimidation," he says. Steve Emmons and Cristina Lee
