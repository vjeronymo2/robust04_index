BFN [Announcer-read report over still of Defense Minister Golhan] [Text] National Defense Minister Mehmet Golhan has said that the delivery of 20 Super Puma helicopters Turkey bought from the French-German Eurocopter company will begin in 1995. Golhan, who is on an official visit to France, toured Eurocopter and saw the helicopters being made for Turkey. Golhan said that this company wants to set up an assembly factory in Turkey, and that this can be done through cooperation with the private sector. Golhan pointed out that a warm dialogue has been set up with his French counterpart, Leotard. Golhan stressed that Turkey has France's full support in its struggle against terrorism.
