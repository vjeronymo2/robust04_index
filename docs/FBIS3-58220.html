Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Munich (DDP/ADN) -- The CSU [Christian Social Union] does not intend to take part in any grand coalition in Bonn in which the CDU is the junior partner. According to the news magazine FOCUS this was made clear by party leader [Finance Minister] Theo Waigel at the closed CSU meeting in Wildbad Kreuth. "There will be no grand coalition in which the CDU is junior partner with the CSU [Christian Social Union]. In that event, they [the CSU] will go over to the opposition," the Munich-based magazine quoted the party chairman as saying.
