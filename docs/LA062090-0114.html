June 20, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

VALENCIA'S SWAIL AN ALL-AMERICAN IN WATER POLO

Julie Swail, a junior at Valencia High School, has been named an All-American in water polo by the National Interscholastic Swimming Coaches of America.

Swail, who started on the Tiger varsity as a field player last fall, was a second-team all-Orange League selection. She also has been a Southern Section qualifier in swimming the past three years.
