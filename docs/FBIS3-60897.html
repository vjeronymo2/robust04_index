Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Interior Minister Nahit Mentese announced that an operation was undertaken to destroy PKK [Kurdish Workers Party] leader Abdullah Ocalan. However, Mentese indicated that they still do not have definite information confirming the assassination of Apo [alias for Abdullah Ocalan]. [Begin recording] [Metin Corabatir] Is there an ongoing operation to kill Ocalan? [Mentese] Of course, there is an ongoing operation to catch him dead or alive. [Corabatir] In fact, though, there is no news just now. [Mentese] Certain indications reached us yesterday, just the way they reached you. I tried to confirm them through the intelligence units and the Foreign Ministry. I have not received any confirmation as yet. [end recording]
