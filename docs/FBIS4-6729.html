BFN [Text] On the afternoon of 21 April, Hans Blix, director general of the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA], sent a message to North Korea expressing the IAEA position on North Korea's proposal for the IAEA to witness the exchange of nuclear fuel bars at its nuclear facilities. It has been learned that Director General Blix said that the witnessing of IAEA inspectors of the exchange of nuclear fuel bars should be technically and perfectly carried out without any obstruction. He also said the inspection of the radiochemical laboratory, which was not carried out during the past inspections, should be fully resumed as well.
