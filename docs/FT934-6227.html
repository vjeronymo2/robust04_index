931125 FT 25 NOV 93 / French rail network on line for deficit The French state-owned railway network, SNCF, revealed yesterday it would suffer a deficit on its operations of about FFr7.3bn (Dollars 1.24bn) this year, writes John Ridding in Paris. The company blamed investments in the modernisation of tracks, the construction of high-speed lines and improvements in safety standards. Spending in these areas is expected to be about FFr6.42bn for the year.
