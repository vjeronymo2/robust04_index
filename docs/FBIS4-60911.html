BFN [Maksim Isayev report under the "In Brief" rubric: "Charges Brought Against a High-Ranking Internal Affairs Ministry Official?"] [Text] Your NEZAVISIMAYA GAZETA correspondent has learned from knowledgeable sources that at the end of last week a criminal lawsuit was filed against Militia General I.A. Shilov, deputy chief of the Internal Affairs Ministry's Main Criminal Investigations Administration, on charges of his alleged ties with criminals. As a measure of preventive restriction, he signed a written undertaking not to abscond. At the same time, a number of other high-ranking Internal Affairs Ministry officials have been detained in connection with the case. At the Internal Affairs Ministry Public Relations Center they have told NEZAVISIMAYA GAZETA that they do not know anything about the matter.
