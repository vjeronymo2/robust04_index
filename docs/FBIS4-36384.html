BFN [Excerpts] President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali received a cable from the participants in the 17th session of the Arab Broadcasting Union [ABU] general assembly held in Tunis on 7 and 8 June 1994, in which they expressed their deepest gratitude for his agreeing that this session be held under his patronage and care. [passage omitted] Participants have blessed the unanimous election of Tunisia for the chairmanship of the next session of the ABU and expressed confidence that the organization will secure more professional gains in response to the aspiration of Arab audiences. The 17th session ended in Tunis last evening, which was held at the invitation of the Tunisian radio and TV enterprise. Held under the patronage of President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali, the session was attended by representatives of radio and TV broadcasting corporations from Jordan, the UAE [United Arab Emirates], Tunisia, Algeria, Saudi Arabia, Sudan, Syria, Iraq, Oman, Palestine, Qatar, Kuwait, Lebanon, the Libyan Jamahiriyah, Egypt, Morocco, Mauritania and Yemen. At its opening session, the general assembly elected Mr. Abdelhafidh El Hargem, director general of the Tunisian radio and TV enterprise, as chairman of the assembly and of the executive bureau of the union. At the end of the session, Mr. Raouf El Basti from Tunisia was reelected director general of ABU for another four-year term.
