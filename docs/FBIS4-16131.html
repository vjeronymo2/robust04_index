BFN [Text] VILNIUS, Apr 13, BNS - Lithuanian Defense Minister Linas Linkevicius says he does not intend to resign yet, although conditions for work are "difficult." In an interview with the leftist TIESA newspaper, Linkevicius, who is a member of the ruling Lithuanian Democratic Labor Party [LDDP], said some of LDDP members have prejudices against national defense. "I see ill-favored criticism in this, although I earlier thought that I am criticized well-wishing," the minister said. Linkevicius recently announced his decision to leave the parliament's National Security Committee, which is dominated by LDDP members.
