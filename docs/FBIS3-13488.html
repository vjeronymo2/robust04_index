Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday morning brother President Abu 'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] visited the martyrs' cemetery in Tunis accompanied by members of the Palestinian leadership and revolutionary cadres. After the brother president and those attending had recited Surah al-Fatihah [opening verses of the Koran] for the souls of the innocent martyrs, brother President Abu 'Ammar gave an address in which he stressed that the waterfall of Palestinian blood would not halt for one moment until the Palestinian flag and the flag of the Arab nation were raised over the walls, minarets, and churches of Jerusalem. Brother President Abu 'Ammar made a pledge to all the martyrs that the revolution, on whose long and difficult road they had fallen, would continue, however great and long the sacrifices and however much the difficulties increased, through the determination and will of our people until victory and as far as Jerusalem.
