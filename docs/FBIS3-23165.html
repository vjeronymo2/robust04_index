Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Mr. Awad Khalifah, the commissioner general of relief and rehabilitation, has appealed to the United Nations and the international community to condemn the rebel movement and adopt a resolute stance towards its aggressive actions. In the course of an interview for our reporter, His Excellency confirmed that the rebel movement had detained five UN employees of Operation Lifeline in the Upper Nile area, saying that the rebel movement habitually violated its covenants and agreements. He said detaining UN employees while they were doing their humanitarian work was aggressive and barbarous. His Excellency condemned the movement for releasing the British UN employee and transporting him to Nairobi through the United Nations without saying what had happened to the remaining employees who are Sudanese.
