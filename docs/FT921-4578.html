920309 FT 09 MAR 92 / International Company News: Pacific Assets Net asset value of the Pacific Assets Trust at January 31 1992 was 258.31p basic per share against 186.83p, or 232.76p diluted up from 172.71p. Net profit for the year was Pounds 329,000 (Pounds 478,000) for earnings per share of 1.7p (2.55p). There is a single final dividend of 1p, against 1.875p.
