Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 1 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Il-song, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and president of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, exchanged New Year's cards with foreign state and party leaders. He exchanged New Year's cards with Glavkos Kliridhis, president of Cyprus; President Shankar Dayal Sharma and Prime Minister P.V. Narasimha Rao of India; Ali Kafi, chairman of the High State Committee of Algeria; Sanni Abacha, head of state and commander-in-chief of the Armed Forces of Nigeria; Nicephore Soglo, president of Benin; Ronald Venetiaan, president of Suriname; B. Yeltsin, president of Russian Federation; Otto Stich, president of Switzerland; Thomas Klestil, federal president of Austria; Nir Mal Sen, general secretary of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers and Peasants Socialist Party of Bangladesh; K.P. Silva, general secretary of the C.C., the Sri Lanka Communist Party; Iqbal Ahmed Khan, secretary-general of the Pakistan Muslim League; Khaled Bagdash, general secretary of the C.C., the Communist Party of Syria; Mamadou El Bechir Gologo, secretary general of the National Direction Committee of the Sudanese Union-African Democratic Rally in Mali; Jorge Ibanez Castro, first secretary of the C.C., the Bolivian Communist Party; Hardial Bains, national leader and first secretary of the Communist Party of Canada (Marxism-Leninism); Rene Mauge Mosquera, general secretary of the C.C., the Communist Party of Ecuador; Vassalo Anthony, general secretary of the C.C., the Communist Party of Malta; Alfred Sant, leader of the Malta Labour Party; Manuel Vargas Loureiro, chairman of the Democratic Renovation Party of Portugal; and Zbigniew Wiktor, chairman of the National Executive Committee of the Polish League of Communists "Proletariat". He also received New Year's cards from Idris Deby, president and head of state of Chad; Mohammad Zahir Khan, chairman of the Bangladesh Nationalist Republican Party; and Peter Symon, general secretary of the C.C., the Socialist Party of Australia. And he exchanged New Year greetings with Hungarian President Arpad Goncz.
