Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A transport aircraft with a group of Odessa military district troops took off from Nikolayev for Sarajevo. This is a second planeload of servicemen who will replace their comrades in the UN peace-keeping force in Bosnia-Herzegovina. A spokesman for the Odessa military district told INTERFAX- Ukraine that a total of four such flights would be flown. Ukrainian women medics are flying for the first time to Sarajevo.
