Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Brigadier General Ilan Schiff, the military judge advocate, says Israel is interested in strengthening the PLO's position in its struggle with Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement]. For this reason Israel must sometimes implement moves which are aimed at having long-term effects. Our correspondent Shim'on Vilna'i says that Brig. Gen. Schiff was referring to the government's decision to release security prisoners. The military judge advocate emphasized that Israel's present policy is not to release prisoners with blood on their hands. Brig. Gen. Schiff spoke at a Criminology Association conference at Bar Ilan University.
