Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Relay from San Antonio de Los Banos Airbase by Reporter Jose Luis Martinez Zamora] [Text] The new year of training for the Western Army began here in the presence of Army General Raul Castro, Minister of the FAR [Revolutionary Armed Forces]. The ceremony took place at the Playa Giron Air Brigade located in San Antonio de los Banos. The Western Army's main objectives during the current training period will be: maintaining a high level of training and readiness; increasing the troops' self-sufficiency, supported by an intensive nutrition program; continuing the weapons and techniques preparedness program; and continuing to refurbish the theater of military operations. During the ceremony, the defenders of the western territory ratified their decision to fight resolutely to achieve the title of best FAR command in 1995.
