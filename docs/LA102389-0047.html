October 23, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Coke to Unveil Product: Coca-Cola Co. announced that it will introduce a caffeine-free Coca-Cola Classic in test markets starting today. The company said the soft drink will be introduced in Charlotte, N.C., and in markets yet to be named the following week. The new product will carry a gold label, while regular Coke Classic has a red label. The company said a date for national distribution will depend on the test market results.
