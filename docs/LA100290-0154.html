October 2, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: GARDEN GROVE;

CANDIDATES FORUM TO BE HELD TONIGHT

Residents are invited to attend a forum for mayoral and City Council candidates tonight at the Garden Grove Community Center, 11300 Stanford Ave.

The two mayoral and seven council candidates will answer questions that have been submitted by residents and the candidates themselves. A reception will follow the question-and-answer session, which will be moderated by Chamber of Commerce member Charlie Wilson.

Sponsored by the Garden Grove Chamber of Commerce, the forum will be broadcast live in Garden Grove on Paragon Cable Channel 3 and will be repeated several times before the election.

The forum is scheduled from 7 to 9 p.m.
