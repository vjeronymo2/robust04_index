Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The State Duma's plenary session on Friday was focused on a discussion of the Russian president's message to the Federal Assembly "On the Strengthening of the Russian State." However, no final document was adopted. State Duma's First Deputy Speaker, Mikhail Mityukov, who chaired the session, proposed to vote for continuation of the session's work after its time had expired (at 18:00). However, the majority of deputies rejected the proposal. The final document may be adopted at the State Duma's next plenary session on March 16 Wednesday.
