BFN [Text] The prime minister, Paul Keating, has again said the government sees no need for an increase in official interest rate arguing that such a rise would only generate new wage claims. But as speculation continues over a possible rise Federal Opposition Leader Alexander Downer has attacked the prime minister's record on financial management. [Begin Downer recording] I would make the point that it was Mr. Keating as the treasurer who deregulated financial markets and wanted Australia to operate in this deregulated environment and there's no point in him turning around, now having deregulated financial markets and attacking those financial markets for not agreeing with him.
