BFN [Text] More than half of the Russian citizens are exposed to harmful substances, whose concentration exceeds the admissible level by 5 to 10 times. The bacterial and viral contamination of the Russian rivers, including the Volga, the Don and the Ob, is tens and hundreds of times higher than the admissible level, Vice Rector of the newly-established Institute of Ecological Rehabilitation Studies Viktor Abdokhin told Interfax. He said that over the past 10 years, the incidence of occupational diseases had more than doubled and that 55 out of every 10,000 workers became disabled every year.
