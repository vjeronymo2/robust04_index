940321 FT 21 MAR 94 / UK Company News: 40% pay rise for Fife chief Mr Gavin Hepburn, chairman of Fife Indmar, the Scottish engineering company, received a 40 per cent pay increase last year to Pounds 129,457. The figure, published in the annual report, included a Pounds 33,000 performance related bonus. Pre-tax profits last year of Pounds 903,000 compared with previous losses of Pounds 1.66m.
