"Fright Night" (1985), directed by Tom Holland. 105 minutes. Rated R. Scary, funny and intentionally campy, "Fright Night" revolves around a teen-ager who thinks his new next door neighbor is a vampire.

Chris Sarandon makes a wise-cracking, smug resident Dracula, and Roddy McDowall plays "The Great Vampire Killer" with verve. A perfect mix of comedy and chills. MIKE BELL
