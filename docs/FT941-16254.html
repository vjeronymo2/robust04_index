940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Pounds 3m wax buy for Burmah Castrol Burmah Castrol has acquired Kerax Holdings, a blender and marketer of speciality waxes, for Pounds 3m. Kerax will join Dussek Campbell, Burmah's existing wax activity. The move is part of the company's plans to expand in the market.
