940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Stat-Plus static Stat-Plus Group, the legal stationery, printing and publishing group, turned in virtually unchanged pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.81m for the first half of 1994, against Pounds 1.82m last time. Sales rose by 3 per cent to Pounds 6.2m. Earnings per share were 5.6p (5.7p) and the interim dividend is raised to 4.32p (4p).
