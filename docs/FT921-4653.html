920307 FT 07 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Adwest raises Dollars 7m from stake sale Adwest Group, which has interests in automotive and electrical engineering, is selling its 11.66 per cent stake in Bendix Espana, the automotive component maker, to Allied Signal, the majority shareholder, for Dollars 7m (Pounds 3.9m) in cash. Adwest's share of Bendix's assets at disposal were estimated at Pounds 4.22m and the share of its profits for the year to June 30 was Pounds 476,000.
