Foreign-Trade Zones Board [Docket 72&hyph;91] Foreign-Trade Zone 76_Bridgeport, Connecticut Withdrawal of Application for Subzone Status for NorMag Steel Electric Transformer Parts Plant Notice is hereby given of the withdrawal of the application submitted by the City of Bridgeport, grantee of FTZ 76, requesting special-purpose subzone status for the electric transformer parts manufacturing facility of NorMag, Inc., located in Bridgeport, Connecticut. The application was filed on November 5, 1991 (56 FR 58354, 11/19/91). The withdrawal was requested because of changed circumstances, and the case has been closed without prejudice.
