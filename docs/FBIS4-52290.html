BFN [Text] Singapore welcomes the signing of the agreement between Israel and the Palestine Liberation Organization, which paves the way for Palestinian self-rule. A Foreign Ministry spokesman said the agreement underscored the commitment [words indistinct] toward a full implementation of the historic declaration of principles. It is a testimony to the vision and courage of the Israeli and PLO leadership. The spokesman said Singapore supports all efforts to secure a just and comprehensive peace in the Middle East. It looks forward to further progress in negotiations between Israel and the PLO and also with Israel's Arab neighbors.
