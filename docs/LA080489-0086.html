August 4, 1989, Friday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Freddie Spencer, former world motorcycle champion in the 250cc and 500cc classes, was dismissed by Marlboro Yamaha just three days before the British Grand Prix in Donington, England.

The 27-year-old American, who in 1985 became the first rider to win the 250 and 500 titles in the same season, was also the youngest winner of the 500cc championship.
