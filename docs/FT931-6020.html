930303 FT 03 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Grahams Rintoul launches new trust The High Income Trust, an investment trust which will invest in convertible securities, is being launched by Grahams Rintoul, the London-based fund management group. The trust will aim for an 8.5 per cent yield, payable quarterly, and will have a six year life. Two classes of shares will be offered, ordinary income and zeros. The trust's board will include Prof Tim Congdon, one of the 'seven wise men' who advise the government on economic issues. Grahams Rintoul also manages an eponymous investment trust and the North American Gas trust.
