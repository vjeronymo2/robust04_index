BFN ["Outstanding Military Strategist" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, April 23 (KCNA) -- Dear Comrade Kim Chong-il is a great military strategist and iron-willed brilliant commander possessed of rare military wisdom, matchless grit and distinguished commanding art. He has published many works including "Let Us Strengthen the People's Army into Matchless Ranks" to give a comprehensive answers to all the theoretical and practical questions arising in building revolutionary Armed Forces, and has strengthened and developed the Korean People's Army [KPA] into invincible Armed Forces with energetic guidance. It is the greatest feat performed by Comrade Kim Chong-il in the great work of Army building that he has trained the KPA into loyal and filial Armed Forces defending the party and the leader, the country and people with their lives. He has attached particular importance to the singlehearted unity of the whole Army based on the idea of the leader in increasing its combat capabilities. He put forward a new policy of modelling the whole Army on the chuche idea to this end. And he trained the whole Army into ranks with steel-like discipline that act as one on the orders and instructions of the party, ranks singleheartedly united in ideology and purpose and in morality and sense of obligation, with his tireless energetic guidance. The integral whole of complete unity in ideology and purpose, the community of destiny, that shares the destiny and bitters and sweets in the struggle for the country and people -- this is the looks of the KPA that has grown up under the loving care of the great brilliant commander. Comrade Kim Chong-il has wisely led the work of building a powerful self-reliant national defence industry to strengthen and develop the KPA into an invincible Army. He, with a deep insight into the features of modern warfare, indicated the direction and ways of building the self-reliant national defence industry and solved all problems with never-ceasing trips of on-the-spot guidance. Thus, the KPA has been strengthened into invincible ranks equipped with modern offensive and defensive means. Another great feat performed by Comrade Kim Chong-il in the great work of Army building is that he has made sure that the beautiful traits of Army-civilian unity is fully displayed throughout the country so that the Army and the people may help each other, and that the arming of the entire people and the fortification of the whole country have been completed. In Korea, under his outstanding guidance and politics of love, the Army and people are united by blood ties like one family and the whole country has turned into an impregnable fortress which no formidable enemy can provoke. The iron will, grit and distinguished commanding art of Comrade Kim Chong-il instill deep conviction of victory in the revolution and fighting spirit into the Korean people. In early March last year a touch-and-go crisis was created on the Korean peninsula owing to the adventurous aggressive war rackets of the U.S. imperialists and the South Korean puppet clique. Progressive mankind of the world was deeply concerned over it. At that time, Comrade Kim Chong-il proclaimed a semi-war state to the whole country, entire people and whole Army and saw to it that a decisive statement on the withdrawal of the DPRK from NPT [Nuclear Nonproliferation Treaty] was issued in the wake of it. And he turned the whole party, the entire people and the whole Army into a powerful combat force and the Korean people rose up like a mountain in hearty response to the call of the illustrious leader. The enemies trembled with fear. This was how peace on the Korean peninsula and the rest of Asia was defended. "We will win because General Kim Chong-il, an iron-willed brilliant commander, is leading us"--this is the unshakable will and faith of the Korean people and the People's Army officers and soldiers.
