930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Louis Newmark cuts loss to Pounds 1.58m With the loss in the second half down to Pounds 584,000, against Pounds 993,000 in the first Louis Newmark's pre-tax losses for the year to April 3 were Pounds 1.58m (Pounds 1.93m). Turnover for the engineering and specialist equipment group was Pounds 23.9m (Pounds 24.8m) including Pounds 1.15m (same) from discontinued activities.
