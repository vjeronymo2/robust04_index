BFN [Summary] At its 7 April session, the Georgian Supreme Council confirmed the following nominees to the Georgian Cabinet of Ministers presented by Eduard Shevardnadze, the Georgian head of state: Zurab Kervalishvili -- a deputy prime minister premier; Pridon Injia -- the minister of communications; Vladimer Kereselidze -- the minister of industry; Valerian Vashakidze -- the minister of refugee and resettlement issues; Giorgi Kvesitadze -- the minister of agriculture and food production; Tedo Ninidze -- the minister of justice; Avtandil Silagadze -- the minister of state property management; Vazha Gujabidze -- the minister of labor and social protection.
