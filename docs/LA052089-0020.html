May 20, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

DARTS AND LAURELS TO THOSE INVOLVED WITH 'ROE VS. WADE'

Kudos to NBC President Robert C. Wright for publicly asking advertisers to support "Roe vs. Wade" and to NBC for saying it would broadcast the movie even if no commercials could be sold.

As a citizen in a free society, I have a right to see a re-enactment of this piece of history, whether or not I agree with the Supreme Court's ruling in the 1973 Roe vs. Wade case. NBC was not trying to rewrite history, only to retell it. It's called freedom of speech.

NANCY MATTHEWS

Woodland Hills
