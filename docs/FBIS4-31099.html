BFN [Text] From Monday the publication PESTI HIRLAP [center-right daily] will be discontinued. The board of trustees of the 150 Years Foundation, the publisher, decided to suspend publication of the paper due to the publisher's difficult financial situation. The foundation's board of trustees, however, is ready to hold talks with its creditors on a debt settlement. It is also ready to hold talks with new investors on a possible restart or sale of PESTI HIRLAP.
