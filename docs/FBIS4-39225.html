BFN [Text] Belarusian rayon and oblast newspapers whose publication is funded by the state budget have been issued the following directive from the Cabinet of Ministers: "In connection with the holding of presidential elections and their objective coverage by information media, please be advised to safeguard the publication of BELINFORM reports in the local press." The directive was signed by Deputy Prime Minister Demchuk. BELINFORM is an official state-budget information agency transformed from the former BELTA (Belarusian branch of TASS) which was closely controlled by the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Belarus, in which Demchuk was an instructor.
