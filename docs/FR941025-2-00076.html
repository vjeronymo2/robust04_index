Proposal: Periodical Estimate for Partial Payment and Related Schedules. Office: Public and Indian Housing. Description of the need for the information and its proposed use: The Periodical Estimate for Partial Payment and related schedules are submitted monthly to the public housing agency (PHA) by a general contractor. The forms are used to establish the amount due on the project from a PHA for work completed during the current month. Form Number: HUD&hyph;51001, 51002, 51003, and 51004. Respondents: State or Local Governments and Non-Profit Institutions. Frequency of submission: Monthly and Recordkeeping. Reporting burden:
