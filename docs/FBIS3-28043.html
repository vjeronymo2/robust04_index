Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 3 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] sent a message of greetings to Robert Hue January 30 upon his election as national secretary of the National Committee of the French Communist Party. The message expressed the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two parties would further develop on good terms in future, and wished him success in his responsible work for the implementation of the decision of the party congress.
