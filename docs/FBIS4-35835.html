BFN [Text] A high-ranking delegation from the Islamic Republic of Iran, led by Mr. 'Ali Borujerdi, deputy minister for Asian-Pacific affairs of the Foreign Ministry of Iran, arrived in Kabul today on an official and friendly visit. The delegation was welcomed at Bagram Airport by esteemed Dr. Najibollah Lafra'i, minister of state for foreign affairs; Mr. Gholamreza Haddadi, ambassador and extraordinary representative of the Islamic Republic of Iran in Kabul; and a number of officials from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
