931022 FT 22 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Revenue up at River & Merc Geared River & Mercantile Geared Capital and Income Trust reported net assets per capital share of 36.05p at September 30, compared with 32.15p at March 31 and 23.94p a year earlier. Net revenue for the six months to the end of September was Pounds 549,000 (Pounds 479,000) for earnings per income share of 3.92p (3.42p). A maintained second interim dividend of 1.4p makes a total for the year to date of 2.8p.
