Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Brussels, March 8 (AFP) -- A NATO transport plane made an emergency landing in Croatia Tuesday [8 March] with an engine on fire, a North Atlantic Treaty Organisation spokesman said here. An American, a Frenchman and a Briton aboard the twin-engined CASA 212 were injured, he said, but added that the crew had not reported being fired upon. However the Spanish defence ministry said the aircraft had been hit in the tail and the right engine. Earlier reports from the United Nations Protection Force in Zagreb said that an unidentified Cessna had been fired upon over Croatia and forced to land.
