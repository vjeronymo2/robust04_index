February 14, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

MELANIE GRIFFITH EXPECTING CHILD

"Working Girl" star Melanie Griffith and her ex-husband, "Miami Vice" star Don Johnson, are expecting a child later this year, Johnson's publicist said today.

Johnson, 39, and Griffith, 31, who were married previously and divorced, announced Dec. 26 that they plan to remarry. No date has been announced.
