BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 29 (KCNA) -- Sh. Gungardorj, Mongolian ambassador to Korea, and his embassy officials visited the Ssuksom Revolutionary Historical Site. Here the great leader President Kim Il-song had a historic consultative meeting with South Korean politicians who had participated in the joint conference of representatives of political parties and public organisations in North and South Korea (April 1948) after the liberation of the country and showed warm loving care for them. After going round the United Front tower and historical relics, the ambassador said he was convinced that the Korean people's cause of reunification would certainly be accomplished before long.
