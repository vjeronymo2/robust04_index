Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] After two days of private meetings, Manuel Hermoso Rojas, president of the government of the autonomous region of the Canary Islands, and his delegation will reembark on 22 February on an agenda of official activities in Havana. Hermoso Rojas arrived in Havana on 16 February at the invitation of the Cuban Government. During their visit, the Canary Islands delegation has followed a busy schedule that included a visit to Jose Marti's home, the inauguration of the new headquarters of the Leonor Perez Cabrera Canarian Association, and the signing of a cooperation accord with the People's Government of Havana under which the Canarian Government pledges to help in the renovation of Leonor Perez Street and El Malecon. Before concluding its visit on 23 February, the delegation is scheduled to visit a polyclinic and a family doctor's office.
