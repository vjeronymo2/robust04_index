Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Lebanese Hizballah, stressing its policy of self-restraint in the face of the Zionist regime's attacks against southern Lebanon, declared: It will respond to those attacks at an appropriate time. Announcing that, Hizballah officials in Beirut said today: The objective of the Zionist regime by those attacks is to drag the fighters of the Hizballah movement into an endless war in order to overshadow the political issues at the coming Syrian and American summit in Geneva. He added: Hizballah, with full awareness of the Zionist regime's ploy, has adopted a policy of self-restraint, but it will retaliate for the aggressions of the occupier Zionists at an appropriate time a place.
