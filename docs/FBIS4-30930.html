BFN [Unattributed report: "Parallel Census?"] [Text] The Democratic Party of Turks in Macedonia [Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia] expressed bitterness and public protest against the structure of the State Census Committee, appointed by the government. The Turk member of the Committee has been appointed without consulting the party, which is for them unacceptable. The party is confirmed the census will be conducted in a manner same as all previous censuses, i.e., it will be partial and doubtful. If this party is neglected in the future, it will announce a parallel census for Turks in Macedonia to be conducted by Turks, the party threatened in its public announcement.
