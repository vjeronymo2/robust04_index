December 30, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

EVOLUTION, CREATIONISM AND PUBLIC EDUCATION

In your editorial "Creationists Should Give It a Rest," " 'creation science,' or the biblical interpretation of human origin . . . " (emphasis mine). As a professor of composition and of The Bible as Literature, may I suggest you change the article the to a or, even more precisely, use the possessive their? As you are certainly aware, other believers in the Bible do not agree with so-called "creation science." By casually using the definitive article the, you imply that there is only one biblical interpretation and thereby strengthen such an absurd position.

RICHARD J. FOLLETT

Associate Professor of English

Pierce College
