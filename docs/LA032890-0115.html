March 28, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BATTLING THE MEDFLY

Los Angeles County will conduct aerial spraying of malathion tonight from 9 p.m. to 3 a.m.,weather permitting.

Area: 48 square miles encompassing Bell, Cudahy, Huntington Park, Lynwood and parts of Athens, Bell Gardens, Commerce, Compton, East Los Angeles, South Gate, Vernon, Walnut Park and Watts.

Precautions: Stay indoors; keep animals indoors; wash animal dishes and toys left outside; cover cars; keep doors and windows closed.

Information: Toll-free numbers for the Agricultural Commissioner: (800) 356-2894; (800)225-1346
