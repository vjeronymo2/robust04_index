BFN [Unattributed report: "We Turned Down 'Arafat Offer to Participate with Four Seats in Authority"] [Excerpt] Amman, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- A senior Hamas official has stressed that his movement turned down an offer from Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat to participate in the self-rule authority. Ibrahim Ghawshah, official spokesman for Hamas, has said: "I would like to point out that Yasir 'Arafat sent (Fatah Central Committee member) 'Abbas Zaki to meet with Hamas officials. And that Zaki conveyed to Muhammad Nazzal (Hamas representative in Amman) 'Arafat's offer to grant Hamas four seats only." But according to Ghawshah, the offer was completely rejected, "because we refuse to participate in the authority, proceeding from our rejection of the self-rule agreements, as they perpetuate the occupation of Arab territory in Palestine."
