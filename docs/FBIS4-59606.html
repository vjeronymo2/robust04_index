BFN [Text] Sanaa, 13 May (INA) -- The Yemeni Defense Ministry has dismissed the reported downing of three warplanes of its Air Force close to Bab el Mandeb at the Red Sea and the sinking of a warship off the port city of Aden. Citing a military source at the Defense Ministry and the Staff Command today, the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', denied there were any boats in the vicinity, labelling the report mere fabrication. The dismissed Yemeni Navy chief, Colonel 'Ali Qasim Talib, yesterday, Thursday, announced the separatist forces Wednesday shot down three warplanes close to Bab el Mandeb and sank a warship. The Defense Ministry said the regular forces are continuing their advance in the Kharaz region, 120 km west of Aden city, having routed the 25th Mechanized Brigade at Kharaz Camp and seized all its weapons and equipment.
