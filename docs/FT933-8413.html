930816 FT 16 AUG 93 / Construction Contracts: Upgrading RAF garrison in Yorkshire TRAFALGAR HOUSE CONSTRUCTION MANAGEMENT has secured two new project management contracts worth Pounds 11m. The largest is a Pounds 8.7m project to oversee the redevelopment of the RAF's Catterick Garrison in North Yorkshire as part of the Ministry of Defence's Options For Change programme. When the two-year contract is completed the base will provide a home for two army regiments currently based in Germany. The second contract is for a Pounds 2.2m cookery school at HMS Raleigh, the new entry training establishment for the Royal Navy at Torpoint, Cornwall.
