[I.D. 032894F] Marine Mammals AGENCY: National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS), National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA), Commerce. ACTION: Issuance of scientific research permit no. 897 (P771#69).

SUMMARY: Notice is hereby given that Dr. Howard Braham, NMFS, Alaska Fisheries Science Center, National Marine Mammal Laboratory, 7600 Sand Point Way, NE., Seattle, WA 98115, has been issued a permit to take beluga whales ( Delphinapterus leucas ) for purposes of scientific research.
ADDRESSES: The permit and related documents are available for review upon written request or by appointment, in the following offices: Permits Division, Office of Protected Resources, NMFS, 1315 East-West Highway, room 13130, Silver Spring, MD 20910 (301/713&hyph;2289); Director, Alaska Region, NMFS, P.O. Box 21668, Juneau, AK 99802&hyph;1668 (907/586&hyph;7221); and Director, Northwest Region, NMFS, NOAA, 7600 Sand Point Way, NE., BIN C15700, Seattle, WA 98115 (206/526&hyph;6150).
SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: On February 23, 1994, notice was published in the Federal Register (59 FR 8602) that a request for a scientific research permit to take beluga whales ( Delphinapterus leucas ) had been submitted by the above-named organization. The requested permit has been issued under the authority of the Marine Mammal Protection Act of 1972, as amended (16 U.S.C. 1361 et seq .), and the Regulations Governing the Taking and Importing of Marine Mammals (50 CFR part 216).
