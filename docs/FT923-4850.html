920904 FT 04 SEP 92 / Report on GCSE is published A SCHOOLS Inspectorate report on GCSE standards issued today confirms critical comments made by Mr John Patten, the education secretary, in advance of its publication. The report says the inspectorate has 'limited confidence that standards are being maintained'. This week Mr Patten said he was 'urgently considering' action to ensure GCSE standards were maintained in view of advice from the inspectorate. A record number of pupils achieved the top three GCSE grades this year. GCSE Examinations: Quality and Standards Summer 1992. HMSO, Pounds 3.60.
