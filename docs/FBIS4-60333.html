BFN [Text] The president, Mr. Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari, left Lahore this morning on a two-weeks visit to the United States. The president was accompanied by the Punjab chief minister, Mr. Manzoor Ahmad Watto, and other members of the entourage. He was seen off at the Lahore Airport by the Punjab governor, Chaudhary Mohammad Altaf Hussain. The senior Punjab minister, Maqdoom Altaf Ahmad, and provincial ministers were also present. The president will have stopovers in London and Paris on his way to the United States. During his stay in the United States, the president, Mr. Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari, is likely to meet a number of U.S. officials, as well as businessmen, to project and explain Pakistan's economic and foreign policies.
