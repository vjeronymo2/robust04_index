941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / Commodities and Agriculture: Coffee plunges to lowest levels Coffee futures prices in London plunged by over Dollars 200 a tonne to their lowest levels for four months as traders sold out of long positions in the absence of buying interest from roasters. London's January robusta contract fell Dollars 208 to Dollars 3,075 a tonne. In New York, March arabica futures were down 9.35 cents at 163 cents a pound in late trading.
