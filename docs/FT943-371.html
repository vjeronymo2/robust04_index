940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Brightstone Props Brightstone Properties, the commercial property investment and management company, yesterday announced its first results since it gained a listing on March 24. In the 14 weeks to June 30 net property income came to Pounds 327,793. Net interest took Pounds 117,100, after which pre-tax profit amounted to Pounds 106,264. An interim dividend of 1p has been declared from earnings of 1.07p.
