921104 FT 04 NOV 92 / International Company News: General Mills to end Nabisco deal GENERAL Mills, the Minnesota-based foods company, said it and Nabisco, a unit of RJR Nabisco Holdings, had mutually agreed to terminate a contract signed on September 1 for General Mills to buy Nabisco's US and Canadian ready-to-eat cold cereal business, Reuter reports from Minneapolis. The companies said the prospect of a lengthy and prolonged review by federal and state regulatory authorities, which now appeared likely, would preclude completion of the transaction within a reasonable period of time. General Mills agreed to purchase the ready-to-eat cereals business of RJR Nabisco for Dollars 450m cash.
