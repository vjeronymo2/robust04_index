October 11, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MISSED STATIONS ON DOUGHNUT QUEST

* I've just finished reading your article on doughnuts and really must recommend another place that is far superior to any of the ones listed. This is the Star Deli & Donut Show, 1456 S. Robertson Blvd., Los Angeles, ironically only a short distance from one you listed. Talk about apple fritters! Out of this world!

ADELINE SCHWAY, Los Angeles
