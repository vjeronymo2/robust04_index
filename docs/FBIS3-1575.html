Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By Cui Yanming (1508 1693 2494): "Mao Zedong Thought and Deng Xiaoping Theory"] [Text] Studying Volume 3 of the Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping and using Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics to arm the whole party is a fundamental task put forth by the 14th CPC National Congress for strengthening party building during the new period. For this reason, it is necessary for us to theoretically clarify relations between Mao Zedong Thought and Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics to increase our understanding, dispel misgivings, enhance our consciousness, and sharpening our sense of urgency for strengthening our study, so that we can grasp in a still better way the origin and essence of the theory. 1. Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics is the latest product of the integration of Marxism and the practical reality in China. It is an inheritance of Mao Zedong Thought under the new historical conditions, and Marxism in contemporary China. Our party is a vanguard of the working class, which takes Marxism-Leninism-Mao Zedong Thought as its guiding thought. In the past, now, and in the future, we unswervingly regard Marxism- Leninism-Mao Zedong Thought as the guiding thought of the whole party. However, in the course of historical development, and in different historical stages, Marxism -- as a unified ideological system -- has different theoretical manifestations. The integration of Marxism with the reality in Russia gave rise to Leninism. The integration of Marxism with the reality in China gave rise to Mao Zedong Thought. The continuation of the development of Mao Zedong Thought has inevitably given rise to a new theoretical summation. This is Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics. This does not mean that the latter negates the former, or that they are completely the same. This can only mean that the latter inherits and develops the former, so that Marxism, as a comprehensive ideological system, can constantly enrich and improve itself, and is full of vigor and vitality with the development of practice. Proletarian revolutionary teachers Marx, Engels, Lenin, and Mao Zedong successfully expounded and solved the problem of how the proletariat must seize state power. Due to restrictions of historical conditions, the problem of building socialism, and, in particular, the problem of how to consolidate and develop socialism in countries which are economically and culturally backward, has not yet been solved well. Chinese communists of the new generation, represented by Comrade Deng Xiaoping, have shouldered this important historical task. They respect practice, the masses, and their pioneering spirit. They have scientifically summed up the experience and creativity of the masses. They have inherited the experiences of their predecessors, broken with outmoded conventions, and led us to successfully carry out reform, opening up, and modernization. In the process of making great achievements, they have gradually formed and established their socialist theories with Chinese characteristics. For the first time they have systematically and initially answered a series of basic questions, including the road of socialist development, stages of development, fundamental tasks, motives for development, leading force, the force which must be relied upon, external conditions for building socialism, political guarantees, strategic steps, unification of the motherland, and so on. They have pointed out the way to carry out socialist construction, consolidate and develop the basic principle of developing socialism, and the way forward. They have inherited and developed Marxism-Leninism-Mao Zedong Thought. 2. It is imperative to correctly comprehend how Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism inherits and develops Mao Zedong Thought, and corrects Comrade Mao Zedong's mistakes committed during his remaining years to enhance our consciousness in implementing the party's basic line. Comrade Deng Xiaoping said: "Since the Third Plenary Session, we have restored those correct things of Comrade Mao Zedong. In other words, we have accurately and comprehensively applied Mao Zedong Thought. This still remains our basic point. Now we are doing things which Comrade Mao Zedong raised, but had not completed. We should advocate matters which he wrongly opposed, and do things which he failed to accomplish. For a fairly long period of time in the future, we must still do these things well. Of course, we must develop, and continue to develop them." Comrade Deng Xiaoping's remarks have actually revealed the three relations between the inheritance and development of Mao Zedong Thought by the theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics, and the correction of the mistakes committed by Mao Zedong during his remaining years. Let us first talk about inheritance. Inheritance means receiving a valuable legacy from predecessors. Inheriting and receiving can be traced to the same origin. For example, seeking truth from facts is our party's ideological line. Both Comrade Mao Zedong and Comrade Deng Xiaoping actively advocate this line. This is a main line linking Mao Zedong Thought and Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics. Comrade Deng Xiaoping said: "I believe in seeking truth from facts advocated by Chairman Mao. In the past, we relied on it to fight the war. Now we must also rely on it to carry out construction and reform." He added: "Seeking truth from facts is the essence of Marxism. We must advocate this, but not book worship." Let's look at another example. The mass line is our party's basic working line. Comrade Deng Xiaoping leads the whole party to inherit and persist in pursuing this line which Comrade Mao Zedong advocated and followed. In the meantime, he arouses the enthusiasm and creativity of the broad masses of people through reform and opening up. This has enabled our country to considerably develop productive forces, and greatly improved the living standard of the people. Attaining the target of common prosperity is no longer an invisible dream. It is precisely because Comrade Deng Xiaoping inherited from Comrade Mao Zedong the fine tradition and work style of the mass line -- and that he is good at applying a good method of summing up the experiences of the masses and refining, processing, and developing them into theories -- that the theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics is close to life and is welcomed and accepted by the masses, just as Mao Zedong Thought was. There is one more example. Independence and initiative is our party's basic principle for developing itself and handling external relations. It is an important component of Mao Zedong Thought. It is also a spiritual fulcrum and theoretical prerequisite for the theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics created by Comrade Deng Xiaoping, and has enabled China to take a road of development different from those taken by other socialist countries. Comrade Deng Xiaoping has repeatedly told the whole party: "Combining the universal truth of Marxism with the practical reality in China, taking our own road, and building socialism with Chinese characteristics is a basic conclusion drawn from our long-term historical experiences." In addition, Mao Zedong Thought also includes rich contents of upholding the socialist road, the people's democratic dictatorship, the communist party leadership, and Marxism-Leninism. With regard to the issue of the four upholds, Comrade Mao Zedong's firm conviction and systematic guidance educated the Chinese communists of the entire generation well. It is Comrade Deng Xiaoping who has inherited this spiritual legacy well, and constantly injected contents of the new era into it, and carried it forward. Thus it can be seen that Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics has fully inherited the living soul of Mao Zedong Thought. Without such an inheritance, the party history will be cut off, or even negated. We will thus lose our basic foothold, and get lost. As far as development is concerned, it means opening up and blazing new trails. It also means that in front of the new situation, we study the new issues, sum up new experiences, generalize new viewpoints, and establish new theories. This shows that Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics tops Mao Zedong Thought. Since the Third Plenary Session of the 11th CPC Central Committee, our country has entered a new historical period, and great changes have taken place in both the domestic and international situation. The new situation and practice has provided us with unprecedentedly favorable conditions and new experiences. This has laid a firm and practical foundation for the development of Marxism-Leninism-Mao Zedong Thought, and the new theory as well. For instance, with the deepening of reform, our party has put forth a series of relevant new viewpoints and theories. They mainly include: The full development of the commodity economy is an insuperable stage of the development of the social economy; the planned economy is not tantamount to socialism, and the market economy is not tantamount to capitalism; socialism can also pursue the market economy, and the aim of our country's economic structural reform is to establish a socialist market economic system, and so on. In the development of Marxist theory, they have theoretically solved, for the first time, the issue of combining commodity economy with market economy and socialism so that we can make a major breakthrough in understanding relations between planning and market. This has brought about unprecedented and fundamental changes in the socialist economic pattern. Revolution means emancipation of productive forces, and so does reform. Reform is also a profound revolution, and the only way for emancipating and developing productive forces under the socialist conditions. In the history of the development of Marxism, they have, for the first time, correctly pointed out the driving force for the development of socialism. This negates the theory which has always taken class struggle as a driving force for the development of socialism. This has enabled us to find a road for rapidly developing socialism and making our country powerful and strong. The essence of socialism is the emancipation and development of productive forces, elimination of exploitation and polarization, and eventual attainment of common prosperity. This is a major supplement and correction of the previous standards on socialism. Lastly, let us talk about correction. Correction means correcting a few inferences wrongly drawn by Comrade Mao Zedong during his later years due to historical conditions, and which have been proved by practice that they no longer conform with the demand of the new era. It means amending, supplementing, and improving certain viewpoints which can no longer conform with the new situation due to changes of conditions. This shows that the latecomer (Deng's theory) is highly responsible for the old timer (Mao Thought), the masses, and history. For example, with regard to the issue of concentrating our efforts on developing productive forces, Comrade Mao Zedong realized this, because it is a common knowledge of Marxism. This was also a problem which he strived to solve all his life. However, owing to the fact that he made a wrong estimation of the class situation in our country and the political situation of the party and state, he adopted a wrong method to deal with it. In this connection, Comrade Deng Xiaoping has repeatedly told the whole party that we must concentrate our efforts on developing productive forces, and treat this as the most fundamental task of the entire party. In a word, Deng Xiaoping theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristic has inherited the living soul of Mao Zedong Thought and developed Mao Zedong Thought and has, through practice, found the road of reform and opening up for the Chinese people with an aim of making their country powerful and strong. The nucleus of the theory is "one center, two basic points," and its nature and essence are emancipating the mind, seeking truth from facts, and everything proceeding from reality. The nucleus of the theory is our political line, and the party's unshakable basic line in the entire initial period of socialism. The essence of the theory is our cognitive line, and an ideological line which our party must stick to under all circumstances. Under the guidance of Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics, let us strive for a new victory in reform, opening up, and modernization!
