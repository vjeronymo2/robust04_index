BFN [Excerpt] An Eritrean ministerial delegation led by Interior Minister Ali al-Sayyid 'Abd-al-Rahman, and accompanied by Foreign Minister Petros Solomon and Mr. (Salih Kapka), undersecretary at the Foreign Ministry, arrived in Khartoum this morning. The Eritrean foreign minister emphasized the strength of relations between Sudan and Eritrea. He pointed out that there are historical relations between the two peoples. He stated that the visit is in keeping with the continuation of visits and contacts between the governments of the two countries. He added that Eritrean-Sudanese relations have special advantages, and he confirmed that the two sides are endeavoring to solve their differences. [passage omitted]
