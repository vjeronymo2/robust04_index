Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tunis, Feb 12 (AFP) -- PLO leader Yasir 'Arafat said Saturday [12 February] he expects to sign a final deal with Israel by the end of the month to launch Palestinian self-rule in Gaza and Jericho, the Palestinian agency WAFA reported. The PLO would continue its efforts to achieve a "precise and faithful implementation" of the September 13 autonomy deal, he said. 'Arafat spoke after briefing Tunisian President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali on the PLO-Israel security pact signed in Cairo on Wednesday, which overcame some of the obstacles to launching self rule. Israel's withdrawal is due to be completed by April 13. The two sides will resume talks in Egypt on Monday to finalize agreement on autonomy.
