April 26, 1990, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

TRAFFIC, TRAFFIC AND MORE TRAFFIC . . .

Residents say traffic is the biggest problem facing North County, according to a recent Times Poll. More than growth, more than the environment, more than drugs or crime.

North County Focus wants to address some of those concerns in upcoming issues. But first, we would like to hear more about them.

What is the most vexing commuting experience you have faced in North County? Which are the worst roads? What questions do you want answered about commuting -- by car or public transportation?

Does it burn you up when you see a single commuter driving in the car-pool lanes? Do you wonder why Interstate 5 backs up so badly during the Del Mar Fair and racing seasons?

Write to us about your experiences and your questions. We will try to address them. Send your letters to:
