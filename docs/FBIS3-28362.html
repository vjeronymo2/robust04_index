Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Phnom Penh AKP February 7 -- France has provided TV technical equipment worth 2.5 million francs to Cambodia. It was the first time that France provided such equipment to Cambodia since the formation of the royal government of Cambodia. The gift was handed over in Phnom Penh last Friday, 4 February, by Parcsel Charlat, French charge d'affaires a.i to Cambodia, to Ieng Muli, minister of information. On that occasion, Minister Ieng Muli expressed thanks to the government of France for its assistance to Cambodia for the development of mass media in the country. Parcsel Charlat said in his reply that the gift was the contribution to the development of TV broadcasting in Cambodia and a token of cooperation between the two countries.
