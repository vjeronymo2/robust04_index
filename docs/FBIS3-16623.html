Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Russian President's Directive no. 129-rp signed by Boris Yeltsin, dated 11 March 1994] [Text] For the purposes of implementing the Moscow Declaration, signed by the Presidents of the Russian Federation and the United States on 14 January 1994, and the Joint Statement of the President of the Russian Federation and the Prime Minister of Great Britain and Northern Ireland of 15 February 1994, the Russian Defense Ministry shall elaborate and, before 30 May 1994, implement measures to organize the daily activity of the strategic nuclear forces with nontargeted missiles. [Signed] B. Yeltsin, president of the Russian Federation [Dated] 11 March 1994 Directive no. 129-rp
