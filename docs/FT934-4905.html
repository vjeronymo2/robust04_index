931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / Swiss group in Olympic deal The Swiss company that makes Swatch watches, Suisse Microelectronique et d'Horlogerie (SMH), said in Atlanta yesterday that it had agreed to be a Dollars 40m sponsor of the 1996 Olympic Games, writes Barbara Harrison in Atlanta. The sponsorship is part of a campaign by the Atlanta Committee for the Olympic Games to raise some Dollars 400m from 10 companies. SMH becomes the only European company to join the line-up of seven sponsors.
