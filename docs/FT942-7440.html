940525 FT 25 MAY 94 / International Capital Markets: Brazilian utility stake to be sold A Brazilian state government is to sell most of its 23.2 per cent stake in Espirito Santo Centrais Eletricas (Escelsa), the electricity utility, in a stock market auction expected to raise around Dollars 100m, AP-DJ reports from Rio de Janeiro. Later this year, Brazil's federal government is expected to sell most of the 72.3 per cent of Escelsa's capital owned by the federal electric holding company, Eletrobras. Foreign investors will be allowed to participate in the sale.
