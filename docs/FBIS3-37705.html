Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 18 Feb -- "In the last few days Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin has been exchanging messages with leaders of the states most actively participating in the search for a solution to the Bosnian problem," says an official statement issued by the presidential press service today. "In these messages the countries involved have expressed their positions with regard to this problem. The Russian president stated his views on the situation that had developed, stressing the need for political settlement and stopping the bloodshed in Bosnia," the press service reports.
