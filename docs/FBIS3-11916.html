Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Madrid, 23 Mar (PL) -- Carlos Lage, vice president of the Cuban Council of State and executive secretary of the Cuban Council of Ministers, is currently in Spain on a working visit. He has already met with Prime Minister Felipe Gonzalez. Cuban diplomatic sources have disclosed that Lage's agenda includes meetings with other officials of the Spanish executive branch. Lage will meet with Foreign Affairs Minister Javier Solana, Economics and Finance Minister Pedro Solbes, and Commerce and Tourism Minister Javier Gomez-Navarro. The same sources indicated that Lage will be in Spain until 25 March, and his visit will focus on the traditional meetings and cooperation between the two governments. Jose Luis Rodriguez, minister chairman of the state committee for finance, adviser Ernesto Senti, and Cuban Ambassador to Spain Rosario Navas are attending the meetings with Lage.
