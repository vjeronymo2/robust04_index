Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In Uttar Pradesh, with the continued improvement of the situation in Kanpur, the Army has been completely withdrawn from the city. Our Kanpur correspondent, Raghunandan Chaturvedi, quoting official sources, says 22 companies of paramilitary and police are keeping a watch in sensitive areas. In the (Babupurva) locality, the curfew has been relaxed for three hours for the first time today in (Dankapurva), except in blocks and transport mega pockets. The situation continued to be peaceful during curfew relaxation. The state government has constituted a one-man commission to go into the incidents in Varanasi during the first week of this month. Meanwhile, Banaras Hindu University administration has ordered an inquiry into the death of a student in the university hospital last Sunday [13 February] and the incidents of violence which followed his death. A retired judge of the Allahabad High Court will conduct the inquiry. The bundh [strike] called by the Students Union in Varanasi against the indefinite closure of the Banaras Hindu University has had a mixed response today. Police arrested over 50 persons, including the Students Union leader, for defying prohibitory orders under Section 144.
