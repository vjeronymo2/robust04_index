January 8, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

A J.F.K. OBSESSION

Lee Green's story about David Lifton's work ("His J.F.K. Obsession," Nov. 20) was a real page-turner. Green pointed out that Lifton's book, "Best Evidence," was given a negative review by Times' critic Kenneth John Atchity, who felt that getting to the truth of the matter won't resurrect J.F.K. I say, the point is not to resurrect J.F.K.; that cannot be done. However, we should not let whoever assassinated him get away with murder.

MONICA WELLESLEY

Huntington Beach
