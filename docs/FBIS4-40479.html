BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Viktor Yelmakov] [Text] Novosibirsk, 16 June -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin made a stopover in Novosibirsk this afternoon on the way to Moscow from eastern Russia. Boris Yeltsin had a conversation with leaders of the oblast. During the conversation, Ivan Indinok, head of the oblast administration, said important problems in the oblast were touched on. He assessed the conversation with the president as very interesting and full. The head of administration said that the president had agreed to a request to send a government commission to the oblast to work with local experts on an analysis of the functioning of the administration and to consider aid issues. Boris Yeltsin also asked about the outcome of the sowing season and expressed concern about the difficulty in harvesting. Ivan Indinok asked for combines to be sent to Novosibirsk, as the pool of vehicles is going to be greatly overloaded. The president gave an instruction that sending a thousand combines to the oblast should be considered. This would be a tremendous support for us, the head of adminstration said about the president's decision. After a short stopover the president's airplane left for Moscow.
