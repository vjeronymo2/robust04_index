Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The following is a report from the Social Communications Ministry: Last night, in the main hall of Government House, President Gonzalo Sanchez de Losada presented the Popular Participation Law to the nation. He did so on a national radio and television network in the presence of an audience of guests specially invited by a [word indistinct] polling company for a question and answer session. As is his custom, the president presented the law during a frank dialogue with the Bolivian people and explained the scope and benefits this law will bring to all municipalities, communities, and cantons. [Words indistinct] of the law, he said that it promotes and consolidates popular participation by incorporating Indian, peasant, urban, and rural communities in the political and economic life of the country. This law will likewise improve the lives of Bolivian men and women with a fairer distribution of wealth and better administration of public funds. It will strengthen the political and economic instruments necessary to improve democracy and will facilitate and make the participation of the people possible. [recorded passage indistinct]
