BFN [Report by Edwin Mayon from Cochabamba] [Text] Bolivian President Gonzalo Sanchez de Lozada on 3 April went to Sipesipe, a provincial village, where he attended Easter Mass. The president was very happy and in good spirits. Speaking to the local press, the president said that this will be a key month because the Popular Participation Bill, the educational reform bill, and the national budget will be approved here. He announced that workers will not receive any more salary increases. The president encouraged leaders of civic committees and municipal authorities to resort to dialogue. [Begin recording] [Lozada] We hope that civic committee leaders will participate in a dialogue with the government because this month we have to approve the Popular Participation Bill and the national budget to allow the economy to operate according to [words indistinct] so that municipalities can start getting funds as of May and improve the standards of living of the people in their counties. The Popular Participation Bill is intended to benefit all the provinces of Bolivia. [passage indistinct] [end recording]
