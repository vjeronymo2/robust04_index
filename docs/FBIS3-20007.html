Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bursa, Mar 25 (AA) -- Five people were wounded in an armed clash in this northwestern city between the supporters of the True Path Party (DYP) and the Motherland Party (ANAP). Additional security troops were sent to the Orhangazi township of Bursa, to quell the unrest. The DYP is the main coalition partner and ANAP is the main opposition party. Both parties are conservative parties and are competing for the March 27 local elections.
