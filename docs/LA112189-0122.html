November 21, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: FULLERTON;

NEW POSTMASTER WILL BE SWORN IN

The Fullerton post office will have a formal ceremony Wednesday to swear in new Postmaster J. Timothy Bomersback.

Bomersback, 42, previously was manager of customer service at the Santa Ana post office. He joined the Postal Service in 1973 as a letter carrier in Long Beach after serving four years in the Air Force. He also was postmaster in Long Beach.

As Fullerton postmaster, Bomersback will supervise about 300 employees and oversee the delivery of more than than 3.2 million pieces of mail per week to more than 130,000 residents.
