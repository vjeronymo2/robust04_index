940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / Czech fugitive stays put Mr Victor Kozeny, the Czech financier being investigated by prosecutors over his role in a blackmail scandal, yesterday denied wrongdoing but said he would not return to Prague from self-imposed exile abroad until he was allowed 'to pursue my job in peace.' Mr Kozeny, who went to Switzerland in March during the trial of a secret service agent he had accused of blackmail, said it was 'simply impossible' to manage his Harvard group of investment funds while 'being branded a criminal.'
