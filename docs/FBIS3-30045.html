Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 23 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- In the city of Princeton in the New Jersey State of the USA there has been held the ceremony of opening the first Mongolian American joint venture "Mongolian Tourism Corporation of America" which was set up by the Mongolian "Juulchin" (Tourist) Company and the American Compass Travel One. The opening of the joint venture makes it possible to increase the amount of Mongolian tourists visiting the USA and is the first instance of capital investment made by a Mongolian organization in America. At the opening ceremony there were taking part President of the "Juulchin" Company B. Delgersuren and the Ambassador of Mongolia to the USA L. Davaagiv.
