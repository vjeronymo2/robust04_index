Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Mar. 9 (A.A.) -- Denis Bauchard, French foreign minister for North Africa and Middle East affairs [name and title as received], arrived in Ankara on Tuesday [8 March] for the four-way multinational force meeting. Officials at the French Embassy welcomed Bauchard at Esenboga Airport outside Ankara. Turkey, the United States, Britain, and France will hold a four-way meeting on March 10 and review the activities of multinational force that was set up to protect Kurds living in northern Iraq. Operation Provide Comfort was in place to protect Iraqi Kurds fleeing the attacks of Saddam Husayn and involved 21,700 troops at its peak on May 21, 1991. After extending the mandate of the multinational force in December Turkey proposed to review the matter in a four-way meeting. The ground troops of the force have been withdrawn and only an air force remains in the region.
