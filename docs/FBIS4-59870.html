BFN [Text] This evening, Prime Minister Dr. 'Atif Sidqi chaired a Ministerial Economic Group meeting to review the implementation of the economic reform program's second phase and to discuss future cooperation between Egypt, the IMF, and the World Bank. In a press statement released today, the prime minister said that Egypt has achieved distinct success in implementing economic reform programs. This, he added, was evident in the Egyptian pounds' steady exchange rates against foreign currencies, especially the dollar. He said the Egyptian Central Bank had a $16 billion surplus and this recently increased by $400 million. Sidqi said the government is in the process of devising a new workers' law to be submitted to the People's Assembly during its current session.
