930621 FT 21 JUN 93 / Japan's response to US pressure Japan should deregulate its economy to open its markets and reconsider its trading practices in response to US pressure for a cut in its trade surplus, said the Bank of Japan's governor, Mr Yasushi Mieno. Any attempt to reduce the surplus rapidly would lead to higher inflation in Japan which would damage the prospects for world growth, he warned. Report, Page 4; Interview with Mr Mieno, Page 16
