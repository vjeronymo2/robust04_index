September 18, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

PRIVATE DONORS CONTRIBUTE TO GROVE

The financially troubled Grove Shakespeare Festival raised a total of $5,895 in private contributions over the weekend, a theater spokesperson said Monday. Donations of $3,200 arrived in the mail "mostly in small checks of $10 to $25."

The rest was been raised through curtain pleas at "Othello," the current Grove production.

However, the fund-raising campaign being conducted by a group of 14 Garden Grove civic and volunteer organizations, to raise as much as $15,000, had yet to produce any revenue by Monday, according to Barbara G. Hammerman, Grove's managing director. Hammerman claimed last week that goal would be met within a few days.

The Grove is caught in a cash-flow crisis and was expected to press the City Council Monday evening for a continuing infusion of emergency funds.JAN HERMAN
