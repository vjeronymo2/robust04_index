December 29, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

REYES HAS CAREER NIGHT IN CHAPMAN'S VICTORY

Chapman College defeated Alaska Fairbanks, 83-72, in the first round of the Chico State basketball tournament Thursday at Chico State.

Center Frantz Reyes scored a career-high 21 points and pulled down a career-high 19 rebounds for the Panthers (7-2).
