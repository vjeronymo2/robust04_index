920108 FT 08 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Simon raises Pounds 6.3m from disposal Simon Engineering, the environmental and industrial services group, is continuing its programme of concentrating on core activities with the disposal of Simon Container Machinery. The business and certain assets of the operation, together with its US affiliate, have been sold for Pounds 6.3m cash to SHS Handling Systems, a company formed in January last year to acquire SCM's materials handling and conveying systems operation.
