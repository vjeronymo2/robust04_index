Spheres 944K1442A Tashkent PRAVDA VOSTOKA in Russian 14 May 94 p 5 944K1442A Tashkent PRAVDA VOSTOKA Russian CSO [Article: "Bernard Schweiger: We've Found Dependable Friends"] [Text] The final phase of negotiations on economic cooperation between representatives of the government of the Republic of Uzbekistan and a delegation from the FRG Ministry for Economic Cooperation and Development was conducted on 13 May in the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan. As we know, this delegation, which was headed by Bernard Schweiger, spent the week in our country. Members of the delegation acquainted themselves with the work of the first Uzbek-German joint ventures, and with the activity of different ministries and departments. The members of the delegation also visited Bukhara, Urgench, Khiva and Nukus. The results of the meetings and negotiations were generalized in the Cabinet of Ministers. A final intergovernmental protocol and an agreement on financial and technical cooperation were signed. The documents were signed in behalf of the Uzbek government by republic Deputy Prime Minister Utkur Sultanov, and in behalf of the German delegation by Bernard Schweiger. The protocol foresees cooperation in areas such as research and personal training, joint agricultural projects, improvement of the communication system, reconstruction of the airport in Tashkent, and assistance in public health. "We found some warm and dependable people here," said the leader of the German delegation on the last day of the talks. "My hope is that our friendship will grow stronger not only in words but also in specific deeds. The documents that were signed today on strengthening our cooperation are aimed at this." German Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary to Uzbekistan Doctor Karl Heinz Kuna [transliteration] participated in the signing ceremony.
