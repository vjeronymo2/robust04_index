940425 FT 25 APR 94 / World News in Brief: BCCI case verdicts in June Verdicts are to be delivered on June 14 by a court in Abu Dhabi in the trial of 13 former Bank of Credit and Commerce International officials in the biggest case to stem from the bank's collapse. Charges include dissipating funds, forging documents, concealing deficits and losses and approving false loans.
