Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] As a result of a terrorist act, Razil Musin, deputy chairman of the Republic of Bashkortostan's Supreme Soviet, perished this morning. An explosive device went off when he left his home for work. Our correspondent Abrek Baykov reports from Ufa that Deputy Internal Affairs Minister Aleksandr Shabrin believes that this was a contract killing. A week ago Razil Musin was appointed to the post of head of administration of Dyurtyuli rayon by a personal decision of the Republic's president against the wish of virtually all local leaders. An investigation of the murder is under way.
