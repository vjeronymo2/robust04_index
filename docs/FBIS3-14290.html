Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Nabil Sha'th, head of the Palestinian delegation to the peace talks currently under way in Cairo, has hoped that it will be possible to reach an agreement with Israel on security for Palestinians in the occupied territories, particularly in the city of Hebron. In statements at the end of the first session of talks with Israeli negotiators, which lasted [words indistinct] hours last night, brother Nabil Sha'th said the negotiations are proceeding well, and that they are not encountering any difficulties. He hoped that an agreement will be reached today to allow for the resumption of the talks on the implementation of the Palestinian autonomy agreement.
