Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Best wishes from the people and political and mass organizations were conveyed by Esteban Lazo, first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba in Santiago de Cuba and Politburo member, during the ceremony commemorating the 35th anniversary of the National Revolutionary Police [PNR]. Lazo presided over the main ceremony in commemoration of this occasion. Several PNR troops were promoted to the ranks of officer and noncommissioned officer. Seals denoting years of vigilance and combativity and the 30th anniversary of the CDR [Committees for the Defense of the Revolution] were presented during the ceremony held in Santiago de Cuba. It was also learned that activities were held throughout Cuba by PNR troops and the population, and political and mass organizations sent their best wishes to the members of the PNR.
