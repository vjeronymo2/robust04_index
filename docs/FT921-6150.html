920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Isotron All sectors of Isotron felt the effects of the recession in the half year to December 31 1991, and pre-tax profit fell from Pounds 1.42m to Pounds 1.28m. The irradiation service group was particularly hit in its medical sector, where turnover was well down on expectations. Overall sales dropped to Pounds 3.14m (Pounds 3.2m). Earnings were 6.9p (7.5p). The interim dividend is raised to 1.37p (1.25p).
