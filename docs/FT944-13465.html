941027 FT 27 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Govett Oriental Net asset value at Govett Oriental Investment Trust was 437.2p per share at September 30, up from 397.6p at the trust's March year end and 341p at end-September last year. Attributable revenue amounted to Pounds 2.7m (Pounds 1.81m) for earnings of 1.5p (1p) per share. To utilise tax credits on UK franked investment income, a 'conventional' and unchanged interim dividend of 0.4p is declared - last year's final distribution was paid as a foreign income dividend.
