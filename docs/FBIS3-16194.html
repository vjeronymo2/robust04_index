Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, March 14 (SNARK)--Today a delegation of the National-Republican Party of Russia, that was invited by the Special Commission of Armenian Supreme Soviet on the questions concerning Artsakh (Karabakh), arrived in Yerevan. Nikolay Lysenko, the deputy of State Duma, heads the delegation. Suren Zolyan, the secretary of the parliament commission, told SNARK the representatives of the National-Republican Party are going to have political consultations with different factions and to exchange experience in the fields, which the fractions are interested in. Today the representatives of National-Republican Party met with political factions and deputies's groups of the Armenian parliament. Tomorrow the representatives are going to leave for Karabakh.
