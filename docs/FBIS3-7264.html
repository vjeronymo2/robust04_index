Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The prime minister, Datuk Sri Dr. Mahathir, says bilateral relations and cooperation between Malaysia and Vietnam over the past few years have brought positive results to be proud of. Bilateral trade between the two countries, which stood at 235.4 million ringgit in 1991, had increased to 473 million ringgit in the first nine months of last year. Speaking at a dinner hosted for the visiting general secretary of the Communist Party of Vietnam, Mr. Do Muoi, in Kuala lumpur last night, Datuk Sri Dr. Mahathir said many Malaysian companies had ventured in Vietnam. As such, the Vietnamese Government should give consideration to these companies, which should help in its economic growth, by further awarding them projects. He also pointed out that Southeast Asian countries should resolve problems among themselves in a peaceful manner while cooperating with each other to bring about mutual benefit. Everyone must realize the dream of a peaceful, stable, and united Southeast Asian region.
