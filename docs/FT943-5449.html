940903 FT 03 SEP 94 / Finance and the Family: Smaller company shares improve - At a glance Smaller company share prices continued to improve this week. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) climbed 0.4 per cent to 1715.16 over the week to September 1. The index is up 1.4 per cent since the start of the year, while the FT-SE-A All-Share is down 4.2 per cent over the same period, and the FT-SE 100 is down 5.9 per cent.
