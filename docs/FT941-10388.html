940209 FT 09 FEB 94 / Bulgarian land reform progress Bulgaria has returned 47.7 per cent of the land confiscated under communism to its original owners and hopes to transfer the rest by the end of this year, the agriculture ministry said, Reuter reports from Sofia. Implementation of land reform, passed in 1991 to end Soviet-style collective farms forcibly set up in the 1950s, was being mostly slowed down by excessive claims, it said.
