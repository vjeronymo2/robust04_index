Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on Cuban economic developments monitored through 8 February. According to the Secretariat for Foreign Cooperation, Havana received more than $4 million in international donations and contributions in 1993. Cuba received aid from more than 300 cities worldwide, especially from Spain. (Havana PRENSA LATINA in Spanish 0239 GMT 3 Feb 94)
