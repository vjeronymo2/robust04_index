BFN [Text] Cheboksary, May 18, RIA -- Russia starts to dismantle production facilities capable of manufacturing chemical weapons, said Vice-Premier Oleg Soskovets after visiting the Khimprom amalgamation in Chuvashia formerly cranking out chemical munitions. The factory stopped producing chemical weapons in 1987, but the production facilities stood in place and could be re-activated when needed. Oleg Soskovets and Chuvashian President Nikolay Fedorov decided to compile a schedule, before September 1, 1994, of phasing out chemical-capable production facilities.
