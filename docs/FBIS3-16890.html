Language: English Article Type:BFN ["News Bulletin" No. 54/94] [Text] Monday, March 21 -- The Defence Ministry does not intend to order ammunition from local firms, ministry vice-chancellor Tarmo Milder announced. He added that state orders will depend on quality and price of the ammunition and mentioned $0.17 cartridge as an acceptable price. South African, Israeli, Chinese and South Korean enterprises have offered their cooperation to the Defence Ministry.
