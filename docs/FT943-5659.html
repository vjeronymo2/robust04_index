940902 FT 02 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Allen Shares in Allen, the Greater Manchester-based construction group, rose 11p to 176p yesterday following its announcement of a good start to the year. Over the summer it had obtained contracts valued at Pounds 21.3m, turnover at its Speedy Hire Centres was 40 per cent above the same period last year and housing reservations were up 13.5 per cent.
