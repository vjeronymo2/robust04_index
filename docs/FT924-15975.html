921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / Observer: Ode mode There once was a bird man from Kiev . . . It seems that 20 pilots from Air Ukraine are being sent to learn English at, of all places, the University of Limerick. That done, they'll move on to Seattle to learn to fly Boeing jets. A bottle of malt to the begetter of the best limerick about an Air Ukraine pilot asking for permission to land at Heathrow.
