Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Armenian Prime Minister Grant Bagratyan today received Iranian Charge d'Affaires in Armenia Ahmad Sobhani. They discussed matters pertaining to trade and economic cooperation between the two countries, in particular the Iran-Armenia gas pipeline, construction of the Megri bridge, organization of border trade, road and air transportation, coordination of customs activities, and investments. The sides expressed satisfaction at the constructive measures taken by the governments of the two countries in these fields, and noted that there are real opportunities to substantially expand the economic ties between Armenia and Iran in 1994. Mutual understanding was displayed on the Karabakh problem. They considered unacceptable the use of force which, by further deepening the conflict, is preventing the establishment of peace in the region. The prime minister highly assessed Iran's efforts for the peaceful solution of the problem. Armen Khachaturyan, coordinator of Armenian Government affairs, and other officials were present at the meeting.
