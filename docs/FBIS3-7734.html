Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Kosova Information Center reports of new movements of Serbian Army in several centers in Kosova. Dozens of trucks have been deployed from a military barracks in Prizren. Meanwhile, 10 tanks and some heavy artillery have been deployed in Hajvali hills near Prishtina. Serbian Army movements have even been observed in the direction of Gjakove. Heavy cannons have also been installed near Prizren, while 18 tanks have been deployed in Tumosh, Podujeve. Low flights by military planes have become more frequent over Prishtina and other cities in Kosova.
