BFN [Text] Shanghai, May 23 (XINHUA) -- The first symposium of international financial centers opened here today. Wang Daohan, president of the Association for Relations Across the Taiwan Straits, is chairman of the on-going conference, and spoke at the opening ceremony. Financiers at the meeting discussed the form, the development laws and trends of international financial centers, and talked about the advantageous conditions for rebuilding Shanghai into an international financial center -- and the steps that needed to be taken. More than 150 Chinese and overseas bankers, economists and representatives of various shanghai-based foreign financial organizations of different countries are attending the symposium, which will last for three days. Chinese Vice-President Rong Yiren, and heads of many world renowned organizations sent personal messages of greetings to the symposium.
