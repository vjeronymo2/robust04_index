July 3, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

PARISHIONERS HOLD SERVICE FOR DETROIT CHURCH CLOSING

Parishioners at a Roman Catholic church held a prayer service Sunday, a week after the church was ordered to close. About 50 members of Mother of Consolation Church were at the service, which was not a formal Mass, said Dario Guidarini, president of the parish council.

The church was among 30 ordered closed or merged with other congregations because of declining memberships, a shortage of priests and the costs of maintaining the large churches.
