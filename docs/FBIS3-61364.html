Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Unattributed article: "Toward Repayment of the Debt"] [Text] Nikolayev--Four large refrigerating trawlers will be turned over to fishermen of the Far East toward repayment of a proportion of the debt of Ukraine for natural gas to the Gazprom concern of the Russian Federation. The crews of these vessels are scheduled to arrive in Nikolayev shortly. The Ukrnefteprodukt association will refund the cost of the vessels in karbovantsy to the shipyard. This impressive barter operation between the two CIS states became possible as a result of negotiations initiated in Moscow and Kiev by specialists from the Black Sea Shipbuilding Plant. During the negotiations, opportunities for the delivery of a total of 15 trawlers with similar arrangements for mutual settlements was discussed.
