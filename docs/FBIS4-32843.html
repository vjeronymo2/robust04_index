BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 27 May (NA) -- The Foreign Ministry reported today that two Argentine citizens are being held "hostage" in the Techint Company offices in the Bolivian town of Camiri which has been blocked as part of a civic strike over salary hike demands. The Argentine citizens are engineers Jose Gasparoni and Juan Quevedo. The Argentine Foreign Ministry is conducting intense negotiations with the Bolivian Government over Gasparoni and Quevedo. In this regard, Foreign Minister Guido Di Tella called his Bolivian counterpart to personally inquire about the situation of these two citizens while the Foreign Ministry has called the Techint Company in our country and is calling the engineers' relatives.
