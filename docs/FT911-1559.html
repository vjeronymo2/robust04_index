910504 FT 04 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Towles falls into red and halves pay-out Towles, the manufacturer of knitwear, hosiery and underwear, fell into the red in the year to February 28. From taxable profits of Pounds 97,000, the company declined to losses of Pounds 56,000 on turnover up at Pounds 15m (Pounds 14.72m). Losses per share were 1.53p (earnings 3.28p) and a reduced dividend for the year of 0.25p (0.5p) has been proposed for the ordinary and A ordinary shares.
