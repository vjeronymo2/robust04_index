BFN [Text] On the morning of 18 May, Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata's advisory "Defense Issues Council" (chaired by Asahi Breweries Chairman Kotaro Higuchi) held a meeting at the prime minister's office. At the meeting, the members agreed to propose a review of the 1976 "National Defense Program Outline" on three subjects: 1) international situation; 2) comprehensive security policies; and 3) tangible measures for defense buildup. On the "international situation," the panel is going to discuss military and social trends of the present world, focusing on "Japan's increasing responsibilities." Regarding the "comprehensive security policies," the panel will advocate "comprehensive measures to appropriately cope with `various threats'" in consideration of both military and economic trends.
