Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN [Text] His Excellency [H.E.] Sieng Lapresse, adviser to the minister of information and chief of the Information Ministry cabinet, gave a news conference on 23 February to national and international reporters on the military situation in the wake of the capture by the Khmer Royal Armed Forces [KRAF] of major Khmer Rouge bases at Anlung Veng and An Seh. He said the KRAF was continuing to mop up Khmer Rouge elements in areas 15 km around the Anlung Veng base and that the Armed Forces had seized 175 assorted firearms, 13 vehicles, 14 field radios, and two tanks of fuel. H.E. Sieng Lapresse further said that at An Seh, the KRAF seized a significant quantity of war materiel, including 15 DK-75 shells, 30 70-mm shells, six APL shells, 30 hand grenades, and 574 cases of cartridges for AK rifles. He also disclosed that during the fighting the KRAF killed 28 Khmer Rouge soldiers, captured three, forced 26 others to surrender, and seized 246 guns.
