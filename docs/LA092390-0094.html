September 23, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

PREP FOOTBALL ROUNDUP

In other nonleague games:

Orange Lutheran 14, Southern California Christian 6 -- Brandon Orman scored on a two-yard run with 46 seconds left for host Orange Lutheran (3-0).

Calvary Chapel 49, Bethel Christian 0 -- Dane Valdez rushed for 118 yards and a touchdown and David Hernandez rushed for 110 yards and scored two touchdowns at Westminster High School.
