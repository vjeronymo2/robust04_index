Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 29 (XINHUA) -- Visiting President of the Republic of Korea (ROK) Kim Yong-sam today described his country's relationship with China as having a long history of thousands of years. Delivering a speech at Beijing University here this morning, Kim said that the two countries are not only similar culturally, close geographically and historically friendly, but also complementary economically. Kim said that the ROK and China established diplomatic ties only about one-and-a-half years ago, but bilateral ties have been boosted in many aspects. He expressed the belief that ROK-Chinese relations will not only blossom fully but also be fruitful in the future. Kim said that it is a great honor for him, as the first president of the ROK, to visit Beijing University, an institution with a brilliant history and tradition. Wu Shuqing, president of Beijing University, also made a speech at the meeting.
