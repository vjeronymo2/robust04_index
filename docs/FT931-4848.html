930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Clondalkin slips to IPounds 13.4m Clondalkin Group, the Dublin-based printing and packaging company, reported 1992 pre-tax profits easing from IPounds 13.6m to IPounds 13.4m (Pounds 13.7m). Trading was difficult throughout the year - particularly in the second half -leaving turnover down at IPounds 149.6m (IPounds 151.1m). Earnings per share were 23.39p (25.08p) and the proposed final dividend of 2.898p makes a total for the year of 4.732p (4.381p).
