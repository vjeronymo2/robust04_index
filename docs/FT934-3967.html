931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / World Trade News: Italy agrees Dollars 1.6bn Russian gas package A state-backed Dollars 1.6bn (Pounds 1.07bn) finance package paving the way for equipment sales by Italian companies to Russia's gas industry was signed in Rome yesterday, Robert Graham reports from Rome. The 13 1/2 -year credit, organised by the state credit institute, Mediocredito Centrale, in conjunction with Banca Commerciale, will be covered by Sace, the export credit guarantee organisation. The main suppliers will be two subsidiaries of ENI, the state oil concern - Nuovo Pignone and Snamprogetti. Italian companies will modernise part of the old Soviet gas production and supply system, with payment through gas supplies. Some equipment is to be produced in former Russian military plants.
