930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Applied Holo sale APPLIED Holographics is to sell the non-holographic hot stamping foils business of Transfer All Purpose Foils, its wholly owned subsidiary, to Whiley Foils of Livingston, Scotland. Whiley Foils, a member of API Group, will make an immediate Pounds 450,000 payment for existing stocks, plus future phased payments depending on how successfully the business transfers from TAPF's Washington, Tyne and Wear, base.
