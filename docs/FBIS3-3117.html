Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [YONHAP from Beijing] [Text] Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen said on 16 March that China has no intention to play the role of mediator in resolving the North Korean nuclear issue. Regarding the nuclear issue, Minister Qian said in a news conference held that day: "China is not playing the role of a mediator for the issue at present, nor does it plan to take on this role in future."
