931223 FT 23 DEC 93 / Pounds 150m proposal at Shepherd's Bush Plans for a Pounds 150m development on a 35-acre site at Shepherd's Bush, London were unveiled yesterday by a consortium including Scottish Amicable, the life assurer, and European Land and Property Corporation, which is headed by property entrepreneur Mr Godfrey Bradman. The proposal includes a 620,000 sq ft shopping centre, leisure facilities, housing and a new M41 junction.
