930416 FT 16 APR 93 / Pounds 5,052m capital issues in March ANNOUNCEMENTS of new capital issues by UK borrowers in March were Pounds 5,052m, of which Pounds 4,122m was denominated in sterling. This was the largest monthly figure for total new issues since the series began in 1986. The Bank of England said the biggest sterling issue announced in March was the fixed-rate bond issue by Abbey National Treasury Services for Pounds 650m, which set a new record for a single euro-sterling transaction.
