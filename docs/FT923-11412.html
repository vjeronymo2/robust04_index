920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Marginal fall in net assets at Derby Tst Net asset value of Derby Trust showed a marginal decline to 339p by June 30, against 343p 12 months earlier. Available revenue in the first half slipped to Pounds 971,480 (Pounds 985,290) for earnings of 8.2259p (8.3429p) per income share, all of which is distributed to shareholders.
