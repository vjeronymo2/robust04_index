Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 9 (KCNA) -- Paek In-chun, chairman of the North side headquarters of the National Alliance for the Country's Reunification [Pomminnyon], Hyon Chun-kuk, chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Journalists Union, and Kang Kwan-son, secretary of the Central Committee of the Korean Democratic Women's Union, published statements Saturday in support of the policy of national reunification set forth by the great leader President Kim Il-song in his New Year address. Chairman Paek In-chun says that the principled stand of the Workers' Party of Korea and the DPRK Government on national reunification clarified by President Kim Il-song in his New Year address is a highly important guideline indicating a clear way of achieving national reunification by the internal strength of the nation in the 90s. "All the people in the Northern half of Korea, together with the fellow countrymen in the South and overseas, will wage a more vigorous struggle to make this year a historic year in opening a new phase for national reunification, meeting all manner of challenges of the imperialists and the South Korean authorities," he states. Hyon Chun-kuk says that the only way of achieving the reunification of the country peacefully is to adopt the confederacy formula based on the three principles of national reunification. "All the journalists and editors in the Northern half of Korea will wield the pen more vigorously to implement the policy," he stressed. Saying that the women should do their share in achieving the unity of the nation under the 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation, Secretary Kang Kwan-son ardently appealed to the Korean women in the North, South and overseas to turn out as one in the nationwide struggle for national reunification.
