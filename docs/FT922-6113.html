920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton & Jute Spot and shipment sales for the week ended 26 May amounted to 394 tonnes against 510 tonnes in the previous week. Sales were average with growths occurring mainly in American, Russian, Central Asian & West African growths.
