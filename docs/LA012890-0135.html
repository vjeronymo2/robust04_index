January 28, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

BRITAIN;

NEW SUNDAY PAPER BEGINS PUBLICATION

A new Sunday newspaper was launched, aimed at winning a largely young, well-educated readership. The Independent on Sunday is the latest product of a newspaper boom fed by computer technology and hopes to win readers from the Sunday Times. "Obviously we will be going for their readers and trying to persuade them to buy our paper," said Editor Stephen Glover, who expects sales of 500,000 copies per issue in the first year.
