March 16, 1989, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPORTS ET CETERA: FULLERTON SQUEEZES PAST SDSU IN NINTH INNING

David Ayala squeezed home the winning run in the ninth inning as Cal State Fullerton defeated host San Diego State, 1-0, in a nonconference baseball game Wednesday.

Rex Peters had doubled and Brent Mayne singled to put runners on first and third.

Erik Plantenberg (2-2) took the loss for SDSU (11-10). Paul Johnson (2-2) won for 16th-ranked Cal State Fullerton (15-6).

Keio (Japan) University took advantage of three Greg Sherman walks and a hit batter to score four runs in the fifth inning of a 5-3 exhibition victory over the University of San Diego at USD.

Hideaki Ookubo doubled home two runs in the fifth for Keio.

VOLLEYBALL

Amy Erben, a junior on the San Diego State women's volleyball team, is to be arraigned Tuesday in San Diego Municipal Court following her arrest for resisting arrest, disturbing the peace and refusing to disperse at an East San Diego party that police were called to break up early Sunday morning.

Police said Erben, 21, was arrested after she refused to leave the party in the 4900 block of 54th Street. Police said that she resisted the officer's attempt to arrest her and had to be subdued by three additional officers. Erben, a 6-foot-2 middle blocker from Seattle, is free on $5,750 bond.
