Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] [Announcer] In the view of Jaroslaw Kaczynski, chairman of the Center Alliance [PC], President Lech Walesa's decision on the recall of the chairman of the National Radio and Television Broadcasting Council [KRRiT], Marek Markiewicz, lacks any legal basis. The leader of the Conservative Party [PK], Aleksander Hall, has also protested against the method of dismissal of Marek Markiewicz. He feels that the activities of the president are, regardless of his intentions, such as to bring disturbance into the public life of the country and to favor destabilization. The press spokesman of the Constitutional Tribunal, Adam Jankiewicz, has reported that the tribnunal may consider an application on the matter of the determination of a ruling on the decision of President Lech Walesa on the recall of the chairman of KRRiT over a period of at least two weeks from its being received by the tribunal.
