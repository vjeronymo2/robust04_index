Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Glazovskaya] [Text] Ashgabat February 14 TASS -- President of Turkmenistan Saparmurad Niyazov has resumed his duties after undergoing a successful operation in the United States. On Monday [14 February] he chaired a Cabinet of Ministers meeting, which was, appropriately, mainly devoted to health care problems. Referring to the famous saying that "every cloud has a silver lining", the president stressed the importance of his stay in the U.S. in terms of establishing further contacts, especially in the field of medicine. According to Niyazov, that would help Turkmenistan soon begin implementing its health care programme. The current level of medical services is poor and makes such a programme necessary. The Turkmenistan president, who had an operation done on his legs, was able achieve agreement on the establishment jointly with American specialists of a clinical centre of health care in Ashgabat.
