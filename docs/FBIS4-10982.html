BFN [Editorial Report] Havana PRENSA LATINA in Spanish at 1737 GMT on 18 April reports that the Venezuelan Armed Forces and the Technical Judicial Police are investigating an attack by unidentified individuals on the Vicente Landaeta Gil military air base west of Barquisimeto. The men tried to break into the base's radar site. In the ensuing shooting, two soldiers were wounded, one of them badly. It has not yet been determined whether the attack was perpetrated by common criminals or was politically moptivated.
