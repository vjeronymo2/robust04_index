Language: English Article Type:BFN [By Bear Le] [Text] Taipei, March 15 (CNA) -- The Presidential Office on Tuesday [15 March] said that it welcomes the coming visit here by Mikhail Gorbachev, the former president of the Soviet Union. Presidential Office Deputy Secretary-general and Spokesman Raymond Tai said that Gorbachev is one of the most-respected figures in the world today, and that his visit shows the regard with which he holds Taiwan. Tai said that the former Soviet [words indistinct] together with former United States President Ronald Reagan and former British Prime Minister Margaret Thatcher, were the three world leaders that brought the Cold War to an end. Gorbachev, who will be accompanied by his wife, Raisa, is scheduled to arrive in Taipei on Sunday for a five-day visit. He will meet President Li Teng-hui, Premier Lien Chan and other officials and deliver several public speeches during his stay here.
