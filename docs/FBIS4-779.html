BFN [Text] A group of about 70 Lebowa state officials have occupied the officers of the Transitional Executive Council [TEC] in Pretoria. A spokesman for the Health and Education Workers' Union, NEHAWU, said that the group would stay there until the TEC management committee had addressed their grievances. The group say the situation in Lebowa has deteriorated since Minister of Regional and Land Affairs Mr. Andre Fourie took over the administration duties of the territory. They've asked for Mr. Fourie to be replaced by a neutral administrator and for their promotions to come into affect immediately.
