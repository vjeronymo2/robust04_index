Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] PRC media carried the following reports on PRC leadership activities from 28 January to 3 February 1994. The source is noted in parentheses after each report.

Li Peng Donates to Jiangxi School

Premier Li Peng recently sent 500 yuan to the Xiwang (Hope) Primary School in Ninggang County, Jiangxi Province. It was built from donations for the Hope Project by personnel from CPC Central Committee organs and state organs. When the school was inaugurated on 19 October 1993, Premier Li Peng inscribed its name in his own handwriting. (Beijing Central Television Program One Network in Mandarin 1100 GMT 28 Jan 94)

Li Ruihuan Visits Scientist

Li Ruihuan, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau Standing Committee and chairman of the CPPCC national committee, called on eminent scientist Qian Xuesen at his home in Beijing on 3 February. Upon entering his house, Li Ruihuan firmly grasped Qian Xuesen's hands and said: "I have been asked by the principal leading comrades of the CPC Central Committee and by some veteran comrades of the CPPCC national committee to wish you a happy Chinese New Year and to convey their season's greetings to you and your wife!" Speaking highly of Qian Xuesen's outstanding contributions to China's natural science and particularly to the space industry, Li Ruihuan said: "China successfully tested an atomic bomb and a hydrogen bomb and launched a satellite shortly after its liberation. They laid a solid foundation for its scientific research as well as the development of its space industry. The great influence they produced is still felt today. China's atomic bomb, hydrogen bomb, and satellite are associated with the name of Qian Xuesen. You have conducted intensive research in not only natural science, but also social sciences." Li Ruihuan said: "Social development is inseparable from the advance of natural science and, meanwhile, requires the leadership to make decisions scientifically. At present, China keeps improving the scientific and democratic decision-making by its leadership. It is hoped that you will make more suggestions in this connection." (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0834 GMT 3 Feb 94)

Liu Huaqing Inscribes New Barracks

Fishermen of Leyuan Village in Changdao County, Shandong Province, pooled over 800,000 yuan's worth of funds to erect a five-story building as barracks for a special task company stationed in the area. Construction of the building was recently completed and villagers helped servicemen move into their new quarters. Central Military Commission Vice Chairman Liu Huaqing was deeply touched by the local fishermen's enthusiastic support for the army. He took pleasure in inscribing the new building's name. His inscription reads: "Yong Jun Lou [Support-the-Army Building]." (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0535 GMT 2 Feb 94)

Liu Huaqing Meets `Hero'

On 3 February, Central Military Commission [CMC] Vice Chairmen Liu Huaqing and Zhang Zhen met with Xu Honggang, 22, squad leader of a signal company of the Jinan Military Region. He was stabbed 14 times when fighting off four armed robbers to rescue a woman passenger on a bus in August 1993. Liu Huaqing said during the meeting: "Your deed of valor is very touching. You are a good son of the people and a hero of the People's Liberation Army. You protected the people and they saved you. It fully shows that, when the army and people are united as one, justice will vanquish evil. We are the people's own army. We must always firmly bear in mind our purpose of serving the people wholeheartedly. We must give top priority to the interests of the people and the party under all circumstances and at all times and must stand ready to sacrifice everything we have for the protection of the partys' and people's interests when necessary. We must have a high degree of such consciousness and cherish such noble value." Liu Huaqing urged all the army's commanders and fighters to learn from Xu Honggang and to make greater contributions to the building of socialist spiritual civilization. Zhang Zhen said: "We must firmly bear in mind the duty of a revolutionary soldier; we must stand all tests; we must always maintain the Red Army's true qualities and carry forward the fine traditions of the people's army; we must set an example of implementing the party's basic line and be a strong pillar of people's democratic dictatorship; we must always meet political qualification." During the meeting, CMC members Chi Haotian, Zhang Wannian, and Yu Yongbo "encouraged Xu Honggang to apply himself to the study of political and military affairs and to keep improving himself, and called on all the army units to promote the drive to learn from Xu Honggang to a new climax." (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1128 GMT 3 Feb 94)
Liu Huaqing, Zhang Zhen Visit Army Unit
Accompanied by Gu Shanqing, political commissar of the Beijing Military Region [BMR], and He Daoquan [0149 6670 3123], deputy commander of the BMR and commander of the Beijing Garrison, Liu Huaqing and Zhang Zhen, vice chairmen of the Central Military Commission [CMC], went to the barracks of a hero BMR company on 31 January to wish its officers and men a happy Chinese New Year on behalf of the CMC. The company have carried on the Red Army's glorious traditions. Liu Huaqing and Zhang Zhen inspected the company's quarters and mess halls, enquired about servicemen's food and living conditions, and required company leaders to show concern for new recruits. They prohibited company leaders from beating or abusing new recruits. During inspection, Liu Huaqing said: "It is hoped that, in the new year, comrades will make continued efforts, establish higher standards, carry forward the traditions of Red Army veterans in a better way, do an excellent job in fulfilling your tasks and in building the party branch of the company, improve ideological education and work style, and successfully conduct military training so as to truly satisfactorily accomplish the mission assigned by the party and people once an order is given." Zhang Zhen lectured the officers and men on the fine traditions of our army in light of the company's actual conditions. He said: "Our army always obeys the party and, under the party's leadership, is capable of withstanding the test of any hardship or difficulty and is invincible." He urged all officers and men of the company to set an example of safeguarding the party's basic line and to resolutely accomplish the mission entrusted by the CMC. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1130 GMT 31 Jan 94)

Chen Xitong Meets NPC Deputies

Some National People's Congress (NPC) deputies from Beijing and Tianjin concluded their inspection of Beijing on 28 January. Chen Xitong, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and secretary of the Beijing municipal party committee, and relevant responsible persons of Beijing municipality held a meeting with the above- mentioned NPC deputies in the afternoon of 28 January and listened to their views and suggestions on Beijing's economic and social development. (Beijing Central People's Radio Network in Mandarin 1030 GMT 28 Jan 94)

Song Jian Visits Beijing Zoo

Accompanied by Hou Jie, construction minister; Xie Zhenhua, State Environmental Protection Bureau director; and relevant leaders of Beijing municipality, State Councillor Song Jian visited the Beijing Zoo in the morning of 3 February to wish some 3,500 zoo personnel a happy Chinese New Year. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1227 GMT 3 Feb 94)

Wu Xueqian at CPPCC Spring Festival Party

The national committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC] hosted a Spring Festival tea party in Beijing on 2 February for widows of deceased members of the CPPCC National Committee who were not CPC members and for the wives of some celebrities. Cheng Siyuan, vice chairman of the National People's Congress [NPC] Standing Committee; Ye Xuanping, Wu Xueqian, and Qian Zhengying, vice chairmen of the CPPCC national committee; and leading comrades of the NPC Standing Committee General Office, State Council General Office, CPC Central Committee United Front Work Department, and State Council's Government Offices Administration Bureau happily gathered with more than 300 ladies to celebrate the coming Spring Festival. Qian Zhengying delivered a speech on behalf of the CPPCC national committee, calling for concerted efforts to further promote China's reform, opening up, and socialist modernization in 1994. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0719 GMT 2 Feb 94)

Zou Jiahua Meets BASF Chairman

China's Yangzi Petrochemical Company and Germany's BASF Company signed agreements in Beijing on three petrochemical joint ventures, including a polystyrene project, on 3 February. State Council Vice Premier Zou Jiahua met with the BASF chairman and his party. Zou Jiahua said: "The Chinese Government welcomes investments by big foreign companies in setting up plants in China. The large petrochemical joint ventures between Germany's BASF and China's Yangzi Petrochemical Company will serve to promote economic cooperation between the two countries as well as the development of China's petrochemical industry." President Sheng Huaren, of the China National Petrochemical Corporation [CNPC], presided over the 3 February agreement-signing ceremony attended by responsible persons of relevant ministries and State Council commissions, the CNPC, Jiangsu Province, and Nanjing city. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1243 GMT 3 Feb 94)

Wu Bangguo, Huang Ju Visit Servicemen

On 1 February, Wu Bangguo, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and secretary of the Shanghai municipal party committee [SMPC]; Huang Ju, deputy secretary of the SMPC and Shanghai mayor; and Chen Zhili, Wang Liping, Chen Liangyu, Chen Tiedi, and other leaders of the SMPC, Shanghai Municipal People's Congress, Shanghai municipal government, Shanghai Municipal Chinese People's Consultative Conference, and Shanghai Municipal Discipline Inspection Commission separately called on a coastal defense brigade of the Shanghai Garrison, units of the Navy and Air Force stationed in Shanghai, an army hospital, an Air Force political academy, and armed police detachments in Shanghai to convey warm regards to officers and men and to wish them a happy Chinese New Year. Shanghai actors and actresses accompanied the above-mentioned leaders in their Spring Festival calls and staged brilliant performances to entertain the officers and men. (Shanghai People's Radio Network in Mandarin 1000 GMT 1 Feb 94)

Wang Hanbin Awards Bridge Players

A bridge tournament jointly sponsored by the Bridge Association of China [BAC] and the Cankun Group was held at the Great Hall of the People on 31 January. Wang Hanbin, adviser of the BAC; He Guangyuan, director of the CPC Central Committee and state organs Bridge Association; and Wu Cankun, president of the Cankun Group, gave awards to winners. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1443 GMT 31 Jan 94)
