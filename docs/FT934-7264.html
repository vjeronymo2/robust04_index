931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Dwyer makes Pounds 34.4m buys and seeks Pounds 12.3m DWYER, the property company, is acquiring seven properties for a maximum of Pounds 34.4m cash and raising Pounds 12.3m net through a 1-for-1 rights issue. The company also announced pre-tax losses for the year to September 30 of Pounds 7.74m, against Pounds 7.13m restated for FRS 3. Operating profits were Pounds 3.28m (Pounds 3.14m) but there was a fall of Pounds 4.24m (Pounds 4.78m) in property values and a loss of Pounds 3.23m (Pounds 4,000) on the sale of properties. The company is also considering moving from the Irish Republic to the UK. Dwyer is buying the Arndale Centre in Middleton, Manchester from P&O Property Holdings and a portfolio from Winglaw Developments, which holds 24.1 per cent of Dwyer. It is issuing 25.7m shares at 50p. Winglaw has undertaken to take up its rights and the balance is being underwritten by UBS, the broker to the issue. The shares closed unchanged at 57p. For the year to September 30 losses per share were 30.18p (25.47p). The rise in operating profits was the result of cutting administrative expenses to Pounds 1.08m (Pounds 1.88m). During the period 12 properties were sold realising Pounds 35.5m and cutting gearing from 206 per cent to 51 per cent at the period end.
