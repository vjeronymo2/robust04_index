Language: English Article Type:BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 457] [Text] Vilnius, March 15, ELTA -- Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas talked on the phone with Polish President Lech Walesa on Tuesday [15 March]. Both the presidents expressed satisfaction about the development of Lithuanian-Polish relations. They emphasized that after Lithuania and Poland signs the agreement, the relations between the countries will be expanded in all spheres. Walesa accepted the invitation of Brazauskas to visit Lithuania. The date of the visit will be fixed later.
