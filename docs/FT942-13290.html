940426 FT 26 APR 94 / Report backs new qualifications New competence-based qualifications can act as a device for motivating staff at various levels in the financial services industries according to a report published today. The report, which includes case studies, was prepared by the Financial Services Lead Bodies Group, which was partly responsible for developing National Vocational Qualifications for its sector. Ms Liz Chiswell, one of the authors of the case studies, said a number of teething problems with NVQs remained, but there were business benefits to be had from the qualifications. Towards the Business Case. NVQs/SVQs. Published by Create, Holly Hill, Vauxhall Lane, Southborough, Kent TN4 0XD. Pounds 11.50.
