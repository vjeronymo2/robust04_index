BFN [Text] Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat praised the fraternal, continuous, and firm support of the Egyptian Government and people under the leadership of President Mubarak for the Palestinian people's just cause and national struggle to effect a durable and just peace in Palestine and the region. In a congratulatory message to President Mubarak on the 12th anniversary of Sinai's liberation, 'Arafat praised Egypt's support for the Palestinian people's struggle to end Israeli occupation, preserve their holy places, and retrieve and exercise their inalienable national rights.
