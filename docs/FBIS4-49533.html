BFN [Text] Bucharest, May 6 (XINHUA) -- Romanian President Ion Iliescu said today that his country wishes to expand cooperations with China in political, economic and military spheres. Iliescu made the remark during a meeting with the chief of staff of the People's Liberation Army of China, Zhang Wannian. Iliescu also said that his tour of the southern Chinese province of Guangdong earlier this year has left him with very deep impression. He said Romania would like to learn from China's experience in economic development.
