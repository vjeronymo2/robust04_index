September 9, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL ROUNDUP;

GRECO PROPELS CRESPI TO VICTORY IN WATER POLO

Junior Vince Greco scored four goals to lead Crespi High to a 9-5 victory over host Montebello in a quarterfinal match of the Bell Gardens water polo tournament Friday.

Crespi built a 5-2 lead in the second quarter and was never threatened. Junior Tom Kretzin added two goals and junior goalie Tim Behrens had nine saves for the Celts.

"There are a lot of juniors on this team," Crespi Coach Jeff Thornton said. "And we've got a pretty good team here."

GIRLS' TENNIS

Hart 12, La Canada 6 -- Kristin Gildersleeve and Laura Eckerman swept their doubles matches for Hart in a season-opening nonleague match at home.

In singles play, Hart's Karyne Ghantous and Cindy Courtemarsh each won two of three sets.

GIRLS' VOLLEYBALL

Burroughs d. La Canada, 15-8, 15-3, 15-10 -- Middle blocker Jennifer Wagner had 10 kills for Burroughs in the opener for both schools at Burroughs.

Teammate Jeannie Russell, a setter, added 19 service points.
