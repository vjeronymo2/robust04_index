Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Nigerian Government has restated its commitment to a policy of good neighborliness with [break in reception]. Brigadier General Fred Chijuka, who stated this, said Nigeria was not an aggressor. The country only carried out a routine relocation of troops on its eastern border, a measure that was not designed to embarrass any of its neighbors. He said that if there was any misunderstanding between Nigeria and Cameroon, the issue was already being resolved at the diplomatic level. The troops' relocation, according to him, was normal and aimed at enhancing the safety of its citizens. The Nigerian foreign minister, Mr. Babagana Kingibe, visited Cameroon last week on the border issue after which both countries agreed on a joint patrol to stem the clashes.
