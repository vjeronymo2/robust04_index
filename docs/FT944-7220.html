941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Osborne & Little rises 60% Continuing recovery in the UK coupled with a 28 per cent rise in US sales enabled Osborne & Little, the wallpaper and furnishing fabrics group, to hoist pre-tax profits by 60 per cent from Pounds 947,000 to Pounds 1.51m in the six months to September 30. With turnover up 28 per cent at Pounds 11.7m (Pounds 9.1m) continued cost controls helped operating margins rise 2 percentage points to 12.8 per cent. Earnings were 15.15p (9.33p) and the interim dividend is raised to 3.5p (2.5p). Sir Peter Osborne, chairman, said the second half continued to perform well. Although the company would incur the initial costs of setting up its French operation and an additional UK warehouse, he was 'confident of an excellent outcome for the year.'
