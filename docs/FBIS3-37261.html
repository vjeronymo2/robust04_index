Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, 12/02/94 ("LRAGIR" daily) -- RA [Republic of Armenia] President Levon Ter-Petrosyan visited the European Bank of Reconstruction and Development on February 10 and discussed the problems of financial support to Armenia with the President of the bank Jack Delarosier. They particularly concerned the problem of reconstruction of the Armenian Nuclear Power Plant and creation of privatization funds in the Republic of Armenia. A group of specialists is being formed in order to visit Armenia soon and to investigate the conditions at the Nuclear Power Plant, to prepare practical measures for setting the second section of the plant in action. An agreement was reached on financing the reconstruction of the cargo transportation section of the Yerevan international airport.
