BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 26 (KCNA) -- Han Tok-su, chairman of the Central Standing Committee of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon), and his entourage arrived here today for a visit to the socialist homeland. They were met at the airport by Vice President Pak Song-chol, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Kim Yong-sun and officials concerned. Yi Chin-kyu, first vice-chairman of the Chongnyon Central Standing Committee, now staying in the socialist homeland, was present at the airport.
