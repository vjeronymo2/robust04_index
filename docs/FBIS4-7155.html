BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 26 (KCNA) -- Senior party and government officials paid calls at units of the Korean People's Army [KPA] and the Korean People's Security Forces [KPSF] on the 62nd anniversary of the founding of the heroic Korean People's Army. Visiting the units of three services of the KPA and the KPSF were senior party and government officials including Premier Kang Song-san and secretaries of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea Kye Ung-tae, Chon Pyong-ho and Han Song-yong, who are members of the Political Bureau of the party Central Committee, chief officials of the commissions and ministries of the Administration Council and central organs and chief officials of the local party and government bodies and administrative and economic organs. The senior party and government officials conveyed to the units gifts sent by the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, chairman of the National Defence Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army. Touched by the boundless love and care shown by Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il, the soldiers renewed their loyal pledge to become guns and bombs defending the party and the leader politically and ideologically and with their lives, and defend the sky, the land and the sea of the socialist motherland as an impregnable fortress. Before seeing the combat and political training of the soldiers and their life, the visitors heard about the history of the units that had covered a road of victory and glory under the tested guidance of Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, holding the great leader President Kim Il-song in high esteem. People's delegations attended colourful functions held at the units of the three services of the KPA and units of the KPSF on the Army Day, deepening the feelings between the Army and the people. With the delegations present, meetings were held at the units to convey gifts from Comrade Kim Chong-il. On the Army Day, more than 20 art groups comprising Pyongyang and local artistes gave performances at the units of the three services of the KPA to warmly congratulate them. On April 25, an evening of men and officers of the Ministry of the People's Armed Forces was held at the Jonsung Square in Pyongyang.
