Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Mammoth popular demonstrations were staged in al-Rasafah, Baghdad, today, through which the masses expressed their full support for the triumphant leader, President Saddam Husayn, until final victory is achieved and the unjust blockade is lifted. The demonstrators were received by Comrade 'Abd-al-Ghani 'Abd-al- Ghafur, member of the Iraq Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party and official in charge of the Ba'th party's organizations in al- Rasafah. During the mammoth demonstrations the slogan of "The people are with the leader to seize our rights to lift the unjust blockade" was raised. The masses of Saddam city renewed full allegiance to the builder of Iraq's great glory and the leader of the march of righteous victory, leader President Saddam Husayn. They stressed that they will continue to be loyal soldiers under the Allahu Akbar [God is great] banner raised by his excellency, and expressed their readiness to do their utmost to break the unjust blockade in defense of great Iraq, its sovereignty, and dignity. Comrade 'Abd-al-Ghani 'Abd-al-Ghafur, member of the Iraq Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party and official in charge of the Ba'th party's organizations in al-Rasafah, stressed that leader President Saddam Husayn's address to the prime minister and ministers was an expression of the conscience of the patient and faithful people who are facing the conspiracy of the unjust blockade with strong faith and consciences. In a statement to INA, he said: Iraq's masses and party, popular, and trade unionist organizations express their full support for what was included in struggler President Saddam Husayn's address in order to sieze our mujahid people's rights and make the UN Security Council lift the unjust blockade after Iraq has fulfilled its obligations toward the UN Security Council resolutions, despite the fact that they are unjust and unfair resolutions. Comrade 'Abd-al-Ghani 'Abd-al-Ghafur noted that the continuation of the unjust blockade under pressure from the United States and Zionism means the continuation of plotting against our Iraq, its present, and the future of its generations. He stressed that the Iraqi people, under the leadership of President Saddam Husayn, will continue to set an example for Arab and world masses in their confrontation of current injustice and tyranny in the world in light of the so-called new world order, proceeding from the historical fact that the Mother of battles was a nationalist, pan-Arab, and humanitarian battle.
