May 15, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: HUNTINGTON BEACH;

FIRE DAMAGE TO HOME ESTIMATED AT $90,000

Fire of undetermined cause did about $90,000 damage Sunday night to a single-family residence at 5171 Gelding Circle, the Huntington Beach Fire Department reported. No one was home at the time of the fire, and no firefighters were injured in quickly extinguishing the blaze, Fire Capt. Mike McKay said.

McKay said the fire was reported at 6:12 p.m. Sunday. Firefighters found most of the blaze already dead for lack of oxygen in the smoke-filled house when they arrived. He said the remainder of the fire was under control within 5 minutes.

The fire appeared to have started in either the laundry or kitchen of the single-story, wood-stucco home, McKay said. He estimated $70,000 damage to the structure and $20,000 to the contents.
