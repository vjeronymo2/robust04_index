Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, February 14 (XINHUA) -- The "B"-share warrant for the Shanghai Chlorine-Alkali Chemical Industrial Co. Ltd. was listed today on the Shanghai Securities Exchange. This is the first "B"-share warrant listed there. The opening quotation of the warrant on the exchange was 0.01 U.S. dollar per share while the highest amounted to 0.012 U.S. dollar. At the close it dropped to 0.01 U.S. dollar again. A total of 4,016 lots of the warrant of the company was traded on the stock market today. Meanwhile, the opening quotation of the "B"-shares of the company was 0.28 U.S. dollars and the closing quotation of the "B"-shares was 0.25 U.S. dollars. A total of 110,000 shares changed hands on the stock market today. The "B"-share warrant of the chlorine-alkali company will be traded on the market from today through to February 18. Each warrant has 100 parts, with each part giving the right to buy 0.4 "B"-shares of the company. After paying for the warrant, investors can sell their bought shares on the Shanghai stock market as of March 14.
