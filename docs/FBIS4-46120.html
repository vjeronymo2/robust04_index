BFN [Text] Last night, an IDF [Israel Defense Forces] force operating deep in Lebanon's al-Biqa' region captured and brought to Israel Mustafa al-Dirani, the head of a radical terrorist organization which took navigator Ron Arad captive in 1986. Al- Dirani heads an organization called the Believer's Resistance. An IDF spokesman reported that all our forces returned safely to base. A REUTERS reporter in al-Biqa' reported that an IDF force flew by helicopter to al-Dirani's village of Qasr al-Nabah), which is located 10 kilometers from Zahlah. The commando force kidnapped al- Dirani from his home. IDF representatives have informed the Arad family about the operation.
