September 21, 1989, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

FOR THE RECORD;

DEUKMEJIAN SIGNED JUDGES BILL IN JULY

The Times incorrectly reported Wednesday that legislation increasing the insurance and retirement benefits for San Diego County judges was signed by Gov. George Deukmejian last week. In fact, the bill, sponsored by Sen. Larry Stirling (R-San Diego), was signed into law July 13 and became effective immediately. Deukmejian on Tuesday announced that he had appointed Stirling as a San Diego Municipal Court judge, a move that will become effective with Stirling's resignation from the state Senate. #YES#
