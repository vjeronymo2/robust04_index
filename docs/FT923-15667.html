920701 FT 01 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Umeco shows sharp decline to Pounds 312,000 Profits of Umeco, the USM-quoted manufacturer of aerospace equipment, fell from Pounds 701,000 to Pounds 312,000 pre-tax for the year ended March 31. Turnover was 28 per cent lower at Pounds 9.95m. Earnings fell to 3.3p (9p) and a final dividend of 1p cuts the total to 1.075p (3.895p).
