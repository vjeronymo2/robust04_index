BFN [Text] Yasir 'Arafat, president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO, today received IMF's delegation, headed by (Dr. George al-'Abid). During the meeting, they discussed a number of important issues, most importantly IMF's aid to the present Palestinian authority and the agreed projects that would bolster Palestinian national authority and strengthen the implementation of the first phase of the peace plan. They also discussed issues of common interest in the Palestinian self-rule territories. The meeting was an opportunity to review the peace process and the means of making it a success.
