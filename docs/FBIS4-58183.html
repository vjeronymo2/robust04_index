BFN [Text] Damascus, 1 May (SANA)--At 1845 today, President Hafiz al-Asad received U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher and his delegation. The delegation includes Dennis Ross, special Middle East peace process coordinator; Christopher Ross, U.S. ambassador in Damascus; and Martin Indyk, special assistant to the U.S. President. The meeting was attended by Faruq al-Shar', the foreign minister; Muwaffaq al-'Allaf, head of the Syrian delegation to the peace talks; and Walid al-Mu'allim, Syria's ambassador in Washington.
