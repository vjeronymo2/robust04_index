940425 FT 25 APR 94 / Company news this week: Shopping list issues for food group -Other Companies Associated British Foods is today expected to show a small advance in interim profits from Pounds 161m to about Pounds 167m, before tax and exceptionals. Investors will look for indications on plans for ABF's Pounds 500m cash pile, and for clarification of disposal rumours surrounding Bakers Oven.
