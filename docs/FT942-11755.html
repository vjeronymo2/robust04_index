940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Charles Letts shows Pounds 785,000 profit In its first results since being bought by its management, Charles Letts Group, the diary maker which has undergone refinancing and restructuring, announced a pre-tax profit of Pounds 785,000 for the year to January 31 1994. The outcome compared with a restated loss of Pounds 7.51m. Turnover was Pounds 26.6m (Pounds 29.9m).
