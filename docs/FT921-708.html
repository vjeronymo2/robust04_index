920327 FT 27 MAR 92 / Election 1992: Labour's Scots lift A SYSTEM Three poll in The Herald in Glasgow today shows a six-point surge in Labour support in Scotland to 44 per cent. The Nationalists are 18 points behind on 26 per cent (24 last time). The Tories, at 22 per cent, show no change, while the Liberal Democrats dropped to 7 per cent (10).
