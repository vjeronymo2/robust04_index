Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Madrid, March 9 (XINHUA) -- The Bank of China and the banking consortium spearheaded by the central American Bank of Spain signed here today an agreement of credit loan totalling 320 million U.S. dollars. The largest-ever credit loan between China and Spain will be used by Henan Province to buy Spainish generator sets for its "Duck River" power plant project. This accord is believed to play an active role in the promotion of the two countries' economic and trade relations.
