920107 FT 07 JAN 92 / MPs on mission to Libya A British politician and an Italian member of the European parliament have begun a fact-finding mission to Libya at the government's invitation to discuss the 1988 bombing of the Pan Am airliner over Lockerbie, Reuter reports from Tunis. A British opposition Labour party MP, Mr Bernie Grant, travelling with Italian Euro-MP Darcia Valent, said that he wanted 'to make sure that the Libyan government position is accurately taken back to the British parliament'.
