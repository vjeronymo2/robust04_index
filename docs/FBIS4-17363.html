BFN [INTERFAX report: "State To Redeem 1990 Loans"] [Text] The Russian Government intends to redeem the state's debt to holders of 1990 interest-free targeted state loans between 15 June 1994 and 1 June 1995. According to the government decree, the Finance Ministry is being instructed to redeem the population's interest-free targeted bonds for consumer goods (excluding cars) "at current consumer prices" and for cars "on the basis of 60 percent of the price." In addition, the Russian Federation Finance Ministry will redeem targeted checks for the acquisition of cars issued prior to 2 January 1992 with right of purchase in 1992 and "Harvest-90" checks "at the rate of 70 times the face value of the check with the transfer of funds into bank accounts."
