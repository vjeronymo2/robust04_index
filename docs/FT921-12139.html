920128 FT 28 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Stavert Zigomala Stavert Zigomala, an investment holding company which also retails furniture and carpets, reported pre-tax profits reduced to Pounds 23,623 for the half year to end-September. The decline from last time's Pounds 33,567 came on turnover of Pounds 384,347 (Pounds 372,678). Earnings emerged at 5.978p (8.232p) per ordinary stock unit and at 4.655p (6.697p) per deferred stock unit.
