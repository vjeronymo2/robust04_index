Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev, 20 January -- An official meeting between the defense ministers of Ukraine and the Republic of Uzbekistan was held at the Ukrainian Defense Ministry. Agreement on this meeting had been reached during the Ukrainian president's stay in Ashgabat at the end of last year. As early as in 1992 the two countries' military departments concluded the Agreement on Cooperation. Today Uzbek Defense Minister Lieutenant General Rustam Akhmedov and Ukrainian Defense Minister Army General Vitaliy Radetskyy signed the bilateral Agreement on Military Cooperation. The participants in the meeting expressed certainty that friendly Ukrainian-Uzbek relations would develop further, which would also be helped by cooperation in the sphere of the defense complex. On behalf of the Ukrainian president, Defense Minister Vitaliy Radetskyy presented Defense Minister Rustam Akhmedov with a personal watch.
