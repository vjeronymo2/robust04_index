August 5, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

BERNSON DESERVES PRAISE

Councilman Hal Bernson should be praised for his efforts to reopen schools in his district while building a school in Porter Ranch at no cost to us taxpayers.

I agree that the school board is "totally out of control."

Where is Kornstein, our so-called representative? She is busy purchasing the Ambassador Hotel and transporting our children on buses for year-round education, and at the same time cutting our educators' salary.

I feel we should rally behind Bernson since he understands our district, which has grown and prospered during his term in office.

RAE BERKOWITZ

Sepulveda
