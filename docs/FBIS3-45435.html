Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Jinan, January 15 (XINHUA) -- China is building its largest iron and steel production base in Shandong Province. According to an agreement signed here yesterday, the Shandong Provincial Government is joining hands with Beijing's Capital Iron and Steel Complex to build the Qilu Iron and Steel Complex. A local official in charge of the project said 50 billion yuan will be required to construct the first phase, scheduled to have an annual production capacity of five million tons of steel. After the second construction phase is completed the complex will have a designed capacity of 10 million tons of steel a year, making it China's largest iron and steel production base. The official noted that the project is being built without state investment. It will practise a share-holding system.
