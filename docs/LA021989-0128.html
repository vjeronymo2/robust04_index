February 19, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION GIRLS' BASKETBALL PLAYOFFS;

WILSON GATHERS 22 POINTS AS HART ADVANCES ON HISTORIC EVENING, 62-35

The Hart High girls' basketball team, ranked second in the Southern Section 5-A Division, won its first-ever playoff game, 62-35, Saturday night in a second-round match-up over visiting Norco.

Six-foot-three-inch forward Sara Wilson scored a game-high 22 points, 14 in the second half, and pulled down 12 rebounds for Hart, which recorded its 12th consecutive victory.

The Indians led, 26-17, at intermission and extended their advantage to 41-22 with 1:31 remaining in the third quarter by posting a 15-5 run, during which Wilson contributed eight points.

Hart widened the margin again to 57-28 in the final period, erupting for a 12-4 run, sparked by Wilson and guard Anjanette Dione, who each scored four points. Dione finished with 16 points.
