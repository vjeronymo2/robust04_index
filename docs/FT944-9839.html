941114 FT 14 NOV 94 / Business Travel: Flight of peace A new Irish air route opens on December 2 in the first big cross-border push for travel business since the IRA ceasefire. Flights between Belfast and Kerry will be a 'concrete example of the peace initiative at work', says Go Ireland, the company promoting the service. It said: 'We would hope this is the first of many such flights, opening new markets north and south of the border.'
