April 29, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

FBI ARRESTS COUPLE WHO ALLEGEDLY FLED SEPARATE DRUG TRIALS

FBI agents Saturday arrested a man charged in Dallas with fleeing from a cocaine indictment.

Agents said they arrested Kevin Pressley Johnson, 23, of Baltimore without incident after he was seen driving near La Palma and Knott avenues.

Johnson was indicted last July in Dallas for possessing cocaine for delivery, agents said. A federal warrant was issued after it appeared that he had fled the state.

Agents also arrested Sarah Dyer, 30, of Buena Park, Johnson's companion.

She is wanted on a Los Angeles County warrant for failure to appear in court on a drug charge and was turned over to Buena Park police, agents said.
