BFN [Text] An Arab League delegation led by brother Major General Muhammad Sa'id Bayraqdar, Arab League assistant secretary general for military affairs, left Sanaa today following a visit to our country. During the visit, Maj. Gen. Bayraqdar conveyed a message to brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council and supreme commander of the Armed Forces, from Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid. In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', brother Bayraqdar explained that the brother Presidential Council chairman yesterday briefed the delegation on the Yemeni situation and on the great opportunities and concessions given to the [Yemeni] Socialist Party [YSP] in order to safeguard Yemeni unity. However, none of these achieved any result, Salih added. Thus, the possibility of cease-fire is ruled out unless the YSP elements which rebelled against legitimacy and which are responsible for these events either leave the country or surrender to legitimacy. Bayraqdar pointed out that the brother Presidential Council chairman and other officials in Sanaa welcomed the role the Arab League is playing. The delegation was seen off by brother [word indistinct] [? Sami], deputy head of the Arab Homeland Department at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs; a representative of the Presidential Council Protocol Office; and brother 'Ata Harun, Egyptian ambassador in Sanaa.
