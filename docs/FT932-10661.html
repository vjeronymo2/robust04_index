930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Maple Leaf Inns acquires 20 pubs Maple Leaf Inns, a joint venture company launched last year by Pubmaster, the independent pub retailer, and Labatt, the Canadian brewer, yesterday announced that it had bought 20 pubs - 15 from Scottish & Newcastle and five reposessed pubs from Allied Irish Bank - to increase its estate to 179 pubs nationally. Mr John Brackenbury, chairman, said that the company, which has already doubled in size this year, planned further acquisitions.
