910417 FT 17 APR 91 / Carat wins Dollars 100m Disney account Carat, Europe's largest media planning and buying company, has won the Walt Disney Company's European account worth up to Dollars 100m a year, writes Raymond Snoddy. The media programme is expected to cover the entire Disney business in both western and eastern Europe including films and home videos, consumer products and the Euro Disney Resort which is due to open in a year's time outside Paris.
