March 28, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Mac's Back: Fleetwood Mac's first album of new material in three years, "Behind the Mask," will be out April 10 from Warner Bros. The band is now on tour in Australia; a U.S. tour is set for May. Current band members include Mick Fleetwood, Billy Burnette, Christine McVie, John McVie, Stevie Nicks and Rick Vito. BETH KLEID
