Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] 'Allamah Muhammad Husayn Fadlallah has demanded that the perpetrators of the Zuq Mikha'il church massacre be tracked down because this crime is not a Christian or Muslim problem. It is a Lebanese-Arab problem. Fadlallah believes that this is a crucial stage and that the situation in the region will be moving toward further mix up. He affirmed that any shake-up in the Arab reality will have echoes in Lebanon.
