940208 FT 08 FEB 94 / International Company News: Barlo purchase and placing Barlo Group, the Dublin-based radiator and plastics maker, is to acquire the radiator manufacturing business of Kingspan Veha, located in Wicklow, for IPounds 4m (Pounds 3.8m). At the same time, Barlo is calling for IPounds 6.45m net via a placing of 7.5m new shares with Irish and UK institutional investors at 88p per share.
