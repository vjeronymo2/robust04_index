941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Manakin Manakin Holdings, the investment trust which has been in members' voluntary liquidation since 1991, had a net asset value of 47p per share at October 15, against 82p a year earlier. Capital distributions of 38p were made during the year making a total returned to date of 380p, at a cost of Pounds 77.9m. After-tax loss for the year was Pounds 557,102 (Pounds 435,445) and deficit per share came to 2.7p (2.1p).
