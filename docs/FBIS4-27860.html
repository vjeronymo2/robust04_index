BFN [Text] According to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on 5 June, Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu arrived in London, Great Britain, on the afternoon of 4 June, local time. There he had individual and three-way telephone talks with U.S. Secretary of State Christopher and Hurd, British secretary of state for foreign and Commonwealth affairs, to discuss North Korea's nuclear issue. Secretary Christopher was attending a U.S.-British foreign ministers meeting there.
