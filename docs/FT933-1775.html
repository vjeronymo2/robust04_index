930922 FT 22 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Dawson cuts jobs in Scotland Dawson International, the textile group that manufactures in the UK and the US, is to cut jobs in Scotland and close a plant in Philadelphia in the US. The cuts will cost Pounds 2.5m and are aimed at reducing the group's cost base in the face of weak trading conditions. Jobs are to go at plants in Hawick and Kinross in Scotland.
