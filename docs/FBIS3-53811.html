Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In coordination with the State Security Detective Agency and the Public Security Department, Cairo detectives have arrested 244 extremists in the al-Khankah and Cairo districts. Acting on instructions from Major General Ahmad al-'Adili, director of the State Security Detectives Agency; Maj. Gen. Nassar Zahir, director of the Public Security Department; and Maj. Gen. Mansur al-'Isawi, director of the Cairo Security Department to purge and strike terrorist and extremist hideouts in the slum areas, an intense security campaign was launched in eastern Cairo areas and in 'Izbat al-Nakhl, al-Marj, and al-Khankah districts. Some 244 fugitive terrorists were arrested as a result.
