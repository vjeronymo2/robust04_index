Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 16 (KCNA) -- A large number of silicate brick dwelling houses have been built in Korea. The construction of silicate dwelling houses for sixty thousand and several thousand families was undertaken and more than fifty thousand flats have been completed in the recent few years. As a result, urban-style silicate dwelling houses were built in-more than 100 districts and blocks, including Kangan-tong in Songyo District, Pyongyang, and Namsinuiju, and the number of silicate dwelling houses increased in rural districts. More than 18,000 families moved into new silicate houses built in Pyongyang including Kwangbok Street, and thousands of families in Namsinuiju. Silicate dwelling houses for ten thousand and several thousand families have been built in Sunchon, Pukchang, Anju and other coal mining districts.
