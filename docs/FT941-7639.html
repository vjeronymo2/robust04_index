940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / Camdessus visits Algeria Mr Michel Camdessus, the managing director of the International Monetary Fund, arrived in Algiers yesterday on a two-day visit, writes Francis Ghiles. Agreement with the IMF on a package of economic reforms is essential for Algerian leaders who are desperate to loosen the burden of foreign debt repayments which, in 1994, will absorb all the country's oil and gas receipts.
