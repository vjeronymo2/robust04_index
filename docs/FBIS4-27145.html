BFN [By correspondent Nam Son-hyon from the UN Headquarters] [Text] UN sources have said that the UN Security Council [UNSC] is preparing to adopt a resolution or a statement urging North Korea to immediately stop the extraction of fuel rods. In a behind-closed-doors plenary session held last night, the UNSC held an emergency discussion on North Korea's nuclear issue. It was reported that the UNSC reached an agreement to actively cope with North Korea's nuclear issue. The permanent members of the UNSC are actively engaged in fine- tuning their opinions over how to take measures on North Korea at the moment, but that China is maintaining a prudent stance.
