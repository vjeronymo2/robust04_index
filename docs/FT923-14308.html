920708 FT 08 JUL 92 / Battersea Leisure dispute settled WANDSWORTH council in south London yesterday reached an out-of-court agreement with Battersea Leisure, the company which faces financial difficulties over redeveloping Battersea power station. No details were given. The dispute had been over Pounds 173,664 allegedly owed in building control fees. Battersea Leisure said it had agreed to the settlement against legal advice because it allowed time to complete refinancing negotiations.
