Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A blue print for the disarmament and encampment of warring factions in Liberia is being worked out by ECOMOG [Economic Community of West African States Cease-Fire Monitoring Group] and the United Nations. The ECOMOG field commander, Major General John Inienger, made this known in Monrovia when the ambassadors of Egypt, Guinea, and Sierra Leone, paid him a courtesy visit. Maj. Gen. Inienger said that a disarmament committee had been set up to achieve the objectives of the peace process. He appealed to Egypt, Guinea, and Sierra Leone to assist [words indistinct] so that the disarmament would commence with the necessary logistic support.
