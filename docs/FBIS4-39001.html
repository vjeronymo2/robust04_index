BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow June 6 -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin issued a decree on Monday reforming the former Russian Management Academy into the Russian State Service Academy, directly under the president of the country. The academy will serve as an educational, research, and analytical centre devoted to problems of civil servants, the president's press service reported on Monday. Yeltsin named Aleksey Mikhaylovich Yemelyanov as dean/president of the academy.
