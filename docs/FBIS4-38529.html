BFN [Valentin Zhdanko report from Minsk: "Vyacheslav Kebich Has Lost His Main Rival"] [Text] On 31 May the Central Election Commission registered the following as Belorussia's [Belarus] official presidential candidates: Premier Vyacheslaw Kebich, ex-speaker Stanislaw Shushkevich, Belorussian National Front leader Zenon Poznyak, deputy Aleksandr Lukashenko, Communist Party leader Vasiliy Novikov, and kolkhoz chairman Aleksandr Dubko. Gennadiy Karpenko, leader of the Party of National Accord, who was considered one of Kebich's main contenders in the elections, did not make it into the candidates' list. Karpenko had polled the required number of signatures but at the last moment 16 deputies unexpectedly recalled their signatures. Yesterday, without waiting for a Central Election Commission decision, Gennadiy Karpenko withdrew his candidacy.
