BFN [Text] The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] has captured three out of the four terrorists who fired shots this morning at a patrol near IDF headquarters in Gaza city. One soldier sustained light to moderate wounds in the attack while a second soldier was lightly wounded. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the four terrorists arrived in a car. The soldiers returned fire, wounding one man, who managed to escape. The other terrorists were captured. A Kalashnikov rifle used by the terrorists was found at the scene.
