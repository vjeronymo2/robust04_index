BFN [Text] The participants in the All-Union Congress of the Union of Officers held in Moscow's suburbs have decided to ask the Russian military leadership to cancel the joint Russian-American military exercises planned for the summer of this year. The Union of Officers regards assisting and participating in the maneuvers as treason. The Congress passed a resolution assessing the recent air strikes against Bosnia as preparing a bridgehead for the mounting of aggression against Russia and other Slav states. In this connection the delegates demanded an immediate convocation of the UN Security Council to end the escalation of the violence and genocide of the Serbian people. This was reported by the Interfax agency.
