940608 FT 08 JUN 94 / Chilean trade surplus grows Chile posted a Dollars 95.2m (Pounds 63m) trade surplus in the first two weeks of May, bringing this year's accumulated surplus to Dollars 334.2m, the Central Bank reported yesterday, AP reports from Santiago. Last year, Chile posted a Dollars 62.2m deficit from January to mid-May. Government officials attributed the unexpected continued surplus mainly to recovery in world markets of the prices for some key Chilean export products, including copper, fruits and fish meal.
