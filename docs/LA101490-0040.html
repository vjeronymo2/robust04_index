October 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

GUBERNATORIAL CANDIDATES DEBATE ISSUES

Two profiles in courage:

Sen. Paul Simon of Illinois is locked in a fierce reelection fight against a female candidate.

Both are pro-choice. Yet he took a stand which might cost him votes and affirmed Judge David Souter.

Wilson is locked in a fierce election battle against a female candidate. Both are pro-choice. Yet he failed to take a stand and abstained on the Souter vote.

Draw your own conclusion.

TERRY L. MALONE

Orange
