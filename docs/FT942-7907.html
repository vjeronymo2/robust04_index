940524 FT 24 MAY 94 / Ugandan fishermen finding gruesome catch Ugandan fisherman pile up some of the thousands of corpses which have floated downriver into Lake Victoria from the killing fields in neighbouring Rwanda. Residents claim as many as 50 bodies an hour are being washed up. Uganda appealed yesterday for international help to assist in collecting the corpses, which are then wrapped in white sheets and buried in mass graves. UN calls off Kigali visit, Page 4
