Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 30 (XINHUA) -- Chinese President Jiang Zemin met here today with a delegation from the U.S. Committee of 100, headed by the committee's governor and well-known architect I.M. Pei. Jiang extended a warm welcome to the delegation on their visit to China. The 20-plus delegation members arrived in Beijing Monday [28 March] at the invitation of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC). The Chinese president said that more exchanges between the committee and the CPPCC National Committee will be helpful toward promoting understanding between the peoples of China and the United States. During the meeting in Zhongnanhai, Pei and other guests voiced their suggestions on China's development. The delegation, during their stay in China, will exchange views on China's economic development, science and technology, education and culture with Chinese scholars and experts as well as government officials. Jiang said he hoped the guests would offer valuable suggestions on such issues.
