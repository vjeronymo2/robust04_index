BFN [Excerpt] The northern forces this morning resumed their shelling of the city of Aden and the suburb of Dar Sa'd and fired a number of Katyusha rockets and artillery shells. Six people were killed and 15 others were injured in the attacks. Maritime sources in Aden have reported that a trawler flying the Japanese flag has been hit by a shell in the port of Aden and that fire has been raging aboard the ship until 1000 this morning Kuwaiti time.
