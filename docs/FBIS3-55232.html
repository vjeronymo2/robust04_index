Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report] [Text] Prime Minister Vyacheslav Kebich has received managers of the Russian joint stock company Gazprom. The meeting was dedicated to the issues connected with gas deliveries from the Russian Federation to our Republic until the year of 2010. An appropriate agreement was signed, in accordance with which Belarus will receive 16 billion cubic meters of gas as early as next year. In the future, the amount will double. Prime Minister Kebich praised the Russian Government for help in resolving the problems of winter gas deliveries to our Republic. The Belarusian side's request to reduce gas prices was received with understanding.
