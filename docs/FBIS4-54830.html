BFN [Text] Sarajevo, May 4 (AFP) -- In a statement released in Bonn prior to news of the gunshots, German Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel described the despatch of the ambassador as "a further signal of our efforts to bring peace and stability to Bosnia-Herzegovina". An important aspect of the German embassy's work in Sarajevo will be the coordination of public and private humanitarian aid from Germany for the people of Bosnia-Herzegovina, Kinkel said. The government of Chancellor Helmut Kohl has also said it wants to help reconstruction work here as the war ends.
