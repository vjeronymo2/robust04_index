BFN [Text] The operations command of the Martyr al-Sadr group has issued a statement announcing its forces had attacked the headquarters of the Ba'thist 18th Brigade and the headquarters of the 215 Artillery Battalion based in the region east of Qal'at Salih and that it captured 90 soldiers. The statement also said that the attacking forces managed to release 55 prisoners who were being held at the headquarters. Another statement issued by the same command the day before yesterday noted that last week the Iraqi mujahidin attacked the headquarters of the 18th and 95th Brigades east of Qal'at Salih and started fires in these two headquarters. The statement said that the attacking forces killed at least 50 Ba'thist soldiers. Among them were the commander of the artillery battalion, named 'Aziz, and the intelligence officer, 'Ali Salman Habib. The statement added that our Mujahidin succeeded in setting fire to one of the oil wells in al-'Amarah.
