May 30, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

JAPANESE CAR EXPORTS SLIP 18%

Japan's automobile exports posted the largest decline in 15 years in April, dropping 18% to 430,216 units compared with the year-earlier period, the Japan Automobile Industry said today.

It was the biggest drop since March, 1975, when Japan's auto exports fell 21.4%.

The association said the decline was due to a large drop in exports to the United States, the largest automobile market for Japan. Export to the United States in April fell 38.2% from a year ago to 136,231 units, the association indicated.

Export of passenger cars declined 15.8% to 321,917 units while truck exports fell 24.9% to 104,968 units.
