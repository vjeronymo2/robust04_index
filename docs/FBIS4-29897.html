BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 18 (KCNA) -- Students in Taejon raided Taejon City and South Chungchong Provincial branches of the "Democratic Liberal Party" and staged a demonstration Friday, protesting against the fascist clique's crackdown on the South Korean Federation of General Student Councils [Hanchongnyon], according to a radio report from Seoul. They arrived there by three trucks and threw some 30 bottles of paint at the buildings.
