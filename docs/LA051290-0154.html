May 12, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

PREP ROUNDUP;

HUNTINGTON BEACH GAINS BOYS' TENNIS BERTH

Dave DiMarino and Bach Nguyen each won three sets to lead Huntington Beach High School to a 12-6 victory over El Toro in a boys' tennis wild-card match at El Toro. The victory earned the Oilers a berth in the Southern Section 4-A playoffs Tuesday against Loyola.

In 3-A wild-card tennis:

Fullerton 12, Loara 6 -- Cameron Lindee swept three singles matches to lead visiting Loara. Brian Brennan and Bobby Valayati of Fullerton swept their doubles matches.

In Academy League softball:

Liberty Christian 7, St. Margaret's 3 -- Melissa Tedisco was 3 for 4 with two runs batted in and Lisa Gray threw a one-hitter with 10 strikeouts as Liberty Christian (17-4, 8-0 in league) rallied from a 3-0 deficit to clinch the league title. St. Margaret's is 6-2 in league.
