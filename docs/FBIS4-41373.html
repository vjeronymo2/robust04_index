BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shchegoleva] [Text] Sochi, 23 Jun -- The question of cooperation between the peacekeeping forces of Russia and the Armed Forces of the Republic of Abkhazia was discussed today at a meeting between Russian Deputy Defense Minister Georgiy Kondratyev, who has arrived in Sukhumi, and Sultan Sosnoliyev, the defense minister of the republic of Abkhazia. An ITAR-TASS correspondent was told of this at the Abkhazian Ministry of Information and Press.
