BFN [Text] President Algirdas Brazauskas left for Bucharest today to attend a four-day conference organized on the initiative of Romanian President Ion Iliescu. The president is accompanied by Transport Minister Jonas Birziskis, Trade and Industry Minister Kazimieras Klimasauskas, and Ambassador Justas Paleckis, presidential adviser on foreign policy. The conference will discuss issues of regional security and economic cooperation of the European countries. Algirdas Brazauskas will deliver a report on economic reforms in Lithuania. He will have meetings with heads of delegations of some countries, and with representatives of the European Union and of NATO. The organizational committee has announced that about 60 delegations of international organizations and countries of Europe, the Mediterranean, and the Persian Gulf will take part in the conference.
