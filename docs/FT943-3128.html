940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Fleming High Inc Net revenue at Fleming High Income Investment Trust fell from Pounds 415,000 to Pounds 297,000 for the three months to July 31. Earnings per share were 0.92p, against 1.29p and the first interim dividend is unchanged at 1.1p. Net asset value per share was 98.9p at the end of the period compared with 99.4p a year earlier.
