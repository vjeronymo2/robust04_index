Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Bullentin No. 1 issued by the Defense Secretariat in Mexico City on 2 January; from an XEW "Special Report" -- read by announcer] [Text] In light of the events of 30 December 1993 in the town of San Miguel, followed by the events that took place in the predawn hours of 1 January 1994 in which groups of outlaws occupied Ocosingo, Altamirano, Las Margaritas, and San Cristobal de las Casas in Chiapas State, all military personnel assigned to the 7th Military Region were ordered to remain in their barracks. They were to await the results of possible talks led by the state government directed at getting the members of the offensive armed groups to return to lawfulness. At 0700 [1300 GMT] today, after leaving San Cristobal, unidentified troops surrounded and attacked the military camp housing the headquarters of the 31st Military Zone located approximately 10 km southeast of the aforementioned town. At 1330 [1930 GMT] the attacks by said armed groups against the military camp continue. The Federal Attorney General delegate was summoned and ordered to file a report on the events as soon as possible.
