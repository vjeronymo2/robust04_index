BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Boris Krivoshey] [Text] Moscow June 9 TASS -- Russia fully shares the intenational community's deep concern over continued violence in Rwanda which is aggravating humanitarian crisis in that country, Grigoriy Karasin, head of the Information and Press Department in the Russian Foreign Ministry, said at a briefing today. The Russian side is taking direct part in operations to provide humanitarian aid to Rwandan refugees in Tanzania, where a truck detachemnt from Russia is already working. The Russian trucks are transporting essential goods to refugee camps. Russia intends to increase its participation in this operation, and in the near future, in agreement with the U.N., intends to send additional 20 trucks and a group of specialists together with different kinds of equipment for this purpose, Grigoriy Karasin said.
