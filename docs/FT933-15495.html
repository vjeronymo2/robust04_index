930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / UK Company News: 43% surge for Leopold Joseph A strong performance in core activities enabled Leopold Joseph, the merchant banking group, to expand net profit by 43 per cent in the year ended March 31 1993. After transfer to inner reserve and tax, profit rose from Pounds 1.1m to Pounds 1.58m. Mr Robin Herbert, chairman, said banking and treasury had particularly good years, taking advantage of increased volumes of business. Earnings rose to 30p (20.9p). The dividend is lifted from 15.85p to 16.6p with a final of 13.5p.
