BFN [Text] The operative units of Russia's Interior Ministry troops will be increased by 52,000 men, said President Boris Yeltsin, addressing the personnel of the operative division of Russia's Interior Ministry troops (the former Dzerzhinskiy Division). He took part in celebrating the 70th anniversary of the division. Those who attended the festivities also included the Federation Council Speaker, Vladimir Shumeyko; the Interior Minister, Viktor Yerin; the Foreign Minister, Andrey Kozyrev; and the Director of the Federal Counter-Intelligence Service, Sergey Stepashin. Boris Yeltsin drew attention to the rampage of crime in the country, which, according to him, began to threaten the state and individual citizens. He pointed to the need to strengthen law and order.
