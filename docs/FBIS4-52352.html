BFN [Text] Australia's foreign affairs minister, Gareth Evans, has opened an ASEAN-Australia forum in Canberra, predicting continued growth in links between ASEAN and Australia. It is the 16th ASEAN-Australia Forum and is being attended by more than 100 government officials and representatives of business. Senator Evans told the forum he expects the growing relationship between Australia and the region to intensify over the next two years. [Begin Evans recording] The trend in ASEAN-Australia relations is already clear. By 2014 we will be, I believe, significantly closer than even we are today, and forming something of a cohesive unit within a broader Asia-Pacific framework. In a changing regional environment, I think we need to look to the future of ASEAN-Australia relations and develop further the structures and the linkages to sustain our political and economic relationship into the next century. [end recording]
