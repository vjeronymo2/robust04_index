941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / International Company News: Foster's considers acquisition programme Foster's Brewing, the Australian beer group, is considering spending up to ADollars 700m (USDollars 517m) on acquisitions, according to the company's managing director Mr Ted Kunkel, writes Bruce Jacques. Mr Kunkel told a business lunch in Brisbane the company was emerging from a period of instability and indebtedness and was clearly 'in growth mode'. He said Foster's would concentrate on beer and beer-related acquisitions in any part of the world, although its strategy was to concentrate on the UK.
