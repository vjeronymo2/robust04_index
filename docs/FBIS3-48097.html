Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 18 (KCNA) -- Party leaders of Guinea and India voiced support for the just cause of the Korean people. El Hadj Boubacar Diallo, general secretary of the Party for Unity and Progress of Guinea, when meeting with the Korean ambassador to his country, fully supported the policy of national reunification reiterated by the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song in his New Year address and the 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation. The United States must know the dear leader General Kim Chong-il and the Korean people, he said, adding: No matter how hard the United States may work it cannot overpower the might of the people in Korea where the leader, the party and the masses are united in one mind. U.P. Singh, member of the parliament and leading member of the Socialist Party of India, when meeting a delegation of the chuche Idea Academy from Korea, said the Socialist Party of India rejoiced over the fact that Korea is confidently pushing ahead with the socialist movement under the guidance of the great leader President Kim Il-song and His Excellency Kim Chong-il. The Socialist Party of India, he noted, is following with keen interest the socialist theory and practice of Korea and fully supporting the Workers' Party of Korea's theory of remoulding men, the theory of firmly uniting the working masses around the party in particular.
