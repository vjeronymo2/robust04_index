March 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

LAZERS PLAY DALLAS TODAY AT FORUM

The Lazers, losers of two straight, will play host to the Dallas Sidekicks today at 1:30 at the Forum.

It has been nearly a week since the Lazers (16-17) last played a Major Indoor Soccer League game, losing to the San Diego Sockers, 8-1, in falling six games behind the first-place Baltimore Blast.

The Lazers haven't fared too well against Dallas, either. The Sidekicks (16-16), a half-game ahead of the Lazers, have won four of five meetings this season.
