941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / Reporting rule for insurance deferred The Accounting Standards Board has confirmed that it will defer aspects of one of its financial reporting rules in certain areas of insurance. Financial Reporting Standard 5, which is designed to make company transactions reflect their real substance, not merely their legal form, will be deferred for two years for insurance broking transactions and financial reinsurance included in the accounts of Lloyd's syndicates.
