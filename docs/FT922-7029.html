920520 FT 20 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Countryside Props falls to Pounds 2.04m COUNTRYSIDE Properties, the residential, commercial and industrial property company, reported a pre-tax profit of Pounds 2.04m for the six months to March 31 1992. The result compares with Pounds 3.06m last time and was struck on turnover up from Pounds 32.7m to Pounds 42.2m and after an exceptional charge of Pounds 1.02m relating to additional provisions against some residential development sites. Group trading profit was Pounds 5.6m, with residential accounting for Pounds 5.9m. Commercial made Pounds 200,000, offsetting a Pounds 500,000 loss by property investment. The interim dividend is being maintained at 1.41p, payable from earnings per share of 3.2p (6.6p).
