BFN [Excerpt] Between 4 and 6 May, representatives of the UN International Narcotics Control Board [INCB] will be paying an official visit to Belarus. The main goal of the visit is to discuss international treaties concerning the fight against drugs, cooperation between Belarus and the INCB, technical assistance to Belarus in this area, and the development of a national mechanism for controlling the production and distribution of drugs and psychotropic substances. It is expected that in the course of the visit, the INCB representatives will meet with Belarusian Interior Ministry leaders and workers from various Interior Ministry departments. [passage omitted]
