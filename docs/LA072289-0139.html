July 22, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

EARNINGS

El Camino Bank, Anaheim, posted net income of $557,000 for the second quarter, a 21% increase from the $459,000 earned for the same quarter last year. The bank's assets rose 8% to $124.3 million from $115 million. Total capital increased 12%, to $11.2 million from $10 million. Net income for the six-month period totaled $1.1 million, 32% higher than the $821,000 reported for the first half of 1988.
