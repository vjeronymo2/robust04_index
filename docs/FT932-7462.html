930524 FT 24 MAY 93 / Cuba and Russia boost ties Cuba and Russia have agreed to try to boost their flagging bilateral economic ties and signed a memorandum outlining joint trade, production and investment initiatives in sugar, oil, machinery parts and fertilisers, Reuter reports from Havana. Cuban state media said the memorandum outlined ways for the two countries to put their trade relations on a new footing by setting up joint ventures and production-sharing initiatives.
