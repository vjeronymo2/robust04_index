930918 FT 18 SEP 93 / Chalker downbeat on overseas aid BARONESS Chalker, overseas development minister, yesterday said there was little immediate chance of the UK increasing overseas aid to the level required by the United Nations. While the government accepted 0.7 per cent of gross national product as a long-term target, it was not realistic to expect further progress 'when the fiscal situation at home is as difficult as it now is'.
