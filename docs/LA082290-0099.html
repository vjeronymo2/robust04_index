August 22, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

PATRIOTIC LANDLORD: The Irvine Co. is telling managers of its 9,000 rental units to reduce rents by $100 a month for any military reservist called up for more than two weeks as a result of the Mideast crisis, to permit conversion of long-term leases to monthly rentals and to refund all but $99 of security deposits. . . . "We believe there might be a financial burden on anybody called up," company spokesman Mike Stockstill said. "We believe it's the appropriate thing to do." Jeffrey A. Perlman and Tom Hamilton
