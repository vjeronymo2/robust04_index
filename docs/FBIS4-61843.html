BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko and Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev 7 May TASS -- A Russian Navy delegation may have to cancel its trip to Sevastopol scheduled for 7 May unless Ukraine changes its mind and decides to let the delegation's plane in. The plane would land at Belbek's airport three miles north of Sevastopol, the main base of the disputed Black Sea Fleet, but Ukraine insists that the delegation led by First Deputy Navy Commander Igor Kasatonov go by train, a source in the Sevastopol city administration said. The source also said that the administration has invited Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk and Russian President Boris Yeltsin to attend Victory Day celebrations to be held on 9 May.
