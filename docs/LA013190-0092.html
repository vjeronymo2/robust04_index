January 31, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Stride Rite Rejects Offer: The Cambridge, Mass.-based manufacturer of children's shoes said its directors have spurned a $900-million takeover bid from a group of investors.

Stride Rite received the $32-per-share offer in December from a group led by Walsh, Greenwood & Co. in Greenwich, Conn. A spokesman said the group will consider what steps to take.
