SUMMARY: In accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1980 (title 44, U.S. Code, chapter 35), this notice announces a request for reclearance of an information collection. Form RI 25&hyph;47, Survey of Continuing Full-Time School Attendance, is used to verify that students who certified they would be enrolled full time are still so enrolled. Approximately 11,000 RI 25&hyph;47 forms are completed annually. It takes approximately 5 minutes to complete. The total annual burden is 917 hours. For copies of this proposal, contact C.
