BFN [Vladimir Gundarov report: "Kursk Launch Ceremony"] [Text] Static tests of the Kursk, a new nuclear missile submarine, began at Severodvinsk on 23 May. Captain First Class V. Rozhkov has been appointed its commander. As Ivan Vasilyev, the senior official responsible for the order, has noted, the nuclear powered vessel has a number of improvements, including in its navigational systems package, and the crew's living conditions have been improved. The new nuclear-powered vessel will go into service with the Russian Navy next December.
