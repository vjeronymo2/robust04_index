920414 FT 14 APR 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Landlords learn how to pull customers - In a Nutshell The upheavals in the brewing industry which followed the publication of the Monopolies and Mergers Commission report in 1989 have prompted increased demand for business training for pub landlords. Inntrepreneur, a pub leasing company owned by GrandMet Estates, provides a pub training course for new licensees for a fee of Pounds 560 plus VAT repayable if the participant takes a pub on through the company. Existing leaseholders in Bass pubs can improve their business skills under a training programme devised by the Sheffield Enterprise Agency (Senta), Sheffield Training and Enterprise Council and the Bass Lease Company.
