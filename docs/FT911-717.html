910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Highcroft Inv Trust advances to Pounds 0.76m Highcroft Investment Trust, which holds properties and securities, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 692,000 to Pounds 759,000 in the year to December 31. Earnings emerged at 9.49p (8.64p) and the final dividend is a proposed 2.4p for a total of 3.9p (3.35p). Gross income came to Pounds 904,000 (Pounds 814,000).
