Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Marj 'Uyun, Lebanon, Feb 7 (AFP)--Hizballah guerrillas killed three Israeli soldiers and wounded three others in south Lebanon on Monday, triggering retaliatory air strikes, security sources said. The ambush took place as an Israeli patrol drove along a road in Israel's self-declared "security zone" in south Lebanon, the sources said. The attack was claimed by the pro-Iranian Hizballah. Shortly afterwards Israeli warplanes raided Hizballah positions near the south Lebanon port city of Sidon, the sources said.
