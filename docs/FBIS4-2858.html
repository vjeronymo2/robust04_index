BFN [Announcer-read report over video; from the "Night News" program] [Text] [Video opens with a medium-range shot of test pilots, then cuts to show a pilot entering an aircraft to prepare for flight. Video also shows aircraft taking off, dropping bombs, and firing missiles, as well as computer rooms which monitor test flights] An Air Force test-flight regiment--China's only regiment which can fly every Chinese-made fighter, bomber, and transport--was recently commended by the Air Force and received a Second Class Merit Citation. Over the past two decades, the regiment has made test flights and issued certificates of inspection for 43 new kinds of Chinese-built aircraft, have completed some 880 major scientific research projects, and have filled in the gaps in nearly 10 aviation projects. Wang Ang, (Hua Jun), (Wang Guanyang), Huang Bingxin, and other model heroes have emerged. The regiment has prevented over 10,000 disasters encountered in some 60,000 scientific research flights, has saved hundreds of million yuan for the state, and has created miracles in the world history of test flights.
