Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Singapore, January 14 (XINHUA) -- General Liu Huaqing, vice chairman of the Central Military Commission of China, left here for Indonesia today, ending his transit visit to Singapore. Before his departure, Liu told XINHUA that he received a very warm and cordial reception from the Singapore Government and leaders during his three-day stay here. He said that he exchanged views with Singapore leaders on bilateral relations and regional issues and the two sides agreed on a wide range of issues. Liu and his 8-member military delegation arrived here on January 11 between his official visits to Thailand and Indonesia. During his first visit here, Liu met with Singapore Prime Minister Goh Chok Tong, Senior Minister Lee Kuan Yew, Deputy Prime Minister Lee Hsien Loong and Minister for Defense Yeo Ning Hong.
