940702 FT 02 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Samuel Heath Samuel Heath, a maker of hardware and giftware, raised pre-tax profits by 44 per cent from Pounds 361,000 to Pounds 520,000 in the year to March 31, on turnover 8 per cent higher at Pounds 7.64m. Earnings per share grew from 8.5p to 11.9p, while the dividend is raised to 6p (5.5p) with a proposed final of 4.5p.
