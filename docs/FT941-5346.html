940307 FT 07 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Neepsend NEEPSEND is selling land at Penistone Road, Sheffield, to Tesco for Pounds 400,000 cash, payable on completion on March 23. The land is sold subject to a restrictive covenant preventing its use other than as a car park. The book value of the land is Pounds 625,000. Proceeds will be used to cut borrowings.
