BFN [Text] Cajnice, 10 Apr (TANJUG) -- Serb fighters this morning liberated Uhotici Brdo, one of the last Muslim army strongholds south of Gorazde, the command of the Herzegovina Corps reported. Units of the Serb Republic Army are reported to be in control of the right bank of the Drina from Vitkovici to Dzindici at a distance of about 700 meters from the river. NATO planes are breaking the sound barrier while flying at low level above the area between Gorazde, Foca and Cajnice -- the scene of heavy fighting over the past 13 days. The command of the Hercegovina Corps, denying Muslim media reports, said today that Serb fighters have not engaged in any military actions in the center of Gorazde.
