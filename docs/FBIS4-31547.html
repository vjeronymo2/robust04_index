BFN [Unattributed report: "Close to Reaching Terms"] [Text] Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] and Greece are close to reaching terms of agreement, the Macedonian Foreign Minister Stevo Crvenkovski stated for the weekly magazine PULS. He pointed out Macedonia's view, insisted on from the beginning, on a need to resolve problems in a package and establish long-term and sound friendly relations, is accepted not only by the other side but by the international community as well. As for the two-day talks with U.S. envoy Nimetz, Crvenkovski said they focused on the next round of negotiations on ministers level, due next week in New York.
