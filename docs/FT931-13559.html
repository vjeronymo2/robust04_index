930122 FT 22 JAN 93 / Industrial Society attacks pay limit THE Industrial Society says the government's 1.5 per cent pay limit for the public sector is unfair and likely to backfire. Mrs Rhiannon Chapman, director of the independent advisory and training organisation, says that the 1.5 per cent pay norm was 'introduced in a manner guaranteed to provoke a hostile reaction from those who could plausibly see themselves as its victims'.
