February 25, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

GLOSSY AND STERILIZED

The Academy Awards spectacle is getting more predictable each year, so much so that the bottom-line is now "Who cares?"

Where are "The Thin Blue Line," "Wings of Desire," "Dominick and Eugene" and "Bagdad Cafe"?

To say that the Oscars determine what the best films in America are is like saying that George Bush is the best-qualified person to run the country because he won the election.

DON JENNETT

Palos Verdes Estates
