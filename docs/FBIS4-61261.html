BFN [Text] Baku (4 May) TURAN -- Heavy fightings, which began yesterday morning, continues, the head of Executive power of Terter city, Sardar Gamidov, told Turan at 1300. He said that on 3 May the Armenian troops resumed offensive towards the city of Terter from the village of Gyzyl-oba (Karmiravan). The Armenian Army is armed with artillery and military hardware and tries to break the defense of the Azeri Army. According to Gamidov, over the past two days the Armenian units lost many lives in this direction but failed to achieve success. At the same time, the Armenian troops try move further along the road Agdam-Barda. Fierce fighting are taking place in the village of Bash-Gervend. The same source reports that last evening the Armenian troops shelled the city of Barda from Grad plants. Wounded and damages are reported. According to unofficial sources, there is no fighting at another sections of the front.
