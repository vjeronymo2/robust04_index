April 5, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Piedmont Airlines has ordered three Boeing 767-200 jets in a deal valued at about $183 million, Boeing Commercial Airplanes announced. Two jets are scheduled to be delivered in March, 1990, and the third in September, 1990 . . . Castle Rock Entertainment said it has secured a $55-million revolving credit facility with Credit Lyonnais Bank Nederland.
