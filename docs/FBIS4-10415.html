BFN [From the "Economic Report" column by Miriam Lage] [Text] Senator Jose Richa, the rapporteur of the patents draft law, on 11 April had lunch with Industry and Commerce Minister Elcio Alvares, who will be leaving for Geneva to sign the final terms of the Uruguay GATT Round. Richa informed Alvares that his report on the patents draft law will be ready by 22 April. After dispensing with further public hearings, the report will be submitted to the Senate Economic Committee. In one month, the Senate plenum could vote on the draft law.
