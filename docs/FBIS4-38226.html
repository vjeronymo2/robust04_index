BFN [Text] His Majesty King Hassan II -- God made him victorious -- accompanied by Prince Sidi Mohamed, his royal highness the crown prince, received Yahya Husayn al-'Arashi, minister of civil service and administrative reforms and envoy of the Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih to his majesty the king, at the royal palace in Skherat this afternoon. The audience was attended by Abdellatif Filali, prime minister and minister of foreign affairs and cooperation, and His Majesty's adviser Abdelhadi Boutaleb.
