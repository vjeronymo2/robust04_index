BFN [Text] Mentana, Fede, and Liguori, the chief editors of the news bulletins broadcast on the three Fininvest-controlled television channels [Canale Cinque, Retequattro, and Italia Uno] handed in their resignations a short time ago as a protest against the statements made by Franco Tato, the managing director of the Fininvest group. He told a newspaper that if the Mammi Law [telecommunications law approved last year by parliament] did not exist, he would have cancelled all the news and information programs on the three channels.
