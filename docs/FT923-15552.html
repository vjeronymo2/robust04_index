920701 FT 01 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Adam & Harvey at Pounds 6.4m ADAM & HARVEY, the international distribution and steel stockholding group formerly known as Stocklake Holdings, returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 6.4m from turnover of Pounds 42.7m for the year to end-March. Last August Stocklake, which returned profits of Pounds 5.96m on turnover of Pounds 36.9m for 1990-91, was voluntarily wound up in order to return Pounds 5.75m of excess cash to shareholders in a tax efficient manner. Along with the cash holders also received shares in Adam & Harvey, which obtained a listing in September. Earnings for the year emerged at 69.9p and a final dividend of 6p makes an 11p total - a 10 per cent improvement over the pro forma dividend of the previous year.
