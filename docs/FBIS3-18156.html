Language: Finnish Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Helsinki has today celebrated the 50th anniversary of the air defense victory of the city. [passage omitted] In his speech at the main festivities, Admiral Jan Klenberg, commander of the Defense Forces, found many similarities between the current air defense of Helsinki and the air defense of 50 years ago. Klenberg said that the spirit of the air defense is still good. However, the commander of the Defense Forces emphasized that the missile system protecting the capital should be renewed.
