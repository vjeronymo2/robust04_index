BFN ["News Release"] [Text] Estonian Ministry of Foreign Affairs considers the attack on April 10, of NATO air force to Bosnian Serbs positions at the region of Goradze as an inevitable decision to guarantee the security of population and UN observers that are located at the Bosnia-Herzegovina security area, determined by UN Security Council, the Ministry statement of Monday [10 April] says. Estonian Ministry of Foreign Affairs considers the above mentioned military operation to be in accordance with the UN Security Council resolutions No. 824 and 836. The Ministry expresses hope that the normalization process that started in February this year in Bosnia-Herzegovina will continue in the direction of a general ceasefire and will lead the conflict that has lasted for two years to a just and peaceful solution.
