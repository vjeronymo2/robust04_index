930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / Observer: What's in a name? Dubbed by Serbian President Slobodan Milosevic's party as the personification of evil, Serb parliamentary deputy Vojislav Seselj was yesterday currying favour with another Slavic strongman when he proudly announced that he had named his newborn son Aleksander, in honour of Yeltsin's arch-rival Rutskoi.
