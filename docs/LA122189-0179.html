December 21, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: FULLERTON;

CITY HALL WILL CLOSE THROUGH HOLIDAYS

Fullerton City Hall will be closed for the holidays, Dec. 25 through Jan. 1. It will reopen at 8 a.m. on Jan. 2.

Essential services, such as police and fire protection, will be unaffected, but the city's libraries and recreation facilities will operate on reduced holiday schedules.

Water bills deposited in the slot outside City Hall will be collected during the holiday week, and maintenance crews will be on stand-by in case of an emergency.

City officials say the reduced schedule saves money by closing offices where demand for services is typically slow during the holidays. Most employees will take the week off without pay.
