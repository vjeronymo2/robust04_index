920807 FT 07 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Racal to publish Chubb details Racal Electronics said it would publish on September 16 details of the proposed spin-off of its Chubb security business. If approved by shareholders at an EGM, the spin-off would take place in October, making Chubb an independent company with a market capitalisation of about Pounds 500m-Pounds 600m.
