Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn today received a telephone call from U.S. President Bill Clinton. During the telephone conversation, the two leaders exchanged views on various regional and international issues, foremost among which are the peace process and the latest developments in this process. The important role played by Jordan to safeguard regional security and stability, as well as Jordan's basic role in the peace process were underscored.
