940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / World news in brief: Hanson surprises stock market with increased payout Hanson surprised the stock market by raising its quarterly dividend from 2.85p to 3p when announcing half-year pre-tax profits up from Pounds 507m to Pounds 683m. 'Our return to profit growth at the operating level is now established,' said the Anglo-American conglomerate's chairman, Lord Hanson. Page 23; Lex, Page 22; Economic recovery aids Hanson, Page 24
