March 1, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Fleetwood Enterprises Inc.: Third-quarter profit fell 28% to $9.1 million on revenue of $347.4 million, up slightly from year-ago revenue of $346.8 million. The Riverside-based maker of motor homes, travel trailers and manufactured housing said the decline resulted primarily from lower profit margins because of competitive market conditions and a shift to lower-priced models. Results for 1990 also include a non-recurring loss related to the sale of two idle plants.
