920618 FT 18 JUN 92 / UK Company News: AF Bulgin reduces loss to Pounds 107,000 AF Bulgin reported reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 107,401 for the year to January 31. For the previous 12 months the loss was Pounds 505,225. Sales in the period under review declined to Pounds 12.2m (Pounds 14.1m). Exceptional costs of Pounds 70,933 (Pounds 562,567) represented the reorganisation of overseas subsidiaries and redundancies. The extraordinary charge of Pounds 60,914 (Pounds 202) reflected termination costs of an overseas subsidiary and provisions against group investment. The electrical and electronic components company is paying an unchanged single final dividend of 0.1p. Losses per share fell to 0.4p (1.21p). The directors said the first quarter of the current year had shown an improvement, but the second half must be regarded with caution.
