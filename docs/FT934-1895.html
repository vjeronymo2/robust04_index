931216 FT 16 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Baggeridge up 12% on interest fall Lower interest charges helped Baggeridge Brick increase pre-tax profits by 12 per cent to Pounds 2.03m in the year to September 30, against Pounds 1.82m. Turnover was ahead 14 per cent at Pounds 29.9m (Pounds 26.3m). Earnings per share were 3.33p (3.03p) and an unchanged final dividend of 2.375p is proposed for a maintained 3.125p. Lower rates and cash generation contributed to a fall in interest payable to Pounds 897,000 (Pounds 1.34m).
