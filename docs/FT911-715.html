910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / UK Company News: City of Oxford Inv asset value at 40.1p Net asset value at the end of the year to March 31 1991 was 40.1p at City of Oxford Investment Trust. This compared with 47.8p at the end of the previous 12 months. Dividends and interest fell from Pounds 1.52m to Pounds 1.47m; other income added Pounds 28,860 (Pounds 3,733). After interest and management expenses of Pounds 204,697 (Pounds 209,412) revenue before tax came to Pounds 1.29m (Pounds 1.31m). Earnings per ordinary income share were 4.74p (4.85p). Directors recommended a final dividend of 1.4625p, making a total of 4.5p. Last year's total came to 4p, plus a special payment of 0.5p.
