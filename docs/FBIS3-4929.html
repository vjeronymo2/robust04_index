Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, march 1 (KCNA) -- Many poems praising the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il have been created in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. From February last year to the end of January this year, the literary and art press group and the Kumsong Youth Publishing House carried more than 200 of poems written by poets and working people of different strata in various publications. In this period, poets of the Korean writing group created "Son of the People", "Pride of Korea", "One Big Family" and many other poems of high ideological and artistic value. A lyric "Son of Paektu" tell about the Korean people's great pride and honor of having the iron-willed brilliant commander Comrade Kim Chong-il, a peerless great man and great son of the country produced by Mt. Paektu. Poets of the group recently held a poem recitation meeting in front of the old home at the Mt. Paektu secret camp with "With the Old Home at Mt. Paektu Secret Camp", "May the Chong-il Peak Shine Through Generations", "Strides of Great Man" and other poems they had recently written on the occasion of the birthday of Comrade Kim Chong-il. Poems praising the peerless great man have been widely created by working people, youth and students and People's Army soldiers. In December last year, Yi In-mo, the incarnation of faith and will, composed poems "Bout my Destiny", "Thinking About Love" and "Laudatory Song of Faith and Will" and presented them to the dear leader. In the poems, Yi In-mo tells about his strong ideological feeling that Korea's cause of socialism, the revolutionary cause of chuche, is invincible as long as there is the wise guidance of dear Comrade Kim Chong-il. Rim Chang-su in Jongpmhong County, South Hamgyong Province, and Kim Chae-song in Unjon County, North Phyongan Province, wrote lyrics "The Sun of the Nation, The Heaven of Mankind" and "Let us Sing of Our Mother Party" which tell about the unanimous resolution and will of the Korean people to hold in high esteem as the great sun Comrade Kim Chong-il who looks after and protects the destiny, future and hope of the Korean nation and to remain loyal and filial to him. Poems created among People's Army soldiers sing of their great pride and honor of having their supreme commander in the person of Comrade Kim Chong-il who is possessed of rare intelligence and outstanding strategy, ever-victorious commandership iron will and matchless grit and warm love of soldiers, and reflect their unshakable faith and will to resolutely safeguard him with arms. Several hundred pieces of poem including "He is Our Supreme Commander", "Highest Glory to the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Chuche" and "We Serve Comrade Supreme Commander!" have been created among People's Army soldiers in the last two years alone.
