March 12, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Call in About Communism: KCRW-FM responds to the changing world by airing with a National Public Radio special call-in program called "The Collapse of Communism" on Wednesday from 9-11 a.m. NPR's Daniel Schorr will host the broadcast; he'll be joined by NPR foreign desk reporters Anne Garrels from Moscow and Deborah Amos from Berlin. The first hour will focus on the Soviet Union, and the second will focus on East Germany. BETH KLEID
