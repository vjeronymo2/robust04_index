BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- The Social Democrats [SPD] will not agree to the Bundeswehr's participation in UN combat missions under any circumstances. SPD Chairman Rudolf Scharping made it clear in Bonn today that his party's political position will not change, even when the legal disputes have been clarified before the Federal Constitutional Court. Following his return from a trip to the United States, Scharping said in Bonn that he assumes the judges in Karlsruhe will insist on involving the Bundestag in a decision on the participation of German servicemen in UN missions.
