941110 FT 10 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Bunzl expands Bunzl, the distribution and cigarette filters group, has made its eighth acquisition in just over two years with the purchase of NL Whittaker. The company, which incorporates the business of AW Holmes (Paper), supplies a range of disposable items, mainly to the hotel and catering trades. Sales of the combined businesses in the year to December 31 1993 totalled Pounds 13m; net assets are estimated to amount to Pounds 2.5m. Mr Anthony Habgood, Bunzl chief executive, said the deal demonstrated the group's continuing commitment to the development of paper and plastic disposables activities, now its largest and most profitable business.
