July 26, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: CULVER CITY;

SUMMER CHILD CARE OFFERED

Children can still enroll in a summer child-care program offered by the Culver City Unified School District.

The program, which runs until Sept. 10 at the district's four elementary schools, offers arts and crafts, music, swimming, games, field trips and other activities for kindergarteners through fifth-graders. It operates on weekdays from 7:30 a.m. to 6 p.m. and costs $75 a week. Children need not be Culver City residents to participate.

For more information, call (213) 559-3631.
