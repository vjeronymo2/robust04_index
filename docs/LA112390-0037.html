November 23, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: PALMDALE MAN DIES IN NEVADA ACCIDENT

A Palmdale man was killed and two members of his family injured when their car spun out of control and overturned on Interstate 80, about 25 miles east of Winnemucca, Nev.

David Read, 34, died when he was ejected from the car Wednesday, the Nevada Highway Patrol said.

His wife, Paula, 31, and 3-year-old daughter, Meagan, were hospitalized with undisclosed injuries.

Four other family members were uninjured.

The cause of the accident was under investigation.
