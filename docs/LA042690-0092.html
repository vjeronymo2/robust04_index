April 26, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

'Nasty' Found Obscene in Ohio: "As Nasty as They Wanna Be," the sexually explicit album by the Miami-based rap group 2 Live Crew, is too nasty for Cincinnati. According to a decision reached Wednesday by the Hamilton County Vice Squad, the controversial million-selling album was deemed a violation of Ohio obscenity statutes.

When police visited the five stores in the county that carried the album, only one of them had the uncensored version. The store promptly removed the album at the police's request. There was no hearing or court proceeding involved in naming the album obscene, only a decision by Sherriff Simon Leif, who was involved in the recent controversy over the Robert Mapplethorpe exhibit in Cincinnati, and the vice squad. BETH KLEID
