931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / International Company News: Cheung Kong cuts stake in Allied CHEUNG KONG (Holdings), the flagship of Mr Li Ka Shing, has cut its shareholdings in Allied Group to 9.98 per cent from 18.21 per cent, Reuter reports from Hong Kong. The move confounded rumours of a takeover of Allied Group by Cheung Kong, with analysts attributing Cheung Kong's interest in the group to its 52 per cent held Allied Properties (HK), which owns several quality properties.
