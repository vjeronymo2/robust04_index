941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Scottish National Net asset value per capital share of the Scottish National Trust stood at 42.9p at the September 30 year end, against 94.9p 12 months earlier. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 14m (Pounds 14.6m) giving earnings of 7.41p (7.83p) per income share. A maintained final dividend of 2.1p is proposed, making 7.5p for the year. Last year's total of 7.75p included a special dividend of 0.25p.
