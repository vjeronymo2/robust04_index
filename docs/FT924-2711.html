921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / Payout for Manx BCCI depositors DEPOSITORS in the Isle of Man branch of the collapsed Bank of Credit and Commerce International will start receiving payments on Tuesday under the island's compensation scheme. Eligible claimants are entitled to compensation of 75 per cent of their deposit, subject to a maximum payment of Pounds 15,000 a depositor. About 3,500 depositors have filed claims.
