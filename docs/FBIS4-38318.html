BFN [Text] Chechnya President Dzhokhar Dudayev's wife and young son received injuries of varying degrees of severity during the assassination attempt on the president on 28 May, according to a statement released by the Chechnya Foreign Ministry today. The Foreign Ministry assesses the act of terrorism, in which the republic's interior minister and his deputy were killed, as an attack on President Dudayev. The terrorists used a remote-control explosive device, which the Russian special services have at their disposal.
