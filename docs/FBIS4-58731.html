BFN [Statement issued by Foreign Ministry; date not given]- [Text] Following the signing of the agreement on self-rule between the Palestinians and the Israelis, the Foreign Ministry has issued the following statement: Tunisia has noted with satisfaction today's signing between the PLO chairman and the Israeli prime minister on the agreement to implement the Palestinian self-rule in Gaza and Jericho, which should initiate the implementation of the Declaration of Principles signed in Washington on 13 September 1993, bolster the peace process in the Middle East, and which is an important step toward a just and comprehensive solution in order to guarantee the legitimate rights of the struggling fraternal Palestinian people and its right to lead a dignified life in a sovereign state. Tunisia, which has always been tireless in maintaining and consolidating the independence of the Palestinian decision-makers, supporting the Palestinian people's just cause, and following up the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations, is hoping that the new agreement will give fresh momentum to continue the peace process at all costs in order to bring about the total Israeli withdrawal from all occupied Arab territories and peace in the region in accordance with the UN resolution and international legality.
