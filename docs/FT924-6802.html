921119 FT 19 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Navan Resources IPounds 30,800 in the red Navan Resources, quoted on Dublin, reported a pre-tax loss of IPounds 30,800 (Pounds 33,700) for the year to end-March, compared with a profit of IPounds 18,800. Losses per share amounted to 0.41p against earnings of 0.2p. During the year the group spent IPounds 892,000 on mineral exploration and development and IPounds 75,900 was written off.
