BFN [Text] Shanghai, April 29 (XINHUA) -- Canadian Governor General Ramon John Hnatyshyn left here for Hong Kong this morning. Hnatyshyn came to pay China an official state visit as guest of President Jiang Zemin on April 25. Sun Yongfu, chairman of the Chinese Government Reception Committee and vice-minister of railways, and Shanghai's Deputy Mayor Sha Lin were among those seeing the Canadian guest off at the airport.
