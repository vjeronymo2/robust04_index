HighMark, a Massachusetts business trust organized on March 10, 1987, is registered under the Investment Company Act of 1940 as a diversified, open-end, management investment company. In the context of registered investment companies, the term ``investment adviser'' generally refers to the entity that has investment management authority with respect to the assets of the investment company. In this regard, subject to the general supervision of the HighMark Trustees, MERUS manages each of the separate investment portfolios within HighMark in accordance with the investment objectives, and policies of each portfolio, makes decisions with respect to and places orders for all purchases and sales of securities, and maintains records with respect thereto.
