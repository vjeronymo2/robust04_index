Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The SACP [South African Communist Party] claims that the National Party [NP] is using intelligence networks to run a smear campaign for electioneering. A member of the SACP Central Committee, Mr. Jeremy Cronin, has called on the NP to confirm or deny that the SACP's facsimile and telephone lines have been tapped. He also called on the NP to explain its connection to the National Intelligence Service and to name all the Broederbond [Secret Afrikaner Brotherhood] members on its election list.
