931204 FT 04 DEC 93 / Investors invited to seek compensation Customers of four investment firms have been invited by the Investors Compensation Scheme to see if they qualify for compensation. The firms are former members of Fimbra, the self-regulating organisation for financial advisers. They are: APAL Financial Services of Barking, Essex; Chartered Financial Services (in liquidation) of Southampton; MS Insurance Services of Northwood, Middlesex; and Special Needs Consultancy of Torquay, Devon. The ICS said yesterday that the cases involved fewer than 50 clients and about Pounds 1m of investments.
