920203 FT 03 FEB 92 / Female condom nears US approval A CONDOM for women patented by Chartex International, a London-based unquoted company, has been recommended for approval by a sub-committee of the US Food and Drug Administration, subject to a contraceptive efficacy study. The condom, known as Femidom in Europe and Reality in the US, is a pliable polyurethane sheath. It will be launched in Switzerland this week. A UK launch is planned for this summer. Chartex, has licensed Wisconsin Pharmacal, a Chicago-based group, to manufacture and market the product in the US. Femidom has been tested clinically 30,000 times by 1,700 couples in 15 countries.
