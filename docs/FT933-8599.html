930814 FT 14 AUG 93 / International Company News: China Pepsi bottler posts strong advance SHENZHEN Shenbao Industrial, the sole bottler of Pepsi-Cola in Shenzhen, China, reported that its net profits rose 133 per cent to Yn14.8m (Dollars 2.6m) for the first six months ended June 30, AP-DJ reports. The directors of the company did not declare any interim dividend. The company's sales advanced to 143.4m yuan from 128.5m last time, a rise of 12 per cent.
