BFN [Excerpt] Orestes Quercia has been nominated presidential candidate for the PMDB [Brazilian Democratic Movement Party]. Quercia defeated former Parana Governor Roberto Requiao in the party internal elections held throughout the country on 15 May. Quercia won the internal PMDB elections by a broad margin. PMDB President Luiz Henrique said that despite the high rate of abstentions, he believes that the result reflects the will of the PMDB grassroots. [passage omitted]
