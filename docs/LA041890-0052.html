April 18, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

ENGLISH MUFFIN IS OK

Question: Different restaurants serve different types of breads with their meals.

I've even been to some restaurants that put down a tray of Danish pastry when you first sit down. I know that Danish is a kind of cake, so I avoid it. But what about an English muffin? Is that considered acceptable if you're trying to watch your weight?

Answer: An English muffin is acceptable. Danish, sweet rolls and coffee cake are not.
