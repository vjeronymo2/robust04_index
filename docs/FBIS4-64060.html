BFN [Text] On Thursday after delivering a speech at the All-Russian conference of the Federal Counter-Intelligence Service (FCIS) President Boris Yeltsin met separately with members of the FCIS collegium. An open discussion about the present state and plans of the special service took place, the FCIS's tasks were specified. Head of the Public Relations Center of the FCIS Alexander Mikhailov told Interfax that the FCIS was satisfied with results of the meetings with the president.
