941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / Local polls hit Berlusconi Italian local elections over the weekend increased the fragility of the right-wing government and turned the spotlight on the possibility of a centre-left coalition. Centre-left opposition alliances won the mayoral races in the run-off for four out of six regional capitals, two of the regional councils and a majority of the small local administrations being contested. Prime minister Mr Silvio Berlusconi's Forza Italia movement showed signs of losing momentum and in the south the neo-fascist MSI/National Alliance surprised every commentator by the poor performance in its heartland.
