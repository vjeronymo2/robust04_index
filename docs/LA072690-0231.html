NATION;

BODY FOUND IN BONDAGE CASKET

Authorities are trying to determine what killed a person whose body was found in a casket in a room equipped with an iron cage and sexual bondage paraphernalia, police said today.

Authorities could not determine immediately whether the decomposed body is that of a male or a female, said Harris County Medical Examiner J. L. Ebdon.

The body was found Wednesday wrapped in heavy plastic and bound at the feet with duct tape.

"It is very badly decomposed but because of the wrapping, we cannot tell how long it has been there," Ebdon said.
