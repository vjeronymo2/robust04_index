BFN [From the "Business SNARK" file] [Text] Baku [no dateline as received] -- An advisory committee headed by Geydar Aliyev had a session June 21 where present problems in Azerbaijan economy were discussed. The President certified that, though the necessary measures were undertaken, the situation in economy is aggravated. Low prices for fuel at domestic market, as well as easy-terms credits played a destructive role in the social and economic sphere. In this connection, positively were estimated the President's orders on quarterly revisions of prices for oil and petroleum products and on granting credits on easy terms. Parliament Chairman Rasul Guliyev, appearing on the session, observed that despite the President's order "On Liberalization of Foreign Trade Prices", an enormous amount of goods in the state warehouses remain non-distributed. Thus the Agricultural Ministry has goods for $400m [million], State Oil Concern for $100m, and State Metal Concern from $15m to $20m. Total price of unsold goods is $600m. Every day products for a sum $5m to $6m turn unfit. Mr. Guliyev proposed to organize a special advisory commission with powers to distribute the stored products in 6 months period. The session also discussed the issue of privatization of unfinished construction sites.
