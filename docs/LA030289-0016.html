March 2, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

ABORTION CONTROVERSY

The issue is not pro-life or pro-choice; abortion is as old as the species and will continue until its extinction, regardless of the current sound and fury. The issue is whether or not all women who are faced with this dilemma will have access to a safe, inexpensive procedure in the most therapeutic of medical and social conditions.

The actions and words of those who would deny them this clearly demonstrate that they have no interest in the fetus, the woman, or society. Their purpose is to impose their beliefs on the rest of us. That cannot be allowed to happen.

LYLE N. WHITED

Lomita
