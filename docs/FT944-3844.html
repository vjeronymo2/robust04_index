941210 FT 10 DEC 94 / De Savary's castle damaged by fire A Scottish castle being developed into a tourist attraction by Mr Peter de Savary was damaged by fire last night. The fire, at Skibo Castle near Dornoch, Sutherland, was brought under control before it reached the main part of the castle but there was smoke damage throughout the building. No one is thought to have been hurt. The building has been undergoing a Pounds 15m refurbishment.
