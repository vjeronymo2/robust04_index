BFN [Text] Mulhouse (DPA) -- Germany and France are planning a joint maneuver with Polish units in autumn, which is to take place in France. The military leaderships are to work out plans for this maneuver, it was heard from delegation circles on the fringe of the Franco-German summit in Mulhouse on Tuesday [31 May]. At the next meeting of the defense ministers of the three countries in Warsaw on 17 and 18 July, these plans will be discussed.
