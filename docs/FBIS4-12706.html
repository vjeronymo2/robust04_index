BFN [Text] Army forces yesterday raided an ammunition store belonging to a Palestinian group in the Sharhabil neighborhood of Sidon and confiscated around five truckloads of ammunition. The ammunition included: Some 343 107-mm rockets; 915 artillery rounds of various calibers; 271 shell charges; 138 hand grenades; and 6,680 rounds of various types of ammunition. The Army forces are continuing to search for weapons and ammunition throughout the Lebanese areas.
