June 30, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

AMC Entertainment Inc.: The Kansas City, Mo.-based movie theater operator posted a loss of $5.3 million for 1989, compared to a $13.8-million loss a year ago. Revenue rose 8% to $465 million. The results reflect the company's decision to sell its United Kingdom unit and United Artists Communications to reduce corporate debt, which it said was down 33% from the previous year.
