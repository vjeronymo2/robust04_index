BFN [Text] It was confirmed that even after the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] adopted a resolution to take sanctions against North Korea, two IAEA inspectors still remain in North Korea and are carrying out inspection activities of the work replacing the fuel rods in Yongbyon. Mr. Kyd, IAEA director of public relations [title as heard], said that the inspectors are watching over the work to replace the fuel rods and are continuing to carry out normal work of installing cameras to determine if nuclear materials have been diverted. He said that North Korea has not yet taken any measures regarding the two inspectors.
