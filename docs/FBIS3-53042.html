Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Two Israeli servicemen, one a soldier and the other a border policeman, have been lightly wounded in Khan Yunus. They sustained their injuries when a fragmentation grenade was thrown at a TV crew working on behalf of the Army spokesman, documenting events in the Strip before the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] pullout. As the area was combed, rioting broke out, during which a young Palestinian was injured and taken to hospital.
