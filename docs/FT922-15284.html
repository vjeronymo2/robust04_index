920402 FT 02 APR 92 / Letter: All knowing Sir, Your supplement on Slovenia (March 30) contained 11 different articles; three of them respectively headed: 'Judy Dempsey reports. . .' 'Judy Dempsey examines. . .' 'Judy Dempsey on. . .' The other eight are signed by Judy Dempsey. Is this a record and is there anything about Slovenia that Judy Dempsey doesn't know?
