Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] At the invitation of the Swedish Ministry of Finance, Latvian Finance Minister Uldis Osis is visiting Sweden. The purpose of the visit is that he acquaint himself with other countries' financial systems, because in Latvia a reform of the budget system is envisaged, with changes in the drafting of the budget, confirming [word indistinct] and monitoring its implementation. Osis has already met Sweden's Finance Minister Anne Wibble, Budget Director Joergen Holmquist, and the parliamentary finance commission. Uldis Osis also visited the Stockholm stock exchange. During his visit, agreement was reached whereby Sweden will provide assistance in training Latvian specialists. It was also confirmed that Sweden is prepared to assign $10 million to improve the Latvian banks' system of operation. The recipients of this money will be the Latvian Universal Bank and the Latvian Savings Bank.
