December 30, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: SAN JUAN CAPISTRANO;

CONTESTANTS SOUGHT FOR CITY PAGEANT

Contestants and sponsors are needed for the annual Miss San Juan Capistrano pageant scheduled for Feb. 16.

Interested persons must be between the ages of 17 and 21 by the pageant date, and must have lived or worked in the city for a minimum of six months. Entrants are also required to have lived in California for one year.

Sponsors can be any person or business willing to pay a $150 entry fee for the contestant.

The deadline for applications is Jan. 12.

Miss San Juan Capistrano acts as a representative for the chamber of commerce and the city. Call (714) 493-4700 for information.
