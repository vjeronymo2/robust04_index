Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Wu Bangguo and Huang Ju Meet Zhou Keyu"] [Text] Lieutenant General Zhou Keyu, political commissar of the People's Liberation Army [PLA] General Logistics Department, paid a courtesy call on leading comrades of the Shanghai municipal party committee and government on 1 March. Wu Bangguo, CPC Central Committee Political Bureau member and secretary of the municipal party committee; Huang Ju, deputy secretary of the municipal party committee and mayor; and Wang Liping, deputy secretary, cordially met with Comrade Zhou Keyun. Zhou Keyu briefed the municipal leaders on his trip to Shanghai to oversee the change of president of the Second Military Medical College. Former president Major General Lu Zengqi was transferred to the General Logistics Department to serve as director of its Health Department. He was succeeded by Major General Wang Qingshun (3769 1987 5293), former vice president of the Fourth Army College. Jia Yunsheng (6328 3387 5281), deputy director of the Political Department of the General Logistics Department; Fu Cuihe, political commissar of the Second Military Medical College; and Zhou Yupeng, secretary general of the Shanghai municipal party committee, attended the meeting.
