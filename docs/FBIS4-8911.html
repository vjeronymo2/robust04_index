BFN [Text] Chaos and street fighting are raging in Gorazde. That was reported this afternoon by local official Esad Obranovic, speaking for Radio Sarajevo via ham radio operators. Obranovic said the Gorazde hospital, some 200 meters from the scene of bitter conflicts on the right bank of the River Drina, was crammed with the wounded and people were still seeking shelter in its corridors. Meanwhile, house-to-house fighting was reported in the suburbs of Gorazde. The Bosnian prime minister Haris Silajdzic said the Serb forces had hit an ammonia storage facility and were continuing to open fire on the town.
