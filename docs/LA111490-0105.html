November 14, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MANNING IS PUT ON INJURED LIST

Danny Manning, who had played in only two of the six Clipper games this season because of tendinitis in his right knee, was put on the injured list Tuesday and will miss at least five more games.

Cedric Ball, who was with the Clippers in training camp before being waived, is expected to join the team today as Manning's replacement.

Recently, Ball played two games with Wichita Falls, Tex., of the Continental Basketball Assn.
