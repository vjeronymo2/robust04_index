August 10, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

INCIDENT ON COURSE SETS GOLFERS ABUZZ

As if the high rough and slick greens weren't enough, Ian Woosnam and Jim Thorpe had to battle another opponent at the PGA Championship: bees.

Both Woosnam and Thorpe were stung on the 12th hole Thursday.

A bee flew up Woosnam's pants leg and stung him. Thorpe was lanced on the knee.
