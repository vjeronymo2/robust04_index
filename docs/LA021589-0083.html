February 15, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

TOWN SEEKING LOST 1939 TIME CAPSULE

City officials hope to open a time capsule, buried nearly 50 years ago, next October -- if they can find it.

City Manager Jim Luckett Jr. said City Council minutes from 1939 refer to the capsule as commemorating the construction of City Hall. But the minutes do not say where the capsule was buried.

Luckett said that either no marker was ever put on the capsule or it was covered during recent remodeling.
