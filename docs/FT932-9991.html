930511 FT 11 MAY 93 / International Company News: Newspaper chief quits Thomson MR Michael Johnston, head of Thomson Corporation's North American newspaper division, has resigned, writes Bernard Simon. He remains a director of the Canadian-controlled publishing and travel group. No successor has been named. Mr Johnston had been spearheading a period of belt-tightening at Thomson Newspapers, which publishes the largest number of daily papers in North America. Operating earnings dropped by 13 per cent last year to USDollars 198m.
