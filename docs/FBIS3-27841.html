Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 31 KYODO -- Aoyama Trading Co., a major Japanese menswear retail chain operator, said Monday [31 January] it will open its first sales outlet in China in February in partnership with a local firm. The Hiroshima-based company said the new store, to be opened in Shanghai, will sell men's suits as well as shirts and casual wear. The company has already opened two stores in Taipei and established a subsidiary in Hong Kong. It said it also plans to expand its domestic sales network from the present 514 outlets to about 750 stores in two years.
