Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 22 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The General Council of the National Democratic Party of Mongolia (MNDP) has discussed at its sitting the present situation created in the country and has drawn a conclusion that the MNDP can not participate in work of the State Great Hural (SGH), preserving its party representative in the parliament the MNDP considers that the supreme legislative organ of the country the State Great Hural failed to be an organ of political pluralism and in consequence of this the meaning of a democratic state system is being lost, the main provisions of the Constitution are being violated, but the MNDP has not adopted a final decision on the issue of recalling their representatives in the parliament.
