930714 FT 14 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Farepak Farepak, the USM-quoted mail order distributor and food processor, intends to seek an introduction to the main market. To comply with the Stock Exchange's principal outstanding requirement for listing - that a minimum of 25 per cent of the company's shares be in public hands - family interests of Mr Bob Johnson, chairman, will place 2.05m shares with institutional investors via Hoare Govett Securities. The shares rose 10p to 300p yesterday.
