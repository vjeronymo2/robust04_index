Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The president, Sardar Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari, has said that Pakistan will continue to support the cause of the people of Bosnia-Herzegovina at all forums in all possible manners. He was talking to the Bosnian prime minister, Mr. Haris Silajdzic, who called on him in Islamabad today. During the meeting, the president conveyed to the Bosnian prime minister deep anguish of the people and government of Pakistan over the continuing aggression against Bosnia-Herzegovina. Mr. Haris Silajdzic thanked the president for Pakistan's support to the people of Bosnia for which, he said, his nation will remain eternally grateful. He briefed the president about the latest situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina, particularly highlighting the problems created by the continuing arms embargo against it. Later, the president hosted a luncheon in honor of the Bosnian prime minister. It was attended, among others, by the chairman of the Senarte, Mr. Wasim Sajjad, Minister for Production retired Brigadier Mohammad Asghar and members of the diplomatic corps.
