921002 FT 02 OCT 92 / Survey of Software at Work - Autumn 1992 (36): Softa - Supplier Profile Softa is a privately owned consultancy, employing 24 people. Its turnover is around Pounds 1m. The company provides systems consultancy and template solutions designed in accordance with client specified hardware and software needs. Softa specialises in the development of systems for sales and marketing applications. The consultancy places particular emphasis on the application of technology to integrate the sales and marketing functions which it believes have previously been seen as distinct and separate. Contacts: Louise Wilson/Stephen Arnold. Address: 9, Townsend Avenue, St Albans, Herts, ALl 3SL; tel. 0727 863234.
