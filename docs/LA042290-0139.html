April 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The Tampa Bay Buccaneers signed running back Gary Anderson to a four-year, $3.6-million contract, finalizing a trade with the San Diego Chargers.

Last year, Anderson rejected a contract offer from San Diego worth $512,000 per season and decided to sit out the entire season. He received a $400,000 signing bonus from Tampa Bay and will receive a salary of $600,000 in 1990.
