Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Xiamen, March 29 (XINHUA) -- China's ocean shipping giant, the China Ocean Shipping Group, opened a new subsidiary Monday [28 March] in Xiamen, a major port in Fujian Province. Xiamen Ocean Shipping Company, the group's eighth major fleet, now owns a French-made luxury passenger liner and two large bulk cargo ships. Company officials said the passenger liner will run between Hong Kong and Xiamen, while the cargo ships will sail between domestic and international ports. China Ocean Shipping Group was newly formed between the China Ocean Shipping Corporation, China Ocean Shipping Agency, China Highway Transport Company and others. The group's fleet of 600 ships now calls at more than 1,000 ports in over 150 countries around the world.
