January 4, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

A 29-year-old San Diego man was found shot to death Tuesday morning in the 3300 block of 42nd Street. The victim has been identified as Carlos Morales of Grape Street, San Diego police said.

The body was found at 6:11 a.m. Tuesday by police, who are investigating.
