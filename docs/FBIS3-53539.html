Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Riyadh, 15 Jan -- The Servant of the Two Holy Places, King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, has received a letter from His Excellency President Bill Clinton, President of the United States of America. The letter was handed by His Excellency Mr. Ronald Brown, U.S. commerce secretary, who was received at King Fahd's office today. [passage omitted]
