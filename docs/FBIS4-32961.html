BFN [Text] Jean-Claude Martineau has said Aristide will side with France and other countries so that he is not blamed for an invasion of Haiti. This weekend on a Montreal radio station, president-in-exile's spokesman detailed the avalanche strategy [strategie lavalassienne], in light of new developments in the Haitian crisis. He almost lost his temper in light of the pertinence of the questions of Compere Raymond Laurent, who had him unveil his government's priorities. Martineau clearly confessed that President Aristide will side with France and other countries so that he is not blamed for an invasion of Haiti.
