Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow January 20 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin signed a decree on Thursday to abolish the apparatus of the Federal Assembly which was set up to handle organisational questions until the new parliament begins operation. The government has been instructed to set up, in cooperation with the presidential administration and the apparatuses of the State Duma and the Federation Council, a commission to employ the staff of the liquidated body, the presidential press service said.
