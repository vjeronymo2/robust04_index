920430 FT 30 APR 92 / UK Company News: Brixton Estate ahead to Pounds 27.4m RECESSION in its sector notwithstanding, Brixton Estate achieved a 16 per cent improvement in pre-tax profits to Pounds 27.4m in the year to December 31. The final dividend is lifted to a proposed 5.2p (4.55p) for a total of 8.1p (7.1p), a rise of 14 per cent. The pre-tax result, up from Pounds 23.6m, was struck after higher administration costs of Pounds 2.92m (Pounds 2.73m) and an advance in interest payable on developed properties of Pounds 20.8m (Pounds 17.5m). Net rental income increased to Pounds 50.7m (Pounds 43.3m). At the year-end investment properties totalled Pounds 724m (Pounds 702m) and net assets were Pounds 373m, giving net asset value of 226p, down 8.1 per cent from the 1990 year-end. By December 31 1991, gearing had grown from 66 per cent to almost 87 per cent. Retained profits for the year came to Pounds 7.46m (Pounds 6.07m) and earnings worked through at 12.64p (10.79p) per share.
