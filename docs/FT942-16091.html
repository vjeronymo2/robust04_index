940412 FT 12 APR 94 / UK Company News: Management buy-in at Ward Sacks Ward Sacks, the paper sacks and carrier bag maker, has been taken over by a new management in a Pounds 3.93m deal. The new team is led by Mr Trevor Maund, formerly finance director of Usher Walker, who becomes managing director, Mr Brian Sutcliffe, at present managing director of Chilwood, who becomes chairman, and Mr Neil Whatmough who is promoted from general manager to sales director. The founders of Wards Sacks, Mr John Ward and Mr Robert Ward, retain a minority holding.
