Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Tel Aviv HA'ARETZ in Hebrew on 25 February, in a page A9 reported by ITIM, states: "Two heroin smugglers from the Galilee village of Julis, Daniyel 'Amad Mukhtar, 25, and 'Amad Nayif Hasan, 30, were sentenced by the Haifa District court to six years imprisonment after being convicted of smuggling 2 kg of heroin from Lebanon to Israel. They were apprehended by the police in May 1993
