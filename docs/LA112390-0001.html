November 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

PETE SCHABARUM ON TERM LIMITS

I'm feeling inclined to kid you people and say that for once I'm agreeing with you.

That was an insightful section dealing with Prop. 140 (Perspectives on Term Limits, Commentary, Nov. 13). Do note that we're Republicans here.

We voters must have just gotten a taste of how frustrating it is to be a legislator, in that the things you vote for have predictably both good and bad results. Things just can't be simple, no way.

DIANE STILES

Redondo Beach
