August 11, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

GIVE THEM SOME CREDIT, BUT ALSO MORE SPEED

During the past 18 years, with Ben Wade as director of scouting, the Dodgers have won more games than any team but the Yankees. Instead of criticism from some in the media for draft picks, which can't fairly be evaluated yet, a hearty thanks and congratulations for a job well done are due.

BOB CONGER

Huntington Beach
