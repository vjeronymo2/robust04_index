Language: Kyrgyz Article Type:BFN [Text] The government's press service reports that Prime Minister Dzhumagulov left for the Republic of Korea on 25 March. He will be attending the second international conference: Peace and the 21st century, from 26 to 29 March. It is expected that Dzhumagulov will deliver a speech on the problems of democratic transitions and economic reforms in Kyrgyzstan. Apart from this, the head of the government intends to establish business contacts between Kyrgyzstan and the Republic of Korea. Dzhumagulov stated that he will try to do everything so as to conclude mutually beneficial agreements with businessmen from Korea and other countries of the world and meet leaders of companies. He will hand over to them a package of proposals on economic cooperation from the Government of Kyrgyzstan.
