920611 FT 11 JUN 92 / UK Company News: M&G Second Dual net asset value up M&G Second Dual Income Trust lifted net asset value from 446.1p to 474.02p per capital share over the year to May 31. Net revenue was virtually unchanged at Pounds 2.39m, equal to earnings of 23.86p (23.79p) per income share. A final dividend of 10.67p makes a total of 23.86p (23.78p) for the year.
