930407 FT 07 APR 93 / China Southern Airlines hijackers taken away at Taipei's main airport The two hijackers who forced a China Southern Airlines Boeing 757 carrying 204 people to Taiwan yesterday are taken away by police at Taipei's main airport. The men, armed with pistols, had stormed the cockpit of a Beijing flight bound for the southern Chinese city of Shenzhen. The aircraft returned to China, but the hijackers, who asked for political asylum, remained in custody.
