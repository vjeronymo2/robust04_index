Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 15 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- At the press conference, which was held at the Ministry for External Relations, the Head of the Department of Press and Information of the Ministry D. Chuluundorj [spelling of name as received] informed that the president of the country P. Ochirbat at the end of February and the beginning of March will be on a visit to India, Thailand, Vietnam and Laos. The President of the Fund for Development of Mongolia D. Byambasuren made at the press conference a short report on the occasion of the first anniversary of the foundation of the fund. Answering the question about his secret visit to Taiwan on the line of the Mongolian Tibetan Committee, D. Byambasuren announced that the visit did not have any secret character and was directed to establishment of contacts with the Mongolian Tibetan Fund. Speaking at the press conference, the Head of the Society for Protection of Consumers' Interests S.T. Endbayar [spelling of name as received] noted that as a result of use of food products which do not meet sanitary and hygienic requirements, two persons become subject to poisoning every week.
