Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Husayn Abu-Salih has confirmed Sudan's belief that the southern issue should be dealt with within the framework of a diplomatic solution. Commenting on the American ambassador to Sudan's remarks at his news conference in Nairobi, Husayn Abu-Salih said diplomatic efforts represent a priority for solving the problem of the south. He added: We will give our attention to the Intergovernmental Authority on Drought and Development initiative. He confirmed Sudan's support for a peaceful solution. On the appointment of an American envoy to Sudan, Husayn Abu-Salih said that no official notification from the American Administration has reached Sudan in this connection. He expressed his hope for earnest and (?successful) cooperation to reach a peaceful, just, and rapid solution in order to end the bloodshed and the exhaustion of resources and to proceed towards development and stability.
