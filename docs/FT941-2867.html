940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Wagon Pounds 6m acquisition Wagon Industrial Holdings, the materials handling, engineering and automotive products group, has paid Pounds 6.1m in cash and shares for the refrigeration, air conditioning and maintenance division of Kenyon UK. According to management accounts the division made profits of about Pounds 1m for 1993. The directors stated that the book value of the assets purchased amount to Pounds 3.7m with no debts being assumed. Up to Pounds 1m of the purchase price will be repaid if certain profit targets are not achieved within the first year.
