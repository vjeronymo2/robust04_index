Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Azerbaijani Army resumed its attacks on the south and southeast of the Karabakh war front today. The fighting is also continuing in the north of Kelbadzharskiy Rayon. The Karabakh formations are preventing the enemy troops from penetrating the Armenian positions. The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic Defense Army Press Service reported that three civilians were killed and five wounded in yesterday's shelling of Martuni with Grad rockets. The shelling resumed today. We have no information on the consequences of shelling. Azerbaijani warplanes attacked settlements in Askeranskiy Rayon yesterday. There are casualties.
