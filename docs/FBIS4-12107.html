BFN [Excerpts] Amman, 5 Apr (PETRA)--Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat arrived in Amman today on a visit to Jordan. [passage omitted] On the resumption of talks with the Israelis, President 'Arafat said: The talks are currently taking place. He added: Concerning the Arab-Israeli talks, they will be resumed soon. Regarding the Jordanian-Palestinian Jerusalem Committee, which the two sides had earlier agreed to form, he said that the committee's work is not routine, but is related to an issue that is sacred to Arabs, Muslims, and Christians. On his return to the autonomy region, the Palestinian president said: I am waiting. Salim al-Za'nun, acting Palestine National Council speaker, arrived with President 'Arafat.
