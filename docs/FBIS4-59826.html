BFN [Text] In a statement to AL-SHIRA', National Defense Minister Muhsin Dallul reiterated his rejection of a Washington-backed Israeli request that Lebanon assimilate the so-called South Lebanese Army [SLA] and integrate its elements in the Lebanese Army as a condition of the Israeli withdrawal from Lebanese territories. The issue is an internal Lebanese affair and no country can tell us what we should do, the minister said. Those who dealt with Israel cannot be equated with other Lebanese militias that were disbanded and assimilated. The former are traitors and must stand trial in Lebanese courts, Dallul stated.
