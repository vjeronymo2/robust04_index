September 29, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Radio listeners with compulsive and addictive behaviors unite! "There Is a Solution," a weekly radio call-in show that will examine the problems of alcohol, drug and food addictions and offer hope for solutions and recovery, debuts Sunday at 9 p.m. on KIEV-AM (870). The show is hosted by Larry W., a recovering alcoholic and compulsive overeater who uses only his last initial in keeping with anonymous-program traditions. The guest for Sunday's premiere will be Roseanne S., the founder of Overeaters Anonymous. DANIEL CERONE
