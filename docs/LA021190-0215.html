February 11, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

MAN DIES IN SANTA ANA HOUSE FIRE

An unidentified man died late Saturday in a house fire in the 900 block of Buffalo Avenue, firefighters said.

Details of the single-alarm blaze were sketchy, but firefighters said they believe the victim was in the house alone.

A neighbor called the Fire Department at 10:03 p.m., according to Santa Ana fire dispatcher Mike Gates.

Three engines, two trucks and a paramedic unit responded, he said. The cause of the fire was under investigation, Gates said.
