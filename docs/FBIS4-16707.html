BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow 18 April, TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin urged on Monday overcoming the deadlock in Russia's relations with its independence-seeking republic of Chechnya. The president entrusted the government to hold consultations with representatives of Chechnya's state bodies as well as all its political movements, and basing on the results of these consultations, to draft a powers-sharing treaty between Moscow and Groznyy, the press service of the Russian president reported on Monday.
