November 9, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Wyse Technology Inc., San Jose, said it is in discussions with a third party regarding the potential acquisition of the company at a proposed cash purchase price of $10 a share . . . Pasadena-based Jacobs Engineering Group Inc. has applied for listing of its common stock on the New York Stock Exchange. The stock is currently traded on the American Stock Exchange under the symbol of JEC . . . The Chicago Mercantile Exchange, Reuters Holdings PLC and the Marche a Terme International de France finalized an agreement under which MATIF becomes a participant in GLOBEX, the automated after-hours transaction system for futures and options.
