November 10, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

COMMUNITY COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

ORANGE COAST BEATS RIVERSIDE TO CLINCH MEN'S SOCCER TITLE

Orange Coast College clinched the Orange Empire Conference men's soccer title with a 3-1 victory over Riverside Thursday at Riverside in the final regular season match.

Andy Strouse scored two goals and Lance Barker added another for OCC (14-4-2 overall and 8-1-1 in conference play). It was the fifth conference title for OCC, which also won championships in 1980-81-82, 1988, and last season.

In another Orange Empire Conference men's soccer:

Fullerton 4, Golden West 3 -- Fullerton (13-7-2, 6-3-1) jumped out to a 3-0 lead and Adrian Nawabi scored what turned out to be the winner. J.R. Ochs scored two goals for host Fullerton. Golden West ends 1-16-2, 0-8-2.

In South Coast Conference women's soccer:

Orange Coast 2, El Camino 1 -- Orange Coast (8-2-2) moved into sole possession of first place. Lara Crowley scored the winning goal one minute into second half. El Camino is 7-2-2 and in second.

In Orange Empire Conference women's volleyball:

Golden West 3, Saddleback 0 -- Maureen Flannigan had 13 kills and five aces as Golden West clinched the conference title at home with a 15-12, 15-5, 15-6. Golden West (15-3 overall and 11-0 in conference) has a two-match lead with one left.

Fullerton 3, Rancho Santiago 0 -- Lisa Papazian had eight kills to lead Fullerton to a 15-5, 15-8, 15-4 sweep at home.
