May 4, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

EARNINGS

CMS Enhancements' earnings for its third quarter ended March 31 rose 69% to $1.1 million from $661,000 a year earlier. The Tustin-based manufacturer of personal computer storage devices said revenue increased 48% to $52.8 million from $35.6 million a year ago. For the 9 months, earnings rose 47% to $3.6 million from $1.5 million. Revenue rose 44% to $157.6 million from $109.2 million.
