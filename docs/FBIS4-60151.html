BFN [Text] Chief Public Prosecutor Judge Munif 'Uwaydat has charged Samir Ja'ja', commander of the dissolved Lebanese Forces Party, with the bombing of the Sayyidat al-Najat church in Zuq Mikha'il. Justice Joseph Furayhah, the judicial investigator of this crime, forwarded the file of the interrogation of Ja'ja' to the Public Prosecutor's office, and will later hear his testimony when Ja'ja is a defendant. Judge Furayhah heard the testimony of retired Major Fu'ad Malik, the general commissioner of the dissolved Lebanese Forces Party, in his capacity as a witness in other cases.
