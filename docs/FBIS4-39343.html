BFN [Text] St. Petersburg June 8 ITAR-TASS -- The Council of the Inter-parliamentary Assembly of the Commonwealth of Independent States has met for a regular session in the Tauride Palace in St. Petersburg. The session is attended by the heads of all parliaments of the Commonwealth member-states, except Uzbekistan. In his opening address to the session, Chairman of the council Vladimir Shumeyko, who is also head of the Upper Chamber of the Russian Federal Parliament reported on the implementation of the assembly's earlier resolution on the role of the assembly and its function in strengthening the Commonwealth." A delegation from the Republic of Moldova led by Moldovan Parliament head Petre Luchinski is attending the session for the first time as its full-fledged member. Another first-comer is Tajikistan Parliament Chairman Emomali Rakhmonov. The delegation from the National Assembly of Azerbaijan is led by Ali Majlis Chairman Rasul Guliyev, that from Armenia by Parliament Chairman Babken Ararktsyan. Belarus is represented by Mechyslaw Hryb, and Georgia by Parliament Speaker Vakhtang Goguadze. The delegation from Kazakhstan is led by the chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the republic, Abish Kekilbayev and that from Kyrgyzstan by Chairman of the Supreme Soviet Meditkan Sherimkulov.
