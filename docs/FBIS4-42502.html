BFN [Text] Rostock (DDP/ADN) -- The FDP [Free Democratic Party] this morning approved its electoral program for the Bundestag election in October. In it, the liberals are recommending themselves to the voters as the party of achievement and committed defenders of the market economy. At the same time, the FDP deliberately took a position of liberal independence from its CDU/CSU [Christian Democratic Union/Christian Social Union] coalition partner in the areas of domestic and social policy and foreigners' affairs. The vast majority of the delegates voted in Rostock for the program; three voted against and there were three abstentions.
