December 3, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

LA QUINTA, TRABUCO HILLS PLAYOFF HOSTS

Paramount High School (12-1), the defending Division III champion, won the coin toss Saturday and will play host to El Toro (12-1) in the championship game at 7:30 p.m. Saturday at Cerritos College.

In Division VI, La Quinta (9-4) will be the home team against Corona del Mar (10-3) at 7:30 p.m. Friday at Orange Coast College.

For the second consecutive year, the Division VIII championship game will feature two Pacific Coast League teams. Trabuco Hills (12-1), the defending champion, will play host to Orange (10-2-1) at 7:30 p.m. Friday at Irvine High School.
