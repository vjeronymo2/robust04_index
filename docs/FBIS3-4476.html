Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hong Kong, March 29 (XINHUA) -- The Agricultural Bank of China, one of China's four principal state-owned specialized banks, opened a representative office here today. This was the bank's first representative office in Hong Kong and the fourth one opened abroad after Tokyo, Singapore and London. It is preparing for the establishment of the fifth one in New York, according to the bank's President Ma Yongwei. The opening of the office was in line with China's open policy and the bank's own strategic plans to internationalize its operations and increase its overseas presence, Ma said. He expressed that his bank would further strengthen, through co- operation, its relations with the financial community of Hong Kong and contribute to Hong Kong's prosperity and stability. As one of the top 50 banks in the world, the agricultural bank now has 54,000 branch offices with 540,000 staff. Its total assets has come to 1.05 trillion renminbi (184 billion U.S. dollars). It also has correspondent banking arrangements with 244 overseas banks in 71 countries and regions.
