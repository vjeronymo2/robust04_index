BFN ["BS-Press" report under rubric "According to Reports From Correspondents and Information Agencies": "The Moscow and Tbilisi Departments of Internal Affairs Plan To Cooperate"] [Text] According to information supplied by the press center of the Georgian Ministry of Internal Affairs, the Tbilisi and Moscow departments of internal affairs will sign an agreement in the near future on mutual assistance and cooperation. It is envisaged that it will provide for the exchange of special information, help in the search for criminals, and also material and technical assistance.
