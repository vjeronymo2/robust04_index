BFN [Text] Finance Minister Eduardo Aninat has said negotiations with the United States over an eventual free trade treaty should start in September or October of this year. Aninat said Chile will wait until the U.S. Congress empowers President Clinton to negotiate such an agreement. He added that official negotiations will be undertaken immediately after this. He explained that the Chilean Government will consult all national sectors prior to signing any agreement. He added that after assessing the impact of a trade agreement on the Chilean economy, the government will decide whether to negotiate a bilateral agreement with the United States or join NAFTA.
