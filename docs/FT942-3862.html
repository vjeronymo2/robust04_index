940613 FT 13 JUN 94 / Business Travel: Tunnel fire An explosion on Saturday caused a serious fire in a rail tunnel being built under the Great Belt waterway between Denmark's main islands at the mouth of the Baltic. Storebaelt A/S (Great Belt), the construction group responsible for the project, said the fire lasted eight hours before being brought under control. The tunnel is part of a 20bn crown (Dollars 3bn) road/rail/tunnel/bridge link between Zealand island and the European mainland, via Funen.
