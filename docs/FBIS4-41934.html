BFN [Text] A joint expanded sitting of the Supreme Kenges committees for economic reform and for finances and budget was held today. Members of the Cabinet of Ministers participated in its work. The program of the government was discussed. The program will be examined by the parliament and then endorsed by the president. The deputies paid attention to specific mechanisms for implementing some provisions of the program, especially in the field of credit and financial policy and monetary circulation. Minister of Economy Mars Urkumbayev and Uraz Dzhandosov, first deputy chairman of the national bank, commented on this. On the same day the committees of the Supreme Kenges discussed the updated republican budget for 1994. Deputy Prime Minister Tulegen Zhukeyev and ministers Derbisov of finance and Altynbek Sarsenbayev of press and mass media advised the deputies on the issue. Prime Minister Sergey Tereshchenko spoke at the sittings of the committees. The government's program and the updated 1994 republican budget will be discussed by the plenary session of the Supreme Kenges tomorrow.
