920114 FT 14 JAN 92 / Power station contract awarded THAMES Power, the independent power generator, has awarded a Pounds 370m turnkey contract for its 1000MW power station at Barking Reach, east London, to a consortium of Balfour Beatty Projects & Engineering and GEC Alsthom Power Plants. Balfour Beatty is part of BICC, the construction and engineering group that jointly owns Thames Power with Canadian Utilities, a subsidiary of Atco of Canada. The new station will include five 123MW gas turbines built by European Gas Turbines, a subsidiary of GEC Alsthom.
