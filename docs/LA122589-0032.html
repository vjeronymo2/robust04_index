December 25, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

TWO SEAMEN MISSING AS FREIGHTER SINKS

Two seamen were missing after a Romanian freighter sank off Spain's northwest coast Sunday, Spanish rescue services said.

A spokesman said the Tololovem went down after being smashed by high waves off Cape Finisterre.

A rescue helicopter plucked 14 of the ship's 16 Romanian crewmen from a lifeboat.

Among the survivors was the captain of the vessel.
