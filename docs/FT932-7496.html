930522 FT 22 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Lex nets Dollars 146.6m in Arrow share sale Lex Service, the motor distribution and leasing company, has sold 4.62m Arrow Electronics shares to a group of underwriters. The shares were sold at a public offering price of Dollars 32.50 (Pounds 21.10) each. Total proceeds to Lex will be Dollars 146.6m. Lex has also granted the underwriters a 30-day option to buy up to an additional 500,000 Arrow shares at the public offering price. If this option were exercised in full, Lex would no longer retain any interest in Arrow.
