Language: English Article Type:BFN [By Ian Graham, Press Association in Belfast] [Text] A bomb exploded in Northern Ireland early today -- just minutes after the midnight end of the IRA's Christmas ceasefire. The bomb went off in the Co [county] Tyrone village of Fintona at 12:16 a.m., said the Royal Ulster Constabulary. A spokesman said there were believed to be casualties after the fire brigade sent to the scene called for an ambulance. The target of the bombing was not known but was thought to be the local police station, which was closed at the time.
