Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 24 -- The 28th joint search for the American servicemen missing in action (MIA) during the war was conducted from Fb. 26 to March 22 in various provinces and cities in Vietnam. The US-Vietnamese joint teams have got a lot of information about MIAs and a considerable number of sets of US servicemens remains. The US side thanked and highly valued Vietnam's effective cooperation in this humanitarian issues.
