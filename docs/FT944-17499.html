941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / International Company News: Reshuffle at Chrysler The board of Chrysler, the US automotive group, has approved several changes to its operations to divide manufacturing responsibilities between its top managers, Reuter reports from Detroit. Chrysler named Mr Gary Henson to head all its North American vehicle assembly and stamping operations. Mr Henson, who left General Motors to join Chrysler earlier this year, will report to Mr Dennis Pawley, who remains executive vice-president of manufacturing. Mr Frank Ewaysyshyn will be vice-president of advance manufacturing engineering. Mr Shamel Rushwin will become vice-president of international manufacturing and mini-van assembly. He was previously head of mini-van platform assembly.
