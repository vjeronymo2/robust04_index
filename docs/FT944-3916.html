941210 FT 10 DEC 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Shares in Johannesburg followed Wall Street's decline in thin volume. The overall index shed 60.1, or 1.1 per cent, to 5,697.9, industrials 47.3 at 6,855.9 and golds 46.8 at 1,904.0. Among individual stocks Anglos lost R5.50 to R223, SAB R2.50 to R95.50 and Kloof R1.75 to R56.
