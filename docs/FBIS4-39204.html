BFN [Text] By an edict of the Georgian Cabinet of Ministers, Valerian Rostomis dze Asatiani [former culture minister] has been appointed the chief of the Cabinet of Ministers office. The Georgian Cabinet of Ministers relieved Leri Vladimeris dze Gigineishvili from the post of administrative manager of the Cabinet of Ministers due to his appointment as chairman of the Socio- Economic Committee of the Republic of Georgia.
