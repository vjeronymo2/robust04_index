940809 FT 09 AUG 94 / International Company News: SNC-Lavalin posts sharp increases SNC-Lavalin, a Canadian-based international engineering and construction group, posted sharp increases in second-quarter and half-year earnings thanks to improving margins and lower interest costs. First-half profit was CDollars 14.7m, or 94 cents a share, up from Dollars 8.8m, or 60 cents, a year earlier, on revenues of Dollars 397m, a rise of 11 per cent. Second-quarter profit equalled 57 cents, against 43 cents on revenues of Dollars 217m, up from Dollars 184m.
