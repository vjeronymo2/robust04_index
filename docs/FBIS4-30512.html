BFN [Text] On 21 June the government issued Decree No. 50/CT to regulate duties, authority, and organization of the Ministry of Marine Products. The decree consists of five articles. The first article says that the Ministry of Marine Products is authorized by the state to carry out state management function in marine products including rearing, catching, processing, protecting, and developing all marine product resources. Article No. 2 determines concrete duties and authority of the ministry. Article No. 3 determines the structure of the ministry consisting of the organizations that help the minister carry out state management role in the sector, and the professional organizations. Article No. 5 stresses that the minister of marine products, other ministers and deputy ministers from ministries and other ministerial level agencies, heads of government agencies, chairmen of people's committees of provinces and cities are responsible for implementing this decree.
