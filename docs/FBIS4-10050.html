BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] President Carlos Salinas de Gortari has welcomed Nicaraguan President Violeta Chamorro to Mexico on a three-day official visit. President Salinas met with President Chamorro at the National Presidential Palace. After listening to both countries' national anthems which were played in the palace gardens, President Salinas said during President Chamorro's visit the two leaders will expand several current agreements to provide greater prosperity and justice for both Mexicans and Nicaraguans. [Begin Salinas recording] During these times of world change, we must join efforts, increase trade, and complement each other's economies. We will move forward by taking specific action. The challenge is to take advantage of our capabilities, our enthusiasm, and the common causes that bond us. [end recording]
