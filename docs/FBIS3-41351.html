Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Feb. 18 KYODO -- Police said Friday [18 February] they have arrested a Colombian clothes dealer they suspect is a central figure in a drug trafficking ring on suspicion of possessing cocaine. Carlos Martinez, 30, a resident in Tokyo, was arrested at a Tokyo hotel on Wednesday for allegedly possessing 614.5 grams of cocaine with a street value of about 43 million yen.
