BFN [Excerpt] Teslic, 23 May (TANJUG)--TANJUG has learned from Serb military sources that members of the [Bosnian] Serb Republic Army routed a sabotage-terrorist group this morning on the Teslic front in the region of Vlasko Brdo. Three terrorists were killed and another two wounded and captured, while the remaining members of the group fled in the direction of Zeljezno Polje. The captured Muslim soldiers disclosed that strong forces were grouping in the region of Zeljezno Polje and Seric, southeast and south of Teslic, under the direct command of General Rasim Delic.
