Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 2 (KCNA) -- Papers here today observe the 17th anniversary of the proclamation of the great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya. The Libyan people have made strenuous efforts to build a new society, firmly defending the gains of the September first revolution under the banner of anti-imperialist independence, MINJU CHOSON in a signed article says, and continues: The Libyan people who set store by the national dignity and sovereignty of the country are accelerating economic construction, while increasing the country's defence capability to cope with the aggressive moves of imperialists. Libya, pursuing the non-alignment policy externally, is striving for the unity and cohesion of the Arab nation. The two peoples of Korea and Libya established and have favourably developed their friendly relations. Their mutual support and cooperation have been conducive to the building of a new society. The Korean people believe that the friendly and cooperative relations between Korea and Libya will further expand and develop in the future, and hope the Libyan people new success in the efforts to build a prosperous new society. NODONG SINMUN in a by-lined article says the Libyan people are making unremitting efforts to defend the sovereignty of the country and build a new society, smashing the manoeuvrings of imperialists against Libya.
