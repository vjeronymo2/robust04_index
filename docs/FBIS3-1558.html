Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Accompanied by Li Zhen, chairman of the Shandong Provincial People's Congress Standing Committee; Xu Xuemeng, vice chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee; Zhao Linshan, secretary general of the provincial people's congress standing committee; and leading comrades of Jinan city, Nong Duc Manh, chairman of the national assembly of Vietnam, and his party visited the Jinan light-duty motorbike group company and (Qinghe) Village; and went sightseeing on Baofuquan, Daming Lake, and Jinan Huanghe bridge on 25 February. On the same afternoon at Qilu Guesthouse, the provincial people's congress standing committee held a banquet in honor of the visiting group. Chairman Li Zhen made an ebullient speech at the banquet. He said: Chairman Nong Duc Manh's visit this time will promote the contacts and cooperation between Shandong Province and the Socialist Republic of Vietnam in the spheres of economy, trade, science and technology, and culture. The visiting group left Jinan for Jiangsu Province by train on the evening of 25 February.
