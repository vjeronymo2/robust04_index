BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 11 (KCNA) -- A national meeting of activists in natural scientific research was held here over April 8-10. The meeting reviewed the achievements and experiences gained in natural scientific research in the past and discussed the tasks and measures to effect a new turn in scientific research conducive to implementing the party's revolutionary economic strategy. The meeting was attended by Kang Song-san, Politburo member of the C.C. [Central Committee], Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] and Premier of the Administration Council, Kim Chung-nin, secretary of the WPK Central Committee, and officials concerned. The meeting pointed out that to thoroughly implement the agriculture-first policy it is necessary to deepen research work contributable to carrying on the rural technical revolution on a higher stage, accelerating the green revolution and developing new branches of agro-science and to promote the industrialization and modernization of agriculture. It laid stress on gaining new success in the research work to successfully solve the scientific and technical matters arising in carrying out the light industry-first policy and to develop foreign trade. The meeting called for solving the urgent scientific and technical bottlenecks, developing the electronics, mechanic engineering, heat engineering of new materials, biology and chemistry onto a higher stage, deepening the research on such new scientific and technical fields as super-pressure, ultracryogenics and laser and putting great efforts to developing basic sciences as mathematics and dynamics. A letter of pledge to the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il was adopted at the meeting.
