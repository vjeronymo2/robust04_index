BFN [Report by Katarina Andric] [Excerpt] The republican commissioner for refugees today held a news conference for foreign and domestic correspondents. There were several topics on the agenda: the revision of the refugees' status in Serbia, humanitarian operations, and cooperation with other international humanitarian institutions. The correspondents were addressed by Bratislava Morina, commissioner for refugees. Katarina Andric reports: [Andric] There are approximately 500,000 refugees in the Republic of Serbia. The majority of them live with Serbian families. The greatest number of refugees are women and children. Almost 200,000 of them are under 18 years of age, 1,200 children are without parental care, and 10,000 babies have been born in exile. [passage omitted]
