930616 FT 16 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Sheriff Sheriff Holdings, the USM-quoted plant hire group, reported pre-tax profits sharply higher at Pounds 465,000, compared with Pounds 161,000, in the six months to March 31. The result - achieved on turnover ahead 21 per cent at Pounds 5.7m - was achieved by strict financial control of the core business and recent acquisitions, said Mr Richard Dunn, chairman. The interim dividend is raised from 1p to 1.25p, payable from earnings per share of 3.5p (1.7p).
