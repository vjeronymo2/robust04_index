940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / International Company News: Sakura Bank links with US accountants Sakura Bank, one of Japan's biggest banks, is linking up with the US accounting firm Arthur Andersen to help meet the growing demand in Japan for advice about investing abroad, AP-DJ reports from Tokyo. Under the agreement, Sakura will refer consulting requests from its Japanese customers to Chicago-based Arthur Andersen, which has offices worldwide. Arthur Andersen's strength in consulting about China is a key reason for the deal, a Sakura official said.
