930810 FT 10 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Dragon Oil sets up link in Philippines Dragon Oil, the oil and gas explorer, has joined forces with a Philippines consortium in a Far East drilling programme. Under the scheme, Dragon has farmed out interests in Korea and Thailand and acquired a minimum 13.75 per cent stake in a Philippines licence. The farm-out is with two Philippine companies, Basic Petroleum & Minerals and Palawan Oil and Gas Exploration.
