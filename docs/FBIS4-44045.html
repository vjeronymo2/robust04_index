BFN [Excerpt] President Rauf Denktas today received Joe Clark, UN secretary general's special envoy in Cyprus. Attending the meeting, which was held at the President's Office at 1100 this morning, were Ozker Ozgur, state minister and deputy prime minister; Atay Ahmet Rasit, foreign affairs and defense minister; Ozer Koray, under secretary of the Foreign Affairs and Defense Ministry; presidential advisers Necati Munir Ertekun and Ergun Olgun; and Gustave Feissel, UN secretary general's assistant special envoy in Cyprus.
