930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Alliance Trust lifts net assets to Pounds 18.46p Alliance Trust had a net asset value of Pounds 18.46p per stock unit at July 31 1993. The figure compared with values of Pounds 17.79p at the investment trust's January year-end and Pounds 14.48p at end-July 1992. After the preference dividend, attributable revenue for the six month period amounted to Pounds 12.1m, up from Pounds 11.5m, for earnings ahead to 24.1p (22.74p) per stock unit. The interim dividend is lifted 0.5p to 14.5p; directors also anticipate some increase in the final dividend, set last time at 31p.
