930407 FT 07 APR 93 / UK Company News: Aran Energy improves to IPounds 2.61m Aran Energy, the Dublin-based oil and gas exploration company, reported a pre-tax profit of IPounds 2.61m for the year to end-December, against losses of IPounds 379,000. The outcome was achieved on sales up IPounds 15.1m to IPounds 45.1m. Earnings per share came out at 1p (0.14p losses). Aran said 1992 also saw completion of a Dollars 197.5m (Pounds 139m) loan facility which would provide funding for the Alba and Gryphon projects.
