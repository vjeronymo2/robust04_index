940203 FT 03 FEB 94 / Letters to the Editor: Crucial word missing on China Sir, While I found your article on the fortunes of Chinese industry interesting ('Long leash for a runaway economy', February 2) it seemed to miss out one key word: democracy. Or are we still at the stage where we only criticise the human rights abuses of those countries with which we do not wish to do business? Keith Flett, 38 Mitchley Road, Tottenham, London N17 9HG
