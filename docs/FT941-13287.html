940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / UK Company News: M&G Dual net assets improve Net asset value per capital share of M&G Dual Trust rose from Pounds 24 to Pounds 31.99 over the 1993 year. Available revenue edged ahead from Pounds 3.97m to Pounds 4.04m. Earnings per income share emerged at 70.34p (69.09p) and a final dividend of 39.79p makes a 70.34p (69.1p) total.
