Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Vladivostok, 14 Mar -- After his meeting with U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher today, Russian Federation Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev left Vladivostok for Tajikistan. Kozyrev told journalists before leaving by air that a meeting of foreign ministers of Russia, Tajikistan, and Uzbekistan, and also representatives of Kyrgyzstan and Turkmenistan will take place in Dushanbe on Tuesday. Emomali Rakhmanov, chairman of the Tajikistani Supreme Soviet, will receive the participants in the meeting. Russia's foreign minister is also scheduled to make a trip to the Tajik-Afghan border.
