BFN [Text] Dr. Nabil Sha'th, Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat's adviser, made a statement to reporters today before the negotiations began in Cairo accusing the Israelis of wasting time by discussing the details of the withdrawal from Gaza and Jericho and the implementation of the limited Palestinian autonomy there. He added that by constantly placing impediments before the Palestinian negotiators, the Israelis are making it impossible to sign the agreement on 13 April, the deadline set by the Palestinian- Israeli Declaration of Principles.
