941219 FT 19 DEC 94 / Arts (Openings): Vienna The magic of the waltz fills the air over the next two weeks as the music of the Strauss family (Johann, the younger) once again sweeps Viennese society off its feet. The festive season culminates in the Imperial Ball on New Year's Eve and the Vienna Philharmonic Orchestra's New Year's Day concert, presided over by Zubin Mehta and televised worldwide.
