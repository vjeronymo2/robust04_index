931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Lister deficit cut to Pounds 268,000 Reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 268,000 were announced by Lister for the six months to September 25. Losses last time for the textile products maker which also has interests in property, engineering and insurance broking, amounted to Pounds 1.62m. Turnover rose from Pounds 16.5m to Pounds 17.6m with textiles accounting for Pounds 16.4m (Pounds 15.5m). Operating losses were Pounds 57,000 (Pounds 509,000) with only the insurance activities showing a profit. Losses per share were cut to 1.85p (9.97p).
