February 18, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

CSUN TO PLAY HOST TO ST. MARY'S IN BASEBALL

The Cal State Northridge baseball team, which suffered its first loss of the season Friday to undefeated Cal State Long Beach, 15-2, will play another Division I opponent today when the Matadors play host to St. Mary's at 1 p.m. The teams will meet again Sunday at noon.

CSUN (4-1) will begin California Collegiate Athletic Assn. play Friday at Cal State Los Angeles.
