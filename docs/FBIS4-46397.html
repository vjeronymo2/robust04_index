BFN [Text] Two persons were killed and 25 others injured in a bomb explosion in a coach at the Delhi-bound Samta Express coming from Vishakhapatnam yesterday evening. The incident occurred near Rajnandgaon in Madhya Pradesh. Twelve of the injured persons were admitted to the Civil Hospital at Rajnandgaon, while others were discharged after first aid. The Central Railway Authorities have pressed into service their medical teams and are arranging a replacement for the affected coach. An emergency counter has been set up at Nagpur with phone numbers of 532417, repeat, 532417 and 532597, repeat, 532597 to handle inquiries from relations of passengers and render assistance. A relief train has also been sent from Raipur to the site.
