BFN [Text] Tashkent, 19 April (XINHUA) -- Premier Li Peng went to visit personnel of Chinese embassy in Uzbekistan tonight. Li Peng briefed them on the situation in China and his visit to Uzbekistan. He said that his trip was very successful and that he had extensively exchanged opinions with President Karimov about the relations between the two countries. He expressed his hopes that all comrades at the embassy will try to do their work better and make new contributions to the development of China-Uzbekistan ties.
