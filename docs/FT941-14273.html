940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / Possible relations Australian government officials yesterday admitted they had been approached by North Korea, over the possibility of restoring diplomatic relations, severed unilaterally by the North Koreans almost two decades ago, Nikki Tait reports from Sydney. But Mr Gordon Bilney, acting minister for foreign affairs, said the talks were 'very informal and very tentative discussions. . . essentially diplomatic-level consultations'. 'There haven't been political contacts of any kind - it's not time for those,' he said.
