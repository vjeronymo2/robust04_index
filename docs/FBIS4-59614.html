BFN [Text] Algiers, May 15 (AFP) -- The chairman of the Arab-Islamic Rally party has been murdered after being abducted, press reports said here Sunday [15 May]. Laid Grine, 49, who was also an Algiers lawyer, was found Saturday morning near Algiers with his throat cut, the reports said. The lawyer had been captured the previous day by unidentified abductors from his house at Bordj El Mokhfi east of Algiers. There has been no official response to his kidnapping and murder, but press reports said he had succeeded Wednesday in gaining the acquittal of an Islamic fundamentalist being tried by one of the country's special "anti-terrorism" courts.
