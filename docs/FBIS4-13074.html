BFN [Text] The Golboddin-Dostam militia once again embarked on aggression against the Islamic army's positions and put the residential regions of Kabul city under a rain of rocket and mortar fire. The military correspondent of the BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY reports that the criminal Golboddin-Dostam militia, following yesterday's failed attack -- launching rockets and firing heavy weapons -- last night also launched hundreds of rockets from the type of SAKAR, BM-12, BM-21, and mortar mine on residential regions of Kabul city. As a result of the launched rockets, which mostly landed on the residential regions of the 11th, 10th, fourth and second wards of the city, a number of our compatriots were martyred and a number were seriously injured. As a result of this lunatic action of Hekmatyar, in addition to loss of life, a number of residential houses, and government buildings located in the above wards were either destroyed or damaged. One mosque located in Khairkhana III was demolished. The report adds that the infamous Golboddin-Dostam militia last night embarked on infantry aggression against the positions of the Islamic state's forces from the direction of Bala Hesar. It was foiled in the same way as their other unsuccessful attacks. With the blessing of Almighty God, courageous resistance by the mojahedin and soldiers of the Islamic army, the enemy was forced to retreat. A responsible security source put the enemy losses at 20 dead and 10 injured. Military observers believe the recent actions by the Golboddin-Dostam mercenary militiamen in Kabul are an effort to recompense and disguise the repeated defeats of militia in the north and to disrupt the peace process in the country. The murderer and adventurist militiamen of Golboddin-Dostam can only see their political-military survival in fighting and in continuation of tension. This claim can be entirely substantiated by Golboddin Hekmatyar's (?utterance) made yesterday at a meeting with the peace delegation consisting of ulema from Saudi Arabia, Pakistan, Bangladesh and some Jehadi leaders and personalities of Afghanistan. In reply to a question by a peace-seeking ulema who asked Hekmatyar to refrain, for the sake of holy Koran, from further bloodshed, destruction, and further defaming of Jehad and Islam, and asked him to open supply routes for the Muslim and innocent people of Kabul and to stop firing blind rockets, Hekmatyar with anger and disrespect said: I will fight for another 25 years against the Islamic sovereignty and people. And I would not reverse until I gain power. This is happening at a time when esteemed professor Borhanoddin Rabbani, president of the Islamic State of Afghanistan, despite unforgivable crimes committed by Golboddin, has displayed flexibility and [words indistinct] in honor of the peace delegation from the fraternal countries of Saudi, Pakistan, and Bangladesh, as well as Jehadi leaders and personalities of Afghanistan. Relying on the usual strategy of the Islamic state, which is resolving the country's issues by talks and understanding, Rabbani declared a unilateral cease-fire in Kabul. But the Golboddin-Dostam militia, ignoring the cease-fire, have been attacking the positions of the Islamic army and residential regions during the past two days with heavy weapons and infantry forces. The Islamic state's forces responded to these attacks by defending their positions and safeguarding the life and property of the civil population in Kabul city.
