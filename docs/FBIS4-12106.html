BFN [Excerpt] Brother Abu-al-Lutf [Faruq Qaddumi], acting chairman of the governing body of the Palestinian Economic Council for Development and Reconstruction, arrived in Vietnam yesterday for an official visit as part of his tour of Southeast Asia and China. Brother Abu-al-Lutf arrived in Hanoi after a three-day official visit to Bangkok during which he met Thailand's foreign minister. A spokesman for the Thai Foreign Ministry announced that agreement has been reached in principle between the PLO and Thailand on opening a PLO office in Bangkok. He said that the two sides have agreed on the opening of the office, but have not yet agreed on the details. [passage omitted]
