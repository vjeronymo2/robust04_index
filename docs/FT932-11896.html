930430 FT 30 APR 93 / UK Company News: Folkes Folkes Group, the Midlands-based property, engineering and building products company, showed a fall in profits from Pounds 2.5m to Pounds 1m pre-tax for the 1992 year. A reduction in the proposed final dividend to 0.925p leaves a 1.5p (2.375p) total. Earnings per share fell to 1.25p (4.93p). Property profits were unchanged at Pounds 2.4m but those of the engineering division fell from Pounds 1m to Pounds 34,000. Building products incurred losses of Pounds 1.23m (Pounds 838,000). Group turnover declined from Pounds 43.2m to Pounds 39.8m.
