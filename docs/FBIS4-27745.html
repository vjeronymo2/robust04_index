BFN [Text] Prime Minister Chuan Likphai warmly received Zheng Hongye, chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and his 16-member private trade delegation at the National Assembly yesterday afternoon. The prime minister also was photographed with the delegation. Chuan warmly welcomed the delegation to Thailand. He said that the warm reception extended to him during his visit to China last August would be engraved in his memory forever. He is satisfied with the smooth implementation of the nine agreements and contacts signed during his visit. Chuan said that many Thai businessmen were investing in China. He hoped that more Chinese entrepreneurs will invest in Thailand. He believed that the visit by the Chinese delegation would further promote friendly Sino-Thai relations. Chief delegate Zheng Hongye expressed his heartfelt gratitude for the cordial audience granted by the prime minister, although he was very busy. He also seconded the prime minister's remarks. He said that amidst rapid economic growth in China, Chinese entrepreneurs are interested in more investment cooperation and development exchanges with their Thai counterparts.
