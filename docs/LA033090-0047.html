March 30, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

JUDGMENTS ON THE KCBS 'OFFICE ROMANCE'

I hope that Bree Walker and Jim Lampley will have a successful marriage, but I don't like what it says about our country's moral climate when the one person at KCBS who thought it was wrong for people who are married to be dating is referred to as "old-fashioned."

CELIA SPIVEY, Big Bear Lake
