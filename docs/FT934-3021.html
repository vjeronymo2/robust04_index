931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Mercury Asset trust launch raises Pounds 425.8m Mercury Asset Management said yesterday it had raised Pounds 425.8m for its World Mining Trust, making it the largest ever investment trust launch in the UK. A total of 380m shares had been placed - with warrants attached on a 1-for-5 basis - with institutions and others. A further 45.8m shares were applied for under the public offer, and will be allocated in full. The trust, which will be managed by Mr Julian Baring, will invest in companies which produce gold and base metals. Dealings are expected to start on December 15.
