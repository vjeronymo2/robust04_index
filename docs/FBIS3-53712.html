Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Manama, 16 Jan (WAKH) -- Staff Lieutenant General Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Ahmad Al Khalifah, defense minister and deputy commander in chief of the State of Bahrain, left Manama early this morning for the United Kingdom on an official visit lasting several days. The Bahraini minister will hold talks with British Defense Secretary Malcollm Rifkind on ways to enhance military ties between the two friendly nations and issues of mutual concern.
