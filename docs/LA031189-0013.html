March 11, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

CONTROVERSY OVER BILINGUAL EDUCATION IN L.A. SCHOOLS

If bilingual teaching has proven to make a positive difference then our schools should have it. But why pay up to $5,000 a year more to just these teachers? Instead, let's raise the salaries of all L.A. teachers to the point where Los Angeles attracts the best and keeps the best!

SUE MATUS

Westchester
