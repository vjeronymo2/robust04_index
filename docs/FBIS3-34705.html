Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin informed Knesset Member Moshe Qatzav, chairman of the Likud Knesset faction, that he adheres to his position that a Palestinian state will not come into being between Israel and Jordan. Our correspondent Hayim Hecht reports that Rabin was replying to Qatzav's request to support a motion to be submitted by the Likud this coming week, requesting that the Knesset pass a resolution stating that a Palestinian state will not come into being.
