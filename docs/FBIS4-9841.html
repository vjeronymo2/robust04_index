BFN [Report by Diego Andres de Gatica] [Text] U.S. citizen (June Diane Tomarweeks), 54, has irreversible brain damage. She is in the intensive care ward of Herrera Llerandi Hospital, Guatemala City. The medical report states her condition has stabilized, but she will have to remain in intensive care. Physicians have reported they had planned to send her to the United States, but the trip will have to wait at least four or five days. A mob that confused her with a child kidnapper attacked the U.S. citizen in San Cristobal Alta Verapaz. The hospital information indicates her condition has stabilized but that the damage to her brain is irreversible.
