July 2, 1989, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

BALANCE COVERAGE

I am intrigued by the fact that your reporter felt it necessary to stress the point that only one of the candidates running for the 76th Assembly seat, Stephen Thorne of Escondido, is an atheist ("11 Candidates Seek Seat Held by Bradley," June 27).

Why weren't the religious affiliations, or lack of religious affiliations, of the 10 other candidates mentioned?

Is this the kind of one-sided reporting that we are to expect in the upcoming election? Let's see some balanced campaign reporting for a change.

JON GARTH MURRAY

Escondido
