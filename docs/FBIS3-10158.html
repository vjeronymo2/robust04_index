Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Global TV News" newscast] [Text] The Narcotics Police arrested nine drug traffickers, five of them Colombians, in the locality of Bella Vista, San Martin Department, today. The detainees were members of a band headed by Colombian drug trafficker Waldo Arias, known as "Ministro" who, according to police reports, was associated with Demetrio Chavez Penaherrera, alias Vaticano, who is in jail at the El Callao Naval Base.
