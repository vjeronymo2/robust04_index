September 26, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO INDUSTRY NOTES

TCS Enterprises has reported record earnings of $210,000 for the third quarter ended July 31, up from $37,000 during the like quarter the year before. Revenue rose to $2.2 million, up from $1.7 million.

Net income rose to $748,000 for the nine-month period ended July 31, up from $394,000 the year before. Revenue for the nine-month period rose to $6.1 million, up from $4.9 million.
