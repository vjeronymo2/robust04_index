930116 FT 16 JAN 93 / Powerful earthquake rocks Japan A powerful earthquake measuring 7.5 on the Richter scale rocked northern Japan yesterday, injuring at least 95 people but apparently causing only minor damage, authorities said, Reuter reports from Tokyo. The tremor, the strongest to hit Japan for almost 11 years, was felt across the country from the Pacific coast to the Sea of Japan, the national meteorological agency said.
