September 3, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

DAN QUAYLE

I'm not a big fan of Quayle, but I do feel that Conrad's cartoon (Quayle under his desk on the phone saying, "Dad, they're calling up the reserves!" Aug. 23) was repulsive. For many, many years I have read editorial cartoons, liked some, disliked others, but this is the first one that has turned my stomach.

ROBERT F. KRAMER

Lompoc
