July 30, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

PACIFIC NOTES

Mitsubishi Corp. has contributed $500,000 to the University of Pittsburgh's Joseph Katz Graduate School of Business to establish the Mitsubishi Doctoral Fellowship Program in International Management.

Mitsubishi earlier this year took a majority equity position in Aristech Chemical Corp. of Pittsburgh through a management-led buyout.

Aristech Chairman Thomas Marshall in June was named the Katz School's Distinguished Alumnus for 1990.
