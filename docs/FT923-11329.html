920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Pittencrieff buys US mobile radio group Pittencrieff, the Edinburgh-based oil and gas and mobile communications group, has acquired J&J group for some Dollars 5m(Pounds 2.61m) of which Dollars 2.08m will be met by the issue of 498,396 shares. The group comprises J&J Systems and Mustang Communications, and provides a specialist mobile radio system throughout Texas. In 1991 the group produced revenue of Dollars 3.46m and gross profit of Dollars 1.89m. Pittencrieff says it has identified substantial potential savings in overheads in the acquisition that can be quickly implemented in the near future.
