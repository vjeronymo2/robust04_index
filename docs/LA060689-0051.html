June 6, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: FAMILY NOTES

Country music singer Reba McEntire was married over the weekend to Narvel Blackstock, her manager, it was announced Monday. The couple was married Saturday in Lake Tahoe at a ceremony attended by family members only. McEntire was divorced in January, 1988, from her first husband, rodeo star Charlie Battles. Blackstock also had been married once. ALEENE MacMINN
