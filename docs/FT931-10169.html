930210 FT 10 FEB 93 / Toyota sales chief resigns MR SIMON Foster is resigning after 12 months as chief executive of Toyota (GB), the sales and distribution company owned jointly by Inchcape Group and Japan's largest carmaker. His resignation takes effect at the end of March. He was director of the Society of Motor Manufacturers and Traders before joining Toyota. He said he 'would prefer a somewhat wider management role in which my industrial experience could also be useful'. Mr Foster's successor will be Mr Trevor Taylor, the deputy chief executive.
