931110 FT 10 NOV 93 / Guilty plea in Denny case A defendant in the Reginald Denny beating case agreed to plead guilty to two misdemeanour charges and one felony count yesterday, in return for a sentence of lengthy probation, Reuter reports from Los Angeles. Mr Antoine Miller, 21, served 17 months in jail while awaiting trial following the beating of Denny, a white truck driver, by a black mob at the start of last year's riots.
