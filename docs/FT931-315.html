930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / Opposition leader's sentence cut Malawi's Supreme Court yesterday reduced the prison sentence of opposition leader Chakufwa Chihana from two years to nine months, enraging the opposition that had hoped he would be freed, Reuter reports from Johannesburg. An opposition official repeated allegations that prison guards were forcing Chihana to work as a labourer despite his poor health. Mr Chihana, 52, is a leading trade unionist and opposition campaigner.
