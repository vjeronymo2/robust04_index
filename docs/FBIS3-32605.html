Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Good Morning From Mexico" newscast] [Text] Government Secretary Jorge Carpizo granted an interview during which he spoke about an agreement for peace, democracy, and justice signed by eight political parties. He said the agreement signed by eight of the nine registered political parties to contribute to the peace process and honest elections in 1994 is well on its way to fulfillment. [Begin Carpizo recording] No party will issue reports on its own. News and reports will be issued jointly. [end recording] He said that the gentlemen's agreement between leaders and presidential candidates is intended to protect and consolidate the peace agreement. Carpizo added that special congressional sessions can be held to reform the Federal Code of Electoral Institutions and Procedures if there is a consensus among the parties to do so. Carpizo was interviewed after a meeting at the government secretariat with leaders of the National Teachers' Union headed by Elba Esther Gordillo.
