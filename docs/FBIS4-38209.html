BFN [Text] The preparatory session of the provinces' delegates Shura was held today in Herat. The correspondent of the BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY reports from Herat that today's preparatory session was attended by esteemed General Mohammad Ismail Khan, general amir of the south-western zone and governor of Herat; esteemed Taj Mohammad Qaribaba, governor general of Ghazni Province; esteemed Mowlawi 'Abdol Latif Mansur, governor of Paktia Province; and delegates from the provinces. In assessing the country's situation at this meeting, esteemed Mohammad Ismail Khan said: Our major responsibility is to eliminate the enemies of the Islamic revolution, firmness vis-a-vis the difficulties, and to convene the provinces' delegates Shura for the restoration of peace in the country. The formal sessions will soon be opened.
