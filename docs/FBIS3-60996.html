Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] During operations conducted against terrorist organizations in Izmir last year, security forces captured 366 terrorists together with large quantities of weapons and ammunition. Of these terrorists, 237 were formally arrested by the Izmir State Security Court. According to information received from the Izmir Security Directorate, during these operations, antiterrorist teams seized 30 rifles, 49 pistols of various calibers, 1,575 rounds of ammunition, 95 hand grenades, 90 fuses, 14 hand-made bombs, 36 dynamite sticks, one 300-cm-long time fuse, and 36 kg of chemicals used in the manufacturing of bombs. Also seized during the operations were four hand radios, three photocopy machines, two duplicating machines, four typewriters, two pairs of binoculars, and one computer.
