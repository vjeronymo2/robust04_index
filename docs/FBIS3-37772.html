Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Khasbulatov's Guard Arrested"] [Text] Estonian security police and an MVD special subunit have arrested Aleksandr Denisov, who was on the wanted list for organizing mass disturbances in Moscow last October, in the Estonian city of Johvi. When he was arrested Denisov, who guarded Khasbulatov and Rutskoy, was found to be carrying a Tokarev pistol. The detainee testified that he was preparing "a base for around 20 people connected with the October events in Russia" in northeast Estonia. Before moving to Russia Denisov worked in the Estonian city of Kohtla-Jarve in the Criminal Investigations Department and later in Tallinn in the Organized Crime Department.
