March 15, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

UPS Orders 10 Boeing Jets: United Parcel Service has ordered 10 more 757-200PF freight aircraft, Boeing Commercial Airplanes of Seattle confirmed. The order is valued at about $500 million. The jet cargo operator in 1985 ordered 20 757PFs with options on 15. The new order represents the exercise of 10 of those options. Deliveries from the new order are to begin in August, 1991, and extend through 1992.
