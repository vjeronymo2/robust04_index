Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Aleksandr Shalnev: "United States Will Provide 30 Million for Destruction of Russian Chemical Weapons"] [Text] The United States will provide nearly $30 million to set up a research center in Russia which would devise the most effective methods of monitoring the destruction of chemical weapons. Accord on this subject was finally reached during U.S. President Clinton's recent visit to Moscow and was formalized by a protocol which is confidential at the moment. According to informed sources, the center will be based at Moscow's Vernadskiy Institute of Geochemistry and Analytical Chemistry. Over one-half of the sum promised by the Americans will go on instruments and equipment.
