930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / UK Company News: GWR improves 25% to Pounds 317,000 GWR Group, the independent radio contractor, raised pre-tax profits by 25 per cent from Pounds 254,000 to Pounds 317,000 in the six months to March 31. The outcome was achieved on turnover up from Pounds 3.46m to Pounds 4.08m. Earnings per share rose 19 per cent to 6.8p and the interim dividend is raised to 4p (3p).
