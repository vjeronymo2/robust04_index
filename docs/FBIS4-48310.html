BFN [Text] PWV [Pretoria, Witwatersrand, Vereeniging] Premier Tokyo Sexwale announcing his 10-member cabinet today said he had also created three extra positions. He said Professor Ben Turok would head a desk responsible for implementing the African National Congress's Reconstruction and Development Program. The other two desks, the youth affairs and gender rights, were still unmanned. The executive committee comprised seven members from the ANC and three from the National Party.
