BFN [Text] President Ali Hassan Mwinyi has carried out a minor cabinet reshuffle. He has appointed a junior minister, two new regional commissioners, changed the jobs of two regional commissioners, and transferred one regional commissioner. A State House statement said Mtwara Regional Commissioner Sylvia Kate Kamba has been appointed deputy minister in the Ministry of Social Development, Women, and Children. National Planning Commission Commissioner Nsa Kaisi has been appointed the new regional commissioner of Mtwara region to succeed Kate Kamba. President Mwinyi has also appointed Mahange MP, Gibson Itatiro, as the new regional commissioner of Morogoro Region replacing Yusuf Makamba, who has been transferred to Kigoma Region to replace Sebastian Kinyondo who has been appointed national commissioner of planning. Mr. Kinyondo replaces Pius Msekwa who was recently elected speaker of the Union Parliament. The statement said the changes will take effect immediately.
