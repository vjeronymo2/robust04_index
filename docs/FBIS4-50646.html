BFN [Text] Hong Kong, May 20 (XINHUA) -- The Airport Committee under the Sino-British Liaison Group (JLG) met here today to discuss arrangements for the airport and airport railway projects. The Chinese team is led by senior Chinese representative to the JLG Guo Fengmin and the British team by Senior British Representative to the JLG Hugh Davies. The last meeting of the airport committee was held last August. Prior to the opening of today's meeting, both sides expressed to reporters the hope that the meeting could make progress.
