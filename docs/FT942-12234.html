940430 FT 30 APR 94 / UK Company News: Champneys health group changes hands Champneys, the health group, has been acquired by Fitness & Leisure Holdings, a holding company which manages Inglewood Health Hydro in the UK and a Canadian leisure development. The price is undisclosed. Champneys Group includes Champneys at Tring, the Spa at Gleneagles and the London Club at Le Meridien Hotel, London. Over the years it has benefited from substantial capital improvements by both Allied Investments and Guinness.
