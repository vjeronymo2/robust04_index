Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A delegation of the Vietnamese Ministry of Heavy Industry led by Minister Tran Lum has paid a visit to China to broaden bilateral cooperation, particularly in engineering and metallurgy. During the reception given to the Vietnamese delegation, Chinese Vice Premier Li Lanqing highly appreciated the plan on extension of the Thai Nguyen iron and steel complex in Vietnam, considering it an important project to promote economic cooperation between the two countries.
