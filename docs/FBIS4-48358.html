BFN [Text] President of the Republic Joaquim Chissano will this morning meet with the Nampula Provincial Government. Chissano arrived in Nampula this morning for a working visit and will afterward leave for Cabo Delgado Province. The president of the Republic is accompanied by Defense Minister Alberto Chipande, Minister in the Presidency Feliciano Gundana, Information Minister Rafael Maguni, Construction and Water Minister Joao Salomao, and Culture and Youth Minister Jose Mateus Catupa.
