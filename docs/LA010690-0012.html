January 6, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BEST-AND-WORST LIST GETS A FEW ADDITIONS

Larry Stewart was 99.9% correct. I reserve the other .1% because he omitted Ken Brett. Brett can be informative for a spell, but when he was relieving Al Conin for a spell, I didn't have the foggiest idea where the ball was going or what happened until I read the paper the next day.

JOHN A. MARTIN, Victorville
