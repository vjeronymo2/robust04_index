Language: English Article Type:BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 460] [Text] Vilnius, March 18, ELTA -- Deputy Defence Minister Gediminas Pulokas returned from Bonn on Friday [18 March]. He signed Lithuanian-German agreement on mutual help in cases of calamities. The agreement also provides for main volunteer aid conditions which encompass sending rescue groups, experts and aid materials. The main directions of the cooperation were discussed during the meeting of representatives of the both sides which was held on March 16. Lithuanians were invited to participate in the joint civilian security training exercises which will be held at the beginning of May.
