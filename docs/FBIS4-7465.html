BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Lojze Peterle has sent a letter to UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali and foreign ministers of the UN Security Concil member-states, asking that the UN Security Council lift the arms embargo. The minister pointed out that the situation in Slovenia is perfectly peaceful, that Slovenia is a sovereign and independent country and a UN member, which consistently meets all international obligations. Our country demonstrates a high level of political stability and observation of human rights. It is a member of the Council of Europe, has signed the Partnership for Peace plan, and is just about to commence negotiations on associate membership in the European Union, Minister Peterle among other things emphasized in his letter.
