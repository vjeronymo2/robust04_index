930329 FT 29 MAR 93 / First Channel Tunnel freight wagons arrive at Folkstone The first freight wagons for transporting heavy goods vehicles through the Channel tunnel have arrived at the terminal near Folkestone. Built in Italy, they will make up shuttles of 28 wagons, each carrying one HGV, which will run up to three times an hour. About 8.5m tonnes of freight are expected to be carried in the first year
