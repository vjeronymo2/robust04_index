February 12, 1989, Sunday, Southland Edition

BAKER, SHEVARDNADZE TO MEET NEXT MONTH

Secretary of State James A. Baker III is to meet Soviet Foreign Minister Eduard A Shevardnadze in Vienna on March 7 for a get-acquainted session, the State Department said. Friday.

Baker is also scheduled to attend the opening in Vienna of new multinational talks on reducing conventional armed forces in Europe set for the same day.
