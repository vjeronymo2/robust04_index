940923 FT 23 SEP 94 / Defection reaction Party leaders shrugged off the defection to Labour yesterday of Mr Tony Armstrong, the party's candidate for Coventry South-East. The timing of the announcement of his switch to Labour added to the embarrassment of Liberal Democrats at the end of a conference dominated by uncertainty over their electoral future. Mr Matthew Taylor, MP for Truro, said Mr Armstrong was no loss because he had been 'inactive for the last two years'.
