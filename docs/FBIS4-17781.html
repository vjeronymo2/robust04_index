BFN [Text] Stepanakert, 26 April (SNARK)--This afternoon the situation became aggravated on north-eastern Mardakertskiy and eastern Agdamskiy direction of Karabakh front. Press-service of the Karabakh Defence Army's Staff told SNARK that here Azerbaijani troops, supporting by military aircraft and great number of armoured hardware, advanced having the aim to return the recently lost positions. By this evening furious fighting did not cease. According to the same source, Azerbaijani side has lost a tank T-72 and about 40 Azerbaijani soldiers were killed. Karabakh side, which controlled the situation confidently, has one killed and six wounded.
