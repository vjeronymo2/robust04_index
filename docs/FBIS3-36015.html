Language: English Article Type:BFN [By BELINFORM-TASS] [Text] Minsk February 1 TASS -- Myechyslaw Hryb, who was elected the leader of the country after Stanislav Shushkevich got a no-confidence vote from the Belarussian parliament, replaced the former speaker also in his position of chairman of the Constitutional Commission. The republican parliament voted for that on Tuesday [1 February].
