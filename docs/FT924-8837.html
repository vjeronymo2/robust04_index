921109 FT 09 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Chinese group in Peruvian deal SHOUGANG Corporation, China's largest steel group, has bought Hierro-Peru, Peru's failing state-owned iron mining company, for Dollars 312m, Reuter reports from Beijing. The deal is the second foreign purchase announced by Shougang over the past few days. It acquired California Steel Industries, whose steel mill will be dismantled and taken to China to replace one of Shougang's own mills. Of the total price tag, Shougang will invest Dollars 150m back into the Peruvian group.
