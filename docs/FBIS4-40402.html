BFN [Text] North Korea will have to face hard times, Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko told journalists on Tuesday [14 June]. Commenting on the decision of North Korea to cease its participation in the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA], Zlenko said he believed it would be very difficult to confront the whole world community which stands for the elimination of nuclear weapons. Zlenko repeated that the position of Ukraine had been made clear during the recent visit of the North Korean foreign minister. He said Ukraine was hoping that North Korea was moving towards a non-nuclear status. On this basis, Kiev is ready to cooperate with North Korea, Zlenko added.
