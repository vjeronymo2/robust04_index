940523 FT 23 MAY 94 / Company News (This Week): Bad debts threaten Japanese banks -Other companies Japanese banks are expected on Thursday to announce a sharp fall in profits for the year to March. This follows aggressive write-offs of their bad debts through the Co-operative Credit Purchasing Company, a bad loan purchasing organisation set up by the banks last year. Brokers James Capel in Tokyo predicts that the banks' transfer of bad debts to the CCPC will total Y2,000bn (Dollars 19bn), with a further Y1,000bn written off by the banks in straight loan-loss provisions.
