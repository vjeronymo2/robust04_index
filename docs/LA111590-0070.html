November 15, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

THE CORNER BAR: MORE BARS:

The Upbeat, 12745 Garden Grove Blvd., Garden Grove. Open daily 6 a.m. to 2 a.m. (714) 530-4101.

This is the kind of country club where Donald Trump would never be accepted as a member. It's more the down-home sort of place where Clint Eastwood could comfortably set up filming should he ever decide to make another sequel to "Every Which Way But Loose." RANDY LEWIS
