BFN ["Exclusive" report by 'Ali Jamalu] [Excerpt] A Western diplomatic source has said that Slovak Foreign Minister Eduard Kukan, who visited Damascus last week and met with senior Syrian officials, has signed an agreement with the Syrian side stipulating the sale of 250 tanks of an advanced type to Syria. The source said Damascus is continuing to modernize the weaponary of its military institutions by both old and new relations with several non-Western nations, including Russia, the PRC, Korea, India, and now Slovakia, which inherited huge weapons factories from the mother country (Czechoslovakia). The source said this policy is based on Damascus' view that "comprehensive peace," stipulated by the resolutions of international legitimacy and the land-for-peace principle, is facing deliberate Israeli obstruction.
