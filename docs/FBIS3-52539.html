Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Multilateral talks between the National Dialogue Commission [NDC] and political parties resumed today. In this context, the NDC held a meeting today with a number of political parties during which the preliminary draft of the national consensus platform, proposed by the NDC, was studied and discussed. The meeting was attended by the Movement for Democracy in Algeria; the Rally for Culture and Democracy; the Movement of Islamic Society -- Hamas; the Islamic Al-Nahdah Movement; and the Constitutional Union, a coalition of 12 political parties. The National Liberation Front [FLN] boycotted the meeting.
