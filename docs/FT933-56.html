930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG reversed Tuesday's falls as some confidence returned to the market, with the industrials index firming 38 to 4,484 and the overall index gaining 21 at 3,792. The gold shares index, however, lost 6 to 1,548. Anglo American added R1 at R129.50 in reaction to its restructuring plans, while Minorco fell back 50 cents to R79.
