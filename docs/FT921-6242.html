920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables Spania lemons are plentiful for Pancake Day (3.03.92) and are priced at 12-20p each (15-20p). Bramley apples at 35-50p a lb (45-55p) and bananas at 48-60p a lb (48-65p). Top quality broccoli is very reasonably priced at 35-40p per 8oz pre-pack (50-65p). Other notable vegetable buys include: carrots at 15-20p a lb (15-20p) and white and red cabbage varieties at 15-25p a lb (15-25p). Round lettuce at 28-40p each (30-45p) is an excellent salad buy, together with tomatoes at 60-78p a lb (50-75p) and spring onions at 35-45p a bunch (45-55p).
