BFN [Report from roundup of ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA and ITAR-TASS reports: "Kabul's Accusations Denied"] [Text] Afghanistan has accused Uzbekistan of supporting the opponent of the country's president, Borhanoddin Rabbani, that is Prime Minister Golboddin Hekmatyar, and his ally General Dustum -- an ethnic Uzbek whose troops also consist of Uzbeks. Kabul radio yesterday broadcast a statement by the Afghan intelligence leadership to the effect that Tashkent has given Dustum aircraft to bomb the Afghan capital and this week they have already struck against Kabul. Surat Mirkasymov, Uzbekistan charge d'affaires ad interim to India, has categorically denied the Afghan intelligence service's statement.
