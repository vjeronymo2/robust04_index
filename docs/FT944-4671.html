941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: TR Property Edge Properties, a subsidiary of TR Property Investment Trust specialising in the retail warehouse sector, has made a series of four property disposals for a total of Pounds 12.75m. In Folkestone, Kent, Edge sold a 40,000 sq ft leasehold store for Pounds 3.75m to SI Pension Trustees and an adjoining 12,000 sq ft retail warehouse for Pounds 1.4m to Midland Bank Pension Trustees. In Milton Keynes, Buckinghamshire, Edge sold a 24,000 sq ft three unit retail warehouse scheme to Haymills Property Investments for Pounds 4m, while Haymills also paid Pounds 3.6m for a 35,000 sq ft retail development at Biggleswade, Bedfordshire.
