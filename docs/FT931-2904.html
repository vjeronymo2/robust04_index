930318 FT 18 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Avonside declines to Pounds 4.56m Avonside Group, the building services and housebuilding group which operates in Scotland, northern England and Wales yesterday reported a pre-tax outcome of Pounds 4.56m in 1992 on almost static sales of Pounds 51.5m. In its first full set of results since flotation last March the group saw a 42 per cent decline from Pounds 7.88m but paid a final dividend of 2.2p for a total of 4p. Earnings per share fell to 7.08p (12.4p). Avonside also reported the Pounds 480,000 cash acquisition of a glazing company to complement its BS heating subsidiary.
