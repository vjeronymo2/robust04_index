BFN [From "Caracol's 0600-0900" newscast] [Text] Santa Fe de Bogota -- A new explosion was reported a short while ago in the northern part of the capital of the Republic. A small explosive device detonated, causing damage in a three-story building near the intersection of 24th Avenue and 75th Street. The bomb was placed at the Electra Drugstore. Several nearby homes also sustained damage, which is being assessed at this time by police and the Santa Fe de Bogota Fire Department.
