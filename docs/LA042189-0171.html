April 21, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

THE PREPS;

ROUNDUP;

HATHAWAY MAKES HOLE IN ONE FOR EL MODENA

Denny Hathaway had a hole in one and shot a one-over-par 34 as El Modena High School defeated Santa Ana, 190-222, in a Century League golf match Thursday at Riverview golf course in Santa Ana.

Hathaway, a senior, had his hole in one on the 163-yard second hole. He used a seven-iron.

El Modena is 12-1 overall and 6-0 in league play.
