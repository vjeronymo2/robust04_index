BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 13 (KCNA) -- Loud songs of loyalty sung by overseas Koreans are ringing out from the stages of the 12th April Spring Friendship Art Festival which is open in Pyongyang in celebration of the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song. Participating in the festival are an art troupe of Koreans in Japan, an art troupe of Koreans in China and an art troupe of the International Federation of Koreans for Reunification. The art troupe of Koreans in Japan is putting on stage "Song of Best Wishes Presented by the 700,000 Compatriots" at the Pyongyang Grand Theatre. The tune, song and dance "Holiday" is the curtain-raiser of their colorful program consisting of soloes, choruses and dances. In "Holiday" the performers say that the 700,000 Koreans in Japan wholeheartedly extend congratulations to the great leader, becoming flowers of loyalty and filial piety, calling the spring holiday in April, the month of his birth, a spring holiday of Korea, a spring holiday of the sun. Hong Yong-wol, a "Kim Il-song Prize" winner and people's actress, passionately sings the song "Leader, Please Accept our Wishes" to be acclaimed by the audience. Merited actor Yi Yong-su in the solo "A Happy Memory" sings loudly that Marshal Kim Chong-il is the father, saying that his deep loving care and broad smiles always give new courage and show a bright road. The dancers in the dance "Spring Flowers" present on a vivid canvas the loyal hearts of the people in the homeland and the 700,000 Koreans in Japan who will always hold the dear leader Marshal Kim Chong-il in high esteem to the tune of the song "Flowers in Full Bloom All Over the Country", with light red fans in their hands. The Korean artistes from China are putting on stage the narration and song "Let us Sing of our Bliss in Having Sagacious Leader" and other numbers, touching the heart's cord of the audience. The performers loudly sing of the pride and happiness of the Koreans who are enjoying all the blessings of the world, though they are living in an alien land, as they have the great leader and the dear leader. The performers of a woman solo and trio, in the song "Our General is the Greatest", highly laud the greatness of General Kim Chong-il who is the dearest father of the soldiers of the Korean People's Army, who calls at posts among bushy young pine trees and travels with them through the sky and the sea and who deals a sledge-hammer blow at the enemy. The artistes of the International Federation of Koreans for Reunification are putting on stage the dance "Night of Pyongyang, Don't Advance", the female duet "The Old Home in the Snow," the female solo "I am Your Eternal Son" and other numbers. The dance "Night of Pyongyang, Don't Advance" makes the audience feel the national pride of living in the socialist homeland under the guidance of the great leader and the dear leader. The audience are deeply impressed by the performances of overseas Korean artistes who find it hard to repress their longing and reverence, embraced in the bosom of the great leader Generalissimo Kim Il-song and the dear leader Marshal Kim Chong-il.
