March 27, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

'Most Wanted' Scores Again: Indiana state police headed to Elko, Nev., on Monday to take into custody a murder suspect who was turned in by viewers of the television show "America's Most Wanted." Indiana state police Sgt. Jerry Parker said several viewers of Sunday night's episode called in to the show to say that the suspect, Joey Higgins, was working at a tire store in Elko. Higgins was arrested by Elko police at his home without incident. Sunday night's profile of Higgins' crime was the third time the case had aired on the show. BETH KLEID
