BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow June 22 TASS -- The State Duma (lower chamber) of the Russian Parliament lacked five votes on Wednesday to approve the federal draft budget under the third reading, but will reconsider it in the evening. With only 221 votes for it, the draft, which was amended after its second reading a few days ago, failed to overcome the 226-vote hurdle. The major disagreement is the defence spendings. The Duma wants to keep them at the 40-trillion rouble level, while the upper chamber, the Federation Council, wants to increase the appropriations to 55 trillion.
