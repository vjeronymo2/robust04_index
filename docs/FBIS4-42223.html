BFN ["breu/DW"-signed report: "Genscher To Lead Dusseldorf FDP in Election Campaign"] [Excerpt] Neuss/Berlin -- North Rhine-Westphalia's Free Democratic Party [FDP] will build its Bundestag election campaign around its most popular leading figure. Former FDP Chairman Hans-Dietrich Genscher was reelected front runner by 95 percent of all delegates at a party conference in Neuss yesterday. Second on the land FDP's list is Otto Count Lambsdorff, followed by Federal Construction Minister Irmgard Schwaetzer, and North Rhine-Westphalian FDP Chairman Juergen Moellemann. [passage omitted]
