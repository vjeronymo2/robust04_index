Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Administration Council Premier Comrade Kang Song-san has sent a congratulatory message to Indian Prime Minister P.V. Narasimha Rao. The congratulatory message sends enthusiastic congratulations on the Republic of India's national day. It expresses the true hope that there will be greater results in his work for the country's independent development and prosperity, and emphasizes the firm belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries will be all the more strengthened and developed.
