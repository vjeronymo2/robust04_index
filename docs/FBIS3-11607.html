Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Portuguese President Mario Soares arrived in Rio de Janeiro today. He will start the first leg of his nine-day visit to the country. He said that the country has progressed since his last visit last year, adding that he trusts the new economic plan will succeed. President Mario Soares expressed support for the proposal by Ambassador Jose Aparecido to create a community of Portuguese-speaking countries. The Portuguese president also will visit Curitiba, Bahia, and Minas Gerais.
