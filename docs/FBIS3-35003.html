Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Our correspondent in western al-Biqa' reports that the villages of Mimis, Marj al-Zuhur, Qilya, and al-Dallafah came under heavy machine-gun fire from Israeli Army and South Lebanese Army [SLA] positions in Zummarayya and Kawkaba at 0800 today. At 0830, a SLA force carried out a sweep along the Hasbani-Zummarayya road. A general alert was declared among the Lebanese Army troops in the Marj al-Zuhur area, Mashgharah, 'Ayn al-Tinah, Qilya, and Mimis. At 0900, Israeli helicopters flew for 15 minutes over Kawkaba, Marj 'Uyun, and al-'Urqub. Our correspondent in Tyre reports that the Israeli forces at dawn today fired 14 mortar shells at the outskirts of al-Mansuri and carried out a large-scale sweep in the fields and valleys near the village, causing material damage. The resistance responded by firing Katyusha rockets at the al-Bayyadah position. Our correspondent in Tyre also reported that two UN officers have been injured by a land mine in the Shama' area, in the western sector of the border strip.
