(m) Fluorescent lamp ballasts. (1) Except as provided in paragraphs (m)(2) and (m)(3) of this section, each fluorescent lamp ballast designed_ (i) To operate at nominal input voltages of 120 or 277 volts; (ii) To operate with an input current frequency of 60 Hertz; and (iii) For use in connection with F32, F40, F96, or F96HO lamps; shall have a power factor of 0.90 or greater and shall have a ballast efficacy factor not less than the following:
