Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] A demonstration is scheduled for 21 January in Port-au-Prince. The National Committee for the Resolution of the Haitian Crisis [Comite National de Resolution de la Crise Haitienne, CNRCH] will stage this demonstration supporting the immediate establishment of a responsible government and the immediate and unconditional lifting of UN and OAS sanctions as well as the lifting of the embargo. Those organizing this demonstration hope to gather several dozens of thousands of people.
