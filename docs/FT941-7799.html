940223 FT 23 FEB 94 / Observer: Two-faced Meanwhile, The Royal Bank of Scotland is giving a whole new meaning to joint accounts. The bank, which pioneered the use of photos on credit cards, says it would be quite happy to issue two cards to transvestites - one featuring a photograph of them as a man and one as a woman. According to Janet Scott, president of the Beaumont Society, which runs helplines for transvestites, having two photo cards could prove very convenient for some cross-dressers. 'Those who are confident enough to go shopping in their female persona would certainly want to have a second card.' And you could still blame the shopping spree on your other half.
