Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] An Israeli civilian was stabbed at an Ashdod gas station this morning, apparently by an Arab with nationalist motives. The condition of the wounded man, a new immigrant from Russia, is not grave. He was transferred to Kaplan Hospital. Our correspondents Yehudit Yehezqeli and Nisim Qeynan report that the assailant escaped on foot. [Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 0505 GMT on 15 February carries an interview with a police officer who says the police have captured an "18-year-old Arab suspect from Gaza, who led police to where he threw the ax he used in the attack."] A Border Police force captured two members of an Islamic Jihad band in Gaza. Two Uzi submachine guns, which were stolen with eight other submachine guns from Moshav Hodiyya in the south about 10 days ago, were found in their possession. Faris Abu-Zakar, a member of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine's Red Eagles in Rafah, wounded Friday in a clash with soldiers, has died of his wounds.
