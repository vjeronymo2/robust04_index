January 23, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

BID FOR GREAT NORTHERN EXTENDED

Georgia-Pacific Corp. said today that it has extended until Feb. 5 its $63-a-share cash offer in a $4.5-billion merger bid for rival timber products giant Great Northern Nekoosa Corp. of Norwalk, Conn.

The offer had been scheduled to expire Monday.
