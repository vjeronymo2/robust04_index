Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "News" program] [Text] Chairman of the Executive Yuan's Mainland Affairs Council [MAC] Huang Kun-hui on Wednesday [5 December] morning said that in order to maintain Taiwan's competitive edge, the government will in the future take a more liberalized stand on trade and economic dealings between the two sides of the Taiwan Strait. However, he said that it did not mark a significant change in government policy.
