Language: Tajik Article Type:BFN [Text] An armed clash took place yesterday in Shurabadskiy Rayon of Kulyab Oblast between the Tajik Government troops and a detachment of the Islamic Rebirth Party. According to the report of our correspondent, quoting the military sources of the Islamic Rebirth Party, nine fighters of the communist regime were killed in this clash, which took place near the village of Yol.
