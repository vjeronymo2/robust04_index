November 22, 1989, Wednesday, Valley Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

ANTELOPE VALLEY BASKETBALL TEAM SUFFERS 1ST LOSS

Freshman forward Joe Mantashigian came off the bench to score a team-high 22 points Tuesday, but that was not enough to help Antelope Valley College as the Marauders fell to College of the Sequoias, 88-77, in a nonconference game in Visalia.

It was the first loss of the season for Antelope Valley (3-1).

Danny Griffin had 14 points, and Dedan Thomas and Paul Ernst each scored 11 points for Antelope Valley.

Sequoias improved its record to 4-2.

Women's Basketball

Azusa Pacific 68, Occidental 48 -- Jaqui Dent paced Occidental with 13 points and Kelly Stephenson added 12 in a losing effort in a nonconference game at Azusa Pacific.

Occidental drops to 1-1; Azusa Pacific is 3-1.

Wrestling

Moorpark 23, Palomar 20 -- Bill Scannell (126 pounds), Robbie Cook (134) and Carson Wollert (190) posted victories to lift Moorpark to victory in a nonconference match at Moorpark.

Two other wrestlers won by default for Moorpark (11-1-1).
