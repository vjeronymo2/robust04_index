Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report on "Parliament" page] [Text] The Defense Committee has finished work on a draft law "On the Procedure for the Provision of Civilian and Military Personnel for Peacekeeping Activities Outside Russia." It will be looked at by the State Duma in the latter part of March. Being prepared are parliamentary hearings on the implementation of the START I Treaty and the prospect of the START II Treaty's ratification, in addition to problems of social security for servicemen. The committee is simultaneously starting work on drafts of a military law and a state of emergency law.
