Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Oleg Velichko] [Text] Moscow January 20 TASS -- "I hope that nobody among new vice-premiers will be in charge of the ministry for nationalities and regional policy," Sergey Shakhray, leader of the Party of Russian Unity and Accord, who was appointed nationalities minister on Thursday, said in an interview with ITAR-TASS later in the day. If any of vice-premiers is appointed to supervise the ministry's work, he added, "I shall not be able to work as minister". Shakhray expressed hope that his ministry will be directly subordinated to the premier.
