BFN [Excerpt] The body of the late Queen Mother Zayn al-Sharaf returned home tonight after she passed away at Lausanne hospital in Switzerland at the age of 86. His majesty the king piloted the plane which carried the late queen back to Amman about 45 minutes ago. The king was accompanied by Queen Nur, Her Royal Highness Princess Basmah, and the chief of the Royal Court. The king was received upon arrival by His Royal Highness Prince Muhammad, his majesty's personal representative, His Royal Highness Crown Prince Hasan, and members of the royal family.
