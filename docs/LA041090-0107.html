April 10, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

STARLIGHT EDDIE: Britain's very own Prince Edward visited Orange County over the weekend to take in "Starlight Express," the Andrew Lloyd Webber musical. . . . Edward, a production assistant for a Lloyd Webber company, popped into the Performing Arts Center via the loading dock, where he was met by center chiefs Henry Segerstrom and Thomas Kendrick. . . . The crowd didn't even seem to notice when his royal highness, seventh in line for the British throne, slipped into a prime seat. He later dined with Starlight cast members at Gustaf Anders restaurant in Santa Ana. Lanie Jones and Eric Bailey
