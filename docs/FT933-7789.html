930819 FT 19 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Jos Holdings Available revenue of Jos Holdings, the reorganised split capital investment trust, rose from Pounds 526,000 to Pounds 751,853 over the year ended July 31. A fourth quarterly dividend of 3.025p, payable on October 8, makes an 11.65p (5.65p) total - earlier in the year a total of 11.5p had been forecast. Earnings per 20p income share emerged at 11.63p (5.69p per 25p share pre-reconstruction).
