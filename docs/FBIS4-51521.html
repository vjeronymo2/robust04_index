BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 28 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Such foundation has been established by the Ministry of Nature and Environment of Mongolia in cooperation with the UNO [United Nations Organization] development programme. The foundation for promoting small businesses is intended for improving the living standard and cultural level of the people residing on the territory of the preserved areas. The world ecological fund has provided the foundation for promoting small businesses some 75 thousand USd [U.S. dollars].
