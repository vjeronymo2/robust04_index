BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 3 (KCNA) -- An art troupe of the International Federation of Koreans for Reunification and a Kazakh art troupe arrived here Saturday to participate in the 12th April spring friendship art festival. They were met at the airport by Choe Yong-ku, vice-minister of Culture and Art, and other officials concerned. Earlier, an art troupe of Koreans in China arrived here by train.
