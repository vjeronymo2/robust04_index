BFN [Text] The task force on hard drugs has mapped out new strategies to eradicate drug trafficking. They include the use of lectures, conferences, seminars, as well as symposia and a massive public enlightenment campaign to educate the public on the consequences of drug abuse. In addition, more tribunals have to be set up throughout the country to try drug offenders. The chairman of the task force, Brigadier General (Ishaya Bamaiyi), gave the information yesterday in Lagos when members of the task force visited some foreign embassies and high commissions in the country. He advised the international community to assist the federal government in its efforts to eradicate drug abuse.
