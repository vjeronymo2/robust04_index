930628 FT 28 JUN 93 / Construction Contracts: Sunley busy BERNARD SUNLEY & SONS has won contracts worth Pounds 14m including works for Royal Mail and Tesco. The Pounds 5.2m contract for Royal Mail includes the construction of a mechanised letter office at Bournemouth. The contract for Tesco Stores, valued at about Pounds 5.5m, involves building a superstore and filling station at Ely.
