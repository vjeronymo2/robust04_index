Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert ("Azg" daily), 03/03/94 -- The RMK [Republic of Mountainous Karabakh] Ministry of Foreign Affairs reports, the legislative bodies in the republic destroyed 107,710 sq.m. of narcotics plantations. 8,619 grams of drugs were captured from the criminals, 10 people were arrested and 186 citizens were officially warned.
