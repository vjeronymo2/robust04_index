940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Health care changes help Nestor-BNA The changing climate of health care in the UK helped Nestor-BNA, the nursing agency, personnel and medical services group, to achieve pre-tax profits of Pounds 4.52m for 1993. The Pounds 1.71m figure for 1992 has been restated in accordance with FRS 3 to include an exceptional Pounds 2.53m write-off. Turnover was 15 per cent ahead at Pounds 111m (Pounds 96.3m). Operating profits rose to Pounds 6.14m (Pounds 5.54m), with 88 per cent coming from health care, 67 per cent in the UK and 21 per cent in the US. Earnings per share rose to 4.46p (0.91p) and a same-again final of 2p maintains the total dividend at 3.15p.
