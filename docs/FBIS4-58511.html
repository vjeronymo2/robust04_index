BFN [Text] Construction and Housing Minister Binyamin Ben-Eli'ezer informed the Knesset today that the planning of the Jericho bypass road has been completed. Our correspondent Hayim Hecht reports that replying to a query from Knesset Member Limor Livnat, Minister Ben-Eli'ezer announced that the Civil Administration has started expropriating lands along the planned road. The tenders will be announced in three days and the works will start within two weeks. The construction and housing minister promised that the road will be completed within three months.
