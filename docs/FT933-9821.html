930807 FT 07 AUG 93 / Deloitte Touche in merger plans DELOITTE TOUCHE, the international accountancy firm that practices in the UK as Touche Ross, is to merge with IDOM Consultants, a Geneva-based banking consultancy. The deal will increase Deloitte's opportunities in eastern European markets. IDOM provides management and information technology consultancy services to banks in Hungary, the Czech Republic and Poland.
