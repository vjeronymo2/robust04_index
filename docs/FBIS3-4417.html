Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] In his speech at yesterday's [5 March] provincial propaganda and ideological work meeting, Acting Governor Zhang Wule stressed: We are shouldering a very important task in our propaganda and ideological work. Through various effective propaganda means, we should arouse people's fighting will so that their spirit can be transformed into a powerful material force to accelerate the pace of pushing our province's economy to a new height. In his speech, Zhang Wule first briefed participants on the present situation of economic operations in our province, and the good prospects of development. He fully affirmed the contributions made by the propaganda and ideological front to the economic and social development of the whole province. He pointed out: In the course of implementing the series of reform measures and policies promulgated by the state this year, correct propaganda and guidance in terms of public opinion are required. He said vividly: According to materialist dialectics, the spirit can be transformed into materials. Doing propaganda and ideological work well means creating wealth for economic development. He called on mass media and propaganda workers to understand the relationship between specific and general interests, partial and overall interests, and immediate and long-term interests; to conduct well the propaganda concerning hot and difficult topics; and to make still greater efforts to create a fine public-opinion environment for reform, opening up, and the economic construction of the whole province.
