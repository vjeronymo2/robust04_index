August 25, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Paul McCartney will kick off his first U.S. tour in 13 years Nov. 27 to 29 at the Forum in Inglewood, the ex-Beatle announced Thursday at a New York press conference. Those dates will launch a three-week, five-city North American swing that will touch down in Chicago, Toronto, Montreal and New York's Madison Square Garden. Tickets for the Forum shows will go on sale Monday at 9 a.m. at Ticketmaster locations, with numbered priority wristbands handed out randomly at 8 a.m. CLAUDIA PUIG and DANIEL CERONE
