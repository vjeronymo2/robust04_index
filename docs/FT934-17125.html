931002 FT 02 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Waterhouse tumbles to Pounds 51,114 losses Waterhouse Group, the USM-quoted company which fits out and refurbishes office interiors, suffered a pre-tax loss of Pounds 51,114 for the half year ended March 31 after exceptional redundancy costs of Pounds 24,560. There were profits of Pounds 55,417 last time. Turnover was little changed at Pounds 1.13m (Pounds 1.17m) but margins came under pressure, Mr Robert Ackland, the chairman, said. Losses per share were 2p (2.2p earnings).
