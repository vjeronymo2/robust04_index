BFN [Text] Groznyy plans to hold tactical military exercises of the Chechen Armed Forces involving aviation, armored vehicles and artillery. This was reported to Interfax by Commander of the General Headquarters of the Chechen Armed Forces, Colonel Aslan Maskhadov. He said that firing exercises will be carried out. According to Maskhadov, the exercises will be guided by Commander-in-Chief, President of the Republic Dzhokhar Dudayev. On the occasion foreign observers were invited to Chechnya, including defense minister of the near and far abroad.
