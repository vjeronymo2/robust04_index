Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Following a private meeting between the foreign ministers of Poland and Russia, Andrzej Olechowski and Andrey Kozyrev, talks between Foreign Ministry delegations began this afternoon at the provincial governor's office in Krakow. Taking part in the meeting are Ambassadors Stanislaw Ciosek and Yuriy Kashlev. According to earlier announcements, the plenary talks are devoted to seeking new forms of bilateral political and economic cooperation. Issues of European security, including the Partnership for Peace initiative, are also being discussed. The Polish side is also expecting Minister Kozyrev to present a new concept of Russia's relations with the states of East-Central Europe. The Russian foreign minister is on a two-day visit to Poland, which is taking place in Krakow.
