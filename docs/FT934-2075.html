931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Brazilian equities registered a 3.6 per cent gain in a heavy day's trade, seeing Monday's central bank interventions in the money and forex markets as a step towards the implementation of the country's new economic plan. The Bovespa index was finally quoted at 30,485, up 1,053. Turnover jumped to Cr131.5bn.
