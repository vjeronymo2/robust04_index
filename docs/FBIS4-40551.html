BFN [Text] Duma Speaker Ivan Rybkin spoke out in favor of Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev's call for a conference on the Korean peninsula. Talking to journalists in Bonn on Thursday, he said that even if sanctions were imposed on North Korea, they should be implemented in stages. In his opinion, sanctions could be applied only after stern warnings. Meanwhile, the head of the Duma's International Affairs Committee Vladimir Lukin said that the lower house expects Kozyrev to report on the situation in North Korea on Friday. And on June 20, the committee will hear from the ambassadors of the countries concerned.
