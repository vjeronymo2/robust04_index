December 18, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLE

The Biggest Bores Around: And speaking of Roseanne, the outspoken actress has topped this year's list of the 10 most boring celebrities, according to the Boring Institute of Maplewood, N.J. Next on the list were two women who spent a lot of time in court in 1989, tax criminal Leona Helmsley and cop-slapper Zsa Zsa Gabor, with Cher ranking fourth on the list and Malcolm Forbes, Elizabeth Taylor's part-time escort, fifth. Rounding out the list were fallen baseball hero Pete Rose, "Today" show weatherman Willard Scott, actor Rob Lowe, former talk show host Morton Downey Jr. and football commentator John Madden. Claudia Puig
