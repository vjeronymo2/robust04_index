931007 FT 07 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Shepherd Neame rises 15% DESPITE discounting by competitors, Shepherd Neame, the independent Kent-based brewer, lifted pre-tax profits by 15.5 per cent over the 12 months to June 26. The increase - from Pounds 4.07m to Pounds 4.7m - was achieved on turnover ahead some 9 per cent to Pounds 39.1m, including trading from the 58 houses leased from Whitbread since May 1992. Sales through the group's tenanted pubs improved 5 per cent. Earnings per Pounds 1 share emerged at 54.4p (45.6p). A final dividend of 13.75p brings the total to 17.5p, an increase of just over 11 per cent.
