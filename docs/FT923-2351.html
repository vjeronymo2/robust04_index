920918 FT 18 SEP 92 / Sterling and the UK Economy: Floating voter thinks again DON'T mention it, commanded Lyn Martin, as the conversation turned to the events of the past two days. Lyn and her husband Tony head the FT's family of floating voters. From their home in Solihull, West Midlands, they watched the spring election campaign and then went their different ways in the polling booth. Lyn voted Conservative while Tony voted Liberal Democrat. Now Lyn is having second thoughts although, she said yesterday, 'you can never see what's going to happen.'
