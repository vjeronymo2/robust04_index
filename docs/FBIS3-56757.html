Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow January 18 ITAR-TASS -- The first meeting of the Inter-Departmental Commission of the Russian Security Council, formed by presidential decree, was held here today. The meeting of the commission, headed by Deputy Interior Minister of the Russian Federation Aleksandr Kulikov, has discussed problems linked with the elaboration of a public security concept. It also endorsed the commission's procedural rules and working plan, envisaging the study of such problems as political extremism, migration of the population, and Russia's hot spots. Taking part in the meeting were representatives of the Procurator-General's office, Justice Ministry, Defence Ministry, Ministry of Culture, Ministry of Social Protection, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the Federal Migration Service, and other federal executive bodies. The public security commission is faced with the task of determining the priorities of state policy and drawing up proposals for the Russian Security Council on ways to ensure the vitally important interests of the Russian society, human rights, as well as forecasting events and preparing recommendations to prevent inter-ethnic and social conflicts, political extremism, and various emergency situations.
