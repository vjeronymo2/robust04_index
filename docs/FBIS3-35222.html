Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn patronized a meeting at the al-Hashimiyah Palace yesterday evening attended by 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Yemeni Presidential Council; his deputy 'Ali Salim al-Bid; and leaders of the Yemeni political forces and parties. In his name and on behalf of all Jordanians, his majesty welcomed the brother members of the Yemeni leadership and expressed his appreciation to this leadership and to all the Yemeni political forces for their historic achievement represented by the document of pledge and accord. This accord is a good beginning for fraternal Yemen and the entire nation, God willing, the king added. During the meeting, the conferees reviewed ways to implement the document of pledge and accord which was signed yesterday evening by all Yemeni forces, organs, and institutions in order to enhance the unionist and democratic march of sisterly Yemen. Attending the meeting on the Jordanian side were His Highness Crown Prince Hasan; Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali; His Excellency al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, chief of the Royal Hashemite Court; Marwan al-Qasim, his majesty's political adviser; Dr. Khalid al-Karaki, his majesty's adviser; and the Jordanian ambassador to Yemen. Representing the Yemeni side at the meeting were 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani, Presidential Council member; Salim Salih, Presidential Council member; Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, the prime minister; Mujahid Abu-Shawarib, deputy prime minister; Sinan Abu- Luhum, head of the National Forces Union; 'Abd-al-Wahhab al-Anisi, secretary of the Yemeni Reform Grouping; Dr. Yasin Nu'man, head of the secretariat office of the Yemeni Socialist Party's Central Committee; 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani, minister of planning and development; and Jarallah 'Umar, minister of culture and tourism.
