Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] Robin Beard, NATO assistant secretary general for defense support, ended his two-day visit to Bulgaria today. Before leaving, he held a final meeting in Sofia with the President's Military Office, at which the participants exchanged information on the preparation of concrete programs for cooperation between Bulgaria and NATO under the Partnership for Peace initiative. At a news briefing given at the Defense Ministry, Mr. Beard summed up the results of his visit, describing it as fruitful. During his visit, he said, he finalized a program for multilateral cooperation that will strengthen the links between Bulgaria and NATO. The program covers issues concerning the relations between civilian and military structures in the fulfillment of military programs and ways to implement state deliveries. It was agreed that three groups of NATO experts would visit Bulgaria with the task of providing assistance in information technology in connection with the administration of joint military programs.
