BFN [Text] Bonn, 11 May (ITAR-TASS) -- A direct, scrambled telephone line will be set up in the nearest future between the Kremlin and the federal chancellor's department. An agreement on this was signed in Bonn today during the official visit of Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin to the FRG. The document was signed by Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev for Russia and by FRG Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel for Germany. "Taking account of the mutual interest in creating the technical wherewithal to hold confidential telephone conversations at the highest level," the agreement provides for, "as soon as technically feasible," setting up a direct scrambled telephone link between the Kremlin and the federal chancellor's department, using two channels laid along geographically separate routes. The agreement comes into force on the day on which it is signed.
