Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ARMENPRESS for TASS] [Text] Yerevan February 2 TASS -- Sporadic fighting went on today along the Karabakh front-line. Azeri Army suffered considerable losses in its night offensive towards the town of Martuni, a source in the Karabakh military headquarters told ARMENPRESS. On Tuesday night Azeri troops attacked the Armenian border settlement of Movses. After a three-hour shooting Armenian border troops pushed the enemy back to their previous positions, the press service of the Armenian Defence Ministry reported.
