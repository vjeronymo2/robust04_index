December 27, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

U.S. AID TO THE SOVIET UNION

You had an article that dealt with undercover men in Russia finding all sorts of "goodies" in storerooms that the KGB and other high-ups have access to.

Another article dealt with the lack of organization in Russia to get waiting food to the Russian people. And our President is asking the World Bank to help. But it's not a pay-back for their support of the gulf policy. Who's kidding whom?

LYNN MERLES

Costa Mesa
