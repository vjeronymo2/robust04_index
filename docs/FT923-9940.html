920801 FT 01 AUG 92 / Doubts registered carmakers fear that August will not bring the long-awaited revival in sales in spite of the arrival today of the K-registration plates. Mr Ian McAllister (above), chairman of Ford of Britain, forecasts sales of about 365,000 new cars during this month, unchanged from last year's depressed level. 'We could be looking at the third year of continuing decline,' he said.
