Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Bulletin No. 23 issued by the Mexican National Defense Secretariat in Mexico City on 20 January; from the "24 Hours Newscast"] [Text] As the process of normalization of the situation in highland, jungle, and border municipalities in Chiapas State progresses, most Army troops remain confined to quarters near San Cristobal de las Casas, Oxchuc, Ocosingo, Altamirano, and Las Margaritas, apart from Army troops stationed at the military installations of Rancho Nuevo and Comitan. This situation has permitted civilian authorities to carry out their normal activities and direct their efforts to provide humanitarian assistance to the population. Soldiers continue to assist the civilian population by transporting and distributing supplies, blankets, and medicine to the affected towns. These actions are also being carried out freely by different social groups, the Red Cross, and nongovernment organizations. The Army will continue to participate in these humanitarian tasks and will assist civil institutions and organizations that require its assistance.
