Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 10 Jan (MENA)--President Muhammad Husni Mubarak received U.S. Senator Richard Shelby and the delegation accompanying him this morning. U.S. Charge d'Affaires in Cairo Edmund Hall attended. The U.S. senator said afterward that he and President Mubarak reviewed Egypt's positive role in the Middle East. He said he informed President Mubarak that he realizes the importance of relations with Egypt for stability throughout the region. Shelby said they discussed bilateral ties and various aspects of U.S.-Egyptian cooperation. The meeting also dealt with expanding bilateral relations in the economic and military areas.
