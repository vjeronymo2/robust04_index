September 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The Athletics Congress plans to review charges in the West German magazine, Stern, of drug use by Olympic champions Carl Lewis, Florence Griffith Joyner and other American athletes.

"We are going to review the current statements of what Darrell Robinson said, along with other evidence . . . so we can get to the bottom of all these things," Frank Greenberg, president of TAC, said.
