920923 FT 23 SEP 92 / Commodities and Agriculture: Palm oil refiner in receivership NALIN Industries, one of the biggest refiners of crude palm oil in Malaysia, has been placed in receivership. Nalin, whose majority shareholder is the Indian Birla merchant family, has been hit by tighter operating margins as Malaysia's crude palm oil refining capacity has rapidly expanded in recent years. Malaysia's refineries now have a processing capacity of more than 10m tonnes a year, though supply is only 6m tonnes.
