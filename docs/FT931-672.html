930329 FT 29 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Moorfield Estates Moorfield Estates, the property group, turned in increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 2.18m for the 14 months to December 31, against a restated Pounds 1.2m in the previous year. The result however, was struck after provisions of Pounds 1.13m resulting from a board review of a March 1992 portfolio valuation. Losses per share were 20.4p (11.4p) and there is no dividend (0.1p).
