920625 FT 25 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Bristol Water turns in Pounds 5.24m BRISTOL WATER HOLDINGS yesterday reported its first annual result since it came to the market in November 1991. Pre-tax profits for the year to end-March came out at Pounds 5.24m and were achieved from turnover of Pounds 47.9m. In the previous year pre-tax profits were Pounds 2.97m and turnover was Pounds 42.1m. A final dividend of 18.7p makes a 28p total. Earnings improved to 68.9p (38.9p). Sir John Wills, chairman, said that following consultation with the Director General of Water Services, prices for 1993 would increase by an average 7.3 per cent - 1.1 per cent below the maximum permitted.
