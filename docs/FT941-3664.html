940315 FT 15 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Merchants Trust Net asset value per ordinary share of the Merchants Trust stood at 302.94p at January 31, an improvement of 28.5 per cent over the restated 235.7p standing 12 months earlier. Net earnings for the year rose from Pounds 10.43m to Pounds 11.3m, equal to 11.04p (10.2p) per share. A final dividend of 2.85p makes an 11p (10.6p) total.
