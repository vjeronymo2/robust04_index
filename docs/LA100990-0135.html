October 9, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

O.C. EARNINGS: QUARTERLY PROFITS UP AT PROVENA

The Santa Ana-based specialty food processor posted third-quarter earnings of $219,138, contrasted with earnings of $69,561 for the period last year.

Sales for the quarter rose 20% to $11.3 million from $9.4 million. Earnings for the nine-month period were $614,166, contrasted with $181,125 for the year-earlier period. Sales rose 23% to $31.1 million from $25.2 million.
