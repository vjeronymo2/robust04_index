BFN [Text] A meeting of the Syrian-Russian group working for economic, trade, and technical cooperation was held at the Central Bank of Syria today. Economy and Foreign Trade Minister Dr. Muhammad al-'Imadi led the Syrian side to the meeting. Oleg Davydov, minister of foreign economic relations, led the Russian side. Attending the meeting were Dr. Muhammad Khalid al-Mahayini, minister of finance, Dr. 'Abd-al-Rahim al-Subay'i, minister of state for planning affairs, Muhammad al-Sharif, governor of the Central Bank of Syria, the assistant to the health minister, a number of officials at the ministries of economy and planning, and the Central Bank of Syria, and the Russian technical delegation. The talks dealt with the economic, trade, technical, and scientific relations, and means of promoting them for the mutual benefit of both countries.
