920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / International Company News: Third-quarter downturn at Rockwell International THIRD-QUARTER net earnings of Rockwell International, a leading US producer of space systems and electronics products fell to Dollars 122m or 55 cents a share, from Dollars 151.4m or 65 cents a year earlier, on revenues of Dollars 2.7bn, against Dollars 2.98bn. This left the group with a nine-month net loss of Dollars 1.17bn or Dollars 5.25 a share . This compared with a profit of Dollars 448m or Dollars 1.91. Revenues were Dollars 8.02bn against Dollars 8.91bn. The latest nine-months, however, include a Dollars 1.17bn or Dollars 6.78 a share charge on a change in accounting for non-pension post-retirement benefits. Mr DR Beall, chairman and chief executive, expects fourth-quarter earnings to improve on those for the third quarter and believes 1992 full-year earnings per share will be about 15 per cent below 1991 levels before the effect of the accounting change.
