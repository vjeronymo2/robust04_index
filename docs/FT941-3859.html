940314 FT 14 MAR 94 / Company News (This week): Societe Generale de Surveillance - Other companies The unique Geneva-based group that inspects shipments and tests products for governments and manufacturers throughout the world, puts itself up for inspection tomorrow morning. Net profits for 1993 are not expected to be much different from the SFr194m (Dollars 131m) earned in 1992, but the assessment of the group's prospects by Mr Thierry Chereau, the new chief executive taken from Source Perrier a year ago, will be inspected carefully.
