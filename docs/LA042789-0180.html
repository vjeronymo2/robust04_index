April 27, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CHARITY SCORECARD

The Celebrity Golf Tournament held at the Santa Ana Country Club on April 17 raised an estimated $15,000 for the Discovery Museum of Orange County. Ken Forsche was chairman.

The golf tournament held at the El Niguel Country Club in Laguna Niguel on April 17 netted an estimated $30,000 for CSP Youth Shelter. Thomas Green was chairman.

The "Oooh La La! Au Bon Temps" dinner dance held at the Le Meridien Hotel in Newport Beach on Saturday raised an estimated $60,000 for the Sound of Music chapter of the Guilds of the Orange County Performings Arts Center. Sandra Beigel and Linda Smith were co-chairwomen.

The "Night Aboard Sea Goddess" dinner and auction held at the Irvine Hilton & Towers on Saturday raised an estimated $50,000 for the Orange County chapter of the National Multiple Sclerosis Society. Warren Johnson was chairman.
