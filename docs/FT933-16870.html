930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / UK Company News: William Jacks William Jacks, the motor dealer, yesterday said that due to insufficient distributable reserves it would pass payment on its 3.5 per cent cumulative preference shares. In April, the group announced pre-tax losses of Pounds 417,000 (Pounds 850,000) for the 12 months to end-January 1993.
