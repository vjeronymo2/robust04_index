921015 FT 15 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Clinton Cards CLINTON CARDS, specialist retailer of greeting cards, cut pre-tax losses from Pounds 2.34m to Pounds 1.29m in the half year to August 1. Turnover improved from Pounds 26m to Pounds 28.4m, excluding VAT, and there was a trading loss of Pounds 931,000 (Pounds 2,04m). The pre-tax loss was after net interest payable of Pounds 393,000 (Pounds 365,000). Losses per share were 4.94p (8.99p) and the interim dividend was maintained at 1.5p.
