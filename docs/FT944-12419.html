941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / Major allows new minister to keep occupation as dentist Mr John Major has given permission to a newly appointed minister to work as a dentist for three days a week to maintain his professional skills. Downing Street said yesterday that the prime minister's arrangement with Sir Paul Beresford, appointed junior housing minister in the July reshuffle, was in line with Questions of Procedure for Ministers, the government's ethical guide.
