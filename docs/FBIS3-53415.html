Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Hekmatyar, prime minister of the Islamic State of Afghanistan, received a large number of commanders of Kabul Province belonging to various parties. He met them yesterday afternoon at the temporary headquarters of the Prime Ministry. A radio-tv correspondent reported that topics concerning the current war in Kabul and the country's military and political affairs were discussed. The prime minister heard the (?opinions) of the commanders and stated the principled stand of Hezb-e Eslami [Islamic Party of Afghanistan] regarding the current situation.
