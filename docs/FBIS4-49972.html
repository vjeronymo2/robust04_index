BFN [Text] Taipei, May 12 (CNA) -- About 207,500 foreigners were living in Taiwan at the end of March, including some 21,012 who were staying in Taiwan illegally, according to the Interior Ministry. The foreign population is up from 1993, when the Ministry of Justice Investigation Bureau estimated that there were 180,000 legal and illegal foreigners in Taiwan. The Interior Ministry said it has approved work permits for 169,417 foreign workers, of which 83,701 had already come here to begin work as of March 28.
