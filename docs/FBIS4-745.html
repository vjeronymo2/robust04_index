BFN [Text] The training of infantry troops for the new army begins on 27 April. This decision was made in Maputo yesterday at the end of a meeting of the Joint Commission for the Formation of the new Mozambique Defense Armed Forces. The meeting also approved the budget of the High Command of the future armed forces. The amount of the budget was not revealed [words indistinct] Mateus Ngonhamo of the Mozambique National Resistance.
