940416 FT 16 APR 94 / World News in Brief: Skating Champion John Curry dies Former Olympic ice figure skating champion John Curry died of an Aids-related illness. He was 44. He won the gold medal in the 1976 Winter Olympics at Innsbruck, where his style revolutionised the sport. Curry's innovative use of classical ballet and acrobatics heralded a new generation of skating stars, including fellow British Olympic champions Robin Cousins, Christopher Dean and Jayne Torvill.
