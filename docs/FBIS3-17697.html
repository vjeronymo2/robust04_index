Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Dmitriy Pavshentsev: "NATO Mission to Tbilisi"] [Text] Tbilisi -- A special NATO delegation led by the Turkish diplomat Tugay Asceni, Turkish permanent representative to the North Atlantic bloc, has arrived in Tbilisi. As David Sumbadze, state counselor in the apparatus of the head of state, told your PRAVDA correspondent, "the visit's purpose is to discuss the conditions for Georgia's participation in the NATO `Partnership for Peace' program, to which Georgian Foreign Minister A. Chikvaidze added his signature the previous evening. The program will help to establish close military cooperation and to attract experts to Georgia to put together the military budget. The plans also envisage joint military exercises. It is possible that cadres for the Georgian Army will be trained in NATO countries."
