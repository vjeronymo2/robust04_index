March 10, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

OVERTURNED MOTOR HOME BLOCKS FREEWAY

An overturned motor home with a trailer were blocking three lanes of the northbound San Diego Freeway near Mulholland Drive late Thursday night, the California Highway Patrol said.

The 32-foot camper and attached trailer "fell over like a big dog" at 8:38 p.m., Officer Bob Weaver said. It was not immediately known what caused the accident, he said.

There were no injuries, and no other vehicles were involved, he said.
