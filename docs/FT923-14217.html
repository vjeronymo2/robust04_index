920709 FT 09 JUL 92 / Nestle to shed 190 canning jobs NESTLE, the food group, is to switch production of canned evaporated milk from its Carnation plant in Dumfries to Ashbourne, Derbyshire, with the loss of 190 jobs. The Dumfries plant will continue to produce Coffeemate and Rowntree's Chococino brands.
