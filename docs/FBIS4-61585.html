BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Lev Grunau] [Text] Moscow 8 May TASS -- Two TU-154 airliners are to leave Moscow for Sanaa, the capital of North Yemen, on Sunday night to evacuate the remaining Russian citizens, a spokesman for the Russian Ministry for Emergency Situations told TASS. This is the second stage of evacuation of Russian citizens from Yemen, currently plagued with bloody feud fighting. A total of 152 Russians are expected to be airlifted from Yemen by this flight, including embassy officials. On Sunday morning, 113 Russian citizens and two Slovaks were brought to Moscow by plane from Djibouti (northeastern Africa).
