Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tianjin, February 26 (XINHUA) -- The development of China will exert great influence on that of Asia and the world at large, Lee Kuan Yew, Singapore senior minister said here today. He told local journalists that he was confident of the future of China's long-term development. Lee also expressed optimism on the prospect of Sino-Singapore ties, saying that great potential exists in bilateral cooperation since the economy of the two countries is mutually-complementary. He said that since Singapore is short of land, and the labor costs are very high, it needs to export its capital, technology and management expertise. Thus, Lee added, to develop friendly cooperation with foreign countries, including China, is part of Singapore's overall strategy. Lee and his party arrived here on Thursday [24 February]. Yesterday, Zhang Lichang, mayor of Tianjin, gave Lee a brief introduction to the development of this largest industrial city in north China. Lee also visited Tianjin's technology development zone and Tianjian port.
