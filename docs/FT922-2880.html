920615 FT 15 JUN 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 28.5m water works awarded to Birse BIRSE CONSTRUCTION has been awarded six contracts, worth Pounds 28.5m, by four of the major water companies. Four of the contracts have been awarded on a design and build basis with Birse Water Engineering providing the mechanical and electrical technical support to the construction company. The largest contract, worth about Pounds 16m, is for Yorkshire Water and involves constructing what is believed to be the first completely underground water treatment works in the country at Holmbridge, West Yorkshire. This contract is being carried out in partnership with Purac. Other contracts include works valued at Pounds 6m for Severn Trent Water at Shelton, Kinsall & Ruyton; water supply and treatment works for Anglian Water at Elsham and Maltby (Pounds 3m), together with extensions at Poole Wastewater Treatment Works for Wessex Water valued at Pounds 3.5m.
