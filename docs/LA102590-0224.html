October 25, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: SANTA MONICA;

CAMP SCHOLARSHIPS FUNDED

National Medical Enterprises Inc., one of the nation's largest health care companies, has donated $3,000 to the Malibu Youth Foundation for summer sports camp scholarships next year.

Craig Dummit, president of the foundation, said the grant will allow 10 children to attend the camp.
