930105 FT 05 JAN 93 / Management (The Growing Business): Small businesses fail to cash in - In a Nutshell Only 18 per cent of UK small businesses have capitalised on Britain's departure from the Exchange Rate Mechanism and the fall in sterling by raising prices in foreign markets, according to a survey of 450 small businesses carried out for Barclays Bank. Thirty-nine per cent of the businesses had made no changes in their marketing plans while 64 per cent had no strategy for managing exposure to foreign exchange risk.
