BFN [Text] Ashkhabad, April 21 (XINHUA) -- Visiting Chinese Premier Li Peng laid a wreath to a monument to martyrs of Turkmenistan here this morning. After that, the Chinese premier started talks with President S.A. Niyazov on bilateral relations, regional and international issues of mutual concern. The session is going on at press time. Li arrived late Wednesday afternoon for a three-day official visit at the invitation of President Niyazov.
