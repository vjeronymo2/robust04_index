940826 FT 26 AUG 94 / World News in Brief: Women's athletics team faces expulsion Britain's women's athletics team could be expelled from next month's World Cup if an initial positive drugs test recorded by 800m runner Diane Modahl is confirmed. The British Athletics Federation revealed that shot-putter Paul Edwards and three unnamed club athletes had also tested positive. Modahl and Edwards flew back to the UK yesterday from the Commonwealth Games in Canada.
