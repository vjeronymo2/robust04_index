Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Vienna, Jan 17 (AFP) -- Talks resumed Monday between the International Atomic Energy Agency and North Korea over allowing inspections of nuclear sites, with Pyongyang officials continuing to refuse permission, an IAEA spokesman said. The talks are due to continue for the rest of the week. ``The North Koreans put forward their points of view about future inspections by the IAEA, and we gave further explanations,'' said the spokesman. [passage omitted] The spokesman said the IAEA delegation was being led by Demetrios Perricos and that of North Korea by Ho Jin Yun, an advisor to the embassy in Vienna.
