BFN [TA SR report: "Checking on Quality"] [Text] Bratislava--Ivan Gasparovic, chairman of the National Council of the Slovak Republic, presented letters of appointment to new members of the Slovak Television Council in Bratislava yesterday. On this occasion, he mentioned their responsibility under the law to check on the quality of television broadcasts. He stressed that this would be one of the council's crucial tasks in the preelection period and voiced the conviction that, in its new composition, the council would build on the positive results of its predecessor. The letters of appointment were taken over by Peter Kollar, Stanislava Benicka, Kornel Foldvari, Terezia Salayova, Kristian Bezak, and Jozef Prusak, who became chairman of the Slovak Television Council. Jozef Prusak told TA SR that the reconstructed council, whose other two members are Eva Kristinova and Dusan Jariabek, wants to become thoroughly conversant with materials being prepared for the parliament, as well as with documents pertaining to the operation of Slovak Television. This applies, in particular, to the television statutes and to the code of ethics of employees of this public corporation that is to be drafted.
