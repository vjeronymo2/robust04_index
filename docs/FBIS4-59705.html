BFN [Text] The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] is slated to complete its withdrawal from the Gaza Strip by Wednesday [18 May]. After the withdrawal and redeployment are completed, the IDF will hold a ceremony in the Qatif Bloc. Senior military sources told our military correspondent that the IDF is satisfied with the way in which the evacuation is proceeding. So far 1,500 Palestinian policemen arrived in the Gaza Strip, and a similar number is expected to arrive by Wednesday.
