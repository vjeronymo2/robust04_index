BFN [Text] The three-day working visit to Moscow by a government delegation of the Republic of Belarus has ended. It was led by Mikhail Myasnikovich, first Deputy chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Belarus. Meetings were held with Russia's Prime Minister V. Chernomyrdin, and his first deputy, A. Shokhin. As a result of the negotiations the decision was made to finalize and sign in stages the documents concerning monetary union.
