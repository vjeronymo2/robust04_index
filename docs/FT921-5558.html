920303 FT 03 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Braime shows 82% advance to Pounds 0.67m An 82 per cent advance in pre-tax profit for 1991 was achieved by TF & JH Braime, a finished metal products, forging, pressing and stamping group. Profit totalled Pounds 668,000 (Pounds 367,000). Trading surplus jumped to Pounds 550,000 (Pounds 233,000), generated from turnover 31 per cent higher at Pounds 7.27m (Pounds 5.57m). Earnings per share doubled to 30.22p (15.61p). The dividend is raised 1.5p to 8.75p, with a final of 6.5p.
