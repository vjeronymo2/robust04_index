June 27, 1989, Tuesday, Southland Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"So long as the membership, which is made up of men and women, agree to these traditions, it's not for the city, or any other government agency, to step in and say that's wrong."

-- John R. Shiner, attorney for the exclusive Jonathan Club, which on Monday announced it will serve women in its formerly all-male bar and grill under pressure from authorities enforcing an anti-discrimination ordinance.
