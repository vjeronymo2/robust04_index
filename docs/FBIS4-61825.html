BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Perkina] [Text] Moscow May 10 TASS -- Two TU-154 airliners touched down at Moscow's Sheremetyevo-2 airport last night [9 May]. They brought home Russian citizens from Yemen. The first plane broght 143 and the second -- 73 passengers. The flights ended the second stage of evacuation of our countrymen from the country engulfed in the flames of the civil war. Press secretary of the Russian Ministry for Emergencies Yekaterina Glebova told ITAR-TASS that another Russian plane is to leave Yemen on Tuesday at 10:00. Flights will be made till all russian nationals are evacuated from that country.
