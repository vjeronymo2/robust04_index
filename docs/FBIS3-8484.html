Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Jan Woroniecki, head of the UN Department in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, has denied a PAP report that Poland has decided to send 150 soldiers from the peacekeeping force to the former Yugoslavia. He explained that Poland has been considering sending additional troops but only to Croatia, where the Polish battalion has already been posted. The UN general secretary has called for the strengthening of the UN forces in Bosnia-Herzegovina.
