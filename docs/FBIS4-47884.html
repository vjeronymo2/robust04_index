BFN [Text] The Transitional Executive Council's subcouncil on defense will today decide on the future of the National Peacekeeping Force [NPKF]. A NPKF spokesman, Colonel Coenie van Rensburg, said a new intake by the peace force will also not be trained at De Brug. He said the various forces comprising the peace force no longer have soldiers available. Col. van Rensburg said members of the peace force, after several incidents since their deployment on the East Rand, have not been limited to their bases but are instead performing supporting roles in conjunction with the Defense Force.
