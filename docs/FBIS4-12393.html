BFN [Text] Three Hebron Muslim Waqf officials will testify before the Shamgar Commission next Sunday [10 April], after habeas corpus warrants were issued against them. They were placed in costudy until they give their testimony. The three Waqf members who have so far refused to cooperate with the commission, will be released from custody only if they post bail to guarantee they will show up for the hearings. This was reported by our legal affairs correspondent Hadar Wismonsky.
