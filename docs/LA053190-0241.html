May 31, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

WESTINGHOUSE PLANS BUYBACK

Westinghouse Electric Corp. said today it will launch a buyback program to repurchase up to 20 million shares of the company's common stock.

Chairman John Marous said the purchases will be made through open-market or private transactions at prices the company considers appropriate over the next 12 to 18 months.

Marous said Westinghouse stock is currently an attractive investment and the buyback is in keeping with the company's strategy to enhance shareholder value.

Westinghouse was listed at $35.25 in early morning trading on the New York Stock Exchange today, up 25 cents.
