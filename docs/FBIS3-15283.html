Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 3 Mar (ITAR-TASS) -- Rumors which have appeared lately in some media sources, to the effect that the Federal Counterintelligence Service is going to be reorganized again, have nothing to do with reality, an informant close to the Russian president's administration told an ITAR-TASS correspondent today. He expressed his concern over the fact that, in conditions when a campaign has been unleashed in the United States around the Ames affair, the publishing of such reports serves in the final analysis the unseemly purpose of misleading the public, exerting a psychological pressure on the staff of counterintelligence bodies, and paralyzing their activities.
