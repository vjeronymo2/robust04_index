May 9, 1990, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

DEPUTIES REASSIGNED PENDING PROBE

Three sheriff's deputies have been assigned to administrative duties pending the investigation of a shooting involving the three officers in El Cajon on Sunday .

Deputies Genaro Guerrero, David Meyers and Aaron Tuatagaloa were given non-patrol duties at the Santee sheriff's station until investigators complete their investigations, Sgt. Glen Revell said Tuesday.

On Sunday, the deputies responded to a report of a man threatening people at a construction yard in the 1700 block of North Magnolia Avenue at 8:05 p.m.

The deputies fired on Kenneth Butterfield, 52, of El Cajon after Butterfield reportedly began shooting at them with a .357 Magnum handgun, according to the sheriff's department.

Butterfield is listed in critical condition at Sharp Memorial Hospital.
