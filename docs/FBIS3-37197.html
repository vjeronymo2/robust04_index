Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Report by Ales Lipay: "What Kind of Station Is It?"] [Text] As BELAPAN has learned at the Belarus Ministry of Foreign Affairs, another round of negotiations between Lithuanian and Belarusian delegations on demarcating the Belarusian-Lithuanian border has been set for March. The negotiations will be held during a visit by a Lithuanian delegation to Minsk. The negotiations are to readdress the issue of the property of the Hadutsishki railroad station, which is currently under the jurisdiction of the Lithuanian Railroad Company. As Stanislaw Ahurtsow, head of the Belarusian delegation and ambassador at large, told BELAPAN, the position of the Belarusian side is unequivocal: The station should belong to Belarus.
