November 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

READERS REACT: TAKING ISSUE WITH TIMES' STANDS;

NOTE: MANY LETTERS WRITERS SUPPORT OUR VIEWS; MANY DO NOT. IN THE SPIRIT OF VIGOROUS DISCUSSION OF THE ISSUES, HERE IS A SELECTION OF LETTERS THAT DISAGREES WITH US.;

TERM LIMITS: PROPS. 131, 140

According to the pollsters, most voters favor limiting the number of terms elected officials are allowed to serve. Also, according to the polls, most voters will be reelecting the incumbents to office. What hypocrisy!

DAVID E. ROSS

Agoura
