BFN [Text] Ukraine's Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko today began his official visit to India. In Delhi he met Prime Minister Narasimha Rao and held negotiations with Foreign Minister Dinesh Singh. Two agreements were signed, on an intergovernmental commission for a number of cooperation areas and on economic and technical cooperation, as well as a protocol on consultations between the Foreign Ministries. During the course of the visit, meetings are being held between Ukraine's and India's business people, our special correspondent reports from Delhi.
