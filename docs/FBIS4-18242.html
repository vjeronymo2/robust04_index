BFN [Text] The long-awaited meeting between Forza Italia and Northern League delegations is reportedly under way at an undisclosed location. Berlusconi and Bossi are expected to come to an informal agreement over the formation of the next government. This will not be easy because Bossi has already said that he wants a Northern League politician [Roberto Maroni] to be prime minister, while Berlusconi is himself bidding for the job. Berlusconi's spokesperson reports that the meeting is proceeding well, while Maroni has said that a joint communique will be issued at the end of the talks.
