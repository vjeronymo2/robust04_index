930724 FT 24 JUL 93 / Armenians take key town Azerbaijan's Defence Ministry said yesterday the strategic town of Agdam had fallen to Armenian forces, who were advancing on a second key centre, Fizuli, further south, Reuter reports from Moscow. Armenian forces have seized the upper hand in a five-year undeclared war with Azerbaijan for control of the disputed territory of Nagorno-Karabakh with a series of emphatic victories in the past few weeks.
