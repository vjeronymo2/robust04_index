April 24, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

ROBERT COCHRAN CURRIER; FOUNDER OF PLAYHOUSE

Robert Cochran Currier, 77, founder of the Kennebunkport (Maine) Playhouse which featured famed actors of the 1930s.

He also was an actor who toured in plays with such actresses as Kathryn Cornell and Cornelia Otis Skinner. Currier had founded the Garrick Players, out of which grew the Kennebunkport repertory group. In Desert Hot Springs, Calif., on April 15.
