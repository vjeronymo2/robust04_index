940523 FT 23 MAY 94 / Labour ahead in Euro-polls An ICM poll in the Daily Express newspaper today indicates that the Tories will lose 13 of their present 32 Euro-seats. It shows Labour on course to win 61 seats in the enlarged European parliament, up from 45, while the Liberal Democrats should win three seats. The poll puts Labour on 46 per cent, Tories on 27 per cent, Liberal Democrats on 22 per cent and others on 5 per cent.
