Language: English Article Type:BFN ["We Will Always Stand Firmly on the Side of the Korean People" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, March 30 (KCNA) -- Political party leaders of Nepal and Peru fully supported the just cause of the Korean people when they met the DPRK ambassadors to their countries recently. Man Mohan Adhikari, president of the Central Committee of the Nepal Communist Party (United Marxism-Leninism), said the situation on the Korean peninsula had become strained again and an atmosphere of war had been created entirely because the United States had violated the agreed conclusions reached at the DPRK-U.S. contact in New York. "The Nepalese people and the world's peaceloving people are voicing full support to the Korean people who are advancing under the banner of socialism and communism, firmly rallied around the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il," he declared. Chairman of the Nepal Workers and Peasants Party Narayan Man Bijukchhe said his party will always stand firmly on the side of the Korean people as in the past and fully support the self-defensive measures of Korea. Javier Diez Cansenco, general secretary of the Mariateguista United Party of Peru, stated that his party will make all efforts in support of the Workers' Party, the government and the people of Korea in their just struggle against the moves of the U.S. imperialists and the International Atomic Energy Agency leading the situation on the Korean peninsula to the brink of war. Alberto Moreno, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Peru (Red Motherland), stressed that his party will always stand firmly on the side of the Korean people.
