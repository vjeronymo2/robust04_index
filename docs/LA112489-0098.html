November 24, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Sinatra to Cross the Line: Frank Sinatra said he will perform scheduled engagements at Bally's in Las Vegas Dec. 4-9 and Dec. 26-31 despite an ongoing strike by musicians. "The holiday season is here and it's time for everyone to get back to work," the singer said in a prepared statement. Sinatra refused to cross picket lines in Las Vegas for previous shows. The singer's decision was seen as a major blow to Musicians Local 369, which has been on strike against Bally's and four other resorts since summer. SHAUNA SNOW
