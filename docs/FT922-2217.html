920618 FT 18 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Devenish mortgage to fund purchases To finance the proposed purchase of assets from Whitbread and to repay bank debt, JA Devenish is raising Pounds 30m through an issue of debenture stock 2017. The assets in question are 18 Roast Inns, one Berni Inn and nine Henry's Cafe Bars, for which the aggregate consideration will be Pounds 16.9m. The balance of the funds raised will repay debt and meet general corporate purposes. Mr Frederick Cannon has bought 1.68m shares, representing 3.13 per cent of Devenish.
