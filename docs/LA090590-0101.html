September 5, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

NEW NAME: Fullerton College is modernizing its programs, so say goodby to the home economics division.

Starting this year, it's the Division of Applied Design and Human Studies. . . . "A name change is all it is," says its dean, Karen Titus. "It was an image problem." . . . Over the last 2 1/2 years, courses were aimed away from home skills toward vocational training, and the new name reflects that, she says. "Women don't want to learn how to cook for their families. They want to cook for their own catering services." Tammerlin Drummond and Rick VanderKnyff
