920903 FT 03 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Tomkins dividend rise incentive Tomkins said yesterday that it planned to increase the interim and final dividends on its ordinary shares for the year to May 1 1993 by not less than 7.5 per cent. The industrial conglomerate wants holders of its 5.6 per cent cumulative convertible redeemable preference shares to convert into ordinary shares. The total dividend for the year to May 2 was 11.34p.
