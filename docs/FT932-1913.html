930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / Increase in level of bad debt A SURVEY of 150 managing directors in Avon has found an increase in the amount of bad debts. A fifth of directors said there had been a fall in bad debts over the past six months, but a third said there had been an increase. The survey was carried out by law firm Burges Salmon for Bristol Chamber of Commerce and Initiative. It defined bad debts as those outstanding for at least six months.
