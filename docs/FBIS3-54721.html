Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Azerbaijan's Government and Armed Forces commit a crime against the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] and Armenia prior to every jubilant occasion or religious festival. Be it a Christian festival or Nowruz [spring festival] or New Year makes no difference. The enemy attacked the defense positions around Tavush in Mardakertskiy Rayon on 26 December. The onslaught was rebuffed. Azerbaijan lost 42 men in the fighting. Another attack took place against Askeranskiy Rayon on the same day. That, too, was rebuffed. Azerbaijan lost 57 fighters in the clashes. The enemy resumed its attacks on the defense positions in Mardakertskiy, Martuninskiy, and Askeranskiy Rayons early on 28 December. The enemy has maintained its onslaught until now. However, the NKR military units have been able to check the advancing forces. The HAY LUR NEWS AGENCY has reported that NKR lost 32 men in the 10-day fighting before 29 December. Azerbaijan's military forces were forced to retreat after losing 650 men at the battlefronts. Foreign mercenaries are fighting among the ranks of Azerbaijan's military units. They are commanded by generals from Turkey. However, Azerbaijan's leaders have denied the presence of Afghan mojahedin in that republic's armed forces. Nevertheless, REUTER has reported that an airplane has recently landed in Kabul to return the dead bodies of the Afghan mojahedin who were killed in the fighting in Nagorno-Karabakh. [passage omitted] Azerbaijan's leaders have recently moved to find a way to draw Armenia into the fighting. The Azerbaijani troops have recently fired on the border towns in Armenia's Idzhevanskiy, Tavushskiy, Araratskiy, and other rayons with various types of arms.
