August 11, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Larry Lemke has been named to head the T-45 Training System program at the Douglas Aircraft Co. division of McDonnell Douglas in Long Beach, according to Tom Ryan, vice president-general manager of a group consisting of the T-45 program, the KC-10, training systems and services, and special products. Lemke, 51, will remain as vice president-deputy general manager of Ryan's group while focusing specifically on the T-45 program.
