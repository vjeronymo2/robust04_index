Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Husni Mubarak has announced that the dialogue with all national forces that he asked for is likely to begin in the second half of February. The president said: The situation in Egypt is much better when compared to other countries, especially as we implement economic reforms that we consider to be in the best interests of most of our citizens. In an exclusive interview with MAYU, he said that the state is eager to protect national industry in every way. He urged Egypt's youth not to be misled by calls of perversion, no matter what the temptation. He also urged the Arab brothers to close the files of the past and look to the future in a spirit of optimism, solidarity, and cooperation.
