Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Appeal No. 163 of the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission "To the People and Leadership of the Chechen Republic," dated Moscow, 5 January 1994 and signed by Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission Chairman N.T. Ryabov, followed by Decree No. 162 of the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission "On Scheduling the Elections of Deputies of the Federation Council and the State Duma of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly in the Chechen Republic," dated Moscow, 5 January 1994 and signed by Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission Chairman N.T. Ryabov and Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission Secretary V.A. Zagulyayev] [Text] The Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission proposes to the people and leadership of the Chechen Republic that they examine and resolve positively the question of the Chechen Republic's participation in the elections to the Russian Federation Federal Assembly in March 1994. In accordance with the Russian Federation Constitution, the Chechen Republic was and still is a fully equal component [subyekt] of Russia and must by rights be worthily represented in the Russian Federation Federal Assembly. By means of their active stance and their daily dialogue with colleagues from other parts of Russia the newly elected Federal Assembly deputies from the Chechen Republic could make an appreciable contribution to eliminating the problems that exist today. For its part the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission is ready to provide organizational and financial backup in holding the elections to the Federal Assembly from the Chechen Republic. We are convinced that, given good will and the desire for happiness and prosperity for the peoples of our common multinational motherland -- Russia -- a second appeal on this question will elicit understanding and a positive decision from the people and the leadership of the Chechen Republic. With very best wishes, [Signed] N.T. Ryabov, chairman of the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission [Dated] 5 January 1994 The elections of deputies of the Federation Council and the State Duma of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly were not held in the Chechen Republic 12 December 1993. For the purposes of ensuring the right, provided for in the Russian Federation Constitution, of citizens of the Russian Federation to participate in the management of affairs of state and to elect and be elected to federal organs of state power, the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission decrees: The elections of the deputies of the Federation Council of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly in the No. 20 two-seat electoral district and of the deputy of the State Duma of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly in the No. 32 single-seat electoral district formed by the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission on the territory of the Chechen Republic are to be scheduled for 13 March 1994. The report of the scheduling of the elections in the Chechen Republic is to be published in the press. [Signed] N.T. Ryabov, chairman of the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission V.A. Zagulyayev, secretary of the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission [Dated] 5 January 1994
