941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / Pounds 1.6m spent on Post sell-off plans The government has spent more than Pounds 1.6m on consultancy work related to the privatisation of the Post Office, Mr Tim Eggar, a junior industry minister, said yesterday. Mr Eggar said the money had been spent between July 1992, when the government announced its intention to privatise Parcelforce, and October this year, when it said privatisation would be postponed. Mr Brian Wilson, Labour trade and industry spokesman, said the money should have been spent on improving the service.
