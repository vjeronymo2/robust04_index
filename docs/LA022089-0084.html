February 20, 1989, Monday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Swtizerland's No. 1 sled won a World Cup four-man bobsled race, edging Austria I by less than half a second at Cervina, Italy.

Switzerland I, composed of Gustav Weder, Bruno Gerber, Lorenz Schindelholz and Curdin Morell, had a two-run time of 2:12.10.
