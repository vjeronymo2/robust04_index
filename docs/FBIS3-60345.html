Language: Japanese Article Type:CSO [Complete text of article obtained through NIKKEI BIZ DATABASE] [Text] On 1 September, Electric Power Development announced that it had reached an agreement with the Chinese government to conduct demonstration testing of desulphurization technology at a coal-burning thermal power plant in Shanxi. A low-cost desulphurization facility will be constructed beginning in FY94. The project is intended to provide environmental technologies to China and for Japan to accumulate technologies for using coal with high levels of sulfur content. The testing will be conducted at Unit No 12, Taiyuan Power Plant No 1, with an output of 300,000 kilowatts, located in Taiyuan, Shanxi. The demonstration testing, commissioned by the Ministry of International Trade and Industry, will be implemented by Electric Power Development. This is the second such project, following that at the Qingdao Power Plant, Qingdao, Shandong.
