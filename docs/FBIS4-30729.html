BFN [Text] Gyula Horn, leader of the Hungarian Socialist Party, who is the most likely candidate for the post of future Hungarian prime minister, said that the next Budapest government will advocate cultural autonomy for national minorities in neighboring states. In cases where a Hungarian minority is in effect a majority, the Hungarian Socialist chairman proposes administrative autonomy. This does not mean, however, we will be seeking to entice Hungarian minorities in neighboring states to strive for territorial autonomy or even separation, Horn added.
