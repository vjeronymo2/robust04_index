BFN [Text] Stockholm, April 29 (AFP) -- A Swedish UN soldier was injured Thursday [28 April] in Sarajevo when he stepped on a land mine near Mount Igman while on patrol duty, the Defence Ministry said Friday [29 April]. 21 year-old Kristoffer Melinder, of Sweden's Ninth Company, suffered a fracture and splinters to a leg and was taken to a French hospital for surgery. The 9th Company, part of the joint Nordic peacekeeping force, is currently in Sarajevo to supervise the ceasefire. In total, there are over 880 Swedes in the Nordic battalion.
