920501 FT 01 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Bridgend declines to Pounds 900,000 Bridgend Group's strategy of replacing interest related income with dependable earnings from businesses within the distribution and service industries began to show through in 1991. Turnover expanded from Pounds 7.85m to Pounds 17.5m for an operating profit of Pounds 878,000 (Pounds 262,000). Net interest received fell from Pounds 819,000 to Pounds 22,000, leaving pre-tax profit for the year at Pounds 900,000 (Pounds 1.06m). Earnings slipped to 2.4p (3.5p); the final dividend is 0.8p for a maintained total of 1.2p.
