940627 FT 27 JUN 94 / Company News (This Week): Broken Hill Proprietary - Other Companies Broken Hill Proprietary, the largest of Australia's natural resource companies, is due to report its full-year earnings on Friday. Estimates for the after-tax profits figure are around ADollars 1.25bn (Pounds 600m) compared with ADollars 991m (excluding abnormals) last time.
