Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 9 Mar (MENA)--Major General Sa'id Bayraqdar, Arab League assistant secretary general for military affairs, and Ambassador Mayhub al-Mayhubi, head of the General Administration of Arab Affairs in the league, today submitted to Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid a detailed report on their mission in Yemen and their meetings with Yemeni leaders. They said the Yemeni leaders appreciated and welcomed the visit. The officials added that they attempted to contain the situation so that Yemen will achieve stability by maintaining its unity.
