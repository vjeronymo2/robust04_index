940818 FT 18 AUG 94 / International Company News: Sandoz sets up Dollars 1.8bn facility Sandoz, the Swiss pharmaceutical and chemicals company, plans to raise Dollars 1.8bn through a one-year loan facility as partial financing for its proposed acquisition of Gerber Products, the US baby foods maker. The loan facility has been fully underwritten by Union Bank of Switzerland and is being syndicated among a small group of the company's relationship banks. The credit facility is due to be signed early next week, when the co-arrangers will be announced.
