BFN [Excerpt] A total closure will be imposed on the territories from 1700 [1400 GMT] this afternoon, following the recent spate of terrorist attacks. Our correspondent Shlomo Raz reports that from that hour onwards, Arabs from the territories will not be permitted to enter the Green Line. This morning, the chief of staff said it is impossible to completely prevent terrorist attacks in retaliation for the Hebron massacre. He praised the soldiers at the Ashdod Junction for showing initiative by shooting and killing the terrorist. The prime minister will convene the Ministerial Committee on Security Affairs for a special session tomorrow in order to discuss the deterioration in the security situation and decide on the necessary steps. The leaders of the opposition factions are meeting at Likud headquarters in Tel Aviv this afternoon to discuss the situation. They will demand the establishment of a commission of inquiry into the wave of terrorist attacks and a halt to the negotiations with the PLO. Tzomet leader Refa'el Eytan called on the government to resign and call new elections. [passage omitted]
