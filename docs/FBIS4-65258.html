BFN [Text] According to a statement by Foreign Ministry Spokesman Richard Duque yesterday, France "strongly urges" North Korea to accept the new International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) mission which might be sent within the next couple of days to inspect Pyongyang's nuclear facilities, where the production of an atomic bomb is suspected to be taking place. During this new mission, the inspections at the Yongbyon reprocessing plant, where IAEA experts were prevented from conducting crucial tests in March, should be continued.
