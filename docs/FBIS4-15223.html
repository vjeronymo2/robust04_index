BFN [Text] On night of April 7 Vladimir Kazimirov, special representative of the Russian president and Ambassador at large, who is a mediator in the settlement of the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict, met with President Heydar Aliyev in Baku. The meeting was held behind closed doors. There is no information about the details of this meeting. On the eve of the meeting Kazimirov reported in his interview with journalists that the major goal of his present visit to Baku is the clarification of the position of the Azerbaijani party to the renewed variant of the Russian plan aimed at the political settlement of the Karabakh conflict. The Russian plan, in particular, provides for a cease-fire and cessation of hostilities to be followed by withdrawal of the troops from the territories occupied in the course of hostilities, restoration of communications, return of refugees to their permanent place of residence, and, at the third stage, political negotiations on the legal status of Nagorno-Karabakh.
