CSO [Text] New Delhi, Feb 8--Being a party to the Montreal Protocol, to check emission of substances which deplete the ozone layer, the Union Government has prohibited import and export of eight substances with countries which are not parties to the protocol. The eight substances are trichloro flouro methane, dichloro difluoro methane, trichloro triflouro methane, dichloro tetrafluoro ethane, chloro pentafluoro ethane, bromo chloro diflouro methane, bromo triflouro methane and dibromo tetrafuoro ethane. Trade in these commodities with countries which are parties to the Montreal protocol is permitted without a licence.
