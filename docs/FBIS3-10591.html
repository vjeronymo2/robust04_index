Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Important Zimbabwean media have released a secret U.S. document in which its diplomatic representative in Havana admits that human rights have not been violated in Cuba. In its evening cast, Harare national television carried a news conference by Cuban Ambassador Pedro Nunez Mosquera, during which he distributed copies and explained the content of the report issued by the U.S. Interests Section in Havana.
