Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] General Ante Rose, chief of staff of the Croatian Defense Council [HVO], has lodged a protest note with General Jean Cot, UN Protection Forces [UNPROFOR] commander for the area of the former Yugoslavia, and General Michael Rose, UN peace force commander in Bosnia-Herzegovina, following cease-fire violations by the Bosnia-Herzegovina army. In his letter General Roso states that Muslim forces launched a heavy attack today on the free territory of Zepce, using mortars and howitzers. One HVO soldier was killed and three wounded in these attacks. In his letter General Roso says that Muslim forces organized and staged an HVO attack on their positions on the eastern side of Mostar. They had in advance positioned TV cameramen who were supposed to film this artillery action, thus gaining a certain political as well as military advantage. General Roso demands that UNPROFOR take measures on the ground in order to thwart similar action.
