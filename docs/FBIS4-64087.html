BFN [Unattributed report: "Russian Federation Central Bank Changes Tactics of Currency Market Regulation"] [Text] The Russian Central Bank plans to change its tactics of regulating the ruble's exchange rate against the dollar by carrying out hidden interventions through networks of authorized banks. At the same time, the Central Bank is going to reduce its presence at the Moscow Interbank Currency Exchange, Igor Knyazev, deputy chief of the bank's currency operations administration, said. This scheme is expected to be more efficient because authorized banks will benefit from putting their into circulation, which, in turn, will create a "cash flow."
