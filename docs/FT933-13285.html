930720 FT 20 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Improvement at Process Systems For the first half of 1993 Process Systems lifted pre-tax income from Dollars 14,000 to Dollars 58,000 (Pounds 39,000). The company, which makes energy measuring and monitoring systems, increased sales from Dollars 5.8m to Dollars 6.9m. The evaluation cycle for the QUAD4 solid state meter had been completed at many electric utilities, and customer acceptance of this product line was being realised. Gross margin remained at 47 per cent. Order rates for the period rose 36 per cent and the order book was currently at Dollars 3.95m.
