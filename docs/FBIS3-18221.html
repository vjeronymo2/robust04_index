Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Brussels, March 1 (AFP) -- The European Union has reached agreement with Sweden on that country's terms for joining the Union, an EU spokesman said here Tuesday [1 March]. The announcement came after 25 hours of non-stop negotiations on the terms for taking Sweden, along with Austria, Finland and Norway, into the Union on January 1, 1995. Sweden was pushing for a gradual phase-in of its contributions to the EU budget. The year-old negotiations were supposed to have been wrapped up at midnight Monday after a final four-day session that started on Friday.
