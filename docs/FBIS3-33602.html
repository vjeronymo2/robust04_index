Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Brasilia, 23 Feb (EFE) -- The Planalto Palace press office today reported that Brazilian President Itamar Franco will attend Chilean President-elect Eduardo Frei's inauguration on 11 March. Franco's agenda in Santiago de Chile has not yet been disclosed. In October in Santiago he participated in the Rio Group presidential summit, the main Latin American political and economic forum, which was attended by 13 countries.
