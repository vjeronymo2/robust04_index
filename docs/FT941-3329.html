940316 FT 16 MAR 94 / International Company News: Heiton pays IPounds 8.1m for F&T Buckley Heiton Holdings has bought F&T Buckley (Holdings), a builders merchant based in the Irish Republic, from Harrisons & Crosfield for IPounds 8.1m (Pounds 7.79m) in cash. Proceeds of the sale will be used by H&C to reduce borrowings. The company will also assume borrowings by Buckleys of approximately IPounds 2.5m. Heiton is also to raise IPounds 9.2m by way of a 4-for-7 rights issue of 16.4m new ordinary shares of 25p at 56p apiece. The issue has been fully underwritten by AIB Capital Markets.
