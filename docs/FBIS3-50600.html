Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on Nicaraguan economic developments monitored through 3 January. Source is cited in parentheses following each item. Prices of oil by-products were raised by the government on 3 January. Regular gasoline price rose from 14.60 cordobas ($2.30) to 14.88 cordobas ($2.34) per gallon. Premium gasoline rose from 16.42 cordobas ($2.58) to 16.90 cordobas ($2.66) per gallon. Diesel rose from 7.14 cordobas ($1.12) to 7.28 cordobas ($1.14) per gallon. The new prices were not officially published by the government. (Hamburg DPA in Spanish 2209 GMT 2 Jan 94)
