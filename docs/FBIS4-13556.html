BFN [Text] In occupied Kashmir, the resistance against Indian atrocities has toughened with the mojahedin killing 10 Indian troops in retaliatory attacks in various places. Eight soldiers were killed when mojahedin attacked their posts in (Chardora) and Badgam. Reports say eight Kashmiris were also martyred during Indian repressive operation in Gandarbal, Kulgam, Shupian, and Baramula.
