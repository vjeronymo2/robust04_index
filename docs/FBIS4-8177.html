BFN [Reporter's dispatch from Gorazde] [Excerpt] This evening is no different from the previous 15 days of the chetnik offensive. The town is still being fired on while the attacks of Karadzic's followers are under way in the surrounding area. At around 1900 the attacks by aggressor's artillery from the direction of Ustipraca intensified. Immediately after that mortars fired on a central part of town. Serb artillery was active throughout the day. Several buildings in the town have been hit, among them a building housing refugees. According to figures released by the authorities, six people had been killed and 27 wounded by 1500. [passage omitted]
