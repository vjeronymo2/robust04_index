930825 FT 25 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Scottish Eastern Net asset value per share, after deducting prior charges at par, of the Scottish Eastern Investment Trust stood at 86.6p at July 31. That compared with 79.2p at the January 31 year end and with 62.8p a year ago. Pre-tax revenue for the half year fell from Pounds 7.7m to Pounds 6.8m, partly due, the company said, to increased investments in Japan. Net revenue emerged at Pounds 5.12m (Pounds 5.55m). Despite the fall in earnings from 0.87p to 0.8p the interim dividend is raised to 0.52p (0.5p).
