August 5, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ASTRO PITCHER GETS OFF SCOTT FREE IN EYES OF HERSHISER, DODGERS

Every time Mike Scott of the Houston Astros plays the Dodgers, the Dodgers give him a bad time and it just isn't fair.

I heard that Orel Hershiser said Scott cheats. Hershiser has no right to malign Scott. Scott is a nice guy, but he doesn't flaunt it like Hershiser.

MARY H. BURKE

Glendale
