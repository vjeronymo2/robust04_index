Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] The labor unions, CATH [Autonomous Organization of Haitian Workers], CGT [General Central of Workers], CTH [Confederation of Haitian Workers], FOS [Federation of Workers Trade Unions], OGITH [General and Independent Organization of Haitian Workers], and KOTA [Confederation of Haitian Blue-Collar Workers] protest the employers' initiative to stop work. The labor union sectors see those arrangements as unilateral and the coup de grace to the workers' organizations, which will become organizations of unemployed people. According to members of the labor unions, the employers' associations want only to avoid their responsibilities in paying the workers. The various labor unions are ready to meet with the other sectors of national life to search for a solution to the Haitian crisis.
