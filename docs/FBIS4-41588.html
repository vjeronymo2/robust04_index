BFN [Text] Baku (June 23) TURAN: Today the State Commission for investigation of the Kusarskiy incident, which took place on June 14, 1994 in the village of Piral, is to meet with the Lezghin community of the Kusarskiy Region. The State Commission is headed by Vice-Premier, Chairman of the State Commission for interethnic relations, Abbas Abbasov. As was earlier reported, there was an armed conflict between the policemen and the citizens of Piral village, as a result of which one person has been killed and several have been wounded. Lezghins demanded removal of the Procurator and the Chief of police of the Kusarskiy Region from their duties and punishment of the policeman involved in murder. Several media reported that Lezghin community intended to conduct a rally in the Kusarskiy Region centre. An employee of Azerbaijan's Ministry of Internal Affairs, Fatulla Guseynov, told TURAN that the Lezghin community did not inform the authorities about their intention to conduct rally in the region centre.
