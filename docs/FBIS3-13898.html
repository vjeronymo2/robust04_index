Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In occupied Kashmir, Indian troops have martyred another 13 people during their repressive operations at various places in the valley. Two brothers were shot dead in Khanbal as they were working in the field, while three people were martyred in a village near Sopor. The rest of the people were victims of Indian atrocities in Hindwara, Baramula, and some other places. During these operations, the occupation troops also torched a number of houses. In response to the growing Indian atrocities, the mojahedin have stepped up their retaliatory attacks and have killed four Indian soldiers on Srinagar-Jammu road. According to reports, a mojahed commander, Farooq Ahmad Butt, attacked a convoy of Indian troops with live grenade. Two soldiers were blown to bits, while 11 others were critically injured. The mojahed leader was also martyred in the attack.
