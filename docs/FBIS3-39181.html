Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Norway supports the NATO decision to use air strikes in Bosnia if necessary, said Foreign Minister Bjoern Tore Godal, but the NATO decision met with opposing views in the Norwegian parliament. Defense Minister Joergen Kosmo confirmed, however, that Norway is prepared to participate if NATO takes military action in Bosnia. Defense Minister Kosmo said also that a plan has been made to evacuate the Norwegian UN force in Bosnia, should this become necessary.
