Department PY1006005394 Madrid EFE in Spanish 2024 GMT 8 Jun 94 PY1006005394 Madrid EFE Spanish BFN [Text] Lima, 8 June (EFE) -- Peruvian security forces today seized almost one tonne of cocaine paste in San Martin Department, located in the jungle region. A police source reported that the cocaine shipment was seized during a Peruvian Police Antidrug Division (Dirandro) operation conducted in the town of San Hilarion, in Bellavista Province. San Martin and Bellavista are located in the Huallaga valley, the largest cocaine paste producing center in the world. The police spokesman reported that during the operation, the police arrested three drug traffickers who were in charge of guarding the shipment. He established that they were two Peruvians and once Colombian, but their identities have not been disclosed.
