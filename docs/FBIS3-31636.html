Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, February 19 (ATA) -- "Our desire is to set an example for others in our relations with Albania," Crvenkovski, the Macedonian foreign minister, said yesterday in a meeting he had with the Albanian Premier Meksi. Mr. Crvenkovski said the former Yugoslav of Macedonia highly appreciates the Albania's support for his country in the international area. Speaking of the restoration and stabilization programs The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia (FYROM) has undertaken, Mr. Crvenkovski expressed the interest for summit contacts and mentioned some of the concrete cooperation projects with Albania which will be implemented very soon. The projects consist mainly in the roads and railways construction and in the electro-energetic field. The Albanian Premier Meksi said the finding of friendly and willing solutions is imperative in the relations extension. "Leaving behind the old mentality, the questions in regard with the population rights, the census and the Albanian rights to be active in the Macedonian state can be solved," Mr. Meksi said. The Premier Meksi expressed the Albania's interest for east-west road projects, the electro-energetic system improving and the trade volume raise. Mr. Meksi said we should be preoccupied to make them concrete as soon as possible.
