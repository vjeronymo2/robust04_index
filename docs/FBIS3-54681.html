Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] The Azerbaijan Presidential Press Service has disclosed that President Hashemi-Rafsanjani has conveyed a letter to President Geydar Aliyev that focused significant attention on the need to strengthen friendship and cooperation between Azerbaijan and Iran. Taking that into consideration, President Hashemi-Rafsanjani invited President Aliyev to visit Iran at a convenient time. It will be recalled that President Suleyman Demirel invited President Aliyev to visit Turkey a day before President Hashemi-Rafsanjani's invitation was received.
