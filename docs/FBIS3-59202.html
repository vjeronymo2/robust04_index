Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Premier Chernomyrdin Meets Pope"] [Text] Vatican City, 28 Jan (ANSA) -- Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin, his wife, and a delegation of Russian officials on Friday morning [28 January] were received by Pope John Paul II at the Vatican, the Holy See announced. As is customary, the Vatican did not release details of the 50-minute meeting, which was described as "cordial" by delegation members.
