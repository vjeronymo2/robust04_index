BFN [Text] The situation has again worsened on the northeast section of the border between Azerbaijan and Armenia. Our correspondent has the details: [Unidentified correspondent] The press service of Armenia's Defense Ministry says that on the night of 4 June, a motorized infantry battalion of the Azerbaijani Army, supported by armored vehicles, attacked positions of Armenia's territorial defense forces close to the village of (Davik) in Noyemberyansiky rayon. Azerbaijani sub-units managed to move onto Armenian territory and even to progress toward the rayon center of Noyemberyan. However, after a regrouping of forces and the arrival of reinforcements, the aggressor was beaten back to its original positions. Azerbaijan's Army lost around one infantry platoon [as heard] and one infantry combat vehicle. The Armenian losses are not yet known.
