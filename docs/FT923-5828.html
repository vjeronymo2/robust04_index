920828 FT 28 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Legal & General's US offshoot ahead Legal & General America, the US arm of Legal & General Group, the insurance company, reported pre-tax profits for the six months to June 30 of Dollars 31m (Pounds 16.3m) compared with Dollars 3.9m. The result was struck after substantial realised investment gains of Dollars 23.1m (Dollars 3.8m) following the restructuring of the investment portfolios of Banner Life and William Penn.
