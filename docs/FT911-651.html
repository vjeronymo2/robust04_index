910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / Rise in number of BTEC students THE number of students registering for Business and Technician Council (BTEC) courses has risen by 7 per cent in the past year. BTEC is the largest validating body of vocational courses in England and Wales, with more than 500,000 students enrolled. The increase was particularly strong for courses in computing (up 37 per cent) and service and leisure (14 per cent). Registrations for engineering courses declined by 5 per cent.
