941228 FT 28 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Danae Inv Trust Net asset value per capital share fell 13 per cent at Danae Investment Trust over the six months to November 30. Net revenue for the period slipped to Pounds 270,000 (Pounds 274,000) for earnings per income share of 3.7p (3.76p). The interim dividend is unchanged at 3.375p and the total for the year is expected to be held at 7.95p.
