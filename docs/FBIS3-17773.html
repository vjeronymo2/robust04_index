Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Brykin] [Text] Moscow March 28 TASS--In response to the appeal of the Russian Government to resume talks between Abkhazia and Georgia on a peaceful settlement of the conflict, the Abkhazian side reiterated its readiness for talks. The Abkhazian leadership has always favoured a political settlement of controversial problems, the press service of the Abkhazian mission in Russia reported today. The Georgian side also expressed readiness to resume talks. According to the Abkhazian press service, consultations at the Russian Foreign Ministry will probably begin this week. From the Abkhazian side they will be attended by Anri Dzhergenia, personal representative of the chairman of the Abkhazian Supreme Soviet, and Igor Abkha, official representative of the Republic of Abkhazia in Russia.
