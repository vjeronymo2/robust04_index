BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 8 (KCNA) -- Students of Kim Il-song University and Pyongyang University of Agriculture held rallies and voiced support to the struggle of the South Korean students and people for checking rice market opening by the authorities. Speakers at the rallies noted that, with the market of farm and livestock products including the rice market sold off to the United States by the South Korean Kim Yong-sam group, South Korean agriculture is facing a total bankruptcy, the 6 million peasants will lose the means of livelihood soon and its catastrophic effect will sweep the economy as a whole. Referring to the staunch struggle of the students belonging to the South Korean Federation of General Student Councils (Hanchongnyon), the peasants and other people of South Korea against rice market opening by the Kim Yong-sam group, they said this was an explosion of anger and grievances against those who seek to prolong their despicable power by selling the people's right to existence and a righteous patriotic action to defend the dignity and interests of the nation. They said the treacherous Kim Yong-sam puppet regime must be overthrown and an independent democratic government be established, if rice market opening is to be checked in South Korea and the dignity and interests of the nation be defended.
