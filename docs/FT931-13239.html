930125 FT 25 JAN 93 / Construction Contracts: Dam development Water engineering contractor BIWATER has won two overseas contracts together worth Pounds 27m. The larger contract, worth Pounds 20m, is from the Nigerian Federal Miniistry of Agriculture for the completion of the Kagara dam and water treatment plant in Niger State. The earth filled dam will provide irrigation in Kagara town and the surrounding area. The second major contract is in Cyprus and has been awarded by the Larnaca Sewerage and Drainage Board. The Pounds 7m turnkey project is to provide wastewater treatment facilities and effluent storage reservoirs.
