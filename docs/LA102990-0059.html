October 29, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Rocky Mountain Film Fest: The Denver International Film Society has established the International Environmental Film Festival to showcase 30 to 50 top environmentally themed films and videos. The festival, which is set to run from March 13-17 in Boulder, Colo., is seeking film and video submissions from amateurs and professionals. The honorary board of directors includes Robert Redford, Jack Lemmon and Ted Danson. CLAUDIA PUIG
