BFN [Text] On 21 April, the people, in cooperation with the National Army of Democratic Kampuchea, attacked and liberated Boeng Trakuon, Phnum Prak and Phnum Chhat areas, and the vicinity of Thmar Puok District. Moreover, the people also joined forces to liberate Kouk Romiet, Trayoeng, Kouk Prich, Kandaol, Thmei, Treas, Don Noy, Ta Soeng, and Sre L'ar villages, and so on. We destroyed four depots of armaments and ammunition; and we seized six warehouses of paddy that they had plundered from the people, a radio-communication 229-H, six field radios, 106 assorted guns, and some ammunition.
