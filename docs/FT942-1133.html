940625 FT 25 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Johannesburg underwent a sharp correction, taking its lead from weaker world markets and the easier bullion price. The overall index was 96 lower at 5,592, industrials lost 109 to 6,477 and golds fell 40 to 4,733. Remgro lost R2 to R26 and SAB fell R3 to R88.
