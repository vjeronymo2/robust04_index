Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] A total of 46 government and three Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] soldiers presented themselves in assembly areas throughout the country 28 March. A total of 48,155 soldiers had presented themselves in assembly areas by 28 March, 23,347 from the government and 14,808 from Renamo. The UN Operations in Mozambique [Unomoz] daily report says that by 28 March 6,356 soldiers from both sides were demobilized, 6,150 from the government and 206 from Renamo.
