940321 FT 21 MAR 94 / Company News (This Week): Kingfisher - Other Companies On Wednesday, the final results of the retail group - which includes Woolworth, Superdrug, Comet and B&Q - will be closely watched after its disappointing trading statement. Analysts have downgraded pre-tax profits forecasts to about Pounds 295m, compared with Pounds 234m, reflecting the Darty acquisition in France.
