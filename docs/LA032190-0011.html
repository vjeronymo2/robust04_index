March 21, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Parlez-Vous Czech?: Czechoslovakia's first commercial radio station went on the air Tuesday -- in French.

Europa Plus, a joint venture between France's Europe 1, state-run Czechoslovak radio and broadcasting authorities, began with a mix of light music, news and advertisements. It began in French, with Europe 1's programs transmitted by satellite to Prague and then re-broadcast. State radio officials said they hoped to begin broadcasting in Czech by May 1. BETH KLEID
