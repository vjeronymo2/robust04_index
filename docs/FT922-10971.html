920428 FT 28 APR 92 / UK Company News: Tesco chief's basic pay rose by 55% The basic pay of Sir Ian MacLaurin, chairman of Tesco, rose by 55 per cent last year to Pounds 606,000. However, because Sir Ian took home less performance-related bonuses, his total remuneration fell to Pounds 1.08m (Pounds 1.48m). Last year, Tesco's earnings per share increased by 20 per cent to 19.95p.
