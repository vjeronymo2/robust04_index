Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sarajevo, Feb 28 (AFP) -- UN soldiers cancelled the opening set for Monday [28 February] of a bridge linking the Serb and Moslem sides of divided Sarajevo, UN General Michael Rose told reporters. The commander of UN forces in Bosnia said he had "an agreement in principle not in writing" with Bosnian Serb authorities and that he felt he needed a written agreement concerning the terms for opening the bridge.
