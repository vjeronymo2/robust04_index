930519 FT 19 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Ramco Oil placing to cut borrowings Ramco Oil Services, the Aberdeen-based energy services company, has placed, through Greig, Middleton & Co., 800,000 new ordinary shares of 10p each with a group of US investors and a UK institution. The shares were placed for cash at a price of 121 1/2 p, representing the mid-market quotation at close of business last Friday.
