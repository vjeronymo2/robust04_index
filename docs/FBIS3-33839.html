Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] Air Force and artillery forces of the Islamic army launched aerial attacks today on the assembly centers of the notorious Golboddin-Dostam militia. As a result, a large number of militia troops were killed. A responsible source of the Ministry of National Defense told a BIA military correspondent that jets of the Islamic army bombed enemy assembly centers in Maranjan Tapa and around Chelstoon, as a result of which heavy losses were inflicted on the enemy. The spokesman added that Dostam-Golboddin militia embarked upon a series of movements and military transportation in the frontline battle zone, which were pushed back as a result of the activity of the Islamic army units. Their dead were left behind on the battlefield, and a number of their positions have been seized by the Islamic army. A spokesman for the Kabul Garrison General Command said that the Golboddin-Dostam militia today fired 464 missiles at Kabul city residential areas. As a result of these savage missile attacks, a number of innocent Kabul citizens were martyred and wounded.
