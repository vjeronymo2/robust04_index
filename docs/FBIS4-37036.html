BFN [Text] Cairo, 15 Jun (MENA)--State Security Intelligence agents have arrested 79 terrorist elements in sweeps carried out in a number of southern governorates. According to an Interior Ministry statement, the sweeps led to the confiscation of five explosive charges weighing 0.5 kg each, 10 sticks of gelignite, seven electric detonators, 14 loaded ammunition clips, bullets of various calibers, a mini-truck with Suhaj license plate No. 10426 belonging to fugitive terrorist Mahmud Salim, and a number of documents. State Security investigators are interrogating the detained terrorists and searching for other individuals believed to have links with them.
