BFN [Text] Security organs have thwarted a terrorist scheme planned abroad to carry out sabotage activities against some installations and assassinate a number of personalities in Egypt. The execution of this plan was entrusted to some terrorists who were trained in foreign countries. The State Security Investigations' Bureau succeeded in arresting some members who were attempting to infiltrate through an Egyptian port. It seized all their documents and files, as well as the passports the defendants used in their travels to numerous countries. A ranking security source told AL-AHRAM correspondent Ahmad Musa that confessions by the terrorist group revealed that their leadership, which has fled abroad, dispatched them inside the country after the successful security blows dealt to terrorist leaders 'Awad Siyam and Tal'at Yasin Hammam. The members of this group were asked to convey very detailed instructions to the terrorist leaders' aides inside, as well as to transfer funds. These funds were to be used to carry out terrorist operations to make the terrorist presence in the arena known.
