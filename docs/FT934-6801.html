931123 FT 23 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Tate & Lyle in Jamaican deal Tate & Lyle and two Jamaican companies, Manufacturers Merchant Bank and J Wray and Nephew, have bid nearly Dollars 18.5m (Pounds 12.4m) for three of the island's four state-owned sugar estates. Tate, which used to control Jamaica's sugar industry but sold out in the 1970's to the Jamaican government, has had management contracts with two of the estates for the past 10 years.
