941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Schroder Korea Schroder Korea Fund reported a net asset value of Dollars 13.34 per share at the end of the six months to August 31, against Dollars 13.13 at the end of the 14 months to February 28. Total revenue came to Dollars 320,000, (Pounds 202,532) against Dollars 215,000 in the six months to June 30 1993. The net loss increased to Dollars 578,000 (Dollars 285,000) or on a per share basis 11.56 cents (5.7 cents).
