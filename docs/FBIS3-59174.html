Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Oslo AFTENPOSTEN in Norwegian carries on page 13 of its 25 January edition a full-page feature profiling the members of the Norwegian Government after the changes necessitated by the death of Foreign Minister Johan Jorgen Holst. The following introductory paragraph outlines the changes that have been made: "Yesterday Prime Minister Gro Harlem Brundtland made the third set of changes to the lineup of her third government. The new foreign minister after the late Johan Jorgen Holst is former Trade Minister Bjorn Tore Godal, who is succeeded by Social Affairs Minister Grete Knudsen. Hill-Marta Solberg takes over after Knudsen."
