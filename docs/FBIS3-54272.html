Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The most important political, economic, social, and cultural problems of the country were discussed during the joint session of the cabinet and Majles deputies, which was held last night in the presence of President Hashemi-Rafsanjani. Our correspondent reports that during this session, President Hashemi-Rafsanjani expressed satisfaction on the formation of the joint cabinet-Majles deputies' session and underlined the need for coordination and the exchange of expert information between the legislative and executive branches before debates on the state budget at the Majles open session. The president said that the government's economic policies conform to the wishes and directives of the esteemed leader. Stressing the need to implement these policies to achieve development and progress and end the budget's dependence on oil income, he stated: During the first Five-Year Plan, many of the country's serious problems were solved, and with the full support of the esteemed leader, the Majles, and the clergymen, and with the extensive presence of the patient and resistant people of the country who have always displayed their political and social maturity in the various aspects of the Islamic revolution, during the Second Five-Year Plan steps will be taken toward economic progress and decreasing the people's welfare problems. During the session, which was held at the president's office, Majles Speaker Nateq-Nuri also described the joint session of officials from the executive and legislative branches as significant because joint efforts and collective exchanges of views on the country's current and future problems are useful and significant. Listing the activities of the various Majles commissions during the reconstruction era, he stressed the Majles deputies' support for the views and directives of the founder of the Islamic Republic of Iran, His Eminence Imam Khomeyni, may his soul be sanctified, and of the esteemed leader. During the session, several Majles deputies also spoke about political, economic, social, and cultural issues.
