940525 FT 25 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Low-level response to CLS offer The intermediaries offer for CLS Holdings, the property company owned by the Swedish Mortstedt family, has been only 15 per cent subscribed. The London-focused property investment company had fully underwritten an offer of 45.05m new shares at 111p, subject to clawback to cover applications for the 11.26m shares offered to intermediaries. Applications were received for 1.64m shares.
