920411 FT 11 APR 92 / Men hurt in oil platform blast At least four men were injured in a gas explosion yesterday on board the Claymore oil production platform 80 miles north-east of Aberdeen. An official of Elf Enterprise, which owns the platform, said none of the injuries was serious. The company said 'a minor emergency' was declared after a small explosion damaged the exhaust system on one of the platform's compressors.
