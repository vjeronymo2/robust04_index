Committee on Equal Opportunities in Science and Engineering; Meeting In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92&hyph;463, as amended), the National Science Foundation announces the following meeting: Name: Committee on Equal Opportunities in Science and Engineering (CEOSE) (1173). Date and time: June 23, 1994; 8:30 a.m.&hyph;5 p.m. (Open); June 24, 1994; 8:30 a.m.&hyph;12 Noon (Open). Place: Room 375, National Science Foundation, 4201 Wilson Boulevard, Arlington, VA 22230. Type of Meeting: Open. Contact person: Wanda E. Ward, Executive Secretary, CEOSE, National Science Foundation, 4201 Wilson Boulevard, Room 805, Arlington, VA 22230. Telephone: (703) 306&hyph;1604. Summary minutes: May be obtained from the Executive Secretary at the above address. Purpose of meeting: To discuss NSF strategic planning activities, the NSF equal opportunity task force report, human resource development national policy issues, and to review issues about and assessments of participation rates of all segments of society in science and engineering. Agenda: June 23: 8:30 a.m. to 12 Noon_Discussion of NSF strategic planning and human resources development national policy issues; 1:30 p.m. to 5 p.m._Discussion of issues about the participation rates of all segments of society in science and engineering and the equal opportunity task force report; 5 p.m._Recess; June 24: 8:30 a.m. to 12 Noon_Review of assessments of participation rates of all segments of society in science and engineering; future directions.
