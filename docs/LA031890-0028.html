March 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ANTI-GAY VIEWS

I am distressed by an item in the March 11 "Pop Eye" column, particularly the noting of Cinderella's Tom Keifer's hateful statements about gays. It is irresponsible of The Times to provide a forum for such views. If Keifer held similar views about blacks, I don't think his vile thoughts would be treated so lightly.

BRUCE DAVIS

Los Angeles
