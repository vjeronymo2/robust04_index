October 12, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

COLLINS' 18 KILLS LEAD UC IRVINE IN VOLLEYBALL

Kim Collins had 18 kills and 19 digs to help UC Irvine defeat New Mexico State in a Big West Conference women's volleyball match Thursday in Las Cruces, N.M.

Irvine, which improved to 6-11 and 2-6 in conference, won, 15-1, 13-15, 15-7, 15-1.
