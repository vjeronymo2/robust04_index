October 13, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CLIPPERS' HANDLING OF KIMBLE IS TYPICAL

Once again the Clippers have demonstrated that nothing has changed and that they will remain a second-class organization into the 1990s.

The Clippers land Bo Kimble, a bona fide player and a marketing dream, then allow their attorney to belittle him in public. Very intelligent.

TERRENCE J. SCHAFER

Huntington Beach
