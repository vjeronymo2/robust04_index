921120 FT 20 NOV 92 / UK Company News: First Spanish shares rise on talks Shares in First Spanish Investment Trust gained 5p to 57p yesterday after Sir William Harding, chairman, wrote to the Stock Exchange saying that the trust was in discussions with SG Warburg Securities, its adviser, about the possibility of altering its structure. The intention is to reduce the discount - 19 per cent at the time of the letter - at which the share price stands to FSIT's net asset value.
