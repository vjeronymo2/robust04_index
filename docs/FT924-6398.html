921121 FT 21 NOV 92 / Apricot plans to create 140 jobs APRICOT Computers, owned by Mitsubishi Electric of Japan, expects to create an extra 140 jobs at its manufacturing plant at Glenrothes, Fife, over the next three years, increasing employment there by 75 per cent. That follows the opening yesterday of a Pounds 5m extension to the plant.
