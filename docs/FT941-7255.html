940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Merlin Green net revenue at Pounds 865,544 Net assets per share at Merlin International Green Investment Trust fell from 106.6p to 74.6p over the 12 months to the end of December. The figure per zero dividend shares, which were issued during the year, was 54.6p. Net revenue for 1993 came out at Pounds 865,544 (Pounds 593,648) for earnings per share of 3.44p (2.36p). A final dividend of 1.7p is proposed for a total of 3.4p (2.1p).
