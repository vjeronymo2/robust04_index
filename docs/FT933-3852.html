930911 FT 11 SEP 93 / Airports report passenger upturn PASSENGER traffic at BAA airports rose 4.7 per cent last month compared with August last year, confirming the recovery in UK air travel. BAA's airports handled 8.6m passengers last month. Stansted showed the fastest growth with a rise of 18 per cent. Traffic at Heathrow, BAA's biggest airport, rose 6.1 per cent.
