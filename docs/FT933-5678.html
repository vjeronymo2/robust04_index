930902 FT 02 SEP 93 / First BCCI trial delayed THE TRIAL of Mr Nazmudin Virani, the former chairman of Control Securities who was arrested in March last year in connection with the collapse of Bank of Credit and Commerce International, has been adjourned until January 17. The trial, the first of the trials to be heard in connection with BCCI, had been due to start yesterday. Mr Virani was charged last year with more than a dozen charges of conspiracy to defraud. Control Securities is not under investigation.
