November 7, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Pepsi Logo Will Appear on Video Game: Next time the kids get thirsty while playing Nintendo, Pepsi-Cola has a new ploy to get them to say Pepsi, please. The soft-drink maker said the Pepsi logo will appear next year in a new, animated electronic video game called "Magic Johnson's Fast Break." The game is made by Tradewest, a licensee of Nintendo of America. The logo will appear twice in the video game -- once in the game's opening title sequence, and later as a court side sign in the animated basketball arena. Details of the agreement were not disclosed.
