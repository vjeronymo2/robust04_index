BFN [Text] Beijing, April 26 (XINHUA) -- Chairman Qiao Shi of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress (NPC) and visiting Canadian Governor General Ramon John Hnatyshyn had an hour- long discussion on furthering Sino-Canadian friendship and cooperation here today. Qiao said that Hnatyshyn's visit provides a good opportunity as well as a forceful push for the deepening of the traditional relations between the two countries. Hnatyshyn told Qiao that he has had fruitful talks with President Jiang Zemin and acting Premier Zhu Rongji since his arrival yesterday. China's highly successful economic reform has won the Canadian people's admiration and praise, he said, adding that Canada has great interests in expanding cooperation with China. Qiao briefed the visitor on the NPC's efforts in improving the legal system for a socialist market economy in China. Qiao said that in its law-making process, the NPC has drawn experience from other countries. He stressed that to continue to expand Sino-Canadian cooperation in various fields is not only the common desire of the two sides but is also very promising because conditions are available. China is ready to work towards this end, he added. Qiao and Hnatyshyn agreed that the NPC and the Canadian Parliament should have more exchanges with a view to deepening understanding and promoting cooperation. After the meeting, Hnatyshyn was escorted on a brief tour of the Great Hall of the People, which is the seat of the NPC.
