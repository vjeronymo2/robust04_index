931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Brit Thornton rises to Pounds 1.15m at midway Profits at British Thornton Holdings, the packaging and furniture group, rose from Pounds 301,000 to Pounds 1.15m pre-tax for the half year ended October 31. Turnover of Pounds 4.88m (Pounds 4.14m) included an acquisition contribution of Pounds 454,000. At period-end costs had been reduced to Pounds 145,000 (Pounds 444,000) and cash in hand totalled Pounds 2.2m. Earnings improved to 6.56p (2p) and an interim dividend of 2.5p is being paid. Shareholders received a 0.25p special last time after adjusting for a sub-division of the shares. Masterform, acquired earlier in the year, failed to live up to expectations following the cancellation of an order from Sega. The price paid for Masterform may be reduced.
