930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / UK Company News: AB Consulting AB Consulting, which provides services to the construction and engineering industries, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.15m for the year to April 30, against profits of Pounds 41,000. The company said, however, that losses in the second half were reduced to Pounds 303,000, including a provision for bad debts of Pounds 165,000, after losses of Pounds 849,000 in the first six months. Net turnover fell slightly to Pounds 11m (Pounds 11.1m) but there was an underlying fall of 17 per cent as the previous year included only six months of Brian Ford Partnership. Losses per share came out at 6.1p, against earnings of 0.3p. The proposed final dividend is cut to 0.7p for a 1p (4.3p) total.
