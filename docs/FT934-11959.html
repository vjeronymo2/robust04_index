931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / World Stock Markets: Mexico MEXICAN equities rose to another intraday record high following good third-quarter results from Telmex, the telecommunications group, but later fell on profit-taking, leaving the IPC index 18.64 off at 2,010.42, after 2,039.29. An analyst said the market had already been discounting the figures from Telmex: the 'L' shares lost 1.8 per cent.
