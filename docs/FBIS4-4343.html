BFN [Text] His Excellency [H.E.] Khieu Kanharit and H.E. Ung Teaseam, respectively state secretary and under state secretary for information, left Phnom Penh on the morning of 29 March for a three-day working visit to the Royal Kingdom of Thailand from 29 to 31 March. In Thailand, their excellencies will lead a number of national and international reporters and TV crew, including those from National Radio of Cambodia, AKP, BBC of Great Britain, VOA, AFP, CAMBODIAN TIMES, ABC of Australia, PHNOM PENH POST, KYODO of Japan, INDOCHINA REPORT, LE MEKONG, and so on to visit an area bordering Cambodia in Surin Province of Thailand. They will hold a news conference there.
