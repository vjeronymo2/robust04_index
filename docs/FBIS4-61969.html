BFN [Text] The Belarusian Trade Union Federation has received an answer to its appeal -- containing a proposal to adopt a decision on a date for early Supreme Soviet elections -- to Supreme Soviet Chairman Mechyslaw Hryb. The answer reads: The issue has been discussed by the Supreme Soviet Commission for State-Building. A decision to shift the discussion of the draft law on introducing changes and addenda to the Law of the Republic of Belarus on Electing People's Deputies was adopted during a regular meeting of the 13th session. Together with the discussion of this draft law, a draft resolution on fixing a date for Belarusian Supreme Soviet elections will be discussed. It will include a date for these elections, the answer reads. As for the opening date of the regular session, it is unlikely that it will be held before we learn the name of the first Belarusian president. This will probably [words indistinct].
