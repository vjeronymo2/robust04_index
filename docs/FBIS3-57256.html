Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN ["Text" of Azerbaijan Ministry of Defense communique on 24 January] [Text] Unable to resist the attacks of our steadfast fighters, the enemy units have begun to retreat after suffering many casualties. The Armenian side has realized the weakness of its military forces and moved to disseminate unfounded information in order to mislead its own people and the world community. Quoting Armenia's news agencies, Russia's television network reported today that one of Azerbaijan's fighter aircraft has been shot down over Khankendi [Stepanakert]. The Azerbaijan Armed Forces Command Headquarters has disclosed that all of the Republic's military aircraft are ready for action.
