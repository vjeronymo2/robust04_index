920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / Somalia waste dumping probe The UN's environmental agency said yesterday it was sending a mission to Somalia to probe reports that European companies had targeted the devastated country as a dumping ground for toxic wastes, Reuter reports from Nairobi. The announcement follows a flurry of reports that Italian and Swiss groups are involved in a huge project to export hazardous waste to Somalia, devastated by 20 months of civil war and famine.
