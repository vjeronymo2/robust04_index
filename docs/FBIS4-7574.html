BFN [Text] Budapest, 31 Mar (MTI) -- State Secretary Geza Entz, president of the Office of Hungarians Beyond the Border, at a Thursday meeting in Cluj conferred the Middle Cross of the Republic of Hungary Merit of Order on Samu Benko, cultural historian and chairman of the Transylvanian Museum Association, on behalf of President Arpad Goncz. Benko has gained distinction as a researcher and publicist in preserving the cultural identity of Hungarians in Romania. During his tour in Transylvania, Entz met representatives of the Hungarian Democratic Union of Romania and other Hungarian organizations with whom he discussed how Hungarian aid had been used and what future changes and improvement should be introduced. The state secretary also met senior officials from over 30 foundations.
