Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, February 28 (AZERTAC)--Seventy-three bullet-proof waistcoats, 200 bottles of incendiary liquid, 42 artillery shells, grenades and other ammunition were found in the basement of the APF [Azerbaijan People's Front] headquarters by officers of a police department in Baku today. According to Lieutenant-Colonel Gusseyn Allahyarov, the head of the Sabail police department, a few cartridges, one bayonet and three bullet-proof waistcoats were found during an ordinary inspection of fire-prevention conditions of the building. Then, by the city's general attorney's sanction, a search of the whole building was made and arsenal of ammunition was found. Criminal case instituted by this fact is under investigation. There is the general attorneys sanction to make a search of all strong points of the APF.
