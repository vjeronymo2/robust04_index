BFN [Text] Warsaw, 24 Jun--On 20 June, Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak signed a decree on preparing the implementation of "Strategy for Poland," the government plan for socioeconomic policy for the years 1994-97, the Government Press Office reported this evening. On the basis of the decree on the introduction and implementation of "Stretegy for Poland", ministers and heads of central administrative offices will, by 25 June, prepare lists of ministerial tasks ensuant upon "Strategy," the drafts of new legislative documents, and indispensible changes to legislation already in existence. Supervision over the preparatory work has been entrusted to the secretary of the Council of Minister's Economic Committee [KERM]. The timetable of activities making possible the introduction and implementation of the government program, prepared on the basis of ministerial materials, will be presented to the Council of Ministers by the KERM secretary by 5 July of this year, reads the Government Press Office communique.
