August 31, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

LOCAL;

NO WINNING TICKETS IN 'LOTTO 6-49'

There were no winning tickets chosen in Wednesday night's installment of the twice-weekly California "Lotto 6-49" game, so the $4.67-million jackpot will be added to Saturday's drawing, state lottery officials said.

Saturday's jackpot will be an estimated $10 million.

Eleven tickets had five of the six numbers chosen plus the bonus number, and each ticket is worth $117,953. There were 210 winners with five of six numbers. Each will receive $3,183.
