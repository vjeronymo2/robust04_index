921114 FT 14 NOV 92 / Gatwick ballot on industrial action London, are to ballot on industrial action after the proposed introduction of pay cuts averaging 25 per cent. The ballot follows BA's takeover of Dan-Air and affects 1,250 BA ground staff and short-haul cabin crew as part of cost cutting restructuring of BA's Gatwick's services. The cut will not affect 450 former Dan-Air staff. Dan-Air doctor, Page 8
