BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondents Yuriy Sizov, Vladimir Malyshev, Yuriy Malinov] [Text] Kerkira (Corfu), 24 Jun -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin signed a historic document here today -- the agreement on partnership and cooperation between the Russian Federation and the European Union. Jacques Delors, chairman of the European Commission, Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou of Greece, which is ending its chairmanship of the European Union, as well as the leaders of the other EU member-countries put their signatures to the agreement. Speaking at the ceremony, which was held in the church of St. George, Yeltsin stressed that it was a question of "a document of tremendous historical importance," of a decisive step in restoring the unity of the European continent. This morning a meeting took place between the Russian president and Jacques Delors, the chairman of the European commission.
