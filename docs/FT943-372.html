940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Regent Inns 73% up Regent Inns, the pubs and restaurants operator which came to the market in April 1993, announced a 73 per cent increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.34m to Pounds 2.31m in the year to July 2. Turnover advanced to Pounds 15.3m (Pounds 13m). Earnings per share worked through at 13.1p (10.5p) and a proposed final dividend of 3.65p (2.25p) makes a total of 5.4p (2.25p).
