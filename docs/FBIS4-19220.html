BFN [Text] Tirana, April 14 (ATA) -- President of the Italian Republic Oscar Luigi Scalfaro met on April 12 with Albanian Minister of Justice Kudret Cela and a group of Albanian judges who are attending in Rome a preparatory course financed by the Italian Government, Radio Tirana reports. The topics treated in the talks between Albanian Minister Cela and Italian Minister of Justice Giovanni Conso were closer cooperation between Italy and Albania, a special attention to the questions of crime, up to extradition of requested individuals, and judicial assistance. According to a statement of the Italian Ministry of Justice, the two ministers also focused on the new Albanian Civil Code.
