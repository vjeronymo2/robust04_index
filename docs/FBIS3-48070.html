Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Phnom Penh 22 Jan (AKP) -- A delegation from the Austrian Foreign Ministry led by its deputy minister, Wolfgang Schallenberg, arrived in Phnom Penh on Friday [21 January] for an official five-day visit to Cambodia. The delegation was greeted at Pochentong airport by Uch Kim-an, state secretary for foreign affairs and international cooperation, and other Cambodian personalities. The Austrian deputy minister will meet with Prince Norodom Sirivut, deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs and international cooperation, and those in charge of the ministries of information and industry. The delegation plans to visit Angkor Wat in Siem Reap Province on Monday before leaving the next day for Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.
