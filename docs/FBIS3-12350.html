Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A horrendous crime with the proportions of a massacre was committed in the Church of Sayyidat al-Najat in Wata Zuq Mich'il a short while ago. A big explosion occurred in the church while worshipers were taking part in a mass. Initial information indicates that a large number of people were killed or injured. Casualties are being taken to the hospitals in the area, particularly the Sayyidat Lubnan Hospital, which is crowded with casualties. The army and security forces have cordoned off the scene of the explosion and the church perimeter amid a state of panic gripping the area. Adjacent buildings were affected by the explosion along with citizens. We will give you further details as available.
