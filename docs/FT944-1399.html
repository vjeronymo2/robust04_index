941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / International Company News: Coflexip merges with Stena unit Coflexip, the French oilfield services company, yesterday completed a Dollars 230m merger with Stena Offshore, the offshore contracting arm of Stena of Sweden, writes David Lascelles. The two companies claimed the deal would create Europe's largest offshore contracting company, and the world's seventh largest by market capitalisation. The merger was financed by issues of shares and convertible Coflexip stock which, when fully exercised, will give Stena 32 per cent of Coflexip. Coflexip is based in Paris and had sales of Dollars 379m and profits of Dollars 33m in 1993. Stena Offshore specialises in sub-sea contracting in the North Sea. Revenues in 1993 were Dollars 321m and operating profit Dollars 34m. The combined company will be known as Coflexip/Stena Offshore.
