BFN [Editorial Report] Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 1200 GMT on 13 March reports that "29-year-old Tzvi Oren from Tel Aviv was apprehended five days ago at Ben-Gurion Airport in possession of over 1.5 kg of cocaine and thousands of Ecstasy pills. The Tel Aviv Magistrate's Court today remanded him into custody for two weeks and canceled a decree banning the publication of details about the affair." Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 1300 GMT on 15 March reports that "the Police Central District's drug squad yesterday captured five members of a gang which smuggled 8 kg of heroin into Israel from Turkey. Central District Command Asaf Hefetz said that the five are the chief smugglers of the drug into Israel. The drugs were found in a hiding place near Ben-Gurion Airport." An ITIM report on page 3 of Jerusalem THE JERUSALEM POST in English on 23 March notes that "two long time drug dealers who conspired to smuggle a ton of cocaine into the country from Colombia, were sentenced yesterday in Tel Aviv District Court. In a plea bargain arrangement, 'Amos Solmi, 42, of Giv'atayim, also convicted of smuggling heroin from Turkey, was sentenced to 18 years in prison. Yuval Shemesh, 31, who generally lives in Colombia, was sentenced to 7 and 1/2 years after agreeing to testify against other drug dealers." Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 1000 GMT on 24 March reports that "customs inspectors at Ben-Gurion Airport last night captured 2 kg of cocaine in the belongings of a U.S. citizen. The worth of the cocaine is estimated at $2 million."
