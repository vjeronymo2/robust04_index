January 7, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

GERMAN POLICE SEIZE TWO BUS HIJACKERS

West German police commandos on Friday stormed a hijacked bus, freeing two hostages and capturing two drunken gunmen who had demanded money, beer and a plane to Brazil, a spokesman said.

One of the four hostages was slightly hurt Friday when a gunman struck him with his pistol butt. A 60-year-old woman was released early in the drama and another passenger escaped during a stop.

The two gunmen, both in their 20s and with police records, seized the bus in a Bonn suburb about midnight, the spokesman said.

The assailants demanded a ransom of up to $550,000.
