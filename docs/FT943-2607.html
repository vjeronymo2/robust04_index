940919 FT 19 SEP 94 / World News in Brief: Four die in bus crashes Two women and a girl died in Glasgow when a double-decker bus taking Girl Guides on an outing hit a low bridge. On the M5 near Clevedon, Avon, a bandsman died when a coach carrying the Plymouth Royal Marines band plunged off the motorway and overturned. Five other people were seriously hurt.
