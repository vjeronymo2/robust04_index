940609 FT 09 JUN 94 / Searchers find hull of Derbyshire A survey team searching for the wreckage of the bulk carrier Derbyshire which sank in 1980 with the loss of 44 lives confirmed yesterday that they had found the hull of the 189,000-tonne vessel in nearly three miles of water off Japan. A remotely controlled diving vehicle observed the letters 'shire' on a section of the bow, the International Transport Workers' Federation said.
