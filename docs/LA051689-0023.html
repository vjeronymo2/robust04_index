May 16, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

RECOGNITION DINNER SCHEDULED TODAY BY PARA LOS NINOS

Para Los Ninos will hold its fifth annual recognition dinner and carnival today in the Grand Ballroom of the Beverly Wilshire. Olivia Newton-John and Ricardo Montalban are honorary co-chairs.

Festivities will begin at 6:30 p.m. with cocktails, carnival entertainment and a silent auction.

Founded in 1980, Para Los Ninos is a social service agency providing assistance to families with children living in the hotels of downtown Los Angeles and Skid Row.

For ticket information call Georgia di Donato at (213) 277-6934.
