Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague, Jan 14 (CTK) -- U.S. President Bill Clinton's visit brought some extra confidence into Central Europe, Foreign Minister Josef Zieleniec told journalists today. The Prague meeting also helped disperse the unease previously felt in the neighboring countries, Zieleniec said, adding that the Partnership for Peace project holds an extreme importance for the building of Europe and hopefully will have a substantial political dimension.
