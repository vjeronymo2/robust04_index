Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Sources close to Defense Minister Haytham Qasim Tahir said in Aden today that the military situation in the southern Abyan region, where armed clashes took place between northern and southern units on 21 February, is close to the point of exploding. The same sources said that the country is now in an undeclared state of war. The tension follows the signing of an accord agreement last Sunday. The agreement aims to end the political crisis that has been plaguing the country since last August.
