Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondent Lev Ryabchikov for TASS] [Text] Simferopol 24 March, TASS -- Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov signed a decree on Thursday ordering the Republic to switch to Moscow time beginning 27 March. The decision will also affect all railway, water, air and inter-city automobile transportation and communication services. Enterprises and organisation will switch to the new regime on Monday, 28 March.
