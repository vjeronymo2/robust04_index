BFN [Text] Beijing, June 16 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Premier Li Peng met here today with four newly appointed foreign ambassadors to China and five outgoing ambassadors. The new ambassadors were Maimu Raka-Nou [spelling of name as received] of Papua New Guinea, Berhan Ekinci [spelling of name as received] of Turkey, Eduardo Arriagada Moreno [spelling of name as received] of Chile, and Jorge Gordillo [spelling of name as received] Barreto of Peru. The outgoing ambassadors were Sheku Dumbuya of Sierra Leone, Tawfiq al-'Alamdar of Saudi Arabia, Mohamed Habib Kaabachi of Tunisia, Abdul Menan Sheka of Ethiopia, and Protais Bigirimana of Burundi.
