Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] [Correspondent] Vladimir Zhirinovskiy's visit to Vukovar and the occupied areas of the Vukovar and Srijem county, i.e. Sector East where a Russian UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] battalion is deployed, was a point on the agenda of a Vukovar and Srijem Assembly session today, from which a protest note was sent to the Russian embassy saying among other things: [Begin recording] [Matej Jankovic, prefect of the Vukovar and Srijem County] Therefore, I consider it your duty to dissociate yourself from the visit and activities of your parliamentarians, while UNPROFOR in which your soldiers are also deployed, has a duty to prevent Zhirinovskiy from entering an area within the internationally recognized borders of the Republic of Croatia without permission by relevant bodies of our state. [end recording] [Correspondent] It should be noted that the Croatian side has never opposed any visits either to Sector East or the UNPROFOR forces that was regularly announced or organized in cooperation with relevant Croatian bodies.
