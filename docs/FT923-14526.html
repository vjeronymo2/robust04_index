920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Prince in an expansive mood - In a Nutshell The Prince's Youth Business Trust (PYBT), which provides loans and grants to help people aged under 26 into business, will in future provide expansion loans as well start-up finance. The expansion loans, of up to Pounds 5,000, will be available to business owners aged up to 31 years. Start-up finance is available as grants of up to Pounds 1,500 and loans of up to Pounds 5,000. Contact PYBT, 5 Cleveland Place, London SW1Y 6JJ. Tel 071 321 6500.
