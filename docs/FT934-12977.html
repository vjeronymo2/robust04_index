931023 FT 23 OCT 93 / Finance and the Family: Amnesty's affinity credit card At a Glance Amnesty International, the human rights campaigning charity, is launching an affinity credit card with the Co-operative Bank. For every account opened, the bank will pay Amnesty Pounds 5, and for every Pounds 100 spent on the card the charity will receive 20p. The Visa card will be free of an annual charge for life, like other Co-op cards.
