Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 8 (KCNA) -- Phosphate fertilizer producers in Korea are effecting an upswing on the threshold of the 30th anniversary of the publication of the rural theses (February 25). According to data available, the output of phosphate fertilizer in the last ten days was up more than 20 percent from the like period of last month. Notably, the Haeju smeltery nearly doubled output by operating all equipment at full capacity. The Phungnyon and Yongyu mines which overfulfilled their January plans on January 25 are these days producing much more phosphorus concentrates than planned.
