BFN [Text] A delegation led by Dr. Sawit Phothiwihok, minister in the Thai Prime Minister's Office, currently visiting Myanmar [Burma] at the invitation of Energy Minister U Khin Maung Thein, called on Minister U Khin Maung Thein at the Ministry on Prome Road at 0830 this morning. They held cordial and frank discussions on matters concerning mutual benefit in the energy sector.
