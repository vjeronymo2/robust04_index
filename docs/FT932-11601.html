930501 FT 01 MAY 93 / US personal income up Personal income in the US in March increased a seasonally adjusted 0.6% from the previous month, the Commerce Department said, AP-DJ reports from Washington. It rose to an adjusted annual Dollars 5.26 trillion (Pounds 3.41 trillion) after rising a revised 0.1% in the previous month to an adjusted Dollars 5.228 trillion. Meanwhile new factory orders in March fell 1.5% to a seasonally adjusted Dollars 254.53bn.
