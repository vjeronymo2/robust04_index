BFN [Text] Tallinn, Apr 08, BNS -- Prime Minister Mart Laar began Friday [8 April] morning a two-day working visit to Norway, the government press service told BNS. Laar will have meetings with Norwegian Premier Gro Harlem Brundtland and the country's export council. He is also scheduled to attend a Conservative Party conference in Bergen. The Estonian premier is accompanied by his aide Jaan Tross.
