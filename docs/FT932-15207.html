930415 FT 15 APR 93 / World News In Brief: Fayeds sell Sears stake for Pounds 60m loss House of Fraser, the department stores group owned by the Fayed brothers, has sold its 10.6 per cent stake in the Sears retailing group for a book loss of about Pounds 60m. Suggestions from City analysts that the sale indicated the group, whose chairman is Mohamed Fayed, was in financial difficulties were 'entirely untrue', said Michael Cole, a House of Fraser director.
