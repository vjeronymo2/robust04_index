BFN [Text] United Nations, April 23 (XINHUA) -- U.N. Secretary-General Butrus Butrus-Ghali will immediately authorize the use of NATO air strikes against Bosnian Serb Forces if they do not withdraw from a 3-kilometer zone from the center of Gorazde by midnight. "Should the required cease-fire and withdrawal fail to take place by 0001 hours GMT April 24, the secretary-general will immediately authorize the use of air strikes to protect the safe area of Gorazde," Joe Sills, spokesman for the secretary-general, said here this evening.
