October 28, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

COUNTY CANDY SCREENING

Again this year, some county hospitals will screen Halloween treats. Parents should accompany their children if they would like candy checked. While X-rays will show the presence of metal objects, they cannot detect glass, wood or chemical/toxic substances. Here is a countywide selection of hospitals that will provide screening:
