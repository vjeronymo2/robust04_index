BFN [Text] Yerevan ("Azg" daily), 04/02/94 -- Yesterday the delegation of CIS interparliamentary assembly, headed by the Chairman of the Kyrgyz parliament M. Sherimkulov, arrived to Yerevan and met the RA [Republic of Armenia] President Levon Ter-Petrosyan and the Chairman of the RA Supreme Council Babken Ararktsyan. The Special Envoy of the Russian President Vladimir Kazimirov, who is also a member of the delegation, yesterday informed us that a new revised version of the initiative on resolving the conflict was presented to the sides. According to the new project, the sides should establish a cease fire in the first stage, they should take the troops apart next, and than develop mechanisms for preventing further resumption of military operations. The next stage of the project plans to withdraw the military equipment and troops from the occupied territories, lifting the transportation and communications blockade, return of refugees. Observers are planned to be placed in the conflict zone when all the mentioned is implemented. The delegation visited Baku on the previous day, and was going to leave for Stepanakert this morning.
