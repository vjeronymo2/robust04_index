940304 FT 04 MAR 94 / Poll puts Tory support at 25% Support for the Conservatives has fallen from 26 per cent to 25 per cent while Labour has risen from 45.5 per cent a month ago to 48.5 per cent, a Gallup poll for today's Daily Telegraph shows. The Liberal Democrats have fallen from 23 per cent to 21 per cent. The poll also found that 66 per cent of voters do not believe the economy is coming out of recession. A total of 68 per cent of Tory voters say the recession is ending, while 25 per cent say it is not. Only 14 per cent of Labour voters believe the recession is ending.
