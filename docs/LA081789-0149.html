August 17, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

READERS TAKE ISSUE WITH FREEWAY LINK AS A SOLUTION

Perhaps Mr. Ristic has forgotten that the Pasadena Freeway, which ends on Glenarm in Pasadena, cuts South Pasadena in two. Perhaps Mr. Ristic hasn't noticed that South Pasadena is a very small town, and barely has enough room to be cut in two, much less four parts. Perhaps Mr. Ristic hasn't noticed that freeways haven't been moving very freely lately, and are probably not going to be a long-term rapid transit solution.

CAROLE LEVENSON

South Pasadena
