March 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

TO THESE FANS, NCAA MEANS NO CLUE AT ALL

I am appalled by the audacity of the NCAA in its refusal to grant Cal State Long Beach basketball a berth in the postseason tournament.

On one hand, these deities harass numerous coaches and their programs for archaic infractions, yet on the other hand, the NCAA cozies up to CBS and blames their subsequent malfeasance on the late timing of the final Big West match.

The officials of the NCAA should be subject to the same scrutiny and virulent judgment that they parcel out -- and heading the inquisition should be Jerry Tarkanian, Neil McCarthy and Joe Harrington. I'm sure those three gentlemen would not be at a loss to locate transgressions of the accused.

LENORE HAYES

Long Beach
