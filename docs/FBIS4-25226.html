BFN [Text] Beijing, June 7 (XINHUA) -- State Councillor Li Tieying met here today with Dan Ikuma, deputy director of the Japan-China Culture Exchanges Association, and his party. During the meeting in the Great Hall of the People, they exchanged views on enhancing understanding and cooperation between China and Japan, which, they agree, is important not only for the two countries, but also for the peace and stability of Asia and even the world as a whole. The six Japanese visitors arrived here last Saturday [2 July] as guests of the Ministry of Culture.
