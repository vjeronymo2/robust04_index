920522 FT 22 MAY 92 / International Company News: BMW to raise CP programme BAYERISCHE Motoren Werke AG (BMW), the German luxury car maker, is raising its commercial paper (CP) programme to DM1.5bn (Dollars 937m) from DM500m, Reuter reports from Munich. The increase is being arranged by Deutsche Bank and Dresdner Bank. BMW added that Standard & Poor's, the US credit rating corporation has assigned an A-1 plus rating to the CP programme.
