920320 FT 20 MAR 92 / Election 1992: Hurd's maths - Ballot Box Douglas Hurd may be a very professional foreign secretary, but should be kept away from anything to do with statistics. He told an election meeting in Southampton on Wednesday that the reason why the proportion of tax paid by the country's top 5 per cent of salary earners had risen during the government's lifetime was that there are more of them. When an FT correspondent gently pointed out to him that 5 per cent is 5 per cent is 5 per cent, the foreign secretary replied diplomatically that he had 'elided' two separate arguments.
