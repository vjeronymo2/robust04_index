Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tallinn, Mar 29, BNS -- Estonia and France may soon conclude an agreement on cooperation in defense, was the outcome of a meeting yesterday between Estonian Defense Ministry Chancellor Hannes Walter and officials from the French Defense Ministry east European department and the French Foreign Ministry. Walter said at the meeting that, in his view, there are no blocks in the way of extending military cooperation between Estonia and France. The bilateral agreement on defense cooperation is ready to be signed, the Defense Ministry informs. The members of the French delegation said at the meeting that after signing the defense cooperation agreement the two countries might discuss cooperation plans for next year and French-speaking Estonian officers' opportunities for studying at French military academies. The French delegation also met with Col. Ants Laaneots, Estonian Defense Forces chief of staff, and head of naval department Capt. Roland Laid, and visited the National Defense Academy.
