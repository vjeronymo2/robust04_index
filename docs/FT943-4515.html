940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Berry Starquest Berry Starquest, the smaller companies investment trust, reported net asset value per share of 226.4p at 31 July, a fall of 5.5 per cent from 239.6p six months earlier. Net revenue for the half year to end-July was up from Pounds 48,000 to Pounds 59,000. Earnings per share were 1.1p (0.9p).
