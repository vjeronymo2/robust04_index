BFN [Text] Cairo, 5 May (MENA) -- Interior Minister Hasan al-Alfi has affirmed that security forces have succeeded in controlling the situation in the Governorate of Asyut in Upper Egypt. They have struck at the terrorist dens and captured the fugitive terrorists. Al-Alfi said during his meeting today with a number of public security commanders and officers who have been transferred to work in the field of criminal investigations and political security in the governorate that the Interior Ministry is currently developing weapons and equipment and is providing training. He clarified that the ministry is rebuilding its structures in the geographic regions of Upper Egypt. He noted that the ministry's current policy is to strengthen the confidence between the people and their executive leaders to isolate the outlaws and confront them. During the meeting, the minister explained the current security policy. He also spoke about the mistakes they committed in the past, how they dealt with them in the security field, and how they learned about these mistakes.
