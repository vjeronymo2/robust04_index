Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague, March 16 (CTK) -- Lubomir Roman, an actor and director of the Nova Scena Theater in Bratislava, was appointed new Slovak culture minister today, at the proposal of the Christian Democratic Movement (KDH). Roman was born on April 12, 1944, in Malacky, west Slovakia. In 1966 he graduated from Bratislava's Academy of Performing Arts and joined the Nova Scena Theater. In 1990 he became director of this famous Bratislava theater and since that time Nova Scena has become one of the most popular cultural institutions. Roman is also an expert assistant at the Academy of Performing Arts. In 1993 he became president of the Slovak Union of Professional Theaters.
