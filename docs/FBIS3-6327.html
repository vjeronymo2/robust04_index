Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 16 KYODO -- The Fair Trade Commission (FTC) has ordered 15 paint manufacturers to pay 470 million yen in fines for operating an unauthorized cartel, FTC officials said Wednesday [16 March]. Among the firms fined by the FTC, Atom Chemical Paint Co. was ordered to pay 87 million yen, Hitachi Chemical Co. 70 million yen and Sekisui Jushi Corp. 51 million yen. According to the FTC, the companies concluded an unauthorized cartel in October 1990 that served as a front for bid-rigging activities and manipulated the price of paint until December 1991. In December 1992, the FTC ordered the firms to stop the unfair practice.
