May 24, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: POMONA;

HAZARDOUS WASTE ROUNDUP

The county Sanitation Districts will sponsor a household hazardous waste roundup at Cal Poly Pomona, parking lot K, Temple Avenue and South Campus Drive, from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. June 16.

Residents may drop off such materials as paints, used motor oil, pesticides, solvents and automobile batteries.
