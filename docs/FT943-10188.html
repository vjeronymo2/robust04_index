940806 FT 06 AUG 94 / UK Company News: French Property lifts net asset value Net asset value per share of French Property Trust improved from 80.99p to 94.07p over the year to June 30. For the six months to June net revenue rose from Pounds 166,515 to Pounds 189,239. Earnings per share amounted to 0.76p (0.67p).
