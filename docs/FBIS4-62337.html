BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Konstantin Zhukovskiy] [Text] Moscow, 16 May (ITAR-TASS) -- Talks between Russia's Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and his Argentinian counterpart Guido Di Tella, who is in Russia on an official visit, took place today at the Russian Foreign Ministry. At the end of the talks, the sides initialled an agreement on the fundamentals of bilateral relations and signed an agreement on simplifying the visa regulations of the two countries.
