930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / World Commodities Prices: Wool Last week's government package of measures helpful to Australian wool producers facing uneconomically low prices was welcomed in the trade as a whole but seems to have done little to improve demand. Only small-scale orders are indicated and prices remain extremely competitive. The Australian Wool Corporation's market indicator was 6 cents higher at 395 cents a kg. on May 5, with finer margins gaining relatively well. The main wool market problem of over-supply remains.
