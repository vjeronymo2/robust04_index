920229 FT 29 FEB 92 / Briefcase, Q&A: Limits on gifts IS IT possible for a parent to gift each child Pounds 3,000 pa without future inheritance tax liability, or is a parent restricted to gifting a total of Pounds 3,000 pa. The Pounds 3,000 annual exemption is restricted to Pounds 3,000 for each donor (plus any unused element of the previous year's exemption - but not more than one year is available). If you make gifts to several people the total of all gifts should not exceed Pounds 3,000: see section 19 of the Inheritance Tax Act 1984.
