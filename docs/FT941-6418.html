940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / Improvements for GNVQs sought Mr Tim Boswell, further education minister, yesterday called for improvements in the general national vocational qualifications which the government is trying to introduce as alternative routes to university. He said it was necessary to: Improve the external testing regime. Provide more training for external verifiers. Clarify the grading criteria. Clarify what knowledge was needed for a GNVQ. Provide clearer guidance for teachers on marking coursework, grading and setting up and designing courses. Tighten criteria for accrediting schools and colleges offering GNVQs.
