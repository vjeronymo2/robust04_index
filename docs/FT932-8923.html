930515 FT 15 MAY 93 / International Company News: Whirlpool forms Taiwan venture WHIRLPOOL, the largest manufacturer of major domestic appliances, yesterday announced that it was forming a marketing and distribution joint venture in Taiwan. Whirlpool will acquire a 40 per cent interest in Great Teco Trade, part of Teco Electric & Machinery. Great Teco has been a Whirlpool distributor for seven years. The announcement is the latest in a series of moves by the US company to step up its inroads into the Asian market.
