Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Hannover (DPA) -- The Lower Saxon IG-Metall trade union has agreed to the pilot agreement in the west Gemran metal industry. In a second strike ballot 63.6 percent agreed to the wage compromise, IG-Metall District Chairman Juergen Peters announced in Hannover on Friday. A total of 41,310 IG-Metall members had been called to the ballot.
