BFN [Text] Vice Premier and Economics Minister Aleksandr Shokhin cannot fly to Korea on Thursday as planned. Interfax learned at Shokhin's office that Yuriy Yarov would go in his place. Officials said that the change had been made at the request of Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin who had given Shokhin urgent work, the nature of which is unknown. They denied reports that the incident was linked to a possible changes in the government. Shokhin had intended to spend two days in Seoul and discuss bilateral trade and economic cooperation.
