Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Russian Federation Security Council"] [Text] Oleg Lobov, secretary of the Russian Federation Security Council, received French Defense Minister Francois Leotard 3 February. They had a thorough, frank conversation on a broad range of questions of maintaining stability and security on the European Continent and in the world as a whole. They discussed problems pertaining to the prevention and cessation of regional conflicts, the preparation and implementation of peacekeeping operations, and the prospects for Franco-Russian cooperation. Pierre Morel, French ambassador to the Russian Federation, took part in the conversation.
