Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The leader of Russia's Liberal Democratic Party, Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, has ignored the demand that he leave Slovenia. He has described the Slovenian foreign ministry's demand as provocative, saying he will leave the country as previously planned on Sunday. The ministry says Zhirinovskiy's escorts were reported by police to have kicked up a row and destroyed property in the resort city of Bled. The ministry reacted with the demand that Zhirinovskiy and his men leave Slovenia.
