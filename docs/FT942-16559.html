940409 FT 09 APR 94 / UK Company News: Booker sells Canadian offshoot for Pounds 6.8m Booker, the food group, has sold Lovell & Christmas (Canada) to Ault Foods of Toronto for CDollars 14m (Pounds 6.8m) cash. Lovell & Christmas, based in Montreal, imports, exports and sells cheese and other dairy products. The sale will reduce Booker's borrowings by about Pounds 5m.
