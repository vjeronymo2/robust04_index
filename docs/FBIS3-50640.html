Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] San Cristobal de las Casas, 4 Jan (NOTIMEX) -- The Mexican Air Force launched today a brief operation in a mountainous area near this southeastern Chiapas city against armed groups dislodged from several towns, witnesses reported. Local clergyman Gonzalo Ituarte Verduzco said that airplanes and a helicopter fired shots between 1700 and 1800 (2300 and 0000 GMT) near El Corralito, southeast of San Cristobal de la Casas, and the armed groups responded from the ground. San Cristobal de las Casas, one of the first cities seized on 1 January and occupied the following day by armed men of the so-called Zapatista National Liberation Army, is under the control of civilian and military authorities. The violent seizure of towns and Army attacks have left 96 dead, including 59 members of the armed groups, 22 policemen, seven soldiers, and five civilians, according to official reports.
