940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Barlows in black Barlows, the Chester-based property company, returned to profits in the six months to June 30 with Pounds 208,000 pre-tax against losses of Pounds 202,000. Turnover was higher at Pounds 927,000 (Pounds 768,000) and there was a profit on sale of properties of Pounds 126,000, against a loss of Pounds 69,000. Earnings per share were 1.04p (losses 1p).
