BFN [Report: "Jiang Zemin, Qian Qichen Inspect Zhuhai"] [Text] Jiang Zemin, general secretary of the CPC and president of the PRC, inspected Zhuhai, Guangdong, 17-19 June. On 18 June, he came to the Zhuhai People's Hospital to visit and extend regards to workers who were injured when the former Zhuhai Shanyuxin textile factory collapsed after catching fire, kindly telling them to concentrate on receiving treatment; the workers were greatly moved. After staying in the wards on the first and third floors for more than 10 minutes, Jiang left in a car. According to an injured worker in a ward, President Jiang asked each patient the following questions: Where are you injured? Where do you live? Do you have any relatives in Zhuhai? He hoped they would concentrate on receiving medical treatment and wished they would recover soon. The injured workers were very glad at the visit by a state leader. In the afternoon of the same day, Qian Qichen, vice premier and foreign minister, came to the site where the textile factory had collapsed so as to gain an understanding of the situation. He expressed appreciation for the Zhuhai Government's efforts to rescue the victims. It has been proved that 24 people died as a result of the accident. The relatives of the dead have begun to identify the remains. Over 50 percent of the accident site has been cleaned up.
