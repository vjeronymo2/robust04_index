BFN [Text] Kiev, 10 May -- A government delegation of Ukraine headed by deputy premier Valeriy Shmarov has left for Washington. The delegation will hold talks with the State Department, the National Security Council, and the U.S. Departments of Defense and of Energy. The aim of the talks is to elaborate the principles of cooperation with Ukraine and the United States in the area of the peaceful development of space, discuss the process of implementation of the trilateral statement and bilateral agreements on issues of conversion and nuclear disarmament, and the state of implementation of other Ukrainian-U.S. agreements.
