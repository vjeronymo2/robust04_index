930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / International Company News: Canada mine groups in central Asian deal CAMECO and Uranerz, Canada's biggest uranium producers, have signed a joint 10-year marketing and technical development agreement with Katep, the uranium producer controlled by the Republic of Kazakhstan, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. As a first step, the Canadian companies will invest USDollars 3m in Katep's uranium facilities to improve efficiency, safety and environmental performance. Acting as agents, they will help Katep sell its uranium concentrates on long-term contract to world customers.
