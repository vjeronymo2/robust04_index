BFN [Text] Brother 'Abdallah 'Abd-al-Majid al-Asnaj, foreign affairs minister of the Democratic Republic of Yemen, said recognizing the Democratic Republic of Yemen has become a matter of time, and was neither a difficult, nor an impossible matter. In an interview with the Lebanese AL-ANWAR, Brother 'Abdallah al-Asnaj expected that recognizing the Democratic Republic of Yemen could take place in a few days, not weeks. Brother al-Asnaj accused the rulers of Sanaa of waging a barbaric war against the southerners with the aim of occupying the south.
