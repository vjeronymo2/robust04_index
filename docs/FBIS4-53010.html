BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 16 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song's famous work "Answers to the Questions Raised by the U.S. CNN News Coverage Group" was reported by the Maltese paper L'ORIZZONT, the Russian paper VECHERNAYA MOSKVA and the Hungarian paper SZABADSAG. The Maltese and Russian papers printed portraits of President Kim Il-song. The work "10-point Programme of the Great Unity of the Whole Nation for the Reunification of the Country" was carried by the Nicaraguan paper AVANCE, and the work "On Further Developing the Communist Policy" by the Mongolian paper UNEN.
