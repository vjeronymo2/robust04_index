940614 FT 14 JUN 94 / Observer: Canny response An Essex man has pleaded guilty to leaving a device comprising a jam-jar, petrol, battery, wires and a clothes peg in a derelict caravan. His solicitor, in a plea of mitigation, told Chelmsford Crown Court that there were others involved in the plot to clear the site of illegal caravans and that his client was 'carrying the can for everybody on that site'.
