Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Mersin, Mar. 24 (A.A.) -- Two unidentified men hurled bombs at the campaign office of the Motherland Party (ANAP) in this southern city on Wednesday. Police said the attack took place at around 8 P.M. In Istanbul, molotov cocktails were hurled at the election office of coalition partner Social Democrat Populist Party (SHP) the same day. There were casualties in the incident.
