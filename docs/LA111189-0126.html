November 11, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

TWA earnings -- An item in Friday's earnings column incorrectly stated the third-quarter results of Trans World Airlines. The airline company reported a net loss of $29.9 million, contrasted with a profit of $88 million in the same period a year ago. Revenue was unchanged at $1.3 billion. The New York-based firm cited increased interest expenses and a non-recurring gain from litigation in the 1988 period.
