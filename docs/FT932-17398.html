930401 FT 01 APR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Education secretary welcomes a 'new era' for further education EDUCATION secretary John Patten yesterday welcomed what he described as a 'new era' for further education, as further-education and sixth-form colleges moved from the control of local authorities to the independent further-education sector financed directly by central government. Mr Patten said that over the next three years the government would fund a 25 per cent expansion in further education. 'Now colleges are free to deliver the growth in opportunities,' he said, launching the changes in London with students from London colleges.
