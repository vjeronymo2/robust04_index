930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Widney shares suspended for purchase Shares in Widney, the maker of windows and water treatment, power transmission and electronic equipment, were suspended at 7p yesterday after it announced it was making an acquisition. At the same time it reported increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 417,000 for the six months to April 3, against Pounds 155,000 restated for FRS 3. Turnover was Pounds 9.88m (Pounds 9.69m). Losses per share were 0.91p (0.43p). The consideration involves an initial payment of Pounds 3.2m satisfied by shares and a further profit-related payment to a maximum Pounds 800,000. The vendors are placing shares worth Pounds 1.76m and Widney is raising a further Pounds 3.8m in a placing to cover acquisition costs and for working capital. Both are subject to open offers to existing shareholders. After the purchase, the company intends seeking approval for cancelling part of the share premium account equal to the debit balance on the profit and loss account allowing proposals for clearance of the arrears of the preference dividends.
