BFN [Excerpt] Lieutenant General Mustafa Talas, deputy commander in chief of the Army and Armed Forces, deputy prime minister, and defense minister, received at his office today Army General Mikhail Kolesnikov, the Russian chief of staff, and his delegation. The meeting was attended by Lieutenant General Hikmat al-Shihabi, chief of staff of the Army and Armed Forces, and Assistant Chiefs of Staff Lieutenant General 'Ali Aslan and Hasan Turkmani. Discussion revolved around the ties of friendship and cooperation between the Armed Forces in Syria and the Russian Federation and means of improving them in a manner that will serve the interests of the two friendly countries.
