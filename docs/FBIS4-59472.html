BFN [Text] 'Adnan 'Imran, Arab League assistant secretary general, has expressed regret and astonishment at the recent unjustifiable U.S. decision to include some Arab states, especially Syria, in the list of states sponsoring terrorism. In an exclusive statement, 'Imran said the U.S. statement has no credibility. Reality proves the error of the information that blemishes Syria's reputation and role. He hoped there will be a fundamental reconsideration of U.S. Middle East policy and that a form of balance and nonalignment will be established.
