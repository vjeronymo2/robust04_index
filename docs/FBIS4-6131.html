BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 15 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- At the invitation of the President of Mongolia P. Ochirbat the General Governor of Australia Bill Hayden has arrived in Ulaanbaatar on an official visit. The General Governor Bill Hayden was met on his arrival at the Buyanuaa Airport by the President of Mongolia P. Ochirbat, the Prime Minister P. Jasray and other officials.
