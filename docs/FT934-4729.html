931202 FT 02 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Millgate poised for USM float Millgate is coming to the USM through the placing of 17.8m shares at 40p to raise a net Pounds 6.6m. Of the proceeds Pounds 6.02m will be used to purchase Laserline Car Alarms (UK), and the balance for working capital. An additional Pounds 400,000 is being raised by the sale of shares at 40p to some directors and Laserline SpA, the Italian manufacturer of the alarms which Laserline (UK) imports. In the six months to May 31 Laserline (UK) reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 455,000 on turnover of Pounds 2.97m. Net assets are warranted at not less than Pounds 666,500.
