940510 FT 10 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Cornwell Parker makes Pounds 1.1m buy Cornwell Parker, the furniture and fabrics group, has acquired certain of the assets of Minty Design Furniture from Mines & West Holdings for about Pounds 1.1m, payable in cash. Minty designs and makes chairs and settees and had sales of Dollars 2.9m (Pounds 2m) in the year to March 1994, of which about 40 per cent were to continental Europe.
