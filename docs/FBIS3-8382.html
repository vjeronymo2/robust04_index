Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] A statement issued by the Academy of Sciences and Arts of Kosova states that despite the sequestration of the building by the Serbian authorities, this highest scientific institution will continue with its public activity. This public statement confirms that there has been concrete support from the European Academy of Sciences and other scientific academies, which is encouraging and show their high regard. These academies are maintaining their contacts with the Academy of Sciences and Arts of the republic of Kosova by rejecting the repressive acts of the Serbian regime against it. This statement expresses special gratitude to the Academy of Arts of Albania and high-level political personalities, who, by denouncing this particular and repressive act of the Serbian regime, express their solidarity with the academy and its future work.
