April 18, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

TUSTIN'S JONES TO PLAY BASEBALL AT NORTHRIDGE

Tustin High School pitcher Monte Jones has signed a national letter of intent to play baseball at Cal State Northridge, Tustin Coach Vince Brown said Monday.

Jones is 6-0 with a 1.34 earned-run average this season. He also has 74 strikeouts.
