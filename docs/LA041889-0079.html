April 18, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY PEOPLE

CDI Temporary Services promoted Sharon Heller to vice president in charge of the company's Southern California operations, which are based in Westlake Village. She previously was district manager of CDI's Simi Valley and Westlake Village branch offices.

CDI, based in Philadelphia, provides workers and other temporary business services. The company has 10 branches in Southern California.
