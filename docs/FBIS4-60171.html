BFN [Text] Jordan today reiterated its support for Yemeni legitimacy and the unity of the Yemeni people, refusing, at the same time, to clearly condemn Aden's decision yesterday to establish an independent state in the south. Government-run Jordanian radio and television have been reporting, without comment, the views of northerners and southerners. However, official Jordanian media have recently referred to the southerners as the separatist forces, the description used by the northerners, who are referred to as the government forces.
