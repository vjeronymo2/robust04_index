August 30, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

FOOD VILLAGES OFFER A FESTIVAL OF PACIFIC RIM FLAVORS

You can enjoy more than the art and culture of each nation sending performing groups to the 1990 Los Angeles Festival: You can also sample its cuisine. Each weekend during the celebration, restaurants and community groups will sell foods representative of the Pacific Rim countries participating in the event.

The food village will be at a different venue each weekend. Here are the locations, dates and types of foods you can expect to find.
