931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / UK Company News: F&C Pep net asset value at 116.9p Foreign & Colonial Pep Investment Trust had a net asset value of 116.9p per share at September 30, up from 97.5p at the inception of dealing in October 1992. Available revenue for the 12 months to end-September was Pounds 1.37m for earnings of 4.32p per share. A recommended final dividend of 2.5p brings the total to 3.4p.
