921016 FT 16 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Tamaris Tamaris, operator of nursing homes, made inroads into its losses in the year ended March 31 1992, cutting them from Pounds 2.29m to Pounds 955,000. Also this time, extraordinary losses were down from Pounds 2.35m to Pounds 661,000, to leave an overall deficit for the year of Pounds 1.62m (Pounds 4.88m). Turnover was Pounds 2.8m (Pounds 3.58m). Losses per share worked through at 6.55p (19.47p) basic and 4.57p (14.77p) fully diluted.
