Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] Representatives of the Kovo trade union, which has 280,000 trade unionists as members, have decided not to sign this year's general agreement with the government. Eugen Skultety, deputy chairman of the trade union, said the reason for the decision was the government's restrictive measures on price regulation due to come into force on 1 April. Citizens will not be able to cope with these measures, he said.
