920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Highcroft Trust advances 15% A Pounds 181,000 increase in gross rental income to Pounds 954,000 helped Highcroft Investment Trust report a 15 per cent rise in attributable profits to Pounds 593,000 for 1991. The dividend for the year is raised to 4.5p (3.9p) via a final of 2.85p. Earnings per share rose 15 per cent to 10.9p.
