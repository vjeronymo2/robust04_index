BFN [By reporters Zhang Jimin (1728 4949 3046) and Qin Chun (4440 2504)] [Text] Beijing, 7 Jun (XINHUA) -- Zhu Guangya was elected president of the Chinese Academy of Engineerng [CAE] at the plenary session of the CAE's academicians' conference today. Zhu Gaofeng, Shi Changxu, Pan Jiazheng, and Lu Liangshu were elected vice presidents. The "Constitution of the Chinese Academy of Engineering" was adopted at the beginning of the meeting to mark the founding of the CAE and at the CAE's first academicians' conference. The Presidium of the CAE was elected according to the stipulations of the Constitution. Members of the Presidium included (listed according to the number of strokes of the Chinese family names): Ding Henggao, Wang Daheng, Lu Liangshu, Shi Changxu, Zhu Guangya, Zhu Gaofeng, Song Jian, Zhang Wei, Zhang Guangdou, Lu Yuanjiu, Fang Weitang, Lin Hua, Luo Peilin, Ji Guobiao, Jin Yilian, Zhou Ganzhi, Hou Xianglin, Yao Fusheng, Qian Yi, Yin Ruiyu, and Pan Jiazheng. Zhu Guangya is the executive chairman of the CAS' Presidium.
