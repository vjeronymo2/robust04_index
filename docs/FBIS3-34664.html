Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The mysterious "Islamic Group" statement warning and threatening foreigners in Egypt has drawn many reactions. In an exclusive statement to AL-'ARABI, Muslim Brotherhood spokesman counselor Ma'mun al-Hudaybi dismissed the statement of the so-called Islamic Group as "a stupid statement issued by an unenlightened quarter." The statement is unacceptable from all religious and human aspects because it harms the interests of the country, he said. A person describing himself as a leader of the so-called Islamic Jihad Organization telephoned AL-'ARABI and strongly denounced Tal'at Qasim's statement, saying it provides an excuse for attacking Islamic groups.
