930429 FT 29 APR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Manchester seats set to be axed GREATER Manchester is to lose two of its 30 Commons seats under boundary commissioners' provisional plans disclosed last night. The two constituencies earmarked for re-organisation are Labour's Manchester Gorton, held by Mr Gerald Kaufman, the former shadow foreign secretary, and Littleborough and Saddleworth, represented by senior Tory Mr Geoffrey Dickens. The commission has to report its plans for the re-drawing of constituency maps for the whole country by the end of 1994.
