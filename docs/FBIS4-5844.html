BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 13 -- A delegation of the Communist Party of Vietnam led by Dao Duy Tung member of the Politbureau and secretary of the party Central Committee left Japan today concluded a week-long visit to Japan. [paragraph as received] While there the delegation was received and given a banquet by Fuwa Tetsuzo, president of the Japan Communist Party's [JCP's] Presidium, and held talks with a JCP delegation. Vietnamese Ambassador to Japan Nguyen Tam Chien also took part in the delegation's activities.
