BFN [Text] Bratislava, June 14 (CTK) -- As President Michal Kovac has caught flu, he has canceled some events planned for this week, presidential spokeswoman Anna Turenicova told CTK today. Kovac was scheduled to visit East Slovakia and open a branch of the Presidential Office in Kosice, East Slovakia. As the illness is not serious, "his visit to the United States, planned for next week, will take place," Turenicova added. Along with his Hungarian counterpart Arpad Goncz, Kovac will be presented the prestigious award of the Institute for East-West Security Studies (IEWSS) on June 22 in New York. They have been conferred the award for their remarkable contribution to international cooperation and understanding between the two countries' nations and effort to settle ethnic problems.
