BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, June 1 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The Asian Bank for Development is granting to Mongolia some 25 million US dollars worth of credits for the purpose of promoting the development of telecommunications system in the country. The talks on this matter were held recently in Manila where participating was Vice President of the Mongolbank S. Chuluunbat. According to him, in granting of those credits other foreign countries are to take part too. For instance, the Government of Norway decided to provide together with some other Scandinavian countries 18 million USD credits part of which would be granted in the form of gratis aid.
