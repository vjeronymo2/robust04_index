BFN [Text] Tehran, June 8, IRNA -- The Vienna conference has decided to include Iran in the coordination group set up for the reconstruction of Sarajevo, envoy of the U.N. secretary-general, William Eagleton announced here Wednesday. Eagleton in a meeting with Deputy Foreign Minister for International Affairs Mohammad Javad Zarif also presented the documents of various projects for reconstruction of Sarajevo, and explained the projects's priorities. Zarif in return emphasized the importance of supporting the reconstruction projects in Sarajevo and said Iran was ready to have active presence in them. The conference on the reconstruction of Sarajevo was held in Vienna on 24-25 May at the invitation of the U.N. secretary general envoy to the former Yugoslavia. Senior officials of the Bosnian Government and representatives from 30 countries including Iran participated in the conference.
