920210 FT 10 FEB 92 / International Company News: L'Oreal sales at FFr33.44bn L'OREAL, the French cosmetics group, reported a 10 per cent increase in 1991 sales to FFr33.44bn (Dollars 6.18bn) and said net profits for the year have grown at a faster rate, writes Alice Rawsthorn. This represents a slight slowdown in the rate of sales growth for L'Oreal. During 1990 L'Oreal, whose brands include Lancome cosmetics and the Helena Rubinstein beauty range, mustered sales growth of 11.7 per cent to FFr30.4bn with net profits growing by 15 per cent to FFr1.69bn.
