BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 14 (KCNA) -- A film week began from June 13 to mark the 30th anniversary of the start of work by the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il at the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea. During the film week all the cinema houses across the country will screen many documentary films including "Great Guidance Leading Our Party Always to Victories," "Great Leadership Glorifying Revolutionary Traditions Through Ages," "Korea, I Will Glorify Thee," and "The Great Paragon," which record the feats of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il in his leadership. The feature films "Guarantee" (Part one and two,) "The Looks of a Party Member" and "Story About a Flower" and the multi-part feature film "The Nation and Destiny" will be screened during the week. The opening ceremony of the film week took place at the Kaeson Cinema House in Pyongyang Monday. An opening address was made by Vice Premier of the Administration Council and Minister of Culture and Art Chang Chol.
