BFN ["Statement" of the National Army of Democratic Kampuchea rejecting slanderous remarks against Democratic Kampuchea concerning missing foreigners -- read by announcer] [Text] I. The allies, communist Vietnamese, and communist Vietnamese puppets recently made remarks slandering Democratic Kampuchea concerning the case of missing foreigners. II. The National Army of Democratic Kampuchea [NADK] spokesman vehemently rejects these slanderous remarks and wishes to clarify the matter as follows: A. We are not involved in this matter. There is no reason for us to commit such an abominable act like the allies, communist Vietnamese, and communist Vietnamese puppets who always claim to be democratic or masters of human rights despite the fact that they have killed hundreds of thousands and even millions of people. B. We have strived and are striving to end the war and to bring about genuine national reconciliation and peace so that the people can lead a normal life and earn their living peacefully. [Dated] 27 May 1994 [Signed] The NADK spokesman
