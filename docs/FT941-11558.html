940203 FT 03 FEB 94 / International Company News: Reader's Digest advances 25% Reader's Digest exceeded Wall Street's expectations by posting a 25 per cent improvement in net income in the three months to end-December, writes Frank McGurty in New York. The publishing group posted net income of Dollars 88.8m, or 76 cents a share, in its second fiscal quarter, compared with Dollars 70.3m, or 59 cents, in the corresponding 1992 period. On an operating basis, profits were 30 per cent up after stripping out currency fluctuations.
