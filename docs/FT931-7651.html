930223 FT 23 FEB 93 / International Company News: Woolworths IPO advisers ADELAIDE Steamship, the Australian investment group, has named the advisers for the initial public offering of Woolworths, although it says there is still no firm timetable for the sale, AP-DJ reports from Sydney. The IPO, expected to raise about ADollars 2bn (USDollars 1.38bn), was deferred in September after the stock market weakened. Adsteam named BT Corporate Finance, a unit of Bankers Trust Australia, strategic adviser. Mr Terry Campbell, of J. B. Were & Son brokerage, and Mr John McIntosh, of McIntosh, have been appointed as broking advisers.
