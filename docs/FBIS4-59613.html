BFN [Text] Sanandaj (Kurdistan Prov.), May 12, IRNA -- The situation in the northeastern Iraqi city of Sulaimaniya is chaotic, sources close to the Islamic Movement of Iraqi Kurdistan reported here today. They added that sporadic clashes continue in northern Iraq among the Kurdish groups. They said that on Wednesday at Dyana and Rawndouz the Kurdish Hizbullah revolutionary forces led by Sheikh Adham Barzani have regained control of Ziweh and Barziweh from the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan (PUK). The Islamic Movement of Iraqi Kurdistan said its forces attacked Khormal, Rouzeh Biareh and Towaila in response to recent aggressions of the Jalal Talebani led PUK. The reports have yet to be confirmed by independent sources.
