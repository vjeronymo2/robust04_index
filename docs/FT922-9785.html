920506 FT 06 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Ward shares suspended Shares in Ward Group, the loss-making structural steel and building components combine, were suspended yesterday at the company's request pending clarification of its financial position. A statement on its future is expected later this week. The shares were suspended at 63p; a year ago they were standing at more than 200p. During the first half of last year Ward incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 3.48m, compared with a profit of Pounds 5.28m, blaming the downturn on the recession in the construction industry.
