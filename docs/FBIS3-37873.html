Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko completed his visit to the Federative Republic of Germany. This morning, our delegation visited the Bundestag in Bonn and met with members of the Foreign Affairs Committee and the working group Ukraine. German legislators were briefed on the situation in Ukraine and on specific ways of developing bilateral economic cooperation. Particularly, this concerned establishing a strong legal treaty basis, granting technical assistance and loans to Ukraine, and holding a meeting between delegations on economic issues in the near future. During his visit to Germany, Anatoliy Zlenko delivered to the German side a memorandum specifying Ukraine's proposals and urgent economic needs. During the visit to Bonn, also held was a meeting between Minister for External Economic Relations Oleh Slepichev and Health Minister Yuriy Spizhenko and representatives of business circles of the Federative Republic of Germany.
