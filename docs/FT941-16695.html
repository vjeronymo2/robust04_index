940108 FT 08 JAN 94 / UK Company News: North Sea Assets Pounds 1.35m purchase North Sea Assets, through its HMB Subwork subsidiary, has acquired Scandive, a leading privately-owned Norwegian operator of remote operated vehicles, for Pounds 1.35m cash. HMB is the largest British-owned operator of remote operated vehicles with a strong presence in the North Sea, west Africa and the Middle East.
