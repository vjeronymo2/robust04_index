BFN [Editorial Report] A report by Arye Egozi on page 14 in Tel Aviv YEDI'OT AHARONOT in Hebrew on 11 April states: ``About 7 kg of a special brand of hashish was seized in the possession of two young men and a young woman as they returned from India yesterday. The three were detained for interrogation.'' An ITIM report on page 5 of Tel Aviv DAVAR in Hebrew on 11 April reports: ``Six bags containing 60 doses of heroin weighing a total of 33 grams were excreted by 24-year-old Ronen Sabag from Haifa, a police investigator yesterday told Peace Magistrate Ehud Reqem. She added that the bags were excreted following a medical treatment.'' A report by Eli Brener on the same page of DAVAR adds that ``the Ayalon District Police yesterday detained 16 suspected drug dealers'' who had operated in Yafo and Bat-Yam. The police acted in cooperation with a former drug dealer, who became a police agent.
