September 4, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

TOM MARTIN PROVIDES INTERESTING OPENING ACT

Opening the show for Paul Rodriguez was Tom Martin, an Orange County comic with a soft style and easy flow.

Here are some examples of why he is worth catching:

He is out to dinner with his family and orders a grilled cheese sandwich, only to be admonished by his mother and told to get something he can't get at home. In that case, "I'll have some understanding and compassion, please."

Talking about how messy he and his college roommate were, he says: "The landlord asked us to wipe our feet before going outside." GLENN DOGGRELL
