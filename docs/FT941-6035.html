940303 FT 03 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Interim lift to Pounds 1.12m at SR Gent A Pounds 1m turnround to profits from related companies' contributions helped SR Gent, the Marks and Spencer garment supplier, lift pre-tax profits from Pounds 986,000 to Pounds 1.12m in the six months to December 31. Turnover grew to Pounds 62.4m (Pounds 59.8m), while operating profits were lower at Pounds 1.79m (Pounds 2.44m). The share of profits of related companies amounted to Pounds 533,000 (Pounds 476,000 losses). Earnings per share were 1.8p (1.5p) and the interim dividend is lifted to 0.9p (0.75p).
