930427 FT 27 APR 93 / UK Company News: 34% upsurge gives Slingsby Pounds 111,000 A 34 per cent increase in pre-tax profit was achieved by HC Slingsby in 1992. From turnover of Pounds 10m (Pounds 9.95m) profit of this truck, ladder and ancillary equipment manufacturer worked through to Pounds 111,000 (Pounds 82,500). Operating profit quadrupled to Pounds 179,000 but had to bear the cost of redundancies of Pounds 105,000. The final dividend is 5.5p, maintaining the total at 7.5p out of earnings per share of 9.3p (4.4p).
