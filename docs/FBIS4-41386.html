BFN [Text] If UN troops come into the Dniester region they will not be perceived by the population as peacekeepers. This was said at a news conference today by Igor Smirnov, president of the Dniester-Moldavia [Moldova] Republic. Speaking about the results of the conference of international cooperation in peacekeeping in regions of conflict on the territory of the CIS, which was held in Moscow from 20-22 June, he expressed the firm conviction that only Russia has the right to peacekeeping activities in the Dniester region.
