July 14, 1989, Friday, Orange County P.M. Final

LOCAL;

SANTA ANA MAN ARRESTED IN SLAYING

An unidentified Santa Ana man was arrested on the Santa Ana Freeway in Anaheim this morning after he was seen fleeing the scene of a shooting death, police said.

The victim, believed to be the suspect's wife, also remained unidentified this morning. Santa Ana police spokeswoman Maureen Thomas said the victim died at her residence in the 5000 block of West Roberts Drive in Santa Ana.

The neighborhood where the shooting took place is in west Santa Ana.

Police cordoned off the neighborhood as part of their investigation.
