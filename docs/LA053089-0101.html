May 30, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County P.M. Final

LOCAL;

GUN VICTIM IN ALLEY SANTA ANA YOUTH

Police today identified a youth who was shot to death then dumped in an alley as Javier Gutierrez, 17, of Santa Ana.

Gutierrez' body was found in an alley in the 1100 block of South Raitt Street on Saturday evening with a single gunshot wound to the head.

Police are investigating the shooting as a homicide and say it is likely Gutierrez was killed somewhere else and his body then dumped in the alley.
