940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / International Company News: Bahrain bank breaks dividend famine Bahrain-based Bank of Bahrain and Kuwait said it proposed to pay its first dividend - BD0.10 - since 1984, Reuter reports from Manama. Directors said the total dividend payout would amount to BD5.69m (Dollars 15.1m). The 1993 profit figures were not yet available, the bank said.
