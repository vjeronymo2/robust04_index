June 15, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Julie Rocheleau-Baumann, a Canadian hurdler, has been suspended for two years after a second test result indicated that she used steroids, the Canadian Track and Field Assn. announced.

The association said it acted after the Sport Medicine Council of Canada had reported that a urine sample from Rocheleau-Baumann contained banned levels of stanozolol, the steroid used by sprinter Ben Johnson, who was banned for two years after testing positive at the Seoul Olympics.
