Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "Presidential Bulletin" feature: Reports by Diplomatic Correspondents Dmitri Voskoboynikov, Igor Porshnev, Vitaliy Trubetskoy, and others] [Text] President Islam Karimov has ordered to create a new committee for running state property and support for private business. His decree published on Tuesday provides for a merger of the existing State Property Committee and the Finance Ministry's State Property Fund. The new body is designed to carry out uniform policies to create a mixed economy and set out plans and priorities in the country's denationalization campaign. The committee is also authorized to protect the individual's property rights and to use the proceeds from the denationalization program for the country's economic restructuring. The committee's other tasks include defining ways of luring foreign participation into the privatization campaign and creation of investment funds, consultancy and auditing services, realty firms and other market structures. Under the presidential decree, the new committee's resolutions will be compulsory for ministries, companies, factories and offices and local government bodies.
