Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] An unidentified person telephoned the Qol Yisra'el newsroom this morning and said he was speaking on behalf of an organization called Shlomtziyon-Yehuda [The Well-Being of Zion-Judaea]. He warned that his organization will retaliate to every terror attack by Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] or the Islamic Jihad with murders, in Israel or abroad, which will be similar to the Cave of the Patriarchs massacre. The man who will carry out the act will never be caught. He will commit suicide to sanctify God's name.
