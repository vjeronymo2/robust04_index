BFN [Text] As reported by the press service of the Defense Ministry, the Ukrainian Defense Ministry has come to know that the ferrying of over 20 Tu-22 missile-carrier combat aircraft to the Russian Federation is being made ready at two Black Sea Fleet aviation regiments. It is planned that the flight will be made against the background of regular tactical flight exercises of the Black Sea Fleet Air Force from the air bases of Oktyabrskoye and Veseloye in Crimea to the Russian military airfield in Krasnodarsk kray's Krymsk. The press service of the Ukrainian Defense Ministry emphasizes that any attempts to transfer materiel and other Black Sea Fleet material wealth outside our state, are against the law and will inevitably lead to exacerbating the situation surrounding issues of dividing the Black Sea Fleet.
