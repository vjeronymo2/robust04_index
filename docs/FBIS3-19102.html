Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- As a result of an incident [the arrest of an agent of the Federal Intelligence Service, BND, in Moscow on suspicion of espionage], BND chief Konrad Porzner ended his visit to Moscow prematurely. State Minister Bernd Schmidbauer has told BILD [12 March issue] that "the subsequent steps" will be considered "quietly" after the return of the BND head.
