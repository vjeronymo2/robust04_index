Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 28 (KNS-KCNA) -- A meeting for inaugurating the society for following secretary Kim Chong-il was recently held in Saitama, Japan. Nobuko Watanabe was elected its chairman at the meeting. The chairman in her report said that all the members of the society would devote themselves to the cause of independence of the popular masses by deeply studying and grasping the ideas and theories and the practical activities of secretary Kim Chong-il. "The harder we study the chuche idea, the deeper we feel that it contains a profound content," she said. A letter to Comrade Kim Chong-il was adopted at the meeting.
