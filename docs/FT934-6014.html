931126 FT 26 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Warnford Invs dips to Pounds 3.47m Warnford Investments, the property investment group, saw profits before tax dip to Pounds 3.47m in the six months to June 24. The outcome, which compared with profits of Pounds 3.67m last time, came from gross rent and service charges of Pounds 5.79m (Pounds 6m). After tax and minorities, earnings per share emerged at 6.01p (6.35p). The interim dividend is maintained at 2.75p.
