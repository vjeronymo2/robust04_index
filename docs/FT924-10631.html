921030 FT 30 OCT 92 / World Trade News: Ecuador woos investors ECUADOR plans to scrap foreign investment curbs and improve rules on property rights and licensing, to attract new foreign capital. Mr Mauricio Pinto, industries minister, said the government wanted to give foreign investors the same opportunities as national investors. Ecuador's economy needed the new technologies they would bring. Foreign ownership in nearly all areas of the economy would be allowed, and state-owned industries be privatised. Foreign investors will no longer need industry ministry authorisations.
