BFN [Text] Knesset Member Yosi Sarid, who was closely involved in the Cairo negotiations, denies that 'Arafat has a secret Israeli letter regarding the future of Jerusalem. Sarid said that the PLO leader does not hold any document or letter whose contents have not been presented to the government, the Knesset, or the public at large. 'Arafat had been told only what had been agreed upon in the Declaration of Principles and nothing more, Sarid said.
