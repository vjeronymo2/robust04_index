BFN [By Central People's Broadcasting Station reproter Liu Zhenying (0491 2182 5391) and XINHUA reporter Zhu Youdi (2612 1635 2769)] [Text] Beijing, 8 Jun (XINHUA) -- The party Central Committee and the State Council will convene a national work conference on education in the middle of June. Li Peng, premier of the State Council, today [8 June] took part in a discussion meeting with responsible persons of various democratic parties and the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce and personages without party affiliation. Li Peng solicited the participants' opinions on the report to the national work conference on education (draft for soliciting opinions). Li Peng said: "Consulting with others before making policy decisions and supervising the work when it is being implemented" is an important part of the multiparty cooperation and the system of political consultation under the leadership of the CPC, and is also a fine tradition of the CPC. Before the implementation of each major policy, we always held full democratic consultations with representatives of democratic parties and nonparty personages. The forthcoming national work conference on education will be the second such conference held by the party Central Committee and the State Council since the beginning of reform and opening up. It is also an important conference in the history of developing China's education. The current conference will certain have a great impact on making all-round arrangements for and implementing the "program for reforming and development China's education," studying and solving major problems in the reform and development of education in our country, realizing the strategic goals of reform and development of education in the 90's, and promoting China's socialist modernization drive. Lei Jieqiong, chairman of the Central Committee of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; nonparty personage Cheng Siyuan; Li Ganliu, vice chairman of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Feng Zhijun, vice chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League; Feng Tiyun, vice chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Jiang Zhenghua, vice chairman of the Central Committee of the Chinese Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party; Luo Haocai, vice chairman of the Central Committee of the China Zhi Gong Dang; Xu Caidong, vice chairman of the Central Committee of the Jiusan Society; Cai Zimin, chairman of the Central Committee of the Taiwan Democratic Self-Government League; and Zhang Xuwu, vice chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, spoke successively at the meeting. In their speeches, they unanimously held that the decision of the party Central Committee and the State Council to convene the forthcoming national work conference on education is very important and timely. It shows the party Central Committee and the State Council have shown concern for and attached great importance to the cause of promoting education. They were convinced that through this conference, China's education can be greatly developed. They put forward many positive opinions and suggestions on how to popularize the nine-year compulsory education, how to vigorously develop adult and vocational education, how to increase investment in education, how to deepen education reform, and how to realize the goals of developing education by the end of this century. Premier Li Peng expressed his gratitude to the participants for their positive suggestions and said that he would further revise the report to the national work conference on education according to the participants' opinions. Also attending the discussion meeting were Li Peiyao, chairman of the Central Committee of the Revolutionary Committee of the Chinese Kuomintang; Ding Shisun, vice chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic League; Feng Kexu, vice chairman of the Central Committee of the China Democratic National Construction Association; Ge Zhicheng, vice chairman of the Central Committee of the China Association for Promoting Democracy; Lu Jiaxi, chairman of the Central Committee of the China Peasants' and Workers' Democratic Party; Wang Songda, vice chairman of the Central Committee of the China Zhi Gong Dang; Jin Kaicheng, vice chairman of the Central Committee of the Jiusan Society; and Huan Yushan, vice chairman of the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce. Vice Premier of the State Council Li Lanqing; State Councillor Li Tieying; Wang Zhaoguo, vice chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and head of the United Front Work Department of the CPC Central Committee; Zhu Kaixuan [minister of the State Education Commission]; and responsible persons of concerned departments attended the meeting.
