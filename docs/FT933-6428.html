930827 FT 27 AUG 93 / UK Company News: English Trust stake in Greyfriars Inv English Trust Company, the investment management and corporate finance group, has acquired 1m shares in Greyfriars Investment at 27 3/4 p per share. This represents 14.29 per cent of the Greyfriars capital. English Trust will extend the offer to all Greyfriars holders at the same price. Jupiter Tyndall Merlin has, on behalf of its discretionary clients who hold 28.33 per cent of Greyfriars, undertaken to accept the offer only to the extent that enables English Trust to acquire over 50 per cent of the equity and to declare the offer unconditional.
