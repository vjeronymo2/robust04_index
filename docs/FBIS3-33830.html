Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 30 Jan (INA)--Iraq has addressed a letter to Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid on the Saudi and Kuwaiti rulers' continued provision of facilities to U.S., British, and French aircraft to violate Iraq's airspace and jeopardize its security, sovereignty, and national unity. In a letter sent to the Arab League chief yesterday, the Iraqi permanent representative to the Arab League said that these aircraft continued on 24-26 January to violate Iraqi airspace, flying from bases in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait. During that period, these aircraft carried out 168 hostile air sorties over Iraqi territory. A list attached to the letter detailed these violations.
