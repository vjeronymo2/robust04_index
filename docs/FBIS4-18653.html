BFN [Unattributed report: "Fereydun Bakhtiari Authorized To Enter Switzerland"] [Text] Bern, 7 Apr (ATS) -- Fereydun Ilkhan Bakhtiar [name as received] has been granted approval to enter Switzerland. He thus will be able to register as an asylum seeker, Federal Refugee Office (ODR) spokesman Heinz Schoeni said Thursday [7 April]. A cousin of the former shah's prime minister, Shapur Bakhtiar, arrived at Geneva Airport without a passport on 22 March. Claiming to be in danger if he was sent back to Tehran, he spent the intervening period in Cointrin's [Geneva airport] transit area, under threat of expulsion back to Iran.
