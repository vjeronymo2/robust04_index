BFN [Excerpt] The first press festival ended this afternoon with a speech by President Hashemi-Rafsanjani and the presentation of prizes to the outstanding press in the country. In his speech, the president considered the progress of the country's press to be towards perfection and described the press festival as a necessity for this stage in the era of the reconstruction of the country. Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani stressed the need for press freedom in the country and added: Press freedom in Iran is at a very high level. Even those in the press who do not have a friendly approach enjoy the same freedom. The president stressed the need for the press to come to the aid of the government when necessary and added: When the British Government embarked on a hue and cry against us in the current month [Iranian month beginning 21 April] as a preemptive action after a bugging device was discovered at the Iranian Embassy, the press did not show any perceptiveness [hassasiyyat neshan-nadadand]. Professionals realize the press should come to the aid of the government at such times. [passage omitted]
