940309 FT 09 MAR 94 / Observer: In a state Michael Cottrell may have been sipping a bit too much of his own product. The chairman of Taunton Cider announced a deal selling his up-market scrumpy into the US market, via Molson USA. A crowing Cottrell reported that, as Molson had an established network of distributors across all 51 states, the fit was perfect. 'As far as I know there are still only 50,' said a puzzled US embassy in London.
