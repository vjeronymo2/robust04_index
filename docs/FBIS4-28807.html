BFN [Text] Tokyo, June 14 KYODO -- Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata indicated Tuesday [14 Jun] he will appeal for continued aid to Russia, eastern Europe and South Africa when he meets leaders of the Group of Seven (G-7) industrial nations at the Naples summit in July. Hata told the House of Councillors Budget Committee the developing countries would have serious difficulty in solving their various problems if the G-7 discontinued its aid. Britain, Canada, France, Germany, Italy, Japan and the United States are scheduled to take part in the summit.
