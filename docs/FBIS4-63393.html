BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow May 20 TASS -- The recent murder of State Duma deputy and member of the new Regional Policy faction Andrey Aizderdzis provoked indignation of Russian political circles and President Boris Yeltsin, says a statement of Presidential Press Secretary Vyacheslav Kostikov. A number of State Duma factions even suggested to remove Interior Minister Viktor Yerin from his post. The president has studied the investigation materials, motives and circumstances of the murder. In his opinion, the Interior Ministry acted swiftly and professionally. The ministry's measures against crime and corruption have improved as well. Vyacheslav Kostikov was authorized to state that the president see no reasons for disciplinary measures as regards Viktor Yerin and has full confidence in the Interior Ministry. "He is confident of competence, high professional and personal qualities of Viktor Yerin. The question of Yerin's removal from his post will not be raised," says the statement.
