BFN [Text] Nairobi, 26 May (KNA) -- A new political party, the National Development Party (NDP), has been registered, KNA has established. According to its certificate of registration, the NDP is led by an economist Mr. Stephen Omondi Oludhe as chairman, Mr. David Obilo, a teacher as secretary, and a businessman Lucas Okuma as treasurer. The registration of the party, under Licence No. 16177 of 6 May 1994, brings to 10 the number of parties registered in Kenya since 1992.
