BFN [Text] Lieutenant General 'Umar Hasan Ahmad al-Bashir, the president of the Republic, has stressed the Sudanese Government and people's preference for a constitutionally legitimate government in Yemen, which calls for the preservation of Yemeni unity. His excellency, while receiving the credentials of the Yemeni ambassador to Khartoum today at the Republican palace, said a strong and unified Yemen represents strength and support for the Arab homeland and the Islamic nation, which urgently requires efforts toward unity and cohesion. He emphasized that the sons of one Yemen in their wisdom and faith are capable of seeking the arbitration of the voice of reason, and of taking the interests of Yemen into consideration. He pointed out Sudan's desire to support and consolidate bilateral relations in all spheres, and to push it to a level where all efforts and capacities would be integrated in achieving the aspirations of the two fraternal peoples.
