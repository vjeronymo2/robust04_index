930112 FT 12 JAN 93 / World News in Brief: Invesco MIM expects proceedings Fund management company Invesco MIM is under investigation by regulators and expects disciplinary proceedings against itself. In 1991 it was fined Pounds 75,000 for poor administration of personal equity plans and is the subject of a writ by trustees of the Mirror Group Newspapers Pension Scheme, a portion of whose assets it managed.
