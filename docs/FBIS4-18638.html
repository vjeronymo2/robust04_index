BFN [Excerpt] Hamburg (DPA) -- Last year and this year dozens of Kurds have been deported from Germany to Turkey without this becoming known to the general public. According to a DPA inquiry on Wednesday [6 April], in January and February 1994 31 Kurds were forcibly sent back to their homeland from Lower Saxony; 24 were deported on the basis of the asylum law and seven on the basis of the aliens' law. The other laender said regarding this problem that deportations are listed not according to ethnic groups but according to citizenship. [passage omitted]
