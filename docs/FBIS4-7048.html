BFN [Excerpt] A ceremony to grant official commendations and gifts from Comrade Kim Il-song, the great leader of our party and people, and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il to the participants of the national meeting of functionaries of the coal industry was held at the People's Palace of Culture on 22 April. Present at the meeting were Comrade Yi Chong-ok [member of the Workers' Party of Korea (WPK) Political Bureau and vice president], Comrade Choe Yong-nim, Comrade Hong Song-nam [candidate members of the WPK Political Bureau and vice premiers of the Administration Council], Comrade Hong Sok-hyong [candidate member of the WPK Political Bureau and chairman of the State Planning Commission], functionaries concerned, and exemplary functionaries and miners of the coal industry from all over the country. After a decree of the DPRK Central People's Committee was read, the title of labor hero of the DPRK was awarded to nine participants, including Hungnyong coal mine manager Ho Yun-chan.
