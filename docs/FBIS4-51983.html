BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 4 (KCNA) -- Mangyongdae prize sports contest which opened on April 4 closed. The traditional sports contest is held every year on the occasion of the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song. Sportsmen of many sports groups participated in more than 50 events of the sports contest. Four sportsmen set new world records in 6 events. In weightlifting, the last world records were broken by Kim Yong-nam with a lift of 203 kg in jerk for the men's 76 g category, Yi Song-hui with a total lift of 155 kilograms (68.5 kg in snatch 87.5 kg in jerk) in the women's 46 kg category, Han Kyong-sil with a lift of 70 kg in snatch for the women's 46kg category. Kim Il-sin set a new world record in the men's 3000 metres of short-track speed skating, and six sportsmen improved Asian records in four events of weightlifing and speed skating. 58 new republican records were set by 48 sportsmen in 49 events.
