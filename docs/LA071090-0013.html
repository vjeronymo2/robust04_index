July 10, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MEMORIAL TO HONOR FORGOTTEN VETERANS OF KOREAN WAR

So The Times thinks the Korean War was right and just.

Perhaps in the light of what has happened to the peoples of Vietnam and Cambodia, The Times will have second thoughts about its anti-Vietnam War stance.

You were wrong about that war and the Contras' efforts to upset the Nicaraguan Sandinistas. Ho hum! Into the round file.

JOHN De PALMA

Lakewood
