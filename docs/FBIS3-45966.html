Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 24 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing met with Toimi Kankaanniemi, minister for development cooperation of Finland, and his party here this morning. The two sides exchanged views on issues of bilateral trade and economic relations. Wu Yi, Chinese minister of foreign trade and economic cooperation, was present at the meeting. Kankaanniemi and his party arrived here January 21 as Wu's guests. In addition to Beijing, they have also visited Baoding, a major city in Hebei Province.
