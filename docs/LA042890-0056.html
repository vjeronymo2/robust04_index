April 28, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

WEEKEND AT AFI FESTIVAL;

SUNDAY

Following are The Times' recommendations for today's and Sunday's schedule of the American Film Institute Los Angeles International Film Festival, with commentary by the film reviewing staff.

Information: (213) 480-3232 or (213) 520-2000.

Recommended:

"THE END OF OLD TIMES"(Czechoslovakia, 1989; Director: Jiri Menzel; Fairfax, 6:30 p.m.). Menzel's distinctively humanist slapstick is only partially on view in this moderately engaging comedy about a man posing as an aristocrat among the hunting-party set in post-World War I Czechoslovakia. (Peter Rainer)
