Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Udon Tantisunthon, member of the House Foreign Affairs Committee, said that a delegation of the Chinese National People's Congress Foreign Affairs Committee was originally scheduled to visit Thailand between 23 February and 2 March. The House Foreign Affairs Committee has made preparations for the visit. However, the Chinese Embassy has just now informed the committee that the delegation will indefinitely postpone the visit and thanked the Thai side for all the preparation.
