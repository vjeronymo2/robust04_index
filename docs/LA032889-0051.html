March 28, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPOTLIGHT: SAN DIEGO BRIEFLY

Two San Diego dancers are part of the American Ballet Theatre's eight-performance run at the San Diego Civic Theatre through Saturday. Dana Stackpole, a six-year veteran, and Laura Hood, who joined up with ABT less than two years ago, can be seen as members of the corps in "Swan Lake" and in several of the repertory works on tap for the troupe's San Diego stint.
