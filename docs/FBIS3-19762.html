Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- The last German Blue Helmet soldier left Somalia on Friday [18 March] at 1400 local time -- 1200 Central European Time. This was stated by the Defense Ministry in Bonn. The last 180 soldiers of the German units were flown to the Cologne and Karlsruhe frigates by helicopter. The naval unit has left for Djibouti, where it is expected to arrive on Tuesday. From there, the soldiers will fly back home on Wednesday, and will be welcome by Defense Minister Volker Ruehe, of the Christian Democratic Union, at the military section of Cologne/Bonn Airport.
