BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad has held two telephone conversations with Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and his deputy 'Ali Salim al-Bid. Talks during the two conversations dealt with the situation in Yemen and the ongoing battles on Yemeni territory. President Hafiz al-Asad stressed Syria's pressing desire that all parties should work to halt the ongoing battles and resort to dialogue stemming from concern about the safety of Yemeni citizens in a way that will preserve Yemen's national unity.
