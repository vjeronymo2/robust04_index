May 14, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: STEPHENS' CONDITION UPGRADED TO SERIOUS

Hall of Fame trainer Woody Stephens was taken off a respirator Sunday, and his condition was upgraded from critical to serious just two days after he underwent quadruple heart-bypass surgery, officials in Louisville, Ky., said.

"He's still in the intensive care unit, but the doctor did say he was improving," said Pam Greer-Ullrich, a spokeswoman at Norton Hospital.

Stephens, 76, has been hospitalized for more than two weeks because of a recurrence of chronic breathing problems. He underwent the heart-bypass surgery on Friday.
