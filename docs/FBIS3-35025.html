Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The formation of a Jordanian popular committee for the resistance of submission to and normalization with the Zionist enemy was announced yesterday. The decision to form the committee was announced at the end of a meeting held at the headquarters of the Jordanian Arab Socialist Ba'th Party on 27 January 1994. Seventy-five political, party, and independent key figures attended the meeting at the invitation of the nationalist Jordanian political parties. After several meetings, the temporary committee agreed on the final draft of regulations and bylaws. These will be presented to the general committee for approval and implementation. In a statement issued yesterday, the temporary committee demanded the formation of a broad Jordanian popular movement, including all those who stand against the agreements of submission and normalization with the Zionist enemy, to work as one team to secure the success of the confrontation plan which expresses the Arab Jordanian people's rejection of capitulation.
