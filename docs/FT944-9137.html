941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / World News in Brief: Baby smugglers' sentence suspended A Romanian appeal court suspended the jail sentence imposed on Adrian and Bernadette Mooney for trying to smuggle a baby out of Romania. The Berkshire couple were ordered to leave the country but may not be allowed home yet. Mr Mooney is pictured in Bucharest with Grace, another Romanian child he and his wife adopted legally in 1991. The baby they had hoped to adopt is in an orphanage.
