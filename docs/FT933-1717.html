930922 FT 22 SEP 93 / World Commodities Prices: Jute and Cotton JUTE C and F Dundee: BTC Dollars 330, BWC Dollars 340, BTD Dollars 310, BWD Dollars 315. C and F Antwerp: BTC Dollars 310, BWC Dollars 310, BTD Dollars 285, BWD Dollars 285. COTTON Liverpool- spot and shipment sales amounted to 426 tonnes for the week ended 17 September, against 48 tonnes in the previous week. Improved demand brought moderate purchases, mainly in American descriptions. Central Asian and Indian growths made some headway.
