Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Statement read by the Assembly Secretary General Avdo Campara and adopted at the session of the Assembly of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina in Sarajevo on 30 March -- live] [Text] On the basis of Article 207, Paragraph 2 of the Constitution of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina, its text amended, the Assembly of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina adopts the following statement at the joint Assembly session held in Sarajevo on 30 March 1994: Proceeding from the (?outline) of [word indistinct] ratification of the Washington agreement, adopted at the session of the Assembly of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina held on 27 March 1994, the Assembly of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina demands of the state delegation of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina -- in the continuation of the peace negotiations on Bosnia-Herzegovina -- to also proceed from the Constitutional Law on the Amendments to the Constitution of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina, which is a basis for establishing a new internal setup of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina on the federal bases on the entire territory of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina within its internationally recognized borders. This statement will be published in the Official Gazetteer of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina, the same as the statement that was read by Mr. Mariofil Ljubic. Thank you.
