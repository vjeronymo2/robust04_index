BFN [Text] Russia is likely to use the Gabalinskaya radar station free in exchange for concessions in other areas to Azerbaijan, where the station is situated. A reliable source in the administration of the Azeri president told Interfax about this preliminary understanding reached during Pavel Grachev's visit to Baku on June 11. The source said that if the talks on the Gabalinskaya station are a success and interests of both sides are taken into account, the station will be operated only by Russian military personnel. The source said that after an understanding is reached in principle, experts of Russia and Azerbaijan will draft the text of a document.
