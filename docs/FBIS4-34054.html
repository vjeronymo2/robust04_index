BFN [Excerpts] Brasilia, 10 Jun (AE) -- Just one day after receiving a report from the military ministers, in which they warn of instability among the troops over low salaries, President Itamar Franco ordered Federal Administration Secretary Romildo Canhim to begin working next week with the Finance Ministry on a timetable to implement the equal salary plan among the three government branches. This decision, which directly benefits the military and civil servants of the executive branch, was made during a nearly two-hour meeting with the four military ministers. "It is impossible to maintain the current salary scale," President Itamar admitted during the meeting that was requested by the ministers who expressed total support for the equal salary treatment law. [passage omitted] Federal Administration Secretary Canhim said he has no idea how long it will take to fully implement the equal salary plan. "Everything depends on the revenue collection capability, which we will now discuss with the Finance Ministry," he said. [passage omitted]
