940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Nightfreight forecast Liverpool-based delivery business, Nightfreight, is coming to the market later this month, and yesterday forecast in its pathfinder prospectus that pre-tax profits for the year ending November will be not less than Pounds 4.5m. Last year the group made an operating profit of Pounds 2.28m on turnover of Pounds 21.4m. Impact day is on May 17. The placing is expected to value the group at about Pounds 50m and raise Pounds 17m, most of which will be used to clear debts of about Pounds 10m. Nightfreight specialises in industrial and just-in-time markets. It is the distributor for The Independent newspaper. NM Rothschild is sponsoring the issue, and Panmure Gordon is the broker.
