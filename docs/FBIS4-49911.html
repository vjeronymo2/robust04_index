BFN [Text] Changsha, May 10 (XINHUA) -- A symposium on scientific and technological cooperation between China and Latin America was held recently in Changsha, capital of central China's Hunan Province. People from Chinese research institutes and provincial and municipal committees of science and technology attended the symposium. China has diplomatic relations with 17 countries in Latin America and has trade relations with 33 Latin American countries and 13 regions. The total trade volume between China and Latin American countries amounted to nearly three billion U.S. dollars last year. Since 1988, the two sides have been undertaking nearly 100 cooperation programs every year. The scientific and technological cooperation not only deepens the mutual understanding of the scientists from the two sides but also benefits their economic construction.
