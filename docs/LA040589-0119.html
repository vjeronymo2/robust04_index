April 5, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

WEDDINGS CAN INJURE PEOPLE, OFFICIALS DECIDE

It's just not safe anymore to get married, and Oklahoma County commissioners are doing something about it.

The commissioners voted Monday to ban a traditional wedding practice -- the throwing of such things as rice and confetti -- in the vicinity of the county courthouse.

Commissioner F. G. Buchanan said the policy was adopted as a safety measure.
