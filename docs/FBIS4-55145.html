BFN [Report by Andrey Sharkov] [Excerpt] Sofia, May 5 (BTA) -- [passage omitted] Speaking before journalists during a reception at the Austrian Embassy here yesterday, Austrian Defense Minister Dr. Fasslabend expressed Austria's high appreciation for Bulgaria's unquestionable contribution to the stability in the Balkans. Mr. Fasslabend stressed the significance the observation of the Yugo-embargo has for preventing an expansion of the conflict. He conferred on Defence Minister Aleksandrov a high Austrian award given for services rendered to Austria. Mr. Aleksandrov, the first foreign defence minister to receive this award, was decorated for his contribution in promoting the stability in the Balkans, Mr. Fasslabend said. The only person entitled to a higher award is the Austrian president, Austrian Embassy sources told BTA. Bulgarian Chief of General Staff Army General Lyuben Petrov and Major General Lyubomir Getchev, too, received awards for their contribution to the regional stability and bilateral cooperation.
