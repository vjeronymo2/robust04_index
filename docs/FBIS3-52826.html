Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 9 Jan (MENA)--Detectives from Asyut's Security Department have arrested fugitive terrorist Salih Muhammad Mahmud al-Tur, wanted in connection with three assaults on other citizens. He is wanted in connection with Misdemeanor Case 3493/1993 for assaulting citizen Mursi Muhammad Mursi, Misdemeanor Case 357/1988 for assaulting citizen Jamal Mikha'il, and Asyut Civil Case number 4 of 4 December 1989 for assaulting citizen 'Isam Sabir. The suspect has been referred to the concerned prosecutor's office so that the proper legal measures can be taken against him.
