BFN [Report by Nicolas Sanabria Garron] [Text] Journalist Judith Munoz was freed yesterday after a judge approved a petition of habeas corpus in her favor. Ivan Miranda, leader of the Bolivian Press Workers federation, said: [Begin Miranda recording] There is no incriminating evidence or accusation against this journalist who was unfairly detained, tortured, and threatened by repressive government elements and by agents who persecuted her. [end recording]
