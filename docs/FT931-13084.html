930126 FT 26 JAN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Landed demand was less strong, reports the Tea Broker's Association. Selected Coloury Assams ruled firm to dearer but others were 2 to 3p easier with dust grades irregular throughout. Bright liquoring Africans remained about steady while mediums opened firm to but closed 2 to 3p lower, sometimes more. Offshore fair demand at easier rates the highest price realised this week was 230p for a Rwanda pf. 1. Quotations: quality 210p/kg, nom good medium 180p/kg, medium 160p/kg, low medium 125p/kg.
