BFN [Text] Beijing, June 20 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier and Foreign Minister Qian Qichen will pay an official visit to Russia, Belarus, Lithuania and Estonia late this month and early July, Foreign Ministry Spokesman Wu Jianmin announced here today. At the invitation of the Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev of the Russian federation, Foreign Minister Pyotr Krawchanka of the Republic of Belarus, Foreign Minister Povilas Gylys of the Republic of Lithuania and Foreign Minister Juri Luik of the Republic of Estonia, Qian will pay an official visit to the above-mentioned four countries from June 27 to July 2, the spokesman said.
