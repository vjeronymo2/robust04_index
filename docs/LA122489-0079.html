December 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COSBY'S COMMENTS

Many kudos to Cosby for saying: "Even if we have to put in our schools more courses in religion and human behavior we have to find ways to convey self-strength. We gotta get that to our children."

He is so right. Unless we get back to the old-fashioned family values, our country will continue to become a wasteland of crime, corruption and unbridled morals.

FREDERICK D. MULLEN

Upland
