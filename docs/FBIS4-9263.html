BFN [Text] Szczecin, April 22 -- President Lech Walesa paying a several-hour visit to Szczecin said that this visit is not the start of his election campaign but a "wise campaign of balance." Asked if he intends to run for presidency if the Non-Party Reform Bloc (BBWR) wins the June 19 local elections, the president replied: "There are still the parliamentary elections," and explained that he would have to have "executive capabilities" to implement his programme. Following a meeting with supporters and members of the BBWR, the president met with the Szczecin-Kamien metropolitan, Archbishop Marian Przykucki. Next the president left for a local military unit for a meeting with officers and representatives of police, border guards and fire fighters.
