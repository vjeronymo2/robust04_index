BFN [Report by correspondent Valeriy Gromak: "Baltiysk Will Become an International Port"] [Text] Baltiysk -- Kaliningrad Oblast Administration chief Yuriy Matochkin has signed a Decree "On the Implementation of Preparatory Work for the Design and Construction of a Port Complex in Baltiysk." It instructs the Baltic Fleet command, the Baltiysk administration, and the Kaliningrad port maritime administration to set up a commission to define the boundaries of the area for the construction of the port complex and to transfer the defined area, together with hydraulic engineering installations, to the use of the maritime administration. The decree also instructs that proposals and a package of documents on opening the commercial port of Baltiysk to foreign ships be prepared and submitted to the Ministry of Transport.
