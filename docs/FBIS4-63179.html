BFN [A.P. report under the "In Brief" rubric: "Joint Collegium of the Federal Counterintelligence Service and the Russian Security Council"] [Text] A joint sitting of the collegiums of the Federal Counterintelligence Service [FCS] and the Russian External Intelligence Service [REIS] is to take place in Moscow today. It will discuss questions concerning cooperation between the two services. Such measures are envisioned under the Agreement of Cooperation between the FCS and the REIS signed this year.
