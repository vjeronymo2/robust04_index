BFN [Text] Paris, June 15, RIA -- [AGENCE] FRANCE PRESSE reported today, citing Western military sources and an anonymous representative of the Bosnian Army, two Russian mercenary pilots, employed by the Serbs, joined the Moslem Bosnian Army. They landed their Mi-17 helicopters at the headquarters of the 3rd Corps of the Bosnian Army in Zenic, bringing along presents designed to show their good will, said a Bosnian officer. The two Russian pilots do not intend to take part in hostilities in Bosnia. It is the first case when Russians fighting on the side of the Serbs turned over to Moslems, the agency writes. The Russian Defence Ministry refused to comment.
