DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION Office of the Secretary Application of Aerial Transit Company For A Redetermination of Its Fitness AGENCY: Department of Transportation ACTION: Notice of Order to Show Cause (Order 94&hyph;7&hyph;40 )

SUMMARY: The Department of Transportation is directing all interested persons to show cause why it should not issue an order finding that Aerial Transit Company continues to be, fit, willing, and able to engage in interstate and foreign all-cargo scheduled air transportation. DATES: Persons wishing to file objections should do so no later than August 8, 1994.
ADDRESSES: Objections and answers to objections should be addressed to the Air Carrier Fitness Division (X&hyph;56, Room 6401), U.S. Department of Transportation, 400 Seventh Street, S.W., Washington, D.C. 20590 and should be served upon the parties listed in Attachment A to the order.
