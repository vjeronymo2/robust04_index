BFN [Excerpts] The plan to strengthen public order in the country, including stricter observance of traffic regulations, will be enforced from 22 May using undercover inspectors. Brigadier-General Syfollahi, commander of the Law Enforcement Force, said this at a news conference today. [passage omitted] Brig-Gen Seyfollahi also referred to the efforts of the Law Enforcement Force to fight smugglers in the past two months. He said: In that period a ship carrying contraband, bound for Iran from Dubai; a 10-[metric]tonne truck carrying technical equipment for smuggling [as heard]; 2,208,681 other contraband goods worth 12 billion rials; 2,533 boats; seven launches; and 78 vehicles were seized and 2,061 smugglers were arrested in Bushehr, Hormozgan, and Chabahar areas in the second phase of the Fajr-3 exercises. Asked about the discovery of narcotics, Brig-Gen Seyfollahi said that since the beginning of the year over 15 tonnes of assorted narcotics have been discovered. In that connection more than 1,400 smugglers and 2,000 addicts have been arrested.
