941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / International Company News: Brazil telecoms group on offer Brazil's Minas Gerais state telecom group Telemig will launch a 1.85bn share subscription, the company said in a statement, reports Reuter. Telemig will offer 1.85bn preferred shares at RDollars 42.56 (Dollars 50) per lot of 1,000 stocks, it said. The shares were left over from a Telemig 1.88bn preferred share subscription announced in October.
