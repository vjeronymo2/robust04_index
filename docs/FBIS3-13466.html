Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Official spokesman's statement dated 11.3.94"] [Text] The resumed fourth summit of the G-15 will take place in New Delhi from March 28-30, 1994. There is agreement that for this summit, as it is a resumed summit, the rules of the quorum (2/3rds) shall not apply. It is expected that a number of these states will participate at the level of the heads of state/government, judging from the responses received. We should, therefore, be assured of a good attendance at the summit level. The preparatory meetings of the personal representatives and of the ministers shall be held on March 25 and 26 respectively. This resumed summit is expected to include discussions on issues relating to the future course of the G-15 in a fast-changing world.
