Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Oleg Velichko] [Text] Moscow January 6 TASS -- Russian First Deputy Prime Minister Yegor Gaydar is against the idea to construct a new building for the Russian parliament and said the issue of appropriating 500 million US dollars for the purpose was not discussed by the government. "If the discussion takes place I shall personally oppose it", he told TASS on Thursday. Gaydar, who is the leader of the Russia's Choice parliamentary group, recalled that his bloc had already proposed to the Russian president to keep the existing parliament building, known as the "White House", for the new legislature. "The position of the bloc on the given issue did not change", Gaydar said.
