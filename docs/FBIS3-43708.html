Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Transitional Executive Council's [TEC] subcouncil on intelligence held its first meeting in Pretoria. At yesterday's meeting the joint coordinating intelligence committee was discussed and constituted. In a statement the TEC says the subcouncil also established three other standing committees -- one dealing with basic principles on intelligence and formulation of a code of conduct, the second to facilitate the transition to a future intelligence dispensation, and a third for suitable legislation. The Joint Coordinating Intelligence Committee consisting of heads of the different intelligence services in South Africa will meet again on Monday.
