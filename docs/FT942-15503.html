940414 FT 14 APR 94 / International Company News: First Chicago hampered First Chicago, the US bank, reported improved first-quarter earnings, but said its results were hurt by its emerging markets trading unit, Reuter reports from New York. 'Extreme volatility in global financial markets in the quarter led to a (Dollars 25m) loss in this unit that more than offset profits in other trading activities,' said Mr Richard Thomas, chairman. A year ago, trading activities resulted in a gain of Dollars 54.5m. First Chicago reported first-quarter fully-diluted earnings of Dollars 2 a share, up from Dollars 1.91.
