940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / Early retirement expectations fall The proportion of people expecting to retire at or before the age of 62 has dropped sharply, a survey by market research group Mintel says. The research, conducted before the announcement of the government's intention to raise the retirement age for women, found that 54 per cent expected to retire before 62 - the figure in 1990, when the research was last carried out, was about two-thirds.
