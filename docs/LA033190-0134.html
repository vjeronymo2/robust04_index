March 31, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: SANTA ANA;

BOY CRITICAL AFTER BEING HIT BY CAR

A 4-year-old Santa Ana boy was in critical condition Friday after being struck by a car when he ran into the street, police said.

Manolo Guerrero was struck by a black Ford Mustang in the 1100 block of South Standard Street about 5 p.m. Thursday, Santa Ana Police Sgt. Larry Wagstaff said.

Witnesses told police that the driver "had no chance of avoiding the accident," Wagstaff said.

Guerrero was taken to Western Medical Center-Santa Ana, where he is listed in critical condition. The driver, Jose Cruz R. Cota, 36, of Santa Ana, was not injured. The accident is still under investigation, Wagstaff said. MATT LAIT
