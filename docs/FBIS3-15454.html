Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Transportation Ministry's press service reported that following the bombing of the railway bridge over the River Khram in Georgia, a group of bandits set up a roadblock on the Sadakhlo highway and thus cut off the shortest motor road from Georgia to Armenia. As part of urgent measures adopted by the Armenian Government, the Transportation Minister Henrik Kochinyan left for Tbilisi today to ensure the use of detours for the transportation of vital goods.
