BFN [Text] This morning at the capital's airport Vnukovo-II, Viktor Chernomyrdin and Israel's Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin signed a trade and economic agreement. It lays the legal basis for large-scale Russian-Israeli economic cooperation. Interfax reports that the the idea for signing such a document had arisen in the course of intergovernmental talks in Moscow on 25 April. The agreement was prepared in the course of 24 hours. After the signing of the trade and economic agreement, the head of the Israeli Government set off for St. Petersburg.
