BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 14 (KCNA) -- Large quantities of fertilizers are now being produced by factories under the Ministry of Chemical Industry in Korea. According to data available, fertilizer output jumped 20 percent in the first ten days of this month over the corresponding period last month, taking the ministry as a whole. The Namhung Youth Chemical Complex increased fertilizer output 68 percent in this period above the same period last month by putting all the equipment in full-capacity operation. The Hungnam Fertilizer Complex is now turning out a large quantity of effective fertilizers suitable to the conditions of soil and the biological characteristics of crops.
