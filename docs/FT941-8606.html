940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / International Company News: Cash flow advances 16% at McCaw McCaw Cellular Communications, the largest cellular telephone service company in the US, reported a 16 per cent increase in fourth-quarter cash flow from its cellular operations. However the group's net loss rose to Dollars 207.5m from Dollars 62.7m after charges for upgrading equipment and early extinguishment of debt. McCaw, which is being acquired by American Telephone & Telegraph, said the number of cellular subscribers in markets where it owns a controlling interest, together with subscribers to its LIN subsidiary, rose 37 per cent to 3.04m, compared with 2.22m at the end of 1992. Proportionate cash flow from cellular operations rose to Dollars 185.4m from Dollars 159.3m in the same quarter of 1992.
