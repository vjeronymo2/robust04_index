931209 FT 09 DEC 93 / International Company News: Premier Group profits up 11% Premier Group, one of South Africa's leading food companies, overcame recessionary trading conditions to record an 11 per cent increase in attributable earnings, to R109.3m (Dollars 32.5m) in the six months to October, against R98.1m a year earlier, writes Philip Gawith in Johannesburg. Earnings per share rose to 132 cents from 119 cents. The dividend was increased to 40 cents per share from 36 cents.
