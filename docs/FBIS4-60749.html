BFN [Text] Three commanders of the infamous Golboddin-Dostam militia have recently joined the Islamic Army and Mojahedin along with their men. According to reports reaching the BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY, three militia commanders, Commander Sayed Taher, Commander Qader Shah, and Commander Hafif who were in the ranks of the infamous Golboddin-Dostam militia in a battalion located around the electricity project in Tashqhorghan, have joined the unit of Commander Hamidullah [word indistinct] along with their weapons and men. They expressed regret over their past activities. The men from the Army post expressed their indignation against the militia and pledged to continue the jihad to the end until the complete elimination of the militia. Our correspondent adds that since the failed coup d'etat of Karmal-Golboddin-Dostam on 1 January until now, dozens of militia commanders and men, having expressed regret over their activities, have ended their hostilities with the Islamic State, and continue their jihad and struggle against the militia alongside the ranks of the mojahedin.
