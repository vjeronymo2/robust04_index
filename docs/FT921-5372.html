920304 FT 04 MAR 92 / No more listings in South Bank NO MORE buildings in London's South Bank Centre, the arts complex, are to be listed as of special architectural or historic interest, the government said yesterday. A certificate of immunity from listing has been issued for the Hayward Gallery, the Queen Elizabeth Hall, the Purcell Room, the Museum of the Moving Image, the National Film Theatre and the walkways connecting them. The Royal Festival Hall and the nearby Waterloo Bridge are listed.
