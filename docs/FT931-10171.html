930210 FT 10 FEB 93 / Halifax cuts savings rates HALIFAX, the UK's largest building society, has announced gross interest rate cuts of between 0.6 percentage points and 0.8 points on its savings accounts with effect from today. The gross rates on its 90-day savings and its instant access accounts are coming down in most cases by 0.65 points and, in some cases, by 0.6 points. That follows a reduction of 0.56 of a point in its mortgage rate. Chelsea Building Society, which cut its mortgage rate by 0.51 points to the market rate of 7.99 per cent on Monday, has cut its gross savings rates by up to 0.75 points. Leeds Permanent is cutting savings rates by between 0.5 points and 0.75 points today, following its cut of 0.56 points in its mortgage rate.
