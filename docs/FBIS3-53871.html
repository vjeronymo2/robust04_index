Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 20 Jan (MENA)--Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat today met with U.S. Commerce Secretary Ronald Brown. Palestinian sources said that the two sides discussed arrangements for revitalizing the economic and social conditions in Gaza and Jericho after power is transferred to the Palestinian people. The meeting was attended by members of the Palestinian and U.S. delegations currently visiting Cairo.
