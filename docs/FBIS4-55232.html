BFN [Text] An official ceremony took place this morning at Cotroceni Palace during which President Ion Iliescu welcomed the president of the Republic of Albania, Sali Berisha, who is making a visit to our country. The talks between the two presidents were followed by an official dialogue between the two delegations, which include members of the two governments and high-level state officials of the two countries. President Sali Berisha attended a wreath-laying ceremony at the Monument to the Unknown Soldier before holding a meeting with representatives of the Cultural Union of Albanians in Romania at the Albanian Embassy in Bucharest. Talks between the Albanian president and the president of the Chamber of Deputies and visits to the Parliament Palace and the National History Museum in Bucharest are scheduled for this afternoon. This evening Iliescu will host an official dinner in honor of the Albanian president. A treaty of understanding, cooperation, and good-neighborliness between Romania and Albania will be signed tomorrow at Cotroceni Palace, our correspondents have reported.
