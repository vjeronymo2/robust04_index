Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Germany will soon send thousands of refugees from Serbia, Montenegro, and Kosovo to their home countries, the German weekly DER SPIEGEL affirms in today's issue. [passage omitted] In connection with the aforementioned information, our editorial office contacted the office of the Romanian foreign minister and has learned that nothing is known about the negotiations that the leadership of the German border guards allegedly had with Romanian authorities. Likewise, contacting the office of Romania's interior minister, we were told that no such talks have taken place between the leadership of the German border guards and Romanian authorities on this issue.
