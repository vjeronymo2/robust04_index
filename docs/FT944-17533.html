941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Mezzanine Capital Mezzanine Capital & Income Trust 2001 said yesterday that it had sold its investment in Polaris Pool Systems to senior management and two US investment firms for a total of Dollars 25.7m(Pounds 16.2m). MCIT has received Dollars 20.4m for its equity interest, which its annual report valued at Dollars 9.4m, and Dollars 5.3m in repayment of subordinate debt. The deal increases the net asset value of the trust's capital shares by about 35p and the income shares by about 4p. At March 31 nav per capital share was 295.3p and 123.3p for the income shares. MCIT also announced yesterday that it had invested about Dollars 14.5m in securities of NRE Holdings, operator of 80 Burger King restaurants in four states.
