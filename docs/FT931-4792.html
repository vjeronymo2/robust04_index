930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / World News in Brief: Recession's silver lining Intrum Justitia, Europe's biggest debt collection agency, improved annual taxable profits by 26 per cent to Pounds 16m. (Dollars 22.7m). 'We get more debtors in recession, but they are less likely to pay back,' said Gunilla Demnert, finance director of the Dutch-based, London-listed company.
