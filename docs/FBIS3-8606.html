Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Article by "red": "The Weekly RESPEKT Will Have a New Publisher"] [Text] Prague--In the very near future, the R-Presse company will become the new publisher of the prestigious weekly RESPEKT. The agreement on founding this company was signed yesterday in the morning by the representatives of the present publisher of RESPEKT (RPK Co.) and the Czech-French MaFra company, which publishes the daily MLADA FRONTA DNES. RESPEKT emerged at the beginning 1990 as the successor to INFORMACNI SERVIS, which began publishing in November 1989. In 1992, it was awarded the prize of American journalists--Magazine of the Year. Previous winners have included THE ECONOMIST, LE MONDE, and the FINANCIAL TIMES. MaFra will hold a 51 percent stake in R-Presse, while the current publisher of RESPEKT will have 49 percent.
