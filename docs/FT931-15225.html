930114 FT 14 JAN 93 / Up to 600 MoD jobs to disappear UP TO 600 jobs are likely to be lost under Ministry of Defence plans to establish a single army personnel centre in Glasgow by 1995. Jobs will be lost in Chester, Exeter, Leicester, York, Ashton-under-Lyme and London, where offices will be closed. Up to 800 extra posts will be created in Glasgow.
