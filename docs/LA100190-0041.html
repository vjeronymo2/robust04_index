October 1, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SMALL SCREEN: Huntington Beach businessman Mel Mermelstein, above, who successfully sued a Torrance-based group that claimed the Holocaust did not occur, is coming to the TV screen. A docudrama, "Never Forget," starring Star Trek's Leonard Nimoy, is being shot locally and is due on the TNT cable network in January. . . . Mermelstein, an Auschwitz survivor who is often consulted on the set, says it was "kind of strange" watching himself portrayed. Even stranger: He's playing his own father. Mark I. Pinsky and Sonni Efron
