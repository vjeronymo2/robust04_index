920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Countryside sells site to Tesco Countryside Properties has sold a superstore site at its White Court, Braintree, project to Tesco Stores. Mr Alan Cherry, a chairman of Countryside, said it was good to have completed a major sale this early on, after only recently agreeing to acquire the land for the project at a fixed price over a six-year period, during which time 'we obviously expect the housing and property markets to improve.'
