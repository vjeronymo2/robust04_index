940420 FT 20 APR 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Shares gained on news that the Inkatha Freedom party would take part in next week's elections. Although industrials saw some profit-taking in the afternoon session the index closed 166 ahead at 5,924. The overall index added 110 to 5,080 while gold weakened 10 to 1,946 as the price of bullion fell. Vaal Reefs lost R6 to R397.
