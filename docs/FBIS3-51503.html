Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Bulletin No. 22 issued by the Mexican National Defense Secretariat on the situation in Chiapas; place and date not given; from the "Vector 21" newscast] [Text] The National Defense Secretariat reports it has knowledge that in the next 24 hours rebel groups plan to stage armed attacks against the communities of Yajalon, Sabanillas, and Venustiano Carranza. Meanwhile, reports have been received from residents of the region that on 17 January approximately 20 armed individuals, who had their faces covered, stole numerous head of cattle from a ranch located between the communities of Copainala and Coapilla. Up until now, soldiers continue guarding the communities mentioned in previous communiques and continue to carry out social work in these communities.
