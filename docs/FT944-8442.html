941119 FT 19 NOV 94 / Weekend Money (Investing for Growth): Directors' dealings Largest sale of the week was at Industrial Control Services, a control systems company, Based in Essex, the group supplies equipment to the oil, gas and petrochemical industries. Its recent final results reflected good development and a strong order book. Yet, the shares have been underperforming over the past year. Two directors have sold stock. Peter Hall, the chairman and chief executive, disposed of 550,000 shares and Brian Dodds sold 250,000, all at 120p. Both remain substantial shareholders, on the board with Hall's holding accounting for more than 9.2m and Dodds 1.2m. Britton Group makes extruded polythene. Interim results, announced at the end of September, reflected good growth, with earnings rising from 2.78p to 4.79p. Profits are forecast to grow by a factor of 4.3 in the next year.
