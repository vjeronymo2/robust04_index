Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow February 26 TASS - "Ames worked in the United States for Russia. He protected Russian interests since he revealed spies stealing Russian secrets to the U.S. He inflicted no direct damage to the Americans and was of benefit for Russia," head of the Russian Armed Forces General Staff Colonel-General Mikhail Kolesnikov told a news conference here today, when asked about CIA officer Aldrich Hazen Ames charged with espionage in the Russian favor. In his turn, commander of the Russian air-borne troops General Yevgeniy Podkolzin said there is no connection between the U.S. arrest of CIA officer Aldrich Hazen Ames and success of Russian diplomacy in settlement of Bosnian crisis.
