BFN [Text] The chairman of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Constituent Assembly and the chairmen of the caucuses of the parliamentary parties in the Constituent Assembly held a meeting yesterday in Sarajevo and discussed the activities and the tasks of the Constituent Assembly in the implementation of the Constitution of the Federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina. The decision was made to include representatives of the parliamentary parties at the talks concerning the implementation of the Federation Constitution that will be held in Vienna. It was also decided that the Bosniak delegation will consist of Haris Silajdzic, Ejup Ganic, Nijaz Durakovic, and Edhem Bicakcic, while the delegation of the Croatian people will consist of Ivo Komsic, Mariofil Ljubic, and Vlado Pandzic.
