Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Leonid Pozdeyev report under the "In Brief" rubric: "Tatarstan May Leave Volga Region and Join the Urals"] [Text] Tatarstan President Mentimer Shaymiyev said at the inauguration of Bashkortostan President Murtaza Rakhimov that Tatarstan may join the Ural Association in the future. The reason is the ineffectiveness of the activity of the analagous structure of the Volga region to which Tatarstan currently belongs.
