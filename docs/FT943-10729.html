940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / UK Company News: High Gosforth bid goes unconditional Northern Racing's Pounds 3.4m agreed offer for High Gosforth Park, which owns and runs Newcastle racecourse, has been declared unconditional as to acceptances. Northern is 65 per cent owned by Stanley Clarke Leisure, a private vehicle of the millionaire businessman; the balance is held by St Modwen Properties, the development and investment group in which Mr Clarke is chairman. It has received acceptances in respect of 83,925 shares, representing 92.27 per cent of the issued capital. The offer remains open.
