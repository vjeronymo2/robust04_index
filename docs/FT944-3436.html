941213 FT 13 DEC 94 / UK Company News: BTR's Pounds 17m sale BTR, the industrial conglomerate, has sold its Viennatone subsidiary to ReSound Corp of the US for Pounds 17m. Viennatone, which makes hearing aids, formed part of the Thomas Tilling Group, acquired by BTR in 1983. Turnover last year was Pounds 19m. Mr Alan Jackson, chief executive of BTR, said the company was continuing its strategy of concentrating on industrial manufacturing.
