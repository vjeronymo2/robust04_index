Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Voz Andes" newscast] [Text] Francisco Acosta, energy and mines minister, announced that Ecuador must reduce oil production because the country will no longer be able to use the Colombian trans-Andean pipeline. Acosta reported that Colombian Petroleum Enterprise will not be able to lease the pipeline to Ecuador to transport its crude oil because it is designed to carry 25 degrees API crude, not 29 degrees API, which is the specific weight Ecuador will soon begin to produce.
