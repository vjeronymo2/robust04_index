BFN [Text] A second battalion of Palestinian police forces, comprised of 300 policemen, left Cairo today for the Gaza Strip to join their colleagues who are taking over self-rule areas in the Gaza Strip and Jericho. 'Abd-al-Hayy 'Abd-al-Wahhab, commander of the Palestinian police force in Cairo, said the main force will leave the 'Ayn Jalut camp in Cairo on 17 May for the Gaza Strip. The force has between 600 and 700 policemen.
