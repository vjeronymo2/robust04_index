BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 13 -- Vietnam and the Federation of Russia on June 9 began their joint 1994 scientific investigation and exploration of Vietnamese water territory. The 18th investigation, launched within the framework of the agreement reached in 1980 between Vietnam and the former Soviet Union, now the Russian Federation continues, aims to gather hydrographic, hydrationic and environmental parametres of the Vietnamese water territory. Although previous investigations were all carried out in autumn and winter, this year, for the first time it is conducted in summer and will last 55 days. As planned, a directory on the meteorological and hydrological conditions of Vietnamese water territory will be published in 1995 to the service of the development of various domains.
