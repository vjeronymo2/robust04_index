Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Havana, 12 Mar (PL) -- Major General Ulises Rosales del Toro said the Cuban area bordering the U.S. Naval Base in the eastern province of Guantanamo has adequate security and is well-guarded by soldiers and the people of the area. The high-ranking officer, chief of the general staff of the Revolutionary Armed Forces (FAR), highlighted the combat readiness and good political and moral state of the troops, following a two-day visit of military units in the region bordering the illegal U.S. military enclave. Today, GRANMA published a report from the area citing statements by Rosales del Toro, who also praised the organization of the population into a system coordinated with the troops to defend the territory in question as well the country.
