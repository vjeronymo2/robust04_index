BFN [Text] The Argentine Government has accepted a Paraguayan proposal to postpone until 16 July the start of filling the Yacyreta Dam. This was announced on 8 June at the end of a meeting at Government Palace between President Juan Carlos Wasmosy, Foreign Minister Luis Maria Ramirez Boettner, and Argentine Ambassador Raul Carignano. During this time all problems on the Paraguayan side of the river must be solved. Carignano said President Wasmosy assured him that the Paraguayan Government will guarantee the fulfillment of the treaty schedule and that the postponement will not affect the date established for the first generator to begin operations -- 1 September.
