BFN [Text] A meeting with Spanish King Juan Carlos was the first official activity that President Frei carried out in Cartagena, Colombia. Commenting on this meeting, Frei said that he discussed the current situation in Chile and his administrations' projects. In addition, Frei reported that King Juan Carlos had expressed interest in paying another visit to Chile, and that some dates were already under discussion. Asked about Chilean support for Cuba's return to the OAS, Frei said that this all depends on Cuba, which must restore democracy and uphold human rights. Cuba ceased being an OAS member in 1962. Regarding his scheduled meeting with Bolivian President Gonzalo Sanchez de Lozada, Frei said that Chile is interested in helping Bolivia obtain an outlet to the world.
