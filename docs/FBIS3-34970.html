Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In their (?focal) efforts to destabilize the tranquillity in the Sudan, the British House of Commons is holding a meeting today with the so-called Sudanese opposition groups approved in the rebel factions. The meeting was called by Christian Solidarity, at their national agency in London, under the auspices of Baroness Cox. The theme of the meeting is to unite all Sudanese opposition abroad, with the aim of overthrowing the legitimate government of the transitional revolution in the Sudan. The organizers of the meeting have rejected the participation of neutral Sudanese and Islamic institutions in the meetings, which are being kept secret. In a separate development, reliable sources disclosed that a meeting of rebel factions earlier had taken place in London, under the patronage of the anti-Islam christian association.
