930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / International Company News: Cott climbs 159% COTT, a soft drinks maker and distributor, reported net profit of CDollars 10.7m (USDollars 8.7m) or 19 cents a share, in the July quarter, up 159 per cent from a year earlier. Sales rose 122 per cent to CDollars 198m, with most of the gain in the US. First half profit was CDollars 16.4m or 30 cents a share, up 165 per cent, on sales of CDollars 327m, up 134 per cent.
