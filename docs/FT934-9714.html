931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / London Stock Exchange: BAT in demand Recent news from the US that tobacco prices could be poised to recover ensured that BAT Industries, the tobacco to insurance conglomerate, bucked the market trend. The stock rose 17 to 478p. BAT announced last week that a cut-throat cigarette price war in the US had depressed its third-quarter profits. The group said the move to reduce prices, started by rival Philip Morris, cost it nearly Pounds 100m in the quarter to the end of September.
