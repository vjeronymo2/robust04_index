BFN [Report by TIA "KHOVAR" correspondent Abdugaffor Kurbonov for TASS] [Text] Dushanbe 16 April, TASS -- The head of the Tajik Parliament, Emomali Rakhmonov has estimated the results of the Moscow summit of the CIS countries as evidence of the strengthening of mutual understanding of the countries of the Commonwealth that has entered the "strongest period of its development." In an exclusive interview with TIA "KHOVAR" on Saturday he noted the Commonwealth of Independent States is moving along the road of the realization of the idea of establishing a real economic union and drawing up a mechanism of its functioning. During the discussion of economic and political issues the meeting showed considerable mutual understanding between heads of CIS countries, said Rakhmonov.
