920817 FT 17 AUG 92 / Construction Contracts: Research centre Two contracts totalling nearly Pounds 9m - both for organisations moving to Swindon - have been won by TARMAC CONSTRUCTION. A Pounds 4.5m research and technology centre is to be built for National Power on the Windmill Hill Business Park currently being developed by St Martin's Properties. Tarmac's second contract, valued at Pounds 4.3m, is to build an archive store for The Royal Commission on the Historical Monuments of England which is moving its headquarters from London to Churchward.
