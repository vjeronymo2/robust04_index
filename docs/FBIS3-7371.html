Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Australian Government has again appealed for calm in the continuing row over its recognition of the Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia [FYROM]. In a statement read to crowds of people celebrating Greek independence day, Prime Minister Paul Keating expressed regrets that Australia's good record of harmonious community relations were under threat. He urged ethnic and religious leaders to appeal for calm and to resolve differences lawfully. There have been a number of arson attacks on shops and churches and by members of both communities.
