920916 FT 16 SEP 92 / Wages 'still outpace inflation' RATES of pay increases are still rising faster than inflation in spite of sharp falls over the last year, according to the Hay Compensation Report compiled by Hay Management Consultants. The report supports last month's evidence from Britain's two main independent pay research groups, Incomes Data Services and Industrial Relations Services, suggesting that the pay and inflation link remains central.
