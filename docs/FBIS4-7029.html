BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 26 (KCNA) -- The whole nation will never pardon the crimes of the South Korean authorities, who blocked the Seoul trip of a delegation of the North side, ruthlessly trampling down even the human morality, the beautiful manners and customs handed down from the ancestors and warm compatriotic feelings, and history will surely pass a stern judgement on them. The North side headquarters of the National Alliance for the Country's Reunification says this in a statement released today in connection with the fact that a delegation of the North side could not participate in the memorial service on the one hundredth day after the death of Rev. Mun Ik-hwan due to the obstructive moves of the South Korean authorities. The statement says: This time, the South Korean authorities proved again that they are separatists who block even a small-scale contact of fellow countrymen, let alone national reconciliation and unity and the reunification of the country. The Korean nation cannot live in peace with the anti-national group left alone, the group which lowers the check-bar before fellow countrymen going to the South for a memorial service, while rendering the situation extremely strained in league with outside forces.
