May 26, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ELSMERE DUMP 'MISGUIDED'

Are the people of Los Angeles willing to get ripped off, to the tune of $125 million paid to the owner of BKK landfills at taxpayers' expense, and at the same time very likely pollute our air and water with methane gas and toxic chemicals? Isn't water much too precious an item to be tampered with, particularly during this terrible drought?

We must speak out against the misguided construction of a landfill at Elsmere Canyon and the expansion of existing landfills.

The only safe and economically sound solution to our waste problem is to recycle, now!

BEN BRONWEIN

Santa Clarita
