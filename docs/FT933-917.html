930927 FT 27 SEP 93 / Syria calls for the tightening of 40-year-old Arab economic boycott against Israel Syria called yesterday for the tightening of the 40-year-old Arab economic boycott against Israel, accusing it of asking for everything in the Middle East peace process in return for nothing, Reuter reports from Damascus. The government newspaper Tishreen and state-run Damascus radio said the boycott could not be abolished before restoration of Arab rights and the recovery of occupied Arab lands.
