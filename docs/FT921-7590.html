920221 FT 21 FEB 92 / World News in Brief: Irish rape issue to go to Supreme Court appeal The parents of a pregnant 14-year-old Irish rape victim are to appeal to the Supreme Court in Dublin against a ban on her seeking an abortion in Britain. The appeal will probably be heard next week and the Irish state is to pay the family's costs. Yesterday's move was welcomed by Irish prime minister Albert Reynolds, who had already launched talks on the case's implications for Ireland's constitution. MEPs to debate abortion row, Page 2
