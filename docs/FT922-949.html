920625 FT 25 JUN 92 / UK Company News: JLI Group advances to Pounds 3.2m JLI Group, the dried fruit, nuts and pickle ingredients processor, raised pre-tax profits from Pounds 2.32m to Pounds 3.17m in the year to March 31, just beating the forecast made with its most recent rights issue. The company made two rights issues during the period, raising Pounds 20.7m net and increasing the share capital by 125 per cent, and made or lined up five acquisitions. group has decided to concentrate on its food processing businesses where it can add value and improve margins. Group turnover in the 12 months fell to Pounds 84.3m (Pounds 90.1m), though this was after the merchanting division sales fell from Pounds 24.1m to Pounds 8.2m before being discontinued. Mr Yoav Gottesman, chief executive, said some of the acquisitions had been loss-making when they joined the group, but had been turned round, Reduced interest payable of Pounds 940,000 (Pounds 1.7m) boosted pre-tax profits. A lower rate of tax partly offset the effect of the increase in share capital, which held earnings per share growth to 16 per cent from a restated 8p to 9.3p. As forecast with the rights, a proposed final dividend of 3p (2.87p) lifts the total to 4.5p (4.3p).
