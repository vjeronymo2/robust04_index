931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Goode Durrant sells Laidlaw Leasing Goode Durrant has sold its vehicle leasing interests, continuing the disposal of non-core assets that began with the sale of its Laidlaw motor distribution subsidiary to Quicks in June. Laidlaw Leasing, which remained with the group following the disposal, has been sold to Highway Vehicle Leasing for an initial Pounds 1.5m cash, including goodwill of Pounds 480,000. There will be further payments over the next three years, depending on the residual values realised for vehicles currently under lease. Laidlaw Leasing's net asset value at April 30 was Pounds 436,802 and operating profit for the year was Pounds 81,578.
