June 20, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Blinder to Change Name: Blinder International Enterprises Inc., the Englewood, Colo.-based parent company of the penny stock brokerage facing a series of fraud investigations, said it is planning a name change. Blinder said it will ask shareholders at the company's annual meeting next month to approve renaming the firm Intercontinental Enterprises Inc. Blinder International is an umbrella company for the penny stock empire of Meyer Blinder and includes the brokerage firm of Blinder, Robinson & Co, which is the target of several state and federal civil investigations.
