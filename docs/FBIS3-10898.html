Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "Argentina in Brief" column] [Text] (DYN-NA) -- Justicialist Senator Jose Bordon said that the days of President Carlos Menem in government are numbered and confirmed he will bid for the Justicialist nomination to be presidential candidate in 1995. Bordon told Salta daily EL TRIBUNO that the economic situation was "unstable both productively and socially." According to Bordon 1995 will mark the end of the Menem administration.
