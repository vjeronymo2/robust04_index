Office of Federal Contract Compliance Programs Reinstatement of Layton Construction Co., Inc. AGENCY: Office of Federal Contract Compliance Programs, Labor. ACTION: Notice of reinstatement, Layton Construction Company, Inc.

SUMMARY: This notice advises that Layton Construction Company, Inc., has been reinstated as an eligible bidder on Federal contracts and subcontracts and federally-assisted construction contracts. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Leonard J. Biermann, Deputy Director, Office of Federal Contract Compliance Programs, U.S. Department of Labor, 200 Constitution Avenue, NW., room C&hyph;3325, Washington, DC 20210 (202&hyph;219&hyph;9475). SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: Layton Construction Company, Inc., Salt Lake City, Utah, is, as of this date, reinstated as an eligible bidder on Federal contracts and subcontracts.
