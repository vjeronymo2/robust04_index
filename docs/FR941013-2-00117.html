[Declaration of Economic Injury Disaster Loan Area # 8365] Massachusetts; Declaration of Disaster Loan Area Essex County and the contiguous counties of Middlesex and Suffolk in the State of Massachusetts and Hillsborough and Rockingham Counties in the State of New Hampshire constitute an economic injury disaster area as a result of damages caused by a fire which occurred on August 7, 1994. Eligible small businesses without credit available elsewhere and small agricultural cooperatives without credit available elsewhere may file applications for economic injury assistance until the close of business on July 5, 1995 at the address listed below: U.S. Small Business Administration, Disaster Area 1 Office, 360 Rainbow Blvd. South, 3rd Floor, Niagara Falls, NY 14303 or other locally announced locations. The interest rate for eligible small businesses and small agricultural cooperatives is 4 percent. The economic injury number assigned to this disaster for the State of New Hampshire is 836600. (Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance Program No. 59002.) Dated: October 5, 1994. Cassandra M. Pulley, Acting Administrator.
