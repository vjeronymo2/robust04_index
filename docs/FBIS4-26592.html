BFN [Text] Cairo, June 22 ( XINHUA)--A Chinese Airforce delegation led by Air Force commander General Cao Shuangming left here today for the United Arab Emirates [UAE] at the end of a week-long goodwill visit to Egypt. During its stay here the delegation called on Marshal Muhammad Husayn Tantawi, Egyptian commander-in-chief of the armed forces and defense minister, General Ahmed Abdul Rahman Nimir [name as received], commander of Egypt's Air Force, and General Ahmed Abu Taleb [name as received], commander of Egypt's air defense forces. The two sides held talks on strengthening friendship ties of cooperation between the two countries armed forces, particularly the air and air defense forces. They also exchanged views on issues of mutual concern. General Cao and his accompanying delegation arrived here on June 15 at an invitation from General Ahmed Abu Taleb, commander of the Egyptian air defense forces.
