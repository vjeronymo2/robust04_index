940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / Observer: Glad to be Vlad He's a card, that Vladimir Zhirinovsky. 'I am often asked if I have any hobbies,' he told a Moscow hack the other day who was questioning the ultra-nationalist about his territorial ambitions. 'Well, I don't fish or collect stamps. But I have a special affiliation to border posts.' Apparently that kind of thing goes down a storm in Ivantheterribleograd.
