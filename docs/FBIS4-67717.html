CSO [Unattributed untitled article datelined Moscow under the "In Brief" rubric] [Text] The capital's Ambulance Service has decided to strike. February 14 has been designated the day on which the strike will begin. The council of the service's labor collective made the respective announcement. It was noted in the announcement that on 25 January 1994 Anatoliy Solovyev, head of Moscow's Main Medical Administration, signed an order regarding reorganization of the Ambulance Service station. The announcement stated that this was done without the consent of the service's personnel and in violation of the law "Regarding the Procedure for Resolving Collective Labor Disagreements (Conflicts)." In accordance with Article 7 of the said law, a conference of the labor collective held on 14 February 1994 will decide the matter of the form and date of the beginning of the strike of the No. 03 Service in Moscow.
