Title: Permanent Program for Inspection and Enforcement_30 CFR 840 OMB Number: 1029&hyph;0051 Abstract: This provision requires the regulatory authority to conduct periodic inspections of coal mining activities, and prepare and maintain inspection reports for public review. This information is necessary to meet the requirements of the Surface Mining Control and Reclamation Act of 1977 and its public participation provisions.
