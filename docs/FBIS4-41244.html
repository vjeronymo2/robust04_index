BFN [Text] Chisinau, BASAPRESS, 18/6/1994 -- A meeting of the departments of foreign relations experts from the Ukraine and Moldova is to be held at the end of July. The meeting will discuss issues concerning the frontiers between the two states, declared to BASA a representative of the Moldovan Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MAE). Initially, the Ukrainian MAE suggested the meeting to be organized at the end of June, but the Chisinau officials requested it to be postponed for a month, for they wanted better to prepare for it. The cited source stated that a visit to Kiev by the vice-premier Valentin Cunev was scheduled for the first half of June. Cunev was to discuss issues concerning interstate collaboration in the fields of transportation, economics, and exploitation of sea vacation houses. The visit was postponed because the new Ukrainian chamber of ministers was not yet formed.
