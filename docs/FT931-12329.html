930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Abtrust Preferred plans share issue Abtrust Preferred Income Investment Trust is planning to issue new zero dividend preference shares with a market value of Pounds 8.5m together with new ordinary income shares to a value of Pounds 8.3m. The company also declared a third interim dividend in respect of the year ended May 31 1993 of 2.90625p per ordinary income share.
