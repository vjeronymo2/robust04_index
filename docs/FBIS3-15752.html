Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, March 8 (SNARK) -- "Main part of the humanitarian relief coming from international organizations and single states for the Azerbaijani refugees is being used by the Azerbaijani authorities to cover the military expenditures and to acquire new kinds of weapons", Kamo Abrahamyan, Head of the Nagorno-Karabakh State Department of National Security, told journalists. According to Mr. Abrahamyan, State Department of National Security is aware of the facts of purchasing new kinds of military hardware in Ukraine spending humanitarian 40 million dollars sent by Turkey Government. 500,000 USD [U.S. dollars] UK sent to the UN fund for the Azerbaijani refugees and 1 million USD supplied by the Irani Central Bank and multiple investments of some oil companies of the world were spent to buy the shells for "Grad" launchers. All the facts show Azerbaijani course to involve all these humanitarian relief suppliers into the war.
