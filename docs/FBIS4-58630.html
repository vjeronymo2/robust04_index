BFN [Text] The two political bureaus [not further identified] today held a joint meeting at Salah-al-Din resort under the supervision of brother Mas'ud Barzani [leader of the Democratic Party of Kurdistan--DPK]. The following points were approved during the meeting: 1. To form a joint committee between the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan [PUK] and the DPK in al-Sulaymaniyah, Karkuk, and Qal'at Dizah, and Raniyah to end fighting and its effects there. 2. To send a joint committee with similar form and authority to Dahuk, Zakho, al-'Amadiyah, 'Aqrah, and all parts of (Shahbinan) to carry out the duties mentioned in the first paragraph. 3. To exchange all detainees. 4. Both sides should return each others offices. 5. To return each other's properties, machines, and equipment as soon as possible and without delay. 6. To account for troop concentration and prevent the mobilization of any new troops against each other. 7. To remove all manifestations of fighting through the media. Both sides are responsible for disseminating information in a fraternal, peaceful, and harmonious spirit. 8. To leave no room for any statements or words without approval from central authorities.
