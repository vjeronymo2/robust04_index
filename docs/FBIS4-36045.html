BFN [Text] At Carthage Palace this morning President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali and Foreign Minister Habib Ben Yahia received a delegation from the U.S. Senate which included Paul Simon, Harry Reid, and Russell Feingold. Following the meeting, which was also attended by U.S. Ambassador John McCarthy, Paul Simon said that during the meeting he and the accompanying delegation expressed their extreme appreciation to his excellency the head of state and their great admiration for Tunisia. He pointed out that the talks dealt in particular with issues which were of concern to the region and the peace process in the Middle East. In this respect, he said we are very keen to express our thanks to President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali and the Tunisian people for the efforts exerted by Tunisia and its president at the United Nations for the benefit of the peace process in the Middle East.
