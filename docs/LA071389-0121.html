July 13, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: SANTA MONICA;

FARMERS' MARKET FESTIVITIES

The Santa Monica Farmers' Market will celebrate its eighth anniversary Wednesday from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m. at 2nd Street and Arizona Avenue in downtown Santa Monica.

The market, which operates each Wednesday at that intersection from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m. during the summer and fall, is one of the largest in the Southland.

The anniversary celebration will feature music by Freeway Philharmonic, a continuous raffle and a cake-cutting ceremony with city officials at noon.
