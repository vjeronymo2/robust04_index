940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / International Company News: Renong posts 12% rise to MDollars 107m Renong, the Malaysian conglomerate which is closely connected to the governing United Malays National Organisation political party, has announced pre-tax profits of MDollars 107m (USDollars 39m) for the six months months to December 31 1993, writes Kieran Cooke in Kuala Lumpur. The six-month profit was a 12 per cent rise on a year ago, though overall group turnover fell 37 per cent to MDollars 136m. Renong, once a diversified group with interests in many areas of the Malaysian economy, has recently been focusing on infrastructure developments. United Engineers Malaysia, the Malaysian construction and engineering company in which Renong has a controlling stake, has announced pre-tax profits for the year ending December 31 1993 of MDollars 237m, an increase of 18 per cent over the 1992 figure.
