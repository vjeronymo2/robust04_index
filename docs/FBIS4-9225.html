BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 21/4/1994 -- "Such actions are welcome for they occasion, through interviews and talks, an extensive exchange of opinions on current issues confronting our countries, especially regarding the process of transition to market economy," is the opinion of Mechyslaw Hryb, president of the Supreme Soviet of the Belarus Republic. "I view this meeting as a beneficial one, proving Romania's wise policy towards openness. It will undoubtedly yield best results for expanding Romania's cooperation ties with other countries, especially economic ones. Romania has overcome the stage of expectations. What we have done here by organizing the Crans Montana Forum comes to prove Romania's real potential. We are seeking for an image much closer to the real one that would open new ways for our country. We are convinced that the Crans Montana Forum would reflect Romania's real potential which could attract foreign investors to our country," stated Romanian Foreign Minister Teodor Melescanu.
