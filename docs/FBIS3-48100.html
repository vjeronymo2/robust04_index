Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 19 (KCNA) -- The General Secretary of the Socialist Party of Peru, Luis Mateo Munos, received the Korean ambassador to Peru on January 10 and said his party expressed unquestionable support and solidarity for the socialist cause of Korea. He said the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song in his New Year address gave a most wise and clear exposition of the nation's economic construction, national reunification and foreign policies. "Though the imperialists are now clamouring about the `end' of socialism, socialism is alive and it is developing in Korea, demonstrating its might more eloquently with each passing day," he said. He stressed that the world must defend Korea, the bastion of socialism, and learn from it. "The Socialist Party of Peru expresses deep respect and reverence for the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, who are leading the world politics with the most wise and just scientific idea and theories and practical activities," he declared.
