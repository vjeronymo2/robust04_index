Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A task force to combat drug abuse has been launched in Monrovia. According to a release from the New African Rescue Center in Monrovia, the rate of drug abuse is on the increase in Liberia due to the civil war. The New African Rescue Center has (adopted) plans to extend its program to combat drug abuse in the rural part of Liberia. Stressing the importance of this campaign, the organization has called upon all peace-loving Liberians, the Interim Government of National Unity, nongovernmental organizations, and private voluntary organizations operating in the country to beef up its efforts by fighting against drug abuse in our society.
