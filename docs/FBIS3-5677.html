Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Malaysian labor-intensive companies have been urged to consider relocating their operations to the Malaysian-initiated export processing zone, or EPZ, to be set up in Danang, Vietnam. The sales launch of the EPZ in Danang with EPZ MASDA joint venture cooperation as developer, will be held on the 22d of this month. EPZ MASDA chairman, Datuk Michael Chen told a news conference in Kuala Lumpur that labor-intensive companies, especially those in the garment and shoe manufacturing businesses, should reconsider relocating to the EPZ. EPZ MASDA is a joint venture between Malaysian South-South Corporation Limited and Quang Nam-Da Nang Export Processing Zone Development Corporation. The EPZ is the brainchild of both the prime ministers of Vietnam and Malaysia -- the result of Prime Minister Datuk Sri Dr Mahathir's visit to Hanoi in 1992.
