CSO ["Election Results in Kirovohrad Oblast"] [Text] On 10 April, in Kirovohrad's Industrialnyy e.d. No. 224, V. Alyoshyn, the Rukh candidate, defeated a member of the CPU; in Tsentralnyy e.d. No. 225, V. Mishura, a CPU member, defeated the Rukh candidate; in Znamyanka e.d. No. 226, A. Salanskyy, a CPU member, defeated another Communist; in Svitlovodsk e.d. No. 228, Volodymyr Yavorivskyy defeated a CPU member; in Bobrynets e.d. No. 229, Vasyl Durdynets defeated a Communist; in Mala Vyska e.d. No. 231, S. Sas, a CPU member, defeated another CPU member; in Novoukrayinka e.d. No. 233, Tselykh, a Communist, defeated the candidate of the Ukraine Bloc; in Oleksandriya e.d. No. 234, Yevhen Marmazov, a leader of the CPU, defeated the chairman of the rayon council. No deputies were elected in three other electoral districts.
