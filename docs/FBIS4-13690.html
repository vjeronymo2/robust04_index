BFN [Text] Israeli gunboats opened fire at the al-'Izziyah area, near al-Qulaylah, after midnight last night. The enemy forces' artillery also shelled the area extending from al-Mansuri to Majdal Zun and the coastal stretch south of Tyre from their positions on al-Bayyad hill and al-Hardun at 0200 today. The shelling was accompanied by Israeli helicopter overflights.
