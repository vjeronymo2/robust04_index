Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 19 (KCNA) -- The general secretary of the Egyptian National Democratic Party, Youssef Amin Wali, when he received the Korean ambassador on March 10, said respected His Excellency President Kim Il-song is a world-famous statesman, an outstanding leader and a true leader of the people. He said the dear leader His Excellency Kim Chong-il is a great man who is wisely leading the party, the state and the Army of Korea and noted the agreement reached at a recent contact between the DPRK and the U.S. is a fruition of his distinguished strategy. The chairman of the Nepal Workers and Peasants Party, Narayan Man Bijukchhe, when he received the Korean ambassador on March 8, said the letter sent by the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song to the National Agricultural Conference indicates the most correct way of the ultimate solution of the rural question under socialism. All the lines and policies put forward by Comrade Kim Il-song, he noted, serve as the genuine guideline of party activities. The general secretary of the Central Committee of the Nepal Communist Party (United Marxism-Leninism), Madhav Kumar Nepal, received the Korean ambassador on March 9 and told him that Korea continues giving full play to the superiority of socialism because its people have respected Comrade Kim Il-song and dear Comrade Kim Chong-il as their great leaders.
