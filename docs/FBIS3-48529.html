Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN [Text] On the morning of 25 January, Prince Krompreah Norodom Ranariddh received at his residence the Austrian delegation led by His Excellency [H.E.] Wolfgang Schallenberg, Austrian deputy foreign minister. The first prime minister talked about the efforts of the Royal Government of Cambodia [RGC] to search for peace, national reconciliation, and particularly in national rehabilitation and reconstruction, under the correct leadership of his majesty the king. Prince Krompreah further said that the RGC has four goals for the investment sector, namely political stability, higher wages, identifying land for leasing, and lower taxes. Concerning the Khmer Rouge issue, Prince Krompreah said that this cannot be considered a big issue. Furthermore, the RGC will make Sihanoukville a duty-free zone for investment. He further said that as for agriculture, the royal government has paid great attention to vigorously promoting the standard of living by paying attention to clearing networks of canals, planting trees and other crops, and ending the clearing of forests. Schallenberg expressed profound thanks for Cambodian efforts to rehabilitate and rebuild the country. He further said that along with providing assistance to rebuild the hydropower station at Kirirom, the Austrian Government will continue to contribute to assisting the Cambodian Government and people within the lofty Austrian-Cambodian ties of friendship.
