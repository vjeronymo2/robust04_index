BFN [Excerpt] At the invitation of Shanghai Municipal People's Government, a Guizhou Provincial Economic Delegation headed by provincial Governor Chen Shineng made an inspection tour in Shanghai from 12 to 18 May. The delegation returned to Guiyang yesterday [19 May]. During its stay in Shanghai, the delegation inspected Pudong, [words indistinct], the Foreign Exchange Swap Center, the Stock Exchange, the Metals Transaction Market, and so on. Shanghai municipal party and government leaders welcomed the delegation. Municipal party Secretary Wu Bangguo; Huang Ju, municipal party deputy secretary and mayor; and several vice mayors held talks with the delegation on bilateral cooperation. Responsible comrades of relevant departments and enterprises also held discussions and signed contracts on cooperative projects. [passage omitted]
