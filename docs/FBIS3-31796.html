Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Petrovac, 23 Feb (TANJUG) -- According to the media in Velika Kladusa, members of the 5th Corps of the so-called Bosnia-Herzegovina army [loyal to Izetbegovic] have launched fierce attacks on positions held by the army of the autonomous province of Western Bosnia [loyal to Fikret Abdic] along the entire demarcation line. According to both Velika Kladusa and Bihac, the fiercest clashes today took place in Trzacke Rastele and Pecigrad. Neither side has mentioned casualty tolls or changes in the frontlines. Muslim radio in Bihac has appealed on several occasions today to supporters of Fikret Abdic to lay down their arms and place themselves at the disposal of the 5th Corps of Izetbegovic's army to defend the sovereignty and integrity of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina.
