BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Social Democratic Populist Party [SDPP] whip Ercan Karakas said that the future of the coalition government depends on whether or not economic, democratic, and social reforms are carried out. He was speaking after the extraordinary meeting of the SDPP National Assembly group which convened to assess the election results. He stressed that the reforms must be carried out in line with a schedule and also be guaranteed. Discussing the meeting, Karakas said that the members assessed the policies of the coalition government, and said that they consider it useful to remain in the government in order to reach the targets of the government program.
