BFN [Text] On 26 April in Tu Hoa Township, the Quang Ninh People's Court tried eight defendants on charges of destroying important national security projects and handling property obtained from other people's criminal activities. The court sentenced Vu Van Vuong to death for his leading role in the conspiracy to destroy high-voltage power line supports. It sentenced Khong Tiet Bao to life imprisonment for destroying important projects and national security equipment. Six other defendants, guilty of handling property obtained from other people's criminal activities, received prison terms ranging from two to six years.
