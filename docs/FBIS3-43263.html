Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "Network Africa" program] [Text] There are reports of an attack against an American oil installation in the northern Angola. A spokeswoman for the oil company, Chevron, which runs the operation at Malongo, said three mortar bombs landed in the base, slightly injuring one person and damaging two vehicles. The Portuguese News Agency quoted Angolan military sources as saying the attack was possibly carried out by forces linked to the UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] rebel movement, but the UNITA spokesman at the United Nations, Marcos Samondo, said he knew nothing about the attack.
