940910 FT 10 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Thomas Walker Thomas Walker, maker of metal smallwares, increased pre-tax profits from Pounds 47,370 to Pounds 221,723 in the year to June 30, on turnover of Pounds 3.99m, against Pounds 3.66m. Earnings per share climbed from 0.45p to 2.8p and a recommended final dividend of 0.5p (0.4p) makes a total for the year of 0.68p (0.58p).
