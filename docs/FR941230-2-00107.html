NUCLEAR REGULATORY COMMISSION Correction to Biweekly Notice Applications and Amendments to Operating Licenses Involving No Significant Hazards Consideration On December 21, 1994 (59 FR 65809), the Federal Register published the Biweekly Notice of Applications and Amendments to Operating Licenses Involving No Significant Hazards Considerations. In the first sentence of the last paragraph in the second column, on page 65809, the date which reads ``January 13, 1995,'' should be corrected to read ``January 20, 1995.'' Dated at Rockville, Maryland, this 22nd day of December 1994. For the Nuclear Regulatory Commission. Frederick J.
