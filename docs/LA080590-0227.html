August 5, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Orange National Bancorp: The Orange-based parent company of Orange National Bank said it lost $28,604 for the second quarter, the result of a $567,627 allocation for loan loss reserves. The company reported net income of $339,937 for the year-ago period. For the six-month period, net income dropped 70% to $218,000 from $728,000.
