930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Great Southern advances 13% Despite a further small decline in the national mortality rate, Mr James Smillie, chairman of the Great Southern Group, funeral services concern, announced a 13 per cent increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 2.55m to Pounds 2.88m for the first six months of 1993. Turnover of this USM-quoted company advanced by 9 per cent to Pounds 16.15m (Pounds 14.76m) while the profit figure was after lower interest of Pounds 503,000, compared with Pounds 654,000. Earnings per share were 14.4p against 12.5p, while the interim dividend is stepped up from 3.6p to 4p.
