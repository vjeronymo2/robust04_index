Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, February 24 (XINHUA) -- Visiting Slovak Prime Minister Vladimir Meciar has wound up his five-day China visit and left here by air today. He came to China at the invitation of Chinese Premier Li Peng. He arrived here from Beijing on Tuesday. Meciar met with entrepreneurs from both China and Slovakia who are attending Sino-Slovak trade talks and introduced his country's policies concerning investment, economics and trade. He visited the Shanghai Stock Exchange, the Yangpu bridge, the Pudong New Area, the Jinqiao Export Processing Zone and the Waigaoqiao free trade zone.
