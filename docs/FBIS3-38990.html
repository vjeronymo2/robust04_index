Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Swiss foreign minister Flavio Cotti says there is no alternative for Switzerland than to eventually join the European Union [EU]. Mr. Cotti, speaking at Zurich University, said such problems in Europe as crime, transport, and environmental policies could no longer be solved on a national or regional basis. He said the EU was still far from being a perfect organization, but was already a crucial guarantor for regional stability.
