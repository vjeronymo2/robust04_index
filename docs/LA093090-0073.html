CRITICS CHOICE: RIGHTEOUS PILGRIM; THE LIFE AND TIMES OF HAROLD L. ICKES BY T. H. WATKINS (HENRY HOLT: $35; 1,024 PP.)

"As Watkins demonstrates in this rewarding and illuminating work, Ickes had no shortage of ego -- but his real fuel was conviction, burning at an octane hardly ever seen in Washington any more." Ronald Brownstein
