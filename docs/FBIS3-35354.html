Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The president of the Republic, Lieutenant General 'Umar al-Bashir, on Monday issued the 10th Republican Decree for 1994, appointing 142 ministers and advisers for the 26 states of the country. According to the decree, every northern state is to have five ministers and the fifth minister, for peace and rehabilitation, is to be part of the state's government. [sentence as heard] An adviser for political affairs has been ordered for northern Bahr al-Ghazal and Jonglei States.
