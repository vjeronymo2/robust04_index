Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [POSTFACTUM report: "Belarus Ready To Hand Over `Beltransgaz' To Become Part of `Gazprom'"] [Text] It has come to light that the Government of Belarus submitted to the Supreme Soviet a draft agreement between the Governments of Belarus and Russia on the merger of the state-owned enterprise "Beltransgaz" with the "Gazprom" joint-stock company. Vladimir Novik, secretary of the Supreme Soviet committee on industry, stated that such a deal violates Belarus' legislation.
