BFN [From the "Telemetro Reports" newscast] [Text] President Guillermo Endara continues his visit to Argentina. Last night Argentine President Carlos Menem hosted a dinner in his honor at the presidential palace. Today (Juan Bautista Joffre), former Argentine ambassador to Panama, will host a luncheon for the Panamanian president. This evening Endara will attend a tango musical performance in the traditional San Telmo neighborhood. Meanwhile, during statements to local media in Argentina, President Endara said that he agreed with the Legislative Assembly's rejection of the draft amnesty bill. Endara said that the manner in which this draft bill has been presented provided a wide loophole through which many criminals could escape.
