940406 FT 06 APR 94 / UK Company News: Home Counties Pre-tax profits of Home Counties Newspaper Holdings fell from a restated Pounds 863,000 to Pounds 459,000 in the year to end-December. The outcome was struck on turnover up from Pounds 22.9m to Pounds 24m. Earnings per share came out at 4.59p (8.36p) and a proposed final dividend of 2.5p (3.25p) gives a total of 4.5p (6p).
