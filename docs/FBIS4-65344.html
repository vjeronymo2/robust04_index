BFN [DPA report: "Defense Ministry Wants To Shed 1,900 Jobs"] [Text] Bonn, 15 May -- Defense Minister Ruehe (Christian Democratic Union--CDU) must reduce the number of his employees in the Defense Ministry from the present 5,500 to 3,300 in the long term, said a Defense Ministry spokesman during the weekend. Ruehe wants to eliminate these posts by means of transfers and early retirements. There should be no dismissals, said the spokesman. Even when Stoltenberg (CDU) was defense minister, ways of reducing the number of employees were considered. BILD AM SONNTAG has reported on these economization plans, referring to a confidential letter Ruehe sent to the Bundestag Defense Committee, announcing the personnel reduction. Ten subcommittees are to be abolished.
