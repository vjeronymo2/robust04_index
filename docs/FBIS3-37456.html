Language: English Article Type:BFN ["News Release"] [Text] Vice-Admiral Viktor Ovchinnikov, the head of the Russian military team at inter-state negotiations with Estonia, arrived in Tallinn on Wednesday. It was Russia's initiative, his Estonian counterpart at the negotiations, Ago Tiiman from the Foreign Ministry, told ETA while adding that although a meeting between them was scheduled for Thursday, he had not been informed of the topics to be discussed. On Wednesday, Ovchinnikov visited the Russian garrison in Tallinn and the former Soviet submarine training base in north west Estonia, Paldiski.
