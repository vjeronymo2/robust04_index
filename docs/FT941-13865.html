940122 FT 22 JAN 94 / Finance and the Family: Pilot trust launches Pep Pilot Investment Trust, the smaller companies fund launched last March and run by Rutherford Asset Management, is introducing a Pep and regular savings plan. Minimum investment in the Pep will be Pounds 250 through a lump sum, or Pounds 25 a month by direct debit. There is an extra 0.5 per cent annual charge on the Pep, on top of the usual 1 per cent management fee, plus a dealing charge of 1 per cent. The Pep will be administered by Sharelink. The savings scheme has a monthly minimum of Pounds 50, or Pounds 250 lump sums, and dividends are automatically reinvested.
