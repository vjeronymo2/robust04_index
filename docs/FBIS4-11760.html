BFN [Text] Brother President Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, has received information that Russia is ready to take part in an international force to protect Palestinian citizens in Hebron. The Palestinian News Agency says that this decision by the Russian leadership has been emphasized in a letter conveyed by the Russian ambassador in Tunis to brother President Abu-'Ammar. Meanwhile, President Abu-'Ammar has asked France to help provide an international protection for the Palestinian citizens in the occupied territories.
