940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Donelon Tyson Donelon Tyson, the construction group, suffered pre-tax losses of Pounds 934,000 for the six months to June 30, compared with profits of Pounds 439,000. The result, however, was an improvement over the Pounds 2.57m deficit at December 31. The company said most of the first-half losses were sustained in the joinery division, which is to be discontinued this year. Turnover rose from Pounds 34.5m to Pounds 45.8m. Losses per share were 1p (0.7p earnings) and there is no interim dividend.
