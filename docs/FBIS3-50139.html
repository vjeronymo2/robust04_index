Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Serbian Assembly will resume its discussion on the nomination of candidates for chairman of the assembly in half an hour. During the recess this morning, 119 deputies of the Socialist Party of Serbia again proposed Zoran Arandjelovic as chairman of the republican parliament, while 30 Depos [Democratic Movement of Serbia] deputies again proposed Gvozden Rosic. Some deputies have expressed the view that this is a stalemate situation. There is a possibility of neither of the two candidates being elected, as was the case last night. The Serbian Assembly is currently in recess. The session is scheduled to resume at 1430.
