Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Gaza City, Occupied, March 4 (AFP)--[dateline as received] Settlers shot dead a Palestinian who attacked them with a knife in a Jewish settlement on the Gaza Strip on Friday [4 March], a settlers spokesman said. Two settlers were hurt, one seriously, in a greenhouse on the edge of the [Gush] Qatif settlement early in the morning after three Palestinians managed to get in. The other two attackers escaped, one winged by a bullet. The army sealed off the area, set up roadblocks and launched a manhunt for the two men.
