Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert ("LRAGIR" daily), 24/02/94 -- The tension at the Karabakh fronts slightly decreased. The conflicting sides refrained from active military operations and have been carrying positional battles only. According to the headquarters of the RMK [Republic of Mountainous Karabakh] Defense Army, no significant changes were reported yesterday along the whole front line. The units of Azeri Army only shelled the defense positions of the Karabakh army from mortars and large caliber machine guns. No victims. The situation in Kelbadzharskiy district is also rather calm. The Omar gorge is completely controlled by the Armenian units. According to the reconnaissance and the military specialists, increasing cases of desertion are reported in the Azeri Army. Reliable sources informed us, that the current reserves of Azeri army only make 12,000 soldiers. According to the specialists, no serious military tasks can be resolved by such forces. We also found out, that some officers of the army of the Islamic Republic of Iran are fighting in the Azeri Army additionally to Afghan mojahedins and slavic mercenaries. One of the Iranian officers was captured as a prisoner of war by the Karabakh forces. The captured officer participated in the Iran-Iraq conflict in time.
