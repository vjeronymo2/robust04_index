March 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

DINING FACILITY DESIGN COMPLETED FOR MARINES

Edward Carson Beall & Associates of Torrance has completed the architectural and interior design on a $2.5-million Las Flores dining facility at the Camp Pendleton Marine Base in Camp Pendleton.

Las Flores is the first of three dining facilities being remodeled at the base. The modernization project took 13 months to complete and involved the addition of a 6,500-square-foot building to an existing 9,800-square-foot structure.
