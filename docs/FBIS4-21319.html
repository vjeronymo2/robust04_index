Mozambique MB2204174694 Johannesburg Radio South Africa Network in English 1600 GMT 22 Apr 94 MB2204174694 Johannesburg Radio South Africa Network English BFN [Text] The Nelspruit narcotics bureau, in cooperation with the Swaziland police, have seized more than 2 million mandrax tablets with a street value of more than 30 million rands. Police spokesperson Colonel Schalk Pienaar said that the tablets had been found in a trailer at the Lomahasha border post between Swaziland and Mozambique. A 35-year-old man has been arrested in connection with the incident.
