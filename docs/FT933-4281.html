930909 FT 09 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Northern Ind'l edges ahead to Pounds 443,880 Northern Industrial Improve-ment Trust, the investment and property company, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 443,880 for the year to the end of March, against Pounds 425,996. Income totalled Pounds 493,896 (Pounds 473,235) and earnings came out at 27.55p (24.7p) per share. A proposed final dividend of 16p makes a total for the year of 25p (23p).
