September 30, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

HAWKS, MCMULLEN COME TO TERMS

Former Hart High and College of the Canyons standout Mitch McMullen has agreed to terms with the Atlanta Hawks and will report to the team's training camp in Memphis, Tenn., on Friday.

"It feels good, but nothing to get too excited about," said McMullen, a 6-foot-10 center. "It's a start, but I'm going to have to play as hard as I possibly can and show that I'm a hard worker."

Terms of the one-year contract were not disclosed. STEVEN HERBERT
