November 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

STALLONE'S STARDOM

I've been reading Calendar for 15 years and never before was I so moved as by the sadness, complexity and innocence of Lawrence Christon's interview with Sylvester Stallone (Oct. 28).

Rocky and Rambo were only Stallone's adolescence; now the real man can shine on his own.

LARI WOLF

Beverly Hills
