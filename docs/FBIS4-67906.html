CSO [Text] New Delhi, March 2 (PTI). The developmental work on ``Akash'' and ``Nag'' missile systems is expected to be completed by 1995-96, after which these missiles are expected to be inducted into service-use, the Lok Sabha was informed today. In a written reply to Mr. Harin Pathak, the minister of state for defence, Mr. Mallikarjun, said the test fire of ``Prithvi'' has not been postponed.
