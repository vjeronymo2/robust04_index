Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Phnom Penh AKP January 20 -- Co-Prime Ministers Prince Norodom Ranariddh and Hun Sen met in Beijing Tuesday [18 January] with Chinese President Jiang Zemin. During talks, Jiang informed the Cambodian prime minsters of the ties of friendship and cooperation between the two countries and peoples and of the works of national construction being carried out in China. Prince Norodom Ranariddh who is in Beijing for the first time together with Hun Sen since the formation of the new government in November last year expressed thanks to the Chinese government for its active contribution to the peace process and national reconciliation in Cambodia. He also praised the Chinese president for his clear-sighted leadership in all-sided development, especially in the field of economy. The meeting was held after wreath-laid ceremonies at the monument for memorial of fallen combatants and at the Mao Zedong mausoleum and after the visit of sewing and freezer-producing factories.
