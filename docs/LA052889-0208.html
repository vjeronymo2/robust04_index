May 28, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BASEBALL, SOFTBALL AND TENNIS PLAYOFF SITES ANNOUNCED

Pairing sites for Tuesday's Southern Section playoffs in baseball and softball and Wednesday's team tennis championship were announced Saturday.

The 3-A baseball semifinal between Kennedy High School (23-5) and Tustin (21-8) is tentatively set for 3:15 p.m. at UC Irvine's Anteater Field. A 5-A semifinal matching El Dorado (21-8) and Lakewood (25-3) will be held at 4 p.m. at Blair Field in Long Beach. That game will be the first of a doubleheader, with the other semifinal between Hacienda Heights Wilson and Long Beach Millikan scheduled at 7 p.m.

In 4-A softball, Western (24-5) will play Kennedy (20-4) at Cypress Park. In 3-A, Mission Viejo (17-8) will play Ramona (22-7) at Alicia Park in Mission Viejo. Both games are set for 3:15 p.m.

The 3-A tennis final Wednesday between Woodbridge (18-4) and Sunny Hills (21-1) will be held at the Sunny Hills Racquet Club at 3:15 p.m.
