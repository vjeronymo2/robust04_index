Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Kondrashov] [Text] Belgrade, 14 Mar -- Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Vitaliy Churkin, special representative of the Russian president, arrived here today following his talks in Zagreb and Sarajevo. Immediately after his arrival he began talks with Serbian President Slobodan Milosevic. In the afternoon, the Russian diplomat is to have a meeting in Belgrade with members of the Bosnian Serb leadership. In the course of another of his missions to the republics of former Yugoslavia, Churkin is discussing the current situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina with the aim of drawing up new initiatives to build upon the positive moves in the process of achieving a settlement in Bosnia.
