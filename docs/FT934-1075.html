931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / International Company News: Guide to World Currencies The FT Guide to World Currencies, previously published on Tuesdays, is now being published on Mondays. In yesterday's paper, it appeared on page 24 in the UK and page 21 overseas. Readers who missed it can obtain a copy by fax. To use this service, dial 0891-437 001 on your fax machine. When the FT fax service answers with an electronic tone, press the start button on your machine and hang up the handset. Calls are charged at 36p/minute cheap rate, 48p/ minute at other times. The service is available in the UK only.
