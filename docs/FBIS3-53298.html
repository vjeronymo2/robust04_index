Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The deadline for subscription in the shares of Solidaire, the real estate company for reconstructing Beirut's downtown, expired two days ago. Today Dr. Nasir al-Shamma', secretary general of the company's founding committee, called the sale of shares a success. Dr. al-Shamma' noted that share purchase offers have totalled $926 million. Most of the buyers, he said, are Lebanese. He explained that the company will return about $300 million to Arab subscribers to give the Lebanese first choice, in keeping with the company's terms of association.
