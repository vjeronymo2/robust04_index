920201 FT 01 FEB 92 / Nagorno-Karabakh fighting flares Azerbaijani and Armenian military units clashed yesterday in Nagorno-Karabakh as fighting intensified for control of the mountainous enclave that is claimed by both countries, AP reports from Moscow. Quoting the Armenian News Service, Russian television news reported that the Azerbaijani army had launched a widescale offensive on the Armenian part of Nagorno-Karabakh.
