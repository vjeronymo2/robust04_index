CRITICS' CHOICE: THE MIND OF SOUTH AFRICA BY ALLISTER SPARKS (ALFRED A. KNOPF: $24.95& 424 PP.)

"With the passion of a true patriot, Sparks looks forward to the day when his country ceases to be a symbol of racism, and instead of squandering its resources on hateful division, is able to tap its potential as an engine of African economic development." Richard Caplan
