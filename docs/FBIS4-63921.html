BFN [Text] Residents of the South Kuril Islands frustrated over economic hardships now contemplate the possibility of breaking away from the Sakhalin Region, the strike committee of a large fish factory on Kunashir Island says. In a message to the Russian leadership, the committee has drawn attention to the factory's plight, run-away prices and endemic pay delays. It asked President Yeltsin to appoint his own representative to the Kurils.
