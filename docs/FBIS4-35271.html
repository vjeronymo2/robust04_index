BFN [Text] Hizballah Secretary General Hasan Nasrallah has called on the Lebanese Government to withdraw from the peace talks in response to Israel's abduction of Mustafa al-Dirani. He affirmed that civilian peace is in the interest of the resistance, coexistence, and building a state to which all Lebanese aspire. Al-Dirani's family yesterday appealed to the International Committee of the Red Cross [ICRC] to act quickly in providing medical assistance to him. In a memorandum submitted to the ICRC, al-Dirani's family held Israel responsible for the deterioration of his health and for preventing the Red Cross from visiting him.
