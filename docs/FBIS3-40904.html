Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Grigoriy Karasin, Russian Foreign Ministry's official representative, has confirmed that Moscow will provide Ukraine with fuel elements for the needs of atomic power engineering as a compensation for the nuclear charges which are being withdrawn. Speaking at a briefing in Moscow he added that the purchase by the United States of a part of the uranium released during the dismantling of nuclear weapons would contribute to solving the financial issues which emerge in this context.
