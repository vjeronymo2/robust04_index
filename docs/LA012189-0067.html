January 21, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

THE INAUGURATION OF GEORGE BUSH;

THE BUSH TEAM IS READY TO GO

President-elect Bush has completed his Cabinet selections. Here is the lineup, subject to Senate conformation.

Secretary of State: James A. Baker III

Treasury Secretary: Nicholas F. Brady

Defense Secretary: John Tower

Attorney General: Dick Thornburgh

Interior Secretary: Manuel Lujan Jr.

Agriculture Secretary: Clayton K. Yeutter

Commerce Secretary: Robert A. Mosbacher

Labor Secretary: Elizabeth H. Dole

Health and Human Services Secretary: Louis W. Sullivan

Housing and Urban Development: Jack Kemp

Transportation Secretary: Samuel K. Skinner

Energy Secretary: James D. Watkins

Education Secretary: Lauro F. Cavazos

Veterans Affairs Secretary: Edward J. Derwinski

Director, Office of Management and Budget: Richard G. Darman

U.S. Trade Representative: Carla A. Hills

Director, Drug Control Policy: William J. Bennett
