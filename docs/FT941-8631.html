940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Intercare buys rest of Montis Intercare Group, the USM-quoted healthcare products supplier, has acquired the minority 25 per cent of Montis Medical, the Netherlands-based mobility products maker. The acquisition was completed through the exercise of the option granted to Intercare in the acquisition agreement in September 1991. Intercare has raised Pounds 1.75m before expenses by an issue of 1.25m shares (4.77 per cent of the existing issued capital) at 140p each. Some Pounds 1.05m will be used for the purchase, with the balance utilised to reduce gearing, strengthen the balance sheet and for small in-fill acquisitions. The shares have been unconditionally placed with institutional investors of UBS and Wise Speke. Certain Intercare directors have also sold 677,500 shares at 140p each.
