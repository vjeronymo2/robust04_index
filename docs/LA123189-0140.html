December 31, 1989, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

San Diego Police seized about $35,000 worth of heroin Saturday and arrested three men in connection with the incident, officials said.

The police found about 750 "balloons" of heroin, several large uncut rocks of heroin, and $1,200 in cash when they conducted a noon-time raid in the 4900 block of Trojan Avenue, officials said.

Three men, Salvador Castillo, 33, Ramon Martin, 21, and Cortez Castro, 33, were arrested.
