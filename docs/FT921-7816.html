920220 FT 20 FEB 92 / Zimbabwe gets Dollars 1bn in aid Zimbabwe was promised Dollars 1bn in aid yesterday to help it overcome drought and persevere with economic reforms, Reuter reports from Paris. Zimbabwe faced its severest drought since 1967, Mr Bernard Chidzero, finance minister said after a two-day meeting with aid donors. The country, traditionally a maize exporter, would need to import 1m tonnes of maize this year.
