May 21, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

THE PROFESSOR HEAD OF THE CLASS AT BELMONT

Team Clover Stable's The Professor saved ground while racing close to the leaders, then came off the rail in mid-stretch under the urging of Chris Antley to beat D'Parrot by a nose in the $119,000 Mike Lee Handicap on Sunday at Belmont Park.

D'Parrot, ridden by Jerry Bailey, prevailed by 3 1/2 lengths over Power Bolt in the race for horses aged 3 and up.

The victory was the second in six starts this year for The Professor, who covered 1 mile on a fast track in 1:36 4/5 to earn $71,400.
