BFN [Text] As part of the Croat-Bosniak negotiations on implementing the Washington agreement, the two delegations have resumed talks about border controls in central Bosnia and Hercegovina. Bosnian Croat delegation head Kresimir Zubak said that there are problems surrounding the issue of the borders and that they could be resolved only as part of a package. The Muslim side has proposed that Herzegovina be divided into two cantons: one Croat and the other with power-sharing on a parity basis. On the other hand, the Croatian side insists on a single Herzegovina. Muslim delegation head Haris Silajdzic said that he expects nothing from today's negotiations in the U.S. Embassy in Vienna.
