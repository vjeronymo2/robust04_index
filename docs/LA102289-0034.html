October 22, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ROCK 'N' ROIL: DEBATES ON WHO'S NO. 1, RACISM, SEXISM AND CRITICISM

Regarding Axl Rose's racial and anti-gay epithets: I find his homophobia remarkable for a man who has borrowed so much attire from gay subculture -- leather chaps, biker cap, etc. Maybe this is one reason he has had "very bad experiences with homosexuals": They mistake him for one.

WILLIAM O'NEAL

Carpinteria
