931206 FT 06 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Property Trust acquisitions Property Trust has acquired the freehold interests in two high street retail parades at 1-30 Streatham High Road, London SW, and 207-213 High Street, Dorking, Surrey for a total of Pounds 3.54m, representing an initial yield of 11.7 per cent. The purchases will be financed by existing cash resources and bank finance. Gross annual rental income from the properties is currently about Pounds 414,000.
