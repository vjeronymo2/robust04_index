930402 FT 02 APR 93 / Italy's inflation at 4.2 per cent Italy's inflation in March dropped to an annualised rate of 4.2 per cent, the lowest since spring 1987, writes Robert Graham in Rome. According to figures from Istat, the official statistics institute, prices in March rose only 0.2 per cent. As yet the effects of the 30 per cent devaluation of the lira against its main trading currencies has not been felt.
