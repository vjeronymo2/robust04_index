940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Finsbury Trust Finsbury Trust, the special situations investment trust, lifted net assets by 41 per cent to 181.1p over the 12 months to March 31. Investment income for the year slipped to Pounds 1.38m (Pounds 1.52m). After-tax revenue dropped from Pounds 872,000 to Pounds 854,000 giving earnings per share of 3.5p (3.6p). The final dividend is an unchanged 2p for a same-again 3.2p total.
