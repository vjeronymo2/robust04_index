940905 FT 05 SEP 94 / Business Travel: Latvia plan Scandinavian Airlines and a US-based investment firm presented plans on Friday for a new international carrier based in Latvia. The plan, which was requested by Latvia and seems assured of approval, calls for replacing two current Lativan airlines with one system based in Riga. The new carrier, which has not yet been given a name, would use the Latvian capital as a hub and initially would fly within the Baltics and neighboring countries.
