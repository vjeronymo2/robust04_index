930927 FT 27 SEP 93 / Alliance Gas signs Hyde field ALLIANCE GAS, one of the independent gas companies challenging British Gas, claims to have secured 10 per cent of the UK market for commercial gas. The company, a joint venture between British Petroleum, Statoil and Norsk Hydro, is due to sign up today for the entire output of the Hyde gas field in the North Sea over the next 23 years. The field is expected to produce more than 200m therms a year.
