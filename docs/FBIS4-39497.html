BFN [Text] The Ukrainian Army should stay aloof from politics, according to Ukrainian President and Supreme Commander of Armed Forces Leonid Kravchuk. "Political feuds should not touch the Army," he told a conference of chief executives devoted to army issues in Kiev today. "According to the requirements of the times, the armed forces and the state machinery must be permanently ready to defend Ukraine, its sovereignty and territorial integrity," Kravchuk said. He pointed out that the Army's main goal was to maintain the country's ability to defend itself and maintain the inviolability of its borders. The Ukrainian Defense Ministry press service told INTERFAX-Ukraine that the conference had been attended by the leaders of government, Defense Ministry and the military complex. The conference touched on a wide range of issues embraced by the single goal to achieve a qualitative change in the management of the Ukrainian armed forces.
