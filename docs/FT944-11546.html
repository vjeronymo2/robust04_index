941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Flagstone Shareholders in Flagstone Holdings, the USM-traded leisure concern, have approved the change of name to Queensborough Holdings and the cancellation of the deferred shares and the share premium. The moves follow the arrival of Mr Kevin Leech as chairman in August having acquired a holding of almost 22 per cent. He hopes the company will return to a full listing by the end of the year. It reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 424,000 (Pounds 730,000) for the year ended January 31 on turnover of Pounds 607,000 (Pounds 521,000).
