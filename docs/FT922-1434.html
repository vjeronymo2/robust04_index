920623 FT 23 JUN 92 / Liberia faces sanctions threat WEST AFRICAN foreign ministers threatened yesterday to impose economic sanctions on Liberia if Liberian groups kept obstructing a peace plan, Reuter reports from Dakar. The ministers also accused Mr Charles Taylor's National Patriotic Front of Liberia (NPFL) of murdering six soldiers in Senegal's peacekeeping contingent on May 28.
