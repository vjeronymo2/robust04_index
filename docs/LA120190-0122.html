December 1, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

BRITAIN;

MAJOR SWORN IN IN PALACE RITES

Prime Minister John Major was sworn into office by Queen Elizabeth II at a Privy Council meeting at Buckingham Palace. During the ceremony, a tradition since the Middle Ages, Major and the five new members of his 22-member Cabinet in charge of government departments kissed the queen's hand and were made lifetime members of the Privy Council, a mostly ceremonial body of royal councilors.
