931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / International Company News: Canadian goldminer ahead 18% AMERICAN Barrick, one of North America's top four gold producers, posted an 18 per cent increase in third-quarter profit, with sharply higher output from the Goldstrike mine in Nevada, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. With initial development of Goldstrike completed and cash resources mounting, Toronto-based American Barrick is focusing its search for new reserves on South America and Asia. It may also develop two projects in Peru. Net profit for the quarter was USDollars 58.3m, or 21 cents a share, up from Dollars 49.5m, or 18 cents, on revenue of Dollars 178m, against Dollars 143.5m. Output was 413,829oz, compared with 343,474oz. The average price realised was Dollars 410, against Dollars 430, and cash cost per oz was Dollars 202, against Dollars 224. Profit for the nine months was Dollars 161.5m, or 57 cents, up from Dollars 109.4m, or 39 cents, on revenues of Dollars 497m, against Dollars 340m.
