921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / Oil-rig modules Two oil-rig modules to sit on top of a 17,500-tonne eight-legged steel jacket built for the North Sea Tiffany field development were floated along the River Tyne yesterday to be assembled at sea. Tiffany's recoverable reserves are estimated at 125m barrels of oil with an unspecified quantity of gas. The development has been handled by Tiffany Contractors, a consortium formed by Amec Process and Energy, the UK engineering group, and Saipem, the Italian contractor
