931022 FT 22 OCT 93 / Former director jailed for fraud MR Philip Pratt, former managing director of Development and Prototype Engineering, based in Bromsgrove near Birmingham, was jailed for 2 1/2 years at Hereford Crown Court yesterday. He pleaded guilty to charges of fraudulent trading, furnishing false information and value added tax fraud. He admitted carrying on the trading of his company, now in liquidation, with the intention of defrauding creditors of more than Pounds 1.7m.
