March 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

IN DEFENSE OF A 'HOMEMAKER FROM THE MIDWEST'

I was surprised to see that you did not publish one letter on March 12 in support of Terry Rakolta's efforts to help maintain traditional standards of decency in broadcasting. My family and several of my friends' families applaud her efforts.

ERIC SNYDER

Westminster

At press time for the March 12 issue Calendar had received no letters supporting Rakolta.
