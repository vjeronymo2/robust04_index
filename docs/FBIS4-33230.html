BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Peru monitored through 2 June. The Peruvian Government received $1.361 billion as a result of the auction of the Peruvian Telephone Company, and the sale of 35 percent of the shares of the National Telecommunications Enterprise of Peru. (Lima LA REPUBLICA in Spanish 18 May 94 Economic Section p 11) The National Statistics Institute on 1 June reported that Peru's industrial installed capacity increased 10 percent from May 1993 to 31 April. The total use of industrial companies' installed capacity rose to 56 percent. The cement and metal industries registered the greatest increases, with 90 percent and 83 percent respectively. The private Apoyo Inc. consulting firm reported that the average usage rate of the industrial installed capacity ranges from 60 to 70 percent. (Madrid EFE in Spanish 1908 GMT 1 Jun 94)
