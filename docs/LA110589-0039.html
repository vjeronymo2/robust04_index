November 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ANOTHER VIEW OF POMONA URGED

I protest the demeaning of Pomona as a crime-ridden and poverty-stricken city (Times, Oct. 29).

You only glorify the crime. Your staff writers need to give both views of Pomona and not constantly state that minorities are always the cause of crime and violence.

The middle class still lives in Pomona maintaining nice homes and careers. Write something for once about Pomona that is positive.

VERLAIN ROY

Pomona
