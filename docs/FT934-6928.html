931123 FT 23 NOV 93 / Malaysia to sell Skyhawk fighters Malaysia has decided to sell through open tender 35 A-4 Skyhawk fighters now being stored in the US, defence minister Najib Razak said yesterday, Reuter reports from Kuala Lumpur. 'With the open tender, we will take whatever offer we can get,' the national Bernama news agency quoted him as saying. The jets were among 88 US-made Skyhawks Malaysia bought without engines, or weapons and navigation systems in 1980 from the US Navy for Dollars 50,000 each. The Royal Malaysian Air Force took delivery of 53 of the Skyhawks after refurbishment between 1982 and 1985.
