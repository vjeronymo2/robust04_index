940706 FT 06 JUL 94 / International Company News: Greek sell-off details The Greek government, which plans to float 25 per cent of the state telecoms company, OTE, will sell around 60 per cent of that total abroad and list the other 40 per cent on the Athens bourse, a national economy ministry official said, Reuter reports from Athens. The sale is likely to be in the form of private placements in Europe, the US and Japan, with direct listing in Athens. OTE has annual profits of Dr150bn-200bn (Dollars 623m-831.6m).
