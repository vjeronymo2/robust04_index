Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Patnos, Agri Mar. 29 (A.A.) -- The death toll rose to five following the clash that broke out between supporters of two separate parties in Patnos, Agri earlier on Tuesday [29 March]. The incident broke out when supporters of the True Path Party (DYP) mayoral candidate Ihsan Hallac, who won the election, celebrated and marched through the town. As they were passing in front of the Prosperity Party (RP) campaign office, angry shouting broke out which quickly turned into an armed clash. Among those killed was the RP mayoral candidate Fahrettin Esin including two members of his family. A curfew was imposed in the city, while a large number of people were detained.
