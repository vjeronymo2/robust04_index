920221 FT 21 FEB 92 / Council rent rises will average 12.6% COUNCIL HOUSING rents are set to rise by an average of 12.6 per cent, almost three times the rate of inflation, according to a survey of 40 local authorities in England. The survey, by the Institute of Housing, shows average rent increases ranging from 4 per cent in Calderdale, West Yorkshire, to 27.4 per cent in Hackney, London. Next year's average council-house rent for the 40 authorities will be Pounds 32.46.
