930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Shipping side hits Global Global Group, the USM-quoted meat and shipping services concern, returned profits of Pounds 1.4m pre-tax for 1992, a downturn of 16 per cent on the previous year's Pounds 1.68m. The food division continued to progress with operating profits ahead 11 per cent at Pounds 1.36m. Shipping services, however, declined from Pounds 1.61m to Pounds 798,000 at the operating level. Overall, turnover improved to Pounds 75m (Pounds 72.3m). A proposed final dividend of 0.3p makes a same-again 0.5p total. Earnings per share emerged at 0.83p (0.95p).
