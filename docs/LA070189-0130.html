July 1, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

UC IRVINE HIRES MURPHY, CARR AS ASSISTANT COACHES

UC Irvine has hired Ernie Carr and Tim Murphy as assistant basketball coaches.

Carr was interim coach at Saddleback College last season and a part-time assistant coach at UCLA in 1987-88. He was basketball coach at Dominguez High School for 12 years, with a record of 195-102.

Murphy is returning to Irvine after being an assistant coach at Pacific Lutheran University in Tacoma, Wash., last year. He was an assistant at Irvine for three years before leaving.
