April 11, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

NATION;

BUSH WILL UNDERGO PHYSICAL

President Bush will undergo his annual routine physical at Bethesda Naval Hospital on Thursday, the White House said today. Bush had his last physical May 10, 1989.

White House physician Dr. Burton Lee, who will direct the physical exam, "describes the current state of the President's health as excellent," White House Press Secretary Marlin Fitzwater said.
