920305 FT 05 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Sun Life Corpn rises 36% to Pounds 47.5m Sun Life Corporation, the life insurer taken over at the end of last year by Rockleigh Corporation, a company jointly owned by Societe Centrale Union des Assurances de Paris and TransAtlantic Holdings, lifted pre-tax profits 36 per cent from Pounds 34.8m to Pounds 47.5m in 1991. A final dividend of 42.4p is proposed to make a total of 56.4p (47p) for the year.
