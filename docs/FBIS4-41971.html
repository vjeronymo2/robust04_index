BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Yuriy Tyssovskiy] [Text] Moscow June 29 TASS -- Talks between Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and Foreign Minister of the Yemeni Republic Muhammad Salim Basindawa who arrived in Moscow on Wednesday, began at the building of the Russian Foreign Ministry today. It is expected that later today the head of the Russian foreign office will hold talks with South Yemeni emissary -- Deputy Secretary General of the Central Committee of the Yemeni Socialist Party Saleh Saleh Muhammad. Valerian Shuvayev, head of the section of countries on the Arabian Peninsula and the department of the Middle East and north Africa of the Russian Foreign ministry, told ITAR-TASS about Moscow's position at the talks. These contacts, he said, are being carried out within the framework of Russian diplomacy's efforts aimed at reaching a ceasefire between the confronting sides in Yemen which would become the first step towards halting a bloodshed. All internal problems of this Arab country must be resolved at a negotiating table. We are prepared to coordinate our actions with all who seek to achieve the same aims, both on a bilateral basis and within the framework of the United Nations to promote the soonest stopping of the senseless fratricidal war, the Russian diplomat pointed out.
