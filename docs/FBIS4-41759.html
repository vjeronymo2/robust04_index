BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Mission Control Centre June 28 TASS -- Russian cosmonauts Viktor Afanasyev, Yuriy Usachev and Valeriy Polyakov are carrying on their mission on board the orbital complex Mir. They carried out several sessions of photography of various regions of the planet under the environmental study programme. Photography is to be continued on Tuesday. The mission commander with the help of the research physician will undergo a comprehensive examination of the cardiovascular system by an ultrasonic method with the use of the pneumatic-vacuum space suit Chibis. Technical maintenance of equipment and instruments of the station is also planned. The handling of the progress M-23 automatic cargo spacecraft is being finalised. On Monday the joint propulsion plant of the station was refuelled and the second fuel component -- oxidiser -- is to be pumped on Tuesday. According to reports from orbit, the flight is proceeding normally and the cosmonauts are feeling well.
