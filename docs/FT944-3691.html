941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / Company News (This Week): All eyes on Trafalgar after stormy voyage - Other Companies The City will pay close attention to the core engineering activities of Trafalgar House, the UK-based group, whose interests range from property and construction to the Ritz Hotel and Cunard liners. Analysts forecast full-year profits of between Pounds 50m and Pounds 55m, after a Pounds 16m restructuring charge, against losses last time of Pounds 347.2m after Pounds 397m exceptional costs. They also expect a 1p dividend when the company reports on Thursday. The results will also be the first public outing for the new chief executive, Mr Nigel Rich.
