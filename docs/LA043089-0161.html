April 30, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

AGRICULTURE CHIEF'S MOTHER DIES OF NATURAL CAUSES

Laura P. Yeutter, the mother of Agriculture Secretary Clayton K. Yeutter, has died at her home from natural causes, a Berryman Funeral Home spokesman said Saturday. She was 92. The spokesman said Yeutter was found late Friday by authorities notified by family and friends unable to reach her by telephone. The spokesman said Yeutter died Thursday.
