Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, Jan. 3, IRNA -- Issues related to registration of Iranian nationals residing in Qatar were discussed in a meeting between Qatari Interior Minister Shaykh 'Abdallah Bin-Khalifah Al Thani and Iran's Deputy Interior Minister 'Ali Akbar Rahmani Sunday [2 January]. Rahmani who is also head of the Civil Registration Organisation arrived here from Kuwait. He is presently on a tour of the Persian Gulf littoral states to examine the issues related to Iranians residing in these countries. Meanwhile, Iran's Ambassador to Doha Seyyed Baqer Safa'i, in a meeting with the special advisor to the amir of Qatar, Shaykh Mohammad Bin-Hamad Al Thani Sunday handed over a message of head of Iran's Mostazfin (oppressed) Foundation Mohsen Rafiqdust. The message dealt with mutual ties and ways to expand Tehran-Doha relations, Rafiqdust invited the advisor to visit Tehran which was accepted.
