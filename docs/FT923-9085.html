920807 FT 07 AUG 92 / Letter: No changes seen in Italy Sir, Robert Graham continues to show stubborn optimism ('Amato plays the chaos card', August 4) that the recent election results in Italy will lead to substantive changes in the system. The sad reality, however, is that not only do the same old faces continue to be seen - but they are up to the same old tricks. Wake up Italians] You do not have very much time. Onesimo Alvarez-Moro, Pontejos 2, 2 puerta 6, 28012 Madrid, Spain
