930921 FT 21 SEP 93 / Pounds 3.2bn sales seen for warehouse clubs WAREHOUSE CLUBS, huge sheds which sell consumer goods at discount prices, could achieve sales of Pounds 3.2bn in the UK - equivalent to 2 per cent of retail sales - according to a report by Verdict, the retail research group. The clubs have been the fastest-growing sector in US retailing. Verdict has located 50 sites for clubs in the UK
