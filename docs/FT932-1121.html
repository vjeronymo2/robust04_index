930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / Tourist offices, airlines and banks hit GERMANY: Munich: Eight gunmen took 21 hostages, later freeing them and surrendering. Demonstrators smashed up Turkish tourism offices, airlines, banks in Berlin, Hamburg, Hanover, Dortmund, Essen, Munster, Hurth near Cologne, Bonn, Frankfurt, Stuttgart, Bremen, Dusseldorf, Mainz, Duisburg, Gelsenkirchen, Karlsruhe, Ulm, Mannheim. FRANCE: Marseilles: About 10 hostages taken in Turkish consulate and released after 3-hour siege. Lyons: Attack by demonstrators. SWITZERLAND: Berne: Demonstrator killed, eight hurt in gunfire between about 60 Kurdish protesters and Turkish embassy staff. Zurich: 78 Kurds attacked consulate. Five attacked Turkish bank and travel agency. Geneva: Fifteen demonstrators invaded and ransacked Turkish consulate. DENMARK: Copenhagen: Windows of Turkish Airlines office smashed. SWEDEN: Stockholm: Tourist office ransacked by five Kurds. BRITAIN: London: Turkish bank invaded and damaged.
