940826 FT 26 AUG 94 / International Company News: Canada's investment firms in reverse Second-quarter profits of Canada's 154 investment firms dropped by half to CDollars 115m (USDollars 83.3m) from the first quarter, reflecting declining stock and bond markets and slower capital market activity, writes Robert Gibbens. The first quarter had set a peak profit of CDollars 238m. First-half profits were CDollars 353m against CDollars 360m a year earlier. Return on equity fell to 30 per cent from 43 per cent. The decline in second-quarter revenues was spread across the industry, said the Investment Dealers Association of Canada.
