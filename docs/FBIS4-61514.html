BFN [Text] The situation on the Russian-Mongolian border on the Tuva Republic sector was discussed in Kyzyl at a meeting of representatives of the border guards, of the bodies of law and order and customs, and heads of the state structures of Russia and Mongolia. The participants in the talks agreed that the situation on the border between the two states had become alarming of late. Border violations have become increasingly frequent. Cattle rustling has become a real calamity for (?natives) who live in the areas along the border. Smuggling is acquiring menacing proportions. It intended to sign a protocol today on the results of the talks, which should initiate the drawing up of a draft document on the settling of cross-border issues.
