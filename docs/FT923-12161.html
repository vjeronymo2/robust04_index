920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Seeking a shelter in a difficult market Two companies involved in the development of sheltered housing have agreed to merge. Secure Retirement has made an offer worth Pounds 5.5m for Pegasus Holdings and has received irrevocable acceptances for nearly 81 per cent of the capital. On completion, Hart Ventures, which is a significant shareholder in Pegasus, will subscribe for 1.9m new shares in Secure costing Pounds 1.25m. Full acceptance of the offer and subscription would mean the issue of 10m shares. In the year ended March 31 Secure cut its pre-tax loss from Pounds 4.38m to Pounds 1.4m. Pegasus made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 820,000 in 1991 (Pounds 670,000). Net assets came to Pounds 3.49m.
