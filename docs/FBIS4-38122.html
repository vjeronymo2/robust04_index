BFN [Text] New York, June 28, INA -- Iraq has protested to the United Nations over continued violation of its airspace by the U-2 spy aircraft under pretext of carrying out aerial survey for the UN Special Commission. In a message to UN Chief Dr Butrus Ghali, Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf said that the U-2 spy aircraft violated the Iraqi airspace four times over May 30 ending June 23. [sentence as received] The minister said that this aircraft is used for purposes contrary to those underlined by the Security Council and its activities prejudice Iraq's sovereignty and security and intercept goals and principles of the United Nations in respecting equity and sovereignty of all member states. He called upon the United Nations to immediately reconsider the use of this aircraft.
