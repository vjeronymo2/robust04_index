940107 FT 07 JAN 94 / International Company News: Beneteau Beneteau, the French pleasure boat company, fell further into the red in its last financial year to August 31 when it made a net loss of FFr20.7m (Dollars 3.5m), against a deficit of FFr4.6m in the previous year. The company, controlled by the Beneteau family, has been badly affected by the European economic recession on the pleasure boat market.
