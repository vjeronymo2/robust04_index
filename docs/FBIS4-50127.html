BFN [Text] Beijing, May 14 (XINHUA) -- Qiao Shi, chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress (NPC), met here this afternoon with a delegation of the State Duma from the Russian Federation headed by Chairman Ivan Rybkin. Extending a warm welcome to Rybkin and his party on their visit to China, Qiao said that China treasures its good-neighborly relations with Russia and wishes such relations will be further developed. The chairman said that it is China's consistent policy to develop friendship with all countries in the world including Russia on the basis of the five principles of peaceful co-existence. Qiao said that the friendship and cooperation between China and Russia has seen a fresh development over recent years, and he hopes that the two countries would continue to strengthen such relations for they share common interests and the prospects for their cooperation are promising. During the meeting, Qiao also briefed the guests on China's economic development. Rybkin said that Russia is conducting reforms and is interested in learning Chinese experience in this regard. Apart from Beijing, the delegation, which arrived in Beijing this morning at the invitation of the NPC Standing Committee, will also visit Shanghai and Shenzhen during their 5-day stay in China.
