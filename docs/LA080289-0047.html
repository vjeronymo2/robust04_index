August 2, 1989, Wednesday, Southland Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"It would have seemed harmless: another young kid who's star-struck. We have tons of those. Lots are lovable; some are crazy. But who's to know?"

-- Mimi Weber, vice president of the production company for "My Sister Sam," upon learning that Robert John Bardo, accused of killing actress Rebecca Schaeffer, appeared at the studio where she was taping a television show two years ago and begged to see her.
