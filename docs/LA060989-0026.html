June 9, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Kasler Corp.: The San Bernardino contractor reported second-quarter income of $28,000, compared to $662,000 from a year ago on revenue of $30.8 million. Revenue rose 14.5%. Company officials said the results reflected problems with certain subcontractors' performances. The firm builds highways, airport runways and other public works projects.
