February 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

WIFE KILLS HUSBAND, SON, THEN HERSELF, POLICE SAY

A woman shot and killed her husband and her 10-year-old son Saturday and then turned the gun on herself, police said.

The victims of the shootings, which occurred about 6 p.m. in the family home in the 1800 block of South Monterey Street, were not immediately identified, Sgt. Andrew Harvey said.

Police were called to the house in the middle-class neighborhood by a report that three bodies were inside.
