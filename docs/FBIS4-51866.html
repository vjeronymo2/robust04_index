BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 3 (KCNA) -- The foreign languages publishing house brought out the book "President Kim Il-song" in foreign languages. Carried in it are the impressions of well-known political and public figures of Japan who had the audience of the great leader President Kim Il-song while visiting Korea. The book contains a part of the two-volume "President Kim Il-song" which was presented to him as a gift on his 80th birthday on April 15, 1992. Carried on the first page of the book is a photograph of the great leader President Kim Il-song. The book gives more than 40 articles including "The Most Distinguished People's Leader" by Tomomi Narita, ex-chairman of the Japan Socialist Party, "The Man Who Moves Asia and the World" by Takeo Takagi, ex-director of the Society for Japan-Korea Cultural Exchange and editorial writer of YOMIURI SHIMBUN, and "The Korean People Who Receive the Guidance of Great President Kim Il-song is Really Happy" by Tsunehiko Antaku, former member of the House of Representatives.
