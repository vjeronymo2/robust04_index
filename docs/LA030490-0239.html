March 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

AN HOUR SAVED

I am writing CBS in addition to you to let everyone know that I will be boycotting "60 Minutes" for the next three months, along with the rest of my family, due to its ridiculous suspension of Andy Rooney (the best part of the show). Should CBS lift its "ban" on Rooney, I'll be glad to tune right back in.

B.L. Isaacs, Whittier
