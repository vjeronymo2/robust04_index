November 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

I watch Keith Olbermann (KCBS) because I will see something I won't see anywhere else -- humor.

His work is original, he has a clever play on words and sometimes he's just plain silly.

More than this, he places events in historical perspective with a knowledge of tradition and an obvious love of the games.

And since in all this there is a recognition that the viewer might actually have some intelligence and awareness of sports and something beyond, I will continue to watch Keith Olbermann.

Lulu Lewis, North Hollywood
