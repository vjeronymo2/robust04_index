940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / UK Company News: TLS Range Strong performances from both its short term and contract hire divisions enabled TLS Range, the USM-quoted vehicle rental group, to increase pre-tax profits from Pounds 34,000 to Pounds 720,000 for the first half of 1994. Turnover was more than doubled at Pounds 10.7m (Pounds 5.03m). Earnings per share came out at 1.5p (nil) and there is an interim dividend of 0.5p.
