920821 FT 21 AUG 92 / French stock market warns polls France's stock market authorities yesterday warned opinion pollsters about allowing details of their polls on next month's Maastricht treaty referendum to be leaked, writes Alice Rawsthorn. The warning follows days of volatile trading on the stock and bond markets reacting to rumours of poll results. The markets fell sharply on Wednesday morning when results of two polls were leaked indicating a fall in support for Maastricht.
