930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / British Horseracing Board holds first meeting Lord Hartington, Jockey Club senior steward (centre), yesterday opened the first meeting of the British Horseracing Board, which has taken over most of the club's functions in monitoring the sport. Lord Hartington, board chairman, was accompanied by Tristram Ricketts, chief executive (left), and Sir Nevil Macready, deputy chairman
