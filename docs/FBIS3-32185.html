Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Given the voluntary abstentions by President Firmin Jean-Louis and Vice President Serge Joseph, the ad hoc office of the Senate, formed in compliance with the Constitution and the internal rules of the Senate, hastens to inform the concerned institutions and the entire Haitian nation that since Tuesday, 11 January, he has been holding regular sessions at the Legislative Palace to take care of the business of the Republic during these grave, difficult times for the Haitian people. Consequently, any decisions and parliamentary actions resulting from these deliberations, duly signed by the first secretary, Senator Ebrane Cadet, will bear the seal of the Senate of the Republic for full and complete effect, states the press release signed by Ebrane Cadet.
