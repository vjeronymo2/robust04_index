December 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $70 when Toby Jug, his $40 win bet, ran third in the sixth race and Colway Rally, his $30 win bet in the Bay Meadows Handicap that was simulcast, finished second. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $875.30.

For today, Bob's bet is a $20 win parlay from Lark's Legacy in the first race to Star Cutter in the fifth.

Saturday's Bankroll: $945.30

Saturday's Loss: $70.00

Current Bankroll: $875.30
