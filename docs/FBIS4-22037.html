BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpts] Alpaslan Pehlivanli, Motherland Party [ANAP] Central Decision-Making and Executive Council member and former Ankara deputy, has been killed in an armed attack near Kirikkale's Keskin District. According to information obtained by a Turkish Radio and Television correspondent from Kirikkale Governor Sahabettin Harput, three shots were fired from the passing car at Pehlivanli who was driving from Keskin to Kirikkale. Pehlivanli was seriously injured in the head and he died on the way to the hospital. Three other persons who were in the car with him did not sustain any injuries in the attack. It is reported that the car from which the shots were fired had a fake license plate and that there were four persons in it. Operations are continuing to capture the assailants. [passage omitted on Pehlivanli's curriculum vitae] ANAP leader Mesut Yilmaz went to Kirikkale following the incident. He went to the hospital where Pehlivanli was taken and was briefed on the incident by Harput and other officials. In a statement at the hospital, Yilmaz said that there is a high possibility that it is a political murder and that the authorities concerned are continuing the investigation. Yilmaz said: The incident is very distressing for us. We are heartbroken. I extend my condolences to the people of Kirikkale and ANAP circles.
