920123 FT 23 JAN 92 / Brussels suspends aid to Zaire The European Community said yesterday it was suspending all aid to Zaire except for emergency humanitarian aid in protest at the suspension of a national conference to guide the country to democracy, Reuter reports from Brussels. It said the decision of Mr Nguza Karl-I-Bond, the prime minister, to suspend the conference of government and opposition parties on Sunday 'puts into question the possibilities of a democratic evolution in Zaire based on consensus.'
