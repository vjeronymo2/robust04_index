930118 FT 18 JAN 93 / World News In Brief: Weather wreaks havoc in Scotland Scores of families were evacuated, dozens of communities cut off and thousands left without electricity in Scotland as floods and gales continued to cause chaos on land and hurricane-force storms lashed the North Sea. Worst affected areas were Perthshire and central Scotland. Weather forecast, Page 12 Off the coast of North Wales, more than 20 people were taken to hospital after the ferry Stena Hibernia was hit by a freak wave.
