940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / Business reports improvements Increases in order books and sales led to higher profits among medium-sized businesses over the second half of last year, according to a survey of Lloyds Bank commercial service customers. Responses from almost 2,000 business customers in November and December showed 45 per cent reporting higher order books, and 35 per cent reporting higher profits. The survey, conducted every six months, covers companies with annual sales between Pounds 1m and Pounds 100m.
