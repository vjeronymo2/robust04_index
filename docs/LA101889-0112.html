October 18, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Eric Dickerson intends to fulfill his contract with the Indianapolis Colts but says he may retire when it expires after the 1990 season.

"Football is a sport that takes a lot out of you mentally and physically," Dickerson said on a television talk show. "After this season, I have one year left. I'll play that for sure. But even if they offered me a new contract, I don't think I'd sign it right now."
