BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- FRG Defense Minister Volker Ruehe (Christian Democratic Union) has welcomed Russia's joining the NATO Partnership for Peace program. In Bonn, the minister spoke of a "historical milestone in a process, which gives Europe more stability and thus safeguards peace." With this, NATO has very quickly taken up the proposals presented by Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev in Brussels on 24 May and turned them into a future-oriented project. The NATO program is proving to be a flexible mechanism, which permits living up to the participants' individual interests in the spirit of the common whole.
