Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 2 Mar (MENA)--A responsible security source at the Interior Ministry said today that at approximately 2015 last night, three unidentified persons opened fire on and threw an explosive device at the Bank Misr branch in the city of Abu Tij, Asyut Governorate. The security source added that the bank's guard returned fire, forcing the gunmen to flee. The source pointed out that there were no casualties. Property damage was confined to the bank's windows, which were smashed. [passage omitted]
