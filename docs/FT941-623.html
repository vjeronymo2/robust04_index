940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Global Profits of Global Group, the USM-traded meat and shipping services concern, improved from Pounds 1.4m to Pounds 1.9m pre-tax for the year to end-December. Turnover rose by Pounds 6.2m to Pounds 81.2m. Earnings amounted to 1.1p (0.83p) and a final dividend of 0.3p makes a same-again 0.5p total.
