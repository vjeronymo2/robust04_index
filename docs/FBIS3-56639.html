Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Gennadiy Dolgachev, head of the press center of the Russian troops in Georgia, has said that units of the Russian army in Transcaucasia will be used as a peace-keeping force in the zone of the Georgian-Abkhaz conflict. He said that everything is ready to start this measure. The agreement to deploy a peace-keeping force in the conflict zone was reached at talks between Georgian and Abkhaz delegations in Geneva. This force will supervise the return of the refugees to Abkhazia, whose number, as you know, exceeds 200,000. The return of the refugees is due to start on 10 February.
