Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 26 -- Vice President Mrs. Nguyen Thi Binh received here this afternoon Mrs. Simone Veil, secretary of state and minister of public health, social and urban affairs of France, now on an official visit to Vietnam. Present at the reception were Minister of Public Health Prof. Nguyen Trong Nhan, and French Ambassador to Vietnam J. Francois Nougarede. During the reception the Vietnamese leader welcomed Mrs. Simone Veil's visit to Vietnam at the time when the friendship and cooperation between the two countries were being developed and broadened in various fields, particularly economic, finance, public health, social affairs and culture. For her part, Mrs Veil expressed her belief that her visit would made favourable conditions for promoting cooperation and exchanging experiences in the fields of common interest. Mrs. Simone Veil arrived here this morning at the invitation of Deputy Prime Minister Nguyen Khanh, who greeted her upon arrival at government guest house. Mr. Khanh welcomed her visit, and said he hoped that it would be successful and contribute to the development of the friendship and cooperation between the two countries.
