940303 FT 03 MAR 94 / Indonesia trade surplus up 29.5% Indonesia's trade surplus rose by 29.5 per cent to Dollars 8.66bn (Pounds 5.8bn) in 1993 from Dollars 6.69bn a year earlier, the trade ministry said, Reuter reports from Jakarta. Total exports for 1993 rose 8.4 per cent to Dollars 36.82bn from Dollars 33.97bn in 1992. Imports rose to Dollars 28.16bn from Dollars 27.28bn. Non-oil exports increased by 16.2 per cent to Dollars 27.07bn from Dollars 23.3bn. Economists say Indonesia has benefited from a shift towards higher investment in manufacturing since the oil price fell in 1986.
