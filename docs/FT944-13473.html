941027 FT 27 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Secs Tst Scotland Net asset value per share at Securities Trust of Scotland slipped to 85.6p in the six months to September 30, compared with 89.2p last time, and 90.1p at March 31 1994. The trust, managed by Martin Currie, had net revenue of Pounds 5.07m (Pounds 5.48m). The tax charge of Pounds 1.66m included provision for a Pounds 293,500 advance corporation tax write-off for the interim period. Earnings per share came out at 1.57p, compared with 1.7p, with the interim dividend unchanged at 1.08p.
