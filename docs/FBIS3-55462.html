Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, January 8 (SNARK)--The unexpected quietness exists at the Karabakh front from the last night. The military experts are of the opinion that quietness is caused by the new Azerbaijani detachments movements into the zone of military clashes. Meanwhile, Press-Service of the Defense Army told yesterday evening the Azerbaijani detachment managed to capture some frontiers and hills at the Iranian border. At the Martuni and Mardakert directions the Karabakh forces met success, Azeris had losses: 80 Azerbaijani soldiers and officers, 2 tanks and a military machine.
