BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 12 (KCNA) -- A seminar of literary men and artistes was held here on the lapse of 30 years since the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il began to work at the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea. Speakers said Korea has met a great heyday of literature and art thanks to the chuche-based literary and art thought and energetic guidance of the dear leader. They noted that with a revolutionary change in film, opera, drama and all other domains of literature and art, immortal classical masterpieces were adapted in various art forms and the theoretical and practical problems arising in creation and representation including the portrayal of the leader in literary and art works were successfully solved. Noting that the Korean writers and artistes have grown up to be ranks of loyalty unfailingly faithful to the party and the leader, the speakers said it is one of big achievements in the efforts to model literature and art on the chuche idea. Referring to the feats of the dear leader who established a regular system of guidance over the party life in the domain of literature and art, they stressed that it was a precious result of the new system's superiority and vitality that writers and artistes remain boundlessly loyal and devoted to the party and the leader.
