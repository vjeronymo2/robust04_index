920402 FT 02 APR 92 / UK Company News: Anglo Pacific reduces deficit Anglo Pacific Resources cut its loss from Pounds 1.67m to Pounds 352,000 in 1991, after exceptional write offs and charges totalling Pounds 431,000, against Pounds 1.43m. The interest in the Kurara Gold Mine was sold in the second half and that accounted for a fall in turnover from Pounds 4.54m to Pounds 2.16m. Consistent with the strategy to concentrate on industrial mineral activities in the UK and Europe, the 43 per cent interest in Menzies Gold was sold. Losses per share came to 0.45p (2.16p).
