BFN [Text] Macao, May 16 (XINHUA) -- Infrastructural construction of Lin Seng Industrial Development Zone on Macao's Coloane Island is going to be started at the end of this month and is expected to be completed by next May. According to an official with the project, the industrial zone covers an area of 337,000 sq m, of which 120,000 sq m are for industrial development, 110,000 sq m for construction of roads, some 8,000 sq m for grassland, 30,000 sq m for public facilities and the rest for commercial and residential use. Costing an estimated 100 million Macao patacas (13 million U.S. dollars), the infrastructural construction mainly covers water routes, roads, afforestation, running water facilities, power and telecommunications network. The official said, at present, enterprises from the Zhongshan harbor high-tech development zone, Guangdong Province, have handed over six project letters to Macao government for approval. These projects cover electronics, machinery, battery-making and others. Macao government stipulated that construction terms for all projects in the industrial zone would end before 1999, the official said. Approved by Macao government in 1975, the industrial zone is expected to boost the multi-facet and high-tech industrial development in Macao.
