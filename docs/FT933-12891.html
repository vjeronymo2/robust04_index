930722 FT 22 JUL 93 / Parliament and Politics: Maastricht's progress February 7 1992: Treaty signed May 21 1992: European Communities (Amendment) bill secures second Commons reading with majority of 244 June 2 1992: Danes reject treaty in referendum September 16 1992: Sterling suspended from ERM November 4 1992: Government wins Maastricht paving motion by three votes December 1 1992: Commons committee stage of detailed line-by-line consideration begins April 22 1993: Commons rejects holding a British referendum by a majority of 239 votes April 22 1993: Committee stage completed after 23 days and 163 hours of debate May 18 1993: Danes back treaty in second referendum May 20 1993: Bill secures Commons third reading with majority of 180 July 14 1993: Lords rejects British referendum by a majority of 269 votes. Lady Thatcher defies party three-line whip for first time in 34 years in parliament July 19 1993: Lord Rees-Mogg wins High Court permission to mount legal challenge to ratification July 20 1993: Bill secures Lords third reading by majority of 112 and receives royal assent July 22 1993: Commons and Lords debate the social chapter, bringing to 74 the number of Maastricht-related votes in the two houses
