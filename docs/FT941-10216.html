940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Warrants and Value shows 65% growth Warrants and Value Investment Trust raised its net asset value per share by 65 per cent from its launch last March to the end of 1993. The shares were issued at 25p and after expenses, initial asset value was 24.18p, which had increased to 39.97p by the year-end. The advance compared with a total return of 34.2 per cent on the FT-SE-A Investment Trust index. Net revenue for the period was Pounds 34,000 and earnings per share came to 0.04p. As stated at the time of the listing, the company does not intend to pay a dividend.
