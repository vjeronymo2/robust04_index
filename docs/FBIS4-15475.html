BFN [Text] Moscow, 7 Apr (TANJUG) -- A Yugoslav Assembly delegation led by Radoman Bozovic, the president of the Chamber of Citizens, arrived in Moscow this afternoon at the invitation of Ivan Rybkin, the president of the Russian parliaments's lower house. The delegation is to have talks at the Russian parliament with Ivan Rybkin, with leaders of some parties, and other Russian officials. Parliamentarian and other forms of cooperation will be discussed during the talks, as well as the situation in the former Yugoslavia and ways of resolving it. It is expected that initiatives announced by the Russian parliament in connection with the lifting of sanctions against Yugoslavia will also be discussed. This is the first visit by a Yugoslav parliamentary delegation since the sanctions were introduced.
