920520 FT 20 MAY 92 / International Company News: Dayton Hudson profits static DAYTON HUDSON, one of the large US retailing groups, yesterday reported static profits in the three months to May 2, at Dollars 35m, against Dollars 34m, writes Nikki Tait. Sales, however, rose from Dollars 3.35bn to Dollars 3.72bn, and - assisted by a relatively upbeat statement from Dayton's chairman, Mr Kenneth Macke - the shares added Dollars 3 1/4 to Dollars 62 1/4 . Target, Dayton's discount chain, performed best. Same-store sales rose by 6 per cent and total sales increased from Dollars 1.84bn to Dollars 2.15bn. Target's operating profits also improved.
