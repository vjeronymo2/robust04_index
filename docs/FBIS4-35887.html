BFN [Excerpt] A joint communique issued by Mr. Driss Basri, minister of state for the interior and information, and Mr. Erik Jensen, the UN representative's special assistant and head of the voter identification commission, which was published today in Rabat, announced that the operation to determine the identity of Saharans involved in the referendum will begin on 8 June 1994 in Laayoune. The joint communique says: After a working session held on 30 May, the minister of interior and information and the chairman of the voter identification commission bring to the notice of all Saharans involved in the referendum that the operation to determine their identity will begin on 8 June 1994 in Laayoune. The regulations will be displayed at the headquarters of the municipality and its annex as well as at the headquarters of the UN Mission for the Referendum in Western Sahara, as soon as they have been drafted.
