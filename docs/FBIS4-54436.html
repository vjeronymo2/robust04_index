BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES, 29/4/1994 -- "Romania's security and defense in the context of its integration within European and Euro-Atlantic bodies with a tradition in the field of security" is the theme of a scientific seminar organized over 27-29 April by the National Defense Collegium. The seminar sets itself through the approached issues to debate Romania's position in Central and Eastern Europe within the framework of European security, the defense strategy in the context of Romania's European and Euro-Atlantic integration, potential implications due to the Russian Federation's adherence to the European security system. The 29 April proceedings of the seminar will be attended by Romania's Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu, by deputies and other Romanian politicians.
