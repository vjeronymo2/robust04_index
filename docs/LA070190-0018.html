July 1, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

HORMONE UNBOUNDED

After reading Peter Rainer's June 24 article on movie violence and viewing the summer lineup of films (all male-in-dire-jeopardy plots), I figured maybe a good title for the whole mess would be "Testosterone Wars," because I suspect a large amount of this hormone has been dumped into Hollywood's reservoirs.

I wondered, where are all the blockbusters about women (who are not whores or psychos)?

Then it came to me, BANG! like a slow-motion, blood-spurting head shot:

Crash + thrash + mash + slash + bash + gash + trash = Cash!

SHARON BAKER

Homeland, Calif.
