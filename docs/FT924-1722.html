921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / UK Company News: US expansion for Bemrose Bemrose Corporation, the security and promotional printer, is expanding in the US through an interest in Lite-R-Line, distributor of nail clippers, pen knives, and emery boards as promotional gifts. Bemrose Yattendon, the 50 per cent owned US associate, has paid Dollars 1.45m (Pounds 928,000) for the assets and goodwill of Lite-R-Line from London International Group. Sales for 1992 will approach Dollars 2m. For 1991, Bemrose Yattendon contributed Pounds 957,000 to group profits, a rise of 67 per cent. In the first half of this year profits were a further 41 per cent ahead of 1991.
