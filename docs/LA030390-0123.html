March 3, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MARSHA WARFIELD TO HOST NEW NBC DAYTIME SHOW

"Night Court" co-star Marsha Warfield will begin double duty as host of a weekday, half-hour NBC talk show to debut March 26.

"The Marsha Warfield Show" will replace "Scrabble," which will have its final telecast March 23.

A spokeswoman at KNBC Channel 4 said that her station has not yet found a time slot for "The Marsha Warfield Show."

"Scrabble," which airs at 10 a.m. on most NBC stations, is shown at 3 a.m. by KNBC. "House Party," a syndicated hourlong show, is currently seen from 10-11 a.m. on KNBC. STEVEN HERBERT
