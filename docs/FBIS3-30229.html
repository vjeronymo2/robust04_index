Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "The Balance of the Clearing Account Just Like at the End of Last Year"] [Text] Prague--The overall balance of the clearing account for trade with Slovakia has reached 5.7 billion korunas or approximately 170 million ECU's, after Thursday [27 January], which is 40 million ECU's over the margin limit. The old lot surplus amounts to 800 million korunas, and the new lot 4.9 billion in favor of the Czech Republic. Last year, at the end of December, when the margin limit was surpassed for the first time, the amounts reached 1.611 billion and 4.106 billion, respectively. The National Bank of the Slovak Republic covered the amount over the allowed margin before the deadline on 10 January.
