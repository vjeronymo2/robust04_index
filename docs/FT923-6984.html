920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / Merger activity spending falls SPENDING on UK merger and acquisition activity declined to Pounds 1.8bn in the second quarter of this year, compared with Pounds 2.1bn in the first quarter and Pounds 1.5bn in the second quarter last year, according to the Central Statistical Office. There were 104 transactions, including 21 valued at more than Pounds 10m. The largest transaction was valued at Pounds 534m.
