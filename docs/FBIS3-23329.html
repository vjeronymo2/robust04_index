Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Kocaeli, Mar. 9 (A.A.) -- Security teams caught 12 separatist terrorists who were preparing to sabotage major industrial plants, including the oil refinery Tupras. The Anatolian News Agency learned that anti-terror teams carried out operations in Izmit and Golcuk, taking 12 people into custody, including an official of the terror organization. The 12 terrorists were determinedly involved in a recent terrorist raid in Tuzla train station, which killed five reserve officers, and various other terror incidents, including the killing of four people in Istanbul and extortion.
