August 4, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

THE MESSAGE OF 'SEALS'

To see a film like "Navy SEALS" defended by someone proclaiming to be a radical feminist is dizzying. What is the message Feigen brings to our youth? That it's OK to maim another human being as long as you're able to spend as much time on your hair as Charlie Sheen does?

ROBERT HANSON

Burbank
