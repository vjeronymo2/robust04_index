Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Serbian Deputy Foreign Minister Zivadin Jovanovic, who is currently on an official visit in Ankara, was received by Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin. Cetin conferred with the visiting deputy minister. In reply to reporters' questions upon arrival at the Foreign Ministry, Cetin declared that the NATO ultimatum has attained its goal and the security of the 20-km radius area surrounding Sarajevo has been guaranteed. Answering another question, Cetin said that the Serbian siege around other towns in Bosnia-Herzegovina must also be lifted and Turkey is continuing its initiatives in various capitals and with NATO in this regard.
