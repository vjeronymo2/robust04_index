Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Oleg Velichko] [Text] Moscow January 11 TASS -- Everything seems to indicate that First Vice-Premier Oleg Soskovets will remain in a new government and retain his job, according to reliable sources in the government. Soskovets directly participated in preparing a presidential decree on the structure of federal executive power bodies, the sources said. Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin and Oleg Soskovets had a meeting on Monday, reaching understanding on government reforming matters.
