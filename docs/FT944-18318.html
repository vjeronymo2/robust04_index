941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Chez Gerard ahead Groupe Chez Gerard, the restaurant operator, reported pre-tax profits up 74 per cent to Pounds 1.67m for the year to June 26, Pounds 50,000 ahead of the forecast made when it came to the market in March. Turnover rose 10 per cent to Pounds 10.2m, against Pounds 9.27m when the pre-tax profit was Pounds 959,000. The company said costs continued to kept under control andoperating margins had increased to 16.4 per cent. The result was also helped by a fall in net interest charges from Pounds 206,000 to Pounds 42,000. During the year capital spending was Pounds 682,000 of which Pounds 380,000 related to the acquisition of Scotts Restaurant. Earnings per share were 7.28p (4.84p).
