BFN [Text] Cairo, 21 Jun (MENA) -- 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani, member of North Yemen's Presidential Council, left Cairo for Damascus this morning after a two-day visit to Cairo. During the visit, he conveyed to President Husni Mubarak a message from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih on the current situation in the region and bilateral relations.
