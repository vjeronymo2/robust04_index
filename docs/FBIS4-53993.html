BFN [Text] The crisis between the Philippines and Indonesia due to the upcoming conference on East Timor is still not over. A high-level meeting between Filipino and Indonesian officials was canceled earlier today. President Fidel Ramos was scheduled to meet Indonesian Industry Minister Tunki Ariwibowo and the Indonesian ambassador. However, the Indonesian Embassy revealed that Minister Ariwibowo is still in the United States, and the meeting was rescheduled. Meanwhile, Malacanang [presidential office] officials admitted that the incident has something to do with the East Timor crisis.
