931111 FT 11 NOV 93 / Australian deficit warning MR Ralph Willis, Australian finance minister, warned yesterday the federal government may have to introduce additional revenue-raising measures to meet its budget deficit forecasts, although he added this would be done with reluctance, Nikki Tait reports from Sydney. 'It will require constant government vigilance to keep the deficit under control,' he said in a speech to the Economic Society of Australia. 'It may also involve some revenue increases.'
