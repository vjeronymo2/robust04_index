August 9, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

REDEVELOPMENT PLANS FOR WATTS

As a resident of South Los Angeles, I am concerned about the Watts redevelopment plan.

They shouldn't be doing this, because there might be a lot of people who don't want to move.

I think that the main thing that should be done to improve this community is for guys and girls to stop being in gangs. I think the people should get together and clean the dirty places. I think the other solution is for drug dealers to stop dealing drugs.

CLAUDIA MAZARIEGOS

Locke High School Student

Los Angeles
