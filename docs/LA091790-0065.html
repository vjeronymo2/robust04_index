September 17, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: MISSION VIEJO;

GENERAL PLAN REPORT AVAILABLE TO PUBLIC

The public will have a last chance Tuesday to comment on the environmental impact report dealing with the city's general plan.

With just weeks left before the deadline for submitting the plan to the state, the Planning Commission will hear final public comments on the report at City Hall at 7 p.m.

Residents wanting to review the document can examine a copy at City Hall, the Oso Viejo Community Center or the county library in Mission Viejo.
