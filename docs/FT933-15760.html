930707 FT 07 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Edmond shares fall on profit warning Shares in Edmond Holdings, the housebuilder, fell 4 1/2 p to 15p after the company warned that profits for the year were unlikely to match current market expectations. Trading in the first half had been in line with expectations, in conditions which had continued to be difficult. However, despite signs of recovery in some geographical locations, the areas in which Edmond operates - Humberside, East Anglia and south Wales - had not experienced any positive effects and lower volumes and weaker prices had resulted. The board did not feel it prudent to anticipate an improvement in trading conditions in the near future. In 1992 the company incurred pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.15m (Pounds 1.73m profits).
