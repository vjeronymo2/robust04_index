921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / Iranian troops in Bosnia offer Iran's armed forces have said they are ready to help Bosnian Moslems in their war with Serb forces opposed to Bosnian independence, Reuter reports from Nicosia. The armed forces command said in a statement on Wednesday night it would not 'deny Moslem sisters and brothers in Bosnia-Herzegovina any kind of material or moral assistance'. The statement was broadcast on Iranian radio.
