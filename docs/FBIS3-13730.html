Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] For the second time in less than 48 hours, forces of the Lebanese Army entered the central administrative headquarters of the Lebanese Forces Party in Zuq Mikha'il at 1200 today. Army personnel forced the administrative officials to leave the building and prevented anyone from entering it. Meanwhile, a Lebanese Army unit entered the students' building in Tabarja today. Our radio tried in vain to contact the party's central headquarters to inquire about the situation there.
