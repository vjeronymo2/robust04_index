BFN [Text] In a news conference, Brigadier General Luis Perez, chief of the Military Industries Union, UIM [Union de Industrias Militares], reported that this year the UIM will earmark 28 percent of its production for the national economy. Gen. Perez added that in 1993, the UIM allotted 25 percent of its production to the internal market and it intends to contribute 40 percent of its production in 1995. He explained that in the past, the UIM had only provided service to the Armed Forces, but that due to the current difficulties, it has decided to help the national economy. These industries manufacture carts for sugarcane transport, cutting tools, agricultural appliances, wheelbarrows, compressors, and other items in great demand.
