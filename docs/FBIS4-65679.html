BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- The Alliance 90/Greens again have more than 40,000 members. This is the result of an increase in members, which has continued since 1993, as the federal headquarters reported on Thursday [19 May]. With 36,320 members, the absolute low of the past few years was December 1992. In the first quarter of 1994, the party had 40,686 registered members -- 37,914 in the west and 2,772 in the east (without Berlin).
