930517 FT 17 MAY 93 / Higher demand lifts factory staffing CROSSLEE, the Halifax-based company best-known for its White Knight tumble dryers, is increasing its workforce by 100, or 20 per cent, to cope with rising demand. The privately owned company, created in a 1986 buy-out from Philips of the Netherlands, took on 60 workers last week. Another 40 will be added by August.
