940409 FT 09 APR 94 / Finance and the Family: What your endowment is worth - At a Glance Will your endowment pay off the mortgage? Securitised Endowment Contracts, a market-maker in traded endowment policies, is offering to supply the answer within 48 hours for Pounds 10.
