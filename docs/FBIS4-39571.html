BFN [INTERFAX report: "Vil Mirzayanov To Be Paid 30 Million Rubles In Compensation"] [Text] The Perovskiy district court in Moscow passed a decision yesterday under which the General Prosecutor's Office should pay Vil Mirzayanov 20 million rubles (R) for causing damage to his moral and physical state, and for illegal prosecution, while the Organic Chemistry Research Institute, where the scientist was earlier in charge of the department for counteracting foreign intelligence services, should pay him a further R10 million. The court rejected Vil Mirzayanov's suit against the Federal Counterintelligence Service [FCS] on the grounds that the FCS -- after the Security Ministry was abolished and primary investigation functions were transferred to the Prosecutor's Office -- is no successor to the Security Ministry. "This is the first case in which the court has defended the interests of a citizen who suffered from the state's high-handedness," said Aleksandr Asnis, the scientist's lawyer.
