Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 10 Jan (TANJUG) -- [Yugoslav] Federal Prime Minister Dr. Radoje Kontic received Konstantin Gorokostapulos [name as received], head of the EU observer mission for the former Yugoslavia, who took over this post on 1 January. During the meeting it was stressed that it is vital to do everything to end the conflicts in the former Bosnia-Herzegovina peacefully as soon as possible. It was pointed out that the EU mission could play a significant role in this connection, above all by interpreting events in the area of the former Yugoslavia objectively. To this end the Federal Government and federal bodies will offer all necessary assistance to the EU mission. Interest was expressed on both sides in the closest possible cooperation between the Federal Government and federal organs and the EU mission, the statement of the Federal Information Ministry says in conclusion.
