BFN [Text] Hong Kong, April 21 (XINHUA) -- The 28th meeting of the Sino-British Land Commission was held here today, according to a press release of the commission. The release said the two sides agreed that the land disposal program for the 1994/95 financial year should amount to 117.27 hectares. The program comprises 31.01 hectares for commercial, residential and industrial development, and 31.43 hectares for home ownership scheme, sandwich class housing scheme, private sector participation scheme, Hong Kong housing society development and village housing. It also includes 17.67 hectares for public utilities, educational, welfare, religious, recreational and other uses and 37.16 hectares for special requirements. Besides, the two sides also agreed to set aside five hectares of land in a supplementary land disposal program, the release added.
