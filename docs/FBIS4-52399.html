BFN [Text] Vietnam's prime minister, Vo Van Kiet, said his country is ready for the restoration of full diplomatic relations with the United States at any time. Speaking to Radio Australia's Hanoi correspondent, Christopher Kramer, Mr. Kiet said normalized relations would be in the interests of not only the two countries, but the entire region. He said full diplomatic ties were inevitable. Mr. Kiet committed his government to continuing political reforms, which, he said, paved the way for economic reforms. But asked if Vietnam was moving toward a multiparty democracy, Mr. Kiet said the question was never raised because the priority was to make the country rich and build a civilized society.
