BFN [Text] The Communist Party of the Philippines [CPP] has claimed responsibility for the assassination of former communist guerrilla commander Leopoldo Mabilangan, alias Comrade Hector. The Philippine News and Features interviewed another communist guerrilla chieftain who is still active in the movement about the Mabilangan assassination. Gregorio Rosal, alias Comrade Roger, spokesman of the CPP in southern Tagalog and Luzon, told the news organization that Mabilangan was ordered killed for his criminal and anti- revolutionary activities. Mabilangan had been charged of extortion and other criminal activities, including kidnapping for ransom. He was expelled from the CPP in February last year. Mabilangan and a bodyguard was shot dead in front of a parish church in Batangas on Easter Sunday [2 April]. Mabilangan was to act as principal sponsor in a wedding ceremony. Three other people, including a traffic policeman, were wounded.
