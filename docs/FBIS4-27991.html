BFN [Text] Tokyo, June 7 KYODO -- Trade Minister Eijiro Hata said Tuesday [7 Jun] that the Japanese economy has been showing some bright signals and predicted there is little chance of it sagging again. Hata, minister of international trade and industry, told a regular press conference after a cabinet meeting that he is paying especially close attention to consumer-spending-related economic indicators such as sales at department stores and supermarkets. Hata's remarks followed the latest official economic diagnosis issued by the Economic Planning Agency earlier in the day, which said some bright signs are visible in Japan's economy despite overall sluggishness.
