Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 29 Dec (XINHUA) -- Decree No. 18 of the President of the People's Republic of China: The "Decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress on the Interim Regulations on Value-Added Tax, Consumption Tax, and Sales Tax Applicable to Foreign-Invested Enterprises and Foreign-Owned Enterprises," adopted at the Fifth Session of the Eighth National People's Congress Standing Committee of the People's Republic of China on 29 December 1993, is hereby promulgated; the law comes into force upon promulgation. [Signed] Jiang Zemin, president of the People's Republic of China [Dated] 29 December 1993
