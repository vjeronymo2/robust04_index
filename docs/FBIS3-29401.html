Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Feb. 14 KYODO -- Farm Minister Eijiro Hata has named Toshihiko Tsuruoka, head of the Food Agency, as new vice minister to succeed outgoing Akio Kyoya, ministry officials said Monday [14 February]. Tsuruoka, 57, joined the Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries Ministry in 1959 after graduating from Tokyo University and has been director general of the Food Agency since July 1992. Hirofumi Ueno, 55, director of the minister's secretariat, will succeed Tsuruoka as the head of the Food Agency, the officials said. The government will approve the appointments February 15 and they will be effective the following day, they said.
