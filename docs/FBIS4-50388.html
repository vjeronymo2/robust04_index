BFN [Text] Bangkok, May 17 (XINHUA) -- Prime Minister of Thailand Chuan Likphai said here today that his government honors a policy of friendship towards China and reiterated Thailand's adherence to the "one China" stance. During a meeting with Chinese Ambassador Jin Guihua today at the Government House, Chuan said Thailand had always seen China as its closest friend and that he personally would make every effort to support and push the close ties forward. He recalled that his visit to China last year had helped develop bilateral ties in both political and other fields, and that contracts signed during the trip were being implemented in earnest and smoothly. The Chinese ambassador said on the occasion that China saw further development of bilateral economic and trade cooperation as a main pillar in ties of the two nations.
