ACTION: Notice of meetings.

SUMMARY: The Secretary of the Department of the Interior (Department) has established a Federal Gas Valuation Negotiated Rulemaking Committee (Committee) to develop specific recommendations with respect to Federal gas valuation pursuant to its responsibilities imposed by the Federal Oil and Gas Royalty Management Act of 1982, 30 U.S.C. 1701 et seq . (FOGRMA). The Department has determined that the establishment of this Committee is in the public interest and will assist the Agency in performing its duties under FOGRMA. DATES: The Committee will have meetings as shown below: Monday, October 24, 1994, 10:00 a.m.&hyph;5:00 p.m. Tuesday, October 25, 1994, 8:00 a.m.&hyph;5:00 p.m. Wednesday, October 26, 1994, 8:00 a.m.&hyph;12:00 p.m.
