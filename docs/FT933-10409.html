930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / UK Company News in Brief: RTZ RTZ: BZW Securities has invited bids for the shares it has agreed to acquire under its cash offer in respect of RTZ's enhanced interim scrip dividend alternative. The shares will have a value of Pounds 30.7m and BZW intends to sell them through a strike price auction, open to member firms of the Stock Exchange and to its own institutional clients.
