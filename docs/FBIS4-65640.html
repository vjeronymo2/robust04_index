BFN [Text] Nicosia, May 18 (CNA)--The Council of Ministers, headed by President Glavkos Kliridhis, decided Wednesday [18 May] the upgrade of the national committee for the prevention of the trafficking and use of drugs. This upgrade aims at the better flexibility of the national committee in taking and implementing its decisions, an official announcement said. Moreover, a ministerial committee will function within the scope of the national committee, aimed at shaping the policy of the Council of Ministers, monitoring the implementation of the policy and coordinating the whole work of prevention. The ministerial committee will be headed by the minister of health and include the ministers of justice and public order, education and culture, and labour and social insurance. The president of the Cyprus youth organisations will also be present. In addition, three scientific committees will be set up with the participation of scientists from both the private and public sectors, to deal with research, prevention, treatment and rehabilitation.
