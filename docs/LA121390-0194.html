December 13, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

BITTER PILL: Although there are many physicians who have come here from Mexico and elsewhere, Southern California's Spanish-speaking immigrants often lack adequate medical care. One problem? Just ask Dr. Alfonso Sandoval (E1). After seven years of medical school and two years of private practice in Mexico, he is still waiting to be licensed here. David Reyes and Steve Emmons
