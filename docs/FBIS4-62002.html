BFN [Text] Today, the Latvian Bureau for Monitoring the Withdrawal of the Russian Army presented a request to the headquarters of the Northwestern Group of Russian Forces to inform the bureau about the schedules for withdrawing from those Russian military sites that are not mentioned on the schedule for troop withdrawal presented by Russia. Ilgonis Upmalis, the head of the bureau, says the schedule provided by Russia does not include 13 Russian military sites in Latvia, nor 26 army units located on warships and submarines in Riga and Liepaja. Upmalis also requested the leaders of the Northwestern Group of Forces to show the numbers and addresses of the military units mentioned in the schedule, as well as providing lists of the military personnel at the army units and their family members. The schedule for troop withdrawal provided by Russia is an appendix to the treaty signed in Moscow on the withdrawal of Russian troops from Latvia.
