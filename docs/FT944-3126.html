941215 FT 15 DEC 94 / Perry seeks to soothe Russian fears on Nato expansion US defence secretary William Perry (pictured, centre, in Brussels with Nato secretary-general Willy Claes, left, and UK defence secretary Malcolm Rifkind) will try to reassure Russian officials about Nato's plans for expansion eastwards in a visit to Moscow starting today. Despite concerns about Nato and Chechnya, Mr Perry is in a mood to do business with the Yeltsin administration. 'We look at this relationship very pragmatically,' said a senior US official accompanying him. 'We have areas where we disagree, and we have a lot of areas where we can and must co-operate.'
