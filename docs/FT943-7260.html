940824 FT 24 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Prestwick rights issue only 25% subscribed The Pounds 4.5m rights issue launched by Prestwick Holdings, the Scottish electronics company, was 25.3 per cent subscribed, the company said yesterday. Despite the low take-up for the 4-for-5 offering, Mr Archie Coulson, executive chairman, said he was pleased with the outcome. 'The issue was fully underwritten and means we now have nine new institutional shareholders and a better balance between institutions and private investors,' he said. 'It is now about 50-50 instead of around 30 per cent institutions and 70 per cent private.'
