940817 FT 17 AUG 94 / UK Company News: TT/Dale TT Group, which has made a recommended offer for Dale Electric International, now speaks for 7.48m shares (32.76 per cent) in the North Yorkshire-based generator manufacturer. The offer of 70.25p for every Dale share values the company at Pounds 16m. There is a 1-for-5 share alternative. The first closing date for the offer is August 19.
