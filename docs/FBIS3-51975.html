Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Pedro Ross Leal, member of the Politburo and secretary general of the Cuban Workers Federation [CTC], has stressed that the 55th anniversary of the CTC is a patriotic celebration which ought to contribute to consummate the workers' commitment to defend the Revolution and the accomplishments of socialism. Ross added that the unity of the labor movement anticipated by the founders of the CTC during the period of the pseudo-republic, inspires today's workers to confront the criminal U.S. blockade. Likewise, Ross pointed out that in this new anniversary of the CTC, Cuban workers are seeking new ways to attain higher economic efficiency and alternatives to streamline the national finances. Pedro Ross also announced that the 55th CTC Anniversary pin will be given, for the first time, to 66 founder members and union leaders on 28 January in recognition of their work in the labor movement.
