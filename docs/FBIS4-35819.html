BFN [Text] One hundred fifty-eight PLO officers and staff last night flew out from Tunis aboard a special Tunisian plane chartered by Saudi Arabia to Egypt, their last stop before they proceed to the Gaza Strip. The group, officially regarded a part of the Palestine Liberation Army, is comprised of personnel who served over the past few years on PLO-related civic duties. They will travel by bus from Egypt to the crossing point in Rafah tomorrow, Sunday, on their way to the Gaza Strip.
