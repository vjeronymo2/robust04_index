Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 15 Mar (XINHUA) -- The decision of the Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress [NPC] on Approving the NPC Standing Committee's acceptance of Yang Yanyin's request to resign as a member of the NPC Standing Committee, Adopted on 15 March 1994 by the Second Session of the Eighth NPC: The Second Session of the Eighth NPC has decided to approve a decision of the Third Meeting of the Eighth NPC Standing Committee on accepting Yang Yanyin's request to resign as a member of the Eighth NPC Standing Committee.
