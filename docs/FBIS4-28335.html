BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 7 Jun Morning Edition] [Text] Speaking at a press conference on 6 June in connection with North Korea's suspected nuclear weapons development, Vice Foreign Minister Saito indicated there was still a possibility that negotiations would be held between North Korea and the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA), depending on the next move by Pyongyang. Saito said: "The IAEA's report says 'North Korea's overall cooperation is necessary to satisfactory inspections.' This means that the possibility of conducting inspections by other means has not been eliminated." He cited special inspections of two nuclear-related facilities which have not been filed for inspection as potential "other ways" in which inspections might be carried out.
