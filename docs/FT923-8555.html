920811 FT 11 AUG 92 / International Company News: Brazil sells its 40% stake in Nitriflex THE Brazilian government has continued the privatisation of its petrochemical sector by selling its 40 per cent share in Nitriflex, a producer of plastic resins and specialised rubber. The sole participant in the auction was Itap, a local manufacturer of plastic packaging, which already held 60 per cent of Nitriflex. Itap paid Dollars 26.9m for the outstanding shares in Nitriflex. It took a majority interest in the company earlier this year by buying the a 30 per cent stake held by Goodyear, the US tyre company.
