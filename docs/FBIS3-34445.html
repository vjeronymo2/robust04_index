Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 8 Feb (MENA)--Muhammad 'Abd-al-Mun'im, head of the Presidential Press Office, said at the end of the session of talks that President Husni Mubarak held with Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres that President 'Arafat and Peres will resume their meetings at the Egyptian Foreign Ministry later tonight in the presence of Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa. He added that the talks will probably be resumed tomorrow morning, Wednesday. He said the Palestinian president's political adviser Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh [title as received] and Israeli Environment Minister Yosi Sarid participated in the approximately one-hour tripartite Mubarak-'Arafat-Peres meeting.
