BFN [By Danielle Yang] [Text] Taipei, June 23 (CNA) -- Burkina Faso President Blaise Compaore is scheduled to visit the Republic of China [ROC] July 18-22, the Foreign Ministry said Thursday [23 June]. Burkina Faso resumed diplomatic relations with the ROC on February 2, 1994. Compaore's visit is at the invitation of President Li Teng-hui. He will exchange views with Li on international situation and matters of mutual concern as well as visit local agricultural and economic establishments.
