April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

As a fan of "Star Trek: The Next Generation," I discovered its March 24 program titled "The Offspring," by Rene Echeverra, to be a poignant fantasy.

The story in which Cmdr. Data (Brent Spiner) fathered and educated a child of his own creation with the hope that he could give something back to the Federation was done with a high degree of insight and joy. I do hope they bring back Data's daughter as this was an outstanding teleplay that should lead to another episode.

David W. Vickson, Alhambra
