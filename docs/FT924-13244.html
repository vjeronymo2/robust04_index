921016 FT 16 OCT 92 / International Company News: Lockheed profit holds steady LOCKHEED, the US defence group, yesterday reported net earnings of Dollars 86m, or Dollars 1.40 a share, in the third quarter, compared with Dollars 81m, or Dollars 1.28, in the same period of 1991, writes Martin Dickson. Mr Daniel Tellep, chairman, attributed the increase mainly to higher programme profits in the aeronautical and electronics sectors. Sales were Dollars 2.5bn, against Dollars 2.4bn. Nine-months earnings totalled Dollars 229m, or Dollars 3.69 a share, on sales of Dollars 7.2bn, up from earnings of Dollars 205m or Dollars 3.23 on sales of Dollars 7.1bn.
