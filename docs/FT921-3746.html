920312 FT 12 MAR 92 / International Company News: Toys 'R' Us shows growth TOYS 'R' Us, the large specialist toy retailer, lifted sales 11.1 per cent to Dollars 6.12bn in the year to February 1, and recorded after-tax profits of Dollars 339.5m, writes Nikki Tait in New York. This compares with sales of Dollars 5.51bn in the previous year, and net profits of Dollars 326m. Earnings per share rose from Dollars 1.11 to Dollars 1.15. In the key fourth-quarter Christmas period, - after-tax profit was Dollars 261.6m, up just over 10 per cent on the previous year, on sales of Dollars 2.86bn against Dollars 2.55bn.
