921010 FT 10 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Fleming Income value 64.03p At September 30, the end of its initial nine months trading period, Fleming Income & Capital Investment Trust had a net asset value per share of 64.03p. In the period gross revenue totalled Pounds 4.47m, and the net available profits came to Pounds 3.13m for earnings per share of 3.4p. Dividends paid and payable absorbed Pounds 1.84m.
