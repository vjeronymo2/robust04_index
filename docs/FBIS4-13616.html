BFN [Report by Hemi Shalev] [Text] Following MA'ARIV's 18 April report on Assistant Foreign Ministry Director General Eytan Bentzur's assessments regarding of the functioning of the U.S. Administration, the foreign minister's bureau sought to clarify that Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres "highly appreciates the efforts of the U.S. secretary of state and the American peace team, who have greatly contributed to the peace process, and thanks the Clinton Administration in general for the warm attitude it has shown Israel." The bureau's statement asserted that the Foreign Ministry "was not party to the opinions of the senior official with regard to the policies of the Administration and the peace team and these opinions are merely his own personal views."
