Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ukraine will shortly join NATO's "Partnership for Peace" program, the Ukrainian foreign minister said. Anatoliy Zlenko, at a news conference held jointly with Hungarian Foreign Minister Geza Jeszenszky in Uzhgorod, Ukraine, on Monday described the program as very promising and said Ukraine would take part both in its preparation and implementation. Jeszenszky said, "Hungary fully shares the position of Ukraine on the program." Answering a question about the Yugoslav conflict, Zlenko said it could only be resolved by negotiation. Jeszenszky said Hungary took a similar view.
