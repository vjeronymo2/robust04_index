April 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

TERRA MAR APARTMENTS SELL FOR $6.47 MILLION

The Terra Mar apartments at 20253 Keswick St., Canoga Park, have been sold by Salco Financial Inc. of Santa Monica for $6.47 million. The Hanes Co. Inc. in Woodland Hills was broker in the transaction.

The 72-unit building was purchased by Herb Nadel, a partnership, of Los Angeles.
