940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Yeoman Inv Trust net assets improve A rise in net asset value per capital share from 342.7p to 507.6p was achieved by Yeoman Investment Trust over the 12 months to December 31. After-tax revenue expanded from Pounds 2.78m to Pounds 3.4m. Earnings per income share amounted to 13.8p (11.28p) and a final dividend of 1.5p brings the total up to the forecast 13.4p (13.6p).
