Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Assistant Foreign Minister for Political Affairs Choe Tong-chin yesterday discussed with visiting Iranian Deputy Foreign Minister Manuchehr Mottaki ways to cut red tape in immigration affairs administration to promote to economic cooperation between the two countries. Choe requested that Iran more readily issue visas to Korean businessmen and construction workers for easier business activities in Iran during talks at the former's office, a Foreign Ministry official said. Mottaki, who flew into Seoul Tuesday [18 January] from Tokyo, called on the Korean government not to require visas for Iranians visiting Korea for 15 days or less, the official said. The Iranian deputy foreign minister will pay a courtesy call on Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu this morning.
