Narcotics Control Strategy Report 6 April 1994 NEAR EAST/SOUTH ASIA IRAN

Permanent Representative to UN Calls Report `Deceitful'

Permanent Representative to UN Calls Report `Deceitful' LD0604102994 Tehran Voice of the Islamic Republic of Iran First Program Network in Persian 0930 GMT 6 Apr 94 LD0604102994 Tehran Voice of the Islamic Republic of Iran First Program Network Persian BFN [Text] Our country's permanent representative to the United Nations [Kamal Kharrazi] described as deceitful the remarks made by U.S. State Department officials about Iran's performance in the fight against narcotics. He stressed: While Iran has come under strong Western criticism for taking strict antinarcotics measures, the United States accuses Tehran of procrastination in the matter. Referring to data and statistics and a report by the International Narcotics Control Board, our country's permanent UN representative said: The Islamic Republic of Iran has taken extensive measures to control narcotics, not only in the region but also globally. Tehran's investment in the matter is of the highest level. The U.S. assistant secretary of state claimed last Monday that the Islamic Republic of Iran is taking no measures to fight narcotics smuggling.
