921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / International Company News: Japanese car venture ELEVEN Japanese automakers and the semi-official Japan Key Technology Centre (JKTC) are to set up a venture to develop a catalyst for reducing nitrogen oxides and carbon dioxides, Reuter reports from Tokyo. The joint venture, to be capitalised at Y2.25bn, will be owned 70 per cent by JKTC and the rest by the carmakers.
