Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Unscheduled news flash] [Text] It has just been announced that Theofilos Yeoryiadhis, 37, a turkologist and chairman of the Committee of Solidarity With Kurdistan, was murdered in Nicosia tonight. Yeoryiadhis was shot in the parking lot of a three-story building on Thoukidhidhou Street in Aglanja where he lived with his family. According to a female witness living at a nearby house, two people were seen escaping on a motorcycle immediately after the shooting. The area has been blocked off by police and the police chief and director of the criminal investigation department are there. Yeoryiadhis is the father of three children aged 13, eight, and 10 months.
