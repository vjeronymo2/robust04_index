BFN [Text] Chairman of the Chinese Xinjian-Uigur autonomous region cooperators' union (Van Xin Tao) arrived in Atyrau on a working visit. He familiarized himself with the work of his Mangistau colleagues and with industrial enterprises and resources of the oblast. He met the head of the oblast consumer cooperation and discussed possibilities of developing bilateral mutually advantageous trade. He signed an agreement with the Kazkor joint stock company on annual procurement of 150-200 tonnes of [word indistinct]. The Chinese also intend to buy black wool. They invited their Mangistau colleagues and representatives of Kazkor to participate in an international fair to be held later in the year in Urumqi.
