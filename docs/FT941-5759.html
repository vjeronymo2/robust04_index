940304 FT 04 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Hillsdown Holdings HILLSDOWN HOLDINGS' subsidiary, Hillsdown International, has sold Vleeswarenfabrik Scheemda, its Dutch pastrami and smoked beef operation, to a new company formed by the shareholders of Vleeswarenfabrik Quartet, one of Scheemda's competitors. The disposal is not material in relation to Hillsdown's net assets.
