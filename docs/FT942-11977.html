940503 FT 03 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Ramus losses up For the half year ended December 31 1993 Ramus Holdings, the USM-traded ceramic wall and floor tile subsidiary of Hong Leong Group of Malaysia, suffered pre-tax losses of Pounds 2.6m, compared with Pounds 1.7m, from turnover down at Pounds 17.3m against Pounds 22.3m. Losses included Pounds 1.5m rationalisation costs, but lower interest of Pounds 257,000 (Pounds 405,000).
