Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 28 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received General Secretary of the Italian Institute of International Relations, Giancarlo Elia Valori, today. Secretary of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea, Kim Yong-sun, was present on the occasion. President Kim Il-song conversed with the guest in a cordial and friendly atmosphere. He was presented with a gift by the guest. He arranged a luncheon for the guest.
