BFN [From the ``ACIR Radio News'' newscast] [Text] Mario Aburto Martinez, who assassinated Luis Donaldo Colosio Murrieta, had maintained ties with several armed groups in various Mexican states, according to his testimony before the attorney general moments after murdering the PRI [Institutional Revolutionary Party] candidate. Aburto refused, however, to name the armed groups and the regions in which they operate or to say whether he acted alone or if the assassination was part of a plot. He also denied belonging in the past or at present to any political party or religious sect.
