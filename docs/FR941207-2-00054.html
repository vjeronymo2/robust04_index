Employment and Training Administration [TA&hyph;W&hyph;30,190] Vought Aircraft Company A/K/A LTV Aerospace & Defense Company, Dallas, TX; Notice of Termination of Investigation Pursuant to Section 221 of the Trade Act of 1974, an investigation was initiated on August 8, 1994, in response to a petition which was filed by the United Auto Workers, Local 848, on behalf of workers at Vought Aircraft Company, Dallas, TX. An active certification, revised November 16, 1994, covering the petitioning group of workers remains in effect (TA&hyph;W&hyph;29,206). Consequently, further investigation in this case would serve no purpose, and the investigation has been terminated. Signed in Washington, DC this 28th day of November, 1994. Victor J. Trunzo, Program Manager, Policy and Reemployment Services, Office of Trade Adjustment Assistance. [FR Doc. 94&hyph;30096 Filed 12&hyph;6&hyph;94; 8:45 am] BILLING CODE 4510&hyph;30&hyph;M
