BFN [From the "Exclusive" program] [Text] On 17 June, the Central Army concluded an exercise for officers and general staff called Moncada 2-94-1, after three days of fieldwork and positive results in strengthening defensive capacities. Organization, command maintenance, and cooperation during combat actions were the primary courses of study during the exercise. For their fulfillment, various tactical episodes were carried out by regular units and local defense forces. The maneuvers are part of the Central Army's preparation system during the current period of instruction and included exercises with live ammunition by some units.
