BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 2 (KCNA) -- Workers and other laboring masses of Korea significantly celebrated May Day, the international holiday of the world working class. Premier Kang Song-san, Vice Presidents Yi Chong-ok and Pak Song- chol and other party and government cadres went to industrial establishments Sunday to enjoy the holiday together with workers. At factories and enterprises, including the Komdok general mining enterprise, the Namgang power station, the Kim Chaek iron and steel complex, the Pyongyang textile combine, the Hungnam fertilizer complex and Nampo Port, they congratulated workers on the new upsurge they were bringing about in the implementation of the party's revolutionary economic strategy, and enjoyed the holiday, mixed with them, while appreciating art performance, dancing and holding colorful events. The party and government cadres saw round products newly manufactured by Korean workers, conversed with labor innovators and paid visits to workers' houses. They expressed the belief that workers would always stamsocialism centred on the masses and strengthen solidarity with the workers in other countries. On the same day, artistes of central art troupes gave performances in parks and pleasure grounds in the capital city of Pyongyang.
