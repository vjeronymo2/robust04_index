Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Senate President Ahmad al-Lawzi has said that Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali reviewed the scope and dimensions of Jordanian policy and the active diplomatic march led by His Majesty King Husayn on the national, pan-Arab, and international levels before the Senate today for two and one-half hours. In a statement to the Jordanian news agency, following a closed session held by the Senate today, al-Lawzi added that Dr. al-Majali answered all questions posed by the Senate members. He answered questions on the recent Jordanian-Palestinian economic agreement, developments in the peace process, and the efforts made by His Majesty King Husayn and his government to restore accord among the Arabs and emphasize their solidarity and unified position on various developments and challenges. Al-Lawzi said that the Senate expressed profound thanks to Dr. al-Majali for his constant emphasis on the need for consultations and cooperation between the legislative and executive authorities. The Senate also expressed adherence to, and support for, the role of Jordan's leadership, message, and people, he added.
