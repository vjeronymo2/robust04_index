BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 10 (KCNA) -- Talks were held here Monday between a military delegation of the DPRK and a high-level delegation of the Ministry of National Defence of Laos. Present on the Korean side were Choe Kwang, Politburo member of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea and chief of the general staff of the Korean People's Army [KPA], Vice-Marshal Kim Kwang-chin and other generals and officers of the KPA, and on the opposite side were head of the delegation Choummali Sai-ngason, Politburo member of the C.C., the Lao People's Revolutionary Party and minister of national defence, Douangchai Phichit, deputy chief of the General Staff of the Laotian People's Army [LPA], Somphet Thipmala, director of the LPA General Logistics Department, Thongmani Thiphommachan, deputy director of the LPA General Political Department, and other members of the delegation. The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.
