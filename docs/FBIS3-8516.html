Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Article by Hajrudin Fermic from Maglaj] [Text] The [Serbian] aggressor this evening resumed his fierce attacks on Maglaj. The town has been under attack from surface-to-surface missiles of horrendous destructive power since 1800. Seven such missiles have been fired exclusively at residential and civilian buildings so far. The destruction of Maglaj in this fashion continues even as we are sending this dispatch, and it is 1850 hours now. A horrible evening in Maglaj!
