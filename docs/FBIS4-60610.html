BFN [Text] Palestinian policemen tried to prevent Brigadier General Doron Almog, IDF [Israel Defense Forces] commander of the Gaza Strip, from entering the autonomy. Our correspondent Shlomi Eldar reports that a Palestinian policeman stopped Gen. Almog near Netzarim and told him to go back from where he came or he would shoot at the wheels of Almog's vehicle. The general telephoned Brig. Ziyad al-Atrash, who arrived at the site. After talking to the Palestinian policeman, al-Atrash informed them that Brig. Gen. Almog could enter the autonomy whenever he wished to do so.
