920807 FT 07 AUG 92 / Rise in full-time education figures THE proportion of 16-year-olds remaining in full-time education rose from 51.7 per cent in 1989 to 59.8 per cent last year, according to the Department of Education. The rate for 17-year-olds rose from 35.4 per cent to 43 per cent. The combined figures for full-time and part-time students rose from 68.3 per cent of 16-year-olds in 1989 to 73.2 per cent last year, and from 53.1 per cent of 17-year-olds in 1989 to 58.8 per cent last year.
