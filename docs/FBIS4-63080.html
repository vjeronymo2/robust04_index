BFN [ITAR-TASS report under "From Our Correspondents and News Agencies" rubric: "Kirghizia [Kyrgyzstan] Has 25 Generals for 15,000 Servicemen"] [Text] Exercises by the Armed Forces of Kirghizia held the other day were marked by a shower of stars on commanders' shoulder boards. By an edict of President Askar Akayev, four Kirghizian Army officers were simultaneously promoted to the rank of major general. Competent sources in the Defense Ministry claim that the Republic, whose Armed Forces number barely 15,000, now has 22 serving majors general and three lieutenants general. True, the actual commander in chief, President Akayev, holds the modest rank of reserve colonel.
