941210 FT 10 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Faupel rises 35% Improved margins and overhead savings in its garment business helped Faupel Trading Group report a 35 per cent advance in pre-tax profits from Pounds 404,000 to Pounds 546,000 in the six months to September 30, on turnover unchanged at Pounds 13.4m. Earnings per share for the USM-quoted clothing and household textiles importer, were 3.13p (2.87p) and the interim dividend is unchanged at 1.85p.
