920627 FT 27 JUN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Europe has been flooded with ginger from Nigeria, reports Man-producten, but most parcels are of inferior quality. Cochin ginger prices are a little firmer after a late moonsoon, which has also lifted the turmeric market. Nutmegs are steady, with increasing demand for West Indian defectives. The fall in the dollar has boosted dollar prices for cassia, and some countries of origin believe the market has started to improve. Cloves are unchanged at Dollars 650 a tonne - the lowest price for 25 years.
