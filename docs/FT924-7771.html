921114 FT 14 NOV 92 / French industry investing less French industrial investment will fall by 11 per cent in volume this year and is expected to stage a fragile 1 per cent recovery in 1993, according to the latest survey by Insee, the state statistics body, William Dawkins writes from Paris. The study, based on a poll of 2,500 companies in October, reflects corporate France's deepening gloom as the growth in domestic demand continues to weaken.
