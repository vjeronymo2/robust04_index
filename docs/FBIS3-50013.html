Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, Jan. 14 -- Representatives of the Polish Ministry of Industry and Trade, and the Russian Federation Committee for Engineering Industry signed in Warsaw on Friday an agreement on cooperation between the engineering industries of both countries, spokeswoman for the Industry and Trade Ministry Grazyna Lewandowska said. "In line with economy development priorities, the contracting parties will encourage industrial, scientific, trade and economic cooperation between firms and scientific institutes of the engineering industry, primarily in the reconstruction and improvement of cooperation links. They will encourage mutual investment and starting joint enterprises, the agreement reads.
