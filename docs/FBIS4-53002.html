BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 14 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received a gift from a delegation of the Communist Party of India (Marxist) on a visit to Korea. The gift was handed to secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Hwang Chang-yop by Politburo member of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Communist Party of India (Marxist) and Prime Minister of West Bengal State Jyoti Basu.
