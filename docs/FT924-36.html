921231 FT 31 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Estates & Agency delays final dividend Estates & Agency Holdings, which owns property investments in the UK, is delaying a recommendation on its final dividend for the year ended June 30 1992 until the position regarding a planned development is clarified. The company said the unusual step has been taken primarily to retain funds, and meanwhile has declared a second interim of 0.5p, absorbing Pounds 30,000, to make 3p to date; total for the previous 18 months was 7.5p costing Pounds 450,000. Results for the year showed gross rental income at Pounds 5.05m, and pre-tax profit of Pounds 787,000, against Pounds 7.14m and Pounds 1.08m respectively for the 18 months. Earnings per share came to 11.57p, compared with 14.24p. Net asset value at June 30 fell to 393p per share, against 430p in 1991.
