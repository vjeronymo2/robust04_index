General Aviation Air-Ground Stations §22.805 Channels for general aviation air-ground service. The following channels are allocated for the provision of radiotelephone service to airborne mobile subscribers in general aviation aircraft. These channels have a bandwidth of 20 kHz and are designated by their center frequencies in MegaHertz.
