931117 FT 17 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Enlarged SW Wood at Pounds 0.75m WITH 'good performances' from all activities SW Wood, the specialist printing and packaging group, achieved a 77 per cent improvement in pre-tax profits to Pounds 750,000 for the six months to end-September. The results of recently-acquired Albert Gait were included for some three months. Turnover advanced from Pounds 4.9m to Pounds 6.27m. The acquisition added Pounds 1m to turnover and Pounds 135,000 to profits. Tax rose to Pounds 247,000 (Pounds 140,000) and earnings emerged at 3.3p (2p). The interim dividend is lifted to 0.75p (0.5p). The directors are proposing a name change to Wyndeham Press following the withdrawal from metal trading.
