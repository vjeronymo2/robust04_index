BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 23 -- A Brazilian economic and commercial delegation led by Minister Calso Mareos Viera de Souza [name as received], head of Brazil's Commercial Development Department, paid a working visit to Vietnam from April 19-23. The delegation included 18 businesspersons of Brazil's leading groups and companies of oil and gas industry, energy, construction, agricultural machines, civil aviation, and import-export. During its stay here, the delegation was received by Vice Foreign Minister Le Mai. It exchanged views with concerned government bodies on the fields of cooperation and investment, trade, agriculture, industry, energy and civil aviation. Speaking on these occasions, Mr de Souza reaffirmed the Brazilian Government's desire to promote economic and commercial relations with Vietnam. He said that his first visit was an important event in the development of these relations. The Brazilian guests left Ho Chi Minh City today, concluding its visit to Vietnam.
