Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Feb. 26 KYODO -- Colombian President Cesar Gaviria Trujillo arrived in Tokyo on Saturday for talks with Japanese Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa. Gaviria will meet with Hosokawa at his official residence Monday evening. During a five-day stay in Japan through Wednesday, the president will also meet Emperor Akihito and Empress Michiko, and hold talks with Japanese business leaders.
