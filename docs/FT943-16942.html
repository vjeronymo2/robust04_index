940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Midland & Scottish Midland & Scottish Resources reduced pre-tax losses from Pounds 113.6m to Pounds 48.3m in 1993, on sales up from Pounds 28.1m to Pounds 65.3m. The results included the Pounds 17m provision against the Emerald Field which was announced at the interim stage. A further provision was made against the carrying value of the floating storage unit which is part of the Emerald facilities. Basic losses per share declined to 24p (55p).
