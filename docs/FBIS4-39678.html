BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Dmitriy Gorokhov, Nikita Yermakov, and Igor Shchegolev] [Text] Paris June 9 TASS -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev left here for Istanbul on Thursday to attend a North Atlantic Cooperation Council session. Within the framework of the session of the Council of the Organisation for Economic Cooperation and Development (OECD), Kozyrev signed a package of documents establishing official contacts between Russia and the OECD and met with OECD leaders and chief delegates of a number of countries. The documents signed open the way for broader effective long-term cooperation between Russia and the OECD, cooperation which meets the interests of the two sides, Kozyrev pointed out, summing up the results of his stay in Paris. "This has become yet another illustration of the international community's support for Russian reforms and yet another step towards our country's integration into the world economy," he said.
