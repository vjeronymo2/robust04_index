Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- A government meeting on public expenditure chaired by German Chancellor Helmut Kohl agreed on further budget cuts of 5 billion German marks [DM] in Bonn this evening. According to initial information, around DM1.2 billion is to be cut from the defense budget. Further cuts were also envisaged in the budgets of the Transportation and Labor Ministries. German Finance Minister Theo Waigel refused to give journalists further details.
