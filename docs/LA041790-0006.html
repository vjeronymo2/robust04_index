April 17, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

GASOLINE TAX

I have always found the Reagan and Bush administrations' inability to logically distinguish between a tax and a user fee to be hilarious, in a sad and cynical sort of way. Apparently, if a Democrat wants to raise revenues, it's a tax. If a Republican wants to raise revenues, it's a user fee.

BRYAN D. LE BLANC

Los Angeles
