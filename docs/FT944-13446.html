941027 FT 27 OCT 94 / International Company News: BCE has strong third quarter lifting income to CDollars 362m BCE, Canada's international tele-communications group, posted sharp third-quarter and nine-month gains, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. Final third-quarter net income was CDollars 362m (USDollars 268m), or CDollars 1.17 a share, against CDollars 128m, or 42 cents, a year earlier on revenues of CDollars 5.2bn, against CDollars 4.8bn. The latest figures included a CDollars 151m, or 49 cents a share, special gain on the acquisition of UK cable-TV assets and a public stock offer. Canadian Telecoms contributed CDollars 220m, little-changed. Northern Telecom contributed CDollars 38m, against a loss of CDollars 23m, while BCE Mobile doubled its contribution to CDollars 7m. Bell Canada International contributed CDollars 154m, against CDollars 5m. BCE's nine-month net profit was CDollars 789m, or CDollars 2.56, against a loss of CDollars 218m, or 71 cents, a year earlier. The 1994 period included the special gain and the 1993 period a CDollars 624m Northern Telecom special charge. Total revenues were CDollars 15.5bn against CDollars 14.2bn. Canadian Telecoms contributed CDollars 628m against CDollars 556m, BCE Mobile CDollars 16m against CDollars 4m, and Northern Telecom CDollars 127m against a loss of CDollars 658m including special charge.
