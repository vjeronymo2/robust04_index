941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / Growth expected in most regions East Anglia, the east Midlands, the south-east and Wales are the regions likely to enjoy the strongest growth next year, according to the Regional Economic Outlook produced by Oxford Economic Forecasting. The outlook says that economic growth has been fairly widespread following a recession which had the greatest impact on the south-east. It expects the south-east to grow slightly faster than the national average, although Greater London is expected to show below-average growth. According to the outlook the slowest-growing region this year will be the north of England, where unemployment has been slow to fall.
