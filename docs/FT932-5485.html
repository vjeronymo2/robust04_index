930603 FT 03 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Danish acquisition for Powell Duffryn Powell Duffryn, the distribution and specialist engineering group, has paid Pounds 7.5m for Svanehoj International, the Danish maker of marine cargo pumps for LPG, petroleum products and chemical tanker applications. Svanehoj, which has an annual turnover of Pounds 17.5m, will be integrated into the marine division of Powell's Hamworthy Engineering subsidiary.
