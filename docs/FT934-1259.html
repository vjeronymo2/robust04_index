931220 FT 20 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Oriflame Mr Jonas af Jochnick, the vice-chairman of Oriflame International, has sold 1.7m shares in the cosmetics and toiletries direct sales company at 322p per share. This reduced his stake to 10.24m shares, or 19.09 per cent of the equity. Of the shares, 500,000 have been bought by Abacus (CI) who act as trustees to the Progress Settlement Trust, in which some members of the Jochnick family have a possible beneficial financial interest. The remaining 1.2m shares were placed with institutional clients of Panmure Gordon.
