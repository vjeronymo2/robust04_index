May 31, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Lech Piasecki of Poland won the 10th stage of the Tour of Italy bicycle race by edging Erik Breukink of the Netherlands by 25 seconds.

Breukink regained the overall lead during the 23-mile stage from Pesaro to Riccione.

Stephen Roche of Ireland finished third, 33 seconds behind Piasecki's time of 46 minutes 18 seconds.
