August 16, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Micro General Corp.: The Santa Ana-based mailing and shipping automation equipment company said it lost $189,543 for the second quarter, contrasted with a net profit of $112,833 for the corresponding 1989 period.

Revenue declined 29%, to $1.2 million from $1.7 million. The company reported a net loss of $137,560 for the first half of 1990, contrasted with a net profit of $318,899 for the same 1989 period. Revenue dropped 24%, to $2.6 million from $3.4 million.
