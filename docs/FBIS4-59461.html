BFN [Text] Judge Sa'id Mirza, who is investigating the assassination of former President Shaykh Bashir al-Jumayyil, has issued a warrant for the arrest of Habib al-Shartuni on murder charges following his escape from prison. Philip Khayrallah, chairman of the Judicial Council, has set 3 June as the date for the start of the trial of 'Abd-al-Hakim Hulayhil, Bilal Kafil, Hasan Mallat, Husayn Sahyun, and Murad Qaddur, who are charged with carrying out terrorist actions on the Dayr al-Balamand road with a view to fanning civil war, sectarian fighting, and sabotage on 22 June 1993.
