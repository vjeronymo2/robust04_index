December 9, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

CREDIT FOR MOM

Your article about the football player in Massachusetts who wanted his mom to attend the father-son banquet (Sports, Dec. 5) has prompted me to write.

As a Mission Viejo mother of three sons who participated in sports from Little League through some professional ball, I felt a similar discrimination. We moms gave a lot of physical and emotional support to our sons and their athletic quests, but when it was time to take a bow, the fathers were the ones recognized.

It wasn't just the activities that we mothers were excluded from, however. When was the last time you, the Los Angeles Times, wrote about "Brett Johnson, son of Bob and Debbie Johnson of Mission Viejo"?

There is one thing you can't take away from us, however. When those TV cameras turn on our sons, they always say, "Hi, Mom."

SAUNDRA SCHMIDT

San Juan Capistrano
