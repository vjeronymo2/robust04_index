930120 FT 20 JAN 93 / Menswear retailer for sale again COLES MENSWEAR, the London retailer, is for sale for the second time in little more than a year. It was put into administrative receivership last week. The retailer traded from nine leasehold shops but only five of them, all in central London, are now trading. Coles was bought by its present management when it went into receivership last year.
