August 29, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

QUOTABLE

"In Los Angeles, the Dimension Cable system recently boosted Nick at Nite from Channel 20 to 33 -- a whopping 65% increase! Among the nation's top five television markets as defined by the A.C. Nielsen Co., Nick at Nite averaged an impressive Channel 29. The so-called Big Three networks? Measly single digits." -- a Nickelodeon channel press release
