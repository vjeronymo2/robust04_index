Language: Lithuanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Information and Press Department of the Lithuanian Foreign Ministry has announced that on 12 January in Caracas, Vytautas Antanas Dambrava, Lithuanian ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary in Venezuela, and [Name indistinct] Bolivian ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary, signed a protocol on the establishment of diplomatic relations. Bolivia recognized Lithuania on 9 September 1991. This is the 87th country establishing diplomatic relations with Lithuania.
