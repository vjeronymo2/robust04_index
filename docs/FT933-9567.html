930809 FT 09 AUG 93 / International Company News: Hilclare cuts loss Hilclare, the USM-traded designer and maker of electronic security and lighting products, recorded a profit of Pounds 16,000 in the second half. That cut the pre-tax loss for the year ended March 31 1993 to Pounds 54,000, compared with a profit of Pounds 58,000 in previously. Results continued to reflect the recession in its markets and the year's turnover fell from Pounds 3.33m to Pounds 2.9m. The second half profit was achieved by reducing the cost base. With the announcement of the first half loss of Pounds 70,000 the company made a 2-for-7 rights issue at 35p to raise Pounds 240,000. The shares are currently trading at 36p. The dividend is held at 0.5p on the increased capital, payable November 1, and is met from losses per share of 1.3p (earnings 2p).
