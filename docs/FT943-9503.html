940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / Germany, Russia in gas talks Russian and German gas industry leaders met in the Black Sea resort of Sochi yesterday to discuss marketing strategies and future co-operation, company sources said. Heads of the German gas companies, Ruhrgas and BASF's Wintershall distribution subsidiary, fierce competitors in their domestic market, met the chairman of Russia's Gazprom monopoly. Wintershall, a relative newcomer to the German gas business, says it has taken an 8 per cent share of the domestic market, traditionally dominated by Ruhrgas. Both companies are supplied by Gazprom.
