920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Alba moves 20% ahead to Pounds 5.06m IN SPITE of 'continuing difficult trading conditions', pre-tax profits of Alba, the consumer electronics group, showed a 20 per cent advance in the year to March 31. The increase, from Pounds 4.2m to Pounds 5.06m, was achieved on turnover down just Pounds 257,0000 to Pounds 110.9m. Mr John Harris, chairman, said that with the exception of Hinari Deutschland all divisions within the group had shown improved performances on the year. Excluding the results from the German offshoot - which was closed during the year - trading profits were up by 73 per cent from Pounds 3.3m to Pounds 5.7m. Strong performances were recorded from Alba, Bush and Hinari in the UK, said Mr Harris, while he was particularly pleased with the 50 per cent sales increase from Harvard Maritime and with the turnround into profits at the French operation. The final dividend is raised to 3.75p for a total of 4.75p (4.35p), covered 1.6 times by earnings per share of 7.79p (7.11p).
