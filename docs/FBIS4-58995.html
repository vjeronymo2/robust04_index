BFN [Text] New Delhi -- The 7,000 strong Indian community in Yemen is safe and some of them have expressed a desire to leave the strife-torn country, an official spokesman said today, according to UNI. The Indian embassy in Sanaa is assisting the Indian nationals to leave Yemen where fierce fighting has erupted between North and South Yemeni forces. India has expressed sincere hope that peaceful conditions will be rapidly restored and all differences resolved on the basis of the Amman reconciliation agreement signed in February.
