November 15, 1990, Thursday, Valley Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: POLICE SEEKING SON OF MAN BEATEN TO DEATH

Los Angeles police are searching for the son of a North Hollywood man whose badly beaten body was found this week dumped in the hills above Studio City, authorities said Wednesday.

Tim Dominguez, 19, was being sought for questioning in the death of his father, Alexander Dominguez, 46, said Lt. Ron La Rue.

Detectives searching the Dominguez residence, which the father and son shared, found evidence of a struggle, La Rue said. But he would not say if the youth was a suspect in his father's death.

The body of Alexander Dominguez, a painter, was found wrapped in a blanket Tuesday in a brushy area near the 7800 block of Mulholland Drive. Police said he had been dead at least two days.
