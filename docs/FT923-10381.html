920730 FT 30 JUL 92 / Debt forces out Finnish minister THE Finnish trade and industry minister, Mr Kauko Juhantalo, asked by his Centre Party to stand down after reports he was being sued for debt, sent an open letter of resignation to President Mauno Koivisto yesterday, Reuter reports from Helsinki. 'I hereby respectfully request I be relieved of membership in the cabinet,' he wrote. Mr Koivisto is scheduled to accept Mr Juhantalo's resignation on Monday, when he will also appoint his successor. The Centre Party meets today to nominate the replacement. Mr Juhantalo, 50, confirmed that companies of his were being sued for more than Dollars 1m in a local court.
