930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / UK Company News: 35% assets rise at Aberforth Smaller Over the six months ended June 30 net asset value at Aberforth Smaller Companies Trust had risen nearly 35 per cent, from 122.8p to 165.1p. At June 30 1992 it had stood at 132.6p. Total revenue for the period came to Pounds 2.11m (Pounds 2.45m), while net revenue of Pounds 1.02m (Pounds 1.42m) led to earnings per share of 1.81p (2.53p). The interim dividend is held at 2.1p.
