920424 FT 24 APR 92 / International Company News: Dr Pepper expects Dollars 600m from issue DR Pepper/Seven-Up, the third largest US soft drinks concentrates company after Coca-Cola and PepsiCo, expects to raise around Dollars 600m through a previously announced initial public offering of shares, writes Nikki Tait. Of the 29.8m shares being sold in the offering, 1.8m are coming from existing institutional shareholders; the rest are new shares being sold by the company.
