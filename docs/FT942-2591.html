940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Grainger Trust Grainger Trust, the property investment and trading company, announced pre-tax profits of Pounds 4.76m for the six months to March 31, against Pounds 1.02m restated for FRS 5. Some Pounds 1.9m of the pre-tax figure consisted of a partial reversal of the Pounds 5.66m write-down of the value of Kennel Farm, Basingstoke, made in 1992. Turnover amounted to Pounds 16.4m (Pounds 15.6m). Earnings per share came through at 15.9p (3.4p) and the interim dividend is lifted from 1.2p to 1.35p.
