940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / Scheme to boost food exports The government yesterday announced a drive to increase food exports to Continental markets in an effort to close Britain's Pounds 6bn food and drink trade gap. Food producers and processors will be able to apply for grants of up to Pounds 150,000 under the government's expanded marketing development scheme, worth Pounds 6.4m over two years. The scheme will be launched in April. Farmers' path, Page 8
