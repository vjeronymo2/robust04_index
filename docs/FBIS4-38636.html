BFN [Text] A note from the Russian Federation Foreign Ministry has been handed to the Latvian Embassy in Moscow. The note says that beginning on 1 June 1994 Russia will extend most favored nation status in trade with Latvia. The note emphasized that this status will become irreversible only after Latvia ratifies bilateral agreements signed on 30 April on the withdrawal of Russian troops from Latvia and on social provisions for Russian armed forces pensioners residing in Latvia.
