Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 9 (XINHUA) -- A preparatory meeting for the Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress (NPC) was held in the Great Hall of the People here today. Qiao Shi, chairman of the Eighth NPC Standing Committee, presided over today's meeting and made a speech. He said the second session of the eighth NPC is extremely important since it is convened at a time when China is establishing a socialist market economic structure and marching on the road of modernization. He said the session will take Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics and the party's basic line as its guiding principles. The session, he said, will implement guidelines mapped out by the 14th National Congress of the Chinese Communist Party (CPC) and the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee. He called for efforts to make the meeting proceed in an atmosphere marked by democracy, unity, pragmatism and openness. Today's meeting elected a 163-member presidium and a secretary- general for the session. The meeting approved an agenda for the session, which includes: --hearing and examining a report on government work; --hearing and examining a report on the implementation of the 1993 national economic and social development plan and a draft plan for 1994; --hearing and examining a report on the implementation of the 1993 state budget and a draft budget for 1994; --examining a draft budget law and a draft decision of the NPC to empower Xiamen city with legislation power; --hearing work reports of the NPC Standing Committee, the Supreme People's Court and the Supreme People's Procuratorate; --electing new members to the Eighth NPC Standing Committee. The session will last 13 days. A total of 2,593 NPC deputies attended today's preparatory meeting.
