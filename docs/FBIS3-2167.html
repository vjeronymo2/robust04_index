Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, March 6 (XINHUA) -- Azerbaijani President Geydar Aliyev left Baku, capital of Azerbaijan, tonight for a five-day official visit to China. During the visit, the two countries will sign a joint statement and agreements on promotion of tourism and cooperation in the economic, techno-scientific and cultural fields. Aliyev leaves for home on Thursday [10 March].
