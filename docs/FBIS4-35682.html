BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 Jun (INA)--Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf met here today with Yusuf Bin-'Alawi, Omani minister of state for foreign affairs. The minister briefed his Omani counterpart on the positive developments that have taken place at the UN Security Council in favor of lifting the Iraq sanctions. He also informed him of Iraq's efforts to lift the sanctions clamped on its people, particularly since the justifications for maintaining the sanctions no longer exist. During the meeting, the two ministers reviewed the current Arab state of affairs, the means of promoting joint Arab action, and the items listed on the agenda of the Nonaligned Movement's ministerial conference.
