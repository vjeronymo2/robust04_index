Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] U.S. President Clinton telephoned President Husni Mubarak after midnight last night. President Mubarak affirmed during the conversation Egypt's condemnation and concern over the heinous crime, the victims of which were innocent Palestinians. President Clinton also condemned the crime committed against the sons of the Palestinian people. President Mubarak discussed with the U.S. President during the telephone conversation the reactions to the incident on the Arab and international levels and the necessary measures that need to be taken to preserve the rights of the Palestinians in the occupied territories to have protection and security for their lives and property. After that, President Mubarak telephoned Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat. He also telephoned Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin.
