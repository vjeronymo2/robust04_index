BFN [Text] Kuala Lumpur, April 5 (OANA-BERNAMA) -- A Chinese delegation arrived here Tuesday to attend the third bilateral meeting between officials of the foreign ministries of Malaysia and China beginning here Wednesday. The delegation led by Chinese Vice Foreign Minister Tang Jiaxuan will be here until Thursday. The informal meeting is a platform for both parties to exchange views and consult one another on bilateral relations and regional and international matters of mutual concern. Tang is scheduled to call on Foreign Ministry Secretary General Ahmad Kamil Jaafar Wednesday and Foreign Minister Abdullah Haji Ahmad Badawi on Thursday.
