February 13, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFLY

Soviets Enter Harvard MBA Program: The prestigious Harvard School of Business, which has launched the careers of some of the world's staunchest capitalists, has admitted the first Soviet students to its coveted MBA program. The school said four Soviet students had been accepted to its two-year Master of Business Administration degree program starting in September, after which they will fulfill a five-year commitment to work for a Soviet state enterprise.
