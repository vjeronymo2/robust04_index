930616 FT 16 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Monarch Resources losses deepen Lower gold prices and reduced output continued to affect Monarch Resources, the London-quoted mining and exploration company with activities in Venezuela, throughout 1992. The combination left the Revemin processing plant with losses of Dollars 1.84m, against profits of Dollars 186,000. Monarch's overall loss increased from Dollars 2.94m to Dollars 4.87m (Pounds 3.2m). Turnover fell to Dollars 6.97m (Dollars 9.25m). Losses per share came out at 33.2 cents (20.6 cents).
