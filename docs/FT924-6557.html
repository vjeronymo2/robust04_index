921120 FT 20 NOV 92 / International Company News: Yasuda in Paine Webber share sale YASUDA Mutual Life Insurance of Japan has halved its investment in a capital tie-up with Paine Webber to Dollars 150m and sold Paine Webber 1.7m shares at the request of the US brokerage, Reuter reports from Tokyo. Profits from the sale totalled Dollars 83m and follow a similar sale in February.
