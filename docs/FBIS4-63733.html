BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko and Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev, 24 May -- Oleksandr Tkachenko, former agriculture minister and member of the Peasants Party, was elected first deputy speaker of the Ukrainian parliament at a closed plenary sitting of the Supreme Council this evening. Oleh Demin, an independent, and vice president of the Ukrainian-Siberian investment corporation, was elected deputy speaker.
