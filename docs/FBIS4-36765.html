BFN [Text] This morning, in the presence of His Royal Highness Crown Prince Hasan, His Majesty King Husayn received 'Abdallah Ahmad Ghanim, the Yemeni minister of justice, who conveyed to his majesty an oral message from President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Yemeni Presidential Council. The message deals with the latest developments in the Yemeni Republic, expressing appreciation for Jordan's pan-Arab stand and its concern for Yemen's unity and achievements. His Majesty King Husayn stressed Jordan's support for fraternal Yemen's unity and democracy. The meeting was attended by His Highness Prince 'Abdallah Bin- Husayn; Dr.'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, the prime minister; al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, the chief of the Royal Hashemite Court; and the Yemeni ambassador to Jordan.
