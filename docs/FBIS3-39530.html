Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Lieutenant General Dhermetzoglou, spokesman of the National Defense General Staff, yesterday repeated that Greece will not get involved, directly or indirectly, in any form of military intervention if there are air raids over Bosnia-Herzegovina. Lt. Gen. Dhermetzoglou made it clear that, based on international regulations, Greek airports will only be open to provide emergency help to any of those aircraft. Our country, he said, will indiscriminately provide any medical help if asked.
