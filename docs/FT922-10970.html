920428 FT 28 APR 92 / UK Company News: Tough second half leaves Toye halved Toye & Company failed to live up to the promise of the early part of 1991, and finished the year with pre-tax profits halved. The dividend, however, is held at 8p. Turnover of the company, which makes civil and military regalia and is involved in other craft industries, showed some improvement to Pounds 12.9m (Pounds 12.4m), but profit worked through at Pounds 306,000 (Pounds 721,000) after holding at Pounds 373,000 in the opening six months. Earnings per share fell to 9.05p (20.02p).
