930604 FT 04 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Group 4 expands in Australia and New Zealand GROUP 4 Securitas (Internat-ional) BV, the parent company for Group 4's international operations, has bought the Australian and New Zealand security arms of TNT Limited, the Australian transport group which has been disposing of non-core businesses. Group 4 is to buy TNT's 73 per cent stake in TNT Security, which has annual sales of ADollars 35m, for an undisclosed amount. Group 4, which has had a shareholding in TNT Security since 1976, will own 100 per cent of the company following the transaction. Group 4 has also bought TNT's 60 per cent stake in TNT Security (NZ) Ltd.
