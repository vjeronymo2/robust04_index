Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Wiesbaden (DPA) -- Special task forces of the police in Frankfurt and Wiesbaden have now arrested nine Kurdish men and seized 2.5 kg of heroin. According to information released on Thursday, police had certain indications that Kurds put large amounts of drugs on the market already at the end of last year. In December, six suspects were arrested and 2.6 kg of heroin were seized. The operation shows that Wiesbaden has meanwhile become a "fixed location on the map of organized crime," the police stated.
