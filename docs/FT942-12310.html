940430 FT 30 APR 94 / Major campaigns in Bury Prime minister John Major answers reporters' questions while campaigning in Bury for next week's council elections. He sought to cover cracks in the Tory party over Europe by stressing a strategy designed to unite all but the most extreme anti-Europeans.
