BFN [Text] Vienna, June 16 (XINHUA) -- The 37th session of the UN Committee on the Peaceful Uses of Outer Space ended here today. China, as a member of the committee, was represented and the head of its delegation, Chen Shiqiu, addressed the meeting. In his speech Chen outlined the efforts and achievements made by China in the peaceful uses of outer space and in the development of space science and technology, stressing that there should be international cooperation on the uses of outer space. Delegates from various countries expressed a keen interest in an Asia-Pacific economic and social council ministerial meeting to promote the region's uses of outer space, which is scheduled for September in Beijing, as well as in the creation of an organization to oversee the uses of outer space in the region. The committee now has 53 member nations and the meeting, which opened on June 6, is the first of its kind since the UN Outer Space Affairs Division was transferred here from New York.
