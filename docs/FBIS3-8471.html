Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The region of Bihac remains the focus of aggressor's offensive. Throughout yesterday the aggressor used howitzers, in particular, and tanks to pound the area. Three civilians were killed and dozens injured. The aggressor also attacked the defense lines and threatened the Bihac-Cazin road. Forces of the so-called "autonomasi" [anti-Izetbegovic forces] have been also been very active. Attacks on Maglaj were of a slightly lesser ferocity but there were civilian fatalities nevertheless. Chetnik snipers, howitzers and mortars killed four civilians. Infantry attacks were repulsed. The humanitarian situation in the town is exceptionally difficult.
