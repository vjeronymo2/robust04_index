931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Bardon completes US debt placement Bardon, the quarry products group, has completed the debt placement with institutions in the United States which it announced at the time of its rights issue in July. The issue serves to provide Bardon with Dollars 75m (Pounds 48.7m) in guaranteed senior notes with an average 6 1/2 -year life. Warrants, which are attached to Dollars 72m of the notes, convert into a maximum of 3.6 per cent of the ordinary share capital and have an exercise price of 60.3p. Together with the rights issue and US disposals, Bardon has raised more than Dollars 215m so far this year.
