BFN [Text] Urumqi, May 18 (XINHUA) -- The airline of northwest China's Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region will operate three new international or regional air routes beginning in June this year. According to local officials, the routes are Tianjin-Urumqi-Novosibirsk-Moscow, Urumqi-Islamabad and Urumqi-Chongqing-Hong Kong. The Tianjin-Urumqi-Novosibirsk-Moscow and Urumqi-Islamabad routes will have their maiden flights on June 10 and July 10, respectively. There will be one flight a week by TU-154s on each of the air routes. Charter flights will be available on the route from Urumqi to Hong Kong via Chongqing, and there will be only one flight a week, said the officials.
