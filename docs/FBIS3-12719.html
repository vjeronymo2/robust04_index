Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, held a telephone conversation this evening with President Hafiz al-Asad of fraternal Syria. During the conversation, the two presidents discussed issues of interest to both fraternal countries and ways of enhancing bilateral relations. The two presidents also reviewed developments in the Arab arena and the Arab stand toward these developments, as well as issues of interest to our Arab nation. They also reviewed the situation in our country in light of ongoing efforts to contain the crisis and turn the accord document into concrete reality to enable our Yemeni people to overcome the political crisis and safeguard the march of unity, democracy, and stability in the homeland. The brother chairman of the Presidential Council expressed his thanks and appreciation for his brother, President Hafiz al-Asad, and the brothers in Syria for their concern about safeguarding the march of unity and democracy in our country and overcoming all challenges imposed by the political crisis that began on 19 August 1993. He emphasized the efforts being made by all sincere people in the homeland to end the regrettable complications of the crisis and to begin to implement the accord document to bolster the march of unity and democracy and build a modern Yemeni state. President Hafiz al-Asad said Syria -- leadership, government, and people -- stands by its brothers in Yemen and supports all efforts to safeguard the march of unity and democracy and bolster stability in Yemen. He said Yemeni unity is a gain not only for Yemen but also for all the masses of our Arab nation, since it enhances their capabilities in the face of challenges.
