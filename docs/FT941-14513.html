940120 FT 20 JAN 94 / Charity staff win 2.8% increase Workers in the charity and voluntary sector have received average pay increases of 2.8 per cent, according to the latest survey by the Reward Group in association with Charity Appointments, a recruitment agency. The survey of 134 charities showed pay increases were almost identical to those given to the private sector, but ahead of the 1.5 per cent ceiling for public-sector pay.
