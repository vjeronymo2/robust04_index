SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE COMMISSION 17 CFR Parts 228, 229, 230, 239, 240, and 274 [Release Nos. 33&hyph;7106; 34&hyph;34923; IC&hyph;20670; File No. S7&hyph;31&hyph;94] RIN 3235&hyph;AE14 Disclosure Concerning Legal Proceedings Involving Management, Promoters, Control Persons and Others AGENCY: Securities and Exchange Commission. ACTION: Proposed rulemaking.

SUMMARY: The Commission is publishing for comment amendments that would expand the types of legal proceedings required to be disclosed in Commission filings, add such disclosure to certain investment company filings, and increase to 10 years the reporting period for such legal proceedings disclosure. DATES: Comments must be submitted on or before January 6, 1995.
