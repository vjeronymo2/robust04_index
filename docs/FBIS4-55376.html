BFN [Text] Bydgoszcz, May 11 -- A delegation of the Chinese People's liberation Army, headed by Chief of the General Staff General Zhang Wannian, visited the Pomeranian Military District (POW) on Wednesday, spokesman for the district commander Janusz Krzysztofik said. In Bydgoszcz, General Wannian met with Commander of the POW General Tadeusz Bazydlo to discuss the tasks and specific features of training in this district. The Chinese guest also visited the missile troops and Artillery Military Academy in Torun to get acquainted with the system of artillery officers' training.
