BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 29 -- Chairman of the National Assembly Nong Duc Manh received here yesterday on separate occasions Malaysian, Belgian and Albanian ambassadors who paid him courtesy visits. Speaking to the Malaysian Ambassador Mr Cheah Sam Kip Chairman Manh noted the great achievements recorded by the Malaysian people, and thanked the government and people of Malaysia for their support and assistance to the Vietnamese people. He affirmed the on-going progress of the relations of friendship and cooperation between Vietnam and Malaysia. He said he hoped that the Malaysia diplomat would successfully fulfill his tasks during his term of office in Vietnam, contributing to the expansion of the friendship and cooperation between the two governments, people and national assemblies. In a cordial conversation with the Belgian Ambassador Benoit Reylandt, Chairman Manh highly appreciated the Belgian Government and people's cooperation and assistance to the Vietnamese people over the past period. He expressed his wish that the fine traditional relations of friendship and cooperation between Vietnam and Belgium would be further developed. In his reception of Albanian Ambassador Ferid Hoti, Chairman Manh thanked the Albanian people for their fine sentiments and precious support to Vietnam in its national defence and construction.
