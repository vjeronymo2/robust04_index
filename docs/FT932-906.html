930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / International Company News in Brief: Corporacion Banesto CORPORACION Banesto, Banesto's industrial holding company, should show a 1993 net profit in line with last year's consolidated result, Mr Mario Conde, chairman, yesterday told the annual shareholders' meeting, Reuter reports from Madrid. Last year, Corp Banesto made a consolidated net profit after minorities of Pta1.85bn (Dollars 14m).
