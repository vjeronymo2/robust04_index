Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to an order by Andrejs Rucs, the head of the local government of the Vidzeme district of Riga, two generals of the North-Western Group of Russian Forces were detained and deported to the state border today. The above-mentioned persons were detained this morning when representatives of the local government of Vidzeme district came to a Russian Army facility in Riga to take it over. Andrejs Rucs spoke to Latvian President Guntis Ulmanis about today's incident and this issue was also discussed at a session of the National Security Council today. Rucs added that the fact that the generals were detained on a day talks were scheduled between Russian and Latvian delegations was a coincidence. He expressed an opinion that the incident might cause the Saeima and the government to demand finally from the Russian side a concrete timetable for the army withdrawal. Sergey Zotov, the head of the Russian delegation, arriving for the talks on army withdrawal today, said that the talks could not begin until the above-mentioned incident is clarified. Martins Virsis, the head of the Latvian delegation for talks with Russia, while waiting for the Russian delegation on the airfield, commented that the incident was a deliberate attempt to disrupt the talks.
