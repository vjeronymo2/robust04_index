September 20, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: SANTA MONICA;

DEMOCRATS' ENDORSEMENTS

The Santa Monica Democratic Club will make its official endorsements for candidates and ballot measures in the November local and state elections Wednesday at 7:30 p.m. at the John Muir School auditorium, 721 Ocean Park Blvd.

Democratic candidates for City Council, Rent Control Board, Santa Monica-Malibu Unified School District board of education and Community College board of trustees have been invited to make presentations, as have representatives of nine local ballot measures, according to club President Mildred Rosenstein.

The public is invited, but only club members may vote for endorsements.
