BFN [By reporters Zhao Zhensheng (6392 2182 5116), Wang Chongyu (3769 1504 3768), and Liu Jing (0491 2533): "Unswervingly Develop Township Enterprises"] [Text] On 7 April, accompanied by Li Bingliang, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee; Gao Yongtang, vice chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee; and responsible comrades of the Langfang city party committee and government; Tian Jiyun, member of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and vice chairman of the National People's Congress Standing Committee, successively inspected Langfang Economic and Technological Development Zone and rural areas in Anci District. During the inspection tour, Tian Jiyun listened to work briefings given by the Langfang city party committee and the city government. On hearing that the output value of Langfang city's township enterprises totalled 17.72 billion yuan which accounted for 70 percent of the total industrial output value, Tian Jiyun said: Developing township enterprises is where the hope of making peasants attain a better-off level of moderate prosperity lies. Without the great development of township enterprises, there can be no rural modernization, and without rural modernization, there can be no national modernization. Therefore, no matter under what circumstances, we should unswervingly develop township enterprises in the course of developing the rural economy. Certainly, it is hard to avoid some problems in the process of developing township enterprises, namely, pollution and low efficiency problems. However, we should not negate it because of the existence of some problems which must be solved in the course of development. We should proceed from the fundamental interests of the state and from the interests of peasants, and never slightly waver in our determination to develop township enterprises. Tian Jiyun pointed out: Langfang is surrounded by Beijing and Tianjin.
