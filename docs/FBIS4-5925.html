BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 13 (KCNA) -- Messages of greetings were sent to the great leader President Kim Il-song by heads of state and party leaders of different countries on the occasion of his birthday. The messages came from Ugandan President Yoweri Kaguta Museveni, Tanzanian President Ali Hassan Mwinyi, Burkinabe president, head of state and President of the Council of Ministers Blaise Compaore, General Secretary of the Party of Revolution of Benin Ali Houdou, chairman of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Burundi Party of Unity for National Progress Nicolas Mayugi and acting leader of the February 18th Movement of Trinidad and Tobago Vincent Cabrea. The messages extend warmest greetings to President Kim Il-song and wish him good health and long life. They express firm solidarity with the Workers' Party of Korea and the Korean people in their efforts for the independent and peaceful reunification of Korea.
