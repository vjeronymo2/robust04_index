Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The AL-MU'TAMAR [Iraqi opposition newspaper] newspaper, citing some sources, has reported that the regime's defense minister, 'Ali Hasan al-Majid, threatened the Kuwaitis during a meeting with tribal chiefs in the city of al-Batha', which is located between al-Nasiriyah and Sinawah, on 25 December. Al-Majid emphasized that Saddam and his henchmen will reoccupy Kuwait.
