940831 FT 31 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Losses widen to Pounds 1.69m at Bensons Crisps Pre-tax losses deepened from Pounds 386,000 to Pounds 1.69m at Bensons Crisps, the Preston-based food manufacturer, for the half year to May 28. The loss was after exceptional costs of Pounds 845,000 relating to the closure of three sites. Turnover edged ahead from Pounds 16.5m to Pounds 17.6m but operating losses were Pounds 448,000 (Pounds 256,000). There were interest charges of Pounds 401,000 (Pounds 130,000). Losses per share emerged at 7.7p (3.2p) and the interim dividend is passed (0.7p).
