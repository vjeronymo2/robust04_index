BFN [Text] Beirut, 5 May (MENA)--The PLO will be taking steps to withdraw its weapons and armed men from Lebanon and to eliminate all evidence of its military and semi-military presence on Lebanese territories. Palestinian sources have said that the PLO will withdraw between 200 and 500 Fatah members in the first stage along with weapons and military equipment. The sources added that they expect the Lebanese army to deploy in the areas where the Palestinian camps are situated with the aim of strengthening the state's authority over all the areas in Lebanon.
