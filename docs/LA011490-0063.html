January 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

PARIS PLEASURE

A travel agent suggested a trip to Paris in early December.

She said that Paris is beautiful at Christmastime and there would be no crowds as there are in summer, spring and fall. She was absolutely right. And there were no lines to wait in at either the Louvre or Musee d'Orsay. The French were friendly and helpful. Winter there was wonderful and I'll jump at the chance to do such a trip again.

MARGARET HASTINGS

La Canada
