November 5, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Mortgage Rates Decline: Fixed-rate mortgages averaged 10.13% in the week ending Saturday, down from 10.17% the previous week, according to a national survey by the Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp. On one-year adjustable-rate mortgages, lenders were asking an average initial rate of 8.15%, down from 8.17%. The rates do not include add-on fees known as points.
