Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The second stage of reform in the Belarusian Armed Forces will be completed in 1994. According to reports from the republic, the strength of the Belarusian Army will be reduced to 100,000 men. This is three times less than the strength of the military grouping inherited from the former Soviet Union. According to the Belarusian defense doctrine law, the strength of the national army should be within the limits needed to ensure the country's sovereignty.
