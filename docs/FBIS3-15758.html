Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] A relative calm is prevailing on most of the war fronts in Karabakh. Tension has been continuing only in the southeastern sector since 3 March. The military operations are still continuing there. The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic Defense Army Press Service has reported that the Azerbaijani troops used mostly howitzers to shell Karabakh positions near Fizuli this morning. There are injured. The enemy halted its military operations after countermeasures were taken by the Armenian forces. The Armenian Defense Ministry Press Service has reported that a relative calm has also been prevailing on Armenia's borders since last night.
