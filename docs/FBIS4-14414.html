BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Boris Krivoshey and Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow March 31 TASS -- Russian Foreign Ministry spokesman Grigoriy Karasin has called for stepping up the search for Russian diplomat Igor Makeyev who went missing in Bangkok on March 22. Karasin said that the Russian Embassy and Thai officials have taken the necessary steps to investigate into the circumstances of the diplomat's disappearance and organise a search for him. However, "the search has been unsuccessful so far," he added. "The news about Makeyev's disappearance was a blow to his next of kin. They were shocked by it," Karasin said. "The feeling of sympathy and humaneness prompt us to take urgent and more active steps to find the missing diplomat," he said.
