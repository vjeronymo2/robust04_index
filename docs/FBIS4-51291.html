BFN [Text] Beijing, May 26 (XINHUA) -- Liu Huaqing, member of the Standing Committee of the Communist Party of China's Politburo and vice-chairman of the Central Military Commission, met here today with a delegation from the National Committee on U.S.-China Relations. The delegation was headed by Robert McNamara, former U.S. secretary of defense and ex-president of the World Bank. The American visitors arrived here May 22 as guests of the China Institute for International Strategic Studies.
