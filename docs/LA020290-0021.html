February 2, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

THOUSAND OAKS PAIR DECIDE ON COLLEGES

Thousand Oaks High football player Steve Rudisill made an oral commitment to San Diego State on Tuesday, crediting his campus visit of Jan. 5-6 for his decision. Rudisill, a Times All-Ventura County selection as a safety, also visited Oregon State and New Mexico.

Teammate Mike Lindsay, who rushed for 687 yards, has orally committed to West Point.
