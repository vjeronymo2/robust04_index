920314 FT 14 MAR 92 / World News in Brief: Young arm of the law Three youngsters who trailed an armed robber to his home even though he threatened them with a knife, were each awarded Pounds 50 by a judge. Marie Telford, 10, her sister Lisa, 9, and Mark Noble, 10, spotted the masked man fleeing from the Co-operative supermarket at Intake, Doncaster, South Yorks.
