920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea TEA There were 13,974 packages for the day, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. There was improved demand but prices were irregular. Landed bright liquoring east Africans and good mediums came to a firm market while lesser mediums proved a strong feature. Central African teas sold readily at last rates with brighter sorts dearer. Offshore more general demand. Most descriptions realised firm to dearer rates although plainer teas attracted less active support. The highest price realised this week was 210p for a Kenya pd. Quotations: quality 165p, good medium 135p, medium 110p, low medium 80p.
