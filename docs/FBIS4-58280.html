BFN [Text] The government has made its position clear that India will support nonproliferation measures which are universal, comprehensive, nondiscriminatory, and verifiable. The minister of state for external affairs, R.L. Bhatia, informed the members in the lower house of parliament today that New Delhi has expressed deep concern to the U.S. Administration at the proposed transfer of F-16 aircraft to Pakistan. He said during the visit to India, the U.S. deputy secretary of state, Mr. Strobe Talbott, was told that the transfer of such sophisticated aircraft to Pakistan will necessitate that India reappraise its own defense capabilities and take adequate steps to safeguard its security.
