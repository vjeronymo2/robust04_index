BFN [Text] Tokyo, April 15 KYODO -- Initial fiscal 1994 budgets of prefectural governments nationwide, including the Tokyo Metropolitan Government, totaled 48,371.1 billion yen, up 3.0 percent from the previous year, the Home Affairs Ministry said Friday [15 April]. The growth rate for prefectural budgets for the year that started on April 1, excluding the budgets of Ishikawa, Kyoto and Nagasaki prefectures, was 3.4 percent, compared with 3.7 percent for the national government's local fiscal program for the year. The three prefectures had compiled only minimal budgets due to their gubernatorial elections. In the prefectural budgets, excluding those of the three prefectures, public works projects financed by local governments alone were expanded 10.0 percent from the previous year to help stimulate the economy. The rate was lower than the 12.0 percent growth rate for the fiscal 1994 local fiscal program.
