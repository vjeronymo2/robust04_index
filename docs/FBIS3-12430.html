Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] According to the arrangement concluded between the Finance and the Labor and Social Welfare Ministries regarding compensation to the families of the Hebron massacre victims, the total amount of funds to be paid will amount to dozens of millions of shekels. This is reported by our correspondent Ya'el Ayalon. The payment will be a one-time compensation to the families of the dead and the wounded. During the first stage, each family who lost one of its members will receive an advance payment of 5,000 shekels. Ora Namir, labor and social welfare minister, says this is a humanitarian step. National Insurance Institute Chairman Yosi Tamir notes that this move is not intended to set a precedent.
