Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Vladimir Zhirinovskiy and Viktor Kobelev, the second most influential person in the Liberal Democratic Party [LDPR], today announced their reconciliation in Duma corridors. Yesterday Kobelev announced his resignation from the LDPR faction. Kobelev and Zhirinovskiy recognize that they have insignificant ideological disagreements, but have confirmed their readiness to cooperate in the future.
