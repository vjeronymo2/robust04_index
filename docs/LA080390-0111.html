August 3, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Petr Klima, formerly of the Detroit Red Wings of the NHL, pleaded guilty to four misdemeanor charges stemming from a 1989 drunk driving arrest.

Klima, now with the Edmonton Oilers, pleaded guilty in Oakland County Circuit Court in Pontiac, Mich., to one count each of driving with a suspended license, driving under the influence of alcohol, escape, and resisting and obstructing a police officer.
