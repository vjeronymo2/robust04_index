BFN [Excerpt] Deputy Antoine Joseph, member of the Movement To Establish Democracy in Haiti and former lower chamber president, has asserted that the Jonassaint government is illegal and unconstitutional and that he does not recognize the current power's authority. Deputy Joseph believes the government that was installed by a group of legislators and not by the entire legislative corps operates beyond the constitutional provisions provided for in that respect. Deputy Joseph, who noted friction between the executive, the parliament, and the position of the political parties, considers that all the conditions have been met to overthrow the Jonassaint government.
