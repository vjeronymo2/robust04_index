July 16, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

Are two of the prerequisites for participation in the game show "Win, Lose or Draw" that one should be devoid of all drawing ability and possess absolutely no knowledge of the geography of the world? On a recent show, the performance of a young female rock star was nothing short of pathetic. On the whole, I find the show an embarrassment as the celebrities' and contestants' deficiencies are revealed before thousands of viewers. What schools have these people attended?

J. M. Jackson, Los Angeles
