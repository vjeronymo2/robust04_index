Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Deputy Foreign Minister Mahmud Va'ezi, the special envoy of our country's president has held a meeting with Helmut Kohl, the German chancellor. In the meeting, a written message from President Hashemi-Rafsanjani was submitted to the German chancellor. The German chancellor welcomed the expansion of ties between Iran and Germany and, expressing satisfaction about the current situation, conveyed his wish to improve and expand the relations between the Islamic Republic of Iran and the European Community. The special envoy of our country's president, for his part, noted that the good relations between the Islamic Republic of Iran and Germany can prove to be a suitable gateway toward the consolidation and expansion of relations between the Islamic Republic of Iran and the European Community.
