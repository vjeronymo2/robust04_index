October 20, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

DESERT-INYO LEAGUE

Bishop Union 6, Paraclete 3 -- Keith Inderbieten's one-yard scoring run in the third quarter was all the Broncos (4-2, 2-0) needed at Antelope Valley College as Paraclete (3-4, 0-2) lost its fourth consecutive game.

Jorge Ordaz led Paraclete with 106 yards in 17 carries. TIM PERERA
