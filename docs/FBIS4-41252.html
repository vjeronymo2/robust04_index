BFN [Text] On 22 June, Prime Minister Vitaliy Masol received U.S. Ambassador William Green Miller at his request. During a sincere conversation touching on a broad range of issues, they deeply analyzed the state of Ukrainian-U.S. relations in the field of economic cooperation. The ambassador was informed about the economic and social problems facing the Government of Ukraine today and about ways to reform the economy taking into account the approaches expounded by the prime minister during the confirmation of his appointment by the Supreme Council. They also discussed the implementation of the agreements on economic assistance to Ukraine -- first and foremost, in organizing the production of medical preparations and on financial support for entrepreneurship. Issues pertaining to the forthcoming discussion by the G-7 leaders regarding energy supplies for Ukraine were also discussed, in particular, on providing help in developing and improving the safety of nuclear power engineering. Ukraine's Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko took part in the conversation.
