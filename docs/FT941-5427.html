940307 FT 07 MAR 94 / EU plan for works meetings attacked European Commission propos-als to force companies to consult with employees on a range of business issues is an 'unwarranted interference' on their right to manage and should be resisted, says the British Chambers of Commerce. The business lobbying group said the need for legislation across the European Union on this subject was 'questionable'. It added that employers should be free to establish their own internal procedures based on what was most appropriate.
