January 11, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: OCEANSIDE

An Oceanside man pleaded guilty Tuesday to first-degree murder in the slaying of a Marine stationed at Camp Pendleton.

Glenn Allan Silva, 33, could receive a life sentence for shooting Cpl. Robert Winters, 23, in the back on Aug. 31 on the base.

Silva is also wanted in New Bedford, Mass., in the killing of a woman there in 1984.

Silva's attorney, Charles Adair, said Silva had been seeing Winters' wife, and the motive for the shooting was a love triangle.

U. S. District Judge Earl Gilliam set sentencing for April 10.

Adair said Silva may plead guilty to second-degree murder once he is extradited to Massachusetts in the other case.
