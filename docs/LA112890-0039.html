November 28, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

To War or Not to War: ABC said Monday that it is planning a TV debate on the question of whether the United States should go to war against Iraq. The special edition of "Nightline" will be broadcast Dec. 7 at 11:30 p.m., continuing as long as it is deemed necessary, a network spokeswoman said. Ted Koppel will moderate the discussion among current and former government leaders and an audience of about 700 people at George Mason University near Washington, D.C. BETH KLEID
