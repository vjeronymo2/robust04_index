August 7, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

The Fabulous Thunderbirds has canceled its Aug. 22 concert at the Coach House and will instead play a free show that night at the Irvine Meadows Amphitheatre. The concert, part of a summer series of free shows at the amphitheater, will begin at 7:30 p.m. Tickets for the Coach House date can be refunded at the point of purchase.
