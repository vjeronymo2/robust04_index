930429 FT 29 APR 93 / International Company News: Randcoal turns in 28% decline RANDCOAL, South Africa's second-largest coal group, reports a 28 per cent decline in first-half earnings and warns that profits for the full year will be considerably lower, writes Philip Gawith. Tighter margins saw operating profit fall by 8 per cent to R151.5m (Dollars 48.2m). Attributable income was 9 per cent down at R88m. The dividend is being reduced from 23 cents to 20 cents a share.
