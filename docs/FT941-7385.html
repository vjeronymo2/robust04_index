940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / Singapore-US fund set up The Singapore government and a US investment company are setting up an investment fund worth up to Dollars 800m (Pounds 548m) to invest in Asia's fast expanding infrastructure sector, writes Kieran Cooke in Kuala Lumpur. The government of Singapore and the state-controlled Temasek Holdings company are to join with interests managed by the American International Group, a global US financial services company.
