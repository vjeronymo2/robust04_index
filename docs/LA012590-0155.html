January 25, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

CALIFORNIA IN BRIEF;

SAN FRANCISCO;

EDISON CUSTOMERS' BILLS TO RISE 3.3%

Customers of Southern California Edison will see a 3.3% increase in their monthly electric bills starting Feb. 1, under an order issued in San Francisco by the state Public Utilities Commission.

The order will increase the typical residential customer's winter bill to $54.75 per month from $52.98. The typical monthly bill for summer will rise to $55.24 from $53.51. The rate hike will add $59.6 million to Edison's annual revenues.
