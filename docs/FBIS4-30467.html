BFN [Text] Mr. Sam Rangsi, vice chairman of the FUNCINPEC [National United Front for an Independent, Neutral, Peaceful, and Cooperative Cambodia] party and economy and finance minister of the two-headed government, stated that he supported Foreign Minister Norodom Sirivut's position that outlawing the Khmer Rouge is contrary to the king's idea. Mr. Sam Rangsi said that if the National Assembly ratifies this law next July all the budget will be used for military purposes and none will be left for national reconstruction.
