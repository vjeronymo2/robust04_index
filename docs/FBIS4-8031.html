BFN [Excerpt] Jelenia Gora, 10 Apr--The prime ministers of Poland and the Czech Republic, Waldemar Pawlak and Vaclav Klaus, met today at Czocha Castle, near Lesna in the Jelenia Gora Voivodship to discuss cooperation between the two countries. Ewa Wachowicz, press assistant to Prime Minister Pawlak, told a PAP journalist that this is a continuation of the talks commenced in Prague during the course of the visit there by U.S. President Bill Clinton.
