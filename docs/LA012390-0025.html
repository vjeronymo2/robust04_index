January 23, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY EARNINGS: BRAJDAS CORP. REPORTS 3RD QUARTER LOSS OF $416,944

Brajdas Corp., the Woodland Hills wholesale distributor of electronic components, said it recorded a loss of $416,944 in the third quarter that ended Nov. 30, compared to a net income of $265,969 a year earlier.

The company's sales for the third period fell 3% to $14.7 million from $15.2 million a year before.

For the nine-month period, Brajdas said it lost $783,346, compared to a profit of $2 million a year before. The company's nine-month sales fell 4% to $46.3 million from $48.4 million a year earlier.

Brajdas attributed the loss to weak sales compounded by an increase in some business costs.
