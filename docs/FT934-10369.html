931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / UK Company News: German Investment assets improve Net asset value per ordinary share of the German Investment Trust rose from 79.5p to 109.4p over the 12 months ended September 30. At the March 31 year-end the figure was 94.2p. Net revenue for the six months to end-September came to Pounds 137,000 (Pounds 253,000), for earnings of 0.35p (0.65p).
