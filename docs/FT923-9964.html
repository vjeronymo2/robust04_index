920801 FT 01 AUG 92 / Typist's testimony damages Collor Brazilian President Fernando Collor came closer to impeachment yesterday over charges that he received money obtained illicitly by businessman Paulo Cesar Farias, writes Christina Lamb in Rio de Janeiro. The claim of Mr Claudio Vieira, a former Collor aide, that the money came from a Dollars 5m loan raised in Uruguay in 1989, was undermined again yesterday by testimony to the Congressional Inquiry into Mr Farias. The secretary of a Sao Paulo businessman confirmed in detail that the loan was fabricated and that she had typed the documents last week.
