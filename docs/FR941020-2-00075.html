Proposal: Management Documents for Multifamily Housing Projects. Office: Housing. Description of the Need for the Information and Its Proposed Use: The Management documents for Multifamily Housing projects are needed by HUD to determine the acceptability of proposed management agents, assure compliance with program requirements, provide leverage for removing poor managers and recover excessive management fees. Form Number: HUD&hyph;9832, 9839A, 9839B, and 9839C. Respondents: Individuals or households, businesses or other for-profit, and non-profit institutions. Frequency of Submission: On occasion.
