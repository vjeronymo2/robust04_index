Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] Danish Defense Minister Hans Haekkerup arrived in Latvia today, for an official working visit. The minister is accompanied by Andres Strodbach, state secretary at the Danish Ministry of Defense, Per Samsten, head of the ministry's foreign relations department, and other officials. The Danish delegation has already met Latvia's Defense Minister Valdis Pavlovskis. Later today the Danish and Latvian ministers of defense will sign a military cooperation agreement. The Danish official also plans to meet Dainis Turlais, commander of the defense forces. The guests will be acquainted with problems facing the Latvian defense forces, and informed about the course of the Russian Army withdrawal, Latvia's wish to integrate into Europe and NATO, and possible Danish assistance with materials and equipment. The Danish delegation will also meet Prime Minister Valdis Birkavs.
