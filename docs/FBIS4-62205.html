BFN [Text] A Ukrainian delegation consisting of Ukrainian Deputy Prime Minister Valeriy Shmarov, Foreign Deputy Minister Borys Tarasyuk, and Volodymyr Horbulin, director of Ukraine's National Space Agency, has begun a working visit in Washington. The delegation members visited the U.S. National Security Council where they held consultations with Senior Director Nicholas Burns and Anthony Lake, national security adviser to the U.S. President. Major emphasis was put on issues related to the implementation of conversion projects for the Ukrainian defense industry and continued Ukrainian-U.S. cooperation in the sphere of nuclear equipment and technology. The delegation also held consultations at the U.S. State Department, where it discussed the implementation of trilateral agreements between Ukraine, Russia, and the United States, as well as other Ukrainian-U.S. agreements. Steps were outlined to hasten this process.
