Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Feb. 25 KYODO -- Xiao Rong, the third daughter of China's paramount leader Deng Xiaoping, will visit Japan on March 7-12 in connection with a book she penned about her father's life, official sources said Friday [25 February]. Xiao has served as an official in the Chinese Embassy in Washington and is a vice president of the China Association for International Friendly Contacts as well as a member of the Eighth National People's Congress, China's parliament. Xiao, who often accompanies her father, is scheduled to make television appearances and hold a press conference, the sources said.
