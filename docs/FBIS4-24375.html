BFN [Text] Beijing, May 30 (XINHUA) -- The National People's Congress (NPC), China's version of parliament, is willing to promote exchanges and expand cooperation with Poland's National Congress, NPC Chairman Qiao Shi said here today. When meeting with Jozef Oleksy, speaker of Poland's House of Representatives, and his delegation, Qiao Shi extended a welcome to the speaker, saying that Oleksy's current visit to China would provide opportunities for enhancing the understanding between the two peoples. The visit, he added, will help push forward the development of Sino-Polish traditional friendship and promote exchanges between the two congresses. There are no major conflicts of interest between China and Poland, he said, and China is ready to increase its cooperation with Poland in areas including the trade and economic fields. On China's economic situation, the NPC chairman said that only a proper handling of relations between reform, development and stability would ensure a healthy and stable growth of China's economy. Oleksy said that Poland and China share much in common and their bilateral cooperation in various fields, including economy and culture, have broad prospects for development. Oleksy and his delegation started their official good-will visit to China yesterday as guests of the NPC Standing Committee. In the morning, Zhu Liang, chairman of the NPC's Foreign Affairs Committee, held working talks with the Polish delegation.
