BFN [Text] A group headed by He Guangwei, deputy director of China's national tourism administration, arrived in Havana today in response to an invitation from Cuba's National Institute of Tourism [Intur]. XINHUA reports that this visit is part of the agreement on bilateral cooperation in tourism, and that its objective is to have the specialists of the two nations exchange experiences in the sphere of tourism. During the week they will spend here, the visitors will meet with Cuban tourism sector officials and will visit tourist installations in Varadero and Santiago de Cuba, as well as in Havana. The Chinese delegation was met at Jose Marti International Airport by representatives of Intur, of the enterprises Cubanacan S.A. and Gaviota S.A., and of the State Committee for Economic Cooperation.
