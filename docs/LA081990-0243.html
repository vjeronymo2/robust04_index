August 19, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

CRITIC'S PICKS

'THE LONELY PASSION OF JUDITH HEARNE'

Maggie Smith (pictured with Bob Hoskins) is brilliant and heartbreaking as a desperate Irish spinster thrown into spiritual crisis. Hoskins is equally fine as the man whose intentions she disastrously misreads.

(Bravo Thursday at 8 p.m., again at 1 a.m.) Kevin Thomas
