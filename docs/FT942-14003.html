940422 FT 22 APR 94 / Union rejects coal package Members of the Union of Democratic Mineworkers have rejected by 2,121 to 177 an offer by British Coal to pay each miner Pounds 6,000 as part of a package including changes in working patterns. The package, which British Coal said would not be improved, would introduce compulsory weekend work and 12-hour shifts. It would also provide redundancy payments of up to Pounds 27,000 from April 30 compared with an existing maximum of more than Pounds 45,000.
