920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Slough makes Pounds 43m deal in Germany Slough Estates, the UK property group, has made a Pounds 43m forward sale of a department store in Cologne's main shopping precinct to an unnamed German institution. This is the largest deal completed by Slough in Germany, where it has operated since 1974. The 94,000 sq ft property, bought by Slough in 1988, has been pre-let to Ludwig Beck, a Munich-based retailer, and is set to be finished in the autumn of 1993.
