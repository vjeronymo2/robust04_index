BFN [Text] Beijing, 8 Apr (XINHUA) -- A Foreign Ministry spokesman said here today: We have taken note of the report on Japanese Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa's resignation, which is Japan's internal affair. He said: "Prime Minister Hosokawa has made precious efforts for promoting the development of friendly relations between China and Japan. We hope that Sino-Japanese relations will continue their steady and healthy development." The spokesman made this statement in response to a reporter's question on reactions to Prime Minister Hosokawa's resignation.
