Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Royal Highness Crown Prince Hasan, the regent, today called his brother His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad of the fraternal Syrian Arab Republic. During the telephone conversation with President al-Asad, his royal highness expressed, in his name and on behalf of the Jordanian Government and people, his most heartfelt condolences on the death of the president's son, the late Major Basil al-Asad. His royal highness said the sorrow of brother President al-Asad and the fraternal Syrian people is the sorrow of us all. His royal highness beseeched God to bestow gracious patience and solace on His Excellency President al-Asad and his noble family, which have suffered this horrible tragedy.
