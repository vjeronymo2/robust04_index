BFN [Text] The major contenders for the Belarussian [Belarusian] presidency Aleksandr Lukashenko and Vyacheslav Kebich have collected correspondingly 44.82 percent and 17.33 percent of the votes in the course of the first round of the presidential elections in Belarus held on 23 June. According to official information from the Central Electoral Commission, their names will be put on ballot-papers for the second round of voting. The Central Electoral Commission is to announce its final decision on the date of the elections on Thursday, however, according to a preliminary opinion of the commission's leaders it will be held on Sunday, 10 June. Ivan Likhach, secretary of the Central Electoral Commission, reported to Interfax that "in the course of verification of the first round of voting the commission has not found any violations which would give reason to hold an investigation."
