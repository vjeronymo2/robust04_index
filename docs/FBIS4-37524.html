BFN [Text] 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani, Yemen's Presidential Council member and assistant secretary general of the General People's Congress, arrived in Damascus this afternoon. On hand to welcome him at Damascus International Airport was Mr. Rashid Akhtarini, deputy prime minister for services affairs. Mr. 'Abd-al-Ghani said upon arrival he was glad to be in Damascus. He also told SANA he was carrying a message from President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih to His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad about Yemeni developments and ties between the two fraternal countries.
