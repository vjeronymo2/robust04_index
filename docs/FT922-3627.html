920610 FT 10 JUN 92 / Channel rail-link review ruled out THE GOVERNMENT has no intention of reconsidering the eastern route into London chosen for the planned Channel tunnel rail link, Mr John MacGregor, transport secretary, said in a written Commons answer yesterday. His statement followed suggestions that the viability of the route could be undermined by the downturn in prospects for commercial development along the eastern route after the financial collapse of Canary Wharf.
