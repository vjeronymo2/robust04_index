BFN [TA SR report: "The Slovak Republicans Have Changed Their Name"] [Text] The Republican Bloc-Republican Party (RBRS) is the new name adopted on Saturday [7 May] by the participants in the Republican Party's Second General Assembly in Bratislava. The RBRS acknowledges the principles of neoconservatism and supports the rapid realization of economic reform. It is offering to cooperate with all right-wing parties in Slovakia. Anticommunism, guaranteeing a law-governed state, and right-wing economic reform are the main tenets of its program. Ivan Duris is RBRS chairman and Imrich Tichy is the party's honorary chairman.
