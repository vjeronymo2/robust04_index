BFN [Text] Majles Speaker Nateq-Nuri arrived in the Sri Lankan capital Colombo from Pakistan last night on the second leg of his Asian tour and was greeted by the Sri Lankan speaker and Iranians living there. During his stay in Sri Lanka, the Majles speaker will meet high-ranking Sri Lankan officials and will discuss issues of mutual interest. Sri Lanka, which is situated in south Asia, has a population of 17 million. Its most important export item is tea.
