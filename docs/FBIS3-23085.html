Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by Hasan al-Shayib] [Excerpts] In one of the largest search campaigns conducted by security forces in all governorates last week, 942 extremists and suspects, including 38 prominent terrorists, were arrested, 274 kg of explosives, nine defensive and offensive hand grenades, 924 unlicensed firearms, and 2,360 pieces of ammunition of various types were seized. [passage omitted] During the campaigns, 38 active terrorists were arrested, including fugitive Sa'id Muhammad Mahmud Hassan al-'Alami, who is suspected of being involved in the assassination of Qina Police Assistant Chief Major General 'Abd-al-Hamid Ghubarah; Chief Investigator Brigadier General Mahmud al-Dib, and State Security Officer Captain Muhammad Nada.
