November 26, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

COLORADO STATE BEATS HAWAII, 30-27

Brian Copeland scored three touchdowns, including the game-winner with 28 seconds left as Colorado State came back to beat Hawaii, 30-27, in a Western Athletic Conference football game Saturday.

The Rams, who will play Oregon in the Freedom Bowl on Dec. 29, completed their regular season at 8-4 and 6-1 in the WAC.

The Rainbows, who finish their season against Brigham Young next week, fell to 6-5 and 3-4.
