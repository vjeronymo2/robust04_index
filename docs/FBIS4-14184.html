BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn has received the following cable of condolences from the custodian of the two holy mosques, King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud: His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal, king of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, Amman, We have received the news of the death of your mother and we express to you, to your brothers, and to all the members of your family our heartfelt condolences. We pray to God almighty to have mercy on her soul and to make paradise here final abode. We are from God and to him we will return. [Signed] The custodian of the two holy mosques, Fahd Bin-'Abd- al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, king of Saudi Arabia.
