930901 FT 01 SEP 93 / World Trade News: US may lift missile sanctions THE US is willing to negotiate lifting sanctions worth Dollars 500m-Dollars 1bn (Pounds 660m) imposed last week on China and Pakistan over the sale of components of M-11 missiles, according to a senior State Department official, Nancy Dunne writes from Washington. Mr Winston Lord, assistant US secretary of state for Asia and the Pacific, yesterday said he was prepared to give China, which sold the components to Pakistan, another chance because there were areas in the guidelines to the Missiles Technology Control Regime that contained 'possible ambiguity'. China, he pointed out, had consistently denied violating the MTCR, to which it is not a signatory.
