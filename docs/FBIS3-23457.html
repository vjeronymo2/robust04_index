Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Batman, Mar. 24 (A.A.) -- A group of terrorists abducted the nephew of a mayoral candidate in southeastern Turkey on Wednesday. The Anatolian News Agency learned that a group of terrorists arrived in Bekirhan, Batman late last night and abducted Kutbettin Demir the nephew of True Path Party (DYP) mayoral candidate Yahya Demir. Faik Demir, another nephew of Yahya Demir, was also kidnapped by terrorists a while ago. Last month, the separatist terrorist organization PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] threatened violence in the southeast leading up to the local elections after the Democracy Party (DEP) announced that it was not contesting for the March 27 local elections. DEP also called on its constituents to not participate in the elections.
