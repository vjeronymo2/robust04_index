November 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BABY INSANE

It's about time there has been something written to tell people how hard gang life is on its members and how most, if given an opportunity, a genuine opportunity, would not be involved.

They are just children, most of them hardened by a life many of us couldn't imagine.

As an African-American, I would like to see and read more about real-life gang success stories. I would like to say to Baby Insane, if he should read this: Hang in there, little brother. Stay on the straight and narrow, and remember there will be many of us who are hoping and praying you continue your new life successfully.

JANICE SMITH

El Toro
