Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Wang Weijun (3769 5898 6511)] [Text] Bangkok, 7 Jan (XINHUA) -- This morning, General Woranat Aphichari, supreme commander of the Thai Armed Forces, and Major General Sombat Rotphonthong, Thai Deputy Defense Minister, respectively, met with and had cordial talks with Liu Huaqing, vice chairman of China's Central Military Commission, and his party, who are here on an official goodwill visit. During the meeting, Woranat said Liu Huaqing's goodwill visit will surely contribute to strengthening the friendly relationship between the peoples and armies of the two countries. He added that the armed forces of the two countries will further develop traditional friendship, increase exchanges, and learn from each other in the future; this, he said, will be most beneficial to both parties. Liu Huaqing said the people and the armed forces of China and Thailand maintain a good relationship and that there is no problem between them. He expressed the belief that such a relationship will surely be further enhanced and developed. During the meeting, Sombat said Liu Huaqing's visit has great significance in strengthening the friendly relationship between the people and armed forces of the two countries. Liu Huaqing briefed Sombat on China's achievements in economic construction and army building. He reiterated that China follows a good neighborly policy, will never seek hegemony, opposes power politics, and stands for settlement of disputes with friendly neighboring countries through peaceful means and dialogue. He added that both China and Thailand, to develop the economy and raise people's living standard, long for a peaceful and stable environment.
