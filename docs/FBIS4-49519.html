BFN [Text] This afternoon, Lieutenant General Choummali Sai-gnason, national defense minister of Laos, and a delegation of 16 high-ranking [Lao] military officers arrived in Beijing for a five-day official friendship visit to the PRC at the invitation of the National Defense Ministry of the PRC. The delegation had earlier stopped off and toured Chengdu, Sichuan Province, where they were accorded a warm welcome by local administrative authorities.
