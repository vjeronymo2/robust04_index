BFN [Text] Last night, President Tudjman held separate frank talks with Bosnia-Herzegovina Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic and with Kresimir Zubak, president of the Croat Republic of Herceg-Bosna presidential council. They discussed the talks in Talloires, implementation of the Washington agreement, the set-up of the Federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina, and its confederal relations with the Republic of Croatia. In the talks with Prime Minister Silajdzic, they also touched on President Tudjman's forthcoming visit to Sarajevo.
