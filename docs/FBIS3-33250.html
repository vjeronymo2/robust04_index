Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "National" newscast] [Text] In Zipaquira, Alcalis de Colombia workers took over the mayor's office and several other public offices demanding the payment of pensions and severance pay after the company was liquidated. In addition, two new guerrilla attacks were registered in the last few hours in the Departments of Cundinamarca and Meta. In La Pena two policemen were killed and six were injured. In San Juan de Arama Municipality, Meta Department, Jorge Carmelo Perez, former Villavicencio mayor and Chamber representative candidate, was kidnapped.
