BFN [Text] In Shalkarskiy Rayon of Aktyubinsk Oblast, near the existing Bazoyskiy underground gas reservoir, the Gazovoy new deposit of natural gas will be developed. Preparatory works are being carried our by the Kazakhburgaz, which is included in the state holding company Kazakh Gas. The state company Aktyubtransgas will maintain the future deposit. Certain amounts of gas will be used from here as fuel, and then Gazovoy will become one more undersurface natural gas reservoir in Aktyubinsk Oblast.
