940524 FT 24 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Eagle Trust Eagle Trust, the film and television services and distribution group, reported a reduced pre-tax loss of Pounds 5.53m for 1993, against Pounds 7.59m. Turnover fell from Pounds 84.4m to Pounds 80.9m, including Pounds 17.8m from discontinued activities. The result benefited from lower interest charges of Pounds 4.6m (Pounds 6.84m). The figures took account of the disposal of the lighting activities of the film and television section and the cessation of trading by D Pavis. There was a pre-interest profit on continuing activities of Pounds 1.92m.
