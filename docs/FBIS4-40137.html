BFN [Text] Kiev, 13 Jun -- The all-Ukrainian political union "State Independence of Ukraine" [DSU] has made public a statement. In order to guarantee peace and calm Ukraine, particularly in Crimea, the DSU demanded the following from the Supreme Council, the president, and Ukraine's leading power structures: - To return the status of Ukrainian oblast to Crimea, dissolve the Supreme Council of Crimea, and abolish the institution of the Crimean president; - To confirm that movable assets (combat ships and auxiliary vessels) and the infrastructure of the shore provision (ship repair yards, firing ranges, airports, and so on) remain the property of Ukraine; - To demand the relocation of the Black Sea Fleet personnel, which did not take the oath of allegiance to Ukraine, beyond Ukraine's borders. In the event that the Black Sea Fleet does not relocate its army beyond the borders of Ukraine, to consider the Russian military contingents as occupational forces, and to demand that Ukraine's Defense Ministry adopt all measures to renew Ukrainian authority in Crimea," reads the DSU statement.
