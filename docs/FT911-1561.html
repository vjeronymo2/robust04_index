910504 FT 04 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Lendu losses as wool and cereal prices fall Lower wool and cereal prices left Lendu Holdings with 1990 pre-tax losses of Pounds 138,088, against profits of Pounds 137,621. There was a retained profit of Pounds 188,995 after an extraordinary gain of Pounds 366,143 relating to land purchase compensation. Turnover for this company with rubber, sheep farming and grain production interests fell to Pounds 407,813 (Pounds 546,343). The pre-tax loss included lower income on investments and interest received of Pounds 180,203 (Pounds 208,269). Losses per share were 1.08p (0.89p). The directors are proposing a single final dividend of 0.3p (0.7p).
