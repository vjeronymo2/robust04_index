BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 2 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received Yang Kwidongnyo, daughter of anti-Japanese revolutionary martyr Yang Song-yong, and her companion on a visit to the homeland. Present on the occasion were Kim Chong-im, vice-director of the party history institute of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea, and officials concerned. President Kim Il-song warmly received Yang Kwidongnyo, with whom he had departed at the Qinggouzi secret camp after the Nanhutou meeting in 1936, and had a cordial conversation with her, recollecting with deep emotion Yang Song-yong, an anti-Japanese revolutionary martyr who was a commanding officer of the Wangqing guerrilla unit, and other unforgettable comrades-in-arms. Yang Kwidongnyo and her companion presented him with a gift. President Kim Il-song gave gifts to the guests and hosted a luncheon for them.
