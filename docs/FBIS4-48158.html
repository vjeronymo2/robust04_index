BFN [Text] The commander in chief of the NPFL [National Patriotic Front of Liberia], former President Charles Gankay Taylor, has called on the LNTG [Liberian National Transitional Government] to arrange for the formation of a national army to take charge of the security of the country. President Taylor has proposed that all factions should provide soldiers for the formation of the new army. He said leaving the security of the country solely in the hands of a foreign army could threaten the national security interests of Liberia.
