BFN [Report by Armonko Grgic for Herceg-Bosna from Busovaca] [Excerpt] The police station in Busovaca has informed us that a bus full of civilians that left Busovaca for Kiseljak at 0930 was turned back from the joint checkpoint in the village of Brestovsko in the Kiseljak municipality because the Muslim side would not let it through to Croat-held territory. The Muslims said that the HVO [Croatian Defense Council] would not allow free movement of humanitarian aid convoys to the Muslim side. All this was caused by an incident in Kresevo where a Muslim sniper killed an HVO soldier. The road between Busovaca and Kiseljak was opened three days ago. [passage omitted]
