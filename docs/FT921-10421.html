920206 FT 06 FEB 92 / International Capital Markets: ACIL registers ADollars 34.8m loss AUSTRALIAN Consolidated Investments (ACIL), yesterday reported net losses of ADollars 34.8m (USDollars 26.7m) for the first half to December, narrowing from ADollars 90.6m a year earlier, AP-DJ reports from Sydney. Sales fell 14 per cent to ADollars 12.6m from ADollars 14.8m, while total operating revenues fell 27.5 per cent to ADollars 36.4m from ADollars 50.2m. ACIL said the result for the six months was abnormally affected by net realised and unrealised foreign exchange losses of ADollars 17.7m. Recently, ACIL agreed to sell its 50 per cent interest in National Brewing Holdings, for ADollars 425m, to Lion Nathan of New Zealand, which owned the other 50 per cent.
