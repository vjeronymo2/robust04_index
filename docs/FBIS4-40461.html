BFN [Russian Federation President's Edict No. 1199 "On Some Measures To Stimulate Investment Activity Including Activity Involving the Enlistment of Foreign Credits"] [Text] With a view to developing national industry and supporting enterprises' investment activity I decree: 1. That it be established that enterprises, institutions, and organizations which import onto Russian Federation territory equipment, machinery, and mechanisms (except for those liable to excise duty) intended for the production development of enterprises, institutions, and organizations under contracts concluded before 1 January 1993 and registered within the established procedure are exempt in 1994 from the payment of value-added tax or the special tax for the maintenance of the most important sectors of the national economy. Sums paid before the adoption of this edict in value-added tax and special tax are subject to reimbursement to enterprises, institutions, and organizations within the procedure established by point 16 of the Russian Federation president's 22 December 1993 Edict No. 2270. 2. That it be established that value-added tax and the special tax for the maintenance of the most important sectors of the national economy are not levied when goods (except for goods liable to excise duty) are imported onto the territory of the Russian Federation within the framework of the use of credits supplied to the Russian Federation by the governments of foreign states and international finance organizations or of goods (except for goods liable to excise duty) imported to pay off state credits supplied to foreign states by the former USSR and by the Russian Federation. 3. That the Russian Federation Government submit for examination by the Russian Federation Federal Assembly the drafts of legislative acts stemming from the clauses of this edict. 4. This edict enters into force from the moment it is published. [signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin, Moscow, Kremlin, 10 June 1994.
