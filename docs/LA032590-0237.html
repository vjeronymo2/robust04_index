March 25, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

* Pacific Mutual Life Insurance Co., Newport Beach, has promoted six executives to vice presidents. They are: Judith L. Brown, policy benefits and services; Eric A. Gritzmacher, capital management; Elaine M. Havens, capital management; Robert G. Haskell, corporate and public affairs; James T. Morris, product design; and Robert D. Russell, real estate investment.
