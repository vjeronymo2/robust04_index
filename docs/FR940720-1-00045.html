Accordingly, Part 32 of Chapter I of Title 50 of the Code of Federal Regulations is proposed to be amended as follows: PART 32_-[AMENDED] 1. The authority citation for Part 32 continues to read as follows: Authority: 5 U.S.C. 301; 16 U.S.C. 460k, 664, 668dd, and 715i. 2. The use of trotlines, snag lines, soap lines, set lines, drops, gigs and jugs is not permitted. 6.
