Language: Russian Article Type:BFN ["Own information" report: "Aleksey Kazannik Not Being Transferred to Constitutional Court"] [Text] Yesterday Aleksey Kazannik met with leaders of structural subsections of the Russian General Prosecutor's Office. They discussed ways of further improving the prosecutorial system. Kazannik indicated the need to make changes in the style of work of prosecutor's office administrations and departments in the new conditions. The numerous mass media items about both the general prosecutor himself and his relations with the prosecutor's office apparatus also did not escape attention. Kazannik denied the rumors of his transfer to the Constitutional Court. The leaders of structural subsections who spoke criticized the publications in the press about the existence in the General Prosecutor's Office of some opposition to Kazannik from the apparatus' middle level, which is allegedly opposing his activities. It was declared that these mass media items did not correspond to the facts.
