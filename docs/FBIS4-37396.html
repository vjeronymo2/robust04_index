BFN [Text] Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid has again called on the Yemeni parties to cooperate with Arab and UN efforts to establish a cease-fire and put an end to bloodshed and destruction in Yemen. He expressed the hope that current efforts would succeed in bringing about a cease-fire there. Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid was speaking at a joint news conference with Lakhdar Brahimi, the UN envoy on Yemen, at the conclusion of their talks today. Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid said that the talks dealt with the latest developments in the situation. He affirmed that UN Resolution 924 and the efforts being made to stop the fighting must be supported by all means as an Arab and not just a UN responsibility. He said there was agreement to pursue contacts with the United Nations and the Yemeni parties to continue the efforts to stop the fighting.
