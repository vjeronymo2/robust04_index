Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 9 Jan (INA)--Iraq has again informed the Arab League of the continued facilities provided by the rulers of Saudi Arabia and Kuwait for the U.S., British, and French aircraft violating the Iraqi airspace, thus posing a serious threat to Iraq's security, sovereignty, and national unity. In a letter to the Arab league secretary general yesterday, Iraq's representative to the Arab League said that on 1, 2, and 3 January U.S., British, and French planes continued to violate the Iraqi airspace after flying from bases in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait. They carried out 101 hostile air sorties. The Iraqi representative enclosed a list detailing these violations.
