940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / World Trade News: GM unit wins Mercedes US contract Automotive Components Group (ACG), a unit of General Motors, announced yesterday that it has won a contract from Mercedes-Benz Project to make parts for the new four-wheel-drive vehicle which is to be built in Tuscaloosa, Alabama, AP-DJ reports from Zurich. ACG will provide cockpit modules, instrument panels and wiring systems, the announcement said.
