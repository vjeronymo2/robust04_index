Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] [Paris] Radio Monte Carlo has reported that Ahmad Jibril, secretary general of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine-General Command [PFLP-GC], has accused the U.S. Administration of deception, disinformation, and hypocrisy in its attempts to cause dissension among the allies opposing the Palestinian limited self-rule agreement. Jibril, who was speaking to AFP, continued: The objective that Washington sought to achieve through the Geneva summit was to neutralize the Syrian position so it could impose the Gaza-Jericho First agreement. However, the strength and firmness of the President al-Asad's position has foiled this objective. President al-Asad stood in defense of the Arab nation's causes and refuted all allegations. In Geneva, President al-Asad was as we have always known him -- an Arab knight faithful to the Arab nation's rights.
