941213 FT 13 DEC 94 / International Company News: Brewer posts NZDollars 93.2m loss DB Group, New Zealand's second-largest brewer which is controlled by Dutch and Singaporean brewery interests, yesterday announced a NZDollars 93.2m (USDollars 59.4m) loss for the 15 months to September 30, mainly due to its involvement in Austotel, Australia's largest pubs group. Writedowns, with redundancy and payments, accounted for NZDollars 113.8m. Operating profit was NZDollars 29.4m compared with NZDollars 16.0m the previous 12 months, for a net after-tax loss of NZDollars 93.2m, against a NZDollars 24.6m loss. Sales of NZDollars 872.9m compared with NZDollars 761.45m.
