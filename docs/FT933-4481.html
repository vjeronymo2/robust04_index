930908 FT 08 SEP 93 / Survey of Software at Work - Autumn 1993 (11): Telecentre profile Deer Park Telecentre was opened on April 27, 1993, as a service to businesses, individuals and guests at the Deer Park hotel. Software: Microsoft Works for Windows, Microsoft Power Point, Terminal Emulation. Hardware: Two DEC PC333, one Digital PC320; supplier: Digital Equipment Corporation (DEC), of Basingstoke, Hants; DEC has provided an administrator's guide for the centre and initial training in the use of the equipment. Users are charged a fixed fee per service used. Contact: Jane Yates, Tel 0404 41266; Fax 0404 46598
