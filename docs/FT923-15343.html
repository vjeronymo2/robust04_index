920702 FT 02 JUL 92 / Observer: Annual boredom Ares-Serono, the Swiss pharmaceutical group known mainly for making drugs to stimulate fertility in human beings, raised eyebrows recently by illustrating its annual report with charming photographs of Swiss cog-wheel trains going through their Alpine paces. Was this a case of an almost fanatical love for Switzerland, Fabio Bertarelli, the Italian born chief executive was asked? 'No, I was just bored with these pictures of the same stupid things in annual reports. Next year, we are going to feature Swiss water.'
