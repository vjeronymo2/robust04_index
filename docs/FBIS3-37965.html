Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yevgeniy Lents] [Text] Vladivostok February 24 TASS -- The presentation of a new public and political movement Russian Far East was held today in Vladivostok, the administrative centre of the Maritime territory. It was created on the initiative of a group of deputies to the State Duma from the Yabloko group, who are now touring the Far East. Speaking at the presentation, leader of the Yabloko parliamentary group Grigoriy Yavlinskiy stressed: "We are pinning much hope on the new movement, which should rally the reform-minded forces in the far eastern region of Russia."
