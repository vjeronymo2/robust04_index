Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 7 KYODO -- A new economic stimulus package under consideration will focus on an increase in public works spending, a government source said Friday [7 January]. The source said the ruling parties in Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa's coalition government intend to hammer out the new package by January 20. The source did not elaborate. Hosokawa promised last December to unveil in mid-January a package of new measures to boost Japan's economy, now in its worst downturn in two decades.
