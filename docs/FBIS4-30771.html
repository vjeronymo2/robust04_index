BFN [Text] After the Serbs based in Bosnia launched a sudden attack on the wider area of Zupanja in the Republic of Croatia with nine destructive shells at 1125 this morning, a general alert was sounded in Zupanja. Around midday Slavonski Samac also came under attack. According to a report from the Croatian border police, the Bosnian Serbs fired around 15 fragmentation shells on Slavonski Samac. There have been no casualties in these attacks, but huge material damage was caused. In another Serb attack on the area of Orasje and Samac [in Bosnia] several civilians were wounded and fresh material damage was caused. The Serbs attacked that area with surface-to-surface missiles that they fired exclusively on civilian targets and business facilities.
