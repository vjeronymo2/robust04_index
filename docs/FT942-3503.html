940614 FT 14 JUN 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea A very strong demand prevailed, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Bright East Africans advanced 5 to 10 pence and sometimes more, while mediums were fully firm to dearer. Selected brighter Ceylons were well supported, plainer sorts were mainly easier. Offshore teas met selective demand. Brightest Africans met active support and moved dearer, others were irregular and sometimes easier. Brighter Ceylons were about steady but other types were easier where sold. Quotations: best available 250p/kg, nom good 165p/kg, good medium 142p/kg, medium 118p/kg and low medium 83p/kg.
