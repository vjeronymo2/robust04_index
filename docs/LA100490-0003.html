October 4, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

FEINSTEIN, WILSON CAMPAIGNS FOR GOVERNORSHIP

Bill Stall's comprehensive article on Wilson, (Sept. 24) omitted mentioning a timely vote by the senator a few years ago when, following an appendectomy operation, he was brought by ambulance from the hospital and into the Senate chamber on a gurney in order to provide the deciding vote against an increase in Social Security allowances for the elderly.

FRED R. HOFELD

Los Angeles
