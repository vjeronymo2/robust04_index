December 10, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

NEWPORT BEACH'S SMITH WINS S.D. MARATHON

Kathy Smith of Newport Beach and Benjamin Paredez Martinez of Mexico were crowned winners Sunday in the San Diego Marathon at the Oceanside pier, earning $2,000 each. Smith finished in 2 hours 43 minutes 5 seconds, a course record. Martinez, 29, finished in 2:19.3, also a course record.
