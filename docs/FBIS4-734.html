BFN [Excerpts] The National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA] once again fired on Cuito's youth today. Its soldiers and commanders do not seem to have been happy with what they did yesterday. As if to complete the task, today they launched shells onto a group of youths commemorating 14 April, youth day. Our correspondent Faria Horacio reports from Cuito: [Begin Horacio recording] [passage omitted] About 1100 [1000 GMT] today, at a time when the youth of this city assembled in front of installations of the Popular Movement for the Liberation of Angola youth wing to commemorate 14 April, youth day, UNITA rebels once again shelled Cuito, killing three people and seriously wounding 14 children aged between five and 14. [passage omitted] [end recording]
