Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Belarusian Parliament today approved 43-year-old Ivan Bambiza as deputy parliament speaker. Bambiza was born in the Gomel Region, graduated from the agricultural institute in 1975. He occupies the post of secretary of the planning and budget and financial commission of the republic's parliament. The parliament has approved Gennadiy Levitskiy as chairman of the KGB committee. Earlier General Levitskiy was deputy chairman of the KGB, then chief of the counterintelligence service. First Vice-Premier Mikhail Myasnikovich proposed to appoint Vladimir Danko as Belarusian Interior Minister, who earlier worked as First Deputy Interior Minister. Danko was approved as Interior Minister by a majority vote.
