930707 FT 07 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Evans of Leeds virtually unchanged at Pounds 8.79m Evans of Leeds, the family-controlled north of England-based property company, yesterday reported virtually static profits of Pounds 8.79m pre-tax, against Pounds 8.52m, for the year to end-March. Net asset value was slightly lower at 196p (201p) per share. Mr Bill Gibson, director, said: 'Property valuations slightly went down. Adjusting for sales and acquisitions the fall is less than 2 per cent.' Gross rental income totalled Pounds 19.2m (Pounds 18.3m). Net rent, after property expenses, reached Pounds 18.1m (Pounds 16.9m). Income from the sale and development of property dipped to Pounds 4.02m (Pounds 4.87m). Interest accounted for Pounds 9.09m (Pounds 9.01m). The company ended the year with gearing at 53 (58) per cent. A proposed final dividend of 3.14p makes a 4.57p (4.15p) total. Earnings per share improved to 11.36p (10.67p).
