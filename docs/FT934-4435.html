931203 FT 03 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Wrexham Water ahead at Pounds 1.74m Profits of the Wrexham and East Denbighshire Water Company rose from Pounds 1.07m to Pounds 1.74m pre-tax for the half year ended September 30. The improvement mainly reflected the completion of remedial work at the company's raw water reservoirs in 1992-93. Turnover expanded from Pounds 5.15m to Pounds 5.52m. The interim dividend on the participating ordinary stock is lifted from 40p to 46p.
