930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / UK Company News: William Bedford William Bedford, the USM-traded antique dealer and restorer, recorded a reduced deficit of Pounds 10,898 for the first half of 1993. The improvement from the comparable loss of Pounds 21,731 came on turnover down to Pounds 651,556 (Pounds 914,179). Mr John Bedford, chairman, said the first five months of the year proved to be 'the most difficult trading period we have ever experienced'. Losses per share were 0.2p (0.5p).
