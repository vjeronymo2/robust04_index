Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Azerbaijani Prosecutor-General Ali Omerov claimed that the Azerbaijani People's Front [APF] headed by Elchibey had been preparing to topple President Geydar Aliyev in a coup on 5 March. For this reason, 100 APF members have been detained since last Sunday. It was also noted that 42 rockets, explosives and an arms cache was discovered during a search of a building used by APF supporters. The APF denied claims of a coup. One of the APF leaders, Nadzhaf Nadzhafov, claimed that the seized arms do not belong to APF. The police raided the news conference being held by Nadzhaf Nadzhafov at the APF headquarters in Baku, and sealed off the building.
