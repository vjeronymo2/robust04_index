BFN [Text] Talks between the representatives of the government of Tajikistan and the Tajik opposition resumed at 1100 today in Moscow. (Ramiro Perez) Ballon, the UN secretary-general's special envoy, and Andrey Kozyrev, Russian foreign minister, while addressing the talks yesterday considered this activity a new stage of peace and tranquillity in Tajikistan. Shukurjon Zuhurov, minister of labor and employment and head of the representatives of Tajikistan, expressed the hope and wishes of the legitimate government of Tajikistan. He pointed out that the government of Tajikistan is taking all measures to return all those people who have fled the country as a result of well-known events. Otakhon Latifi spoke on behalf of the opposition. We hope that the good intentions of our newly independent nation will come true at these talks and our compatriots will return to their permanent places of residence.
