921127 FT 27 NOV 92 / UK Company News: 38% rise to Pounds 1.55m for Scantronic Further progress within all its principal markets enabled Scantronic Holdings to lift pre-tax profit 38 per cent, from Pounds 1.11m to Pounds 1.55m, in the six months ended September 30 1992. The result was achieved on sales 10 per cent better at Pounds 18.5m (Pounds 16.8m). They were split as to Pounds 14.5m in Europe and nearly Pounds 4m in North America, while the operating contributions were profit Pounds 1.73m and loss Pounds 15,000 respectively. In North America profitability had been improved substantially. Earnings per share rose to 1.84p (1.01p). The interim dividend is again 0.79p.
