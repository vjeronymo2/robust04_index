BFN [Text] In a statement on Voice of the Arabs Radio today, Dr. 'Abd-al-Aziz al-Dali, member of the politburo of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] and secretary of the YSP Central Committee, called for the need to stop the fighting first and then to separate the combatants. He stressed to Egyptian President Husni Mubarak that he was convinced of the importance of giving the Arab League and the Arab brothers latitude for finding a solution to the Yemeni crisis. It is worth mentioning that Brother Dr. al-Dali has delivered a number of letters from Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, the deputy chairman of the Presidential Council and secretary general of the YSP Central Committee, to his brother leaders of fraternal Arab states.
