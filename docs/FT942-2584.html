940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Microgen Microgen Holdings has paid Pounds 1.5m cash to acquire Scottish Computer Services from Clydesdale Bank. Assets of Glasgow-based SCS were about Pounds 1m at the end of the year to September 30. It made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 35,325 (Pounds 901,001 loss) on turnover of Pounds 2.87m (Pounds 3.12m).
