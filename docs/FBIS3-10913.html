Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Interior Ministry this morning issued a communique, addressed to the public, on the events scheduled for 15 March. The communique notes that in view of the various demonstrations by various sectors of society, the authorities responsible for domestic security and public order report -- in keeping with the people's constitutional rights and guarantees -- that they will guarantee the people's right to peacefully meet and demonstrate. The communique also points out that the deployment of police patrols during these demonstrations will serve to protect the demonstrators and others who have nothing to do with the demonstration and will prevent acts of vandalism against public and private interests. The communique ends by reminding those participating in the demonstrations that they must act within the limits established by their rights and prerogatives, without violating the rights of third parties and that the sponsors of these demonstrations are responsible for the development and outcome of the demonstration and the collective attitude of those participating.
