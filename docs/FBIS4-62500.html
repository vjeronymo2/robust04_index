BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 17 May (ITAR-TASS) -- The situation on the Tajik-Afghan border has undergone virtually no significant change and remains tense. Military sources report that on 16 May Russian border guards foiled two attempts by small groups of Tajik [opposition] fighters to cross the border in the Pyandzh and Khorog sectors. A day earlier, in the Shuroabad sector of the Moskovskiy border troop detachment, Russian border guards eliminated a group of Tajik fighters who penetrated into Tajikistan in order to perpetrate acts of sabotage. In the same sector, Russian border guards discovered a major clandestine arsenal of rockets belonging to the Tajik mujaheddin.
