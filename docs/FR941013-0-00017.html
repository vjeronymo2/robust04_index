The FAA estimates that 944 engines installed on aircraft of U.S. registry will be affected by this AD, that it will take approximately an average of 4 work hours, based on fleet configuration mix, per engine to accomplish the required actions, and that the average labor rate is $55 per work hour. Required parts will cost approximately $7,235 per engine. Based on these figures, the total cost impact of the AD on U.S. operators is estimated to be $7,037,520.
