941013 FT 13 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Blue chip Canadian stocks led a strong rally in Toronto, fuelled by expectations of strong third-quarter earnings on both sides of the border. Big name companies such as Alcan, Magna, Methanex and Newbridge Networks all posted solid gains. Analysts said growing confidence in the US over earnings prospects has been buoying the markets there, and the spillover is helping Canadian markets. Investors will be watching for today's US September producer prices and tomorrow's consumer prices to get a reading on the strength of the US economy and an inkling of where interest rates are headed. The TSE 300 index finished 39.6 ahead at 4,355.0 after reaching 4,363.4, and rises led falls by 435 to 348 after volume of 65.4m shares.
