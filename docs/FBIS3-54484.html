Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Jan (MENA)--Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat's flight from Cairo to Tunis was canceled tonight because of bad weather in Tunis. 'Arafat headed for Cairo Airport late tonight to fly to Tunis after a two-day visit to Egypt. But strong winds were reported around Tunis Airport that would delay the landing there. The trip was postponed until tomorrow morning.
