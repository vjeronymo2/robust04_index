931231 FT 31 DEC 93 / International Company News: Electrolux Electrolux, the Swedish white goods maker, is to exercise an option for a further 10 per cent stake in AEG Hausgeraete, bringing its total share to 20 per cent, Reuter reports from Stockholm. AEG Hausgeraete is the domestic goods unit of AEG. Electrolux said it had been offered the chance to buy all shares in AEG Hausgeraete by AEG on December 8. 'Negotiations over a final agreement on the basis of AEG's offer are continuing,' Electrolux said. 'Pending the outcome of these negotiations, Electrolux has decided to exercise its option, which means that Electrolux will from the new year own 20 per cent of the shares in AEG Hausgeraete,' it said.
