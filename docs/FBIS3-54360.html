Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Resistance fighters this morning attacked the Bra'shit position with machine guns and rockets. Following the attack, Israeli troops shelled Bra'shit village, killing a boy named Muhammad Kamal Shihab and wounding a woman, Wajihah Ibrahim Farhat, who was taken to Beirut for treatment, and a student, Murtada Zayn al-Din.
