BFN [Text] A Myanmar [Burmese] delegation led by Brigadier General Abel, minister of national planning and economic development, returned to Yangon [Rangoon] by UB-222 flight this afternoon after attending the GATT organization's ministerial meeting on Uruguay trade negotiations held in Marrakech, Morocco, and the phase-3 meeting on upgrading economic cooperation among six regional countries which was sponsored by the National Planning Committee of Vietnam and the Asian Development Bank and held in Hanoi. In Marrakech, Brig. Gen. Abel, leader of the Myanmar delegation and minister of national planning and economic development, signed the final agreement of the Uruguay Round and an agreement on subjects [bathayat saing-ya sagyoke]. He also acted as a chairman and delivered an address in Marrakech.
