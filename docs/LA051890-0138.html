May 18, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

FOR SALE: The $17.6-million Irvine Theatre, due to open in October at UCI, is up for sale -- seat by seat. . . . A $1,000 donation will get your name on one of the upholstered chairs, says executive director Douglas C. Rankin. "We're hoping to raise at least $500,000 this way." . . . For a gift of only $999,000 more, you can get your name on the entire auditorium -- museum directors still don't have a million-dollar donor. Mark I. Pinsky and Jan Herman
