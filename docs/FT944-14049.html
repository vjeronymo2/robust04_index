941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / Jobseeker scheme The government unveiled its Jobseeker's Allowance yesterday, estimating a Pounds 100m net cut in spending on unemployment in its first financial year. Mr Peter Lilley, social security secretary, said it would 'help Britain generate more jobs and give better value for taxpayers' money', but Mr John Monks, TUC general secretary, condemned it as 'mean-spirited'.
