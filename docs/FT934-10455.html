931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / Kvaerner buys Scots companies NORWAY'S Kvaerner group has agreed to acquire two Aberdeen-based offshore engineering companies - Aberdeen Marine Oil Development and McAlpine Humberoak - at an overall cost of about Pounds 4.7m. The acquisitions follow Kvaerner's purchase of the UK onshore and offshore engineering businesses of Humphreys & Glasgow and Earl and Wright in February 1992 for Pounds 15.5m to form Kvaerner H&G Offshore.
