940621 FT 21 JUN 94 / Flood toll tops 400 People in the small town of Yingde, in China's southern Guangdong province use a boat to make their way down the street after severe flooding which has caused many deaths. The floods rose higher yesterday as rains lashed the southern provinces of Hunan, Guangdong and Guangxi. State media assessments at the weekend put total deaths in excess of 400, with an estimated 160,000 homes destroyed and economic losses running into the tens of millions of dollars, and the lives of 20m people affected.
