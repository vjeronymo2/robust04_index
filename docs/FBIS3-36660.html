Language: English Article Type:BFN [By KHOVAR-TASS] [Text] Dushanbe, February 8 -- A volunteer of the international nongovernmental organisation "Physicians Without Frontiers" [PWF] [Doctors Without Borders] was killed in southern Tajikistan by unidentified gunmen, the national news agency KHOVAR reported today. The man was a Tajik national who had worked for the organisation for three weeks only. Organisation coordinator Bruno Meesen told KHOVAR that the man, whose name was not disclosed, was killed last Thursday when a truck with PWF insignia was carrying building materials. The truck was stopped by gunmen who shot and killed the volunteer. The driver and an assistant expert, also Tajik nationals, escaped unscathed.
