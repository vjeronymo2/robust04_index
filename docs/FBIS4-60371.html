BFN [Text] The Knesset has defeated the opposition's motions of no confidence in the government. Our correspondent Hayim Hecht reports that in the vote on 'Arafat's Johannesburg remarks, the coalition was supported by 50 Knesset members [MK's], versus 44 for the opposition. In the vote on the United Torah Jewry's no-confidence motion on the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] troops withdrawal from Gaza on a Sabbath and a holiday, 50 MK's supported the government and 42 supported the opposition. Our correspondent notes that the YI'UD [Independent and Democratic Israel] MK's abstained in this vote, the SHAS [Torah Observing Sephardim] MK's were absent, while SHAS MK Yosef 'Azran voted with the opposition.
