Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 17 (XINHUA) -- The State Council, China's highest governing body, has issued its 151st decree, on the implementation of regulations on rewarding people for teaching achievements. The 16-article regulations, which was signed by Chinese Premier Li Peng and made known today, will reward collective units and individuals. The aim is to encourage more teachers to go in for research in education and improve concrete teaching activities. The awards are divided into four grades, special and first, second and third classes, and the winners will be presented with certificates and medals as well as monetary awards. All schools and universities, academic bodies and other social organizations, as well as teachers and other individuals can apply for prizes for teaching achievements in accordance with the stipulations stated in the regulations.
