941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Riva refinancing Riva Group, the USM-quoted supplier of electronic point of sale systems, has completed arrangements with its bankers which improve the value of shareholders' funds by Pounds 1.4m and reduce gearing. The profit and loss account should also benefit by about Pounds 1.3m. FennoScandia Bank, Securum Finance and Mr Tom Milne, a former director, will be repaid Pounds 911,000, with the Pounds 1.31m balance of the debt to FennoScandia and Securum waived. The Co-operative Bank is providing funds and increasing its facilities to the group. It will also subscribe Pounds 100,000 for the issue of 888,889 new ordinary shares at 11 1/4 p against a market price of 10p. This will reduce Riva's debt to it.
