BFN [Text] Bonn -- Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel considers Hungary's and Poland's membership applications to be one of the central issues during Germany's European Union [EU] presidency. Following Hungary's recent application, Poland was the second of the Central and East European countries to apply for membership on Friday [8 April]. He will consider himself as advocate of these applications since political and economic stability in the Central and East European states is "in our very own interest," Kinkel said.
