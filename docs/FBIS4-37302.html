BFN [Text] Lebanese Army units continue to be deployed in specific areas in [Jabal Lubnan]. Military sources told the NATIONAL NEWS AGENCY that the deployment process will continue for three days. They said that approximately 8,000 officers and personnel from the 3d, 6th, 10th, and 11th brigades and the 3d intervention regiment have been deployed in several villages beginning from Qabb Ilyas. The Lebanese Army Guidance Directorate issued the following statement: Within the framework of implementing the council of ministers' resolutions relating to consolidating security, extending the state authority, and returning displaced people to their homes and villages, at dawn today the Lebanese Army began the final deployment operations in Jabal Lubnan from the al-Awwali river in the south and the Upper al-Matn area in the north. The objectives and tasks of this operation are to create a suitable and permanently secure atmosphere and a return to a normal life for the return of the displaced people to their homes, and to strengthen national coexistence, which is one of the main components of accord. On this occasion, the army command calls on the citizens, both returnees and residents, to contribute to this historic event, bring about family reunions, renounce past grudges, and aspire to a prosperous future for themselves and their sons under the umbrella of security, law, and justice.
