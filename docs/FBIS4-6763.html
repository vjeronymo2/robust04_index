BFN ["Message from the Royal Government to His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk"; dated 22 April -- read by announcer] [Text] Your Majesty: After receiving your 22 April statement, both of us, the first and second prime ministers of the Royal Government of Cambodia [RGC], would like to declare our support for your initiative to convene a roundtable meeting for peace and national reconciliation at the Royal Palace in Phnom Penh from 2 to 7 May 1994. We are ready to participate in the meeting to be held under Your Majesty's lofty chairmanship. Your Majesty and revered queen, please accept our true loyalty and highest respects. [Signed] First Prime Minister Norodom Ranariddh; Second Prime Minister Hun Sen. [Dated] Phnom Penh, 22 April 1994.
