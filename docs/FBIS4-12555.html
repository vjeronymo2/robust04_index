BFN [Text] A meeting between a Syrian economic delegation led by Dr. 'Abd-al-Rahim Subay'i, [minister of state for planning affairs], and an Austrian economic delegation led by Dr. (Erich Betman), assistant minister for economic affairs at the Ministry of Public Economy and Transport, was held at the State Planning Commission this morning. Talks focused on ways to promote and enhance bilateral economic, trade, and technical relations by benefiting from the large available capabilities. The two sides stressed the importance of establishing joint firms in fields of common interest, as well as benefiting from Austrian experience by training Syrian cadres and promoting bilateral trade exchanges. The meeting was attended by representatives from the concerned ministries, establishments, and chambers of commerce, as well as Syrian and Austrian businessmen.
