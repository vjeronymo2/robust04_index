BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Andrey Bychkov and Andrey Krylov] [Text] Moscow June 16 Tass -- "I hope that all questions concerning cooperation between Russia and Vietnam will be resolved in the course of the present round of talks between Russia and Vietnam," Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin said at the start of his talks with Vietnamese counterpart Vo Van Kiet in Moscow. A source close to the government of the Russian Federation told ITAR-TASS that "the road is now clear" to the signing of the treaty of friendly relations between the Russian Federation and the Socialist Republic of Vietnam. This major document and a number of specific Vietnamese-Russian agreements are expected to be signed after Viktor Chernomyrdin and Vo Van Kiet wind up their talks.
