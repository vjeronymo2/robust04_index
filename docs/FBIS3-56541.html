Language: English Article Type:BFN [By BELINFORM correspondent for TASS] [Text] Minsk January 15 TASS -- Upon the completion of his negotiations with Belarusian Leader Stanislav Shushkevich and Prime Minister Vyacheslav Kebich, U.S. President Bill Clinton met representatives of Belarusian opposition parties and movements. The meeting was attended by leader of the Belarusian National Front Zenon Poznyak and Chairman of the Belarusian United Democratic Party Aleksandr Dobrovolskiy.
