Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 10 Jan (WAKH)--United Arab Emirates President His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan has received a message from Russian President Boris Yeltsin, dealing with relations of friendship between the two countries and means of promoting them in all fields. Shaykh Hamdan Bin-Zayid Al Nuhayyan, minister of state for foreign affairs, received the message when he met today with Oleg Derkovskiy, the Russian ambassador to the United Arab Emirates.
