Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Belgrade, 29 Jan (TANJUG) -- Zoran Djindjic, hitherto chairman of the Democratic Party's [DS] executive committee is the party's new president beginning this evening. The extraordinary assembly of the DS, which has been in session since this morning [29 January], elected Djindjic as the party's new president this evening. The session was attended by 357 delegates and 286 voted in favor of Djindjic. [passage omitted]
