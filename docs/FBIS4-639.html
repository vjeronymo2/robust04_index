BFN [Excerpt] A new political party has been formed in Mali, namely the Malian Alliance for Democracy and Progress-Faso Dambe [Alliance Malienne pour la Democratie et le Progres-Faso Dambe]. The party, which was legalized on 24 February, was founded by former activists of the Party for Democracy and Progress [PDP]. Its secretary general is Karim Traore. [passage omitted]
