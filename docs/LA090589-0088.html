September 5, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: COUNTYWIDE;

NEW GROUP TO PROMOTE PLANTING OF MORE TREES

Treepeople, an international organization dedicated to tree planting and preservation, will organize a county chapter to increase the number of trees here by 80 in the next 15 years.

The new Tree Society of Orange County will hold its first meeting at 2 p.m. Sept. 17 in the Ruby Gerontology Center at Cal State Fullerton.

Sponsored by the Fullerton Arboretum, the county chapter will organize tree plantings and present information on fast-growing trees.

More information is available by calling Tree Society President Nancy Bruland at (714) 771-1403 or vice president Tom Larson at (714) 651-9601.
