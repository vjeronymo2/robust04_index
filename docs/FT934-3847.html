931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Vega benefits from contracts expansion Vega Group saw interim profits jump by an effective 20 per cent as the software and systems engineering company won new contracts in the space and aerospace sector and moved into new markets. On turnover ahead to Pounds 4.52m (Pounds 3.62m), the pre-tax line for the six months to October 31 advanced to Pounds 757,000. The restated comparable figure of Pounds 404,000 was struck after an exceptional charge of Pounds 227,000 related to the expenses of its Stock Exchange flotation in June 1992. The group's cash balance at the period end amounted to Pounds 782,000 (Pounds 191,000). The interim dividend goes up to 1.04p (0.87p), payable from earnings of 3.54p (1.34p).
