Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Feb. 24 -- Vice Prime Minister Phan Van Khai received here yesterday Mr. Eisei Ito, vice minister of construction of Japan who is on a working visit to Vietnam. Speaking to his guest, Vice Prime Minister Phan Van Khai expressed his satisfaction at the quick and fine development of the relations between Vietnam and Japan. Mr. Khai highly appreciated the official development aid (ODA) given by the Japanese Government to some Vietnamese key projects. He hailed the further promotion of cooperation between the two Construction Ministries. For his part, Mr Ito expressed his pleasure at multi-faceted achievements recorded by the Vietnamese people in the present period of renovation. Mr. Ito wished that the cooperation between the two Construction Ministries would be further promoted and an exchange of personnel would be implemented. Also present at the reception were Vietnamese Vice Minister of Construction Pham Sy Liem and Japanese Ambassador to Vietnam Hiroyuki Yushita.
