[Declaration of Disaster Loan Area # 2748; Amendment # 2] Texas; Declaration of Disaster Loan Area The above-numbered Declaration is hereby amended, effective November 8, 1994, to establish the incident period for this disaster as beginning on October 14, 1994 and continuing through November 8, 1994. All other information remains the same, i.e., the termination date for filing applications for physical damage is December 19, 1994 and for economic injury the deadline is July 19, 1995. (Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance Program Nos. 59002 and 59008) Dated: November 21, 1994. Alexander W. Moore; Acting Associate Administrator for Disaster Assistance.
