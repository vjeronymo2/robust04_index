920410 FT 10 APR 92 / UK Company News: Textile slide pulls down Beckman A fall in pre-tax profit from Pounds 686,000 to Pounds 556,000 was reported by A Beckman for the half year to December 31. From turnover of Pounds 5.03m (Pounds 6.02m) the textiles side saw its trading profit slide to Pounds 173,000 (Pounds 353,000). Trading profit in property came to Pounds 574,000 (Pounds 492,000) on turnover of Pounds 779,000 (Pounds 720,000). Earnings per share share fell to 3.1p (3.8p) and the interim dividend is again 1.63p.
