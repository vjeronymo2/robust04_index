August 13, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: WEIGHTY MATTER

The casting call was for a woman of 5-foot-4, size 22, about 230-260 pounds -- a Roseanne Barr body double for "She Devil." There was a catch: She had to be nude.

Plus Models Ltd. of NYC -- which specializes in larger-size models -- took the call from the film's production office. The Orion comedy, which just wrapped in Gotham, stars Meryl Streep as a romance novelist who comes between clumsy housewife Barr and hubby, Ed Begley Jr. -- leading Barr to plot revenge.

"But we couldn't find any girls who were interested (or the right size)," said Plus Models prez Pat Swift.

"Most of our girls start at 5-foot-8 1/2. Then there's the fact they wanted the girl to pose nude. You know, the bigger a girl gets, the more reluctant she tends to be about taking her clothes off."

Swift also said the potential pay was well below usual for a day's nude/semi-nude work -- an initial offer of $125, later raised to $175.

We aren't sure if a size 22 body double was ever found, or filmed. At press time, calls to the film's production office, Orion and Barr's publicist had not been returned. Pat H. Broeske
