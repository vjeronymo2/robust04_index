BFN [Text] The Finnish Foreign Ministry has granted supplementary aid totaling 8.7 million Finnish markkas to the former Yugoslavia. The aid will be channeled via the Red Cross, the church's Development Aid, and the Mission's Aid to Developing Countries. Finland has granted a total of 45.7 million Finnish markkas in aid to the former Yugoslavia during the past three years. This year's allocation was 40 million markkas before the supplementary allocation was granted.
