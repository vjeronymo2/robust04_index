941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Contracts Samsung Aerospace Industries and 31 other South Korean companies have formed a consortium to make commercial aircraft with China. A 100-seat pilot aircraft will be manufactured by 1998 at a cost of Dollars 1.2bn, to be shared between the Chinese side and the South Korean consortium. Members of the consortium include Korean Air, Daewoo Heavy Industries, and Hyundai Corp's Hyundai Technology Development.
