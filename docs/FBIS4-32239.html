BFN [Report by "krzem": "Clinton With the Ten"] [Text]President Clinton's July visit to Warsaw will be an opportunity to hold a ministerial regional summit meeting of East and Central Europe. Minister Andrzej Olechowski said yesterday that he and U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher have invited foreign ministers of nine countries -- the Visegrad Group, Albania, Bulgaria, Romania, Macedonia, and Slovenia -- to come to Warsaw on 7 July. Bill Clinton will be in Poland on 6 and 7 July. Among other issues, the ministers will discuss the region's security, including "Partnership for Peace," and their countries' relations with NATO.
