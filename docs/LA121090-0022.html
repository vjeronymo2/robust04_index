December 10, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Fat Boys Charged: A member of the rap group The Fat Boys and two members of the group's entourage have been charged with sexually abusing a minor at a suburban Philadelphia party. Singer Darren (Buffy) Robinson, 23; his brother and manager, Curtis Robinson, 27; and a bodyguard, Pell (Pony Boy) Wimeley, 31, all of New York City, face a Dec. 20 preliminary hearing. The three were freed on bond after they surrendered to police Wednesday. CLAUDIA PUIG
