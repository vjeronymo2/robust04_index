941103 FT 03 NOV 94 / World News in Brief: Renault sets share price for privatisation issue Renault shares will go on sale to the public today at FFr165 (Dollars 32.18) a share, valuing the company at slightly under FFr40bn (Pounds 4.7bn). The state is selling nearly 30 per cent of Renault's capital but will retain a 50.1 per cent stake. The sale to institutions will end today while the public offering will last until next Thursday.
