BFN [From the "Biznews" program] [Text] Russia can sell 3,000 tonnes of natural uranium per year on the American market for the next two years. This amendment to the anti-dumping agreement for uranium has been adopted by Moscow and Washington. However, the present amendment has a number of restrictions. In particular, the Russian side has the right to sell only 50 percent of the volume indicated in a contract. The remainder of the supplies are carried out by American producers. In addition to this, Russia can sell 500 tonnes of natural uranium on the U.S. market per year at a price of not less than $13 a pound. At present the price of natural uranium on the American market is approximately $10.
