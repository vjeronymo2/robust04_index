January 4, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

SOUTHLAND;

BABY-SITTER, HUSBAND ARRESTED IN DEATH OF 2 1/2-MONTH-OLD GIRL

A 61-year-old Van Nuys baby-sitter and her 75-year-old husband were in jail this morning after an infant in their care died and investigators found possible signs of child abuse, Los Angeles police reported.

Theresa and Jose del Gado were arrested on suspicion of murder Wednesday night after 2 1/2-month-old Evelyn Figueroa stopped breathing in their apartment and was later pronounced dead at Panorama Community Hospital, said Detective Jim Vojtecky.

The couple were being held without bail in the Van Nuys jail.

Investigators were called to the hospital at 5:30 p.m. after staff members found bruises on the head and face of the child, Vojtecky said. Vojtecky said the cause of the child's death is unknown and the results of an autopsy will likely determine whether the Del Gados will be charged with murder.

"The injuries appear minor," the detective said. "We are talking about slight bruising. When you are dealing with a young child, it is difficult" to determine if bruises are fatal injuries.

Vojtecky said the Del Gados denied injuring the child, who was left in their care by her mother, Susana Espina, 24.
