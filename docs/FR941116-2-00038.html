SUMMARY: There will be a 2&hyph;day meeting of the Federal Insecticide, Fungicide, and Rodenticide Act (FIFRA), Scientific Advisory Panel (SAP) to review a set of scientific issues being considered by the Agency in connection with a draft proposal rule for 40 CFR part 158, Pesticide Registration Data Requirements. Much of the proposed rule would implement changes in practice already made by the Office of Pesticide Programs in the course of registration and reregistration.
