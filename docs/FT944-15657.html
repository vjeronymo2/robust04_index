941017 FT 17 OCT 94 / Company News (This Week): Credito Italiano - Other companies The recently privatised Italian bank launches an issue of new shares and bonds today to raise up to L1,520bn (Dollars 965m) for future acquisitions. The discounted share issue is priced at L1,500 a share, against a current market price of about L1,970. The exercise of warrants attached to both bonds and shares could raise a further L1,120bn by the end of 1997.
