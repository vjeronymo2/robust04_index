931016 FT 16 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Recession leaves Baris on Pounds 9,000 Baris Holdings, the USM-quoted building services company, reported profits of Pounds 9,000 for the half year to August 31, against losses of Pounds 70,000 which included exceptional costs of Pounds 358,000. Continued recession in the construction industry had left a reduced level of work with turnover down at Pounds 8.14m (Pounds 11.3m). The company said further progress had been made in reducing operating costs in line with the lower workload.
