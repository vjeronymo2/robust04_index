931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Courtyard Leisure Courtyard Leisure, the wine bar and restaurant operator, reported increased losses of Pounds 578,000 for the year to March 31, against Pounds 288,000. The result was struck after a provision of Pounds 195,000 against the loss on the sale of a wine bar. Turnover was Pounds 2.22m (Pounds 1.9m). Losses per share were 5.4p (3.1p). Mr Ali Safa, chairman, said that trading was difficult. Turnover increased because of the acquisition of a wine bar in the City but its performance was hit by the bombing of the nearby Baltic Exchange.
