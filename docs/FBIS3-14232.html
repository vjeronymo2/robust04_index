Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The closure on the territories will continue until after the Passover holiday [2 April], which begins on Saturday night [27 March]. Police Commissioner Rafi Peled told Qol Yisra'el that when the holiday ends, the security forces will reassess the situation. But in the meantime, he said, the closure has calmed things down in the territories. He said that when it is lifted, the security situation is expected to get worse, particularly in Jerusalem.
