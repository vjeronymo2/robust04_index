941111 FT 11 NOV 94 / International Company News: Prudential Securities agreement postponed The Ecuadorean government has postponed the signing of an agreement with Prudential Securities, the brokerage arm of US life insurance company Prudential Insurance, after it admitted that it had misled investors in selling limited partnerships, writes Raymond Colitt in Quito. Prudential had been named as the investment bank advising the government over its planned sale of the state airline, Ecuatoriana. Government officials said Prudential Securities was sending a delegation to Ecuador to explain the situation.
