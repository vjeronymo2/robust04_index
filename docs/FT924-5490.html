921126 FT 26 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Dunedin Worldwide net assets edge up The net asset value of Dunedin Worldwide Investment Trust edged ahead from 595.3p to 603p per share - over the 12 months to October 31. Net revenue dipped to Pounds 3.3m (Pounds 3.46m), equivalent to earnings of 9.71p (10.17p) per share. The total dividend is held at 9.5p, via a maintained final of 7.1p.
