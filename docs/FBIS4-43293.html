BFN [Text] Federal Economics Minister Guenther Rexrodt has called on Iran to continue promptly settling the claims of German creditors. It will only make sense to consider holding another meeting of the German-Iranian Economic Commission once the debt rescheduling agreement that was concluded in February has been fulfilled, Rexrodt said at a meeting with Foreign Minister Velayati in Bonn. According to official information, Rexrodt and Velayati agree that both sides are interested in the further development of economic relations.
