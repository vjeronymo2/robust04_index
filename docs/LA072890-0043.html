July 28, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

DIGEST LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: NEW POSTMASTER FOR RESEDA NAMED

A 17-year veteran of the U.S. Postal Service was installed Friday as the new postmaster for Reseda.

Alan M. Taylor will be responsible for all postal operations for the Reseda Post Office, which delivers about 57 million pieces of mail to 70,000 customers along 46 mail routes, Postal Service spokesman John Conte said.

Previously, Taylor served as the manager of customer service for the Glendale Post Office, Conte said.
