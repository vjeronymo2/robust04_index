Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] Lieutenant General Khin Nyunt, secretary-1 of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] of the Union of Myanmar [Burma], received Japanese Senator Mr. (Shigito Nagano) and his party, who are currently in Yangon [Rangoon], at the Ministry of Defense's Dagon House at 1300 this afternoon. Brigadier General Abel, minister of national planning and economic development; U Ohn Gyaw, minister of foreign affairs; Thura U Aung Htet, director general of the Protocol Department; and Takashi Tajima, ambassador of Japan to Myanmar, were also present at the meeting.
