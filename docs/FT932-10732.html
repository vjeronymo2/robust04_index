930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / People: Cadbury Schweppes John Sunderland (above), a founding director of Coca-Cola & Schweppes Beverages who was responsible for the merger and development of Trebor Bassett Group, has been appointed md of CADBURY SCHWEPPES' confectionery stream and joins the main board. He succeeds David Wellings who becomes group chief executive and is succeeded as md of Trebor Bassett by John Taylor.
