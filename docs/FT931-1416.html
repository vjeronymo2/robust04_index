930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / International Company News: New World advances 48% NEW World Development, the Hong property development and hotels group controlled by Mr Cheng Yu-tung, yesterday reported a 48 per cent increase in net profit to HKDollars 1.39bn (USDollars 181m) for the six months to December 1992, up from HKDollars 939m in the previous year. The bulk of earnings growth came from property sales, which contributed HKDollars 951m to operating profit, up from HKDollars 645m in 1991. Rental income showed a slight decline, due to the sale of several floors of the Convention and Exhibition Centre, in Wanchai. However, average occupancy stood at 95 per cent. The company declared an interim dividend of 25 cents per share, up from 21 cents in 1991.
