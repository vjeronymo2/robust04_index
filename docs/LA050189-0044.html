May 1, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

PRESIDENT VISITS PEPPER AT WALTER REED HOSPITAL

President Bush on Sunday visited 88-year-old Rep. Claude Pepper (D-Fla.) at Walter Reed Army Medical Hospital, where Congress' oldest member has been receiving treatment and undergoing tests for a stomach disorder.

Bush made the unscheduled stop on his return to the White House after attending ceremonies in New York commemorating the 200th anniversary of George Washington's inauguration.
