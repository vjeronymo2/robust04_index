921127 FT 27 NOV 92 / Accountant keeps mum THE man likely to be charged with calculating the royal tax bill for Ma'am was keeping mum last night, writes Andrew Jack Mr Michael Peat, a senior partner at accountants KPMG Peat Marwick and a descendant of one of the founders of the firm, is auditor to the privy purse, like his predecessors since Edward VII. He has been on secondment to help streamline palace finances for two years. 'Of all the clients we say little about, this is the one we say least about,' one executive said. Another added: 'We couldn't talk about it. The partner involved might lose his 'K' for that,' referring to a potential knighthood.
