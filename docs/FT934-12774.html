931025 FT 25 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Lowe (Robert H) LOWE (ROBERT H) is selling its lossmaking childrens wear division to Delta Textiles (London), a subsidiary of Delta Galil Industries of Israel, by way of the sale of the issued share capital and repayment of group indebtedness. About Pounds 4.2m will be repaid, of which Pounds 500,000 will be deferred. Estimated book value of the division's assets are Pounds 5.7m. Lowe will use the proceeds to reduce borrowings.
