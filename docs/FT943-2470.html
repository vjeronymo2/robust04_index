940919 FT 19 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Conrad continues recovery with Pounds 0.1m Conrad, the Manchester-based sports, leisure and consultancy company, continued the recovery seen at the half year and for the 12 months to end-June achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 100,230 compared with losses of Pounds 1.48m. Mr Rodney Walker, the chairman, said that with all subsidiaries now operating profitably the intention was to grow both organically and by acquisition in each area. Turnover jumped to Pounds 5.3m (Pounds 770,000) with Pounds 328,000 from acquisitions. Earnings per share were 0.15p (3.26p losses).
