920122 FT 22 JAN 92 / Billingsgate marks 10th anniversary of move Setting a sprat: this week marks the 10th anniversary of the move by London's Billingsgate fish market to Docklands. To celebrate, a trawler delivered its catch of 2 tonnes of sprats directly to the market for the first time for more than 50 years. The trawler, Shepherd Lad, is owned by Peter Saunders (above).
