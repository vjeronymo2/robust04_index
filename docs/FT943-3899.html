940912 FT 12 SEP 94 / Arts (Openings): Madrid The Fundacio la Caixa marks the 50th anniversary of the deaths of Kandinsky and Mondrian with an exhibition at its Sala de Exposiciones, opening on Friday. Entitled 'Two roads towards abstraction', the show aims to illustrate the parallels and differences in the stylistic evolution of two pioneers of modern art. It includes loans from public and private collections around the world, and will move to Barcelona in November.
