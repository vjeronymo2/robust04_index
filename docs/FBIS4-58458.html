BFN [Text] During a session led by Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali this morning, the cabinet decided to form a delegation, led by Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs, along with Nayif Sa'ud al-Qadi, the Jordanian ambassador to Egypt, to represent Jordan at the signing of the Palestinian-Israeli agreement ceremony which will take place in Cairo tomorrow, Wednesday. During its session, the cabinet reviewed the minutes of meetings of the committee in charge of preparing for the meetings of the Joint Jordanian-Egyptian Higher Committee. The higher committee began meetings at the Ministry of Planning yesterday to discuss fields of cooperation between the two fraternal countries in preparation for discussing and approving these minutes during its meetings on Thursday under Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali and Egyptian Prime Minister Dr. 'Atif Sidqi.
