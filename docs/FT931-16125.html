930108 FT 08 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Recovery continues at Abbey ABBEY, the Dublin-based housebuilder and plant hirer, maintained its improvement with a pre-tax profit of IPounds 501,000 (Pounds 522,000) for the six months to October 31, against IPounds 367,000 last time. The result was struck after a IPounds 1m provision, mainly reflecting a revaluation of a recently let investment property. At the trading level, there was a turnround from losses of IPounds 81,000 to IPounds 729,000 profits. Interest received added IPounds 772,000 (IPounds 448,000). The company said it still retained a sizeable cash position. Earnings per share were 0.43p (0.63p). There is no interim dividend, but the company hopes to be able to recommend payment of a final.
