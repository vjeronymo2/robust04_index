BFN [Excerpt] There has been no official reaction to the report carried by REUTER that President Glavkos Kliridhis expressed the intention to resign if the UN Security Council decides to continue talks on the confidence-building measures. The government spokesman avoided making any comment and said that the letters that the president has sent to President Clinton and British Prime Minister John Major are confidential. REUTER reported that President Kliridhis' threat to resign is outlined in these letters. [passage omitted]
