BFN [Text] Mr. Va'ezi, our country's deputy foreign minister in charge of Euro-American affairs, met with Foreign Minister Alain Juppe today. Mr. Va'ezi, who is visiting France, yesterday met his French counterpart for talks regarding issues of mutual interest and international affairs. Richard Duque, spokesman for the French Foreign Ministry, in his Friday [10 June] news conference, said the meeting was held within the context of dialogue between member countries of the European Union with Iran, and that France consults regularly with Iran at this level.
