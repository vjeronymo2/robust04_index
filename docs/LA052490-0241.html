May 24, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

CHERUB-BIN THRIFT SHOP SEEKS DONATIONS

Donations of clothing, appliances, furniture and household goods are being sought by the Cherub-Bin Thrift Shop to raise money for Pacific Clinics.

The Cherub-Bin sells the donated items and gives the proceeds to Pacific Clinics, a nonprofit, community-based mental-health agency that provides outpatient services to children, adults, senior citizens and the homeless of the San Gabriel Valley.

All donations are tax deductible.

The Cherub-Bin Thrift Shop is located at 909 S. Fair Oaks Ave., Pasadena. For pickup or more information, call (818) 792-4362 between 10 a.m. and 4 p.m.
