930322 FT 22 MAR 93 / Fimbra suspension FIMBRA, the self-regulatory organisation for independent financial advisers, has suspended one of its members who was simultaneously operating as an independent financial adviser and an appointed representative of a life company. Fimbra said that Mr Thomas Kelly of DD Financial Management, based in Warminster in Wiltshire, was 'not fit to carry on investment business in that his company, DD Financial Management, has been operating as an appointed representative of a life company and as an appointed representative of DBS Financial Management, a Fimbra member, at the same time.' He had been ordered 'to cease conducting and soliciting investment business regulated by Fimbra', it said.
