BFN [Presidential Council Decree No. 21 issued on 9 May -- read by announcer] [Text] The chairman of the Presidential Council, cognizant of the Constitution of the Republic of Yemen, and upon the recommendation of the acting prime minister, in view of the requirements of public interest, and after the approval of the President Council, decrees the following:

Article I.
Staff Brigadier General 'Abd-Rabbuh Mansur Hadi shall be appointed minister of defense.
Article II.
This decree will be valid as of date of issue and will be published in the official gazette. Issued at the Presidency in Sanaa on 29 Dhu al-Qi'dah, 1414 of the hegira, 9 May 1994. [Signed] Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the President Council Dr. Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar, acting prime minister
