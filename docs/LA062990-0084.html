June 29, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NATION IN BRIEF;

FLORIDA;

7 COAST GUARDSMEN INDICTED FOR DRUGS

Seven current and former members of the U.S. Coast Guard have been indicted by a federal grand jury in Miami for reselling cocaine and marijuana seized from drug traffickers, U.S. Atty. Dexter Lehtinen said. According to the indictment, six of the seven members conspired to possess and distribute about six kilograms of cocaine.
