920715 FT 15 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Prism returns record Pounds 975,000 Prism Leisure Corporation, the music and computer games group traded on the USM, returned record profits of Pounds 975,000 pre-tax for the 52 weeks ended March 29. The 28 per cent improvement over the previous year's Pounds 761,000 was achieved on the back of an 8 per cent rise in turnover to Pounds 13.5m. Earnings rose by 5p to 16.8p and an increased final dividend of 3.95p makes a 5.45p (4.95p) total. Year-end cash balances totalled Pounds 800,000.
