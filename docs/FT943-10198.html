940806 FT 06 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Wholesale Fittings ahead 15% Pre-tax profits of Wholesale Fittings, the Essex-based distributor of industrial and commercial electrical products, showed a 15 per cent increase from Pounds 2.02m to Pounds 2.32m in the year to end-April. The outcome was achieved on turnover up by Pounds 10m to Pounds 72.4m and was struck after interest payable of Pounds 76,000, against income of Pounds 174,000. The company said trading margins remained healthy, though there had been some erosion as it continued to gain market share. Earnings per share came out at 11.22p (9.83p) and a same-again final of 8.47p is proposed to maintain the total dividend for the year at 11.7p.
