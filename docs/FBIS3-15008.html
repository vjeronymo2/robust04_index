Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] A press conference has been held in the Azerbaijan Ministry of Foreign Affairs on the outcome of President Geydar Aliyev's visit to Britain. The press conference was held for foreign and local journalists. President Aliyev said that several important documents were signed during his visit and that the agreements between Azerbaijan and Britain will create a basis for the improvement of relations between the two countries. President Aliyev also stressed that opportunities have been established for that purpose. It was noted that the documents that were signed and the agreements that were concluded in Britain will contribute to the consolidation of Azerbaijan's statehood. It was also noted that Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov is currently visiting Bonn. The itinerary for his visit includes talks with Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel, a visit to the German Bundestag, and talks with Minister of Economic Cooperation Carl-Dieter Spranger and representatives of Germany's business circles.
