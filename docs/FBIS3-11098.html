Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] After attending a meeting with the congressional Agriculture Committee, Economy Minister Jorge Camet said that the bonus for schoolchildren will be paid to workers at the beginning of April, and will amount to something between 120 and 150 soles. [passage omitted] The minister added that the funds raised from the privatization process will be used on social programs. [passage omitted] The economy minister also reported that the government is preparing the last details of the letter of intent it will sign with the IMF. He stated categorically that Peru will not insist that the United States comply with its offer to participate in the support group. [Begin Camet recording] I regret to say that the United States is the only country that has not complied with its promise to join the support group. All the other countries that had promised to do so complied with their promises, (?except) for the U.S. Government. It is their decision, and we respect it. Any money we can get is welcome, but if someone promises to give money and now does not want to give it... [end recording]
