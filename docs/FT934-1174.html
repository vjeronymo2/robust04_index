931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / Family finance left to women More than half of women are responsible for the everyday bills and budgeting in their households, although only one in three takes charge of the long-term financial decisions, Mintel, the market research group, says. Women in social groups A and B are less financially active than others. Mintel's report, to be published next month, says that while women make up nearly half of the workforce they still take almost all of the responsibility for household chores.
