February 12, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

DEPUTIES SEEK IDENTITY OF SLAIN LATINO MAN

Sheriff's deputies Saturday worked to learn the identity of a Latino man found shot to death on Domingo Avenue in San Juan Capistrano.

Lt. Jay Mendez said the body of the unidentified man was discovered shortly after 9 p.m. Friday near the Capistrano Beach post office. He said the man had been shot several times in the head.

Authorities said deputies were seeking several men who had been seen in the area at about the time of the shooting, and were also looking for anyone who may have witnessed it.
