August 11, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

FILM AT 11 . . . BUT RARELY ANY GOLF

I don't care who you are, anyone can join the Junior Golf Assn. or many U.S. Golf Assn. golf clubs and get to play on the most private clubs where many tournaments are held. I believe a private club is for people who want to be together: Leave them alone.

The media are blowing this Shoal Creek situation way out of proportion compared to other urgent needs, that is, to educate young blacks so that they can get better jobs and economically have their own private clubs.

SANDY COHEN

Woodland Hills
