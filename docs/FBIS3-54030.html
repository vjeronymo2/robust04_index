Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Surt, Ay Annar [January] 24, JAMAHIRYA NEWS AGENCY--The general meeting of the people's congresses, the people's committees, unions, syndicates and professional associations - the General People's Congress - last night resumed its proceedings in the city of Surt to formulate the decisions of the basic people's congresses of their 2nd session for 1403 A.P.D. The five committees, set up by the congress, including the committees of laws, the committee of follow up and reports, the committee of public affairs the committee of foreign policy and the committee of budget and trilateral programme resumed the sorting out of the decisions of the basic people's congresses putting them under the appropriate items and reading out and debating the remarks coming from the basic people's congresses for approval in their final form.
