930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / UK Company News: BZW net assets improve to 118.13p Net asset value per share of BZW Convertible Investment Trust improved to 118.13p as at July 31 1993. This is compared with 115.26p six months earlier and 94.44p at July 31 1992. Available revenue was little changed at Pounds 4.3m (Pounds 4.32m) for the year to end of July giving earnings per share of 8.52p (8.54p). The dividend is maintained at 7.8p for the year with a same-again final payment of 3.3p.
