Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] The situation became exceedingly tense on the Karabakh fronts this morning. The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] Defense Army Press Service reported that the Azerbaijani Army launched an offensive toward Agdam in the east of the confrontation line at 0315 on 28 March. Considerable enemy forces and military aircraft are taking part in the operation. According to reports received at noon, NKR Defense Army units are resisting the enemy's pressure and inflicting heavy losses on him. No substantial changes have taken place on the other fronts in the past few days.
