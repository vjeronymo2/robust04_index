BFN [Text] Beijing, April 26 (XINHUA) -- A party workers' delegation from the Communist Party of China (CPC) left here this afternoon on a visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK). The delegation, which is led by Bai Keming, deputy head of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee, is paying the visit at the invitation of the International Department of the Workers' Party of Korea.
