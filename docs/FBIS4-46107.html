BFN [By Yi Il-hwa from Tokyo] [Text] Quoting a Japanese military source, YOMIURI SHIMBUN reported today that North Korea is making moves to test-fire a missile. According to this military source, a U.S. information satellite caught these moves by North Korea, and the U.S. Government informed the Japanese Government of this. The military source said it is highly possible that North Korea will soon carry out the second missile test fire which follows its first test fire of the Nodong No. 1 missile toward the East Sea in May of last year. Regarding these moves by North Korea at a time when it is the focus of the international community because of its nuclear issue, the Japanese military source analyzed that North Korea is trying to test-fire a missile while weather over the East Sea is calm.
