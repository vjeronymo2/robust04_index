January 28, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

OFF-THE-WALL STREET

John Rothchild's claim that authors consider the Stock Market too dull to write about is without merit. Has he never heard of Emma Lathen?

She is -- or they are -- a mystery-writing team . . . and the 20 novels she -- they -- have written are filled with humor, making the world of banking and the market fascinating.

BEATA P. SPEARS

SANTA MARIA
