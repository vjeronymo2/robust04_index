May 11, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE

HQ Office Supplies Warehouse Inc., a retail office supply and equipment chain, has opened its first Orange County store.

The store, at the corner of Harbor Boulevard and Imperial Highway in Fullerton, offers printing, engraving, facsimile and mailing services. It also sells business machines, furniture, software and office supplies.

The chain's other Southern California stores are in Alhambra, Bellflower and Culver City.

The Long Beach-based company announced this week that it plans to open up to 16 stores by the end of this year. Of those, six should open within the next 3 months, said Brad R. Tukey, company president and chief executive.

"Our goal is to establish and maintain a clearly dominant position for office supplies" in the region, Tukey said.
