September 14, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

SAN DIEGO WINS '92 ALL-STAR GAME

The 1992 All-Star Game will be played at San Diego's Jack Murphy Stadium on Tuesday, July 14, National League President Bill White said today following a meeting of baseball's executive council in Milwaukee.

The Padres announced the selection in a statement this morning.

San Diego, which last played host to the All-Star Game in 1978, beat Houston, Montreal and Philadelphia as potential sites for the game.

San Diego business and community leaders made a presentation last June in Kansas City.
