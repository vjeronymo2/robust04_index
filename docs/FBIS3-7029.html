Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Tension Further Strained" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, March 24 (KCNA) -- Some 400 Japan-based U.S. fighters and fighter-bombers, assault planes, observation planes and reconnaissance planes of the U.S. Airforce present in South Korea and the South Korean puppet airforce staged provocative joint air war exercises in the sky above Chunchon, Pyongchang, Yongwol and Kongju of South Korea on March 21 and 22, according to military sources. Meanwhile, the South Korean puppet army on the same days fired more than 50 shells with tank guns at the area near the demilitarized zone from Paju county, and on the 22d fired some 2,000 bullets with large-calibre machine-guns and automation rifles, advancing towards the Military Demarcation Line from Paju County and Yonchon County. They launched exercises for surprise strike at the targets and disturbance in the rear, repeatedly dropping "commandoes" in Paju county on March 21 and 22. The tension on the Korean peninsula is being screwed up by the war provocative moves of the U.S. imperialists and the South Korean puppets at a time when the schemes for "sanctions" against the DPRK are being hatched up in the arena of the United Nations and the U.S. aggression forces are moving towards the Korean peninsula from the U.S. mainland and U.S. military bases in the Pacific region.
