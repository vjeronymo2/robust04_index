May 2, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The Atlanta Braves put first baseman Nick Esasky on the 15-day disabled list because of vertigo.

The move was retroactive to April 22, one day after Esasky was sidelined with an injured right shoulder. He will be eligible to rejoin the roster next Monday.

Persons with vertigo experience dizziness and the sensation in which all things seem to be swirling. Esasky had a .171 average in nine games this year.
