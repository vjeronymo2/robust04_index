CSO [Text] Tehran, April 26 (IRNA)-- A total 8,296 kgs of narcotics were seized during the last Iranian month (March 21-April 21), registering an increase of 25.83 percent compared with the same period last year. A report by the Anti-Drug Campaign Hq. at the Presidential Bureau here Monday, said that the haul included 7,327 kgs of opium, 674 kgs of hashish, 243 kgs of morphine, 49.5 kgs of heroine, and 2.5 kgs of other drugs. It said 2,134 drug traffickers were arrested in the same period, adding that 63.6 percent of the narcotics were seized in Sistan-Baluchestan, 10.6 percent in Kerman and 8.5 percent in Khorasan provinces.
