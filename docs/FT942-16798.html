940408 FT 08 APR 94 / Pension-scheme trustees urged to check on investment returns Company pension-scheme trustees have been urged to keep a regular check on investment returns from additional voluntary contributions. Bacon & Woodrow, the actuaries firm which advises 700 companies on pension arrangements, made the call as it published its 1994 AVC survey. Trustees often spent little time choosing the AVC provider, it said, but the decision could have a significant impact on the pension payable on retirement.
