July 12, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

THE NEIGHBORHOOD EATERY: MORE EATERIES:

Villa Margarita, 103 Santa Isabel, Costa Mesa. Open daily 4 p.m. to midnight, weekends till 2 a.m. (714) 631-6602. This is the sort of place you want to take three or four friends, scarf up some excellent nachos and drink some very reasonably priced and generous-on-the-good-stuff margaritas. The sort of place where the neighborhood folks throw birthday parties. M. FLAGG
