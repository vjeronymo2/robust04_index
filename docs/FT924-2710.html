921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / BA Gatwick ballot UNIONS representing British Airways staff at Gatwick airport, West Sussex, said yesterday they would ballot members in their dispute over new pay terms after BA's takeover of Dan-Air. BA said talks were continuing and ground staff had been offered a 20 per cent cut with a lump sum in compensation of up to Pounds 14,500, or could remain on their existing scales.
