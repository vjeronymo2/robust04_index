July 4, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

GIANTURCO ON PROPS. 111, 108

In response to "On the Road Again With Props. 111, 108," by Adriana Gianturco, Commentary, June 25:

Gianturco's comments were interesting in that she and Gov. Jerry Brown combined to "strangle" our state transportation system with their moratorium on new highway construction during their years in office.

The freeway ramps in progress you pictured are probably the ones being completed that Brown and Gianturco left hanging eight years ago. Gianturco was referred to as "Giant Turkey" during her tenure as director of Caltrans. The name still fits. Props. 111 and 108 will be good for all Californians.

JOHN MALLOY

Los Angeles
