April 19, 1990, Thursday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: SIMI VALLEY;

MAN'S BODY FOUND INSIDE PARKED CAR

A 32-year-old man was found dead early Wednesday inside a car parked on a Simi Valley street, police said.

Officers discovered the man, whom they identified as Jose Antonio Garcia of Moorpark, slumped inside a blue station wagon in the 4200 block of Adam Road about 9:30 a.m., said Lt. Neal Rein of the Simi Valley Police Department.

Neighbors called police after they saw the man in the car and became suspicious, Rein said.

Garcia does not appear to have been the victim of an assault, he said.
