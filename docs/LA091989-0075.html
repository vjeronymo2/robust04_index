September 19, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Three-time world speedway motorcycle champion Erik Gundersen of Denmark was critically injured in the first heat race of the World Team Cup in Bradford, England.

Gundersen was placed on a life-support system after a crash with Lance King of Fountain Valley, Jimmy Nilsen of Sweden and Simon Wigg of Britain in the first heat race.

All four riders were hospitalized.
