BFN [Text] Yoshiro Mori, secretary general of the opposition Liberal Democratic Party [LDP], met with Koichi Kato [former chief cabinet secretary), representative of the "New Century Group," a suprafaction action group for new policies in the LDP, and others at a Tokyo restaurant in the evening of 18 May. During the meeting, Mr. Mori said: "If the people ask the LDP to forge an alliance with the opposition Social Democratic Party of Japan [SDPJ] after the Hata cabinet resigns en masse, the LDP would be willing to do so. The LDP also intends to forge an alliance with Sakigake [Harbinger Party]." In this way, Mr. Mori indicated that the LDP will examine the possibility of forging a coalition with the SDPJ and Sakigake after it submits a no-confidence motion against the Hata cabinet to the Diet.
