July 7, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Hey There: Stage-film-TV actor Pat Harrington will be on stage as one of the stars of the California Music Theatre production of "The Pajama Game," which is booked into the Pasadena Civic Auditorium Oct. 4-22. He will provide the comedy lines in his role of Hines, "the time-study man." Harrington, who co-starred for nine years on CBS' "One Day at a Time," recently was on stage for a 17-city tour in "The Odd Couple." ALEENE MacMINN
