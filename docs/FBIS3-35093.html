Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Islamabad, Feb. 17, IRNA -- Iran's ambassador to Pakistan Mohammad Mehdi Akhund Zadeh called on Pakistan's Foreign Minister Asif Ahmad Ali here today. The two sides discussed issues of mutual interest and exchanged views on the recent developments in Afghanistan as well as ways to help settle the problems of this country. Pakistan foreign minister also briefed the Iranian ambassador on the situation in Kashmir.
