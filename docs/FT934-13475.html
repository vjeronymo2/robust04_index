931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Bennett & Fountain losses at Pounds 5.92m Bennett & Fountain Group, the wholesaler and retailer of electrical goods which is being acquired by Marlowe Holdings in a recommended Pounds 2.1m offer, announced pre-tax losses of Pounds 5.92m for the year to June 30. There were restated losses of Pounds 8.03m last time. The difficult trading conditions of the first half had continued into the second, the company said. In addition, the high level of provision against bad debts had increased in the second half. The company did not as yet see any signs of improvement. Turnover amounted to Pounds 64.7m (Pounds 66.1m). Losses per share amounted to 5.6p (7.2p). The shares were unchanged at 2 3/4 p. Marlowe's cash offer is 2p per share.
