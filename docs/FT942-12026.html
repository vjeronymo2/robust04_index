940503 FT 03 MAY 94 / Business Travel (Update): UK is best The UK has the best facilities for business travellers of any European country and Italy has the worst, according to a poll carried out by Mori for Thomas Cook. The poll of purchasers of travel services in 281 companies in eight European countries found 13 per cent thought the UK had the best facilities, followed by Germany on 10 per cent and Switzerland on 6 per cent. Nine per cent, mentioned Italy as having the worst facilities.
