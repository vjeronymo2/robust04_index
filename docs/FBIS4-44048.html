BFN [Text] Defense Minister Konstandinos Iliadhis returned from his trip to France satisfied. Iliadhis visited the arms systems exhibition Eurosatori 1994 at the invitation of his French counterpart, Francois Leotard. Iliadhis said he contacted many manufacturing companies in Paris and exchanged views on various arms systems. He said his contacts aimed to create good relations with these companies, something that is necessary for maintaining the National Guard's operational capability at a high level. The minister added that the government's goal is to purchase arms systems directly from the manufacturing companies. Answering a relevant question, the defense minister said he had no contacts with representatives of the SOFMA company.
