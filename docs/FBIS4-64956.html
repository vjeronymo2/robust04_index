BFN [Text] Ankara-Sanliurfa, May 6 (A.A) -- Murat Karayalcin, Social Democratic Populist Party leader and deputy prime minister, said today that cooperation among left-wing parties for an election had a high chance of realization. He was speaking to journalists on board a plane on his way to the southeastern city of Sanliurfa. On the cancellation of three mayoral elections in Istanbul, of which two were won by the pro-Islamic Prosperity Party, Karayalcin said that this decision of the High Election Board created an opportunity. He recalled that the board has thus far canceled results of 15 mayoral elections. `We shall spend efforts, by heeding the calls for the unification of social democrats, toward realizing cooperation in the left,' he said.
