BFN [Text] The Islamic Republic of Iran has been elected to head the working group of the Group of 77 countries. The group is responsible for preparing various topics of common interest to the Group of 77 for the agenda of the decisionmaking UNIDO sessions and in particular its draft resolutions. Preparing the draft resolutions for the Group of 77 and the agenda of the sessions is the responsibility of the working group of the Group of 77 countries.
