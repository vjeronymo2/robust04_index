BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received Salim Salih Muhammad, member of the Yemeni Presidential Council and assistant secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP]; and Dr. 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Dali, member of the YSP Political Bureau, at 1300 today. The meeting was attended by Deputy Prime Minister Rashid Akhtarini and Yemeni Ambassador in Damascus Muhammad 'Abduh Shatfah.
