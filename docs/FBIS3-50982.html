Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Ricardo Trejos from "The City" newscast] [Text] President Carlos Salinas de Gortari met a few minutes ago with Salvadoran President Alfredo Cristiani and Honduran President Rafael Leonardo Callejas. This meeting had been agreed upon in early December 1993. The meeting, which lasted a couple of hours, was held at the official residence of Los Pinos in an atmosphere of cordiality and frankness. They reviewed the regional situation, bilateral relations, the outlook for the free trade agreement, and various topics of interest to the three countries, as is explained in a communique issued a few minutes ago by the Presidency General Media Directorate. The governments expressed their desire to cooperate and to provide mutual support to achieve greater development in the region. The Salvadoran and Honduran presidents will return to their countries today. The communique does not specify whether the presidents discussed the topic of Chiapas.
