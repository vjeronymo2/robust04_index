910501 FT 01 MAY 91 / Appointments: Chairman of Texaco Mr Glen Tilton (pictured), a vice president of Texaco Inc, has been appointed chairman of TEXACO LTD, London, the company's largest wholly-owned foreign subsidiary. Mr Tilton, who is president of Texaco Refinig and Marketing Inc, Houston, succeeds Mr Peter Bijur. Mr Bijur, president of Texaco Europe, returns to New York from July 1, where Texaco Europe has its divisional headquarters. ***** Mr Jean-Luc Biamonti is joining CREDIT LYONNAIS from May 13, and will assume responsibility for all the cross-border merger and acquisition activity of the group. He also becomes director of mergers and acquisitions of Credit Lyonnais Capital Markets in London, and vice president in charge of international affairs at Clinvest in Paris. He was with Wasserstein Perella. ***** PERSIMMON has appointed Mr Stewart Badger as managing director of a new subsidiary, Persimmon Homes (East Midlands), Peterborough. He was sales director of Persimmon Homes (Midlands). Mr John White becomes chairman of the new company. He was managing director of the Midlands subsidiary, where he is succeeded by Mr Richard Wright who was area director. ***** WESTPORT GROUP has appointed Mr Ralph Kanter as non-executive chairman. He is non-executive chairman of Hambro Countrywide Security, and was group managing director of Britannia Security Group. ***** FIRST NATIONAL FINANCE CORPORATION has appointed Mr Michael Comninos as a non-executive director. He is a director of NM Rothschild & Sons. ***** THE DE LA RUE COMPANY has appointed Mr Robert McGowan as group treasurer. He was treasurer of Reedpack and of Vickers, and succeeds Mr Chris Mansell who leaves in the summer to pursue other interests. ***** Mr Peter Davies has been elected deputy chairman of PRINCIPALITY BUILDING SOCIETY, Cardiff. He is corporate finance executive director of NM Rothschild and Sons, Cardiff office. ***** Mr John A. Fordham and Mr George W. Stuart-Clarke have been appointed joint heads of corporate finance at LLOYDS MERCHANT BANK. They replace Mr Richard CG Fortin who is resigning to pursue other interests. ***** Mr Martin Hampson has been appointed a director of FLOWLINE GRILLES, a subsidiary of Air Movement Group, Dudley. He was an account executive in the group's special products division. ***** Mr Peter Davies has been appointed finance and administration director of BACS (Bankers Automated Clearing Services), Dunstable. He was financial director of SKF (UK). Mr Peter Gordon becomes marketing and business planning director. He was media and marketing co-ordinator at Lyons Tetley. ***** Mr Clive Hillier has been appointed to the board of LADBROKE CITY & COUNTY LAND. He is manager of the company's Cwmbran shopping centre. ***** THE INSTITUTIONAL SHAREHOLDERS' COMMITTEE has appointed Mr Julian Potter as secretary general, succeeding Mr Maurice Epstein. ***** UNIFACE UK, Marlow, has appointed Mr Steve Nash as sales director. He was sales manager, southern region. ***** UNITED ARTISTS CABLE TELEVISION INTERNATIONAL has appointed Mr Peter Saunders as managing director of United Artists Communications (London South). He was on the board of the Allders Department Stores Group. ***** Mrs Sheam Fyfe has been appointed technical services director of CLARKE & TILLEY. She was commercial services manager. ***** Mr Anthony Mallin has been appointed a vice chairman of HAMBROS BANK. He will continue to be responsible for the asset finance and leasing division. Mr Edward Adeane and Mr Arthur Brignall have been appointed executive directors, and Mr Brian Curtis and Mr Anthony Taylor become directors. ***** Mr John Clement, chairman of Unigate, and Mr Derek Bucknall, recently a director of British Aerospace, have been appointed non-executive directors of RANSOMES. ***** Mr Charles W. Santoro has been appointed a managing director, and head of cross border investment for SMITH BARNEY, HARRIS UPHAM & CO INC, New York. He will be operating out of London and New York. Mr Santoro was with Morgan Stanley International as co-ordinating officer of its European mergers and acquisitions department.
