BFN [Text] Sarajevo, 12 Apr (TANJUG)--One of the two "F-18" planes attacking Serb positions around Gorazde yesterday bombed the town center by mistake, it has been learned from a radio message sent by a UN monitor to the pilot, which was intercepted at a radar base in neighboring Yugoslavia, SRNA reported early this morning. This caused panic among the people of Gorazde and Muslim soldiers who began to flee toward the surrounding hills. Several inhabitants of Gorazde who made it to Serb positions said that the explosion of a bomb caused a horrific scene, large number of casualties and huge material damages.
