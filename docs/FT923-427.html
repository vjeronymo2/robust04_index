920929 FT 29 SEP 92 / UK Company News: 9% increase for Joseph Holt Joseph Holt, the Manchester-based brewer, lifted pre-tax profit 9 per cent for the first half of 1992. Turnover came to Pounds 12.4m (Pounds 10.6m) to produce an operating profit of Pounds 2.67m (Pounds 2.41m). With Pounds 122,000 (Pounds 28,000) surplus on realisation of investments, the pre-tax surplus was Pounds 3.63m (Pounds 3.33m). Earnings per share worked through at 80.56p (72.88p) and the interim dividend is raised by 1p to 10p.
