Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] Members of the Combined Committee for Economic Cooperation between Hokkaido and the Russian Far East are now meeting at a sea resort area in the suburbs of Sapporo, Japan. Representatives of the local governments of the Coastal Region, Khavarovsk, and Sakhalin, and the central government's Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations, Fisheries Committee, and the Chamber of Commerce are attending the meeting from Russia. This Combined Committee was activated in 1992 to discuss promotion of economic relations between Hokkaido and neighboring Russia's territories at the proposal of the Hokkaido governor. The working-level meeting held in the suburbs of Sapporo is a continuation of positive Russian-Japanese exchanges begun at various levels last week. In following with this current of the times, there are now formal and informal talks being held in Tokyo between the representatives of the foreign ministries, defense ministries, and border security-related offices of the two countries.
