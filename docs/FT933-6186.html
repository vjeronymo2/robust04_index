930828 FT 28 AUG 93 / Poster contractor to be privatised LONDON Transport Advertising (LTA), the capital's largest poster contractor, is to be privatised. LTA, an internal department of London Transport, generates Pounds 23m a year from the sale of poster space on Underground stations, trains and buses. It is to be a subsidiary company within London Transport employing all existing staff of 300. This company will be offered for sale.
