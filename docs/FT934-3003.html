931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Resort Hotels gets extension for accounts Resort Hotels, shares in which were suspended in mid-July, has said it will be unable to present its accounts for the year to April 30 at the annual meeting on December 31. It said the accounts would be considered at an extraordinary meeting some time after the annual meeting. It had obtained from the trade and industry secretary an extension of the period allowed for delivering the accounts until February 28 1994. It hoped soon to be able to give details of the group's financial position and events that led to the suspension.
