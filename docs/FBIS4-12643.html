BFN [Text] Cairo, 12 Apr (MENA)--The Palestinian-Israeli negotiations relating to the implementation of the Gaza-Jericho agreement resumed in Cairo this morning. The Palestinian side was headed by Dr. Nabil Sha'th, political adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, and the Israeli side by General Amnon Shahaq, Israeli deputy chief of staff. In a statement today ahead of the talks, Dr. Sha'th said that any agreement that might be reached during today's meetings will be announced after the talks. Three committees--the Security Committee, the Civilian Affairs Committee, and the Legal Affairs Committee--will stem from the Gaza-Jericho meetings.
