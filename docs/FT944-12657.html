941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / World Trade News: Taiwan's trade deficit jumps Taiwan's trade deficit with Europe rose to Dollars 2.36bn during the first nine months of this year, compared to Dollars 752m during the same period last year, sparking concern among the island's trade authorities. Exports to Europe from January to September fell 3.7 per cent to Dollars 9.25bn, while imports climbed 12.1 per cent to Dollars 11.61bn. Germany was Taiwan's biggest trading partner.
