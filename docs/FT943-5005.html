940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Acquisition helps Calderburn advance Calderburn, the Preston-based office furniture group, reported pre-tax profits up from Pounds 1.41m to Pounds 2.84m for the six months to June 30. The outcome was struck on turnover up from Pounds 12.9m to Pounds 28m, and included a first contribution from Specialised Banking Furniture, acquired in December last year. Earnings per share emerged at 7p (3.6p), or 6.1p (3.5p) fully-diluted, and the interim dividend is raised to 2.9p (2.8p).
