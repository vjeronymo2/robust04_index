August 8, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"These people have arrest records from here to the end of the hall. There is no one we've gone after who has not been arrested at least twice. Some have been arrested nine to 10 times."

-- ACLU attorney Carol Sobel saying the civil liberties group will seek civil contempt charges against 100 to 200 "hard-core" anti-abortion activists in the next two weeks.
