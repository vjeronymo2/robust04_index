Language: English Article Type:BFN [By BELINFORM for TASS] [Text] Minsk January 4 TASS--Belarussian leader Stanislav Shushkevich and Russian President Boris Yeltsin had a telephone conversation today to discuss a possibility of their working meeting. According to a preliminary agreement, they will meet in Moscow before January 11, sources close to the Belarussian parliament head told BELINFORM.
