Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Sarajevo, 29 Mar (TANJUG) -- President of the Srpska republic Radovan Karadzic received a delegation of the Russian parliament led by Sergey Baburin in Pale today. Following the meeting, Karadzic stated that the Russian parliamentarians' visit was important for many reasons. He recalled that Russia was involved in the adoption of all resolutions on the Bosnian crisis and that Russian soldiers are present in this area. Asked whether they discussed the possibility of dispatching fresh Russian troops to Bosnia, president Karadzic said the Serb side is making steps in that direction, in order to act as a counterbalance to soldiers from other countries. The Russian parliamentarians visited members of the Russian UN Protection Force battalion deployed in Grbavica.
