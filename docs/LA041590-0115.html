April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BELL HIGH CLASSES PLAN 50TH REUNION

The Bell High School winter and summer classes of 1940 will hold their 50th-year reunion on May 5. The reunion is scheduled to take place at the Embassy Suites Hotel, 8425 Firestone Blvd., Downey. For more information, call R. Lawrence at 928-2491 or J. Kinkade at 560-3593.
