BFN [Message from Interior Minister Yu Hokkri -- recorded] [Text] My respects to the two supreme patriarchs, all venerable monks, and my beloved compatriots: I, Yu Hokkri, minister of the interior, would like to present the following announcement from the Interior Ministry: In compliance with the message dated 15 May of His Majesty Norodom Sihanouk Varaman, revered king of the Kingdom of Cambodia, in which he appealed to all brothers, sisters, children, grandchildren, and great-grandchildren to not hold the demonstration on 16 and 17 May so as to avoid any incidents which might bring about adverse consequences that will further complicate the solution that we are seeking for our motherland through peaceful means in line with Lord Buddha's teachings, Venerable Monk (Vichea Ratanapannha Wilson) and (Sit Kosangsin), representatives of the Kampuchea Kraom Buddhist Association, and the Cambodian Youth Association have decided to cancel their planned demonstration. At the same time, a group of people have distributed false letters calling on the public to join demonstrations or meetings on 17 May in the name of the two associations mentioned above. Venerable Monk (Vichea Ratanapannha Wilson) and (Sit Kosangsin) have rejected any undertaking that run counter to the wish and desire of the revered his majesty the king, who is the father of national peace. The Interior Ministry would like to stress also that until now, the ministry has not received any new requests for holding the demonstration. It is with the goal of ensuring security and public order that I would like your venerable personages and the public to please be informed. Thank you. Issued in Phnom Penh on 16 May 1994
