930602 FT 02 JUN 93 / International Company News: News Corp sale NEWS Corp, the global media and entertainment group, has reduced its shareholding in TNT, its partner in domestic airline carrier Ansett Australia, from 10.19 to 8.29 per cent, Reuter reports from Sydney. News Corp told the Australian Stock Exchange it sold the shares at between 94 cents and ADollars 1.45 from December 20 1991, to May 26 1993.
