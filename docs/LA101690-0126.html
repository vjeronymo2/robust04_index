October 16, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

NEW PROSPECT: Larry Agran lost his job as Irvine mayor last election but may be getting a new job at UC Irvine. . . . Officials confirm that he's being considered by the Social Ecology Program as an adjunct professor. "That's a fancy term for instructor," Agran says. He's taught at the UCLA law school and the UCI graduate management school, but this time he's interested in foreign policy. Chris Pasles and Tom McQueeney
