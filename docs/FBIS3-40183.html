Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Summary] In 1993 aquatic product output in Jiangxi Province exceeded 500,000 tons, a 21 percent increase over 1992. The output value of the fishery industry reached 2.27 billion yuan, a 30 percent increase over 1992. In 1993 the aquatic products breeding area was 4,328,600 mu, a 7.1 percent increase over 1992.
