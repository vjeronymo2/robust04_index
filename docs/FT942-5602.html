940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / UK Company News: ABI Leisure Profits of ABI Leisure, the caravan maker, rose from Pounds 1.27m to Pounds 1.7m pre-tax for the half year ended February 28. The increase was achieved from a 19 per cent rise in turnover to Pounds 34.8m, helped by a 55 per cent improvement in exports. Earnings per share emerged at 4.2p (3.1p); the interim dividend is lifted to 1.35p (1.25p).
