Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Pristina, 7 Mar (TANJUG)--Slavisa Nikolic, a trainee policeman, killed Faik Maljoku and wounded Bejzak Dzevat after a short argument in a restaurant in Kosovo Polje, a TANJUG reporter learned today from the regional prosecution office in Pristina. According to the reports from the prosecution office, Officer Nikolic fired two bullets from his service gun at Maljoku, who died in a Pristina hospital soon afterwards. Dzevat, who was injured during the shooting incident, is stable. Nikolic has been detained. An official investigation is under way.
