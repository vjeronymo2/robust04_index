April 18, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Because of an editing error the founder of the city of Torrance, Jared Sidney Torrance, was misnamed in an article that appeared Sunday. The article also incorrectly said that last month regional water quality officials ordered Mobil Oil Corp. to clean up gasoline contaminating ground water around its Torrance refinery. The cleanup order was issued in March, 1988.
