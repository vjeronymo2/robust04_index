920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Armitage Brothers advances 20% Reduced interest charges allowed Armitage Brothers, the pet products maker, to lift pre-tax profits by 20 per cent, from Pounds 708,000 to Pounds 851,000, in the year to May 30. Turnover was slightly lower at Pounds 21.75m (Pounds 21.81m) and operating profits slipped to Pounds 993,000 (Pounds 1.06m). The pre-tax result was after interest payments of Pounds 142,000 against Pounds 352,000. Earnings per share improved from 14.2p to 16.4p and a final dividend of 3.4p is recommended for a 6p (5.7p) total.
