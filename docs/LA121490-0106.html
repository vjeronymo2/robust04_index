December 14, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SEA WHAT? Yet another weird sea creature has made a rare appearance along the county's coast -- this time a pair of sea mice. Found dead by county Marine Institute researchers, the "mice" are actually gray, furry, 4-inch-long worms that have flippers for swimming and burrowing. . . . They'll be displayed at the institute starting Monday. Dallas M. Jackson and Lily Eng
