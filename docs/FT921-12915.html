920124 FT 24 JAN 92 / Decision on Newcastle quay PROCTER & Gamble, the US consumer products group, and Mr Leser Landau, a Swiss architect, have been refused leave to appeal to the House of Lords against plans by the Tyne and Wear Development Corporation to acquire by compulsory purchase order land they own on Newcastle upon Tyne's Quayside. The corporation said infrastructure work should start in March to prepare the 25-acre site for redevelopment expected to cost Dollars Pounds 160m.
