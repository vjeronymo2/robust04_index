930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / International Company News in Brief: Rhone-Poulenc Nissan Chemical Industries and Sumitomo Chemical, two Japanese chemicals groups, are to set up a joint venture with Rhone-Poulenc, a French chemicals group, Reuter reports from Tokyo. The venture, yet to be named, will be launched in Lyons to develop and sell agricultural chemicals in France. It will be 60 per cent owned by Sumitomo, 30 per cent by Nissan Chemical and 10 per cent by Rhone-Poulenc and aims at sales of FFr180m (Dollars 32.1m) a year.
