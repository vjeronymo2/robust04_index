940205 FT 05 FEB 94 / Scottish steel company fails Clydeshaw, Scotland's last commodity steelmaker, based at Craigneuk, near Motherwell, Lanarkshire, went into receivership yesterday. The company, which made large steel castings and ingots, had a Pounds 12m annual turnover. The receivers, accountants Ernst & Young, said debts amounted to about Pounds 7m.
