930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / The FT Review of Business Books (23): Correction In the Summer edition of the Financial Times Review of Business Books, in the story headlined Salty Scottish tales on Page X, the correct title of Michael Strachan's history of the Ben Line shipping group is The Ben Line 1825-1982: An Anecdotal History, published by Michael Russell, Pounds 17.95, 248 pages.
