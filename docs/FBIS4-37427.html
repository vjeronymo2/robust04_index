BFN [Excerpts] Latest reports received from Mashhad indicate that the holy shrine of Imam 'Ali Bin Musa al-Reza, peace be upon him, was opened this morning to the pilgrims eager to pay their respects. Our colleague has dispatched the following report: In the wake of the bloody tragedy of Mashhad and the massacre inside the holy shrine of Imam Reza, the shrine opened at 1130 today to the enthusiastic pilgrims. [passage omitted] Funeral ceremonies for the Ashura day martyrs in Mashhad will be staged in the city tomorrow morning.
