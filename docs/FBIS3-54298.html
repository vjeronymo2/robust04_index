Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, Jan. 24, IRNA -- Deputy Foreign Minister for Legal, Consular and Parliamentary affairs Manuchehr Mottaki and Speaker of the Supreme Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK) Yang Hyong-sop [name as received] today discussed expansion of Tehran-Pyongyang parliamentary cooperation. Mottaki who is visiting North Korea, said Yang's recent visit to Tehran had been successful, adding that Iran's Majles Speaker 'Ali Akbar Nateq-Nuri is also to visit DPRK in future. Yang said his country supported the Islamic Republic of Iran's efforts to restore peace and security to the Persian Gulf region.
