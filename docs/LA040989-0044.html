April 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CITY LINES: BEYOND WESTWEEK

Beyond the speeches, the symposiums, the awards, the networking and the socializing, Westweek is at heart a grand furniture and furnishings show.

As such, it is a time for interior and industrial designers, architects and store owners to see, touch and feel the latest products of an expanding, style-conscious industry.

Eventually, much of what was unveiled in the "to-the-trade-only" showrooms of the Pacfic Design Center in West Hollywood during Westweek will find itself into the more tony stores everywhere over the next year.
