Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Vilor Niyazmatov and Georgiy Shmelev] [Text] Tashkent February 8 TASS -- The second day of Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev's stay in Uzbekistan began with his visit to the Russian Embassy in the Uzbek capital. After the conversation in the embassy Kozyrev told ITAR-TASS there was an exchange of opinions about the best ways of discussing with the Uzbekistan leadership a number of problems of bilateral relations, the questions of citizenship, migration, right of property. Kozyrev believes that although problems still exist and some of them are acute, their discussion with Uzbek leaders is calm and constructive, without mutual recriminations. He believes that since his latest visit to Tashkent last year, the acuteness lessened and misunderstanding decreased. From the Russian Embassy Kozyrev headed for the Uzbekistan Foreign Ministry and began negotiations with Uzbek Foreign Minister Saidmukhtar Said Asymov.
