BFN [Announcer-read report over still of President Suleyman Demirel] [Text] Bosnia-Herzegovina President Alija Izetbegovic has called President Suleyman Demirel on the telephone and briefed him on the situation in Gorazde and asked for Turkey's help in preventing the attacks on Gorazde. According to a statement issued by the Press Center of the President's Office, Demirel, within this framework, sent messages to U.S. President Bill Clinton, Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin, French President Francois Mitterrand, King Fahd of Saudi Arabia, Egyptian President Husni Mubarak, German Chancellor Helmut Kohl, and British Prime Minister John Major.
