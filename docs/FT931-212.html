930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: MacGregor sets BR objectives MR JOHN MacGregor, transport secretary, yesterday gave British Rail a one-year set of objectives to cover the period before privatisation starts to take effect in April 1994. InterCity has been set a profit target of Pounds 12m for 1993-94 compared with Pounds 2m in 1991-92, the last year for which figures are available. Network SouthEast is being asked to cut losses before grant to Pounds 128m in the same period (Pounds 181.9m in 1991-92) and Regional Railways to Pounds 411m (Pounds 583.6m).
