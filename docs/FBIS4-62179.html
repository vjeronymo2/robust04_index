BFN [Unattributed report under the "Business News" rubric: "Will We Sell Arms to Kuwait?"] [Text] The Kuwaiti Government is planning to buy military equipment in Russia, including 20 BMP-2 and 60 BMP-3 armored personnel carriers, spare parts, and possibly 27 300-mm rocket launchers. The purchase of Russian weapons represents a radical change in Kuwait's previous position; in the past, it relied solely on Western countries for the organizational development of its armed forces.
