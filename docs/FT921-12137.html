920128 FT 28 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Samuel Heath Samuel Heath, the building and giftware group, reported a 66 per cent decline in pre-tax profits, from Pounds 210,000 to Pounds 72,000, in the six months to end-September. Turnover fell from Pounds 3.67m to Pounds 3.46m. In spite of a fall in earnings per share to 1.6p (4.5p) the interim dividend is maintained at 1.5p.
