August 20, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

CUTTING FUNDS FOR THOSE WHO HELP ILLEGAL ALIENS

Well, three cheers for good ol' Costa Mesa! Imagine any city having the courage to put its citizens' rights ahead of the freeloaders who not only come uninvited to the table, but demand dessert as well.

Outraged, indeed. Many of us have long been outraged at the gall and greed of the interlopers who expect us not only to support them, but also whine when we don't love them.

Our homeless problem can be attributed directly to the law-breaking illegals who grab all the low-cost housing, thus depriving our own people of the facilities.

Let other cities take heart and follow suit by denying taxpayer funds to the illegals, and maybe they will stay home and help their own country.

ALICE SLUTZ

Laguna Hills
