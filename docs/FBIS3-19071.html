Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Responding to questions on the Skopje [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM] issue, government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos today said that American and European efforts on the Skopje issue tend to be in the same direction on the matter of how they should formulate the preliminary conditions for dialogue between the two sides. He stressed that the coordinator of the efforts is Cyrus Vance. The government spokesman also said that there is no possibility for a temporary lifting of the measures against Skopje and that Greece will lift the measures only when Skopje gives practical signs of goodwill, as demanded by the Greek side. A temporary suspension of the measures to allow a resumption of the dialogue is not in the government's plans, Venizelos said.
