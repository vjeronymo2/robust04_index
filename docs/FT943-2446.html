940919 FT 19 SEP 94 / Emerging Markets: News round-up - Fund launches Templeton Emerging Markets of the US has launched a Dollars 120m Vietnam Opportunities Fund on the New York Stock Exchange. Underwriters include Merrill Lynch, Kidder Peabody and Nomura Securities. Hambro Pacific Fund Management is launching the Smaller Asian Companies Trust on the London Stock Exchange on Wednesday, with dealings on October 7. The trust will invest predominantly in companies with a market capitalisation of less than Dollars 500m.
