Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Item by Russian Ministry of Defense Information and Press Directorate: "Russian Ministry of Defense Collegium Session"] [Text] A routine session of the Russian Federation Ministry of Defense Collegium considered the question of the status and organization of alert duty in the air defense system. Colonel General of Aviation V. A. Prudnikov, Commander-in-Chief Air Defense Troops, gave a briefing. The Ministry of Defense Collegium session also analyzed problems of further development of Military Transport Aviation in light of requirements of the "Basic Provisions of Military Doctrine" and Russian Federation Minister of Defense orders. Colonel General of Aviation V. V. Yefanov, commander of Military Transport Aviation, gave a briefing on this matter. Results of the Collegium session were summed up by Russian Minister of Defense General of the Army P. S. Grachev.
