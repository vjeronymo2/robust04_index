BFN [By reporter Zhang Sutang (1728 1372 1016) ] [Excerpts] Beijing, 10 May (XINHUA) -- Liu Jiachen, vice president of the Supreme People's Court, made an announcement in Beijing today: People's courts in various places have completed the trial of four serious cases involving theft and damage to railways, oilfields, electric power equipment, and communications facilities. Six law offenders were sentenced to death and were executed by shooting in Daqing, Liuzhou, Zhumadian and Huanggang respectively today. At a news briefing held by the Supreme People's Court, Liu Jiachen said: Recently, serious crimes involving theft and damage to railways, oilfields, and electric power and communications facilities have been rampant. Those criminal activities are very harmful to public security, social stability, state property, and the smooth building of the national economy. The carrying out of the death sentence of the six criminals who committed heinous crimes is a demonstration of the resolute and forceful struggle against such activities waged by the people's courts as state adjudication organizations. [passage omitted] After the people's courts passed their initial judgments, all six criminals appealed. The higher people's courts in Heilongjiang, Guangxi, Henan, and Hubei rejected the appeals and affirmed the original judgments in their final decisions. [passage omitted]
