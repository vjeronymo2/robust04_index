Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Strikes in Kazakhstan"] [Text] The situation in Ust-Kamenogorsk has deteriorated, The oblast's metallurgical workers have held a big rally by way of an ultimatum and demanded that the region return to the ruble zone. Major metallurgical enterprises have no money in their accounts and workers have not been paid for five months. To date metallurgical workers are owed over 35 million tenges. Ten enterprises are standing idle. And 15 are on the verge of bankruptcy. The number of unemployed in the republic has doubled.
