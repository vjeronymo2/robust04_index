BFN [Text] Yesterday [4 June], Li Ximing, vice chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, inspected the Changsha Breeding Hog Farm, an affiliate under the Hunan Provincial Tianxian Industrial General Company, the Hunan's Xin Shengming Pharmaceutical Factory, the Changsha Chicken Farm, and the Hunan's Tianxian Rolling Mills Industrial Development Company, Ltd. The establishment of the Hunan Provincial Tianxian Industrial General Company was approved by the State Planning Commission and the Hunan provincial people's government in 1987. In recent years, the company has transformed its operational mechanisms and achieved fine social and economic efficiency. It has now become the biggest food basket enterprise in our province, which covers the businesses of fodder, breeding, and food processing with emphasis on fodder. After the inspection, Vice Chairman Li Ximing fully confirmed the method adopted by the Hunan Provincial Tianxian Industrial General Company of forming an integrated system for the procurement of supplies, production, and marketing.
