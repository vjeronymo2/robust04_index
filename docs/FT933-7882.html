930819 FT 19 AUG 93 / Nigeria sets earlier deadline Nigerian Vice-President Augustus Aikhomu said yesterday the government had brought forward by two days - to August 25 - the date its military-dominated cabinet will hand over to an interim government, Reuter reports. But the confusion since annulment of the June 12 poll remained. President Ibrahim Babangida has still not said if he will quit or who new leaders will be.
