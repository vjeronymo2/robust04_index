Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Khartoum, 23 Jan (SUNA) -- The former secretary general of Anyanya Two Movement, Ben Lail Mendeng [name as received], returned home Saturday [22 January] from abroad, ending years he spent at the ranks of the so-called Sudanese opposition abroad. Mendeng was received following his return by the secretary general of the council for International People's Friendship, Dr. Mustafa 'Uthman Isma'il, welcoming him home and explaining that the opposition abroad has fallen at the hands of forces that do not want to see peace prevail in the Sudan.
