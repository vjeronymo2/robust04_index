January 4, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

HOT INTERVIEW: The Rev. Louis P. Sheldon, above, the Anaheim-based crusader for "traditional values" and a well-known foe of gay rights measures, is the subject of a major piece in Los Angeles magazine. . . . In the article, entitled "Son of Falwell," Sheldon says he wants to create "a movement, not an organization" and asserts that "I am the inspiration and prophet" of that movement. . . . Much space is devoted to Sheldon's personal sexual experiences, including at least two approaches by homosexuals. . . . Sheldon said he was shocked by the way the piece was edited, calling it "a poor attempt to distort my values and beliefs. The true Rev. Lou Sheldon did not come forth in that article." Dana Parsons and Mark I. Pinsky
