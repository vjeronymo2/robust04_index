November 8, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

TERRORIST ABBAS DIRECTS THREAT AT PRO-WESTERN ARABS

Palestinian guerrilla leader Abul Abbas has threatened unspecified reprisals against pro-Western Arab states in the Persian Gulf he accused of expelling Palestinian residents.

Abbas, head of the Baghdad-based Palestine Liberation Front best known for masterminding the 1985 hijacking of the Achille Lauro cruise ship in the Mediterranean, accused some gulf states of repressive measures against Palestinians.
