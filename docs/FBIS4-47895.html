BFN [Text] Public officers are now free to contest the election into the national constitutional conference. The chairman of the conference election committee, Mr. Bernard Mba, disclosed this today in Abuja at a news conference. Mr. Mba said the officers will be given leave of absence by their employers to contest. He said the electoral guidelines for the election at the ward and district levels will be open ballot, while delegates are to be elected at the ward level, (?and) the constituent district level will elect a delegate for the conference. [sentence as heard] The chairman stated that all Nigerians who have attained the age of 18 are eligible to vote. Mr. Mba disclosed that a committee will be constituted as part of local government [word indistinct] to administer the elections. He also called on Nigerians to ensure the success of the exercise.
