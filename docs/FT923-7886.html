920814 FT 14 AUG 92 / Separatist bombing in Thailand A bomb, alleged to have been planted by Moslem separatists, exploded at Hat Yai railway station, near the Malaysian border, in southern Thailand yesterday, killing three and injuring more than 70, Peter Ungphakorn writes from Bangkok. Police said they found at the site a letter identified as coming from the Pattani United Liberation Organisation (PULO), an outlawed group seeking independence for Thailand's four southern-most provinces.
