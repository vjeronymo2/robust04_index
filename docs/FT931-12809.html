930127 FT 27 JAN 93 / UK Company News: John D Wood cuts deficit to Pounds 77,000 John D Wood, the USM-quoted estate agent, reported a reduced pre-tax deficit of Pounds 77,000 for the six months to end-October. That compared with a loss of Pounds 194,000 and was incurred on turnover down by 7 per cent from Pounds 2.56m to Pounds 2.37m. Losses per share came out at 0.9p (1.9p).
