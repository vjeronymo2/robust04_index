940426 FT 26 APR 94 / Arms push up Kuwaiti deficit The Kuwaiti government forecasts a 40 per cent increase in its budget deficit in fiscal 1994/95 starting on July 1, a finance ministry official said in remarks published yesterday, Reuter reports from Kuwait. Finance ministry under-secretary, Mr Abdulmohsen al-Hunaif, said most of the rise was due to the inclusion for the first time of secret allocations for arms procurement that have hitherto been off-budget.
