BFN [Text] Beijing, June 28 (XINHUA) -- Wan Guoquan, vice-chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), met with a delegation headed by Khalid Tarawne [name as received], vice-president of the Jordan- China Friendship Association, here this morning.
