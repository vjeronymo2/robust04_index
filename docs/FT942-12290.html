940430 FT 30 APR 94 / 92 trade unionists murdered The extent to which trade union rights are denied across much of the developing world emerged in a report yesterday which found that 92 trade unionists were murdered last year in 14 countries. The annual survey of the International Confederation of Free Trade Unions, published yesterday (FRI), listed 91 countries with poor trade union records and recorded about 1,000 attacks on trade unionists in 22 countries.
