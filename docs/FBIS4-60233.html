BFN [Text] We have just received the following from the central news desk: Yemen President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih has announced his compliance with President Husni Mubarak's appeal to end the fighting in Yemen. President Salih affirmed to President Mubarak that a decision to end the fighting will be made in the coming hours. This was said in a telephone call that President Mubarak received from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih a few moments ago. During the telephone call, President Mubarak stressed the need to make a decision to end the fighting among all parties and along all fronts. He emphasized that the Yemeni dispute can be resolved through dialogue, understanding, and amity. President Mubarak stressed that the fighting will not decisively settle the current crisis and noted the need to safeguard Yemen's unity and the interests of the Yemeni people. Meanwhile, President Mubarak has received a telephone call from 'Ali Salim al-Bid, in which he underlined his commitment and compliance with Yemen [as heard].
