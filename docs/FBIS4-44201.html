BFN [Text] Cooperation Minister Michel Roussin arrived in Libreville yesterday to attend the zone franc summit. He stressed that African countries have expressed their solidarity with France for its intervention in Rwanda: [Begin Roussin recording] I believe that African countries have expressed their views, in any case, our partners did so. Everyone expressed its support for France, there are no hidden agendas or reservations. I believe that everyone spoke, all our friends supported France, and I believe that this was important, but this is not the reason behind our meeting. [end recording]
