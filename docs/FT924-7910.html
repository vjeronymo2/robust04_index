921113 FT 13 NOV 92 / International Company News: Sparebanken reports nine-month losses more than halved SPAREBANKEN, Norway's biggest savings bank, reported nine-month losses more than halved to NKr206m (Dollars 31.91m) from NKr523m, despite a slight increase in credit losses to NKr1.07bn from NKr1.02bn, due to a NKr100m increase in third-quarter losses. Capital adequacy was at 9.1 per cent of risk-weighted assets, exceeding the 8 per cent required minimum.
