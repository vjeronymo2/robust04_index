Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN ["Press communique" issued by the Guatemalan National Revolutionary Unity on 3 January; place not given] [Text] In view of the events in Chiapas, the Guatemalan National Revolutionary Unity (URNG) wants to state that at no time has it participated in those events. It also vigorously and categorically denies reports irresponsibly disseminated by Guatemalan Army spokespersons trying to use this situation to implicate the URNG and create an unfavorable climate at a time when, under a call from the United Nations, preliminary peace talks will be resumed with the Guatemalan Government and Army in Mexico on 6 January. The URNG has been, is, and will be respectful of Mexico's sovereignty. Therefore, our forces have no responsibility in these types of events. [Signed] URNG [Dated] 3 January 1994
