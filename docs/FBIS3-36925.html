Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report under the "Hold the Front Page!" rubric: "Poltoranin Holds Consultations"] [Text] MOSKOVSKIY KOMSOMOLETS has learned that the other day Mikhail Poltoranin had an unofficial meeting in a private club with chairmen of journalists unions of Moscow and Russia. They discussed today's problems of the press, and in particular, questions of the newspapers' economic situation. Poltoranin, who is now chairman of the Committee on Information Policy and Communications, asked the journalists to support the committee created within the Duma framework. According to information available to the editorial office, such meetings will be held continually.
