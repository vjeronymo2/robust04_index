CSO ["Election Results in Khmelnytskyy Oblast"] [Text] On 10 April, in Khmelnytskyy's Tsentralnyy e.d. No. 406, Pavlovskyy, the URP candidate, defeated Ya. Biryuk, the Rukh candidate; in Kamyanets-Podilskyy e.d. No. 407, Yablonskyy, the Rukh candidate, defeated a member of the CPU; in Shepetivka e.d. No. 409, Verkhohlyad, a collective farm chairman, defeated another collective farm chairman; in Volochysk e.d. No. 410, Holovko, an official of the agricultural administration, defeated a member of the SPU; in Horodok e.d. No. 411, Vinskyy, a CPU member, defeated the Rukh candidate; in Dunayivtsi e.d. No. 412, the deputy minister of agriculture (SPU) defeated the URP candidate; in Letychiv e.d. No. 414, Chyzh, a member of the SPU, won the parliamentary seat; in Chemerivtsi e.d. No. 416, the seat was won by Svyato, an associate of the Institute of Nuclear Research of the Academy of Sciences of Ukraine (the latter two candidates defeated collective farm chairmen). Runoff elections will be held in four electoral districts.
