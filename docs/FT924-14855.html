921008 FT 08 OCT 92 / Sterling gains 2 1/2 pfennigs STERLING GAINED 2 1/2 pfennigs yesterday, bolstering hopes that the government may be able to cut interest rates later this year, writes Peter Marsh. It climbed to DM2.4725 by the close in London, after a rise of more than 5 pfennigs on Tuesday. In New York it finished at DM2.4815. On the London stock market, speculation about an easing in credit rates lifted the FT-SE 100 index 28.7 to 2,517.1, the second successive daily rise after the sharp fall of more than 100 points on Monday.
