October 6, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

NOTES

Fluor Daniel of Irvine has received a $30-million contract from Novacor Chemicals Ltd. to provide engineering, procurement and construction management services for a polyethylene pelletizing unit. The project is expected to be completed in the first quarter of 1991. The company, a unit of Fluor, also received a $6.5-million contract from the Ajinomoto USA for work on expansion of an amino acids production facility.
