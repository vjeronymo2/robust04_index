Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over still of President Demirel] [Text] Israeli President 'Ezer Weizman called President Suleyman Demirel on the telephone yesterday and expressed his regrets over the massacre of Muslims in Hebron. A statement issued by the Presidential Press Center says that Weizman told Demirel that he went to the site of the incident and personally extended his condolences to the people. He also told Demirel that the Israeli Government is taking all the necessary measures. Demirel, in turn, expressed his deep regret over this grave incident and stressed the importance of taking every kind of measure to prevent the occurrence of such incidents.
