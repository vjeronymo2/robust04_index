930422 FT 22 APR 93 / UK Company News: Young seeks Pounds 15m from debenture Young & Co's Brewery, the south London-based real ale brewer, is to raise Pounds 15m through a placing of long-dated debenture stock. The proceeds will be used to repay existing bank loans and for 'general corporate purposes'. The debenture stock carries a coupon of 9.5 per cent and matures in 2018. The gross redemption yield is 9.557 per cent.
