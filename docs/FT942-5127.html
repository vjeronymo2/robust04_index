940606 FT 06 JUN 94 / Company News (This Week): KPN - Other companies The price of shares in Koninklijke PTT Nederland, the state-owned Dutch postal and telecommunications company, will be announced today, ahead of the start of a three-day subscription period. Analysts expect the price to be fixed in the bottom half of the Fl 46-Fl 52 range established by the underwriting syndicate last month. The Dutch state is selling a 30 per cent stake.
