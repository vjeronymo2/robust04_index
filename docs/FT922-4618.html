920604 FT 04 JUN 92 / 'Green' call over company reports COMPANIES SHOULD provide annual information on their environmental impact and the risks they face, the 100 Group of leading finance directors recommended yesterday. The group's environmental working party said companies should normally devote at least one page in the narrative section of their annual reports to 'green' issues. Environmental reporting. 100 Group, c/o KB House, Great West Road, Brentford, TW8 9BD. Free.
