920222 FT 22 FEB 92 / EC mission to CIS states THE European Community will start to examine the practical difficulties of establishing political links with the former Soviet republics tomorrow when Mr Frans Andriessen, the external affairs commissioner, begins a six-day visit to the Commonwealth of Independent States, writes Andrew Hill in Brussels. Mr Andriessen plans to visit Minsk, capital of Belarus, Kiev in Ukraine and Alma Ata in Kazakhstan. 'The main purpose is political: to explore on the spot what kind of relationship we might establish,' said an official.
