BFN [Unattributed BELAPAN report: "On Some Results of a Poll"] [Text] A group of sociologists, headed by Academician Babosaw, included a question on presidential elections in a poll that was conducted among the workers of 15 largest industrial enterprises of Belarus. Some 53 percent assured that they will participate in the elections. Around 28 percent considered that the elections of a president authorized with wide powers will have no impact on the improvement of the economic situation in Belarus. Over 10 percent of the industrial workers stated categorically that they will not participate in presidential elections. Only 12 percent of the respondents pin their hopes for improvement of living standards on the presidential elections.
