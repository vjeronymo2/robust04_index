Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to Yegor Gaydar, leader of Russia's Choice Party, the amnesty act adopted on 23 February was illegal. It was adopted by a majority of deputies in the State Duma with the aim of destabilizing the situation in Russia. Gaydar was speaking in Moscow at an assembly of democratic members of society devoted to the situation which has developed in Russia since the amnesty. Approximately 1,000 people took part in the assembly. These included representatives of the Democratic Russia Movement, the Living Ring Union, and other democratic organizations.
