September 10, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

LOCAL;

SEX PARTY RAIDED; 4 ARRESTED

Sheriff's deputies acting on an anonymous tip broke up a party involving about 200 men and up to 20 women performing a variety of sexual acts, authorities said today.

Four women ranging from 21 to 25 years old were arrested for investigation of lewd conduct in a public place after the 6 p.m. Sunday raid at a banquet hall near Los Angeles International Airport, Sheriff's Deputy Gabe Ramirez said.

Deputies said they saw between 16 and 20 nude women engaging in a number of sexual acts with the men on the floor and on various tables.

About 30 men were detained for questioning, Ramirez said.
