921012 FT 12 OCT 92 / International Company News: Hewlett to cut 2,000 jobs in US HEWLETT-Packard, the US computer group, plans to cut 2,700 jobs, including 2,000 in the US, through a voluntary severance incentive programme, Louise Kehoe reports from San Francisco. A fourth-quarter charge of about 40 cents a share will cover the cost of programme.
