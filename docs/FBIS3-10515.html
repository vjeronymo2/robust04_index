Language: French Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The members of the Haitian Episcopal Conference [CEH] will go to Rome around 15 March reportedly to meet with Pope John II. Relations between the Aristide government and the Vatican have always been very strained. However, over the past weekend, President Aristide said he hopes for a coming together of his government and the Holy See. Jean-Bertrand Aristide labeled the contacts started last week between Vatican and his government a step in the right direction. [passage omitted]
