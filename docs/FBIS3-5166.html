Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, March 3 (KPL) -- Lao Trade Minister Sompadit Volasan yesterday had talks with the trade and industry minister of Mongolia who is member of the Mongolian president's delegation to Laos. The two trade ministers discussed trade activities and exchanged experience on trade management system during the period of shifting to a new mechanism. They shared the view that the bilateral trade relations should be reviewed. During the visit, the two countries have yet to sign an agreement to restore their trade relations and cooperation, which have been halted since 1989. In the morning of the same day, the president of the Lao National Chamber of Commerce and Industry, Mr. Sisouk Sisombat also met with his Mongolian counterpart, and businessmen of 14 Mongolian companies, both state and private, involved in three sectors: trade industry, agriculture and services.
