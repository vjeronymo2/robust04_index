BFN [Text] No progress was made in the Cairo talks today, our correspondent Moti Amir reports. The Palestinians presented a detailed working paper on water matters, and Israeli experts will go to Cairo tonight to study it. Differences surfaced in the subcommittee on communications and following the intervention of the delegation heads, the Palestinians withdrew their demand to reopen issues already agreed upon two months ago. Muhammad Dahlan told our correspondent that six deportees would arrive in the Gaza Strip tomorrow, and another 10 on Friday. He noted that there has still not been any progress on the release of Palestinian detainees. He said Israel now agrees to release only 4,000 detainees although last week it agreed to free 5,000. The PLO is demanding the release of 8,300 detainees, including Hamas activists.
