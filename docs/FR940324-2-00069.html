In addition, funding preference will be given to applicants that are based at, or have relationships with, entities providing comprehensive health services to people who are infected with HIV. Coordinating center applications recommended for approval by the IRG and the appropriate advisory council will be considered for funding on the basis of overall technical merit as determined through the review process, suitability for working with the services demonstration projects funded, and the availability of funds.
