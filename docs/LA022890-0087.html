February 28, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

A SUPER TRIFECTA PAYOFF -- $25,430

The Super Trifecta at Sportsman's Race Course Tuesday produced a payoff of $25,430 to the holder of the sole $1 winning ticket.

The winner had in correct order the 1-2-3 finishers in the fourth race and the 1-2-3-4 finishers in the fifth race.
