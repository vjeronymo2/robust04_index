920205 FT 05 FEB 92 / International Capital Markets: Salomon official joins rival firm MR George Balduzzi, a senior official in Salomon Brothers' New York equities operations, has joined a wave of defections from the troubled securities house and joined rival CS First Boston, writes Martin Dickson. Mr Balduzzi headed the block trading desk in Salomon's stocks department. First Boston said he would jointhe firm as a stock trader.
