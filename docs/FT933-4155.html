930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / EC move on Lome Convention The European Commission yesterday unveiled plans to overhaul the Lome Convention, the much-criticised regime governing trade and aid with 69 African, Caribbean and Pacific countries, writes Lionel Barber in Brussels. Mr Manuel Marn, EC commissioner responsible for development policy and a Spanish socialist, said it was vital to introduce tighter controls to counter inefficiency and corruption, while bolstering democratic reform and the private sector.
