940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / International Company News: Petron shares up for auction The Philippine government will auction to the highest bidder 300m shares in Petron, the country's biggest oil refiner, Reuter reports from Manila. Mr Delfin Lazaro, Energy Secretary, said: 'It is possible that a single bidder can acquire all 300m shares.' A further 700m will be sold at a fixed price of between seven and 11 pesos (US 40 cents). The government plans to retain 40 per cent of Petron through Philippine National Oil. Petron is expected to be listed on the Philippine Stock Exchange by September.
