Language: Thai Article Type:BFN [Text] Speaking to reporters at Government House this morning about the report on the terrorist responsible for the bombing of the U.S. World Trade Center hiding in Thailand, General Charan Kunlawanit, said that was the story presented by a news agency. However, the problem of terrorism seems to exist in all countries. Anyone who has information on this issue is requested to contact the authorities. Thai authorities are ready to conduct an investigation into the matter and arrest such a person. Thailand has been cooperating with foreign countries and agencies in the prevention and suppression of international terrorism.
