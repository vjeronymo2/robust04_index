930128 FT 28 JAN 93 / British naval task force arrives in Adriatic A British naval task force headed by the aircraft carrier Ark Royal arrived in the Adriatic on Tuesday, a Royal Navy spokesman said yesterday, and was now off the coast of the former Yugoslavia, Reuters reports. The task force is also carrying reinforcements for the 2,400 British troops helping to protect UN aid convoys in Bosnia. France is also sending an eight-ship aircraft carrier battle group to the Adriatic as support for some 5,000 French troops serving with UN peacekeeping forces in the ex-Yugoslavia. The French force, headed by the aircraft carrier Clemenceau, is expected in the Adriatic this weekend.
