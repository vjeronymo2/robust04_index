940601 FT 01 JUN 94 / European Elections: FT election opinion poll - how Europeans see the future of their continent The poll, commissioned by the Financial Times in association with Suddeutsche Zeitung (Munich), Les Echos (Paris), Il Sole 24 Ore (Milan), La Vanguardia (Barcelona), de Volkskrant (Amsterdam), Svenska Dagbladet (Stockholm), and Diario de Noticias (Lisbon), was carried out by Harris Research. It is based on telephone interviews carried out between May 11 and May 24 with 6,439 people aged 18 and over throughout Europe. The following telephone interviews were carried out: Belgium (501), Denmark (500), France (501), west Germany (501), east Germany (426), Greece (500), Ireland (500), Spain (504), UK (600). Further details available from Harris Research. Tel 081-9485011. fax 081-9486335.
