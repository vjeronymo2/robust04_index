920407 FT 07 APR 92 / Election 1992: FT Election Share Index AS THE market as a whole recovered from recent gloom, both parts of the FT Election Share Index fell. The biggest loser among the 'Tory gainers' was Prudential, the insurance company, which fell 6 1/2 p to 202p. 'Labour gainer' APV, the manufacturer of food processing machinery, fell 4p to 101p. The Tory portion of the index is now nearly 14 per cent below its level at the start of the campaign.
