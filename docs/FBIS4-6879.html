BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 22 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- At the Ministry of Trade and Industry of Mongolia there has been signed an agreement on establishing jointly by the ministry and the embassy of the USA to Mongolia of a joint fund for crediting. The agreement was signed by the Director General of the Department of Industry and Foreign Investment of the Ministry of Trade and Industry of Mongolia H. Ganbaatar and the Director General of the "Mongol Daatgal" Bank S. Goohuu. The financial resources of the fund worth 300 million tugrik would be assigned for the development of small and medium businesses, private sector in the five western aymags of Mongolia. Present in signing ceremony of the agreement were the Minister of Trade and Industry T. Tsogt the Ambassador of the USA to Mongolia J. Johnson and other officials.
