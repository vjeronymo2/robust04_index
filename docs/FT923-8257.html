920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / World News in Brief: Woman succeeds Halford Alison Halford is to be succeeded by Pauline Clare as an assistant chief constable with Merseyside police. Chief Superintendent Clare is the sixth woman to reach ACC or equivalent rank in Britain. Ms Halford is to retire early after an industrial tribunal case in which she alleged sex discrimination ended last month with a deal struck between lawyers.
