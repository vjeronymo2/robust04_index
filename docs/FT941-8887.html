940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Pains Fireworks acquisition Pains Fireworks, the UK's oldest firework company, has acquired Bournemouth-based Nationwide Fireworks and as a result, now claims to be the country's leading firework display company. Pains said the purchase was a logical move that would bring significant marketing benefits. Eight displays for the English Philharmonic Orchestra and two other substantial concert displays are among the events that have been added to Pains' 1994 schedule following the acquisition.
