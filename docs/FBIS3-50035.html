Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] Continuing his talks in Tirana today, Greek Alternate National Economy Minister Papandoniou was received by Deputy Prime Minister Bashkim Kopliku. The Kopliku-Papandoniou talks concentrated on Albania's needs for investments and, in this framework, they discussed what both parties can profit from the EC project on transports. Mentioning the fund of 350 milliard [billion] ECU that the EC is offering for the European project on transportation, for road, air, and naval transportation, and especially for ports, something which is favored by the position of Greece as EC president, Mr. Papandoniou stressed the interests of both sides and the need to profit in time. Albanian Deputy Prime Minister Kopliku made an outline of the present state of the development of the reform and the need that this phase presents for investments. They also discussed concrete fields of cooperation and stressed the need to strengthen economic cooperation, something which favors the strengthening of bilateral relations in general.
