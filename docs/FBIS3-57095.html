Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary corespondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow January 21 TASS -- The Russian State Duma turned down on Friday a proposal to adopt an official statement on the new composition of the government, but agreed to discuss the situation in former Yugoslavia and consider the resumption of the "Parliament Hour" tv programme which the former legislature introduced to advocate its anti-presidential views. The Duma will also consider the composition of the geopolitical committee headed by a representative of the party of nationalist leader Vladimir Zhirinovskiy. It is the only out of 23 committees which the Duma failed to approve on Thursday [20 January].
