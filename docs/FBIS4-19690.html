BFN [Text] BTA reports that today Turkish President Suleyman Demirel received Aleksandur Tomov, leader of Civic Alliance for the Republic [GOR]. Demirel and Tomov discussed bilateral political and economic relations during their talk, which lasted longer than planned. They outlined specific ideas and ways for extending economic cooperation between Bulgaria and Turkey. They also examined the situation in the Balkans and the conflict in Bosnia- Herzegovina in particular. Mr. Tomov explained to President Demirel the Bulgarian position of absolute nonintervention in the Yugoslav conflict in any form whatsoever.
