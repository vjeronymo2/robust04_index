BFN ["Today" -- BTA lead] [Excerpt] Sofia, April 3 (BTA) -- The 41st congress of the Bulgarian Socialist Party (BSP, the former communists) will be held June 3-5, the party's leadership and the parliamentary BSP decided at a meeting behind closed doors today. In a televised interview the party's ex-leader Aleksandur Lilov said that is is obvious that the congress needs to adopt a programme of the party. The party's attitude to the government was also discussed at today's meeting. [passage omitted]
