Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] PRC Ambassador to Mozambique (Li Chi Heng) has announced that PRC entrepreneurs are interested in investing in various social and economic areas in Mozambique. The PRC diplomat said that there are two joint fishing companies in operation in Mozambique at this stage and that PRC entrepreneurship is involved in those companies. Commenting on bilateral cooperation, the PRC ambassador said that there are nine PRC medical doctors working in Maputo Central Hospital, two of whom are on contract.
