Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] A strong homemade device exploded at 2330 yesterday at the Petmezas and Anglogalos car showroom in Amfiali, Pireaivs. The explosion damaged six cars inside the showroom and another two that were parked outside. One month ago, a homemade device that exploded was planted at another Petmezas showroom for air conditioning units.
