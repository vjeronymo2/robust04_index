April 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: FOOD, CLOTHING DRIVE FOR THE NEEDY SCHEDULED

M:25, a Cal Poly Pomona student organization dedicated to helping the needy, will begin a food and clothing drive on April 16 and will distribute the contributions to orphanages and needy families in Baja, Mexico, on April 21 and 22.

Individuals or groups interested in contributing may contact Hector Wong at (818) 447-1891 or Diana Lugo at (714) 869-3606.
