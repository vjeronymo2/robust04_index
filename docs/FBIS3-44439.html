Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Gao Dezhan, secretary of the municipal party committee, received Cheng Tong Fatt, ambassador of Singapore in China, in the Guoji Building noon on 22 December. During the reception Gao Dezhan said: Tianjin is further broadening its program of opening up and accelerating the utilization of foreign capital, and cooperating with foreign countries in all directions, in multiple fields, and at multiple levels. The municipality welcomes investments and projects of foreign countries in every corner in the world. Gao Dezhan welcomed the arrival of Ambassador Cheng Tong Fatt. He said: Opening the municipality to the outside world and utilizing foreign capital represents a fundamental plan for the municipality to accelerate its development. Tianjin has many land resources and foundations of industry and commerce. It also possesses scientific and technological forces and convenient air, maritime, and highway transportation facilities. Based on existing cooperation with Singapore, we are willing to further more extensive, in depth cooperation on a larger scale. In order to become one of the country's best, most attractive regions for foreign investments, Tianjin must make it easy for foreign businessmen and firms to invest their money in the municipality, and make things convenient and profitable. We welcome Singaporeans to make more fact finding visits in the municipality and to learn about the municipal investment environment in order to broaden cooperation and exchanges between Tianjin and Singapore. Attending the reception were Ye Disheng, vice mayor of the municipality, and responsible persons from the departments concerned.
