930114 FT 14 JAN 93 / UK Company News: TGI sells remaining stake in Goodmans Inds TGI, the professional and consumer audio manufacturer, has sold its remaining investment in Goodmans Industries for Pounds 1.55m. That represented a Pounds 1m premium over book value. The consideration is being satisfied as to Pounds 1.43m cash with the balance payable in September 1993. Goodmans returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 432,000 for the year to January 31, 1992. Net assets at that date amounted to Pounds 2.09m.
