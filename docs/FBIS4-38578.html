BFN [Text] Tomorrow, 1 June, the [pro-Georgian Tbilisi-based] Council of Ministers of the Autonomous Republic of Abkhazia is moving office to Zugdidi, from where it will continue to perform its duties. The Council of Ministers of Abkhazia has issued the following statement. In connection with the start of peacekeeping operations in Abkhazia and preparation for the first stage of the return of refugees and displaced persons to their homeland, the Council of Ministers of the Autonomous Republic of Abkhazia requests the governors of the town of Gali and Gali Rayon villages, the heads of enterprises, institutions, and organizations, and representatives of government structures to take part in a republican meeting which will be held in the governor's office in the town of Zugdidi at 1200 on 3 June.
