Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Jan. 17 -- Admiral Charles R. Larson, commander-in-chief of US Pacific Command, arrived here on Sunday for a three-day visit to Vietnam. He is the most senior US active-duty officer to visit since the end of the Vietnam War in 1975. Larson's visit is aimed at getting more first-hand information about Vietnam and about the MIA issue. Yesterday afternoon, Admiral Larson and his entourage were received by Vietnamese Deputy Minister of Defence Nguyen Thoi Bung who reaffirmed that Vietnam regarded, the MIA issue as a humanitarian one. `No matter how', said Deputy Minister Bung, `the relations between the United States and Vietnam are developing, Vietnam would always actively cooperate with the US in the MIA issue'.
