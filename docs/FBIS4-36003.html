BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 2 Jun (WAKH) -- His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, United Arab Emirates [UAE] president, this evening received a telephone call from 'Ali Salim al-Bid, leader of the Yemeni Socialist Party. During the conversation, they discussed developments in Yemen. President 'Ali Salim al-Bid once again expressed his appreciation for His Highness Shaykh Zayid's continued efforts and his eagerness to spare the blood of the Yemeni brethren, save the Yemeni people from the woes of the war raging there, and achieve security and stability in Yemen.
