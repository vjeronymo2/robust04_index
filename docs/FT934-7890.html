931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / Eggar backs market forces A STRONG restatement of the government's determination to allow market forces to rule in the energy market was given yesterday by Mr Tim Eggar, the energy minister. He said the government wanted to ensure that people and businesses got the energy they wanted at competitive prices. 'This is achieved most efficiently and effectively through competitive markets, which expose real costs and lead to keenest pricing.'
