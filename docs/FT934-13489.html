931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Estates & Agency ESTATES & AGENCY: The contract to sell a 10-acre freehold in Plymouth, Devon, to J Sainsbury has become unconditional. The consideration is Pounds 18.1m cash and will be used to repay short-term borrowings. The site was revalued at Pounds 7.5m in E&A's accounts for the year to June 30 1992.
