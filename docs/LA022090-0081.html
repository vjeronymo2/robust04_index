February 20, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

DOMINO WATCHER: Newport Beach Republican C. Christopher Cox is Lithuania bound today with three other U.S. congressmen to monitor Saturday's legislative elections. . . . Cox predicts that candidates advocating independence from the Soviet Union will win many of the 141 seats on the Supreme Soviet. . . . "They really expect . . . a lot of turnover," said Cox, one of two Republicans and two Democrats appointed to monitor the polling. "We may see the first set of dominoes falling along the Baltic." Dana Parsons and Kristina Lindgren
