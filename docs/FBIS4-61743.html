BFN [Russian Federation Presidential Edict No. 847, signed by President Yeltsin and dated 29 April 1994: "On Additional Measures To Streamline the Recruitment and Utilization of Foreign Labor in the Russian Federation"] [Text] With a view to streamlining the recruitment and utilization of foreign labor in the Russian Federation and to ensuring that Russian Federation citizens take priority in filling vacancies, I decree that: 1. Russian Federation Presidential Edict No. 2146 of 16 December 1993 "On the Recruitment and Utilization of Foreign Labor in the Russian Federation" (SOBRANIYE AKTOV PREZIDENTA I PRAVITELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII, No. 51, 1993, p 4934) be extended to cover foreign corporate bodies implementing contracts on Russian Federation territory, with the exception of foreign corporate bodies which have seconded their workers to the Russian Federation to install (or to supervise the installation of) equipment they have supplied. 2. This edict comes into force from the moment it is published. [Signed] B. Yeltsin, president of the Russian Federation Moscow, the Kremlin 29 April 1994 No. 847
