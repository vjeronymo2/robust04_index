Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] [Announcer Sergey Vozianov] (Vasil Koberidze), an officer of the Georgian Ministry of Security was killed in Tbilisi last night. This is the fourth terrorist act in the past two weeks carried out against high-ranking members of Georgia's "power" structures. None was investigated. Deputies of the parliament demanded that Eduard Shevardnadze and Giorgadze, security minister, report on the progress of investigating the terrorist acts.
