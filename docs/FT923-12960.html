920716 FT 16 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Another French buy for Brake Bros Brake Bros, the supplier of frozen foods to the catering industry, is to purchase the Cogehalles group, an independent supplier of frozen food to caterers in the Paris area. This is Brake's second French acquisition this year. The consideration will include a maximum of 150,000 ordinary shares, with the balance in cash. Cogehalles produced turnover of FFr113m in 1991, and its net assets were FFr3.5m.
