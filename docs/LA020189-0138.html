February 1, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

EIP MICROWAVE POSTS LOSS FOR QUARTER

EIP Microwave, Newport Beach, reported a loss of $192,000 for its first quarter ended Dec. 31, 1988, contrasted with a year-earlier loss of $729,000. Sales rose 26% to $3.1 million from $2.5 million.

The current year's first-quarter loss included a pretax charge against operations of $1.1 million resulting from the write-down of certain assets associated with the company's realignment of technical, financial and management resources. Partially offsetting the loss was a gain of $748,000 resulting from an accounting change.

The company manufactures microwave test and measurement instruments used in the defense and telecommunications industries.
