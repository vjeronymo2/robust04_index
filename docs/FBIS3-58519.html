Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Death Threat Against Mayor Zilk"] [Text] A death threat against Vienna Mayor Helmut Zilk, who is still in the hospital after a letter-bomb attack, was received at the headquarters of the Social Democratic Party of Austria [SPOe] on Friday [14 January]. An anonymous caller threatened that Zilk will suffer the same fate as city councillor Heinz Nittel, who was shot dead on 1 May 1981. Party secretary Grete Dumser stated that the caller told the central office of the SPOe that he wanted to "make a political statement." He was then connected with the party's service office where he voiced his threat. The state police have initiated investigations.
