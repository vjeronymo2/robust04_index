931013 FT 13 OCT 93 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton and jute JUTE C and F Dundee: BTC Dollars 355, BWC Dollars 365, BTD Dollars 330, BWD Dollars 340. C and F Antwerp: BTC Dollars 330, BWC Dollars 330, BTD Dollars 300, BWD Dollars 300. COTTON Liverpool- spot and shipment sales amounted to 32 tonnes for the week ended 8 October, against 40 tonnes in the previous week. Subdued offtake did not bring many operations. Support was forthcoming in certain specialist styles notably in Turkish range.
