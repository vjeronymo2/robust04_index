BFN [Text] Guatemala City, 2 Apr (NOTIMEX) -- Constitutionality Court President Epaminondas Gonzalez was killed this morning by a group of unidentified gunmen who shot him as he entered his home, a local radio station reported today. Sources close to the Gonzalez family said the magistrate was attacked this morning in the downtown Zona Dos neighborhood of the capital a few blocks from the National Palace as he entered his home. He died early this morning. Gonzalez sustained a fatal gunshot wound to the left side of his chest, according to Radio Sonora. Last week the Constitutionality Court had declared the electoral law reforms being debated by the congressional deputies unconstitutional because "the reforms totally change the law." The reforms were agreed upon by the deputies and President Ramiro de Leon Carpio as part of a package of reforms to the Constitution which was approved in the January referendum.
