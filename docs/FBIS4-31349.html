BFN [Text] Tirana, June 7 (ATA) -- Representatives of the Albanological Institute received a letter from the so-called prefect of the Kosova circle, Milosh Simovio, addressed to the director of the institute Dr. Sadri Fetiu. The letter says that the decision on the conditions and sequestration of the building of the institute have been re-examined and as a result the previous decision taken is invalid. According to the letter, the present user of the building of the Albanological Institute should evacuate the building until June 10 this year to hand it to the Kosova circle. After the building was sequestered from the institute and the Serbian violence, the Albanological Institute of Prishtina continued its work in a private building.
