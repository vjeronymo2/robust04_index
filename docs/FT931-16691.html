930105 FT 05 JAN 93 / International Company News: Keppel unit to invest in China holiday resort STRAITS Steamship Land, part of the diversified Singapore-based Keppel conglomerate, has agreed with the local authorities in China's Yunnan province to develop a large holiday and golf resort. Straits is to invest an initial USDollars 10m in the project, and is committing Dollars 40m for a later stage. Temasek Holdings, the Singapore government's investment holding company, will also take up a stake.
