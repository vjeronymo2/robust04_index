October 13, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

JURY VERDICTS IN CINCINNATI, FT. LAUDERDALE

I couldn't have said it better than you did in your editorial.

However, the headline is odd. The "ugly lyrics" came from the Florida court more than they did from a popular recording. The beautiful principle appeared in your last paragraph. Without freedom of speech, there is no freedom.

KENNETH H. FLEISCHER

Los Angeles
