Language: Estonian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Foreign Minister Juri Luik flew to Copenhagen an hour ago. The foreign ministers of all three Baltic countries will be meeting there today at an invitation from the Danish foreign minister. I have asked Juri Luik by telephone what exactly is going to happen in the Danish capital. [Begin Luik recording] A quadripartite meeting will take place, with foreign ministers of the three Baltic countries and Danish Foreign Minister Niels Helveg Petersen attending. We will discuss the Baltic countries taking part in the work of international organizations, the Baltic security policy will be discussed, with the key issue being Estonian, Latvian and Lithuanian integration into NATO and the European Union. [passage omitted] There will definitely be talk of the security situation in the Baltic region, with the recent Russian statements, [Russian Foreign Minister] Andrey Kozyrev's latest, very worrying statements and interviews under discussion. I think that we will formulate a joint position on this matter and I am almost certain that it will be negative [words indistinct] [end recording]
