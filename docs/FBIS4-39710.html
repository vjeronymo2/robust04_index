BFN [Text] The Savings Bank of Belarus issued a report for Supreme Soviet consideration concerning [word indistinct] the indexation of individual deposits. This document is a reply to Prime Minister Vyachaslaw Kebich's statement regarding the necessity to issue 100 percent compensations for people's depository losses prompted by inflation. According to experts' estimates, over 1 trillion rubles is required to cover the losses -- caused by the devaluation of individual deposits -- by 100 percent. It goes without saying that it is necessary to repay the population what it lost not through its own will, believes Alyaksandr Barkun, deputy chairman of the Savings Bank of Belarus. However, two issues -- by exactly what sum the deposits will be increased and who will pay -- remain unresolved.
