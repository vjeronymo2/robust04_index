BFN [Text] Belgrade, 12 Apr -- The Federal Yugoslav Information Ministry said in a statement this evening that accreditations would be withdrawn from the French AFP news agency correspondent and the correspondent of the American CNN TV network. The statement reads: ``Federal Information Minister Slobodan Ignjatovic has decided to take legal measures against the correspondents who have been waging a fierce media war against Yugoslavia, and this on the territory of Yugoslavia. This practically means that accreditations will be withdrawn from the AFP and CNN correspondents and they will not be permitted to do any further work from tomorrow, 13 April. "Acreditations will also be withdrawn in the next few days from other foreign media correspondents who have been demonizing the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia and the Serb people for months, and some of them for years," the statement says.
