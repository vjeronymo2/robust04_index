Here is a report by Senada Cumurovic: [Begin recording] [Cumurovic] At the beginning of the radio link Sead Ohranovic said that it is mostly calm in Gorazde today although there are some chetnik provocations. [Ohranovic] These reports are in no way correct. The chetniks have recently been using some ammunition, that is, explosive ammunition that has [words indistinct] to shoot behind one's back. As regards the humanitarian situation, the food supplies are somewhat improved. [Ohranovic] However, the greatest problems that the citizens of Gorazde are now facing are is the accommodation of the population that has been streaming from the occupied regions into the town and the problem of water. In such unhygienic conditions, this will be fatal if something is not done urgently. [Cumurovic] In three days, 265 wounded people have been evacuated from Gorazde but the need for further evacuation is still great. At this moment, UNPROFOR teams are visiting villages in the commune in order to transport people to Gorazde and then further on. At the end of the radio bridge, Ohranovic said that the security situation in Gorazde has nevertheless been stabilizing.
