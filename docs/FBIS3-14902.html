Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Mircea Dascaliuc report from Chisinau] [Text] After having entered into the computers the election results coming from 82 percent of the total number of electoral constituencies on the entire territory of the Republic of Moldova, this evening the Central Electoral Commission released for the mass media the rating of the first seven political forces, as follows: The Agrarian Democratic Party -- 43.21 percent; The Bloc of the Socialist Party and the Edinstvo Unity Movement -- 21.77 percent; The Bloc of Peasants and Intellectuals -- 8.98 percent; The Christian-Democratic Popular Front Alliance -- 7.31 percent; The Social-Democratic Party -- 3.51 percent; The Democratic Party of Labor -- 2.55 percent; The Reform Party -- 2.46 percent. The chairman of the Central Electoral Commission said that tomorrow they will proceed to examine the electoral documents that were sent to them by each electoral constituency. The chairman also pointed out that he firmly rejects all assertions circulating false data claiming that the source was the Central Electoral Commission. He also mentioned the fact that the results that have been published until now do not have an absolute value and do not represent the final results of the 27 February elections.
