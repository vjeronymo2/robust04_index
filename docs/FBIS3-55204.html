Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The strike to be staged by Russian newspaper journalists, timed to coincide with the parliamentary week of 10-17 January, may actually take place. A POSTFAKTUM correspondent has learned that this evening all the editorial offices on the territory of Russia will receive faxes containing an appeal to strike from the two unions of journalists, of Russia and of Moscow. ITAR-TASS provides information about a number of demands being submitted to the government of the Russian Federation by journalists of the organizations, with yet another appeal to Viktor Chernomyrdin. Journalists think it necessary to establish purpose-orientated subsidies for all editions for the first half of 1994 to provide for the publication of issues that have already been paid for by subscribers.
