BFN [Excerpt] Kiev, 13 Jun -- The Supreme Council Presidium held a meeting chaired by Oleksandr Moroz. The following new commission chairmen were agreed: - Commission for Procedures, Deputy Ethics, and Safeguards of Deputy Activities: chairman Petro Sheyko; - Commission for Chernobyl Catastrophe Issues: chairman Volodymyr Yatsenko; - Commission for Defense and State Security: chairman Volodymyr Mukhin; - Commission for Nuclear Policy and Nuclear Safety: chairman Mykhaylo Pavlovskyy.
