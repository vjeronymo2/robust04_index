Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Taking account of its geopolitical situation, independent and democratic Ukraine will play an important part in creating security on the continent. This was stated by Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko while on an official visit to Brussels. He met journalists during the ceremony of signing a document on Ukraine's participation in the Partnership for Peace program. What can our country offer NATO as a partner? Answering this question, the head of Ukraine's Foreign Ministry said that participation in the Partnership for Peace would be in three directions: Primarily, this is joint military personnel exercises and training; participation in peace-keeping operations; and the development of these peacekeeping operations. He emphasized that Ukraine would take part in all programs linked with (?maintaining peace). Journalists also showed interest in Zlenko's opinion with regard to what the reaction of Russia would be to Ukraine's involvement in the Partnership for Peace. To all appearances it will be positive, Anatoliy Zlenko answered.
