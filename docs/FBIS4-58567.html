BFN [Excerpt] A PLO official has said that the Palestinian command has postponed the entrance of the police force to the Gaza Strip and the Jericho area until next week for technical reasons dealing with preparing the police force to go there. In a telephone intervierw with our radio station, the PLO official said that Saudi planes will transport the police members from their locations in Arab countries to the Egyptian city of al-'Arish on the borders with the Gaza Strip and that they will enter from there in full military dress with their weapons and equipment.
