Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Council of Ministers held a meeting today under First Deputy Prime Minister Dr. Hasan Makki and discussed the Document of Pledge and Accord, issued by the Political Forces Dialogue Committee. The Council of Ministers reviewed the document, which includes measures to build a Yemeni state, and expressed its satisfaction with what had been achieved. It also voiced its gratitude to the brothers in the dialogue committee and to those who contributed to persisting efforts that have produced that historic document. [passage omitted]
