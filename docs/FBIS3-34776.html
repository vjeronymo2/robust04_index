Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Feb. 14, IRNA -- Iran's Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati here Monday appointed Mohammad Hashemi as his first deputy. Hashemi has served as head of the Islamic Republic of Iran Broadcasting (IRIB) for the past 13 years. In a decree on Sunday, leader of the Islamic revolution Ayatollah 'Ali Khamene'i appointed former Islamic Guidance Minister 'Ali Larijani as the new IRIB head.
