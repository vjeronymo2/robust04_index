August 16, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Ten cities have filed formal applications for the next two NHL expansion franchises.

Eight U.S. cities -- Milwaukee, Seattle, San Diego, Phoenix, Houston, Miami, Tampa and St. Petersburg, Fla., and two Canadian entries, Hamilton and Ottawa, Ontario -- submitted applications and the $100,000 filing fee, $65,000 of which is refundable. The two teams accepted into the league will pay a $50-million franchise fee.

The NHL is expected to announce its choices in December.
