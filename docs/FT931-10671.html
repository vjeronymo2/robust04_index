930206 FT 06 FEB 93 / Finance & The Family: Sharelink US shares service - At a glance Sharelink is launching an instant dealing service in US shares for UK investors. The service will be open during US market hours (2.30 to 9pm UK time). Minimum commission (on deals under Dollars 2,533) is Dollars 38 (around Pounds 26); on deals between Dollars 2,534 and Dollars 5,000, the rate is 1.5 per cent; the rate is then 0.75 per cent on the next Dollars 5,000; and 0.1 per cent on additional amounts over Dollars 10,000. For further details, call 021-200-4888.
