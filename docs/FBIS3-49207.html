Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] A UN High Commission for Refugees [UNHCR] convoy, which left Metkovic this morning for Zenica and Tuzla, was stopped at the Croatian Defense Council's [HVO] border checkpoint because of its failure to ask for a permit from the mixed commission and to fulfill other conditions required by the protocol on the organization of humanitarian operations, the Croat Republic of Herceg-Bosna government office for cooperation with the UN Protection Forces [UNPROFOR], the European Union [EU], and international organizations says in its statement. The convoy was instructed to return to Metkovic because the vehicles arrived at the border checkpoint without the necessary documents required by the regulations and agreements on humanitarian convoys and because the cargo was not checked at the point of departure.
