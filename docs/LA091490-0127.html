September 14, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

GLENN'S 13 KILLS LEAD CHRIST COLLEGE IRVINE

Julie Glenn had 13 kills to lead Christ College Irvine to a three-game sweep of Redlands in a nonconference women's volleyball match at Christ College Irvine Thursday.

Christ College Irvine won, 15-9, 15-8, 15-11. Miriam Paulus had 10 kills for Christ College Irvine (4-8).
