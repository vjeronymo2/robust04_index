BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 16 (KCNA) -- A seminar of the Ministry of People's Armed Forces was held Wednesday to commemorate the 30th anniversary of the start of work by Comrade Kim Chong-il, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, at the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK]. Choe Kwang, Politburo member of the C.C. [Central Committee], the WPK and chief of the General Staff of the KPA [Korean People's Army], and several other generals made speeches on the subjects including "Holding in high esteem respected Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il at the head of our party is historical event which brought great switchover in accomplishing the revolutionary cause of chuche," "Great leadership of respected Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il in making the whole Army crystal of singlehearted unity." The speakers said that today all the officers and men of the People's Army have grown up to be loyal subjects and filial soldiers resolutely defending the party and the leader at the cost of their lives and the People's Army strengthened and developed into the invincible revolutionary Armed Forces capable of defeating any formidable enemy at one stroke. This is entirely a noble fruition of the great leadership of Comrade Kim Chong-il, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, they stressed. Noting that the last 30-year course of the People's Army is shining as proud annals in which the singlehearted unity of the whole Army centering on the great brilliant commander has been cemented as firm as a rock and has emerged victorious with its might, they pointed out that the supreme commander has resolutely defended the traditions of the singlehearted unity and more firmly realized the ideological and purposeful unity of the whole Army centering on the leader from the first period of his leadership over the revolutionary Armed Forces. The speakers said that Comrade Kim Chong-il has strengthened and developed the revolutionary Armed Forces into a true army of the leader and the party. They stressed that the People's Army is exalting its might as a powerful a-match-for-a-hundred army thanks to the great brilliant commander Comrade Kim Chong-il's chuche-based idea of army building and his extraordinary leadership, and herein lie his undying feats which will shine for ages with the victorious advance of the revolutionary cause of chuche.
