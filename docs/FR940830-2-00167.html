Chairperson, David L. Jordan, Acting Chief Counsel; 2. Dennis I. Foreman, Deputy General Counsel; 3. Sarah Hall Ingram, Associate Chief Counsel (Employee Benefits and Exempt Organizations); 4. Martha Sullivan, Central Regional Counsel; 5. Mark S. Kaizen, Assistant Chief Counsel (General Legal Services); 6. William F. Hammack, Dallas District Counsel.
