Language: English Article Type:BFN [By Lilian Wu] [Text] Singapore, Jan. 5 (CNA) -- Republic of China [ROC] Premier Lien Chan will return to Taipei Wednesday [5 January] after concluding a week-long private visit of Malaysia and Singapore. Lien attended a dinner party hosted by Senior Minister Lee Kuan Yew Tuesday night as he wrapped up the Singapore leg of his tour. Guests in the party included Singaporean officials and ROC Director- General Jason Hu of the Government Information Office. Lien started his vacation in Malaysia on Dec. 30. He met there with Prime Minister Mahathir Bin Mohamed and other high-ranking Malaysian officials. During his four-day visit in Singapore, Lien also met with Prime Minister Goh Chok Tong and cabinet ministers.
