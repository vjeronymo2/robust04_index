BFN [Text] Meeting (?Umberto Pestalozzi), Italian ambassador to Latvia, Prime Minister Valdis Birkavs today said that on 12 April Latvia asked the Vienna Forum of the CSCE in Europe to consider the new situation in Latvian-Russian relations, which has come about in connection with Russian President Boris Yeltsin's order of 5 April. Birkavs stressed that the Latvian side will be prepared to resume further talks on the signing of the treaty on the Russian Army withdrawal and agreements related to it only when an official confirmation of the cancellation of the order is received from the Russian president. Latvia will also insist for the agreement on social guarantees for discharged Russian military personnel to be supplemented with a (?clause) on the conditions for a voluntary repatriation of such people. It is planned to set up a special fund to finance the repatriation. Latvia, Russia, and third countries will be able to participate in this fund. Latvia will control the fund's money. Birkavs has called on (?Pestalozzi), as a representative of a country which is holding the CSCE Presidency, with an appeal to support the repatriation of discharged military personnel, help rehabilitate the Skrunda area, and to send CSCE representatives to observe the work of the Skrunda facility.
