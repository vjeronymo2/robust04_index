930903 FT 03 SEP 93 / Arts: Arts award THE SHORT list was announced yesterday for the 1993 Prudential Awards for the Arts which is worth Pounds 100,000 writes Antony Thorncroft. The five on the short list all of whom receive Pounds 25,000, are Shobana Jeyasingh, who merges dance styles; The Series (a fusion by Birmingham Contemporary Music Group and Birmingham Jazz); Opera North; Theatre de Complicite; and Portfolio Gallery. The Pounds 10,000 Arts Council award for an individual to commission a new work will go to either Sir Peter Wright, of the Birmingham Royal Ballet; Nancy Meckler, of Shared Experience; Oliver Knussen, of the Aldeburgh Festival; Sir Edward Downes, of the Royal Opera House; or Jenni Lomax, of Camden Arts Centre.
