Fedorov: "Drugs Are Counted in the Hundreds of Kilograms"] [Text] Cases of board and customs services in Kaliningrad Oblast uncovering attempts to import drugs from Lithuania have become more and more frequent. Things have reached the point that last year the customs house in Chernyakhovsk was rated third in Europe and first in Russia in terms of the amount of confiscated drugs. What is the total amount of confiscated "white death?" As Nikolay Petrovich Iliyn, chief of the Department for Work with Border and Customs Services at the Oblast Administration Division for Public Security and Territorial Protection, has specified, the figure came to 387 kg in 1993.
