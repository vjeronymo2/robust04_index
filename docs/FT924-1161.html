921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Acquisition puts New London in Dollars 764,000 loss New London, the UK-quoted oilfield services company, blamed its recent acquisition, International Drilling Fluids, for its fall into pre-tax losses of Dollars 764,000 (Pounds 503,000) in the six months to September 30, compared with profits of Dollars 2.27m. The company also announced the sale of its US oil and gas properties to a Texas corporation. The net proceeds will be Dollars 7m against a book value of Dollars 10m. An extraordinary provision of Dollars 3.5m has been taken against the sale, leaving the loss for the year of Dollars 4.9m (profit Dollars 1.14m). Turnover for the six months was Dollars 69.7m (Dollars 38.7m), most of the increase being due to IDF. Losses per share came out at 1.4 cents (earnings 1.1 cents).
