Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] On the threshold of the 15th anniversary of the victory of the Islamic revolution, Ayatollah Ahmad Jannati, the secretary of the Coordination Council of the Islamic Publicity Organization [IPO] was interviewed by reporters this morning. He explained how the 10 Days of Dawn ceremonies will be celebrated. Ayatollah Jannati said: Among the aims of the ceremonies are to keep alive the memories of the historic days of the victory of the Islamic revolution, encourage happiness and liveliness among the people, particularly the young generation, explain and display the achievements of the revolution, praise the status of Velayati and the leadership, the martyrs, war-disabled, display the people's presence on various scenes of the revolution, and stress the need for the continuation of the path of the imam [Khomeyni], may Almighty God be satisfied with him, and the revolution principles, as well as the struggle against world arrogance. He said that paying attention to prayers and the struggle against the cultural onslaught are among the outstanding characteristics of this year's ceremonies. [passage omitted] In the news conference, the secretary of the IPO Coordination Council referred to some of the special ceremonies planned for this year's 10 Days of Dawn, including the ceremony called: To Welcome Light, Marking the Return of His Eminence the Imam, may Almighty God be satisfied with him, on 12 Bahman of 1357 [1 February 1979]. At that time bells will toll at 0930 in mosques, schools, offices, and churches. [passage omitted]
