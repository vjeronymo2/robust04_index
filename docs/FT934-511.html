931224 FT 24 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Abbey Panels cuts deficit to Pounds 1.38m Abbey Panels Investments, the Coventry-based engineer, reported reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.38m for the year to September 30, against Pounds 1.82m. Turnover slipped from Pounds 12.4m to Pounds 12.3m. After a reduced tax credit of Pounds 107,000 (Pounds 510,000) losses per share came out at 63.53p (65.54p).
