May 28, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ARSENIO HALL

A hearty rooof, rooof, rooof to Arsenio Hall for his efforts to entertain audiences while maintaining his own cultural uniqueness and style. I suggest that the so-called mainstream WASP America give African-Americans and other people of color the dignity of their own cultural expression. It would go a long way in relieving you of the burden of your own cultural elitism and at the same time expose you to a diversity of ethnic expression that can only be a learning experience for the better.

RONALD BROWN

Hawthorne
