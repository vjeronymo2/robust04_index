920716 FT 16 JUL 92 / Governor Bill Clinton in strong lead GOVERNOR Bill Clinton surged to a strong lead over presidential rivals in polls yesterday. An ABC-Washington Post survey of 1,001 voters gave him 45 per cent of the vote, up from 30 points on July 8. President George Bush's rating dropped from 35 to 28 per cent and support for Mr Ross Perot, the prospective independent candidate, fell from 30 to 20 per cent. A poll published last night by the New York Daily News, with a 500-strong sample, gave Mr Clinton 40 per cent, Mr Bush 31 per cent and Mr Perot 20 per cent.
