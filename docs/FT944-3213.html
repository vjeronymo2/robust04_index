941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Templeton Emerging Net asset value per share of Templeton Emerging Markets Investment Trust stood at 370.8p fully diluted at the October 31 interim stage, against 346.2p six months earlier and 343.57p at October 29 last year. Investment income and interest for the period amounted to Pounds 8.15m, compared with Pounds 5.79m at the interim stage last time.
