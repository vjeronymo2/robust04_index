BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Taras Lariokhin] [Text] The Hague June 22 TASS -- A Russian parliamentary delegation led by Deputy Chairman of the Russian State Duma Alevtina Fedulova arrived here on Wednesday to take part in a two-day conference of European parliamentary speakers to begin on Friday. Preliminary consultation within the forum's framework will begin on Thursday [23 June] evening. Parliamentary speakers of the bulk of East and West European states will take part in the conference to be held behind closed doors. The agenda will include issues of the work of supreme legislatures, parliamntary control, the role of mass media in the democratic society and others.
