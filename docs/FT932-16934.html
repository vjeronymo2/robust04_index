930402 FT 02 APR 93 / BT subsidiary signs BP contract SYNCORDIA, the US-based BT subsidiary formed to manage communications services for international companies, has strengthened its relationship with British Petroleum through a multi-million pound contract to handle a significant part of BP's communications infrastructure. It will offer voice, data video and messaging services to BP Exploration and to BP's corporate operations. The value of the contract is thought to be more than Pounds 10m. Syncordia has four other customers - the computer company IBM; Amadeus, the airline reservation company; BP Chemicals; and Firmenich, the Swiss maker of fragrances and flavourings.
