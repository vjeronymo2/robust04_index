November 2, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: CENTURY CITY;

SEMINAR FOR CARE-GIVERS

A free seminar will be held Sunday at Century City Hospital to help care-givers of persons with dementia or Alzheimer's disease cope with the special strains of the upcoming holidays.

The program, called "Home for the Holidays," will feature a panel of experts who will talk about such topics as family relationships, expectations, home safety and communication. Handouts will be available on choosing appropriate gifts, travel and other topics of concern.

The half-day program is sponsored by the Los Angeles chapter of the Alzheimer's Assn. and the Westside Consortium of Senior Services. It will be held at the hospital at 2070 Century Park East from 9:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. Continental breakfast will be served and parking validated.

Space is limited to 75 people.

For more information or to make reservations, call 201-6788.
