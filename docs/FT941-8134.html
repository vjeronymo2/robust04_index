940222 FT 22 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Rights and Issues Net asset value per capital share at Rights and Issues Investment Trust advanced from 392.5p to 545.7p over the 12 months to December 31. Net revenue rose to Pounds 410,499 (Pounds 339,674) for earnings per income share of 10.177p (7.737p). A final dividend of 6.3p makes an increased total of 8.7p (8.3p).
