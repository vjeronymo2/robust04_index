941119 FT 19 NOV 94 / Gonzalez's allies accused Political scandals in Spain spread yesterday from the ruling Socialist party in Madrid to its Catalan nationalist allies in Barcelona where Mr Josep Maria Cullell resigned as a member of Catalonia's government, the Generalitat, after allegations of corruption. The development, which brings to the forefront the simmering rivalry in Catalonia between the governing nationalists, Convergencia i Unio and the local Socialist party, could strain the alliance between prime minister Felipe Gonzalez and CiU leader Mr Jordi Pujol, the president of the Generalitat. Mr Cullell stepped down as the Generalitat's minister of public works amid newspaper reports that he had pressured a town council into re-zoning and buying land owned by his brother-in-law.
