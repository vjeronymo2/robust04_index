940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / International Company News: Provigo ahead Provigo, Canada's second biggest food distributor, lifted first-quarter net profits to CDollars 10.3m (USDollars 7.4m), or 10 cents a share, from CDollars 3.5m (two cents) a year earlier, writes Robert Gibbens. Its sales were CDollars 1.4bn, against CDollars 1.42bn. Provigo, formerly known as Univa, has sold the last of its non-food assets. The company is 37 per cent held by the Quebec Pension Fund manager, the Caisse de Depot.
