BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Maynayev] [Text] Bishkek May 12 TASS -- The largest military one-day exercise involving Kirgizian [Kyrgyz] Armed Forces, national guards, interior, security, and civil defense forces, began on Thursday in the south of the country. The aim is to train how to close the border with Tajikistan which has become a transit place for illegal drugs from Afghanistan, according to the press service of Kirgizian President Askar Akayev who is inspecting the exercise.
