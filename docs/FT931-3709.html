930315 FT 15 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Midland Ind Newspapers buys 8 titles Midland Independent Newspapers is to buy 8 titles in the Thomson Regional Newspapers north division. The titles, which have a distribution of almost 500,000, would double the size of MIN's free weekly operation. The free weeklies are all based in the East Midlands and form part of Thomson Free Newspapers' Herald and Post series. MIN, formed after a management buy-out of the Birmingham Post and Mail and Coventry Evening Telegraph in November 1991, announced a fourfold increase in trading profits to Pounds 7.3m for the first half of 1992.
