November 13, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

ON THE SIDELINES;

MANSDORF, DAVIS LOSE IN OPEN

Amos Mansdorf of Israel and Scott Davis of Largo, Fla., lost their first-round matches today in the South African Open tennis tournament.

David Pate of Las Vegas beat fifth-seeded Mansdorf 6-3, 6-3, and Kelly Jones of San Diego beat Davis 4-6, 6-1, 6-4.

In other first-round matches, No. 3 Christo van Rensburg beat fellow South African John de Jager 6-1, 6-3, and South African Michael Robertson beat Leif Shiras of Milwaukee 6-0, 4-6, 6-4.
