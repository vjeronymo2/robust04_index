BFN [Text] Al-Khadim al-Wajih, staff member of the House of Representatives Speakership, returned to Sanaa today after leading a House delegation to the United States and the Republic of France. During the visit, the delegation met with several U.S. official in Washington to explain what is taking place in Yemen and that the current confrontation is between the Yemeni people who support unity, legitimacy, and democracy on one hand and the rebels who are encroaching on law, the constitution, and legitimacy on the other. [words indistinct] In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', brother al-Khadim al-Wajih said that the Yemeni parliamentary delegation sensed an understanding by the U.S. and French sides of the House of Representative's position, as well as issues included in the messages that House of Representatives Speaker 'Abdallah Husayn al- Ahmar addressed to the U.S. Congress and the French National Assembly regarding current events in Yemen. The parliamentary delegation also met with the Yemeni community in the cities of New York, Detroit, and Buffalo and explained to them what is taking place in our country, as well as the whole Yemeni people's adherence to unity, constitutional legitimacy, and democracy. The delegation sensed during these meetings the Yemeni community's support for our country's unity. The parliamentary delegation also met with a number of ambassadors of fraternal and friendly countries to the United States, explained to them developments in the Republic of Yemen, and asked them to urge their countries to support Yemeni unity, which is a factor of security and stability for the whole region.
