Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to information received from the regions of Kalesija, Celic, Teocak, Mostar, and Sarajevo, 10 people have been killed and 52 wounded in the last 24 hours. The situation concerning food, medicine, medical kits, and winter clothing and shoes is the worst in Tesanj, Maglaj, Kladanj, Zavidovici, Olovo, Konjic, Mostar, and Gorazde. The hygiene and epidemiological situation in the republic was unfavorable in January -- there were 1,715 cases of infectious diseases. The infectious diseases that have been spread the most are enterocolitis, infectious hepatitis, and prurigo.
