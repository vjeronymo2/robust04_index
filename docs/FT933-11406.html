930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / International Company News: Ecuatoriana de Aviacion Ecuatoriana de Aviacion, Ecudor's state airline, is to be the first company in the government's economic reform programme to be privatised, writes Raymond Colitt in Quito. A newly-formed corporation is to replace the debt-laden airline. It is to take over the operation within 60 to 90 days. Some 60 to 70 per cent of its shares are to be sold. International airlines as well as Ecuadorian entrepreneurs have shown interest in buying shares. The airline has routes to New York and Miami.
