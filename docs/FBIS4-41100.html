BFN [Text] The first large Russian industrial enterprises have been entered on the state register of insolvency. The list of enterprises was drawn up by the Russian Committee on Machine-Building. About 40 plants were registered. These crisis-ridden plants are unable to pay taxes, settle up with suppliers of raw materials, or pay wages. These enterprises will now have to change the way they run their business or else acknowledge their insolvency and go into voluntary self-liquidation.
