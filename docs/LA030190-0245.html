March 1, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

'89 HOLIDAY BOWL RICHEST EVER

Penn State and Brigham Young University each received more than $1 million from the Holiday Bowl, the largest payout in the bowl's 12-year history.

The Nittany Lions defeated BYU, 50-39, in the Dec. 29 game at San Diego's Jack Murphy Stadium. Fifteen Holiday Bowl records were set during the contest.

The total payout of $2,018,076 was divided evenly between the schools for checks of $1,009,038.

The bowl's previous highest team payout was in 1988, when Oklahoma State and Wyoming each received $907,613.
