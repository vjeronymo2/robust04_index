Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 13 (KCNA) -- The U.S. imperialists on March 11 staged a war exercise targeted against the North in the sky above Kunwi, Yoju and Sangdong, South Korea, with the mobilisation of formations of Pacific-based assault planes and above 400 fighter-bombers, observation planes and reconnaissance planes belonging to the U.S. Air Force present in South Korea and the puppet Air Force, according to military sources. On the same day, armed bandits of the puppet Army in Yonchon County and Paju County, Kyonggi Province, and Chorwon County and Inje County, South Korean Kangwon Province, fired at random large-calibre machine gun and automatic rifles toward the Demilitarized Zone along the Military Demarcation Line, thus whipping up war hysterics.
