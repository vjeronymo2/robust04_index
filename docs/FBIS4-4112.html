BFN [By reporter Ju Mengjun (0215 1322 6511)] [Text] Bishkek, 25 Apr (XINHUA) -- Premier Li Peng today met with the staff of Chinese Embassy in the Republic of Kyrgyzstan. Li Peng said: "I have held fruitful talks and meetings with Kyrgyz leaders, and the visit has been very successful." He said: China and Kyrgyzstan share a 1000-km common border, and relations between the two countries have always been good. The two countries are currently devoted to economic development, and need a good peripheral environment. Kyrgsyzstan has good natural conditions and rich resources. The two countries are strongly supplementary to each other and possess very great potential to develop friendship and cooperation. Li Peng expressed hope that the embassy staff would work hard and contribute toward further development of Sino-Kyrgyz friendly relations.
