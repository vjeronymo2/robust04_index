941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / US-China arms talks progress Talks between the US and China on arms control had helped to narrow differences, the US said yesterday. Mr John Holum, director of the US Arms Control and Disarmament Agency, said he had invited vice-foreign minister Liu Huaqiu to Washington in the spring for more talks. One of the issues discussed in detail was reaching an early agreement on a comprehensive test ban treaty.
