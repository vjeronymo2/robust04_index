921217 FT 17 DEC 92 / Somali clan gunmen kill 10 Somali clan gunmen killed 10 people and wounded 10 others at a feeding centre for the starving in the southern town of Bardere, an aid worker said yesterday, Reuter reports from Baidoa. Unidentified armed men attacked the feeding centre on Monday after they found no food to plunder from relief stores and became angry. 'Some food warehouses were attacked. When they found nothing they got angry and went on this wanton rampage,' said Mr James Fennell of the international agency Care.
