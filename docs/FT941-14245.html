940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / Engineering wage rises still low The average level of wage rises in the engineering industry was the lowest for 10 years at 2.2 per cent in the final quarter of last year, the Engineering Employers' Federation said yesterday. The median rise over the same period was 2.5 per cent. There were 159 settlements covering 15,330 employees reported to the federation between October and December.
