Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The relative calm which descended on the area of the conflict in Karabakh in the past few days has prevailed over the past 24 hours, Interfax was told by the press service of Nagorno- Karabakh's defense army. Routine exchanges of fire, which had no impact on the line-up of the warring sides, took place only at a few sectors, largely in the north and northeast of Karabakh.
