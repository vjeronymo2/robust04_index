December 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TREES, NOT HOUSES

Somebody is trying to cut down (200-plus) oak trees! Help Fryman Canyon. I'm 7 and 3/4. We need places to hike and see animals and plants. No more houses!

(Los Angeles) Councilman Michael Woo, help us keep Fryman Canyon for nature.

SARAH BETH GETTINGER

Los Angeles
