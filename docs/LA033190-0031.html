March 31, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MESSING WITH THE RULES IS STRICTLY BUSH LEAGUE

What credibility is there in major league baseball? First, we had the nonsensical delay of games.

Then they can't figure out how to play 162 games. They could play old-fashioned doubleheaders, but of course, that would be giving the fans two games for the price of one. Don't want to do that too often, do we?

AL BENSON

El Cajon
