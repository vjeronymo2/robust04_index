920506 FT 06 MAY 92 / China and Russia in trade pact Russia plans to trade machinery, aircraft and factory equipment for Chinese food and consumer goods under a pact on economic ties signed in Beijing yesterday, Reuter reports from Moscow. The protocol, according to the Itar-Tass news agency, specified the volume of goods that the two will supply to each other through direct trade, local deals over the border, barter and exchange agreements and other means.
