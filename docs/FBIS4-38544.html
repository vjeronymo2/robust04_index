BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yevgeniy Sobetskiy] [Excerpt] Kysyl, 31 May -- The Tuva Republic has joined the Treaty of Social Accord. The procedure of signing this important document by representatives of local government of all levels, heads of the People's Hural and parliamentary factions, political parties, public movements, and religious organizations has taken place here on 31 May. Among the signatories is the republican communist organization, which joined the treaty even though Moscow leaders of the Russian Communist Party expressed negative attitude towards the treaty.
