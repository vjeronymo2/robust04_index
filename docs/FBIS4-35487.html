BFN [Text] During the joint cabinet-Majles session yesterday afternoon, the need for cooperation between the executive and legislative branches was emphasized. The Central News Unit reports that during this joint session--which was attended by President Hashemi-Rafsanjani, Majles Speaker Nateq-Nuri, cabinet members, Majles deputies, and the vice presidents--economic problems, the government's economic policies, and the second plan for the country's economic, social, and cultural development were discussed. At the beginning of the session, Nateq-Nuri said that such sessions speed up the country's development and thanked the cabinet members for their presence in the Majles. Then 10 Majles deputies stated their views on the country's economic, cultural, and social problems. President Hashemi-Rafsanjani then took the podium and spoke about the role and position of the Majles in advancing the affairs of society. He said: With coordination among the Majles, the government, and the judiciary any problem can be tackled and the system's plans and policies will succeed. Referring to the government's plans and programs to fight high prices and profiteering, the president stated: To be successful in this, cooperation with the Majles is needed. He added: We are now in the phase of controlling the market. The government is fully monitoring all affairs and applies the necessary policies in each phase. President Hashemi-Rafasanjani expressed hopes that the new policies on controlling the foreign exchange rate will continue with the support of the Majles until the interests of all the people are ensured. Referring to the Second Five-Year Plan and to the specialized study of the plan by the Majles deputies, President Hashemi-Rafsanjani announced the government's readiness to implement the priorities listed in the plan.
