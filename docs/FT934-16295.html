931007 FT 07 OCT 93 / Police warn blood sport enthusiasts after bomb POLICE yesterday warned blood sports enthusiasts to be on their guard after a package incendiary device apparently posted by animal rights activists damaged a Post Office sorting machine. A further three devices were found at the sorting office in Watford, Herts, addressed to people in various parts of the country connected with 'country-type pursuits', Herts police said.
