931027 FT 27 OCT 93 / International Company News: Computer groups link NATIONAL Semiconductor of the US has entered a technical and marketing partnership with Novell, the US software group, Reuter reports. National, a leading computer chip maker, said that it was going into the end-user networking market with a set of products which had just been launched. Earlier, Microsoft and Cisco Systems of the US announced a similar alliance to expand networking options for end-users. Mr Ray Noorda, Novell's chief executive, said that Novell and National would provide customers with network solutions that set new standards for technical innovation, compatibility, ease of use, and price.
