BFN [Unattributed report under the "NEGA Reports" rubric: "Crimea"] [Text] Two more deputy factions have been set up at the Crimea Supreme Soviet session. These are the "Reform" faction made up of 12 deputies under Vladimir Shevyev, cochairman of the Crimean Economic Revival Party, and the 10-seat Agrarian faction under Yuriy Velyukh, a well-known Crimean entrepreneur operating in the agro-industrial sector. It is expected that yet another faction -- "Republic" -- will be set up that will fully support the Crimean Tatar Assembly faction. According to observers, it may include some deputies from the "Russia" faction. The session has also elected chairmen of nine of the Crimea Supreme Soviet's 12 standing commissions and formed the parliament's Presidium made up of the Supreme Soviet chairman, his two deputies, and nine chairmen of Supreme Soviet standing commissions.
