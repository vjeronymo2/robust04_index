931223 FT 23 DEC 93 / UK Company News: TR High Income set to maintain dividend TR High Income Trust has declared a fourth interim dividend of 1.5p for the year to December 31 1993, making 4.5p to date. The company said it intended to maintain the full payment for the year at 6p. The board added that from 1994 there would be four quarterly payments in July, October, January and April, in place of the present five.
