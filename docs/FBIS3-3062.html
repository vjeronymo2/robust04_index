Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The Second Session of the Eighth Jiangxi Provincial People's Congress concluded successfully this afternoon after fulfilling various items on its agenda. With a feeling of great joy, people's deputies walked into Nanchang's 1 August Auditorium at 1430 to attend the closing ceremony of the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress to exercise again the sacred right conferred on them by the people of the whole province. The executive chairmen of the presidium -- Mao Zhiyong, Zhu Zhohong, Lu Xiuzhen, Wang Zhaorong, Chen Guizun, Wang Guoben, Wang Zhongfa, and Hu Dongtai -- were executive chairmen of the session, and were seated on the front row of the rostrum. Those who were seated on the front row also included Wu Guanzheng, Shu Shengyou, Huang Zhiquan, Zhong Qihuang, Feng Jinmao, Zhang Liangyu, Zhang Yunchuan, Huang Maoheng, Ye Xueling, (Yan Yulong), Wu Yongle, Huang Liqi, Luo Ming, Jiang Guozhen, (Li Zhicheng), (Pan Ruiji), (Li Qiuyan), (Xie Guishan), Zhang Chuanshi, (Pan Jiluan), (Li Fangyuan), (Zhao Yuxing), (Peng Shengxi), Wang Zemin, (Tian Jiaming), Pei Dean, Huang Xiandu, and other members of the presidium. [passage omitted] The session maintained: In 1993, the whole province further emancipated its minds, sought truth from facts, seized the fine opportunity, and accelerated development. Marked achievements were made in our province's economic construction, reform, opening up, and various undertakings in society. In the course of organizing economic work of the whole province, our provincial government gained a clear understanding of the situation very early, took prompt action, and grasped its work expeditiously. Governments at all levels did much effective work. The session pointed out: The guiding thought, tasks, targets, policies, and measures defined by our government work report for our national economic and social development in 1994 conform with reality, and can be achieved through our hard work. The session stressed: This year is a crucial one for the establishment of the socialist market economy system. It is also an important year in which we will concentrate our efforts on speeding up infrastructure projects and pushing Jiangxi's economy to a new height. We must correctly handle the relationship among reform, development and stability; seize the favorable opportunity; deepen reform; expand opening up; promote development; and safeguard stability. We must accelerate the pace of establishing our socialist market economy system, and must strengthen and improve macroscopic regulation and control so as to maintain the sustained, rapid and healthy development of our national economy. We must promote the building of our socialist spiritual civilization, and the all-round progress of the society. The session called on people of the whole province to arm their minds with Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics. Under the leadership of the CPC Central Committee, with Comrade Jiang Zemin as its core, they must unite as one, be of one heart and one mind, explore their way forward, and make still greater contributions to invigorating Jiangxi. Participants in the session unanimously voted for six resolutions by a show of hands. [passage omitted]
