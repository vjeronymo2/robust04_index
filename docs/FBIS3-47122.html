Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Jan 10 -- The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Vietnam has sent a message of condolences to its Lao counterpart on the demise of Phoumi Vongvichit, advisor to the Central Committee of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party (LPRP), former Politburo member of the LPRP Central Committee, and former acting president of Laos, former deputy prime minister and minister of education and former president of the Lao Front for National Construction. "Comrade Phoumi Vongvichit is an outstanding leader of the Lao party and state, and a staunch fighter who strived much for national independence and the people's happiness. He was a close friend of the Vietnamese people and made many precious contributions to the consolidation and development of the special friendship and solidarity between the two countries", the message says.
