930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / Citizen's Charter payouts top Pounds 2m MORE than Pounds 2m has been paid in compensation under the prime minister's Citizen's charter initiative, figures show. The Benefits Agency, which handles social security benefits including income support and state pensions, has paid out the most - more than Pounds 1m in just five months. Next was British Rail, which handed out Pounds 1m in the first year.
