BFN [Text] An ECOMOG [Economic Community of West African States Cease-Fire Monitoring Group] force carrying reinforcements to quell the factional fighting in ULIMO [United Liberation Movement for Democracy in Liberia] has been ambushed in Tubmanburg, Bomi County. The force, which was carrying troops to the troubled headquarters of ULIMO to stop the fighting between Alhaji Koromah's Mandingo and rival Roosevelt Johnson's Krahn supporters, was reportedly hit by rockets on its way to Tubmanburg. ECOMOG has also suffered casualty in the Tubmanburg incident.
