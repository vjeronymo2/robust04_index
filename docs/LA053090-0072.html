May 30, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

First in the U.S.S.R.: The world premiere of "False Identity," a thriller by RKO/Pavilion Pictures starring Stacy Keach, will take place today in Moscow, and producers are saying the film is the first American movie ever to premiere in the Soviet Union.

The Great White Hall of the Soviet Filmmakers' Club is the setting for the premiere. Executive producer Ted Hartley is expected to attend the premiere, and actor Jon Voight, who is making a film in Moscow, is also expected to appear. "False Identity" will be released in the United States in June. GREG BRAXTON
