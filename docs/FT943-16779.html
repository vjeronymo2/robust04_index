940702 FT 02 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Jermyn Inv More than doubled rental income and improved results from associates enabled Jermyn Investment, the property investor, to report pre-tax profits of Pounds 151,222 for 1993, against losses of Pounds 833,972. Rental income was Pounds 742,598 (Pounds 334,477). Over the year net assets increased to Pounds 5.22m (Pounds 4.18m). Earnings per share came out at 5.32p (45.24p losses).
