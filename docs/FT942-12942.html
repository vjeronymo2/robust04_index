940427 FT 27 APR 94 / UK Company News: I&S Enterprise lifts net asset value 17% Ivory & Sime Enterprise Capital, an investment trust aiming at capital growth through venture capital situations, reported a fully diluted net asset value of 118.85p as at March 31. The figure represented an increase of 17 per cent over the six months to end-March. Net losses for the period from July 12 - the date of incorporation following the restructuring of Independent Investment - to March 31 were Pounds 228,000.
