930420 FT 20 APR 93 / International Company News: Henkel sales fall 2% to DM2.6bn in first term SALES at Henkel, the German chemicals, cosmetics and household goods cleaning products group, fell by 2 per cent in the first quarter of 1993 to DM2.6bn (Dollars 1.6bn). All operations showed a 'small drop in sales' except for household cleaning products and cosmetics, which accounted for 43 per cent of the total, Mr Hans-Dietrich Winkhaus, chief executive, said. The company said it would pay an unchanged dividend of DM7 on common stock and DM10 on preferred stock at its stockholders' meeting on May 17. Mr Winkhaus said that he did not expect sales this year to fall in spite of difficult trading conditions.
