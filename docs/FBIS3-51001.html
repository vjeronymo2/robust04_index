Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] During a special session, the Colorado Party Executive Board on 10 January confirmed Eugenio Sanabria Cantero as Colorado Party president, abiding by party statutes. The ANR [National Republican Association -- Colorado Party] therefore reshuffled its Executive Board after [former party President] Blas N. Riquelme's resignation. In descending order, the post of first vice president went to Juan Manuel Cano Melgarejo, the post of second vice president went to Enrique Gonzalez Quintana, and the post of third vice president went to Juan Carlos Galaverna, who was supported by the majority of the 64 members present. In addition, the post of political secretary went to Cristina Munoz, who is the first woman to occupy this post. The session was held in a calm atmosphere and most secretaries of state participated.
