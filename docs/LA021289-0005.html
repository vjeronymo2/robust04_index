February 12, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

After watching the newly extended "Home" program, I've decided to express my opinion. I am disappointed as to how it has changed. Those things that made the show a success just aren't there anymore, such as the information on improving one's home or creative ideas for one's hobbies. Seems as if "Home" has become just another talk show. Also, Sandy Hill was a wonderful hostess and I think Cristina Ferrare should have stayed on "AM."

Elizabeth Sanchez, Norwalk
