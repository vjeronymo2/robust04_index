BFN [Text] Police are investigating the possibility of a spying case against the Republic. The suspects are three Albanians who crossed into the free areas from the occupied areas and were arrested this morning near a military camp in Nicosia. The three Albanians crossed into the free areas from the buffer zone in Nicosia but did not tell the interrogators their exact point of entry. They testified that they flew to the occupied areas from Turkey. The three Albanians were arrested at 0800 this morning in the vicinity of the Hilton Hotel. A military camp is in this area. A taxi driver gave police information on the movements of the three Albanians. The Albanians had asked him to take them to Agros; as soon as they arrived there, they asked him to bring them back to Nicosia. The three Albanians did not have any identity cards or passports and gave contradictory testimony to the police, who are investigating a case of illegal entry to the Republic. The suspects will appear in court tomorrow morning so that a warrant for their detention can be issued.
