920415 FT 15 APR 92 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton and Jute COTTON Liverpool-Spot and shipment sales in Liverpool for the week ending 10 April amounted to 211 tonnes against 186 tonnes in the previous week. Quiet trading persisted with interest being shown in American, Russian and West African growths.
