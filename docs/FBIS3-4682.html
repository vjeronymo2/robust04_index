Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] Both Japan and the United States have been discussing the resumption of vice ministerial negotiations on the automobile and auto parts sector to be held under the Japan-U.S. framework talks. A senior official in the Ministry of International Trade and Industry, however, made clear on 28 February that it has become difficult to hold the vice ministerial negotiations. Japan has asked the United States to hold the vice ministerial negotiations when U.S. Commerce Undersecretary Jeffrey Garten visits Japan on his tour of Asia, but it has replied to Japan that "he would have no time to hold the negotiations."
