BFN [Text] In Chengdu yesterday [19 April], the people's armed forces committee under the provincial party committee held its 14th meeting to relay and study the instructions of the Central Military Commission on the work of the people's militia reserve service, and to study problems arising in our provincial people's militia reserve service that should be solved immediately. The meeting also conscientiously discussed the issue of further carrying forward the fine tradition of the party administering the armed forces under the new situation of further developing the market economy, so that the work of the people's militia reserve service in our province can scale new heights. Governor Xiao Yang, chairman of the people's armed forces committee under the provincial party committee, attended the meeting and spoke on further strengthening our province's national defense reserve forces.
