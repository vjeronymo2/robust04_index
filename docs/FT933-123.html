930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Business Technology Business Technology Group, which sells and services office equipment, announced a pre-tax loss of Pounds 233,000 for the half year to June 30, against a deficit of Pounds 3.72m last time. Turnover amounted to Pounds 8.76m (Pounds 11.1m). Losses per share came out at 1.15p (20p).
