940319 FT 19 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Exceptional boosts Perry to Pounds 5.16m Exceptional profits on a property sale of Pounds 1.85m, against restructuring costs of Pounds 3.28m, enabled Perry Group, the motor dealer and repairer, to report 1993 profits of Pounds 5.16m pre-tax compared with losses of Pounds 3.22m. Mr Richard Allan, chairman, said the year saw a recovery in profits on the sale of new and used cars resulting in their share of group profits rising from 13 per cent to 27 per cent. He added that the first two months of the present year had shown a significant improvement on the comparable period. 'Despite the continuing uncertainty over the strength of the economic recovery I believe that 1994 will prove a year of good progress.' Turnover improved to Pounds 311.3m (Pounds 289.3m). Interest charges fell to Pounds 1.88m (Pounds 3.04m). Year-end cash balances were Pounds 2m. The company raised a net Pounds 9.6m by a rights issue in September. Earnings per share were 18.2p (losses 14.7p). A final dividend of 4.25p is recommended for a total of 7p (6p).
