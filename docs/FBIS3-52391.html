Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Senate resumed discussion today on president's address to the joint session of the parliament. Dr. Basharat Elahi and Syed Fasi Iqbal appreciated the policies of the government toward Kashmir. They complimented the government for bringing the issue into limelight. Syed Fasi Iqbal, who is the chairman of standing committee on information, on a point of order congratulated the government for strongly presenting Pakistan's point of view during the recent Pakistan-India talks. Dr. Basharat Elahi taking part in the debate pinpointed lack of consensus between the government and the opposition on some national issues. He called for more attention for the promotion of education, science and technology, defense, and population planning. He stressed that Pakistan should not sign the Nuclear Nonproliferation Treaty. Mr. Rashid Rabbani appreciated the initiatives of the government to restore the economic conditions of the country leading to prosperity of the masses. He said that the government has taken steps to arrest price escalation and solve the problem of unemployment.
