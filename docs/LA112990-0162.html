November 29, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: RETAIL / TOURISM

Santa Claws is coming: Does Fluffy want a new rawhide chew bone for Christmas?

She can bark her wishes to Santa this weekend at Laguna Hills Mall.

A group of veterinarians will dress up as Santa Claus -- or, as they say, Santa Claws -- and pose for photographs with shoppers and their pets at the mall. Proceeds from the photo sales will benefit the San Clemente Animal Shelter.

The "Santa Claws and Paws" event will be from 8-10 a.m. and 8-10 p.m., Friday, 8-10 a.m. and 7-9 p.m. Saturday and 8-10 a.m. and 6-8 p.m. Sunday.
