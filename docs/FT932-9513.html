930513 FT 13 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Tomkinsons 12% ahead at Pounds 430,000 Tomkinsons, the yarn and carpet group, returned profits of Pounds 430,000 pre-tax for the six months to April 3, an improvement of 12 per cent over last time's Pounds 384,000. Turnover improved from Pounds 9.92m to Pounds 10.4m with exports ahead by 17 per cent. Interest income of Pounds 10,000 compared with a previous Pounds 14,000 charge. Earnings edged ahead to 4.7p (4.2p) and the interim dividend is a same-again 3.5p.
