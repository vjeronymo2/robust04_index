Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan 31, IRNA -- Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati heading a high-ranking delegation left here for Sofia this morning at the invitation of his Bulgarian counterpart, Stanislav Dakalov. The Iranian foreign minister will meet with Bulgarian President, Zhelyu Zhelev, foreign minister and a number of high-ranking officials. Issues of mutual interest as well as important European problems such as recent Balkan developments will be among topics to be discussed in Velayati's meetings with Bulgarian officials. During the two-day formal visit a commercial agreement as well as other documents in different fields are expected to be signed between the two countries.
