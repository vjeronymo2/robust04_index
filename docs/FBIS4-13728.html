BFN [Text] A full regiment, the Rashtriya Rifles, of the Indian Armed Forces, which was previously deployed in the Indian Punjab, has now been moved to Srinagar so that it can participate in crushing the freedom struggle in occupied Kashmir. Meanwhile, nine more people, including four freedom fighters, one civilian and two Indian soldiers, have been killed in fresh clashes at Baramula and other places. Two persons were killed while under police custody in Gandarbal. Many people have been injured as a result of indiscriminate firing by the Indian forces at Batapura in Badgam. The freedom fighters attacked the Army patrols in Kundi, Karibamalu, and the Kariba Zohra area of Shupiyan, injuring several soldiers.
