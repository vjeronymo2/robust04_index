Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 24 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il was presented with a gift by Li Shuzheng, head of the International Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, staying in Korea. The gift was handed to Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, by the head of the International Liaison Department.
