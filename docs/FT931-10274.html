930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Net asset setback at Eng & Caledonian English & Caledonian Investment, which invests mainly in unlisted UK securities, saw net asset value dip to 162p by December 31. The figure compared with a value of 181.8p a year earlier. Net revenue for the six months to end-December amounted to Pounds 116,000, down from Pounds 148,000 last time. The interim dividend is maintained at 1.25p, payable from earnings of 2.59p (3.3p) per Pounds 1 share.
