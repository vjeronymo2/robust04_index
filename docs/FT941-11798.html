940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Five Oaks Pounds 15m debenture issue Five Oaks, the property investment and development group, is issuing Pounds 15m of debenture stock to refinance its short-term borrowings. The issue of First Mortgage Debenture 2019 is underwritten by NatWest Capital Markets which is also acting as the sole placing agent. Dealings are expected to begin on February 9. The group also announced yesterday the purchase of a 26,300 sq ft office building from Sydney & London Properties for Pounds 3.45m, reflecting a net initial yield of 8.9 per cent.
