Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 1 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Chong-il, Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, on February 28 met Ho Chong-man, chief vice-chairman of the Central Standing Committee of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon) staying in the socialist homeland, at his request. Comrade Kim Chong-il had a conversation with him in an amicable atmosphere. He arranged a dinner for him.
