DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION Federal Aviation Administration 14 CFR Part 39 [Docket No. 94&hyph;NM&hyph;83&hyph;AD] Airworthiness Directives; Aerospatiale Model ATR72&hyph;101, &hyph;102, &hyph;201, and &hyph;202 Series Airplanes AGENCY: Federal Aviation Administration, DOT. ACTION: Notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM).

SUMMARY: This document proposes the adoption of a new airworthiness directive (AD) that is applicable to certain Aerospatiale Model ATR72 series airplanes. This proposal would require an inspection to determine the model and orientation of certain flight control rods, and replacement with modified rods, if necessary. This proposal is prompted by reports of corrosion found on the pitch and rudder trim and rudder travel limiter fail-safe rods. The actions specified by the proposed AD are intended to prevent problems associated with corrosion of the flight control rods, which could compromise the required strength of these items.
