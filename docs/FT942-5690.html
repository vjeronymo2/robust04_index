940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / Scott rejects Labour plea Lord Justice Scott yesterday rejected a call from Labour to extend the terms of reference of his arms-for-Iraq inquiry. In a letter to Mr Michael Meacher, Labour's public service spokesman, the judge said it was in the public interest for his final report to be published as soon as possible. Earlier this week Mr Meacher urged the judge to extend his inquiry to bankers and businessmen who have been involved in Britain's arms trade with the Middle East.
