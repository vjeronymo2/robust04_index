930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Sidlaw Sidlaw Group, the oil services, packaging and textiles concern, is paying Pounds 3.7m to acquire Deeside Packaging (Stonehaven) and Shield Packaging. Deeside and Shield are established manufacturers of flexible packaging for a range of consumer product markets, most notably bread, frozen and processed foods and soft tissue products. Consideration for Deeside, a privately-owned company based near Aberdeen, amounts to Pounds 1.7m, of which Pounds 610,000 has been paid in cash and the rest satisfied by the issue of variable rate unsecured loan stock 1994-2000. The Pounds 2m price for Shield, located in Washington, near Newcastle, has been paid in cash.
