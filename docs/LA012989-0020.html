January 29, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES

Speaking of directors, screenwriter John Patrick Shanley ("Moonstruck," "January Man") is about to make his movie directing debut in May, with Steven Spielberg producing, Tom Hanks starring and Warner Bros. releasing. It's "Joe vs. the Volcano," which the author describes as "a modern-day Faust saga with a fair amount of pyrotechnic effects." Leonard Klady
