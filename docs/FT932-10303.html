930510 FT 10 MAY 93 / Taking on the world A worker at Silver Knight Exhibitions, the Coventry-based display company, puts the final touches to one of the models designed for the UK pavilion at Expo '93. The first exhibits have just been sent to Taejon, Korea, where the trade fair is due to open on August 7. Other exhibits include a Williams car driven by Nigel Mansell in last year's world motor-racing championship and the Olympic-winning Lotus bicycle.
