August 29, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NATION IN BRIEF;

FLORIDA;

HURRICANE SWINGS INTO OPEN ATLANTIC

Hurricane Gustav marched northward into the open Atlantic with its 105-m.p.h. winds after curving away from the northeastern Caribbean Islands, the National Hurricane Center in Miami said.

Tropical Storm Hortense trailed about 1,100 miles to the east, a danger only to shipping. All hurricane and tropical storm warnings for the Caribbean islands were dropped. But forecasters said the northeastern islands would still feel rough seas and swells.
