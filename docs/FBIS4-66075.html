BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpts] President Suleyman Demirel has stated that he referred back to the Assembly the bill on housing for civil servants because it would exacerbate already difficult conditions in Turkey. Demirel held a breakfast meeting with reporters in Isparta. [passage omitted] Reacting to a 25 percent cutback of U.S. aid to Turkey, Demirel said claims of human rights violations and the Cyprus issue were given as the reasons for the aid cut. Stressing that there is no question of Turkey violating human rights, Demirel said that the same measures used against human rights violations in the United States are being implemented in Turkey. On the Cyprus impasse, Demirel said that Turkey is not responsible for the stalemate.
