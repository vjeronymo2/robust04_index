940620 FT 20 JUN 94 / Company News (This Week): Wessex forecast to set a water record - Other companies Wessex Water is expected to draw the sector's reporting season to a close tomorrow with the biggest dividend increase of all the top 10 water companies. Forecasts are for a pay-out of between 23.5p and 24p, against last year's 21.5p. The main issues, as with its fellow companies, will be cost savings and the performance of the non-regulated waste management business. Wessex is expected to score on both counts. Profit is estimated to rise by 20 per cent to about Pounds 103m.
