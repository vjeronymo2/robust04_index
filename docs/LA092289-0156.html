September 22, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

NEWSMAKERS

Elizabeth Pearson has been appointed vice president, director of marketing for Shearson Lehman Hutton Mortgage Corp.'s consumer, wholesale and institutional products divisions. She previously served as a consultant to the company. In her new position, Pearson will oversee marketing, telemarketing, customer service and product development activities and manage the in-house advertising agency. The company, headquartered in Irvine, is a wholly owned subsidiary of Shearson Lehman Hutton.
