930422 FT 22 APR 93 / Rent-a-cop plan considered SOUTH Wales police are considering hiring police to local businesses which are concerned about rising crime, in the same way as extra police manpower is provided for football matches and large-scale pop concerts. Mr Robert Lawrence, the chief constable, told borough councillors in the Cynon Valley he would like companies experiencing problems to consider 'buying in' police services before going to commercial security groups.
