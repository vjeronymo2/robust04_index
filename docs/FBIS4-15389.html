BFN [Text] Moscow, 8 Apr (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin has signed an edict on the Russian Federation's presidential expert analysis service. The Expert Analysis Council and the Analysis Center attached to the Russian Federation president as well as the Information Technology Center for Analytical Research within the presidential administration are being set up in order to improve provision of analytical and information services for the Russian Federation president. Members of the Expert Analysis Council will be working on voluntary basis. Sergey Filatov will act as its chairman. Yevgeniy Yasin is appointed head of the Analytical Center and Marat Guriyev head of the Information Technology Cente for Analytical Research. The press service of the Russian president reports that the General Policy Analysis Center and the Socioeconomic Policy Analysis Center within the Russian Federation presidential administration have been abolished. The edict establishes provisions for the Expert Analysis Council and the Analysis Center attached to the president of the Russian Federation.
