Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 10 KYODO -- Following are the main points of a draft action program on improving access to Japan's construction market for foreigners obtained Monday [10 January] by KYODO NEWS SERVICE: -- Transparency and competitiveness in bidding for public works projects should be boosted. The principle of nondiscrimination against foreigners should be implemented thoroughly. -- Steps specified in the action program will be carried out starting in fiscal 1994, which begins on April 1. -- Public works projects valued at more than specified sums should be put to open bidding. -- Documents on bidding should be published in English as well as in Japanese. -- New guidelines on bidding for public works will be drafted by around this summer.
