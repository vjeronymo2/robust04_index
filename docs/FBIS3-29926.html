Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 24 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to His Highness Jabir al-Ahmad al-Jabir al-Sabah, amir of the State of Kuwait on the occasion of the 33rd anniversary of its national day. President Kim Il-song in the message wished his highness and the Kuwaiti people success in the work for the sovereignty and prosperity of the country and expressed the conviction that the friendly relations between the two countries would further develop.
