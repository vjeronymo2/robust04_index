Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Dec. 30, IRNA -- Uzbek Foreign Minister Qasemov [name as received] Wednesday [29 December] underlined the importance of Tehran-Tashkent ties and called for further dynamism of the Economic Cooperation Organization (ECO) and cooperation among its member states. Qasemov made the remark in a meeting with the visiting director general for Western Asia at the Iranian Foreign Ministry, Mir Mahmud Musavi. During the meeting, Musavi handed over a message from Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati inviting his Uzbek counterpart to take part in the forthcoming ministerial meeting of ECO, scheduled here next month. Qasemov expressed hope that the ministerial meeting would take effective steps to materialize agreements so far reached in the framework of ECO. The two side also discussed ways to bolster Tehran-Tashkent bilateral relations.
