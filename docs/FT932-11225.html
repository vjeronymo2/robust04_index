930505 FT 05 MAY 93 / UK Company News: AAH acquires 13 retail pharmacies AAH Holdings has acquired 13 retail pharmacies in north-east England from Park Chemists for an initial Pounds 3.88m cash. A further cash sum will be payable when the value of stock is confirmed. AAH said it already had a strong retail pharmacy presence in Durham and Northumberland would will enable the Park outlets to be integrated smoothly. Turnover of the 13 pharmacies for the 12 months to February 28 1992 was Pounds 7.1m, partly serviced by AAH wholesale and partly by a local competitor.
