BFN [Text] In Hanoi on 21 April, General Secretary Do Muoi cordially received the Lao People's Revolutionary Party [LPRP] Central Committee Organization Department delegation led by its chief Thongsing Thammavong currently on a visit to Vietnam. Comrade Thongsing Thammavong said his delegation held working session with the Communist Party of Vietnam Central Committee Organization Department and other agencies concerned to exchange experiences and views on various issues. He said he was pleased to see that in the new situation the two parties, states, and peoples have cooperated consistently and helped each other to fulfill the objectives set by their respective parties. General Secretary Do Muoi warmly welcomed the Lao party delegation and hailed the success scored by the delegation during the visit. He said this has further strengthened the close, fraternal relations between the two parties and peoples. Comrade Do Muoi briefed the Lao delegation on the party building task in the new situation, and expressed the hope that both sides will continue to consistently exchange experiences and expand cooperation, thereby contributing to consolidating and developing the special friendly relations between the two parties and nations.
