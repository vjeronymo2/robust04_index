930726 FT 26 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Grosvenor Dev Cap Net asset value per share of Grosvenor Development Capital stood at 190.3p at June 30 1993 against 147.2p six months previously and 146.7p at May 31 1992. The attributable result was a Pounds 154,000 deficit compared with a profit of Pounds 2,000. Losses per share emerged at 1.91p (0.02p earnings).
