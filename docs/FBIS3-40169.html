Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Summary] In 1993 gross output of cotton in Jiangxi Province reached 150 million kilograms. The 1993 cotton area was 2.5 million mu. The area sown to fine varieties of cotton accounted for 85 percent. In 1993 the per unit area yield of ginned cotton increased from 73.2 kilograms in 1992 to 75.5 kilograms.
