BFN [Text] The foreign ministers of Belgium and Romania have signed a joint political declaration. The two foreign ministers have tackled the prospects of the Partnership for Peace program, bilateral cooperation, and Romania's ties with the European Union. The Belgian minister, Mr. Claes, underlined the necessity for Romania to extend its initiatives for closer cooperation with the European Union. Mr. Claes has decided to cut short his visit in Romania and return to Belgium to examine the situation in Rwanda where Belgian blue helmets have been killed. Romanian Foreign Minister Teodor Melescanu has said that among the Belgian blue helmets deployed under UN aegis in Rwanda there is also a group of Romanian observers who are safe.
