BFN [Text] Commander in Chief Fidel Castro, first secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Cuba and president of the Councils of State and Ministers, sent a message to the Third Manuel Ascunce Domenech Teaching Contingent, urging the contingent to continue defending the Revolution's achievements. The letter was sent on the occasion of a provincial ceremony in Pinar del Rio commemorating the contingent's 20th anniversary. Education Minister Luis Ignacio Gomez was present at the ceremony.
