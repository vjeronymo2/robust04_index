Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Summary] Beginning 31 December, Anhui Province set the following sale prices for grain and edible oil: The retail price for early polished long-grained nonglutinous rice may not exceed 65 yuan per 50 kilograms, standard flour may not exceed 59 yuan per 50 kilograms, and second grade vegetable oil may not exceed 300 yuan per 50 kilograms.
