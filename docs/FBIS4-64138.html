BFN ["BS-PRESS" report under rubric "According to Reports From Correspondents and Information Agencies": "State Commission on Legal Reform Created"] [Text] By a decree of the Chairman of the Georgian Parliament-Head of State [Eduard Shevardnadze], a state commission for legal reform has been created within the Ministry of Justice in order to prepare codes and other important bills. The bills that have been submitted and that belong to a part of those acts that are necessary for legal reform have been prepared by a group of specialists headed by Professor Tengiz Liluashvili.
