Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 4 (XINHUA) -- Qiao Shi, chairman of the Standing Committee of China's National People's Congress [NPC], will pay an official goodwill visit to Germany, Switzerland and Austria from January 16 to 30, at the invitation of the Presidium of the Bundestag of Germany, President Gret Haller of the National Council of the Federal Assembly of Switzerland, and President Heinz Fischer of the National Council of Austria. A Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman made the announcement here today. This will be the first visit ever paid by the chairman of China's NPC Standing Committee to west European countries and will be of great importance to promoting China's relations with Germany, Switzerland and Austria, the spokesman said.
