BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 2 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The newspaper, ARDIYN ERH informs that discussion of the draft law on state service at the State Great Hural (SGH) spring session is being continued. The newspaper informs that at the government regular sitting a particular attention has been paid to the issues of food supply of the population. A report on this issue has been made by the Minister of Food and Agriculture of Mongolia T. Oold. The government has pointed out to necessity of consolidation of some progress in food supply of the population. As the newspaper informs, the government has instructed the minister of external relations to make certain amendments in the agreement on the regime of border communication between Mongolia and China. The newspaper informs that the government has decided to submit for consideration to the State Great Hural an issue about Mongolia's joining the New York convention concerning activity of arbitration organizations. The newspaper comments the speech made by the Deputy Chairman of the Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation R. Abdulatipov in connection with the statement of this political figure about territorial claims of Mongolia to Russia. The Vice Minister of External Relations of Mongolia J. Choinhor has expressed bewilderment of Mongolian side and expressed regret at the fact that high ranking officials permit themselves to make such statements. The newspaper informs that the director of the press service of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Russia G. Karasin has noted in his statement at the press conference that there are no disagreements of territorial issues between Russia and Mongolia. According to information of the officials given in the newspaper, in the country a big attention is being paid to problems of exploitation of oil deposits. As the newspaper notes, measures are being taken of abolition of shortcomings and omissions in activity of the company, which is responsible for exploitation of old deposits. The Mongolian Army newspaper, TUSGAAR TOGTNOL, publishes a report made by the vice minister of defence, chief of the General Staff of the Armed Forces of the country Major General R. Gabaa at the grand meeting devoted to the 73rd anniversary of foundation of the present Armed Forces of Mongolia. In the report a great attention is paid to solution of social problems of military servicemen. Vice doctor of historical science, Colonel B. Badarch writes in the newspaper about tasks of the Armed Forces in renewal of their activity, strengthening of discipline and order.
