Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 27 (XINHUA) -- Visiting President Kim Yong-sam of the Republic of Korea (ROK) and his party arrived here by special plane from Shanghai this afternoon. Wu Jichuan, chairman of the Chinese Government Reception Committee and minister of posts and telecommunications, accompanied President Kim Yong-sam on the journey. The ROK president was welcomed at the airport by Chinese Vice- Foreign Minister Tang Jiaxuan and others.
