940805 FT 05 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Anglo & Overseas net assets at 472.8p Anglo & Overseas Trust had at net asset value of 472.8p per share at June 30, down from 506.6p at the December year end. The value at end-June 1993 was 427.1p. The Morgan Grenfell-managed trust reported net revenue of Pounds 3.79m for the six-month period, down from Pounds 4.32m last time. The interim dividend is maintained at 1.75p, payable from earnings of 3.28p (3.75p) per share.
