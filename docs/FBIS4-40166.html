BFN [Text] [No dateline as received] A joint course of instruction [uchebno-metodicheskiye sbory] involving division commanders from the Group of Russian Troops in the Transcaucasus and brigades of the Georgian Armed Forces begins in the Autonomous Republic of Ajaria on 16 June. The course program includes a regimental tactical exercise [polkovoye takticheskoye ucheniye] with a display of elements of the modern integrated all-arms battle and the sharing of experience in military training and army personnel education. The exercise will be attended by Russian army observers and senior military ranks from the Russian Federation Armed Forces.
