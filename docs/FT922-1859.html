920619 FT 19 JUN 92 / The Lex Column: TV companies Yesterday's agreed offer by Yorkshire for Tyne Tees comes as little surprise. After all, both companies paid through the nose for their TV franchises last autumn, and desperately need cost savings to enhance shareholder returns. The comparison with Granada, with an annual payment obligation to the Exchequer of around Pounds 27m, is instructive. Across the Pennines, with a broadly similar share of ITV advertising revenue, the merged moguls will need to find Pounds 63m.
