BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Yezhov] [Text] Moscow June 1 TASS -- Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin vacationing on the Black Sea will continue working on the second package of economic decrees which is now being drawn up by the government, governmental spokesman Valeriy Grishin said on Wednesday. Grishin said that first vice-prime minister Oleg Soskovets will substitute for the premier for the duration of his two-week vacation which he will spend in the Black Sea resort city of Sochi. The spokesman said that until the last minute it was not clear whether or not Chernomyrdin will take his leave now, mainly because of his active involvement in the work on the package of economic decrees aimed at continuing economic reforms in russia. The first package was recently signed by president Boris Yeltsin. Grishin denied reports that Chernomyrdin would work on his book during his vacation. "There will be simply no time left for that. Besides, he needs rest too," he said.
