Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Sevinj Adbullayeva and Viktor Shulman] [Text] Baku, 28 Jan -- The Armenian command has been compelled to throw into battle the last reserves, located in Stepanakert, Azerbaijan's Defense Ministry asserts. The latest ministry bulletin, issued this evening, reports failed attempts by the enemy to break through the Azerbaijani army's defense line in the Terter sector. According to the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry, static fighting has been continuing in the Agdam sector; no substantial changes have been reported today on the other sections of the front.
