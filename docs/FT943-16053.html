940706 FT 06 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Shani improves to Pounds 1.7m Shani Group, the USM-traded fashion wear company, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.71m for the nine month period to April 30 1994. The group is changing its year-end to October 31 reflecting the seasonal nature of LAS, the women's coat and suit business; comparisons cover the six months to end-January 1993. The outcome, up from Pounds 1.34m last time, was achieved on turnover ahead from Pounds 11.5m to Pounds 16m. Mr Martin Hollis, chairman, said the second period of the 15-month year had started well with turnover continuing to increase on a like-for-like basis. The interim dividend goes up 67 per cent, from 1.8p to 3p, payable from earnings ahead to 8p (6.3p) per share.
