BFN [Text] A session of the Council of the Belarusian Popular Front [BNF] was held in the conference hall of the Minsk Palace of Arts. The BNF delegates officially nominated their leader Zyanon Paznyak as a candidate in the upcoming presidential elections. In addition to this, a BNF statement was adopted. It states that the BNF will conduct a civilized election campaign, without discrediting and humiliating political opponents. The delegates have proposed that other Belarusian political parties and movements adopt corresponding statements.
