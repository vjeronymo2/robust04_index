930617 FT 17 JUN 93 / International Company News: US tobacco trader expands in Hungary UNIVERSAL Leaf Tobacco, the US tobacco trader and processor, is to take over Nyidofer, Hungary's dominant supplier of tobacco for cigarette makers. Universal's offer involves the acquisition of a 76 per cent shareholding, an immediate capital increase, a subsequent injection and substantial support for hard-pressed Hungarian tobacco growers. Total investment including commitments would amount to about Ft2bn (Dollars 22m), Hungary's State Property Agency privatisation authority said yesterday.
