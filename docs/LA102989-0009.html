October 29, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

After forcing myself to sit through "Roseanne," I know why Roseanne Barr didn't receive an Emmy nomination. She didn't deserve one and never will unless they put in a whining category. The entire cast needs basic acting lessons, which still would do little to improve the show because it is written on the level of 6th-grade bathroom humor. I cannot believe "Roseanne" gets better ratings than "The Cosby Show." I hold little hope for the future of TV.

Sharon Boudreau, Costa Mesa
