Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] On Monday [17 January], [Supreme Council Deputy] Mykola Porovskyy, committee chairman of the organization Crimea With Ukraine, said: I believe the presidential elections in Crimea run counter to the Ukrainian constitution. He said that the future course of events in Crimea can develop into a conflict. It is crucial, he said, to take necessary measures to prevent destabilizing processes in Crimea. Two candidates, Yuriy Meshkov and Mykola Bahrov will compete against each other in a runoff on 30 January.
