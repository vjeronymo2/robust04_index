920127 FT 27 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Saatchi US buy Saatchi & Saatchi has acquired the outstanding shares in GDLW, an advertising agency based in Houston, Texas. It will be merged with Campbell-Mithun-Esty to give significant cost savings. Consideration is Dollars 3m (Pounds 1.6m) to be satisfied by 13.07m shares. Further unconditional payments will be made over the next two years of Dollars 920,000.
