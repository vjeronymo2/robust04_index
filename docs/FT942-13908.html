940422 FT 22 APR 94 / UK Company News: Capitol coming to market via placing with Pounds 11m valuation Capitol Group, the security specialist, yesterday announced that it is coming to the market via a placing with institutional investors likely to value the group at about Pounds 11m. The pathfinder flotation prospectus for the group was issued yesterday. Capitol was founded in 1983 by ex-policeman Mr Ken Dulieu, managing director. It specialises in security and related services in investigatory, audit and stocktaking, and port and ferry security. The group announced pre-tax profits of Pounds 838,000 (Pounds 341,000) for the year ending March 31 1994. Turnover was Pounds 6.64m (Pounds 5.12m) and operating profit, before exceptional and non-recurring items, was Pounds 1m (Pounds 714,000). The proposed flotation is to be sponsored by Granville Davies. Impact day is planned for May 5 with dealings expected to commence on May 17.
