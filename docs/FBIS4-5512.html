BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 9 (KCNA) -- Foreign newspapers dedicated articles to the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song. The Indian paper INDIAN TIMES in an article headlined "President Kim Il-song, Elder Statesman of the World" said: His Excellency President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea was known as the youngest leader in the world in the 1940s and is lauded as the most veteran and authoritative leader today half a century since then. President Kim Il-song is a great person who has a nearly 70-year long history of political activities which can be said to be the whole life of a man. He is the only statesman and practician in the world history of politics who founded the chuche idea and has applied it to practical activities. The Korean people deem it a pride and happiness in national history to have such a leader. The Bangladesh paper BANGLA BANI in an article headlined "Best Wishes for Longevity to President Kim Il-song" said: History has recorded many famous leaders, but does not know such a great leader as President Kim Il-song. He is a great thinker and theoretician and leader who has attained reputation as a true leader of the people. He has performed immortal feats in the struggle for the cause of the independence of the popular masses. We wish a long life in good health to President Kim Il-song for the prosperity of Korea and the progress of mankind. SANGBAD, FIREZ KAGOZ, JANATA, GONO GONTU and other leading papers of Bangladesh dedicated articles to the April 15 fete. The Zimbabwean paper THE STAR carried an article titled "Poem of Praise Written by President Kim Il-song".
