Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, February 1 (SNARK)--Sergey Davidyan, chairman of the Karabakh Federation of Professional Unions, told SNARK the Karabakh professional unions introduce their initiative to transfer a part of the expenditures the professional unions possess to the Fund of Defence. The professional unions of some Karabakh enterprises and organizations are ready to send 20-25 per cent of the employees' salaries to the Fund of Defence. According to Davidyan, the Karabakh people are ready to send food from their stores to the front. During the period of last 2-3 days about 10 million rubles were transferred to the Fund of Defence.
