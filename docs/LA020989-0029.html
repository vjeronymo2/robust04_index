February 9, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

TITANS TO MEET NO. 19 NEVADA LAS VEGAS

Cal State Fullerton, coming off an upset of UC Santa Barbara that was the Titans' biggest win of the basketball season, faces a bigger challenge tonight as the Titans play host to 19th-ranked Nevada Las Vegas in a Big West Conference game tonight at Titan Gym.

The Rebels (15-5 overall and 10-1 in the Big West) handed Santa Barbara a 77-61 loss Monday in a game that was never close after the first 10 minutes.

Fullerton (9-10 and 4-6) lost the first meeting with UNLV this season, 66-63, on a desperation 3-pointer by Greg Anthony just before the buzzer. ROBYN NORWOOD
