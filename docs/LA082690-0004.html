CURRENTS: A GUIDE TO THE BEST OF SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA;

MAPS;

THE 3-D EFFECT

UPON first glimpse of a Raven oversized detailed wall map, many people run their fingers across the surface to prove to themselves that it's not a raised relief.

The digital landscapes, a unique combination of relief shading and elevation tinting, have a 3-D effect. The maps run from $20 to $65.

Raven Maps are sold at Geographia, 4000 Riverside Drive, Burbank; (818) 848-1414. Or telephone (800) 237-0798.
