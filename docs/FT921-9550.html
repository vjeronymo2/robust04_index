920211 FT 11 FEB 92 / UK Company News: MSI pays Pounds 1.5m to settle FKI dispute MS International has agreed terms with FKI Electricals for settling the long-running dispute relating to claims and counter-claims arising from the disposal of Laurence Scott and Electromotors and Laurence Scott and Electromotors (Wolverhampton) to FKI in June 1986. MSI will pay a total of Pounds 1.5m in three equal interest-free instalments this month, in July and next January. Both sides will waive any further claims and bear their own costs. The settlement will lead to an extraordinary charge of Pounds 2m in the accounts of MSI for the year ending May 2.
