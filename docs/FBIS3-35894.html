Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, 31 Jan (ARMENPRES) -- Bloody battles continued yesterday in a number of war fronts in Karabakh. The Karabakh Defense Army completely stopped the enemy's offensives towards Mardakert, Martuni, and Gadrut. On the same day, the Armenian fighters succeeded in taking under their control the previously lost positions near Omar pass in the north of Kelbadzharskiy Rayon. Six fighters were killed and 18 were injured on the Karabakh side during battles yesterday. The enemy lost nearly 150 soldiers and six armored cars, including four tanks. The military operations resumed in the north of Kelbadzharskiy Rayon this morning. The Azerbaijani Air Force planes bombed the settlements in Martuninskiy Rayon at noon today. According to preliminary reports, there are casualties.
