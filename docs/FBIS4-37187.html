BFN [Excerpt] Jeddah, 16 Jun (SPA) -- King Fahd Bin-'Abd al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, custodian of the two holy mosques, received at his residence outside the city of Jeddah today Haydar Abu Bakr al-'Attas who was accompanied by 'Abdallah al-Asnaj. [passage omitted] [Riyadh SPA in Arabic at 1812 GMT on 16 June, in a Jeddah-datelined item, adds: "His Royal Highness Prince Sultan Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, second deputy prime minister, minister of defense and aviation and inspector general, received at his highness's palace in Jeddah this afternoon Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas and 'Abdallah al-Asnaj."]
