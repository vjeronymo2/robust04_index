January 25, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

HEMOGLOBIN COUNT

Question: Please explain what is meant by the "hemoglobin count." All I know is that it has something to do with the blood.

Answer: The hemoglobin count is an indication of the amount of the substance in red blood cells that carries oxygen.
