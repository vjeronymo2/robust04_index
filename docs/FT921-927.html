920326 FT 26 MAR 92 / Doubt cast on Scots recovery SCOTLAND is escaping the worst effects of the recession, but there is unlikely to be any real sign of recovery in the Scottish economy in the first half of this year and very little growth in the year as a whole, according to a forecast yesterday. Quarterly Economic Bulletin March 1992, Fraser of Allander Institute, Curran Building, 100 Cathedral St, Glasgow G4 0LN. Pounds 10.
