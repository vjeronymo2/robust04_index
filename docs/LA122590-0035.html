December 25, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

IS IT JUST MUZAK OR MUSICAL MIND CONTROL?

If the purpose of Muzak ("Humming Right Along," Dec. 19) is to "reduce stress and increase job efficiency," then why do I feel like killing somebody after being exposed to it for a few minutes?

Face it. The whole idea behind Muzak is nothing but thought control. It's a wonder that Hitler or Stalin never thought of it.

As for me, I would just as soon hear the ear-shattering garble from some idiot's boom box.

FRANK. G. WILLIAMS, Independence, Calif.
