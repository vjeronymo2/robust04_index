941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Transformed Whessoe lapses to Pounds 179,000 loss Whessoe, which has withdrawn from heavy engineering and contracting and is now an instrumentation and control company, reported a Pounds 179,000 pre-tax loss for the year to September 30. There were profits of Pounds 7.14m last time. Turnover of Pounds 118m (Pounds 99.7m) included Pounds 37.3m (Pounds 56.7m) from discontinued operations while operating profits of Pounds 2.19m (Pounds 7.03m) included losses of Pounds 3.93m (Pounds 3.3m profits) on those operations. There was a Pounds 970,000 provision for restructuring Autronica, the Norwegian marine instrumentation and fire detection company acquired for Pounds 21.8m in October 1993. Net interest charged was Pounds 1.39m against a Pounds 38,000 credit. Losses per share came through at 3.76p (18.8p earnings) and there is no final dividend (5.9p). For the half year there was an unchanged interim of 2.3p.
