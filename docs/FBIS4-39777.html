BFN [Unattributed report: "The Presidents Have Conferred With Yuriy Skokov in Cheboksary"] [Text] Skokov has met in Cheboksary with the presidents of three republics: Nikolay Fedorov of Chuvashia, Mintimer Shaymiyev of Tataria [Tatarstan], and Murtaza Rakhimov of Bashkiria [Bashkortostan]. The meeting took place behind closed doors but ITAR-TASS reported that the political situation in Russia in the run-up to the 1996 presidential elections was discussed. Problems related to the end of the voucher privatization and transition to a new stage of reform were also considered.
