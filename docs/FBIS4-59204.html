BFN [By JORDAN TIMES staff reporter Elia Nasrallah] [Excerpt] Amman--Royal Jordanian's [RJ] twice weekly flights to Sanaa and Aden in north and south Yemen are suspended until the airports in the Arab country are officially operating normally again, according to a spokesman for the national airline Friday [6 May]. The military in Yemen have taken control of the airports and closed them to civilian flights until further notice; until then no RJ aircraft can fly to Yemen, Munib Toukan, RJ assistant vice president for public relations, told the JORDAN TIMES.
