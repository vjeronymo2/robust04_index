920908 FT 08 SEP 92 / International Company News: Australian TV network ahead NINE Network Australia, a television network controlled by Mr Kerry Packer, the Australian businessman, through his Consolidated Press Holdings group, yesterday reported a 101 per cent rise in net earnings to ADollars 60.5m (USDollars 43.20m) for the 12 months to June 30, from ADollars 30.1m a year earlier, agencies report from Sydney. Sales were 10 per cent ahead at ADollars 519m, against ADollars 470.8m a year earlier. Earnings per share advanced to 19 cents from 3 cents, and although the company is again not paying a dividend, it announced a one-for-20 bonus issue which represents a payout of 17 cents a share. During the year the company had a one-for-five share consolidation. At the year-end, net tangible asset backing per share was ADollars 2.28 compared with ADollars 2.10.
