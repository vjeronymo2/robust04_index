930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Property disposals aid Union Square The disposal of certain low yielding properties and the decline in interest rates helped Union Square further reduce pre-tax losses in the six months to September 30. On turnover of Pounds 940,000 (Pounds 19.2m), the pre-tax deficit of Pounds 9,000 compared with Pounds 686,000 in the previous first half and Pounds 483,000 in the year to end-March 1992. The company also announced that discussions with its bankers had been concluded. As a result, Thompson Investments (London) had acquired the outstanding loans and was prepared to extend the facilities on these loans for at least another year.
