930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Brokers Association reports, Landed strong general demand. Best East Africans were often much dearer with quality. Bright mediums were irregular but closed higher. Coloury mediums were strong and often 3 to 6p dearer and plainer sorts were also fully firm. Bright ceylons were also generally dearer. Offshore prices were fully firm to dearer. Quotations: quality 160p/kg, nom good medium 130p/kg, medium 108p/kg, low medium 90p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 162p for a Burundi pd.
