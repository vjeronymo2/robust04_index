BFN [Text] Havana, 4 Apr (PL) -- Today, official sources reported Argentine Secretary of State Alberto Lestelle is in Cuba to sign an agreement on drug addiction and drug trafficking. The National Information Agency reports that Lestelle said the Cuban and Argentine Governments agreed to sign a formal mutual assistance protocol in which both parties will be jointly responsible in combating this problem, which transcends their national borders. The Argentine leader said that, after months of working together with the Cuban Justice Ministry, Argentina has identified points of mutual interest in which they can cooperate. He added that Argentina has been implementing a mutual assistance program on the American continent and many governments have requested assistance to train specialized personnel. He noted that this has been done in Costa Rica, Guatemala, and the Dominican Republic, and he announced this will soon be established in Honduras and Nicaragua.
