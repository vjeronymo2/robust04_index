940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / UK Company News: High Gosforth High Gosforth Park, the racecourse operator, announced pre-tax profits of Pounds 49,675 for the year to end-December, against a deficit of Pounds 21,150 last time. Turnover rose from Pounds 1.13m to Pounds 1.2m. Net interest receivable and similar income increased to Pounds 179,931 (Pounds 40,936). After tax of Pounds 39,656 (Pounds 4,080) earnings per share emerged at 11p (losses 27.7p).
