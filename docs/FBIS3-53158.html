Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, custodian of the two holy mosques, has received a letter from his brother His Majesty King Hassan II of Morocco. The letter was handed to the custodian of the two holy mosques by Moroccan Minister of Awqaf and Islamic Affairs Abdelkebir Alaoui M'Daghri when he was received by the King at his office in al-Yamamah Palace today.
