Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, MARCH 12. (SNARK) -- The spread by the Georgian mass media information about the meeting of the Eduard Shevardnadze, the Georgian leader, with Vladislav Ardzinba, the Abkhazian leader, that allegedly has taken place in the USA isn't the true. SNARK was informed about it by the Abkhazian Representatives' Office. According to them such an information has a provocative character and times for the adopted by the Georgian parliament decree on the dissolution of the Abkhazian parliament.
