BFN [Text] The Executive Committee of the KNSB [Confederation of Independent Trade Unions in Bulgaria] decided today to call off mass strike actions until parliament reaches a final decision on the fate of the Lyuben Berov cabinet, but the KNSB structures are to remain ready to strike. The protests that are planned to begin on 25 May in various parts of the country are to be supported by KNSB union federations and regional structures. This decision was adopted after the Executive Committee had reached the view that at the moment no parliamentary majority exists and that the country has no legitimate government able to adopt responsible decisions.
