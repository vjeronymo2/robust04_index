Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Army Command, acting on a notice from the Military Prosecution Office, today circulated a composite picture of a suspect in the bombing of the Phalangist headquarters. [see item NC2012164193 in previous Terrorism Report] The Army Command asked citizens who have information about the person identified in the composite picture to apply in person at or telephone the Military Prosecution Office at any time. Added to the composite picture is the following description: He is between 27 to 30 years old with a round face and brownish complexion. His hair is black, combed back. He has a full head of hair. He has a broad and swollen chin. He is approximately 175 cm high and is heavily built.
