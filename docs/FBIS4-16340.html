CSO ["Election Results in Volyn Oblast"] [Text] The elections held on 10 April in Volyn Oblast's Lutsk e.d. No. 64 were won by Deputy Prime Minister Mykola Zhulynskyy (who defeated Stanislav Pkhydenko, first secretary of the oblast committee of the CPU); in Kovel e.d. No. 67, People's Deputy of Ukraine Andriy Mostyskyy defeated Hrytsenko, the SelPU candidate; in Horokhiv e.d. No. 69, Kateryna Vashchuk, head of an agrofirm, defeated Petro Kralyuk (URP), dean of the philology department at Volyn University; in Kamin-Kashyrskyy e.d. No. 70, Anton Buteyko, head of the president's service for international affairs, defeated Mykola Pinchuk, deputy minister for the protection of the population from the aftermath of Chernobyl; in Stara Vyzhivka e.d. No. 72, Vasyl Kornelyuk, head of the Ratne Rayon State Administration, defeated Mykola Shevchuk (SelPU), a collective farm director. No deputies were elected in Tsentralnyy e.d. No. 65, Novovolynsk e.d. No. 68, and Kivertsi e.d. No. 71.
