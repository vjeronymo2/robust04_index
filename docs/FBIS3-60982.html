Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] A fire, which was probably caused by a Molotov cocktail, early this morning destroyed the PASOK [Panhellenic Socialist Movement] offices on 16 Boukouvalas Street in Gizis [Athens suburb]. Another fire, which was probably also caused by arson, broke out shortly afterward in a car belonging to the municipality of Zografos [Athens suburb] and was parked on the junction of Ioannis Theologos and Amfitritis Streets. The car sustained serious damage. The Fire Service Investigations Department is conducting investigations.
