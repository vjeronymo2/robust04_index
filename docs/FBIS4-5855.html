BFN [Text] A high-ranking 130-person Malaysian delegation led by His Excellency [H.E.] Prime Minister Dr. Mahathir Mohamed, and his wife, arrived in Phnom Penh this morning for a three-day official visit. Upon arrival at Pochentong Airport, the delegation was cordially and warmly welcomed by Prince Krompreah Norodom Ranariddh, first prime minister; Princess Norodom Marie Ranariddh; Samdech Hun Sen, second prime minister, and wife; Prince Kromkhun Norodom Sirivut, deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs and international relations; H.E. Sar Kheng, deputy prime minister and co-minister of the interior; and many other officials and ethnic groups. It is expected that during his visit, Mahathir will have an audience with His Majesty Norodom Sihanouk Varaman, call on Samdech Chea Sim, chairman of the National Assembly; and hold talks with his counterparts, Prince Krompreah Norodom Ranariddh and Samdech Hun Sen. The delegation also laid a wreath at the independence monument.
