BFN [Excerpt] Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali received at his office today Ilyas Frayj, Dr. Zakariya al-Agha, Dr. 'Azmi al-Shu'aybi, and Dr. 'Abd-al-Hafiz al-Ashhab, who have been appointed members of the Palestinian National Authority [PNA], as well as the Palestinian ambassador in Amman. Dr. al-Majali stressed to the Palestinian delegation Jordan's firm support for the PNA in its new task in Gaza and Jericho. He emphasized that Jordan views coordination with the PNA as an indispensable need for which there is no alternative. The Palestinian delegation expressed its great appreciation for His Majesty King Husayn and his stands, which reflect the sincerity of his majesty, the Jordanian political leadership, and the Jordanian people toward the Palestinian people.
