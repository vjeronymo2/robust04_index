Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A Georgian government delegation led by Prime Minister Otar Patsatsia arrived in Moscow later in the day on Tuesday, Georgian embassy officials told Interfax. The two sides are expected to discuss economic cooperation with each other and how to clear the ground for President Yeltsin's visit to Tbilisi early next month. The Georgian team includes the ministers for industry, trade, and supplies, the heads of railway, oil and gas departments, the deputy minister for the agriculture and food industry, and the first deputy foreign minister.
