Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Albert Kochetkov] [Text] Tbilisi February 3 TASS -- Georgian Deputy Defence Minister Nikolay Kekelidze was killed early on Thursday [3 February] by an explosion in his own apartment in downtown Tbilisi, the Defence Ministry said and added that this was evidently an act of terrorism. Kekelidze supervised the military-industrial complex and law enforcement agencies. [Paris AFP in English at 0915 GMT on 3 Feb in a Moscow-datelined report adds: "The blast, which occurred around 4:00 a.m. (0100 GMT), also killed another member of the minister's family."]
