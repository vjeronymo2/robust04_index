Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, March 3 (XINHUA) -- South Korea said today that it considers the suspension of "Team Spirit '94" joint military exercise with the United States conditional. The South Korean Defense Ministry said in a statement that one condition is that the inspection of nuclear sites in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK) by U.N. Inspectors is "completed with success." Another condition is that the DPRK and South Korea exchange special envoys to discuss "concretely" the nuclear issue, said the statement. According to an agreement reached on February 25 in New York between the United States and DPRK, the United States would suspend "Team Spirit '94" joint military exercise with South Korea.
