Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 23 (KCNA) -- An art troupe of China arrived here Tuesday to participate in the 12th April Spring Friendship Art Festival. It was met at the airport by Kim Chong-ho, vice-minister of Culture and Art and vice-chairman of the organising committee of the festival, Sin An-pang, vice-chairman of the Pyongyang Municipal Administrative and Economic Committee, officials concerned and artistes in the city.
