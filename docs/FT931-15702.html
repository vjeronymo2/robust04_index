930112 FT 12 JAN 93 / Britain may reinforce its troops in Bosnia Britain will today consider reinforcing its 2,400 troops in Bosnia to coincide with the expected introduction of a UN-backed no-fly zone, Philip Stephens reports from London. Prime Minister John Major will meet cabinet colleagues to discuss options to strengthen the force's security. Officials said options included heavier equipment for ground forces, and sending the aircraft carrier Ark Royal to the Adriatic.
