October 2, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

LABOR PARTY SCRAPS A-ARMS STANCE

Britain's opposition Labor Party today scrapped its longstanding commitment to unilateral nuclear disarmament, handing leader Neil Kinnock a key victory in his drive to steer the party on a new moderate course.

A revised defense strategy which abandons a unilateral approach and commits Labor to joining global disarmament talks was approved by a 3-2 margin on the second day of the party's weeklong annual conference.
