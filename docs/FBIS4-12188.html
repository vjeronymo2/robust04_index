BFN [Text] A responsible source has stated to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', that a number of news agencies and newspapers have carried inaccurate reports on the proceedings of the meetings that took place in the Omani city of Salalah between brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman, and brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council, in the presence of His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id of Oman, and the subsequent bilateral meeting between the brother chairman of the Presidential Council and his deputy. The source regretted the unjustified confusion and anxiety that those incorrect reports have caused among the public. The source indicated that although those meetings did not emerge with the results that all Yemenis had looked forward to, they did help stress the need to abide by the pledge and accord document and to swiftly implement the decisions that the Council of Ministers made during its meetings in the economic and trade capital [Aden], especially regarding security and military issues.
