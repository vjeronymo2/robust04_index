Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "A Hospital Is Sought To Treat Husayn Kamil"] [Text] Amman, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- Iraqi sources have confirmed that Husayn Kamil Hasan al-Majid, the Iraqi president's son-in-law [and industry minister], has been moved from Jordan with a view to giving him intensive treatment after the first brain surgery he underwent 10 days ago. The sources believe al-Majid may end up in a Swiss hospital, as the French authorities refused to grant him an entry visa to their territory. The Iraqi Embassy in Amman sought to move the Iraqi president's son-in-law to Switzerland, Cuba, Romania, or Russia.
