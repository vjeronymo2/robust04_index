Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] With a visit to the port of Karachi by Iranian Navy personnel, who are participating in the first joint maneuvers between Iran and Pakistan, the maneuvers continued today. The deputy in charge of cultural, propaganda, and defense affairs at the headquarters of the commander in chief, according to a report by Central News Unit, said that in the continuation of the joint maneuvers between Iran and Pakistan, the participating destroyers and submarines of the two forces carried out tactical practices of several submarines, communication operations, safety training, submarine operations, marine defense, damage control, and combat with marine fire.
