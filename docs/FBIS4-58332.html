Therefore, in order to lift the arms embargo from the Bosnian Muslims, to halt the Serbs' aggression in Gorazde, and to quell all the trouble making against the Muslims, one should fight against the United States and scorch the roots of its mischief-mongering. It is an immutable reality that "the United States is the mother of all evils of the century."
