BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Demidetskiy] [Text] Kishinev June 25 TASS -- "In keeping with the current legislation all investigative and operational activities will affect people's rights in one way or another. Without this it will be impossible to fight crime," Russian Counterintelligence Service Chief Sergey Stepashin said. In an exclusive interview with ITAR-TASS on Saturday, Stepashin said that "any operational activities inevitably limit the rights of criminals. This is an axiom. In this connection I would like to say again that I am for violation of the `right' of criminals to kill, rob, rape and encroach upon the rights of other people and their life. I am for such limitations, provided all procedural norms ensuring the calm life of the citizens of Russia are observed."
