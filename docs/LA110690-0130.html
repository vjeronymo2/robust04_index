November 6, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL ROUNDUP: POLLUTION RESEARCH PLANS TO OPEN PLANT IN SPAIN

Pollution Research & Control Corp. in Glendale announced plans to establish a manufacturing plant in Spain to help it penetrate the Eastern European market.

The company makes devices for monitoring air pollution, and its new factory would be in Madrid. Pollution Research also said Sir S.A., a Spanish company, awarded its new plant a $250,000 contract for pollution-monitoring instruments.
