Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Interior Minister Salamah Hammad has stated that the criminals who planted explosives at the Cinema Rivoli in Amman, Cinema Salwa in al-Zarqa', and the al-'Akrush Supermarket in the town of Safut have been apprehended. The explosive charge planted in Safut was discovered and defused, he noted. In a statement to the Jordanian News Agency, Petra, the interior minister added: Muhammad 'Ali Shihadah, alias Abu-'Abbad, a construction worker aged 26 years, and 'Id Salih al-Jahalin, a plumber aged 31 years, have admitted that they committed these crimes. They are part of a group that will be uncovered later. [passage omitted]
