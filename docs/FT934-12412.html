931027 FT 27 OCT 93 / Kirin Brewery plans to form beer production venture in China Kirin Brewery, Japan's largest beer maker, is planning to form a beer production joint venture in China with a Chinese beer-maker based in Dalian and a Hong Kong trading company, writes Emiko Terazono in Tokyo. The move follows a rush of leading foreign beer companies into China's beer market, the third largest in the world. Kirin said it was negotiating with Dalian Bohai Brewery and Van Yu Trading of Hong Kong, but it planned to invest Dollars 27m in beer lines with production capacity of up to 60,000 kilolitres.
