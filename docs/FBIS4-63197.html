BFN [Unattributed report from ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA, ITAR-TASS column under the "Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow" rubric: "Ilyushin On Order"] [Text] A new-type Il-103 developed by the S. Ilyushin Experimental Design Bureau has made its maiden flight at an airfield in Zhukovskoye, near Moscow. This small, multirole aircraft fitted with an engine manufactured by the U.S. firm Teledyne Continental Motors is destined to carry passengers or freight, for pilot instruction and training, environmental monitoring, or the delivery of emergency medical aid. Series production will be carried out at the Lukhovitsy Aircraft Plant. There are already plenty of orders for the aircraft, including some from foreign companies.
