BFN [Excerpts] Belgrade, April 13 (AFP) -- The Yugoslav government officially withdrew press accreditations from AFP correspondents here Wednesday [13 April], accusing them of waging a "media war" against Belgrade. The information ministry withdrew accreditations from bureau chief Nicolas Miletitch and three other journalists to cover events in Serbia and Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], but not for Serb-held areas of Croatia and Bosnia. On Tuesday the ministry accused AGENCE FRANCE-PRESSE and Cable News Network (CNN) of "waging a merciless media war against Yugoslavia, from its territory." [passage omitted] The AFP journalists whose accreditation was withdrawn can continue to coordinate coverage of events in Macedonia and Albania.
