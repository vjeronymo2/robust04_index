Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Report by Corban Costa] [Text] Fernando Henrique Cardoso bade farewell as finance minister to President Itamar Franco last night. He announced that his platform as a presidential candidate will be his economic plan. Cardoso agreed to be the Brazilian Social Democracy Party presidential candidate during a meeting with party leaders yesterday. He will today hand the president of the Republic his resignation letter. Cardoso said the economic plan will not be changed by the installation of Ambassador Rubens Ricupero as the new finance minister. [Begin Cardoso recording] He has already talked to the economic team and he is keeping them all in their positions. We have high esteem for Minister Ricupero; we believe he will help very much in the implementation of the government action plan, which was proposed by me but is the government plan and belongs to the entire country. The plan will not be changed. [end recording] Minister Fernando Henrique Cardoso will grant a news conference later today and then leave for Sao Paulo where he will spend the Holy Week holidays. The new finance minister will be installed next week.
