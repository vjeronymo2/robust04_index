The amendments are embodied in the following OAC regulations: Definitions, Rule 3745&hyph;21&hyph;01; Attainment dates and compliance time schedules, Rule 3745&hyph;21&hyph;04; Control of emissions of volatile organic compounds from stationary sources, Rule 3745&hyph;21&hyph;09; and Compliance test methods and procedures, Rule 3745&hyph;21&hyph;10.
