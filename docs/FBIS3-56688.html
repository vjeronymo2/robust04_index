Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report from ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA, ITAR-TASS roundup: "Condolences to Victims' Families"] [Text] Russian President Boris Yeltsin has sent the U.S. President a message expressing condolences to the families of those who died in the powerful earthquake which hit southern California causing deaths, extensive destruction, and material damage. This has been reported by the Russian president's press service. The message expresses the confidence that such qualities as the determination and fortitude shown by Americans in such emergency situations will help them quickly overcome the aftermath of the disaster.
