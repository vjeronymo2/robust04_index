BFN [Text] Moscow, 9 June, IRNA -- Turkmen President Saparmurat Niyazov on Thursday [9 June] expressed satisfaction with Tehran-Ashkhabad relations and underscored the "importance" of cooperation between the two neighbors in the Caspian Sea. Niyazov made the remarks in a meeting with the visiting Iranian Deputy Foreign Minister Morteza Sarmadi in Ashkhabad. Sarmadi said relations between Iran and Turkmenistan were a model among other regional states and emphasized that bilateral relations could not only benefit both nations but could also play an "effective" role in maintaining security and stability in Central Asia. The two sides also exchanged views on ways of implementing agreements previously reached between Iranian and Turkmen presidents in the holy city of Mashhad, outcome of Niyazov's recent visit to Indonesia and Malaysia, map of Sarakhs-Tajan railway which links Central Asia to east Asia and the latest developments in the Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS).
