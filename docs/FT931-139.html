930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Culver turns in Pounds 326,000 Culver Holdings, which was formed to make a recommended offer for Wyndham Group in July 1991, achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 326,000 in the year to December 31. For the previous six months there were profits of Pounds 130,000. Turnover for the group, which operates BMW and Honda motor dealerships, amounted to Pounds 21.4m (Pounds 10.3m for six months). Earnings per share came through at 0.56p (0.29p) and, following the Budget changes, a second interim of 0.1p is declared for a total of 0.2p.
