930529 FT 29 MAY 93 / Betws miners bid for their pit REDUNDANT miners at Betws colliery, near Ammanford, Dyfed, are bidding to buy their pit, which stopped production yesterday. The pit is among the 10 British Coal is being allowed to shut following a High Court ruling on Wednesday. The 95 miners have formed a co-operative to mount a counter-bid to moves by private contractors interested in taking over the drift mine.
