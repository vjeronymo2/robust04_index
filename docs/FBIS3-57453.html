Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Mikhail Shevtsov] [Text] Moscow, 24 Jan -- Despite the work that has been done by the Russian Ministry of Defense on building the Russian armed forces, certain errors have been made in organizing the management of the forces, and in personnel and training work. Military communications are giving rise to serious complaints, and cooperation with local administrations in resolving matters concerning the draft has not been properly established in all places. This was stated at a working conference of administrative bodies of the General Staff and the Defense Ministry held today by Russian Defense Minister Army General Pavel Grachev. An ITAR-TASS correspondent was told at the Defense Ministry's information and press directorate that the main aim of the conference was to reveal the ministry's shortcomings and errors in time, and to assess them. Among the positive results of the ministry's work since it was set up, note was made of the elaboration of plans for the development of the armed forces, for their strategic deployment and the organization of rear supplies, and the creation of a blueprint for a military training system. On conclusion of the conference, Defense Minister Grachev stressed that the correct conclusions would be drawn in the armed forces and every measure would be taken effectively to ensure the security of the state.
