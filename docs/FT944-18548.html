941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / Emerging Markets: News round-up - Brazil Mr Ciro Gomes, Brazil's Economy Minister, said that the government had the means to slow the flow of foreign capital to local markets, but preferred not to comment whether any action of the sort was being considered. Investors in Brazilian markets are concerned over the possibility that the government may raise a flat tax on investments directed to the stock market and fixed-income instruments.
