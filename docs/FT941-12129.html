940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / Observer: Out of touche Palestine Liberation Organisation chairman Yassir Arafat and Shimon Peres, Israel's foreign minister, may have made a bit of a splash at the World Economic Forum in Davos, but they didn't register with everybody. George Westropp, the eyes and ears of accountants Touche Ross, swears that he overheard the following question and answer session between a delegate and his trophy wife: Wife: 'Who's that, dear?' Delegate: 'Shimon Perez.' Wife: 'Sherman Persia, never heard of him.'
