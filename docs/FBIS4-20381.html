CSO [Summary] In 1994 the planned early rice area in Zhejiang Province is 13 million mu, an increase of more than 1 million mu over 1993. Gross output of grain will reach 30 billion jin. Early rice accounts for 40 percent of grain output in Zhejiang, and 70 percent of procurement. The provincial government has stipulated that the procurement price for contract orders of early indica rice will be 42 yuan per 50 kilograms, plus an additional price of 3.74 yuan. If the state procurement price is lower than 42 yuan, Zhejiang will maintain 42 yuan, if the state price is higher than 42 yuan, Zhejiang will implement the state price, the additional price will not change.
