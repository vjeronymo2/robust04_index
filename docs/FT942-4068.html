940611 FT 11 JUN 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Black pepper prices continued to rise this week, in contrast to those for white pepper, which suffered from a lack of buying support, reports Man Producten. The black ASTA grades remained steady from most origins, like Indonesia, India, Malaysia and Brazil. But faq grades went up sharply in response to to strong demand from Singapore, Japan and the Middle East. In the US market spot Indian black pepper rose to 88 cents a pound, June/July shipment to 84 cents. In Europe the faq grade increased to Dollars 1,650 tonne. ASTA was traded at Dollars 1,825, spot, Rotterdam. Other spice were generally unchanged.
