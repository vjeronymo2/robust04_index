940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / World Cup '94: Tournament marks high and low points World Cup highlights after 44 games: Most goals in one game by one player: Oleg Salenko, Russia, five goals against Cameroon, June 28; Russia won 6-1. Most cards in one game: 10 cards in Italy-Nigeria, July 5 (nine yellows, one red), and 10 in Mexico-Bulgaria, July 5 (eight yellows, two reds). Largest crowd: 93,869 at US-Romania, Pasadena, June 26. Some 2.9m spectators watched the first 44 matches. Smallest crowd: 44,132, Nigeria-Bulgaria, June 21. Most-watched game in the US: America's 1-0 second-round loss to Brazil, July 4, was the highest-rated soccer telecast ever in the US, Nielsen Media Research said. ABC Television estimated that 32m viewers tuned in. Penalties: 13 have been awarded in regulation time, and all produced goals.
