940309 FT 09 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Instem Improved performance and the completion of several large contracts during the year helped Instem, the USM-quoted information systems group, lift pre-tax profits for the year to December 31 to Pounds 1.02m. This compares with Pounds 575,000 last time, when the figure was hit by an exceptional Pounds 386,000 for bad debts. Turnover was up from Pounds 14.3m to Pounds 19.3m and earnings per share came out ahead at 14.8p (8.5p). A same-again final of 1.8p is recommended, to give a total maintained at 3.1p.
