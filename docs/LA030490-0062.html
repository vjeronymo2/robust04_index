March 4, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

POLICE OVERKILL

The San Diego Police Department is understaffed? They claim that a response time of 30 minutes or longer is very common, yet they send 30 cars, motorcycle units and a helicopter to corral 200 secondary students conducting an orderly protest. Can this be the same understaffed force?

This overreaction should be an embarrassment to the SDPD and the citizens of San Diego.

JIM WRIGHT

San Diego
