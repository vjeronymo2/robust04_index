920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / Murdoch gas field output purchased KINETICA, the independent gas supplier, has purchased the entire output of the Murdoch gas field in the North Sea. The company, a joint venture between Conoco, the North Sea producer, and PowerGen, the UK electricity generator, already supplies more than 1,000 sites in the UK. The Murdoch field has reserves of 320bn cu ft, and is expected to have a peak production rate of 140m cu ft a day.
