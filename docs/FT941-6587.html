940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Goldsborough issue result Applications for shares in Goldsborough Healthcare, the nursing home, hospitals and homecare group, through the public offer were allotted in full after the issue was marginally undersubscribed. Of the 12.8m shares available to the public there was demand for 12.6m shares. Of these 3.4m shares were applied for in the preferential offer. Shareholders of Kunick, the leisure group which sold its 50 per cent stake in Goldsborough in the float, were able to apply for preferential shares.
