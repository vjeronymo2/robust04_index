March 6, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

TOP 20 DEFENSE CONTRACTORS

McDonnell Douglas Corp. is still the nation's biggest defense contractor, but Tenneco Inc. has plummeted from fourth to 21st on the Defense Department's list of arms makers, the Pentagon said. The department released its 1989 totals of prime contract awards to companies, showing St. Louis-based McDonnell Douglas with $8.6 billion of work last year led the list.
