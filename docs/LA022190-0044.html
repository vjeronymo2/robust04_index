February 21, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: GRAMMY WATCH

The Post-Grammy Bash: After the Grammys, the National Academy of Recording Artists' annual party at the Biltmore Hotel will feature five acts: Mary's Danish, the Count Basie Orchestra directed by Frank Foster, the Steve Morse Band, Jack Sheldon and Red Holloway, and soul artist Vesta Williams. BETH KLEID
