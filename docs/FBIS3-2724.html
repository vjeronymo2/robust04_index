Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 10 (XINHUA) -- Chinese State Councillor Li Guixian met a delegation from the State Control Service of Albania here today. The delegation, led by chairman Blerim Cela of the State Control Service, arrived here on February 8 at the invitation of the Chinese Ministry of Supervision. Chinese Minister of Supervision Cao Qingze took part in the meeting.
