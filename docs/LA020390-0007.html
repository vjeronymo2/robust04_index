February 3, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

HERE ARE 10 REASONS WHY BRONCOS WERE NO MATCH FOR 49ERS

The curse of John Elway, lucky No. 7, apparently has been tied to unlucky 13. Elway's margin of defeat has increased by 13 points in each of his Super Bowl losses (from 19 against the Giants to 32 against the Redskins to 45 against the 49ers).

ARNO KEKS, El Monte
