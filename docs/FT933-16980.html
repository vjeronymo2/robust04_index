930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / Shake-up urged for civil justice A RADICAL shake-up in the civil justice system to cut costs, reduce delays and provide more user-friendly justice was called for yesterday by the Bar and the Law Society. Ms Hilary Heilbron QC, who chaired a joint working party of barristers and solicitors, said civil justice was available only to the very rich or the very poor. Even for those who could afford to litigate, costs had escalated, delays had increased and trials had become more complex and took longer, she said.
