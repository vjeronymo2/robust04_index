921125 FT 25 NOV 92 / South Korean GNP up 3.1% South Korea's gross national product grew 3.1 per cent in inflation-adjusted terms in the third quarter, down from 7.8 per cent a year earlier, Reuter reports from Seoul. The provisional Bank of Korea figure compares with a 5.9 per cent growth in the second quarter and 7.4 per cent in the first quarter of 1992.
