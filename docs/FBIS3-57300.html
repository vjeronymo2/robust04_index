Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] General Ibragim Suleymenov, chairman of the Chechen National Salvation Committee, has refuted rumors about a peace deal between the armed opposition and General Dzhokhar Dudayev's regime. He said to the ANI agency that the opposition is not going to renounce the use of force, although it would prefer avoiding bloodshed. Ibragim Sulelymenov said that the National Salvation Committee formations are well armed and have reached agreements about joint actions with other armed groups.
