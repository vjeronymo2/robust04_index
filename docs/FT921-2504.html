920319 FT 19 MAR 92 / UK Company News: WSP shows sharp decline to Pounds 1.06m WSP Holdings, the consulting engineer, saw pre-tax profits tumble almost 30 per cent to Pounds 1.06m in 1991 on turnover down from Pounds 11.5m to Pounds 9.6m. A rise in interest charges of Pounds 108,000 reflected a full 12 months on the commercial mortgage to acquire the Bristol offices compared with seven months in 1990. Earnings emerged at 7.2p (10.1p) and the final dividend is 1.8p maintaining the total at 2.9p.
