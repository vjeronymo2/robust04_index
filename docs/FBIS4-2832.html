BFN [Text] Bonn, April 13 (XINHUA) -- German Chancellor Helmut Kohl expressed his satisfaction at the Sino-German economic cooperation while meeting with a visiting Chinese mayor here today. He also expressed his confidence that China's present economic policy will succeed. Kohl asked Huang Ju, mayor of China's biggest city of Shanghai, to convey his greetings to Chinese leaders. Huang, who arrived in Germany on Sunday for an eight-day visit, told Kohl that Shanghai plans to set up an international group corporation in Europe with its headquarters in Hamburg.
