September 30, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

EIGHT-MAN

First Lutheran 22, L.A. Lutheran 18 -- Senior quarterback Paul Stark completed three of five passes for 165 yards and two touchdowns to lead First Lutheran in a Heritage League game at L. A. Baptist.

Stark threw scoring passes of 52 and 75 yards in the second quarter to give First Lutheran (4-0, 2-0 in league play) a 14-12 lead. Stark's scoring strikes were to Rob Arndt and Mark Cortez, respectively.

Matt McGraw returned a kickoff 60 yards for a touchdown in the second quarter to bring L. A. Lutheran (2-2, 2-1) within 14-12 at halftime. ROB SONNER
