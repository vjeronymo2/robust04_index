BFN [Text] Diplomatic sources in Beijing reported on 2 May that North Korea recently drastically strengthened vigilance at the border area with China to prevent North Korean people from escaping, and that it instructed the military and police that if escapees are discovered, they are to be immediately shot to death. The North Korean authorities moved the agents of the (?Security Department) and Ministry of Public Security, who were serving in the rear, toward the border area of North Hamgyong Province and Chagang Province. They are intensely watching over the moves of the residents and soldiers. It was learned that because of such vigilance, the number of North Koreans escaping to China has decreased drastically.
