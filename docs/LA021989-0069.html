CRITICS' CHOICE: THE BLOODING BY JOSEPH WAMBAUGH (PERIGORD PRESS/ WILLIAM MORROW: $18.95; 288 PP.)

"Wambaugh, as always, tells a good story. But one cannot read 'The Blooding' without some qualms as to what is happening to British law enforcement: 'The Blooding' is reportage." John Sutherland
