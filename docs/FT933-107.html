930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / International Company News: French cigarette group improves to FFr232m SEITA, France's monopoly maker of cigarettes which is slated for privatisation, reported profits of FFr232m (Dollars 40m) for the first half of this year, compared with FFr168m in the same period of 1992. Turnover rose 2.2 per cent in the half, compared with the corresponding period last year.
