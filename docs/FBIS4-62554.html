BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Grigoriy Arslanov and Pavel Spirin] [Text] Beijing May 16 TASS -- Russian Federation State Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin, who is in Beijing at the head of a delegation of Russian lawmakers, had a meeting today with Chairman of China Jiang Zemin. During the talks they exchanged views on a wide range of questions of bilateral relations. The Chinese leader expressed confidence that the great people of Russia can overcome all their difficulties, and success awaits them. "There is a need of stability and concord. They are prerequisites of any kind of reforms," Jiang Zemin emphasised. "I am very glad that the State Duma maintains friendly ties with All-China Assembly of People's Representatives. This will, undoubtedly, play an important role in the development of our relations," the Chinese leader added. The State Duma chairman conveyed greetings to the Chinese chairman from Russian President Boris Yeltsin and on his behalf reaffirmed invitation to Jiang Zemin to visit Russia. He expressed the hope that during his visit to Russia the Chinese leader will meet the State Duma deputies and tell them about the experience of Chinese reforms. During the meeting Ivan Rybkin elaborated on work of the State Duma. In particular, he told that Russian lawmakers have already held readings on Russian-Chinese ties. "Irrespective of political bias, we have decided that relations with great China are of top priority for us," he noted.
