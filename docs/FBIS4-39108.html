BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan June 3 TASS -- Eduard Zuloyan, 37, has been appointed Armenian deputy foreign minister and included in a state delegation at the talks with the Russian Federation. Zuloyan graduated from the Yerevan Polytechnical Institute. He worked in the Yerevan Physics Institute and the Armenian State Security Committee, and was a foreign affairs adviser to the republican minister of transport and communications. Since July last year he has been heading the military-political department at the Armenian Foreign Ministry. Zuloyan is married and has two children.
