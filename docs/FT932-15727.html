930410 FT 10 APR 93 / UK Company News: Reduced losses at Carlisle Carlisle Group, the property services company, reported a reduced loss of Pounds 946,945 pre-tax for the year to December 31. The outcome, which came from reduced turnover of Pounds 5.15m (Pounds 6.03m), compared with a deficit of Pounds 1.16m in 1991. Mr Jonathan Harris, chairman, said the result masked an improvement in the second half. The group had countered recession by cutting overheads and staffing. The waiving of contractual salary entitlements by directors and the sale of investments also contributed. Losses per share were 2.8p (3.2p).
