940912 FT 12 SEP 94 / Arts (Openings): Warsaw The Warsaw Autumn contemporary music festival, opening on Thursday, offers tributes to three recently deceased Polish composers - Witold Lutoslawski, Andrzej Panufnik and Roman Haubenstock-Ramati. Founded in 1956, the festival has emerged from the vicissitudes of the past decade with renewed vigour and a broader international outlook. Bright Sheng and Magnus Lindberg are among this year's foreign composers, and the line-up of performers includes Anne-Sophie Mutter.
