931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Landed with less demand, reports Tea Brokers' Association. Apart from a few selected brighter North Indians which remained firm all others lost 5 to 15 pence. East Africans opened about steady but prices declined as the sale progressed and closed up to 15 pence lower. Offshore good competition but at easier rates. Quotations: quality 200p/kg, good medium 130p/kg, medium 115p/kg and low medium 100p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 242p/kg for an Assam pf.
