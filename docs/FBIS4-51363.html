BFN [Text] This afternoon, Xie Fei, CPC Central Committee Political Bureau member and Guangdong Provincial CPC Committee secretary, met in Zhudao Hotel and held cordial talks with some locally based model workers. Comrade Xie Fei said: Guangdong should strive to build a competent and high-quality contingent of workers, and advocate and develop five fine spirits, namely, the spirit of acting as masters of the country and the enterprise, the spirit of selfless devotion, the spirit of reform and innovation, the spirit of hard work, and the spirit of solidarity and mutual assistance. To this end, government departments concerned and trade unions at all levels across the province should show concern for the livelihood of workers and make every possible endeavor to help workers with difficulties in their daily lives overcome those difficulties. Provincial leaders including [provincial Governor] Zhu Senlin, Zhang Guoying, Huang Huahua, Zhang Gaoli, Zhong Qiquan, and others were also present during the meeting.
