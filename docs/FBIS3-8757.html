Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 14 Mar--Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, leader of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia [LDPR], left Warsaw for Moscow on Monday afternoon. Zhirinovskiy and his entourage were seen off at Warsaw Okecie airport by Janusz Bryczkowski, leader of the Polish National Front, at whose invitation the LDPR leader was staying in Poland. Zhirinovskiy's departure, in contrast to his arrival on Friday evening, took place without incident because there were no other people present in the airport departure hall apart from security service men, both plainclothes and uniformed, and journalists.
