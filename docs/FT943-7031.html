940825 FT 25 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Gibbs & Dandy up to Pounds 425,000 Against a background of fierce competition, sales at Gibbs & Dandy, the builders' merchant, rose by 5 per cent to Pounds 11.9m and pre-tax profits climbed from Pounds 292,000 to Pounds 425,000. Mr John Dandy, chairman, said the sales trend had continued and reflected the tentative recovery in the construction industry. Earnings per share advanced from 2.5p to 4.6p. The interim dividend is raised to 1.2p (1p) and a final of at least 1.8p (1.5p) is forecast.
