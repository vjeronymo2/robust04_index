Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres says the disagreements between Israel and the PLO lie in the sphere of security, including control of border crossings, road security, and the size of security areas. Mr. Peres says that to bridge these gaps, Israel is prepared to view new proposals, provided they are in line with Israel's security concepts. The foreign minister made these remarks to our political correspondent Shlomo Raz prior to his departure for Oslo, where he will meet with U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher and PLO head Yasir 'Arafat and attend the funeral of Norwegian Foreign Minister Johan Jorgen Holst.
