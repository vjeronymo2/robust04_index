BFN [Text] Since last night, Israelis have been banned from entering the Jericho autonomy region until a new announcement is issued. Our correspondent Ron Nesi'el reports that the reason is concern about attempts to attack Israelis after hundreds of Palestinian prisoners arrived in the town. Jericho itself is quiet. There is a large Palestinian police presence, especially in the area of the mosque, but there have been no reports of disturbances.
