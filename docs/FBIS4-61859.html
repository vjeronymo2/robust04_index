BFN [Text] The expenditure of the military-industrial complex in the draft federal 1994 budget has been increased from 37 trillion to 50 trillion rubles. The draft is scheduled to be considered today by the State Duma. Aleksandr Piskunov, deputy chairman of the Duma defense committee, said the amendment was introduced after a roundtable meeting of deputies and government members. The meeting was convened on 5 May after a personal request by Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin to President Boris Yeltsin.
