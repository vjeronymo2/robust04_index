Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Fourteen people have been killed and two seriously wounded in an attack at Creighton in Southern Natal. Our Durban news staff reports that a group of canvassers, mainly young people, were collecting fees in preparation of an ANC [African National Congress] voter education program planned for today. The people were sleeping in a hut when they were attacked early this morning. No further details are known at this stage.
