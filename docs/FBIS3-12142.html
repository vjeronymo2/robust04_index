Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 28 Feb (MENA)-- Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa has expressed his regret at the attack against a Maronite church in Beirut yesterday. It is a regrettable and sad incident and we hope Lebanon will not be subjected once again to that wave of assaults and disturbances, he said. Musa expressed Egypt's condolences to the victims of that painful incident.
