920907 FT 07 SEP 92 / Construction Contracts: Teacher training JOHN SISK & SON has won contracts worth Pounds 11m. Heading the list is a Pounds 4.1m design and build project to construct a school of teaching studies at the North London Polytechnic. Other work includes a Pounds 3.5m fit-out at the shopping mall at Centre West in Hammersmith and a Pounds 1.2m office fit-out for the RNIB at Peterborough.
