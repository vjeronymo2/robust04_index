November 28, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

HEADING BACK: Three minutes after election of Britain's new prime minister (A1), Sir Eldon Griffiths (above) was phoning Orange County, saying he'll be back here by Sunday.

Griffiths, a Conservative member of Parliament from Suffolk and president of Orange County's World Affairs Council, was recalled to London to vote for prime minister. . . . The result seems "very brutal, but it is very efficient. In two weeks, we changed from a longstanding prime minister who was getting into trouble to a man who has the support of the entire party. If you have a President who falls out of favor, you're stuck with him." Jerry Hicks and Jeffrey A. Perlman
