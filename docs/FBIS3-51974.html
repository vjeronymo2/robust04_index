Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Carlos Lage, member of the Politburo and secretary of the Council of Ministers, has affirmed that an increase in efficiency, conservation, and discipline also constitute resources. During an address at the workers' assembly at the Antillana de Acero factory in Havana, Lage stated that more can be accomplished with the resources available to Cuba. Likewise, Pedro Ross Leal, member of the Politburo and secretary general of the Cuban Workers Federation, stated at the meeting that the program of measures to solve the current economic situation has to be understood by each and every worker. The workers agreed to implement several measures to attain higher levels of efficiency and productivity in the factory, and also proposed measures to confront the difficult economic situation in the nation.
