BFN [Text] Hangzhou, June 25 (XINHUA) -- Huaxia Township Enterprise Institute, the first college in China with share-holders, was inaugurated at the Zhejiang Agricultural University here today. The institute was set up jointly by the Township Enterprise Department of the Ministry of Agriculture, the Township Enterprise Bureau of the Zhejiang Provincial Government and Zhejiang Agricultural University. It is to train technical personnel for rural industrial enterprises. He Kang, former minister of agriculture, was elected honorary chairman of the board of the institute. Liu Xirong, vice-governor of Zhejiang Province, was elected honorary president. Xu Xingguan, vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of Zhejiang Provincial People's Congress, was elected chairman of the board.
