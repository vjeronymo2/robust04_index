June 22, 1989, Thursday, Valley P.M. Final

LOCAL;

N. HOLLYWOOD LOTTO TICKET A WINNER

A Lotto player who purchased a ticket in North Hollywood picked all six winning numbers in the midweek Lotto 6/49 drawing for the $4.9-million jackpot, state lottery officials said today.

In the five-of-six plus bonus category, there were four winners, each of whom will receive $339,682. Officials said the tickets were purchased in Long Beach, Fontana, Glendora and Santa Barbara. Players will be identified after they present their claims at lottery offices.

Sales for Wednesday's game were $12.7 million, spokeswoman Kathy Manzer said. The jackpot for Saturday's drawing is estimated at $5 million.
