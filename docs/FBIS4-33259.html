BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Paraguay monitored through 2 June. Yesterday, Presidential spokesman Guillermo Yalik told reporters that the inflation rate in May was 0.5 percent. Total inflation for the year now stands at 9.8 percent. (Asuncion ABC COLOR in Spanish 31 May 94 p 13) The Central Bank reports that total international reserves at the end of May stand at $901 million, and that the average interest the bank receives for its deposits is 3.8 percent. (Asuncion ABC COLOR in Spanish 31 May 94 p 13)
