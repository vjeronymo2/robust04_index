Business Development Center Applications: Boston, MA AGENCY: Minority Business Development Agency, Commerce. ACTION: Notice (Cancellation)

SUMMARY: The Minority Business Development Agency (MBDA) is cancelling the announcement to solicit competitive applications under its Minority Business Development Center (MBDC) Program to operate a Boston, Massachusetts MBDC for a three (3) year period, starting December 1, 1994 to November 30, 1995 (closing date, August 25, 1994).
