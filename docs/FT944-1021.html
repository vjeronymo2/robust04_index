941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Stewart & Wight Stewart & Wight, the property investment group, reported pre-tax profits down from Pounds 170,064 to Pounds 162,192 for the six months to September 30. Gross rental income rose to Pounds 214,700 (Pounds 201,528) and interest paid was Pounds 12,993 (Pounds 6,846). Earnings per share were 137.31p (145.29p).
