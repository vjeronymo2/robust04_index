930614 FT 14 JUN 93 / Construction Contracts: US highway EVERED BARDON has been awarded an USDollars 8.9m (Pounds 5.77m) contract to resurface an eight-mile section of Interstate 95 in Massachusetts. Bardon Trimount will be prime contractor on the project, for which it will be supplying 140,000 tons of coated stone from its nearby coating plants.
