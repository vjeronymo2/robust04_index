Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Four deputies of the Democratic Left Alliance [SLD] who did not adhere to the whip applied by this parliamentary club in the vote on the budget [today] and voted against the law have been deprived of their membership in the club. These deputies are: Piotr Ikonowicz, Andrzej Lipski, Cezary Mizejewski, and Sranislaw Wisniewski [sitting, respectively, for the Lublin, Warsaw, Warsaw, and Warsaw multimember constituencies]. There are currently 167 deputies in the Democratic Left Alliance [SLD] parliamentary club in the Sejm.
