November 6, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Lost and Found: It's been musical chairs -- and directors and actors -- at the Matrix Theatre, where Saturday night's opening of Constance Congdon's much-anticipated "Tales of the Lost Formicans" was postponed for the second time. Gone with the elaborate set and rear-screen projection effects are actress Bette Ford and director Michael Arabian. K Callan is in for Ford, Kristoffer Siegel-Tabori and Lee Shallat are the new directors. The opening has been re-scheduled for Sunday. Claudia Puig and Aleene MacMinn
