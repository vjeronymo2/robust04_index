940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Net assets increase at Brit American Film At December 31 1993 net asset value per share, excluding film rights, at British & American Film Holdings had risen to Pounds 11.81p, against Pounds 10.23p at the end of 1992. The company said that by April 29 net asset value had risen to Pounds 12.01p. Pre-tax profits of the investment company jumped to Pounds 1.2m (Pounds 380,000 restated under FRS 3). Films and videos revenue fell to Pounds 79,000 (Pounds 144,000), but investment income increased to Pounds 1.43m (Pounds 1.31m). Other income contributed Pounds 4,500 this time, but interest receivable fell to Pounds 10,000 (Pounds 35,500). The loss on the sale of investments was reduced to Pounds 51,000, against Pounds 870,000. The final dividend is raised to 9.3p, which makes an improved total of 13.575p (12.05p). After tax of Pounds 397,000 (Pounds 219,000) earnings per share advanced from 5.75p to 28.78p.
