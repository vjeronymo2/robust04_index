BFN [Article signed RHR/MPN: "Enlargement: Dehaene Welcomes European Parliament's Approval"] [Text] Brussels, 4 May (BELGA) -- Prime Minister Jean-Luc Dehaene immediately expressed his satisfaction following the European Parliament's (EP) approval, on Wednesday [4 May] afternoon, of the Accession Treaties to the European Union (EU) for Sweden, Finland, Austria, and Norway. "This approval marks a major step along the road to rapid and effective EU membership for these countries. By the vote of the representatives directly elected by European citizens, the EP has thus shown that the Union is effectively opening up to such an enlargement. In so doing, Europe is giving a very clear signal to the citizens of Sweden, Finland, Austria, and Norway, confirming that they are welcome within the Union," the Belgian leader stated in a communique. "The Belgian Government, which had made EU enlargement one of the priorities of its presidency, would like to thank the EP for having voted on enlargement at this opportune moment."
