BFN [Statement issued by "a responsible source" at the Interior Ministry; place and date not given] [Text] In the light of compliance with the cease-fire decision, and out of concern to allow the brother citizens in Aden to move freely and safely to other parts and governorates of the Republic, the Interior Ministry informs all the brother citizens in Aden that they can travel to any area of the Republic they wish, including travel to and from 'Adan al-Sughra [a suburb of Aden]. The Interior Ministry, in cooperation with the Armed Forces, will provide all facilities to the brother citizens during their travel to and from Aden.
