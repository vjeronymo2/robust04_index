940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / International Company News: Wesfarmers boosts interim profit 43% Wesfarmers, the Perth-based conglomerate, posted a 43 per cent rise in net profit to ADollars 37.4m (USDollars 26.8m) in the six months to December 31, Reuter reports from Perth. The company said improved performances were recorded in rural trading, insurance, fertilisers, chemicals, hardware and forest products as well as dairy and transport activities. However, it said energy and retail operations were flat.
