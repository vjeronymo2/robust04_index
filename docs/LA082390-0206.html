August 23, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: NEWPORT BEACH;

CANCER CENTER OFFERS FREE COMEDY SHOW

Cancer patients and their families are invited to attend a free afternoon of comedy this Saturday at Hoag Hospital's Grace Hoag Conference Center.

The event, sponsored by Hoag Cancer Center and the Irvine Improvisation, will feature comedian Pat Hazell performing from 4:30 to 5:30 p.m. For reservations call (714) 760-5542.
