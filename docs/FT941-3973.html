940314 FT 14 MAR 94 / Where the profits are --------------------------------------------------------------------- WHERE THE PROFITS ARE --------------------------------------------------------------------- (North-south divide is disclosed by percentages of businesses making profits) --------------------------------------------------------------------- A Dun & Bradstreet regional survey confirms that the south fared worse than the north in the recession.
