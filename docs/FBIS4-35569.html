BFN [Text] Yoram Lizovsky, chairman of the Israel Manufacturers Association, has divulged that PLO political elements are demanding that foreign companies wanting to maintain business relations with the Palestinians refrain from cooperating with Israel. Mr. Lizovsky also was disappointed and concerned about the decision of PLO political elements not to involve Israeli companies in investments in the autonomous area. Our correspondent Yafa Glick reports that Mr. Lizovsky, addressing the Ra'ananna Economic Club, noted that the only benefit that the Israeli economy is reaping from the political process is an increase in the number of foreign economic delegations coming to Israel.
