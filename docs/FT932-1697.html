930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / Turks held over Paris shoot-out French police have arrested three suspected members of the Turkish extreme left-wing political group, Devsol, after a shoot-out in central Paris in which one man was was killed, a magistrate said yesterday, Reuter reports from Paris. The three were arrested on Saturday following the shooting on June 15 involving up to 15 gunmen from rival Devsol factions. They have been placed under investigation on several serious charges including the murder, the authorities said.
