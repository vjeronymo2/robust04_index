940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / Pounds 1.5m libel award against magazine An award-winning boat designer, his wife and their company yesterday won Pounds 1.485m libel damages in the High Court over a trade magazine article and editorial comment on their revolutionary Planesail trimaran design. The amount is the second highest jury award in England after the Pounds 1.5m awarded to Lord Aldington in 1989 over allegations about his war conduct. Mr John Walker, his wife Jean and Walker Wingsail Systems, based in Plymouth, Devon, sued Yachting World, published by IPC Magazines. The magazine, which also faces costs, denied libel and has said it will appeal.
