920204 FT 04 FEB 92 / Dan-Air to launch four new services DAN-AIR, the independent airline, yesterday announced it will start scheduled services to Stockholm, Athens, Rome and Barcelona. These are the first new routes since Davies and Newman, Dan-Air's parent company, was refloated on the stock market in October. The group nearly went into liquidation in 1990. It intends services to Istanbul and Cairo when regulatory approval is given.
