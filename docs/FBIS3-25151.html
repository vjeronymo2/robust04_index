Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The Freedom Alliance says its leaders are unable to make further concessions to reopen the constitutional negotiations with the government and the ANC [African National Congress]. Our political staff reports that it is now almost certain that the group will not participate in the election. [passage omitted] The government's chief negotiator, Mr. Roelf Meyer, said the Alliance was making a big mistake in withdrawing from the negotiations. He said progress had been made in the past two weeks with the ANC and the government having made concessions. He said parties which did not take part in the election were minimizing their political roles. Mr. Meyer invited individual parties in the Alliance to take part in more negotiations.
