930619 FT 19 JUN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices The market was fully steady for both black and white, at origins as well as in importing countries, reports Man Producten. In the European white market Muntok, spot, was fetching Dollars 1,950 a tonne, with June/July at Dollars 1,850 and July/Aug Dollars 1,865. No white pepper offers were available from Sarawak, China and Brazil. Among the black peppers Sarawak black label was unquoted on the spot market but June/July was offered at Dollars 1,215 a tonne. Neither was there a spot quotation for Sarawak yellow label. June/July was at Dollars 1,265 a tonne. Brazil grade 1, spot, was quoted at Dollars 1,225 a tonne, with June/July at Dollars 1,150.
