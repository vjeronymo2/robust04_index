Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Piranshahr, West Azarbaijan, Jan. 12, IRNA -- Members of the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan (PUK) operating in Iraq's Kurdistan last Monday attacked a military base of a Naqshbandi group in Haj Omran, northeast of Iraq, an Iraqi Kurd said here today. The same source told IRNA here that right before the attack the group's leader Sheikh Mostafa Naqshbandi was entertaining PUK members. The source added that PUK members confiscated the arms at the disposal of the Naqshbandi group. Although the PUK has denied any involvement in the incident, sources close to Jalal Talebani have disclosed that the Naqshbandi group was attacked because the group was an Islamic one. The self-styled parliament of the Iraqi Kurdistan had passed a law earlier for public disarmament. Observers feel that the incident is a prelude to new inter-group clashes.
