930107 FT 07 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Cassidy Bros rises 9% to Pounds 716,000 Cassidy Brothers, the USM-quoted toys and nursery goods manufacturer, achieved a near 9 per cent improvement in pre-tax profits to Pounds 716,055 for the half year to October 31. Directors said the increase was achieved by holding down costs in addition to investment of over Pounds 400,000 on non-recurring capital projects to further strengthen infrastructure and increase production efficiency. Turnover rose from Pounds 4.07m to Pounds 4.35m. Earnings emerged 9.02p (8.39p) and the interim dividend is held at 0.75p.
