BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Albert Kochetkov] [Text] Tbilisi, 12 Apr--Eduard Shevardnadze told the Georgian Information Agency today that NATO air strikes against Serb positions in Bosnia, "especially when backed by the undertakings and peacemaking efforts of the international community, are a serious warning to militant nationalism and separatism." He said, however, that it was necessary "to observe the tradition of holding political consultations in such cases, especially when it concerns UN Security Council member states.
