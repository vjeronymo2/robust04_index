Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [From the "Diplomatic Report" column by Luiz Orlando Carneiro] [Text] On 3 February, Portuguese President Mario Soares and the Portuguese Assembly of the Republic ratified the Extradition and Mutual Aid in Penal Matters treaties that were signed by the two countries. The Extradition Treaty simplifies the formalities for the extradition of persons who have been sentenced to prison terms of more than one year in either country. The Mutual Aid Treaty establishes cooperation rules between the two countries' judicial authorities.
