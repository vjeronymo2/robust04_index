920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables FRUIT & VEGETABLES Spania plums are excellent this week at 40-60p a lb (60-80p). Bananas at 45-55p a lb (45-55p), English raspberries at Pounds 1.00-1.20 a lb (Pounds 1.30-1.50) and pineapples at 80p-Pounds 1.50 (80p-Pounds 1.50) each, are all good buys. There is a bumper summer-crop of English cauliflower in the shops this week at 30-40p a head (50-60p). Homegrown new potatoes at 10-15p a lb (10-15p), broad beans at 25-35p a lb (30-40p) and carrots at 12-20p a lb (12-15p) are all in peak season. English and Dutch peppers are this week's best salad buy at 70p-Pounds 1.00 (95p-Pounds 1.10). All types of lettuce are plentiful with English Iceberg at 55-65p a head (45-60p).
