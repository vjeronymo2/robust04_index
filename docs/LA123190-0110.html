December 31, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

SPOTLIGHT / FOCUS: FOOTHILL BUSINESS PARK: A WEEKLY LOOK AT OFFICE AND INDUSTRIAL REAL ESTATE IN THE COUNTY.

Foothill Business Park is a 74,765-square-foot development in the 700-acre Baker Ranch in El Toro, next to Irvine Spectrum. It is the second completed project in South Orange County for Newport Beach-based Hillman Properties, which recently opened the neighboring 130,000-square-foot Vista Terrace Business Park.
