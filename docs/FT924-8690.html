921110 FT 10 NOV 92 / Business and the Law: Fraud in firms - Legal Briefs Touche has been offering advice on how to tackle the growing problem of fraud by solicitors. Among its suggestions to the Law Society are: each firm to maintain a specific level of partner's capital; compulsory review of a firm's management systems before it is allowed to hold clients' money; and partners to confirm compliance with accountants' reports rules.
