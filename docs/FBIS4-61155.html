BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan, 3 May -- Heavy fighting is continuing on the northern and northeastern sectors of the Karabakh front, an ITAR-TASS correspondent has been told by the Karabakh information and press department. The main incidents are located in the direction of Mardakert and Agdam, where each of the sides is trying to take the initiative, Stepanakert stressed. According to official sources in the capital of the republic of Nagorno-Karabakh, "Karabakh forces are cutting off every attempt by the Azerbaijani side to take control of the situation." Judging from reports coming in, both sides are sustaining losses.
