940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: EBC falls to Pounds 0.36m A fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 562,000 to Pounds 356,000 was announced by EBC Group, the construction and development concern, for the half year to June 30. Turnover was little changed at Pounds 28.2m (Pounds 28.4m) but operating profits dropped to Pounds 294,000 (Pounds 452,000). Earnings per share slipped to 1.8p (3.03p) but the interim dividend is being held at 1.75p.
