Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 19 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, sent thanks to the soldiers of the Yo Chong-ku, Chang Song-chun, Kim Son-tu, Han Chae-son and Kang Sang-sop units of the Korean People's Army who had set examples in assisting the countryside. Commanding officers and soldiers of the units have contributed to the impelementation of the party's agriculture-first policy by sending large quantities of aid materials to cooperative farms and transporting their goods this year and deepened the feelings of army-people unity, looking after the life of the families of fallen soldiers and servicemen.
