Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 25 Feb (MENA) -- Zuhdi al-Qidrah, the Fatah representative in Cairo, has condemned the shooting early this morning of Palestinians praying at Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron City in the occupied West Bank. He considers this shooting incident a violation of the spirit of peace that we are working to realize in the region. In a statement to MENA today, the Palestinian official asked the Israeli Government to stop these acts the Israeli settlers are committing. He affirmed that these acts compel the international community to shoulder its responsibility in the march to peace by providing the Palestinian people inside the occupied territories with international protection until Israel withdraws from the Palestinian territories.
