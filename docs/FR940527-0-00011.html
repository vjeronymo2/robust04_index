The General Counsel shall furnish necessary advice to Corporation officials and staff as to their obligations under this part and shall take such other actions as may be necessary or appropriate to assure a consistent and equitable application of the provisions of this part by and within the Corporation. The Freedom of Information Officer, with concurrence from the appropriate program officials of the Corporation, is authorized to grant or deny requests under this part.
