940806 FT 06 AUG 94 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Sao Paulo stocks rallied as rumours suggesting weekend news reports will damage the campaign of leftist Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva increased confidence that Senator Fernando Henrique Cardoso will be elected president. The Bovespa index gained 1,146 points, or 2.6 per cent, to 44,695. Traded volume was RDollars 199m. Stock prices ended the week 6.3 per cent higher.
