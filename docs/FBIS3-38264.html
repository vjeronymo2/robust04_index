Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "New `National Unity' Party Announced"] [Text] Rome, 28 Jan (ANSA) -- Yet another party is about to enter the increasingly crowded "moderate" area of the Italian political spectrum in the run-up to the March 27 elections. A movement calling itself National Unity on Friday [28 January] announced it will form an official party, grouping a wide array of moderate forces with the aim of "leading Italy out of the deep crisis in which it has sunk" through a "constituent legislature and executive." The new party, its aims and its programs will be unveiled at a press conference Thursday, February 3 at the Rome press office in Piazza San Silvestro, a communique announced, adding that National Unity wants to help create an alliance between "political and cultural forces" from different backgrounds to give Italy a "modern political system truly based on alternatives, more freedom, more democracy and wider justice."
