Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] This morning, New York time, Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz met with UN Security Council President Jean-Bernard Merimee, the French ambassador to the United Nations. After a one-hour and fifteen minutes meeting, Tariq 'Aziz said his meeting with the UN Security Council president was very positive and useful, and that it starts the consultations he will hold with UN Security Council members during his visit to the United Nations. The deputy prime minister told journalists that he handed the UN Security Council president a message signed by him. Later tonight, Tariq 'Aziz will meet with British Ambassador David Hannay at the deputy prime minister's place of residence.
