Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev, February 28 (XINHUA) -- Ukraine said today that it is concerned over the downing of four aircraft in Bosnia by fighters of the North Atlantic Treaty Organization. A statement issued by the Foreign Ministry said that Ukraine will follow closely developments in Bosnia and consult with parties concerned on the issue. At this stage, all sides should join their efforts to seek a political solution to the conflicts, prevent occurrence of any provocative action and unconditionally comply with the relevant UN Security Council resolutions, the statement said.
