September 18, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

DUCKS' BERRY TO BE OUT 3-4 WEEKS

Oregon fullback Latin Berry, who suffered a knee injury in the Ducks' 44-6 victory Saturday over Iowa, will be sidelined three or four weeks, a spokesman for Oregon said.

Berry was believed to have suffered a partial ligament tear in his left knee but will not undergo surgery.

Center Scot Boatright tore a left knee ligament against Iowa. The severity of the injury was not immediately determined.

Oregon plays Stanford in a Pacific 10 conference game Saturday.
