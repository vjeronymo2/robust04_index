March 5, 1990, Monday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: PLATING COMPANY FIRE CAUSES $50,000 DAMAGE

A fire at a Burbank plating company caused $50,000 damage Sunday, authorities said.

Four fire companies responded to the 9:26 a.m. blaze at A-H Plating in the 1800 block of Victory Place, said Jean Osmond, a dispatcher for the Burbank Fire Department. It was contained within 20 minutes, she said.
