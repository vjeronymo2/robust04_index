Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Good Afternoon From Mexico" newscast] [Text] The situation in the State of Chiapas is as follows: The Mexican Army currently controls the city of San Cristobal de Las Casas. Clashes that took place last night in the municipality of Ocosingo resulted in 27 guerrillas and two soldiers dead. The city of Altamirano remains under EZLN [Zapatista National Liberation Army] control while Army aircraft continue to overfly the region. According to the latest reports, the community of (Wisan) remains under guerrillas control. In addition, the rebels withdrew from (Otchuc) without Army intervention. At least 2,000 additional soldiers arrived in Tuxtla Gutierrez which was already under control by the Army. In Las Margaritas, the Army entered to retake the city, but it had already been abandoned by the guerrillas. The community of Chanal was already abandoned voluntarily by the EZLN. In concluding, the guerrillas announced they had mobilized in Venustiano Carranza and were present in other communities located along the Chiapas mountain range and the Guatemalan border.
