Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 4 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to Liamine Zeroual upon his election as president of the Algerian Democratic and People's Republic. President Kim Il-song sincerely wished the Algerian president success in his work for the stability and prosperity of the country.
