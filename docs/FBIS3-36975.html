Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [POSTFACTUM report: "Russian Federation Foreign Ministry on Collaboration With State Duma"] [Text] At the request of chairmen of the State Duma committees on international affairs, CIS affairs, and relations with compatriots, the Foreign Ministry has prepared a list of international treaties and agreements that are subject to ratification by parliament. Marked in the list are treaties and agreements that, according to the Foreign Ministry, should be ratified on a priority basis. These are documents that regulate Russia's relations with a number of states. Some documents included in the list deal with disarmament questions, confidence-building measures, and Russia's accession to a number of conventions and protocols.
