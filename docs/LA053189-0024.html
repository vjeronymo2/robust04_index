May 31, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV/VIDEO

Actress Kelly McGillis is working hard to increase awareness on the subject of rape. McGillis, a rape victim herself, recently hosted "Against Her Will," a documentary on the Lifetime cable network about rape on college campuses. "The fact that so many men (1-in-13) admitted to forcing women into sex was amazing to me," she says. "I thought it was incredibly high. The number of women who had been forced into sex and didn't know it (73%) at the time was amazing too." McGillis also recently worked with a Santa Monica rape crisis center to make a video that will be distributed on college campuses, and she and her husband are working on another video for victims of violent crimes. ALEENE MacMINN
