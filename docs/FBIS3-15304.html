Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ivan Plyushch, chairman of Ukraine's Supreme Council, today received Dr. Alexander Arnot, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the Federal Republic of Germany to Ukraine, at the latter's request. A frank and constructive conversation took place, the main subjects of which were Ukraine's further steps along the path of nuclear disarmament and the upcoming elections. Ivan Stepanovych Plyushch also today met Luis Mareno, ambassador of the Commission of the European Communities to Ukraine. The two discussed ways of strengthening relations between our state and the European Union.
