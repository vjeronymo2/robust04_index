BFN [Text] Beijing, May 31 (XINHUA) -- Ren Jianxin, president of the Chinese Supreme People's Court, met with Lamine Sidime, president of the Guinean Supreme Court, and his party and hosted a dinner in their honor here this evening. Sidime and his party arrived here today as guests of the Chinese Supreme People's Court. They are expected to visit Shanghai and Guangzhou besides Beijing.
