Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The persistent aggressive language used by some executive branch members against Congress reveals a lack of understanding of the process we are undergoing. This was stated by [National Encounter] Senator Diego Abente, who thus referred to the defense minister's comment that the president of the Republic will continue to make "abusive" use of his right to veto laws. Sen. Abente said: "The veto is a constitutional tool that must be used moderately and with prudence. It may not be used only when the executive branch deems a draft bill unconstitutional." Throughout history, this tool has been used in the world when the executive branch and congress' interpretations differ on how to solve a specific problem. [passage omitted]
