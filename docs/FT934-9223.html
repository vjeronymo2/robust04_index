931111 FT 11 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Glynwed makes Pounds 1.5m German buy Glynwed International, via its plastics division's Italian subsidiary, Formatura Iniezione Polimeri, has acquired a substantial part of the assets of Sed Armaturen GMBH, of Bad Rappenau, Germany for DM3.78m cash (Pounds 1.5m). The new business has been renamed Sed Ventilsysteme. It employs 20 people, has an annual turnover of DM4.6m (Pounds 1.85m) and specialises in the development and manufacture of diaphragm valves and their automatic control systems.
