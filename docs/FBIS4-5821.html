BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 13 (KCNA) -- Talks between the delegations of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] and the October 8 Revolutionary Movement of Brazil were held here Tuesday. Present at the talks on the Korean side were Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the WPK Central Committee [C.C.], Kim Yang-kon, vice-director of a department of the C.C., WPK, and other officials concerned, and on the opposite side were members of the delegation led by Sergio Rubens, member of the Political Commission and secretary of the October 8 Revolutionary Movement of Brazil. At the talks the sides informed each other of the activities of their parties and exchanged views on a series of matters of common concern. The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.
