920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / Survey of Software at Work (8): Cap Sesa systems house - Supplier Profile Cap Sesa is the French company of Cap Gemini Sogeti, a French group formed in 1975 and now the biggest computing services company in Europe and the fourth biggest in the world. The group is represented in the UK by Hoskyns, which it bought from GEC Plessey in 1990. Turnover: FFr10,000m in 1991-92; FFr9,175m in 1990-91. Net profit: FFr560m in 1991-92; FFr623m in 1990-91. Staff: 20,000 in 15 European countries and the US; 6,000 of these are in Cap Sesa. Services: consultancy; software development; system building or integration; facilities management (running part or all of a company's computing).
