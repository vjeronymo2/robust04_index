August 9, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

"thirtysomething," noted for occasionally flouting TV dramatic conventions with dream sequences and offbeat fantasy episodes, may break the mold again next season with a live episode, producers Marshall Herskovitz and Ed Zwick said Tuesday. Although they would not reveal the plot, Herskovitz said, "So much of what we do in our show is to interpret reality for people. The notion of doing something live . . . gives us certain opportunities you can't get on film." The episode would air live on the East Coast and be seen on tape delay here in the West. ALEENE MacMINN
