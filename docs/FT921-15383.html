920110 FT 10 JAN 92 / Cement group criticises EC probe Belgium's biggest cement company, CBR, yesterday criticised the European Commission's investigation into alleged price-rigging by European producers as showing a 'limited understanding' of the industry, writes Andrew Baxter. The Commission has written to 76 European cement producers and organisations claiming breaches of competition law in the 1980s. In line with EC rules, the producers have two months to provide explanations.
