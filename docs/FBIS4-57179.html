BFN [Text] The Ministry of Basic Industry's Electric Union has announced that the deficit in electricity will continue this week and will be similar to what it has been to date, but with a slight increase on 14 and 15 May because a unit from the Carlos Manuel de Cespedes Thermoelectric Plant in Cienfuegos will go off-line. The Electric Union also announced that the parts needed for repairs to the thermoelectric plants in order to stabilize generating capacity are continuing to enter the country. At the present time, the maintenance program is progressing well and the generating capacity is gradually being increased; the average deficit is 25 percent lower than last April's.
