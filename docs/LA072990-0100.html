July 29, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

ROSEANNE FALLOUT

I booed Roseanne Barr. I was one of the working women supposedly being honored at the stadium.

(I don't know too many women who don't work.)

She was just being herself, sports fans.

We are always hoping for heroes. What we always get is a promoter choosing our heroes, hoping to make a buck and humiliating and demeaning us with the tastelessness of a Roseanne Barr.

I hope the judgment by Tom Werner was a one-time excusable error. If not, look out Padre fans, last place could look good to us.

NOMA PINTO

San Diego
