BFN [Excerpt] Hakki Atun has been reelected leader of the Democratic Party [DP]. The first DP congress was held in Lefkosa [Nicosia] today. Atun, who was the only candidate for party leadership, was reelected unanimously at the congress which was held at the Ataturk Sports Palace today. In a statement after his reelection, Atun thanked the delegates for electing him to this honorable post which requires great responsibility. [passage omitted]
