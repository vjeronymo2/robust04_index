940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: DCS DCS Group, computer software supplier formerly known as Nesco Investments, reported pre-tax profits down from Pounds 121,000 to Pounds 41,000 for the six months ended December 31, reflecting a big increase in administrative costs. Gross profit showed a 16 per cent rise to Pounds 1.4m (Pounds 1.21m) despite a fall in turnover from Pounds 2.9m to Pounds 2.6m, resulting from a realignment in product mix, with a greater proportion being in high margin software. Earnings per share were 0.61p (1.36p).
