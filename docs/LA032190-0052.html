March 21, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

THE RED ROLLS WINS AT GULFSTREAM

The Red Rolls, the 3-5 favorite, rallied for a 1 1/2-length victory over Shuttleman Tuesday to win the $24,000 For Halo Purse at Gulfstream Park. The winner, ridden by Dave Penna, ran seven furlongs in 1:23 4/5 and paid $3.20. Mister Modesty finished third.
