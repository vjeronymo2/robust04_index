BFN [Text] The French Foreign Ministry has expressed its outrage at the announcement of another massacre in Rwanda -- 170 people, including many children, were kidnapped and killed in Kigali. It is urgent that, during the OAU summit in Tunis, the countries in the area do their utmost in order to persuade the belligerents in Rwanda to put an immediate end to the fighting, the Foreign Ministry said. It has also called on the reinforced UN mission to be deployed as soon as possible in order to intervene between the two sides.
