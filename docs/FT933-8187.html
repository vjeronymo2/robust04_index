930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / International Company News: Reinsurance unit no longer up for sale THE PRUDENTIAL Insurance Company of America said it was no longer actively seeking to sell its subsidiary, Prudential Reinsurance, in a private transaction, Reuter reports from Newark. 'Prudential Reinsurance is a very valuable business with a strong industry reputation,' Mr William Link, executive vice-president, in a statement. 'We are now exploring other options for maximising the company's value, including, not limited to, alternatives in the public market.' The company is the largest US broker-oriented reinsurance company with Dollars 797m in 1992 premiums. Prudential Reinsurance has assets of about Dollars 3.1bn.
