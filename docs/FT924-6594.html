921120 FT 20 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Forward Group jumps to Pounds 491,000 Forward Group, the USM-traded circuit board maker, staged a recovery, with pre-tax profits ahead from Pounds 203,000 to Pounds 491,000 in the half year to July 31. Turnover rose Pounds 1.1m to Pounds 5.77m and generated an operating profit of Pounds 597,000 (Pounds 316,000). Earnings per share expanded to 4.5p (1.9p) and the interim dividend is raised to 1.3p (1.1p).
