Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Speaking in the open Majles session today, speaker Nateq Nuri referred to the establishment of relations between Vatican and the Zionist regime and the Pope's recognition of the regime. Nateq Nuri said: this act and the approval of crimes committed by the regime occupying al-Quds [Jerusalem] was expected, but the silence of the Christian community on the subject was not. Referring to the Afghan civil war, Nuri said the recent events in Afghanistan reveal that Afghan leaders and ringleaders lack political maturity, which allows for foreign intervention and continued clashes.
