Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 17 Mar (TANJUG) -- The Serbian Parliament this evening elected a new republican government with Mirko Marjanovic as prime minister. Of the 224 deputies present, 128 voted for Marjanovic's cabinet, 77 were against, and 30 abstained. The government was elected in a public vote after a debate lasting three days in which 135 deputies took part. The new Serbian Government consists of the prime minister, five deputy prime ministers, 20 department ministers, and four ministers without portfolio.
