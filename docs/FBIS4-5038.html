BFN [Text] President Ramos has made additional appointments for the Armed Forces. Major General Orlando Soriano of the Philippine Army will lead the Southern Command. He will replace Lieutenant General Thelmo Cunanan, who is going to retire on 14 April. Brigadier Edgardo Batenga will be the new Northern Luzon Commander. He will replace Soriano. Batenga's replacement as 5th Infantry Division chief is Brigadier General Romeo (Padiernos). Nineteen officials, including General Lisandro Abadia, will officially retire this year.
