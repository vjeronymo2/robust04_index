930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / MMC gas review publication date set THE conclusions of the Monopolies and Mergers Commission reports on the gas industry will be published on August 17, the government announced yesterday. The commission might recommend breaking up British Gas, as a way to encourage competition in the market for household supply, or it might opt for the status quo. It has completed a year-long review of the industry and is due to deliver two reports by the end of the week. The reports, formally separate but closely linked, will go to Mr Michael Heseltine, trade and industry secretary, and Sir James McKinnon, director-general of Ofgas, the regulator. Ofgas, British Gas and the Department of Trade and Industry then have 14 days to delete any commercially sensitive information. Mr Cedric Brown, chief executive of British Gas, welcomed the reports: 'A long period of uncertainty is coming to an end.'
