920506 FT 06 MAY 92 / UK Company News: In brief EAST MIDLANDS Electricity has, through a subsidiary, acquired the fixed assets, certain ongoing contracts and the Robert Heyworth name from the receiver for a maximum of Pounds 1.1m cash. Heyworth designs, supplies and installs heating, ventilation and air conditioning systems. ***** HICKING PENTECOST has acquired Thompson Horseley Bridge Water Storage Business from Northern Engineering Industries, a subsidiary of Rolls-Royce. Consideration will be satisfied in cash and, depending on stock values at completion in May, will not exceed Pounds 500,000. In 1991 Thompson Horseley made operating profits of Pounds 145,000 on turnover of Pounds 2.8m. ***** I&S UK Smaller Companies Trust saw net asset value fall from 97.71p to 88.96p over the year to March 31. It had, however, risen to 94.13p at April 29. Earnings amounted to 4.03p (2p) per share. The dividend is held at 2p on increased capital. ***** IPECO has acquired Pulse for a maximum Pounds 565,000 cash. Pulse designs and makes power conversion and energy storage devices, mainly for the UK defence industry. ***** PARK FOOD Group: Park Food (Warrington), a wholly-owned subsidiary, has sold its freehold interest in a 7.66 acre industrial estate at Hawarden, Clwyd, to Kusie for Pounds 1.8m, of which Pounds 1m has been received in cash and added to group deposits. ***** RATHBONE BROTHERS is to seek approval to move to the Official List. It is anticipated that the shares will be dealt in with effect from June 8. A 1-for-1 scrip issue has also been recommended.
