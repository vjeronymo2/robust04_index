930712 FT 12 JUL 93 / International Company News: Woolworths An initial public offering of shares in Woolworths the Australian retailer has closed heavily oversubscribed, Reuter reports. Woolworths offered 1bn shares at ADollars 2.45 each. Investors applied for 2.67bn shares, more than 2 1/2 times the number available. Domestic institutions applied for 875m shares, but were allocated 153m. Overseas institutions requested 772m shares. They got 40m. Woolworths, which is not related to the US group Woolworth group, has allocated shares to 339,000 investors. That gives it the biggest shareholder base of any listed Australian company.
