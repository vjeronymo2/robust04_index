BFN [Text] The new service of Ukraine's border troops reported that a Coast Guard ship detained the Turkish schooner Bayrakhtar 20 miles from Zmiyinyy Island. The schooner was in Ukraine's exclusive sea economic zone with its tackle ready for fishing. The schooner was escorted to the frontier post in Kamyshova Bay in the city of Sevastopol to undergo full examination. In addition, a large number of fishing nets with radar beacons were discovered in the vicinity of Zmiyinyy Island. The sea border guards keep the entire Black Sea region under observation.
