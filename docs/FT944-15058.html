941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / Domestic gas prices 'to fall' Domestic consumers should see gas prices fall by between 8 per cent and 12 per cent once the market is fully deregulated, independent gas companies told MPs yesterday. At a hearing of the trade and industry committee managers of companies which hope to enter the gas market once British Gas' monopoly is ended said the average price cut across the country would be 10 per cent.
