920314 FT 14 MAR 92 / Former Liverpool leader interviewed Mr Keva Coombes, former leader of Liverpool City Council, was interviewed by fraud squad officers yesterday after police launched a new phase of an investigation into alleged council corruption. Merseyside police said Mr Coombes had not been arrested but had attended a police station of his own accord for interview. Two people in Merseyside and one in London were arrested yesterday in the fourth phase of Operation Cheetah, a long-running probe into alleged corrupt land deals. Mr Rex Makin, Mr Coombes' solicitor, said his client had not been charged or had any allegations put to him.
