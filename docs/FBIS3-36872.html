Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] A BELAPAN correspondent reported from the Republican Council of Strike Committees, preparations to stage a political strike are continuing. The strike will begin at 0900 GMT, on 15 February. The Council received information that the Belarusian Defense Ministry ordered military exercises for that same date. In this connection, the Interior Affairs Ministry forces were put on combat alert. The Council members believe that the Cabinet of Ministers is considering harsh actions against participants in the political strike.
