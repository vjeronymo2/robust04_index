940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Inchcape expands activities in China Inchcape, the motors, marketing and services company, is expanding in China through the acquisition of a motor distributor and the creation of a consumer products joint venture. For Dollars 8m (Pounds 5.3m) it is buying 60 per cent of Nanjing Hong Kong Changjiang. It is also taking an 80 per cent stake in Inchcape (Nanjing) Development.
