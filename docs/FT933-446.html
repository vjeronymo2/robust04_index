930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / The Labour Party Conference: Shadow defence minister resigns MR GEORGE FOULKES yest-erday resigned as shadow defence minister in a move that will deprive the Labour front bench of one of its most eloquent and irrepressible Commons performers, David Owen writes. The 51-year-old Carrick Cumnock and Doon Valley MP took the step before appearing in court where he admitted being drunk and disorderly and assaulting a policeman. Mr Foulkes was fined Pounds 1,050 with Pounds 500 costs and compensation by London's Bow Street magistrates' court. Mr John Smith, the Labour leader, said yesterday he had accepted Mr Foulkes' resignation 'with great regret'.
