940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: JO Walker in loss Losses of Pounds 20,000 pre-tax were announced by JO Walker & Co, the importer of timber, wallboards and plywood, for the half year to June 30. There were profits of Pounds 162,000 last time. Turnover improved to Pounds 7.8m (Pounds 7.17m) and operating profits jumped to Pounds 102,000 (Pounds 15,000). Losses per share were 2.5p (20.2p earnings).
