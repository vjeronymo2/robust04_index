BFN [Text] The new Bosnia contact group -- which is designed to put political "punch" into the quest for a peace settlement -- is to visit the battle zones soon for urgent discussions with the warring parties. This was decided at the first meeting of the group at the Foreign Office in London today. It comprises representatives of Russia, the United States, the European Union [EU] and the United Nations. After the meeting a spokesman said: "They agreed their priority was to try to restore the momentum of the peace process. They will explore urgently the prospects for a cessation of hostilities and engaging the parties in negotiations about a settlement. ``Their work will be conducted within the framework given by the EU and UN and the group will travel to Bosnia shortly for discussions with the parties,'' the spokesman added.
