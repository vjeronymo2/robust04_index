May 16, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY EARNINGS: SIERRACIN CORP. EARNS $475,000 IN 1ST QUARTER; UP BY 10%

Sierracin Corp. earned $475,000 in the first quarter ended March 31, up 10% from a year earlier. The Sylmar company, which makes such aerospace products as cockpit windows, said its revenue grew 7% from the year-earlier quarter, to $20.6 million.
