BFN [Text] Moscow, June 3 (YONHAP) -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin will visit South Korea in the near future, President Kim Yong-sam said Friday [3 June]. Kim told South Korean correspondents here that Yeltsin had accepted his invitation to visit Seoul and expected that it would take place soon. Considering that Yeltsin's term of office expires in June 1996, he is expected to visit South Korea next year, a senior member of Kim's entourage said.
