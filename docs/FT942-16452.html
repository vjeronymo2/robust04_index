940409 FT 09 APR 94 / Czech secret agent is jailed Mr Vaclav Wallis, a former Czech secret service agent, was sentenced to 37 months in jail by a military court yesterday for selling confidential government information to a leading businessman, writes Patrick Blum from Vienna. Mr Wallis was arrested in December 1992 during a sting operation while passing information to and accepting Kcs100,000 from Mr Viktor Kozeny, head of Harvard Capital & Consulting (HC&C), who had informed the secret police.
