BFN [Text] Beijing, May 3 (XINHUA) -- Zhang Siqing, procurator-general of the Chinese Supreme People's Procuratorate, held working talks with Jamstiyn Choyjantsan, deputy procurator-general of the national procurators' office of Mongolia, here today. The two sides exchanged views on issues of common interest. The Mongolian visitors arrived here yesterday as guests of the Chinese Supreme People's Procuratorate. Apart from Beijing, they are also expected to travel to Shanghai and Hangzhou.
