Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Two people were killed and six wounded in the free part of Mostar in last night's large-caliber artillery attacks from Croatian Army and HVO [Croatian Defense Council] positions, the 4th Corps press center reports. Today the ustashas directed most of their attacks on Blagaj, targeting it with more than 100 destructive shells and causing fresh casualties and considerable material damage. This town, which is also inhabited by several thousand refugees, was targeted by the ustasha artillery for two hours. Fourteen people were wounded in these bestial attacks by crazed supporters of the vampire from Grude [reference to Herceg-Bosna president Mate Boban]. Despite the increasing aggressor attacks on the free part of the Mostar municipality, the demarcation lines have remain unchanged and firmly controlled by the 4th Corps units.
