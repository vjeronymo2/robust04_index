FEDERAL COMMUNICATIONS COMMISSION 47 CFR Part 73 [MM Docket No. 94&hyph;22; RM&hyph;8438] Radio Broadcasting Services; Jackson, LA AGENCY: Federal Communications Commission. ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: The Commission, at the request of Roosevelt Gremillion, allots Channel 283A to Jackson, Louisiana. See 59 FR 13919, March 24, 1994. Channel 283A can be allotted to Jackson, Louisiana, in compliance with the Commission's minimum distance separation requirements without the imposition of a site restriction. The coordinates for Channel 283A at Jackson are 30&hyph;50&hyph;18 and 91&hyph;13&hyph;00. With this action, this proceeding is terminated. DATES: Effective November 21, 1994. The window period for filing applications will open on November 21, 1994, and close on December 7, 1994. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Pamela Blumenthal, Mass Media Bureau (202) 634&hyph;6530.
