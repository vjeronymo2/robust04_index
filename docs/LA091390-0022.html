September 13, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

The Stuff of Nightmares: Imagine Jason of "Friday the 13th" fame, Dr. Alien and the director of "Darkman," plus other stars from gruesome horror and B-movies all in one room.

Tonight, these scary stars will gather for Blood Savage Horror Relief, a fund-raiser for the Celebrity Outreach Foundation which coordinates celebrity charity events. Celebs from "The Fearless Vampire Killers" and "Hollywood Chainsaw Hookers" will be on hand at L.A.'s Vertigo nightclub. Tickets are $25. BETH KLEID
