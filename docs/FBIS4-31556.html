BFN [Text] Tirana, June 9 (ATA) -- Yesterday's seance of the trial against the 10 former high communist functionaries marked the end of the phase of the judicial investigation. In the beginning of the seance, the chief judge, Andi Celiku, called witness Sulejman Abazi, taking into consideration the request made by the defence attorney of the defendant Zylyftar Ramizi. Witness Abazi testified on the platform of the operative work in the organs of the former Interior Ministry, and also on the incident in Qafa e Barit. The chief judge stated that the other witnesses, who have been called, have had healthy reasons or have been abroad. The jury decided that their testimonies be read in the Prosecutor's Office. Prosecutor Fana read the testimonies of 16 witnesses who did not come to this seance. The next seance will be on June 11.
