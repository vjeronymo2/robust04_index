Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Singapore -- The Singapore Malays National Organization (PKMS) condemned the Hebron massacre of Palestinians as an act of cowardice in a statement issued on Saturday. It said that the act was especially offensive as it was committed in a mosque. "We hope the United Nations will conduct a full investigation and deploy a peacekeeping force in order to preserve peace and protect the Palestinians who are vulnerable to armed Israeli settlers." The perpetrators should be tried, said the party in the statement signed by acting president Atan Rafie. The statement comes in the wake of internal divisions and power struggles within the party.
