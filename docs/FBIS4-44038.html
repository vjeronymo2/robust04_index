BFN [Text] A homemade explosive device went off at 0220 [2320 GMT on 23 June] today at the building housing the European Union [EU] offices in Athens, at the corner of Amalias Avenue and Xenofondos Street. The explosion caused minor damage. An unidentified man telephoned ELEVTHEROTIPIA newspaper 25 minutes earlier, warning of the bomb and claiming responsibility on behalf of the Revolutionary People's Struggle organization. The area was secured by police and there were no injuries.
