930115 FT 15 JAN 93 / International Company News: IBM rating downgraded by S&P IBM's senior debt credit rating has been downgraded by Standard & Poor's from triple-A to double-A-minus. About Dollars 19bn of rated debt is affected, writes Louise Kehoe in San Francisco. S&P said that the downgrade reflected the 'significantly lower-than-anticipated results' which were projected by the company last month. IBM announced that it would take Dollars 6bn in restructuring charges. IBM will report its year end results next week.
