BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Ivanova] [Text] Moscow June 15 TASS -- The parliamentary faction of the Party of Russian Unity and Accord [PRES] accepted the resignation of Konstantin Zatulin as the faction's secretary in the State Duma, the press service of the lower house of the Russian parliament reported today without divulging the reasons for the resignation. Zatulin retains the post of the chairman of the State Duma Committee for Commonwealth Affairs and Relations with compatriots abroad. Vladimir Kozhemiakin, who headed Sergey Shakhray secretariat before his election to the State Duma, will replace Zatulin as secretary of the PRES faction in parliament.
