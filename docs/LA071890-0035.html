July 18, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Hey Dudes, Turtles to Strike Again: The four Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles will bust out of the sewers and onto the movie screens again.

New Line Cinema and Golden Harvest, who joined together for "Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles," announced they will team up again for the sequel. The tentative release date is March 15, 1991, which coincides with students' spring break. It will have an estimated budget of $20 million. The first installment of the bodacious turtles' adventures, which was released March 30, has grossed more than $128 million. BETH KLEID
