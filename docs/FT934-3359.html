931209 FT 09 DEC 93 / Report says five per cent of employers use teleworkers Five per cent of employers use teleworkers, and half of those employ some form of home-based worker, says a Department of Employment report on 1,000 organisations, Lisa Wood writes. The report says the idea that teleworking is spreading steadily is 'open to doubt'. It says: 'There is a shifting population of schemes which come and go in response to changing individual and corporate requirements.'
