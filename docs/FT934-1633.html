931217 FT 17 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Bradstock rises 3% to Pounds 7.65m Bradstock Group, the insurance broker, yesterday reported pre-tax profits up 3 per cent to Pounds 7.65m in the year to September 30. Turnover increased by 11 per cent to Pounds 27.1m. Earnings per share rose to 8.9p (8.5p) and a recommended final dividend of 3.95p, gives an 8 per cent rise to 5.5p (5.1p). Direct insurance brokerage income increased to Pounds 17.9m (Pounds 12.8m) while reinsurance broking fell to Pounds 9.22m (Pounds 11.6m). Pre-tax profits on ordinary activities were Pounds 6.65m (Pounds 3.69m) on direct insurance and Pounds 996,000 (Pounds 3.74m) on reinsurance. Investment and other income was lower at Pounds 2.79m (Pounds 3.34m). Administrative expenses amounted to Pounds 22.3m (Pounds 20.3m). Profits included Pounds 27,000 in income from associated undertakings, compared with Pounds 5,000 last year.
