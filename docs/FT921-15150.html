920111 FT 11 JAN 92 / Steel production falls by 7.6% UK STEEL output fell 7.6 per cent last year from 17.84m tonnes to 16.48m, according to British Steel and the British Independent Steel Producers' Association. The decline underlines the severity of the recession in the industry and left output last year down 13 per cent from the 18.95m tonnes produced in 1988. That was the best year for production since 1979.
