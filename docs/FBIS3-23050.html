Language: Tagalog Article Type:BFN [Text] The Moro National Liberation Front [MNLF] is not involved in kidnappings, bombings, and other terrorist activities in Mindanao. This statement came from Nur Misuari himself, MNLF chief, during an interview with our GMA correspondent inside the MNLF camp in Indanan, Sulu. Prior to his departure for Tripoli, Libya, Misuari hoped that the long cherished peace agreement between the government and MNLF will be achieved. [Begin Misuari recording in English] We are telling the people that it is not to the interest of the MNLF, nor to the interest of peace to create any unnecessary disruption to the peace process.
