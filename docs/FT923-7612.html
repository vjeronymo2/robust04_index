920817 FT 17 AUG 92 / Construction Contracts: New shopping centre for Edinburgh BOVIS CONSTRUCTION (SCOTLAND), a P&O company, has been appointed project manager for the building of the Pounds 34m Gyle Shopping Centre in Edinburgh. The development is being jointly undertaken by Edinburgh District Council, Gazeley Properties, and Marks & Spencer. It will provide 300,000 sq ft of shopping at a key location on the west side of the city. The anchor stores will be Marks & Spencer and Asda, with a further 60 units in the shopping mall, together with a food court. The external car parks will accommodate 2,625 cars and a dedicated petrol station will also be provided as part of the overall development. Work has already commenced on site and along with the completion of the Gogar underpass and road improvements, the centre is scheduled to be operational by Christmas 1993.
