930907 FT 07 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Castle Mill debt restructuring Castle Mill International, the loss-making clothing and household textiles company, has negotiated a financial restructuring package. The company's shares shed 1p to 6 3/4 p yesterday. The proposals entail satisfying Pounds 1.5m of bank debt by issuing 6.31m ordinary shares and restructuring the repayment timetable for the balance, and the settlement of three items of litigation by cash repayments.
