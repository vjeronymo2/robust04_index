Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, Jan 29 (A.A) -- Four terrorists from the outlawed separatist terrorist organization were killed and 24 separatists were arrested in the southeast on Saturday [29 January]. The Emergency Regional Governor's Office told AA that the terrorists were killed in a clash with security forces in Genc township in Bingol and in Malazgirt township in Mus Province. Twenty-four separatists who were responsible for aiding and abetting and providing logistic support to the terrorist organization were arrested in Adiyaman, Diyarbakir, Mardin, Sirnak and Van Provinces. Meanwhile, three terrorists from the same organization surrendered to security forces in Silopi township in southeastern Sirnak Province.
