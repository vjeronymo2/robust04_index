920205 FT 05 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Losses mount at Albrighton The recession in the UK construction industry pushed Albrighton, a building products manufacturer, deeper into loss in the half year ended September 30, with its problems compounded by falling order books, squeezed margins and mounting interest charges. Operating profit fell from Pounds 1.08m to Pounds 430,000 on turnover down from Pounds 12.7m to Pounds 6.85m; after interest charges of Pounds 426,000 (Pounds 355,000) the company turned in a loss of Pounds 347,000 (Pounds 257,000) before tax. Losses per share came out at 5.5p (2.9p).
