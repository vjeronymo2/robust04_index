BFN [Text] The seven-member friendly delegation of Shandong Province headed by Lu Maozeng, chairman of the provincial Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference committee and president of the provincial people's association for friendship with foreign countries, returned to Jinan on the afternoon of 13 May after successfully concluding their friendly visits to Australia and New Zealand. During their visits, the delegation was cordially received by the speaker of the New Zealand Parliament. The premier of South Australia held a talk with Chairman Lu Maozeng. The former premier of Australia took a special trip to meet all members of the provincial delegation in Sydney. At the same time, the provincial delegation also held discussions with South Australian authorities on projects to dam rivers for farmland irrigation, to introduce fine animal strains, and to exchange students and professors. It also signed a two-year agreement on cooperation and exchanges with the friendship association of South Australia.
