BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin yesterday sent PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat a letter in which he replies to the three messages sent by the PLO chairman over recent days. The Rabin response was sent to 'Arafat through a Palestinian mediator. Earlier this week the prime minister said 'Arafat's letters dealt with issues relating to the autonomy agreements and problems that have arisen in the territories. Qol Yisra'el's diplomatic correspondent says it appears the issues include the closure on the territories, and clashes in Hebron between Palestinians and Israeli soldiers and civilians.
