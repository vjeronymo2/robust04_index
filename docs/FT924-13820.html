921014 FT 14 OCT 92 / Old and new This 1911 Delahaye fire engine will feature in an auction of historic and classic vehicles on October 24. It is expected to sell for between Pounds 8,000 and Pounds 10,000. The Delahaye engine, pictured outside Soho Fire Station in London, is contrasted with a state-of-the-art Volvo Saxon fire engine. The Saxon costs Pounds 100,000 off the shelf and un-equipped. The sale - organised by auction house Sotheby's - will take place at the RAF Museum, at Hendon, north London.
