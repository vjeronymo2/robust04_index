BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 15 (KCNA) -- Secretary of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] Hwang Chang-yop Thursday met and conversed in a friendly atmosphere with Vice-president of the socialist Workers' Party of Romania Maria Lazar. On the same day, he met L. A. Golub, first secretary of the Khabarovsk territorial committee of the Communist Party [CP] of the Russian Federation, and a delegation of the Maritime Territorial Committee of the CP of the Russian Federation and a delegation of the Amur Regional Committee of the CP of the Russian Federation led by its First Secretary G. E. Gamzar, and conversed with them in a friendly atmosphere. Vice-director of a department of the C.C., the WPK Chi Chae-yong was on hand.
