930706 FT 06 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Abtrust New Dawn Undiluted net asset value per share of Abtrust New Dawn Investment Trust improved from 125.79p to 177.98p over the 12 months ended April 30. Attributable revenue amounted to Pounds 385,655 (Pounds 182,722), equal to basic earnings of 1.29p (0.61p) per share. The dividend for the year is lifted from 0.5p to 0.75p.
