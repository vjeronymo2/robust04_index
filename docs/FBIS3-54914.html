Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Law "On the Procedure of the Exit from and Entry into the Republic of Belarus of Citizens of the Republic of Belarus" comes into effect on 1 January 1994. It lifts all restrictions on the issuance of a general civil foreign travel passport. In order to obtain it, one will not have to produce private or service invitations at local visa and registration departments. A written application will be sufficient, and a passport will be issued in due course. Moreover, there will be no need in the new year for a separate permission entry [in the passport] stating the country and the time limits for going there. The new passport will be valid to go to any country in the world a number of times. This was the topic of a news conference held in Minsk with the participation of representatives of the Foreign Affairs Ministry Consular Directorate, the Internal Affairs Ministry Passport and Visa Service, and the republic's Border Troops Chief Directorate.
