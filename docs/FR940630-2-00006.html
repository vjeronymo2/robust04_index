BIPARTISAN COMMISSION ON ENTITLEMENT AND TAX REFORM Meeting Notice is hereby given, pursuant to the Public Law 92&hyph;463, that the Bipartisan Commission on Entitlement and Tax Reform will hold a meeting on Friday, July 15, 1994 from 1:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m in the Cannon House Office Building, Room 210, Washington, DC. The meeting of the Commission shall be open to public. The proposed agenda includes discussion on entitlement expenditure growth and its relation to long-term fiscal and budget policy. Records shall be kept of all Commission proceedings and shall be available for public inspection in room 825 of the Hart Senate Office Building, 120 Constitution Avenue, NE., Washington, DC 20510. J.
