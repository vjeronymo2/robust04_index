July 6, 1990, Friday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: ART

Cabinet Fetches $15.8 Million at Auction: The world's most expensive piece of furniture is an 18th-Century Italian Baroque cabinet that was sold Thursday to Barbara Johnson, widow of the heir to the Johnson & Johnson Pharmaceuticals fortune. Johnson, paid $15.8 million for the cabinet at an auction in London. GREG BRAXTON
