BFN [Text] A responsible source at the Armed Forces Command has told the Yemeni news agency in Aden that a few hours after the issuance of the resolution of the UN Security Council calling for an immediate cease-fire, a development welcomed by our country which declared that it will fully observe all its points and will welcome the dispatch of the investigating committee formed for this purpose, the forces of the ruling military regime in Sanaa today launched a number of surface-to-surface missiles on the capital, Aden. These missiles were intercepted by our air defenses. Fragments of one of the missiles fell on one of Aden's mosques where worshipers were engaged in dawn prayers. A number of them were seriously injured and were subsequently taken to the hospital for treatment. Later a number of Arab, foreign, and local correspondents gathered at the scene as eyewitnesses to this [word indistinct] incident.
