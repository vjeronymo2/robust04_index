BFN [Text] Cairo, 5 Apr (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa has stressed that a full Israeli withdrawal from the Golan would mean a peace agreement between the Syrian and Israeli sides could be reached. In a statement he made today, Musa added: I believe that everybody agrees on this. Asked if he thinks progress could be made on the Syrian-Israeli track following Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad's visit to Egypt that ended today, Musa said: Naturally, on the basis of land for peace. Minister Musa was asked if this means that the ball is now in the Israeli court, he said: As far as the Israeli withdrawal is concerned, the ball is indeed in the Israeli court now.
