930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / UK Company News: FT-SE Actuaries changes THE FT-SE Actuaries UK Indices committee announced yesterday that the following constituent changes to the UK Series of the FT-SE Actuaries Share Indices would be made on June 21. (Rankings by capitalisation are shown in brackets): - FT-SE 100. For inclusion: Wolseley (82); SG Warburg Group (84); British Aerospace (85); RMC Group (86). For exclusion: Fisons (125); Kwik Save (116); Lasmo (106); Southern Electric (104). FT-SE Mid 250 and FT-SE Actuaries 350. For inclusion: WPP Group (235); Templeton Emerging Markets (314); Travis Perkins (324). For exclusion: Huntingdon International Holdings (388); Dunedin Income Growth (372); Raine (370). Companies promoted from the FT-SE Mid 250 to the FT-SE 100 will be replaced in the FT-SE Mid 250 by those companies excluded from the FT-SE 100. Companies excluded from the FT-SE Mid 250/FT-SE Actuaries 350 will be included In the FT-SE SmallCap. Approved for inclusion in the FT-SE SmallCap from June 21 were the following new issues: Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Investment Trust; Merrydown; Motor World Group; OIS International Inspection; Pilot Investment Trust; Stonehill Holdings; Trio Holdings. The next quarterly review will be held on September 8.
