BFN [Text] The organizational committee of a new party has been established in Belarus -- the Belarusian Humanitarian Party. Kanstantsin Syalchonak, known to many of our listeners as an astropsychologist, has been elected chairman of the new party. Although the party has already held its constituent congress, its statute and program are still being examined by the Ministry of Justice. The party founders are convinced that they will contribute to the quick and effective improvement of our lives.
