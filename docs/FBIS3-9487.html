Language: Slovene Article Type:BFN [Text] In a letter he sent today, Prime Minister Janez Drnovsek informed the Social Democratic Party of Slovenia that he had sent the State Chamber his proposal on the replacement of Defense Minister Janez Jansa. Namely, the government commission investigating into the Smolnikar case has established that members of the Defense Ministry have overstepped their powers. Therefore, the defense minister must take responsibility for that activity. According to Drnovsek, permitting such activity would mean deviating from the rule of law. The prime minister suggests that the Social Democratic Party should continue participating in the government coalition.
