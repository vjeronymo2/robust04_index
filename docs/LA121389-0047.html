December 13, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

ROTATOR CUFF INJURIES

Question: Anyone who follows sports for any length of time soon becomes as interested in the hospital reports as in the statistics of the game. At some time or other, it seems that every professional ball player is going to have an injury, and one of the most popular ones today is the rotator cuff injury. Why do so many ball players in so many different sports have a problem with this?

Answer: There is some disagreement about whether rotator cuff injuries occur because of some congenital problem or simply result from overuse. The injury occurs in all athletes who repeatedly use an overhead arm motion: baseball players, football quarterbacks, tennis players and swimmers.
