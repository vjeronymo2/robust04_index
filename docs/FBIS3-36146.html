Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] The Interfax agency reports, quoting a well-informed source close to Anatoliy Chubays: In the course of Chubays's recent telephone conversation with Boris Yeltsin, the president reacted negatively to the possibility of bringing in academicians [Leonid] Abalkin, [Nikolay] Petrakov, [Stanislav] Shatalin and others to work on adjusting reforms. According to Interfax's information, these economists have recently drawn up a report with a negative assessment of the course of economic reforms pursued for the past two years, and concluding that it was necessary to draw up a new socio-economic strategy.
