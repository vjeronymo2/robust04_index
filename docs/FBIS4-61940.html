BFN [Text] Simferopol, 9 May -- An extended assembly of the coordinating council of the Congress of Ukrainians of Crimea has taken place. Representatives of the peninsula's democratic parties, civil organizations, and movements took part. They decided to form a parliamentary type representative body of the nationalist democratic forces of with a state orientation: the Ukrainian Civil Council of Crimea [Ukrayinska hromadyanska rada Krymu]. The newly formed organization aims to guarantee the inviolability of Ukraine's borders, the protection and strengthening of Ukrainian statehood in Crimea, and maintaining political stability peace and inter-ethnic accord on the peninsula. The participants of the assembly were motivated to form such a body by the deepening crisis of Crimea's bodies of power which, in the opinion of the autonomy's representatives of the national democrats, has recently acquired a dangerous characteristic.
