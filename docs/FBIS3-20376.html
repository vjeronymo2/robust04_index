Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Robert Lamb, the U.S. State Department special coordinator on the Cyprus issue, arrived in Cyprus this afternoon aiming to give a new push to the proximity talks on the confidence-building measures [CBM's]. In an arrival statement at Larnaca Airport, Lamb said that he is not bringing any new proposals on the CBM's, adding that he believes there are many ideas on the negotiating table. He said his goal is to see whether he can assist in bridging the current differences. In response to a question, Lamb said he did not come to Cyprus to put pressure on one or the other side.
