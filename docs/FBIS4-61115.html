BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Mainayev] [Text] Bishkek May 3 TASS -- An important result of the recent inter-Tajik negotiations is striving of their participants for a political dialogue as the only way to achieve national reconciliation, says a statement of the Kyrgyz Foreign Ministry published here today. "Kyrgyzstan is a close neighbour of Tajikistan, and it cannot remain indifferent to the Tajik situation," says the document. The statement reaffirmed the invariability of Bishkek policy on Tajikistan which is based on non-interference in internal affairs of the sovereign state, development of bilateral relations in accordance with universally recognized international norms, the U.N. Charter and Kyrgyz obligations as a member of the Commonwealth of Independent States.
