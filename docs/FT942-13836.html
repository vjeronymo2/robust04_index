940422 FT 22 APR 94 / International Company News: Asian Hotels plans Dollars 100m Colombo project Asian Hotels, a hotel and property developer and one of Sri Lanka's leading companies, is planning to invest USDollars 100m in a four-tower office and housing complex in Colombo. The project, the second largest private-sector investment in Sri Lanka, is seen as a vote of confidence in the island's economic future, especially as it comes at a time of political uncertainty. Asian Hotels is making the investment through Crescat Development, a subsidiary, and will fund it through a share issue, aimed principally at overseas shareholders of Asian Hotels.
