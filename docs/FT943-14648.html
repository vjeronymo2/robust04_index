940713 FT 13 JUL 94 / International Company News: Australian court blocks takeover The ADollars 501m (USDollars 366m) takeover bid by Rank Commercial, the New Zealand wholesaler, for Foodland Associated, the Australian grocery group, has been temporarily blocked by an Australian court, writes Bruce Jacques. The Federal Court yesterday granted an injunction blocking the bid for two months to allow a final hearing on the matter on July 29. The injunction was sought by the Trade Practices Commission, the Australian regulatory authority.
