BFN [Text] The Senate has issued a statement deeply deploring the sad events pitting brother against brother in Yemen. The Senate, the statement said, regrets the high toll of casualties and the scale of destruction caused to the infrastructure that was built thanks to the sacrifices of the brotherly Yemeni people as they marched toward unity, development, and construction. The Senate called on the Yemeni brothers to let wisdom and dialogue prevail and address the roots of their disagreements to preserve the country's unity and spare the lives of Yemenis so that they can pursue the process of unity and construction and Yemen can continue to play its key pan-Arab role.
