910503 FT 03 MAY 91 / Appointments: Kobe Steel moves Mr Reg Clark, financial general manager of Kobe Steel in Europe, has been appointed managing director of newly-established European venture capital and development company, LONDON OXFORD & KOBE DEVELOPMENT COMPANY. He is a former director of JP Morgan Securities in London. Chairman of the new subsidiary is Mr Hiroshi Gamada, president of Kobe Steel Europe. ***** McLARENS, loss adjusters, has appointed to the main board: Mr IV Muress, Mr C. Smith, Mr RR Thomson, and Mr M. Weatherhead. ***** Mr Paul D. Taylor has been appointed president of LEEDS & HOLBECK BUILDING SOCIETY. He is a partner with Touche Ross & Co, and chairman of Wagon Industrial Holdings and a director of John Foster & Son. Mr Edward Holroyd becomes vice president. He is chairman of Holroyd Construction Group, Holderness Energy Group, and Atalanta Engineering.
