August 19, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

HAIRCUT-A-THON TO BENEFIT NONPROFIT AIDS AGENCY

Project Ahead will hold its second annual Haircut-a-Thon on Sept. 16 at a number of participating salons.

Individuals can donate by getting a haircut at one of the participating salons. All proceeds will go to Project Ahead, a nonprofit social service agency dedicated to case management and the well-being of people with AIDS and AIDS Related Complex (ARC) in the Long Beach area. For more information and a list of the participating salons, contact Project Ahead at 590-9019.
