Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Faysal al-Husayni, one of the leaders of the Palestinians in the territories, said that when Yasir 'Arafat told Shim'on Peres that he was not talking about a Palestinian state but rather about a Jordanian-Palestinian confederation, 'Arafat was referring to a confederation between two states. This is the best practical solution for us, al-Husayni explained, because we want a Palestinian state. Al-Husayni told Qol Yisra'el that a confederation agreement with Jordan would be possible only after the Palestinians and the Jordanians approved it in a national referendum.
