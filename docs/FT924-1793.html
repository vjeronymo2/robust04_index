921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Hardys & Hansons shows 5% decline Hardys & Hansons, the Nottingham-based brewer, yesterday reported a 5 per cent downturn in annual profits. On turnover ahead 4 per cent to Pounds 30.3m, pre-tax profits for the 12 months to October 2 amounted to Pounds 6.72m, down from Pounds 7.04m in the previous 53 week period. The outcome was struck after dividends and net interest receivable of Pounds 962,000 (Pounds 1.63m). After tax and the preference dividend, earnings per 5p share emerged at 18.035p, down from 19.271p. A recommended final dividend of 5.1p lifts the total for the year to 7.9p (7.5p).
