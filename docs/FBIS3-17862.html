Language: Lithuanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Seimas ratified an agreement today between the Republic of Lithuania and the European Economic Community on the development of fishing. This agreement was signed in Brussels on 17 December 1993. The sides agreed to cooperate closer in the preservation and rational use of the stock of fish in the areas under the jurisdiction of both sides and the neighboring areas. The EEC promises to contribute to the improvement of the knowledge of the Lithuanian fishing industry workers and allocate financial assistance for their training. The agreement envisages that the sides will encourage the establishment of joint fishing industry enterprises.
