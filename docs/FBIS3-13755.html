Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Kuwait, 21 March -- In a press statement published here today, Minister of State for Cabinet Affairs 'Abd-al-'Aziz al- Dakhil has denied reports of the existence of an Iraqi offer to exchange Kuwaiti prisoners still in the Iraqi regime's prisons for 11 Iraqis being tried in Kuwait charged with attempting to assassinate former American President George Bush. The newspaper AL-ANBA quoted the minister of state for cabinet affairs as saying that the State of Kuwait "has not and will not agree to this type of maneuvering and trickery, which is undertaken by the Baghdad regime from time to time to create a stir and give the international community the impression that it is seeking to find stability in the region." He pointed out that "the aim behind this attempt is to lift the economic sanctions that have been imposed on Iraq for some time now." [passage omitted]
