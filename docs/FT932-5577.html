930603 FT 03 JUN 93 / Bridgewater juror speaks of injustice THE FOREMAN of the jury that found four men guilty of the 1978 murder of Carl Bridgewater said yesterday he now believed they were innocent. Mr Tim O'Malley said on ITN: 'At the time I thought we had done the right thing. Now I feel completely convinced they are innocent and there has been a terrible miscarriage of justice which must be put right for those men and for British justice itself.'
