Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "Presidential Bulletin" feature] [Text] Prime Minister Abbas Dzhumagulov signed Monday the government resolution "On Measures to Strengthen Public Order and Curb Crimes." According to the document, the republic's Interior Ministry was ordered to work out urgent measures to strengthen public order and increase control over the activities of the police and the Interior Ministry servicemen and conduct in the first half of the year an appraisal of the Interior Ministry's leadership. Among the priorities of the police is to enforce order in public transport and toughen control over the observance of the passport regime.
