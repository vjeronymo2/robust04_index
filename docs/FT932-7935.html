930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / Seoul seeks N Korea meeting South Korea sought to open a channel of communication with an increasingly isolated and intransigent North Korea yesterday to defuse the row over nuclear inspections before a June deadline, Reuter reports from Seoul. Mr Hwang In-sung, South Korean prime minister, in a letter to Mr Kang Song-san, his northern counterpart, proposed a high-level meeting next week 'for the sake of national well-being'.
