Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Konstantin Pribytkov] [Text] Geneva February 9 TASS -- Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Vitaliy Churkin met in Geneva on Wednesday with Foreign Ministers Karolos Papoulias of Greece and Beniamino Andreatta of Italy and Co-Chairmen of the Coordinating Committee of the Conference on Former Yugoslavia Thorvald Stoltenberg and David Owen to discuss Bosnian settlement. The talks focused on problems concerning the settlement of the Yugoslav crisis, including the situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina. The participants in the talks expressed concern about the aggravation of the situation in the region, following the bomb explosion in Sarajevo marketplace last Saturday which killed 68 and wounded over 200 people.
