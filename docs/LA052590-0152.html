May 25, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

DUTCH VOLLEYBALL TEAM TO PLAY AT UC IRVINE

The Dutch women's team Dynamo will play the International Ambassadors in the Flo Hyman memorial volleyball match Thursday at the Bren Center at UC Irvine.

The international team features several local players, including Rita Crocket of Newport Beach, a 1984 Olympian; Stella Berkabile of Westminster and Belinda Hilleary of Laguna Hills. The match will start at 7:30 p.m.
