931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Chrysalis sale Chrysalis, the music and media group, has reached final agreement to sell a 25 per cent stake in its new record label, The Echo Label, to Pony Canyon, the music division of Fujisankei ,the Japanese media and communications company, for Dollars 17.5m (Pounds 11.7m). Chrysalis will retain a 56.25 per cent stake.
