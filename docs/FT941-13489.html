940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / World Commodities Prices: Precious metals slide continues - Market Report Precious metals continued to slide yesterday with London GOLD losing Dollars 5 at Dollars 380.25 a troy ounce and SILVER 12 cents at Dollars 5.08 1/2 an ounce. Gold found support after fixing at Dollars 378.80, with some encouragement being taken from the relatively steady tone on the New York silver futures market. At the London Commodity Exchange March COFFEE rallied Dollars 12 to Dollars 1,192 a tonne, encouraged by news that Central American producers had set minimum export prices. Compiled from Reuter
