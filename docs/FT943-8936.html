940815 FT 15 AUG 94 / French employment up Further evidence emerged yesterday of the recovery in the French economy with the news that the number of salaried employees, rose by 60,000, or 0.4 per cent, to just over 9m in the second quarter of this year, according to the Employment Ministry. This continues the upward trend of the first quarter, when salaried employment rose by 0.2 per cent. It follows the news that the overall level of unemployment experienced its biggest fall for four years in June with the number of jobless dropping by 13,000 to 3.33m, or 12.6 per cent of the workforce. Economic growth in France during the first quarter was higher than expected at 0.7 per cent rather than its original estimate of 0.5 per cent.
