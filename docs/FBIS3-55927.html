Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] In accordance with the decree "On the structure of the federal bodies of executive authority," signed by the Russian president, the Russian State Committee for Emergency Situations has been redesignated as a ministry. This has been reported by ITAR- TASS. According to the new structure of the federal bodies of executive authority, that department is assigned to the block of power ministries. The former committee's press service has said that from today the functions of the abolished committee for the social support of citizens and the rehabilitation of territories that have suffered from Chernobyl and other radiation catastrophes have been transferred to the new ministry, as well as the functions of the committee for carrying out special-purpose under-water work. Also transferred to the ministry as a state institution is the Russian Federal Aerospace Search and Rescue Service.
