August 7, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL PEOPLE

Anne Lieberman has been named vice president of home video and business development for DIC Enterprises Inc., a leading producer of animated children's television programming based in Burbank. Lieberman, who held a similar position at DIC from 1985 to 1988 before leaving to become senior vice president of business development and acquisitions at Leonard and Associates Inc., will develop new distribution and promotional tie-ins with fast-food and packaged goods companies, DIC said.
