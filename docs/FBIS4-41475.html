BFN [Text] Russian border guards in Tajikistan have been put on military alert and the units on guard where Russian border guards and their families live have been reinforced, Vladimir Novikov, chief of staff of the directorate of the Russian border guards in Tajikistan, said today in Dushanbe. He also said that some of the Russian border guards at the Tajik-Afghan border will be transferred to Dushanbe to protect Russian servicemen and ethnic Russians living in the city.
