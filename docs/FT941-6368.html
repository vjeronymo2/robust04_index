940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / People: Constructive careers Terry Roydon has been appointed deputy chairman and chief executive of PROWTING; he is succeeded as group md by David Brill, chairman of Galliford Homes. David Brill, md of Galliford Homes, has been appointed a director of Prowting following its acquisition of Galliford. Tony Betney, md of Galliford (UK), has been appointed to the main Galliford board.
