BFN [Text] The Higher Jordanian-Egyptian Committee has begun its two-day meetings at the prime minister's office in Amman today. Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali and his Egyptian counterpart are cochairing the talks. Issues on the agenda of the joint committee include bolstering bilateral relations in the economic, trade, cultural, and scientific fields. Over the past two days, a preparatory committee, branched off the higher committee, held a series of meetings at the Ministry of Planning.
