940621 FT 21 JUN 94 / World Cup '94: Romania tighten up team security Romania clamped down on media access to its team after a television crew from Colombia broke into a player's hotel room, the team's press officer said yesterday. The incident happened at a Los Angeles hotel before Romania's first game against Colombia, which Romania won 3-1 with some eye-opening play. The Romanians have banned journalists from all the side's practice sessions before tomorrow's Group A game against Switzerland in Detroit.
