920529 FT 29 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Sheldon Jones calls for Pounds 2.15m SHELDON JONES, the USM-quoted pet foods, garden and timber products group, plans to raise about Pounds 2.15m net through a conditional placing and open offer of Pounds 2.45m convertible loan stock to be issued at par. At the same time it announced a pre-tax loss of Pounds 663,000 for the six months to December 28 compared with profits of Pounds 44,000. The loss was after an exceptional Pounds 240,000 (Pounds 57,000), being the costs associated with the resignation of the previous chairman and chief executive. There was also an extraordinary charge of Pounds 310,000 (nil) arising from the disposal of Seed Producers and an adjustment to write off goodwill from its acquisition, previously offset against capital reserves. Turnover amounted to Pounds 6.29m (Pounds 6.57m). Losses per share amounted to 11.2p (0.6p earnings) and there is no interim dividend (1.35p). Mr Richard Sheldon, chairman, said the group was currently trading profitably.
