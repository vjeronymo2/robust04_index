920306 FT 06 MAR 92 / Despatch of UN troops to proceed despite growing tension Serbs at one of many checkpoints near the Bosnian capital, Sarajevo. The action has raised tension before the arrival of UN troops. The plan to despatch 14,000 United Nations peacekeeping troops to Croatia will proceed despite growing ethnic turmoil in neighbouring Bosnia, Mr Cyrus Vance, the UN envoy said yesterday, writes Laura Silber in Sarajevo. Mr Vance appealed for restraint from Bosnia's Moslems, Serbs and Croats. Some 700 troops to be stationed in the Bosnian capital.
