Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev, 1 Feb -- As Vasyl Bilous, press adviser of the Ukrainian National Assembly has stated, Yuriy Meshkov's victory in the Crimean presidential elections will lead to a civil war. In his words, detachments of the UNA-UNSO [Ukrainian National Assembly-Ukrainian National Self-Defense] are at present on full combat alert: "We are prepared to react to all events in the Crimea."
