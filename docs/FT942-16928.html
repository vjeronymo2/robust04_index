940407 FT 07 APR 94 / International Company News: Foodland in red but holds payout Foodland Associated, the Australian grocery group, is holding its interim dividend at 12 cents a share in spite of extraordinary charges which sent it into the red for the seven months to January. Net earnings rose marginally to ADollars 18.8m (USDollars 13.4m) from ADollars 18.1m, including a ADollars 7.5m abnormal loss. But a ADollars 32.2m extraordinary loss sent the company ADollars 12.4m into the red for the period.
