BFN [Text] Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat last night received Ibrahim 'Allam, Egypt's Ambassador to Tunisia. Following the session, Ambassador 'Allam said that he and President 'Arafat reviewed the Palestinian-Israeli talks currently under way in Cairo and Paris, the current efforts designed to overcome the obstacles at these talks, as well as preparations for 'Arafat's visit to Cairo due to take place today, Wednesday. Meanwhile, the Palestinian news agency WAFA reports that the Egyptian ambassador conveyed an important message to 'Arafat from the Egyptian leadership dealing with recent political developments in the region.
