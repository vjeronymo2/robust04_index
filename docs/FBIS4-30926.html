BFN [Text] Prishtina, May 27 (ATA) -- In the context of the programs for colonization of Kosova, Serbian authorities warned that they will give 200 construction sites to the Serbian settlers in the Istog commune. The settlers are mainly refugees who have fled from Bosnia or Croatia. The sites will be delivered in Istog, Dubrave, Muzhevine, Gjurakoc, Banje, and other areas. On the other side, the Albanians in the Istog commune has been given no construction sites.
