BFN [Text] The number of cases of so-called nonregulation relations between servicemen in the Belarus Army is growing at a threatening rate. Procurator General Vasil Shaladonaw was forced to state this sad fact at the latest meeting of the republican procuracy collegium. Over the past year alone the number of fights between soldiers increased by 47 percent and the number of servicemen involved in these obscene acts by 55 percent. Two servicemen have been killed.
