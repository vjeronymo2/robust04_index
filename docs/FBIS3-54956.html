Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report over video of test flight of new fighter aircraft; from the "Polygon" program] [Text] [Unidentified correspondent over video of fighter aircraft on tarmac and taking off] No awesome name has yet been thought up for this aircraft. Its specifications remain secret for the time being, but even the first test flight has made it clear that what we see here is the super-fighter of the 21st century. In terms of flight specifications this fighter has no parallel in aviation history. Russian scientists and military people believe that this super-fighter will largely determine the development of world aircraft building. It is amazing that this machine has appeared now, during a period of protracted economic crisis, Deputy Defense Minister Andrey Kokoshin and Russian Air Force Commander in Chief Petr Deynekin stressed in our interview. [Kokoshin] The first flight has been very successful. I consider this an important milestone in the emergence of essentially a new-generation aircraft. [Deynekin] I believe that we can confidently and objectively claim today that Russia always had, has, and will continue to have wings.
