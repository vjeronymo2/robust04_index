931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Burford Pounds 29m debenture issue Burford, the property investor and trader, is raising Pounds 29m via an issue of first mortgage debenture stock dated 2018. Lead manager to the issue is Barclays de Zoete Wedd with Barclays de Zoete Wedd Securities acting as listing sponsor. Payment for the stock is due on December 13.
