921128 FT 28 NOV 92 / Union to ballot at Sweb Retail ABOUT 300 members of Nalgo are to be balloted at Sweb Retail, the jointly owned retailing arm of South Western Electricity and South Wales Electricity. The ballot is over moves to change terms and conditions for 1,100 employees from those centrally negotiated in the electricity supply industry to those more in line with competitors in retailing.
