920818 FT 18 AUG 92 / Lufthansa offers cuts on US fares LUFTHANSA, the German state airline, yesterday began offering cut-price first and business class fares to the US for passengers from London, Birmingham, Manchester and Glasgow. Travellers to any of nine US cities must change aircraft in Frankfurt. A typical reduction is Manchester to New York on business class at Pounds 1,940, instead of a direct fare of Pounds 2,122.
