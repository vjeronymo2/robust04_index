BFN [Text] An Iraqi opposition source in Damascus has stated that the Iraqi regime forces have tightened the cordon they imposed on Mosul in anticipation of civil disobedience by the city's inhabitants. Bayan Jabir, the Supreme Assembly of the Islamic Revolution in Iraq's representative in Damascus, said the situation in the city is very tense. He added that the executive committee of the Unified Iraqi National Congress will meet in Salah-al-Din in Iraqi Kurdistan on 16 April to discuss the deteriorating situation in Iraq in light of these developments.
