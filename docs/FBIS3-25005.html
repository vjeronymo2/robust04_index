Language: French Article Type:BFN [Communique issued by the Territorial and Security Administration Ministry on 4 February; place not given] [Text] In line with the first round of the legislative elections slated for 6 February, the land borders with Benin and Burkina Faso will be closed from 1800 on 5 February to 1200 on 7 February. The western border [with Ghana] remains closed.
