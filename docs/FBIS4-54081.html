BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 24 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to his Majesty King Husayn the first of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan on the occasion of the 48th anniversary of the independence of Jordan. President Kim Il-song in the message expressed the conviction that the friendly relations between the two countries would continue to develop in accordance with the desire and aspiration of their peoples, and wished the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan prosperity.
