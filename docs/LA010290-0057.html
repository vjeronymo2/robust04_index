January 2, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski won $80 Monday when his pick in the seventh race, Weldnaas, finished first and paid $5.20. Bob wagered $50 to win on Weldnaas and his mythical bankroll, which started at $1,000, is now at $1,182.

Monday's Profit: $80
