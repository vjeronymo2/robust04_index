Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 10 (KCNA) -- A Nepalese national seminar on the chuche idea took place in Katmandu on the occasion of the birthday of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. A report by Manik Lal Shrestha, director of the Nepal Institute for Chuche Studies, was followed by speeches at the seminar. Noting that Comrade Kim Chong-il has developed and enriched the immortal chuche idea with new profound ideas and theories, they said he is a genius of thought and theory. They said it is because it is advancing under the banner of the chuche idea that Korea is firmly defending its socialism and demonstrating its fame to the whole world although socialism suffered a setback in some countries. They stressed that a large number of works authored by Comrade Kim Chong-il serve the world's revolutionary people as a weapon of struggle, as a priceless asset, and resolved to deeply study his classical works. A message of greetings to Comrade Kim Chong-il was adopted at the seminar.
