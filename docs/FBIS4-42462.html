BFN [By Makoto Otsuka] [Text] Vienna, 2 Jun -- Councilor Yun Ho-chin of the DPRK (North Korean) Embassy in Vienna said to an ASAHI SHIMBUN reporter on 2 June that "we will be obliged to cancel the special stand (that we are holding back our decision to withdraw from the Nuclear Nonproliferation Treaty [NPT] if economic sanctions are imposed." In this way, he disclosed the DPRK's intention to officially withdraw from the NPT as soon as sanctions are imposed on the DPRK. Meanwhile, the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) authorities disclosed on 2 June the fact that North Korea continued to remove fuel rods from an experimental reactor in Yongbyon on the same day as well. Moreover, it is said that North Korea still has not responded to a technological proposal which the IAEA made on 31 May regarding fuel rod inspections.
