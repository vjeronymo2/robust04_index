940825 FT 25 AUG 94 / International Company News: Bang & Olufsen posts record annual profit Bang & Olufsen, the Danish television and audio products manufacturer, reported its best year in the 12 months to May 31. Net profits of DKr90m (Dollars 14.1m) compared with a loss of DKr60m in the previous year. Sales increased 13 per cent to DKr2.40bn from DKr2.12bn. The result after net financial costs of DKr24m was a profit of DKr132m compared with a loss of DKr38m last year. The board proposed a DKr10-per-share dividend, the first to be paid for four years. Cash flow increased to DKr540m from DKr97m. There was a return on equity capital of 13 per cent after negative returns in each of the past three years. The preliminary statement said the improved performance was in spite of a generally weak market in audio-visual products.
