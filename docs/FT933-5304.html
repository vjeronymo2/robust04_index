930903 FT 03 SEP 93 / World Trade News: Sony to make video cameras in China SONY Corporation, the Japanese electronics giant, is to set up its first joint venture in China to make video cameras this month, under an agreement with the Shanghai Video & Audio Electronics Co (SVA). Sony, which has been a somewhat reluctant investor in China, will provide 70 per cent of the total Dollars 5m investment in the new Shanghai Suoguang Electronics Company. SVA will be responsible for the rest. Production will begin in November. The new company plans to produce 150,000 8mm video cameras annually. It will also turn out components for video tape recorders.
