BFN [Text] The brother members of the Yemeni Socialist Party Political Bureau are currently paying field visits to a number of battlefronts, and to the leadership of the forces defending unity, democracy, and modernization, which are pursuing the forces of the al-Ahmar clan's gang [words indistinct]. The Yemeni news agency, SABA', has learned that the visits by the brother members of the political bureau to the battlefront are being made in order to implement a recently made decision by that same organization. The soldiers of the units defending unity and democracy have expressed great satisfaction and appreciation concerning these visits, and with this wise and sincere decision made during these hard times, which were brought about by the criminal and oppressive war imposed on the Yemeni people by the bloody al-Ahmar gang, which has turned the southern and eastern governorates into their battlefields.
