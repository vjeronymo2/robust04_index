930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / International Company News: French exchange body fines Conso THE French stock market authorities yesterday issued a harsh indictment of Mr Pierre Conso, the prominent businessman who last year resigned as chairman of the Ciments Francais cement group, after a scandal over the discovery of FFr2bn (Dollars 354m) of off-balance sheet dealings, writes Alice Rawsthorn. He was also fined FFr400,000 for his part in the affair, which led to heavy losses at the group.
