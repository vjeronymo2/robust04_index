BFN [Text] Beijing, may 3 (XINHUA) -- Zhang Siqing, procurator- general of the Chinese Supreme People's Procuratorate, met and feted Purwoto Gandasubrata, president of the Indonesian Supreme Court, and his party here today. Gandasubrata and his entourage arrived here May 1 at the invitation of the Chinese Supreme People's Court. Besides Beijing they are also scheduled to visit Shanghai, Guangzhou and Shenzhen.
