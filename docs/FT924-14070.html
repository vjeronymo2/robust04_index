921012 FT 12 OCT 92 / International Company News: Lawson Mardo acquires Cartonajes Suner LAWSON Mardon, the Canadian packaging group, has acquired Cartonajes Suner, one of Spain's largest packaging companies, Bernard Simon reports from Toronto. Lawson is paying CDollars 13.2m (USDollars 10.5m) cash for Suner plus a CDollars 51.5m non-interest bearing subordinated bond. The seller is Cragnotti & Partners Capital Investments, the Italian investment group, Lawson's controlling shareholder since early 1991.
