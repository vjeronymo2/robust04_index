Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The delegation of Paraguayan senators visiting the PRC, headed by Carlos Alberto Gonzalez and made up of Antonia Nunez de Lopez, Diego Abente, Fernando Pfannl, Luis Guanes, Amado Yambay, and Arnaldo Rojas Sanchez, received the promise of Vice Foreign Minister Liu Huaqiu that commercial offices will be established in Asuncion and Beijing as soon as the Paraguayan and Chinese Governments reach an agreement. The offices will promote economic, commercial, investment, cultural, and personal links between China and Paraguay, as well as grant visas to the two countries' citizens.
