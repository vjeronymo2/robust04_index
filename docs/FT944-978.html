941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / International Company News: 'No intention' to sell Plaza stake, Trump says Mr Donald Trump, the US property developer, said yesterday he had no intention of selling his 51 per cent stake in the landmark Plaza Hotel in New York, but said his company had been approached by people interested in acquiring a stake in the Plaza, Reuter reports from New York. Mr Trump told CNBC cable television that his company had had contacts with the representative of the Sultan of Brunei, who is considered one of the world's richest people. 'The Sultan of Brunei, his people have spoken to us, various other people have spoken to us and we may do some kind of partnership or something,' Mr Trump said. The New York Post had earlier reported that the sultan and his partners had bid more than Dollars 300m for the Fifth Avenue hotel. However, Trump Organisation chief executive Mr Nicholas Ribis said there had been no substantive discussions.
