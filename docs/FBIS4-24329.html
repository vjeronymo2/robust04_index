BFN [Text] The attorney general at the Supreme Court has informed the general public as follows: By an order dated 27 June 1994, the chamber of the Supreme Court responsible for investigations in the case of Moussa Traore and Others versus the State, has ordered the release on bail of the following accused persons: Abdoulaye Diallo, former minister; Moulaye Mohamed Haidara, former minister; Tienan Coulibaly, former minister; Djanka Kaba Diakite, Central Executive Bureau member of the erstwhile Democratic Union of Malian People; and Abdoulaye Traore -- alias Tout Petit -- a businessman.
