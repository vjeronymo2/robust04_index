930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / World Commodities Prices: Jute and Cotton JUTE August/September c and f Dundee BTC Dollars 315, BWC Dollars 325, BTD Dollars 295, BWD Dollars 300; c and f Antwerp BTC Dollars 305, BWC Dollars 305, BTD Dollars 280, BWD Dollars 280. COTTON Liverpool- Spot and shipment sales amounted to 172 tonnes for the week ended July 30, against none in the previous week. Improved demand brought moderate purchases mainly in CIS descriptions. West African growths made some headway.
