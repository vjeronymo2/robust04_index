March 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

MEASURING A GOOD SERIES

The Writers Guild of America appreciates good writing, whether or not it surfaces on a major network. Proof is the WGA nomination of "Measure of a Man," a beautiful script by Melinda M. Snodgrass for "Star Trek: The Next Generation." Now perhaps those behind the Emmy nominations will recognize the simply exquisite acting of Patrick Stewart, as well as the talents of the other people responsible for this fine series, one which consistently presents entertaining drama with comedic touches.

Lynne Page, Thousand Oaks
