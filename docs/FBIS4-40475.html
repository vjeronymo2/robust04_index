BFN [Text] Russia keeps organizing conspiracies against Chechnya, Chechen Foreign Minister Shamsudin Yusef said today. He accused Moscow of refusing to normalize relations between the two countries. He mentioned the recent attempt on Dzhokhar Dudayev's life as an example of anti-Groznyy actions. In the meantime, the military commandant of Chechnya has today demanded that illegal armed formations disband themselves and their leaders give themselves up. This decision by Sultan (Geleskhanov) is connected with recent clashes in Groznyy.
