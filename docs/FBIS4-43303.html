BFN [Unattributed article from Brussels EUROPE: "Free-Trade Agreement With Lithuania Should Be Initialled Before End of Month"] [Text] The third round of negotiations with a view to concluding a EU/Lithuania free-trade agreement led to "substantial progress" between the Community delegation (led by Mr. Guggenbuhl of DGI) and the Lithuanian delegation led by Mr. Grybauskaite of the Foreign Ministry. So much so that the agreement could probably be initialled before the end of the month. Defined, notably, were: the text of the draft agreement, the list of industrial products for which Lithuania will benefit from a period of transition, the provisions on textiles, processed agricultural products, customs cooperation and rules of origin. Moreover, the positions aligned more as to the reciprocal concessions for agricultural products and fishing, said a note from the Commission.
