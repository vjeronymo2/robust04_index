March 2, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CHARITY SCOREBOARD

"An Affair of the Heart" dinner Feb. 15 at the Four Seasons Hotel in Newport Beach netted about $25,000 for the Heart Institute of Saddleback Hospital & Health Center. Nan Owen was chairwoman.

The Orange County Law Auxiliary's "Mardi Gras Matinee" fashion show Feb. 18 at the Irvine Hilton & Towers netted about $6,000 for Victim-Witness Assn., Orange County Sexual Assault Network and Shortstop, a juvenile delinquency diversion program. Donna Saltarelli was chairwoman.

The "First Ladies on Parade: Inaugural Gowns" luncheon and fashion show Friday at the Anaheim Hilton & Towers netted about $10,000 for the county chapter of Freedoms Foundation. Carole Engstrom was chairwoman.

The "Ambassador of Life" recognition dinner Saturday at the Anaheim Marriott netted about $25,000 for the American Cancer Society's county unit. Dixie Bullington was chairwoman.

The "Nordstrom Spring Trend Show" on Saturday at Nordstrom South Coast Plaza, Costa Mesa, netted about $5,000 for KOCE-TV, Channel 50, of Huntington Beach. Jo Caines was chairwoman.

The dinner-dance on Saturday at the Irvine Hilton & Towers netted about $83,000 for Tri-County chapter of the Leukemia Society of America. Kevin Spence and Peter DiMicelli were co-chairmen.

"A Caribbean Night With Jean-Michel Cousteau" on Saturday at the Balboa Bay Club netted about $10,000 for the Handicapped Scuba Assn. Patricia Derk Gatacre was chairwoman.

"Wine Fest '89" on Sunday at the Anaheim Marriott netted about $15,000 for the Town & Country Committee of the Orange County Philharmonic Society. Marcia Harris was chairwoman.
