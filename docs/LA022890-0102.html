February 28, 1990, Wednesday, Southland Edition

DU PONT PLANS TO CLOSE 2 AUTO PAINT PLANTS

The Du Pont Co. said it plans to close two plants that supply paints for the automotive industry by early next year.

The closings of the high-performance coatings plant in Moberly, Mo., and a finishes operation plant in Parlin, N.J., will affect about 250 employees, the announcement said.

The company said the operations performed at the two plants will be moved to other facilities.
