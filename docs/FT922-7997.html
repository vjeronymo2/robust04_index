920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Moorfield agrees Pounds 700,000 bid Moorfield Estates yesterday announced a recommended all share offer for Grosvenor Terrace Developments valuing the property company at Pounds 700,000, writes Richard Gourlay. Moorfield is offering 19 new shares for every 8 Grosvenor shares, valuing each share at about 74p. As part of the deal Granville, the investment house, will place 63 per cent of Moorfield's existing share capital belonging to some of its directors with institutional investors and the British Land Company. The placing price of 42p represented a 35 per cent premium to Moorfield's current share price.
