920423 FT 23 APR 92 / UK Company News: Densitron Intl drops into loss Densitron International suffered losses of Pounds 377,000 in 1991 and has dropped the dividend payment. In the previous 12 months pre-tax profits were Pounds 587,000 and the company, which makes a diverse range of electronic components, paid a total dividend of 1.7p. Turnover, less intra-group, amounted to Pounds 38m (Pounds 31.4m) and losses per share worked through at 5.55p (1.83p earnings).
