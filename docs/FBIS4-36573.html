BFN [Excerpt] Aden, Yemen, June 10 (AFP) -- Northern heavy artillery gunners shelled the southern stronghold of Aden on Friday, a day after the collapse of the latest ceasefire in Yemen's civil war. Shells crashed into the Khormaksar district, near the airport used by southern warplanes, an AFP correspondent said. Ambulances rushed to the scene, but there was no immediate casualty toll available. The bombardment cut off electricity supplies to several districts of the capital of the south's breakaway state.
