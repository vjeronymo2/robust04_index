920801 FT 01 AUG 92 / Olympic News In Brief: Ecuador official dies An official of Ecuador's Olympic team died of a heart attack early yesterday. Wilson Malo Harris, 61, vice-president of the national shooting federation, suffered the attack in his room in the Olympic village. It was the third death reported during the Olympics. Peter Karnaugh, father of US swimmer Ron Karnaugh, died of a heart attack while watching the opening ceremonies and officials said another spectator had died.
