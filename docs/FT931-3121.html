930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / World Trade News: Henan awards power plants China's Henan province has signed a memorandum of understanding with Wing-Merrill International of the US to develop three coal-fired power plants, Andrew Baxter writes. Two of the plants, each with a capacity of 1,400MW, will be sited near the provincial capital of Zhengzhou and will be built, owned and operated by a joint venture company to be established under the agreement. The third station will be built near Henan's northern border. Members of the consortium include Bechtel, Westinghouse and Riley Stoker from the US.
