Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Islamic Republic of Iran has declared its readiness to assist the victims of the Hebron black Friday. The Central News Unit reports that Mr. Mohajerani, head of the Committee for Supporting the Palestine Islamic Revolution [CSPIR], in an exclusive interview stated: The Islamic Republic of Iran is ready to send pharmaceutical and food aid to those who suffered from the recent Zionist atrocity in Hebron through international relief organizations. On the effects of the atrocity on the process of the Gaza-Jericho accord, the head of CSPIR said: The territory of occupied Palestine has witnessed a new move and fervor within the intifada. Though Nazareth used to be considered a safe city for the Zionists so far, it has witnessed popular revolt in recent days. Referring to the fact that the recent Zionist action has once again exposed the false claims of those professing to support human rights, Mr. Mohajerani said: In line with other Islamic nations, Islamic states must also make a serious decision to resolve the Palestine issue. In conclusion, he called on the people of Iran to take another practical step toward supporting the oppressed Palestinians by their solid presence on Jersusalem Day.
