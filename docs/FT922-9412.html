920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Overseas Investment asset value declines At March 31 1992 the net asset value per share of Overseas Investment Trust had slipped to 274.5p. Six months earlier it was 279.3p and the year before 277.6p. In the half year to end-March net income rose from Pounds 254,000 to Pounds 469,000 for earnings per share of 1.23p (0.67p). The interim distribution is lifted from 0.75p to 0.8p.
