931127 FT 27 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Vistec declines 14% to Pounds 1.31m Pre-tax profits of Vistec Group, the USM-quoted supplier of computer systems, fell by 14 per cent from Pounds 1.31m to Pounds 1.26m in the six months to end October 31. Turnover was down by 6 per cent to Pounds 17.6m (Pounds 18.7m). Earnings decreased to 0.62p (0.73p) and the interim dividend is maintained at 0.125p. Mr Arthur Morton, chairman, said trading had been 'patchy'.
