Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Islamic Resistance Movement, Hamas, has issued a statement in which it congratulated the masses of our Arab and Islamic nations on the occasion of the blessed month of Ramadan. It also urged the sons of our Palestinian Arab people to carry out the following activities: 1. To consider 9 February a day of comprehensive strike to mark the beginning of the 75th month of the blessed intifadah. 2. To consider 13 February a day of popular gatherings and seminars in protest of the ominous Gaza-Jericho agreement. 3. To consider 14-16 February days of confrontation against the enemy's forces and settlers. 4. To consider 17 February a day of comprehensive strike in solidarity with strugglers in the Zionist enemy's prisons. 5. To consider 27 February a day of a distinguished escalation against enemy forces and settlers on the anniversary of the great Badr battle [ancient Islamic battle].
