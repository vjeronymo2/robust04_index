Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Text] On the afternoon of 29 January, Oudom Khatthi-gna, Political Bureau member of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party [LPRP] Central Committee and personnel in charge of guiding mass organization work, received, at the Office of the Lao Front for National Construction Central Committee in Vientiane, a courtesy call from Do Vanh Tai, deputy head of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the Communist Party of Vietnam Central Committee, and his delegation, which was on a friendship visit to our country. On this occasion, Oudom Khatthi-gna welcomed the delegation, whose visit, he said, has brought about the further promotion and enhancement of the special relations of fraternal friendship between Laos and Vietnam. On the same occasion, Do Vanh Tai informed Oudom Khatthi-gna of the success of the visit and the outcome of his delegation's talks with a delegation of the Foreign Relations Committee of the LPRP Central Committee led by Somsavat Lengsavat, deputy head of the Foreign Relations Committee. He said: Following the talks, the two sides have unanimously agreed to improve and broaden cooperation with friendly countries as well as peace-loving countries in the world.
