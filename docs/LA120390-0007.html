December 3, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MCA BUYOUT BY JAPANESE

I would not worry about the sale of an American business to Japan.

The U.S. has plenty of foreign ownership all over Europe and elsewhere.

On my recent trip to France, I tried to buy some children's T-shirts with French writing on them. They were impossible to find but everywhere I found T-shirts from the U.S. Jeans, grocery products, cosmetics, etc. from America were available everywhere.

I must admit, they love everything American. Now it is our turn: Can we learn to love Japanese entertainment?

YVETTE CARR, Glendora
