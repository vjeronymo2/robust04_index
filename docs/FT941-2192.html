940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / UK Company News: BPI buys Nelson Packaging for Pounds 1.3m British Polythene Industries has acquired Nelson Packaging from St Ives for about Pounds 1.3m in cash. Nelson, which makes carrier bags, had operating profits of Pounds 203,000 on turnover of Pounds 5.4m in the year to July 30 1993.
