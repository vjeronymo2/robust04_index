941024 FT 24 OCT 94 / Business Travel: Aberdeen express A low-cost air service linking Aberdeen and London Stansted is due to start next Monday. Aberdeen London Express (Alex) will leave Aberdeen at 7.15am on weekdays, getting passengers to Liverpool Street by train from Stansted by 9.30am. In the evening it will leave Stansted at 7pm (6pm on Fridays). Alex is an Aberdeen company formed to challenge British Airways and Air UK, which Aberdonians and oil company executives claim charge too much. The new company will charge Pounds 60 single and Pounds 120 return and offers a Pounds 99 return for companies that make block bookings, with a Pounds 95 weekend return.
