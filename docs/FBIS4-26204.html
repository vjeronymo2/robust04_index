BFN [From the "Provincial News Hookup" program] [Text] A national postal and telecommunications conference opened in Hangzhou today. Minister of Posts and Telecommunications Wu Jichuan and Vice Governor Zhang Qimei of Zhejiang attended and spoke at the conference. The conferees called for improving the organizational planning and structural readjustment of first-class postal trunk networks this year, by rationalizing the postal transportation system, actively setting up independent and exclusive air postal routes, ensuring a good job in the readjustment and distribution of motor vehicle postal transportation networks, and accelerating the completion of auxiliary equipment for heavy-duty trucks and other supplementary facilities in a bid to raise the postal transportation capacity. They said: During the Ninth Five-Year Plan period, there will be 20 postal transport planes, 36,000 km air trunk postal routes operated by the ministry, 9,700 motor vehicles for trunk postal transportation, and 280,000 km of motor vehicle trunk postal routes operated by the ministry, thereby gradually setting up a relatively independent postal transport system which comprehensively utilizes planes, motor vehicles, trains, and ships and which combines routes operated by the ministry with those operated by other departments consigned by the ministry.
