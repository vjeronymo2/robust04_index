EFFECTIVE DATE: March 17, 1994. SECURITIES AND EXCHANGE COMMISSION 17 CFR Part 200 [Release No. 34&hyph;33573A] Regional Office Reorganization; Correction ACTION: Correction to final rule amendments.

SUMMARY: This document contains corrections to final rule amendments which were published February 9, 1994 [59 FR 5942]. The amendments pertain to the organization and operation of the Commission's regional offices. EFFECTIVE DATE: March 17, 1994. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: James Clarkson, Director, Regional Office Operations, (202) 272&hyph;3090; Anne Sullivan, Office of General Counsel, (202) 272&hyph;7525.
