Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Doha, 25 Feb (WAKH) -- The State of Qatar has condemned today's ugly massacre against worshipers in the Ibrahimi Mosque, terming it a terrorist act threatening the Palestinian citizens who are entitled to live on their land and enjoy what other citizens enjoy in their country. In a press statement here this evening, Qatari Foreign Minister Shaykh Hamad Bin-Jasim Al Thani expressed his great displeasure at the massacre, hoping that it will not affect the peace process.
