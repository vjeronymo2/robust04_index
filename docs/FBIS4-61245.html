BFN [Text] According to the State Committee for Statistics and Analysis, between January and March 1994, GNP in Belarus dropped by 38-percent compared with the corresponding period last year. The volume of industrial production decreased by 35 percent, construction -- 27 percent, and agriculture -- 4 percent. Investments fell by 29 percent, retail trade -- 47 percent. A particularly acute decline was seen in production of such goods as feed-preparing and animal husbandry machines (86.5 percent), agricultural machines (60.7), slate (79.3), light roofing materials (72.7), paper (70.7), sulfuric acid (68.1), chemical fibers and threads (44.1), tractors (46.4), tracks (45.4), textiles (56.8), and sausage (48.2).
