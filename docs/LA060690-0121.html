June 6, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: BUENA PARK;

RAISE IN TRASH FEES VOTED BY COUNCIL

To offset increasing costs of trash disposal, the City Council approved a boost in trash fees.

"The county is raising the dump fee, so we are just passing it on," Director of Public Works Don Kemp said.

The monthly fee for residents will increase from $7 per month to $7.92. The increase for businesses will run from $7 to $40 per month, depending on the number of pickups.

Kemp said it may not be the last time the county raises its rates this year. The county increased its prices twice in 1989, and increases were passed on to residents. Since January, 1989, rates have gone up more than $1.50 per household.

The new rate will take effect immediately.
