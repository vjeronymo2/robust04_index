Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow February 17 TASS -- Boris Yeltsin issued a decision on Thursday to set up a public chamber under the Russian president. In compliance with this document, this body will be "based on the public chamber of the constitutional conference". The presidential press service notes in its report that the public chamber under the Russian president will be set up to ensure cooperation between the federal bodies of executive power in the Russian Federation and mass organisations to prepare decisions by the Russian president and government on important social and political issues, taking into account public opinion. The president's decision approves regulations on the operation of this body.
