BFN [Text] The executive phase of the Guerrillero-94 exercise began on 5 April in Guantanamo. The purpose of the exercise is to evaluate the efficiency of the Unified Vigilance and Protection System. The exercise will enhance the control and operation structures of the system at each level and increase cohesion at workplaces, organizations, and enterprises to preserve economic resources. The Executive Secretariat of the Committees for the Defense of the Revolution in Holguin has called upon its members to honor their commitment to the people's surveillance program. The measures include performing the entire watch, staying alert, and not letting other tasks distract from the effectiveness of the watch, as well as fulfilling the watch during power outages.
