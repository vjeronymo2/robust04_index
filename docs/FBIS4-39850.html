BFN [Text] The Kyrgyz Government has adopted a resolution on approving a feasibility study report, amendments and changes to the general agreement on the Kumtor-Gold project. The resolution says that the legalility and the validity of every document, which is mentioned by the above resolution complies with Kyrgyzstan's legislation both in terms of its conditions, provisions and legal powers of those, who have signed each of these documents: on behalf of the government of the Republic -- the state concern "Kyrgyz-Altyn" [gold], the Kumtor-Gold company, the Kumtor-Mounting company, and the Kumtor Operation company. In accordance with this resolution, each side is granted all powers and [word indistinct] benefits, which are envisaged by the legislation of Kyrgyzstan and documents of the Kumtor-Gold project. State bodies, organizations, enterprises, and officials should take all the nessessary steps so as to implement provisions of the document and render comprehensive support and assistance in the implementation of the Kumtor-Gold project in accordance with the relevant documents. The abovementioned resolution, will operate during the time of the Kumtor-Gold project's work. Republican ministries, state committees and departments should ensure proper control over activities of the joint venture Kumtor in accordance with a procedure, which is stipulated by Kyrgyzstan's existing legislation. The resolution is signed by Kyrgyz Prime Minister Abbas Dzhumagulov.
