Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bushehr, Jan. 19, IRNA -- The amphibious maneuvers of operational units of the Islamic Revolution's Guards Corps (IRGC), code named 'Martyr Raisali Delvari' continued in the waters of the northern Persian Gulf here Wednesday. The four-day maneuvers, started last Monday, aim at achieving the pre-determined objectives and improving the combat power of the IRGC forces. Units of the air force of the Islamic Republic army are also participating in the maneuvers.
