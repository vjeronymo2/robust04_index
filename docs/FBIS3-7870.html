Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Polish Ministry of Foreign Affairs [MSZ] is deeply concerned about the serious increase in tension in the Middle East. At the same time, the MSZ expresses its profound condemnation of the act of terror that took place at the Hebron mosque, a statement issued by the Ministry says. According to the Polish MSZ, this tragic incident and its consequences have harmed the atmosphere of the peace process in the Middle East and are threatening to undermine the results achieved so far.
