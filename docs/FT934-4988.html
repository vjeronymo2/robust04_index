931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Clayhithe sells Aylesbury site Clayhithe, the investment company, has entered into a conditional agreement for the disposal of a 14 acre development site in Aylesbury, Bucks, to Persimmon Homes, a subsidiary of the York-based housebuilder for Pounds 3.05m cash, payable in three instalments.
