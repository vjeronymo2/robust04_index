BFN [Text] Indonesia hopes that the PRC Government does not doubt its determination and ability to swiftly cope with the riots in Medan, North Sumatera, which have threatened the safety of the ethnic Chinese community. Foreign Minister Ali Alatas said this in Jakarta today in response to a statement issued by (Wu Chiaming), spokesman of the PRC Foreign Ministry, calling on the Indonesian Government to immediately curb the demonstrations by workers in Medan which led to anti-Chinese riots. The Indonesian Government's determination to deal with the riots was reflected in the remarks by President Suharto and other high-ranking officials who sought to take strong action against the rioters because the demonstrations led to destruction acts based on the sensitive SARA [tribal, religious, racial, and intergrouping conflicts] issues.
