Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] According to our military correspondent, South Lebanese Army [SLA] positions in al-Qantarah, al-Shumariyah, Sujud, and 'Arab Salim came under shell and rocket fire this afternoon from the terrorists' positions north of the border area. The SLA and Israeli forces retaliated, scoring direct hits on the sources of fire. The Amal Movement admitted that terrorist Hasan 'Ali Amin 'Awwadi was killed today.
