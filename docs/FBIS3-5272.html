Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, March 7 (KPL) -- Cambodian National Assembly President Samdech Chea Sim, on March 5, afternoon visited Lao PDR [Lao People's Democratic Republic] Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon at his office. Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon and Samdech Chea Sim discussed several issues concerning the development of the relations and cooperation between the two countries during the process of renovation. The Lao prime minister attached value to the visit to Laos by the Cambodian National Assembly president referring to it as a new landmark in the friendly and neigbourly relations between the two countries. Both affirmed the continued strengthening of the traditional relations of friendship and solidarity between the governments [words indistinct] of Laos and Cambodia. Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon also spoke of the unchanged stance of the Lao PDR government in support of the process of national reconciliation as well as the task of national development in the Kingdom of Cambodia, making it an independent, peaceful, neutral, non-aligned and prosperous country having friendly relations with all countries, particulaly its neigbours.
