930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / Management (The Growing Business): Extending support for young people - In a Nutshell The Prince's Youth Business Trust, which provides loans, grants and business advice to unemployed or disadvantaged people aged 18 to 25, is to extend its support to 29-year-olds from July 1. Many of the unemployed are in this older age group, the trust said. PYBT, 5 Cleveland Place, London SW1Y 6JJ. Tel. 071 321 6500.
