December 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: EL MONTE;

REVITALIZATION GRANT OKD

The City Council approved $105,000 in community development grant money for its Main Street revitalization program.

Mayor Don McMillen and other council members praised the program, part of a plan started five years ago to upgrade the older downtown area of El Monte.

The Main Street project, begun last year, concentrates on the Valley Mall along Valley Boulevard between Ramona and Santa Anita avenues. The money pays for an office and staff to attract major retailers to the mall.

"We all feel progress has been substantial the last two years," Planning Director Harold Johanson said. "We want to keep the ball rolling, so to speak, in the business district."
