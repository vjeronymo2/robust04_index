941019 FT 19 OCT 94 / Observer: Rail strike This year, the shiny autos at October's Birmingham motor show have been joined - apparently for the first time - by a train. Or at least a pale imitation. East Coast Main Line is exhibiting a mock-up of one of its 125 mph locomotives in an effort to 'push the boat out' for the railway. No doubt in an effort to attract customers floating between road and track.
