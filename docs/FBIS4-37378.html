BFN [Text] This afternoon, the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] directed artillery fire at targets in the northern part of the security zone's central sector. This morning, targets between al-Nabatiyah and Jazzin were shelled. Beirut Radio reported that Israeli planes flew over al-Nabatiyah and Iqlim al-Tuffah. Our correspondent was told that several katyusha rockets and mortar shells landed near al-Mansuriyah in the eastern sector.
