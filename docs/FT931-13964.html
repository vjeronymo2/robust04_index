930121 FT 21 JAN 93 / Croatia and Slovenia join IMF CROATIA and Slovenia were yesterday admitted to the International Monetary Fund, allowing the two former Yugoslav republics access to loans from the IMF and to join its sister institution, the World Bank, writes George Graham in Washington. Croatia will have a quota of SDR261.6m (Pounds 237.51m) once the IMF's general quota increase is completed, while Slovenia's quota will be SDR150.5m.
