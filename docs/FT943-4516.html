940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Northern Industrial Northern Industrial Improvement Trust total income fell from Pounds 493,896 to Pounds 469,345 in the year to March 31. Profit after all charges, including tax, came to Pounds 312,387 (Pounds 336,338). A final dividend of 16p is proposed, making an unchanged total of 25p on earnings of 25.6p (27.55p).
