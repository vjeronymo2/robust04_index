931127 FT 27 NOV 93 / Yemenis kidnap US envoy A US diplomat has been kidnapped in the Yemeni capital Sanaa, apparently by tribesmen from a lawless region at odds with an American oil company, Reuter reports from Sanaa. Mr Haynes Mahoney, director of the US Information Service office in Sanaa, was snatched on Thursday evening as he went to his car after a British Airways party at a downtown hotel.
