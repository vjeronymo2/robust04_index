October 19, 1990, Friday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: OXNARD;

WOMAN ARRESTED IN THEFT, BOY'S KIDNAP

Cheryl Renae Dawson, 20, of Oxnard was arrested Thursday in connection with the kidnaping of a 6-month-old child and on suspicion of grand theft, police said.

The child, Steven Burton, was safely returned to his grandmother, Margaret Lewis, 46, Thursday afternoon by a woman she didn't know after he was missing overnight, Oxnard police said.

Lewis had left the child in Dawson's care Wednesday night, and when she returned at 9:30 a.m. Thursday, the child was missing along with Lewis' television set, microwave and video recorder, police said.

The child's parents are in the U.S. Army and are stationed in Germany. GERRY BRAILO SPENCER
