Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The government has noted with concern the reported death of a number of Ghanaians among other nationalities following an incident in a police compound in Libreville, Gabon. The acting Ghana high commissioner in Lagos, whose mission oversees Gabon, has been directed by the minister of foreign affairs to proceed to Gabon immediately to express government's concern over the incident and to ask the authorities for clarification and information and the circumstances surrounding the incident. He is also to conduct an independent investigation and to report to the minister by the end of the week.
