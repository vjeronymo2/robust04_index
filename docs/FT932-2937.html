930616 FT 16 JUN 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazil SAO PAULO closed 1.6 per cent higher in heavy trading after investors reacted positively to the austerity measures announced by the Brazilian government on Monday night. The Bovespa index was 730 firmer at 45,893. News that the government would speed up the sale of its power utility system lifted Eletrobras 8.2 per cent to Cr6,600.
