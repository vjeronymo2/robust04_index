BFN [Text] According to the Haiti Press Agency, AHP, the situaton remains confusing in the northern Haitian village of Le Borgne following a shoot-out that took place last weekend between a number of armed civilians and a number of military from the Le Borgne outpost. The Haitian military authorities have yet to make public any official version of the incidents in Le Borgne. AHP says that reports by Inter Press Service mention several people being killed during the attack, without specifying which side suffered the alleged losses. AHP reports, further, that a number of sources, which coincide, make mention of many arrests in the Le Borgne area, including that of Le Borgne mayor Belizaire Fils-Aime and a rural policeman. Several houses were also reportedly burned down. It should be noted that there is no telephone link between Port-au-Prince and Le Borgne.
