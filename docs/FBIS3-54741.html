Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ruslan Khasbulatov, the former speaker of the Russian parliament, who was arrested in connection with the events of 3 and 4 October in Moscow, and his lawyer Vladimir Fomichev, have put in an application to have the criminal charges quashed and to be released from custody owing to the absence of a corpus delicti in the actions of Khasbulatov. It was reported to INTERFAX today by lawyer Fomichev that since the arrest on 4 October Khasbulatov has still not had evidence submitted to him confirming his guilt.
