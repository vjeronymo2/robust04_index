Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Following the head of state's message of 14 January, several political parties have been analyzing it. The Chistian Democrats Party's [PDC] interim chairman, Boboliko, chaired a meeting over the weekend. After the meeting Vice Chairman (Mbesi Mbenatubi) spoke to reporters. He told reporters that no son of Zaire can any longer take it upon himself alone to decide for the country. According to Mr. Mbenatubi, one cannot stand on the signing of a draft protocol agreement to dissolve the High Council of the Republic, which emanated from the sovereign national conference.
