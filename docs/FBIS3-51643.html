Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] The General Command of the Haitian Armed Forces advises that it received the members of the office of the Haitian people's Congress for National Salvation on 20 January. During this meeting, the delegates informed the General Command that representatives of the 41 districts of the Republic had voted final resolutions condemning the embargo and its effects, and demanding the formation of a government of public security. They also informed the military institution that the original of those resolutions had been entrusted to the Chamber of Deputies, but presented a copy of this document to the General Command. This information was released in a communique from General Headquarters.
