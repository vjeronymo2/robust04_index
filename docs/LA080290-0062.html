August 2, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SWEET RUN WINS AT ARLINGTON

Sweet Run overtook 8-5 favorite Mighty Milk nearing the wire Wednesday for a half-length victory in the $26,500 Magnetic Type Purse at Arlington International Racecourse.

Monocle Miss, a 23-1 outsider in the 1 1/16-mile turf race, finished third in the field of seven fillies and mares.

The winner, ridden by Shane Sellers, paid $10, $4.20 and $3.20 and was timed in 1:45 1/5.
