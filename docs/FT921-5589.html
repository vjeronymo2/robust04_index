920303 FT 03 MAR 92 / Royal Viking Queen arrives in London Expensive flotation: the world's most costly cruise ship, the Pounds 70m Royal Viking Queen, arrived in London yesterday on its maiden voyage from Hamburg to Miami. Passengers pay an average Pounds 9,500 for a two-week trip. The ship had to travel up the Thames and under Tower Bridge stern first because of the way it had to negotiate the port of London.
