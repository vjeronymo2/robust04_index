BFN [Text] Cairo -- On the sidelines of the meetings of the 11th nonaligned foreign ministers's conference, Syrian Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' today received Cypriot Foreign Minister Alexandros Mikhailidhis. During the meeting, discussions dealt with the situation in the region, the Middle East peace process, the situation in Cyprus, and bilateral relations. The two officials exchanged views on topics listed on the agenda. Foreign Minister al-Shar' also received 'Abd-al-'Aziz Kamilov, Uzbek first deputy foreign minister. Discussions dealt with bilateral relations and ways of promoting them.
