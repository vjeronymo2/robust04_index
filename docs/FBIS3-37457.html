Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report under the "NEGA Reports" rubric: "Estonia"] [Text] The first picketing of the Russian troops headquarters building in Tallinn took place at the call of the Estonian Party of National Independence. Party activists called for picketing the center of Russian troops in Estonia every Monday until they have been completely withdrawn from the republic's territory. Taking part in the action were many State Assembly deputies including former foreign minister Trivimi Velliste. All in all, nearly 200 people took part in the picketing.
