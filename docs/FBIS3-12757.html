Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' received Faruq Qaddumi, head of the PLO Political Department, this evening. PLO Executive Committee member Muhammad Ghunaym and PLO representative in Damascus Mahmud al-Khalidi attended the meeting. Talks dealt with the situation in the region and the occupied Arab territories in the wake of the massacre the Israelis perpetrated in the Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron and its impact on the peace process.
