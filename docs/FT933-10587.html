930803 FT 03 AUG 93 / International Company News: US oil concern sells unit stake OCCIDENTAL Petroleum, the US oil group, is to sell 20 per cent of Occidental Exploration of Argentina to Astra Compania Argentina de Petroleo for about Dollars 24m, Reuter reports from Los Angeles. Occidental said Astra had also agreed to buy for Dollars 10m an option, exercisable in July 1994, to acquire the remaining 80 per cent. Occidental Exploration on Argentina owns Occidental's Argentine oil and gas operating interests. At the end of 1992, it had 35m barrels of proven reserves.
