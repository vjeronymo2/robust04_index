920213 FT 13 FEB 92 / Japanese premier rebuffs appeal for aid to Soviet Union Prime Minister Kiichi Miyazawa yesterday rebuffed a renewed appeal from German Foreign Minister Hans-Dietrich Genscher for large-scale aid to the former Soviet Union, AP-DJ reports from Tokyo. Instead, Mr Miyazawa asked Mr Genscher to support Japan's demand that Russia return the Kuriles, four small islands seized by the Soviet Union at the end of the Second World War. Mr Genscher is on a three-day visit to Tokyo.
