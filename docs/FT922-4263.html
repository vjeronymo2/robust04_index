920606 FT 06 JUN 92 / International Company News: Correction - Hongkong Telecom IN yesterday's Financial Times the notes to a table showing the effect of lower prices for international telephone calls on Hongkong Telecommunications' earnings were incorrect. The words 'optimistic' and 'pessimistic' were transposed during compilation. In addition, Hongkong Telecom's international revenues in 1992 were HKDollars 11,084m. The corrected table will be published in Monday's edition.
