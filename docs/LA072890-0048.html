July 28, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

CLOSING OF ICE SKATING RINK OPPOSED

There are only three full-size ice rinks in the Los Angeles area and only two with seating for events.

One is going to lose its lease shortly, one is likely to be sold to settle family differences, and the third is Pickwick in Burbank.

That a rink that has served the public by providing wholesome activities for 30 years suddenly is hopeless is unbelievable.

Please join the Save the Ice Committee in helping to keep sports available by doing whatever you can to keep Pickwick open.

MARY GRACE CONTRERAS

Los Angeles
