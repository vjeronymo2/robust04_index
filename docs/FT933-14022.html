930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / European plan for Open University THE OPEN UNIVERSITY is to run courses for students throughout mainland Europe from its regional centre in Newcastle. It expects the number of students studying overseas covering all subjects taught in English to rise from 2,000 to 10,000 by 1996. Courses for the university's Russian and Hungarian students taught in their own languages will be administered by academic institutions in their respective countries.
