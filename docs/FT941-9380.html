940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Loctite buy Loctite UK, a wholly owned subsidiary of Loctite Corporation, has acquired Plastic Padding Holdings for an estimated Pounds 10m. Loctite Corporation is a speciality chemicals company whose principal markets include the industrial, automotive and consumer sectors. Plastic Padding, which achieved sales of Pounds 10.8m in 1993, is a producer of automotive aftermarket chemicals.
