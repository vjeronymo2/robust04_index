Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 22 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea received a solidarity message from Jose Eduardo Dos Santos, president of the Republic of Angola, supporting the struggle of the Korean people for peace and a peaceful reunification of the Korean peninsula. The message dated March 5 says the Angolan Government follows with deep concern the great efforts made by President Kim Il-song and the DPRK Government for the reunification of Korea and the regional peace and security. The message expresses the conviction that the question of the Korean peninsula will be successfully resolved by the energetic efforts of the DPRK Government in conformity with the interests of the Korean people. In the message he expresses the firm belief that the reunification of Korea, the most acute problem, would be solved successfully through dialogue under the wise guidance of President Kim Il-song with a far-reaching plan.
