BFN [Text] Moscow May 28 TASS -- Russian Defence Minister Pavel Grachev returned to Moscow on Saturday from Belgium where he was on an official visit. During the visit Grachev addressed NATO leaders in Brussels to present them with Russia's concept of partnership with the West and its new military doctrine and held talks with NATO officials. On the way back to Russia Grachev made a stopover in Wuensdorf where the Western group of Russian troops is stationed to discuss the withdrawal of troops due to be completed this August.
