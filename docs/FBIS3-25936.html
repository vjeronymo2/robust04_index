Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday afternoon Governor Chen Shineng, other provincial leading comrades, and people in charge of the relevant departments took part in the discussions of various delegations to solicit their opinions and suggestions on government work. To make the People's Congress session more successful, the secretariat of the session had collected the delegation's opinions and demands in advance. Prior to their visit, these provincial leading comrades had summoned people in charge of the relevant departments to a meeting to clarify that they should carefully listen to the deputies' opinions and factually answer their questions. Governor Chen Shineng listened to the opinions and demands of the delegation of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference. Three members of the delegation raised suggestions on highway construction, railway construction, grain and cotton production, animal husbandry development, assisting the poor, and combating corruption for a clean administration. Governor Chen Shineng said that he would seriously consider their suggestions. People in charge of the relevant departments also factually answered their questions. Vice Governor Mo Shiren went to Liupanshui's delegation to solicit their opinions. Members of the delegation requested listing Liupanshi City as an open city in the hinterland. Mo Shiren and comrades in charge of the relevant department answered their questions. Vice Governors Hu Xiansheng, Zhang Yuqin, and Yao Jiyuan respectively went to the delegations of Tongren, Qiannan, and Zunyi to solicit their opinions.
