Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 17 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The independent newspaper CAPITAL informs that according to Vice President of the Mongolbank O. Chuluunbat, the central bank and other commercial banks of Mongolia are carrying out dealers operations including the arbitration ones at the international currency market. But he told nothing neither about the time since which such international arbitration operations having been stopped by the autumn of 1991 for the reason of the heavy losses relating to the hard currency reserves of the country nor by whose permission they are being carried out. In the course of the first judicial hearing on the sensational case concerning the dealers and gold taken place by the end of June to the beginning of July 1993 in the city court of the Mongolia capital it was announced that the dealers transactions at the international market are not still being allowed yet.
