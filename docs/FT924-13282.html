921016 FT 16 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Bourne End Props Bourne End Properties, the investment and development group, reduced its pre-tax loss by Pounds 250,000 to Pounds 543,000 for the first half of 1992. Interest charges were Pounds 1.99m, against Pounds 2.44m. Rental income was down at Pounds 1.89m (Pounds 1.96m) as a result of property sales last year but there was no property sale profit this time, against Pounds 57,000. Losses per share were 6.5p (9.6p). The interim dividend is again passed. Last year there was a single final payment of 1p.
