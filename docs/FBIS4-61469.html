CSO [Unattributed article under the heading "Panorama": "Iraqi Embassy in Azerbaijan Is Closing"] [Text] In the course of a meeting with Minister of Foreign Affairs of Azerbaijan Hasan Hasanov, Faruq Salman Da'ud, head of the Iraqi Embassy in Baku, stated that the Iraqi diplomatic mission in Azerbaijan is being temporarily closed because of the difficult economic situation that has developed recently in that country. As the foreign policy department noted while informing the ANS-PRESS agency about this, F.S. Da'ud stressed that in view of a shortage of foreign exchange funds in Iraq, the government of that country has also been compelled to wind down the operation of diplomatic missions in [word illegible], Hungary, Romania, Poland, India, and a majority of African states. With reference to the significance of reinforcing Azerbaijani-Iraqi relations, the diplomat expressed the hope that the financial situation of his country will shortly make it possible to resume operation of the Iraqi Embassy in Baku.
