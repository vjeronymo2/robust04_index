930821 FT 21 AUG 93 / Finance and the Family: Australian fund seeks UK investors - At a glance A new Australian-listed closed end investment fund is trying to recruit UK and European investors. The fund is called Investment Australia Ltd and is managed by Dicksons. The company will invest in the 50 leading stocks, representing 75 per cent of the Australian market's capitalisation. The fund will have a dividend yield of 3.25 per cent (which can be paid in sterling if the investment is large enough) and a management charge of 0.5 per cent. The UK broker is the London-based Panmure Gordon.
