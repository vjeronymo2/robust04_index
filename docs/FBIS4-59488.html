BFN [Text] Chairman Yasir 'Arafat, chairman of the Palestinian national authority, today issued a decision stipulating that Friday of each week is to be an official public holiday in Palestine. The following is the text of the decision: In accordance with the Executive Committee's decision, and according to the prerogatives invested in him, the chairman of the Palestinian national authority, chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, decides the following: Firstly, Friday of each week to be an official public holiday in Palestine. Secondly, this decision takes effect as from this date, and those involved in implementing it shall be informed.
