Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Officials of the Republics of Azerbaijan and of Armenia have reached a new agreement on ending the conflict between the two countries. The Central News Unit reports from Baku that on the basis of an agreement reached between the heads of the Armenian and Azerbaijani parliaments, Armenian forces will withdraw from occupied Azerbaijani territories within 20 days. The television of the Republic of Azerbaijan has announced that on the basis of this agreement, the Lachinskiy corridor will remain in the hands of Armenian forces as a contact route to Nagorno-Karabakh, while Armenia is to lift the economic siege on the Autonomous Republic of Nakhichevan. The Azerbaijani and Armenian foreign ministers are to meet in Moscow to discuss the new agreements.
