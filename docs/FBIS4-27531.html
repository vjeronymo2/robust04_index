BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 27 (KCNA) -- Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly Yang Hyong-sop sent a message of greetings to Pius Msekwa upon his election as speaker of the National Assembly of the United Republic of Tanzania. Expressing the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two parliaments will grow stronger and develop, the message wished the speaker greater success in his work for national unity and the prosperity of the country.
