930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Waterglade debt restructured The debt restructuring of Waterglade International Holdings with Bank of America was completed last week. Under terms of the agreement the bank has bought from the company the Meridian Shopping Centre, Havant. Waterglade will pay Pounds 1.75m to the bank over the next two years; the first instalment was paid on completion. At the end of the period, provided all payments have been made, the bank will waive repayment of the remaining balance. During the period Waterglade is precluded, inter alia, from paying dividends without the consent of the bank.
