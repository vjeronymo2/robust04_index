BFN [Unattributed article: "Brinkman Intends To Stay On in Controversial Job"] [Excerpt] Elco Brinkman, CDA [Christian Democratic Appeal Party] leader, for the time being, will stay on as a board member of Arscop BV, a company that manages the accounts of a firm whose management is suspected of tax fraud. When the company was raided a pistol was also found. "I am in contact with my fellow board members about this situation and the accusation that has been made," Brinkman said last weekend. In a program broadcast Friday evening [8 April] on the KRO [Catholic Radio Broadcasting Company] series "Reporter," it was maintained that Brinkman must have known about the alleged fraud.
