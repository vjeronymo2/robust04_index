Language: Persian Article Type:CSO [Text] Zahedan--IRNA. 3 Azar [21 December]--The Sistan va Baluchestan General Office of Ports and Shipping has opened a radio communications station in Chah Bahar with an advanced system. This naval communications system can provide telegraph, telephone, and telex service between domestic and foreign representative offices, shipping companies and foreign ships in all the four naval districts designated by the International Maritime Organization. At this station's inauguration ceremony, the executive director of the Ports and Shipping Organization gave a speech. He said: The Port of Chah Bahar is quite important as a transit area for goods moving between Afghanistan, Central Asia, and Iran. He said the communications system created in this port is one of the most advanced maritime communications facilities in the world. He said: This system will be installed and put into operation within two months by domestic specialists and experts.
