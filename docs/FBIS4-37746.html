BFN [Text] The national dialogue and the economic and political reforms [in the country] were on the agenda of the talks between Foreign Minister Mohamed Salah Dembri and U.S. officials. More details on Dembri's talks in the United States with Wahiba Benchihab: [Benchihab] Mr. Dembri asserted yesterday at his meeting with Arab journalists working in the United States, that his talks with U.S. officials dealt with the national dialogue and the efforts under way to reach a national concord. He reiterated, furthermore, that Washington supports the economic and political reforms under way in Algeria and is very interested in the process of the transitional period. Mr. Dembri also reviewed, at a dinner given by the Senate Foreign Affairs Committee, the efforts of the Algerian authorities to broaden dialogue in the search for national concord and the outcome of his talks with U.S. officials.
