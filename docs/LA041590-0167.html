April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEW HOMES ON VIEW: LOS ANGELES COUNTY

CALABASAS: Phase 1 of Palatino, 22 of 120 2-4-bedroom single-family houses; 3 plans; 1,463-1,877 square feet; $393,000-$499,000; builder, the Lusk Co.; July move-ins; $267.22 monthly association dues for gate-guarded community, slopes, common-area landscaping; open daily 10 a.m.-6 p.m. at 24807 W. Calle Cedro, reached from the southbound Parkway Calabasas exit of the Ventura (101) Freeway.

GRANADA HILLS: Phase 3 of Cascade Canyon, 10 of 107 4-5-bedroom single-family houses; 4 plans; 2,700-3,800 square feet, $464,000-$611,000; builder, Watt Pacific; $75 monthly association dues; September/October move-ins; open 10 a.m.-5 p.m. daily at 12462 Jacqueline Place, north of Rinaldi Street, reached from the northbound Balboa Boulevard exit of the Simi Valley (118) Freeway.
