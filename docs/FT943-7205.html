940824 FT 24 AUG 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Gold shares climbed off lows by the close to end little changed in a session marked by a lack of news. The gold index shed 1 to 2,245, industrials gained 24 to 6,617 and the overall index added 11 to 5,858. De Beers slipped 20 cents to R109.80.
