Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] Senior General Than Shwe, chairman of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] of the Union of Burma, accepted the credentials of Mr. Mordechai Karni, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of Israel to the Union of Myanmar, at the Ministry of Defense's Dagon House at 1000 today. Present at the occasion were Lieutenant General Khin Nyunt, SLORC secretary-1; U Ohn Gyaw, minister of foreign affairs; and Thura U Aung Htet, director general of Protocol Department.
