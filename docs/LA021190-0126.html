February 11, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

AIR TERMINAL, AIR TRAFFIC AND NAME CHOICE

Re Pat Mott's humorous article about the naming of the John Wayne Airport/Terminal (Jan. 28): When amused visitors ask why an airport was named after a movie star, I point to the Saddleback Mountains and tell them the county supervisors named them after John Wayne's horse.

That's usually good for a horselaugh.

EPH ROBECK

Laguna Hills
