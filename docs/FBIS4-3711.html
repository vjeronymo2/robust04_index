BFN [Text] India and China have discussed further measures for the maintenance of peace and tranquility along the Line of Control and border areas. During the two-day meeting of India-China experts group which concluded in Beijing yesterday, the two countries expressed satisfaction at the peaceful situation on the Line of Actual Control following the confidence-building measures initiated by the two countries. The two sides reached an agreement on the work regulations of the experts group spelling out the mandate, tasks, and modalities to be followed. The document was signed yesterday.
