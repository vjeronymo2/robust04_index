940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / Chaplin costume fetches Pounds 35,600 The costume worn by Charlie Chaplin in his film The Great Dictator, complete with jackboots and swastika armbands, sold for Pounds 35,600 at Sotheby's auction of pop and movie memorabilia yesterday. Top price at the auction, which continues today, was Pounds 51,000 for an extensive archive of Jimi Hendrix material.
