921121 FT 21 NOV 92 / Finance & The Family: At A Glance - Setback for smaller companies Smaller company shares suffered a setback this week, ending their recent rally. The Hoare Govett Index (capital gains version) fell 0.6 per cent from 1111.5 to 1104.34 over the week to November 19, while the County Index dropped 0.3 per cent from 857.9 to 854.99 over the same period.
