920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / World Commodities Prices: Jute and Cotton JUTE August/September: C and F Dundee; BTC Dollars 395, BWC Dollars 405, BTD Dollars 360, BWD Dollars 360. C and F Antwerp: BTC Dollars 380, BWC Dollars 380, BTD Dollars 350, BWD 350. COTTON Liverpool-spot and shipment sales for the week ended 7 August amounted to 95 tonnes, against 128 tonnes in the previous week. Slow offtake brought relatively low demand, but support was mentioned in West African and Russian styles.
