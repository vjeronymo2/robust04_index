BFN [Text] Cairo, 16 Apr (MENA)--Ending a 20-year rift, the Coptic Orthodox Church and the Ethiopian Orthodox Church have agreed to normalize relations, according to a source at the papal seat of the Coptic Church. The Coptic synod, led by Pope Shanudah III, approved a protocol regulating relations between the two churches. The protocol was prepared by a joint committee and discussed by both sides. The protocol will open a new chapter in relations between the two churches, stressing their amity, proximity, and common traditions of faith, according to the same source.
