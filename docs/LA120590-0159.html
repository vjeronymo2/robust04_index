December 5, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

FOOTBALL CARDS ON WAY TO TROOPS

About 50 tons of football cards will be flown to U.S. forces in Saudi Arabia.

Pro Set, Inc. is sending more than a million packs of its NFL Pro Set cards as Christmas presents. Among the specials is a Vince Lombardi hologram card.

The cards were trucked from Dallas to an air base in California for shipment to the Persian Gulf, Pro Set President Lud Denny said today.
