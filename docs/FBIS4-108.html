BFN [Text] The movement of government and Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] forces to assembly areas has become increasingly slower over the past few days. Since the beginning of this week, only 68 government troops and four from Renamo arrived at assembly areas. A total of 33,369 soldiers from the government had been confined by yesterday. This represents 54 percent of the total number of soldiers to be confined. On Renamo's side, a total of 14,809 soldiers have already been confined. This represents 77 percent of troops to be confined.
