BFN [Text] Mas'ud Barzani, leader of the Kurdistan Democratic Party in Iraqi Kurdistan, met in Irbil today with Shaykh 'Uthman 'Abd-al-'Aziz, leader of the Islamic Movement in Kurdistan. Barzani said that implementing a cease-fire in northern Iraq is not possible without 'Uthman's approval. An Islamic Movement spokesman reported that the two leaders emphasized their united position on the current fighting in Iraqi Kurdistan. The spokesman added that they also discussed the cease-fire in Irbil and the prospect of forming an executive committee, made up of representatives of the three warring parties in northern Iraq and the Iraqi National Congress, to implement a cease-fire in al-Sulaymaniyah.
