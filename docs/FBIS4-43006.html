BFN [Text] Wiesbaden (DPA) -- During the first quarter of 1994, the west German GDP increased by 2.1 percent compared with the same period last year. Compared with the last quarter of 1993 the economic output of the west German economy rose by 0.5 percent. These figures were announced by the Federal Statistical Office in Wiesbaden on Tuesday [7 June]. According to the statistics, the increase of the GDP compared with the same period last year is the first increase since the last quarter of 1992.
