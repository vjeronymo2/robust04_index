930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Headline Book advances 68% Pre-tax profits at Headline Book Publishing climbed 68 per cent from Pounds 1.22m to Pounds 2.05m in 1992. Mr Tim Hely-Hutchinson, managing director, said it had been a difficult year. Sales in 1993 had started better than expected, rising between 1 per cent and 2 per cent on the previous year. Operating profits rose to Pounds 2.03m (Pounds 1.24m), including Pounds 354,000 from the Bookpoint acquisition. Group turnover increased 40 per cent to Pounds 15.7m (Pounds 11.3m), with Bookpoint contributing Pounds 1.7m. Earnings per share rose 47 per cent to 11.9p (8.1p) and a proposed final dividend of 3p gives a total of 4.5p (3p). The figures were prepared under FRS 3. The reported 1991 pre-tax figure was Pounds 1.3m.
