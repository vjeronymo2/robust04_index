Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pakistan has called upon the United Nations Human Rights Commission to investigate the massive atrocities unleashed by Indian forces in occupied Jammu and Kashmir. Speaking at the Commission's current session in Geneva, Pakistani delegate Mr. Shafqat Kakakhel accused India of misusing the right of state to protect their territorial integrity for justifying brutal repression against the population of Jammu and Kashmir, whose final status has to be determined in accordance with the wishes of the people of Jammu and Kashmir.
