930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Oliver Resources OLIVER RESOURCES: Applications for the 166.2m shares made available under the open offer have been received in respect of 27.9m shares (16.8 per cent). Of the balance 84.1m shares have been placed at the open offer price of 2.25p, resulting in the raising of some Pounds 2.5m.
