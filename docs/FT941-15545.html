940114 FT 14 JAN 94 / Technology (Worth Watching): Squeezing in the compact kitchen An Italian company which makes kitchen appliances for the US space shuttle has turned its attention to the business and domestic market with a compact unit combining a refrigerator, freezer and microwave. Taking up only a quarter of a square metre, it is designed mainly for offices, hospitals and surgeries, hotels, factories and apartments. Berloni's product, called Kitchoo, needs only one plug and is mobile. Priced at FFr5,000 (Pounds 574), it was developed after a study by ESSEC, the French business school. Kitchoo Group: France, 45 488383.
