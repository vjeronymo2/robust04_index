BFN ["Possible Attempt on the Life of Archbishop Janullatos by Greek Terroristic Groups" -- ATA headline] [Text] Tirana, May 5 (ATA) -- "There are voices about a possible attempt on the life of the Archbishop Janullatos by Greek terroristic groups in order to aggravate the relations between Albania and Greece," DITA INFORMACION reports today. REPUBLIKA of May 1 also writes about this possibility comparing the Archbishop Janullatos with the Archduke Ferdinand, whose killing served as a cause for the beginning of the Second World War [as received].
