March 1, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: LOS ALAMITOS;

CASA YOUTH SHELTER RECEIVES $2,000 GIFT

The Casa Youth Shelter, a temporary home for runaway teen-agers and other youths in crisis, has received a $2,000 donation from depositors at Union Federal Savings Bank in Seal Beach.

This is third consecutive year that the bank's depositors have contributed money to the shelter. Under a charity program, depositors can designate nonprofit organizations as beneficiaries of annual donations based on the size of an account.
