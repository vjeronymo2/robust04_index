BFN [Text] President Hashemi-Rafsanjani today separately received the French and Indonesian ambassadors at the end of their missions in Tehran. According to a Central News Unit report, at his meeting with the president, French Ambassador Mr. De Verdiere said: France is fully aware of Iran's important role in the region and the world. It attaches a great deal of importance to the expansion of its diplomatic, economic, and cultural cooperation with this country, especially in the construction period. Thanking the French ambassador for his good work toward the expansion of cooperation between the two countries Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani expressed the hope that, with more consultation between the two countries' authorities, the way to the expansion of ties will be smoothed in the future. Also at a meeting with Mr. Sudarsono, the Indonesian ambassador, the president described the recent visit by that country's president to Iran as very effective in deepening mutual ties. Referring to Indonesia's important role in the Nonaligned Movement and the religious and cultural common denominators of the peoples of the two countries the president welcomed future expansion of cooperation. The Indonesian ambassador said Iran was important owing to her possession of rich natural and human resources. He said: I have gained the best experience of my life witnessing Iran's great progress. I hope that experience will be effective in further deepening the cooperation between Iran and Indonesia.
