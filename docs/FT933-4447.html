930909 FT 09 SEP 93 / World News in Brief: College to sell a second masterpiece Royal Holloway College, criticised in February for selling a Turner landscape to a US museum for Pounds 11m, is to auction Gainsborough's 'Peasants Going to Market' (detail left). The college in Egham, Surrey, will use the sale proceeds to restore and maintain its listed Founder's Building. Auctioneer Christie's said the Gainsborough was offered to the National and Tate galleries, and to the National Gallery of Scotland, but failed to attract any positive interest.
