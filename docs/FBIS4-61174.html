BFN [Text] Oleh Bilous, Ukrainian ambassador to the United States, has met with Nicholas (Bergs), U.S. National Security Council director. During their conversation, the officials concentrated on the implementation of economic agreements reached during the official visit of Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk in the United States in March of this year. In particular, they talked about the next visit of the U.S. [word indistinct] economic delegation to Kiev. Nicholas (Bergs) briefed the Ukrainian ambassador on plans and the agenda of the American delegation in Kiev. During the negotiations in Kiev, it is expected that problems will be discussed at the highest level related to the progress of economic reforms in Ukraine and U.S. aid intended to solve those problems. The Ukrainian and American officials will consider and try to agree upon specific terms and forms for extending financial and economic aid to Ukraine to implement concrete economic projects as part of the development of the market economy. Major emphasis at the next negotiations in Kiev will be put on issues related to intensifying trade between both countries and acceleration of U.S. investments into Ukrainian economy.
