BFN [Text] President Yeltsin has signed a decree "On Measures to Protect the Interests of Citizens During the Transition From Voucher to Cash Privatization," setting the rules to privatize state enterprises until a new state program for post-voucher privatization is adopted. The decree says that after July 1 the shares of enterprises which have been registered before that deadline shall be sold at auctions and stock exchanges. The decree says that the labor collectives of enterprises corporatized before July 1 retain all the benefits established by the privatization program. The post-voucher privatization program setting the rules of privatization as of July 1, 1994 is to be put into effect by a presidential decree before June 31. However, according to the Deputy Chairman of the State Property Committee Dmitry Vasiliev the program had not yet been submitted to President Yeltsin for signature late Monday night.
