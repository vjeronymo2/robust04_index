September 22, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

L.A. FANS ARE THRILLED (IN THEIR SPECIAL WAY) THAT RAIDERS WILL STAY

It's sure nice to find out that all the hard-working politicians and others interested in community betterment have finally got Al Davis' signature on a contract to ensure "our" hallowed Raiders playing in the Coliseum until at least somebody comes by with a better offer.

Nice job, Spectacor, Coliseum Commission and and all concerned. Look for me in Anaheim.

BOB GEORGE

Covina
