BFN [Excerpts] Deputy Defense Minister Mordekhay Gur toured Gush 'Etzyon regional council settlements today. Shilo Gal, head of the regional council, presented to Gur a detailed plan for the annexation of Gush 'Etzyon to Israeli territory in the context of the permanent arrangement. Gur said the extent to which such a plan conforms with the government's policy must be checked. Our correspondent in the territories Golan Yokhpaz reports: [Begin recording] [Yokhpaz] Gal pointed to two large maps on the wall. This is the Gush 'Etzyon area, he told Deputy Defense Minister Gur. We hereby present to you the plan to link Gush 'Etzyon to Israel's territory in the context of the permanent arrangement, he said. [passage omitted] Gal proposed to establish a settlement bloc in Gush 'Etzyon, made up of two districts linked by a corridor, and link it to Israel. For his part, Gur chose not to use the word annexation. The plan must conform with the government's resolutions, he said, and that is why I do not have a clear answer to give you. [Gur] Nobody mentioned annexation, but talked about adding settlements or expanding settlements with new neighborhoods, each one of which could be considered a settlement. Therefore, we must check very well how much this plan conforms with the government's policy. [passage omitted] [end recording]
