940411 FT 11 APR 94 / Company News (This Week): Accor - Other Companies The French hotels and tourism group will also announce last year's results on Wednesday. The group, which has been hit by recession and fierce competition in the international hotels business, has warned that it expects to show a decline in profits. Estimates of net income range from FFr550m to FFr650m (Dollars 110m), depending on exceptional items, compared with FFr802m in 1992.
