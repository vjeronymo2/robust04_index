BFN [Report by Alexander Barrios, from the "Union Radio News" newscast] [Text] There was student violence in Caracas for a fourth consecutive day. The disturbances began early Tuesday morning at Moran Avenue when students from the Jose de San Martin Vocational School began protesting the death last Thursday of German Sotillo, from the Fermin Toro School. The students also protested over the announced hike in student transportation rates which might begin within the next few days, according to Juan Landaeta, a student from the Jose de San Martin Vocational School. [passage indistinct] The students detained a grocery truck, with license plate number 624XCO, from the Diaz Supermarkets. After residents of the area looted the truck, the students burned it. Five public transportation buses were also detained for over three hours at the Jose de San Martin School. After several hours of clashes, the students tried to call for a truce in exchange for the release of some of their fellow students who had been arrested, but this did not occur. Students from the Andres Bello, Santos, Michelena, and Rafael Urdaneta Schools later joined the protests at Mexico Avenue, in front of the Andres Bello School. One store was looted during this demonstration. The Metropolitan Police are conducting investigations.
