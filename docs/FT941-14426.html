940120 FT 20 JAN 94 / UK Company News: NFC makes Pounds 2.7m French purchase NFC, through its subsidiary Exel Logistics France, has acquired Transports Leridon for FFr24m (Pounds 2.7m). Leridon, which has annual revenues of about Pounds 8.5m, is one of the leading temperature-controlled transport and distribution companies in the Paris region. It employs 140 people and operates a 10,000 sq m temperature-controlled warehouse at Rungis, near Orly, and a fleet of 60 vehicles. An initial Pounds 2m in cash has been paid with the balance of Pounds 700,000 due in January 1995.
