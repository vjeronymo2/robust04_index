940813 FT 13 AUG 94 / Finance and the Family: Investment trust schemes attract more business - At a glance Investment trust savings schemes continue to be popular with private investors who poured Pounds 122.1m into them in the first half of the year, an increase of 20 per cent on the same period last year. The Association of Investment Trust Companies, which published the figures, said that the number of regular savers (as opposed to lump sum investors who can also invest via the savings scheme) has increased every quarter since the end of 1992, to 79,000 from just under 56,000. The average amount invested by regular savers is Pounds 80 a month.
