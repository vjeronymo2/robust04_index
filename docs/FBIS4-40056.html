BFN [Excerpts] The regular session of the Presidium of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Tajikistan took place today under the chairmanship of Abdujalil Samadov. Akbar Turayev was appointed first deputy minister of culture and Sherali Hakimov was appointed deputy minister of economy and forecasting. The concept of the national school of Tajikistan was approved and a decision on reducing the number of working hours of teachers was adopted in the session. [passage omitted] Similarly, other issues relating to the social and economic development of the Republic were discussed in the session.
