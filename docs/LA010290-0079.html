January 2, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

CALIFORNIA IN BRIEF;

BERKELEY;

BUNDLE OF BABES IS GIFT OF THE '80S

An Alameda couple became parents of test-tube quadruplets on the last day of the 1980s. Ted and Maria Lusignan, who had been trying to have a baby for six years, now have three girls and a boy born in four minutes. The babies, 10 weeks premature and ranging in weight from 2 pounds, 12 ounces to 1 pound, were in stable condition in Alta Bates Hospital's intensive care unit.
