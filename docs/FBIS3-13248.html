Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Jalal Talabani, secretary general of the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan [PUK], yesterday afternoon visited the coalition forces coordination office in Zakho. Talabani was accompanied by Dr. Fu'ad Ma'sum, member of the PUK Political Bureau; (Shaykh al-Hiwazi), alternate member of the Steering Committee; and (Shart al-Qazzaz), representative of the PUK in Ankara. The discussion during the visit dealt with the Iraqi regime's recent military movements near the border area of the Kurdistan region. The brother secretary general arrived in Dahuk yesterday evening. Brother Talabani returned to liberated Kurdistan the day before yesterday following a visit to Syria to attend the memorial service marking the 40th day since the death of Basil al-Asad, son of Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad. He met there with a number of Syrian officials and representatives of the national and Islamic forces in the Iraqi opposition based in Syria.
