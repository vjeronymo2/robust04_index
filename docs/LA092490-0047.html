September 24, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

POPULATION BOOM: UC Irvine, celebrating its 25th anniversary (A1), has seen enrollment mushroom 938% since classes opened in 1965. More growth is ahead -- another 58% by the year 2005. . . . Nearly constant building has spawned the campus quip that UCI really stands for "Under Construction Indefinitely."
