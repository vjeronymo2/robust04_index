November 27, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

MAN KILLED AS MOTORCYCLE SKIDS

A young man was killed Sunday evening when he lost control of his motorcycle and skidded into a curb, police said.

Officials did not identify the victim or give details of the accident, which occurred about 7 p.m. in the 1600 block of Harbor Boulevard.

The victim, said to be in his 20s, was not wearing a helmet, police said.
