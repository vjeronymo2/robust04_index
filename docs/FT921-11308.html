920201 FT 01 FEB 92 / UK Company News: European Motor incurs Pounds 0.74m loss European Motor Holdings, the motor group formerly known as Cargo Control which bought the motor retail division of Thomas Robinson Group for Pounds 7.1m in November, has reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 735,000 for the six months to September 30. This compares with losses of Pounds 2.72m incurred in the 15 months to March 31 1991. Group turnover fell to Pounds 3.7m (Pounds 8.85m), with turnover on continuing businesses down less sharply at Pounds 3.06m (Pounds 5.33m). Operating losses on continuing activities were reduced to Pounds 442,000 (Pounds 527,000) - those on non-continuing activities were Pounds 93,000 (Pounds 1.61m) - and interest payable was cut to Pounds 115,000 (Pounds 410,000). Losses per share for the six months were 2.58p (18.11p for the 15-month period).
