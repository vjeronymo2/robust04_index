October 22, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TWO VIEWS OF SECTION

I have been a longtime reader of The Times and especially enjoy the Real estate Section on Sundays. I just wanted to take the time to let you know how valuable this section has been to me. As a novice in real estate investments, I have learned so much from this section. I rerally feel it has evolved into a first-class source of up-to-date information.

RICHARD A. RETTAMMEL

Santa Monica
