Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] President Rauf Denktas has said that the reason for the shuttle diplomacy being carried out by Gustave Feissel, the deputy special envoy in Cyprus of the UN secretary general, is to determine the priority of the issues to be discussed during the actual negotiations. Denktas held a one-hour meeting with Gustave Feissel today. In a statement after the meeting, Denktas said that they exchanged views on how to determine the priority of the issues to be discussed during the actual negotiations. The aim of this exercise, Denktas said, is to find out?how the package will affect the Turkish Cypriot community, what its advantages are, and whether the benefits promised by the United Nations and by the UN experts are real. The president pointed out that if the answers to these questions are positive and satisfactory, then the package of the confidence-building measures can be implemented. He added that he considers it useful to discuss these issues. Denktas announced that he will meet with Feissel again on Friday [11 February], and that the talks planned for 15 February will be held on 17 February because of the postponed Greek Cypriot National Council meeting. Feissel, in turn, said that they held a beneficial meeting with President Denktas, adding that they are working on the agenda and issues of the talks planned to begin next week. Replying to a question, Feissel said that the confidence-building measures package is being regarded as a whole.
