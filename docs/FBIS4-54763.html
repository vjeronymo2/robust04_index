BFN [Text] Sofia, May 4 (BTA) -- Today the head of the Yugoslav diplomatic mission, Dr. Rados Smilkovic, made a protest before Deputy Foreign Minister Dimitur Ikonomov in connection with the recent speech delivered by Albanian President Sali Berisha at the Bulgarian parliament, the press office of the Embassy of Yugoslavia in Sofia announced. Mr. Berisha's speech is a violation of the basic rules of diplomacy, Dr. Smilkovic is cited as saying. Addressing parliament on April 28 this year during his official visit to Bulgaria, Mr. Berisha said that the war in Bosnia was a result of the decision of the communist dictatorship in Belgrade to establish Greater Serbia by aggression.
