930401 FT 01 APR 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Newmark (Louis) NEWMARK (LOUIS) proposes to raise Pounds 2.03m and Pounds 2.28m net through the issue of 833,333 ordinary shares at 60p each and the issue of Pounds 1.5m of convertible loan stock both by way of subscription, and the issue of up to 847,030 ordinary shares at 60p each via a non-underwritten rights issue on a 2-for-7 basis.
