December 21, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

OVERKILL: Another view came from Sister Carmen Serati, a Catholic nun in Santa Ana with relatives in Panama. . . . She called Bush's action a lopsided attempt at United States intervention based on improper intelligence about the location of Manuel A. Noriega. "I think they went after a mosquito with a cannon," she said. "And the mosquito, they found, is in the other house." Dana Parsons and Mark I. Pinsky
