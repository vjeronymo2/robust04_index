BFN [Text] Tehran, May 3, IRNA -- Some 17.61 million tons of non-oil commodities were imported into Iran over the past 12 months. 91 percent were exported [as received] through ports, while the rest were exported through land routes. The goods included 10.5 million tons of non-basic and 7.1 million tons of basic goods. 98 percent of the goods were imported through land, and the remaining two percent through land routes. According to public relations department of Roads and Transportation Ministry a total of 15.2 million tons of goods were loaded within the period which included 6.4 million tons of basic and 8.8 million tons of non-basic commodities.
