May 11, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

REILLY REACHES BOXING SEMIFINALS

Pepe Reilly of Glendale advanced to the semifinals of National Golden Gloves Tournament of Champions boxing championships in Miami on Thursday, defeating William Grathwohl of Cincinnati on a decision.

Reilly, 18, decisioned Frank Gentile of Milwaukee earlier in the tournament. Reilly, a senior at Hoover High, has won two junior and three senior California Golden Gloves titles.
