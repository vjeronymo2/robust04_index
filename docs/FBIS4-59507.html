BFN [Text] The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] evacuated the division headquarters in downtown Gaza early this morning. Only few installations in the Gaza Strip have still not been handed over to the Palestinians. These installations will be evacuated today or tonight. The evacuation will be completed tomorrow in a ceremony in the Qatif Bloc. Our military affairs correspondent Karmela Menashe reports that 24 hours after the evacuation is completed the joint IDF and Palestinian police patrols will start.
