930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Waverley Mining Waverley Mining Finance, the Edinburgh-based investment company, reported a pre-tax deficit of Pounds 63,411 from gross income of Pounds 141,433 for the year to end-March. That compared with a loss of Pounds 195,061 from income of Pounds 103,664 last time. Losses per share came out at 0.6p (1.7p).
