920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Grafton Grafton Group, the Dublin-based builders' merchant, experienced a 32 per cent fall in profits to IPounds 3.55m (Pounds 3.3m) pre-tax for the year to December 31. Interest charges rose to IPounds 1.29m (IPounds 564,000) but tax took IPounds 740,000 less at IPounds 874,000. Earnings emerged at 17.1p (22.9p) and a proposed final dividend of 3.75p makes a 6.25p (6p) total. Turnover advanced to IPounds 94.4m (IPounds 86.3m).
