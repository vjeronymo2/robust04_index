931123 FT 23 NOV 93 / UK Company News: S Staffs Water rises 13.7% South Staffordshire Water Holdings achieved a 13.7 per cent rise in pre-tax profits, from Pounds 5.1m to Pounds 5.8m, for the half year to September 30. Turnover advanced from Pounds 25.9m to Pounds 27.8m. Mr Lindsay Bury, chairman, said the current year had seen significant progress in its core water supply business while non-regulated businesses also made steady progress. Earnings per share advanced to 80.8p (78.7p) and the interim dividend is lifted from 15p to 16.5p.
