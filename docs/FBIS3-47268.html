Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 8 (KCNA) -- The employees of the Kim Chaek iron and steel complex, a nation's leading producer of iron and steel in the northern region, and the farmers of the Ohyon Cooperative Farm in Yonan County, South Hwanghae Province, a granary, held meetings to implement the militant tasks put forward by the great leader President Kim Il-song in his New Year address and challenged the working people of metal industry and agriculture to a socialist emulation. The employees of the complex at their meeting Friday discussed measures to overfulfil the production plan of iron and steel for this year, which is set 20 percent above last year. The reporter and speakers said "Let us hasten the general march of socialism in the revolutionary spirit of self-reliance and fortitude!" is the call of our party and a militant slogan which our people must uphold, and resolved to bring a fresh revolutionary upsurge in the production of iron and steel in the spirit displayed in the days of the great upsurge of Chollima, united around the party and their leader in one mind. They challenged the metallurgical factories across the country to a socialist emulation for overfulfilling the yearly plans. Meanwhile, the farmers of the Ohyon Cooperative Farm at their meeting on January 6 vowed to adorn this year which marks the 30th anniversary of the publication of the rural theses with signal successes in agricultural production, in hearty response to the party's agriculture first policy. They challenged the agricultural working people throughout the country to a socialist emulation to overfulfil the yearly quotas in agricultural production.
