September 21, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Philip N. Papaccio, deputy general manager of TRW Systems Integration Group, has assumed responsibility for all activities related to the Global Change Initiative for TRW Space & Defense Sector, Redondo Beach. The initiative is a worldwide effort to collect information on Earth's natural processes and study records of past environmental changes. It is aimed at establishing a scientific database that can be used to develop policies on global change issues.
