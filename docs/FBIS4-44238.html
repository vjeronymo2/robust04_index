BFN [Text] Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos attributed the opposition parties' criticism of the government for the results of the EU [European Union] summit on Corfu to cowardice and ignorance. The opposition parties' criticism of the Greek presidency and the results of the EU summit, Venizelos said, is a reflection of party cowardice, ignorance, party demagoguery, as well as international developments. The public has seen and can judge for itself, Venizelos said. He added that being in opposition does not mean criticizing the government to win points. The opposition, he concluded, is not a simple contract which is carried out, but a democratic function.
