March 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SIMON IS TRAPPED, TOO

I absolutely hate that narrow-minded men such as Roger Simon totally misunderstand the reasons why Elizabeth Morgan chose to protect her child from the child's father even to the lengths that she was willing to spend two years in prison.

Please, Mr. Simon, open your mind to the fact that there are fathers who sexually abuse their daughters, and there are judges, prosecutors and editorialists who simply can't believe this horror is happening. God help the children of this country who aren't being protected.

JANIS BRITTENHAM, Newbury Park
