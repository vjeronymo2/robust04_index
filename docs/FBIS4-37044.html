BFN [Text] Tax relief for 60 percent of the country's economic activities will be cancelled in the second five-year economic development plan. According to Economics and Finance Minister Morteza Mohammad-Khan the above mentioned activities currently include water, electricity, gas, communications, oil, and other sectors currently tax exempt. All such tax exemptions are to be scrapped according to the second development plan. Mohammad-Khan stated that oil revenue constitutes the country's highest source of income. He said: Tax incomes are very low in comparison; during the past five years the country's economy has moved from a disorderly and irregular situation to a disciplined, steady, and structured economy.
