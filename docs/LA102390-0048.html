October 23, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

PRO FOOTBALL REPORT;

WEEKDAY UPDATE;

RAIDERS;

GOAL LINE, NOT THE 20, IS SHELL'S CONCERN

The San Diego Chargers had five drives inside the Raiders' 20-yard line Sunday but came away with only nine points.

Is it dangerous for opponents to get inside the 20 so many times?

"It's dangerous if they get in the end zone," Coach Art Shell said. "If they don't get in the end zone, it's not dangerous. They got down there, and our defense wouldn't let them in."

Shell on the Raiders scoring 24 points for four consecutive weeks: "If we could score three points and win the football game, that's all I care about," he said. "I don't care about statistics, I just want to win." . . . The Raiders are 4-0 against AFC West opponents. CHRIS DUFRESNE
