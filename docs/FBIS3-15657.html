Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] After taking a village in Azerbaijan's Fizulinskiy rayon, Armenian armed forces today attempted to continue their attack after sending reinforcements. The press service of Azerbaijan's Defense Ministry, which reported this, said that Armenian sub-units had also stepped up their activity in other areas of Azerbaijan under their control. In particularly, Armenian attacks in the area of the strategically important (Amar) crossing in the north of Kelbadzharskiy rayon are being reported.
