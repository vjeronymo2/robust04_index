May 2, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

HOUSE SPEAKER JIM WRIGHT

Gingrich, GOP whip, who is the prime mover in the removal of Wright, is actually doing the Democrats a favor. Wright will be succeeded by the most capable and very personable House Majority Leader Tom Foley (D-Wash.) Foley may well be the Democratic candidate in 1992.

I can foresee the morning-after election headline: "Bush Defoleyated".

THOMAS R. DONAHUE

Bellflower
