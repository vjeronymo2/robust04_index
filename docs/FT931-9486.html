930213 FT 13 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Asset value rises at Anglo & Overseas Net asset value per share at Anglo & Overseas Trust stood at 379.6p at December 31 compared with 317.3p a year earlier. Net revenue for the 12 months improved from Pounds 7.35m to Pounds 8.97m for earnings per share of 7.78p (6.36p). The directors are recommending a final dividend of 5.1p (4.7p), payable April 21, to lift the total pay-out from 6.45p to 6.85p.
