Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Both chambers of the Russian Federation Assembly will work in harmony and in close cooperation. This was stated today at the morning session of the Federation Council by its Chairman Vladimir Shumeyko. He said that this morning he had a telephone conversation with Ivan Rybkin, chairman of the State Duma. The deputies of the upper chamber sent a telegram to the chairman of the State Duma congratulating him on his election, the Russian Information Agency reports. At today's session of the Federation Council the deputies will continue to form the committees which yesterday's session decided to set up. The upper chamber of the Russian parliament will have 10 committees.
