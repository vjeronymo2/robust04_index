910430 FT 30 APR 91 / UK Company News: Net assets lower at Fleming Universal Netasset value of Fleming Universal Investment Trust stood at 233.7p at March 31 1991 compared with 276.6p a year earlier. At the half year the figure was 216.9p. Gross revenue for the year came to Pounds 4.9m (Pounds 3.6m) and net available revenue rose from Pounds 1.56m to Pounds 2.26m for earnings per share of 3.45p (2.4p). Directors recommended an unchanged final dividend of 2.2p, maintaining the total at 2.8p.
