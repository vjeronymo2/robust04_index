BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 16 (KCNA) -- The Administration Council hosted a reception Friday for the foreign artistes and Korean artistes abroad, participants in the 12th April Spring Friendship Art Festival on the occasion of April 15. Present there were Politburo member of the Central Committee [C.C.] of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] and Premier of the Administration Council Kang Song-san, Politburo member of the C.C., the WPK and Vice-President Pak Song-chol, Vice-Premier of the Administration Council, Minister of Culture and Art and Chairman of the Organizing Committee of the Festival Chang Chol and other officials concerned. Speeches were made there.
