Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The inhabitants of Nizhnevartovsk, Siberian oilmen's largest city, have declined to elect deputies to the City Duma and the Duma of the Khantiy-Mansiysk Autonomous Okrug [of Tyumen Oblast]. As RIA was informed at the City Electoral Commission, because of the failure of voters to turn out, the 6 March elections are recognized to have taken place in only one of the electoral districts of Nizhnevartovsk. As a result, only one deputy has been elected to the City Duma.
