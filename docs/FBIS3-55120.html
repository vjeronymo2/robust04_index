Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Lvov -- The [Lvov] region's procurator, Zenon Kotyk, informed a UNIAN correspondent today that his office has no official information about criminal proceedings instituted in the case of the sale by the region's state administration of the former missile base near Brody to Lvov's private firm Konza for $13,000. Vasyl Dzera, a member of the regional council's commission for the inspection of the foreign trade activity of the oblast state administration, asserted this to be the case at a 3 January meeting of the workers of Lvivprylad production association that nominated him as a candidate for election to the Ukrainian Supreme Council. Zenon Kotyk officially told a UNIAN correspondent that the base remains in the regional communal property fund's ownership.
