Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] Upon a telephone invitation from Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], the brother president, a delegation from the cadres and members of the Fatah Movement in the Nablus region will this morning head for Tunis to discuss and examine issues of interest to the movement in the region; this is what Sarhan Duwaykat, head of the delegation, said. He affirmed that numerous problems have been accumulating in the region for years and now required the necessary treatment in consultation with the Palestinian leadership in Tunis. In addition to Duwaykat, the departing delegation would be composed of: Jamal al-Tayarawi, Mu'adh al-Masri, Muhammad 'Awdah, Ahmad Dhawqan, Abd al-Rahim al-Dib'i, Mu'in Hilal, Abbud al-Hindi, and Amjad Abu Salihiyah.
