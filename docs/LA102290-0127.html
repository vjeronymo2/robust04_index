October 22, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

DIRECTORY BOUGHT BY AMERITECH

Industrial directory publisher Ameritech Industrial Yellow Pages Inc. said today that it purchased the German industrial directory publisher "Wer Liefert Was?" for an undisclosed amount.

Family owned and operated "Wer Liefert Was?" -- "Who Supplies What?" -- publishes German and Austrian editions and plans to introduce a directory in Switzerland, Ameritech Industrial Yellow Pages President Tom Kaeding said.

Ameritech Industrial Yellow Pages publishes the Ameritech Industrial Purchasing Guide, made up of the 14 regional directories used by purchasers and suppliers.
