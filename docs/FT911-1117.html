910508 FT 08 MAY 91 / Daimler-Benz to build vehicle test track Daimler-Benz, the German motor conglomerate, was yesterday given approval to build a DM300m (Pounds 101m) vehicle test track for lorries and cars across moorland in Lower Saxony near Papenburg. The decision is likely to attract the wrath of the country's active ecology lobby, writes David Marsh in Bonn.
