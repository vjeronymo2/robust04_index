940328 FT 28 MAR 94 / Goldstar electronics deals The South Korean electronics company Goldstar has signed two contracts involving Dollars 159.5m (Pounds 109.2m) to produce television parts, video-cassette recorders and video cameras in China, an official report said, AP-DJ reports from Beijing. Goldstar said the contracts signed on Friday represented the biggest overseas investment by a South Korean electronics company. It is negotiating to set up joint-venture projects to manufacture audio equipment and air conditioners. Goldstar entered a Dollars 19.5m co-operation deal with Shanghai Tape Recording Equipment Factory to produce VCRs and video cameras. The other contract was for a Dollars 140m venture with Shuguan Electron Group.
