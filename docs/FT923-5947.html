920827 FT 27 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Irish Continental losses at IPounds 4.1m Irish Continental, the Dublin-based shipping company which acquired the B&I operation in January, reported losses of IPounds 4.09m (Pounds 3.87m) in its seasonally unfavourable first half. Directors said the pre-tax line for the six months to April 30 was not comparable to previous periods; the group incurred losses of IPounds 1.49m a year earlier and profits of IPounds 3.21m at the October year-end. Turnover amounted to IPounds 22.8m (IPounds 6.87m). Losses per share worked through at 24.2p.
