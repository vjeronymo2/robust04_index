920409 FT 09 APR 92 / UK Company News: Black & Edgington Black & Edgington Group, a manufacturer and supplier of tubular steelwork, announced reduced losses, from Pounds 5.78m to Pounds 1.96m pre-tax, for the six months to January 31. Mr David Gordon, chairman, pointed out that the group traditionally incurred a loss in the first half. Turnover of this USM-quoted group, which changed its name from Tubular Edgington in January, was Pounds 2.88m (Pounds 4.77m) and losses per share worked through at 0.4p (8.1p).
