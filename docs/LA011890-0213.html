January 18, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

UC IRVINE WOMEN LOSE TO HAWAII, 80-54

Judy Mosely had 29 points and 17 rebounds as the University of Hawaii defeated UC Irvine, 80-54, Wednesday in a Big West women's basketball game at the Bren Center.

Freshman Yvonne Catala had 14 points and eight rebounds for Irvine (1-13 overall, 0-5 in conference). Hawaii improved to 13-2, 4-2.
