931112 FT 12 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Reuters in Pounds 13m computer expansion Reuters Holdings, the international news and information group, has paid Pounds 13.3m for Vamp Health, a supplier of computer facilities for UK doctors. Vamp is a privately owned south London company serving about 2,000 general practices around the country. For the year to January 31 1993 it returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.75m on a turnover of Pounds 13m.
