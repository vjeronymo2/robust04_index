May 14, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

FIELDY SCORES 1 1/4-LENGTH WIN AT BELMONT PARK

Fernwood Stables' Fieldy circled the field on the turn and drew away in the final yards for a 1 1/4-length victory over Summer Secretary in Sunday's $87,900 Beaugay Handicap at Belmont Park.

Fieldy, sent off under jockey Craig Perret as the betting favorite in the field of five fillies and mares ages 3 and up, won for the second time in five starts this year.

The Irish-bred 7-year-old mare by Northfields out of Gramy earned $52,740 and returned $4.40, $2.60 and $2.20.

Summer Secretary paid $3 and $2.20 and Lady Talc, another six lengths back in third, paid $2.40.
