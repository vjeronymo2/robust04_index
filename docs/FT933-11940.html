930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Beckenham Beckenham Group, the USM-traded heating and ventilating engineer, saw pre-tax losses reduced to Pounds 186,000 in the six months to April 30. The deficit at this restructured group, cut from Pounds 771,000 helped by reduced interest charges of Pounds 112,000 (Pounds 364,000), came on turnover of Pounds 17.4m (Pounds 15.3m) including a Pounds 620,000 contribution from acquisitions. Losses per share worked through at 0.2p (1.3p).
