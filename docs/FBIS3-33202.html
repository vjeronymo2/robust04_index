Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] San Cristobal de las Casas, 21 Feb (Notimex) -- The dialogue between Manuel Camacho Solis, peace commissioner for the southeastern State of Chiapas, and 19 delegates from the Zapatist National Liberation Army began here today two hours late. The parties began the official talks in private at 1100 (1700 GMT) at the San Cristobal cathedral under strict security measures and with the mediation of Bishop Samuel Ruiz, according to a spokesman for the bishop. Francisco Hernandez, spokesman for the diocese, added that a news conference will be held at 1800 (0000 GMT) at the cathedral, which is being guarded by military police, nongovernment organizations, as well as the Mexican and international Red Cross.
