930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Quicks recovery continues Despite a fall in turnover from Pounds 208.7m to Pounds 198.5m, Quicks Group, the motor distributor, continued its recovery and finished 1992 with pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.06m. This compared with previous losses of Pounds 952,000. Profits at halfway totalled Pounds 1.11m. The year-end results were produced under accounting standard FRS 3. The 1991 figures were adjusted accordingly. After tax of Pounds 738,000, compared with a Pounds 154,000 credit, earnings per share were given as 7.8p (5.2p losses). The dividend for the year is lifted to 4.5p (3p). This includes an increased final of 2.75p.
