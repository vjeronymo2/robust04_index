Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, February 7. (SNARK) -- Azerbaijan receives great military help from Turkey and Afghanistan, Vahan Papazyan, Armenian Foreign Affairs Minister told Armenian TV. According to Mr.Papazyan, the mercenaries from the CIS are fighting the Azeris side. So, Azerbaijan hopes Karabakh conflict could be solved by force. Rapid changes take place with the Azerbaijani authorities, but no changes take place with the political course of the state. "It seems as if only Abulfaz Elchibey, during the last months of his power was taking the course of peaceful regulation of the conflict", said Vahan Papazyan. Mr. Papazyan reaffirmed the position taken by the Armenian side -- military ways of the conflict solution will give no results.
