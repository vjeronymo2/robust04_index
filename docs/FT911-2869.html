910427 FT 27 APR 91 / UK Company News: Secure Trust in Pounds 0.5m purchase Secure Trust, the Birmingham-based financial services group, has agreed to buy OBC Insurance Consultants for Pounds 500,000 cash, including Pounds 100,000 on a deferred basis. OBC, based in the West Midlands, has 34 offices. In the first half of last year it made pre-tax profits of Pounds 16,800, and on the acquisition date had net liabilities of about Pounds 1.51m.
