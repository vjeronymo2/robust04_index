Language: French Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] According to the Belgian Ministries of Foreign Affairs and Defense, there are no plans to send reinforcements to Bosnia. Moreover, the United Nations has not made any request to this effect, nor is there any question of moving to Bosnia a certain number of the peacekeeping troops currently being deployed in the Baranya region. Furthermore, France, the Netherlands, and Great Britain are all refusing to send additional troops to Bosnia.
