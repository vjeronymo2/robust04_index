940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / Timebomb under Syria peace plan Five members of Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhak Rabin's Labour party yesterday put a timebomb under his plans for a peace accord with Syria, by tabling a bill that, if passed, would require him to win an almost impossible parliamentary majority for withdrawal from the Golan Heights. Foreign Minister Shimon Peres said the five were making 'a fatal mistake' that could cause the collapse of the peace process. Mr Rabin had warned on Wednesday that, if the bill were passed, 'I would have no choice but to tell the Americans it is impossible to pursue negotiations with Syria.'
