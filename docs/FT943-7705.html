940822 FT 22 AUG 94 / BAA wants to buy Gatwick station BAA, the airports operator, is interested in buying the railway station at Gatwick airport from Railtrack, the state-owned rail network company. BAA wants to improve access and facilities and may spend up to Pounds 3m. Railtrack is unlikely to sell the station outright but might agree to BAA investment to improve facilities. BAA is not considering a bid for the Gatwick Express train franchise.
