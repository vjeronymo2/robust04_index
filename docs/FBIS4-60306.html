BFN [Text] The U.S. State Department yesterday denounced al-Bid's secessionist announcement and called for political dialogue. According to AFP, State Department Spokesman Michael McCurry said the United States will not reply to al-Bid's Saturday announcement as it does not consider such basic issues can be decided while battles are continuing. The spokesman added that the immediate result of the secession announcement will be that of perpetuating battles and increasing the number of victims and the suffering of civilians.
