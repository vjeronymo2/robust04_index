920513 FT 13 MAY 92 / International Company News: UPI accepts US Media bid BANKRUPT news agency United Press International and its creditors have accepted a Dollars 6m cash bid by US Media, a cable TV and radio network company controlled by television evangelist Mr Pat Robertson, Reuter reports. The news agency had made the bid in a US court hearing. It had been the only offer for the news agency as a whole. Mr Robertson said his interest in UPI included its overseas news gathering operation and radio network, which would be 'complementary'. US Media Corp's holdings include a cable television network.
