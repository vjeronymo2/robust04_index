BFN [Text] According to the press service of the Crimean Ministry of Internal Affairs, 29 APCs arrived on Sunday at Simferopol train station. After unloading, the APCs headed for the territory of the Simferopol military school, escorted by Crimean highway inspection officers. Colonel Oleksandr Kostyukov, commander of the National Guard brigade, confirmed in an interview for UKRINFORM that the APC's did arrive. He said: However, they are not for the military school but for our subunits. Their arrival accidentally coincided with the developments in the wake of the decision by the Crimean Supreme Council session to restore the Constitution of 6 May 1993. In reality, it was part of a scheduled process of equipping the brigade.
