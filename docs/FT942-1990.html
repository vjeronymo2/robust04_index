940622 FT 22 JUN 94 / World News in Brief: First round Wimbledon defeat for Graf Five-times champion and world number one Steffi Graf was beaten 7-5 7-6 in the first round of Wimbledon by unseeded American Lori McNeil in the biggest upset in 101 years of the women's singles tournament. Coupled with her defeat to Mary Pierce in the French Open semifinals, it was the first time since January 1985 that Miss Graf, 25, had lost two consecutive matches.
