June 26, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

The Farrah and Ryan Show: Farrah Fawcett and Ryan O'Neal have been signed to star in a new half-hour comedy to premiere next season, CBS Entertainment President Jeff Sagansky said Monday. The untitled show was created for Fawcett and O'Neal by multi-Emmy Award winner Alan Zweibel ("Saturday Night Live," "It's Garry Shandling's Show"). GREG BRAXTON
