BFN [Text] The Russia's Choice parliamentary group approved at its Thursday meeting the proposal made by Boris Fedorov, the leader of the unregistered group December 12 Union, that the two groups start closer cooperation on most important issues on the agenda of the lower house. Russia's Choice leader Yegor Gaydar also told his supporters that the December 12 Union was short of members to be registered officially. It had 29 while 35 were needed for official registration. The Russia's Choice adopted a decision saying that it would not punish its members who voluntarily join the December 12 Union. Gleb Yakunin called for a closer bloc between the two groups. Gaydar supported the idea and said that he would help the setting up of a club of democratic deputies. Gaydar also said that he had called on Grigoriy Yavlinskiy, leader of another pro-democracy faction, to work closer together and that Fedorov had done the same.
