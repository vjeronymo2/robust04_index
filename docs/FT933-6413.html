930827 FT 27 AUG 93 / International Company News in Brief: Irish Continental IRISH CONTINENTAL, the ferry group, reported a seasonal trading deficit of IPounds 5.37m (Pounds 5.04m) for the six months to June 30. The outcome, achieved on turnover of IPounds 36.5m (IPounds 22.8m), was not comparable to the previous IPounds 4.09m which included only a three-month winter contribution from B&I Line. Losses per share, adjusted for FRS 3, were 30.7p (34.6p). A maiden interim dividend of 1p is declared.
