BFN [Text] Following the satisfaction recorded by the European troika, the European Union is to order the release of a $150 million loan to Algeria. The amount is the final installment of an overall loan of $400 million granted to Algeria in 1991. Algeria and four other countries will also benefit from loans from the Islamic Development Bank [IDB]. The IDB is to grant Algeria a $6.5 million loan to be spent on papier mache exports.
