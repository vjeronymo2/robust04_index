Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Jordanian Transport Minister Adib Halasah has said that Jordan has prepared a plan to cooperate with Israel in the transport area, especially in the Elat-Aqaba Gulf region. Our correspondent said that the Jordanian minister made the statement during a brief conversation with (Elie Danon), spokesman for the Israeli Transportation Ministry, who encountered the Jordanian minister on the sidelines of a European conference on transportation held in the Greek island of Crete.
