930511 FT 11 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Excalibur talks aborted SHARES OF Excalibur Group yesterday closed 5p down at 14 1/2 p following the announcement that discussions in relation to an approach for the jewellery maker, announced on April 20, had been terminated. Dealers had speculated that Suter was the other party after it took an 11.2 per cent stake in Excalibur earlier this year.
