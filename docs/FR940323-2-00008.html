DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE Agency Form Under Review by the Office of Management and Budget DOC has submitted to the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) for clearance the following proposal for collection of information under the provisions of the Paperwork Reduction Act (44 U.S.C. chapter 35). Agency: Bureau of the Census. Title: March 12 Employment From IRS Form 941E. Form Number(s): IRS Form 941E. Agency Approval Number: 0607&hyph;0203. Type of Request: Extension of the approval date of a currently approved collection without any change in the substance or in the method of collection. Burden: 5,000 hours. Number of Respondents: 50,000. Avg Hours Per Response: 6 minutes. Needs and Uses: The Internal Revenue Service (IRS) uses Form 941E to determine taxes for employers not covered under the Federal Insurance Contributions Act. These include state and local governments, payers of supplemental unemployment benefits, certain churches and church&hyph;controlled organizations, and certain payers of annuities and sick pay. These employers prepare and submit a Form 941E, quarterly, to the IRS. The Census Bureau sponsors and uses responses to Question 1 on the Form to update the Standard Statistical Establishment List (SSEL). The SSEL, as a universal sampling frame of U.S. business activity, requires employment data from all sectors of the economy. Question 1 reads as follows, `` Complete for First Quarter Only Number of employees (except household) employed in the pay period that includes March 12th.'' Affected Public: State and local governments, Businesses or other for&hyph;profit organizations, Small businesses or organizations. Frequency: Annually. Respondent's Obligation: Mandatory.
