September 5, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

DIGEST /LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: DEADLINE EXTENDED FOR $25,000 ARSON BOUNTIES

The Los Angeles County Board of Supervisors Tuesday extended the time period to qualify for rewards for identifying arsonists who set fires this summer in Glendale and the Santa Clarita Valley.

The reward period for both fires will be extended to Oct. 1.

The board had earlier approved $25,000 rewards for the apprehension and conviction of the arsonists who set the Glendale fire on June 26 and the Santa Clarita Valley fire on July 14.

The Glendale blaze destroyed 46 homes and partially damaged 20 others. In the Santa Clarita fire, 625 acres were burned and several firefighters sustained minor injuries.
