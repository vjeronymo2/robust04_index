931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Morris Ashby rises to Pounds 839,000 On turnover up 42 per cent from Pounds 8.13m to Pounds 11.6m in the six months to September 30, Morris Ashby, the USM-quoted diecasting and machining group, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 839,000, an increase of 31 per cent on the previous Pounds 639,000. Mr Norman Gardner, chairman, said: 'Overall, only one of our top 20 customers placed less business with us.' He added that the long term future looked healthy with a strong forward order book, but some customers were showing less confidence than had been expected. Earnings per share were 6.3p (5.5p) and the interim dividend is raised to 2p (1.7p).
