Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES, 23/3/1994 -- "The Ministry of Foreign Affairs has learned with great satisfaction of the U.S. President's decision to lift the restrictions on arms sales to Romania and other states in the region", reads a release presented to the news conference given on 23 March at the Foreign Ministry. "The Foreign Ministry welcomes the decision as being an important step both in the development of the Romanian-American cooperation in the military domain, and in the dynamics of the relations between Romania and the NATO, within the Partnership for Peace. "The Foreign Ministry considers this decision made by the U.S. President to be a practical confirmation of the American stand according to which Romania's security is important for the United States security, being a matter of direct and material interest to the U.S. and the NATO", the release concludes.
