Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Edouard Balladur arrived in Zagreb, Croatia on Sunday morning [13 March] for a surprise visit to the former Yugoslavia. The prime minister, accompanied by Defense Minister Francois Leotard, will visit French UN forces deployed in the Bihac pocket (northwestern Bosnia). Edouard Balladur will also pay his respects in Zagreb to the remains of the French soldier who was killed on Friday near Bihac. There are 1,300 French troops stationed in the Bihac pocket, which is home to by 300,000 Bosnian Muslims.
