931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / World News in Brief: Two incendiary devices explode in London An incendiary device exploded in a pub in Victoria, central London, forcing the evacuation of a coach station being used by hundreds of peak-hour passengers. Another device exploded at the Royal Mail's Mount Pleasant sorting office on the northern fringes of the City, also leading to its evacuation. Both devices caused small fires but there was little damage and no injuries, police said.
