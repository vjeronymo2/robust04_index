Language: Tagalog Article Type:BFN [Text] The Moro Islamic Liberation Front [MILF] is ready to sign an agreement with the Philippine Government for the implementation of separate cease fires. Government panel member Eduardo Ermita made this announcement after receiving hints from the MILF headed by Hashem Salamat. Earlier, government panel Chairman Manuel Yan disclosed that the government will hold separate peace talks with the MILF after the negotiation with the MNLF [Moro National Liberation Front] headed by Nur Misuari.
