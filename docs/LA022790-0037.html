February 27, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Cher Dresses for Dinner: Cher has a new video coming out titled "Heart of Stone," and this one should generate much less controversy than her "If I Could Turn Back Time" video. In the new video -- instead of a skimpy leather strap -- she is wearing a black minidress, blazer, stockings and shoes, what has been referred to as the kind of outfit one might wear out to dinner. SHAUNA SNOW
