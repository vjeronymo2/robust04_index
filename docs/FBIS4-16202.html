BFN [Text] The Ossetian-Ingush talks at the level of working groups of the two republics' experts were renewed in Nalchik (the capital of Kabardino Balkaria). Head of the Ossetian negotiation, Georgiy Jikgayev, told Interfax that a draft treaty on implementation of the Russian president's decree on return of refugees and displaced persons to their homes on North Ossetian and Ingush territory was being coordinated at the meeting of the experts. The document had been adopted in general at the first round of the talks with the participation of Ossetian President Akhsarbek Galazov and Ingush President Ruslan Aushev in Nalchik in early April. Jikgayev was certain that experts would manage to work out a single draft document which the two presidents would discuss at their summit in Nalchik on April 14.
