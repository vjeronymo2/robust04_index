BFN [Text] Kiev -- The Cabinet of Ministers of Ukraine and National Bank of Ukraine [NBU] adopted a joint resolution on 28 May "On the Withdrawal from Circulation of Coupons with a Nominal Value of 10 and 25 Karbovantsi." Proceeding from the inexpediency of further using these banknotes in cash settlement operations, the Cabinet of Ministers and the NBU have obliged regional boards of the NBU and commercial banks to ensure that the 10 and 25 karbovantsi banknotes are withdrawn from circulation by 1 August. Banknotes that have been withdrawn will be kept in regional NBU branches until the NBU decides on the issue of their recovery. The banknotes remain legal tender by all entities of entrepreneurial activity, regardless of their form of activity, by banks, and by enterprises of the Ministry of Communications until the time limit determined by the resolution. The resolution says that in connection with this, from 1 June the setting of retail prices for goods, services, and works should be carried out in figures that are a multiple of 50.
