Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Jan. 31 -- The Vietnam-Romania Intergovernmental Committee for Economic, Scientific and Technological Cooperation held its 14th session here this morning. Present at the meeting were, on the Vietnamese side, Mr Le Van Triet, minister of trade, chairman of Vietnam's sub-committee and other members of the Vietnamese Government's economic delegation and on the Romanian side, Mr. Cristian Ioneseu, minister of trade, chairman of the Romania's sub-committee, and head of an economic delegation of the Romanian Government currently on a visit to Vietnam, and the Romanian charge d'affaires in Vietnam. During the meeting, the two sides reviewed the implementation of economic-commercial, scientific and technological cooperation between the two countries over the past time and discussed cooperation programme for the 1994-1995 period. Reports on the preparation of documents namely agreement on investment encouragement and protection, agreement on double tax avoidance and documents on the committee's organization and statute were presented at the meeting. Other relating matters including the settlement of debt by convertible ruble, next session's agenda and time, etc. were also discussed at the meeting.
