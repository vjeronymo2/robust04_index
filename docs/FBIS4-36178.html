BFN [Text] Cairo, 7 Jun (MENA) -- 'Abdallah al-Asnaj who has been appointed deputy prime minister and foreign minister in the Aden government has reiterated his country's commitment to implementing Security Council Resolution 924 on the crisis in Yemen. In statements to Voice of the Arabs Radio today, he welcomed Sanaa's declaration of a cease-fire on all combat lines, noting the importance of a serious and actual commitment to the new date. He called for sending international observers to ensure that the cease- fire is not breached. Al-Asnaj's statements are the first southern reaction to Sanaa's unilateral decision to cease fire as of midnight (?last night).
