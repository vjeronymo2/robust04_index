931216 FT 16 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Dartmoor Trust Net asset value per share of Dartmoor Investment Trust surged to 115.2p at October 31, compared with 74.2p a year earlier. After-tax revenue dropped from Pounds 919,000 to Pounds 853,000 for the six-month period, giving a per share value of 3.55p, against 4.02p. The second interim dividend is held at 2.5p.
