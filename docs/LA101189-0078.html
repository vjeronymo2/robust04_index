October 11, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The Kansas City Royals exercised their option for next year on Bret Saberhagen, the leading candidate for the American League Cy Young Award after a 23-6 season.

Saberhagen will get $1.375 million in the option year. His previous two-year contract paid him $1.1 million in 1988 and $1.25 million in 1989.
