Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] The Armed Forces general command asks that the cycle of violence created by division be ended. Without naming the serious incidents at the beginning of the week in Cite Soleil, the military institution asks all sons of the country for more peace and tolerance and expresses hope that the coming year will be the beginning of a new era for all, auguring the establishment of a real democracy and a state of law guaranteeing more justice to all Haitians and better prospects for their children. It should be noted that more than (5,000 million) gourdes have been collected within the framework of humanitarian assistance for the victims of Cite Soleil. Furthermore, the U.S. Government is studying the possibility of a long-term reaccommodation for those people. A protocol of assistance was signed on 29 December between the United States, the Haitian Government, and the health centers.
