BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- German Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel has called for increased pressure on the Serbian side in the Bosnian conflict. Russia would have to continue to take part in this, Kinkel said today following talks in Bonn with his Russian counterpart Andrey Kozyrev. He stressed the need to bring forward the peace process in Bosnia-Herzegovina. Kozyrev said it was important to work for a cease-fire and to protect the United Nations' safe areas in the long term.
