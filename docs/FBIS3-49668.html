Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] An exchange of prisoners of war took place this afternoon at the UN Protection Force checkpoint near Lipovac. The Croatian side handed over 11 prisoners, mainly members of the Stit [Shield] terrorist group. One of the prisoners refused to go over to the Serbian side. In addition to eight people from the Sombor group, another five prisoners were released from Serbian prisons. Thus the agreement signed by Panic and Franjo Greguric in Budapest in 1992 has been realized.
