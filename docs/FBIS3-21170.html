Language: Japanese Article Type:CSO [Text] PNC, which has been conducting the 11th periodic inspection of the ATR Fugen nuclear power plant reactor (prototype reactor, rated output 165,000 kW) since 18 August, restarted the reactor and achieved criticality on 14 November. Today PNC will begin adjustment operation, which is the final stage of the periodic inspection process. Engineers will check out plant performance as they slowly increase output, and they plan to return to full operation after the government's final inspection in the middle of December. In this periodic inspection, engineers inspected the reactor facility, turbine equipment and electrical equipment, and they confirmed there were no abnormalities.
