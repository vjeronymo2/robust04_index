930903 FT 03 SEP 93 / Ciampi warns union leaders The Italian government of Mr Carlo Azeglio Ciampi yesterday held the first of a series of meetings with union leaders aimed at tackling rising unemployment and improving the competitiveness of Italian industry, writes Robert Graham in Rome. The government is attempting to head off industrial unrest over unemployment. At the same time Mr Ciampi is anxious to ensure greater flexibility in the labour market so Italian companies can take advantage of any recovery early next year. The prime minister told union leaders measures to combat unemployment had to be in the context of continued austerity.
