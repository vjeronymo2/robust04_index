940910 FT 10 SEP 94 / Finance and the Family: Boost in demand for metals - At a Glance The encouraging growth in world economic activity is boosting demand for metals such as copper, aluminium, lead and zinc, and minerals such as coal and titanium dioxide - which gives the whiteness to paint, some plastics and tiles. All these are produced by RTZ, the world's biggest mining company, which this week pleased the market by reporting interim profits at the top end of analysts' expectations: a 30 per cent increase to Pounds 242m in earnings adjusted to reflect the underlying business performance by excluding exceptional items. The group, usually a conservative forecaster, also said the global economic outlook had not looked as favourable since the 1980s.
