BFN [Text] Tehran, April 17, IRNA -- Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko, arrived here Sunday at the head of a high ranking politico-economic delegation on a three-day official visit. Zlenko, who is carrying a message from Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk for President Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani, was greeted at Tehran airport by his Iranian counterpart 'Ali Akbar Velayati. Talking to reporters upon his arrival, Zlenko, said he was here to further expand bilateral relations in various fields. Zlenko said his visit provided a good opportunity for talks with Iranian officials on ways of consolidating and promoting bilateral relations and on issues of mutual interest.
