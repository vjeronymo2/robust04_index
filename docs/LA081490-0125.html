August 14, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

TICKETS, PLEASE: The Irvine Barclay Theatre is giving new meaning to the term "advance sales." . . . Tickets are already available for the 750-seat theater on the edge of the UC Irvine campus, even though it's still under construction. Because the theater is the size of a small Broadway playhouse, "we encourage people to purchase their tickets early to make sure they get seats," says UCI's Barbara Davidson. . . . The first performance, Nov. 2, will feature the Bella Lewitzky Dance Co. introducing a dance honoring UCI's 25th anniversary. Kristina Lindgren and John Needham
