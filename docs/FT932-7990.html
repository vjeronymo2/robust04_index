930520 FT 20 MAY 93 / International Company News: Corporacion Banesto announces Pta147m first-quarter pre-tax profits Corporacion Banesto, the industrial holding owned by the Spain's Banesto banking group, reported first-quarter pre-tax profits of Pta147m (Dollars 1.8bn), against a Pta4.6bn loss in the same period of 1992. The holding said turnover was down 30 per cent to Pta69.8bn and that the sharp turnaround was in part due to lowered debt servicing costs.
