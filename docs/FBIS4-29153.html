BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 15 -- A Vietnamese Government delegation led by Deputy Prime Minister Phan Van Khai left here this morning for an official visit to Canada at the invitation of the government of Canada. The delegation includes Chairman of the State Planning Committee Do Quoc Sam, Vice Foreign Minister Le Mai, Vice Minister of Commerce Nguyen Xuan Quang, Vice State Bank Governor Chu Van Nguyen and ranking officials. The delegation is expected to meet with Prime Minister Jean Chretien and senior officials of Canada and to visit Vancouver, British Colombia, Calgary, Alberta, Montreal and Quebec. The high level talks during the visit will focus on cooperation, other issues aimed at increasing commercial contacts between the two countries, particularly in the fields of transportation, telecommunications, and engineering services.
