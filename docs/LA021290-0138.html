February 12, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

ON THE SIDELINES;

GRIDDER JAMES ROONEY DIES AT 85

James P. Rooney, the brother of the late Art Rooney and a former University of Pittsburgh football star, died today after a lengthy illness.

He was 85.

Considered one of the Rooney family's most outstanding athletes, James Rooney lettered at Pitt in 1926, 1928 and 1929. A running back, he also was one of the nation's leading punters.

James Rooney later was elected a member of the Pennsylvania Legislature. Art Rooney died in 1987.

A funeral Mass will be held in St. Paul's Monastery on Wednesday. Burial will follow in Pittsburgh's Northside Catholic Cemetery.
