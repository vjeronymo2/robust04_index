BFN [Unattributed article: "Vatican/Rwanda: Holy See Wants UN Security Zone at Kagbayi"] [Text] (ANSA) New York, June 1 -- The Holy See on Wednesday [1 June] asked the United Nations Security Council to set up a security zone around Kabgayi, the religious community in southwestern Rwanda where an estimated 38,000 refugees of the Tutsi tribe have sought shelter from Hutu militias. "To save 38,000 lives, the UN must declare Kabgayi a security zone and must ban the entry of armed units there," the Vatican's UN representative, Renato Raffaele Martino, said.
