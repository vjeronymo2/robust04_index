BFN [From the "RPC Television News" newscast] [Text] Businessman Gabriel Lewis Galindo confirmed to RPC Television this afternoon that he has accepted Ernesto Perez Balladares' offer to hold the post of foreign minister beginning on 1 September. Gabriel Lewis Galindo was Panamanian ambassador to Washington in the last months of negotiations on the canal treaties and during the entire process of the ratification of the Torrijos-Carter Treaties. His actions were crucial to the ratification of the treaties. The Panamanian industrialist's business relations in the Americas, Europe, and Asia, as well as his diplomatic maneuvering ability, will be exploited by the Perez Balladares administration in its efforts to strengthen diplomatic and trade ties with all countries. Lewis Galindo's acceptance of the Foreign Ministry post is the first confirmation of the formation of the multiparty cabinet promised by Dr. Perez Balladares during his presidential campaign.
