February 19, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFING: PRESIDENT'S DAY CLOSINGS

Monday is observed as President's Day, marking the birthdays of Presidents George Washington and Abraham Lincoln. Following is a list of local closings.

GOVERNMENT: Federal, state and county offices will be closed Monday. City offices in Westminster will be open Monday; all other city offices will be closed.

MAIL: Mail will not be delivered Monday.

TRASH: Trash will not be picked up on Monday in Anaheim, Brea, Buena Park, Cypress, Garden Grove, Placentia and Yorba Linda. In all other cities, pickup schedules remain unchanged.

COURTS: County, state and federal courts will be closed Monday.

BANKS: Most banks will close on Monday.

SCHOOLS: In the Saddleback Valley Unified School District, schools will be closed Monday through Wednesday. All other public schools will be closed only Monday.
