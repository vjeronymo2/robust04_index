Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 17 (KCNA) -- A film show took place here Wednesday on the occasion of the 45th anniversary of the signing of the agreement on economic and cultural cooperation between the DPRK and the Russian Federation. Invited there were Yuriy Fadeyev, Russian ambassador to Korea, and embassy officials. Present there together with working people in the city were Yi Pong-hui, vice-chairman of the DPRK Radio and TV Broadcasting Committee and chairman of the Korea-Russia Friendship Association, and other officials concerned. The participants saw a feature film "Last Way of a Poet".
