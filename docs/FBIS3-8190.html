Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Text] The Czech Republic does not intend to apply officially for admission to the European Union [EU] in the coming months. This was announced by foreign Ministry spokesman Vit Kurfurst. He added that the Czech Republic will apply when conditions for its successful integration into this structure have been achieved. Speaking about Hungary's decision to officially apply for full EU membership, which was announced in Budapest yesterday, the spokesman said that this is a sovereign decision, and the Czech Foreign Ministry will not comment on it in any way.
