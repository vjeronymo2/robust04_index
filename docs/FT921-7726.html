920221 FT 21 FEB 92 / UK to help develop Bulgaria's tourism The UK's know-how fund for central and eastern Europe is to be used to help Bulgaria upgrade its tourist industry, writes Michael Skapinker. It is the first time that the Pounds 50m (Dollars 87.5m) fund, which was set up in 1989, has been used to support the development of tourism in eastern Europe. Consultants from the UK will begin work next month, helping Bulgaria draw up a strategic plan on the future of the industry.
