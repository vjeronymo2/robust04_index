941024 FT 24 OCT 94 / Fleetwood's fish hall seagull-proof The fish auction hall at Fleetwood has been made seagull-proof at a cost of Pounds 180,000, enabling a relaunch of the Lancashire port's fish facilities, which declined after the cod wars of the 1970s. The hall has been refurbished to comply with European Union regulations by Associated British Ports, Fleetwood's owner and operator.
