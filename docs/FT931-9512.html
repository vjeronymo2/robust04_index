930213 FT 13 FEB 93 / Beer output down BEER production in November last year was 2.99m barrels, 6.2 per cent lower than the corresponding month in 1991. Adjusted production - including imports less exports - was 6.5 per cent down at 3.21m barrels. Production for the 12 months to November was 36.1m barrels, a 3.3 per cent fall on the previous year.
