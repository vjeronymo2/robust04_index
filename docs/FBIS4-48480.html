BFN [Text] On the third day of the strike organized by the Federation of Labor Unions of Niger, the national mediating committee for peace and national unity which, as you know, is made up of two human rights associations, yesterday appealed to the two sides to resume dialogue. The National Association for the Defense of Human Rights and the DLD [expansion unknown] recalled the economic, financial, and social crisis shaking our country and urged the government and the trade union organizations to resume negotiations.
