921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / UK Company News: ICI expands its European network ICI Paints is expanding its European decorative business with the acquisition of a network of 30 trade branches in France. The deal also includes sales through a number of independent wholesalers. The network has been purchased from Master Peintures, a paint maker based in south-west France, and will trade under the name of Master Distribution. The network, which has annual sales approaching Pounds 20m, will provide the base for further growth in the trade sector. The value of the acquisition is less than 1 per cent of ICI's assets.
