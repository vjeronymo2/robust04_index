BFN [Excerpt] At a session chaired by Mirko Marjanovic, the Government of the Republic of Serbia concluded that the stable conditions for economic activity have been successfully maintained in the three and a half months since the beginning of the implementation of the economic program. The exchange rate of the dinar is unchanged and is accompanied by a gradual increase in foreign exchange reserves and industrial output, which was up 2.7 percent in April. The deficit in the republican budget is being reduced, as is total public expenditure. Average wages and other earnings have increased in real terms. In the first two weeks of May there was a slight increase in retail prices and the prices of agricultural products at vegetable markets. Accepting the report on the results achieved in the implementation of the program of reconstruction of the monetary system and the strategy of economic recovery of Yugoslavia, the government concluded that the faster adaptation of companies and banks to the requirements of the market, the strengthening of financial discipline and legal security, the elimination of illegal financial transactions, and the rationalization in the sphere of public spending are of key importance in the second stage of the program's implementation.
