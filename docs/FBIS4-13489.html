BFN [Text] Two other Iraqi suspects, Consul 'Ali Darwish and embassy employee Hadi Hassun al-Najm, who had intended to travel to Jordan along with the three diplomats, have been handed over to the Internal Security Forces. Two arrest warrants had been issued against Consul Darwish and employee al-Najm on charges of taking part in the murder of Shaykh Talib Suhayl al-Tamimi. Thus the number of Iraqis arrested on charges of committing the murder or taking part in it rises to four [as heard].
