Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Fukuoka, March 1 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice Premier Zhu Rongji arrived today in Fukuoka on his last leg of a nine-day visit to Japan. Fujuoka, capital of Fukuoka Prefecture, is located in the southwestern Kyushu area widely known as Japan's Silicon Valley. Zhu is scheduled to visit a ceramics maker and a robot manufacturer Wednesday [2 March] in Kitakyushu, an industrial city known for its high-tech. Earlier today, the vice premier visited the Seto Ohashi Bridge, the world's longest bridge for combined use of highway and railway.
