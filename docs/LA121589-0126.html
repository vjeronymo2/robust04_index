December 15, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Pinnacle West Capital Corp. announced that its board decided at a special meeting to reject an acquisition proposal by PacifiCorp, which it called inadequate . . . Benton Harbor, Mich.-based Whirlpool Corp. said it has agreed to acquire 28.4% of Toronto-based Inglis Ltd. that it does not already own for about $40.
