930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / Lloyd's Names form action group NAMES on one of the oldest syndicates at the Lloyd's of London insurance market are forming an action group to press for compensation for losses. More than 4,000 Names, individuals whose assets support the market, face losses of more than Pounds 110m between 1988 and 1990 as a result of their membership of Secretan syndicate 367.
