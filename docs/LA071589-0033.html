July 15, 1989, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

WHAT NBC NEEDS IS RETURN OF GARAGIOLA

Bring back Joe G.

NBC coverage of the All-Star game (Anaheim '89) was excellent. Scully, as usual, was superb. However, "Dutch" (to be expected) has lost his touch with sports announcing as well as bucking broncos. And Seaver was difficult to understand due to poor enunciation (mumbling) . . . bring back the bald-headed guy.

CHET LARSON

Solana Beach
