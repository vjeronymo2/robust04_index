921125 FT 25 NOV 92 / World Trade News: India urged to scrap rail plan The Indian government was yesterday urged to scrap a Dollars 190m (Pounds 125.8m) contract to import high-technology railway locomotives from Asea Brown Boveri (ABB), writes Stefan Wagstyl in New Delhi. India's railway advisory panel said it should develop its own advanced locomotives instead of buying foreign imports. The Dollars 4m spent on commitment payments to ABB should not inhibit reconsidering the contract. ABB declined comment before studying the report.
