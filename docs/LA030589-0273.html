March 5, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

SOFTBALL ROUNDUP;

VILLA PARK, WESTERN WIN IN SEMIFINALS

Monique Hernandez pitched a four-hitter to lead Villa Park High School to a 6-1 victory over Fullerton in a semifinal game of the Fullerton softball tournament Saturday at Lion's Field in Fullerton.

Michele D'Amato had three hits, including a bases-loaded triple and a double, and four RBIs for Villa Park, which plays Western Monday for the championship.

In other Fullerton tournament games:

Western 2, Esperanza 0 -- Western advanced in the winners' bracket behind Beth Queypo's shutout.

Magnolia 4, Santiago 0 -- Magnolia's Amber Elliot pitched a no-hitter with eight strikeouts to beat Santiago in the losers' bracket.

In nonleague softball:

Garden Grove 1-5, Ocean View 0-3 -- Stephanie Smith pitched a four-hit shutout in the first game for host Garden Grove. In the second game, Brandie Novak doubled in two runs to break a 3-3 tie in the seventh inning.

Canyon 12-8, Los Amigos 1-1 -- The Comanches limited Los Amigos to three hits and two unearned runs in the season-opening doubleheader at Canyon. Canyon's Sara Roberts gave up a bunt single to the first batter she faced -- Renee Hernandez -- and didn't allow another hit the rest of the way. Kristin Heizer pitched a two-hitter in the second game. Canyon first baseman Tina French had five hits and four RBIs on the day. Krissy Kyriazis had three hits and scored four runs.
