Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today in Chisinau the Penal Judiciary Collegium of the Supreme Court of the Republic of Moldova examined the case and annulled the sentence passed on 9 December 1993 against the [Ilie] Ilascu group. The case is to be returned to the State Prosecutor's Office of the Republic of Moldova in view of opening penal procedures against the judges belonging to the anti-constitutional court in Tiraspol, who participated in the passing of the illegitimate sentence that jeopardized the life, freedom, and health of those sentenced. At the same time the Moldovan body decided to terminate the preventive arrest of the six [members of the Ilascu group]. In a statement made to our correspondent, Nicolae Gore, a member of the Supreme Court of Justice of the Republic of Moldova, emphasized the fact that today's court ruling annuls the Tiraspol sentence and all its consequences. According to the laws of the Republic of Moldova, as soon as the sentence was annulled, the Ilascu group has to be released from jail, added Nicolae Gore.
