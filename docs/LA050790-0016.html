May 7, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

ANIMAL RIGHTS MOVEMENT AND MEDICAL EXPERIMENTS

The weakness of your argument lies in couching it in "either-or" terms.

You view life as a hierarchy. It's not -- it's a monarchy. When we seek to advance humankind by experimenting, inflicting pain, and, ultimately killing a fellow creature we do so by sacrificing all semblance of human character. We are called as stewards, not despots. To advocate that these must die that we may live is the mentality that has turned the earth into a dunghill.

RICHARD DUNKERLY

Whittier
