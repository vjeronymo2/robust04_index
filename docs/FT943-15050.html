940712 FT 12 JUL 94 / Housebuilders consortium shortlisted for Docklands development Wimpey Homes and a consortium of housebuilders - Barratt, Fairclough and Ideal Homes - have been shortlisted for one of the single largest housing developments in London's Docklands. A decision on the identity of the developer of the first phase of West Silvertown Urban Village, on the south side of Royal Victoria Dock, is due to be announced next week by the London Docklands Development Corporation. The first phase, providing up to 1,200 homes, is expected to have a value of more than Pounds 100m. It will be the biggest development so far undertaken in the former Royal group of docks.
