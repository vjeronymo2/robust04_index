921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / World Trade News: Halcrow in HK project HALCROW Asia Partnership has won the contract to design two stretches of railway tunnel, totalling 2.65km, between Hong Kong and the new airport at Chep Lap Kok, Andrew Baxter writes. The company is part of Halcrow, one of Britain's largest consulting engineers. Construction should begin in about 12 months.
