931126 FT 26 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Sterling Industries down at Pounds 1.69m Sterling Industries, the engineering group with a 9.7 per cent stake in Caledonia Investments, saw pre-tax profits dip from Pounds 1.88m to Pounds 1.69m for the half year to September 30. Earnings per share rose from 4.37p to 4.62p and the interim dividend is lifted from 1.5p to 1.8p. Turnover was down from Pounds 18.5m to Pounds 18.3m.
