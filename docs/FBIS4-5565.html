BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 10 (KCNA) -- An evening of working people on Pyongyang was held at the Kim Il-song Square Saturday to celebrate the 1st anniversary of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il's election as chairman of the National Defence Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. The evening was overflowing with deepest respect and reverence for General Kim Chong-il, supreme commander, always leading the Korean people and People's Army to victory, exalting the dignity and honour of the fatherland and nation with his rare military wisdom and prominent commanding art. The evening was a sea of beautiful flowers and dances. With a solemn chorus of the song "We Will Win Because You Are Leading Us" all the participants of the evening sang and danced, renewing the faith that they are afraid of no formidable enemy and will certainly win as long as they are guided by General Kim Chong-il possessed of outstanding military wisdom and matchless grit.
