BFN [Excerpts] Foreign Minister Mohamed Salah Dembri was received in Athens today by Greek Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou to whom he conveyed a message from Algerian President Liamine Zeroual. Mr. Mohamed Salah Dembri held talks with his counterpart Karolos Papoulias and held a news conference in which he talked about the cooperative relations between Algeria and Greece and the European Union [EU]. [Begin Dembri recording] We have today set, with His Excellency Minister Papoulias, Algeria's requests for the upcoming EU meetings, and he has, I think, himself mentioned them again quite well, and I do not need to go over them again. [passage omitted] [end recording] With regard to Maghreban issues, Mr. Mohamed Salah Dembri said that Algeria would always maintain its stance and believe in peaceful solutions and humanitarian support for the Saharan people.
