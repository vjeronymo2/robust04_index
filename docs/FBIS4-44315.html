BFN [Text] [Yemeni] Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah met in Paris today with his French counterpart, Alain Juppe. They reviewed the developments of the situation in the Republic of Yemen. During the meeting, the French foreign minister stressed the need for a cease-fire and the need to resolve the crisis in our country within the framework of maintaining unity and democracy. He stressed that unity and democracy are the Yemeni people's option, which has been realized by their own free will.
