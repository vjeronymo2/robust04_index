BFN [Text] Attorney General Mikha'el Ben-Ya'ir has ruled that after the transfer of powers to the Palestinian authority in Gaza and Jericho, Knesset members [MK] will no longer enjoy parliamentary immunity and special privileges in those areas. In all other areas of Judaea, Samaria, and Gaza MK's enjoy special privileges stemming from their position, and the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] authorities must respect them. Our correspondent Hayim Hecht reports the attorney general emphasized that in the event of a clash between security needs in the area and the right of an MK to freedom of movement, the security needs will prevail. Mikha'el Ben-Ya'ir was addressing the Knesset House Committee.
