940406 FT 06 APR 94 / People: Finance moves Robert Morrow (below), 44, takes over next week from Riad Ghali, 48, as the London-based head of Bank of America's operations in its EMEA area. Morrow, an executive vice president who has worked at Wells Fargo and Merrill Lynch, joined Bank of America in 1990 and since August has been responsible for relationship management in EMEA.
