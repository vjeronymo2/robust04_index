920901 FT 01 SEP 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Helping the blind entrepreneur - In A Nutshell The Royal National Institute for the Blind has teamed up with the Prince's Youth Business Trust to provide assistance to young, visually impaired people who are self-employed. The two organisations will provide joint business advice while the trust, which already helps handicapped entrepreneurs aged under 30, will make its grants available.
