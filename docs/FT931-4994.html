930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / World Trade News: Sanctions against Taiwan sought Taiwan trade officials arriving in Washington yesterday were met with calls by a coalition of US businesses for trade sanctions, for Taipei's alleged failure to protect US patents, copyrights and trademarks, Nancy Dunne writes from Washington. Taiwan signed an agreement last June with the US trade representative's office promising to provide improved levels of intellectual property rights protection. The meetings yesterday and today are part of the US commitment to monitor the enforcement of the agreement. NEWS IN BRIEF
