Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, Jan 9 (AA) -- Thirty-one terrorists from the outlawed separatist organization and 10 people from security forces were killed in clashes in southeastern Sirnak, Mardin and Siirt Provinces. Emergency Regional Governor's Office told AA that 16 terrorists were killed in operations in Dargecit township in Mardin Province, in Guclukonak township in Sirnak and Ormanardi village in Siirt Province and a first lieutenant was also killed during operations. Fifteen terrorists were killed in clashes when they attempted to raid Sariziyaret and Okcular military posts in Sirnak and Siirt Provinces. Four rockets and 14 hand grenades were seized after the clashes. Nine people from security forces were killed during the clashes.
