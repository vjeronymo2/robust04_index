920630 FT 30 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Optometrics in black at Dollars 0.2m Optometrics, the USM-quoted optical systems specialist based in Massachusetts, reported pre-tax profits of Dollars 200,000 (Pounds 108,000) from turnover of Dollars 3.56m for the year to end-March. This compares with a loss of Dollars 136,000 from sales of Dollars 3.44m last time and consolidates the return to profits of Dollars 77,000 at the interim stage. Mr Frank Denton, chairman, said the improving financial position was allowing the company to invest more aggressively in new product development and sales marketing. Earnings per share came out at 1 cent (1 cent losses).
