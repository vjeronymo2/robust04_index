May 30, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

BRITON ASSAILS JEWISH SETTLERS

British Foreign Secretary Douglas Hurd said today it is wrong for Soviet Jews to settle on Israeli-occupied Arab land.

Speaking shortly before leaving London on an official visit to Jordan and Saudi Arabia, Hurd said: "We have worked for a long time to help the Jews leave the Soviet Union. They've been suffering a great injustice.

"But what we say to them and to the Israelis is, 'Don't correct one injustice by creating another.' It isn't right. It's not just, that Jews should go from the Soviet Union and settle in the occupied territories."

Israeli Immigration Minister Yitzhak Peretz was quoted Tuesday as saying the migration would reach 250,000 this year.
