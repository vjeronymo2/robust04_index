921202 FT 02 DEC 92 / International Company News: Receivers in at Canadian hotel SUTTON PLACE, one of Canada's best-known luxury hotels and part of the Kempinski world franchise, has gone into receivership. The owners are German investors in partnerships managed by Lehndorf, a Toronto property group trying to restructure heavy borrowings. The Toronto hotel carries about CDollars 30m (USDollars 23.3m) in debt, partly held by a group led by Fuji Bank Canada. It will continue to operate and has two potential buyers, Lehndorf said.
