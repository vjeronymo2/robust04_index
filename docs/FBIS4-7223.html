BFN [Text] At a meeting on 27 April with foreign diplomats accredited to the Kingdom of Cambodia, Prince Krompreah Norodom Ranariddh, first prime minister of the Royal Government of Cambodia [RGC], said that the RGC respects and fully supports the initiative of His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk, king of Cambodia, on the coming roundtable meeting in the royal palace. The prince krompreah further said that this roundtable is a meeting between the RGC and the Khmer Rouge [KR] and not talks. He clearly specified that the RGC will do all it can for the roundtable meeting to proceed smoothly by ensuring 100 percent security and safety. The prince krompreah said that if the roundtable meeting did not achieve results, a consultation will be held with the cochairmen of the Paris international conference to implement the third part of the Paris agreement. Concerning the Khmer Rouge's abduction of some foreigners in rural areas, Prince Krompreah Norodom Ranariddh promised to work for the safe return of these foreigners to Phnom Penh.
