BFN [Text] Talks between the Jordanian and U.S. officials, which began last night, continued this morning on the details related to bilateral relations. In a statement to the Jordanian news agency PETRA, Finance Minister Sami Qammuh said that the two sides are currently discussing bilateral economic issues, adding that there is a good U.S. readiness to discuss the details of financial and economic issues. He said: We will define the fields in which we can adopt serious and quick measures to strengthen Jordan's economy, revenues, and balance of payments. He said a serious study is under way of Jordan's debt to the United States and other countries. He said the meetings will continue over the next two days to complete discussion of the issues at the U.S. State Department and the headquarters of the Jordanian delegation.
