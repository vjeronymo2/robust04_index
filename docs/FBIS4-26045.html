BFN [Text] Beijing, 16 Jun (ZHONGGUO XINWEN SHE)--The Voice of China of China's Central People's Radio will officially start broadcasting from 18 June. Relying on advanced and excellent broadcasting equipment and media, its signal can cover Hong Kong, Macao, the Zhu Jiang Delta region, and remote islands. Concerned figures of the Central People's Radio said today: The Voice of China aims at serving compatriots in Hong Kong and Macao and vast numbers of listeners in the Zhu Jiang Delta region with timely and accurate news reports, transmission of all-round and high-quality information, and varied and colorful programs of entertainment and appreciation under the guidance of the policy of "one country, two systems" while holding aloft the banner of patriotism. It is known that the Voice of China, which merges news and information, entertainment, and services into an organic whole, will broadcast 21 hours a day, with the focus on three major special live programs: The "Golden Channel," a program mainly on news, current affairs, and economic and trade information service; the "Scenery of China," a program mainly on exhibiting China's folk customs; and the "Tender Night of China," a program mainly on cultural information and entertainment and appreciation. The reception frequency of the Voice of China is: Medium wave 1215 kilohertz, FM 104.9 megahertz.
