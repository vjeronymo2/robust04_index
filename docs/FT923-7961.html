920814 FT 14 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Interest cut boosts Ayrshire Metal Reduced interest costs enabled Ayrshire Metal Products to almost treble pre-tax profits to Pounds 115,000 in the first half of 1992, against Pounds 41,000. Sales fell to Pounds 14.4m (Pounds 15.6m) and the trading profit to Pounds 188,000 (Pounds 204,000) after redundancy charges of Pounds 74,000. Interest costs fell to Pounds 73,000 (Pounds 163,000). Earnings per share doubled to 0.8p.
