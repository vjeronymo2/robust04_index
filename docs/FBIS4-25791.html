BFN [Text] Beijing, June 14 (XINHUA) -- Wang Hanbin, vice-chairman of the National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, met here today with Mustofoev Buritosh [name as received], general procurator of Uzbekistan. Buritosh and his party arrived here on June 8 for a week-long visit to China as guests of the Chinese Supreme People's Procuratorate.
