BFN [Text] Alfredo Jordan Morales, Agriculture Minister and Politburo member, has said that the achievement of full integration of scientific centers is a priority, especially concerning the tasks of making furrows. At the national meeting on miscellaneous crops, the agriculture minister said that the accomplishments and experience achieved so far are rather dispersed. It was decided at the meeting that the next planting campaign in the province [not further identified] will be designed with the participation of experts in the area. Jordan presided over the creation of the 5 de Septiembre and 19 de Abril farms for miscellaneous crops in Jobabo Municipality in Las Tunas. These two farms work 50 and 40 caballerias, respectively.
