Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [POSTFACTUM report: "Georgia Ready To Guarantee Autonomous Entities' Rights"] [Text] Vakhtang Rcheulishvili, vice speaker of Georgia's parliament, leading a parliamentary delegation to Moscow, has stated that the Georgian side appreciates and shares Russia's anxiety over the status of Abkhazia and South Ossetia and is "ready to recognize all the rights that should be guaranteed to the autonomous entities." However, he pointed out, inside the republic there are opponents of such a viewpoint -- "political circles advocating only a unitary state, or the granting to Abkhazia and South Ossetia cultural autonomy alone."
