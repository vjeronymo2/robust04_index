Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 15 March (TANJUG) -- The UN High Commissioner for Refugees Sadako Ogata has said that her talks with Serbian President Slobodan Milosevic "were very comprehensive and beneficial." President Milosevic today received Ogata, who is staying in our country at the invitation of the federal government. In a brief statement after the meeting, Ogata said that she had talked with President Milosevic about problems relating to humanitarian convoys for ex-Bosnia-Herzegovina and about the problem of refugees. Ogata stressed that she had received assurances from President Milosevic that he would help in solving the problems facing humanitarian convoys.
