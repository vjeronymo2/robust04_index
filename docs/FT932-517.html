930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / World News in Brief: Sterling strengthens against D-Mark Sterling performed strongly against the D-Mark in London yesterday, rising 2 1/4 pfennigs amid suggestions that the pound could be one of the strongest performers on the currency markets in the next three months. The pound, which closed at DM2.5375, was also strong against the dollar, closing in London at Dollars 1.493 from Dollars 1.478 and in New York at Dollars 1.49625. Currencies, Page 29
