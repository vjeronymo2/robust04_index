921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / Construction Contracts: Remote monitoring A detailed design contract for remote monitoring of a production platform in the North Sea has been won by KVAERNER ENGINEERING, part of Norway's Kvaerner group. Placed by Ekofisk operator Phillips Petroleum, the NKr5m contract (Pounds 470,000) relates to the 2/4 Delta platform - also known as West Ekofisk.
