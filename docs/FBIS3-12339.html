Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A security meeting was held at the Army Intelligence Directorate last night to review the security situation in light of the church bombing and discuss coordination among the various agencies regarding investigations. The meeting was attended by heads of the judiciary and security services of the Army, Internal Security, Public Security, and State Security. A representative of the Syrian forces also attended. In Juniyah, Nasri Lahhud, the government's delegate to the military court, chaired a security meeting attended by Jabal Lubnan Public Attorney Tarabayh Rahmah, who noted there was a scheme to bomb churches. He said a suspect had been remanded earlier and he will be interrogated.
