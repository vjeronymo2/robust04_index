930519 FT 19 MAY 93 / Cut-price advice plan for jobless MANCHESTER Training and Enterprise Council yesterday launched a cut-price redundancy counselling service for non-executive workers after trials suggested it might reduce the time taken by some people to find new jobs. The service, claimed to be the first of its kind by a Tec - the bodies which administer government training in England and Wales - costs an employer Pounds 200 a day for up to 15 places on a tutorial session with a qualified counsellor.
