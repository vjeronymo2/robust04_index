[Declaration of Disaster Loan # 2730; Amendment # 2] Florida; Declaration of Disaster Loan Area The above-numbered Declaration is hereby amended, effective July 29, 1994, to establish the incident period for this disaster as beginning on July 2, 1994, and continuing through July 29, 1994. All other information remains the same, i.e., the termination date for filing applications for physical damage is September 8, 1994 and for economic injury the deadline is April 10, 1995. The economic injury number for Florida is 829500. (Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance Program Nos. 59002 and 59008.) Dated: August 12, 1994. Michael E.
