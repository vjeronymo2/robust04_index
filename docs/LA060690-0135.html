June 6, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

FIVE HIGH SCHOOL SWIMMERS ARE HEADED TO UCI

Five high school All-Americans have decided to attend UC Irvine and swim for the men's swimming team.

The five are:

-- Jason Sakamoto of San Marino High. He set a Southern Section 3-A record in the 100 breaststroke while leading his team to the title.

-- Kevin Bates of Ygnacio Valley High in Concord, another breaststroke specialist.

-- Eric Walton of Corona High. He set a 2-A Division record in the 50 freestyle, winning the event.

-- Bret Foreman of Wilson High in Tacoma, Wash. He finished second at the state meet in the 100 backstroke.

-- Pat Keenan of Richardson High in suburban Dallas. He was sixth in the state in the 100 butterfly.
