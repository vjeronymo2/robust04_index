931025 FT 25 OCT 93 / Construction Contracts: Sunley posts two new deals THE SUNLEY TURRIF GROUP has gained two Royal Mail contracts totalling Pounds 11m. The first involves the Pounds 5.6m conversion and fitting-out of two buildings, alterations and fitting-out of a third building and the completion of external works over 36 weeks beginning October 4. Architects are WM Saunders Partnership. The second contract, valued at Pounds 5.4m, includes the construction of a sorting and delivery office and road transport workshop. Work is due for completion April 1994. Sunley is at present working on a Pounds 5.2m Royal Mail contract, building a mechanised letter office with ancillary accommodation. Architects are Abbey Hanson Rowe.
