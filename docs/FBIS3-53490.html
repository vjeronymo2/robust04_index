Language: French Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] There was a huge demonstration this morning in Tizi Ouzou in Kabylia -- 150,000 people according to the police, 500,000 according to the organizers. The march was in favor of recognition of Berber culture, and, above all, against fundamentalism. The demonstrators carried portraits of Algerian intellectuals who have been assassinated in the last few months. The demonstration was led by Said Saadi, general secretary of the Rally for Culture and Democracy. [passage omitted]
