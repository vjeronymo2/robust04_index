February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE: LOS ANGELES COUNTY

A newly expanded board at Kings Road Entertainment reinstated Stephen Friedman as chairman and chief executive three weeks after the previous board relieved him of his duties.

Last week, Friedman reported boosting his stake in the Los Angeles-based independent movie production firm to about 41.5%, increasing the total shares to 2.1 million from 1.8 million. Friedman, who founded the company in 1981 and took it public four years later, also said in his filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission that he wanted to increase the size of the board and elect his own nominees to any new positions.

The company said the board was expanded to nine members from six before its meeting Monday, with written authority of holders of a majority of its shares, including Friedman. New directors are Robert L. Oppenheim, Martin Davidson and Ira Altcheck.
