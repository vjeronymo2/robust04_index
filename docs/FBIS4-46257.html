BFN [Text] Fatah-the Revolutionary Council group, led by Abu-Nidal [Sabri al-Banna], has announced that Jordan has released some of its members who were accused of involvement in the assassination of a Jordanian diplomat, Na'ib al-Ma'ayitah, in Beirut in January. The group, however, did not specify the number of the detainees who were released. The Jordanian police on 25 February announced that it arrested 25 members of Fatah-the Revolutionary Council within the context of this case.
