Claims Bet Hagay Shootings

Hamas Squad Claims Responsibility

TA1705083494 Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew 0800 GMT 17 May 94 TA1705083494 Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el Hebrew BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] In a telephone call to Qol Yisra'el's Arabic-language Department, the Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] 'Izz-al-Din al-Qassam terrorist squad claimed responsibility for this morning's attack near Hebron. The callers gave details about the way the attack was perpetrated. Army forces, headed by Major General Sha'ul Mufaz, IDF [Israel Defense Forces] commander in Judaea and Samaria, were rushed to the scene of the attack. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the vehicle in which the terrorists were traveling was found totally burned a short time ago in Hebron. [passage omitted]
