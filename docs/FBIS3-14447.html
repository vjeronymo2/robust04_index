Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz holds Israel responsible for the lack of progress in the peace process. He noted that the foreign ministers of Syria, Lebanon, and Jordan, who are now present in Cairo, may hold consultations to set an initial date for a meeting among themselves to coordinate their countries' policies on the peace talks. Buwayz noted that U.S. envoy Robert Pelletreau, who visited Beirut recently, told him that Israel and the PLO may sign an agreement for limited self-rule in Gaza and Jericho in April. Buwayz called on Israel to announce its commitment to withdraw from the occupied part of southern Lebanon.
