Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Article by "ryc": "According to Klaus, the Deliberations About the RFE Moving Are Premature"] [Text] Prague--Prime Minister Vaclav Klaus described on Wednesday [23 March] the deliberations on the conditions for moving the Free Europe Center (Radio Free Europe--RFE) from Munich to Prague as definitely premature because the U.S. party has not yet made its decision. Klaus said this before reporters in response to a RUDE PRAVO question on Wednesday. He added that the original promises that this matter "will be settled in October, December, February, and March are being constantly postponed." Klaus considers the transfer of the RFE from "a clearly government, state institution to another" an additional problem. Businessman Soros would "participate as a co-owner" in dealing with the latter problem. If this happened, the entire problem would move, according to the prime minister, to an entirely different level.
