January 26, 1989, Thursday, Valley Edition

CAL STATE NORTHRIDGE, CLIPPERS POSTPONE TALKS UNTIL FEB. 6

Cal State Northridge administrators have been asked to reschedule a meeting with officials from the Los Angeles Clippers.

The subject of the meeting, which twice has been postponed, is a possible cooperative venture in the construction and use of a proposed basketball arena on the CSUN campus.

Elliot Mininberg, a vice president for administration at CSUN, expected to meet with officials of the National Basketball Assn. franchise Tuesday. The parties tentatively are scheduled to meet Feb. 6. MIKE HISERMAN
