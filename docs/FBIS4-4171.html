BFN [Text] Kuala Lumpur, April 26 (XINHUA) -- Malaysian Prime Minister Mahathir Mohamed met with Jia Qinglin, chairman of the Standing Committee of the People's Congress of Fujian Province, China, here today. In a friendly and cordial conversation, Mahathir expressed his hope that the Fuzhou-Quanzhou highway project under the cooperation between Fujian Province and two groups of investors of Malaysia will be a success. The experience from the project can promote friendship and cooperation between the two countries, he said. Jia thanked Mahathir for his care and support for the project. He also briefed the Malaysian leader on Fujian Province and its success in carrying out the economic reform. Jia Qinglin arrived here on Saturday. During his stay, he has called on several ministers of Malaysia and met with Malaysian businessmen to discuss economic cooperation between Fujian and Malaysia.
