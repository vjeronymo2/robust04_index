May 28, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

SCIENTIFIC ATLANTA BUYS SITE IN BUSINESS PARK

Scientific Atlanta Inc. has purchased a 7-acre site in Pardee Construction Co.'s Sabre Springs Business Park, San Diego, for $2,587,000. Coldwell Banker represented the seller, and Cushman & Wakefield represented the buyer.

The buyer plans to build a $10-million, 108,000-square-foot manufacturing facility on the site.
