BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Blagoveshchensk [center of Amur Oblast], 14 Jun -- Boris Yeltsin is concerned that a region of such rich natural potential as Amur Oblast should be living off subsidies from the federal budget. The Russian president stated this today on his arrival in Blagoveshchensk. Replying to a question from an ITAR-TASS correspondent, Boris Yeltsin said that the Amur Oblast administration chief had visited him recently and they had put their heads together over how to revitalize the region. The president said that one of the aims of his trip to Amur Oblast and the Tuva Republic was to study the potential of these regions with a view to identifying measures to help extricate their economies from crisis.
