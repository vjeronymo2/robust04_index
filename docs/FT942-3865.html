940613 FT 13 JUN 94 / Business Travel: Mexico growth The growth of air traffic between the southern states of the US and Mexico in the wake of the signing of the Nafta free trade agreement was confirmed last week by USAir. It will start non-stop services between Tampa Bay, Florida and Mexico City on June 11. Flights will operate daily through the summer and then be reduced to three a week from September, reflecting seasonal travel patterns.
