BFN [Excerpt] The government has launched a campaign to deal with drug traffickers, with effect from today, and has called on Tanzanians to expose all those involved in drug trafficking. Addressing Mlandizi residents in Kibaha District, the deputy prime minister and minister for home affairs, the Honorable Mrema, said that the government had resolved to launch the campaign when it realized that Tanzania had now been turned into a transit point for drugs. The Honorable Mrema stated that the government would reward those who expose drug traffickers. However, he did not specify the amount to be paid to those who would assist the government in arresting the traffickers. He pointed out that, after five or 10 years, Tanzania would have many mentally destabilized youth as a result of drug abuse. [passage omitted]
