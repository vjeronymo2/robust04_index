April 30, 1990, Monday, Valley P.M. Final

LOCAL;

RAIL VICTIM FOUND ALONG TRACKS

A man who apparently fell off a train in the Canyon Country area was run over and killed by a rail car, Los Angeles County sheriff's deputies said today.

The body of the unidentified man, believed to be in his 20s, was discovered about 8 p.m. Sunday next to tracks near the Lang railroad station at Shadow Pines Boulevard and the Antelope Valley Freeway, deputies said.
