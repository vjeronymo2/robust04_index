National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases; Notice of Cancellation of Meeting Notice is hereby given of the cancellation of the meeting of the National Diabetes Advisory Board, National Institute of Diabetes and Digestive and Kidney Diseases, October 17, 18, 1994, Bethesda Marriott, 5151 Pooks Hill Road, Bethesda, Maryland, which was published in the Federal Register on September 1, 1994 (59 FR 45296). The meeting was canceled due to the expiration of the Charter. Dated: October 7, 1994. Susan K. Feldman, Committee Management Officer, NIH.
