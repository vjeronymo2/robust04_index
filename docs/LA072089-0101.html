July 20, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Renaldo Nehemiah, world record-holder in the men's 110-meter high hurdles, has been added to the field for Saturday's New York Track and Field Games.

Nehemiah will replace Olympian Arthur Blake, who was forced to drop out because of a groin injury and a hip pointer.

PattiSue Plumer, the U.S. record-holder in the women's 5,000 meters, also withdrew from the meet because of a virus.
