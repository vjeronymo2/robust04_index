920520 FT 20 MAY 92 / UK Company News: H Young advances 13% to Pounds 427,000 H Young Holdings, the optical, automotive, horticultural and electronics group, showed a 13 per cent advance from Pounds 377,000 to Pounds 427,000 in pre-tax profits in the half year to end-March. Turnover was up by Pounds 1m to Pounds 15.6m. At the operating level profits advanced by 51 per cent from Pounds 422,000 to Pounds 638,000 but the interest charge surged from Pounds 45,000 to Pounds 211,000. After tax of Pounds 141,000 (Pounds 101,000) earnings per share were unchanged at 1.7p. The dividend is held at 1.7p. During the year the optical division acquired Shades sunglasses business and the distributorship of Nikon equipment for eye testing.
