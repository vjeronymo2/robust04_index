940112 FT 12 JAN 94 / Tietmeyer new Group of Ten Chairman Mr Hans Tietmeyer, the Bundesbank president, will be the new chairman of the Group of Ten central bank governors who gather at the Bank for International Settlements in Basle each month to discuss international monetary issues, writes Peter Norman. Mr Tietmeyer succeeds Mr John Crow, the outgoing governor of the Bank of Canada, at the end of this month.
