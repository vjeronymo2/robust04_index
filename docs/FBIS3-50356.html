Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The National-Social Alliance of Slovenia [NSSS] last night sent an invitation to Alija Izetbegovic to visit Slovenia and meet Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, president of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia, who will be in Slovenia from 27 to 31 January, the Slovene press agency reports. The NSSS, which had originally invited Zhirinovskiy to Slovenia, announced that he would put forward new proposals for the settlement of the crisis in Bosnia-Herzegovina. The Slovene Foreign Ministry today denied, in a statement for HINA, reports that Liberal Democratic Party Leader Vladimir Zhirinovskiy will meet Slovene Foreign Minister Lojze Peterle during his visit to Slovenia. The Foreign Ministry said that Zhirinovskiy's visit to Ljubljana is private and that no meetings with any official bodies are scheduled.
