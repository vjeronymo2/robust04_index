October 15, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

BIG BEAT

As Anita Crane (wife of former announcer Fred Crane) says in her Oct. 1 letter, Tom Dixon, Tom Cassidy, Crane, the one and only mellifluous-voiced Carl Princi (she omitted the personable and witty Doug Ordunio) -- all announcers in the class days of KFAC -- do not have master's degrees in music.

If Mary Fain, with her stilted gibberish and fractured pronunciation of foreign names, was one of the two recent announcers touted as having master's degrees in a Sept. 17 letter from a KFAC executive, both she and the other one should demand refunds from their colleges.

LUCILLE BARNETT

Oceanside
