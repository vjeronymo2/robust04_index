Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 6 (KCNA) -- A son of Jihad al-Araj, third secretary of the Palestinian embassy here, was born at the Pyongyang maternity hospital on February 16. Mr. and Mrs. Araj wrote a letter to the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, asking him to give a name to their second child born at the Pyongyang maternity hospital. Upon receiving the letter, Comrade Kim Chong-il named the baby "Solmae," wishing him to grow in good health with a strong will and principle unyielding to any adversity. A ceremony took place at the Palestinian embassy Saturday to convey the loving care of Comrade Kim Chong-il for Solmae. A souvenir sent by Comrade Kim Chong-il was handed to the parents of Solmae amid a loud applause of the participants. Addressing the ceremony, the Palestinian ambassador expressed heartfelt thanks to his excellency the dear leader Kim Chong-il, who had once named a Palestinian girl "Jindallae," for naming the baby Solmae this time. The father of Solmae spoke next. Noting that his excellency Kim Chong-il with extraordinary leadership ability gave a name to his son, he said this greatly encourages the fighting Palestinian people. He vowed to fight with strong will and braveness for the victory of the common cause of Palestine and Korea. He said he would bring up Solmae to be strong as wished by his excellency the dear leader Kim Chong-il. He asked a Korean official concerned to convey a gift carrying his family's best wishes for longevity to his excellency Kim Chong- il.
