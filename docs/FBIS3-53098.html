Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riyadh, Jan 9, SPA -- Second deputy premier, minister of defence and aviation and inspector general Prince Sultan Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz reveived here today visiting Mali Foreign Minister Ibrahim Boubakar Keita who conveyed to him a verbal message from the Malian President Alpha Oumar Konare to the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz. The meeting was attended by the Malian Ambassador to the Kingdom Mahmoud Zouber.
