930115 FT 15 JAN 93 / US Panama pledge The US will withdraw almost half its 10,000 troops from Panama by the end of 1995 to comply with the Panama Canal treaties, the US military announced yesterday, Reuter reports. General George Joulwan, commander-in-chief of the Panama-based Southern Command, said the troop reduction was part of a treaty implementation plan drawn up by US and Panamanian officials.
