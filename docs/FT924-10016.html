921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / Record visitors to motor show 45 per cent up on the 452,704 who visited the last show in 1990. The numbers were thought to have been swelled because the Ideal Home Exhibition was staged alongside the show at the National Exhibition Centre. Sir Hal Miller, chief executive of the organisers, the Society of Motor Manufacturers and Traders, said it was estimated that more than half the visitors planned to buy a new car in the coming months. He added: 'To reach this level of success at a time of unprecedented recession is a remarkable achievement.'
