940222 FT 22 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Growth at Johnson Fry Utilities In its first interim report since flotation last July, Johnson Fry Utilities Trust raised net asset value per share by 30 per cent to 122.4p in the period from July 14 to January 31 1994. The company, which invests mainly in UK utilities, reported net revenue of Pounds 1.19m and earnings per share of 3.92p. A second interim of 1.6p makes 3.2p to date and a forecast total of 7.2p is reaffirmed.
