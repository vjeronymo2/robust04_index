BFN [Excerpt] A three-week training workshop on drug abuse and trafficking ended this morning. The workshop was organized on 5 April by the Togolese minister for territorial administration and security, with technical assistance from the UN Program for International Drug Control [UNPIDC]. It was conducted by the National Drug Laboratory. Here with the details is Gbode Elekonawo: [Elekonawo) The training seminar was attended by about 20 Togolese security force agents, who were initiated in various techniques for identifying illicit drugs. According to the organizers, drug enforcement agents often have problems identifying the many different kinds of drugs. That is why, as part of this assistance project to fight against drug trafficking and abuse, the UNPIDC donated identification kits to the gendarmerie and the police. [passage omitted]
