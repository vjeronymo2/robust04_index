BFN [Text] Kiev, [no dateline as received] -- The embassy of the State of Israel in Ukraine has informed UNIAN that it is planned that a document, drawn up in Kiev between 6 and 8 June by an Israeli delegation, led by the Uri Barner, deputy director general for cultural and scientific relations of that country's external political department, will be signed during Israeli Foreign Affairs Minister Shimon Peres's official visit to Ukraine on 16-17 June. This is a cooperation program between Israel and Ukraine in the culture and education sphere, aimed at implementing the agreement on cooperation in the above field, signed in Jerusalem on 12 January 1993. The program plans specific cooperation in education and culture (cinema, theater, music, fine art, literature, museums, archives), science, sports, TV, and radio, as well as exchanges of students, youth delegations, and specialists.
