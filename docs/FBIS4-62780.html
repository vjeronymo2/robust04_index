BFN [Text] Anatoliy Krasikov, the head of the Russian president's press service, informed a correspondent of the POSTFACTUM agency today that at 1730 the press service did not have any information regarding Boris Yeltsin's acceptance of Sergey Shakhray's resignation from the post of the deputy prime minister. Krasikov also confirmed information that today the president and Sergey Shakhray had a meeting. However, for the time being the head of the presidential press service did not report details of this meeting.
