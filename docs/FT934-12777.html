931025 FT 25 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Cathay International CATHAY INTERNATIONAL Holdings: Valid acceptances for the open offer have been received in respect of 2.15m new ordinary shares, about 14.2 per cent of the new ordinary to which qualifying shareholders, other than Cathay International Investment, were entitled. CCI has applied to take up 1m of its 33.7m entitlement. The balance has been conditionally placed by Baring Brothers.
