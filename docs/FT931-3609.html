930315 FT 15 MAR 93 / Australian share prices open lower following Labor's election victory Australian share prices opened sharply lower today following Labor's election victory with the All Ordinaries index falling 25.9, or 1.56 per cent, to 1635.6, Reuter reports from Sydney. The market had risen 7 per cent during the election campaign largely on expectations of victory by the Liberal/National party coalition.
