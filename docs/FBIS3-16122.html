Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Viktor Chernomyrdin, head of the Russian Government, ends his visit to Finland today. Touching upon Russia's relations with the Baltic countries at the concluding news conference in Helsinki, he expressed anxiety concerning the situation of the Russophone population in Lithuania, Latvia, and Estonia. Russia, said Chernomyrdin, does not intend to impose any kind of sanctions against the Baltic countries and does not intend to keep troops there, but these countries must observe human rights.
