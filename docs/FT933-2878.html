930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Bredero shows profit of Pounds 11,000 Bredero Properties, the development company 49 per cent-owned by Slough Estates, turned in a pre-tax profit of Pounds 11,000 for the first half of 1993, against a Pounds 4.43m loss last time. There were nil earnings per share (13.4p losses). Bredero is prohibited from paying any dividend to ordinary and preference holders until September next year.
