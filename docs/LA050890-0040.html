May 8, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

CAL POLY POMONA NEARS NCAA TENNIS TITLE

Cal Poly Pomona won seven tiebreakers and moved to within one victory of its first national title with a 5-2 semifinal win over fourth-ranked Abilene Christian at the NCAA Division II Women's Tennis Championships Monday.

In today's final, the top-ranked Broncos (24-1) play UC Davis, which beat Cal Poly San Luis Obispo, 5-4.

"This is a group of athletes that play great as a team," Bronco Coach Ann Lebedeff said. "They are very competitive."

That competitive edge was apparent after All-American Onnaca Heron (21-7) lost to Abilene Christian's Michelle King in the No. 1 singles, as Pomona won four of the next five matches.
