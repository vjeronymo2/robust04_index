930803 FT 03 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Scottish National The split capital Scottish National Trust had a net asset value of 67.3p per capital share at June 30. The figure marked a sharp recovery from the value of 21.9p a year earlier and just 2.8p at the trust's September year-end. Net revenue for the nine months to end-June declined to Pounds 9.24m (Pounds 11.32m), equivalent to earnings of 5.11p (6.44p) per income share. A third interim dividend of 1.8p reduces the total so far to 4.9p (5.8p).
