BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 15 (KCNA) -- A military delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea held talks with the high-level military delegation of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam in Pyongyang today. Present at the talks on the Korean side were O Chin-u, member of the presidium of the Political Bureau of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea and minister of People's Armed Forces, Vice Marshal Kim Kwang-chin, Col. Gen. Pak Chae-kyong, Lt. Gen. Pak Sung-won and other generals and officers of the Korean People's Army. Present on the opposite side were Gen. Doan Khue, Politburo member of the C.C., the Communist Party of Vietnam and minister of national defence, who is the head of the delegation, and Lt. Gen. Phan Thu, vice-minister of national defence, Lt. Gen. Le Hai, deputy head of the General Political Department of the Vietnam People's Army, Lt. Gen. Tran Hanh, deputy chief of the General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army, who are members of the delegation and suites and Vietnamese Ambassador to Korea Duong Chinh Thuc. The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.
