BFN [Text] Flight Control Centre (Moscow region) June 24 TASS -- The Russian cosmonauts continue their mission aboard the Mir space station. They conducted geophysical, astrophysical, technological and medical experiments this week. The crew was also preparing the ballistic capsule of the Progress M-23 ferry for its return to the earth. Friday's programme of work includes astrophysical experiments to study gamma-radiation flows of galactic origin and investigation of properties of various materials in open space. Under the medical control programme, investigations will be conducted to check up water-salt metabolism and to take clinical blood analysis of cosmonauts who experienced zero-gravity conditions for a long time. Work in space proceeds according to a preset programme. The cosmonauts are healthy and feel well.
