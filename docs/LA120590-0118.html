December 5, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWS WATCH

WILD & CRAZY: Comedian Steve Martin, quoted in the current Buzz magazine on his new movie "L.A. Story": "Certainly I'm no expert on L.A. I actually grew up in Orange County (Garden Grove), which I think of as a different animal than L.A. When I came up to Los Angeles in the late '60s, it was quite a shock. . . . You know, you'd see some guy riding a bicycle with a pyramid on his head." Tom Hamilton and Cathy Curtis
