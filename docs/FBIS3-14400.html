Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Mar (MENA)--During the past few days the security forces have seized 60 automatic weapons, a large number of pistols, and many other firearms of primitive manufacture. They also arrested 250 political suspects, some with earlier political convictions. This news came in an announcement today by Major General Mansur al-'Isawi, assistant foreign minister and Cairo security director. He said this latest campaign, carried out on the instructions of Interior Minister Hasan al-Alfi, focused primarily on Cairo's al-Matariyah, al-Basatin, al-Sharabiyah, and Manshiyat Nasir districts. Maj. Gen. al-'Isawi added that the security forces also rounded up 350 other people suspected or convicted criminals. He added that counternarcotics agents who assisted in the security campaign uncovered 350 narcotics cases in which various types of illegal drugs were involved. Two days ago one kg of heroin with an estimated value of two million Egyptian pounds was seized. Al-'Isawi reported that the security sweeps began several days ago and spread over the northern, southern, eastern, and western sectors of Cairo. The campaign, in which units from the Central Security Forces, Public Security, Police, State Security Detective Force, and the Traffic police participated, was designed to tighten the noose around fugitive criminals. The Cairo security director said that the campaign was fruitful.
