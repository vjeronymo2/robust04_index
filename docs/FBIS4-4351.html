BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 30 -- Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet received here today a high-level military delegation of Cambodia led by General Tie Banh and General Tea Chamrat, co-minister of national defence, now on a visit to Vietnam. During the reception, Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet highly appreciated the relations of friendship between the two countries and the two national defence ministries. He noted that the Vietnamese people are very glad at the current development in Cambodia, and expressed his deep sympathy at difficulties and trials being undergone by the Cambodian people. The Vietnamese prime minister reaffirmed that the Vietnamese people would do all they could to, together with other countries in the region, support the Cambodian people in national construction and concord. [sentence as received] On behalf of the Cambodian military delegation, General Tea Chamrat expressed his hope that the close neighbourliness between the peoples and militaries of Vietnam and Cambodia would be further developed in conformity with the aspiration of the two peoples and for peace, stability and prosperity. General Tie Banh informed Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet of the situation of the Cambodian military, and expressed his belief that the people and military of Cambodia will be determined to overcome the difficulties and trials to build a peaceful and prosperous life. In the same day, the Cambodian guests paid a tribute to Ho Chi Minh's mausoleum and called on his resident. [sentence as recieved]
