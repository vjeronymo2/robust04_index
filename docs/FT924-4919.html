921130 FT 30 NOV 92 / Construction Contracts: Fitting out headquarters in London A Pounds 10m management contract has been awarded to the P&0 company BOVIS CONSTRUCTION to undertake the fit out of a new headquarters building for the Chemical Bank at 125 London Wall, London EC2. Work will begin next month, with completion due in August 1993, ready for phased occupation by 800 staff from existing London offices. Fitting out the 165,000 sq ft of floor space on six upper and two basement floors involves internal partitioning, ceilings, carpeting, decoration, lighting and air conditioning.
