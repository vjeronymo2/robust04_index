920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Allegheny Ludlum - Correction An article in the Financial Times on April 25 incorrectly stated that Allegheny Ludlum Corp was in bankruptcy proceedings. The article should have said Allegheny International was in bankruptcy proceedings; Allegheny Ludlum is a profitable producer of specialty materials, which emerged from Allegheny International via a management buy-out in 1980.
