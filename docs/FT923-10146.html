920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / UK Company News: William Ransom advances by 68% William Ransom & Son, the pharmaceutical products manufacturer, reported pre-tax profits 68 per cent higher in the year to March 31. On turnover 15 per cent ahead at Pounds 6.91m pre-tax profits were Pounds 677,000 (Pounds 402,000). Earnings per share came out at 3.04p (1.84p) and the proposed final dividend of 1.245p raises the total to 1.77p (1.694p).
