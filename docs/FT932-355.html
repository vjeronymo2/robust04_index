930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Culver's placing and acquisitions Culver Holdings is raising Pounds 1.53m in a placing and open offer of 25.5m shares at 6p. Shareholders can claw back on the basis of 5-for-8. The company is acquiring Belle Vue (Holdings), a Newport-based BMW dealership, for Pounds 750,000, Flower Garages, a Nissan dealer in Cardiff, for Pounds 629,000, and the minority in Culver's insurance businesses at a cost of Pounds 629,000.
