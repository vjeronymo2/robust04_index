Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] President Juan Carlos Wasmosy is at this moment departing for Ecuador, where he will meet his colleague Sixto Duran-Ballen and congressional authorities in that country. After the signing of several bilateral cooperation agreements, Wasmosy will travel to Lima, Peru, where he will be received in a public session by the Peruvian Congress. The president will return to our country during the first days of February.
