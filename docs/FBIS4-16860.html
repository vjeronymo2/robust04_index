BFN [Excerpts] Li Peng, chairman of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, arrived in Tashkent yesterday [18 April] on an official visit. Uzbek President Islam Karimov and his wife met Li Peng and his wife at the airport. [passage omitted on reception ceremony] In his speech at the airport the Chinese leader said: I am very happy about my visit to Tashkent, the most ancient and famous city in the world. The visit by Uzbek President Islam Karimov to our country in 1992 began a new chapter in the history of friendly relations between our countries. I hope that the documents that are to be signed during our visit will still strengthen even more those friendly ties and will raise our economic cooperation to a new level. Welcoming the chairman of the PRC State Council to our country, President Islam Karimov said that Li Peng's visit to our country at a time when our political, economic, trade, and cultural contacts are strengthening day by day and developing [words indistinct] mutual relations between Uzbekistan and China.
