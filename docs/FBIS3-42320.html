Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Istanbul, Feb 6 (AA) -- Unidentified gunmen shot and slightly wounded Murat Bozlak, secretary general of the Democracy Party (DEP). AA was told that Bozlak was hit by four bullets in Kecioren District after midnight. Police said the assailants fled. Hospital officials said Bozlak was in good condition and recovering from his wounds. No one immediately claimed responsibility for the attack.
