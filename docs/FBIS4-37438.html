BFN [Text] China has expressed its willingness to support entry of India into the Asia-Pacific Economic Caucus, APEC. This was conveyed by the Chinese minister for foreign trade and economic cooperation, Ms. Wu Yi, when she called on the prime minister, Mr. Narasimha Rao, in New Delhi today. During the meeting, the two leaders felt that there is enormous potential for bilateral trade between the two countries. They appreciated the complementary qualities between India and China and said that there is a rich potential for establishing joint ventures for third-country markets. It was also emphasized that both countries should work for increasing direct bilateral trade and to avoid as far as possible the reexport trade.
