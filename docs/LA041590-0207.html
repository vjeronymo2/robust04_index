April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

DIANNE FEINSTEIN

Thank you for exposing the Feinstein mystique.

The facts, as the article pointed out, are that Dianne loves to portray working-class ethics only when she can benefit.

Yet she blames others for her lackluster showing.

Her golf picture says it all. There's Dianne using a one iron on a putting green, wearing shoes that are so new they are not even bent in the toes.

When Feinstein says it's not easy to break into Southern California politics, it reminds me of something my grandmother used to say: You can only gain the respect of others when you have learned to work as hard as they do.

JOHN L. RAMIREZ

Port Hueneme
