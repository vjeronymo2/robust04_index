Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Addis Ababa, Al Nowar [Feb] 4 -- JAMAHIRIYA NEWS AGENCY -- The assistant secretary general of the Arab League has called for the lifting of the harsh measures imposed on the Great Jamahiriyah by resolutions manipulated through the so-called `Security' Council. He said that the continuation of these sanctions would be a blatant injustice and a mockery of small nations, principles of international law and the United Nations Charter. In a speech to the 59th session of ministers of the Organisation of African Unity in Addis Ababa, the assistant secretary general of the Arab League said that we call for all resolutions adopted by the Arab League, the Organisation for African Unity, the Islamic Conference Organisation and the Nonaligned Movement to be complied with.
