October 21, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

TRI-VALLEY LEAGUE

Fillmore 33, Oak Park 14 -- Fillmore running back Tory Cabral took an inside handoff on the first play of the fourth quarter and sprinted 54 yards for a touchdown to spark a 20-point quarter.

Trailing, 13-0, at halftime, Oak Park (4-3, 0-2) took its second possession of the third quarter and drove 66 yards, scoring on a 38-yard pass from Mike Stern to Chris Ruhle.

However, Fillmore (7-0, 2-0) quickly put the game out of reach. Cabral followed his 54-yard run with a four-yard touchdown run, then linebacker Mike Kozar picked off a Stern pass and returned it 32 yards for a touchdown. GREG GUSS
