930109 FT 09 JAN 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa DE BEERS again led Johannesburg higher with a 5.7 per cent gain as its shares rose R3.50 to R64. Since Wednesday's announcement of better-than-expected 1992 diamond sales figures the group has risen nearly 11 per cent. The overall index put on 48 to 3,382, industrials firmed 58 to 4,536 but gold slipped 3 to 790 on a weaker bullion price with Vaal Reefs losing R2 to R141.
