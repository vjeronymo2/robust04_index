BFN [Text] Goettingen (DPA) -- On Thursday afternoon [31 March] about 150 members and sympathizers of the Goettingen "Solidarity Alliance for Kurdistan" tried in vain to block the Superhighway A 7 near Goettingen with bicycles. Police closed off the access points to the superhighway. According to the police, among the demonstrators there were numerous autonomous persons, but only very few of the about 400 Kurds living in Goettingen. Three Germans were temporarily detained. The action was to be aimed against German arms supplies to Turkey and the threatened deportation of Kurds. Having played cat and mouse with the police for several hours, the demonstrators ended their attempts to get to the superhighway via industrial roads and slopes with a protest demonstration in front of the headquarters of the police task force in the evening.
