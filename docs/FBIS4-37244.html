BFN [Text] Sanaa, June 17, INA -- Yemeni Planning Minister 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani warned against recognizing the separatists in the southern provinces. In press statements published by Yemeni press here today, Mr. al-Iryani said his government would retaliate against such recognition including the severing of diplomatic relations with any country recognizing the separatists.
