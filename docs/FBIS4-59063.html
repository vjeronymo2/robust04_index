BFN [Excerpt] Head of State His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan received His Excellency Robert Pelletreau, assistant U.S. secretary of state for Near East and North African Affairs, at the president's office this morning. The two sides discussed ways to promote relations between the United Arab Emirates [UAE] and the United States and the scope of cooperation between the two countries in all fields. They also discussed developments in the Arab and international arenas, and how to achieve stability in the region in the service of the interests of its people. [passage omitted]
