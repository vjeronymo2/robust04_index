910426 FT 26 APR 91 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables FRUIT AND VEGETABLES Pineapple at 85p-Pounds 2.00 each (70p-Pounds 2.00) is this week's best fruit buy reports the FFVIB. Cape black seeded grapes are abundant, and are priced at 80p-Pounds 1.00 a lb (80p-Pounds 1.00), along with grapefruit at 12-25p each (12-25p). English onions at 15-25p a lb (15-25p) are excellent value for money. Spania asparagus at Pounds 1.25-1.40 a lb is a good buy, together with cabbage at 20-30p a lb (20-30p) and potatoes 8-15p a lb (8-15p). Dutch and English tomatoes are superb this week at 65-90p a lb (65-90p). Spring onions are also a good buy at 35-50p a bunch (35-50p).
