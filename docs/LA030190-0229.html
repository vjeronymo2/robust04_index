March 1, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

STATE;

LOTTERY JACKPOT AT $12 MILLION

Nobody had all six winning numbers in the mid-week California "Lotto 6-49" game, so the $5.46-million jackpot rolls over to build a Saturday jackpot as rich as $12 million, lottery officials said today.

The numbers picked in the twice-weekly game were 26, 23, 5, 38, 30, 47 and the bonus number, 40.

There were four tickets with five correct numbers plus the bonus number, each worth $375,083 shares of a $1.7-million prize pool. Another 221 with five of six will share $773,000, each receiving $3,497.

There were 12,395 tickets with four of six, each worth $56 shares of a $702,000 prize pool.
