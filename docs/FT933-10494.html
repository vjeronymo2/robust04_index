930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / House prices rise in July HOUSE PRICES rose by a seasonally adjusted 0.9 per cent in July after falls in May and June, according to the Halifax building society's monthly index. The figures contrast with those from the Nationwide on Monday, which said prices rose by 0.7 per cent in July, and for the fifth month running. The Halifax said that the annual rate of house price inflation in the UK was now minus 3.3 per cent, compared with minus 4.5 per cent in June. The overall trend remained flat.
