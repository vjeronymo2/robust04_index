February 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: CULVER CITY;

EX-CITY ATTORNEY HIRED

The Culver City Council approved contracting legal assistance from former city attorney Joseph Pannone for projects such as Marina Place, Playa Vista and the city's environmental regulations.

Pannone's firm, Kane, Ballmer and Berkman, will be paid $115 per hour for his services. Pannone joined the firm last November after 10 years with the city.

Eleanor Egan will become city attorney on Feb. 27.
