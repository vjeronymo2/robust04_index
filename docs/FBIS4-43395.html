BFN [Text] The police discovered weapons and ammunition on board a yacht in Ibiza. This weaponry could have been destined for use by the Northern Ireland terrorist group, IRA. RNE in the Balearic Islands reports: [Unidentified correspondent] Customs officers and the Civil Guard intercepted a consignment of weapons on a yacht moored at the Marina de Botafoc leisure harbor in Ibiza. Four people were arrested during the operation: a Briton, an Irishman, a Canadian, and a New Zealand woman. Among the materiel seized were assault rifles and machine-guns, bullet-proof vests, and 10,000 cartridges. The police have not revealed where this materiel came from nor where the yacht was headed. The yacht had been moored at Ibiza's leisure harbor for some days. Initial investigations point to a possible link between the four arrested and the IRA terrorist organization.
