July 31, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

DUKES TRY TO END SKID AGAINST HEAT

The Newport Beach Dukes will try to end a two-match losing streak when they play host to the Charlotte Heat at 7:30 tonight in a Team Tennis match at the John Wayne Tennis Club.

The Dukes lost consecutive matches to the Los Angeles Strings Friday and Saturday. Trevor Kronemann, a four-time all-American at UC Irvine under Dukes Coach Greg Patton, is a member of the Heat.

A volleyball exhibition with Olympic gold medalist Randy Stoklos that was scheduled during a break in tonight's tennis action has been cancelled.
