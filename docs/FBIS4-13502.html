BFN [Text] An Islamic resistance group ambushed a joint Israeli-Lahdist patrol on the al-Rayhan road at 0900 today. Fighting ensued between the group and the patrol personnel and continued for 30 minutes. Preliminary reports indicate that a large number of casualties were inflicted on the enemy troops. Meanwhile, Israeli occupation forces inside the occupied region exchanged shelling with resistance fighters in Kawkaba and Ibil al- Saqy in Western al-Biqa'. The agents' radio admitted the resistance operation, but gave no details.
