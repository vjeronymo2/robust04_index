March 5, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

RANCHO SANTIAGO TO PLAY AMERICAN RIVER AT UCI

Rancho Santiago College will play American River in the quarterfinals of the community college men's state basketball tournament Thursday at 7 p.m. at UC Irvine's Bren Center.

Rancho Santiago (28-3) advanced with an 86-84 overtime victory over Glendale Saturday.

In the other quarterfinals at the Bren Center: Chabot (24-10) vs. Moorpark (24-10) at 3 p.m.; West Valley (29-2) vs. El Camino (23-10) at 5 p.m., and Imperial Valley (32-1) vs. Merced (22-10) at 9 p.m.
