920505 FT 05 MAY 92 / Warning to Tajikistan opposition Tajikistan's Communist-dominated government yesterday warned the Islamic and democratic opposition that it would use force unless they ended their month-long demonstration in Dushanbe, capital of the central Asian republic, writes Gillian Tett in Dushanbe. Government helicopters flew over the city dropping leaflets claiming the government had 'all the necessary force to break up the demonstrations'.
