BFN [Text] 'Ali Salim al-Bid, secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, has announced that he will resign once the war in Yemen ends and that he will call for new elections. In a press interview published today, al-Bid noted that people should not be dragged into the conflict. He said that President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih imposed on him a fait accompli after declaring war against the South on 27 April. Al-Bid added that he will never again take the hand of 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih.
