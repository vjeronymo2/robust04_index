October 6, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

RESIGNATION OVER SENTENCING LAW

The tough new sentencing guidelines aren't dehumanizing; exactly the opposite, they hold some promise that criminals pay dearly with hard time behind bars, providing a small measure of hope the rest of us might live a more human (read: secure) existence.

I believe there are many like myself who hope President Bush will replace Judge Irving with a fair-minded, energetic judge who will have no problem with tough sentencing, with luck, one who will also have "fun" doing so.

JEFF GHOLSON

Long Beach
