BFN [Text] Copenhagen, April 25 -- Polish Foreign Minister Andrzej Olechowski arrived in Copenhagen on Monday [25 April] for one-day working talks with Danish officials. Olechowski met with Peter Duetoft, the head of the parliamentary Foreign Affairs Committee and with members of the committee. Before noon he is also to hold talks with his Danish counterpart Niels Helveg Petersen, and then with Prime Minister of Denmark Poul Nyrup Rasmussen. The sides are expected to exchange views on the current situation in Europe and on Poland's foreign policy.
