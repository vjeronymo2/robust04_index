920513 FT 13 MAY 92 / International Company News: Singapore brokers in merger deal SINGAPORE brokerage company J Ballas Holdings will take over the Singapore and Hong Kong offices of the brokerage Citicorp Vickers, Bloomberg reports from Hong Kong. As part of the merger deal, Citicorp Vicker's parent Citicorp will take a stake of about 30 per cent in the enlarged JBH group and appoint three directors to its board, Citicorp said. The current Citicorp Vickers subsidiaries in Hong Kong and Singapore will operate under the name Vickers Ballas. Citicorp, which has not given any indication of the price of the merger, says that its Citicorp Vickers offices in other countries will not be directly affected.
