930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / Russia in Crisis: Tension rises in Ukraine THE shock waves caused by the spiralling crisis in Russia rolled over the neighbouring state of Ukraine yesterday when a senior parliamentary figure said Ukraine could not now rid itself of nuclear weapons while Russia constituted such an unstable neighbour, writes John Lloyd. Mr Dmytro Pavlychko, influential head of the foreign affairs committee, said that while Ukraine would sign the start treaties, 'we can rule out joining the Nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty, which makes Ukraine a non-nuclear state, primarily because of the situation in Russia'.
