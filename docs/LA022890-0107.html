February 28, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

ENTREPRENEURS: The editor of Irvine-based Entrepreneurial Woman, a new magazine out this week (D1), says women want something different from a business magazine. . . . "Women don't have as many role models as men," Rieva Lesonsky says. "They want to hear about who is doing these things, as well as what they're doing." . . . Elsewhere on the entrepreneurial front, Inc. magazine of Boston rates Anaheim as the 10th-most-entrepreneurial city in the nation, based on "job growth, business starts and proportion of high-growth companies." Kristina Lindgren and Mark I. Pinsky
