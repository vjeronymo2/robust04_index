November 17, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: ANAHEIM;

FLU SHOTS FOR SENIORS

Anaheim senior citizens can receive free flu shots Tuesday at the West Anaheim Senior Citizens Center.

The vaccines will be administered on a first-come, first-served basis by the Orange County Health Care Agency in cooperation with the city Parks, Recreation and Community Services Department.

Residents must be at least 55 years old to receive the free shots. The shots will be offered from 1 to 4 p.m. at the Trident Adult Education Center, 1800 W. Ball Road.
