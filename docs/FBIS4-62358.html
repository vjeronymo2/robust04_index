BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan 14 May TASS -- Relative calm prevailed in the Karabakh conflict zone last night and Saturday [14 May] morning. No combat actions were reported along the entire front line as of 1100 on Saturday, Nagorno-Karabakh information and press department told ITAR-TASS referencing reports of the Karabakh Army press service. Nonetheless, sources in Stepanakert reported that Karabakh positions were under fire in the Mardakert district of Karabakh. Stepanakert is looking forward to the arrival of Chairman of the CSCE's Minsk group for Nagorno-Karabakh Jan Eliasson and the Russian president's special representative Vladimir Kazimirov. The situation is likewise quiet on the Armenian border, where no incidents have been registered lately, ITAR-TASS learned from the press service of the Armenian Defence Ministry.
