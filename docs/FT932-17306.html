930401 FT 01 APR 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Kleen-e-ze Holdings KLEEN-E-ZE HOLDINGS: Sale announced of the business and certain assets of its industrial cleaning products business, Kleen-e-ze Hygiene to a subsidiary of Vikan of Skive Denmark, shortly to be renamed Kleen-e-Quip (UK). Consideration is expected to be Pounds 1.1m. Also announced is the purchase by its Innovations Mail Order subsidiary of Stockingfillas from the receiver for Pounds 700,000.
