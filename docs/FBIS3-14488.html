Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Yemeni Foreign Affairs Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah met with brother Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad al-Sabah, Kuwaiti first deputy prime minister and foreign affairs minister, in Cairo yesterday evening on the sidelines of the current Arab League Council session. During the meeting, the two ministers discussed fraternal bilateral ties, means to promote them, as well as issues of common interest.
