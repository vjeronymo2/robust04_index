June 12, 1990, Tuesday, Southland Edition

BUSH WILL MARK 66TH BIRTHDAY AT GOP BASH

George Bush turns 66 today and, like Barbara Bush, who turned 65 Friday, he planned no family celebration.

But Republicans were set to give him a birthday tribute with a big fund-raiser, the President's Dinner.

More than 4,000 people were expected to attend the $1,500-a-plate gala. Proceeds will go to Republican challengers in this fall's elections, when a third of the Senate and the entire House will be up for grabs.
