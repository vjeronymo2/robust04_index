BFN [Text] A short while ago Francois Leotard, French minister of state for defense, arrived in Bucharest at the invitation of his Romanian counterpart, National Defense Minister Gheorghe Tinca. Mr. Leotard was met at the airport by Romanian National Defense Minister Gheorghe Tinca; Petre Roman, chairman of the Chamber of Deputies Commission for Defense, Public Order, and National Security; Nicolae Manolescu, chairman of the Civic Alliance Party, His Excellency Bernard Boyer, French ambassador to Romania; and other members of the embassy, reports correspondent Daniela Simionescu.
