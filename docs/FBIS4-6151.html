BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 18 April Evening Edition] [Text] Commenting on the morning of 18 April on the process of selecting a successor to Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa, Tsutomu Hata, deputy prime minister and foreign minister, said, "Our job right now is to convey this message to the world: That Japan's policies remain unchanged, and that we intend to carry on with free trade." He reiterated that in the process of selecting a successor to Hosokawa, he would attach importance to an agreement in basic policy.
