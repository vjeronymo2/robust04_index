Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Tehran, Jan. 15, IRNA -- The first helicopter made by defence industries of the Islamic Revolution Guards Corps (IRGC) will be put into operation by the end of the current calendar year (March 20). Head of IRGC industrial organization, Reza Irankhah, talking on the eve of `Revolutionary Guards Day', said that all parts of the 2- seater helicopter except its engine had been made in Iran. He said the IRGC industries were engaged in military researches, which included building and revitalizing military equipments. Various kinds of boats, landing crafts and cargo and military vessels were among the equipments being produced by the IRGC industries, Irankhah added. [passage omitted]
