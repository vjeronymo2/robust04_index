BFN [Text] Dr. Wilfred D'Souza has been reinstalled as the chief minister of Goa. He was administered the oath of office and secrecy by the governor, Mr. B. Rachaiah, in Panaji this evening. Five other ministers also took office. They are: Mr. Vasu Paik Gaonkar, Mr. Suresh Parulekar, Mr. Ashok Naik Salgaonkar, Dr. (Karno Pigado), and Mr. Vinay Kumar Usgaonkar. Talking to newspersons after the swearing-in ceremony, the chief minister said he will act according to the wishes of the party high command on the induction of five dissident ministers who resigned last week.
