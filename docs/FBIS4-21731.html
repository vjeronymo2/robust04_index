BFN [Excerpts] Mexico City, 29 Mar (EFE) -- Today the Attorney General's Office (PRG) accused former policeman Tranquilino Sanchez Venegas of complicity in the murder of Luis Donaldo Colosio. [passage omitted] The suspicion that he participated is based on an analysis of the videotape broadcast on television and on graphic material in the Police's possession, where it appears that Sanchez Venegas opens a path for the murderer through the crowd while watching -- without acting to prevent -- as confessed murderer Mario Aburto Martinez shoots Colosio in the right temple. Various PRG officials, who asked to remain anonymous, indicated that the arrest of the former policeman was made after a lady friend of Aburto Martinez declared that on 11 March the two individuals met in a Tijuana park. According to official reports, the former policeman was employed by the Tijuana PRI for ``crowd control'' at the event were Colosio was murdered. [passage omitted] The PRG has indicated that for now no one else has been arrested as an accomplice in the murder, although several individuals are being investigated.
