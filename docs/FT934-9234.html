931111 FT 11 NOV 93 / UK Company News: BICC in Russian telecoms deal BICC, the international cables and construction group, has entered into a joint agreement to set up a communication cable business in Voronezh, central Russia. The new business is a joint venture between KWO Kabel, BICC's German cable-making subsidiary, Svyazstroy, the Russian state-owned telephone cable installation company, and the Cable Research Institute, Moscow. KWO will take a 25 per cent interest in the enterprise and will provide project management, technical know-how, manufacturing equipment and a training programme for employees. Its input in the form of machinery and expertise is valued at DM10m (Pounds 4m). The factory will start production in 1994 and will employ some 250 people.
