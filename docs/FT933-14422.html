930714 FT 14 JUL 93 / International Company News: IBM launches Risc server IBM is moving to capitalise more broadly upon its Risc (reduced instruction set computing) technology for high performance microprocessors with the introduction yesterday of a powerful Risc desktop server computer for use in networks of workstations, writes Louise Kehoe in San Francisco. The company also unveiled several new data storage products from its Adstar business unit, that provide faster access to data and reduce storage costs for workstation users. IBM announced price reductions of up to 34 per cent on a range of Risc System/6000 workstations and peripherals. The new server is priced in the US at Dollars 13,200.
