940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / Business Travel: China's rail plan China plans to begin work this year on a Yuan 6bn (Dollars 697m) railroad linking the commercially growing southern towns of Canton and Zhuhai, a Chinese-backed Hong Kong newspaper reported on Friday. Electric trains should cover the 138km route in about an hour. The link, which is scheduled to open in 1997, is part of a development plan for the buoyant Pearl River delta, which includes Canton and Zhuhai.
