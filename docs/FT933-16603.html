930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Bristol Scotts Bristol Scotts saw pre-tax losses jump from Pounds 562,257 to Pounds 2.58m in 1992. Turnover dropped from Pounds 11.5m to Pounds 10.1m. Interest paid took Pounds 1.41m (Pounds 1.9m), attributable losses came to Pounds 2.66m (Pounds 269,753) and deficit per share to 45.43p (4.69p).
