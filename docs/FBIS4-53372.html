BFN [Text] His Majesty Norodom Sihanouk Varaman, king of Cambodia, sent a message to His Excellency Khieu Samphan, chairman of the Party of Democratic Kampuchea [PDK] on 18 May. The message reads as follows: Excellency, I would like to deeply thank you for sending me a message in the name of the PDK to support my suggestion of a roundtable at my Chhanghsu-on residence near Pyongyang. I ask Your Excellency to give me a very clear and specific answer to the following question: Will Your Excellency attend the roundtable for national reconciliation and peace in Cambodia, that is, the roundtable which will be held at Chhanghsu-on on 27 May? With my deepest thanks, Excellency, please accept my highest regards. [Signed] Norodom Sihanouk
