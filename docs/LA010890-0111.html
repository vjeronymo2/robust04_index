January 8, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

INJURED BIRD ON DAY-TO-DAY BASIS

Celtics star Larry Bird, who sprained his left ankle last week, will skip practice today, according to team trainer Ed Lacerte.

"I saw Larry, and he had a mild amount of swelling and a moderate amount of pain," Lacerte said Sunday.

"He won't practice (Monday) so we'll make our evaluations on a day-to-day basis."
