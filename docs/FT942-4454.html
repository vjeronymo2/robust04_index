940609 FT 09 JUN 94 / International Company News: Newbridge Networks surges 85% Newbridge Networks, the Canadian supplier of high-speed data networking equipment, reports fourth-quarter net profits of CDollars 47.3m (USDollars 34.3m), or 59 cents a share, up 85 per cent from CDollars 25.5m or 32 cents a year earlier. Revenues were CDollars 164m, up 63 per cent, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. For the year ended April, net profit was CDollars 157.8m, or CDollars 1.98 a share, against CDollars 60m, or 81 cents, a year earlier. Annual sales were 80 per cent higher at CDollars 552m.
