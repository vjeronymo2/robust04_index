June 20, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NEWSMAKERS

Dirk Eastman has been named senior vice president, worldwide sales, for Western Digital Corp. Previously, he was vice president. Before joining Western Digital in 1987, Eastman was a marketing manager for International Business Machines Corp., president of National Computer Sales, general manager of ITT Courier and owner of his own computer company. Western Digital, headquartered in Irvine, manufactures computer products.
