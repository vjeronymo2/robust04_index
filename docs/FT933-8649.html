930814 FT 14 AUG 93 / Adjusted jobless figures THE FIGURES given in yesterday's regional breakdown of unemployment were the non-seasonally adjusted series. The seasonally adjusted figures for the regions and the UK as a whole are as follows: Northern Ireland 14 per cent North 12.2 per cent West Midlands 11 per cent North-west 10.8 per cent Yorks & Humberside 10.3 per cent Wales 10.3 per cent South-east 10.2 per cent Scotland 9.7 per cent South-west 9.8 per cent East Midlands 9.5 per cent East Anglia 8.4 per cent UK 10.4 per cent.
