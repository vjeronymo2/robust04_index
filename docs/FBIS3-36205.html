Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow February 3 TASS -- A French military delegation led by Defence Minister Francois Leotard arrived in Moscow on Wednesday. This is the first official visit to Russia by Leotard since his appointment French defence minister in the spring of 1993. On February 3, the French delegation will meet with First Deputy Defence Minister Andrey Kokoshin and Chief of the Russian General Staff Mikhail Kolesnikov. Leotard will visit the Moscow All Arms Military School. Upon his return from Tbilisi where he flew today for talks, Russian Defence Minister Pavel will take his French counterpart on a tour of an airborne division in Tula near Moscow. The visit is part of the Russian-French bilateral agreement signed during Grachev's visit to France in January 1993 on mutual contacts, delegation exchanges and training Russian servicemen in France and their French colleagues in Russia. Leotard also plans to meet with First Vice-Prime Minister Oleg Soskovets and Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev who visited France in the autumn of 1993. During the visit they signed bilateral agreements on cooperation to dismantle nuclear weapons in Russia and ensure their safe transportation.
