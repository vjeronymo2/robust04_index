June 22, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

ORYX ENERGY MAKES OIL, GAS DISCOVERY

Oryx Energy Co. said it made an oil and gas discovery in the Gulf of Mexico that flowed at a combined peak total rate of 11,356 barrels of oil and 9 million cubic feet of gas a day.

Oryx said the well, about 140 miles southeast of New Orleans, is in 1,726 feet of water.
