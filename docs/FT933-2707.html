930917 FT 17 SEP 93 / Labour turns its fire on Lib Dems LABOUR turned its fire on the Liberal Democrats yesterday, saying their local government record contrasted with the stance of the party's MPs. Mr Jack Straw, shadow environment secretary, ridiculed Mr Paddy Ashdown, Liberal Democrat leader, as posing as a UK Ross Perot. 'Our very own anti-politics populist,' he said, was like the former US presidential candidate in claiming to be an outsider taking on the political establishment.
