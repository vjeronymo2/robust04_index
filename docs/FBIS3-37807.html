Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] On Thursday [17 February] evening, Belarusian Prime Minister Vyacheslav Kebich had a telephone conversation with his Russian counterpart, Viktor Chernomyrdin. According to the press service of the Belarusian Cabinet of Ministers, issues regarding the unification of Belarusian and Russian monetary systems and the work of experts who are drafting the corresponding documents were discussed. The premiers agreed to meet as a matter of urgency and discuss in detail the final draft agreements. Prime Minister Vyacheslav Kebich left this morning for Moscow.
