930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / UK Company News: GEI Pre-tax profits at GEI, the specialist engineer, fell to Pounds 2.1m for the year to March 31, compared with Pounds 2.8m. The figures were produced on an FRS 3 basis which cut the comparable by Pounds 300,000. Turnover on continuing operations improved from Pounds 76.7m to Pounds 78.2m, with a further Pounds 516,000 (Pounds 1.22m) from discontinued activities. About half the turnover and the majority of operating profits was achieved overseas. This has resulted in difficulties with the short term recovery of advance corporation tax and the company has written off Pounds 1.5m of ACT through the P&L account. That has resulted in retained losses of Pounds 2.42m (Pounds 540,000). Losses per share were 1.62p (5.86p earnings). The proposed final dividend is cut to 2.47p making a total of 4.94p (7.32p).
