BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Nikolay Kondaratenko, Natalya Cherepanova] [Text] St. Petersburg April 5 TASS -- Swedish Foreign Minister Margaretha Af Ugglas arrived in St. Petersburg on Tuesday. Ugglas, who arrived in Russia on a working visist, will make a trip to the city of Pskov where she will meet with Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev. Margaretha Af Ugglas is a deputy of the Swedish parliament from a moderate coalition party. Earlier, Ugglas was a deputy of the European Council. She has a degree of an economist, but for some time worked as a journalist. Presumably, the schedule of her two- day visit to St. Petersburg was arranged taking into account her professional interests. Ugglas will meet city leaders to discuss problems of regional cooperation, including projects financed by Sw6den, and take part in a bank seminar, seminars on export technique and a seminar entitled "Journalism and Democracy." The foreign minister will visit one of St. Petersburg enterprises, visit the St. Petersburg State University and meet representatives of the press.
