Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Turkey's participation in the peace force in Bosnia-Herzegovina registers it in the international arena as a country that contributes effectively to the installation of peace and stability in the region. In his weekly news conference, Foreign Ministry acting spokesman Ferhat Ataman said that now that Turkey is joining the peace force in Bosnia-Herzegovina, it will be able to contribute more effectively to the establishment and protection of peace and stability in the region. Turkey also directly participates in activities such as the implementation of the UN no-fly zone in the region, Ataman recalled, adding that these efforts will continue in the future as well.
