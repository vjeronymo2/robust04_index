BFN [Text] The German charge d'affaires in Tehran met the deputy foreign minister for Europe and America affairs this afternoon and handed over a message from German Chancellor Helmut Kohl to Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani. In his message the German chancellor welcomes the deepening of ties between his country and Iran and stresses the strategic importance of the Persian Gulf region and the necessity for maintaining security in the region by Persian Gulf littoral states and for regional and international cooperation.
