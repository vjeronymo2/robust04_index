June 28, 1990, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPORTS ET CETERA: SYNCHRONIZED SWIMMING

The San Diego Sweetwater Dolphins' top finishers took three firsts and a second at a national age-group synchronized swimming meet in St. Louis.

In the 13-14 division, Emily Marsh, Jolie Taylor and Amy McDaniel finished first in trio competition. Marsh and Taylor also won as a duet.

In the 11-12 division, Tammy Taylor was first in compulsory figures and teamed with Amber McDaniel for second in duet.

RUNNING

The Firecracker 10 kilometer, a popular summer run for the past 10 years, has been canceled this year according to the Oceanside Chamber of Commerce. It will be reorganized in 1991.
