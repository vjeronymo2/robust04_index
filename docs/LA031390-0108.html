March 13, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

HARNESS DISPUTE IS HEARD

Both sides in the dispute over the disqualification of about 150 harness-racing drivers at Los Alamitos presented their case before an administrative law judge during a six-hour hearing Monday in Costa Mesa.

The judge, Amanda Behe, may submit her opinion in time for the California Horse Racing Board meeting in Albany on March 30.

The board can either accept or reject Behe's decision.
