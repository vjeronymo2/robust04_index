941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / UK Company News: 15% rise at Prop Partnerships Property Partnerships, the developer and hotel owner, announced a 15 per cent rise in pre-tax profits, from Pounds 994,000 to Pounds 1.14m, in the six months to September 30. Turnover grew to Pounds 3.5m (Pounds 3.19m). At the operating level the hotels side contributed Pounds 416,000 (Pounds 238,000) while property investment gave Pounds 839,000 (Pounds 845,000). The interim dividend is 2.7p (2.6p), payable from earnings of 7.44p (6.5p) per share.
