Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Leaders of the Yavlinskiy bloc, prominent economist Grigoriy Yavlinskiy told Interfax on Sunday that he knew nothing about the possibility of replacing Gaydar in the reorganized government. "No one has talked with me about this," he said. The news about Yavlinskiy's possible government appointment was revealed by Yegor Gaydar during a briefing on Sunday with reference to his talks with Prime Minister Chernomyrdin. Asked by Interfax if he would accept such a proposal if it were made, Yavlinskiy said: "I don't think so, although it's early yet to speak about this."
