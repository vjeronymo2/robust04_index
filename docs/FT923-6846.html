920821 FT 21 AUG 92 / Technology (Worth Watching): A presentation transformation Electronic presentation technology, using computer-generated slides, graphics and video can transform an ordinary business seminar into an impressive event. A guide to the latest innovations in electronic presentation technology, published by Barco, a UK audio-visual communications specialist, provides a simple but useful description of the equipment and methods that can be used to enhance business presentations. As well as describing the technology, the booklet includes practical advice on the basic aspects of presentation such as room size, seating layout and the right environment. Barco: UK, 0734 664611.
