940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / Six candidates for Eastleigh poll Six candidates are standing in the Eastleigh by-election, to be held on June 9 to coincide with the European elections. As well as candidates from the three main parties and veteran by-election fighter Lord Sutch, there are candidates from the UK Independence party and the Natural Law party. The Conservatives are defending a general election majority over the Liberal Democrats of 17,702.
