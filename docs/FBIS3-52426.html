Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, PLO Executive Committee member, conferred yesterday with Mr. John McCarthy, the U.S. ambassador to Tunis. During their meeting, they reviewed the latest development in the Middle East peace process, the progress of current Palestinian-Israeli negotiations to implement the Declaration of Principles, Israel's obstructions, and efforts being exerted to implement the declaration and bring about an Israeli withdrawal.
