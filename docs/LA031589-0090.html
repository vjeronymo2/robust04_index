March 15, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

RADIO STATION SAYS MICHIGAN'S FRIEDER WILL BE NAMED COACH AT ARIZONA STATE

Michigan Coach Bill Frieder will be named coach at Arizona State, a Phoenix radio station reported Tuesday night.

Station KTAR, which broadcasts the school's sports, quoted unnamed sources as saying Frieder would appear at a news conference scheduled by Arizona State officials for today.

Arizona State officials announced they would hold a news conference at 8 a.m. on an undisclosed topic.

Earlier Tuesday, Purdue's Gene Keady said he turned down an offer to coach at Arizona State.

The Sun Devils finished the season under interim coach Bob Schermerhorn.

Frieder is in his ninth year at Michigan.
