Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Everyone has been waiting to hear what Russian Finance Minister Boris Fedorov has to say in response to Yegor Gaydar's announcement that he is quitting -- or rather that he is not accepting -- the job of first deputy prime minister. Boris Fedorov said today in an interview with the Ekho Moskvy radio station that he looks upon Yegor Gaydar's decision with understanding. Fedorov called this move the beginning of a turning point. As far as he himself is concerned, in the finance minister's words, he has not yet received an offer to enter the new government. If he does receive one, Fedorov remarked, he will reach his decision this Monday or Tuesday.
