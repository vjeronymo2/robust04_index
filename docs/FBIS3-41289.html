Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [INTERFAX report under "From Our Correspondents and News Agencies" rubric: "Incidence of Drug Addiction in Maritime Kray 29 Times the Russian Average"] [Text] The scale of the spread of drug addiction in Maritime Kray has become socially dangerous in nature. The incidence of this affliction is 29 times the Russian average, INTERFAX was informed by the kray internal affairs administration. The estimated value of the 1,247 kg of drugs seized en route to the black market is over 8 billion rubles. During the year the number of drugs-related crimes increased 22.6 percent.
