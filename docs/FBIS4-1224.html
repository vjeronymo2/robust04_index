BFN [Report by Craig Doonan] [Text] Durban April 24 SAPA -- African National Congress President Nelson Mandela will vote in Durban on April 27, according to ANC Women's League official Dr Nkosazana Zuma. Dr Zuma, wife of ANC Natal premier-elect Jacob Zuma, told reporters after Mr Mandela addressed a massive rally at Durban's King's Park on Sunday that he would vote in area, but she would not reveal at which polling station.
