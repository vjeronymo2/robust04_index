BFN [Excerpt] Prague, May 12 (CTK) -- The Assembly for the Republic-Czechoslovak Republican Party (SPR-RSC) said today "it must call on Vaclav Havel to immediately resign over the international shame which he has caused by the choice of his friends and collaboators for important posts" at the Presidential Office (KPR). In its statement given to CTK by a member of the extreme-right SPR-RSC leadership, Josef Krejsa, the party said that in connection with the report by the Supreme Inspection Office (NKU) on the KPR's financial management it would also "have to lodge with the NKU a complaint of (KPR head) Josef Dobrovsky." "The strange management of state property, probably involving bribes and unjustified payments, are the best proofs of the character of not only Havel's collaborators, but of himself as well," the statement says. According to the NKU report, the KPR made last year unsubstantiated payments amounting to 769,753 crowns (about $25,500) from its budget. [passage omitted]
