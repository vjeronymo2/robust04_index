920922 FT 22 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Weaker pound welcomed LORD HOLLICK, above, managing director of MAI, the international services group, said the weakening of the pound would help in the translation of group profits from overseas. North America was the biggest profit earner in geographic terms last year with Pounds 33.2m of the Pounds 71.2m pre-tax total. The UK contributed Pounds 22.1m. While money and securities broking provided the majority of the profits, the smaller divisions contributed as follows: retail financial services - mainly credit for car purchases and car insurance - made Pounds 6.5m (Pounds 6.9m); information, Pounds 4.7m (Pounds 3m); media (the Avenir stake), Pounds 2.2m (Pounds 6.6m); property, Pounds 3.4m (Pounds 2.3m).
