Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] President Eduardo Frei Ruiz-Tagle yesterday confirmed some appointments in Defense Undersecretariats. He also confirmed Admiral Patricio Arancibia as National Defense chief of staff. Thus, lawyer Jorge Burgos will remain as War under secretary; lawyer and political scientist, Carlos Eduardo Mena, is the Navy under secretary; lawyer Luciano Fouillioux is the Carabineros under secretary; lawyer Mario Fernandez is the Air Force under secretary; and retired Brigadier Jorge Pantoja is the Investigative Police under secretary. Lieutenant Colonel Ramon Iturra (Army), Corvette Captain Antonio Idiaquiz (Navy), Group Commander Javier Cancino (Air Force), and Commander Fernando Vidal (Carabineros) become The Armed Forces and Forces of Order aides de camps of Defense Minister Edmundo Perez Yoma.
