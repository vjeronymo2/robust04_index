July 22, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

IRVINE MEADOWS PARKING

After a rock concert on July 8, it took me 2 3/4 hours to get out of the parking lot of Irvine Meadows Amphitheater. This was a deterioration from two months earlier, when it took just under two hours to get out after a show.

Once the concert managers have our ticket money and concessions money, they don't seem to care what happens to us; after all, we are a captive audience when the concert is over.

JEANNE DOUGLAS

Los Angeles
