BFN [Report by N. Lewis] [Text] Johannesburg April 28 SAPA -- The Transitional Executive Council [TEC] on Thursday night ruled that voting in six regions be extended to 7PM on Friday, April 29. The regions are the Transkei, Ciskei, Venda, Lebowa, Gazankulu and KwaZulu. A statement said Friday would not be a public holiday and the extension now meant that counting of votes would begin at 6AM on Saturday, April 30.
