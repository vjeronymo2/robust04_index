940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Progress continues at Alphameric Alphameric, the information technology group, continued to progress through the second six months and for the year to end-March swung from losses of Pounds 321,000 to profits of Pounds 433,000 pre-tax. Turnover improved from Pounds 6.83m to Pounds 10.8m. Earnings per share worked through at 1.1p (losses 1.4p). There is again no dividend but the cash position remains healthy with nil gearing.
