December 28, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

The law firm Irell & Manella has named the following partners: Richard H. Abramson, Wayne M. Barsky and Catherine H. Helm, Century City office; Ede C. Ibekwe, Los Angeles; Michael G. Ermer and Patrick J. Evans, Newport Beach, and Gladys H. Monroy, Menlo Park.
