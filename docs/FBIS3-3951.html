Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The Second Session of the Eighth Yunnan Provincial People's Congress successfully wound up in Kunming this morning. The session called on state organs at all levels, as well as people of all nationalities in Yunnan to continually and profoundly study Volume 3 of the Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping; conscientiously implement the spirit of the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee; fulfill all tasks laid down at the current session; acquire a clearer understanding of the current situation; further emancipate their minds; work harder; carry out work in a down-to-earth manner and with one heart and one mind; live a plain life; strive to more expeditiously build a socialist market economy structure; and fulfill all the economic and social development tasks set for this year. This morning's closing meeting was presided over by the second session's executive chairman, Yin Jun. Pu Chaozhu [secretary of the provincial party committee] also attended this morning's closing meeting. [passage omitted] The closing meeting adopted the following resolutions: 1) Resolution on the Yunnan Provincial People's Government Work Report; 2) Resolution on the Implementation of the 1993 Yunnan Provincial National Economic and Social Development Plan and on the 1994 Yunnan Provincial National Economic and Social Development Plan; 3) Resolution on the Implementation of the Yunnan Provincial Financial Estimates and on the 1994 Yunnan Provincial Financial Estimates; 4) Resolution on the Yunnan Provincial People's Congress Standing Committee Work Report; 5) Resolution on the Yunnan Provincial Higher People's Court Work Report; 6) Resolution on the Yunnan Provincial People's Procuratorate Work Report. Yin Jun delivered a speech in which he maintained: The current congress session is being held at a crucial moment and at a time when China is entering a new stage of reform, opening up, and economic development. The participants in the current session have held discussions on such topics as reform, development, and stability; drawn on collective wisdom; absorbed all useful ideas; acted as masters of the country; and demonstrated a strong sense of mission. A large number of work reports and resolutions have been deliberated on and adopted at the current session, thereby affirming our achievements; summing up experiences; laying down clear-cut tasks, goals, and work measures for this year; and fully embodying the will and aspirations of the people of all nationalities in Yunnan. The current congress session has been a successful, democratic, united, truth-seeking, and confidence-boosting meeting, capable of mobilizing the people of all nationalities in Yunnan to work with one heart and one mind, continually carry out explorations, and constantly forge ahead. Now that the current congress session has laid down clear-cut goals and tasks for Yunnan's reform, construction, and development in 1994, the crux of the matter lies in carrying out these goals and tasks to the letter and in conducting our work in a down-to-earth manner. Yin Jun concluded his speech by saying: Let us unite more closely around the CPC Central Committee, with Comrade Jiang Zemin as the core; hold aloft the great banner of Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics; and mobilize and unite the people of all nationalities across the province to bravely forge ahead to build a new socialist economy structure and to fulfill all the economic and social development goals set for this year under the leadership of the Yunnan Provincial CPC Committee! He Zhiqiang [governor] also attended this morning's closing meeting. [passage omitted]
