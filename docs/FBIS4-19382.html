BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] Turkey has announced that in the existing sensitive atmosphere in the region, it is important that all Balkan countries refrain from actions that will further strain existing tensions. In reply to a question, Foreign Ministry spokesman Ferhat Ataman said that reports on a raid organized on 10 April against a military training camp on Albanian territory near the Greek border cause regret and concern for the future of the region. Expressing understanding for the concern felt by Albania in the face of provocations and threats, Ataman remarked that countries that plan to pressure their neighbors through the actions of terrorist groups should realize that they cannot be left out of the negative developments that stem from such provocations.
