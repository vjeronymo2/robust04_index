Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Some 400 Cape Verdeans were involved in clashes last night in Prior Velho near Loures [north of Lisbon]. The clashes caused the death of one person, and three Republican Guards were injured. The incident started at a cafe and went on for four hours: [Paula Colaco] Last night was an agitated one in Prior Velho. One dead and three injured policemen are the results of four hours of shooting and stone throwing between the Republican Guards and some 400 people of African origin. It all started in a cafe, where the residents of the Quinta da Serra shantytown usually meet. According to the Republican Guards, the disturbances started at about 2000 [2100 GMT], when the cafe owner ordered a misbehaving couple to leave. The couple refused to leave the premises and the incident developed from there. But some of those present tell a different story: [Begin recording unidentified Cape Verdean witness] The couple was doing nothing. We were watching football on TV. The boy was sitting and the girl was sitting on his lap. [end recording] [passage omitted] For the present, the cafe owner is under arrest, but the Quinta da Serra residents are threatening to take justice into their own hands. [passage omitted]
