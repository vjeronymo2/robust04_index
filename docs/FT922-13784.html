920409 FT 09 APR 92 / UK Company News: Sykes-Pickavant Sykes-Pickavant Group, the USM-quoted manufacturer and distributor of automotive, industrial and DIY tools, saw a further deline in profits in 1991 following a 25 per cent fall in 1990. The 39 per cent setback this time to Pounds 731,000 (Pounds 1.19m) pre-tax came on turnover virtually unchanged at Pounds 18.2m. Directors said that the results had been particularly affected by the state of the motor industry. Earnings per share emerged at 5.25p against 8.33p but the total dividend is held at 5.75p with a proposed maintained final of 3.5p.
