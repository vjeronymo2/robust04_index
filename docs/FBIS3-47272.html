Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 9 (KCNA) -- The New Year address of the great leader President Kim Il-song has been widely reported by foreign media. The Russian paper RABOCHAYA TRIBUNA, the Iranian papers RESALAT, KAYHAN INTERNATIONAL and JAHON ISLAM and Radio Iran noted that President Kim Il-song in his New Year address showed ways for the settlement of the nuclear and reunification issues on the Korean peninsula. The Lao News Agency conveyed in detail the words of President Kim Il-song that the Korean people must thoroughly implement the agriculture-first, light industry-first and foreign trade-first policies and the DPRK shall consistently adhere to the foreign policy of independence, peace and friendship this year. The New Year address was also reported by papers JOURNAL DE MADAGASCAR, IMONGO VAOVAO, TRIBUNE and the ANTA News Agency and TV of Madagascar, papers MZALENDO, SUNDAY NEWS, THE EXPRESS and SHIHATA News Agency and a radio of Tanzania, the Togolese paper TOGO-PRESSE, radios and TV of Zimbabwe, the JANA News Agency of Libya, a radio and TV of Equatorial Guinea, the U.S. papers WASHINGTON POST and NEW YORK TIMES, the British paper DAILY AWAZ, the AFP News Agency of France and German-language radios of Switzerland from January 1 to 3.
