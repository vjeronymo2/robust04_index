BFN [Text] Istanbul, May 7 (A.A) -- Two people were wounded when unidentified people threw a bomb at the office of the fundamentalist Prosperity Party (RP) in Bayrampasa District of the town. AA reporter was told by security officials that the attacks took place around 21:00 hours local time when two people threw the bomb at the office and escaped. Windows of near-by buildings were shattered and the office suffered great material damage, it was reported. It was later determined that the bomb was a Russian made "fragment effect" bomb. The wounded were immediately taken to hospital, police said adding a wide-scale operation was started in the area to catch the assailants.
