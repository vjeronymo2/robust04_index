BFN [Text] Knin, 21 Apr (TANJUG) -- At a session in Knin this afternoon, deputies of the republic of Serb Krajina [RSK] assembly elected a government of national unity proposed by Borislav Mikelic. Fifty deputies voted in favor of the government, 14 were against, and nine abstained. The election of the government was preceded by a brief discussion during which the deputies gave overall support to the program and composition of the government. There will be no representatives of the Serbian Radical Party in Mikelic's cabinet, but Mikelic himself said that a reconstruction of the government could be expected very soon because of the construction of new ministries. Mikelic added that the first government session will be held tomorrow to put the new program into action. RSK President Martic told TANJUG that the election of the new government greatly contributes to the stabilization of internal affairs in Krajina.
