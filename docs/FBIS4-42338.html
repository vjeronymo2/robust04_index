BFN [Text] Greece has warned Ankara that its slanderous and unfounded accusations against Greece cannot coexist with the appeals for a climate of friendly relations. The accusations also threaten stability in the region and create serious questions about Turkey's role and intentions. Greek Ambassador in Ankara Alexandros Filon conveyed this strong warning to the Turkish Foreign Ministry during a meeting with the Turkish deputy foreign minister yesterday. Filon was reacting to Ankara's accusations about supposed training of PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] guerrillas in Greece.
