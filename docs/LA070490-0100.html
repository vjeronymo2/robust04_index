July 4, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Pickens Vows to Hike Koito Stake: Texas oilman T. Boone Pickens Jr. said he will move quickly to boost his stake in Koito Manufacturing Co. to 33% from 26%, despite another defeat of his attempt to gain seats on the board of the Japanese auto parts maker. Pickens, who lost his bid to secure four board seats at Koito's annual stockholders meeting in Tokyo last week, said he plans to buy more Koito stock that could cost him an additional $160 million at about $20 a share. By increasing his stake to 33%, Pickens said he would, under Japanese law, be allowed participate in decisions made by management.
