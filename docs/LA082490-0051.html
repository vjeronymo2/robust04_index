August 24, 1990, Friday, Southland Edition

BRIEFLY

$52-Million Fine upheld: Russell County Circuit Judge Donald McGlothin Jr. ordered the filed a joint motion to dismiss the fines after settling their 10-month-long strike Feb. 19.

/The walkout involved 1,200 striking miners in Virginia and Kentucky. McGlothin agreed to dismiss $11.2 million in fines to compensate Pittston for losses caused by union efforts to limit coal production. He upheld the remaining $52 million owed to the state and to Dickenson and Russell counties in Virginia, where most of the strike action took place, union leaders payments by the United Mine Workers although both Pittston Coal Group Inc. and the union had said they were reviewing McGlothin's ruling.
