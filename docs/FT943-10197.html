940806 FT 06 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Macfarlane buys Centurion for Pounds 5.5m Macfarlane Group (Clansman), the Glasgow-based packaging group, has acquired Centurion Packaging (Holdings), a maker of film products for packaging, for Pounds 5.5m. Consideration comprises Pounds 972,500 in cash and 1.86m ordinary shares. Centurion made pre-tax profits of Pounds 517,000 in the year to May 31 and net assets at that date amounted to Pounds 2.03m.
