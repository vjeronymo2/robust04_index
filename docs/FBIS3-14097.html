Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Military Committee, headed by committee chairman Staff Colonel 'Ali Muhammad Salah, has resumed meetings and visits to camps and complexes in the Shabwah Governorate to clear the atmosphere and restore normality. During these visits, the committee met with Shabwah Governorate officials, Shabwah axis commanders, the security director, representatives of parties and political organizations, the protests committee in the governorate, and residents of the city of 'Ataq. In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', Staff Col. 'Ali Muhammad Salah, chairman of the Military Committee, said matters had calmed down, that the situation was normal, and that the Military Committee found that everybody was eager to see a normal situation. He pointed out that the Military Committee will continue to carry out its duties, appealing to everyone to cooperate to allow it to carry its duties successfully.
