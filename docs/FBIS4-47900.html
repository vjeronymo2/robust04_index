BFN [Text] The general strike urged by the Federation of Chadian Trade Unions and followed by Chadian civil servants since 26 April in order to compel the government to sign a social pact is still being observed. It spread yesterday to the Posts and Telecommunications Corporation where employees have stopped work. Journalists and technical personnel in the public media are also on strike so radio and television are carrying only minimal programming. The government has consequently asked soldiers to operate these technical installations.
