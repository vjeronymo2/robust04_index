Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Turkmenistan's President Saparmurad Niyazov left for a visit to the Islamic Republic of Iran early yesterday morning. Here is a report by our correspondent from Ashgabat Airport: [passage omitted] [Begin Niyazov recording] [Words indistinct] we will visit many cities of Iran, we will meet people, we will meet Turkmens. We hope that the two peoples will live in peace and will not think about their tomorrow. We have important agreements between the two states on completing the railroad, on transporting gas; we have separate agreements on various branches of economy. During this visit, the issue of the border between the two countries is among the agreements to be signed. These are for the benefit of the Turkmen people. We are trying to help the Turkmen people to live without any worry. We wish this for the Iranian side, too. Therefore we will sign that agreement. We are very happy that the Iranian state and its president, our brother Rafsanjani, invited us. This is an important invitation [words indistinct]. This visit of ours is historical. I hope that this trip will enable the two countries to have continuous economic relations and [words indistinct]. [end recording] President Saparmurad Niyazov was seen off at the airport by Sahatmuradov, speaker of the Turkmenistan Assembly, and other officials.
