Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 10 -- National Assembly Chairman Nong Duc Manh today received Dutch Foreign Minister Peter Kooijmans who is here for an official visit to Vietnam. Deputy Foreign Minister Nguyen Dy Nien, Dutch Ambassador to Vietnam D. Ader were on hand. Speaking at the reception, Chairman Manh and F.M. Kooijmans compared notes on issues of common concern, including those relating to the socio-economic development in Vietnam in the renovation period. Chairman Nong Duc Manh wished for the further development of the Vietnam-Netherlands friendship and cooperation.
