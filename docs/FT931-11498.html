930203 FT 03 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Enterprise Computer statement Enterprise Computer Holdings yesterday issued a statement concerning the appointment of receivers at three of its subsidiaries. The statement says that on January 19 a winding-up order was made in respect of Enterprise Digital. This was followed on January 29 by the appointment of joint administrative receivers. A winding-up order was served on the company on February 1. On December 22 1992 Enterprise began the procedure in France to appoint an administrative receiver at Enterprise Computer Services (Sarl), and on January 23 Enterprise applied to Barclays Bank to appoint an administrative receiver at Chase International Computer Services.
