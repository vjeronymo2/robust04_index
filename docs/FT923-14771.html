920706 FT 06 JUL 92 / UN team in Baghdad stand-off Iraq denied UN inspectors access to a government ministry building yesterday and their team leader said Baghdad was violating UN ceasefire resolutions, Reuter reports from Baghdad. During the stand-off outside Iraq's Ministry of Agriculture and Irrigation, Ms Karen Jansen, the UN team leader, said the Iraqis were obliged under the UN resolutions to give 'unimpeded access to all sites designated by the Special Commission'.
