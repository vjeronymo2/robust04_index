940523 FT 23 MAY 94 / International Company News: Alcan Aluminium buys sheet maker Alcan Aluminium has bought Leichtmetallwerk Nachterstedt, an aluminium sheet business in former East Germany, from the Treuhandanstalt for an undisclosed sum, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. The plant will be restructured to make automotive sheet and building and industrial sheet products. It will become a finishing plant for Alcan's joint venture rolling mills in West Germany and will serve emerging east European markets, Alcan said.
