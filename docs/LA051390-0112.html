May 13, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

HOME BUYERS FAIR

With the various mortgage loans, lenders, rates, terms and government programs now available, financing can be the most confusing aspect of home buying.

But it's one of the most important, and it's vital that you understand the dollars and sense of your home purchase.

For help in sorting out financing options, come talk to experts -- lenders, mortgage brokers, state and federal government representatives -- at this Saturday's home buyers fair.

DATE: Saturday, May 19.

TIME: 9:30 a.m. to 5:30 p.m.

PLACE: The Marriott Woodland Hills.
