930901 FT 01 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Stakis in Pounds 10.5m casinos buy STAKIS, the hotels and casinos group, has acquired three casinos from the privately-owned Rubicon Group for Pounds 10.5m. The casinos are in Northampton, Wolverhampton and Coventry, and bring the company's casino portfolio to 21. Stakis has paid Pounds 7m cash and issued loan notes worth Pounds 3.5m at 0.75 per cent above Libor repayable in 2003. Under the deal, Stakis has acquired all the share capital of Victoria Casinos, the holding company, debt-free for Pounds 6.8m. Victoria will repay third party and inter-company debt of Pounds 3.7m. Stakis currently operates two casinos in London and 18 in the provinces, where it said the gambling business was more consistent and less volatile than in London.
