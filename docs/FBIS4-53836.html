BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 22 (KCNA) -- Some 1,500 students under the South Korean Federation of General Student Councils (Hanchongnyon) held a rally for succeeding the spirits of the May 18 popular resistance and fighting against the United States at Seoul City University on May 21 and urged a halt to the U.S. pressure for nuclear inspection of the North, according to a report from South Korea. In a resolution they denounced the United States for unreasonably putting pressure on the North for its own interests, and strongly demanded that it withdraw the nuclear weapons of its forces from South Korea and stop the pressure for nuclear inspection of the North. At the end of the rally they staged a street demonstration.
