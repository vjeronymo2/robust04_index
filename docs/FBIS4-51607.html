BFN [Text] A Myanmar [Burma] delegation led by Brigadier General Win Tin, minister of finance and revenue, left Yangon [Rangoon] by Myanmar Airways International this evening to attend the 27th annual meeting of the Asian Development Bank [ADB]to be held in Nice, France on 3 May. The minister and delegation was seen off at Yangon airport by Brig. Gen. Abel, minister of national planning and economic development; U Khin Maung Yin, minister of construction; Mr. Francois Sirdon, acting charge d'affaires of the French Embassy; responsible personnel from the Ministry of Finance and Revenue and Department of Foreign Economic Relations, and family members.
