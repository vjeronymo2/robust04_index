June 21, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: BANKING / FINANCE

Landmark Bank in La Habra is offering a new certificate of deposit that allows depositors to continue adding money to the account during the term of the CD.

Typically, depositors cannot add to a CD during its term.

Landmark's new savings program, called the CD Plus, requires a minimum opening deposit of $20,000, allows additional deposits in minimum increments of $1,000 and limits the total balance to $500,000. The CD is renewed automatically at the interest rate prevailing at renewal time.
