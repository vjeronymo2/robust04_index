931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / Romanian unions strike again Thousands of Romanian union members yesterday threatened a general strike and demanded the resignation of the government for the second time in 10 days, Virginia Marsh writes from Bucharest. Two of Romania's largest confederations, called out thousands of members in Bucharest and other large towns in protest at an inadequate minimum wage, unemployment, corruption, the government's lack of a programme to relaunch the economy and its failure to achieve a dialogue with the unions.
