BFN [Text] Moscow April 15 TASS--The Russian and Ukrainian presidents agreed on Friday to divide the disputed Black Sea Fleet. Boris Yeltsin and Leonid Kravchuk signed an agreement on a way to split the fleet stage-by-stage. The Russian Black Sea Fleet and the Ukrainian Navy, which is to get up to 15-20 per cent of the fleet's ships, will be based separately, the agreements said. It was specified that the details of the fleet division will be elaborated in 10 days.
