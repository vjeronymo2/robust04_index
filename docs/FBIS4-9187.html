BFN [Excerpt] Cajnice, 22 Apr (TANJUG) -- The Muslim military and civil leadership from Gorazde has rejected the Serbian side's offer whereby all combat activities would be halted and the warring parties would be separated by a demarcation line running along the Drina, TANJUG reporters have learned from Serbian military sources. The Serbian side's proposition was rejected late last night. Heavy fire from all weaponry was opened on Serbian positions shortly afterward. Fighting resumed this morning along the demarcation lines on the left and the right banks of the Drina, the Herzegovina Corps [of the Bosnian Serb army] reports. It adds that one Serbian fighter was killed and several were wounded in last night's clashes. [passage omitted]
