Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Massive marches were held in Baghdad and the governorates today. Demonstrators shouted their absolute support for the historic leadership of His Excellency leader President Saddam Husayn until final victory is achieved and the evil embargo lifted. Their chants voiced condemnation of and anger at the sanctions that are being kept in place at the urging of the United States and the Zionists. The demonstrators also shouted slogans against the conspiracies targeting great Iraq, its future generations, and its advancement, with the aim of weakening its sovereignty and national dignity.
