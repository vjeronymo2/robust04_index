BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Terrorists threw concussion grenades at the fast tramway station in Aksaray, Istanbul. Two persons were injured in the incident. Two terrorists placed a concussion grenade at the main entrance gate of the fast tramway station around 1745. Two as yet unidentified persons were slightly injured in the explosion. The explosion also caused slight damage at the station. Meanwhile, a bomb placed in a garbage can in front of a fast-food counter in Aksaray also exploded around the same time. No one was killed or injured in the explosion. Security forces are continuing their search operations to apprehend the terrorists.
