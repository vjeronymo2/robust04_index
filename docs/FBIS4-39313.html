BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anatoliy Yurkin] [Text] Moscow June 6 TASS -- The talks on settling state and legal relations between Georgia and Abkhazia, which were scheduled to begin in Geneva on June 9, have been put off until June 15. The Abkhazian Supreme Soviet press centre informed ITAR-TASS that the decision was necessary due to the suspension of the deployment of CIS peacemaking forces into the confrontation zone on the Inguri river, in compliance with the agreement on ceasefire and disengagement of troops which was undertaken in Moscow.
