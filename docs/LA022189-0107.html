February 21, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

SADDLEBACK DEFEATS GLENDALE IN BASEBALL

Saddleback College defeated Glendale, 9-4, in a nonconference baseball game Monday afternoon at Saddleback.

Tony Spaan led the Saddleback hitting attack with two hits, including a home run in the third inning. Larry Maddux also had a home run for Saddleback (4-1).
