931111 FT 11 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Glengate makes Pounds 80m disposal Glengate Holdings, the privately owned property development company, has sold the Plaza on Oxford Street, London for about Pounds 80m to an undisclosed overseas buyer. The shopping centre and office development produces an income of Pounds 6.69m a year which is expected to rise to Pounds 7.5m when current rent reviews are completed and additional space let.
