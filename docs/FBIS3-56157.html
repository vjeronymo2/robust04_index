Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Kiev -- A joint news conference was held on 12 January at Ukraine's Ministry of Internal Affairs between Andriy Vasylyshyn, Ukraine's minister of internal affairs, and Moshe Shahal, the Israeli minister of police and of energy and infrastructure. [passage omitted] On the same day a protocol of intent was signed at the State Committee of Ukraine for Oil and Gas. Guidelines for development and the timescale for submitting specific proposals from the two sides were outlined. A similar agreement, but only verbally, was reached at Ukraine's Ministry of Power Engineering and Electrification. Proposals were received on cooperation with the Ukrainian State Committee on Nuclear and Radiation Safety. "This is the first attempt by Ukrainian and Israeli power engineers to establish relations between both countries' power engineers," said Amnon Ainav [name as received] scientific director of Israel's Ministry of Power Engineering, in an interview with a UNIAN correspondent. A meeting at the level of experts is planned for the end of March, where cooperation for using solar energy and other nontraditional forms of energy will be agreed.
