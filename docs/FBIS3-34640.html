Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The negotiations to finalize the security and civilian issues of the Gaza-Jericho agreement will resume on Monday, 14 February. At the same time, the talks between Israel and the PLO on economic issues will resume in Paris. This was decided today in a phone conversation between PLO leader Yasir 'Arafat and Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres. The two men also decided to meet again in Cairo if the need arises to break through a stalemate in the talks. Mr. Peres conferred today tete-a-tete with the prime minister. They were later joined by IDF [Israel Defense Forces] commanders and senior Foreign Ministry officials. Speaking to our political correspondent Gadi Sukenik, Mr. Peres said: We overcame mountains of tension and suspicion, and the negotiations with the Palestinians may now be held at a faster pace.
