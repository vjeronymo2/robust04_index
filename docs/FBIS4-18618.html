BFN [Unattributed report: "Mock: `Make Free Elections Possible'"] [Excerpt] Vienna -- On the occasion of the visit by his South African counterpart Roelof ("Pik") Botha to Vienna, Foreign Minister Alois Mock has appealed to the political leaders in Pretoria to make violence-free elections possible. Austria is watching the events in South Africa with great interest but also with considerable concern, Mock stressed in a press release on Thursday [7 April]. The Austrian foreign minister said that the meeting between President De Klerk, African National Congress President Mandela, Zulu King Zwelithini, and Inkatha Chief Buthelezi is decisive for the preparation of the general elections on 27 April and for containing the wave of violence in South Africa. Mock called on the participants in the meeting to do everything in their power to make it possible for all South African citizens to participate in the elections without fear of intimidation.
