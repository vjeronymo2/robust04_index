BFN [From the "RPC Television News" newscast] [Text] The Higher Court of the Second Judicial District based in Penonome has sentenced Melbourne Constantino Walker Nevans, Eugenio Nelson Magallon, and Nivaldo Madrinan Aponte to 15 years in prison for the murder of Father Hector Gallego. The court ruled that Walker Nevans and Madrinan Aponte are entitled to a reduction in their sentences for time already served in connection with this case. In addition, the Higher Court of the Second Judicial District acquitted Oscar Agrazal Jimenez of the charges against him.
