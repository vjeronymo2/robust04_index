May 30, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

The Newport Harbor Art Museum has purchased a $100,000 sculpture by Robert Irwin, a nationally known artist from Los Angeles. The untitled work, a 12-foot-tall acrylic column made in 1970, refracts light from its surrounding environment and complements California light and space pieces in the museum's permanent collection, spokeswoman Maxine Gaiber said. Museum officials hope to display the work this summer.
