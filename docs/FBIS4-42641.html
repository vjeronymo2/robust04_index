BFN [Text] Gianfranco Fini [leader of the National Alliance] said: "I am happy that [Israeli Foreign Minister] Shim'on Peres has said that there is no hostility on the part of the Israeli Government toward the Italian Government, and I am convinced that the concerns of [European Commission President Jacques] Delors will diminish in a short time when everyone has understood the values that are guiding both the government and the National Alliance." These are the values of "liberty, democracy, social justice, and tolerance," Fini explained, "in which all Italians believe." Fascism "ended in 1945 and cannot return," and "the National Alliance is neither a fascist or neofascist force."
