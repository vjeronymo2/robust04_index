BFN [Text] Here is a report from Nimit-Poipet battlefield on Route 5. On the morning of 26 and 27 April, we attacked three positions of the two-headed government near Poipet and the artillery site, and another position at Daeum Pou. We killed 15 government troops and wounded eight others, destroyed 11 assorted guns, set two military trucks on fire, and damaged six others. The Nimit-Poipet stretch of Route 5 has been cut off completely by our forces. This area remains under our control.
