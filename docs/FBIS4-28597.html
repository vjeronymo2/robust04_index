BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 9 -- With financial assistance from the former Czechoslovak government's job creation programme, the Ministry of Labour, War Invalids and Social Affairs has approved 219 projects with a total loan capital of VND 25.9 billion to provide 10,120 people with jobs. The programme includes jobs for 2,628 guest workers returning from Czech and Slovakia. The labour ministry has passed 31 free-of-charge job training projects for 1,594 workers returning from Czech and Slovakia with total funds of more than VND 4.1 billion. Many job-training courses were held in 23 provinces and cities. In 1994 around 500 more projects will be implemented. They have loan capital of about vnd 60 billion to create jobs for 12,000 people, 5,000 of them former workers from Czech and Slovakia. - Germany has funded nearly VND 3.96 billion to 28 projects of Vietnamese guest workers returning from Germany in Vinh Phu Province to help them set up their businesses or participate in other businesses. Thanks to self-generated income and loan capital 28 private businesses were set up or expanded their production and business, providing nearly 650 people with jobs.
