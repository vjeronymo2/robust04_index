July 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

Brent Spiner deserves praise for his portrayal of Lt. Data on "Star Trek: The Next Generation." I'm sure it's not easy bringing an android character "to life," but Spiner does it with skill and sensitivity, making Data one of the more enjoyable characters on the show. I hope his work is recognized when the nominations go out for Emmys.

Julie Hoffman, Huntington Beach
