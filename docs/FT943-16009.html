940706 FT 06 JUL 94 / International Company News: Nokia completes NYSE listing Nokia, the telecommunications group, has become the first Finnish company to be listed on the New York Stock Exchange, following the completion of its 5.25m global share offering, arranged by CS First Boston, writes Tracy Corrigan in London. About 70 per cent of the shares were sold in the US, in the form of American depository shares (with two ADSs per share). The shares were priced at FM432 per preferred share on Friday, which corresponded with the closing price in Helsinki. Each ADS was priced at Dollars 40 3/8 .
