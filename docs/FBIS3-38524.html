Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana arrives today in Bosnia, where he will visit two of the four detachments belonging to the Spanish contingent in the area. Tomorrow in Zagreb, he will meet the representative of the UN secretary general in former Yugoslavia. A few minutes before leaving Javier Solana stated that our country will reconsider the presence of Spanish soldiers in Bosnia at the end of the winter. [Begin Solana recording] Maybe when winter ends we will have to do a detailed analysis of the situation; we will do it with our colleagues, with the countries that also have troops in the field, and make a decision, if a peaceful solution to this terrible conflict has not been found. [end recording]
