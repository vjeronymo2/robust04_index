Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The visiting director general of the OPEC Fund for International Development, Dr. Sayyid 'Abdallahi, has been received by the vice president, Dr. Samson Kisekka. The meeting took place at the vice president's office in Kampala yesterday. Dr. Kisekka expressed gratitude for the assistance which the OPEC Fund for International Development has given to Uganda to date. He briefed him about developments in Uganda today and the intention of the government to restore the country to normalcy. He informed him that the government is now trying to renew as much as possible all acquaintances after the political turmoil of the past. Dr. 'Abdallahi expressed satisfaction with the cooperation his organization enjoys with Uganda. He said his delegation is in Uganda to make an on-the-spot inspection of their various projects in the country. He said that there are other areas which have been identified for cooperation and looked forward to the further strengthening of relations. [passage omitted]
