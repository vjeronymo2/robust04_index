930513 FT 13 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Whitbread Investment Whitbread Investment Company reported a 10 per cent increase in 1993 net revenue to Pounds 10.5m. Earnings per share rose from 15p to 16.6p and net asset value rose from 675.6p to 683.4p. A proposed final dividend of 11.9p increases the total by 10 per cent to 16.5p.
