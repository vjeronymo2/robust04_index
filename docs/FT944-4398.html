941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Crown Eyeglass up Crown Eyeglass, the spectacles maker, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 303,000 to Pounds 336,000 in the six months to September 30. Turnover grew from Pounds 2.54m to Pounds 3.45m. Earnings came out at 14p (12.7p) and the interim dividend is raised to 3.5p (3p).
