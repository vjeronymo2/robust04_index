Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] This morning, after intense rocket and artillery preparation, Azerbaijani troops renewed their offensive in the north of the Nagorno-Karabakh Mardakertskiy rayon. The LUR agency reports that, as of 1200, the Nagorno-Karabakh defensive forces are managing to check the enemy's advance. According to the information available, the Azerbaijani army has sustained serious losses in men and equipment. Intense firing from fixed positions is going on in other sectors of the front.
