931007 FT 07 OCT 93 / People: Trafalgar House Engineering Ted Bavister, one of the best-known names in the UK engineering contracting world, is retiring from his post as deputy managing director of Trafalgar House Engineering. Bavister has just ended a year as president of the Institution of Chemical Engineers. He held senior posts at Davy and became chairman of John Brown Engineers & Constructors in 1981.
