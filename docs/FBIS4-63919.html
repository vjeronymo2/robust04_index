BFN [By ITAR-TASS freelance correspondent Semen Ivanov] [Text] Moscow, 26 May -- The Cosmos-2281 satellite, launched yesterday from Plesetsk cosmodrome by the Tsiklon-3 booster-rocket, has not been placed into orbit. The ITAR-TASS correspondent was told this at the press center of the Russian Military Space Forces this morning. The launch was being made in the interests of the Ministry of Defense of the Russian Federation. An inter-departmental commission has been set up to investigate the reasons for the failed launch.
