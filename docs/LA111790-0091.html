November 17, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP;

DIVISION VII

La Mirada 21, Harvard 14 -- Quarterback Carlos Jimenez completed eight of 18 passes for 155 yards, including two touchdown passes to sophomore Mike Moschetti, as the Matadors (9-2) rallied past Harvard (9-2) at Burroughs High.

The Saracens took a 14-9 lead on a two-yard run by junior Jeremiah Aguolu with 15 seconds left in the third quarter. LISA GRALL
