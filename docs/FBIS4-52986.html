BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 11 [date as received] (KNS-KCNA) -- Kim Chong-su, chairman of the Association of Koreans in Japan for Peaceful Reunification, published a statement Wednesday, in which he branded the criminal acts of the South Korean authorities in opposing the proposed convocation of national conference and rushing headlong towards war against the fellow countrymen as a challenge to the entire nation desirous of reconciliation, unity and reunification and a full disclosure of their color as flunkeyist traitors seeking only confrontation and division and war against the North. "If the South Korean authorities have the intention to achieve peaceful reunification," he said, "they must no more do acts upsetting the expectation of the entire fellow countrymen for a convocation of national conference." "They must also stop at once the criminal war moves intended to annihilate the fellow countrymen in league with outside forces", he demanded.
