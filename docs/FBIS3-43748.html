Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The IFP [Inkatha Freedom Party] said it would continue negotiations with the ANC [African National Congress] for as long as it was worth doing so. Clarifying a speech delivered by Dr. Mangosuthu Buthelezi yesterday, in which he said the IFP and the KwaZulu government should reconsider further talks with the ANC, the IFP's Dr. Ziba Jiyane said this wasn't a call to pull out of talks with the ANC. He also said a decision on whether the IFP should participate in the elections or not would be made when the party met at the end of the month.
