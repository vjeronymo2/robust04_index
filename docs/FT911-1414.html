910504 FT 04 MAY 91 / Finance & The Family: Unit trust sales pass Pounds 1bn Unit trust managers are smiling again as sales soared past the Pounds 1bn mark in March to reach Pounds 1.19bn, following Pounds 1.32bn in February. Although investors cashed in Pounds 881m worth of units during the month, net new investment was a satisfactory Pounds 308m in March. Sales in the first quarter of this year are well up on the first quarter of last year, while cash-ins are down, leaving net new investment for the quarter of Pounds 1.27bn compared with just Pounds 415m last year. The value of unit funds had reached Pounds 56.2bn by the end of March - the highest level since January last year. Conditions are also buoyant in the investment trust sector with investments in savings schemes rising to Pounds 26.2m in the first quarter of this year from Pounds 17.8m in the previous quarter. The number of investors using these savings schemes has now reached 101,300. Eric Short
