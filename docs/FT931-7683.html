930223 FT 23 FEB 93 / UK Company News: F&C Enterprise net assets advance 20% Foreign & Colonial Enterprise Trust raised net assets per share by 20 per cent from 44.8p to 53.8p in 1992. The trust said it had actively invested in the UK, where conditions remained strongly in favour of the cash buyer. It also benefited from a number of profitable disposals, the most important of which was the flotation of Olicom in the US. Income from the portfolio remained strong and the proposed dividend is 0.44p -last year's payment was 0.4p and there was also a special 0.2p. Total revenue was little changed at Pounds 2.67m (Pounds 2.71m), but the after-tax figure was lower at Pounds 426,000 (Pounds 905,000) giving earnings per share of 0.45p (0.96p).
