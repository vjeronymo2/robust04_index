BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- The prime minister of Singapore, Goh Chok Tong has arrived for a visit to Bonn on Tuesday [12 April]. On Wednesday, he is due to open a joint economic forum together with Federal Chancellor Helmut Kohl. The city state is one of Asia's strongest export nations. The prime minister will also visit Baden-Wuerttemberg and Berlin. On Tuesday, Goh Chok Tong will carry out a private program.
