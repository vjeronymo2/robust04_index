920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / Lamrani named Moroccan PM KING HASSAN of Morocco yesterday named Mr Mohammed Karim Lamrani, 72, as the country's prime minister, Reuter reports from Rabat. Mr Lamrani will lead a 28-member government appointed to supervise elections due later this year. A prominent economist and businessman who served as prime minister in 1971 and 1984, Mr Lamrani replaces Mr Azeddine Laraki, 63, whose centrist government was dismissed by the king on Monday after being in office for seven years. The king told the outgoing government Morocco was on the eve of important political developments including a referendum on a new constitution, followed by rural, municipal and legislative elections.
