BFN [Text] Santo Domingo, 18 May (EFE) -- According to a Central Electoral Board [JCE] report, President Joaquin Balaguer leads opposition candidate Jose Francisco Pena Gomez by 1.41 percent. JCE Bulletin No. 14, issued after midnight last night, reported that Balaguer and his allies had 1,099,772 votes while Pena Gomez' coalition had 1,063,508. Those figures represent 86.87 percent of the voting sites. The JCE reports that Balaguer has received 42.57 percent and Pena Gomez has received 41.16 percent of the valid votes.
