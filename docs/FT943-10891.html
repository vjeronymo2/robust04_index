940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / Markets boosted by recovery hopes Equity and bond prices rose strongly across Europe yesterday, reflecting optimism about economic recovery and interest rates. The UK stock market enjoyed the strongest session of the year, with the FT-SE 100 gaining 60.1 points to close at 3,157.5. The dollar, meanwhile, recovered above Y100 to close in London at Y100.415.
