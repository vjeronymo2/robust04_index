January 1, 1990, Monday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Mark Calcavecchia shot a final-round 71 for a one-stroke victory in the Spalding Invitational Pro-Am golf tournament at Scottsdale, Ariz. His four-day total was a 10-under-par 276.

Colleen Walker finished the best among five LPGA players in the mixed field, shooting a 71 for a 283.
