940728 FT 28 JUL 94 / Outdated resorts 'hitting tourism' Outdated resorts are damaging the reputation of more attractive English tourist attractions, making it difficult to persuade UK residents to take their holidays at home, the English Tourist Board said yesterday. The board said that over the past decade the UK's tourism balance of payments had fallen from breakeven to a deficit of Pounds 3.1bn. The deficit had grown in spite of the increase in foreign visitors to the UK.
