Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA January 31 -- A Hungarian Government economic delegation led by government special envoy, Mr. Gilyan Gyorgy, has arrived here for a working visit to Vietnam. The delegtion also includes a number of Hungarian businessmen. On Jan. 29, the Vietnam-Hungary Friendship Association and Hungary's trade office in Hanoi had a get-together with the visiting delegation and representatives of Vietnamese offices concerned, businesses, scientific workers who had studied in Hungary. The participants were briefed about Hungary's economic situation and business possibilities between Hungary and Vietnam. It was reported that at present two-way trade value between the two countries is between USD 28 million and USD 40 million. The Hungarian businessmen are interested in garment manufacture and farm and forest products processing in Vietnam.
