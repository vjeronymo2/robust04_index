DEPARTMENT OF HEALTH AND HUMAN SERVICES Administration for Children and Families Hearing of the U.S. Advisory Board on Child Abuse and Neglect AGENCY: Administration for Children and Families, DHHS. ACTION: Notice of hearing.

SUMMARY: The U.S. Advisory Board on Child Abuse and Neglect will hold a hearing on child maltreatment-related fatalities on June 16, 1994, from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. The hearing is currently scheduled to be held at the Grand Hyatt Hotel, East 42nd Street, New York, New York. This meeting is open to the public. If a sign language interpreter is needed, you may contact David Siegel at (202) 401&hyph;9215. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Eileen H. Lohr, Program Analyst, U.S. Advisory Board on Child Abuse and Neglect, Room 303&hyph;D, Humphrey Building, Washington, DC 20201, (202) 690&hyph;6053. SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: In connection with the development of its upcoming fatalities report, the Advisory Board will conduct a one-day hearing on child maltreatment-related fatalities.
