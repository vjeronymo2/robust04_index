921015 FT 15 OCT 92 / New Kuwait cabinet KUWAIT's new cabinet will include six elected members of the National Assembly - the most elected members ever represented in a Kuwaiti cabinet - under a plan agreed by the crown prince and parliamentary deputies, the official Kuwait News Agency (KUNA) reported yesterday, our Middle East Staff writes. The award of six cabinet seats to deputies follows the election of an opposition majority in the October 5 elections, the first full poll since the last parliament was suspended in 1986. The ministries to be taken up by the six were not known. Announcement of the full cabinet is expected by the weekend.
