930720 FT 20 JUL 93 / International Company News: Maybelline eyes Yardley YARDLEY GROUP, which is owned by Old Bond Street of the UK, has entered negotiations to be acquired by Maybelline, the US cosmetics and personal care company 70 per cent owned by Wasserstein Perella Management Partners. Yardley already has strong ties to Maybelline. Maybelline previously acquired the right to the Yardley of London trade name and related intellectual property rights in North America. Wasserstein beneficially owns all of the stock of Old Bond Street through limited partnerships. A committee of outside directors of Maybelline has been established to consider the possible transaction. Maybelline said yesterday talks were at a preliminary stage.
