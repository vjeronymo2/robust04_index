BFN [Text] Turkey has prepared a plan to occupy all of Cyprus and has asked Islamic countries for support. Two of them have already replied positively. The Arab newspaper AL MUHARRIR, which is published in Paris, has revealed this information crediting well-informed Turkish diplomatic sources. The newspaper adds that, according to the Turkish sources, Cyprus has been selected as a center for laundering dirty money made from drug trafficking, secret arms selling, and violations of the embargo against Yugoslavia. They also claim that there is cooperation between Russia, Yugoslavia, and Cypriot Orthodox people to suppress the Muslims.
