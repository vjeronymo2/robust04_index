BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 471] [Text] Vilnius, April 5, ELTA -- Lithuanian Government indexed the wage rate and other social payments. From April 1 the minimum standard of living has made 48 litas a month per person. The minimum wage rate has made 53 litas, the minimum pay per hour should not be lower than 0.32 litas. Pensioners who do not work also have been allotted bigger pensions. The pensions for them have been increased by five percent.
