931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / World Trade News: Lens factory for Limerick Vistakon, a subsidiary of Johnson & Johnson, the US healthcare corporation, is to spend IPounds 130m over the next three years in creating a European manufacturing base at Limerick, in the west of Ireland, for its range of disposable contact lenses, Tim Coone reports from Dublin. Mr Ruari Quinn, Ireland's minister for enterprise and employment, said this week the new plant was 'among the top investments ever in the healthcare industry in this country'.
