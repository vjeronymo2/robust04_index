August 23, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: THE REGION;

SMALL-BUSINESS FORUM

Expo '90, a free program sponsored by Rep. Esteban Torres (D-La Puente) to help small-business owners find new markets, will be held from 8:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. today at the Rio Hondo College Campus Inn in Whittier.

Representatives of federal, state and local governments and 20 large corporations will discuss business opportunities. The program will also include seminars on marketing and Small Business Administration loans.
