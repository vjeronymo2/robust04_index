930814 FT 14 AUG 93 / Finance and The Family: Smaller companies on the rise - At a Glance While the FT-SE 100 index was setting records this week, small company shares were also doing well. The Hoare Govett Small Companies Index (capital gains version) rose 2.5 per cent from 1511.33 to 1548.91 over the seven days to August 12, and is now 27.6 per cent higher than its level at the start of the year.
