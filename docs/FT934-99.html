931231 FT 31 DEC 93 / Three charged with card fraud Three men have been arrested and charged with conspiring to defraud Midland Bank and the TSB over an alleged Pounds 2.5m credit card fraud, West Midlands police said yesterday, John Mason writes. Mr Himish Patel of Peel Road, Wembley, north London, Mr Rajesh Patel of Chadderton, Greater Manchester and Mr Nitesh Patel of Wellingborough, Northants, appeared before Birmingham magistrates earlier this week and were remanded in custody until January 5. Further arrests are expected within the next three weeks, the police said.
