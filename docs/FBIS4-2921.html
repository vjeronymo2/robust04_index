BFN [Text] Asmara, April 14 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese products fair opened here today. The fair, organized by the China National Aero-Technology Import and Export Corporation and the Red Sea Trading Corporation of Eritrea, is the first of its kind in Eritrea since last May when China established diplomatic relationship with Eritrea. Eritrean President Isayas Afewerki opened the fair, which includes cars, bicycles, machinery, textiles and stationeries.
