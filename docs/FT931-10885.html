930205 FT 05 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Bostrom pays up to Pounds 2.1m for AJW Bostrom is paying up to Pounds 2.14m for AJW Holdings, a fellow automotive components maker. The initial consideration of up to Pounds 1.79m is being satisfied by the issue of 1.41m shares, placed at 127p. Bostrom shares were unchanged at 132p. The balance of a maximum of Pounds 350,000 cash is profit related. In the year to April 30, AJW recorded an operating loss of Pounds 109,000 on turnover of Pounds 6.53m. However, for the seven months to November 30 operating profits were Pounds 2,000 on turnover of Pounds 4.15m.
