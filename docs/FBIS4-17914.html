BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Alma-Ata [Almaty], 28 Apr -- Having completed his official visit to Kazakhstan, Li Peng, the prime minister of the State Council of the People's Republic of China, left Kazakhstan's capital this morning. It remained unclear until the last minute whether the Chinese delegation would fly to Mongolia, where the situation is tense at the moment, or to China. It has just been revealed by well-informed sources that the delegation went to Ulaanbaatar, from where it is to return to Beijing tomorrow night. Kazakhstan's President Nursultan Nazarbayev and other officials saw Li Peng off at the Alma-Ata airport.
