940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Pounds 177,000 pay rise for ICI chief Sir Denys Henderson, chairman of ICI and Zeneca, saw his total pay jump from Pounds 527,000 to Pounds 704,000 last year. The Pounds 177,000 increase was largely the result of a Pounds 149,000 bonus linked to the demerger of Zeneca from ICI and the performance of both companies. Mr Ronnie Hampel, chief executive of ICI, received a total of between Pounds 475,000 and Pounds 480,000 up from Pounds 350,000-Pounds 355,000. His salary rose from Pounds 340,840 to Pounds 425,000.
