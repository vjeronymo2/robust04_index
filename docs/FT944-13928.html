941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / NYC Mayor supports democrat re-election New York City Mayor Rudolph Giuliani, in a blow to fellow Republicans, yesterday threw his support behind Democratic Governor Mario Cuomo's bid for re-election, Reuter reports from New York. The move came as Mr Cuomo was seven points behind Republican George Pataki in the polls. Mr Giuliani said he could not back Mr Pataki because of his positions on aiding the city.
