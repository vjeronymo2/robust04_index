930213 FT 13 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Bucknall losses trimmed A DIFFICULT period for Bucknall Group saw the construction consultancy group trim its interim pre-tax losses from Pounds 752,000 to Pounds 745,000, after continuing rationalisation to eliminate loss-making businesses. Operating losses from continuing businesses fell to Pounds 411,000 (Pounds 528,000) for the half year to October 31, on turnover of Pounds 7.15m (Pounds 7.56m). After restructuring costs - mainly for redundancies - overall operating deficit was Pounds 607,000 (Pounds 571,000). Interest took Pounds 138,000 (Pounds 181,000). Losses per share emerged at 5.7p (5.6p). There was again no interim dividend. Mr Richard Miles, chairman, said considerable progress had been made in stabilising the group and he was confident that results would show further improvement in the next six months.
