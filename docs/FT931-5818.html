930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / Hatton will not call evidence MR DEREK Hatton, former Labour deputy leader of Liverpool City Council, and three others on trial on fraud charges are to call no evidence in their defence, Mold Crown Court was told yesterday. Mr Hatton and Mr John Monk, a 45-year-old businessman, face two charges of conspiring to defraud the council over the leasing of land in the city for car parks. Former city councillors Mr John Nelson and Ms Hannah Folan each face one charge relating to the car park deals.
