September 16, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS: SANTA ANA'S GENOVATION DATA-ENTRY COMPANY NAMES JOSEPH MESHI AS DIRECTOR

* Joseph Meshi has joined Genovation Inc. as executive vice president and a director. He will be primarily responsible for directing the Santa Ana-based company's marketing, sales, finance and administrative efforts. Meshi was previously president of Micro General Corp., vice president of AM Documentor and co-founder and vice president of Conographic Corp. Genovation develops custom keyboards and other data-entry devices.
