920325 FT 25 MAR 92 / Two die in Istanbul bus attack Guerrillas with automatic rifles fired on a bus in Istanbul yesterday, killing two Turkish intelligence officers and wounding seven others, Reuter reports from Istanbul. The Dev-Sol (Revolutionary Left) group claimed to have carried out the attack. The group has joined forces with the separatist Kurdish Workers party (PKK), which has vowed to spread its rebellion to big cities. At least 55 people have been killed in south-east Turkey since Saturday in clashes between government forces and PKK rebels.
