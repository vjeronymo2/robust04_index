920817 FT 17 AUG 92 / Construction Contracts: Expansion at London hospital A 13.7m contract to construct two buildings in the grounds of Hammersmith Hospital in west London has been awarded to MOWLEM SOUTH EAST, a division of John Mowlem Construction. The larger of the two is an eight-storey 9,500 sq metre clinical research building for the Medical Research Council. The building will have six storeys of fully fitted laboratories for research into the basic science of disease, with two storeys of plant above. The second is a three-storey 3,500 sq metre cancer centre for The Hammer Cancer Trust. This will have treatment rooms and wards grouped around a central atrium.
