BFN [Text] Kiev -- In the past few days army units and establishments within Ukraine's Ministry of Defense received an order by Army General Vitaliy Radetskyy, Ukrainian defense minister, that introduces "regulations on penal battalions in Ukraine's Armed Forces." It sets out the procedure and conditions for punishing compulsory service soldiers and also defines the procedure for the penal battalion's activity. The regulations provide for the commander of the penal battalion to be appointed personally by the Ukrainian defense minister and determine that the military district commander is directly responsible for its leadership. According to the resolution, the duration of service of officers in the penal battalion should not exceed five-six years for a battalion commander and 3-4 for other posts. Today, there are two penal battalions in the Armed Forces of Ukraine: in Kiev's Darnitskiy Rayon and in Chernomorskiy settlement in Crimea.
