BFN [Text] Leader President Saddam Husayn has ordered that brain and nervous system surgeons be granted monthly allowances equal to 300 percent of their total monthly salaries. This has been announced to the Iraqi News Agency by Health Minister Dr. Umid Midhat Mubarak. He said the leader president has also ordered that brain and nervous system surgical operations conducted at the Health Ministry's hospitals be excluded from the current charge rates. He added that this generous gesture by leader President Saddam Husayn reflects appreciation and support for this scientific specialization, which requires meticulous precision and extreme and distinguished care for brain and nervous system patients. He said that this generous gesture will encourage this specialization, which is an important lifesaving specializations.
