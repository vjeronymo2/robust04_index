920413 FT 13 APR 92 / Bank suspect surrenders THE former president of a savings institution, whose alleged embezzlement of Dollars 13.5m (Pounds 7.8m) from it led to one of the worst US banking crises, turned himself in yesterday after 17 months as a fugitive, officials said, Reuter reports from Rhode Island . Mr Joseph 'Puppy Dog Junior' Mollicone, 48, ran Heritage Loan and Investment, whose collapse drained a private deposit insurance fund of capital and set off a crisis that caused the closure of 45 Rhode Island banks.
