September 27, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

HIGH SCHOOL ROUNDUP;

BIRCH LEADS ESTANCIA GIRLS PAST FOUNTAIN VALLEY IN TENNIS

Dana Birch swept in singles, helping Estancia High School defeat Fountain Valley, 11-7, in a nonleague girls' tennis match Tuesday at Fountain Valley.

Estancia is ranked ninth in the Southern Section 4-A poll.

Birch, who is 27-1, won, 6-4, 6-1, 6-1. In doubles, Liz Collins and Molly Romm won, 7-6, 6-0, 6-2, and Shannon Curren and Sam Joyner won, 6-2, 7-5, 7-6, for Estancia (6-3).

In other nonleague tennis:

Sunny Hills 12, La Mirada 6 -- Joy Fulton and Nandita Sapre swept, 6-4, 7-6, 7-1, to lead Sunny Hills (7-1) at La Mirada.

Rosary 16, Valley Christian 2 -- Mindy Gomez won, 6-3, 6-0, 6-0, for Rosary ( 6-0).

Capistrano Valley 13, Edison 5 -- Page Bartelt won, 6-1, 6-0, 6-1, for visiting Capistrano Valley (5-0).

In Empire League tennis:

Katella 13, Esperanza 5 -- Lynn Vonusa won, 6-0, 6-1, 6-0, for host Katella (9-1, 1-0).

Loara 14, Cypress 4 -- The sisters doubles team of Haliel and Euneil Kim won, 6-1, 6-0, 6-0, for Loara (4-0, 1-0).

In Orange League tennis:

Valencia 12, Western 6 -- Julie Williams won, 6-0, 6-0, 6-0, and Allison Granger won, 6-2, 6-2, 6-1, for Valencia (7-2, 1-0).
