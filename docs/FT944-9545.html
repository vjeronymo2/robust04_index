941115 FT 15 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Newport purchase Newport Holdings, the property investment company, has announced the conditional disposal of a Birmingham freehold property for Pounds 2.29m, which gives a profit over book value of Pounds 92,000 before expenses and tax. It also releases Pounds 992,000, before expenses, after the discharge of the mortgage on the property, which will be available for further investment.
