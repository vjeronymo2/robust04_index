920529 FT 29 MAY 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables Imported cherries are superb this week at Dollars 1.20-2.00 a lb (Dollars 1.20-2.60) reports the FFVIB. Other good buys include Cape Granny Smith apples at 55-60p a lb (45-55p) Spania apricots at 70-90p a lb (Pounds 2.00-2.20) and South American Mangoes at 85p-Pounds 1.00 each (85p-Pounds 1.00). English new potatoes at 15-20p a lb (20-30p) are this week's best vegetable choice. Flat mushrooms at 60-70p a 1/2 (60-70p) and cup mushrooms at 50-70p a 1/2 (50-70p) along with French courgettes at 50-70p a lb (65-75p) round up the best vegetable buys. British tomatoes at 65-75p a lb (80-90p) along with the beef variety at 70-85p a lb (80-90p) are excellent this week.
