921111 FT 11 NOV 92 / Cutbacks at bearings plants RHP Bearings yesterday announced it is to make 300 people redundant in its industrial bearings plants at Newark, Nottinghamshire, Ferrybridge, West Yorkshire, and Blackburn, Lancashire. Scottish Power, the privatised electricity company, is shedding 95 jobs in its engineering resources division and projects group because of project delays outside Scotland.
