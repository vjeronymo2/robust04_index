Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Report by Enes Rasusic and Faruk Hajder from the Information Service of the 4th Army Corps] [Text] Exploiting the attention of the international media and politicians on Sarajevo to the maximum, members of the HVO [Croatian Defense Council] and the Croatian Army continued their merciless artillery attacks on the lines of separation and civilian parts of the free parts of the Mostar commune. A sudden mortar attack on Blagaj from the positions of the Ustasha forces left one person dead. Several persons were wounded in this treacherous attack. The central part of the town and the defense lines in this part of the town were subjected to intensified attacks by the aggressor all day yesterday and throughout the night. Over 80 tank and mortar shells were fired on the central part of the town from the enemy's positions on the left side [of the Neretva River]. These were accompanied by constant fire from infantry weapons. In addition to firing dozens of mortar projectiles on the Bijelo Polje region yesterday, the Ustashas attacked civilian targets in Potoci with multiple rocket launchers on three occasions yesterday, killing two civilians. At 0815 this morning, Ustashas fired several projectiles from multiple rocket launcher on the city center, fortunately, without tragic consequences. Despite the stepped up attacks by the aggressor, the attained defense lines remain unchanged and are under the control of the 4th corps.
