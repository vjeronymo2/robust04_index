October 20, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

SAN FERNANDO VALLEY LEAGUE

Harvard 24, St. Francis 14 -- Harvard scored 21 unanswered points in the second half to rally from an 11-point deficit at St. Francis.

Harvard (6-1, 2-0) drove 80 yards, capped by a 13-yard scoring run by Jeremiah Aguolu, to cut the deficit to 14-10 at the end of the third quarter.

The Saracens took the lead, 17-14, on a 24-yard touchdown run by Kent Dahn. The play was set up when Stafford Lee gained 25 yards on a fake punt from the Saracen 44.

Harvard finished the scoring when Dahn raced 21 yards late in the fourth quarter. Dahn rushed for 77 yards and Harvard quarterback Steve Kezirian completed nine of 27 passes for 95 yards.

James Yun intercepted a Kezirian pass and returned it 32 yards for a touchdown and a 14-3 third-quarter St. Francis (4-3, 1-1) lead. St. Francis opened the scoring on a two-yard touchdown run by Larry Duenas in the first quarter. KEVIN ASSEO
