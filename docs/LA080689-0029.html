August 6, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

FIXER-UPPER ART

Concerning "Fixer-Upper Art," by Suzanne Muchnic, July 23: article

The fact that many modern art works are falling apart gives new meaning to the "Temporary Contemporary."

In the real world, when something falls apart, you go get your money back from the maker.

It's ironic that conservator Sharon Blank is also used to fixing Barbie dolls and Tupperware. Caveat emptor.

JOHN DEGATINA

Los Angeles
