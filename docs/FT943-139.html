940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Tullow Oil ahead Tullow Oil, the Irish oil and gas exploration and production company, more than doubled profits from IPounds 290,159 to IPounds 628,027 (Pounds 622,000) for the first half of 1994. Turnover jumped 81 per cent to IPounds 1.7m, compared with IPounds 938,000. Earnings per share doubled to 0.04p (0.02p). The company said its three producing areas in Senegal, the Czech Republic and the UK all performed well.
