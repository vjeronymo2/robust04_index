BFN [Text] Ten Palestinian police officers entered Jericho today via the Allenby Bridge. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the officers are slated to take over the local police station today. IDF [Israel Defense Forces] officers in the area ordered the soldiers to evacuate the hundreds of residents who waited for the policemen in the city square and to close the shops in the city. A riot erupted in the area, and IDF officers forcefully evacuated correspondents and journalists, including a Qol Yisra'el crew. Three officers dragged Qol Yisra'el's correspondent for about 20 meters, and shouted and threatened him with loaded guns, causing him slight bruises. The residents of Jericho started to riot, forcing IDF officers to disperse them with rubber bullets.
