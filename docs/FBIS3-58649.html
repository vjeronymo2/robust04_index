Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] A report has come out envisaging the reactivation, under certain conditions, of the Super-Phoenix [fast breeder reactor]. (Andre-Claude Lacoste) of the Nuclear Safety Directorate says in this report, which was submitted to the Industry and Environment Ministry this morning, that from the point of view of safety, the reactivation of the reactor could be authorized given two conditions: that the work on combating sodium leaks is completed successfully, and that the reactor is brought into service at reduced power for a few months. In other words, Super-Phoenix will be reactivated.
