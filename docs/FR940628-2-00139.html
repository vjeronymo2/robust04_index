Eligibility and Threshold Requirements Applications are invited from Indian tribes for assistance under the emergency shelter grants set-aside program. Private nonprofit organizations are not eligible to apply directly to HUD for a grant, but may receive funding from a grantee if the grantee determines that the nonprofit has the financial and organizational capacity to carry out the proposed activities.
