Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow March 29 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin and speaker of the Federation Council had a meeting in the Kremlin on Tuesday. Current political, organisational and personnel problems have been discussed, Vyacheslav Kostikov, press secretary of the Russian president, told ITAR-TASS. Shumeyko informed the president about the activity of the Federation Council. Special attention was given to the work on the agreement "on the achievement of civil concord". Ways of its coordnation and signing were discussed. During the discussion of economic aspects, Yeltsin expressed concern with possible negative consequences of the government's decision on customs duties, also for broad sections of the population. The president said he had discussed the matter with Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin and they had arrived at a consensus about the need for adjustment on a number of important aspects, without discarding the principle of reasonable protectionalism regarding Russian manufacturers and dealers. Yeltsin mentioned the possibility of holding meetings of the Russian Federation's Security Council with the participation of heads of chambers of the Federal Assembly. The president said this would widen a political base of decision-making on important problems having the bearing on security. Ways of settling the Ossetian-Ingush conflict were also discussed during the meeting.
