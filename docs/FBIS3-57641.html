Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, January 27 (SNARK)--Tomorrow Vladimir Kazimirov, the ambassador for special missions and the special envoy of Russian president, will leave for the zone of Karabakh conflict. SNARK was informed about it by diplomatic sources. It is supposed that he will visit Yerevan, Baku, Stepanakert where he will meet with the leaders of Armenia, Azerbaijan, Karabakh and will discuss Moscow's new plan of peaceful settlement of the region conflict.
