August 17, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

THE HUNTED: The late Andrija Artukovic of Seal Beach, a convicted Nazi war criminal whose treatment by the Justice Department is being investigated by the FBI (A1), is not the only Orange County resident to have faced such charges. . . . Bruno Karl Blach of La Habra, who was extradited seven months ago, remains in a West German prison, says his attorney, Ronald Parker. No decision has been made on whether to charge Blach, who is accused of murdering concentration camp prisoners.
