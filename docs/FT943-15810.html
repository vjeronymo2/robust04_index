940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Danae lifts net assets Danae, the split capital investment trust, reported a net asset value of 74.78p per capital share as at May 31, a rise of some 28 per cent. Earnings were 7.98p (7.8p) per income share. The total dividend is held at 7.95p via an unchanged final of 4.575p.
