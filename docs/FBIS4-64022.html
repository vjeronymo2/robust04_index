BFN [INTERFAX report: "State Duma Committee Rejects Proposal for Increasing Budget Expenditure"] [Text] The State Duma Committee on the Budget, Finances, and Banks has not supported the Economic Policy Committee's proposal for increasing expenditure in the draft 1994 federal budget by 28.6 trillion rubles, Budget Committee chairman Mikhail Zadornov said. In his words, today, when the budget bill will be considered by the State Duma in the second reading, an overwhelming majority of the budget committee members are going to stick to the key stances on the budget deficit, revenue, and expenditure that were outlined when the bill was discussed in the first reading.
