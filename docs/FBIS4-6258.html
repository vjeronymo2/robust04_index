BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 18 (KCNA) -- Former Governor-General of Canada Edward Schreyer on a visit to Korea told a press conference here on April 15 that the reports of the West about Korea were distorted and the impressions he got from his current visit were so deep that they were beyond wordy description. "What I have seen in your country is quite different from what I have heard through the Western media," he said, and continued: "While staying in your country, I have been deeply impressed by the fact that the Korean people live with smiles and enjoy happiness to the full unlike what I have heard in the West. "Your people live with their own aim and have successfully built their country with industrious and creative activities. "The grand and beautiful architectures built by your people made me think a great deal. "The wonderful architectures are rich in content and peculiar in architectural beauty, to say nothing of the external style. "We do not want the outbreak of a war in your country but hope that the Korean people will live in peace"
