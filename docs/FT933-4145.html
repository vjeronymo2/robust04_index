930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / Israel and Palestinians End Hostility: UN sets up task force for Gaza and Jericho The UN said yesterday it had set up a high-level task force to focus on economic and social development of the Gaza Strip and the West Bank town of Jericho, due shortly to come under Palestinian self-rule, Reuter reports. The group was established after requests from PLO chairman Yassir Arafat and Israeli foreign minister Shimon Peres, a spokesman said.
