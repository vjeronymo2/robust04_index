940525 FT 25 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Rise at Leveraged Opportunity Trust Leveraged Opportunity Trust, which specialises in US companies which have undertaken capital reorganisations, reported a net asset value - with loan stock converted and warrants and options unconverted - of 155.1p at March 31, a rise of 22 per cent over the 127.4p a year earlier. Fully diluted the value was 143.4p (121.6p). The net loss in the six months to March 31 was Pounds 41,000 (Pounds 33,000), giving losses per share of 0.55p (0.44p).
