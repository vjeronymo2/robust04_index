921125 FT 25 NOV 92 / Correction: Indian trade figures Trade figures for India were wrongly stated in the Financial Times yesterday. The trade deficit in the six months to September was in fact Dollars 2.62bn compared with Dollars 1.02bn for the same period of 1991. Exports were Dollars 8.55bn against Dollars 8.11bn and imports rose to Dollars 11.17bn from Dollars 9.13bn.
