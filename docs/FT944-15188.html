941019 FT 19 OCT 94 / International Company News: Navistar chairman to step down in March Navistar International, the Chicago-based manufacturer of trucks, diesel engines and school-bus chassis, has announced that chairman and chief executive Mr James Cotting will retire on March 30 1995, Reuter reports. Mr John Horne, president and chief operating officer, will succeed Mr Cotting as chief executive, Navistar said. Mr Cotting, who joined Navistar in 1979 as a senior vice-president of finance and planning, will continue as non-executive chairman of the company's board of directors and chairman of its executive committee.
