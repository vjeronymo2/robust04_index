940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / Foster Wheeler wins China deal Foster Wheeler Energy Corp said it has been awarded a contract worth about Dollars 185m (Pounds 123m) to supply two 300MW anthracite coal-fired steam generators for the Ezhou power project in Hubei Province, China, Reuter reports from Clinton, New Jersey. Foster Wheeler Energy said the contract marks its third big power plant project in China.
