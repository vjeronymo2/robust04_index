July 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

WRONG ON BOSTON

As a former resident of Boston for 38 years, I enjoyed Robert Burns' articles on the city in your July 1 issue ("Boston Pops!"). But I'd like to point out that the principal photo you used was incorrectly captioned. The building shown in the photo was not Faneuil Hall but Quincy Market, which is just east of the hall and very close to it.

FELIX DOHERTY

Bellflower
