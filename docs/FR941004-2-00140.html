Employment and Traininig Administration Labor Surplus Area Classification Under Executive Order 12073 and 10582; Time Extension for the Annual List of Labor Surplus Areas; Notice of Time Extension for the Annual List of Labor Surplus Areas AGENCY: Employment and Training Administration, Labor. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: The annual list of labor surplus areas for the period October 1, 1993 through September 30, 1994, is extended until further notice. The Employment and Training Administration will continue to accept exceptional circumstance petitions for labor surplus area designations on the basis of the criteria in effect for the current annual list which is being extended. Upon completion of a conversion on the labor surplus area program, a new labor surplus area list will be announced and published in the Federal Register . FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: William J.
