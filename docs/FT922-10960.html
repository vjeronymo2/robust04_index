920428 FT 28 APR 92 / UK Company News: Berry Starquest net asset value ahead Berry Starquest, an investment trust managed by GT Management, had a net asset value per share of 181.2p at January 31 compared with 143.2p a year earlier. Income totalled Pounds 425,000 (Pounds 500,000) and net revenue for the year fell from Pounds 217,000 to Pounds 157,000 for earnings per share of 3p (4.2p). The proposed dividend is 2p (1.8p).
