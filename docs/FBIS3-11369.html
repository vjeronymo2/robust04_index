Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Caracol's 0600 to 0900" newscast] [Text] AD/M-19 [19 April Movement Democratic Alliance] members of Antonio Navarro's presidential campaign have just made the following announcement: Antonio Navarro does not plan to withdraw his candidacy. AD/M-19 congressmen, contrary to what has been reported in some media, have confirmed their solidarity with Antonio Navarro. The AD/M-19 congressmen will meet in Santa Fe de Bogota at 0900 today to begin a new course, a new boost to Antonio Navarro's candidacy. Jose Cuesta is one of the AD/M-19 leaders proposing Navarro give up his candidacy and that Ramon Jimeno should be the presidential candidate and Gustavo Petro should be the vice presidential candidate for the AD/M-19, but Gustavo Petro has said he does not accept Jose Cuesta's proposal and supports Antonio Navarro.
