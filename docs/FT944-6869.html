941128 FT 28 NOV 94 / Tesco protest at Sainsbury action Tesco, the supermarket chain, has protested to its rival J. Sainsbury over the order to remove a Tesco 20-page Christmas advertising supplement, containing Pounds 7.50 worth of vouchers, from copies of the Radio Times on sale in Sainsbury stores. Sainsbury sent out the order by computer but soon withdrew it. It said it had been 'an error of judgment'. Sir Ian MacLaurin, Tesco chairman, yesterday wrote to Mr David Sainsbury, his opposite number, expressing his surprise at the initial move. He said: 'This seems a most improper, if not illegal, action. It denies your customers the opportunity to learn of the quality and value of the Christmas range at Tesco.'
