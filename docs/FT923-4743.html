920904 FT 04 SEP 92 / International Company News: Ames revises reorganisation AMES DEPARTMENT Stores, which went into bankruptcy over two years ago, yesterday filed a revised reorganisation plan, writes Nikki Tait in New York. This would offer creditors - whose claims total Dollars 1.6bn - some Dollars 325m in cash over four years, Dollars 35m of secured notes, certain litigation units and new shares in the reorganised company.
