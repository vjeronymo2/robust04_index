BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] Eleven terrorists have been killed in clashes with the security forces in Sirnak, Bingol, Batman, Diyarbakir and Elazig. Twelve persons abetting the terrorists have been captured in Bitlis. One terrorist involved in many incidents of murder and arson in Igdir and its vicinity has been captured in the Autonomous Republic of Nakhichevan and another one in Aydin. According to a statement issued by the Igdir Governor's Office, during the interrogation of the terrorist who was captured by the authorities in Nakhichevan and handed over to Turkey,and the one captured in Aydin, it was determined that they martyred a 1st lieutenant and a policeman, injured four soldiers and killed three citizens. It was also determined that they were involved in many cases of arson in the region. A revolver and large amounts of ammunition were seized.
