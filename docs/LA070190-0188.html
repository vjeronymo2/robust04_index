July 1, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ROAD CLOSURES

Caltrans plans the following closures on area freeways this week:

Two westbound lanes of the Pomona Freeway between Crossroads Parkway and Peck Road in the City of Industry will be closed from 6 a.m. to noon Sunday for road work.

The Atlantic Boulevard off-ramp of the San Bernardino Freeway in Alhambra will be closed from 9 a.m. to 2 p.m. Monday for landscaping.

Two westbound lanes of the Pomona Freeway between Garfield Avenue and Atlantic Boulevard, the Garfield and Atlantic on-ramp and off-ramp and the Findlay Avenue on-ramp in Monterey Park will be closed from 9:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. Monday for road work.

One lane of the eastbound Foothill Freeway connector to the eastbound San Bernardino Freeway in San Dimas will be closed from 9:30 a.m. to noon Monday for bridge repair.

The eastbound Garfield Avenue off-ramp of the San Bernardino Freeway in Alhambra will be closed from 9 a.m. to 2 p.m. Tuesday for landscaping.

The westbound Foothill Boulevard off-ramp of the Foothill Freeway in La Verne will be closed from 9 to 10:30 a.m. and the eastbound off-ramp will be closed from 11 a.m. to 1 p.m. Tuesday for stenciling.

The southbound lanes of the Corona Expressway at Garey Avenue in Pomona will be closed from 9:15 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. Friday for stenciling.
