920602 FT 02 JUN 92 / International Capital Markets: Protests likely over CBS plan for new charges CBS, the US television network that is controlled by the Tisch family, announced yesterday that starting next year it would levy new charges on it 214 affiliated stations. The announcement, which CBS justified in the light of adverse competitive and economic conditions faced by both networks and affiliates, is an unprecedented move that may trigger protests. CBS said the new charges would be based on programming and market sizes. The network estimated it would generate Dollars 20m to Dollars 25m of new net revenues. Wall Street reacted favourably to the new charges, marking CBS stock Dollars 12 5/8 up to Dollars 204 5/8.
