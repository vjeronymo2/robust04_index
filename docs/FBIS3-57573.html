Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Russian Federation State Duma Decree No. 16-1 GD signed by Rybkin and carried under the general heading "At the State Duma": "On the Adoption of a Decree by the Russian Federation Government"] [Text] The Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma decrees that: With a view to creating the requisite conditions for building up production of agricultural raw materials and foodstuffs, and for ensuring stabilization and the steady development of the agro-industrial complex under market-economy conditions, the Russian Federation Government shall be instructed to adopt the decree "On the Functioning of the Russian Federation Agro-Industrial Complex in 1994" within five days. [Signed] I.P. Rybkin, chairman of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma [Dated] Moscow, 19 January 1994 No. 16-1 GD
