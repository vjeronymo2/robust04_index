940810 FT 10 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Hemingway Properties back in black Hemingway Properties returned to the black in the six months to June 30, with pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.25m compared with a loss of Pounds 581,000. The group, which in June spent Pounds 30.2m on nine properties, saw turnover rise 48 per cent from Pounds 4.58m to Pounds 6.79m. Rental income contributed most to the increase, rising from Pounds 2.19m to Pounds 4.19m. Property sales remained stable at Pounds 2.6m (Pounds 2.39m). Hemingway now has a property portfolio worth Pounds 140m, some Pounds 120m of which was acquired in the past year. Mr Stanislas Yassukovich, chairman, said the group would 'continue to seek out property transactions'. Earnings per share worked through at 0.66p (losses 1.07p). Mr Yassukovich said the board expected to be able to recommend a final dividend of 0.4p.
