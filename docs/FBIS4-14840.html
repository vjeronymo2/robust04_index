BFN [Text] President of Armenia Levon Ter-Petrosyan agrees to the concept at the heart of the (?Russian) peace plan and CSCE program for a settlement to the Karabakh conflict, which envisages discussion of Nagorno Karabakh's status after a cease-fire, lifting of the communications blockade and withdrawal by forces from occupied territory, the acting press secretary for the Armenian president, Zurabyan, told a briefing in Yerevan. He said that Azerbaijan also agrees to this approach, but at the same time is not recognizing Nagorno Karabakh as an equal party in the negotiations and thus not accepting the political [word indistinct]. Zurabyan stressed that Armenia believes that the CIS can considerably influence a Karabakh settlement. The best guarantee of peace in Karabakh will be troops and observers to supervise demarcation, who should be deployed in the conflict zone.
