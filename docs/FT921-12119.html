920128 FT 28 JAN 92 / International Company News: Earnings tumble for US energy groups THE trend in sharply lower 1991 earnings from US oil companies continued yesterday as Arco and Unocal, both Los Angeles-based energy groups, reported seriously reduced profits. Arco's full-year net profit slumped by 64.7 per cent to Dollars 709m. The company said this occurred after taking Dollars 295m in net charges related to personnel reductions and property sales. Arco's fourth-quarter earnings fell by 52.7 per cent to Dollars 268m. Fourth-quarter revenues were 16 per cent lower at Dollars 4.8bn, while full-year revenues were 3.5 per cent down at Dollars 18.2bn. The share price closed unchanged at Dollars 108. Unocal, meanwhile, said 1991 net earnings dropped by 81.8 per cent to Dollars 73m. Revenues for the year fell to Dollars 19.9bn from Dollars 11.8bn in 1990. The company suffered a fourth-quarter loss of Dollars 16m, compared with a Dollars 38m net profit in the last quarter of 1990. Revenue in the quarter declined by 18 per cent to Dollars 2.77bn. Unocal's share price fell Dollars 3/8 to Dollars 22 5/8 .
