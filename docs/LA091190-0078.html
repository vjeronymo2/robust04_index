September 11, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

ALL NETWORKS WILL CARRY BUSH'S TALK

President Bush's address to a joint session of Congress tonight will be carried by the major television networks and Cable News Network.

Representatives of ABC, CBS, NBC and CNN said they will interrupt regular programming for the 6 p.m. PDT speech, in which Bush is to discuss Persian Gulf policy and his meeting with Soviet President Mikhail S. Gorbachev.
