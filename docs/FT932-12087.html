930429 FT 29 APR 93 / Benefits awareness campaign launched A CAMPAIGN to inform unemployed people that they can still claim benefits if they take upon a low-paid job was launched by the government yesterday. Mr Peter Lilley, social security secretary, denied the move was cosmetic and said the measures would help smooth the transition from unemployment into work. About 1.7m unemployed people could still qualify for family credit payments if they found work, he said.
