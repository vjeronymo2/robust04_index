December 12, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

'Mini-Season' for Bastille House: Paris' troubled Bastille Opera house is to raise the curtain on its first production next March but close its opera hall three months later for further technical fine-tuning. Directors of the new "People's Opera" commissioned by Socialist President Francois Mitterrand said Monday that the inaugural mini-season would feature two operas, concerts and recitals by some of the world's leading singers.Shauna Snow
