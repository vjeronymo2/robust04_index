BFN [Text] Moscow, May 30, RIA--The Duma faction of the Agrarian Party of Russia considers as "political indecency" the far from proper word used by former Russian Secretary of State Gennadiy Burbulis in the Sunday Itogi television programme to describe the way the new Constitution of Russia was adopted. This is said in a statement issued by the faction. The faction believes that Burbulis insulted millions of people who on December 12 voted for the new fundamental law. "The Agrarian Party was firmly against the adopted version of the Constitution. Mr Burbulis must have forgotten the vigour and persistence with which the presidential team, in which Mr Burbulis had no last role to play, was pushing through its draft of the fundamental law," said the statement. The document also mentions "Mr Burbulis's sinister role in the history of Russia of the recent period".
