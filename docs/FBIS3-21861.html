Language: Urdu Article Type:CSO [News Report: "Major Operation Against Narcotics Planned"] [Text] Karachi (NNI) -- On special instructions from Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto, a major operation to clean up narcotics in the country is being launched. According to reliable sources, a sensitive agency in our country and the cooperating U.S. drug enforcement cell have declared the lists prepared by the former government to be incomplete and have expressed their dissatisfaction. At the prime minister's instruction, a new list has been prepared about the people involved in the present drug Mafia and its three syndicates. This list was given to the Narcotics Control Board after approval by the prime minister. According to the new list, about 200 important persons are involved in the deplorable drug trade, and some of them have already been arrested. According to these sources, important information about heroin factories in Ghair area have been collected and a major anti-narcotics operation will be launched within a few days.
