Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 16 (KCNA) -- Messages of greetings came to the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il from foreign party leaders on the occasion of the New Year 1994. The messages came from general secretary of the C.C. [Central Committee], Workers' Party of Bangladesh Rashed Khan Menon, President of the Bangladesh People's League Garib Newaz, General Secretary of the People's Unity Party of Tunisia Mohamed Bel Hadj Amor, General Secretary of the Congolese Communist Party Kamba Raymond, Chairman of the Slovak Communist Party Vladimir Dado, President of the National Committee of the League of Communists-Movement for Yugoslavia Dragomir Draskovic, General Secretary of the New Yugoslav Communist Movement Branko Kitanovic, General Secretary of the People's Unity Party (Unionist) of Jordan Talal Hafun Ala Mai, General Secretary of the Executive Committee of the African Party for the Solidarity and Justice of Mali Mohamedoun Dicko, General Secretary of the Socialist Revolutionary Party of Peru Guzman Rivera Castaneda, National Coordinator of the Left Democratic Movement of Peru Oscar Ugarte Ubilluz, General Secretary of the People's Electoral Movement of Venezuela Adelso Gonzalez Urdaneta, General Secretary of the "New Alternative" of Venezuela Guillermo Garcia Ponce, General Secretary of the C.C., People' Vanguard of Costa Rica Humberto Vargas Carbonel, General Secretary of the United Left Movement of Dominica Miguel Mejia and Chairman Victor Sanchez Bilagra and General Secretary Ricardo Estigarribia of the February Revolutionary Party of Paraguay. The messages express the hope that the Korean people will achieve success this year, too, in socialist construction and in the struggle for national reunification under the wise leadership of Comrade Kim Chong-il. They wish Comrade Kim Chong-il good health and long life.
