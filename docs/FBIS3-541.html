Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The president, Sir Ketumile Masire, and the Zimbabwean president, Mr. Robert Mugabe, have been holding talks at the office of the president. President Robert Mugabe is on a four-day state visit. The Zimbabwean delegation at the talks includes the foreign minister, Mr. Nathan Shamuyarira; the deputy minister of trade and industry, Mr. F. K. Moyo; and the Zimbabwean high commissioner to Botswana, Miss (Lucia Mudingire). The Botswana delegation to the talks included the minister for presidential affairs and public administration, Lieutenant General Mompati Merafhe; the minister for foreign affairs, Dr. Gaositwe Chiepe; the assistant minister of agriculture, Mr. Roy Blackbeard; and other senior government officials. A state banquet will be held in honor of President Robert Mugabe at the Gaborone Sun Hotel this evening.
