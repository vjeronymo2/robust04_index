941112 FT 12 NOV 94 / International Company News: BHP in venture with US group The steel division of Broken Hill Proprietary, the Australian resources group, and North Star Steel, part of the privately-owned Cargill group in the US, are joining forces to build and operate a new steel mini-mill in the US Midwest at a cost of around USDollars 400m, writes Nikki Tait in Sydney. The two companies will have 50-50 interests in the venture.
