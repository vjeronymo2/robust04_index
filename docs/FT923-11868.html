920722 FT 22 JUL 92 / Go-ahead for Hyde gas field THE government gave the go-ahead yesterday for development of the Hyde gas field in the North Sea 40 miles east of Humberside. The field - developed by BP, Statoil, the Norwegian state-owned company, and main contractors UIE Scotland and Kvaerner H&G - has proven reserves of 143bn cubic ft, and is due to start producing in October next year. BP also announced a Pounds 1.4m study with eight other oil companies to explore the feasibility of an integrated development of four oil and gas fields in the central North Sea.
