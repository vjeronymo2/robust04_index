BFN [Unattributed POSFACTUM report in the "In Brief" column] [Text] Moscow -- On 20 April the Party of Constitutional Democrats (PCD) will hold a press conference at the International Moscow Press Club, subject: "New Order." According to party chairman Viktor Zolotarev, the press conference is going to hear the PCD leadership announce a change in its methods of political activity. As of now the PCD will rely on populism and scandal. "We are going to fight fascism with its own weapons, and we hope to do this as early as this year," V. Zolotarev stated.
