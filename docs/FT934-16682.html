931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: North British Canadian ahead North British Canadian Investment raised net asset value to 137.1p at the end of August, against 118p six months earlier and 95.9p the year before. Net earnings dropped from Pounds 505,000 to Pounds 471,000 for the six months period and earnings per share were 1.74p (1.87p). The interim is again 0.94p.
