BFN [Text] Jeddah, June 8, SPA -- The council of executive directors of Islamic Development Bank (IDB) ended its 151st session here today under the chairmanship of Osama Faqih, the IDB president. During its session, the council approved the bank's general budget, the independent annual budget for its programmes, the budget and plan for the Islamic research and training institute, an affiliate of the bank for 1415 ah [Arabic year from June 1994-June 1995]. The council approved a total financial lending of nearly 83.5 million U.S. dollars, which would benefit eight member states of the bank and three Islamic societies in non-member countries.
