November 12, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

HOT PROPERTY: "Love You to Death," a television miniseries recounting the bizarre David Brown murder case, is in production in L.A. and due to air by spring.

Republic Pictures is billing the plot as "one of the most shocking crimes of the decade." . . . Brown, an Anaheim Hills computer entrepreneur, is serving a life sentence for persuading his 14-year-old daughter to murder his fifth wife. The girl went to prison for four years while Brown collected his dead wife's life insurance and married her sister. Dave Lesher and Janet Eastman
