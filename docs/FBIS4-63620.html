BFN [Text] The weekly ARGUMENTY I FAKTY still remains the most popular newspaper in Russia. INTERFAX has received this information from the All-Russian Center for the Study of Public Opinion. In mid-May the center conducted a poll among 1,600 people in Russia. This showed that 23 percent of those polled always read this weekly. KOMSOMOLSKAYA PRAVDA has moved to second, while the third spot is held firmly by the SPID-INFO magazine, read by 11 percent of those questioned.
