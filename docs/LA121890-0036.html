December 18, 1990, Tuesday, Southland Edition

CHRYSLER, NV PHILIPS TO FORM JOINT VENTURE

Acustar Inc., the parts-making subsidiary of Chrysler Corp., said it had worked outthe final details of a joint venture with Dutch electronics giant NV Philips to devlop, produceand sell automotive radios and sound systems.

Philips Car Stereo International, a unit of NV Philips, will hold a 51% stake in the newcompany, called Philips and Acustar Auto-electronics GmbH & Co.

The new company will be headquartered in Wetzlar, Germany, and Farmington Hills, Mich.
