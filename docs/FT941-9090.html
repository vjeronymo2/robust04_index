940216 FT 16 FEB 94 / International Company News: Mining boost North Broken Hill Peko, the diversified Australian mining group, is to accelerate underground mine production at its Northparkes gold and copper project, which it is developing in conjunction with the Sumitomo group of companies. The development will enable process plant production capacity to rise from 45,000 tonnes a year of copper contained in concentrate to 65,000 tonnes.
