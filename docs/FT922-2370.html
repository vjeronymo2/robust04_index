920617 FT 17 JUN 92 / Five killed in Kuwait blast Five men working in Kuwait for a British company clearing munitions left over from the Gulf war have been killed in an explosion, AP reports from Kuwait. The Briton and four Indians were working near an ammunition dump 33 miles south of Kuwait City on Monday when the explosion occurred, said Mr Gary Dickson for the company, Royal Ordnance. Royal Ordnance is nearing completion of a year-long contract believed to be worth Dollars 100m to clear leftover ammunition.
