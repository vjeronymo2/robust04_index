March 15, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $25 Wednesday when Maid O Gold, the first half of his win parlay, ran out of the money in the third race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $900.80.

Bob's bet today is $40 to win on My Niriko in the fourth.
