Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] On 12 January, Chilean Foreign Minister Enrique Silva Cimma met in private with Humberto Hernandez, newly appointed Cuban consul in Santiago de Chile, with whom he spoke about the status of bilateral ties. Following the meeting, Hernandez stated that both governments see as very positive the progress made in ties between the two nations since their reestablishment at consular and economic and trade levels.
