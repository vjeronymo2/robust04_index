Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Nanjing, December 30 (XINHUA) -- China's first law office to specialize in cases concerning tourism recently opened business in Nanjing, the capital of east China's Jiangsu Province. The Jinling Law Firm offers services ranging from international tourism, foreign trade and economic cooperation to intellectual property rights. With headquarters in Nanjing, it has set up agencies in the United States, Japan and Hong Kong. The office aims at clients who are either domestic or overseas tourist agencies, or foreign tourists, investors and businessmen.
