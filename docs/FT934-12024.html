931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Majedie lifts net asset value to 221p Majedie Investments, the self-managed trust which concentrates on listed investments, had a net asset value of 221p at September 30, up from 169p a year earlier. Net revenue improved to Pounds 2.81m (Pounds 2.64m) for earnings of 5.35p (5.03p) per share. Sir John Barlow, chairman, said the performance benefited from the 'judicious taking' of scrip dividends which accounted for Pounds 419,000 of income in the year. A recommended final dividend of 3p brings the total to 5p (4.75p).
