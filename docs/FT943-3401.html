940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Norweb re-purchase Norweb yesterday became the sixth regional electricity group to re-purchase its own shares by buying back 5m of its own shares representing 2.89 per cent of its issued capital, at 795p a share. Meanwhile South Western Electricity bought back a further 500,000 of its shares at 766p bringing the total shares it has repurchased in recent weeks to 4.75m shares representing about 3.9 per cent of its capital.
