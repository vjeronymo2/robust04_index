Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Geneva, Jan 17 (AFP) -- A UN spokeswoman here denied Monday [17 that the commander of UN forces in former Yugoslavia, French General Jean Cot, is to be replaced. A United Nations official in New York, who declined to be identified, had said earlier that Cot, in command of the UN Protection Force (UNPROFOR) since July 1, "will soon leave his position." But Therese Gastaut, spokeswoman here for UN Secretary-General Butrus Butrus-Ghali, retorted: "That is not correct." Asked straight out whether the secretary-general had dismissed Cot, she replied: "No." [passage omitted]
