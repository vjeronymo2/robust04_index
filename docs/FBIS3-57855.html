Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] President Glavkos Kliridhis has hailed the new Greek-Cypriot defense doctrine's significance, stressing that peace in Cyprus will be more secure as Greece becomes more involved. The president said the situation would change significantly when the invader knows that every new aggressive action in Cyprus implies a Greek-Turkish war. Kliridhis said the defense doctrine will be go into effect during the visit by Greek Defense Minister Yerasimos Arsenis and a delegation of senior military officials in early January. The president said this to National Guardsmen this morning when he visited National Guard posts in Nicosia. Defense Minister Kostandinos Iliadhis and National Guard Chief General Yeoryios Siradhakis accompanied Kliridhis.
