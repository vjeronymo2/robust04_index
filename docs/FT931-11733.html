930202 FT 02 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Whitbread sells last of Euro Pizza Huts Whitbread, the brewing and retailing group, has completed the disposal of its European Pizza Hut operation with an agreement to sell its 17 outlets in France to Pizza Hut International, the PepsiCo subsidiary which owns the brand. Whitbread and Pizza Hut International are continuing to expand their joint venture in the UK, which now has nearly 300 restaurants and delivery outlets.
