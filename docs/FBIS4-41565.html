BFN [Text] Robert Young, assistant under secretary of state at the British Foreign Office, is visiting Lithuania. The guest has met Foreign Minister Povilas Gylys; Homeland Union Chairman Vytautas Landsbergis; Seimas Deputy Chairman Egidijus Bickauskas; Kazys Bobelis, chairman of the Seimas Foreign Affairs Committee; Internal Affairs Minister Romasis Vaitekunas; and representatives of the European PHARE [Poland-Hungary -- Actions for Economic Reconversion] program, the UN development program, and the IMF in Lithuania. Today the guest from Great Britain will visit Kaunas and Klaipeda. He will leave for Latvia on Sunday.
