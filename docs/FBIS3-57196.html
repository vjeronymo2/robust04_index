Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow January 24 ITAR-TASS -- Aleksandr Yakovlev from the Institute of State and Law of the Russian Academy of Sciences was appointed by President Boris Yeltsin as his representative in parliament, the press service of the Russian leader said on Monday. Yakovlev was born in 1927 and is doctor of law. Aleksandr Maksimovich Yakovlev is not to be confused with Aleksandr Nikolayevich Yakovlev who had been appointed by the president as head of the "Ostankino" TV network.
