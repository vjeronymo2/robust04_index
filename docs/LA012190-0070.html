January 21, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

PARK'S TRAFFIC IDEA

Michael Eisner, Disney's chairman, is expecting federal government assistance to build roads and solve traffic problems in Anaheim or Long Beach before he can consent to building a new Disneyland Park.

This is a charming idea. Since we in Orange County are unable to find federal money to solve our existing freeway problems, Eisner, if successful in obtaining federal money, might consider sharing Disneyland profits -- so we can solve our traffic problems.

DAVID G. BAKER

Yorba Linda
