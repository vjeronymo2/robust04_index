940331 FT 31 MAR 94 / UK Company News: NB Smaller Cos NB Smaller Companies Trust increased net asset value per share by 31 per cent from 118p to 154.5p in the 12 months to February 28. Mr Lawrence Tindale, chairman, said it had been 'a vintage year for smaller companies, reversing four years of under-performance.' Gross revenue came to Pounds 1.47m (Pounds 1.54m) but borrowing costs fell to Pounds 31,000 (Pounds 157,000). Management expenses took Pounds 128,000 (Pounds 106,000) leaving pre-tax revenue of Pounds 1.31m (Pounds 1.27m). The final dividend of 2.63p makes a total of 3.57p (3.45p) for the year. Earnings came through at 3.79p (3.53p).
