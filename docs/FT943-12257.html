940726 FT 26 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Storm Storm Group, which is involved with intellectual property rights relating to toys and cartoon characters, has announced the result of its placing and open offer to raise Pounds 2.3m. Of the 16.97m shares offered at 15p each, some 5.83m (34.4 per cent of the issue) were placed firm with institutional investors. Valid acceptances from shareholders were received for 3.92m (23.1 per cent) and the remaining 7.21m (42.5 per cent) will be taken up in accordance with the placing agreement. Storm's shares trade on the USM.
