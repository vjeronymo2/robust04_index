920828 FT 28 AUG 92 / UK Company News: New Guernsey lower with Pounds 12,623 New Guernsey Securities Trust, an investment holding company, suffered a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 19,759 to Pounds 12,623 in the six months to June 30. Net profits amounted to Pounds 9,817 (Pounds 15,366) for earnings per share of 0.5p (0.8p). Net asset value per share stood at 91.2p against 89p a year earlier.
