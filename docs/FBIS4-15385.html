BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] Moscow April 8 TASS -- Former Russian prosecutor general Aleksey Kazannik confirmed his resignation on Friday on his own will and no pressure was exerted on him, according to speaker of the Federation Council Vladimir Shumeyko. "Kazannik adopted the decision on his own initiative", he told TASS adding that the former prosecutor asked to be given the floor for his statement why [as received] talking to a group of deputies. "The essence of his speech was important to me and I realised that regardless of political colours the essence was correct and aimed at achieving civic peace in society", Shumeyko said. "That is why we have agreed to give the floor to Kazannik", he added. Shumeyko believes that in a couple of weeks the upper chamber will return to the issue of the prosecutor general. It will accept Kazannik's resignation and will consider the new candidate nominated by the president - Aleksey Ilyushenko.
