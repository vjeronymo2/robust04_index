Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] This afternoon in Carthage Palace, President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali received Major al-Khuwaylidi al-Humaydi, Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi's personal envoy to his excellency the president. Maj. al-Khuwaylidi al-Humaydi said after the audience that he was very pleased to meet his excellency the president and convey to him the respectful greetings and appreciation of his brother, Col. Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, leader of the September revolution. He pointed out that he also conveyed to President Zine El Abdine Ben Ali a verbal message from the leader of the Libyan revolution concerning bilateral relations and ways of developing them, as well as with the march of the Arab Maghreb Union and scopes of its development for the good of the peoples of the region. Maj. al-Khuwaylidi al-Humaydi added that the meeting was an occasion for more dialogue over several international and Arab issues of common concern. He said: On this occasion, I have stressed the eagerness of the Arab Libyan leadership and people to promote relations and cooperation between the two peoples because we are convinced that this is a fateful and strategic option confirmed by events and time. After mentioning the common objectives, hopes, and destiny of the Tunisian and Libyan peoples, which call for more cohesiveness between them, Maj. al-Khuwaylidi al-Humaydi concluded his statement saying: I have found with President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali the same interest, feelings, and satisfaction regarding the situation of relations between the September revolution and that of 7 November [overthrow of Tunisian President Habib Bourgiba].
