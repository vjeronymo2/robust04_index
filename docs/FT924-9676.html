921104 FT 04 NOV 92 / Commodities and Agriculture: Boll weevil hits Chinese cotton A SERIOUS outbreak of cotton boll weevil on a crop already highly stressed by drought has cut the estimate for China's 1992-93 cotton crop from 25.3m bales (480 lb each) to 19.9m, according to a US agricultural attache said, Reuter reports from Washington.
