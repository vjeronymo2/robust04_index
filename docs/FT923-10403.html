920730 FT 30 JUL 92 / International Company News: Endesa ahead at Pta54bn EMPRESA Nacional de Electricidad (Endesa), Spain's state-controlled electric utility, lifted net consolidated profit by 17 per cent to Pta53.5bn (Dollars 568.8m) in the first six months of 1992 compared with Pta45.8bn in the same period the previous year, AP-DJ reports. Earnings per share advanced to Pta205.74 against Pta176.1 the previous period. Revenues for the period went ahead to Pta356.3bn, against Pta341.8bn. The group's capital expenditures climbed 2.2 per cent to Pta32.49bn, against the first half of 1991, but financial investments fell 79 per cent to Pta12.08bn.
