920908 FT 08 SEP 92 / Survey highlights travel time PROFESSIONAL and manag-erial workers spend more than three years of their working lives travelling - time estimated by British Telecommunications to be worth more than Pounds 7.5bn a year. A BT survey of 300 executives found they spent their travel time dozing, daydreaming or reading the sports page. Fewer than two in five said they worked while travelling.
