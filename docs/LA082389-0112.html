August 23, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Two-Year Notes Auctioned: The Treasury Department said it has accepted $9.5 billion in tenders for two-year notes at an interest rate of 8.25%. The average yield on the notes, which will be issued Aug. 31, will be 8.37%. The interest rate is the highest since the May 24th auction, when it was 8.75%.
