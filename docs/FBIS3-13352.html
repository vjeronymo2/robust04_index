Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Regarding presidential activities, President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali at noon today at the Carthage Palace met with the Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat. [passage omitted]. President Yasir 'Arafat said that his meeting with His Excellency the President dealt with the deliberations of the UN Security Council regarding the massacre of the al-Ibrahimi mosque. In this connection he said that the fact that the Council has not adopted a resolution seriously affects the situation inside the occupied territories as a whole. He added that he had informed President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali of the aspects of the message he received last night from the U.S. Secretary of State Mr. Warren Christopher which dealt with the importance of the continuation and movement forward of the peace process. President 'Arafat renewed in this regard the Palestinian stance which stresses the necessity of issuing a strong resolution by the Security Council which takes into account the issue of Jerusalem and the condemnation of the attack on the al-Ibrahimi mosque and the adoption of immediate international measures to protect the Palestinian people. Mr. Arafat said that he had thanked President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali for the efforts he had made one more than one level to overcome these difficulties, hoping to arrive at a resolution that protect the Palestinian people from all attacks.
