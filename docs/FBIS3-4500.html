Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 28 (XINHUA) -- Qiao Shi, chairman of the Standing Committee of China's National People's Congress (NPC), met here this evening with a delegation of the Assembly of the Council of Europe, led by its president, Miguel Angel Martinez. On behalf of the NPC Standing Committee, Qiao welcomed Martinez, saying that the delegation's visit would promote the development of relations between China and the European Council, and between China's NPC and the Assembly of the European Council. Martinez said that he had had pleasant contacts with the Chinese side, which enhanced mutual understanding. He said that with the end of the Cold War, people will build a peaceful and cooperative world, and China is an important partner in building a new order for the world. He noted that he and his delegation came to China with much respect to develop cooperation with China. Vice-chairman of the NPC Standing Committee Ni Zhifu and chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the NPC Zhu Liang were present at the meeting. After the meeting, Qiao hosted a banquet for Martinez and his party. This morning, Zhu Liang held talks with Martinez. This afternoon, Vice-Premier and Foreign Minister Qian Qichen also met with Martinez. Qian said that it was very important to strengthen mutual understanding and cooperation between China and countries of the European Council, hoping that the European Council would play a positive role in this regard. Martinez said that the European Council pays much attention to relations with China, hoping to expand exchanges and cooperation with China, which met appreciation from Qian. The delegation of the Assembly of the European Council arrived here on March 27 on a friendly visit to China at the invitation of the NPC Standing Committee.
