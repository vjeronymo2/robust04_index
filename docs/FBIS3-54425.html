Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] An official round of Yemeni-Indonesian talks was held at the Foreign Ministry today. Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah led our country's delegation to the talks, and Foreign Minister Ali Alatas led the Indonesian delegation. The two sides reviewed bilateral relations, means of promoting them, regional and international developments, ways to promote the Nonaligned Movement's role, and the establishment of relations of cooperation between developing and industrialized states. The two sides also signed an economic, technical, scientific, and trade cooperation agreement as a general framework to organize relations between the two countries.
