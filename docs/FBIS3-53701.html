Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 18 Jan (MENA)--Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, head of the PLO Information Department, arrived in Cairo this evening to attend the meetings of the ministerial follow-up committee formed by the second Islamic conference of the Islamic Conference Organization countries' information ministers. The committee is chaired by Information Minister Safwat al-Sharif and will begin its meetings in Cairo tomorrow, Wednesday. 'Abd-Rabbuh stated that the meeting will follow up on the objectives that were set during previous meetings and will work to bolster cooperation between the Islamic countries to serve their joint causes. He said that convening the meetings in Cairo has a special significance since that city is currently the venue for meetings and conferences that have influence on various levels and in many fields. He noted that Cairo is the heart of every media and political activity. He affirmed the interest of the Palestinian side to always coordinate with Egypt on all levels to achieve the necessary progress in the peace process.
