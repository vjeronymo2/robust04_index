March 6, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

TOP FIVE: A ranking of joint tax returns filed by couples puts Orange County fifth behind Bay Area counties in median income for 1988, the state reports. . . . Statewide, the median joint income was $35,962.

1988 MEDIAN INCOME

For California couples filing joint tax returns, by county.

Rank County 1 Marin $53,147 2 Santa Clara $47,647 3 Contra Costa $47,320 4 San Mateo $46,580 5 Orange $43,181

Source: California Franchise Tax Board Kristina Lindgren and Mark I. Pinksy
