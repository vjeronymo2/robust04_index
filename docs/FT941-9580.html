940214 FT 14 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Simon access production moves to Gloucester Simon Engineering, the maker of access equipment including fire rescue gear, is concentrating production at its Gloucester site. The group said that the assembly and administration operations now carried out at Dudley in the West Midlands would be transferred to Simon Gloster Saro, which would become the centre of operations for Europe. Up to 150 jobs are likely to go from Dudley. The restructuring costs of Pounds 2.5m will be charged as an exceptional item in the 1993 accounts. Simon is in the middle of a restructuring programme to cut debt.
