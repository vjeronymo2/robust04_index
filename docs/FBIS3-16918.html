Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shchegoleva] [Text] Sochi, 22 Mar (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin left Sochi by air for Moscow this afternoon. Speaking to journalists prior to his departure from Sochi airport, the head of the cabinet described the fabrications about a deterioration in the Russian president's health as "insulting." The prime minister strongly denied recent rumors that Boris Yeltsin's health has sharply deteriorated. "Yesterday Boris Nikolayevich and I did almost four hours' work together. The work was continued this morning," Viktor Chernomyrdin said. "We resolved a whole series of questions -- both economic and political -- and discussed the situation in the south of the country." "Somebody has an interest in destabilizing the situation," he stressed.
