May 3, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: TWO ROOMMATES FOUND SLAIN EXECUTION-STYLE

The mother of one of two roommates found the two slain execution-style in an Inglewood house where police found drugs and evidence of drug dealing, officials said Wednesday.

Killed were Welton Moore Jr., 25, and Stacey Glover, 28, said Police Sgt. Harold Moret.

"They were tied hand and foot, and both were shot through the head," Moret said. He added that a small quantity of cocaine was found on a coffee table of the home in the 9600 block of 2nd Avenue, and other evidence also led detectives to conclude it was the site of drug dealing.

Moore's mother discovered the bodies Wednesday morning after she came to the house because she had been unable to get through by telephone.
