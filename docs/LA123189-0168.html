December 31, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

LOYAL PAL WINS AT HAWTHORNE

Loyal Pal, an 11-1 shot, withstood the stretch charge of Ankles to post a two-length victory Saturday in the $52,550 Chicago Handicap at Hawthorne Race Course.

The winner, ridden by Carlos Silva, ran the 1 1/16 miles in 1:45 over a sloppy track and paid $24.00, $8.40 and $4.80.
