BFN [Text] Seoul, April 9 (YONHAP) -- South Korea and China signed a memorandum in Beijing Saturday on agricultural information exchange, cross-visits of experts and other cooperation measures. Kim Kwang-hui, head of the rural development administration, signed the memorandum of understanding with Wang Lianzheng, president of the Academy of Agricultural Sciences in China, as one of the follow-up measures to the agreement at last November's Science-technology Cooperation Committee meeting, officials here said. Seoul and Beijing will establish systematic ways of exchanging farming technology as well as latest information on rural science. Officials said they especially hope for access to wide range of China's rich variant agricultural products.
