Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The border guards of the 12th and 10th posts, Pyanj Unit, thwarted the attempts of two groups of fighters trying to cross into Tajikistan from Afghanistan in the early hours of February 4, in one case artillery was used by the guards and in the other, fire from small arms. The groups retreated into Afghanistan. There were no casualties among the border guards, officials in the Russian Border Guard's press bureau told INTERFAX. In the area patrolled by the 12th post of the Moskovskiy Unit the border guards have thwarted an attempt to build a cableway designed to transport weapons and ammunition from Afghanistan into Tajikistan.
