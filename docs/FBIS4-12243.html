BFN [Excerpt] Brother Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine, met in Cairo this morning with his brother Muhammad Husni Mubarak, president of the brotherly Arab Republic of Egypt. During the meeting they discussed the latest developments in the peace process and ways of removing the obstacles which still stand in its path. The meeting was attended on the Palestinian side by brothers Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, member of the PLO Executive Committee; Dr. Nabil Sha'th, political adviser to brother President Abu-'Ammar; Sa'id Kamal, Palestinian ambassador in Egypt; and Zuhdi al-Qidrah, Fatah representative in Egypt.
