Language: Urdu Article Type:CSO [News Report: "Industrialist Arrested; 4 Kilograms of Heroin Recovered"] [Text] Karachi (Staff Reporter)--Inspector Noor Hussein Pathan at the instruction of Basharat Hussein Somroo and Manzur Ali Rana, group leader at the Pakistan Narcotics Control Board [PNCB], set up a check point and searched a car with tag number M-8385. He recovered four kilograms of heroin from the car and arrested local industrialist Irshad Ali Siddiqui who was riding in that car. According to PNCB sources this heroin was being smuggled to Los Angeles (America). The accused is suspected of being involved in drug smuggling for a long time. The PNCB has taken a 7 day remand of the accused and is investigating the case. Mohammed Umar Moreo, regional PNCB director, has announced the award of commendations to the team that arrested the criminal.
