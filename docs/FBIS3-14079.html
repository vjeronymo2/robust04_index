Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres said that the government would publicly announce its decision when it decided whether or not to evacuate the Jews from Hebron. The government does not just have intentions, it also reaches decisions, Peres said. The foreign minister added that there is only one address for the security of all Hebron residents, both Arabs and Jews, and that is the Israeli Government. Minister Peres was speaking at a political debate in the Knesset.
