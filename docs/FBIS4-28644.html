BFN [Text] Seoul, June 9 (YONHAP) -- The government has decided to send cultural and sports attaches to six cities in five foreign countries. "With President Kim Yong-sam's approval on last May 23, we have since early this month been consulting with the Foreign Ministry and other related offices on the dispatch of cultural and sports attaches," Culture and Sports Minister Yi Min-sop told reporters on Thursday. He said a cultural and sports attache will be posted to each of New York, Los Angeles, Paris, Tokyo, Moscow and Beijing. The attaches will be selected from among those officials armed with specialized knowledge and good foreign language proficiency in the areas of culture and sports, Yi said. The duties of the cultural and sports attaches will be to excavate, restore or study Korea's historical relics abroad, support overseas cultural, art, sports and youth programs, and assist in the protection and exports of Korea's cultural pieces.
