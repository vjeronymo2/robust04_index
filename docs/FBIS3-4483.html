Language: English Article Type:BFN [By Stanley Cheung & Benjamin Yeh] [Text] Hong Kong, March 25 (CNA) -- Central News Agency made its mass-media news system available in Hong Kong Friday [25 March]. Users of the system will be provided with round-the-clock coverage of Chinese- and English-language news, business reports and futures updates from around the world, and color photos. The service will be transmitted by telephone to the customers' computers. The semi-official news agency will formally introduce the system to potential Hong Kong customers at a display in mid-April. CNA soon will introduce to Hong Kong an on-time news service, which is already available in Taiwan.
