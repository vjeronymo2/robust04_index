940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Magnolia makes Czech buy Magnolia Group, the picture frame manufacturer and publisher, is buying a 51 per cent interest in Lira, a Czech picture frame company, for Kcs52.5m (Pounds 1.2m). Magnolia also warned that its results for the first half of 1993 would be disappointing. Increased marketing costs and delayed orders were blamed. The shares fell 9p to 83p.
