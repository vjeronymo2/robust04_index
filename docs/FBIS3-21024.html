Language: French Article Type:CSO [Unattributed article: "A Matra-Cap Systems Image-Processing Station for Sweden"] [Text] Paris--Matra Cap Systems just signed an agreement with the Swedish company Satellitbid covering the supply of a satellite-image processing and storage station for the Kiruna and Esrange Center, the subsidiary of Matra Hachette and Cap Gemini Sogeti announced in a press release. This highly-automated new-generation station, which in a first stage will produce images from Spot-1 to Spot-4, will have the capacity required to eventually process other images from optical (visible and infrared) satellites or from radar satellites. Matra Cap Systems employs 900 people and achieves sales of one billion French francs.
