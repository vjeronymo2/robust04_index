July 30, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: COMING ATTRACTIONS

Today: Box-office results will determine whether the No. 1 film "Ghost" scared off its competitors, "Die Hard 2" and "Presumed Innocent" in the summer movie sweepstakes.

Wednesday: KABC-AM (790) kicks offs its 30th birthday celebration with a special broadcast, from 5 to 9 a.m., of the "Ken & Bob Morning Show" from Ed Debevic's Beverly Hills diner.

Thursday: The big mystery surrounding today's announcement of the nominations for the 42nd annual prime-time Emmy Awards is how many nominations the quirky soap "Twin Peaks" will receive.

Sunday: Madonna brings her much-debated concert presentation to the living rooms of the United States when HBO and Westwood One broadcast a simulcast of her performance in Nice, France.GREG BRAXTON
