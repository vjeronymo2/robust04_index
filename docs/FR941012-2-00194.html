ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: The FTA is required by 49 U.S.C. 5307(d)(1) (formerly section 9(e)(2) of the Federal Transit Act, as amended) to obtain specific annual certifications for its formula assistance program for urbanized areas. Through this Notice, FTA has compiled the certifications and assurances in one document applicable to all of the FTA grant programs (see Appendix A.) EFFECTIVE DATE: October 12, 1994.
