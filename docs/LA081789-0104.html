August 17, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

THE STATE

Police said a 23-year-old man apparently leaped to his death near Leggett in Mendocino County two days after stabbing his girlfriend's sister to death in her Walnut Creek home. Kendall Dean Ramsel's body, found 200 feet below a bridge, was identified by fingerprints and a short suicide note, according to investigators. Walnut Creek Police Lt. Ross Sutton said officers have no idea why Ramsel apparently stabbed Laura Wang, 23, to death before taking his own life.
