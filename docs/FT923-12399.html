920718 FT 18 JUL 92 / Coalition bid Macedonian President Kiro Gligorov yesterday asked Social Democrat deputy Petar Gosev to form a new coalition government for the former Yugoslav republic, according to the Belgrade-based Tanjug news agency, Reuter reports from Belgrade. Tanjug said Mr Gosev had 20 days to name his cabinet, but had already secured the support of a big majority in the 120-seat Macedonian parliament.
