BFN [Text] Ankara, June 27 (AA) -- Foreign Ministry spokesman Ferhat Ataman said on Monday [27 June] that a demonstration of the separatist terrorist PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] organization held in Frankfurt has turned to a terrorist activity. Ataman said the demonstration was a kind of terrorist activity in spite of the guarantee that the German Government has given adding that Ankara felt disappointment on the issue. Ataman said necessary initiations were made in Ankara, Bonn and Frankfurt on the issue. He said "we think Germany's decision to ban PKK should not be diluted and this terror organization should not be encouraged" adding that "we will be in close connection with the German officials." During the demonstration, separatists carried a picture of the leader of PKK and pieces of clothes symbolizing colors of the separatist terrorist organization. Turkey earlier expressed pleasure to see determination of Germany to extradite supporters of terrorists who violated German laws.
