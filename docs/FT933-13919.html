930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Druck overcomes first half setback Despite a first-half setback, Druck Holdings improved turnover and maintained pre-tax profits in the year to March 31. Turnover of this USM-quoted concern came to Pounds 27.9m (Pounds 27.3m) and the pre-tax surplus was static at Pounds 4.69m. Earnings per share were 48.1p (46.5p) and the final divided is 6.6p for a total of 10p (9.4p). First-half profits were down at Pounds 1.75m (Pounds 2.41m).
