941010 FT 10 OCT 94 / Company News This Week: Union des Assurances de Paris - Other Companies One of France's largest insurance groups is due to publish first-half results on Wednesday, with analysts predicting profits up to FFr1.1bn-1.3bn (Pounds 132m-Pounds 150m) from FFr1.1bn. Profits on UAP's insurance activities may be dragged down by losses at Banque Worms.
