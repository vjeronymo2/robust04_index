930108 FT 08 JAN 93 / International Company News: DSM warns of further job cuts DSM, the Dutch chemicals group, is to seek an additional 800 to 1,000 job cuts in 1993 as part of its continuing cost-cutting and reorganisation drive, writes Ronald van de Krol. The company, which has reduced its workforce by nearly 2,000 to around 23,000 in recent months, said that some of the fresh job losses might involve compulsory redundancies unless the unions agreed to proposals on early retirement and outplacement schemes.
