930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Graystone redeems Pounds 3.25m of loan note Graystone, the engineering components company, has completed negotiations for the redemption of half of the Pounds 6.5m loan note issued to Prospect Industries in payment for its acquisition in November. Graystone has redeemed Pounds 3.25m nominal of the loan note for Pounds 2.08m. In addition it has agreed revised terms with Prospect in the event of a redemption of the balance. Graystone also said it intended to apply to the High Court to reorganise and reduce its share capital.
