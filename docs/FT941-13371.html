940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / Ambulance staff win compensation Settlements totalling Pounds 50,000 have been paid by Northumbria Ambulance Service in the past 12 months to four workers who were unfairly sacked, Unison, the public service union, said yesterday. The ambulance service confirmed that four settlements had been paid but declined to give figures. It did not accept that the dismissals were unfair, but did accept that in two cases dismissal procedures had not been followed.
