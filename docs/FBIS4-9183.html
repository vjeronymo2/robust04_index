BFN [TA SR report: "A Slight Decline in Unemployment"] [Text] Bratislava--Some 370,493 job seekers, of whom 98,666 were receiving social security, were registered at labor offices in Slovakia at the end of March. According to a report from the Ministry of Labor, Social Affairs, and the Family, the unemployment level in March fell from 14.76 to 14.54 percent. The highest unemployment level, 26.3 percent, is in the Rimavska Sobota District; it is followed by the Spisska Nova Ves and Vranov nad Toplou Districts. The lowest unemployment level is still being recorded in the Bratislava-City (4.27 percent), Trencin, and Kosice-City Districts. The number of vacancies increased in March, but, despite this, there are 40 job seekers per vacancy.
