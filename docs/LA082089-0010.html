August 20, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE QUEST FOR PASTRAMI PARADISE

Regarding Reichl's second article in the Aug. 6 issue, "Great Pastrami Taste-Off Matches Sandwiches From L.A. Top Delis," which rates eight delis:

The Stage Deli (No. 3 in Reichl's survey) piles its meat on so-called "rye" bread that contains no seeds. The purveyors of this stuff should be consigned for life to counting the caraway seeds in every Carnegie creation.

BERYL E. ARBIT

Encino
