Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Text] Vladimir Dlouhy, minister of industry and trade, and Uladzimir Radkevich, Belarusian Cabinet member and chairman of the State Committee for External Economic Relations, signed an agreement on trade and economic relations in Prague today. The document is designed to adapt trade and economic relations to the new political and economic conditions of the two countries. On the basis of the present agreement, more agreements could be signed--for example, on support and protection of investment or on prevention of double taxation.
