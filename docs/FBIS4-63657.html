BFN [Report over video by correspondent Vladimir Plotnikov; from the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] A meeting between an official delegation of the Crimean Supreme Soviet and Ukrainian parliamentarians took place in Simferopol today. It was held behind closed doors. Supreme Soviet Chairman Sergey Tsekov expressed hope that the two sides will reach mutual understanding and will be prepared to make sensible compromises. They will discuss Ukraine's recognition of the restored Crimean Constitution of 6 May 1992 and the appointment of ministers of internal affairs and justice.
