Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] The Hague (DPA) -- North Rhine-Westphalian Minister President Johannes Rau held a surprise meeting with Palestinian leader Yasir 'Arafat in The Hague on Friday [18 February]. As Rau told the press, his meeting with the PLO head was arranged at short notice. Rau added that the Middle East peace process was the most important issue. Rau said that they also discussed how Germany, and North Rhine-Westphalia, in particular, could help the Palestinians establish and extend administrative structures in the Gaza Strip, on the West Bank, and in Jericho.
