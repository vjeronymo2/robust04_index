920117 FT 17 JAN 92 / Mohamed Boudiaf, Algeria's new head of state, returns after 28 years' exile in Morocco Mohamed Boudiaf, Algeria's new head of state, returned to Algiers yesterday to a hero's welcome after 28 years' exile in Morocco. Boudiaf, 72, a veteran of Algeria's independence war, warned Islamic fundamentalists not to use religion to pursue their own interests. He will head the five-man High Council of State set up following the resignation of President Chadli Bendjedid on Saturday. Story, Page 4
