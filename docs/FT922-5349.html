920601 FT 01 JUN 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 24m orders awarded to Hall & Tawse HALL & TAWSE, the construction division of Raine Industries, has been awarded Pounds 24m worth of contracts. Nottingham-based Hall & Tawse Eastern has won a Pounds 2.7m contract to construct a two-storey science and technology building at Derbyshire College of Higher Education - plus a Pounds 1.6m contract to build a research centre in Loughborough for British Gas. Hall & Tawse Scotland has secured two contracts totalling Pounds 3.1m to fit-out Marks & Spencer stores in Paisley and Inverness. A 69-bed hospital is being constructed in Reading by Hall & Tawse Southern for Independent British Hospitals at a cost of Pounds 2.7m. The Stourbridge-based Hall & Tawse Western has been awarded refurbishment contracts for Pounds 600,000 with the West Midlands Police Authority, and for Pounds 400,000 with Birmingham City Council, to upgrade Cockshut Hill School in Yardley. Hall & Tawse Partnership, the social housing specialist, has won a Pounds 1m contract from Redland Housing Association, to design and build 25 dwellings in the Avon village of Camerton.
