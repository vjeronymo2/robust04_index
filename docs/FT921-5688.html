920303 FT 03 MAR 92 / UK Company News: National Counties Building Society National Counties Building Society made a reduced pre-tax profit of Pounds 8.08m for 1991. The figure was down from Pounds 8.77m in the previous year after mortgage loan loss provisions were increased from Pounds 145,000 to Pounds 878,000. Total assets rose from Pounds 305.8m to Pounds 339.8m. The society had a cost:income ratio of 17.2 per cent, up from 16.3 per cent. Reserves were 20.3 per cent of its share and deposit liabilities, among the highest for the industry.
