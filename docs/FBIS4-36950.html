BFN [Text] Ladies and gentlemen: A short while ago, we learned that Lieutenant General Dr. Samir Farraj, the private physician of His Majesty King Husayn, has stated that his majesty the king left the Mayo Clinic medical center in Rochester in the United States this morning after undergoing extensive medical tests. The medical tests have shown that his majesty the king is enjoying good health, and that there are no traces of the disease which prompted surgery for his majesty the king two years ago. Consequently, such tests will be conducted once per year, instead of once every six months, Dr. Farraj added.
