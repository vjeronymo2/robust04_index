940624 FT 24 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Tops Estates Tops Estates, the shop and office property company, plans to raise about Pounds 19.4m net via the placing of a further tranche of loan stock in order to help refinance the recent Pounds 61m purchase of the Bond Street centre in Leeds. Paribas has conditionally placed the tranche of Pounds 16m nominal 7.5 per cent convertible unsecured loan stock 2020 (with warrants) at 125p per Pounds 1 nominal of stock. But Pounds 15.8m of the stock will be made available to holders of ordinary shares and convertible unsecured loan stock.
