March 2, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

DR. NEIL SOLOMON: FAT IN FISH

Question: Is there much difference in the amount of calories and fat to be found in different kinds of fish? My family enjoys red snapper and swordfish, for example, and I could prepare either one.

Would the calories and fat make any difference?

Answer: A 3-ounce broiled serving of red snapper provides 104 calories and 1.2 grams of fat. A similar portion of swordfish provides 128 calories and 4.3 grams of fat.
