BFN [ITAR-TASS report under the "Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow" rubric: "Relying on CIS Mechanisms"] [Text] The achievement of economic independence by Uzbekistan "will require time," and in the meantime the country, relying on CIS mechanisms, must develop economic integration and also attract foreign aid, Republic President Islam Karimov stated in an interview in Tashkent with a group of Japanese journalists on the eve of his first visit to Tokyo, beginning 16 May. He noted that the idea of setting up a "Eurasian community" in the place of the CIS, proposed by Kazakhstani President Nursultan Nazarbayev, "is only a new slogan, deliberately put forward with the aim of strengthening this leader's personal authority."
