930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Ex-Lands Ex-Lands, which is involved in a number of projects in Europe for the construction and development of golf courses in conjunction with Mr Mark McCormack's International Management Group, recorded a pre-tax profit of Pounds 822,000 for the six months to December 31, against Pounds 33,000. Mr David Marshall, chairman, said that adjusted net assets, after incorporating the unrealised valuation surpluses on projects at June 30 1992, amounted to about Pounds 31m or 45p per share. Earnings were 1.19p (0.05p) per share.
