930802 FT 02 AUG 93 / Construction Contracts: Defence work LAING CIVIL ENGINEERING has been awarded a contract (worth about Pounds 7.5m) for the new Ministry of Defence development at Stoke Gifford near Bristol. The contract, awarded by the MoD, involves infrastructure works including site clearance, roads and car parks plus the capping of an existing mine shaft. The contract is scheduled for completion in July 1994.
