930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / Parliament and Politics: IR announces tax offices changes ADMINISTRATIVE changes in the Inland Revenue which will affect some offices dealing with financial services companies were announced yesterday. Mr Stephen Dorrell, financial secretary to the Treasury, told the Commons that from April 1 next year the tax offices in London responsible for some of the largest groups of companies, principally in the financial sector, would be brought together in a new executive office.
