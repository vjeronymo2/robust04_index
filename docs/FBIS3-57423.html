Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Yeltsin Suspends Planning of New Parliamentary Center in Moscow"] [Text] INTERFAX has learned from informed sources in the Kremlin that Russian President Boris Yeltsin has issued a directive suspending the elaboration of plans for the construction of a new parliamentary center in Moscow. Some $500 million had been allocated for its construction. Former First Vice Premier Yegor Gaydar, leader of the "Russia's Choice" bloc, had objected strongly to the plan to construct the new parliamentary center and the allocation of $500 million, describing the decision as one reason why he refused to join the reorganized government.
