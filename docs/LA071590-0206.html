July 15, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

SPECIAL SECTION TO APPEAR TUESDAY

A special section on the Richard M. Nixon Library and Birthplace, which opens this week in Yorba Linda, will be included in copies of Tuesday's Times Orange County Edition. The section examines the long struggle to build the library, compares it to other presidential libraries and offers an architectural critique. It also contains a two-page diagram of the library interior and birthplace and assesses whether yet another "New Nixon" has emerged since his days in the White House.
