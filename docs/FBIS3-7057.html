Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 24 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, sent thanks to the Soryong No. 2 fish shop and the residents in Soryong-tong No. 2, Taedonggang district, Pyongyang municipality, who had fully displayed the beautiful tradition of unity between the army and people. The shop employees and residents have distinguished themselves in aiding People's Army soldiers for years. Comrade Kim Chong-il also sent thanks to the workers and technicians of the Kumsong general tractor plant and the Sungni general motor works who had set examples in the production of tractors and lorries and, to different units including the Central Committee of the League of Socialist Working Youth of Korea and the Tokchon City Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea which had contributed to the production of tractors and lorries.
