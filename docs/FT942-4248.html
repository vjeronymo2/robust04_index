940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Thomas French down at Pounds 0.55m Thomas French & Sons, the home decorative accessories group, reported pre-tax profits down from Pounds 725,000 to Pounds 551,000 in the half year to April 2. Turnover was Pounds 256,000 ahead at Pounds 7.78m. There was an exceptional charge this time of Pounds 185,000 relating to unrecoverable deferred consideration after the purchaser of Kaz Krafts, sold in January last year, went into receivership. The interim dividend is unchanged at 1.45p from earnings per share of 4.12p (3.99p).
