BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 25 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il, chairman of the National Defence Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army [KPA], today visited the Unit 564 of the Korean People's Army and congratulated officers and men there on the occasion of the 62nd anniversary of the founding of the heroic Korean People's Army. The officers and men of the unit warmly welcomed President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il, raising thunderous cheers of "hurrah !". President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il were presented with bunches of flowers carrying boundless reverence of the entire KPA officers and men. President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il were greeted on the spot by O Chin-u, first vice-chairman of the DPRK National Defence Commission and Minister of People's Armed Forces; Choe Kwang, vice-chairman of the DPRK National Defence Commission and Chief of General Staff of the Korean People's Army; Yi Ul-sol, Kim Pong-yul and Kim Kwang-chin, members of the DPRK National Defence Commission and vice marshals of the Korean People's Army; Vice Marshals of the KPA Choe In-tok, Paek Hak-nim and Kim Ik-hyon; generals of the KPA Yi Ha-il, Kim Tu-nam, Yi Pong-won and Kim Myong- kuk; and other generals of the KPA and commanding officers of the Army unit. President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il warmly congratulated the KPA officers and men on the 62nd founding anniversary of the KPA and met soldiers to acquaint themselves with their life in the Army. They noted with deep satisfaction that the People's Army, forming an integrated whole with the party and the leader, was successfully discharging its honorable duty and mission as a reliable defender of the revolutionary cause of chuche, the cause of socialism, and encouraged the soldiers of the Army unit who were firmly preparing themselves as a-match-for-a-hundred combatants. President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il posed for a souvenir picture with the officers and men of the unit.
