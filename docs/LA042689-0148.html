April 26, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

ANNE GAINS STATUS WITH PAPARAZZI

Britain's Princess Anne was mobbed by photographers and reporters as she arrived today for an Olympic sports meeting.

Princess Anne, president of the International Equestrian Federation, normally does not attract media attention when attending sports meetings. But her appearance here came shortly after personal letters written to her by a Buckingham Palace staff member disappeared from her palace office and wound up in a British tabloid.

The princess chatted with officials from other federations as she passed by swarming photographers and reporters but made no public comment.
