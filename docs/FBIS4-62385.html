BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Leonid Vinogradov] [Text] Vladivostok, 15 May -- Admiral Georgiy Gurinov, commander of the Pacific Fleet, has issued an order that troops should open fire to hit anyone looting in the settlement of Novonezhino and neighboring villages from which all the civilian population were evacuated today after the explosion at the Fleet's aviation munitions dumps. There were no victims resulting from the explosion. The situation in the emergency zone is under control. All the military installations and housing have been taken under the protection of the military and militia. During the night the Kray Commission for Emergency Situations will provide the affected population with everything they need. Protected by the armor of two tanks, military personnel carried out a reconnaissance of the fire site. Yevgeniy Nazdratenko, head of the administration of Maritime Kray, Vice Admiral Igor Khmelnov, first deputy commander of the Pacific Fleet, Colonel Vladimir Ipatov, head of the Internal Affairs Directorate, and General Viktor Kondratov, head of the Directorate of the Federal Counterintelligence Service for Maritime Kray, are now at the scene of the accident. The Pacific Fleet press center confirmed to ITAR-TASS that there are no nuclear or chemical weapons in the munitions dumps.
