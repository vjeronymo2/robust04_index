Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, March 1 (AZERTAC)--Vladimir Kazimirov, the Russian presidents personal representative charged to negotiate on peaceful resolution of the Karabakh conflict, arrived to Baku last night. According to the confidential sources, President Aliyev and the Russian diplomat had one-hour in camera talk. Today Vladimir Kazimirov also met with Azerbaijani Minister of Defence Mamedrafi Mamedov. There is no information about subject of the talks. Apparently the matters of realization of a protocol, which had been recently signed in Moscow, were discussed during the talks. The protocol provides cessation of hostilities in the zone of the conflict.
