920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Continental Assets revenue ahead Continental Assets Trust reported a basic net asset value of 170.68p per share as at June 30, down from 177.59p a year earlier. The fully diluted value declined to 159.14p (164.92p). Net revenue amounted to Pounds 289,000 (Pounds 214,000) for the six months to end-June.
