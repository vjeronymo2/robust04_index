Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpt] A bomb exploded at the Tuzla train station in Istanbul this morning, killing four and injuring 40 Tuzla Infantry School non-commissioned officer cadets. According to a TRT correspondent, Tuzla Infantry School cadets arrived en masse at the Tuzla train station to leave on their weekend vacation. The students, who were waiting for the train to arrive from Gebze, sustained various injuries when one or two powerful bombs placed in a garbage bin exploded. Forty-four persons, most of whom are seriously injured, were taken to various hospitals. Four of the injured died in hospital. They have not been identified yet. [passage omitted]
