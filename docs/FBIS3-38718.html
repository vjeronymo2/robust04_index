Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Tansu Ciller today received Iranian Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati, who is in Istanbul for the tripartite meeting of the foreign ministers of Turkey, Iran, and Syria. Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin was also present. In a statement after the meeting, Cetin said Ciller expressed her deep regret over the casualties caused in certain villages close to the Iranian border during the Zhallah operation. Ciller reportedly said Turkey will do whatever is necessary. Cetin said the prime minister stressed that this incident will not affect cooperation and relations between the two countries.
