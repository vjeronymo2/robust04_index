940805 FT 05 AUG 94 / International Company News: BPA rejects BCP stake offer The executive board of Banco Portugues do Atlantico, Portugal's second largest bank, has formally rejected a public offer by Banco Comercial Portugues, a smaller competitor, for a controlling stake of BPA. In a letter to Portugal's stock exchange commission (CMVM), the board said the bid was not in the best interests of BPA shareholders. BCP is offering Es132bn for 40 per cent of BPA.
