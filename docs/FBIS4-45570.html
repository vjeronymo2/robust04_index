BFN [Text] Fukuoka, June 19 KYODO -- Police and maritime safety officials on Sunday [19 June] confiscated some 152 kilogram of illegal stimulants, worth about 28 billion yen on the market, from a fishing boat at a port on Amami Oshima Island, Kagoshima Prefecture. Police arrested Takamasa Kurimoto, 41, a local yakuza syndicate leader, and four others on the vessel for allegedly violating a drug control law. Police suspect the involvement of a large-scale smuggling organization.
