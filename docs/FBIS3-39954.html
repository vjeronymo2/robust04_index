Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin arrived in Lisbon 30 minutes earlier than expected. He was welcomed at the airport by Portuguese Foreign Minister Durao Barroso. Rabin did not say much on arrival, but he did say that any statement on the withdrawal of Israeli soldiers from the Gaza Strip and Jericho can only be made after the meetings with the Portuguese authorities. Raquel Abecassis reports. [Abecassis] The Israeli prime minister said he did not want to speak to journalists, but ended up agreeing to a news conference later after, his meetings with the Portuguese prime minister and foreign minister. [Begin Rabin recording, in English] First, I am in Portugal as a guest of the prime minister. I am just now beginning my visit. I believe it will be better to discuss questions relating to the peace negotiations first with the prime minister and government officials; only then will I make a public statement. Thank you. [end recording] [passage omitted]
