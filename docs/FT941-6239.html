940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / International Company News: Bundaberg Sugar raises offer for crushing mills Bundaberg Sugar, the Australian sugar producer, yesterday raised its ADollars 87m (USDollars 62m) bid for two crushing mills in northern Queensland. Bundaberg is increasing its offer for the Tully Sugar mill from ADollars 1,600 a share to ADollars 2,160 a share. Its offer for South Johnstone goes up from ADollars 9.40 to ADollars 12.25, increasing the total value of the bids by around ADollars 26m, to approximately ADollars 113m. The mills said that the offers would be referred to their financial advisers.
