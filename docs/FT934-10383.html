931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Caverdale buys motor dealership Caverdale Group, the motor and industrial consumables group, is buying JJ Leadley, a motor dealership group based in the Midlands, for a maximum Pounds 1.65m. Consideration is satisfied as to Pounds 1.5m by the issue of 10m new Caverdale ordinary at 15p each, and a maximum Pounds 150,000 in cash.
