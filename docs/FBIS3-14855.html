Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow March 1 TASS -- Experts from Russia's Foreign Intelligence Service regard the publication of information about Aldrich Ames' alleged involvement in the transfer of technology to Russia for the development of a supersecret weapon as a drive launched by U.S. secret services with a view to attracting the public attention to the "Ames case". The reason for this and other actions, the Russian experts believe, is the more understandable as, according to data available with them, U.S. secret services so far have no real evidence of Mr. Ames' involvement in spying for Russia.
