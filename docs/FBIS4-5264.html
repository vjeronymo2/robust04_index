BFN [Text] According to AFP, the U.S. State Department on 6 April issued a statement asserting that U.S. teams did not find any signs of American servicemen being alive in the 10 detention camps that the U.S. side had inspected over the past six weeks. The statement rejected the allegations made by the three American citizens who left Vietnam on 3 April that U.S. inspectors were not allowed to have access to Vietnamese prisons. The U.S. State Department statement asserted that members of the Hanoi-based Task Force for U.S. Servicemen Missing in Action had asked for permission to visit ten detention camps in six weeks and had been allowed to go to all of those camps.
