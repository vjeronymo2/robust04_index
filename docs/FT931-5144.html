930308 FT 08 MAR 93 / Clean up Australia Day The leader of Australia's conservative Liberal/National party coalition, John Hewson, dumps rubbish he collected yesterday from a Sydney beach as part of Clean Up Australia Day. Mr Hewson received a blow to his hopes in next Saturday's federal elections when he lost a televised debate with Labor's Paul Keating, Kevin Brown reports from Sydney. Mr Keating won 54 per cent of votes cast by a studio audience compared to Mr Hewson's 33 per cent.
