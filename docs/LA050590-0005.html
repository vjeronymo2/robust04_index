May 5, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MEDIA TREATMENT OF THE 'HILARY IN HIDING' CASE

I want to thank Rosenberg for restoring some of my faith in objective reporting through his comments about the Morgan-Foretich child custody case.

He points out how the media can influence, either knowingly or unknowingly, such matters.

The media seem to have taken up Morgan's side. Thanks to Rosenberg for his voice. Only time will tell in this complicated case.

DAVID SUTTON, Los Angeles
