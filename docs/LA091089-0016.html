September 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: QUIBBLES & BITS

. . . Comic books continue their adult ways: The upcoming first issue of "Ripper" -- about a young man obsessed with vengeance, following the brutal rape and murder of his fiancee -- is so violent that it will be sealed in a clear plastic wrapper. To ship in November from Aircel publishing, it will carry a "mature readers" advisory.
