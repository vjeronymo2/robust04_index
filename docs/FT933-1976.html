930921 FT 21 SEP 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Landed a good demand prevailed, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Coloury medium and selected best liquoring North Indians were firm but plainer sorts eased, particularly pd,S. Medium East Africans were a strong feature while brighter types opened irregularly lower but closed about steady. Dust grades were, however, easier throughout. Ceylons sold readily at last rates. Offshore improved competition, tending dearer. Quotations: quality 200p/kg nom, good medium 125p/kg, medium 113p/kg, low medium 93p/kg and nom the highest price realised this week was 253p/kg for an assam pf.
