930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / Growth seen in industry spending CONFIDENCE about growth prospects in manufacturing has edged up in the past month, a survey published today by the Chartered Institute of Purchasing and Supply has found. The survey of industry purchasing managers spending more than Pounds 250bn a year says companies are increasing their buying of goods and services in anticipation of a steady recovery. The purchasing managers' index, which shows sentiment in industry about an upturn, was calculated at 53.7 last month, up from 53.0 in May, signalling better growth prospects.
