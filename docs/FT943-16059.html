940706 FT 06 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Neepsend up to Pounds 761,000 Neepsend, the light engineer, reported pre-tax profits for the year to March 31 of Pounds 761,000, against losses of Pounds 99,000. Turnover increased from Pounds 16.7m to Pounds 18.6m. The company said the improvement had continued into the present year. The shares rose 3p to 41p. The result was after lower interest charges of Pounds 283,000 (Pounds 411,000) and an exceptional cost of Pounds 253,000 (Pounds 275,000) relating to the sale of land on which the company will be entitled to extra payment of Pounds 1m or Pounds 2m depending on planning permission. Neepsend is also in talks on the disposal of its Canadian subsidiary which should result in proceeds of Pounds 375,000. Earnings per share were 2.12p (0.32p losses) and a final dividend of 0.75p is recommended for a total of 1.25p (0.75p).
