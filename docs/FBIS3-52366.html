Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] General Krishna Thaba, commander of the UNIKOM [United Nations Iraq-Kuwait Observer Mission] forces, said that UNIKOM members have authority to use force in order to prevent any violations or sabotage operations across the border area. In a statement to Kuwaiti television, Gen. Thaba said that his forces will set up observation points on the border and will continuously monitor the demilitarized zone. He said they will also carry out random inspections of all cars and vehicles in the area in order to prevent any attempts at sabotage or to stir up any problems. The UNIKOM commander added that a security trench will prevent any individual attempts on the part of the Iraqi regime to breach these borders.
