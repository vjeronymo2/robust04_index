930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Charter Over the six months ended May 31 1993 the net asset value per share of the Kleinwort Charter Investment Trust rose from 190.7p to 204.9p. At May 31 1992 it stood at 188p. On June 3 the trust raised some Pounds 15m through the issue of 9 1/4 per cent debenture stock 2023. Using a pooled vehicle, Fintrust Debenture, a total of Pounds 85m was raised in conjunction with three other Kleinwort Benson managed investment trusts. Revenue for the six months to May 31 came to Pounds 3.43m (Pounds 3.8m) and earnings per share worked through at 2.2p (2.42p). The interim dividend is unchanged at 1.25p.
