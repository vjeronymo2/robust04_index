Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Colonel Mustafa al-Kharrubi, member of the Revolution Command Council and inspector general in the Libyan Arab Jamahiriyah, left Sanaa today following a three-day visit to our country. During his visit he conveyed messages from brother Libyan leader Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi to both President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and Vice President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. The messages deal with issues of joint interest to both fraternal countries and with current developments in our country. He was seen off by Deputy Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Wahhab al-Anisi, and a number of officials. An Arab League delegation, led by Staff Major General Muhammad Sa'id Bayraqdar, assistant secretary general and head of the Arab League's Department for Military Affairs, also left Sanaa today following a visit of several days to our country. In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', the league military official said that he met with officials in our country and discussed with them ways to end the military repercussions that have recently prevailed in the country. He described his visit to our country as positive and successful, and that he had formed a good impression. He also wished Yemen and the Yemeni people progress and prosperity. He was seen off by brother Yahya al-Shami, deputy head of the Arab Homeland Department at the Yemeni Foreign Ministry, and a representative of the Presidential Council's Protocol Department.
