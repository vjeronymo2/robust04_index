Business Development Center Applications: Las Vegas, NV; Notice AGENCY: Minority Business Development Agency. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: The Minority Business Development Agency (MBDA) is revising the closing date and the performance period for the Las Vegas MBDC. The new closing date will be July 29, 1994. The revised performance period will be from November 1, 1994 thru October 31, 1995. The original Announcement was published in the May 11, 1994, issue of the Federal Register . The new project I.D. number will be 09&hyph;10&hyph;95004&hyph;01. A pre-bid conference will be held in Las Vegas, Nevada on July 8, 1994, at 1:30 P.M. at the following address: City Hall, 400 East Stewart, General Services Conference Room, 1st floor, Las Vegas, Nevada 89101. The mailing address for submission is: San Francisco Regional Office, Minority Business Development Agency, U.S. Department of Commerce, 221 Main Street, San Francisco, California 94105.
