920218 FT 18 FEB 92 / Nottingham renewal plan is launched NOTTINGHAM City Council was yesterday given formal approval for a Pounds 37.5m urban renewal City Challenge plan - the first such programme to go ahead. The five-year project is to regenerate two depressed areas of the city, Sneinton and St Ann's. Public and private investment totalling Pounds 160m is expected in the five years. Mr Michael Heseltine, the environment secretary, said at the launch of the programme yesterday: 'Nottingham's plan is a genuine partnership with local businesses, local people and all the local agencies that have a role in regeneration.' Mr Heseltine will today announce the second phase of City Challenge - the government's urban regeneration initiative under which councils bid with projects for funding.
