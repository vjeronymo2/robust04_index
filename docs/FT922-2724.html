920616 FT 16 JUN 92 / World Trade News: Nigeria pact for US gas sales Nigeria signed an agreement yesterday to sell Distrigas of the US 700m cu metres of liquefied natural gas (LNG) a year from 1997, Reuter reports from Lagos. It was the fourth and final sales agreement for Nigeria's major LNG project, costing an estimated Dollars 4bn (Pounds 2.1bn) in the oil-rich Niger Delta region of Rivers State.
