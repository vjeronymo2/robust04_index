Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] With the signing of a friendship accord with Havana City on 21 February, Cuba became the first Latin America country to conclude a cooperation agreement with Kiev. Leonid Kosakivsky, mayor of Kiev and envoy of the Ukrainian president, expressed his satisfaction with the accord, which will promote contacts between economic sectors, as well as the exchange of information and experiences in science, urban planning, construction, public health, the environment, culture, and professional education. At the ceremony last night at the former home of General Maximo Gomez in La Quinta de los Molinos, Havana, Pedro Chavez, president of the People's Administration Council, presented the distinguished guest award to Kosakivsky.
