Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Belgrade, 9 Mar (TANJUG) -- The general staff of the Serb army of Krajina [SVK] stated today that SVK units had not fired at a Spanish plane above the region of Plaski [southern Croatia] yesterday, ISKRA [Croatian Serb news agency] has reported. The general staff issued the statement in reaction to various reports and by the Croatian media in particular, that it was the SVK antiaircraft units that had fired at the Spanish plane. According to ISKRA, the statement goes on to note that the SVK had no prior notice of the Spanish flight yesterday, and that in any case the plane was not spotted above the republic of Serb Krajina.
