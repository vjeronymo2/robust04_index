BFN [Text] President Liamine Zeroual, president of the Republic of Algeria, received the brother secretary of the Libyan Arab Brotherhood Bureau in Algeria and charged him with conveying his brotherly greetings to the brother leader of the revolution [Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi]. President Liamine Zeroual once more renewed Algeria's siding and solidarity with the Great Jamahiriyah in confronting the unjust measures. During his reception of the brother secretary of the Libyan Arab Brotherhood Bureau in Algeria at the end of his tour of duty, the Algerian president said he was proud of the depth of the fraternal relations which connected the sons of the one Arab people in Algeria and those of the Great Jamahiriyah, praising the historic stance of the Libyan Arab people with the fraternal Algerian people at the time of liberation revolution in Algeria.
