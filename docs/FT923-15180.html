920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Unilever makes Argentine purchase Unilever, the Anglo-Dutch consumer products company, has acquired Establecimientos Fabriles Guereno, Argentina's largest laundry detergents producer, for an undisclosed sum. Guereno, which also makes soap, has annual sales of about Dollars 90m (Pounds 49m) and 1,500 employees.
