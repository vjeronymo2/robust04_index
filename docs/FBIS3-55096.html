Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] In accordance with an instruction from the Georgian head of state, law enforcement officers of the republic today left for Zugdidi rayon which, according to preliminary information, was the place of Zviad Gamsakhurdia's death. The group of prosecutors and experts is led by Deputy Prosecutor General Anzor Baluashvili. The group will be joined on the spot by representatives of the interior and security ministries and by the state minister for western Georgia. The population of Zugdidi rayon where, according to one report, the former president's death took place, has no accurate information in this respect.
