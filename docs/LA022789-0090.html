February 27, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

STARLITES RALLY TO DEFEAT NEW YORK

Margaret Grant had 18 kills as the Los Angles Starlights rallied to defeat the New York Liberties, 15-8, 2-15, 12-15, 15-13, 15-10, Sunday night in a Major League Volleyball match at UC Irvine's Bren Center.

Anna Vorwerk had 12 kills and Linda Chisholm 11 for the Starlites (2-1). Karolyn Kirby had 22 kills for the Liberties (1-2).
