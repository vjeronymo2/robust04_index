November 27, 1990, Tuesday, Southland Edition

PROCTER & GAMBLE SELLS ITS CALIENTE CHILI UNIT

Procter & Gamble Co. has sold its Caliente Chili Inc. subsidiary in Austin, Tex., to William B. Reily & Co. for an undisclosed price.

Procter & Gamble acquired Caliente Chili as part of its merger with Noxell Corp. in November, 1989.

Caliente's product line includes Wick Fowler's 2-Alarm and Family Style Chili Kits.
