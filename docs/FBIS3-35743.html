Language: Kyrgyz Article Type:BFN [Text] Before leaving for Switzerland yesterday, where an international economic forum will take place, President Nazarbayev telephoned President Askar Akayev. Nursultan Nazarbayev expressed support for the policy pursued by Askar Akayev and wished him every success in the upcoming referendum. Answering the questions of Kazakh and foreign journalists on the referendum in Kyrgyzstan, Nazarbayev noted that the democratic reform which is being implemented in Kyrgyzstan has gained recognition among not only Asian states, but also of the whole world. In conclusion, he said that he had supported and supports Akayev's policy, and he is sure that the nation of Kyrgyzstan will support Akayev at the referendum.
