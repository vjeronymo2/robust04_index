Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Damascus, 9 Jan (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa held consultations in Damascus this afternoon on the sidelines of the Damascus Declaration foreign ministers' conference. Musa met with Saudi Minister of Foreign Affairs Sa'ud al-Faysal and discussed ways to develop the Damascus Declaration in light of the conditions and challenges facing the Arab nation. They also discussed the Arab and international situations and the developments in the peace negotiations. Musa is scheduled to meet with Syrian Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' before the conference of the Damascus Declaration foreign ministers begins at 1800 today.
