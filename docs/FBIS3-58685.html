Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] The Cypriot Government has welcomed Belgian Foreign Minister Willy Claes' statement, which says that if there is no clear indication of a Cyprus solution by January 1995, the negotiations for Cyprus' accession to the European Union [EU] will begin immediately. Speaking to the Cyprus Broadcasting Corporation, Foreign Minister Alekos Mikhailidhis described this position as very positive. Claes made his remarks during a meeting of the Council of Europe's Political Committee, which he chairs. Claes also made it clear that the EU observer to the Cyprus issue negotiations will report directly to EU Commission President Jacques Delors.
