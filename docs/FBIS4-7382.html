BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 29 (KCNA) -- A symposium of men of the press was held here Thursday to mark the 20th anniversary of the publication of the May 7, 1974, work of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. In the work Comrade Kim Chong-il defined our party's media as a powerful ideological weapon conducive to modelling the whole of society on the chuche idea and gave a comprehensive exposition of its basic mission and tasks and ways for their fulfilment. He gave perfect answers to all the questions arising in the building of the media including those of adopting the principle of chuche as the keystone, conducting one's activities strictly under the guidance of the party Central Committee and "selecting the right seed" and conducting a speed campaign. The speakers said that the work of Comrade Kim Chong-il is an immortal grand program which clearly indicates the features of the socialist press, the function and role which it should play in compliance with the requirements of modelling the whole of society on the chuche idea, and shows the road of serving for the accomplishment of the revolutionary cause of the leader which is succeeded through generations, being the first in history to put the building of the socialist media on the basis of the revolutionary outlook on the leader. It is immortal feat of our party that our media is making true contributions to the accomplishment of the cause of socialism and registering great achievements in revolutionizing newspapers, news services and publications to meet the requirements of the developing revolution in a new, higher stage, the speakers stressed.
