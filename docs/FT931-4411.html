930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Motion attacks lease law SEVEN Labour MPs yesterday tabled an early day motion calling for the repeal of a 'draconian' law under which anyone who signs a lease on commercial premises can be forced to pay the rent owed by a subsequent tenant who defaults. The motion 'noted with dismay' the failure to implement the 1988 Law Commission report in lifting 'the burden of medieval privity law'.
