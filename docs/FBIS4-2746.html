BFN [Text] Seoul, April 12 (XINHUA) -- South Korean National Assembly Speaker Yi Man-sop today expressed appreciation for China's achievements made since the implementation of the open-door policy. Yi made the statement when meeting with Wu Xueqian, vice-chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (PPCC). Yi said China's success is attributable not only to the resolute determination of the Chinese Government to carry out the open-door policy but also to the support of the Chinese people. Yi visited China in January. Both sides said they are satisfied with the development of bilateral relations since the diplomatic relations were established in August 1992. They hold that fuller cooperation between the two sides will be conducive to mutual prosperity. South Korea's ruling Democratic Liberal Party (DLP) Leader Kim Chong-pil will host a dinner in honor of Wu later today. Wu arrived here Sunday for an eight-day goodwill visit at the invitation of Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu.
