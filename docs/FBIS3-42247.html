Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, February 3, RIA -- An explosion took place today at 0400 in the apartment of the deputy minister of defence of the Republic of Georgia for the military-industrial complex, Nikolay Kekelidze. According to preliminary information, both Kekelidze and one more person died. An investigation has been started. The information was received by an RIA correspondent in the press centre of the Georgian Defence Ministry. There has followed no comment from the press centre.
