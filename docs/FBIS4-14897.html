BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Ivanov] [Text] Moscow, 5 Apr -- Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin's conference with members of the government, which was to have been held in the Kremlin today, has been "postponed for a few days by mutual agreement." Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin told an ITAR-TASS correspondent this today. Speaking of the reasons for rescheduling the conference, at which important decisions on Russia's socio-economic development in 1994 were to have been made, Viktor Chernomyrdin said that in view of the importance of the matter, a certain amount of further revision had to be done.
