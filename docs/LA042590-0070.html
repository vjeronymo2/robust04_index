April 25, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

Correction Appended

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Wynn's International Inc.: The Fullerton-based supplier of automotive air conditioners, O-rings, seals, molded rubber products, petrochemical specialties products and builders' hardware reported a 44% decline in net income for the first quarter, to $1.8 million from $3.2 million for the like period a year earlier. The 1990 first-quarter results included a one-time net gain of $1.4 million from the sale of a joint-venture interest. Sales dipped slightly, to $72.5 million from $73.2 million.
