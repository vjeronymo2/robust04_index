940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Vistec Vistec Group, the USM-quoted supplier of computer systems, has acquired Data Logic Communication Services, a computer network design, installation and maintenance company, for Pounds 2.5m cash. DLCS incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.06m before exceptional costs of Pounds 970,000 on turnover of Pounds 6.8m in the year to end-December 1993. However, a substantial restructuring in the latter part of the year brought about a return to profitability.
