Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sofia, March 2 (BTA) -- The Defence Ministry yesterday and today met with officers of the First, Second and Third Armies, BTA learnt from the Defence Ministry Press Office. They discussed a broad range of topics springing from the need to press on with reform in the Armed Forces. At a special meeting the Ministry of Defence and the General Staff analyzed the issues raised by military officers in the course of the meetings, especially pay prospects, housing policy, improvement of living conditions and the need to accelerate the structural reform in the Armed Forces. The Defence Minister assigned subordinates to suggest measures for the settlement of the most acute problems, despite money limitations. The officials discussed violations of military regulations and discipline by servicemen.
