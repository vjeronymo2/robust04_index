940719 FT 19 JUL 94 / BR's 80% hope in strike-hit region British Rail is expecting to run about 80 per cent of train services on the InterCity Great Western region during tomorrow's sixth 24-hour stoppage by signalmen. It hopes to run more than 20 per cent of services on the whole network. Mr John MacGregor, transport secretary, told the Commons yesterday that he hoped the two sides could resume talks. He said that not all the issues had been thoroughly discussed and negotiated.
