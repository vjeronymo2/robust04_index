August 18, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

A STRAIT PROTEST

I demand that The Times find someone to review country music who knows what he/she is doing.

I attended the George Strait concert and I'm very disappointed with Robert Hilburn's Aug. 14 potshots at a singer just named country entertainer of the year!

I am not a barefoot country bumpkin. I am a professional in Beverly Hills who grew up in L.A. There are a lot of us who appreciate the values, morals and smooth presentation of country music.

CAROL SANCHEZ

Beverly Hills
