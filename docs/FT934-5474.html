931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / Print industrial tribunal opens An industrial tribunal hearing opened in Bristol yesterday into claims of unfair dismissal by workers who were sacked by the printing company JW Arrowsmith. Their union, the GPMU, which is no longer recognised by the company, has maintained pickets at the plant since 120 people were dismissed in April during a pay dispute. The tribunal hearing is expected to last 10 days.
