940505 FT 05 MAY 94 / UK Company News: German Inv Trust matches benchmark The German Investment Trust had a net asset value, fully diluted, of 116.3p per share at March 31. The figure represented a rise of 22 per cent on the 95.2p standing at end-March 1993, virtually matching the improvement over the same period of the FT-A World Index for Germany in sterling terms. Available revenue for the year dropped to Pounds 22,000 (Pounds 165,000). A proposed single distribution of 0.08p (0.4p) is uncovered by earnings of 0.06p (0.42p) per share.
