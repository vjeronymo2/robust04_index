May 19, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

THE 'SATURDAY NIGHT LIVE' CAST PROTEST

It is time to take a stand against the eroding of values, and the entertainment industry is perhaps the worst offender. Clay has a right to say what he feels, but I disagree with TV executives who allow him to reach a mammoth audience.

Why is this country so eager to make itself look, in the eyes of the world, like bunch of foul-mouthed morons? Don't our politicians make us look bad enough?

STEPHEN A. GEARY, Los Angeles
