March 1, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

GREENHOUSE EFFECT

I have read many articles about the Greenhouse effect. It frightens me that despite constant warning by scientists, no concerted effort is being made by the nations of the world to prevent it.

When we gamble on the validity of the Greenhouse effect, it's like playing Russian roulette. The Greenhouse gun is aimed at our world. Is the barrel loaded or empty? If nothing is done, we'll soon find out.

BING LEE

Laguna Niguel
