Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 1 (KCNA) -- This year marks the 30th anniversary of the publication of the socialist rural theses in Korea. To honor this occasion the farm machine factories throughout the country are boosting production. The Kumsong General Tractor Works is waging a vigorous drive for increased production, having set itself the goal of producing 10,000 more tractors this year than last year. Now efforts are directed to keep the production of castings ahead of processing. Having completed several automatic moulding machines, it is now successfully promoting the work for manufacturing one more automatic moulding machine and installing another cupola furnace, which is aimed at automatizing the production of large castings. Increased production has been reported from tractor parts factories and trailing farm machine factories in Hamhung, Sariwon, Anju and other places of the country from the beginning of the year. In the first 20 days or more of the new year the Pyongyang and Haeju tractor parts factories raised the output of tractor accessories 20 and 30 pegcent respectively above the correscondingperiod last year. The Pyongsong Farm Machine Factory is topping its daily assignments 20 to 30 percent entering this year. The workers there have set themselves the goal of producing more than 1,000 rice-transplanting machines by early May. The workers of the Sungri General Motor Works have increased the speed of assembling 50 percent above that toward the end of last year in order to supply an increased number of lorries to the socialist countryside.
