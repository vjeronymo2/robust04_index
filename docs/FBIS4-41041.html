BFN [Cristian Nita dispatch from Chisinau] [Text] Today, in accordance with a decree by Mircea Snegur, the president of the Republic of Moldova, the Committee for Relations of the Republic of Moldova With Romania was set up, with the intention, quote, of ensuring efficient control over accords concluded by the Republic of Moldova and Romania in the field of economics, unquote. Mr. Ion Gutu, deputy prime minister of the Republic of Moldova, was appointed president of the committee.
