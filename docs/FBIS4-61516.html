BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS string correspondent Zurab Argun] [Text] Sukhumi 5 May TASS -- Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Boris Pastukhov and Deputy Defence Minister Georgiy Kondratyev met with Abkhazian parliament speaker Vladislav Ardzinba and Prime Minister Sokrat Dzhindzholia in the town of Novy Afon on Thursday [5 May]. They tackled various issues, including preserving peace in Abkhazia, exact stationing of peace-making forces on the line of the current stand-off between Abkhazian and Georgian troops (that is, along the Inguri River). The discussion took place behind closed doors. Pastukhov and Kondartyev left for Moscow on Thursday night.
