BFN [Excerpts] His Excellency the President of the Republic Maaouiya Ould Sid'Ahmed Taya, this evening at the presidential palace, received His Highness Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad al-Jabir, first deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs in the brotherly State of Kuwait. After the meeting, the minister made the following statement, to our colleague Mohamed Mokhtar Ould Yiahi, in which he said that he had delivered to his excellency the president of the republic a letter from his brother Shaykh Jabir al-Ahmad al-Sabah, emir of the State of Kuwait. [Begin al-Jabir recording] I was delighted at meeting his excellency the president and was honored to carry this letter, which I delivered to him from his his highness Shaykh Jabir. I am glad that we exchanged points of view regarding bilateral relations, as I said, and the developments of the situation in our region which is the Arab Gulf region. [passage omitted] [end recording] Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad al-Jabir arrived in the capital this evening from Algeria. [passage omitted] In a statement to our colleague Mohamed Mokhtar Ould Yiahi, the Kuwaiti minister expressed his happiness with this visit and said: [Begin al-Jabir recording] Being on the good land, the Arab Republic of Mauritania, I can only express my thanks and esteem for my brother Ahmed Salem Ould Lekhel [Mauritanian foreign affairs minister], for renewing the invitation to me to visit this country from which I have memorable souvenirs and where I have several friends whom I have not and do not want to forget. In fact his highness honored me by asking me to deliver a written letter from him to his brother the president. I will also discuss with the minister for foreign affairs and cooperation, brother Mohamed Salem, all the issues of interest to both the countries in all spheres, particularly bilateral relations and the developments in the Arab world. [passage omitted] [end recording]
