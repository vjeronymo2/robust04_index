Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova] [Text] Moscow March 17 TASS -- The Russian Parliament's upper house, the Federation Council, suggests that President Boris Yeltsin should inform the chamber within a week about the resignation of Aleksey Kazannik and present a new nominee for the post of Russia's prosecutor general, the Federation Council said in a resolution passed on Thursday after two-day debates over the issue of the prosecutor general.
