930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / Parliament and Politics: Brown attacks boardroom pay MR GORDON Brown, the shadow chancellor, called for 'new standards' in industry yesterday after compiling a survey showing boardroom executives taking pay rises of up to 212 per cent. 'Banks, building societies, insurance companies, and companies close to the Tory party are all responsible for pay awards that cannot be justified on economic grounds and in some cases where profits are falling,' he said.
