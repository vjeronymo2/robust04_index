Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 22 (KCNA) -- Talks were held here today between a military delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the visiting high-level delegation of the Defence Ministry of the Royal Government of Cambodia. Present there on the Korean side were Minister of People's Armed Forces O Chin-u and generals and officers of the Korean People's Army and on the opposite side were the members of the delegation headed by Co-Ministers of Defence Tia Banh and Tia Chambrat and councillor of the Cambodian embassy in Pyongyang. The talks proceeded in a friendly atmosphere.
