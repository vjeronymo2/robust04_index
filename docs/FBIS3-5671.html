Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 9 KYODO -- Japan will sponsor an international forum on security on March 15 and 16 in Tokyo to discuss cooperation on security in the Asia-Pacific region, the Foreign Ministry said Wednesday [9 March]. Security experts from 13 nations will attend the two-day session. Among them will be South Korea, China, Vietnam, Thailand, the United States and Russia, the ministry said. Participants will also discuss various problems in Northeast and Southeast Asia and Japan-U.S. relations, it said.
