920218 FT 18 FEB 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Cash flow in the workshop -In A Nutshell In May the Institute of Directors is to launch its first series of training workshops specifically aimed at the small business director. The IOD plans initially to offer one-day workshops in six different aspects of directing a small business. Market research has shown that help with cash-flow management is likely to prove the most popular module. The expected cost of the workshops is Pounds 100-Pounds 150.
