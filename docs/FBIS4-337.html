BFN [Text] The ANC-aligned trade union, Popcru [Police and Prison's Civil Rights Union], has threatened to arrest members of the South African Police Union, SAPU, if they proceed with a planned strike to back their demand for higher salaries. SAPU is a mainly white trade union with about 20,000 members. The threat was made by the chairman of Popcru, Mr. Gregory Rockman, a former police officer.
