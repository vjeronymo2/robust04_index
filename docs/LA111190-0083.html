November 11, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

NOMINATION FOR CRUELEST-TOWN CROWN

Move over Santa Ana. You no longer hold the crown for the most heartless city in the Southland.

You earned it fair and square when you initiated the official harassment of the homeless.

Not only was your intolerance condoned, but your people were apparently proud of it.

However, you must give the trophy to Santa Barbara. Not only is it bigger and richer, but it proved its heart is colder and harder than the diamonds worn by its fine ladies. Its new sleepless law gives it the championship as the city that most vigorously displays its inhumanity to man.

I hope its citizens will drop a note to their City Council telling them how proud they are to possess their new title.

ROBERT L. PORTER, Anaheim
