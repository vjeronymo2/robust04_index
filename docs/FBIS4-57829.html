BFN [Text] The Army General Staff has held a summit. High-ranking Armed Forces officers from units and garrisons of both Port-au- Prince and provincial towns gathered for a meeting at Army General Headquarters on 24 May. The meeting lasted several hours and focused on several current issues, said a source close to the military leadership. Approximately 30 unit commanders gathered around the Army High Command to analyze the nation's overall situation. The measures taken after this meeting are unknown. Nevertheless, it should be recalled that the Haitian Army has been on maximum alert since 20 May, a few hours before the total embargo ordered by the United Nations against Haiti went into effect.
