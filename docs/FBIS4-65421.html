BFN [Excerpt] United Nations, May 17 (A.A.) -- The UN Security Council will review the trade embargo imposed on Iraq since the Gulf War, here today. Turkish Ambassador to the United Nations Inal Batu met with Iraqi Foreign Minister Tariq 'Aziz [title and name as received]. The ANATOLIA Agency learned that during the talk which took place at the request of 'Aziz, Batu briefed him on the talks he had a week ago, with the British and French UN representatives. 'Aziz reiterated his government's stand on the issue. The two exchanged views on draining the oil remaining in the Kirkuk-Yumurtalik pipeline and flushing out the pipeline. [passage omitted]
