March 16, 1989, Thursday, Valley Edition

NO FAN OF HOUSE OF THE FUTURE

As a homeowner who has been trying to save 10% of water usage every year and finding it difficult to do it, I find it irritating to read (Around the Valley, March 1) that homes are being built with indoor swimming pools and waterfalls.

If you have the money, build waterfalls and swimming pools and more high-rises in the Valley, and I will be asked to save 10% again and have to ask for a glass of water at my restaurant.

STANLEY GARDNER

Reseda
