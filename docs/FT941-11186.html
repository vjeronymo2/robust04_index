940205 FT 05 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Further expansion for Malaya Group In its seventh acquisition in just over a year, Malaya Group, the motor retailer, has bought three businesses from the Silverstone Motor Group. The businesses comprise three franchises: Porsche, Citroen and Nissan, and operate from adjacent premises on one site in St Albans, Hertfordshire. The consideration, to be satisfied in cash, is the aggregate of Pounds 500,000 and the net asset value of the businesses as at December 31 1993, to be ascertained by completion accounts.
