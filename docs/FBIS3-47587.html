Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Japanese Reactionaries Warned Not To Act Rashly" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, January 14 (KCNA) -- The DPRK Government and people resolutely denounce the ever-more undisguised anti-DPRK, anti-socialist campaign of the Japanese reactionaries. The Korean people will sharply watch the campaign with high vigilance. We warn the Japanese reactionaries not to act rashly. This warning is served by today's issue of MINJU CHOSON, the organ of the DPRK Government. The author of the commentary titled "Don't act rashly" says: The Japanese reactionaries have defined the DPRK as "potential enemy no.1" and "most dangerous country" and declared that Japan would involve itself in an emergency on the Korean peninsula, should it arise. This is an open revelation of their hostile policy. They raised again the "nuclear issue" and "missile threat" from the beginning of the year in an attempt to justify their military power buildup and nuclear armament on that pretext and realise their ambition for overseas aggrandizement. The DPRK and the United States have recently agreed to solve the problems for a fundamental solution of the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula in a package deal at the third round of talks. So Japan is not requested to obtrusively make a fuss over the "nuclear issue" of the DPRK. Japan must not take issue with the peaceloving DPRK but make a sincere apology and compensation to the Korean people for its past crimes and refrain from further acts obstructive to peace and peaceful reunification of Korea.
