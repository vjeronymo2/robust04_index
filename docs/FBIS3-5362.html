Language: English Article Type:BFN ["`Secret of North Korea'" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, March 7 (KCNA) -- The Iranian paper KAYHAN INTERNATIONAL on February 14 pointed to the secret to the powerful existence of North Korea under the complicated international political situation. The secret is in the great politics of President Kim Il-song, the paper said, and went on: He is the great politician who won victory in the struggle against Japan and the United States and the leader of people who enjoys the firm support of the popular masses. President Kim Il-song has devoted his all to the freedom and liberation of people. Pressure and "sanction" on North Korea are impotent.
