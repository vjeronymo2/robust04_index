May 16, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY ROUNDUP: WESTERN WASTE INDUSTRIES TO ACQUIRE G.I. INDUSTRIES

Western Waste Industries, a Carson-based trash-collection company, has agreed in principle to acquire G.I. Industries, a Simi Valley-based trash-collection firm.

Terms of the agreement were not disclosed.

G.I. is controlled by the Asadurian family, which has trash collection operations in Ventura County.
