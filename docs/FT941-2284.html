940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / Value of overseas acquisitions rises UK companies spent Pounds 2.2bn on acquisitions of overseas companies in the fourth quarter of last year, figures published yesterday by the Central Statistical Office show. Expenditure on overseas acquisitions for the whole of last year was Pounds 9.3bn, compared with Pounds 7.2bn in 1992. While the value of transactions increased last year the number of deals fell to 452, from 679 in 1992. The CSO said US acquisitions accounted for 53 per cent of the value of deals done during the year, compared with 29 per cent in 1992. Spending on UK acquisitions by overseas companies rose to Pounds 4.7bn, from Pounds 4.1bn in 1992.
