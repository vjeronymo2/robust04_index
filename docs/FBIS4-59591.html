BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher yesterday evening. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' and Dennis Ross, the U.S. coordinator for the Middle East peace process. The attendees discussed the Middle East peace process. President al-Asad listened to what Christopher conveyed and presented Syria's viewpoint on the peace process and its objectives. It was agreed to resume the talks when Christopher returns to Damascus.
