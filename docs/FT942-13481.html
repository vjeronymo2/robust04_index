940425 FT 25 APR 94 / Business Travel (Update): Break-up The division of state-owned Russian airline Aeroflot into more than 200 smaller airlines has had extremely negative consequences on internal flights, a Western aviation official warned last week. Fernand Francois, Executive Director of the European Business Aviation Association (EBAA), told a seminar: 'The creation of a new, decen-tralised system, together with privatisation, has led to an increase in the cost of tickets, a reduction in the frequency of flights and the abolition of unprofitable routes. 'There is no interconnection between the more than 200 Russian carriers with operating licences,' he said.
