920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Kleen-e-ze cuts losses to Pounds 1.1m Kleen-e-ze Holdings, the consumer goods group, continued its rehabilitation. Losses were reduced from Pounds 4.28m to Pounds 1.14m after exceptional charges in the year to August 30 1991 and it is looking for 'modest profitability' in the current term. After last September's rights issue, directors now hold 66.56 per cent of the capital. At end-August net borrowings represented 39 per cent of shareholders' funds. Turnover came to Pounds 45.5m (Pounds 48.3m). Continuing activities achieved break-even at the operating level, against a loss of Pounds 599,000. Exceptional charges were Pounds 153,00 (Pounds 2.65m). After extraordinary costs of Pounds 675,000 (credit Pounds 2.43m) the attributable loss was Pounds 1.82m (Pounds 1.6m). Losses per share were 16.73p (58.82p). The group operates the Innovations mail order business and homecare direct selling. The small sales promotion company was sold and the US operation closed. Operating losses of the two amounted to Pounds 637,000 but will not recur.
