BFN [Text] Tiraspol BASAPRESS, 10/6/1994 -- A meeting of the Union of Veterans of the Afghanistan War was held yesterday in Tiraspol. The meeting was attended by representatives of the Afghanistan war veterans' unions of Moldova, Ukraine, and Russia. The participants criticized the Tiraspol authorities for leaving the veterans and the families of Afghanistan war victims without social protection. Aleksandr Karaman, the vice president of the self-proclaimed Dniester Republic declared at the meeting that due to a very severe economic situation, when internal production has fallen by 26 percent, the leadership of Tiraspol is forced to make cuts in some social assistance programs. Aleksandr Lebed, the commander of the 14th Russian Army [currently stationed in Dniester], who also took the floor, declared that the leadership of Dniester is made up of swindlers who pretend that certain social organizations, including the Union of Afghanistan War Veterans, are taking an active part in the political life -- especially in the future elections to the leadership [sentence as received]. The meeting adopted a declaration which announces its intention to take part actively in the political life and future elections.
