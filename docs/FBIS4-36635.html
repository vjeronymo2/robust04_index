BFN [Text] The General Directorate of Public Security decided tonight to stop the Future Television station's transmission due to violations of the cabinet decision of 23 March 1994, which banned the audio and visual media from transmitting direct or indirect political news and programs. Future Television transmitted on its news program "Tonight's World" a report on the inauguration of the Sidon municipality building. The report included the political speech delivered by Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri, thus violating the cabinet decision on the transmission of political news. The public security organ contacted the station and asked it to stop transmission. Future Television stopped its transmissions at 2030 today [1730 GMT] in obedience to this decision.
