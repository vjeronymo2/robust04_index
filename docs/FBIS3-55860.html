Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Sevindzh Abdullayeva, Viktor Shulman] [Text] Baku January 11 TASS -- The Azeri Defence Ministry reported today the on-going Armenian attempts to penetrate inside Azerbaijan. The Armenians repeatedly attacked the Azeri army positions in the Terter and Fizuli regions located north and south of Nagorno- Karabakh this morning. All the attacks have been fully repulsed. According to the ministry, the Armenians had considerable losses. Some Armenian units leave the front on their own and are trying to escape to Armenia, the Azeri Defence Ministry's press service reports with a reference to intelligence information from the occupied republican areas. It also claims a number of deserters have been shot in the Armenian rear.
