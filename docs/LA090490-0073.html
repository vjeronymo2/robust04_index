September 4, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

EPIDEMIC: Medicine still hasn't curbed the county's measles epidemic, meaning unvaccinated kindergartners will again be turned away from school. . . . Despite signs of some improvement, there have been about 600 measles cases in the county this year, nearly twice those in all of 1989 and 50 times those in 1987. Kristina Lindgren and Tony Marcano
