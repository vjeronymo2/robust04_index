Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on epidemics and diseases monitored by Paraguay Bureau from 26 November to 2 December.

Brazil
On 30 November, health authorities admitted that a meningitis meningococcus epidemic has been detected in Rio de Janeiro State. They already are discussing with the Health Ministry the need to import Cuban vaccine for an immunization campaign early next year. Rio de Janeiro and other Baixada Fluminense municipalities show the highest rate of the disease. A total of 2,209 meningitis cases were registered in the state from January to 24 November, 499 fatal. Of this total, 725 people had meningitis menincoccus and 148 died. (Rio de Janeiro JORNAL DO BRASIL in Portuguese 1 Dec 93 p 19)
