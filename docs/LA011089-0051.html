January 10, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY ROUNDUP: NOVEMBER HOUSE SALES FALL IN CONEJO VALLEY

November sales of existing houses in the Conejo Valley fell 12% from the previous month but remained 24% ahead of a year earlier, the Conejo Valley Board of Realtors said.

The board said 230 single-family houses were sold in November, down from 262 in October but up from 186 in November, 1987. Sales of condominiums followed a similar pattern, totaling 330 in November, which was down from 361 in October but up from 245 a year earlier.

The average price of a house, meanwhile, fell to $296,906 in November from a record high of $303,900 in October, the board said. A year earlier, the average price was $236,797.

The report covers sales in Agoura Hills, Westlake Village, Thousand Oaks and Newbury Park.
