CSO ["Decree of the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission: On Establishment of the Results of the Election of Deputies to the Council of the Federation of the Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation for Tatarstan's Two-Seat Electoral District No. 16"] [Text] On 13 March 1994, the election of deputies to the Council of the Federation of the Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation was held for Tatarstan's Two-Seat Electoral District No. 16. Based on the official report of the district electoral commission for Tatarstan's Two-Seat Electoral District No. 16 on the election of deputies to the Council of the Federation and in accordance with Article 32 of the Statute on Elections of Deputies of the Council of the Federation of the Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation in 1993, the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission has determined that the following individuals have been elected as the result of the election of deputies to the Council of the Federation of the Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation for Tatarstan's Dual-Seat Electoral District No. 16: Farid Khayrullovich Mukhametshin, born 1947, Tatar, higher education, not affiliated with a party, and Mintimer Sharipovich Shaymiyev, chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tatarstan, born 1937, Tatar, higher education, not affiliated with a party, president of the Republic of Tatarstan. The Central Electoral Commission of the Republic of Tatarstan decrees: 1. That the election of Farid Khayrullovich Mukhametshin and Mintimer Sharipovich Shaymiyev as deputies to the Council of the Federation of the Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation from Tatarstan's Two-Seat Electoral District No. 16 is valid. 2. That this decree will be published in the press. [Signed] N.T. RYABOV, chairman of the Central Electoral Commission of the Russian Federation I.V. GALUSHKO, secretary of the Central Electoral Commission of the Russian Federation Moscow, 25 March 1994
