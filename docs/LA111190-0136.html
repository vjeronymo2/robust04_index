November 11, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL ROUNDUP

Western Christian 20, Village Christian 0 -- Senior Dinari Williams scored touchdowns on a 57-yard run and a 43-yard pass from Terry Noice to lead Western Christian (5-5, 4-1) in an Alpha League game at Kennedy High. The win clinched second place for the Fighting Lancers.

Chad Everett led Village Christian (6-3-1, 3-2) with 54 yards rushing. SCOTT KATCHER
