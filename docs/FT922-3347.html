920611 FT 11 JUN 92 / International Capital Markets: Record issues at Fannie Mae THE FEDERAL National Mortgage Association has issued a record Dollars 19bn of mortgage-backed securities in May, up from Dollars 17.9bn in April, Reuter reports from New York. Of this, a record Dollars 16.7bn was backed by new loans, as opposed to mortgages in Fannie Mae's portfolio, the association said. Lender-originated mortgage issues were Dollars 16.5bn in April. Fannie Mae mortgage-backed securities outstanding grew to Dollars 404.4bn at May 31, up Dollars 8.6bn or a 26 per cent annualised rate from a month earlier.
