940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / MoD official accepted bribes A senior Ministry of Defence official was found guilty yesterday of accepting bribes in an armaments contract fraud. Mr Bernard Trevelyan, head of the ministry's light armoured engineering systems, used a front company to leak technical and financial secrets on orders, Southwark Crown Court heard. Mr Trevelyan was convicted on four counts of corruption involving a total of Pounds 8,652 and one charge of attempted corruption, which could have netted him Pounds 28,580. He was acquitted on three corruption charges. Sentence was adjourned until April 22 for reports.
