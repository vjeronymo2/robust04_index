BFN [Text] Seoul, May 15 (YONHAP) -- Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu left the country Sunday morning on an official trip that will take him to the Philippines, Thailand, Vietnam and Indonesia. During the visit until May 25, Minister Han is expected to discuss matters of mutual concern with his counterparts in the countries he visits. The issues they will review would include the North Korean nuclear question, the coming APEC (Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation) summit and questions of regional cooperation in the Asia-Pacific area. He will visit Manila on May 15-17, Bangkok May 17-19, Hanoi May 19-22 and Jakarta May 22-25.
