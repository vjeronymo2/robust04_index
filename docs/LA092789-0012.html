September 27, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

U.S. COMMITMENT TO ISRAEL

Thank you for publishing "No Peace for the Peacemakers" by Hillel Bardin, Op-Ed Page, Sept. 16.

The original Zionist myth was "There are no Palestinians" period. Recently the Israeli government replaced the original with one that states: "There are no Palestinians who are willing to talk peace."

Bardin's column went a long way to shatter this myth. He eloquently presented the case of only five Palestinians. Like them, there are thousands who have been jailed, expelled, or even killed by the Israelis.

We not only have a moral stake in the peace between Israel and the Palestinians, we also have a huge financial stake. Our moral obligation is to make sure that our money is not used to silence the peacemakers.

SAMI M. ODEH

Orange
