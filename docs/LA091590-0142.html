September 15, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFLY

Boycott Urged: Angered by a price increase announced last week, some users of the Prodigy electronic communication service asking other subscribers not to buy products promoted through advertisements on the service. A large-scale boycott could be a setback for Prodigy, a joint venture of Sears, Roebuck & Co. and International Business Machines that has yet to turn a profit. The company's new pricing system raises monthly rates by $3-top $12.95-for those who pay on a monthly basis.
