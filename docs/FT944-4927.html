941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / International Company News: Suez Suez, the French financial services group, has regrouped its stake in Belgium's flagship holding company Societe Generale de Belgique as part of an internal restructuring, a statement published by the Brussels bourse said, Reuter reports. The group's total stake now amounts to 61.53 per cent against 60.85 per cent in 1992.
