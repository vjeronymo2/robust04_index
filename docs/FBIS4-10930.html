BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on Panama's economic developments monitored through 19 April. During the first quarter of 1994, Panamanian exports rose by 4 percent in comparison with the same period of 1993. So far this year, Panamanian exports have totaled $74.7 million, while in 1993, exports for the same period amounted to $71.9 million. (Panama City LA PRENSA in Spanish 13 Apr 94 p 11A) The Panamanian Foreign Trade Institute has reported that Panama's annual export income from fishing operations last year exceeded $85 million, which represents more than 18.2 percent from the previous year's exports. Shrimp exports alone amounted to $60 million, second only to the $200 million derived from banana exports. (Panama City ACAN in Spanish 1757 GMT 16 Apr 94)
