Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by Najwa 'Abd-al-'Aziz] [Text] The Interior Ministry yesterday started to reinforce the al-Qalyubiyah security agencies with "commandos" and threw tightened security cordons around the area of al-Khankah. Strict security measures were taken around the homes of three extremists sentenced in connection with the attempt to kill Prime Minister Dr. 'Atif Sidqi. The convicts are Salah Sulayman and Tariq Hasan al-Fahl, sentenced to death, and Muhammad Khalil, sentenced to life with hard labor. The security agencies stepped up search campaigns in the underground terminals and on roads in the area of al-Marj to prevent weapons and explosives from reaching the families of the three convicts through extremist group members.
