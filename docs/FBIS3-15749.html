Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, March 8 (AFP) -- Armenia said Tuesday it backed the withdrawal of Armenian separatist forces from Nagorno-Karabakh from territory conquered from Azerbaijan in return for the deployment of an international buffer force. Armenian deputy foreign minister Zhirair Liparityan told a press conference here that he backed a combination of Russian and Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe (CSCE) peace plans for Nagorno-Karabakh, which provide for peacekeeping troops and observers in the region after a ceasefire.
