931231 FT 31 DEC 93 / UK Company News: SP and Avon break equity link SP Tyres, the Birmingham based manufacturer of Dunlop tyres, and Avon Tyres, part of Avon Rubber, are breaking their equity links of four years standing, writes Paul Cheeseright. SP is selling back to Avon Rubber its 20 per cent stake in Avon Tyres, while Avon Rubber is selling back to SP its 30 per cent holding in Motorway Tyres and Accessories, a tyre distribution company which was 70 per cent owned by SP. The transfers result in a Pounds 4.5m profit for SP.
