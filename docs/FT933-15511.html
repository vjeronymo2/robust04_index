930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / UK Company News: English & Overseas Properties ENGLISH & Overseas Properties, through its new subsidiary English & Overseas Investments, is to acquire three freehold warehouse investments. The consideration of Pounds 1.78m has been satisfied partly by the issue of 150,000 shares in the subsidiary at 30p each. The properties are in Leigh, Greater Manchester, King's Lynn, Norfolk and Gateshead, Tyne and Wear.
