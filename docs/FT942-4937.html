940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / International Company News: Aros to quit bonds market-making Aros Fondkommission, the Swedish broker, plans to withdraw from market-making in Swedish government bonds, Reuter reports from Stockholm. It said it was withdrawing because of the difficulties of making a profit in recent turbulent conditions. Aros, owned by engineering group Asea Brown Boveri, is mainly involved in stockbroking, corporate finance and commercial paper programmes. In April and May the Aros made a positive result. It said it would continue to make a market in state T-bills and related derivatives as well as commercial paper.
