BFN [Report by Fahd al-'Arrak] [Text] Informed sources of the Chamber of Commerce and Industry have denied that any of its members recently paid a working visit to Israel along with a delegation of Gulf businessmen. These sources told AL-RA'Y AL-'AMM that what has been published recently about a visit by businessmen from the Gulf Cooperation Council to Tel Aviv last week may be correct, but not a single Kuwaiti businessman participated in this visit. It is noteworthy that the board chairman of the Egyptian Airlines Company, ZAS [name as published], has recently stated that his company had carried businessmen from the Gulf states to Tel Aviv to discuss spheres of cooperation and joint opportunities in light of peace prospects in the region.
