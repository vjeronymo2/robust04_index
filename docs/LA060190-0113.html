June 1, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The Cuban men's water polo team is scheduled to arrive here today for a series of three games with the U.S. international team.

The games are set for Sunday, 4 p.m., at Newport Harbor High School; Wednesday, 7:30 p.m., at the Belmont Plaza in Long Beach; and Thursday, 7:30 p.m., at the Olympic Swim Stadium at USC.
