BFN [Text] Seoul, June 20 (YONHAP) -- The government, in a cabinet meeting on Monday, approved a bill providing for the country's joining of the Marrakesh agreement on the establishment of a World Trade Organization (WTO). The bill, if approved by the president, will be referred to the National Assembly on June 22 for review and approval. A World Trade Organization is to be set up as a permanent organization in place of the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (GATT).
