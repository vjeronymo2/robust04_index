930427 FT 27 APR 93 / UK Company News: Berry Starquest asset value lower Net asset value of Berry Starquest was 170.7p at January 31, a 5.8 per cent fall from the 181.2p value of a year earlier. Net revenue for the year improved to Pounds 160,000 (Pounds 157,000) for earnings per share of 3.1p (3p). The recommended dividend is lifted to 2.2p (2p).
