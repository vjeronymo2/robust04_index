940922 FT 22 SEP 94 / Two societies raise loan rates Rises in mortgage rates were announced by two building societies yesterday. The standard variable rate charged by Alliance & Leicester, the fourth-largest society, will go up by 0.46 points to 8.1 per cent. National & Provincial, the eighth-largest society, is raising its standard variable rate by 0.45 percentage points, taking it to 8.09 per cent.
