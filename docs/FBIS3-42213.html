Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Algiers, Feb 3 (AFP)--Algeria's security forces widened a ban on possessing rifles to the whole country Thursday after the fifth political assassination in six days. The widening of the ban from 10 departments in central Algeria to the whole country follows the killing Wednesday of two political activists. Yahia Bouguettaya, the former mayor of Mohammadia, south-east Algeria, and Mohamed Touali, an activist in Algeria's Ettahadi (Challenge) political party, were assassinated Wednesday. [passage omitted]
