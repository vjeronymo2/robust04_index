940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / UK Company News: English & Scottish lifts asset value English & Scottish Investors lifted its net asset value per ordinary and B share from 106.7p to 144.4p over the year to January 31. Net available revenue for the 12 months improved from Pounds 3.16m to Pounds 3.18m for earnings per ordinary share of 1.96p (1.95p). A second interim dividend in lieu of a final was declared last month for an unchanged 1.75p total. A maintained interim of 0.010938p per B share was also declared.
