921107 FT 07 NOV 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Pepper prices have remained steady after the recent uptrend, reports Man-producten. Some origins, including Indonesia and India, reduced their prices slightly and this made buyers more cautious. Brazil - normally the main supplier at this time of year - reports that the crop might be halved from last year's level. White pepper is offered at Dollars 1,550 a tonne cif and only slightly lower on shipment basis. Black pepper is available at Dollars 1,350 to Dollars 1,400 spot and at Dollars 1,250 to Dollars 1,350 cif on shipment basis.
