Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Damascus--His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad last night received a telephone call from His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. The two leaders discussed the barbaric attack against Palestinian worshipers while they were performing dawn prayers at the Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron. They also discussed the extent of Israel's seriousness with regard to the peace process.
