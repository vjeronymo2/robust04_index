March 18, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ART AND ANIMALS

I am horrified at the "Eco-Systems" art exhibition (" 'Eco-Systems' Exhibit Lets Life Imitate Art," by Zan Dubin, March 5).

How dare Betty Klausner justify using live animals for art!

In this day and age of growing concern and awareness of the plight of all animals include bees and guppies, to allow this to take place is inexcusable.

SHELLEY BUTLER

West Los Angeles
