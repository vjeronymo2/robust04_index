Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Feb.24 (A.A) -- The General Assembly of parliament adopted a bill which approves protocol No.4 which foresees additional human rights, on Wednesday [23 February]. Under the protocol which has been adopted, no person will be expelled or banned from entering to the country which he is a citizen. In addition, the protocol bans the mass expulsion of foreigners from the country. The protocol ensures that everyone who is living legally in a country is entitled to circulate, choose his residence, or leave the country, freely. These rights can not be restricted unless they threaten national unity, public order, and the freedoms and rights of others.
