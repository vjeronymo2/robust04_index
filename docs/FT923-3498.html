920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Art side gives Parambe a boost Pre-tax profits of Parambe, the investment company, rose from Pounds 1,475 to Pounds 68,642 for the half year ended June 30. Turnover of Pounds 152,619 (Pounds 103,815) was boosted by a sharp rise in works of art income from Pounds 28,574 to Pounds 107,627. The interim dividend is a same-again 0.55p and is paid from earnings of 0.83p (0.02p). Net asset value per share at period-end amounted to 60.1p (57.2p).
