Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Article by Meng Fansen [1322 4907 2773] and Li Dan [2621 0030]: ``Nation's Computer Compiling Technique Strides into Advanced Ranks''] [Summary] The high-level microcomputer C-Ada compiling system perfected by a Beijing Sanjiu [0005 0046] Enterprises Corporation research team led by Professor Li Xin [2621 6580] recently passed the formal appraisal conducted by a panel of 15 software experts. An especially interesting feature of this system is the originality of its parallel processing capabilities, in which--it is reported--China ranks second in the world.
