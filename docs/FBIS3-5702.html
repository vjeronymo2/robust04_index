Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Text] The friendship visit to the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR] by a high-level Kingdom of Cambodia National Assembly delegation headed by Chairman Samdech Chea Sim, which began on 5 March, ended with brilliant success this morning. Samdech Chea Sim and his delegation then left Vientiane by special plane for Bangkok to pay an official friendship visit to the Kingdom of Thailand. On 6 March, Samdech Chea Sim visited many production establishments; the construction site of the Mekong Friendship Bridge; and Vientiane Province, where he was welcomed by His Excellency [H.E.] Governor Mounkeo Olaboun and his staff. During its visit to Vientiane Province, the delegation also toured the Nam Ngum Dam hydropower plant. In the evening, Samdech Chea Sim granted an audience at the guest house to representatives of the Laos-Cambodia Friendship Association led by Chairman Son Khamvanvongsa. During their conversation, the two discussed ways to consolidate and strengthen the friendly relations between Laos and Cambodia, both now and in the future. Before the delegation left for Wattai Airport, a grand official ceremony was held at the office of the National Assembly in the capital, Vientiane. It was attended by H.E. Saman Vi-gnaket and H.E. Khambou Sounisai, chairman and vice chairman of the LPDR National Assembly, and several high-level cadres from various relevant services. Mak Nguon-li, charge d'affaires ad interim at the Embassy of the Kingdom of Cambodia, and embassy staff members were also on hand to see off the delegation.
