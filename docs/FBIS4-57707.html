BFN [Text] Defense Minister Edmundo Perez Yoma has invited the Army to cooperate in building a national defense state policy. He extended this invitation during a ceremony at which President Eduardo Frei Ruiz-Tagle decorated four high-ranking Army officers. The chief of state awarded the President of the Republic medal to Brigadier Generals Rofolfo Sanchez, Juan Cheyre, Hernan Reyes, and Manuel Vitis. The defense minister then addressed the event. He stated that nobody in the past few years has the moral superiority to judge the various governments of the past three decades. He said that the time has come to positively fill the gap that separates politicians from military men by building a state national defense policy. [Begin Perez recording] The times of impositions are long gone. We do not believe in a formal subordination based on the authority of whoever signs a decree. We believe the subordination of military men to the political authority is the way a democratic society keeps a legitimate space to implement a military policy with full respect for the Constitution. [end recording] President Frei earlier opened a seminar which dealt with emerging enterprises and the society of the future. He said the strategy of his government's productive modernization plan is to improve national enterprises, enhancing their international competitiveness. Today President Frei met with Army Commander General Augusto Pinochet Ugarte who informed him of a trip abroad he will make soon.
