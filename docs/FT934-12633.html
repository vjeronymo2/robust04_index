931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / Management (The Growing Business): Essex helps firms to go Dutch - In a Nutshell Essex-based businesses should find it easier to trade with the Netherlands following the creation of a Dutch Desk by Essex Training and Enterprise Council. The desk will provide services such as establishing contact with Dutch companies. Some services will be free, others offered at a discount to the commercial rate. Contact Essex Tec, Redwing House, Hedgerows Business Park, Colchester Road, Chelmsford, CM2 5PB. Tel. 0245 450123.
