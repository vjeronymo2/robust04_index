Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "'Abd-al-Hamid al-Bakkush"] [Text] London, AL-HAYAH -- Libyan opposition sources have said that they have lost contact with former Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Hamid al-Bakkush for a week. AL-HAYAH contacted al-Bakkush's family in Cairo. The family said that "he left the Egyptian capital a week ago for a destination which he did not reveal as usual." The family pointed out that "it is not worried about him at all, because he is used to traveling for long periods without revealing his destination."
