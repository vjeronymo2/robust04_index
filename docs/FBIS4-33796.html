BFN [From the NTV newscast] [Excerpt] Army General Raul Castro continues to tour the Central Army region. On this occasion he visited areas of the Victoria de Giron Citrus Enterprise in Jaguey Grande, Matanzas Province. Raul and his companions visited farm number 2 of Jaguey's citrus program. The delegation held a meeting with Agriculture Minister Alfredo Jordan, member of the Politburo; provincial party leaders, enterprise managers; and the general staff of the 102d Division of the Youth Labor Army [EJT]. In the meeting, an agreement was reached to incoporate citrus plantations into EJT farms, which have already demonstrated their efficiency in this region and other parts of Cuba. The EJT already manages six of the 10 farms of the enterprise, the remaining four will be put under EJT control later in the year. [passage omitted].
