BFN [Text] It is being reported from the UN Headquarters that in letters to President Glavkos Kliridhis and Turkish Cypriot leader Rauf Denktas, UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali will stress that the 21 March UN document is the final method to implement the confidence-building measures, and will clarify the points that were discussed with Denktas last week. This information was included in a report from the REUTERS News Agency, citing diplomats at the UN Headquarters. According to the same report, Butrus-Ghali hopes that his letters will help both sides to finally accept the confidence-building measures. The Cyprus Broadcasting Corporation correspondent reported from New York that either Joe Clark, the UN secretary general's special representative in Cyprus, or Alternate UN Secretary General Marrack Goulding, is expected to convey the letters to Kliridhis and Denktas next week. Government Spokesman Ioannis Kasoulidhis said the government has no official information that the UN secretary general intends to send letters to Kliridhis and Denktas.
