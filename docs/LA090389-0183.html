September 3, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

12 MEN ARRESTED IN PROSTITUTION SWEEP

Twelve men were arrested on suspicion of soliciting a prostitute in a Friday night sweep of Harbor Boulevard north of the San Diego Freeway in Costa Mesa.

The men were arrested after allegedly propositioning two female undercover officers who were decoys in "Operation Bellhop," according to Lt. Gary Webster. The police operation in the past has concentrated on prostitution and drug arrests, particularly at hotels along Harbor, Webster said.

"There were many customers for those arrested," Webster said. "Once arrests began taking place, those in the area could see what was going on. They took off."
