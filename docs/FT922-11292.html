920427 FT 27 APR 92 / Construction Contracts: Breakwaters FAIRCLOUGH MARINE has been selected by the main contractor, Kier Construction, to provide two protective breakwaters at the entrance of Sovereign Harbour at Eastbourne, East Sussex, under a 42-week contract valued at Pounds 2.6m. The company will supply and place 150,000 tonnes of rock armour in the construction of the 200-metre breakwaters.
