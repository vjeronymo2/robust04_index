BFN [Text] Phnom Penh, May 22 (XINHUA) -- Khieu Samphan, president of the Democratic Kampuchea Party (DKP) has been invited to attend a dinner hosted by King Norodom Sihanouk in Beijing on May 25. A representative of the DKP's president said here today. The representative said that Khieu Samphan, who will be accompanied by a 6-member delegation, will go to the king's residence in Beijing immediately after his arrival in China's capital on May 25 on the way to attend a round-table meeting on peace and national reconciliation in Pyongyang. The 71-year-old king proposed the convening of the round-table talks from May 27-30 at the royal residence of Chang So-on in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK) without any condition before he returned Beijing from Cambodia on May 18 for medical treatment. The representative said that DKP supported the king's peace efforts and respected the people's hope on peace and rebuilding the country after 20-year-old war. It is a good thing to start talks on the peace between the royal government and the Khmer Rouge.
