Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] An extraordinary congress of the Ukrainian Civic Congress of Crimea [Ukrayinskyy Hromadyanskyy Konhres Krymu] has taken place in Simferopol. An election program was discussed and confirmed, and so was a list of candidates for deputy of the Supreme Council of the Republic of Crimea. It was noted that all of them would defend the idea of developing statehood and would speak in favor of the inviolable unity of Crimea with Ukraine and in favor of peace and international accord in this region of Ukraine.
