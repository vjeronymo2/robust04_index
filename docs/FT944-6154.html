941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Murray Enterprise net assets rise Murray Enterprise raised net assets per share on a fully diluted basis from 124.42p to 125.35p in the year ended September 30. During the year, the trust extensively changed its portfolioin order to invest in UK smaller companies. After-tax revenue grew to Pounds 571,000 (Pounds 64,000) and fully diluted earnings per share were 2.5p (0.26p). The final dividend of 1.4p makes a total of 2.1p (1.505p).
