BFN [Text] By a vote of 23 to 8, the Constitution and Justice Committee of the Chamber of Deputies has decided to shelve the prosecution proceedings on former Deputies Joao Alves, Genebaldo Correia, Cid Carvalho, and Manoel Moreira, who were charged with having been members of the budget gang. They resigned their posts early in the week to prevent being dismissed. With the exception of Joao Alves, who has no party, they can run for a seat in the Chamber in the next elections.
