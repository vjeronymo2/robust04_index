BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 5 (KCNA) -- Pacific-based U.S. F-15 fighter bombers, FA-18 pursuit-assault planes and warplanes of other types and warplanes of the U.S. Airforce in South Korea and the puppet Airforce, over 450 in all, staged a military exercise against the North in the sky above the area near the Military Demarcation Line on May 3, according to military sources. Meanwhile, tanks of the South Korean puppet Army deployed in Kumpa-ri, Paju County, fired about 20 shells at Hapo-ri of the same county and large-calibre gun units that had taken position in Mago-ri of Yonchon County, Yongsan-ri of Paju County, and Yulli-ri of Chorwon County of South Korean Kangwon Province fired some 70 shells into the area adjacent to the Demilitarized Zone. The war moves of the U.S. imperialists and the South Korean puppets getting more wanton prove that they are rushing toward confrontation and war, going against peace and detente in Korea.
