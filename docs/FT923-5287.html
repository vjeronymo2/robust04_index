920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / Economy likely to contract by 0.6% THE ECONOMY will contract by 0.6 per cent this year, according to the latest compilation of private-sector forecasts drawn up by the Treasury. The consensus forecast for next year is for growth of 1.6 per cent. The forecasts are gloomier than the comparable compilation of a month ago, which said output would fall 0.2 per cent this year and increase 2.0 per cent next year.
