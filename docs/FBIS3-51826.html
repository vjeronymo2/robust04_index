Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Fourth Latin American and Caribbean Meeting for Solidarity, Sovereignty, Self-Determination, and the Lives of our People has just been inaugurated at the Palace of Conventions in Havana. Commander in Chief Fidel Castro is present at the event which is also being attended by more than 1,000 representatives of the social and people's movements of all the countries of the continent and the Antilles. The participants at the event will discuss possible alternatives to the structural crisis confronted by the region.
