BFN [By reporter Kwon Sang-un] [Text] On 26 June, the Office of National Railroads [ONR] disclosed its plan to mete out strong disciplinary measures-- including the deprivation of their status or dismissal from their posts--on the members of the National Council of Locomotive Engineers [NCLE] who have not yet reported to their work after the deadline set for their return has passed, by classifying them into several categories, depending on the degree of their active participation in the strike. The ONR has also decided to dismiss some 500 employees, including about 210 who have not yet returned to work after they were released by the police with a warning, and the executives of the NCLE headquarters and its 20 nation-wide branch offices.
