[Docket No. RP94&hyph;425&hyph;002] Tennessee Gas Pipeline Company; Tariff Filing December 1, 1994. On November 23, 1994, Tennessee Gas Pipeline Company (Tennessee) filed information in response to the order issued October 27 in the referenced docket, 69 FERC ¶61,094 (1994), and the following revised tariff sheets to be effective November 1, 1994 to reflect the revisions to the GSR component of the Rate Schedules IT, IT&hyph;X, and PAT: Sub 1st Rev Sheet No. 21A 6th Rev Sheet No. 22 Sub 1st Rev Sheet No. 22A 6th Rev Sheet No. 24 Sub 9st Rev Sheet No. 30 Tennessee states that the tariff revisions respond to the order issued November 18, 1994 in Docket Nos. RP94&hyph;309, et al. (69 FERC ¶61,234) where the Commission directed Tennessee to file tariff sheets reflecting the elimination of GSR costs allocated to its IT rates based on the original amortization period reflected in Docket Nos. RP93&hyph;151 and RP94&hyph;39. Tennessee states that it is filing concurrently in Docket Nos. RP91&hyph;203 and RP94&hyph;309 tariff sheets reflecting the same adjustment for periods prior to November 1, 1994. Any person desiring to protest said filing should file a protest with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, 825 North Capitol Street, NE., Washington, DC 20426, in accordance with Rule 211 of the Commission's Rules of Practice and Procedure. All such protests should be filed on or before December 8, 1994. Protests will be considered by the Commission in determining the appropriate action to be taken, but will not serve to make protestants parties to the proceeding. Copies of this filing are on file with the Commission and are available for public inspection. Lois D. Cashell, Secretary.
