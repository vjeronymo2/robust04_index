Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] At today's session of the Croatian Government, its minister in charge of humanitarian affairs, Ivica Kostovic, spoke about the efforts Croatia has undertaken for the release of POW's from Serbia's detention camps. So far 6,700 Croats have been released, Kostovic said, and we trust the others will also be freed. He added that normal constructive negotiations on this issue conducted with the representatives of the so-called Federal Republic of Yugoslavia had opened other possibilities of successful negotiations in the future.
