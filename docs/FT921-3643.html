920313 FT 13 MAR 92 / UK Company News: QED Technology has been sold QED Technology, the Maxwell Communication Corporation offshoot which creates computer systems for publishers, has been sold to Cromas Trading of the US. Price Waterhouse, administrator to MCC, gave no details of the price. QED had sales of Pounds 4.6m in the year to March 31 1991. MCC sought US bankruptcy protection and entered administration in Britain in December. It owns Official Airlines Guide and MacMillan, both US publishers. Price Waterhouse is selling non-core units and attempting a restructuring of the company.
