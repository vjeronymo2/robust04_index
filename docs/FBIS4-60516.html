BFN [Text] Suha 'Arafat will not revert to [her maiden name] Suha Tawil. 'Arafat and I will remain together until death do us part. With these words, the wife of Palestinian leader Yasir 'Arafat denied rumors of her intention to divorce following differences with her husband over the arrest of her maternal uncle, who was detained for five days and then released after he was interrogated on charges of squandering public funds.
