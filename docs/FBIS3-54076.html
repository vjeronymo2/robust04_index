Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Interior Minister Besharati arrived in Doha, the capital of Qatar, this morning at the head of a high-ranking political-economic delegation. During the first round of talks, the Qatari interior minister described Besharati's visit to Qatar as an indication of the importance the Islamic Republic of Iran attaches to the expansion and consolidation of relations with Qatar, adding: We must not allow certain impure hands to throw a wrench in the good relations between the two countries. He declared his country's full readiness to expand relations between the two countries in the political, security, economic, and commercial fields and in the battle against narcotics. He expressed the wish to benefit from the Islamic Republic of Iran's experience in combating narcotics. Besharati, for his part, referred to the good ties between Iran and Qatar and expressed the Islamic Republic of Iran's readiness to cooperate with Qatar and neighboring countries in various fields.
