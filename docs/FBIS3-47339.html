Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 11 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- On the occasion of scheduled exploitation of the oil "Tsagaan Els" deposit in the region of Zuunbayan on the territory of the Dornogobi Aimak the director of the Mongolian Petroleum Company has applied with an official letter to the head of the local administration. The letter contains a request not to allow the wasting of the pipes and equipment having been applied at the enterprise acting there earlier. Meanwhile just for the last year from here there has been taken away out of the country through the southern border several dozens of trucks with metal.
