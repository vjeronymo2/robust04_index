Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Item from roundup of correspondents' and ITAR-TASS reports under the "Inform" rubric: "Bryansk Residents Cut `Grass'"] [Text] According to State Customs Committee data, among the Russian customs offices the Bryansk Customs occupies one of the top places in terms of the quantity of drugs detained. It has attained this position in a record short time: Just two months ago poppy straw was discovered for the first time on one of the trains crossing the republic's border. It was as though a dam had burst. By the end of the year staffers of the customs office's passenger department had confiscated 574 kg of poppy straw.
