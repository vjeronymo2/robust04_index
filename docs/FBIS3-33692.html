Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A serious radioactive leak has taken place at Israel's Dimona Reactor in the Negev desert. Responsible Egyptian sources have disclosed that the Egyptian border region may be affected by the leak. There is fear of nuclear fallout [tarsibat] in the area from al-'Arish to Rafah. The responsible quarters have instructed experts in chemical warfare to conduct a full survey of the border region and measure the degree of radiation.
