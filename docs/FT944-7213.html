941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Scottish Investment net assets dip 6% Net asset value per share of Scottish Investment Trust fell by 6 per cent from 280p to 263.2p over the year to October 31. For the 12-month period, net revenue edged ahead to Pounds 14.2m (Pounds 14.1m). Gross income totalled Pounds 29.8m (Pounds 27m). Earnings per share improved from 5.48p to 5.49p and a final dividend of 3.39p (3.2p) is recommended for a total of 5.15p (4.9p).
