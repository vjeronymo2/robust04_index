930921 FT 21 SEP 93 / Scheme to help long-term jobless A Pounds 630,000 initiative to help the long-term unemployed was launched yesterday by North Yorkshire Training and Enterprise Council and the Employment Service. Operation Employment aims to help more than 1,500 people unemployed for more than six months. They will all be eligible for a Pounds 150 payment in their first week's employment to help cover initial expenses and loss of benefits while awaiting their first pay packet.
