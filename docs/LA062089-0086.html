June 20, 1989, Tuesday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE: NAMES IN THE NEWS

Former Ram Les Josephson was inducted into the Dakota Relays Hall of Fame at Sioux Falls, S.D. As an Augustana College high jumper, Josephson set the state record at 6 feet 5 inches in 1964. His 10-year career as a Ram running back began that fall.
