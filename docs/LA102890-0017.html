October 28, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

IS IT JUST AN X WITH ANOTHER NAME? READERS SOUND OFF ON THE NEW NC-17 MOVIE RATING;

THE RECENT CHANGE IN THE MOVIE RATING SYSTEM PRODUCED A NUMBER OF RESPONSES. HERE ARE SOME.

A wolf in sheep's clothing is still a wolf. An X-rated movie with an NC-17 rating is still an X-rated movie.

I respectfully ask you to not advertise NC-17 movies.

Otherwise, our family will be forced to get our news elsewhere.

LINDA MURRAY, Sun Valley
