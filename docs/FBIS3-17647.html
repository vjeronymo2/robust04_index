Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] A report of the Central Electoral Commission has just been brought into our studio. Here is the first data on the progress of voting: 20 percent of the electorate have already voted in Sevastopol, Crimea; 34 percent in Zaporozhye; 40 percent in Ivano-Frankovsk; and more than 50 percent in Chernovtsy Oblast. The Central Electoral Commission also reported that 186 candidates have withdrawn their candidacies, thus facilitating the task facing the electors or, maybe, disappointing some of them. According to the Central Electoral Commission, preliminary data on the number of those who voted will be announced at 1600 and 2000.
