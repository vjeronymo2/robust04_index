931006 FT 06 OCT 93 / World Trade News: China power station deal for Gibson GIBSON GROUP, the Glasgow-based power-plant contractor, has reached preliminary agreement to supply a 120MW power station to Liaoning province in China. Final negotiations will start in the next few weeks on the contract, which would be worth about Pounds 65m. The group's main contracting company, Gibson Wells Engineering, has been working on the project for the past three years. The company is also discussing further contracts in China for power stations ranging from 100MW to 700MW. It said it expected to reach agreement soon with 'a substantial US company' which was looking to invest in power projects in China.
