April 28, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

'REPULSIVE' BUT ARTISTIC?

I've just read the April 13 review of "The Cook, The Thief, His Wife & Her Lover" ("Cook": Grossness From Great Talents") in which Kevin Thomas calls the film "repulsive," with scenes of cannibalism and of a man smeared with excrement. Quick! Call the National Endowment for the Arts! I'm sure it will want to sponsor the sequel in the name of artistic freedom!

BILL FARIS

Lake Forest
