BFN [Text] Belgrade, 14 Apr (TANJUG) -- A group of 17 opposition deputies to the Federal Assembly's Chamber of Citizens today called on Federal Prime Minister Radoje Kontic to dismiss Information Minister Slobodan Ignjatovic in connection with the withdrawal of accreditation from AFP and CNN. The signatories of this initiative are from the Serbian Renewal Movement, the Democratic Party [of Serbia], the Social Democratic Party of Montenegro, and the People's Party of Montenegro. They maintain that the ban on AFP and CNN causes inestimable damage to the FRY [Federal Republic of Yugoslavia] and its citizens, and ask that this decision be revoked. Explaining their proposal, the deputies say that the FRY is in this way coming into direct confrontation with the most powerful information media and indirectly with the United States and France.
