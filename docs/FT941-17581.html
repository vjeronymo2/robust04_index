940104 FT 04 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: All-Share classification revised Today's stock market trading will be the first to be reported under the revised FT-SE Actuaries classification of industry sectors. The traditional All-Share categories have been given their first thorough overhaul in two decades. From tomorrow the new arrangement will also apply to the FT's share price listings.
