931016 FT 16 OCT 93 / Speed cameras cut road deaths SPEED cameras introduced just a year ago have helped reduce deaths and serious accidents by more than 40 per cent, it was announced yesterday. A network of cameras was installed in west London last October. The Department of Transport said that in the first nine months fatal and serious casualties had fallen by 41 per cent, casualties by 22 per cent and the number of accidents by 24 per cent.
