September 29, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

OIL'S ROLE IN PERSIAN GULF CRISIS

Our soldiers are in the Middle East to secure next year's oil at this year's prices for last year's cars.

The cost could be terrible. After this is past, let's all do our best to assure that, when the next crisis happens, "last year's cars" are more efficient. Buy smart; vote smart.

EDGAR ROSS

Newbury Park
