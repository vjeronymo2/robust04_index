941119 FT 19 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Thomas Locker The absence of losses from activities now disposed of and improved results from several continuing businesses resulted in a return to pre-tax profit at Thomas Locker, the general engineering group, in the half-year to September. On sales of Pounds 14.4m (Pounds 13.5m) from continuing businesses, pre-tax profit amounted to Pounds 104,000. The previous first half saw a loss of Pounds 329,000, including Pounds 200,000 from discontinued activities, on total sales of Pounds 17.3m. The interim dividend is maintained at 0.2p, payable from earnings per share of 0.15p (0.67p losses). The company said that the profits turnround included Pounds 309,000 from the elimination of losses from Associated Perforators and Weavers and Locker Asia.
