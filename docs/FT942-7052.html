940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / Monks calls for union change The battle for full employment requires unions to change their behaviour, Mr John Monks, general secretary of the Trades Union Congress, said yesterday. He told the Royal Society of Arts that unions had given too little attention to job security. 'All too often pay is at the top of the bargaining agenda and job security effectively at the bottom,' he said. 'Some of the excessive rigidities in our labour market such as systematic overtime, predominantly a male phenomenon, are as much the result of employer preferences as trade union demands, but nevertheless we share the blame.'
