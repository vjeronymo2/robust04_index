BFN [Text] Sri Lanka's external assets rose substantially by $735 million. According to Central Bank statistics, the official reserve has also recorded an increase. This increase of $736 [as heard] million reflects the growing capital inflows and remittances. The country's foreign assets increased to 2,298 million rupees by the end of February this year. The remittances of foreign expatriates have also recorded an increase of 16 percent. The tourist arrivals recorded an upsurge of four percent.
