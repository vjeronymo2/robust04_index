931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / Soccer inquiry panel is named MR Robert Reid QC, a crown court recorder, Mr Rick Parry, chief executive of the English football Premier League, and Mr Steve Coppell, former chairman of the Professional Footballers' Association and former manager of Crystal Palace, are to carry out the league's inquiry into allegations over transfer dealings. Mr Parry is to prepare a report for the Premier League Board, setting out recommendations on the regulation of transfers and the use of agents, and details of any alleged breach of league rules.
