Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 28 (KCNA) -- The Dear Leader Comrade Kim Chong-il extended thanks to 47 war veterans including Mun Song-gyun, participants in the past Fatherland Liberation War, in the Hwayang co-op farm, 14 veteran party members including Chu Tong-yun, head of the sub-workteam of veteran party members in the Nyongpho co-op farm in Chongdan county, 11 veteran party members of the 13th party cell of Kangnyong county seat households including Han Thae- il, and a farmer Sin Nyon-yong at the workteam No. 6 of the Songwol co-op farm in Ongjin county, who helped boost the production of grain, in south Hwanghae Province. Out of the desire to live up to the loving care of the party and the state, they have cultivated lands to do farming and produced a large amount of rice. Comrade Kim Chong-il also sent thanks to a worker Choe Song-gol of the Kwangdok mine and a worker Kang Hi-chun of the Ni Yong-rok enterprise, who rescued five children from the danger of drowning. To devote individuals to the society, the collective and revolutionary comrades has become a social trend in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea.
