Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 22 KYODO -- Japan has agreed with the United States to continue producing under license UH-60J combat search and rescue helicopters, the Foreign Ministry said Tuesday [22 March]. The ministry said the diplomatic notes relating to the accord were exchanged earlier in the day between Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata and U.S. Ambassador to Japan Walter Mondale. The utility helicopters will be produced by Mitsubishi Heavy Industries Ltd. for the Maritime Self-Defense Force (MSDF) and the Air Self-Defense Force (ASDF), which have plans to add three more to their helicopter fleets by April 1996.
