June 10, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB;

MAN VS. MICROCHIP

Maybe this money isn't so lucky after all.

Classic Overlay, who was just that at 9-1, broke slowly and never menaced while finishing fifth in the fourth race.

Patches, the 2-1 choice in the Railbird Stakes, couldn't get by Forest Fealty in the final furlong, so there went another $60.

Trying to recoup the $80 I lost Saturday and then some, I'll bet a $20 exacta box in the third from Tu Eres Mi Heroe to Cherokee Returns.

Bob's Saturday Bankroll: $2,150

Saturday's Loss: $80

Current Bankroll: $2,070
