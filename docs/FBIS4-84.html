BFN [Text] More than 400 suspected members of the Police and Prisons' Union, Popcru, have been arrested in Pretoria after an alleged illegal gathering in front of the head office of the Department of Correctional Services. A spokesman for the department, Lieutenant Rudy Potgieter, said that no memorandum had been handed over and that the reason for the protest was not known. Most of the protesters were in uniform.
