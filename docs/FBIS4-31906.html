BFN [Text] Tirana, June 14 (ATA) -- The group of the Dutch diplomats, headed by the secretary of the Netherlands's Embassy in Belgrade, Stela Roner, recently visited Prizren, a city in western Kosova. The leaders of the LDK [Kosova Democratic Alliance] branch in Prizren familiarized them with the grave situation of the Albanians and savage police repression against them. The Dutch diplomats concerned themselves especially about the needs and possibilities of sending aid, particularly to those in the field of education and information.
