BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Shanayev] [Text] Vladikavkaz June 18 TASS -- "Additional investigation should be held" in the murder of Viktor Polyanichko and his associates, Acting Russian Procurator General Aleksey Ilyushenko said here today. Ilyushenko has completed a trip to Northern Ossetia and Ingushetia as part of preparations for a Russian Government meeting on problems of these republics. He said that "several versions of murder of former head of the temporary administration Polyanichko and the accompanying officials have been analyzed but not confirmed." The temporary administration head and the accompanying military were killed on August 1, 1993, near the Tarskoye settlement of the North Ossetian Prigorodnyy region. Unidentified persons put under automatic gun fire the car of Polyanichko and head of the Vladikavkaz garrison Major-General Anatoliy Koretskiy. Both died of wounds on the spot. The fire exchange also fatally wounded body guard of the temporary administration head Senior Lieutenant Viktor Kravchuk.
