Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A delegation from the European Community has arrived in Amman on a two-day visit to Jordan. The delegation, headed by Eberhardt Rhein, will meet a number of Jordanian official for discussing cooperation between Jordan and the EC. Jordan signed a financial and technological protocol in 1991 with the Community, granting the kingdom 126 million European monetary units, including donations, loans, and economic reform aid.
