920509 FT 09 MAY 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices International markets seem to be dormant, reports Man-producten. Cloves market weak, Madagascan available below Dollars 775 fob for large quantities. Nutmegs/mace: Indonesian abcd Dollars 1,750, shrivels Dollars 1,325 and bwp Dollars 1,000. Cassia unchanged. Seychelles cinnamom bark Dollars 1,350 cif Europe. Madagascar cinnamom not available until new cuttings out of June/July shipment, quoted at FF 6 cif. Ginger: Indian cochin Dollars 1,100, Nigerian split selected grade between Dollars 600 and Dollars 800 ex-warehouse. Jamaican pimento spot Rotterdam Dollars 2,450, shipment Dollars 2,395 cif, Mexico new crop Dollars 1,750 for October shipment. Madras turmeric firm.
