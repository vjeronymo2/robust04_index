BFN [Text] Shaykh Subhi al-Tufayli, former secretary general of the Hizballah terrorist organization, has again claimed that Israeli pilot Ron Arad was killed by his captors or died in captivity. In an interview with the Lebanese newspaper AL-DIYAR today, al-Tufayli said he does not believe that anyone stands to gain anything by detaining Arad. He added that the Syrians have denied that they are holding Arad; in fact, they are searching for him.
