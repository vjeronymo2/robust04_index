941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Bolivia, Brazil gas line tender - Contracts & ventures The state oil companies of Brazil and Bolivia have put out to international tender a contract to supply equipment and services for the planned Brazil-Bolivia gas pipe line. Work on the first stage of the pipeline, from Rio Grande in Bolivia to Porto Alegre in Brazil, is expected to start next August. The project is expected to use 2,200km of pipeline and will cost Dollars 2.2bn. The pipeline should provide income of Dollars 200m a year for Bolivia. The two companies have already selected a number of large international companies as strategic partners in the project.
