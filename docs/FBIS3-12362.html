Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] In the context of his contacts after the al-Ibrahimi Mosque massacre, Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat arrived in our country today, accompanied by an important delegation. [passage omitted] President 'Arafat will be received by State President Liamine Zeroual. [passage omitted] According to information gathered at the VIP lounge of the airport, 'Arafat's visit comes in the framework of inter-Arab consultation following the recent developments of events in the occupied territories in the wake of the massacre of Palestinians performing prayers at the al-Ibrahimi Mosque in al-Khalil [Hebron]. Directly after seeing State President Liamine Zeroual, Mr 'Arafat will leave Algeria, and it is expected that he will be making a statement in the VIP lounge dealing with the latest developments in the occupied territories and their effects on the negotiations process. It will also deal with the PLO's reaction to the American and French proposals, to the measures taken by the Israeli administration concerning the Palestinian demand that Israeli settlers be disarmed, and to the request for international protection of the Palestinians in the occupied territories. [passage omitted]
