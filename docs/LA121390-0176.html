December 13, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: GLENDORA;

EAGLE LAND'S PLAN OKD

The City Council approved a subdivision of 55.8 acres in northeast Glendora into 16 large lots for single-family homes.

The vote Tuesday was unanimous, with Councilman David Bodley absent.

The subdivision, proposed by Eagle Land Co. of Glendora, would extend Morgan Ranch Road east toward the Glendora border.

The council also approved an environmental impact report and grading plan for the proposed development.
