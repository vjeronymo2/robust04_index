930128 FT 28 JAN 93 / Pounds 95m Ulster textile investment A LEADING Far Eastern textile group announced yesterday it was investing Pounds 95m in a project in Northern Ireland, with the aim of boosting its presence in Europe. Texmaco-Polysindo, the Indonesian manufacturer of polyester filament yarn and garments, is locating production facilities at a site in Antrim formerly owned by Akzo, the Dutch chemical company. It hopes to create 900 jobs over the next three years.
