921128 FT 28 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Fleming Far Eastern asset value down Fleming Far Eastern Investment Trust had a net asset value per share of 219.5p at September 30 against 229.8p a year earlier. Gross revenue for the six months fell to Pounds 5.17m (Pounds 5.52m) with net revenue at Pounds 1.54m (Pounds 1.71m) for earnings of 0.98p (1.09p) per share.
