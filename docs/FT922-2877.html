920615 FT 15 JUN 92 / Construction Contracts: Building Taunton superstore WALLIS WESTERN, part of the CHB Group, has started work on a Pounds 5.5m contract for J Sainsbury to carry out main building works for a new superstore at Bathpool in Taunton, Somerset. The 6,527 sq metre store, adjacent to Junction 25 of the M5, includes a petrol station, coffee bar and extensive car parking, and is due for completion by November.
