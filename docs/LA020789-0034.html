February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

AUTO INSURANCE CONTROVERSY

Your article on inflated auto insurance claims was great. It showed that the real cause of high auto insurance rates is greedy accident "victims" and their equally greedy lawyers and doctors. But why did you wait until now to come out with this story? Why didn't you come out with it when it could have made a real impact -- before the elections?

Who knows, if you had, we might now have the no-fault type insurance we need to eliminate the higher costs brought on by such inflated claims.

ALFRED A. BARRIOS

Culver City
