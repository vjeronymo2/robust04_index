BFN [Text] A service brigade is to be established to absorb members of Umkhonto we Sizwe [Spear of the Nation; MK -- ANC Military Wing] and the defense forces of the former homelands who do not qualify for integration into the South African National Defense Force [SANDF]. A spokesman for the (Walmansdaal) base north of Pretoria, Colonel Johan Preller, is quoted by THE CITIZEN newspaper as saying that the volunteers will undergo training in skills such as bricklaying and mechanics for a period of 18 months. He said they could then be taken up in the auxiliary services of the SANDF. It was reported earlier that more than 3,000 former MK members would begin their training at (Walmansdaal) on Monday.
