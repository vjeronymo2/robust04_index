August 14, 1990, Tuesday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Barry Bonds and R.J. Reynolds, fined $1,500 apiece for missing the Pittsburgh Pirates' team-sponsored charity auction, plan to appeal.

Fans attending paid an admission fee plus an additional charge depending on the number of autographs they obtained.

"Charge a kid for an autograph?," Reynolds said. "I don't play that game."
