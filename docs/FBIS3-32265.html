Language: Spanish Article Type:CSO [Text] The General Budget of Revenues and Expenditures of the Republic for fiscal year 1994 will total 6,183,096,752 lempiras, according to the report on the matter that was the subject of an initial debate in the National Congress yesterday. Of that total, 67,094,494 lempiras is budgeted for the legislative branch, 65,551,100 for the judicial branch, and 6,024,951,158 for the executive branch. In addition, the Electoral Body (National Tribunal of Elections) will receive 25.5 million lempiras, while the Presidency of the Republic will be allocated 23,961,805 lempiras.
