BFN [Text] Asked how the next government should be created, Watanabe, former deputy prime minister and foreign minister, of the Liberal Democratic Party [LDP], in an interview with reporters at the Diet building on 25 June, stressed the need to create a "conservatives-conservatives alliance" -- composed of such conservative forces as Shinseito [Japan Renewal Party] and the LDP -- saying: "I hope a stable government will be formed with those who share the same views. Even if the right and left wingers get together, they will agree with the plan in principle but oppose it in practice. What we need are tolerate minds to iron out minor difference of opinions for the best common interests."
