940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Aberforth Smaller Basic net asset value per share of the Aberforth Smaller Companies Trust stood at 188.6p at the December 31, against 165.1p at the interim stage and 122.8p at end-1992. Total revenue for the year fell to Pounds 4.72m (Pounds 5.57m). Available revenue amounted to Pounds 2.46m (Pounds 3.27m), giving earnings per share of 4.36p (5.8p). A same-again final dividend of 2.5p is proposed to maintain the total at 4.6p.
