940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / World News in Brief: India delays test before Rao's US visit India said it had postponed a missile test ahead of a six-day visit by prime minister PV Narasimha Rao to the US, but said there was no intention to stop missile trials in spite of pressure from Washington. The US has been pressing both India and Pakistan to cap their nuclear programmes, but the action has caused a furore in the Indian media and political opposition. Rao is due to meet President Clinton on May 19. Rao tries to build bridge, Page 4
