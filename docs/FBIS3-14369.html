Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 27 Mar (INA)--Iraqi Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf met here today with Abdellatif Filali, Moroccan minister of state for foreign affairs. The two ministers discussed bilateral relations, ways to strengthen them, the current Arab situation, and the agenda of the current session of the Arab League Council. Al-Sahhaf also met with Tunisian Foreign Minister Habib Ben Yahya and discussed with him bilateral relations and a number of Arab issues of common interest. An INA correspondent in Cairo said that al-Sahhaf informed Filali and Ben Yahya of Iraq's efforts to lift the blockade, the developments that took place in the Security Council in Iraq's favor, and Iraq's clear stand on the Security Council resolutions.
