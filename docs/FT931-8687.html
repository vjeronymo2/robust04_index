930218 FT 18 FEB 93 / Central Circuits sold by receivers FORWARD Group, the circuit board maker based in Tamworth, Staffs, yesterday secured the future of 200 jobs in Telford, Shropshire, when it paid Pounds 1.7m for the business and assets of Central Circuits from the receivers, Mr Ken Jones and Mr Andrew Menzies of Robson Rhodes, accountants.
