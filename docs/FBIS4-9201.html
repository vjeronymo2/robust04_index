BFN [Excerpt] Knin, 21 Apr (TANJUG) -- The Serb Democratic Party [SDS] of Krajina has won the largest number of portfolios in the republic of Serb Krajina government. This party has won seven ministerial posts, while its leader, Milan Babic, has been appointed foreign affairs minister. The Serb Democratic Party of Serbian Lands has won two portfolios while the socialists and the social democrats have won one portfolio each. Six ministries, including the Ministries of Defense, Internal Affairs, and Finance will be headed by nonparty personalities. [passage omitted]
