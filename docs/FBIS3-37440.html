Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The State Duma, the lower house of the Russian parliament, has set up a commission to investigate the events of September 21-October 4, 1993. The commission's 11 members represent all parliamentary groups. It includes Yuriy Vlasov from the Russian Path group and Stanislav Govorukhin from the Democratic Party group. Meanwhile, the Liberal Democratic Party group drafted a resolution entitled "The Political and Economic Amnesty in Connection with the Adoption of the Constitution of the Russian Federation," which would quash the prosecution of all those involved in the attempted coup of August 1991, the Moscow riots of May 1, 1993, and the conservative revolt of October 3-4, 1993. The draft will be put before the State Duma on Thursday. The house took no decision on Wednesday on an amnesty resolution proposed by President Boris Yeltsin.
