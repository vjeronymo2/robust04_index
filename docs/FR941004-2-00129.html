SUMMARY: The persons listed in the information section of this notice have claims pending against the Islamic Republic of Iran which are before the Foreign Claims Settlement Commission (FCSC) for adjudication as authorized under Title V of the Foreign Relations Authorization Act, Fiscal Years 1986 and 1987 (Pub. L. 99-93, approved August 16, 1985, 99 Stat. 437 (50 U.S.C. 1701 note); the ``Iran Claims Act''), and the Settlement Agreement in Claims of Less than $250,000, Case No. 86 and Case No. B38, Award No. 483 (1990); the ``Settlement Agreement''). However, these persons have failed to inform the FCSC of their current addresses. The claims of the persons listed below will be dismissed by the FCSC, unless current addresses are provided to the FCSC by October 31, 1994.
