930827 FT 27 AUG 93 / EC inflation climbs two points The annual rate of inflation in the European Community rose two points to 3.5 per cent in July from June's 3.3 per cent, the first rise in more than a year, Reuter reports from Brussels. The EC said that the annual rate of inflation had fallen steadily since May 1992. In July last year it was already down to 3.9 per cent.
