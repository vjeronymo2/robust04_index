BFN [Text] In Sanaa, the Yemeni authorities today announced a new cease-fire as of midnight tonight. The Yemeni News Agency said that the Defense Council announced this new cease-fire initiative following a meeting held under the chairmanship President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. The news agency added that the U.S. and French military attaches will directly supervise the cease-fire in its first phase.
