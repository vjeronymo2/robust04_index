BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow May 27 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin addressed a meeting held in Moscow on the occasion of the day of border guards, expressing a principal position on problems of social welfare of servicemen and their families. Yeltsin pointed out that as the Russian president, he will not allow any social or legal cuts for people wearing shoulder straps. Yeltsin pointed out that social problems faced by servicemen are the result of the economic situation in the country. The Russian president heartily congratulated border guards on the occasion of the day of border guards, he pointed out that over the past 1.5 years, the title of the Hero of the Russian Federation was conferred upon six border guards, and over 400 border guards were awarded with medals and orders. During the meeting a minute of silence was observed in memory of border guards who were killed defending Russian borders.
