August 18, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Famous Amos Moves Headquarters: Famous Amos Chocolate Chip Cookie Corp. has moved a "lean corporate staff" of fewer than 20 employees to San Francisco from Torrance, according to Keith R. Lively, chief operating officer. Famous Amos, a pioneer in the premium cookie business, will maintain a two-person regional office in Southern California and will continue to produce its cookies in the area on a contract basis. Last year, Famous Amos was bought by the Shansby Group, a San Francisco-based buyout fund. Lively said nearly all the corporate officers were from the San Francisco area and "wanted to return."
