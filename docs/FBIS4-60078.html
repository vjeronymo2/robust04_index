BFN [Text] Australia will provide technical assistance of 40 crore 58 lakh taka, equivalent to $1.6 million, for the wheat development project of the Wheat Research Center of the Bangladesh Agricultural Research Institute. A memorandum of understanding to this effect was signed in Dhaka today. The Australian high commissioner in Bangladesh, (Kenneth W. Askiner), and the joint secretary of the Economic Relations Division of the Ministry of Finance, A.S.M. Alamgir, signed the memorandum on behalf of their respective governments. This technical assistance will be utilized for research and training and for increasing the institutional authority of the Wheat Research Center.
