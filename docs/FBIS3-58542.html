Language: German Article Type:BFN [Report signed "Kl.": "Record Results For Johannes Rau"] [Excerpt] Bielefeld -- With an impressive vote, which will, however, possibly only be valid for a short time, the North Rhine-Westphalian Social Democratic Party of Germany [SPD] confirmed Minister President Johannes Rau as its chairman for another two years. At the land party congress in Bielefeld, as a sign of gratitude for his work and additional backing for his candidacy for the office of federal president, Rau received 310 out of 312 votes (99.4 percent) and thus achieved the best results of his 16-year term in office as the chairman of the largest SPD land association.
