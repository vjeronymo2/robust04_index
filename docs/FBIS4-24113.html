BFN [Text] Lagos, June 23 (AFP) -- Hundreds of youths chanting anti-government slogans and calling for the immediate release of Chief Moshood Abiola late Thursday [23 June] held a peaceful march along major streets in the commercial nerve-centre of Lagos. They carried placards reading "release Abiola our president now," "Abacha must go," and "away with military regime." The police did not disrupt the march, which was the first physical reaction to the arrest of Chief Abiola in the morning hours of Thursday.
