BFN [Text] Budapest, 11 May (MTI) -- The Christian Democratic People's Party [KDNP] has asked its supporters and those of parties with a similar way of thinking to support the candidates of "nationally committed political forces" who qualified for the May 29 run-off of the parliamentary elections, the KDNP Executive Committee said today. The committee did not name any specific party in the appeal. After the committee's session, KDNP Chairman Laszlo Surjan said he had agreed with Hungarian Democratic Forum [MDF] Deputy Chairman Peter Boross and Chairman Lajos Fur on a proposal that an MDF candidate should withdraw in favor of a KDNP candidate in Nograd county, while a KDNP candidate should withdraw in favor of an MDF candidate in Nyirbator.
