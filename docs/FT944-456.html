941228 FT 28 DEC 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Johannesburg was mixed after drifting for most of the day in listless trade. The overall index finished 7.1 better at 5,809.8, industrials gained 5.7 at 6,931.9 and golds were 1.2 lower at 1,953.9. M-Net lost 50 cents to R14.50 after Friday's sharp gains following reports that it would merge its operations with the Swiss Richemont group.
