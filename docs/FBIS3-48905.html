Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, January 28 (KPL) -- Khammouane and Vietnam's Quang Binh Province have agreed to open two more border checkpoints in the District of Boualapha, namely Khun Se and Nong Ma. This is aimed to facilitate communication contacts and border trade. The decision was reached at a border meeting between this central Lao province and Quang Binh on January 26. At the meeting, the two sides also reached a consensus to inspect and repair the damaged demarcation posts.
