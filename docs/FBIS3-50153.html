Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Text] General Henning von Ondarza, commander in chief of NATO Allied Forces for Central Europe, today discussed in Prague concrete cooperation in the context of the Partnership for Peace plan. Gen. von Ondarza and Major General Jiri Nekvasil, chief of the General Staff of the Czech Army, agreed, among other things, that three Czech officers would attend a working meeting in NATO's Brussels Headquarters at the beginning of February. On the basis of bilateral agreements, an exercise of a Czech and Dutch motorized platoons will take place in our country as early as in March, and an exercise of a Czech and a French motorized platoon will take place at the end of May and the beginning of June.
