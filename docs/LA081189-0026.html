August 11, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Robert B. Wilhelm, managing director of San Francisco's Westin St. Francis hotel for the past 14 years, announced that he will retire Aug. 31, after his 62nd birthday. Wilhelm, a 40-year veteran of Westin Hotels & Resorts, began his career with the company as a cashier at the Benjamin Franklin Hotel in Seattle and served in Oregon, California, Montana, Colorado and Washington before going to the St. Francis.
