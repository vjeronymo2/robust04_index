Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Alexandra Ortiz for the "TV Today" newscast] [Text] Foreign Minister Noemi Sanin de Rubio abruptly returned to Santa Fe de Bogota this evening to evaluate the situation caused by the Venezuelan helicopter crash in Arauca. The foreign minister, who interrupted her vacation in England, told "TV Today" reporters that she will talk with President Cesar Gaviria and high-ranking military commanders to learn exactly what happened. She will also study reports from members of the Attorney General's Office and the Foreign Ministry commission that traveled to the crash site. The foreign minister also announced that a statement will be issued tomorrow by the Colombian Government in connection with the incident.
