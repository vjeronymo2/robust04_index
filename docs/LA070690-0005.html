July 6, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

LOTTERY WINNINGS

How much have you won on the California Lotto this year?

My wife, Helen, is now ahead exactly $520, and that's only since the first week of January. After years of trying, the most she ever won was a fiver (dollars, that is) and that was last December.

So, instead of going to the local 7-Eleven, she started putting her $10-twice-a-week "investments" into a glass jar.

That's how she got $520 ahead, last count.

Pretty soon, now, she's planning on taking that grubstake to Las Vegas where she's bound to be better entertained and, possibly, even win a little.

JACK B. KENNETT

Laguna Hills
