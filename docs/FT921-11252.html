920201 FT 01 FEB 92 / Finance & The Family: Savings rates cut HALIFAX and Nationwide building societies have cut interest rates on savings and chequebook current accounts from 1 February. Halifax has cut only particular tiers on certain accounts: on 90-Day Extra, for example, it will pay 10.95 per cent, down from 11.0 per cent on accounts over Pounds 50,000. Nationwide Anglia has cut its savings rates by an average of 0.53 percentage points, but has increased rates for Asset Reserve, its small business and club account, by between 1.1 per cent and 0.1 per cent. The biggest reduction comes on the Nationwide Tessa accounts where rates fall by 0.6 percentage points.
