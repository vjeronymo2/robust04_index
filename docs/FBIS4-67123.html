BFN [Text] Baltasar Garzon has told a news conference why he tendered his irrevocable resignation as secretary of state for the national plan on drugs. Justice and Interior Minister [Juan Alberto] Belloch did not agree to his request for additional powers to combat drugs, and that is why Garzon decided to resign. At his news conference, he also announced that he is resigning his seat as a deputy. In the last elections Garzon ran on the Spanish Socialist Workers' Party list in number two position, after Felipe Gonzalez.
