930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / UK Company News: FT-SE Actuaries changes THE FT-SE Actuaries Industry Classification has agreed the following changes to constituent companies' classifications, effective from October 1: J Saville Gordon to Property (FT-A group 69) from Metals and Metal Forming (8); Sidlaw to Packaging, Paper and Printing (31) from Oil and Gas (51). In addition, the following companies will be moved to new groups in the London Share Service on October 1: Aegon to Insurance (Life) from Insurance (Composite); Berry Birch & Noble to Other Fiancials from Insurance Brokers; Hawtin to Congolmerates from Textiles; Sleepy Kids to Media from Hotels and Leisure; West Trust to Food Manufacturers from Textiles.
