940414 FT 14 APR 94 / UK Company News: Scruttons Scruttons, the OTC-traded port, shipping, engineering and security company, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 882,000 from turnover of Pounds 30.1m for 1993. That compared with profits - restated for FRS 3 - of Pounds 417,000 from turnover of Pounds 26.9m in 1992. Earnings came out at 14.5p (7.2p) and a same again final dividend of 7p is proposed to maintain the total at 13p.
