931008 FT 08 OCT 93 / UK Company News: McLaughlin McLaughlin & Harvey, the County Antrim-based construction group, said that Mr Eric Bell and Mr Philip Sykes of BDO Binder Hamlyn had been appointed joint administrative receivers. The company's shares were suspended at 28p. McLaughlin, which incurred losses of Pounds 6.8m (Pounds 5.4m) in 1992, had earlier said it was in consultation with its principal bankers.
