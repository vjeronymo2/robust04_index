940409 FT 09 APR 94 / Italian economy 'turning corner' Conditions are right for Italian economic recovery, Mr Carlo Azeglio Ciampi, the prime minister, said yesterday, writes Andrew Hill in Milan. A report by the budget minister confirmed that gross domestic product in Italy slipped 0.7 per cent in 1993, and forecast an increase of 1.3 per cent this year. Mr Ciampi - who has only a few more days to serve as prime minister - said the economy had passed the turning point. The report also pointed to a drop in inflation to 3.5 per cent.
