920429 FT 29 APR 92 / UK Company News: UB develops sweet tooth in Italy United Biscuits has established a presence in the Italian confectionery business by acquiring 74 per cent of Aura, a privately-owned manufacturer of chocolate and praline confectionery, for an undisclosed cash sum. Aura, which will operate as part of Terry's, UB's confectionery subsidiary, had sales last year of Pounds 17m and is said to be profitable. It has a factory near Genoa with 150 employees. UB plans to distribute some of its existing confectionery range through Aura and to supply Aura's products through its network in other parts of Europe. UB expects eventually to take full control of the Italian company.
