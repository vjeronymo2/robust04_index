BFN [Text] President Akayev has sent a message of greetings to Russian President Yeltsin. Akayev congratulated Yeltsin on the occasion of the anniversary of the declaration of independence and stressed that Russia is, will, and should be Kyrgyzstan's main partner. Akayev also expressed his gratitude to Yeltsin for an interest free credit of 400 million rubles which has been granted to Kyrgyzstan's Chu Oblast by Moscow Oblast. Moscow oblast is now celebrating festival days. The festival will contribute to further strengthening of fraternal ties between the two countries.
