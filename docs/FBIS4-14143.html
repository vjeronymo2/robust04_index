BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Husayn Sulayman Abu-Salih received in Khartoum in his office at noon today the British charge d'affaires. Bilateral relations and ways of strengthening and developing them, were discussed at the meeting. The charge d'affaires reaffirmed the British Government's support for the Intergovernmental Authority on Drought and Development initiative to establish peace in the country. He said Britain did not recognize anything other than the present borders of Sudan, nor did it give any consideration to declarations by the rebel John Garang about what he called the modern Sudan.
