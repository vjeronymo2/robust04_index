September 6, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

CONTROVERSY OVER HAYDEN AND 'BIG GREEN'

I am one voter who will try to keep my prejudices from coloring my vote on Proposition 128.

What remains of our environment is too precious to allow my personal bias to bleach Big Green into Grungy Gray.

Besides, I am always suspicious of writers of anonymous letters or of anonymous radio advertisements. Are the writers of the advertisements too ashamed to acknowledge authorship and to obey the law which requires identification of the sponsors of political ads?

MARGARET LORENZ ROMANI

Los Angeles
