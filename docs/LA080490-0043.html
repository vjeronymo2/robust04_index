August 4, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

BODY FOUND NEAR I-5

The body of a woman was found Friday in the brush along the Main Street on-ramp to Interstate 5 in Chula Vista.

The woman's identity was not available.

A Border Patrol agent discovered the body at 2:55 p.m. and notified Chula Vista police, who called in the Metro Homicide Task Force, officials said.

Police are investigating the death as a homicide or traffic fatality.

No further information was available.
