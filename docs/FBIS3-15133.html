Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] This year, according to a plan by the Finance Ministry, defense expenditure will remain virtually unchanged compared with last -- 20.4 percent of the overall volume of expenditure. At the same time financial support for the territories will grow substantially -- to 13.5 percent. This is connected with the transition to a new system of inter-relations between the center and the component parts of the Federation. But spending on sociocultural measures and science remain at the old level -- 2.2 percent.
