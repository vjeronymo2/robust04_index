Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vietnam's political leader, Do Muoi, arrived in Kuala Lumpur today for a four-day official visit aimed at intensifying bilateral and economic relations between Kuala Lumpur and Hanoi. The 77-year old Secretary General of Vietnam's Communist Party was met on arrival at the airport by Foreign Minister Datuk Abdullah Ahmad Badawi. Accompanied by a 33-member delegation including politicians, businessmen, and journalists, Do Moui is scheduled to meet Datuk Sri Dr. Mahathir for talks on bilateral, regional, and international issues in the afternoon. During his stay here he will attend a briefing at the Petronas [National Petroleum Corporation] headquarters and tour refineries in Melaka. He will also visit the Proton automobile plant in Shah Alam, near Kuala Lumpur.
