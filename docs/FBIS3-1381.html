Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The head of state, General Sani Abacha, left Abuja this morning at the head of a Nigerian delegation to the summit of the Group of 15 which opens tomorrow in New Delhi, India. He is being accompanied by the ministers of external affairs, Alhaji Babagana Kingibe; works and housing, Alhaji Lateef Jakande; and that of finance, Dr. Kalu Idika Kalu, as well as other top government officials. A Radio Nigeria State House correspondent reports that the summit of the Group of 15, which is an economic community of developing nations, will end on Wednesday [30 March].
