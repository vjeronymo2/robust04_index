930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / World News in Brief: Everest target for UK woman climber Rebecca Stephens, a 31-year-old FT journalist who started climbing only four years ago, aims to become the first British woman to scale Everest. The expedition, which leaves for the Himalayas today, will follow the route taken by the first successful conquerors of the world's highest peak 40 years ago.
