BFN [Text] A military source made the following statement this morning: At 0200 today, an Israeli enemy commando force, using two helicopters, kidnapped resistance official al-Haj Mustafa al-Dirani from his house in Qasr Naba village. The Israeli force used the road separating Kasrawan and al-Biqa' to carry out its action. It should be recalled that Israeli helicopters were monitored flying at high altitude last night. The Lebanese army and Syrian forces were placed on alert. Patrols pursued the Israeli commando force in Qasr Naba and al-Furzul. Ground defenses opened fired at the helicopters as they were retreating toward the sea. This operation is part of the enemy's piracy and continued violations of international laws and norms.
