BFN [Text] The police have raided three right-wing weapons caches near Pretoria and Brits. Newspaper reports say the weapons were found on farms near Brits and in Lynnwood, Pretoria. The latest finds follow the arrests of 32 people in connection with alleged right-wing terror attacks. Members of the AWB [Afrikaner Resistance Movement] Ystergarde [Iron Guard] are among the suspects.
