BFN [Text] Beijing, April 19 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese Foreign Ministry Spokesman here today expressed regret about the recent permission by the Japanese Government to allow the Dalai Lama to pass through Japan and carry out illicit political activities there. "We oppose the Dalai Lama, by any status, going in for political activities in other countries, to disrupt China's integrity and its national unity," said the spokesman. He said that China regrets that the Japanese Government, in disregard of the representations of the Chinese Government, recently allowed the Dalai Lama to pass through the territory of Japan and conduct illicit political activities.
