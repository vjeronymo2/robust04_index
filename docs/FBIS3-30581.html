Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Our Ministry of Foreign Affairs [MSZ] has received with satisfaction the approval by the Ukrainian parliament of the Lisbon Protocol, which provides, among other things, for disarming nuclear missiles stationed in that country. Wladyslaw Klaczynski, head of the MSZ press office, told us about a statement on the matter. [Begin Klaczynski recording] The ministry stresses that this is a significant step toward consolidation of world and European security. This step will undoubtedly open wider possibilities for comprehensive cooperation of the Ukraine with the entire Western world. For us, for Poland, this is an indispensable factor for stabilization in the region. We would like to express hope, and the ministry expresses such hope, that the speedy implementation of the right and very important decision will take place.
