920530 FT 30 MAY 92 / On the road McLaren, the British racing-car manufacturer, yesterday launched its first road car in Monaco. Priced at Pounds 530,000, the F1 is a three-seat, mid-engined coupe with a central driving position. It is powered by a 550hp V12 engine built by BMW Motorsport in Germany. Production will start late next year at a rate of 50 a year
