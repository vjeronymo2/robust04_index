Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 27 (KCNA) -- An agreement on cooperation between the DPRK Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Mali Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Malians Abroad, and African Integration was signed in Bamako, Mali, on March 22. The agreement was signed by Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs of the DPRK Kim Yong-il and General Secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Malians Abroad, and African Integration Noumou Diakite.
