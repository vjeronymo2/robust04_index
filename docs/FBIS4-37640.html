BFN [Text] In an exclusive interview with our correspondent today Interior Minister Besharati divulged more information on the recent bomb explosion by the terrorist group, the hypocrites [monafeqin -- reference to Mojahedin-e Khalq], at Imam Reza's shrine. Referring to the fact that the number of martyrs in the incident was 24, the Interior Minister said: Nine of the martyrs were from Mashhad and 15 were from other towns. He said the amount of explosives used was 10 lb, about 4.5 kg, of the C-4 variety. He added: The bomb's timer was set for 10 minutes. He added: Thus far a number of suspects have been arrested in this connection.
