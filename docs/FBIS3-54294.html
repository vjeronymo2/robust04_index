Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In an interview published in Kuwaiti paper AL-SIYASAH today, Yemeni Prime Minister Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas has voiced concern for his personal safety if he returns to Sanaa, in spite of the political agreement that has settled the political crisis between President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and the leaders of southern Yemen. Al-'Attas emphasized the need to obtain direct guarantees to implement the agreement and to charge a minicommittee with this task.
