930128 FT 28 JAN 93 / International Company News: Freeport gains Indonesian lift FREEPORT-McMoRan, the Louisiana-based consolidated metals, mining, fertiliser and natural resource company, reported fourth-quarter earnings of Dollars 19.1m, or 14 cents a share, up from 12.6m, or 9 cents, in the corresponding 1991 quarter, writes Laurie Morse in Chicago. The company said earnings for the quarter and the year were enhanced by strong copper and gold sales from the expanded Indonesian mining and milling operations of its copper and gold company subsidiary. Fourth-quarter sales were Dollars 413m, compared with Dollars 369m in the year-ago period. For the year, Freeport-McMoRan had earnings of Dollars 169.1m, or Dollars 1.17, compared with Dollars 40m, or 29 cents, in 1991.
