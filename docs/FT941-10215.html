940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Govett Emerging sets conversion rate Govett Emerging Markets Investment Trust yesterday announced that following its listing in November, all the C shares will be converted into ordinary shares at the close of business on February 25 at a rate of 0.677. One new warrant will be issued for every five new ordinary shares arising on conversion. The total number of new ordinary shares and new warrants to be issued will be 11.8m and 2.4m respectively. Dealings in the new shares and warrants are expected to start on February 28.
