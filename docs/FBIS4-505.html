BFN [Text] The National Peacekeeping Force members based at Koeberg in the Western Cape have completed their training five days ahead of schedule. They are to take a week's leave before starting duty. A spokesman for the force said the contingent was just over a thousand-strong and would be deployed a week before the election. The force is made up of former MK [Umkhonto we Sizwe -- Spear of the Nation, ANC military wing] cadres, and members of the Defense Force and the police.
