930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / London Stock Exchange: Inchcape tumbles International trading group Inchcape tumbled 27 to 538p to register the day's biggest drop in the FT-SE 100 after it reported interim results at the bottom end of market expectations, leaving analysts to downgrade full year forecasts. Profits rose from Pounds 117.1m to Pounds 130.9m, but as well as being less than hoped for by the market, were accompanied by a statement saying market conditions remained tough. Investors were also disappointed by the slight improvement in the dividend and analysts moved to downgrade annual figures. The list of reductions included that from BZW, where Mr Nyran Scott - Malden cut his full year estimate by Pounds 11m to Pounds 277m excluding exceptionals. However, he remains positive about the company and said: 'The results were disappointing but the fundamentals remain intact.'
