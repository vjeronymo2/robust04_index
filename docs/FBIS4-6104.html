BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 16 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Today staying here on an official visit at the invitation of the President of Mongolia P. Ochirbat the Governor General of Australia B. Hayden has met with the Minister of External Relations of Mongolia T. Gombosuren. Also the Governor General of Australia B. Hayden was paid visits by the head of the National Development Department and Minister of State C. Ulaan, as well as the leaders of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, the National Democratic and Social Democratic parties.
