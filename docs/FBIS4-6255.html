BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 19 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song on April 19 received the inspector-general of Armed Forces of Libya, Mustafa al-Haroubi, who is a special envoy of the leader of the great September first revolution of the great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya and his entourage on a visit to Korea. Present there were Vice-premier and Foreign Minister Kim Yong- nam, chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army Choe Kwang, Vice-minister of Foreign Affairs Chang Song-kil and acting Secretary of the People's Committee of the Libyan People's Bureau in Korea Ramadan Muhammad al-Ra'Ubi. The special envoy warmly congratulated President Kim Il-song on his birthday, wishing him good health and a long life, and conveyed to him a personnel letter of His Excellency Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, leader of the great September first revolution of the great Socialist People's Libyan Abab Jamahiriya. Expressing thanks for them President Kim Il-song conversed with the guests in a cordial and friendly atmosphere. President Kim Il-song arranged a luncheon for the guests.
