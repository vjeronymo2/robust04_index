BFN [Text] Tirana -- The development and current situation of the free press in Albania, based on the experience of Albanian journalists themselves, was the topic of a discussion held in the Palace of Congresses with a group of Albanian journalists representing various newspapers, organized by the delegation of European Democratic Union -- of which Professor Doctor Andreas Khol was a member. Acknowledging the birth of a free press in Albania over the last few years, the journalists highlighted in their discussions the existence of a dual tendency: on the one hand, abuse of the freedom of the press and, on the other, efforts to restrict it. Some of the speakers pointed out that the media in general was still politicized, and the independent press remained a small minority. It is precisely the latter -- having financial support from neither political forces nor government -- that is extremely endangered by the merciless law of insolvency of the market economy.
