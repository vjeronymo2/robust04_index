May 25, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Dick Trickle grabbed the pole for Saturday's Champion Spark Plug 300 stock car race at Charlotte, N.C., Motor Speedway, driving his Pontiac at 168.219 m.p.h. around the 1.5-mile oval track.

Harry Gant, at 167.400 m.p.h., will line up on the outside of the front row.
