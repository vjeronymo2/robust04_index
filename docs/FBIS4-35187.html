BFN [Text] Tunis, 28 May (MENA) -- The PLO Executive Committee, meeting under the chairmanship of Yasir 'Arafat, today approved the Palestinian National Authority's program of action for the coming stage. Executive Committee member Samir Ghawshah said after the meeting that the Committee introduced some amendments to the program that the Palestinian National Authority Council had discussed and approved last night. Ghawshah added that this is an interim program that will be used until general elections are held in the West Bank and Gaza. He declared that the program demonstrates that the National Authority will carry out its tasks through the concerned departments in a spirit of national commitment and will rely on Palestinian experts in various fields. Ghawshah announced that the Palestinian National Authority is committed to all the agreements, treaties, and charters signed by the PLO and that it will seek to implement the Palestinian-Israeli Declaration of Principles, "the Gaza-Jericho Agreement," as a first step toward the restoration of the Palestinian people's legitimate rights. He said the program approved today emphasizes Palestine's Arabness and the National Authority's desire to nurture close ties with the Arab countries. He noted that the program also stresses the National Authority's adherence to UN resolutions, particularly Security Council Resolutions 242 and 338.
