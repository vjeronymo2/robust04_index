BFN [Text] In compliance with the GATT agreement, Thailand will not promulgate new laws or regulations to discriminate against foreign nationals in the service industry. Minister of Finance Tharin Nimmanhemin said that it is a basic commitment among member countries signing the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade. Minister Tharin said that liberalization of service industry is different from other businesses. He said it involves licenses issued for businesses. He said the Thai Government plans to allow aliens to enjoy the same rights as Thai nationals in doing business in Thailand. He said the Finance Ministry will allow over five licensees of the Bangkok International Bank Facilities, or BIBF, to transform into commercial banks by 1997.
