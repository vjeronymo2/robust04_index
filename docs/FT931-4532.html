930310 FT 10 MAR 93 / World News In Brief: Middle East peace talks to resume in April Middle East peace talks - stalled since Israel expelled 415 Palestinians to Lebanon in December - are to resume in Washington on April 19, according to Israel's state television. The US State Department had decided on the date, the report said, and had 'unofficially' notified the partners to the talks - Israel, Syria, Lebanon, Jordan, and the Palestinians.
