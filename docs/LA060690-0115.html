June 6, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SHOCKING: South Coast Rep's revival of "Frankie and Johnny in the Clair de Lune" at the Singapore Festival of Arts includes raw language, partial nudity and simulated sex. . . . Such elements are almost unheard of in Asia on the legitimate stage. But officials simply labeled the play "adult fare" and decided not to check theatergoers' ages. Tony Marcano and Shannon Sands
