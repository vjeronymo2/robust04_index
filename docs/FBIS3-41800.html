Language: English Article Type:BFN [UK press release from the Scottish Office: "latest AIDS Figures for Scotland"] [Excerpt] An additional eight AIDS cases were reported in Scotland during October making the cumulative total 446. The figures issued by the Scottish Office show that two further deaths from the disease were reported during October, the cumulative total therefore rises to 303. In the UK the number of reported cases of AIDS has risen to 8,252 -- an increase of 137 over the September 1993 figures. The total number of deaths from the disease reached 5,250 last month compared with 5,153 in September. Of the 446 reported cases in Scotland 44 percent are related to sexual intercourse between men and 33 percent to injecting drug use. The comparable figures for the rest of the UK are 76 percent and three percent, respectively. [Passage on notes to news editors omitted]
