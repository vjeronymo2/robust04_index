Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Mr. Siye Abraha, minister of defense, called on donor governments and organizations to assist the efforts being made to rehabilitate members of the defense force of the transitional government, who would be reduced as part of the reorganization of the new national defense force representing all nations and nationality groups. The defense minister made the call here last Thursday [10 February] at a briefing he gave to ambassadors and representatives of international donor organizations on ways and means of rehabilitating members of the defence force to be sacked in the restructuring process. Ambassadors and representatives of international donor organizations who attended the meeting pledged to assist government efforts of restructuring the country's defense force.
