940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / Sunderland FC seeks investors Sunderland football club, heading for a loss of up to Pounds 2m in the current year on turnover of about Pounds 5m, has advertised for substantial investors. The club says it is seeking new investors to help it realise its ambitions. Club secretary Mr Geoff Davidson said yesterday: 'We aren't really looking for anybody prepared to put in less than Pounds 500,000.'
