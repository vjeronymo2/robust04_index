940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / International Company News: Genentech upbeat Faster profits growth at the end of last year at Genentech, the US biotechnology company, is likely to continue, into this year, Mr Kirk Raab, president and chief executive, said, writes Richard Waters. Fourth-quarter net income was Dollars 18.7m, or 16 cents a share, against Dollars 6.2m, or 5 cents.
