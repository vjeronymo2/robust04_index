BFN [Text] Reports from Prishtina confirm that at about 1100 today, large Serbian forces blocked all entries and exits to the market of the capital of the Republic of Kosova. Thousands of people were in the market simply trading, the majority preparing for the [Muslim] festival of Bairam. This Serbian police operation lasted for more than four hours. It is reported that the Albanian traders had more than 500 German marks stolen. In addition to this thievery, the Serbian police arrested many young Albanians and sent them to the command headquarters of the Serbian security bodies in Prishtina.
