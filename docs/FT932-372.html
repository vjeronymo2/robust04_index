930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Alvis approval The Pounds 17.1m sale of Alvis' electro-optics business to Avimo Singapore, its 51 per cent subsidiary, has been approved by minority shareholders. The defence contractor will receive a first payment of 14.9m Avimo Singapore shares and Pounds 1.71m cash in July and a further Pounds 6.49m in mid-September.
