Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Banja Luka, 13 Mar (TANJUG)--Despite Bairam, members of the Muslim Army resumed their fierce attacks on Srpska republic army positions on the Brcko front [vital Serb corridor connecting Serbia and Serbs in Croatia and Bosnia, which passes through the town of Brcko] this morning, the information service of the 1st Corps of the Srpska republic army reported. The attack was launched at 0530 this morning when Muslim units from Gornja Brka, nine kilometers southwest of Brcko, fired about 20 82-mm and 120-mm mortar shells. Another 15 mortar shells have been fired on the Ulice district from the direction of the Bukovac train station, west of Brcko. Muslim forces also launched a fierce 82-mm mortar attack in the direction of Lipovac. HVO [Croatian Defense Council] units have joined the attacks, opening fierce infantry fire from the direction of Kopanica in the wider area of Orasje [Bosnian Croat-held municipality north of the Serb corridor] on Serb positions in Krepsic. [passage omitted] Regular units of the Republic of Croatia have not been withdrawn from former Bosnia-Herzegovina even though the UN Security Council deadline expired on 17 February.
