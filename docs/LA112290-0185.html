November 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SCHOOLS SWAP STUDENTS

Sixth-graders from the Sinai Akiba Academy and the St. James School are taking turns visiting each other's schools to participate in religious services and talk about stereotypes, prejudice and each other's beliefs .

The first event took place at the Jewish school in Westwood on Nov. 6, with the next scheduled for Tuesday at St. James' Mid-Wilshire campus.

Plans call for a similar exchange of second-graders in December.
