Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Svetlana Ivanova] [Text] St. Petersburg, Feb 25 (TASS) -- Secret police in the second biggest Russian city said on Friday it had arrested a group of terrorists and found out that they had planned an attempt on the life of Mayor Anatoliy Sobchak and an act of terror against the government building in Moscow. Head of the anti-terrorist unit of the St. Petersburg Counterintelligence Service Aleksandr Kuznetsov told reporters that on February 2 five terrorists were caught red-handed after they had seized five revolvers and 2,000 cartridges during an attack on the guards of the "Zarya" enterprise. According to Kuznetsov, all the five participated in the armed opposition rebellion in Moscow last October. He identified their leader as Captain 3rd Rank in reserve Aleksandr Bogdanov.
