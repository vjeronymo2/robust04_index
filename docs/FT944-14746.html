941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / Observer: Undercovering More funny goings on in the Lloyd's of London insurance bazaar, where gossip of rifts between chairman David Rowland and his chief executive, Peter Middleton, continues to flare up despite repeated denials. The suspicion at the insurance market's Lime Street headquarters that a few angry Lloyd's Names are trying to cause mischief by spreading rumours has credibility - after all, Lloyd's is turning the screws on thousands of lossmaking members who owe it money. But would they get involved in burgling the chief executive's mansion? As Middleton should know, that sort of thing only happens in spy thrillers.
