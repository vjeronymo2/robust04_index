BFN [Text] Khartoum, 29 May (SUNA) -- A ship loaded with 1,800 tonnes of foodstuffs will leave from Kosti in the White Nile State to Tonga and Fanjak in southern Sudan, said Dr. Bakhit Abdullah Ya'qub, the commissioner for refugees and rehabilitation. He pointed out in a statement to AL-SUDAN AL-HADITH daily newspaper of the day that another ship will leave to Malakal in the Upper Nile State loaded with agricultural input and equipments for the rehabilitation of Malakal Hospital, saying that the coming days will witness a relief airlift to Wau.
