BFN [Text] The State of Kuwait called today for an end to the fighting in Yemen and urged self-restraint to spare the blood and lives of its sons. It hoped that logic and wisdom will prevail as a means to overcome the acute crisis in the country. An authoritative source at the Foreign Ministry has told the Kuwaiti News Agency that Kuwait is watching with concern the regrettable development of the situation in the Yemen Republic and is urging Yemeni brothers to exercise self-restraint to end the fighting and spare the blood and lives of the country's sons. The source has added: Kuwait is hoping that logic and wisdom will prevail in order to overcome this acute crisis.
