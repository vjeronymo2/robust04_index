BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- The German Cabinet today authorized Federal Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel to vote in favor of the enlargement of the European Union [EU] at a vote to be taken by the EU members in Brussels on 16 May. As expected, the Government decision was unanimous, Government Spokesman Dieter Vogel announced. The European Parliament has already voted in favor of the admission of Finland, Norway, Austria, and Sweden to the EU.
