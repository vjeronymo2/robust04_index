940528 FT 28 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Everards Brewery Concentration on the more profitable elements of the business and tight cost controls helped Everards Brewery, the Leicester-based independent brewer, achieve pre-tax profits up from Pounds 539,000 to Pounds 1.5m for the 26 weeks to March 26. Turnover was slightly lower at Pounds 17.2m (Pounds 17.7m). Earnings per share were 48.3p (23p).
