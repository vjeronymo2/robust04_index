920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Celestion goes back in the black Celestion Industries, a maker of clothes and electronic consumer goods, which supplies Marks and Spencer, has returned to the black with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 34,000 for the first half of 1992 compared with a loss of Pounds 771,000. The loss for the whole of last year was Pounds 1.06m. The company announced the acquisition of Vaga, which also supplies Marks and Spencer, for Pounds 2.04m.
