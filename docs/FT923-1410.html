920923 FT 23 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Seafield Resources rises to Pounds 190,000 Strong results from its interest in the Victor gas field helped Seafield Resources, the oil and gas exploration and production company, jump to a net profit of Pounds 190,000 for the six months to June 30 against a loss of Pounds 21,000 last time. Turnover was up to Pounds 3.62m and earnings per share came out at 0.4p against losses of 0.3p. The company joined the USM in December last year.
