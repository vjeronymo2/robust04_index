BFN [Text] Seoul, June 6 (YONHAP) -- The U.N. Command called on North Korea Monday to send a delegate to the official meeting of senior secretaries of the Military Armistice Commission [MAC] to discuss its recent unilateral withdrawal from MAC, the command said. The command, citing clause 61 of the Armistice Agreement which stipulates that any changes require the consent of the two sides' commanders, noted that the commander of the U.N. forces has not concurred with North Korea's unilateral alteration of the agreement.
