September 22, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

THE WORLD

Palestinians stoned a bus carrying West German tourists through Bethlehem and injured two of them, the Israeli army said. The attack on the tourists was the second in two days by activists in the uprising in the occupied areas. Several West Germans were slightly hurt Wednesday night when their bus was stoned in Bethlehem. Tourists have become a target since Israeli troops disguised as tourists shot and killed an activist in Bethlehem last month.
