Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Munich (DPA) -- Roman Herzog, president of the Federal Constitutional Court, will run for election 23 May as federal president. In a fax message to Christian Social Union [CSU] Chairman Theo Waigel, Herzog said today that he is prepared "to run for election and to assume the office of federal president if successful." CSU General Secretary Erwin Huber stated this at the Munich press club. Leading members of the Christian Democratic Union and the CSU nominated Herzog as their joint candidate to succeed Richard von Weizsaecker yesterday evening.
