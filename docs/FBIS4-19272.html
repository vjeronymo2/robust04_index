BFN [Text] Robert Lamb, the U.S. State Department's special coordinator on the Cyprus issue, left Cyprus empty-handed this morning. Lamb had a lengthy meeting with Turkish Cypriot leader Rauf Denktas yesterday. In a statement to reporters afterward, Lamb said that Denktas still has problems with the informal UN document of 21 March. Pressured by reporters, the U.S. diplomat said his meetings in the occupied area reached no conclusion, adding that he is neither a negotiator nor did he bring any new proposals.
