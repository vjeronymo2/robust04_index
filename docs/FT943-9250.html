940812 FT 12 AUG 94 / Record month for Heathrow airport Last month was the busiest ever at London's Heathrow airport where more than 5m passengers helped to push passenger numbers at BAA's UK airports to a record 9.1m. The Heathrow figure was 8.7 per cent up from July last year. Gatwick's figures rose 7.1 per cent to 2.4m, Stansted 21.4 per cent to 367,000, Southampton 11.2 per cent to 48,200, Glasgow 15.3 per cent to 690,000 and Edinburgh 10.8 per cent to 299,000. Aberdeen airport fell 3.8 per cent to 199,000.
