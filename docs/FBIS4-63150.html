BFN [Text] A lull continues in the zone of the Armenian-Azeri conflict, Interfax was told on Thursday [19 May] by a spokesman for the Defense Army staff of Nagorno-Karabakh. The two sides are observing the cease-fire from 0000 hours of 17 May. A source in the Azeri Defense Ministry also reported to INTERFAX on the relative calm in the zone of the conflict. The source said the Armenians had fired on 18 May and on the morning of the 19th at the positions of the Azeri Army in the Terter and Geranboy directions. Some border villages of Azerbaijan were shelled from the territory of Armenia. The source claimed that the Azeri Army, in compliance with the conditions of the truce, did not retaliate.
