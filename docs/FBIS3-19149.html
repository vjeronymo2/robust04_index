Language: Finnish Article Type:BFN [Text] In Helsinki, Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin has assured that Russia will fully repay its debts to Finland, so that this question, which has been a stumbling block in the relations between the two countries, will be resolved. Prime Minister Chernomyrdin today started a two-day official visit to Finland. The first day's talks have concentrated on the bilateral questions and especially on questions of economy and trade. An agreement was signed today with which Russia will pay 450 million Finnish markkas [FMk] of its debt with arms deliveries. Finland's receivables from Russia are altogether nearly FMk8 billion. Prime Minister Esko Aho was of the opinion that the payment of the debt to the Bank of Finland with arms deliveries was an excellent arrangement that suits Finland well.
