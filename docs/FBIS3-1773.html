Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 28 (XINHUA) -- A national working conference on China's civil aviation opened here today. Premier Li Peng sent a letter of greetings to the conference, calling on the staff members of the civil aviation services to enhance the sense of "safety first, punctual flights and best quality services". He noted that China's civil aviation services are witnessing rapid growth. And he urged China's aviation services to deepen reform, carry out readjustment, and improve management so as to open up a new era in their work. At the conference, Chen Guangyi, director of Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC), said that last year China's aviation services transported 33.71 million passengers and 696,000 tons of goods and mails, respectively up by 16.8 and 21 percent over the previous year. Last year the air services in China also opened 63 new air routes and completed some airport extension projects. Chen said that China's civil aviation services must practise scientific management with high standards and strict measures. He also addressed the conference about the measures in the improvement of service quality in civil aviation services. On the reform of civil aviation services, he said that the first step will focus on the quality of service. He called for streamlining the relations between various airlines and subsidiaries and perfecting the contract system. He said that the second step will focus on solutions to some deep-lying problems involving the whole administrative system and the functions of the various civil aviation administrations.
