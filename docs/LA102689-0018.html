October 26, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: DANCE

This weekend's engagement of SLASK, the National Folk Ballet of Poland, has been canceled because tour producer Zofia Sklepinski and company members were injured in an automobile accident in Philadelphia. The group was to perform Friday, Saturday and Sunday at the Wiltern Theatre. Refunds are available at the box office. The national tour began Sept. 17 in New Jersey and will resume Nov. 3 in Detroit. ALEENE MacMINN
