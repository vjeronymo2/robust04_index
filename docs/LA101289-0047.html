October 12, 1989, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO EARNINGS

Mitek Systems: The San Diego-based manufacturer of computer peripheral devices that meet TEMPEST government security standards, said that it expects to report about $18 million revenue for the fiscal year ended Sept. 30, a 70% increase over 1988 sales. The company did not disclose its profit expectations. The company will report audited financial results in November, president Phillip Thomas said.
