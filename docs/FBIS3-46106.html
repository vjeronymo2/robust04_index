Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Summary] According to the statistical data provided by the provincial statistical bureau, in 1993 the industrial enterprises at or above the township level throughout Hebei Province realized 162.1 billion yuan of total output value calculated in current prices, which showed a 17.53 percent increase over 1992 and ranked the province 14th in the country. The rate of production and sale among their products reached 98.19 percent; this ranked the province third in the country behind Shanghai and Tianjin municipalities.
