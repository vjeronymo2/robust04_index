July 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: QUIBBLES & BITS

. . . Nelson Entertainment's "The Taking of Beverly Hills" wrapped earlier this month on locations outside Mexico City -- but not before bandidos held up an armored van on the way to the set, getting away with $50,000 worth of pesos intended for crew members being paid in cash.

. . . Get out your handkerchiefs: In the just-out "Ghost," Demi Moore has at least seven major crying jags following the death of her true love, Patrick Swayze. Meanwhile, hunk Swayze puts in three shirtless scenes.
