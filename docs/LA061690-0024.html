June 16, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

DODGERS SHOULD CENTER ATTENTION ON BULLPEN

It has come to my attention that the Dodgers have four center fielders (Gibson, Gonzalez, Javier and Gwynn). Since Fred Claire doesn't believe in buying or trading for quality relief pitchers, maybe some of the center fielders should be given a chance in the bullpen.

BRAD STERN

Santa Monica
