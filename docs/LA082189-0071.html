August 21, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

RAY IS SELECTED ANGEL OF THE WEEK

Johnny Ray, who had nine hits in 23 at-bats last week (.391 average) with a home run and four RBIs, is The Times' Orange County Edition Angel of the Week.

Ray, who has 32 multiple-hit games and 14 three-hit games this season, has raised his average to .302.

The Times will donate $200 in Ray's name to the Boys and Girls Clubs of Orange County.
