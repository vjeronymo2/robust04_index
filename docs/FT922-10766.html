920429 FT 29 APR 92 / Rationale for Trident questionable, say MPs THE RATIONALE for Britain's Trident strategic nuclear deterrent can now legitimately be questioned in view of US and Russian disarmament, a cross-party parliamentary committee said in a report yesterday. The government had maintained its view that Trident was a minimum deterrent, and this had apparently been accepted by Mr Boris Yeltsin, the Russian president. HMS Vanguard, Britain's first Trident missile-carrying boat, is to be named by the Princess of Wales tomorrow. Defence Committee, Fifth Report, The Progress of the Trident Programme, HMSO.
