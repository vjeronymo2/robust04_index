BFN [Text] The brother president Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] received his excellency the British ambassador in Cairo yesterday evening. He gave him an important and urgent verbal message for the British Government related to the impasse reached by the negotiations with regard to implementing the agreement on the declaration of principles because of the obstacles placed by Israel in the way of implementing the agreement, which threatens the peace process. The brother president also sent an important and urgent message to President Mitterrand and the French Government, related to the impasse reached by the negotiations with regard to the implementation of the agreement on the declaration of principles because of the obstacles placed by the Israeli Government in the way of implementing the agreement within the fixed deadlines.
