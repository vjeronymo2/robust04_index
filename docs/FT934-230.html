931230 FT 30 DEC 93 / Pounds 2.5m credit card fraud is uncovered A Pounds 2.5m fraud controlled from the west Midlands and involving bogus credit cards has been uncovered, police disclosed yesterday. TSB and Midland Bank were among several financial organisations thought to have suffered losses. Both declined to comment. The bogus cards were used in France, Belgium and the Netherlands and in parts of England and Wales. The police expected to make a number of arrests during the next few days.
