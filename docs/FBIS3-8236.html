Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ljubljana, 7 Mar (TANJUG) -- The leader of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia, Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, who, it has been learned, arrived in Slovenia this morning, has been held at Ljubljana's Brnik airport. Slovene authorities are not allowing Zhirinovskiy to enter the country although the leader of the Russian Liberal Democrats has a valid diplomatic passport of the Russian Federation. It is not clear whether the Slovene authorities will deny Zhirinovskiy entry, or whether they will let him stay, as is claimed, for a private visit to Slovenia.
