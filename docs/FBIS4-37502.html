BFN [Excerpt] Today, for the first time ever, a group of Jordanian citizens who are not of Palestinian origin was allowed to cross into Israel. In coordination with the International Red Cross, the Jordanians crossed the border over the Allenby Bridge where they were given special entry visas for three days only. They came here to visit Jordanian relatives held in Israeli prisons. One of the visitors came to visit his cousin who is serving a 25-year sentence for his role in the terrorist attack on the Israeli Embassy in Singapore. [passage omitted]
