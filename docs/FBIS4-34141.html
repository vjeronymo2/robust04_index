BFN [Text] Army General Raul Castro Ruz, minister of the Revolutionary Armed Forces [FAR], has chaired [as heard] the ceremony marking the 35th [as heard] anniversary of the creation of the Western Army. The ceremony was held at the Playa Giron Fighter Brigade in San Antonio de los Banos, Havana. During the ceremony, Division General Nestor Lopez Cuba, chief of the FAR political directorate, read a letter of congratulations from Raul in which he acknowledged that they have satisfactorily fulfilled complex and important missions for three decades. The letter praises the fact that the Western Army has had significant participation in important tasks such as internationalist missions in Angola and Ethiopia, supporting the food program, and preparing the theater of operations, among others.
