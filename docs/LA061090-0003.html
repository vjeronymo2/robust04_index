June 10, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

EARTH FIRST!

In "The Monkeywrenchers," I came across the sentence: "Thanks in part to the Reagan Administration's anti-environmentalist policies . . . " It was laying there like undisputed fact.

This was supposed to be a truthful, accurate story. What is here is opinion. Opinion needs to be on the Opinion Page, or at least so noted. It is a shame that what could otherwise be a great newspaper stoops to blending its politics with the news.

BOB W. JAMES

La Mirada
