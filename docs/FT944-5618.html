941202 FT 02 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Azlan lower at Pounds 1.1m Azlan Group, which sells networking computer products, reported pre-tax profits down from Pounds 1.88m to Pounds 1.08m for the six months to September 30, after a sharp increase in distribution costs to Pounds 5.97m, against Pounds 3.1m. Turnover advanced by 32 per cent to Pounds 37.7m compared with Pounds 28.5m. Earnings per share dipped to 3.7p (6.3p), but an interim dividend of 1p has been declared. Mr Chris Martin, managing director, said that with positive results from actions taken in the UK and the strong performance in continental Europe, the group was confident of improving profitability.'
