940526 FT 26 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Approach to High Gosforth High Gosforth Park, which owns and runs Newcastle Racecourse, said it had received an approach which might or might not lead to an offer for the company. It advised shareholders to take no action for the time being. Last week the company's application for planning permission to develop its green belt site was turned down by Mr John Gummer, the environment secretary. In the light of that decision, the directors said they were reviewing all possible options.
