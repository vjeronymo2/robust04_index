941006 FT 06 OCT 94 / Veteran press chief to retire Mr Bert Hardy, managing director of Associated Newspapers, is to retire at the end of the year after more than half a century in the industry. Mr Hardy, 65, began his Fleet Street career in 1942 when at 14 he became a copy boy at the Picture Post. From there he ascended to elevated positions including chief executive of the Mirror Group, News International and London's Evening Standard.
