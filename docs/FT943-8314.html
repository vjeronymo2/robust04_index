940818 FT 18 AUG 94 / Indian MPs end boycott Indian opposition parties yesterday ended a two-week boycott of both houses of parliament, after the government backed down in a dispute over the findings of a parliamentary inquiry into the Dollars 1.28bn Bombay stock market scandal. The opposition walked out three weeks ago over what it alleged was a cover-up after the government partly rejected the recommendations of an all-party report on the scandal.
