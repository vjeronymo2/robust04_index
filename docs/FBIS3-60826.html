Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] Since the signing of the agreement with the PLO there has been an increase in the number of stone-throwing and petrol bomb incidents and a decrease in the number of shooting and stabbing incidents: 180 petrol bombs were thrown in the three months preceding the agreement, whereas 315 were thrown since the signing of the agreement on 13 September. On the other hand there has been a sharp fall in the number of shooting and stabbing incidents. These figures were relayed from the prime minister's bureau to Likud Chairman Binyamin Netanyahu at the latter's request. The Likud claims that the government is hiding the figures in order to justify the claim that the intifadah has subsided.
