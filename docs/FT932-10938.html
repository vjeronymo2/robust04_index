930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Buy-out at Inenco for Pounds 10m Inenco, a consultancy group specialising in cost-savings on energy, water and telecommunications, has been bought from its founders by its management for about Pounds 10m. The deal, which ensures independence and management succession, is backed by 3i and Montague Private Equity with Pounds 6m of equity and loan finance. Barclays is providing Pounds 3.5m of debt facilities. Inenco is based in Lancashire. It has 3,500 customers and last year achieved Pounds 20m of savings for them, making Pounds 1.5m profit on Pounds 8m turnover.
