940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / International Company News: Minproc plans capital shake-up and cash call Minproc Holdings, the troubled Australian pigment and coal group, is planning a ADollars 352m (USDollars 251m) cash call and is changing its name to Ticor. The par value of its shares is to be cut to 10 cents from 20 cents then consolidated into ADollars 1 units, and 220m shares issued at ADollars 1.60 each.
