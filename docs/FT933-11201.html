930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Villiers makes Pounds 5m rights VILLIERS Group, formerly Caspen Oil, is to raise Pounds 5.04m net via a rights issue in order to develop existing engineering interests and to acquire small engineering companies. The issue, fully underwritten by Marshall Securities, is of 50.4m new shares at 10 1/2 p each on a 1-for-1 basis. It will eliminate net debt, while in the short term part of the proceeds will be used to support the reorganisation of borrowings related to the oil business. USM-quoted Villiers recently announced a pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.15m (Pounds 6,000 profit) for the six months to January 31, struck after exceptional charges of Pounds 1.1m (Pounds 169,000 credits).
