BFN [Excerpt] At noon today, Brother President Yasir 'Arafat met Mr. Marin, vice-president of the European Commission. 'Arafat reviewed with Mr. Marin the situation and the developments in the Palestinian territories, and the means of support given by the European Commission to help consolidate the foundations of the Palestinian national authority in order to set up Palestinian institutions. The Palestinian and European sides signed two memoranda of understanding. The first one is related to making available the necessary support for elections in the national authority and its institutions, and the training of cadres concerned with this issue. The second one is related to providing the necessary support for the Palestinian police force and security. [passage omitted]
