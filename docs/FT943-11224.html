940801 FT 01 AUG 94 / Pay increases remain at 2.5% Fears that pay pressures would be heightened by the April tax increases, as employees sought to restore spending power, have proved unfounded, a report by Industrial Relations Services, the independent pay analysts, says. Its figures show that pay awards remained at 2.5 per cent in the second quarter. Half of the 214 pay settlements were between 2 per cent and 3 per cent.
