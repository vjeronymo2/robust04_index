931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / Women boost student numbers THE EXPANSION in university student numbers has been fuelled by extra women taking places to read arts courses, statistics published yesterday show. 'Old' universities - which excludes those that have gained university status in the past two years - expanded by 33 per cent in the four years to 1992-93. Women made up 47 per cent of full-time undergraduates compared with 41 per cent 10 years earlier. But only 22 per cent of academic staff were women (14 per cent a decade earlier). Students and Staff.
