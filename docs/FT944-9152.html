941116 FT 16 NOV 94 / Survey of Derivatives (9): Try a plain vanilla, or maybe a butterfly The astonishing growth of the emerging debt market has given birth to a new generation of derivatives, says Ms Alexandra McLeod, of Bank of America, Illinois. Plain vanilla put and call options and structures such as straddles, strangles, bull spreads and butterflies are some of the exotic names of products developed in the rocket science laboratories of the world's leading investment banks
