941024 FT 24 OCT 94 / Company News (This Week): Japan Airlines - Other companies The Japanese airline will release its interim earnings this Friday. Although the company has not forecast official figures for the first six months to September, analysts expect an improvement in operational profits thanks to cost-cutting efforts and the return of business-class passengers. From this interim, companies are required to disclose unrealised losses on foreign exchange trading, and the company's losses could be up to Y45bn (Dollars 464m).
