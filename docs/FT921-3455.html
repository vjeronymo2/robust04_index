920314 FT 14 MAR 92 / Burma refugees worry Bangladesh Mrs Khaleda Zia, prime minister of Bangladesh, goes to the US next week for discussions with President Bush and Mr Boutros Boutros Ghali, the UN secretary general, on the influx of Moslem refugees from neighbouring Burma and violations of human rights by the military government in Rangoon, writes Reazuddin Ahmed in Dhaka. Bangladesh is seeking UN Security Council intervention to resolve the problem.
