930329 FT 29 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Garton Engineering Garton Engineering, the engineering components and special fasteners group, announced a sharp fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.36m to Pounds 254,561 in 1992. The result last time was boosted by exceptional receipts from insurers following a fire at its Midlands site. Turnover improved to Pounds 20.2m (Pounds 19.3m). Earnings per share fell from 25.84p to 4.83p and the proposed final dividend is cut to 3p (5.25p) for a reduced total of 4p (7p).
