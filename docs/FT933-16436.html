930703 FT 03 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Vodafone chief's pay tops Pounds 0.5m Mr Gerry Whent, chief executive of Vodafone, the cellular telephone group, saw his remuneration for the year to March 31 rise by 7.5 per cent to breach the half-a-million a year barrier. His pay rose from Pounds 479,237 to Pounds 515,144 excluding pension contributions.
