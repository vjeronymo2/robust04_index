April 24, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Matt Scoggin and Wendy Wyland won the 10-meter platform titles at the U.S. Diving Championships at Boca Raton, Fla.

Scoggin, from Great Falls, Va., won with 616.77 points. Patrick Jeffrey of Madison, N.J., was second with 582.18.

Wyland, of Boca Raton, Fla., held off defending champion Wendy Lian Williams of Bridgeton, Mo., to earn her first national title in five years. Wyland had 428.01 points to 423.63 for Williams.
