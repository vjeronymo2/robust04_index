930717 FT 17 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Fleming Fledgeling net assets improve Net asset value per ordinary share of the Fleming Fledgeling Investment Trust rose from 260.4p to 337.3p over the 12 months to end-June. At the December 1992 year-end the figure stood at 288.4p. Available revenue for the six months ended June 30 amounted to Pounds 193,000 (Pounds 274,000), equal to earnings of 1.53p (2.18p). The interim dividend is a same-again 1p.
