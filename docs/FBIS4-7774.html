BFN [Text] Today, Presidency President Alija Izetbegovic received Dr. Sulejman Ugljanin, chairman of the Sandzak Muslim National Council, who had taken part in the work of the Assembly of Bosnia-Herzegovina. Dr. Ugljanin informed President Izetbegovic about the serious situation in Sandzak and the demand the Muslim National Council sent to the international peace conference on the former Yugoslavia for the Sandzak problem to be solved simultaneously with the solving of other issues that the conference deals with. In the conversation with Dr. Ugljanin, President Izetbegovic pointed out that our fellow countrymen in Sandzak are considered part of this Bosniak people of ours and that we here are all vitally interested in their destiny.
