BFN [Text] Iceland's minister for social affairs has resigned over differences with her party leader, the Icelandic Foreign Minister Jon Baldvin Hannibalsson. Johanna Sigurdardottir has been in conflict with Mr Hannibalsson and has challenged his leadership at this month's Social Democratic Party conference. In a controversial move the party recently adopted eventual membership of the European Union as its aim. Johanna Sigurdardottir has been a member of the Icelandic parliament since 1978 and was the only woman in the current government.
