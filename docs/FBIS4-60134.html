BFN [Text] The brother secretary of the General People's Committee for Unity met the AMU [Arab Maghreb Union] ambassadors to the Great Jamahiriyah last night. The development of the situation in Yemen, the means to preserve Yemeni unity as a pan-Arab gain which should not be abused, the AMU role in preserving Yemeni unity, and the implementation of an initiative of the AMU presidency to resolve the differences between the sons of the one people in brotherly Yemen were discussed.
