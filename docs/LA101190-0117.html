October 11, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

NEIGHBORHOOD EATERY: MORE EATERIES

Donut World, 34852 Doheny Park Road, Capistrano Beach. Open Mondays through Saturdays 5 a.m. to 5 p.m., Sundays till 3 p.m. (714) 496-2454.

There are nearly two dozen varieties available, but most who come here order a doughnut only as an afterthought. Breakfast sandwiches are a hot item, as are massive breakfast plates with four strips of bacon. JOHN WEYLER
