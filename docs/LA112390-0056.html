November 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

Disney Deal: The creators of ABC's "The Wonder Years," husband-and-wife team Neal Marlens and Carol Black, signed an exclusive deal to create movies and television shows for Walt Disney Studios. The agreement, rumored earlier this fall but officially announced this week by Disney, calls for the couple to be involved in projects for four years. The financial terms of the deal weren't released, but early reports estimate the couple will be paid at least $15 million. BETH KLEID
