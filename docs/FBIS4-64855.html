BFN [Unattributed article from Brussels EUROPE: "EU Commission Organises Emergency Response Exercise in Case of a Nuclear Catastrophe"] [Text] The European Commission, the German Laender of Rheinland-Palatine and Baden-Wurttemberg, as well as the nuclear power plant of Karlsruhe (Germany) are organising from Thursday [5 May], near the nuclear power plant of Phillipsburg, an emergency response exercise, called EUROPA 94, to last until Saturday 7 May inclusive. The object of the exercise, in which representatives from EU [European Union] Member States, international organisations and experts from some Central and East European countries will take part, is to test the implementation of different emergency plans provided for in case of a nuclear catastrophe; it is also intended to encourage an exchange of views and experiences between those responsible for emergency services in the different countries, so as to guarantee a maximum amount of cooperation and communication in case of a real catastrophe. The European Commissioner responsible for European policy in matters of civil protection, Ioannis Paleokrassis, stressed the importance of such an exercise.
