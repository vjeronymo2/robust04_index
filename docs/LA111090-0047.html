November 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MURRAY CALLS IT FUN, READER CALLS IT PREJUDICE

In regards to Jim Murray's column of Oct. 28 ("A Guide to Olympic Diction"):

Jim Murray has constantly professed to deplore prejudice, but he is the most prejudiced sportswriter I have ever known of. Jim obviously thinks it is perfectly acceptable to be prejudiced against one group of people, but just terrible to be prejudiced against another.

R. JAMES, Los Angeles
