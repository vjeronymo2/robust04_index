December 10, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

'DEMOCRATS FIND NO LIFE IN THE PARTY'

Re "Orange County Democrats Find No Life in the Party" (Nov. 26): Even without money, there is more life in the Orange County Democratic Party than the Republicans can buy. Next time visit one of our Central Committee meetings or other events to see just how much life we do have before you write such an inaccurate headline.

Your reporter who wrote the story visited our last Central Committee meeting and told me that he thought the meeting was exciting. It's too bad the Los Angeles Times decided to focus on the negative.

MIKE BALMAGES

Chairman, Orange County Democratic Party
