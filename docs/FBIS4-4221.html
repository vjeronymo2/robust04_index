BFN [Text] Brasilia, April 26 (XINHUA) -- Senior Chinese Communist Party official Hu Jintao and Brazilian parliament leaders expressed their desire today to expand friendship and cooperation between the parliaments of the two countries. Hu, member of the Standing Committee of the Politburo of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee, met with President of the Senate Humberto Lucena and President of the Chamber of Deputies Inocencio de Oliveira today respectively. Lucena praised the China's success in the development of its economy, saying that China is an example to follow by developing countries for their economic development. De Oliveira said he expects a bigger exchange between the legislative powers of China and Brazil. Hu said that China accelerates its economic development, it perfects its juridical system. [sentence as received] China has started to implement programs for juridical construction, planned during the recent years. Hu Jintao stressed that China and Brazil, two big countries of Asia and Latin America, should play roles in safeguarding peace and stability in the world and in the development and progress of mankind. Hu and his delegation arrived in Brasilia last night at the invitation of the Brazilian Government.
