October 6, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

RAMS' BRASS NEEDS A NEW BATTLE PLAN

The way I see it, when the general isn't too smart, the army isn't going to win.

John Robinson used Curt Warner for more than two quarters and thoroughly messed up the whole Ram offense. General John should have realized that Curt no longer had the magic touch, nor is he likely to regain it in the future. If Robinson ever realizes that Cleveland Gary is the man of the future, then we might start winning the war again.

BOB HUTTON, Burbank
