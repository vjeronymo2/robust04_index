930520 FT 20 MAY 93 / Small businesses cut travel costs THE RECESSION has forced two-thirds of small UK businesses to cut the cost of employee travel to Germany, according to research by Equator, a market research subsidiary of Saatchi & Saatchi. Mr John Boulding, managing director of GTF Tours, the UK-based company which commissioned the research, said 80 of the 100 businesses surveyed now used three-star hotels.
