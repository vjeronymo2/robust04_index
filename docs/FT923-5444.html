920901 FT 01 SEP 92 / Management (The Growing Business): IOD backs share schemes - In A Nutshell The Institute of Directors (IOD) will launch a campaign to further the cause of employee and director share ownership schemes at a one-day conference entitled Sharing for Success on October 1. The IOD said such schemes would help achieve greater involvement by employees in their companies. IOD, 116 Pall Mall, London SW1Y 5ED. Tel. (071) 839 1233.
