930913 FT 13 SEP 93 / IM in S Korean car import deal IM GROUP, the vehicle importer and distributor controlled by entrepreneur Mr Bob Edmiston, has signed a letter of intent with Ssangyong of South Korea to import four-wheel-drive rivals to Land Rover's Discovery and Range Rover models from next year. The deal with Ssangyong would compensate IM for its pending loss of the Japanese Isuzu importership to Vauxhall, General Motors' UK subsidiary.
