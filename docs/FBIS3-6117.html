Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] A film show was held on 7 March at the DPRK Embassy in Cuba to mark the eighth anniversary of the visit to the DPRK by Comrade Fidel Castro, first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba [PCC] Central Committee and president of the Councils of State and Ministers of the Republic of Cuba. PCC Central Committee members Jesus Montane, political affairs adviser to the supreme commander, and Pedro M. Perez Betancourt, vice minister of the Revolutionary Armed Forces and Naval commander; Nicolas Rodriguez, vice foreign minister; Alberto Rodriguez, general arbitrator of the International Affairs Department of the PCC Central Committee; Candelaria Rodriguez, woman lawyer; and other political and social figures were invited to the film show. Kim Song-yong, DPRK ambassador to Cuba, and embassy employees also took part in the film show. The DPRK film "The 40th Anniversary of the Victory of the Great Fatherland Liberation War" was shown and was followed by a small banquet. Participants of the banquet toasted for the long life of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song; the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il; and Comrade Fidel Castro, the supreme leader of the Cuban revolution.
