October 1, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

MISSION VIEJO RECALL

We hold no brief for City Councilman (Robert A.) Curtis of Mission Viejo, but we do resent the fact that the Mission Viejo Co., William Lyon Co. and Baldwin Co. are trying to buy his recall with money and a paid political consultant. All these companies are interested in is profit, and they'll let no one stand in their way.

It's bad enough that every hill and gully in Mission Viejo is covered with their ticky-tacky houses; they only want to make it worse!

RAY KOVITZ

Mission Viejo
