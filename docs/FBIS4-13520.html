BFN [By Khalil Khurmah] [Text] Amman--Palestinian leaders in the Jordanian capital have revealed to AL-BAYAN correspondent that Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres has sent a proposal to the PLO leadership containing Israel's approval of a declaration of establishing a Palestinian state in the Gaza Strip should the PLO agree to share subterranean water in the Palestinian territories proportional to the size of population, give up the Dead Sea bank in full to Israel, relinquish the heights overlooking the Jordan Valley in the West Bank, and announce its acceptance of Jerusalem as a united city under Israeli control, in addition to agreeing to the establishment of a military alliance between the Palestinian state and Israel. These Palestinian leaders say that the Palestinian leadership is studying this proposal, and that a number of senior PLO officials support it.
