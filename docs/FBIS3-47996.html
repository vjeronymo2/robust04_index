Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The submarines which Russia has sold to North Korea recently were confirmed to be decommissioned submarines without any strategic value. A relevant Foreign Ministry official revealed that the Russian Government informed our government that the submarines that Russia recently sold to North Korea were decommissioned submarines from which weapons and equipment were all removed. Therefore, these submarines were sold in accordance with a commercial contract, not a contract for weapons supply, and this contract prescribed that the Russian side will inspect the work of dismantling the decommissioned submarines.
