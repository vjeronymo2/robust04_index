920925 FT 25 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Reduced deficit at Sentry Farming Sentry Farming Group reported reduced losses in its seasonally unfavourable first half to June 30. The USM-quoted group saw the pre-tax deficit cut to Pounds 286,000 from Pounds 361,000 last time. Turnover improved to Pounds 1.86m (Pounds 1.52m). The directors expect to report a profit for the full year. Losses per share amounted to 7.14p (9.17p).
