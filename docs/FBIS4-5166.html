BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 7 (KCNA) -- The seventh session of the Ninth Supreme People's Assembly [SPA] of the DPRK entered its second day today. The session continued to discuss the first agenda item "on successfully fulfilling the tasks of socialist economic construction in the period of adjustment set forth by the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK]". The speakers said that the revolutionary economic strategy of the WPK fully conforms with the basic interests of the revolution and construction in Korea and correctly reflects the desire and demand of its people. They stressed that, when the strategy was thoroughly implemented, it would consolidate the foundation of the independent economy of the country as firm as a rock and open before the Korean people a bright prospect for the capture of a higher height of socialism. A decision of the SPA of the DPRK "on successfully fulfilling the tasks of socialist economic construction in the period of adjustment" was adopted at the session with the unanimous approval of the deputies. Then the session went over to the discussion of the second item on the agenda "on the review of the performance of the state budget of the DPRK for 1993 and the state budget for 1994". Deputy Yun Ki-chong, minister of finance, delivered a report on it. Deputy Han Song-yong, chairman of the Budget Committee of the SPA, made a report on the deliberation of the review of the performance of the state budget of the DPRK for 1993 and the state budget for 1994. Speeches were made on the second agenda item. The speakers noted that the state budget last year had been correctly performed and fully approved and supported the state budget for 1994, saying that it had been properly worked out whereby to successfully fulfil the first year tasks for the period of adjustment by implementing the agriculture-first, light industry-first and foreign trade-first policies to the letter, giving precedence to the coal and power industries and railway transport and steadily developing the metal industry. The session discussed the third item on the agenda "on the endorsement of the laws adopted by the DPRK SPA Standing Committee during the recess of the SPA." The session adopted with the unanimous approval of the deputies ordinances of the SPA of the DPRK--"on the endorsement of the decision of the Standing Committee of the SPA of the DPRK `on the adoption of the law on the preservation of cultural relics of the DPRK,'" "on the endorsement of the decision of the Standing Committee of the SPA of the DPRK `on the adoption of the law on lawyers of the DPRK'" and "on the endorsement of the decision of the Standing Committee of the SPA of the DPRK `on the amendment and supplement to the law on joint venture of the DPRK'". The session continues.
