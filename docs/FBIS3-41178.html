Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Zahedan (Sistan-Baluchestan), Feb. 4, IRNA -- A number of armed miscreants blocked the Zahedan-Zabol road in Tasuki region in this southeastern province last night killing four passengers of a passing bus and looting other passengers. According to the local IRNA correspondent the miscreants have kidnapped three soldiers retuning to Zahedan from leave. The provincial law enforcement official rushing to the scene immediately mobilised all resources to track down the miscreants, resulting in the arrest of two of bandits along with weapons and several rounds of ammunition. The panic-stricken passengers of the bus were being questioned on details of the attack. A law enforcement official told IRNA that the miscreants were agents of a notorious drug trafficking gang leader known as 'Hamid Nahtani. Nahtani leads an international gang which uses Iran soil as a transit route to smuggle drugs to Europe. At the moment, said the official, all roads leading to Zahedan have been reopened and the security forces are keeping a vigilance. A special group was also trying to track down the rest of the miscreants, he added. The smugglers had blocked the Khash-Zahedan, Kerman-Zahedan and Zabol-Zahedan roads in the past weeks several times looting people's properties.
