BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Excerpt] Dushanbe, 2 Jun -- The 47-year-old lieutenant colonel Yuriy Shkipkin of the directorate for routine personnel duties of Tajikistan's Defense Ministry was killed in Dushanbe at about 9 p.m. local time on 1 June. An ITAR-TASS correspondent was told at the republican Interior Ministry that Shkipkin's body was found by local residents not far from his home. The motives of the murder have not been established. An investigation team of the Interior Ministry has started inquiry. [passage omitted]
