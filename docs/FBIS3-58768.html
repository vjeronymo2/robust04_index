Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Paris, Jan 20 (AFP) -- The French Alcatel telecommunications group has set up two joint venture companies in China with the Chinese Telecommunications Ministry in Beijing and Chengdu, the company said on Thursday [20 January]. Alcatel China and the ministry would have equal shares in the companies set up to work on equipment for transmission by Hertzian wave and by cable. The first of these, called Alcatel of Beijing Transmission Systems, would produce and distribute Hertzian transmission equipment and portable equipment of the same type for the Chinese market. The second, Alcatel of Chengdu Communication Systems, would produce and distribute cable transmission equipment for the Chinese market. The Chinese market in these sectors is believed to be worth one billion dollars during the next five years, Alcatel said. The Chinese telecommunications infrastructure grew by 40 percent between 1992 and 1993, the company said. Alcatel is a leader on the Chinese market for digital transmission equipment, holding 50 percent of the market. France and China put their relations on a normal footing on January 12 following a period of tension which ended with a decision by France to cease selling weaponry to Taiwan.
