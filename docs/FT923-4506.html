920907 FT 07 SEP 92 / World News In Brief: Making music history Composer Mike Oldfield is set to make rock history for the second time in 20 years, with his album Tubular Bells II topping the charts after selling 300,000 copies in the first week of release, the BBC said. The original Tubular Bells topped the charts for a year, making Oldfield a millionaire at 19.
