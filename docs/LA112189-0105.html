November 21, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY EARNINGS: DASIBI ENVIRONMENTAL POSTS LOSS FOR QUARTER

Dasibi Environmental Corp., the Glendale maker of air pollution detection devices, said it posted a loss of $178,309 for the first quarter that ended Sept. 30, compared to a profit of $167,987 a year before. Revenues increased to about $1.3 million from about $1.1 million in the prior period.

Dasibi attributed the loss to start-up costs and charges having to do with a series of acquisitions and new ventures.
