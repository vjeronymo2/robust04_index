BFN [Text] Commenting on a report today in the Athens newspaper ETHNOS, government spokesman Ioannis Kasoulidhis said there is no chance of President Glavkos Kliridhis resigning. According to the report, President Kliridhis is ready to submit his resignation to protest the secret deliberations of UN Security Council members with Rauf Denktas' side, and the negative decisions that are promoted at the United Nations related to the Cyprus issue.
