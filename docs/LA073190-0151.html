July 31, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Camarena trial -- A story in Friday's editions incorrectly reported that two government witnesses, Hector Cervantes Santos and Enrique Plascencia Aguilar, had testified that they had seen Honduran drug kingpin Juan Ramon Matta Ballesteros at 1984 meetings where the kidnaping of U.S. Drug Enforcement Administration agent Enrique Camarena was discussed. In fact, only Cervantes testified that he had seen Matta at the meetings.
