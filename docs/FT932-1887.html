930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Aitken confident over Euro fighter A CONFIDENT forecast that the European fighter aircraft would make a successful first flight this year was made by Mr Jonathan Aitken, minister for defence procurement, yesterday, Ivor Owen writes. He dismissed speculation that the cost of the project to Britain and her partners had increased by 50 per cent as 'completely wide of the mark'. Mr Aitken, speaking in the resumed debate on the 1992 defence estimates, said the actual real-terms increase since the start of the project in 1988 was about 10 per cent. Left-wing Labour MPs defied the party leadership in the Commons last night by voting against the 1992 defence white paper, which was approved by a majority of 243 (278-35).
