Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Urgent" item -- By ITAR-TASS correspondent Oleg Kuzmin] [Text] Tehran January 28 TASS -- "Kazakhstan bears responsibility for its nuclear weapons itself. It does not intend to eliminate them or transfer them to other places until the non-use of such weapons is guaranteed at the world level and the planet gets rid of them," Ambassador of Kazakhstan in Iran Myrzadai Zholdasbekov said in an interview with the Iranian "JOMHURI-YE ESLAMI" newspaper. The ambassador added that he was expressing the official position of Kazakhstan about which U.S. President Bill Clinton will be informed during President Nursultan Nazarbayev's visit to the United States. The Kazakh Embassy in Tehran opened on January 25.
