BFN [Text] Sarajevo, 14 Apr (TANJUG) -- The supreme command of the Srpska republic armed forces this evening issued a statement calling on citizens of Muslim and Serb nationalities from the Gorazde area to take refuge behind the Serb lines, where they will be guaranteed security and protection. Full security and protection is also guaranteed to all members of military and police units who end attacks on Serb positions and surrender their arms to the Srpska republic army, reads a statement of the supreme command of the Srpska republic armed forces.
