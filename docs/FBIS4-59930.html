BFN [Text] Today, Israel and the Czech Republic signed a memorandum on security cooperation. Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin lauded Czechoslovakia for assisting Israel during the Independence War by sending weapons, without which it would have been much harder for us to win our independence. Czech Defense Minister Antonin Baudys said that the Czechs assisted Israel in the past and are seeking Israel's assistance today. Our correspondent Karmela Menashe reports that the Czech minister began his official visit to Israel today.
