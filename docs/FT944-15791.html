941017 FT 17 OCT 94 / Business Travel (Update): Brussels train service Belgian railways expects to start a regular high-speed service from Brussels' Midi station to Waterloo, London, in the middle of next month, following last week's inauguration of Belgium's cross-Channel passenger train service. The service can provide up to 15 return journeys a day, although to start there is likely to be only one. Eurostar trains, carrying up to 794 passengers, leave from a new terminal at Midi station. Fuller details, including fares, between London, Paris and Brussels are expected to be published today.
