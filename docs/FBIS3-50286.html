Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] At its session in the Kolodeje district of Prague today, the Czech Government reached an agreement on general principles of one single document on the concept of the reform of the territorial administrative setup. This was announced by Czech Prime Minister Vaclav Klaus at the end of the session. He added that there was no fundamental dispute among the four coalition parties about the powers of the higher-level territorial administrative entities. The ministers agreed, above all, on powers to be transferred from central bodies to regional bodies and on the principle that regional self-administration bodies would not deprive villages of their current powers.
