930329 FT 29 MAR 93 / Former MP dies SIR Michael McNair-Wilson, 62, Tory MP for Newbury until the 1992 election, died yesterday. He became Tory MP for Newbury in 1974, and held the seat until he resigned last year for health reasons. A by-election is to be held in Newbury following the death last month of Mrs Judith Chaplin, who succeeded Sir Michael.
