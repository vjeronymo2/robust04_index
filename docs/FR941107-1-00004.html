DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE Commodity Credit Corporation 7 CFR Part 1413 RIN 0560&hyph;AD42 1995 Extra Long Staple Cotton Program AGENCY: Commodity Credit Corporation, USDA. ACTION: Proposed rule.

SUMMARY: This proposed rule would amend the regulations to set forth the acreage reduction percentage used in administering the acreage reduction program (ARP) for the 1995 crop of extra long staple (ELS) cotton. This action is required by section 103(h)(5) of the Agricultural Act of 1949 as amended (the 1949 Act).
