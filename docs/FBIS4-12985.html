BFN [Text] The body of the terrorist who perpetrated the Hadera suicide attack has been identified. He was 23-year-old 'Ammar Salah 'Amarani from the Samaria village of Ya'bad. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the man was a known Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] activist, but he had been never detained by the security forces. Hamas has announced in a leaflet in Gaza that members of the 'Izz-al-Din al-Qassam squad carried out the Hadera attack in revenge for the Hebron massacre.
