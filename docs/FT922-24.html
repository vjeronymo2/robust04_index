920630 FT 30 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Reconstructed Alphameric sees return to black Alphameric, the Surrey-based computer hardware group which successfully underwent a financial reconstruction earlier this year, reported a pre-tax deficit of Pounds 2.08m on turnover of Pounds 5.14m for the 12 months to March 31. Mr Alan Benjamin, chairman, reiterated that the group, now concentrating on custom keyboards, electronic point of sale terminals and satellite data broadcast and display systems, should return to profit in the current year. Losses per share were 28.8p.
