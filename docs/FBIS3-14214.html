Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Council of Ministers tonight issued a series of decisions, including one disbanding the Lebanese Forces Party and banning news broadcasts in the audio-visual media, apart from those on Lebanon Television and Radio. The decision becomes effective at midnight tonight and will remain in effect until the media organization law is issued. Consequently, Radio Free Lebanon announces to its listeners that it will abide by the Council of Ministers decision issued on 23 March suspending the transmission of news and political programs in the audio-visual media apart from Lebanon Television and Radio until the media organization law is issued. We will announce news times after this law is issued. Promising listeners to stay on your side, something to which you have always been accustomed.
