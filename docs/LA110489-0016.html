November 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

REGARDING THE RAMS: NEXT QUESTION, PLEASE

Why are the Rams so predictably frustrating?

Why is it that, even when they were undefeated they didn't seem like a dominant team? Why do they always seem to play at the level of their opponent? Why is John Robinson considered a great motivator when his team lacks intensity?

Why have I been a loyal Rams' fan for 20 years?

CHRIS GRISANTI, Pasadena
