940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports, landed - 9,478 pkgs. (527,578 kgs) strong demand. Assams sold well often gaining 10/20p. Bright East Africans firm to dearer, coloury mediums 5/10p up, plainer types tended lower. Brighter ceylons advanced 4/8p, others about steady. Averages:- landed all tea 115.22p., N. India 111.55p., Kenya 120.40p., Tanzania 99.82p., Uganda 94.02p., Rwanda 147.53p., South India 74.00p., Sri Lanka 104.62p., Madagascar 87.49p., Quotations: best available 185 - 212p/kg (nom), good 145 -160p/kg, good medium, 125 - 142p/kg, medium 108 - 120p/kg, low medium, 80 -95p/kg. Top price 212p for a Rwanda pd. Offshore: cif - 3,800 pkgs (214,556 kgs) good demand, firm rates. Averages:- all tea 95.90p cif, Sri Lanka 104.11p, Kenya 108.07p, Zimbabwe 98.03p. Malawi 79.70p.
