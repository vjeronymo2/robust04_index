BFN [ITAR-TASS report: "Ready for Exercises"] [Text] Washington, 16 June -- The U.S. and Ukrainian defense ministers have declared their intention to conduct joint military exercises to work through maneuvers that might be involved in peacekeeping operations. "I expect these exercises to be held in the very near future," Vitaliy Radetskiy said during a visit with William Perry to the naval base in Norfolk, Virginia. The Ukrainian minister is visiting the United States within the framework of the U.S.-Ukrainian program for cooperation in the defense sphere. The U.S. defense secretary paid an official visit to Ukraine in March this year, during which he inspected Ukrainian missile positions.
