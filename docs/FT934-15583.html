931011 FT 11 OCT 93 / International Company News: Slingsby jumps to Pounds 302,000 HC Slingsby, the trucks and ladders maker, reported a strong advance in interim pre-tax profits for the half year to June 30. The company said there had been a marked increase in activities coupled with cost rationalisation. On turnover 14 per cent ahead at Pounds 5.9m pre-tax profits advanced from Pounds 28,000 to Pounds 302,000. Earnings per share were 20.9p (1.4p) and the interim dividend came to 3p (2p), payable January 4. However, the company warned that present indications were that second half profits would be more modest than the first.
