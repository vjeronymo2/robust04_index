940101 FT 01 JAN 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa A becalmed gold price and strong financial rand took the sparkle off Johannesburg. Gold shares paused after Thursday's rally although good demand continuedfor second-tier industrials. Golds shed 12 to 2,164, while industrials rose 11 to a record 5,573. The overall index gained 4 to 4,893. Barlows lost 50 cents to R54.75 while Anglos added R2 to a new high of R219.50.
