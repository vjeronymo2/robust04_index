BFN [Unattributed report: "Russian Planes From the Korean Conveyor Belt"] [Text] Russian airplanes will soon be produced by plants in South Korea, thanks to a joint company set up by the South Korean "Khendz" [name as transliterated] corporation and the Russian A.S. Yakovlev Experimental Design Bureau. Production of "Yak" aircraft is expected to begin in the course of the year. The "Khendz-Yak Aerospace" joint venture, in which the South Korean side will have a 51-percent share and the Russian experimental design bureau 49 percent, plans to produce medium-sized and small aircraft. It is expected that in the future the joint venture will be able to sell its output abroad, notably in the United States and European countries.
