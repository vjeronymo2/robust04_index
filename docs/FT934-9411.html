931110 FT 10 NOV 93 / Pakistan Lower House gutted FIRE swept through Pakistan's parliament yesterday, gutting the National Assembly (lower house) chamber on the eve of a session, Reuter reports from Islamabad. Sabotage has not been ruled out. Ms Benazir Bhutto, who took office as premier three weeks ago after winning a hard fought poll, ordered an inquiry into the fire, state radio said. Saturday's presidential elections will still go ahead.
