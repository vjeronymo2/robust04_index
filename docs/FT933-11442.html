930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Hotspur Invs Hotspur Investments, the Cazenove-managed trust which seeks to achieve long-term capital growth through an international equity portfolio, reported a net asset value, excluding income, of 382.82p per share at June 30, up from 319.61p a year earlier. Directors said the 7.9 per cent increase compared with a rise of 5 per cent in the FT-A All-Share Index. Net revenue for the six months to end-June was Pounds 26,549 (Pounds 22,437), equivalent to earnings of 4.3p (3.63p) per share.
