940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Hartlepools Water rises to Pounds 1.46m Hartlepools Water achieved a 20 per cent rise in profits to Pounds 1.46m pre-tax for the 12 months ended March 31. Turnover of Pounds 5.52m compared with Pounds 5.31m. Earnings worked through at 143p (126p) and a final dividend of 37p makes a 60p (55p) total.
