BFN [Text] The ruling coalition has gained the chairmanships of the Chamber of Deputies committees. The outcome of the voting was a foregone conclusion, except for the the foreign affairs committee, where Mirko Tremaglia from the National Alliance won over Emma Bonino from the Radical Party by a single vote. Following is the full list of chairmen of the parliamentary committees: Gustavo Selva (constitutional affairs) and Paolo Agostinacchio (finance) from the National Alliance; Paolo Bampo (defense), Roberto Calderoli (social affairs), Marco Sartori (labor), Alberto Paolo Lembo (agriculture), and Francesco Formenti (environment) from the Northern League; Tiziana Maiolo (justice), Silvio Liotta (budget), Vittorio Sgarbi (culture), and Alessandro Rubino from Forza Italia; and Sante Perticaro (transport) from the Christian Democrat Center.
