Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Following the plot by a limited number of counterrevolutionaries on Tuesday who had congregated in one of the streets in Zahedan and intended to create agitation and disturbances, the Secretariat of the Supreme National Security Council issued a statement last night, the text of which is as follows: A number of elements who--in the course of the measures taken by the security forces of the Islamic Republic of Iran in the eastern border areas of the country--perceived that they could attain their lost interests congregated in one of the streets in Zahedan. They broke the windows of some residential buildings and damaged some vehicles passing on the streets, including those of the law enforcement forces. In performing their legitimate duties and in order to restore law and order, the law enforcement forces therefore intervened and began dispersing these people and arresting the disruptive elements.
