BFN [Text] The prime minister, Mr. Narasimha Rao, today reiterated that the [Nuclear] Nonproliferation Treaty [NPT] should be nondiscriminatory and have universal application. During his talks with the visiting Ukrainian foreign minister, Mr. Anatoliy Maksymovych Zlenko, who called on him, Mr. Rao said there is no need for any precipitative action when the NPT is coming up for a review next year. He assured the visiting dignitary of full cooperation in providing training facilities in different areas and in setting up banking institutions in Ukraine. Mr. Zlenko sought India's help in banking, veterinary, and pharmaceutical sectors.
