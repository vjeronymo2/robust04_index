Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The daily EL DJOUMHOURIA confirmed on 10 February the killing of two policemen by unknown extremists last Monday [7 Feb] in the suburb of Annaba, east of the capital Algiers. According to the Algerian dailies EL HOURIA and EL WATAN, a policeman was killed yesterday in the town of Mouardia by three unknown armed men. The same sources added that the incident also left two female students dead and another seriously wounded.
