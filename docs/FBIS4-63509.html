BFN [Text] Valeriy Gorbatov, the Ukrainian representative in Crimea, has told INTERFAX-Ukraine that the Crimean Republic's Interior Ministry on Saturday [21 May] took control of the main government buildings in the Crimea. According to Gorbatov, the Crimean Interior Ministry units have been put on alert and have been given a basic supply of ammunition after a report that units of the National Guard in Simferopol were allegedly put on condition one combat readiness. Valeriy Gorbatov said in this connection that in fact there has been no ground for the Interior Ministry's actions. In his words, units of the National Guard are in barracks and no measures to bring them to condition one combat readiness have been taken.
