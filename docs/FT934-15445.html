931012 FT 12 OCT 93 / Management (The Growing Business): Keeping the family happy -In a Nutshell Personal relationships can strengthen the family-owned business, but they can also be a source of tension. Understanding family dynamics is one of a series of evening seminars to be run by the Stoy Centre for Family Business this autumn. Other subjects to be covered include financing the family business, planning to sell and arranging the hand-over from one generation to the next. Contact Diane Deacon, Stoy Centre for Family Business, 8 Baker Street, London W1M 1DA. Tel: 071 486 5888.
