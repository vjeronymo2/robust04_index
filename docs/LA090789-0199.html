September 7, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

STATE;

NO BIG WINNERS IN LOTTO DRAWING

There were no winners in Wednesday night's $17.8-million Lotto jackpot drawing, boosting Saturday's prize to $25 million, the California Lottery said today.

Five players each won $397,356 for picking five of six plus the bonus number. The tickets were purchased in San Carlos, Wilmington, San Dimas, Los Angeles and Alhambra. In the five of six category, 304 winners received $3,367, and 15,827 players picking four of six won $58.
