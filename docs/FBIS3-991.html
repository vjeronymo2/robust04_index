Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by L. Elias] [Text] Johannesburg March 17 SAPA -- The Independent Electoral Commission [IEC] has given the North West Christian Democratic Party permission to lodge its amended list of candidates by 4.30 PM on Thursday [17 March], the IEC said in a statement. "The party has also been informed that the IEC will afford it and its office bearers or candidates the same recognition and protection as it affords all parties," the statement said.
