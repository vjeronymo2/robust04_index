930107 FT 07 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Property Security grows to Pounds 3.9m Property Security Investment Trust lifted pre-tax profits 29 per cent rise to Pounds 3.88m in the first half to September 30. Total rents grew to Pounds 9.5m (Pounds 8.63m) and dealing profit improved to Pounds 115,000 (Pounds 43,000). The interim dividend is maintained at 1.5p.
