940831 FT 31 AUG 94 / International Company News: Czech savings bank ahead Ceska Sporitelna, the largest Czech savings bank, posted pre-tax profit for the first half of 1994 of Kcs1.76bn (Dollars 63m), Reuter reports from Prague. Revenues were Kcs29.76bn against liabilities of Kcs28bn. Comparative interim results for 1993 have not been published. Sporitelna reported Kcs300m after-tax profit for all 1993, and said it expected after-tax profit to grow to Kcs2.5bn for 1994.
