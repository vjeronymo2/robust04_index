921028 FT 28 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Third pay-out for Parkfield creditors Unsecured creditors of Parkfield, the entertainment and engineering group which collapsed in July 1990, are to receive a third interim dividend of 2p, bringing the total so far to 26p in the pound. Parkfield was placed in liquidation last year with liabilities of over Pounds 320m. Dividend cheques were being sent out from Monday. Further dividends would be determined by the level of final agreement of claims, but the total target for creditors remained at 30p in the pound.
