March 22, 1990, Thursday, Valley P.M. Final

LOCAL;

STAB VICTIM FOUND IN STREET DIES

An unidentified man died of stab wounds early this morning after being found late Wednesday in a San Fernando street, police said.

No arrest has been made.

Police were called to the 1000 block of Arroyo Avenue at 11:36 p.m., Wednesday, where a man lay next to the curb, unconscious and stabbed in the left side. He was taken to Holy Cross Medical Center in Mission Hills, where he died four hours later.

Police have been unable to identify the victim, a white male, 35 to 40 years old, with gray hair. He never regained consciousness, police said.
