921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / UK Company News: United Newspapers in Dollars 18.5m US acquisition UNITED Newspapers is expanding its US computer and software development markets through the acquisition of M&T Publishing for an initial cash payment of Dollars 18.5m (Pounds 11.6m). Further consideration up to Dollars 1m is payable subject to profit achievement. The acquisition is being carried out through Miller Freeman, the US sub-sidiary. California-based M&T publishes magazines and books for computer software users. Its parent is Markt & Technik Verlag of Germany. In 1992 M&T is expected to make a pre-tax profit of Dollars 622,000 on gross revenues of Dollars 33.1m. Last year there was an operating loss of Dollars 368,000 following the integration of Computer Metrics, and a pre-tax deficit of Dollars 597,000.
