BFN [Text] "Only the Latvian Republic can decided whose military bases can be stationed on its territory," says a message distributed by the Lithuanian Foreign Ministry on Wednesday evening after the publication of the Russian president's order on the creation of Russian military bases on the territories of CIS states and the Latvian Republic "to ensure security of the Russian Federation and the above mentioned states and to test new armaments and military equipment." On April 6 a source in the Kremlin reported to Interfax that the wording about the creation of a military base in Latvia "is the result of an inappropriate phrase which was included into one of the documents of the Defense Ministry and then appeared in a corresponding order of the Russian president." The message of the Lithuanian Foreign Ministry underlined that "unilateral actions by Russia aimed at the creation of military bases in the Latvian Republic despite the will of this sovereign state contradicts the principles of international law and international obligations taken by Russia as a member of the UN and CSCE."
