BFN [Report by Eldar Zeynalov under the "Azerbaijani News" rubric: "Interethnic Tension in Kusarskiy Rayon"] [Text] There has been a sharp increase in nationalistic propaganda in Kusarskiy rayon, which has a concentrated population of Lezgins, following the 15 June killing of four Lezgin policemen by Azeris. The body of one of them was set up in the central square of Kusary, the rayon center, causing local Lezgins to gather.
