BFN [Text] Kiev, 3 Jun -- Ukraine's President Leonid Kravchuk met representatives of the Japanese multibusiness corporation ("Shiba-6") that expressed readiness to cooperate with Ukraine. In particular, (Moritoni Nakamura), the president of the corporation "Shiba," said about the corporation's intention to supply oil to Ukraine. Ukraine's President Leonid Kravchuk expressed satisfaction with the intentions of the Japanese businessmen and stressed that the trend in the work offered by the Japanese had good prospects for the future. Leonid Kravchuk pointed out after a thorough discussion that Ukraine will be ready for cooperation.
