BFN [Text] By the evening of May 26 the Abkhazian formations had won back their positions in the Kodor Gorge (Abkhazia) lost in the morning when the Georgian troops launched an attack. Sukhumi reported that the Georgian troops had succeeded in breaking through by about 300 meters near the village of Verkhnya Lata. A representative of the Abkhazian Defense Ministry told Interfax that the Georgian units had suffered significant losses and had retreated. He did not mention Abkhaz losses. The night of May 26-7 and following morning were relatively calm in the Kodor Gorge. The Georgian Defense Ministry has denied attacking the Kodor Gorge.
