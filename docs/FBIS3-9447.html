Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Albanian Helsinki Committee [KShH] raises its voice of protest over the arrest by Serbian police of Kosovar publicist and writer Shuaip Beqiri, who is also editor-in-chief of the weekly independent FORUMI, which is published in Prishtina. The KShH appeals to international institutions to seriously assess the dangerous escalation of violence in Kosova, as this escalation foreshadows new tragedies that can happen in the Balkans and the wider region.
