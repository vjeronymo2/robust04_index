BFN [Text] The Council of Ministers has passed a major package of economic decrees which renews a number of expiring decrees, as well as introduces new regulations designed to streamline job placement, make direct recruitment easier, and provide fresh funds for Law 44, dealing with youth employment. The announcement was made by Labor Minister Mastella. He announced that a total of 500 billion lire has been earmarked for Law 44, 100 billion lire in 1994, and 200 billion each in the years 1995 and 1996.
