BFN [Report from the ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA and ITAR-TASS column: "Duma Moving"] [Text] The latest Duma Council session, scheduled for 10 May, will be held in a new venue -- in the former USSR State Planning Committee building on Okhotnyy Ryad. The first plenary session of the Duma will be held there 11 May. Valentin Kovelev, deputy chairman of the chamber, told us that "the move is going ahead actively, a number of services have already occupied their new offices."
