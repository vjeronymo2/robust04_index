BFN [Text] The Committee for Peaceful Uses of Atomic Energy has issued permission for the recommissioning of the Reactor Unit 1 at the Kozloduy Nuclear Power Plant, which was shut down 16 days ago because of a leak in the air vent of its the main circulating pump. The unit is already connected in parallel to the national grid. Following a successful check on its condition, the No. 2 Reactor is also being prepared for start-up.
