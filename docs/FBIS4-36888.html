BFN [Text] Faysal al-Husayni is establishing a shadow government in East Jerusalem to be made up of nine ministries. A Palestinian source told our correspondent Moti Amir that the shadow government will be headed by a prime minister, two deputy prime ministers, and a financial inspector general who will deal with the expenses of the government and coordinate it with the expenditures of the Palestinian Authority in the self-rule areas. The shadow government will also include a political affairs ministry and a defense and general security ministry, contrary to the Palestinian Authority in the self-rule areas.
