941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / International Company News: US media groups lifted by higher ad revenues US media groups Knight-Ridder and Times Mirror yesterday reported strong third-quarter results on the back of strong advertising revenue, AP-DJ reports from New York. Both groups, like most other US newspaper companies, reported surging revenue in their newspaper divisions but also showed improvement in other sectors. Times Mirror reported net income for the quarter of Dollars 52.3m, or 41 cents a share, including a gain of 3 cents. Net income was Dollars 77.7m, or 60 cents, for the same period last year, including a gain of 37 cents from asset sales. Knight-Ridder, which publishes 29 daily newspapers in the US, reported net income of Dollars 37.3m, or 69 cents, up 19.2 per cent from last year's Dollars 31.2m, or 57 cents.
