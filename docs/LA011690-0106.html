January 16, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

TITAN WOMEN LOSE FIFTH IN ROW, 73-61

Judy Mosley, the nation's fourth-leading scorer, had 25 points and 11 rebounds to lead the University of Hawaii to a 73-61 victory over Cal State Fullerton Monday in a Big West Conference women's basketball game at Titan Gym.

It was the fifth consecutive loss for the Titans (6-9 overall, 1-5 in conference) and their longest losing streak since the 1986-87 season.

In Western Christian Athletic Assn. men's basketball:

Pacific Christian College 110, Southwestern College 50 -- Steve Welch led six players in double figures, scoring 17 points in the victory for Pacific Christian (12-8, 5-0) at Phoenix.
