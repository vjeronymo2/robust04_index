940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / UK Company News: HSBC enhanced scrip take-up HSBC Holdings said the reference price in respect of the enhanced scrip dividend alternative to the 1993 final cash dividend is 728.87636p. Of the 69.1m shares to be issued under the alternative, the cash offer by NatWest Markets has been accepted in respect of 17.8m shares and bids have been procured for 55 per cent of these.
