Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Three Israelis were wounded by terrorist gunfire in Halhul a short while ago. One of them is in critical condition and the other two in moderate condition. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that as a Subaru passed the three men's car at the entrance to Halhul from the direction of Hebron, some 20 bullets were fired from it. A leaflet signed by the 'Izz al-Din al-Qassam squad was found on the site. The Subaru was later found abandoned in Hebron. The security forces have imposed a curfew on the area. [passage omitted]
