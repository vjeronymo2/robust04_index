940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Molynx shares fall on warning Shares of Molynx Holdings fell by 10 1/2 p to 25p yesterday after the closed circuit television, security and building energy management systems maker warned of 'a significant loss' for 1993. The company also said that discussions with Silvermines, the Dublin-based electrical and property group, regarding a possible recommended bid for Molynx had not been finalised but the offer would be on a share exchange basis. Silvermines shares were unchanged at 49p. In 1992 Molynx incurred losses of Pounds 486,000 but returned to the black in the first half of 1993 with pre-tax profits of Pounds 21,000.
