Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev February 25 UKRINFORM-TASS -- The United States has begun implementing its aid programme to Ukraine within the frame of the agreement on Safety and Security in Dismantling Nuclear Weapons in Ukraine (SSD). This agreement was signed by the U.S. and Ukraine in October of last year. The American aid has arrived in the form of fuel for transportation and for the operation of equipment to be used in the dismantling of Ukraine's nuclear weapons.
