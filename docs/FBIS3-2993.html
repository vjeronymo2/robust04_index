Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 16 (YONHAP) -- Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen on Wednesday denied press reports that North Korean President Kim Il-song will soon visit China. "Those reports are not true and China is not preparing to receive his visit," Qian said at a press conference. Qian also said China has no intention of mediating in North Korea's nuclear dispute with the United States and other countries. He urged all concerned parties to remain patient and resolve the issue through dialogue.
