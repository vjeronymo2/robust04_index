921203 FT 03 DEC 92 / People: Finance moves Henrik Bjorn, head of corporate banking, has been appointed chief executive of UNIBANK in London. ***** Gordon Waddell has been appointed chairman of the TOR INVESTMENT TRUST on the resignation of John Woollam. ***** Yoichiro Furuse, formerly director and general manager of the international planning department of SUMITOMO BANK in Tokyo, has been appointed director and general manager of its London branch; he succeeds Masahiko Koido, who becomes general manager of the main office in Osaka. ***** Anne Cobb has been appointed executive vice-president, strategy, for VISA INTERNATIONAL in Europe, Middle East and Africa; she moves from Eurocard International. ***** Peter Hillyard has been appointed md, head of global precious metals, and Chris Lambert head of precious metals, London, at BARCLAYS METALS GROUP. ***** Hugh Hughes has been appointed chief executive of Societe Generale Equities International. ***** Oliver Baker has been appointed to the board of CAMPBELL LUTYENS HUDSON & Co.
