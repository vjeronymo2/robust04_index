Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] According to a report, peace efforts are continuing to bring about a permanent cease-fire between rival factions and ways are being explored to establish a lasting peace in Afghanistan. A group of 43 prominent commanders belonging to various Afghan groups met President Borhanoddin Rabbani in the suburbs of Kabul yesterday. They reviewed all aspects of the current fighting and discussed the possibilities of a dialogue among rival factions for lasting peace. President Rabbani told the commanders that a representative council comprising all provinces will be set up which will be empowered to make a final decision about the future structure of the country. According to the AFGHAN ISLAMIC NEWS AGENCY, the Afghan president said he will resign if directed by the council to do so.
