BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] A bomb exploded in the cargo department of a travel agency in Kadikoy, Istanbul, causing material damage. No one was killed or injured in the incident. An explosion occurred around 0945 this morning in the cargo department of a travel agency on Kadikoy's Rihtim Street. The explosion shattered the glass and triggered a fire. Firefighters extinguished the fire before it spread any further. Officials reported that a bus left for Harem shortly before the explosion and a bag believed to have been forgotten by the passengers was taken to the cargo department. Officials noted that a bomb which was inside the bag exploded and that a 27-28-year-old- woman, who is believed to have left the bag, is being sought.
