920829 FT 29 AUG 92 / UK Company News: 32% downturn at Stavert Zigomala Stavert Zigomala, furniture and carpet wholesaler and retailer, reported a pre-tax profit of Pounds 44,000 for the year ended March 31 1992. That was a 32 per cent drop on the previous Pounds 65,000, and was generated on turnover of Pounds 804,000 (Pounds 848,000). The dividend is held at 18.75p per share, although earnings had fallen from 15.87p to 9.23p.
