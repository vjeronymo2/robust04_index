Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev, Feb 1 (AFP) -- Crimea's new President Yuriy Meshkov, who wants the autonomous Ukrainian Republic returned to Russia, will meet Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk in Kiev on Tuesday, reliable sources here said. The Ukrainian presidency had not officially confirmed the meeting. Meshkov was elected president of Crimea on Sunday [30 January] with 72.9 percent of the votes in the republic's first direct presidential elections. He announced he would hold a referendum on Crimea's future status on March 27, the same day as Ukrainian parliamentary elections.
