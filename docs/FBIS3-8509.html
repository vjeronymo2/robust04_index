Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sarajevo, March 10 (AFP)--Bosnian Serbs launched artillery fire at French UN forces in the western Moslem enclave of Bihac Thursday [10 March], a UN Protection Force spokesman said. Major Rob Annink said the French troops responded and called for air support by NATO planes. UNPROFOR commander General Sir Michael Rose ordered an investigation but subsequently decided an air strike was not necessary.
