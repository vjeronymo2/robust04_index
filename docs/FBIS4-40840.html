BFN [Yuriy Rogozhin report: "Prospect of Closed Cities"] [Text] The State Duma council has decided to hold hearings, before the parliament recess, on the problem of closed cities -- there are more than 100 of them in Russia and their population is several million. All the nuclear-and defense-related cities face the same problems -- falling production, unemployment, environmental pollution. The committee for industry, energy, and transport is planning, as part of preparations for the hearing, to hold a conference in Tomsk-7 at the end of June with representatives of the other closed cities and interested departments.
