930408 FT 08 APR 93 / Funding Sources: Correction A REPORT (Financial Times, March 17) on the winding-up of Funding Sources International Limited (FSI) mentioned DTI warnings about Prime Bank Notes offered by the company. Mr Howard Curtis, former director of FSI, faces charges under the Companies Act. He asks us to make clear that he has not been charged with any offence regarding wrongful obtaining or retention of fees, that it is not suggested he has been involved in any way therewith, and that he has not been convicted of any charge in connection with FSI.
