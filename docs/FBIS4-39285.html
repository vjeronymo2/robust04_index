BFN [Text] Tashkent, 6 Jun (ITAR-TASS) -- Kim Yong-sam, president of the Republic of Korea, issued a warning to the DPRK today over the North Korean nuclear problem. Speaking to journalists in Tashkent, he said: "I warn North Korea that any attempt to launch a reckless adventure will lead to its self-destruction." Kim Yong-sam announced that after his return from his foreign tour on Wednesday, the South Korean cabinet will meet on 8 June in Seoul to discuss problems of national security. The South Korean president is currently on an official visit to Uzbekistan. On Tuesday he will visit Khabarovsk and Vladivostok. He will then leave Russia for South Korea.
