931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / International Company News: Restructure at FLS FLS Industries, supplier of equipment to the cement-making industry, is being restructured. The move will increase group turnover by 18 per cent, writes Hilary Barnes in Copenhagen. Aalborg Portland Holding, a building materials group, is to become a part of FLS. Turnover should rise by DKr2.2bn to DKr14.3bn (Dollars 2.09bn) on the basis of 1992 accounts.
