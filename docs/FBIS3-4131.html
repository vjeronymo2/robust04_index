Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday morning, State Councillor Chen Junsheng stressed in a full meeting of the Guangdong delegation that Guangdong must concentrate its efforts on the issue of grain to ensure the acreage producing grain, increase grain output, and raise the self-support level of grain to guarantee the settlement of the issue of feeding Guangdong's people. Chen Junsheng pointed out: The key to stabilizing grain production and increasing the self-support level of grain lies in stabilizing the acreage under grain. He said: Now there are too many development zones, and they have occupied a lot of good farmland. If the situation remains unchanged, grain output will drop. This situation must be changed very rapidly.
