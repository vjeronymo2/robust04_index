940602 FT 02 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Cedardata ahead of forecast with Pounds 2.71m In its first set of results since flotation in March, Cedardata, the Surrey-based supplier of financial accounting and commercial computer software, lifted pre-tax profits by 52 per cent from Pounds 1.78m to Pounds 2.71m for the year ended March 31 - just ahead of forecast. Turnover expanded 16 per cent to Pounds 6.12m (Pounds 5.26m). Earnings per share were 6.3p (4.2p). At its share placing - priced at 105p valuing the company at Pounds 32.2m - the directors forecast pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.65m. Mr Leon Fattal, managing director, said that sales prospects were significantly ahead.
