Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 29 Mar (MENA)--MENA has learned that Dr. Nabil Sha'th, political adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, and General Amnon Shahaq, Israel's deputy chief of staff, met at 1900 today at a secret location in Cairo. The three-hour meeting resulted in a decision to form a small committee to draft a special agreement on security arrangements and international protection for the Palestinians in Hebron in accordance with UN Security Council Resolution 904. Sources close to the talks told MENA that the committee includes the following: Dr. Sha'th, 'Abd-al-Raziq al-Yahya, Jamil al-Tarifi, Hasan 'Usfur, Zuhdi al-Qidrah, Rami Sha'th, Yo'el Singer, legal adviser at the Israeli Foreign Ministry, 'Uzi Dayan, deputy director of the Israeli Administration in the West Bank, and Gadi Zohar. According to the same sources, the document produced by the drafting committee will consider modifications that the PLO requested in view of the conditions in the occupied Arab territories. The drafting committee began its meeting at approximately 2200 and is expected to work late into the night. The remaining members of the Israeli and Palestinian delegations have retired to their quarters.
