Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By Ziyad 'Abbas] [Excerpt] Three leading Hebron region Fatah activists have denied a report published by HA'ARETZ on 9 January that three Hebron Fatah leaders -- Jibril al-Bakri, Dhiyab al-Sharabati, and Ihsan al-Nazir -- had withdrawn their resignations, submitted three days earlier, in the wake of a telephone conversation they held with Yasir 'Arafat on Friday [7 January] night. The three stressed that their resignations were still in effect and that the HA'ARETZ report was unfounded. [passage omitted]
