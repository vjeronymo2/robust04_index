Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, December 31 (KCNA) -- The Ministry of People's Armed Forces [PAF] gave a cocktail party Thursday evening for the military attaches of different embassies here on the occasion of the New Year 1994. Present at the party together with the guests were Kim Chong-kak, Vice-Minister of People's Armed Forces, General Chon Chae-son and other generals and officers of the Korean People's Army. Speeches were made there.
