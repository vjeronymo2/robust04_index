930731 FT 31 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Kleinwort High Income lower Net earnings of Kleinwort High Income Trust for the year ended June 30 dropped from Pounds 2.59m to Pounds 2.37m, equivalent to 7.89p (8.64p) per share. The fourth quarterly dividend is 1.875p (same) for a maintained total of 7.5p for the year.
