930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Significant rise in Independent value Over the six months ended December 31 1992 the net asset value per share of The Independent Investment Company rose 21.5 per cent, and over the full 12 months it increased 26 per cent, from 57.7p to 72.5p. The North American investments performed well as the economic recovery showed signs of continuing and interest rates were lower. In the UK the decision to leave the ERM was beneficial. In the half year gross income was Pounds 2m (Pounds 1.5m).
