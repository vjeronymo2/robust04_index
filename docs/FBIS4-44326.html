BFN [Text] Vatican City, 28 Jun (INA)--[Iraqi] Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz met here today with His Holiness Pope John Paul II. The deputy prime minister explained Iraq's position with regard to Security Council resolutions and the importance of both honoring these resolutions and commitment to their implementation on the part of the Security Council. During the meeting, the pope emphasized the Vatican's position, which rejects the blockade, and expressed his sympathy with Iraq.
