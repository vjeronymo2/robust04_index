Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] The Turkish side made new allegations yesterday by claiming that there were incidents on the confrontation line. In an Istanbul dispatch, ANATOLIA News Agency reported that the Rauf Denktas regime reported to the UN Forces in Cyprus [UNFICYP] that the Turkish positions were fired upon by Greek Cypriots. According to the news agency, the so-called incident took place at noon yesterday. A UNFICYP spokesman to CyBC [Cyprus Broadcasting Corporation] said last night that he has no information about the Turkish allegations. At the same time, the National Guard General Staff stressed that the Turkish allegations are a new attempt to create problems.
