930306 FT 06 MAR 93 / Nuclear fuels director resigns MR HAROLD Bolter, company secretary and director of corporate affairs at British Nuclear Fuels, resigned yesterday after part of the bill for decorating his house was charged to the company. Mr Bolter resigned after an internal inquiry into the reasons for the bill. He told the inquiry he should have been more careful in checking what he thought was an attractive tender for the work. He said he was not aware BNF had been billed for the work by the contractor, who also worked for the company. He added: 'Ignorance is no excuse for what has happened.'
