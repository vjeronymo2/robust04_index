BFN [Text] The Inter Agency Task Force for Coastal Environmental Protection for the Ilocos region was recently activated during a coordinating conference held at the headquarters of the Naval District I in San Fernando, La Union. Commodore Nicasio Blancas, naval district I commander, said the task force had chosen Lingayen Gulf as its pilot project. The inter-agency task force was activated following a presidential order to protect the country's coastal waters from all forms of destructive fishing, over exploitation and pollution. The task force is composed of the Departments of Interior and Local Government, Environment and Natural Resources, Agriculture, Justice, Philippine National Police and the National Economic Development Authority.
