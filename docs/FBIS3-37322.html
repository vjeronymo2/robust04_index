Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Talalayev] [Text] Moscow, 16 Feb -- The official ceremony of saying good- bye to John Major, prime minister of Great Britain who is in Moscow at the invitation of Russian President Boris Yeltsin, took place this morning in St.George's Hall of the Grand Kremlin Palace. The state anthems of Russia and Great Britain were played, and Boris Yeltsin accompanied John Major to the ceremonial entrance of the Grand Kremlin Palace where they parted warmly. The official part of the visit of the British prime minister ended. He left the Kremlin for the "Vnukovo-2" Airport. He is accompanied by Viktor Chernomyrdin, the head of the Russian government. John Major will fly out to Nizhnyy Novgorod.
