August 13, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TIME TO WAKE UP

Mary Kay Blakely went into a coma, and that entitles her to make sweeping generalizations about the plight of women in America? Wait, I forgot -- she's involved in the women's movement. Well, those are certainly credentials enough for me. ("To the Edge and Back" by Elizabeth Mehren, July 31).

Could you please try to feature people who can talk about something else besides themselves (or their gender, their group, their life style) and how hard their life is?

KIM LARSEN

Santa Ana
