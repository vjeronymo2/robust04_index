BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received Salim Salih Muhammad, member of the Yemeni Presidential Council and assistant secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], and Dr. 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Dali, member of the YSP Political Bureau, at 1300 today. The meeting was attended by Rashid Akhtarini, deputy prime minister for services affairs, and Muhammad 'Abduh Shatfah, Yemeni Ambassador in Damascus. Talks dealt with the Yemeni situation and its complications. The president stressed the pan-Arab importance of safeguarding Yemen's unity and the need to resolve the existing differences and complications through dialogue. Salim Salih Muhammad and the accompanying delegation left Damascus this afternoon following a three-day visit to Syria. He was seen off at Damascus International Airport by Rashid Akhtarini, deputy prime minister for services affairs; Muhammad 'Abduh Shatfah, the Yemeni ambassador in Damascus; and the Yemeni Embassy staff. Muhammad made a statement to a SANA [Syrian Arab News Agency] correspondent, noting: Today, we had the honor of meeting with His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad. We discussed the current situation in Yemen and the efforts being made to circumvent the existing crisis and safeguard Yemen's unity. He lauded Syria's pan-Arab positions on Arab issues under the leadership of President Hafiz al-Asad, as well as Syria's pioneering role in supporting the unity of Arab ranks and achieving Arab solidarity. He said: We sensed from his excellency a great understanding of what is taking place in Yemen, and his excellency stressed Syria's interest in, and constant care for, Yemen's unity.
