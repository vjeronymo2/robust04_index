BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Maynayev] [Text] Bishkek 16 May TASS -- A law on state secrets has come into force in Kirgizia [Kyrgyzstan]. The secrets include information relating to Kirgizia's economic and political interests. It is prohibited to make public and discuss, in particular, any information on the technical state of the roads, the state of health of airline pilots, epidemics, and losses of cattle.
