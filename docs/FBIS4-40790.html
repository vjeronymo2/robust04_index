BFN [Text] Bryansk, June 17, RIA -- Issues pertaining to the development of the Russo-Ukrainian order were discussed in Bryansk by the regional administration, Colonel Viktor Mishchenko, chief of staff of the Western Group of Frontier Forces of Russia, and Sergey Bekov, deputy chairman of the State Customs Committee. They discussed the creation of check-points and collaboration of the customs personnel and frontier guards at check-points in Troebortny and Yurkovichiy, said Vladimir Karpov, head of the Bryansk region administration.
