May 24, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

Correction Appended

MARTINEZ'S 'LOVE IN THE LIGHT'

Regarding the column by Al Martinez and his greater understanding of men who love men, Bravo!

I have been a loyal fan since his column first appeared in your paper.

He has reached new heights of compassion with this insightful piece. May his blessings be many.

As a gay man who is HIV, I am grateful to have escaped this scourge. I have seen too many friends and associates die slowly and painfully, both physically and emotionally from AIDS.

As a volunteer at Project Angel Food where we are feeding upwards of 80 desperately ill men and women each day who have been inflicted by this dread disease, it continues to be an ongoing struggle.

I thank God for Mr. Martinez and his enlightenment.

DAVID GRAHAM

Los Angeles
