BFN [Text] Haifa Port Director (Mandi Zalsman) has revealed that a team of Jordanian businessmen recently visited Israel and expressed interest in the facilities provided by the Port of Haifa to act, in the time of the desired peace, as an outlet for Jordan on the Mediterranean. In statements to our correspondent Shafiq Mansur, the Haifa Port director said the Jordanians showed they are very well aware of the achievements scored by the port and the development projects there. The team asked if Haifa Port can provide services such as storage, shipping, and unloading of Jordanian goods and containers and if there was a plan to set up a free zone in Haifa Port.
