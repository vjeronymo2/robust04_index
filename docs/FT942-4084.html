940611 FT 11 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Willoughby's ahead at interim stage Helped by higher gold production and an increased gold price, Willoughby's Consolidated, the mining and ranching group, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 225,000 to Pounds 608,000 in the six months to March 31. Mining profits jumped from Pounds 192,000 to Pounds 485,000, but there was a loss from ranching of Pounds 39,000 (Pounds 66,000 profit). Earnings per share climbed from 2.8p to 4.6p, while the interim dividend has been doubled to 1p.
