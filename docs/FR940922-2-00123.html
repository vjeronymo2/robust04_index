DATES: September 30, 1994, 10:00 am to 11:00 am. National Women's Business Council Notice of Meeting

SUMMARY: In accordance with the Women's Business Ownership Act, Public Law 100&hyph;533 as amended, the National Women's Business Council announces a forthcoming Council Meeting. The meeting will cover a summary of activities of the National Women's Business Council in Fiscal Year 1994, including but not limited to, increasing procurement opportunities and access to capital for women business owners.
