930707 FT 07 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Dwyer to sell Abbeycentre Dwyer, the property investment group, is to sell the Abbeycentre in Belfast for Pounds 18.5m cash, subject to shareholder approval. The Abbeycentre represents 30 per cent of Dwyer's property portfolio by value, considered to be too large for a single investment. The sale will reduce borrowings by Pounds 11m, cutting gearing by half to 106 per cent, and make more than Pounds 7m available for investment.
