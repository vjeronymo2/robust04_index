Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, March 3 (SNARK)--This morning, at 5.00 a.m., Azeris' troops being supported by a great amount of tanks and other military hardware, launched an attack in Fizuli direction, at the southern-east of the Karabakh front, Press-Service of the Karabakh Defense Army told SNARK. A sudden character of the offensive let the attackers break the front defense borders of the Karabakh defenders. According to the same source of information, the Karabakh troops should follow the order to neutralize the enemy and come to the initial positions. Hard clashes are going on, by 11.00 a.m. Thus, during the period of last three days the Azerbaijani side currently violated the armistice achieved under the Russian Defense Ministry's mediation from 1st of March.
