920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Louis Newmark loss reduced PRE-TAX losses at Louis Newmark, the maker of electrical, mechanical engineering and specialist equipment, were reduced from Pounds 1.07m to Pounds 980,000 in the year to April 4. The result came on lower turnover of Pounds 24.8m (Pounds 36.6m), which reflected the loss of the Swatch agency and was after depreciation of Pounds 1.16m (Pounds 1.6m). Losses per share were 29.9p (34.8p).
