930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables The start of the English soft fruit season has started with homegrown strawberries now in the shops at Pounds 1.20-1.40 a lb (Pounds 1.40-1.60). Cherries at Pounds 1.00-1.20p (Pounds 1.00-1.20 a lb) are a good buy along with pineapples at 90p-Pounds 1.30 each (90p-Pounds 1.30). The first plentiful supplies of new season English Broad beans have arrived and are priced at 35-40p a lb (55-65p a lb). Spring greens at 40-45p a lb (40-45p) and English potatoes at 15-22p a lb (12-20p) are also good vegetable buys. English Iceberg is the best salad buy at 50-65p each (55-65p). Other salad produce worth looking out for are: English & Dutch tomatoes at 45-60p a lb (55-65p).
