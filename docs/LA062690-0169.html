June 26, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

'PEAKS' ACTOR FACES DRUG COUNT

Michael J. Anderson, who danced and babbled in backward English during a dream sequence on ABC's "Twin Peaks," has been arrested on a charge of hashish possession.

The 3-foot-7 actor was arrested Monday after a car in which he was a passenger was stopped for a bent license plate, police said. Anderson, 36, was freed on bail. He is in the area for rehearsal of a play to open in August in Stockbridge.
