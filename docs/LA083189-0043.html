August 31, 1989, Thursday, Valley Edition

MASTER'S COLLEGE ADDS HUFF TO BASKETBALL STAFF

Mike Huff, 29, has joined the Master's College men's basketball coaching staff. Huff, a former player and assistant coach at Pacific Lutheran University in Tacoma, Wash., will be responsible for recruiting and scouting.

Huff, who also played professionally in West Germany, replaces Dave Montgomery, who left the Mustang staff to pursue a teaching career.
