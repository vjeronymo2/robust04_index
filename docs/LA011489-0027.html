January 14, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

IT'S DIFFICULT TO CUSHION AN EMBARRASSING BLOW

Jim Murray seems to speak for all of Los Angeles by writing, "And we don't throw cushions . . . " and "It's never been our style . . . "

Why does Murray use "we" and "our"?

It wasn't all of us.

No, the blame falls directly on those individuals from USC -- the University of Spoiled Cushioneers.

JON BLANK

Agoura
