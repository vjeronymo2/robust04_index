Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Basseterre, St. Kitts, 17 Jan, CANA -- A career civil servant, Eustace John, has been sworn as the new deputy governor general of Nevis. He succeeds the late Weston Parris, M.B.E. who died in office almost two years ago. John was sworn in by resident Puisne judge, Velma Hylton, in the presence of prime minister Dr. Kennedy Simmonds, Nevis Premier Vance Amory, family members, friends, and well wishers. The new deputy governor general is a former director of audit. He was also general manager of the state-owned ZIZ Radio and Television. John holds a certificate in Mass Communications from the Caribbean Institute of Mass Communications (Carimac) at the University of the West Indies (UWI) Mona Campus.
