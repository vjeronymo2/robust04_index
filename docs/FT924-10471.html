921030 FT 30 OCT 92 / Observer: Shome mishtake Not an organisation renowned for its openness, the Foreign Office on Wednesday took the unusual step of issuing journalists with an internal memorandum throwing doubt on the acceptability of Danish proposals for clarifying the Maastricht treaty. But it was all an unfortunate mistake, embarrassed officials now declare. The memo was stapled to a batch of documents on Hong Kong prepared for release to the press. So even the Foreign Office could not deny its existence. But, true to form, it stated that the memo was already out of date.
