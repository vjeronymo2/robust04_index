Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] National Defense Minister Abdelaziz Ben Dhia held talks this morning with his Turkish counterpart Mehmet Golhan, who started his official visit to Tunisia yesterday. During the meeting, the two ministers reviewed a number of issues of common interest, particularly those relating to bilateral relations. After the talks, the two ministers attended the opening of the proceedings of the Joint Military Committee that will examine several aspects of Tunisian-Turkish cooperation in the military field. On this occasion, the two ministers highlighted the importance of this committee and stressed the understanding that always prevailed during its meetings. This, they said, allowed the development of bilateral cooperation in this field and opened new avenues.
