March 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BORDER DITCH CONTROVERSY

Only time will tell whether the controversial border ditch will really reduce drug and illegal alien traffic from Mexico. But, the arrogance and hypocrisy of the Mexican government in opposing the project is outrageous.

The ditch will be entirely on U.S. land. It will have absolutely no effect on aliens who are in the U.S. legally, or on any legitimate commerce between the two countries. But, it will hurt the drug lords and the coyotes who deal in human misery.

Let's dig the ditch! It's worth a try!

BILL ROSENTHAL

Huntington Beach
