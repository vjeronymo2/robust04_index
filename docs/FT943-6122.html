940831 FT 31 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Wates Leisure Wates Leisure, part of the privately owned Wates Building Group, has raised about Pounds 15m in expansion capital ahead of a stock market flotation within the next five years. The company owns and manages five health and fitness clubs in the south-east of England. Phoenix Fund Managers led the Pounds 5.5m equity injection and the Bank of Scotland will be providing up to Pounds 12m of debt. Retained earnings will bring to about Pounds 20m the funds available to double the size of Wates Pinacle health and leisure clubs over the next three years.
