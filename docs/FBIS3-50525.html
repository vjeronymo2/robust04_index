Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 3 Jan (NA) -- Jorge Baeza, the Defense Ministry's new military affairs secretary, today installed Ricardo Lagorio as policy and strategy under secretary and Roberto Ahumada as institutional affairs under secretary. Thus Lagorio and Ahumada succeed Virgilio Beltran and Eduardo De Simone, whose resignations were accepted by the Executive Branch. Defense Ministry Planning Secretary Guillermo Etchechoury and ministry departmental directors participated in the ceremony.
