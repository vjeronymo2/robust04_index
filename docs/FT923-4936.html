920903 FT 03 SEP 92 / UK Company News: London Wall Pounds 1m down at nine months London Wall Holdings, an underwriting agency at Lloyd's, suffered a fall in profits from Pounds 1.45m to Pounds 445,000 pre-tax over the nine months to June 30. Turnover of Pounds 3.28m (Pounds 4.86m) was made up as to agency fees Pounds 2.59m (Pounds 2.64m), profit commission Pounds 615,000 (Pounds 2.16m), other commission Pounds 25,000 (Pounds 24,000) and investment management fees Pounds 44,000 (Pounds 32,000). Earnings per share emerged at 6.51p (16.15p). The shares are traded on the over-the-counter market.
