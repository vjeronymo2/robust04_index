Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, JANUARY 6 (TURAN) -- Today President Geydar Aliyev of Azerbaijan received the commander of the Transcaucasian group of Russian troops General-Colonel Fedor Reut on his request. The meeting took place behind closed doors. One of the representatives of the Presidential team told TURAN agency that during the talks President Aliyev touched upon the problem of participation by Russian troops stationed in Armenia fighting on Armenia's side. The general said that the Russian Army is not interfering in the conflict. In addition, he said that he will strictly control the troops who serve under him. General Reut thanked the President for his assistance to the Russian party for the transportation of military goods through the territory of Azerbaijan. Other problems of the region were also discussed during the talks.
