941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Yorklyde up 14% Yorklyde, the Huddersfield-based clothing manufacturer, reported pre-tax profits up 14 per cent at Pounds 1.7m for the six months to July 31, against Pounds 1.49m. The company said the benefits of its investment programme were now being felt and the continuing development of the product range had helped maintain a healthy level of interest. Turnover was Pounds 9.22m (Pounds 7.87m) a rise of 17 per cent. Earnings per share were 11.4p (10.2p) and the interim dividend is raised to 2.4p (2.2p).
