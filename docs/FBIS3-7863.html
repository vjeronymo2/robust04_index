Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Srpska republic is as of today a part of the unified financial system of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia [FRY], Vladimir Lukic, the Srpska prime minister, said after a meeting in Banja Luka with representatives of banks, the chamber of the economy, the customs administration, the economic institute, and large companies in the republic. The Srpska republic's government has adopted all regulations from the program of financial reconstruction and economic recovery of Yugoslavia, Lukic said. He added that now it is only a matter of technical details as to when the new dinar would appear and become the only means of payment in the Srpska republic.
