CSO [Text] Yinchuan -- Second-phase construction began recently on the Daba Power Plant in the Ningxia Hui Autonomous Region, a key project in the Eighth Five-Year Plan (1991-95). The work--to install two generators each with a capacity of 300,000 kW--will cost more than 1.5 billion yuan ($172 million) and will take 36 months. When the second phase is completed, the plant will have 1.2 million kW of generating capacity, making it the largest in Ningxia.
