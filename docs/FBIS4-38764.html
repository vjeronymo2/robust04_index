BFN [Text] [No dateline as received] -- In accordance with a resolution of the Government of Uzbekistan, from tomorrow the price of gasoline will rise from 600 to 1,000 sum-coupons a liter, electricity for residential premises from 10 to 15 sum-coupons a kW hour. The cost of travel on urban transportation will double -- for a journey by bus, trolleycar, tramcar, and metro will be 100 sum- coupons. The price of tin-baked bread is set at 400 sum-coupons compared with the present 105. At the same time, from 1 June the minimum wage will be increased by 50 percent, bringing it up to 75,000 sum-coupons.
