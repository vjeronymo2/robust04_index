March 20, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

ABC will return "Moonlighting" to the air April 2, making it a Sunday instead of a Tuesday show as in the past. "Moonlighting" will be seen at 8 p.m., pitting Maddie and David (Cybill Shepherd and Bruce Willis) against Jessica Fletcher (Angela Lansbury) of CBS' hit, "Murder, She Wrote." "Moonlighting" has been on hiatus for the last month. ALEENE MacMINN and CLAUDIA PUIG
