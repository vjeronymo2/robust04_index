940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / Technology (Worth Watching): Combined CD and mini-disc system The latest in Japanese consumer electronics is the combined compact disc and mini-disc system which enables music lovers to compile their own mini-disc from a selection of their favourite CD tracks. Developed by Sharp, and resembling a radio-cassette player, the MD-Z1 dubs CD tracks on to the recordable mini-disc at the touch of a button. Sharp: Japan, 06 625 3007.
