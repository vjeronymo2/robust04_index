920704 FT 04 JUL 92 / Finance & The Family: Jargon-free tax guide The Lloyds Bank Tax Guide for 1992/93 is now available. It offers 'jargon-free' information about tax, including 50 ways to save tax and a quick guide to checking your PAYE code and tax bill. The authors are Sara Williams, and John Willman, public policy editor of the Financial Times. Price Pounds 7.99.
