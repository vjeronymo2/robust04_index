Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] President Suharto received a courtesy call from Madame Ursula Seiler Albring, junior minister in the German Foreign Ministry at Bina Graha Presidential Office in Jakarta this morning. [passage omitted] Earlier, the head of state received S. Jayakumar, Singapore's new foreign minister, who was accompanied by Edward Lee Kwong Foo, Singapore's ambasasador to Indonesia. Responding to reporters, Foreign Minister Jayakumar said that President Suharto has instructed that the long-established close ties between Singapore and Indonesia should be further strengthened. On the occasion, Jayakumar also briefed President Suharto on the ASEAN foreign ministers meeting scheduled to be held in June.
