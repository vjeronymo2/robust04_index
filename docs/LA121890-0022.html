December 18, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL ROUNDUP: POLLUTION RESEARCH SELLS 9% STAKE TO L. A. INVESTOR

Pollution Research & Control Corp., a Glendale maker of pollution-monitoring equipment, said it raised $160,000 by selling a 9% stake in the company to Los Angeles investor Lee Sion.

In a filing with the federal Securities and Exchange Commission, Pollution Research said it sold 400,000 shares of its common stock to Sion for 40 cents each. The company also granted Sion a three-year option to buy an additional 200,000 shares at an exercise price of 40 cents a share.
