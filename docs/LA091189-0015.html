September 11, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The Calgary Flames were outskated by the Czechoslovakian national hockey team, 4-2, at Prague, Czechoslovakia, in the first game for a National Hockey League team in Eastern Europe.

In a game featuring an NHL-like 58 minutes in penalties and two brawls in the final minutes, the Czechoslovakians overcame an early 2-0 deficit.
