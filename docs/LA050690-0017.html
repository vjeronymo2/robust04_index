May 6, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

EARTH DAY PROTEST

Earth Day, 1990. San Diego Gas & Electric? General Dynamics? The Republican Party? What were they doing there?

Carolyn Chase, director of the San Diego Earth Day Coalition, rationalizes that people who are part of the problem need to be part of the solution.

Perhaps, but they do not need (nor deserve) to be part of an environmental event.

SDG&E, General Dynamics and the Republican Party are responsible for immeasurable harm to our environment. Not only were they allowed to set up sizable booths, but they were given prominent exposure, whereas the grass-roots environmental groups were positioned close to a mile away from the main event.

Allowing SDG&E, General Dynamics and the Republicans to participate at the Earth Day event is like making the racist South African government the guest of honor at a "Human Rights" event.

Shame on you, San Diego Earth Day Coalition!

BARBARA GARTNER

La Jolla
