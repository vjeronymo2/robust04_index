Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Report by Jasna Niksic] [Text] According to the latest information from Dobrinja Hospital, this morning in Dobrinja, Chetnik shells killed eight people, including two children, and 26 people were seriously or lightly wounded. After they were administered first aid, six people were immediately transferred to the City Hospital, and others received medical treatment and were then hospitalized in Dobrinja Hospital. The first results of the expert military teams in Dobrinja say that three shells were fired. Two of them were certainly of 120-mm caliber and the third is assumed to be of 82 mm. As the first results of the investigation show, the bombs were fired from Lukavica, that is, the Chetnik lair of the Slobodan Princip-Seljo Barracks. I hope we shall be able to provide even more precise information in our next newscast.
