BFN [Text] Budapest, 3 May (MTI) -- Over 1,000 people demonstrated silently in front of the Budapest center of the Hungarian Socialist Party [MSZP] on Tuesday evening, urging voters to remember the Socialist past and calling for party chief Gyula Horn's resignation. Spokeswoman Nora Keresztes read out a statement every 15 minutes to warn that "ignorance or forgetfulness might bring bad decisions with grave consequences". The statement urged Socialist Party Chairman Gyula Horn to examine his past and abandon politics and the MSZP chairmanship.
