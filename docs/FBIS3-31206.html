Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES, 15/02/1994 -- Croatian President Franjo Tudjman, now visiting Romania, had an interview at the Chamber of Deputies headquarters with the parliament's president and speaker, Oliviu Gherman and Adrian Nastase, on February 15 morning. They exchanged information and views on the organization and functioning of the legislative institutions in the two countries. Romanian parliamentary representatives offered the Romanian legislative forum's involvement in the peace process in the former Yugoslavia, especially in Bosnia-Herzegovina. The Croatian president showed interest in the Romanian offer. The interview was attended by Ioan Lup, vice-president of the Senate, and Ion Ratiu, deputy speaker of the Chamber of Deputies. President Tudjman will meet today also with Romanian Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu, will take a short trip to the Prahova Valley, and meet with representatives with the Croatian minority of Romania later in the evening.
