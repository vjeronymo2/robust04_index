BFN [Text] Yahiya Husayn al-Arashi, the Yemeni minister of civil service and administrative reform, arrived in our country today bringing a message from President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih to his majesty the king. The Yemeni envoy said in a statement to the television: [Begin al-Arashi recording] His Excellency President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih has entrusted me with delivering a written message to his brother, His Majesty King Hassan II. This is no doubt an opportunity to give his majesty a picture of developments in the situation in the Yemeni arena and the discussions that are taking place in connection with the national issue and the democratic process in Yemen. It is also a suitable opportunity to report on the recent developments in this sphere, and as usual we will be listening to his majesty on matters which would be of help to us. We know that he follows the Yemeni issue closely. Moreover, the Yemeni issue concerns everyone in the Arab arena, and particularly in sisterly Morocco. [end recording]
