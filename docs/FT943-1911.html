940922 FT 22 SEP 94 / World Trade News: Whirlpool to invest in German operations Whirlpool, the US white goods group, is investing DM300m (Dollars 191m) in its German operations after year-long talks with employees and trade unions which will lead to improved productivity and competitiveness at its German plants, writes Andrew Baxter. The investment underlines the US company's commitment to manufacturing in Germany following appreciation of the D-Mark against other European currencies. Production of a range of built-in freezers is to be concentrated at Calw in Germany.
