BFN [Text] Beijing, June 23 (XINHUA) -- China sincerely hopes that the parties concerned in Yemen will realize a cease-fire as soon as possible and find a solution through negotiations, a Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman said here today. The spokesman was commenting on the fact that mediation by UN Special Envoy Lahdar Al Ibrahimi [name as received] on the question of Yemen was foiled and that the fighting between the North and South is continuing, at the weekly press conference here this afternoon. "We hope mediation efforts by the parties concerned will be successful," the spokesman said.
