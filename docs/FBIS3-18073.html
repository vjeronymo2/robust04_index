Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Defense Minister Julian Garcia Vargas is being kept up to date about the situation following the downing of the Serb aircraft, so Defence Ministry sources have told Radio Nacional. Garcia Vargas is in constant touch with the commanders of the Madrid Group [the current Spanish UN Protection Force [UNPROFOR] detachment] in Bosnia and with the NATO and UNPROFOR chiefs. The UN High Commission for Refugees, UNHCR, has decided to suspend humanitarian aid convoys for the present. The Spanish blue helmets remain watchful, awaiting developments.
