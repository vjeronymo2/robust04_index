930720 FT 20 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Bankers cut holding in IBC to 8% International Business Communications (Holdings), the publishing, conference and training group, has been informed that Williams de Broe, its broker, has agreed to acquire 4.48m shares in the company from certain of the group's bankers. The agreement follows the allotment of new ordinary shares to be issued pursuant to the placing, open offer and conversion of debenture stock and the consolidation of the existing ordinaries. Of those shares, which will represent 11.5 per cent of the enlarged and consolidated share capital, 4.28m have been placed with institutional and other investors. As a result of the acquisition and placing, the aggregate shareholding of the banks will be reduced to 8 per cent.
