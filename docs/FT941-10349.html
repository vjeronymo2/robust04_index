940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / Cost-cutting at JAL Japan Airlines (JAL) is studying the 'system' of the official labour adjustment fund as an 'option' in its large-scale cost-cutting plan, a company spokesman said yesterday, AP-DJ reports from Tokyo. But it has not decided whether to apply for the subsidy, which is paid by the government to cover part of the labour costs in struggling industries, he added.
