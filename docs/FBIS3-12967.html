Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 7 Mar (INA)--A responsible source at the Iraqi Central Bank has confirmed that foreign currency can be bought and sold through the branches of the licensed commercial banks in Baghdad and the governorates and through the companies licensed to buy and sell foreign currencies. In a statement to INA, the source said that these branches and companies have the financial and technical resources to help citizens buy and sell foreign currencies easily and in a legitimate way. Such dealings, the source added, will also ensure a safe dealing in national and foreign currencies.
