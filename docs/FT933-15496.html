930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / UK Company News: EFM Japan Trust net assets jump 65% EFM Japan Trust, which was launched in July 1992 with the aim of providing long-term capital growth through investment in Japanese equities, reported a net asset value of 158p per share as at May 31. The figure represented a rise of some 65 per cent since the trust's incorporation on June 8 1992. Net revenue for the period amounted to Pounds 62,000, largely interest received on cash prior to investment. The trust has been fully invested since September. Earnings per share emerged at 0.62p and a dividend of 0.4p is proposed.
