Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 7 Mar (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin has sent a message to the Balkar people in connection with the sad anniversary of their forced resettlement. On the solemn 50th anniversary of the forced resettlement of the Balkars, the text of the message circulated today reads: "I bow my head low to the memory of those who died an untimely death in exile and express my sincere sympathy and condolences to their families and friends." As president of the Russian Federation, the message continues, "I offer my apologies for the injustice committed."
