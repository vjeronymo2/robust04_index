920225 FT 25 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Capita Capita Group, which provides management services to the public sector, continued its growth in 1991 with a 37 per cent increase in pre-tax profits. The outcome of Pounds 3.45m (Pounds 2.51m) was generated from turnover ahead 23 per cent to Pounds 24.7m (20.1m). Earnings per share worked through at 15.7p (13.7p). The final dividend is 3.6p on increased capital for a total of 5.4p (4.5p).
