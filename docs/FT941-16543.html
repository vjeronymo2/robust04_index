940110 FT 10 JAN 94 / New Aslef leader takes over today Mr Lew Adams today takes over as general secretary of Aslef, the train drivers' union. Mr Adams has been assistant general secretary since 1990. He succeeds Mr Derrick Fullick, who retires after four years. 'With the passing of the privatisation legislation we face an unsettled and unsettling period on the railways,' Mr Adams said yesterday.
