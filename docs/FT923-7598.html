920817 FT 17 AUG 92 / International Company News: Beazer settles Koppers case Beazer, the construction company, has agreed to pay a Dollars 760,000 (Pounds 398,000) penalty to settle a civil case filed in association with the acquisition of Koppers Co., the US Justice Department announced. The government charged Beazer with failing to notify the Justice Department and the Federal Trade Commission that it had acquired more than Dollars 15m in Koppers securities. Beazer violated the regulations in 1987 and 1988, the government claimed. Beazer, which paid Dollars 1.8bn for Koppers, was purchased last year by Hanson.
