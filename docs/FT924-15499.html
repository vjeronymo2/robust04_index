921003 FT 03 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Carlisle interim losses deepen Carlisle Group, the property services company, reported increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 707,000 in the first half of 1992, against Pounds 549,000. Turnover fell 22 per cent from Pounds 2.54m to Pounds 1.98m. Mr Jonathan Harris, chairman, said trading conditions had been extremely difficult and the cutting of costs continued. Losses per share were 2.1p (2.7p) after a tax credit this time of Pounds 143,000. Again there is no dividend.
