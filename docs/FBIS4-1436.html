BFN [Report by E. van Wyk] [Text] Pretoria April 27 SAPA -- Freedom Front leader Constand Viljoen was accompanied by a large group of former police and defence force generals when he cast his vote for his party at the Voortrekkerhoogte Town Hall on Wednesday [27 April]. Gen Viljoen told the media that he condemned the Jan Smuts Airport bomb blast and urged those responsible to stop the bombings and rather vote for the Freedom Front to strengthen the party's hand in future negotiations. Gen Viljoen described his voting in the country's first ever democratic election as the "last possible peaceful way to achieve the goal of self-determination".
