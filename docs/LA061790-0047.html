June 17, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SANTA FE VIEWPOINT

My daughter and I just returned last week from a trip to Santa Fe, which (made me) interested me in your June 3 article on that town by Jerry Hulse.

I would agree with your restaurant recommendations, with the exception of the Coyote Cafe. We had been looking forward to a return visit to this trendy restaurant since our first visit in 1987. What a disappointment! We suggest an alternative just a block away: the restaurant in the Hotel St. Francis. You'll find real Santa Fe hospitality there in addition to Old World charm.

SONJA ROBINSON

Lomita
