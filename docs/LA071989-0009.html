July 19, 1989, Wednesday, Valley Edition

CSUN STUDIES REPORT ON CAMPUS ARENA

Cal State Northridge might announce as early as Friday the results of a feasibility study on an on-campus, indoor sports arena.

The report could result in a cooperative venture between the school and the Ogden Corp., a New York-based conglomerate that has been gathering data on the proposed facility for more than a month.

Jane Kato, executive assistant to Elliott Mininberg, a CSUN vice president, said Tuesday that the school had received a detailed report from Ogden on Monday and might respond publicly to its findings by the end of the week.
