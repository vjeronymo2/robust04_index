BFN [Excerpt] Sofia, May 5 (BTA) -- [passage omitted] The government approved a draft agreement on cultural cooperation between the ministries of culture of Bulgaria and Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] in 1994-1996. Minister of Culture Ivaylo Znepolski was authorized to sign the agreement drafted "in the official languages of the two countries". Macedonian Minister of Culture Guner Ismail is expected to visit Bulgaria early in May but the date has not been specified yet, the government Press Office announced.
