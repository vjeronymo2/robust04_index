921221 FT 21 DEC 92 / US and Russia make more cuts in nuclear weapons The US and Russia have made 'real progress' in talks on additional cuts in long-range nuclear weapons but are not yet ready to sign a Start-2 treaty, President George Bush said yesterday, Reuter reports from Washington. Russian President Boris Yeltsin surprised officials in Washington last week when he said a Start-2 pact would be signed before Mr Bush stepped down.
