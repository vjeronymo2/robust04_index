December 8, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Bogus Bogie Coat: Christie's East, the New York auction house, has withdrawn an item listed as the raincoat Humphrey Bogart wore in "Casablanca" from next week's sale of Hollywood memorabilia. It turns out that Bogart didn't really wear the trench coat in "Casablanca," only in a promotion for the movie. Christie's experts had been expecting the coat to bring as much as $20,000. SHAUNA SNOW
