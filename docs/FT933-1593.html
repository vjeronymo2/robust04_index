930923 FT 23 SEP 93 / People: London East Training and Enterprise Council London East Training and Enterprise Council has appointed Susan Fey as its new chief executive following a period of several months during which the Tec had an acting chief executive. Letec, whose former chief executive resigned, has had a difficult time financially because of a substantial claw back of public funds after it fell foul of government accounting procedures. Fey was formerly chief executive of the City Technology Colleges Trust and she brings a wide experience of working in secondary and further education and training.
