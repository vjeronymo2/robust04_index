BFN [Text] PRC Vice Foreign Minister Tian Zengpei arrived in Damascus today on a visit to Syria that will last for several days. He was received at Damascus International Airport by Rislan 'Allush, the assistant foreign minister, a number of Foreign Ministry officials, and the PRC ambassador in Damascus and embassy staff. In a statement to the SANA correspondent, Tian Zengpei expressed his extreme happiness at visiting Syria and said: China and Syria are two friendly countries whose peoples are tied by traditional friendship. Over the past 38 years of diplomatic relations between our two countries, our friendship and cooperation witnessed satisfactory progress based on the five principles of peaceful coexistence. Our two countries continued to exchange sympathy and support on the international arena, holding close or identical views on many important international issues. The Chinese official said: Increasing visits and consultations between our two countries in this age, which is characterized by a changing international climate, assumes great importance for increasing understanding and strengthening friendship. I am looking forward to meeting with the Syrian officials concerned to exchange views on international and regional issues of common concern and to look for ways of developing bilateral relations. I will witness the great achievements realized by the Syrian people under the leadership of President Hafiz al-Asad in the field of expanding national production and developing the country. Concluding his statement, Tian Zengpei said: I am certain that this visit of mine will contribute toward further increasing and strengthening ties of cooperation and friendship between our two countries.
