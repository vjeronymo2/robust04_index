BFN [Text] Afonso Dhlakama, leader of the Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo], has reiterated that should his party win the October elections, it will form a government of national unity. In an interview with Radio Mozambique, Dhlakama stressed that the strategy of his movement is within the framework of national reconciliation. He added that he would like all parties to be voted to participate in the government, because, he said, only by consolidating efforts and everybody's experience can we build our country. Dhlakama was speaking when he returned to Maringue yesterday, where he attended Renamo's special congress, which confirmed him as a candidate for the October presidential elections.
