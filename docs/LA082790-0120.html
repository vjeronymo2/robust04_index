August 27, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

TEAM USA HOCKEY COACH REHIRED

Former Minneapolis Southwest High School hockey Coach Dave Peterson has been rehired to guide Team USA in the 1992 Winter Olympics at Albertville, France.

Peterson's 1988 team didn't qualify for the medal round and finished seventh overall. He coached at Southwest for 29 years before he became an assistant to Coach Lou Vairo for the 1984 Winter Games.
