BFN [Text] An air route between Tianjin and Moscow via Urumqi and Novosibirsk will begin service on 10 June. Thus, one more flight connects Moscow, the capital of Russia, with the inland cities of China. The air route is 1,080 km long. A Model-15 passenger plane possessed by the (?Xinjiang) Air Line will be put into flight and take off from the Tianjin Airport at 1310 on every Friday.
