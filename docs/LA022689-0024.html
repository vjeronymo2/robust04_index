February 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

GOLDEN OLDIE

My wife, who disapproves of my own cigar smoking, does not believe George Burns smokes them and says he uses them only as props. Can you reaffirm for me that George does, indeed, smoke 20 cigars a day?

CARROLL F. MARQUARD

Rancho Sante Fe

Margy Rochlin responds: After spending considerable time with Burns, I consider myself something of an expert on his habits. Please assure your wife that he definitely smokes -- but doesn't inhale -- 15 to 20 cigars a day, and I have the dry-cleaning bills to prove it.
