920908 FT 08 SEP 92 / Management (In a Nutshell): Library directory is just the ticket A comprehensive guide to the business information available in public and academic libraries is to be published by the Business Information Network (BIN), an association of libraries and other information providers. The Directory of Members, to be launched later this month, will include information on the availability of statistics, trade journals and on-line services. The network, set up last year, now has 60 members in the UK as well as contacts with libraries in Denmark and Czechoslovakia. The directory costs Pounds 150 including four quarterly updates. Contact BIN, 25 Southampton Buildings, London WC2A 1AW.
