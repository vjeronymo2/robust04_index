Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Armenian Defense Ministry press service reports that a relative calm has prevailed on Armenia's borders for the last two days. No incidents have been registered. Azerbaijani planes violated the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic's [NKR] air space several times today. The NKR Defense Army press service reported that cluster bombs were dropped on Stepanakert's suburbs at around 1330. The consequences of the bombing is being determined. The enemy intensively shelled the Karabakh positions in the north of Mardakertskiy Rayon along Matagis-Akop-Kamari line this afternoon. The Armenian fighters resorted to counter measures. No substantial changes took place on the rest of the fronts; a relative calm was maintained.
