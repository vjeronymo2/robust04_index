Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] Speaking at a news conference on 27 March, Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata commented on countermeasures Japan will take if the UN Security Council decides to impose economic sanctions against North Korea over the issue of suspected development of nuclear weapons, and said: "We would like to take responsible measures to deal with the situation as a member of the United Nations should the UN show us some kind of direction." In this way, he stressed a view that Japan will act in accordance with the UN decision.
