Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today Ruslan Khasbulatov, Aleksandr Rutskoy, Albert Makashov, Andrey Dunayev, Viktor Anpilov, Vladislav Achalov, and Ilya Konstantinov left the investigation ward of the Lefortovo prison. Aleksandr Barkashov was released today from the Matrosskaya Tishina prison. They have been released in accordance with the State Duma's anmesty resolution. What is the mood of the former prisoners who came back to freedom today? Here are some statements of those released: Anpilov said that if the prices during his imprisonment have gone up, he will struggle on to the victorious end. He sees his task in summoning the working collectives to fight against the regime. Replying to the question on what he intends to do in the near future, Aleksandr Barkashov said he would do the same as before. Rutskoy described his release from prison as a return to the people. Khasbulatov told journalists that he did not think he was to blame. Makashov said that he intends to go home to Samara for a rest.
