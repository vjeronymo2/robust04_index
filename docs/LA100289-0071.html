October 2, 1989, Monday, San Diego County Edition

TWO BODIES FOUND IN LEUCADIA HOME

Sheriff's deputies called to a Leucadia home Sunday morning made a gruesome discovery. The decomposing bodies of two family members were found in separate parts of the home on Eolus Avenue.

The county coroner's office identified the dead as John Michael Flagg, 46, and his mother, Sybil Flagg.

Homicide Sgt. Chuck Curtis said the two may have been dead for more than a week.

Another resident of the home, Sally Flagg, sister and daughter of the deceased, told homicide detectives that her brother had come home drunk earlier Sunday and had tried to break into her room. She was being held for questioning.
