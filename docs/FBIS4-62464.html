BFN [Text] There was an explosion in the hall of the Tbilisi Shaumyan Armenian drama theater today at about 1000 GMT just before the beginning of a performance. According to a report received from the Isani district police of Tbilisi, the explosion did not cause deaths. The hall has been damaged. Fourteen wounded people have been taken for treatment to the No. 1 city hospital. The site of the incident has been visited by the leaders of the republic and representatives of the Interior Ministry and the Security Service. A formal criminal case has been instituted and a criminal investigation has been launched in connection with this event.
