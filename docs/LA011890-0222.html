January 18, 1990, Thursday, South Bay Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Water supply -- A story in the Jan. 14 South Bay Edition said incorrectly that most of the South Bay's drinking water is drawn from local aquifers. According to the West Basin Municipal Water District, wells provide about a quarter of the South Bay's drinking water. The rest is drawn from water supplies diverted to the Los Angeles area from Northern California and the Colorado River.
