BFN [Text] A party functionaries delegation of the Communist Party of China [CPC] led by Bai Keming, deputy head of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee, left Pyongyang by train on 4 May. Kim Yang-kon, vice director of a department of the Workers Party of Korea Central Committee; and the counselor of the PRC Embassy in our country saw off the delegation at Pyongyang Station.
