BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondent Viktor Dmitriyev] [Text] Santiago May 24 TASS -- "Chilean President Eduardo Frei Ruiz Tagle shares my opinion that only strong presidential power, combined with fruitful parliamentary activities, can ensure changes in a country," speaker of the Russian parliament upper chamber Vladimir Shumeyko said on Monday after a meeting with the Chilean leader at the La Moneda Palace. Shumeyko leads a Russian delegation touring Latin America. Replying to journalists, Shumeyko said that he conveyed a message by Russian President Boris Yeltsin to the Chilean president. According to the Russian speaker, the letter points to the importance of strengthening relations between countries which took a similar road of development -- Russia and Chile. "Our country has used to some extent Chilean experience in a search for civil accord," the message notes. Shumeyko expressed confidence that interstate and intergovernment relations between the two countries will be actively developed. He specially pointed to the importance of developing cooperation between Russian and Chilean businessmen, stating that Russia, as well as Chile, banks on the private sector in its economy. Later in the day, Shumeyko and other delegation members had a meeting with president of the Chilean Senate Gabriel Valdes, where the sides also pointed to the importance of boosting Russian-Chilean relations. According to Valdes, Chile has record (for Latin America) investments in such foreign countries as Peru, Brazil, Argentina, etc. There is a possibility of making big Chilean investments in the Russian economy in future, the Senate president said. "We do this not because we are rich but due to the fact that our economy is open," Valdes stressed.
