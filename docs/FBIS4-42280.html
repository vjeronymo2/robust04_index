BFN [Statement by State of Emergency Region Governor's Office on 1 June -- read by announcer] [Text] During operations and searches, conducted by security forces in the state of emergency region on 31 May and 1 June with the aim of apprehending and neutralizing militants of the illegal separatist terrorist organization, 38 terrorists -- one of them a woman -- were killed in Alanduzu region to the east of Hakkari Province. In addition, 12 terrorists were killed in the rural sector of Siirt's Baykan District, and six terrorists were killed in the Ovakoy border region in Sirnak's Silopi District. The terrorists' rifles, rocket launchers, and hand grenades were also seized. Six members of the security forces were martyred during the operations.
