931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / UK Company News: F&M may exercise long lease option Fortnum & Mason has made proposals to George Weston Holdings, its parent, for the exercise of an option allowing it to acquire the long lease, which has 113 years to run, on its Piccadilly premises for Pounds 1.75m. F&M has been advised that the open market value of the long lease would be Pounds 9.73m while that of its exisiting short lease was Pounds 7.25m.
