BFN [Text] Gyula Horn has become the MSZP [Hungarian Socialist Party] candidate for the post of prime minister. At the emergency congress which has just ended 431 delegates voted for him out of the 450. The delegates also decided unanimously that they will ask the SZDSZ [Alliance of Free Democrats] to become the MSZP coalition partner. They have already sent the letter outlining this to Ivan Petoe, chairman of the SZDSZ. In the letter the MSZP emphasizes that the interest of the country would be served by a joint government by the two parties. The Socialists are ready for a compromise in every essential issue, the letter states.
