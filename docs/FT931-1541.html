930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Sunleigh Sunleigh, the USM-quoted leisure group, sharply reduced its pre-tax losses from Pounds 2.47m to Pounds 557,000 in 1992 on turnover ahead 42 per cent from Pounds 9.09m to Pounds 12.9m. The group's two largest subsidiaries returned to operating profits. Losses per share improved from 3.91p to 0.86p.
