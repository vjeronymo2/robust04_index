930807 FT 07 AUG 93 / Brussels soda ash inquiry The European Commission yesterday said it was opening an anti-dumping inquiry into US soda ash imports into the European Community, Reuter reports from Brussels. It acted on a complaint from the European Chemical Industry Council, which claimed the imports concerned had risen to 618,469 tonnes last year from 269,225 tonnes in 1991 and 48,269 tonnes in 1990.
