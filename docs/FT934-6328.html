931125 FT 25 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Tex Tex Holdings, a provider of consumables to the plastic, steel and energy industries, returned to the black in the six months to September 30. Profits before tax of Pounds 486,000 were achieved after charges of Pounds 104,000 for factory closure and redundancy costs. Last time losses were Pounds 146,000. Turnover expanded to Pounds 12.3m (Pounds 7.9m). Earnings were 5.1p (1.6p) per share.
