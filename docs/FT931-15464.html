930113 FT 13 JAN 93 / International Company News: Murdoch recruits Hinton Mr Rupert Murdoch has recruited Mr Les Hinton, a long-time executive of News Corporation, as chairman and chief executive of Fox Television Stations, the US network. Mr Hinton, who will also oversee Fox News, is a former editor of The Star, a US tabloid magazine. Since 1991 he has run News America Publishing. which publishes the TV Guide and the Boston Herald. Mr Hinton will be flanked by Mr Van Gordon Sauter, president of Fox News, and Mr Mitchell Stern, who was named president of Fox Television Stations.
