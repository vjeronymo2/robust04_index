December 7, 1990, Friday, Southland Edition

BRIEFLY

Boeing Gets 3 Orders for 747-400: Cargolux Airlines International of Luxembourg has ordered three Boeing 747-400 freighters and taken options on three others, an order worth a potential $1 billion, Boeing reported. Engine selection will be announced later, Boeing said. Total orders for the 747-400, Boeing's biggest and most profitable plane, stand at 397.
