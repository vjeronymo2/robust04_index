BFN [SEGODNYA report: "IAEA Establishes Contacts With the FIS"] [Text] Yevgeniy Primakov, director of the Russian Foreign Intelligence Service [FIS], met yesterday with IAEA General Director Hans Blix, who is in Moscow on an official visit, the FIS press bureau has said. The collection of information about the proliferation and development of weapons of mass destruction has always been among special services' priority tasks, and the IAEA draws on intelligence data of the U.S. and British intelligence services it has been receiving not only through their governments; this is why Mr. Blix is also planning to establish close cooperation with the Russian intelligence service.
