Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] I shall continue in my post until I am released in writing by the president in accordance with the Constitution, Marek Borowski said today in an interview for the Polish Radio Third Program. [Begin Borowski recording] My resignation is final. The objective of the meeting of the government coalition yesterday was not to negotiate the matter but to explain the problem to one another and to think about what to do next so that problems like this will not arise in the future, as well as to part without anger. I think we have succeeded in doing this. I shall stay on in the post until I am released in writing by the president in accordance with the Constitution. [end recording]
