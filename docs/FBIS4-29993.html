BFN [Text] A delegation from the Hanoi Agricultural and Forestry Service of the SRV, headed by its chief Nguyen Ton Tao, arrived in Vientiane on 16 June along with a number of technicians to visit Laos at the invitation of Thongmi Phommisai, head of the of Vientiane Municipality Agricultural and Forestry Service. Delegations from the two sides will consult and exchange technical experiences with one another with the aim of improving the efficiency of agricultural and forestry cooperation between the two capitals -- Vientiane and Hanoi -- in the immediate future. At the same time, during its one-week stay in Laos the Hanoi agricultural and forestry delegation will visit such production establishments as the Thongvai Fruit Trees and Seeds Center, the Nongheao Seeds Experimental Center, and the Tha Ngon Plain Production Center.
