BFN [Text] Two people have been slightly injured after four [number as heard] concussion grenades [ses bombasi], set with a timing mechanism, exploded in Istanbul's Harem Station. The grenades, planted at the train station by unidentified persons, went off at short intervals beginning at approximately 2300 last night. Sevki Mustafaoglu was injured in the leg and taken to Haydarpasa Hospital, and Murat Yaman was treated on the scene. Officials stated that one of the concussion grenades was placed inside a telephone booth, one at a coffee shop, and another at the back entrance to a police station in the train station. An investigation is under way. Meanwhile, at around the same time, a Molotov cocktail was thrown at Sisli's Bag-Kur [Social Security Association for Tradesmen, Artisans, and Independent Workers] building. No one was injured in the incident.
