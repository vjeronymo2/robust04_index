July 12, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

LOCAL;

12-HR. SWEEP NETS 120 PROSTITUTES

A task force of 80 vice officers arrested more than 120 male and female prostitutes in Hollywood and surrounding neighborhoods during a 12-hour period ending early today, police said.

The prostitutes were arrested between 2 p.m. Tuesday and 2 a.m. today in the Hollywood and Wilshire areas, vice officer Chuck Kiel said.
