Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Today's AL-INQADH AL-WATANI newspaper carries a report from Kampala saying that a group of 51 armed rebels from rebel Garang's faction attacked the camp housing Sudanese refugees at (Dango) in Zaire, with the aim of liquidating some elements. Four citizens in the camp were killed. Firing continued for over (?an hour). The report states that the Zairian forces succeeded in capturing one of the rebels, after which the group of rebel Garang threatened to kill the official in charge of (Dango), the police officer, and the official in charge of the refugees, unless a detained rebel who had participated in the attack was handed over. The Zairian authorities have condemned the aggression inflicted by the rebels on refugee camps and refused the rebel demand. In response to the rebel provocations, they have sent a large force of Zairian soldiers to secure the camp and the surrounding area. UNHCR headquarters in Geneva has been informed about the incident.
