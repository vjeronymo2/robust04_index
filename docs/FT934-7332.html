931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: Hopes and fears of a working prince Michael Cassell of the FT spent six days travelling in the Middle East with Prince Charles. He was given access to the prince's meetings with royal families, ministers and business leaders. In Monday's FT, Cassell reports on the prince's hopes and fears. His report provides a rare insight into the working side of the prince's life.
