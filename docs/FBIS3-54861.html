Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev--The Central Electoral Commission registered the Liberal Democratic Party of Ukraine [LDPU] on 3 January as the 20th party that will nominate candidates during the upcoming elections to the Ukrainian parliament. As Volodymyr Klymchuk, the party's leader told a UNIAN correspondent, the "LDPU is cautious about forming blocs with other political forces. It is, above all, afraid of the efforts that have been announced by some blocs to form excessively rigid structures that could hinder the opportunity to operate independently. However, this does not mean that the liberal democrats will halt contacts and consultations with other parties." According to Klymchuk, the communists and socialists are the only ones considered by the LDPU to be opponents.
