940312 FT 12 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Alliance Trust lifts asset value to Pounds 21.33 The net asset value of Alliance Trust was Pounds 21.33 per share at January 31 1994 compared with Pounds 17.79 a year earlier. Net available revenue for the year moved ahead to Pounds 23.9m (Pounds 23m) for earnings per share of 47.28p against 45.7p. A final dividend of 32.5p (31p) is proposed for a 47p (45p) total.
