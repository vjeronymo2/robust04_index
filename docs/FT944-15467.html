941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / International Company News: BES Engineering advances 10% at pre-tax level BES Engineering, Taiwan's recently-privatised construction company, reported pre-tax profits of TDollars 398m (USDollars 15.2m) in the first quarter ended September 30, up 10 per cent from a year earlier, writes Laura Tyson in Taipei. BES was one of the first state-owned companies to be privatised. As one of two government-owned contractors, BES was until 1992 granted preferential access to infrastructure contracts. Faced with challenges from legislators and private construction companies, it has since been obliged to participate in open bidding for projects. The company is estimated to have orders amounting to some TDollars 20bn
