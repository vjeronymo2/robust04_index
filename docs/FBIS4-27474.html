BFN ["Announcement" signed by King Phumiphon Adunyadet and countersigned by House of Representatives President Marut Bunnak; issued on 27 May, place not given] [Text] His royal signature: Phumiphon Adunyadet Whereas a royal decree was issued on 30 October 1992 appointing Police General Praman Adireksan as opposition leader in the House of Representatives, Police Gen. Praman has resigned as leader of the Chat Thai Party, thus disqualifying him as opposition leader in the House of Representatives. Therefore, he loses his position as per Article 116, Paragraph 3 of the 1991 Constitution. The king deems that Banhan Sinlapa-acha possesses the qualifications required by Article 116, Paragraph 3 of the 1991 Constitution and is suitable to be leader of the opposition in the House of Representatives. Therefore, he appoints Banhan Sinlapa-acha as opposition leader in the House of Representatives from this moment onward. Announced on 27 May 1994, the 49th year of the reign of the present king. Countersigned by House of Representatives President Marut Bunnak.
