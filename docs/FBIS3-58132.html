Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Bonn (DPA) -- A meeting between the Bosnian and Croatian presidents is to begin at the Petersberg government guesthouse in Bonn this evening, the Foreign Ministry has announced. Foreign Ministry Spokesman Martin Erdmann stated that the delayed departure of Bosnia-Herzegovina President Alija Izetbegovic has been confirmed by the Geneva office of the UN High Commissioner for Refugees. Izetbegovic left Sarajevo at 1435. Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel will probably welcome Izetbegovic and Croatian President Franjo Tudjman to Petersberg at around 1730. No further details are available. [passage omitted]
