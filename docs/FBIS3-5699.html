Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 9 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The Japanese Government renders as a gratis assistance to Mongolia agricultural machines and fertilizers for development of agricultural production. Thus, in 1953 [year as received] before the sowing works Japan has delivered to the country 14 tractors and 500 tonnes of fertilizers. Recently more than 20 tractors with ploughs have been dispatched from Japan to Mongolia.
