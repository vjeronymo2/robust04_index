941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / Sell-off plans for research body The Department of Trade and Industry plans to privatise the Laboratory of the Government Chemist by April 1996. Mr Michael Heseltine, trade and industry secretary, said last April that his preferred option was for the laboratory to become an independent non-profit-making company. But an advertisement by the DTI in today's Financial Times says that the government is also looking for a commercial buyer who will 'preserve LGC's independence'.
