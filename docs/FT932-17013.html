930402 FT 02 APR 93 / UK Company News: Pentland pays special interim Pentland Group, the sportswear distributor, is to pay a special interim dividend of 12p. The payment is as a result of exceptional profits of Pounds 47.3m made in 1992 from foreign exchange contracts entered into in the unfulfilled expectation that Pentland would acquire the balance of Adidas, and from the sale of its stake in the group.
