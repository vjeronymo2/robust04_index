October 23, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

SWISS JOBLESS RATE 0.5%

The number of unemployed in Switzerland declined another 2.6% in September to a total of 15,222 people, the Federal Industry and Labor Bureau reported today.

The overall jobless rate remained stable at 0.5%, it said.

Available jobs registered with employment offices also rose, however, increasing 2.3% to 17,677.
