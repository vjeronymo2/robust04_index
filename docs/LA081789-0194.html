August 17, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

SOUTHLAND;

LOTTO EXPECTED TO HIT $10 MILLION

No one picked all six winning numbers in Wednesday's $4.9-million Lotto game, state lottery officials said today.

There were three tickets with five correct numbers plus the bonus number, each worth $466,995. There were 212 tickets with five correct numbers, each worth $3,404 to the holders.

There were 11,507 tickets with four winning numbers, worth $57 each, and 227,943 tickets with three correct numbers, each worth an automatic $5.

Lottery officials said the six-of-six prize pool will roll over to Saturday's jackpot, making it worth an estimated $10 million.
