920317 FT 17 MAR 92 / Fraud charge DR Gerald Smith, a former chief executive of Farr, the Westbury-based construction company which went into liquidation in 1990, was charged by the Serious Fraud Office with 14 offences of theft totalling Pounds 1.2m. The charges follow an investigation by the SFO and Metropolitan Police. The indictment alleges that Dr Smith stole cash and shares from the Farr Group Pension Fund. Dr Smith appeared at Bow St Magistrates' Court yesterday and was remanded on bail of Pounds 100,000 until July 16.
