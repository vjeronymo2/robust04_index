Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] On the night of 21 January, 43 puppet military policemen and Para [Khmer People's National Liberation Front] soldiers together with over 200 people, who are enraged with the Vietnamese communist puppet ringleaders and the puppet district officials, attacked and lobbed grenades at the Vietnamese communist puppet ringleaders at Kompong Th'kov market in Kralanh District, the seat of Siem Reap Province. They killed a captain, two first lieutenants, two second lieutenants, and wounded some others. The surviving ringleaders and Vietnamese communist puppets' district officials fled in great panic. At the same time, the puppet and Para soldiers together with the people burned all the houses of the Vietnamese communist puppet ringleaders and seized the rice that they had plundered from the people.
