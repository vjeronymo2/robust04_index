940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: Winston Silcott appeal refused Home secretary Michael Howard decided not to refer the case of Winston Silcott, serving life for the murder of boxer Anthony Smith in 1984, back to the Court of Appeal. Silcott's conviction for the murder of Pc Keith Blakelock in the 1985 Broadwater Farm riots in Tottenham, north London, was quashed in 1991.
