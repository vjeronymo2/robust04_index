Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 21 Feb (MENA)--A high-level source in the Arab League said today that the Libyan Jamahiryah has sent an official memorandum to the secretariat general asking for the inclusion of the Libyan-Western crisis on the agenda of the Arab League Council meeting to be held on 27 March. The memorandum was sent following recent U.S. statements that Libya represents a threat to American national security.
