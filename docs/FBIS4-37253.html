BFN [Text] In the name of God the Compassionate, the Merciful. Republican Decree No. 11: After taking cognisance of the document declaring the Democratic Republic of Yemen and of the Constitution, and upon the proposition submitted by the prime minister's office and the defense minister, the Presidential Council has decided the following: Staff Colonel 'Umar Ali al-Attas is appointed chief of the general staff. This decree shall become effective from its date and shall be published in the official gazette. Signed: 'Ali Salim al-Bid, chairman of the Presidential Council
