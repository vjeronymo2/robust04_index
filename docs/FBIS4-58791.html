BFN [Text] Dr. Nabil Sha'th, head of the Palestinian negotiating team, has denied reports that he apologized to PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat for not wanting to join the new Palestinian national authority which will run the self-rule in Gaza and Jericho. In an interview with our correspondent Sa'id Mu'addi, Sha'th said he was not nominated to join this authority in the first place and that news agency reports on this issue were only rumors. He noted that no appointments have been announced yet.
