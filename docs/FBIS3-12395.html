Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 27 Feb (MENA)--Assistant Foreign Minister Badr Hammam today met with David Sultan, Israel's ambassador in Cairo. The Israeli ambassador stated after the meeting that they had discussed the attack on Palestinian worshipers at the al-Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron. He said that the Israeli Government is trying to take measures to prevent a repetition of such incidents. He added that the march to peace must not stop, because if it does that would mean that the extremists have succeeded in achieving their goals. In this regard, he affirmed the importance of resuming the peace march as quickly as possible so that the extremists do not reach their objectives.
