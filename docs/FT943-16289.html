940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / UK Company News: David Brown buys French gear makers David Brown Group, via its David Brown France Engrenages offshoot, has acquired three French industrial gears manufacturing and repair businesses, Weco, Cellard and DSN. The price is FFr12.6m (Pounds 1.5m), together with assumption of finance lease obligations of some FFr2.5m. Net assets being acquired had a value of about FFr11.9m as at June 1 1994; estimated operating profits for the 11 months to November 30 were FFr3.6m.
