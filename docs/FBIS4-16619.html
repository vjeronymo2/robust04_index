BFN [Text] Almaty 18 April, TASS -- Kazakhstan's President Nursultan Nazarbayev signed a decree appointing Kanat Saudabayev Kazakh foreign minister. Before the appointment, Saudabayev worked as the Kazakh ambassador to Turkey and before that was Kazakhstan's minister of culture. Saudabayev will replace Tuleutay Suleymenov who, according to an official version, has been assigned to another job.
