VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: SIMI VALLEY;

PLANNERS ENDORSE 3 HOUSING PROJECTS

The Simi Valley Planning Commission has recommended that the City Council approve three housing projects that would add nearly 600 homes to the city.

Commission members approved two large projects proposed by Milner Development Co. and VTN West Inc.

Milner Development is proposing 237 single-family houses on 62 acres near Tierra Rejada and Madera roads. VTN West is proposing 350 single-family houses on a 277-acre parcel south of the Arroyo Simi and Royal Avenue.

The commission also recommended approval of a request by Fred Madjar to divide a 2.73-acre lot into 11 single-family lots on the south side of Presidio Drive near Lathrop Avenue. KAREN McKEAN
