October 21, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

ALPHA LEAGUE

Village Christian 31, Marshall Fundamental 21 -- Rick Nickles scored two fourth-quarter touchdowns to lead Village Christian (4-3, 1-1) at Pasadena City College. Nickles' four-yard blast with 7:49 remaining increased Village Christian's lead to 24-13.
