August 5, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Mariners Bancorp: The San Clemente parent company of Mariners Bank posted net income of $253,212 for the second quarter, a 10% gain from the $230,214 reported for the 1989 second quarter.

Revenue rose 32% to $2.5 million from $1.9 million. Net income for the first half was $479,294, up 15% from $418,586 last year. Six-month revenue increased 27% to $4.7 million from $3.7 million. Total assets, as of June 30, were $71.6 million, up 28% from $56.1 million a year earlier.
