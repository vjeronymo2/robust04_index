BFN [Report from Lusaka by correspondent Alves Antonio] [Text] Members of the UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] delegation to the peace talks have already left Lusaka for Huambo. On board the UN aircraft that took off from the Lusaka International Airport at about 1500 on 20 May were Eugenio Manuvakola, Jorge Valentim, Isaias Samakuva, and others. Only Joao Vaikene and (Evaristo) Oliveira stayed behind. The talks are scheduled to resume either on 24 or 25 May.
