Description of Proposed Action: The existing project provides storage for downstream projects in the Kennebec River Basin, and consists of: (1) A gated, 19-foot-high concrete dam 570 feet long; (2) three smaller concrete closure dams; (3) a 140-foot-long earthen and concrete dike; (4) a 2,231-acre impoundment (known as Lake Moxie or Moxie Pond); and (5) appurtenant facilities. There are no power generation facilities. Licensees propose to sell the project facilities to the town of Forks Plantation, Maine (the town in which the project is located), after licensees perform certain structural improvements and repairs. Thereafter the town will operate the project for non-jurisdictional purposes such as environmental, recreational, and aesthetic enhancement, according to the licensees. l.
