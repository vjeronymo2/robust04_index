930225 FT 25 FEB 93 / Parliament and Politics: Committee to examine pensions THE COMMONS social security committee plans to examine the handling of the pension funds of a number of formerly state-owned companies, as part of an inquiry into the government's plans for the pension funds of soon-to-be-privatised businesses. Harland & Wolff, the Belfast shipbuilder, Swan Hunter, its Tyneside counterpart, and National Bus Company are among the once public-sector entities whose pension funds the cross-party body plans to look at.
