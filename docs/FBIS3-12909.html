Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By Sa'd al-Silawi] [Excerpt] Informed Palestinian sources have mentioned that intensive contacts are under way to convene a meeting of the cordon states in Cairo. The sources did not specify the proposed date for this meeting. However, they emphasized that all parties have agreed to hold such a meeting in which the foreign ministers of Jordan, Syria, Lebanon, Egypt, and the PLO will take part. The sources said that the meeting's agenda will cover the suspension of negotiations with Israel in light of the 25 February Hebron massacre. [passage omitted]
