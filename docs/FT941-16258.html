940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Dragon Oil back in the black Dragon Oil, the Dublin-based mineral prospecting and oil and gas exploration group, turned round from a Dollars 313,000 loss to an after-tax profit of Dollars 506,000 (Pounds 342,000) in the year to October 31. The company said the acquisition of Kirkland in May had created an international exploration and production company with a strong focus on the Far East. Production volumes had averaged 1,480 barrels of oil equivalent per day since the Kirkland purchase. The raising of Dollars 9.8m of equity capital had been completed in June to support an international exploration programme including drilling at least 7 wells over the next year.
