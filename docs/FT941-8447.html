International Atomic Energy Agency board of governors meeting in Vienna. Financial Times holds 'London Motor Conference'. The Euro Disney banks will receive final version of an audit of the group's finances. President's Day holiday in US. TUESDAY: Capital expenditure and stockbuilding (fourth quarter-provisional). UK output, income and expenditure (fourth quarter). US consumer confidence (February); budget deficit. Walt Disney Co holds annual shareholders meeting in Orlando. Asian Aerospace and Defence Technology Exhibition opens in Singapore. WEDNESDAY: European parliament in session in Brussels (until February 24). Mr Zhu Rongji, vice-premier of China, starts visit to Japan. London Fashion Week starts (until Saturday). THURSDAY: Engineering sales and orders at current and constant prices (December). Energy trends (December). New vehicle registrations (January). US durable goods (January). EU discusses the admission of Austria, Finland, Norway and Sweden at a special ministerial meeting in Brussels. Mr Yasuhiro Nakasone, former Japanese prime minister, to address Chatham House in London on the changing role of Japan. British Gas results. FRIDAY: CBI monthly trends enquiry (February). Balance of trade with countries outside the EU (January). Prime ministers of Finland, Sweden and Norway meet in Helsinki. Final date for acceptance of Granada's bid for LWT.
