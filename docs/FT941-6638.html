940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / Management: Government urged to produce report - In a Nutshell Sir Michael Grylls MP, chairman of the Conservative backbench committee on trade and industry, has been persuaded to withdraw a private members' bill calling for the government to produce an annual report on smaller companies. But the government is still likely to produce some form of report. Lord Strathclyde, small business' minister, says he is keen to have such a report.
