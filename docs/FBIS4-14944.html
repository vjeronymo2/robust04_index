BFN [Text] Radio broadcasting to Ukraine of programs by the radio stations Mayak, Rossiya-1 and Radio Moscow was stopped today from 15:00. Well-informed sources from the Ukrainian Communications Ministry suggested in an interview with Interfax-Ukraine that this was due to Russia's debt for relaying of the radio programs. The same sources said the debt of Russia amounted to 2.3 Bn rubles. The Ukrainian Radio and TV Company's Vice President, Aleksey Karpenko, sent a telegram to Moscow today saying that the broadcasting would not be renewed until the debt was paid.
