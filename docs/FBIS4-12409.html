BFN [Text] At 1230 today, His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad received Hojjat ol-Eslam Hasan Ruhani, Iranian deputy Majles speaker and chairman of the Majles Foreign Relations Committee, and the delegation accompanying him. The meeting was attended by Dr. 'Adil Jamus, deputy speaker of the People's Assembly; Shakir Is'ayyid, chairman of the Arab and Foreign Relations Committee of the People's Assembly; and Iranian Ambassador in Damascus Mohammad Hasan Akhtari-Foruqi. Also at 0930 today, Vice President 'Abd-a-Halim Khaddam received Hojjat ol-Eslam Hasan Ruhani and his delegation. The meeting was attended by 'Adil Jamus, Shakir Is'ayyid, and Mohammad Hasan Akhtari-Foruqi. The talks dealt with the situation in the region, the latest developments in the Arab-Israeli conflict, the international state of affairs, and bilateral ties.
