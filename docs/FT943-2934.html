940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / Observer: Reassuring So Lloyd's thinks that someone somewhere could come up with a better stamp for the reinsurance company which will ringfence Names' old year liabilities. So far provisionally known by the Orwellian title NewCo, the company will be one of the biggest reinsurers in the world, alongside Munich Re and Swiss Re. As part of its plan to have the operation ready for DTI authorisation next year, Lloyd's has held a competition among staff to come up with a more fitting moniker. One unkind suggestion doing the rounds at the reinsurers' Monte Carlo rendezvous last week was Pen-u-Re.
