Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Text] Work on the main portion of the Daheko hydropower station has officially begun. The station will have an installed capacity of 69,000 kilowatts. Total investment will be 337 million yuan. Located in the Qian Jiang region, Youyang County [of Sichuan Province], the station will be on the Apeng River, a tributary of the Wu Jiang. The height of the dam will be 84.5 meters and the reservoir capacity will be 115 million cubic meters. The station should generate some 375 million kilowatt-hours of electricity a year.
