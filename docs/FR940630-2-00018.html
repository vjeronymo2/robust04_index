Department of the Navy Board of Visitors to the United States Naval Academy; Meeting Pursuant to the provisions of the Federal Advisory Committee Act (5 U.S.C. App. 2), notice is hereby given that the Board of Visitors to the United States Naval Academy will meet on July 25, 1994, at the Russell Senate Office Building, Washington, DC, at 8:30 a.m. The session will be open to the public. The purpose of the meeting is to make such inquiry as the Board shall deem necessary into the state of morale and discipline, the curriculum, instruction, physical equipment, fiscal affairs, and academic methods of the Naval Academy. For further information concerning this meeting contact: LCDR Timothy A. Batzler, U.S. Navy, Secretary to the Board of Visitors, Office of the Superintendent, United States Naval Academy, Annapolis, MD 20404-5000, telephone (410) 293-1503. Dated: June 24, 1994. Lewis T.
