Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:CSO [Report by HINA: "Biography of Zubak"] [Text] Kresimir Zubak, president of the presidential council of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna, was born on 29 November 1947 in Doboj and is a lawyer by training. He entered the political life of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna as a representative of the Croatian Defense Council of Usora. Before the war he carried out duties in judicial bodies. Immediately before the outbreak of the war he was president of the Supreme Court in Doboj. He has served as vice president of the Croatian Community of Herceg-Bosna -- as it was called at that time -- and was later elected minister of justice and administration of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna.
