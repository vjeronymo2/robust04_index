BFN [By reporter Liang Rongke (4731 2837 4430)] [Text] Montevideo, 20 Apr (XINHUA) -- A CPC delegation headed by Hu Jintao, member of the Political Bureau Standing Committee and Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, flew out of here this morning for a visit to Argentina, after successfully concluding a two-day official goodwill visit to Uruguay. Seeing them off at the airport were Jose Luis Puig, president of Uruguay's ruling Blanco Party; Daniel Hugo Martins, minister of national defense; and others. PRC Ambassador to Uruguay Xie Rumao also was present at the airport. During his visit, Hu Jintao held successive meetings with Uruguayan President Lacalle, Vice President and Congress President Aguirre Ramirez, Blanco Party President Puig, and former president Sanguinetti. In addition, he also met with Uruguay's foreign affairs minister, economy and finance minister, Montevideo City mayor, Chamber of Deputies speaker, and other government officials and political leaders. The two sides briefed each other on their domestic situation and exchanged views on international issues of common concern and on further developing bilateral relations. The visit was a complete success for the Chinese guests, who were accorded a warm and friendly reception. Uruguay was the first stop during the delegation's trip to three South American countries, which also includes Argentina and Brazil.
