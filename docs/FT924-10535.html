921030 FT 30 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Castle Mill losses up to Pounds 948,000 Castle Mill International, the textile and clothing designer, recorded a pre-tax loss of Pounds 948,000, against Pounds 913,000, for the six months to June 30. The outcome included a deficit of Pounds 787,000 (Pounds 529,000) from discontinued businesses; losses on continuing activities were reduced from Pounds 384,000 to Pounds 161,000. Overall turnover fell to Pounds 3.75m (Pounds 4.07m) with continuing activities contributing Pounds 2.65m (Pounds 2.32m). Losses per share were 7.13p (5.82p).
