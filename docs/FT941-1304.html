940325 FT 25 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Net assets rise at Thornton Asian Net asset value per share at Thornton Asian Emerging Markets Investment Trust improved from 87.1p to 167.5p over the year to December 31. Net earnings for the year was Pounds 886,000 (Pounds 322,000) for earnings per share of 0.88p (0.32p). A single final dividend of 0.5p (0.3p) is proposed.
