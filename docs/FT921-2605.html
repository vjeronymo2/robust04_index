920318 FT 18 MAR 92 / UK Company News: R&M American Capital assets up Net asset values per capital and income share of River & Mercantile American Capital & Income Trust stood at 37.27p and 65.64p respectively at end-February. The figures compared with 30.05p and 61.21p a year earlier. Available income for the 12 months rose to Pounds 605,000 (Pounds 583,000), equal to earnings per income share of 8.07p (7.77p). A proposed final dividend of 2.4p makes a same-again 7.8p total.
