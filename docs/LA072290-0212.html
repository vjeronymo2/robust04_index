July 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

FREEWAY REPAIRS;

ON THE ROAD;

CALTRANS ISSUES ADVISORY ON THIS WEEK'S REPAIR SITES SO MOTORISTS CAN STEER CLEAR

Caltrans advises motorists that it plans the following freeway work in the Southeast and Long Beach area this week. Repair locations listed below are keyed to the nearest city or cities and are spotted on the map by number.

ARTESIA FREEWAY (91)

1. Bellflower /Cerritos: Eastbound No. 1 diamond lane; Woodruff Avenue to San Gabriel River Freeway (605); Tuesday, 8:30 a.m. to 1 p.m.; landscaping.

2. Bellflower /Cerritos: Eastbound No. 6 lane; Woodruff Avenue to San Gabriel River Freeway (605); Wednesday, 8:30 a.m. to 1 p.m.; shoulder repair.

HARBOR FREEWAY (110)

3. San Pedro: Northbound No. 1 lane; Gaffey Street to Channel Street; Monday, 8 a.m. to 2 p.m.; raised markers.

4. San Pedro: Southbound alternating lanes; Channel Street to Gaffey Street; Tuesday, 9:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.; raised markers.

5. San Pedro: Westbound No. 3 lane, Harbor Boulevard to Terminal Island separation, Harbor Boulevard on-ramp and westbound Terminal Island connector to northbound Harbor Freeway (110); Wednesday, 9:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.; raised markers.

LONG BEACH FREEWAY (710)

6. South Gate: Southbound No. 4 lane; Firestone Boulevard to Los Angeles River; Friday, 9:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.; guardrail repair.

SAN GABRIEL RIVER FREEWAY (605)

7. Pico Rivera / Whittier: Southbound No. 3 and 4 lanes; Peck Road to Whittier Boulevard; today, 6 a.m. to noon; raised markers.

8. Pico Rivera /Whittier: Northbound No. 1 lane; Washington Boulevard to Beverly Boulevard; Monday, 9:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.; raised markers.

9. Pico Rivera / Whittier: Northbound No. 1 lane; Beverly Boulevard to Peck Road; Tuesday, 9:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.; raised markers.

10. Santa Fe Springs: Northbound No. 3 lane; at Florence Avenue and Florence Avenue on-ramp; today, 7 to 11 a.m.; bridge repair.

SANTA ANA FREEWAY (5)

11. Downey: Southbound shoulder at the northbound Lakewood Boulevard on-ramp; Wednesday, 8:30 a.m. to 1 p.m.; landscaping.

12. La Mirada: Northbound No. 4 lane; Artesia Boulevard to Rosecrans Avenue; Thursday, 8:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m.; landscaping.

13. Norwalk: Northbound ramps; westbound Norwalk Boulevard and Imperial Avenue off-ramp; Monday, 8 a.m. to 2 p.m.; landscaping.

14. Santa Fe Springs: Northbound and southbound No. 1 lanes; Alondra Boulevard to Valley View Avenue; today, 5:30 a.m. to 1 p.m.; guardrail repair.

TERMINAL ISLAND FREEWAY (103)

15. Long Beach: Northbound all lanes; Anaheim Street to Pacific Coast Highway, Anaheim Street on-ramp, Pacific Coast Highway off-ramp and on-ramp; detour northbound Terminal Island Freeway (103) traffic from Anaheim Street to Pacific Coast Highway; Monday, 8 a.m. to 2 p.m.; bridge repair.

16. Long Beach: Northbound No. 1 lane; Seaside Avenue to Anaheim Street; Thursday, 9:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.; raised markers.
