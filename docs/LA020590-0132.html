February 5, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

UCLA SHUTS OUT HAWAII 7-0

John Sutherland, Scott Schanz and Dave Zancanaro combined to pitch a seven-hitter as UCLA blanked Hawaii 7-0 in the finale of a three-game, non-conference baseball series Sunday night.

Mannie Adams had three of the Bruins' 13 hits, scored three runs and hit a solo home run in the fourth inning to give UCLA a 2-0 lead.

David Tokhelm and David Ravitz each cracked two doubles as the Bruins pounded out seven extra base hits.

The Bruins also turned three double plays to blunt any Hawaii rallies.
