920912 FT 12 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Swallowfield almost halved to Pounds 421,000 Swallowfield, the manufacturer of perfumes and cosmetics, reported pre-tax profits almost halved, from Pounds 804,000 to Pounds 421,000, in the 24 weeks to June 13. The outcome was achieved on turnover down by some Pounds 900,000 to Pounds 8.6m. Earnings emerged at 2.2p (4.6p) per share and the interim dividend is being maintained at 2.2p.
