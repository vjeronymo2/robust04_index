January 26, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: CULVER CITY;

INTERIM CITY HALL SITE

The City Council reached a preliminary decision to move City Hall offices to a site at Culver Boulevard and Overland Avenue while demolition and construction proceeds at the current City Hall site.

A final decision on the interim site will not be made until next month's council meeting, after owners of property next to the Culver-Overland and other possible downtown sites have been notified of the move.

The city plans to raze its 71-year-old City Hall and a build a new 3,000-square-foot L-shape building on the grounds of the present building at Duquesne Street and Culver Boulevard.
