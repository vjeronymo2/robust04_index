Language: English Article Type:BFN ["NODONG SINMUN Lambastes U.S. Plan To Set Up 'Radio Free Asia'" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, January 31 (KCNA) -- The U.S. scheme to set up "Radio Free Asia" in East Asian region is apparently aimed at infringing upon the sovereignty of the Asian socialist countries including the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and other nations, interfering in their internal affairs, bringing the wave of "liberalization" to those countries and gratifying its dominationist ambition, says NODONG SINMUN today. The analyst says: "Radio Free Asia" is a variant of the notorious "Radio Free Europe." Through "Radio Free Asia," the United States intends to foster illusion about the West among the Asian people, contaminate their spirit and morality, plunge the society into confusion and disorder and thus stifle the socialist nations in this region as it did in Europe. The U.S. ruling quarters want to have such an effect of "Radio Free Europe" in the Asian socialist countries including the DPRK and some other countries. Their attempt, however, stands little chance of success. The decision of U.S. congress to fix a wave for the DPRK is an intolerable challenge to the high dignity and sovereignty of the DPRK. The United States, which has rendered the situation of the Korean peninsula strained with a hue and cry over "suspected nuclear development by North Korea," is now attempting to stifle the DPRK under the slogans "freedom," "democracy" and "human rights." This is nothing but a desperate bid of those scared by the might of Korean- style socialism. It goes without saying that any false propaganda will have no effect in face of the iron will and faith of the Korean people who are determined to win the righteous fight against the enemy with the might of singlehearted unity, fully armed with the chuche idea, and nobody will lend an ear to the bourgeois propaganda.
