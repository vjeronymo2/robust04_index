BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 3 (KCNA) -- A meeting celebrating May Day took place at Tongguk University in Seoul at noon on the first of May with about 20,000 workers in attendance, a Seoul-based radio reported. The ralliers at the meeting sponsored by the National Council of Representatives of Trade Unions opposed the plan of wage negotiation put forward by the South Korean General Federation of Trade Unions, a subsidized organization. They strongly demanded the recognition of the independent trade union and repeal of labour-related evil laws.
