930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Grand Central advances 71% Grand Central Investment Holdings, the food group with interests in Asia Pacific, raised pre-tax profits by 71 per cent from Pounds 922,000 to Pounds 1.57m in 1992, although profitability continued to be affected by losses from its commodity based businesses. Turnover rose 32 per cent to Pounds 66.6m (Pounds 50.6m). Earnings per share came to 0.04p (0.73p losses). The dividend is increased to 1.25p (1.15p) with a recommended final of 0.8p. The reorganisation involving the injection of Upali (Malaysia) and Network Foods (Malaysia) into Sunshine Allied Investments, the listed Singapore subsidiary, was completed during the year.
