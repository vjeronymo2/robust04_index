920302 FT 02 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Benson Olympic adviser Kleinwort Benson, the international merchant bank, has been appointed financial adviser to Manchester city council and the Manchester Olympic Bid Committee in their efforts to bring the Olympic Games to the UK in the year 2000. The bank has been advising Manchester council on the financial and risk management aspects of hosting the Games, on the financing of the associated Pounds 1bn construction programme and on opportunities for involving the private sector.
