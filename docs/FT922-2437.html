920617 FT 17 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Bradford Property steps up dividend Bradford Property Trust is raising its dividend from 3.7p to 4.45p for the year ended April 5 1992, as earnings improved from 10.6p to 10.76p. Pre-tax profit was static at Pounds 23.1m (Pounds 23m). Surplus from property rentals came to Pounds 9.79m (Pounds 8.32m) and profit from sales of dealing properties fell to Pounds 12.2m (Pounds 13.6m). The final dividend is 2.45p
