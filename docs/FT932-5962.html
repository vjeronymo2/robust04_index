930601 FT 01 JUN 93 / Construction Contracts: Southend Hospital scheme A Pounds 10.8m contract for an extension to Southend Hospital has been won by a joint venture partnership of LAING EASTERN and CROWN HOUSE ENGINEERING. The project includes the construction of a 325-bed three-storey nucleus ward block with links to the existing hospital. When complete in May 1995 the building will house ante-natal, obstetrics and gynaecology units plus facilities for children and the elderly.
