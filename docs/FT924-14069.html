921012 FT 12 OCT 92 / International Company News: Telecom NZ names new chief TELECOM Corporation of New Zealand, which is controlled by Bell Atlantic and Ameritech, has announced the appointment of Mr Roderick Deane as chief executive and managing director. The appointment has caused widespread surprise. Mr Deane, who was formerly chief economist and deputy governor of the Reserve Bank of New Zealand, has headed Electricorp Corporation, the state enterprise, since 1987.
