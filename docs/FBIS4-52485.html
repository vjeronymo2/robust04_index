BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 11 KYODO -- Major Japanese commercial banks Mitsubishi Bank and Sanwa Bank will open representative offices in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, on Thursday [12 May], bank officials said Wednesday. The offices, each staffed by three officials, will collect information on the Vietnamese economy as the country shifts to a market economy, the officials said. The opening of the offices will bring to seven the number of Japanese banks which have representative offices in Vietnam.
