930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Demand was fair at easier rates, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Landed: a few bright and coloury east Africans remained firm but the majority, which were light liquoring, lost 5-10p with some withdrawals. Offshore: fair competition with prices 2-3p lower. The highest price realised this week was 186p for a Rwanda pd. Quotations: quality 150p, good medium 130p, medium 122p, low medium 96p.
