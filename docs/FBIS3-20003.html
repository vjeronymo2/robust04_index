Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bitlis, Mar25 (A.A.) -- Eleven separatist terrorists have been killed in two-day clashes with security forces near Hizan township in the southeastern province of Bitlis, officials said on Friday [25 March]. They told the Anatolian Agency that Thursday's death toll of four rose to 11 on Friday in continuing clashes. Two privates were also wounded. Helicopters are taking part in the security forces' operation. Over 11,000 people, including terrorists, members of security forces and civilians, have been killed in southeastern Turkey since 1984 when the separatists launched their bloody campaign for an independent state in the region.
