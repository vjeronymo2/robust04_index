BFN ["Official statement"] [Text] On 24 May, according to the schedule of the Civilian Defense of Minsk Oblast, a planned quarterly exercise was held to observe laboratory control networks, as well as to practice a complex of radiation protection measures in Myadel Rayon of Minsk Oblast. According to (?information) received from the State Hydrometeorological Committee [Halowhydromet], the State Committee for Nuclear Control [Dzyarzhatamnahlyad], and the management of the Ignalina Nuclear Power Plant, at 1200 [0900 GMT] on 25 May, the radiation level around the Ignalina Nuclear Power Plant was within the normal limits. At the station itself, beginning in April, one energy unit has been under a scheduled repair. Operation of the second energy unit was halted for economic reasons, and it does not produce electric energy.
