930401 FT 01 APR 93 / International Company News: Apple ranks third in Japan PC market APPLE Computer, the US company, has overtaken Toshiba as the third-largest supplier in the Japanese personal computer market, according to Dataquest, the market research group, writes Michiyo Nakamoto in Tokyo. Apple has concluded sales agreements with distributors including Minolta Camera and Mitsubishi Corp, the trading house. It has worked closely with third-party software developers to localise software applications. In 1992, it increased its share of the Japanese market by 2.9 points to 8.3 per cent, against a share of 7.6 per cent for Toshiba, Dataquest said. This puts it in third place, after NEC, which took a 53.4 per cent chunk of the market, and Fujitsu with 9.8 per cent. Apple was significantly ahead of IBM Japan.
