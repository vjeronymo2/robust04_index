Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Minister Ya'ir Tzaban has said that in tomorrow's cabinet session he will again demand that all of the Kahanist organizations be outlawed and that the concentrations of settlers be moved out of Hebron. Minister Amnon Rubinstein also said that tomorrow he will support the suggestions to outlaw Kakh members and supporters, to distance them from Qiryat Arba', disarm them, and not draft them to the IDF [Israel Defense Forces]. He told our correspondent Hayim Hecht that the upsetting remarks Kakh members made on Qol Yisra'el's weekly newsreel prove that the extremist settlers' moral standards have drastically deteriorated.
