September 29, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

IF HE WERE A PROGRAM, THEY'D CHANGE CHANNELS

Regarding the review of the Denver-Kansas City game on Monday, Sept. 17, is Larry Stewart on the NFL payroll or is he blind? A couple of thrills at the end do not make up for a poorly coached, poorly played, poorly announced and now poorly reviewed game.

HERBERT MILLER, Hermosa Beach
