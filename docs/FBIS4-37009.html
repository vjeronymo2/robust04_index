BFN [Excerpt] The Council of Ministers held its periodic meeting this morning under the chairmanship of Acting Prime Minister Dr. Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar. The council discussed several issues listed on its en TMF1 including the supply issue. After reviewing ideas on how to deal with supply problems in a radical and successful way, the council decided to task the Economic Council with working out ways to deal with the issue in an accurate manner after assessing all [word indistinct]. The Council of Ministers has recently noticed that a number of merchants are tampering with prices and exploiting the current situation in the country to tamper with citizens' food. Therefore, the government appeals to every one to shoulder his responsibility under the country's current circumstances imposed by the [word indistinct] homeland. The government, at same time, warns against raising or tampering with prices, and considers such behavior an economic crime, the most serious of which is involvement in hoarding commodities. The council sent cables to the Ministry of Supply and Trade, the Ministry of Interior, the public prosecutor, the security forces, and all competent authorities to take deterrent measures against tamperers. The council also expects the brother citizens to cooperate with these institutions and to inform the competent authorities of anyone tampering with prices. [passage omitted]
