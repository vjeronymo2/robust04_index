November 16, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

STATE;

NO 'LOTTO 6-49' JACKPOT WINNER

Nobody chose all six winning numbers for the $4.6-million jackpot in the California Lottery's twice-weekly "Lotto 6-49" game, officials said today.

The $4,630,021 jackpot from Wednesday night's game will be rolled over to Saturday's drawing, estimated to be worth $10 million, the officials said.

Seven players correctly picked five numbers plus the bonus number and will divide a pool of $1.3 million.
