October 13, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

ON THE RECORD

"I'm the best witness there is as to whether or not there was any wrongdoing, any impropriety whatsoever. I'll tell you this. There wasn't. There hasn't been."

-- Mayor Tom Bradley, commenting in an interview with The Times, on a federal grand jury investigation of his personal finances and business ties. He predicted he will not be charged with any criminal misconduct.
