921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Multitone little changed at Pounds 875,000 Multitone Electronics yesterday reported little changed pre-tax profits of Pounds 875,000 against Pounds 864,000 in the first half to October 31, in what it described as further worsening trading conditions. Sales of the paging systems and equipment maker, rose 6 per cent to Pounds 12.1m (Pounds 11.4m) and development expenditure was increased to Pounds 1.11m (Pounds 946,000). A higher interim dividend of 1.5p (1.25p) is payable from earnings per share of 4.03p (3.73p).
