941228 FT 28 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Pay rise for Frederick Cooper chief Mr Ed Kirk, chairman of Frederick Cooper, the architectural hardware, metal finishing and electrical products group, received a 60 per cent rise in emoluments for the year to July 31. Although his basic salary edged up to Pounds 126,000 (Pounds 122,000), and benefits rose to Pounds 7,000 (Pounds 5,000), this time he gained Pounds 52,000 in performance-related pay. The company also lifted its contribution to Mr Kirk's pension to Pounds 50,000 (Pounds 20,000). In the period, pre-tax profits and fully diluted earnings per share advanced 53 and 59 per cent respectively, to Pounds 6.31m and 9.2p. Basic earnings more than doubled to 8.2p (3.4p). Pre-tax profits from 'underlying operations' grew a more modest 30 per cent to Pounds 5.45m, while earnings on a fully diluted basis expanded 31 per cent to 7.7p (5.9p) and basic 71 per cent to 6p (3.5p).
