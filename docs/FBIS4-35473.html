BFN [Excerpt] Baghdad, 30 May (INA) -- The IRAQI NEWS AGENCY has learned that Iraq will organize a conference for Iraqi expatriates in Baghdad in December. This conference will discuss issues of concern to the expatriates in various fields. The Expatriates' Affairs Department was created at the headquarters of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on 28 May 1994, following the Council of Ministers' approval. [passage omitted]
