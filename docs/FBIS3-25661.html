Language: English Article Type:BFN [By Guy Rogers] [Text] Johannesburg Feb 23 SAPA -- There is no chance of an election pact between the African National Congress [ANC] and the Pan Africanist Congress [PAC], ANC Secretary General Cyril Ramaphosa said on Wednesday [23 February]. Answering a question at an election rally in the coloured township of Ennerdale, south of Johannesburg, Mr Rampahosa said the PAC had repeated taken positions in opposition to the ANC. The PAC, moreover, "won't even win one region in this country". "There is no chance of an election pact between the ANC and the PAC."
