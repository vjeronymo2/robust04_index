Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Radio Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Summary] Slavyanka is to run a regular feature on the fourth Wednesday of each month entitled "The Russian Arsenal" devoted to Russian advanced weaponry. The Su-34 front-line bomber is to replace the Su-24 and Su-24M. It has a range of 4,000 km. Its on-board computer and other radio-electronic equipment with multiple back-ups can bring the aircraft to its destination in any weather conditions with a precision of one meter. Its homing missiles and bombs can be launched from a range of up to 250 km. The two-man crew sit alongside each other in a comfortable air-conditioned cockpit. It has an artificial intelligence, an active safety system which allows acrobatic maneuvers at a speed of 1,400 km/hr and at very low altitudes. It can overcome air defense systems. The cockpit has 17-mm armor to defend the airmen from stray bullets. Many of the aircraft's other features are still classified.
