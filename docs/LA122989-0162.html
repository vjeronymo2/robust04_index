December 29, 1989, Friday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

UECKER BACK TO PITCH WISCONSIN

Sportsman and actor Bob Uecker will be the head pitchman in Wisconsin's annual effort to attract spring and summer tourism.

The former Milwaukee Braves baseball player -- better known for his appearances in commercials -- agreed to donate his services to the 1990 campaign, as he has in the past, officials announced Thursday.

One of the advertising agencies involved in the project suggested dumping Uecker this year, but the state Tourism Council, mindful of the success of previous campaigns, decided to use him again.

"Record responses for Wisconsin tourism information came as the result of past Uecker spots," said Richard Matty, administrator of the state Division of Tourism Development.

Uecker broadcasts Milwaukee Brewers games.
