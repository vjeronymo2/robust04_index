BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 28 (KCNA) -- "10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation for the reunification of the country", a classical work of the great leader President Kim Il-song, was carried by the Iranian papers RESALAT and TEHERAN TIMES, the Tunisian paper LES ANNONCES and the Nigerian paper OBSERVER. Radio Cadena Havana of Cuba and Radio Victoria of Peru reported the same work. The Malaysian paper SIN CHEW JIT POH printed a portrait of President Kim Il-song and carried the full text of his work "Let the North and the South open the way to peace and reunification of the country in a united effort". The Russian paper ROSSISKAYA PRAVDA and the Ghanaian paper FOCUS carried detailed gists of "For the ultimate solution of the rural question under the banner of the socialist rural theses", a letter sent by President Kim Il-song to a national agricultural conference, and the Nepalese paper MAJADUR KISSAN carried the full text of the same work. The Mongolian paper ULAN BATOR gave a gist of his work "Let us implement the public health law to the letter".
