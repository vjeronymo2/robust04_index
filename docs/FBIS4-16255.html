BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow 14 April, TASS -- The agreement on merging of the Russian and Byelorussian [Belarusian] monetary systems, which provides for solving of certain military problems as well, is above all "to the advantage of the Russian servicemen temporarily deployed on Byelorussian territory," according to the Republic's Defence Minister Pavel Kazlowski. After the meeting of the Council of CIS Defence Ministers, Kazlowski noted in an interview with TASS that all nuclear weapons stationed on Byelorussian soil belong to Russia, which is withdrawing them strictly according to the schedule. The minister believes the entire nuclear arsenal will be removed to Russia by 1999, as scheduled, or even earlier.
