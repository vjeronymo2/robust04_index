920620 FT 20 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Henderson Strata asset value rises Net asset value per ordinary share of Henderson Strata Investments amounted to 185.6p at end-April assuming full subscription from outstanding warrants. That compared with 185.4p six months ago and with 166.6p at April 30 1991. Net asset value of the warrants rose from 66.6p to 85.6p over the year to April 30. Attributable revenue for the six months to end-April totalled Pounds 56,532 (Pounds 67,995), equal to earnings of 0.37p (0.45p).
