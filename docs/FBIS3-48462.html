Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Destiny of Motherland and Nation, Destiny of All Koreans at Home and Abroad" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, January 24 (KCNA) -- NODONG SINMUN today in a signed article quotes the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song as saying in his New Year address: "The destiny of our motherland is immediately the destiny of our nation, as well as the destiny of our compatriots at home and abroad." This teaching serves as a highly important guideline elucidating the ground and necessity for the entire compatriots in the North and the South and overseas to launch a nation-wide struggle for national reunification, rallied under the banner of the great unity of the whole nation, the article notes, and goes on: The destiny of our motherland is immediately the destiny of our nation, as well as the destiny of the entire nation, because the independence of the country and the nation is a prerequisite to realizing the independence of the popular masses and the struggle for the independence of the country and the nation is immediately the struggle for independence of the popular masses. As the life of everybody, a member of the nation, belongs to the life of the country and the nation, the independence of the country and the nation makes it possible to realize the independence of individuals. The popular masses can successfuly carve out their destiny in conformity with their desire and demand only when the independence of the country and the nation is realized. Independence of South Korea and the independent reunification of the country, this is the way for the complete realization of national independence in our country. The destiny of our nation is closely linked with national reunification. The reunification of our country is not a matter of ideology and system but a matter of national character as the matter of putting an end to the domination and interference of outside forces, completely realizing the sovereignty of the Korean nation and achieving national unity. The entire compatriots at home and abroad should unite with one will to defend the independence of the country and the nation with their lives, linking their destiny with the destiny of the motherland and the nation. Our fellow countrymen, who have lived for over 5,000 years as a homogeneous nation with tan gun as its father, are able to build a united state of the whole nation by means of confederation that can promote coexistence and co-prosperity, leaving the present two systems and two governments in the North and the South intact if they pool their wisdom and strength, giving precedence to national ideal. The entire compatriots in the North and the South and abroad should unite as one, transcending the differences of idea and ideal, political views and religious belief under the banner of the 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation and vigorously struggle to found a united state of the whole nation by means of confederation.
