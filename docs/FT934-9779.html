931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Clean break for Owners Abroad OWNERS ABROAD, the holiday company that narrowly escaped being taken over early this year by rival Airtours, has made a clean break from the advisers and brokers who led the defence. Mr Michael Julien, Owners new chairman since August, has replaced Samuel Montagu with Lazard Brothers as advisers and UBS Phillips & Drew with SG Warburg as brokers.
