921002 FT 02 OCT 92 / Survey of Software at Work - Autumn 1992 (45): Quanvert v5d -Product Profile Product: Quanvert v5d was released at the beginning of 1992. Quanvert is a package designed to enable non-technical market research or marketing executives to query their data and produce their own reports interactively in seconds. Supplier: Quantime was founded in the UK in 1978; it is privately owned by its executive directors. Turnover, worldwide: Pounds 4.45m in 1992. The company has offices in London, New York, Cincinnati, plus agents in Germany, Italy, Japan, Greece, Sweden and Australia. Employees: 60 in UK, 85 worldwide. Number of users: 1200 or more, worldwide at 300 different sites; over 350 in the UK. Hardware and operating systems: IBM compatible PCs (286 or above), HP9000s, RS6000s, VAX, IBM mainframe. User views: 'Excellent support . . . product easy to use, constantly updated.' Other users mentioned the 'limitation of 400 axes,' (currently being adjusted to support 750; minor irritations with user-interface, again being answered by new Windows version.
