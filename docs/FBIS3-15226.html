Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau, BASAPRESS 1/3/1994 -- President Mircea Snegur met today with a NATO experts' group, arrived in Moldova to negotiate over the program "Partnership for Peace," [PFP] states the Presidential Press Service. Von Moltke, ambassador, deputy secretary general of NATO in charge of political problems, emphasized the importance of the program in stopping ethnic conflicts. In the opinion of Snegur, European structures should elaborate a mechanism of not admitting conflicts that threaten Europe. "From this point of view, the program `Partnership for Peace' seems to propose real solutions," said the president. The president reaffirmed his intention to sign this month the "Partnership for Peace," states the press service.
