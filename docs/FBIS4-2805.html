BFN [Text] Jinan, April 12 (XINHUA) -- Weihai, a port city in east China's Shandong Province and China's nearest city to the Republic of Korea (ROK), will have regular trade fair or discussions with the ROK. This has been been approved by the Ministry of Foreign Trade and economic cooperation recently. So far this is the only officially ratified annual discussion of its kind in China. In July last year, Weihai succeeded in holding the '93 Sino-ROK economic and trade discussion. The first of subsequent discussions this year has been scheduled for in September. The Ministry of Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation and the local government have pinned high hopes on this year's discussion, as 2,000 foreign businessmen, including 1,000 from the ROK, will be invited to the fair. The others will be from Japan, Singapore, Taiwan, Hong Kong, Macao, Europe and the United States.
