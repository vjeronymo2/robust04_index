930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Ladbroke Ladbroke Group announced a basis price of 175.75p per share for new shares to be issued to shareholders who elected for the enhanced share alternative to the 1992 final dividend. At this price, a total of 53.23m new shares will be allotted under the enhanced alternative, with 12.42m of those shares being purchased by SG Warburg Securities and Smith New Court Securities under their cash offer - as subsequently increased by Swiss Bank Corporation. Dealings in the new shares are expected to commence on June 25.
