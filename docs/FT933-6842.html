930825 FT 25 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Instant Zip Up Instant Zip Up, a supplier of scaffolding and powered access platforms, has been acquired by its management from Upright of the US in a deal valued at Pounds 2.7m. Equity funding was provided by 3i, the investment capital group. Management was advised by Arthur Andersen and debt facilities provided by TSB Commercial Finance and National Westminster.
