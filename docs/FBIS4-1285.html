BFN [Text] Pretoria April 25 SAPA -- Two people were killed and 29 injured in a bomb attack, apparently by whites on blacks in Pretoria on Monday [25 April] night. Police spokesman Lt David Harrington said two youths made the attack on diners at Solly's Restaurant in 7th Avenue, Marabastad. They then sped off in a blue Toyota RSI, according to initial reports. The injured were taken to H F Verwoerd Hospital. It was not immediately clear what type of explosive was used, but the blast was heard for a considerable distance. A police investigation was immediately begun, Lt Harrington said.
