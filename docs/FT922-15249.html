920402 FT 02 APR 92 / UK Company News: Norcros in Pounds 39m property disposals Norcros, the building materials and print and packaging group, has disposed of a number of properties for Pounds 39m cash. The disposals include two group occupied properties - at Braintree, Essex, and Wantage, Oxfordshire - for Pounds 22.75m. Both have been leased back to group companies at initial rents amounting to Pounds 2.6m per annum. The remaining properties - three office buildings, two industrial estates and a retail park - will realise Pounds 16.2m. Total proceeds will reduce group borrowings to below Pounds 120m, representing gearing of some 80 per cent.
