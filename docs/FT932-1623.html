930623 FT 23 JUN 93 / Welsh investment projects backed SIX INDUSTRIAL projects in Wales involving more than Pounds 12m of investment and expected to create 240 jobs were announced yesterday. The projects are backed by a total of Pounds 3.7m of regional selective assistance from the Welsh Office. The biggest project is a Pounds 6.5m expansion of production facilities by Solvay Automotive at Treforest, Mid Glamorgan. About 130 jobs are expected to be created at the plant, which makes plastic parts for the motor industry.
