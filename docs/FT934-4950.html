931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / International Company News: Loss deepens at NZ group Tasman Properties, the financially-troubled property group, yesterday reported a loss of NZDollars 54.6m (USDollars 30.3m) in the six months to September 30, writes Terry Hall in Wellington. The result follows further big write-downs in the value of its substantial property portfolio in Australia and New Zealand. A year ago the loss was NZDollars 12m.
