BFN [Editorial Report] Johannesburg WEEKEND STAR in English on 23-24 April on page 8 carries a full-page election advertisement from the Inkatha Freedom Party [IFP]. The upper half of the page shows a picture of State President F. W. de Klerk flanked by a picture of the old South African flag coming down a flagpole and on the other side of De Klerk the communist flag going up a flagpole, under the words "HE'S MADE THE CHANGE" in large white letters. Under De Klerk's picture, also in large white letters: "THE IFP IS YOUR ONLY GUARANTEE" next to a large IFP logo. At the bottom of the page on the right in a box: "If you want to help us build a better future, you can do so by supporting us financially. Our account details are: "First National Bank, Greyville Branch number 22 27 26 Account Number: 6000040444 Account Name: IFP Election Fund" In the left corner is a samll IFP logo, IFP leader's Buthelezi picture, then a large X. Above, in large white letters "VOTE IFP" and below the picture: "A Power for Good."
