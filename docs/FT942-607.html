940628 FT 28 JUN 94 / International Company News: Cargill expects company veteran to succeed Hutter Cargill, the privately-held international commodities marketing and financial services group headquartered in Minnesota, expects its board of directors to name Mr Ernest Micek, a career Cargill veteran, as president and chief operating officer when Mr Heinz Hutter retires from the post in August. Mr Whitney MacMillan, a member of Cargill's founding families, will remain chief executive officer. Employee shareholders and Cargill's board will vote on the nomination on August 9. Mr David Raisbeck, president of Cargill's trading sector, is also expected to be named to the board. Cargill had earnings of Dollars 348m last year on sales of Dollars 47bn, conducting business in more than 58 countries.
