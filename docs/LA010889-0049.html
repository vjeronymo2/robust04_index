January 8, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TRAVEL NEWS AND BRIEFS

A slide travelogue on "Canada and Alaska -- Northern Neighbors" will be presented by Ross Harrington from 7 to 9 p.m. Tuesday and Jan. 17 in Room 311 of Redondo Union High School, Redondo Beach. Fee is $6 for the two nights. For more information and registration, call Harrington at (213) 316-8683.

-- -- --

An open house and lecture about an upcoming Western Caribbean cruise will be held by North Orange County Community College at 7:30 p.m. Thursday in the Board Room, District Education Center, 1000 N. Lemon St., Fullerton. For more information, call (714) 871-4030, Ext. 15.

-- -- --

Israel will be the subject of a lecture by travel photographer Mort Loveman from 1 to 2:30 p.m. Thursday at Roxbury Memorial Park, 471 S. Roxbury Drive, Beverly Hills. Admission free. For more information, call (213) 550-4761.

-- -- --

A slide show on Africa will highlight a clinic sponsored by REI, an outdoor gear and clothing store, at 6:30 p.m. Thursday at 3029 University Ave., San Diego. Admission free. For more information, call (619) 295-7700.

-- -- --

The Central Coast Travel Exposition will be held from 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. Saturday at Veteran's Memorial Building, San Luis Obispo. Admission $2. For more information, call (805) 543-9400.

-- -- --

Fly-fishing opportunities in Alaska will be presented in video and slides by Northern Wilderness Adventures from 2:30 to 5 p.m. next Sunday at 14423 Burbank Blvd. Admission free. For more information, call (818) 785-7306.

-- -- --

"How to Travel Thailand and Malaysia" will be the subject of a lecture and slide show sponsored by Maps to Anywhere Travel Bookstore next Sunday at 1:30 p.m. at the store, 1514 N. Hillhurst Ave., Hollywood. Admission $5. For more information and reservations, call (213) 660-2101.

-- -- --

The Bon Voyage Travel Club will present a slide show and lecture on adventures in Australia and New Zealand at 7:45 p.m. Jan. 20 in the Community Room, 9696 Wilshire Blvd., Beverly Hills. Admission $5. For more information, call (213) 476-4869. NORMAN DASH
