BFN [Text] Jordan rejected Israeli accusations that the country was assisting the Islamic Hamas Movement, and said such accusations were rash and baseless. A senior Jordanian official said Israel's accusations are not helping the peace process. He said Hamas attacks were planned inside the occupied Arab land. Hamas claimed responsibility for two suicide attacks which killed 14 Israelis inside Israel over the past 10 days. Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin called a news conference late last night and warned against what he called continued activities of Hamas in Jordan. Rabin and his Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres said Israel will not tolerate the activities of Hamas.
