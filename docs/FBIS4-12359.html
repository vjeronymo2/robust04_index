BFN [Text] Cairo, 6 Apr (MENA)--Dr. Yusuf Ghali, minister of state in the cabinet for international cooperation affairs, left Cairo today for Washington to join the Egyptian economic delegation led by Dr. Kamal al-Janzuri, deputy prime minister and planning minister. Dr. Ghali will participate in the Egyptian delegation meetings with senior officials of the World Bank and the IMF to prepare the final report that will be submitted to the World Bank management in mid April.
