BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 20 -- Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam today held talks with Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu of the Republic of Korea (ROK) who is here on an official visit to Vietnam. The two sides exchanged views on international and regional issues of common concern, and measures to further strengthen the relations between the two countries, they also discussed how to further promote the Vietnamese-ROK bilateral cooperation in trade, investment, technology and science, culture, education and labour and other fields, including the implementation of the agreements the two countries had reached on the ROK's official development assistance (ODA) to Vietnam. Foreign Ministers Cam and Han affirmed the two countries' desire to contribute to the consolidation of peace, stability and cooperation in Asia-the Pacific and in the world as a whole. Also present at the talks were Deputy Foreign Minister Nguyen Dinh Bin and ROK Ambassador Park No Su.
