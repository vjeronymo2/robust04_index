Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] A police company assigned to the Nyanga Battalion stationed in Magude, Maputo Province, on 19 January took part in violent action, using AKM weapons and mortars. The media office of the Maputo Provincial Police Command told NOTICIAS that the troops were demanding payment of annual bonus for 1993. There are no reports of vandalism or deaths, but the use of weapons resulted in Magude residents abandoning the area.
