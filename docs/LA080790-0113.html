August 7, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: CAR PLUNGES OFF FREEWAY ONTO STREET, KILLING DRIVER

A car plunged off the Harbor Freeway onto a city street Monday, killing the driver, authorities said.

The vehicle went off the northbound Harbor Freeway onto Grand Avenue near 69th Street in South Los Angeles about 5 p.m. California Highway Patrol spokesman Vince Zambrano said.

A woman was killed in the accident, Zambrano said. Additional details were not immediately available. The outside lane of the freeway was closed for less than an hour after the accident.
