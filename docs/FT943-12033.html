940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / UK Company News: M&G Dual M&G Dual Trust, a split-level investment trust, announced an increase in asset value of capital shares from Pounds 2,668 to Pounds 2,814 at the end of the period to June 30 1994. Earnings per income share were unchanged at 30.55p which translates into an interim dividend of the same amount. Net revenue from ordinary activities was Pounds 1.76m (same).
