920318 FT 18 MAR 92 / Election '92: FT election share index AS THE stock market as a whole moved up for the first time since the election announcement the shares in the 'Labour gainers' section rose faster than the 'Conservative gainers' yesterday. This continues the consistent out-performance by the Labour shares, which are nearly back at their level on the announcement day. Shares chosen are based on analysts' predictions of sectors that will gain or suffer from a change of government.
