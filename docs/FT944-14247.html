941024 FT 24 OCT 94 / Company News This Week: Imperial Chemical Industries - Other Companies Imperial Chemical Industries: Followers of the chemicals sector will be examining ICI's third-quarter results on Thursday for evidence that commodity chemicals prices are recovering. The company should show a pre-tax profit above Pounds 125m, compared with last year's Pounds 73m. The star performer should be the industrial chemicals division benefiting from a rise in ethylene prices and recovery in continental Europe. There may be further details of EVC, joint-owned with Italy's Enichem, scheduled for flotation next month.
