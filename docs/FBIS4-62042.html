BFN [By BUSINESS-TASS] [Text] Moscow May 11 TASS -- The State Duma has adopted in the first reading a draft law on federal budget. Under the law, federal budgetary expenditures were endorsed at 193.3 trillion roubles and revenues - at 124.4 trillion roubles. The top limit of the budget deficit was established to reach the amount equivalent to 35.6 per cent of budgetary expenditures for the current year.
