Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by N. Patterton] [Text] Pretoria March 11 SAPA--Bophuthatswana areas north-west of Pretoria such as Winterveld, Mabopane and Garankuwa were torn by violence, looting and shooting on Thursday [10 March] night and Friday morning, according to witnesses. Reports said 20 people had been admitted to Garankuwa hospital, five with gunshot wounds. The SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] reported that one victim died and that there was rioting at Hlabane near Rustenburg. Residents in Winterveld in central Bophuthatswana reported shooting through the night, and that shopping complexes had been looted and torched. Bophuthatswana police shot at people in the streets, they said. Witnesses in Winterveld claimed that homeland police participated in the looting. No comment on any of these events could be obtained from the Bophuthatswanan police headquarters in Mmabatho. Earlier, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] news reported that a section of a multi-million rand shopping complex in Mabopane in Bophuthatswana was set alight on Friday morning. This brought to three the number of buildings torched since Thursday night, the first two being government administration buildings.
