CSO [Editorial Report] Guayaquil EL UNIVERSO 0F 30 May announces that the results of a series of opinion polls conducted by Informe Confidencial will be published on page 1 of the newspaper, four times a week: Monday, Tuesday, Saturday, and Sunday.
