920205 FT 05 FEB 92 / World Trade News: Belarus order for Italy FILTECO, an Italian textile machinery maker, has won a Dollars 125m (Pounds 69m) order to build a turnkey plant for nylon fibres and carpets in Belarus, part of the former Soviet Union, Haig Simonian reports from Milan. The contract will raise by 50 per cent the output of nylon fibres at P O Chimvolokno Grodno. It makes about 40,000 tonnes of nylon fibres a year for carpets, tyres and other products. The first tranche of the deal is for Dollars 26m, with payment by cash and goods. A downpayment has been made in hard currency, the balance to be covered by Belarus products for sale by Filteco.
