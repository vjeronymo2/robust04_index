BFN [From the "990 Morning News" program] [Text] Wu Bangguo, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and secretary of the Shanghai Municipal CPC Committee, returned to Shanghai from Beijing by plane yesterday [17 June] afternoon after leading a Shanghai municipal delegation on visit to Canada and a CPC delegation on visit to Cuba. He was greeted at the airport by Huang Ju, member of the CPC Central Committee, deputy secretary of the municipal party committee, and Shanghai mayor; Wang Liping, deputy secretary of the municipal party committee; vice mayor Sha Lin who had accompanied Wu Bangguo on visit to Canada and had returned home already; Yu Yongliang; (Feng Guoqing); and Cuba's acting consul general in Shanghai (Sherda). When Wu Bangguo arrived in Beijing, he was met at the airport by Li Shuzheng, alternate member of the CPC Central Committee and director of the International Liaison Department; and Cuban Ambassador to China Guerra.
