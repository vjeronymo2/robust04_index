Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, January 13 (ATA) -- In yesterday's press conference, the chairman of the Union of the Independent Trade Unions of Kosova, Dr. Hajrullah Gorani, informed reporters that Serbia has to compensate with 3 billion U.S. dollars the Albanian workers who were violently dismissed from their jobs. Reporters were also informed of a document from the heat-power stations of Kosova, which are under Serbian violent measures, according to which the finance sector is ordered to take 2 per cent of the income of each worker for the war.
