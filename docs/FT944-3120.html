941215 FT 15 DEC 94 / Economic Watch: French surplus at FFr7.7bn France's current account showed a seasonally adjusted surplus of FFr7.72bn (Pounds 912m) in September, the economy ministry said, against an unrevised deficit in August of FFr291m. The ministry said that the seasonally adjusted surplus in the first nine months of 1994 was FFr43.6bn, compared with a FFr39.1bn in the same period of 1993. The ministry said the seasonally adjusted current account surplus in the third quarter was FFr15.07bn compared to FFr5.91bn in the second quarter and FFr22.89bn in the third quarter of 1993. The Finnish unemployment rate was 19.2 per cent in November compared with 19.1 per cent in October and 20.3 per cent in November 1993.
