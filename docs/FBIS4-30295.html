BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 27 (KCNA) -- A literature containing "10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation for the reunification of the country" which was published by the great leader President Kim Il-song was distributed in the Cheju area, the southernmost part of South Korea, a Seoul-based radio report said. It was reportedly sent to HALLA ILBO and other local newspapers at midnight on June 24. Upset by this, the South Korean authorities mobilized prosecutors and police in an investigation.
