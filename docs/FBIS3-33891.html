Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Palestine Liberation Organization leader Yasir 'Arafat has warned that further delays in the withdrawal of Israeli troops from the occupied territories could threaten the whole Middle East peace process. Mr. 'Arafat told a news conference in Geneva that Israel had asked for six days to review a weekend agreement reached with the Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres in the Swiss resort of Davos. He was speaking shortly after giving a speech to the UN Human Rights Commission, in which he said Palestinians still faced abuses in the occupied territories. The PLO leader said he still hoped the agreement on implementing Palestinian self-rule could be signed next week when he meets Mr. Peres in the Egyptian capital, Cairo. [passage omitted]
