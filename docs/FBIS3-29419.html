Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 17 (KCNA) -- The Chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Social Democratic Party [KSDP], Kim Pyong-sik, met and had a friendly talk with the visiting delegation of the Social Action Party of Thailand led by its Deputy Leader Bunphan Kaeowatthana at the Mansudae Assembly Hall Wednesday. The head of the delegation told him that his delegation has come to Korea to celebrate the 52nd birthday of the dear leader His Excellency Kim Chong-il and hoped that the Korean people will make great achievements in accordance with their just idea.
