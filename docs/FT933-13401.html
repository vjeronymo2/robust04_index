930720 FT 20 JUL 93 / South African gunmen massacre six South African police said six people were killed by gunmen with AK-47 rifles who stopped a mini-bus east of Johannesburg yesterday and ordered the occupants to state their political affiliations, Reuter reports from Johannesburg. A seventh man was seriously wounded. All the victims were said to be members of the Zulu-based Inkatha Freedom Party.
