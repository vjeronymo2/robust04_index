930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / International Company News: Apple Computer in Chinese expansion APPLE Computer of the US yesterday announced plans to expand in China by opening a representative office, naming a Chinese distributor and opening a computer training centre, AP-DJ reports from Beijing. With its fast-expanding economy, China 'holds an enormous amount of potential for us,' said Mr Michael Spindler, Apple's president. Mr William James, Apple managing director for eastern Asia, estimated about 10,000 Apple Macintosh personal computers are already installed in China. He said sales should more than double annually in coming years, and he predicted the company would soon be selling more than 10,000 units a year in China.
