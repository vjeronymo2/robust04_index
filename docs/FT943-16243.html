940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / World Trade News: Court victory for P&O A consortium led by P&O of the UK has won the first round in a court battle to win control of two terminals in the port of Buenos Aires, writes John Barham in Buenos Aires. In June the government cancelled P&O's offer to manage the two terminals as a 25 year concession despite having made the highest bid. However, a Buenos Aires federal judge ruled that P&O's local ally Port Investments did meet bidding terms.
