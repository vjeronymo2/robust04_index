BFN [Text] Beijing, April 7 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Premier Li Peng will pay official visits to four central Asian countries and Mongolia from April 18 to 30. Li was invited by President Islam Karimov of the Republic of Uzbekistan, President Saparmurad Niyazov of Turkmenistan, President Askar Akayev [name as received] of the Republic of Kazakhstan, and Premier Puntsagiyn Jasray of the Government of the State of Mongolia. The visits were announced by Wu Jianmin, spokesman of the Chinese Foreign Ministry, at a press conference here today.
