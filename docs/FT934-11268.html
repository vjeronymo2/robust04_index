931102 FT 02 NOV 93 / Growth expected in coal exports BRITISH COAL is asking for a government subsidy after securing an order to export 50,000 tonnes to Stadtwerke Bremen in west Germany. It expects to export 1m tonnes this year against 600,000 tonnes last year. The corporation has also concluded an agreement with the National Health Service to supply about 200,000 tonnes to 76 hospitals.
