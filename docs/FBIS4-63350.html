BFN [Text] Vladimir Zhirinovskiy's nationalist Liberal Democratic Party of Russia (LDPR) has expressed indignation over Ukrainian troops' "attack on the building of the Interior Ministry of (Ukrainian autonomous republic of) Crimea in order to change it into a so-called Central Department for Crimea of the Interior Ministry of Ukraine." "Your government, Mr. Kravchuk, has already plundered Ukraine and run down what was a rich land," the LDPR says in a statement received by Interfax on Friday. "Now it is stealing Russian and Turkmen gas and oil transported through your territory." Kravchuk "wishes to sell the Black Sea Fleet (disputed by Russia and Ukraine) for scrap metal, to sell everything that can be sold, in order to flee the devastated country when its people rise and demand reunification with Russia," the party says.
