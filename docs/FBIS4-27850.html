BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 6 (KCNA) -- A military delegation of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea led by Choe Kwang, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, vice-chairman of the National Defense Commission of the DPRK and chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, left here today to visit China. It was seen off at the airport by Vice Marshal Kim Pong-yol, General Yi Pyong-ok and General Yi Pong-won of the KPA and Chinese Ambassador to Korea Qiao Zonghuai.
