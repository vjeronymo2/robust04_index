November 16, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

ON THE SIDELINES;

NEW ZEALAND YACHT TAKES LEAD

A New Zealand yacht skippered by Chris Dickson won all four of its races today and took the opening day lead in the Nippon Cup Premium International Yacht Match Race.

Two American yachts skippered by Gary Jobson and Peter Isler each won three races over the 2.2-mile course in Sagami Bay off Hayama Marina, southwest of Tokyo.
