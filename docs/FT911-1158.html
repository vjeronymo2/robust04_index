910508 FT 08 MAY 91 / International Company News: Qantas places Dollars 34m account D'ARCY Masius Benton & Bowles, the privately-owned US advertising agency, has won a Dollars 34m international advertising account for Qantas, the Australian airline. The Qantas account, the biggest single advertising account in Australia and previously held by the Chiat Day/Mojo agency, will be co-ordinated from DMB&B's Sydney agency, DMB&B/Weekes Morris Osborn. Qantas advertises in 25 countries, and the account will be handled by the local DMB&B agency. The agency will produce a worldwide comprehensive marketing strategy for Qantas DMB&B won the account after a competitive pitch against Chiat Day/Mojo and two other agencies - McCann-Erickson, part of the US-owned Interpublic group, and George Patterson, a subsidiary of Saatchi & Saatchi of the UK.
