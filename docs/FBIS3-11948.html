Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Politburo member Alfredo Hondal Gonzalez, first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba [PCC] in Ciego de Avila Province, called for efficiency in the sugar industry during a plenum of that organization in Ciego de Avila. Hondal added that there are real possibilities of being able to meet the assigned sugar plan; to accomplish this, the industry must grind efficiently, now that the harvest is in its most productive period. The plenum also reviewed the results obtained in 1993 regarding defense, production by local industries, recreation, and tourism. With regard to defense, the party plenum highlighted the need to increase political work, with the help of party cadres, and to boost the work of declaring zones defense-ready in the second stage.
