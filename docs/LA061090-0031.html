June 10, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

DICEY ICY TRUTH

Regarding the ton of ice in the June 3 News and Briefs, a 2,000-pound ice cube contains approximately 35 cubic feet.

Doing some rough computations, I find that a one-ton ice cube is about three feet and three inches on a side. A meter. That is the "Fact."

ALBERT P. PETRAITIS

Escondido
