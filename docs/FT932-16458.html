930406 FT 06 APR 93 / Probe for prisoner services contract MR DEREK LEWIS, the director-general of prisons, last night ordered an inquiry into hitches that marred the launch of the privatisation of prisoner-escort services. Group 4, the security company, took over the transportation of prisoners between prisons and courts yesterday in the east Midlands and Humberside. Other areas in England and Wales will be put out to tender next year.
