November 19, 1989, Sunday, South Bay Edition

CHADWICK WINS SMALL SCHOOL VOLLEYBALL TITLE

Chadwick and Coach Anita Drennen won the first-ever Small Schools Division volleyball title, defeating Mayfield, 15-8, 15-9, 15-3, Saturday at Marina High in Huntington Beach.

The Dolphins, 19-0, were led by Shannon Davenport's 18 kills and a strong defensive effort by Jessica Tong.
