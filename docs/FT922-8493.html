920513 FT 13 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Manakin repays 295p per share Manakin Holdings, the venture capital company formerly known as Abingworth, reported net asset value of 111p at April 15, against 383p six months earlier. During the period shareholders have had returns of capital totalling 295p. The company is in members voluntary liquidation but will maintain its listing to allow an orderly disposal of its unquoted investments. During the period its gilts holdings were sold for Pounds 51.6m against a cost of Pounds 50.5m. Holdings in quoted US companies raised Dollars 14.6m (Pounds 8.2m) having cost Dollars 3.7m but unquoted company shares raised Dollars 596,000 against a cost of Dollars 1.33m. The liquidator said further sales would be made and another capital repayment would be made before the end of 1992. Earnings per share for the six months to April 15 were 1.25p. The net profit of Pounds 256,594 has been transferred to revenue reserves.
