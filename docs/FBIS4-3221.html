BFN [Text] Shenzhen, April 18 (XINHUA) -- An export fair of machinery and electronic products opened here today, with 400 manufacturers from all over China attending. Overseas firms can order machine tools, general-purpose machines, food packing machines, motor vehicles and parts, printing machines, aviation equipment, farm machinery, meters and instruments and electronics products on display during the fair, the largest of its kind in China. Government statistics show that China's exports of machinery and electric products soared to 22.7 billion U.S. dollars in 1993 as against 1.68 billion dollars in 1986, rising at an annual rate of 21 percent. These products accounted for nearly 25 percent of China's exports in 1993, up 19 percentage points from 1985.
