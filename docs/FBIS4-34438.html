BFN [Text] Samuel Ruiz, bishop of San Cristobal de las Casas, has issued a communique endorsing the efforts that Manuel Camacho Solis carried out for five months and six days in favor of peace in Chiapas. President of International Consultants Julio A. Millan thinks the country's economy should not be affected by Camacho Solis' resignation. [Begin Millan recording] There are no losers. The people are not affected. In addition, all of the candidates still have freedom of movement. A citizen has resigned from a post, and this is similar to the resignation of any other official. The important thing now is to limit to the Chiapas area the causes and activities related to the problem. [end recording] Felipe Solis, Federal Electoral Institute executive director, says there is no reason for Camacho Solis' resignation to affect the 21 August elections.
