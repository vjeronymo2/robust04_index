920502 FT 02 MAY 92 / Markets (At a glance): British Gas Pep is launched British Gas has launched a Pounds 6,000 corporate Personal Equity Plan and a Pounds 3,000 single company Pep for those who wish to invest in its shares. There is no initial charge and the management charge is 0.25 per cent plus VAT; dealing commission is 0.25 per cent plus VAT. Current Gas shareholders can transfer their shares into the Peps for a flat charge of Pounds 10. Plan manager is National Westminster Bank, from whom application forms can be obtained at: NatWest Pep office, 55 Mansell Street, London E1 8AN.
