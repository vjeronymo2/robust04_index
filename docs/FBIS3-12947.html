Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn left Amman for the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia this morning to perform the minor pilgrimage on the blessed al-Qadr night [the night on which the koran was revealed] and visit the tomb of Prophet Muhammad, God's peace and blessings be upon him. The king was seen off by His Highness Crown Prince Hasan, Senate President Ahmad al-Lawzi, House of Representatives Speaker Tahir al-Masri, his majesty's Political Adviser Marwan al-Qasim, his majesty's adviser Dr. Khalid al-Karaki, the crown prince's private adviser, the deputy prime minister, the deputy prime minister and higher education minister, some ministers, the Saudi charge d'affaires in Amman, the chief of the Royal Household, the secretary general of the Royal Court, his majesty's adviser for tribal affairs, and other officials. His majesty is accompanied by His Highness Prince 'Abdallah Bin-al-Husayn, Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, Royal Court Chief al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, Chief Chamberlain Prince Ra'd Bin-Zayd, his majesty's military adviser Prince Talal Bin-Muhammad, His Highness Prince Ghazi Bin-Muhammad, his majesty's Private Secretary Prince 'Ali Bin-Nayif, a number of the nobility, his majesty's advisers, the chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff, and the Hashemite family imam.
