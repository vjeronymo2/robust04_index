Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The presidium of the Supreme Council of the Republic of Tajikistan decrees: It is permitted, as an exception in conditions of a state of emergency, for the Union of Collective Farmers of the Republic of Tajikistan to hold the fifth congress of the collective farmers of the Republic of Tajikistan on 25-26 February in Dushanbe. (Yoqubjon) Salimov, internal affairs minister of Tajikistan, and (Saidamir) Zuhurov, chairman of the National Security Committee of Tajikistan, are to take measures to ensure the security and safety of the participants in the fifth congress of the collective farmers of Tajikistan.
