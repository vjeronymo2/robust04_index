Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 24 Feb -- The press centre of the Russian State Duma has expressed "surprise and regret" with regard to the statement from the Russian president's press secretary, who condemned the lower chamber's decision to grant an amnesty to the participants in the attempted coup. The statement released by the press center to the media says that Vyacheslav Kostikov's statement "casts a shadow over the emerging constructive cooperation between the State Duma and the president, the government, and the Constitutional Court aimed at consolidating all power structures and political forces, and also impedes national accord and the revival of Russia."
