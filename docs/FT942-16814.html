940408 FT 08 APR 94 / Probe urged into investigation group Mr Michael Meacher, shadow Citizen's Charter minister, has called for an investigation into a company claiming to provide 'virtually unlimited access to all manner of supposedly confidential data'. He has sent to Mr William Waldegrave, Citizen's Charter minister, a leaflet from ACT Investigations Group. Services offered include information on corporate research programmes, merger and acquisition targets and bank accounts.
