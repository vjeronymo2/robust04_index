Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Duma Reluctant To Listen to Ex-Prosecutor"] [Text] The State Duma has no intention of hearing Aleksey Kazannik's reasons for resigning as Russian gerneral prosecutor. Deputies Aleksandr Nevzorov and Sergey Baburin pressed for this information. At yesterday's plenary session the lower parliamentary chamber rejected their suggestion that the question of Aleksey Kazannik's resignation be included on the agenda.
