BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 15 (KCNA) -- The first "Iltangbaek prize" sports competitions of the Korean People's Army (KPA) opened at the May Day Stadium here Tuesday. Football, basketball, volleyball, marathon, shooting, boxing, wrestling, judo and many other events will be competed by sports teams of all the units of the Korean People's Army. In his opening address, Choe Kwang, Politburo member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and chief of the general staff of the KPA, said that the sports competitions for "Iltangbaek prize" are a significant one contributing to enhancing the national sports technique as a whole and strengthening the militancy of the People's Army by developing sports work within the KPA. A football game took place after the opening ceremony. The competitions will be held annually here to mark the anniversary of the victory in the fatherland liberation war.
