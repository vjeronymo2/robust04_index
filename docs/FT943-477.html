940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / Reject Shop fined over copyright The Reject Shop was yesterday fined Pounds 2,500 and ordered to pay costs of Pounds 24,250 for criminal breaches of copyright law, John Mason writes. Earlier this month the chain was convicted at Bow Street Magistrates Court of five breaches of the Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988 after it was found guilty of selling tins, photograph albums and notebooks which infringed the copyright of Mr Robert Manners, a tile designer.
