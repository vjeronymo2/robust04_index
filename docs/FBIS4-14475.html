BFN [By KAZTAG correspondent Yelena Miroglova for TASS] [Text] Almaty, 30 Mar -- The CIS consultative council on labor, migration, and social protection of the population finished its work here today. Its attention was focused on problems connected with the development of a common labor market. It discussed reports by labor ministers on migration processes, the social and labor situation in CIS states and measures being undertaken in this sphere, as well as matters relating to cooperation and interaction in training and raising the qualifications of staff employed in central labor bodies. The council decided to create joint training programs and a data bank on labor and social protection matters. It examined a number of draft agreements, in particular a multilateral agreement on reciprocal actions and cooperation in work safety. Draft bilateral agreements were prepared between Russia and Kazakhstan on regulating migration processes, employment of the population, investigation of production accidents and also a number of others of importance for the two states.
