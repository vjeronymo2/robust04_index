Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The National Peace Keeping Force [NPKF] is to cost the taxpayer a total of 384.5 million rands, 315 million rands less than expected. This was revealed at a TEC [Transitional Executive Council] meeting in Pretoria yesterday, when the details of the budget of the NPKF was announced for the 1994-1995 financial year. The budget from 1 April this year until 30 June, is 255 million rands.
