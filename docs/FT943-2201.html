940920 FT 20 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Turnpyke recovery continues Turnpyke Group, the West Midlands-based springs and pressings manufacturer, reported continued recovery in the first half of 1994 with pre-tax profits of Pounds 16,000, the first reported profit since the mid-1980s, against losses of Pounds 185,000. Mr Eric Cater, chairman, said with the sale of a property improving the cash position, the company expected the better performance to continue in the second half. Turnover was Pounds 1.25m (Pounds 1.34m). Earnings per share were 0.22p (losses 2.88p).
