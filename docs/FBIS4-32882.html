BFN [Excerpt] Lima, 29 May (EFE) -- Peruvian Economy and Finance Minister Jorge Camet and German Suarez, president of the Central Reserve Bank, today signed the letter of intent with the IMF, as was announced by President Alberto Fujimori on Saturday, 28 May. The letter of intent, listing the economic targets Peru intends to attain during 1994, will be submitted for approval to the IMF management. [passage omitted]
