BFN [Text] Cairo, 10 May (MENA) -- Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah arrived in Cairo this evening on a brief visit to Egypt. Upon his arrival he stated that he is carrying a written message to President Husni Mubarak from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. The Yemeni minister was received at the airport by Assistant Foreign Minister Ambassador Badr Hammam, Master of Ceremonies Hasan al-'Ajayzi, and the Yemeni ambassador in Cairo.
