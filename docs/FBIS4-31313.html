BFN [Text] Ohrid, 7 Jun (TANJUG) -- Matthew Nimetz, special envoy of the U.S. President in the Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM]-Greece dispute, arrived on a two-day working visit to Macedonia today to hold talks in connection with the resumption of the New York negotiations between Athens and Skopje on 13 June. Nimetz, who arrived from Athens, was welcomed at Ohrid's Podmolje airport by FYROM Foreign Minister Stevo Crvenkovski and Ivan Tosevski, an official of this ministry. Nemitz and Crvenkovski immediately started talks in Ohrid. The U.S. envoy will be received in Skopje tomorrow by FYROM President Kiro Gligorov.
