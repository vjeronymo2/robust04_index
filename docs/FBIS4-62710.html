BFN [Unattributed report] [Text] On 30 April 1994, the Ukrainian president signed the edict "On Trade and Economic Missions Within Ukraine's Diplomatic Delegations Abroad." In accordance with the edict, trade and economic missions will be created within Ukraine's diplomatic delegations abroad. They will represent and protect Ukraine's interests in the sphere of external economic activity in the relevant countries. Ukraine's Cabinet of Ministers has been authorized to ensure the implementation of measures for organizing the work of the trade and economic missions. The edict approves the Regulations on the Trade and Economic Missions Within Ukraine's Diplomatic Delegations Abroad. These Regulations specify the main work and functions of the trade and economic missions, their structure, and procedures for financing and resolve the question of interrelations between the missions and ministries and other central organizations of Ukraine's state executive authority, and so on. In view of the creation of trade and economic missions within Ukraine's diplomatic delegations abroad, Ukrainian Presidential Edict 313 of 17 August 1993 "On the Regulations on Ukraine's Trade Missions Abroad" has been invalidated.
