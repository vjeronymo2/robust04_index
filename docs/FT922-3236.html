920612 FT 12 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Wagon Industrial in Pounds 1.19m acquisitions Wagon Industrial, the materials handling and automotive group, has completed two acquisitions with a maximum combined payment of Pounds 1.19m. Barrett Storage Systems is being purchased from Henry Barrett Group for an initial Pounds 570,000 cash plus further payment up to Pounds 275,000 conditional on turnover targets. Barrett Storage designs and supplies storage systems, mainly for the retail market. The other acquisition is Weldem, which is involved in metal fabrication including the manufacture of storage tanks for petrol forecourts. Consideration is Pounds 350,000 cash. In April Wagon purchased Westward Holdings for Pounds 1.1m cash, plus settlement of loans amounting to Pounds 265,000.
