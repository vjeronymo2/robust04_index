940924 FT 24 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Increased exports behind Goodwin rise Increased exports helped Goodwin, the mechanical and refractory engineering group, to almost double pre-tax profits from Pounds 208,000 to Pounds 383,000 in the year ended April 30, on turnover 9 per cent higher at Pounds 14.75m. Earnings per share were 2p higher at 3.8p and the single dividend is held at 0.655p.
