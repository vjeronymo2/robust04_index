Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali has received Hakam Bal'awi, member of the Fatah Central Committee. The two sides reviewed the latest developments in the peace process and Jordanian-Palestinian relations. Dr. Jawad al-'Anani, minister of state for cabinet affairs and information minister, and al-Tayyib 'Abd-al-Rahim, Palestinian ambassador in Amman, also attended the meeting.
