940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / Japan's PM taken ill Japan's 70-year-old prime minister, Mr Tomiichi Murayama, was rushed to hospital last night after being taken ill during a ceremonial dinner for G7 leaders. A spokesman said the prime minister had complained of stomach pains but his condition was not serious. It was not clear whether Mr Murayama would be able to attend today's talks.
