Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin and Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin have held a meeting in Sochi with Nikolay Yegorov, the head of administration of Krasnodar Kray, and Sochi Mayor Nikolay Karpov. It was decided to set up a working group to draw up a treaty between the federal agencies of state power and the bodies running the kray concerning demarcation of functions in line with the Constitution of the Russian Federation and the Federation Treaty, taking account of the particular features of Krasnodar Kray. The Russian Federation's Security Council has been instructed to prepare a package of questions on Krasnodar Kray for consideration at one of its upcoming sessions.
