940708 FT 08 JUL 94 / Observer: Brought to account Red faces all round. The publisher of Capital Account, the magazine of the London Society of Chartered Accountants, has gone into receivership. Its unpaid bills include money owed to the society itself. Worse still, one of Capital Account's directors is David Haigh, author of a book called Marketing Your Practice, published by the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England and Wales. It describes him as having extensive experience in marketing and communications - but not presumably in adding up.
