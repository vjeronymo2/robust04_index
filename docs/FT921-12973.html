920123 FT 23 JAN 92 / International Capital Markets: Share offer by Chemical Banking priced CHEMICAL Banking Corporation, the US bank resulting from the merger of Chemical Bank and Manufacturers Hanover Trust, pushed ahead with its Dollars 1.36bn share offering yesterday, the largest international equity deal to emerge so far this year. The share issue was priced at Dollars 27.25 per share, representing a discount of 2.2 per cent on Tuesday's closing price of Dollars 27.875. The issue was for 50m shares, including 5m shares available for international investors, and there is a 15 per cent over-allotment option.
