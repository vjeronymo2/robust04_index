930921 FT 21 SEP 93 / International Company News: Cascades sells carton plants THE international Cascades paper and packaging group has sold three US carton plants and two Canadian plants to Shorewood Packaging of the US for CDollars 144m (USDollars 109m), almost all in cash, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. The deal completes a series of Paperboard Industries' asset sales intended to reduce the company's debt. Cascades acquired Paperboard Industries last December, and took it public. Last month it sold several of its western Canada packaging plants to a Canadian group for almost CDollars 100m.
