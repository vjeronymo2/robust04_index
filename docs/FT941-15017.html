940118 FT 18 JAN 94 / World Trade News: ABB in Dollars 100m Leipzig deal ABB Asea Brown Boveri, the leading power engineering group, has won an order worth more than Dollars 100m (Pounds 67.5m) for a combined heat and power plant at Gera near Leipzig, reports Ian Rodger in Zurich. The order, from Kraftwerke Gera, is the seventh ABB has received for building or refurbishing power plants in eastern Germany since its integration into the federal republic. The power station will deliver 76MW of electric power and 250MW of thermal energy for the local district heating system. ABB will supply two gas turbines, two steam turbines and the entire electrical plant. The plant is to be in operation by mid-1996.
