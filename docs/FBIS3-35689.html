Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The relations between the Democratic Russia movement and President Boris Yeltsin have become uneasy, Lev Ponomarev, a co-chairman of the movement, told a RIA correspondent. We are extremely worried by the fact that the president did not consult with the democratic forces, particularly over the key issue of selecting members of government, he said. According to Ponomarev, Democratic Russia is now forced to communicate with the president only through the press.
