November 3, 1989, Friday, Southland Edition

ARENS TO VISIT TOKYO

Israeli Foreign Minister Moshe Arens will visit Tokyo next week to discuss Mideast peace proposals and bilateral ties, the Foreign Ministry said Thursday. Arens will arrive Nov. 6 and meet with Prime Minister Toshiki Kaifu and Foreign Minister Taro Nakayama on Nov. 9 before returning home Nov. 11.
