920312 FT 12 MAR 92 / World Trade News: Rolls in China deal Rolls-Royce has made a breakthrough in the Chinese market for its Tay engine, with an agreement to supply Pounds 20m of engines to China Eastern Airlines, Paul Betts writes. The Shanghai-based airline is buying seven Fokker 100 twin-engine regional jets, to be powered by Rolls-Royce Tay 650 engines.
