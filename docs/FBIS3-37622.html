Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Byrkin] [Text] Moscow, 18 Feb -- The highest level of combat readiness was declared today in the Abkhazian Armed Forces. According to a report of the press service of an authorized spokesman of the Abkhazian republic in Russia, this is in connection with the active regrouping of Georgian troops on the Georgia-Abkhazia border along the Inguri River. According to the same information, early this morning the Coast Guard forces of Galskiy Rayon averted an attempted landing by Georgian assault forces. The Abkhazian Defense Ministry believes that there is still a danger of an incursion by subversive groups.
