January 5, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Adobe Systems Inc.: The computer software company said earnings for fiscal 1989 rose 60% to $33.7 million. Revenue totaled $121.4 million, up 45% from 1988. The Mountain View, Calif., firm said the increased results were due in part to new software products aimed at Apple Computer's Macintosh market.
