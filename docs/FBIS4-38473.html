BFN [Excerpt] Finnish President Martti Ahtisaari began his two-day state visit to Estonia, when his aircraft landed at Tallinn airport two hours ago. At 1100 an official welcoming ceremony took place at Kadriorg presidential residence, attended by Estonian President Lennart Meri, Premier Mart Laar, and Ambassador to Finland Jaak Joeruut. The Finnish president is accompanied by Foreign Minister Heikki Haavisto and Finnish Ambassador to Estonia Jaakko Kaurinkoski. After the ceremony Martti Ahtisaari and Lennart Meri held talks. They discussed Estonian-Finnish relations and European Union membership. No particular attention will be paid to Estonian- Russian relations during Martti Ahtisaari's visit. [passage omitted]
