920418 FT 18 APR 92 / Consultancy is suspended FIMBRA, the financial advisers' regulator, has suspended Securicare Financial Consultants of Ilford, Essex, and Mr Barry Lehaney, the firm's owner. The firm was authorised to give investment advice and to arrange transactions, but not to handle clients' money. Fimbra said it had issued an urgent suspension instruction in order to protect investors.
