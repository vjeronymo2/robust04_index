940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / International Company News: Nykredit rises to DKr2.14bn Nykredit, the Danish mortgage institution, lifted pre-tax profits last year to DKr2.14bn (Dollars 319.4m) from DKr141m and net profits to DKr1.32bn from DKr46m, writes Hilary Barnes in Copenhagen. A rise in security values contributed DKr1.6bn to earnings compared with a loss of DKr312m in 1992.
