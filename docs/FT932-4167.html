930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Lyons Irish grows to IPounds 9.4m Lyons Irish Holdings, the tea and coffee wholesaler, raised pre-tax profits by 20 per cent in the 52 weeks to March 6 from IPounds 7.87m to IPounds 9.43m (Pounds 9.3m). Sales of IPounds 24.9m compared with IPounds 25.1m for the 53 weeks to March 7 1992. The results received a fillip from a 42 per cent rise in interest receiveable to Pounds 3.78m (Pounds 2.66m). The dividend for the year is raised to 9.45p (8.6p) via an increased final of 6.3p. Earning per share came through at 27.41p (24.46p).
