BFN [Text] The Arab League regrets the new eruption of fighting in Yemen only a few hours after Sanaa accepted a cease-fire as announced by its foreign minister, Muhammad Salim Basindwah. Counselor Tal'at Hamid, the Arab League secretary general's press spokesman, said that the resumption of military action in Yemen does not help create the appropriate circumstances for negotiations. He added that the Arab League supports any Arab or international effort to stop this destructive war in line with UN Security Council Resolution 924. Hamid reported that the Arab League secretary general is constantly in touch with Yemeni leaders in the north and south and with several other Arab countries to implement the Security Council resolution.
