920429 FT 29 APR 92 / UK Company News: S&U advances to Pounds 4.3m S&U STORES, the Birmingham-based consumer credit house and hosiery manufacturer, lifted pre-tax profits by 25 per cent over the 12 months to January 31. The increase - from Pounds 3.43m to Pounds 4.31m - was achieved on turnover ahead to Pounds 52.3m (Pounds 46.3m). Earnings per share improved to 27.89p (21.53p) and a recommended final dividend of 6.8p lifts the total for the year to 9p (8p). A 1-for-10 scrip issue is also proposed. The group intends to change its name to S&U reflecting its move into the financial sector.
