September 16, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

THE MANY VIEWS OF 'DAYS OF RAGE'

On every other issue of concern to Americans, no matter how controversial, both sides have been publicly presented: pro-life vs. pro-choice; hawks vs. doves over Vietnam; pro-glasnost vs. anti-communists, to name but a few. Why must the Palestinian-Israeli conflict be the exception? Those who have protested against "Days of Rage" on the ground of bias should consider that sometimes the facts are biased.

MOHAMMED BARAKAT

Los Angeles
