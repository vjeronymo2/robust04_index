September 5, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

The Time Warp Again: The 15th anniversary of the release of the cult classic "The Rocky Horror Picture Show" will be celebrated with a big bash.

The party will be on Oct. 20 on the 20th Century Fox lot, complete with music and stage performances against the backdrop of a 35mm screening of the film at midnight. Richard O'Brien, one of the stars of the film with Tim Curry and Susan Sarandon, will host the event. Invitations will be given to fans who attend the Friday and Saturday midnight shows held around the country. BETH KLEID
