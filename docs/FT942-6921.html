940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Progress takes Gieves to Pounds 1.38m Further progress in the second half at Gieves Group resulted in pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.38m in the year to January 31, against a Pounds 5.07m loss last time. The directors of the retailer, licensor and publisher, have resumed dividend payments at 1p. Earnings per share were 6.2p (35.6p losses). Turnover of continuing operations rose from Pounds 17.1m (restated under FRS 3) to Pounds 18.1m. The results contained a sum of Pounds 630,000 representing the release of part of a provision no longer required. Yesterday, the shares added 3p to 66p.
