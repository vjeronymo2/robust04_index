January 25, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

STATE SENATE COMMITTEE ASSIGNMENTS

Committee assignments for members of the state Senate were announced Monday by the Senate Rules Committee, headed by President Pro Tem David A. Roberti (D-Los Angeles). Here are the assignments of Senate members who represent Los Angeles County Districts:

Ruben S. Ayala (D-Chino): Agriculture and Water Resources (chairman); Appropriations; Local Government; Revenue and Taxation; Veteran's Affairs.

Robert G. Beverly (R-Manhattan Beach): Appropriations; Banking and Commerce; Elections; Governmental Organization; Rules; Veteran's Affairs.

William Campbell (R-Hacienda Heights): Appropriations; Bonded Indebtedness and Methods of Financing; Governmental Organization; Health and Human Services; Industrial Relations.

Ed Davis (R-Valencia): Banking and Commerce; Budget and Fiscal Review; Education; Insurance, Claims and Corporations; Judiciary.

Ralph C. Dills (D-Gardena): Governmental Organization (chairman); Appropriations; Bonded Indebtedness and Methods of Financing; Education; Veteran's Affairs.

Cecil N. Green (D-Norwalk): Public Employment and Retirement (chairman); Agriculture and Water Resources; Education; Insurance, Claims and Corporations; Local Government; Transportation.

Bill Greene (D-Los Angeles): Industrial Relations (chairman); Budget and Fiscal Review; Elections; Governmental Organization; Toxics and Public Safety Management.

Gary K. Hart (D-Santa Barbara): Education (chairman); Budget and Fiscal Review; Constitutional Amendments; Energy and Public Utilities; Natural Resources and Wildlife; Transportation.

Bill Leonard (R-Big Bear): Budget and Fiscal Review; Natural Resources abnd Wildlife; Transportation.

Joseph B. Montoya (D-Whittier): Business and Professions (chairman); Banking and Commerce; Energy and Public Utilities; Health and Human Services; Insurance, Claims and Corporations; Industrial Relations.

Alan Robbins (D-Tarzana): Insurance, Claims and Corporations (chairman); Banking and Commerce; Budget and Fiscal Review; Transportation.

David A. Roberti (D-Los Angeles): Rules (chairman); Judiciary.

Don Rogers (R-Bakersfield): Agriculture and Water Resources; Bonded Indebtedness and Methods of Financing; Natural Resources and Wildlife; Public Employment and Retirement; Veteran's Affairs.

Herschel Rosenthal (D-Los Angeles): Energy and Public Utilities (chairman); Business and Professions; Elections; Governmental Organization; Health and Human Services; Industrial Relations; Toxics and Public Safety Management.

Newton R. Russell (R-Glendale): Banking and Commerce, Energy and Public Utilities; Local Government; Transportation.

Art Torres (D-Los Angeles): Toxics and Public Safety Management (chairman); Appropriations; Education; Governmental Organization; Health and Human Services, Housing and Urban Affairs; Judiciary.

Diane Watson (D-Los Angeles): Health and Human Services (chairwoman); Budget and Fiscal Review; Education; Judiciary; Public Employment and Retirement.
