930604 FT 04 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Rowlinson 14% ahead to Pounds 0.7m HIGHER RENTAL income and lower interest costs enabled Rowlinson Securities, the property company, to report pre-tax profits 14 per cent ahead for the year to March 31. On turnover of Pounds 8.96m (Pounds 8.41m) pre-tax profits were Pounds 722,000 (Pounds 631,000). Rental income was Pounds 3.42m (Pounds 3.2m) and finance charges were Pounds 1.55m (Pounds 1.84m). However there was a tax charge of Pounds 143,000 this time against a credit of Pounds 49,000, leaving earnings per share at 4.64p (5.44p). The company paid a second interim in lieu of a final in April resulting in a total payment for the year of 1.5p (1.35p).
