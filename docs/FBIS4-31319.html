BFN [Excerpt] Pristina, 7 Jun (TANJUG) -- The District Public Prosecutor's Office in Pristina today charged seven Kosovo Albanians with the criminal act of threatening the territorial integrity of Serbia and Yugoslavia. Charges were filed against Fehmija Ljestrani, Skeljzen Bajrami, Ljuan Heta, Nedzmedin Sadriju, Becir Muljeci, Isni Franc, and Bajram Galapani. According to the indictment, during the second half of 1992 and last year, while in the Republic of Albania, the group "in cooperation with other persons, plotted and carried out actions aimed at helping the secession of Kosovo and Metohija, and other parts of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia populated with members of the Albanian minority with the ultimate aim of setting up a separate state, and its integration with neighboring Albania."
