BFN [Text] Brother Shaykh 'Abdallah Bin-Husayn al-Ahmar, speaker of the House of Representatives, today received the French Senate delegation headed by (Robert Jejacques), member of the Senate and deputy chairman of the Yemeni-French Friendship Society, and the accompanying delegation currently on a visit to our country. The speaker of the House of Representatives welcomed the friendly French delegation, expressing our country's appreciation for all French stands vis-a-vis our country, people, and unity on all official and popular levels, particularly the stands of the Senate and the Yemeni-French and Arab-French Friendship Societies. The speaker of the House said: Our country will not forget the French friends' lofty stands, which support Yemeni unity, democracy, and the positive change that took place in our country after establishing Yemeni unity. He added that our country appreciates these stands and will reciprocate them in the future. The French official reiterated the support of the Yemeni-French Friendship Society and the Arab-French Friendship Society for our Yemeni people to safeguard unity and democracy entrenched following the parliamentary elections held on 27 April 1993. He expressed the Friendship Societies' confidence in ending the crisis in our country as soon as possible in a way that will preserve Yemeni unity and democracy and save Yemen from internal conflicts. He noted that the Yemenis can resolve the crisis within Yemen and maintain Yemeni unity. The meeting was attended by (Marcel Lupin), French Ambassador to our country, and brother Muhammad (?al-Qatim), member of the Yemeni- French Friendship Society.
