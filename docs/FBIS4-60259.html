BFN [Excerpt] An investigation has taken place into a breakout from Tazoult prison on 10 March, the circumstances in which the incident occurred, and the share of responsibility for the incident of public officials. Following the investigation, the governor of Batna Province, the head of security of Batna Province, the head of security of Tazoult district, the director of re-education affairs at the Ministry of Justice, and the Tazoult police chief were dismissed today. The first results of the investigation revealed that the terrorists had benefited from the assistance of two persons working at the police station. [passage omitted]
