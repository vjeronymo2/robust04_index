BFN [Unattributed ADS report: "Yuzhuralzoloto and the British RTZ Set Up a Joint Venture To Develop the Svetlinsk Gold Deposits"] [Text] The Chelyabinsk-based production association Yuzhuralzoloto and the British gold-producing company RTZ have signed a contract in London on creating a joint venture to develop the Svetlinsk gold deposits, Nikolay Popov, chief of the material resources department at the oblast administration's economics committee, announced on 7 April. The deposits' estimated reserves are 70 tonnes of gold. The decision on their joint development under RTZ technology was made back at the end of 1991 during a visit to London by the head of the Chelyabinsk Oblast administration as part of a Russian Government delegation. According to Mr. Popov, the joint venture's planned charter fund will be 100 million rubles. RTZ's share will be not less than 50 percent. Under the contract, the development of the deposits will begin after RTZ has conducted a geological survey and prepared a feasibility report. Deductions from the state budget for the use of mineral resources will be 4 percent of the value of the gold produced. Of this sum, 75 percent will stay in Chelyabinsk Oblast.
