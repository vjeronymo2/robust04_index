BFN [From "The News" newscast] [Text] Ruth de Krivoy, president of the Central Bank of Venezuela, has resigned, and the bank's entire Board of Directors has followed suit. Omar Bello will serve as acting president of the Central Bank of Venezuela. [In a related 250-word, Caracas-datelined report, Paris AFP in Spanish at 0013 GMT on 27 April cites Caracas radio stations in reporting that Krivoy has resigned with her entire team of advisers and technicians. The AFP report adds that Krivoy sent her letter of resignation to President Caldera, stating that she "does not wish to stand as an obstacle in the way of government plans to restructure its banking policy."]
