941128 FT 28 NOV 94 / Business Travel: BT in the air Britain's BT has announced an air-borne fax and data service for airlines and business jets that is said to operate at twice the speed of existing facilities. Initially the service will be available across an area from the Pacific to the Persian Gulf, via satellite consortium Skyphone. The service will become global when software is installed shortly at ground earth stations in Singapore and Norway. BT said: 'The lack of a faster fax and data facilitiy has held some (airline) operators back from (installing) on-board passenger communications.'
