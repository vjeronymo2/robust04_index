Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] New Delhi, Feb 20 (UNI) -- Indian and Russian Naval ships conducted a two-day exercise during a goodwill visit by three Russian ships to Bombay this week. The exercise was conducted between Indian Naval ships 'Ganga' a frigate, and Ranvir, a destroyer, and Russian ship Admiral Vinogradov, an anti-submarine destroyer. Such bilateral exercises have been undertaken by the Indian navy in the past also with warships of the Indian Ocean Littoral States and friendly countries, official sources today said. The other Russian ships included a landing ship L. Vilkov and a merchant tanker V. Kolechitskiy.
