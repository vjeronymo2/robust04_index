BFN [Unattributed report: "Final Document Discussed"] [Text] A working conference on state support for the institutions of a civil society organized by the Russian president's staff has completed its work near Moscow. The Russian president's Operational Information Center announced that the participants discussed the working conference's final document, prepared using material from the previous two days' sessions. The final document covers interconnected blocs of legislation governing the relationship between citizens and the state in spheres like the economy, the social program, the electoral system, information policy, and the system creating the legal foundations for the activity of a civil society.
