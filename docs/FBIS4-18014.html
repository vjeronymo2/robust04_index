BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sevindzh Abdulayev] [Text] Baku, 28 Apr--This evening (at about 1800 hours local time) a delegation of the CSCE Minsk group on Nagornyy Karabakh [Nagorno-Karabakh] was fired upon by Armenian armed formations at Terter, in the center of Terterskiy Rayon in the southwest of Azerbaijan. After arriving in Azerbaijan two days ago, the delegation went this afternoon to this frontline rayon to acquaint itself with the situation on the spot and to study the possibility of deploying CSCE observers there. According to a confidential source, two missiles from a Grad launcher fell close to the building housing the local administration at the time when the CSCE representatives were there. According to preliminary information, there are no casualties.
