920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Trio in talks to buy money broker Trio Investment Trust, a shell investment trust brought to the market 18 months ago by a firm of PEP specialists, said it is in talks to acquire Martin Bierbaum Group, the international money broker. Trio's shares have been suspended during the negotiations. Martin Bierbaum is a leading foreign exchange broker specialising in dollar/D-mark trades.
