920321 FT 21 MAR 92 / Election 1992: Real oratory - Ballot Box Michael Foot explained on Channel 4's A Week in Politics last night why he will become the first leader of the Labour party since the war to retire from the Commons and not go to the Lords. It is not just that he wants to abolish the upper house. He said: 'There are other places to speak. Hyde Park Corner or Tower Hill where Donald Soper speaks, lots of good places to speak, much better than the House of Lords. Much better audiences at any rate.'
