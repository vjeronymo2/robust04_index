BFN [Report by UKRINFORM-TASS] [Text] Kiev, 23 Apr -- An edict issued by the president of Ukraine entitles its citizens to a free share of the state property. According to this edict, in 1994 the citizens of Ukraine will be able to pay for the property to be privatized either from their privatization deposits in the banks or by privatization certificates. Beginning 1 January 1995, property privatization certificates will remain the only tender in such payments. The Ukraine Savings Bank is instructed to ensure that these certificates are issued to the citizens of Ukraine entitled to receive them in accordance with Ukraine legislation. The property privatization certificates are valid until 31 December 1996.
