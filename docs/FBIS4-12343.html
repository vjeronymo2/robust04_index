BFN [Text] Brother Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, received his excellency the Russian ambassador in Cairo this afternoon. The brother president entrusted the Russian ambassador with a message for President Boris Yeltsin on the latest developments in the peace negotiations and the obstacles the Israeli Government is producing to hinder the implementation of the declaration of principles accord, signed in Washington, and the agreements signed in Cairo, a matter which exposes the peace process to danger.
