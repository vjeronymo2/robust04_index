BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 4 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent thanks to Sin Sung-pom of the Pangyo County Security Department in Kangwon Province and his family and Han Sun-pung, an official of the party committee of the State Commission of Science and Technology, and his wife who had done good things for the socialist countryside and to the medical workers of the Kowon coal mine hospital who had greatly contributed to the promotion of the health of workers. Comrade Kim Chong-il also sent thanks to the scientists and technicians in Kanggye who had been successful in the researches into animal feed and to residents who had introduced the success to effect innovation in meat production.
