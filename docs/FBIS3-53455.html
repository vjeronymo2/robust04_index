Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The PLO Executive Committee last night decided against resuming the economic talks with Israel, which were due to be held in Paris this week. This was imparted by Palestinian sources to Sa'id Ma'adi, a Qol Yisra'el in Arabic correspondent. The sources noted that the PLO has further decided to discontinue all talks dealing with civilian matters in a bid to focus on security issues. Our correspondent notes that last night the PLO leadership held a debate on the results of the Taba negotiations. The participants received a report from Dr. Nabil Sha'th, the chief Palestinian delegate. The Taba talks are slated to resume tomorrow.
