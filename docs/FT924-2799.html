921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / Survey of Software at Work - Winter 1992 (12): Company Snapshot Fisher Park Fisher Park was founded in 1977 by its joint directors, and is based in Kings Cross, London. There are six employees. Nature of the company's business: Fisher Park designs spectacular backgrounds for rock tours and other musical events (such as Pavarotti in the Park), using a mixture of architecture and engineering. They are one of the leading three-dimensional designers in their field and are pace-setters in bringing their professional disciplines to this form of theatre. Turnover: Pounds 500,000 Key personnel: Two directors - architect Mark Fisher, engineer. Jonathon Park TECHNOLOGY FILE Software: Autodesk's AutoCAD release 11; Animator Pro; 3D Studio Release 2 Hardware: 3 seats, 2 Dell 386s PC plus one 486 (currently on loan). Cost of project: Pounds 20,000 inclusive of software and hardware. Support: The consultancy, Deus Ex Machina implemented the software and helped with front-end menus tailored to Fisher Park's needs. There has been no formal software training for anyone in the company.
