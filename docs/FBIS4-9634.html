BFN [Text] The ninth session of the Intergovernmental Bulgarian-Iranian Commission on Technical and Economic Cooperation has ended with the signing of specific agreements. The leaders of the two delegations -- Transportation Minister Ermenkov on the Bulgarian side and 'Ali Akbar Torkan, Iranian minister of roads and transportation -- signed a memorandum and agreement on air communications which will serve as a legal basis for opening regular air routes between Sofia and Iran. The two ministers also initialed an agreement on alleviating double taxation.
