July 19, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

THE NEIGHBORHOOD EATERY: MORE EATERIES:

Henry's Restaurant, 10549 Stanford Ave., Garden Grove. Opens Mondays through Saturdays 6 a.m. till between 10 p.m. and 2 a.m., depending on the crowd.

Kitchen closes at 5 p.m. on weekdays and 1 p.m. on Saturdays. (714) 537-9884. A tightknit but friendly crowd -- off-duty cops, construction workers, bikers, letter carriers from the nearby post office -- gathers for breakfasts, blue plate special lunches, cold beers, retirement parties, you name it. Newcomers will feel welcome. JAN JONES
