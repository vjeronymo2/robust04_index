March 31, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPOTLIGHT: SAN DIEGO ENTERTAINMENT BRIEFS

Ex-Martha Graham principal Ellen Segal will return to the Westminster Theater on May 18-20 with "Mostly New Garage Dances," a concert that picks up where the dance maker left off last year. As the title suggests, most of the works on this program will be premieres, including a work by guest artist Betzi Roe of Three's Company. Also scheduled for this concert is a revival of an early Segal signature work, "Teaming," which comes to grips in pure dance terms with the crippling illness that sidelined Segal for more than a decade.
