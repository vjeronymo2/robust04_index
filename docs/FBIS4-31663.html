BFN [TA SR report: "A Realistic Green Philosophy"] [Text] The Slovak Green Party has been registered under the altered name of Slovak Green Alternative (SZA). The SZA's objective is to constitute the party on a realistic approach to green philosophy, a philosophy that will push for the optimum relationship between Slovakia's economic development and a healthy environment. This is the guarantee for a healthy and also socially secure individual and whole of society. The SZA Preparatory Committee is organizing the party's constituent congress on 18 June in Nitra. A decision will also be made on a coalition partner at the congress. The Preparatory Committee has held informative coalition talks with representatives from the Association of Slovak Workers (ZRS) and the Slovak National Party (SNS). Coalition talks will also be held in the near future with representatives of the Movement for a Democratic Slovakia.
