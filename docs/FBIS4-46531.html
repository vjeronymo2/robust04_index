CSO [Report by KRASNAYA ZVEZDA correspondent Vladimir Gundarov under the rubric "Correspondents of KRASNAYA ZVEZDA and Telegraph Agencies Report": "The `Kursk' in Champagne Bubbles"] [Text] On 23 May dock trials began at Severodvinsk on the new nuclear missile submarine `Kursk' commanded by Capt 1st Rank V. Rozhkov. As pointed out by Ivan Vasilyev, in charge of releasing the order, the nuclear submarine has a number of improvements, including the navigational system, and improved living conditions for the crew. The new nuclear submarine will become a part of Russia's Navy in December of this year.
