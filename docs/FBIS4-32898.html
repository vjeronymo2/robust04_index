BFN [Statement by Humberto De la Calle, Colombian Liberal Party vice presidential candidate, at the Orquidea Real Hotel in Santa Fe de Bogota on 29 May -- recorded] [Text] Tonight, we are promising Colombia that we are going to win the runoff elections. [applause] Let there be no doubt about this. The liberals will rise up again and give us their strong support on 19 June. Tonight, dear friends, my message is one of optimism, happiness, and liberal faith. We are going to succeed; we are going to win. We are full of vigor and we want you to know that tonight. This is my emotional message of gratitude to all the people who gave us their strong support in today's elections. [applause, cheers] Long live the Liberal Party. [Crowd shouts: "Viva!"] Long live the alliance for Colombia. [Crowd shouts: "Viva!"] Thanks, dear friends. A thousand times, thanks.
