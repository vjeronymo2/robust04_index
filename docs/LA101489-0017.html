October 14, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

SOUTH AFRICAN SPORTS BAN IS FAR FROM BEING EQUAL

Can someone explain why civilization would be threatened if South African track and field athletes, rugby or soccer teams were allowed to compete outside their own country?

If they are being ostracized for their government's policies, how come South African tennis players, golfers and prizefighters compete everywhere without detectable ill effects?

Zola Budd was banned for attending a track meet as a spectator. Did anybody check to see if Gary Player or Gerry Coetzee watched a golf tournament or a boxing match there?

GUY R. TURGEON, Los Angeles
