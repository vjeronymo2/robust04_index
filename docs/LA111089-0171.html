November 10, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

OCEAN VIEW HAS TO RALLY TO BEAT MARINA

Ocean View High School rallied for 15 points in the fourth quarter to avert an upset and claim a share of the Sunset League championship with a 15-7 victory over Marina.

For three quarters, the Seahawks (7-3 overall, 4-1 in league) self-destructed, getting penalized 11 times for 130 yards.

Marina (2-8, 0-5) scored on its first possession for a 7-0 lead.

Sean Rogers capped a 52-yard drive with a three-yard touchdown run but on the conversion, Rogers sliced his kick wide right and Marina held a 7-6 lead with 9:19 to play.

But after Ocean View's defense held, Seahawk quarterback Randy Karliner found Scott Caringella behind the Marina secondary for a 60-yard touchdown pass that put Ocean View ahead, 12-7. MICHAEL ITAGAKI
