930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Pressac Better margins were reflected in the half year results to January 31 at Pressac Holdings, a manufacturer of automotive, telecommunications and lighting components. Turnover declined by Pounds 500,000 to Pounds 15.9m but at the pre-tax level profits rose 2 per cent to Pounds 619,000. The interim dividend is maintained at 0.75p from earnings of 1.55p (1.53p).
