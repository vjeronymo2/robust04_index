930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / Lucas SEI set to create 150 jobs LUCAS SEI, a joint-venture company of Lucas Industries and Sumitomo of Japan operating in Newcastle-under-Lyme, Staffordshire, is to take on 150 employees as production of car wiring systems increases. The company has contracts to supply the new Toyota plant at Burnaston, Derbyshire, and Rover and Honda. Since last autumn, Lucas SEI has recruited 250 people for its Newcastle-under-Lyme factory, which opened yesterday.
