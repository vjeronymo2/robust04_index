920307 FT 07 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Feltrim capital restructure Feltrim Mining is making an open offer to existing shareholders of 5.45m new ordinary shares at par (5p) and a further placing of up to 8m shares, also at par. The current 20p shares are to be sub-divided into one new 5p ordinary share and one deferred share. The company is to change its name to Minmet.
