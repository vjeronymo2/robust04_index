920122 FT 22 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Intl Media recovers International Media Communications achieved its first profitable half year since 1988 after benefiting from the delivery of the greater part of the British Airways order for Skyview, its project involving the installation in aircraft of individual video players. As a result, the group turned in pre-tax profits of Pounds 301,000 for the six months to October 31, compared with a Pounds 597,000 loss for the same period of 1990 and a Pounds 715,000 deficit for the last full year. Turnover doubled to Pounds 1.02m (Pounds 499,000). Earnings per share emerged at 0.13p, against losses of 0.51p.
