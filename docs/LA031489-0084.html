March 14, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

MARINA'S ROSS NAMED TOPS IN SUNSET LEAGUE

Sandra Ross of Marina High School has been named player of the year in the Sunset League in a vote of the league's girls' soccer coaches.

Ross, a senior sweeper, helped guide Marina to the league championship, making it the only team in eight years to beat Edison, this season's runner-up, for the league title. Ross, a two-year starter, contributed to 10 shutouts.
