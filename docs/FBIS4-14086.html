BFN [Text] Tehran, April 26, IRNA -- Majlis Speaker 'Ali Akbar Nateq-Nuri here Tuesday [26 April] praised the nation for its readiness to assist Bosnian Muslims and said the Islamic Republic of Iran was the first country to extend practical support to Bosnian Muslims. Speaking to a group of workers gathered in front of the Majlis to announce readiness to defend Bosnian Muslims, Nateq-Nuri stated that Majlis deputies, in compliance with the supreme leader's call, are also prepared to give any assistance to the Bosnian Muslims. He criticized the arrogant powers for their aid to the Serbs and the United Nations for its silence toward the crimes being committed by the Serbs in Bosnia-Herzegovina. Nateq-Nuri expressed hope that the Bosnian Muslims who are resisting with empty hands against the armed-to-the-teeth aggressor Serbs will be victorious and will bring disgrace to the UN and to those who backed the aggressors.
