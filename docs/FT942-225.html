940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / World Trade News: Bombay contract for Zimmer Zimmer, the Frankfurt engineering and construction group, has been awarded an DM85m (Pounds 34.2m) contract by Bombay company Rajashee Polyfil to build a polyester plant in Gujarat, reports Valerie Sylvester. The project is a joint venture between the Indian Birla Group and Akzo Nobel Fibres. Zimmer will supervise detailed engineering, construction and start-up of the plant.
