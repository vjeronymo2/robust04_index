BFN [Text] Ukraine's Minister of Foreign Affairs Anatoliy Zlenko flew to Turkey today, a special correspondent of the Novyny television and radio agency reports. He will take part in a session of the NATO Cooperation Council, which will be held in Istanbul. The organization was set up at the initiative of NATO heads of states in November 1991 and has been holding consultations on key political issues and security problems. The Ukrainian delegation will also attend the opening of the Ukrainian Embassy in the Turkish capital, Ankara.
