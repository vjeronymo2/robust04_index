BFN [Text] The government urgently sent Choe Tong-chin, first assistant foreign minister, to Russia on 20 April to hold working-level discussions with the Russian Government for the early return of North Korean loggers to the ROK. Assistant Minister Choe will meet with high-ranking authorities of the Russian Foreign Ministry and the Internal Affairs Ministry to confirm the loggers' will to defect and to discuss measures for their security. The ROK Foreign Ministry will form a working-level council headed by Assistant Minister Choe as soon as he returns on 23 April.
