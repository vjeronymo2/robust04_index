October 20, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

CITY SECTION ROUNDUP

Sylmar 35, Van Nuys 0 -- Sylmar (5-0, 3-0) took advantage of six Van Nuys turnovers and quarterback Daniel Terry ran for two touchdowns and threw two more at Van Nuys.

Terry scored on two one-yard runs in the first quarter and completed touchdown passes of five and 24 yards to Da'Ron Comier and Brian Roberson.

Terry completed three of six for 46 yards and carried the ball 13 times for 50 yards. Tobaise Brookins gained 54 yards in 16 carries.

Van Nuys fullback Robert Franklin carried the ball 10 times for 92 yards and caught three passes for 111 yards as the Wolves (1-4, 1-2)rolled up 276 yards. STEVE WILLIAMS
