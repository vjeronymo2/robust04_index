940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): Pearson - Other companies The City is expecting the media and leisure conglomerate whose interests include the Financial Times to turn in pre-tax profits between Pounds 65m and Pounds 73m on Friday for the six months to June 30, sharply up on last time's Pounds 46.3m. Unlike last time, acquisitions such as Thames Television and Extel will be included but not the demerged Royal Doulton and Camco.
