BFN [Text] Leader President Saddam Husayn has ordered the allocation of 5 million dinars for poor families within the geographic area of the al-Rashid Branch Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party. Comrade Fadil Mashhadani, secretary of the al-Rashid Branch of the Ba'th Party, told an INA correspondent that this generous contribution on the part of leader President Saddam Husayn embodies his excellency's care for the sons of his people to ease their suffering as a result of the continuation of the unjust blockade. He said the people's councils, in coordination with the party's agencies, have started distributing the president's contribution to poor families in al-Karradah, Baghdad al-Jadidah, al-Mashtal, al-Za'faraniyah, al-Mada'in, and al-Amin.
