930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Capita makes Pounds 4m software acquisition Capita Group, which provides services to the public sector, is to acquire West Wiltshire Software, a division of West Wiltshire District Council, for Pounds 4m cash. The council has also awarded Capita a Pounds 3m contract to manage its computer facilities until March 31 1997. West Wiltshire Software had income of Pounds 4.9m in the year to April 5 and generated profit of Pounds 3.1m.
