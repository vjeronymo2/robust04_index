Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 22 Mar (TANJUG) -- Federal Prime Minister Dr. Radoje Kontic has informed the Yugoslav Assembly about changes in his cabinet. According to Dr. Kontic, Nikola Sainovic, hitherto Serbian prime minister, and Uros Klikovac, currently a federal deputy and a member of the federal government's legal council, have been appointed as deputy prime ministers. At the same time, the federal prime minister informed the Assembly that he has relieved Asim Telacevic from his duties as deputy prime minister because he is taking up another post.
