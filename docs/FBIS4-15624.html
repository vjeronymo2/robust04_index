BFN [By UKRINFORM correspondent Mikhail Stetsyuk for TASS] [Text] Sevastopol April 11 TASS - The command of the Ukrainian Navy has issued an appeal to the Crimean leadership, urging to "protect the military from political games." The document said that "Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov is counting on the Black Sea Fleet in putting into effect the results of the survey, held in the Crimean Peninsula on March 27." The command of the Ukrainian Navy is concerned over a possible drawing of the armed forces into the implementation of the reform in the Crimea. The appeal stressed that the Black Sea Fleet is subordinated to the Ukrainian and Russian presidents and that citizens of the two fraternal countries are serving there.
