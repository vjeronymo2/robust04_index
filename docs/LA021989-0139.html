February 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TRAVEL NEWS AND BRIEFS

A slide show on Greece and Turkey will be held by Pasadena's Hill Avenue Branch Library at 1 p.m. Tuesday at the library, 55 S. Hill Ave., Pasadena. Admission free. For more information, call (818) 796-1276.

-- -- --

"On the Road to Morocco" will be presented by travel photographer and lecturer Mort Loveman from 1 to 2:30 p.m. Wednesday at the Roxbury Recreation Center, Roxbury Memorial Park, 471 S. Roxbury Drive, Beverly Hills. Admission free. For more information, call (213) 550-4761.

-- -- --

"How to Stretch Your Travel Dollar" will be the subject of a seminar at 9 a.m. Saturday at Rancho Santiago College, Santa Ana, phone (714) 667-3096, and at 9 a.m. March 4 at Irvine Valley College, Irvine, phone (714) 559-3333. Admission $20 a person at Rancho Santiago and $20 a person or $25 a couple at Irvine Valley. Call the college for registration. NORMAN DASH
