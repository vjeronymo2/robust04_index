920801 FT 01 AUG 92 / Markets: At a Glance - Stevens in Invesco MIM move Lord Stevens has resigned from his post of chief executive at Invesco MIM, the fund management group formerly known as MIM Britannia. He will continue as chairman until next April. Invesco MIM has been dogged by adverse publicity over its role as a manager of Maxwell pension funds and over the problems of one of its investment trusts, Drayton Consolidated. The new chief executive will be Charles Brady, who built up Invesco's US operations.
