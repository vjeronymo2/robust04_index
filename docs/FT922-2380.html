920617 FT 17 JUN 92 / World Trade News: Qatar in ethylene project QATAR Petrochemical (Qapco) plans to invest about Dollars 400m (Pounds 220m) to expand its ethylene plant, Reuter reports from Manama. A company statement said the project was aimed at raising plant production capacity from 300,000 tonnes annually to 470,000 tonnes. Qapco is a joint venture between Qatar General Petroleum, which holds an 80 per cent stake, the French company Atochem and Italy's Enichem, which each hold a 10 per cent interest. Last week Qapco signed a Dollars 2m contract with the American company ABB Lummus Crest and France's Technip for basic engineering and a turnkey proposal for the expansion.
