941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Lufthansa CityLine - Ventures and contracts Lufthansa CityLine, the German airline's commuter arm, has ordered eight Canadair Regional Jets (RJs) from Montreal-based Bombardier. The order, valued at Dollars 146m, is in addition to the 15 twin-engine RJs already operated by Lufthansa. About 48 RJs are in service worldwide, with firm orders for a further 41.
