May 2, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

FINANCIER MICHAEL MILKEN'S PLEADS GUILTY TO 6 FELONIES

One need not plead guilty to felonies to be a good family man, nor pay criminal penalties and civil fines to be a charitable person, nor go to prison because he is a financial genius, nor have double standards to be famous and wealthy.

And now I, as a taxpayer who will provide him with room and board for the next five years, ask Milken to write books and articles as to why the next generation of stockbrokers should not follow in his footsteps.

KENNETH Z. LAUTMAN

Los Angeles
