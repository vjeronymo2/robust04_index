December 2, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

EX-NHL COACH HAS HEART ATTACK

Michel Bergeron, former coach of the Quebec Nordiques and New York Rangers, was in stable condition Saturday after suffering a heart attack.

Bergeron, 44, was taken to a hospital near his home in Rosemere, Quebec, after complaining of intense chest pains Friday night. He had been watching the Montreal-Washington game on television.
