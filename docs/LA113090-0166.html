November 30, 1990, Friday, Orange County P.M. Final

LOCAL;

WOMAN'S BODY FOUND IN TUSTIN

The partially clothed body of an unidentified woman was found this morning at a construction site at Jamboree Road and Edinger Avenue.

Tustin police said the woman's body, which was naked from the waist down, was found near three large earthmovers at about 6:20 a.m. by workers who were arriving at the site.

Throughout the morning, detectives were searching the area for clues, including tire tracks in the dirt lot, where a business park is being built.

Police speculate that the woman may have died somewhere else and then been thrown from a car.
