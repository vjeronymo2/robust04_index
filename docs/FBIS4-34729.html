BFN [Text] Moron, Venezuela, 22 Jun (AFP) -- Riots, during which two grocery stores were looted, rocked Moron and Puerto Cabello today. Meanwhile, student protests were staged in Caracas. The disturbances took place in Moron after a march was called to protest the high cost of living, but the situation is already under control, according to statements to the media by Governor Henrique Salas. Similar protests were staged in nearby Puerto Cabello. Valencia--the country's third largest city located 100 km west of Caracas--was left without bus service because of a strike. Drivers are demanding higher bus fares, according to the governor. Around noon, protests were staged near the Central University of Venezuela in Caracas, and local journalists reported that police had arrived on the scene.
