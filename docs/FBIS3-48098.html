Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 19 (KCNA) -- Angolan President Jose Eduardo dos Santos received the Korean ambassador to his country on January 14 and said his government and people would invariably support the Korean people's cause of national reunification. He sincerely congratulated the Korean people on their achievements last year in the struggle for resisting the political and military pressure of the imperialist allied forces and achieving national reunification under the wise leadership of President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il.
