BFN [Text] Sofia, June 14 (BTA) -- Bulgarian border guards intercepted an attempt to smuggle three trucks loaded with petrol across the Bulgarian-Yugoslav border at 10 AM today. The trucks refused to obey the border guards who were forced to use their guns. An unidentified person was wounded 25 m from the border on Bulgarian territory, his life being out of danger. He was rendered medical aid. An extraordinary meeting with Yugoslav border authorities is being held on the site of the incident to clarify all circumstances. Experts of the regional directorate of the interior in the town of Montana have been sent to the site of the incident, the Press Office of the Interior Ministry reported.
