Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] The Cyprus Committee of Solidarity With Kurdistan revealed that in the past Theofilos Yeoryiadhis received many telephone calls from people speaking Turkish who threatened to kill him. It was also revealed that, according to information at the committee's disposal, one of the next possible targets of Turkish secret agents is Lieutenant General Dhimitrios Matafias, former National Guard Commander, who is considered to play a leading role in Greek activities in support of the Kurds. The committee has briefed the Greek police about this information. These revelations were made by members of the Committee of Solidarity With Kurdistan at a news conference this afternoon on the abominable assassination of Theofilos Yeoryiadhis. The news conference was given in the House of Representatives building in the presence of representatives from all the parliamentary parties, which condemned the assassination and praised the personality of the fighter for human rights, Theofilos Yeoryiadhis. On behalf of the House of Representatives, Vasos Lissaridhis, EDEK [United Democratic Union of Cyprus] chairman, said that a House delegation will visit New York to condemn the ongoing oppression of the Kurdish people and the assassination of Theofilos Yeoryiadhis to the UN secretary general. Mr. Lissaridhis also said that the infiltration of Turkish agents into the free areas will be raised for debate by the House plenary session. Speaking on behalf of the Committee of Solidarity With Kurdistan, Lazaros Mavros said that the action of the Turkish secret agencies has also extended into the free areas of Cyprus.
