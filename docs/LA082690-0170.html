August 26, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

NEIGHBORLY THING TO DO? SHOW A LITTLE HEART AND SOUL

Now I've heard everything! What happened to compassion and caring about one's fellow men? No wonder this couple do not want their names used -- a human life is not as important as the value of a home? If this couple don't have anything else to do besides checking on what their neighbors are doing, give them a cause. God knows, there are plenty of them to zero in on.

LOUISE OKEY

Huntington Beach
