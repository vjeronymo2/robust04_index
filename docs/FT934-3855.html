931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Rise at Crown Eyeglass Crown Eyeglass saw its shares rise 12p to 145p yesterday after reporting pre-tax profits up by more than 10 per cent from Pounds 274,000 to Pounds 303,000 in the six months ended September 30. Turnover of the USM-quoted group, which makes spectacles and ready-made reading glasses, grew 42 per cent to Pounds 2.54m (Pounds 1.78m). Earnings per share came to 12.7p (11.6p) and the interim dividend is stepped up to 3p (2.5p).
