February 14, 1990, Wednesday, Southland Edition

COJINX ROMPS IN 1990 DEBUT

Cojinx, a stakes winning 4-year-old filly making her 1990 debut, romped to 3 1/4-length victory in the $23,000 Discreet Purse at Laurel Race Course Tuesday.

The 4-5 favorite in a field of six ran six furlongs in 1:11 1/5 with jockey Marco Castaneda to beat Sapgaedra and pay $3.60 to win.
