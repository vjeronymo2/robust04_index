Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] In the morning of 3 February, Defense Agency [DA] Director General Kazuo Aichi met with Prime Minister Hosokawa at the prime minister's official residence. Regarding the expense in purchasing an airborne warning and control system (AWACS), which the DA is demanding to be included in a budget for fiscal 1994, Aichi pointed out, among other things, the need to give consideration to Japan-U.S. relations. He demanded that the expenses be included in the budget, saying: "I want the expenses to be approved when the Finance Ministry's draft budget will be unofficially announced. The ministry is scheduled to do that on 10 February prior to your trip to the United States."
