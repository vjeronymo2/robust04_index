BFN [Text] Admiral Feliks Gromov, commander in chief of the Russian Navy, in a conversation with journalists, said: Efforts by a group of naval experts to estimate the fleet's strength, particularly its floating capability, is under way. The specialists will determine the parameters for the basis of the fleet's division, focusing on the categories of ships and their technical condition. Feliks Gromov pointed out that this is not a meeting of delegations but work done by experts. The work is difficult because every ship has to be examined. About his meeting with Admiral Volodymyr Bezkorovaynyy, the Ukrainian Naval Forces commander, Feliks Gromov reported that their points of view do not coincide, and it is necessary to do everything possible to reach a mutual understanding.
