BFN [By Riyad Mansur in Amman] [Excerpt] The Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine [PFLP] and the Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine [DFLP] have called for electing a new political leadership for the Palestinian people, rebuilding the Unified National Leadership of the Intifadah on new foundations, as well as holding an Islamic-Christian conference to defend the Arabism of Jerusalem. This is part of a draft plan for national salvation which is being debated by the cadres of the two fronts as a prelude to endorsing it within the framework of their unionist plan. This constitutes a step along the road of unity between the Palestinian left and its democratic and revolutionary forces. The program asserts that the two fronts are willing to set up an alliance under this program with all forces in the Palestinian arena. [passage omitted]
