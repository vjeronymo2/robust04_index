930602 FT 02 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Brent Walker sells Brighton casino Brent Walker has completed the sale, for Pounds 700,000 cash, of the capital and assets of Cotedale and Preston Street Properties, to Mr Roy Kerr. The companies own and operate the International Casino Club in Brighton. In 1991 Cotedale made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 2,318 and had net assets of Pounds 1.7m, while Preston Street showed a pre-tax loss of Pounds 3,798 and negative assets of Pounds 4,897. Brent Walker will use the proceeds to reduce debt.
