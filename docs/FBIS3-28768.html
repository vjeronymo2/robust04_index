Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 10 (KCNA) -- Cuban Ambassador to Korea Juan Jose Leon Vega and his embassy officials visited the Kimchongilia Greenhouse Wednesday on the threshold of the auspicious February holiday. After going round the greenhouse, the ambassador said he was very pleased to visit it on the occasion of the birthday of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il and the Cuban people regarded his birthday as an auspicious holiday as the Korean people do. He stressed that it is an expression of respect and reverence of the world people for the dear leader that Kimchongilia has found its way into more than 50 countries since it made its appearance. The visitors worked at the greenhouse.
