931209 FT 09 DEC 93 / People: Commercial retirement Sir Martin Jacomb, deputy chairman of Commercial Union, is to retire from the board with effect from 31 December 1993. Sir Martin, who is now 63, has served as a non-executive director since 1984 and as deputy chairman since 1988. In October it emerged that Jacomb was to retire from his post as deputy chairman at Barclays Bank. He had been with Barclays since 1985. Jacomb is a director of the Bank of England. He has been chairman of the British Council since 1992.
