October 11, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MISSED STATIONS ON DOUGHNUT QUEST

* Enjoyed your article on doughnuts. But are all the places you talked about really good enough to be mentioned in the same breath as Bob's in Farmer's Market? I mean, there are doughnuts, and there is Bob's.

The best other place I know of that you didn't mention is the Donut Factory on Burbank Boulevard and Hazeltine Avenue in Van Nuys.

CHARLES J. THOMAS, North Hollywood
