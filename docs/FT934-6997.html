931122 FT 22 NOV 93 / International Company News: IDV agreement with Polychem International Distillers & Vintners, the drinks arm of Grand Metropolitan, has reached agreement with Polychem, part of the Kilachand Group, to establish a joint venture company to produce, market and distribute IDV brands in India. ID India, the new company, in which IDV will have a 60 per cent holding, will begin production early next year of Smirnoff vodka, Malibu coconut rum, Archer's schnapps and Chelsea gin, and will bottle Spey Royal Scotch whisky.
