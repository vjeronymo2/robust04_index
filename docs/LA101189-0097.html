October 11, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Metrobank, an independent bank based in Los Angeles, has promoted five executives. Robert P. Bulseco was named president and chief administrative officer. He was an executive vice president. Other executives promoted: Robert Robie, vice chairman and chief credit officer; David Malone, executive vice president and chief financial officer; Paul Alexander, executive vice president of the loan division; Sharon Canup, corporate senior vice president, and Michael Provencio, corporate senior vice president.
