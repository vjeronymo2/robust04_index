Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] His Majesty King Husayn last night received a telephone call from 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Yemeni Presidential Council. They discussed the Jordanian efforts and good offices to enable the Yemeni brothers overcome their differences in accordance with what was agreed among the parties to the dialogue in the pledge and accord document. Jordan's support for Yemen's march of unity and democracy was affirmed.
