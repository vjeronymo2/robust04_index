Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, January 27, RIA--President Boris Yeltsin has signed an instruction on appointing Gen. Dmitriy Volkogonov as chief of a presidential commission to investigate cases of missing foreigners and Russian citizens missing elsewhere. The president has also signed an instruction on appointing Yekaterina Lakhova as chief of a presidential commission for female, family and demographic affairs, reports the presidential press service.
