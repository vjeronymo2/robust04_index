BFN [Text] President D.B. Wijetunga yesterday appointed Gamini Dissanayake as the minister of mahaweli development and Ronnie de Mel as the minister of ports and shipping. Yesterday's changes included the creation of two new ministries -- the Ministry of Peoplization of Public Enterprises and the Ministry of National Reconciliation. Mr. Joseph Michael Perera, who was previously the minister of fisheries, was appointed minister of peoplization of public enterprises; Anura Bandranaike as the minister of national reconciliation in addition to his previous portfolio of higher education. Mr. Gamini Athukorale retains two out of three of his portfolios mainly forestry and irrigation while mahaweli development was transferred to Mr. Dissanayake. Alick Aluvihare, who held the portfolio of ports and shipping, declined to accept the new ministry and was dropped from the cabinet.
