October 26, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Freudian Fever: Native son Sigmund Freud is still loved in Vienna.

Advance ticket sales for the new musical "Freudiana" have been brisk, even outpacing early sales for Andrew Lloyd Webber's "Cats" 7 years ago. The musical, based on the life of the father of modern psychology, opens Dec. 19. More than 59,000 tickets -- 47% of the available seats for the first four months of performances -- were snatched up in the first 30 days. CLAUDIA PUIG
