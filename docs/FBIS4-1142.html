BFN [From the press review] [Text] The DAILY GRAPHIC has an exclusive story on its front page about an alleged coup plot. According to the paper, its special investigation team has discovered that an alleged threat to editors of some private papers is actually a ploy of diversion for a coup plot to take place between the 7th and the 15th of this month. A special report named the editors as that of the STATESMAN, Mr. Haruna Atta, and Mr. Eben Quarcoo of the FREE PRESS. It also mentioned Major Courage Quashiegah, a leading member of the New Patriotic Party, as another target of the alleged assassination threat.
