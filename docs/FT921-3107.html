920316 FT 16 MAR 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 69m work for Trafalgar House TRAFALGAR HOUSE CONSTRUCTION (REGIONS) has been awarded over Pounds 69m of new business since early February. The Guildford and Bristol offices have picked up Pounds 27m. Guildford's largest project is from Tower Hamlets Borough Council in east London to build two teaching blocks for Whitechapel School. Bristol is undertaking a Pounds 3.8m Dolby laboratories project. The Cambridge office successfully tendered for a Pounds 10.3m batch of orders, including a Pounds 7.7m college complex for the county council. In the Midlands, the Ashby area office has received work worth almost Pounds 11m, including a Pounds 3m car park at Birmingham's Hall 10 for the National Exhibition Centre; the Tonbridge region has notched up over Pounds 6m; Middlesborough, Darlington, the north-west and Exeter have contributed contracts worth around Pounds 11.5m, and the Cardiff and Birmingham areas have recorded gains of Pounds 4m on their order books.
