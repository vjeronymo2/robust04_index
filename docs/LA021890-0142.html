February 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEXT TIME, THE TRAIN

In writing of the delights of Virginia's Shenandoah Valley (Jan. 28), Beverly Beyer and Ed Rabey once again neglect the train as a means of arriving at a delightful destination in a delightful way. Amtrak's Cardinal provides several access points to the Shenandoah Valley. Those who avail themselves of the Cardinal will witness the most beautiful U.S. scenery east of the Rockies.

TOM BROWN

Santa Ana
