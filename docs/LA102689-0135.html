October 26, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

An assistant coach and a football player at St. Genevieve High School in Panorama City have been suspended for two games and one game, respectively, for their involvement in a fight during last Friday's 45-6 loss to Chaminade.

Another player and coach have been reprimanded by St. Genevieve Coach Mark Lovett in connection with the fight, which started when a St. Genevieve player blindsided a Chaminade punt returner after the latter had called for a fair catch and the whistle had blown. The names weren't released.
