BFN [Text] Kuwait, 26 May (KUNA) -- Kuwait security sources said today that the Interior Ministry's Coast Guard administration has arrested eight Iraqis on Tuesday [24 May] afternoon after entering the country from the sea in a wooden boat. The sources added that the initial investigations revealed that the detainees claimed that they entered the Kuwaiti territorial waters for fishing. The sources said that the detainees have been referred to the competent bodies.
