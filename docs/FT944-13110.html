941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto stocks closed a busy session with light gains as soft precious metals shares tarnished an upward drive in energy and conglomerates. The TSE 300 composite index closed up 5.51 at 4,265.46 in turnover of 78m shares worth CDollars 1.12bn. Advances outran declines 416 to 332, with 291 issues steady. Index performance was mixed, with seven of 14 sub-indices ending higher. The gold and silver group tumbled 235.40, or 2.27 per cent, to 10,124.18. Toronto's oil and gas group dropped 113.37, or 2.52 per cent, to 4,607.41. Energy stocks dominated the winners list on the TSE.
