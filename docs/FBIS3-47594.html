Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 13 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received telegrams from foreign party leaders on the new year. The telegrams came from National Coordinator Oscar Ugarte Ubilluz of the Left Democratic Movement of Peru, General Secretary Luis Mateo Munos of the Socialist Party of Peru, Chairman Victor Sanchez Bilagra and General Secretary Ricardo Esli Garribia of the February Revolutionary Party of Paraguay, General Secretary Adelso Gonzalez Urdaneta of the People's Electoral Movement of Venezuela, President Angel Castro Lavarello of the National Executive Committee of the Left Revolutionary Union of Peru, Secretary General Ali Houdou of the Party of Revolution of Benin, National Chairman Emile Kibala Bey A Nsien of the Workers' Party of Zaire, National Chairman Otete Gaston Mboyo of the National Movement of the Genuine Lumumbist Combatants of Zaire, National Chairman Lambert Mende Omalanga of the Lumumba Congolese National Movement-Original of Zaire, Secretary General Mohamedoun Dicko of the Executive Committee of the African Party for the Solidarity and Justice of Mali, Chairman Vladimir Dado of the Slovak Communist Party, General Secretary Youssef Amin Wali of the Egyptian National Democratic Party, Chairman Klaus Riis of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Denmark (Marxist- Leninist), Secretary General Amadou El Bechir Gologo of the National Direction Committee of the Sudanese Union-African Democratic Rally in Mali, and President Andriantiana Rakutuvau and General Secretary Gisele Rabesahala of the Party of the Congress for the Independence of Madagascar. They extend warmest greetings to President Kim Il-song on the new year and wished him a long life in good health. They wish the Korean people success in the building of socialism and the efforts for national reunification under the wise leadership of President Kim Il-song.
