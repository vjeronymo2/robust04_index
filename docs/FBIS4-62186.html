CSO [NOYAN TAPAN report: "Vaan Avakyan Arrested"] [Text] The arrest of the former first deputy chief adviser to the president of the country on national security, Vaan Avakyan, in a Moscow airport where he had arrived from Bulgaria was announced in Yerevan. Avakyan was in Sofia until recently. The arrested person was turned over to Yerevan. Vaan Avakyan stands accused of trying to take documents containing state secrets outside the country. The arrest was coordinated with the Procuracy of Russia and the Russian special services. The investigation is being conducted by the investigatory department of the State National Security Administration of the Republic of Armenia. It is assumed that on 9 May, Avakyan will be permitted to see a lawyer.
