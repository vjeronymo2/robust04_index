930514 FT 14 MAY 93 / Commodities and Agriculture: Windwards banana earnings down 20% THE WINDWARD Islands, the main source of bananas imported by Britain, exported 12 per cent less of the fruit in the first quarter of this year and earnings fell by 20 per cent to USDollars 23.9m, according to the Banana Growers Association. Exports by the four islands (St Lucia, St Vincent, Dominica and Grenada) totalled 64,300 tonnes in the first quarter, with shipments from St Lucia, the largest producer in the group, falling to 27,700 tonnes.
