Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday in Vientiane, Foreign Affairs Minister Somsavat Lengsavat of the Lao People's Democratic Republic received Victor L. Tomseth, new ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the United States to Laos. The new U.S. envoy paid a visit to the Lao foreign minister after he presented a letter of credentials to Laos' President Nouhak Phoumsavan on 8 January. The conversation between the guest and the host focused on the improvement of efficiency in promoting and expanding the relations of friendship, cooperation, and assistance between the two countries. Moreover, Somsavat Lengsavat wished the Amrican ambassador successes in performing diplomatic duties in Laos.
