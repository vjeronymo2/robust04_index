Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Taha Yasin Ramadan, Revolution Command Council member and vice president, today received a French parliamentary delegation led by Serg Mathew, member of the French Senate and mayor of (Coreil of Julie). During the meeting, Taha Yasin Ramadan reaffirmed that Iraq, under leader President Saddam Husayn, will remain alive, strong, and effective, and that day after day, Iraq is proving to the world that its will is too strong to be weakened by any means of military, economic, or media aggression, He indicated that Iraq's strategic achievements, despite the circumstances of the unjust blockade imposed on Iraq, offer practical pieces of evidence about the Iraqis' resolve and adherence to their stands and principles to break the blockade and abort all links of plotting and aggression. The head of the French parliamentary delegation expressed great appreciation for leader President Saddam Husayn and his courage. He also voiced great appreciation for the Iraqis' courage, ability to overcome difficulties, and adherence to their principles. He said that his visit to Iraq falls within the framework of strengthening relations with the Iraqi National Assembly. He said that his meetings with Iraqi officials are productive and useful for the future of the Iraqi-French relations. The meeting was attended by the comrade head of the Foreign Relations Bureau of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party.
