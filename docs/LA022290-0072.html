February 22, 1990, Thursday, Valley Edition

WOMEN'S BASKETBALL PLAYOFFS

Golden West 115, Antelope Valley 61 -- Sophomore Shawna Armstrong scored a career-high 31 points to lead Golden West over Antelope Valley at Golden West.

Armstrong scored 23 points in the second half, including 18 on layups.

Armstrong scored nine consecutive points to help Golden West expanded a 57-33 halftime lead into a comfortable 68-35 advantage with 15:39 left. Donna Gondringer, who scored 21 points, had the other basket in the run.

It was the fifth consecutive game in which Golden West has scored 100 or more points. The Rustlers, the top-ranked team in the state with a 33-1 record, are averaging 94.1 points.

Molly Jonnum scored 18 points and Susan Hervey added 15 for Antelope Valley, which finishes 17-11. STEVE KRESAL
