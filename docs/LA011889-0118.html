January 18, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

GIRLS' BASKETBALL ROUNDUP;

IN OTHER CENTURY LEAGUE GAMES:

Santa Ana Valley 46, El Modena 23 -- Francesca Rumps scored 24 points, including 3 3-pointers, for the Falcons (9-5, 3-1) at El Modena.

Villa Park 41, Santa Ana 27 -- Susanah Thrasher had 16 points and 9 rebounds for Villa Park (5-9, 1-3). Stephanie Larson had 10 points for the Saints (7-7, 1-3).

In the Academy League:

Liberty Christian 55, St. Margaret's 14 -- Jennifer Caruso scored 19 points to lead Liberty Christian (9-3, 3-0) at St. Margaret's.

In the Angelus League:

Mater Dei 56, Bishop Montgomery 41 -- Kelly O'Brien had 25 points and 15 rebounds to lead host Mater Dei (14-5, 4-0). Cori Martin had 13 points and 11 rebounds for the Monarchs.

In the Empire League:

Esperanza 67, El Dorado 30 -- Karen Dalla Rosa had 15 points and 11 rebounds and Jennifer Harney had 14 points and 9 rebounds to lead host Esperanza. Chrissy Nieman scored 13 points for El Dorado.

Katella 38, Loara 35 -- Joni Easterly scored 18 points to lead host Katella (16-3, 4-0). Kim Yoshioka scored 11 points for Loara (12-8, 2-2).

In the Freeway League:

Fullerton 43, Sonora 32 -- Sommer McLain had 12 points and 15 rebounds and Lynn Gilbert had 11 points and 15 rebounds to lead Fullerton (10-6, 3-1) at Sonora.

In the Olympic League:

Orange Lutheran 35, Capistrano Valley Christian 21 -- Jackie Bagatourian scored 10 points to lead host Orange Lutheran (7-5, 3-3).

In the Orange League:

Brea-Olinda 85, Western 33 -- Allison Bickel scored 24 points and had 10 rebounds to lead host Brea-Olinda (14-2, 3-0). Tammy Blackburn had 16 points and 8 steals for the Wildcats. Jill Salazar scored 11 points for Western.

Savanna 58, Magnolia 25 -- Four players scored in double figures for the Rebels (12-5, 2-1) at Magnolia. Michelle Drew scored 14 points to lead the Rebels.

Valencia 102, Anaheim 25 -- Kristen McDonald had 30 points and 10 rebounds for host Valencia (10-7, 2-1). McDonald made 14 of 16 shots. Nancy Amoroso scored 18 points for the Tigers.

In the Pacific Coast League:

Costa Mesa 59, Laguna Hills 31 -- Maureen Moore scored 23 points to lead Costas Mesa (5-11, 1-2) at Laguna Hills. Denise Surmon scored 11 points and Kim Good 10 for the Mustangs. Good also had 12 assists.

Woodbridge 74, Orange 44 -- Leslie Rathman had 23 points, 7 rebounds and 7 assists to lead host Woodbridge (13-5, 3-0). Lisa Wehren scored 20 points for the Warriors. Lori Sallinger scored 10 points for Orange.

In the Sea View League:

University 43, Tustin 39 -- Denise Gandara had 15 points and 13 rebounds for host University (14-4, 3-0). Guard Teresa Perkins scored 15 points to lead Tustin (9-8, 2-1).

In the South Coast League:

Capistrano 74, Irvine 27 -- Holly Anderson scored 25 points to lead host Capistrano Valley. Shelley Zierhut had 21 points and 15 rebounds for the Cougars (14-5, 2-2). Danielle Ralston scored 12 points for Irvine (1-12, 0-4).

El Toro 71, Dana Hills 26 -- Kari Yoshioka scored 20 points, had 8 assists and 9 steals to lead El Toro (12-7, 3-1).

Mission Viejo 60, San Clemente 42 -- Derrie Nuta scored 21 points to lead host Mission Viejo (16-3, 4-0). Tina Ranker added 15 points for the Diablos.

In the Sunrise League:

Connelly 61, Our Lady of Loretto 27 -- Christa Tomacheski scored 16 points to lead Connelly (8-3, 4-1) at Our Lady of Loretto.

In the Sunset League:

Fountain Valley 69, Marina 55 -- Stacey Hisaki scored 20 points to lead host Fountain Valley (13-7, 3-1). Melisa Sortino scored 27 points for Marina (15-5, 3-1).

Huntington Beach 60, Westminster 42 -- Jennifer Ambrose had 20 points and Janet Davis 16 for Huntington Beach (3-12, 1-3). at Westminster.
