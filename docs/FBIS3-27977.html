Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Feb. 1 -- National Assembly Chairman Nong Duc Manh received here this afternoon Indonesian Ambassador to Vietnam Mr. Juana who paid him a farewell visit at the end of his term of office in Vietnam. At the reception, Chairman Nong Duc Manh highly valued Mr. Juana's contributions to the promotion of the friendship and cooperation between Vietnam and Indonesia. Chairman Manh asked Indonesian ambassador to convey his regards to Indonesian leaders and wished for further development of friendship and cooperation between the states, the legislatures and the people of the two countries, thus contributing to the peace, stability and prosperity in the region and the world as a whole.
