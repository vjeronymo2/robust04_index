Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 25 (KCNA) -- "The South Korean puppets staged a trial on February 21, wielding the `National Security Law' and cried that the North, though it is a `dialogue partner,' is `an anti-state organisation' in every respect. It is a mockery of and insult to the North," says the Secretariat of the Committee for the Peaceful Reunification of the Fatherland [CPRF] in its Information No. 644 published Thursday. It notes: "As for the `National Security Law' in which the South Korean puppets define the North as `an anti-state organisation', it is an anti-national and anti-reunification fascist law which should have been thrown into the dust bin of history long ago. "We cannot but take a serious view of the fact that the South Korean puppets made the rude remarks blatantly getting on our nerves when they were talking about the resumption of the North-South dialogue. "Entirely attributable to the South Korean authorities' dishonest stance of dialogue is the grave situation in which the North-South agreement and the joint declaration on denuclearization have not been implemented and all dialogues between the North and South have been totally broken up. "If the South Korean authorities truly want the improvement of the North-South relations and dialogue, they must change the confrontation policy into a reconciliation policy and abolish the fascist `National Security Law' against the nation and reunification."
