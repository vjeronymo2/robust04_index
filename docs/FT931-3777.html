930313 FT 13 MAR 93 / London Stock Exchange: Trafalgar busy Turnover in Trafalgar House rose to a total of 9.9m as the shares followed the market lower, the ordinaries easing a penny to 78 1/2 p, and the 'A' shares also down a penny at 75p. Swiss Bank Corporation was said to have been a heavy buyer of both classes of Trafalgar stock late in the session. The Seaq delayed ticker revealed a block of 4.4m ordinaries had changed hands at 76p and a block of 1.8m 'A' shares had traded at 75p just before the close of business. The broker has been acting for HongKong Land to raise its stake in Trafalgar to around 29 per cent.
