940425 FT 25 APR 94 / Company news this week: ICI - Other Companies A pure chemicals company since last summer's demerger from Zeneca, first-quarter figures - out on Thursday - should reflect both cost-cutting and the health of the European bulk chemicals market. Underlying trading is still tough with manufacturers struggling to push through price increases. But this will be more than made up for by cost-cutting and the disposal of the loss-making fibres operation in return for DuPont's acrylics business. Pre-tax profits should rise from Pounds 63m to near Pounds 100m.
