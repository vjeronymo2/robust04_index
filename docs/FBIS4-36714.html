BFN [Text] In Afghanistan, President Borhanoddin Rabbani's government has submitted a two-point peace formula for the restoration of durable peace in the country. The formula provides for the ceasing forthwith of rocket attacks on Kabul, honoring the cease-fire, and opening up all highways leading to Kabul. It also proposes the setting up of an authoritative nongovernmental commission to initiate work on the Representative Council and Grand Assembly.
