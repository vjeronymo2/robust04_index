BFN [Text] Pakistan and Germany signed an agreement on economic cooperation under which Germany will provide assistance worth 85 million marks [DM] to Pakistan for various projects in the social sector. The agreement was signed in Bonn yesterday. This assistance will be in addition to the DM190.4 million pledged by Germany, which has not yet been utilized. German State Secretary (Jargon Trunk) said this agreement reflects the desire of the German Government to play its role in the economic development of Pakistan, especially in the sectors identified as priority sectors by the Benazir Bhutto government.
