Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Ministry of Defense declares that the letter addressed by President Dr. Zhelyu Zhelev's [of 22 November 1993] to the minister of defense, which is not secret, settles the functional duties of the president's military cabinet as well as the mutual relations between the aforementioned cabinet and the relevant departments of the Ministry of Defense. The announcement of the Defense Ministry further stresses that no differences on the military policy of the Republic of Bulgaria and on the leadership of the Armed Forces exist between the president and the Defense Ministry's commanding staff.
