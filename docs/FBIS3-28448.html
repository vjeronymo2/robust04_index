Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 5 (KCNA) -- Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam sent a message of greetings to 'Umar Mustafa al-Muntasir upon his reelection as secretary of the General People's Committee for Foreign Relations and International Cooperation of the Great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriyah. Expressing the belief that the excellent ties of friendship and cooperation existing between the two countries would be further consolidated and developed, the message wished him bigger success in his work.
