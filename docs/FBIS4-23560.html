BFN [Text] The members of the Electoral Court were sworn in by President of the Republic Joaquim Chissano in Maputo today. The Electoral Court will be responsible for assessing contentious issues stemming from decisions made by the National Elections Commission. Speaking on the occasion, the Mozambican head of state said the task of the Electoral Court is to uphold the Electoral Law and guarantee that the Mozambican elections take place in strict adherence to that law. The Electoral Court has two Mozambican magistrates and five international judges appointed by the UN secretary general on recommendation of the Security Council.
