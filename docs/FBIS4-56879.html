BFN [By Huang Kwang-chun] [Text] San Jose, Costa Rica, May 8 (CNA) -- The Republic of China [ROC] and Costa Rica will continue strengthening bilateral cooperation and support, ROC President Li Teng-hui said over the weekend. President Li made the remarks during a news conference held following a breakfast meeting with Costa Rican President Jose Maria Figueres. Li said that during the meeting, he and Figueres exchanged opinions on matters related to existing and future cooperation between the two countries. Both of them are satisfied with bilateral relations and agreed to strengthen ties in the future, Li added. The Governments of the ROC and Costa Rica have traditionally supported each other in international affairs, Li said, adding that Costa Rica has demonstrated its sincere willingness to help forward the ROC's bid to enter the United Nations. President Li also pledged during the meeting to increase cooperation with Costa Rica during Figueres' presidency. In response, Figueres assured Li that Costa Rica will continue to cooperate with the ROC on the international stage and fight for human rights, democracy and freedom.
