Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 20 Jan (MENA)--The Higher State Security Prosecution decided today to detain terrorists 'Ali Muhammad Ahmad Farhan and Samir Isma'il Salih, Palestinian nationals, for 15 days pending investigations for committing the terrorist crime in al-Ma'sarah yesterday morning. The prosecution charged the two men with murdering Police Officer Sayyid 'Abd-al-Fattah Hasan, the attempted murder of Police Officer Khalid 'Adli, and possession of unlicensed firearms, explosives, and ammunition. The prosecution also decided to put the wounded terrorist 'Ali Muhammad Ahmad Farhan under guard in Qasr al-'Ayni Hospital. The prosecution took statements from members of the police unit that was at the scene of the incident yesterday. Members of the police unit also gave details about what was found in the apartment rented by the two suspects in al-Ma'sarah. Large quantities of bombs, explosives, and material used to manufacture explosives with the intention of carrying out terrorist acts and endangering security and public order were discovered in the apartment.
