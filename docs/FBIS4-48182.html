BFN [Text] Reports from inside Tanzania tell us that Republican National Movement for Democracy and Development [MRND] youth-wingers have been separated from the rest of the refugees and given a separate refugee camp, and some who have been directly accused of committing atrocities have been arrested and are now waiting for trial. This is a necessary step that has been taken by Tanzanian Government, because the youth-wingers have been committing atrocities against humanity, and should be arrested anywhere in the world. Apart from this, the youth-wingers are murderers who pose security risks to other Rwandese refugees in Tanzania, and security risks to the entire nation of Tanzania.
