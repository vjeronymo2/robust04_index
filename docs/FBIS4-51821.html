BFN [Text] Cairo, May 3 KYODO -- U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher urged Japan to work out a domestic demand stimulus package by July's Naples summit of leaders of the world's seven major economic powers, Japanese officials said. Christopher made the demand during a 35-minute meeting with Japanese Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa in Cairo. Kakizawa told Christopher that Japan's new cabinet of Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata will make sincere efforts to settle the long-standing trade row between Japan and the United States, the officials said.
