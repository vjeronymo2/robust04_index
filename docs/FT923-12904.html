920716 FT 16 JUL 92 / Israelis raise campus tensions Israel's new government yesterday became embroiled in an angry confrontation with Palestinians in the West Bank, underscoring the tensions that persist in the occupied territories despite talk of peace, Hugh Carnegy writes from Jerusalem. Troops laid siege to about 3,000 students and staff at an-Najah University in the West Bank town of Nablus, insisting on searching people because they suspected armed activists were on the campus.
