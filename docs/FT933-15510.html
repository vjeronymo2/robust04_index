930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Olives Holdings OLIVES HOLDINGS: Following sales by Kent Holdings, a company in which Mr Michael Kent, a non-executive director of Olives, is deemed to have a beneficial interest, and in which Mr Gerald Jiggins, a non-executive director of Olives, is deemed to have a non-beneficial interest, it has an interest in 3.87m shares, or 22.64 per cent of the issued capital.
