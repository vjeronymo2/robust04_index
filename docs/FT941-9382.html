940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Throgmorton 1000 Throgmorton 1000 Smallest Companies Trust reported a net asset value of 136.59p per share at the December 31 interim stage, against 118.41p six months earlier and 98.4p at end-1992. Assuming exercise of warrants the figures were 130.5p compared with 115.34p and 99.03p respectively. Earnings per share were 1.16p (1.34p).
