Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [By Moriyasu Okazaki] [Text] Beijing, 14 Mar -- Chinese President Jiang Zemin met with Susumu Nikaido, former vice president of the Liberal Democratic Party who is now visiting China, in Beijing on 14 March. During this meeting, Jiang emphatically repeated: "Even if China continues to grow and becomes powerful in the future, it will never become a military threat." He thus refuted growing warnings by China's neighbors, who are now alarmed by its sharp increase in military spending, that the country will become a threat to them. Jiang also indicated he was looking forward to Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa's visit to China starting on 19 March, and said: "I want to discuss various issues with Prime Minister Hosokawa, too."
