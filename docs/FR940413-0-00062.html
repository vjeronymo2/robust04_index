Nos. 61&hyph;95 and 141&hyph;5] RIN 2120&hyph;AF13 Renewal of Flight Instructor Certificates AGENCY: Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), DOT. ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: This final rule amends the Federal Aviation Regulations (FAR) governing the renewal of flight instructor certificates by permitting holders of flight instructor certificates to renew their certificates by completing an approved number of hours of ground or flight instruction, or both, in an approved flight instructor refresher course (FIRC). The effect of this final rule will provide an equivalent level of safety while reducing the financial burden placed on individual flight instructors.
