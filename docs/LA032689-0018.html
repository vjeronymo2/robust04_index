March 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

WALK LIKE A MAN

Imagine what the entertainment industry and the world of classical ballet would be without the male klutzes who drip pools of sweat onto the floors of dance studios across the country.

Should I start a list? Gregory Hines, Mikhail Baryshnikov, Fred Astaire, Ray Bolger, Balanchine. . . .

JIM STARRETT

Venice
