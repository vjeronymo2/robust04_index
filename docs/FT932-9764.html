930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Gartmore European Gartmore European Investment Trust had a net asset value of 115p per share as at March 31, up from 99.4p at the trust's September year-end. The net deficit for the six months to end-March was Pounds 77,000, against revenue of Pounds 16,000. Losses per share worked through at 0.38p (earnings of 0.09p). Directors expect a positive return for the full year.
