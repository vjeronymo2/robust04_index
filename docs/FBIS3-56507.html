Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] The situation remains tense all along the Karabakh battle front. The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] Army units are waging heavy defensive battles. The NKR Defense Army press service has reported that yesterday the Azerbaijani troops continued their incessant attacks against the Karabakh positions. The fiercest battles took place in the direction of Askeran-Agdam. The enemy used scores of armored cars. After destroying one tank, one armored car, and more than 60 soldiers, the Karabakh fighters forced the Azerbaijanis to retreat to their initial positions. Two were killed and seven injured on the Armenian side. No substantial changes took place in the other sectors of the confrontation line. The Karabakh positions at the eastern and southeastern sectors of the front have been coming under intensive artillery and rocket shelling since this morning. Also, the Azerbaijani Air Force has again become active. A number of Azerbaijani warplanes tried to attack Stepanakert, the capital of the NKR, between 1000-1200 today. Details are still being verified. The Armenian Defense Ministry Press Service has reported that it has been relatively calm on the Republic's border since yesterday night. No serious incidents have been reported.
