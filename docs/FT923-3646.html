920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / Owen arrives in Sarajevo In the war zone: Lord Owen, chief European negotiator for the former Yugoslavia, in helmet and flak jacket, walks past Bosnian militiamen in Sarajevo on his way to talks with the Bosnian president Alija Izetbegovic. He arrived in Sarajevo with Cyrus Vance, United Nations special envoy, after a hazardous journey from the Croatian port of Split. Details, Page 18
