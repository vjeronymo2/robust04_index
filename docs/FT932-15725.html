930410 FT 10 APR 93 / UK Company News: Chepstow jumps 57% to Pounds 247,069 Chepstow Racecourse lifted pre-tax profits by 57 per cent, from Pounds 156,971 to Pounds 247,069, in 1992 on turnover that advanced from Pounds 1.65m to Pounds 1.83m. Earnings per share amounted to 41p against a restated 27.2p in accordance with FRS 3. The proposed dividend for the year is held at 1p.
