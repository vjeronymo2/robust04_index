Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Belarus Government today found 31 billion rubles to service its debt to Russia for its gas and oil, which, as is known, exceeds 400 billion rubles. The Republican Government is prepared to apply compulsory measures to those Belarus companies that owe Russia, said Mikhail Myasnikov, the first deputy chairman of the Council of Ministers. In the deputy prime minister's words, the Belarus Government will do everything possible to clear the major part of its debt to Russia in the near future.
