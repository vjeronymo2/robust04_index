Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 8 Jan (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa met today with Jordanian Ambassador in Cairo Nayif Sa'ud al-Qadi. Al-Qadi said after the meeting that he briefed Musa on the results of the Palestinian delegation's talks with Jordanian officials in Amman. Referring to the signing of an economic accord between Jordan and the PLO, he affirmed his country's eagerness for solid relations with the PLO, for an agreement to be reached on all topics, and for the two sides to have a common vision for the future. He noted that relations should be normal because this will serve both the Jordanian and Palestinian sides. The ambassador said that he and Musa discussed ways to boost bilateral relations and to prepare for convening the Joint Higher Committee soon under the chairmanship of the two countries' prime ministers.
