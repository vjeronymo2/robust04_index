930923 FT 23 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Second Market asset value improves Net asset value per share of the Second Market Investment Company rose to 370.4p as at June 30 1993, compared with 289.7p a year earlier. Assuming full conversion of the convertible loan stock the figure increased from 286.1p to 365.6p. At the December year end it amounted to 310p. Earnings per share were 2.36p (1.58p) from available revenue of Pounds 242,858, against Pounds 156,718.
