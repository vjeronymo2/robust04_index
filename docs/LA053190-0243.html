May 31, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

OPERATORS PLUG IN INFORMATION

In name only, Directory Assistance is no more at Michigan Bell.

Call it Information, please.

That's what most people call it anyway. Now, Michigan Bell is changing the name of its service from Directory Assistance to Information. The service goes beyond giving out telephone numbers, to offering area code information or emergency numbers, Michigan Bell said.

When the service began shortly after the turn of the century, it was called Information. In 1967, it was changed to Directory Assistance. In 1971, the name was changed to New Number Information. A decade later, it returned to Directory Assistance.

The return to Information completes the circle, Eileen Dixon, a Michigan Bell spokeswoman, said.
