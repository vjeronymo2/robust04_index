930224 FT 24 FEB 93 / UK Company News: M&G Recovery At December 31 1992, M&G Recovery Investment Trust had a net asset value of 46.43p per capital share and 49.25p per zero dividend preference share. The trust was launched in March 1992. It yesterday declared a third interim dividend of 1p, making 2.7p to date; a total of 3.75p is indicated for the full year. Net revenue was Pounds 4.3m for earnings of 3.31p per income share.
