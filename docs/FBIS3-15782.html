Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belarusian Supreme Soviet Chairman Mechyslav Hryb has held a working meeting with Romanian President Ion Iliescu, who stopped over in Minsk on his way to Seoul. During the meeting, questions of bilateral relations and international cooperation were discussed. Participants in the meeting expressed mutual interest in the broadening of economic, political, and cultural ties between the two countries. According to the Supreme Soviet Press Service, President Iliescu invited Mechyslav Hryb to visit Romania. The meeting was attended by First Deputy Prime Minister Vyacheslav Kuznyatsow, Foreign Minister Petr Kravchanka, and other officials.
