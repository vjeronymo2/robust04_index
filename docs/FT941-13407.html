940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / World Trade News: Dollars 120m power order for GE General Electric of the US has announced orders totalling more than Dollars 120m to supply power generation equipment for projects in Egypt and Saudi Arabia, Andrew Baxter reports from London. The largest of the orders is from the Egyptian Electric Authority for two 627MW steam turbine-generators worth nearly Dollars 85m to be in a new power plant near Cairo.
