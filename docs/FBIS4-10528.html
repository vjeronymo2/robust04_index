BFN [Text] The Haitian Anti-Drug Service announced that it seized 12 kg of cocaine intended for the U.S. market at the Port-au-Prince International Airport on 12 April. The drug was concealed in the luggage of a Haitian citizen who was going to Miami, Florida, on a commercial flight. He was arrested and imprisoned. Two other people, who were with the owner of the luggage, were questioned. In recent days, more than 60 kg of cocaine coming from Colombia and intended for the U.S. market has been seized in Port-au-Prince by the Haitian Anti-Drug Service, which is working jointly with the local branch of the U.S. anti-drug office, Drug Enforcement Agency [DEA] [previous four words in English].
