BFN [Text] President Le Duc Anh cordially received Palestinian Foreign Minister Faruq Qaddumi who, now on an official friendship visit to Vietnam, paid him a courtesy call. Minister Faruq Quaddumi expressed his honor as this is his first visit to Vietnam. He also expressed his admiration and joy over the achievements scored by the Vietnamese people in their renovation process and in national construction at present as well as in their struggle for national independence in the past. President Le Duc Anh welcomed the visit to Vietnam of Foreign Minister Faruq Quaddumi, viewing it as a manifestation of the lasting friendship between the peoples of Palestine and Vietnam.
