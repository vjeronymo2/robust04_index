Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan ("AZG" daily), 02/03/94--The RA [Republic of Armenia] representative to Lebanon Yervand Melkonyan declared after meeting the Lebanon Ministry of Foreign Affairs Secretary General Ja'far Hasan, the Embassy of the Republic of Armenia will be opened in Beirut starting from March 1. The embassy will support the establishment of political, cultural and economic relations between the two countries. Approximately 200,000 Armenians live now in Lebanon. Eight out of 108 members of the Lebanon parliament and two State ministers are Armenians.
