Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] The Defense Agency held the first meeting of the "Conference on Defense Capability" with Director General Kazuo Aichi as its chairman on 1 March. In his speech, Aichi stressed: "Discussions at the council will be very important in deciding Japan's optimum defense capability." The Defense Policy Council, a private advisory body to Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa, has also started reviewing the "National Defense Program Outline" to study the optimum defense capability. The Defense Agency's study group will proceed independently with discussions on medium- to long-range policies.
