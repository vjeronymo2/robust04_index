November 1, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

PRESIDENT'S VETO OF CIVIL RIGHTS BILL

For whom is this country administered? Certainly not the "little people" as witnessed by President Bush's veto of the civil rights bill. Certainly not for the "little people" as witnessed by the refusal of Congress to reinstate the progressive income tax, which would require the wealthy to pay their share.

This is a government of the people, by the people and for the people? Ha!

MARTHA KUHN

La Mesa
