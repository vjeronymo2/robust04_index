BFN [Text] The Rwanda Patriotic Front [RPF] would like UN Mission in Rwanda [UNAMIR] to stay in Rwanda, and has no objection to the presence of Belgian blue helmets in the force. We spoke to Jean-Baptiste Ndahumba, RPF spokesman in Brussels: [Begin recording] [Ndahumba] We think that UN forces have to stay in Rwanda, UNAMIR must continue its mission, perhaps under different circumstances, but we need the help of the international community, we need the help of Belgium, which must return to our country once security is guaranteed. We want to maintain cooperation with the international community and with the United Nations. [Unidentified correspondent] So the blue helmets will always be welcome? [Ndahumba] They can stay if their security is guaranteed, and we think that as soon as the foreign nationals have been evacuated, we will do everything to bring back peace, order and security to Kigali, and to the rest of the country. [end recording]
