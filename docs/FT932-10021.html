930511 FT 11 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Donelon Tyson halved at Pounds 1.6m A SHARP setback in profits was experienced by Donelon Tyson, the construction group, over the 1992 year. with the outcome halved at Pounds 1.6m pre-tax, compared with Pounds 3.1m. Turnover fell from Pounds 80.9m to Pounds 63.5m and there was an operating profit of Pounds 2.15m (Pounds 4.14m) after charging Pounds 491,000 (Pounds 542,000) interest relating to income producing property and crediting a contribution from claims against the Department of Transport of Pounds 720,000. In addition Pounds 871,000 (Pounds 1.45m) was written off below the line, largely reflecting completion of the withdrawal from the industrial insulation contracting business. A maintained 1.2p dividend is payable from earnings of 2.4p (4.6p) per share.
