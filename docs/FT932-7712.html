930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / International Company News in Brief: US Shoe SHARES in US Shoe fell sharply as the retailer reported a first-quarter net loss of dollars 9.7m compared with earnings of dollars 2.8m. Net sales fell 1.1 per cent to dollars 640m, our New York staff writes. The company said the losses were primarily due to the women's clothing retailing division, which had operating losses of dollars 20.3m against dollars 3.3m. A new president of these operations had recently been appointed, the company added. On Wall Street, the shares dropped dollars 1/2 to dollars 9, a 52-week low.
