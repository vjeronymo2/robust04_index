BFN [Unattributed article: "European Union/Finland: European Membership Terms Ratified"] [Text] (ANSA) Copenhagen, June 15 -- The Finnish parliament has approved the terms under which that nation agrees to join the European Union [EU], in an unofficial confidence call for Prime Minister Esko Aho's center-right government. The tally was 97 in favor, 80 against, with 18 abstentions and 4 absentees. Aho had said that failure to approve the terms of Finnish adherence to the EU would seriously delay that country's timetable for joining.
