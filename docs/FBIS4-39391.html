BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Nikolay Morozov] [Text] Bucharest, 7 Jun -- A protocol on the establishment of diplomatic relations between the Republic of Moldova and the State of Palestine was signed in the Moldavian Embassy in the Romanian capital today. The document was signed by counsellor to the envoy, Moldovan charge d'affaires ad interim in Romania (Ion Kepetsyne) and by Palestine's ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to Romania (Fuad al-Bittar).
