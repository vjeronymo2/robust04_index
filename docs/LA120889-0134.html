December 8, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

UNDER WRAPS: Draped in plastic, the five large pillars at the corner of Jamboree Road and Campus Drive in Irvine have been baffling passers-by. . . . Turns out they are marble figures from Greek mythology by sculptor Paolo Borghi. . . . Purchased from a hotel in Dallas, they'll be unveiled this month, says Mola Development Corp., builder of an office and commercial project on the site. . . . When the project is completed, they'll be moved from the corner into the complex. Dana Parsons and Mark I. Pinsky
