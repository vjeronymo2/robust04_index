Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat has left Amman after attending the signature of the Yemeni reconciliation and reform accord. Mr. 'Arafat paid tribute to the efforts exerted by his majesty the king in bringing about the agreement between the parties of the political spectrum in Yemen. He described the signing of the accord as a historic and important incident that supported the pillars of the Yemeni unity. He said the ratification of the accord by Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and his deputy 'Ali Salim al-Bid is considered a beneficial action not only to Yemen but to the Arab nation as a whole.
