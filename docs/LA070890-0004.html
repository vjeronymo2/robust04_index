July 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

OK, you finally got me to write. After 30 years of subscribing to The Times, I finally protest and am sending back to you the "Tales of the Crypt" cover of our TV guide for the week of June 3.

The cover was disgusting and not a sight I wanted to see every time I picked up the guide.

In the future, please be more aware of how this affects small children (my grandchildren, to be specific) and at least not be as graphic.

I still love The Times and will forgive this one mistake, but I just felt you should know how one subscriber felt about this sort of cover.

Patricia A. Akers, Stanton
