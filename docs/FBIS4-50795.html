BFN [Text] Brussels, May 21 (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese Peoples' Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), ended his five-day goodwill visit to Belgium today. Belgium is the last leg of Li's five-nation European tour which also took him to Finland, Sweden, Norway and Denmark. Before departure for home, Zhu Xun, CPPCC secretary-general who accompanied Li during his five nation tour, described the visit as "successful." The visit, the first of its kind by a CPPCC leader, has promoted understanding, exchanges and friendly cooperation between China and the five countries, Zhu said.
