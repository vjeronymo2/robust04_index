May 17, 1990, Thursday, Valley Edition

5 OCCIDENTAL PLAYERS ON SCIAC TEAM

Three Occidental College baseball players have been named to the All-Southern California Intercollegiate Athletic Conference first team.

Sophomore pitcher Dave Stoll, senior catcher Mike Knisely, and Mark Harris, a junior infielder, were named to the team.

Outfielder Jason Busk and designated-hitter Scott McDonald represented Occidental on the second team.
