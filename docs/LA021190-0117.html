February 11, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

THE GOOD IGNORED

The news media are blowing out of proportion gang happenings and painting the wrong picture of Southeast San Diego.

For all the drive-by shootings, there are as many good things happening, such as the Regional Youth Center that provides summer jobs for the low-income families. The Palace, formerly called the Boys' Club, provides recreational activities and dance parties on the weekends. The Bandini Center is a continuation program that helps students make up classes.

Southeast San Diego is not always as the newspaper and TV describe it. I live there and I like it.

FREDDRICK BROWN

San Diego
