931204 FT 04 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Celltech undersubscribed Celltech, the emerging bio-technology company, received applications for 1.84m of the 7m shares available to the public in its flotation. Last week, Celltech successfully placed the entire 20m shares, raising Pounds 50m. Celltech said yesterday the shares were not expected to be a short-term investment. The flotation raised Pounds 27.3m after expenses. Dealings are expected to begin on December 9.
