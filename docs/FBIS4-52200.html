BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 7 (KCNA) -- The congratulatory group of Koreans in Japan led by Yi Chin-kyu, first vice-chairman of the Central Standing Committee of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan [Chongnyon], left here today by air after participating in celebrations of April 15. It was seen off at the airport by Kim Yong-sun, Chang Chol and other officials concerned.
