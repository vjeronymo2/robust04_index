November 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

HOTEL ADDITIONS

Your roundup of hotels in my hometown, Manhattan, prompts me to submit a couple of my own: the Salisbury Hotel, 123 West 57th St. -- pleasant surroundings, prompt room service and a staff that caters to the clientele. Also the Gramercy Park Hotel, 2 Lexington Ave. (at 21st Street), has Old World charm among the skyscrapers.

SHERRY TERZIAN

Los Angeles
