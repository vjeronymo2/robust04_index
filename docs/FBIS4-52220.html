BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 7 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received a visiting relative to the anti-Japanese revolutionary struggle Hu Zhenyi, widow of Chai Shirong, Chinese anti-Japanese revolutionary martyr, and her family members. Present were were Kang Sok-sung, director of the the Party History Institute of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, and officials concerned. President Kim Il-song looked back with deep emotions on unforgettable Chinese comrades-in-arms who fought shoulder to shoulder with him against Japanese imperialism, the common enemy of the Korean and Chinese peoples, under the uplifted banner of anti-Japanese struggle, highly appreciated their feats and had a warm conversation with the guests. The guests presented a gift to President Kim Il-song. President Kim Il-song arranged a luncheon for the guests.
