BFN [Excerpt] Knin, 6 Apr (TANJUG) -- In western Slavonia, both sides [Krajina Serbs and Croats] today started to withdraw heavy weapons 10 or 20 km, Officer Branko Opacic from the Serb Krajina army's general staff told TANJUG. The withdrawal -- envisaged by the Zagreb cease-fire agreement -- had been delayed because of a misunderstanding concerning the interpretation of a number of clauses of this agreement, but all problems were resolved on the spot by joint commissions.
