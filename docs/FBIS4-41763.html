BFN [Text] Armenian State Directorate for Underground Resources and the Russian Committee for Geology and Exploitation of Underground Resources signed an agreement in Moscow on cooperation in the geological study and exploitation of underground resources. The principal aim of the agreement is to draw up and implement a mutually-advantageous policy for the geological study of the underground resources. In accordance with one of the points in that agreement, a protocol was also signed in Moscow on the Armenian Republic's participation in the exploitation of oil and gas fields in Russia. The Armenian side is mostly interested in participating in the exploitation of one of the oil and gas fields in western Russia. It will make certain investments and participate in the work by establishing an Armenian-Russian joint venture with any Russian organization certified to exploit the underground resources. In return, the Armenian side is ready to examine the issue of the participation of the Russian enterprises in the geological study and exploitation of the oil and gas fields in Armenia.
