920826 FT 26 AUG 92 / Kabul attempt to mediate Two senior members of Afghanistan's ruling Leadership Council yesterday announced they would go to Kabul to negotiate an end to fighting between rebel mujahideen and Afghan government forces that has killed thousands, agencies report from Islamabad. Vice President Mohammad Nabi Mohammadi and Younis Khalis, leader of a Hezb-e-Islami splinter faction, planned to go to Kabul or its surrounding province today, officials said.
