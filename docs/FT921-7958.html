920220 FT 20 FEB 92 / World Trade News: Venezuela signs letters of intent for oil development CHEVRON of the US has signed letters of intent with Petroleos de Venezuela on development of heavy oil and other opportunities in the country. The signing, which comes only two weeks after an abortive coup attempt in the Latin American nation, provides for the creation of joint study teams. They will make recommendations for upstream and downstream activities, as well as on potential for the transfer of technology. Mr Kenneth Derr, Chevron chairman, said: 'We are pleased to take this important step by joining forces with Venezuela in further developing its tremendous oil reserves'.
