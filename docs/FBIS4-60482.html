BFN [Text] The prime minister has expressed satisfaction on his visit to the United States. Addressing a news conference in Washington D.C., Mr. Narasimha Rao said though essentially a goodwill mission, the visit has both political and economic content. His discussions with President Clinton had convinced him that India and the United States can develop bilateral relationship of maturity and strength. It can contribute not only to mutual good but also to the world as a whole. His current visit has helped towards developing better understanding between the two countries, Mr. Rao observed.
