930924 FT 24 SEP 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables Crunchy English, Dutch and Belgian conference pears are this week's best fruit buy at 25-50p a lb (25-50p). Italian black seeded grapes are also good value at 60p-Pounds 1.50 a lb (60p-Pounds 1.20). Bananas from Central and South America, are 40-60p a lb (40-60p). Cabbages have benefited from ideal growing conditions. English and Dutch white and Dutch red cabbages are 20-30p a lb (20-30p). Dutch savoy cabbages are also 20-30p a lb (20-40p). This week's best salad buy is English celery at 30-45 a head (30-45p). Other good salad buys are English beetroot at 20-35p per 12 oz bunch. NB last week's prices shown in brackets.
