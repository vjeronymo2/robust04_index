BFN [Text] Beijing, May 17 (XINHUA) -- Two American correspondents were questioned for unauthorized news coverage this morning by Chinese department concerned according to law. The two correspondents, Lucky Severson and Gary Fairman, are from the American National Broadcasting Corporation (NBC). They conducted news covering activities without first going through the prescribed formalities, said an official from the department. The official said that the two correspondents admitted that they conducted news coverage without going through the required formalities and promised to obey Chinese laws and rules in future. They were let go after the inquiry.
