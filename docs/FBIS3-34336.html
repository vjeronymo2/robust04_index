Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] His Eminence Ayatollah Khamene'i, the eminent leader of the Islamic revolution and the commander in chief of the Armed Forces, has issued an edict appointing Interior Minister 'Ali Mohammed Besharati as the commander in chief's deputy in charge of the Law Enforcement Forces of the Islamic Republic of Iran for a period of three years. Here follows the text of the message from the eminent leader: In the name of God, the merciful, the compassionate. Your Excellency 'Ali Mohammed Besharati, the esteemed interior minister. Taking into consideration your revolutionary background, commitment, trustworthiness and caring efforts toward your duties, and to give greater operational power to the interior minister in establishing security and welfare for the people, I appoint you as the commander in chief's deputy in charge of the Law Enforcement Forces of the Islamic Republic of Iran for a period of three years. I hope that you succeed in carrying out your responsibilities through the extensive authority entrusted to you, and the interest and support of the Forces' commanders and its dynamic personnel. [Signed] Seyyed 'Ali Khamene'i
