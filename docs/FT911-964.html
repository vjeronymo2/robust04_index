910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Wiggins Teape arm makes US disposal Appleton Papers, a subsidiary of Wiggins Teape Appleton, the paper group, is selling the business of East Shore Chemical Co, of Michigan, for about Dollars 40m (Pounds 23.21m). The business is being sold to a partnership of Mitsui Toatsu Chemicals of Japan, and Yamamoto Chemicals.
