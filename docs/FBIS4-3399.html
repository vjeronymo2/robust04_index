BFN [Text] Beijing, April 19 (XINHUA) -- Xiao Yang, China's minister of justice, and his Argentine counterpart, Jorge Luis Maiorano, signed a memorandum of understanding here today on judicial cooperation and exchanges between their countries. They pledged to exert further efforts for bilateral cooperation, especially between their ministries. At the signing ceremony today, Xiao said that the memorandum laid the foundations for further development of relations between the two ministries of justice. Maiorano said that the Argentina-China memorandum was the first of its kind argentina has signed with an Asian country, and was also the first a Latin American country has signed with China. He added that the memorandum would facilitate direct exchanges between the two ministries.
