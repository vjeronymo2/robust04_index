Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The political department of the Croatian Army's operational zone of Split has reported that the Serbs repeatedly attacked Croatian Army's positions in the Zadar hinterland today. At around 1200, they fired several mortar shells on Kasic and Lakici. Between 1300 and 1420, they fired around 10 artillery shells on the Maslenica area. At the same time they also fired on Novigrad. The Croatian Army has protested to the UN Protection Force over these Serb attacks, the strongest in the last three months.
