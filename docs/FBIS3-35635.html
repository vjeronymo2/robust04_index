Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, January 29 (SNARK) -- The press-service of the Karabakh Defence Army headquarters has told today SNARK an unexpected calm set in at the Karabakh front from yesterday evening. The observers say the relative calm is connected with the visit of Vladimir Kazimirov, the Russian President's Special Ambassador, to the region of the Karabakh-Azerbaijani conflict. Meanwhile, the headquarters of the Defence Army reports the Karabakh forces managed to reject all the enemy's attacks in different parts of the front. Moreover, they threw back the Azerbaijanis from a number of strategically important hills and took favorable positions in the north of Kelbadzharskiy and Mardakertskiy region. The both sides had losses. Yesterday evening the Azerbaijani aircraft bombarded the Karabakh territory. The consequences are cleared out.
