930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Baillie Gifford Japan net assets rise Net asset value at Baillie Gifford Japan Investment Trust improved from 436.9p to 769.9p, fully diluted over the 12 months to August 31. The retained loss for the year to end-August was halved at Pounds 169,000 (Pounds 342,000) for losses per share of 1.54p (3.11p).
