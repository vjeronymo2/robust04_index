930105 FT 05 JAN 93 / Management (The Growing Business): Ready for the new VAT regime - In a Nutshell The removal of most border controls and customs checks has led to a radical change in the way businesses must collect VAT data and trade statistics. A handy guide* to the new VAT regime called 1993 - Are you Ready? has been provided by accountants Price Waterhouse. It describes the information companies will be required to file, explains the new terminology and provides examples of new VAT registration number formats in the EC countries. *27 pages. Free.
