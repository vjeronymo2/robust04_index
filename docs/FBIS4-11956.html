BFN [Text] Army sources told Qol Yisra'el that they do not expect any reduction in army service in the Hebron area in the wake of the agreement. It will take time for the Norwegian, Danish, and Italian observers to establish some framework for operating in the town with the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] and the Palestinians. In any event, Israeli officials are stressing that this is not a precedent and that responsibility for security remains with the IDF.
