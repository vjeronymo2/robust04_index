BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 14 (KCNA) -- Korean cadres saw a circus show by acrobats from different countries participating in the 12th April Spring Friendship Art Festival at the Pyongyang Circus Theatre on Wednesday evening. The show was seen by Premier Kang Song-san, Vice-premier and Minister of Culture and Art and Chairman of the Festival Organizing Committee Chang Chol, Minister of Finance Yun Ki-chong, Chairman of the State Commission of Physical Culture and Sports Pak Myong-chol, other officials concerned and working people and artistes in the city. The performers put on stage colorful numbers including "aerial plastic stunt," "plastic trio," "hand feats," "the wedding day of a girl," "spear revolving" and "aerial flight on trapeze" which were acclaimed by the audience. A floral basket was conveyed to the acrobats for their successful performance.
