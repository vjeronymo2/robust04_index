Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sofia, March 25 (BTA) -- "We can only express deep regret at this decision," Bulgaria's Foreign Minister Stanislav Daskalov said in connection with the decision of the UN Security Council that Turkey should be allowed to contribute a contingent of blue helmets to UNPROFOR [United Nations Protection Forces] in Bosnia-Herzegovina. Bulgaria's position on this issue has been expressed clearly many times; a declaration was issued and President Zhelev sent a letter to UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali, which was later presented to the Security Council as well, he pointed out.
