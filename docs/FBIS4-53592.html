BFN [Text] The Office of the National Assembly held a news conference today to announce the agenda of the Ninth National Assembly's fifth session. Comrade Vu Mao, chairman of the Office of the National Assembly, said that the fifth session of the Ninth National Assembly will open on 26 May and continue for about one month. The main agenda includes debating and passing a code of law and five draft laws, examining the situation regarding the implementation of the 1994 tasks during the first months of the year, and other matters. Also at the news conference, Comrade Vu Mao presented a National Assembly Standing Committee resolution promulgating coordination between state authorities in directing and organizing the implementation of the National Assembly resolution on practicing thrift and fighting wasteful spending, corruption, and smuggling.
