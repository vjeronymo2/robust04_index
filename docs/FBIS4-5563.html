BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 11 (KCNA) -- Foreign artistes and Korean artistes overseas participating in the 12th April Spring Friendship Art Festival gave their premiere before full houses at the Mansudae Art Theatre, the February 8 House of Culture, the Ponghwa Art Theatre, the East Pyongyang Grand Theatre and other theatres in Pyongyang Sunday, separated into several groups. The colorful program of the festival, which began at a time when the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song was significantly being celebrated, include songs, dances and instrumental music. Participating in the current festival are more than 100 international and domestic contest prize winners and other famous actors and actresses and many art troupes and artistes who have participated in the art festival on several occasions to become intimate with the Korean people. Foreign artistes put on the stage songs written and composed by themselves in praise of the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il and Korean songs, delighting Korean people celebrating the holiday. The Bangladeshi artistes sang "Song of Praise to the Great Leader" they had long prepared for the Pyongyang festival, winning the acclamation of the audience. The trio of the Congo sang the song "Happiness of Mankind" reflecting the ardent desire of the world revolutionary people to always revere President Kim Il-song as the sun of mankind together with the Korean people, though they are different nations and have different languages and colors. A Cuban vocal soloist Mario Travieso, a domestic contest prize winner, sang in Korean the Korean song "Good Health and Long Life to the Leader" and Mongolian artistes Korean songs "It Is Snowing" and "Soil and Soldier" in Korean, deeply impressing the audience. Head of a delegation of the Japan-Korea music and art interchange society Mitsuko Ogasawara who has participated in the festival on several occasions to be well known to the Korean people sang the Korean song "We Will Go Together, Singing of Joy" to be applauded by the audience. N.A. Tlendiev, the conductor of the Kazakh art troupe and former people's actor of the Soviet Union, in the orchestral music "Thought of Comrade Kim Il-song" he wrote and composed after reading the reminiscences of the great leader "With the Century", emotionally presented his idea of "Believing in the People as in Heaven." Artistes from China, Russia, Cambodia, Yugoslavia, France, Nigeria, Malaysia, the Netherlands, Switzerland, and other countries put on the stage songs and dances reflecting the customs of their peoples. Korean artistes overseas, happy to be embraced in the bosom of the great father, the homeland, ardently sang of their reverence for the respected leader President Kim Il-song. China-resident Korean artistes in a narrative and song "Let Us Sing of Fortune in Having the Sagacious Leaders" sang of the Korean nation's pride and happiness in having President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il and enjoying good luck, to be enthusiastically applauded by the audience. The festival continues.
