Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine and PLO Executive Committee chairman, has said that he remains optimistic the differences with Israel concerning the implementation of the Declaration of Principles can be worked out. His remark came in an interview with the Britith Sky Television network screened last night. The disagreements between the PLO and Israel should not stand in the way of implementing the Gaza-Jericho accord, 'Arafat stated. It is an international document the signing of which was witnessed by the cosponsors of the Middle East peace process, President Bill Clinton, and the whole world, he noted.
