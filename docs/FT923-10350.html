920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / Banker rebuffed MR LORRAIN OSMAN, the UK's longest-serving unconvicted prisoner, failed yesterday in his latest attempt to block government moves to extradite him to Hong Kong, where he is wanted for trial on fraud charges. Mr Osman, 61, a banker who has been in prison 6 1/2 years fighting extradition, is considering an appeal against the High Court ruling.
