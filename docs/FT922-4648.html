920604 FT 04 JUN 92 / Vienna police seize uranium POLICE yesterday seized weapons-grade uranium smuggled out of the former Soviet Union and apparently offered to Arab clients, the Austrian news agency APA said, Reuter reports from Vienna. An Austrian, two Czechoslovaks and four Hungarians were held. The seizure was made in a central Vienna car park. The uranium, in a package weighing 1.2kg was found in a suitcase. Police said it contained 261 pellets of so-called UO2 comprising nearly 5 per cent uranium 235 and 55 grams of pure uranium 235.
