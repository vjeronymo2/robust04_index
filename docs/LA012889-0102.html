January 28, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

THE PREPS;

ROUNDUP;

JASON OCHS LEADS BREA-OLINDA PAST ANAHEIM IN SOCCER

Jason Ochs scored 2 goals to help the Brea-Olinda High School boys' soccer team tighten its grip on first place in the Orange League with a 6-2 victory over second-place Anaheim Friday at Anaheim.

The Wildcats (11-3-1, 5-0) went ahead, 3-0, in the first 25 minutes on goals by Ochs, Scott Maurer and Jeff Ortiz. Anaheim (8-4-3, 2-2-1) pulled to within 3-1 just before the half as Mike Barnas scored the first of his 2 goals.

Ochs, J.R. Ochs and Marco Hernandez scored second-half goals for Brea-Olinda.

Goalie Jeff Bettencourt made 6 saves for the Wildcats. Jose Escobedo made 13 saves for Anaheim.

In another Orange League match:

Magnolia 4, Western 1 -- Enrique Rodriguez scored twice and Brice Hoy and Jeff Speakman each had a goal for host Magnolia (8-7-2, 2-2-1). Josh Baugh scored in the final 2 minutes of the first half to tie the score, 1-1, for Western (1-12, 0-5).

In the Angelus League:

Mater Dei 3, Bishop Amat 2 -- Brian Oram scored 2 goals to lead the host Monrachs (8-8, 4-1). Oram gave Mater Dei a 1-0 lead early in the first half, but Bishop Amat tied the score on a goal by Jess Sandoval. Oram and Ryan Robobitaille scored in the second half to give Mater Dei a 3-1 lead.

In the Century League:

El Modena 1, Canyon 1 -- Randy Campbell scored in the first half for host Canyon and Mark Gonzales scored in the second half for El Modena. Chris Snitko had 5 saves for Canyon (12-2-5, 2-0-4) and John Eastman had 7 saves for El Modena (2-3-1 in league play).

Villa Park 3, Santa Ana Valley 0 -- Mike Noble, Ron Lou and Keith Amen scored for host Villa Park (8-4-6, 1-2-3).

In the Empire League:

Katella 2, Loara 0 -- Armando Orizaba and Dave Hardy scored for Katella (9-5-4, 3-1-2) at Loara. Loara is 4-8-4, 0-5-1.

In the Garden Grove League:

Kennedy 4, La Quinta 0 -- Freshman John Medina scored 2 goals for host Kennedy (8-4-3, 6-1-1). Diego Abbes and Mike Daniels also scored for the Fighting Irish.

In the Olympic League:

Santa Margarita 2, Valley Christian 1 -- Steve Feeney and Jason Forman scored for host Santa Margarita (9-7-1, 4-5). J.B. McCormick scored for Valley Christian (6-2-1 in league play).

In the Pacific Coast League:

Woodbridge 2, Costa Mesa 0 -- John Sczcuka scored 2 goals, 1 in each half, for visiting Woodbridge (7-4-4, 7-1-3).

Laguna Hills 1, Laguna Beach 1 -- Shan Gray of Laguna Beach scored 12 minutes into the second half and Chad Muhr of Laguna Hills scored 10 minutes later. Jeff Calvert had 9 saves for Laguna Hills (7-3-7, 4-2-5) and Dan Gebler has 12 saves for Laguna Beach (2-5-3 in league play).

In the Sea View League:

Saddleback 2, Estancia 1 -- Al Quintero scored with about 30 seconds left to break a 1-1 tie for visiting Saddleback (12-4-4, 5-0-1). Freshman Jose Escudero scored his 14th goal of the season in the first half for Saddleback.

Tustin 3, Newport Harbor 1 -- Matt Alexander, Scott Casey and Matt Hardy scored for Tustin (5-4-6, 1-2-2) at Newport Harbor. David Pattison scored for Newport Harbor (5-5-4, 2-3-1).

In Angelus League girls' soccer:

Mater Dei 6, Connelly 0 -- Goalies Michelle Lodgya and Julie Tarnay each made 1 save in shutting out Connelly at Mater Dei. Debbie Lewis, Christine Awadallah, Diana Awadallah, Tina Manfree, Carla Cichocki and Robyn Camarillo scored for the Monarchs (15-4-2, 6-1-1).

Rosary 3, St. Paul 0 -- Karen Miltenberger scored 2 goals and Amy Polmaszek scored 1 for visiting Rosary (5-2-2 in league play).

In Garden Grove League girls' basketball:

La Quinta 68, Kennedy 40 -- Amy Jalewalia had 21 points, 14 rebounds and 6 steals for host La Quinta (16-3, 9-0). Heather Brannan had 20 points and 14 rebounds for the Aztecs. Michelle Harris scored 20 points for Kennedy (6-3 in league play).

Los Amigos 56, Pacifica 32 -- Amy Pearson scored 21 points and had 13 rebounds and Angela Fischbeck scored 20 points and had 20 rebounds for host Los Amigos (7-10, 5-4).

Rancho Alamitos 60, Santiago 32 -- Jenny Newsome had 23 points and 16 rebounds to lead host Rancho Alamitos (15-2, 7-2).

In the Olympic League:

Whittier Christian 81, Capistrano Valley Christian 34 -- Cherilyn Culp scored 18 points to lead host Whittier Christian. Carrie Shannon scored 18 points to pace Capistrano Valley Christian.
