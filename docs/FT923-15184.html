920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Murray Split net assets at 159.7p Murray Split Capital Trust reported net asset value per capital share of 159.7p at May 31 - the end of its first year of trading. Net asset value of the income shares stood at 52.3p and zero dividend preference shares 109.5p. Net revenue in the 52 weeks to end-May amounted to Pounds 844,214, equal to earnings per income share of 10.55p. A final dividend of 2.5p makes an 8.3p total.
