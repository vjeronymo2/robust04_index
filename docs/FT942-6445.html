940530 FT 30 MAY 94 / UN officers report further rebel gains in Rwanda civil war Soldiers of the Rwanda Patriotic Force pictured near Kigali. UN officers reported further rebel gains in the civil war and diplomats said the government had fled its refuge south of the capital at Gitarama. However, there were still hopes that peace talks would be held today. Meanwhile, negotiations were going ahead on evacuating 40,000 refugees who are thought to be trapped in the capital.
