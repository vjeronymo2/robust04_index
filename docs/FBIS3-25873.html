Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 24 January (ZHONGGUO TONGXUN SHE)--According to statistics released by the State Patent Bureau, a total of 5,261 patent right petitions from Taiwan Province were sent to the State Patent Bureau in 1993, registering a 55 percent increase over the figure of 3,391 during the same period of the previous year. Thus, the number of patent right petitions from Taiwan ranks second among those sent by other provinces and municipalities across the country. The State Patent Bureau issued a "Circular on the Handling of Patent Right Petitions by Taiwan Compatriots" in December 1987, which met with a warm response among people from Taiwan's scientific and technological as well as industrial and commercial circles. In 1988 alone, the State Patten Bureau received 182 patent right petitions submitted by people in Taiwan. Since then, the number of petitions for patent rights have been increased yearly. Over recent years, an 106-percent increase has been registered in the number of patent right petitions sent from Taiwan, a most rapid growth rate in the entire country. It has been learned that most of the petitions from Taiwan are sent by individuals. As of the end of 1993, the State Patent Bureau has handled 11,546 patent right petitions submitted by Taiwan compatriots, and granted patent right to 6,409 petitioners.
