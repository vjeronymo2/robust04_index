940311 FT 11 MAR 94 / Observer: Unaccountable Terrified that you are going to miss the lengthy and arcane debates of the US Financial Accounting Standards Board? Don't despair. The FASB is offering punters the chance to listen by telephone as history is being made at this month's public hearings. Eavesdropping on accountants at Dollars 175 per half-day might sound excessive. Even so some overpaid US executives might be tempted to listen in since the latest session will cover the vexed subject of stock options.
