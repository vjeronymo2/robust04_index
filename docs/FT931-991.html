930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / World News in Brief: Eurotunnel Eurotunnel, the Channel tunnel group, claimed an important victory when the International Chamber of Commerce in Paris released an arbitration verdict preventing contractors Trans Manche Link from pursuing a global claim for extra payments for fixed tunnel equipment. Eurotunnel said the chamber had rejected contractors' requests to be paid on a cost-plus basis and said they would have to justify individual items separately.
