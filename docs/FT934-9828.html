931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / Business and the Law: Cadbury rebuff - Legal Briefs A survey of 1,000 companies in the UK shows that 43 per cent of listed companies are not intending to comply in full with the Cadbury Report on corporate governance, in spite of the fact that they will soon have to disclose non-compliance. The survey, for London and Oxford-based solicitors Manches & Co, also found that more than 50 per cent of companies fail to seek legal advice on key management issues, such as contracts of employment and leasing agreements.
