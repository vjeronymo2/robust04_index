930218 FT 18 FEB 93 / UK Company News: URS Intl shares suspended at 1/2 p URS International, the US professional services group which is quoted on the Unlisted Securities Market, yesterday called a halt to dealings in its shares at 1/2 p. The company said that this decision was taken pending an announcement of an acquisition and fund raising. A further statement, including the preliminary announcement of the company's results for the year 1992, will be made in due course.
