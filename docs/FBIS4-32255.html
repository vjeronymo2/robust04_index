BFN ["Macedonian Government and Parliament Asked To Change the Law on Census Completely" -- ATA headline] [Text] Tirana, June 22 (ATA) -- The Press and Information Department at the Foreign Ministry reports that in a communique issued after a meeting held a day before the census in the Former Republic of Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], the Party for Democratic Prosperity, the People's Party, the Liberal Party, and the Party of Democratic Action-The Islamic Road and the group of deputies of the Party for Democratic Prosperity asked the Macedonian Government and Parliament to change and complement the law on census as well as to postpone the schedule of the census. The communique says that the Albanian political parties hold that without meeting these conditions, the census cannot be realised.
