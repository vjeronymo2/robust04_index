Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Damascus, 12 Mar (SANA)--The unified command of the Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine [DFLP] and the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine [PFLP] has condemned the U.S. role which is obstructing the Security Council's role by preventing it from adopting a resolution condemning Israel for the barbaric massacre it perpetrated in the holy al-Ibrahimi Mosque. In a statement issued today, the command said that PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat's retreat from demanding international protection for the Palestinians, the dismantlement of settlements in the occupied Arab territories, and evacuation of settlers is a new violation of the Palestinian national consensus that negotiations with Israel should be stopped. In its statement, the command said that it renews its call for 'Arafat's resignation as a necessary step to learn lessons from the Israeli massacre and to maintain Palestinians' national rights.
