Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Text] President Suharto says it is impossible for Indonesia to send troops to Bosnia-Herzegovina because of the different war doctrines [doktrin perang] of the two countries. Discussing the outcome of today's talks between President Suharto and visiting Bosnian President Alija Izetbegovic in Jakarta, Minister-State Secretary Murdiono said that even though Indonesia will not send troops to the former Yugoslav republic, it supports the Bosnian people's struggle against Serbia and Croatia.
