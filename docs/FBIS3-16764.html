Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The leader of the State Duma Liberal-Democratic Party of Russia [LDPR] faction Vladimir Zhirinovskiy considers that the country's political situation will deteriorate sharply in April. Speaking today at the eighth regional party conference in Moscow, he suggested that unconstitutional actions would again be undertaken. Vladimir Zhirinovskiy is convinced that the LDPR will be victorious at the presidential elections. In his opinion, neither the Communists nor the new Zorkin-Rutskoy coalition nor any other opposition politicians have any chance of winning today.
