Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 3 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The Mongolian President P. Ochirbat's official visit to the LPDR [Lao People's Democratic Republic] ended as it was noted by the president of Mongolia P. Ochirbat and the president of the LPDR Nouhak Phoumsavan, the both sides are fully satisfied with the outcome of the bilateral meeting and negotiations. Before leaving the country, the presidents signed the treaty on friendly relations and cooperation between Mongolia and the LPDR. Signed were also the agreement on encouragement and protection of investments and the plan of cooperation between the Ministries of Health of the two countries for 1994-1996 by the heads of the corresponding departments. Within the framework of the visit of the Mongolian president the meetings of parliamentarians of the two countries were held.
