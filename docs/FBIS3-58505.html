Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Geneva, Jan 18 (AFP) -- PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat and Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres will attend a roundtable conference on the Middle East late this month, the president of the World Economic Forum said Tuesday [18 January]. Klaus Schwab said a direct meeting of the two men at the Forum scheduled to open in Davos, Switzerland, on January 27 could not be ruled out. Israeli-PLO talks on Palestinian autonomy in the Gaza Strip and Jericho have been deadlocked over the size of the Jericho area in the West Bank, security for Israeli settlements in the Gaza Strip and control of border crossings between the strip and Egypt and Jericho and Jordan.
