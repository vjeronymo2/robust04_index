Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Islamic Resistance announced that an explosive charge was detonated as an occupation patrol passed along the Shama' road in the security strip a few minutes after 0900 today. The occupation forces shelled Wadi al-Nafkhah and (Wadi Hinn) from their position in Tayr Harfa. The shelling continued intermittently until 1030. The Resistance also claimed the detonation of another charge at 1230 today as a patrol of the militia [South Lebanese Army, SLA] passed along the Shama'-Tayr Harfa road. The blast destroyed a red Mercedes 280 in which the militia's official in Tayr Harfa was riding. Last night, Resistance fighters attacked the Lahd [SLA] position in the central sector. A position of the Fijian contingent of the UN Interim Force in Lebanon in al-Mansuri was shelled from the militia [SLA] position in Jisr al-Hamra' at dawn today. The outskirts of Buyut al-Sayyid, al-Mansuri, and Majdal Zun came under shelling from the occupation forces' position in al-Bayyadah at dawn today. Israeli military planes overflew these areas and raked the nearby forests with machine-gun fire. Enemy planes overflew Zibqin, Kafra, Yatar, and Haris in dawn missions in which they fired flares. Israeli gunboats raked the area extending from al-Hamra' to al-Mansuri with machine-gun fire.
