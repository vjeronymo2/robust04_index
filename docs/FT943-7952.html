940819 FT 19 AUG 94 / World Commodities Prices: Copper price break-out fails - Market report failure of the three months delivery COPPER price to maintain an early afternoon break through resistance around Dollars 2,420 a tonne injected some caution into late trading at the London Metal Exchange. 'Most metals looked strong in the first rings (organised trading sessions) but when copper fell back it brought out profit-taking and buyers backed off,' a trader said. NICKEL was again a busy market as it continued its upward correction after the past month's heavy losses. Three months ALUMINIUM reached Dollars 1,493 a tonne as the market continued its recent steady advance off support which had built around Dollars 1,470. Compiled from Reuter
