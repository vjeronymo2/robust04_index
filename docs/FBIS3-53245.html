Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] A message from President Hashemi-Rafsanjani has been submitted to President Borhanoddin Rabbani of Afghanistan. According to a Central News Unit report from Kabul, our country's ambassador to Afghanistan delivered the message at a meeting with Mr. Rabbani, and declared the Islamic Republic of Iran's readiness to help resolve the Afghan civil war. The same report says that Mr. Rabbani, in turn, expressed gratitude for the efforts made by the Islamic Republic of Iran to resolve Afghanistan's problems, and thanked Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani for paying special attention to these matters. He said: We welcome any useful and constructive suggestion in this connection. The representative of the Islamic Republic of Iran also met Afghan Prime Minister Hekmatyar last night, in order to discuss ways of bringing the recent clashes in Kabul to an end.
