930623 FT 23 JUN 93 / UN to send force to Rwanda border The Security Council yesterday decided to send about 100 United Nations peacekeepers to monitor part of Uganda's border to stop Rwanda rebels from smuggling in weapons or reinforcements, AP reports from the UN in New York. The border monitor force could become the vanguard of a broader UN truce supervision mission of around 2,000 if a peace accord is signed tomorrow, UN officials said.
