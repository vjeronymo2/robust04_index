April 6, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

DIGEST: LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

TV'S DR. ULENE TO BE AT LOS ROBLES HOSPITAL

Dr. Art Ulene, whose health reports appear nationally on NBC-TV's Today Show, will be on hand to kick off the American Medical Assn.'s Campaign Against Cholesterol at HCA Los Robles Regional Medical Center in Thousand Oaks at 7 p.m. Monday.

The classes will run from 7 p.m. to 9 p.m. on Mondays through May 8. Cost is $75 per person, $100 per couple. For information call (805) 379-5464.
