Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] The two Italians held hostage in Somalia since last Saturday [12 February] were released this morning. The information was released by Defense Minister Fabio Fabbri. The two men, Gianfranco Stefani and Sergio Passadore, are currently on their way to Mogadishu by helicopter. It is yet known whether the $50,000 ransom demanded by the kidnappers was paid.
