BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] Today, President Carlos Salinas de Gortari appointed Hector Hernandez Llamas as executive spokesman for the Chiapas peace efforts, which is a recently established position. In order to carry out his duties, Mr. Hernandez Llamas will have to coordinate with Chiapas Governor Javier Lopez Moreno who has become responsible for conducting all efforts related to the peace process.
