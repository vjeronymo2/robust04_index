February 7, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SACRAMENTO

Gov. George Deukmejian Monday reappointed Jan R. Anton and Raymond G. Saatjian to the Del Mar Fair Board.

Anton, 46, of Del Mar has served on the board since 1985. He is a senior marketing associate for Iliff Thorn & Co. of San Diego, a commercial real estate firm.

A Republican, Anton is a member of the Urban Land Institute and the Commercial Industry Council and is a director of the Child Abuse Foundation of San Diego.

Saatjian, 45, of San Diego has also been on the fair board since 1985. A lawyer, he has been in private practice in San Diego since 1973. He is a Republican.

The new terms of Anton and Saatjian will expire Jan. 15, 1993.
