930525 FT 25 MAY 93 / International Company News in Brief: Saks Fifth Avenue MR PHILIP MILLER, currently chief operating officer at Saks Fifth Avenue, the upmarket US stores group, is to become chairman and chief executive at the end of June. Mr Miller, who ran Chicago-based Marshall Field, when it was owned by Britain's BAT Industries, will replace Mr Melvin Jacobs who is retiring, writes Nikki Tait. Saks was also owned by BAT until 1990, when it was bought out by Investcorp, an Arab-led investment consortium.
