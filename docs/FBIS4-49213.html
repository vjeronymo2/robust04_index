BFN [Text] Fuzhou, May 3 (CNS) -- Fuzhou, capital of Fujian Province, staged a fair on land development today offering 101 pieces of land for development with nearly 300 domestic and foreign real estate businessmen attending the meeting. Among the 101 pieces of land with a total area of 4600 hectares, 61 are offered for real estate development, totaling 300 hectares and the rest for industrial purposes and whole pieces of land development, totaling 4300 hectares. Among those for real estate development, 15 pieces of land are located in urban districts of Fuzhou while most of the rest pieces of land in the economically developed coastal counties and cities. The investors are allowed to use the offered land to build up commercial housing, hotels and villas. Those pieces of land used for industrial purposes and for whole pieces of land development mainly located in various economic developing zones, and they can be used to develop hi-tec items, factory workshops and warehouses as well as for agricultural and processing industrial purposes. The terms of utilization of these pieces of land are: 70 years for residential housing; 40 years for commercial, tourism and entertainment purposes and 50 years for those purposes of industry, education, science, technology and culture as well as public health and sports.
