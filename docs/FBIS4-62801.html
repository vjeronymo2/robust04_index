BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] The rise in the rate of inflation was under 10 percent in April. According to a report on the state of the Russian economy prepared by experts from the Economics Ministry, in the first quarter of 1994 Russia's industrial output fell more sharply than ever. The number of loss-making enterprises more than doubled over the period and amounted to 18.6 per cent of all industrial enterprises by the end of March. Another trend recorded in the first quarter was more promising. According to the RosBusiness Consulting agency, release prices in Moscow fell for the first time in April. Inflation expectations also fell for the first time.
