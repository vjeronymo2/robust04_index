920604 FT 04 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Restructured St James's Place Capital moves ahead from Pounds 4.3m to Pounds 18.4m PRE-TAX PROFITS at St James's Place Capital, the dealing and investment business chaired by Lord Rothschild, rose from Pounds 4.3m to Pounds 18.4m over the year to March 31. The company was created by a demerger of J Rothschild Holdings in 1990, and has been used as a vehicle for corporate finance work by Lord Rothschild. Much of the profit increase came from the sale of Bishopsgate Progressive Unit Trust Management, to J Rothschild Assurance which brought in a profit of Pounds 4.9m. There has been extensive restructuring - investment income declined from Pounds 24.7m to Pounds 13.4m, while fund management income increased from Pounds 5.1m to Pounds 8.3m. The final is 1.5p. Net asset value per share rose from 91.7p to 95.5p over the year.
