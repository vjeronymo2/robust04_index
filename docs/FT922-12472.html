920418 FT 18 APR 92 / UK Company News: ASH sells its 26% stake in Scantronic In line with its policy of disposing of businesses 'no longer necessary for' growth, Automated Security (Holdings), a supplier of burglar alarms and other security products, has sold its entire stake in Scantronic Holdings for Pounds 3.6m. It comprised 9.12m ordinary shares, representing 26.44 per cent of Scantronic.
