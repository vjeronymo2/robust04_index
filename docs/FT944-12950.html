941029 FT 29 OCT 94 / Briefcase, Q&A: Shares can't go into a Pep I am a 25 per cent taxpayer, and I hold a modest portfolio of blue-chip shares. Can I transfer Pounds 12,000 of these into a personal equity plan? Apart from new issues, it is not possible to transfer stock into a Pep. You would, therefore, have to sell Pounds 12,000 worth of shares and re-invest the proceeds for yourself and your wife. (Murray Johnstone).
