September 15, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

U.S. OPEN SERVES TENNIS WITH POTENTIAL PROBLEM

The U.S. Open tennis tournament was entertaining at its best, but the magnificent performance of Pete Sampras has served to place a dark cloud on the horizon for this and other tournaments.

His awesome serve was so dominant that it could be the forerunner of a trend to place too high a dividend on that one facet to the detriment of finesse and many other exciting nuances of the game.

Perhaps some thought should be given to raising the net a few inches or shortening the service area to prevent the power play from making a mockery of the game.

BILL KRAUSE

Palm Springs
