BFN [Text] Authorization has been given for the flotation of one of the largest Russian rocket and space firms, the Energiya research and production association [RPA] named after Korolev. In pursuance of a government decision, the Energiya rocket and space corporation named after Korolev is being created from the Energiya RPA and its subsidiary in the town of Primorsk in Leningrad Oblast in the form of an open joint-stock company. Fifty-one percent of the ordinary shares, amounting to 38 percent of the total number of shares issued by the corporation, have been assigned to the federal authorities. Ten percent of the total number of shares are set aside for purchase by the corporation's work force. In accordance with a stipulation from the State Committee for the Management of State Property [Goskomimushchestvo], the organization of a sale of 12 percent of the corporation's shares at a closed check auction is being changed. The Energiya corporation's articles of association should be presented to the Russian Government within a month. Yuriy Semenov, general director of the Energiya RPA, has been appointed president of the corporation until the first shareholders' meeting.
