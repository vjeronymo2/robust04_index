Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Report: "Zhou Nan Denies Rumor That He Will Be Transferred to Beijing"] [Text] Zhou Nan, director of the XINHUA Hong Kong Branch, yesterday clarified the rumor that he was to be transferred to Beijing. According to Zhou Nan, a rumor of this kind appears about once a year and may reappear in the future. He said the principle for coping with it was very simple -- "just ignore it." In addition, Deputy Director Zhang Junsheng said that the news was untrue and inaccurate.
