930224 FT 24 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Multitrust Multitrust, an investment trust which concentrates its portfolio in smaller UK companies and other investment trusts, had a net asset value of 35.25p per share at the end of December. The figure compared with 33.2p at the trust's June year-end and 35.64p at the end of 1991. The value has risen by a further 9 per cent since the latest year-end, according to Mr Andrew Perloff, chairman. Earnings per share for the six months edged ahead to 1.46p (1.45p). The interim dividend is maintained at 1.5p; a final of at least 1.2p is forecast.
