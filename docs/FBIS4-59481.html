BFN [Excerpt] It has been announced today that President Yasir 'Arafat had issued a decision on 10 December 1993 which stipulated the appointment of Major-General (Musbah Hanafi Saqr) director of preventive security. WAFA learned that this decision was not made public at the time to protect the secret presence of brother Major-General Saqr in the occupied Palestinian territories, whereby his martyrdom was announced in 1968 in order to protect the secrecy and continuation of his work.
