BFN [Text] Belgrade, 12 May (TANJUG) -- The criminal charges in the legal proceedings against the 11 members of the French humanitarian organization Premiere Urgence, accused of smuggling arms for the Muslims, will be requalified to their advantage, SRNA has learned from sources close to Radovan Karadzic, president of the republic of Srpska. The entire case will be taken over by the republic of Srpska's civilian judiciary. SRNA claims that the French people will be released from detention as early as next week.
