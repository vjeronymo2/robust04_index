941118 FT 18 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Graseby postpones US flotation Graseby, the instruments group, has postponed indefinitely the flotation of Graseby Andersen in the US. The company blamed weakness in the US environmental market and unfavourable stock market. The costs of Pounds 900,000 will be written off in the 1994 results. The company also announ-ced the sale and leaseback of a property in Florida for Dollars 4.7m (Pounds 2.86m), giving a profit of about Pounds 1.3m. For the nine months to September 30 it reported static sales with increases in medical and product monitoring offsetting falls in technology.
