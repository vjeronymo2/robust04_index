Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Saeima [Latvian Parliament] Foreign Affairs Commission today listened to a report by Martins Virsis, the head of the Latvian delegation for talks with Russia on agreements initialled yesterday. He stressed that the agreement reached was a hard compromise for both sides and it would be rather difficult to achieve its ratification by the parliaments of the two countries. However, irrespective of this the Russian Army withdrawal will be completed by 31 August 1994.
