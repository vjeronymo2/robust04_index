Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 23 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il extended thanks to the medical workers and researchers of different units including the hospital attached to Pyongyang University of Medicine, the Kangwon Provincial People's Hospital, the Wonsan City People's Hospital No. 1, the Hoeyang County People's Hospital, the Songpyong District, Chongjin, people's hospital, the Hoeryong City Sanatorium No. 2 and the Pak Song-un Institute. They gave sincere medical service and assistance to People's Army servicemen and their families. Comrade Kim Chong-il also sent thanks to various industrial establishments including provincial general bureaux of local industry, the Korea General Tobacco Corporation, Pyongyang and Hamhung vinyl chloride footwear factories, Pyongyang children's foodstuff factory, Sunchon leather shoes factory, shoe-making factories in Sinuiju, Kanggye and Hyesan, the Wonsan disabled soldiers' plastic daily necessaries factory, Wonsan and Sinchon tobacco factories and the Kaesong cosmetics production cooperative, which had distinguished themselves in implementing the policy of making revolution in the light industry. They have fulfilled their every monthly and quarterly plans of the national economy, actively contributing to the improvement of the working people's living.
