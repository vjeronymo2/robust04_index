920822 FT 22 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Fleming High net asset value down Net asset value per share at Fleming High Income Investment Trust was 78.1p at July 31, down from 90.2p 12 months earlier and 88.7p at its April year end. Earnings for the three months to July were 1.49p (1.6p) and the first quarter dividend is held at 1.45p.
