Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn has received a cable from Lebanese President Ilyas al-Hirawi in which he condemned the criminal action of assassinating martyr Na'ib 'Umran al-Ma'ayitah, the counselor at the Jordanian Embassy in Beirut. In his cable, President al-Hirawi emphasized that Lebanon condemns violence and is eager to avoid damaging relations between Jordan and sisterly Lebanon or any act against security and stability in Lebanon. President al-Hirawi told His Majesty King Husayn that the competent Lebanese organizations have embarked on the necessary arrangements and measures concerning the criminal incident, and that these organizations were instructed to coordinate with the concerned Jordanian authorities to identify the perpetrators of this criminal action.
