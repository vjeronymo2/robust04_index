Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Head of State Lamine Zeroual is continuing his meetings with the various political parties, national figures, and social and economic organizations. Following the statement issued yesterday by a political party that indicated that the leaders of the dissolved Islamic Salvation Front [FIS] were to participate in the current national dialogue, a source close to the presidency said that this matter was related to a biased interpretation of the content of Zeroual's talks with the various parties that he has received to date. The same source recalled the content of the statement issued by the presidency on 18 March on the eve of the resumption of the national dialogue. [passage omitted] It is worth mentioning that Head of State Lamine Zeroual has met to date with the National Liberation Front [FLN], the Movement for Democracy in Algeria, the Islamic Society Movement-Hamas, the Ennahda Movement, and the Party of Algerian Renewal [PRA].
