BFN [Text] Sevastopol [no date as received] -- A UNIAN correspondent learned today from reliable sources close to the Black Sea Fleet staff that a Black Sea Fleet marine infantry brigade has been brought to a "military danger" state of increased combat readiness. In accordance with this degree of combat readiness, the personnel have been issued with weapons, dressings, and bullet-proof vests and have been confined to barracks. Duties are increased for the purpose of emergency reaction to changes in the operational situation. The declaration of "military danger" in the brigade continues the series of combat preparations. The Black Sea Fleet Command decided even earlier to transfer a tank battalion and a surface to air missile battalion to the brigade in Sevastopol.
