BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 30 (KCNA) - A national meeting of active stockbreeders was held in Pyongyang over April 28-29. Attending the meeting were Politburo member of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] and Premier of the Administration Council Kang Song-san, Secretary of the C.C., the WPK So Kwan-hi and Vice-Premier Kim Chang-chu. The meeting reviewed the achievements and experiences registered in past years in implementing the party's policy of stockbreeding and discussed measures to make a new progress in livestock production in conformity with the demand of the party's revolutionary economic strategy. The meeting dealt with matters of consolidating the material and technical foundations of stockbreeding, raising more domestic animals at workteams and families, placing stockbreeding on a scientific and technical basis and improving veterinary and anti- epizootic work, so as to boost the output of meat and eggs. A letter of pledge to the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il was adopted at the meeting.
