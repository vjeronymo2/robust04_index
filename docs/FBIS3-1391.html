Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg March 28 SAPA -- A spokesman for the SADF's [South African Defense Force] Witwatersrand Command said no army units had been called to Johannesburg's city centre to help contain violence surrounding the Zulu march in the central business district on Monday morning. Maj Christo Visser said soldiers were lending assistance to police in Soweto's Meadowlands where violence had apparently flared up on monday morning.
