BFN [Text] President Akayev received a Japanese delegation led by Naito Masahisa, director general of the international trade policy department of the Ministry of International Trade and Industry on 18 May at Government House. President Akayev thanked the government and people of Japan for the invaluable economic assistance rendered to Kyrgyzstan in this trying time. He said in particular that the Japanese are now looking into the complete reconstruction of Kyrgyzstan's banking system and the building of a complex of medical enterprises. He focused his attention in particular on the humanitarian aid Kyrgyzstan is now receiving. Akayev expressed his hope that Japanese business circles will actively invest in the Kyrgyz economy. The Kyrgyz side will generate all conditions to this effect. In his turn, the head of the Japanese delegation thanked the host for a warm reception and said the specialists who are visiting the Republic will do their best to facilitate an uplift in the Kyrgyz economy. On the same day, the Japanese delegation was received by Prime Minister Apas Dzhumagulov. The meeting was held in a businesslike and constructive atmosphere.
