921119 FT 19 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Gloomy outlook at M Currie European Net asset value of the Martin Currie European Investment Trust was 82.6p at October 31, compared with 83.8p a year earlier and 85.1p at the April 30 year end. Net revenue for the six months to end-October advanced from Pounds 153,000 to Pounds 174,000 for earnings per share of 0.76p (0.66p). Sir Mark Russell, chairman, described short-term prospects as 'unappetising'.
