930113 FT 13 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Wellcome Wellcome, the pharmaceutical group, announced that the number of shares held by BNY (Nominees), which underlie American Depositary Receipts issued by the Bank of New York, had fallen to 50.9m, some 5.9 per cent of the group's share capital. This is a fall from a figure of 58.9m, or 6.8 per cent, in October.
