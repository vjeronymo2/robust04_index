BFN [Text] Israel Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres, who arrived in Ankara today on an official visit, has started to hold contacts. He first visited Motherland Party leader Mesut Yilmaz. Replying to journalists' questions during the meeting, Peres said that with its modern and secular structure, Turkey is exemplary in the Islamic world. Replying to a question, he said that Turkey can assume an effective role in the Middle East peace process. He added that Turkey can participate in the presence to be deployed in Jericho and Gaza. Asked what Israel expects from Turkey, Peres replied: Turkey is the only Muslim country that is friends with both the Arabs and Israel. Consequently, it can constitute a bridge factor. Shim'on Peres then met with Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin for 30 minutes. Cetin later gave a dinner in honor of the visiting foreign minister.
