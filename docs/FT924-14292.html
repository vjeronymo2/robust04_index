921010 FT 10 OCT 92 / Finance & The Family: Halifax launches new account Halifax Building Society is launching a new investment account, Premium Xtra, on Tuesday, offering better returns on deposits of Pounds 10,000 and upwards combined with a reasonable degree of access: the right of one withdrawal of up to Pounds 5000 each month, provided that at least Pounds 10,000 is left in the account. Premium Xtra pays 8.80 per cent gross (6.6 per cent net) on Pounds 10,000, rising by stages to 9.60 per cent gross (7.20 per cent net) on balances of Pounds 100,000 and more.
