931004 FT 04 OCT 93 / International Capital Markets: Russia plans to trade gold bonds RUSSIA plans to start trading gold-backed bonds this week as part of efforts to develop a domestic bond market, Reuter reports from Moscow. A finance ministry official said that bonds worth more than Rbs200bn had already been ordered. Russia's market in government securities is so far limited to three-month Treasury Bills which are traded on the Moscow Interbank Currency Exchange.
