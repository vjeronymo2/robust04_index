921202 FT 02 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Lyons Irish shows 11% rise to IPounds 4.29m Lyons Irish Holdings, the cash-rich Irish tea and coffee wholesaler, has reported an 11.5 per cent increase in pre-tax profits for the 28 weeks ended September 19 1992. The rise was from IPounds 3.85m to IPounds 4.29m, or Pounds 4.68m, on turnover ahead 3 per cent to IPounds 13.5m (IPounds 13.1m). As in previous years, investment income contributed significantly to the result, accounting for IPounds 1.37m (IPounds 1.15m). Earnings per share rose to 12.95p (11.64p) and the interim dividend is lifted to 3.15p (2.85p).
