BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Washington June 23 TASS -- U.S. President Bill Clinton has met chairman of the Russian Government Viktor Chernomyrdin in the White House on Thursday. Chernmomyrdin leads a Russian delegation attending a session of the intergovernment Russo-American commission for economic and technological cooperation. The press was not admitted to the talks. Later in the day, co-chairman of the commission, U.S. Vice- President Albert Gore, and the Russian prime minister are expected to sign a number of agreements on the expansion of cooperation between the two countries in different fields and give a joint news conference.
