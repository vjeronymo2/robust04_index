920630 FT 30 JUN 92 / Observer: Foot-dragging The European Community's internal divisions over the Uruguay Round of GATT trade talks were starkly illustrated in this weekend's different language versions of the Lisbon summit communique. As British and German governments sweat blood to find a compromise ahead of next week's G7 summit in Munich, the English text called for an agreement 'as soon as possible'; the French text spoke of reaching an agreement 'by the end of the year'.
