930624 FT 24 JUN 93 / Hani trial postponed The trial of three right-wing whites accused of murdering South African Communist party leader Chris Hani was postponed yesterday to October 4, Reuter reports from Johannesburg. Mr Hani was shot outside his suburban Johannesburg home on April 10. The killing touched off mass violence in black townships in which about 100 people died. The three are accused of of murder, conspiracy to commit murder and illegal possession of arms.
