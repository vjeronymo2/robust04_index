920204 FT 04 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Welsh Industrial net assets growth At October 5 1991, net asset value of Welsh Industrial Investment Trust had risen to 151p, against 145p six months earlier. Over 60 per cent of assets were represented by government securities and cash, and it was intended to hold to that policy until the recession abated. Earnings per share worked through at 3.99p (3.06p).
