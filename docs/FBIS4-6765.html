BFN ["Message from the Cambodian People's Party to the Sacred King Father"; dated 22 April -- read by announcer] [Text] To His Majesty Norodom Sihanouk Varaman, king of Cambodia. Your revered Majesty: All of us, your children of the Cambodian People's Party [CPP], would like to inform you about our support for your 22 April 1994 statement. This is your finest initiative for the supreme cause of peace and national reconciliation and the interests of your children, grandchildren, and great grandchildren throughout the Kingdom of Cambodia. At all times, we pledge to give heed to Your Majesty's finest initiative. Your Majesty, please, accept our highest respects and loyalty. Please forgive any inappropriateness. [Signed] Chea Sim. [Dated] Phnom Penh, 22 April 1994.
