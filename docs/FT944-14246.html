941024 FT 24 OCT 94 / Company News This Week: Moss Bros - Other Markets Moss Bros: The clothing outfitter is expected to build on last year's growth, with pre-tax profits rising from Pounds 625,000 to between Pounds 1m and Pounds 1.3m in the first half. Analysts will be looking at expansion of the group's Savoy Taylors Guild outlets when the company reports today.
