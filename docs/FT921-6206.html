920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / Somali truce promised Rival Somali warlords were reported yesterday to have agreed to stop fighting in the next 48 hours to allow a United Nations-led peace mission into the Somali capital Mogadishu, Reuter reports from Addis Ababa. Mr Amr Moussa, the Egyptian foreign minister, told the final session of a five-day meeting of the Organisation of African Unity the warring factions had assured UN officials they would halt artillery exchanges to allow the team freedom of movement.
