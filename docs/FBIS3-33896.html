Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, Feb 2 (AFP) -- Israeli and PLO negotiators have not yet set a date for further talks on Palestinian autonomy, PLO chief negotiator Nabil Sha'th said here Wednesday [2 February]. Sha'th denied an earlier Israeli television report that talks would resume in Cairo Wednesday. The two sides are due to meet again to finalise an accord on launching Palestinian self-rule in the Gaza Strip and Jericho, following the meeting in Davos, Switzerland, last weekend between PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat and Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres.
