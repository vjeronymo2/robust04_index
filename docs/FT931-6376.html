930302 FT 02 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Abell inquiry cost DTI Pounds 0.5m THE Department of Trade and Industry's investigation into Mr David Abell, chairman of Suter, the industrial conglomerate, cost the government Pounds 501,000, Mr Neil Hamilton disclosed last night. In a written Commons answer Mr Hamilton, an industry minister, said no proceedings were contemplated against Mr Abell or anyone else as a result of the four-year enquiry into share transactions. The investigation report was published last month.
