930407 FT 07 APR 93 / UK Company News: Headlam acquisition and cash call Headlam Group, the floor coverings and fabrics distribution group, is raising Pounds 5.1m through a 1-for-4 rights issue at 92p to finance part of the cost of its acquisition of Shulman & Sons, a Glasgow-based floor coverings distributor. The group also announced a jump in 1992 pre-tax profits from Pounds 304,000 to Pounds 1.11m, in accordance with FRS 3. Earnings per share came through at 3.85p (2.21p) and the proposed final dividend is 1.85p for an increased total of 2.6p (2.4p). Turnover was Pounds 58.8m (Pounds 21.7m). Consideration for Shulman is about Pounds 2.1m, to be satisfied in a mixture of shares, cash and loan notes. The rights issue, of up to 5.88m new shares, will be underwritten by Robert Fleming and brokers are Albert E Sharp.
