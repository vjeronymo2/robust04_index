BFN [Unattributed article: "Rwanda: Western European Union To Hold Emergency Meeting"] [Text] (ANSA) Brussels, June 17 -- The permanent council of the Western European Union [WEU], the European Union's defence arm, will be meeting here this afternoon to discuss what European contribution can be made to UN peace operations in Rwanda. Diplomatic sources said the meeting had been convened at Italy's and France's request. Yesterday, France said it was prepared to organise its own peace-making force to halt the massacres and protect civilians in Rwanda. Italian diplomats were instructed to ask for an urgent session of the council on UN peace operations and humanitarian actions. The WEU includes all the EU nations except Denmark and Ireland.
