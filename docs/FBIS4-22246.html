Shumeyko, chairman of the Federation Council of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly, on 26 February 1994 in Moscow, No 64-1 SF] [Text] For purposes of ensuring the activity of the deputies of the Federation Council, the Federation Council hereby decrees: 1.
