920310 FT 10 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Small advance at Mucklow to Pounds 5.34m A&J Mucklow, the property and construction group, reported pre-tax profits slightly higher at Pounds 5.34m, against Pounds 5.29m, for the six months to December 31 despite weak industrial and commercial markets. Gross rents were up from Pounds 7.12m to Pounds 7.63m. Turnover was down from Pounds 1.79m to Pounds 1.04m. There is an increased interim dividend of 2.822p (2.56p). Earnings per share came out ahead at 3.81p (3.78p).
