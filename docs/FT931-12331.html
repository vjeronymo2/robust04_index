930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Asset value growth for Derby Trust At the end of December net asset value of Derby Trust capital shares stood at 382p, an 11.4 per cent increase over the 343p reported the year before. In 1992 gross revenue totalled nearly Pounds 3m (Pounds 3.27m). Earnings per share fell from 18.4348p to 16.6346p, and all of those are distributed as dividend with the second interim being 8.4087p.
