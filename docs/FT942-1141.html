940625 FT 25 JUN 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices In general the pepper markets were rather slow this week in the absence of fresh buying of forward positions. The price of white pepper declined marginally to about USDollars 2,775-2,800 a tonne, cif, for August/December shipment from origin. Spot white pepper commanded apremium of some USDollars 150 a tonne, which was regularly paid for smaller lots. Black pepper prices were under little pressure from rpoducer offerings and remained fully steady. Here too smaller lots were traded on the spot market at about USDollars 1,700 a tonne, mainly for the fair average quality grades.
