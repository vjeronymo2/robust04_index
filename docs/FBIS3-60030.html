Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bangkok, Jan 8 (AFP)--Two Americans and a Barbados national were arrested Saturday on charges of possessing and trafficking heroin, police said. The two Americans, identified as Yahya Ali Salim, 41, John Charles Hulten, 29, and Daniel Game Mungah, 28, from Barbados, were arrested in downtown Bangkok when police stopped a taxi they were traveling in. Police said they seized 1.3 kilograms (2.86 pounds) of heroin. In Thailand convicted drug traffickers face death penalty, but the sentence is usually commuted to a lengthy prison term. About two-thirds of the 1,200 foreigners in Thai jails have been jailed for drug offences.
