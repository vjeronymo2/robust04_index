November 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEW COMICS

Eight new comics join The Times' Sunday lineup today. They are "Beetle Bailey," "Blondie," "Hagar the Horrible," "Hi and Lois," "Broom-Hilda," "Marmaduke," "Shoe" and "For Better or Worse." Next Sunday the new lineup will join The Times' regular color comics section.
