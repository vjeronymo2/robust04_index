A428 Lavendon bypass, Bucks; A428 Turvey bypass, Beds; A523 Leek bypass-Rushton Spencer improvement, Staffs; In Cheshire the A556-M6-A559 improvement, and A556 Delamere-Oakmere/Norley Lane-A49; A580 corridor improvement (Liverpool-Manchester); A585 Norcross-M55 link, Lancs.
