Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Foolish Act To Frighten DPRK With Strength" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, March 26 (KCNA) -- U.S. Defence Secretary Perry declared on March 24 that if the United Nations Security Council decided to take "sanctions" against North Korea, the United States would send additional troops and weapons to South Korea, according to a report from Washington. The South Korean paper CHOSUN ILBO March 25 quoted "U.S. military sources" as saying that a plan was being worked out by the U.S. military to hurl 600,000-strong military strength including two marine divisions, more than 200 warships, over 1,600 planes, etc. to the Korean peninsula from the U.S. mainland within 80 days in "case of emergency." Meanwhile, U.S. Deputy Undersecretary of Defence Slocombe said on March 24 that a U.S. carrier task fleet was in "full readiness for action" to cope with "a possible contingency" on the Korean peninsula and the mobilization system of the U.S. imperialist aggression forces in South Korea was under reexamination. Facts show that the U.S. warmaniacs are rendering the situation of the Korean peninsula extremely strained. But their attempt to frighten the Democratic People's Republic of Korea with strength is really a foolish act. Our people and People's Army want peace but are never afraid of war. The U.S. imperialists would be well advised to act with discretion and stop running riot with proper knowledge of Korea. Our people and People's Army, who are united closely around the party and the leader, are keenly watching the military moves of the U.S. imperialists.
