Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Radio Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] In accordance with the obligations undertaken by Russia within its rights as legal successor to the former USSR, the last four of 40 TU-95 MS strategic missile-carrying aircraft based near Semipalatinsk have left Kazakhstan. Some old-model TU-95 aircraft remain in Kazakhstan that experts believe no longer represent a real threat to anyone.
