BFN [Text] Shenzhen, June 17 (XINHUA) -- Estonian President Lennart Meri and his wife toured the international trade building, the industrial exhibition hall and some scenic spots here today. The Estonian guests arrived here from Shanghai yesterday. Shenzhen Mayor Li Youwei hosted a banquet for President Meri and his party on their arrival. He briefed Meri on Shenzhen's development in the past 14 years and extended a welcome for Estonian investment here. Meri expressed congratulations on Shenzhen's achievements. Estonia hopes to conduct economic and trade cooperation with Shenzhen, he told Li.
