Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Sevastopol, 15 Feb -- Citizens of Crimea should undergo military service on the territory of the Republic of Crimea. Yuriy Meshkov, president of the republic of Crimea, has sent this instruction addressed to the command of Ukraine's Navy. With regard to this, representatives of Ukraine's navy have stated that they are subordinate to Ukraine's Ministry of Defense and to Leonid Kravchuk, Ukraine's president and commander of the Ukraine armed forces. In their opinion, the president of the Republic of Crimea does not have jurisdiction over the command of Ukraine's Navy.
