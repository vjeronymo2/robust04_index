BFN [Text] Asyut, 11 May (MENA)--Asyut Security forces have carried out several security raids over the past few days during which they seized weapons and ammunition of various types from Muhammadi Jalal, the former chairman of Sidfa's Local Council. Included in the weapons seized were two Russian-made machine guns, an automatic rifle, a Lee Enfield rifle, another Russian firearm, and ammunition of various calibers. Jalal, who currently works as a real estate agent in Cairo's al- Salam District, was remanded for four days for interrogation by Muhammad Shakir, Sidfa's chief prosecutor.
