Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Former Tehiya Knesset Member Elyaqim Ha'etzni says the YESHA' [Hebrew acronym for Judaea, Samaria, and Gaza] settlers council has received intelligence information that Foreign Minister Peres made verbal promises to Yasir 'Arafat during the Oslo talks to admit 300,000 Palestinian refugees and to start the process of dismantling the settlements. The YESHA' Council yesterday decided to draw up a list of volunteers who will be available at short notice to physically resist the evacuation of settlements. The move followed comments by Foreign Minister Peres questioning the viability of the Gaza Strip settlement of Netzarim. [passage omitted]
