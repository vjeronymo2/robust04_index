CSO [Summary] In 1993 gross output of grain in Jiangxi Province totaled 30.34 billion jin, an increase of 1.34 billion jin over the assigned task; cotton 3,124,000 dan, a 5.3 percent increase; oil crops 778,100 tons, a 4.9 percent increase; 18,110,000 hogs were removed from stock, a 15.2 percent increase; and aquatic products output 554,900 tons, a 34.3 percent increase. Per capita net income of farmers was 870 yuan, a net increase of 102 yuan over 1992. Planned grain output in 1994 is 30 billion jin. The planned cotton area is 2.6 million mu, an increase of 600,000 mu over 1993, and cotton output 4 million dan. Planned aquatic products output is 650,000 tons, and per capita farmer income will reach 1000 yuan.
