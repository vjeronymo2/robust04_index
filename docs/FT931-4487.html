930310 FT 10 MAR 93 / UK Company News in Brief UNICHEM has acquired, through its E Moss retail arm, two independent pharmacies for a maximum Pounds 1.03m, including Pounds 600,000 cash. Combined turnover of Richard Wake in South Shields and Smith & Luckhurst in Irvine amounts to Pounds 1.18m and nets assets Pounds 350,000.
