Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Radio Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] Army General Pavel Grachev, Russian defense minister, has made the decision to reorganize certain structures in the Defense Ministry. The main directorate for work with personnel has been reorganized into the directorate for indoctrination and the main directorate for training and placement of cadres is to become the main directorate for personnel. The reorganization of these bodies is being undertaken to improve indoctrination, combat, and moral-psychological training and military education in the Russian Armed Forces.
