BFN [Text] The prime minister and first vice president, Honorable John Malecela, has thanked Britain for assisting Tanzania in the field of health in order to cope with the influx of refugees from Rwanda. Honorable Malecela paid the tribute after receiving a report on that country's assistance to disaster stricken countries of eastern and central Africa. According to the report, Britain will provide a grant of 25,000 pounds sterling to the African Medical Research Foundation, AMREF, two nurses, and logistical support to (Morugwanza) Hospital in Ngara District, Kagera region for three months.
