921126 FT 26 NOV 92 / UK Company News: City of London PR improves Profits of City of London PR Group, the USM-quoted investor relations and market research specialist, edged ahead from Pounds 281,000 to Pounds 292,000 pre-tax for the six months ended September 30. Turnover of Pounds 1.54m compared with Pounds 929,000 previously. Operating profits recovered by 33 per cent to Pounds 121,000 in spite of absorbing a Pounds 36,000 loss incurred by the Paris market research operation. Earnings rose to 2.75p (2.48p) and the interim dividend is increased to 1.15p (1.04p). The directors are forecasting a 10 per cent increase in the final to 2.354p.
