940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Aerostructures Hamble shares end first day of trading up 1/2p Shares in Aerostructures Hamble Holdings, the former British Aerospace aircraft components subsidiary, closed 1/2 p above the 120p issue price on the first day of dealings yesterday. Last month the group offered 33.33m ordinary shares, 20.8m placed with institutions and the remainder subject to clawback to meet retail demand through intermediaries. The Pounds 26.5m raised through the issue of 23.33m new shares will be used alongside a new revolving credit facility of Pounds 10m to repay Pounds 35m of debt incurred during the Pounds 47.6m management buy-out in 1990.
