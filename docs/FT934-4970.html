931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Metrotect falls to Pounds 559,000 Shares of Metrotect Industries fell 24p to 130p yesterday on news of a 27 per cent decline in interim pre-tax profits to Pounds 559,000. The company, which came to the market in June, makes and supplies pipeline protection products. After a first half that began slowly due to delays in project commencements the directors anticipated a 'very busy' second six months. Turnover slipped to Pounds 8.28m (Pounds 9.6m). Interest charges accounted for Pounds 118,000 (Pounds 276,000) and tax for Pounds 188,000 (Pounds 256,000). Earnings emerged at 1.49p (2.4p) and an interim dividend of 1.15p is declared.
