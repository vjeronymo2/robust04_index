930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / Evidence from SE chief sought SIR Andrew Hugh Smith, the chairman of the Stock Exchange, is to be invited to give evidence to the cross-party Commons employment committee on exchange rules which prevent companies alerting officials about planned large redundancies before informing the market. It is investigating whether the Department of Employment and the 83 Training and Enterprise Councils in England and Wales can be more effectively involved in managing large redundancies.
