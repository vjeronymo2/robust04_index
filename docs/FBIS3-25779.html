Language: English Article Type:BFN [By Debbie Kuo] [Text] Taipei, Jan. 28 (CNA) -- Huang Yen-Chao, director-general of the Board of Foreign Trade [BOFT], left Friday [28 January] for Jakarta to attend a senior officials meeting of the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation [APEC] forum. The meeting begins Monday and ends Friday. BOFT Deputy Director-General Lin Yi-fu is accompanying Huang. The meeting is the first ever of APEC senior officials, and is also the first in a series of APEC meeting to be hosted by Indonesia this year, Huang said at the airport. The other meetings will be held in May, September and November. The November meeting will probably be a ministerial or even presidential meeting, Huang said. Among the discussion topics at next week's meeting will be a joint proposal by Taiwan and the United States that APEC develop guidelines on small- and medium-business development cooperation, Huang said. The APEC Investment and Trade Committee, established at the November APEC meeting in Seattle, will also convene for the first time in Jakarta, he added.
