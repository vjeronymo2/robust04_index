920623 FT 23 JUN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There was a strong and more widespread demand this week, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Landed all bright liquoring East African met improved competition at dearer rates. The better medium teas were also well supported and prices were often appreciably higher. Offshore good demand in the cif section with price levels about steady. Fob teas from Mauritius were mostly easier where sold while secondary grades were neglected. The highest price realised this week was 202p for a Kenya pd. Quotations: quality 165p/kgnom good medium 142p medium 108p low medium 85p.
