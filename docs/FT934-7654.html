931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / Armenia to issue its own currency Armenia, one of the smallest and poorest of the 12 members of the Commonwealth of Independent States, says it will issue its own currency, the dram, leaving Belarus and Tajikistan as the only republics committed to keeping the Russian rouble as their own currency, writes Leyla Boulton from Moscow. Armenia will receive financial support from the IMF for the switch.
