Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan 2, IRNA -- An official of Iran's Foreign Ministry has denied the allegation that the Islamic Republic of Iran has had any contact with November 17th group, a terrorist group in Greece. Director General of the Department of Western European Affairs at the Foreign Ministery, Ali Ahani, today denied the Italian daily CORRIERRE DELLA SERA's recent report on Iran's alleged contacts with certain members of a Greek terrorist group known as "November 17th". Ahani made the remark in a meeting with Greek charge d'affaires to Tehran, Ioannis Pediotis. The two sides also discussed expansion of Tehran-Athens relations.
