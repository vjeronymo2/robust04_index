BFN [Text] Beijing, April 14 (XINHUA) -- President Jiang Zemin had a 20-minutes meeting with Lord Young, chairman of the Cable and Wireless and president of the Hong Kong Telecommunications Ltd., here this afternoon. Young, who arrived here at noon today, is scheduled to sign a contract tomorrow on launching the Sino-British Sub-Marine System Company Ltd. with the telecommunications administration under the Chinese Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications. Present at the meeting were Wu Jichuan, Chinese minister of posts and telecommunications, and Chen Ziying, deputy director of the Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office under the State Council.
