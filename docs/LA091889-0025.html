September 18, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

SAMMY DAVIS JR. CONCERT IN ANAHEIM CANCELED

Sammy Davis Jr.'s concert scheduled for Sunday at the Celebrity Theatre in Anaheim has been canceled due to the singer's treatment for throat cancer. Ticket refunds are available at point of purchase, though a spokesman for the theater said that it is hoped the show will be rescheduled. If so, tickets bought for the canceled show will be honored on the new date.
