Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin, visiting Rome, has expressed his willingness in principle to withdraw Israeli troops from the Golan Heights within a global framework of security and guarantees for the Jewish state. Earlier Rabin was received by John Paul II and had met Prime Minister Carlo Azeglio Ciampi. Commenting on his meeting with the Pope, Rabin stressed that he had not asked the Vatican to take any diplomatic initiatives. He added, however, that the Holy See would exert moral pressure for the resumption of peace talks.
