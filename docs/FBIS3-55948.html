Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [RIA untitled report under the "In Brief" rubric] [Text] Moscow -- The first session of the Russian Federation Security Council's interdepartmental commission on economic security took place in Moscow yesterday. Andrey Zverev, head of the economic department of the Russian Federation Government staff, was appointed chairman of the commission by a decree of Russian President Boris Yeltsin. The session also examined questions connected with the elaboration of an economic security state doctrine, and the progress of economic reform in the country among other problems. Taking part in the session were the heads of ministries and agencies represented on the commission.
