September 12, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

NATION;

WACKY WOOING EARNS $237 FINE

Max Hedglin is anything but a wallflower when it comes to expressing his feelings.

He hired a helicopter to drop 2,500 carnations and 10,000 love letters on the lawn of his former girlfriend.

Unfortunately for Hedglin, 38, who owns a convenience store in nearby Greenville, the $3,000 tribute on Saturday failed to take root with Bonnie Adams. But then, a week earlier she had married City Councilman Mike Adams.

Authorities charged Hedglin with littering. On Tuesday, he pulled up outside the Police Department in a stretch limo and paid a $237 bond.
