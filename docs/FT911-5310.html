910415 FT 15 APR 91 / International Company News: Olives Hldgs falls Taxable profits at Olives Holdings, the paper making and property development and general investment group, fell from Pounds 1.49m to Pounds 854,000 in the year to December 31. However this was struck after an exceptional credit of Pounds 1.22m (debit Pounds 621,000) relating to the disposal of a holding in a subsidiary, and after losses of Pounds 305,000 from the company's 40 per cent interest in Continental Paper, an associated undertaking. Continental also accounted for an extraordinary charge of Pounds 218,000 (nil). Turnover declined to Pounds 9.82m (Pounds 13.73m) and earnings dropped from 12.98p to 9.06p per share. As Continental is now showing signs of profit, the directors are holding the final dividend at 6p for a 9p total (same).
