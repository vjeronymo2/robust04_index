BFN [Text] Citizens in the rest of the country say ECOMOG [Economic Community of West African States Cease-Fire Monitoring Group] and United Nations Observer Mission in Liberia [UNOMIL] are responsible for the slow pace in the Liberian peace process. In a survey conducted by LCN [Liberia Communications Network] News in Gbarnga, the citizens said, although fighting is taking place between the so-called Liberian Peace Council and the NPFL [National Patriotic Front of Liberia] in the southeast of the country, ECOMOG and UNOMIL have not moved in to stop the fighting.
