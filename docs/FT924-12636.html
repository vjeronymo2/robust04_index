921020 FT 20 OCT 92 / Conflict threat over legal aid THE Law Society yesterday appeared to be heading for a conflict with the government following the publication of proposals for the introduction of legal aid franchising in England and Wales. The Legal Aid Board hopes to agree the final proposals in time to process applications for franchises from July next year. The Law Society, the solicitors' governing body, says the transaction criteria are almost completely untested, so 'it would be intolerable to insist solicitors comply with them'.
