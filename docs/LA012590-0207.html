January 25, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

FAX CUT: Will serve-yourself fax machines in post offices around Orange County (D11) touch off a local price war? The Postal Service is planning to charge $8.50 to send six pages. . . . Sending the same pages via storefront facsimile services costs an average of $16. . . . But local fax managers don't fear the competition. "Faxing is all about convenience," said one, "and post offices are not always the handiest places in the world." Pinsky and Steve Emmons
