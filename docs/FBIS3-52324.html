Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn this evening received at Raghadan Palace Qatar's Foreign Minister Shaykh Hamad Bin-Jasim Bin-Jabr Al Thani, in the presence of His Highness Crown Prince Hasan. During the meeting, the two sides exchanged views on various regional and international issues and relations between the two fraternal countries and means to reinforce and develop these relations in various fields. Moreover, they stressed the need to achieve Arab solidarity and to consolidate efforts in this field to serve the one Arab nation's issues and the future of its generations. The two sides also reviewed the latest developments in the peace process and the need to achieve a just and comprehensive peace in the region. Attending from the Jordanian side were Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, the prime minister; al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, chief of the Royal Hashemite Court; Marwan al-Qasim, his majesty's political adviser; Talal al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs; and Tirad al-Fayiz, Jordan's ambassador to Qatar. Attending from the Qatari side were members of the accompanying delegation and Nasir 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Nasr, Qatar's ambassador in Amman. His Majesty King Husayn awarded the Qatari foreign minister the al-Kawkab medal, first class. The guest minister, the accompanying delegation, and those attending had dinner with His Majesty King Husayn.
