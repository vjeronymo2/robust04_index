BFN [Text] Moscow April 6 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin signed a decree on Wednesday obliging the government to submit a draft law on the basic principles of obligatory insurance in Russia to the State Duma by September 1, 1994. The draft law will have to define the concept of obligatory insurance and its goals. The cabinet has also been instructed to prepare and submit draft laws on the procedure for and terms of specific forms of obligatory insurance. The move aims at ensuring civil liberties and rights granted to the people of Russia by the Constitution, the presidential press service said.
