930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / UK Company News: European Assets European Assets Trust, quoted on both the Amsterdam and London markets, had a net asset value of FL 8.31 (94p) at June 30, up from Fl 7.07 at the same stage of 1992. The trust, also advised by Ivory & Sime, reported net income for the half year to end-June of Fl 2.83m (Fl 3.49m) for earnings of Fl 0.11 (Fl 0.14) per share. The interim dividend is maintained at Fl 0.08.
