940806 FT 06 AUG 94 / Finance and the Family: More smaller companies cheer - At a glance It has been another good week for UK smaller companies. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) climbed 1.3 per cent to 1,673.74 over the week to August 4. The index is down by 0.8 per cent since the start of the year but compares relatively well to the FT-SE-A All-share index, which is down by 4 per cent.
