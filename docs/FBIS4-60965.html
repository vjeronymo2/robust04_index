BFN [Excerpts] As has already been reported, a summit of the Presidents of Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, and Uzbekistan took place in Karakol. Such summits have become traditional. [passage omitted] The main result of this meeting is Kyrgyzstan's joining the agreement on a common economic space between Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan. Now this is a tripartite agreement. [passage omitted] The presidents said that this agreement represents a new level of economic integration of the Central Asian states. There will be no obstacles, such as customs barriers, double taxation. Currency exchange points will be opened. Steps taken by the governments to improve goods turnover and goods exchange as well as regulation of labor inflow and outflow between the states will positively influence the economic situation in the region. Further enhancement of economic cooperation will lead to a better mechanism, which will facilitate constructive interaction and help resolve a number of political problems that are common in the region as well. It concerns efficient, mutually beneficial and peaceful co-habitation of different ethnic groups in Central Asia, which will become a stabilizing factor of the ethnic situation in the region. The participants of the meeting expressed their readiness to continue their search for new forms of interstate cooperation in the interests of the three nations. Within the framework of the CIS summit decisions to render help to Kyrgyzstan, the presidents discussed concrete measures to be taken in order to eliminate damage inflicted on the Kyrgyz economy by recent mud slides. The presidents expressed their satisfaction with the fruitful talks and their hope for these talks to further stimulate friendship and cooperation in the region. [words indistinct]
