March 29, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Direct Flights to Vietnam: Air Vietnam and Can-Vietnam International Charter Airlines Inc. have signed an agreement to open direct service between Vietnam and Canada, a news report said. The once-a-week flights between Ho Chi Minh City and Vancouver will begin in June, the official Vietnam News Agency said. More than 250,000 Vietnamese live in Canada, said the report, which was monitored in Bangkok.
