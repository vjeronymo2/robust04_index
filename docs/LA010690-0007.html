January 6, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

GORBACHEV AND CHANGES IN EAST BLOC

Neo-Prussianism should be spotlighted as much as neo-Nazism ("East Germans Warned of Neo-Nazism," Part A, Dec. 28).

Already East Germans are demanding return of parts of former Prussia now in Poland.

Union with the European Community at Strasbourg is the road to the future. Reunion of the Germanys through the Brandenburg Gate is the road back to a bloodstained past.

RICHARD CASTELAR SIMONSON

Washington, D.C.
