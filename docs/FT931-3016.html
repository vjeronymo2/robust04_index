930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / The Budget (Government Statements): Cash accounting The turnover limit for companies using the cash accounting scheme is being raised from Pounds 300,000 to Pounds 350,000 a year from 1 April. Companies joining the scheme will no longer be required to make a written application to Customs and Excise. A consultation document has been issued inviting comments on how the operation of the scheme could be made more attractive to businesses and simpler to use.
