BFN [Text] Tehran, April 27, IRNA -- A Persian daily Wednesday [27 April] lauded the heroic resistance of Muslims in Gorazde and said such a withstanding heralds a bright prospect for Islam in the heart of Europe. The daily `JOMHURI-YE ESLAMI' urged Muslim nations and governments to feel responsible more than ever and assist the resisting Bosnian Muslims through any possible means. The daily called on hajj pilgrims to raise the issue of the oppression of the Bosnian Muslims by the Serbs during the annual hajj rituals. It hailed the measures taken by certain Islamic countries, especially Iran, in recent days to pressure the UN and supporters of the Serbs in abating the Serbs crimes in Gorazde. This is indicative of the fact that both Muslim nations and governments own potentials that, if used properly and on time, can prevent numerous crimes that the arrogant front is committing against Muslims in various parts of the world. The editorial in conclusion called on hajj pilgrims to try to find suitable and practical ways for offering economic, political, military and psychological assistnce to the Bosnian Muslims.
