941126 FT 26 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Cleveland Trust Cleveland Trust, the industrial property investment concern, increased pre-tax profits from Pounds 60,000 to Pounds 382,000 for the six months to September 30. Turnover grew to Pounds 911,000 (Pounds 393,000) and earnings per share came to 2.5p (1.7p). The interim dividend is 2.4p (nil).
