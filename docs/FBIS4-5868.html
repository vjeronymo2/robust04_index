BFN [Text] Tokyo, April 15 KYODO -- A Canadian subsidiary of Nissan Motor Co. will take delivery of U.S.-built pickup trucks from Nissan Motor Manufacturing Corp. U.S.A. (NMMC) late this month, according to Nissan motor officials. It will be the first time Nissan has exported U.S.-built pickup trucks to Canada. NMMC is a U.S. manufacturing arm of the Japanese automaker. Nissan said some 3,300 trucks will be shipped to Canada from NMMC in the business year that started on April 1.
