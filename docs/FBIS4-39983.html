BFN [Text] The authorized strength of the Russian Armed Forces would be 1.9 million by 1 October 1994, Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev told Interfax. At present, the authorized strength of the Russian Armed Forces is 2.2 million. Grachev noted that it was not only the rank-and-file and officer corps which were to be cut -- general officers were to be cut, too. He said that 270 generals' posts would be cut by the end of the year.
