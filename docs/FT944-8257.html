941121 FT 21 NOV 94 / Company News (This Week): Tate & Lyle - Other companies The sugar producer expects on Wednesday to report pre-tax profits for the year ended September of around Pounds 265m, against Pounds 223.5m a year earlier. A recovery in cereal sweeteners and starches will be the main factor. AE Staley, its US subsidiary, will likely report record profits. An increase of 1p in the full-year dividend to 14p is expected.
