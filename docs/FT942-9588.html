940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / Finance and the Family: ProShare conference plan - At a glance ProShare, the organisation which aims to promote deeper and wider share ownership, is staging a conference with ShareLink for investment clubs and private investors. The conference will be held on Saturday July 16 at the International Convention Centre in Birmingham. Tickets cost Pounds 30 plus Vat (Pounds 25 for ProShare members or ShareLink customers). Details: 021-200 7755.
