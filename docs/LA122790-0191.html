December 27, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: SCIENCE / TECHNOLOGY

Making a Mark: TouchStone Software Corp., the Huntington Beach maker of diagnostic programs for personal computers, said it has signed an agreement with software maker Markt & Technik AG to publish TouchStone's CheckIt software in Germany.

Under the publishing agreement, CheckIt 3.0, a program that allows PC users to check the performance of a computer, will be translated into German language versions and sold by M & T in Germany and Austria.
