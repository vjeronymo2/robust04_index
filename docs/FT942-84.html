940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Sharp setback for Falcon Holdings Falcon Holdings, the valve and pipeline equipment distributor, reported a 41 per cent decline in pre-tax profits during the 12 months to March 31. On turnover 6 per cent lower at Pounds 6.69m, the pre-tax line dropped from Pounds 612,000 to Pounds 360,000. A recommended final distribution of 2p maintains the total for the year at 5p, covered 2.3 times by earnings per share of 11.4p (15.6p).
