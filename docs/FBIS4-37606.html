BFN [Text] Saudi Foreign Minister Sa'ud al-Faysal Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud arrived in Morocco this evening carrying a letter from the custodian of the two holy mosques, King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al-Sa'ud, to his brother King Hassan II. The Saudi minister made the following statement on arrival at King Mohamed V International Airport: [Al-Faysal] In fact I am carrying a message from the Custodian of the Two Holy Mosques to His Majesty King Hassan II. As the practice has always been between the two leaders, consultation on all issues related to Arab issues becomes a duty, particularly when important issues are raised in the Arab arena. I will, God willing, be honored by meeting His Majesty the King to convey this message to him. I hope that the efforts of our two leaders in both countries play an influential role in keeping the peace and the security of our Arab nation and our two brotherly peoples.
