940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Bourne End sells six properties Bourne End Properties has secured the sale of six properties, in three separate transactions, for a total of Pounds 3.57m. The largest deal was a portfolio of four Magnet units in Crawley, West Sussex, Banbury, Oxon, Newport in Gwent, and Barnsley, South Yorkshire - sold to Johnson Fry's Income Booster Fund for Pounds 3m. The freehold interest of a supermarket in Gidea Park, Essex, was sold for Pounds 375,000.
