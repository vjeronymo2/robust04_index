March 13, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

SURE SIGN LOOKS FOR THIRD WIN

Sure Sign will be looking for her third win of the year when she takes on a tough field of seven in the $3,600 distaff pacing feature Wednesday night at Los Alamitos.

The eighth race main event is a claiming handicap with the prices ranging from $6,000 to $8,000. Starlitas, Palladay Road, Sassy Sapphire and Call Holding receive an age allowance and race with a claiming price of $7,500.

Sure Sign will race for a claiming tag of $8,000 and thus must start from the outside.
