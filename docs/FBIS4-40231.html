BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 15 Jun -- Mikhail Osipov, commander of the 191st company of the 201st Motorized Infantry Regiment of the collective peacekeeping forces, was killed on Tuesday [14 June] in the Tajik town of Kurgan-Tyube (the administrative centre of Khatlon region). ITAR-TASS learned at the press centre of the joint command of the collective peacekeeping forces that the 30-year-old lieutenant was shot at about 23 hours local time (2200 hours Moscow time) from an automatic weapon by unidentified persons while checking on a street patrol. The motives of the murder are being established. An investigation into the circumstances of the crime has begun.
