941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Talks over BS expected today Talks are expected today in an attempt to resolve the uncertainty over the management of BS Group, the property and leisure company, writes Roland Adburgham. Scotts Holdings, a Singapore-based property group, has agreed to buy a 26.97 per cent stake from three directors, all members of the Kerman family, for Pounds 3.1m. As part of the agreement, Scotts seeks to replace the Kermans on the board. Sir Ian Rankin, BS chairman, opposed the sale of the entire Kerman stake to Scotts and there is speculation that he might be asked to resign.
