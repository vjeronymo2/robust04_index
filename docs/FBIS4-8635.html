BFN [Text] Belgrade, 14 Apr (TANJUG) -- Yugoslav Foreign Minister Vladislav Jovanovic left Belgrade today on a working visit to Greece at the invitation of Greek Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias. The Federal Ministry for Foreign Relations announced that in talks with the foreign minister and other Greek officials, Minister Jovanovic will examine the resolution of the crisis on the territory of former Yugoslavia, the civil war in Bosnia-Herzegovina, the continuation of the peace process, and the main issues of bilateral relations.
