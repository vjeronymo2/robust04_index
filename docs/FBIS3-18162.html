Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stockholm, Feb 27 (AFP) -- The number of Swedes in favour of European Union [EU] membership is growing, according to a new Temo public opinion poll published Sunday [27 February] as Sweden's EU membership negotiations drew to a close in Brussels. According to the poll, which questioned 1,000 people in January and February, 35 percent were in favour of joining the EU, 42 percent were opposed and 23 percent were undecided. The same poll conducted last September showed 29 percent in favour, 45 percent opposed and 26 percent undecided.
