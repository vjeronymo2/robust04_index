May 20, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

IT'S IN THE MALE

My daughter-in-law and I both said, "How true! How true!" when we read "It's in the Male."

One could pursue further the author's husband's statement, "A man's ego is quite large. But it's very delicate." I am thinking of writing a book titled "Fathers, Husbands and Sons: Walking on Eggshells in Three Generations."

JACKIE WILLIAMS, Huntington Beach
