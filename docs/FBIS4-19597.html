BFN [Text] Paris, April 18 (AFP) -- French President Francois Mitterrand urged Monday [18 April] UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali to revive peace talks on Bosnia as soon as possible, a statement from the president's office said. It said Mitterrand, who discussed the current Bosnian crisis with U.S. President Bill Clinton, had sent a message to the U.S. leader as well as to Russian President Boris Yeltsin, Butrus-Ghali and to current European Union President Andreas Papandreou, the premier of Greece.
