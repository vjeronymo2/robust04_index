December 2, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ALTHOUGH IT'S LEGAL, ALCOHOL IS A DRUG

On Nov. 18, the headline ran: "Alcohol -- Not Drugs -- Detected in Tarpley."

Please inform your headline writer, copy editor, the sports staff and the general public that alcohol is a drug. Period.

Alcohol is a drug. Alcohol is a legal drug. Alcohol is the most used and abused drug in our nation.

HUGH M. STEVENSON, Los Angeles
