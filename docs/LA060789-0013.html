June 7, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: EXIT LINES

Actor-comedian Robin Williams, in a two-part interview to be broadcast Thursday and Friday on ABC's "Good Morning America," says comedy is a great shield. "It's a great way to keep people out. Comedy is a defense and also a weapon. You can use it to decimate people and to hold people back, to defuse them . . . if they laugh, they won't hurt you." ALEENE MacMINN
