BFN [Text] As BELAPAN has learned, the State Committee for Statistics and Analysis has disseminated among the top echelons of power a document providing the main indicators of the Belarusian economy in January-February 1994. According to the document, the GDP decreased by 33 percent compared to the corresponding period last year. In particular, production fell by 35.5 percent in industry, in construction works -- 29 percent, and in the agricultural sector -- 9 percent.
