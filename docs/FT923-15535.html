920701 FT 01 JUL 92 / International Company News: McDonald's woos the small investor McDonald's, the world's leading fast-food company, is offering an innovative programme to attract and retain small investors, writes Barbara Durr. Under the programme, shareholders who own less than 50 shares will be able to purchase additional shares, sell them or donate their shares to selected charities without paying brokerage commissions. The initial offering period for the programme will be 30 days. McDonald's will also match donations equivalent to the value of all shares donated, up to a combined maximum of Dollars 100,000. At the end of 1991, McDonald's had 372,000 shareholders.
