BFN [Text] Joe Clark, the UN secretary general's special representative in Cyprus, arrived this afternoon and will meet President Glavkos Kliridhis tonight. In a statement after his arrival, Clark said he wants to discuss with the leaders of the two communities the current state of the confidence-building measures and other fundamental issues. Clark was answering a question on the contents of his mission, given that he is not carrying any letters from UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali.
