July 24, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

JOYNER NAMED ANGEL OF WEEK

Wally Joyner, who had three game-winning hits and one game-winning run batted in on a walk last week, is The Times' Orange County Edition Angel of the Week.

Joyner had two home runs and nine RBIs during the week and batted .448 with 13 hits in 29 at-bats.

The Times will donate $200 in Joyner's name to the Boys and Girls Clubs of Orange County.
