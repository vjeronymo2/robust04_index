BFN [Text] "On the matters which were the subject of your letter (foreign policy, the unity of the country and the defense of the principles of social solidarity), I intend to exercise without reservation the powers and responsibility of the coordination and guidance of the executive which the Constitution entrusts to the prime minister of the Republic." This was how Silvio Berlusconi replied to a letter sent to him by President Oscar Luigi Scalfaro (the president's office released details of both letters). "Furthermore I assure you," Berlusconi writes, "that none of the persons who are being considered for government positions have principles contrary to those to which you drew my attention."
