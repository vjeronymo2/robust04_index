July 13, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

During the Nebraska State Junior Championships at Omaha's York Country Club, 16-year-old Derek Nannen made two holes-in-one and an eagle in a span of six holes.

The first hole-in-one came on the 11th hole, a 153-yard par-three. On the next hole, a 497-yard par-five, Nannen made an eagle 3 when he holed his third shot from about 80 yards. The second ace came on the par-three, 135-yard 16th hole. Nannen finished the day with a par 70.
