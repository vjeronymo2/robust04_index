941031 FT 31 OCT 94 / Company News (This Week): Den norske Bank - Other Companies Norway's biggest commercial bank will report nine-month figures tomorrow followed on Wednesday by Christiania Bank and on Friday by Uni Storebrand, the country's largest insurer. Results are expected to reflect continued improvement of the economy but could be hit by bond losses. Also due tomorrow is Hafslund Nycomed, the pharmaceuticals group.
