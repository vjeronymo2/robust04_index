BFN [Text] The Press Center of the Bulgarian Socialist Party [BSP] has announced that Zhan Videnov, chairman of the BSP Supreme Council, who is a member of the Consultative Council on National Security, has sent a letter to President Zhelyu Zhelev in which he sharply protests the fact that the problem of the transshipment of the UN Protection Forces equipment has not been discussed in the Consultative Council of National Security. The letter points out that the president has once again proven his intention to turn the council into a declarative organ or a curtain behind which machinations are conducted. Similar actions cannot be described as a manifestation of concern with the country's security, the protest letter of Zhan Videnov to the president of the Republic points out.
