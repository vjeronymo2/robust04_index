Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] A Vietnamese delegation led by Hoang Duc Nghi, minister and chairman of the State Commission for Ethnic Minorities and Mountain Region of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, left Yangon [Rangoon] by air at 1345 today. The delegation was seen off at the airport by Foreign Affairs Minister U Ohn Gyaw, a high ranking official from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and the Ministry of Progress of Border Areas and National Races and Development Affairs, and the charge d'affaires of the Vietnamese Embassy.
