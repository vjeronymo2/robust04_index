BFN [Excerpt] Baghdad, 19 Jun (INA)--Sa'd Qasim Hammudi, secretary general of the Arab Popular Forces Conference [APFC], today received the visiting Yemeni House of Representatives' delegation led by Muhammad 'Abdallah al-Kibsi. In a speech he delivered, Sa'd Qasim Hammudi reiterated that all nationalist and pan-Arab forces in the Arab homeland support Yemen's unity and it constitutional legitimacy against secessionist colonialist attempts, which are backed by reactionaries and are aimed at disrupting Yemen's unity, which is a pan-Arab accomplishment that has been made through huge sacrifices. He added that Yemen's unity constitutes a strength for the Arab nation in its confrontation of the evil, hostile, and reactionary forces threatening the nation's present and future. [passage omitted]
