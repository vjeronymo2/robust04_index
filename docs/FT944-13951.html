941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa weaker South African equities were generally weaker, although the underlying sentiment remained steady. Gold stocks were marked down on the softer bullion price and amid a lack of clear direction in local currencies. The overall index lost 24 to 5,751, the industrial index slipped 21 to 6,603 after reversing earlier gains and the gold index shed 31 to 2,319. De Beers lost R1 to R100.50, and JCI slipped R3 to R107. Remgro shed 60 cents to R26.25, Barlows lost 50 cents to R31.25, SAB was 50 cents softer at R92.75. Vaal Reefs was R5 softer at R430 and Dries shed 25 cents to R66.75. Gencor lost 10 cents after Friday's 60 cent gain, closing at R15.00, on news that its former chairman and the former government finance minister, Mr David Keys, was to become chairman of its new acquisition Billiton International.
