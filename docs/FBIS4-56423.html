BFN [Text] At the International Small Island States Conference being held in Barbados, the Caribbean group of Non-Governmental Organizations [NGO] has advocated promoting alternative development strategies as well as environmental education. The proposals of the Caribbean organizations include giving nongovernmental groups a voice in the decision-making process regarding sustainable development, and the ratification of Accord 169 of the International Labor Organization on the rights of indigenous peoples. Other proposals include restructuring military budgets to allocate those resources to social services and environmental protection. The Caribbean group of Non-Governmental Organizations, which is holding sessions parallel to the Small Island State Conference, by consensus appointed the Cuban delegate as its spokesman.
