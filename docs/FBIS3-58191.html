Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The government completely rules out any form of secret diplomacy on the issue of Skopje, but it is leaving the door open for a dialogue in case the Greek conditions are satisfied. The government spokesman yesterday repeated that there are certain issues which are obviously open to negotiation and certain other issues which are not. The issue of the name is one of the issues not open to negotiation. He also said that "the government does not accept secret diplomacy." [passage omitted]
