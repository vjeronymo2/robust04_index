November 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

PROPOSITION 103 STILL ON HOLD

Let's tell it like it really is. The insurance rate conundrum in California would not require Prop. 103 or any other type of voter revolt if our legislators would simply require the DMV to "tie" vehicle registration to proof of insurance.

If the DMV can track a smog check and require proof of the check before issuing a car registration, certainly the same can be done for proof of insurance. This would take all of those uninsured motorists off the road and would curb the losses to the insurance carriers which, in turn, would lower our premiums.

DIANE GRAY

Beverly Hills
