941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / Business and the Law: After the bomb - Legal Briefs City solicitors Norton Rose, bombed out of Bishopsgate by the IRA in April 1993, will return to base on November 27 after a Pounds 20m refit. The firm has taken advantage of its enforced absence to redesign its working environment. Roger Birkby, managing partner, says the use of the latest information technology and communications systems should improve efficiency and keep down costs.
