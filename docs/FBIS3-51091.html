Language: French Article Type:BFN [Report by Jacmel correspondent Voltaire Rene -- recorded in Creole] [Text] Captain Yves-Marie Souffrant of the Haitian Armed Forces [FADH], head of the Jacmel district command for one year, has been transferred to FADH general headquarters. This transfer has caused great sadness among the sporting population, especially the Star of Jacmel Soccer Team. During his time in the area, Capt. Souffrant's technical assistance gave a great boost to sports throughout the southeastern part of the country.
