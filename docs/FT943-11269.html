940801 FT 01 AUG 94 / US troops set for round-clock Rwanda airlift US Defence Secretary William Perry talks to US soldiers yesterday at a US-installed water purification plant in the border town of Goma, Zaire. Earlier, Mr Perry had visited Kigali, the Rwandan capital, where the first main body of US troops arrived yesterday. They had within hours repaired the mortar-bombed runway for round-the-clock operations to ease the plight of millions suffering in the country's civil war. French may delay Rwanda pullout, Page 3
