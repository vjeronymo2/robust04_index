Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cape Town March 24 SAPA -- Narcotics detectives acting on information seized 70,000 mandrax tablets with an estimated street value of more than R[Rand]1 million in Khayelitsha in the Cape Peninsula on Wednesday. The policemen searched the canopy of a bakkie [pick-up truck] and found the tablets hidden in a secret compartment. Four men were arrested.
