BFN [Text] Almaty, April 26 (XINHUA) -- Visiting Chinese Premier Li Peng and Kazakhstan President N.A. Nazarbayev signed a border agreement between the two countries here today. After the signing ceremony, Li called the agreement "a historic document," which defines the over-1,700-kilometer-long border between the two countries in a legal form. This, he said, would serve to boost the development of the existing bilateral relations of friendship. Nazarbayev said the agreement symbolizes a long-term good- neighborly relationship between the two countries. He expected the Sino-Kazakhstan border to be one of peace and friendship. Also signed were an agreement on China providing loans to Kazakhstan, notes to be exchanged on China presenting a batch of materials to Kazakhstan, and a summary of talks on developing international railway transportation between the Chinese Ministry of Railways and Kazakhstan's Ministry of Communications.
