BFN [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk, meeting on Wednesday the head of a delegation of U.S. economists, Nicholas Burns, expressed the hope that Ukraine's newly-elected parliament would ratify the Ukrainian-American agreements signed during his January visit to the United States. The American economists have come to Ukraine, where they are due to stay till 6 May, to find out how the country could use the economic aid to a sum of $350 million the United States plans to give it. According to Kravchuk, America is going to help Ukraine reform its economy, make investments in it and step up trade with the country. Following his meeting with Kravchuk, Burns said a Ukrainian-U.S. fund was due to be set up this summer to give loans to private Ukrainian businesses.
