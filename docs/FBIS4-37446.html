BFN [Text] Baghdad, 20 Jun (INA) -- Dr. 'Abd-al-Razzaq al-Hashimi, head of the Foreign Relations Office in the Iraq Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party and head of the Iraqi-French Friendship Society, today met with the delegation of French businessmen currently visiting Iraq. Al-Hashimi reviewed Iraqi-French relations and the need to return them to normal in a way that will serve the peoples of both friendly countries. He also pointed to the repercussions of the continued unfair blockade imposed on the Iraqi people. This blockade, he added, is a flagrant breach and a blatant violation of all international laws, charters, and norms. Al-Hashimi urged the French firms to make an effective move in order to end the Iraqi people's suffering and promote bilateral economic and trade relations. The French delegation asserted that its visit to Iraq aims to discuss the Iraqi-French trade and economic relations and promote them by all means.
