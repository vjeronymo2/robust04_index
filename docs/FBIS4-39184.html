BFN [ITAR-TASS report: "Most Polled Kazakhstanis Want Return to Ruble Zone"] [Text] Many Kazakhstanis think it was a mistake to bring in their own currency last November. This is shown by an opinion poll taken in the Republic by Alma-Ata's [Almaty's] Giller Institute. Some 62.5 percent of those residents of the capital who were polled are convinced that the country should return to the ruble zone. Only 21 percent of respondents categorically oppose this step. In the six months the national currency has been in existence, its value has fallen more than eight times over.
