BFN [Text] President Borhanoddin Rabbani of Afghanistan announced his readiness last night to hand over power to a transitional government. According to a report of the Central News Unit from Kabul, Rabbani announced that while attending the Herat meeting, to be held among various Afghan group leaders, he will hand over power to the person elected by this meeting. Rabbani's term of office ended yesterday, but the Supreme Court of Afghanistan has demanded that Rabbani stay in power for another six months.
