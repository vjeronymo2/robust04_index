Language: Swedish Article Type:BFN [Text] Russia's debt to Finland, which runs into the billions, will be partly reduced by Finland buying weapons from Russia. This involves weapons and ammunition worth 450 million Finnish Marks granted in a supplementary budget last year. Finland is to receive portable anti-aircraft missiles, among other things. The deliveries will be specified in a couple of weeks, the Defense Ministry says. The total Russian debt to Finland is just over 6 billion Finnish Marks.
