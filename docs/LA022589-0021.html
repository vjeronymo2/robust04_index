February 25, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ELLIOTT'S PAC-10 MARK SHOULD GET AN ASTERISK

While I agree that Sean Elliott is quite a basketball player and scoring machine, it would logically appear to me that either he, Reggie Miller and the other top Pac-10 scorers should have an asterisk by their records, or Lew Alcindor should have one.

Alcindor made his record in three seasons, while the others took four.

Fair is fair.

RON SEERY

Los Angeles
