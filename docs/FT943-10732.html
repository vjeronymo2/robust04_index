940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Edinburgh Small net asset value at 103p Edinburgh Small Companies Trust, admitted to the Official List in August 1993, reported a net asset value of 102.88p per share at June 30. The trust, which aims for long-term capital growth through a portfolio of small UK quoted companies, achieved net revenue of Pounds 373,000 for the period from July 9 1993 to end-June 1994. Earnings per share were 0.72p and a final distribution of 0.21p is recommended. Edinburgh New Tiger Trust, incorporated in January 1994 and launched in March, announced a 6 per cent fall in net asset value to 45.28p from launch to the end of June. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 944,000 for earnings per share of 0.337p.
