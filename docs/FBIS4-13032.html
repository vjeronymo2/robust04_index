BFN [Text] In its issue today, KUIRDISTAN NWAY cites a source [not further identified] as saying that head of the Iraqi regime Saddam Husayn has ordered the execution by firing squad of General Salim al-Hamduni on charges of planning a coup. The paper says that the body was handed over to the family but no prayers were allowed. The paper notes that Gen. al-Hamduni had been awarded the al-Qadisiyah and nine other medals for valor. KURDISTAN NWAY points out that other ranking commanders might also have been killed during the campaign.
