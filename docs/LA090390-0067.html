September 3, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT

Wall Art: Drawings and pastels from the 1982 film "Pink Floyd the Wall" will go on display at Christie's Los Angeles in Beverly Hills on Tuesday.

Artwork from the film's animated sequences, designed by Gerald Scarfe, will be sold at Christie's South Kensington on Sept. 21. Pre-sale estimates range from $270 to $2,700. This auction will also include a selection of signed items and props from the "The Wall" performance that Pink Floyd gave this summer at the Berlin Wall, which will be sold to benefit the Memorial Fund for Disaster Relief.BETH KLEID
