BFN [Text] A military delegation headed by Defense Minister Pavel Kazlowski departed this morning for the Lithuanian Republic on a one-day official visit. The Defense Ministry press center has informed BELAPAN that the Belarusian delegation will meet with Seimas [parliament] Chairman C. Jursenas and Defense Minister L. Linkevicius. The program also envisions a visit to the Motorized Landing Brigade "Iron Wolf" and a meeting with leaders of the Volunteer National Defense Service.
