BFN [Text] Baku, June 11 (TURAN) -- Today TURAN was told by Helen Agapova, Russian Defense Minister's assistant for public relations, that Russia does not plan to set up military bases at Azerbaijan's territory. According to Agapova, Russia does not intend to obtain the status of military base to Gabala radio-location station as well. "It is a separate radio-technical unit which we want to use ourselves without Azerbaijan and free of charge. But we need Azerbaijan's agreement for that", she said.
