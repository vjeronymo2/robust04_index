BFN [Text] The document of principles of the multilateral arms control committee will be discussed in a working meeting of the committee members in a special session in Vienna. This has been decided at the conclusion of this round of talks, held in Qatar. Qol Yisra'el correspondent Karmela Menashe reports that another working team of the multilateral committee will convene in France in the near future to discuss other issues which have not been finalized. David 'Ivri, director general of the Defense Ministry, described the Qatar meeting as successful. He noted that among other things, it was decided to hold a naval search and rescue maneuver. David 'Ivri noted that the Saudis and several other parties tried to delay the rate of progress, because they believe the talks are proceeding too fast.
