CSO ["Edict of the President of the Republic of Tatarstan on Appointing N.G. Aminov Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Tatarstan in the Russian Federation"] [Text] In accordance with Article 111 of the Constitution of the Republic of Tatarstan and for the purposes of implementing Article 8 of the Treaty of the Russian Federation and the Republic of Tatarstan "On the Limitations of Subjects for the Conduct and Mutual Delegation of Powers Between Organs of State Authority of the Russian Federation and Organs of State Authority of the Republic of Tatarstan," and following the directions of the Statute "On the Plenipotentiary of the Republic of Tatarstan in the Russian Federation," which was confirmed by Edict of the President of Tatarstan No. 384 of 13 June 1994, I resolve: To appoint Nail Galeyevich Aminov plenipotentiary of the Republic of Tatarstan in the Russian Federation. [Signed]President of the Republic of Tatarstan M. Shaymiyev. Kazan Kremlin 13 June 1994

* * * *
Nail Galeyevich Aminov was born in 1945. He is a native of
Kazan. He graduated from the Kazan Chemistry and Technology Institute and the Academy of Social Sciences. He is an economic sciences candidate. He began his work activity as an electrician at the Kazan fur combine. He worked in Kazan from 1964 to 1968 at the agricultural institute and at the medical instruments plant. After completing the institute, he was assigned to the city of Sterlitamak in the Bashkir ASSR [Autonomous Soviet Socialist Republic], where he worked at the Avangard plant. Afterwards, he worked in republican organs of the Bashkir ASSR--as the chief of a department of the Council of Ministers and deputy minister and minister of housing and municipal services of the republic. In 1990, N.G. Aminov was elected deputy of the Supreme Soviet of the Russian Federation; he worked on the staff of the Supreme Soviet as the chairman of a subcommittee, the deputy chairman of a committee, and the manager of a department. In 1993, he was appointed deputy chairman of the Goskomfederatsiya [State Federation Committee] of Russia.
