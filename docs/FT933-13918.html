930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Explaura listing restored Dealings in Explaura Holdings, which quarries limestone aggregates in Newfoundland, Canada, were restored yesterday following the USM-quoted company's announcement on Wednesday evening of a rights issue to raise Pounds 2.44m. The shares, suspended at 3 1/2 p, edged ahead to 3 3/4 p.
