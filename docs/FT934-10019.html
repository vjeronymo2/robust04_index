931108 FT 08 NOV 93 / Construction Contracts: Ballast Nedam Construction A contract for Pounds 13.5m to design and build a shopping centre in Ashton-under-Lyne has been awarded to BALLAST NEDAM CONSTRUCTION by developers MAB (UK). Work is to be completed by August 1995. Piled foundations will support the in situ concrete frame (to be faced in brickwork with natural and reconstituted stone details).
