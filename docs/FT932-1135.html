930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / World News in Brief: Lendl out Ivan Lendl, number seven seed in the Wimbledon lawn tennis championships, was knocked out in the second round by Frenchman Arnaud Boetsch, 4-6, 7-5, 6-3, 6-4. Outside hope Chris Bailey of the UK, ranked 263 in the world, lost to number five seed Goran Ivanisevic in five sets.
