April 6, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Ted R. Capener, vice president for university relations at the University of Utah, has been nominated to become the next board chairman of the Public Broadcasting Service. If elected at the next board meeting on Wednesday, Capener will succeed New York real estate investor-operator and WNET board member Alfred R. Stern, who has served four one-year terms as chairman. Capener will give PBS leadership an all-Utah cast. He (class of '53) and PBS president Bruce L. Christensen ('68) are both University of Utah graduates.ALEENE MacMINN
