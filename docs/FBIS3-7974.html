Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Government Session"] [Text] At its yesterday session, the Macedonian Government determined the program on supporting the development of agriculture in 1994. The planned irretrievable sum of 75 million denars will be used for the creation of artificial fields, development of the cattle breeding and the fishing in Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], financing of small accumulating dams, and so on. The government also proposed regulations in founding donator and charitable funds.
