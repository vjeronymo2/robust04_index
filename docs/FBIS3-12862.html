Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Shaykh Muhammad Mahdi Shams-al-Din, deputy chairman of the Higher Islamic Shiite Council, has renewed his accusation that Israel engineered the massacre at Sayyidat al-Najat Church. He stressed that this is a type of crime that only Israel commits. He did not rule out the possibility that the perpetrators may be Lebanese Christians, Maronites, or Muslims, adding: We exonerate no one; the crime happened and its perpetrators are at large in our society. And Israel has tools and agents in Lebanon. He went on to call on the government to uncover the criminals because we want to learn the identity of these tools of Israel. Grand Ja'farite Mufti Shaykh 'Abd-al-Amir Qabalan has said that the total condemnation of the Sayyidat al-Najah Church crime has fortified Lebanon anew, enhanced the Lebanese people's vigilance and cohesion, and consolidated their national unity.
