National Heart, Lung, and Blood Institute; Meeting of the National Heart, Lung, and Blood Advisory Council Pursuant to Public Law 92&hyph;463, notice is hereby given of the meeting of the National Heart, Lung, and Blood Advisory Council on September 1&hyph;2, 1994, at the National Institutes of Health, 9000 Rockville Pike, Building 31, Conference Room 10, Bethesda, Maryland 20892. The Council meeting will be open to the public on September 1 from 9 a.m. to approximately 3:30 p.m. for discussion of program policies and issues. Attendance by the public is limited to space available. In accordance with the provisions set forth in sections 552b(c)(4) and 552b(c)(6), Title 5, U.S.C., section 10(d) of Public Law 92&hyph;463, the Council meeting will be closed to the public from approximately 3:30 p.m. to recess on September 1 and from 8:30 a.m. to adjournment on September 2 for the review, discussion and evaluation of individual grant applications. These applications and the discussions could reveal confidential trade secrets or commercial property such as patentable material, and personal information concerning individuals associated with the applications, the disclosure of which would constitute a clearly unwarranted invasion of personal privacy. Individuals who plan to attend and need special assistance, such as sign language interpretation or other reasonable accommodations, should contact the Executive Secretary in advance of the meeting.
