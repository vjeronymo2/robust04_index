BFN [Text] Mircea Snegur, president of the Republic of Moldova, has decided to join the Moldovan Agrarian Democratic Party [PDAM], winner of the parliamentary elections that took place two and a half months ago, our correspondent Alexandru Beleavschi reported, quoting sources in the Moldovan Presidency. Thus, President Snegur's decision makes it possible today for all Chisinau's leaders to be united under the banner of the PDAM. Prime Minister Andrei Sangheli is one of the PDAM leaders, Petru Lucinschi, president of the Moldovan Parliament, also ran on the lists of this party at the recent parliamentary elections, and Dumitru Motpan, president of the party, is the vice president of the Parliament in Chisinau.
