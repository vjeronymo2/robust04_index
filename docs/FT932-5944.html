930601 FT 01 JUN 93 / International Company News: Philip Green back in discount trade MR PHILIP Green, ousted from cut-price retailer Amber Day last year following institutional pressure, has returned to the discount business with the purchase of Parker and Franks, the loss-making hardware toys, and gift store chain. It went into receivership on May 12. Mr Green paid 'a substantial cash sum' - reported to be Pounds 3.3m - for Parker and Franks, which last year made sales of Pounds 30m. He has taken 54 of the 60 shops, employing 800 of the original 1,000 staff.
