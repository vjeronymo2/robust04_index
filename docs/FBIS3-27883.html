Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] In connection with rumors that U.S. aircraft carriers would be deployed in the vicinity of the Korean peninsula, an ROK Government official said today that the ROK Government has not been informed of such deployment. He also said he knew this is not true. This official also said that when the Team Spirit exercise is conducted, U.S. forces aircraft carriers are normally deployed near the Korean peninsula. He added: No decision has been made on whether to conduct the Team Spirit exercise this year. Therefore, no decision has been made to deploy aircraft carriers near the Korean peninsula.
