Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 10 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Today here the three days conference on the theme "The Issues of Reforming the Local Governing ", which was organized by the nongovernment Center of Social Science, Civic Education and Human Rights with assistance of the US Conference is to promote formation of democratic system of local governing and management, especially from theoretical- methodical and conceptual aspects. In the first day of the conference presented were four basic reports of the agenda, including a report by the Mongolian parliament member, one of the elaborators the country's new Constitution B. Chimid "The Basic Principles of the Local Governing System and its Democratic Bases in the Constitution, Ways of Their Realization", a report by expert in politics and sociology S. Erdenetuul "General Regularity of Formation of Principles of Local Self-Governing by International Experience and Reflection of Peculiarities of the Country". During the hearing and discussion of the reports a number of conceptual differences were revealed on the issues of local governing, contradictions in interpretation of legal concepts between law-makers and experts, in particular, in definition of a status of the Hural of representatives of citizens at Somon and Aymag level, its inter-relations with a head of administrative governing, distribution of powers and functions between them. Presence of such theoretical contradictions, confusion at a conceptual level were confirmed in speeches of practical workers secretaries of Aymag, City Hurals of representatives of citizens. As they said, representatives of local elected authorities are practically fully dependent on heads of administrations, do not have a real power to solve any local issue. The State Great Hural members, specialists and lawyers of different institutes and state establishments, secretaries of all City and Aymag Hurals of representatives of citizens are participating in work of the conference.
