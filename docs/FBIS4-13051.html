BFN [Text] The Israeli delegation for the multilateral talks on water has arrived in Oman. They were greeted by Omani Foreign Ministry officials. The talks are due to get under way on Sunday [17 April]. The Israeli delegation is headed by Deputy Foreign Minister Yosi Beilin, who is expected to meet with senior Omani officials during his stay. Beilin said on his arrival that the visit is important because it broadens Israel's horizons, enabling the country to reach new areas. The Israeli team flew to Muscat on an Egyptian plane from Cairo and not on a direct flight from Israel.
