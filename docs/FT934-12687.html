931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / TerreBlanche convicted A South African court yesterday convicted right-wing extremist Mr Eugene TerreBlanche of public violence during a demonstration against President F W de Klerk, Reuter reports from Johannesburg. Three white extremists were killed in clashes between right-wingers, police and black passers-by when Mr TerreBlanche's Afrikaner Resistance Movement (AWB) tried to disrupt a speech by Mr de Klerk in August 1991.
