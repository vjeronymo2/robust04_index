BFN [Text] The 10 Palestinian opposition groups, the Hamas movement, and the Palestinian Islamic Jihad have declared a strike in the Palestinian camps in the Sidon area, the 'Ayn al-Hulwah camp, and the al-Miyah wa Miyah camp. Shops inside the camps closed and classes in United Nations Relief and Works Agency schools were suspended to protest the signing of the agreement between Israel and the PLO in Cairo today. Car tires were set on fire at the entrances to the camps this morning.
