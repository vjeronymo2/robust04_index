Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Defense Ministers Council of the Commonwealth of Independent States at its session in Moscow on Thursday [24 February] discussed military cooperation and ways of reinforcing defenses along the Tajik-Afghan border. Russia's Deputy Defense Minister Boris Gromov is quoted as saying the ministers have managed to form a legal basis for further military cooperation within the Commonwealth. Azerbaijan, Georgia and Belarus are taking part in the session as full fledged members of the council following their admission to the CIS.
