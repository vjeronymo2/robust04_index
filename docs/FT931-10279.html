930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / Mafia suspect arrested Ms Rosetta Cutolo, 56, one of the 'Godmothers' who allegedly helped turn the Naples Mafia, the Camorra, into a vast criminal empire, was arrested yesterday after 13 years on the run, Reuter reports from Naples. She had been sentenced in her absence to nearly 10 years in jail for a string of offences, including murder.
