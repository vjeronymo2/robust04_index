December 20, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski starts the final week of the Hollywood Park meeting $144.70 in the hole. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is down to $855.30.

For today, Bob's bets are $30 to win on Rusty Attitude in the fifth and $20 to win on Jazz Island in the seventh.
