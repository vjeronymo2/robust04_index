BFN [Text] Arber Xhaferri, chairman of the Party for Democratic Prosperity [PDP], has arrived for a one-week visit in Copenhagen to hold meetings at the Danish Foreign Ministry with representatives from CSCE and the Danish Social Democratic Party. During a meeting today with the leadership of the PDP branch in Denmark, Mr. Arber Xhaferri presented the current situation in The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia [FYROM] and the activities of this party, the largest of the Albanians in this Republic, about the extraordinary census and future parliamentary elections.
