BFN [Text] Prague, April 13 (CTK) -- The Czech Government view of the NATO air strikes against Bosnian Serb targets near Gorazde on April 10-11 was identical to the Foreign Ministry's, Premier Vaclav Klaus told journalists after a cabinet meeting today. In a communique issued on Monday [11 April] the Foreign Ministry supported the United Nations and NATO decision to bomb Bosnian Serb positions near Gorazde in Bosnia. At the meeting the government approved Klaus's visit to South America. Klaus will visit Chile, Brazil and Argentina. Klaus said the government considered Latin American countries to be very important markets for the Czech Republic and the territory where problems similar to transformation of Central and East European countries were being solved. Klaus will leave Prague on April 17 to return on April 27.
