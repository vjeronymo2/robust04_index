940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Chartfield completes reverse takeover The reverse takeover of Bell Court Fund Management by Chartfield Financial Holdings has been completed. Mr Mark Thomas, Chartfield's managing director, said the company had taken the step of becoming a public company in order to facilitate further acquisitions and mergers. Chartfield currently had Pounds 60m under management, he said, and was at an advanced stage of negotiation with five other broker fund managers ranging from Pounds 10m to Pounds 80m under management.
