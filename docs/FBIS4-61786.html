BFN [Unattributed RIA report: "The Act of Transfer of the Skrunda Radar Station to Latvia Has Been Signed"] [Text] The Russian military has presented documentation on the new building of the Skrunda radar station as well as a model of the site with all the structures to Latvian Prime Minister Valdis Birkavs, who visited the Russian military installation accompanied by Valdis Pavlovskis, defense minister; Girts Lukins, environmental protection and regional development minister, and other officials. The aim of the visit is to establish which material assets will stay at Latvia's disposal and which Russia will evacuate. Documents on the transfer of the installation, which spreads over a territory of 42 hectares, were also signed.
