921203 FT 03 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Reduced losses for Harrison Industries The enlarged Harrison Industries group, specialist engineer, reduced its pre-tax losses from a pro forma Pounds 594,000 to Pounds 455,000 in the six months ended September 30 1992. These are the first interim results since the enlarged group was formed by the takeover of Harrison by Antares Group and since the subsequent management and name changes. Turnover was Pounds 25.8m (Pounds 26.1m) and losses per share were 0.3p (0.48p).
