November 4, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP;

HIGH SCHOOL FOOTBALL

Bishop Diego 35, St. Bonaventure 25 -- Ricky Demarest scored four touchdowns, including one on an 84-yard interception return that gave Bishop Diego a 21-7 halftime lead in a Tri-Valley League game at Larrabee Stadium.

Peter Houston completed 10 of 16 passes for 141 yards and two touchdowns for St. Bonaventure. Toby Noblin carried 17 times for 96 yards. MIKE BARTLETT
