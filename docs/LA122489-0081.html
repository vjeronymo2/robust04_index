December 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COSBY'S COMMENTS

Cosby complains because his daughter Erinn rejects his philosophy and the strings attached to it, which include a magnanimous educational opportunity that, evidently, his other four children readily accept. Apparently Bill's university degrees haven't helped him much, because even parents in the poorest straits intuitively give support and love to all their children -- even those who stray from the mold.

FRANK L. BURKE

Los Angeles
