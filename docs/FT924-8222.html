921112 FT 12 NOV 92 / UK Company News: King & Shaxson well ahead King & Shaxson Holdings, the discount house, said that for the six months to October 31 it had made 'excellent profits' as opposed to a small profit last time. Accordingly, the interim dividend is lifted from 2.5p to 4p. Included in the results are those of wholly-owned Smith St Aubyn (Holdings), the discount house.
