BFN [Text] During a meeting with French President Francois Mitterrand in Paris on Tuesday [12 April] evening, Latvian President Guntis Ulmanis expressed readiness for integration into the European Union [EU] and a willingness to implement EU requirements. However, Ulmanis stressed, Europe will have to rely more on Latvia in the integration process. It is important that both Denmark and France are ready to discuss the issue of setting up a fund to support the closure of the Skrunda radar base and for the repatriation of Russian military pensioners to Russia, according to Martins Virsis, parliamentary secretary of the Latvian Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Francois Mitterrand admitted during the talks that Latvias is a small nation but has a unique European culture. Europe is interested in preserving countries like that, Mitterrand said.
