930322 FT 22 MAR 93 / International Company News: Celsius posts 7% rise to SKr545m CELSIUS, the Swedish state-owned defence group now being privatised, increased 1992 profits by 7 per cent to SKr545m (Dollars 7.1m) from SKr507m, writes Christopher Brown-Humes in Stockholm. The group said its order book at year-end totalled SKr19.5bn, SKr1bn more than at the end of 1991. Its recent purchase of Nobel Industries' defence electronics activities has increased this total to SKr26bn.
