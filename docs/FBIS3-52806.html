Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Taba, 10 Jan (MENA)--Heads of the Palestinian and Israeli delegations to the Taba talks, Palestinian president's adviser Nabil Sha'th and Israeli Deputy Chief of Staff Amnon Shahaq, held a private meeting in Taba immediately after the Israeli delegation arrived at the hotel today. MENA learned that they discussed a plan for the negotiations in this round, which is scheduled to continue until Thursday or Friday morning and then resume in the next two weeks to reach an agreement on implementing Palestinian self-rule in Gaza and Jericho. Members of the two delegations joined the meeting later. During the afternoon session, the delegations are due to form subcommittees to discuss the disputed issues, basically, the border crossings, the size of Jericho, and the security of the Israeli settlements. Sea, air, and road security and how to control it in accordance with the Palestinian-Israeli Declaration of Principles signed in Washington on 13 September will also be discussed. The Civil Committee concerned with the transfer of the self-rule power, which formerly met in al-'Arish, is scheduled to open meetings in Taba on Tuesday morning.
