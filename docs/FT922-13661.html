920410 FT 10 APR 92 / UK Company News: Receivers in at Isis Group Mr Robert Birchall of the Bristol office of Cork Gully has been appointed administrative receiver to Isis Group. The main trading company within the group is Isis Construction, which specialises in commercial and industrial building work. It has a turnover of some Pounds 30m and employs about 120 staff. The other two companies are relatively small; Wessex Guild, a maker of architectural metalwork, with turnover of Pounds 3m employing 25 staff and Isis Pneumatics, which has turnover of Pounds 4m and about 50 employees. Mr Birchall said the group had been in difficulties for some time. There had been negotiations with companies interested in the business but these had not been concluded. Shares in Isis, an over-the counter stock, were last dealt in June 1991 at 40p through Granville Securities.
