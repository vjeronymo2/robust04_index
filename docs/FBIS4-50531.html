BFN [Text] Beijing, May 19 (XINHUA) -- China sincerely hopes that the Cambodian leaders of various parties will, with the spirit of mutual understanding and mutual accommodation, make joint efforts for national reconciliation. Foreign Ministry Spokesman Wu Jianmin made the remarks at a weekly news briefing here this afternoon when asked to comment the report that King Sihanouk has once again proposed a round table meeting in Pyongyang, and invited Mr. Chea Sim, Mr. Norodom Ranariddh, Mr. Hun and Mr. Khieu Samphan to attend the proposed meeting. "We highly appreciate and support King Sihanouk's efforts in realizing national reconciliation and maintaining peace and stability in Cambodia," he said. "We sincerely hope that setting store by their national interests, the Cambodian leaders of various parties will, with the spirit of mutual understanding and mutual accommodation, make joint efforts for national reconciliation," the spokesman stated.
