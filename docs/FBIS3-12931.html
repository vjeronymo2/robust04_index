Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] University students in Egypt have issued a statement calling for Egyptian volunteers to be sent to occupied Palestine to engage in jihad against the Zionists. Dr. Badr-al-Din Ghazi, head of the Cairo University Teachers Association, announced the formation of an university committee for the boycott of the Zionist entity. The committee will press for the severing of ties between Egypt and the Zionist entity and the expulsion of the Zionist ambassador in Cairo. Islamic student societies and the Cairo University Teachers Association have announced plans to stage a massive march tomorrow [8 March] following a prayer service for the souls of the victims of the al-Ibrahimi Mosque massacre. The march will proceed to the Zionist embassy in Cairo to express outrage at the Hebron massacre.
