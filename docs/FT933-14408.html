930714 FT 14 JUL 93 / International Company News: Reebok to buy back more stock REEBOK International, the footwear company whose shares dropped last month after it warned that second-quarter figures would be below forecasts, is to repurchase up to Dollars 200m of its stock, writes Martin Dickson. This augments an existing Dollars 200m buy-back plan under which 5.6m shares have been purchased for about Dollars 183m. Its stock closed at Dollars 27 5/8, down 1/8.
