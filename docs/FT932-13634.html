930422 FT 22 APR 93 / UK Company News: No MMC probe for TIP Europe offer The trade and industry secretary yesterday announced that the recommended cash bid by GE Capital, the financial services arm of General Electric of the US, for TIP Europe, the trailer rental company, would not be referred to the Monopolies and Mergers Commission. Shareholders were yesterday sent the offer document; they have until May 12 to accept the 42p a share offer, which values the company at Pounds 71m.
