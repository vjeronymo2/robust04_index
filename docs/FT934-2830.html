931211 FT 11 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Equity Consort Net asset value per ordinary share of Equity Consort Investment Trust stood at 721p at October 31, compared with 611p a year earlier. Asset value per deferred share rose from Pounds 10.23 to Pounds 12.42. Net revenue for the half year fell to Pounds 769,116 (Pounds 894,559) for earnings per ordinary share of 15.04p (16.78p) and per deferred share of 20.49p (24.55p). The trust is maintaining its interim dividend of 11.0625 per ordinary and 13.125p per deferred share.
