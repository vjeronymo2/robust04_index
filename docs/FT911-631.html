910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / Parliament and Politics: Ashdown presses PM on disasters THE prime minister pledged yesterday to seek a better way of coordinating international relief in disasters like those afflicting Bangladesh and parts of Africa. With MPs pressing for a debate on the cyclone in Bangladesh, Mr Major said he would do what he could to bring about a better system of international response. Mr Paddy Ashdown, leader of the Liberal Democrats, protested that while Britain's leading response to the Bangladesh cyclone was laudable, the government and the press continued to 'ignore the incalculably greater scale of the disaster now unfolding in Africa'.
