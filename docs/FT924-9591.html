921105 FT 05 NOV 92 / International Company News: St Lawrence Cement tumbles into red ST Lawrence Cement, eastern Canada's biggest cement producer, suffered losses in the third quarter and first nine months because of the recession, writes Robert Gibbens. The company, controlled by the Swiss Holderbank group, does not see much hope of recovery until the second half of 1993, and is retrenching further. St Lawrence Cement recorded a loss of CDollars 2m (USDollars 1.7m), or 5 cents a share, in the third quarter, against net profit of CDollars 6.2m, or 15 cents a share, last time. Sales slipped to CDollars 151m, compared with CDollars 168m. The nine-month net loss was CDollars 21.7m, or 51 cents a share, on sales of CDollars 33lm, against a loss of CDollars 4.8m, or 12 cents a share, on sales of CDollars 367m a year earlier.
