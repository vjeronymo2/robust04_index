Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Dakar, 11 Jan (AFP) -- The CFA franc was devalued by 50 percent, it was announced this evening in Dakar by the 14 member countries of the franc zone. The devaluation allows for a series of backup measures which were not immediately published. The decision was published after an extraordinary summit was carried out behind closed doors yesterday and today including representatives of 14 member countries of the franc zone (10 heads of state, three prime ministers, and a minister of finance from 14 African countries).
