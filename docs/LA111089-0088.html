November 10, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEW REVIEWER

Former Herald Examiner film critic Peter Rainer joins The Times today, writing film criticism and commentary. Today, he reviews "Staying Together." If you think you've seen this film before, you have, says Rainer. The movie is englobed in the sentimentality of a hundred coming of age dramas, and the familiarity breeds not contempt but boredom. F1
