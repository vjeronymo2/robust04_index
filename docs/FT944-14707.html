941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Cradley at Pounds 1.2m Pre-tax profits at Cradley Group Holdings, the lithographic printing company, fell 27 per cent from Pounds 1.64m to Pounds 1.2m in the year to June 30 on sales down 20 per cent to Pounds 23.7m, against Pounds 29.6m. The group said the sales reduction resulted from the completion of a large one-off contract. The profits fall included a Pounds 290,710 loss on the closure of De Mandus Print. The dividend for the year is unchanged at 1.15p. Earnings per share were 2.5p (3.3p).
