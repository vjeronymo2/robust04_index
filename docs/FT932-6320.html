New season carrots are good this week at 35-40p a lb (35-40p), along with Jersey Royals and Cornish new potatoes at 25-30p a lb (25-35p) and broccoli at 50-65p per 8oz prepack (50-70p). Spring greens at 35-40p a lb (35-40p) round off the best vegetable buys. English tomatoes at 60-65p a lb (65-70p), Spring onions at 35-40p a bunch (30-35p) and Chinese leaves at 60-80p each (75-80p) are all superb quality.
