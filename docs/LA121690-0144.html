December 16, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

O.C. NEWSMAKERS: AIRLINE EXECUTIVE JOINS BOARD OF FIDELITY NATIONAL

J. Thomas Talbot has joined the board of directors of Fidelity National Financial Inc. He is president and chief executive of Hawaiian Airlines and its subsidiaries.

Fidelity National, which is headquartered in Irvine, issues title insurance and performs other title-related services in 31 states and the District of Columbia.
