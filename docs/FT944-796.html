941223 FT 23 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Seafield sells Pounds 1.8m gas field interest Seafield, the USM-quoted exploration company, is selling its 10 per cent interest in North Sea Block 43/30, including the Schooner gas field, to Eastern Natural Gas (Offshore), a subsidiary of Eastern Group, for Pounds 1.82m cash. The company has also been granted a 25 per cent interest in Permit WA 259P in the Camarvon Basin, offshore Western Australia.
