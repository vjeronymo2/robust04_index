Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shchegoleva] [Text] Sochi, 9 Feb -- The Republic of Abkhazia's Armed Forces are completing the operation to neutralize the inidividual groupings of Georgian armed formations which infiltrated Gali Rayon, Abkhazia. An ITAR-TASS correspondent has learned from a well-informed source within republican leadership that Abkhazian formations have taken under guard the main cascade of the Inguri GES, a motor road bridge across the river Inguri as well as a number of important industrial facilities. Local operations are being conducted in individual sections of the coastal area of Gali Rayon. According to the above official, combat operations in the republic's border rayon will be completed in a day or two. Earlier, in the morning, two coaches with Abkhazian servicemen exploded on a mine in the center of the town of Gali. The explosion killed four people and injured nine.
