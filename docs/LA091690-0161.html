September 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

AIRLINE PASSENGERS URGED TO WRITE TROOPS

Continental Airlines passengers who travel through Newark International Airport will be offered free stationery and postage to write troops in the Middle East.

"We're hoping a lot of people will take time between their coffee and boarding to jot a little note," Continental spokeswoman Andrea Pass said.

The airline says 35,000 passengers travel through the Newark airport each day on its planes.
