940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: High-Point Pre-tax profits of High-Point, the specialist consultancy and project promotion company, fell from Pounds 166,000 to Pounds 93,000 in the six months to November 30. The outcome was achieved on turnover down from Pounds 26.1m to Pounds 22.6m, however, last year's figure included almost Pounds 5m from discontinued operations. Earnings per share emerged at 0.8p (3.1p).
