March 16, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

SEOUL CABINET RESIGNS TO AID ROH

The Cabinet resigned today to give President Roh Tae Woo a free hand in reorganizing the government after the merger of his party with two opposition groups.

Government officials said Roh is expected to announce a new Cabinet on Saturday after consultations with his two fellow governing party leaders -- Kim Young Sam and Kim Jong Pil.
