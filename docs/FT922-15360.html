920401 FT 01 APR 92 / UK Company News: Carpet tile decline hits Gaskell The decline in the carpet tile market left Gaskell, the Lancashire-based carpet maker, with pre-tax profits down from Pounds 1.33m to Pounds 255,000 for 1991. Demand for the company's own brand fell by 23 per cent while other sales fell by 53 per cent. Turnover was down 25 per cent at Pounds 30.2m (Pounds 40.3m). Interest charges fell to Pounds 609,000 (Pounds 868,000) as result of lower rates. Earnings per share were 3.1p (16.1p) but the final dividend is maintained at 5.5p for an unchanged total of 8.5p.
