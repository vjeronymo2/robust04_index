June 30, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

EARNINGS

Alpha Microsystems reported a profit of $579,000 for the first fiscal quarter ended May 28, a decline of 14% from the $672,000 posted for the like quarter last year. Sales rose 16% to $14.1 million from $12.1 million. Robert B. Hitchcock, chairman and chief executive, said the company in the first quarter absorbed most of the initial financial impact of acquiring Rexon Business Machines. Alpha Microsystems, headquartered in Santa Ana, manufactures computer hardware and software products.
