Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN [Text] Some days ago, puppets of communist Vietnam and the two-headed government gathered troops at Kouk Tbeng and Ta Sanh villages on south Sisophon battlefield [Battambang Province] in an attempt to attack and rob people's property and attack Democratic Kampuchea in the liberated zones. On 25 February, National Army of Democratic Kampuchea forces and the people joined together, attacked, and routed these troops. As a result: 1. More than 10 AK rifles, over 300 hand grenades, five SK rifles, three RPD guns, an M-18 gun, two B-40 rocket launchers, 10 rucksacks, 10 field radios, and other materiel were seized; 2. Eighteen of the attacking troops were killed and 15 others wounded; more than 10 AK rifles, [words indistinct] were destroyed. Three soldiers were captured.
