April 26, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

1990 RAISES FOR L.A. COUNTY DEPARTMENT HEADS

The Los Angeles County Board of Supervisors voted Tuesday for pay boosts ranging from 4% to 11% for 30 general managers and department heads as part of a management incentive program. These pay increases will cost county taxpayers approximately $178,097.
