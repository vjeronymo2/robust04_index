930513 FT 13 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Finsbury Trust net assets improve The net asset value of Finsbury Trust advanced from 111.1p to 128.7p over the 12 months to March 31. Mr Jamie Borwick, chairman of the trust, which invests in strategic holdings and special situations, said the quoted portfolio had performed in line with the FT-A All-Share Index but that unlisted investments fared less well. Attributable revenue improved from Pounds 776,000 to Pounds 872,000, for earnings of 3.6p (3.2p) per share. A recommended final dividend of 2p lifts the total to 3.2p (3p).
