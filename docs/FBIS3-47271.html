Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 8 (KCNA) -- The Workers' Party of Korea Publishing House is publishing Complete Works of Kim Il-song which contain immortal classic works of the great leader President Kim Il-song systematically in chronological order. It has recently brought out its 7th volume. Carried in it are 64 works including historic reports, speeches, concluding addresses, statements, talks and instructions of President Kim Il-song from January to April 1948. The year 1948 was a historical period when the Korean people waged a struggle to consolidate the revolutionary base of the northern half of Korea politically, economically and militarily, accelerate the building of a prosperous independent and sovereign state and reunify the country. The 7th volume consummates the outstanding ideas, theories and policies set forth by President Kim Il-song in that period to hasten the revolution and construction under the guidance of the Workers' Party of Korea with a deep-going scientific insight into the requirements of the developing revolution and precious successes and experiences attained in the work for their realization.
