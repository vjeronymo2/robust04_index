Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [YONHAP from Beijing] [Text] According to a Chinese source on 21 January, North Korea has been secretly extracting a considerable amount of plutonium while it held nuclear negotiations with the United States, and that it is highly possible that North Korea has already produced a small nuclear bomb and is hiding it in an underground warehouse. This source well-versed in North Korean affairs said: "I recently heard from a senior North Korean official that North Korea is hiding nuclear weapons in an underground warehouse in the mountains near Pyongyang and that any thorough inspection of North Korean nuclear facilities [haek sisol] by the United States and the International Atomic Energy Agency will fail to locate them."
