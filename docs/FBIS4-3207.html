BFN [Text] Beijing, 15 Apr (XINHUA) -- Responding to some residents' doubts when buying two-year treasury bonds recently, the Finance Ministry issued a public notice. Its full text follows: A public notice is hereby issued as follows: Wordings of first-phase or second-phase are printed on the back of every two-year treasury bond issued in 1994. Treasury bonds with either first-phase or second-phase wordings are all two-year treasury bonds, that earn an annual interest rate of 13 percent, effective 1 April 1994.
