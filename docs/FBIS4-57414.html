BFN [Text] The 15th Cuba-94 Tourism Convention was inaugurated on 17 May at Varadero Beach, Matanzas Province. There are 88 tourism companies participating. Boris Reyes, president of the organizing committee, gave the opening address: [Begin Reyes recording] We hope that each one of you fulfills the promotional and commercial aspects of your trip to Cuba and that your stay at the beautiful blue beach of Varadero will be gratifying as well as unforgettable. [end recording] Important foreign associations, hoteliers, journalists, businessmen, and personalities of the tourism industry have come to the convention to exchange experiences, explore Cuba's tourist product, and complete negotiations that will benefit Cuba's economy. The event will also serve to introduce the publications (SECCIONES) and GRANMA INTERNATIONAL, in German, to the Ministry of Tourism's Puerto Sol, Isla Azul, and Horizontes hotel chains. Tourism Minister Osmany Cienfuegos, Politburo member Yadira Garcia, and other personalities of the tourist industry were present at the convention's opening.
