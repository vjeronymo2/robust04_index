October 21, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

HOMEOWNERS NEAR AIRPORT

There's been so much discussion of late about changing the airport name back to Orange County Airport that I would like to offer another idea. I think the airport should be named after someone who typifies Orange County, someone who symbolizes the county's greed and devotion to the haves taking it all from the have-nots.

With this in mind, I would like to suggest that the airport be named the Donald Bren Airport.

DONNA LAMPSON, Irvine
