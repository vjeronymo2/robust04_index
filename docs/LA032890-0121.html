March 28, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: YORBA LINDA;

ROAD PROJECT CLOSES LANES AT INTERSECTION

The city has temporarily closed left turn lanes on eastbound Yorba Linda Boulevard at Imperial Highway to complete a $1.1-million street improvement project.

The Yorba Linda Boulevard Beautification Project includes widening the streets, constructing median islands and installing decorative crosswalks at the intersection. The construction began this week and is scheduled to continue through mid-May.
