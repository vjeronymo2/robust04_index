BFN [Text] At a briefing after the extraordinary meeting of the Union of Democratic Forces [SDS] National Coordinating Council, the SDS confirmed its intention to prompt the resignation of the government, which it blames for the financial crisis in the country. If the parliament does not adopt an appropriate decision on the cabinet's policy, the SDS will propose a new vote of no-confidence. The SDS will organize a rally on 5 April at St. Aleksandur Nevski Square in Sofia.
