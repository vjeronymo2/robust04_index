921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / Survey of Software at Work - Winter 1992 (25): Let us hear from you - Software at work, Future issues / Events move so fast in the computer industry that users find themselves faced with an increasing range of software options SOFTWARE is a management issue. Nearly two years ago, Software At Work set out with this message, explaining how technology was being used by individual businessmen and women to benefit their businesses. The product reviews contained in the seven issues to date have come from live users of software products. Through extensive user interviews, stories of successful (and struggling) business software users have been uncovered, and through the accompanying 'consultants' critiques,' the reasons for success (or failure) have been explained. The Software at Work writing team now wants to hear from you.
