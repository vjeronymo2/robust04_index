920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / UK Company News: AH Ball to expand with Pounds 2.87m buy AH Ball Group, the USM-quoted civil engineer, yesterday announced it had conditionally agreed to acquire KD, a manufacturer of process control systems for the water industry. The consideration of Pounds 2.87m is to be satisfied by the allotment of 2.15m ordinary shares and the issue of a loan note for Pounds 25,000. To cover the costs of the acquisition the company also plans to raise a further Pounds 470,000 by the issue of 385,241 new shares. KD made profits of Pounds 427,000 on turnover of Pounds 2.8m in the year to end-April. Net assets at that time were Pounds 175,000. Ball also announced it was to apply to move from the USM to the official list.
