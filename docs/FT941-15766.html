940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Dudley Jenkins 12% ahead Profits of USM-traded Dudley Jenkins rose from Pounds 383,000 to Pounds 430,000 pre-tax for the six months ended October 31. Turnover of Pounds 6.4m compared with Pounds 5.18m. Directors said the 12 per cent profits improvement reflected 'fine tuning' of each operation - the company's principal activity is supplying direct mail services. The interim dividend is lifted to 1.05p, against 1p, from lower earnings per share of 2.33p (2.55p) per share.
