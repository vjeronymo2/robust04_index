Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] The Democratic Movement of Haitian Youth [MODEJHA] favors the 4 February demonstration. In a statement on Signal FM, Gerard Mecklembourg invites the people to go out into the streets to say no the embargo which he calls criminal. [Begin recording] To patriotically say no to the international threat and to the criminal masquerade of Haiti's four great enemy countries, to the merciless embargo against the Haitian people; to protest this genocide skillfully prepared against the Haitian nation by the international community, MODEJHA totally and unreservedly supports the demonstration scheduled for 4 February against the macabre embargo imposed on an entire defenseless people. [end recording]
