April 30, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: BENN BEATS DEWITT FOR WBO MIDDLEWEIGHT TITLE

Nigel Benn won the World Boxing Organization middleweight title by stopping defending champion Doug DeWitt in the eighth round of a scheduled 12-round bout Sunday afternoon.

Referee Randy Newman, following WBO rules, halted the bout when Benn floored DeWitt three times in the eighth. Benn's final knockdown came from a left uppercut 44 seconds into the round.

Earlier, Benn dropped DeWitt with a right uppercut and with a left hook-right combination.

Benn, 26, based in London, is 26-1 with 24 knockouts. DeWitt, 28, and fighting out of Yonkers, N.Y., is 32-7-4 with 19 knockouts.
