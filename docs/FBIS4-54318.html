BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 27 (KCNA) -- A roundtable conference for peace and national reconciliation of Cambodia has opened in Pyongyang, the capital of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, today. It was presided over by His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk. Present there are H.E. Prince Chea Sim, acting head of state and president of the National Assembly, H.R.H. Prince Norodom Ranariddh, first prime minister, H.E. Prince Hun Sen, second prime minister, from the Royal Government side and H.E. Khieu Samphan, president of the Democratic Cambodia side.
