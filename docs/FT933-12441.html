930723 FT 23 JUL 93 / Clinton has talks with exiled Haiti president PRESIDENT Bill Clinton yesterday met Haiti's exiled president, Fr Jean-Bertrand Aristide, to discuss plans to return him to power, saying: 'There's a major potential for a victory for democracy.' Mr Clinton was expected to announce US participation in an international force to help retrain Haiti's army and work on military construction. Fr Aristide, due to return to office on October 30, is expected to name Mr Robert Malval, a respected businessman and a centrist, as his prime minister.
