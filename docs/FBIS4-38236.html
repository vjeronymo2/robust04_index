BFN [Text] Cairo, 28 Jun (MENA)--The Higher State Security Prosecution today decided to detain Lawyer Muntassir al-Zayyat for 15 days of interrogation under the supervision of the Prosecution. The security department had referred al-Zayyat to the State Security Prosecution for interrogation about charges that he belongs to an extremist religious group. Al-Zayyat is considered one of the most prominent lawyers defending the extremist Islamic groups in Egypt.
