BFN [Text] One of the main promoters for the formation of a new government through the application of Constitution articles is trying to obtain popular support. On 29 April, Senator Bernard Sansaricq sent a call to the population to support the application of Article 149 of the Constitution, the only chance, according to him, to spare the country a social explosion. Sen. Sansaricq promised the populace the formation of a nationalist, competent government, with new people for a new start.
