930203 FT 03 FEB 93 / UN-EC peace talks Talks on the UN-EC peace plan for Bosnia will resume at the UN tomorrow or Friday and could go on for weeks, Mr Cyrus Vance and Lord Owen, the co-chairmen, said yesterday, writes Michael Littlejohns in New York. They voiced concern that the US had not yet determined its policy in the crisis but they also appeared willing to make allowances for a new administration. Lord Owen again strongly opposed waiving the arms embargo imposed on the states of former Yugoslavia.
