July 30, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

LIKE FATHER, LIKE SON

Regarding "How Fathers Figure," by Paul Ciotti: If one were stupid enough to rely on the documented findings of the male psychologists and physicians Ciotti refers to, one would conclude that boys raised by their mothers are unfriendly, immature, disobedient, cowardly, intellectually deficient and suffer low self-esteem. Because Ciotti can't come up with anything deep about fatherhood, he resorts to mother-bashing.

Shame on you.

BLAKE LATIMER

Venice
