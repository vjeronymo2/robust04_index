June 16, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SO LONG, PAT RILEY: LAKER FANS SAY IT WAS GREAT WHILE IT LASTED

First the Lakers lose to Phoenix in five games in the playoffs. Now Pat Riley resigns.

Tell me, how much more trauma will Laker fans have to endure this year? Next thing we know, Magic Johnson will be traded to the Pistons for Bill Laimbeer.

JAN ZAHLER LEBOW

Los Angeles
