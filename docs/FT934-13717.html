931020 FT 20 OCT 93 / International Company News: Polaroid hit by poor third term SHARES in Polaroid, the US photography group, fell sharply yesterday after it posted a 10 per cent downturn in third-quarter earnings. For the three months to end-September, net earnings were Dollars 24.9m, or 53 cents a share, against Dollars 27. m, or 59 cents, in the corresponding period of 1992. Profits fell in spite of a 4 per cent increase in revenues to Dollars 533.9m. On Wall Street, the stock closed at Dollars 32 5/8 , down Dollars 1 1/2 on the day. The Massachusetts-based company attributed its lacklustre performance to currency fluctuations and the cost of launching its Captiva instant camera system.
