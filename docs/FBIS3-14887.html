Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday, President Leonid Kravchuk received Ms. (Anne Leuvergaunt), personal representative of French President Francois Mitterand. The visit came as a result of a recent telephone conversation between the Ukrainian and French presidents. Its purpose is to determine specific programs of cooperation between our countries. The meeting was attended by Michel Peissik, French ambassador in Ukraine. Leonid Kravchuk presented his ideas about the development of Ukrainian-French relations. The Ukrainian president and the French president's personal representative agreed on a mechanism for devising and implementing specific projects and proposals that will later become the groundwork for negotiations soon to be conducted by a Ukrainian delegation that will visit Paris. Leonid Kravchuk reconfirmed his official invitation to French President Francois Mitterrand to visit Ukraine at a time convenient for him.
