941024 FT 24 OCT 94 / Four die in Saudi kidnapping Saudi police stormed a villa of a prominent businessman, Youssef al-Rajhi, in Riyadh where Filipino hostage-takers demanding Dollars 10m had killed three people, including his two-month-old son. The other victims were a Filipina maid and an Indonesian driver. Rajhi's wife and another son were wounded. The kidnappers had demanded weapons and ammunition, Dollars 10m in a suitcase, a helicopter to take them to the airport and an aircraft to take them to an undisclosed country where they would free the hostages. Police killed one kidnapper and wounded the others after they refused to talk with police or a Philippines embassy official.
