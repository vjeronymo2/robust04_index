Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Demonstrations have been staged in a number of Yemeni cities to protest the armed attacks on the population of Mudiyah in the valiant Abyan Governorate. Raised placards called for an end to the armed aggression and continuing attacks on the population. They also called for the military units to be withdrawn to their bases in compliance with the Military Council's decisions. The demonstrators emphasized that the Armed Forces are the property of the people and protectors of the revolution's gains and national unity. They stressed the need for commitment to the accord document and that they wanted legitimacy, the constitution, and the democratic choice to be consolidated in the interest of national unity.
