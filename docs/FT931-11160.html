930204 FT 04 FEB 93 / Kabul clashes as UN retreats Guerrillas bombarded Kabul with rockets yesterday, killing 14 people, as mediators tried to negotiate a peace between President Burhanuddin Rabbani and the fundamentalist leader, Mr Gulbuddin Hekmatyar, Reuter reports from Kabul. The UN said it had withdrawn staff from Jalalabad and Kandahar a day after gunmen killed three UN staff and a Dutch consultant.
