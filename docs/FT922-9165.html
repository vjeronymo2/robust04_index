920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / International Company News: Hughes may buy General Dynamics missiles unit HUGHES Aircraft, the aerospace subsidiary of General Motors, is negotiating to buy the missiles operations of General Dynamics, the large US defence contractor, writes Martin Dickson in New York. General Motors confirmed the discussions yesterday, 24 hours after General Dynamics told its annual meeting it planned to sell off the business and a deal could be close. Raytheon, another large US missiles manufacturer, has also acknowledged holding discussions with General Dynamics, but added that General Dynamics was currently negotiating 'with another party'. Hughes makes tactical guided missiles. General Dynamics' missiles division, with annual sales of around Dollars 1.3bn, makes tactical products.
