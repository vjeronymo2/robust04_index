BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow May 27 TASS -- The State Duma Refused to follow the agenda for Friday and consider the draft federal budget for 1994. According to Mikhail Zadornov, chairman of the Duma Committee for the Budget, Taxes, Banks and Finances, his committee received the bulk of 300 amendments to the budget on Thursday afternoon and had no time to prepare for the Friday session. The Duma will resume its discussion of the draft budget on June 8.
