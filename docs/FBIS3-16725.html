Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, March 18 (SNARK)--Russia must become the guarantee of recognition of the republic of Nagorno-Karabakh, Nikolay Lysenko, the chairman of the National-Republican Party of Russia, said today in Yerevan. Commenting the results of the visit to Stepanakert, Lysenko said "According to the international regulations Karabakh has a right to establish sovereign republic." On his words Armenia, Russia, Karabakh are connected by geographical strategic interests. He said "the policy, pursued by Turkey in Transcaucasia, must be considered as directed against Russia. The aggression of Azerbaijan against Karabakh wouldn't be possible if Turkey didn't support Azerbaijan." According to him there are a few people in the State Duma that agree with national-republicans in the question concerning to Armenia and Karabakh. Today the delegation of National-republican party leave for Moscow.
