BFN [Text] Chisinau, BASAPRES, 23/06/1994 -- Today, the Moldovan Parliament passed (67 votes, against 12) the Program on Governmental activity for 1994-97. The opposition factions comprising the People's Christian Democratic Front Alliance and Peasants and Intellectuals Block voted against. Premier Andrei Sangheli stated during the parliamentary session that the main strategic objective of the Program is Moldova's economic stabilization in 1994-95, the accomplishment of reform on a large scale, and the assurance of economic growth that would better people's welfare in 1996-97.
