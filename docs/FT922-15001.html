920403 FT 03 APR 92 / International Company News: RBC plans trust expansion THE Royal Bank of Canada plans to become a leading force in the country's trust services business through its newly-acquired International Trust, which is to be renamed Royal Bank of Canada Trust. The company has CDollars 4bn (Dollars 3.3bn) assets under administration, and the bank may make another acquisition before long. It will offer fiduciary services to wealthy individuals across Canada and also specialised services to corporation. Under Canada's new federal financial institutions regime, banks can operate trust companies. Two other Big Six chartered banks also plan trust affiliates.
