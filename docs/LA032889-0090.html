March 28, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

THE PREPS;

EL TORO DEBUTS, LAGUNA BEACH LEADS IN POLL

The El Toro High School boys' volleyball team made its first appearance in the Orange County high school volleyball coaches poll, ranked No. 7.

Laguna Beach holds the top ranking, as it has all season.

The coaches' poll:

1. Laguna Beach (Pacific Coast): 4-0

3. University (Sea View): 3-0
