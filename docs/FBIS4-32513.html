BFN [Report by Marica Lalovic] [Text] We learned from the republic of Srpska army today that it has no information on any NATO aircraft action on Serbian positions or any part of the territory of our republic yesterday, today, or last night, as asserted by UN spokesman Major Rob Annink at a news conference in Sarajevo this morning. He said that the members of UNPROFOR [UN Protection Force] called for support by NATO aircraft, which fired warning missiles at the positions of the Serbian fighters and the Serbian territory. At the same time, the Army has denied the statement by Rob Annink that Serb forces yesterday attacked a Danish humanitarian convoy near Teocak. Despite the fact that the convoy was not announced, it arrived on the Muslims territory without problems, it unloaded humanitarian aid, and went back, again without problems.
