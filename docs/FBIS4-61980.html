BFN [Text] Despite the declaration of a cease-fire as of 0000 on 9 May, the Armenian side launched new combat operations in the Agdamskiy Rayon sector of the front line at 0900 today. Following a preparatory artillery bombardment, the enemy attacked Akhmadagaly in Agdamskiy Rayon. Armored vehicles supported the onslaught. Violent clashes are taking place around that town at the present time. Uninterrupted positional fighting has continued in Gorenboyskiy, Terterskiy, and Fizulinskiy Rayons during the past two days. The Armenians suffered many casualties in their unsuccessful attacks against Gorenboy. The enemy units lost more than 50 men. They abandoned 12 machine guns and grenade launchers and fled from the battlefield. The border observation posts in Kedabekskiy Rayon were fired on from Armenia last night. The enemy also fired on our positions in the Omer Pass region in the northern part of Kelbadzharskiy Rayon.
