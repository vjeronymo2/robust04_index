920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Forward Grp stages second-half recovery A recovery in the second half enabled Forward Group, the USM-traded printed circuit board manufacturer, to report profits of Pounds 622,000 pre-tax for the 12 months to January 31. The outcome compared with Pounds 955,000, including exceptional credits of Pounds 118,000, and was, according to Mr Ray Chamberlain, chairman, a 'most satisfactory' result given the economic climate. Interest charges fell from Pounds 399,000 to Pounds 231,000. The current year had continued the improvements of the second half, he said and trading subsidiaries had started the year 'materially better' although there was still 'fierce pressure' on prices reflecting competition. Turnover declined 11 per cent to Pounds 9.33m (Pounds 10.5m). Earnings per share emerged at 5.69p (8.95p) and a proposed final dividend of 1.5p brings the total to 2.5p (2.2p).
