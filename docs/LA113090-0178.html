November 30, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

JIM MARSHALL FACES DRUG CHARGE

Former Minnesota Viking Jim Marshall was scheduled to be arraigned today on suspicion of cocaine possession, a prosecutor said.

Assistant St. Louis County Attorney Vern Swanum confirmed that Marshall was in custody at the St. Louis County Jail after being arrested Thursday.

Marshall was expected to be charged this afternoon with one count of third-degree cocaine possession, Swanum said.

Further details on Marshall's arrest weren't immediately available.
