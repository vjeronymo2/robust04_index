930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / International Company News: Yokohama Rubber slips 6.5% for year YOKOHAMA Rubber, the rubber products producer which is Japan's second-largest tyremaker, yesterday reported a 6.5 per cent fall in pre-tax profits to Y11.9bn (Dollars 102.5m) in the year to December on sales down 2.8 per cent to Y286bn. The company said it had been hit by a slowdown in the automotive industry, although sales of higher-value tyres, such as radials, had increased.
