Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] PRC media carried the following reports on PRC leadership activities from 4 March through 16 March. The source is noted in parentheses after each report. Jiang Zemin, Chen Yun, Others Condole Liu Ningyi's Death -- The remains of Liu Ningyi, late vice chairman of the Standing Committee of the Third National People's Congress, was cremated in Beijing on 4 March. Hu Jintao, Wei Jianxing, Ren Jianxin, Ni Zhifu, Wang Guangying, and Zhao Puchu paid homage to his remains at the Babaoshan Cemetery for Revolutionaries. Jiang Zemin, Chen Yun, Qiao Shi, Li Ruihuan, Liu Huaqing, Rong Yiren, Li Tieying, Wei Jianxin, Bo Yibo, Song Renqiong, and Song Jian expressed their condolences to Liu Ningyi's family after his death. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0839 GMT 4 Mar 94) Li Lanqing Greets Forum on Political Education for Students -- A forum on the first publication of SHI SHI [CURRENT EVENTS] magazine and education in current events and policies for middle school students was held on 4 March. Vice Premier Li Lanqing cabled a message to the forum. He hoped that "leaders at all levels and educational workers will pay more attention to and constantly improve the political class at schools, and intensify education in national conditions, with the focus on current events, policies, and situations, to the large number of students, so as to make contributions to cultivating more conscious workers for the socialist cause." (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1217 GMT 4 Mar 94) Li Ruihuan Addresses CPPCC Meeting -- The fifth Standing Committee meeting of the Eighth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC] closed in Beijing on 5 March. Chairman Li Ruihuan spoke at the meeting which was chaired by Vice Chairman Ye Xuanping. Also attending today's meeting were Vice Chairmen Wu Xueqian, Yang Rudai, Wang Zhaoguo, and others. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1248 GMT 5 Mar 94) Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, Others Inscribe for Heroic Soldier -- Many state and military leaders recently wrote inscriptions for heroic soldier Xu Honggang. Jiang Zemin's inscription reads: "Learn from Comrade Xu Honggang." Li Peng's message says: "Learn from Comrade Xu Honggang, a Heroic Soldier Who Takes up the Cudgels for a Just Cause and Defies Brute Force." Liu Huaqing's inscription says: "Serve as a Good Soldier of the Party and the People, with Comrade Xu Honggang as the Example." Zhang Zhen inscribed: "Learn from Comrade Xu Honggang and Carry Forward the Fine Traditions of the Red Army." (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0209 GMT 6 Mar 94) Li Lanqing Meets Women's Representatives -- Vice Premier Li Lanqing met with some members of a national model workers report group at Zhongnanhai in the afternoon of 8 March. Li Lanqing extended his congratulations and respects to them. He said: "As the representatives of outstanding Chinese women, you have made remarkable contributions to your work posts. I hope you will make persistent efforts to continue carrying forward your fine character and work style." He also inquired in detail about their work and their life. (Beijing Central Television Program One Network in Mandarin 1100 GMT 8 Mar 94) Li Lanqing Meets Hong Kong Entrepreneur -- Vice Premier Li Lanqing met Hu Yingxiang, member of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and general manager of the Hehe Group of Hong Kong, and his party of four at Zhongnanhai on 9 March. Li Lanqing said during the meeting: "China is constantly deepening reforms, opening itself wider and wider to the outside world, and improving its investment environment. We are stepping up the training of personnel of all specialties necessary to the establishment of a socialist market economic structure." Hu Yingxiang briefed Li on the business of the Hehe Group. Wan Shaofen, deputy director of the Central United Front Work Department, was present at the meeting. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0837 GMT 9 Mar 94) Wu Bangguo Meets Model Worker -- Wu Bangguo, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and secretary of the Shanghai Municipal CPC Committee, and Shanghai Mayor Huang Ju received model worker Bao Qifan on 9 March. Wu Bangguo called on the people in Shanghai to learn from Bao Qifan's socialist pioneering spirit. (Shanghai People's Radio Network in Mandarin 2300 GMT 9 Mar 94) Jiang Zemin Inscribes Titles of Books on Wang Zhen -- A forum on the publication of documentary, pictorial, and commemorative specials respectively entitled "General Wang Zhen," "Wang Zhen," and "Comrade Wang Zhen -- A Model of Respecting Teachers and Valuing Education" was held in Beijing on 12 March. Jiang Zemin inscribed all these titles. Li Lanqing, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and vice premier, wrote a preface for the commemorative special "Comrade Wang Zhen -- A Model of Respecting Teachers and Valuing Education." Attending the forum were Li Lanqing, Li Tieying, Song Renqiong, Lei Jieqiong, Tomur Dawamat, and others. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1054 GMT 12 Mar 94) Zhang Zhen, Other PLA Generals Plant Trees in Beijing -- Fifty- seven general-grade officers from the Central Military Commission, the three People's Liberation Army [PLA] general departments, and major PLA and armed police units in Beijing took part in planting trees in Beijing's suburbs on 12 March. They included Zhang Zhen, vice chairman of the Central Military Commission; and Chi Haotian, Zhang Wannian, Yu Yongbo and Fu Quanyou, members of the Central Military Commission. It is Tree-Planting Day today. The all- people's voluntary tree-planting campaign, initiated by Comrade Deng Xiaoping, has entered its 13th year. According to information from the department concerned, the PLA has planted more than 400 million trees over the last decade or so. "On the PLA's achievements in this regard, Fu Quanyou, who is also director of the General Logistics Department and chairman of the All-Army Greening Committee, said happily that the greening work is an important task to benefit future generations, and covering the country with trees is the common aspiration of all PLA officers and men." (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0959 GMT 12 Mar 94) Liu Huaqing, Zou Jiahua, Song Jian Write Inscriptions -- "The China National Defense Scientific and Technological Information Center entered its 35th year of glorious service today. Liu Huaqing, member of the Standing Committee of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and vice chairman of the Central Military Commission, wrote an inscription to greet its anniversary -- `Step up scientific and technological intelligence work to serve the modernization of national defense.' Vice premier Zou Jiahua and state councillor Song Jian also wrote inscriptions for the occasion." (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1348 GMT 14 Mar 94) Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, Others Watch Modern Drama -- "Jiang Zemin, General Secretary of the CPC Central Committee and State President, and Premier Li Peng called on the literary and art workers and watched a modern drama "Ga La Alley" at the People's Art Theater in Beijing in the evening of 15 March. They were accompanied by Ding Guangen, Li Tieying, and Chen Xitong in watching the performance." Jiang Zemin and Li Peng met with the playwright and the director, as well as the actors and actresses, of the drama. Jiang Zemin said: "Literature and art should strive to reflect reality, the rich and colorful life and the main trend of the present times. I hope that you will make persistent efforts to create more literary and art works with realistic themes." Li Peng said: "I was moved by the drama. The people's government should perform more deeds for the people." (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1452 GMT 15 Mar 94) Wu Bangguo, Huang Ju Send Gifts to Well-Known Painter -- A centennial birthday party was held in Shanghai on 16 March for Liu Haisu, a well-known fine art educator and painter. While Wu Bangguo, secretary of Shanghai Municipal CPC Committee, and Shanghai mayor Huang Ju are attending the Second Sessions of the Eighth National People's Congress and the Eighth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference in Beijing, they have asked Shanghai deputy party secretary Chen Zhili to forward their gifts to him. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1009 GMT 16 Mar 94)
