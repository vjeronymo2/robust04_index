Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri received a telephone call from U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher this evening. Christopher briefed al-Hariri on the preliminary results of the Geneva talks today between U.S. President Bill Clinton and Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad. Al-Hariri's sources said the U.S. secretary affirmed to the prime minister that the two presidents discussed the Lebanese issue seriously and that this year will be one of prosperity and peace for the Middle East. The sources added that Christopher also affirmed to al-Hariri the positive results of the U.S.-Syrian summit. [Paris AFP in English at 2021 GMT on 16 January adds the following: "The prime minister told AFP that Christopher telephoned him from the plane on his way back to the United States, stressing that the Lebanese question was discussed at length in Geneva. "Hariri said he was optimistic."]
