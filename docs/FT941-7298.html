940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / Technology (Worth Watching): Light and rugged workstation Tadpole Technology, based in the UK's Cambridge Science Park, has developed a portable workstation aimed at engineering and commercial users. The light, rugged Sparcbook 3 notebook computer workstations cost Pounds 7,650 each (Pounds 5,350 with mono screen and no telecoms link). Tadpole Technology: UK, 0223 566200
