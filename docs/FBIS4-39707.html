BFN [Text] Prospects for development, privatization, price formation, and other issues were discussed at a Republican seminar- conference on trade issues. Head of the Belarusian Government Vyachaslaw Kebich delivered a report. Speaking about prices for goods, the prime minister stated that the government failed to stabilize them thus far. The key reason is an exterior factor, however, the minor reason is that trade also had its own expenditures. Many middlemen -- boosting prices -- have emerged. Their numbers can and should be minimized. According to the prime minister, the government should bridle price formation through the Department for Prices. The head of the government also met yesterday with the working collective of the Kirov factory.
