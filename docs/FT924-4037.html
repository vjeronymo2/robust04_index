921204 FT 04 DEC 92 / Workers call off steel strike WORKERS at the Spartan Redheugh steelrolling mill in Gateshead, Tyne and Wear, voted yesterday to accept new terms and conditions and return to work, ending their five-month strike. The 2-1 vote at a meeting of 88 of the remaining 96 strikers out of an original 116, came after they were promised that job cuts in the package would be by voluntary, not compulsory, redundancy.
