Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] This morning at 0645 [0545 GMT] the Bosnian Serb air force launched a missile attack on the Muslim-held Bratstvo factory in Novi Travnik. Two of the three planes which flew in to Novi Travnik and returned via Vlasic and Donji Vakuf fired four missiles. Thick smoke is still rising above the factory, the political department of Herceg-Bosna's Defense Ministry said.
