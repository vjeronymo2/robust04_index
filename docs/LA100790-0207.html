October 7, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CITY ATTY. MYERS IS 'COMPETENT, DECENT'

I commend Julio Moran on his article "Political Brawl Over Homeless," Sept. 27.

He did an excellent job of describing the situation in Santa Monica.

As a 14-year resident, I would like to add my own observation about City Atty. Robert Myers. He is a very competent attorney and a person who behaves in a strikingly decent manner which transcends politics. He has always put his reputation where his beliefs are. One cannot help but admire such an individual. There are few in public office.

NAN LEWIS

Santa Monica
