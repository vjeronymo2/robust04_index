Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Ilyas al-Hirawi chaired an expanded security meeting at the B'abda Palace this morning. Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri, Defense Minister Muhsin Dallul, Interior Minister Bisharah Mirhij, and security officers were present. At the end of the meeting, Interior Minister Mirhij made the following statement: [Begin recording] [Mirhij] The meeting, which was held at the presidential palace this morning under the chairmanship of the president, was devoted to discussion of all aspects of the security situation in the country. The conferees dealt with all topics pertaining to security throughout the country. The conferees decided to instruct all security services to adopt measures that would safeguard the public security in the country and the citizens' rights in Lebanon. All security topics on the agenda were discussed in depth. We also discussed the elections in the north and construction violations. We touched on issues on the south as well as all issues related to security. [Unidentified correspondent] As for the repeated Israeli attacks, can we know what measures were taken at today's meeting to deter the repeated Israeli attacks on the south? [Mirhij] The whole Lebanese state is in a state of resistance. It rejects occupation and mobilizes all its efforts for the sake of ensuring the withdrawal of the Israeli enemy from our land in the south. We do not approve of any operation that harms the state's responsibility in south Lebanon. [Correspondent] Your Excellency, concerning the bombing of the Phalangist Party headquarters, have the investigations revealed anything in this regard? [Mirhij] The investigations into the bombing of the Phalangist Party headquarters are still going on. The Lebanese judiciary is now in charge of the case and it continues its work in this regard. [end recording]
