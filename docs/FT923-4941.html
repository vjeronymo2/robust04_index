920903 FT 03 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Milder winter helps Grafton advance Grafton Group, the Dublin-based builders' merchant and DIY concern, lifted pre-tax profits 14 per cent to IPounds 1.85m (Pounds 1.75m), in the six months to June 30. Turnover improved to IPounds 48.7m (IPounds 45.7m) generating trading profits of IPounds 2.55m (IPounds 2.17m). Interest charges rose to IPounds 698,000 (IPounds 553,000). The interim dividend is stepped up to 2.75p (2.5p), payable from earnings of 8.6p (7.5p) per share.
