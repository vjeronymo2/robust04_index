BFN [Text] Geneva, April 11 (AFP) -- Bosnian Serbs on Monday continued shelling the mainly Moslem town of Gorazde, reportedly injuring some 200 people, a UN High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) spokeswoman said here. Shells hit a school that was being used as a refugee centre and some 200 people were said to be injured, Christiane Berthiaume said, citing reports from four UNHCR personnel in the Moslem enclave in Bosnia-Herzegovina.
