August 5, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

WOODLAND HILLS ASTROS WIN TOURNAMENT GAME

UCLA players Adam Schulhofer and David Tokheim had two hits each as the Woodland Hills Astros defeated a team representing Mexico, 12-2, Saturday to advance to the third round of the U.S. Open Baseball tournament at Galvin Field in Ontario.

The game was stopped after five innings because of the 10-run rule.

Schulhofer had a double and a home run and Tokheim drove in two runs with a pair of singles. The Astros will play a Las Vegas team today at noon.
