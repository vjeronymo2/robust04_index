BFN [Text] Baghdad, 16 Apr (INA) -- The aircraft of the U.S.-led anti-Iraq coalition dropped two flares in the afternoon of last Tuesday [12 April] on the Ba'shiqah region in Ninawah Governorate, 400 km north of Baghdad. INA's correspondent in the governorate cited informed sources as saying the two flares burned out before reaching the ground without causing any damage. It is worth mentioning that the Ninawa Governorate includes the archaeological city of al-Hadar. The first al-Hadar festival opened on 14 April with the participation of dozens of Arab and foreign art groups.
