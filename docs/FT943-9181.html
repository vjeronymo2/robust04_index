940812 FT 12 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Creston Land buys four business centres Creston Land & Estates, the commercial property auditor, is buying Industrial & Commercial Estates, a new company with four freehold properties. Creston is acquiring ICE for a nominal consideration and ICE is funding the Pounds 10.6m purchase of the properties, including outstanding debts, from the receivers of City Land by way of 100 per cent non-recourse loans.
