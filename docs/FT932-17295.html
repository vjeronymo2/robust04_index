930401 FT 01 APR 93 / UK Company News: Norman Hay loses Pounds 3.05m SHARES IN Norman Hay, the engineering group, fell 8p to 33p after increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 3.05m were announced for the year to December 31. In 1991 losses amounted to Pounds 2.16m. The result was after an exceptional restructuring charge of Pounds 1.47m (Pounds 2.17m). Directors said the losses were greater than anticipated because moving the business had coincided with and been compounded by recession, the company had been obliged to meet new environmental regulations, and, partly because of those requirements, the project had overrun by about six months. Turnover fell to Pounds 9.52m (Pounds 10.7m). Losses per share deepened from 11.34p to 19.75p and there is no dividend (1.14p).
