940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / UK Company News: John I Jacobs to purchase Sheertruck John I Jacobs, the shipowning and shipbroking group, has agreed in principle to acquire Sheertruck International, a vehicle transportation company. Initial consideration for the acquisition will be via the issue of 7.8m shares. Further consideration of up to a maximum 2.2m shares is dependent on profits. Sheertruck, which has a fleet of 58 transport vehicles and capacity to transport up to 120,000 motor vehicles a year, is effectively owned 50:50 by Mr Michael Kingshott, co-founder of Sally Line, and Mr Colin Parry Williams, Sheertruck's managing director. Mr Kingshott has joined the board of Jacobs as chief executive. At the same time he has agreed to buy 700,000 Jacobs shares (3.2 per cent) from Salenia. Shares closed up 5p at 61p. See People
