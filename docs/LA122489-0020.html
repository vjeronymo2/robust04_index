December 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CRITICS' CHOICE: LODZ GHETTO: INSIDE A COMMUNITY UNDER SIEGE COMPILED AND EDITED BY ALAN ADELSON AND ROBERT LAPIDES (VIKING: $29.95; 526 PP.)

"Let anyone who still dares to deny that the Nazi genocide took place, let anyone who is still tempted to trivialize the suffering of 6 million Jewish men, women and childen, let anyone who allows himself the luxury of forgetting pick up a copy of 'Lodz Ghetto' and stare into the human face of the Holocaust." Jonathan Kirsch
