931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / Cost of law and order nears Pounds 1bn Northern Ireland Office spending on law and order will edge closer to Pounds 1bn next year, figures released yesterday show. Proposed spending for 1994-95 will amount to Pounds 931m, compared with Pounds 902m for 1993-94 and Pounds 912m pencilled in for next year under original plans. Sir Patrick Mayhew, the Northern Ireland secretary, said next year's total - which excludes the cost of the heavy army presence in the province - reflected the priority he attached to defeating terrorism. He said: 'It saddens me to see the substantial resources which, year after year, are consumed in our fight against terrorism.'
