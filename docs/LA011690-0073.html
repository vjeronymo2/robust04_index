January 16, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski earned $57 when Hour Finder won the third race after Bat Out Of Hell was disqualifed. The original bet was a parlay from Hour Finder to Georgeous in the eighth, but Georgeous was scratched. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $1,143.
