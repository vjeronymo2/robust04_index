BFN [Text] Beijing, May 3 (YONHAP) -- Seoul opens a culture center in Beijing on May 6 to promote Chinese people's awareness of South Korea. The "Korean Culture and Public Information Center" is located on the seventh floor of the Beijing International Conference Center in the Chinese capital. It will be equipped with a mini-library, and visual, audio and recreation rooms to serve as sort of a data bank for a wide range of information. Seoul and Beijing normalized relations in August 1992.
