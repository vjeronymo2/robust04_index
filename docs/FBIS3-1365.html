Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] President Yoweri Museveni of Uganda has proposed a regional conference to discuss the escalating tribal violence in Burundi. Addressing the press in Kampala on his return home from a brief visit to Tanzania, Mr. Museveni suggested that the leaders of Tanzania, Uganda, Kenya, Zaire, and Zambia hold a meeting immediately to find a lasting solution to the Burundi conflict. He said the formerly minor historical issue in Burundi had over the decades been complicated by mismanagement. The Ugandan leader blamed some foreign countries for the mismanagement of affairs in both Burundi and Rwanda. [passage omitted]
