930410 FT 10 APR 93 / UK Company News: Bilton improves to Pounds 17.7m tax profits ahead from Pounds 17.4m to Pounds 17.7m for 1992. This followed a 7 per cent rise to Pounds 8.88m at the interim stage. Turnover on trading activities fell from Pounds 17m to Pounds 10.4m while profit on ordinary activities, which included trading and investment, showed a marginal decline from Pounds 21.7m to Pounds 21.6m. A partial professional valuation of the group's portfolio showed a total of Pounds 352.22m against Pounds 347.27m. The estimated net asset value per share (excluding the trading division's operations) was 739p, compared with 731p. The total dividend is increased to 18.9p (18p) with a final of 13.23p. Earnings per share were 28.3p (27.7p).
