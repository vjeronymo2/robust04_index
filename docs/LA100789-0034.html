October 7, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Easy Goer, who has won four consecutive races beginning with the Belmont Stakes, is a 3-5 favorite in today's $1 million-added Jockey Club Gold Cup at Belmont Park in Elmont, N.Y.

Prized, who upset Sunday Silence in the Swaps July 23 at Hollywood Park, is a 5-1 third choice for the 1 1/2-mile race. Forever Silver is 4-1 in the seven-horse field.
