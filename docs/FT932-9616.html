930513 FT 13 MAY 93 / London rail hopes HOPES of saving CrossRail, the Pounds 1.7bn London rail project, increased yesterday when London First, the pro-London lobbying organisation, claimed two reports for the government supported the line. London First said a financial report from SG Warburg, the merchant bank, suggested that the private sector might contribute up to Pounds 1bn, while a construction report from Bechtel, the US engineering group, found no flaws in the plans.
