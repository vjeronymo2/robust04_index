940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / UK Company News: BZW invites bids for Burmah shares BZW plans to sell the shares it has agreed to purchase under its cash offer in respect of Burmah Castrol's enhanced share alternative through a strike price auction. On Tuesday, BZW received acceptances of its cash offer in respect of 46.8m ordinary shares, representing 26 per cent of elections under the enhanced share alternative.
