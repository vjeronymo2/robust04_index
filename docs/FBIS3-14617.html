Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ahvaz (Khuzestan), March 29, IRNA -- Iraqi military forces arrested a number of villagers around Sulaymaniyah in the northeast on charges involvement in an attack on Iraqi Army personnel near the city. A source close to Iraqi mujahideen said today that Iraqi forces also destroyed houses of villagers and set ablaze their farms. He added that the villagers in retaliation killed a number of Iraqi forces. The source neither clarified the date of the recent arrest nor the time when the clashes flared up around Sulaymaniyah.
