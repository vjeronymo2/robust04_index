BFN [Text] The parliamentary Functional Group has asked all sides, particularly security forces and demonstrrators, to exercise self-restraint. Several members of the group made the appeal during a news conference in Jakarta today. On the occasion, Abdullah Alatas Fahmi, chairman of the Department of Information, Publication, and Mass Media of the Functional Group Central Executive Council, asked the security forces to handle demonstrations proportionally and not execessively. On the other hand, the demonstrators were asked to exercise self-restraint in order not to complicate the situation. Meanwhile, Abu Hassan Sazili, vice chairman of the parliamentary Functional Group, stressed the need for all sides to coolly respond to the situation in the wake of the license revocation of several publications. Sugeng Wijaya, member of parliamentary Commission I [Roman numeral one, in charge of foreign affairs, defense, security, and information] reminded the press circles not to be afraid of carrying out their duties and roles if they are carried out in accordance with the journalistic ethics.
