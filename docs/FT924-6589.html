921120 FT 20 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Henderson Strata net assets static The net asset value of Henderson Strata Investments showed a marginal improvement - from 185.3p to 186.3p - over the 12 months to October 31. The investment trust produced attributable revenue of Pounds 221,000, against Pounds 237,000, for earnings of 1.45p (1.56p) per share. The single distribution for the year is maintained at 1.4p.
