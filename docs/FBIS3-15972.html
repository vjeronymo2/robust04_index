Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Leonid Vorontsov and Aleksey Korinenko] [Text] Berlin, 11 Mar -- Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev's visit to Germnay has been described by the heads of the military departments of the two countries as "very successful and friendly." Speaking today at a concluding joint news conference in Berlin, Pavel Grachev and Volker Ruhe stressed that the accords and documents signed during the visit have laid the foundation for the development of cooperation between the two countries in the military sphere after the completion of the withdrawal of Russian troops from Germany. "An opportunity has arisen, which must not be missed under any circumstances," the Russian minister said. "This is a chance to considerably improve bilateral relations in all spheres. Regarding the military sphere, many practical measures have been planned and both sides are ready to carry them out in full."
