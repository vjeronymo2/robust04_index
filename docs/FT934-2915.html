931211 FT 11 DEC 93 / World News in Brief: Freed Britons reunited with families Two Britons freed from an Iraqi jail arrived in Britain last night. Paul Ride had been reunited in Amman with his two-year-old son William (above) and wife Julie, and Michael Wainwright with members of his family, after the intervention of former prime minister Edward Heath. A third Briton freed, Simon Dunn, was travelling with his parents to their home in Dubai.
