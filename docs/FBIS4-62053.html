BFN [Unattributed report under the "Official" rubric] [Text] By an edict of the Russian Federation president, Yuriy Ivanovich Seliverstov has been released from his post as head of administration of the city of Belgorod in Belgorod Oblast in connection with his election as chairman of Belgorod Oblast Assembly of National Representatives (Point 5 of Article 29 of the Russian Federation Labor Law Code). The Belgorod Oblast head of administration has been instructed to submit a candidacy for the post of acting head of administration of Belgorod city in Belgorod Oblast within the next seven days.
