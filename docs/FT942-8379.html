940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / UK Company News: York Waterworks 11% ahead at Pounds 2.6m York Waterworks, the water supplier, returned profits of Pounds 2.56m pre-tax for the 12 months ended March 31, an improvement of 11 per cent over 1992's Pounds 2.3m. Turnover of Pounds 7.79m compared with Pounds 7.3m. Earnings per share emerged at 26.8p (24.2p) and a proposed final dividend of 6.7p makes a 10p (9.25p) total.
