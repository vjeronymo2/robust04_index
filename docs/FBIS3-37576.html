Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow February 18 TASS -- The State Duma today endorsed its following work agenda: meetings of the chamber's council will henceforth be held every Monday, Tuesday and Thursday. It is planned to hold meetings of factions and parliamentary groups twice a week -- on Tuesdays and Thursdays. Parliamentary committees and commissions will work on Mondays and Thursdays. Parliamentary hearings will be held on Tuesdays. Plenary meetings of the State Duma will be held as before twice a week -- on Wednesdays and Fridays. The "government hour" will be held on the same days, as well as political debates, including statements by factions, parliamentary groups and committees, and speeches by individual parliament members.
