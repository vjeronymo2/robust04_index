Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] An unexpected change has taken place in Antelco [National Telecommunications Administration]. According to Decree No. 1834, engineer Miguel Horacio Gini Espinola was appointed today as Antelco board of directors chairman, replacing Colonel Eduardo Yasuo Kishi Ichikawa, a general staff school graduate, who tendered his resignation from the post. As a matter of fact, these changes within Antelco further aggravated the already tense relations between President Juan Carlos Wasmosy and [Colorado Party President] Blas N. Riquelme. It was reported that the Colorado Party president challenged the removal of his brother-in-law, Jose Luis Yanez, from the Antelco board of directors.
