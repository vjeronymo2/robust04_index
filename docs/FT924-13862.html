921013 FT 13 OCT 92 / Romania re-elects President Iliescu Romania's President Ion Iliescu has been re-elected with a comfortable majority in a peaceful ballot which foreign monitors described as democratic and free, Reuter reports from Bucharest. He won 60.5 per cent of the vote, after ballots were counted from 88 per cent of the polling stations, in a second round contest with Mr Emil Constantinescu, of the Democratic Convention centrist opposition, who got 39.5 per cent.
