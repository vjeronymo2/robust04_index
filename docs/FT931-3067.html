930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / Observer: Green handcuffs Still on the subject of green shoots that have not been spotted before, Malcolm Pike of Manchester lawyers Addleshaw Sons & Latham reports a noticeable business turnaround. Having spent the last couple of years doing little else but negotiate individual redundancy packages, over the past couple of months he has come across three firms preparing to take legal action to prevent key staff being poached by competitors.
