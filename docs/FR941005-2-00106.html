DEPARTMENT OF LABOR Employment and Training Administration [TA&hyph;W&hyph;30,303] Pathfinder Mines Corporation; Shirley Basin, WY; Notice of Termination of Investigation Pursuant to Section 221 of the Trade Act of 1974, an investigation was initiated on September 12, 1994, in response to a worker petition which was filed on August 30, 1994, on behalf of workers at Pathfinder Mines Corporation, Shirley Basin, Wyoming. The petitioner has requested that the petition be withdrawn. Consequently, further investigation in this case would serve no purpose, and the investigation has been terminated. Signed in Washington, D.C. this 26th day of September, 1994. Victor J. Trunzo, Program Manager, Policy and Reemployment Services, Office of Trade Adjustment Assistance.
