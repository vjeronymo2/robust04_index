940708 FT 08 JUL 94 / International Company News: Nippon Shinpan in loans move Nippon Shinpan, the consumer credit company, plans to sell securitised loans in the form of bonds as early as August, an official said, Reuter reports from Tokyo. It is the first such sale by a Japanese firm. The company is planning to issue Y20bn to Y30bn (Dollars 204m-Dollars 306m) in eurobonds backed by its automobile loans through a special-purpose company. Goldman Sachs will lead manage the bonds. The company said it was taking this step in order to vary its means of fund-raising.
