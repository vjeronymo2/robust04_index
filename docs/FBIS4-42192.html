BFN [Report signed PVB: "Kurdish Office in Brussels"] [Excerpt] An International Bureau of Solidarity with the (Kurdish) Democratic Party (DEP) was opened in Brussels on Thursday [26 May]. The Turkish Government threatens to ban the DEP. Five DEP parliamentarians have been accused of treason and separatism and might be sentenced to death. Yasar Kaya, DEP honorary president and founder, is the owner of the OZGUR GUNDEM (Free Agenda), which ceased publication after several trials and harassments. Brussels Bureau Director Kaya says that the Bureau aims at defending the rights of his people, seeking international solidarity, and serving as an information center. [passage omitted]
