Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Boris Krivoshey and Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow January 6 TASS -- A Russian high-ranking official will arrive in Latvia next week to discuss a draft agreement on the status of the disputed radar station in Skrunda, Grigoriy Karasin, Russian Foreign Ministry spokesman, told a briefing in Moscow on Thursday. Sergey Zotov, who heads the Russian delegation to the talks with Latvia, will arrive in Riga on January 10 to discuss a draft agreement on the radar station after the Russian troops have left Latvian territory. Karasin said that the absence of such an agreement hampers the signing of the prepared main document which sets the deadline of the troop withdrawal and two other agreements on social welfare of the Army personnel and the retired military and members of their families.
