Karpov] [Text] The TMK-3 trenching machine is designed to dig trenches in unfrozen and frozen ground in field fortification activities for troop positions. Its complement includes: A K-703MV (Kirovets) wheeled tractor, a non-bucket type rotary trenching device, and a turning bulldozer blade. Cutters with wear-resistant plates made of VK-15 alloy are installed on the bucket wheel. The bucket wheel unloads with the aid of two tray-type unloading devices on both sides of the trench being dug. The maximum dump of dirt from the center line of the trench is up to two meters. The equipment is controlled from the cab by means of a hydraulic drive. The GST-90 hydraulic transmission ensures an operating speed of 90 to 1600 meters/hour.
