940608 FT 08 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Ross sales to raise over Pounds 5m Ross Group, the consumer electronics and technical services group, is making two sales worth a total of about Pounds 5.5m. The moves mark a further focusing on core activities. Volex Accessories, a Hanson subsidiary, has acquired Traveller International, the travel accessories company, for about Pounds 4.15m, including stocks. Ross acquired Traveller, which made 1993 profits of Pounds 750,000 on sales of Pounds 2.2m, for Pounds 700,000 in March 1991. Ross also plans to sell In-Flight Supply Services (International) to Mr Ross Marks, the founder and an executive director of Ross. He will cease as an executive director becoming a non-executive and consultant. The consideration will be Pounds 225,000 cash on completion, a minimum royalty of Pounds 100,000 per annum for five years and a further sales-related payment up to a total of Pounds 250,000. Mr Marks will also assume responsibility for the borrowings of In-Flight, currently Pounds 350,000.
