BFN [Text] Kwangju, May 4 (YONHAP) -- Some 400 college students staged a surprise anti-U.S. demonstration near the American Center in this southwestern city at 5:30 A.M. Wednesday, declaring that "We oppose the United States creating a war atmosphere." The students, members of Namchongnyon, an activist organization comprising students from Chonnam National and Choson Universities as well as other schools in Kwangju and South Cholla Province, tried to storm into the American Center but were blocked by riot police firing tear-gas. A clash between the police and students wielding paint bottles and rocks ensued, injuring some 10 policemen and students who were taken to nearby hospitals for treatment. At 6:10, the students moved to the South Cholla police agency to demand the release of their arrested fellows, while throwing over 20 paint bottles at the agency building.
