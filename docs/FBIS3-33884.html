Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] An official spokesman has said in Jammu that Pakistan troops have fired about 36 rockets so far from across the border. At least 16 civilians, including three school children, were injured in (Digwar) village in Punch District. The spokesman said Pakistan's denial of firing rockets in the Punch and Rajauri sectors is another attempt to perpetuate falsehood and cover up its provocative actions and nefarious designs.
