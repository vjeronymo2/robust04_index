BFN [Text] Staff Lieutenant Colonel Muhammad Bashir Sulayman, the official spokesman of the Armed Forces, issued a statement today in which he referred to reports appearing in Western media about Israeli military support for the Sudan People's Liberation Army [SPLA] rebel movement and the visit of rebel John Garang to Israel in search of military and political support. The statement stressed that the military support for the two camps of the rebel movement was not new and that Israel has been one of the principal sources of supply to the rebel movement since the latter was founded.
