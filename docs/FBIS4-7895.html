BFN ["Bulgaria, Georgia Promote Trade, Economic Cooperation" -- BTA headline] [Text] Sofia, April 6 (BTA) -- The Bulgarian Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Trade Valentin Karabashev today received the First Deputy Foreign Minister of Georgia Mikhail Ukleba, said the Government's Press Office. They discussed the legal framework of bilateral trade and economic cooperation, stressing that the bilateral trade and economic cooperation draft accord has been initialed. The accord envisages the signature of agreements on air, sea and land transport cooperation, on the avoidance of double taxation and on reciprocal investment protection and promotion. A Bulgarian government delegation is scheduled to visit Tbilisi this June, said the Press Office. The Georgian delegation completed their four-day visit here today. In the course of the visit, the sides discussed matters of bilateral cooperation and the Black Sea Economic Zone. They signed a protocol on cooperation between the two Foreign Ministries. The Georgian diplomat delivered a message to Bulgarian President Zhelyu Zhelev by Georgian President Eduard Shevardnadze, expressing a wish to strengthen bilateral relations.
