BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondent Lev Ryabchikov for TASS] [Text] Simferopol May 5 TASS--No mine has been found in the building of the Crimean local government. By 2:00 on Thursday afternoon, field engineers of the Black Sea Fleet's 126th Coastal Guard Division completed inspection of the building but failed to detect the reported explosive device. The inspection was prompted by a telephone call this morning. An unidentified person said a mine had been planted in the government building, after which a government session was broken off and everybody was evacuated from the building.
