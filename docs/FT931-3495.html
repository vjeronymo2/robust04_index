930316 FT 16 MAR 93 / UK Company News: William Bedford losses rise to Pounds 0.27m With insufficient turnover to make use of its showrooms and ancillary services 1992 pre-tax losses at William Bedford, the antique dealer and restorer, increased from Pounds 210,000 to Pounds 272,000. The USM-quoted company added that unless there was an unforeseen increase in turnover the present year would again be difficult. Turnover was Pounds 1.57m (Pounds 1.63m). Losses per share were 5p (2.6p).
