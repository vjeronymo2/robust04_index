Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN [Text] On 25 February, inhabitants at Ap Village in Kang Meas District of Kompong Cham Province intercepted a group of Vietnamese communist puppet troops led by some generals and senior officers, who were rounding up the people's husbands and children to serve as soldiers and die in their place. The people killed six of them, including a general and a senior officer. We also seized two pistols and four AK rifles. And on 24 February, people and village guards ambushed a group of Vietnamese communist puppet troops from Chamka Leu District in Kompong Cham Province. The troops attempted to terrorize and massacre the people and plunder their property at Phkai Meas Village in the district. Four enemy soldiers were killed and eight wounded. We also seized a field radio and some documents.
