BFN [Text] Resolution by Kazakh President Nazarbayev on the state program for supporting and encouraging entrepreneurship in the Republic of Kazakhstan for 1994-96: In accordance with the Resolution No. 1655 of the Republic of Kazakhstan of 13 April 1994 "On the State Commission of the Republic of Kazakhstan for Supporting and Encouraging Entrepreneurship," I decree: 1. To approve the state program for supporting and encouraging entrepreneurship in the Republic of Kazakhstan in 1994-96. (enclosed [KAZTAG note; not further explained]) 2. The Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Kazakhstan should take the nessessary steps to fulfill the state program for supporting and encouraging entrepreneurship in the Republic of Kazakhstan in 1994-96, and within two weeks it should elaborate on and endorse a draft plan of measures and tasks aimed at implementing the program. If necessary, it should make annual adjustments to the plan of measures and tasks aimed at implementing the program. 3. Heads of regional, Almaty, and Leninsk city administrations should elaborate on specific measures aimed at implementing the state program for supporting and encouraging entrepreneurship in the Republic of Kazakhstan in 1994-96, which will take into consideration regional characteristics. [Signed] President Nazarbayev Almaty, [Dated] 10 June 1994.
