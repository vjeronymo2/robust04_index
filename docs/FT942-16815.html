940408 FT 08 APR 94 / Labour extends lead to 25 points Labour's lead over the Conservatives has risen to 25 points, a Gallup poll in The Daily Telegraph today shows. Although Tory support has risen to 26 per cent from 25 per cent in late February, Labour support is 51.5 per cent (48.5 per cent). The Liberal Democrats have fallen to 17.5 per cent (21 per cent) and other parties are on 4.5 per cent (5.5 per cent). Only 21 per cent are satisfied with Mr John Major while just 12 per cent approve of the government's record.
