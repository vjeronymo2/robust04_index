921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Acorn Computer back in black Acorn Computer Group, the computer maker in which Olivetti of Italy has a majority stake, started the year well, recording interim pre-tax profits of Pounds 511,000, compared with losses of Pounds 420,000. The result was helped by a fall in interest costs from Pounds 538,000 to Pounds 139,000. Turnover for the six months to July 3 was up 19 per cent at Pounds 21.2m, against Pounds 17.9m, and earnings per share were 0.8p (losses of 0.6p).
