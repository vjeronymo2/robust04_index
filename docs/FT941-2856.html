940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Ernest Green falls to Pounds 207,000 Profits of Ernest Green and Partners Holdings, the USM-traded structural, civil and environment engineering consultancy, declined from Pounds 302,000 to Pounds 207,000 pre-tax for the half year ended December 31. Turnover was virtually static at Pounds 3.66m (Pounds 3.64m). The share of profits of the associate rose to Pounds 119,000 (Pounds 83,000), while interest receivable slipped to Pounds 68,000 (Pounds 135,000). Earnings fell 1p to 1.8p, but the interim dividend is being maintained at 2.75p. Directors said the order book was improving and the group remained financially strong.
