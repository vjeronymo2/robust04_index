940920 FT 20 SEP 94 / International Company News: BZW commodities trust prospectus The pathfinder prospectus for the BZW Commodities Trust was published yesterday. The offer price is 100p per ordinary share and warrants are attached to the shares on a one for five basis, writes Antonia Sharpe. There is a minimum investment of Pounds 5,000. The offer closes on October 20 and dealings commence on the London Stock Exchange on October 27.
