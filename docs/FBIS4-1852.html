BFN [Text] Beijing, 4 Mar (XINHUA) -- Decree No. 150 of the People's Republic of China State Council: The "Provisions for Managing the Collection of Mineral Rights Fees," adopted by the State Council's sixth executive meeting on 29 June 1993, is hereby promulgated for enforcement beginning on 1 April 1994. [Signed] Premier Li Peng [Dated] 27 February 1994
