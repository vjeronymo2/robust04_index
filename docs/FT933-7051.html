930824 FT 24 AUG 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Ex-Lands EX-LANDS has received acceptances in respect of Pounds 6.87m nominal of the Pounds 15m issued under the open offer and placing of 7.5 per cent convertible unsecured loan stock 2020. Applications, together with stock placed firm, total Pounds 10.69m nominal (77.04 per cent). Balance will be taken up by placees.
