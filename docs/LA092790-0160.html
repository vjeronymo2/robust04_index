September 27, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WHO'S HOT, WHO'S NOT

* Dominguez, which held Verbum Dei to 42 yards in a 25-0 victory. The Dons (3-0) have outscored opponents 109-14.

* Mayfair, which won its third consecutive game, 14-7, over Mountain View.

Damion Watson ran for more than 100 yards for the Monsoons.

* Compton, which scored its most points in seven years in defeating Centennial, 39-16.

* La Mirada, which has two shutouts in a row after beating La Serna, 27-0. The Matadores are 3-0.

* Whittier Christian, which is now 3-0 after edging California, 44-43.

Who's Not

* Pius X, whose losing streak reached 22 when the Warriors lost to Laguna Beach, 16-0.

* Santa Fe, which was beaten, 50-0, by Buena Park. The Chiefs (1-2) have not scored since defeating Pius X, 17-7, in their opening game.

* Lynwood, which made six turnovers in losing to Carson, 37-14. It was the Knights' second consecutive loss.

* Millikan, which also lost the ball six times in a 35-6 loss to Dana Hills. The Rams dropped to 1-2.

* St. Anthony, which is 0-3 after being edged, 8-7, by Ontario Christian.
