February 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $30 when Akrokerami finished second in the third race Wednesday. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $707.80.

Bob's bet today is a $9 triple from Haughty Notion, He's Illustrious and Broadway Pete in the sixth to Rolling Donut and Perforce in the seventh to Paper Princess in the eighth.
