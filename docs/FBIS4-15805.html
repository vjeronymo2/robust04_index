BFN [Text] Our correspondent Yevgeniy Novikov was told by the Black Sea Fleet's press center that as a result of talks between representatives of the fleet command and Ukraine's naval forces, the arrested Russian officers and civilians were released yesterday evening. They were seized by Ukrainian special purpose troops at the fleet's reserve division near Odessa. However, the division remains under the control of 60 Ukrainian special purpose commandos who are preventing representatives of the Black Sea Fleet command from approaching the division's most important facilities.
