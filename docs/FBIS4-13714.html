BFN [Text] General Sakhi, known as Sakhi Broot, a militia general, was killed by Golboddin's militia in Dasht-e Saqawa south of Kabul in Logar Province. Security sources reported to BIA's correspondent that this professional criminal some days ago fought Golboddin's militia over looted goods; as a result of which he was killed along with his three bodyguards. The general's body still lies in Dasht-e Saqawa and is decomposing. No one has come to remove his body. Sakhi Broot some time ago stole the carpets of Sayed Abdolqader Jaylani mosque in Sayd Nur Mohammad Shah Mena, killed an innocent shepherd in the same area, and divided up his sheep among the militia. He was a notorious Dostum general and had a hand in many crimes.
