931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / International Company News: Federal Express delivers sharp earnings rise Federal Express, the biggest overnight delivery company in the US, posted a sharp rise in earnings to Dollars 59.7m, or Dollars 1.07 a share, during its second quarter, up from Dollars 35.4m, or 65 cents, a year earlier, Reuter reports from Memphis. Earnings per share outstripped analysts' forecasts ranging from 90 cents to Dollars 1. Revenues for the period ending November 30 climbed 8 per cent to Dollars 2.12bn from Dollars 1.96bn, while operating income rose 33 per cent to Dollars 149.7m from Dollars 112.7m. International operating profit was Dollars 2.1m on revenues of Dollars 583.7m, while last year international operations lost Dollars 37.8m on revenues of Dollars 564.3m. Domestic profit slipped to Dollars 147.6m from Dollars 150.5m, despite a 10 per cent revenue gain to Dollars 1.54bn.
