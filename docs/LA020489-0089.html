February 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

GAUCHOS TRY TO REBOUND AGAINST TITANS

UC Santa Barbara, coming off its second loss of the season, will try to rebound against Cal State Fullerton in a Big West Conference basketball game at 7:30 tonight in Titan Gym.

The Gauchos (15-2 overall and 7-2 in conference), were upset by New Mexico State in a game at Las Cruces, N.M., last Saturday, 74-68. Their only other loss was at Utah State.

Fullerton, 8-10 and 3-6 after a 77-73 loss to UC Irvine Saturday, has lost 2 in a row.

Santa Barbara beat Fullerton, 69-59, last month at home. ROBYN NORWOOD
