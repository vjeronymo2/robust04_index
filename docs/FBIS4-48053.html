BFN [Text] Johannesburg May 7 SAPA -- Pan Africanist Congress [PAC] stalwart Philip Johnson Mlambo has resigned his position on the National Assembly, according to the final list of representatives released by the Independent Electoral Commission [IEC] on Saturday [7 May]. Mr Mlambo was the PAC's number two on an initial National Assembly list released by the IEC. Mr Ahmed Gora Ebrahim now moves into the number two position behind PAC President Clarence Makwetu. PAC Secretary General Bennie Alexander is another change, moving out from number three for his organisation on the National Assembly to serve in the PWV [Pretoria, Witwatersrand, Vereeniging] regional parliament, according to the IEC. Ms Patricia de Lille has been elevated to number three for the PAC on the National Assembly and Mr Richard Sizani to number four.
