Efforts AU1204193694 Prague CTK in English 1810 GMT 12 Apr 94 AU1204193694 Prague CTK English BFN [Excerpts] Prague, April 12 (CTK) -- Cooperation in fighting the drug mafia and drug terrorism was discussed by Czech Foreign Minister Josef Zieleniec and visiting Colombian Foreign Minister Nohemi Sanin Posada at their meeting today, the two ministers told journalists after the meeting. The integration structures of Europe and America such as, for example, the Partnership for Peace project, the Organization of American States, NATO and the North American Free Trade Agreement (NAFTA) were also discussed, Posada said, adding that Columbia was interested in joining NAFTA. The two ministers discussed bilateral Czech-Colombian relations. Posada mentioned the prospects of concluding mutual agreements, especially an agreement on investment protection. The necessity of strengthening concrete cooperation between the two countries' businessmen and cooperation in the cultural sphere was stressed. [passage omitted]
