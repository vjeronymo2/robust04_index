920612 FT 12 JUN 92 / International Company News: Quaker Oats to keep Italian unit QUAKER Oats, the US foods group, has decided not to sell its Italian edible oils business, Reuters reports from Chicago. Garma, an enterprise of Mr Raul Gardini and Mr Giulio Malgara, former president of Quaker's European grocery products business, had shown interest in buying the Italian oils business and distributing Quaker products in Italy. Mr William Smithburg, chairman, said: 'After evaluating Garma's proposal and prospects for the oils business, we decided it is in the current best interest of our shareholders to keep it.'
