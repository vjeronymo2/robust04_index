April 12, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

HBO Pictures is adding three new productions to its roster of 1989 films. Albert Finney will play a TV news personality facing a personal crisis in "The Image," scheduled to start shooting in May. Pierce Brosnan, Tom Skerritt and Wendy Hughes star in "Heist," a race track robbery suspense film under way in San Diego and Del Mar. And beginning next week in Toronto will be "Perfect Witness," a court system drama with Brian Dennehy, Aidan Quinn and Stockard Channing. Recently completed in Atlanta and New Orleans was "Traveling Man," with John Lithgow, which will debut June 25 on HBO. ALEENE MacMINN
