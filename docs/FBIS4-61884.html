BFN [Excerpts] People throughout the world are celebrating the day of victory in World War II today. [passage omitted] In our Republic, this day is also celebrated as the Day of Europe. During this week concerts, plays, and music festivals will be held in the Ali Sheri Navoi Theater. Arts, crafts, and porcelain exhibitions and a week of European films will also be held.
