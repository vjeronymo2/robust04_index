920617 FT 17 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Melville Street net assets rise to 150p Melville Street Investments, the development capital group, saw its net asset value increase from 146p to 150p over the 12 months to April 30. During the year a total of Pounds 2.1m was realised and two new investments were made as well as providing Pounds 500,000 for existing investments. Revenue for the year fell to Pounds 1.52m (Pounds 1.59m). Income from investments rose to Pounds 1.46m (Pounds 1.38m). Earnings were 4.2p (4.7p) and a final dividend of 2.5p makes a total of 4p (4.5p).
