Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Aleksandrova] [Text] Moscow January 17 TASS -- The Liberal Democratic Party [LDPR] of nationalist leader Vladimir Zhirinovskiy will not get key positions in the State Duma and Zhirinovskiy himself will not head the Foreign Affairs Commission which he planned to do, according to Kirill Ignatyev, a member of the Russia's Choice bloc. He told reporters on Monday [17 January] that all factions, including the LDPR, had agreed that Zhirinovsly's people will not head the commissions on foreign affairs, on defence and security, on federation affairs and the budget. In response Zhirinovskiy proposed to create a special commission for him which would deal with geopolitical issues, according to Ignatyev. "We could not react to it in any other way than with sarcasm", he added.
