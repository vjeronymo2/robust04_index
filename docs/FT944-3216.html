941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Novo bounces Novo Group, the distribution and film entertainment company, announced a 73 per cent expansion in interim pre-tax profits from Pounds 649,000 to Pounds 1.13m for the six months to end-September. Turnover rose just 4.5 per cent to Pounds 8.79m (Pounds 8.4m). The previous outcome was depressed by losses on disposal of a French associate and provisions for litigation with BET over alleged licensing and copyright irregularities. Earnings per share improved to 1.7p (0.97p) but the interim dividend is held at a nominal 0.1p.
