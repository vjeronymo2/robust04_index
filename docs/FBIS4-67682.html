BFN [Text] The Binh Luc District of Nam Ha Province has eliminated leprosy in the entire district. Since 1983, the district has trained 1,400 medical staff in villages and communes, provided information to 24,000 students, and put up 28 information boards on leprosy. It has conducted surveys, established lists, and provided treatment to lepers. It has also discovered early cases of leprosy infection and provided timely treatment. After more than 10 years, the Binh Luc District has completely eliminated leprosy and has changed the attitude of the local people to this disease.
