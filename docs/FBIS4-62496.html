BFN [Text] Another newspaper is published in West Kazakhstan oblast. The BURLINSKIYE VESTI newspaper, with a circulation of 7,000 copies, is published in Aksay city [Burlinskiy rayon]. Its cofounders are the local administration and the rayon maslikhat. The paper comes out in two languages -- Kazakh and Russian -- and will inform its readers on news of the rayon, the work of local authorities, the life of the rural population, and the working collectives which are developing the Karachaganak gas and condensate deposit.
