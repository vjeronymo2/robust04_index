BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 8 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent a letter to the staff of the DPRK Radio and Television Broadcasting Committee. Upon receiving kind solicitude again from the dear leader for the strengthening of the broadcasting service, the staff of the committee had written him a letter carrying their unanimous resolution. They noted in the letter that the dear leader always with a deep attention to broadcasting service has constantly shown loving care and warm favors for the broadcasting lorkers. They vowed to conduct broadcasting service strictly as intended by the party and the leader in order to repay the recent solicitude and devotedly work for the accomplishment of the cause of socialism, the revolutionary cause of chuche. After reading the letter from the staff of the committee, Comrade Kim Chong-il sent the following letter to them: "Thank you. Let us all work hard together for victory in the revolutionary cause. Kim Chong-il. April 23, 1994." The letter of Comrade Kim Chong-il was conveyed to the staff of the DPRK Radio and Television Broadcasting Committee with a due ceremony at the committee Saturday.
