Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Villavicencio -- General Luis Bernardo Urbina, 7th Brigade Army commander, has claimed he is working earnestly to safely rescue Jorge Carmelo Perez Alvarado, candidate to the House of Representatives; Leovigildo Criollo, former San Juan de Arana mayor; and Jose Lozano, mayor of Puerto Rico, who were kidnapped by the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia this weekend. Gen. Rubina said the situation is worrisome, but they are strengthening all actions to try to deter terrorism from escalating and to ensure tranquility.
