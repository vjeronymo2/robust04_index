November 12, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

The latest issue of Traveling in Style magazine, compiled biannually by Travel Editor Jerry Hulse, appeared in the Oct. 22 edition of The Times and brought the usual assortment of "reviews" from readers. Herewith a sampling of their comments:

I kept staring at the map (accompanying Geoffrey Dean-Smith's article: "London's Little Known but Lush Lodgings") and wondering what on earth was wrong, when I discovered that Piccadilly Circus was west of Green Park instead of east.

The queen would be upset to find that noisy place in her back yard.

ROBERT I. FARRAR

La Jolla
