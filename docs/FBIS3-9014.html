Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Belgrade, 17 Mar (TANJUG) -- After a debate lasting several days, the federal parliament's Chamber of Citizens today adopted the federal budget for 1994 totalling 1,675 million dinars (the new Yugoslav dinar is officially pegged to the German mark at a 1:1 ratio). [passage omitted] As far as expenditures are concerned, most of the funds (around 75 percent) have been allocated for the needs of the Yugoslav Army, the federal bodies (around 11 percent) and veterans' benefits (around 7 percent).
