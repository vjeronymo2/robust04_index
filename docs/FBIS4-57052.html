BFN [From the "24 Hours Newscast"] [Text] Paul Ames, the son of Maria del Rosario Casas who is under arrest in the United States charged with espionage, will be granted Colombian citizenship. This afternoon, Foreign Minister Noemi Sanin confirmed this in Manizales. [Begin Sanin recording] Yesterday, we were visited by Maria Casas' mother. She explained her daughter's situation. We are studying her petition to reacquire citizenship and we have promised to grant it in the case of her son because he is an innocent person who has Colombian blood and we should all protect this young and innocent child.
