940427 FT 27 APR 94 / UK Company News: Cosalt swings back to profit Stronger performances from its fibres, holiday homes and workwear divisions enabled Cosalt to swing from losses of Pounds 220,000 to profits of Pounds 765,000 pre-tax for the half year ended February 27. The turnround was also aided by a Pounds 132,000 reduction in interest charges to Pounds 397,000. Earnings of 4.11p compared with previous losses of 1.64p and the interim dividend is lifted from 2.125p to 3p. Turnover of continuing activities rose from Pounds 28.7m to Pounds 30.9m - the group's other interests take in safety and protection and residential property development. The shares closed 8p higher at 153p.
