November 10, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

CITY SECTION ROUNDUP;

MID-VALLEY LEAGUE

Canoga Park 7, Van Nuys 0 -- Rafael Palacios caught a 23-yard pass from Phoumano Luangnam with 8:30 left to lead the host Hunters (1-7, 1-5).

The score came on a fourth-and-four play. The touchdown was set up when Eric Custudio recovered Robert Franklin's fumble at Van Nuys' 28-yard line. GARY MORENO
