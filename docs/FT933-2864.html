930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / International Company News: NYSE firms' profits ahead 38% STRONG markets helped securities firms belonging to the New York Stock Exchange to a 38 per cent increase in second-quarter profits to Dollars 1.57bn, the exchange reported. Revenues rose to Dollars 17.95bn from Dollars 15.62bn. First-half profits totalled Dollars 3.08bn, up from Dollars 2.51bn in 1992, while revenues were Dollars 35.69bn, up from Dollars 32.1bn.
