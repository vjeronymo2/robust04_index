BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 23 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received a gift from the friendship visiting group of the Communist Party-KPML (R) [The Communist Party of Marxist-Leninist Revolutionaries] in Sweden. The gift was handed to an official concerned by Roger Gross, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the party and head of the visiting group.
