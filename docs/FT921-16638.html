920102 FT 02 JAN 92 / Suez transit fees increased The Suez Canal authority raised its transit fees by between 11 and 18 per cent from yesterday, Reuter reports from Ismailia. Mr Ezzat Adel, the authority's chairman, said the move would raise 1992 revenues to a record Dollars 1.89bn (Pounds 1bn) against Dollars 1.77bn last year.
