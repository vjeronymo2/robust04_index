BFN [Text] Prague, June 15 (CTK) -- Speaker of the Romanian Assembly of Deputies Adrian Nastase today concluded his four-day visit to the Czech Republic. During his stay he held talks with President Vaclav Havel, Foreign Minister Josef Zieleniec, and Chairman of Parliament Milan Uhde, on whose invitation he came to the country. Uhde and Nastase agreed to promote cooperation between the two countries' parliaments, especially between the parliamentary economy and foreign affairs committees. They also supported the exchange of visits by state representatives and the exchange of information on building the democratic and economic reform in both countries.
