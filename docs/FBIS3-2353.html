Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Zhang Huanli (1728 3562 0448)] [Text] Fukuoka, 3 Mar (XINHUA) -- Vice Premier Zhu Rongji and his party called on the staff of the Chinese Consulate General in Fukuoka, Japan, this evening. After briefing them on the development of economic reform at home, Zhu Rongji encouraged them to continue to work hard. He also posed for a group picture with the consulate staff. This afternoon, Isao Nakauchi, vice chairman of Keidanren [Japan Federation of Economic Organizations] and president and chairman of Daiei Inc., of Japan, gave a luncheon at Hakata Hotel New Otani to welcome Vice Premier Zhu Rongji and his party. At the luncheon Nakauchi said that in future Daiei Inc. will continue to expand cooperation with the Chinese side. Zhu Rongji replied that he welcomed such a move and wished it success.
