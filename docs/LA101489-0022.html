October 14, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

CS FULLERTON HOPES TO END ROAD SLUMP

Cal State Fullerton, winless on the road in four games this season, will play the University of the Pacific in a Big West Conference game at 2 p.m. today in Amos Alonzo Stagg Memorial Stadium.

Fullerton (2-3-1) will be trying to balance its offense after last week's 34-23 loss to Utah State in which quarterback Dan Speltz passed for 481 yards and the Titans were held to 29 yards rushing. Fullerton is 1-1 in the Big West.

Pacific is 1-5 overall, 1-3 in the conference, with the only victory coming against Cal State Long Beach. ROBYN NORWOOD
