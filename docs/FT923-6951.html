920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Unilever negotiating Dutch acquisition Unilever is in an advanced stage of negotiation to acquire Im van den Berg, a Dutch frozen food and ice cream products trading company. Over the past 20 years the Nijkerk-based company has built up a trade-focused service organisation in the Netherlands providing a range of frozen products including fish, vegetables, potato products, ice cream, pastry confectionery and snacks.
