September 15, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Talk show host/singer Dinah Shore landed a "scoop" while taping an interview with "Jeopardy!" host Alex Trebek this week. When Shore remarked that Trebek was one of TV's most eligible bachelors, he broke the news that "I am going to be married ... and this is the first time I've said it out loud." His intended is Jean Currivan, a commercial real-estate agent in Los Angeles. Trebek's office confirmed Thursday that a wedding is planned, but said no date has been set. Shore was taping the show here for later airing on "A Conversation With Dinah," her new series on TNN cable. ALEENE MacMINN
