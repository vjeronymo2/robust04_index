BFN [Text] Islam Karimov, president of the Republic of Uzbekistan, addressed his countrymen on the occasion of the celebrations of the great victory. The text of the address will be published in the press. On the same day, the president of the Republic of Uzbekistan signed an edict on awarding the participants in the war against Fascism and those who worked on the home front during 1941-45 on the occasion of Victory Day. The address reads: For the service of the participants of the war in the struggle against fascism and those who worked on the home front during 1941-45 for freedom and independence, peaceful skies, and the flourishment of our country, to award on the occasion of the Victory Day festival and the creation for the first time of the orders and medals of the Republic of Uzbekistan, the medal Jasorat [courage] to the servicemen, partisans, those serving in the underground organizations who participated in military activities on the fronts in the war against fascism, servicemen and those who had served during the period of war in the ranks of the Armed Forces, and those who were awarded with medals for victory over Germany in the Great Patriotic War 1941-45. The medal Shuhrat [glory] for the citizens of the Republic of Uzbekistan who worked for the national economy not less than six months from 22 June 1941 to 9 May 1945, those who were awarded the medals for good work during the Great Patriotic War 1941-45 carrying the sign of residents of the blockade of Leningrad, the under-age members of the labor camps, [word indistinct], and other places of imprisonment created by the Fascists and their allies during the period of the Second World War. The awarding of the medals Jasorat and Shuhrat and the corresponding cash sum will [word indistinct] the independence day of Uzbekistan. [Signed] Islam Karimov, president of the Republic of Uzbekistan.
