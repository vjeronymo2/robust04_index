930130 FT 30 JAN 93 / Finance & The Family: Good week for smaller companies - At a Glance It was another good week for smaller company shares (see Bottom Line). The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies index (capital gains version) rose 2.7 per cent from 1263.48 to 1297.74 over the week to January 28, while the County index rose 2.8 per cent from 985.11 to 1012.93.
