Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A general strike is being observed in all Lebanese areas today to mark the first week since the Ibrahimi Mosque massacre in Hebron in which 43 Palestinians were killed and hundreds were wounded. Studies have stopped in schools and universities. Banks, government departments, and shops have closed their doors. Traffic on the streets is light. The strike covers areas under the Israeli occupation in the south and western al-Biqa'.
