930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / Construction Contracts: Burnley hospital scheme LG MOUCHEL PARTNERS has been appointed by the Burnley Health Care Trust, as civil and structural engineer for the Pounds 6m Phase IV development of Burnley General Hospital. The scheme comprises four new nucleus templates. Accommodation will be provided for paediatric, surgical day case, ear, nose and throat and oral surgery and general acute wards.
