BFN [Excerpt] A joint meeting of the DPRK Government, political parties, and public organizations for accelerating the independent and peaceful reunification of the country by thoroughly implementing the 10-point program of the great unity of the whole nation for national reunification was held at the People's Palace of Culture on the afternoon of 11 April. Placed to the front of the meeting locale was a portrait of the great leader of our party and our people Comrade Kim Il-song. Slogans read "Long live the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song," and "Long live the glorious Workers Party of Korea [WPK]." There were also slogans reading "Let all the fellow countrymen be united under the banner of great national unity in order to reunify the country," and "Let us add luster to the year of 1994 as a year of great national unity." Attending the joint meeting were functionaries of the party, power organs, public organizations, religious organizations, and central organizations. Appearing on the rostrum were Comrade Kang Song-san, member of the WPK Central Committee Political Bureau and premier of the State Administration Council; Kim Pyong-sik, vice president of the DPRK and chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Social Democratic Party; Comrade Yang Hyong-sop, alternate member of the WPK Central Committee Political Bureau and chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly [SPA]; Comrade Kim Yong-sun, secretary of the WPK Central Committee and chairman of the Reunification Policy Committee of the SPA; Comrade Chang Chol, vice premier of the State Administration Council; Yu Mi-yong, chairman of the Central Committee of the Chondoist Chongu Party, and responsible functionaries of public organizations. SPA Chairman Yang Hyong-sop first made a report at the joint meeting. [passage omitted]
