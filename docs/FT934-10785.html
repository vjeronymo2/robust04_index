931103 FT 03 NOV 93 / International Company News: Lloyd's capital plans still on course THE SPONSORS of two of the biggest investment trusts at the Lloyd's of London insurance market will today press ahead with plans to raise some Pounds 400m in corporate capital despite setbacks to some smaller schemes in the last few days, writes Richard Lapper in London. Backers of the larger schemes, sponsored by Samuel Montagu and Barclays de Zoete Wedd, will issue prospectuses today and are confident about raising capital from a range of institutions, including pension funds, life assurance companies and retail investors.
