940505 FT 05 MAY 94 / Usdaw censure vote defeated A vote to censure the executive council of Usdaw, the shopworkers' union, for reversing its opposition to Sunday trading has been narrowly defeated at the union's annual conference in Blackpool. The vote of 116,000 against the motion to 99,775 in favour showed the extent of the division among the union's membership. The executive relied heavily on the support of its large proportion of women supermarket workers, many of whom are in favour of Sunday working. Its earlier decision to reverse its policy against Sunday working had however angered many of its members.
