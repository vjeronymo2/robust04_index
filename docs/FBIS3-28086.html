Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Feb. 3 (YONHAP) -- President Kim Yong-sam stressed on Thursday Korean overseas missions should now be the center of market exploration and collection of trade and technical information. At a Chongwadae [presidential residence] dinner he hosted for overseas mission chiefs and their spouses, Kim said the organization and function of overseas missions should change for the sake of new diplomacy. "You should do actively anything that can be of help to strengthening the country's competitiveness," the president said. Stating he is planning to have summit meetings with the leaders of major countries and would attend an APEC (Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation) summit meeting taking place in November in Indonesia, President Kim asked the senior diplomats to try to ensure national interests solidly in the course of preparing these and other events. Korean mission chiefs posted in the American and Asian areas are now visiting the home country to attend an annual mission chiefs meeting.
