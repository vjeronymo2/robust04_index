Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Lithuania Seeks To Negotiate Association Agreement"; as released by Brussels TEXTLINE database] [Text] Mr. Januska, Lithuania's minister of foreign affairs, has sent a letter to Mr. Carolos Papoulias, Greek foreign minister and president of the Council of the [European] Union, requesting that the Council authorize the European Commission to negotiate an association agreement with Lithuania as soon as possible. "The support of the presidency of the European Union" to this effect "would be of great importance for the future prosperity of Lithuania, as well as for the stability and security of the whole Baltic region," the letter states. It will be recalled that the Twelve found the negotiating directives proposed by the European Commission for the Baltic states to be too limited and have expressed the intention of going further in creating closer ties with these countries.
