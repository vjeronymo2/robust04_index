November 21, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: DANA POINT;

TALK TO KEY ON STATE OF SOCIAL SERVICES

Mayors of South County cities will join county officials, business leaders and community activists in a conference detailing the gaps and needs in social services for the area.

The daylong conference is to begin at 7:45 a.m. today at the Dana Point Resort Hotel.

Topics of discussion are mental health care, homelessness, hunger, youth, senior citizens, affordable housing, day care, drug abuse, immigration, crime, public health and the disabled.
