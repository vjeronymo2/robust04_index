920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / Roadchef staff breach Pounds 3.40 THE LOWEST-paid workers in Roadchef, the motorway services organisation, have breached the Pounds 3.40 an hour barrier - the level the Labour Party selected for a national minimum wage - after a 6 per cent pay rise for all staff. As part of the two-year deal it was agreed staff would get a rise related to the retail prices index next year.
