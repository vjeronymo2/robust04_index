April 7, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

AGASSI, CHESNOKOV TO PLAY TONIGHT

Andre Agassi will play Andrei Chesnokov of the Soviet Union in an exhibition at 7:30 tonight at the Forum as part of the Forum Tennis Challenge.

Agassi is ranked No. 4 after winning two tournaments this year and has three victories over other players in the top 10. Agassi's 16-2 record in 1990 is his best start. Chesnokov is ranked No. 21.

Pepperdine teammates David Wells-Roth and Alejo Mancisidor will play in an earlier match.
