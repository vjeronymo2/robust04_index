BFN [Text] Beijing, May 28 (XINHUA) -- Chinese State Councillor and Secretary-General of the State Council Luo Gan met with and hosted a banquet in honor of Vullnet Ademi, secretary-general of the Council of Ministers of Albania, and his party here today. The two officials exchanged views primarily on Sino-Albanian cooperation in economic, trade and other fields. Ademi and his party arrived here on May 26 as Luo's guests. During their stay here, the Albanian visitors met with officials from the Foreign Ministry, the State Commission for Economic Restructuring and the China National Council of Light Industry.
