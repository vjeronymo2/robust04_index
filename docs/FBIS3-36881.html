Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau ROMPRES, 9/2/1994 -- The appeal of President Snegur to citizens of Moldova of 7 February denotes a provocation Chisinau has prepared against Tiraspol, declared to BASA Alexandru Caraman, Dniester vice-president. The appeal warns about possible new clashes and bloodshed in Dniester region. In the opinion of Caraman, "Moldovan authorities will provoke bloodshed and blame Tiraspol leaders, pretending to be doing their best to solve the Dniester conflict." He estimated that "the present leadership of Moldova does everything to recruit electors from the left bank. Asked to comment the information saying that some 40 people from the former Russian Security Ministry are engaged in Tiraspol service, Caraman said "I do not work in these organs and can neither confirm or deny such data."
