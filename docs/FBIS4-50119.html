BFN [Text] Beijing, May 13 (XINHUA) -- Thirty families from different parts of the country were chosen as the country's "model families" here today. It was the third such selection and was significant as this year is the "International Year of the Family". The selection is aimed at promoting the development of family culture and guiding people to pursue a civilized, healthy and scientific lifestyle, said Chen Muhua, vice-chairwoman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and president of the All-China Women's Federation (ACWF) at an award ceremony held for the model families here today. The 30 families represent a happy family atmosphere and colorful family life. All the family members are actively taking part in China's economic construction and making contributions to society, said Chen. Among the 30 families, ten were awarded gold and silver prizes and 20 awarded honorary prizes through a public ballot and specialists' appraisal, according to Chen. The selection process was jointly organized by the ACWF and China central television, and financially supported by an enterprise in Sichuan Province.
