920611 FT 11 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Chief quits at Northumbrian Fine Foods Mr Richard Adams has resigned as chief executive of Northumbrian Fine Foods, the USM-quoted biscuit and snack maker. He has held the post for 22 years writes Angus Foster. Mr Kevin O'Keeffe, a non-executive director, will become non-executive chairman. Mr John Gibson, group finance director, has been appointed acting chief executive while a new chief executive is sought. Mr O'Keeffe said Mr Adams had decided Northumbrian's enlarged size called for a new style of management. 'There was no bust up,' he said. Northumbrian's shares fell 2p to 43p. The company said pre-tax profits for the year to March 31, due to be announced in July, are expected to be about Pounds 700,000, compared to Pounds 264,000 in the previous period.
