Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In south Lebanon, it has been disclosed that three terrorists were killed and four others wounded in two separate incidents. Qol Yisra'el's reporter in the north says in one incident on Thursday morning [27 January], a group of terrorists tried to penetrate into the security zone and walked into a mine field, wounding four of them. They were rescued by local villagers. In the other incident on Friday morning, three terrorists were killed in a firefight with IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldiers. None of the soldiers was hurt. The clash took place in Wadi al-Tannur, in the western sector of the security zone.
