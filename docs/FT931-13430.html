930123 FT 23 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Geared Income revenue improves Net revenue in the nine months to December 31 at Geared Income Investment Trust improved slightly from Pounds 1.3m to Pounds 1.34m. Earnings per share were 6.11p, against 5.93p and the third interim dividend is an unchanged 2p. Net asset value over the 12 months to the end of December fell to 76.15p (88.46p).
