921207 FT 07 DEC 92 / Civil Engineering: Installing infrastructure The Midlands office of CLUGSTON CONSTRUCTION has been awarded a contract to implement infrastructure works on Bass Developments Centrum 100 site at Burton upon Trent. The contract forms phase one of larger infrastructure works amounting to some Pounds 10m. Clugston will be constructing a roundabout on Wellington Road and the first section of the principal spine road, which will provide access to up to 70 acres of serviced land.
