BFN [Text] Beijing, June 7 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Premier Li Peng met here this afternoon with Seiji Tsutsumi, chairman of Saison, a Japanese corporation. During the meeting in Zhongnanhai, Li expressed appreciation over the positive attitude the Japanese corporation had taken in developing economic cooperation between China and Japan. After briefing the guests on China's current economic situation and relevant policies, the Chinese premier gave a detailed account of the basic characteristics of China's socialist market economy. The Saison corporation, a leading Japanese firm in the fields of services and marketing, has now launched various joint centures in Beijing and Guangzhou as well as in Jiangsu Province since it started business cooperation with China in the 1970s.
