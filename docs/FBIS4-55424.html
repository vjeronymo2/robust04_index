The meeting took place in the UN embassy in Geneva before the formal ceremony held to mark the Croat-Muslim agreement. Ministers Granic and Christopher talked about the situation in the UNPA and about bilateral relations. After a cooperation agreement was reached they stressed a need for more specific agreements between Croatia and the United States in the future.
