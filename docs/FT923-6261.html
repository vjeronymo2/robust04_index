920826 FT 26 AUG 92 / UK Company News: EFT advances 38% to Pounds 706,000 Against a 'background of continuing weak demand for capital investment', EFT Group, the financial services company, achieved a 38 per cent increase in pre-tax profits for the six months to June 30. The rise, from Pounds 510,000 to Pounds 706,000, was achieved on revenue marginally ahead to Pounds 3.7m (Pounds 3.66m). Earnings came through at 1.49p (1.09p) per share and the interim dividend is lifted to 0.4p (0.33p).
