March 16, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

'WAR AND REMEMBRANCE' TO CONCLUDE IN MAY

ABC said Wednesday that the final 11 1/2 hours of its mega-miniseries "War and Remembrance" will air over a five-night period starting Sunday, May 7.

"Remembrance" is the $110-million sequel to ABC's 1983 blockbuster "The Winds of War." It proved a ratings misfire last November when its opening 18-hour installment aired.
