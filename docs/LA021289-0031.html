February 12, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

MUSIC, SWEET MUSIC

While driving to work one morning recently, something extraordinary happened.

In between the commercials, jokes, concert announcements, traffic reports, weather forecasts, real news, ski reports, contests, questionable news, surf reports, listener comments, inane disc jockey banter, time checks and other meaningless information, I actually heard a rock 'n' roll record played from beginning to end, without interruption!

JOEL ZESERSON

Marina del Rey
