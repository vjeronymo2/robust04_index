920201 FT 01 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Neepsend slides by Pounds 570,000 Continuing to be hit by the recession, Neepsend has seen its pre-tax profit fall from Pounds 742,000 to Pounds 172,000 in the half year ended September 30 1991. Turnover of this engineering group dropped from Pounds 8.4m to Pounds 7.7m. The profit was struck after heavier interest and crediting Pounds 45,000 from the sale of machinery. Earnings per share were 0.66p (2.45p) and the interim dividend is held at 0.5p.
