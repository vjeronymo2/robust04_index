920229 FT 29 FEB 92 / UK Company News: London Securities Losses at London Securities, the main asset of which is the 18-hole Mill Ride Golf Club at Ascot, Berkshire, widened from Pounds 3.4m to Pounds 5.27m pre-tax for the six months ended September 30. The loss included exceptional provisions amounting to Pounds 1.71m (Pounds 1m) which mainly reflected a further write-down of unquoted investments. Losses per share totalled 16.2p (5.9p). To coincide with the end of season of the golf course the company's year-end is being switched to September 30 and the current accounting period will run for 18 months.
