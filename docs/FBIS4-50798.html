BFN [Text] Bonn, May 21 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese health delegation headed by Peng Peiyun, state councilor and minister in charge of the State Family Planning Commission, ended a week-long visit to Germany today. During her visit, Peng held talks with Rita Suessmuth, president of the German Bundestag. They agreed that health specialists of the two countries, and all the people of China and Germany, should continue to develop cooperation and exchange. Peng also met with German Minister of Health Horst Seehofer and Minister of Women and Youth Angela Merkel.
