930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / Indonesia eases visa curbs Seeking to attract more tourists, Indonesia yesterday began allowing visitors from 45 countries to stay two months without visas, the immigration office announced, AP reports from Jakarta. The visa-free visits cannot be extended or used for business purposes, said Mr Roni Sikap Sinuraya, director- general of immigration. In 1992, more than 3m foreigners visited Indonesia, up from 2.5m in 1991. Tourist earnings rose from Dollars 1.4bn in 1989 to Dollars 3.3bn in 1992.
