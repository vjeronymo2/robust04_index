Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 6 Mar (MENA)--Talks between President Husni Mubarak and Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat began at the presidential office this evening. Members of the Egyptian and Palestinian sides attended. The two sides reviewed the latest developments in the Palestinian problem in light of recent events in the area, particularly the massacre in the Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron. The Egyptian side to the talks consists of Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa and Dr. Usamah al-Baz, first Foreign Ministry under secretary and director of the president's Office for Political Affairs. The Palestinian side includes PLO Executive Committee member Jamal Surani, Palestine Ambassador in Cairo Sa'id Kamal, and Fatah representative in Cairo Zuhdi al-Qidrah.
