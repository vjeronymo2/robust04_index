920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Allied-Lyons makes French acquisition Allied-Lyons, the brewery and food group, is buying Picard through Lyons, its food manufacturing arm. The price was not disclosed but was said to be not material in relation to Allied-Lyons' net assets. Picard, based in France, is a family-owned company which manufactures long-life toast and biscotte products. It has a leading share of the French market where it sells under the Auga brand. Nearly 20 per cent of production is exported throughout Europe.
