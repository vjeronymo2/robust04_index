January 18, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: BALDWIN PARK;

REFINANCING NETS $2 MILLION

The city's Redevelopment Agency refinanced three redevelopment projects, generating $2.3 million, part of which will pay back a city loan.

The $300,000 loan was made last month, when the agency was not able to meet obligations for the San Gabriel River, Puente-Merced and Central Business District redevelopment areas.

"The agency is now in a position to meet all its obligations," said Mayor Pro Tem Bette Lowes.
