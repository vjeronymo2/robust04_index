930421 FT 21 APR 93 / More than 1m covered by PRPs MORE THAN 1m people are participating in profit-related pay schemes, Mr Norman Lamont, the chancellor, said yesterday, Philip Coggan writes. Mr Lamont, launching a Treasury booklet drawing together details of the various schemes for promoting the financial participation of employees, said one in five of the labour force was covered by at least one scheme. PRP schemes have increased substantially since the tax benefits were increased in 1991. The number of employees covered by PRP schemes rose 20 per cent to 1,167,400 in the first quarter of this year, while the number of schemes rose 11 per cent to 4,615.
