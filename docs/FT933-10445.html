930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / Observer: Media morsels A couple of weeks ago Observer highlighted an apparent correlation between the financial performance of media-buyer Aegis and the type of refreshment it serves up at its agms. The fancier the comestibles, the better the results - and vice versa. Now BBJ Media Services - one of Aegis's many children - has picked up the largest media-buying account to change hands so far this year: Ranks Hovis McDougall's annual advertising spend of Pounds 18m. Given the brands within the RHM portfolio - Bisto gravy, Paxo stuffing, a host of Chinese and Indian chutneys and sauces, and Mr Kipling's ready-made cakes - Observer feels slightly queasy at the thought of what kind of fare will be dished out at Aegis's next presentation.
