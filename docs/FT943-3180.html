940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / Observer: Downpour It would seem sensible for companies to choose appropriate muzak to pipe over their telephone line to entertain or otherwise those callers left on hold. Walt Disney pumps out theme music from its films. British Airways trills arias from its television commercials. But the prize for the most apposite piece of music must surely go to the embattled French bank, Credit Lyonnais. Its current choice is Gene Kelly's classic, Singing in the Rain.
