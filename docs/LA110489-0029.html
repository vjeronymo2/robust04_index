November 4, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

PACIFIC LEAGUE

Arcadia 24, Glendale 7 -- Arcadia scored on its first two possessions of the second half to turn a seven-point halftime lead into a rout at Glendale.

Arcadia (8-1, 4-0) held Glendale to 121 yards. Glendale's leading rusher, Pathon Rucker, was limited to 27 yards in 11 carries. Glendale (4-5, 2-2) scored its only touchdown on a one-yard run by Hector Maya. Dynamiter quarterback Erik Kiesau completed seven of 11 for 91 yards. BRYAN RODGERS
