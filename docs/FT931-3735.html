930313 FT 13 MAR 93 / Lloyd's losses put at Pounds 2.4bn CHATSET, the company that analyses Lloyd's results, said yesterday that losses on the insurance market could reach Pounds 2.4bn for 1990 according to its preliminary estimates, Andrew Jack writes. The company plans to release figures by early April. Lloyd's dismissed the figures as 'pure speculation'. Its results will be released in June.
