BFN [Excerpts] [Passage omitted] Lao Deputy Foreign Minister Souban Srithirat told foreign journalists his ministry has received an official letter from the Royal Government of Cambodia [RGC] in April asking Laos to cooperate by closing its border passes to block the smuggling of Cambodian logs into or through Laos into Thailand. Souban Srithirat added that the Lao Government has already responded to the RGC's request and that Laos would implement the policy of blocking log smuggling from Cambodia through Laos in all areas bordering Laos and Cambodia, particularly in the Stung Treng-Champassak, Ratanakiri-Attopeu, and areas in Preah Vihear Province bordering Laos.
