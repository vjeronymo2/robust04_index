Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 31 Dec (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak held a telephone conversation with King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, custodian of the Two Holy Mosques, this evening. The two leaders exchanged views on the latest developments in the peace process and current contacts on the Palestinian issue. President Mubarak congratulated King Fahd on the inauguration of the Saudi Shura Council. President Mubarak and King Fahd also exchanged greetings for New Year.
