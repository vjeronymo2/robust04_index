930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / UK Company News: River Plate Trust Over the 12 months ended April 30 net asset value of River Plate and General Investment Trust rose from 108.1p to 124.7p. For the half year to that date, gross revenue totalled Pounds 3.15m (Pounds 3.32m) and earnings per share worked through at 4.01p (4.14p). The interim dividend is again 3p.
