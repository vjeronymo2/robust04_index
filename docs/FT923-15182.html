920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Abtrust New Dawn net assets increase Abtrust New Dawn Investment Trust, which specialises in Pacific region markets, had a net asset value of 125.79p per share at April 30, compared with 123.86p a year earlier. The figure stood at 104.02p at October 31. Net revenue for the year was Pounds 182,722 (Pounds 157,882). Earnings per share rose from 0.53p to 0.61p and the dividend is maintained at 0.5p.
