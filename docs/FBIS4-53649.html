BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 20 (KCNA) -- Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK], today met and had a friendly talk with the visiting delegation of the party school of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China led by Vice-president Liu Shengyu. Present on the occasion were Vice-president of Kim Il-song Higher Party School U Tal-ho and a councillor of the Chinese Embassy here.
