BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamiatina] [Text] Moscow May 11 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin has left for an official visit to Germany on Wednesday. The complete withdrawal of Russian troops from the German soil is expected to dominate the agenda of the visit. The two countries' leaders are expected to sign an agreement on a joint official farewell. During the visit, the sides are also scheduled to sign a number of bilateral economic agreements. Helmut Kohl has invited Boris Yeltsin to visit his native town Oggersheim, ITAR-TASS learned from sources close to the Russian president.
