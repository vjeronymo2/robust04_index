920912 FT 12 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Pounds 99,000 loss at Thomas Walker Thomas Walker, a maker of metal components for the clothing industry, swung from profits of Pounds 56,000 to losses of Pounds 98,735 pre-tax for the year to end-June. Losses per share of 1.42p compared with previous earnings of 0.38p and a reduced final dividend of 0.4p (0.7p) makes a 0.58p (0.88p) total. Turnover slipped to Pounds 3.42m (Pounds 3.48m).
