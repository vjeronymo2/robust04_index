940920 FT 20 SEP 94 / UK Company News: GT Japan net assets advance GT Japan Investment Trust raised net asset value per share from 241.9p to 288.3p over the year ended June 30. After-tax revenue for the 12 months fell from Pounds 1.48m to Pounds 1.04m for earnings per share of 1.67p (2.38p). The recommended final dividend is again 0.85p, but a reduced special payment of 0.25p (0.5p) makes a lower total of 1.5p (1.75p).
