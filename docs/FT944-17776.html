941006 FT 06 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Arcadian at Pounds 0.12m Arcadian International, the hotel operator and leisure developer, announced pre-tax profits of Pounds 116,000 for the six months to June 30. For the previous eight months there were losses of Pounds 471,000. The results are the first since November's substantial capital increase and acquisition of the Clipper and Hidden hotels. Turnover amounted to Pounds 5.58m (Pounds 1.28m) with just Pounds 197,000 from continuing operations. Earnings came out at 0.1p (1.2p losses). The directors intend to recommend a dividend for the full year.
