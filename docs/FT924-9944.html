921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / Management (The Growing Business): VAT inspectors rake in a record - In a Nutshell Value added tax inspectors made fewer visits to businesses in 1991-92 but collected more tax. The number of visits fell from 440,000 to 426,000 in the year ended March 31, 1992 but additional tax collected rose from Pounds 1.16bn to a record Pounds 1.46bn, according to the annual report of Customs & Excise. An extra Pounds 27m was collected in the form of serious misdeclaration penalties while Pounds 43m was levied in default interest charges.
