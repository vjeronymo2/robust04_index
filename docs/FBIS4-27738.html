BFN [Text] For its strong effort to stave off the holding of the Asia-Pacific Conference on East Timor in Manila, the Ramos administration is asking Indonesia to return the gesture. President Ramos is asking for the immediate release of some 90 Filipino fishermen detained since last month in North Sulawesi. President Ramos expressed hope that Jakarta would accede to Manila's request. He evoked the phrase ASEAN cooperation and brotherhood, the same words he used to ban foreign delegates on the East Timor conference.
