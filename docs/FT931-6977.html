930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Honeysuckle ahead to Pounds 601,000 Honeysuckle Group, the USM-quoted designer of women's wear,lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 589,000 to Pounds 601,000 in the half year to November 30, although turnover fell to Pounds 8.83m (Pounds 11.11m). Mr David Serr, chairman, said that spring 1993 orders had been 30 per cent up on last year and forward orders for next winter also showed 30 per cent growth. Accordingly, an interim dividend of 0.75p (nil) is declared, payable from earnings of 5.5p (4.7p) per share.
