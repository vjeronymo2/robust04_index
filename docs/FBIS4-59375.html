BFN [Text] Staff General Husayn Kamil Hasan, minister of industry and minerals, has announced the arrival of a ship on 11 May, carrying 236 harvesters and a number of tractors. In a statement to INA, he said these harvesters and tractors will be immediately distributed by a specialized committee to farmers in all the country's governorates. They will be used in the current agricultural and harvest campaign. Staff Gen. Husayn Kamil Hasan said the Ministry of Industry and Minerals and the industrial sectors have always been the first to support the agricultural sector, with equipment, requirements, fertilizers, and pesticides, with the objective of developing agriculture, increasing production in our country, and improving its quality. He said all this is pursuant to the instructions of leader President Saddam Husayn, may God watch over him, and his excellency's continuous care for developing the agricultural sector and for increasing its capacity and capabilities in order to secure abundant food for our lofty Iraqi people and to break the blockade imposed by the unjust ones on our struggler country. May God grant us success to serve our people under the leadership of the architect of Iraq's glory, leader President Saddam Husayn, may God preserve him. A shipment of harvesters and tractors arrived earlier this year, and was distributed to farmers. Other shipments of harvesters and tractors will arrive later.
