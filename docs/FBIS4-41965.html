BFN [Text] A delegation of the International Committee of the Red Cross [ICRC] led by Jean de Courten, the general representative of this organization in eastern Europe and Central Asia, arrived in Dushanbe on a short working visit today. [passage indistinct] Jean de Courten met with Erkin Rahmatulloyev, first deputy foreign minister of Tajikistan, today. Rahmatulloyev highly praised the activities of ICRC representatives in the country and expressed the hope that beneficial relations between Tajikistan and the ICRC will be further developed. The issues related to repatriating Tajik refugees from Afghanistan, rendering assistance to them, and providing them with accommodation were discussed in the course of the meeting.
