BFN [Text] Official talks between the Syrian Arab side headed by Interior Minister Dr. Muhammad Harbah and the Turkish side headed by Turkish Interior Minister Nahit Mentese were resumed at the Ministry of Interior yesterday evening. Members of the two delegations, as well as the Turkish ambassador in Damascus attended. The two sides continued their discussion of the issues tackled during the previous round of talks. They reiterated the need to continue dialogue and direct contacts between both sides to address all issues that concern both countries. The two sides also commended the existing ties of cooperation between the two ministries in the areas of security and combating crime. They also stressed their eagerness to further promote ties to safeguard the two countries' security and stability. Afterward, specialized committees held separate meetings to address administrative and security issues of concern to both countries.
