Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Sochi March 15 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin, who arrived in Sochi on Monday on a brief vacation, on the same day met with the regional head of the Krasnodar region, Nikolay Yegorov. The two discussed a wide range of economic, political and management issues. Before departure from Moscow Yeltsin told journalists that he is going to Sochi "in order to work there, rather than for medical treatment." In particular, during his vacation the president intends to work out a mechanism of the realisation of the presidential message to the Federal Assembly and consider the problem of preparations for a draft agreement on civil peace and accord.
