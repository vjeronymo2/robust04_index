Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] A delegation of the Workers Party of Korea [WPK] led by WPK Secretary Hwang Chang-yop returned home by train on 17 March from a visit to Vietnam and Laos. Receiving the delegation at Pyongyang station were Kim Yang-kon, deputy director of the WPK Central Committee; Duong Chinh Thuc, ambassador of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam to our country; and Qiao Zonghuai, PRC ambassador. The delegation left Vientiane on 13 March after winding up its Laos visit. At the airport, Thongsing Thammavong, member of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee and chairman of the Party Organization Committee of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party, other functionaries concerned, and our country's ambassador to Laos saw the delegation off.
