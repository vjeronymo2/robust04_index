930721 FT 21 JUL 93 / Letters to the Editor: To the point Sir, The Financial Times Style Guide (Hawks and Handsaws, July 10/11) will no doubt be a power for good in the journalistic world. In this, I wonder, will it bring about the demise of the war-time euphemism 'in short supply' and restore the simple word 'scarce' to media parlance? Raymond Nottage, 36E Arkwright Road, London NW3 6BH
