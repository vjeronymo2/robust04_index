Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cape Town Dec 31 SAPA -- Police have found the getaway car of five gunmen who on Thursday night sprayed a Cape Town pub with automatic rifle fire, leaving four people dead and five others wounded. The car, a blue Opel Rekord, was found in Guguletu township outside the city. Police have hailed the find as a major breakthrough in their investigations into the massacre at the Heidelberg tavern in Observatory. SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reported police roadblocks were in place throughout the Cape Province and as far afield as Transkei on Friday morning. No-one has accepted responsibility for the attack.
