921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / Observer: Missing link 'Is your father in?', the consultant asked the small boy who answered his call to a client in a seaside town one evening. 'No, he's out with the coastguards,' came the reply. 'Well, is your mother there?' - 'No, she's up at the police station.' 'What are they doing?' asked the puzzled caller. 'Looking for me,' the boy said.
