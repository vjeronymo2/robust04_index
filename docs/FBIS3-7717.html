Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] Brigadier General Abel, minister of national planning and economic development, explained the situation of Myanmar's [Burma] economic developments to visiting prime minister of Singapore, Mr. Goh Chok Tong, his delegation, and the Singaporean businessmen who accompanied the prime minister, at the International Business Center on Prome Road, Yangon [Rangoon], at 0900 this morning. The ceremony was attended by Deputy Prime Minister Vice Admiral Maung Maung Khin; Forestry Affairs Minister Lieutenant General Chit Swe; Hotels and Tourism Minister Lt. Gen. Kyaw Ba; Livestock Breeding and Fisheries Minister Brig. Gen. Maung Maung; Finance and Revenue Minister Brig. Gen. Win Tin; Communications, Post, and Telegraphs Minister U Soe Tha; deputy ministers; Myanmar Ambassador U Phone Myint; Charge d'Affaires U Kyaw Myint; responsible personnel from the Foreign Ministry; and invited guests. Firstly, Brig. Gen. Abel explained the structure of Myanmar and its natural resources, prevailing economic development situation, figures relating to economic and social activities, foreign investment situation and opportunities, and Myanmar's economic developments. Next, Minister Brig. Gen. Abel and Minister Brig. Gen. Win Tin answered queries raised by the visiting prime minister, his delegation, and the Singaporean businessmen.
