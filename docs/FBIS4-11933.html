BFN [Text] One person died and 10 others were injured in a limited fire that broke out this morning at the Forte Grand Hotel. Civil Defense teams arrived at the scene within minutes and managed to bring the fire under control and evacuate occupants in record time not exceeding several minutes. Colonel Mahmud Ahmad al-'Abbadi, assistant director of the Civil Defense for operations, said the person who died is Italian. Those injured were suffering from broken limbs, smoke inhalation, and minor burns.
