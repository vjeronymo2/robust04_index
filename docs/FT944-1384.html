941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / World Commodities Prices: Base metals down - Market Report Base metals prices rallied from their lows at the London Metal Exchange yesterday but still ended with net losses, despite strong buying of ALUMINIUM. The cash premium in the COPPER market narrowed sharply as concern about approaching options declarations eased. An unsettled day at the London Commodity Exchange COFFEE market ended with the March futures price up Dollars 10 at Dollars 2,648 a tonne, after trading between Dollars 2,607 and Dollars 2,690. Talk of a new producers retention scheme provided some support. Compiled from Reuters
