August 5, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

U.S. ANTI-SATELLITE PROGRAM: 'SPACE IS PART OF OUR SECURITY'

I totally go along with Rowny's idea that "We are entering an era in which space will be looked on as a medium rather than a barrier to man's progress. . . . " We don't need the B-2, we should cut aid to foreign countries.

If we could build two space-based platforms, we can accomplish the mission of both foreign aid and the B-2.

RICHARD FOND

Alhambra
