BFN [Text] UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] will allow the release of all foreign nationals, particularly those of French and British nationality, held as hostages since Wednesday [15 June] in Huambo. UNITA insists their exit from Huambo has to be well organized. UNITA Spokesman Jorge Valentim made the announcement when reacting today to disagreement between his organization and the United Nations relating to the situation of 56 foreign nationals who have been held in Huambo under unexplained circumstances. Huambo has been under UNITA control since March of last year.
