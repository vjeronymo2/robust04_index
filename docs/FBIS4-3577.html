BFN [Text] Hohhot, April 21 (XINHUA) -- A forest fire which broke out in a lumbering site of the Hulun Buir League (prefecture) in the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region is still raging on after six days concerted efforts of fighting. Two fires started in the operational area six days ago. One was reported to have been brought under control and the other is still going on due to strong winds and difficult terrains. Acting Premier Zhu Rongji and State Councillor Chen Junsheng are being kept up-to-date with reports on the firefighting. They have sent messages of encouragement to fire fighters via the regional government of Inner Mongolia and the Ministry of Forestry. Xu Yufang, China's forestry minister and Zhu guangyao, the vice-minister, as well as Zhang Tingwu, vice-chairman of the Inner Mongolia Regional Government, are on the spot to direct fire fighting operations.
