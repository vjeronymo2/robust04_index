940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / Thais 'forced refugees home' A senior Thai officer admitted yesterday that the 25,000 refugees whom the Thai military repatriated to Cambodia last week were returned against their will, AP reports from Chantaburi, Thailand. Thai officials previously had said the refugees returned voluntarily. On Monday, the UN High Commissioner for Refugees lodged a protest with the Thai government, saying the repatriation put the lives of the Cambodians at risk and violated international standards.
