BFN [Text] Finance Minister Eneias Comiche has told the MOZAMBIQUE INFORMATION AGENCY, AIM, that the Mozambican Government has not allocated 20 million hectares of land to the Maharishi Heaven on Earth Development Corporation based in the Netherlands. However, Comiche confirmed that last year the Mozambican Government and that organization signed an agreement. The finance minister said that contrary to reports made by a number of Mozambican and foreign journalists over the past few days that 20 million hectares of land were allocated to the organization, this agreement does not allocate or grant to the organization a specific portion of land in Mozambique. Eneias Comiche added that if the corporation chooses to carry out ventures in Mozambique, it will have to follow all the procedures stipulated by the current laws in the country.
