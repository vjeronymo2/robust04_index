Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The brother President Yasir 'Arafat received at midday today a telephone call from U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher. The latest developments regarding the Ibrahimi mosque massacre were discussed during the telephone conversation. The brother president stressed the need for the UN Security Council to issue a resolution that guarantees the safety and security of Palestinian citizens in the occupied Palestinian territories, including Holy Jerusalem, and entrusting an international force with this task.
