Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yevgeniy Tkachenko] [Text] Chelyabinsk, 28 Feb -- Chelyabinsk Oblast Prosecutor Gennadiy Likhachev has submitted his resignation. Speaking on local radio today, he stressed that this step was brought about by the fact that he did not agree with the political amnesty declared by the State Duma. In his view, "the procuracy has once again been made hostage to political intrigues."
