Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Kuala Lumpur, Tue -- Nineteen Army officers and men today left for Mogadishu to assume duties under the United Nations Operations in Somalia (UNOSOM II). Two of them, Lieutenant Colonel Liew Wai Tong and Captain Abdul Razak Abdul Rahim, will be attached to the UNOSOM II headquarters in Mogadishu. They will be responsible for the logistic needs of some 27,000 troops from 27 countries. [passage omitted]
