Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Text] Deputy Arpad Matejka has become the new chairman of the Republican Council of the Movement for a Democratic Slovakia [HZDS]. He is replacing Roman Kovac, who has been expelled from the HZDS. This decision was made by the leadership of the ruling movement in Trnava today. Vladimir Meciar indicated that he was striving to add another party to the government coalition. However, he did not say which party he had in mind.
