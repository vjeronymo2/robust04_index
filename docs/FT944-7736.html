941123 FT 23 NOV 94 / UK Company News: China Investment China Investment Trust raised net assets per share by 26 per cent to 139.9p at September 30, against 111p a year earlier. After-tax revenue for the six months was Pounds 191,243, compared with Pounds 47,259 for the shorter period from May 25 to September 30 1993. Earnings per share quadrupled to 1.2p (0.3p).
