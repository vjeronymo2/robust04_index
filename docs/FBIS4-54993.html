BFN [Text] Sarajevo, May 6 (AFP) -- A Bosnian Serb tank disappeared from UN surveillance Friday [6 May] less than a kilometre (half a mile) from the centre of Sarajevo while travelling from the Serb stronghold of Pale to barracks at Lukavica, on the capital's outskirts, a UN military source said. The source said the tank disappeared close to Grbavica, a downtown suburb held by the Serbs, in the exclusion zone around Sarajevo in which the United Nations has banned heavy weapons.
