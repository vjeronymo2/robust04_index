BFN [Excerpts] President Hashemi-Rafsanjani arrived in Esfahan this afternoon to meet the people and to open several economic and scientific projects. Here is a report from our colleagues in the Isfahan Center. [Correspondent] Mr Hashemi-Rafsanjani, president of our country, arrived in Isfahan this afternoon. [passage omitted] During his visit, Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani opened a miniature nuclear reactor at the Esfahan Atomic Center. This miniature reactor is equipped with a pneumatic system for sample transfer which facilitates the use of such applications as analysis by neutron excitation and production of radio isotopes. It is worth mentioning that the reactor is operating under the supervision of the International Atomic Energy Agency. [passage omitted]
