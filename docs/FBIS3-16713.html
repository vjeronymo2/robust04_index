Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, March 19 (AFP) -- At least six people were killed and 56 others were wounded Saturday in an explosion in a metro station in the Azerbaijani capital Baku, the Azeri news agency Assa Irada said. The agency said the blast was a "terrorist" action, although no further details were immediately available. For more than five years Azerbaijan has been fighting a bloody war with Armenian separatists over the disputed enclave of Nagorno-Karabakh in south western Azerbaijan.
