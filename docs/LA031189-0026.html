March 11, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

CSUN STUDENT PRESIDENT'S RESIGNATION

As a former president of the Associated Students at Cal State Northridge, I feel a need to comment on the revelation (Feb. 28) that this year's president failed to be enrolled as a student.

Every president knows the bylaws backward and forward and is aware of the requirement to be taking seven units. Had Lynn Westlund been a student activist or a minority, the CSUN administration probably would have attempted to remove her from office, as it attempted to remove four senators from office in 1983 just for failing to sign our financial statements.

If Westlund's actions are not criminal fraud or misrepresentation for taking a stipend and allocating funds, then they should be.

ZEKE ZEIDLER

Studio City
