BFN [From the "Evening Information Review" newscast] [Text] Death squads are being blamed for the murder of Guatemalan Constitutionality Court President Epaminondas Gonzalez in Guatemala City on the night of 1 April. According to Magistrate Edmundo Vasquez Martinez, paramilitary groups in Guatemala have not been dismantled, and assassinations and actions against peace and security continue. Meanwhile, it has been reported that Air Force planes are overflying Guatemala City, heavily armed soldiers have cordoned off the city, and Constitutionality Court magistrates are discussing the situation.
