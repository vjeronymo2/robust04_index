Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Palestinian sources in Tunis have informed our correspondent Yoni Ben-Menahem that the delegation of Palestinian figures, headed by Dr. Haydar 'Abd-al-Shafi, will meet tonight with PLO Executive Committee members in order to sum up the talks on PLO internal reforms. The figures have expressed their satisfaction with the results of the meetings that have taken place so far. They said that the PLO leadership has agreed, in principle, to set up a special consultative body to support the Executive Committee. A statement on the results of these talks is expected to be issued tomorrow.
