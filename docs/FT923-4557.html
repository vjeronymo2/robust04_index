920905 FT 05 SEP 92 / Dunkel plea on Korean rice South Korea should end its ban on rice imports and allow outsiders to supply up to 5 per cent of national consumption, Mr Arthur Dunkel, director-general of the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade, said yesterday during a visit to Seoul. Seoul has come under strong international pressure to lift the ban to help conclude the long-stalled Uruguay Round of Gatt talks, multilateral negotiations on world free trade.
