Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By 'Ala' al-Saftawi] [Excerpt] Gaza -- Muhammad Dahlan, head of the Security Committee in the talks with the Israelis, has denied a report published several days ago by HA'ARETZ concerning cooperation in the security sphere between Palestinian and Israeli security agencies. Contacted by phone at the Taba Hilton Hotel, Dahlan said that he had not made any statement to nor was he interviewed by this Israeli paper and that what had been attributed to him was false and without foundation.
