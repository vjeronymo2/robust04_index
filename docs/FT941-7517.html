940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Fairway advances to Pounds 2.25m Fairway Group, the USM-quoted provider of print facilities and distributor of educational supplies, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.25m for 1993. The result was not comparable to the Pounds 1.51m achieved in 1992 as it included a full year's contribution from Spectrum Marketing Services and for Castle Print Finishers from July 2 1993. Turnover amounted to Pounds 42.3m (Pounds 29.3m) including Pounds 225,000 (Pounds 1.22m) from acquisitions. With the exception of Fairway Business Forms all the group's principal companies contributed to the growth. Earnings per share rose from 2.9p to 4.79p and the total dividend is lifted to 3.35p (3.15p) with a proposed final of 2.25p.
