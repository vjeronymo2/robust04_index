BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Yezhov] [Text] Moscow, 24 May (ITAR-TASS) -- An intermission until 1400 has been announced in the talks between the heads of government of Russia and Ukraine on dividing up the Black Sea Fleet and settling the situation in Crimea. Crimean Deputy Prime Minister Yevgeniy Saburov took part in today's discussion of the problems of Russian-Ukrainian relations, which is into its second day today between delegations headed Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin and Ukrainian acting Prime Minister Yukhym Zvyahilskyy.
