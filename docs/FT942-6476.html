940530 FT 30 MAY 94 / International Company News: Canadian bank rises National Bank, Canada's sixth biggest bank, reported second-quarter net profit of CDollars 51.9m (USDollars 37.6m), or 27 cents a share, up 27 per cent from CDollars 40.1m, or 23 cents, a year earlier, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal First-half profit was CDollars 102.5m or 54 cents a share, an increase of 20 per cent from the previous year's CDollars 85.1m or 51 cents.
