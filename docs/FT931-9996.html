930211 FT 11 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Overseas net asset value rises Kleinwort Overseas Investment Trust had a net asset value of 224.64p at December 31, compared with a value of 185.39p some 12 months earlier. Net revenue increased from Pounds 2.68m to Pounds 2.84m. This produced earnings per share of 3.54p (3.34p). The proposed final dividend is lifted to 1.8p, bringing the total to 3.3p (3.2p).
