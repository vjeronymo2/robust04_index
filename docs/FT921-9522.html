920212 FT 12 FEB 92 / Credit figure: Correction OUTSTANDING consumer credit from finance houses, building societies and on bank credit cards that are part of the Visa or Mastercard system grew by Pounds 370m in 1991, compared with Pounds 2.9bn in 1989, and more than Pounds 3bn in 1990. In yesterday's FT it was incorrectly stated that the amount outstanding at the end of the year was Pounds 370m.
