930406 FT 06 APR 93 / Israelis raid village Israeli helicopters attacked a south Lebanon village yesterday minutes after guerrillas fired five Katyusha rockets into northern Israel, security sources said. Reuter reports from Sidon. Two helicopter gunships fired several rockets at the village of Yater on the tip of Israel's self-styled security zone in the south, they said. There were no casualties. In Tunis, the Palestinian leadership yesterday postponed a decision on whether its negotiators would attend the next round of Middle East peace talks with Israel.
