BFN [Unattributed report under the "NEGA Reports" rubric: "Altay Republic"] [Text] The third session of El-Kurultay -- the State Assembly -- has begun in Gornoaltaysk. The following questions have been submitted for the deputies' consideration: the republic's plan of action and guidelines of socioeconomic development in the period from 1994 to 1996; on standing commissions of the State Assembly of the Republic of Altay; the draft law "On the Right of Citizens To Acquire for Private Ownership and To Sell Plots of Land for Personal Subsidiary Farms, Summer Houses, Gardening, and Individual Housing Construction;" and the draft law on culture. In addition, it is planned to make amendments to the Law of the Republic of Altay "On Natural Resources and Their Use" and to consider the procedure of granting licenses for the use of the republic's natural resources.
