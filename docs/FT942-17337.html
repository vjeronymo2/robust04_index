940405 FT 05 APR 94 / Company News (This Week): Hewden Stuart - Other companies The UK's biggest independent plant hire group should provide a further indication of the recovery in UK construction on Thursday. The Glasgow-based company is expected to report pre-tax profits of about Pounds 18m for the year to January 31, well up on the previous year's Pounds 12m. As well as focusing on current trading, analysts will be interested in how the group is getting on with integrating the Hireplant assets, bought last July for Pounds 11m from BET.
