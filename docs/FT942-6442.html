940530 FT 30 MAY 94 / Company News (This Week): Horten - Other companies The stores group is holding its annual press conference in Dusseldorf today. German department stores sales dived in April after an unexpectedly good first quarter. Optimism about prospects for the rest of the year has taken a knock now that the 1994 pay round, resulting in real wage cuts, is starting to tell on spending power. Also today in Dusseldorf, the Mannesmann engineering and mobile telecoms group will hold a results press conference.
