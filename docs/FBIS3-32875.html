Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [From the "Diplomatic Report" column by Luiz Orlando Carneiro] [Text] No less than 16 Russian bankers have arrived in the country. Their main objective is to obtain detailed knowledge of our banking system's advanced computer network. Through 10 February, the Russian bankers will be visiting Sao Paulo. They are going to visit Febraban [Brazilian Federation of Banks], Bradesco [Brazilian Discount Bank] installations, the Sao Paulo State Federation (Fiesp), the informatics department of Itau Bank, and the Sao Paulo Commercial Association. The Russian bankers and executives represent the following financial organizations: Incombank, Promsstroibank, Roskombank, Unicombank, Chasprombank, Evrobank, Kuzbasprombank, Tarhany, Volgoprombank [all names as published], and the Mortgage Joint-Stock Bank [title in English].
