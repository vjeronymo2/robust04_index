Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 18 Jan (INA)--Leader President Saddam Husayn has received a cable of thanks from his brother Sudanese President Lieutenant General 'Umar Hasan Ahmad al-Bashir in reply to the cable of congratulations his excellency sent to him on the 38th anniversary of Sudan's independence. The cable states: With happiness and joy, we received your kind congratulations on the 38th anniversary of Sudan's independence. We thank you for your congratulations and take this opportunity to send to you our sincerest wishes of continued good health and happiness, and progress and prosperity to your fraternal people.
