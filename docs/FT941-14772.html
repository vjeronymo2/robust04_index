940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / Control Securities ex-head on trial The trial of Mr Nazmudin Virani, the former head of Control Securities, over his alleged role in the collapse of the Bank of Credit and Commerce International is due to start today at the Old Bailey. Mr Virani denies 14 charges including conspiracy to defraud, theft, furnishing false information and false accounting. The jury was sworn in yesterday. The trial is expected to last three months
