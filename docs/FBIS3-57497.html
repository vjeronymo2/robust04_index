Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Liliya Kuznetsova] [Text] Moscow, 26 January -- A meeting took place today between Russian President Boris Yeltsin and Ella Pamfilova, minister of social protection of the population. The president's conversation with the minister was connected with her recent statement that she refused to enter the new government. The minister's office stated that Ella Pamfilova's resignation had not yet been accepted and officially she is on leave. As for the meeting that has taken place, the participants did not reach agreement on conditions for Ella Pamfilova to enter the restructured government.
