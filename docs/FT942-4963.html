940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Prime People loss increases Prime People, Manchester-based specialist training group, reported a pre-tax loss up from Pounds 63,000 to Pounds 117,000 for the half year to end April. Mr Peter Hearn, chairman and chief executive, said it was anticipated that the group would continue to make a loss. The US business has been closed and the trading loss in respect of this discontinued business was Pounds 51,000. Turnover of the USM-traded group was down from Pounds 696,000 to Pounds 420,000. Losses per share were 0.2p (same).
