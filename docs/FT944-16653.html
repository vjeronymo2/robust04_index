941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Throgmorton Dual The split capital Throgmorton Dual Trust reported a rise in net asset value -from 718.3p to 727.3p per capital share - during the year to July 31. Net revenue also showed little change at Pounds 1.58m (Pounds 1.57m), for earnings of 6.86p (6.82p) per income share. A maintained final dividend of 1.85p holds the annual total at 7.1p.
