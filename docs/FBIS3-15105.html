Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow March 2 TASS -- The team of prosecutors at the trial of 1991 Soviet coup plotters plans to appeal against the court decision to drop criminal proceedings against the accused. On Tuesday the court dropped criminal charges against the defendants following the Duma-proclaimed amnesty. "The prosecution believes that the court decision contradicts the criminal-procedural code of the Russian Federation, according to which any court investigation which is underway should be carried out to the end and a guilty or non-guilty verdict passed", the office of the prosecutor general said in a statement received by TASS on Wednesday.
