Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] In the struggle against corruption, the Beijing Municipal Intermediate People's Court has persisted in sternly cracking down on serious economic criminals. On 7 February, it meted out punishments to three economic criminals in the first trial. Full-time researcher of the steel rolling institute of the Shoudu Iron and Steel Complex, Yang Liyu, was sentenced to death with a two-year reprieve and without political rights for life on the charge of taking bribes. Zhao Dongxiang, commissioner of the raw material section of the Beijing Steel Company of the Shoudu Iron and Steel Complex, was sentenced to 15 years of imprisonment with political rights deprived for three years for taking bribes. Wang Ning, director of the finance section of the Beijing No. 10 Radio Component Plant, was sentenced to death with a two-year reprieve and had his political rights taken away for life for bribe-taking, embezzlement, and misuse of public funds.
