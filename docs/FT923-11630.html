920723 FT 23 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Holders Tech edges ahead to Pounds 198,000 Holders Technology, the USM-quoted importer and distributor of equipment for the electronic engineering industry, lifted profits to Pounds 198,000 for the half-year to May 31. The increase from the previous Pounds 181,000 was achieved on turnover of Pounds 1.65m (Pounds 1.53m). The interim dividend is maintained at 2p, payable from earnings of 4.23p (3.84p).
