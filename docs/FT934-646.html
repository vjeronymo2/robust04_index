931223 FT 23 DEC 93 / UK Company News: API sells heating offshoot for Pounds 1.5m API Group, the packaging, coatings and office products company, has sold Diffusion Environmental Systems, its heating and ventilating business, to Benson Group, for Pounds 1.5m cash. Surrey-based Diffusion will be integrated into Benson's environmental control division where it will complement the existing businesses of Energy Technique and Benson Heating. Diffusion's turnover was Pounds 3.93m in the year to October 2 and its after-tax profit came to Pounds 116,000.
