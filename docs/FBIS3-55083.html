Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku and Stepanakert are still reporting great losses during fierce fighting in the zone of the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict. A well informed source in Azerbaijan's Defense Minister asserted in his interview with Interfax that several dozen Armenian soldiers were killed on Tuesday [4 January] during the hostilities waged near the Murovdag mountain ridge on the border of the Kelbadzharskiy and Agderin (Mardakertskiy) regions. Two artillery guns of the Karabakh army have been destroyed. According to Baku, the Armenians lost two tanks and an APC [armored personnel carrier] on other sections of the front. Meanwhile, sources in Stepanakert assert that the Azerbaijani army lost more than 70 soldiers, 4 tanks and one APC during the hostilities Tuesday. Stepanakert also asserts that the Karabakh party lost only three servicemen.
