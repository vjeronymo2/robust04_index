BFN [Text] Foreign Affairs Minister Mohamed Salah Dembri received today the Yemeni parliamentary delegation led by Mr. 'Abd al-Wahab Mahmud, deputy speaker of the parliament and chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee. The delegation is visiting Algeria in the framework of a mission aimed at informing and consulting with the Transitional National Council. A Foreign Ministry statement said that the talks dealt with the latest developments in Yemen. In this respect, the Yemeni delegation gave the parliament's view on the fratricidal fighting that is tearing the country apart. The delegation also expressed the parliament's desire to use every means to achieve a settlement of the conflict with the help of sister and friendly countries. The statement added that Mr. Dembri had insisted on the pressing need for the warring sides to stop the fighting in order to provide the minimum conditions for the resumption of the dialogue and open the way to a peaceful settlement of the conflict. In this context, the minister affirmed Algeria's readiness to contribute to the efforts aimed at ending the conflict between the brothers in Yemen.
