April 27, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

THANK YOU FOR NOT BASHING CHRISTIANS

Bravo! Bravo! There's been so many nasty things said recently about religion, Christianity in particular.

Some deserved, some not deserved. I was pleasantly shocked by Susan Christian's piece "The Last Temptation" (April 5). It was good, fair journalism. No Christian bashing. I almost fainted.

I, for one, am glad the Church is offering these services to the gay community. After all, it's terribly convenient to donate a few bucks to the (AIDS) cause, sit back, put your feet up and blame God by assuming that homosexuals don't want any help . . . and couldn't change even if they did. Some folks call it a cop-out.

Thanks again (Susan and The Times), and give us more.

Wiley B. Burnes,

Westminster
