941115 FT 15 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Bullers share sale Under court order, 5m shares (8.9 per cent) of Bullers owned by Marbank Marmara Bankasi have been sold to various investors, including certain UK institutions, a Paris-based investment bank and certain private individuals. Marmara's remaining 3.99m shares (7.1 per cent) may be sold in due course but not in the next six months.
