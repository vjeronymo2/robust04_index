January 12, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Federal National Mortgage Assn.: Fannie Mae said its profit for fiscal 1989 rose 59% to $807.3 million. The congressionally chartered private firm, which buys mortgages from banks and other lenders and repackages them as investment instruments, said "lower interest rates created a strong secondary market for fixed-rate mortgage purchases at attractive spreads and also provided an excellent environment for issuance of mortgage-backed securities."
