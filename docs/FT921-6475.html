920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / State cash for energy saving THE GOVERNMENT yesterday announced a Pounds 13m three-year scheme to help small and medium-sized businesses achieve energy savings. The Energy Management Assistance Scheme will meet 10 per cent of the project costs involved in improvements which are capable of achieving a 10 per cent improvement in energy efficiency. The scheme is open to companies employing up to 500 people. Mr John Wakeham, energy secretary, said large companies had responded to the call to use energy more efficiently, but smaller businesses had not.
