940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / UK Company News: EFM Income net asset value expands EFM Income Trust, the split capital investment trust, reported net asset value per ordinary share of 50.7p at April 30, against 45.9p a year earlier, after providing for the repayment cost of the zero dividend preference shares. The figures for the zero preference shares were 48.8p (43.7p). Total net assets showed an 11.2 per cent advance against 13.8 per cent for the benchmark FT-SE-A All-Share Index. Net revenue for the the year to the end of April was Pounds 688,000 (Pounds 755,000) for earnings per share of 4.59p (5p). A reduced final dividend of 1p is proposed making 4p (4.875p) for the year. To reflect the name change of its manager it is planned to change the name of the trust to Edinburgh Income Trust.
