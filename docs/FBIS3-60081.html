Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 13 KYODO -- Giorgio Giacomelli, undersecretary general of the United Nations International Drug Control Program [UNDCP], will visit Japan from January 18 to 23 to hold talks with Japanese officials on drug problems, Foreign Ministry officials said Thursday [13 January]. Giacomelli, who visited Japan in January 1992, is likely to call for further contributions from Japan to fight the drug problems, the officials said. He is expected to hold talks with Japanese Foreign Ministry officials, authorities on drugs and some Diet members, they said. He is also likely to seek the Japanese public's understanding on the importance of an international drug control system during his six-day stay in Japan, they said.
