Language: Thai Article Type:BFN [Text] A Yala-based Public Relations Department correspondent reported that Prime Minister Chuan Likphai and his delegation met Muslim leaders at the Yala Provincial Red Cross Society in Yala Province last night. On the occasion, the prime minister said that the government has tried to distribute wealth to rural areas throughout the country. The government has in particular promoted the growth triangle development project in southern provinces along with Malaysia and Indonesia. This project will help bring prosperity to this region. The prime minister said that the government has used a considerable amount of funds developing Yala Province in all fields. It has also tried to support religious activities. In conclusion, the prime minister thanked all the religious leaders for the cooperation they had given the government. In addition, the prime minister will preside over a ceremony to open new air routes in Tambon Sateng, Muang District, Yala Province, to help ease traffic problems.
