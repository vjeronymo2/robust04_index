930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Beradin Beradin Holdings, the rubber and palm oil producer, doubled its dividend to 1.1p after reporting a jump in 1992 pre-tax profits from Pounds 221,650 to Pounds 476,175. The company said that average palm oil prices during the year were about 15 per cent above 1991 levels and firmness had continued into 1993. Sales advanced to Pounds 1.02m (Pounds 617,556) and estate profit rose to Pounds 510,671 (Pounds 214,323). Earnings per share came through at 1.69p (0.74p).
