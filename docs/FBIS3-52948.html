Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Aden, Yemen, Jan 9 (AFP)--A gunman sprayed the Yemeni vice-president's house here with automatic fire but there were no casualties, informed sources said Sunday. The gunman was arrested after the dawn attack on Saturday, and it was not known if Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid was home at the time. Al-Bid has been at loggerheads with President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih for months, demanding the introduction of economic and political reforms before he will return to the capital Sanaa.
