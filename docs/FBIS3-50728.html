Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Council of State of the Republic of Cuba, at the suggestion of Culture Minister Armando Hart, has presented the Juan Marinero Order to prima ballerina Alicia Alonso. The order was given in recognition of the significant accomplishments attained during her brilliant artistic career and her invaluable contribution to the development of culture on the occasion of the 45th anniversary of the Cuban Ballet. This was also a happy coincidence for Alonso who is celebrating her birthday today. Likewise, the National Culture award was presented to a group of artists and technicians of the Grand Theater of Havana who have dedicated their lives to this important artistic endeavor.
