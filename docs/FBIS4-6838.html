BFN [Text] A dispute over the National Assembly's approval of the appointment of the acting prime minister is expected between the ruling and opposition parties. The Democratic Liberal Party [DLP] will hold a high-ranking post-holders meeting today to confirm the position that the president's decision to accept the resignation of Prime Minister Yi Hoe-chang has no legal or political flaw as it was an exercise of the president's own sovereign power. The DLP is expected to confirm the determination to consolidate the party's power to achieve the national goal of strengthening the country's competitiveness under closer party cooperation after the appointment of the new prime minister. The Democratic Party [DP] will hold an emergency executives' meeting today to demand that the prime minister's rights be stipulated in the Constitution and finalize its position to oppose the approval of the appointment of new acting Prime Minister Yi Yong-tok by the National Assembly. Fierce dispute between the ruling and opposition party is expected over the approval of the prime minister's appointment and over the National Assembly's investigation on the Sangmudae political fund case.
