Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn, January 19 (XINHUA) -- China's National People's Congress (NPC) has officially set up a China-Germany friendship group for parliamentary members. This was announced today by Qiao Shi, visiting chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, at a luncheon given by Peter Schmitz, chairman of the Germany-China Group for Parliamentary Members, in honor of Qiao here. Dai Jie, member of the NPC Standing Committee and a leader of the Financial Committee of the NPC, who is accompanying Qiao on the visit to Germany, is appointed head of the China-Germany Friendship Group. Dai, who attended the luncheon, said the China-Germany group will work for closer links and cooperation with its German counterpart.
