930122 FT 22 JAN 93 / International Company News: Dania victim of Danish crisis DENMARK'S agricultural crisis claimed a new victim yesterday when Dania Holding, the country's largest agricultural machinery group, with 950 employees and a turnover of approximately DKr1bn (Dollars 162m), suspended payments, writes Hilary Barnes in Copenhagen. Dania's main subsidiary is Dronningborg, manufacturer of combine harvesters, most of which are produced for sale in Europe by Massey Ferguson. Dania, which is an unlisted company, will try to arrange a financial reconstruction.
