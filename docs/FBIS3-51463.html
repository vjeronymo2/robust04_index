Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] ITAR-TASS has reported that on 14 January Rear Admiral Pedro Perez Betancourt, chief of the Cuban Revolutionary Navy, visited military sites in St. Petersburg during his third day of his visit to Russia. Perez Betancourt is visiting Russia as a guest of Admiral Feliks Gromov, commander of the Russian Fleet. He visited the Kustnezov Naval Academy and the main headquarters of Navigation and Oceanography in the former Leningrad.
