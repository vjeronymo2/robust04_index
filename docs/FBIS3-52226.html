Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Jordanian side, led by His Majesty King Husayn, and the Palestinian side led by Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, held a meeting soon after 'Arafat's arrival in Amman this afternoon, in which the peace process and latest developments were reviewed. The two sides emphasized the need to start a new stage of serious, long-term, and clear coordination in a manner that secures the rights of the Palestinian people on their soil, enables them to remain steadfast, alleviates their sufferings under occupation, and secures a free and honorable life with the aim of attaining a comprehensive, lasting, and just peace in the region. From the Jordanian side, the meeting was attended by the prime minister, the Royal Hashemite court chief, and his majesty's advisers. From the Palestinian side, the meeting was attended by [PLO Executive Committee members] Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh and Yasir 'Amr, and ['Arafat's adviser] Akram Haniyah. 'Arafat left Amman a short while ago.
