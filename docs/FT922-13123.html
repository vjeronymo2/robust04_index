920414 FT 14 APR 92 / International Company News: Microsoft keeps up pace of growth MICROSOFT sales and earnings for the first three months of this year grew at about the same pace as in the last quarter of 1991, said Mr Bill Gates, president of the US computer software group, AP-DJ reports from Paris. Microsoft turned in net fourth-quarter income of Dollars 79.9m, a rise of 76 per cent.
