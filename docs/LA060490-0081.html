June 4, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY: CONSUMER NEWS

Emissions Control Recall: General Motors Corp. said it is recalling 410,000 1985-model Buicks, Oldsmobiles and Pontiacs to correct a problem with their electronic emissions control systems. Affected by the recall are three models equipped with the 3.0-liter V-6 engine: Buick Regal Somerset, Oldsmobile Calais and Pontiac Grand Am. Also affected are four models with the 3.8-liter V-6: Buick Century, Buick Electra Park Avenue, Oldsmobile Cutlass Ciera and Oldsmobile 98.
