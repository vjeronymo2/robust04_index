941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / World Trade News: Algeria and Italy in gas deal Algeria's Sonatrach energy group and Italy's Ente Nazionale Idrocarburi (ENI) reached a partnership agreement yesterday which will almost double Algerian natural gas exports to Italy to 26bn cu m by 1997. Sonatrach's managing director, Mr Abdelhaq Bouhafs, said the agreement was aimed at 'reinforcing co-operation, developing hydrocarbon prospecting and production'. Sonatrach and ENI would produce 200,000 tons of liquefied petroleum gas (LPG) per year starting in 1995 for domestic consumption and export. The agreement was initialled in Casablanca yesterday, and would be signed in Rome in a week's time, Mr Bouhafs said.
