BFN [Vitaliy Denisov report from Tbilisi under the "News" rubric: "Russian Troops in the Transcaucasus: What Do We Call You Now?"] [Text] The talks of a delegation of the Georgian parliament with deputies of the Russian Federation State Duma on Russian military bases have ended in failure. As Revaz Adamiya, member of the Georgian parliament, has stated, the draft agreement proposed by the Russian side was turned down over fundamental differences in views on the status of Russian troops in the Transcaucasus.
