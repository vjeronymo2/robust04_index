BFN [Text] Beijing, May 16 (XINHUA) -- A delegation of the Chinese National People's Congress (NPC), headed by Tian Jiyun, vice-chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, returned here today after concluding its official goodwill visit to New Zealand, Western Samoa, Papua New Guinea and Australia. Meeting the delegation at the airport were secretary-general of the NPC Standing Committee Cao Zhi, deputy secretary-general of the NPC Standing Committee Wang Shuming, and diplomatic envoys from Australia, Papua New Guinea and New Zealand.
