940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Safeland improves to Pounds 925,000 Safeland, the property investment and trading company, reported pre-tax profits up from a restated Pounds 156,000 to Pounds 925,000 in the year to the end of March. There was a further acquisition under the facilities agreed with Norwich Union. The group is paying Pounds 610,000 for a retail property in Cambridgeshire, with gross rental income of Pounds 75,000. Turnover was static at Pounds 13.8m. Earnings per share were 2.93p (0.49p) and a final dividend of 0.79p makes a total of 1.39p (0.5p). The company is ultimately controlled by Safeland Holdings Corporation of Panama.
