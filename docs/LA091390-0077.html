September 13, 1990, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPORTS ET CETERA

Point Loma Nazarene (1-2) defeated Redlands, 2-0, in a nonconference match.

Blake Oakland scored after 64 minutes on an assist by Andre Strandberg, who scored at 89 minutes with Steve Cokkinis assisting PLNC is 1-2; Redlands is 0-1-1.

The UC San Diego men won their sixth consecutive shutout, defeating host Whittier College, 8-0, in a nonconference match. The Tritons (6-1), ranked fourth in Division III, have allowed only one goal, to USD, in a 1-0 opening loss.
