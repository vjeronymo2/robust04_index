920715 FT 15 JUL 92 / International Company News: GM office staff job cuts begin GENERAL MOTORS said it would eliminate about 4,725 jobs in the reorganisation of its marketing, corporate communications and other corporate staff functions Reuter reports. GM said the cuts were part of the 9,000 white collar jobs it had planned to eliminate by the end of 1992. It said the job cuts and other changes were the latest big management move in the creation of GM's North American Operations (NAO), which now manages GM's North American vehicle and components operations. GM plans to reduce its current 13,500-employee central corporate staff by 70 per cent.
