941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / Government to sample lettuces The Ministry of Agriculture will take random samples of UK-grown lettuces this winter to monitor the use of fungicides. The move follows a report by a government working party that showed that 26 per cent of lettuces surveyed last year contained unacceptable pesticide residues. This was an increase from 17 per cent the previous season. The Department of Health has said the residues pose no risk to human health.
