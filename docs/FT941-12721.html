940128 FT 28 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Govett American Smaller assets up Govett American Smaller Companies Trust reported a net asset value of 176.59p per share at December 31, up from 137.89p at the end of 1992. The trust, which aims to achieve capital growth through a portfolio of North American smaller companies, incurred an available deficit of Pounds 42,000 (profit of Pounds 24,000) for the six months to end-December. Losses per share were 0.17p (earnings of 0.1p).
