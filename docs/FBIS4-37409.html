BFN [Text] Comrade 'Abdallah al-Ahmar, assistant secretary general of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party, received this morning the visiting Yemeni parliamentary delegation, headed by Sultan Sa'id al-Burkani. The meeting was attended by Shakir Is'ayyid, vice chairman of the Executive Committee of the Syrian Arab Parliamentary Branch. Talks centered on latest developments in the Yemeni arena. They also stressed the need to halt fighting among the Yemeni brothers and to return to dialogue in order to save Yemen from destruction and fragmentation and to preserve the Yemeni people's unity.
