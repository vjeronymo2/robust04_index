940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / Radio 5 opens for business Two of the main business programmes on BBC Radio 4 are to move to the new Radio 5 Live news and sports network due to launch on March 28. The Financial World Tonight and The Financial Week are moving to the new network as part of its expanded financial and business coverage. Ms Jenny Abramsky, the network's controller, promised that it would be fun, would take risks and would try to involve listeners by putting them in touch with people in the news and BBC correspondents.
