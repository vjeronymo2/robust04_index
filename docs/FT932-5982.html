930601 FT 01 JUN 93 / Management (The Growing Business): EC and CIS exchange ideas -In A Nutshell Chambers of commerce in the European Community are to carry out a bilateral programme of visits and training with their counterparts in the Commonwealth of Independent States. The exchange programme, known as Mercure TACIS, will involve 40 western participants. The contacts are expected to lead on to mutual assistance deals between chambers and enhanced co-operation between businesses in the EC and the CIS.
