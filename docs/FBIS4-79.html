BFN [Text] The Independent Electoral Commission [IEC] is now dealing with 385 complaints of electoral code breaches, and has issued 13 summonses to political parties. Fifteen matters have been referred to mediation, while 50 complaints files have been closed because they were frivolous or settled, investigations unit head Kgomotse Moroke said at an IEC briefing yesterday. It was proving difficult to get statements from witnesses. The directorate's head, Albert Mokwena, said voter education gaps has been identified among women in rural areas, the informal sector and public servants, particularly the army and police. The IEC was penetrating kwaZulu by way of educating scholars, who in turn were educating families.
