940725 FT 25 JUL 94 / Company News (This Week): Second-quarter growthputs BAT on course - Other companies BAT Industries: The tobacco and financial services group, is expected on Wednesday to announce an increase in underlying interim profits to about Pounds 900m before tax, although the range stretches from about Pounds 880m to Pounds 920m. Last year's figure was Pounds 902m, but that included a Pounds 123m exceptional profit from a brand swap deal.
