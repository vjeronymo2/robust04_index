March 16, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Penn & Teller, known for thrills and chills, held an underwater press conference to promote their two-week run at the Wiltern that starts March 27. Penn Jillette, left, did the talking while Teller apparently held his breath underwater for about 7 1/2 minutes. Proceeds from the opening night will benefit the Actors' Fund.ALEENE MacMINN
