930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Spending on Charter rises CENTRAL spending on the Citizen's Charter was Pounds 2.14m last year, the government said in the Commons yesterday. Mr William Waldegrave, the Citizen's Charter minister, gave the 1992-93 figures in a question time clash with Mr Tony Banks (Lab Newham NW). Mr Waldegrave said the spending was Pounds 1.6m in 1991-92. Other costs were reflected under individual public services' figures, he added. So far 32 Charters have been produced with one planned this year for further and higher education students.
