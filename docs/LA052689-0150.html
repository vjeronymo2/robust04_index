May 26, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

DALEY LEAVES MAGNOLIA FOR FULLERTON COLLEGE JOB

Paul Daley has resigned as baseball coach at Magnolia High School to become an assistant at Fullerton College, Magnolia Athletic Director Bob Rishel said Thursday.

Magnolia was 16-31-1 during Daley's two seasons.

Daley was a four-year starter for Magnolia from 1980-83. He also played two years at Fullerton College.

"We're looking for people right now," Rishel said. "Our biggest problem is that the job doesn't include a teaching position."
