BFN [Text] The Organization of the Islamic Revolution Army for the Liberation of Palestine [munazzamat jaysh al-thawrah al-islamiyah li tahrir filastin] has claimed responsibility for the operation it carried out against the Zionist occupation forces in the Rshaf area in the occupied zone of southern Lebanon on Saturday [14 May]. In a statement, the organization said its Gaza Fedayeen group penetrated the Zionist enemy's front in Rshaf, killing and wounding a number of Zionist soldiers. Following this heroic operation, the Zionist enemy forces shelled the liberated areas adjacent to the occupied zone in southern Lebanon with mortar and artillery.
