BFN [Except] Kamal al-Shadhili, minister of People's Assembly and Consultative Council affairs and assistant secretary general of the National Democratic Party [NDP], has announced that the president of the republic has issued a decree which incorporates the leaders of the opposition parties into the National Dialogue Committee, which will be set up by a decree in the next few days. The 35-member committee will consolidate the march of democracy and involve all sectors of the public in serving the national interest. [passage omitted]
