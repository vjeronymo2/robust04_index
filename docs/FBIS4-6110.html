BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 15 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Another nine people have joined the going on here at the main square of the Mongolian capital political hunger strike demanding the resignation of the government which is carried out by the Mongolian Democratic Association, its number reaching now 31. So far any information referring to a worsening health state of anyone participating in the action since 13 April has not been reported.
