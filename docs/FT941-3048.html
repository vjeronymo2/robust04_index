940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / London Stock Exchange: Other big moves Profit-takers took the shine from Sleepy Kids' return to profits, the shares sliding 12 to 105p. Improved profits and a French acquisition from Spandex, a supplier to the sign industry, helped the stock surge 35 to 585p.
