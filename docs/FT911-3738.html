910423 FT 23 APR 91 / Sir Geoffrey Howe SIR Geoffrey Howe, (above) who resigned from the government last November in protest at Mrs Margaret Thatcher's stance on Europe, is to join Jones Day Reavis and Pogue, a US law firm, as a London-based consultant on European matters. Sir Geoffrey, a Queen's Counsel, is aged 64 and has announced that he will not stand for his Surrey East parliamentary seat at the next general election. He has recently taken on non-executive directorships at Glaxo and BICC and has joined the International Advisory Council of the Institute for International Studies at Stanford University, California. Sir Geoffrey's role with the firm will be to act as a special adviser on strategic and investment activities in Europe, the Middle East and Asia.
