BFN [Text] Tunis, 8 May (MENA)--PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat has issued a decree considering all Palestinian employees in various administrations in the occupied territories as employees of the Palestinian National Authority. Jibril al-Rujub, 'Arafat's adviser for the occupied territories affairs, said 'Arafat had decided to appoint Lawyer Ghassan al-Shak'ah as provisional mayor of Nabulus until elections are held in the self-rule areas in the future.
