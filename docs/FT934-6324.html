931125 FT 25 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Anglo Irish Bank Anglo Irish Bank lifted pre-tax profits to IPounds 9.25m (Pounds 8.8m) for the year ended September 30. Last year's profits of IPounds 6.38m were restated in accordance with FRS 3. Mr A Gerard Murphy, chairman, said that despite the turbulent conditions of the first four months the loan book emerged relatively unscathed from high interest rates. Earnings per share worked through at 5.7p (4.36p) and a final dividend of 2p is recommended for an unchanged 3.36p total.
