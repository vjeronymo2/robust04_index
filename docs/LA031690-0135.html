March 16, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

WATER SPORT: First BMWs, then hotel boxing matches and now it's . . . underwater vacations: Orange County scuba shops (C15) report a business boom as a result of young professionals taking scuba diving courses. But few are setting a webbed foot in California waters. . . . "I do not cold-water dive," insisted Diane Stingham of Travel Adventures, who books tours to warm-water climes. . . . Favorite vacation spots: Montego Bay, Cozumel and Micronesia. Mark I. Pinsky and David Reyes
