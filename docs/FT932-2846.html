930617 FT 17 JUN 93 / BR puts Red Star on the market BRITISH RAIL yesterday put its loss-making Red Star parcels business up for sale. Red Star operates from 250 stations, employs 1,200 people and specialises in same-day and next-day deliveries of parcels carried by train. The sale was authorised by the government last year. Prospective buyers have until August 13 to bid and the sale will be completed by the end of the year.
