Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shchegoleva] [Text] Sochi March 14 TASS -- Georgian troops continue to mass along the Inguri River separating Georgia from Abkhazia, according to the Abkhazian Defence Ministry. The ministry said subversive groups are infiltrating the Galiy District. To combat crime and prevent possible acts of sabotage, the Abkhazian Government has ruled to establish special OMON [Special Purpose Militia Detachment] troops, Abkhazian Ministry of the Press and Information officials told ITAR-TASS on Monday. Tuesday, March 15, will mark a year since the failure of the Abkhazian offensive on Sukhumi, capital of the independence-seeking republic, which resulted in mass casualties and heavy damage. Tomorrow residents of Abkhazia will mourn the victims of the war.
