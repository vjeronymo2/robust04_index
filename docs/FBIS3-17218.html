Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Armenian Foreign Ministry Press and Public Relations Service has reported that First Deputy Foreign Minister Zhirair Liparityan today received a high-ranking NATO delegation led by Tugay Ozseri, deputy secretary general of NATO. The principles of the Partnership for Peace program offered by NATO to former East European and Soviet republics in January were discussed during the meeting. The Armenian authorities are examining Armenia's participation in that program.
