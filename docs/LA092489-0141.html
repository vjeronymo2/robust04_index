September 24, 1989, Sunday, Southland Edition

BOY SHOT WHILE FATHER WAS TESTING A GUN DIES

A 9-year-old boy died early Saturday from wounds suffered when he was shot in the head by his father, who was testing a gun for a friend in another room and apparently did not know it was loaded. The bullet from a .357 magnum crashed through a wall, ricocheted off some furniture and hit the boy Thursday night.
