June 26, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Donald E. FitzGerald has been named to the position of vice president, provider relations for California Dental Health Plan, Tustin.

He was previously with Newport Dental Plan, where he served in a similar capacity for four years.

In his new position, FitzGerald will be responsible for recruitment, quality assurance and administrative activities for the company's more than 900 dental offices and for the provider aspects of the company's prepaid vision plans.
