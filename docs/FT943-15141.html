940711 FT 11 JUL 94 / UK Company News: BCE raises Pounds 2.5m for acquisition BCE Holdings, the USM-quoted distributor of snooker, billiards and pool products, is raising a net Pounds 2.5m through an underwritten 5-for-4 rights issue at 8p a share to help pay for an acquisition. The company, where a new management took charge in December, is buying Keabeech, which operates amusement arcades, for Pounds 1.89m, satisfied by Pounds 1.2m cash and the balance in loan notes. The vendors are retaining three arcades in the north east of England leaving BCE with the 10 London sites. In 1993 the 10 London arcades achieved operating profits amounting to Pounds 685,000 on turnover of Pounds 1.67m. Net assets at the end of the period were Pounds 2m. BCE is also selling two properties for a total of Pounds 580,000. However, the sales will result in book losses of Pounds 227,000.
