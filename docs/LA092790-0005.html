September 27, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

REASONS FOR S&L CRISIS

To quote from The Times: "The S&L calamity will cost the average American family more than $20 a month for the next 30 years," and, "Vice President George Bush headed a task force on 'regulation of financial services' that could have plugged the hole as early as 1983."

Read my lips, indeed!

PETER M. WILLIAMS

Del Mar
