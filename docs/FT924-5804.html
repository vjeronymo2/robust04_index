921125 FT 25 NOV 92 / Company failure figures fall ALTHOUGH fewer businesses started up in the third quarter of this year than in the same period of 1991, fewer companies failed, according to Barclays Bank's Small Business Bulletin. A total of 109,000 new businesses started up, a decline of 10 per cent on the same period of 1991 while 129,000 businesses closed, 9 per cent fewer.
