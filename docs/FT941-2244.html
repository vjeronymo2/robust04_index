940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / Observer: High table What is it about even the most sophisticated women's networks that makes them ever so slightly naff? After last Friday's seminar on Power and the Media, organised by the pukka women's networking organisation Forum UK, delegates were allocated to 'dine-arounds' (ugh]) in members' houses, while on Saturday there followed an 'optional' lunch with Lord and Lady Astor. Despite the cover charge of Pounds 75 a head, Lord Astor's private Kentish home was 'oversubscribed'. Luckily, Lord Mountbatten volunteered to feed and water the overflow.
