BFN [Text] The Netherlands is prepared to contribute a million dollars to a salvage operation for the Russian nuclear submarine Komsomolets in order to prevent an ecological disaster. The Komsomolets sank five years ago in the Barents Sea. It is rusting so quickly that there is a danger of plutonium being released from the wreckage this year. The Netherlands has already had extensive talks with Russia about the nature and the costs of a salvage operation. If the two countries agree, the Dutch salvage companies Smit Internationale and Heerema Offshore will both be involved in the operation.
