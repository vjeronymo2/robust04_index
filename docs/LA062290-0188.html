June 22, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

EGYPTIAN KILLS SELF OVER TEAM'S LOSS

A 40-year-old Egyptian man hanged himself over Egypt's elimination from the World Cup finals in a 1-0 loss to England Thursday, police said.

"My life came to an end when Egypt's match with England ended," a suicide note said.

Police said today that Sayed Abdel-Al, a government employee said to have been suffering from depression, was found dead in his apartment in Damietta.
