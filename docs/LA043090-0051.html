April 30, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

2 KILLED, 2 WOUNDED BY VENGEFUL TEEN-AGER

A teen-ager enraged by youths he felt had insulted his girlfriend lured them to a Bronx schoolyard with a promise of drugs, forced them to kneel and gunned them down, killing two and wounding two, police said Sunday.

The alleged gunman surrendered Sunday morning to detectives and was charged with two counts of murder, two counts of attempted murder and other charges.
