BFN [Text] Jerusalem, May 4 KYODO -- Japanese Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa arrived in Tel Aviv on Wednesday [4 May] afternoon from Cairo as part of his Middle East trip, which will also take him to Jordan, Syria and the occupied territories. In Cairo, he met with Egyptian President Husni Mubarak and PLO Leader Yasir 'Arafat and attended the signing ceremony of a historic agreement on limited Palestinian self-rule for Gaza and Jericho.
