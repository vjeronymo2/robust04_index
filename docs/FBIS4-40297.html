BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Yuriy Kozlov and Boris Krivoshey] [Text] Moscow June 16 TASS -- Russia would like to encourage closer cooperation and further integration between the country- members of the Commonwealth of Independent States. "We are ready for as much integration as our partners are", announced Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev on Thursday. Speaking at a foreign policy council meeting devoted to the further integration of the CIS countries and to Russian interests, Kozyrev noted the appearance of a lot of far-reaching ideas which "show the urge for closer integration". "At the same time, we would like to avoid a situation in which the proposal and discussion of such promising ideas hinders us in resolving concrete pressing problems, or distracts our attention from them", Kozyrev said.
