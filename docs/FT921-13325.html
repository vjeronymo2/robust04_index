920122 FT 22 JAN 92 / International Company News: UK bank takes stake in Turkish brokerage KLEINWORT Benson, the British merchant bank has bought a 7 per cent stake in Turkey's Global Menkul Degerler brokerage for Dollars 523,000, Reuter reports from Istanbul. The sale was part of a 43 per cent private placement of Global shares to increase its capital by TL10bn to TL15bn. Others buyers were Pictet & Cie, a leading private Swiss bank, GT Hong Kong, with a total of USDollars 12bn in assets management, investment firm Emerging Markets Washington and Global employees. Global's 1991 trading volume exceeded TL1,800bn.
