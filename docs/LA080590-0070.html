August 5, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

L.A. VS. ORANGE COUNTY

As far as the rest of the country is concerned Orange County is nothing, nil, zero, zilch.

Plain and simple it does not exist. This country revolves around two cities: New York and L.A. Sorry, but that's the way it is.

If one can put up with a sea of provincial mental midgets, Orange County is a pleasant place to live.

ROBERT HUNT

Garden Grove
