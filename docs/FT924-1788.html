921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Pittencrieff in Dollars 6.2m acquisition Pittencrieff, the oil and gas services company, has expanded its interests in mobile communications by paying Dollars 6.2m (Pounds 4.1m) cash for Commercial Radio Service and its sister company Mounts Tower Leasing. In the year to September 30 1991 profits for the two companies were Dollars 810,000. Gross billings are about Dollars 305,000 a month.
