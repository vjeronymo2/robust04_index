BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow May 12 TASS -- Russia called on warring Yemeni leaders to stop fighting and said it is ready to promote an end to the conflict and help ensure peace in the country. "The government of the Russian Federation again insistently calls on the warring sides to immediately cease fire and reach an agreement on armistice", the Russian Government said in a statement on Thursday. "The Yemeni leaders should find strength to make the final choice towards a peaceful political settlement of contradictions. Moscow is sure that peaceful ways out of the Yemeni crisis do exist", according to the statement. "Moscow believes that Yemeni leaders realise the inadmissibility and senselessness of the continuing bloodshed, the inevitable consequences of the fratricidal war for the future of the Yemeni people", it added. "In the current critical situation it is especially important to be above emotions and settling accounts, to show state wisdom, abide by common sense and long-term interests of the country and its citizens", according to the document. The government stressed that the tragic developments in Yemen cause great concern and reiterated that it is ready to actively promote efforts aimed at a peaceful settlement of the conflict, at restoring peace and quietness in the ancient country.
