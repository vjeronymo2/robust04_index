930514 FT 14 MAY 93 / International Company News: Aisin Seiki ahead Cost-cutting helped Aisin Seiki, Japanese car parts manufacturer, to a 7.2 per cent increase in pre-tax profits to Y15.3bn for the year to the end of March, writes Charles Leadbeater in Tokyo. Aisin said it offset a Y6.5bn increase in personnel and foreign exchange losses by eliminating other costs worth Y7.5bn. Aisin, affiliated to Toyota, reported a 1.4 per cent drop in sales to Y545bn.
