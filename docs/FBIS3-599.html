Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bus loads of people have begun arriving at Empangeni on the Natal north coast for a march organized by the Inkatha Youth Brigade. A march will be held in support of Zulu King Goodwill Zwelithini's demand for a sovereign kingdom in Natal-kwaZulu. Motorists have been advised to use the new toll road between Umtunzini and Empangeni, as the old N2 will be occupied by the marchers.
