930724 FT 24 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Tesco Tesco, the food retailer, has exchanged contracts for the purchase of a 50,000 sq ft retail and office investment in Cheapside, in the City of London. Tesco is to pay the vendor, Wates City, Pounds 12.7m for the property, and has also exchanged contracts with the existing ground and first floor occupiers - Austin Reed, Lloyds Bank and Alfred Marks - to enable it to create a 16,000 sq ft Metro store. The remaining parts of the building are fully let to Lloyds Bank and Alfred Marks, who will continue with their tenancies. The development is Tesco's fourth London Metro, and will be ready to open in early 1994.
