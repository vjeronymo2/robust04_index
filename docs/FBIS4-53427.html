BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 18 -- Vice Prime Minister Phan Van Khai received here on May 17 Danish Minister for Development Cooperation Ms Helle Degn who was leading a Danish trade delegation on a Vietnam visit. Vice Prime Minister [P.M.] Phan Van Khai hailed the results of the meetings between Minister Degn with Vietnamese Government agencies and organizations concerned on the friendly relation and economic and trade cooperation between the two countries in the coming years. The vice prime minister stressed that Vietnam would do its best, together with Denmark to effectively carry out the programme of economic and trade cooperation between the two countries for mutual interest. Minister Degn informed Vice P.M. Khai that the Danish Government had decided to take part in a long term programme for development and cooperation with Vietnam. She said that members of her delegation were satisfied at the results of their contacts with their Vietnamese partners in order to establish cooperation in various forms. During her stay in Hanoi, Minister Degn and her entourage also had working sessions with the State Planning Committee and the Chamber of Commerce and Industry and visited some economic and cultural establishments.
