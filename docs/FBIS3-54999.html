Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valentina Ivanova] [Text] Cheboksary, 5 Jan -- Boris Yeltsin greeted Nikolay Fedorov on his election as Chuvashia's first president. "I am confident that you will proceed further with economic reforms for the sake of making the life of the Chuvash people dignified and that you will facilitate the strengthening of Russia's constitutional foundations. I look forward to carrying out constructive work together," emphasized the Russian Federation president in his telegram. Today SOVETSKAYA CHUVASHIA published the presidential greetings as the first message on a whole page of similar greetings which continue to arrive for the 35 year old former Russian Justice Minister Nikolay Fedorov, who was elected to Chuvashia's highest state post on New Year's eve.
