Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, Jan. 24 KYODO -- Retail rice prices rose an average 0.6 percent to 1.6 percent in December, the Food Agency said Monday [24 January], citing its nationwide survey. The agency said the price of top-grade glutinous rice gained 39 yen to 6,323 yen per 10 kilograms, while that of the second highest- grade rice moved up 83 yen to 5,754 yen. The price of middle-grade rice rose 8.3 yen to 5,379 yen per 10 kilograms, the agency said. In Kanagawa Prefecture, west of Tokyo, the price of the highest- grade rice climbed as much as 584 yen, or 10.9 percent, to 5,948 yen per 10 kilograms, the agency said.
