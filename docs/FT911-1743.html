910503 FT 03 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Gecos to sell Empire stake to Redoute Gecos, the Italian food retailer, will sell its 24.2 per cent stake in Empire Stores, the UK home shopping business, to Redoute Catalogue, the French mail order company. Redoute has already declared its 125p per share cash bid wholly unconditional after receiving valid acceptances for 50.1 per cent of Empire's share capital.
