930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / International Company News in Brief: Singapore Aerospace Singapore Aerospace, the aircraft maintenance group, unveiled net profits of SDollars 24m (Dollars 15m) for 1992, up 13 per cent on the previous year's SDollars 21.2m, AP-DJ reports from Singapore. The directors expect profits to advance at a similar rate in 1993. Sales rose 13 per cent in 1992 to SDollars 421.3m from SDollars 373.8m. The dividend is being maintained at 2.5 cents a share on earnings per share 13 per cent ahead at 8 cents against 7.1 cents.
