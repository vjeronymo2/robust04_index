Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] [Announcer] Argentine President Carlos Menem arrived in Spain at a few minutes after 1000 [0900 GMT] for a four-day official visit to our country. At Barajas airport, President Menem made his first statements on Spanish soil, and our colleague Hector Arenas has them. Good morning. [Arenas] Good morning. I am happy to be in Spain, a country that I learned about beginning with my childhood, said Argentine President Carlos Menem on his arrival in Madrid. He said that Spanish investment in Argentina has doubled, and is expected to exceed $6 billion in the next few years. As for the purchase of a further share in Aerolineas Argentinas by Iberia, Menem told Radio Nacional that it is practically resolved and that there will be a solution. [Begin Menem recording] Practically everything is completed between Spain and Argentina. Some instruments have to be improved. Things are going very well; there are no problems. [end recording]
