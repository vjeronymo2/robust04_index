Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 21 Jan (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin held a telephone conversation today with FRG Federal Chancellor Helmut Kohl. Both leaders exchanged views on certain topical questions concerning bilateral relations and world politics. Individual aspects of the Russian president's visit to the FRG, due in May this year, were discussed, along with the planned festivities marking the completion of the Russian troops' withdrawal from Germany, the press service of the president of the Russian Federation reports. Boris Yeltsin informed Helmut Kohl about the domestic political situation in Russia and about the start of the Federal Assembly's work. President Yeltsin emphasized the invariability of the Russian leadership's course toward democratic transformations in the country.
