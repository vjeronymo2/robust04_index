Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Damascus, 13 Feb (MENA) -- U.S. Ambassador in Syria Christopher Ross has stressed his country's seriousness in establishing a comprehensive, just peace in the region. He said: Let us wait and see the outcome of the Arab-Israeli talks in Washington the day after tomorrow, that is, Tuesday. In a statement to the MENA correspondent in Damascus, Ross ruled out the possibility of U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher's touring the region's countries at present.
