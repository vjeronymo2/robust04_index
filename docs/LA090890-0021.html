September 8, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The fifth annual Greater Los Angeles Criterium, conducted by the L.A. County Dept. of Parks and Recreation, will be held on the streets of Monterey Park and East Los Angeles on Sunday, with the first race scheduled for 7 a.m.

Eight of the races are open to riders licensed by the U.S. Bicycling Federation. Two others are open to the public, with the men competing at 11:45 a.m. and the women at 12:45 p.m.
