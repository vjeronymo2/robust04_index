May 3, 1990, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: CARLSBAD

A man and a woman were found dead Wednesday in an apparent murder-suicide, police said.

Derick Wesley White, 38, and Diana Lynn White, 43, were found about 6:30 a.m. in the master bedroom of a home in the 2700 block of Flower Fields Way, according to police. A handgun was found in the bedroom.

A preliminary investigation revealed the incident to be a murder-suicide, police said, with both victims suffering gunshot wounds. The investigation is continuing.
