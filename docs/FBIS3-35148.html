Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, 17 Feb (INA)--Sa'd 'Abd-al-Majid al-Faysal, first under secretary of the Iraqi Foreign Ministry, arrived in Tehran today. Al-Faysal's visit to Tehran seeks to resume the high-level bilateral negotiations which were halted in November. The week-long talks in Tehran will deal with Iraqi prisoners of war whom Iran is still holding in violation of Islamic law and international norms. The talks will also deal with expanding bilateral relations.
