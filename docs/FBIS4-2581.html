BFN [Text] A Lesotho ministerial delegation to the People's Republic of China led by the honorable minister of foreign affairs, Mr. Molapo Qhobela, has arrived in Beijing. The minister has been invited by his Chinese counterpart for discussions on the resumption of diplomatic links between Lesotho and China. The minister is accompanied by the principal secretary for foreign affairs, Miss (Ntabiseng Mmonoko), and the acting director for political affairs in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Mr. (Victor Muteto). The delegation is due to return home on 17 April.
