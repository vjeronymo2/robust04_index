BFN [Text] It is quite probable that in the near future the Defense Ministry of Kirghizia [Kyrgyzstan] will embark on the formation of another subunit forming part of the CIS peacemaking forces. As an RIA correspondent has been informed, it is envisaged that Kirghiz servicemen will defend the interests of the Commonwealth on the mobile [podvizhnaya] border between Georgia and Abkhazia. This was occasioned by the agreement between Russia and Georgia on introduction of Russian peacemaking forces.
