Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 8 Jan -- An IBERIA correspondent learned that during a distribution of a humanitarian aid in front of the office of the Georgian Greens party, in Mushtaidi Park in Tbilisi, a hungry, emaciated crowd of people stormed the office and plundered the humanitarian aid. Two citizens suffered serious wounds and the owner of the humanitarian aid, (Kostas Papanastasiu) saved himself by running away.
