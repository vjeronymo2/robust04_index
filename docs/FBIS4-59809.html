BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received Arab League Secretary General 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid in the People's Palace at 1330 today. Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' attended the meeting. The Talks dealt with Arab conditions and the Arab League's role at the present juncture. Discussion also touched on the regrettable situation in Yemen and the Middle East peace process. The Arab League secretary general briefed the president on the results of the 26th round of the joint Arab action higher committee which was held in Damascus and the decisions and recommendations it adopted on joint Arab action in the next phase.
