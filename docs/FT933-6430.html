930827 FT 27 AUG 93 / UK Company News: ICI director makes Pounds 0.17m on shares sale Mr Chris Hampson, executive director of Imperial Chemical Industries, gained Pounds 172,218 after exercising share options granted under its senior staff share option scheme. Mr Hampson exercised 114,812 share options at 540p a share and sold them at 690p. He still has options over 67,638 shares.
