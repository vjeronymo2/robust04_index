Agency Information Collection Activities Under OMB Review The GSA hereby gives notice under the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1980 that it is requesting the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) to renew expiring information collection 3090&hyph;0243, Fixed Price Contracts. The General Services Administration Acquisition Regulation (GSAR) prescribes the use of an economic price adjustment clause in Federal Supply Service multiple award schedule (MAS) contracts. This clause is used to adjust MAS contract price and requires a MAS contractor to furnish certain pricing information when the MAS contractor requests a price adjustment under the MAS contract.
