BFN [Text] Moscow, 19 May -- A hotline is to be set up between the residences of the presidents of Russia and France -- between the Kremlin and the Elysee palace. An agreement on this was concluded today in Moscow by Russia's Minister of Foreign Affairs Andrey Kozyrev and his French counterpart Alain Juppe. The solemn ceremony of signing the document took place after the conversation between the heads of the foreign policy departments which lasted for two and a half hours at the mansion of the Russian Ministry of Foreign Affairs had ended.
