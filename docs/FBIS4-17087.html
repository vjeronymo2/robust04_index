BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Excerpts] The well-known diplomats David Owen and Thorvald Stoltenberg arrived in Moscow today. This evening they had talks with Andrey Kozyrev. [Correspondent Mikhail Aleksandridi] A news conference was held after their talks. [passage omitted] [Aleksandridi] In Thorvald Stoltenberg's opinion, regular consultations between the co-chairmen of the Coordinating Committee and the Russian side had played an important role in the quest for a peaceful solution of the Bosnian problem. However, in the existing situation, David Owen thinks, it is necessary in order to attain this goal to employ a carrot-and-stick policy, that is to combine the threat of armed pressure with the lifting of the anti-Serb sanctions in the event of the Serbs preferring peace to war.
