BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Viktor Lebedev] [Text] Kuwait, 18 May (KUNA) -- Marshal of Aviation Yevgeniy Shaposhnikov, who visited Kuwait as Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin's special envoy, left for home at the end of his three-day visit to this Arab country. The Russian guest was received by the emir of Kuwait, Jabir al-Ahmad al-Jabir Al Sabah. Ye. Shaposhnikov handed a personal note from Boris Yeltsin to the head of the Kuwaiti state. The emir of Kuwait accepted an invitation in the note to pay an official visit to Russia, the date of which will be agreed in due course. During his meetings with the leadership of the emirate and representatives of business and financial circles, Boris Yeltsin's special envoy reaffirmed Russia's continued support for Kuwait's independence and security. He stressed the need for Iraq to fulfill the UN Security Council resolutions which drew a line under the Kuwaiti crisis. During the conversations, Yevgeniy Shaposhnikov told an ITAR-TASS correspondent, questions connected with the future development of contacts between the two countries at various levels in the interests of building up multilateral Russian-Kuwaiti cooperation were examined.
