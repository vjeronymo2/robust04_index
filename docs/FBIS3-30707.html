Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bratislava, February 5 (CTK) -- Seventeen percent of Slovaks support Premier Vladimir Meciar's Movement For a Democratic Slovakia (HZDS), while 14 percent support the Democratic Left Party (SDL) and just over 7 percent the Christian Democratic Movement (KDH), according to a poll by the Focus agency whose results were broadcast by Slovak radio today. President Michal Kovac emerged as the most trustworthy politician (25 percent). Next came Vladimir Meciar with 21 percent and third SDL Chairman Peter Weiss with 14 percent. The poll was conducted between January 26 and February 1, and therefore its results were not influenced by the present political crisis in the Slovak parliament, the radio warned.
