BFN [Report by Didi Tatlow] [Excerpts] A Foreign Ministry spokesman yesterday dismissed as "sheer fabrication" a report that a major reshuffle is to take place within the ministry. The report, carried yesterday by the SING TAO DAILY NEWS, said that Foreign Minister Qian Qichen was to be replaced by Luo Gan, secretary-general of the State Council and Premier Li Peng's "right-hand man". [passage omitted] The spokesman declined to comment further on the report, which said that the reshuffle was part of a plan by Mr Li to strengthen his control over China's foreign affairs.
