BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 2 -- Vietnamese and American specialists held their regular technical meeting on the issues of the American servicemen Missing in Action (MIA) during the Vietnam war here on March 31. The two sides unanimously valued that the 28th Joint Search for the MIAs, the first after the lifting of the US's trade embargo on Vietnam, which was conducted from Feb.26 to March 22, gained positive results, and that the US specialists continued to receive effective cooperation search for MIAs will be conducted from April 21 to May 24 in some provinces in north and central Vietnam.
