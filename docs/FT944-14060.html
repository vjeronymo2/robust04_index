941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / Observer: Close shave Britain's barristers may not be in danger of losing their wigs, but their opposite numbers in the Emerald Isle may have to dump their ancient head-gear if Willy O'Dea, Ireland's justice minister, gets his way. He is firmly of the opinion that 'a race meeting is where you'd expect to see horse hair - not a court of law'.
