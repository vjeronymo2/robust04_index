BFN [Arnold Pushkar report: "Arms Dealers Caught in Tula Militia's Nets"] [Text] Tula -- Staffers of the Tula Internal Affairs Administration's Administration for Combating Organized Crime have detained an inhabitant of one of the Baltic countries who brought 20 "TT" pistols to Tula for sale. As V. Podolskiy, chief of the Administration for Combating Organized Crime, reported, the majority of arms on sale are produced in local plants, though some are imported. Over the four months of this year more arms have been confiscated than during the whole of last year.
