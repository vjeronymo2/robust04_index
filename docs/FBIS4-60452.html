BFN [Text] Cairo, 24 May (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa met with Edmund Hull, charge d'affaires of the U.S. Embassy in Cairo, this evening. After the meeting, Hull said they discussed several regional issues of mutual interest, especially the situation in Somalia and Yemen. He added that Egypt and the United States are making contacts and efforts to control the situation and to halt the fighting in Yemen. [Cairo MENA in Arabic at 0950 GMT on 25 May in a similar Cairo-datelined report adds: "Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa today met Edmund Hull, charge d'affaires of the U.S. Embassy in Cairo, for the second time in 24 hours. ["Hull said afterward that they discussed developments in Yemen and coordination between Egyptian and American positions to stop the fighting there and launch a dialogue between the warring parties. ["We hope that we can realize this objective, Hull said. He refused to discuss any details as to how fighting can be stopped. He said Egypt and the United States are trying hard to end the fighting."]
