May 25, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

THE WEEKEND: The holiday weekend gets off to a big start at 1 p.m. today when the 32nd annual Strawberry Festival begins at Village Green Park in Garden Grove (B2). . . . It's expected to draw 300,000 people through Sunday.

On Saturday, two days of Scottish games, food and music begin at the Orange County Fairgrounds in Costa Mesa. Tickets: $1 to $9.50. Info: (213) 395-0063. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
