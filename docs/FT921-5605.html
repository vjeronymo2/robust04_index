920303 FT 03 MAR 92 / Water survey finds support for meters NEARLY three quarters of customers in water-metering trials throughout England say metering is a satisfactory method of paying for water, according to a survey by WS Atkins, management consultants, for Ofwat, the industry's economic regulator. The survey covered 1,031 customers of the 57,000 households involved in national metering trials in 12 areas over the past three years.
