941230 FT 30 DEC 94 / Bosnian Serbs endorse accord The Bosnian Serb parliament yesterday endorsed an agreement their leader, Mr Radovan Karadzic, signed with Mr Jimmy Carter, former US president, earlier this month, backing a Big Power peace plan as a basis for further negotiations. 'The parliament informs the Contact Group and the international community of the readiness on the part of (Bosnian Serb) Republika Srpska to enter peace negotiations on the basis of agreement with Carter,' said a statement issued after a parliament session.
