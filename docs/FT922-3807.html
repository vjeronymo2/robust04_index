920609 FT 09 JUN 92 / Observer: Demob happy? Those close to Robert Louis-Dreyfus, Saatchi and Saatchi's 45-year-old chief executive, have begun to notice an extra spring in his step, and an even-readier tendency to break into broad smiles. What on earth can it be? True, the group is no longer on the brink of collapse following its recapitalisation. But it is hardly yet out of the doldrums. Nor can it simply be that his wife is expecting their first child. Perhaps Wednesday's annual general meeting will reveal all.
