[I.D. 021594B] North Pacific Fishery Management Council; Meeting AGENCY: National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS), National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA), Commerce. ACTION: Notice of public meeting.

SUMMARY: The North Pacific Fishery Management Council's Halibut Working Group will hold a meeting on March 10&hyph;11, 1994, in NMFS main conference room on the 4th floor of the Federal Building, 907 West 9th Street, Juneau, AK. The meeting will begin at 1 p.m. on March 10, and adjourn by 4 p.m. on March 11. The Working Group will begin to develop appropriate elements and options for a proposed moratorium on the entry of new charter vessels into the halibut fishery off Alaska. The meeting is physically accessible to people with disabilities. Requests for sign language interpretation or other auxiliary aids should be directed to Judy Willoughby, (907) 271&hyph;2809, at least 5 days prior to the meeting date.
