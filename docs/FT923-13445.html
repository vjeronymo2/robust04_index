920714 FT 14 JUL 92 / Tory media chief MR Tim Collins, 28-year-old press minder to Mr John Major in the election campaign, has been appointed Conservative party director of communications, succeeding Mr Shaun Woodward. Mr Tim Smith, Tory MP for Beaconsfield, becomes a party treasurer in succession to Lord Beaverbrook, and Mr Tony Garrett succeeds Sir John Lacy as head of campaigning.
