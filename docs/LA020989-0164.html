February 9, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Syracuse basketball player Derrick Coleman pleaded guilty in Syracuse City Court to harassment and disorderly conduct charges stemming from a brawl and break-in incident on campus last December. Coleman was sentenced to 50 hours of community service and restitution for damages he caused.

Because both of the charges are violations, which are non-criminal offenses, Coleman will have no criminal record, his attorney, Donald Martin, said.
