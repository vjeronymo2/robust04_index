921120 FT 20 NOV 92 / World Trade News: Turkmenistan deal for Laing JOHN LAING of the UK, with Alsim Alarko, a Turkish engineering company, has won a Pounds 54m contract in Turkmenistan, the first time Laing has worked there, Andrew Taylor reports. The deal is to complete a terminal at Ashgabat Airport. Lockheed Airport Terminal of California and Burc Electronics of Ankara are also involved. Cemindia, part of the UK's Trafalgar House, has won Pounds 5m orders at India's Tuticorin Port Trust and Gandisagar Dam.
