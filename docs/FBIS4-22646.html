CSO [Order of 3 March 1994 confirming Statute on Civilian Nuclear Power Plant Construction] [Text] Order Moscow, 9 March 1994, No 26 Approval of the Statute on the Procedure for Issuing Provisional Permits from Russian Gosatomnadzor for Construction of Nuclear Power Plant Units for Civilian Purposes I order: That the attached guideline document, "Statute on the Procedure for Issuing Provisional Permits from Russian Gosatomnadzor for Construction of Nuclear Power Plant Units for Civilian Purposes (RD-04-07-94) be approved. [Signed] A.T. Gutsalov, first deputy chairman of Russian Gosatomnadzor
