Language: Thai Article Type:BFN [Text] The Foreign Ministry's Information Department has reported on Foreign Minister Squadron Leader Prasong Sunsiri's visit to the Republic of Chile between 25 and 27 January. The foreign minister stressed Thailand's policy of friendship with all countries, especially its relations with other countries in ASEAN and APEC [Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation]. Chile will become an APEC member in 1994. Thailand wants to see Chile become an economic and trade center in Latin America or serve as a commercial gateway for ASEAN as well as Indochinese countries. The foreign minister visited Argentina between 27 and 30 January. He met with the Argentine foreign minister during the visit. The two sides discussed ways of further strengthening bilateral relations and cooperation as well as economic and investment cooperation. The two sides set a target for bilateral trade at U.S. $200 million annually. In addition, the two sides discussed ways of strengthening bilateral contacts and exchanges of officials in the cultural, linguistic, political, diplomatic, trade, and technological fields; they also discussed cooperation among members of the private sector. Meanwhile, the foreign minister and his delegation is visiting New Orleans in the United States. He will proceed to Los Angeles on 2 February.
