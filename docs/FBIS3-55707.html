Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] Today the authorities in Baku stated that they have proof that foreign mercenaries are taking part in combat actions on the side of the Armenian armed forces. A well-informed source in the apparatus of the Azerbaijani president informed INTERFAX that the republic's leadership is preparing a confidential message to the leaders of Russia, France, the Baltic countries, Syria, and Lebanon with a request to take measures and recall their citizens from the Armenian Armed Forces.
