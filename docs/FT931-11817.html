930201 FT 01 FEB 93 / International Company News: Wintrust sees brighter outlook According to Mr Richard Szpiro, managing director of Wintrust, the merchant banker, the financial outlook has ceased to deteriorate. 'With a fair wind this should be reflected in our future figures.' He made his comments while reporting lower pre-tax profits for the six months to September 30 of Pounds 1.01m (Pounds 1.54m). He added that the balance sheet had strengthened and the loans and advances to capital ratio had fallen from 2.5:1 to 1.5:1. Earnings per share were 5.53p (10p) and the interim dividend is unchanged at 3p.
