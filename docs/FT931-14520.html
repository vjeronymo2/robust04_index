930118 FT 18 JAN 93 / Observer: Prime evil Meeting up with an architect and a politician, a doctor claimed his profession was older than theirs, saying: 'God performed a surgical operation when he removed Adam's rib to create Eve.' 'You're wrong,' the architect objected. 'At least a week earlier, he'd acted like any good architect in producing order out of chaos.' 'And who made the chaos?', the politician replied.
