April 9, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

TITAN GYMNASTS ADVANCE TO NCAAS

Cal State Fullerton's women's gymnastics team qualified for the NCAA championships for the second consecutive year, posting a season-high score of 191.70 in the Western Regional at UCLA Saturday night.

The score, an average of 9.05 per event, was the fourth-best performance in Fullerton history, and gave the Titans a second-place finish to UCLA.

Fullerton will be the seventh-seeded team at the NCAA championships, April 20-21 at Oregon State.
