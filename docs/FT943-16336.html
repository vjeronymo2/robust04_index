940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / Observer: Shaggy but true And now, a variant on the man-bites-dog theme. A dog in Poland has - accidentally, presumably - shot its owner in the back as he left his gun unattended while setting up a target. Surgeons removed five dozen pellets from the man, who is recovering in hospital. The dog is now taking a course in - barksmanship.
