921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / International Company News: Dow Jones sees net income rise DOW JONES, the US pub-lishing and information group, expects to report 1992 net income of between Dollars 1.03 and Dollars 1.07 a share, against 71 cents in 1991, after a net charge to earnings of about Dollars 25m, or 25 cents a share, for new accounting standards and certain write-downs, according to Mr Peter R. Kann, chairman and chief executive, AP-DJ reports.
