BFN [Text] Shaykh Muhammad al-Ghazali has denied a BBC report attributed to him on the morning of 8 April that he is engaged in hostile acts against the Egyptian Government. Shaykh al-Ghazali told ROSE AL-YUSUF that the BBC cited an interview with him conducted by a British journalist from THE GUARDIAN. There was a mistake in the translation, he said. AL-GHAZALI stressed that the report is unfounded. He said he is serving the heritage and message of Islam within the limits of public order. He said he never thought of violating the state's general law.
