National Committee on Vital and Health Statistics: Meeting Pursuant to Pub. L. 92&hyph;463, the National Center for Health Statistics (NCHS), Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), announces the following committee meeting. Name: National Committee on Vital and Health Statistics (NCVHS). Times and Dates: 1 p.m.&hyph;5 p.m., October 26, 1994, 9 a.m.&hyph;5 p.m., October 27, 1994, 9 p.m.&hyph;1 p.m., October 28, 1994, Place: Room 703A, Hubert H. Humphrey Building, 200 Independence Avenue, SW., Washington, DC 20201. Status: Open. Purpose: The purpose of this meeting is for the committee to consider reports from each NCVHS subcommittee; to receive reports from offices of the Department of Health and Human Services; to explore information needs for health reform; and to address new business as appropriate. Contact Person for More Information: Substantive program information as well as summaries of the meeting and a roster of committee members may be obtained from Gail F. Fisher, Ph.D., Executive Secretary, NCVHS, NCHS, CDC, Room 1100, Presidential Building, 6525 Belcrest Road, Hyattsville, Maryland 20782, telephone 301/436&hyph;7050. Dated: October 4, 1994. William H. Gimson, Acting Associate Director for Policy Coordination, Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).
