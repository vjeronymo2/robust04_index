BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent] [Text] Moscow June 10 TASS -- Artur Chilingarov, a noted polar explorer, was elected to become the fifth deputy speaker of the Russian State Duma on Friday. 247 lawmakers voted for him, 19 against and 28 abstained. An ethnic Armenian, Chilingarov is a member of the "New Regional Policy" faction, the president of the Association of Polar Researchers and a member of the all-Russian "Renewal" union. In Duma he represents the Nenets Autonomous Region.
