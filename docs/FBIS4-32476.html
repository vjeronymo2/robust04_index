BFN [Unattributed report: "Intelligence Agency -- Clean"] [Text] Yesterday, the Parliament Committee for control over the work of the State Intelligence Agency announced the results of its surveillance of the agency documents. The Committee confirmed the intelligence service had not undertaken any operative or technical methods towards VMRO-DPMNE [Internal Macedonian Revolutionary Organization-Democratic Party for Macedonian National Unity], nor towards any other political party. The document presented to the public by Ljupco Georgievski is a forgery, as there is no original or any other document in the intelligence documentation under that number and date. The intelligence does not undertake measures and methods, but on individuals connected with foreign intelligence services or because of activities aimed at endangering the constitutional establishment of Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], the Committee report states.
