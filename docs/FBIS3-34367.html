Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A press source at the Embassy of Palestine has said that the Egyptian Embassy in Amman has started handing out forms to be filled by evacuees from the Gaza Strip in order to carry out a census. This procedure is aimed at maintaining the evacuees' right to return to their homeland, their identity, as well as all their acquired rights. It should be noted that completing this form does not affect the evacuee's right to residency, work, commercial activity, and housing. For this purpose, Gazans should report to the Egyptian Embassy in Amman.
