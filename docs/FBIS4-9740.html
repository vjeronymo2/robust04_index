BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on developments related to the Colombian elections scheduled for 22 May 1994. While campaigning in Giron, Santander Department, Conservative Party presidential candidate Andres Pastrana pledged to strengthen Colombia's industrial sector and to eliminate contraband. Pastrana said: "The country must wage a frontal assault on contraband to prevent it from plunging thousands of people into poverty and unemployment, as is now happening in Colombia." (Santa Fe de Bogota Inravision Television Cadena 1 in Spanish 0000 GMT 26 Mar 94) Ernesto Samper now is the sole leader of the Liberal Party, which he is reorganizing. He is forming a broad-based party that will include all sectors. Carlos Lleras de la Fuente, Rodolfo Gonzalez and David Turbay, former presidential hopefuls who vied for the party's nomination, will be Samper's direct advisers. (Santa Fe de Bogota Inravision Television Cadena 1 in Spanish 1730 GMT 25 Mar 94)
