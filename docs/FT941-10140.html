940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / Balladur losing support The popularity of Mr Edouard Balladur, French prime minister, has dropped sharply since the beginning of the year and he now has the support of just over half of voters, according to an opinion poll published yesterday, Reuter reports from Paris. The Gallup survey for the weekly L'Express said 52 per cent of those questioned approved of Mr Balladur's policies, down from 59 per cent a month ago. The percentage of those who disapproved rose 8 points to 39 per cent.
