December 8, 1989, Friday, Valley P.M. Final

LOCAL;

SEPULVEDA WOMAN SLAIN IN HOME

A 50-year-old Sepulveda woman was found stabbed to death in her home, which apparently had been ransacked by her assailant, Los Angeles police said today.

Nancy Gail Bronston was found dead about 5:30 p.m. Thursday when police investigated a call from a neighbor in the 8500 block of Decelis Place who suspected something was wrong, police said. The victim apparently was stabbed when she returned home, and might have surprised a burglar, police said.

There were no arrests or suspects in the case. Police said Bronston's 1981 Cadillac was missing; it was unknown what property, if any, was taken from the house.
