940712 FT 12 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Hamlet advances to Pounds 5.3m Hamlet Group, the clothes importer which gained a stock market listing last October, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 5.31m for the year ended March 31. This represents an increase of 37 per cent on the Pounds 3.88m for the previous 11 months. Mr Malcolm Dagul, chairman, said this was the fourteenth consecutive year of increased turnover and profits for the subsidiary Hamlet International. Group turnover was Pounds 71.9m (Pounds 54m). Earnings per share were 11.7p (10.93p). The proposed dividend is 4p. Mr Dagul said net assets increased by 65 per cent to Pounds 18.9m which has enabled the group to continue the expansion programme.
