921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / The Labour Party at Blackpool: Conference business YESTERDAY: Conference approved the setting up of an NEC working party on Labour's trade union links; Approved increase in individual subscriptions from Pounds 15 to Pounds 18; Called for a study of best practice in funding, management and service delivery in the National Health Service; Backed increases in the state pension; Called for 'a vigorous, effective and forceful campaign against the implementation of the council tax'; Expressed concern at the party leadership's attitude towards rural voters and non-key seats in the run-up to the general election; Backed measures to invigorate the housing market. TODAY: Conference will debate electoral reform, criminal justice, racism and international affairs.
