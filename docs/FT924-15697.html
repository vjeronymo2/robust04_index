921002 FT 02 OCT 92 / Survey of Software at Work - Autumn 1992 (24): Hoskyns' Insight - Supplier Profile The Insight Division of Hoskyns Group is a leading computing services company formed in the UK in 1964 and now owned by Europe's biggest computing services group, Cap Gemini Sogeti. Turnover: Pounds 201m in 1991; Pounds 223m in 1990. Profit: Pounds 14.3m in 1991; Pounds 17.3m in 1990. Employees: 3,000. Product: Insight HRM, described as a human resource management system with modules for recruitment, appraisals, job evaluation, training, absence and occupational health and safety. Links can be provided to other companies' payroll products. Microsoft Windows is offered as an option. Hardware: IBM AS/400 and System/38. Price: from Pounds 9,950; maintenance is 12.5 per cent of initial price. Sales and support: sold and supported direct by Hoskyns. Customers: 60 in the UK; 510 worldwide.
