930422 FT 22 APR 93 / Tarmac venture wins Pounds 85m order AN Anglo-US joint venture involving Tarmac, Britain's biggest construction and building materials group, is part of a consortium which has won an Pounds 85m order to build a 100MW gas-turbine power station in Brunei. Tarmac, Black & Veatch, the US power plant engineers, and General Electric of the US which will supply turbines, are to build the power station in Bandar Seri Begawan, the Brunei capital. It will be close to the existing Gadong power station.
