940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Tesco/Wm Low Tesco has received valid acceptances for its revised offer for William Low in respect of 51.9m ordinary shares, representing about 95.84 per cent of William Low's issued ordinary capital. Acceptances for the revised convertible preference offer have been received in respect of 31.35m shares, representing about 96.71 per cent of the issued convertible preference capital.
