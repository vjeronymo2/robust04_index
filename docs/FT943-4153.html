940910 FT 10 SEP 94 / Competency tests suggested for pre-secondary school pupils Pupils should have to pass a test of 'minimum competence in maths and English' before moving on to secondary school, a leading education professor told the meeting. 'If a pupil does not reach that level, then there is a case for extra tuition in the evenings, holidays or, in a few cases, repeating a year,' said Prof Alan Smithers of Manchester University's Centre for Education Research.
