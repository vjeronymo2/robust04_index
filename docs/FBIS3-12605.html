Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] (A.S.), a 24-year old originally from Hebron and a resident of Amman, has stabbed Dr. 'Umar al-Far, who works in a private clinic, and his wife Olga, who is a Russian national and works as a nurse at Malhas Hospital, with a knife while they were downtown near the Habiba sweets shop near a shoe store. The doctor was injured in the chest while his wife suffered wounds to the back. A youth who works inside the shoe store tried to intervene and was also injured. All are reported to be in good condition. Police arrested the assailant.
