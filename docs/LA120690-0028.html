December 6, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

PRO FOOTBALL REPORT;

WEEKDAY UPDATE;

AROUND THE NFL;

MAJKOWSKI TESTS ARM; STATUS UNCERTAIN

Green Bay Packer quarterback Don Majkowski threw for 10 minutes before practice Wednesday and said he was encouraged.

But whether he will be able to to play Sunday against the Seattle Seahawks remains uncertain. Majkowski suffered a deep bruise in the rotator cuff of his throwing shoulder Nov. 18 at Phoenix. He has not started the last two games and has been unable to throw in practice.
