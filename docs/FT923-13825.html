920710 FT 10 JUL 92 / World News in Brief: Sterling gains against D-Mark The pound gained nearly a pfennig against a weaker D-Mark, closing in London at DM2.88 after the government reaffirmed its long-term commitment to placing sterling in the narrow band of the European exchange rate mechanism. The dollar gained more than 2 pfennigs against the German currency, closing at DM1.519. In New York, the dollar closed at DM1.512 and the pound stood at DM2.8741 in late trading. Currencies, Page 33
