BFN [Text] Sanaa, 6 May (MENA)--A responsible Yemeni source this evening denied reports that the Presidential Palace in Sanaa came under air strikes from the South Yemeni Air Force. In a statement to SABA'', the northern source said that the forces in the south fired five long-range Scud missiles at Sanaa, noting that two of the missiles fell in mountainous areas, one fell near the airport in Sanaa, and two missiles failed to explode. The source affirmed that the missiles caused no material or human losses.
