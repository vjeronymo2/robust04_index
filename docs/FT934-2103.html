931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / International Company News: Great Eagle plans reorganisation Great Eagle Holdings said it was considering reorganising its commercial and office property development activities and spinning them off into a single unit to be listed on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange, Reuter reports from Hong Kong. Other units of Great Eagle will continue to be primarily involved in investment in residential, industrial and retail properties and hotels. The new commercial property unit will remain a subsidiary of Great Eagle after the listing, the company said. The stock exchange has not yet considered the proposals or application for listing, the company said.
