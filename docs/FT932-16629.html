930405 FT 05 APR 93 / Construction Contracts: Mansell busy MANSELL has recently been awarded contracts worth Pounds 34m. The latest projects extend from Portsmouth to Birmingham and include new build, refurbishment, design and build, fitting out and maintenance. The company has also been successful in novating contracts from the receivers of Peter Ling with a gross value of Pounds 17m covering new build, refurbishment and design and build.
