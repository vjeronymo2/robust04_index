941124 FT 24 NOV 94 / Southern Electric to give Pounds 19m rebate Southern Electric, the power distributor and supplier, yesterday announced a Pounds 19m cash rebate for its 2.5m household and business customers, Michael Smith writes. Quarterly tariff customers will have Pounds 7 credited against their bill and more than 12,000 monthly billed customers will be credited with Pounds 100. The company said the rebate would mean the total benefits passed on to tariff customers in real terms since April last year was Pounds 112m.
