Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Havana, 20 Feb (NOTIMEX) -- Carlos Martinez Salsamendi, the president of the Cuban Chamber of Commerce, has reported here that Cuba registered more than 500 foreign corporations and 112 joint enterprises as of the end of 1993. Martinez Salsamendi said that 78 new corporations joined the chamber last year. He made the announcement during a meeting with businessmen from the Canary Islands. The business leader also said that 28 of the joint enterprises are engaged in tourism activities. During the meeting, the businessmen and entrepreneurs from the Canary Islands expressed their interest in doing business with Cuba. The group is accompanying President Manuel Hermoso of the Government of the Canary Islands, who began a six-day official visit to Cuba on Friday. This is the third trip by a Spanish autonomous government leader to the island, following tours by Gallaecian President Manuel Fraga Iribarne and Asturian leader Juan Luis Rodriguez Vigil in 1991.
