930203 FT 03 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Sales drop and provisions leave Eleco in the red A sharp drop in sales in November and December, plus further substantial provisions against the cost of withdrawing from property development, pushed Eleco Holdings into a loss of Pounds 2.21m for the six months to end-1992. Directors said current trading was showing some improvement over last year. Sales fell to Pounds 23.6m (Pounds 26.4m). After provision of Pounds 1.4m (Pounds 649,000) the loss compared with a restated profit of Pounds 513,000. Losses came to 7.5p (earnings 2.6p). The interim dividend is omitted (2.3p). Short-term order books were better in a number of companies.
