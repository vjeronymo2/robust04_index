BFN [Text] Yaounde, 30 May (AFP) -- President Paul Biya left Yaounde today for Paris to begin an official visit that will last several days, an official source announced in the Cameroonian capital. The Presidency of the Republic did not disclose the head of state's schedule in France. Before leaving for Paris, Mr. Biya donated 15 million CFA francs to the national soccer team, the Indomitable Lions, and directed the government to release as early as possible the sum of 100 million CFA francs for the team's preparation for the USA-hosted World Cup, the said source added.
