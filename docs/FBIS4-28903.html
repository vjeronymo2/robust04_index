BFN [Text] Tokyo, June 14 KYODO -- Japan and the United States will hold vice ministerial-level meetings on the bilateral trade framework talks on June 22 and 23 in Tokyo, Foreign Ministry sources said Tuesday [14 June]. Deputy U.S. Trade Representative Charlene Barshevsky will arrive in Japan on June 21 to attend the meeting with Deputy Foreign Minister Sadayuki Hayashi, the sources said. Prior to the meeting, working-level talks on telecommunications equipment will be held on June 16 and 17 and medical equipment talks will be held on June 21 and 22, both in Tokyo, the sources said. Since agreeing to resume the stalled framework talks, Japan and the U.S. have held working-level talks on telecommunications equipment and medical equipment earlier this month, reporting some progress.
