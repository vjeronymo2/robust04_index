October 14, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

CASTING QUESTION

Re the Oct. 7 article on the movie "War Party":

Let me see if I understand this. In the entire United States of American were there no actors other than Kevin Dillon (a white) and Billy Wirth (whose great-great grandmother was American Indian) qualified to play the leading American Indian roles in this film?

I realize that name recognition is important to producers, but surely these fine young actors could have played the leading white roles.

I wonder if it has occurred to the film's producers to contribute some (probably tax-deductible) portion of the film's proceeds to improve the possibility that the people they are exploiting will survive into adulthood?

EVA SNIDER, Pasadena
