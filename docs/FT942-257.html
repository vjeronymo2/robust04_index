940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / European Parliament seeks meeting with German Chancellor The European Parliament stepped up pressure yesterday to have a say over who should become the new European Commission president, urging talks with German Chancellor Helmut Kohl, Reuter reports from Brussels. Mr Egon Klepsch, Parliament president, was preparing to contact Mr Kohl to propose talks ahead of a summit planned for Brussels on July 15 on Mr Jacques Delors' successor. An early task of the parliament, which meets on July 19, will be to approve the EU's new president. Ms Pauline Green, president-designate of the socialist group, said parliament must know the identity of the proposed president before it meets.
