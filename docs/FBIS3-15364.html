Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (March 3) TURAN -- Early this morning units of the Armenian Army armed with great amount of military hardware launched a large-scale offensive towards south-western section of the front. Earlier the Armenian Army bombarded the positions of the Azeri Army. Military sources told Turan that the Armenian units attack in the direction of Beylagan region centre. Presently the Azeri units try to hold Armenia's attack. Defense Ministry of the republic confirmed this information. The same source told Turan that today due to violation of the conditions of the truce by Armenia, Azerbaijan Defense Ministry will issue the statement.
