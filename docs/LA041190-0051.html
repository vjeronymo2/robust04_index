April 11, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

GRATEFUL TO SUPERVISORS

I wish to publicly express gratitude to Supervisors Mike Antonovich, Pete Schabarum, and Deane Dana for denying a petitioner's request for a zoning change at the Lost Hills Business Center.

Their denial of the change prevented a defense contractor from opening a gyroscope-manufacturing business in which hazardous chemicals, gases and toxic wastes would have been stored near homes and A.E. Wright Middle School, where 1,250 students are enrolled.

I want to thank the supervisors for their insight in recognizing the potential risks this change represented and their wisdom in denying the petitioner's request.

SHARON BROWN

Woodland Hills

Brown is president of A.E. Wright Middle School Parent-Faculty Club
