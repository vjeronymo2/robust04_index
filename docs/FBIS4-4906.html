BFN Pyongyang, April 5 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea today sent a message of greetings to Dogu Ferincek, chairman of the Workers' Party of Turkey [WPT], on the fifth founding anniversary of the WPT. Expressing the belief that the relations of fraternal friendship existing between the WPK and the WPT would grow stronger and develop in the future in accordance with the idea of independence, peace and friendship, the message wished the WPT and its chairman big success in their responsible work for the strengthening and development of the party.
