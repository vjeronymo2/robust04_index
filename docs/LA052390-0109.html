May 23, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

SEATTLE BANK TO USE ATMS TO DISPENSE STAMPS TO CLIENTS

Seattle-First National Bank will begin Saturday to dispense postage stamps at its automated teller machines -- a move that will spark increased interest in the future of ATMs as a source of products other than cash.

If the highly touted joint venture between Seattle-First and the U.S. Postal Service proves successful, it may open doors for similar efforts across the country.

Until now, banks and other institutions have made a variety of efforts to branch into products besides cash at ATMs. Products being tested include movie tickets, coupons, ski-lift tickets and other items that can be tailored to the size and thickness of currency.
