920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / East Timorese gets 10 years AN EAST TIMORESE student was yesterday sentenced to 10 years in jail for his part in a non-violent protest against the massacre of more than 50 pro-independence activists by soldiers in East Timor last November, reports William Keeling in Jakarta. Mr Joao Freitas de Camara was found guilty of organising a protest in Jakarta on November 19. He was charged with subversion.
