BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] St. Petersburg June 8 TASS -- In line with a decision taken on Wednesday by the council of the CIS interparliementary assembly, the CIS private law research and consulting centre will begin to elaborate a model civil code of CIS countries. This is expected to lay groundwork to bring the national civil legislations of the CIS countries closer together on such key economic integration-related matters as the legal status of commercial organisations, freedom of goods traffic and transactions, the proteciton of foreign investors' interests, the development of legislation on contracts and guarantees of the discharge of obligations, and the establishment of a similar procedure to resolve economic disputes. A model civil code is to be referred for adoption to the CIS interparliamentary assembly session in October.
