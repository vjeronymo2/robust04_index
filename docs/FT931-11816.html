930201 FT 01 FEB 93 / International Company News: Dana moves back into the black Dana Exploration, the Dublin-based mineral exploration company, reported pre-tax profits of IPounds 1.27m (Pounds 1.4m) in the six months ended July 31 against losses of IPounds 82,664. The result was after an exceptional IPounds 1.29m credit, being the net effect of the compromise settlement agreed with the company's creditors in November 1992 whereby all Dana's liabilities were discharged for about IPounds 100,000. The retained deficit carried forward increased from IPounds 534,353 to IPounds 1.58m. Proposals for a restructuring of the share capital will be put to shareholders at annual and extraordinary meetings on February 15.
