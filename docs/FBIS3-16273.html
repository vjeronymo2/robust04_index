Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [POSTFACTUM report: "Federal Counterintelligence Service Director Sergey Stepashin Hospitalized"] [Text] Lieutenant General Sergey Stepashin, Federal Counterintelligence Service director, was taken to the hospital in an ambulance from his own office after, according to some information, becoming sick due to a worsening of his stomach ulcer. The confidential source, who would rather remain anonymous, intimated that the reason was not the illness but the meeting with President Yeltsin, soon after which Sergey Stepashin was sent to the departmental hospital.
