BFN [Text] Haitian People's Power Youth Organization [AJEPOPOA] leader Jude Leon Jean-Jacques is delighted with the Senate's decision to invest Justice Emile Jonassaint with presidential powers. This, he said, is a big step. [Begin Leon Jean-Jacques recording in Creole] The AJEPOPOA secretary general's office congratulates the Senate of the Republic for this important decision, following so many agreements, so many thoughts, and for its sense of responsibility for the higher interests of the nation by appointing Justice Emile Jonassaint as head of the state, considering the presidential vacuum under Article 149 of the Constitution. [end recording]
