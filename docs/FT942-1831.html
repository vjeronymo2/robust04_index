940622 FT 22 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Total Systems Total Systems, the USM-quoted software supplier, reported a recovery in the second half of its year to the end of March, resulting in a return to operating profits. The pre-tax figure, however, was down at Pounds 142,000, against Pounds 833,000, on turnover of Pounds 2.09m (Pounds 2.87m). Earnings per share came out at 0.96p (6.08p) and the final dividend is passed as was the interim. Last time there was a total payment of 3p.
