921201 FT 01 DEC 92 / Electronic plant for Scotland A LEADING US manufacturer of printed circuit boards, a component of electronic products, is to set up a plant at Livingston, West Lothian, which is expected to employ 400 people within five years. Jabil Circuit, of St Petersburg, Florida, is to spend Pounds 13.2m on developing a 33,000 sq ft plant, which will be its first outside the US.
