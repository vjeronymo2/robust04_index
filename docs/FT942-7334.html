940526 FT 26 MAY 94 / Nominations close for by-elections Nominations closed last night for four of the five by-elections being held on June 9, the same day as the European polls - the Labour-held constituencies of Barking, Dagenham and Newham North East in London and Bradford South. Nominations for Eastleigh, Hampshire, held by the Tories, close today. Labour is fielding former Newham council leader Mr Stephen Timms at Newham North East, Islington's ex-leader Mrs Margaret Hodge at Barking and city council leader Mr Gerry Sutcliffe at Bradford South. Mr John Tyndall of the far right British National Party is fighting Dagenham. The National Front is standing in Barking.
