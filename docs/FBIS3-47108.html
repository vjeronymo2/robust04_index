Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] His Excellency [H.E.] Jacques Chirac, former French prime minister and mayor of Paris, and his colleagues arrived in Phnom Penh, capital of the Kingdom of Cambodia [KOC], at 1900 on 9 January for a three-day visit from 9 to 11 January. At 1730 on the same day, H.E. Jacques Chirac and his colleagues paid a courtesy call on Prince Norodom Sirivut, deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs and international cooperation, at the Foreign Ministry. On this occasion, Prince Norodom Sirivut thanked the French Republic government for supporting the KOC in the past and its continued assistance in Cambodia's rehabilitation. The prince informed the guests about the current political and economic situation in Cambodia. Prince Norodom Sirivut called on the French Government for economic cooperation, namely to get France's specialist organization for investment abroad to recognize Cambodia as a stable country to encourage French investors to do business in Cambodia.
