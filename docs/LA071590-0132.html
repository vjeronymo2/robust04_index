July 15, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

CRITIC'S PICK: DIRTY ROTTON SCOUNDRELS

This 1988 comedy is a pleasant enough way to tour the south of France; it's blithe and witty, with as many twists as a Riviera roadway. Michael Caine, left, stars as a con man extraordinaire challenged by a seemingly klutzy American con man (Steve Martin).

TMC Monday at 10 p.m., early Tuesday at 3:35 a.m. Sheila Benson
