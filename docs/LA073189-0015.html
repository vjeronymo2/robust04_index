July 31, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Actress Linda Hunt will make her Los Angeles stage debut in Emily Mann's one-woman drama, "Annulla," opening Oct. 21 at the Court Theatre in West Hollywood. Hunt, who won an Academy Award in 1983 for her performance in "The Year of Living Dangerously," will play the title role of a Holocaust survivor; playwright Mann (whose credits include "Execution of Justice," on the 1979 Dan White murder trial) will direct. Claudia Puig
