EFFECTIVE DATE: This regulation is effective on June 30, 1994. Federal Crop Insurance Corporation 7 CFR Part 406 Nursery Crop Regulations AGENCY: Federal Crop Insurance Corporation, USDA. ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: The Federal Crop Insurance Corporation (FCIC) hereby amends the Nursery Crop Insurance regulations, effective for the 1995 and succeeding crop years, by making a change in the policy to clarify the method of determining loss. The Nursery policy contains the term ``said damaged crop'' which has been interpreted, in some forums, to refer to specific damaged portion of the crop instead of the unit as was the intent.
