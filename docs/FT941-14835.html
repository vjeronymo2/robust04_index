940118 FT 18 JAN 94 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Brazilian equities were driven 8.5 per cent higher yesterday on hopes that the economic package would be approved by congress. The Bovespa index ended 4,699 up at 59,805. Turnover was strong at Cr87.2bn. Telesp jumped 17.2 per cent to Cr157, while Telebras preferred climbed 11.5 per cent to Cr16.40. State mining giant Vale do Rio Doce preferred rose 9.1 per cent to Cr50.20, buoyed, traders said, by news that Salomon Brothers had given the stock a buy rating.
