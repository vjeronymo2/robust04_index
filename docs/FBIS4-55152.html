BFN [Text] Warsaw, May 9 -- First Deputy Defence Minister Jerzy Milewski expressed his satisfaction with the re-establishment of Polish-Chinese military relations after having met with the chief of the general staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army General Zhang Wannian, who has begun a three-day official visit in Poland. According to Milewski the Chinese official's visit to Poland and the fact that "China is noticing us" proves that "great China wants to develop cooperation with us and we are open to this." He added that the 38 year-long break in relations in this sphere may be regarded as being wasted time.
