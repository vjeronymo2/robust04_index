October 21, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BEER BAN AT COLISEUM

What is it with Raiders fans and beer? Are they so pathetic that they can't have a good time without drinking?

Isn't it illegal to drive with an open container? I'll bet officers could arrest every other driver at the entrance to the parking lot.

I may drink moderately on social occasions, but this extreme dependency is disgusting and immature, especially in light of the resultant behavior.

JOY JONES

San Luis Obispo
