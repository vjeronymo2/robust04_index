920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / Fininvest out of Channel 5 contest FININVEST Group, controlled by Mr Silvio Berlusconi, the Italian TV entrepreneur, yesterday said it would no longer submit a television licence bid for Channel 5, which is scheduled to start broadcasting by early 1995. Its decision leaves The Entertainment Channel - a consortium including the UK commercial breakfast broadcaster TV-am, the Daily Telegraph group and Time Warner - and Five TV - a company backed by Mr Moses Znaimer founder of City TV in Toronto - as declared intended bidders. Bids must be with the Independent Television Commission by July.
