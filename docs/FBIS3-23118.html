Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Islamic Jihad Organization in Palestine has announced that it will begin mounting acts of revenge and retaliation because of the massacre. The group issued a statement saying that the Palestine Liberation Battalions [kata'ib tahrir filastin] and the Jihad Eagles Group [majmu'at nusur al-jihad] have been placed on a state of maximum alert. The statement adds: We vow that in less than 24 hours these groups will have begun taking revenge upon the enemy inside and outside the occupied territories.
