BFN [Text] The United States has voiced great concern over the fighting in Yemen. A statement issued yesterday by the U.S. State Department speaks of mounting concern over the grave consequences of continued fighting in that country. The State Department's statement indicates that the United States is trying to involve itself in the situation in Yemen and dictate certain policies on that country. Instead of getting alarmed over Yemen, the United States would do well to stop interfering in its affairs and hatching plots against its unity.
