June 22, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

DRIVING HOME NEED FOR ALTERNATIVES TO CARS

Many thanks for Connie Koenenn's inspiring story on people choosing to give up their cars for more sustainable and healthful modes of transportation ("Shifting Gears," June 19).

I tell folks quite regularly that switching to sustainable (non-fossil-fueled) transportation is going to be one of our next big movements for social and environmental change.

Usually, people just laugh at that. After all, we are the car-consuming capital of the world.

I'm pleased that now I'll have something in print proving that lots of ordinary folks are making the extraordinary choice simply to do what's sensible for personal and planetary health.

LOIS ARKIN

Executive Director

Cooperative Resources & Services Project

Los Angeles
