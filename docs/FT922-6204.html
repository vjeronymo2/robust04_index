920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / Commonwealth of Independent States From today, the FT is dividing its coverage of the constituent republics of the former Soviet Union between the European and International news pages. Coverage of the central Asian republics - that is, Kazakhstan, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan and Kyrgyzstan - will be carried on International; Russia, Ukraine, Belarus, Moldova, Azerbaijan, Armenia and Georgia will remain on Europe. This new division is designed to reflect the different regional and political contexts in which the member states of the Commonwealth of Independent States are developing.
