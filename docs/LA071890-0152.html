July 18, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

MANDELA HOME, CALLS 6-WEEK TOUR A SUCCESS

African National Congress leader Nelson Mandela returned home today after declaring his six-week tour of three continents a "tremendous success."

He arrived on a Mozambique Airlines Boeing 737 more than two hours late at Johannesburg's Jan Smuts Airport, where about 100 supporters waited to greet him on his 72nd birthday.
