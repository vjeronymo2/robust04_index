December 24, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: LOS ALAMITOS;

BASKETBALL LEAGUE OFFERED IN JANUARY

The Los Alamitos Recreation and Community Services Department is offering a men's 5-man basketball league from January to March.

The fee for the league is $400. All teams will classification games the week of Jan. 7 and then will be divided into 6 team divisions. Divisions and nights of play are Monday, Wednesday and Friday.

All games will be played at Oak Academy Gymnasium.

For information, contact Jim Ivanick, adult sports coordinator, (213) 430-1073 or (714) 827-9010.
