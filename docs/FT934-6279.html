931125 FT 25 NOV 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Brazilian equities rose by 4.5 per cent in moderate activity on continued 'safe-haven' buying, ahead of possible anti-inflation measures and a drop in interest rates. The Bovespa index of the 48 most active stocks ended at 27,013, up 1,174. Newspaper reports saying that the government had issued a decree aimed at limiting legal objections to future government measures, for example on tax reforms, also provided support.
