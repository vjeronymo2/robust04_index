BFN [Text] According to Alfredo Saenz [chairman of Banesto] speaking today in the Congress of Deputies, the sale of Banesto's stake in media companies will "generate losses." Banesto's chairman confirmed that negotiations for selling Banesto's stake in Antena 3, Grupo Zeta, and Tele 5 are under way with Bankers Trust. The American bank will not be responsible for the sale of EL MUNDO and EPOCA. According to Saenz, the Banesto corporation "does not correspond to what an industrial group should be" and until definitive data is available, its 1993 loses may amount to 120,000 million pesetas.
