931217 FT 17 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Arlen disposes of distributor for Pounds 3.6m Arlen, the light fittings and electrical accessories company, is disposing of its Highland Electronics subsidiary to EAO Holding, a Swiss switch manufacturer, for about Pounds 3.6m in cash. Highland, which distributes electronic switches and components, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 262,000 for the year to March 31 1993. It is changing its year end from March 31 to December 31. There will be no announcement of interim results to September 30 1993.
