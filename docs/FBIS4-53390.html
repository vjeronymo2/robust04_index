BFN [Text] A vessel carrying Vietnamese refugees was found stranded between Tanjung Keluang and the West Waringin city of Kumai in Central Kalimantan. Bibit Sundari, Central Kalimantan public relations chief, said the refugees had a wooden vessel 20 meters wide and 60 meters long. There were 30 adults and 21 children aboard the vessel. The vessel was out of fuel and food. The boat people requested three [word indistinct], 200 liters of oil, 300 kg of rice, drinking water, and sufficient cooking oil. They needed the supplies to continue their effort to reach Australia.
