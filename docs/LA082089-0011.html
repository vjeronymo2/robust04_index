August 20, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE QUEST FOR PASTRAMI PARADISE

Forget hunting for the perfect West Coast pastrami sandwich. You gotta go to New York for a New York pastrami on rye!

Parenthetically, how could the testers have really gotten a good taste of pastrami at The Times? By the time the runner got back, the pastrami must have been cold. And no one with any sense of self will eat cold pastrami.

SHEILA KNECHT
