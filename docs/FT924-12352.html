921021 FT 21 OCT 92 / Sharp rise in business failures BUSINESS failures have soared this year and will continue to rise next year, according to Britain's leading private credit insurer. Failures in the first nine months reached 42,000 and are expected to pass 60,000 for the year as a whole - compared with 48,000 for the whole of last year, according to NCM Credit Insurance, which insures some Pounds 14bn of UK trade every year from 6,000 UK companies.
