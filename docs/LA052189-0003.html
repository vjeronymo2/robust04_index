May 21, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

MOVIES TO TAPE

The Adventures of Sherlock Holmes (Channel 11 Wednesday at 1 a.m.): With Basil Rathbone as Holmes and Nigel Bruce as Dr. Watson, both perfectly cast. (2:00)

The Picture of Dorian Gray (Channel 5 Wednesday at 1:30 a.m.): Elegant film version of the Oscar Wilde novel starring Hurd Hatfield and featuring a delightful, very young Angela Lansbury. (2:30)

Johnny Guitar (Channel 11 Wednesday at 3 a.m.): The Nicholas Ray cult Western with Joan Crawford at her most intense. (2:00)

Kanal (Z Wednesday at 3 p.m.): The second film in Andrzej Wajda's World War II trilogy, it is an unsparing account of the final days of the Warsaw uprising. (2:00)

The Boy Friend (TNT Wednesday at 5 p.m.): Ken Russell's stylish, engaging film of the '20s-era musical, starring a delectable Twiggy. (2:50)

To Have and Have Not (Channel 5 Saturday at 3:30 a.m.): Howard Hawks' smoldering love story about a skipper-for-hire starring Humphrey Bogart and Lauren Bacall. (2:00)
