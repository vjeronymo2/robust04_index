BFN [Text] The president, His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, last night telephoned his brother, His Excellency President Muhammad Husni Mubarak of the sisterly Arab Republic of Egypt, and exchanged views with him on the Yemeni developments and ways to contain the crisis and stop the internecine fighting in fraternal Yemen. They also discussed the latest Arab developments within the two presidents' continual contacts and consultations to serve the Arab nation and support Arab solidarity and causes.
