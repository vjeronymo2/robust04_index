BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Maynayev] [Text] Bishkek May 5 TASS -- Negotiations between lawmakers from Azerbaijan, Armenia, Kyrgyzstan, Russia and representatives of Nagorno-Karabakh have gave no result, because the Azerbaijani party refused to sign the final protocol on Thursday. [sentence as received] "We worked day and night to put together the two-page document, but failed to reach an agreement," Vladimir Shumeyko, chairman of the Federation Council (Russian parliament's upper house), who led the Russian delegation at the talks, told ITAR-TASS despondingly as he walked out into a corridor inside the Bishkek governmental palace, where the talks were held. The participants in the talks refused to talk with the journalists. Next time, they will tackle the Nagorno-Karabakh issues in the St. Petersburg-based headquarters of the CIS Interparliamentary Assembly. They are to leave Bishkek for their home countries on Thursday.
