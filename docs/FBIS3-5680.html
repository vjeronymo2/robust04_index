Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] The government plans to map out a package of measures to open the Japanese market in March. Commenting on the package on the morning of 7 March, a government source said: "Other sectors are all right, but the problem is with the automobile sector." In this way, he indicated that the most important task is to take measures for the automobile and auto parts sector, a priority sector, in the Japan-U.S. economic framework talks.
