931009 FT 09 OCT 93 / UK Company News: A Cohen tumbles to Pounds 36,000 SHARES in A Cohen fell by 30p to 360p yesterday after the company reported a sharp decline in pre-tax profits from Pounds 496,000 to Pounds 36,000 in the first half of 1993. The company, which makes non-ferrous metal ingots and is involved in the reclamation and trading of recyclable materials, has also omitted its interim dividend. Last year, an interim of 3.4p was followed by a final of the same amount. Turnover rose to Pounds 43.5m (Pounds 38.1m). A tax charge of Pounds 181,000 (Pounds 477,000) left losses per share at 8.96p (1.18p earnings).
