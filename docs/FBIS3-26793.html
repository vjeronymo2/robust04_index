Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 15 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing here today met separately with Ray Dalio, president of the Bridgewater Associates Inc. of the United States; Rin Kei Mei, vice-chairman of Malaysia's Eon Group and Mitsuo Hattori, president of Japan's Mitsubishi Motors Corporation. The U.S. visitor arrived here yesterday at the invitation of the China International Enterprises Cooperative Corporation. The Malaysian and Japanese visitors arrived here last Saturday [12 February] as guests of the office of the special zones of China's State Council.
