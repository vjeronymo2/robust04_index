920312 FT 12 MAR 92 / International Company News: News Corp seeks alliances MR Rupert Murdoch's News Corporation is negotiating with consumer electronic and computer companies to form alliances under which News Corp would supply electronic data for distribution to individual and company customers, writes Alan Friedman. To launch the business area, Mr Murdoch was forming a division to be called News Electronic Data (NED) with Mr John Evans, former head of the Murdoch magazines division, as president. Mr Evans said the new business would have non-exclusive access to the world-wide data bases of News Corp and would provide personal computer owners with the data in digital format, by way of telephone modem links.
