Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [ARMENPRESS correspondent -- TASS] [Text] Yerevan, 18 Feb -- Heavy fighting is continuing in the north of Karabakh. An ARMENPRESS correspondent was told in the defense headquarters of the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic that today Karabakh units succeeded in repulsing the enemy and drove them back beyond the Omar pass. The Azerbaijanis left dozens of dead on the field of battle. Azerbaijani forces today again attacked in the Mardakert Rayon sector. Karabakh units are containing the attacks. Other sections of the front are relatively quiet.
