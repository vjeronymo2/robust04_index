BFN [Text] Budapest, June 4 (MTI) -- At its one-day closed-door session on Saturday [4 June], the National Board of the Hungarian Democratic Forum [MDF] discussed parliamentary election experiences and the party's internal situation. A statement issued to the press after the conference, party chairman Lajos Fur announced the resignation of the MDF Presidium at the party's next special national convention, the date for which will be set later on. The statement says, "The board considers it necessary to thoroughly examine both the party's unsatisfactory election result and its future policy." Spokesman Karoly Herenyi told journalists that the board meeting heard over 30 contributions on reasons behind the election failure and ways of proceeding ahead. The MDF, adapting to current conditions, should shape its policy in a different style as an opposition force, it was stated at the session.
