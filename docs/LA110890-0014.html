November 8, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

IRELAND;

POPE NAMES BISHOP WHO OPPOSES IRA

Pope John Paul II appointed a prelate who has condemned the Irish Republican Army as evil to be the new spiritual leader of the 4 million Catholics on both sides of the Irish border. Archbishop Cahal Daly's denunciation of the IRA and pursuit of reconciliation between Ireland's so-called "two traditions" were believed major factors in his choice as archbishop of Armagh and primate of All Ireland.
