920214 FT 14 FEB 92 / Letter: Some do bounce back Sir, Lex made a telling point in reviewing Amstrad (February 12) by ending: 'As the old maxim has it, when they fall that far they never come back.' An excellent maxim, but it is the joy of investing to find the occasional exception - would that I had bought Grand Metropolitan at 20p in the 1974 shake-out. E G Abel, 56 Telford Avenue, London SW2 4XF
