Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] An hour ago, in the Lithuanian town of Palanga, the meeting of the presidents of the Baltic countries -- Algirdas Brazauskas, Guntis Ulmanis, and Lennart Meri -- began. The Lithuanian president held a lunch for his honored guests. Afterward, the meeting of the three Baltic countries' delegations and the presidents' talks began. During the meeting, they are going to discuss the integration of the Baltic countries into Western European structures, coming closer together with NATO, relations with neighboring states -- Russia -- and the withdrawal of Russian troops from Latvia and Estonia. Several documents are also due to be signed. The meeting will conclude with a ceremonial dinner.
