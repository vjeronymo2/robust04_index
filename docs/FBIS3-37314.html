Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow February 16 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin has sent a message of congratulations to Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas on the occasion of the Lithuanian national holiday -- the day of the Lithuanian state's restoration, the press service of the Russian president reports. "I hereby express my conviction that our joint efforts will ensure the further progressive development of Russo-Lithuanian relations, based on principles of sovereignty, equality, good-neighbourliness and respect of human rights. The Russian side is ready to traverse its part of the road in this direction," the message says.
