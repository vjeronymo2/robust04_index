June 27, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

BROKAW'S THE ONE: NBC News considers the Nixon Library opening such a good story that it will send anchorman Tom Brokaw to Yorba Linda that day (July 19) to do the NBC Nightly News broadcast there. . . . Spokesmen for rival ABC and CBS had identical reactions: "no plans at this point" to follow suit. Jeffrey A. Perlman and Steve Emmons
