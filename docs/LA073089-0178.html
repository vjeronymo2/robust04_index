July 30, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

A Houston woman has filed a paternity suit against Washington Redskins defensive end Dexter Manley, claiming that he is the father of her 11-year-old twin daughters.

The lawsuit filed by Ruby M. Lair is seeking child support and health care expenses for her and the two girls. She also is seeking to have the last name of the children -- Tamera and Tameka Lair -- changed to Manley.

Lair alleges that Manley, 30, fathered the children when he was living in Houston. The twin girls were born in January 1978.
