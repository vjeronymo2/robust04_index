920529 FT 29 MAY 92 / UK Company News In Brief ANDREWS SYKES Group has taken a 51 per cent stake in Kocoverk, a Swedish manufacturer and hirer of portable heating and air conditioning equipment, for Pounds 150,000 cash. The balance will be acquired for a maximum Pounds 500,000 over the next three years. Sykes has also paid Pounds 160,000 cash for Isis Pneumatics which services and supplies industrial compressors. ***** GARTMORE AMERICAN Securities: Net asset value 35.2p (40.3p) per ordinary and 69.3p (61.3p) per zero dividend preference at March 31 1992. Earnings per share for year 4.26p (4.12p). Fourth interim dividend of 1p makes total of 4p (3.7p).
