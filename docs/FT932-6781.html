930527 FT 27 MAY 93 / US offers olive branch to N Korea US OFFICIALS yesterday offered an olive branch to North Korea ahead of next week's talks at the United Nations on North Korea's withdrawal from the nuclear non-proliferation treaty. Mr Robert Gallucci, assistant secretary of state for political-military affairs, told a Senate committee yesterday that the US would be willing to consider improved economic and political links if North Korea allowed international nuclear inspectors into its reactor and nuclear fuel processing plant at Yongban. Mr Gallucci said the US was also prepared to address North Korean concerns.
