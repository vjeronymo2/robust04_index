BFN [Excerpt] A protocol of agreement for cooperation in various health fields was signed in Tripoli today between the Great Jamahiriyah and Malta. The agreement was signed by the secretary of the General People's Committee for Health and Social Security and John Rizzo Naudi, parliamentary secretary for health in the Republic of Malta. The protocol provides for the strengthening and development of cooperation existing now between the two countries and also the creation of new programs for the consolidation of cooperation in the various health fields, including the training of doctors and paramedical staff, the joint participation in the manufacture of medicines, and quality control.
