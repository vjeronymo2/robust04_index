930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / Slovakia gets Ecu40m from EC Slovakia will receive Ecu40m (Pounds 30m) in aid from the EC's eastern and central European PHARE assistance programme for 1993, EC and Slovak officials said yesterday, Reuter reports from Bratislava. The aid will be used to develop the private sector, human resources, infrastructure, development of energy efficiency and environmentally sound projects, and technical assistance programmes.
