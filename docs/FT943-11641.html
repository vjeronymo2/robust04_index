940729 FT 29 JUL 94 / Auction houses report higher sales A slow improvement in the international art market was confirmed yesterday when Sotheby's announced sales of Dollars 1.38bn in the 1993-94 season, a rise of 15 per cent, while Christie's increased turnover by 11 per cent to Dollars 1.17bn. In sterling terms Sotheby's was 19 per cent up at Pounds 919m. and Christie's 14 per cent up at Pounds 781m. The two smaller London-based auction houses also registered improvements, with Phillips gaining 12.5 per cent to Pounds 88m and Bonhams 21 per cent to Pounds 33.9m.
