931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Pounds 51m raised for Fairbairn European Trust Providence Capitol has raised Pounds 51m for the Fairbairn European Smaller Companies Index Trust, the first in a planned series of indexed investment trusts, writes Philip Coggan. The trust will track the James Capel European Smaller Companies Index Trust, which covers 17 countries and 1,000 stocks. Dealings in the shares are expected to start on December 16.
