BFN [Text] Majles Speaker ['Ali Akbar] Nateq-Nuri, who is currently visiting Malaysia, met with his Malaysian counterpart Muhammad Zahir Bin Haji in Kuala Lumpur today and discussed the expansion of bilateral relations, particularly at parliamentary level. During their talks, the speakers of the two countries' parliaments reached agreement on setting up parliamentary friendship groups between the two countries. Mr. Nateq-Nuri also visited the International Islamic University in Kuala Lumpur and was briefed on the teaching activities of this university and the conditions of the Muslim students.
