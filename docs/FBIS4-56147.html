BFN [Text] Budapest, 25 May (MTI) -- The National Election Committee approved, by unanimous vote, the final result of the first round of the parliamentary elections, which -- after legal proceedings were ended -- does not differ from the preliminary results. Accordingly, of the 7,959,228 eligible voters, 5,485,538 cast their votes on May 8.
