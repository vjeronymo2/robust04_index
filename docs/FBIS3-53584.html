Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In cooperation with Egypt and Jordan, the PLO has embarked on a process of counting Palestinian refugees who left the West Bank and Gaza Strip after the 1967 war as a result of Israel's occupation. The Palestinian news agency, WAFA, explains that this count constitutes the first step toward ensuring the return of those refugees. WAFA says that the PLO offices and the Egyptian embassies abroad have begun to count refugees from the occupied Gaza Strip. Newspapers will publish announcements in this regard. The agency adds that the PLO and Jordan will soon begin a similar process in Jordan to count the refugees from the occupied West Bank.
