December 19, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

AZTECS GIVE COACH CONTRACT EXTENSION

San Diego State football Coach Al Luginbill received a one-year contract extension to lead the Aztecs through the 1993 season, Athletic Director Fred Miller announced.

Luginbill directed San Diego State to back-to-back winning seasons for the first time since 1981-82. The Aztecs were 6-5 this season.
