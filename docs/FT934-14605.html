931015 FT 15 OCT 93 / International Company News: Elf Aquitaine selects advisers ELF Aquitaine, the French oil and gas group, yesterday announced that it had appointed Banque Nationale de Paris and Banque Paribas as its advisers for its forthcoming privatisation, writes Alice Rawsthorn in Paris. The news of the appointments intensified speculation on the Paris stock market that Elf will be the next company to be sold in the Balladur government's privatisation drive. Elf has already been named as one of the first four sale candidates. The first issue, the privatisation of Banque Nationale de Paris, will be completed today.
