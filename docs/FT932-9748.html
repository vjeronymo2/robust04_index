930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / International Company News: Philip Morris appointment PHILIP MORRIS, the US tobacco and food group, yesterday appointed Mr William Webb to head its international cigarette operations, writes Nikki Tait. Mr Webb, who was previously president of the Asia/ Pacific region for Philip Morris International, will report to Mr Geoffrey Bible, who was recently appointed head of the worldwide tobacco operations. The move is the latest in a series of top-level executive changes at Philip Morris. The company sent shockwaves round the consumer products sector when it announced large price-cuts in its leading cigarette brands, including Marlboro, last month.
