920221 FT 21 FEB 92 / International Company News: Levi Strauss advances 42% LEVI Strauss, the privately-held but largest apparel-maker in the world, yesterday reported after-tax profits of Dollars 356.7m for the year to November 24 - a 42 per cent advance on the previous 12 months, writes Nikki Tait in New York. Operating profits, however, increased by a more modest 15 per cent to Dollars 772.2m. In the fourth quarter alone, net profits rose by 40 per cent, to Dollars 105.5m, while operating profits rose 12 per cent at Dollars 208.4m. Sales for the year were Dollars 4.9bn, against Dollars 4.25bn.
