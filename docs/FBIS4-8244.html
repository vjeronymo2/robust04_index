BFN [Text] Tirana, April 8 (ATA) -- The delegation of the ad hoc committee of the Council of Europe led by Lord Finsberg, met on April 7 with the Albanian foreign minister, Alfred Serreqi. Mr. Finsberg took interest in the new dimensions of the Albanian policy, the events in Kosova and the stand of Albania towards Kosova and in Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM]. "Albania's integration into Europe is a priority of the Albanian foreign policy," Mr. Serreqi said, adding that Albania is making great changes and overcoming the grave consequences of the communist dictatorship. "We appreciate the aid and support by the european union", said Mr. Serreqi. "We are resolute and convinced of a successful cooperation with Europe".
