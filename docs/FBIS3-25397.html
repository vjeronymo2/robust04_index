Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Measures have been taken to ensure that the second round of the legislative elections proceed smoothly. The Togo-Benin and Togo-Burkina Faso land borders will therefore be closed as of midnight today until midnight on 21 February. Those are the contents of the communique issued by the minister for territorial administration and security, which also states that the western border is and will remain closed.
