930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / Team of Bahrain central bankers to visit South Africa A team of Bahrain central bankers and finance ministry officials will next week visit South Africa, taking what was described in Manama as a 'major step' towards developing trade and investment ties, Mark Nicholson writes from Cairo. It will be the most substantial economic and diplomatic overture towards South Africa from the Gulf since Pretoria began dismantling apartheid, and it crowns a series of initiatives, led by Bahrain, to attract South African investment into the region.
