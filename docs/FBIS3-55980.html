Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, January 11 (TURAN) -- A large amount of humanitarian assistance from Switzerland -- 240 thousand tons of wheat flour is to be delivered to Azerbaijan in the near future. On News Year's Eve, Azerbaijan received 580 tons of wheat flour from Switzerland. The shipment of assistance from Switzerland to Azerbaijan has been going on since December 29, 1993. The UN World Food Program (UNWFP) organized the delivery of 27 tons of food parcels from Switzerland to Azerbaijan. Prior to that, 39 tons of food were delivered for Azerbaijani refugees and displaced persons by the UNWFP . The United Nation's High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) and its Humanitarian Assistance Commission will begin the distribution of assistance following the delivery of the last lot. In addition, TURAN NEWS AGENCY was told at the UNO [United Nations Orgaization] Office in Azerbaijan that 6 containers, each of which include 20 tons of food, arrived. These goods were given to Azerbaijan by the European Community. An additional 314 containers will be delivered to Azerbaijan under the aegis of the European Community Assistance Programme in the near future.
