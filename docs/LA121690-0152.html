December 16, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

PREP SOCCER ROUNDUP

In the Orange boys' tournament:

Anaheim 2, La Serna 1 -- Mike Tsarnas and Julio Langarica scored for Anaheim in the semifinal victory.

Orange 2, Servite 1 -- Jason Smith scored the winner on a header with about 15 minutes to play for Orange (5-0-2), which will meet Anaheim next Saturday for the championship.

In the Cerritos girls' tournament:

Los Alamitos 8, Lakewood 0 -- Julie Harris had three goals and three assists and Brooke Davis scored three goals for Los Alamitos in a first-round game.
