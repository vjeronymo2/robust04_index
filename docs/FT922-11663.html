920424 FT 24 APR 92 / UK Company News: Wyevale buying Lewes operation Wyevale Garden Centres is increasing the number of centres under operation to 41 with the acquisition of Swanborough Garden Centre of Newhaven Road, Lewes, Sussex, for about Pounds 1.78m. Consideration will be satisfied from the proceeds of a placing of 946,872 shares at 171p each to raise Pounds 1.62m. The shares will not rank for the final dividend.
