940207 FT 07 FEB 94 / World News in Brief: Prince Charles in new scare The Prince of Wales was involved in a security scare last night when a man tried to spray him with an aerosol can in Auckland, New Zealand. A 58-year-old man was later charged with disorderly conduct. Twelve days ago a student fired two shots from a starting pistol as the prince was taking part in Australia Day celebrations in Sydney.
