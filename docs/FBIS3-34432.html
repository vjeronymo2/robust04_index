Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] There have been important developments in the investigation into the assassination of Jordanian diplomat Na'ib al-Ma'ayitah nine days ago. Security sources this afternoon confirmed a REUTER report that the Lebanese police have arrested a Palestinian, who admitted that he assassinated al-Ma'ayitah. Citing security sources, REUTER reported that the accused belongs to a Palestinian fedayeen group, and that he is currently being questioned. The agency added that the investigation by security men has led to the arrest of two people belonging to a Palestinian organization in connection with al-Ma'ayitah's assassination last Saturday. When Interior Minister Bisharah Mirhij was asked about additional information on this matter, he replied that the case is in the hands of the judiciary, and that the investigation is continuing.
