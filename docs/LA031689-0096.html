March 16, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WELL-RESTED LAZERS FACE LEAGUE-LEADING BLAST

Having spent more than a week off after losing their fourth straight game, the Lazers will try to rebound tonight against the Baltimore Blast at 7:30 at the Forum.

During their latest Major Indoor Soccer League tumble, the Lazers (16-19) have fallen eight games behind the first-place Blast.

Baltimore (24-11), coming off a 4-3 overtime victory over the Tacoma Stars last Sunday, is 5 1/2 games ahead of its nearest challenger.
