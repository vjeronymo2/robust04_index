931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / International Company News: CEA Industrie advances 12% CEA Industrie, the industrial arm of France's atomic energy commission, yesterday announced first-half pre-tax profits of FFr1.21bn (Dollars 216m), an increase of 12 per cent over the same period in 1992. The increase was achieved on sales of FFr19.33bn, an increase of just under 10 per cent on the first half of last year. Operating profits increased to FFr986m from FFr401m over the two periods. CEA Industrie is a holding group for businesses which have grown out of CEA research projects or have been inherited from the public sector.
