940503 FT 03 MAY 94 / Company News (This Week): Creditanstalt Bankverein - Other companies Austria's second largest bank will publish annual results today. These are expected to show a strong growth in earnings for 1993 due to a steep rise in trading activity. The state has a 49 per cent stake in Creditanstalt but over 70 per cent of the vote, and the bank is waiting for the government to decide on its privatisation which is expected this year.
