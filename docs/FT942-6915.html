940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / UK Company News: London Smaller net assets advance London Smaller Companies Investment Trust reported net asset value per share of 104.4p at March 31, against 87p a year earlier. Net revenue for the year to the end of March was Pounds 1.48m (Pounds 1.52m) for earnings per share of 3.25p (3.33p). A final dividend of 2.39p is proposed for a total of 3.17p (3.07p).
