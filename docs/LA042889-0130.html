April 28, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

NEWSMAKERS

Pacific Mutual, a Newport Beach-based life insurance company, has promoted three executives in its personal financial services division from vice president to senior vice president. They are John L. Hoffman, senior vice president, insurance administration; Lynn C. Miller, senior vice president, insurance actuarial and finance, and Walter J. Ridlon III, senior vice president, chief marketing officer. In addition, Kenneth D. Martinelli was appointed vice president, marketing. Previously, he was field vice president, sales.
