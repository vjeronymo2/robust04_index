Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Mate Granic, Croatian deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs, today received the U.S. President's special envoy Charles Redman. Minister Granic and Ambassador Redman had talks about the Bosnia-Herzegovina Assembly session and positively assessed developments in the field. They also spoke about specific moves in connection with the implementation of the Washington agreement and the possibility of the Serb side joining the future federation. They stressed it is very important to maintain the current pace of negotiations.
