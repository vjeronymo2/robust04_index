Language: French Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Interparliamentary Union Conference in Paris Supports UN Resolution on Hebron Massacre"] [Text] On Monday, 21 March, [French] National Assembly Speaker Philippe Seguin expressed the "support" of the Interparliamentary Union Conference for the UN resolution condemning the massacre of Palestinians at the Hebron mosque. Mr. Seguin emphasized that the conference is "very sensitive to massacres," not only in Hebron, but also in Bosnia and South Africa, and called for a minute's silence "to render homage to the memory of all these victims."
