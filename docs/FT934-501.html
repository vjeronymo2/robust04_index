931224 FT 24 DEC 93 / UK Company News: CA Sperati rises 31% to Pounds 54,336 CA Sperati, the buttons and trimmings company, lifted pre-tax profits by 31 per cent, from Pounds 41,414 to Pounds 54,336, for the year ended October 31. Turnover improved to Pounds 856,791 (Pounds 777,447) and after tax of Pounds 15,687 (Pounds 11,376) earnings came through at 38.65p (30.04p) per share.
