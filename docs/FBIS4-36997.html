BFN [Text] Doha, 14 Jun (WAKH)--His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad al- Thani, amir of Qatar, received here this evening Lakhdar Brahimi, envoy of the UN secretary general to Yemen. The Qatari News Agency said that during the meeting they discussed the situation in Yemen in light of the efforts being made as well as the implementation of UN Security Council Resolution 924, which provides for returning security and stability to Yemen.
