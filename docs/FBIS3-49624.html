Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, January 11 (ATA) -- Kosova Premier Bujar Bukoshi sent a letter to the premiers of NATO member states appealing on NATO to exercise its influence to take the following actions: Return of international human rights observers in Kosova, stationing of peace-keeping forces in the region, and continue to put pressure on Serbia and bring to the court all the criminals of mankind. Bukoshi observes that lack of immediate action in this regard means postponement of the inevitable, whose price will be dearer.
