TRAVEL PROGRAMS

Costa Rica, El Salvador and Guatemala will be among the regions explored by travel photographer and lecturer Mort Loveman in a slide presentation Wednesday 96at 1 p.m. at the club house, Roxbury Memorial Park, 471 S. Roxbury Drive, Beverly Hills. Admission is free. For more information, call (213) 550-4761.
