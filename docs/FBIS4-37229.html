BFN [Text] Tripoli, 19 Jun (TANJUG): Libyan leader Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi today received Dusan Simeonovic, Yugoslav ambassador to Tripoli, who handed him a message by Zoran Lilic, the president of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia. The ambassador passed on the high regard of the Yugoslav leadership and the FRY Government for the objective and constructive position the head of the Libyan state has had since the beginning of the Yugoslav crisis and Libya's for consistently friendly relations with Yugoslavia. Thanking him for the message, Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi reiterated his position of respect and support for the efforts the FRY Government is making toward a peaceful and just resolution of the conflict in the former Yugoslavia. They laid particular stress on the usefulness of the ongoing high-level dialogue between the two countries and the interest both sides are showing in its resumption.
