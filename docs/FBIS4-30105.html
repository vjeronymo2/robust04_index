BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 21 (KCNA) -- Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army Comrade Kim Chong-il sent thanks to many units and working people including the Sunan District, Pyongyang municipality, committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK], the Songyo District Garment Office in Pyongyang, the Segori Senior Middle School in Pyongyang, the Pukchang County, South Pyongan Province, committee of the WPK, the hot rolling shop of the Kim Chaek Iron and Steel Complex and Kim Myong-chol, a cameraman of the physical science library of the state commission of physical culture and sports, for their full display of the traditional traits of army-people unity. In the DPRK where the tradition of army-people unity is in full bloom, the army and the people are defending the socialist motherland, closely rallied behind the party and the leader in one mind and purpose. Upon receiving the thanks, the officials of the units and working people are hardening their determination to bring about a new turn in the performance of their revolutionary assignments and better their assistance to the People's Army.
