Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Edict No. 2282 of the Russian Federation president "On Financing State Expenditure from the Russian Federation's Republic Budget in the First Quarter of 1994," dated Moscow, the Kremlin, 24 December 1993 and signed by Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin] [Text] For the purposes of ensuring continuous financing of state expenditure from the Russian Federation's republic budget until the adoption of the Russian Federation Law "On the Republic Budget of the Russian Federation for 1994" and in accordance with Points 2 and 3 of the Russian Federation president's Edict No. 1598 of 7 October 1993 "On Legal Regulation During the Period of Stage-by-Stage Constitutional Reform in the Russian Federation," I decree that: 1. The Russian Federation Council of Ministers-Government is to be permitted to finance state programs and measures in the first quarter of 1994 proceeding from the Russian Federation republic budget's expenditure for the fourth quarter of 1993, established by the Russian Federation president's Edict No. 2234 of 21 December 1993 "On Amplifying the Indicators of the Russian Federation Republic Budget for the Fourth Quarter of 1993" and with due regard for the 90-percent increase in the minimum compensation as of 1 December 1993 in accordance with the Russian Federation president's Edict No. 2115 of 5 December 1993. 2. In the first quarter of 1994 part of the revenues from excise duties and value-added tax is to be transferred to the budgets of the components of the Russian Federation according to the normatives laid down in Article 4 of the Russian Federation Law "On the Republic Budget of the Russian Federation for 1993." 3. The Central Bank of the Russian Federation is to grant credit to the Russian Federation Council of Ministers-Government in the first quarter of 1994 to finance the expenditure of the Russian Federation's republic budget in the sum of 7.5 trillion rubles [R], including R300 billion to finance defense industry conversion programs on a return basis and R400 billion to finance investment programs, for a 10-year period, with repayment in equal instalments starting in 1998, calculated on the basis of 10 percent per annum. 4. The Russian Federation Ministry of Finance is to: ensure the financing of the expenditure of the Russian Federation's republic budget in the first quarter of 1994 as revenues enter the budget, with due regard for Central Bank of the Russian Federation credit; transfer from the Russian Federation's republic budget to the budgets of the components of the Russian Federation in the first quarter of 1994 subsidies and subventions to equalize the level of social provision for the population in the volumes envisaged for these purposes in the fourth quarter of 1993 and corrected for the increase as of 1 December 1993 in the minimum compensation in accordance with the Russian Federation president's Edict No. 2115 of 5 December 1993 and for the size of the Russian Federation republic budget arrears formed during 1993 in relation to this expenditure. 5. In order to change the terms for granting long-term credit allocated in accordance with Point 1 of the Russian Federation Supreme Soviet Decree No. 3125-1 of 26 June 1992 "On Granting Long-Term Credit to the Russian Federation Ministry of Finance" and Point 8 of the Russian Federation Supreme Soviet Decree No. 4130-1 of 18 December 1992 "On Bringing into Force the Russian Federation Law 'On Amplifying the Indicators of the Russian Federation Republic Budget for 1992,'" the Russian Federation Ministry of Finance is to be permitted to start paying interest as of January 1994 to the Central Bank of the Russian Federation calculated on the basis of 10 percent per annum. 6. The Russian Federation Ministry of Finance is to be entrusted, jointly with the Russian Federation Pension Fund and with due regard for the results of the implementation of the Pension Fund budget in 1993, with examining the question of extending to the first quarter of 1994 the validity of Article 7 of the Russian Federation Law "On the Republic Budget of the Russian Federation for 1993." 7. This edict is to be submitted to the Russian Federation Federal Assembly for examination. 8. This edict comes into force the moment it is published. [Signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin [Dated] 24 December 1993
