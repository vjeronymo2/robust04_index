Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister brother Muhammad Salim Basindwah received a number of friendly states' ambassadors in Sanaa at noon today. He briefed them on the situation in Yemen as a result of the current political crisis. He expressed the thanks of the president, leadership, government, and people of Yemen for their support for Yemen's unity and democracy, for their interest in our country's security, safety, and stability, and for their respective countries' sincere and unbiased stands on the crisis. He said he appreciated all friends' sincere efforts to contain the crisis, especially those that are being exerted by the United States. He emphasized the call for their respective countries to deal directly with the Foreign Ministry in its capacity as our country's main channel of contact and representative of the constitutional legitimate authority of the Republic of Yemen.
