940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Gartmore Emerging Pacific advances Gartmore Emerging Pacific Investment Trust lifted net asset value per share to 191.7p at the end of 1993, against 81.5p a year earlier. After-tax revenue increased from Pounds 187,000 to Pounds 279,000 in 1993, equivalent to earnings per share of 0.44p, compared with 0.3p. The single dividend is increased to 0.17p (0.15p).
