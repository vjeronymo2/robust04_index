CSO [Article by Chen Zhenhong [7115 6297 7703]: ``MEI's Institute 23 Develops Reeling Fiber Optic Cable, Reeling Apparatus''] [Summary] The reeling optical cable and reeling apparatus developed by MEI's Institute 23 has been formally certified. This cable, containing four to six fibers, has an external diameter of 4 mm and comes with a burn-resistant jacket. The reeling apparatus, consisting of a tractor and a reeling machine, can be manually operated or remote-controlled (for high overhead cable laying).
