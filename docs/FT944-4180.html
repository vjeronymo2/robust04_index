941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / Observer: First and last Whatever one thinks of the cogency of the arguments advanced by British Gas to support chief executive Cedric Brown's remuneration levels, the logic at work in the lower echelons of the utility is certainly less than perfect. Promoting a monthly direct debit system for settling the gas bill, the company writes to an FT reader proudly announcing that, 'as a valued customer, we thought you should be among the first to know about an important new way to save money'. Sadly, the impact, if any, was rather blunted by the accompanying sign-off: 'Act now and join the other three million customers who already use this method . . .'
