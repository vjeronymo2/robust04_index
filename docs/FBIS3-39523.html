Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] The proximity talks on the confidence-building measures began this morning with a meeting between President Glavkos Kliridhis and UN officials. The UN officials were led by Joe Clark, the UN secretary general's special representative on Cyprus. Participating in the UN team were Gustave Feissel, the UN secretary general's deputy special representative on Cyprus, and UNFICYP [UN Forces in Cyprus] political adviser John Paul Cavanagh. Also attending the talks were Foreign Minister Alekos Mikhailidhis, government spokesman Ioannis Kasoulidhis, Deputy Minister to the President Pandelis Kouros, and Attorney General Mikhalakis Triandafillidhis. Clark met with President Kliridhis for two and a half hours. Speaking after the meeting, Clark said that President Kliridhis and the UN officials dealt with all the issues on the agenda. He added that the seven key points and the maps were discussed.
