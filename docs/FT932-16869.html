930403 FT 03 APR 93 / Probe into MPs' psychology ONE IN six MPs display 'quite poor psychological health' shown by dissatisfaction with their job performance, a feeling of not being in control, and a tendency towards irritability and lack of tolerance, according to a survey. A total of 124 MPs replied to a questionnaire from Mr Ashley Weinberg, a psychologist at Manchester Royal Infirmary. Two-fifths of respondents said they worked more than a 70-hour week and eight out of 10 worked more than 55 hours.
