BFN [Excerpt] U.S. naval inspectors have turned back a ship bound for the Port of al-'Aqabah carrying a consignment of 3,943 tonnes of soya feed for a Jordanian merchant. The vessel had been intercepted at the Strait of Tiran. The inspection team explained that the lower quarters of the vessel had proved inaccessible to them. The ship had to return for refueling at the Port of Suez. [passage omitted]
