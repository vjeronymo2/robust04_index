Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, January 25 (SNARK)--"In case Great Britain starts to supply Azerbaijan with armaments, it will contradict the UN Security Council resolutions on Nagorno-Karabakh", asserted the Head of Foreign Organizations Department of the NKR [Nagorno- Karabakh Republic] Ministry of Foreign Affairs Masis Mayilyan. These resolutions, added he, insistingly call states to refrain from supplying conflicting sides with any types of arms and ammunition which can lead to escalation of the tension. The information about intentions of the English business circles to conclude a bargain on arms supplies to Azerbaijan recently was spread by London daily THE INDEPENDENT.
