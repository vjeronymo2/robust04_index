BFN [Text] The march planned by the Association of Young People's Power of Haiti [AJEPOPOA] has been canceled. The march against the embargo, which should have taken place today, 23 June, will not occur. AJEPOPOA Secretary General Jude L. Jean-Jacques justified his decision to cancel the demonstration citing the discovery of a plot hatched by diplomatic mission members in Port-au-Prince. [Begin Jean-Jacques recording, in Creole] Those teams have decided to create mass confusion. They are even considering dropping explosives on this occasion and creating panic in several areas of the capital. We are taking this opportunity to denounce it and say that this plan will not work. [end recording]
