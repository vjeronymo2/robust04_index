BFN [Text] Australia is putting $1.5 million towards new facilities at (Kakoda) in Papua New Guinea [PNG]. The prime minister, Paul Keating, says Australia and the PNG Government will jointly build facilities expected to include a new airport terminal and a medical center. Mr. Keating says the facilities will commemorate the sacrifices made by both countries during bloody Second World War battles with Japanese forces. He has paid tribute to Australian personnel who served at (Kakoda) and praised the unquestioning help, courage, and selfless humanitarianism of Papua New Guineans.
