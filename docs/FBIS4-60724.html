BFN [Text] Transport Minister Adib Halasah has praised relations between the two fraternal countries, Jordan and Qatar, and the high level of these historic relations, whose foundations were laid down by His Majesty King Husayn and Qatari Amir His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani. In a statement to the Jordan News Agency during the inauguration of the first air route between Jordan and Qatar, he said the bilateral transportation network allows the two countries to cooperate in various fields. Shaykh Hamad Bin-'Ali Al Thani, director general of the Qatari airlines authority, praised the inauguration of this important air route, stressing that the inauguration of this route continues the firm cooperation between the two countries and provides new prospects to promote this cooperation.
