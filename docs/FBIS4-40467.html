BFN [Text] The anti-submarine ship Admiral Chabanenko was launched on Wednesday evening at the Yantar ship building works in the Kaliningrad Region. The Baltic Fleet press center announced to Interfax that the ship had been constructed with utilization of high technologies. It can carry out any tasks in any places of the world ocean. Completion of the work will take about two years. Afterwards, the crew will start carrying out military tasks. Baltic Fleet Commander, Admiral Vladimir Yegorov said that this was a precious gift on the eve of the 300-year anniversary of the Russian Navy.
