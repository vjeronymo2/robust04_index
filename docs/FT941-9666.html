940214 FT 14 FEB 94 / California quake money unspent since 1989 Nearly Dollars 3bn (Pounds 2bn) in bonds and tax money raised since the 1989 earthquake to make California safer is still unspent, AP reports from San Francisco. The San Francisco Examiner found that Dollars 2.8bn of the Dollars 5bn raised by the public in the aftermath of the 1989 quake remained unspent. At the same time, some 1,000 projects to strengthen buildings, bridges and roads have been stalled by paperwork, engineering obstacles or political controversy, it reported.
