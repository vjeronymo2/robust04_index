940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / People: BET business services Graham Kettle, 46, managing director of Initial Cleaning Services, has been appointed sector director for BET's business services (cleaning and guarding) Europe. Peter Wakeham, 47, currently managing director of BET management services and Initial healthcare services, has been appointed sector director for business services (healthcare, facilities management, personnel and catering) Europe. Both join the group management committee.
