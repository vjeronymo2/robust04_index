BFN [Text] Defense Minister Haytham Qasim Tahir has issued a ministerial order calling for the general mobilization of human and material reserves in all governorates. The first article of the order provides for the declaration of a general mobilization of human and material reserves in all governorates. The third article of the order (?commits) the general administration to mobilize its reserves and branches in the governorates to implement the mobilization tasks for which they are responsible, and to organize callups and scrutinize specializations in accordance with unit needs for human and material resources with effect from 6 May. The sixth article of this order stipulates that all military personnel must proceed to their military units within a period of 24 hours. The seventh article of the order commits the general administration to mobilize its reserves, and through its branches in the governorates to coordinate with large organizations, the public sector, and government organizations to ensure the execution of the tasks stipulated in this order. The eighth article of the order stipulates that the order will become operative with effect from its date of issuance, and that it shall be published in the official gazette.
