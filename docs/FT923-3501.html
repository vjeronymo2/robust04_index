920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Robinson Bros sees weak second half Robinson Brothers (Ryders Green), the unquoted West Bromwich-based chemicals group, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 736,000 for the six months to June 30. The slight decline from the comparable Pounds 758,000 came on turnover of Pounds 12.9m (Pounds 12.1m). Directors described trading as 'fitful' and anticipated a weaker second half. Earnings per Pounds 1 share were unchanged at 25p.
