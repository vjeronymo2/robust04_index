April 13, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

ANTI-ABORTIONIST STIRS STRONG REACTIONS

McMillan says, ". . . I murdered a child." I suggest she mitigate her offense by hopping in her Mercedes 380SL, driving up to Central L.A. and adopting one of the hundreds of black, Hispanic, or physically challenged babies whose mother practiced what McMillan preaches.

That would accomplish so much more than attempting to deny a woman the right to choose. Better yet, McMillan should gracefully bow out of the fight. Her thread of credibility is hopelessly frayed.

DENNIS D. JAMES, Ranch Santa Margarita
