Language: Slovene Article Type:BFN [Text] This evening, the prime minister's cabinet reported that U.S. Ambassador Alan Wendt was received by Prime Minister Dr. Janez Drnovsek today. The prime minister praised highly the accomplished level of cooperation in the political and economic spheres and at the same time informed Mr. Wendt that our government today explained its cooperation and contacts with the U.S. diplomatic mission in Ljubljana to the public. Ambassador Wendt emphasized that a clear stance of the Slovene Government is very important for further strengthening of relations between the states and especially for the support of the United States for Slovenia's integration in international political and economic institutions.
