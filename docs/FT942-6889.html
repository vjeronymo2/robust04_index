940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / International Company News: AirTouch in Belgian move AirTouch Communications, the US wireless communications group, is to spend up to Dollars 147m to take a 25 per cent stake in Belgacom Mobile, a new mobile communicatons company being formed by Belgacom, the Belgian telecommunications company. The joint venture company will include Belgacom's analog and digital cellular businesses. Belgacom launched a new ditigal service, using the European GSM standard, last January. It now serves over 30,000 customers and covers over 80 per cent of Belgium. AirTouch, previously knowns as PacTel - was selected by Belgacom to provide support for the GSM launch. Mr Sam Ginn, chairman of AirTouch, said yesterday's agreement in principle would allow the two to 'formalise our relationship and continue to build on our achievements'.
