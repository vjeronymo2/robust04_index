December 6, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

KEATING DONATION

In regard to the article (Nov. 23) detailing the donations by Charles Keating to various politicians: It seems incredible that Sen. Cranston and this group of recipients can stand there and look the taxpayers in the eye and say, "This money belongs to me, not the depositor or the bond holder."

As I surmise the situation, the recipients of Mr. Keating's largess, have received stolen property, and it should be returned immediately.

GEORGE MOFFAT

La Habra
