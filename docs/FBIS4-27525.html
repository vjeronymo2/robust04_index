BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 30 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea sent messages to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India (Marxist) and the Central Committee of the October 8 Revolutionary Movement of Brazil in reply to their greetings on the occasion of the 62nd founding anniversary of the Korean People's Army [KPA]. In the reply messages it expressed deep thanks to them for the wholehearted congratulations they extended to the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, on the 62nd founding anniversary of the KPA, and expressed the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the WPK and these parties would grow stronger and develop.
