941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Tinsley Robor An 'improved performance from all operations' was reported by Tinsley Robor, with pre-tax profits advancing from Pounds 12,000 to Pounds 731,000 for the six months to end-September. Turnover at the printing and packaging group was up from Pounds 13.1m to Pounds 14.7m, and after an interest charge unchanged at Pounds 231,000, earnings per share came out at 1.8p (0.2p losses). The company is returning to the dividend list with a 0.333p distribution. The shares closed 2p ahead at 37p. Mr John Rose, chairman, said prospects for the second half were encouraging, with every sign of margins being maintained.
