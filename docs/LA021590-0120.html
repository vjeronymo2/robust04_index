February 15, 1990, Thursday, Valley Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

HOUSTON LEADS PIERCE OFFENSIVE SURGE, 26-5

Pat Houston almost hit for the cycle Wednesday -- in the first inning -- in Pierce College's 26-5 nonconference baseball victory over visiting Compton.

Houston hit a single, double and triple and drove in six runs as the Brahmas (4-2) batted around twice and scored 20 runs in the first inning.

In another baseball game:

Cal State Northridge 9, The Master's 7 -- Mike Solar's two-run double in the fifth inning proved decisive in the Matadors' nonconference baseball win at home.

Solar's hit to right field gave CSUN (4-4) an 8-5 lead.

WOMEN'S BASKETBALL

Antelope Valley 65, Victor Valley 62 -- Molly Jonnum had 21 points and 12 rebounds as Antelope Valley (17-10, 11-1) clinched a tie for its first Foothill League title.

MEN'S BASKETBALL

Antelope Valley 122, Victor Valley 84 -- Seven players scored in double figures for the Marauders.
