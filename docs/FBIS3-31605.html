Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Serbs in Macedonia Making Threats: Americans Unsafe in Macedonia"] [Text] "The eventual bombardment of the Serbs in Bosnia means a bombardment of all Serbs, and with that, the safety of the U.S. soldiers in Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] is brought into question," the president of the Democratic Party of the Serbs in Macedonia Dragisa Miletic, threatened yesterday. At the press conference, Miletic accused the Macedonian authorities of lying to the Serbs and Montenegrins in Macedonia, for the past three years, that they will be "returned" in the Constitution. The party's vice president Stojan Trajkovic announced that the Serbs will boycott the forthcoming census in Macedonia, unless two census lists and the participation of representatives of the Serbian people, in the census commissions, is provided.
