Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Armenian Supreme Council Chairman Babken Ararktsyan today received the Iranian Charge d'Affaires ad Interim Ahmad Sobhani at Sobhani's request. The diplomat congratulated the Supreme Council chairman on the new year and handed him a message from Nateq-Nuri, the speaker of the Iranian Parliament. He also expressed Iran's readiness to establish parliamentary cooperation. Armenian-Iranian ties, prospects for developing mutually advantageous cooperation, and regional issues were discussed at the meeting.
