May 18, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Irvine Sensors Corp.: The Costa Mesa developer of infrared devices and computer electronics reported a second-quarter loss of $165,900, contrasted with a year-earlier loss of $262,500.

Revenue for the quarter ended April 1 rose 22% to $869,700 from $710,200. For the six-month period, the company lost $307,300, contrasted with a loss of $456,500 a year earlier. Revenue increased 29% to $1.8 million from $1.4 million.
