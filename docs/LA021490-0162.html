February 14, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

RUDY GATLIN TO GET MARRIED

Country singer Rudy Gatlin is getting married May 12 to the woman he has been dating since last spring.

The ceremony will take place in Dallas, hometown of the bride-to-be, 29-year-old Kim Harmon, said a publicist for the Gatlin Brothers. Rudy Gatlin is 37; his brothers, Larry and Steve, are married. Among the groups' hits are "All The Gold in California," "Broken Lady" and "Houston."
