January 15, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

NOT HERE: Air travelers could be taxed $2 or $3 each time they board a flight, the money going for local airport development, if the nation's airport operators get their way. . . . Sentiment for a departure tax is building in Washington -- but not at John Wayne Airport, despite its $310-million expansion. . . . Even if Congress authorized the tax, "we already have our sources of funding lined up," Airport Manager George A. Rebella said. "We probably wouldn't levy the tax." Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
