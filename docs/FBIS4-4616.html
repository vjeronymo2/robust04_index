BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 3 (KCNA) -- The national conference of party cell secretaries which was held in Pyongyang from March 31 to April 2 was a historical conference which marked an occasion of a new turn in further strengthening and developing the Workers' Party of Korea and successfully accomplishing the socialist cause of chuche by enhancing the function and role of the party cells in compliance with the developing reality, declares NODONG SINMUN today in an editorial. The editorial says: It was a proud conference which fully demonstrated the wisdom of the guidance of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, who have strengthened the cells and other basic organizations of the party and built up the party to be an impregnable one by founding the chuche-oriented idea of party building and energetically guiding the struggle for its materialization. It also demonstrated the revolutionary faith and unshakable will of all the party cell secretaries and party members to complete the building of Korean-style socialism with the might of singlehearted unity. Today the party cell secretaries remain faithful to the leadership to the party and the leader and are successfully discharging their noble mission and duty in increasing the party's might and carrying forward the revolutionary cause of chuche. The conference powerfully demonstrated that the WPK with hundreds of thousands of militant cells unfailingly loyal to the party and the leader is an ever-victorious, veteran and experienced party, a great party that leads the cause of independence in our age to victory, smashing any anti-socialist moves. The editorial calls on the whole party, the entire people and all the Army to turn out as one in the struggle for frustrating all the provocative moves of the imperialists and defending and developing Korean socialism like an impregnable fortress, united in one mind around the great General Kim Chong-il.
