BFN [Text] Tehran, April 21, IRNA -- Iran and Libya Thursday called for the strengthening and expansion of fraternal relations between the two Muslim countries within the framework of their joint commission. The visiting member of the Libyan Command Council, Mustafa al-Kharrubi, and the Iranian vice-president for legal affairs, 'Ata'ollah Mohajerani, in a meeting here this morning also discussed matters of mutual interest. The Libyan official who is carrying a message for Iranian President Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani from his Libyan counterpart Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi, arrived here Wednesday heading a political and economic delegation.
