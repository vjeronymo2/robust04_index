Combatting Drug Smuggling LD2905055594 Almaty Kazakh Radio Network in Kazakh 0400 GMT 28 May 94 LD2905055594 Almaty Kazakh Radio Network Kazakh BFN [Text] Vice President Yerik Asanbayev received yesterday Giorgio Giacomelli, UN deputy secretary general and executive director of the UN fund for drug abuse. Under discussion was the issue of combatting drug smuggling. Giorgio Giacomelli said new sovereign states that appeared on the world's map after the break-up of the USSR are now turning into drug dealing centers. They also discussed the UN policy with regard to this issue. The vice president spoke about Kazakhstan's vision of its participation in the UN programme. Yerik Asanbayev emphasized that drug smuggling has to be addressed urgently. He said Kazakhstan has turned into a drug trading center, but does not have the equipment and experts to combat it. Therefore Kazakhstan would be grateful for any assistance that could be rendered to it by international organizations. This year Kazakhstan plans to send its envoy to Vienna who will deal with this issue. Kazakhstan is willing to cooperate with the United Nations in tackling this issue.
