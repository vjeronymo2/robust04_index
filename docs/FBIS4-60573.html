BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' today received Viktor Posuvalyuk, Russian President Boris Yeltsin's envoy. Attending the meeting were Dr. Ghassan Raslan, director of the East Europe Department at the Foreign Ministry, and Aleksandr Zotov, Russian ambassador in Damascus. The talks dealt with developments in the peace process, Israel's arrogant policies, and its stands obstructing the achievement of a just and comprehensive peace in the region. The talks also dealt with relations between Syria and Russia and means of promoting them.
