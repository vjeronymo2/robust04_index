920319 FT 19 MAR 92 / Election 1992 (The Manifestos): Revisionism - Ballot Box Labour party headquarters at London's Walworth Road is advertising a gala performance of the Kirov opera, ballet, orchestra and chorus at the Royal Opera House. The show will be attended by the Princess of Wales, the date is April 9 and the performance is called Welcome Back St Petersburg, which may be an appropriate way for Labour to spend election night.
