940613 FT 13 JUN 94 / Business Travel: BA services British Airways is launching an eighth daily flight between London and New York from September 12, the company announced last week. The daily Boeing 767 flight will leave Heathrow's Terminal Four at 8.45am, arriving at the airline's Kennedy terminal in New York at 11.45am. The return service will leave Kennedy at 10pm reaching Heathrow at 10.20am the next day.
