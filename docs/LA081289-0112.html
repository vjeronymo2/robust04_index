August 12, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNT DIGEST: FULLERTON;

GROUP OPPOSING 6-LANE ROAD PLANS MEETING

A group opposed to the widening planned for Bastanchury Road will meet Aug. 17 to plan an initiative drive to put the issue on the ballot.

The Save Our Bastanchury group will meet at 7:30 p.m. at Emmanuel Episcopal Church parish hall, 1145 Valencia Mesa Drive.

Any resident opposed to the plan to widen the road to six lanes between Euclid Street and Harbor Boulevard is invited to attend.

The Fullerton City Council on July 5 turned down the group's request to stop the project, whose cost is estimated at $4 million.

The group plans to appear before the council to ask that the issue be put before voters. The group has planned its meeting to discuss the initiative drive in the event that the council does not agree to put the matter to a vote.

The group says that widening the road will increase noise and traffic and will ruin its scenic, rural nature.
