940506 FT 06 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Chartwell Dealings in Chartwell Group, the manufacturer of carpet tiles and toilet cubicles, were suspended at 48p yesterday at the company's request 'pending shareholder approval of a proposed substantial acquisition, the terms of which are currently being finalised'. Chartwell has been linked recently with Sir Tim Bell, and his Lowe Bell public relations company. There has been speculation that Lowe Bell could be seeking a listing by reversing into Chartwell.
