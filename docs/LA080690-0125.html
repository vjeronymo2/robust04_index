August 6, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

$260,000 STOLEN AT KIDS CONCERT

Three robbers made off with $260,000 in cash from souvenir sales at a New Kids on the Block concert, police said.

Police spokesman Jacques Morrisseau said the robbers, armed with revolvers and a bogus hand grenade, entered a room in Olympic Stadium where 12 employees were counting money Sunday after a show by the teen-age pop group.

The robbers handcuffed the employees and placed the fake grenade near a door, warning that the device would explode if someone tried to open the door.
