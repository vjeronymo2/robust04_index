Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad received Lebanese Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz at 1245 today. Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' attended the meeting. Talks during the meeting centered on future concerted efforts to be taken within the framework of the peace process, as well as the tasks entrusted to the heads of the Syrian and Lebanese delegations to the Washington bilateral peace talks. They also touched on the expected meeting in Geneva between President Hafiz al-Asad and U.S. President Bill Clinton. [passage omitted]
