Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] The Azerbaijani Security Ministry press service reports that the police have prevented the smuggling of 40 tons of aluminum from Azerbaijan in the last three days. After they took measures, 2.3 kg of narcotic material was seized at Astara border post this morning. The material was found in a secret compartment of an Ikarus bus with license plate 02AAQ and belonging to Baku Intourist Firm. The bus was going from Baku to Syria via Nakhichevan. Drivers Mirzeyev Atabala Eshrefogly, Aziziv Azizbala Teymushagly, and Chilinger Murselifrasim Misirogly were jailed and an investigation was launched. An attempt to smuggle large amounts of goods through Dzhalilabad Rayon was also prevented. Mirzeyev Kabil, a resident of Dzhalilabad whose activities gave rise to suspicion, was detained while trying to illegally cross the state border. Some $15,000 and 1.5 million manats were confiscated from him. His efforts to bribe the border guards was in vain. He was jailed and an investigation launched. Efforts are underway to ensure our republic's security and the inviolability of the state borders.
