May 20, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION BASEBALL PLAYOFFS;

3-A ROUNDUP;

TUSTIN'S JONES PITCHES NO-HITTER

Senior right-hander Monte Jones pitched a no-hitter and struck out 15 to lead Tustin High School to a 4-0 victory over Calexico in the opening round of the Southern Section 3-A baseball playoffs at Tustin.

Jones, who walked eight batters, also had two runs batted in. Eric Grove went two for four for Tustin (18-8). He had a double and scored a run. Calexico finished its season 11-10.

Jones is 10-2. He led Orange County in strikeouts with 110 during the regular season.
