BFN [Text] The leader of Russia's Liberal Democratic party Vladimir Zhirinovskiy is planning to leave Moscow on 8 May for Belgrade. The press secretary of the Liberal Democratic faction in the Russian State Duma Grigoriy Serebrennikov says that Zhirinovskiy will be accompanied by a deputy chairman of both the Liberal Democratic party and the State Duma Aleksandr Vengerovskiy and Liberal Democrats who are either doing active duty or are retired servicemen. According to Serebrennikov, the Zhirinovskiy delegation will be expected to hold consultations with Bosnia's Serb leaders.
