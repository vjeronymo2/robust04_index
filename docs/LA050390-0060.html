May 3, 1990, Thursday, Nuestro Tiempo Edition

HISPANIC JOURNAL RECEIVES ATTENTION

Harvard alumni and Southland Latino leaders met in Los Angeles recently to call attention to the publication of the Spring 1990 Journal of Hispanic Policy, an annual report on the progress of the Latino community by the Hispanic Students Caucus at Harvard University's John F. Kennedy School of Government.

The publication is funded by corporate and individual contributions. Eight members of its 25-student staff are from Southern California.
