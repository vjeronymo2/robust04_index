December 25, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Clarification: In a Dec. 16 story about a man challenging his criminal conviction, public defender's investigator John V. Depko told The Times that of 2,000 cases he has worked, "this is the only one where I feel the defendant is innocent." Depko said he did not mean to imply that all his clients are guilty; only that of those convicted, he believes this one to be innocent.
