940423 FT 23 APR 94 / Finance and the Family: FT Quarterly Review of Personal Finance - At a glance The collapse in interest rates has created a Catch 22 situation for pensioners: few who need to rely on investment income can afford to adopt the most sensible long-term strategy. Over the long term, equities have held up well against inflation, but many investors need a high income at once. The spring issue of the FT Quarterly Review of Personal Finance, to be published with next Friday's paper and repeated with Saturday's, considers some sources of immediate income. The survey section of the Review focuses on high street investment.
