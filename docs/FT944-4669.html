941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Acal advances Strong growth in northern Europe, particularly Germany, helped Acal, the electronics distributor, almost triple interim pre-tax profits. The figures were also boosted by the purchase of 70 per cent of EAF. On turnover for the period to September 30 up by a third to Pounds 50.3m (Pounds 37.7m) profits were Pounds 3.47m (Pounds 1.31m). EAF contributed turnover of Pounds 7.91m and operating profits of Pounds 1.25m to a total of Pounds 3.77m. Earnings per share came out at 10.5p (5.3p) and the interim dividend is being raised to 2.44p (2.25p).
