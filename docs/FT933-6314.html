930828 FT 28 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Surrey Group incurs Pounds 0.9m loss Surrey Group, the USM-quoted bookmakers, ran up a loss of Pounds 937,000 pre-tax for the year to end-March. That compared with profits of just Pounds 5,000 last time. Turnover declined from Pounds 49,571 to Pounds 47,081. Losses per share emerged at 0.48p (earnings 0.07p) and there is again no dividend. The deficit before interest from the bookmaking activities amounted to Pounds 715,000 and profits realised from betting office sales and other income totalled Pounds 522,000. That resulted in a net operating loss of Pounds 193,000. The pre-tax results was struck after deducting interest of Pounds 744,000. The directors said that since the beginning of the year trading conditions in the bookmaking industry had improved. They added that the group was currently trading at a 'small profit' but warned that it was 'too early to say whether this will lead to a return to net profitability for the current half year as a whole.'
