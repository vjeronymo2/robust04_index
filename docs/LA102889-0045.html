October 28, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

FOOTHILL LEAGUE

Burroughs 3, Alhambra 0 -- Jeff Gierok kicked a 30-yard field goal with 2:25 left at East Los Angeles College. Burroughs (2-6, 1-2) had blocked a 35-yard field-goal attempt in the first half. Andre Banks rushed for 111 yards in 16 carries for Burroughs. LISA GRALL
