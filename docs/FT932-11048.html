930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / Parliament and Politics: Major welcomes press proposals MR JOHN MAJOR, the prime minister, yesterday welcomed 'unreservedly' new measures from the newspaper industry to strengthen self-regulation. He told the Newspaper Society, which represents regional and local newspapers, that the proposals from the Press Standards Board of Finance, which co-ordinates self-regulation policy, represented a positive move that would take the debate forward. Mr Major also emphasised that he would be reluctant to introduce a statutory press complaints tribunal.
