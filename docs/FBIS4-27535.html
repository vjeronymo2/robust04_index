BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 31 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, sent thanks to the State External Economic Affairs Commission, the Radio and TV Broadcasting Committee of the DPRK, the Haeju city committee of the Workers' Party of Korea in South Hwanghae Province, the Chokdong senior middle school in Pyonggang County, Kangwon Province and many other units, officials, workers and students who had set examples in assisting the People's Army. The people demonstrate the might of singlehearted unity of the Army and the people around the party and the leader by assisting the People's Army well in the DPRK where the traditional trait of unity between the Army and the people is in full bloom.
