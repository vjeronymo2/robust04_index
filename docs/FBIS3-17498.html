Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Ministry press service reported that in his letter to the Azerbaijani Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov, Egyptian Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa said that Egypt resolutely condemns the occupation of the Azerbaijani lands, recognizes Azerbaijan's sovereignty and territorial integrity, and calls on foreign troops to withdraw from these lands in keeping with the relevant UN Security Council resolutions. The Egyptian foreign minister also said that his government decided to sent humanitarian assistance to Azerbaijan as a manifestation of the Egyptian people's support for and solidarity with the Azerbaijani people. The Cultural, Science, and Ecology Department of the Republic's Foreign Ministry informed the AZERTAC correspondent that humanitarian assistance is on the way and is expected to arrive in Baku in the coming days.
