BFN [Text] Shanghai, April 8 (XINHUA) -- Former U.S. Secretary of State Cyrus Vance left here today after winding up his three-day tour of the city. During his stay in Shanghai, Vance visited the Minhang Economic and Technological Development Zone, the New Pudong Area and the Shanghai Securities Exchange. Vance arrived here April 5 from Xian, capital of northwest China's Shaanxi Province.
