941013 FT 13 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Bulgin 77% ahead AF Bulgin, the electrical components manufacturer, reported a 77 per cent rise in interim pre-tax profits from Pounds 280,000 to Pounds 497,000. Turnover for the half year to July 31 was 15 per cent ahead, at Pounds 9.03m (Pounds 7.84m). Mr Ronald Bulgin, chairman, said that all parts of the group had performed well in what had been the most profitable half year for a number of years. Earnings per share were 1.26p (1p).
