Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, Feb 14 (AFP) -- Police killed three suspected Moslem militants and arrested a fourth in a shootout here Monday, police said. A gang of six militants was shadowed by police for several hours until they were cornered in the poor Sayidah Zaynab neighbourhood, a spokesman said. In the resulting clash three were killed, one was captured and two escaped, the official said. Arms and ammunition were found on the dead militants, police said. [passage omitted]
