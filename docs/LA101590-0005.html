October 15, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

ADMINISTRATION, CONGRESS WRANGLE OVER THE BUDGET

The breakdown of the budget negotiations and its consequences may be my fault.

As a non-voter, Congress does not know or care how I feel on any issue. Well, things are going to be different. It is never too late to use our "people" power to give notice to the government. I urge all eligible citizens to get off their duffs and vote. This is the only way to show politicians that they have to take their responsibility to the electorate seriously.

RAY MARRERO, San Diego
