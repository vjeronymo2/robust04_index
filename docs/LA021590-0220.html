February 15, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

STATE;

LOTTO WASHOUT, NOW $31 MILLION

No one picked all six numbers correctly in Wednesday night's Lotto 6/49 game, boosting the jackpot for this weekend's drawing to a projected $31 million.

The numbers drawn in Wednesday night's Lotto 6/49 game were 16, 18, 20, 33, 42 and 49 with the bonus number 8.

Twelve people selected five of six plus the bonus number, each winning $189,811. Another 346 gamblers picked five of six to each win $3,391.

Sales for Wednesday's game were $21.3 million.
