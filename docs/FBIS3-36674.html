Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Russia's ambassador to the United Nations, Yuliy Vorontsov, has proposed putting Sarajevo, the capital of Bosnia-Herzegovina, under control of a UN administration, instead of hitting air strikes at positions of the Bosnian Serbs around it. After a closed-door meeting of the Security Council, Yuliy Vorontsov said that under the Security Council's resolution, air strikes are possible only as a means of protecting the UN peacekeeping forces, but not as a means of punishing one of the warring Bosnian sides.
