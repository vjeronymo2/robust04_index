920407 FT 07 APR 92 / UK Company News: Chepstow R'course gallops ahead 35% The Chepstow Racecourse lifted pre-tax profits 35 per cent, from Pounds 47,445 to Pounds 63,873, in 1991. Turnover grew from Pounds 1.62m to Pounds 1.65m. After a sharply increased tax charge of Pounds 22,117 (Pounds 6,439) and grants no longer repayable at Pounds 93,098 (Pounds 97,352), earnings worked through at 8.43p (8.28p). It is proposed to maintain the dividend at 1p.
