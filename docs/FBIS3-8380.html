Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Kosova Democratic Alliance [LDK] Presidency has issued a communique condemning the Serbian police acts in Fushe-Kosova, which resulted in the killing of Faik Maloku and seriously wounding Xhevat Bajzaku. The LDK Presidency expresses its condemnation and profoundest indignation against the campaign of organized state crime and terror that is being waged against Albanians throughout Kosova.
