January 5, 1990, Friday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Not Just a Wedding Band Anymore: Donny Dulce & Fusion, the four-person band that's been rocking theatergoers at "Tony n' Tina's Wedding," will make its West Coast headline debut Jan. 22 at the Coconut Teaszer on the Sunset Strip. "Tony n' Tina's" cast members will be on hand -- in character, of course -- to lend their support. DANIEL CERONE
