940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / International Company News: Samsung Electronics doubles surplus Samsung Electronics, South Korea's largest electronics company, yesterday reported a 113 per cent increase in net profits to Won154.8bn (Dollars 191m) for 1993 as sales climbed by 32 per cent to Won8,155bn. The jump in earnings reflected strong export sales by its semiconductor division, with a 94 per cent rise in turnover to Won2,600bn. Samsung is the world's seventh biggest manufacturer of semiconductors and ranks first globally in the production of memory chips. Samsung Electronics also makes consumer electronics, telecommunications equipment and computers.
