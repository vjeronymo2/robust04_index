940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / International Company News: US healthcare group signs hospital deal Abbott Laboratories, the Illinois-based healthcare company, yesterday signed a Dollars 500m contract with SunHealth Alliance of Charlotte, North Carolina. The move adds momentum to the US trend of creating package deals for US health service suppliers. The agreement includes pharmaceuticals, diagnostics, drug delivery systems, intravenous solutions, nutritionals, medical-surgical products, critical care and anaesthesia products. The 280-hospital five-year deal is the latest in a series which usually involves big discounts for the purchasers in return for preferential buying from one supplier such as Abbott.
