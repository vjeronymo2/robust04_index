941116 FT 16 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Winding-up at Waterglade A winding up order was issued on November 9 against Waterglade International Holdings, the property development group, its directors announced yesterday. Liquidators will be appointed shortly to the subsidiaries. Shares in Waterglade were suspended on October 10. On October 19 the directors reported that a rescue plan was impossible, given the group's financial situation. Liabilities are Pounds 29m. The directors do not anticipate that any funds will be available for shareholders.
