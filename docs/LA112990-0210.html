SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: PASADENA;

SUBMARINE TOURS SET

The Navy's new submarine, the Pasadena, based in San Diego, will visit Long Beach Friday through Sunday . Officers will conduct a series of 45-minute tours of the boat.

The ship's officers and crew also will attend a reception in their honor Saturday at the Tournament of Roses house in Pasadena.
