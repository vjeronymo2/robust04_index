BFN [Text] An official spokesman has described the allegations against the president of Pakistan in a so-called fact sheet issued by Nawaz Muslim League as a forgery and a crude and disgraceful attempt to malign the head of state. The spokesman said the people of Pakistan were shocked by the wanton and irresponsible behavior of this defeated and discredited group of politicians to malign the head of state when he is abroad and is meeting world leaders. The spokesman said the callous attitude toward national interest is characteristic of this section of the opposition.
