920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / Canada's inflation rate slows Canada's inflation rate slowed to 1.1 per cent in June, its smallest year-on-year increase in 30 years and the lowest rate currently prevailing among the main industrial countries, Bernard Simon reports from Toronto. Canadian banks were yesterday expected to reduce their prime lending rate from 7 per cent to 6.75 per cent, down from a peak of 14.75 per cent in mid-1990.
