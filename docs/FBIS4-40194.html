BFN [Report from ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA-ITAR-TASS column: "Beneficial to Foreign Companies"] [Text] The question of the possible closure of the Chernobyl Nuclear Electric Power Station has been discussed increasingly often recently by the world community, not only at an environmental and social level but also at a political level. Circles close to the Russian Federation Ministry of Atomic Energy have stated that if such a step is taken it will have primarily political overtones. In the opinion of the experts, this will be beneficial first and foremost to the leading foreign nuclear power generation companies.
