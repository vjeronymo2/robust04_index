930112 FT 12 JAN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There were 12,340 packages for the day reports the Tea Brokers Association. There was very strong and widespread demand. Landed Assams met increased competition with prices 5 to 15p dearer. All medium and brighter liquoring African teas were actively supported and frequently showed advances of 10 to 20 pence. Offshore good general demand with Ceylons firm and Kenya price levels 10 to 15p higher. The highest price realised this week was 250p for a Rwanda pf. 1. Quotations:quality 220, good medium 180, medium 165, low medium 125.
