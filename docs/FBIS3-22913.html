Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The hostage drama at Truro House in Durban is over. Twelve people were arrested a short while ago after the hostages were released. The drama started early this afternoon when three House of Delegates employees were taken hostage by a group of seven (Bunela) residents at Truro House. They demanded that charges of trespassing against them be dropped. They had been charged with illegally occupying houses at Cato Manor near Durban. The suspects will appear in court tomorrow.
