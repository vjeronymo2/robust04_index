BFN [Text] Foreign secret services, primarily from Germany, the United States, Japan, and South Korea, are continuing to increase their activity in Irkutsk Oblast, according to (Valeriy Shestak), director of the regional Federal Counterintelligence Service [FSK] directorate. Last year the FSK directorate uncovered 10 secret service agents and 20 employees of foreign intelligence services among foreigners visiting the Angara region. The Eastern and Western secret services are particularly interested in progress in privatization and conversion in the area.
