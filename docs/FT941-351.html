940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / UK Company News: OIS Intl Inspection OIS International Inspection, which provides technical inspection services to the construction, petrochemical and power generation industries, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.21m on turnover of Pounds 46.7m for 1993. The company came to the market at the end of 1992 and there are no directly comparable figures. However, the businesses which became wholly owned subsidiaries at the time of flotation made pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.74m on turnover of Pounds 46.8m in 1992. Earnings per share were 5.4p basic and 5.1p fully diluted. A final dividend of 1.4p is proposed for a total of 2.1p.
