Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Diplomatic relations between the Czech Republic and Peru were further consolidated with the recent signature of two bilateral agreements. This was stated by Czech Republic Vice President Jan Kalvoda, who has been in Peru since 15 March on an official visit. One of the agreements signed will promote and guarantee investments, which will benefit financial exchange, especially for national development products. The other agreement eliminates the requirement for visas for diplomatic passports. During a news conference, the Czech vice president pointed out the trade exchange with Peru, which he described as important and balanced.
