940616 FT 16 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Wrexham Water Wrexham and East Denbighshire Water lifted pre-tax profits by 37 per cent, from Pounds 2.6m to Pounds 3.57m, in the year to March 31, on turnover 4 per cent higher at Pounds 10.7m. Earnings per Pounds 1 stock unit were 490p, against 399p or 345p after adjusting for a Pounds 265,000 surplus on the sale of fixed assets. The recommended final dividend is 141.45p for a total of 189.9p (134.9p). The company is calling an EGM in order to convert to plc status. This change follows similar decisions in the rest of the industry.
