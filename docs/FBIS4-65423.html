BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Spanish Defense Minister Julian Garcia Vargas who arrived in Ankara yesterday has started to hold official contacts. This morning, Vargas first visited National Defense Minister Mehmet Golhan. At the meeting, Golhan stressed that Armenia's occupation of Azerbaijan and the Serbs' occupation of Bosnia-Herzegovina are unacceptable. [Begin Golhan recording] Mr. Minister, in such a region in the world, Turkey is the only stable and powerful country that respects all kinds of human rights, that has a liberal economy, and that implements a democratic order. It is important not only for Turkey's security, but for the security of the European continent as well for Turkey to remain an element of stability of this region. [end recording] Vargas then visited Chief of the General Staff General Dogan Gures.
