920224 FT 24 FEB 92 / Construction Contracts: Resort hotel in Oman WIMPEY ALAWI, part of the international construction group George Wimpey, has secured a Pounds 4.5m contract to build a luxury resort hotel in Sohar. The project, designed to reflect traditional Omani architecture, must be completed before November 1 when the hotel plays host to senior national dignitaries. Wimpey Alawi's work inolves the construction of a main reception building, which will house a total of five exclusive guest suites including a presidential type suite. A futher eight accommodation buildings will be built to provide facilities for a total of 60 guests. The contract includes construction of a swimming pool, service compound and living quarters for hotel staff.
