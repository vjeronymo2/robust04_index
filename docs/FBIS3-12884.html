Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday, in Yemen, four political opposition parties called for the use of civil disobedience to pressure both northern and southern leaders whose differences threaten the unity of the country. The call came in a statement signed by the National Opposition Grouping [al-takattul al-watani lil mu'aradah], the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party, the National Forces Federation [ittihad al-quwa al-wataniyah), and the Strugglers of the Yemeni Revolution [Munadili al-thawrah al-yamaniyah. The statement calls on the Armed Forces to disobey orders in order to prevent internecine fighting.
