Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Lebanese question moved to Syria yesterday, where Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri met with Syrian Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam over an Iftar banquet held in al-Hariri's honor. According to unconfirmed reports from Damascus, the prime minister, who stayed the night in Syria, may meet with President Hafiz al-Asad before returning to Beirut today.
