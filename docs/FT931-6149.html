930303 FT 03 MAR 93 / NBC chief quits NBC News yesterday said its president, Mr Michael Gartner, would resign, in an attempt to limit damage to the news division of the television network from the rigging of a broadcast test of a General Motors truck last year, Karen Zagor writes. An NBC programme aired pictures of a blazing GM truck without saying it had attached small incendiary devices to the truck to make it burst into flames. It later apologised to GM.
