BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 17 (KCNA) -- The national committee for inheritance of the spirit of the May 18 Kwangju popular uprising and probe into the truth in South Korea published a statement on May 14, demanding probe into the truth behind the Kwangju massacre in May 1980, according to a radio report from Seoul. The committee urged the Kim Yong-sam group to amend the "special law" on the Kwangju incident at an early date and make a strict investigation into the principal culprits of the Kwangju massacre.
