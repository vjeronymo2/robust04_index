BFN [Excerpt] Warsaw, 25 June--The Sejm passed a law on redenomination of the zloty today. With effect from 1 January 1995, "old zlotys" will be gradually replaced by new notes and coins. The new zloty will be divided into 100 groszy, and its value will be the equivalent of the present 10,000 zlotys. The law was passed with 306 votes for, one against, and one abstention.
