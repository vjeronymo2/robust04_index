BFN [Text] Cairo, 15 Apr (MENA)--A crude homemade device exploded tonight in front of building No. 3 in Madinat al-Ikha' in [Cairo's southern] neighborhood of al-Ma'adi. There were no damages, injuries, or casualties. This was stated by an Interior Ministry security source which said that the explosion happened at 1830 today and that the Cairo Security Directorate officials and the criminal laboratory experts immediately went to the site. It added that the search for the culprits is under way and that the public prosecution office has been notified so as to start its investigation.
