931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / Observer: Two steps up Concerning Gerald Corbett, the man who moves from the finance director slot at Redland to the same position chez GrandMet, the market is agreed on one point - that he is extraordinarily ambitious. But, while news of his departure took a bit off the Redland share price, GrandMet's share price wobbled more yesterday. Both markdowns can't be right and the super-confident Corbett could not help sounding mildly unsettled. The difficulty seems to be establishing when an accomplished finance director becomes a numbers man who is simply too clever by half. While the food and drinks conglomerate has a high level of disclosure, the complex figures still need a lot of explaining. But then, for an upwardly mobile fluent communicator, surely that is half the fun?
