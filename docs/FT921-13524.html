920121 FT 21 JAN 92 / Plans for French TV news channel THREE French television stations - TFI, Canal Plus and M6 - are considering launching a news channel to replace La Cinq, the French TV company which filed for bankruptcy three weeks ago, Alice Rawsthorn writes from Paris. The proposed news channel, to be staffed by journalists from La Cinq, would provide a French-language news service on international affairs to be broadcast in France and other French-speaking countries. The announcement comes at a critical time for the French government. Ministers are anxious to avoid a confrontation with Mr Silvio Berlusconi, the Italian media mogul who was one of the principal shareholders in La Cinq and is now mooting plans for its rescue.
