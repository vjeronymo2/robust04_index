940414 FT 14 APR 94 / Teleworking gains popularity slowly Teleworking - working from home using computing or telecommunications equipment - remains a minority activity but is gaining in popularity, Incomes Data Services, the pay and employment analysts, said. In a report IDS said that the consensus view is that between 5 per cent and 15 per cent of organisations employ staff who spend at least half of their working time at home.
