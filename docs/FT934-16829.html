931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / Hungarian PM on sick leave Prime Minister Jozsef Antall of Hungary, eastern Europe's longest serving leader, is taking sick leave for four weeks from tomorrow in order to receive treatment for cancer in Germany, Nicholas Denton reports from Budapest. Yesterday's announcement follows persistent speculation about Mr Antall's health, the leadership of the Hungarian Democratic Forum and the conservative coalition government it dominates. Mr Antall, 61, suffers from non-Hodgkin lymphoma, a curable cancer.
