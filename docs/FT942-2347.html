940620 FT 20 JUN 94 / Arts: New York Lyceum Sir Ian McKellen premieres his one-man show, Ian McKellen: 'A Knight Out at the Lyceum' on Broadway tomorrow. The show is described as 'a mixture of stand-up, classical acting and gay activism.' It is one of over 50 events in the 10-day Cultural Festival of Gay Games IV, which mark the 25th anniversary of the 1969 Stonewall riots in New York City.
