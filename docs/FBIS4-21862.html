BFN [Text] Tegucigalpa, 5 Apr (ACAN-EFE) -- The Honduran Armed Forces thwarted an alleged plan which would reportedly be executed by three Nicaraguan citizens to assassinate President Carlos Roberto Reina. Army Spokesman Colonel Napoleon Santos announced today military intelligence learned about the plan after Nicaraguan citizens Luis Enrique Quintero, Santiago Rivas, and Roger Arguello entered the country on 7 March. According to the high-ranking military officer, who said Enrique Quintero was a member of the intelligence department of the Sandinist People's Army (EPS), the three Nicaraguan citizens had planned to burn a paint factory and kidnap three businessmen.
