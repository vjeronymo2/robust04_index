940212 FT 12 FEB 94 / UK Company News: GTI makes fourth quarter progress GTI Corporation, the 58 per cent-owned US subsidiary of Telemetrix, turned in fourth quarter net income of Dollars 3.31m (Pounds 2.26m) against Dollars 3.14m, raising the 1993 total by 46 per cent from Dollars 9.03m to Dollars 13.2m. Earnings per share for the quarter were unchanged at 32 cents, making Dollars 1.32 (93 cents) for the year - a 42 per cent rise. Total sales reached Dollars 127.6m (Dollars 104.8m) with Dollars 30.6m (Dollars 29.4m) coming in the final period.
