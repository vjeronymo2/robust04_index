January 13, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

KINGDOM DAY PARADE ROUTE

The 1990 Kingdom Day Parade will be held Monday to commemorate the Jan. 15 birthday of slain civil rights leader Martin Luther King Jr.

Martin Luther King Jr. Boulevard will be closed to traffic and parking.

Parade will begin at 11 a.m.

Starting point is at Crenshaw and Martin Luther King Jr. boulevards.

Reviewing stand for judges will be at Martin Luther King Jr. Blvd. near McClung Drive.

Termination point will be at the Los Angeles Coliseum at Menlo and 39th streets.

Registration and parade assignments will be at Martin Luther King Jr. Boulevard and Marlton Avenue.

Grand marshal will be Ruth Washington, publisher of the Los Angeles Sentinel.

More than 4,000 people are expected to participate.
