Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By 'Awdah 'Awdah] [Excerpt] A senior Palestinian diplomatic source in Amman has confirmed to AL-RA'Y that Faruq Qaddumi, alias Abu-al-Lutf, head of the PLO Political Department, will arrive in Amman tomorrow morning from the Egyptian capital. The source said it is not known yet who will accompany Qaddumi but the delegation is expected to include several PLO Executive Committee members, including Sulayman al-Najjab, Yasir 'Amr, and others. The source said the talks Qaddumi will have with senior Jordanian officials are extremely important, pointing out that His Majesty King Husayn and President 'Arafat agreed last month to hold such high-level meetings in Amman. The Palestinian source stressed the PLO's concern about having clear and frank relations with the Jordanian Government to serve the higher interests of Jordan and Palestine. [passage omitted]
