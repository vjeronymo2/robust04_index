931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / International Company News: Samsung buys European TV parts producer Samsung has acquired a former east German manufacturer of glass for colour TV picture tubes as part of the South Korean conglomerate's efforts to expand television production facilities in Europe. Samsung Corning will take over Fernsehglas Tschernitz (FGT) from Treuhand, the privatisation agency for east German companies. Samsung is expected to pay Dollars 30m for the acquisition and an expansion of facilities, but a final price will not be determined until early next year when a valuation of FGT assets is completed.
