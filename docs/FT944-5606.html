941202 FT 02 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Borthwicks dips The Pounds 212,000 cost of developing its Hong Kong operation caused pre-tax profits of Borthwicks, the natural flavours concern, to dip from Pounds 1.13m to Pounds 924,000 in the six months to October 1. Sales were unchanged at Pounds 14.9m, though there was a 2.6 per cent rise excluding last year's Pounds 362,000 from discontinued businesses. Earnings per share were down from 1.6p to 1.3p, but the interim dividend has been held at 0.5p.
