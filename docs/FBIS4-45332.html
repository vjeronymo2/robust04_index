Antidrug Efforts LD3105103194 Almaty Kazakh Radio Network in Kazakh 0600 GMT 31 May 94 LD3105103194 Almaty Kazakh Radio Network Kazakh BFN [Text] A meeting was held in the settlement of Georgiyevka in Zhambyl Oblast between Kazakh Interior Minister Vladimir Shumov and Kyrgyz Interior Minister Abdibek Sutalinov. They discussed issues related to a quick operational response against the criminal world, coordination of joint efforts, and the possibility of combatting drug smuggling in the two republics. As result of the meeting, an agreement on cooperation in combatting crime was signed between the Kazakh and Kyrgyz Interior Ministries.
