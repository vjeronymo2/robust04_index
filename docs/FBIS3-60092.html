Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Today" -- BTA lead] [Excerpt] Sofia, January 13 (BTA) -- Italian nationals Chiro Francesco Saulo from Cetraro and Francesco Piccone from Trino were caught in an attempt to smuggle 7.44 kg of heroin at the ferry checkpoint on the border with Romania. The drug was hidden in the tank of their Rover Austin, Vasko Vasilev, deputy chief officer at the Vidin customs, told BTA. The Italians were detained and the car confiscated. This is the seventh drugs seizure in Vidin since customs officers seized 40 kilos of narcotics in late 1992. So far Vidin officers have seized 150 kilos of drugs. [passage omitted]
