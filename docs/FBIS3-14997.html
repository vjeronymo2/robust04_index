Ryabchikov: "Parties Are Ready To Help the President"] [Text] On 19 February, Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov met with leaders of officially registered parties. During their meeting, participants detailed their stances, the attitude toward the program of the president, changes in the Republic, and what assistance they require. The leaders of the parties, including the Party of Crimean Economic Revival and the Ukrainian parties, declared their readiness to promote the implementation of economic reform. The meeting was held in a constructive atmosphere, stated Agrarian Party Deputy Chairman Mikhail Frolov. We favor social justice for workers of the agricultural sector, the issuing of additional benefits which are necessary for the development of the agricultural sector. In this respect, the stance of the president coincides with ours. We came with a package of proposals, said Yefim Fiks, chairman of the Party of Social Guarantees, and the president suggested that edicts be drafted by us on the basis of our proposals. Participants in the meeting spoke of the need to adopt a joint statement of all the political forces of the Republic in support of the president's actions. When Sergey Nikulin, presidential adviser for relations with parties and public organizations, commented on this meeting, he noted that Yuriy Mechkov asked all the participants to forward proposals for candidates to vacancies in all state bodies. In the opinion of Nikulin, the consolidation will make it possible to create a powerful bloc supporting the course of the president. This will be a new kind of legislature, he stressed.
