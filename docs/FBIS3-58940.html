Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] The Federal Navy intends to send ships to Somalia in mid-February to support the withdrawal of German blue helmet soldiers. According to information received by the Bonn studio of Second German Television, ZDF, this is intended to be a precautionary measure for the safety of the Bundeswehr units. It was said that the troops would board the ships in Mogadishu, while the equipment that was taken to Beledweyne will return to Germany on civilian freighters.
