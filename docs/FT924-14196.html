921012 FT 12 OCT 92 / Inkatha members demonstrate in workers' hostel in Tokhoza Members of the Zulu-based Inkatha Freedom party make a show of arms inside the gates of a workers' hostel in Tokhoza, South Africa, as an African National Congress march passed the hostel en route to a rally yesterday. Soon after, Prince Mhlambi, a regional leader of Nelson Mandela's ANC, and three youths were killed nearby when gunmen fired on their car, Reuter reports
