BFN [Text] The higher committee for receiving donations met yesterday under Shaykh 'Abd-al-Majid al-Zindani, Presidential Council member and the committee's chairman. The committee members discussed a number of issues on the committee's agenda which aim at activating the higher committee's work and expanding its activity outside the homeland in a bid to allow our expatriate citizens to join in the honor of participating in the popular move to support the efforts to defend unity and constitutional legitimacy. In order to achieve that goal, the higher committee for receiving donations set up a committee to contact the brother expatriates in sisterly and friendly states. In a related development, Brother Judge Hamid al-Hitar, member of the higher committee for receiving donations and head of the health care committee, said in statements to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', that a medical convoy yesterday left for the governorates of al-Bayda' and Abyan, with a shipment of medicines and supplies needed by the hospitals in the two governorates to enable them to offer medical services and full health care.
