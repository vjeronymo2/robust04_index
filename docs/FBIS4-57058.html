BFN [Text] Agriculture and Fishing Minister Pedro Saravia has signed technical and cooperation agreements in the PRC. Saravia held working meetings with the Chinese agriculture vice minister, the head of the veterinary department, and the general director of the Chinese University of Agricultural Sciences. The official made progress in the project to exchange special technological missions, strengthened links with wool companies, and discussed the export of various industrial products. Saravia traveled to Mongolia and to Shanghai, where he visited agricultural stations. On 16 May Minister Saravia will inaugurate the second ordinary session of [words indistinct] in Paris.
