930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Laser-Scan back to profitability A strong performance in the second half returned Laser-Scan Holdings to profitability in 1992. Pre-tax profit for the year was Pounds 80,000, after losses of Pounds 408,000 in the opening half and Pounds 83,000 for the whole of 1991. Earnings per share worked through at 0.5p (losses 0.5p).
