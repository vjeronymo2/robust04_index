941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / International Company News: Saudi groups improve Two of Saudi Arabia's cement companies have reported higher profits for the first nine months of this year on the back of a construction spree following the Gulf war in 1991, Reuter reports from Manama. The Southern Province Cement Company reported a 5.6 per cent rise in net profit to SR218m (Dollars 58.1m) up from SR206.4m in the corresponding 1993 period. The Arabian Cement Company said its net profit for the first nine months rose to SR84.3m from SR77.3m a year earlier.
