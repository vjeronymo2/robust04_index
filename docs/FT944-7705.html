941123 FT 23 NOV 94 / International Company News: Tobacco company ahead Scandinavian Tobacco Company, in which BAT of the UK holds a 32.1 per cent stake, reported net profits of DKr937m (Dollars 154m) for the year ending June 30 1994, up 5 per cent on the previous year's DKr892m, writes Hilary Barnes in Copenhagen.
