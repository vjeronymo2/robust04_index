Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Ukrainian Trade Union Presidium has approved the organization's platform for the upcoming legislative elections. The trade unions advocate civilized market reform carried out under the slogan: Humane life for the man of labor. The platform, in particular, stipulates the formation of a reliable legislative footing for an efficient social system for the people, a radical improvement of working conditions and living standards, the introduction of flexible work schedules, a shorter work week, and an increase in social payments.
