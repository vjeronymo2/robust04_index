May 17, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

NO WINNER -- LOTTO AT $17 MILLION

The $10.4-million jackpot in the California Lottery Lotto 6/49 game went unclaimed Wednesday night and will roll over to an estimated $17 million for Saturday night's drawing, Lotto officials said.

The winning numbers chosen in the 6/49 game were 48, 35, 41, 8, 18, 9, and the bonus number, 13.

There were six tickets with five of six winning numbers and the bonus number. They are worth $254,065 each and split a $1.5-million prize pool.

There were 263 tickets with five of six numbers, and they win $2,986 each. The tickets divided a prize pool of $785,000.
