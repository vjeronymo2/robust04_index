941111 FT 11 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Lofs advances Despite continuing difficult trading in tanker markets, London & Overseas Freighters reported pre-tax profits of Dollars 3.76m (Pounds 2.29m) for the six months to September 30, against Dollars 1.56m, helped by an advance in the second quarter from Dollars 308,000 to Dollars 2.08m. Half-year turnover for the Bermuda-based but London listed shipping company was Dollars 16.4m (Dollars 12.7m) with second quarter figures of Dollars 8.43m (Dollars 7.46m). Earnings per share were 5 cents (6.4 cents) and a second interim of 0.25 cents makes 0.5 cents to date.
