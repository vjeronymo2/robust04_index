Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] President 'Ezer Weizman this morning toured Qiryat Arba' and met the heads of its council. The president explained to them the gravity of the incident in the Cave of Patriarchs, and said that it stands in contradiction to every Jewish concept as well as the security doctrines as the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] teaches them. Earlier, the president toured Hebron. He met the mayor and spoke to passersby. The Hebron mayor thanked President Weizman for his visit, and asked the Hebron massacre be thoroughly investigated, and that conclusions be drawn.
