Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today President Zhelyu Zhelev held a working meeting that was attended by Prime Minister Lyuben Berov, Foreign Minister Stanislav Daskalov, Defense Minister Valentin Aleksandrov, and Interior Minister Viktor Mikhaylov; General Lyuben Petrov, chief of the Bulgarian Army General Staff; Nikolay Slatinski, chairman of the Parliamentary National Security Commission; and Yunal Lyutfi, interim chairman of the Parliamentary Foreign Policy Commission. Here is what the president's spokesman Valentin Stoyanov had to say about the results of the meeting: [Begin Stoyanov recording] The participants in the conference discussed the theses for the president's meeting tomorrow with Ms. Albright, the special envoy of the U.S. President, namely that through the Foreign Ministry declaration issued yesterday, Bulgaria has expressed its approval of the Partnership for Peace initiative; that in the very near future our country will accept the invitation to sign a framework Partnership for Peace agreement; and that the Council of Ministers will form a working group to draft proposals for Bulgaria's specific participation in the partnership. (end recording)
