930603 FT 03 JUN 93 / Worried Tories to meet Clarke LEADERS OF the Conservative backbench finance committee will have their first meeting with Mr Kenneth Clarke, the new chancellor, soon after the Commons re-assembles next week. Pressures on Mr Clarke to accord a bigger role to tax increases in reducing the Pounds 50bn public sector borrowing requirement than those announced by Mr Norman Lamont, his predecessor, have alarmed some Tory MPs.
