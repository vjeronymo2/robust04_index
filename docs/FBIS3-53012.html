Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The minutes of an agreement for economic and trade integration between the Great Jamahiriyah and fraternal Sudan were signed in Tripoli this morning. The agreement was signed by the brother secretary of the General People's Committee for Planning, Trade and Treasury and brother 'Abd-al-Rahim Muhammad Husayn, the interior minister of fraternal Sudan. [passage omitted] The minutes point out the need to bolster the ties of integration in all economic and commercial spheres between the Great Jamahiriyah and fraternal Sudan in implementation of the recommendations which were agreed upon at the second meeting of the ministerial committee for integration between the Great Jamahiriyah and fraternal Sudan.
