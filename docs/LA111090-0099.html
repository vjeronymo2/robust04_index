November 10, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

DESERT-INYO LEAGUE

Kern Valley 27, Paraclete 0 -- Paraclete's Jorge Ordaz surpassed the 1,000-yard rushing mark for the season, but the Spirits lost their seventh consecutive game.

NONLEAGUE

Santa Clara 21, Righetti 14 -- Danny Lopez intercepted a pass in the Santa Clara end zone with 27 seconds left to play to preserve the victory in Santa Maria.

Lopez also caught a 58-yard touchdown pass from Christopher Macias late in the second quarter. CRAIG ERNST
