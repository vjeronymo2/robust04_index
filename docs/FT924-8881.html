921109 FT 09 NOV 92 / Construction Contracts: Arms training centre TRAFALGAR HOUSE and TAYLOR WOODROW have been awarded the master plan study for the construction of a combat arms training centre by the Malaysian Government. The contract is for the development of a plan which will provide accommodation, training and support facilities on a 38,000 acre site at GEMAS, astride Negeri Sembilan and Johore, some 170 kilometres south of Kuala Lumpur.
