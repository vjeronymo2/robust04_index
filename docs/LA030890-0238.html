March 8, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

MCFERRIN TO CONDUCT SYMPHONY

Bobby McFerrin usually makes his own sound effects but on Sunday he will have the San Francisco Symphony to back him up. The Grammy-winning singer will conduct Beethoven's Symphony No. 7 and also will perform his "headTales," a composition for voice and cello, with the celebrated cellist Yo-Yo Ma. The program also features McFerrin's new 10-voice a cappella ensemble, Voicestra.
