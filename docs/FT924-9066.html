921107 FT 07 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Arthur Shaw makes inroads into losses ARTHUR SHAW, which serves the building industry, continued in loss for the half year ended October 4, albeit at a considerably reduced rate, and is passing the interim dividend. After exceptional charges, the pre-tax loss was Pounds 124,000 compared with a deficit of Pounds 523,000 in the preceding six months and a profit of Pounds 113,000 in the opening half of 1991. Losses per share came to 1.03p. Earnings last time were 0.86p and an interim dividend of 1.3p was paid - but no final. Mr Gordon Pearson, chairman, said the cost savings that had been made fell predominantly in the second half. Turnover in the half year was Pounds 8.05m (Pounds 7.79m) and the operating profit came to Pounds 99,000 (Pounds 202,000).
