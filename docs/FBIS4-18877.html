BFN [Excerpt] Bonn (DPA) -- Ukraine may interrupt the disarmament of its nuclear weapons. This was indicated by Ukrainian Defense Minister Vitaliy Radetsky in a talk with journalists during his visit to the Defense Ministry in Bonn on Monday [11 April]. He pointed out that, so far, Russia has not fulfilled its promise of delivering nuclear fuel rods for Ukrainian power stations. In return, Kiev had promised nuclear disarmament. If Moscow does not provide the nuclear fuel, everything must be renegotiated, Radetsky stressed. [passage omitted]
