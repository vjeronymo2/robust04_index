931216 FT 16 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Polar Pre-tax profits of Polar, the USM-quoted designer and distributor of electronic components, rose 44 per cent from Pounds 902,000 to Pounds 1.31m in the year to October 1. Turnover was ahead at Pounds 21.2m (Pounds 17.4m). Earnings per share amounted to 10.7p (7.3p) and the final dividend is increased to 2.85p (2.5p), making a total of 4.85p (4.5p).
