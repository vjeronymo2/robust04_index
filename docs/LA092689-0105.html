September 26, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

SOUTH COAST REPERTORY TO HOLD SCRIPT CONTEST

Costa Mesa's South Coast Repertory is seeking unproduced new scripts for its second annual California Playwrights Competition and Play Festival next spring.

The contest, open to California residents, has a top prize of $5,000 and runner-up prizes of $3,000 and $2,000. The winning script may also be chosen for production on SCR's Second Stage next May.

Deadline for script submissions is Nov. 15. Writers need to include a cast list, a 300-word play synopsis and a brief biography. Large-scale musicals, children's plays, translations and foreign-language plays will not be considered. Winners will be announced Jan. 30.
