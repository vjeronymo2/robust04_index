BFN ["Today" -- BTA lead] [Excerpts] Sofia, April 5 (BTA) -- [passage omitted] The rights of Bulgarians in Aegean Macedonia and Thrace in Greece are still being violated, Stoyan Boyadzhiev, chairman of the extraparliamentary Internal Macedonian Revolutionary Organization-Union of Macedonian Societies, told a press conference. To support his claim, he circulated a Bulgarian translation of a Greek document, entitled "Instructions for the Assimilation of Macedonian Bulgarians." It was issued by the Greek State Security Service with the Ministry of Public Order and signed by Maj. Gen. Dimitrios Kapelaris, according to Boyadzhiev. The document recommends security services to ban the speaking of local dialects.
