March 28, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

CROWN CORK & SEAL TO BUY CANNING UNITS

Crown Cork & Seal Co. has agreed to buy the food and beverage metal canning units of Continental Can Co. in a purchase that will make Crown the nation's largest maker of metal packaging.

Crown, based in Philadelphia, said it would pay $336 million for the two divisions, which have more than 30 U.S. plants and employ 4,000 workers.
