October 5, 1989, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Former President Reagan has narrated a half-hour documentary called "The Voice of Hope" about Project Hope's programs around the world. The film, shot on location in China, Swaziland, Brazil and Grenada, tells about the nonprofit organization's dedication to improving the health of the world's poor. The film will be released in late October to cable, network affiliates and civic organizations around the United States. Reagan showed he was an old pro at narration -- it took him only one read-through. ALEENE MacMINN
