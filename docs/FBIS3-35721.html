Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Minsk, 28 Jan (BELINFORM-TASS) -- Speaking about his attitude to the current developments in the Belarusian Parliament, Vyacheslav Kebich, the chairman of the Belarusian Council of Ministers has said that in accordance with the results of the vote of confidence in the prime minister, he has a full moral right to carry out a drastic government reshuffle, which he in fact intends to do.
