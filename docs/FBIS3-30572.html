Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Zagreb, Feb 7 (TANJUG) -- Croatian President Franjo Tudjman and Bosnian Moslem leader Alija Izetbegovic will not meet in Rome in the middle of the week as earlier announced. TANJUG has learned in Zagreb that the two sides have agreed that Bosnian Moslem Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic and Croatian Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Mate Granic meet instead in Geneva on February 9. It is only if the Silajdzic-Granic talks produce some results that a decision on a Tudjman-Izetbegovic meeting will be taken. The venue and date of the meeting would be subsequently set.
