931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Intl Comm PSB Group, the directing marketing company, has acquired a further 500,000 shares in International Communication and Data. The seller was Mr G Thain, a founder of ICD, and Checksolo. They also sold warrants to PSB for a further 931,392 shares. PSB said it intends to requisition an EGM to replace three ICB directors with three of its own nominees and to seek control of ICD.
