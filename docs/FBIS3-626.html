Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Progress is still being registered at the Lusaka peace talks. The National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA] and the government continue to discuss national reconciliation. A plenary meeting was held today and there is even talk of progress, but the information blackout imposed by mediator Alioune Blondin Beye continues to be respected. It is known, however, that the parties discussed aspects of specific principles of national reconciliation.
