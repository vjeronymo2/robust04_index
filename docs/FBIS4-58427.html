BFN [Text] Cairo, 4 May (MENA)--Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat refused to sign the map attached to the self-rule agreement delineating the area of Jericho. When Israeli Prime Minister Rabin noticed that President 'Arafat did not sign, he consulted with Foreign Minister Peres, who in turn consulted with President 'Arafat. Rabin then refused to sign the map.
