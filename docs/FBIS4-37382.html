BFN [Text] Kuwait, 18 Jun (KUNA) -- The Kuwaiti and French Air Forces began joint exercises here today, code-named "Desert Mirage". These are the first exercises to begin from Ahmad al-Jabir air base since it was rebuilt. French Mirage 2000 aircraft, which arrived in Kuwait yesterday, are taking part in the two-day exercises. These fall in the framework of a joint defense cooperation agreement signed between Kuwait and France, which is aimed at strengthening military cooperation between the two countries, raising the fighting capability, and increasing the military competence of the Kuwaiti forces.
