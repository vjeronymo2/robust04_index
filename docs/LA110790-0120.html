November 7, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

A Tree Grows on Cable TV: The Discovery Channel has formed a partnership with Global ReLeaf, an educational campaign by the American Forestry Assn.

Starting this week, by dialing (900) 740-TREE viewers can have a tree planted on public lands designated by the association. The call will cost $5, which is tax-deductible. The organization is leading a drive to plant 100 million trees across the country by 1992. CLAUDIA PUIG
