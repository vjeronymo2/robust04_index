BFN [Text] Hangzhou, April 25 (XINHUA) -- A modern mobile telephone production line started operating early this month in Hangzhou in east China's Zhejiang Province. It is expected to produce the world's up-to-date 9900X mobile phones for this country's large telecommunications market. With an annual production capacity of 400,000 mini mobile phones, the production line is another important landmark for Hangzhou Telecommunications Equipment Factory, which last April put into operation an 8900X production line, using technology from Motorola, the U.S. blue-chip company. According to sources from the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, China has registered 480,000 subscribers of mobile telephones, 2.67 times the total figure for all the previous years.
