920505 FT 05 MAY 92 / UK Company News: O&Y to reveal asset sales this week OLYMPIA & York Developments, the beleaguered international developer, said it plans to announce the sale of assets later this week as part of its debt restructuring programme, AP-DJ reports from Toronto. The company said it continues to seek additional cash from its bankers as part of the restructuring. 'O&Y also is pursuing other alternatives to address its liquidity problems, including the sale of assets, some of which will be announced later this week,' the company said. An O&Y spokesman declined to elaborate. The company also said it missed making an interest payment of about CDollars 17m (USDollars 14.2m) due yesterday. The payment was to be made to holders of its Series 1 and 2 bonds, secured by its First Canadian Place office tower in Toronto. The bonds have a principal value of CDollars 325m.
