BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast: Announcer-read report over video of Maritime Kray arms depot explosion aftermath] [Text] The scale of the damage resulting from the explosion at the Pacific Fleet's aviation depot has become known today. Maritime Kray Governor Yevgeniy Nazdratenko held a briefing for journalists. According to his words, more than 1,500 tonnes of high explosive bombs, missiles of various classes, and anti-infantry mines and projectiles went up in the air over several hours. A number of projectiles and missiles fell on Bolshoy Kamen which is located many kilometers from the scene of the explosion. Nazdratenko said that sapper units from the entire Far East are being summoned to Maritime Kray to comb through the area and defuse munitions which failed to explode.
