BFN [Text] Daugavpils, April 12, LETA -- From April 15 the Consulate of the Russian Federation is due to begin performing its duties in the town of Daugavpils, reported the Consul of the Russian Federation in Daugavpils, Gennadiy Kaznacheev. He also said that the officials of the consulate will provide the entire complex of its services. The consul will receive residents personally jointly with his aid. During the coming days technical workers are expected to come from Russia to assist.
