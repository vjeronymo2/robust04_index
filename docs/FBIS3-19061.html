Language: Finnish Article Type:BFN [Text] Finland will send more peacekeepers to the former Yugoslavia. The Defense Ministry reports that a group of 39 guard soldiers will travel to Pleso near Zagreb, the capital of Croatia, in just under a week. The Finns will start guarding the Pleso base and airport. So far, the Nordic peacekeepers in The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia [FYROM] have guarded Pleso intermittently. Finland already has 230 peacekeepers in the area of the former Yugoslavia, in FYROM.
