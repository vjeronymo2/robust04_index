BFN [Text] An official spokesman for the Central Committee of the Palestine National Liberation Movement, Fatah [dissident], has asserted that statements made by 'Abd-al-Razzaq al-Majayizah, member of the so-called committee negotiating Palestinian security and one of 'Arafat's supporters, on disarming our people's masses expose the mission and role of the self-rule police. He said that 'Arafat's police are doing what the Zionist enemy has failed to do over the past years to harm our people's steadfastness and resistance. The spokesman urged the sons of our people and their active forces to be vigilant toward such statements and their dimensions.
