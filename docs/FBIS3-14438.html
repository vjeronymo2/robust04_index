Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The first cabinet meeting of the new [Iranian] year was held this afternoon under the chairmanship of President Hashemi-Rafsanjani. At the start of the meeting, President Hashemi-Rafsanjani conveyed his new year greetings to all his colleagues and government officials, and he described the new year as a time for completing the first [five-year] plan and as a time for hard work and the development of the country. The president continued by referring to the new year message issued by the eminent leader [Khamene'i] and he stressed that the four principles of production, thrift, a working conscience, and working discipline must be diligently pursued as the fundamental requirements for the improvement of society and the advancement of the country. The meeting continued with an examination of the executive regulations of the import/export law, and 29 of its articles were approved. It was also announced in today's cabinet session that, in view of the time when the new moon marking the start of the month of Shavval was sighted, Wednesday, 17 Farvardin [6 April], coinciding with the 25th of Shavval, will be a public holiday in commemoration of the anniversary of the martyrdom of his holiness Imam Ja'far Sadiq, peace be upon him.
