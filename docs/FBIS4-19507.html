BFN [Unattributed article: "More Than 140 Caught in Northern Anti-Mafia Operation"] [Text] (ANSA) Milan, April 18 -- More than 140 people were arrested in pre-dawn raids around the northern city of Milan and in several southern regions in a police operation code-named Scorched Earth, aimed against organized crime groups specializing in arms and drug trafficking, robbery and fencing stolen goods, police announced Monday [18 April]. Police carried out more than 300 searches and confiscated an unspecified number of weapons, drugs and ammunition in the raids, which were a follow-up to Operation North-South which a few months ago struck at the drug and arms pipeline between south and north. Most of the arrests, police said, were made in the northern region of Lombardy and in the poorer outskirts of Milan, where drug dealers are most active.
