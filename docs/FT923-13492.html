920713 FT 13 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Manweb chairman's salary jumps The total remuneration received by Mr Bryan Weston, chairman of Manweb, the Chester-based regional electricity company, jumped by 37 per cent from Pounds 156,000 to Pounds 214,000 in the year to March 31 1992. The annual report and accounts show that his salary rose by 21 per cent to Pounds 175,000 (Pounds 145,000). In addition, there was a performance-related bonus of Pounds 32,000 (Pounds 8,000) and benefits in kind worth Pounds 7,000 (Pounds 3,000). The figure excludes pension contributions. Manweb's pre-tax profits for the year rose by 61 per cent to Pounds 94.7m (Pounds 58.9m). Mr Weston is the highest paid director.
