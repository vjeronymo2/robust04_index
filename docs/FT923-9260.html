920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / Dounreay nuclear reactor to be shut THE government will close its prototype fast nuclear reactor at Dounreay, Caithness, in March 1994, in spite of a plea from the nuclear industry to save the research project there. The Nuclear Utilities Chairmen's Group, which comprises the chairmen of the four main nuclear companies, had argued that Dounreay was generating electricity worth more than Pounds 15m a year.
