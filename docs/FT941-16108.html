940112 FT 12 JAN 94 / Chief of arts body on fraud charge The head of the govern-ment-funded Business Sponsorship Incentive Scheme for the arts has been charged with obtaining Pounds 150,000 by deception, police said yesterday. Mr Nicholas Wood, 34, of Chiswick, west London, will appear at Southwark Crown Court in London on January 19, Scotland Yard said. Fraud squad officers began investigating the alleged loss of the money, thought to be cash from the national heritage department, last summer. The scheme is run by the charitable Association for Business Sponsorship of the Arts to attract donations from corporate sponsors.
