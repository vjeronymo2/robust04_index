920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / Observer: Share alike 'Sharing knowledge] The key to the future success of commerce and industry - everywhere' trumpets Siemens from the pages of yesterday's Wall Street Journal. Knowledge-sharing happens to be a bit of a sore point with the electrical and electronics group, now you mention it. While firmly denying that they have done anything improper, the Germans have just agreed to pay a Minneapolis-based rival manufacturer of pacemakers a hefty Dollars 75m plus 10 years' of royalties for alleged infringement of copyright - as is reported in a news item handily placed above the ad.
