920317 FT 17 MAR 92 / Observer: Utopia coined More numbers from the Fancy Figures Department. IMI, which a year ago bought the Birmingham Mint and makes about 800m coins a year, is a small player in the international mint league, although it has re-coined Lithuania. Gary Allen, the chief executive, clearly sees EC monetary union as a Utopia where IMI and all its counterparts are kept busy without competing with each other. In the EC, he says, there are about 160bn coins. 'If we ever go to the ecu that will keep our mint going, and every other mint, for 15 years.'
