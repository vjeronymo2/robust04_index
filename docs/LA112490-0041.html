November 24, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MAGIC, HEARN CHARGED WITH WRONG RESPONSE TO ARREST OF WORTHY

Millionaire James Worthy, whose business is playing basketball with the Lakers, is arrested for soliciting a prostitute or prostitutes in Houston, we learn after the game.

Chick Hearn wouldn't breathe a word of it. In all events, Hearn bends over backward to make us believe the Lakers belong essentially to a church. It's too late for these kinds of lies.

ROBERT L. GREENFIELD, Goleta
