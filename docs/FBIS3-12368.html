Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] There was a happy ending this evening to today's hijacking of the Air Algerie Boeing-727 to Spain, following the surrender of the three hijackers to the Spanish authorities and the release of all passengers. [passage omitted] The hijackers made contact with the Spanish authorities and later released 12 passengers, including 8 women, 2 men, and 2 children. According to the Spanish minister of transport, the hijackers asked for political asylum. Permanent contacts were established between the Algerian Embassy in Spain, represented by the Consul-general, and the Spanish authorities in Alicante. Immediately after the plane was hijacked, the Algerian Foreign Ministry set up an emergency committee to follow up developments of the affair and take the necessary measures. The hijacking, which saw a happy ending, lasted only a few hours and is now over.
