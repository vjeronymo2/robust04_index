July 7, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CHOCK FULL O' NUTS BUYS PEANUT PACKER

Chock Full O' Nuts Corp. announced that it has completed the acquisition, through its subsidiary Jimbo's Jumbos Inc., of Everfresh Products Inc., a processor and packer of in-the-shell peanuts.

The price was not released.

Everfresh, in Erlanger, Ky., sold more than 5 million pounds of peanuts in fiscal 1989, the company said.
