July 16, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MEDIA BIAS ON ABORTION ISSUE

The articles were outstanding. I can only hope that this is a new beginning for The Times and other media. I can only hope that this isn't just a bone thrown to the dog so that he will stop barking and go away.

All any "pro-life advocate" has ever asked is that there is fair reporting. That's all that is asked now.

The "liberal media" and the "conservative anti-abortion activists" need to consider two things when they are speaking out. Women who find themselves in a crisis pregnancy situation need compassion, understanding and very practical help. And unborn babies who are in danger of being aborted also need advocates to speak out in their defense. Unborn children are valuable and they have the right to our compassion and protection.

GAIL M. HAMILTON

Northridge
