BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko and Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev, 15 Jun--Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk today received Arkadiy Volskiy, leader of the Russian Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs, at the latter's request. Ukrainian Deputy Prime Minister Vasyl Yevtukhov and Leonid Kuchma, president of the Ukrainian Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs, participated in the meeting, which was held behind closed doors. ITAR-TASS has learned that there was discussion during the meeting regarding regulation of the customs regulations between Russia and Ukraine, taxes, and also bilateral payments. For this purpose, entrepreneurs of the two states intend to sign a contract today on setting up a Ukrainian-Russian bank.
