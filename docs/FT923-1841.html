920921 FT 21 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Restructuring boost for Business Tech The benefits of a restructuring programme are reflected in the interim results of Business Technology Group, the office equipment concern, which show a pre-tax profit of Pounds 212,000 for the first half of 1992, against a Pounds 154,000 loss last time. The profit was achieved on a reduced turnover of Pounds 11.1m (Pounds 30.4m). Earnings per share were 0.75p (0.42p losses). There is again no interim dividend, but dividend policy will be reviewed in the light of the full year results.
