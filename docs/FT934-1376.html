931218 FT 18 DEC 93 / Managers buy out bus company Managers of Fylde Borough Transport have bought the bus company which was previously owned by Fylde Borough Council, Lancashire. The company has 82 buses operating in Lytham St Annes and Blackpool, and 12 coaches trading as Seagull Coaches. It is the 24th local-authority bus company to be sold - a further 24 remain in public ownership.
