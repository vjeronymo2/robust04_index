Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Sitnikov] [Text] United Nations, 8 Mar -- On Monday UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali had a meeting with Abdujalil Samadov, chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Tajikistan. During the talks the situation on the Tajik-Afghan border was discussed, as well as a number of problems of political settlement in Tajikistan in the light of setting in motion the mechanism of inter-Tajik talks under UN auspices and with the active participation of Russia. R. Piriz Ballon, special envoy of the UN secretary general in Tajikistan, and L.K. Kayumov, permanent representative of the Republic of Tajikistan in the United Nations, were present at the meeting.
