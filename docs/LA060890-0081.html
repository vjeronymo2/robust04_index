June 8, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

A Superior Court judge in Paterson, N.J., Thursday cleared the way for a heavyweight title fight between champion Buster Douglas and Evander Holyfield by rejecting a request that would have prevented promoters from offering bids to stage the fight.

Judge Amos C. Saunders' ruling caps a nearly three-month legal battle by Holyfield's attorney to prevent rival promoters from interfering with the bids for the championship fight.
