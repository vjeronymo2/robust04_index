Language: Tajik Article Type:BFN [Text] Today, Imamali Rakhmanov, head of state and chairman of the Supreme Soviet of Tajikistan, received U.S. Ambassador Stanley Escudero. In the course of the meeting, Escudero passed on the friendly greetings from American leaders to Imamali Rakhmanov and said that the United States is ready to cooperate with Tajikistan in all branches of the national economy.
