931211 FT 11 DEC 93 / World News in Brief: Gunman holds 10 hostage A gunman was holding 10 people hostage at a dentist's surgery in Hay-on-Wye, Powys, early today. Police said they wanted to interview him in connection with an attack on a 60-year-old woman. The incident began when police attempted to arrest the man at the local branch of the National Westminster Bank.
