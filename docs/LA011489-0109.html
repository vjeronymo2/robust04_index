January 14, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

THE COLLEGES;

COMMUNITY COLLEGE WOMEN'S BASKETBALL;

CROSSKEY'S 25 POINTS LEAD FULLERTON, 83-63

Lisa Crosskey scored a season-high 25 points to lead Fullerton College to an 83-63 victory over Riverside Friday in an Orange Empire Conference women's basketball game at Fullerton.

Viv Perez added 13 points and Angie Nelsen 10 for Fullerton (18-1 overall, 2-0 in conference).

Rancho Santiago 62, Orange Coast 55 -- Vanessa Early scored 22 points and had 15 rebounds to lead visiting Rancho Santiago (7-13, 1-2).
