BFN [by ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Washington, 21 Jun -- Viktor Chernomyrdin, chairman of the government of the Russian Federation, arrived in Washington today for a visit. He was met at Andrews Air Force Base by U.S. Vice President Albert Gore and other officials. The Russian premier will take part in the work of the next session of the Russian-U.S. Inter-governmental Commission on Economic and Technological Cooperation.
