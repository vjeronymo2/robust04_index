November 10, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

BUENA PARK'S FOLEY RESIGNS FOOTBALL POST

Rick Foley, Buena Park High School's football coach, resigned Thursday night after his team ended the season with a 35-12 loss to Sunny Hills.

Foley said he has moved to Moreno Valley and will look for a coaching job in the area.

Foley was at Buena Park for three seasons, his teams compiling a 7-22-1 record. Buena Park was 2-8 this season.
