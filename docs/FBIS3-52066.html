Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] San Cristobal de las Casas, Mexico 29 Jan (EFE) -- Manuel Camacho Solis, peace and reconciliation commissioner, today (Saturday) reported seven points [puntos] of the Mexican Government to begin peace talks with the rebel Zapatista National Liberation Army (EZLN). During a news conference in San Cristobal de las Casas, approximately 750 km southeast of the Mexican capital, Camacho Solis said conditions do exist for peace, but he concluded he does not know whether prompt negotiations will begin with the rebels.
