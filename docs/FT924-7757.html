921114 FT 14 NOV 92 / Newspapers agree TV schedule deal NATIONAL AND regional newspapers yesterday agreed an out-of-court settlement with BBC Enterprises and Independent Television Publications for a licence fee structure for the publication of television schedules. The deal, subject to court approval, should end two years of controversy and litigation. Under the deal the Copyright Tribunal's award of 0.003p per copy for each title a year has been increased to 0.004p. The minimum payment for each publisher has been increased from Pounds 200 to Pounds 250.
