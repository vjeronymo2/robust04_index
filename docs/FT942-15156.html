940416 FT 16 APR 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Non-gold shares registered mild gains late in the session, but activity was said to be muted ahead of the weekend. The overall index rose 14 to 4,969, industrials put on 26 to 5,732 while the gold index eased 13 to 1,941. De Beers improved R2.50 to R109, Anglos R4.50 to R211 and SAB R1.50 to R82.50.
