Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] The Slovak Telecommunications [company] has reversed its decision to terminate its contract with Radio Free Europe [RFE] on 31 January 1994. The company will take steps toward solving technical and legal issues concerning RFE broadcasts via transmitters in the Slovak Republic. According to a memorandum of understanding signed by RFE Deputy Director Gary Thatcher and Vladmir Ondrovic, director of Slovak Communications, in Bratislava today, the RFE is open to further negotiations.
