BFN [Text] A new umbrella group of political parties, comprising parties from the socialist and Lumumbist paths, has just been set up in Zaire. It is called the Union of Congolese Leftists [Union de la Gauche Congolaise]. According to lawyer Mukenge Ndibu, one of the creators of the movement, the left has an inactive military wing that can be easily mobilized with moral strength and an interest that only needs to be awakened, end quote.
