BFN [Text] 'Abd-al-Qadir Qaddurah, the chairman of the Executive Committee of the Syrian Arab Parliamentary Branch, this afternoon received a Yemeni parliamentary delegation, led by 'Abbas al-Mu'ayyad, member of the House of Representatives. Shakir Is'ayyid, the vice chairman of the Executive Committee, attended. Discussions dealt with the latest developments in fraternal Yemen and the need to stop the fighting, dialogue, and maintaining Yemeni unity.
