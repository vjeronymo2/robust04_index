BFN [Text] On 30 May 1994, the government promulgated Decree No. 44-CP amending Article 2 of Decree No. 79-CP dated 6 November 1993. Under the newly-promulgated decree, Article 2 of Decree No. 79-CP has been amended as follows: "The state will not censor publications before they run off the press, except in cases deemed necessary by the prime minister of the government. In such cases, the minister of culture and information will bear responsibility before the prime minister for the censorship of and action against the publications involved."
