Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The National Assembly today approved the State General Budget with 92 percent of votes in favor, 14 abstentions and no vote against. With this approval the government's socioeconomic program's chapter is closed. The draft law on the State General Budget has been discussed since yesterday in the Congress Palace, and this morning it was approved by parliament. The government spent the day responding to concerns raised yesterday by the deputies, and afterwards the State General Budget was approved. [passage omitted]
