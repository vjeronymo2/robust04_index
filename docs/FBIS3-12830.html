Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 7 Mar (MENA)--Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat has received French President Francois Mitterrand's adviser, Bruno Dulain [name and title as received], at his residence in Cairo. Palestinian sources in Cairo told MENA that during the meeting they discussed the obstacles hindering the Palestinian-Israeli peace talks in light of the massacre the Israeli settlers committed against the Palestinian worshipers in the al-Ibrahimi Mosque and the world reaction to it. Palestinian Ambassador Sa'id Kamal attended the meeting, which was held yesterday evening.
