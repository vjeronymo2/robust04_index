BFN [Text] Lionel Soto, vice president of the Cuban Council of Ministers, arrived in Moscow from Kazakhstan, where he examined with Kazakhstan authorities the state of bilateral relations. A member of the high ranking Cuban delegation told PRENSA LATINA that means for the development of economic-trade relations between the two states were analyzed. Lionel Soto was received by Kazakhstan Prime Minister Sergey Tereshchenko and met with high ranking officials of the parliament and the ministries of Foreign Affairs and Foreign Economic Relations. PRENSA LATINA also reports that the Cuban delegation will remain in Moscow until next week when they will then begin a visit to Belarus.
