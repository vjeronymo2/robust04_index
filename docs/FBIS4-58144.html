BFN [Text] Reacting to claims by Jordan Valley settlers that they would no longer be permitted to drive through Jericho as of Wednesday [4 May], an IDF [Israel Defense Forces] Central Command spokesman clarified that there would be no traffic changes in the Jericho area after the inception of autonomy. The spokesman clarified that the road through Jericho would remain open to Israeli vehicles. Major General Ilan Biran, the commander of the Central Command, nevertheless decided to accede to the settlers' request and to open the road along the border near Jordan, which is currently being renovated.
