August 12, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

TONY SERRA

I am enraged at Tony Serra's statement that people killed by drug overdoses and infants born addicted to drugs are expendable.

Has this man no concern for human life?

I am also appalled that such a self-righteous crusader for justice flouts the laws against drugs, deliberately does not pay parking tickets and for many years did not file a tax return.

There are two words for people like Serra, and those words are criminal and hypocrite. Serra is just as bad as the people he rails against who hunted down and killed Indians like coyotes.

ROBERT E. PARK

Los Angeles
