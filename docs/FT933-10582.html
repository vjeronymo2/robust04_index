930803 FT 03 AUG 93 / International Company News: RH Macy warns on July results RH Macy, the bankrupt New York-based department store group, yesterday predicted July results would fall short of expectations and said it was approaching the autumn season cautiously because of weak consumer confidence, writes Karen Zagor in New York. The company narrowed its net loss for June to Dollars 40.8m, or Dollars 25.23 a share, from Dollars 64.7m, or Dollars 40.01, a year earlier.
