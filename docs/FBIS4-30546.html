BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 26 (KCNA) -- MINJU CHOSON today dedicates a by-lined article to the 34th anniversary of the independence of Madagascar. The article says: Since the independence the Madagascan people have directed efforts to removing the colonial aftermath left over by the imperialists in social life and economic field, achieving national unity and stability and developing the national economy. Pursuing non-aligned policy externally, Madagascar is developing friendly relations with many countries and striving to make the Indian Ocean a peace zone. The Korean people rejoice over the achievements made by the Madagascan people in the building of a new society. The Korean people believe that the bonds of Korea-Madagascar friendship forged in the struggle to build a new society will grow stronger and develop in the future. The Korean people warmly congratulate the Madagascan people on their national day and wish them fine success in their work for the stability and prosperity of the country.
