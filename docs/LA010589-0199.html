January 5, 1989, Thursday, Late Final Edition

NATION;

UNION SEEKS WORK-AT-HOME BAN

The International Ladies Garment Workers Union today asked a federal court to prohibit the Labor Department from implementing regulations that would allow working at home in five apparel-related industries.

The suit, filed in federal district court here, argues that Labor Secretary Ann Dore McLaughlin acted arbitrarily in approving the regulations.
