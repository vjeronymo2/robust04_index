921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / UK Company News: 20% rise for MMT Computing MMT COMPUTING, the USM-quoted software house, lifted pre-tax profit by nearly 20 per cent in the year ended August 31, although sales rose only 5 per cent. Trading conditions remained difficult, but the cost base was firmly under control, directors stated. Turnover came to Pounds 6.22m (Pounds 5.92m) and profit to Pounds 1.5m (Pounds 1.26m). Expenses were cut and investment income and the associate's contribution increased. Earnings per share were 7.9p (6.8p) and the final dividend is 2.4p for a total of 3.5p (3.2p).
