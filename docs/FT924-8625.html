921110 FT 10 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Regent Inns edges ahead to Pounds 913,000 Regent Inns, which operates 36 pubs, hotels and restaurants, mainly in London and the Home Counties, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 886,725 to Pounds 913,212 over the 12 months to June 30. The outcome was struck after a loss of Pounds 60,841 (profits of Pounds 187,551) on disposal of fixed assets. Turnover was virtually unchanged at Pounds 11.9m. After a reduced tax charge, earnings per share emerged at 7.8p (6.7p). The single distribution for the year goes up 0.5p to 2p. The shares are traded on a matched bargain under Rule 535 (2).
