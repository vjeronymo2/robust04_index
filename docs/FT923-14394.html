920708 FT 08 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Enlarged RPH falls to Pounds 82.1m RPH, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Reed International, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 82.1m from turnover of Pounds 483.8m in the year to end-March. This compared with profits of Pounds 91.2m from turnover of Pounds 542.4m last time and was struck after a reduced interest charge of Pounds 10.6m (Pounds 12.7m). Tax took Pounds 28m (Pounds 21.5m) after which earnings per share came out at 31.8p (41.1p). During the year RPH acquired The Estates Gazette, Newpoint Publishing and What to Buy from Reed International. To finance these acquisitions RPH issued 14.6m ordinary shares its parent.
