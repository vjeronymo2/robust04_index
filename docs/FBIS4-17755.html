BFN [Text] The management of the All-Russian State TV and Radio Company has received notice from Energonadzor director (Pavel Sinyukov) that, because of the non-payment of bills for power supply, from today the transmitter operating on 261 kHz on the long wave has been switched off. This is the most powerful transmitter in Russia covering the entire European part of our country. At the technical service of our company we were told that the switch-off occurred at 1429.
