BFN [Text] The visiting four-member Iraqi delegation, led by National Assembly Speaker Sa'di Mahdi Salih, called on Prime Minister Ms. Benazir Bhutto in Islamabad yesterday. They exchanged views on matters of bilateral interest and regional and international issues. The Iraqi speaker commended the steps taken by the Pakistani prime minister for socioeconomic reforms. He hoped that the Islamic countries will end their differences and closely cooperate with each other for the welfare of their people. Ms. Benazir Bhutto paid tribute to the Iraqi people for facing the present difficulties with patience and hoped that Iraq will soon be able to overcome these difficulties. She said Pakistan has always supported the territorial integrity of all countries, and it will welcome normal relations among all Islamic countries.
