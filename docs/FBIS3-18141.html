Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Umberto Bossi, [secretary of the Northern League], has started the election campaign in Piacenza today and has attacked [Berlusconi's] Forza Italia: "It is the creature of the old Christian Democratic Party. We understood that immediately and we captured it with the trap of a free-market alignment [polo liberista]," he said. He then made a direct appeal to voters: Beware, within the freedom alignment [the alliance with Berlusconi], there is only one free-market group: The League. Bossi made his forecast: In a month's time, the League will have 120-150 members of parliament. The first bill we will present will be the expropriation of the assets that parties gained during Bribe City.
