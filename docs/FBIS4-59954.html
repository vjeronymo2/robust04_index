BFN [Text] Mashhad (Khorasan) May 18, IRNA -- Over the past week 806 kgs of opium and 3.5 kgs of heroin were seized from traffickers near the desert areas of this northeastern border province. According to Colonel Sardar Hamid-Reza Musavi of the Khorasan law enforcement personnel, the latest drug haul was the result of the `saeqa' (lightning) operations staged simultaneously in 23 security regions of the province. Last year 130 clashes occurred with armed bandits which resulted in the death of 61 `death merchants' and arrest of 58 others, he added. Also during the same period 55 different weapons and 100 grenades were seized from the traffickers.
