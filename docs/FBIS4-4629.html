BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Apr 1 -- A delegation of the Czech parliament led by Mr Milan Uhde, speaker of the lower house, left Hanoi for the south this morning, continuing its official visit to Vietnam. The delegation was accompanied by deputy head of the National Assembly's External Relations Commission Tran Van Phac. During its stay in Hanoi, the Czech delegation met with Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam and called at Dong Nam A Brewery. Meanwhile Mrs Milan Uhde visited some cultural, social establishments and place of historic interest.
