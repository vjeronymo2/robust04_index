BFN [Text] Only one parliamentary faction, Yabloko, will not sign the agreement on public accord, presidential aide Georgiy Satarov declared at a briefing on Friday in the Kremlin commenting on Boris Yeltsin's meeting with representatives of Russia's leading political forces. In his words, all other factions and parties are looking for their own ways to sign this document. Satarov underlined that the working group in charge of the preparation of this agreement for signing still receives proposals both from political structures and individuals. As he said, the final text of the agreement is due to be ready on April 26. On April 27 the State Duma will have to pass a decision on the text which cannot be changed. Satarov believes that the overwhelming majority of the Russia's constituent regions and republics will sign the agreement. According to available information, a total of nine regions have still not reported about their decision to sign this document. Satarov informed journalists about the conversation held between Yeltsin and participants of this meeting. He said that Yeltsin had declared that his attitude to any group not signing this agreement would not change so as to avoid a new spiral of confrontation.
