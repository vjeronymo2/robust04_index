931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / International Capital Markets: SBC in Mexico venture Swiss Bank Corporation and Mexico's largest private financial group, Grupo Financiero Banamex-Accival, are to form an alliance to trade Mexican equity derivative products. SBC said the move would strengthen its presence in Mexico, where it expects to see more initial public offerings and investments by global money managers. Starting next year, the joint venture plans to make markets in over-the-counter options on equities, exchange-listed warrants, futures and forwards, equity swaps and convertibles. Grupo Financiero's two main subsidiaries are the National Bank of Mexico and Accival, the securities broker.
