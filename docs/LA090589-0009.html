September 5, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

DIGEST /LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: SEPULVEDA WOMAN DIES IN SALT LAKE CITY

A44-year-old Sepulveda woman died during the Labor Day holiday weekend from injuries she received in a hit-and-run accident in a crosswalk in downtown Salt Lake City, police said.

The victim of the accident, which occured at 9:40 p.m. Friday, was identified as Gai Gardiner.

Gardiner was in a crosswalk when she was struck by a westbound pickup truck that fled, officers said. Gardiner was taken to Latter-day Saints Hospital, where she died Saturday night from head and internal injuries.
