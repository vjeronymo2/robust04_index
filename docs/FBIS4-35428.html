BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 Jun (MENA) -- The committee in charge of preparation for the national dialogue held its first meeting at the People's Assembly headquarters today under the chairmanship of Dr. Mustafa Khalil, deputy leader of the National Democratic Party [NDP] and the committee moderator. The meeting was attended by Dr. Yusuf Wali, deputy prime minister and NDP secretary general; Minister of Information Safwat al-Sharif; Kamal al-Shadhili, minister of state for the People's Assembly and Consultative Council affairs; party and trade union leaders; and a number of university professors and public figures. The committee will discuss the organization of the national dialogue conference and its agenda, as well as determining the national forces who will participate in it. Upon the completion of its task, the committee will submit a report to President Husni Mubarak on the issues to be agreed upon before calling the general conference to session. MENA has learned that the parties participating in the dialogue have compiled working papers explaining their views on the issues they will raise during the national dialogue.
