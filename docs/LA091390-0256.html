September 13, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

FOREMAN SET TO FIGHT ANDERSON

Heavyweight Terry Anderson of Tampa, Fla., was named the new opponent for former world champion George Foreman on Sept. 25.

Anderson, who has a 19-2 record, replaces Walter Masseroni of Argentina, who pulled out of the fight, promoter Frank Warren said today.

Anderson, 30, last fought in February, when he knocked out countryman Joe Adams in the first round. His 19 victories include 17 knockouts.
