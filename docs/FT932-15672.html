930410 FT 10 APR 93 / Finance & The Family: Small companies slip from peak - At a glance Small company shares fell over the pre-Easter week, with the Hoare Govett Small Companies Index (capital gains version) dropping 0.8 per cent, from 1376.75 to 1366.19, over the six days to April 7. However, Hoare Govett reports that the recent rally has taken the index back up to pre-recession levels.
