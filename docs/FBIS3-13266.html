Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] A public opinion poll conducted by the Dahaf Institute directed by Dr. Mina Tzemah posed the following question: In your opinion, who is best suited to be prime minister today? Yitzhaq Rabin -- 44 percent; Binyamin Netanyahu -- 22 percent; Refa'el Eytan -- 12 percent; none of them -- 20 percent; did not reply -- 2 percent. The poll was conducted on 9 March among 507 respondents who constitute a representative sample of the adult population of Israel. The maximum margin of error is 4 percent.
