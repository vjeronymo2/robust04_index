August 5, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

MORE ROSEANNE

With respect to Roseanne Barr's national anthem escapade, I am truly embarrassed by the people who booed her.

However, embarrassment turned to confusion when President Bush publicly concluded that her singing was a disgrace.

Isn't it a greater disgrace for Bush's son to be involved in the billion-dollar Silverado Banking, Savings & Loan Assn. scandal? To me, that is a real profound disgrace.

At least Roseanne knew the words to the "Star-Spangled Banner." Do we have such short memories that we can't recall Pia Zadora's miserable attempt to sing the national anthem over network TV? She forgot the words, or didn't know them.

My hat is off to you, Rosie. You sang your best and I, for one, appreciate your effort.

CYNTHIA McMILLAN

San Diego
