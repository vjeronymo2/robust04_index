BFN [Text] Tirana, June 10 (ATA) -- Statements by a senior official of the Socialist Party of Serbia, Bogdanovic, who is at the same time deputy chairman of the Chamber of Republics in the Assembly of the self-declared Yugoslavia, that there will be no talks with Rugova are not only discouraging but they close all possible ways for a dialogue between Pristina and Belgrade, the newspaper BUJKU of Pristina writes. In one of its commentaries entitled "With Whom Will the Serbian Party Talk?" BUJKU stresses that if Bogdanovic's statements are the final say of the Belgrade government, then the Serbian party has nothing to do but to negotiate with ghosts.
