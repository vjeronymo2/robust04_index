BFN [Text] According to sources at the Moncloa Palace, Spanish Prime Minister Felipe Gonzalez has decided to postpone his official visit to Paraguay, due to take place at the beginning of next week. Felipe Gonzalez's trip to Paraguay was scheduled for 16-18 May. Ten days ago Gonzalez also postponed his trip to Romania and Bulgaria, due to take place last week, because of the political crisis unleashed by the flight of Luis Roldan, the former head of the Civil Guard, and by the fact that the former governor of the Bank of Spain and Manuel De La Concha, former head of the Madrid stock market, were sent to jail, as well as by the resignations of Interior and Agriculture Ministers Antoni Asuncion and Vicente Albero.
