BFN [Unattributed report: "Intensified Economic Cooperation With Hungary"] [Text] A delegation of the Macedonian Foreign Ministry, led by Under Secretary Dmitar Belcev, held talks in Budapest on coordinating the Agreement on Economic and Trade Cooperation between Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] and Hungary. The agreement specifies ways to intensify and develop economic cooperation, especially in the field of metallurgy, chemical industry, and the agriculture. Belcev informed in detail of the damages caused by the Greek embargo against Macedonia.
