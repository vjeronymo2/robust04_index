AGENCY FOR INTERNATIONAL DEVELOPMENT Housing Guaranty Program; Investment Opportunity The U.S. Agency for International Development (USAID) has authorized the guaranty of loans to the Government of Pakistan (``Borrower'') as part of USAID's development assistance program.
