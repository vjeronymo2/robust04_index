BFN [Unattributed article: "China/Italy: Export Credit Granted"] [Text] (ANSA) Beijing, March 25 -- An export credit line worth 75 million dollars has been granted to the Industrial and Commercial Bank of China by a group of Italian banks led by Crediop, part of the San Paolo di Torino group. The credit line is part of a 300 million dollar financing operation for Chinese purchases of European tools and technology, aimed at enhancing Sino-Italian trade which last year more than doubled. A first tranche of 150 million dollars went to the Bank of China, and the second, of 75 milion dollars, went to the People's Construction Bank.
