BFN [Text] By a large majority the Knesset today approved the appointment of Efrayim Sne as health minister. Our correspondent Hayim Hecht reports that supporting the appointment were 45 Knesset members [MK's], including YI'UD [Independent and Democratic Israel] members and the National Religious Party's Yig'al Bibi. Opposed were 19 MK's. The health minister is currently holding his first work meeting with ministry officials.
