BFN [Report by Ivan Hladkin from the Central Electoral Commission] [Excerpt] [Passage omitted] I want to make all our listeners happy by telling them that according to preliminary calculations just provided by the Central Electoral Commission, the presidential elections in Belarus have taken place. In Belarus in general, 63.6 percent of electors cast their votes by 1200 GMT. Turnover was highest in Brest Oblast -- 70.3 percent; in Vitebsk Oblast -- 64.4 percent; in Gomel Oblast -- 65.4 percent; in Grodno Oblast -- 63.8 percent; in Mogilev Oblast -- 64.4. percent; in Minsk Oblast -- 65 percent; in the city of Minsk -- 53 percent. In the Republic in general, elections have been completed at 240 polling stations. [passage omitted]
