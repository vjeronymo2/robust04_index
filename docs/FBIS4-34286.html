BFN [Excerpt] The Chamber of Deputies finally approved the constitutional reform bill this evening. The Chamber of Deputies president released a note this afternoon explaining the reasons that prompted the drafting of the current text, which gave rise to some observations by the Colorada and Batlleist Union. The bill was carried by 78 votes to one--that of Helios Sarthou of the MPP [People's Participation Movement]. The electoral court will now make arrangements for the plebiscite. [passage omitted]
