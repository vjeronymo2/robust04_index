BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Boris Krivoshey] [Text] Moscow May 18 TASS -- Finnish President Martti Ahtisaari arrived for an official visit to Moscow on Wednesday. He was welcomed on the tarmac by Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin. During his two day visit, the Finnish president is due to meet President Boris Yeltsin and other leaders of the government and parliament. The talks are expected to centre around bilateral relations, European security and problems relating to the settlement of the crisis in the former Yugoslavia and the situation in the Baltic countries.
