BFN [Excerpts] The provincial meeting to commend model workers was held solemnly in the provincial people's hall on the morning of 27 April. [passage omitted] Present were provincial leaders,including Li Changchun, Ren Keli, Song Zhaosu, Lin Yinghai, Wu Guangxian, Song Guochen, (Zhang Dehuang), Fan Qinchen, (Ma Jianzhang), Zhang Wenbin, (Hou Jingxu), Li Chengyu, Yu Jiahua, Liu Yujie, Tu Jiaju, Yao Ruxue, Mei Yangzheng, (Wang Quanshu), and (Lu Maosheng). [passage omitted] Vice Governor Li Chengyu read out the decision of the Henan Provincial People's Government on commending model workers. [passage omitted] After the namelist of model workers was read out, provincial leaders distributed awards to the representatives of model workers. One thousand model workers were cited at the meeting. [passage omitted] Provincial party Secretary Li Changchun delivered an important speech. [passage omitted] Li Changchun said: The provincial party committee and government called on the people throughout the province to learn from the model workers cited by the current meeting and from the advanced figures who have continuously emerged on every front of endeavor, base themselves on their jobs, learn from and catch up with the advanced, strive to achieve first-rate accomplishments, and make greater fresh contributions for new victories in this year's reform and modernization and for the realization of the magnificent goal of forging ahead in unity to rejuvenate Henan's economy.
