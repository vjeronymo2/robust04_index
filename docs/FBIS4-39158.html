BFN [Text] Kiev, 3 Jun--The "Ostankino" television company has disseminated information that subunits of Ukraine's National Guard supposedly arrived with fighting equipment on the Crimean peninsula on 3 June. Guard Colonel Oleksandr Kostyukov, commander of the Simferopol brigade of Ukraine's National Guard, has reported that this information does not correspond to reality. These subunits are not being brought in, and this is not envisaged in the future. The press service of the commander of Ukraine's National Guard has reported this.
