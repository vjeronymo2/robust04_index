April 11, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

The Brea Civic and Cultural Center Gallery is calling for entries from artists working in all media for "The Real Thing," a representational art exhibit that will open May 12.

The juror is Louis Newman, director of Louis Newman Galleries in Beverly Hills. Awards totaling $650 and purchase awards will be presented, and winning artists will be encouraged to offer their work for sale. Hand-delivered entries will be accepted at the gallery, Number One Civic Center Circle, on April 20 and 21. The fee is $6 per entry; free for the first two pieces submitted by each Brea artist. For information and entry forms, call (714) 990-7730.
