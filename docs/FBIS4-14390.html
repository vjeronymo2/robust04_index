BFN [Text] This morning the head of government, Prime Minister Mokdad Sifi, met with the representatives of four pilot municipalities in his office. The representatives were chosen from the Governorates of Algiers, Batna, Sidi Bel Abbes, and Illizi. The meeting was the first of its kind. It was attended by the minister of interior and localities as well as by a number of governors. The meeting is an opportunity to come closer to the real problems facing municipalities. Also, this meeting would be an opportunity to put the last touches on his government's program, due to be announced in the next few days, by drawing on the citizen's concerns and problems in deprived municipalities. According to information reaching us from the government palace, the government was going to allow the citizens to participate tangibly in the management of the local affairs through the newly formed 10,000 district committees, which have been set up throughout the country over the past few months.
