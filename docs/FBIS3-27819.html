Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 30 (KCNA) -- Papers here today observe the 25th anniversary of the opening of diplomatic relations between Korea and Equatorial Guinea. The Equatorial Guinean people are now striving for national unity, social stability and economic redressment, rejecting imperialists' intervention, says NODONG SINMUN in a by-lined article, and goes on: The Government of Equatorial Guinea maintains the principle of solving its problems by its people's efforts. The Korean people rejoice at successes made by the Equatorial Guinean people in the building of a new life. To strengthen and develop the Korea-Equatorial Guinea friendship is beneficial to the building of a new society in the two countries. A signed article of MINJU CHOSON says that the Korean people wish the Equatorial Guinean people greater success in their struggle to build an independent new Equatorial Guinea, believing that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two peoples will grow stronger and develop in the future.
