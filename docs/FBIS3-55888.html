Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow January 11 TASS -- The Russian State Duma has announced a day and a half break to convene again at 12:30 [0930 GMT] on January 13. The break was suggested by Deputy Sergey Shakhray, who said deputy factions, groups and their leaders need some time for organizational work in view of the upcoming elections of the State Duma speaker and his deputies, formation of the State Duma committees and election of their heads and adoption of the State Duma time-limit. Deputies have agreed to hold their morning sessions from 10 a.m. through 2 p.m. With a 30-minute break at noon and afternoon sessions from 4 p.m. through 6 p.m.
