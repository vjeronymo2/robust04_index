Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] In January 1994, the people in Ampil District of Oddar Meanchey Province launched attacks to liberate their villages and communes from the Vietnamese communist puppets and the U.S. puppets, who jointly invaded and seized the district in August 1993. Since the beginning of January, the National Army of Democratic Kampuchea joined forces with the people to mount attacks and liberated 68 villages in the area from Route 68 to the west [words indistinct], from Ampil to Phnum Srok, and from Phnum Srok to Banteay Chhmar.
