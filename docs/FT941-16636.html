940108 FT 08 JAN 94 / Finance and the Family: New trust focuses on Asia - At a glance Edinburgh Fund Managers is launching a new investment trust to specialise in smaller companies in emerging Asian markets. The New Tiger trust will invest in companies with a market capitalisation of less than Dollars 500m, mainly in Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, India, South Korea, China, the Philippines, Pakistan and Sri Lanka. The fund aims to raise Pounds 50m through a placing and offer for subscription of shares at 50p, with one warrant attached to every five shares. The minimum investment is Pounds 1,000, and the annual charge will be 1.2 per cent.
