[Docket No. QF86&hyph;138&hyph;005] Hanford L.P.; Amendment to Filing April 6, 1994. On March 22, 1994, Hanford L.P. (Applicant) tendered for filing an amendment to its filing in this docket. No determination has been made that the submittal constitutes a complete filing. The amendment modifies the application by naming Hanford L.P. as the Applicant and provides additional information pertaining to the ownership structure of the small power production facility. Any person desiring to be heard or objecting to the granting of qualifying status should file a motion to intervene or protest with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, 825 North Capitol Street, NE., Washington, DC 20426, in accordance with rules 211 and 214 of the Commission's Rules of Practice and Procedure. All such motions or protests must be filed by April 29, 1994, and must be served on the applicant. Protests will be considered by the Commission in determining the appropriate action to be taken but will not serve to make protestants parties to the proceeding. Any person wishing to become a party must file a petition to intervene. Copies of this filing are on file with the Commission and are available for public inspection. Lois D. Cashell, Secretary.
