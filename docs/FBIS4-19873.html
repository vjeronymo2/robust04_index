BFN [Text] The Navy's search for foreign submarines in the waters off Norrkoeping has not led to any results, but according to the defense staff there are several indications of alien underwater activity. The military have not completed their analyses yet, but according to some reports, the analyses concern mainly propeller noise. The submarine search force, which has been practicing in the archipelago off Norrkoeping for some time, will remain in the area for a few more days.
