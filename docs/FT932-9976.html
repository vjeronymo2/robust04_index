930511 FT 11 MAY 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There was good general demand, reports the Tea Brokers Association. Brightest liquoring East African teas and better mediums were fully firm and often dearer. Plainer descriptionsproved irregular and tended easier. Offshore demand was selective. Kenya teas attracted fair support but Central Africans met less competition at easier rates. Quotations quality 155, good medium 120, medium 110, low medium 88. The highest price realised this week was 165p for a South Indian tgfop.
