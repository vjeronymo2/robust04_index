December 28, 1990, Friday, Home Edition Southwest Airlines is cutting fares between Phoenix and seven other cities to $20, during what is traditionally a slow time of year for the airline industry. The $20 fares are good each way between Phoenix and Las Vegas, Albuquerque, El Paso, Los Angeles, San Diego, Oakland and Ontario. The tickets must be purchased before Jan. 21 for travel from Jan. 3 through April 5.
