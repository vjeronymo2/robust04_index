Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Doboj, 8 Mar (TANJUG)--Muslim snipers today killed two civilians in Serb villages on the right bank of the River Bosna, the information service of the Srpska republic army's 1st Krajina corps has reported. The statement says that the Muslim forces were firing at the Serb positions with small arms and mortars along most demarcation lines in the wider area of Doboj. [passage omitted]
