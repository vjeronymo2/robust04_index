930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / World News in Brief: Gooch quits as England cricket captain Graham Gooch resigned as England cricket captain after Australia won the fourth Test at Headingley, Leeds, by an innings and 148 runs. The victory gave Australia a 3-0 lead in the series and assured them of the Ashes, with two more matches to play. A new captain has yet to be named for the fifth Test at Edgbaston, Birmingham, which begins on August 5. In a women's World Cup match at Guildford, Surrey, England beat Australia by 43 runs.
