October 28, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ONTARIO CHRISTIAN RIPS ORANGE LUTHERAN, 35-6

Ontario Christian scored a 35-6 Olympic League victory over Orange Lutheran Friday night.

Orange Lutheran, led by Gil Green's 79 yards in 17 carries, dropped to 2-6 overall and 0-4 in league play.

Sophomore Lenny Gavin scored on a five-yard run to cap a 10-play, 62-yard Lutheran drive with 4 minutes 25 seconds left in the third quarter. That cut the deficit to 21-6, but Ontario Christian scored twice in the fourth quarter.
