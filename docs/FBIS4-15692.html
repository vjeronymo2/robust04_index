BFN [By CRIME-PRESS correspondent Vladimir Dmitriyev for TASS] [Text] Moscow April 11 TASS -- An aide of Chechen President Dzhokhar Dudayev was wounded in a gangland shootout and placed into the central hospital in Groznyy, CRIME-PRESS learned on Monday from Chechen opposition members from the Nadterek region of the southern republic who are currently staying in Moscow. The sources added that R. Labazanov, who is Dudayev's aide on most important issues and a close friend of the rebel president, is being guarded by over 200 armed people. According to the sources, Labazanov is heading a gang of 500 gunmen whose major business is kidnapping of girls who are then sold as prostitutes to Central Asia, the Middle East or northern Africa. The girls are reportedly smuggled out via Georgia and Azerbaijan. The sources said that the Labazanov's gang is especially active in the Nadterek region which is known for its resolute opposition to Dudayev. They try to intimidate the people by violence, robberies and murders and thus establish control over the region, they added.
