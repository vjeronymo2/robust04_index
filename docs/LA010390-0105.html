January 3, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

POTSHOTS: Familiar local institutions are being noticed in the popular culture this week. . . . Troubled Irvine-based Lincoln Savings and Loan (D1) is being lampooned in Doonesbury (E5) and the Crystal Cathedral's Robert Schuller makes an ironic appearance in the hit documentary "Roger & Me," giving a pep talk to unemployed auto workers over scenes of people being evicted. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
