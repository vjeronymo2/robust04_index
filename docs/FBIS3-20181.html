Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] A Romanian truck that was stopped at the Suben superhighway checkpoint carried 2,300 (!) firearms. "Wooden boxes" was tersely written on the declaration of goods that the driver presented. However, the German customs officers became suspicious and had a closer look at the 166 boxes. They contained carbines, guns, machine guns, semiautomatic guns, ammunition, and parts of weapons. The destination would have been a commercial firm in North Rhine-Westphalia. The freight was confiscated and criminal proceedings were instituted against the "receiver" and the truck driver.
