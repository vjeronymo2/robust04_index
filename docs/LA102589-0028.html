October 25, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

EARNINGS SURVEY OF COMPANY REPORTS

Salomon Inc.: The securities and investment banking concern posted third-quarter net income of $177 million, up 172% over the 1988 period. Revenue totaled $2.6 billion, up 103%. The New York company said the results reflect a continuation of the second quarter's turnaround in principal transactions revenue and a 50% increase in investment banking revenue.
