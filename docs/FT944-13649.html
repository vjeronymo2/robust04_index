941026 FT 26 OCT 94 / World News in Brief: Protests on eve of Israel-Jordan accord A member of Jordan's parliament addresses protestors in the centre of Amman as part of a campaign to undermine the country's peace accord with Israel, due to be signed today. The rally, attended by several thousand supporters of the powerful Moslem Brotherhood in the Hashimiyah square, was organised secretly as the Jordanian authorities had banned protests.
