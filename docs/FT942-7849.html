940524 FT 24 MAY 94 / University pay deal of 2.85% accepted Representatives of the Association of University Teachers have voted to accept a 2.85 per cent pay deal for its members, made up of a 2.5 per cent wage increase plus a one-off cash payment worth 0.35 per cent of salary. Starting salaries rise from Pounds 13,601 a year to Pounds 14,690. 'There is nothing generous about this settlement,' said Mr David Triesman, the AUT's general secretary. 'In broadly accepting the settlement we must renew our insistence on a review of academic pay.'
