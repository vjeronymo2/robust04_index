January 5, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

THIS WEEKEND: The star of the Sports-Vacation & RV Show opening Saturday at the Anaheim Convention Center is "REVCON 4 X 4," billed as "the world's first off-road motor home. . . ." The Carson-built behemoth comes in sizes up to 31 feet and can cost as much as $225,000 when such options as a basement, satellite dish, cellular phone, oak plank flooring and cedar-lined closets are included. Dana Parsons and Mark I. Pinsky
