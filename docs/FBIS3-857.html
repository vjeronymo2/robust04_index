Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Gilberto Catema, Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] political delegate for Cabo Delgado Province, has reiterated accusations that the government is providing military training to some 800 men in the Republic of Tanzania. Catema said the Mozambique Liberation Front, Frelimo, party is giving military training to men to fight Renamo, should the latter win the elections scheduled to be held in October of this year. The Renamo political delegate for Cabo Delgado Province also said that some Frelimo Party officials had gone to military training centers in Tanzania last month.
