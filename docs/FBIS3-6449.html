Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 15 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- As the Mongolian mass media informed today, the Mongolian Democratic Union will organize a rally on one of the central squares of the Mongolian capital in March 18. It is quite possible that by this meeting actions of protest of opposition forces of Mongolia against activity of the State Great Hural and government will start.
