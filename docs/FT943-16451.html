940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / Communists back Kuchma Ukraine's Communist party said it would back Mr Leonid Kuchma, a former prime minister, in the race for the presidency against Mr Leonid Kravchuk, the incumbent. The party's support could be crucial in the run-off poll on Sunday. Mr Kuchma trailed Mr Kravchuk by only 6.5 percentage points in the first round last month , when the Communist vote gave 13 points to another Socialist candidate. Mr Kuchma favours closer relations with Russia while his rival appeals to more nationalist sentiment in the country. Ukraine's Communists are the single largest bloc in parliament and have attracted enough independents to create a clear legislative majority. 'The Communists are the only well-organised political party in Ukraine,' said a western diplomat. 'Their support could be a significant factor.'
