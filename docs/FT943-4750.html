940907 FT 07 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Kingspan Improved trading margins enabled Kingspan Group, the Irish Republic-based building products group, to report pre-tax profits for the six months to June 30 ahead by 57 per cent to IPounds 2.05m (Pounds 2.03m), compared with IPounds 1.3m. Turnover improved from IPounds 29m to IPounds 30.4m. Earnings per share came out at 6.44p (3.88p) and the interim dividend is being raised to 1.2p (1p).
