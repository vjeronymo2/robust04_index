Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, Feb 4 (AFP) -- An armoured Zil limousine carrying Russian Defence Minister Pavel Grachev and his visiting French counterpart Francois Leotard crashed into a truck on a icy road in Moscow Friday [4 February] but nobody was hurt. The car hit a truck in icy conditions as the two defence chiefs were heading to Olabino to visi the Tamaskaya guard, an AFP correspondent in the motorcade said. The front of the armoured Zil car was badly crushed. The ministers later transferred to another vehicle.
