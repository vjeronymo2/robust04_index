940816 FT 16 AUG 94 / Observer: Chilled out So how was your Woodstock? Bet it didn't come up to the exacting standards of Steven McBride, from Buffalo, who confided to the Washington Post: 'Me individually, as an individual, I say we're together, interactingwise. I've met a cop and a robber, a poet and a scholar. Listen, I came here because I generally love people, they amuse the hell out of me . . . Did you ever read Albert Camus? This is The Stranger, man, people are getting stranger and stranger, together.'
