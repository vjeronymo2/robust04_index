931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / UK Company News: More funds for Lloyd's SYNDICATE Capital Trust, the Lloyd's investment vehicle sponsored by Rafael Zorn Hemsley, the stockbrokers, yesterday became the fourth Lloyd's investment company to raise capital from institutional investors this week. In the last two weeks institutional investors have committed more than Pounds 500m in corporate capital allowing the insurance market to underwrite an additional Pounds 1bn in insurance premiums next year. Two other trusts plan formal launches today, raising a further Pounds 80m in funds. Rafael Zorn Hemsley placed Pounds 32.5m of shares in Syndicate Capital Trust, with institutions and was seeking to raise a further Pounds 17.5m through an open offer for subscription. SCT has secured some Pounds 60m of capacity on 15 Lloyd's syndicates. The trust's capital will be managed by John Govett. Abtrust, the Abderdeen based fund management company, expects today to publish full listing particulars for Abtrust Lloyd's Insurance Trust. A Pounds 30m placing and intermediaries offer is to be handled by Peel Hunt, the stockbrokers. The trust has Pounds 30m in allocated capacity. Delian Lloyd's Investment Trust, sponsored by Hill Samuel, also expects to complete a placing with institutions to raise up to Pounds 50m in capital today. Meanwhile two other trusts both published pathfinders yesterday. Nelson Lloyd's Trust, yesterday issued a pathfinder prospectus. It plans to raise Pounds 60m through a placement and offer for subscription and will provide Pounds 113m in capacity for Lloyd's syndicates. Premium Trust and Premium Underwriting also issued a pathfinder and said it had raised its capital raising target by 10 per cent to Pounds 33m.
