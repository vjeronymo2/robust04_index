BFN [Text] A high-level military delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia led by Senior Generals Tie Banh and Tea Chamrat, co-ministers of defense, arrived in Hanoi yesterday on an official visit to our country at the invitation of Senior General Doan Khue, minister of national defense. A solemn reception was held at the Ministry of National Defense guesthouse. Senior General Doan Khue, Vietnam's minister of national defense, heartily welcomed the two co-ministers of defense and the high-level military delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia. He said he believed the visit will help consolidate the friendly relations between the two countries and two armies. Senior General Tie Banh, speaking on behalf of Senior General Tea Chamrat and members of the delegation, expressed his thanks for the solemn reception by the Vietnamese National Defense Ministry. He also said he was confident the visit will have successful results. In the afternoon, the high-level military delegation of the Kingdom of Cambodia held a meeting with a high-level Vietnamese military delegation.
