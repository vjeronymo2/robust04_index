Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Supreme Soviet Chairman Mechyslaw Hryb has signed today the Constitution of the sovereign and independent Republic of Belarus. We would like to note that the parliament finally adopted our basic law on 15 March. This was a historical day for the Republic and was pronounced a holiday. Following the signing ceremony, the Supreme Soviet chairman held a news conference. Mechyslaw Ivanavich Hryb responded to journalists' questions and briefed them on his visit to Paris where he participated in a debate of the Interparliamentary Assembly.
