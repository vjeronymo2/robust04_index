Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, March 16 (SNARK)--"Georgia will never say "yes" to the Abkhazia's separation from Georgia. Abkhazians won the war, but it's the temporary victory", Valerian Advadze, Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Ambassador of Georgia to Russia, told yesterday March 15 at his press-conference in Moscow. According to Valerian Advadze, the danger of separatism exists not only for Georgia but also for the other countries of the world. "The separatism in Abkhazia should be curbed, otherwise it will bring to civil wars". So, the Russian Federation should support Georgia otherwise Russia will solve the same problems. Mr. Advadze considers the visit of the Georgian leader Eduard Shevardnadze to the UN "very successful". The main aim of the visit was to convince world community of the necessity to introduce the peace-making forces into the zone of the conflict, to get the economical aid and political support of the Shevardnadze's course.
