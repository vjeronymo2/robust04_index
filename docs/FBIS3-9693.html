Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Ljubljana, 28 Mar (TANJUG) -- Around 10,000 supporters of compromised Slovene Defense Minister Janez Jansa continued their rally in support of Jansa in front of the parliament building in Ljubljana this evening. Surrounded by a police cordon, angry demonstrators are demanding the removal of President Milan Kucan and Prime Minister Janez Drnovsek. Jansa's supporters this evening started to collect signatures for a petition demanding that the parliament not dismiss Jansa. Serious threats were voiced at the expense of those who see Slovenia's future without Jansa and disagree with his political guidelines. [passage omitted]
