910504 FT 04 MAY 91 / Finance & The Family: House prices fall in April House prices dropped by 0.6 per cent in April, according to the index produced by the Nationwide Anglia Building Society. The index has now fallen by 8.5 per cent over the past year, with the average house price now Pounds 57,731, compared with Pounds 63,085 last April. Nationwide still expects house prices to rise by 5 per cent this year. Philip Coggan
