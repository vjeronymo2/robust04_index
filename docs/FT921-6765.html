920226 FT 26 FEB 92 / Malta's EC membership target Maltese entry into the EC is the top priority for the island's new Christian Democratic government which won a second five-year term at the weekend, Mr Eddie Fenech Adami, the prime minister, said yesterday, writes Godfrey Grima in Valletta. Mr Fenech Adami's Nationalist Party won an absolute majority of 51.8 per cent of the vote to secure a three-seat parliamentary majority. The Labour Party, which opposes full EC membership, saw its share of the vote slip to 46.5 per cent.
