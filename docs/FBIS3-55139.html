Language: Russian Article Type:BFN ["Directive of the Russian Federation President on Measures To Assist the Activity of the Russia-U.S. Enterprise Fund," signed by President B. Yeltsin, and dated 27 December 1993, No. 783-rp] [Text] In connection with the creation of the Russia-U.S. Enterprise Fund (hereafter -- the Fund), whose aim is to assist the development of private business in Russia: 1. The Russian Federation Council of Ministers-Government is to offer the Fund every help in its activity. 2. The Russian State Committee for the Management of State Property is charged with coordinating work to assist the Fund's activity, liaising with the Russian Ministry of Finance and the Russian State Committee for Antimonopoly Policy and Support of the New Economic Structures. 3. The Russian State Committee for the Management of State Property is to resolve the question of granting the Fund premises on lease terms in the city of Moscow and in one city in the Far East region (to be suggested by the Fund) as official premises. [Signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin [Dated] 27 December 1993, No. 783-rp
