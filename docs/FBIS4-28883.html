BFN ["Day of Portugal Observed" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, June 10 (KCNA) -- Papers here today observe the day of Portugal. The Korean people wish the Portuguese people greater achievements in their endeavors for the prosperity and development of the country, MINJU CHOSON in a signed article says, and goes on: The Korean and Portuguese peoples have established bonds of friendship. The friendly and cooperative relations have grown stronger and developed after the two countries established diplomatic ties at ambassadorial level in April 1975. The Korean people believe that the relations between the two peoples will develop still further in the future, too, on the basis of the idea of independence, peace and friendship.
