Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "This Morning" newscast] [Text] After reaching important agreements with the Government of Nicaragua, the 3-80 Northern Front led by Jose Angel Talavera, alias El Chacal, on Thursday signed a document to demobilize its troops. The agreements reached between the rebels and the government, through the mediation of the Catholic Church and the CIAV [International Commission of Support and Verification]-OAS, establish the following main points: progressive disarmament of the 3-80 troops between 2 March and 8 April, demilitarization of the area, and former recontras joining the command posts of police headquarters in the municipalities of Jinotega and Nueva Segovia. Likewise, the document signed between the government and the rearmed rebels establishes various social and economic development programs in the area. The agreements were signed after nearly 6 hours of intense negotiations and several foiled attempts in recent days.
