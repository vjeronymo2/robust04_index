930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / People: Mersey Docks & Harbour Company Gordon Waddell (left) will become chairman of the MERSEY DOCKS & HARBOUR COMPANY when William Slater, chairman since 1987, retires on September 7. Waddell, who joined the board in April as a non-executive director, is chairman of Shanks & McEwan Group and the Fairway Group, and a non-executive director of a number of companies including Cadbury Schweppes.
