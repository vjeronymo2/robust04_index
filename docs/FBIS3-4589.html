Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The government welcomed today the agreement between North Korea and the United States on the schedule for nuclear inspections and expressed its expectation that this will help in the fundamental resolution [kunbonjogin haegyol] of the North Korean nuclear issue. In a comment released today, Foreign Ministry spokesman Chang Ki-ho welcomed that, as a result of North Korean-U.S. contacts, nuclear inspections are going to be realized and said that this is the result of the fact that the ROK and the United States have had close consultations and kept their invariable position. Spokesman Chang said: I consider this result the first step in resolving the nuclear issue [haekmunje haegyorui ilchajok chochoro pyonggahanda]. I hope this step will be helpful for the fundamental resolution of the North Korean nuclear issue.
