941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / UK Company News: H Young improves H Young Holdings, the marketing and distribution company, reported an 84 per cent advance in pre-tax profits from Pounds 787,000 to Pounds 1.45m for the year to September 30. Turnover increased 29 per cent to Pounds 42m (Pounds 32.7m) and after interest payable of Pounds 359,000 (Pounds 305,000) earnings came out at 5.5p (3p) per share. A final dividend of 2.4p (1.3p) is proposed for a 3.6p (3.3p) total.
