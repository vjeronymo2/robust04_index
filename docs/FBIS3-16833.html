Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The political bloc "Sevastopol-Crimea-Russia," which has been founded in Sevastopol, is in favor of Crimea's close political and economic unity with Russia, and has as its slogan "Our homeland is Russia" [Nasha rodina -- Rossiya]. Aleksandr Kruglov, a Crimean people's deputy, who has been elected the leader of the new association, told a RIA correspondent this by phone. According to Kruglov, the "Sevastopol-Crimea-Russia" bloc supports the Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov and his decree on holding a referendum on the Republic of Crimea's independence among the peninsula's population on 27 March.
