BFN [Statement issued by 'Abd-al-Rahman 'Ali al-Jifri, "vice chairman of the Presidential Council"; place and date not given] [Excerpt] [Passage omitted] The refusal by the Sanaa regime to accept all the resolutions and appeals of the world has been accompanied by moves on the battlefronts to launch successive attacks on our brave forces who are defending Aden, its vital installations, and the civilians from among the sons of our people in a foolish and amazing insistence they [Sanaa regime] continue to attempt impossible advances aiming to occupy the steadfast city. As I announce this to the world I would like to stress that the government of the Democratic Republic of Yemen has been asking for the necessity of convening an urgent meeting of the UN Security Council so that steps may be taken to put an end to the Sanaa regime's defiance of the resolutions of the Security Council and the appeals of the international community. I also appeal to the fraternal Arab and Muslim countries and the friendly countries, as well as the five permanent members of the Security Council, to take decisive measures with regard to the Sanaa regime which is rejecting everything and insisting on the logic of force, particularly after this has been demonstrated to everyone.
