BFN [Text] As has already been reported, Belarusian Prime Minister Vyachaslaw Kebich is on a short working visit to Austria. The intention of the visit is to sign a document securing a $100 million credit to our Republic from the Austrian Government. Those credits will be used to purchase Russian oil to meet Belarusian needs and fund its subsequent processing at the Novopolotsk Oil Refinery. It is planned that a [word indistinct] portion of the gasoline and other oil products will be sold on European markets, which will provide hard currency to repay the credit. Vyachaslaw Kebich held a prolonged meeting with Austrian Chancellor Franz Vranitzky. Also discussed at the negotiations was the issue of Belarus joining the GATT. Agreement was reached on all issues.
