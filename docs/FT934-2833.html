931211 FT 11 DEC 93 / UK Company News: New Zealand Trust The New Zealand Investment Trust reported net assets per share of 214.63p at October 31, against 130.73p 12 months earlier. In the year to end-October net revenue was Pounds 174,106, compared with Pounds 231,098. Earnings per share were 1.74p (2.31p). A proposed unchanged final dividend of 0.6p makes a maintained total of 2.1p.
