Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The meeting between Czech President Vaclav Havel and Indian Prime Minister Narasimba Rao scheduled to be held in Delhi today has not taken place. According to the president's spokesman Ladislav Spacek, the reason is the illness which has suddenly affected the Indian prime minister. The Czech president therefore continued his four-day state visit to India by sightseeing in the capital Delhi. [passage omitted]
