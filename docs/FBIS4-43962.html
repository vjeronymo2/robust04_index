BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, June 23 (A.A.) -- Regional Emergency Supra-Governor Unal Erkan said on Wednesday [22 June] that the separatist terrorist PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] organization was abducting children rather than the adults. `The terror organization has massacred 4,000 citizens in 10 years,' Erkan said. `A thousand of these have been women and children.' Erkan arrived in Mus to receive information on the problems of the region. After being briefed by Mus Governor Ahmet Refik Tekerek, he visited the mayor in his office. Proceeding to the Varto township, Erkan chatted with the residents. Addressing citizens gathered at a coffee house, Erkan said that they will eradicate the terror organization. `Do not let the collaborators of this organization survive. A single collaborator can bring turmoil to the whole place. Do inform the state whenever you see one,' he said. `The separatist organization is dragging children, not adults to mountains. It has so far massacred 4,000 people here. Is this the state they are promising?' He said. `The state will construct 12,000 houses in this region this year for the citizens fleeing terrorists,' he said.
