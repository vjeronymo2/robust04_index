940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / Kuwait 'paid over market price' Kuwait paid twice the market price for the US Santa Fe drilling company in 1981, the emirate's state Audit Bureau has told the parliament, Reuter reports from Kuwait. The bureau described Kuwait Petroleum Corp's (KPC) largest foreign acquisition as 'hasty and controversial', according to newspaper reports. The report on the US investments of the state-owned KPC said Santa Fe had made losses of Dollars 2.89bn (Pounds 1.9bn) between 1981 and 1990. It noted this exceeded the Dollars 2.5bn KPC had paid for the company.
