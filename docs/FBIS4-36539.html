BFN [Text] His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad received Lithuanian Parliament Speaker Cheslovas Yurshenas at the People's Palace at 1830 today. People's Assembly Speaker 'Abd-al-Qadir Qaddurah attended the session. Mr. Yurshenas conveyed a message from Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas underscoring the importance of increased ties between the two countries. His excellency the president thanked the envoy and asked him to convey his greetings to the Lithuanian president. Discussions during the meeting dealt with the Middle East and the Baltic. His excellency the president laid out to him the efforts being made to bring about a just and comprehensive peace to the region and the obstacles placed by Israel in the path of that objective. His excellency the president also clarified Syria's sound position on the peace process predicated on UN resolutions and international legitimacy. Mr. Yurshenas said his country wholly supports the implementation of UN Security Council resolutions and the restitution of Syrian and other Arab territory.
