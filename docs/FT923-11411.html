920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Bexbuild declines to Pounds 133,000 Bexbuild Developments, the USM-quoted property investor, recorded a pre-tax profit of Pounds 133,000 in the year to March 31, compared with Pounds 622,000. The profit was struck after Pounds 51,000 (Pounds 86,000) exceptional charges, being written off trading stocks, while the previous year included Pounds 469,000 realised surplus on property. Turnover came to Pounds 1.62m (Pounds 714,000). Earnings per share were 3p (8.6p) and the final dividend is 1.7p for a total of 2.7p (2.5p). Net asset value per share remained at 139p, after writing down property values by Pounds 189,000 and allowing for shares bought for cancellation.
