December 11, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: COLLEGE FOOTBALL

Two Ohio State football players, one of them starting defensive end Alonzo Spellman, will miss the Liberty Bowl for disciplinary reasons, a television station reported.

Ohio State defensive coordinator Bill Young told WBNS-TV that Spellman and a redshirt player he did not identify will not play in the bowl game against Air Force on Dec. 27 but declined to say why.
