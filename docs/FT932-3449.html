930614 FT 14 JUN 93 / International Company News: RH Macy R. H. Macy, the bankrupt New York-based department store group, made a Dollars 54m operating loss in its third quarter, to May 1, down from a Dollars 60m deficit in the same period last year, writes Nikki Tait in New York. The after-tax loss stood at Dollars 228m, but this includes 'reorganisation' items of Dollars 182m, related to store closures and other changes. The net loss in the same period of 1991 was Dollars 226m after reorganisation items of Dollars 166m.
