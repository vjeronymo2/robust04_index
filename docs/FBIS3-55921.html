Language: Lithuanian Article Type:BFN [Report by Gytis Vaidila: "Transit Without Permit"] [Text] As the National Defense Ministry has reported, at 2155 [1955 GMT] on 28 December, 101 Russian Fleet seamen, two warrant officers, and one third rank captain crossed the border check point in Turmantas [Lithuanian border crossing with Latvia] without a transit permit on a passenger train heading from Kaliningrad to St. Petersburg. This train stood for half an hour in Kybartai [Lithuanian border crossing with Kaliningrad Oblast]. According to the chief of the shift on duty there, border guards did not have enough time to check the entire train, and the Russian servicemen might have slipped across the border in this way.
