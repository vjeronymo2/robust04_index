BFN [Text] Macao, April 13 (XINHUA) -- Guo Dongpo, director of Xinhua News Agency Macao branch made a brief speech here today congratulating the 7th anniversary of the signing of the Sino- Portuguese Joint Declaration and hoping the Governments of China and Portugal to further their friendly cooperation on the Macao issue. Saying it a historic document with profound significance, Guo said that the Sino-Portuguese Joint Declaration has set a good example for handling questions between countries left over by history. He said, bilateral relations between the Governments of China and Portugal have reached a new historical stage following the signing of the joint declaration. The friendly relations of cooperation between the two countries have developed continuously and smoothly. Guo pointed out that experiences have shown that the Sino- Portuguese Joint Declaration has played a role of guaranteeing the smooth transition and stable development of Macao. He also expressed the hope that the Governments of China and Portugal will continue to implement the joint declaration conscientiously, in a bid to guarantee the smooth settlement of various matters in the transitional period, stable development and smooth transfer of power in Macao.
