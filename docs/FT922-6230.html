920526 FT 26 MAY 92 / Construction Contracts: Sorting office WALLIS WESTERN has been awarded a Pounds 4.2m contract by Royal Mail Property Holdings West to construct three buildings in Exeter, Devon. Work has already begun to build a 5,867 sq metres mechanised sorting office, a three-storey administration block covering 1,970 sq metres and a 665 sq metres stores building with a motor transport workshop.
