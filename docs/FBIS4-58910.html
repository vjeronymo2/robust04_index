BFN [Text] The talks between the prime minister, Mr. P.V. Narasimha Rao, and the U.S. President, Mr. Bill Clinton, on the 19th of this month in Washington are expected to cover a wide range of bilateral and international issues. While stating this, the External Affairs Ministry spokesman said in New Delhi today that substantial preparations are being made to make the visit successful. He said the visit will lead to enhanced cooperation between the two countries. Denying reports in a section of the press about the reported ultimatum or preconditions being laid down by the USA for the visit of the prime minister, the spokesman said the report is full of errors and distortions.
