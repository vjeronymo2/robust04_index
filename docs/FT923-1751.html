920921 FT 21 SEP 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 36m work awarded to Wiltshier WILTSHIER CONSTRUCTION GROUP has won new contracts totalling Pounds 36m during the month of August. The largest single project is a Pounds 16.6m fit-out for a major city finance house. Also in central London is a Pounds 8.8m fit-out of West Court in Holborn Bars for Prudential Development Management. Other major gains by the group include a Pounds 4.1m Sainsbury's supermarket to be built in East Grinstead, Sussex; a Pounds 1m primary school for Oxfordshire County Council in Abingdon and a Pounds 1.9m sheltered housing project for the Elder Cottage Hospital in Glasgow.
