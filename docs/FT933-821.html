930927 FT 27 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Slight fall at Breedon PRE-TAX profits at Breedon, the limestone quarrying and housebuilding group, slipped by Pounds 80,000 to Pounds 960,000 in the six months to July 31, while turnover edged up from Pounds 4.94m to Pounds 5.05m. Directors said Breedon had concentrated on high volume stone sales at the lower end of the quality market which had enabled it to prepare working faces in readiness for the industry moving out of recession. Indications were that house prices were stabilising, they added. The interim dividend is held at 1.75p, payable from earnings per share of 2.26p (2.45p).
