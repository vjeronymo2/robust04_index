941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Asahi Techno Vision - Contracts and ventures Asahi Techno Vision, wholly owned by Japan's Asahi Glass, is building a second glass panel manufacturing plant in Singapore at a cost of SDollars 300m (USDollars 202m). The plant will have an annual capacity of 13m panels and is due for completion by May 1995.
