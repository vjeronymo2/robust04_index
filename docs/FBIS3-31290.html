Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] A spokesman for the Tirana Court of Appeal reports that an appeal was held in Tirana on 15 February against the criminal convictions of defendants Qirjako Mihali, Pali Miska, Besnik Bekteshi, Vangjel Cerrava, Prokop Murra, Llambi Gegprifti, Muho Artunda, Hajredin Celiku, Foto Cami, and Lenka Cuko . As senior political and government leaders at the head of a dictatorship, by deliberately abusing their positions the defendants granted economic privileges to themselves and their families, thus causing serious economic damage to the state and the people's interests. For these reasons, under Articles 106 and 31, Paragraphs B and D of the Penal Code, the defendants were convicted as follows: Qirjako Mihali -- eight years of imprisonment; Pali Miska -- six; Besnik Bekteshi -- five; Vangjel Cerrava -- five; Prokop Murra -- five; Llambi Gegprifti -- seven; Muho Artunda -- five; Hajredin Celiku -- five; Foto Cami -- six; Lenka Cuko -- seven. They were also stripped of their decorations and honorary titles.
