940601 FT 01 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Shares had early gains pared and ended slightly weaker on balance on end-of-month portfolio adjustments. The IPC index of 37 top shares lost 1.68 points at 2,483.73. It rose to 2,498.74 initially before falling back due to technical resistance at the 2,500 level. Turnover amounted to 93.72m shares, of which 54m corresponded to the heavily weighted Telefonos de Mexico, whose 'A' and 'L' shares both ended up 0.19 per cent. In the broader market falling issues outscored advances by 46 to 38. Declining issues were led by Grupo Financiero Interacciones 'B' shares, down 9.21 per cent, followed by rival group Internacional BCP series, which slid 4.98 per cent, Autlan 'A' shares, off 4.61 per cent, and Apasco 'A' shares, 4.03 per cent lower.
