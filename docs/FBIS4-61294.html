BFN [Text] Russian First Deputy Defense Minister Andrey Kokoshin praised the results of his South Korean counterpart Yi Pyong-tae visit to Russia that ended Tuesday [3 May]. He told Interfax that the Republic of Korea was a very important partner for Russia in consolidating security in the Asian-Pacific region. He said that Russia's relations with the Republic of Korea were a major factor helping Russia enter what he called the Asian-Pacific integration system. The South Korean defense minister met with Kokoshin and Colonel General Mikhail Kolesnikov, chief of the General Staff, to discuss bilateral military political relations and cooperation in providing security in the region.
