BFN [Excerpt] Sofia, April 14 (BTA) -- The parliamentary Defence Industry Committee established that as a result of the closing down of academic institutes and the pensioning off of researchers from the institute of military technology with the Ministry of Defence who are directly involved in programmes concerning defence and national security, the military industry would have to face serious problems mobilization readiness would be impaired. The commission will present a statement to the government and the Academy of Sciences and will insist that urgent measures be taken to preserve Bulgaria's scientific potential. [passage omitted]
