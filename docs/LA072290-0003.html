July 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BRODERICK MURDERS

Betty Broderick is a pathetic, vindictive and psychotic ex-wife who deserves to be prosecuted to the full extent of the law, including the death penalty.

Not a soul should shed a tear for this cold-blooded and deranged killer. The only sympathy I feel is for her badgered and verbally abused children, who bore the brunt of her tantrums over the years.

Dan and Linda Broderick were never allowed to begin their new life together. And Betty stands to pay the price.

BRIAN J. BANCROFT

San Clemente
