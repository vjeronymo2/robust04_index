Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Rabat, Jan.07-(MAP)-Moroccan Premier Mohamed Karim Lamrani received Thursday Malian Minister of Foreign Affairs, Malians Abroad and African Integration Boubakar Keita who conveyed him a message from Malian President Oumar Konare to King Hassan II. During the audience, attended by Moroccan Foreign Minister Abdellatif Filali, the two sides reviewed the situation in the sub-region as well as the development problems and prospects of inter-African economic integration.
