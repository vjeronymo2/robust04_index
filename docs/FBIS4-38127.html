BFN [Text] Ahmad Shakir, president of the Society for the Advancement of Arab-French Relations, today arrived in Baghdad leading a delegation on a visit of several days to Iraq. In a statement to the IRAQI NEWS AGENCY, Shakir said the purpose of his visit is to hold talks with Iraqi officials on the prospects for cooperation between Iraq and France in all fields and means to promote this cooperation. He added that during the visit, the delegation will study the effects of the continued embargo and the Iraqi people's suffering as a result of the acute shortage in food, medicine, and other essential supplies. He stressed that the French people have began to understand the Iraqi people's suffering as a result of the blockade. The delegation comprises Senator (Jean Jacques), deputy chairman of the French Senate's Iraq-France Relations Committee, and a number of French businessmen.
