Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [From the "Diplomatic Report" column by Luiz Orlando Carneiro] [Text] The Chamber of Deputies has authorized the executive branch to send an infantry battalion to Mozambique to join the UN peacekeeping force in that country. Deputy Joao Fagundes (Brazilian Democratic Party Movement- Roraima), who acted as rapporteur for the Constitution and Justice Committee, bears the rank of colonel in the Army engineering corps. Fagundes stated that Brazil cannot fail to participate in this type of mission, which is internationally significant and would show that Brazilian servicemen "are not only prepared for war but also to engage in the permanent battles for peace."
