June 25, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

IN DEFENSE OF CABO

As frequent visitors to Cabo San Lucas, our experience has been totally different from criticism that appeared in your Letters section recently.

Prices are not sky high in most places. We were there in May and had many delicious meals for under $10. Cabo is still a place of great natural beauty with a superior climate and a picturesque bay, crystal-clear water and clean, uncrowded beaches. It's so inviting and romantic that we can't wait for our next trip.

HARLEY and KAY PATTISON

Santa Monica
