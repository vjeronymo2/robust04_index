Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over still of Interior Minister Nahit Mentese] [Text] Interior Minister Nahit Mentese has announced that during operations conducted throughout the country from 1 to 23 January, 197 terrorists were killed and 707 were captured. In a statement issued today, Mentese reported that of these terrorists, 176 were killed and 422 captured in the state of emergency region. Mentese said that during these operations, security forces seized 169 rifles, 63 pistols of various calibers, 268 bombs, and 7,224 rounds of ammunition. Stressing that in no country in the world have terrorist organizations overpowered the state forces, Mentese remarked that in Turkey, too, terrorist organizations will be buried in the dark pages of history as a result of the determined and dedicated struggle waged by security forces. The interior minister said that the state is the counterpart of movements directed against the country's integrity and the principles of the republic, regardless of where these movements originate from. These movements will be stopped and the matter will be resolved with speed, Mentese added. He declared: We will overcome this nuisance of terrorism with the help of our great people and we will certainly bring about an atmosphere of tranquillity and security, which our people long for.
