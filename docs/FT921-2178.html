920320 FT 20 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Recovery continues at World of Leather Following its return to profits in the first half, World of Leather, the USM-quoted furniture retailer, reported a further advance in the rest of the year to December 31 ending with pre-tax profits of Pounds 115,000, against losses of Pounds 823,000. Earnings per share were 0.9p (10.5p losses) but the dividend is again passed. Turnover was static at Pounds 24.6m despite the sale of two units cutting the number of stores to 27.
