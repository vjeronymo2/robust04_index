940507 FT 07 MAY 94 / UK Company News: JO Walker in black with Pounds 61,634 For the year ended December 31 JO Walker, the timber importer, achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 61,634. The figure took account of second half losses of Pounds 100,366 and an exceptional credit of Pounds 226,484, being the maturity of an endowment policy. The profit compared with losses for 1992 of Pounds 402,033. Turnover rose from Pounds 12.16m to Pounds 14.22m over the 12 months. Earnings per share were 7.7p, against 37.4p losses and again there is no dividend.
