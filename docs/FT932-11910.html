930430 FT 30 APR 93 / UK Company News: Singer & Friedlander buys Lloyd's broker Singer & Friedlander, the merchant banker and property group, has acquired Edgar Hamilton Group, the holding company of Edgar Hamilton, an established Lloyd's broker. Singer paid Pounds 2.74m cash for the convertible preference share capital of EHG, which carries the right to convert into 35 per cent of the ordinary equity. Singer will acquire the ordinary capital over the next eight years at a price determined on profits, subject to a maximum Pounds 17.4m cash. For the 1992 year EHG returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 872,000. Net assets at the balance sheet date were in excess of Pounds 2m.
