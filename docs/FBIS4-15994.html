BFN [Text] Kiev, 12 Apr -- According to the Election-94 press center, 339 deputies have been elected after two rounds of elections to the Supreme Council of Ukraine. The election revealed a high turnout: around 76 percent in the first round, and 70 percent in the second. The largest number of seats in parliament was won by the Communist Party of Ukraine (75) and the People's Movement of Ukraine [Rukh] (26).
