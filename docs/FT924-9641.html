921104 FT 04 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Hungarian Inv lapses into losses Hungarian Investment Company, the fund managed by John Govett which was floated in London in February 1990, reported net losses of Dollars 5,128 (Pounds 3,146) for the six months to August 31. Losses per share amounted to 0.5 cents. In the previous six months to February 29 there were net profits of Dollars 235,026 for earnings per share of 23.5 cents. Net asset value per share was Dollars 106.35 (Dollars 103.85).
