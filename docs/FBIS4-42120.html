BFN [Text] Government Spokesman Evangelos Venizelos today repeated Greece's desire to improve its relations with Albania and stressed that this improvement inevitably depends on respect for the rights of the Greeks of Albania. Venizelos stressed: We have legal political and diplomatic means at our disposal which we will use if we find ourselves in a position to do so. The international community and Albanian Government know very well what these means are, he said. The spokesman made the above statement when he was asked by correspondents in Thessaloniki to clarify Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou's statement made on Mega Channel to the effect that there are other measures our country can take if the Albanian side continues with the same stance.
