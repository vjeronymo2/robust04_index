930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / Commodities and Agriculture: Tailings plant to boost Chilean copper output CHILE COPPER Corporation's (Codelco) Chuquicamata, the world's largest copper mine, has started processing tailings which should boost output by 50,000 tonnes a year, reports Reuter from Santiago. A new tailings plant came on line yesterday and will reach full capacity by the end of March. Processing tailings, which have accumulated for decades, is one of Codelco's most lucrative projects, according to chairman Alejandro Noemi.
