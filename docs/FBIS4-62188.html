BFN [Report by AZERTAJ correspondent Gekchekhaunum Tagiyeva for TASS] [Text] Baku 12 May TASS -- Azerbaijani troops have been engaged in bloody fights for the settlements of Seisulan and Gapanly (the Tertery district, Azerbaijan) for more than 24 hours. These settlements were occupied by Armenian forces on Wednesday [11 May]. According to the Azerbaijani Defence Ministry, its troops managed to drive the enemy out of Gapanly by the end of the day on Wednesday. By 1200 on Thursday, Azerbaijani troops managed to force their way to the southern outskirts of Seisulan. Armenian troops continued to shell Azerbaijani defences in the Geranboi and Kelbajar districts, the ministry said.
