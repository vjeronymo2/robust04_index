BFN [Russian Federation Presidential Directive No. 300-d, signed by President Yeltsin and dated 14 June 1994] [Text] A commission to draw up a draft bilateral treaty on the demarcation of areas of responsibility [predmety vedeniya] and the reciprocal delegation of powers between organs of state power in the Russian Federation and the Republic of Bashkortostan is to be set up with the following members: S.M. Shakhray, deputy chairman of the Russian Federation Government, commission leader; R.G. Abdulatipov, deputy chairman of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly Federation Council (by agreement) [po soglasovaniyu]; S.A. Shapovalov, chairman of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly State Duma Committee for Federation Affairs and Regional Policy (by agreement); A.G. Shapovalyants, Russian Federation deputy minister of economics; A.M. Pozdnyakov, Russian Federation deputy minister for nationalities affairs and regional policy; A.V. Maslov, deputy head of the Russian Federation president's State Legal Administration; A.P. Kozlov, head of the Russian Federation Government Apparatus's Department for Cooperation with Russian Federation Regions; V.A. Pechenev, deputy head of the Russian Federation Presidential Staff Administration for Work with the Territories. [Signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin [Dated] 14 June 1994 No. 300-d
