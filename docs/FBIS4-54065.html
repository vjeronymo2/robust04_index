BFN [Excerpt] Vienna, May 25 (AFP) -- Talks have begun between North Korean officials and inspectors from the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) on the refuelling of a controversial nuclear reactor, the IAEA announced Wednesday [25 May]. IAEA spokesman Hans-Friedrich Meyer said the talks being held in the North Korean capital of Pyongyang would continue Thursday. The IAEA delegation to the talks is made up of two special envoys who arrived in North Korea Tuesday and two inspectors who arrived there May 17. Meyer would not disclose who was representing the North Korean government at the talks. [passage omitted]
