Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bus loads of ANC supporters were taken to Curries Fountain in Durban this morning for the march through the city center that got underway a short while ago. Our reporters on the scene say the atmosphere is peaceful but that police are maintaining a strong presence and the area had been cordoned off. Shops in central Durban have been closed since 10 AM [0800 GMT] and workers have been sent home. The ANC has said the purpose of the march is to call for free political activity, especially in areas under the administration of the kwaZulu government.
