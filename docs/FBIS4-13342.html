BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 19 Apr (MENA)--The six members of the Egyptian Embassy staff in Rwanda returned to Cairo this evening headed by Ambassador Samih Darwish on an Egyptian plane coming from Nairobi. The Egyptian Embassy has temporarily closed down in the Rwandan capital, Kigali, after the civil war there intensified between the Hutu and Tutsi tribes following the deaths of the Rwandan and Burundian presidents in the airplane incident on 6 April.
