930305 FT 05 MAR 93 / International Company News: Quarter of Argentaria to go on sale ARGENTARIA, the state-owned Spanish banking corporation, has outlined plans for an international share sale through which up to a quarter of the group will be sold. In a similar move, Repsol, the Spanish energy group, intends raising nearly Dollars 1bn by early next month. Argentaria said yesterday its shares would be offered to retail investors in Spain and to institutional investors through five geographical tranches. Morgan Stanley will co-ordinate the international sale. The Spanish tranche will be spread between Argentaria, Banco Bilbao Vizcaya, Banco Central Hispano and Banco Santander de Negocios. UK tranches will be led by SG Warburg Securities and BSN, for Continental Europe by Union Bank of Switzerland, and for the the rest of the world by Merrill Lynch.
