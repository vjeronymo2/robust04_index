Language: Tagalog Article Type:BFN [Text] The chairman of the government peace panel negotiating with the Moro National Liberation Front [MNLF] said the bombing incident in Davao City will not affect the peace talks. Ambassador Manuel Yan said the joint ceasefire committee headed by MNLF Chairman Nur Misuari and Local Governments Undersecretary Alexander Aguirre will continue its discussions to formulate an interim ceasefire agreement in Zamboanga City. The committee will resume in their discussions tomorrow.
