Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [HINA report: "Josip Manolic Removed as Member of the Croatian Democratic Community Presidency and member of the Central Committee of the Croatian Democratic Community"] [Text] Zagreb -- On the basis of Article 2, Paragraph 33, and in accordance with Article 3, Paragraph 11 of the Croatian Democratic Community [HDZ] Statute, and on the recommendation of the HDZ Presidency and Main Secretariat, HDZ President Franjo Tudjman passed a decision stripping Mr. Josip Manolic of his duties as a member of the HDZ Presidency and a member of the HDZ Central Committee because of his repeated public disagreement with the policy of the HDZ as expressed in Croatian state policy, which contributed to the strengthening of Croatia's international position and internal democratic organization. This decision is also a request for the initiation of legal proceedings before the appropriate court of honor. The decision is effective immediately. This statement was issued by the HDZ on Thursday [24 Mar] afternoon.
