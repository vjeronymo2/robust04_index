Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Leonid Bagdatiev] [Text] Perm March 13 TASS -- Former Russian Deputy Prime Minister and Economics Minister Yegor Gaydar does not rule out the possibility of his "third advent" to the Russian Government, Gaydar told journalists in the Perm airport late on Sunday. He arrived in Perm at the invitation of Perm Region Governor Boris Kuznetsov. "I firmly believe Russia's main problem now is linked with the slowing down of the social life transformation process", Gaydar said. "I am convinced the ongoing transformations can be given a new impetus. I am ready to work on this in the government when there are political preconditions for it", Gaydar said. Gaydar plans to meet on Monday with the regional administration and entrepreneur's to discuss the situation in Russia, prospects for its development as well as the most acute problems the region and the whole country are facing. Gaydar also plans to visit the school his grandmother went to and to acquaint himself with the Perm period of his grandfather's -- renowned Russian writer Arkadiy Gaydar -- life.
