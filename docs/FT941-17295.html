940105 FT 05 JAN 94 / Letters to the Editor: Lottery - a route to riches, and honours Sir, Perusal of the new year honours list, as with previous lists, reveals a number of names which appear to have been chosen at random. Formalising this by inclusion of honours as prizes in Britain's national lottery would do much to ensure its success. Les Pook, 21 Woodside Road, Sevenoaks, Kent TN13 3HF
