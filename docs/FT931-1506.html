930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / International Company News: Shanghai Vacuum profits surge SHANGHAI Vacuum, the first Chinese company to offer shares to foreigners, reports a 311 per cent leap in attributable profits on doubled turnover for the year to December, writes Tony Walker in Beijing. But the surge was due to an increase in 'investment income', it said, not to a boom in its business of manufacturing tubes for televisions. Investors marked down its B shares by 5 US cents to 45 cents yesterday.
