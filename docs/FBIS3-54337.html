Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 25 Jan (MENA) -- Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and his accompanying delegation arrived in Cairo this afternoon. This is his third visit to Egypt within a week. In an arrival statement, 'Arafat described his meeting in Riyadh yesterday with King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, custodian of the two holy mosques, as "important, positive, and wide-ranging." 'Arafat said he thanked King Fahd for the support given by Saudi Arabia to the Palestinian people and their cause on various regional, Arab, and international forums. 'Arafat praised the principled and unshifting position of King Fahd and Saudi Arabia concerning the issue of Jerusalem, the capital of Palestine.
