August 20, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

MOVIES TO TAPE

Paper Moon (Channel 11 Sunday at 4 p.m.): Peter Bogdanovich's delightful Depression-era comedy, with Ryan O'Neal as a con man who latches on to a smart-alecky -- and Oscar-winning Tatum O'Neal. (2:00)

Gigi (Channel 13 Monday at 11:30 a.m.): Vincente Minnelli's elegant belle epoque musical, written by Lerner and Loewe and starring Leslie Caron as Colette's young heroine, being groomed as a grand courtesan. (2:30)

Klondike Annie (Channel 5 Friday at 4 a.m.): Vintage Mae West, in which she's on the lam in Alaska posing as an evangelist. Co-starring Victor McLaglen, whom she tells, "You're no earl paintin,' but you're a fascinatin' monster." (1:30)

Under Capricorn (Channel 11 Saturday at 3 a.m.): Highly experimental and moody Hitchcock period piece, set in Australia and starring Ingrid Bergman and Joseph Cotten. (2:30)
