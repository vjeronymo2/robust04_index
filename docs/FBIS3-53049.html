Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Bank of Israel will assist the Central Bank of Kenya as part of an agreement reached in Jerusalem this morning. This was worked out by the governors of the two banks, Israel's Ya'aqov Frenkel, and Kenya's (Mika Hezram). Aid will be given in market development and foreign currency transactions, as well as other related monetary matters. The meeting of the governors followed talks between Israel's prime minister and the visiting president of Kenya.
