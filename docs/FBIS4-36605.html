BFN [Excerpt] Lieutenant General Hassan 'Abd-al-Rahman Ali, the defense minister, has stated that the recapture of the town of Kajo Kaji yesterday marked the beginning of the end of the rebel movement. His excellency said: Our people's Armed Forces are now at the outskirts of the town of Nimule, the recapture of which will be announced soon. The defense minister was speaking yesterday to thousands of citizens. They had gathered in front of the general headquarters of the people's Armed Forces directly after hearing the news of the recapture and cleansing of the town of Kajo Kaji from the filth of the outlaws and that the armed and Popular Defense Forces were at a maximal state of readiness to defend Sudan and to restore every inch of its territory which had been usurped.
