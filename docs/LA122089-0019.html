December 20, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORE MEDFLY SPRAYING

This continuous spraying of our population with poisonous substances is like killing the chickens in order to keep the coop clean!

Granted, the Medfly infestation is a serious problem. But if we possess the technology to land men on the moon, surely we can devise a less-hazardous method of eradication. Any politician supporting aerial spraying of malathion will certainly not get my vote.

VIVO BENNETT

Los Angeles
