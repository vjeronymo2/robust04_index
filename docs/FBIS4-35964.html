BFN [Text] Salah al-Din resort town--Brother Struggler Mas'ud Barzani, leader of our Kurdistan Democratic Party [KDP], today, 4 June 1994, received at the Political Bureau headquarters in Salah al-Din resort town, a delegation from the Kurdish Democratic Party of Iran [KDPI] led by 'Abdallah Hasan Zadah, deputy secretary general and political bureau member of the KDPI. Discussions during the meeting dealt with the current situation in Kurdistan and our people's efforts to restore normal conditions. The meeting was attended by Sami 'Abd-al-Rahman and 'Izz-al-Din Barwari, members of the KDP political bureau; and (Faransu Hariri), KDP Central Committee member and official in charge of national relations.
