January 5, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

CBS READY FOR S.F. EMERGENCY

CBS plans to have an emergency generator at Candlestick Park in San Francisco for Saturday's game between the 49ers and the Minnesota Vikings just in case there is another earthquake.

A deadly 7.1 earthquake rocked the San Francisco Bay Area on Oct. 17, 31 minutes before the scheduled start of Game 3 of the World Series in Candlestick. ABC, which was broadcasting the baseball game, was knocked off the air and needed to borrow a generator from ESPN.
