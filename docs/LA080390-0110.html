August 3, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Eric Dickerson, who once threatened to retire rather than play for the Indianapolis Colts, now says he is ready to return to the NFL, even if it means remaining a Colt.

Dickerson, expected to make $1.45 million this year with an option year left on his contract, will not report to camp until his dispute with the Colts is resolved.
