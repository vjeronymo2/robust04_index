BFN [All quotation marks as received] [Text] Phnom Penh 18 Jun (AKP) -- At the invitation of Sukarno, president of the Republic of Indonesia, a high-ranking delegation of the Royal Government of Cambodia [RGC], will leave for Indonesia for an official three-day visit" according to a communique made public in Phnom Penh on 17 June. "The high-ranking Cambodian delegation will be led by Prince Krompreah Norodom Ranariddh and Samdech Hun Sen, RGC first and second prime ministers. "This visit will take place from 20 to 22 June," specifies the communique.
