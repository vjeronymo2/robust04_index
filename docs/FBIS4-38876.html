BFN [Item from column of ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA/ITAR-TASS correspondent reports: "Conference in Khabarovsk"] [Text] The role and tasks of the representatives of the Russian Federation president and the organs of state power in the realization of the Treaty on Social Accord at the level of federation components are being discussed at a zonal conference in Khabarovsk. The Russian Federation president's representatives in krays and oblasts of the Far East and Eastern Siberia have gathered here. Conference participants note that the current situation dictates the need to amend the activity of the president's representatives. The functional obligations of all organs of power at federal and regional levels must be clearly defined so that they work in harmony, without obstructing one another.
