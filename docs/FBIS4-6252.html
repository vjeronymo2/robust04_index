BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 19 (KCNA) -- A friendly gathering between officials of the Korea-Ireland Friendship Association and the visiting chairman of the Ireland-Korea Friendship Association took place in Pyongyang on April 18. Present there were Sean Garland, chairman of the Ireland-Korea Friendship Association, and Pat Quearney, general secretary of the Workers' Party of Ireland. Kim Yong-nam, vice-chairman of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea and chairman of the Korea-Ireland Friendship Association, and officials concerned were present. The participants had a conversation in a friendly atmosphere.
