930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / UK Company News: BLP BLP, the USM-quoted maker of wood laminates, veneers and mouldings, cut pre-tax losses from a restated Pounds 14.8m to Pounds 559,000 for 1992, including exceptional costs of Pounds 260,000 relating to the mandatory offer by BLP (Jersey). The result last time included an exceptional Pounds 12.2m loss on the disposal of Berg Mantelprofilwerk in June 1991. Turnover fell to Pounds 25m against Pounds 38m, which included Pounds 16m for discontinued operations. Losses per share were reduced to 14.7p (215.1p).
