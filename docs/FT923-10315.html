920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / Observer: Dying fall For all his knowledge about original sin, Bishop Michael Marshall has been snared by a sin whose origins he wot not of. As a result, he has arrived home from running an Anglican mission in the US without his treasured piano. On applying to Washington for an export licence for the instrument, he was told that since it had ivory keys, he must specify where the sin of killing the elephant they came from had been committed. As he didn't know, the licence was refused. And when his secretary protested, the bureaucracy's only reply was: 'The bishop should not have bought a piano with ivory keys.'
