Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Almaty, January 11 (XINHUA) -- Kazakhstan's Defense Minister Sagadat Nurmagambetov is heading a military delegation to pay a four-day visit to China. Upon his departure at the airport, Nurmagambetov told reporters that his visit would boost friendly relations between the two countries' armed forces. This is the first visit to China by a Kazakhstan military delegation since the establishment of diplomatic ties between the two countries in January 1992.
