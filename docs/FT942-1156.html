940625 FT 25 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Unilever in Cuban joint venture Unilever has formed a 50-50 joint venture to make and market detergents and toiletries in Cuba. Manufacture will be undertaken by Suchel, the Cuban state-owned company, with technical help from Unilever. Brands in the venture will include Omo detergent, though not in its controversial new 'Power' formulation. The agreement, involving three of Suchel's seven factories, is described as an equal partnership in operational terms. Profits will be split on a 50-50 basis.
