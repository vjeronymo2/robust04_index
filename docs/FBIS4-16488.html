BFN [Text] Moscow, 16 Apr (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin signed an edict on Friday instructing the Russian Government to hold consultations with representatives of the state authorities and all the political movements of the Chechen Republic to find a way out of the existing sociopolitical situation. On the basis of these consultations, a draft treaty will be drawn up on demarcating areas of jurisdiction between the federal state authorities and the Chechen authorities and on a mutual delegating of powers.
