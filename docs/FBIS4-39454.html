BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Chistov] [Summary] Brasilia, 8 Jun -- Russia has signed a memorandum of mutual understanding with the Organization of American States (OAS). Russia has been a permanent observer with the OAS since 1982. The memorandum sets the guidelines for cooperation between Russia and the OAS. Russia Deputy Foreign Minister Georgiy Mamedov, who signed the memorandum on Russia's behalf, also delivered to the OAS general secretary, Juao Clemente Baena Soares, a message from the Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev.
