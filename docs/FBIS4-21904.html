BFN [Text] The chief minister of Uttar Pradesh [U.P.], Mr. Mulayam Singh Yadav, today escaped an attempt on his life in Lucknow when one person attacked him. An official spokesman told our Lucknow correspondent that the chief minister was meeting the public at his residence. Security personnel nabbed the assailant when he rushed toward Mr. Mulayam Singh with a knife in his hands. The culprit said he belonged to Pune. Interrogations are continuing. The U.P. Congress-I has strongly criticized the incident.
