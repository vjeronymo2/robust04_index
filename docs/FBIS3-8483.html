Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Economic Info"] [Text] According to the State Statistics Institute, the January industrial production in Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], as compared with last year's monthly production figures, has decreased in average by 19 percent. The production of manufacturing machinery is marking the biggest fall of 37.5 percent, followed by the production of reproduction materials (8 percent) and the consumer goods (2.4 percent). The January prices of the industrial products have gone up by 13.1 percent, the market prices by 22 percent and the living costs by 18.8 percent.
