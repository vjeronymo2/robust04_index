Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] Girts Kristovskis, minister of internal affairs of Latvia, and Heiki Arike, Estonian minister of the interior, discussed various problems concerning interests of the police and inhabitants of the two countries at the Latvian Ministry of Internal Affairs today. During the meeting the ministers acknowledged that it was necessary to work out a unified policy of the Interior Ministries of the Baltic states at conferences of the Interior Ministries of the CIS member states, in which representatives of the Baltic states are involved only as observers. The ministers also discussed problems related to the activities of the Russian army in Latvia and Estonia, as well as other issues.
