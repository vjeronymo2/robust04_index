Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Lithuanian Seimas has urged the parliaments of Serbia, Bosnia-Herzegovina, and Croatia to do everything they can to stop the killing in the territory of the former Yugoslavia. On Tuesday the Lithuanian parliament made a statement condemning the act of terrorism carried out in Sarajevo on 5 February, when tens of civilians were killed in the capital city of Bosnia-Herzegovina. The Lithuanian parliament has also voiced support for the efforts of the United Nations, NATO, and other international organizations to normalize the situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina.
