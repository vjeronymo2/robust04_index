920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / Observer: Courting Guess who British Aerospace has invited to Wimbledon this weekend? None other than German defence minister Volker Ruhe - he who pulled the plug on his country's involvement in the Eurofighter project. Is BAe throwing good money after bad? Or is this a last-ditch try at salvaging its most important military contract? BAe's increasingly poor shareholders deserve to be told.
