920917 FT 17 SEP 92 / International Company News: Schindler lifts revenues 6.6% SCHINDLER, the Swiss elevator and rolling stock group, said its consolidated revenues rose 6.6 per cent in the first half to SFr2.1bn, (Dollars 1.68bn) and forecast that net profit for the full year would be the same or only slightly higher than last year's depressed SFr87.3m, writes Ian Rodger. Sales of elevators and escalators rose 7.7 per cent to SFr1.9bn while sales of rolling stock declined 7.4 per cent to SFr138m. The group's order backlog at June 30 remained at SFr3.1bn, unchanged from the end of last year.
