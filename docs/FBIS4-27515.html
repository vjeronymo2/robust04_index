BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 2 (KCNA) -- Activities to support and implement the "10-point Programme of the Great Unity of the Whole Nation for the Reunification of the Country" have been conducted vigorously within the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon), an authoritative organization of overseas citizens of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, in the last one year or a little more since the programme was laid down by the great leader Generalissimo Kim Il-song on April 6, 1993. A statement of the Central Standing Committee of Chongnyon and 14 press statements by Chongnyon Chairman Han Tok-su and personages of various strata supporting the 10-point programme were released immediately after the programme was published. Meetings to support and implement the 10-point programme have been held at 42 prefectural headquarters since the central meeting of the Korean residents in Japan. It was stressed in unison at the meetings that the 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation is a common reunification charter of the nation and a great political programme which makes it possible to tide over the present rigorous difficulties and achieve peace and reunification of the country by the united efforts of the whole nation. Rallies, study meetings, lectures and seminars were held widely by organizations of Chongnyon and 4,173 cases of mass rallies, street demonstrations, motorcade and bicycle parades were staged. 324,122 Korean residents in Japan participated in the signature campaign supporting the 10-point programme in only three months.
