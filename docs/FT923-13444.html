920714 FT 14 JUL 92 / Support for Smith THE overwhelming support for Mr John Smith and Mrs Margaret Beckett in Labour's leadership and deputy leadership elections this Saturday was highlighted in the list of 'supporting nominations'. Mr Smith has won the support of at least 163 MPs, 12 unions or other affiliated organisations, and 56 constituency organisations. Mr Bryan Gould, his rival for leader has received the public support of no unions, only 10 constituency parties and 62 MPs.
