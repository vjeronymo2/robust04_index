930128 FT 28 JAN 93 / Parliament and Politics: First reading for tobacco ads bill A BACKBENCH Bill to ban tobacco advertising was given a formal first reading by 206 votes to 61, a majority of 145, in the Commons yesterday. The Tobacco Advertising Bill, introduced by Mr Hugh Bayley, Labour MP for York, has all-party support but stands no chance of becoming law due to pressure on parliamentary time.
