910425 FT 25 APR 91 / International Company News: Sun Micro shares up on profit jump SHARES in Sun Microsystems, a maker of computer workstations, jumped by Dollars 1 1/4 to Dollars 33 1/2 after the California-based company reported a sharp advance in third-quarter profit to Dollars 58.7m after tax compared with Dollars 36.7m in the year-ago period, writes Nikki Tait. The company, which had sales of Dollars 848.3m in the three months to March 29, up from Dollars 632.2m, said that its operating margin improved by 2 1/2 percentage points from the second quarter, pushing this above 10 per cent. Earnings per share were up to 56 cents from 40 cents.
