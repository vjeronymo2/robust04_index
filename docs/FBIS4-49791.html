BFN [From the "National News Hookup" program] [Text] Li Ruihuan, chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference who is currently on a tour of Sweden, arrived by special airplane in Stockholm, the capital of Sweden, at midday 8 May local time. Greeting him at the airport were officials from the Swedish Foreign Ministry and the Chinese Embassy staff in Sweden. [video shows Li, accompanied by Chinese and Swedish officials, walking down the ramp from the airplane, cutting to show him shaking hands with well-wishers and leaving the airport in a limousine] In the afternoon, Chairman Li Ruihuan met with (B. Vromberg), a renowned Swedish entrepreneur in industrial, commercial, and financial circles. Chairman Li Ruihuan expressed his appreciation for Mr. (Vromberg's) foresight and judiciousness in developing economic relations with Chinese, as well as his long-term, unremitting efforts to develop Sino-Swedish friendly relations and cooperation. Li Ruihuan said: China's economy is developing in a sustained, rapid, and healthy manner. Sweden possesses ample experience and certain advantages to help China develop, on a priority basis, a variety of sectors, such as transportation, communications, energy, and raw and semifinished materials. I am convinced that through joint efforts by the two sides, Sweden will be able to make even greater progress in developing cooperation with some Chinese provinces and municipalities, as well as cooperation between enterprises in the two countries. In the evening, Chairman Li Ruihuan cordially met with local Chinese nationals and student representatives at the Chinese embassy. [video shows Li chatting with them in a well-lighted hall] During his tour of Gothenburg, Chairman Li Ruihuan visited Volvo Automobile Company, one of the 60 largest companies in the world.
