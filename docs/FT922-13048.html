920414 FT 14 APR 92 / Dillons launches fresh discounts DILLONS, the bookshop chain owned by the Pentos group, will today renew its attack on the net book agreement by launching a fresh discount campaign including paperbacks and previously-published hardbacks. The list of 25 titles which will attract a 25 per cent discount were chosen from imprints owned by Reed, which has decided to publish outside the net book agreement. The NBA is a voluntary publishers agreement on minimum retail prices.
