BFN [Text] Vladimir Zhirinovskiy intends to have the document on Russia's participation in the NATO PFP [Partnership for Peace] program annulled. According to the leader of the LDPR [Liberal Democratic Party of Russia], the document has no legal force. Zhirinovskiy again confirmed that he will demand the resignation of Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, in this case, for signing the framework document with NATO. Kozyrev had no right to sign this program since the State Duma was categorically against, Zhirinovskiy said.
