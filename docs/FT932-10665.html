930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Branches lift MMT Computing 22% The branch companies of MMT Computing helped to take up the slack of its London operations in the first half and the USM-quoted software house achieved another profits rise. On sales of Pounds 3.17m (Pounds 3.01m) from continuing operations the pre-tax figure increased 22 per cent from Pounds 712,000 to Pounds 865,000 in the six months to February 28. The interim dividend is lifted 14 per cent to 1.25p (1.1p). Earnings per share came through at 4.8p (3.9p) The share price added 6p to 115p.
