BFN [UKRINFORM report: "The President Has Confirmed His Intention"] [Text] The Ukrainian president met with the collective of KIYEVSKIYE VEDOMOSTI, familiarized himself with the technological process of preparing the newspaper, and answered journalists' questions. Leonid Kravchuk confirmed his intention not to take part in the presidential elections. He will stand up in the parliament for their postponement. The president expressed a hope that the new Supreme Council would support him. "This is not just my opinion. Dozens of local councils, hundreds of labor collectives, and some political parties spoke in favor of postponing the elections," stressed the head of the Ukrainian state. Touching upon the further operation of the Chernobyl Atomic Electric Power Plant, the president pointed out: All conditions have been created for its normal work. There can be no question of closing it down now because, first, there are no funds for that and, second, Ukraine has found itself in an energy crisis.
