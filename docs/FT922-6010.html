920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / International Company News: Avesta holding talks with British Steel AVESTA, the Swedish stainless steel group, has said it is holding talks with British Steel on possible co-operation, but it intended to remain an independent company, Reuters reports from Stockholm. Avesta said in a brief release through the Stockholm bourse that further information was expected within a few weeks. In 1991, Avesta made a loss of SKr248m (Dollars 43.5m) after financial items on turnover of SKr7.39bn.
