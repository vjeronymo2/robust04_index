Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 2 (KCNA) -- The mass gymnastic display "Great Victory of the Rural Theses" performed by 10,000 students and school children took place at the Pyongyang indoor stadium today on the occasion of the 30th anniversary of the publication of "Theses on the Socialist Rural Question in Our Country." The stadium was gorgeously decorated with letters "1964-1994" and "celebration of 30th anniversary," pictures depicting rich harvests and illumination. When the great leader President Kim Il-song appeared in the box amid the welcome music, enthusiastic cheers of "hurrah!" and thunderous applause resounded through the stadium. Kimilsongia and Kimchongilia and letters "Glory to You," "Agriculture Is the Great Foundation of the Country" and "Celebration of the 30th Anniversary of the Socialist Rural Theses" appeared on the background and waves of flowers rolled on the floor. Watching the performance were member of the presidium of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee [C.C.] of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] and Minister of People's Armed Forces O Chin-u, Politburo member of the C.C., the WPK and Premier of the Administration Council Kang Song-san, Politburo members of the C.C., the WPK and Vice-presidents Yi Chong-ok, Pak Song-chol and Kim Yong-chu and other party and state officials and the participants in the national agricultural conference. The display successfully showed on great epic canvases the imperishable feats performed by President Kim Il-song, who published a unique, great programme of socialist rural construction and set an example of the century in the solution to the rural question to build a prospering socialist countryside in this land and the happiness of the Korean agricultural working people, who are enjoying an independent and creative life thanks to the rural theses. It also demonstrated the unshakable will of our agricultural working people and all other people to finally solve the rural question and hasten the complete victory of socialism by remaining loyal to the party's leadership and thoroughly carrying out the tasks set by President Kim Il-song in his letter to the national agricultural conference. The performance made up of gymnastics, gymnastic dances, acrobatics and slide-background was acclaimed by the audience for its high ideological and artistic value and high technique, organization and discipline. President Kim Il-song congratulated the performers on their successful performance and waved to the cheering crowd.
