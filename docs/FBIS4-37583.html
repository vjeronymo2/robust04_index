BFN [Text] The third group of 300 Pakistani troops left Lahore today for the Slovak Republic for onward deployment in Bosnia on UN peacekeeping duties. At Lahore airport, the contingent was seen off by senior army officers. The group will have the requisite training in the Slovak Republic for a period of two weeks and will then take up duties in the war-torn Bosnia-Herzegovina. The remaining troops of the main body will directly fly to Bosnia under a phased program expected to be completed by the end of July.
