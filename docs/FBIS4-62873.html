CSO ["FAKT" report under the "Yesterday and Today" column: "And Still No Voters..."] [Text] The 15 May elections for vacant seats in the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Armenia were marked by extreme voter passivity. At electoral district No. N59 of the city of Yerevan at 1300 hours only 48 of the 4800 voters had voted, and at electoral district No. N13 only 561 of the 10,000 registered voters showed up. No elections took place in electoral district No. N8 of the city of Yerevan where only one candidate was registered, and he withdrew his candidacy a day before the elections.
