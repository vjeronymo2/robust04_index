BFN [Text] Tiraspol BASAPRESS, 9/6/1994 -- The press service of the president and government of the self-proclaimed Dniester Republic made yesterday, 8 June, an official statement concerning the negotiations on the granting of a special juridical status to the Dniester region. According to the statement, Igor Smirnov accuses Moldovan President Mircea Snegur of avoiding and slowing the negotiations. The Tiraspol authorities also deny any correlation between the solution of the Dniester dispute and the solution of the southern districts' [ethnic Gagauz'] problems. The press service reports that "Smirnov asked Vladlen Vasev, special envoy of the Russian president to the negotiations, to put pressure on President Snegur in order to force the latter to speed up negotiations."
