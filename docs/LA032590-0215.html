March 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

VIEWERS VIEWS

I have often wanted to write and tell a television producer exactly how I feel.

However, in the Feb. 25 edition of Viewers' Views, someone beat me to it. Bernice Bowers is the woman who commented about Deborah Norville's performance on "Today." I agree with her -- Deborah is a horrible replacement for the seasoned and even-natured Jane Pauley. I'm glad Bryant Gumbel is there to improve things.

Cheryl Bailey, Long Beach
