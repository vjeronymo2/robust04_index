Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Ivanov] [Text] Moscow February 10 TASS -- The draft budget for 1994 will be finalised in the near future, Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin said on Thursday at a meeting with representatives of the parliamentary bloc New Regional Policy, which has the second largest faction in the State Duma. The faction members who represent the interests of Russian regions brought up the problem of non-payments, state support for the Far North, inflation and the problem of refugees at the meeting. Chernomyrdin said that the draft budget for 1994 may envisage a "big deficit." He urged the enterprises to provide their guarantees that they will continue making vital products in the required volume. He stressed the need to foster closer "working" ties between the government and the Federal Assembly.
