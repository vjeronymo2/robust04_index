BFN [Text] Bonn, May 5 KYODO -- Japanese Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata arrived in Bonn from Paris on Thursday [5 May] afternoon on the third leg of his tour of four European countries. Hata will meet with German Chancellor Helmut Kohl later in the day to discuss trade and economic relations between the two countries, officials said. The prime minister has already visited Italy and France and will proceed to Belgium after Germany.
