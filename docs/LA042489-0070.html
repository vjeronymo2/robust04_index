April 24, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

The four head writers and co-executive producers of "The Golden Girls" are moving on to greener pastures. Kathy Speer, Terry Grossman, Mort Nathan and Barry Banaro have formed KTMB Productions and signed an exclusive agreement with Walt Disney Studios to develop and produce TV and feature film projects. All four are Emmy winners. Claudia Puig and ALEENE MacMINN
