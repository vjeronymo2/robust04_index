CSO ["Law of the Tatarstan Republic: On Endowing the President of the Tatarstan Republic With Additional Authority in Connection With the Socioeconomic Situation That Has Taken Shape in the Tatarstan Republic"] [Text] With the aim of enhancing the operational effectiveness of decisionmaking in the economy, of securing the further course of reforms in the Tatarstan Republic, and of strengthening social protection for the populace, and in accordance with Point 17 of Article 111 and Article 112 of the Constitution of the Tatarstan Republic, the Supreme Soviet of the Tatarstan Republic decrees: Article 1. To extend to the president of the Tatarstan Republic, until such time as appropriate laws regulating legal relations in the financial, economic, social, and administrative spheres of the Tatarstan Republic are adopted, the right to issue edicts with respect to the above-mentioned issues. Article 2. This law enters into force as of its date of publication and remains in effect until 1 April 1995. [Signed]President of the Tatarstan Republic M. Shaymiyev City of Kazan, 8 June 1994
