Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Wang Weijun 3769 5898 6511] [Text] Bangkok, 6 Jan (XINHUA) -- General Woranat Apichari, supreme commander of the Thai Armed Forces, held a banquet for Liu Huaqing, vice chairman of the Central Military Commission of the People's Republic of China, and his party this evening at Bangkok's Oriental Hotel. General Woranat spoke at the banquet. He congratulated Liu Huaqing for receiving an Order of the Knight, First Class, from the King of Thailand. He expressed the hope to further strengthen friendship and cooperation between the peoples and armed forces of Thailand and China in the future. In his speech, Liu Huaqing said that as an Asian nation, China sincerely hopes that the region will become one of stability, peace, and common development. He added that China is willing to make positive and unremitting efforts to achieve that goal together with other nations in the region. Liu Huaqing and his party arrived here today and began their six-day official goodwill visit to Thailand.
