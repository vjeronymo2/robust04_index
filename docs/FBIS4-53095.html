BFN [Text] Tokyo, May 16 KYODO -- Major Japanese electronics firm Hitachi Ltd. said Monday [16 May] it will establish an electronic device design subsidiary in britain next month to bolster its operations overseas. The Tokyo-based company said the new firm, Hitachi Micro Systems Europe Ltd., will engage in designing computer chips and support tools as well as development of application technologies. The company said the new undertaking followed a capital increase of 1.5 million dollars in April by Hitachi Micro Systems Inc., its large-scale integrated circuit design unit established in California in 1978. The company said it will continue to strengthen its design and development operations in the U.S. and Europe to meet the needs of local customers. The company has been producing semiconductors at its German subsidiary established in 1980. It said its production base in the U.S. will start manufacturing microcomputers this fall at a monthly volume of 300,000 units.
