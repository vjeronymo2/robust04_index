Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] At the initiative of the group of Agrarian Party deputies, the State Duma has advised the government to adopt a decision within the next five days on the functioning of the agro-industrial sector during the current year. The resolution was adopted by the State Duma after a second vote on this issue, taken after Yegor Gaydar, leader of the Russia's Choice group, called on his colleagues to approve the document, INTERFAX reports. The agrarian group circulated a statement on the state of affairs in the agro-industrial sector. This says that the state still owes farmers over 1 trillion rubles for produce purchased from them, including over 600 billion rubles for grain.
