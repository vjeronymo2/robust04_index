BFN [Editorial Report] Madrid EFE in Spanish 2129 GMT 23 April 94 reports that the 23 April edition of the newspaper EL UNIVERSO of Guayaquil reported that the Armed Forces sold 30 percent of the shares of the General Ruminahui Bank, which they own, to a private financial group. A few days ago, Defense Minister Jose Gallardo said military enterprises might be privatized.
