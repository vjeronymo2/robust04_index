July 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TRAVEL PROGRAMS

Travel in Malaysia will be the subject of an open forum on Thursday beginning at 9 a.m. at the Bel Age Hotel, 1020 N. San Vicente Blvd., West Hollywood.

The free program, jointly offered by the Malaysia Tourist Information Center and Malaysian Airlines, will feature speakers describing new air services within Malaysia, as well as the various festivals scheduled for the year ahead. There will also be a screening of the "Visit Malaysia Year 1990" film.

For reservations or more information, contact Scott Feinerman at (213) 854-1111 or the Malaysia Tourist Information Center at (213) 689-9702.

Photographing the wilderness will be the topic addressed by travel photographer and lecturer Mort Loveman on Wednesday at 1 p.m. at the Roxbury Park clubhouse, 471 S. Roxbury Drive, Beverly Hills. Admission is free. For more information, call (213) 550-4761.

Narrated travel slides of U.S. national parks will be shown on Monday at 1:15 p.m. at the Valley Storefront, 12821 Victory Blvd., North Hollywood. Sponsored by the Jewish Family Service of Los Angeles, the program is free. For additional information, call (818) 984-0276.
