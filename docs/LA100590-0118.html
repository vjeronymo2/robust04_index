October 5, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Guard Dale Ellis of the Seattle SuperSonics, convicted of drunk driving and reckless driving, was sentenced to a day in jail, fined $500 and ordered to attend a driving school and perform 60 hours of community service.

Seattle District Judge Laura Inveen also told Ellis, 30, he must appear before a panel of victims and admonished him for failing to set a good example for youngsters.
