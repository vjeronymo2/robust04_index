March 17, 1989, Friday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

WARY OF SOVIET ECONOMY: KISSINGER

Former Secretary of State Henry A. Kissinger warned businessmen today against investing in the Soviet Union, saying the country is suffering an economic crisis.

Kissinger also told a symposium on the Soviet Union that the West should maintain its military guard in case current economic reforms fail and Soviet leader Mikhail S. Gorbachev is ousted from office.
