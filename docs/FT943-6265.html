940830 FT 30 AUG 94 / World News in Brief: Israel hands more power to West Bank Palestinians Israeli Major General Danny Rothschild and Nabil Shaath, the chief Palestine Liberation Organisation negotiator, signed an accord transferring the control of education, taxation, health, welfare and tourism affairs in the occupied West Bank from Israeli to Palestinian hands. The deal is the most important step in the Israeli-PLO autonomy process since self-rule for the Palestinians of Gaza and Jericho came into effect four months ago.
