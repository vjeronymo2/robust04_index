August 19, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Odetics Earnings -- For the first quarter of fiscal 1990, Odetics Inc. reported net income of $36,000. The year-earlier figure was $438,000, which included a $400,000 credit from an accounting change. An earnings report in Friday's paper reversed the first quarter net incomes for fiscal 1990 and 1989.
