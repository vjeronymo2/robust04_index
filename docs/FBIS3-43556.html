Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulundi Jan 8 SAPA -- The Inkatha Freedom Party [IFP] announced on Saturday it will hold a special general conference at the end of this month. The central committee decided on Friday to hold the conference on January 29 and 30, according to a statement from the office of party leader Chief Mangosuthu Buthelezi. The IFP Central Committee said on Friday the organisation will not participate in forthcoming elections unless certain conditions are met. The decision has been criticised by the National Party and the African National Congress.
