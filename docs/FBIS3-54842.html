Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valery Shanayev] [Text] Vladikavkaz December 30 TASS -- The situation in the state of emergency zone of Northern Ossetia remained complicated in the last days of the outgoing year. According to military sources, a group of unidentified men repeatedly opened fire from automatic weapons and grenade-throwers on a customs post near the mountain village of Nizhny Lars, located on the Georgian military highway. Two agents of the North Ossetian Interior Ministry were wounded by grenade splinters. Seven night-time gun battles were fought in several settlements of Prigorodny district. No casualities were reported. According to information released by the investigation team of the Russian Interior Ministry, several submachine-guns, fourteen pistols, many grenades and explosive devices, some blank weapons, and hundreds of cartridges were discovered in a dwelling house in Malgobek district of the Ingush republic in the course of searches carried out there.
