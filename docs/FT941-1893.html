940323 FT 23 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Berry Starquest Berry Starquest, an investment trust, had a net asset value per share of 239.6p at the year ended January 31, against 200.1p six months earlier and 170.7p at the previous year end. Net revenue for the year fell from Pounds 160,000 to Pounds 126,000 for earnings per share of 2.4p (3.1p). An unchanged dividend of 2.2p is proposed.
