Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 16 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen said today that at present there is no possibility for him to meet with his British counterpart, Douglas Hurd. In response to a question raised by a Hong Kong reporter at a news conference hosted by a spokesman for the ongoing session of the Chinese parliament, Qian said that in the future there will be opportunities for a meeting with him, at least in the United Nations.
