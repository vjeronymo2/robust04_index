April 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

WORD OF HIS DEMISE WAS PREMATURE

Re: The "late" Lawrence Clark Powell.

In the April 8 View section you printed a letter to the editor that referred to the "late Lawrence Clark Powell, UCLA Librarian."

On Sunday, April 8, Lawrence Clark Powell was not only not dead, he was having a party -- attended by almost 1,000 -- at Singing Wind Bookshop in Benson, Ariz., to celebrate the publication of his latest book, "Southwest: Three Definitions."

LINDA CIVITELLO

Culver City
