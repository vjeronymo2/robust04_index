941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There was a good general demand, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Good liquoring Assams were again well supported at fully firm rates but plainer mediums tended easier. Bright east Africans were strong, opening firm to dearer although prices eased by the close. Coloury mediums and central Africans were fully firm but plainer sorts lost some ground. Offshore: Fair demand at firm rates. Quotations: best available 210p/kg, good 145p/kg, good medium 130p/kg, medium 117p/kg, low medium 88p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 215p/kg for an Assam pf.
