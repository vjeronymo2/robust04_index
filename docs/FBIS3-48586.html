Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] On the afternoon of 22 January, Comrade Nong Duc Manh, member of the Political Bureau and chairman of the National Assembly, received at the National Assembly Building a delegation of cadres in charge of village and city grass-roots establishments in Bac Son District, Lang Son Province. Chairman Nong Duc Manh was briefed by the comrade head delegate on the socioeconomic situations in all villages in the district. Next, two delegates -- one from the village that scores the best achievements and the other from the village that faces the most difficult problems -- briefed him on the living conditions, economic growth, and party-building work at the grass-roots establishments under their charge. Chairman Nong Duc Manh was very happy to learn about those changes for the better. He warmly commended grass-roots party organizations and administrative organs in Bac Son District for having upheld the traditions of a revolutionary base and scored encouraging economic achievements. The planting of fruit trees and industrial crops has helped with the production of commodity goods. Small-scale hydroelectric power plants are working fairly well. All those achievements have contributed to improving the living conditions of ethnic minority compatriots to an important degree. Chairman Nong Duc Manh expressed his hope that grass-roots party committee echelons and administrative organs in Bac Son District would continue to develop their revolutionary traditions and direct the people in satisfactorily carrying out the renovation cause and in materializing the goals of making the people rich and the country powerful and of bringinging about justice and civilization in society.
