Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] President Arpad Goncz, commander in chief of the armed forces, said that due to the southern Slav fighting, Hungarian soldiers must prepare themselves to defend peace. At a meeting with military leaders, Defense Minister Lajos Fur said that despite shrinking financial resources, the capability to mobilize and to react to unexpected challenges must be preserved. In connection with the NATO Partnership for Peace, Fur stressed that it is in Hungary's basic interest to take advantage of this opportunity for cooperation since this will lead us to full membership. He said that it is necessary to prepare for joint training and joint military exercises, as well as Hungary's participation in peacekeeping.
