Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Feb 17 (A.A) -- Turkey on Thursday [17 February] expressed concern over Greek decision taken toward Macedonia to impose sanctions on this country. Foreign Ministry officials said "Turkey believed that Greek decision taken toward Macedonian Republic was unjust." Officials said the decision was taken when the Balkans needed peace, stability, and cooperation. It went on saying that the decision recalled "Cold War" period. Greece said on Wednesday it was stopping The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia from using the Greek port of Thessaloniki for its supplies, except for humanitarian aid.
