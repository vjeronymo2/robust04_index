BFN [Excerpt] Sarajevo, 18 May (TANJUG) -- Eleven members of the French humanitarian organization Premiere Urgence were released from detention on bail today, although criminal charges against them have not been dropped, [Bosnian] Serbian military sources in Lukavica told TANJUG today. Following a decision by the republic of Srpska military authorities, they were released from detention and left the territory of the republic of Srpska at around 1600. According to the republic of Srpska's military tribunal, the French aid workers released today must neither leave their place of permanent residence nor go into hiding. [passage omitted]
