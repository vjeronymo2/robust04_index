BFN [Text] The Russian Government made a statement in which it expressed particular satisfaction that the opposing Georgian and Abkhaz sides signed in Moscow on 14 May an agreement on cease-fire and the separation of forces. Russia is expressing confidence that the sides in conflict will steadily follow the responsibility undertaken by them. The breach of agreements -- which had taken place before -- should not happen again. Russia, for its part, realizes the responsibility entrusted to it and together with the United Nations is ready to assist the achievement of a wide-scale settlement of the conflict and secure that fresh efforts for the restoration of peace in the region and mutual understanding follow the present agreement.
