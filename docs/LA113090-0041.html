November 30, 1990, Friday, South Bay Edition

BUSINESSES ASKED TO DONATE GIFTS

The Gardena Recreation and Human Services Department is seeking donations of items for "gift bags" that are given away at an annual Christmas Ball Dance for the mentally retarded.

The bags are filled with gift donated by businesses, but the city is still looking for other tax-deductible items, such as discards, small promotional gifts or gift certificates.

For further information, contact Anna Hudson or Kelly Fujio at 217-9541.
