Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The chairman of the Kashmir Committee of the National Assembly, Nawabzada Nasrullah Khan, has called upon the United Nations to fulfill its obligation for the implementation of its resolution which was adopted unanimously for a plebiscite in Kashmir which was also supported by the late Indian prime minister, Mr. Jawaharlal Nehru. In an interview with Radio Pakistan's representative in Lahore tonight, he said the world opinion should also take note of the Indian attitude who always attended the dialogue on the Kashmir issue just to gain the time. He said after the announcement of the seventh round of talks at the foreign secretaries level, a new wave of brutality and torture was initiated by the Indian forces in occupied Kashmir.
