940427 FT 27 APR 94 / Tarmac bids for tunnel link work Tarmac construction, the building materials group, yesterday joined the bidders for the contract to build a Pounds 2.7bn high-speed link between London and the Channel tunnel. Tarmac said it was supporting a bid led by Barclays de Zoete Wedd. Mr Neville Simms, chief executive, said the group was seeking a position as project manager rather than equity partner.
