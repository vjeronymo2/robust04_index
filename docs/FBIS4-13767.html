BFN [Text] New Delhi, 21 Apr (INA) -- Iraqi Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf arrived in New Delhi from Singapore today. He was welcomed at Indira Gandhi International Airport by senior Indian Foreign Ministry officials, the Iraqi ambassador in New Delhi, several Iraqi Embassy staff, and a number of Arab ambassadors. The INA correspondent in New Delhi said that al-Sahhaf is scheduled to meet with Indian External Affairs Minister Dinesh Singh this evening. The Iraqi foreign minister met with Syed Hamid Albar, Malaysian justice minister and acting foreign minister, in Kuala Lumpur on 15 April. Al-Sahhaf handed him a technical report containing details of Iraq's fulfillment of its obligations according to Part C of Security Council Resolution 687.
