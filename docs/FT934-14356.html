931016 FT 16 OCT 93 / Finance and the Family: Housing advice - At a glance SHAC, the UK housing advice agency, has a number of publications on issues to do with housing rights and property tax. The Council Tax and You (50p) is a leaflet answering the most common questions about the tax. Guide to Council Tax (Pounds 10.50) is a new reference book aimed at advisers and administrators, and covers all aspects of the tax. The ninth edition of Rights Guide for Homeowners now also covers Scottish housing law. For more information on these and other publications, write to SHAC, Kingsbourne House, 229-231 High Holborn, London WC1.
