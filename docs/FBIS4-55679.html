BFN [Report by Enes Osmancevic in Tuzla] [Text] At 1330 the chetniks from Mt. Majevica used tanks to fire at civilian targets in the city. According to initial reports, there have been no casualties, but the chetnik shells caused considerable material damage. This chetnik tank attack on the protected area of Tuzla took place in front of the cameras of many world television teams.
