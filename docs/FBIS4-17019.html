BFN [Text] Uzbek President Islam Karimov has issued a decree on regulations of export and import operations in foreign currency. The decree provides for an efficient control over currency earnings and its utilization when exporting services and commodities. In accordance with the decree, Uzbek enterprises and organizations cannot settle their accounts on export and import with foreign partners through foreign banks. The decree forbids legal entities to sell or pass over the quotas on export of services and commodities to other participants in the foreign economic activities. The Uzbek Ministry of Justice has been proposed to establish juridical responsibility of legal entities and individuals for violation of the order imposed on export and import operations in foreign currency.
