BFN [Text] Tbilisi, June 17 (TURAN)--Introduction of the Russian peacemaking forces to the Georgian-Abkhazian conflict zone has been preparing. Georgian source report that the transportation of the Russian field-engineering battalion to Georgia began yesterday. Beginning in June 19 the battalion will be engaged in mine clearing and placing of peacemaking forces in the conflict zone. Two motor-infantry battalions from the Group of Russian troops for Transcaucasia have already been transported to Georgia. When the Council of Russian Federation passes the final resolution concerning this issue, these battalions will be introduced into the frontline, along the river of Inguri. Military source also report that Russia and Georgia adopted a joint resolution concerning restoration of the bridge through Inguri river, which has been blown up in 1993 during actions.
