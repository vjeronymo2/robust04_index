Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing and Seoul are expressing concern about the U.S. plan to deploy the anti-missile Patriot system of the U.S. Forces in the ROK. The United States is planning to introduce Patriot missiles into the ROK under the fabricated pretext [nalchodoen kusillo] that the DPRK has missile attack capability. According to AFP reports from Seoul, ROK political circles are concerned about the possibility that nuclear talks with Pyongyang will go amiss [patan] due to the deployment of those missiles. Beijing has officially opposed the attempt to deploy missiles. A spokesman for the PRC Foreign Ministry stated that the PRC opposes any action [imuiui haengdong] detrimental to peace and security of the Korean peninsula.
