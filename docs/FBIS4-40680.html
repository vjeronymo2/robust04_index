BFN [Text] Baku, June 18 (TURAN)--A letter of U.S. State Secretary Warren Christopher sent to Gasan Gasanov, Azerbaijan Foreign Minister reads that Christopher appreciates adoption by Azeri Government the last proposal of CSCE Minsk group, which provides ceasefire and location of observers in the region. Christopher notes in the letter that the Azerbaijan agreement with CSCE proposal witnesses Azerbaijan's intention to peaceful settlement of Karabakh conflict. "We demanded from Armenia and Karabakh Armenians to accept this proposal as well", notes Christopher. The letter reads that representatives of U.S. Government, addressing at the Congress expressed their unsatisfaction by Article 907 of the Act support of freedom. According to Christopher "despite that, it would be very difficult to take away limitations imposed by Congress on the providing assistance to Azerbaijan". Christopher also supported U.S. readiness to help the CSCE Minsk group.
