Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 14 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing met with a delegation from U.S. Chrysler Corporation here this afternoon. The delegation, led by Peter C. Badore, director-general of the Asian-Pacific region of the Chrysler's International Department, came to celebrate the 10th anniversary of the Beijing Jeep Corporation, Ltd (BJC), which was the first Sino-foreign joint- venture in China's automotive industry. Li said that BJC has made marked achievements since it was inaugurated in January 1984. He expressed the hope that the corporation will continue to enlarge its production scale, enhance its ability of developing new products and raise its localization rate so as to bring more competitive products into the third country markets. Over the past 10 years, BJC has turned out a total of 350,000 automobiles. The localization rate of its Cherokee has up to date exceeded 60 percent.
