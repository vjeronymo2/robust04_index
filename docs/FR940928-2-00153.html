Internal Revenue Service (IRS) OMB Number: 1545&hyph;1432 Survey Project Number: IRS PC:V 94&hyph;007 Type of Review: Revision Title: Early Intervention Contact Interview Description: The Internal Revenue Service is looking at ways to encourage and assist taxpayers to more promptly resolve their tax liabilities. To this end, the Collection function is currently conducting a major test to examine the strategy of interrupting the notice stream to make contact with delinquent taxpayers. The strategy employed is to make personal telephone calls in place of third notice correspondence on a sample of balance due and return delinquency cases.
