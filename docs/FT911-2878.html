910427 FT 27 APR 91 / UK Company News: Downiebrae progresses Profits before tax of Downiebrae Holdings, a steel profile and pipe flange manufacturer, advanced from Pounds 542,890 to Pounds 633,693 for the year to end-December. Turnover was little changed at Pounds 1.5m (Pounds 1.48m). Tax accounted or Pounds 73,764 (Pounds 156,027). A proposed dividend of 1p (0.5p) is being paid from earnings of 3.19p (2.21p) per share.
