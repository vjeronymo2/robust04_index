May 6, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

PROS AND CONS OF 'DR. GRIMES' BITTER PILL'

The article about Dr. Grimes and RU-486 was timely and informative.

It was also upsetting. Dr. Grimes stated that women in Bangladesh have more reproductive choices than do women in the United States. How sad, scary, and upsetting that life-saving medication for women is not readily available.

I admire Dr. Grimes for his courage, perseverance, and concern for women.

What can women do to demand the availability of this breakthrough drug?

KATHRYN S. TARBELL, Huntington Beach
