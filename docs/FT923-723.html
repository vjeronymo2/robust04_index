920926 FT 26 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Sheldon Jones loss deepens to Pounds 885,000 At the operating level second half profits from Sheldon Jones showed a significant recovery. But exceptional charges and interest payments pushed up the year's loss from Pounds 221,000 to Pounds 885,000. With losses per share at 14.79p (0.89p) for the year to June 30 the dividend is being passed. Previously there was an interim of 1.35p followed by a final of 1.21p. Turnover of the USM-quoted group, which makes pet foods and distributes garden and timber products, came to Pounds 15.4m (Pounds 15.3m) but generated an operating deficit of Pounds 42,000 (profit Pounds 254,000). There were exceptionals of Pounds 345,000 (Pounds 75,000) for further reorganisation, and Pounds 87,000 for prior year items. Interest took Pounds 411,000 (Pounds 400,000).
