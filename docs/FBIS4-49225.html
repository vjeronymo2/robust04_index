BFN [Text] Shanghai, April 26 (XINHUA) -- The Shanghai Shaoxing Opera troop will tour Taiwan next month, marking a further advance in cultural exchange between China's largest municipality and the island province. Shanghai conducted 63 cultural exchange projects with Taiwan last year, and so far this year exchanges have included a visit by its symphony orchestra. The 65-member opera troupe, scheduled to leave Shanghai for Taiwan May 2, includes noted performers Yuan Xuefen, Xu Yulan and Wang Wenjuan. The troupe will perform traditional operas including "A dream of Red Mansions", "The Western Chamber", "Meng Lijun" and "Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai".
