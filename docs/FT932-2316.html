930619 FT 19 JUN 93 / Moscow money Russia said yesterday it would challenge a Luxembourg court decision to freeze Dollars 279.3m (Pounds 184m) in Luxembourg bank accounts belonging to Russian government ministries, central bank, and trade institutions, Leyla Boulton. The funds were frozen after the court ruled in favour of a Swiss trading company, Noga, which claimed it was owed Dollars 250m for delivery of consumer goods which were to be paid for with oil.
