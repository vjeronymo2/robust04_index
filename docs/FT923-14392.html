920708 FT 08 JUL 92 / UK Company News: AB Engineering Pounds 73,000 lower Despite good results from Middle East activities, Associated British Engineering recorded a small second half loss to finish the year to March 31 with pre-tax profits Pounds 73,000 lower at Pounds 35,000. Last year's figure was struck after an exceptional charge of Pounds 132,000. The Middle East operations turned round from Pounds 62,000 losses to profits of Pounds 543,000. However, marine diesel spares and services slipped to Pounds 272,000 (Pounds 387,000), while catering equipment and diesel power engineering incurred losses of Pounds 141,000 (Pounds 195,000 profits) and Pounds 12,000 (Pounds 448,000 profits) respectively. Turnover slipped to Pounds 34.2m (Pounds 36.5m). Losses per share edged up from 0.02p to 0.07p and the dividend is halved to 0.05p (0.1p).
