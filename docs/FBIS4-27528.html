BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 31 (KCNA) -- A ceremony for the publication of the book "Traveller to Korea, the Land of Morning Calm" written by Prof. and Dr. Alexandru Mika was held at the Dacia bookstore in Bucharest on May 27. Addressing the ceremony Alexandru Mika said that Korea with a long history of over 5,000 years is now prospering under the wise leadership of President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il. He elaborated on the brilliant cultural tradition and present achievements of the Korean people. Noting that Korea remains divided due to the interference of outside forces, he stressed: "The Korean people will surely achieve the reunification of the country by confederacy formula proposed by President Kim Il-song."
