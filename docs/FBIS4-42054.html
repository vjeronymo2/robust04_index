BFN [Text] The Georgian Prime-Minister Otar Patsatsia has categorically denied Abkhaz reports about the beginning of the mass uncontrolled arrival of Georgian refugees in the Gali region. At a meeting with the local administration and law enforcement officers in Zugdidi on Wednesday he said false reports are spread to undermine the normal return of refugees. The prime-minister said that the most important stage after the appearance of peace-keeping forces in the security zone is the return of refugees. Any obstacles to the process should be removed. Everything is going to be done in keeping with the four-sided agreements.
