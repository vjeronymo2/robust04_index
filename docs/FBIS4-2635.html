BFN [Text] Beijing, April 11 (XINHUA) -- A third group of 50 consultants on Hong Kong affairs is to be engaged by the Hong Kong and Macao Affairs Office of the State Council and the Hong Kong branch of XINHUA NEWS AGENCY. A list of the names of the 50 consultants was announced here today. Included are legislative members Peggy Lam and H.C. Cheng, Lee Guo-wei, chairman of the Hang Seng Bank Ltd., and Cheng Yin-chung, director of the city polytechnic college. An appointment ceremony for the consultants will be held here in the middle of May.
