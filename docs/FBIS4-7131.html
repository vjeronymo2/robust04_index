BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 23 (KCNA) -- A delegation of officials of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon) headed by Yu Kwang-su, a department director of the Chongnyon Central Standing Committee, a delegation of Chongnyon officials headed by Chong Chun-sik, vice-director of a department of the Chongnyon Central Standing Committee, a home-visiting group of Korean students in Japan headed by Chin Il-nam and the 210th short-term home-visiting group of Korean residents in Japan arrived in Wonsan on April 22 by the ship "Mangyongbong-92" for a visit to the socialist homeland.
