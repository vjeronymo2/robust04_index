931218 FT 18 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Chelsfield 4.86 times subscribed Of the 16.13m Chelsfield shares which were placed under the company's offer, valid applications were received in respect of 78.47m shares from 103 intermediaries, equivalent to Pounds 121.6m, making the offer 4.86 times subscribed. On this basis, the allocation will be 205 ordinary shares for every 1,000 shares applied for.
