Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 20 Mar -- During the night there was a big clash between Russian border troops and Tajik opposition fighters in one of the sectors of the Pyanj border detachment. According to military sources, intruders, whose numbers were not established, attempted to cross from Afghanistan into Tajikistan with weapons and ammunition. Their attempt did not succeed, and abandoning their loads and leaving several dead, the fighters returned to the other side of the border. There are no casualties among the Russian border troops. According to the Russian military, the past 24 hours have been relatively quiet in the other sectors of the Tajik-Afghan border. Military equipment and subunits of fighters are being concentrated along the border in the northern provinces of Afghanistan.
