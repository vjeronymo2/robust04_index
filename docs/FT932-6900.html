930526 FT 26 MAY 93 / International Capital Markets: Teleglobe completes credit facility TELEGLOBE, Canada's overseas telecommunications group, has completed a CDollars 400m credit facility with a syndicate of financial institutions, Reuter reports from Montreal. The five-year revolving credit facility replaces four existing banking facilities totalling about CDollars 300m and will provide additional credits to fund investment, mostly in telecommunications. Teleglobe also arranged a USDollars 95m non-recourse financing with Toronto-Dominion Bank for the USDollars 400m Cantat-3 transatlantic telecommunications cable project. The financing is guaranteed by BCE. BCE will receive fees and a 25 per cent interest in the Teleglobe's unit holding its investment in Cantat-3. Cantat-3 that will be brought into service in 1994.
