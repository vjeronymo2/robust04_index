930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / International Company News: Anheuser Busch to step up dividend ANHEUSER-BUSCH, the biggest US brewer, posted essentially flat second-quarter net income on sales which edged 1.3 per cent higher, and said it would raise its quarterly dividend by 12.5 per cent. For the 1993 second quarter, the company had net income of Dollars 308.6m on sales of Dollars 3.43bn, compared with earnings of Dollars 308.4m on sales of Dollars 3.38bn a year earlier. Fully diluted earnings per share rose 4.7 per cent to Dollars 1.11 from Dollars 1.06. There were fewer shares outstanding in the latest quarter. The company also said it would increase its quarterly dividend on common stock to 36 cents a share from 32 cents. First-half net income was Dollars 502.7m, or Dollars 1.80, against Dollars 446.8m, or Dollars 1.54, last time on sales of Dollars 6.3bn against Dollars 6.41bn. Last year's earnings included charges of Dollars 76.7m for accounting changes.
