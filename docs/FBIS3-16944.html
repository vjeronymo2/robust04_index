Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] It was learned today that an attempt was made on the life of the Ingush Republic's justice minister at 2000 on Monday. His car, in which the deputy minister was also a passenger, was shot at from a passing motor vehicle in the settlement of Nizhniye Achaluki. Neither the minister nor his deputy were wounded but the driver was wounded and died in the hospital. An investigative group of the Russian Interior Ministry has started work at the scene of the incident.
