July 29, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Timothy Kelly, managing editor at the Orange County Register since 1987, is leaving the newspaper to become executive editor of the Lexington Herald-Leader in Lexington, Ky. No date for his departure has been set, although he is scheduled to leave within the next month. Before joining the Register, Kelly, 41, was editor of the Daily News, based in Woodland Hills.
