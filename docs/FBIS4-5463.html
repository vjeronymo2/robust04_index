BFN [Text] His Majesty the King has appointed Admiral Winyan Santiwisat to replace Admiral Santiphap Muming as Deputy Royal Thai Navy Commander in Chief, the Prime Minister's Office announced yesterday. The Royal appointment, which was made on April 2 and countersigned by Prime Minister Chuan Likphai, is retroactive to March 1, when Adm. Santiphap died of liver cancer and heart failure, the office said. Before the appointment, Admiral Winyan was Admiral of the Fleet under the Navy Commander in Chief, which post will be taken over by Admiral Chalin Sakhonsin.
