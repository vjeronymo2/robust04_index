BFN [Text] Relations between Ukraine and Russia should be build on a long-term basis. The head of our state, Leonid Kravchuk, declared this in an interview for the Moscow-based newspaper TRUD. According to him, relations between the two countries should be sincere and those of partnership, truly friendly and equal. Leonid Kravchuk flatly denied a possibility of any military clash between Ukraine and Russia. He devoted a significant part of his interview to the Crimean issue. It was again emphasized that he comes out against any force methods of solving it.
