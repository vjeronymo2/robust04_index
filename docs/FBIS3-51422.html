Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Mexico City, 18 Jan (EFE) -- Chiapas Provisional Governor Elmar Setzer presented his resignation to the state congress today. A Chiapas government source reported the congress has appointed Javier Lopez Moreno to replace him. Setzer was appointed provisional governor in January 1993 when Governor-elect Patrocinio Gonzalez was designated government secretary. Lopez Moreno is the second provisional governor in Chiapas and takes Setzer's place as a result of the armed conflict in the region.
