CSO [AZERTADZH report: "Agreement Signed"] [Text] An agreement on the mutual handover of convicted persons between the Russian Federation and the Azerbaijan Republic has been signed in Russia's Justice Ministry. "It is intended to promote the interests of justice such that foreigners be afforded an opportunity to serve their sentence in the country of their citizenship," Yuriy Kalmykov and Ilyas Ismaylov, the ministers of justice of the two countries declared at a news conference held in this connection.
