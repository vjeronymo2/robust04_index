BFN [Reports by Lutfi Shatarah in London, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT bureaus in Aden and Sanaa, Salwa al-Ustuwani in Damascus, Sawsan Abu-Husayn in Cairo, and Khalil Matar in Washington: "Reports Claiming Yemeni Defense Minister Killed in Road Accident"] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] According to unconfirmed reports, Colonel 'Abd-Rabbuh Mansur Hadi, the Yemeni defense minister appointed by President Salih in the place of Brigadier General Haytham Qasim Tahir, has been killed in a road accident in Sanaa along with Muhammad 'Abdallah al-Batani, minister of social affairs and security, and a number of southern military commanders loyal to former President 'Ali Nasir Muhammad. There has been no confirmation of these reports. The reports also said that the Yemeni president had appointed his brother Colonel 'Ali Muhsin al-Ahmar as defense minister. Yesterday was the fourth anniversary of unity between the north and the south. [passage omitted]
