BFN [Text] Federal Administration Secretary Romildo Canhim has said there is no Army intervention in the Federal Police because the military are not fulfilling the functions of federal policemen, like controlling and issuing passports. Civil Household Chief Henrique Hargreaves said government action is not against the strike, but against disorder and indiscipline. Hargreaves added that their demand for salary parity with those of civil policemen is illegal.
