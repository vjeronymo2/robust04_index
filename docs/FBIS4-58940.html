BFN [Text] Cairo, 9 May (MENA)--A security source in the Interior Ministry has stated that at around 0900 this morning, unknown gunmen opened fire on the checkpoint outside the Sadafa police station in Asyut Governorate in Upper Egypt. Two citizens who were passing by were slightly injured and taken to hospital for treatment. The source said that the Asyut security department command and the investigations department went to scene of the incident. The source noted that the police are continuing their efforts to arrest the perpetrators. The prosecution has been informed and started investigating the incident immediately.
