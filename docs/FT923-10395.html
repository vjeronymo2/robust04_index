920730 FT 30 JUL 92 / International Company News: Bristol-Myers to sell cleaning products unit BRISTOL-MYERS Squibb, the US drug company, is putting its Drackett subsidiary - which makes household and cleaning products such as Drano, Windex and Vanish - up for sale, The parent company said the business is 'no longer consistent with Bristol-Myers strategic direction, which increasingly is focused on health and personal care products'. The Drackett subsidiary was acquired in 1965. It is based in Cincinnati and employs 1,600 people in North America. Bristol-Myers' toiletries and household products division saw overall sales of almost Dollars 1.8bn last year, this includes personal care items - from heated rollers to hair spray.
