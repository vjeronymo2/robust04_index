940613 FT 13 JUN 94 / Arts: Ravinia Festival - Openings The summer music festival at Highland Park outside Chicago opens on Thursday with the first of six jazz concerts featuring the Count Basie Orchestra, Wynton Marsalis, Cleo Laine and Dave Brubeck. The Chicago Symphony Orchestra then takes up residence for eight weeks, with soloists including Hermann Prey, Midori, Gidon Kremer and Shura Cherkassky. Apart from the music, Ravinia's assets are its informal country air and quick transport links with downtown Chicago.
