February 27, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

STATE;

BOMB SCARE CLEARS S.F. CITY HALL

Hundreds of office workers were evacuated from City Hall today after authorities found an unattended briefcase they thought might contain a bomb.

The municipal workers were allowed to return about 45 minutes later, however, when a police bomb squad blew the briefcase open and discovered that it contained a harmless dictation machine.
