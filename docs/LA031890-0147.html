March 18, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

UCI HOUSING FOR MARRIED STUDENTS FILLS KEY NEED

Rather than uselessly attempting to force Chancellor Jack Peltason to break away from the UC system's policy of allowing only married students to reside in the family housing complex, why don't they address the basis of the problem and lobby the state Legislature to legalize the marriage of gays and lesbians?

SUSAN VON SEGGERN

Newport Beach
