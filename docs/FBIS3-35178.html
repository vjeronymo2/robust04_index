Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres says the negotiations with the Palestinians are close to a conclusion within a short time, due to the fact that the Palestinian representatives at the negotiations, including 'Arafat, have adopted a businesslike attitude. Our correspondent Hayim Hecht reports that speaking at a meeting of the Labor Knesset faction, Peres again emphasized that leaving the settlement of Netzarim in place is, in his words, unreasonable. He maintained that the two companies the Army will have to deploy to ensure the security of Netzarim could be better deployed on the Trans-Samaria highway, where they will safeguard traffic.
