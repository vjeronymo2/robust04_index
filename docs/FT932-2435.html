930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Thomas Locker Despite charging Pounds 202,000 redundancy costs, Thomas Locker has increased its pre-tax profit from Pounds 347,000 to Pounds 421,000 in the year ended March 31 1993. Turnover of the group, which specialises in the manufacture of handling and filtration equipment, rose to Pounds 37.6m (Pounds 36m). Operating profit was Pounds 331,000 (Pounds 231,000 loss) but share of associates' profits fell to Pounds 292,000 (Pounds 578,000). Earnings per share came to 0.42p (0.44p). The final dividend is 0.65p for an unchanged total of 1p.
