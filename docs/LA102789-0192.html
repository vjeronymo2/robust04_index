October 27, 1989, Friday, P.M. Final

LENDL, SOUTHLAND'S CHANG IN ANTWERP SEMIFINALS

Top-seeded Ivan Lendl and Placentia teen-ager Michael Chang scored straight-set victories today to set up a semifinal match in the $1-million Community Championship.

Lendl defeated Austrian Horst Skoff 6-3, 6-3, and Chang ousted Alberto Mancini of Argentina 6-4, 6-3.

Their meeting in the semifinal will be their first since Chang defeated the world's No. 1 player on his way to the French Open title.
