Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today Ms. Madeleine Albright, U.S. ambassador to the United Nations, had a meeting with President Zhelev that lasted longer than planned. The subject of discussion at the meeting was the U.S. Partnership for Peace Initiative, which has already become a NATO initiative. Ms. Albright delivered to President Zhelev a special letter on behalf of U.S. President Bill Clinton that acquainted the president with the general nature of the initiative. On behalf of Bulgaria, President Zhelev stressed our country's readiness to join in this initiative. They also discussed the situation in the Balkans and the nature of the bilateral relations between Bulgaria and the United States. At this moment, Ms. Albright is having talks with Prime Minister Professor Lyuben Berov.
