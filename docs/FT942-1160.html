940625 FT 25 JUN 94 / UK Company News: M&G Second Dual net assets rise M&G Second Dual Trust, the split capital investment trust, reported net asset value per capital share up from 549.37p to 601.01p over the year to May 31. Net revenue for the year to the end of May was Pounds 2.4m (Pounds 2.39m) for earnings per income share of 24.03p (23.87p). A proposed final dividend of 10.75p makes a total for the year of 23.97p (23.86p).
