May 31, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

LA SERNA HIGH TO PLAY TUSTIN FOR THE 3-A TITLE

La Serna High's baseball team will try for the Southern Section 3-A title when it plays Tustin at 1 p.m. Saturday at Anaheim Stadium.

The Lancers, coached by Vern Brock, reached the finals by defeating Orange, 10-1, to improve their record to 27-5. A two-run homer by Steve Holm highlighted a seven-run seventh inning.
