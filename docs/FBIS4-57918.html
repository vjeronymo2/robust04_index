BFN [Text] Havana, 24 May (PL) -- Today, a German Communist Party delegation headed by party copresident Heinz Steht began a visit to Cuba to hold talks on bilateral relations with representatives of the Communist Party of Cuba [PCC]. The visit corresponds to an invitation from Cuban political authorities. During the visit, the Germans will meet with leaders of Cuban popular organizations as part of the periodic exchanges scheduled to take place between the two parties. Dennys Guzman, European coordinator of the International Relations Department of the PCC Central Committee, welcomed the German delegation on its arrival yesterday at this capital's airport.
