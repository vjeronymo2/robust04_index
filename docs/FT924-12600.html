921020 FT 20 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Value and Income net assets at 74.7p Net asset value per share at Value and Income Trust stood at 74.7p at September 30 against 81p a year earlier and 70.6p at the March year end. Net revenue for the six months fell from Pounds 804,000 to Pounds 790,000 and earnings per share were 1.87p (1.82p). An interim dividend of 1.8p (1.62p) is declared and a final of 1.8p (1.62p) is proposed.
