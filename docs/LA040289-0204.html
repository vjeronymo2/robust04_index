April 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

WANG SIGNS LEASE FOR SANTA ANA OFFICE SPACE

WANG Laboratories of Lowell, Mass., has signed a five-year, $2.8-million lease for 32,800 square feet of office space at 4 Hutton Centre Drive, Santa Ana, the recently completed 203,144-square-foot office tower at Santa Ana's Hutton Centre complex.

A worldwide supplier of computer-based information processing systems, WANG will establish its Sierra District office at Hutton Centre. It is a $35-million business unit servicing Orange, San Bernardino, Riverside, Imperial and San Diego counties.
