BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 25 (KCNA) -- Party and government cadres, People's Army soldiers and working people today laid wreaths at the Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery on Mt. Taesong and the Patriotic Martyrs Cemetery on the occasion of the 62nd founding anniversary of the heroic Korean People's Army [KPA]. Wreaths from the great leader President Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il, chairman of the DPRK National Defence Commission and Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, were lying at the wreath-laying stand. Present at the wreath-laying ceremony were Pak Song-chol, Kim Yong-nam, Choe Kwang and other party and government cadres. A wreath was laid in the joint name of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, the Central People's Committee and the Administration Council of the DPRK. Wreaths were also laid in the name of the Ministry of People's Armed Forces, the Ministry of Public Security, commissions and ministries of the Administration Council, working people's organizations, central organs, the joint organization of the Korean Children's Union, Korean People's Army units, educational and health institutions and press organs, party and power bodies, administrative and economic bodies, factories and cooperative farms in Pyongyang. Delegations and visiting groups of Koreans overseas on a visit to the socialist homeland and the military attaches corps here laid wreaths at the Revolutionary Martyrs' Cemetery on Mt. Taesong, and the KPA delegation and the delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers [CPV] of the Korean-Chinese side to the Military Armistice Commission wreaths at the KPA Martyrs' Cemetery and the CPV Martyrs' Cemetery in Kaesong municipality today.
