940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / International Company News: Takeover of Danish stockbroker Benzon & Benzon, the Copenhagen stockbroker which suspended payments last week, will be acquired by Girobank, the state-controlled bank, writes Hilary Barnes in Copenhagen. Benzon & Benzon, one of the few remaining Copenhagen stockbroking firms not controlled by a commercial bank, suspended payments when its parent company, Benzon Bankier, filed for bankruptcy.
