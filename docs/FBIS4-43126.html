BFN [Text] "We have obtained this result because the Italians want change and we can provide it," Berlusconi said in a telephone interview from his house in Arcore. He was not surprised by Forza Italia's result ("our polls had forecast it"), but he thought "the Center would lose more." "In any case, the results show that there is a gap between the voters of the Center and its leaders, who are too close to the Left," he added. On the League's losses, Berlusconi was very severe: "They should examine their conscience. People like those who respect pacts."
