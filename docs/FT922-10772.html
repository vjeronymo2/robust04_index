920429 FT 29 APR 92 / Tunnel groups are fined FIVE COMPANIES building the Channel tunnel were yesterday fined a total of Pounds 90,000 following the death of a workman. Maidstone Crown Court heard grouter Mr William Cartman was crushed in 1990. Balfour Beatty Construction, Costain Civil Engineering, Tarmac Construction, Taylor Woodrow Construction and Wimpey Major Projects admitted a charge of failing to ensure the health, safety and welfare of employees.
