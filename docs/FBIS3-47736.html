Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Thailand and Saudi Arabia have been maintaining good understanding and cordial relations. Deputy Spokesman of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Rawi Hongpraphat says the two counties have steadily improved their relations and mutual understanding and normal relations have been fully restored. Mr. Rawi says a football team from Saudi Arabia will come to compete in Bangkok on 23 January at the invitation of a major commercial bank in Thailand. The match is meant to strengthen relations between Saudi Arabia and Thailand. Apart from football players, about 20 Saudi businessmen will come to meet their counterparts in Thailand. The football teams from Qatar, Bahrain, and Lebanon will also participate in the upcoming tournament in Thailand.
