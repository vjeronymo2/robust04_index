931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Vardon pays Pounds 3.5m for two attractions Vardon, the visitors attractions and bingo group, has bought the Kingdom of the Sea attractions in Hunstanton and Great Yarmouth, Norfolk, for Pounds 3.5m. The kingdoms were opened in 1989 and 1990 as Sea Life centres under a franchise agreement which was terminated in 1991. Since then they have been operated independently of Sea Life by Pleasureworld. During 1992 the two centres attracted 370,000 visitors, and made pre-tax profits of Pounds 288,000, after costs of Pounds 153,000.
