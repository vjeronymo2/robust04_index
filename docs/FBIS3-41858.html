Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] India today dismissed as motivated Pakistan's threat of a nuclear war in South Asia if the Kashmir issue is not resolved peacefully. Responding to queries from newsmen on Pakistan foreign minister's statement in Uzbekistan, an External Affairs Ministry spokesman said in New Delhi that Islamabad has only one-point agenda. Its repeated stand on Kashmir issue is also well-known. He said on the other hand, India's position on the issue is crystal clear. Responding to a question, he said India is always open for negotiations to resolve all the outstanding disputes with Pakistan.
