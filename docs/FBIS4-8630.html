BFN [Text] Josip Manolic, president of the Croatian Assembly's Chamber of Counties, has refused to convene a session of the Chamber in eight days' time, Chamber of Counties Secretary Anton Rajcevac told journalists today. At the proposal of some Croatian Democratic Union deputies to convene a session, Manolic, referring to the standing orders, said he did not consider that the reasons to convene a session of the Chamber of Counties in eight days' time were justified and that he would convene it when he found it necessary for this assembly chamber to perform its constitutional obligations.
