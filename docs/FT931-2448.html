930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / Reuter man charged in Algeria A Reuter correspondent in Algeria, John Baggaley, was charged yesterday with spreading false information following an incorrect report about a political assassination, Reuter reports from Algiers. Mr Baggaley, a 54-year-old Briton, was detained by gendarmes for nearly 24 hours after being summoned to the Communications Ministry on Wednesday to explain the report.
