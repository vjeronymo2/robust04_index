BFN [From the "Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] Colonel General Yevgeniy [as heard] Podkolzin, commander of the Russian airborne troops, has spoken in extremely negative terms about NATO's decision to carry out air raids in Bosnia. The general said that the Russian servicemen see their task in this conflict as bringing peace to people, rather than resorting to such extreme measures, which are capable of causing a military conflagration in the entire Balkan region.
