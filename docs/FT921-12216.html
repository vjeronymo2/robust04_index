920128 FT 28 JAN 92 / World Trade News: US group wins missile contract TEXAS Instruments, the US semiconductor and electronics manufacturer, has won a Dollars 252m (Pounds 139.2m) contract to build motors for 1,328 high-speed anti-radiation missiles (Harm) for the US, Italian and South Korean military forces, the US Navy said yesterday, Reuter reports from Washington. The navy will receive 749 of the missiles, the US Air Force 465, Italy 74 and South Korea 40. They are to be built by October 1994.
