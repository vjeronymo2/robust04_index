May 6, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

BOXING'S MICHAEL NUNN TO GUEST ON RADIO SHOW

Michael Nunn, the International Boxing Federation middleweight champion, will be tonight's guest on The Frazer Smith Show on radio station KLSX (97.1 FM).

Nunn, who fights for the Ten Goose Boxing Club of Van Nuys, will be in studio from 10 p.m. until 2 a.m. and will take calls from the public.
