930424 FT 24 APR 93 / Letter: Riveting insights into life in the country Sir, I have been studying, with awe, Brenda Polan's article, 'Never wear you Barbour to shop at Peter Jones' (April 17). It was not until I was on the third reading that it occurred to me - for I am, you will understand, only a slow-witted countryman - that the fascinating people that Brenda Polan describes for us could well be related to Robin Lane Fox's celebrated friends, the Sloane-Wallys. I do hope that this is so and that we are to be regaled with more riveting insights into country life in Hertfordshire, that most rural of counties. Luke Churchouse, Stoodley, Holne, Devon TQ13 7RY
