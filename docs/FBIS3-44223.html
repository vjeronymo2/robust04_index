Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Senegal and South Africa [RSA] have agreed to open representation bureaus in Pretoria and Dakar. These bureaus, which will become operational within a few days, will be mainly devoted to commercial, economic, and consular transactions. The Senegalese Government has appointed Amadou Kebe to head the bureau. He was previously first advisor at the Foreign Ministry and is currently director of the Africa-Asia Bureau of that ministry. For its part, the RSA Government has appointed Ms. (Wanda Wald) who will assume the same duties in Dakar. These bureaus will be upgraded to embassy level probably after the South African elections.
