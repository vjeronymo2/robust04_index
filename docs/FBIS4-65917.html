BFN [Text] Brussels, May 25 (A.A.) -- Defense Minister Mehmet Golhan, currently in Brussels for NATO meetings, on Tuesday [24 May] held a bilateral meeting with his German counterpart Volker Ruehe. During the meeting, which was held at the request of Ruehe, bilateral relations were discussed in detail. Sources said that Ruehe said any set-back in German arms shipments to Turkey have been overcome and regional issues also came under discussion. The two ministers also agreed on cooperation in defense industries it was reported.
