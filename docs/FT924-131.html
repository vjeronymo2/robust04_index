921230 FT 30 DEC 92 / International Company News: O&Y units face lawsuit over NY office complex DICKSTEIN & Co said it and Third Avenue Value Fund have filed a lawsuit against Olympia & York Maiden Lane, Olympia & York Maiden Lane Finance and certain of their affiliates - units of the troubled Olympia & York property developer - relating to the 44-story office building complex located at 59 Maiden Lane, New York, AP-DJ reports. Dickstein and Third Avenue Value Fund own over Dollars 16m of the outstanding 10 3/8 % secured notes due 1995 of Olympia & York Maiden Lane Finance. In a press release, Dickstein said there were Dollars 200m principal amount of the 10 3/8 % notes outstanding which are secured by a mortgage of the same amount on the property. The complaint alleges Olympia & York Maiden Lane, the owner of the building, is insolvent because the Dollars 200m mortgage on it is far in excess of its market value. . Olympia & York declined comment on the suit but added that a Dollars 20.75m payment due this week will be made.
