BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow May 11 TASS -- The Russian Government said Russia will pay 4.7 billion U.S. dollars as part of its foreign debt payments in 1994. The State Duma adopted a law on Wednesday allowing the government to borrow as much in order to keep the balance and grant not more than 400 million dollars in credits to other countries. Aleksandr Zhukov, one of the authors of the law, said this will be enough to finish facilities in foreign countries, the construction of which has been suspended.
