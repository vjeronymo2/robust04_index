February 18, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

MEDICINE AND SEX

Howard Rosenberg's Feb. 13 article, " 'Nightingales' a Hospital Case," struck me as hilarious -- prim and proper nursing supervisors and recruiters griping about the "Nightingales" TV series giving a falsely sexy picture of nurses.

From indirect but close experience, I can say the truth is more outrageous than anything on "Nightingales."

THEODORE O. McCALEB

North Hollywood
