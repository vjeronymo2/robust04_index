920716 FT 16 JUL 92 / UK Company News: C and W in Russian joint venture Cable and Wireless, the telecommunications group, yesterday announced the launch of a direct satellite service from St Petersburg. The new company, Baltic Communications, is a joint venture in which C and W is the largest shareholder with a 40 per cent stake. The other partners are the San Francisco/Moscow Teleport of California and three of the main state telecommunications companies in St Petersburg. The venture is St Petersburg's first fully digital satellite service and is designed to meet the needs of the business and tourist industries.
