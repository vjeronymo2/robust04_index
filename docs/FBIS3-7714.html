Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] A delegation led by the prime minister of Singapore, Mr. Goh Chok Tong, which arrived in Yangon [Rangoon] on 28 March for an official visit to Myanmar [Burma] at the invitation of Senior General Than Shwe, chairman of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] of the Union of Myanmar and prime minister, left Yangon by special aircraft this afternoon. The delegation was seen off at Yangon airport by Sr. Gen. Than Shwe, SLORC chairman and prime minister; General Maung Aye, SLORC member, deputy commander in chief of defense services, and army commander in chief; Vice Admiral Maung Maung Khin and Lieutenant General Tin Tun, SLORC members and deputy prime ministers; Lt. Gen. Khin Nyunt, SLORC secretary-1; Lt. Gen. Tin U, SLORC secretary-2; Lt. Gen. Myo Nyunt, SLORC member, minister of religious affairs, and chairman of Yangon division law and order restoration council; Lt. Gen. Thein Win, minister of transport; Brigadier General Abel, minister of national planning and economic development; Brig. Gen. Myo Thant, minister of information; U Ohn Gyaw, minister of foreign affairs; U Nyunt Swe, deputy minister of foreign affairs; U Phone Myint, Myanmar [Burmese] ambassador to the Republic of Singapore; U Kyaw Myint, charge d'affaires of the Myanmar Embassy in Singapore; Brigadier General Chin Siat Yoon, ambassador of Singapore; Mr. Anthony Chng Chye Tong, charge d'affaires of Singapore Embassy; the dean of the diplomatic corps and diplomats; responsible personnel from the civil and military service; and personnel from the Singapore Embassy.
