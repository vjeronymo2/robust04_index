Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A high-level Russian army delegation is now in the capital on a six-day goodwill visit. The deputy defense minister and commander in chief of the Russian land force, Colonel General Vladimir Semyonov, is leading the delegation. They will have wide-ranging discussions with the army chief, General B.C. Joshi, and call on the minister of state for defense, Mr. Mallikarjun. The Russian delegation will be visiting some Army formations and training establishments, besides places of historical interest in Jaipur, Agra, and Poona.
