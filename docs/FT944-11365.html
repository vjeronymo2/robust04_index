941107 FT 07 NOV 94 / Marketing salary increases slow Annual pay increases in the marketing and sales sector averaged 3 per cent in the 12 months to August, compared with 4 per cent in the previous 12 months, a survey by Reward Group, the pay analyst, shows. The study covered 1,000 members of the Chartered Institute of Marketing and found that 10 per cent received no pay increases in the 12 months to August.
