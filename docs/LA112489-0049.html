November 24, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

DIGEST

Sandra Bartley of Van Nuys will compete in the Sports Car Club of America's annual Fall Classic Enduro on Saturday at Willow Springs International Raceway near Rosamond.

Bartley, a veteran Indy Pace Car Team driver and celebrity spokesperson for the California Assn. of Highway Safety Leaders, will team with Doug Dwyre of Palos Verdes on the nine-turn, 2.5-mile road course.

The three-hour race will have a field of 70 cars from the Improved Touring and Showroom Stock classes. This is the final weekend of the Cal Club Region of the SCCA.
