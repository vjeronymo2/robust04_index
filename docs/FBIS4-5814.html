BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 14 (KCNA) -- The South Korean Federation of General Student Councils (Hanchongnyon), a vanguard organization of one million students of South Korea, called a press conference in Seoul on April 12 and announced that the second pan-national rally against farm market opening will be held on April 16 jointly with opposition organizations including the headquarters of the pan-national movement for the protection of our rice, according to a radio report from Seoul. The first pan-national rally was held on April 9. Hanchongnyon declared that it will appoint ten delegates among the students now on a hunger strike in the Myongdong Cathedral and send them to Chongwadae [presidential offices] to convey to the traitor Kim Yong-sam a letter urging the re-negotiation of the Uruguay Round of multilateral trade talks and opposition to the signing of the final act of the Uruguay Round. People of various strata of South Korea are waging a struggle with united force to smash the treacherous opening of the farm market by the Kim Yong-sam traitor clique.
