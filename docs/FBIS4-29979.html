BFN [Excerpt] Diplomatic sources in Phnom Penh say the communist Vietnam has set up another special police force named F-91. This group closely operates with the Second Bureau of the two-headed government army. This Vietnamese special force commands and give orders to all puppet troops. It gives orders to Ke Kimyan, Pol Saroeun and other men in the General Staff office. There are also Vietnamese giving orders behind the back of Tie Banh in the National Defense Ministry. These sources also specify that it is this Vietnamese special force that carries out arrests and the most barbarous executions of people. [passage omitted]
