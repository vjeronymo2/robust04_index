941215 FT 15 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Aker wins Phillips contract Aker Verdal, a yard owned by the Aker group of Norway, yesterday was awarded a NKr240m (Dollars 35.3m) contract by Phillips Petroleum Norway to build a steel substructure for the 2/4X oil platform to be installed as part of the Norwegian North Sea Ekofisk upgrading project.
