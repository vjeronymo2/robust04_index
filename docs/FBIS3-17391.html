Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] To repay the debts of Ukrainian enterprises for energy resources, and in connection with the lack of currency resources, the Council of Ministers has authorized the State Committee for Oil and Gas to strike barter [word indistinct] deals with the Russian stock holding association GAZPROM, state enterprise ROSNAFTA, oil and gas extracting associations, and other enterprises that export natural gas and oil to Ukraine, to deliver goods from Ukrainian enterprises for (?gas and oil) debts. The list of these goods has been formulated by the government's resolution. In particular, these goods include vehicles, various machine-tools, equipment, agricultural vehicles, means of communication, consumer goods, food stuffs, etc. The State Customs Committee is authorized to ensure the working out of customs formalities for exporting the goods in exchange for natural gas and oil without preliminary payments and taking customs duties for these operations.
