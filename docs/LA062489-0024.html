June 24, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

JOHNSON'S ADMISSION MIGHT ENCOURAGE USE

The Ben Johnson steroid hearings in Canada could turn out to be a Pandora's box.

Johnson now admits usage over the years and indicates that he wishes to run again without using drugs.

Doesn't the possibility of his losing present a serious problem?

Will it not be an indication to others that drugs did play a significant role in his success and thus encourage the very usage he is hoping to help correct?

WILLIAM KRAUSE

Palm Springs
