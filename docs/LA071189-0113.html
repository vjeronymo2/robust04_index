July 11, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: CHAMBER OF COMMERCE CHOOSES CITIZEN OF YEAR

Michael D. Martin, local area manager for Southern California Edison Co., has been selected Citizen of the Year by the Greater Westminster Chamber of Commerce.

Martin's volunteer activities include participation on the boards of directors for United Way of West Orange County, Humana Hospital-Westminster, the Westminster Boys and Girls Club and the Volunteer Center of West Orange County.
