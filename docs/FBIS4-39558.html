BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Kulbitskiy] [Text] Mainz June 9 TASS -- All reports in the Russian press about possible changes in the leadership of the Russian Government, including allegations concerning Viktor Chernomyrdin's possible resignation as prime minister, are "absolutely at variance with reality and no more than inventions", according to sources close to the head of the Russian Government, who is now undergoing medical examination and treatment in the urological clinic of Johann Gutenberg University. The sources said the medical consultations, which followed the preliminary medical examination in connection with the prime minister's nephrolithiasis, proceeded successfully. According to the same sources, some forces were obviously seeking to bank on the current situation.
