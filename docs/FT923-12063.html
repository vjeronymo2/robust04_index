920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / Rover announces component deals MR MICHAEL HESELTINE, trade and industry secretary, yesterday opened a Pounds 200m 360,000 sq ft production centre at the Rover car company's plant at Cowley, Oxford. A new Rover model, a family saloon named Synchro, will be produced at the new plant later this year. Mr John Towers, Rover Group Managing Director, said Rover was placing contracts worth Pounds 236m with 138 component suppliers in the UK and Europe for the Synchro.
