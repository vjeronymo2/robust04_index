August 6, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

G'DAY AUSTRALIA

In the article on immigration to Australia the persons interviewed did not represent all major Australian cities and states, so I can understand the writer's omission of Tasmania as a place where Americans have found a new home. However, the failure to include Tasmania, probably the most beautiful and geographically varied of Australia's states, on the map of the country is inexcusable.

PAUL O. PROEHL

Sebastopol
