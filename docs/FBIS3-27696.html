Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Zhang Huanli (1728 3562 0448)] [Text] Tokyo, 25 Feb (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice Premier Zhu Rongji, who is making a formal friendly visit to Japan, paid a courtesy call on Japanese Emperor Akihito at the palace here this morning. Expressing a warm welcome to Zhu Rongji, Emperor Akihito said: "I was glad to hear that Vice Premier Zhu has met a number of people from both Japanese Government and nongovernment organizations during the past two days and achieved progress in promoting bilateral friendship." Zhu Rongji conveyed to Emperor Akihito sincere greetings and good wishes from President Jiang Zemin and Premier Li Peng. Emperor Akihito expressed his thanks and asked Zhu Rongji to convey his greetings to President Jiang Zemin and other Chinese leaders. Emperor Akihito pleasantly recalled his visit to China in 1992. He said: "During my trip to China, I went to Xian and Shanghai. When I was in Shanghai, I was impressed by China's rapid economic growth." Zhu Rongji briefed Emperor Akihito on Sino-Japanese relations, and particularly the rapid development in China's economic cooperation and trade with Japan. Emperor Akihito was pleased with the rapid development of Sino-Japanese relations. Present at the meeting was Chinese Ambassador to Japan Xu Dungxin.
