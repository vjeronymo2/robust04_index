BFN [Text] Alma-ata [Almaty], April 27 (XINHUA) -- Visiting Chinese Premier Li Peng today met again Kazakhstan President N. A. Nazarbayev and informed him of China's reform and open-up policy. In the over-two-hour-long meeting, Li briefed Nazarbayev on China' reform and open policy as well as its efforts to build up a socialist market economy. Nazarbayev said that his country is also exploring the path of reform and hopes to learn from China's practice in this regard. Earlier today, Li Peng held talks with Prime Minister S. A. Tereshchenko of Kazakhstan on ways to strengthen bilateral trade and economic cooperation, particularly measures to improve transportation between the two countries.
