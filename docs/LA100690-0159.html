October 6, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

SAILORS TOP ESTANCIA WITH BIG FIRST HALF

Senior quarterback Steve Walker completed 10 of 17 passes for 132 yards and two touchdowns to lead Newport Harbor High School to a 31-7 victory over Estancia Friday night at Newport Harbor.

Marc Tagliaferri, a 6-foot-3, 180-pound running back, added 105 yards and a touchdown in 13 carries for the Sailors (4-1).

Newport Harbor broke the game open with 2:42 left in the first half when Walker hit senior tight end George Greenwalt with a 25-yard touchdown pass. Josh Klein's extra point made it 14-7 Newport Harbor. On the next series, junior linebacker Darin Mangnall recovered a fumble at the Estancia 17-yard line. Two plays later, Walker and junior wide receiver Branon Coluccio teamed on a four-yard touchdown pass with 1:26 left in the half.

Klein added a 44-yard field goal as time ran out in the half to give Newport Harbor a 24-7 lead.

Estancia (1-4) scored its only touchdown with 6:41 left in the half on a third-and-eight at the Newport Harbor 22-yard line. Senior running back David Hong caught a pass from sophomore quarterback Matt Johner at the Eagle 35, broke two tackles and ran 78 yards for the touchdown. LEN HALL
