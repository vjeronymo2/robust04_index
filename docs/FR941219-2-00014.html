DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE Office of the Secretary Marine Mammals AGENCY: Advanced Research Projects Agency. ACTION: Notice of public hearing on draft environmental impact statement; change in public comment period.

SUMMARY: The Advanced Research Projects Agency (ARPA), in cooperation with the National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS), and the University of California, San Diego (UCSD) as the state lead agency, announced notice on December 2, 1994 (59 FR 61882) of a public hearing on a draft environmental impact statement/environmental impact report EIS/EIR) for the California Acoustic Thermometry of Ocean Climate (ATOC) Project and its associated Marine Mammal Research Program. This notice is to extend the public comment period to January 31, 1995. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Advanced Research Projects Agency, Attn: Dr. Ralph W. Alewine, III, (703) 696&hyph;2246.
