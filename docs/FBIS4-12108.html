BFN [Text] The Jordanian side, led by His Majesty King Husayn, and the Palestinian side, led by President Yasir 'Arafat, held a session of talks at the al-Hashimiyah Palace today. His Highness Crown Prince Hasan attended the talks. During the meeting, the two sides discussed the latest developments in the Middle East peace process and Jordanian-Palestinian relations. The prime minister, the Royal Court chief, and a number of senior Jordanian and Palestinian officials participated in the meeting. The meeting is still in session.
