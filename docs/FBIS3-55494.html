Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] We will familiarize you with the edict of the head of state of the Republic of Georgia on the introduction of a shortened working day for budgetary organizations and establishments during the winter time. In view of the grave socioeconomic situation and the shortage of fuel, as of 1 January 1994 until 1 March 1994, the working day will be shortened by one hour for employees of budgetary organizations and establishments. Heads of budgetary organizations and establishments have the right to shorten the working day by one more hour in lieu of a lunch break after it has been discussed with the local trade union bodies and the members of staff.
