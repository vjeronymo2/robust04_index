August 1, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

NATION;

EASTERN ADDS PILOTS, MORE FLIGHTS

Eastern Airlines, crippled for months by a machinists' strike, increased its daily flights from 225 to 350 today, bolstered by 291 newly hired pilots and another 750 waiting in the wings.

The airline had initially set a goal of 390 flights by Aug. 1 and acknowledged that it had to modify the schedule because of a pilot shortage.
