930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Golden Vale bid for Leckpatrick Golden Vale, the Irish dairy group, has made an agreed Pounds 21.7m offer for Leckpatrick Holdings, a private dairy processor in Northern Ireland, through its UK investment vehicle Annora Holdings. The offer consists of 10-year loan notes, redeemable after one year, valuing each Leckpatrick share at 332p and includes a partial cash alternative up to a total of Pounds 4.34m. The deal will increase group turnover by 30 per cent to almost IPounds 300m. Leckpatrick's board and other shareholders, representing 36.3 per cent of the issued share capital, have made irrevocable undertakings to accept the bid.
