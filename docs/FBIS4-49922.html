BFN [Text] Beijing, 12 May (XINHUA) -- Following are namelists of removals and appointments by the National People's Congress Standing Committee adopted at the seventh meeting of the Eighth National People's Congress Standing Committee on 12 May 1994: 1. Nie Li (female) has been appointed member of the Internal and Judicial Affairs Committee of the Eighth National People's Congress. 2. Yue Xiang has been relieved of his duties as secretary general of the Legislative Affairs Commission of the National People's Congress Standing Committee. 3. Wang Zhuqian has been relieved of her duties as deputy secretary general of the Legislative Affairs Commission of the National People's Congress Standing Committee. 4. Bai Fuzhong, Fei Xianding, Li Shuqing (female), Ma Dong, Li Hui (female), Li Yingchun, Liang Guohai, Bao Shengqing, Guo Yanzhen, Wu Jianwei (female), Yu Hongwu, Zheng Xuelin, Li Jian, Song Xiaoming, Zhang Yongping, Jiang Wei, Zhang Aiqun (female), Liu Xuewen, Wang Hequan, Zhao Daguang, Hu Renhao, Shao Wenhong (female), Lu Wenxiu, Zhou Jianping, Gao Jinghong, Yu Lingyu, and Wang Wei have been appointed judges of the Supreme People's Court. 5. Fu Xumei has been relieved of his duties as deputy chief judge and judge of the Communications Division of the Supreme People's Court. 6. Yu Lianze, He Guoxun, Xin Yuhuai, Li Mengtao, Dong Chengyu, Sun Kuiyuan, Li Guoqin, Yang Wei, Jia Zhenjiang, Ma Xiaoqiao, Chen Jiabin, Chen Yezhen, Zhang Tongzhen, Wu Xunsong, Chen Ruicheng, Xu Lisheng, Shen Guangsheng, Liu Guohong, Liu Peiqin, and Xu Jinhuai have been relieved of their duties as judges of the Supreme People's Court. 7. Cai Ziyu, Zhang Jianjun, Na Shuyuan (female), and Peng Dong have been appointed public procurators of the Supreme People's Procuratorate; and 8. Sun Chaoying and Fang Jie have been relieved of their duties as public procurators of the Supreme People's Procuratorate.
