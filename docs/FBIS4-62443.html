BFN [Video report by Natalya Kondratyuk] [Excerpts] In the first week of its proceedings, the newly-elected 13th Ukrainian parliament tried to sort out organizational matters of its own work. [passage omitted] The UNIAN agency reports that as of today seven contenders for Ukrainian presidency have collected the required number of signatures in their support and are now continuing their election campaigns. They are: Leonid Kravchuk; Leonid Kuchma, a former prime minister and cochairman of the interregional bloc for reforms; Ivan Plyushch, former speaker of the last parliament; Petro Talanchuk, Ukraine's education minister; Oleksandr Moroz, leader of the socialists; Volodymyr Lanovoy, market center; and Valeriy Babych, president of the Ukrainian financial group, at the age of 40 the youngest of the candidates.
