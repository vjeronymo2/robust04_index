Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanaa--Yemeni Planning Minister 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani today declared that the national accord that has been reached to end the political crisis in Yemen is in a precarious situation. In a news conference, al-Iryani ruled out the possibility of signing that accord in Jordan next week. Al-Iryani said that no agreement has been reached on the arrangements for the signing of the accord, in Jordan or in Yemen. He said that this is because there are conditions that have been set by the Yemeni Socialist Party.
