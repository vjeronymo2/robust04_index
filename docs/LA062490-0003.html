June 24, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

The June 3 TV Times cover on the nightmarish "Tales From the Crypt" turned into a parent's nightmare, or should I say children's nightmare.

I guess I could have ripped the cover page off our TV Times or try to hide it.

I guess what I'm saying is I'd just wish a slightly less scarier picture had been used so our entire family, which includes my 4-year-old son, could have enjoyed it. I also have an 8-year-old and we often peruse TV Times together to encourage reading.

Margie Simmers, Manhattan Beach
