October 23, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SIGNS OF SUCCESS: Stanton City Council candidate Rand Hogan thinks he "must be doing something right." Otherwise why would anyone bother to steal virtually all 1,000 of his campaign signs from local lawns overnight? . . . "Everybody expects to lose a few signs -- kids will get their percentage of them -- but this was clearly a systematic destruction," he said. The Sheriff's Department reports no suspects so far. John Nalick and Mark Landsbaum
