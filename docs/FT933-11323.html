930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / World Trade News: Athens acts on airport THE Greek government has selected a German consortium led by Hochtief to build and operate a new airport for Athens in a Ecu2bn (Pounds 1.51bn) project, due to be completed by 1998, Kerin Hope reports. The Hochtief consortium will cover 65 per cent of the financing, with Greece contributing the remainder through an Ecu400m grant from the EC and an airport departure tax.
