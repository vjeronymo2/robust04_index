BFN [Unattributed report: "Bulgarian-Austrian Rapprochement"] [Text] During a meeting in Vienna between Labor Minister Josef Hesoun and Evgeni Kirilov Matinchev, the deputy prime minister and labor minister, a new chapter in Austrian-Bulgarian relations was opened. "Austria has a good and modern social system. We gladly want to use Austria's experiences in social politics as a model," the Bulgarian deputy prime minister stated after the meeting, according to the Social Ministry's press release. Hesoun promised Bulgaria full support and proposed that a team of Bulgarian experts will aquaint themselves with the social system in Austria.
