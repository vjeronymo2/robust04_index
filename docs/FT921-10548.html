920205 FT 05 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Expansion for Lloyds Chemists LLOYDS CHEMISTS yesterday announced further expansion through the acquisition of a further 11 stores, financed via a share placing to raise Pounds 3.43m. Nine of the stores are being bought for Pounds 2.93m from Lidstore, which operates in and around London. The stores achieved turnover of Pounds 3.91m over the year to September 1991. The other two stores are being acquired for Pounds 500,000 from AG Shepherd, which operates in Gloucestershire and had turnover of Pounds 895,000 for 1990. The share placing, by Panmure Gordon, is of 1.09m new ordinary shares at 314p each. Lloyds Chemists' shares closed 3p higher yesterday at 326p.
