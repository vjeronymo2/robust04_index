930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea broker's Association reports, Landed good demand. Quality Assams sold well at firm to dearer rates but others were irregular with plainer North Indians often easier. Bright and coloury medium East Africans were well supported and prices tended higher. Plainer types remained firm. Ceylons met good competition but prices were often 2 to 4p easier. Offshore good demand at firm rates. Quotations: quality 200p/kg nom, good medium 128p/kg, medium 112p/kg, low medium 93p/kg the highest price realised this week was 253p for an Assam
