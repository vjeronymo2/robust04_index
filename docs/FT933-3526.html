930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / Brazilian party chooses leader Relations between President Itamar Franco and Brazil's largest political party, the Party of the Brazilian Democratic Movement, looked set to remain tense after the party approved a new leader whose supporters back a split with the government, writes Angus Foster from Braslia. Mr Luiz Henrique, a deputy from Santa Catarina state, won the presidency of the PMDB.
