BFN [Text] Two fortified positions of Dostam's militia were captured this morning by the mojahedin and the forces of the Islamic army. Security sources reported to the BIA military correspondent that on top of gaining control of two fortified positions of Dostam's militia, capturing some strategic points, and killing dozens of the militia near the old town of Baghlan, the mojahedin and the forces of the Islamic army continue their advance toward the old city of Baghlan. In the course of this operation some heavy and light weapons of the militia have been captured by the devotees of Islam. Security sources added that due to the loss of the control of those positions, the moral of the militia was extremely weakened and that they mostly abandon their positions and trenches without any resistance and escape.
