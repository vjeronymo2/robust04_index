930906 FT 06 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Coats in further Chinese ventures Coats Viyella, the textiles and clothing company, announces that the number of its joint ventures in China had risen to five following two further agreements, worth a total of Pounds 16m. In the first of the two new joint ventures, Coats has taken a 50 per cent stake in a yarn spinning mill it currently manages. Under the terms of the second venture, the company is to take a 60 per cent stake in a new factory to produce industrial wool yarn for the export market, in line with its other joint ventures in China.
