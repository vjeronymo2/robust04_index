BFN [Text] Robert Lamb, U.S. State Department coordinator for the Cyprus issue, left Cyprus for Washington this morning without succeeding in ending the deadlock in the talks on the confidence-building measures. Mr. Lamb yesterday had separate meetings with President Glavkos Kliridhis and Rauf Denktas. According to a statement by government spokesman Ioannis Kasoulidhis, Mr. Lamb attempted to persuade the Greek Cypriot side that if certain changes were made to the documents, an agreement could be reached. President Kliridhis reiterated that our side would accept neither changes to the nonpaper nor a new document.
