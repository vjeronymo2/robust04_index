BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 19 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today received Mutsuko Miki, the wife of a former Japanese prime minister, and her family on a visit to Korea. Present on the occasion was Chon Yon-ok, vice-chairwoman of the Korean Society for Cultural Relations with Foreign Countries. President Kim Il-song had a cordial and friendly talk with the guests. The guests presented a gift to him. President Kim Il-song gave a luncheon for the guests.
