Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Unemployment in Germany has reached a new record high. At the end of December, 3.7 million people in Germany were registered unemployed. The new data was presented today by the Ursula Engelen-Kefer, deputy chairwoman of the German Trade Unions Association [DGB], who is also a member of the Federal Labor Office's administrative board. According to these figures, the number of unemployed rose in the west by 25 percent to 2.5 million over the last year. In the east, 1.175 million were registered unemployed. This is an increase of 75,000. The official unemployment figures will be published tomorrow.
