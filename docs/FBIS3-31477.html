Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] At the end of its session in Mostar this evening, the Chamber of Deputies of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna passed a decision on a unilateral cease-fire and truce in Mostar for the next 15 days. [passage omitted] At the proposal of Defense Minister Perica Jukic, the Chamber of Deputies passed a decision on a unilateral cease-fire and truce in the Mostar area for a period of 15 days. The Presidential Council, the Defense Ministry, and the HVO [Croatian Defense Council] main headquarters were charged with implementing this decision, which specifies that the HVO is allowed to open fire only in self defense. The Chamber of Deputies also passed a decision to open an office of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna to take care of all issues relating to the rights and status of Croats. The office will be located in Pale and also in Banja Luka, Tuzla, and Sarajevo. [passage omitted]
