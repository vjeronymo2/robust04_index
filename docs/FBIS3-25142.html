Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Raul Domingos, head of the Mozambique National Resistance, Renamo, Political Affairs Department and Renamo's second man, has been replaced and will now be involved full time in the Supervision and Control Commission, CSC. In turn, Jose de Castro has been removed from his post as head of the Renamo Foreign Affairs Department. He is now in the National Elections Commission. Jeronimo Malagueta and (Fight) have been appointed to replace those Renamo officials on an interim basis. A Renamo source has explained that those changes within the party aim at increasing Renamo's ability to deal with important issues pertaining to the country's peace process.
