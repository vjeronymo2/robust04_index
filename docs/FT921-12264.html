920128 FT 28 JAN 92 / International Company News: Suzuki Motor expansion SUZUKI Motor, the Japanese maker of small cars, is to expand production in India and Pakistan, AP-DJ reports from Tokyo. In India, Suzuki will increase its stake in Maruti Udyog, the car-maker's joint venture with the Indian government, to 50 per cent from 40 per cent by acquiring newly-issued stock for Rs220.4m (Dollars 8.5m). With the latest move, Maruti Udyog will expand production facilities to an annual capacity of 200,000 units from 130,000 now. In Pakistan, passenger car production will start in late February at a plant at Pak Suzuki Motor, a joint venture between Suzuki and the Pakistani government.
