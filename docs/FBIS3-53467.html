Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 16 Jan (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak received a telephone call from U.S. President Bill Clinton at 2055 Cairo time. The U.S. President was on board the President's plane, which had just taken off from Geneva. Clinton informed President Mubarak of the details of his summit with Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad. Presidents Clinton and Mubarak agreed to continue contacts and consultations to achieve progress and bring viewpoints closer with a view to achieving lasting peace and stability in the region.
