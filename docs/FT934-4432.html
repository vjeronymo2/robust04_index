931203 FT 03 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Monks Inv Trust net asset value rises Net asset value per share at Monks Investment Trust increased from 472.2p to 575.5p over the six months to October 31. Net revenue for the period was Pounds 3.53m (Pounds 4m) for earnings per share of 4.55p (5.15p). The interim dividend is maintained at 2p.
