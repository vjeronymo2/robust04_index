930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / UK Company News: GPG lowers stake in Brown Shipley Guiness Peat Group, the UK investment vehicle of Sir Ron Brierley, the New Zealand entrepreneur, has sold 4,100 shares in Brown Shipley. The sale follows talks with the Takeover Panel. GPG had inadvertently acquired a total percentage of voting rights in BS totalling 30.02 per cent. The panel has confirmed that the purchase through 30 per cent of the voting rights was an inadvertent mistake and that following the disposal no Rule 9 obligation will result. GPG now owns 4.8m ordinary BS shares, or 29.99 per cent of the voting rights.
