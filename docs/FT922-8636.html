920512 FT 12 MAY 92 / Halford hearing expected today AN INDUSTRIAL tribunal claim by Miss Alison Halford, an assistant chief constable in the Merseyside police, against sexual discrimination is likely to go ahead today in spite of an attempt to postpone it. Lawyers for Mr James Sharples, Merseyside chief constable, had applied for an adjournment so that an appeal over admissibility of evidence could be heard in 10 days.
