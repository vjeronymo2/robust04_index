June 22, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

CARS: Several local dealers are among the national leaders in used car sales, says Auto Age magazine (D5). . . . House of Imports in Buena Park sold $25 million worth of used cars last year, ranking it ninth in the nation. Toyota of Garden Grove ($12.5 million) and Jim Slemons Imports of Newport Beach ($12.2 million) also made the top 100 list. . . . As for total cars sold (above), three local Toyota dealers made the top 100 list, although they're well behind dealerships in Downey and Cerritos.

TOP USED CAR DEALERS

Three local dealers were in the top 100 in the U.S. But they were all well behind the No. 1 dealer, which sold 7,837 cars.
