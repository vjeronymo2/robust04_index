930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / UK Company News: BAT chief sees pay rise to Pounds 980,679 Sir Patrick Sheehy's pay as chairman of BAT Industries rose from Pounds 638,416 to Pounds 980,679 in 1992, the annual accounts showed. Of this, Pounds 356,250 was performance-related, against Pounds 57,970 last year. Total directors' emoluments were Pounds 6.4m (Pounds 4.6m), of which Pounds 2.1m (Pounds 900,000) was performance-related. The group reported a 68 per cent advance in pre-tax profits to Pounds 1.65bn in 1992. Earnings more than doubled to 58.7p and the dividend was up 11 per cent at 37.2p.
