930802 FT 02 AUG 93 / Second-half boost for oil production UK OFFSHORE oil production will average 1.9m barrels a day this year, up from the previous year's 1.82m b/d, broker Wood Mackenzie forecasts. It says that output in the first half of the year was down to 1.75m b/d but second-half output will rise above 2m b/d as new fields come on stream.
