August 2, 1990, Thursday, Southland Edition

JUSTICE BLACKMUN ENTERS MAYO CLINIC FOR CHECKUP

Supreme Court Justice Harry A. Blackmun was registered as a patient Wednesday at Mayo Clinic to undergo his annual checkup, a spokeswoman said. Blackmun, 81, a former Rochester resident, served as the clinic's legal counsel prior to his appointment to the high court 20 years ago.
