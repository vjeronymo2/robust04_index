940926 FT 26 SEP 94 / Observer: Inglish as spoke Look out for a hot new journal called Financial Engineering and the Japanese Markets, soon to produce its first number. The official publication of the Japanese Association of Financial Econometrics and Engineering, it promises two unmissable research papers. One treats 'Asset Price Prediction Using Seasonal Decomposition', while the other is all about 'Estimating Unknown Join Points: Determination of the Yen-Dollar Exchange Rate'.
