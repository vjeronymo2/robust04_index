BFN [Text] Bratislava, April 12 (CTK) -- U.S. Undersecretary of State Strobe Talbott will meet with top Slovak politicians in his one-day visit to the capital today. He will be received in the morning by President Michal Kovac, then meet in the afternoon with Premier Jozef Moravcik, Parliament Chairman Ivan Gasparovic, and Foreign Minister Eduard Kukan. Talbott and Kukan will hold a press conference at the conclusion of their meeting. Kukan says in the Slovak press that Talbott's visit is significant not only because of his close personal relationship with President Bill Clinton, but as a signal with regard to the current situation in Slovakia. He says he expects his discussion with Talbott to revolve around international questions and Slovakia's economic situation, particularly in regard to opportunities for American capital and businesses.
