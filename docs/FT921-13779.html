920120 FT 20 JAN 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 24.4m highway development in Wales COSTAIN BUILDING & CIVIL ENGINEERING has been awarded a Pounds 24.4m contract to construct a 5.7 kilometre dual carriageway by Gwent County Council. The new road will replace the existing A472 and will run from Maes-y-cwmmer to Newbridge in Gwent, south Wales. Work will include the construction of 10 bridges and 27 retaining walls as well as all drainage, fencing and all associated works. Extensive cement grouting of old mine workings will be carried out prior to construction. The contract will begin in late January and is due for completion in January 1994.
