941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / World Trade News: Petronas signs agreement with Korea Gas - Contracts Petronas, the Malaysian state oil and gas company, has signed an agreement with Korea Gas for the supply of about Dollars 900m worth of liquefied natural gas (LNG) over the next five years. A sale and purchase agreement signed by the two companies says Petronas will supply up to 5.26m tonnes of LNG to Korea Gas. Under an earlier, separate agreement Petronas will supply 2m tonnes of LNG a year to Korea Gas for 20 years starting next June.
