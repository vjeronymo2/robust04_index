BFN [Report attributed to the Belarusian Defense Ministry press center] [Text] As part of a military and technical cooperation plan for the Defense Ministries of Belarus and Poland, a delegation from the Management and Development Administration of the Polish Defense Ministry, headed by its deputy chief Jan Fedoruski, has paid a visit to Minsk. During negotiations with Colonel Vasiliy Pugachev, chairman of the Scientific and Technical Committee at the Belarusian Defense Ministry, problems related to prospective technical equipment for both states' Armed Forces were discussed. The sides conveyed their experiences in developing military science and examined issues concerning joint scientific research and design projects. The Polish military delegation visited the Belarusian Optical Amalgamation, the Means of Automation Scientific Research Institute, and the Minsk Tractor Plant.
