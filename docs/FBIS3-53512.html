Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Council of Ministers held a meeting under the chairmanship of Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi at 1100 today. Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' briefed the council on the positive results of the summit meeting between Presidents Hafiz al-Asad and Bill Clinton. The foreign minister reviewed the work and results of the summit as well as its repercussions on the peace process, the overall situation in the region, and on Syrian-U.S. relations. Al- Shar' noted that the summit reaffirmed the need to achieve a just and comprehensive peace based on the resolutions of international legitimacy and the principle of exchanging land for peace. The Council of Ministers expressed its great appreciation of and pride in the wise and courageous leadership of President Hafiz al-Asad and his pan-Arab stands embodied in the historic Geneva meeting.
