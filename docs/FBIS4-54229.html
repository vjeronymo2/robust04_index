BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 25 -- President Le Duc Anh received here today a delegation of the General Political Department of the Lao People's Army (LPA) led by Sr. Lt. Gen Siphon Phalikhan, member of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party (LPRP) Central Committee, vice minister of national defence, and chairman of the General Political Department of the LPA, now on a visit to Vietnam. Among those present at the reception was Sr. Lt. Gen Le Kha Phieu, Politburo member and secretary of the Communist Party of Vietnam (CPV) Central Committee and chairman of the General Political Department of the Vietnam People's Army (VPA). During the reception, General Phalikhan expressed his pleasure at the VPA's constant growth, thus contributing to the achievements recorded by the Vietnamese people in the current renovation process. This, he said, is the source of encouragement and precious experience for the Lao people in general and the LPA in particular in the reconstruction and defence of their country. President Le Duc Anh, for his part, expressed his desire to strengthen the exchange of activities between the two countries in various fields, thus contributing to further consolidating and developing the friendship and special cooperation between the two peoples.
