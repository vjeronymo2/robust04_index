930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Engineering boost for Richards A SUBSTANTIAL increase in activity in its engineering side helped Richards Group return to profit in the six months to June 30. After losses in the previous six months pre-tax profits were Pounds 62,000, against Pounds 256,000. Turnover increased 15 per cent to Pounds 6.38m (Pounds 5.54m) with the other division, steel support systems, showing some signs of an upturn. Mr Peter Hodgson, chairman, said the recovery had started at the beginning of the year and was gathering momentum, although margins were still under pressure. Earnings per share were 0.55p, against 2.29p. The interim dividend is reduced from 1.65p to 1p.
