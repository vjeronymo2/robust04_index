920827 FT 27 AUG 92 / Lebanese quit Two Christian ministers in the Lebanese government resigned yesterday in protest at a government decision to go ahead with elections in the teeth of opposition from most of the Christian community, Reuter reports from Beirut. Posts and Communications Minister George Saadeh, head of main Christian party, the Falange, and Foreign Minister Faris Bouez both said they would resign after they failed to win a delay for the poll.
