931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / Car sales recovery forecast New car sales in west Europe are expected to begin a weak recovery next year following the steepest fall in demand in the post-war period in 1993, writes Kevin Done. According to forecasts released yesterday by DRI, the London-based automotive analysts, new car sales in west Europe will rise by around 3.5 per cent next year to 11.88m.
