Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Baghdad, 4 Jan (INA)--It was announced here today that the two oil tankers, al-Ba'th and al-Khalidah, have been repaired by the mujahidin of the Iraqi Oil Tankers Company and their mujahidin comrades from other oil companies and establishments in a record time. The announcement was made in a cable President Saddam Husayn received from Oil Minister Safa' Hadi Jawad, who informed his excellency that the two oil tankers have been repaired. [passage omitted]
