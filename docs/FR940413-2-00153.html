The Commission believes that the proposed rule change is a necessary and appropriate measure to prevent fraudulent and manipulative acts and practices and the appearance of fraud and manipulation in the municipal securities market by eliminating ``Pay to play'' arranged underwritings. The proposal represents a balanced response to allegations of corruption in the municipal securities market; it provides specific prohibitions to help ensure that underwriter selection is based on expertise, not on the amount of money given to a particular candidate for office.
