BFN [Text] In Hanoi on the afternoon of 19 April, Dr. Ho Si Thoang, general director of the Vietnam Oil and Gas Corporation [VOGC], signed a production-sharing contract with representatives of the U.S. (NJP) Group and Japanese companies for oil and gas exploration and exploitation in Lot 05-1B on the Vietnamese continental shelf. Participating in the contract are the VOGC's Oil and Gas Exploration and Exploitation Company and an oil and gas company of the Russian Federation. This is 27th production-sharing contract signed between the VOGC and foreign companies since the introduction of the state Investment Law; it is the first contract signed with a major U.S. oil company. Lot 05-1B in the Thanh Long oil field is located on the Vietnamese continental shelf. It lies about 400 km southeast of the Vietnamese coast next to the Dai Hung oil field. It is considered a promising spot for oil and gas. The U.S company searched and explored here in early 1975 before withdrawing in April 1975. Present at the signing ceremony were Deputy Prime Minister Tran Duc Luong, many high-ranking Vietnamese Government officials, and diplomatic representatives of the concerned countries.
