940823 FT 23 AUG 94 / UK Company News: World Fluids World Fluids is raising about Pounds 1.9m via a placing and open offer of four new shares for every nine ordinary held at 7.6p. The specialist chemicals and additives maker which came to the market late last year following a reverse takeover by Kells Minerals, the Dublin-based exploration company, said the move would provide it with sufficient working capital to fund and expand its core business and deal with cashflow problems. The company said its Libyan debt problems had been resolved.
