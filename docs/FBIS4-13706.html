BFN [Text] A convoy of relief aid for the Republic of Azerbaijan's Muslim refugees, comprising 36 trucks, today left the border post at Parsabad, in Moghan. This is the third convoy of aid sent by the Imam Khomeyni Relief Committee to the Republic of Azerbaijan. According to an IRNA report the convoy is carrying 170 tonnes of potatoes, 150 tonnes of flour, 1,000 blankets, 500 stoves and other facilities. The total value of the aid is 130 million rials. The relief aid dispatched to the Republic of Azerbaijan was collected by the imam relief committees in the provinces of Gilan, East Azarbaijan and Ardebil.
