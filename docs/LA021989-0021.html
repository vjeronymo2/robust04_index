February 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE BIG MIX

I believe we all want the various ethnic groups of the Los Angeles area to nurture, support and thus perpetuate their roots.

But then: Assuming all of the minorities develop their own museums, theaters, etc., will they join the soon-to-be-minority white upper and middle classes in filling the seats at the Music Center and elsewhere?

Will the existence of Disney Hall and the Dance Gallery be justified? Will MOCA and LACMA continue to be successful? Interesting thought, yes?

CONRAD J. DOERR

Los Angeles
