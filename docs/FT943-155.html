940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Radiotrust progress Net asset value at Radiotrust came to 72.7p at the end of the half year to July 31, against 73.1p at January 31 1994 and 48.6p at the end of the previous first half. In the six months under review net profits jumped to Pounds 26,160 (Pounds 10,662). Earnings per share were 0.3p (0.12p).
