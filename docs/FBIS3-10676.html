Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Indonesian Government has condemned the human rights campaign against our country and supported the resolution to end the U.S. blockade imposed on the island for more than three decades. During the meeting with Indonesian President Suharto, Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina, on behalf of Cuban President Fidel Castro, conveyed Cuba's appreciation to Indonesia, and stressed Cuba's satisfaction over current relations between the two countries. Robaina said that our people appreciate the Indonesian food assistance, namely the shipment of rice. After a 48-hour visit to Indonesia, Robaina left for Laos where he will begin the second leg of his tour of India, Cambodia, Malaysia, Vietnam, and Myamar, the former Burma.
