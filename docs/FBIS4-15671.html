BFN [Excerpts] Tallinn, Apr 08, BNS -- The Estonian government on Thursday [7 April] accepted the resignation of Col. Ants Laaneots, the chief of the country's defense forces' general staff. The Cabinet expressed hope that the Estonian Defense Forces "will not be deprived" of Laaneots' services. Laaneots tendered his resignation March 24 and Defense Forces chief, Maj. Gen. Aleksander Einseln accepted it a week later. President Lennart Meri will make the final decision on Laaneots' resignation. [passage omitted] The Tallinn-based EESTI EKSPRESS weekly claims in its latest issue that many of Laaneots' fellow officers and superiors in the Soviet Army were not aware that his father in 1944 fought against Russians in a border guard regiment formed by the Germans and after the Red Army invaded Estonia joined the so-called "forest brethren" (partisans) in the southwest Estonian Parnu region.
