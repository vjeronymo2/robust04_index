EFFECTIVE DATE: April 19, 1994. DEPARTMENT OF THE INTERIOR Bureau of Reclamation 43 CFR Part 230 RIN 1006&hyph;AA26 Rules and Regulations for Reclamation of Arid Lands by the United States AGENCY: Bureau of Reclamation, Interior. ACTION: Final rule; rescission.

SUMMARY: 43 CFR Part 230 pertains to the management of water rights for individuals receiving benefits from Bureau of Reclamation (Reclamation) irrigation projects. This regulation is outdated because current procedures for managing water rights conform to State requirements rather than procedures stated in this regulation. This regulation is no longer applicable to Reclamation programs, and so it is being removed from the Code of Federal Regulations. EFFECTIVE DATE: April 19, 1994.
