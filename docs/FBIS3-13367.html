Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 12 Mar (MENA)--The Egyptian national unity committee denounced last night's criminal attack on the Dayr al-Muharraq in al-Qusiyah, one of Egypt's sacred religious places respected by all Egyptians, both Muslims and Christians. In a statement it issued today, the committee stressed that this aggression is a grave escalation of the attempt to divide Egyptians and threaten their national unity, adding that it is an aggression against Egypt's Muslims and Christians. The statement appealed to Muslim and Christian forces and bodies to rise to responsibility, condemn this criminal act and its perpetrators, confront this trend, and work assiduously for the unity of all Egyptians under the slogan of: Religion belongs to God and the homeland belongs to everyone.
