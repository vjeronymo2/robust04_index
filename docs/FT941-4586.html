940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Northern Leisure transfers Pounds 8.65m Northern Leisure, formerly known as Whitegate Leisure, the entertainment and ten pin bowling group, said the High Court had sanctioned a transfer of Pounds 8.65m from the special reserve account to the profit and loss account, thus eliminating the deficit there. Directors said that first-half 1993-94 results would show a satisfactory improvement.
