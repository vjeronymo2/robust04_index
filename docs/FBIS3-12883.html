Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Aden, QNA [Qatari News Agency]--'Ali Salim al-Bid, Yemeni vice president and leader of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], has announced a unilateral halt to what he termed vituperations and media campaigns, as of yesterday. In a message to the Yemeni people, broadcast over Aden Radio last night, al-Bid said the decision was made by the YSP Political Bureau in observance of the citizens' sorrow over the death of former Yemeni President 'Abdallah al-Sallal and out of respect for the last holy days of Ramadan. The vice president said in this regard: The decision, made under serious circumstances, reflected great sorrow over the death of Field Marshal 'Abdallah al-Sallal and our feelings about this mournful event.
