930713 FT 13 JUL 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Forte FORTE has a 96.5 per cent holding in French catering company, Corela, after its public bid. Corela, which runs motorway catering facilities, was formerly owned by Accor Unit Compagnie Internationale Des Wagons-Lit Et Du Tourisme, which sold its controlling stake to Forte last December.
