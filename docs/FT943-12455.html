940725 FT 25 JUL 94 / International Company News: Expansion for French lift truck group Manitou BF Group, the world's largest producer of rough-terrain lift trucks, is expanding into Germany by buying the wheeled loader division of Ahlmann Maschinenbau, the medium-sized German construction equipment producer. The deal, for which terms were not disclosed, is an important move for Manitou, which is quoted on the French second market and had group turnover and net profits of FFr1.25bn (Dollars 230m) and FFr42.1m respectively last year. The takeover will give Manitou additional turnover of FFr300m a year.
