January 25, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

McKesson Corp: The diversified drug, liquors and housewares firm's third-quarter profit slipped 3% to $30 million from a year earlier.

Revenue rose 13% to $2.02 billion. The prior year's results included after-tax income of $6.8 million from discontinued operations. The San Francisco company cited strength in its drug distribution business and a continuing turnaround in its service merchandising operations.
