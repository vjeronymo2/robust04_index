940912 FT 12 SEP 94 / Company News (This Week): Nestle - Other companies The food and beverages giant maintained sales at SFr27.3bn (Dollars 20.60bn) in the first half in spite of the very strong Swiss franc, winning renewed investor support. First-half results for the period, which are to be announced on Thursday, are also expected to show little or no growth, unless the group takes in all or part of the gain on its recent FFr4.8bn sale of assets to L'Oreal.
