Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau, BASAPRESS, 28/1/1994 -- Mircea Snegur had today a meeting with the mission of the International Bank for Reconstruction and Development [IBRD] led by Josue Tanaka, states a communique of the Presidential Press Service. Tanaka estimated that IBRD is very attentive to the changes in Moldova in promoting the reform, being deeply interested in their success. Mission members have highly appreciated the competence of banking factors, who guide the monetary mass, not admitting the reduction of Moldovan leu's course. They asserted that Moldova, having finest wine reserves, could stand the stabilization period by selling it if the menace of devaluation appears. Mircea Snegur said he is aware of the grave problem of social protection of vulnerable layers, for whose solution special efforts are being made. The President declared his dissatisfaction with the rhythm of privatization, adding that he will sign a series of decrees to hasten the process.
