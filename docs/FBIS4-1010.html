BFN [Text] The European Union, EU, has presented 20.5 million rands to the Independent Electoral Commission [IEC]. The head of the union's delegation in South Africa, Mr. Erwan Fouere, handed the money to the chairman of the IEC, Mr. Justice Johan Kriegler, at a ceremony in Johannesburg this morning. Mr. Fouere said the donation was proof of the union's commitment to transition in South Africa, and its confidence in democracy. Just over half of the money will be used for specific educational programs. The rest of the money will go to the IEC's election fund and will be allocated to parties taking part in the election.
