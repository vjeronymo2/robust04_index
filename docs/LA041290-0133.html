April 12, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CHARITY SCORECARD

The "Under the Big Top" auction/dinner-dance held at the Disneyland Hotel in Anaheim on March 31 raised an estimated $65,000 for the Boys Club of Fullerton.

Mary Latta was chairwoman.

The "Fool's Fantasy" auction/brunch held at the Hacienda Restaurant in Santa Ana on April 1 raised an estimated $30,000 for the Assistance League of Tustin. Jann Holmes was chairwoman.

The "Crystal Circle Brunch" held at the Orange home of Stephen Hemstreet on April 1 raised an estimated $14,000 for the Pacific Symphony Orchestra. Lynda Gome and Denise McNeil were co-chairwomen.
