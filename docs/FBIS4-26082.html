BFN [By Danielle Yang] [Text] Taipei, June 17 (CNA) -- The Investment Commission approved 49 Taiwan investment projects in Mainland China worth NT$1.6 [new Taiwan dollars] billion (U.S.$59 million) during its screening meeting Thursday [16 June], indicating that Taiwan's investment fever on Mainland China has not cooled. The approval brought the total number of investment projects approved since the Qiandao incident to 233, worth more than NT$5 billion (U.S.$185 million), a commission official said. The Thursday meeting followed a May 27 meeting that approved 93 investment projects worth NT$1.9 billion (U.S.$70 million). Meanwhile, United Microelectronics Corp. also gained approval to introduce industrial technical personnel from Mainland China to the island. This is the first time a local enterprise is being allowed to import mainland expertise to Taiwan, the official noted.
