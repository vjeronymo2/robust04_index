921223 FT 23 DEC 92 / Union may urge action at TSB THE BANKING, Insurance and Finance Union said it expected to call for industrial action in TSB after employees voted to protest at the loss of 840 jobs from the merger of its insurance and retail banking operations. The vote in favour of industrial action was 7,169 to 2,172, out of the 19,000 Bifu members balloted. TSB, which has 25,000 employees, was negotiating with the union yesterday.
