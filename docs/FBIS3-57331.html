Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 25 Jan -- President of the Russian Federation Boris Yeltsin has signed an edict appointing Vladimir Ignatenko presidential representative in Maritime Kray. This was reported by the Russian presidential press service today. On the nomination of the chairman of the Russian Government, Boris Yeltsin has appointed by edict Gennadiy Merkulov to the post of head of administration for Ryazan Oblast. At the same time the Russian president signed an edict removing Lev Bashmakov from the post of head of administration for Ryazan Oblast. Lev Bashmakov was removed from his post on representations from the chairman of the Russian government for systematically exceeding his powers, failing to ensure implementation of the edicts of the Russian president aimed at building a market economy, and also for taking decisions which contribute to the destabilization of the political situation in the country.
