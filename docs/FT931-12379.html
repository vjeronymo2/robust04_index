930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Jurys Hotel slightly ahead at IPounds 2.15m Jurys Hotel Group reported pre-tax profits slightly ahead at IPounds 2.15m (Pounds 2.36m) in the six months to October 31, against IPounds 2.07m. The Dublin-based company said that the period had started well but there were increased difficulties in the later months. Turnover was IPounds 14.5m (IPounds 14.2m). Earnings per share were 7.6p (7.3p) and the interim dividend is unchanged at 2p.
