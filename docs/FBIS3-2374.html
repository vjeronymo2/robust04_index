Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 9 (XINHUA) -- Visiting Azerbaijani President Gaydar Aliyev said here today that his country desires to establish direct economic and trade ties with large-sized Chinese enterprises. He made the remark at a meeting with the leaders from more than 20 large Chinese companies here this morning. The president said that during his visit to China he had exchanged views with Chinese leaders on a wide range of issues. The two countries also signed a series of important documents aimed at promoting the growth of bilateral ties, economic co-operation and trade in particular, he said. Aliyev said these agreements should be fully implemented through concerted efforts by the relevant departments of the two countries. He said China is a big country while Azerbaijan is a young country, and both of them have great potential for economic development. Azerbaijan, situated at the junction of the continents of Europe and Asia, has enjoyed traditional contacts and ties with the Asian countries including China. So it can play the role of a "bridge" in linking these two continents. Viewed from Azerbaijan's geographical position, there exist good conditions for furthering bilateral economic co-operation and trade. in his speech, Aliyev also briefed the Chinese entrepreneurs on his country's advantages in industry and agriculture, which, he said, will lay a basis for bilateral economic co-operation and trade. Zheng Hongye, president of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, noted that a good beginning has been made for the pursuit of China-Azerbaijan economic ties and trade. He expressed the hope that the entrepreneurs of the two countries would frequently exchange visits and deepen their mutual understanding so as to further promote such a relationship.
