BFN [Report by Husayn al-Harbi] [Text] A team from the Criminal Investigation Department has thwarted an attempt to smuggle in 100 kg of hashish and 1,000 crates of whiskey by sea. The authorities were tracking the would-be smugglers from the beginning of the operation. The leader was apprehended at sea with a part of the contraband. His partner fled and was later caught, after midnight, following a shootout with Interior Ministry personnel assisted by al-Ahmadi patrols. A car driven by the second suspect, along with the balance of the shipment, was also seized.
