Section 86.130&hyph;96 of subpart B is amended by revising paragraphs (a) and (b), to read as follows: §86.130&hyph;96 Test sequence; general requirements. (a) (1) Gasoline- and methanol-fueled vehicles. The test sequence shown in figure B96&hyph;10 shows the steps encountered as the test vehicle undergoes the procedures subsequently described to determine conformity with the standards set forth. The full three- diurnal sequence depicted in figure B96&hyph;10 tests vehicles for all sources of evaporative emissions. The supplemental two-diurnal test sequence is designed to verify that vehicles sufficiently purge their evaporative canisters during the exhaust emission test. Sections 86.132&hyph;96, 86.133&hyph;96 and 86.138&hyph;96 describe the separate specifications of the supplemental two-diurnal test sequence. (2) Gaseous-fueled vehicles. The test sequence shown in figure B96&hyph;10 shows the steps encountered as the test vehicle undergoes the procedures subsequently described to determine conformity with the standards set forth, with the exception that the fuel drain and fill and precondition canister steps are not required for gaseous-fueled vehicles. In addition, the supplemental two-diurnal test and the running loss test are not required. (b) The vehicle test for fuel spitback during fuel dispensing is conducted as a stand-alone test (see §86.146). This test is not required for gaseous-fueled vehicles. * * * * *
