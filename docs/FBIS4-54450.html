BFN [Text] Prishtina, April 25 (ATA) -- A group of Albanian refugees that the Swedish authorities had sent back to Kosova on April 20, in the Serb-Bulgarian border took their passports [as received], military ID. and membership document of KDL [Kosova Democratic Alliance], issued by Stockholm branch. The refugees were stopped by the Serbian police even in Podujeve, Kosova district. They were robbed of their belongings after the search. KDL branch in Prizren informs that Enver Shemshedini, from Prizren, Serbian police took 800 DM [German marks], tape recorder, and a camera [as received].
