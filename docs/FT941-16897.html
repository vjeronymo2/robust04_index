940107 FT 07 JAN 94 / People: Storehouse Properties Nigel Franks, property director of Texas Homecare, has been appointed BhS property director and md of STOREHOUSE Properties. Christopher Coe has been promoted to become sales director of BhS. Frank Cox, a former director of Berketex Holdings and latterly a consultant, has been appointed chief executive of Blazer, also part of Storehouse.
