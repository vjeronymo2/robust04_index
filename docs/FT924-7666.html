921114 FT 14 NOV 92 / Food and Drink: Armchair shopping guide for sybarites - Nicholas Lander compiles a list of top food and drink retailers who will deliver goodies to your door FOOD shopping can be great fun, particularly if you go with an open mind as to what you will eventually cook. But at Christmas, when menus are less flexible and the numbers to be cooked for considerably larger than normal, struggling home with the raw ingredients can be tough going. What follows is a guide to some of this country's top food producers and retailers who will deliver to your door via carrier or post and help take the strain out of shopping for the festive season.
