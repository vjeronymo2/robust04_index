Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anatoliy Yurkin] [Text] Moscow March 28 TASS -- Russia is neither developing nor producing biological weapons, a spokesman for the information and press department of the Defence Ministry told ITAR-TASS today, following reports in a number of foreign and Russian mass media organs, which maintain that Russia is allegedly continuing to implement a programme of creating biological weapons, specifically new "super-plague", to which the West has no antidote. The foreign mass media quote reports of an anonymous intelligence source, which maintains that the programme is being implemented under the control of the Russian Defence Ministry. In the opinion of experts from the Defence Ministry, it is rather astonishing that the forgery, circulated in the West in search of a sensation, was thoughtlessly repeated by some Russian mass media organs, which had ample opportunity to contact specialists and check the correctness of the reports.
