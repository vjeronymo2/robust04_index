BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk stated on arrival in Moscow that the incident involving the Black Sea vessel and seamen in Odessa is over and done with. Kravchuk noted: As far as politicians are concerned, the main thing was to regard it precisely in this light. Moreover, the incident is not as big as it was made out to be. The Ukrainian president's view is that it is a signal to begin solving the Black Sea fleet's problems without delay.
