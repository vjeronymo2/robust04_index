941119 FT 19 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Development of the takeover battle September 29: VSEL announces takeover talks with unnamed party, subsequently confirmed as BAe. October 12: BAe launches agreed Pounds 480m offer worth about Pounds 12.60 a share with cash alternative of Pounds 11.40. October 18: VSEL announces 5 per cent rise in interim pre-tax profits to Pounds 30.2m. October 21: Takeover Panel asks GEC to clarify its intentions towards VSEL. October 28: GEC makes Pounds 14 a share cash bid valuing VSEL at Pounds 532m. November 18: BAe increases value of share offer to about Pounds 14.80 and raises cash alterative to match GEC at Pounds 14.
