940818 FT 18 AUG 94 / International Company News: Colgate buys unit from Ciba-Geigy The Indian unit of Colgate-Palmolive said it had signed an agreement to buy the oral hygiene business of Hindustan Ciba-Geigy for Rs1.31bn (Dollars 42m), Reuter reports from Bombay. Hindustan Ciba-Geigy is 40 per cent owned by Ciba-Geigy of Switzerland.
