930525 FT 25 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Bowthorpe expands with Pounds 1.5m purchase Bowthorpe Group has, through its Protimeter subsidiary, acquired Ashworth Instrumentation as a going concern from Sycamore Holdings. The purchase price is Pounds 1.5m cash. Ashworth is a manufacturer and supplier of on-site moisture measurement equipment which, Bowthorpe says, will complement the Protimeter range of electronic moisture measurement instruments.
