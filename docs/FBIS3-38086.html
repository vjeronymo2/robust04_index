Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 26 Feb -- Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev was in touch with PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat today. He expressed condolences in connection with the tragic death of scores of totally innocent people in Hebron (al-Khalil), which has aroused the indignation of Palestinians, Arabs, and the entire Muslim world. We resolutely condemn this crime, the minister said. Andrey Kozyrev expressed the hope that neither the Palestinians nor the Israelis would give in to the provocations of extremists and that the vigorous search for an all-embracing peace in the Near East would be continued. In this connection Yasir 'Arafat stressed that he was interested in Russia's vigorous efforts. He requested the UN Security Council to support the Palestinian proposals. Andrey Kozyrev replied that Russian representatives at the UN would urgently be given instructions on this score. They agreed to maintain day-to-day contact on these issues both by telephone and via diplomatic channels.
