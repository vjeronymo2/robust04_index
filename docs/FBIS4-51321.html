BFN [Text] Lisbon, May 24 (XINHUA) -- President of the Portuguese Assembly Barbosa De Melo said here today that the rapid development of China was of great significance not only to China itself but also to the whole world. He made the remarks at a meeting with a visiting Chinese delegation headed by vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress (NPC) Wang Guangying. De Melo spoke highly of the great progress China has made in recent years in the economic field, particularly in agriculture. Both sides expressed satisfaction with bilateral relations and with their cooperation concerning Macao. Macao is now enjoying a period of smooth transition thanks to the Sino-Portuguese joint declaration on Macao, and people are confident about its future, said De Melo. The cooperation between China and Portugal on Macao should be viewed as an example in solving outstanding problems through cooperation between countries, said Wang. At a separate meeting the same day, the Chinese delegation held talks with the economic, financial and planning committee of the Portuguese assembly on strengthening cooperation between the two parliaments. The Chinese delegation arrived here on Monday.
