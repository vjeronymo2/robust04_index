940207 FT 07 FEB 94 / Company News (This Week): BOC - Other companies Its shares missed out on the bull market of 1993 as sales of industrial gases to recession-bound foreign markets slowed. First-quarter figures, due on Thursday, will also show the harm done to the healthcare division by competition from generic alternatives to its big selling anaesthetic gases. The City is expecting a quarterly pre-tax profit of Pounds 75m, down from Pounds 90.8m, excluding a provision of Pounds 85m for restructuring announced last month.
