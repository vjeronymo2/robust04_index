920220 FT 20 FEB 92 / London television news service CARLTON TELEVISION and LWT (Holdings) yesterday announced a joint venture to operate a seven-days-a-week news service for the London region and joint transmission of programmes. Carlton Television will take over the London weekday ITV franchise from Thames Television at the beginning of next year. LWT retained its weekend franchise. Mr Nigel Walmsley, chief executive of Carlton, said yesterday that a regional news service for London had been a vital feature of Carlton's licence application.
