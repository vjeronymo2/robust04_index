BFN [Text] Vilnius, Apr 22, BNS -- Lithuanian and Kazakh officials on Thursday [21 April] completed talks on legal assistance and legal relations in civil, family and criminal cases. The final round of talks was held in Kazakhstan's capital Almaty. Lithuanian Deputy Minister of Justice Ginataras Svedas and his Kazakh counterpart Sergey Tikhonov initialed a bilateral agreement on mutual legal assistance.
