931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Gartmore Value lifts net assets Gartmore Value Investments had a net asset value of 44.3p per ordinary share at October 31, up from 30.4p at the trust's April year-end. Net revenue for the six months amounted to Pounds 922,000 (Pounds 779,000) for earnings of 1.85p (1.57p) per share. As already announced, a second interim dividend of 0.9525p maintains the total to date at 1.905p. The trust has been restructured following a successful offer from Gartmore Shared Equity Trust.
