BFN [Text] Comrat, BASAPRESS, 6/6/1994 -- Turkish President Suleyman Demirel's visit to Comrat and Ceadar-Lunga districts is of historic importance. He was the first Turkish president to visit the region inhabited by Gagauzians, stated to BASA Stepan Topal, president of the self-proclaimed Gagauz Republic. According to him, "this visit will strongly foster Gagauzians' national rebirth in many areas as well as the parliamentary settlement of the problem regarding Gagauzians' self-determination." The credit worth $32 million, which Turkey granted Moldova to develop the Southern districts will accelerate the social-economic development of these districts, said Topal.
