BFN [Text] Today Moldovan President Mircea Snegur and Chief of the Russian Service of Foreign Intelligence, Yevgeniy Primakov, signed an agreement in Kishinev [Chisinau] on cooperation between the Moldovan National Security Ministry and the Russian Service of Foreign Intelligence in the sphere of struggle with terrorism, organized crime, drugs and illegal arms traffic. After the signing Snegur told journalists that the present socio-political situation in Moldova was favorable for the settlement of the armed conflict in Transdniestria which started in 1992. Primakov stressed that the approach displayed by the Moldovan leadership when seeking a peace settlement was a good example for former Soviet republics plagued by armed conflicts.
