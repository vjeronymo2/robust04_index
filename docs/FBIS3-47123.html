Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Jan. 8 -- National Assembly Chairman Nong Duc Manh received here today on separate occasions ambassadors of Myanmar [Burma], Israel and Sudan who paid him courtesy visits. Speaking to Myanmar's Ambassador U Aye, Chairman Nong Duc Manh expressed his wish for further development of the relations between Vietnam and Myanmar. Talking with the first Israel's ambassador to Vietnam Mr. David Matnai, the chairman hoped that the ambassador would contribute to the strengthening of the friendship, cooperation and mutual understanding between the two peoples. Chairman Nong Duc Manh also expressed his belief that the Sudanese ambassador, Mr. Salih Abdu Salih Mashamoun, would contribute to the expansion of the cooperative and friendly relations between Vietnam and Sudan.
