BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] Algerian Foreign Minister Mohamed Salah Dembri delivered a speech which revolved around the issues of Palestine and Middle East issues. The main points of this address are in a report prepared by Mohamed Soukou: [Begin Soukou recording] In his address to the OAU foreign ministers, Mohamed Salah Dembri highlighted Algeria's support for the Palestinian people's struggle to regain their legitimate national rights, considering the Washington agreement on self-rule in Gaza and Jericho as the first step toward imposing peace in the Middle East region. On the other hand, he considered the agreement signed in Cairo on the implementation of this verdict an important achievement toward the establishment of Palestinian authority in reality within the framework of an independent Palestinian state with Jerusalem as its capital. When he reviewed the situation in the Middle East, Dembri pointed out that Algeria saw that the peace process in the Middle East necessarily hinged on a comprehensive Israeli withdrawal from all the occupied Arab territories. At the end of his speech, Dembri called on the African countries to send a collective call to the peace-loving countries in the Middle East and the international community to support the peace process in the region.
