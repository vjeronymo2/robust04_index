November 2, 1990, Friday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: VENTURA;

AUTHOR CANCELS TALK DUE TO HEALTH WOES

An environmental discussion with author-photographer John Nichols scheduled for Nov. 7 at the Ventura Bookstore has been canceled.

Notice of the program ran in Thursday's Ventura Life section. Nichols is unable to make the appearance because of health problems, and it will not be rescheduled.
