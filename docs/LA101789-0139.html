October 17, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

WOOD SPARKS UC IRVINE TO A VOLLEYBALL VICTORY

Ali Wood had 24 kills as the UC Irvine women's volleyball team defeated William & Mary, 15-9, 15-11, 15-9, in a nonconference match Monday at Crawford Hall.

The Anteaters improved to 14-6, William & Mary dropped to 16-3.

In nonconference women's soccer:

Chico State 5, Chapman 0 -- Chico State scored three goals in the last nine minutes of the match at Chapman (4-6-2).
