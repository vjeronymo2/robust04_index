September 8, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

Nordhoff 16, Moorpark 6 -- Sophomore quarterback Steve Saum completed six of 10 passes for 96 yards and a touchdown to lead Nordhoff.

Saum, in his first varsity start, completed three passes to wide receiver Mike Bowen for 63 yards, including a 35-yard touchdown with 1:12 left in the third quarter to give Nordhoff a 16-0 lead. LES HERNDON
