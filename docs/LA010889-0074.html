January 8, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

LARRY HULL HEADS APARTMENT ASSN.

Larry A. Hull, a Santa Clara-based property manager and real estate investment analyst, has been elected president of the California Apartment Assn., succeeding John West of Covina.

Other officers are Hugh Smith, retired retail executive, Pasadena, president-elect; John Alioto, senior vice president, the Walters Management Co., San Diego, vice president; Dwight Long, president, Tri-Cal Investment & Management Co., Fresno, secretary, and Robert A. Diaz, president, Capital West Real Estate & Investment, Orange County, treasurer.
