March 11, 1990, Sunday, Bulldog Edition

20 DIE IN RETALIATION RAID ON SOUTH PAKISTAN VILLAGE

Bandits killed 20 people in a raid on a village in southern Pakistan, police said Friday.

The raiders opened fire indiscriminately in Hashimjahbhan on Thursday night, apparently in retaliation for the death of their gang leader in a gun battle with police two days ago.
