September 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

FOR SOME, SCHWARZENEGGER COMES ACROSS AS A BARBARIAN

They are still laughing. No, not Schwarzenegger's movie fans, but those of us burdened with taste and intelligence. Like his predecessors -- Heston, Bronson, Eastwood and Stallone -- as an actor he is still a large joke.

TOM STEWART

Culver City
