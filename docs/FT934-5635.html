931129 FT 29 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Dart Group Dart Group, the aviation services and distribution concern, made progress in the six months to September 30 with pre-tax profits up 30 per cent from Pounds 965,000 to Pounds 1.25m. Turnover rose 27 per cent to Pounds 27m. Earnings per share increased from 4.4p to 5.8p, while an unchanged interim dividend of 1.3p is declared. Mr Grant Westcott has been appointed finance director with effect from September 22 1993.
