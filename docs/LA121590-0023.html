December 15, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SIMPSONS' SERVICE

There is something a little disconcerting about the U.S. Council for Energy Awareness' complaint about "The Simpsons" tweaking the nuclear industry (Morning Report, Dec. 10).

Shouldn't the nuclear industry be tweaked? Shouldn't they be aware that they are being watched? Shouldn't they be aware of the potential power they have for accidental catastrophe?

JOHN DEGATINA, Los Angles
