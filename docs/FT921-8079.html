920219 FT 19 FEB 92 / UK News (Employment): Engineering pay levels fall MEDIAN pay settlement levels in engineering companies fell to 4.5 per cent in the three months to January. They dropped from 4.9 per cent in the quarter to December, according to the Engineering Employers' Federation. The November-January figures, collated from 258 settlements, include 15 settlements where no increase is to be paid for the foreseeable future. The federation has also reported 16 other short-term deferments which have not been included. The upper quartile of the November-January settlements was 5.1 per cent against 5.5 per cent in October-December. However, the lower quartile increased from 3.85 per cent to 4 per cent.
