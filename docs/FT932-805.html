930626 FT 26 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Wickes WICKES announced that SG Warburg has arranged for the sale to Hass Corporation of 1.875m warrants to subscribe ordinary 25p shares of Wickes at a price of 23 1/2 p per warrant. Mr Henry Sweetbaum, chairman and chief executive of Wickes, has an interest in Hass.
