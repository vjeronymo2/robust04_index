920707 FT 07 JUL 92 / Survey of Software at Work (24): Logica systems house - Supplier Profile Logica is a leading UK systems company and consultancy, formed in 1969. Bespoke systems account for 70 per cent of its business, and finance is its biggest commercial sector, accounting for nearly 30 per cent of turnover. Logica has a dozen offices in the UK, which accounts for half its business, plus nine in the US, three in the Netherlands, two in Germany and in Australia, and others in Sweden, Belgium, Italy, Malaysia and Hong Kong. Turnover: Pounds 197.8m in 1991; Pounds 190.8m in 1990. Profit/loss: profit before exceptional items and tax Pounds 10.1m in 1991; Pounds 10.8m in 1992. Restructuring and redundancy costs cut pre-tax profit to Pounds 3.7m in 1991. The company made a loss after tax of Pounds 967,000 in 1991, compared with a Pounds 5.3m profit in 1990. Changes in accounting policy relating to sterling conversion of overseas activities resulted in a net loss of Pounds 5.2m in 1991. Staff: 3,592 in 1991; 3,656 in 1990.
