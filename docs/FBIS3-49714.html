Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bratislava, Jan 13 (CTK) -- The most trusted institution in Slovakia is the Slovak Army, according to an opinion poll conducted by the Slovak Statistical Bureau. After omitting answers such as "I don't know," the following institutions were deemed most trusted by Slovaks: Army, president, Church, unions, Police, Parliament, courts, and prosecutor, and, lastly, the government. The research showed a higher level of mistrust than trust in state bodies. Only two out of eight institutions earned more trust than mistrust: the Army and the president.
