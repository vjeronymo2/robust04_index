Language: Danish Article Type:BFN [Report by Bo Jeppesen and Bent Winther: "Juncker Triggers Crisis Among Center Democrats"] [Excerpts] The Center Democrats' Bente Juncker, who yesterday was forced to give up her dream job after only two weeks as social affairs minister, is now being threatened by her own colleagues in the Center Democrat Folketing group. Bente Juncker was forced to resign after having circulated unproven sexual insinuations about Poul Thofner, the head of the Fyrreparken institute for the mentally subnormal in Naestved. He is now threatening a court case for defamation. Prime Minister Poul Nyrup Rasmussen (Social Democratic Party) did not protest at the social affairs minister's resignation: "I agree with Bente Juncker's decision," he said. "It was a sad day, but let us look forward instead of being too depressed." [passage omitted] The new social affairs minister will be former Center Democrat Folketing deputy Yvonne Herlov Andersen. She comes from a job as head of an institution for the mentally handicapped and is the first trained social worker to fill the post.
