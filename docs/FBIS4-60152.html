BFN [Text] A spokesman of our country's Foreign Ministry condemned the Zionist enemy's aggressionist and terrorist action in kidnapping the leader of the Lebanese Believers' Resistance Movement, calling it a violation of the independence and territorial integrity of Lebanon. He said: The reaction of America and other protectors of the Zionist regime clearly shows the level of adherence of those countries to international laws and the fight against state terrorism. He added: The kidnapping of Mr. Mustapha al-Dirani by the Zionist regime's commandos and other terrorist actions of that regime inside and outside occupied Palestine and within the sovereign bounds of other countries demonstrate the true meaning of the peace and coexistence between the Zionist regime and Arab countries which America pursues. He added: This is a warning to those who think by surrendering to the wishes of the Zionist regime peace and security would be maintained in the region.
