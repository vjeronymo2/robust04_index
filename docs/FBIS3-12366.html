Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The security services have reported today that our colleague Abdelkader Hireche, a journalist for Algerian television, was assassinated by three gunmen in the Broukna area of Gue de Constantine district, east of Algiers, at around 2130 yesterday evening. The security services have added that two citizens who were accompanying the victim were also hit by bullets. One of them is in a critical condition and the other one is not.
