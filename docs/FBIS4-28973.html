BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 13 (KCNA) -- A protocol of the 19th meeting of the Intergovernmental Economic and Scientific-Technological Consultative Committee of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Republic of Bulgaria was signed in Sofia on May 31. It was signed by Vice Chairman of the State External Economic Affairs Commission of the DPRK Ku Pon-tae and Vice minister of Commercial Affairs of Bulgaria Kiril Velev.
