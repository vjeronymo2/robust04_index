940315 FT 15 MAR 94 / Somerset jobs venture launched A public and private-sector partnership launched yesterday to promote Somerset has set itself the target of helping to create 25,000 jobs by the year 2000. The Somerset Economic Partnership brings together the county and district councils, chambers of commerce and industry and Training and Enterprise Councils. It is the latest of a series of initiatives in the west country designed to develop economic strategy and to lobby on issues such as regional aid.
