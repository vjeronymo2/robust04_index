941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / International Company News: Browning-Ferris advances 41% on acquisitions Acquisitions and a strong improvement in recycling prices helped Browning-Ferris Industries, the US waste company, to report a 41 per cent jump in annual net income to Dollars 278.7m, writes Peggy Hollinger. Mr William Ruckelshaus, chairman and chief executive, said he was pleased with the results, which included a 55 per cent increase in fourth-quarter net income to Dollars 82.3m. Sales for the quarter were 34 per cent ahead at Dollars 1.24bn, and 24 per cent up for the full year at Dollars 4.3bn. 'A successful acquisition programme, improved commodity prices and an expanding economy contributed to these results,' he said. BFI, which is pursuing a hostile Pounds 364m bid for Attwoods of the UK, bought 25 companies during the final quarter, adding about Dollars 100m in extra sales. Net income per share rose to Dollars 1.49 from Dollars 1.15.
