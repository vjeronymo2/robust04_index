BFN [Text] Northern Iraq, June 25 (INA)--Developments of the Barzani-Talabani cliques fighting in northern Iraq warn of a bloody civil war to erupt in the short run. Nashirvan Mustafa, an aide of Jalal Talabani uncovered that eight brigades affiliated to the Kurdistani National Union were preparing to thrust the Barzani headquarters in the resort of Salah-al-Din. Bitter differences roused between the two parties over the pro-Barzani clique reluctance to share by halves the Talabanis a sum of ID 750 million [Iraqi dinars] taken as customs tax from the individuals, cars and trucks passing through the Kurdistani area crossing the Iraqi-Turkish borders, Mustafa clarified.
