April 10, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Opera Pacific will audition dancers who sing and character actors for its June production of "My Fair Lady." The dance audition -- May 1 at the First Methodist Church of Hollywood, 6817 Franklin Ave., Los Angeles -- is an open call: Men should arrive at 10:30 a.m. and women at 2 p.m. Actors 50 and over who wish to audition for character roles should send resumes and pictures (head shots) to Janne Hammel, Opera Pacific. 3187 Red Hill Ave., Suite 230, Costa Mesa, Calif. 92626. Information: (714) 546-7372.

The Orange County Center for Contemporary Art is holding a benefit wine tasting and jazz party on May 6 from 3 to 6 p.m. at the Old Dana Point Cafe and Wine Bar, 24701 Del Prado, Dana Point. David Warrick-Jones and Cindy Mills will perform, and there will be door prizes and an auction. Tickets are $20 and can be purchased either by mail (OCCCA, 3621 W. MacArthur Blvd., Space 111, Santa Ana, Calif. 92704) or at the door. Information: (714) 549-4989 Wednesdays through Sundays between 11 a.m. and 4 p.m.
