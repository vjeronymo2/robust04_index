BFN [Text] President Hashemi-Rafsanjani today received David Andrews, Irish minister for defense and marine. In this meeting, the Irish minister for defense and marine conveyed the respects of his country's president and prime minister to the Iranian Government and people. He reported good progress in his talks with officials of the Ministry of Construction Jihad on the expansion of economic and trade relations between the two countries. He called for the signing of a memorandum of understanding between the two countries on the practical expansion of cooperation between Iran and Ireland. In turn, Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani welcomed the expansion of relations with Ireland, and he expressed the hope that greater exchanges of political and economic delegations between the two countries will lead to an increase in future cooperation.
