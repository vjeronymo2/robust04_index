Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Deputy Prime Minister Marek Borowski said that it was he who determined the price of the Bank Slaski shares and that he is responsible for it. He added that laying the blame for this on Deputy Finance Minister Stefan Kawalec or the ministry director, Slawomir Sikora, is groundless. At the same time, Deputy Prime Minister Marek Borowski appealed to the shareholders to keep calm and to be very careful because the present stock market index is risky.
