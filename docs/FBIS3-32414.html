Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Morning Information Review" newscast] [Text] Qatari Foreign Minister Hamad bin Jasim bin Jabir Al Thani said his meeting with Cuban President Fidel Castro was very fruitful and positive. They discussed issues of common interest during the meeting. The foreign minister said: I was very impressed by this meeting, and we reached an understanding on how to continue our work to foster bilateral relations. The Qatari foreign minister delivered to President Castro greetings from Qatari Amir Khalifa bin-Hamad Al Thani and from the crown prince, as well as his wishes of prosperity and well-being for the Cuban Government and people. The two parties expressed their satisfaction with the development of their bilateral relations, whose diplomatic ties were established in 1989. They also discussed the prospects of stimulating trade ties and the exchange of visits by businessmen. The Qatari foreign minister, who headed a delegation of government officials, ministers and businessmen, just ended his official visit to Cuba.
