October 28, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SPELLING SURPRISE

Having been a longtime reader of your usually excellent paper, I am shocked (my husband also) by the misspelling of the Bay of Campeche (incorrectly spelled "Campece") on the map accompanying the story "A Village's Rite Blends the Joyous and the Grave" (Oct. 7).

MARGARET and WILLIAM BARKER, Ojai
