931012 FT 12 OCT 93 / International Company News: Fruit of the Loom in Dollars 133m acquisition FRUIT OF the Loom, the US clothing manufacturer, has agreed to buy Salem Sportswear, a maker of sports and children's wear, for Dollars 12.75 a share in cash, valuing the company at Dollars 132.6m. Salem's shares, which are quoted on the Nasdaq over-the-counter market, rose Dollars 3 1/8 to Dollars 12 3/8 . Salem, which makes sportswear licensed by the four big US professional sports leagues, is expected to earn about 75 cents a share in the year to next August. Fruit of the Loom believes the price paid for the company will be justified by the opportunity to expand its range of sports and children's wear and through the expansion of its channels of distribution.
