September 29, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

WHY DON'T THEY ROOT, ROOT FOR HOME TEAM?

I would like to point out a shortcoming in Sunday's college football "Spotlight."

Washington's Greg Lewis racked up totals in both rushing and receiving that should have placed him on both lists. He was on neither. Could it be because his opposition was The Times' beloved Trojans?

BRIAN D. LEEN, San Pedro
