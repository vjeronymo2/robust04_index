921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Westport returns to the black In a 'difficult business climate' Westport Group, the USM-quoted exhibition, photographic services and markets specialist, turned round from pre-tax losses of Pounds 373,000 to profits of Pounds 111,000 in the half year to October 30. Turnover improved by 9 per cent to Pounds 7.98m (Pounds 7.31m). Earnings per share amounted to 0.09p (0.12p losses). Looking ahead, Mr Ralph Kanter, chairman, said he anticipated the group returning to profit for the full year. Losses were Pounds 925,000 in 1991-92.
