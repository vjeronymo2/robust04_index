BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 15 (KCNA) -- The South Korean puppets, at around 09:15 Tuesday, brought 90-mm recoilless guns into the demilitarized zone [DMZ] near Komsa-ri, Chorwon County, South Korean Kangwon Province, and installed them at their position and took a firing posture at the North side, seriously threatening the safety of soldiers of the Korean People's Army who were on their routine duty at the post, according to military sources. On the same day, a crowd of puppet Army bandits armed with automatic weapons, crawled into the demilitarized zone in front of Mt. Taedok in Changpung County on the western sector of the front and whipped up war hysterics, running about the area. These were deliberate and premeditated acts of the South Korean puppets to render the situation of the DMZ strained and incite North-South confrontation. The South Korean puppets must stop acting rashly, pondering over the grave consequences to be entailed by their reckless military provocations.
