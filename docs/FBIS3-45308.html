Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Special dispatch: "123 Illegal Emigrants Will be Repatriated From the United States to Xiamen Today"] [Text] Xiamen, 17 Jan (TA KUNG PAO)--U.S. immigration authorities will repatriate a group of Chinese illegal immigrants to China directly from Los Angeles by plane tomorrow (18 January); it is expected that the plane will land at Xiamen's Gaoqi International Airport at about 1400. A relevant person disclosed here that about 123 Chinese illegal immigrants will be repatriated, among them most are peasants from Fujian Province's Fuzhou City and Changle County, and one of them may be a Cantonese. U.S. immigration authorities have hired a special plane this time and appointed three officials to escort the illegal immigrants to China to handle repatriation formalities. There will also be 130 security personnel on board the plane. Last year, the U.S. side repatriated a group of Chinese illegal immigrants, and the destination was Xiamen. However, unlike the repatriation last time, the plane will fly directly to China after taking off from the United States, without making a detour. Originally, the repatriation work had been scheduled for 15 January, but was changed to 18 January for some reason. This afternoon, the relevant authorities of Fujian Province and Xiamen city convened a meeting again to make arrangements for the concrete matters of repatriation and reception tomorrow.
