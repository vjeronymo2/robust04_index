BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Olga Semenova] [Text] Moscow June 29 TASS -- Hans Blix, director of the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA), met with Director of the Russian Foreign Intelligence Service (SVR) Yevgeniy Primakov on Wednesday and sources in the SRV indicated that Blix wants Russian intelligence information on nuclear non-proliferation problem. "The Foreign Intelligence has never concealed that monitoring the proliferation of nuclear weapons is among our priority tasks", a high ranking intelligence official told TASS. He added that the IAEA relies in its activities on the information received from us and British secret services not only through their corresponding governments, but also directly from the intelligence bodies. "It is quite logical that the IAEA director intends to establish closer cooperation in the given field with the Russian intelligence as well in order to get a more objective picture", the official said adding that the "exchange of such information is in mutual interests".
