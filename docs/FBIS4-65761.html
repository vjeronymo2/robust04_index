BFN [Text] Ankara, May 21 (A.A.) -- Prime Minister Tansu Ciller said on Saturday [21 May] that the government will transfer 250 million dollars to Eximbank and called industrialists to apply to Eximbank to take credit to have exportation. Ciller said exportation is extremely important nowadays and this credit that we have transferred to Eximbank is a good opportunity for our industry to continue its production toward exportation. Ciller said `We have allocated this money from public sector's resources'. She said the difficulties have not finished yet but Turkey is resolved to overcome all its problems step by step.
