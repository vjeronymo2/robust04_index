920320 FT 20 MAR 92 / Observer: Speechless An unexpected visitor turns up in heaven. St Peter asks for some identification. The new arrival is a little taken aback, and insists that he is who he says he is. St Peter is still not sure. Suddenly, the new arrival spots someone he knows. 'I'm sure that's Mrs Thatcher over there - she'll be able to vouchsafe for me.' Non-plussed, St Peter replies: 'That's not Mrs Thatcher, that's God.'
