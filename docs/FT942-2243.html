940620 FT 20 JUN 94 / World News in Brief: South Yemen 'attacks city' Southern Yemeni jets were said to have attacked a northern city after days of shelling of the southern city of Aden. A northern statement said 17 people were killed in the air raid on Mukha. Meanwhile, in Cairo, a UN mediator failed to persuade northern and southern Yemenis to discuss a ceasefire.
