March 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

'HOME' ON THE WANE?

Why would ABC change a successful show? I'm talking about "Home."

Please bring back Robb Weller. An added bonus would be to bring back Sandy Hill, but I'm not counting on a miracle!

D. Bustamante, Alhambra

It wasn't ABC's decision: Weller chose to leave the show to give himself time for other projects.
