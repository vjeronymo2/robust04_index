November 1, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

BERRY SUES OVER NUDE PHOTOS

Rock 'n' roll pioneer Chuck Berry is suing the magazine High Society, saying it published stolen nude photos of him with several women.

In the suit filed Monday in St. Louis Circuit Court, Berry claims that the magazine published eight photographs of him posing with unidentified nude women as the cover story for its January edition.

The suit says the photos were stolen and claims their publication violated Berry's privacy.

Calls to the magazine's office Wednesday were not returned.
