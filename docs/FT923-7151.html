920819 FT 19 AUG 92 / Local pay move in health trust A JOB evaluation system for healthcare employers seeking to introduce local pay bargaining has been developed jointly by Manchester Central Hospitals, one of the first National Health Service Trusts, and KPMG Peat Marwick, the accountancy firm. The firm hopes to sell the system to other trusts. The system will form the basis of a single pay spine for all the trust's staff.
