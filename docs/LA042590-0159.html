April 25, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

BUSH PLANS 5 SPEECHES

President Bush will deliver commencement addresses at five universities over the next month, including the Rev. Jerry Falwell's Liberty University, the White House said today.

Bush will speak at Oklahoma State University in Stillwater, Okla., May 4; Texas A&I University, Kingsville, Tex., May 11; University of South Carolina, Columbia, S.C., May 12; Liberty University, Lynchburg, Va., May 12, and University of Texas, Austin, May 19.
