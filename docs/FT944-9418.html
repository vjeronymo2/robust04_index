941116 FT 16 NOV 94 / Azerbaijan oil deal ratified The Azerbaijan parliament yesterday ratified a Dollars 7.4bn (Pounds 4.6bn) contract with a foreign oil consortium to exploit oil reserves of some 500m tonnes in the Caspian Sea. The consortium includes the National Oil company of Iran and several US and UK companies. Ratification removes the last legal obstacle in Azerbaijan to the project but leaves open the vexed issue of Russia's stance. The Russian foreign ministry, a vocal opponent of the contract, said yesterday the link with Iran was causing 'great concern.'
