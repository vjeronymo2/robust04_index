Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Sergey Ivanov report under the "Inform" rubric: "Patriotic Mood"] [Text] New York -- U.S. Under Secretary of Defense Frank Wisner has announced the Pentagon's plans to site Patriot ABM launchers on the border with the DPRK in order to reinforce the 18-mile line separating the two Koreas. In his opinion, this will not hamper the talks between the interested sides. This is supposedly being done exclusively for self-defense purposes to support the 36,000-strong U.S. military contingent and the South Korean Army. But, as commentators point out, the Patriots clearly will not contribute to the slight progress that had become apparent in the "Korean nuclear problem."
