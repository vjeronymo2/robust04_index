Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Budapest, 8 February (MTI) -- Hungary and Ukraine are to cooperate within a peace-partnership scheme, says an agreement reached between the two defense ministers in Budapest on Tuesday [8 February]. Minister of Defense Lajos Fur of Hungary and Army General Vitaliy Radetskyy of Ukraine, held a press conference and said their talks also focussed on regional partnership issues, from which they do not wish to exclude Russia. In joint arms manufacturing, Hungary is mainly interested in components for aircraft, tanks, and armored vehicles. Ukraine hopes to use Hungarian experience in reforming its Army. This was the second visit of a Ukrainian minister of defense to Hungary within two years.
