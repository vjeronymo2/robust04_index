940105 FT 05 JAN 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Mexican stocks rebounded late in the day to close sharply ahead on reports that the armed peasants in Chiapas had fled for the hills in an apparent pullout. The IPC index of 36 leading shares, after retreating some 50 points more by mid-morning, ended a net 56.66, or 2.26 per cent, up at 2,558.89, principally on foreign buying.
