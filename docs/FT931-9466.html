930213 FT 13 FEB 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa GOLD shares, which have enjoyed a spectacular week on overseas buying, fell back 5 per cent as profits were taken ahead of the weekend. The index lost 53 to 964, still 11 per cent ahead on the week. Vaal Reefs lost R10 to R180. The overall index fell 58 to 3,492 and industrials slipped 31 to 4,572.
