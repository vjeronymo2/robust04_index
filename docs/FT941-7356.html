940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / First fuel rods handed over at Sizewell B The first fuel rods were handed over yesterday at Sizewell B, in Suffolk - and local residents were invited to watch the assembly. Sizewell B is the only nuclear power station currently under construction in the UK. The 1200MW station, Britain's first using the pressurised water system, is due to start up this summer using fuel manufactured by British Nuclear Fuels. Residents Pat Jones and Buffy Sones are pictured examining a fuel rod.
