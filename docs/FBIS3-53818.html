Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report from the "36 News Items" section of the "Political Week" page] [Text] 'Abbud al-Zumur [leader of al-Jihad's military wing] has renewed an offer he made earlier to the Ministry of Interior. He is willing to help stop the bloodshed involving the police and the Islamic organizations and the operations against tourism in return for improving the treatment of inmates and releasing a number of the detained [Islamic] leaders. Information affirms that the security sections have described the offer as an individual initiative not worth pursuing, particularly after the Islamic Group has announced that al-Zumur has joined its ranks and relinquished his leadership of one faction of the al-Jihad Organization.
