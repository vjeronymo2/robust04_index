920326 FT 26 MAR 92 / UK Company News: S Daniels reduces loss to Pounds 48,000 Following a review of its operations, S Daniels, a supplier of food and drink products, reported reduced losses of Pounds 48,000 before tax in the year to end-December. The result compared with a loss of Pounds 70,000 last time and came from turnover down Pounds 750,000 to Pounds 30.8m. However, at the operating level, there was a small profit of Pounds 26,000 (Pounds 336,000 loss). Losses per share came out at 0.7p (1p) and a single dividend of 0.25p is proposed for the year. Last year an interim dividend of 1.25p was paid but the final was passed.
