920615 FT 15 JUN 92 / International Company News: Cash call and Pounds 1m acquisition for Dudley Jenkins Dudley Jenkins, the USM-quoted supplier of names to advertisers responsible for the growing junk mail industry, is calling for Pounds 2.4m via a 4-for-5 rights issue at 55p per share. DJ is also paying Pounds 1m in a mixture of shares, loan stock and cash for Market Location, a supplier of information covering employee numbers, fleet usage, purchasing contracts and data for the fuels industries.
