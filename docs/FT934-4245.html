931204 FT 04 DEC 93 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Mexican share prices soared to close at an all-time high, propelled by local and foreign buying of market heavyweight Telmex. The 36-share IPC index gained 53.31, a rise of 2.35 percent, to 2,324.09. Trading in Telmex accounted for 53m of the total. Telmex ADRs rose 7/8 to 58 1/4 , while in the local market the L shares gained 2.04 per cent and the As 1.81 percent.
