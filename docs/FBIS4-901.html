BFN [Text] A member of Parliament has described as selfish political motives suggestions by some opposition members that Zambia should change electoral rules to provide for two voters' rolls, one for the rich and another for the poor. Kafulu member of parliament Dr. (Yusuf Dadat) said yesterday that the proposed idea was pathetic and [word indistinct] on some political figures trying to fulfill their selfish ambitions. Dr. (Dadat), who is on a tour of his constituency, told a public meeting that it was surprising that when the concept of one man, one vote was acceptable the world over, some people wanted change only to suit their selfish interests. He said the MMD [Movement for Multiparty Democracy] government should not allow a situation whereby the privileged determined the destiny of the majority.
