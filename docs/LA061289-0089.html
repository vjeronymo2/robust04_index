June 12, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: EXIT LINES

Comedian Chevy Chase greeted the graduating class of the Shipley School in Bryn Mawr, Pa., with a "good morning, you little twerps," as he began his commencement speech Friday for 12th graders at the private, co-educational school. Chase told them, "The most important quality in life, as taught to me by my father, was to have a sense of humor." Claudia Puig
