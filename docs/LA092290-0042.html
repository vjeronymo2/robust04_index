September 22, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION FOOTBALL;

ROUNDUP

Cantwell 27, St. Genevieve 12 -- St. Genevieve's Bobby Kite completed 14 of 32 for 172 yards and two touchdowns, but the Valiants fell behind, 20-0, in the first half and could not make up the deficit. Fernando Barba caught six passes for 104 yards and both St. Genevieve touchdowns. KEVIN ASSEO
