BFN [Text] Today, in a commentary in the name of Chang Ki-ho, a spokesman for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, the government expressed regret and worry over the fact that it has been confirmed that North Korea has begun replacing fuels at the 5-mw atomic reactor. The government also urged North Korea to fulfill its duty of implementing safety measures in connection with the replacement of fuels at the 5-megawatt atomic reactor through immediate negotiations with the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] which says that there remains the possibility of the implementation of safety measures if North Korea no longer pushes ahead with replacing fuels. The government also made it clear that it will work out further countermeasures through close consultations with friendly countries, while watching the results of negotiations between the IAEA and North Korea.
