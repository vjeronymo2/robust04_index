January 7, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

AUBURN WOMEN DEFEAT NO. 1 TENNESSEE

Carolyn Jones and Vickie Orr each scored 18 points Friday night as third-ranked Auburn put itself in position to take over the No. 1 spot in women's basketball with a 67-59 victory over top-ranked Tennessee.

The Tigers (13-0) should be voted No. 1 in next week's poll because No. 2 Louisiana Tech also lost this week, falling to Colorado, 61-60.
