BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 9 (KCNA) -- Railway workers of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea are greeting their holiday (May 11) with an increase in transportation. According to data available at the Ministry of Railways, the daily freight haulage entering this month is 6,000 to 7,000 tons more than in the same period last month. The General Bureau of Western Railways is topping the daily plan of freight turnover by 2,000 to 3,000 tons through well organized and commanded transportation. The Kaechon General Bureau of Railways is overfulfilling the daily transport plan at an average of 107 percent. The General Bureaus of Eastern and Northern Railways are carrying more freight than the plan by effectively controlling the locomotives, wagons and facilities. Many railway stations including the Sariwon Youth Station, the Sinanju Youth Station, the Nanam Station and Pungnyon Station have hit the transport targets for the first half of the year more than two months earlier than schedule. Railway builders have effected new innovations in the construction of railways including the Namdong-Onchon line.
