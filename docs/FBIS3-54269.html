Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At 1000 today, Dr. Salim Yasin, deputy prime minister for economic affairs, received Mr. Robert Urbain, Belgium's minister of foreign trade, and his delegation. The Belgian ambassador in Damascus attended the meeting. At the outset of the session, Mr. Urbain extended sympathies on behalf of himself and the Belgian Government to the bereaved President Hafiz al-Asad and his dignified family and the Syrian people and government. He thanked the Syrian Government for letting his visit go ahead, despite the mourning the country is observing over the death of the president's son, the golden knight, Staff Major Basil al-Asad. Dr. Salim Yasin thanked him for his sympathies. The two sides then discussed bilateral trade and relations between Syria and the EC in general, and explored avenues to diversify and strengthen these ties. They agreed on a number of measures to boost cooperation between Syria and Belgium and the EC nations.
