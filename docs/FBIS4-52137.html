BFN [Text] A delegation on study tour led by Thai Education Minister Mr. Samphan Thongsamak left Yangon [Rangoon] by air this evening. The Thai education minister, Dr. Samphan Thongsamak, was seen off at Yangon airport by Education Minister U Pan Aung, deputy education ministers -- Colonel Kyi Maung and Dr. Than Nyunt, Deputy Culture Minister U Soe Nyunt, Thai ambassador to Myanmar [Burma], and responsible personnel from the Ministry of Education.
