Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague Feb 3 (CTK) -- A fault in the transformer on the third block of the nuclear plant in Dukovany, South Moravia, caused a fire at 0700 [0600 GMT], Petr Brandejs, a nuclear security official, told CTK today. The fire which was extinguished at 0900 did not result in any leakage of radioactive substances and no one was injured, Brandejs reported. The fault, which caused reactor output to sink to 30 percent, did not affect the nuclear safety of the bloc, Brandejs said. The fault has been provisionally rated as zero on the INES scale.
