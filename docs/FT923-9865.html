920803 FT 03 AUG 92 / Construction Contracts: Oil refinery project The Aden Refinery Company has appointed CONSOLIDATED CONTRACTORS INTERNATIONAL COMPANY (CCC) of Athens as contractor for the tank farm development at Little Aden oil refinery in Yemen. Rendel Palmer & Tritton is responsible for design verification and construction supervision. The contract, the latest phase in the development of the refinery, entails the construction of 16 storage tanks for crude oil and refined products.
