941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / International Company News: Chief of M&S offshoot in US quits The North America offshoot of Marks & Spencer, the UK retail group, said last night that Mr William Roberti, president and chief executive officer of its Brooks Brothers subsidiary, has resigned, Reuter reports from New York. The company said MrRoberti resigned to pursue other opportunities and it is looking for a replacement. The resignation is effective from December 1.
