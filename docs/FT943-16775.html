940702 FT 02 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Platignum Hit by interest charges doubled from Pounds 155,000 to Pounds 312,000, pre-tax profits of Platignum, the stationery, furniture and housewares manufacturer, halved to Pounds 212,000 for the year to March 31, against Pounds 453,000 restated for FRS 3. Turnover amounted to Pounds 21.3m (Pounds 15.1m) with acquisitions contributing Pounds 5.33m (nil). Earnings per share dipped to 1.09p (1.83p) but an unchanged final dividend of 0.25p slightly increases the total from 0.5p to 0.53p.
