January 26, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

BEEF BUNDLES GIVE MOM THE NIGHT OFF

This idea for Beef Bundles is an ideal way to give Mom the night off. Let the children help prepare the packets, then just whisk the packets into the oven.

Cut 1 pound top round steak into 4 serving-size portions. Dust each with mixture of 2 tablespoons flour combined with 1 teaspoon salt, 1/4 teaspoon thyme and dash black pepper and dash paprika. Place each portion in center of 12-inch, double-thick square of heavy-duty foil. Combine 1 cup sliced carrots, 1 cup sliced zucchini and 1/4 cup sliced green onions and place even amounts of vegetable mixture in meat packets. Fold foil over and seal edges tightly. Roast at 325 degrees about 1 hour or until steak is tender.
