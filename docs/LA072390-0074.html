July 23, 1990, Monday, Ventura County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: FILLMORE;

MAN ARRESTED IN THREATS WITH PISTOL

A Fillmore man was arrested Sunday after he threatened two brothers with a handgun during an argument over a girlfriend, authorities said.

Leo Medina, 21, was taken into custody about 4:45 p.m. outside his home in the 300 block of B Street, Ventura County Sheriff's Lt. Kathy Kemp said.

Kemp said Medina began arguing with Steve Segovia, 26, of Fillmore over a former girlfriend.

The argument became heated and Medina brandished a 9-millimeter semiautomatic handgun, Kemp said, adding that he threatened to shoot Segovia and his brother, Jessie, 26.

Sheriff's deputies arrived before any shots were fired.

No one was injured. AARON CURTISS
