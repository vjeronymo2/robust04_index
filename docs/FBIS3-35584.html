Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Rome January 29 TASS -- Upon the completion of his official visit to Italy Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin left Rome for Switzerland today where he will take part in an annual session of the World Economic Forum in Davos. On the eve of his departure the Russian prime minister told ITAR- TASS that he was fully satisfied with the results of his meetings with the Italian leaders. The people and government of Italy have again stated about their support of Russia. At the same time they heard firm assertions that we intend to continue the course of reforms.
