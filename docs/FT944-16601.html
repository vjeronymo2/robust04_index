941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico gains 1.6% Equities closed up 1.68 per cent driven higher by Wall Street's strong rally and in a technical rebound after accumulating losses of 7 per cent since Sept. 28. The IPC index closed up 43.96 points at 2,665.59. Volume totalled a moderate 1.13bn new pesos on 83.4m shares traded. Traders said investors bought into the market on the back of huge gains on Wall Street. The value of foreign holdings of Mexican stocks rose nearly 1 per cent in September to Dollars 55.9bn, the Mexican stock exchange said. So far this year the foreign investment figure was up 2.34 per cent.
