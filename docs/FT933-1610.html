930923 FT 23 SEP 93 / Old BSB offices let to US company PART of Marco Polo House, a prominent building Battersea, London, and the former headquarters of British Satellite Broadcasting, has been let to QVC, a US home shopping television organisation. The BSB section of the 90,000 sq ft building was vacated shortly after the company - in which Pearson, owner of the Financial Times, had a stake - merged with Sky Television in 1990. The Observer newspaper occupies the other part of the building.
