910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Sales raise Pounds 7.9m at Cray Electronic Cray Electronic Holdings, the telecommunications, software and instrumentation group, has agreed to sell GRP Material Supplies for Pounds 5.1m cash to Wellmar, a company controlled by Mr Les Kyle, GRP's managing director. Cray has also sold the freehold of its factory in Gosport, Hampshire, for Pounds 2.8m cash. The group said the disposal of Portsmouth-based GRP, which distributes reinforced materials and other associated products, was part of the strategy of focusing on its core business.
