April 13, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

BUSH PITCHING

As President Bush throws out the first ball signifying the start of the baseball season and our national pastime, let us all hope that when the World Series rolls around and George sits down about that time to talk to Mikhail Gorbachev that he manages to hold his own in the "World Serious" talks.

We certainly can't afford a "Bush League" performance when he makes that "pitch."

WILLIAM KRAUSE

Palm Springs
