930814 FT 14 AUG 93 / UK Company News: CSC Investment asset value 111.12p CSC Investment Trust had a net asset value per share of 111.12p at June 30 1993 compared with 112.47p a year earlier and 101.63p at December 31 1992. Net revenue for the half year fell from Pounds 30,139 to Pounds 19,180. An unchanged interim dividend of 1.5p is declared, uncovered by earnings per share of 1.16p (1.83p).
