Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Russian President Boris Yeltsin today signed an edict on the members of the Russian Security Council. The presidential press service reports that the council's members are confirmed in their posts. They are federal counterintelligence service director Nikolay Galushko, Defense Minister Pavel Grachev, Interior Minister Viktor Yerin, Justice Minister Yuriy Kalmykov, Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, and also Foreign Intelligence Service Director Yevgeniy Primakov, Sergey Shakhray, nationalities and regional policy minister, and Sergey Shoygu, civil defense and emergency situations minister.
