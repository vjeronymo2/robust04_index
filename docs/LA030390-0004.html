March 3, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

HERE'S ONE AWARD HE HASN'T WON YET

The NHL season is almost over and people are starting to wonder about postseason awards.

According to the latest stats, there is a tie for the coveted Lord Robitussin trophy, between Wayne Gretzky and Larry Robinson of the Kings.

The Lord Robitussin, of course, is awarded to the player who coughs up the puck the most times during the season.

Good luck to both of you. Either one of you truly deserve it.

STAN PAGE, Los Angeles
