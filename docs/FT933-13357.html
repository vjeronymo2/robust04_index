930720 FT 20 JUL 93 / Parliament and Politics: MPs' costs paid to organisations ABOUT two thirds of the 651 MPs this year authorised payments from their Office Costs Allowances direct to companies or other organisations, including constituency associations, Mr Tony Newton, leader of the House, said last night. He was responding to Mr Bob Cryer (Lab Bradford S), a member of the committee on members' interests, who asked how many MPs 'have their secretarial allowance paid into private limited companies'. MPs may claim up to Pounds 40,380 a year on the allowance.
