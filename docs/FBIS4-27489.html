BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 28 -- Vice Premier Phan Van Khai received in Hanoi yesterday German Minister of Cooperation and Development Carl-Dieter Spranger and his party now on a working visit to Vietnam. Speaking to his guests, Vice Premier Phan Van Khai reiterated the fine development of Vietnam-Germany relations in recent years. On behalf of the prime minister, Vice Premier Khai expressed his thanks to the German Government and people for their assistance and cooperation with Vietnam in socio-economic development, especially in afforestation, environment protection, poverty alleviation and hunger eradication and in generation for workers returned from Germany. The vice premier highly valued the possibility in capital and technology of Germany and noted that the Vietnam-Germany relations of cooperation and friendship in the interest of the two countries should be further promoted in accordance with the potential of Vietnam and Germany. He also said that Vietnam will create favourable conditions for German businessmen to invest into Vietnam.
