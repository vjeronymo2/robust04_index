Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [SVOBODNAYA GRUZIYA "Blitz Poll" -- figures as published] [Text] How do you assess the results of the Georgian-Abkhazian negotiations in Geneva [on 11-13 January]? The Georgian side was forced to make concessions -- 36.5 percent. The Abkhazian side was forced to make concessions -- 1.6 percent. The Georgian side achieved indisputable success -- 7.5 percent. The Abkhazian side achieved indisputable success -- 5.1 percent. Both sides made compromises -- 23.5 percent. Difficult to say -- 25.9 percent.
