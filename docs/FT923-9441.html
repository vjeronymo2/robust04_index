920805 FT 05 AUG 92 / World News in Brief: US marines land in Kuwaiti port for manoeuvres Hundreds of US marines landed armoured vehicles and heavy equipment at Kuwait's Shuwaikh port at the start of Exercise Eager Mace, which will involve more than 5,000 US troops in live-fire and tank manoeuvres this month. The exercises come at a time when Kuwaitis have been unsettled by Iraq's restating of its claim to the emirate. UN Council under fire over Iraq, Page 3
