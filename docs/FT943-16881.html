940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / World Stock Markets: S Africa picks up from lows Johannesburg picked up from the day's lows as the gold bullion price posted a gain, but investors remained nervous about the short term outlook. The overall index lost 17 at 5,404 and industrials shed 22 to 6,260 but the golds index managed a 19 rise to 2,088. De Beers dipped 50 cents to R107. The shares had been marked lower on Nasdaq overnight following Wednesday's announcement of first-half sales figures from its Central Selling Organisation which were slightly lower than expected. Anglos appreciated 50 cents to R226.50 but Gencor softened 50 cents to R10.50. Kloof moved forward 50 cents to R52.75, while Pepkor was down R1.25 to R23 in heavy volume.
