940112 FT 12 JAN 94 / International Company News: Videotron starts year with fall Videotron, Canada's second biggest cable television group, has reported net profit of CDollars 6.2m (USDollars 4.7m), or seven cents a share, for the first quarter ended November 13, down from CDollars 8.6m, or 13 cents, a year earlier, in spite of a 4 per cent gain in revenues to CDollars 159m. The group said the drop in overall profit was due to depressed Canadian broadcasting operations.
