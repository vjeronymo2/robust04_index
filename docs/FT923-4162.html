920908 FT 08 SEP 92 / The TUC At Blackpool: Scargill calls NUM to oppose all 'anti-union legislation' A CALL by Mr Arthur Scargill, president of the National Union of Mineworkers, to oppose all 'anti-union legislation' introduced since 1979 was rejected by the Trades Union Congress yesterday. However, the annual conference carried a motion calling for unions to determine rulebooks in accordance with International Labour Organisation conventions. Leftwingers interpreted the motion as proposing the dismantling of laws that force unions to elect officials and ban secondary action.
