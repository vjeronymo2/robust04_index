BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Unal Erkan, governor of the state of emergency region, has said that 300 terrorists were killed in operations carried out in the region last month. Opening the new Security Directorate buildings in Hizan and Mutki, two districts of Bitlis, Erkan said that the terrorist organization has been almost totally destroyed. He stressed that the antiterrorist struggle will be stepped up even more from now on. The governor pointed out that the rate of participation in the local elections was high in the region, adding: This is because the people believe in democracy. Erkan called on the citizens to help the newly elected local officials.
