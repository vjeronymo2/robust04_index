Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 2 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to Hassan the second, king of Morocco, on the occasion of the national day of the Kingdom of Morocco. President Kim Il-song wished the king and people of Morocco greater achievements in the work for building a prosperous Morocco. He expressed the belief that the friendly relations between the two countries would develop on good terms.
