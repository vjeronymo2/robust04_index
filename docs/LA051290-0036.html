May 12, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

MAN'S REMAINS FOUND NEAR RURAL RAINBOW

Hikers have discovered the skeletal remains of a man in the rural community of Rainbow in San Diego County, authorities said.

Sgt. Steve Wood of the Sheriff's Department said the hikers found the body -- which appeared to be that of a young male -- on a brushy hillside Thursday, about a quarter-mile west of Interstate 15 near South Rainbow Valley Boulevard.
