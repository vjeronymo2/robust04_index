910511 FT 11 MAY 91 / Appointments: Senior posts at HTV Group HTV GROUP has made changes in its senior management. Mr Huw Davies ceases to be chief executive of HTV Cymru/Wales to concentrate on his duties as group director of television. Mr Emyr Daniel has been appointed managing director of HTV Cymru/Wales, and to the group board. He continues as director of programmes for HTV Cymru/Wales. Mr Ted George, a main board director, succeeds Mr Ron Evans on his retirement as managing director of HTV West. Mr Steve Matthews becomes director of programmes, HTV West, when Mr Derek Clark retires next year. Mr John Elfed Jones has been appointed group deputy chairman. He is chairman of Welsh Water, and will succeed Mr Idwal Symonds when he retires as chairman of HTV Cymru/Wales at the end of next year. Joining the board of HTV Cymru/Wales is Professor Lord Griffiths of Fforestfach, chairman of the Centre of Policy Studies. Mr Colin Atkinson and Mr Symonds have resigned as joint vice-chairman of the group but remain as directors. ***** Sir Timothy Harford has been appointed deputy chairman of WAGON INDUSTRIAL HOLDINGS, Telford. He is chairman of KwikSave group, and deputy chairman of both Wolseley, and Wesleyan Assurance Society. ***** Mr Michael Munro has been appointed a non-executive director of FIFE INDMAR, Edinburgh. He is an executive director of Quayle Munro merchant bank, and a director of The Life Association of Scotland, Shanks & McEwan Group, and Pittencrieff. ***** SWISS BANK CORPORATION has appointed Miss Marta Rokosova as head of the property finance group, merchant banking division, London office. ***** RAMCO OIL SERVICES, Aberdeen, has appointed Mr Steven Bertram as financial director. He was financial controller and company secretary. ***** Mr David Heywood (pictured) has been elected a non-executive director of RENTOKIL GROUP following the retirement of Mr Michael Carr. Mr Heywood is executive deputy chairman of British-American Tobacco Co. ***** ASSOCIATED BRITISH PORTS HOLDINGS has appointed Mr James Shaw as managing director of its subsidiary, Grosvenor Square Properties. He was managing director of Thorn High Street Properties, part of Thorn EMI. ***** The first management committee of the LONDON PLATINUM AND PALLADIUM MARKET has been elected: Mr Colin A. Griffith, Credit Suisse; Mr Richard M. Lowish, Engelhard Metals; Mr David N. Saunders, Mase Westpac; Mr Philip Clewes-Garner, NM Rothschild & Sons; Mr Alan Austin, Johnson Matthey; and Mr Ernest C. Watkins, Sumitomo Corporation (UK). ***** FAMILY GUARANTEE CORPORATION, Brighton, has appointed Mr Martin Fielding as managing director. He was a senior manager, corporate finance division, Deloitte Haskins & Sells. ***** Mr Peter Yates has been appointed divisional executive director of SMURFIT UK print and packaging division, Windsor. He was chief executive of Smurfit Cartons (Witham). ***** Mr Michael Wilmot, global business unit director, UK, for ALEXANDER STENHOUSE, has additionally taken on responsibility for City branch business development. Mr Henry Koszarek has been appointed national marketing director. He continues as director responsible for major accounts, captive and technical operations. ***** Sir Greville Spratt has been appointed chairman of NATIONAL WESTMINSTER BANK'S City and West End regional advisory board, succeeding Mr Alan McLintock who has retired. Sir Greville has been on the board since 1989, is a former Lord Mayor of London, and a non-executive director of The Williams Lea Group. ***** TESTECH, Poole, has appointed Mr Tony Davies to its board. ***** BANK OF IRELAND has appointed Mr Michael Connolly as executive vice-president and head of commercial banking at First NH Bank, New Hampshire. He is managing director of Bank of Ireland Finance, and joined the bank in 1968. ***** NOP posters division, part of the MAI Information Group, has appointed Mr Richard Hearn as managing director. He was director of marketing at Arthur Maiden. ***** Sir John Wickerson (pictured) has been appointed non-executive deputy chairman of R. MANSELL, Croydon. He is managing partner of Ormerod Wilkinson, and replaces Mr Leon Hill who has retired. ***** Mr John Morris succeeds Mr Bill Havers as refinery manager at ELF OIL's Milford Haven refinery from June 1. ***** BET has appointed Mr Andrew Mills as director of corporate affairs. He has been with BET for a year, and previously was an associate director with Barclays de Zoete Wedd. ***** Mr David Poole, managing director of AMAG Aluteam UK, has been appointed vice-chairman of the ALUMINIUM STOCKHOLDERS ASSOCIATION.
