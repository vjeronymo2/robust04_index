Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] Azerbaijan has expressed its desire to establish better economic relations with Pakistan. This was stated by Azerbaijan Foreign Minister Gasanov while addressing the members of the Executive Committee of the Pakistan Federation of Chambers of Commerce and Industry in Karachi yesterday. He assured the Pakistani traders that every possible assistance will be provided to them in promoting bilateral trade relations. He called on the Pakistani businessmen to take advantage of the huge market for machines and daily consumer goods in Azerbaijan.
