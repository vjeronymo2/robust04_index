920807 FT 07 AUG 92 / Technology (Worth Watching): Portable PCs make the bestseller list Tired of reading paperbacks? Panasonic Business Systems, part of Japan's Matsushita consumer electronics group, has launched a portable palm-top player for books published electronically on 8cm compact discs. Similar in size to a paperback novel, the battery- or mains-powered Panasonic KX-EBP1 player includes a keyboard, LCD screen, audio and video output and works with electronic books produced to the EB-G (text and graphics) and EB-XA (text, graphics and sound) standards. It will also play standard audio CDs. The clamshell player, which is already available in Japan, is aimed at the business, educational and consumer markets. Panasonic: UK, 0344 853550. Matsushita Electric Industrial: Japan, 06 908 1121.
