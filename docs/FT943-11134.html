940801 FT 01 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Savills expands in Nottinghamshire Savills, the property surveyor and adviser, is buying Walker Walton Hanson, a Nottinghamshire-based surveying, agency and auctions company, for at least Pounds 1.8m. An initial Pounds 1.2m has been satisfied by Pounds 75,000 cash and 1.5m shares. The total consideration depends on net asset value at completion but will not be more than Pounds 1.9m.
