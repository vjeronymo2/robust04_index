941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / International Company News: Lyonnaise des Eaux set for cable-TV deal Lyonnaise des Eaux, one of France's largest construction and utlities groups, is expected to acquire the majority of the cable television network owned by Caisse des Depots, the state-controlled holding group, writes Andrew Jack in Paris. The purchase - expected to be for about FFr800m (Dollars 147.6m) - is likely to be finalised by the end of the year, sources close to the deal indicated yesterday.
