940309 FT 09 MAR 94 / Observer: Altogether now Far from the European Union resulting in some boringly homogenised bureaucratic idiom, it has actually thrown up some riveting divergences. Thus British proposals about unemployment have been published with translations adapted to local sensibilities. In the English text, Europe is referred to as the (pre-Maastricht) European Community; the French version calls it the (post-Maastricht) European Union. Thus are sensibilities soothed.
