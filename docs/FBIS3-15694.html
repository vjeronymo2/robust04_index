Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] At the Writers Union today, the Baltic Information Agency is being set up. The agency is being created as an open public foundation in accordance with the law of the Republic of Latvia on the regulations for public organizations and associations. The purpose of this foundation is to spread information about the Republic of Latvia, the activities of its government and non-government organizations, to inform the public about current events in Latvia and the world. It is being created as a non-government institution. It is not bound by government directives. In other words it will not express only and solely the official viewpoint of the government.
