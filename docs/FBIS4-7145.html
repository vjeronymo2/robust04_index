BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 22 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] sent a message of greetings on April 17 to Andreas Papandreou upon his reelection as president of the Panhellenic Socialist Movement. Expressing the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the WPK and the Panhellenic Socialist Movement would grow stronger and develop in conformity with the idea of independence, peace and friendship, the message wished the president a new success in his responsible work for the strengthening of the Panhellenic Socialist Movement and the progress of the country.
