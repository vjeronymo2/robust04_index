940712 FT 12 JUL 94 / UK Company News: TBI makes Pounds 13.8m property sale TBI, the property investment and development company, has sold its office building at Borehamwood, Herts, for Pounds 13.8m in cash to Postel, generating a Pounds 1.05m surplus to the net book value. The rental income from the building, which totals 79,502 sq ft with 286 parking places, is Pounds 1.17m per annum. Proceeds will be used to reduce bank debt.
