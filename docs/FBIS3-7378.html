Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, March 26 (KPL) -- The Nordic Development Fund (NDF) has signed here an agreement to release a U.S. $6.5 million loan to Laos' communication sector. The loan is to be used in the construction of nine new bridges on Road 13-south between the ferry point to Pakkading. The Swedish International Development Authority (SIDA), in this connection, also agreed to give a grant of six million kronas for hiring experts to supervise the construction of the bridges. The signing of the agreement was done following the review of the cooperation in the field by SIDA mission and the Lao Ministry of Communication, Transport, Post and Construction [CTPC] carried out recently. Signing the memorandum of understanding were Mr. Khamlouat Sitlakon, deputy minister of CTPC and the charge d'affaires of Sweden to Laos, Mr. Rolf Carlman.
