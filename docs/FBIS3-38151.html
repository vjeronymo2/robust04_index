Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] The indirect talks on the confidence-building measures drawn up by UN Secretary General Butrus-Ghali to help solve the Cyprus problem will soon begin in Lefkosa [Nicosia]. Based on diplomatic sources in Lefkosa, ANATOLIA reported that with the second letter he sent to the UN secretary general, President Rauf Denktas narrowed Greek Cypriot leader Glavkos Kliridhis' area of maneuvering. ANATOLIA reports that unless Kliridhis creates a new conflict issue, there is no reason why the indirect talks should not begin soon.
