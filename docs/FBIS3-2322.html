Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 4 (XINHUA) -- Hu Jintao, member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party (CPC), today stressed the need to deepen the reform of party school education so as to raise it to a new height. At a national working conference of party schools, Hu noted that in this vital period in historical development, the ideological construction of the party should be put high on the agenda. Hu, president of the party school of the CPC Central Committee, said that the party schools of all levels are responsible for cultivating party officials. He said that it is the basic task of the party to use Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics for theoretic study, publicity work and training of party officials. He called for improving the quality of the education, enhancing scientific research in the theoretic field, grasping the need for the party's education of cadres in this new period, and gradually setting up and improving the system of party school education in China.
