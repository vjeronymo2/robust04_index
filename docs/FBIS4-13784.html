BFN [Text] The Algerian ambassador in Madrid, Hafad Nasreddine, said today that the proposal on convening an international conference to bolster the peace plan in the Western Sahara deserved respect and appreciation, because it endeavored to consolidate the principles of human rights, peoples' right of freedom, independence, and their right of self-determination. The Algerian ambassador in Madrid said he regarded the conference as essential for enlightening and sensitizing the world public opinion with respect to the issue of peoples' liberation and their efforts to regain their dignity.
