Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] According to unofficial reports, the Supreme Court of Justice, during a plenary session, has appointed the members of the Military Court for the 1994-99 term. Army General Juan Manuel Bogado Velasquez was appointed Military Court president, replacing Gen. Ubaldo Jose Lopez Barrios. The magistrates have appointed as nonvoting members Navy Captain Francisco Hedia Robledo and Army Colonel Ramon Moreno Natera, an investigator in the 4 February events. The third nonvoting member will be an Air Force officer yet to be appointed. The court liaison will be selected from among these three officers, and the court reporter will still be Navy Captain Freddy Jose Ribas Pacheco, an investigator of the 27 November events and of some of the most important cases in military justice. Navy Captain Francisco Ortega Jugo will be assistant reporter. Ortega is currently a Defense Ministry legal adviser and was a Permanent War Council magistrate in Caracas.
