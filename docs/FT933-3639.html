930913 FT 13 SEP 93 / Construction Contracts: Foundations CEMENTATION PILING & FOUNDATIONS has won new contracts worth Pounds 6m. The largest is for piled foundations for a waterside cold store development at Tilbury Docks. More than 1,800 cast in situ piles will be constructed to depths of up to 21 metres with over one third being founded in the concrete inverts of the infilled dock and lock structures.
