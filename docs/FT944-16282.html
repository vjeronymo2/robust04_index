941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / World Trade News: GEC Alsthom in Romania - Contracts & Ventures GEC Alsthom, the Anglo-French engineering group, is setting up a joint venture in Romania for refurbishing the country's rail transport rolling stock. The new company, GEC Alsthom Faur Transport, will be 51 per cent owned by GEC Alsthom and 49 per cent by Faur, a leading Romanian rail equipment manufacturer. Financial terms were not disclosed.
