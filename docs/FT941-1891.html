940323 FT 23 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Lon & St Lawrence Net asset value per share of London & St Lawrence Investment Trust stood at 182.45p at February 28, against 175.32p six months earlier and 157.06p at the previous year end. Net revenue for the year more than trebled from Pounds 513,323 to Pounds 1.69m and earnings per share were 5.14p (2.7p). In November last year the trust merged with its associate company, Practical Investment Company, and in line with its stated intention to pay an interim dividend in May each year, the trust has announced a pay-out of 3.12p this time.
