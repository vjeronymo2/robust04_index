930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / International Company News: Henderson Land Development announces rise in net profits HENDERSON Land Development, one of Hong Kong's leading residential property developers, has announced a 47 per cent rise in net profits to HKDollars 2.04bn (USDollars 264.9m) for the six months to December 1992, up from HKDollars 1.38bn, writes Simon Davies in Hong Kong. Operating profits were HKDollars 1.83bn, and there was an increase to HKDollars 654m in the contribution from associate companies.
