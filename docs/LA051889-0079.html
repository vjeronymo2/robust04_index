May 18, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

No Results From Gibraltar Financial: The Beverly Hills-based parent of Gibraltar Savings said that it is not able to release its financial results for the quarter ended March 31 and that it cannot predict when, if ever, it will have enough information to allow it to prepare financial statements that fairly represent the company's condition. Gibraltar Savings has been placed in conservatorship by federal regulators, and Gibraltar Financial said its access to information has been severely restricted as a result.
