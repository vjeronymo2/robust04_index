920207 FT 07 FEB 92 / India denies receiving heavy water belonging to Norway India yesterday denied it had received 12.5 tonnes of heavy water that Norway said was diverted from a 1986 shipment it sold to Romania, AP-DJ reports from New Delhi. A Foreign Ministry official said all of India's heavy water was legally imported only from the Soviet Union and was used in atomic power reactors under international safeguards. 'The question of smuggling of heavy water does not arise.'
