Language: Somali Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] After failing in its harmful activities in Somalia, the UN Operation Somalia II [UNOSOM] wants to illegally collect taxes at the country's ports and airports to the detriment of the objective of the struggling people, Abdi Hasan Awaleh Abdi Qaydid, chairman of the Somali National Alliance [SNA] high committee for internal affairs, said in an address to a rally at the 1 July Independence Square in Mogadishu. [passage omitted] First, the rally was addressed by Abdulkadir Haji Mohamed, alias Luga-Dhere, member of the United Somali Congress [USC] Central Committee, who conveyed greetings to the people from Mohamed Farah Aidid, SNA and United Somali Congress chairman. He briefed the rally on new developments that occurred during the chairman's visits to some countries abroad. Luga-Dhere also said that UNOSOM has failed to trade on the Somali name to achieve its objectives. In conclusion, he said the world had been told clearly that UNOSOM would be held responsible for any problem that it might bring to the Somali people. Abdi Hasan Awale Abdi Qaydid said Admiral Jonathan Howe must shoulder the responsibility for his problems. He said the Somali people will never forget the destruction Howe has brought upon them. He appealed to the world to bring him to justice. Abdi Qaydid also spoke of the illegal export of wild animals through the country's ports and airports. [passage omitted]
