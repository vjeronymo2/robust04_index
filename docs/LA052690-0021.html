May 26, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

DON'T CAUSE TROUBLE

When will the papers start printing something about the 98% of the people in the Valley who don't try to cause trouble.

I'd like to see the papers ask people what types of things they'd like to see in the future. Porter Ranch is an example.

With a new mall planned for that area, why don't the papers ask what kind of stores we shoppers would like to have? Or how about asking businesses if they would be interested in locating there?

I will put my vote in now for Porter Ranch to have a Nordstrom so I won't have to drive so far to shop there.

LUCINDA DERANGO

Canoga Park
