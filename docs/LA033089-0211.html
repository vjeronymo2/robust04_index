March 30, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

NATION;

BUSH TO MEET PAN AM VICTIMS' KIN

President Bush will meet Monday with four family members of Americans killed when Pan Am Flight 103 exploded over Lockerbie, Scotland, the White House said today.

Bush will "hear their concerns," presidential spokesman Marlin Fitzwater said. The families are planning a memorial service that afternoon in Lafayette Park, across the street from the White House. Many have expressed indignation that warnings about a threat to an American airliner were circulated to embassies but not to the general flying public.
