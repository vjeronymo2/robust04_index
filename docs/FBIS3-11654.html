Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Carlos Lage, vice president of Cuba's Council of State, is on a working visit to Spain. He has met with Spanish Prime Minister Felipe Gonzalez and will meet with Foreign Minister Javier Solana, Economics and Finance Minister Pedro Solbes, and Tourism and Commerce Minister Javier Gomez-Navarro. Lage will be in Spain until 25 March. His visit reflects the normal ties and cooperation between the Spanish and Cuban Governments, as well as those between the governments of the cities of Madrid and Havana.
