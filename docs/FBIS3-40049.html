Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Before Final Round: Still Bare Majority for Entry Into European Union"] [Text] The latest opinion poll of the Integral Institute carried out on behalf of KURIER, indicates a bare, but clear agreement of the Austrians to the European Union [EU]. In a referendum, about 53 percent would currently decide in favor of Brussels. With 58 percent, men are even more prepared to say yes to Europe, women are more skeptical. The Austrians regard the field of agriculture and the farmers as the biggest problem in the EU negotiations.
