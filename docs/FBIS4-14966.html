BFN [Text] An hour ago, UN Secretary General Butrus-Ghali arrived in Minsk on an official visit. The honorable guest was met at the Minsk airport by Belarusian Foreign Minister Pyotr Krawchanka; Alyaksandr Herasimenko, chairman of the Minsk City Council; UN Envoy to Belarus Matthew Kahane, and other officials. Today in the evening at the House of External Economic Relations, the UN secretary general will hold talks with the foreign minister, and later he will meet with the CIS executive secretary. Butrus-Ghali is also expected to visit today the UN Mission to Belarus.
