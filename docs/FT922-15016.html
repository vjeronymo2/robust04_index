920403 FT 03 APR 92 / UK Company News: Overseas side puts Campari on Pounds 5.3m Campari International, the sporting leisure wear group, again benefited from its expansion into continental Europe with pre-tax profit ahead 10.5 per cent in 1991. For the 13 month period, turnover rose 37 per cent to Pounds 53.7m (Pounds 39.3m). Overseas accounted for 67 per cent, against 58 per cent, with particular advances in Benelux and Germany. Profit came to Pounds 5.27m (Pounds 4.77m). Earnings were 39.24p (37.39p) per share and the dividend is raised 1p to 12p, with a final of 9p.
