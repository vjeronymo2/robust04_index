December 28, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

1990 ORANGE COUNTY;

CHALLENGE FOR THE 1990S

Community leaders were asked what will be the biggest single challenge facing Orange County in the next decade and also their top five issues.

"Health Care:

"In the coming decade we need to take a fresh look at the challenges we currently face in the area of health care and find innovative approaches to respond to them. We have witnessed in Orange County the increasing costs of medical care for ourselves and our loved ones, as well as the increased demands being placed on our health care systems by uninsured or underinsured."

-Harriet M. Wieder

Orange County supervisor, 2nd District

Top 5 Issues:

* Air quality.

* Transportation.

* Health care.

* Water quality and quantity.

* Solid waste and hazardous waste.
