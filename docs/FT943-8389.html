940817 FT 17 AUG 94 / International Company News: S Korean refiners up Yukong, South Korea's largest oil refiner, reported net earnings up 22.4 per cent to Won37bn (Dollars 46m) for the first half of 1994, while sales rose 7.4 per cent to Won2,857bn, writes John Burton in Seoul. Lucky, the petrochemical and consumer products group, had a 61 per cent increase in profits to Won41.9bn, while sales rose 20 per cent to Won1,301bn. Ssangyong Oil, the third largest Korean oil refiner, reported 61 per cent profit growth to Won70.8bn. Sales rose 10.6 per cent to Won1,101bn.
