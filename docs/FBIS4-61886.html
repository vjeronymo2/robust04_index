BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan, 10 May -- Armenian Trade Minister Tigran Grigoryan was arrested in Armenia today. An ITAR-TASS correspondent learned this from sources close to the republic's Interior Ministry. It is reported that the minister was detained while receiving a bribe. Tigran Grigoryan is 46 years old. He worked for many years as a commodity researcher in Yerevan's fruit and vegetable trading system, becoming chief of this department in 1986. In 1992, he became first deputy minister, and has been the republic's minister of trade since May 1992.
