Language: Lithuanian Article Type:BFN [Text] Vilnius police detained three persons in a city apartment where they discovered and confiscated a kilogram of opium.... This is the largest amount of narcotic substances, worth almost $10,000, that has been detected in Lithuania. Over the last year, the police found 663 grams of illegally kept opium. According to medical people, opium is the most widespread drug in Lithuania. Narcotics are supposed to arrive in Lithuania from Asian countries. There are attempts to transit drugs through Lithuania as well. While inspecting a passenger train in Kybartai recently, customs officers confiscated five kilograms of ground poppy heads from three Russian citizens. Three Ukrainian residents were found carrying 32 kilograms of narcotic substances by car at the Latezeris customs house.
