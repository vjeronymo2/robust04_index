940525 FT 25 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Non-core disposals continue at Costain Costain, the construction and mining group, has continued its withdrawal from non-coal mining activities in the US with the disposal of Montana Talc Company for about Dollars 14m (Pounds 9.3m). The proceeds will be used to repay debt. In 1993, the talc mining business made profits before interest of Dollars 1.7m and at April 30 had a book value of Dollars 13.9m.
