BFN [Text] Today, National Assembly deputies continued work in the Ba Dinh Conference Hall to debate and pass chapters six to 17 of the draft labor code under the direction of National Assembly Vice Chairman Phung Van Tuu. The majority of deputies agreed to pass chapter six on wages, chapter seven on working hours and break times, chapter eight on work discipline and material responsibility, chapter nine on working safety and sanitation, chapter 10 on separate stipulations for female workers, chapter 11 on separate stipulations for juvenile and other workers, chapter 12 on social insurance, chapter 13 on worker unions, chapter 14 on labor disputes, chapter 15 on state management over labor, chapter 16 on state inspection of labor and punishment for labor law violations, and chapter 17 on the implementation clause. The drafting committee for the draft labor code presented the draft after receiving opinions from National Assembly deputies. At the conclusion of this afternoon's session, the National Assembly agreed to pass the entire draft labor code and empowered the National Assembly Standing Committee to reexamine all details a final time before presenting it to the state president for promulgation. Tomorrow, the National Assembly will continue its work in the conference hall.
