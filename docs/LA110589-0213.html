November 5, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL FOOTBALL ROUNDUP

Montclair Prep 55, Maranatha 0 -- Montclair Prep (9-0, 4-0) scored 42 points in the first half in eight plays and ran only 16 in the game at San Marino High.

Montclair Prep's Derek Sparks and Michael Jones rushed six times each. Sparks finished with four touchdowns and 132 yards. Jones had 108 yards and three scores. ANDY MEYERS
