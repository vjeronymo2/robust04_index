August 26, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Linebacker Chip Banks of the Indianapolis Colts was arrested and charged with drunk driving after speeding passed an Indiana state police car on Interstate 69 near Anderson, Ind., where the Colts' training camp is located.

Police said Banks registered a .10% blood-alcohol level on a portable test and again on a certified machine at the State Police post at Pendleton, Ind.
