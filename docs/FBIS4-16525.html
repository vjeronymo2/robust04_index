BFN [Text] Moscow, 15 April (AFP) -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin and Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk signed a deal Friday [15 April] to share out the disputed Black Sea Fleet on a 50-50 basis. But the agreement states Ukraine will use only between 15 percent and 20 percent of the fleet for its own purposes and will lease the remainder of its share to Russia, Ukrainian Defence Minister Vitaliy Radetsky said at a press conference.
