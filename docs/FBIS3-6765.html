Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 20 (KCNA) -- Papers here today observe the 38th anniversary of the national day of the Republic of Tunisia. MINJU CHOSON in a by-lined article notes that the achievement of independence by the Tunisian people was a fruition of their long-drawn-out struggle for freedom and liberation, and it marked an important occasion in opening a new chapter in the history of the Tunisian people. The article says: The Tunisian people are now making strides forward in their efforts to consolidate the national independence and build the national economy and culture so as to build an independent and prosperous new society. Although Korea and Tunisia are far away from each other geographically, they have the bonds of friendship. After the establishment of diplomatic relations, the two peoples have developed relations of friendship and cooperation on the principles of independence, equality and mutual benefit. The Korean people will, in the future, too, make every effort to expand and develop the friendly relations with the Tunisian people in various domains. They wish the Tunisian people greater success in their struggle for the independent development and prosperity of the country.
