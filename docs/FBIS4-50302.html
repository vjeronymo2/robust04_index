BFN [Text] Beijing, May 16 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Premier Li Peng met here this afternoon with five outgoing and new ambassadors to China. They are Turkish Ambassador Resat Arim, Argentine Ambassador Carlos Lucas Blanco and Venezuelan Ambassador Eduardo Soto-Alvarez, who are going to leave their current posts. The new ambassadors are Abdullah Jaafar from Brunei and Alessandro Quaroni from Italy.
