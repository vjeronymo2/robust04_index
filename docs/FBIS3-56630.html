Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] President Geydar Aliyev received Mahmud al-Sa'id, UN representative in Azerbaijan, on 17 January. Aliyev briefed his guest on the serious situation in the Republic and Azerbaijan's recent gains on the front lines. Stressing that Azerbaijan's troops control Fizulinskiy, Agdamskiy, Agderinskiy, and Kelbadzharskiy Rayons, Aliyev noted that the world has to be accurately informed on the situation in Azerbaijan. He also recalled his meeting with President Levon Ter-Petrosyan in Ashgabat and stressed that he wishes to find a peaceful solution to the dispute. President Aliyev said: We want to find a peaceful way to retrieve our territory. However, we shall not allow Armenia's occupationist forces to continue to tread on even an inch of Azerbaijan's territory. Later, Aliyev thanked al-Sa'id for the UN aid to Azerbaijan's refugees and said that he appreciated his visit to the front lines to meet with the refugees. Mahmud al-Sa'id said that the UN organization plans to maintain its humanitarian aid to Azerbaijan in 1994. He also said that UN aid will be maintained until the dispute is resolved. State Adviser Vefa Gulizade attended the meeting.
