Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, February 5 (ATA) -- Albanian minister of transports, Fatos Bitincka, has paid a one-day visit to Bonn at the invitation of Mr. Wissmann, German minister of transports. During the talks, the two sides agreed in principle to sign as soon as possible an agreement on roads, goods and passengers and a maritime agreement. Mr. Wissmann said that Germany will continue to make its contribution to the development of Albanian infrastructure through its support for Albania in international organizations. Mr. Bitincka also met with Mr. Schmidtbauer, minister at the cabinet of Chancellor Kohl. They focused on the development in Albania and the situation in the region. Mr. Schmidtbauer assured the Albanian minister of a continuous support by the German Government for Albania in the bilateral and multilateral aspects and presented some concrete possibilities of short-term, mid-term, and long-term cooperation. A working meeting was also held in the German Ministry of Foreign Economic Cooperation.
