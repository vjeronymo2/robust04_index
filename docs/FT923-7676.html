920815 FT 15 AUG 92 / Singapore journalists quizzed Three journalists on the newspaper Business Times are being questioned by officers of the Internal Security Department, the government said yesterday, AP reports from Singapore. Mr Mano Sabnani, managing editor of English and Malay newspapers for Singapore Press Holdings, said that Mr Patrick Daniel, editor of Business Times, associate editor Margaret Thomas, and senior reporter Ms Anna Teo had been taken to the Internal Security Department for questioning. Officers of the department searched the offices of Business Times on Thursday, the Home Affairs Ministry said. It said an investigation of alleged use of classified information was being conducted under the Official Secrets Act.
