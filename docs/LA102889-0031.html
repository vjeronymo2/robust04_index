October 28, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BAY AREA QUAKE AFTERMATH AND FREEWAY SAFETY

What in the hell is the federal government doing to help? Loans to help? The countless victims are in desperate need of help now -- not a loan. How about giving money to help? Taxpayers deserve a break from the government now!

When foreign countries asked the U.S. for help, we gave millions and millions away of our tax dollars. Here at home, it's a loan. One has to qualify for a loan. How can one qualify when he is totally poor now?

I am also sick of people gouging at a time like this. Where is their compassion for those who have an urgent need? They should hang by their fingers!

TIM LUM

San Francisco
