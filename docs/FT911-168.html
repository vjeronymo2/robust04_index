910514 FT 14 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Wm Cook buys MS offshoot MS International, the diver-sified engineering group, is to sell Mech Cast, its steel alloy foundry business, to William Cook for Pounds 4.2m in cash. Mr Michael Bell, MS chairman, said that the disposal would transform the MS balance sheet and leave the group without borrowings. In the year to April 28, 1990 Mech Cast incurred a loss before tax of Pounds 57,528 from sales of Pounds 4.83m. However, indications are that in the year 1990/91, Mech Cast made operating profits of about Pounds 250,000.
