940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Metal Bulletin A 39 per cent increase in pre-tax profits, from Pounds 871,300 to Pounds 1.21m, was announced by Metal Bulletin, the business publisher, for the six months to June 30. Turnover rose from Pounds 6.91m to Pounds 7.96m. Earnings per share worked through at 8.1p (6p), and the interim dividend is 4p (2.9p).
