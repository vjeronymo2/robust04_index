March 1, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

Bush Cancels Paramount Visit: President Bush, who had been scheduled to take a tour of Paramount Studios this morning, instead will visit the new North County Correctional Facility in the hills below Castaic, the White House said. A spokesman said that the studio visit was scrubbed for logistical reasons and that Bush would still be able to talk to studio executives at a breakfast speech Friday before the Academy of Television Arts & Sciences. CLAUDIA PUIG
