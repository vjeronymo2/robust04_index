Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Communique issued by Communications Minister William Jaramillo in Santa Fe de Bogota; date not given; from the "TV Today" newscast] [Text] The communications minister reminds all radio broadcasting media, television stations, cable television companies, and regional television organizations to comply with the following electoral rules: 1. The broadcast of video or voice campaign advertisements is prohibited as of 0000 on 12 March until the polls are closed on 13 March 1994. 2. There is a ban on radio and television interviews with the candidates during the same period. 3. Only public order reports confirmed by official sources may be broadcast on election day. 4. In accordance with Decree No. 397 of 1994, Article 3, the media may disseminate information on the electoral results only after the polls close. 5. During the voting, the media may only report on the number of people who cast their votes and indicate the corresponding voting tables. 6. In accordance with National Electoral Council Resolution No. 066 of 1994, Article 1, no results or predictions based on data obtained from exit polls may be broadcast. 7. In accordance with National Electoral Council Resolution No. 066 of 1994, Paragraph 1, no predictions or projections may be broadcast, even if they are based on polls or official data. 8. The Communications Ministry will ensure compliance with Law 84 of 1993, Decree Nos. 397, 463, and 540 of 1994, National Electoral Council Resolution Nos. 075 of 1993 and 066 of 1994, and current legal provisions to guarantee the integrity of the electoral process.
