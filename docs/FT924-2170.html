921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 7m orders won by Willmott Dixon WILLMOTT DIXON SYMES' order book has been given a boost with awards of Pounds 7m worth of building work in London. In Covent Garden Willmott Dixon Symes is working on the refurbishment of two adjoining Victorian buildings at the junction of Maiden Lane and Southampton Street. The Pounds 1.2m contract, for National Provident Institution, involves knocking the two buildings into one, carrying out a total internal refit and considerable structural work on the exterior of the buildings to restore their period brick facades. A similar contract, worth Pounds 867,000, is under way in Hammersmith, where a Georgian building is being converted into a Deals restaurant as part of the Bredero Centre West development.
