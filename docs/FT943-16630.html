940704 FT 04 JUL 94 / Korean summit media ban North and South Korea have agreed to exclude foreign journalists from accompanying 80 South Korean reporters who will travel with President Kim Young-sam to Pyongyang later this month for a meeting with North Korean President Kim Il-sung, writes John Burton in Seoul. The decision was taken as part of an agreement at the weekend between the two Koreas on detailed procedures for the summit on July 25-27.
