Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 21 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent a 60th birthday table to Chon Chae-yun, head of the Kim Chong-tae Electric Locomotive General Enterprise Branch of the Rolling Stock Design Office under the Ministry of Railways. Chon Chae-yun, a labour hero and a merited designer, projected "Red Flag No. 1", the first electric locomotive in Korea, and is now devoting himself to introducing heavy rail, mordernizing railways and consolidating the material and technical foundations of railways.
