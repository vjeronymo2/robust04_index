E. Technical Amendments The final rule amends the definitional provisions of §§552.13 and 563.22 of the regulations to reflect the expanded authority conferred by new section 10(s) of the HOLA. In addition, as noted above, the final rule makes additional technical and conforming changes throughout these sections to simplify and clarify the application and notice procedures applicable to all mergers and other combinations involving savings associations.
