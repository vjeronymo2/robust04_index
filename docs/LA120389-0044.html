December 3, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

BAN OF FETAL TISSUE EXPERIMENTATION NOT POSSIBLE UNDER THE CONSTITUTION

Schrage's cold and merciless article on the use of aborted babies' bodies for medical research makes the new breed of horror movies look like a little girl's tea party.

The final irony was the phrase "the gift of life" in a quote by Dr. Willard Gaylin. I don't think it is a fitting remark for such circumstances.

Schrage remarks that, for the record, he is pro-choice. He need not have told us.

MARION FORD

GLENDALE
