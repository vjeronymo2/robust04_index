921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Increased deficit at Bristol Scotts Bristol Scotts, which owns the Bristol greyhound stadium and runs the Scotts restaurant chain, reported a deficit of Pounds 500,000 before tax for the six months to June 30. The outcome, which compared with a loss of Pounds 480,000, came on turnover of Pounds 5.09m (Pounds 5.75m). Losses were 9.07p (8.52p) per share.
