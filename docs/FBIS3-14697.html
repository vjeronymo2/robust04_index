Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 1900 GMT on 29 March reports: "Two IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldiers were slightly injured when stones were thrown at them in the al-Jalazun refugee camp near Ramallah tonight. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that in Khan Yunus, terrorists fired several rounds at a Border Police base. No one was hurt. The policemen returned fire and the terrorists fled. "An explosive device was hurled at an IDF patrol in the al-Nusayrat refugee camp. The device exploded, but no one was injured. Several Molotov cocktails were thrown at IDF patrols in the village of Awsarin near Nabulus and in Qabatiyah near Janin. There were no casualties in either incident. "Border policemen forcibly dispersed dozens of settlers who tried to enter the Cave of the Patriarchs and to pray there tonight." An announcer-read report on Jerusalem Israel Television Channel 1 Network in Hebrew at 1800 GMT on 29 March states that "a total of 75 Palestinians were wounded in the territories today. In a leaflet disseminated today, the Gaza chapter of Fatah calls on residents to clash with IDF troops and to hold a three-day general strike." Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 1500 GMT on 29 March reports: "In the wake of riots staged in the vicinity of schools in most the West Bank today, the defense establishment has decided to close all the Arab schools in Judaea and Samaria. The Nabulus University was closed by its board of directors after riots and clashes with soldiers were staged near the university."
