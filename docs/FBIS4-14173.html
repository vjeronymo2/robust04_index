BFN [Text] Cairo, 27 Apr (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak has asked the Yemeni leaders to exercise self-restraint and to avoid intensifying the problems in Yemen. He warned of the gravity of any escalation in the situation and any acts of violence. Mubarak made this statement during two telephone calls he received this afternoon from Yemeni Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid and from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. He told Salih and al-Bid that confrontation between Yemeni brothers and forces will be detrimental to Yemeni interests and will threaten the country's security, stability, and unity of its sons. He called for settling their differences through dialogue and reason.
