921002 FT 02 OCT 92 / Survey of Software at Work - Autumn 1992 (14): Percom PMS - Product Profile Supplier: Percom, a UK company formed in 1982 and owned by the founders, plus five financial institutions. Percom has 40 employees. Turnover: Pounds 2.5m in 1991-92; Pounds 2.2m in 1990-91. Customers: more than 1,000 in the UK and Europe. Product: Percom PMS (Personnel Management System), version 3.61, launched 1985. Add-on modules include recruitment, training administration, organisation management and grade management. Percom offers two-way links to other systems. Hardware: IBM-type personal computers running the DOS operating system. Price: from Pounds 5,400; maintenance is 15 per cent of the software price. Sales and support: sold direct; Percom offers consultancy, implementation, project management, training and help-desk services. Various users' views: 'A professional company . . . an established product; did not require bespoke work; good back-up; good training and manuals; the split of the database into people and posts is simple and convenient.' No users of other products, interviewed by Software At Work, had specific reasons for rejecting Percom, although users of Peterborough Software's personal computer package said they found that more flexible.
