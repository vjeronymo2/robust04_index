930313 FT 13 MAR 93 / JCB wins green belt battle THE GOVERNMENT is to allow JC Bamford Excavators, one of the UK's largest privately owned groups, to build a factory in the green belt near Cheadle, Staffordshire. The Department of the Environment ruled that although the development was inappropriate 'in this most exceptional of cases' economic benefits outweighed such harm as might be caused to the green belt.
