Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] As of today, the activities of the Khabarovsk Kray inner soviet are discontinued. RIA reports that a resolution on this was signed by Viktor Ishayev, the head of administration of Khabarovsk Kray . Meanwhile, the apparatus of the dissolved soviet will function until 13 March. Elections to the kray's bodies of representative power and local self-government are to be held on that day. It is anticipated that specialists on the apparatus of the former inner soviet will help organize the elections and hand over documentation to the new body.
