Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The government will continue to urge Pakistan at every available opportunity to stop its support to subversion and terrorism directed against India. The minister of state for external affairs, Mr. R.L. Bhatia, informed the Rajya Sabha in a written reply that New Delhi has apprised the international community of Pakistan's role in these activities. He termed this approach by Pakistan as totally unacceptable and in violation of the Simla agreement and universally accepted norms of international conduct. The Lok Sabha was told that there has been an increase in firings by the Pakistani troops at Indian posts in the Uri, Punch, and Rajaori sectors during the past few months. The minister of state for defense, Mr. Mallikarjun, told the house in a written reply that all such developments are carefully assessed and appropriate and timely steps taken. He told the members that our troops are fully prepared to meet any eventuality.
