Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] On Tuesday in the Kremlin, Russian President Boris Yeltsin met with Chairman of Russian Federal Assembly's Federation Council Vladimir Shumeyko. Our Interfax correspondent learned that during the conversation the officials discussed issues related to the work plans of the parliament's upper chamber. In particular, they spoke about settlement of the Ossetian-Ingush conflict, in light of upcoming discussion of this issue in the parliament, they also touched upon relations with Chechnya. Shumeyko also informed the president about the likelihood of the Civil Code coming up for consideration in the Federation Council. Yeltsin and Shumeyko discussed the situation in the country in detail in the context of the amnesty announced by the State Duma. On the same day Yeltsin is to meet with the State Duma speaker Ivan Rybkin.
