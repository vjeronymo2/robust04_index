BFN [Text] The three-day international seminar on Indian Ocean security and stability in post cold war era has ended in Islamabad today. Addressing the concluding ceremony, Defense Minister Aftab Shaban Mirani said Pakistan remains steadfast in its commitment to the goal of nonproliferation and our nuclear program is entirely peaceful. He said Pakistan will continue its endeavors toward creation of an equitable and nondiscriminatory and nonproliferation regime in South Asia.
