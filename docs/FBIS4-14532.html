BFN [Text] A crowd of about one thousand supporters of late ex-president of Georgia Zviad Gamsakhurdia held a rally in Tbilisi on Thursday to mark his birthday and three years of Georgian independence. A big crowd had also gathered outside his home in the city with demonstrators later staging a march along the central street of the Georgian capital. They carried slogans condemning government policies including Georgia's entry into the Commonwealth of Independent States. No incidents were reported.
