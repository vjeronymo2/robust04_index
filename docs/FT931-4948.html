930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / Management (The Growing Business): China backs capital venture - In A Nutshell What is claimed to be the first government-backed venture capital company in China, the Guangdong Science & Technology Venture Capital Company, has been launched. The company has two funds: a Dollars 7m, (Pounds 4.8m) open-ended fund in which the Industrial Commercial Bank of China, CITIC Industrial Bank, Guangdong Development Bank and Guangdong Science & Technology Commission, are investors; and a Dollars 10m, 10-year closed-end fund in which the commission and an unnamed US investment company are investors. The funds aim to develop technology, help finance small and medium-sized companies and find global markets for products and services in areas such as communications, new materials and textile manufacturing.
