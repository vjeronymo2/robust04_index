BFN [Text] The commissioning ceremony of the Bangladesh Naval Ship [BNS] Shaheed Ruhul Amin was held at BNS Isa Khan base at Chittagong port this morning. Addressing the ceremony, President Abdur Rahman Biswas said the government has taken initiatives to build up a strong navy for safeguarding sovereignty of the country. President Biswas disclosed that a plan for setting up a marine research institute and procuring a survey vessel is now under active consideration of the government. He further said there is possibility of adding more imported vessels soon to Bangladesh naval fleet to protect marine resources within the maritime boundary of the country. Our Chittagong representative says the ship procured from the United Kingdom having most modern surveillance equipment will be used for safeguarding about 44,000 miles of coastal area and maritime boundary of Bangladesh.
