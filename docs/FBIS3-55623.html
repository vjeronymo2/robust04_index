Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Former Ukrainian Prime Minister Leonid Kuchma Has Arrived in Minsk"] [Text] On 10 January, former Ukrainian Prime Minister Leonid Kuchma arrived in Minsk at the invitation of the Belarusian Confederation of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs. He met with Mikhail Lawrinovich, president of the Belarusian Scientific-Industrial Association [BSIA]. During talks, the parties discussed cooperation between the BSIA and the Ukrainian Association of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs [UAIE], headed by Leonid Kuchma. On 11 January, Leonid Kuchma will meet with a group of deputies of various levels, representatives of government structures, and various economic and public organizations. A press conference regarding the results of this meeting will be held after the end of the talks. Tomorrow, Leonid Kuchma is going to visit one of the larger enterprises in Minsk; it will likely be the Minsk automobile factory. BELAPAN has learned that the parties are going to sign a cooperation agreement between BSIA and the UAIE. The visit by Leonid Kuchma will conclude on 12 January.
