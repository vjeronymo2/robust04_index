BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Moscow April 28 TASS -- The Russian President's National security Adviser Yuriy Baturin denied mass media reports that President Boris Yeltsin was dissatisfied with his yesterday's meeting with intelligence chiefs. In an interview with ITAR-TASS on Thursday, Baturin said that the meeting was held in a candid atmosphere and helped expose many problems and set new tasks. The president was satisfied with the meeting, he added.
