920124 FT 24 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Wm Ransom 7% higher at Pounds 0.25m A small rise in turnover coupled with control of costs helped William Ransom & Son, the Hertfordshire-based manufacturing chemist, increase interim pre-tax profits by 7 per cent from Pounds 235,000 to Pounds 251,000. The company said that sales for the third quarter showed a considerable increase on the comparable period. Turnover in the six months to September 30 was Pounds 3.17m (Pounds 2.99m). Earnings per share were 1.16p (1p) and the interim dividend is unchanged at 0.525p.
