Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Indian Prime Minister P.V. Narasimha Rao met with Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina. During that meeting in New Delhi, Robaina gave Rao a message from Commander in Chief Fidel Castro, president of the Councils of State and of Ministers. Rao said that his country is interested in strengthening its bilateral relations with Cuba. After his meeting with the head of the Indian Government, Robaina stated that Cuba opposes foreign intervention in India's domestic affairs and highlighted India's important role in the Nonaligned Movement. Robaina concluded his official visit to India and departed for Malaysia as part of his tour through Asia, which will conclude in Vietnam.
