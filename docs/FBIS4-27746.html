BFN [Text] Zheng Hongye, chairman of the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade, and his 20-member Chinese economic and trade delegation, visited the Council of Trade of Thailand [CTT] on 31 May. The delegation was warmly welcomed by Acting Chairman of the CTT Zheng Weichang [Thai name: Wichian Techaphaibun] and other CTT executive members. In his welcoming speech, Zheng Weichang said: The CTT chairman asked me to receive you because he is leading a delegation on visits to Western European and South American countries. I, on behalf of the CTT, extend a warm welcome to the Chinese economic and trade delegation. The CTT and the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade have maintained close contacts for a long time. CTT Chairman Suwit Wangli led a delegation to visit China last year in July. The visit achieved great success because of the assistance provided by the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade. Zheng Weichang continued: I am very glad to learn that your delegation is visiting Thailand to open the Thai-Chinese International Trade Promotion Center. The CTT would like to take this great opportunity to welcome this cooperation between the two sides. This is a concrete achievement of trade, business, and investment cooperation between Thailand and China. I wish this joint venture bright prospects and great success. After that, the CTT hosted a luncheon in honor of the guests. The two sides exchanged views on trade and investment between the two countries.
