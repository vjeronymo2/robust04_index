Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] The presidents of the three Baltic countries met today in the Lithuanian town of Palanga. As the presidents noted in their concluding communique, the meeting proceeded in a friendly atmosphere of mutual understanding. The leaders of the Baltic countries expressed satisfaction at what the Baltic countries have achieved during the period of closer cooperation that has passed since their last meeting in Tallinn last December. The talks were especially concerned with questions of Baltic relations with the European Union, NATO and other international organizations. An agreement was signed on the creation of the consultative coordinating organ, the Baltic Presidential Council.
