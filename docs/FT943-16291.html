940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / UK Company News: TR Technology net assets rise Net asset value per share of TR Technology rose by 40 per cent to 241.17p over the year to April 30. After-tax revenue amounted to Pounds 3.67m (Pounds 3.19m) and earnings emerged at 3.71p (2.7p). The dividend is lifted from 1.75p to 2.75p, which includes a 1p special.
