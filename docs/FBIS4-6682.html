BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 19 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The statement issued by the MSDP [Mongolian Social Democratic Party] applying to the parliament of Mongolia and government notes the complicated political situation in the country due to a complete ignorance by the parliament and government of the numerous demands for the resignation of the government on the part of the MDA [Mongolian Democratic Association], UMS [Union of Mongolian Students] UNP [Union for New Progress] and MSDP. The life of some 39 people announced a hunger strike for the future of the country is endangering. [sentence as received] Joining in the political struggle for justice by a leader of the MSDP and MP [member of parliament] R. Gonchigdorj is an unprecedented event not ever met before in the states of democracy. The responsibility for the aftermath of the present crisis, the statement says, lies in the Great State Hural and government.
