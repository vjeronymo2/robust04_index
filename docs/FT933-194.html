930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / The Labour Party Conference: European action urged on jobless MR Ingvar Carlsson, leader of Sweden's Social Democratic Party, told delegates that 'unchecked capitalism' was to blame for the growth in unemployment throughout Europe. He called for a concerted drive by the Labour movement across the continent to stand up to market forces. Mr Carlsson advocated a European strategy for production and employment, with investment in new technology, infrastructure, public services and the environment.
