930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / UK Company News: John Haggas shows 8% rise to Pounds 981,000 John Haggas, the West Yorkshire-based worsted spinner, lifted pre-tax profits by 8 per cent in the six months to December 31. The increase to Pounds 981,000 (Pounds 911,000) was achieved on turnover up from Pounds 16.4m to Pounds 17.6m. However, directors said margins in the spinning division were still under pressure and 'likely to remain so'. The fabric side reported improved sales and profits. Earnings per share edged ahead to 3p (2.8p); the interim dividend is held at 1p.
