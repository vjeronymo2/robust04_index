BFN [Text] Damascus, June 27, IRNA -- The Iranian ambassador to Damascus, Mohammad Hassan Akhtari, here Sunday called on the Syrian Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' and discussed bilateral issues and the current developments in the region. Expressing his condolences over the martyrdom of scores of pilgrims in the holy shrine of Imam Reza in Mashhad, Al-Shar' condemned the savage act of bomb blast by the terrorist Mojahedin-e Khalq Organization (MKO). Referring to friendly relations between the two countries, the Syrian foreign minister voiced his country's support for Iran's stances in all domains.
