BFN [Text] For most people in Russia, there will be four days off during the upcoming May Day holiday: Saturday, 30 April; and Sunday, Monday, and Tuesday, 1-3 May. In accordance with a government decision, the management of any particular enterprise or organization may, in agreement with the trade unions, declare 3 May a working day. If citizens work on that day, they will receive an additional day off or monetary compensation at double time. Nikolay Chishenko, head of the department of labor in the government, clarified this for ITAR-TASS.
