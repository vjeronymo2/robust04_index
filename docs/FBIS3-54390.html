Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The conference of national consensus resumed today at the Palais des Nations. Here is our correspondent to give you the latest: [Unidentified correspondent] Good morning, listeners everywhere. The news from the conference of national consensus is that Abdelkader Bensalah, the official conference spokesman, held a news conference about 20 minutes ago. The main points at the news conference were, first, Mr. Bensalah's announcement that no final decision has been made with regard to the presidential body which will succeed the Higher State Council. Irrespective of everything the issue will be decided by the Higher Security Council before 31 January. Also, Mr. Bensalah stated that Youcef Khatib, chairman of the National Dialogue Commission and chairman of the conference, informed the conference participants that Abdelaziz Boutaflika, whose name has been mentioned a great deal in recent days in connection with the post of head of state and who has expressed his acceptance in principles, has reconsidered his position. This is the news from this morning's session, which continued behind closed doors.
