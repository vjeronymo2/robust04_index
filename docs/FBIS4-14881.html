BFN [Text] Moscow 5 April, ITAR-TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin received Czech Prime Minister Vaclav Klaus in the Kremlin on Tuesday morning. Welcoming the Czech premier, Boris Yeltsin noted that partnership between Russia and the Czech Republic is based on the treaty of friendship and cooperation between the two countries signed in Prague last August. According to the Russian president, the two countries continue to pursue their policy of cooperation despite certain difficulties in bilateral relations.
