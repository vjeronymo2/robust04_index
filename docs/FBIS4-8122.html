BFN [Text] Belgrade, 11 April (TANJUG) -- The Yugoslav Army's General Staff today stated that the bombardment of civilian and military targets in the Srpska republic carried out by the NATO air force last night was an act that might bring about unforeseeable military consequences. The Yugoslav Army is following the developments caused by this aggressive act. Bearing in mind the latest circumstances, all necessary measures have been taken to protect the territorial integrity of the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia, the Yugoslav Army's General Staff says in its statement.
