May 28, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

10 HURT DURING BRAWL AT PRISON IN NEW YORK

At least seven prisoners and three corrections officers were injured when a brawl broke out as hundreds of black and Latino inmates milled about in a prison yard, authorities said Saturday.

A small group of inmates started a fight that quickly escalated Friday at Greene Correctional Facility, a corrections spokesman said. About 80 inmates were transferred to other facilities Saturday.

Prison officials were investigating what caused the violence.
