Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 29 (KCNA) -- The New Year address of the great leader President Kim Il-song was reported by the British paper NEW WORLD, the Romanian paper NATIUNEA, the Brazilean paper A CLASE OPERARIA and Radio Citta Aperta of Italy. Seminars on the New Year address were held by the group for the study of the chuche idea of the Commercial College of Uganda, the group for the study of the chuche idea of teachers of the Chambogo Polytechnical College of Uganda, the Makonde group for the study of the chuche idea in Chinhoyi, Zimbabwe, and the chuche idea study group of the Voluntary Work Camps' Association of Nigeria. The speakers quoted President Kim Il-song as saying in his New Year address that last year the Korean people foiled the enemy's challenge and aggressive moves and honorably defended the dignity and security of their socialist motherland, thus fully demonstrating the unbreakable spirit of chuche Korea, that this was a victory of the revolutionary line of the Workers' Party of Korea and a great victory of the single-hearted unity of the leader, the party and the masses. They said however desperately the imperialists may attempt to destroy the socialist system of Korea, it is a mistake and stressed that the just cause of the Korean people is invincible. A reading session and a lecture on the New Year address were held at the cultural centre of Egypt.
