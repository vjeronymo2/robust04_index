BFN [Text] Ankara, June 9 (A.A.) -- Ukranian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko arrived here today to officially inaugurate the Ukraine Embassy. Zlenko was welcomed at the Esenboga Airport by Ambassador Bilal Simsir and Ukranian Ambassador Igor Turyanskiy. The visiting minister who is in Turkey to participate in the North Atlantic Cooperation Council is paying an unofficial visit to Ankara, Simsir said. Zlenko will return to Ankara in the afternoon [as received].
