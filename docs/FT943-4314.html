940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / Observer: Phil me in No doubt studying for an M Phil in the Evaluation of Public Services at Middlesex's Brunel University is a fascinating exercise. There is certainly a wealth of material on the doorstep. A research company ringing to inquire about the contents of the course was greeted by a voice on the switchboard professing ignorance of any such discipline. The caller was put on hold for some minutes before the operator returned. 'You still there? What course was it again?' A last-ditch attempt was made to oblige. The caller was transferred to the university's department of government - where an ancient answering machine cranked into action.
