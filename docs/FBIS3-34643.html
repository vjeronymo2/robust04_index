Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali today received the Palestinian president, Yasir 'Arafat at the Palace of Carthage. Yasir 'Arafat made the following statement after the audience: [Begin 'Arafat recording] I held a good, positive and warm meeting with the president. I acquainted him with all that has recently been agreed upon in Cairo between us and the Israelis, and the agreement which we signed. On my arrival in Tunis I sent the president a copy of this agreement so that he would be totally informed of its details. I also pointed out to the president the significance of our continuing consultations and contacts so that we could reach a final agreement, which we expect at the end of this month, God willing. This would enable us to precisely and faithfully implement what we signed in Washington last year -- last September. I listened to the advice and directives of the president, which I value highly. I thanked him, in my own name and in that of the Palestinian people, for all that Tunisia -- president, government and people -- gives to the Palestinian issue and to the Palestinian people. [end recording]
