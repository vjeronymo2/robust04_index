ACTION: Order to Show Cause.

SUMMARY: This document directs an Order to Show Cause against Roy E. Henderson, permittee of Station KHEN, Caldwell, Texas, to show cause why his license should not be modified to specify operation on Channel 297A in lieu of Channel 236A. Such modification would permit consideration of a Channel 236C2 allotment in College Station, Texas. The reference coordinates for a Channel 297A allotment are 30&hyph;33&hyph;31 and 96&hyph;34&hyph;50. See 56 FR 11982 (March 21, 1991). DATES: Comments must be filed on or before October 7, 1994.
