BFN [Text] Dr. Nabil Sha'th, official in charge of the international portfolio, held a series of meetings at the headquarters of the Palestinian Authority in the city of Gaza last night. He met with the commanders of the Palestinian police, as well as the head of the Palestinian security forces in the Strip to acquaint himself with problems facing the Palestinian security force since its deployment in the self-rule area. Dr. Sha'th had arrived in Gaza last night. He told Journalists that he was carrying an amount of money to pay the salaries of the employees of the Palestinian Authority's self-rule and policemen. He added he would prepare for the arrival of brother President Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] in the Palestinian self-rule area.
