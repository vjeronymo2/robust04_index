Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Mr. Asif Ali Zardari, MNA [member of National Assembly], is proceeding to Baghdad tomorrow as the prime minister's special envoy. He will hold talks with the Iraqi leaders on the release of three Pakistanis who have been sentenced to jail there. Two of the Pakistanis were engaged in mine clearing operations along the Iraqi-Kuwait border and the third was working with a Kuwaiti commercial establishment. They inadvertently entered the Iraqi territory.
