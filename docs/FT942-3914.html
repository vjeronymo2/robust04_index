940613 FT 13 JUN 94 / 'Brain drain' in science continues The 'brain drain' of scientists continues unabated, with one in five of the UK's recently qualified PhD scientists going overseas for further study or employment, the Institute of Manpower Studies says today. The IMS found, however, that UK industry is suffering no shortage of high-quality scientists, while unemployment levels for recently qualified PhDs are only about 3 per cent.
