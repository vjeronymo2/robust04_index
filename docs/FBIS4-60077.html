BFN [Text] The visiting three-member Pakistan naval delegation led by its navy chief, Admiral Sayeed M. Khan, met President Abdur Rahman Biswas at Bangabhaban [President House] in Dhaka yesterday. While exchanging views with Adm. Sayeed, President Biswas appreciated the cooperation between the navies of the two countries. The Pakistan Navy chief assured to continue their help to Bangladesh Navy for its development. He appreciated the progress of the Bangladesh Navy. Bangladesh Navy chief Rear Admiral Mohammad Mohaimenul Islam was present.
