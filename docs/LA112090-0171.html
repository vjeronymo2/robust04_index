November 20, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Woman misidentified -- An article Saturday misidentified a woman arrested in connection with the seizure of a Lancaster area marijuana farm.

The woman, Kathleen Gegax, is the wife of Michael Gegax of Arizona, federal drug agents said. The article identified her as the wife of Frank Gegax, a Lancaster man who was also arrested. Frank Gegax's wife also is named Kathleen Gegax, but she was not arrested.
