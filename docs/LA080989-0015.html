August 9, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

STATEHOOD FOR WASHINGTON, D.C.

The nation does not need another state that is smaller than Rhode Island. And the way to end the "colonial status" of Washington is to let it revert to Maryland, the way the south portion reverted to Virginia.

It sounds like Jesse Jackson is planning on running for governor of "New Columbia" and not the mayor of D.C.

LARRY BRIGGS

Twentynine Palms
