920929 FT 29 SEP 92 / International Company News: Korea approves open-ended Dollars 10m investment trust SOUTH Korea's Finance Ministry has approved a USDollars 10m open-ended investment trust by Korea Investment Trust for foreign investors, Reuter reports. The five-year trust is expected to be expanded to Dollars 30m, with investment spread over the Seoul stock market (80 per cent), the won call market and certificates of deposit.
