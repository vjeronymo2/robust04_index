920511 FT 11 MAY 92 / Construction Contracts: Runway repairs Wimpey is undertaking its first project in Czechoslovakia following the award of a contract to resurface one of the country's main airfields. The Pounds 5.3m contract, secured by Wimpey Asphalt from the state civil engineering contractor Inzinierske Stevey, forms part of an extensive refurbishment programme at Kosice Airport.
