Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] It is already one week since the government delegation of Tajikistan arrived in Moscow. Abdulmadjid Samadov, chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Tajikistan, who is at the head of the delegation, met Viktor Chernomyrdin, the prime minister of Russia, yesterday. The issues related to entry of the Republic of Tajikistan to the ruble zone of a new type were discussed in the course of the meeting. Before the meeting Abdulmadjid Samadov met Andrey Kozyrev, the minister of foreign affairs of the Russian Federation. Issues concerning relations between two states were discussed in the course of the meeting.
