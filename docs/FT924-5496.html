921126 FT 26 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Merrydown sees sparkle in new cider MERRYDOWN Wine, the East Sussex-based cider group, has signed a national distribution deal for its new Premium Draught Cider with the Gaymer Group, writes Graham Deller. Gaymer, the recent management buy-out from Allied-Lyons, will sell, keg and distribute the cider to the UK licensed trade for a minimum of five years. Mr Richard Purdey, Merrydown chairman, said the new brand 'performed extremely well in its test marketing, and we believe it offers exciting national potential.'
