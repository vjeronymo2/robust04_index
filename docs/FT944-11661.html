941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / International Company News: Kemira offering values group at FM4.56bn Shares in Kemira, the state-owned Finnish chemicals group, have been priced at FM38 each, raising FM1.14bn and valuing the whole company at FM4.56bn (Dollars 988m) in Finland's biggest initial public offering, writes Martin Brice. Pricing of the 25 per cent of the group to be sold was expected to be between FM37 and FM44 each for the 30m shares in the offering.
