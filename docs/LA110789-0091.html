November 7, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

CONLISK SAYS HE'LL SIGN WITH ARIZONA STATE

Robert Conlisk, a 6-foot-11 senior center at Los Alamitos High School, has orally committed to Arizona State and said he will sign a national letter of intent on Wednesday.

Conlisk averaged 17.9 points and 9.9 rebounds for Los Alamitos as a junior and was a first-team, All-Empire League selection.
