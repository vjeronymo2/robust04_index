BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Yezhov] [Text] Moscow May 31 TASS -- The recent package of presidential economic decrees was caused by the government's inability to achieve the quick adoption of economic bills at the State Duma, Russian Vice-Premier and Minister of Economy Aleksandr Shokhin told a news conference here today. The government had to ask the president to approve measures necessary for further economic reforms by his decrees, said Shokhin. "We plan to use presidential decrees and government resolutions for swift reaction to Russian economic processes," stressed the vice-premier. "Another package of presidential decrees will soon be signed". The package will include the prepared draft presidential decrees on sales of enterprises-debtors and regulation of the consumption fund of enterprises, as well as the decrees under elaboration on legal basis of foreign economic activities (to eliminate personal benefits in this sphere) and regulation of banking activities. The president is ready for joint and quick solution of major economic problems, stressed Shokhin.
