940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / Arts (Openings): Berlin This year's Berlin Festival, opening tomorrow, marks the German rediscovery of Berthold Goldschmidt, the 91-year old composer who began his career in Berlin before being driven out by the Nazis. He settled in London and became a British national in 1947. The festival includes performances of his Violin Concerto and two operas, Beatrice Cenci and Der gewaltige Hahnrei.
