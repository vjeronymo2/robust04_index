940108 FT 08 JAN 94 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Brazilian shares closed up 7.2 per cent on strong foreign activity. The Bovespa index of the 54 most-active shares finished at 47,640 points, up 3,223 from the previous close. Petrobras, the state oil monopoly, led the rally and soared 17 per cent to close at 56.50 cruzeiros reais.
