BFN [Text] Sirte, 27 Jun (MENA)-- Dr. Usamah al-Baz, first Foreign Ministry under secretary and director of the President's Office for Political Affairs, expressed his belief that a solution could be reached on the Lockerbie problem between Libya and the Western countries. In a statement he made before leaving Sirte with the delegation accompanying President Husni Mubarak, al-Baz said that a number of proposals on this issue were discussed during the meetings held by the Egyptian and Libyan sides. He stressed that a solution that upholds the two sides' sovereignty can be reached. He added that it was agreed to have the higher Egyptian-Libyan committee chaired by the two countries' prime ministers meet in Cairo during the first week of August and its meeting will be preceded by a meeting of the preparatory committee.
