November 25, 1989, Saturday, Southland Edition

LOBSTER BOAT AND CREW OF 5 MISSING IN ATLANTIC

The Coast Guard on Friday searched the Atlantic about 200 miles south of Martha's Vineyard for the five-man crew of a missing Connecticut lobster boat.

The Heidi Marie left its home port of Stonington, Conn., on Nov. 17. The boat's owner contacted the Coast Guard after the fishermen failed to make a scheduled radio call.
