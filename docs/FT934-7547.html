931119 FT 19 NOV 93 / Hopes rise at Swan Hunter for EU funds THE PROSPECT of Swan Hunter, the Tyneside shipbuilder in receivership, gaining eligibility for European Union intervention funding subsidy looked brighter last night. Mr Tim Sainsbury, industry minister, yesterday met Mr Karel Van Miert, EU competition commissioner, and argued for the first time that Swan Hunter should be eligible alone, rather than as part of funding for all the UK's warship yards. Mr Sainsbury said it had been a positive meeting and expected a decision within the next month. Access to intervention funding, which provides a 9 per cent subsidy on merchant shipbuilding orders, would enhance the sale prospects of the yard, which has been in receivership since May.
