920414 FT 14 APR 92 / International Company News: Aiwa takes stake in US electronics group AIWA, the Japanese consumer electronics company, is expanding its involvement in industrial electronics by taking a Dollars 5m, 26 per cent equity stake in Core International, the US maker of computer peripherals equipment. Aiwa said the investment was aimed at deepening co-operation. Aiwa supplies computer data cartridges for Core on an original equipment manufacturer basis. The deal repeats a familiar pattern in which Japanese companies acquire stakes in US software companies to try to supplement Japanese strengths in manufacturing and marketing.
