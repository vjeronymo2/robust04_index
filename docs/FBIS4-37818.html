BFN [Text] Doha, 23 Jun (WAKH) -- Qatari Foreign Minister Shaykh Hamad Bin-Jasim Bin-Jabr al-Thani today met with Yemeni Deputy Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Wahhab al-Anisi, who is currently visiting Doha. Doha radio reported the two officials discussed relations between the two countries and means of developing them, as well as the current conditions in the region. The Yemeni official left Doha today.
