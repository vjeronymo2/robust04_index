BFN [By Chris Moncrieff, PRESS ASSOCIATION political editor] [Text] President Clinton telephoned John Major tonight to express "profound sympathy" at the two British casualties in the helicopters disaster over northern Iraq. The Prime Minister said how sorry he too was to learn of the accident. The two leaders agreed on the importance of the work which the coalition team had been carrying out in the area on behalf of the Kurdish people. They also both expressed their sorrow that people devoting themselves to this work had lost their lives in this way, and their deep sympathy with the families of all those on the helicopters.
