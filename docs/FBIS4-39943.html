BFN [Text] Two Russian servicemen were killed in Malgobek in the Ingush Republic on 12 June. They were members of the peacekeeping forces deployed in the state of emergency area. The two were killed at midday in the town's market place, in front of witnesses. Vadim Moyseyev, major in the interior service, died from two shots in the back. Militia sergeant Danie Baydashev sustained seven bullet wounds. The killers escaped from the scene of the crime in a motor vehicle. However, their identity has been established through the efforts of the investigation group from the Russian Interior Ministry and Ingushetia's Interior Ministry and Procuracy. The search for the criminals is under way, RIA reports.
