921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / Construction Contracts: Manchester sports arena BOVIS NORTH is to build the Pounds 50m Victoria Station indoor sports arena. An essential element in Manchester's bid to bring the Olympics to the city in the year 2000, the arena will provide the high standard of facilities needed to stage a wide range of sports activities with seating for 18,000. Bovis North has started preliminary demolition. Building work will begin in January, with the first of four new platforms due to open in June.
