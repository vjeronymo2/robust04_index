Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 6 (KCNA) -- Over 500 members of 20 odd organizations reportedly held a rally in Seoul Saturday in protest against the treacherous opening of the agricultural market, defying suppression by the traitor Kim Yong-sam clique. "The ruin of agriculture subsequent upon market opening would cause serious problems including environmental pollution and destruction of culture," warned the ralliers in a statement, strongly urging the puppet authorities to have a "Uruguay Round Renegotiation". Meanwhile, students and members of labour organizations held a protest against the agricultural market opening in front of Seoul railway station and launched a signature campaign amid the active response of citizens.
