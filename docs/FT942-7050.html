940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / Prosecution over radiation incident The Health and Safety Executive is prosecuting Amersham International, the health science group based in Little Chalfont, Bucks, following an incident at the company's radiography plant at Harwell, Oxfordshire. It is alleged the company failed to take all reasonably practicable steps to restrict an employee's exposure to ionising radiation and during transfer of a radiography source an employee received an estimated radiation dose of about 4 per cent of the annual limit.
