Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to RID information from the U.S. Embassy in Minsk, on 14 February the Embassy will open a consular section for issuing all kinds on nonimmigrant visas. In some individual cases, the term for receiving visas may be up to 15 days. Therefore, the Embassy recommends applying for a visa at least 2 weeks ahead of a scheduled trip.
