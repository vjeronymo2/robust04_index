Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Awaiting Answer From Frckovski"] [Text] The urgent session of the Tetovo city parliament, held March 17 on the occasion of arresting several Albanians from Tetovo and its surroundings, and also attended, on insistence of the deputies, by [Interior] Minister Frckovski, brought several resolutions. The city officials demanded unconditional releasing of the arrested citizens, beginning with ex-PDP [the Party for Democratic Prosperity] Secretary Mithat Emini and ending with the most recently arrested group of Albanian citizens on March 16. Furthermore, they requested arrests and oppression of Albanians to cease in future, because, they claimed political process are fabricated in order to discredit NDP [the Peoples' Democratic Party] and PDP and the Albanians at all. They accuse the government of creating an atmosphere of anxiety with an aim to put PDP members under control and to present the Albanians as a factor introducing instability in the country. The session was interrupted to be continued March 22, when Internal Minister Ljubomir Frckovski and Justice Minister Tuse Gosev will appear in person and inform the Tetovo parliament of their decision on the submitted demands.
