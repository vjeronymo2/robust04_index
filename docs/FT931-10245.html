930210 FT 10 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Platon Intnl Platon International's board put shareholders on hold yesterday following Monday's bid from Wills Group. Shareholders were urged to do nothing until the directors had considered the offer with their financial advisers. The shares rose 5p to 26p yesterday. Mr Robin Meyer, Platon's chairman, will be writing to shareholders in the next few days.
