BFN [Text] Beijing, May 13 (XINHUA) -- Chen Muhua, vice-chairwoman of the Standing Committee of China's National People's Congress (NPC) and president of the All-China Women's Federation (ACWF), met with and hosted a dinner for a delegation from the National Council of Women (NCW) of Thailand here this evening. The five-member delegation, headed by Khunying Duangduen Bisalputra [name as received], president of NCW, is here as guest of the ACWF.
