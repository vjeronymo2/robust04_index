BFN [Text] The Liberian People's Party [LPP] has elected Dr. Togbah-Nah Tipoteh as the national chairman of the party. At an emergency meeting of LPP's high decision-making organ, the organizing committee, Dr. Tipoteh was elected by acclamation. A release from the LPP's office says Dr. Tipoteh's [word indistinct] comes in the wake of the former chairman, Dr. Wolokollie's request to the organizing committee to find a full-time chairman to prepare the party adequately for the pending elections. The organizing committee, in the release, commended Mr. Wolokollie for the leadership provided the party in its most difficult period and hoped that in his new assignment as special liaison of the party with the LNTG [Liberian National Transitional Government], Mr. Wolokollie will exhibit equal commitment in the discharge of his duties. Dr. Tipoteh, in his thanks to his solid partisans for his unanimous election, pledged continuity in the implementation of the general policies of the party. Meanwhile, Dr. (Joseph Volloh) has been named chairman of the policy council of the party. He replaces Dr. Togba-Nah Tipoteh.
