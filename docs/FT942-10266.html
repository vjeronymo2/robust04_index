940511 FT 11 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Wardle Storeys denies parachute speculation Wardle Storeys, the plastics and safety equipment group which makes specialist parachutes, issued a statement yesterday denying its GQ parachutes were involved in a weekend incident involving paratroopers injured when they landed on hard ground during a Nato exercise in Sardinia, writes Paul Taylor. The company issued the statement after its shares fell amid market speculation about the incident. The shares, which initially fell to 398p, subsequently rallied to close 10p lower on balance at 400p.
