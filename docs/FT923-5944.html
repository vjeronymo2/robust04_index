920827 FT 27 AUG 92 / UK Company News: BTR to buy Pirelli's Brazilian offshoot BTR, the industrial holding company, is buying Getoflex Metzeler, the Brazilian industrial rubber product business, from Pirelli of Italy for about Pounds 10m cash. In 1991 Getoflex, which has three factories in Sao Paulo, incurred an operating loss of Pounds 800,000 on sales of Pounds 24m. Net assets being acquired amount to Pounds 22m. BTR has also completed on schedule the acquisition of Pirelli's European sealing and anti-vibration businesses following clearance from the EC.
