BFN [Text] A meeting of the Jordanian-Egyptian Political Coordination Committee was held at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs today as part of the meetings of the Experts Committee, which is holding its preparatory meetings in Amman. The Jordanian side was led by Nayif al-Hadid, secretary general of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, whereas the Egyptian side was led by Ambassador Badr Hammam, assistant foreign minister for Arab affairs. The two sides stressed the importance of enhancing bilateral relations in all fields and on various Arab, regional, and international issues as well as exchanging views on them. The two sides also reviewed the new Arab and international changes and their impact on the Arab region on various political, economic, and other levels. Talal al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs, received and welcomed the Egyptian delegation. He stressed the deep bilateral ties that contribute to serving the supreme Arab interest.
