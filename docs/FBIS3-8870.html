Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bratislava, March 16 (CTK) -- Radio Free Europe (RFE) resumed its broadcasts in Slovakia today, at 3:00 PM, Director of Slovak Telecommunications Vladimir Ondrovic told journalists. He said that the stoppage of RFE broadcasts decided on Tuesday by Vladimir Zeman, chief director of the Telecommunication Department in the Transport Ministry, was unlawful and contradicted the valid agreement. He said that any interference in the current state could be made merely by the competent parties -- the Slovak telecommunications and RFE. Slovak officials on Tuesday stopped relaying RFE broadcasts from two transmitters, in violation of the still valid agreement providing for medium-wavelength RFE broadcasts.
