BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Oleg Velichko] [Text] Ulyanovsk May 18 TASS -- First Vice-Premier of the Russian Government Oleg Soskovets expressed concern over the safety of flights and the condition of the aviation industry during his visit to the "Avesta" joint stock society on Wednesday. He pointed out that plans made by foreign companies to enter the Russian market are becoming a reality. "Foreign planes are directly purchased and leased from abroad which means that we are beginning to lose the CIS market," Soskovets said. The director general of the Aviastar joint stock society, Viktor Mikhailov, said the auctioning with the involvement of other investors had failed to produce the desire economic effect. According to Mikhailov, part of the planned credit allocated by the state was used for new technologies. "The shortage of completing parts remains one of the worst problems today," Mikhailov said.
