Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 20 Jan (MENA)--Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat arrived in Cairo this morning on a visit to Egypt. During his visit, he will meet President Husni Mubarak to discuss the latest developments in the peace process and the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations. 'Arafat's visit comes within the framework of efforts to overcome the obstacles impeding the implementation of the Palestinian-Israeli Declaration of Principles and the Gaza-Jericho Agreement.
