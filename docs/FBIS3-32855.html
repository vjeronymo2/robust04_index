Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Good Morning From Mexico" newscast] [Text] Commissioner for Peace and Reconciliation in Chiapas Manuel Camacho Solis announced on 13 February that the EZLN [Zapatist National Liberation Army] has elected its delegates to the peace talks. Camacho Solis asked federal and state government agencies to abstain from carrying out any action that could hinder the peace process. He also asked journalists to be patient with regard to the announcement on the place and date for the meeting, which he said will be held in Chiapas State.
