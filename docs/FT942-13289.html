940426 FT 26 APR 94 / Amec wins Pounds 22m bridge contract Amec Process and Energy has won a contract worth nearly Pounds 22m to build a 3,000-tonne bridge module for the Tyra West field in the North Sea, the company announced yesterday. The contract, awarded by Maersk Oil og Gas A/S, is the first Danish order won by Amec's large fabrication yard on the Tyne. Mr Tony Eckford, construction operations chairman of Amec Process and Energy, said the Danish sector of the North Sea was undergoing a 'resurgence' of construction work.
