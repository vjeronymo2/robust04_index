January 24, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

COLLEGE BASKETBALL ROUNDUP

In other men's basketball games:

UC San Diego 77, Occidental 62 -- Chris Anderson had a team-high 18 points and six blocks, but Occidental fell to 5-12.

Cal Lutheran 91, Christ College-Irvine 84 -- Jeff deLaveaga scored 28 points and Mike Demeter added 23 points and 15 rebounds for Cal Lutheran (4-15) at Irvine.

WOMEN

Christ College-Irvine 68, Cal Lutheran 64 -- Leslie Stevens scored 20 points and Joy Fuller added nine rebounds for Cal Lutheran.

Pomona-Pitzer 69, Occidental 60 -- Caryn Cranston scored 27 points to lead visiting Pomona-Pitzer (2-0 in conference) to its second consecutive Southern California Intercollegiate Athletic Conference victory.
