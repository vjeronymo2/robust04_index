BFN [Text] Tokyo, April 27 KYODO -- The Japan Association of Corporate Executives (Keizai Doyukai) on Wednesday [27 April] elected as its Vice Chairman Jiro Ushio, chairman of special lamp maker Ushio Inc., and Yoshihiko Miyauchi, president of leasing firm Orix Corp. The election came at an annual general meeting of the leading Japanese business organization, officials said. Ushio and Miyauchi replaced Joichi Aoi, chairman of Toshiba Corp. and Josei Ito, president of Nippon Life Insurance Co., who both retired as vice chief of the organization. The meeting also approved the reappointment as vice chairman of Tasuku Takagaki, president of the Bank of Tokyo, Tatsuro Toyoda, president of Toyota Motor Corp. and Takashi Imai, president of Nippon Steel Corp.
