Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Sarajevo, March 19 (AFP) -- Three people were wounded in a mortar attack on Sarajevo Saturday, the press service of the Moslem-dominated Bosnian government said, but the UN said the blast was caused by a grenade. If the mortar attack were confirmed, it would be the first serious breach of a NATO-imposed heavy weapons exclusion zone around the capital. But the United Nations Protection Force quickly denied that a mortar shell had been fired, blaming the blast on a grenade. [passage omitted]
