Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] A meeting of the Russian Security Council, chaired by Boris Yeltsin, is today discussing a program for development and production of new armaments and military hardware. Observers note that not all the members of the Security Council are attending the meeting. Those absent are Defense Minister pavel Grachev, Interior Minister Viktor Yerin, as well as Yegor Gaydar and Finance Minister Boris Fedorov. Interfax reports that Fedorov arrived for the meeting, but, having learned he was not invited, left again. He told journalists in an interview that he had not expected such a development.
