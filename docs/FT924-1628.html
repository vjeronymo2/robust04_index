921217 FT 17 DEC 92 / Parliament and Politics: Maastricht bill is scheduled THE GOVERNMENT yesterday signalled its determination to press ahead with ratifying the Maastricht treaty, indicating that the European Communities (Amendment) Bill is to return to the floor of the Commons within three days of MPs' getting back from the Christmas recess. Mr Tony Newton, Commons leader, told MPs that the bill's committee stage would continue on both Wednesday, January 13 and Thursday, January 14.
