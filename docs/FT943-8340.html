940818 FT 18 AUG 94 / World News in Brief: Plan to curb world population Worldwide spending on family planning needs to treble to Dollars 17bn (Pounds 11bn) by the end of the decade if the world's population, now 5.7bn, is to be stabilised at about 7.8bn, the United Nations population fund said.
