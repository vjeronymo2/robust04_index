931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Waterglade Waterglade International, the property developer, reported a pre-tax loss of Pounds 4.09m for the year to March, against a loss last time of Pounds 5.24m before a Pounds 13.26m property provision. Turnover dropped to Pounds 9.56m (Pounds 24.6m) and net rents receivable to Pounds 1.6m (Pounds 1.73m). Losses per share were 16.1p (84.5p). The company has changed its year end to May 31 and a fuller report will accompany those figures shortly. Progress on the fund raising exercise announced in August has been slower than expected, the company said.
