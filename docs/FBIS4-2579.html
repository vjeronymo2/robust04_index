BFN [Text] Luanda, April 9 (XINHUA) -- Angolan Foreign Minister Venancio de Moura said here today that his recent tour to China has strengthened cooperation between the two countries. De Moura made the remarks upon his arrival at the Luanda airport after ending his visit to China and India. He said that during his stay in China he held talks with Chinese leaders over issues of common concerns. He also asked China to help promote the Angolan peace process. De Moura visited China from March 29 through to April 3.
