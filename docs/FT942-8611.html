940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / International Company News: Dutch paper group surges KNP BT, the Dutch paper and packaging group, posted net profit of Fl 50m (Dollars 26.7m) in the first quarter of 1994, reversing a F 113m loss before extraordinary items recorded in the same period of 1993. The figures confirm forecasts made at the recent annual shareholders' meeting. Operating profit in the quarter was up 87 per cent at Fl 129m, reflecting cost-cutting, an improvement in performance by all sectors, and a rise in sales volume. Turnover rose 13.8 per cent to F 13.1bn, against Fl 2.74bn a year earlier. KNP BT reiterated that it expected to see a substantial improvement in net profit for the full year. In 1993, the company recorded a net loss of Fl 343m, reflecting Fl 320m in extraordinary charges related to restructuring.
