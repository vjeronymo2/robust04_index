941029 FT 29 OCT 94 / UK Company News: MG Equity Income Morgan Grenfell Equity Income Trust is raising its final dividend to 2.8p, bringing the total for the 12 months to September 30 to 4.8p - a rise of 6.7 per cent over the previous total of 4.5p. The distribution is payable from undiluted earnings of 4.67p (5.13p) achieved on available revenue of Pounds 1.6m (Pounds 1.25m). Net asset value was unchanged at 139.7p per share.
