Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vilnius, Mar 14, BNS -- A false warning about a bomb placed inside the culture center of the Lithuanian town of Ignalina interrupted a public meeting with Vytautas Landsbergis, leader of the Lithuanian parliament's rightist opposition. The opposition's spokeswoman confirmed that a policemen came into the auditorium of the center at the very beginning of the meeting and announced that an unidentified person threatened with a bomb explosion. The police searched the entire building but found no explosives. No more incidents occurred during the remaining part of the gathering.
