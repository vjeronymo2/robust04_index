940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Wilton In a year of change, Wilton Group, the USM-quoted cinema and television group, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.54m, against Pounds 7.45m. The comparable figure was struck after exceptional charges of Pounds 6.56m relating to discontinued activities against a net charge of Pounds 284,000. Net interest payable fell to Pounds 284,000 (Pounds 1.41m). Turnover for 1993 was Pounds 9.2m (Pounds 30.5m) including Pounds 8.4m (Pounds 29.6m) from discontinued activities. Losses per share came out at 0.16p (1.22p). The discontinued activities mainly relate to the halving of the holding in Glenchewton, the wholesaler, which is now treated as an associate. It now has interests in the creation of a cinema chain in south-east Asia and a Chinese language television channel. It is proposing to change its name to Pacific Media.
