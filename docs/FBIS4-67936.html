BFN [Text] The commander of the Strategic Forces Division, General (Mosad Sagoyev), says that Russia is loyal to its international obligations under the SALT II treaty [as heard]. A group of journalists was allowed to visit the division, that is deployed in the Volga region. They visited the headquarters and got acquainted with combat equipment of the division. General (Sagoyev) reported that the SS-20 medium range missiles had been liquidated in keeping with the treaty. As for the strategic missiles, they are targeted nowhere. They will be assigned a special task, the commander said, only in case of an attack on Russia.
