November 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

As weekly viewers of "Murphy Brown" (we haven't missed an episode), we want to express our thanks to the producers and writers for having Fred Travalena as the guest star on the Oct. 30 show. His portrayal of Don Sarasota, the absolute worst lounge performer, was a classic. The prime-time exposure given Travalena was long overdue.

Charles and Inez Scott, Ventura
