June 3, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

FLAMES DESTROY L.A.'S HISTORIC PAN PACIFIC AUDITORIUM

I was both saddened and appalled as I heard the fire trucks and helicopters trying to save the burning Pan Pacific building. This beautiful Art Deco building has remained empty and in disrepair for a number of years. If this disregard for buildings is how the people of Los Angeles treat their historic landmarks, then maybe the people of Los Angeles do not deserve them.

SUSAN SCHWARTZ

Los Angeles
