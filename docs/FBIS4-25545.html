BFN [Text] Beijing, June 12 KYODO -- Chinese Foreign Minister Qian Qichen told visiting Japanese Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa on Sunday [12 June] Beijing doubts the effectiveness of economic sanctions planned for North Korea over its suspected nuclear arms development, Japanese officials said. Qian made the comment during a meeting with Kakizawa, who arrived from Seoul earlier in the day after talks with South Korean leaders. Kakizawa told Qian China's positive commitment is important in the debate over what response the international community takes through the United Nations Security Council to North Korea's rejection of unimpeded access to its nuclear facilities, the officials said.
