Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Mikhail Shevtsov] [Text] Moscow February 14 TASS--There is nothing strange that Georgian leader Eduard Shevardnadze has decided to take over as the republic's defence minister. He has long been the supreme commander-in-chief, Shevardnadze's aide Teimuraz Stepanov, who is currently in Moscow, told ITAR-TASS on Monday. Stepanov believes that Shevardnadze has made this decision in order not to create new tension in society. Perhaps in conditions of instability this is the only correct decision, the aide added.
