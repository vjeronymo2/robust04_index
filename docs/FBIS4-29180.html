BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 15 Jun Morning Edition] [Text] The DPRK (North Korea) has declared its withdrawal from the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA). Concerning this fact, the Social Democratic Party of Japan [SDPJ] announced on 14 June a statement in the name of Chairman Tomiichi Murayama, which reads: "From our standpoint of turning Northeast Asia into a nuclear-free zone and of achieving peace and prosperity in this region through international cooperation, we would like to express our regret."
