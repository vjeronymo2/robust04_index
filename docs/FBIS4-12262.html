BFN [Text] Here is an updated report on the terrorist attack at the Ashdod Junction: At least four Israelis were wounded in this attack. Two people were seriously wounded, while another two sustained moderate injuries. The wounded were taken to Rehovot's Kaplan Hospital where the hospital director later told us that one of the casualties had died of his wounds. The nationality of the dead man remains unknown. One of the terrorists was killed, but his accomplice or accomplices managed to escape. According to the reports, the terrorists fired from a bus or car at people standing at a hitchhiking stop at the Ashdod Junction. The dead terrorist was killed by bystanders who shot at him from the junction.
