Nevertheless, the puppets are brutally suppressing the people's righteous struggle for existence at the point of the bayonet. This is really a fascist outrage fully revealing the ferocity of the "civilian" dictatorship. Their suppression of people opposing the building of a nuclear waste dump is a brigandish act for forcing them to reconcile themselves to nuclear harm. Unpardonable is the crime of the Kim Yong-sam group which is going to build a nuclear waste dump and inflict irrevocable nuclear disasters upon the people, after converting South Korea into a nuclear base of the United States.
