Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] Economics Minister Ojars Kehris reported on returning to Latvia that during a visit to Baku an official Latvian government delegation established diplomatic relations with Azerbaijan and concluded a trade and economic cooperation agreement. The Latvian delegation met Azerbaijani President Geydar Aliyev and representatives of the government. Due to the unstable political situation and the state of war in Azerbaijan, possibilities for cooperation are limited, but successful development cannot be ruled out in the future. Latvia also plans to conclude similar agreements with Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan.
