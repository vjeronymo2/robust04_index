December 1, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Different museum -- The San Diego Museum of Contemporary Art in La Jolla was incorrectly identified as the San Diego Museum of Art in Friday's Calendar. As part of "A Day Without Art," the San Diego Museum of Contemporary Art is donating today's admissions to AIDS groups and asking visitors to wear a special pair of vision-reducing glasses while touring the galleries. Also, the name of artist Alfredo de Batuc was misspelled.
