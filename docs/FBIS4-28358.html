BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 8 Jun Morning Edition] [Text] A policy study group comprising of 44 dietmen from both the Liberal Democratic Party [LDP] and Social Democratic Party of Japan [SDPJ] was begun on 7 June. Among its leaders are LDP Deputy Secretary-General Kamei and Vice Chariman Yasuo Yamashita of the SDPJ Diet Policy Committee. Five SPDJ dietmen and 30 LDP dietmen attended the group's first session held at the Dietmen's Hall on 7 June. It was decided at the meeting that the group will meet once a week to exchange views on such topics as basic policies on foreign affairs, security, and other issues as well as prospects for the political situation.
