931224 FT 24 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Hartons French disposal Hartons Group, engaged in plastic sheet and film, is to sell its 99.7 per cent stake in VT Plastics, its French offshoot, to Descours & Cabaud, a distributor of industrial products, for an undisclosed sum. For the 10 months to October 31 VT incurred a deficit before exceptional items of FFr10.25m (Pounds 1.21m) on turnover of FFr83.8m.
