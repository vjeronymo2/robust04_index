Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hong Kong, February 17 (XINHUA) -- Hong Kong has decided to begin reciprocal speedpost service with Vietnam as from February 28, local Postmaster General Mike Pagliari announced here today. The speedpost service will cover 16 Vietnamese cities, including the capital city of Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City. The new service will bring the total number of overseas destinations served by speedpost to 62.
