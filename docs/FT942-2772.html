940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Brockhampton Brockhampton Holdings, the parent of Portsmouth Water, lifted pre-tax profits by 22 per cent from Pounds 4.57m to Pounds 5.58m in the year ended March 31. Turnover rose by 8 per cent to Pounds 25.7m. Fully diluted earnings per share climbed from 33.6p to 42p and a recommended final dividend of 7.5p makes a total dividend 40 per cent higher at 10.5p (7.5p).
