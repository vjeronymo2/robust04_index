Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Political violence broke out in Libreville yesterday. Radio-Tele Liberte was destroyed early yesterday morning by the Armed Forces, which stormed the radio station located at Terre-Nouvelle. After Radio-Tele Liberte was destroyed, opposition supporters retaliated by killing (Jean Kamougue-Komo), a member of the Gabonese Democratic Party [PDG]. The government daily L'UNION has reported the death of an unidentified person at Yayi crossroads, who was killed as the security forces were destroying a barricade. Tension has been high in Libreville since 21 February when the Federation of Free Trade Unions called for an indefinite general strike.
