BFN [Report by correspondent A. Kondrashov] [Excerpt] Abdulat Mikitayev, chairman of the Commission on Issues of Citizenship under the Russian president, has arrived in Almaty. He will meet republican leaders and tour regions where Russians are worried about their future citizenship. Speaking at a press conference, Mikitayev announced that the Russian and Kazakhstan Foreign Ministries were drawing up a comprehensive agreement on migration, the protection of rights of citizens of both states, and a simplified procedure for applying for the Russian citizenship in Kazakhstan. It will be based on the memorandum which was signed by Presidents Yeltsin and Nazarbayev. [passage omitted]
