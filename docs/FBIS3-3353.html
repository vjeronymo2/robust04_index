Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 21 (XINHUA) -- Japanese Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa returned home today after a three-day visit to China for talks with Chinese leader on bilateral and international issues. During his stay in China, Hosokawa held separate talks with Chinese President Jiang Zemin and Premier Li Peng on fostering future-oriented and friendly relations between the two countries. The visit, which came at a time when Sino-Japanese ties have entered a new stage, will undoubtedly boost the existing political and economic cooperation between China and Japan.
