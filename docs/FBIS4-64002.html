BFN [By unidentified ITAR-TASS special correspondent] [Text] Brussels, 26 May -- Russia not only wishes to, but will also remain a great power, stated Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev, replying to journalists' questions at a joint news conference with his Belgian counterpart Leo Delcroix, held in Brussels today. The minister stressed furthermore that Russia does not intend to bring any pressure to bear on the countries of Central and Eastern Europe or to dictate its will to them. We favor equal and mutually beneficial cooperation with these states, he added.
