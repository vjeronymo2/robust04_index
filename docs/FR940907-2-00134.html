The utilization of NRSRO ratings, therefore, is an important component of the Commission's regulatory program. Initially, the Commission solicits comment as to whether it should continue to employ in its rules the term ``NRSRO'' and the ratings assigned to various debt and other rated securities by NRSROs. The Commission also invites commentators to consider alternative means by which the Commission could distinguish among various grades of debt and other rated securities. In addition, with the advent of limited scope ratings of the type applied, for example, to ``cash flow securities,'' 15 and with the proliferation of structured securities subject to substantial non-credit payment risks, it is appropriate to review the regulatory use of ratings and to specify, if necessary, what types of ratings fall within each of the regulatory provisions that refer to specific ratings.
