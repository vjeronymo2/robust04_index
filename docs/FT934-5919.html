931127 FT 27 NOV 93 / Staggering Sir Edwin Landseer's Scene In Braemar - Highland Deer is expected to raise Pounds 750,000 when it is sold at Christie's in March. The 8ft high painting was last in the saleroom in 1888 when it sold for Pounds 5,456. It is the Victorian artist's most important work to be auctioned since The Monarch of the Glen was bought in 1916.
