BFN [Text] The 29 Ukrainian Army armored personnel carriers that arrived in Simferopol have been distributed around various parts of the city, a RIA correspondent learned this morning from (Petr Chernyekhevich), the Crimean president's advisor on military affairs. He said that a group from the Ukrainian Internal Affairs Ministry made yet another attempt to enter the Crimean Internal Affairs Ministry premises. Their minister issued an instruction appointing them to various top posts in place of their Crimean counterparts. Guards at the building prevented this, however. Law enforcement staff report from the grassroots that they intend to fulfill only the instructions of Valeriy Kuznetsov, Crimea's internal affairs minister. It also became known today that Major-General Stepanov, deputy commander of the Odessa District, accompanied by 50 staff officers, is arriving in Crimea. They will inspect the Ukrainian 52d Army Corps, which is garrisoned on the peninsula. A delegation of the Ukrainian Supreme Council is expected to arrive in Crimea. Petr Chernyekhevich confirmed that all vital facilities in Simferopol are under reinforced guard. Crimean Cossacks are carrying out guard duties alongside militiamen, on a voluntary basis.
