Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Rauf Denktas, president of the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus, who is currently on a working visit in Turkey, met and conferred with Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin. Cetin and Denktas declared that full agreement was reached during the talks between delegations, which ended last night. Cetin also conveyed get well wishes to Denktas, who underwent ophthalmological examinations at Gulhane Military Hospital this morning. Denktas will call on Prime Minister Tansu Ciller this afternoon. Denktas and his delegation will leave Turkey tomorrow.
