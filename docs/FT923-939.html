920925 FT 25 SEP 92 / Observer: Touching base Sad to see that Uli Schmidt, one of South Africa's few world class rugby players, has pulled out of his team's forthcoming two-month tour of France and England. Pressure of work - he's a surgeon - is partly to blame. But so is the ANC's increasing interference in the game. 'As a player, if you are denied the symbols of your country, the flag, and the anthem, you are denied your identity,' says Schmidt. But isn't that what white South Africans have been doing to their black countrymen for years?
