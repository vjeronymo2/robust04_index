930212 FT 12 FEB 93 / Mirror journalists in strike vote A MAJORITY of journalists who took part in a strike ballot at Mirror Group Newspapers have voted in favour of striking over issues ranging from the sacking of 100 casual staff to individual dismissals of staff journalists. Out of 122 votes cast there was a majority of 60 per cent in favour of striking. In a separate vote 79 per cent backed industrial action short of a strike out of a total of 124 votes. A total of 235 ballot papers were distributed. The NUJ journalists union has given Mirror management a deadline of Tuesday morning to 'start proper negotiations'.
