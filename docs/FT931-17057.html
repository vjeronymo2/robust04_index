930102 FT 02 JAN 93 / Finance and the Family: New fund watching service - At a glance A new service is being provided by Boyton Financial Services. Fund Watch is a fortnigthly publication which will detail charges, discounts and commission on new unit trusts, investment trusts, pensions, Peps and other investments. The service costs Pounds 90 per annum, and is available by post or fax from: Boyton Financial Services, PO Box 14, Halstead, Essex. CO9 4BY. Tel: 0787-61919.
