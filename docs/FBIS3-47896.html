Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 17 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- In connection with a completing here autumn session of the Great State Parliament of Mongolia Deputy Chairman of the Parliament J. Gombojab has held a press conference for Mongolian and foreign newsmen. He said that the Mongolian Parliament has approved 19 new laws and made changes and amendments to the 12 laws and issued 40 resolutions during the autumn session. He admits that the Parliament has not succeeded to discuss all the laws scheduled to be considered at first which was connected with a lack of a conception of legislative initiative on the part of the Great State Hural and the insufficient quality of the draft laws themselves. A number of most significant laws of civil code including those on land and securities are being worked out in order to submit them to the Parliament during its autumn session.
