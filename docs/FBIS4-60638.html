BFN [Presidential Decree No. 26 issued in Sanaa on 26 May] [Text] The Presidential Council chairman, After taking cognizance of the Republic of Yemen Constitution, at the recommendation of the acting prime minister, in accordance with the requirements of the public interest, and following the approval of the Presidential Council, decides:

Article One: The following brothers shall be appointed to
the Council of Ministers in the posts appearing opposite their names. 1. 'Abd-al-Qadir 'Abd-al-Rahman Ba Jammal, deputy prime minister 2. Faysal 'Uthman Bin-Shamlan, minister of oil and mineral resources 3. Ahmad Musa'id Husayn
Article Two: This decree shall go into effect as of the
date of its publication. It shall be published in the official gazette. Issued at the Presidential Office in Sanaa on 17 Dhi al-Hijjah 1414 Hegira, corresponding to 26 May 1994 [Signed] Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman; Dr. Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar, the acting prime minister
