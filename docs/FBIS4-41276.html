BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan June 22 TASS--The Armenian parliament ratified today a treaty on the legal status of Russian troops deployed on the territory of the republic. However lawmakers imposed several conditions. The number and features of the conditions are not clear so far. 135 MPs voted for the decision, and only four members of the Armenian Communist Party voted against it. The latter insisted that the treaty should be ratified without any further conditions.
