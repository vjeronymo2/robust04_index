March 24, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

THE COLLEGES;

PONY SOFTBALL TOURNAMENT;

FULLERTON WINS PAIR OF SHUTOUTS

Ann Van Dortrecht pitched a two-hitter, striking out eight, to give Cal State Fullerton a 5-0 victory over Utah in the Titans' second game Thursday in the Pony softball tournament at Fullerton.

Buffy Sanchez got two hits for the Titans (22-5) in that game.

Earlier Thursday, Shireen Campbell pitched a one-hitter as Fullerton defeated Akron, 1-0.

The Titans scored in the third inning. Sanchez led off with a single and Missy Coombes tripled to right.

In other tournament games:

Texas A&M 7, Akron 6 (10 innings) -- Michelle Mayfield's sacrifice scored Erika Erickson with the winning run in the bottom of the 10th for Texas A&M.

Utah 8, Texas A&M 0 -- Utah scored five runs in the first inning, two on a triple by Karen Donaldson.

Fresno State 8, Bowling Green 0 -- Former Edison High School star Terry Carpenter pitched a two-hitter, striking out seven, for the Bulldogs. It was the fifth consecutive shutout for Carpenter (12-1), who has thrown 36 consecutive innings without allowing a run.

Fresno State 4, Oregon 1 -- Former Sunny Hills High School player Gina LoPiccolo hit a solo home run and Carie Dever pitched a one-hitter for the Bulldogs.

California 4, Cal Poly Pomona 1 -- Erin Cassidy (8-5) pitched a three-hitter for the victory.

California 6, Northwestern 0 -- Robyn Burgess pitched a three-hitter.

Northwestern 7, New Mexico State 6 -- The Wildcats scored four runs in the bottom of the seventh for the victory.

New Mexico State 1, Cal Poly Pomona 0 -- Cindy Michik hit a home run in the top of the seventh to give New Mexico State the victory over seventh-ranked Pomona.

Oklahoma State 6, Cal State Long Beach 0 -- Oklahoma State scored six runs in the fifth, two on a bases-loaded double by Shari Johnson. Michele Smith (6-1) pitched a three-hitter for the Cowboys.
