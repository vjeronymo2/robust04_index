May 11, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Police recovered a car owned by Clipper center Benoit Benjamin four hours after it was stolen at gunpoint.

Three men were arrested at about 11:45 p.m. for investigation of allegedly stealing the car.

Police said Benjamin was visiting a friend in the Athens area of Los Angeles at about 7:45 p.m. when a man approached him and demanded the keys to his Mercedes-Benz, which is valued at $75,000.
