Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Badr Hammam, envoy of President Muhammad Husni Mubarak and assistant to the Egyptian foreign minister, arrived in Sanaa today on a working visit to our country. He will meet with brothers in the political leadership, as well as senior state officials. In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', the Egyptian official expressed his happiness to visit his second homeland, Yemen, pointing out that the visit is part of the strong brotherly relations between the two fraternal Yemeni and Egyptian peoples. He also stressed the eagerness of the Egyptian leadership and people to support their Yemeni brothers at all times and under all circumstances and to share with them what is happening on their land as brothers, who wish to see Yemen stable and prosperous. He then voiced his strong confidence that the political leadership, as well as all forces within and outside the coalition, are more than eager to achieve security and stability and to preserve Yemen's unity. He stressed that Yemen would move ahead on the path of unity, democracy, and construction, wishing the Yemeni people more development and prosperity under the young Yemeni republic. He was received at the airport by Ahmad Dayfallah al-'Uzayb, Foreign Ministry under secretary for political affairs; several Foreign Ministry officials; a delegate from the Presidential Protocol; and Egyptian Charge d'affaires in Sanaa 'Adil Subhi.
