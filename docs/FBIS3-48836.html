Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN [Report on results of the people's attack which destroyed Samraong, the seat of Oddar Meanchey Province] [Excerpts] [passage omitted] Over 2,000 people were divided into groups to attack the Vietnamese communist puppet administrators and ringleaders from the western part of Samraong District along Route 69. They attacked and destroyed the police post and the site of an army engineering unit, which was in charge of preparing the way for suppressive operations. Another group of the people's forces attacked an artillery position at Don Ken village south of Samraong District hall. Another group attacked the central area, totally destroying the Oddar Meanchey provincial hall, the provincial seat, and Samraong District hall. Another group attacked the camps of the Vietnamese communist puppets at O Russei, destroying two positions belonging to the 286th Division and Nhoek Bunchhai's forces. Another group attacked positions in the suburban area, totally destroying the positions at Bos Sbouv and Trabek. All groups in this people's attack linked up at Samraong market and occupied this area for over 3 hours. The people's forces destroyed the puppets' heavy military machines which were being used for building roads for their military trucks, as well as armored personnel carriers and tanks used for launching operations in the eastern and western parts of Route 68. They destroyed three [words indistinct], five [words indistinct] tractors, four ten-wheeled and thirteen-wheeled trucks, an oil truck, a water truck, two ammunition depots, two 122-mm and B-30 cannon, three 82-mm mortars, four 12.7 guns, and two DK-75 guns. They also killed 36 of the enemy on the spot and wounded over 60 others. [passage omitted].
