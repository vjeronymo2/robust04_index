BFN [Text] Riyadh, May 11, SPA -- Custodian of the two holy mosques King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz received a telephone call from Yemeni President Lt. General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih on Tuesday [10 May]. They reviewed bilateral relations and the current developments in Yemen, security in the Gulf region and the mutual interests of the kingdom and the Republic of Yemen. On Wednesday, visiting Yemeni Chairman of the House of Deputies Sheikh 'Abdullah bin Husayn al-Ahmar and Planning and Development Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani conveyed a message to King Fahd from Lt. General Salih during their audience with the monarch at al- Yamama Palace here. The audience was attended by Second Deputy Premier, Minister of Defence and Aviation and Inspector General Prince Sultan Bin-'Abd- al-'Aziz, the special adviser to the monarch Ibrahim al-Anqari and the Yemeni ambassador to the kingdom Ghalib 'Ali Jameel [name as received]. Sheikh al-Ahmar and Dr. al-Iryani arrived here last night and were greeted at the airport by al-Anqari and Jameel.
