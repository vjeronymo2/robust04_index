920618 FT 18 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Asset growth for Finsbury Trust Net asset value per share of Finsbury Trust hardly moved over the 12 months ended March 31 1992, but has since risen by 14.6 per cent. At March 31 it stood at 111.1p, compared with 110.8p a year earlier after expanding to 119p at September 30 1991. By May 29 1992, however, it moved up to 127.3p. The trust produced earnings per share of 3.2p for the year ended March 31, compared with 2.9p, and is raising the dividend to 3p (2.85p) through a final of 1.8p (1.65p). Total income was Pounds 1.5m (Pounds 1.57m) and pre-tax revenue Pounds 1.01m (Pounds 1.07m).
