941223 FT 23 DEC 94 / International Capital Markets: IFC plans Dollars 5m investment in Balkan fund The International Finance Corporation has announced a Dollars 5m investment in the Euro-Merchant Balkan Fund, a venture capital fund capitalised at Dollars 27.3m, writes Richard Lapper. The fund will invest in small and medium-sized companies in Bulgaria and possibly other countries in the region. It is sponsored by Euro-Merchant Bank, a privately-owned Greek bank, and managed by Global Finance, an Athens-based venture capital group partly-owned by Baring Venture Partners. The fund will be incorporated in Luxembourg as a closed-end investment company with a life of 10 years. Private investors are providing 51 per cent of the fund's capital, with the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development investing Dollars 8.3m.
