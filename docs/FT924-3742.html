921205 FT 05 DEC 92 / Finance and the Family: Cheer for smaller companies The Hoare Govett smaller companies index (capital gains version) was up 1.15 per cent at 1122.54, and the County NatWest smaller companies index up by 0.97 per cent at 866.01 on Thursday. But John Houlihan, of Hoare Govett, still cautions against over-confidence. He says: 'Each recession grows its crop of corporate zombies and the current one is set to be more fruitful in that regard than most.'
