940729 FT 29 JUL 94 / International Company News: Bid speculation hoists Williams Shares in Williams Companies, the diversified US energy group, rose yesterday amid speculation that LDDS Communications had increased its offer for WilTel, Williams' telecommunications unit, to Dollars 2.5bn from Dollars 2bn. WilTel, with revenues last year of just under Dollars 1bn, operates the fourth largest US adavnced national fibre optic long-distance telecommunications network. Williams rejected the original, unsolicited, offer from LDDS, the Mississippi-based telecommunications group, in May. Williams shares were up Dollars 1 1/2 at Dollars 32 3/4 in early trade.
