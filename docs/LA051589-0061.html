May 15, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

PASSINGS;

C.S. HARDING MOTT; HELPED START CHARITY FOUNDATION

C.S. Harding Mott, 82, who with his father developed and sustained what is now the nation's 12th-largest charitable foundation. Harding Mott, chairman emeritus of the Charles Stewart Mott Foundation in Flint, Mich., played a low-key but crucial role with the foundation begun in 1926 by his father, who at one time was the largest individual shareholder of General Motors Corp. Charles Mott supported the foundation decades after its formation. But it burgeoned when Harding Mott, an original foundation trustee, persuaded his father in 1963 to donate 1.8 million GM shares, worth $129 million at the time. When Charles Mott died in 1973 at age 97, the foundation received all but $1.3 million of his $43.3-million estate. In Fort Lauderdale, Fla., on Wednesday.
