920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Allied-Lyons sells Spanish brandy side ALLIED-LYONS' wine and spirits arm, Hiram Walker, is selling its brandy and distribution interests in Spain to Pedro Domecq for Pounds 50m in cash and additional Domecq shares. Hiram will then own 5.66 per cent of Domecq and 50 per cent of the 53 per cent of Domecq held by Hiram Walker Europe, a Spanish joint venture with the Mora-Figueroa family. Domecq, a private company, makes about Pta13bn (Pounds 71m) annually on turnover of Pta145bn.
