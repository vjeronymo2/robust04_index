April 14, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

LET REBELS ENJOY THEIR TITLE . . .

Believe it or not, Michael Hannin (Viewpoint, April 7), UNLV won the NCAA championship.

Your frustrations are evident. As a former player of Coach Tarkanian, I was honored, along with his present players, for their accomplishments. Duke Coach Krzyzewski gave credit where credit was due, an example to others.

PETE M. LANGO SR., Bakersfield
