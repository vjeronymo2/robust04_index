931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Allied London property buy Allied London Properties has acquired for Pounds 34.4m a portfolio of six investment properties from Provident Mutual in exchange for properties valued at Pounds 21.6m, plus Pounds 12.8m cash. Rental income from the properties, which comprise a mixture of office, industrial and retail, is Pounds 3.8m a year. Annual rental income from properties given in exchange is Pounds 2m, so rental income, directors pointed out, will increase by Pounds 1.8m a year.
