Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] KATHMANDU, March 4 (AFP) -- Prime Minister Girija Prasad Koirala regained consciousness half an hour after being taken to hospital Friday [25 February] after he collapsed while delivering a speech in the house of representatives, a hospital source said. "He is being thoroughly checked in order to determine his physical condition," the source said. Earlier a medical source said Koirala had suffered a heart attack. The prime minister was addressing parliament during a special session to debate a no-confidence motion introduced by the opposition. A parliamnetary source said the special session had been postponed until March 7.
