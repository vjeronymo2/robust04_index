Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Jan. 18 (YONHAP) -- Opposition Democratic Party [DP] Chairman Yi Ki-taek has decided to shelve temporarily his plan to visit Beijing late this month due to difficulty in arranging meetings with Chinese leaders, a DP official said Tuesday. Yi sent an aide to Beijing on Jan. 7 to arrange meetings with President Jiang Zemin and other Chinese leaders, but the aide failed to get a schedule fixed, according to the official. "I've heard that Yi's aide returned home on Monday and explained the results of his trip to Beijing to Yi," the official said. "The aide pushed for meetings between five Chinese leaders and Yi, but he could not fix a schedule."
