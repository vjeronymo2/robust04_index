Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] At about 1500 today, Serbian police intervened in the Kosova Information Center [QIK] offices in Prishtina. The offices are situated in the Qafa complex of business buildings in Prishtina. Today, they were held under the continuous supervision of the Serbian police. Serbian civilians, who first entered the QIK offices, played the role of uninformed citizens. Then, at about 1500, two plainclothes policemen entered the offices and took personal notes from three QIK reporters. At 1510, four policemen entered the offices. Two of the policemen were holding automatic weapons. They ordered the reporters to stop work. Meanwhile, the Serbian policemen confiscated personal notes from seven QIK reporters. The QIK reports that the Serbian police showed interest in the work performed at the office and left, taking the notes with them.
