Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 17 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The Mongolian Stock Exchange has carried out for the 120th time the sale of securities. During the sale there were sold 335,543 securities of eight joint stock companies. The total turnover of the sale amounts for 27.5 million tugrik which is by 13 per cent more in reference to their nominal outcome than during the previous sale. The maximum rate of securities comes to 171255 tugrik this time while the lowest one accounts for 1825 tugrik each. Next sale of securities at the stock exchange is to be held on 24 February.
