BFN [Text] Sofia, May 14 (A.A) -- A bomb that exploded in front of the Turkish Consulate in the Bulgarian city of Plovdiv caused material damage but no injuries, it was reported on Saturday. AA reporter said that the bomb which was under a car 30-meters from the consulate building exploded causing heavy material damage to the environs, but none to the consulate building. Regional Security Chief Lieutenant Ivan Georgiev said the incident had nothing to do with the Turkish Consulate, adding it was determined that the car owner had been repeatedly threatened by the Mafia.
