Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Jose Raul Amaro, the president of the People's Supreme Tribunal, released details of ruling No. 104 of 1994 for Guantanamo Province, which stipulates strict sanctions against anyone attempting to leave the country illegally through the border with the Yankee base in Caimaneras. According to reporter Leonel Perez Moya, who is in Guantanamo, the president of the People's Supreme Tribunal stated that the new legislation establishes policies for the imposition of more severe sanctions, although such severity will be be effected within the framework of the law without neglecting procedural guarantees. Amaro Salup stated that they are not trying to repress for the sake of repression but to dissuade and protect people who commit this serious crime, as well as to ensure the safety of the border guards and the integrity of national territory.
