BFN ["News Release"] [Text] Tallinn, 31 March -- Estonian Defence Forces press service informed ETA, on Thursday [31 March], that the head of the Estonian Defence Forces Headquarters Colonel Ants Laaneots has handed in his resignation and his authorities will end on 18 April. Colonel Laaneots did not wish to comment upon the reasons of his resignation. Colonel Laaneots will be followed by Colonel Arvo Sirel, who up to now was the deputy head of the Headquarters and head of the 1st, operative department of the Defence Forces Headquarters. Colonel Sirel (49) was born in Tallinn. He has served in the former Soviet missile troops and graduated from the Moscow Missile Troops Academy. Since 1993 he has been in the service of Estonian Defence Forces Headquarters.
