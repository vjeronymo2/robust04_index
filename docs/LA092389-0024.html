September 23, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

PEOPLE'S RIGHTS AND TARZANA-ENCINO MEETING

Madeline DeAntonio (Letters, Sept. 21) inaccurately rebuts Gary A. Waldman's account of the Encino-Tarzana meeting on Aug. 17 with Councilman Marvin Braude.

Thank you, Los Angeles Times, for extending to DeAntonio the constitutional rights which she so brazenly denied the citizens of Tarzana as president of the Encino Hillside Traffic Safety Organization.

JEANNETTE VOGEL

Los Angeles
