BFN [Text] Industry Minister Liliana Canale has informed this radio station from Beijing on the possibility of joint investment projects in the fishing, agroindustrial, and manufacturing products with China. She said Peru is the main Latin American beneficiary of Chinese loans: [Begin Canale recording] We talked about possible joint investments which I believe could materialize if the talks continue in the sectors that are of greatest interest for the two countries. These include fishing, agroindustrial, and light manufacturing. As you know China already considers Peru one of the main beneficiaries of investments in Latin America. [end recording]
