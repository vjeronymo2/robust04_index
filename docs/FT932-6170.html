930529 FT 29 MAY 93 / UK Company News: TDS Circuits cuts losses to Pounds 0.78m TDS Circuits, the USM-quoted maker of printed circuit boards, reduced its pre-tax losses for the year to February 28 from Pounds 921,000 to Pounds 779,000, after an exceptional Pounds 85,000 for further reorganisation costs. Turnover improved slightly to Pounds 6.25m (Pounds 6.02m) 'against a background of relentless price pressure in the PCB market.' Export sales now represented more than half of this as a result of the continuing strategy to internationalise. Losses per share fell to 11.99p (13.57p).
