BFN [Viktor Yadukha report: "Conference of Centrists To Be Held in Crimea"] [Text] Preparations are under way in Sevastopol for a conference of centrist political forces of Russia, Ukraine, and Belorussia [Belarus] with Kazakhstan and Moldavia [Moldova] showing interest in it. According to Yuriy Komov, one of the conference organizers and spokesman for the Crimean Party of Social Guarantees, the conference is supposed to raise the question of a new union within the CIS framework. Taking part in the preparations on behalf on Ukraine is the Interregional Kuchma-Grinev Bloc of Reforms, and the Civil Congress of Ukraine. Representing Russia are the Party of Russian Unity and Accord and the Russian Movement for Democratic Reforms. Also invited were Social Democrats and Populists. "The threat of a conflict in Crimea may bring to naught centrists' unification efforts," Yuriy Komov stated. "Our plan is to get all the documents signed at the Livadia Palace on the 50th anniversary of the Yalta Conference."
