Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 23 Jan (MENA)--Palestinian president Yasir 'Arafat held a meeting today with Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa at al-Andalus Palace where 'Arafat is staying. The two discussed the peace process in the Middle East, particularly the Gaza-Jericho talks. 'Arafat then left Cairo for Saudi Arabia.
