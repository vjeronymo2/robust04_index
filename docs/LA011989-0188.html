January 19, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

BOYS' ROUNDUP;

BREA-OLINDA STAVES OFF WESTERN RALLY, WINS AT THE BUZZER

Aaron Charlton scored at the buzzer to give Brea-Olinda High School a 68-66 victory over Western Wednesday in an Orange League basketball game at Brea.

The Wildcats (13-6, 2-1) led, 66-64, but Western's Ed Serranno tied the score with 7 seconds left on a layup. After a time out, Charlton took the inbounds pass and drove through the middle for a layup to win the game.

The Pioneers (12-4, 1-2) trailed, 50-35, at the start of the third quarter but rallied behind the shooting of Brian Baber, who scored 12 of his 14 points in the final quarter.

Brea led, 27-24, at halftime and outscored Western, 23-11 in the third quarter. Matt Chamberlain scored 12 of his 17 points in the third quarter.

Keith Walker scored 30 points for the Wildcats.

Joe Romero each scored 14 points for Western.

In other Orange League games:

In the Empire League:

Cypress 69, Katella 56 -- Mark Johnson and Mike Flowers scored 20 points each to lead host Cypress (9-9, 4-0). Brian Yourstone and Tim Hall led Katella (6-10, 2-3) with 11 points each.

El Dorado 73, Loara 53 -- Matt Luke scored 8 points in the first quarter as El Dorado led, 20-12. Luke finished with 18 points and 15 rebounds. Mike Renaud and Mike Bradach added 15 each for El Dorado (14-4, 4-0). Tes Whitlock led Loara (2-13, 0-4) with 25.

Los Alamitos 62, Esperanza 59 (OT) -- Los Alamitos (7-12, 2-2) trailed by 14 points with 5 minutes to play but battled back. Ted LaBouff, a 5-11 guard who came up from the junior varsity for the game, hit a 3-point shot with 5 seconds remaining to tie the score at 59. His 3 free throws accounted for the only points in overtime. LaBouff finished with 11 points and teammate Mike Wilson scored 25. Greg Pope led Esperanza (6-12, 0-4) with 15 points.
