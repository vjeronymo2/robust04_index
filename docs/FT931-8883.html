930217 FT 17 FEB 93 / UK Company News: County Smaller net assets at 84.44p County Smaller Companies Investment Trust reported a net asset value of 84.44p per share at December 31; a year earlier the value stood at 94.4p. Net revenue for the six months to end-December amounted to Pounds 173,815 (Pounds 236,086 for period from incorporation on April 10 to December 31 1991) with earnings at 1.06p (1.44p) per share. The interim dividend is unchanged at 1.125p.
