BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 7 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today called on His Majesty [H.M.] King Norodom Sihanouk of Cambodia at the guest house. He was accompanied by Vice-Premier and Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam, Vice-Minister of Foreign Affairs Cho Kyu-il, DPRK Ambassador to Cambodia Kim Hyong-yol and officials concerned. President Kim Il-song was greeted at the guest house by H.M. King Norodom Sihanouk and deputy prime minister, minister of the royal palace and Minister of Agriculture Kong Sam-ol, Cambodian Ambassador to Korea Oum Mannorine, Minister of State and Secretary General of the king's cabinet Chhorn Hay and others. President Kim Il-song had a cordial and friendly talk with H.M. King Norodom Sihanouk. H.M. King Norodom Sihanouk showed President Kim Il-song the feature film "Distressed Peasants" of his own making and arranged a luncheon at the guest house. H.M. King Norodom Sihanouk expressed deep thanks to President Kim Il-song for the hospitality during his visit. President Kim Il-song exchanged greetings with H.M. King Norodom Sihanouk at parting. President Kim Il-song presented a gift to H.M. King Norodom Sihanouk.
