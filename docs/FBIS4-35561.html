BFN [Text] Cairo, May 30, INA -- Foreign minister, chairman of the Iraqi delegation to the Non-Aligned conference, Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf today conferred in Cairo with his Venezuelan counterpart Burelli Rivas. During the meeting, Mr. al-Sahhaf briefed the Venezuelan official on developments of relation between Iraq and the Security Council and the U.N. special commission and Iraq's implementation of its reciprocal commitments pursuant to the Security Council Resolutions. He also explained Iraq's assessment of the positive discussions by the Security Council members during the recent session of the Council held in New York, confirming that Iraq demands the next routine review must be decisive as for lifting the blockade imposed on the country since August 1990. INA Cairo correspondent said that the Iraqi and Venezuelan ministers had also discussed bilateral ties and means of resuming them. They also exchanged view points on a package of regional and global issues of common concern. Present at the meeting were Iraq's Foreign Undersecretary Wisam al-Zahawi and Iraq's Arab League Permanent Delegate Dr Nabil Najim.
