BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin and Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres have instructed their bureau officials and aides to cease all working contacts with Dr. Ahmad al-Tibi, who is described as PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat's adviser. The instructions came in the wake of a legal opinion regarding al-Tibi's status as an Israeli citizen. This was reported by our political correspondent Shlomo Raz.
