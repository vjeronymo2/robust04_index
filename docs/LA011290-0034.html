January 12, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

SOME THOUGHTS ON UNTRASHING L.A.

I enjoyed the article regarding the highly respected waste organizer, Joan Edwards, hired by L.A. City to reduce the voluminous mountains of trash currently dumped into the landfills.

I hope she is able to bring about discipline to our disposable society.

I also hope she can bring about some change in the bottled drink industry. It bothers me that only the beer and soda companies have a deposit/return system to encourage recycling, while the juice industry, with those heavy, valuable glass bottles, offers no incentive at all.

I support Joan Edwards' ideals and trust that her office will remain free of special interests.

RICHARD WRIGHT, Rowland Heights
