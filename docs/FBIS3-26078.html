Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 2 (XINHUA) -- An agreement on a government commodity loan to be provided by China to Georgia was signed here today. Shi Guangsheng, Chinese vice-minister of foreign trade and economic co-operation, and visiting Georgian Vice-Minister of Finance Mirian Murjigleli [name as received] signed the agreement. Murjigleli and his party arrived here on January 29.
