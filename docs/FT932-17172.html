930402 FT 02 APR 93 / World News in Brief: Dollar falls against DM The dollar fell sharply against the D-Mark after the release of weaker than expected US indicators. It bottomed out in London at DM1.5958, recovering to close at DM1.5995, more than 1 1/4 pfennigs down. It closed in New York at DM1.592. Sterling gained 2 cents to Dollars 1.5255 in London, and rose to Dollars 1.53 in New York. Pressure on the dollar continued this morning, with the US currency hitting a record low of Y113.65 shortly after the Tokyo opening.
