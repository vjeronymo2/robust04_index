Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Bayt al-Maqdis Movement [The Islamic Jihad Movement-Bayt al-Maqdis] has issued a statement, which said: The injustice, killings, and displacements that have been taking place in Palestine for decades and that climaxed in the killing and wounding of hundreds of worshipers at the Ibrahimi Mosque clearly shows that Jews are Jews and that their hate for Muslims is intense and their resentment of God and his messenger is ever deepening.
