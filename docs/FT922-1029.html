920624 FT 24 JUN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton and Jute COTTON Liverpool-Spot and shipment sales for the week ending 19 June 1992 amounted to 100 tonnes against 126 tonnes in the previous week. Lack of enterprise persisted and dealings were exceedingly rare. JUTE June/July C and F Dundee: BTC USDollars 395, BTD USDollars 365. C and F Antwerp: BTC USDollars 380, BWC USDollars 380, BTD USDollars 355, BWD USDollars 355.
