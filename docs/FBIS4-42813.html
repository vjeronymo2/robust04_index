BFN [Text] This morning, Silvio Berlusconi paid a visit to the Bambino Gesu hospital where 11 Rwandan children have been admitted. When asked about the possible solutions to the Rwandan tragedy, Berlusconi said: [Begin Berlusconi recording] I believe that if we have a true conscience as world citizens, then I believe we must find a way to intervene. We must find a way to intervene adequately, quickly, in an effective way; I believe that the world, those countries that are democratic and civilized, must find a way of setting up an army, a permanent task force for exactly this type of intervention. This task force could be made up perhaps of ordinary troops or volunteers. [end recording]
