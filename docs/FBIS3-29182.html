Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] Lieutenant General Khin Nyunt, secretary-1 of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] of the Union of Myanmar [Burma], received visiting United States Congressman Bill Richardson at the Ministry of Defense's Dagon House at 0830. Also present at the occasion were U Ohn Gyaw, minister of foreign affairs, and U Nyunt Swe, deputy minister of foreign affairs. The U.S. congressman was accompanied by Mr. Franklin P. Huddle Jr., charge d'affaires of the U.S. Embassy, and Mr. Andrew Simon, political and economic affairs attache.
