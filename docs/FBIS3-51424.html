Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Bulletin No. 21 issued by the Mexican National Defense Secretariat on the situation in Chiapas on 18 January; place not given; from the "Vector 21" newscast] [Text] In the Chiapas highlands, the Mexican Army continues to control San Cristobal de las Casas, Rancho Nuevo, Ocosingo, Altamirano, Las Margaritas, Comitan, and Oxchuc. The Army simultaneously does social work for these communities. Residents of Las Islas, San Juan Tenampa, Santa Rita, common lands Eden and La Revancha, and delegates of the cattlemen's unions in Altamirano, Las Margaritas, Ocosingo, Comitan, and San Cristobal, have been reporting looting and cattle rustling by armed groups.
