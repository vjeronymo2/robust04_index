920710 FT 10 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Kode International Kode International has paid pounds 3.2m to acquire DCM Services, and will fund this by raising pounds 3.66m via a 13-for-20 underwritten rights offer at 100p per share. The principal activity of DCM is maintenance for the commercial personal computer market. Mr Stephen Day, chief executive of Kode, said that trading had improved on the previous year. The directors intended to raise the interim dividend to 1.5p (1p).
