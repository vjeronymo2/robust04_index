940110 FT 10 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Whitbread opens more Pizza Huts Whitbread has agreed plans with Compass, the catering group, to open about 50 new Pizza Hut outlets during the next 18 months in UK airports, railway stations, workplaces and other off-high street venues, writes Philip Rawstorne. Whitbread opened 40 Pizza Hut outlets last year to bring the total to nearly 350. The company also intends to continue the expansion of its other retail operations.
