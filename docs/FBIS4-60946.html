BFN [Text] Moscow April 30 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin signed a decree on Friday to streamline the use of foreign labour in Russia and ensure the priority right of Russian citizens to fill vacancies. The document expands the December 16, 1993 decree on the use of foreign labour in Russia to cover foreign legal entities operating in Russia under contracts. The new regulations will not apply to those organisations which send employees to Russia to install their equipment.
