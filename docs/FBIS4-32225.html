BFN [Report by Ozren Jorganovic, Zoran Gurgurevic, and Predrag Curkovic from the Ozren, Teslic, and Vlasic battlefields] [Excerpt] [Jorganovic] Muslims have been launching fierce attacks with strong artillery support, targeting Serb positions in Lokve on the southern part of the Vozuca front, and Jugovac and Glavica on the eastern part of the same front. However, Vozuca's legendary defenders have successfully stopped the attacks from the direction of Banovici, forcing the Islamic forces to withdraw. [Grgurevic] Muslims are also constantly attacking along the Teslic front and shelling the town center of Teslic and residential areas in the suburbs. Major material damage has been caused by the shelling. Muslim forces have been regrouping from the direction of Gracanica and Tuzla on the Trebava part of the front, east of Doboj. Military experts expect a fresh attack from this direction. [passage omitted]
