January 27, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

AME Inc., a Burbank videotape services concern that has tentatively accepted a $37-million takeover bid from its founder and chairman, Andrew M. McIntyre, said a third party has expressed interest in making a rival offer. AME did not identify the third party and said no formal proposal has been made. McIntyre is offering $13.75 a share for the 55% of AME he does not own.
