August 30, 1990, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: ESCONDIDO

The body of a 50-year-old man was found Wednesday in a reservoir in Escondido, a Sheriff's Department spokesman said.

His name is being withheld until the next of kin can be notified.

The body was found about 10:25 a.m. in the reservoir east of the 2400 block of North Broadway. The body showed no signs of violence, Lt. Scott McClintock said.
