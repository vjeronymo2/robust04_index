BFN [Text] Bonn, (DDP/ADN) -- Chancellor Helmut Kohl has stressed that Great Britain and Germany have a "great responsibility" for the development of a Europe that is oriented toward a market economy and is socially fair. This is particularly true at the present time, which is "characterized by far-reaching political changes throughout Europe," Kohl underlined in a welcoming address, published in Bonn today, to the participants of the 44th Koenigswinter conference in Cambridge. He is sure that this German-English political discussion will provide fresh and "forward-looking stimuli" in both countries.
