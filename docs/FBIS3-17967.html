Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by BELINFORM correspondent for TASS] [Text] Minsk, 30 March (TASS) -- The Belarusian Popular Front officially nominated on Wednesday its candidate for the upcoming presidential elections. The presidential nominee is Zenon Poznyak, the front's leader and a people's deputy, coordinator of the parliamentary opposition Sergey Naumchik told a briefing in parliament. He noted that as soon as all the necessary formalities are completed, the Belarusian Popular Front will get down to collection of signatures in support of Poznyak.
