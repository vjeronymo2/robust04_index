BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow June 16 [TASS] -- A grenade was thrown at the window of the "Obyedinyonny bank" (United Bank) of Moscow at four o'clock this afternoon [1200 GMT]. Preliminary reports state that some people have been injured by the resulting blast in the bank on Arbat street. In particular, one woman was cut by slivers of glass. The Obyedinyonny Bank was registed by the Russian Federation Central Bank on January 2, 1992, and has a license to perform all banking operations on Russian territory and abroad. The bank's 1993 turnover in roubles and foreign currency was reported to be 366.6 billion roubles (about 334 million U.S. dollars). The bank has correspondent accounts in Finland, Germany, Great Britain and Switzerland.
