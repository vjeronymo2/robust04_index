Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tabriz, E. Azarbaijan Prov., Jan. 12, IRNA -- Foreign Ministry Director General for CIS and Caucasia Affairs, Morteza Bank, left here for Yerevan Tuesday. He will visit Baku after Yerevan. Bank is to submit a message from Iran's Foreign Minister, 'Ali Akbar Velayati, to his Armenian and Azeri counterparts. He is making the visits following the one by Russian special envoy on Karabakh crisis, Vladimir Kazymirov, to Iran last week. Both Kazymirov and the Iranian Foreign Ministry officials stressed prevention of the Karabakh crisis from growing into an international issue and underlined the continuation of joint cooperation in settling the dispute.
