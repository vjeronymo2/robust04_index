921005 FT 05 OCT 92 / International Company News: Ash & Lacy margins under pressure Pressure on margins pushed pre-tax profit of Ash & Lacy, the West Midlands-based galvanising and metal pressings group, down 14 per cent in the six months ended July from Pounds 2.31m to Pounds 1.97m on increased turnover of Pounds 28.72m (Pounds 27.83m). Mr David Fletcher, chairman, said that despite the continuing effect of the recession, cash flow remained good and the balance sheet healthy. With strong presence in each of its major markets, the company remains well positioned. Earnings fell to 5.09p (6.04p). The interim dividend was maintained at 2.5p.
