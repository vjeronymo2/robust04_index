930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Revised Resort terms value County at Pounds 3.5m Resort Hotels is bringing forward its purchase of County Resort Hotels for a revised consideration of 8.5m shares, worth Pounds 3.49m at yesterday's price of 41p. County owns seven hotels in the south of England with a total of 329 bedrooms. Resort has operated those under a management contract, making them an integral part of its network. Mr Robert Feld, managing director of Resort, said benefits of the acquisition to group earnings would not be immediate.
