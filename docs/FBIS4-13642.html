BFN [Text] Cairo, 21 Apr (MENA)--The Israeli delegation participating in the Israeli-Palestinian peace talks led by Deputy Chief of Staff Amnon Shahaq left Cairo this afternoon after a visit of several days and the end of a round of talks with the Palestinian delegation. In a statement made before his departure, Shahaq said that the talks will resume in Cairo on Sunday to follow up on the previous rounds. He said that progress was made on the civilian and legal issues, noting that the talks were held in a positive atmosphere.
