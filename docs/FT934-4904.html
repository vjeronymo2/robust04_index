931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / IIC secures Japanese loan The InterAmerican Investment Corporation, the private sector lending and equity arm of the InterAmerican Development Bank, signed yesterday a Dollars 75m (Pounds 50.3m) loan agreement with Fuji Bank of Japan, writes Stephen Fidler, Latin America Editor. Mr Wolfgang Schaefer, the IIC's vice-president for finance and administration, said the loan underlined confidence in the corporation.
