Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] On 17 February President Zhelyu Zhelev received representatives of the Rakovski Bulgarian Officer Legion, who conveyed to him alarming details related to the financial situation of the Armed Forces. They demanded transparency and openness in reporting the budgets of the Army and the Ministry of Interior and insisted on decreasing the apparatus of the General Staff and the Ministry of Defense. The issue of restricting certain civil rights of the military in the Defense and Armed Forces Bill, which was adopted at the first reading, was addressed. Those issues, President Zhelev said, will be placed on the agenda of the first meeting of the Consultative Council on National Security.
