920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / Pakistan pledge on Kashmir Mr Nawaz Sharif, Pakistan's prime minister, said yesterday the country had a mission to free the Himalayan region of Kashmir from Indian rule, Reuter reports from Islamabad. Pakistan would fulfil its commitment to Kashmiri people, Mr Sharif told a rally in Pakistan-ruled Azad (Free) Kashmir before leading the crowd in chanting 'Kashmir will become Pakistan'. India rules two-thirds of Kashmir and Pakistan the remainder. The two countries have fought two of their three wars since independence from Britain in 1947 over the region.
