BFN [Excerpts] Cajnice, 12 Apr (TANJUG) -- According to the command of the Bosnian Serb Herzegovina Corps, a paramedic team was hit in the American bombing raid on civilian targets and positions of Herzegovina Corps soldiers yesterday instead of tanks, as NATO representatives claimed. Milomir Ceranic and Radomir Maljevic, paramedics from Gacko, were killed on this occasion. Two houses in the Serb village of Bare, two km south of the Gorazde town center on the right bank of the Drina river, were also destroyed. [passage omitted] No one was killed in the attack launched by American planes on Preljuca [15 km south of Gorazde] but one passenger car was destroyed. Serb forces' tanks were not hit in this attack either, contrary to claims by NATO representatives. [passage omitted]
