Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The subject of the main disagreement during the meeting in the Kremlin between the president of the Russian Federation and Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin, at which they discussed the composition of the government, was Boris Fedorov. An ITAR-TASS correspondent learned this from a high-ranking source in the president's apparatus. The prime minister offered Boris Fedorov the post of finance minister in the reshuffled government. In response, Fedorov stipulated his conditions: the resignation of Deputy Prime Minister Aleksandr Zaveryukha and Central Bank Chairman Viktor Gerashchenko. According to the same source, Viktor Chernomyrdin and First Vice Premier Oleg Soskovets did not accept Boris Fedorov's demands. As a result, Boris Fedorov announced his resignation. Thus, the composition of the government remains unknown.
