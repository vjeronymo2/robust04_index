Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vietnamese Foreign Ministry announced on Thursday that Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet will pay visits to Thailand and Cambodia. His visit to Thailand is made at the invitation of Thai Prime Minister Chuan Likphai. The spokeswoman of the Vietnamese Foreign Ministry, Ms. Ho The Lan said: The visit to Cambodia is to promote friendship and cooperation, especially in the fields of economy, trade, education, science, an technology. The two sides will discuss issues of territorial integrity and the status of Vietnamese living in the Vietnamese-Cambodian borderline.
