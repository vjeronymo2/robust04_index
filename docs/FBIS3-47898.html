Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 17 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Chairman of the State Great Hural of Mongolia N. Bagabandi has sent telegrams to V.F. Shumeyko and I.P. Rybkin in which he congratulated them in connection with their election to the posts of the chairman of the Council of the Russian Federation and the chairman of the State Duma respectively. In the telegrams he has expressed confidence that the Mongolian- Russian relations including the interparliamentary cooperation would be hence developing and strengthening for the benefit of the two countries.
