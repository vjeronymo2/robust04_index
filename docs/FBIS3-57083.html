Language: English Article Type:BFN [by ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow January 18 TASS -- Deputies of the Russian State Duma have refused to discuss the liberation of Aleksandr Rutskoy, Ruslan Khasbulatov and others arrested on charge of involvement in the Moscow October revolt. The proposal put forward by Deputy Leonid Petrovskiy from the Communist Party faction was supported by 145 parliament members. Petrovskiy claimed their arrest and custody violates Russian legislation.
