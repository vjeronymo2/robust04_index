931124 FT 24 NOV 93 / MPs warn on merchant fleet MPs warned yesterday that the UK could be vulnerable in wartime because of falls in the merchant fleet and the number of British seafarers. In 1970 the UK had the biggest merchant fleet in the world, with 1,800 ships. Now it is 14th largest in the world. The Commons' employment committee said: 'Any further decline in the number of ships or trained sailors at the disposal of Her Majesty's government may have a marked and detrimental effect on the UK's ability to defend itself.'
