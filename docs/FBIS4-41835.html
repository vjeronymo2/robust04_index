BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow, 24 June -- Anatoliy Chubays has reacted very quickly to deputies' demands in the State Duma today to create a solid legal basis for the privatization process. The chairman of the state committee for the management of state property sent a telegram which was circulated as quickly among the deputies during the plenary session. "Taking into consideration the wishes of the deputies and on the basis of the presidential resolution of 10 June 1994, I think it would be expedient for the government to submit for the State Duma's consideration the bill "On the state program of privatization of state and municipal enterprises," Anatoliy Chubays wrote. He told the parliamentarians that the state committee for the management of state property had already prepared such a bill and it could be submitted to the State Duma immediately after Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin's return from his foreign business trip on 25 June.
