BFN [Text] At 0600 today, the South Lebanese Army's [SLA] al-Qantarah position came under bursts of 12.7-mm machine-gun fire from terrorist positions in Qabrikha. The al-Qantarah post also came under automatic weapons and rocket fire at 2215 yesterday. In both cases, there were no casualties among the SLA soldiers, who replied with tank guns and artillery fire.
