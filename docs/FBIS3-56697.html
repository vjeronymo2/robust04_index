Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [SEGODNYA report: "Russian-Belarusian Agreements To Remain Valid"] [Text] The Russian Foreign Ministry issued a statement yesterday on Russian-Belarusian interstate relations. The document says that these remain priority relations for Russia, as they are aimed at fostering friendship and comprehensive political, economic, and humanitarian cooperation. According to a high-ranking Russian diplomat, the Russian Federation Foreign Ministry's statement is designed to assure the Belarusian side that earlier agreements between the two countries will not be revised depending on political expediency.
