940921 FT 21 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Healthcall expansion Healthcall, the duty doctor organisation, is buying, through its Healthcall Services subsidiary, Medical Support Personnel for up to Pounds 1.85m. Medical Support's trading arm, Euromed reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 326,000 on turnover of Pounds 4.1m in the year to October 31. Net assets at the end of the period were Pounds 132,000. Mr Maurice Henchey, Healthcall's chief executive, said the acquisition fitted his company's strategy of expanding the services it offers to its 9,500 GPs and patients. He said Healthcall intended to continue to expand both organically and through acquisition. An initial Pounds 1.07m will be paid on completion and satisfied by Pounds 603,000 cash, Pounds 220,000 in loan notes and Pounds 250,000 in shares. A further payment of about Pounds 750,000 in cash will be related to post tax profits. Euromed specialises in placing medical professionals, such as chiropodists, dieticians and psychologists on temporary and occasionally permanent placements with hospitals, health centres, GPs and industrial clients. Healthcall's share price closed unchanged yesterday at 104p.
