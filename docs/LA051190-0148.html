May 11, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ROBERSON IS TOP COUNTY SCORER IN GIRLS' DIVING

Susie Dorrough of Woodland Hills Louisville High School won the Southern Section 2-A Division girls' diving title Thursday with a score of 433.55 at the Industry Hills Swim Center in the City of Industry.

In the boys' 2-A Division, Matthew Dahl of Calabasas won with a score of 412.95.

Shannon Roberson of Trabuco Hills finished seventh (349.40) and Rachel Wacholder of Laguna Beach was ninth (335.90) in the girls' competition.

Robert Smith of Woodbridge finished 13th (262.40) in the boys' competition.
