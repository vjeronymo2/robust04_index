BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondent Sergey Karmalito] [Text] Simferopol, 26 May (ITAR-TASS) -- "Guarantees of stability and peace in Crimea exist and they will be provided for," Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov said today at a session of the Crimean parliament which took place here. "There are no fears, no threats, `the hot heads' are surrounded by normal, calm people and they will not be permitted to work their evil deeds either in Crimea or Ukraine, or anywhere else," he stressed. The president of Crimea appealed to representatives of the mass media to reassure their listeners, viewers, and readers.
