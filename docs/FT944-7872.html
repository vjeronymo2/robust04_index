941123 FT 23 NOV 94 / World Trade News: Ingersoll-Rand - Contracts & ventures Ingersoll-Rand, the US industrial equipment group, has won a Dollars 6.5m contract from Gasprom, the Russian gas monopoly, for German-made Klemm ground engineering drills, UK-made portable compressors and other equipment. Delivery will take place early next year.
