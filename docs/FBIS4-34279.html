BFN [From "The Voz Andes Newscast"] [Text] President Sixto Duran-Ballen has appointed Miguel Rosero Barba to be the new national police commander general. One of the new commander general's main tasks will be to strengthen ties with the media through open-door policies and by the necessary creation of a public relations division that will answer journalists' questions. The new police commander general, Manuel Rosero, believes having the people's trust and confidence is an important condition for successful police work.
