Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 20 Mar (MENA) -- Unidentified gunmen opened fire tonight on a police vehicle in Markaz Sidfa, Asyut Governorate, Upper Egypt. Four of the vehicle's passengers were martyred and five others were injured. A responsible source in the Interior Ministry said that two citizens who happened to be in the area were also injured.
