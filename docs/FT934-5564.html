931129 FT 29 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: European Monetary System The Irish punt moved above the D-Mark on the back of a stronger UK pound. The Dutch guilder remained the strongest currency, but the gap between it and the Danish krone, the weakest member, narrowed to 4.32 per cent from 4.51 per cent. The Portuguese escudo slipped back to close just above the krone after heavy selling. Currencies, Page 31
