February 6, 1989, Monday, Southland Edition

HUSSEIN SAID TO PLAN VISIT TO U.S. SHORTLY

King Hussein of Jordan will visit the United States soon to discuss the Palestinian problem with President Bush, Prime Minister Zaid Rifai was quoted as saying Sunday.

In an interview with the Kuwaiti newspaper Al Watan, Rifai gave no date for the visit but said the talks will cover latest developments in the Middle East, the Palestinian problem and the Arab-Israeli conflict.

There have been reports Bush plans to invite leading Middle East leaders for considerations on a settlement of the 40-year Arab-Israeli problem.
