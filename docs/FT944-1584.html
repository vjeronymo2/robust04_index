941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / Chancellor opens new LSE headquarters Kenneth Clarke, the chancellor, yesterday opened the London Metal Exchange's new headquarters and trading ring in Leadenhall Street in the City. Mr Clarke (pictured left next to Mr Raj Bagri, the LME chairman) said: 'The LME is a great British success story. Its impressive performance ensures its predominant position as the world's premier base metals market.'
