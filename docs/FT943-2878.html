940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / UK Company News: BZW Convertible BZW Convertible Investment Trust reported net asset value down from 118.13p to 116.26p in the year to July 31. Net revenue slipped from Pounds 4.3m to Pounds 3.98m and earnings per share were 7.84p (8.52p). The final dividend is 3.3p (same) for an unchanged total of 7.8p.
