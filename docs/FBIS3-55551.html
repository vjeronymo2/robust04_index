Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Addressing the group of independent deputies in the new parliament, Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin said that the government will never align itself with the extreme right or with the extreme left, but will always stick to its reform policies. He said that the country will no longer be subjected to shock treatment. Inconsistent and impetuous behavior is intolerable, especially where it concerns the economy, he said. At the same time Viktor Chernomyrdin stressed that the government does not intend to use populist methods either.
