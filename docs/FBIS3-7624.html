Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 30 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent thanks to Changhun-tong No. 1, Mangyongdae District, Pyongyang, the Sangnong mine, the eighth workteam of the garment factory of the Ministry of Railways, Chon Pong-ho and his wife O Chong-pok residing in Changhun-tong No. 1, Yi Ho-kyu, deputy director of a department of the Korean Central News Agency, and his wife Yi Pok-nyo, and Ko Chi-song, secretary of the primary party committee of the Pyongyang Circus, and his family and relatives, who had set examples in displaying the traditional traits of army-people unity. These units, officials and working people visited People's Army units on various occasions and deepened the feelings of kinship. Comrade Kim Chong-il also sent thanks to officials of the national directorate of tourism who had contributed to promoting the conveniences of railway passengers.
