920204 FT 04 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Drayton Far Eastern assets up The net asset value of Drayton Far Eastern Trust improved by some 11 per cent over the 12 months to December 31, to 96.3p against 86.9p. Net revenue improved from Pounds 711,000 to Pounds 1.05m for earnings of 0.92p (0.64p) per share. A proposed final distribution of 0.5p lifts the total for 1991 to 0.625p (0.6p).
