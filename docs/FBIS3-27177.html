Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 21 (XINHUA) -- The Chinese Ministry of Culture hosted a banquet here this evening to mark the 35th anniversary of the cultural cooperation agreement between China and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK). Chinese Minister of Culture Liu Zhongde, Charge d'Affaires Ad Interim of the DPRK Embassy in Beijing Pae Yong-chae, and nearly 40 other honorable guests attended the banquet.
