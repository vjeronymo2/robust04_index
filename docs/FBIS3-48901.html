Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Text] Reports that ASEAN will hold economic talks with Taiwan have been dismissed as untrue. In a press statement issued in Jakarta today, ASEAN Secretary General Datuk Ajit Singh said ASEAN has no plan to hold any official talks with Taiwan. The ASEAN secretary general stressed that it is normal for ASEAN businessmen to hold consultations with third parties at international meetings. In this connection, Datuk Ajit Singh said ASEAN officials may hold hold informal talks with Taiwanese counterparts at an APEC [Asia Pacific Economic Cooperation] meeting in Jakarta at the end of this month.
