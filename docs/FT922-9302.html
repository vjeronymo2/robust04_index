920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / UK Company News: New Frontiers shows strong asset growth A strong performance by Latin American and south-east Asian markets helped New Frontiers Development Trust report a 17 per cent advance to 73.5p in net asset value over the six months to March 31. Net revenue fell to Pounds 309,000 (Pounds 442,000) for earnings of 0.31p (0.44p) per share.
