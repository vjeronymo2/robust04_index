Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The presidency of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina held a session today, chaired by President of the Presidency Alija Izetbegovic. The presidency passed a decision on changes in the territorial organization of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army: A new, 7th Corps of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army has been set up, and the 6th Corps has been abolished, or rather, merged with the 4th Corps [operating in the Mostar area]. Brigadier Mehmed Alagic, former commander of the 3d Corps [operating in the Zenica area] of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army, has been appointed commander of the 7th Corps. Brigadier Sahib Mahmudin, former chief of staff of the 3d Corps of the Bosnia- Herzegovina Army, has been appointed commander of the 3d Corps of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army. The presidency accepted the resignation of Mufid Memija from the post of director general of Sarajevo Radio-Television. Amila Omerpasic, electrical engineer, has been appointed acting director general. [passage omitted]
