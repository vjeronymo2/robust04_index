September 3, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TALKING ABOUT THOSE RADIO TALKERS

As to KFI's general manager Howard Neal's statement, "We see ourselves as the Arsenio Hall of talk radio and KABC is Johnny Carson":

Everyone knows that the truest comparison would be KABC as the "MacNeil/Lehrer News-hour" and KFI as "The Newlywed Game."

TODD SOMERS

Pasadena
