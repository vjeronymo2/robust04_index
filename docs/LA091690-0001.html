September 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

KNBC, which presents good newscasts in general, is a pleasure to watch.

But may I offer a small suggestion to Kelly Lange? Please, Kelly, update your makeup and hairdo. The long, bushy hair is so outdated. You are a very attractive lady, but a shorter, neater, smarter hair style would be an immense help. And please dump those big, circular, dangling earrings.

This is a personal opinion, of course, but many of my friends have expressed the same opinions.

Caitlin Adler, Santa Monica
