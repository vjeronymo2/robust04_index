931012 FT 12 OCT 93 / French inflation gathers pace French retail price inflation accelerated in September, as prices rose 0.4 per cent during the month, taking the annualised increase to 2.3 per cent, according to provisional figures from Insee, the statistics institute, writes Alice Rawsthorn in Paris. The figures show a quickening of the recent trend. French consumer prices were virtually static over the summer, rising by 0.1 per cent in July and stabilising in August.
