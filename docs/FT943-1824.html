940922 FT 22 SEP 94 / People: Balfour Beatty Peter Mason, chief executive of Balfour Beatty, BICC's construction arm, has been appointed chairman with immediate effect, taking over from Sir Robert Davidson who is retiring. Mason, 48, joined Balfour Beatty from Norwest Holst in 1992. Sir Robert has been appointed chairman of Devonport Management, the company formed in 1987 to manage Devonport Royal Dockyard, and continues as a non-executive director of BICC.
