940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / World News in Brief: Brian Lara makes highest cricket score West Indian Brian Lara celebrated making the highest score in first class cricket when he reached 501 not out for Warwickshire against Durham at Edgbaston, Birmingham. The previous best was 499, made 35 years ago by Hanif Mohammad of Pakistan. Lara's success came seven weeks after his world record Test innings of 375 against England in the West Indies. At Trent Bridge, Nottingham, yesterday, England won the first Test against New Zealand by an innings and 90 runs.
