February 16, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

PACIFIC TO PLAY HOST TO UC IRVINE TONIGHT

UC Irvine, which has won three of its last four games, will face the University of the Pacific, which has a nine-game losing streak, at 7:30 tonight in a Big West Conference basketball game in the Spanos Center.

Pacific has won only once in 11 Big West games and has a 5-16 overall record. Irvine (9-12, 6-6) beat the Tigers, 90-88, Jan. 21 when center Mike Doktorczyk made all 12 shots from the floor and scored 28 points. Doktorczyk is hampered by a severe thigh bruise, but probably will play tonight.

The Anteaters' new inside threat, Ricky Butler, scored 20 or more for three consecutive games before being triple-teamed by Cal State Fullerton Saturday night. He was held to 13 points as Irvine lost, 78-75. JOHN WEYLER
