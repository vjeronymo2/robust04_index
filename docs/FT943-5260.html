940905 FT 05 SEP 94 / International Company News: Irish Permanent pays Pounds 6.7m for banking arm Irish Permanent Building Society is buying Guinness & Mahon, the Dublin-based private banking arm of Guinness Mahon, for Pounds 6.7m. The purchase by IPBS, Ireland's largest building society with assets of IPounds 2.7bn, follows its acquisition of Prudential Life of Ireland, and comes in the run-up to the publication of the prospectus it will issue as part of its flotation. IPBS is the first Irish society to convert to plc status, and is to be floated on the London and Dublin Stock Exchanges.
