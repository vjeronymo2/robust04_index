June 26, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Mid-June Vehicle Sales Down 3.6%: Sales of North American-made cars and trucks for the period lagged 3.6% behind a lackluster performance in the year-ago period, the companies announced. Overall, domestic auto makers said they sold vehicles at an average daily rate of 37,962 during the June 11-20 period, compared to an average of 39,392 a day during the mid-June period last year.

Car sales trailed by 4.5% and light truck sales lagged 2.2% for the period. General Motors, Ford and Chrysler said their mid-June sales dropped 6.8% to an average daily rate of 34,655 from an average 37,192 a day for mid-June last year. Sales of U.S.-made cars with Japanese company nameplates jumped 54.7% from a year earlier.
