Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report in "In Brief" column] [Text] Moscow -- According to Aleksey Vorobyev, press secretary of the Democratic Party of Russia, on 6 January representatives of State Duma factions formed by deputies of the Russian Agrarian Party, the Democratic Party of Russia, and the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia intend to meet to discuss prospects for resuming the "Parliamentary Hour" TV program and according ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA the status of a parliament publication. The meeting will take place in Agrarian Party headquarters. The deputies plan to ask the remaining five factions in the State Duma to support their initiative.
