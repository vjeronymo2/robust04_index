910502 FT 02 MAY 91 / World News in Brief: S Africa strike threat Jay Naidoo, general secretary of the South African Congress of Trade Unions, the country's biggest labour federation, told a May Day crowd he would call a nationwide strike if the government failed to curb township violence. Later, a gang of men sprayed a taxi-stand with gunfire in a black Johannesburg township, killing at least six people. Nine other people were found dead in the townships.
