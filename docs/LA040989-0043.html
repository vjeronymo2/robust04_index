April 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CITY LINES: STRIKING ARMOIRE

A striking armoire/entertainment center in cherry rosewood with black lacquer trim, featuring a top circle mirror, was one of a variety of new products featured at Westweek by the firm of Ambience. The design was a cooperative effort by the firm's New York office. It was seen in the Mandel & Co. showroom. SAM HALL KAPLAN
