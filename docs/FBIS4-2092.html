BFN [From the "990 Morning News" program] [Text] A Shanghai delegation led by Mayor Huang Ju yesterday [4 April] left Shanghai for goodwill visits to Italy and Germany. Officials in charge of concerned departments in Shanghai including Xu Zhaochun, Hua Jianmin, (Chen Zhenmin,) and (Fu Weiguo) were among members of the delegation. (Balairo), Italian consul-general in Shanghai, accompanied the delegation to Italy. The delegation will visit Milan, Italy and Hamburg, Germany at the invitation of the governments of the two cities, which are sister cities of Shanghai.
