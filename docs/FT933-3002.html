930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / DSS undermines morale, MP says EXPANSION OF the role of the state in welfare provision had resulted in excessive public expenditure and undermined individual responsibility, Mr Michael Portillo, chief secretary to the Treasury, argued last night. He said: 'For many people the role of government has sapped from them - one might almost say confiscated - their sense of responsibility towards other people.' Mr Portillo, who is primarily responsible for limiting government expenditure, maintained that too broad a benefit system could undermine the morale of those who received help.
