BFN [Text] Chinese Deputy Foreign Minister Tang Jiaxuan and his eight-member delegation arrived in Bangkok late last night for a two-day visit to Thailand. Tang Jiaxuan is scheduled to hold talks with Foreign Ministry Permanent Secretary Pracha Khunakasem this morning. He will also call on Foreign Minister Prasong Sunsiri and Deputy Foreign Minister Surin Phitsuwan to exchange views on bilateral relations, regional issues, and matters of common concern. Tang Jiaxuan will leave Bangkok tomorrow morning to continue his trip to Malaysia and Brunei.
