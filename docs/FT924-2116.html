921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / Warning over support for Tecs SIR Brian Wolfson, one of the leading businessmen behind the establishment of the Training and Enterprise Councils, has described his disappointment at the lack of government support for Tecs. Sir Brian, chairman of Wembley PLC and of the National Training Task Force, told the Institute of Manpower Studies that Tecs, which administer government training schemes in England and Wales, were 'fighting to make do with the crumbs left on the table'.
