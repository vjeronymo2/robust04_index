USAF Scientific Advisory Board; Meeting The USAF Scientific Advisory Board (SAB) Aircraft Self Defense Against IR Missiles Panel will meet from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. on 7&hyph;8 April 1994 at Phillips Laboratory, NM. The purpose of these meetings are to receive briefings and to have discussions concerning IR missiles. These meetings will be closed to the public in accordance with section 552b of title 5, United States Code, specifically subparagraphs (1) and (4). For further information, contact the SAB Secretariat at (703) 697&hyph;8404. Patsy J. Conner, Air Force Federal Register Liaison Officer.
