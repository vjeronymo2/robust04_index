April 21, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NUNN SHOWS NOTHING TO GET EXCITED ABOUT

Michael Nunn is getting a bit too arrogant for his own good.

His reaction to the fans' boos -- "They don't take any punches" -- indicates complete disregard for their feelings. One can reply, "He gets paid for it."

Until he defeats someone of the caliber of Sugar Ray Leonard, Tommy Hearns or Marvelous Marvin Hagler, let him realize that so far he isn't the second coming of Muhammad Ali.

HY GLASS, Sherman Oaks
