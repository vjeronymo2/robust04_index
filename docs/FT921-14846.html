920114 FT 14 JAN 92 / International Company News: Peugeot Citroen registers fall in net profit PEUGEOT Citroen's 1991 net profit was probably 'somewhat below' that of the previous year, Mr Jacques Calvet, chairman of the French car maker, said yesterday, AP-DJ reports. However, he reaffirmed his belief that the result would be favourable compared with those of other leading car makers. Mr Calvet, added that Peugeot's consolidated revenue was fairly stable last year, against that of the previous year. Peugeot posted net profit after payments to minority interests of FFr9.3bn (Dollars 1.75bn) in 1990, down 10.5 per cent from 1989. Revenue rose 4.6 per cent to FFr160bn.
