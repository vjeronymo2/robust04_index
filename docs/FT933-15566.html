930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / Charts blamed for QE2 grounding A REPORT on the grounding of the cruise ship Queen Elizabeth II off the US east coast last August said yesterday that the ship's master was too reliant on inaccurate charts. The Department of Transport's Marine Accident Investigation Branch said the master also omitted to take full account of the effect of squat, a nautical term for the effect of speed on the draught of a ship in shallow water.
