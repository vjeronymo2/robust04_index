BFN [Text] Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos expressed his concern over the upsurge in violence in Turkey following yesterday's bomb attack against the Ecumenical Patriarchy in Istanbul. Venizelos stressed a point in U.S. President Bill Clinton's letter to Greek Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou and Turkish Prime Minister Tansu Ciller in which Clinton raises the issue of the status and protection of the Ecumenical Patriarchy.
