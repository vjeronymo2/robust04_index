930429 FT 29 APR 93 / UK Company News: Moorgate Smaller raises Pounds 25m Moorgate Smaller Companies Investment Trust has raised a total of Pounds 25m through a placing of 25m units. Of this amount Pounds 10m has been subject to clawback. The trust said public applications were received for 4.43m units, which will be satisfied in full and have been clawed back from institutions. A total of 19.7m new shares will be allotted under the placing and offer. This is based on the undiluted net asset value at the close of business on April 22 of 123.39p per share. Dealing in the shares is expected to begin today.
