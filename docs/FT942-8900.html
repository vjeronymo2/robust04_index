940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / UK Company News: James Smith Ests James Smith Estates, the property investment company, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.57m for the year to March 24, against Pounds 1.45m. During the period net assets per share increased from 114p to 129p after taking into account a revaluation and the Pounds 12.4m placing and open offer in March. Turnover fell slightly to Pounds 2.35m (Pounds 2.39m) resulting from a small number of voids. Recent purchases have increased the rent roll to Pounds 3.25m. Profits from sale of flats on long leases rose from Pounds 125,000 to Pounds 195,000. As expected, the proposed final dividend is 3.1p making a total of 4.65p (4.2p) from earnings per share of 6.8p (6.3p).
