BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 13 (KCNA) -- A national seminar was held here Tuesday on the occasion of the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song. The seminar was attended by Politburo member of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] and Vice- President Yi Chong-ok, Secretary of the C.C., WPK Kim Ki-nam, Vice- Premier of the Administration Council Chang Chol, officials concerned, party cadres and men of science, education, culture and art and the press. Speeches were made at the seminar on various subjects including "The Respected Leader Comrade Kim Il-song Is a Great Thinker and Theoretician Who Has Laid an Ideological Foundation of Socialism Centred on the Masses", "The Great Leader Comrade Kim Il-song Is a Genius of Creation and Construction Who Has Laid Solid Material and Technical Foundations of Socialism" and "Respected Comrade Kim Il- song Is a Great Leader of the Revolution Who Has Provided Basic Guarantee for Carrying Forward the Socialist Cause of Chuche To Accomplishment". The speakers were unanimous in saying that the respected leader whom the whole world looks up to as the sun of mankind is a distinguished leader of the era of independence who started and has led to victory the socialist cause of chuche with his unique idea and great revolutionary practice, regarding it as his maxim in life to "believe in the people as in heaven", and a benevolent father of the people who is bringing into reality an independent and creative life, the age-old desire of mankind, with his politics of trust and love. Noting that the respected leader is a great thinker and theoretician who has provided the lifeblood of the Korean-style socialism by creating the immortal chuche idea, the speakers pointed out that he has successfully built a great pyramid of socialism by consistently applying the political idea of independence with his unswerving revolutionary faith and firm will. They explained that the great leader set forward the most correct guideline for the working-class army building and military activities by founding the chuche-based military thought with self- reliance in independence as the core and fostered invincible military forces capable of guaranteeing the socialist cause with credit. Saying the great leader has, for the first time in the history of the communist movement, perfectly solved the question of carrying forward the guidance of a political leader and provided a basic guarantee for the completion of the socialist cause, they stressed that this is the greatest of exploits performed by him.
