BFN [Text] Hamburg, April 16 (CTK) -- Czech Defense Minister Antonin Baudys, on a short visit to the north German city of Hamburg, and his German counterpart Volker Ruehe today agreed on carrying out a joint military exercise, the two ministers told journalists at a joint news conference. Baudys and Ruehe said they were satisfied with the two countries' military cooperation so far. However, no details concerning the place or the date of the planned joint exercise have been given. Ruehe visited the Czech Republic in October last year.
