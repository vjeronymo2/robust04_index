BFN [Text] Egypt has invited the foreign ministers of the states that took part in the peace conference in Madrid in October 1991 to attend the ceremony that will be held under President Mubarak's sponsorship on Wednesday [4 May] morning to sign the Palestinian-Israeli agreement to begin the implementation of the Declaration of Principles. Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin will arrive in Cairo on Tuesday afternoon to meet with Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, who will arrive on the same day, to discuss the two remaining points concerning the area of Jericho and the Allenby Bridge.
