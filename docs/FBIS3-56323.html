Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The speaker of the Russian Federation Council Vladimir Shumeyko has said that he backs Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin in terms of the disputed Northern Territories. He believes that the islands of Kunashir, Iturup, Shikotan and Habomai, claimed by Japan, "belong to Russia and there is no question of handing them over now." Shumeyko was giving his first news conference as Speaker of the Upper House of the Russian Federal Assembly. Shumeyko said that the prime minister's view was shared by the other Cabinet members. At the same time Shumeyko admitted that this view was different somewhat from the official one.
