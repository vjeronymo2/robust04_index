BFN [Text] Boris Yeltsin has appointed Georgiy Golikov head of administration of the city of Belgorod for the period till the relevant local self-rule bodies are formed. This was reported today by the presidential press service. By another decree he released Nikolay Yegorov from the post of head of administration of the Krasnodar Kray in connection with his assignment to another post. The press service gives no information as to who has been appoined instead of him.
