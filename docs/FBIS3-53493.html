Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The brother leader of the revolution received brother Safwat al-Sharif, information minister in sisterly Egypt and personal envoy of President Husni Mubarak of the Arab Republic of Egypt. The personal envoy delivered to the brother leader of the revolution a message from President Husni Mubarak in connection with the fraternal relations between the two fraternal countries as well as a number of Arab and international issues of common interest.
