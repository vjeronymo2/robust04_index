940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Pacer Systems in the black Pacer Systems, the US-based electronics group traded on the USM, announced a pre-tax profit of Dollars 390,000 (Pounds 251,600) for the first half of 1994, compared with a loss of Dollars 235,000. Turnover was lower at Dollars 13.1m (Dollars 14.29m). Earnings per share were 4 cents (3 cents loss). The dividend is unchanged at 3.5 cents. The order book at June 30 was Dollars 76m (Dollars 74m).
