Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Higher State Council [HCE] Chairman Ali Kafi received Abdelmalek Benhabyles, the chairman of the Constitutional Council, at the latter's request today. According to a Constitutional Council statement, Mr. Benhabyles conveyed a memorandum on proposals concerning the transitional period. At the presidential palace today, the HCE chairman also received Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat, who is currently visiting Algeria, and the delegation accompanying him. After the meeting, the Palestinian president stated that he had briefed Mr. Kafi on the progress of the peace talks and the obstacles facing the Palestinians in their current discussions with the Israelis. President 'Arafat added that the Israelis are trying, through their maneuvers, to avoid the implementation of the agreement signed by the Palestinians and the Israelis to withdraw fully from Gaza and Jericho as a first step toward a full withdrawal from all the occupied territories and Jerusalem. When the meeting was over President 'Arafat left Algiers at the conclusion of his brief visit.
