BFN [Text] Chiefs of the Ukrainian, Russian, and Belarusian border protection agencies approved in Kiev today [word indistinct] of memorandum and a joint declaration by the leaders of those countries regarding cooperation in border protection, which is to be signed in Moscow on 15 April. The documents were drafted by the following experts: Lieutenant General Yevgeniy Bocharov, commander of the Belarusian Border Troops; Colonel General Andrey Nikolayev, commander of the Russian Border Troops; and Army General Valeriy Hubenko, commander of the Ukrainian Border Troops. The experts were received today by Ukrainian President Kravchuk. He praised the work of the border troops commanders and emphasized that at this point, no country is in a position to resolve on its own problems connected with the protection of people and territory, not to mention the fight against illegal contraband. This can only be accomplished by combined efforts of the special services of all three states.
