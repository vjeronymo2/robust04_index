BFN [Text] The first seating for the trial of Muhammad Abdullah 'Abd-al-Rahman al-Khulayfi and the co-defendant Muhammad al-Mahi in the al-Thawra mosque incident [4th February 94] was held this morning at the Hall of Imam Ali at the judiciary headquarters under the presidency of Judge 'Abd-al-Rahman Muhammad 'Abd-al-Rahman Sharafi, the high court judge. At today's seating the court listened to the prosecution case that was followed by the investigation report. It is to be recalled that the accused had executed the assassination of several worshippers at the Omdurman mosque incident.
