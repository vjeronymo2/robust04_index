BFN [Text] Tokyo, April 22 (XINHUA) -- Japan's ruling coalition today picked Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata to succeed Morihiro Hosokawa as prime minister. The decision was made at a meeting here this afternoon of the coalition parties which agreed to present the 58-year-old Hata as their joint candidate in a parliamentary vote next Monday. Observers here believe that the veteran Hata is certain to win because of the coalition's majority in the decisive lower house.
