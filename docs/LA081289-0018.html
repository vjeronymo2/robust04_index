August 12, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BRADLEY'S FIGHT FOR POLITICAL SURVIVAL

Regarding the article on Bradley's financial investments -- I think his personal business affairs are of no concern to the public. The article was mean and uncalled for. Bradley has made mistakes (who hasn't?), but he has done a good job for Los Angeles. He has many friends and supporters even if we do not make a lot of noise.

HELEN R. SMITH

Los Angeles
