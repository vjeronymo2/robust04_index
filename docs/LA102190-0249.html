October 21, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

SET YOUR VCR

A handful of dust (TMC Wednesday at 6 a.m.) Director Charles Sturridge and his adapters have done a breathtakingly fine job of bringing to the screen Evelyn Waugh's spare, masterly novel about a sudden wrong turn of a marriage among the British smart set, circa 1932. With James Wilby, Kristin Scott Thomas, Rupert Grave and Alec Guinness.
