940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Porth Porth Group, the USM-traded decorations, packaging and framing products concern, incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 380,000 for the year to end-December. That was an improvement on the midyear deficit of Pounds 1.89m and compared with 1992's overall loss of Pounds 1.23m which included exceptional provisions of Pounds 1m. Turnover rose by Pounds 1m to Pounds 18.7m. Losses per share were cut from 6.4p to 2p.
