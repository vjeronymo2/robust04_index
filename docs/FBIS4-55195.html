BFN [Text] No secret agreement exists between the current government coalition and ethnic Hungarian political groups, nor any other document overstepping the framework of the government policy statement which was endorsed by parliament, said Lubomir Lintner, director of the government's Press and Information Office, in an interview with a TA SR correspondent today. Lintner was reacting to the statement of former Prime Minister Vladimir Meciar in the "Kroky" television program on Sunday in which Meciar said that the current government is a vassal of the Hungarian political parties.
