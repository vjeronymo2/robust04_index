August 23, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

MALL COUNTRY: Need a mall for your movie? Look no farther than Orange County.

For the last few days Arnold Schwarzenegger and company have been filming at MainPlace/Santa Ana for his new movie "Kindergarten Cop." . . . In the comedy, maverick cop Schwarzenegger goes undercover as a kindergarten teacher. "We needed a beautiful upscale environment in which Arnold's character will be noticeably out of place," said movie publicist Katherine Moore. Tom Hamilton and Shannon Sands
