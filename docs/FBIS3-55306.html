Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report in the "In Brief" column] [Text] Moscow -- Under an order by Colonel General Vasiliy Vorobyev, chief of the Main Military Budget and Finance Directorate of the Russian Federation Defense Ministry, as of 1 January 1994 the allocation of funds in Russian or foreign currency from the Russian Federation Defense Ministry budget for the payment of allowances, salaries, material values, and communal and other services to Russian Federation military courts will be terminated. This order was issued "in line with the 24 December 1993 letter by the Russian Federation Finance Ministry" and in execution of the Russian Federation Government decree on approving of the Statute on the Russian Federation Ministry of Justice. According to this statute, the Justice Ministry is tasked with ensuring the requisite conditions for the functioning of general jurisdiction and military courts. As has become known, however, this decision has come as a total surprise for the ministry's leadership because the statute says nothing about financing military courts.
