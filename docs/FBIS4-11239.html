BFN [Text] Defense Ministry extended the state of emergency in Huancabamba Province, Piura Department, for another 60 days to facilitate the implementation of counterterrorism operations in this region of the country. In view of this, civil rights such as the inviolability of residences, election of place of residence, the right to hold peaceful gatherings without guns, and the right of not being arrested without a court warrant are suspended. According to the supreme government decree published today in the official newspaper EL PERUANO, the Armed Forces will guarantee the control of internal order in keeping with the established law.
