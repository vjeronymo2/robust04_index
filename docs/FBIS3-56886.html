Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Mission Control Centre January 18 TASS -- Viktor Afanasyev, Yuriy Usachev and Valeriy Polyakov worked for nine days during a flight on board Mir space station. Medical experiments held an important place in the flight programme this week. Their purpose was to study the adaptation of the human organism to orbital flight and to assess the effects of weightlessness on psychophysiological reactions and work of cosmanauts. The ultrasonic method was used on Tuesday to determine the characteristics of the functioning of the heart and blood circulation in vessels of internal organs. During the day the physician will undergo a similar check with the use of means for the prevention of unfavourable effects of weightlessness on the human organism. The flight proceeds according to schedule. All the three cosmonauts are in good health and feel well.
