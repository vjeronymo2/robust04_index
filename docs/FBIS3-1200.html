Language: Somali Article Type:BFN [Text] The U.S. forces stationed at the gate of Afisyoni yesterday at 4:00 P.M. shot at a Toyota vehicle, causing death and injury. The vehicle had left the 1 May estate of Waberi District and was driven by Ali Ahmad Ayanle. Ayanle was accompanied by Hasan Oholow Weheliye and Hasan Hashi Weheliye. Hasan Oholow Weheliye, 30 years old, died there, may God rest his soul in peace. Dhibane Hasan Hashi Weheliye, Ali Ahmad Ayanle, and Asho Umar Mahmud, age 5, who lived on the estate, were injured.
