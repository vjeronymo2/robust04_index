Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The head of state, General Sani Abacha, today in Abuja received a special message from his Togolese counterpart, President Gnassingbe Eyadema. The message which was delivered by a five-man delegation headed by the minister of defense, Mr. Inoussa Bouraima, was not made public. The delegation later held a closed-door discussion with the head of state, Gen. Sani Abacha.
