941128 FT 28 NOV 94 / Arts (Openings): National Theatre, London In a fertile year that has already seen its 'The Three Lives of Lucie Cabrol' and its revival of 'The Street of Crocodiles', Theatre de Complicite opens its latest show, 'Out of a house walked a man ..', a co-production with the National Theatre, on Thursday at the Lyttelton. It features musical scenes from the writings of Danill Kharms and stars Kathryn Hunter and Marcello Magni.
