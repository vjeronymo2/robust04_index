BFN [Untitled POSTFACTUM report from the "Events of the Week" roundup -- from the FINANSOVYYE IZVESTIYA section page I] [Text] Textile and light industry enterprises are hoping for a budget advance of 400 billion rubles [R]. Following the recent meeting between Premier Viktor Chernomyrdin and Tatyana Sosninaya, chairman of the Textile and Light Industry Union, a letter signed by Viktor Chernomyrdin and proposing "by way of an exception to resolve in a favorable manner the question of offering textile and light industry enterprises a budget advance of R400 billion, issued in stages as of May 1994, for the purchase of raw materials" was sent to the Russian Ministry of Finance. Apart from this, it is recommended that "the examination and signing of a sector wage agreement be speeded up." According to Tatyana Sosninaya, a wage agreement may be signed before 26 April. In which case, the obstacles preventing the signing of the Treaty on Social Accord by the textile workers' union will be removed.
