October 7, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BEGGARS AND PEDDLERS

I was interested in your News and Briefs article Aug. 12 about the improved conditions of the beggars and peddlers in Acapulco.

I just returned from there and was saddened by the number of beggars and peddlers on the streets. You could not walk five steps without being accosted by someone. You cannot help them all. There are too many.

I saw a woman nursing a baby as she sold gum. I saw women dragging children who were so tired I could have cried for them.

It seems to me that the authorities should clean up the area, not just talk about it. It is a shame.

J. MOELICH

Las Vegas
