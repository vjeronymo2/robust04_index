BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Interior Minister Nahit Mentese has said that the PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] is desperate and for this reason they are attempting bombings in cities. On a study tour in Yalova, Mentese discussed the issue of terrorism at a meeting he held with citizens. [Begin Mentese recording] PKK leader Apo [Abdullah Ocalan] is continuously inciting his followers and his men. He is inciting them on the radio with his messages. He is saying: Go and plant bombs at tourist sites. Go and cause disturbances in Istanbul, Izmir, and Ankara. Aim at big cities. Apo is avoiding engagement because he is losing considerably in the southeast. We are taking every measure necessary. We are ready and thanks to our intelligence we are capable of capturing the terrorists. As you can see we capture terrorists everyday but our people should also assist us and they should also be careful. [end recording]
