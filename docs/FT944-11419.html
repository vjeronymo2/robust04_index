941107 FT 07 NOV 94 / World News in Brief: Hill win sets scene for a tense showdown England's Damon Hill withstood pressure from his German rival Michael Schumacher to win motor racing's Japanese Grand Prix and set the stage for a tense world championship showdown. The result left Schumacher just one point ahead in the title contest, with one race to go, in Australia next week. Mystery of Formula One, Page 13
