BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 21 (KCNA) -- All kinds of flowers are in full bloom and trees grow green at the April 15 children's flower garden as spring came. The flower garden covers an area of more than 100 hectares around the Mangyongdae school children's palace in Pyongyang. It was opened in April, 1992. Tens of thousands of trees and 50,000 plants of flowers of more than 130 kinds from all parts of the country and many countries of the world have taken roots, giving off fragrance. The garden serves as a fine base for the cultural and emotional life of the school youth and children and working people, as a base for botanical education.
