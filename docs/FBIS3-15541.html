Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Barter and Dollar Will Become the Foundation for Mutual Settlements Between Belarusian and Ukrainian Armies"] [Text] The Belarusian military delegation, led by Belarusian Defense Minister Colonel General Kozlovskiy, returned from Ukraine where it held negotiations with Ukrainian Defense Minister Radetskyy. During the visit, it discussed prospects for cooperation between the two Armies in the materiel and technical sphere, logistic support, and training of military specialists. The economic crisis in our republics sharply curtailed the ability of their Armies to purchase new equipment and weapons. It is the times and situation that call for the states to cooperate on a par basis. The barter system and the dollar will become the foundation for mutual settlements between Belarusian and Ukrainian Armies. The sides reached an agreement that Ukraine will commit itself to training engineering personnel for the Belarusian Army, in particular, electronics specialists. For its part, Belarus is ready to offer its military college facilities for the training of Ukrainian lieutenants. Also, military equipment surplus will be exchanged or sold to each other. The sides agreed to continue working meetings and consultations.
