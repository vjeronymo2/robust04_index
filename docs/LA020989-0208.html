February 9, 1989, Thursday, Late Final Edition

NATION;

BUSH HAS HORSESHOE PIT INSTALLED

President Bush has ordered a 40-foot horseshoe pit installed near the tennis courts on the White House south grounds. Not one to sit in the Oval Office, Bush already has strolled down to inspect the work, which is being handled by a National Park Service crew, and an aide confirmed today that the pit will be a 40-footer. The game of horseshoes apparently is one of the President's favorite forms of relaxation, and the aide said Bush also had a pit installed at Camp David.
