DATES: Comments must be received on or before October 17, 1994 in order to be assured of consideration. DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE Commodity Credit Corporation 7 CFR Part 1413 RIN 0560&hyph;AD39 1995 Upland Cotton Program AGENCY: Commodity Credit Corporation, USDA. ACTION: Proposed rule.

SUMMARY: The Agricultural Act of 1949 (1949 Act), as amended, requires the Secretary of Agriculture to implement an Acreage Reduction Program (ARP) for the 1995 crop of upland cotton which will result in a ratio of carry-over to total disappearance of 29.5 percent. This proposed rule would amend the regulations to set forth the acreage reduction percentage for the 1995 crop of upland cotton.
