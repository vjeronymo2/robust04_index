Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] An agreement on military cooperation between Romania and Ukraine was signed today by Gheorghe Tinca, Romanian minister of national defense, and General Vitaliy Radeskyy, Ukrainian minister of national defense. At a subsequent news conference, Tinca said that the treaty covers a large sphere of activities of cooperation in the military, scientific, and educational spheres. In his turn, General Vitaliy Radeskyy expressed the hope that good cooperation between the armies of the two countries can contribute to good cooperation in the political sphere, something which both Romania and Ukraine can put to their use in the future.
