Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pretoria Jan 4 SAPA -- The exact circumstances of the death of South Africa's ambassador to Bulgaria were being investigated in cooperation with that country's authorities, Foreign Affairs Spokesman Jaques Malan said on Tuesday. Mr A. P. Burger was found dead in his motor vehicle on Monday morning at his official residence in Sophia. There was no suspicion of foul play, said Mr Malan.
