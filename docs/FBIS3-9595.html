Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] We begin the news with a report from the session of the chamber of deputies of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna, held in Mostar: Today, in a warm and quiet Mostar, the Chamber of Deputies of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna approved the constitution of the federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina and tasked the Croat deputies -- members of the constituent assembly [of Bosnia-Herzegovina] -- with approving the constitution at the Bosnia-Herzegovina Assembly session in Sarajevo on 28 March. [passage omitted] Following the opening speeches, the discussions were held behind closed doors. A committee for monitoring the discussion this evening proposed the following conclusions: To form a delegation of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna, comprising Kresimir Zubak, Jadranko Prlic and General Ante Roso, which will attend the work of the constitutent assembly in Sarajevo on Monday, in addition to the deputies. A body for conducting political talks with the Muslim side on all issues is also to be formed, and will include Kresimir Zubak, Ivo Zivkovic, Perica Jukic, and Ivica Lucic. In addition to these items, the agenda of the Chamber of Deputies includes several draft laws. Discussion is under way.
