931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Lowland net assets up 41% Lowland Investment reported a net asset value, after deducting prior charges at par, of 282.4p per share as at September 30, up from the year-ago figure of 200.8p. The 41 per cent increase compared with a rise in the FT-A All-Share Index over the same period of 24.9 per cent. Attributable revenue edged ahead to Pounds 2.22m (Pounds 2.06m), equivalent to earnings of 9.45p (8.75p) per share. A proposed final dividend of 5.8p brings the total for the year to 9p (8.5p).
