931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Ross warns of Pounds 2m non-recurring charge Shares in Ross Group dived 6p to 16p yesterday as the industrial holding company warned that profits for the current year would be below earlier expectations, after charging non-recurring costs of at least Pounds 2m for restructuring. The company said trading conditions in October were worse than expected and with the exception of Gemini and Tadmod where growth had continued, measures had been and continued to be taken to reduce further overheads, albeit at a substantial short term cost. The company yesterday completed the purchase of Cascade and Keenon from Tomei of Hong Kong for Pounds 4m, payable by the issue of 13.3m new ordinary shares at 30p. In addition, Tomei has subscribed for a further 2.33m shares in Ross also at 30p.
