Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Report by correspondent Nikoletta Dhoukaki] [Excerpt] National Defense Minister Yerasimos Arsenis arrived at Corfu Airport last night at the invitation of the PASOK [Panhellenic Socialist Movement] Municipal Committee in Kerkira. Arsenis was accompanied by Mrs. Louka Katseli. Speaking at the Municipal Committee's annual meeting, Arsenis said he is convinced that PASOK will win the two 1994 elections and stressed that the government has the willingness and experience to deal with the challenge of the Greek presidency successfully. Referring to the government's foreign policy, Arsenis stressed that Greece will regain its international credibility and defend its national rights. [Begin Arsenis recording] We proceeded with courage on the Cyprus issue by announcing the joint Greek-Cypriot defensive area. Without many words and much publicity, we have passed everywhere the message that Greece is not joking, that we--the government and the people--are ready to deal with the danger from the East because we are not willing to give up even an inch of territory, airspace, or sea to Turkey. We will defend our national rights. [end recording] Discussing the Armed Forces, the national defense minister stressed that they belong to the Greek people and are not to be used in anyone's feud. He added that the new law passed by the government overturned New Democracy's injustices. [passage omitted]
