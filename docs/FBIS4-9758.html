BFN [Text] Brigadier General Silvano Colas Sanchez, communications minister, has visited the Provinces of Granma and Santiago de Cuba. In Granma, the general described several Communications Ministry enterprises' work in installing new television transmitters as very positive. This has upgraded signal reception, mainly in Bayamo City. In Santiago de Cuba, the minister discussed aspects of the project to install two new television transmitters, which will be conducted shortly, and the progress of expanding new telephone lines, which is being conducted at the main telephone station in Santiago de Cuba. During his extensive trip, the minister met with the workers of each facility, discussed quality service indexes, and criticized subjective problems that worsen the effect of the resources shortage and the special period. Finally, he urged the workers to improve telephone and postal services and to close ranks against those who hurt the prestige of the workers of this sector.
