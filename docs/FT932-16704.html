930403 FT 03 APR 93 / UK Company News: Public support for Holiday Chemical The offer to the public of shares in Holliday Chemical Holdings was subscribed 2.4 times. Some 10.2m shares were under offer and applications were received for 24m with a total value of almost Pounds 50m. Applications for 250 and 500 shares will be met in full; for 750, 1,000 and 1,500 the allocation is 500; those seeking 2,000 and 2,500 will receive 750; for 3,000 and 3,500 the return is 1,000; and applications for 4,000, 4,500 and 5,000 come away with 1,500 shares. For applications of more than 5,000 the allocation will be about 32 per cent. The 10.2m shares were the clawback element in a total of 40.7m shares, valued at Pounds 79.4m at the issue price of 195p, placed with institutional and other investors. Allotment letters are expected to be posted next Wednesday, and dealings to start the following day.
