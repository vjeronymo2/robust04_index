940802 FT 02 AUG 94 / World Trade News: Italy to upgrade ticket system Italy's state railroad has awarded Olivetti, the Italian computers group, and IBM of the US a contract to modernise the ticket-dispensing system in the 500 main Italian train stations, AP-DJ reports from Milan. The state railroad said that when the multi-year supply agreement was completed the value of the accord should exceed L400bn (Dollars 256m).
