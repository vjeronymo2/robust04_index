941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / International Company News: Ford to launch its smallest car Ford, the US carmaker, is to expand its range by entering a new segment of the European car market with the launch of a new car smaller than the Fiesta, its smallest car. Mr Alex Trotman, Ford chairman and chief executive, said that the new small car would be launched within the next three years in Europe. The company said that no decision had yet been made on a production location, but it is understood that the plant at Valencia, Spain, is front runner. Production volumes are likely to exceed 100,000 a year.
