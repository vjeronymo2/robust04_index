930513 FT 13 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Scottish TV chief paid Pounds 0.3m MR GUS Macdonald, managing director of Scottish Television, was paid a total of Pounds 319,700 last year, making him the highest paid director of the ITV company. The sum included a bonus of Pounds 131,166 for winning the franchise, based on one's year salary at the 1991 rate. Scottish retained its franchise with a bid of just Pounds 2,000 a year in the 1991 tenders. The company also has to pay 2 per cent of its advertising revenue to the government. This year the payments are expected to total Pounds 1.6m.
