BFN [Text] Ankara, June 16 (A.A.) -- Hans van den Broek, who is responsible for the European Union's [EU] foreign relations, visited Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin on Thursday [16 June]. Cetin, speaking during the meeting, said he was pleased to see van den Broek in Turkey, adding that Turkey's relations with the European Union will be discussed. Cetin said Turkey and EU relations are going through an intense stage adding that decisions beyond the customs union should be taken. The foreign minister also said Turkey and the EU have joint interest to several regional problems. "We will evaluate these problems," he added. Van den Broek said the main reason of his visit is to show support to Turkey which is going through economic difficulties. Recalling that this was his third visit to Turkey in 15 months, van den Broek said this reflected close Turkish-EU relations.
