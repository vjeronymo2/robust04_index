921026 FT 26 OCT 92 / International Company News: Morgan Grenfell Equity assets rise Net asset value of Morgan Grenfell Equity Income Trust stood at 94.15p at end-September. The trust's shares were first listed in November 1991 and the figure compares with 89.7p at end-March 1992. Available revenue for the year amounted to Pounds 922,000, equal to earnings of 3.7p per share. A final dividend of 2.45p makes a 3.45p total.
