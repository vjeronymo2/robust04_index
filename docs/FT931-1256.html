930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / World Trade News: Power order Hyundai Engineering and Construction, South Korea's largest building company, has won a Dollars 1.6bn order for four 300MW thermal-power plants in Libya, John Burton writes from Seoul. The contract exceeds the amount of total foreign orders that Hyundai received last year, worth Dollars 1.13bn. It is also equivalent to almost 60 per cent of all foreign contracts that South Korean construction companies obtained in 1992, which totalled Dollars 2.78bn.
