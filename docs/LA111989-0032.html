November 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

BARGAIN CORNER: CONSOLIDATORS OFFER CUTS IN ROOM RATES

Consolidators -- companies that sell low-fare air tickets and cruises -- also deal with hotel rooms.

One such company is Hotel Connections, established in 1985 and based in Petaluma, Calif. It offers discounts of $20 to $50 a night off regular room rates at about 70 hotels in the United States. Some of the properties are part of chains -- Hilton, Hyatt, Sheraton and Radisson.

Another chain the company works with is Chatwal Inns, which has seven properties in Manhattan.

Among the cities with participating hotels are San Francisco, San Diego, Scottsdale, New York, Atlanta, Dallas, Chicago, Denver, Orlando, Boston, Washington, D.C., and Las Vegas. The discounts are available all year including weekends, but there may be sold-out dates varying from property to property.

Reservations are made through a toll-free number to Hotel Connections, which processes the information to the hotel. You get a confirmation number over the phone, but no written confirmation. However, Hotel Connections reconfirms your reservation a day before your arrival. Your initial reservation has to be made at least 48 hours before arrival.

Payment must be made by credit card, with full payment required at the time of booking. No refunds are available for cancellations or date changes, but Hotel Connections will process a change of dates at no extra charge.

You can also call Hotel Connections to have a free brochure and rate sheet sent to you that lists the regular rate, discount rate and amount of tax for properties around the nation. Sold-out dates for each hotel are also listed. The rates quoted are for single or double occupancy. This rate sheet is upgraded quarterly.

You can also get a directory of the hotels working with Hotel Connections, including details about their amenities.

There is no service charge this year, but Hotel Connections is planning to start such a surcharge in 1990 that will probably be in the $7-$15 range per transaction. The same $20-$50 range of discounts for rooms will be available next year, according to a spokesman.

Contact Hotel Connections at 6 Petaluma Road North, Petaluma, Calif. 94952, or call toll-free (800) 456-1969. JACK ADLER
