BFN [Text] Sanaa, 4 May (MENA) -- Fighting broke out tonight in the city of Dhamar, 100 km south of Sanaa, between the Republican Guard forces (northern) and the garrison of the Basahib camp (southern). An official source at the Yemeni Socialist party [YSP] in Dhamar said that, at about 1930, the Republican Guard Forces attacked the Basahib camp with missiles, artillery, and tanks. Clashes are still taking place in front of the camp with all types of weapons. An electricity station was shelled and the camp's munitions and fuel depots were burned, according to the source. Meanwhile, a responsible source at the General People's Congress in Sanaa said that the YSP forces in Dhamar fired at Republican Guard camps and that no information is yet available. Power cuts have taken place in most parts of Sanaa since 2030 tonight.
