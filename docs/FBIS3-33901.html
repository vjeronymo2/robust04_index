Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Kuwait, 29 Jan, KUNA -- Muhammad Sediq Jakari, the Afghan president's envoy and the delegation accompanying him, arrived here today in order to convey a message from President Borhanoddin Rabbani for Shaykh Jabir al-Ahmad al-Sabah, the amir of the State of Kuwait. 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Dakhil, the Kuwaiti minister of state for cabinet affairs, welcomed him at the airport.
