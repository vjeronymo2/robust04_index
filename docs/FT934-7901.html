931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / Minister sued over 'trade restraint' A GUNSMITH is to sue Mr Michael Heseltine, trade and industry secretary, over alleged restraint of trade after a judge ruled his case should go to the High Court. Mr John Slough, managing director of JSL (Hereford) is seeking Pounds 300,000 for loss of sales, alleging that the Department of Trade and Industry blocked exports of sporting guns to Germany. The case arose because of confusion over documentation needed under a European directive.
