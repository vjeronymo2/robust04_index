920818 FT 18 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Plasmec mounts recovery to Pounds 307,000 Higher sales and operating profits, backed up by reduced interest charges, helped USM-quoted Plasmec recover in the first half of 1992. Pre-tax profit came to Pounds 307,000, compared with just Pounds 5,000 last time and with Pounds 202,000 in 1990. As prospects for the second half indicated a similar trading level, the interim dividend is raised to 1.5p, against 1p and 1.8p respectively. Turnover rose to Pounds 7.32m (Pounds 6.18m). Sales of injection moulded components rose 27 per cent to Pounds 1.6m, electronic and electromechanical equipment 12 per cent to Pounds 2.9m and electrical components and luxury gifts 21 per cent to Pounds 2.8m. Operating profits rose to Pounds 430,000 (Pounds 191,000) and interest costs were Pounds 123,000 (Pounds 186,000). Earnings per share came to 4.3p (nil).
