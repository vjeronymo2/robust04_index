BFN [Excerpt] Oleg Soskovets, first deputy prime minister of the Russian Federation, accompanied by Dr. Kamil al-Baba, minister of electricity, today visited the Tishrin thermal power station near Damascus. On arrival at the station, he was received by Sayf 'Alaw, assistant to the electricity minister, the station's director, and the Russian chief expert. Soskovets and his delegation toured the station's various sections and were briefed on the progress of the station's work. At the end of his tour, Soskovets congratulated the Syrian people on the inauguration of this station, which is considered one of the vital and important projects Syria has carried out in cooperation with the Russian Government. He expressed his country's readiness to cooperate with Syria to execute such joint projects. [passage omitted]
