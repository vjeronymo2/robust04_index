940428 FT 28 APR 94 / International Company News: AMR in PWA deal AMR, the parent of American Airlines, has signed a services agreement with Canadian Airlines, an offshoot of PWA, through which AMR will make an equity investment of CDollars 246m (USDollars 178.2m) in Canadian for a one-third economic interest, Reuter reports. The deal is for a comprehensive, 20-year services agreement and in the first full year the revenue value to AMR would be about USDollars 115m.
