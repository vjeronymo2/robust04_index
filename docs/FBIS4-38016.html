BFN [Text] His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id of Oman yesterday received [Yemeni] Presidential Council member brother 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani who conveyed to him a message from Presidential Council Chairman brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. The message deals with developments in our country and the bilateral friendly relations. The meeting was attended by His Excellency Qays Bin-'Abd-al-Mun'im al-Zawawi, [Omani] deputy prime minister for financial and economic affairs; and His Excellency Yusuf Bin-'Alawi Bin-'Abdallah, minister of state for foreign affairs in the fraternal Sultanate of Oman.
