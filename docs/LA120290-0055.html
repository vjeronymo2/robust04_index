December 2, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

'BOOTLEG' HOUSING STORY DRAWS FIRE, CLAIMS OF 'BIAS'

In the article by (staff writer) Vicki Torres, a 4-month-old baby is the central focus of two pictures with the article.

Is this baby responsible for the bootleg housing, illegal structures and businesses and blight on Woodbury Road?

The article was slanted and the pictures did not focus on the real issues at hand.

JERALDINE CHAMLIS

Pasadena
