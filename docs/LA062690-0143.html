June 26, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Quality Systems Inc.: The Tustin computer company reported a loss of $849,600 for the fiscal year ended March 31, contrasted with the loss of $313,500 reported for the previous year. Revenue rose 9%, to $9.6 million from $8.8 million. The company said that new product introductions during the fiscal year resulted in higher expenses that offset the revenue increase.
