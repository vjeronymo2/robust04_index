921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Schroders launches its first UK investment trust Schroders, the merchant bank and fund management group, plans to launch its first investment trust based on UK equities in the new year. The new trust, called the Schroder Split Fund, will have a split capital structure and Schroders will link a personal equity plan to the high-yielding income shares. The only investment trusts Schroders has launched to date are based on specialist areas such as Greece and Korea.
