BFN [Text] Acting Mayor Tian Fengshan met with a delegation from Hokkaido Development Consulting Company Ltd, of Japan this morning. Led by the former governor of Hokkaido, (Uchiko Nagahara), the delegation was invited by the provincial communications department to attend the ceremony marking the establishment of (Beilong) technology development company--a Sino-Japanese joint venture. Acting Governor Tian Fengshan extended a warm welcome to the visiting delegation. He said: Heilongjiang and Hokkaido have established a long-term friendly cooperative relationship and profound understanding. Heilongjiang is a major province with rich natural resources. Its economic development over the past few years can not be separated from its achievements in highway construction. The establishment of this (Beilong) joint-venture company to engage in highway design and development is very conducive to the construction and development of our province's communications undertakings. He expressed the hope that the Hokkaido Development Consulting Company Ltd, will conduct further cooperation with our province in agricultural development and urban construction in Harbin, including the building of a subway train. Also attending the meeting were Cai Qi, deputy secretary general of the provincial government.
