March 13, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

FULLERTON COLLEGE FORFEITS 7 VICTORIES, TIE

Fullerton College's softball team has forfeited seven victories and a tie for using an ineligible player, the college announced Monday.

After a 4-3 loss to Cypress Monday, the Hornets are 0-15 overall, 0-3 in the Orange Empire Conference. Pitcher Valerie Finley, who pitched in the eight games, was found to be academically ineligible.
