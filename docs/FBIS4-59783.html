BFN [Text] Cairo, 16 May (MENA) -- President Husni Mubarak received a telephone call from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih this morning during which they discussed the latest developments in the Yemeni conflict. The call is part of President Mubarak's efforts to stop the fighting and deal with the situation in fraternal Yemen to preserve the higher Arab interests and the region's stability.
