BFN [Statement issued by "a responsible source at the Presidential Council"; place and date not given] [Text] Despite the appeals of the world to the rulers of Sanaa to cease fire and to stop the shelling of vital economic installations and residential quarters, the Sanaa regime continued throughout today its shelling and barbarous acts and (?initiated) attacks on all front at about 1730 this evening. Our brave Armed Forces, as well as volunteers from the sons of our steadfast people, are repulsing them. We announce to the world our legitimate right to defend our territory and the honor of our womenfolk since the Sanaa regime does not respect any laws or values. Our Armed Forces, with God's will and assistance and in cooperation with the sons of our people, will repulse the attacks of the aggressors and will protect dauntless Aden.
