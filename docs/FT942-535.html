940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / OECD doubts over Greece Greece will continue to suffer from slow growth and high budget deficits this year, the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development said in its half-yearly report. GDP would grow by only 1 per cent this year and 1.6 per cent in 1995. The government's target for the 1994 budget deficit would be hard to attain because government revenue growth would be slow despite tax legislation introduced in April. 'The (realised deficit) is likely to exceed the budget target unless measures recently taken to improve tax compliance prove unusually successful,' the OECD said.
