Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Feb. 23 -- Secretary General of the ASEAN organization Datuk Ajit Singh and his wife arrived here on Feb. 21 for a week-long official visit to Vietnam as guests of Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam. Mr. Datuk Ajit Singh was received here yesterday by Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet. He held talks with Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam. The two sides informed each other of the measures to promote the multi-sided cooperative relations between Vietnam and ASEAN in the spirit of the agreement reached by the two sides at the 26th ASEAN Foreign Ministers' Conference last July in Singapore. The ASEAN secretary general informed the Vietnamese chief diplomat of the ASEAN Standing Committee's decision to invite Vietnam to participate as observer in six professional cooperation committees and a number of cooperative projects of ASEAN in the fields of science, technology, culture, information, public health, environment, tourism and training. In order to increase information and mutual understanding, Mr. Datuk Ajit Singh added, ASEAN Secretariat will send its officials to Vietnam to exchange views with the Vietnamese side on concrete matters relating to ASEAN. The ASEAN secretary general also exchanged views on Vietnam-ASEAN relations with representatives of various ministries, branches and research institutes. Mr. Datuk Ajit Singh and his wife left here yesterday afternoon to visit Hue, Da Lat and Ho Chi Minh City.
