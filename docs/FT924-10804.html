921029 FT 29 OCT 92 / International Company News: Kodak in black as sales rise 5% EASTMAN KODAK, the world's biggest producer of photographic equipment, turned in third-quarter net earnings of Dollars 189m, or 58 cents a share, against a deficit of Dollars 118m, or 37 cents, a year ago. Excluding one-time charges in both years, Kodak said underlying net earnings rose 4 per cent to Dollars 330m, or Dollars 1.01, from Dollars 317m, or 97 cents. Sales in the quarter were up 5 per cent to Dollars 5.18bn from Dollars 4.93bn. Kodak warned this month that it would take a Dollars 140m, or 43 cents, charge in the third quarter, when analysts expected a moderate charge of Dollars 45m, or 14 cents.
