BFN [Text] Bavarian Interior Minister Beckstein announced in Bonn today that the Free State will continue to deport violent Kurdish criminals. Expulsion orders will also be issued. After the riots and the Augsburg superhighway blockade, Bavaria expects to issue a total of 20 deportation orders, eight of which have already been issued, he said. The interior minister stressed that, in his opinion, there is no general obstacle to deportation to Turkey. Therefore, he intends to enforce such measures.
