Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [From the "National Hookup" program] [Text] Chairman Qiao Shi met with Yi Man-sop, speaker of the National Assembly of the ROK, this afternoon in Beijing. Yi Man-sop's visit, the first to China by an ROK parliament speaker, is of great significance in enhancing friendly exchanges between the two countries' parliaments. Vice Chairman Tian Jiyun was present at the meeting.
