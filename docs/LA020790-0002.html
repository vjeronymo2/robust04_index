February 7, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

The advertising agency J. Walter Thompson has created two posts that broaden the responsibilities of two top executives in New York. James B. Patterson, 42, who is chief executive of JWT/USA, has been named chairman of JWT North America, which also includes the agency's operations in Canada. Ronald S. Burns, 45, who is chairman of JWT Canada, has been named chief executive of JWT North America.
