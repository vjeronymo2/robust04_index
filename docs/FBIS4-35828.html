BFN [Text] On the death anniversary of His Eminence Imam Khomeyni, may his soul be sanctified, last night the tomb of the great leader was the scene of a large number of people who had gathered there to mourn him and to pay homage to him. Speaking to the mourners, Majles Speaker Nateq-Nuri pointed out that five years after the death of the founder of the Islamic Republic, Imam Khomeyni's line and path are continuing strongly, he said that the secret behind the success of the Islamic revolution is the bond between the ummah and the supreme leader. He added: With all the knowledge that it has of the Imam, the Islamic Republic can still not fully understand the various aspects of his character. The Majles speaker said: It is a source of pride for the rule of the supreme leader and for the Islamic Republic to have victoriously stood up for eight years in front of the entire world of arrogance to defend its religion and homeland. Later in his remarks, Nateq-Nuri stressed that our government and nation would defend the country's territorial integrity with its life and said: We believe that regional security is beneficial to all the regional countries and that irresponsible remarks and satanic overtures by some regional countries would not benefit peace and security in the region. The head of the legislative branch referred to the stances of the esteemed leader in support of the Muslims and the oppressed of the world and said: As far as Palestine is concerned, the only alternative is that the Zionists leave the occupied territories and that the Palestinians' sovereignty be established over their land.
