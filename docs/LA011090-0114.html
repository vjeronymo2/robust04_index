January 10, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SUCH A DEAL: When Disneyland signed up former President Ronald Reagan for its 35th anniversary celebration Thursday (A1), it received Reagan's bargain rate. . . . He charged a Japanese media conglomerate $2 million for appearances last October, but his trip to the Magic Kingdom is costing the park only $50,000 in the way of a donation to the Pediatric AIDS Foundation. . . . Reagan was invited, along with actor Robert Cummings and TV personality Art Linkletter, because the three men hosted the park's opening-day telecast on July 17, 1955. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
