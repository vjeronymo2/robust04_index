Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] During a 25 March meeting with young pilots held at the Antiaircraft Defense and Revolutionary Air Force [DAAFAR] Museum in Havana, Division General Enrique Carreras highlighted the high level of combat readiness of the new generations of pilots. [Begin Carreras recording] At the Bay of Pigs, that small air force made a significant contribution to victory. I believe that the DAAFAR in Angola proved that those young people have demonstrated in the field, in reality, during operations and all the difficult moments we faced there, the great competency of our DAAFAR forces. [end recording] Other issues discussed at the meeting were the efforts made by our Air Force during the search for Commander Camilo Cienfuegos, as well as Bay of Pigs operations and the 5 September 1957 events. The meeting was held to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the graduation from pilot school of Division General Enrique Carreras, Hero of the Republic of Cuba.
