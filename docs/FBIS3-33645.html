Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres and PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat will meet in Cairo on Sunday [6 February]. After a further round of talks in Switzerland last night, Peres said he was optimistic that the differences between the two sides can be bridged before next week's meeting, where it is hoped an agreement on implementing the Gaza-Jericho First plan can be signed. Mark Weiss reports: [Begin recording] [Weiss] [passage omitted] Addressing the economic forum, Foreign Minister Peres had this message for Yasir 'Arafat: [Peres] May I tell Chairman 'Arafat, who himself personalizes the great suffering of the Palestinian people, that we on our side, on the Israeli side, are decided to enable as much as it depends on us to make the Palestinian story a story of success. [Weiss] Peres praised 'Arafat's determination to reach agreement and said that although both sides had compromised, Israel had made no concessions on security issues. [passage omitted] [end recording]
