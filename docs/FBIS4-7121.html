BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 23 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received a letter of thanks from Eason Jordan, senior vice president of CNN, who had visited the DPRK together with former heads of state and government and politicians of different countries. Expressing thanks to President Kim Il-song for welcoming CNN to the DPRK, the letter dated April 19 says: Our visit was a memorable one, with the high point our meeting with you on April 16th. At the beginning of our trip, I wrote to you to reaffirm CNN's commitment to fair, balanced and objective coverage of the DPRK. After a week of reporting here, including a live broadcast from DPRK TV, I hope you will agree that we have maintained those high standards. In addition, I hope our reports have made some contribution to greater international understanding of your country and to the cause of world peace. With this visit, I hope we have laid the groundwork for a long-term, positive and mutually beneficial relationship qbetween CNN and the DPRK. We stand ready to return to Pyongyang at any time when warranted by important news developments, and we hope your government will continue to give positive consideration to our requests to travel here. Again, many thanks for all you have done to help us.
