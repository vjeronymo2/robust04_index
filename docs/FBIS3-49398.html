Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, January 6 (ATA) -- The spokesman of the Ministry of Public Order informed ATA on January 5 that in the region of D-14/18 border sign, only 5.5 metres in the Yugoslav territory, at about 1030 [0930 GMT], the Albanian citizen Ramadan Ibrahim Mustafa, 25 years, from the village Kosharisht of Kukes was killed. Regarding this incident, the local mixed commission for resolution of border incidents gathered immediately. From verification in the spot it came out that the victim had passed the border while scything, immediately he was hit by Serbian patrol at a very small distance of 15 metres. The commission also found in the spot nine shells of a machine gun of the type 1-K, 1976. The victim has been hit from behind with five bullets starting with the hips up to the shoulders. The victim has been carried along for 6-7 meters, parallel with the border line. His coat and cardigan were put off his body. The spokesman reports of another border incident on January 2, in the vicinity of the border, 30 meters in the Albanian territory, in the border sign D/6 in the zone of Tropoje, at about 1530. Regarding this second incident, the spokesman reports that the Serbian border patrol shot on the Albanian national Hamit Ram Sallaku, 20 years, while he was with his sheep. The wounded has been sent immediately in the Tropoje hospital where he is being treated. Since the beginning of 1993, the number of Albanian victims in the Yugoslav-Albanian border amounts to 21.
