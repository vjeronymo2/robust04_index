Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Events" newscast] [Text] Yesterday, Military Prosecutor Lester Gallo ordered the arrest of two of Army Commander General Humberto Ortega Saavedra's bodyguards in connection with the investigation into the death of a young man by the name of Jean Paul Genie. Captain Roberto Danilo Chacon Rivas and First Lieutenant Alejandro Cesar Lopez Rodriguez must be temporarily detained for 10 days, while the trial is being held. Yesterday, when these two men appeared at the Court of the Judge Advocate to make a statement, the prosecutor ordered their arrest. Another four of General Ortega's bodyguards are to testify today and tomorrow. Captain Chacon Rivas and First Lieutenant Lopez Rodriguez were sent to the Disciplinary Unit of the Sandinist People's Army on the road to Masaya. The bodyguards' defense attorneys have criticized the arrest order.
