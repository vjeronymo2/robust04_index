POWER OF THE PRESS

Thank you for your continued efforts to put readers in touch with the many small inns and lodges in Southern California. You recently published a letter, "Small and Beautiful" (Nov. 13), from David Greenfield, one of our satisfied guests who was pleased with his accommodations at the San Lorenzo, 777 San Lorenzo Road, Palm Springs 92264, (619) 325-5166.
