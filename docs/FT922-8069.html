920514 FT 14 MAY 92 / Lloyd's faces satellite claim UNDERWRITERS AT Lloyd's of London stand to pay claims of around dollars 20m (pounds 11.2m) if attempts to resuce the Intelsat VI satellite are unsuccessful. Astronauts from the space shuttle Endeavour were scheduled to make a final effort to rescue it last night. Lloyd's underwriters insured the rescue mission on behalf of a number of Intelsat's 122 partners. The policy, 45 per cent if which is placed in Lloyd's, covers dollars 44m of the dollars 147m cost of the rescue mission.
