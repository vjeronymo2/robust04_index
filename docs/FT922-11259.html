920427 FT 27 APR 92 / UK Company News: Clayton pays less Clayton Son & Co (Holdings), the engineering group, finished 1991 with a pre-tax loss of Pounds 491,000, after Pounds 314,000 at the half way stage, and is cutting the final dividend to 3p. The interim was omitted. In the previous year the interim was 2.5p and the final 9.3p, from pre-tax profits of Pounds 543,000. Turnover in 1991 improved from Pounds 17.4m to Pounds 19.7m but generated a loss of Pounds 202,000 (profit Pounds 642,000). Net interest payable rose to Pounds 297,000 (Pounds 42,000). Losses per share were 8.89p (earnings 13.8p).
