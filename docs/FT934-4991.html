931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / Eastern's dividend rises 20% Eastern Electricity jolted the market with a 20 per cent dividend increase to 6.6p, auguring well for the regional electricity company results season. Eastern's share price gained 8p to close at 596p. Rigorous cost cutting enabled the company to raise interim profits by 14.5 per cent to Pounds 77.4m despite sluggish economic activity in its area which includes north-east London and East Anglia. Earnings per share rose from 18.4p to 20.9p.
