BFN [Text] Sarajevo, 17 Apr (TANJUG) -- Srpska republic president Radovan Karadzic said in Pale this evening that an agreement has been reached on an immediate cease-fire, "certain separation of the forces," and a maximum of restraint from military operations in the Gorazde area. After his talks with Yasushi Akashi, the UN secretary general's special envoy, Karadzic said that if the cease-fire is observed, it could be said that "we are coming closer to peace in Gorazde."
