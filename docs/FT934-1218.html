931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / Ukraine begins dismantling its SS-24 nuclear missiles Ukraine announced yesterday that 17 of its 46 modern SS-24 strategic nuclear weapons had been deactivated, Jill Barshay in Kiev and agencies report. Mr Valery Shmarov, deputy prime minister, said the warheads had been moved away from the launchers. 'We are prepared to remove all SS-24s from military alert, but only if conditions discussed at these talks (with Russia and the US) are fulfilled.' Ukraine has set financial compensation, dismantlement aid and security guarantees as conditions for giving up its nuclear arsenal of more than 1,600 warheads.
