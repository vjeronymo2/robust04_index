941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / Schools' League Tables: Terminology A-LEVEL: Advanced Level General Certificate of Education, the main examination for university entrance in England and Wales. UCAS POINTS: Based on ratings of A-levels by the Universities and Colleges Admissions Service, awarding 10 points for an A grade at A level, 8 for a B, and so on. GCSE: General Certificate of Secondary Education, taken by 16-year-olds in England and Wales. A grade C is equivalent to an old O-level pass.
