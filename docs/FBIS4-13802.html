BFN [Text] Cairo, 24 Apr (INA)--Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat has sent messages to the heads of the Security Council permanent member states, the chairman of the Nonaligned Movement, and the secretary general of the Arab League in which he complained about the Zionist entity and the terrorist methods it employs against the Palestinian people and the PLO. A Palestinian official told an INA correspondent in Cairo that in his written message, 'Arafat said the Zionist entity maneuvers to swallow the Palestinians and to politically liquidate them, and that the PLO has offered the Zionist entity all it asked for.
