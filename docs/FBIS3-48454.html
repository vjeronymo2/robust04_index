Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 24 (KCNA) -- The New Year address of the great leader President Kim Il-song was carried by the Indian papers SAVERA and PROLETARIAN ERA, Yemeni papers AL-RAYY AL-'AM and AL-MUSTAQBAL, the Tunisian paper ANNONCES, the Egyptian paper MIDDLE EAST OBSERVER, the Senegalese paper LE TEMOIN, the Nepalese paper JAN SANGARUSHA, the Finnish paper TYOKANSAN SANOMAT, the Ghanaian paper GHANAIAN TIMES, the Syrian paper NIDAL SHAAM, the Burkinabe paper SIDWAYA and the Palestinian magazine PALESTINE AL-SHURA. Seminars on the New Year address were also held by the society for the study of the chuche idea of Guinean youth and students and at the Harare, Zimbabwe, Centre for the Study of the Chuche Philosophy. The reporters and speakers said that the New Year address, a highly important work indicating the way for the DPRK to achieve greater victory and success, is of universal significance to all the progressive countries. The idea of establishing new international political and economic orders and developing South-South cooperation set forth in the New Year address is a fighting programme to which developing countries should hold fast. A lecture on the New Year address was given by the Lugazi branch of the Uganda-DPRK Friendship Association and reading sessions on it were held by the Maputo city committee of the Momzambique Liberation Front Party and the West African Examinations Council branch of the Ghanaian National Institute of the Chuche Idea.
