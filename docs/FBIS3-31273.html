Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Rome, Feb 16 (AFP) -- A U.S. military fighter plane from NATO's base in Aviano, northern Italy, crashed in Slovenia Wednesday though the pilot managed to eject from the craft and escaped unhurt, informed sources here said. The F-16 crashed while the pilot was attempting to make an emergency landing in the former Yugoslav republic bordering Italy though it was not immediately known why. Planes at the North Atlantic Treaty Organization base in Aviano have been patrolling Bosnian skies for months and are among those that would be used should NATO go ahead with its threat to launch air strikes on Bosnian Serb forces besieging Sarajevo.
