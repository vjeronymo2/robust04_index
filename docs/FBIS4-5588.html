BFN [Text] Cheju, April 12 (YONHAP) -- The maritime police on Tuesday rescued eight crew members from a stranded Chinese fishing boat off the southwestern part of Cheju island, the Cheju Maritime Police said. The maritime police said lifeboats were sent to waters off Mosulpo, Namcheju-kun, Cheju-do, around 8 A.M. to rescue Chinese fishermen from the vessel "Luyu 107," which ran aground earlier in the morning, the police said. According to the police, the 80-ton fishing boat registered with Weihai City, Shandong Province, China, was swept away by high waves earlier in the morning while seeking a safe harbor in bad weather. Three of the 11 fishermen on the Luyu 107 had already swum to the port of Mosulpo, the police said.
