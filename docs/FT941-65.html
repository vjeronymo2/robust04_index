940331 FT 31 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Moorfield Estates Pounds 24m buy Moorfield Estates is acquiring a property portfolio for Pounds 24m, financed partly by a net Pounds 12.2m placing and open offer, writes Vanessa Houlder. The company also announ-ced a reduction in 1993 pre-tax losses from Pounds 1.98m to Pounds 494,000. Net asset value increased to 44p per share at the period end, compared with 39p last September. The company is buying six retail, industrial and office properties, with a current rental income of Pounds 1.93m, from Mobax. The deal will leave Moorfield Estates with gross property assets of Pounds 62m and reduced gearing of 133 per cent. Beeson Gregory, the company's brokers, are placing 33.62m new shares at 38p, with shareholders being able to take part in the open offer on a 1-for-1 basis. The shares were unchanged at 43p. The balance of the acquisition price will be financed by a new loan facility and by existing resources. The company plans to seek a full listing in the near future. Losses per share fell from 18.3p to 2.9p. No dividend has been recommended for 1993, but a 1p payment is forecast for 1994.
