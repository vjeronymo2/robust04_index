Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Amman--QNA--An informed source in Amman has confirmed that that His Majesty King Husayn will visit fraternal Qatar on 20 March. The source told a Qatari news agency correspondent that his majesty will discuss with His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of Qatar, ways of bolstering fraternal ties within the single Arab family and promoting Arab solidarity. They will also discuss Arab, regional, and international issues. This visit signifies the continuing development of fraternal ties between the two countries.
