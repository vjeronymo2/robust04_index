December 14, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Broadcast News: The BBC will teach Polish broadcasters Western radio and television techniques as part of Britain's $80-million aid package, it was announced Wednesday. Thirty-six broadcasters, chosen by Polish Radio and Television, will begin six-week training programs starting in February. The fund was authorized by Parliament in June after Polish leader Gen. Wojciech Jaruzelski's visit to Britain. CLAUDIA PUIG
