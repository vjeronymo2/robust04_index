Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Mexico City, 1 Jan (EFE) -- Rebels from the self-styled Zapata Liberation Army [Ejercito Zapatista de Liberacion -- EZL] have taken over the mayoral offices in the towns of Ocosingo, San Cristobal de las Casas, and Las Margaritas, where the rebels have set fires and destroyed the files of the Attorney General's Offices in these three towns. According to a Mexican television station, the rebel group issued a communique this morning. In the communique, the group proclaims itself a guerrilla movement and declares war on the Mexican Army, which it challenges to fight in the mountainous area of the southern state of Chiapas. So far, there are no reports of any casualties, but the rebels have closed off the entries and exits of those three towns.
