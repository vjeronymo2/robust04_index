BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow, 9 Jun -- The rules of using Russia's air space have been violated 109 times in the past year. This was done 22 times by aircraft of the former USSR states, and 27 times by planes belonging to other foreign states. The air defense troops have been forced to take decisive measures with regard to transgressors. Forty five aircraft were forced to land en route or to return to their place of departure. These figures were cited in an appeal by the command of the air defense troops to all ministries and departments, as well as to the management of Russian commercial and foreign airlines. The document, which ITAR-TASS received today from the press service of the air defense forces main command, requests that the rules for the use of Russian Federation air space be strictly observed, and stresses that only concerted action can prevent emergency situations, including those involving human losses.
