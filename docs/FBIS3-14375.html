Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 27 Mar (MENA)--In a statement released today, the Arab League Council welcomed UN Security Council Resolution 904 providing protection for the Palestinians. It called on the international community to pressure Israel to immediately implement the clauses of the resolution and provide international protection for the Palestinian people in the occupied Arab territories, including Jerusalem. The Arab League Council called on the two cosponsors of the peace conference to shoulder their responsibilities to guarantee the safety of the Palestinian people and their property in the occupied territories and to prevent recurrence of the criminal acts committed by Jewish settlers and conspirators. The Arab League Council's 101st session hailed the Palestinian people's steadfastness under the occupation and their insistence on obtaining their freedom and continuing their steadfastness until the Israeli occupation forces withdraw from all the occupied Arab and Palestinian territories, including Jerusalem. The statement noted that the Arab League Council heard numerous statements and speeches regarding the ugly crime committed in the al-Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron by Israeli settlers in suspicious collusion with Israeli Army units.
