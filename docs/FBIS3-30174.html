Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Paul Keating has expressed his sympathy for victims of the massacre of Palestinians in the West Bank town of Hebron. More than 50 people were killed in the attack by a Jewish gunman. In his response, Mr. Keating emphasized the need for peace in the region. He said he was appalled by what had happened and could only hope that it did not derail the reconciliation in the Middle East. Earlier, the foreign minister, Gareth Evans, urged both Israel and the Palestine Liberation Organization to intensify their efforts for peace.
