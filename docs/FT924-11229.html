921027 FT 27 OCT 92 / International Company News: Anglo-French packagers plan US purchase CARNAUD METALBOX, the Anglo-French packaging group, has announced a preliminary accord to purchase Anchor Hocking Packaging of the US from Newell Corp, the consumer hardware company, for an undisclosed price, AP-DJ reports from Paris. Anchor Hocking, which specialises in metal capsules for bottles, is expected to post 1992 revenue of Dollars 160m, representing 17 per cent of the US capsules market. Carnaud Metalbox said the purchase, subject to approval by US authorities, would lift the group's metal capsule turnover to FFr2bn (418.8m). The company produces capsules at five sites in Europe, but it has not entered the US market. Carnaud Metalbox has an annual revenue of FFr25bn.
