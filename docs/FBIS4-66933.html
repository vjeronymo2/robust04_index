CSO FBIS has available to U.S. Government readers of the FBIS Arms Control Report a dictionary of Chemical Weapons Convention terminology. The original of this document was published by the United Nations in October 1991. The terms are in English alphabetical order and are given for the following languages in addition to English: French, Spanish, Russian, Japanese and Arabic. To order a copy of this unique dictionary please call Beth, the Arms Control Report editor, on (703) 733-6468.
