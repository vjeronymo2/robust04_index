Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] Igor Kasatonov, chief of staff of the Russian Navy, and Vladimir Yegorov, commander of the Baltic Fleet, arrived in Liepaja today. They are accompanied by several high-ranking officers of the Russian Navy. The delegation of the Russian Navy Headquarters is visiting Liepaja to discuss issues concerning the Russian Navy withdrawal from Latvia by June this year. Gaidis Zeibots, commander of the Latvian naval forces, will meet the heads of the Russian Navy later today.
