December 27, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

STATE;

3 HURT IN GORMAN PLANE CRASH

Three people aboard a five-passenger plane received moderate injuries today when their single-engine Beechcraft crashed east of Interstate 5 in the Gorman area.

But two other passengers walked away from the crash site without any injuries. County fire emergency equipment raced to the scene about five miles east of Interstate 5 and west of California 138 in the northwestern area of Los Angeles County.

It was not immediately known what caused the crash.
