Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa has said that Egypt is holding consultations with the parties concerned with the Middle East peace process as well as with UN Security Council members. The subject of the consultations is the holding of a Security Council session to discuss the attack against worshipers at the al-Ibrahimi Mosque and the passing of a resolution condemning the massacre. Palestinian Ambassador to Cairo Sa'id Kamal said that Minister 'Amr Musa today gave him an urgent message for Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat concerning the consultations Egypt is holding on a UN Security Council meeting to denounce the massacre. The Palestinian envoy said that the Palestinians are unwavering in their call for the condemnation of the atrocity committed by the settlers against the Palestinian people and for international protection of the Palestinian people living in the occupied territories. Ambassador Kamal said that Palestinian and Egyptian consultations are continuing to achieve this end.
