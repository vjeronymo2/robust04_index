BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 27 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Tomorrow arriving on an official two-day visit at the invitation of the Mongolian premier is the premier of the State Council of the People's Republic of China Li Peng. In the course of his visit it is expected to sign a number of official documents including the Treaty on Friendly Relations and Cooperation between Mongolia and PRC. The forthcoming official visit of the premier of the State Council of the PRC is the first one after the 1960 visit to Mongolia by the head of the government of China. The mass media means of Mongolia are giving much prominence to the forthcoming visit of the honoured Chinese guest. The newspaper "DZASGIYN GADZRYN MEUEE" published in its latest issue the detailed biography of Li Peng, Chinese premier of the State Council.
