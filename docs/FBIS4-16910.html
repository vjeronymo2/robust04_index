BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow April 19 TASS -- The international communist [as received] must do its best to achieve a political settlement of the Bosnian crisis, says a statement of Russian President Boris Yeltsin circulated here today by his press service. "The conflict in Bosnia and Herzegovina is on the verge of dangerous escalation despite active diplomatic efforts of Russia taken together with the United Nations and other members of the international community," says the document. "The Bosnian Serb leadership must fulfill its promises given to Russia, stop the attacks and withdraw from Gorazde announced by the United Nations a `safety zone', enable the introduction of U.N. forces in the area and unblock the U.N. personnel in Bosnia and Herzegovina." The Russian president "confirmed the proposal to hold a summit meeting with participation of Russia, the United States and the European Union and in the presence of U.N. representatives". "No doubt, such a meeting must be thoroughly prepared. Corresponding instructions have been given to the Russian Foreign Ministry," says the statement.
