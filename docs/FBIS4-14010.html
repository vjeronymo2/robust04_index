BFN [Text] Cairo, 25 Apr (MENA) -- The Foreign Ministry has received a proposal by the Norwegian Government to hold a high-level meeting in Cairo in the first week of May involving donors of economic aid to help establish Palestinian authority in Gaza and Jericho. The proposal covers all the issues pertaining to assistance for the Palestinian police and addressing all its needs once it takes over control in Gaza and Jericho in accordance with the Palestinian-Israeli Declaration of Principles. Donor states met in Cairo on 24 March and discussed ways to assist the Palestinian police deployment in Gaza and Jericho.
