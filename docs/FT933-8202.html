930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Foreign & Colonial Special Utilities The recent share placing and offer by Foreign & Colonial Special Utilities Investment Trust was 2.81 times oversubscribed. Applications worth an aggregate of Pounds 35.1m were received for shares worth Pounds 12.5m. Following the oversubscription, the maximum number of income shares and capital shares, 50m in each case, will be issued, representing gross proceeds of Pounds 50m. Applications were received for 28.8m income shares, 3.95m capital shares and 16.2m package units, with commitments for 37.5m income shares and 37.5m capital shares.
