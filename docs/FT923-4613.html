920905 FT 05 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Ensor tumbles Pounds 1.38m into the red Ensor Holdings, which is engaged in the building and motor vehicle industries, ran up a pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.38m in the year ended March 31 1992 against a profit of Pounds 327,000. Also there was an extraordinary charge of Pounds 1.65m for writing down assets and other losses consequent on the closure of the aggregate manufacturing business. Turnover fell from Pounds 52.7m to Pounds 46.6m. Losses per share were 9.14p (earnings 2.3p). Profits in vehicle dealership were Pounds 538,000 (Pounds 925,000), in distribution Pounds 2,000 (Pounds 218,000) and in metal products manufacture Pounds 154,000 (Pounds 229,000). The blocks and bricks side incurred a loss of Pounds 1.11m (Pounds 107,000).
