Language: French Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Russia: Zhirinovskiy Persona Non Grata in France"] [Text] European Minister Alain Lamassoure said yesterday that France "cannot tolerate" the statements made by Russian ultranationalist leader Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, indicating that he could not, in this context, enter French territory, contrary to his expectations. According to the minister, "the statements made on Bulgaria, the Romanian people, the Baltic states, Finland, Turkey, and other countries are unaccceptable." Zhirinovskiy warned Germany -- which denied him an entry visa -- that he would launch a "Third World War" if he ever came to power in Moscow, according to the weekly WELT AM SONNTAG. Zhirinovskiy announced that he would send "300,000 Russian troops" to Germany if he became head of government.
