December 3, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

LOSER IN POLAND URGES BACKERS TO VOTE FOR WALESA

Prime Minister Tadeusz Mazowiecki on Sunday blamed Lech Walesa for his painful defeat in Poland's presidential elections but called on his supporters to vote for the Solidarity leader in a runoff next Sunday.

He also asked his campaign officials to organize a new party called Democratic Union to contest the elections. Mazowiecki finished behind Walesa and outsider Stanislaw Tyminski in last week's vote.
