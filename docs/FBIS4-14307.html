BFN [Text] Political sources in Sanaa said today that fierce battles have resumed for the second consecutive day between Yemeni Army units positioned north of Sanaa. This fighting follows the collapse of the cease-fire declared last night. According to the sources, heavy artillery and tanks are being used in the fighting between units loyal to President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and units who support Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid. Well-informed sources in Aden confirm the news of the fighting, saying that the southern forces, stationed north of Sanaa, are in control of the situation. These forces seized 30 tanks belonging to the northern forces and destroyed 15 others, according to the same sources.
