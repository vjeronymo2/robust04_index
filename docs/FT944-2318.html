941216 FT 16 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Feedback in red Reduced turnover and the cost of a number of 'policy initiatives' resulted in a sharp lapse into the red at Feedback, the USM-traded electronic and electrical equipment group. Turnover for the six months to September 30 amounted to Pounds 4.18m, down from Pounds 4.47m; losses totalled Pounds 374,400 against pre-tax profits last time of Pounds 267,900. Losses per share were 3.87p (earnings of 2.13p) but the interim dividend is maintained at 0.5p.
