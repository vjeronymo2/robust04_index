931223 FT 23 DEC 93 / Staff complete Pounds 3m bus buy-out Managers and employees of Southampton City Transport have completed a Pounds 3m buy-out of the company from Southampton City Council. The company, which trades as Southampton Citybus, expects to make a pre-tax profit of Pounds 300,000 on revenues of Pounds 10m this year. More than 330 of its 420 staff put up Pounds 1,200 each to help fund the buy-out.
