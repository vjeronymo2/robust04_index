921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Electric & General asset value ahead Net asset value per share of Electric and General Investment Company stood at 161.2p at November 30 against 139.1p a year earlier and 152.6p at the May 31 year end. Net revenue for the half year amounted to Pounds 1.71m (Pounds 1.55m) for earnings ahead to 1.89p (1.71p) per share. The interim dividend is being held at 1.5p.
