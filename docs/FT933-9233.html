930811 FT 11 AUG 93 / Azerbaijan to drop rouble Azerbaijan will abandon the rouble completely on September 1 when its own manat currency becomes sole legal tender in the republic, Itar-Tass news agency said yesterday, Reuter reports from Moscow. The full exit from the rouble zone was ordered in a decree by Mr Suret Guseinov, the prime minister. The manat has circulated alongside the rouble since its introduction last year. In Minsk, Belarus officials are reported to be studying a proposal from the central bank that would lead to the creation of a national currency.
