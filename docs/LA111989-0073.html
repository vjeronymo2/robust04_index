November 19, 1989, Sunday, Bulldog Edition

VIETNAM REFUGEE IN OHIO HITS LOTTERY FOR $6 MILLION

A 23-year-old Vietnamese refugee won $6 million in an Ohio Lottery game last week, six years after he fled his homeland with only the clothes on his back.

Anhtinh Ton Giang will receive $300,000 a year, before taxes, for 20 years, and will quit his $5.75-an-hour job.
