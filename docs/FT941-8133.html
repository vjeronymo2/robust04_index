940222 FT 22 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Wills ahead Wills Group, the distributor of fluid handling equipment, reported doubled pre-tax profits of Pounds 575,000 for the six months to January 1, against Pounds 287,000. The result was achieved on turnover up 44 per cent from Pounds 8m to Pounds 11.5m. Wills also announced a further acquisition with the purchase of Cellar Services Technology, a Manchester-based supplier to the brewing industry, for Pounds 60,000. In the year to May 31 it made pre-tax profits of Pounds 64,000 on turnover of Pounds 1.5m. The figures were the first to include a full contribution from Platon, acquired in April last year. At the beginning of November, SRL, based in Lyon, was acquired helping the French activities to show a good performance. In Italy buoyant results in some areas balanced downturns inothers. Trading in Germany and the Netherlands was poor, while in the UK there has been some sign of improving market conditions but this has yet to show through in significant increases in demand. Earnings per share improved to 0.36p (0.28p) basic. or 0.35p (0.24p) fully diluted. The interim dividend is raised from 0.1p to 0.115p.
