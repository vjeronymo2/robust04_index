48 CFR Part 13 [FAR Case 90&hyph;32] Federal Acquisition Regulation; U.S. Government Credit Cards AGENCIES: Department of Defense (DOD), General Services Administration (GSA), and National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA). ACTION: Proposed rule; withdrawal.

SUMMARY: The Department of Defense, General Services Administration, and National Aeronautics and Space Administration have decided to withdraw a proposed rule, FAR case 90&hyph;32, U.S. Government Credit Cards, in light of the pending legislation on streamlining of the procurement process. This rule was published in the Federal Register on June 27, 1990 (55 FR 26342).
