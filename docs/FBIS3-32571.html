Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Up-to-Date" newscast] [Text] A search has been under way since last night for a Salvadoran Air Force A-37 Cessna airplane with two soldiers on board which, according to official sources, crashed into the Pacific Ocean. The cause of the crash is not known yet. The Armed Forces' Center for Communications and Protocol [Centro de Comunicaciones y Protocolo del Ejercito] disclosed today the pilots on board are Captain Julio Cesar Godoy and Technician Juan Carlos Garcia. The airplane crashed into the ocean near Amatecampo Beach, La Paz Department. According to the official note, Navy and Air Force units have been searching for the airplane ever since the accident was reported. The pilots were conducting night training when the accident occurred.
