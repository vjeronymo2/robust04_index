December 20, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

ROMANIANS: In 1982, Daniel Branzai lost his Romanian citizenship over alleged anti-government activities. . . . Today, he's the pastor of the 200-member Romanian Baptist Church in Anaheim, site of a prayer service this Friday night for those killed in last weekend's Romanian protests (A1). "We want change, but without shedding of blood," says Branzai, who recently re-entered his homeland for the first time since 1982. . . . Orange County is home to 15,000 Romanian emigrants, Branzai says. Dana Parsons and Mark I. Pinsky
