Language: French Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] [Announcer] President Jose Eduardo dos Santos of Angola is in France. He met Prime Minister Edouard Balladur, Cooperation Minister Michel Roussin, and Interior Minister Charles Pasqua yesterday. Today, the Angolan head of state will have lunch with Francois Mitterrand. [passage omitted] [Correspondent Monique Mas] France will take part in the reconstruction of Angola, but with the help of all Angolans, including those who do not have any power. They will have to play a role in the country's political future, French Cooperation Minister Michel Roussin said. He added that before relaunching its cooperation [programs], the international community should make sure that any agreement between the government and UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] be a stable one. [passage omitted] The French interior minister wanted to clarify the situation about UNITA's friendship with France. It [the friendship] has been apparently replaced by state-to-state relations. However, the military embargo remains. [passage omitted]
