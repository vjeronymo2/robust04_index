BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 13/5/1994 -- During the two months of the Parliament's activity, 99 important documents were adopted, stated Dumitru Motpan, chairman of the Moldovan Agrarian Democratic Party (PDAM), at a press conference held today by the governing party. The PDAM leader pronounced himself in favor of conducting elections to the local administrative organs in the near future and excluding from the future Constitution the words "federalization" and "confederalization." Motpan promised that "no later than November the Constitution will be adopted." Referring to detainee Ilascu's [Ilie Ilascu, sentenced to death by a Dniester court] problem, he expressed the certainty that Ilascu will not be executed. Motpan believes that Tiraspol tries to increase the price for Ilascu's release. He said that he knows nothing about some alleged guarantees made by Tiraspol leaders that Ilascu's life is out of danger. He also pointed out that during the last meeting between Moldovan and Gagauz representatives, "Gagauzians sent some extremists who want more the more they receive."
