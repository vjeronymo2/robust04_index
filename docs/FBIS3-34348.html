Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 8 Feb (MENA)--Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres, who is visiting Cairo, has stated that negotiations with the Palestinian delegation will continue in Cairo today and tomorrow if necessary. Israeli Environment Minister Yosi Sarid said the Palestinian and Israeli negotiators have faced no obstacles requiring consultation with their superiors. Talks resumed this morning between the Palestinian delegation led by Ahmad Quray', alias Abu-'Ala', and the Israeli delegation led by Uri Savir, director general of the Israeli Foreign Ministry. The talks started yesterday evening and continued into the night. A meeting is expected later today among Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa, Shim'on Peres, and Yasir 'Arafat. Elsewhere, Shim'on Peres and Yosi Sarid visited Filastin Hospital in Cairo, where an international conference is being held on psychiatric treatment for patients in the occupied territories.
