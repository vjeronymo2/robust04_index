940606 FT 06 JUN 94 / Teacher training chief is named The headteacher of an academically successful independent school has been named chairman-designate of the quango which will oversee plans to overhaul teacher training. Mr Geoffrey Parker, high master of Manchester Grammar School, will head the Teacher Training Agency when it is established in September, subject to royal assent to the education bill. The bill proposes to transfer responsibility for training new teachers from higher education to schools.
