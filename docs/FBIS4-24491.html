BFN [By Flor Wang] [Text] Taipei, May 27 (CNA) -- Premier Lien Chan has decided to leave for Central America on Sunday [29 May], one day earlier than originally scheduled, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced Friday. Lien will make a stopover in Los Angeles on Monday, and in Miami on Tuesday on his way to El Salvador, where he is scheduled to attend the inauguration ceremony of newly elected President Armando Calderon Sol on Wednesday. He will proceed to Guatemala on Thursday. During his visit in Guatemala, Lien will discuss issues of mutual concern with Guatemalan President Ramiro de Leon Carpio, who will confer a medal of special order on Lien. Lien's entourage will include his wife Fang Yu, Vice Foreign Affairs Minister Fang Chin-yen and his wife, Economic Affairs Minister P. K. Chiang, Council of Agriculture Chairman Sun Ming- hsien and Government Information Office Director-General Jason Hu. Lien and his party will return on June 7.
