930302 FT 02 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Castle Mill director quits Mr Marcus Evans has resigned as joint managing director of Castle Mill International and sold his holding of 1.2m shares in the handbag and fashion wear manufacturer. The stake was sold to Mr Brian Rousell, who now holds 23.03 per cent of Castle Mill. Subject to certain conditions Mr Evans will repurchase Silver Collins Exhibitions, the exhibition promoter which is 75 per cent owned by Castle Mill, for Pounds 1.
