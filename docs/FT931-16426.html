930107 FT 07 JAN 93 / Swedish economy may improve Sweden's economic picture, though gloomy at present, would start to lighten in 1993, Premier Carl Bildt said yesterday, Reuter reports from Stockholm. His remarks came after the central bank said it was lowering its marginal lending rate to 10.5 from 11 per cent. Mr Bildt's coalition remained committed to joining the EC, he added.
