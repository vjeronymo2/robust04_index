Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] There has been an escalation of fighting between warring factions in the Afghan capital of Kabul. According to reports, at least 25 persons were killed. Of them, four were killed in cluster bomb attacks launched from aircraft on a popular market. More than 20 persons were also injured in the attack. Scores of rockets were fired on the capital of which one landed on a group of mourners at a funeral, killing nine of them. The fighting began on Friday evening and is reported to be the most intensive in more than two weeks.
