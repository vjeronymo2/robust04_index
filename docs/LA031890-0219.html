March 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

FRESH FAMILY FARE

First "Where Pigeons Go to Die" and then "A Family for Joe," two terrific family movies.

Are the networks finally wising up? I hope TJoe' turns into a series.

Mrs. Eula E. Alvarez, Santa Fe Springs

Actually, a sitcom based on "A Family for Joe" is in production. Look for the show under that title or "Regular Joe" on NBC sometime this spring.
