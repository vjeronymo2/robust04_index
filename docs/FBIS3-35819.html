Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Item from roundup of ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA, ITAR-TASS reports: "Central Bank Has Reserves to Control Ruble Exchange Rate"] [Text] "The Central Bank of Russia has sufficient foreign currency reserves to enable it, even in the current situation, to control the ruble's movements against the dollar," Aleksandr Khandruyev, deputy chairman of the Central Bank of Russia, has stated. The Central Bank of Russia has recently spent around $1 billion to stabilize the ruble. The bank created its foreign currency reserves largely thanks to its vigorous purchases of foreign currency last year. According to preliminary Central Bank of Russia estimates, the surge of inflation in January 1994 reached approximately 20-25 percent.
