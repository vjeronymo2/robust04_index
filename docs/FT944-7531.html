941124 FT 24 NOV 94 / People: Mercury Asset Management Vicky Sleddon, 27, who has been managing Kleinwort Benson's new Pounds 500m European Privatisation Trust, has been poached by Mercury Asset Management to help manage its Pounds 575m European Privatisation Trust. The two firms set up their new trusts within a few weeks of each other and to date Mercury has performed slightly better.
