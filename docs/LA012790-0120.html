January 27, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Lou Dias, an advertising executive for Compton College's sports department, has organized the Women's Sports Assn. Professional Basketball League, scheduled to begin play March 15.

The sites will be Compton, Long Beach, Cerritos, Santa Ana, Ventura, Los Angeles, Culver City, Pasadena, Montebello and San Bernardino.
