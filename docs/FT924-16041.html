921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Bostrom advances to Pounds 796,000 Bostrom, the vehicle seating and specialist engineering group, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 736,000 to Pounds 796,000 in the first half of 1992. Turnover fell to Pounds 17.1m (Pounds 18.9m). There had been no improvement in the economies of the UK or countries served by the group, Mr Martin Makey, the chairman, said. The interim dividend is being held at 2.5p, payable from earnings of 3.9p (3.8p).
