931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / UK Company News: APV APV is to sell its 82.5 per cent stake in Ortmann and Herbst, a German maker of bottle filling and washing equipment, for DM76.1m (Pounds 30.4m) net of repayment of DM7.4m of intra-group loans due to O&H. The buyer is Max Kettner Verpackungsmaschinenfabrik. APV bought its initial stake in O&H in 1989 for Pounds 4m as its first move into that sector of process equipment for the food and drink industry.
