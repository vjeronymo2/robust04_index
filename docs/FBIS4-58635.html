BFN [Text] At 1145 today, President Hafiz al-Asad received Lebanese President Ilyas al-Hirawi, Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri, Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri, and Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz. Attending the meeting were Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam, People's Assembly Speaker 'Abd-al-Qadir Qaddurah, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi, and Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar'. The talks dealt with a host of issues and regional and Arab developments, particularly the peace process, developments in its tracks, and current contacts in this regard. The Lebanese officials were briefed on the contents of the talks U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher held in Damascus, and on the Syrian position which adheres to a just and comprehensive peace in all tracks. The two sides stressed the importance of coordination and cooperation among the Arab parties to the peace process, and of their adherence to the objective set for this process. The talks also dealt with issues of interest to both Syria and Lebanon. A working lunch was also held. Lebanese President Ilyas al-Hirawi returned to Beirut today. Also returning with him were Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri, Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri, and Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz.
