920917 FT 17 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Eidos reduces loss to Pounds 87,641 Eidos, the USM-traded computer video editing equipment manufacturer, incurred a pre-tax deficit of Pounds 87,641 over the six months to June 30. The outcome, struck on turnover of Pounds 54,625, compared with losses of Pounds 169,034. Mr Michael Briggs, chairman, said that deliveries of the company's products only began towards the end of the period. Losses per share were cut to 3.98p (7.68p).
