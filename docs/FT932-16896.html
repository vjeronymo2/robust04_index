930403 FT 03 APR 93 / Delors calls for worker consultation MR Jacques Delors, president of the European Commission, yesterday called on EC social affairs ministers to adopt, next Tuesday, legislation to compel large companies to consult workers on important strategic decisions, writes Andrew Hill in Brussels. Meeting representatives of the European Trade Union Confederation in Brussels yesterday, he and Mr Padraig Flynn, social affairs commissioner, said it would be a short-term gesture that the workers of Europe had not been forgotten during the current economic crisis.
