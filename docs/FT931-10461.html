930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Scottish National net assets recover Net asset value of Scottish National Trust recovered to 48.8p per capital share at December 31. The figure at the trust's year-end in September was just 2.8p. Net revenue for the three months to end-December amounted to Pounds 2.29m (Pounds 3.05m). The first interim dividend is reduced from 1.85p to 1.55p, still uncovered by earnings of 1.55p (1.85p) per income share.
