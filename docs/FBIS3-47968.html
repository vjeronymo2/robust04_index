Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] The Office of the party Central Committee has just made public a communique indicating that to carry on the work of the party Central Committee's Sixth Plenum, the Communist Party of Vietnam [CPV] Central Committee convened a two-day session from 17-18 January 1994 to make the final preparations for the Mid-Term National Party Conference. The delegates discussed a number of issues relating to personnel organization and the draft statute and draft action program for submission to the Mid-Term National Party Conference. They elected addititional members of the Political Bureau. These include Comrade Le Kha Phieu, Comrade Nguyen Manh Cam, Comrade Do Quang Thang, and Comrade Nguyen Ha Phan. The delegates also considered the case involving Comrade Vu Ngoc Hai and decided to remove him from his position as member of the party Central Committe because of his serious mistakes. Comrade General Secretary Do Muoi concluded the session by evaluating the results achieved. He pointed out the great unanimity of mind of the party Central Committee regarding those issues to be submitted to the Mid-Term National Party Conference.
