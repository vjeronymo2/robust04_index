Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Viktor Chernomyrdin has said to INTERFAX that the main topic of his meeting with Boris Yeltsin today was the budget for the fourth quarter of this year. Chernomyrdin arrived in Orel to take part in a meeting of oblast leaders from Central Russia. He said that he and Yeltsin did not talk about the new cabinet. Speaking about the candidacy of Boris Fedorov for the post of finance minister, Viktor Chernomyrdin said that he had spoken to him about it on both Sunday [23 Janaury] and Monday. During these conversations he asked Fedorov to finally decide whether he was going to join the government or not.
