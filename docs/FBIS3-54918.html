Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, January 5 (TASS) -- The Russian Government dismissed Pyotr Chernomord from the post of deputy finance minister in connection "with a transfer to another job". Aleksandr Aleshin was dismissed from the post of the first deputy communication minister "at his request". The government decided to institute yet another post of first deputy social security minister and Lyudmila Bezlepkina was appointed to occupy it.
