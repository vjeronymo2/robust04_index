Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] An Italian officer, Lieutenant Giulio Ruzzi, 29, was killed today in Somalia. A soldier from the same Italian contingent, Franco Ratenni, 22, was seriously injured. According to information from the Defense Ministry, a column from the Italian contingent, UNOSOM [UN Operation in Somalia] 2, which was on its way from Bulo Burti to Balad carrying food, was attacked by a hail of bullets at 1700. Lieutenant Ruzzi died while being transported to the hospital in Giohar.
