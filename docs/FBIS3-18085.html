Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vienna, Feb 26 (AFP) -- Inspectors from the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) Saturday [26 February] received visas to visit North Korea to inspect seven nuclear sites, the agency said in a statement. A seven-member IAEA inspector team would leave Vienna Sunday, and arrive in Pyongyang Tuesday, following North Korea's acceptance that inspections could go ahead from March 1, the statement added.
