940407 FT 07 APR 94 / Bundesbank eases lending costs The Bundesbank yesterday continued its gradual easing of lending costs by shaving a further three basis points off the securities repurchase rate at which it supplies financial markets with short-term funds. The move down to 5.73 per cent was seen as a further signal that reductions in the internationally important 5.25 per cent discount lending rate could still be expected. But analysts were divided on the timing. In the recent past the German central bank has developed a knack for making cuts when markets least expect them.
