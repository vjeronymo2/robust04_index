Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Council of Ministers has studied the draft media law in light of the recommendations by the ministerial committee entrusted with the task of drafting the bill. The council also discussed the text of Legislative Decree No. 104 amending the law on publications. The Council asked the ministerial committee to redraft it. The Council of Ministers asked Minister of Information Michel Samahah to submit a statement to the cabinet at its next session, defining the circumstances in which the radio and television networks, except for Radio Lebanon and Tele-Liban, were banned from broadcasting direct and indirect political news, as stipulated in the cabinet decisions. The cabinet held its session at the B'abda Palace at 1800 today. The session was chaired by President Ilyas al-Hirawi and was attended by Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri. It ended at 2215.
