BFN [Text] After visiting and meeting southwestern zone officials in Herat, the UN peace delegation, headed by Mahmud Mestiri, left for Bamian Province this morning. During its stay in Herat the delegation met with esteemed General Mohammad Esmail Khan, the officials of the southwestern zone's provinces, and different strata of people of the zone in connection with the current situation in the country. The delegation was seen off by General Azimi, the southwestern zone spokesman. On the result of the visit of UN's peace delegation, esteemed Azimi told a BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY correspondent that the talks between the delegation and the southwestern zone officials were positive and raised hopes. For instance, Mr. Mahmud Mestiri, head of the delegation, in a news conference said had I been in place of the Afghans, I would have chosen Herat as the venue for the upcoming gathering of the Afghans. General Azimi also said the UN delegation will include the facts mentioned in the proposal by the southwestern provinces Peace Council in any decision it will be making.
