940604 FT 04 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Brown & Jackson Shareholders in Brown & Jackson, owner of the lossmaking Poundstretcher chain of discount stores, yesterday approved a rescue offer from Pepkor, the South African retailing group, which could involve a capital injection of up to Pounds 56.2m in return for a 63 per cent stake. Shareholders representing 98.4 per cent of the votes cast and 50 per cent of the issued share capital voted on a poll in favour of the resolution, which was unanimously backed by the board.
