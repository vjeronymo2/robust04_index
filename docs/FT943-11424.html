940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / Three jailed for fraudulent trading Three businessmen were yesterday jailed for fraudulent trading at Extra Special Vehicles, based at Banbury, Oxfordshire, which collapsed in 1990 with Pounds 6m debts. Mr Robert Colman, chairman, and Mr Moshe Hochenberg, finance director, were both jailed for three years. Mr Robert Knight, a financial adviser, was jailed for 4 1/2 years. They were convicted after a 12-week trial at Northampton Crown Court.
