930305 FT 05 MAR 93 / Observer: Overtaken United Biscuits might have timed things better with its glossy interim report, the cover sporting a map of Europe and the Americas fashioned from group products: crisps, sweets, chocolates, peanuts, raisins and all manner of biscuits. Of those representing the UK, the only two identifiably branded are both made by Terry's, sold to Kraft on Wednesday.
