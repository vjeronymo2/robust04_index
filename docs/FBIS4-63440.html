BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Dzyublo] [Text] Moscow May 21 TASS -- The situation in the Galskiy District where, according to the Georgian Defence Ministry, concentration of Abkhazian troops was registered on Friday, has not changed, Georgian Defence Minister Vardiko Nadibaidze said in an exclusive telephone interview with ITAR-TASS on Saturday. According to the minister, the situation "is closely monitored", and no additional massing of troops and combat materiel was registered. As the Georgian Defence Minstry reported earlier, the concentration of armed groups numbering over 500 and an increase in combat materiel were registered in the upper zone of the Galskiy District on Friday. However, apprehensions about "a planned large-scale operation" have not been confirmed so far.
