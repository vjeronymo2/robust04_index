940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Wescol Group Wescol Group, the USM-quoted steel products company, reported pre-tax profits up from Pounds 13,000 to Pounds 30,000 for the six months to January 31 helped by a Pounds 40,000 fall in interest charges to Pounds 164,000. Turnover for this Halifax-based company was static at Pounds 8.15m (Pounds 8.18m). Earnings per share were unchanged at 0.1p.
