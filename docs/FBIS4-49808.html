BFN [Text] Shanghai, May 11 (XINHUA) -- A professional auditors office regulations went into effect here yesterday. The sources said the regulations contain 24 articles describing the legal position, business range, rights and responsibilities of professional auditors offices, and the qualifications needed by practitioners. According to the regulations, auditors offices may undertake business from Sino-foreign joint ventures, private enterprises and individuals. Shanghai now has 54 such offices, employing some 2,000 professionals. They undertook 37,500 items of auditing and consultancy services last year, saving 236 million yuan in construction expenses for their customers, said the sources.
