March 29, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

TITANS SWEEP, VAN DORTRECHT WINS TWO

Cal State Fullerton's Ann Van Dortrecht pitched a five-hitter to win the first game, then won in relief in the second and the Titans swept a Big West Conference softball doubleheader from UC Santa Barbara Wednesday at Fullerton.

Fullerton won the first game, 3-0, when Van Dortrecht (17-4) gained her 10th shutout of the season.

She pitched the final three innings of the second game as Fullerton won, 1-0, in 17 innings.

Nikki Lewis and Diana Foreman had back-to-back doubles with one out in the 17th for fourth-ranked Fullerton (26-9, 6-3) in the second game.

Santa Barbara is 15-13, 3-4.

In a California Collegiate Athletic Assn. baseball game:

Chapman 10, Cal Poly San Luis Obispo 1 -- Mike Kelly had two hits, including a two-run home run in the seventh inning for Chapman at Hart Park.

Kelly also scored three runs and had three runs batted in for Chapman (18-12, 9-5).

Jason Rolish had three hits, two RBIs and Doug Yates hit a two-run home run and Dave Paveloff (6-4) pitched a three-hitter for Chapman.

In nonconference men's tennis:

UCLA 5, UC Irvine 1 -- Richard Lubner won, 3-6, 6-3, 6-3, for visiting UC Irvine's only point.
