941229 FT 29 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Stanelco cuts loss Stanelco, the USM-traded thermal processing equipment group, saw pre-tax losses narrow to Pounds 47,000 in the six months to August 31. The outcome, which compared with losses of Pounds 84,000 last time and Pounds 160,000 for the full year, came from turnover static at Pounds 631,000 and after exceptionals of Pounds 1,000 (Pounds 15,000). Directors said that the group's order book stood at Pounds 383,000 at the period end, up from Pounds 244,000 at the start of the year. It continues to 'search actively' for acquisitions. Losses per share were unchanged at 0.01p.
