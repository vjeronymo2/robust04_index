September 8, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEW ANCHORMAN WILL BEGIN WORK AT KCBS

Michael Tuck, a controversial anchorman and commentator from the ABC affiliate in San Diego, will finally begin work at KCBS Channel 2 on Monday. Tuck, who was signed by KCBS almost a year ago, will co-anchor the station's 6 p.m. newscast with Tritia Toyota and do regular reporting on the 11 p.m. broadcast.

Chris Conangla, who had been anchoring at 6 p.m., will move to the station's early morning newscast and continue his duties on the station's noon show.
