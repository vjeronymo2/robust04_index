July 11, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS: EVERSOLE APPOINTED SENIOR VP AT TOSHIBA

Dennis E. Eversole has been named senior vice president, finance and administration, for Toshiba America Information Systems Inc. He succeeds Nobuteru Baba, who returned to Toshiba Corp., the Japanese parent company. Eversole had previously been vice president, finance, and corporate controller for the Irvine-based manufacturer of office products and telecommunication systems. Before joining Toshiba America in 1984, he was corporate controller for Citicorp/financial services.
