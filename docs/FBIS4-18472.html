BFN [Unattributed article: "Malta/Italy: Scalfaro Congratulates Bonnici on Election"] [Text] (ANSA) Rome, April 5 -- Italian Head of State Oscar Luigi Scalfaro has sent a message of congratulations to Ugo Mifsud Bonnici for his election as president of the Republic of Malta and stressed the historic ties of friendship between the two nations, a statement from the presidential Quirinale Palace said here Tuesday [5 April]. "In the tradition of the ancient ties of friendship which, for geographic nearness, similar historic and cultural patrimony which have always united our two peoples" Scalfaro said in his message, "I remain convinced that in the future the excellent relations between Italy and Malta can continue to develop in all fields." "I am comforted in this" the Italian president continued, "knowing your profound awareness of our culture and its constant, sincere attention towards developing cooperation between our two countries." "In forwarding best wishes for your mandate and the well-being of the Maltese people" Scalfaro concluded, "please accept, Mr. president, my most heartfelt greetings."
