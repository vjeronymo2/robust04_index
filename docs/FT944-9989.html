941112 FT 12 NOV 94 / Weekend Money (Companies): Directors' dealings Buying at Everest Foods, noted last week, has continued. The most recent transactions, by Christopher and John Fox Davies, came at 56p, the same as they paid previously. There has been no movement in the share price. The three directors buying at Country Casuals, the women's clothing retailer, were taking the chance afforded them by the fall in the share price after the announcement of interim results which reflected a fall from pre-tax profit to loss. The directors paid 84p; since then, the shares have rallied to over 90p. Back in the spring, Richard Johnston, chief executive of Wyko Group, bought 120,000 shares at 45p. Since then, the company has done well, outperforming the market by 26 per cent over the past three months. Now, chairman Philip White and Roger Edwards, a non-executive director, have sold 400,000 shares each at 61.5p.
