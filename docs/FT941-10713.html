940208 FT 08 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Slimma gains 19p on first day - Issue News Shares in Slimma, the women's clothing and outsize garments manufacturer, yesterday closed up 19p at 141p on their first day of trading. The closing figure represented a 21p premium over the placing price. The flotation proceeds of Pounds 2.65m will be used to reduce gearing and increase output.
