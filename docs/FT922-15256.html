920402 FT 02 APR 92 / UK Company News: Exceptionals boost House of Lerose An exceptional credit of Pounds 2.96m, representing a pension fund surplus, enabled House of Lerose, the womens wear group, to return pre-tax profits of Pounds 3.63m for the 1991 year. Prior to the exceptional item, profits worked through at Pounds 675,000, a downturn of 21 per cent on the previous year's Pounds 854,000. Turnover declined from Pounds 16.8m to Pounds 13.3m. Earnings emerged at 39.5p (11.6p) per share after tax of Pounds 1.38m (Pounds 196,000). A proposed final dividend of 7.3p makes a same-again 10.3p total. An extraordinary charge of Pounds 136,000 (Pounds 334,000) represented the closure of certain garment production divisions. The shares rose 3p to 137p.
