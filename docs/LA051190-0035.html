May 11, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

AT&T HUNG UP

The United States has the highest incidence of teen-age pregnancy in the Western world.

AT&T gave $50,000 to Planned Parenthood.

Planned Parenthood used the $50,000 to help reduce the number of teen-age pregnancies through education, not abortion.

Someone lied to AT&T.

AT&T knuckled under.

AT&T took the $50,000 away from Planned Parenthood.

Poor women do not make long-distance calls, only small cries for help.

The United States has the highest incidence of teen-age pregnancy in the Western world.

BOBBIE CORBETT

Garden Grove
