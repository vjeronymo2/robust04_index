Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 27 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- As the MONTSAME special correspondent C. Burenbayar informed, the President of Mongolia P. Ochirbat, who is on an official visit to the Kingdom of Thailand, together with his spouse and the persons, accompanying him, visited in January 26 the king's grand palace, the Buddha's Emerald Temple, the Vimanmek Mansion Museum. In the afternoon the same day the President P. Ochirbat with his spouse paid a visit to the King of Thailand Phumiphon Adunyadet and his spouse in their residence. In the evening the Princess Maha Chakri Sirindhorn [name as received] gave a dinner in honour of the President of Mongolia P. Ochirbat and his spouse. The President P. Ochirbat during the talk with the princess said that her visit to Mongolia in 1992 has given an important impulse to development of Mongolian-Thailand friendly relations. The president expressed on behalf of the Mongolian state, government and the people cordial thanks to Thailand for regular participation of this country in the meetings of donor-countries, for rendering food assistance to Mongolia, training the Mongolian specialists on the line of technical cooperation, supporting by this the processes of reforms in Mongolia, and highly appreciated contribution of Her Highness the Princess Maha Chakri Sirindhorn for this cause. On behalf of the Mongolian children the President P. Ochirbat expressed cordial thanks to the princess for the delivery of medicines, vitamins, iodined salt, iodine to Mongolia in 1993 according to her initiative as a gift. During the talk with the Princess Maha Chakri Sirindhorn the president pointed out to the suggestion of the Ministry of Health of Mongolia to cooperate with the organization, which is headed by the princess, in introduction of technology of iodining salt at the table-salt enterprises, foundation of a laboratory for extraction of iodine, foundation of a shop on production of wrappings for preserving salt dry, and keeping iodine not evaporated, for realization of the national programme on solution of the problem of dearth of iodine of the population. The Mongolian President P. Ochirbat invited Her Royal Highness Princess Maha Chakri Sirindhorn to visit again Mongolia in summer time. The princess gave as a keepsake her book about Mongolia "Airag" to the Mongolian president P. Ochirbat.
