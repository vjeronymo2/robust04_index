Language: Tagalog Article Type:BFN [Text] Another bombing incident occurred at dawn in a Muslim mosque in Davao City. The incident was allegedly the work of two men riding a motor bike. The two men reportedly threw an M-203 grenade. There were no reports of anyone killed or injured at the scene. The incident happened at about 3:30 a.m. Government authorities cannot determine if the incident is related to another grenade throwing incident, which happened at a cathedral also in Davao City.
