Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The first lady today received Her Majesty Queen Nur al-Husayn of Jordan; their Royal Highnesses Princess Basmah and Princess Haya; Ghayda' Talal, Prince Talal Bin-Muhammd's wife; Jami al-Majali; Jordanian Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali's wife; and Nawzat Shakir, wife of Royal Court Chief al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir. She accepted their condolences on the homeland's loss of Staff Major Engineer Paratrooper Basil al-Asad. The meeting was attended by the wives of the vice president, the prime minister, and the foreign minister, who also received and bade farewell to Her Majesty Queen Nur al-Husayn at the airport.
