Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] On the evening of 27 February, provincial leaders Gu Jinchi, Quan Shuren, and Yue Qifeng respectively travelled to Liaoning Mansion and [words indistinct] Guesthouse to visit some people's deputies attending the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress. After inquired deputies about their working and living situation in details, provincial leaders said: You are here to attend the current session on behalf of the people. It is hoped that you will express the aspirations of the people and better exercise the rights invested on people's deputies. When visiting the Dalian deputation, Gu Jinchi said: More than four months have passed since I came to Liaoning. Since then, I have inspected more than 60 large and medium-sized enterprises, but only a few of them have suffered losses. During the current people's congress session, you should offer as more as possible the suggestions from the deputies of money-losing enterprises. Provincial leaders also visited the deputies from the scientific and technological circles, the cultural and educational circles, democratic parties, and [words indistinct], and enthusiastically discussed with them on the issues of invigorating large and medium- sized enterprises and developing township and town enterprises.
