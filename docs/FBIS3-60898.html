Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, Dec 29 (AA) -- Sixteen people were wounded in bomb explosions in Southeastern Diyarbakir Province on Wednesday. AA was told that the incident happened when two bombs planted in Dicle University cafeteria by the outlawed separatist terrorist PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] organization exploded wounding the students and school personnel and destroying the building. Terrorists planted the bombs into garbage cans.
