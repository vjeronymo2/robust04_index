March 23, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Three more shows have been added for Neil Diamond's upcoming stand at the Forum. With the first three concerts (June 28-30) having sold out within three hours, additional shows were added for July 5-7, and there may be more to come. In 1983, Diamond's seven-night stay at the Forum sold-out just as quickly. ALEENE MacMINN
