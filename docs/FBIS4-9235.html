BFN [Text] Tirana, April 21 (ATA) -- On April 20, the People's Assembly ratified the document of Partnership for Peace [PFP] between Albania and NATO. The document between Albania and NATO was signed on February 23, 1994 in the NATO headquarters by the President of the Republic Sali Berisha. According to the agreement, Albania will cooperate with NATO in questions of defence planning and budget, security and democratic control of the armed forces, in the capacities and readiness to contribute to the UN or CSCE operations, in training and manoeuvres for peace-keeping, search-saving, and humanitarian operations, as well as in the development of forces to enable them to operate with those of the NATO countries.
