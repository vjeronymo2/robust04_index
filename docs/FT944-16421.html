941013 FT 13 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Intl Inv Tst of Jersey advances The International Investment Trust Company of Jersey had pre-tax profits of Pounds 560,000 for the six months to June 30, compared with Pounds 390,000. Investment activities contributed Pounds 150,000 (Pounds 216,000), while the share of profits of associated companies added Pounds 176,000 (Pounds 77,000). Surplus on sale of investments was Pounds 273,000 (Pounds 131,000). Earnings per share came out at 8p, compared with 6.2p, and an interim dividend of 7.6p is payable, up from 6.4p.
