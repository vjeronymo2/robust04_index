(1) If no cracking is detected, repeat the ultrasonic inspection thereafter at intervals not to exceed 18 months, until the requirements of paragraph (b) of this AD are accomplished. (2) If cracking is detected during any inspection required by this paragraph, prior to further flight, accomplish either paragraph (a)(2)(i) or (a)(2)(ii): (i) Replace the cracked bolt with a new bolt made of H&hyph;11 material and continue to inspect in accordance with this paragraph at intervals not to exceed 18 months, until the requirements of paragraph (b) of this AD are accomplished. Or (ii) Replace the cracked bolt with a bolt made of Inconel, and replace the associated hardware, in accordance with the service bulletin.
