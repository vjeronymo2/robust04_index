February 6, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Grete Waitz, sidelined from marathon competition for more than a year because of injuries, will run in the fifth Los Angeles Marathon on March 4.

Others in the women's elite field are Raisa Smekhnova of the Soviet Union, Julie Isphording of the United States and Blanca Jaime of Mexico, the 1988 winner.
