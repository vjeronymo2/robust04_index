October 27, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

EL SALVADOR CONTROVERSY

It appears that Rosenberg's sympathies are with the communist FMLN in El Salvador and not with the elected Christani government.

I am not condoning the murder of those priests. But at a time when communism is disintegrating in many parts of the world, I fail to understand his logic. He is out of step.

FREDRICK D. MULLEN

Upland
