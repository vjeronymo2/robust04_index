930426 FT 26 APR 93 / Woodland battle CAMPAIGNERS fighting to save the 8,000-year-old Oxleas Wood in south-east London from a road development are to take the battle to Brussels tomorrow in a move to secure European Community backing against the government. The move follows a High Court defeat for the residents, backed by environmentalists, opposed to a Department of Transport plan to build a motorway through the wood.
