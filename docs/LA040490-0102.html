April 4, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

The Omar, Bayne, Art Angles, Saddleback Fine Arts and Lori Kaye art galleries will hold an art auction to benefit the Garden Grove Symphony Thursday at 7 p.m. at the Courtyard Center, 12732 Main St., Garden Grove. Admission is free. Information: (714) 534-1103.
