930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / International Company News: Warsaw SE to welcome first foreign member THE WARSAW stock exchange plans to welcome its first foreign member this year and hopes to be included in a key emerging markets investing index in 1994, Reuter reports from New York. Mr Wieslaw Rozlucki, the exchange president, said he expects Creditanstalt, the big Austrian bank, to become a member of the Warsaw exchange this year. Speaking at a presentation sponsored by the US-Poland Chamber of Commerce, Mr Rozlucki said he expects the exchange to be included in the IFC emerging markets index next year. Polish companies, eager to attract international investment, are also interested in establishing an American Depositary Receipt (ADR) programme, he said.
