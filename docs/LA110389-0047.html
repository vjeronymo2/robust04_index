November 3, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

CLOTHESLINES: SHRUG OFF THE FUR TRIM

Question: Are fur-trimmed coats fashionable now? I have an old sable shrug that could become the collar and cuffs on a cloth coat, but I hate to go to the expense if the look is dated.

H.I.

Answer: Fur collars and cuffs are not exactly the last work in coats, but fur sleeves are. How about taking a page from Paris designer Christian Lacroix's sketchbook and converting your shrug to sleeves, as illustrated here?
