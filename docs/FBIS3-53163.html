Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, today received a telephone call from brother Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid, secretary general of the Arab League, who called to reassure himself about developments in our country. The brother Arab League secretary general expressed the league's readiness to try to help bring views closer together, enabling our country to overcome the challenges facing it as a result of the continuation of the current political crisis and safeguarding Yemen's unity, which constitutes a gain for the sons of the Arab nation. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council, also received a telephone call today from brother Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid, secretary general of the Arab League, who told the vice chairman of the Presidential Council that the Arab League was ready to exert every effort to bring views close together and help our country overcome the current political crisis and find solutions to its issues and problems in order to enable our country to preserve unity, democracy, and stability. He also stressed that Yemen's unity, which was achieved on 22 May 1990, is considered a pan-Arab gain in which all the sons of the Arab nation take pride. The Presidential Council vice chairman expressed his appreciation for the Arab League's eagerness to help our country overcome the crisis and the obstacles facing it.
