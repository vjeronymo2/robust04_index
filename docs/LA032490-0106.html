March 24, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

HOURLONG 'TRIANGLE' TO REPLACE 'WISEGUY' APRIL 18

"Triangle," an hourlong drama starring series veterans Bruce Boxleitner ("Scarecrow and Mrs. King") and Jameson Parker ("Simon & Simon") will debut on CBS April 18.

Boxleitner and Parker portray friends who have been in competition since their school days, and continue to compete for the attentions of their dream woman (Daphne Ashbrook).

"Triangle" replaces "Wiseguy" in the 10 p.m. Wednesday time slot. "Wiseguy" will air its final original episode of the season April 11.
