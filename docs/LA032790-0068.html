March 27, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

HIGH SCHOOL BASEBALL;

MONTE VISTA, STONE HAND GROSSMONT FIRST DEFEAT OF YEAR

Robby Stone allowed just four hits to previously undefeated Grossmont (11-1) as Monte Vista High defeated the Foothillers, 4-2, Monday in a nonleague game at Monte Vista.

Stone, a 6-foot-6 junior, had seven strikeouts to improve to 3-1.

In the third inning for Monte Vista (9-3), catcher Vern Mullis (three for four) singled in Steve Ryan with Ernie Reyes scoring on an ensuing overthrow. Mullis scored on Darren Rucker's single.

Santana 7, Helix 2 -- Santana's Ferdie Hollis (2-0) had a three-hit shutout going before allowing two runs in the seventh inning at Helix. Santana remained undefeated at 10-0. El Capitan 11, West Hills 1 -- El Capitan's Jim Marchesi was three for five with two doubles and two runs, and Greg Ransweiler (1-1) scattered four hits and struck out nine in six innings.

El Cajon Valley 3, Mt. Miguel 0 -- John Hayward pitched a two-hit shutout at El Cajon.

Valhalla 3, Granite Hills 3 -- Valhalla scored a run in the top of the seventh to tie Granite Hills. The game was called because of darkness after seven innings.
