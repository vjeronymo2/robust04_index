BFN [Text] Decision of the Provisional Council for National Salvation No. 2 [as heard]/1994 concerning the setting up of the State Presidential Council: In the name of the people, after taking cognizance of Articles 1, 13 and 14, Paragraph B of the document on the proclamation of the Democratic Republic of Yemen, and the two decisions of the Provisional Council No. 1/1994 regarding the election of a chairman for the council, and No. 2 [as heard]/1994 for the proclamation of the Democratic Republic of Yemen, and the decisions of the Provisional Council for National Salvation at its meeting on 21 May 1994 concerning the election of the State Presidential Council, the Provisional Council for National Salvation has decided the following: Article 1: The Presidential Council of the Democratic Republic of Yemen is formed as follows: 'Ali Salim al-Bid, Salim Salih Muhammad, 'Abd-al-Rahman 'Ali al-Ja'fari, 'Abd-al-Qawi Hasan Makkawi, and Sulayman Nasir Mas'ud. Article 2: The Presidential Council assumes all its tasks and prerogatives in accordance with the provisions of the Constitution, and the document of the setting up of the Democratic Republic of Yemen. Article 3: This decision takes effect from the date of its issuance. Article 4: This decision will be published in the official gazette.
