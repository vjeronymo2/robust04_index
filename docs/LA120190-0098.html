December 1, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Little Rock Barton High's winning streak was ended at 63 games in a 27-20 defeat by Rison Friday night in the final of the Class-A Arkansas playoffs.

Barton became the team with the longest winning streak in the country when Animas, N.M., lost to Lordsburg, N.M., on Oct. 26, ending a 69-game streak.
