BFN [By Yi Tong-sik from Beijing] [Text] China has reasserted its position of opposing the deployment of Patriot missiles to the ROK. In a news briefing this afternoon, Wu Jianmin, spokesman for the PRC Foreign Ministry, said that it is not considered desirable to conduct any exercises or deploy missiles that would harm the security of the Korean peninsula, and that they [United States and the ROK] should refrain from conducting an act that would heighten tensions on the peninsula. Spokesman Wu Jianmin said that regarding the recent reports on China's envoy for Kim Il-song's 82d birthday, China would not send a high-level envoy to North Korea.
