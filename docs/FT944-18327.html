941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / UK Company News: FR acquisition FR Group, the maker of in-flight refuelling equipment, has completed the acquisition of the business assets and certain liabilities of Sargent Fletcher for Dollars 10.6m (Pounds 6.7m), including Dollars 1.1m cash. Net book value of assets being acquired amount to about Dollars 8.3m including liabilities of Dollars 2.1m. Sargent Fletcher makes external fuel tanks and air-to-air refuelling systems.
