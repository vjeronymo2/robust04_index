940509 FT 09 MAY 94 / Company News (This Week): Roche - Other companies Roche: Directors of the Swiss pharmaceuticals group may shed more light on the rather high Dollars 5.3bn price they bid last week for US drugs group Syntex at the annual press conference tomorrow. Roche has revealed a 29 per cent rise in 1993 net income to SFr2.48bn (Dollars 1.7bn) but has yet to indicate whether it will continue defying the industry-wide profit squeeze.
