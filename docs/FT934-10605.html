931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Audax Properties AUDAX PROPERTIES: Pre-tax profits for half year to September 30 were Pounds 219,000 (Pounds 179,000 losses) after Pounds 145,000 gain (Pounds 387,000 loss) on disposal of properties. Earnings per share on investing activities 90p (278p). Audax is wholly-owned subsidiary of Value and Income Trust.
