FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Barbara Chappelle, Publications Branch, (202) 418&hyph;0310. SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: Need for Correction As published, the final regulation document contains an error in calculating the close window filing date and is in need of clarification. Correction of Publication Accordingly, the publication on October 13, 1994, of the final regulations, which were the subject of FR Doc. 94&hyph;25307 is corrected as follows: On page 51868, in the second column, in the DATES section, the close window period for filing applications should be ``December 23, 1994'' in lieu of ``December 7, 1994''.
