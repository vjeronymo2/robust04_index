920627 FT 27 JUN 92 / Finance & The Family (At a glance): Tessas top Pounds 10bn total More than 3m people have invested a total of Pounds 10.41bn in Tax Exempt Special Savings Accounts since their introduction in January 1991, according to figures published by the Inland Revenue. Just over Pounds 5bn was invested in the first three months of their introduction. This year more than Pounds 3bn was invested from January to March, compared with Pounds 497m from October to December 1991. This is because Tessa-holders were limited to a maximum of Pounds 3,000 in the first year but were able to add a further Pounds 1,500 in the second year.
