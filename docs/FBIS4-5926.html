BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 15 (KCNA) -- The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] and president of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, today received foreign high-level delegations and delegates at the Kumsusan Assembly Hall who paid a congratulatory call on him. They were a delegation of the National Democratic Congress of Ghana headed by its general secretary Huudu Yahaya, a delegation of the Socialist Party of Kazakhstan headed by its co-chairman P. V. Svoik, a delegation of the Workers' Party of Ireland headed by its general secretary Pat Quearney, general secretary of the Workers' Party of Turkey Yalsin Buyukdagli, a delegation of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China headed by its secretary Gu Jinchi, a delegation of the October 8 Revolutionary Movement of Brazil headed by member of the political commission and secretary of the movement Sergio Rubens, vice-president of the Socialist Workers' Party of Romania Maria Lazar, vice-chairman of the Liberal Democratic Party and deputy speaker of the state Duma of the Federation Council of Russia A. D. Vengerovskiy and his entourage, a delegation of the Maritime Territorial Committee of the Communist Party [CP] of the Russian Federation headed by its first secretary V. V. Grishukov, first secretary of the Khabarovsk Territorial Committee of the Communist Party of the Russian Federation L. A. Golub, a delegation of the Amur Regional Committee of the CP of the Russian Federation headed by its first secretary G. E. Gamza, a delegation of the Japan-Korea Friendship Data Center headed by Yoshiyuki Oe, chairman of the center and president of the S.B.B. Company, Ltd., of Japan, director general of the International Institute of the Chuche Idea Shuhachi Inoue, secretary general of the World Federation of Trade Unions Alexandr Jarikov, a delegation of the secretariat of the Society for the Study of the Chuche Idea of Japanese Teachers headed by Masagazu Yokobori, advisor and secretary general of the Japan Liaison Council of Societies for the Study of Kim Chong-il's Works, a delegation of the Kim Chong-suk Kindergarten in Dalandzadgad City, Omnogov Province, Mongolia, headed by its superintendent Altantuya, a delegation of the Karachi branch of the Pakistan-Korea Friendship Association headed by Abdul Razak Molai and chairman of the Mongolian Study Centre of the Chuche Idea Y. O. Maxim. Present on the occasion were Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the WPK Central Committee and officials concerned. The guests presented a flower basket to President Kim Il-song in celebration of his birthday and wished him long life in good health. President Kim Il-song expressed thanks for this and had a cordial and friendly talk with them.
