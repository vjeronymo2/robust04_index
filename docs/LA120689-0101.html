December 6, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Regulators Seize Brookside Savings: Federal regulators have taken over Brookside Savings & Loan, a four-branch thrift in Los Angeles that got into trouble because of problems with real estate loans and property owned in Arizona, Texas and Nevada. The savings and loan will operate under normal business hours, with the federal Resolution Trust Corp. as receiver. The thrift, declared insolvent by the Office of Thrift Supervision, has $629.9 million in assets and $574.4 million in deposits.
