DATES: This withdrawal is effective upon publication. 14 CFR Part 71 [Airspace Docket No. 94&hyph;AGL&hyph;11] Proposed Class D Airspace Modification; Traverse City, MI AGENCY: Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), DOT. ACTION: Notice of proposed rulemaking; withdrawal.

SUMMARY: This action withdraws the Notice of proposed rulemaking (NPRM) which proposed to modify Class D airspace near Traverse City, Michigan to change the operating times of the controlled airspace from continuous to parttime. The NPRM is being withdrawn because the modification is contained in another regulatory airspace action, Docket No. 94&hyph;AGL&hyph;16. DATES: This withdrawal is effective upon publication. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: 0 Robert J. Woodford, Air Traffic Division, System Management Branch, AGL&hyph;530, Federal Aviation Administration, 2300 East Devon Avenue, Des Plaines, Illinois 60018, telephone (708) 294&hyph;7568. SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: The Proposed Rule On May 6, 1994, an NPRM was published in the Federal Register to modify the official description of the Class D airspace associated with the Cherry Capital Airport, Traverse City, Michigan. The modification was to allow a variation to the class D airspace effective times to coincide with the operating hours of the Traverse City ATCT (99 FR 23642). The NPRM is being withdrawn since this section is contained in Airspace Docket No. 94&hyph;AGL&hyph;16, which was published in the Federal Register on May 12, 1994 (59 FR 24906). List of Subjects in 14 CFR Part 71 Airspace, Incorporation by reference, Navigation (air). Withdrawal of Proposed Rule Accordingly, pursuant to the authority delegated to me, Airspace Docket No. 94&hyph;AFL&hyph;11, as published in the Federal Register on May 6, 1994 (59 FR 23642), is hereby withdrawn.
