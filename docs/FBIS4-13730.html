BFN [Text] The Supreme Court of Pakistan has by a majority vote held that on the revival of the Provincial Assembly of the NWFP [North-West Frontier Province] on the lapse of the proclamation issued by the president, the chief minister and his cabinet will stand revived. The court clarified that it would be open to the governor to refix the date and time in accordance with Article 130 (5) of the Constitution, requiring the chief minister to obtain a vote of confidence from the assembly. The two dissenting judges were of the view that the petition [by the former chief minister] was not maintainable and would therefore stand dismissed.
