BFN [Text] A state of top alert prevails among the Israeli occupation forces and their puppet militia, on one side, and the Lebanese Army and the Lebanese resistance groups, on the other. The NATIONAL NEWS AGENCY has learned that the UN Interim Force in Lebanon has placed the personnel manning its checkpoints on alert. It also beefed up the checkpoints with additional troops. The occupation forces have mobilized additional troops inside the occupied border strip. Convoys of tanks, military vehicles, and troop carriers have been deployed on the hills overlooking the liberated areas near the international border. According to security sources, the Israeli occupation forces have deployed long-range mobile artillery on the Muhaybib and al-'Abbad hills, and Jabal Humayd. AFP reported today that large numbers of Israeli tanks, armored vehicles, artillery pieces, and troops carriers were deployed on the Lebanese-Israeli international border but did not cross. Meanwhile, contrary to some media's claims, no flood of refugees has been reported from the areas under attack despite the atmosphere of tension there.
