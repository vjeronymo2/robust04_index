930401 FT 01 APR 93 / Possible face lift for Wimbledon Wimbledon, home of tennis stars past and present such as Michael Stich (left) and Rod Laver could have a face-lift if proposals unveiled yesterday by the All-England Lawn Tennis Club are approved. The plans, by consultants Building Design Partnership, aim to improve facilities for spectators, players and officials. Under the proposals Number One court will be demolished to make way for a facilities building, and a new show court will be built. Planning permission is being sought and work could begin by 1995. The project will be financed by the issue of debentures.
