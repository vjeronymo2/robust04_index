940920 FT 20 SEP 94 / World Trade News: FlyingCat contract won by Kvaerner Fjellstrand Kvaerner Fjellstrand of Singapore, part of the Kvaerner group, has secured a Dollars 5m contract for a high-speed FlyingCat catamaran for Hong Kong Ferry (Holdings), Extel reports from Singapore. The vessel will be the third fast craft of this type delivered by the Singapore company to the shipping group.
