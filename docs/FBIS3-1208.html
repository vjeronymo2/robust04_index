Language: French Article Type:BFN [Summary From Poor Reception] "The head of state, President Mahamane Ousmane, left this morning for Abuja, Nigeria, to attend the eighth summit of the Lake Chad Basin Commission." In addition to General Sani Abacha of Nigeria and Idriss Deby of Chad, who arrived this afternoon, four other heads of state are expected to attend the meeting. The Cameroonian head of state was expected to take part in the meeting, but his "trip was canceled at the last minute." The organization includes six member states. They will discuss the development of the Lake Chad Basin.
