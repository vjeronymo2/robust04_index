April 10, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

NORTHWEST OFFERS FREE AMENITITES ON FLIGHTS

Northwest Airlines said it has begun complimentary cabin amenities on all flights across the Pacific and to and from Hawaii as part of a $442-million program to enhance the airlines' customer service.

The move to free in-flight enteratinment and complimentary drinks will extend to all transatlantic flights May 1.

"Northwest is the first U.S. airline to offer this customer-friendly approach on all its transoceanic services," said A. B. Magary, executive vice president of marketing.
