BFN [Text] "A government cannot govern if its policies are not accepted in both chambers of parliament. Were that to happen, there would be no other solution than to go back to the electorate with a majority that would be confirmed and strengthened by the experience. I hope this does not prove necessary." Silvio Berlusconi has thus added his voice to those who think there might be a new general election if Giovanni Spadolini were to win. Earlier the Northern League's Roberto Maroni had warned: We will have another general election and "we will say that the PDS [Democratic Party of the Left] and the PPI [Italian Popular Party] were in cahoots to bring the institutions to a standstill and we will ask the nation for greater power."
