BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 12/4/1994 -- The ruling Party of Social Democracy (PDSR), presented a document at a Tuesday, April 12, press conference of the party treating about the social-economic priorities of the PDSR programme, that were proposed for discussions with economic experts of other parties. The eight chapters of the document, said PDSR Executive Chairman Adrian Nastase, refer to the main objectives of the economical-social activity, to the speeding up of privatisation, to in-depth restructuring of the fiscal and budgetary policy, to the monetary and hard-currency policy, to the putting in place of the objectives and priorities regarding foreign economic relations, to measures aimed to further the reform in the other sectors of the social activity, in fields such as the salary policy and welfare. The PDSR executive chairman said that the governing party was aware of its responsibilities and it would show responsibility, consistence, interest as far as the country's destiny is concerned, in order to translate into deeds the guiding lines and priorities of the economical-social strategy, with a view to continue the consolidation of the state of law and ensure real social security.
