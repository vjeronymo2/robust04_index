931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / World Trade News: French radio system sold to UAE THOMSON-CSF, the French defence electronics company, has won a Dollars 100m (Pounds 67m) order to supply the United Arab Emirates with its PR4G military radio system. The system, which has already been sold to the Netherlands, Spain and Switzerland, will be used in various vehicles, including the Leclerc tank ordered from Giat of France.
