920827 FT 27 AUG 92 / Tube workers face pay cuts MORE than 6,000 London Underground staff face pay cuts under a proposed shake-up of pay and conditions. London Underground admitted that 29 per cent of staff would lose out under the plan but said that all employees would benefit from salaried status and a 35-hour week. The RMT rail union said that it had not agreed to the new terms and conditions.
