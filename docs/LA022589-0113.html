February 25, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

NO DRUG CENTER IN LAKE VIEW TERRACE

Iam opposed to the planned Phoenix House/Nancy Reagan Center's being located in Lake View Terrace.

As a resident of Lake View Terrace, I am familiar with the proposed site and think that far more suitable locations are available elsewhere. The site in question, a former medical center, is surrounded by single-family homes and has two schools nearby.

I realize the Valley needs such a facility. However, it is time another community assumes this responsibility. Lake View Terrace already contains several facilities for the treatment of drug and alcohol abuse, a sanitarium for the mentally ill, a youth correctional facility and the Lopez Canyon Landfill, which accepts the majority of L.A.'s trash. OUR BACK YARD IS ALREADY FULL! IT'S SOMEONE ELSE'S TURN!

THOMAS D. FAY

Lake View Terrace
