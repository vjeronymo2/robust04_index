Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] A session of the CIS Council of Defense Ministers has been held in Moscow. The Belarusian military delegation, headed by Colonel General Pavel Kazlowski, the republic's defense minister, examined and decided on questions concerning integration [words indistinct] of CIS countries in the military sphere. Primarily there was a discussion of documents regulating the activity of the coordination bodies of military cooperation -- the Council of Defense Ministers, its secretariat, and also the headquarters for coordinating military cooperation. The defense ministers of Belarus and the Russian Federation signed an agreement on interaction of air defense forces and means. The next session of the Council of Defense Ministers is planned for the middle period of March. It is intended that it will be held jointly with the CIS Council of Foreign Ministers.
