August 19, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

Kudos to KNBC for its excellent coverage of the opening of the Richard M. Nixon Library in Yorba Linda.

My health prevented me from attending in person, but thanks to Channel 4, I was still able to enjoy the ceremonies.

This was a historic event regardless of how a person feels about the former President. The four Presidents together (Nixon, George Bush, Ronald Reagan, Gerald Ford) made quite a picture, as did their First Ladies. Thanks again, KNBC. I think you've won a new viewer of your news.

Brenda Hester, Garden Grove
