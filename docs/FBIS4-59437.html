BFN [Text] Doha, 12 May (INA)--Information Minister Hasan al-Lawzi today said that the elements who ignited the military situation in the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] must leave the country or stand trial. In a statement over the telephone to the QATARI NEWS AGENCY, al-Lawzi said that the opportunity is now open for the limited number of elements who ignited the situation to leave the country in order to spare the blood of the people and to stop destroying the country's resources. He added that there is a chance for these elements, who are a minority in the YSP, to leave the country so that they will not cause any further losses in the southern governorates of Aden and Lahij. He continued by saying: If they stay, these elements who are involved in the crime of engineering a secession and destroying the country must be interrogated and punished for violating the constitution and the law. On Wednesday [11 May], President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih announced his willingness to give orders that would allow the secessionists to leave Yemen.
