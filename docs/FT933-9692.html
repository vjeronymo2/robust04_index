930807 FT 07 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: Palace opens to public Buckingham Palace opens 18 of its staterooms to the public for the first time today. When the eight-week opening period ends, some 400,000 people are expected to have visited. The Pounds 8 entry fee will help the Queen raise almost Pounds 3m towards the restoration of Windsor Castle.
