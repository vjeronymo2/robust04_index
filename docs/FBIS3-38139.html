Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] In his opening address at the "Italian Socialist Party (PSI) General Assembly", party chairman (Labor Minister Gino) Giugni was applauded when he told the 6,000 delegates in Rome that "the old Socialist Party leadership does not live here any more." He added: "That leadership" was responsible for "a destructive moral pollution" and for "a series of hastily taken, wrong political choices." Before the assembly, Party Secretary (Ottaviano) Del Turco met Giorgio Bogi of the Democratic Alliance (AD). He told him that the PSI intends to stand together with the AD in the progressive alliance in the forthcoming general election.
