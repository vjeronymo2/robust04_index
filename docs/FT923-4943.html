920903 FT 03 SEP 92 / UK Company News: ICS beats earnings forecast INDUSTRIAL Control Services, the electronic safety systems manufacturer which obtained a listing in May, yesterday reported full year earnings slightly ahead of its prospectus forecast. Profits for the 12 months to May 31 increased by 29 per cent to Pounds 4.6m (Pounds 3.6m) pre-tax on turnover ahead 25 per cent at Pounds 64.4m. Earnings per share of 9.1p (7.3p) compared with the prospectus forecast of 8.9p. Net assets at the year-end stood at Pounds 18.4m, and with the Pounds 6.6m proceeds from the flotation, gearing was reduced to 26 per cent. As foreshadowed, a special dividend of 0.3p is declared.
