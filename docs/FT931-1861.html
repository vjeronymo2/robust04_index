930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / Meeting on bank in administration A GROUP of customers of Equatorial Bank, the which was placed into administration last week, are expected to meet administrators this week. Ms Valerie Townsend, secretary of a depositors' group, said customers were likely to meet the administrators Ernst & Young to discuss an orderly run-down of the bank. She believed depositors were likely to regain their money. The bank, which has about 3,000 Asian small-business customers in Britain, is thought to have been affected by a run on deposits.
