Language: English Article Type:CSO [Article: "Heroin Seized"] [Text] Madras, Jan. 7. Heroin, valued at about Rs 3 crores in the international market, has been seized by officials of the Narcotics Control Bureau (NCB) South Zone, Madras, from two gangs of drug traffickers operating independently in Madras and Madurai. According to Mr P.V.K. Reddy, Deputy Director of the NCB, South Zone, Madras, on the basis of specific intelligence, the officers intercepted a private omni bus near the Tambaram checkpost on Wednesday and seized 2 kg of high quality heroin from two passengers. The bus was bound for Tirunelveli. The heroin was concealed in the false bottom of two zipper rexin bags. It was to be exported from the southern coast to Sri Lanka.
