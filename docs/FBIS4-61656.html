BFN [Text] People's Deputy Alyaksandr Lukashenka yesterday began his meetings with Mahilyow residents. In the morning, he visited the oblast hospital and addressed medical personnel and patients who gathered in the hospital conference room. In a 30-minute speech, he presented his opinion on the unification of the Belarusian and Russian monetary systems, privatization, and the Republic's sovereignty. Alyaksandr Lukashenka did not speak of his presidential program. He only outlined its certain aspects and ways of implementing it, explaining that it was not an electoral address as such. Regarding the work of the Supreme Soviet commission he heads, Alyaksandr Hryhorovich pledged to conclude the corruption case.
