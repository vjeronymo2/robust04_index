941228 FT 28 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Raglan in Pounds 14m property swap Raglan Properties and Schroder Exempt Property Trust have swapped two property portfolios in a deal worth a total of Pounds 14m. Raglan is acquiring five shops and a small industrial estate for Pounds 7.72m while Schroder Exempt will get five shops for Pounds 6.22m, the Pounds 1.5m difference being satisfied in cash.
