Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 2 KYODO -- Japan Foundation, the public corporation promoting cultural exchange under the jurisdiction of the Foreign Ministry, opened a Beijing liaison office on Monday [28 February]. Chinese Vice Culture Minister Cheng Changben and Japan Foundation head Shinichiro Asao were among those present at an opening ceremony for the office, foundation officials said. The Beijing office aims to promote Sino-Japan cultural exchanges, they said. The foundation maintains a cultural center in Jakarta, cultural institutes in Cologne and Rome, and liaison offices in Bangkok, Kuala Lumpur, London, Los Angeles, Mexico City, New York, Paris, Sao Paulo, Sydney and Toronto.
