920321 FT 21 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Martin Currie Euro net assets improve Net asset value per share of the Martin Currie European Investment Trust rose from 282.8p to 297.2p over the 12 months ended February 28. The improvement was due largely to a reduction from 52 per cent to 29 per cent in exposure to the 'poor performing' Japanese stock market and an increase from 13 per cent to 27 per cent in the trust's weighting in Hong Kong. Earnings declined to 1.1p (1.43p) and the dividend for the year is 0.4p (0.75p).
