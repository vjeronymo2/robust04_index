BFN [Text] Tokyo, April 4 (KNS-KCNA) -- The chairman of the Association of Koreans in Japan for Peaceful Reunification, Kim Chong-su, voiced support for the appeal of the Central Committee of the Democratic Front for the Reunification of the Fatherland (DFRF). The DFRF Central Committee issued an appeal to the compatriots in South Korea and overseas on March 29 denouncing the criminal moves of the U.S. imperialists and the South Korean puppet clique bringing an extremely grave danger of a nuclear war to the Korean peninsula and the Korean nation. In a statement released on April 2, Kim Chong-su declared that Koreans in Japan would unite closely with the compatriots in the homeland and overseas on the basis of "10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation for the reunification of the country" and strengthen solidarity with the justice-loving and peace-loving people around the world to invigorate the struggle for thwarting the nuclear war provocation moves of the U.S. imperialists and the South Korean puppet clique, definitely preserving peace on the Korean peninsula and promoting national reunification.
