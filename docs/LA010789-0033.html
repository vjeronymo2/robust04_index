January 7, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

TROJANS' SWAN SONG IN ROSE BOWL HAD A FAMILIAR REFRAIN

During the Michigan game, I heard comments about USC still hurting from the loss to Notre Dame. When the Trojans continue to self-destruct during the big games of the 1989 season, will they blame it on their loss to Michigan?

PETER BRITTAIN

Laguna Beach
