930305 FT 05 MAR 93 / World News in Brief: Guppy admits VAT fraud Gem dealer Darius Guppy, 28, and his business associate, Benedict Marsh, admitted at a London court that they falsely claimed nearly Pounds 200,000 in VAT on consignments of gold. Last month they were convicted of a separate Pounds 1.8m gems swindle. Sentence was adjourned.
