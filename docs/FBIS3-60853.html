Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Urgent" item by ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Byrkin] [Excerpts] Moscow December 24 TASS -- Bad weather prevented the Mi-8 helicopter of the Russian Air Force where terrorists are staying with a group of children taken hostage from flying to Makhachkala, the capital of the Russian Republic of Daghestan, bordering on Azerbaijan on the Caspian Sea, TASS learned from the press centre of the Russian Air Force which added that kidnappers planned to refuel in Makhachkala and fly to Iran. The air force experts believe one of the terrorists has a certain flying experience which is proved by the demand to provide flight documents and accumulators. [passage omitted] The helicopter unexpectedly took off from Mineralnyye Vody on Friday, but then returned back. It became known that special commando groups were flying after the helicopter with terrorists all the way from Rostov-on-Don.
