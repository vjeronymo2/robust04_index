BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 7 June Evening Edition] [Text] At a news conference after the cabinet meeting on 7 June, Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa commented on the possible ban on North Koreans in Japan making remittances to their home country, a move which is being considered as part of economic sanctions against the DRPK over the issue of its suspected development of nuclear arms. He said: "Similar cases in the past have provided for some exceptions on humanitarian grounds." He indicated that if sanctions were be imposed, some exceptions would be provided on humanitarian grounds.
