BFN [Text] Nuremberg (DDP/ADN) -- Unemployment in Germany went down sharply in May. In western Germany, the number of unemployed fell by 84,400 to 2,505,900. In eastern Germany, the number of unemployed fell by 56,700 to 1,159,500. Bernhard Jagoda, president of the Federal Labor Office, said in Nuremberg today that the unemployment rate, based on all persons engaged in civilian employment, fell by 0.8 percent to 15.4 percent in eastern Germany and by 0.3 to 8.1 percent in western Germany. Jagoda said that "the negative effects of the recession are gradually weakening" in western Germany, while demand for manpower in eastern Germany is reviving.
