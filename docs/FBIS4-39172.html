BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondent Alexey Trotsenko] [Text] Kiev, 4 June (TASS) -- A major principle of CSCE is inviolability of borders; this principle should be the basic one in the search for mutually acceptable solutions of Ukrainian-Crimean problems, CSCE High Commissioner for Ethnic Minorities Max Van der Stoel said here today. The high commissioner has been to Ukraine on a four-day visit to meet Ukrainian and Crimean officials and discuss the situation around the Crimea. In his words, CSCE will render comprehensive assistance to the settlement of Ukrainian-Crimean problems on the basis of inviolability of borders.
