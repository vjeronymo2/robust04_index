BFN [Text] Budapest, 3 May (MTI) -- A lot of people live in worse circumstances than during the last few years of the Kadar regime, yet the country should continue along the path that it chose in 1990, Federation of Young Democrats [Fidesz] prime ministerial candidate Viktor Orban told a forum of the Fidesz in Szolnok on Tuesday [3 May]. Despite disillusionment and pessimism, the past four years can by no way be regarded as wasted time. In this parliamentary cycle the country made more progress than ever before in this century, Orban said. If the politicians of the former regime return after the elections, many people will be bitterly disappointed again, the politician said.
