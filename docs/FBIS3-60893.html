Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Unidentified persons placed explosives at the Ankara provincial headquarters of the True Path Party [TPP] this evening. Four people were wounded in the incident. According to information obtained from the authorities, unidentified persons arrived at the TPP's Ankara provincial headquarters at 2030 this evening and threw two hand grenades. One of the grenades exploded inside the building and the other outside. The explosion caused damage to the building and shattered windows in the surrounding buildings and in cars parked nearby. Four people, among them one security official, were wounded in the incident, and they were taken to various hospitals in Ankara for treatment. The incident is being investigated. [passage omitted]
