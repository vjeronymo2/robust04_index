Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "United States Inspectors Are Staying in Minsk"] [Text] BELAPAN has learned at the National Agency for Control and Inspection within the Belarusian Defense Ministry that a group of inspectors from the United States is staying in Belarus. This group, according to a pact on medium and short range missiles, is conducting an inspection of a missile complex in Kolosovo, Minsk Oblast. One more group of inspectors-- from Hungary -- left today for their motherland. According to the CFE treaty, this group conducted an inspection of the Bobruysk military hardware storage base No. 193.
