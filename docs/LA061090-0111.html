June 10, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NON-TRADITIONAL JOB?

Do you or someone you know make all or part of your living through a non-traditional job or occupation (maybe it's even a bit shady)? I'd like to hear from you.

I'm studying how Americans make a living and I'll treat your reply with total confidentiality. Just send an address to "Jobs," P.O. Box 171, Eureka, CA 95502. I'll send you a short questionnaire so that you can become a part of a national study of the American entrepreneurial spirit.

PETE PETERSON

EUREKA
