910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Kewill makes German purchase Kewill has bought Weigang MCS of Germany for an initial DM1.2m (Pounds 400,000) cash; subject to profit achievement an additional payment of up to DM1.3m may become due. Weigang develops and distributes manufacturing systems software throughout Germany, and Kewill said the purchase quickened the pace of its development in Europe. Since the interim figures, which showed pre-tax profits up 34 per cent, trading at Kewill had continued satisfactorily.
