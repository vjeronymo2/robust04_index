Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The economic talks between Israel and the PLO in Paris have run into a stalemate. In their meeting last night, Finance Minister Avraham Shohat and PLO representative Abu-al-'Ala' [Ahmad Quray'] failed to overcome the disagreements between the sides. Our correspondent Yo'av Tocker reports that the meeting lasted five hours ending without results. The main dispute concerns the issue of joint customs duties to be levied by Israel and the autonomy.
