Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Feb (MENA)--The death penalty was carried out in the Appeals Prison today against 'Abd-al-Shafi Ahmad Muhammad Ramadan, who was convicted of assassinating Dr. Faraj Fudah. Two other prisoners, Hamdi 'Abd-al-Fattah Ibrahim and Faraj Ramadan 'Abd-al-Mun'im, convicted by Cairo's Criminal Court in Kidnapping Case number 8534/92, were also executed. The execution of the three was carried out in the presence of the director of East Cairo's Prosecution Office, the director of al-Sayyidah Quarter's Prosecution Office, and the medical examiner. The execution lasted for one and a half hours.
