July 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

WHERE TO START THE QUEST

How to get there: Iberia Airlines, Aviaco and Air Europa fly to the Balearic Islands from Madrid or Barcelona, which is only 30 minutes away. Flights and hotels are often hard to come by in the peak months of July and August, so book in advance.

In May, June, and September, the weather is still fine and there are fewer crowds.

Accommodations: For reservations at Ibiza's Hotel Hacienda, contact Marketing Ahead, 433 Fifth Ave., New York 10016, (212) 686-9213.
