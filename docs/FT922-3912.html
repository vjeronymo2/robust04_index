920609 FT 09 JUN 92 / UK Company News: David Brown moves ahead to Pounds 8.3m David Brown Corporation, the specialist engineering group which was the subject of a Pounds 46m management buy-in, more than doubled pre-tax profits from Pounds 3.8m to Pounds 8.3m in the year to January 31. Turnover was Pounds 80.9m (Pounds 77.5m).
