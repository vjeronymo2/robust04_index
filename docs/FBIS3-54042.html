Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf this evening received Mr. (Muhaydi al-Muhaydi), chief of the Public Department of Arab Affairs at the Arab League, as an envoy of the Arab League secretary general. They exchanged views on how to consolidate cooperation between the foreign relations (?organizations) and the Arab League to advance Arab interests. The meeting was attended by the under secretary at the Foreign Ministry and the head of the Legal Department there.
