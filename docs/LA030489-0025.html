March 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"The people who hate acupuncture and who have always hated acupuncture will have closed minds and say, 'I always told you those guys were no good.' And the people who believe in acupuncture will say, 'Well, there goes the establishment again trying to stamp out anything that's not traditional.' "

-- Dr. Ronald Katz of the UCLA School of Medicine, after investigators from the Los Angeles County district attorney's office arrested 16 Korean acupuncturists and issued 47 warrants charging that bribes were paid to obtain answers for acupuncture-licensing exams.
