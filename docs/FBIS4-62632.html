BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Boris Krivoshey and Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow May 17 TASS -- Russia decided to set up a mobile unit in Tanzania to deliver aid to Rwandan refugees, a spokesman of the Russian Foreign Ministry told a briefing on Tuesday, adding that the implementation of the action, which was agreed upon by the Russian Ministry for Emergency Situations and the department of UN High Commissioner on Refugees, will begin on Wednesday. "The Russian diplomacy resorts to active efforts in order to find a way out of the Rwandan crisis", Grigoriy Karasin said. He stressed that Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev had sent a letter to his counterparts in Tanzania, Uganda and Zaire calling to use the prestige of their countries and stop the fratricidal war in Rwanda. Besides, Russia urged the warring sides to immediately cease fire, resume negotiations and continue the settlement process, he added.
