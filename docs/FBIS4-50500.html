BFN [By reporter Wang Wei (3769 5588) and reporter trainee Han Qinghua (7281 3237 5478)] [Text] Beijing, 28 Apr (XINHUA) -- A meeting to report on the 40th founding anniversary of the Shoudu Youth Shock Brigade was held in Beijing [date not given]. Those attending the meeting included Li Ruihuan, member of the Standing Committee of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; Chen Xitong, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and secretary of the Beijing Municipal CPC Central Committee; and other leading comrades. Prior to the meeting, Li Ruihuan had a discussion with the representatives of veteran leaders of the Shoudu Shock Brigade and the present leaders of the youth shock brigades, meritorious workers in construction. At the meeting, representatives of the Shoudu Youth Shock Brigade presented "the brigade's special medals of honor" to Li Ruihuan and Zhang Baifa. The meeting also awarded "the brigade's medal of honor" to Wang Xueli and 30 other persons who served as leaders of the shock brigades in the 1950's and who made important contributions to the construction of the capital. The meeting also named the Wang Mingang and 14 other shock brigades, which had made outstanding contributions to construction and reform in the capital, as the "Shoudu Meritorious Youth Shock Brigades." "Medals of Construction" were awarded to Li Zhihui and 38 other outstanding shock brigade leaders, young project directors, and young architects. Meanwhile, 12 shock brigades were commended for maintaining their title of pacesetter in 1993; also commended were Beijing's 200 outstanding youth shock brigades, 47 construction projects carried out by young engineers, and 44 outstanding young project directors. Leading comrades at the meeting presented medals and silk banners to shock brigade leaders of the older generation and members of the youth shock brigades. Chen Xitong and Beijing Mayor Li Qiyan addressed the meeting. Hou Jie, minister of construction, and Li Keqiang, first secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Youth League, also attended the meeting.
