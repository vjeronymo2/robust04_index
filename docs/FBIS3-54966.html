Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 3/01/1994 -- The eighth round of Moldovan-Russian negotiations on the statute, condition, and terms of the 14th Army's withdrawal will take place at the end of January in Chisinau, states the Press Secretariat of the Moldovan Ministry of Defense. According to sources at this Ministry, the previous round was suspended because Moldovan representatives insisted, among others, on compensating the 14th Army presence in Moldova. Moldovan representatives also insisted on 1 July as the final day of the Russian troops' withdrawal. Previously, the Press Secretariat of the Ministry of Defense made a protest declaration denying Stepan Kitsak's affirmations. Stepan Kitsak declared that the Moldovan Ministry of Defense took the property and equipment of the Battalion 2 Cable And Radio of the 14th Army.
