Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] A meeting of Belarusian and U.S. officials at the Defense Ministry level began in Minsk today. Delegations from the two countries will discuss a number of acute problems facing Belarus. These problems, first of all, concern difficulties Belarus is encountering in dismantling military equipment and the need to reclaim and return land that was cleared following reductions in our own troops and the withdrawal of foreign ones. According to our information, the U.S. delegation has come to Minsk with a package of proposals which can be used to adopt measures aimed at resolving the above-mentioned problems. The results of the meeting will be made known in a week's time.
