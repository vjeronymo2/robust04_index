920408 FT 08 APR 92 / UK Company News: QS QS Holdings, the clothing retailer, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 7.15m to Pounds 7.79m in the 53 weeks to January 31. Mr Marc Walters, chairman, said that turnover advanced to Pounds 45.6m (Pounds 42.3m), resulting in a virtually unchanged net margin of 17 per cent. Earnings worked through at 12.47p (11.65p) and the final dividend is 3.3p for an increased total of 4.75p (4.31p).
