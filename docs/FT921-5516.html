920304 FT 04 MAR 92 / Protests halt army pull-out in Karabakh ARMED groups and civilian protesters halted the withdrawal of Red Army troops from their base in the disputed enclave of Nagorno-Karabakh yesterday, Reuter reports from Moscow. Earlier a soldier died in sporadic shooting around the base in Stepanakert. Armenian residents say only the army can prevent an all-out Azerbaijani attack.
