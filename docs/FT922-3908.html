920609 FT 09 JUN 92 / UK Company News: United Drug moves ahead to IPounds 1.4m United Drug, the Irish pharmaceuticals distributor which obtained a full listing on the London and Dublin stock exchanges in March, reported a 15 per cent advance in pre-tax profits, from IPounds 1.22m to IPounds 1.4m (Pounds 1.28m), for the half-year to March 31. Turnover advanced 21 per cent to IPounds 36.4m. Earnings per share rose to 7.61p (7.26p) basic and to 7.36p (6.57p) fully diluted. An interim dividend of 2.05p (1.85p) is declared.
