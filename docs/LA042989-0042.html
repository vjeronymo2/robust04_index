April 29, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

A GOOD DEAL FOR MCNALL, A BETTER DEAL FOR FAN

Bruce McNall paid millions of dollars to acquire Wayne Gretzky.

The $750 or so that I paid to see the Kings this season probably wouldn't pay for a season's supply of his hockey sticks.

So why do I feel like I got the better end of the deal?

RICHARD GRAHAM

Venice
