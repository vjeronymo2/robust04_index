930825 FT 25 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Hungary helps lift Navan Navan Resources, the Dublin-based mining company, achieved pre-tax profits of IPounds 297,000 (Pounds 280,000) in the half year to June 30. The advance, from IPounds 15,000 for nine months, was on turnover of IPounds 3.1m (IPounds 1.6m) resulting from the six month contribution from the Hungarian industrial mineral operations acquired on October 1. The directors hope to acquire an additional mining operation in Hungary, bringing the number there to five operating mines.
