941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / Lottery jackpot estimated at Pounds 5m Camelot, the National Lottery operator, last night revised its estimate of Saturday's jackpot prize to Pounds 5m as ticket sales continued at an unexpectedly high level. By 4pm yesterday, the third day of ticket sales, 20m Pounds 1 tickets had been sold at more than 10,500 retail outlets. Lottery experience around the world suggests a further surge on Saturday in the hours before the first draw is made live on television.
