Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Judicial sources have revealed to our radio the names of the three Palestinians accused of assassinating Na'ib 'Umaran al-Ma'ayitah, first secretary at the Jordanian Embassy in Beirut. They are Yusuf Mahmud Sha'ban, alias Wa'il Muhammad 'Ubayd, whose name has already been announced; Bassam 'Abdallah Muhammad 'Atiyah; and Qasim Mihyu. The sources said that the accused Palestinians will be handed over for questioning to Sa'id Mirza, first investigating judge in Beirut, within the next few hours.
