August 20, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CRITICS' CHOICE: SHOW YOUR TONGUE BY GUNTER GRASS (HARCOURT BRACE JOVANOVICH: $34.95; 214 PP., ILLUSTRATE)

"The work startles both in its density of detail and in its overarching anticipation of its own self-chosen task. The spectacle of Third World Poverty -- its gaudy declaration of need -- is by now surely too familiar a Western stereotype to generate any sense of novelty, but Grass' powerful meditation explodes the stereotypical in order to address the nature of the cliches in which he may be complicit." Sara Suleri
