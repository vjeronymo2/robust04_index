BFN [Text] Cairo, 14 May (MENA) -- Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid, today received Mohamed Ali Hamed, foreign minister of the provisional Somali government. After the meeting, Hamid said that he and Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid reviewed the latest developments in Somalia and the efforts toward reconciliation. He noted that a number of ideas are currently being studied and will be submitted to Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid after the 'Id al-Adha holiday. He pointed out that the meeting also dealt with the situation in Yemen and the Arab efforts to contain the situation. He expressed regrets over the events in Yemen and the Yemenis' failure to learn from the tragic experience in Somalia.
