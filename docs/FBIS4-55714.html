BFN [Nord-Est Press report: "Russian Ultranationalist Leader Vladimir Zhirinovskiy Will Not Receive Entry Visa to Romania"] [Text] On 14 May the leadership of the PNT [National Peasant Party]-Lambru, a small political group that withdrew from the PNTCD [National Peasant Christian-Democratic Party], made public its intention to invite Russian ultranationalist leader Vladimir Zhirinovskiy to visit Romania, parliamentary sources announced. The reason for this invitation, according to the PNT-Lambru, is the fact that the Russian politician is "a person who has a thorough knowledge of the true history of Romania." Authorized sources at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs informed us a few days ago that it is out of the question that the Russian extremist can receive an entry visa to Romania. We wish to remind our readers that a few months ago the Russian leader sketched a "map of Europe" in which Romania is not included at all as a state.
