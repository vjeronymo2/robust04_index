BFN [Excerpt] Berlin (DPA) -- Norbert Geis (Christian Social Union), legal policy spokesman of the Christian Democratic Union/Christian Social Union Bundestag group, harshly criticized the amnesty of Red Army Faction terrorist Bernhard Roessner by President Richard von Weizsaecker. In an interview with the Berlin daily BERLINER ZEITUNG the Christian Social Union politician said that this could be misunderstood as a signal for another wave of politically motivated releases of terrorists from prison. Decisions about the early release of prisoners are the business of the judiciary, according to Geis. [passage omitted]
