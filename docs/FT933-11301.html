930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / Scottish poll puts Tories on 17% CONSERVATIVE support in Scotland is 17 per cent, a System 3 opinion poll for The Herald newspaper in Glasgow today shows. This is a fall of nine points since last year's general election but compares with 16 per cent last month. Labour has 45 per cent support, six points up on the election but a point down on last month. The Scottish National party has 24 per cent, and the Liberal Democrats 13 per cent.
