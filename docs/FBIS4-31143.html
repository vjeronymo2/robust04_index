BFN [Text] At 2025, chetniks from strongholds in the occupied areas of Bosnian Posavina launched a fresh attack on the town of Zupanja and the village of Bosnjaci. Several large-caliber shells caused fresh material damage, but no one was wounded. Chetniks also attacked the Zupanja Posavina area shortly before 1300. They fired a dozen shells on Zupanja and Bosnjaci.
