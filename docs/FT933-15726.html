930707 FT 07 JUL 93 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton & Jute COTTON Spot and shipment sales in Liverpool amounted to 262 tonnes for the week ended 2 July against 84 tonnes in the previous week. Improved demand brought moderate purchases mainly in Central Asian and American descriptions. Pakistani growths made some headway. JUTE July/Aug: C and F Dundee:- BTC USD315, BWC USD325, BTD USD295, BWD USD300. C and F Antwerp:- BTC USD305, BWC USD305, BTD USD280, BWD USD280.
