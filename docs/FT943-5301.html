940905 FT 05 SEP 94 / Arts (Openings): New York New York City Opera starts its autumn season at the Lincoln Center on Thursday. The main interest in the opening week is the first night on Saturday of a new production of Borodin's 'Prince Igor', not seen in New York since City Opera's last production in 1969. The cast is headed by several Russian principals, and Damian Woetzel of City Ballet will choreograph the Polovtsian Dances. The season runs till November 20.
