BFN [Text] Seditious printed materials containing the program teachings of North Korea's Kim Il-song are being distributed. The ROK police and prosecutors are investigating the source of the material. Local newspapers in the Cheju area, including HALLA ILBO, received seditious printed materials describing Kim Il-song's 10-point program for the reunification of the fatherland through facsimile transmission from Hanoi, Vietnam around midnight 24 June.
