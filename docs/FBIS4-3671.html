BFN [Text] Beijing, April 22 (XINHUA) -- Buhe, vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of China's National People's Congress (NPC), met here this afternoon with a delegation from the Culture and Arts Foundation of the Republic of Korea headed by its president Yi Song-chae. They exchanged views on issues of common interest. The five-member delegation arrived here on April 19 at the invitation of China's Ministry of Culture. They are scheduled to visit Shanghai and Xian.
