BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 14 (KCNA) -- Foreign newspapers carried "Answers to questions raised by the general director of the Latin America News Agency `PRENSA LATINA'," a famous work of the great leader President Kim Il-song. The Tunisian paper AL WAHETA carried the work with a picture of President Kim Il-song in his office room. It was carried by the Bulgarian paper TRIBUNA, the Chinese HEILONGJING DAILY, the Russian paper VETERAN, the Laotian paper PASASON and the Indonesian paper MERDEKA. The Kazakh paper LIK carried an article explaining his work "10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation for the reunification of the country".
