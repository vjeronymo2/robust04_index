920826 FT 26 AUG 92 / Reserves rise in Mexico MEXICO'S international reserves rose to Dollars 18.02bn (Pounds 9bn) as at August 21, from Dollars 17.55bn at the end of 1991, according to Mr Miguel Mancera, director-general of the Bank of Mexico, Reuter reports from Acapulco. Mr Mancera told the annual Mexican Banking Association convention that the rise reflected Mexico's improved economic outlook. Earlier this month it joined the North American free trade agreement with Canada and the US.
