940601 FT 01 JUN 94 / Office scheme for Birmingham Richardson Barberry Properties is planning the biggest speculative office building in the central business area of Birmingham since before the recession. The company is a joint venture between the Richardson brothers, the Black Country property developers who built the Merry Hill shopping centre in Dudley, and Barberry Properties of Stourbridge. It said yesterday that it had lodged a planning application with Birmingham City Council. The 22-storey building will have 200,000 sq ft of offices and will cost Pounds 75m. It is next to a similar unit by the same company which is intended for the Lord Chancellor's Department and Coopers & Lybrand, the accountancy firm.
