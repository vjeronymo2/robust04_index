BFN [Text] Moscow, 21 Apr (ITAR-TASS) -- Today Boris Yeltsin had a meeting with leaders of component Russian republics. The participants at the meeting expressed support for the prompt signing and conscientious implementation of the agreement on civic accord, and they adopted a corresponding statement to this effect. They noted the importance of constructive cooperation between all political forces and all federation subjects to solve the problems presently facing the country. The president noted that the present Constitution creates a firm basis for the implementation of all natural rights and interests of republics without detriment to the unity and prosperity of our common fatherland, the Russian Federation. Also discussed were the basic principles of the federal center's regional strategy and problems of regional policy and interregional integration. The participants at the meeting exchanged views on a number of economic issues. The president devoted special attention to the problem of mutual relations between federal and regional bodies of authority in the budgetary sphere, and specified the tasks republican leaders face in overcoming the non-payments crisis and stabilizing the economic situation. ITAR-TASS was informed of this today by the Russian president's press service.
