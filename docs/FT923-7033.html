920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / World Commodities Prices: Wool WOOL The market remains steady with very slightly firmer tendency in Australia. Currency rates for the Australian dollar are less weak than they were a few days ago but currency is certainly still a factor creating an impression of an easier tendency for buyers in most major consuming countries. Coupled with a recession which if not deepening is showing no sign of widening recovery, the wool-using sectors are shoing caution in operating even though wool is agreed to be cheap. The Australian Wool Corporation's. market indicator on August. 19 was 544c a kg. compared with 530 a week before
