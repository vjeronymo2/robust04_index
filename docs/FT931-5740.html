930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Fleming Emerging asset value up 16% Net asset value rose to 121.1p per share at Fleming Emerging Markets Investment Trust in the half year to December 31. This represented a 16 per cent advance on the 104.5p at June 30. Net revenue was Pounds 53,000 (Pounds 492,000) for earnings per share of 0.09p (0.82p).
