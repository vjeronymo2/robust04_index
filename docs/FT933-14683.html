930713 FT 13 JUL 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Mowlem (John) Construction MOWLEM (JOHN) Construction is to purchase Noreast Building Management, the Government owned PSA Building Management Organisation, for Pounds 8m, to be paid in deferred instalments. The agreement with the secretary of state for the environment also contains provisions for the department to pay up to an agreed figure for any redundancy payments which might arise within the five years after the acquisition.
