Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] With the approval of the propaganda department of the provincial party committee and of the provincial press and publication bureau, QINGDAO SHANGBAO [QINGDAO COMMERCIAL PAPER] recently formally started publication. This is a comprehensive economic paper run by the financial and trade office under the Qingdao city government. This paper will energetically propagate the principles, policies, and pertinent rules and regulations concerning economic work of the party and the state, positively explore the law of the development of market economy and the theory of market economic management, and reflect the demands and voice of consumers.
