BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, June 1 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Recently the Deputy Minister of External Relations of Mongolia J. Choinhor has visited four countries of the Southeastern Asia including Singapore, Indonesia, Philippines and Malaysia and has reached an agreement with those countries on mutual visa free exchange of citizens. According to the deputy minister, under the agreement it is stipulated that Mongolian citizens having foreign passports of any kind can stay visiting without visa Singapore and Indonesia for a two week period, Philippines three week and Malaysia one month.
