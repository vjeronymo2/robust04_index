921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Barbour Index in Pounds 0.6m acquisition Barbour Index has paid Pounds 600,000 for the business, subscription base and fixed assets of Dargan Bullivant Associates. Dargan is a partnership providing three database services on microfiche relating to the management of hospital premises. It has some 500 subscription contracts with about 250 subscribers, principally health authorities. Unaudited accounts for the year ended April 30 1992 indicated turnover of Pounds 319,000 and profits before partners' drawings and tax of Pounds 36,000.
