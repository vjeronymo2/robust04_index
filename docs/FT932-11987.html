930430 FT 30 APR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Nelson warns on regulation THE PACE of change in financial markets should not be allowed to get ahead of the ability of managers and reg-ulators to deal with it, Mr Anthony Nelson, financial secretary to the Treasury, said yesterday. Mr Nelson told a London conference: 'I share some of the CSFI's concern that at times the understanding of what is happening in the financial community is in danger of lagging behind the dynamics of the markets.'
