BFN [Text] Pretoria May 13 SAPA -- Defence Minister Joe Modise held lengthy discussions with SA National Defence Force [SANDF] head Gen Georg Meiring and Armscor [Armaments Corporation of South Africa] chief executive Tieman de Waal on Friday. A statement from the Defence Ministry said Mr Modise lauded the role played by the SANDF at President Nelson Mandela's inauguration and thanked permanent and part-time forces who reported for duty during the elections.
