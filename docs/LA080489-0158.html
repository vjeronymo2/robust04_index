August 4, 1989, Friday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

MOTHER-SON CAR RACE INJURES FIVE

A midnight motor race between a tipsy West German housewife and her son ended when the woman's car plowed into a column of marching soldiers, seriously injuring five, police said today.

The 41-year-old woman lost control of her car on a bridge near this southern town Thursday night. It hit eight soldiers, flinging some into the river. Four soldiers were in critical condition in a hospital. Neither the woman nor her 20-year-old son was hurt.
