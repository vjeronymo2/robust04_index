920220 FT 20 FEB 92 / UK Company News: ECC expands in US with Dollars 20.5m buy ECC Group, the world's largest supplier of china clay which also has interests in concrete and aggregates, is to expand in the US via a Dollars 20.5m (Pounds 11.6m) acquisition. The group has conditionally agreed to acquire the sand and gravel mineral reserves of Walt Flanagan and the business and fixed mobile plant of Flanagan Readymix of Denver, Colorado. The cash acquisition is expected to be completed by next month. Flanagan is one of Denver's leading producers of ready mixed concrete, and the mineral assets acquired consist of some 22m tons of sand and gravel reserves.
