BFN [From the "Exclusive" program] [Text] The progress report assemblies of delegates to constituents concluded in the Cruces Municipality in Cienfuegos Province with the participation of over 20,000 voters, 85 percent of the total. The 82 assemblies planned were held in this locality where 230 issues were debated, of which 212 reached solutions or answers, while 15 are to be solved with the support of the people and 18 are to be worked out with provincial and municipal organizations. Among the issues most debated during this process were the difficulties presented by water distribution, the quality of bread, garbage pick-up, and the state's distribution of food supplies. Cruces Municipality government leaders described the progress reports assemblies as positive, where each assembly was a neighborhood party. The peoples' council of (Isla de Pino-La Trocha) was the most outstanding of the seven in the locality.
