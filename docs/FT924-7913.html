921113 FT 13 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Molyneux Estates reduces deficit Molyneux Estates, the USM-quoted property investment group, cut pre-tax losses from Pounds 645,310 to Pounds 493,692 in the year to June 23. The result was after losses of Pounds 548,965 (Pounds 445,640) from the associate, Overgate Centre, and interest charges of Pounds 2.06m (Pounds 1.87m). Turnover improved to Pounds 2.27m (Pounds 1.85m). Losses per share amounted to 2.78p (3.71p) and there is no dividend (0.5p).
