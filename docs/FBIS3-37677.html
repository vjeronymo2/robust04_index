Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Kondrashov] [Excerpts] Belgrade, 20 Feb -- The Russian contingent of "Blue Helmets" arrived in Sarajevo today. A convoy of several dozen APCs [armored personnel carriers] and trucks has completed a 250-km march from near Vucovar along Bosnia's snow-bound roads and across several difficult spots in the mountains. [passage omitted] The 400 Russian" Blue Helmets" sent to Sarajevo will be stationed in Lukavica, a district in Sarajevo. As of Monday [21 February] they will conduct military patrols along the disengagement lines and in the areas where their heavy guns, which are being brought under UN control, are concentrated.
