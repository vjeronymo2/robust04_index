Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] President Borhanoddin Rabbani has expressed the confidence that peace and normalcy will return soon to Kabul. According to a wireless message received in the office of Nangarhar Shura [council] this evening, the president told a group of commanders which called on him again in the outskirts of Kabul today that he agrees to the proposal for setting up an interim government. He said he was willing to sacrifice his office in the best interest of the Afghan nation. Talking to Radio Pakistan's representative in Peshawar today, a top aide of the Afghan prime minister said that Mr. Golboddin Hekmatyar and the Supreme Coordination Council composing parties opposed to the president is prepared to work for the early return of peace and normalcy to Kabul. Meanwhile, the chief of Hezb-e Eslami [Islamic Party of Afghanistan] Khales group, Mowlvi Muhammad Younes Khales, told Afghan Islamic Press in Peshawar that leaders of all mojahedin groups should sit together to find out a solution to [word indistinct] end crisis.
