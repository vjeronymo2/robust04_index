February 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE BIG MIX

Don Snowden's article on ethnic radio failed to mention KPCC, FM 89.3. Among our ethnic broadcasts are the "Sancho" show, which celebrates Los Angeles' Mexican heritage; "Rhythm & Blues Times Capsule" and "Rhapsody in Black," which chronicle black music; "Echoes of Greece," "Polish/American Show," "German/American Show," "European Music Box," "Asian Hour" and "Ebony '89."

RENE ENGEL, KPCC

Assistant Program Director
