MARTIN LUTHER KING, JR., FEDERAL HOLIDAY COMMISSION Meeting AGENCY: Martin Luther King, Jr., Federal Holiday Commission. ACTION: Notice of postponement of meeting.

SUMMARY: In accordance with the Federal Advisory Act, Public Law 92&hyph;463, as amended, the Martin Luther King, Jr., Federal Holiday Commission announces the postponement of the Commission's Quarterly Meeting. The Commission's Quarterly meeting originally scheduled for April 22, 1994, at the Westin Bonaventure Hotel in Los Angeles, California has been postponed. This meeting will be rescheduled for late Spring, 1994. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Gerrie MacCannon, Executive Officer, Washington Office (202) 708&hyph;1005. Dated: March 28, 1994.
