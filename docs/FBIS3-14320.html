Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Magistrate Munif 'Uwaydat, the Appeal Court's public prosecutor, has affirmed that media reports on the bombing of Sayyidat al-Najat Church in Zuq Mikha'il do not originate from judicial authorities and their auxiliary security agencies. He said measures will be taken against anyone who publishes false reports to harm security and mislead the investigation. Magistrate 'Uwaydat said he will sue the AL-KHALIJ AL-'ARABI newspaper because it published a false report after identifying the individuals who fabricated such news that harms the country and the revered clergymen's reputation. He added that the paper's editions will be confiscated and measures will be taken to ban the entry of such newspapers into Lebanon.
