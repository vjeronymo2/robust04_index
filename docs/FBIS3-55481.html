Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Groznyy, 8 Jan -- An IBERIA correspondent learned that Manana Archvadze, the wife of Georgia's former president, Zviad Gamsakhurdia, said in a conversation with journalists that she had spoken with the men who had personally witnessed how Zviad Gamsakhurdia had ended his life by committing a suicide. "However sad it may be, we have a 100% proof of this fact", said Manana Archvadze-Gamsakhurdia. According to her, the body of the deceased is in a secure place and she is waiting for the right moment which will make it possible to bury Zviad Gamsakhurdia's corpse in Groznyy. Manana Archvadze refused to meet the representatives of Georgia's law enforcement bodies as, according to her, they would force her to hand over Zviad Gamsakhurdia's corpse for an examination; the results of which, Eduard Shevardnadze would use to his own advantage.
