November 30, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

PRACTICAL VIEW;

TURNING A MINUS INTO A PLUS

Have you bought a jacket for $200 one week only to see it marked down to $150 the next?

A survey of local stores reveals that you can collect the difference.

* Saks Fifth Avenue: refund of the difference between sale and regular price can be collected within three weeks of purchase.

* The Broadway: customers with receipts can collect the difference if the garment goes on sale within 10 days of the date of purchase. Neiman Marcus and I. Magnin have the same policy.

* Robinson's: refunds difference within one week of purchase.

* May Co. and The Limited refund a difference at any time. CINDY LaFAVRE YORKS
