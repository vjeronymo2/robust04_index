Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 29 Mar -- The Russian Federation Ministry of Foreign Affairs is keeping a close watch on the deteriorating situation in Algeria. A senior spokesman for the Russian Foreign Ministry told an ITAR-TASS correspondent that the Foreign Ministry is in constant contact with the Algerian side. He pointed out that the circumstances of the terrorist act against the Russian citizen are being investigated, and that exhaustive information from the Algerian side is expected on the affair.
