Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Paraguay monitored through 30 December. On 29 December Inter-American Development Bank President Enrique Iglesias signed in Asuncion a $18.6-million loan to finance a package of projects that will benefit municipalities and departmental governments. (Asuncion NOTICIAS in Spanish 30 Dec 93 p 17) The first "electrointensive" metalworking company of Paraguay run by Brazilians will begin operations in January with an initial investment of $50 million. The plant belongs to the Brazilian group Ioschpe and is located in Limpio, near Asuncion. It has the capacity to produce 60,000 metric tons per year. (Sao Paulo GAZETA MERCANTIL in Portuguese 14 Dec 93 p 11)
