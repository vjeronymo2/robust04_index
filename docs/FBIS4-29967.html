BFN [Text] A seven member Chinese military goodwill delegation led by General Li Jiulong, commander of the Chengdu Military Region of the People's Liberation Army [PLA], arrived at Yangon [Rangoon] international airport by China Airlines at 1230 this afternoon. The delegation was welcomed at Yangon airport by Lieutenant General Tin U, commander of No.1 Bureau of Special Operations of the Defense Ministry and army chief of staff; Lt. Gen. Myo Nyunt, commander of Yangon military command; Rear Admiral Tin Aye, navy chief of staff; Major General Tin Ngwe, air force chief of staff; and senior military officers; Mr. Liang Feng, ambassador of the People's republic of China [PRC]; Colonel (Li Hua Ziang), military attache; and responsible personnel. General Maung Aye, deputy commander in chief of the Defense Services and army commander in chief, received the PRC goodwill delegation led by General Li Jiulong, commander of PLA's Chengdu Military Region, at the Ministry of Defense's Dagon House at 1230 this afternoon. Also present at the meeting were Vice Admiral Than Nyunt, navy commander in chief; Lt. Gen. Thein Win, air force commander in chief; Lt. Gen. Khin Nyunt, chief of strategic studies of the ministry of defense and director of the Directorate of Defense Services Intelligence; Lt. Gen. Tin U, commander of No.1 Bureau of Special Operations of the Defense Ministry and army chief of staff; and Col. (Li Hua Ziang), PRC military attache. Lt. Gen. Tin U hosted a dinner in honor of the guests at the Defense Services Guest House in the evening.
