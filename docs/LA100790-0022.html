October 7, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

PRIMED FOR NEWS

I agree with Don Petersen of Chino (Viewers' Views, Aug. 5); KCAI's Prime 9 News is awful.

There are so many mistakes, pauses and just plain stupidity that at times it is difficult to imagine that Jerry Dunphy would be a part of such a disaster. And more important, the former team of Wendy Gordon, Tom Lawrence and Scott St. James certainly could have been integrated into the new format.

I, too, have switched to Channel 5's "News at 10" for consistency and, most of all, credibility.

Carol A. Ross, Lomita
