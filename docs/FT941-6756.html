940228 FT 28 FEB 94 / Company News (This Week): S-E Banken profits back in view - Other companies Skandinaviska Enskilda Banken, Sweden's leading commercial bank, will be out to show it is firmly back on the path to profits today, even if its 1993 figures remain in the red after the near-catastrophic losses of SKr5.4bn (Dollars 670m)in 1992. Lower interest rates and an improving economy have transformed the outlook after huge loan losses almost sank S-E Banken early last year.
