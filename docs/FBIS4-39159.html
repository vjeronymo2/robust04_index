BFN [Text] Kiev, 3 Jun [date as received] -- Lieutenant General Lev Hnatenko, the chief of armaments staff, made public new state programs for designing, manufacturing, and repairing armaments for the Armed Forces of Ukraine. The Malyshev plant in Kharkov will build most sophisticated tanks, the Antonov plant in Kiev will build aircraft, the "Arsenal" plant in Kiev and "Pivdenmash" in Dnepropetrovsk will manufacture small fire arms. Ten new naval ships are being built and modern "air-to-air" missiles are being designed.
