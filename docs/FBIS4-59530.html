BFN [Text] A visiting Arab League delegation, led by Major General Muhammad Sa'id Bayraqdar, the Arab League assistant secretary general for military affairs, this afternoon visited the site where a missile exploded. The missile was fired by the secessionist, rebellious gang of the Socialist Party last Tuesday [10 May] on a densely populated quarter of the capital, Sanaa. The Arab League delegation saw the destruction caused by the missile's explosion over the citizens' homes which fell over the heads of their residents. They also saw the damage caused to many neighboring houses. The delegation listened to citizens on the material and human losses sustained in the explosion, which claimed the lives of 23 martyrs and left dozens others injured. This is in addition to the panic caused for women, children, and old people who were asleep the moment the missile of the secessionists exploded in the capital of the unity state, targeting the lives of secure civilians.
