940614 FT 14 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Hemingway buys Hemingway Properties is to acquire the head leasehold interest in Templars Square shopping centre at Cowley, Oxford, for Pounds 16m. Templars Square, which was developed in 1965 and refurbished in 1989, consists of 87 individual units providing a total of about 240,000 sq ft of retail space. Net initial income is Pounds 1.4m a year, expected to increase to about Pounds 1.6m a year over the next three years.
