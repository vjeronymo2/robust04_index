July 18, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

IRAN HANGS 13 MORE DRUG TRAFFICKERS

Iran hanged 13 drug dealers today, labeling them "merchants of death" and bringing to 700 the number of people executed by Tehran this year for drug trafficking.

Iran launched a campaign against drugs in January with tough laws that sanctioned death for anyone caught with one ounce of heroin or 11 pounds of opium.
