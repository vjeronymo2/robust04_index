July 12, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

CHINESE PROTESTERS

In response to the false accusation made by the Los Angeles Chinese Consulate ("Chinese in L.A. Trade Allegations: Consulate's Charges of Payment to Protesters Angrily Denied," Metro, June 28), I would like to make a proposal:

Let the Los Angeles Chinese Consulate offer $100 per person to support the Chinese government for its "justified" actions. I would like to know how many participants the Consulate can get.

It is an insult to all who participated the demonstrations, including myself.

I finally realized what level of intelligence those people are at after they killed and jailed those who are better. I am really sad and ashamed, as a Chinese, to see China be ruled by people who are this mindless.

A ruler who rules a country without heart will never prevail.

TSENG HU

Northridge
