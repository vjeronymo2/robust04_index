941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / Sri Lanka prisoner exchange The Sri Lankan government and Tamil guerrillas are expected to exchange prisoners in a goodwill gesture to mark the start of talks to end the 11-year civil war, officials said. They said a peace team which flew to the northern Jaffna peninsula had got a warm welcome. Previous talks about ending the war, in which more than 30,000 people have been killed, were held either in Colombo or neighbouring India.
