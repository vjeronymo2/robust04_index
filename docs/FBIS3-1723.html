Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 1 (XINHUA) -- The fifth meeting of the Standing Committee of the Eighth National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) opened here today. The five-day meeting is to make preparations for the convention of the second session of the CPPCC National Committee which is scheduled to open March 8. CPPCC National Committee Chairman Li Ruihuan said at the opening that the current meeting is mainly to examine and discuss a work report prepared by the Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee, a government work report (draft for soliciting opinions) and the revised draft constitution of the CPPCC. The participants will also hear a report by Wei Jianxing, member of the Political Bureau and the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC), on the current fight against corruption and future plans in this respect. Chen Jinyu, deputy secretary-general of the CPPCC National Committee, gave an explanation of the work report of the Standing Committee of the CPPCC National Committee today. The report drafting group will make further revisions, in accordance with the participants' opinions and suggestions, and then submit it to the Standing Committee for approval, said Li. The report is mainly on the past one year's work of the Standing Committee and its tasks for the new year. Meanwhile, an explanation of the revised draft constitution of the CPPCC was made by Deputy Secretary-General Lu Zhichao at today's meeting. Li said that the revisions to the CPPCC Constitution have been made in accordance with the demands of CPPCC members and local CPPCC organizations, and reflect the demands of the current situation. The meeting accepted the resignation of Song Demin as Secretary-General of the CPPCC National Committee. Song has been in hospital with cerebral thrombosis since last September. Wang Zhaoguo, vice-chairman of the CPPCC National Committee, introduced a list of new members to be added to the Eighth CPPCC National Committee.
