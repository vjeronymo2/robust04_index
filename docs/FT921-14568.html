920115 FT 15 JAN 92 / Iraqi airline resumes flight An Iraqi Airways aircraft made a trial run to the southern city of Basra yesterday for the first time since the Gulf War broke out almost a year ago with the aim of resuming domestic flights, Reuter reports from Baghdad. The Iraqi News Agency quoted Mr Nouruddin Safi, the director general of the company, as saying there would be another flight to Basra today before regular flights resume tomorrow.
