Authority: 38 U.S.C. 4301, et seq., and 5 U.S.C. 8151. Subpart A_General Provisions §353.101 Scope. The rights and obligations of employees and agencies in connection with leaves of absence or restoration to duty following military service under 38 U.S.C. 4301 et seq., and restoration under 5 U.S.C. 8151 for employees who sustain compensable injuries, are subject to the provisions of this part. Subpart A covers those provisions that are common to both of the above groups of employees. Subpart B deals with provisions that apply just to military duty and subpart C covers provisions that pertain just to injured employees. Subpart D covers the appeal rights of both groups.
