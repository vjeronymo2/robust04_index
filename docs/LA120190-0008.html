December 1, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MILKEN'S SENTENCE

Judge Wood's sentence for Milken appears to be more of government vengeance than justice. Her reasoning that he needs to be made of an example of is quite unfair in view of more serious crimes that are going unpunished. Why not apply and enforce the same standards of the law against all politicians in this country who accept campaign contributions and then influence legislation to benefit their donors? Does anyone come to mind?

DON FEIN

Brentwood
