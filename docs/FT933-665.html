930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / Observer: Grain of truth Officials tending the uncrowded Brewers Society stall at the Labour party conference began to ask themselves if they had not been a shade over-zealous in fending off thirsty visitors with a grain of toasted barley. But there were no such concerns at the nearby Scotch Whisky Association stand, which was running a private book on the number of comments it received about Labour MP George Foulkes. Foulkes, it will be remembered, got into a spat with the police after drinking at one of the Association's summer parties. So far, inquiries from delegates are outnumbering requests for free samples.
