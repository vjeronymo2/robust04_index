December 1, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

FANS WILL FIGHT ON ABOUT TROJANS LOSING TO NOTRE DAME AGAIN

After suffering through another USC loss to an overrated Notre Dame team, I have come to the belief that Larry Smith is a Big Ten coach masquerading as a Pac-10 coach.

I question a game plan that does not attack the most vulnerable part of a defense. The weakest link of the Irish defense is the secondary. So what happens? Not one deep pass.

Larry Smith should realize that the lack of a deep passing game will prevent the Trojans from winning any national championship.

SIDNEY BAKER

Los Angeles
