Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The council of ministers this morning held a session chaired by Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali. During the session, Dr. al-Majali reviewed Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat's short visit to Jordan and his meeting with top officials. The prime minister said the meeting was frank and cordial and that an agreement was reached on restoring and reviving Jordanian- Palestinian coordination in a manner that is in line with the requirements of the current stage. He said the Palestinian side will send a delegation to Jordan very soon to continue the process of coordination and to agree on basic issues the two sides had previously discussed.
