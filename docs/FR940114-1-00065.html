DATES: Comments must be filed on or before February 22, 1994, and reply comments on or before March 9, 1994. 47 CFR Part 73 [MM Docket No. 93&hyph;319; RM&hyph;8404] Radio Broadcasting Services; Omak, WA AGENCY: Federal Communications Commission. ACTION: Proposed rule.

SUMMARY: The Commission requests comments on a petition filed by Omak Community Broadcasters proposing the allotment of Channel 282C2 to Omak, Washington, as that community's second local FM transmission service. Channel 282C2 can be allotted to Omak in compliance with the Commission's minimum distance separation requirements with a site restriction of 16.5 kilometers (10.2 miles) south to avoid short-spacings to vacant allotment 281B, Trail, British Columbia and Station KAFE, Channel 282C, Bellingham, Washington. The coordinates for Channel 282C2 at Omak are North Latitude 48&hyph;15&hyph;44 and West Longitude 119&hyph;31&hyph;58. Since Omak is located within 320 kilometers (200 miles) of the U.S.-Canadian border, Canadian concurrence has been requested.
