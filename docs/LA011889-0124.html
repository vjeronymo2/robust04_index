January 18, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

NEWSMAKERS

George Hughes has been named vice president of Newport Pacific Construction, Newport Beach. He will be responsible for evaluating, bidding and administering the more than $250 million in construction projects the company has in planning and construction phases. Previously, Hughes was manager of operations. Before that, he worked for John Bates Associates, Architecture & Land Planning, and was director of construction for Berger Construction Co.
