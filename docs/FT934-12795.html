931025 FT 25 OCT 93 / Observer: Sic What embarrassed Commonwealth officials have termed a 'misunderstanding' has provided an intimate glimpse into the lives of the leaders attending the organisation's biennial conference in Cyprus. Journalists paging through the official list of delegates were astonished to find copious notes on the dietary preferences and medical ailments of the heads of government. The document was hastily replaced by an expurgated version. No prizes for guessing that John Major prefers his food 'plain and simple'.
