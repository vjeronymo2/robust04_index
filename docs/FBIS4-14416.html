BFN [Text] Budapest March 31 TASS -- Russian Premier Viktor Chernomyrdin arrived in Hungary on an official visit on Thursday. At the Budapest Airport, the Russian premier was welcomed by Hungarian Foreign Ministry Secretary of State Andras Kelemen, Hungarian Ambassador to Russia Gyergy Nanovscki, Russian Ambassador to Hungary Ivan Aboymov and other officials. Chernomyrdin and members of his delegation headed from the airport for the parliament building where there will be a welcoming ceremony for the Russian high guest.
