BFN [Text] The Dutch minister for overseas development, Jan Pronk, has called for aid to be airlifted to Rwanda to provide food for the hundreds of thousands of refugees there. Mr. Pronk made his appeal shortly after visiting the Rwandan capital, Kigali. The minister called on the United States to take the lead in organizing the airlifts. He also called for the deployment of an international peacekeeping force to protect relief convoys. After his visit to Kigali, Mr. Pronk compared the situation in Rwanda with the killing fields of Cambodia, where huge numbers of Cambodia's population were slaughtered in a civil war.
