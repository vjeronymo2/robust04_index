BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- Chancellor Helmut Kohl has announced a deregulation offensive in the European Union [EU] under the German presidency in the second half of 1994. Decisions should be made in Brussels only if they have to be made there, Kohl today told a conference of the Central Federation of German Commercial Travelers' and Brokers' Associations in Bonn. At the same time, the chancellor again supported European unity. It is essential not only for the German economy. Europe is also insurance against a relapse into chauvinism, ethnic conflicts, and religious fanaticism, which are present not just in the Balkans.
