BFN [Text] Tiraspol, ROMPRES, 15/4/1994 -- Yesterday, 14 April, Igor Smirnov [Dniester leader] reported to the session of parliament of the self-proclaimed Dniester Republic the items he discussed with President Snegur in their recent meeting. Smirnov said all problems concerning relations between Chisinau and Tiraspol, except the status and the terms of withdrawal of the 14th Army, have been discussed. Negotiations on the special status of Dniester will be held on the basis of suggestions of the CSCE mission and Russian Federation, said Smirnov, mentioning that the suggestions of Russia are more favorable for the Dniester republic. He also pleaded for the Ukraine to enter negotiations, mentioning that he will continue to stand for the confederalization of Moldova. According to the declarations of the leader from Tiraspol at the mentioned meeting both leaders accepted that Moldova cannot yet have a single currency in circulation. The negotiators agreed.
