930720 FT 20 JUL 93 / British Rail to sell Devon quarry BRITISH RAIL yesterday announced that it was inviting bids for Meldon Quarry, one of the last of its non-core businesses. The quarry, on a goods-only branch line near Okehampton in Devon, supplies the stone used as ballast underneath BR's railway tracks. It has also started supplying aggregates to the general construction market. The quarry employs 25 people and had a turnover of Pounds 3m in the year to March. BR hopes to sell the quarry by the end of October. It said prospective buyers should submit bids to Lazard Brothers, its merchant bank advisers, by September 8.
