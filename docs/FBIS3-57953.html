Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Bonn (DPA) -- The Foreign Ministry in Bonn has today moved to dispel the impression that Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel would be willing to meet the right-wing extremist Russian Liberal Democrat Vladimir Zhirinovskiy. Regarding reports about comments made by Kinkel in interviews over the weekend, Foreign Ministry spokesman Martin Erdmann said the minister had only said he would not shy away from a political dialogue with Zhirinovskiy and his party. Given the election success of the right-wing extremist in Russia, this dialogue could not be avoided in the long-term. [passage omitted]
