February 25, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

AN EARLY CAMPAIGN OF CURTIS RECALLED

I have watched, with much bemusement, the current trials and tribulations of Mission Viejo Council Member Bob Curtis and the recall election instigated by the so-called "Alliance For Mission Viejo." It brought me back to my high school days when I helped Bob in his first campaign for a seat on the student council.

He hasn't changed all that much.

I have to agree with his critics that Bob can be rude, arrogant and quite adamant in his convictions. So what? History has shown us that many of our leaders have behaved similarly. It is kind of scary to think that some self-serving companies, corporations and individuals are so scared of Bob opening his mouth that almost $440,000 has been spent to silence him.

DAVE HUTCHINSON

Mission Viejo
