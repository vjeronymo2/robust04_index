941123 FT 23 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Quiligotti back in the black Quiligotti, the terrazzo floor tile manufacturer, returned to profit in the six months to September 30. The Pounds 230,000 pre-tax result compared with a Pounds 1.27m loss last time and a Pounds 2.53m loss at the year-end. Mr Ken Hodgson, chairman, attributed the turnround to the new management team, saying that it had 'paved the way for the future'. The purchase of R Cristofoli in April resulted in goodwill of Pounds 294,000. Turnover was 8 per cent ahead at Pounds 7.55m (Pounds 7.01m). Earnings per share came out at 0.59p (16.45p losses), with no interim dividend.
