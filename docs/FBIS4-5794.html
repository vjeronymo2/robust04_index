BFN [Text] Tokyo, April 14 KYODO -- Japan's imports of table-use rice to deal with a poor rice crop in 1993 topped one million tons as of Wednesday, the Food Agency reported Thursday [13 April]. It said the imports totaled 1,007,300 tons, comprising 458,200 tons from Thailand, 325,200 tons from China, 175,900 tons from the United States and 48,000 tons from Australia.
