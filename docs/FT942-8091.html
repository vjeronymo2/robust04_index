940523 FT 23 MAY 94 / World News in Brief: Eurotunnel on track for Pounds 850m rights issue Eurotunnel, operator of the Channel tunnel, is planning to launch its Pounds 850m rights issue this week, having received promises from its syndicate of 220 banks that they will provide Pounds 700m of senior bank debt. The company's financial advisers, Morgan Grenfell and Warburg in the UK and Banque Indosuez in France, are in the process of finding underwriters for Eurotunnel's biggest ever share issue.
