Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, Mar 28 (AA) -- Ten terrorists were killed in southeastern provinces during operations by security forces, it was reported on Monday [28 March]. A statement from the Regional Governor's Office said that in operations carried out in the provinces of Mus and Sirnak 10 terrorists were killed when they entered into clashes with security forces. In Mardin two members of the outlawed fundamentalist Hezbollah organization were also captured during security operations.
