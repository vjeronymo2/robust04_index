Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Cusco, Peru, 12 Jan (AFP) -- The Peruvian police have reported that at least 15 high-ranking military officers died today when a helicopter crashed at the international airport of Cusco, the former capital of the Inca empire. The helicopter was a total wreck after crashing at the end of the airport's runway, apparently, for lack of fuel, according to preliminary information, the source reported.
