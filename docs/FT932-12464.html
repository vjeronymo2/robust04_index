930428 FT 28 APR 93 / UK Company News: Leeds Building Society shows 5.2% decline Pre-tax profits at Leeds Permanent Building Society fell 5.2 per cent, from Pounds 95.1m to Pounds 90.2m, in the six months to March 31, after the society doubled its charge for bad and doubtful debts to Pounds 84.1m, against Pounds 40.8m, writes John Gapper. Leeds Permanent, which declares half-year results because it has issued permanent interest bearing shares, increased the value of its assets - mainly mortgage loans - by 12.8 per cent to Pounds 19.4bn (Pounds 17.2bn). The society achieved a 28 per cent rise in operating profits before bad debts to Pounds 174.3m (Pounds 135.9m). However, its management expenses rose by 18 per cent to Pounds 105.4m (Pounds 89.2m).
