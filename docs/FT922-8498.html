920513 FT 13 MAY 92 / UK Company News: European Motor 90% rights take-up European Motor Holdings' recent Pounds 17.4m rights issue has been accepted in respect of 15.7m shares (89.9 per cent). Some 17.5m new shares were offered at 105p per share. Subscribers for the balance of shares not taken up have been procured at a price of 129p per share.
