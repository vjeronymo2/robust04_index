940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / China may enter Gatt 'this year' Sir Leon Brittan, European trade commissioner, said he believed China could enter Gatt this year and that trade and human rights should be dealt with separately, Reuter reports from Beijing. Sir Leon was speaking after two days of talks with Chinese officials on Sino-EU economic relations. There would be complex negotiations to agree the nature of the commitments more precisely and the schedule of timing acceptable to the EU, he added.
