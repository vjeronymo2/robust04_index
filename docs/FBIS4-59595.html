BFN [Excerpt] At President Hafiz al-Asad's instructions, two Syrian airplanes carrying the third and fourth group of families of Syrian teachers working in sisterly Yemen arrived at Damascus International Airport yesterday afternoon. They were welcomed at the airport by Education Minister Muhammad Ghassan al-Halabi, Transportation Minister Dr. Mufid 'Abd-al-Karim, and senior officials from the Education and Transport Ministries and the Syrian Arab Airlines.
