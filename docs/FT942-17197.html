940406 FT 06 APR 94 / UK Company News: A Beckman Profits at A Beckman, the textile and property group, rose from Pounds 342,000 to Pounds 507,000 pre-tax for the half year ended December 31. Turnover totalled Pounds 10.4m (Pounds 5.73m). Earnings emerged at 2.9p (2.2p) and the interim dividend is a same-again 1.2p. In the opening quarter of 1994, the company experienced a collapse in retail demand for clothing produced by its UK customers, resulting in a 'significantly lower' volume of turnover. The shares fell 5p to 56p yesterday.
