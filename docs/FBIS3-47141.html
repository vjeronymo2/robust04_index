Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 8 (KCNA) -- Some 200 former soldiers of the South Korean puppet Army who had suffered injuries during the U.S. imperialists' war of aggression on Vietnam demanded compensations from seven U.S. chemical weapons manufacturers, according to a radio report from Seoul. During the Vietnam War the U.S. imperialists had committed a criminal act of spraying a leaf-drying chemical substance at random to devastate woodland that stood in the performance of their operations. Former puppet Army soldiers who had been taken to the war are still suffering from the sequelae of the chemical harmful to human body.
