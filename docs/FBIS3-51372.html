Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Guillermo Potoy and Alfredo Cesar, president and secretary of the Social Democratic Party [PSD], respectively, have decided to expel, without prior consultation, all PSD militants holding government posts as well as PSD deputies who have decided to again take up their parliamentary posts. Potoy and Cesar adopted this decision because this past Sunday, the PSD National Executive Committee issued a document calling for the election of new party authorities and backing the reforms to the Constitution. The expelled PSD members will give a news conference today.
