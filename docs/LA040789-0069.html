April 7, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

Expecting to hear the opening of Brahms' Violin Concerto, Music Director Andre Previn raised his arms Thursday to begin his morning rehearsal with the Los Angeles Philharmonic. What did he hear? The familiar strains of "Happy Birthday to You," as played by 100-plus members of the orchestra. Thursday was Previn's 60th birthday -- he was born in Berlin, April 6, 1929 -- and the date was not overlooked by his Philharmonic colleagues. At the break following the Brahms rehearsal, Previn, violin soloist Joshua Bell and the orchestra enjoyed birthday cake. ALEENE MacMINN
