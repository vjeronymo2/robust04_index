BFN [Text] The Islamic Republic's Radio and Television Corporation deputy director for foreign radio service affairs has announced the inauguration of Voice of Bosnia radio as of tonight. The aim of the new radio service is to disseminate the voice of the suffering Bosnian people to the world. The Voice of Bosnia radio will begin its broadcast as of 2300 today, Tehran time [1930 GMT], through a 500-kilowatt transmitter and on a wavelength of 31 meters, or a frequency of 9745 kHz.
