BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Temnenkov] [Text] Bonn 25 April TASS -- German Defence Minister Volker Ruehe met with his Kazakh counterpart Army General Sagadat Nurmagambetov in Bonn on Monday to discuss bilateral relations. The sides also discussed the situation in various trouble spots, nuclear and conventional arms control and prospects for Kazakhstan's accession to NATO's Partnership for Peace programme, the German Defence Ministry said in a press release. The programme of Nurmagambetov's visit which ends on Wednesday also includes a tour of several Bundeswehr units.
