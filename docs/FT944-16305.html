941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / Press Conference on Middle East peace progress held US secretary of state Warren Christopher and prime minister Yitzhak Rabin held a joint press conference on the Middle East peace mission yesterday. Mr Christopher said he was deeply concerned over the kidnap of an Israeli soldier by Moslem guerrillas that has sparked a crisis between Israel and the Palestine Liberation Organisation. PLO leader Yassir Arafat (top right), saluted a contingent of Palestinian police cadets in Gaza yesterday. Under intense pressure to act over the kidnap, he later ordered his police forces to arrest 200 Islamic militants.
