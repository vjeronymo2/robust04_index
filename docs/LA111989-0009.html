November 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

AN END TO CHARITY

In their Nov. 5 letters, Elizabeth Gaines and John Termini expressed disgust at the treatment of animals in the filming of "The Bear." I agree completely with them.

I was glad to learn from an editorial note at the end of their letters that the Fund for Animals and the Wildlife Fund had endorsed this film; I am now closing the trust funds I have established for each of these supposedly humane groups. I regret that for 30 years I have contributed generously to both.

I have been in the motion-picture industry for 40 years and have witnessed the way humane officers are dealt with on sets. The film makers simply tell him or her to "take a walk" when they plan a cruel shot, so the humane officer does not see it.

E. L. FULLERTON

Woodland Hills
