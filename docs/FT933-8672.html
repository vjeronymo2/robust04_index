930814 FT 14 AUG 93 / Afghan-Tajik border accord Tajikistan's foreign minister said yesterday that Afghanistan had promised at talks in Dushanbe to make 'maximum efforts' to prevent conflict on the troubled border, Reuter reports. 'This is a successful step forward,' Mr Rashid Alimov said after meeting Afghan Foreign Minister Hidayat Amin Arsalla in the highest-level talks since border fighting intensified last month.
