BFN [Text] The Shamgar Commission of Inquiry into the Hebron massacre held closed hearings this afternoon. It heard testimonies from the head of the Jerusalem district in the Shin Bet. Earlier Colonel Moshe Giv'ati, the former commander of Judaea, told the commission that the Army's open-fire orders were unclear, and most soldiers did not understand them. A spokesman for the prime minister said Yitzhaq Rabin has been called to testify next Wednesday [6 April], but he will also be questioned behind closed doors.
