920131 FT 31 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Guinness links with Canaries brewer Guinness offshoot Guinness Brewing Worldwide has reached an agreement to acquire 30 per cent of Sical, a Gran Canaria-based brewer. Sical, founded in 1924, produces the 'Tropical' brand. Its capital is held entirely in the Canaries with distribution throughout the archipelago.
