BFN [Text] With God's care and protection, His Majesty King Husayn today arrived in Damascus on a brief visit to the Syrian Arab Republic. Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad led those who received his majesty at Damascus International Airport. Also receiving the king were Vice Presidents 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam and Zuhayr Mashariqah, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi, the deputy prime ministers, Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar, ministers, and several senior Syrian officials, as well as Ahmad al-'Adayilah, Jordan's ambassador in Damascus, and his embassy staff. Arriving with the king were Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, the prime minister; al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, chief of the Royal Hashemite Court; and Marwan al-Qasim, his majesty the king's political adviser. After shaking hands with those who were on hand to receive him, the king, accompanied by President Hafiz al-Asad, left for the People's Palace, where talks between the two parties started. The talks are cochaired by His Majesty King Husayn and President Hafiz al-Asad.
