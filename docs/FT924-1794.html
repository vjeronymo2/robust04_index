921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Brasway more than halved at Pounds 330,000 Pre-tax profits of Brasway, the tube and hydraulic hose and fitting manufacturer, more than halved from Pounds 708,000 to Pounds 330,000 in the six months to October 31. Turnover was down 6 per cent at Pounds 16.4m compared with Pounds 17.5m. Mr Mark Swaby, chairman, said that benefits of new products should be seen in the second half, but he warned that the immediate trading outlook was still tough. The interim dividend is maintained at 0.24p, payable from earnings of 0.28p (0.62p) per share.
