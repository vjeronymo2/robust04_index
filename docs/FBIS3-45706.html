Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 24 (XINHUA) -- Chakufwa Chihana [name as received], chairman of the Allies for Democracy (AFORD) of Malawi, paid a goodwill visit to China from January 10 to 24 as guest of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship with Foreign Countries (CPAFFC). During his stay in Beijing, Chihana met respectively with Wang Zhaoguo, vice-chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee, Huan Guoying, deputy head of the International Liaison Department of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee, Li Qizhong, vice-president of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, and Han Xu, president of the host association. Chihana briefed his Chinese hosts on Malawi's economy, trade, agriculture and irrigation. He expressed his hope that China would import more tobacco from Malawi, to which China promised to pay positive consideration. Chihana also toured Chinese cities of Guangzhou, Shenzhen and Shanghai, where he visited factories, schools and rural areas.
