Language: English Article Type:BFN [By unidentified ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Dushanbe March 11 TASS -- The Tajik illegal opposition denounced today's murder of Vice-Premier Mayonsho Nazarshoyev. A statement of the Tajik opposition leaders voiced their "complete non-involvement" in the crime and said "Nazarshoyev was expected to head the Dushanbe Government delegation at negotiations with the opposition on March 16". The murder "is beneficial only to numerous mafia-criminal structures", says the statement. "We need negotiations. They will enable our supporters to return home, to Tajikistan. Negotiations is peace," notes the document.
