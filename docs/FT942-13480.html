940425 FT 25 APR 94 / Business Travel (Update): Airport traffic Flughafen Wien AG, the Vienna Airport company, says its business has picked up strongly in the first quarter of this year - despite price competition between airlines which it described as ruinous - after a difficult 1993. In 1993, passenger numbers rose by 5.3 percent to 7.2 million, while takeoffs and landings rose 9.8 percent to 117,000.
