BFN [Text] A 22-year-old Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] activist has been murdered in Bayt Lahiyah in the Gaza Strip. The man's associates claim that the murder was committed by collaborators. The victim's body was found riddled with bullets in a Bayt Lahiyah street. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan reports that the Palestinian police launched an investigation. The incident has enraged Hamas activists in the Gaza Strip.
