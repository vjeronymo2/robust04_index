921207 FT 07 DEC 92 / Names to discuss legal action MR STEPHEN MERRETT, the new deputy chairman of the council of Lloyd's of London, the insurance market, may face legal action after a meeting today of Names on his underwriting syndicate 417/418. Losses incurred by more than 4,000 Lloyd's Names on syndicate 418 in 1985 alone amount to Pounds 136.3m.
