Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 11 (XINHUA) -- U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher and his party arrived here this evening, starting his four-day official visit to China. W. Christopher came as guest of Chinese Vice-Premier and Foreign Minister Qian Qichen. Among those greeting W. Christopher and his party at the airport were Chinese Vice-Foreign Minister Tian Zengpei and U.S. Ambassador to China J. Stapleton Roy.
