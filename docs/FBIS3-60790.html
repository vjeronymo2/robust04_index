Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Carlos Enrique Villarreal] [Text] The explosion of a car bomb has caused much destruction at China de Maquinaria y Equipos del Peru, a firm located on block No. 11, East Javier Prado Avenue in La Victoria District. According to initial reports, one person was wounded -- the security guard of the firm in question. A paramedic unit of Peru's volunteer fire service are treating the guard. Many policemen are guarding the area. Approximately 60 percent of the installations of China de Maquinaria and Equipos del Peru was destroyed. In addition, nearby buildings belonging to auto dealerships were also affected. A light blue Volkswagen was used in this attack. Its license plate number is DI-8009.
