Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] According to the latest reports from the south, a Palestinian was wounded in the Israeli raid on a position belonging to the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine-General Command [PFLP-GC] on the al-Na'imah hills this afternoon. However, Palestinian sources said that the wounded person is a Lebanese civilian who happened to be driving his car in the vicinity during the raid. He was wounded in the hand. Our correspondent in al-Nabatiyah reports that the Iqlim al-Tuffah hills, particularly Mlita, 'Ayn Bu Siwar, and al-Luwayzah, came under intermittent bombardment from the Israeli positions in al-Zaffatah. The Israeli artillery emplacements on the hills of Sujud and al-Suwayda' raked opposite positions. Two Israeli helicopters overflew the hills facing the city of al-Nabatiyah.
