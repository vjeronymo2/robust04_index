BFN ["(zl)"-signed and TA SR report: "The Christian Democratic Movement Is Intimidating the Voters"] [Text] Bratislava--At a movement press briefing yesterday, Jan Carnogursky, chairman of the Christian Democratic Movement [KDH], said that, in conversations with him, his foreign partners have expressed the view that, if Meciar's Movement for a Democratic Slovakia [HZDS] wins the elections in Slovakia, the process of the Slovak Republic's integration into European structures will be slowed down. In reply to a question from SLOVENSKA REPUBLIKA on whether he thinks it is democratic for foreign states to influence Slovak voters, he said that these countries have the right of veto in admitting new members into the European Union and that we cannot prevent them from doing this. In reply to other questions from SLOVENSKA REPUBLIKA, which has obtained information that government members have increased their salaries without the agreement of parliament, he said that this is not true, and, in reply to a question on whether he thinks it is right that the government has exempted the daily NARODNA OBRODA from tax, he said that he knows nothing about it. He did not rule out the possibility of forming a postelection coalition with the Party of the Democratic Left. He said apropos of the HZDS: "A coalition with Chairman Meciar is not being taken into consideration for the KDH."
