CSO [Text] Petroleum products and flammable materials in the Siyahrud River in Qa'emshahr caused a fire on this river Friday. Mohammad 'Ali Lotfi, a fire-fighting official in Qa'emshahr, said: The petroleum products came into this river via the sewage channels from Textile Factory No. 1. He added: The fire started when someone passing by threw his cigarette flame into the river. Lotfi said: The fire immediately covered a large part of the river with 20-meter flames. He added: The fire was put out by fire-fighting officials, and the petroleum products were also cleaned out of the river.
