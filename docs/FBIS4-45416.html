Fight Drug Trafficking PA0606221394 Santa Fe de Bogota Inravision Television Canal A Network in Spanish 1800 GMT 6 Jun 94 PA0606221394 Santa Fe de Bogota Inravision Television Canal A Network Spanish BFN [From the "Cripton Newscast"] [Text] Latin American narcotics chiefs agreed to create an organization to fight the scourge of drug trafficking, which is increasingly more interconnected with another illegal business: gunrunning from Europe to Latin America. The decision was adopted at a meeting in the Dominican Republic. Here is a report by Chelo Garcia: [begin recording] Garcia: There is concern that the ties between Latin American drug traffickers and gunrunners are increasingly getting closer. They work in teams and support each other. Sophisticated long- and short-range weapons of every caliber, mostly from Europe, are flooding the Latin American black markets. The narcotics chiefs of Latin America decided to make efforts to break this deadly alliance during the drug summit held in the Dominican Republic. Colombian counternarcotics chief Colonel Leonardo Gallego: The most important things were the plans for new methods and means to strengthen this struggle against drug trafficking. Garcia: It was also proposed at the drug summit that the Latin American countries form a single bloc of support to control drug cultivation, production, processing, and trafficking. They will also work shoulder to shoulder to prevent the use of aircraft, vessels, and ground transportation to supply materials that Latin American drug traffickers need to produce drugs. [end recording]
