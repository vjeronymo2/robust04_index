November 10, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

MISSION LEAGUE

St. Paul 24, Alemany 14 -- St. Paul (5-3-2, 5-0-1) scored 21 consecutive points in the second half and claimed its first league championship since 1981.

Alemany (4-5, 3-3) scored in each of the first two quarters to take a 14-3 halftime lead at Tight end Richard Dice had four- and eight-yard scoring receptions. MIKE GLAZE
