BFN [Text] The Serbian aggressor's soldiers yesterday on two occasions opened fire on a British base near Maglaj, it was reported in the Sarajevo headquarters of the UN Protection Forces [UNPROFOR] today. The British responded with fire and there were no casualties among them, UNPROFOR spokesman Major Rob Annink announced. He confirmed that the Serb aggressor shelled Travnik and Turbe yesterday and that several aggressor grenades were also fired at Zenica.
