930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / Jobs boost seen by Teesside body THE TEESSIDE Development Corporation helped create nearly 1,000 jobs, 65 per cent of which were in manufacturing, in 1992-93, the development corporation's annual report said. The total number of jobs generated by the corporation since its inception in 1987 is 12,000. The corporation yesterday announced another new development, a Pounds 10m hotel to be built in Stockton by the Swan Group, a consortium of Norwegian companies.
