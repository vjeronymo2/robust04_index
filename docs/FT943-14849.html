940712 FT 12 JUL 94 / International Company News: CSR expansion CSR, the Australian building products and sugar group, is to spend ADollars 30m (USDollars 21.9m) on the construction and development of a raw sugar mill in the Ord River district of Western Australia, writes Nikki Tait. The mill will be capable of crushing 560,000 tonnes of cane annually, producing 70,000 tonnes of sugar.
