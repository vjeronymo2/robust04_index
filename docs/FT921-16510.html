920103 FT 03 JAN 92 / Privatisation boost reported PRIVATISATIONS in the UK account for about a third of all government sell-offs in the world, the Adam Smith Institute said in a report yesterday. The rightwing think tank said UK-based accountants, merchant banks, stockbrokers and lawyers were advising on nearly half the privatisations taking place abroad.
