941010 FT 10 OCT 94 / Observer: Speaking volumes Telephone sex with a difference. In Switzerland, the PTT telecoms company is now running a commercial for mobile telephones. In it, a young woman tells her boyfriend that, alas, she failed to reserve cinema tickets because she could not find a payphone. Another young woman, eavesdropping on this sad event, whips out her mobile phone, books the tickets and captures the man. In the next scene, as the couple emerge from the cinema, the woman raises her eyes to her hero and says . . . different things, depending on whether the ad is for the German or French speaking market. In the German version she says: 'And where will we go for a drink afterwards?' In the French it's: 'And now, your place or mine?'
