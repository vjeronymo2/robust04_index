August 5, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

HUNTINGTON PARK BEGINS YEARLONG CLEANUP DRIVE

This month the city's Chamber of Commerce will begin its Clean Up Huntington Park campaign, a yearlong effort.

The chamber, together with service clubs and businesses, will be providing more street trash cans. Each month the chamber will present certificates of recognition to contributing businesses.

The chamber is asking businesses to refrain from distributing leaflets or ads on sidewalks or in store lobbies and to pick up litter in front of and around their business.
