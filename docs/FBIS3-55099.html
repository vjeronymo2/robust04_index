Vardzelashvili; from the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] The press center of the Georgian Ministry of Defense has issued a statement in which it rejects rumors circulating in Tbilisi, as well as throughout the republic, that allegedly a split has taken place among the Georgian Armed Forces that could lead to armed conflict. The statement of the press center says that such rumors are absolutely not in accordance with reality. Nevertheless, tension is being felt in Tbilisi, particularly since the explosion in the building of the Ministry of Security. As for the general situation in the republic, measures are continuing in West Georgia to confiscate weapons from the population.
