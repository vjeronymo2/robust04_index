BFN [Text] A knowledgeable aide to Azeri President Heydar Aliyev said there were neither Russian troops nor Russian military facilities in Azerbaijan, so there was no reason for Baku to respond to President Yeltsin's order for the creation of Russian army bases in Commonwealth republics. According to the source, discussion continued of issues related to the use of the Gabalinskaya radar station still manned by Russians. Discussion focused on the possible lease of the radar station in northern Azerbaijan to the Russian military. The source stressed that the Gabalinskaya radar station was not an army base but a military facility.
