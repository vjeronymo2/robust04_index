Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 13 Jan (CRIME-PRESS) -- The Russian Internal Affairs Ministry's Public Relations Center reports that a meeting was held between Russia's Internal Affairs Minister Viktor Yerin and Moshe Shahal, Israel's minister of police and minister of energy and infrastructure, who is in Moscow on an official visit. During the conversation the problems of combating crime and the possibility of cooperation between the two countries in this sphere were touched upon. The signing of a protocol on the talks is scheduled for today.
