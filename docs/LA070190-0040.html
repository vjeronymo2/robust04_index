July 1, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ENTICING VISUAL TASTES

We are very excited about our latest review by Betty Martin in her article, "Try These Visual Tastes of Hungary and Greece," June 3.

Martin did a beautiful job, and her review of our "Eternal Greece" has sparked a great deal of response from Times' readers. We are grateful for being included in your continuing exposure of travel videos.

We also enjoyed Rich Roberts' article, "Drought Is No Deterrent to River Rafters So Far," in the same issue. In fact, your entire Travel Section was a joy to read.

DIANNE KAHM

The Traveloguer Collection

Englewood, Colo.
