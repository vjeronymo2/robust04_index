941124 FT 24 NOV 94 / Honoured members Parliamentarian of the Year trophies were awarded yesterday to back bencher of the year, Labour's Roger Berry, minister to watch Gillian Shephard, campaigner of the year, Tory Edwina Currie and member to watch, Labour's Peter Mandelson. Ulster Unionist James Molyneaux, parliamentarian of the year, was unable to attend
