920826 FT 26 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Needler cuts interim loss to CDollars 1.5m Needler Group, the USM-quoted Canadian building materials company, turned in a reduced pre-tax profit of CDollars 1.39m (Pounds 610,000) for the second quarter to June 30, down from CDollars 1.46m for the comparable period last year. Sales for the quarter were ahead at CDollars 13.1m (CDollars 12.5m). Earnings per share increased to 4.9 cents (4.3 cents). This brings the loss for the half year to CDollars 1.5m against Dollars 2.02m. Sales improved from CDollars 17.4m to CDollars 18.6m. Losses per share came out at 5.6 cents (8.2 cents) and the interim dividend is again 2.5 cents.
