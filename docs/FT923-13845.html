920710 FT 10 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Stewart & Wight Stewart & Wight, the property investment group, reported pre-tax profits ahead from Pounds 310,303 to Pounds 351,834 over the 12 months to March 31. The outcome reflected record rental income of Pounds 400,290 (Pounds 330,979). Earnings per Pounds 1 share advanced from 263.76p to 299.04p and the single dividend for the year is raised to a proposed 120p (105p). An extraordinary profit of Pounds 72,277 related to the sale of a property in south London. The net asset value per share amounted to Pounds 50.25.
