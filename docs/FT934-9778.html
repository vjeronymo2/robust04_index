931109 FT 09 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Adam & Harvey Profits of Adam & Harvey, the international distribution and steel stockholding group, rose from a depressed Pounds 2.49m to Pounds 2.55m pre-tax for the half year ended September 30. Turnover improved from Pounds 20.23m to Pounds 21.32m. Earnings per share rose by 4.5p to 34.5p and the interim dividend is being lifted by 1p to 6p.
