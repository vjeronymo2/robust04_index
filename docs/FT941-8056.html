940222 FT 22 FEB 94 / Russian gas sell-off The Russian government is to sell up to 45 per cent of the shares of the giant gas-producing monopoly, Gasprom, to the company's employees, while keeping a 40 per cent stake for itself for three years, Interfax news agency said yesterday, Leyla Boulton writes from Moscow. Another 5 per cent will be offered to ethnic minorities of the gas rich Yamal Nenetsk peninsula, while 10 per cent will be kept by Gasprom itself.
