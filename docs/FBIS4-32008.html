BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES, 16/6/1994 -- The Romanian parliamentary delegation, headed by Adrian Nastase, speaker of the Chamber of Deputies, returned on 15 June from an official three-day visit to the Czech Republic. On arrival at Otopeni Airport, Speaker Nastase told the press that the Prague visit was a "success." "We have felt there much warmth and a great readiness for revigorating the political, economic and cultural relations between the two countries." Also, Adrian Nastase assessed as "encouraging" the messages got on the occasion, referring in particular to the Czech standpoint according to which Romania has to become member of the "Central-European initiative." "These aspects, associated with the visit President Vaclav Havel will pay the next few days to Bucharest, prove a real will at this moment for an intensification of the bilateral relations", Adrian Nastase stated.
