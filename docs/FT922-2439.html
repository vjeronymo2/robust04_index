920617 FT 17 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Harland Simon sells Vickerys for Pounds 8.1m Harland Simon Group is to sell its Vickerys division to Thermo Fibertek for Pounds 8.1m cash. Thermo Fibertek is a subsidiary of Thermo Electron. the US-based mechanical engineering company. The disposal will enable Harland Simon to reduce borrowings and concentrate on its main activities, electronic and electrical engineering.
