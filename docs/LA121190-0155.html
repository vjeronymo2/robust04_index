December 11, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

NATION;

7 KILLED, 50 HURT IN TENN. PILEUP

At least seven people were reported killed and 50 injured in a 50-vehicle pileup this morning on a fogbound Interstate 75.

Wrecked cars, some of them burning, littered a five-mile stretch of the Knoxville-to-Chattanooga interstate hours after the 9:30 a.m. accident.

Some troopers responding to the massive pileup were overcome by fumes from a burning truck.
