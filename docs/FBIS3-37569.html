Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Yuriy Shatalov report: "Aleksandrov Presents"] [Text] Regular broadcasting from a local television studio has begun in Aleksandrov in Vladimir Oblast. The studio was founded by the city administration. The Aleksandrov Radio Plant, the largest in Central Russia, provided the equipoment, apparatus, instruments, and editorial building. The power of the transmitter enables the television signal to carry over a distance of around 10 km. The local studio's programs will be watched not only by residents in the city of Aleksandrov, but also in outlying villages and hamlets. For the time being the studio is to air twice a week, but in the future there will be daily broadcasts, Aleksandr Shornikov, head of the city's television studio, remarked.
