Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow March 16 TASS -- State Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin has denied media allegations that he had discussed the introduction of the economic state of emergency in the country with Russian officials. "I have not had any discussions of this subject with anyone," Ivan Rybkin said at today's plenary meeting of the State Duma, responding to a question from one of the deputies.
