November 7, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Colgate to Acquire Vipont: Colgate-Palmolive Co. will acquire Vipont Pharmaceutical Inc. for about $94 million, enabling Colgate to strengthen its oral hygiene product line, the companies announced. The merger agreement calls for the Ft. Collins, Colo.-based Vipont to sell Colgate all of its outstanding common shares for $14 each. Vipont also will spin off to its shareholders on Dec. 1 a total of 65% of the outstanding shares of Vipont Research Labs Inc., Vipont's research subsidiary.
