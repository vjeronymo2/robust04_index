940812 FT 12 AUG 94 / Barclaycard to build Pounds 45m HQ Barclaycard, the credit-card issuer owned by Barclays Bank, is to build a Pounds 45m headquarters at the Northampton Business Park. The headquarters will bring together staff split between three leased buildings in the town centre. Barclaycard's 2,600 employees make it Northampton's largest employer.
