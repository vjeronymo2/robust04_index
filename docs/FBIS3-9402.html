Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgian Foreign Minister Willy Claes arrived in Prague about an hour ago. More from our reporter Milan Repka at the airport: [Repka] Willy Claes, vice prime minister of the Belgian Government, foreign minister, and a member of the Flemish Socialist Party, said in an interview for Czech Radio that this is his first visit to the Czech Republic and that the main points of his agenda will deal with matters related to the Czech Republic's incorporation into the European Union as well as the crisis situation in former Yugoslavia and bilateral cooperation. Claes is paying a two-day official visit to Prague.
