BFN [Text] Barinas, Venezuela, 25 May (AFP) -- A mass transit strike today paralyzed the city of Barinas, 420 km southwest of Caracas, where student disturbances were reported even though the National Guard had occupied the city since early morning, according to the national news agency VENPRES. Groups of students set several vehicles on fire and erected barricades at strategic points in the city to protest an unauthorized fare hike and a failure to apply reduced student bus fares. Police spokesmen told AFP that as of noon, there were no reports of injuries.
