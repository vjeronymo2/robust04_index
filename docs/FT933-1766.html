930922 FT 22 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Ivernia reports reduced loss Ivernia West, the Irish minerals exploration company, announced a reduced loss for the year of IPounds 460,000 (Pounds 433,000), down from IPounds 1.48m, and confirmed its belief that the Lisheen joint venture, in which it has a 47.5 per cent interest, was the largest lead/zinc discovery in Europe for decades. Feasibility and environmental studies at Lisheen are nearing completion.
