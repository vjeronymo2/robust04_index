October 21, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

COMMENTS ON THE TIMES' NEW FORMAT

Friends, I just got through reading today's (Oct. 11) Times, which I have subscribed to for about 25 years. I was thoroughly disappointed with your new concept of publication.

Why a really great newspaper like The Times would want to mimic a publication like USA Today is beyond my sense of belief.

Your second page Top of Today's News will only discourage many people from reading your paper in depth. I will never buy another USA Today and if The Times continues to fall to that level I will certainly discontinue my subscription to The Times.

MICHAEL PISCITELLI

Temple City
