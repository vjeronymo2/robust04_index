940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / Pounds 2.16bn capital issues in August New capital issues worth Pounds 2.16bn were announced by the financial and corporate sector last month, with a total of Pounds 851m denominated in sterling, Bank of England figures showed yesterday. Gross issues by UK borrowers totalled Pounds 2.52m, the Bank said. After redemption net issues totalled Pounds 1.59bn. The largest issue announced during the month was for Aircraft Lease Portfolio Securitisation for Dollars 999m in nine tranches of fixed-rate bonds and floating-rate notes. The biggest share issue was by Kleinwort European Privatisation Investment Trust for Pounds 200m. It was the last instalment on the issue announced in January. Halifax Building Society and HMC Mortgage Notes 12 announced floating-rate notes of Dollars 500m and Pounds 210m respectively. The Bank said fixed-rate bond issues were also made last month by British Telecom Finance for Pounds 300m and Dollars 375m, and by Halifax Building society and European Investment Bank for Pounds 200m each. These had all been announced in July.
