BFN [Text] Charges have been brought against two high-ranking officials of the Federal Intelligence Service [BND]. The State Prosecutor's Office in Hamburg today confirmed a report on the ARD news magazine "Panorama," which will be broadcast after this news bulletin. The charge is violation of the law controlling the export of war weapons. The two allegedly attempted in 1991 to ship two antiaircraft tanks of the NVA [former GDR's National People's Army] to Israel declaring them as agricultural machinery.
