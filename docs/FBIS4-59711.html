BFN [Text] The prime minister's bureau reports that Knesset Member [MK] Eli Dayan, the Labor caucus chairman, has not been authorized to negotiate with the PLO representatives he conferred with at a Vienna gathering. The talks held by Dayan and the other MK's attending the gathering are being held on their own initiative, the announcement by the prime minister's bureau said. Our correspondent Shlomo Raz reports that the seminar Dayan and the other Labor MK's are attending is discussing a permanent settlement between Israel and the Palestinians, among other issues.
