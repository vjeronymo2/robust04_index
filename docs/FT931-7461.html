930224 FT 24 FEB 93 / UK Company News: River/Merc Smaller Net asset value at River & Mercantile Smaller Companies Trust was 116.75p per share at January 31, against 107.7p a year earlier and 99.27p at July 31 1992. Available revenue for the six months to end-January amounted to Pounds 443,000 (Pounds 254,000) for earnings of 1.7p (1.01p) per share. The interim dividend is held at 1.25p.
