920805 FT 05 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Law Debenture asset value ahead Law Debenture Corporation had a net asset value per share of 444.8p at June 30 - ahead from the 426p value a year earlier and 426.3p at December 31. For the six-month period net revenue rose to Pounds 2.11m (Pounds 1.82m) for earnings of 9.26p (7.98p). The interim dividend is lifted to 6.25p (6p). Directors said they hoped the final would be no less than the 11.5p paid last time.
