April 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

UNFAIR PROPERTY TAX BASE

So the grandchildren aren't living as well as the grandparents -- what's wrong with that?

Today's "children" want it all, now. Mom and dad did not start out with what they now have. They worked hard for it.

Their children want $300,000-$400,000 homes with a BMW in the driveway, not what Ms. Goodman calls "affordable" housing.

Great, let's get rid of Proposition 13 and tax old mom and dad right out of their home. After all, if the kids can't live in the manner in which they are accustomed why should the old folks?

KATHLEEN STANLEY

Agoura Hills
