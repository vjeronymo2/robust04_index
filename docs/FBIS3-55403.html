Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [ITAR-TASS correspondent Grigoriy Arslanov, Vyacheslav Tomilin] [Excerpt] Beijing, 6 January -- Ivan Korotchenya, the CIS executive secretary, has arrived in Beijing today on a week-long visit. Although the high-ranking representative of the Commonwealth is visiting the PRC at the invitation of the nongovernmental Chinese People's Diplomacy Association, he will hold talks with Tian Zengpei, Chinese first vice minister of foreign affairs, as well as official meetings with a number of other Chinese statesmen. Issues relating to the situation in the CIS will be in the focus of their attention, together with the prospects of developing cooperation between China and the Commonwealth countries. In addition to Beijing, he will also visit the city of Guangzhou and the special economic zone of Shenzhen in the south of the country where results of the economic reform are especially noticeable.
