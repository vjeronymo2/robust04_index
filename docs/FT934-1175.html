931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / Warning over fall in number of nurses The number of student nurses has fallen by 35 per cent since 1983 and the government's plans for a further cut of 20 per cent by 1997 will put increased pressure on qualified nurses and place patients at risk, a report by the Royal College of Nursing says. The research, based on government statistics, found that there are 56,870 nursing students now, compared with 87,500 a decade ago.
