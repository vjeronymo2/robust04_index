August 28, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Chrysler Workers Vote: Members of United Auto Workers local 110 at a Chrysler Corp. plant in Fenton, Mo., have voted to allow the union to start bargaining with the company over a four-day work week, the union said. If the plan is approved, the Fenton mini-van assembly facility would become the first U.S. Chrysler plant to operate on such a system. The plant now runs five days a week on two shifts.
