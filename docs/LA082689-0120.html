August 26, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

TITANS, COSMOS PLAY TO TIE IN EXHIBITION

The Cal State Fullerton men's soccer team and the Cosmos soccer club played to a 1-1 tie Friday in an exhibition match at Titan Field.

The Titans scored first on James Griffin's penalty kick, 18 minutes into the game. Robert Navarro scored for the Cosmos at the 42-minute mark on an assist by Oswaldo Rodriguez.
