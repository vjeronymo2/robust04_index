940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / China clay closure costs 230 jobs ECC International Europe is to shed 230 jobs at its china clay operations in Cornwall and Devon. Most of the job losses will be at St Austell, Cornwall, where the unemployment rate is already 14.4 per cent. The company said 130 white-collar jobs and 100 manual jobs would be lost. It was seeking voluntary severance but could not rule out compulsory redundancies.
