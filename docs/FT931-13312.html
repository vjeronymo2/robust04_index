930123 FT 23 JAN 93 / International Company News: ENI to sell coal production unit ENTE Nazionale Idrocarburi, the Italian state energy group, has put Agip Coal, its coal production subsidiary, up for sale, Mr Franco Bernabe, the ENI managing director, said yesterday, AP-DJ reports from Milan. Agip Coal, with a turnover of L550bn (Dollars 369m) in 1992, has mining operations in the US, Australia and continental Europe. The Italian government is moving towards privatisation of ENI and has announced its intention to sell Nuovo Pignone, the mechanical engineering subsidiary Earlier this month, the government asked ENI to draft a proposal for a stock market listing of its Agip and Snam oil and gas subsidiaries by the end of March.
