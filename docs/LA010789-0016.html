January 7, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BAN AGAINST AMERICAN BEEF IN EUROPE

European countries care enough about good health to ban hormone-treated beef. No one knows how rising U.S. rates for cancer and other illnesses are affected by a lifetime diet of beef and chicken treated with hormones and antibiotics.

Meantime, small businesses specializing in imports must now close up shop, thanks to new U.S. tariffs.

Why build up all this ill will?

Our government should immediately ban hormones and antibiotics added to meats -- a gesture of good will to our own people and to Europe.

RUTH LORING

Calabasas
