Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Report by Olena Yehorova under the ``Life Details'' rubric: ``Drug Trafficking Increase?'' -- first paragraph published in boldface] [Text] According to the news center of the State Customs Committee of Ukraine, between 4 and 10 December alone, customs services confiscated over 25 kg of marijuana (not including other drugs). Over 11 months of this year, only 28.5 kg of marijuana were seized. It seems that the worst prognoses begin to be corroborated. During the same week, 518 actions against smugglers and violators of customs regulations were brought to court; customs officers confiscated foreign currencies worth over 1.5 million karbovanetses, precious metals worth 283 million karbovantses, and nearly 50 million Russian rubles.
