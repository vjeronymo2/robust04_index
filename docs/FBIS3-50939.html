Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina is on an official visit to Ghana. Upon arrival, he was greeted by Ghanaian Foreign Minister Obed Asamoah. In Ghana the Cuban foreign minister begins his five-country tour that will include Angola, Zimbabwe, Zambia, and Namibia. During his 48-hour stay in Ghana, Foreign Minister Robaina will meet with Ghanaian President Jerry John Rawlings and other high-ranking Ghanaian officials. Robaina also will meet with members of various friendship organizations as well as with the Cuban ambassadors to Cape Verde, Guinea, Guinea-Bissau, Nigeria, Benin, and Burkina Faso.
