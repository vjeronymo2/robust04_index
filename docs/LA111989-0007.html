November 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

PRUNES EXHUMED

Re Richard Cromelin's Nov. 5 Introducing column: In discussing the band Gavin Friday and the Man Seezer, Cromelin implied that the Virgin Prunes group was no longer in existence. Mary D'Nellan, a member of the Virgin Prunes, wrote to us on Oct. 3: "We've been recording in Dublin all summer -- our new record is coming out in Europe next week. . . . It's called "Nada" and we're all very proud of it. . . ."

For a band about which so little information is available, a bit of misinformation in a newspaper as widely read as The Times can be very harmful.

ARIEL C. ROSENTHAL

Claremont

JENNIFER H. SIMPSON

Upland
