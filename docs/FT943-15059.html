940712 FT 12 JUL 94 / Nepali elections called Nepal's King Birendra has dissolved parliament and announced elections for November 13, Reuter reports from Kathmandu. The king earlier consulted constitutional experts as politicians pressed him to ignore a demand for early elections made by Mr Girija Prasad Koirala, outgoing prime minister. Mr Koirala resigned on Sunday after his party lost a vote in parliament.
