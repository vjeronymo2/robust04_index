March 25, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

TOO BUSY FOR WORDS

In regard to the March 8 edition of "48 Hours," I was depressed, maddened and confused to see those various episodes of people so busy, busy, busy.

Get it done! Let's move in the fast lane! Gotta stay on the go-that's the name of the game in this day and age! All very efficient.

So much so that their children are ignored. "Well, the kids have a busy schedule too."

OK. But where and when does all this busy efficiency filter down to the common folk? Every time I call someone, I get a recording saying they're busy, "but we'll get right back to you" -- rarely is that the case. A letter mailed with an enclosed self-addressed, stamped envelope is seldom returned. I have an automobile accident (not my fault), and it takes weeks just for an insurance man to look at it.

Just what is being done by these movers and shakers?

William W. Campbell, Glendale
