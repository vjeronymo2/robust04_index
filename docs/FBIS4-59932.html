BFN [Excerpt] Amman, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- In a statement to AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT, Jordanian Deputy Prime Minister Dr. Ma'n Abu-Nuwar has denied that Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali submitted his resignation to the Jordanian monarch, as claimed by some local papers recently. He said that the repetition of such rumors is a matter of speculation by some people, and that the decision in this connection is up to the king. Abu-Nuwar did not rule out the possibility of a cabinet reshuffle after 'Id al-Adha, saying that: "The reshuffle is up to the prime minister. That is possible if he sees a need for it."
