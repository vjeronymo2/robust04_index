Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] After refusing to continue to fight for greater influence within the PMDB [Brazilian Democratic Movement Party], of which he was a founding member, Senator Nelson Carneiro yesterday joined the Progressive Party [PP]. According to PP sources, 200 other people have joined the PP, including Councilwoman Laura Carneiro, who is Senator Carneiro's daughter. Carneiro will be allowed to nominate 21 candidates for the PP ticket in Rio de Janeiro; that is, 15 candidates for state deputies, and six candidates for the Federal Chamber of Deputies.
