BFN [Text] In Hebron, 11 residents were wounded in incidents this afternoon. A group of settlers was pelted with rocks, and one of them opened fire. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that, according to various reports, three Palestinian residents were apparently injured by the shooting. Another eight were injured when the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] moved in to put out riots which followed the shooting incident in Hebron. One of the wounded is in grave condition, two suffer medium wounds, and the rest were lightly wounded. Representatives of the Temporary International Presence in Hebron arrived at the site of the incident and began collecting evidence from the Arab residents of Hebron. The Jews did not testify about the incident. Also, the IDF has not yet detained any settler for questioning, but an IDF unit is on its way to the Shavey Hevron Yeshiva to investigate which of the settlers fired the shots.
