941029 FT 29 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Bula Resources Bula Resources, the Dublin-based oil exploration and production company, is raising a minimum IPounds 6m (Pounds 5.9m) through a placing and open offer to fund the purchase of an option to acquire 51 per cent of Aki-Otyr from the Russian Corporation. Davy Corporate Finance is placing, mainly with institutional investors, 80m new ordinary shares at 2 1/2 p to raise IPounds 2m. The offer to shareholders, of 500m new ordinary shares, is on a 2-for-3 basis.
