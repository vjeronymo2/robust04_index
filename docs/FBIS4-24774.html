BFN [Text] Bangkok, June 2 (XINHUA) -- Gen Charun Phunsanong, chief of staff of the Royal Thai Armed Forces, left here today on a week-long visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese Defense Ministry. This is Gen Charun's first visit to China since taking office as chief of staff in October last year. It is disclosed that Gen Charun will first visit Shanghai, the biggest city in China, and will then visit Beijing. During his visit, Gen Charun will hold talks with Chinese military leaders over bilateral cooperation.
