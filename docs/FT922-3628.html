920610 FT 10 JUN 92 / Engineers report rise in optimism ENGINEERING companies in Scotland reported a modest rise in optimism yesterday. The intake of orders and capital investment had increased, with the electronics industry and medium-sized engineering companies reporting growth while heavy engineering had a drop in orders, James Buxton writes. Scottish Engineering, which published its quarterly survey for the three months to June of its 300 members as well as some electronics companies that are not members, said the survey showed a 'positive move out of the depths of recession across the industry'.
