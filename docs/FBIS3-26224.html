Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Reporter Yang Zhaobo (2254 0340 3134) and correspondent Wu Lebin (0702 2867 2430)] [Excerpt] Beijing, 29 Dec (XINHUA) -- The 1994 Work Conference of the Chinese Academy of Sciences closed today. Li Peng, premier of the State Council, sent a congratulatory letter. The congratulatory letter states: Basically speaking, we need to rely on scientific and technological progress to realize our country's socialist modernization. The broad mass of scientists and technical workers shoulder a glorious yet arduous historical mission. It is my earnest hope that the broad mass of scientists and technical workers will, following the close of the conference, further implement the guidelines set forth at the 14th CPC National Congress and at the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee; deepen reform of the scientific and technical structure; constantly promote scientific and technical progress in line with the scientific and technological development trend in the world and in integrating with the reality in our country; and make even greater contributions to the sustained, rapid, and healthy development of our country's economy. Li Peng also states the following in his letter: As the Spring Festival, the traditional festival of the Chinese people, is approaching, I would like to wish you in advance a happy new year. Through you, I would also like to wish a happy new year in advance to all comrades throughout the nation who work hard on the scientific and technological front. [passage omitted]
