BFN [Excerpt] Moscow, April 7 (A.A.) -- Turkey and Russia on Wednesday signed a protocol during a joint economic commission meeting concerning energy, transportation, communications and other fields. The signatories of the protocol were Housing and Public Works Minister Onur Kumbaracibasi and Russian Economic Affairs Minister Oleg Davydov. The protocol foresees improving cooperation in transportation, communication and other fields. Kumbaracibasi speaking during the signing ceremony said the two sides reviewed the subjects in a positive atmosphere. Kumbaracibasi said Turkish-Russian relations improve day-by-day and these two sides have the goodwill to overcome the existing problems in economic fields. Davydov said with the protocol, trade volume between Turkey and Russia will reach 2 billion U.S. dollars. 'Turkey will continue to be our best trade partner,' Davydov said. [passage omitted]
