BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow June 15 TASS -- Russian Parliament Speaker Ivan Rybkin left for Germany on an official visit on Wednesday at the invitation of the Bundestag chairwoman Rita Suessmuth. During the four-day visit the Russian delegation led by Rybkin plans to study the activity of the German parliament and exchange views on the development of interparliamentary relations between the two countries. The agenda of the visit also includes meetings and talks with members of the German Government, the leaders of the leading political parties' factions in the German parliament. Russian parliamentarians are also expected to visit one of the units of the western group of Russian troops.
