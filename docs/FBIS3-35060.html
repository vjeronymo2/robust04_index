Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The city of Karkuk, the center of the al-Ta'mim Governorate, experienced one of Ramadan's happiest days today when leader President Saddam Husayn, may God preserve him, paid an auspicious visit to the city. When his excellency arrived at the governorship, he was welcomed by al-Ta'mim Governor Hisham Sabah al-Fakhri. Following a brief respite, the leader president toured the city. During the tour, he visited a residential district, amid citizens' happiness, welcome, and chants. His excellency inspected a citizen's house and inquired about his conditions. His excellency also listened to a number of citizens as they explained their problems, and instructed that these problems be resolved. Afterward, the leader president, may God preserve him, accompanied by the al-Ta'mim governor, toured a number of markets in the governorate. He inspected some private shops. Wherever the leader president arrived, the masses assembled and shouted: We Sacrifice Our Souls and Blood for You, Saddam. You Are Most Welcome. During the tour, the leader president waved his noble arm to greet the masses who rallied around him. Afterward, the leader president, may God protect him, left the city. He was seen off by comrade Khadr 'Abd-al-'Aziz Husayn, member of the Iraq Command, and Hisham Sabah al-Fakhri, al-Ta'mim governor.
