940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / Move to win Sudan peace Sudan's government and rebels were in Nairobi yesterday at the start of a fresh initiative to try to end 11 years of civil war, Reuter reports from Nairobi. The presidents of Kenya, Uganda and Ethiopia and the foreign minister of Eritrea, co-sponsors of the initiative, met Sudanese leader Omar Hassan al-Bashir, who then left without seeing the rebel leaders.
