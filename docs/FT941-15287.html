940115 FT 15 JAN 94 / Markets: UK investment trusts launch - At a glance Schroder and Gartmore are both launching UK growth investment trusts next month, aiming at Pep investors as the tax year-end draws near. The Schroder UK Growth Fund fund will have ordinary shares with one free warrant attached to every five ordinary shares. Gartmore's British Income and Growth Trust, is a split capital investment trust, with two classes of share.
