April 17, 1990, Tuesday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

George Foreman, who scored his first pro victory in 1969 when Mike Tyson was 2, will continue his boxing comeback against one of Tyson's sparring partners.

Foreman, who hopes to fight Tyson, is 65-2 with 61 knockouts in a career that began 21 years ago. He will face Mike Jameson, who is 17-14 with six knockouts, in a scheduled 10-round fight at Caesars Tahoe.
