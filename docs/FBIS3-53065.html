Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Muhammad Tawfiq Rahim, minister of humanitarian aid and cooperation, has received Mr. Stephen (Shamal), U.S. State Department official responsible for Iraqi affairs, and Mrs. Barbara Schell, political adviser to the coalition forces at Incirlik Air Base in Turkey. The discussion during the meeting dealt with the current economic, political, and social situation in Kurdistan as well as the living conditions of the people in the region. During the one-hour meeting, the two parties emphasized the need to defend and safeguard the democratic experience in Kurdistan.
