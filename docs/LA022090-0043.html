February 20, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO INDUSTRY NOTES

The Price Co. reported that it has acquired 500,000 shares, or about 1%, of its common stock outstanding as of Dec. 24, the close of the company's first quarter of the current fiscal year. The company paid an average of about $39 per share. The stock repurchase program was announced in February, 1989.
