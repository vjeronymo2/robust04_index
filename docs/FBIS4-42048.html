BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Oleg Kuzmin] [Text] Tehran June 29 TASS -- President of Azerbaijan Geydar Aliyev arrived in the Iranian capital Tehran on a four-day official visit today. At Tehran's Mehrabad airport, Aliyev told journalists that "Azerbaijan regards Iran as a powerful and influential neighbour, and is ready to develop cooperation with it in all spheres." Aliyev said he will hold talks with Iranian President 'Ali Akbar- Rafsanjani and other Iranian leaders to discuss issues of regional and world policy as well as prospects for expanding bilateral relations between the two countries. Receiving Aliyev, Hashemi-Rafsanjani emphasised the historical, religious and ethnic closeness of the two people. The Iranian head of state noted that many accords, which were achieved during the Iranian president's visit to Baku last year, are in the stage of implementation, and the remaining will require some time to be put into life. The Iranian president also expressed concern over the unabated bloodshed in the Trans-Caucasus region.
