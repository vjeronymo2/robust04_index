BFN [Text] The Greek Foreign Ministry described a Macedonian News Agency [based in Thessaloniki] dispatch from Washington that says the ministry promotes the idea of a more loose interception of Turkish planes that violate Greek airspace and that at a certain time missiles will be taken off our planes as totally untrue. The Foreign Ministry also noted: As it has been very clearly stressed in the government's policy statement and as Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias has repeatedly stressed, Greece's firm and unmoved position is the defense of our national sovereignty and the complete exercise of the right of defense with all necessary means.
