December 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $30 when dream Traveler, the front horse in his parlay, ran out of the money. Pretender's Gold, the other half of the parlay, finished second. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $830.30.

For today, Bob's bet is a $60 parlay from Ortega in the sixth to Stephen's Sooner in the seventh.

Saturday's Bankroll: $860.30

Saturday's Loss: $30.00

Current Bankroll: $830.30
