BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 14 (KCNA) -- Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK], Wednesday met and had a friendly conversation with a delegation of the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China led by its Secretary Gu Jinchi. On hand were Kim Yang-kon, vice-director of a department of the WPK Central Committee, and Qiao Zonghuai, Chinese ambassador to Korea.
