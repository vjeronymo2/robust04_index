[Ex Parte No. MC&hyph;206] Revision to Accounting and Reporting Requirements for Motor Carriers of Property AGENCY: Interstate Commerce Commission. ACTION: Decision.

SUMMARY: The Commission is modifying Annual Report Form M&hyph;2 (Form M&hyph;2) for class II common and contract motor carriers of property. The revised Form M&hyph;2 is set forth below as an appendix. The intent of these changes is to provide the Commission with sufficient data from these carriers so as to permit it to fully carry out its regulatory oversight functions. EFFECTIVE DATES: The modifications to the Form M&hyph;2 are effective October 25, 1994. Use of the Form M&hyph;2 will commence with the reporting year beginning January 1, 1994.
