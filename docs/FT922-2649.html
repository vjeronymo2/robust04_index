920616 FT 16 JUN 92 / International Company News: Oriental Holdings sees lower profits ORIENTAL Holdings, which assembles and sells Honda Motor cars in Malaysia, said it expected turnover and profit for 1992 to be lower than last year's, due to lower car sales, Reuter reports from Kuala Lumpur. Oriental recorded a pre-tax profit of MDollars 254.1m (USDollars 101.2m) for 1991 on turnover of MDollars 1.25bn.
