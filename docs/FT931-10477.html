930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Butte Mining losses double to Pounds 13.5m BUTTE MINING, which is claiming damages of at least Dollars 325m (Pounds 214m) in the US against more than 70 former directors, advisers and investors, saw losses before tax for the year to June 30 nearly double from Pounds 7m to Pounds 13.5m, writes Kenneth Gooding. The latest figures included an exceptional Pounds 8.7m loss from writing down all assets to 'values that should be realisable in today's economic climate,' said Mr David Lloyd-Jacob, chairman. He said that the new management team had investigated circumstances surrounding two acquisitions in Montana and the purchase of Gramcol-Zircon in the UK. 'Serious deficiencies were discovered,' he said. Litigation and warranty claims have been started and information passed to the Serious Fraud Office, 'which is investigating all three transactions.'
