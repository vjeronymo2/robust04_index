BFN [Text] Tirana, May 10 (ATA) -- The ten accused Albanians on the charges of "conspiracy in subversive activity" in the Republic of Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] will continue to stay in detention, because their trial planned for May 9 was postponed, reports Albanian TV, according to the information from Skopje. The reason for the postponement of the trial for the second time is the continuation of attorneys' strike. The trial will start on May 23. In the Albanian opinion prevails the idea that this is a fabricated case were facts are missing to verify their charges.
