November 24, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: LAGUNA HILLS;

GROUP HOSTS ANNUAL HOLIDAY BOUTIQUE

The Laguna Village Homeowners Assn. will hold its annual holiday boutique today at the complex's clubhouse.

The boutique, to be held from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m., will offer more than 75 booths selling arts, crafts and gifts. The clubhouse is at 23300 Santa Vittoria Drive in Laguna Hills. Some of the proceeds will go to help a needy family.
