BFN [Text] Beijing, May 11 (XINHUA) -- A Sino-U.S. symposium on anti-dumping legal provisions, the first of its kind in China, opened Monday [9 May] in Beijing. Experts from the Commerce Department of the United States will introduce the U.S. legal provisions on anti-dumping to Chinese participants during the 10-day symposium. Officials from foreign trade and economic cooperation committees in China and more than 100 representatives from enterprises in northern and northeastern China are attending the symposium. The symposium is expected to give Chinese personnel engaged in foreign trade a better idea about the legal provisions on anti-dumping in the United States as well as anti-dumping tendencies in the world, said Wang Dianguo, an official from the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation. It will also help strengthen the management of Chinese exports, he added. The symposium will hold further discussions in Shanghai and Guangzhou.
