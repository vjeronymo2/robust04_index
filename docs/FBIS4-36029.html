CSO [Text] In separate orders by Engineer Akbar Torkan, minister of roads and transportation, Engineer 'Abdollah Mohammad Hashem was appointed deputy minister for building and airport construction, Engineer Mohammad Esma'il Qa'em Maqami was appointed deputy minister for planning and ministry economic affairs, and Engineer Mohammad Madad was reappointed for three years as executive directory of the Ports and Shipping Organization. In a separate order from Yahya Al-e Eshaq, minister of commerce, Engineer 'Ali Sa'idlu was chosen as deputy minister for ministry exports and chief of the Iran Export Development Center.
