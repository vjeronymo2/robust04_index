930105 FT 05 JAN 93 / Drug ills blamed on deprivation THE BISHOP of Hulme, whose diocese includes the rundown Moss Side area of Manchester where a 14-year-old boy was shot dead at the weekend, blamed unemployment and deprivation yesterday for the scale of drug trafficking and violence in the area. The Rt Rev Colin Scott warned that his own fear was that the rising scale of violence in the area might drive away vital traders and businesses.
