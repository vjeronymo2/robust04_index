Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan March 30 TASS -- Armenia is expressing regret over the fact that the Iranian Foreign Ministry came up with a statement concerning the crash of the Iranian plane before the end of the work and the drawing of a final conclusion by a special commission of Iran and CIS, which investigated the circumstances of the accident, acting press secretary of the Armenian president Levon Zurabyan stated today. The statement of the Armenian side came two days after the statement of the Iranian Foreign Ministry, which said that the Iranian plane had been brought down on March 17 by a missile, fired by the Armenian troops deployed in Nagorno-Karabakh. According to the Armenian Foreign Ministry, circulated on the next day after the accident, the Iranian military-transport plane, which was flying from Moscow to Tehran, deviated from his course by about 100 km, instead of entering Armenia's air space. After that it found itself in the air space of Nagorno-Karabakh, where it crashed near Stepanakert. According to the preliminary information of the Armenian side, something went wrong with the mechanisms of the plane, which was denied, however, by experts from the Foreign Ministry, civil aviation and the air force of Iran, who visited Stepanakert. All the 32 Iranian citizens, who were on board the plane, including women and children, died. After official honours in Yerevan, their remains were sent to Tehran.
