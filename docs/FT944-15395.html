941019 FT 19 OCT 94 / Queen's visit to Russia Queen Elizabeth, on the first full day of an historic visit to Russia, tours Moscow's Red Square yesterday with the city's mayor, Mr Yuri Luzhkov. In the evening the queen toasted Mr Yeltsin at a state banquet - the first black-tie event in the Kremlin since the 1917 revolution - saying 'there is a complicated history behind us but the message for our peoples is simple - in future we shall work together, together we shall build a better future'.
