930707 FT 07 JUL 93 / World Trade News: Mexico to open its financial sector MEXICO is expected to open up its financial sector to European financial institutions to ensure they are not discriminated against under the proposed North American Free Trade Agreement, writes Damian Fraser in Mexico City. Mr Michael Portillo, chief secretary to the UK Treasury, said yesterday in Mexico City that he believed some direct access would be given if quotas set aside for US and Canadian houses were insufficient for European banks to compete. Mr Portillo met President Carlos Salinas in an attempt to increase awareness of opportunities in Mexico for British businessmen.
