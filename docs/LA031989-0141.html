March 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: NAMES IN THE NEWS

Pete Weber became the third man to win bowling's triple crown when he beat Dave Ferraro, 221-216, for the title in the $280,000 Professional Bowlers Assn. National Championship at Toledo, Ohio. Weber, who also won the 1987 Tournament of Champions and the 1988 U.S. Open, joined Billy Hardwick and Johnny Petraglia as triple crown winners.
