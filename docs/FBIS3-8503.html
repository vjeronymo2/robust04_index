Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Belgrade, 11 Mar (TANJUG)--At noon today, Greek Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias arrived in Belgrade, where he is to have talks with Serbian President Slobodan Milosevic. He was welcomed at Belgrade aiport by Federal Deputy Prime Minister Zeljko Simic. Milosevic and Papoulias are expected to exchange views on the problems of the peace process on former Yugoslavia, particularly on the crisis in former Bosnia-Herzegovina after the signing of the Washington plan for the creation of the Muslim-Croat federation and a confederation with the Republic of Croatia.
