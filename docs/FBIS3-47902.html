Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Telegram of condolences from Comrade Fidel Castro Ruz, first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba Central Committee, president of the Council of State, and president of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Cuba, to Comrade Khamtai Siphandon, chairman of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party [LPRP] Central Committee and prime minister of the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR]; and Comrade Nouhak Phoumsavan, president of the LPDR, on the demise of Comrade Phoumi Vongvichit, adviser to the LPRP Central Committee -- date not given] [Text] Beloved comrades: We are extremely saddened to be informed of the passing away of Comrade Phoumi Vongvichit. During the revolutionary movement, he established an unforgettably fine model practice in the history of the struggle of the courageous people. From that period, he devoted himself to the tasks against colonialism and imperialism for the sake of national independence. Comrade Phoumi Vongvichit always honestly adhered to his ideal of patriotism and [word indistinct]. This was shown during the highly responsible performance of his duties throughout the period when a new life for Laos was being created and the party and state were being built, as well as in other activities. The passing away of Comrade Phoumi Vongvichit means the Lao People's Revolutionary Party has lost an honest and virtuous senior citizen. It is also the loss of an intimate friend for the communists and people of Cuba. However, we are confident that the comrades who participated in the past struggles with him and the determined and courageous Lao people will carry on his tasks, to which he devoted himself throughout his life. Through the comrades, we convey our deepest sorrow to the Party Central Committee, the government, and the people of Laos, as well as to his relatives and family.
