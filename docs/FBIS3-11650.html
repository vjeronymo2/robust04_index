Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The annual session of the Chinese People's Congress ended today with a call to continue political and economic reforms. The 3,000 deputies were urged to work to balance the national reforms, development, and stability. Premier Li Peng has warned the United States that China is a sovereign country and will not negotiate or agree to anything in detriment to its self-determination. Li Peng said he expected better results during the visit of U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher between 11 and 14 March. That trip was preceded by a broad campaign against alleged Chinese human rights violations, in which Beijing was threatened with withdrawal of most favored nation status in bilateral trade.
