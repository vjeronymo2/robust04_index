BFN [Text] Yerevan, April 25, RIA -- The existing power shall not, and will not be deposed by violence, President Ter-Petrosyan said to parliament today to propose a provisional arrangement before the new constitution is passed. According to it, parliament shall offer for referendum, without preliminary debates, either of the two constitutional drafts or both--the official version from the president-headed Constitutional Commission, and six opposition parties' alternative draft. If the latter is adopted, the president will immediately resign. If his variant passes, Armenia will need new presidential and parliamentary elections, holds Mr. Ter-Petrosyan. He also deems it necessary for parliament to pass the citizenship and election bills within the current session, reports SNARK news agency.
