940421 FT 21 APR 94 / UK Company News: CRP Leisure CRP LEISURE: After taking a 22.3 per cent stake at the end of March, Dolmen Holdings is making an unsecured interest free loan of Pounds 150,000 to be repaid from a placing, underwritten by Dolmen, to raise a minimum of Pounds 400,000. Dolmen is to be represented on the CRP board.
