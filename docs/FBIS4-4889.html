BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 1 (KCNA) -- The South Korean opposition Democratic Party on March 29 demanded a hearing of the National Assembly to lay bare the injustice of the "Uruguay Round negotiations" as part of the struggle against the "parliamentary ratification" of the farm market opening, according to a Radio report from Seoul. The Democratic Party inaugurated the "Committee of the Struggle To Check Ratification of Uruguay Round Accord" with democratic members of the "National Assembly" and declared it would convert the party system into an emergency system to check the "parliamentary ratification" of the farm market opening.
