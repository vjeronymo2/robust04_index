930115 FT 15 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Kleen-e-ze settlement Kleen-e-ze Holdings has agreed a settlement with Fanfare Electronics in respect of an alleged breach of an agreement by one of its subsidiaries. The settlement involves the purchase of stock valued at some Pounds 250,000 which will be paid for over three years. The settlement was provided for in Kleen-e-ze's accounts for the 1991-92 year. Fanfare had claimed Pounds 1.3m together with interest and costs.
