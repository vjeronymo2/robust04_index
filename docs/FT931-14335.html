930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Barlo improves to IPounds 390,000 Barlo Group, enlarged via the acquisition last July of IRG, the plastic processing company, returned pre-tax profits of IPounds 390,000 (Pounds 410,000), for the six months ended September 30. That compared with IPounds 319,000 last time which included exceptional income of IPounds 268,000. Higher interest charges of IPounds 225,000 (IPounds 49,000) resulted from the IRG purchase. Turnover totalled IPounds 15.1m (IPounds 6.14m) with the radiator division ahead by 34 per cent at IPounds 8.22m. Earnings per share worked through at 0.46p (0.44p).
