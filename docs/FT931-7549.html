930224 FT 24 FEB 93 / Basic pay in City banks up just 0.9% BASIC SALARIES for international bank employees in the City rose by just 0.9 per cent last year. But when bonuses are included the rise was 2.9 per cent, say pay consultants Day Associates, Diane Summers writes. The survey of more than 120 international banks and investment banks found: A drop in basic salaries in the capital markets sector of 2.7 per cent. But bonuses produced an overall earnings increase of 2.9 per cent. Fund management staff did better than other groups, with a basic increase of 3.6 per cent - 6.4 per cent with bonuses. Back-office salaries rose 1.5 per cent - 3.6 per cent with bonuses. There was little movement in pay for corporate finance and legal jobs.
