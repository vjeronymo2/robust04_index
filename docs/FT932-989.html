930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Aviva Petrol seeks Dollars 11.3m Aviva Petroleum, the Texas-based oil and gas company with a London quote, is seeking to raise a net Dollars 11.3m (Pounds 7.5m) in its second fund raising in a year. As part of a refinancing it has placed 12.6m shares at 95 cents (63p). The shares were unchanged yesterday at 69p. The refinancing is dependent on investors taking part being granted options on a further 2m shares held by an existing shareholder. Aviva raised Dollars 5.5m in a 60.65 per cent subscribed rights issue in October last year. In April it announced increased pre-tax losses of Dollars 9.83m (Dollars 8.81m) for 1992.
