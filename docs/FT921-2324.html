920319 FT 19 MAR 92 / The Lex Column: Eureko The pattern of the great European insurance shake-up is becoming clearer with confirmation of the four-way alliance between the UK's Friends Provident and Dutch and Scandinavian partners. As with Royal, AMB and Fondiaria, cross-border synergies look a long way off. In the short term, the deal provides cover against single market risk.
