940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Applied Distribution Applied Distribution Group, which came to the market in March, announced a rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.01m to Pounds 1.63m for the six months to July 2. Turnover for the warehousing and distribution group amounted to Pounds 15.3m (Pounds 12m). A dividend of 1.3p is being paid from earnings of 5.96p.
