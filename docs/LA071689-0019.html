July 16, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

JAZZ, L.A. STYLE

The July 9 Leonard Feather article, "Showing Plenty of Pizazz in New York," put into sharp focus the difference between N.Y. and L.A.

In New York, you get to see the top-name jazz artists in intimate indoor places such as Carnegie, Avery Fisher and Alice Tully Hall. Here, you get to see jazz in an 18,000-seat outdoor ballpark named the Hollywood Bowl.

ROGER REMICK

Studio City
