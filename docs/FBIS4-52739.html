BFN [Excerpt] Vientiane, May 13 (KPL) -- Lao Foreign Minister Somsavat Lengsavat, on May 12, returned home from South Africa where he attended the ceremony of inauguration of the new South African President Nelson Mandela. The attendance of the Lao foreign minister was a gesture of the Lao Government's support to the new sociopolitical change for national reconciliation in South Africa. [passage omitted]
