Second National Federal Savings Association, Salisbury, MD; Replacement of Conservator With a Receiver Notice is hereby given that, pursuant to the authority contained in Subdivision (C) of section 5(d)(2) of the Home Owners' Loan Act, the Office of Thrift Supervision has duly replaced the Resolution Trust Corporation as Conservator Second National Federal Savings Association, Salisbury, Maryland (``Association''), with the Resolution Trust Corporation as sole Receiver for the Association on September 16, 1994.
