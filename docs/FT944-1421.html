941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Burford purchase Burford Holdings is to buy the long leasehold interest in the Victoria Circus Shopping Centre, Southend, Essex, from Sanfield Lynton for Pounds 11.4m cash. The property extends to 3.5 acres and comprises 270,000 sq ft of offices and a 680-space multi-storey car park.
