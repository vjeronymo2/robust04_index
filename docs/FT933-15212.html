930709 FT 09 JUL 93 / International Company News: Proton profit slides 24% PERUSAHAAN Otomobil Nasional (Proton), the Malaysian car maker, reports a steep drop in pre-tax profits for the year ended March, 1993. However, it is holding its dividend at 16.25 sen a share, AP-DJ reports from Kuala Lumpur. Revenues improved by 4.6 per cent, to MDollars 2.29bn (USDollars 891m), but pre-tax profits tumbled from MDollars 408m to MDollars 311m, a fall of 24 per cent.
