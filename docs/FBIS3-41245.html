Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Summary] Sichuan in mid-August created an overall counternarcotics strategy. Since then, it has solved 888 cases, of which 168 were major or extraordinary cases, and seized 133.242 kg of heroin, opium, and caffeine. In all, 1,329 drug criminals have been caught. According to statistics for 1993, by late November, Sichuan had solved 1,263 drug cases of all types, of which 319 were major or extraordinary cases. Officials had seized 215.742 kg of drugs and captured 1,918 drug criminals. Also by late November, investigative components at all levels throughout Sichuan had formally arrested 296 drug criminals, and courts at all levels had tried and sentenced 186 drug criminals. Nine criminals were put to death.
