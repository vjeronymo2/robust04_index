Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today, Belarusian Supreme Soviet Chairman Mechyslaw Hryb held a news conference addressing the results of the work of the Belarusian delegation at the session of the Council of the Inter- Parliamentary CIS Assembly, which was held in St. Petersburg. Journalists were also interested in hearing the legislative leader's opinions on issues related to Belarusian domestic life. In particular, speaking about the political strike scheduled for 15 February, Mechyslaw Hryb said: My attitude toward strikes is extremely negative. I am for the resolution of disputable issues through diplomatic means. Speaking about the work of the Interparliamentary CIS Assembly, Mechyslaw Hryb said that all the sessions were held in an atmosphere of mutual respect and constructive work. A range of issues -- in particular, on the convening of a regular meeting in April or May in Beshkek -- were discussed.
