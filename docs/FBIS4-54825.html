BFN [Report by Mirza Sadikovic from Bihac] [Excerpt] The joint aggressors have not stopped attacking the Bihac region. Karadzic's chetniks last night stepped up artillery attacks on the defenders of the Grabez part of the front and the Ggrmusko-Srbljanski plateau. They also started shelling the suburbs and central part of Cazin at 2300. They fired some 20 mortar and howitzer shells. There was also some shelling on the Bosanska Krupa and Buzim front. Anti-aircraft weapons and heavy machine guns are being used constantly. The paramilitary formations of the Kladusa traitors and rebels have stepped attacks last night and this morning. They are attacking Trzac, Liskovac, Krivaja, and Pecigrad in the Cazin commune, and Todorovo and Varoska Rijeka with 82 mm and 120 mm mortars and guns. [passage omitted]
