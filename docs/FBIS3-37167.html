Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Eduard Popov] [Text] Vladivostok February 15 TASS -- Under conditions of a severe energy crisis the Administration of the Maritime Region decided to look into possibilities of the use of nuclear reactors installed on board submarines of the Russian Pacific Fleet for energy supplies to the regional centre. A conference attended by representatives of the regional administration, the Pacific Fleet and Far Eastern scientific institutions was held in Vladivostok on Tuesday to discuss technical and economic aspects of this unusual project. Experts believe that the high quality of domestic technologies and a guaranteed system of maintenance will ensure full radiation and ecological safety of the population. Representatives of the fleet said that the energy potential of one submarine is enough to ensure energy supplies to no lees than 50 big apartment houses.
