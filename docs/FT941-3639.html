940315 FT 15 MAR 94 / International Company News: Alcan to sell two extrusion plants Alcan Aluminium is selling two of its US extrusion plants to Cressona Aluminum of the US for an undisclosed sum, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. The facilities employ 300 people and have annual capacity of 45,000 tons of extrusions. Alcan is also negotiating to sell its remaining North American extrusion operations, including three plants and a die-making operation in Canada and an extrusion plant in the US.
