Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 17 (XINHUA) -- At the invitation of Chinese Premier Li Peng, a delegation from the Government of the Kingdom of Cambodia led by First Prime Minister Norodom Ranariddh and Second Prime Minister Hun Sen arrived here this afternoon on a five-day official visit to China. This is the first visit to China by the two prime ministers since Cambodia resumed peace and formed its new government. Among those greeting them at the airport were She Jianming, chairman of the Chinese Government Reception Committee and vice- minister of the State Planning Commission, and Chinese Vice-Foreign Minister Tang Jiaxuan.
