BFN [Text] Tajik military subunits have been withdrawn from the collective peacekeeping forces in Tajikistan, Colonel General Valeriy Patrikeyev, commander of the collective peacekeeping forces in Tajikistan, said on Saturday. At the same time, the commander reported that the term for the presence of peacekeeping forces in the republic had been extended until 31 December. At present, the total number of CIS peacekeeping forces is 9,000 men less than previously planned. The commander expressed concern that Kazakhstan had not yet allocated its own battalion to join the forces.
