April 3, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL ROUNDUP

In other baseball games:

Van Nuys 3, Grant 2 -- Carlos Gonzalez tripled with two out in the seventh inning to spark a two-run Van Nuys rally in a Valley Pac-8 Conference game at Van Nuys.

Gonzalez' hit drove in a run to tie the score, 2-2, and he scored on an infield single by Carlos Gutierrez. Tony Gonzalez struck out three and held Grant to three hits.

Birmingham 8, North Hollywood 1 -- A dropped fly ball triggered a three-run second inning for visiting Birmingham in an East Valley League game.

Josh Brown scattered four hits, struck out five and walked none for Birmingham (5-5-1, 4-4). Braves Matt Mowry and Noah Kirschbaum each had two hits.

Poly 11, Monroe 5 -- Will Molina and Marlon McKinney each had two hits and combined for four RBIs to pace host Poly in a Valley Pac-8 game.

David Giatti (1-2-1), a senior right-hander, entered in relief of starter Rodrigo Dorame in the fourth inning for Poly (10-2, 8-0).

Christian Ascencio and Mark Montelongo each had two hits for Monroe (3-3, 3-3).

Marshall 6, Verdugo Hills 4 -- Jaime Chavez had two hits -- including a home run -- and three RBIs to lead visiting Marshall (4-3, 2-0) in a Northern Conference game.

Junior right-hander Jon Bishop (1-2) struck out 12 and allowed no earned runs, and teammate Pat Eubanks hit a three-run home run for Verdugo Hills (1-7, 1-2).

Providence 22, Yeshiva 2 -- Senior designated-hitter Greg Hurley was four for four with two RBIs and hit for the cycle to lead Providence (6-4-1, 3-2-1) in a Liberty League game at Yeshiva.

BOYS' VOLLEYBALL

Harvard d. Chaminade, 15-2, 15-11, 15-6 -- Middle blocker Rick Osterloh had 15 kills to lead Harvard (5-1, 3-0) in a San Fernando Valley League match.

Reseda d. Grant 15-12, 15-13, 15-10 -- Scott Kent had a match-high 25 kills and Shad Dockery added 14 for host Reseda (3-5, 2-2) in a Valley Pac-8 conference match.

Mark Jomsky led Grant (5-3, 2-2) with 14 kills and 17 assists.

Chatsworth d. Kennedy, 15-4, 15-11, 15-9 -- Setter Greg Saltzburg had 23 assists and Mike Nash had 19 putaways as Chatsworth won its third consecutive Northwest Valley Conference match.

The Chancellors (7-2, 3-0) received strong defensive performances from Ryan Labovitch and Scott Yager.

Taft d. Cleveland 15-0, 15-6, 15-9 -- Cody Hein (10 kills and 14 assists), Adam Friedman (18 assists) led visiting Taft in a Northwest Valley Conference match.

In the first game, Aubry Miller had 11 consecutive service points and five aces for Taft (6-1, 3-1).

Yan Huang had 10 assists to lead Cleveland (0-4, 0-2).

Van Nuys d. Sherman Oaks C.E.S. 15-5, 15-13, 15-5 -- Brad Schultz (14 kills) and Sam Saythathongsack (seven kills) led host Van Nuys in a Valley Pac 8 match.

Birmingham d. North Hollywood 15,11, 15-10, 13-15, 15-12 -- James Gould (27 kills) Stanley Yu, (22 kills) and Hye Kwon (11 blocks) led Birmingham (6-2, 4-0) in a Valley Pac-8 match.

La Salle d. Notre Dame 14-16, 15-5, 15-9, 15-17, 15-13 -- Dan Palmquist (21 kills), Dindo Valencia (21 kills) and Duc Nguyen (65 assists) led La Salle in a San Fernando League match.

BOYS' SWIMMING

Reseda 93, Kennedy 30 -- John Van Duinwyk, Tim Schaeffer, Lee Vu each won three events to lead Reseda (6-0) in a West Valley League meet at Birmingham.

SOFTBALL

Notre Dame 4, Chaminade 3 -- Senior Becca Berline pitched a complete game and pounded two triples to pace the Knights in a San Fernando Valley League opener.
