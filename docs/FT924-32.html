921231 FT 31 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Acquisition search costs hit Stanelco Stanelco, manufacturer of induction heating and welding equipment, made Pounds 14,000 pre-tax profits in the six months to August 31, on Pounds 791,000 sales. The search for acquisitions cost Pounds 30,000. Interest receivable amounted to Pounds 10,000. The USM-quoted group was reorganised at the end of 1991.
