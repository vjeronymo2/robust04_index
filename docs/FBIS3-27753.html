Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Jan.30 -- The armed forces last year increased their participation in economic production and business with the total turnover of VN dong 1,800 billion (roughly USD 170 million), up by 44 per cent compared with 1992 figure. They contributed VND 120 billion to the state budget, an increase of 30 per cent, and gained an export value of USD 90 million, up by 47.5 per cent over 1992. They also made a combined investment of USD 147 million in some joint venture projects with foreign partners. Moreover they joined in the state's economic projects and programmes of afforestation, the construction of a motor highway linking Hanoi and the international airport of Noi Bai, the construction of the Yali Hydro-Electric Plant in central Vietnam, the construction of an export processing zone in Haiphong port city, and the 500 kv trans-Vietnam power line.
