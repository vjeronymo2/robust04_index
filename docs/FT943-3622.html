940913 FT 13 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Aspen at Pounds 1m after all-round growth All-round growth enabled Aspen Communications to report pre-tax profits of Pounds 1m for the half year to June 30. Profits of Pounds 1.29m last time included an exceptional credit of Pounds 1.1m. Turnover for the USM-quoted provider of printing, media and communications and marketing services, advanced by 15 per cent to Pounds 34.9m (Pounds 30.2m). After redundancy costs, operating profits amounted to Pounds 1.16m (Pounds 381,000). Earnings per share worked through at 4.8p (6.6p) and the interim dividend is lifted to 2.15p (2p). Mr Henry Meakin, chairman, said the directors believed trading margins were capable of doubling over the next two years.
