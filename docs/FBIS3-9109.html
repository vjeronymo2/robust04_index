Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Knin, 20 Mar (TANJUG) -- Djordje Bjegovic, prime minister of the republic of Serb Krajina (RSK), tendered his irrevocable resignation to the position of prime minister at a session of Krajina's assembly today. Under the RSK Constitution, the prime minister's resignation entails the resignation of the whole government, which will however remain in power until a new government is elected.
