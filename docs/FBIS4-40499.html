BFN [Text] A meeting took place on Sadarak Rayon's border on 14 June between Namik Gasanov, chairman of Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic Supreme Mejlis, and two representatives of the Armenian Republic: Shakhnazaryan, the Armenian president's plenipotentiary representative; and Arutyunyan, chief border guards commissar. An agreement was reached during the two-hour-long meeting to maintain peace on the border. It is being stated in connection with the Armenian side's proposal to open a joint trade center on the border that this is not possible between countries at war and when a part of our territory is under occupation.
