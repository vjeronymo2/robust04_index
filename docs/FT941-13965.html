940122 FT 22 JAN 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Nutmeg and mace prices were firm this week with only limited offers emerging from Indonesia, reports Man Producten. Spot Europe prices were USDollars 1,550 a tonne for bwp and Dollars 1,700 for shrivels and Dollars 1,800 for abcds. The spot price for mace was Dollars 3,000 a tonne, with shipments at higher levels. Grenadan nutmegs increased their cif prices: guns were at Dollars 1,400 a tonne and suns Dollars 1,900 for February shipment, while mace No. 2 was offered at Dollars 2,650 a tonne for shipment only after April. Indonesian cassia prices were unchanged with ka/va sticks at Dollars 2,300 a tonne, spot and Dollars 2,150, shipment, and kb br/cl at Dollars 1,600 spot, Dollars 1,475, shipment, with discounting on later shipments. Madagascan cinnamon was offered at FFr5.70 a kg, with Seychelles at Dollars 1,400 a tonne, cif.
