BFN [Text] Today, the Republic of Cuba and the Sultanate of Oman established diplomatic relations at the ambassadorial level for the purpose of continuing to work for the benefit of their respective countries and peoples. Fernando Remirez de Estenoz, and Salim Bin-Muhammad al-Qusaybi, permanent representatives to the United Nations for Cuba and Oman respectively, signed the documents relating to that sovereign decision by the two countries during a simple ceremony held at UN headquarters in New York. During the ceremony, the representative of Oman described the moment as historic, adding that it would contribute to the prosperity of the two nations and peoples. Remirez, first vice minister of Foreign Relations, also agreed in describing this step as historic for the two states, governments, and peoples.
