Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] (Han Sang-u), deputy chief of the DPRK mission in the UN Headquarters in Geneva, yesterday denounced the United States for having unilaterally canceled the DPRK-U.S. high-level talks and hindered the thorough resolution of the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula. (Han Sang-u) stated that the attitudes of the United States and the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] had complicated this matter and that it was difficult to predict their consequences. On 16 March, the IAEA denounced Korea for having rejected the IAEA specialists' [words indistinct] inspection of nuclear facilities. Using this as an excuse, the United States later declared that it would cancel the 21 March DPRK-U.S. high-level talks. The ROK and the United States are reportedly holding negotiations on the Team Spirit joint military exercise.
