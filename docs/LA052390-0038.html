May 23, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Knebworth Benefit on MTV: MTV will broadcast the Knebworth 1990 benefit concert July 14. The concert will feature performances by Genesis, George Michael and Tears for Fears, as well as a jam session with Paul McCartney, Elton John, Eric Clapton, Mark Knopfler and Pink Floyd. The concert, which will be held June 30 in Knebworth Park in England, will benefit the Nordoff-Robbins Music Therapy Centre, which treats autistic, mentally and physically handicapped children through music therapy. GREG BRAXTON
