940902 FT 02 SEP 94 / World News in Brief: FT-SE 100 down 35 points amid gloom over rates The FT-SE 100 share index closed 34.8 points down at 3,216.5 as pessimism about the future direction of interest rates gripped world financial markets. The gloom was prompted by poor US price data, strong German output figures and the Bundesbank's decision not to change official rates.
