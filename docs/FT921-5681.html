920303 FT 03 MAR 92 / International Company News: Baer Holding plans share split BAER Holding, the parent company of Bank Julius Baer, plans to split both its SFr100 registered shares and its SFr500 bearer shares 10 for one following changes in Swiss law, writes Ian Rodger from Zurich. The group said the move would make the shares easier to trade while preserving family control.
