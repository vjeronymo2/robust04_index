BFN [Text] Russian and Ukrainian experts meeting in Sevastopol have completed their work on the classification of the Black Sea Fleet vessels. This has been announced by Ukrainian Vice Admiral Vladimir Bezkorovaynyy. Also discussed was the issue of bases. The chief Ukrainian negotiator, Deputy Prime Minister Valeriy Shmarov, said the Ukrainian city of Sevastopol in the Crimea would remain the base of the Russian part of the fleet. So far it is unclear where the Ukrainian warships will base.
