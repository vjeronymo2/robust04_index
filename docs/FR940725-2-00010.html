Office of the Secretary Defense Advisory Committee on Military Personnel Testing; Meeting action: Notice. Pursuant to Public Law 92&hyph;463, notice is hereby given that a meeting of the Defense Advisory Committee on Military Personnel Testing is scheduled to be held from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. on September 22, 1994, and from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. on September 23, 1994. The meeting will be held at The Colonial Inn, 910 Prospect Street, La Jolla, California. The purpose of the meeting is to review planned changes and progress in developing paper-and-pencil and computerized enlistment tests and Department of Defense's Student Testing Program. Persons desiring to make oral presentations or submit written statements for consideration at the Committee meeting must contact Dr. Jane M. Arabian, Assistant Director, Accession Policy, Office of the Under Secretary of Defense (Personnel and Readiness), Room 2B271, The Pentagon, Washington, DC 20301&hyph;4000, telephone (703) 697&hyph;9271, no later than August 29, 1994.
