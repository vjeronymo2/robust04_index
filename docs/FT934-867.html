931222 FT 22 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Mosaic achieves turnround to Pounds 0.4m Following the restructuring and a relisting of its shares in May, Mosaic Investments achieved a turnround to pre-tax profits of Pounds 416,000 for the half year to October 31. After exceptional costs of Pounds 3.43m, losses last time were Pounds 2.64m, restated for FRS 3. Turnover for the display products, packaging and marketing services group fell to Pounds 11.3m (Pounds 18.3m), including a Pounds 995,000 (Pounds 8.14m) contribution from discontinued operations. Earnings per share worked through at 0.24p (17.92p losses).
