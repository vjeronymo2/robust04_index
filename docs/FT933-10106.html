930805 FT 05 AUG 93 / EC imposes chemical duties The European Commission has imposed preliminary dumping duties on imports of ethanolamine from the US, AP-DJ reports from Brussels. Ethanolamine is used to make detergents. The Commission said it would provisionally impose duties of between Ecu584 (Dollars 660) and Ecu652 a metric ton on US ethanolamine imports.
