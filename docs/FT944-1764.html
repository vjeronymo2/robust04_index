941219 FT 19 DEC 94 / Discounters' sales 'grow by 20%' Sales of non-food discount retailers are expected to have risen by 20 per cent this year, far outstripping the growth of the rest of the retail market, says retail research group Verdict. Discounters, which sell at low prices and rely on large volumes, account for Pounds 8bn sales, or about 8 per cent of non-food spending - compared with 22 per cent in the US. Verdict says a new generation of cut-price operators is set to make the UK market resemble the US more closely. Verdict on Discounters. Verdict, 112 High Holborn, London WC1. 071 404 5042. Pounds 890.
