940902 FT 02 SEP 94 / World News in Brief: Gadaffi's salute marks 25 years in power Libyan leader Muammar Gadaffi waved to the Tripoli crowds as tanks rolled passed to mark 25 year since he overthrew King Mohammed Idris in a coup and seized power. The show of military might comes at a time when Libya is subject to UN sanctions imposed for its refusal to hand over two men suspected of involvement in the 1988 bombing of an airliner over Lockerbie, Scotland.
