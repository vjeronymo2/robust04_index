Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council, met in the economic and commercial capital, Aden, today with the brother representatives of the Shabwah Governorate expanded popular gathering, who briefed him on the results of the gathering held on 5 February under the slogan: "For the sake of a United and Effective Stand of the Governorate's Sons To Defend Unity, Democracy, Justice, and Social Development." Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid delivered a speech in which he welcomed the brother representatives of the popular gathering of the sons of the Shabwah Governorate and blessed the successes and results that the gathering has produced. He also congratulated them, as well as the masses of our Yemeni people on the arrival of the month of Ramadan. On this blessed occasion, he said, we wish you many happy returns, and we hope that this month will return to us while our country is in a good situation, God willing. The Presidential Council vice chairman explained the importance of the accord document initialed in Aden. He said: We initialed the document. We will sign it expeditiously and finally and we will work along with all honorable people to implement and embody it on the ground and on the path of the homeland's progress toward a better future. He urged all the political parties and social figures and all good people to make the accord document a national program for themselves. He stressed that the Yemeni Socialist Party will be at the forefront of those who are working to implement the document. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid then stressed the importance of unity and cohesion among the sons of the Yemeni people from Sa'dah to al- Mahrah, as well as fighting the idea of division in the minds of the Yemenis, pointing out that popular gatherings in the governorates are the ones to which the document will go after being finally signed. He urged the sons of the Shabwah Governorate to be at the forefront of those who call for forming a broad front to glorify the document and comply with its contents. He blessed the efforts of the sons of the governorate to patch up differences and resolve the issues of revenge before the end of this year. Concluding, brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid wished the Shabwah Governorate sons every success. the meeting was attended by Deputy Prime Minister Muhammad Haydarah Masdus and several officials.
