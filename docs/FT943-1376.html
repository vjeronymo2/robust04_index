940924 FT 24 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Molyneux returns to the black Molyneux Estates, the USM-traded property investment company, was back in the black at the June 23 year-end, with pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.11m. Last year losses were Pounds 2.53m. Turnover rose 26 per cent from Pounds 3.97m to Pounds 4.99m. Four acquisitions, costing Pounds 12m, were made during the year. The company's property portfolio has been independently valued at Pounds 64.6m, giving an increased net asset value per share of 79.52p (62.75p). The final dividend is 1.25p, making a total of 2p (1p).
