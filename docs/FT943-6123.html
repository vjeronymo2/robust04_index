940831 FT 31 AUG 94 / UK Company News: EIS EIS Group, the specialist engineer, has acquired 76 per cent of the share capital of Henrich of Herborn, Germany, a maker of wire drawing and coiling machinery. Consideration is DM750,000 (Pounds 316,000) with an obligation to refinance current bank borrowings of DM12.5m. The price may be adjusted following a net asset valuation audit. In 1993 Henrich incurred operating losses of DM3.2m on turnover of DM48.6m.
