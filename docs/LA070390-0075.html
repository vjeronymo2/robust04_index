July 3, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

U.S. LEFT OUT OF IRAN'S 'THANK YOU' MESSAGES

Iranian President Hashemi Rafsanjani sent messages Monday to 98 world leaders thanking them for helping the victims of last month's earthquake -- but he left out the United States.

The U.S. government provided $760,000 in aid for the earthquake survivors, according to the State Department, with private American groups sending $4 million.

Also missing from the list were Saudi Arabia and Iraq, both of which sent aid.
