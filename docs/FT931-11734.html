930202 FT 02 FEB 93 / UK Company News: 33% asset rise at Fleming American Fleming American Investment Trust saw its net asset value per share rise 33 per cent to 279.3p for 1992. There was an increase in small company exposure to 25 per cent, where there is additional potential for capital growth, said Mr Iain Saunders, chairman. Gross revenue rose from Pounds 3.87m to Pounds 4.11m. Higher expenses and interest led to a cut in earnings from 1.26p to 0.73p, and the dividend is reduced from 1.25p to 0.7p with a final of 0.35p.
