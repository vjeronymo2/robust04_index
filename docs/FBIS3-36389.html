Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Units of the Chechen armed opposition have surrounded General Dudayev's residence in Groznyy, ANI agency reports. The leader of the opposition, Gen. Ibrahim Suleymenov has ordered combat maneuvers to be started near Dudayev's residence. Taking part in these maneuvers are helicopters rehearsing air strikes against enemy positions. In an interview with ANI, Gen. Suleymenov confirmed that he was ready for national reconciliation on the condition of the immediate resignation of Gen. Dudayev from all his posts and the return to Chechnya of the funds from Dudayev's secret accounts in foreign banks.
