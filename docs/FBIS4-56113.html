BFN [Excerpts] The Hungarian Democratic Forum [MDF] and the Christian Democratic People's Party [KDNP] have concluded a cooperation agreement for the second round of the elections. Under the Christian-democratic agreement, they are to back the candidates of the Independent Smallholders' Party [FKGP] in some places as well. Agnes Pap reports. [Pap] [passage omitted] MDF Spokesman Karoly Herenyi said that not only was joining forces between the parties at issue but also the necessity to make a broader agreement. The leading bodies of the KDNP and the MDF want the Christian-democratic pooling of forces to come about among the voters, including those who voted for the FKGP. Herenyi said that the MDF's voters would support the FKGP's candidates in 42 places where they have a chance to win. Under agreements concluded with the FKGP county chairmen in Somogy and Tolna counties [southern Hungary], the FKGP candidates are to support the MDF candidates in five places. [passage omitted]
