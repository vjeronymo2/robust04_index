940322 FT 22 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Beazer offer 1.3 times subscribed The public offer of shares in Beazer Homes, the UK's fourth largest housebuilder being floated by Hanson, attracted applications for 91.8m shares, 1.3 times the 70.2m available, writes Maggie Urry. A further 210.6m shares were placed firm with institutions. The shares were priced at 165p, making the retail offer worth Pounds 115.8m and valuing the company at Pounds 463.4m. Applicants in the public offer for 105,000 shares or less will receive the full amount. Those who asked for more than that will receive 105,000 shares. Most larger investors bought in the placing. On this basis 99.8 per cent of the 33,615 applications under the public offer will be met in full. Dealings in the shares start on March 25.
