BFN [Text] Professor of Law Valeriy Tikhinya -- who was recently elected deputy chairman of the Republican Constitutional Court -- had to provide answers to many complicated questions during his meeting with officers and generals of the Defense Ministry Chief Headquarters. Valeriy Tikhinya told the military about the first steps of the Constitutional Court, the difficulties which they have to overcome to make the higher court of the state a genuine lever for upholding lawfulness at all levels. The experienced lawyer managed to detail the officers' rights in numerous vital sectors among which housing, social, and legal issues were the key ones.
