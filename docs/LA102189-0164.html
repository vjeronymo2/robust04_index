SPOTLIGHT /COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE: A WEEKLY LOOK AT OFFICE AND INDUSTRIAL REAL ESTATE IN THE COUNTY.;

FOCUS: 2020 BUILDING

Hutton Development's 2020 Building in Santa Ana contains 110,283 square feet and is 85% leased. The five-story office building is close to the Santa Ana Freeway, just west of the interchange of the Santa Ana and Costa Mesa freeways. Designed by McMahon Partners, it features blue reflective glass and contrasting peach-colored columns.
