931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / International Company News: Hoogovens share issue raises Fl 362m Hoogovens, the Dutch steelmaker, said its two-stage issue of new share certificates over the past week raised Fl 362m (Dollars 190m), AP-DJ reports from Amsterdam. The company said the offer was oversubscribed. Proceeds will be used to shore up Hoogovens' capital base. All 9.05m share certificates on offer were placed. The issue price was Fl 40 per certificate. Certificates are a unique Dutch form of depositary receipt representing shares that many companies in the Netherlands place with trusts friendly to management as an anti-takeover defence.
