930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / International Company News: Jennings Group Jennings Group, the Australian housebuilder, has agreed a restructuring plan involving Fletcher Challenge, its major shareholder, and a consortium of banks, writes Bruce Jacques. The banks will convert ADollars 90m of debt into a 39 per cent equity stake in Jennings, while Fletcher will release or convert debt of ADollars 150m and dilute its equity holding in the company from 47 to 43 per cent. Other shareholders will have their equity diluted from 52 per cent to 18 per cent.
