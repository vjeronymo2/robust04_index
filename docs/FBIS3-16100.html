Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vladivostok March 14 TASS -- A U.S. Air Force transport airplane with U.S. State Secretary Warren Christopher on board, has landed in Vladivostok. It arrived from Beijing. Warren Christopher was welcomed on the tarmac by Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, Maritime Territory administration head Yevgeniy Nazdratenko and U.S. Ambassador to Russia Thomas Pickering. Talks between Kozyrev and Christopher began in the airport building directly after the welcoming ceremony. The two-hour talks are expected to cover an extensive range of political, economic and other problems. After the talks Kozyrev and Christopher are expected to hold a 10-minute press-conference for Russian and foreign journalists who have arrived in Vladivostok to cover the event.
