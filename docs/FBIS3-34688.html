Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn and Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat held a meeting today attended by His Highness Crown Prince Hassan. A number of top officials from both sides attended. The Palestinian president briefed His Majesty King Husayn on the outcome of the negotiations between the Palestinian and Israeli sides held in Cairo during the past few days. He explained the points on which the Palestinians and Israeli sides have agreed as well as the points that are still pending and need further discussion. His Majesty King Husayn affirmed to the Palestinian president the importance of continued coordination and consultation because this coordination enables Arabs, particularly Palestinians, to restore their rights and reinforce their negotiating stand. His Majesty King Husayn noted Jordan's constant stand based on supporting the Palestinian brothers in every way possible until they achieve their rights on their national soil and as part of an effort to alleviate the suffering of the kinsfolk under occupation. Also attending the meeting were Dr.'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, the prime minister; Al-Sharif Zayd Bin-SHakir, chief of the Royal Court; Marwan al-Qasim, his majesty the king's political adviser; Dr. Khalid al-Karaki, his majesty's adviser; Dr. Jawad al-'Anani, minister of information; Dr. Fawwaz Abu-al-Ghanam, deputy minister of state for foreign affairs, Dr. Fayiz al-Tarawinah, Jordan's ambassador in Washington and head of the Jordanian negotiating delegation. Attending on the Palestinian side were Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, head of the PLO Information Department; Ahmad Quray', head of the Economic Department; Akram Haniyah, President 'Arafat's special adviser; Faysal al-Husayni, head of the Palestinian negotiating team; and the Palestinian ambassador in Amman. Afterwards, His Majesty King Husayn and Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat held a closed meeting for some time. Following the meeting, his majesty the king and President 'Arafat responded to reporters' questions. The Palestinian president left Amman this afternoon. His Majesty King Husayn and His Highness Crown Prince Hasan bade him farewell. Also present to bid him farewell were Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, the prime minister; al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, chief of the Royal Court; Marwan al-Qasim, his majesty the king's political adviser; Dr. Khalid al-Karaki, his majesty's adviser; the minister of information; the deputy minister of state for foreign affairs; Dr. Fayiz al-Tarawinah, Jordan's ambassador in Washington; and the Palestinian ambassador in Amman. Upon bidding President 'Arafat farewell, His Majesty King Husayn made statements to reporters. He said: [Begin Husayn recording] Through this short visit to his second homeland, His Excellency President 'Arafat provided us with a good opportunity to be briefed on what has taken place and, indeed, he did so. It was also an opportunity for me to affirm that our stand is constant in terms of fully supporting our kinsfolk and brothers in Palestine -- as he is the leader of their march -- to the best of our abilities during these circumstances and in the long run, God willing. I have noticed and I am pleased that our contacts are continuous and coordination between us is being upgraded to the level required to confront challenges and fully shoulder our responsibilities. [end recording] Upon his departure from Amman, Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat made statements to reporters. He said: [Begin 'Arafat recording] First of all, I would like to thank his majesty the king for his warm embracement of all our issues and problems. He also shoulders even our concerns. I would like to express my thanks and appreciation to you. Of course, this is not something strange coming from him. He is always shouldering the concerns of the one Jordanian-Palestinian family. We feel proud and are grateful to his majesty the king for shouldering this family's concerns in the face of all changes and developments. I am pleased to be here today to put his majesty the king in the picture on developments and the talks I held with Peres, as well as talks between the Israeli delegation and the Palestinian delegation. I presented to his majesty the king the official documents that were signed. Moreover, I listened to his good views and ideas on confronting all future issues which we should address through joint efforts and action. This Jordanian-Palestinian family is a united family under his majesty the king's continuous care, God willing. [end recording]
