BFN [Text] The explosion of a bomb in the vicinity of Tehran's Sa'di Avenue caused some damage today. According to the Central News Unit, the bomb was apparently planted inside a car that was parked under Sa'di Bridge. As a result of the explosion, one vehicle was destroyed, a number of passing vehicles suffered some damage, and the windows of several shops were broken. Fortunately, the incident did not cause any deaths. According to witnesses, a number of passers by were injured in the incident. Reliable security sources have announced: The Iraqi regime has recently sent four persons to Iran plant bombs for the purpose of sabotage, and today's explosion was caused by these same saboteurs.
