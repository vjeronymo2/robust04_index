Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The province has accelerated the pace of absorbing foreign firms and investment. Last year, the province topped $1 billion in utilizing foreign funds, an increase of 76 percent over the previous year, and a record high in utilizing foreign funds since the commencement of reform and opening up. Last year, the province continuously improved the investment environment and increased the dynamics of absorbing foreign firms and investment, thus bringing forth a dramatic atmosphere in absorbing foreign firms and investment. Last year, the province approved a total of 1,718 projects that utilize foreign investment, an increase of 95 percent over the previous year. The source of foreign firms and foreign investment and the scale and scope of foreign investment were wider than in the past. Some internationally noted financial groups, transnational corporations, and large corporations arrived in the province to investigate and discuss investment projects. Thus far, more than 100 foreign corporations have set up agencies in the province, and more than 40 foreign countries and regions, including Hong Kong, Macao, Taiwan, the ROK, and the United States, have set up investment projects in the province. The province has also witnessed a good trend that large- and medium-sized state-owned enterprises have utilized foreign funds to conduct renovations. Thus far, more than 500 large- and medium-sized enterprises have conducted renovations. By the end of 1993, the province approved a total of 3,383 projects invested in by foreign corporations, with the contracted value of foreign investment reaching $2.38 billion.
