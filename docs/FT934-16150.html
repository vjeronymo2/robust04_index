931007 FT 07 OCT 93 / World News in Brief: Michael Jordan quits at the top US basketball player Michael Jordan announced his retirement, saying he had reached the pinnacle of his career. Jordan, 30, whose annual earnings and endorsements from companies such as the Nike sports shoe group were put at Dollars 50m, was top scorer seven times in his nine seasons in the National Basketball Association with the Chicago Bulls team. His departure deprives the NBA of its most charismatic attraction.
