October 21, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BEER BAN AT COLISEUM

Hurray, hurray, viva.

Finally the decision to curtail and control the sale of beer at football games and other events is catching up.

Indeed it is a great relief toward the enjoyment of sports and entertainment, rather than fights and violence, and it lessens the impact of alcohol on the physical and psychological lives of the drinkers and victims of alcohol-related problems.

A sober community is a healthy society.
