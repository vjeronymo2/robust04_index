941108 FT 08 NOV 94 / World News in Brief: Search for crew as trawler sinks off Netherlands Helicopters and lifeboats searched off the Netherlands in thick fog last night for a skipper and his five Dutch crew. They were unaccounted for when their British-registered trawler sank after a collision with a Maltese-registered bulk carrier. Meanwhile one woman from a Russian ship died but 17 were rescued by helicopter from a drifting lifeboat at Lerwick harbour in the Shetland Islands.
