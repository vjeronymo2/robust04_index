921002 FT 02 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Anglo-Eastern jumps to Pounds 530,000 Anglo-Eastern Plantations, which has cocoa, oil palm and rubber plantation interests in Indonesia, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 106,000 to Pounds 530,000 in the six months to June 30. Turnover was Pounds 2.26m, compared with Pounds 1.3m. The company said cocoa production had been about 10 per cent above and rubber production 10 per cent below budget. Average selling prices of palm oil improved significantly, while cocoa and rubber prices remained static. Earnings per share improved to 1.4p (0.3p). An interim dividend of 0.375p has already been paid.
