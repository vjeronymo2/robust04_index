941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / Arts (Openings): Copenhagen The distinguished Danish choreographer Flemming Flindt has turned his talents to opera for the first production of the new season at the Royal Theatre. He is staging the Danish premiere of Prokofiev's The Love for Three Oranges, which opens on Friday. The conductor is Jan Latham-Koenig, and the cast includes Mikael Melbye and Inger Dam Jensen.
