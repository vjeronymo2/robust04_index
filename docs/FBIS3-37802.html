Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "Diplomatic Panorama" feature] [Text] Moscow is prepared to start talks on joining the "Partnership for Peace" program at any time, a senior member of Russia's Foreign Ministry told Interfax on Monday [21 February]. According to him, the conference that was to be held between Russian and NATO experts on Monday has again been postponed because of developments in the Balkans. Russia would like to clarify some issues before joining the NATO program, Interfax's interviewee said. "This is why the upcoming conference will have the nature of a briefing," he noted. NATO experts will have to elucidate their vision of Russia's participation in the partnership program," he said.
