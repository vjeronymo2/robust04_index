941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / International Company News: Matsushita to use IBM chip Matsushita, Japan's largest maker of consumer electronic products, yesterday became the fourth Japanese company to adopt International Business Machines' microprocessor technology for multimedia products. Matsushita has signed a letter of intent with the US computer group jointly to develop multimedia products using IBM's PowerPC chip, a fast 64-bit microprocessor for personal computers.
