930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Ticketing Group moves back into black HELPED BY a strong contribution from acquisitions, the Ticketing Group staged a recovery in the first half to June 30, reporting a pre-tax profit of Pounds 273,000 for the six months. The turnround, from a deficit of Pounds 11.2m last time, was achieved on turnover up from Pounds 3.38m to Pounds 20.9m and included the results of Ticketing (UK) for the first time. The USM-quoted group, which operated as a joint venture between Expedier and Wembley until early this year, is involved in retail ticket sales, event management services, and the provision of computerised ticketing systems. The directors said the process of consolidating the company's position following the rights issue in January continued throughout the period. Earnings per share came out at 0.04p (12.28p losses). As explained at the time of the rights issue, there is no dividend.
