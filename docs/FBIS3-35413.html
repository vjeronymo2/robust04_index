Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 24 Feb (INA)--Staff Lieutenant General Ahmad Ibrahim Hammash, assistant to the chief of staff of the army for administration, has denied imperialist and Zionist propaganda about drafting reserves or disbanding others who have completed their compulsory service. In a statement to AL-QADISIYAH published today, the assistant to the chief of staff said those conscripts born in 1973 and who have completed their service will be released in three groups in accordance with a timetable. He said that reports of a draft of new reserves are baseless.
