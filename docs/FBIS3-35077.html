Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by Amjad Mu'alla] [Text] Finance Minister Sami Qammuh has said continued use of the Jordanian dinar in the occupied territories after establishment of the Palestinian entity will not affect its value, but will keep the dinar strong and stable. Answering questions posed by journalists yesterday, Qammuh said the stability of the dinar inside and outside Jordan is guaranteed by the foreign currency cover. He expressed the hope that coordination and cooperation in all fields will continue between Jordan and the Palestinian entity in the future. Qammuh held a news conference at the Customs Department in which he explained all aspects of the implementation of the sales tax and its impact on the Jordanian economy and citizens.
