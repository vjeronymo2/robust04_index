930116 FT 16 JAN 93 / Pensions change is proposed THE government could increase tax revenues by Pounds 1bn a year by ending the exemption to those who take a portion of their pension in a lump sum on retirement, the Institute for Fiscal Studies, said yesterday in a report financed by the National Association of Pension Funds. But it said there was no case for substantial changes to the tax structure for occupational pension funds. The government hopes to encourage people who opt out of the state scheme to stay out of it until they reach retirement age. Taxation of Private Pensions. Institute for Fiscal Studies, 7 Ridgmount Street, London WC1E 7AE. Pounds 10.
