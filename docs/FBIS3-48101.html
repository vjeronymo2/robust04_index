Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 19 (KCNA) -- A film show was held here Tuesday to mark the 29th anniversary of the start of the Palestinian revolution. Invited there were Palestinian Ambassador to Korea Shahir Mohammed and his embassy officials. Chu Song-il, chairman of the C.C. [Central Committee], the General Federation of Trade Unions and chairman of the Korean-Palestinian Friendship and Solidarity Committee, and other officials concerned were present at the show together with working people in the city. The participants saw Korean documentary and feature films.
