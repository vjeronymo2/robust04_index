BFN [Text] Beijing, May 13 (XINHUA) -- Chinese State Councillor and Minister of Defense Chi Haotian met here this morning with Air Chief Marshal Siriphong Thongyai, commander in chief of the Royal Thai Air Force, and his party. Extending his welcome to the Thai guests, Chi said that he visited Thailand three times and friendly feelings of the Thai people and armed forces towards the Chinese people and armed forces left him a deep impression. Chi said that China and Thailand, two important countries in Asia, enjoy very good relations with constant bilateral exchanges. The development of Sino-Thai friendly relations and cooperation is of great significance to safeguarding peace in Asia as well as the world. He noted that Siriphong's visit to China will further friendly relations between the two countries and the two armed forces, and wished for the friendship to last as long as China's Chang Jiang River and the Thai Chao Phraya. Siriphong expressed his thanks for the warm reception granted him and said that Thailand and China, both located in Asia, are very important partners sharing many similar points. He said that friendly relations between the two countries and the two armed forces will continue to be consolidated. General Cao Shuangming, commander of the Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army, was present at the meeting. The Thai guests arrived here yesterday at the invitation of Cao.
