940512 FT 12 MAY 94 / UK Company News: British Linen surges Pre-tax profits of British Linen Bank, the merchant banking arm of Bank of Scotland, showed a marked increase from Pounds 1.5m to Pounds 10.8m in the year to January 31. Mr Eric Sanderson, chief executive, said that levels of provisioning and non-performing debt continued to drop, and the bank had seen a significant improvement in leasing and fund management.
