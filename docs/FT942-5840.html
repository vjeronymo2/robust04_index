940602 FT 02 JUN 94 / International Company News: US copper producer sells gold interest Phelps Dodge, the US copper producer, has sold its 72.25 per cent interest in the Seven-Up Pete joint venture gold project in Lincoln, Montana, for Dollars 150m, Reuter reports from Phoenix, Arizona. It said it agreed to sell the stake to a unit of Echo Bay Mines and a unit of Canyon Resources. Echo Bay said separately it was acquiring from Phelps a 55 per cent interest in the gold project for Dollars 114m. It said Canyon Resources, which owns a 27.75 per cent interest in the venture, would increase this to 45 per cent.
