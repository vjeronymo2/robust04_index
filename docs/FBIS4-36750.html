BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 4 Jun (MENA)--The Consultative Council's Arab and Foreign Affairs and National Security Committee has stressed the importance of halting the current fighting in Yemen and exerting efforts by all sides to stop the escalation of the crisis there to preserve the fraternal Yemeni people. At a meeting this evening under its chairman Dr. Mufid Shihab, the committee urged a quick halt to the civil war there to prevent foreign interventions in fraternal Yemen. Meanwhile, the committee members affirmed their absolute eagerness to improve the brotherly relations with the Sudanese people. They also affirmed the need to take the necessary measures to prevent the escalation of the Sudanese officials' provocative stands. [passage omitted]
