930429 FT 29 APR 93 / UK Company News: Ptarmigan suspended at 10p Shares in Ptarmigan were suspended yesterday at 10p pending announcement of an acquisition. Last October trading in the company was suspended temporarily at 2p pending shareholders' approval of a reorganisation. Ptarmigan announced a change in activity with the acquisition of three engineering companies. In the year to June 30 1992 a reduced pre-tax loss of Pounds 735,000 was announced.
