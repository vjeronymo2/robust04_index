BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf met at the Foreign Ministry this morning with Rolf Ekeus, chairman of the UN Security Council's Special Commission. During the meeting, they reviewed the achievements made since the visit of Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz to New York in mid-March. The meeting was attended by Engineer 'Amir Muhammad Rashid, the director of the Military Industrialization Organization, and Dr. Riyad al-Qaysi, the Foreign Ministry under secretary. Technical talks between Iraq and the UN Special Commission are still going on at the Foreign Ministry.
