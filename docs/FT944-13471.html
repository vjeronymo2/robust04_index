941027 FT 27 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Bridport-Gundry Bridport-Gundry reported a more than fourfold increase in pre-tax profits for the year ended July 31, from Pounds 162,000 to Pounds 753,000. Sales at the technical textile manufacturer were flat at Pounds 27.1m (Pounds 27.4m), with the proportion of revenue from overseas rising from 65.4 per cent to 68.4 per cent. Operating profit from continuing operations was 46 per cent ahead at Pounds 1.19m (Pounds 813,000). Net interest and similar charges almost halved, from Pounds 603,000 to Pounds 318,000, as net borrowings were reduced to Pounds 1.8m (Pounds 3m). Earnings per share came out at 6.16p (0.84p). The recommended final dividend is 1.75p, bringing the total to 3p (2.5p).
