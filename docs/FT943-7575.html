940822 FT 22 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): ING/ABN AMRO - Other companies ING Group, the big Dutch financial services concern, releases its second-quarter results on Thursday. This is followed by ABN AMRO, the Netherlands' largest bank, which is due to publish first-half results on Friday. The health of the Dutch economy is expected to help underpin steady growth in both groups' results.
