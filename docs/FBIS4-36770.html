BFN ["Text" of cable from from Saudi King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, custodian of the two holy mosques, to King Husayn; date not given -- read by announcer] [Text] Your Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal, king of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan: I have received your majesty's cable on the occasion of the advent of the Islamic New Year. I am pleased to exchange congratulations with you on this dear occasion, wishing that this occasion will return while your majesty enjoyed good health, while the fraternal Jordanian people enjoyed prosperity under your wise leadership, and while our Arab and Islamic nation realized their noble hopes and goals. Peace and God's mercy and blesses be upon you. [Signed] Your brother Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, custodian of the two holy mosques and king of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia
