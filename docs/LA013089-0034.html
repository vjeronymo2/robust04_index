January 30, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

CS FULLERTON TO MEET FRESNO STATE

Cal State Fullerton will try to improve its record to better than .500 tonight when the Titans play Fresno State in a Big West Conference game in Selland Arena.

Since breaking a 7-game losing streak more than a week ago, Fullerton (8-8 overall, 3-4 in Big West Conference play) has 3 straight victories.

Fresno State (9-8, 4-4) broke a 3-game losing streak Thursday with a 69-52 victory over the University of the Pacific.

Fullerton beat Pacific, 79-75, in overtime Saturday.

Jervis Cole, a 6-foot 6-inch senior forward, is averaging 18 points a game for the Bulldogs, and freshman guard Wilbert Hooker is averaging 12.

Fullerton forward Cedric Ceballos is averaging 21 points and 9 rebounds a game, and guard Mark Hill is averaging 16 points. ROBYN NORWOOD
