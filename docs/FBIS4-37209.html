BFN [Text] Palestinian-Israeli talks have started in the Palestinian self-rule areas to settle the issue of hundreds of Palestinian detainees still in Israeli prisons, in addition to the issue of Palestinian deployment at crossing points, and completing the implementation of the Palestinian-Israeli agreement. Within this framework, Dr. Nabil Sha'th, President 'Arafat's political adviser and minister of planning and international cooperation in the Palestinian authority, today headed for Jericho, where he will meet with the head of an Israeli delegation.
