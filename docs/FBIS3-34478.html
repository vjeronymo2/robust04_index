Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Fatah-the Revolutionary Council [FRC], led by Abu-Nidal, denied today that it is connected with the murder 10 days ago of a Jordanian diplomat in Beirut. In a statement released in Beirut, the movement said: We affirm that we have no connection, either closely or distantly, with the assassination of the first secretary [as heard] of the Jordanian Embassy in Lebanon. The FRC statement asserted its confidence in the Lebanese judiciary and its integrity. The statement accused various agencies and parties of being behind this well-devised scenario, as the FRC statement put it.
