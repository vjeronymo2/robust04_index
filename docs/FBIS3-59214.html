Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Munster (DDP/ADN) -- Following the example of the French-German armed forces, a joint corps will now be set up with the Netherlands. Federal Defense Minister Volker Ruehe and his Dutch counterpart, Relus ter Beek, reached agreement on further details in Munster today. A joint corps staff is to be established from October and the multinational corps is to be operational from 1995. A Dutch officer is to be the first commanding general. The official language is English, and German and Dutch are to be treaty languages.
