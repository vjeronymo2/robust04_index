Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] Financial aid for the satellite program of the Albanian Radio-Television. The Albanian Cultural Association Dardania, based in Mannheim in Germany, which is made up of Kosovar emigrants and acts as an independent association, as an Albanian chamber, is active in its solidarity with the aim of preserving our language, culture, and traditions. This Association has recently assisted the Albanian Radio-Television Satellite program with 3,505 German marks. The money was gathered by the Association led by its chairman, Mr. Idriz Hoti, and our compatriots. We thank them for this assistance.
