October 19, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH JANICE;

THE REMATCH

My longshot, Pocket Veto, did little to balance my budget for the day as she finished eighth in Thursday's fourth race costing me $30. Today, I will bet $25 to win and place on Hajasala in the fifth race.

Janice Goldinger's Thu. bankroll: $2,126

Thursday's Loss: $30

Current Bankroll: $2,096

SURELOCK, wishing trifecta wagering were legal, was a winner when his exacta box of Really Brilliant, Saratogan and Charging Raj ran one, two, three in the eighth. Today, he will try to continue his success with $40 to win and place on Comical in the seventh.

SURELOCK's Thu. bankroll: $700

Thursday's Profit: $14

Current Bankroll: $714

Note: Janice Goldinger, a horse owner, does not bet on races her horses run in. SURELOCK does not own horses.

Both players started with a $2,000 bankroll.
