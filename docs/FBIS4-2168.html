BFN [Text] New York, April 6 (XINHUA) -- The First National Bank of Chicago and Bank of China today jointly announced the closing of a 261 million U.S. dollar transaction, backed by an Export-Import Bank of the United States (Eximbank) guarantee, to finance the purchase by China Southern Airlines (Group) of seven Boeing 737 aircraft. Under the deal, which marks the first time that a Chinese company will raise long-term funds in the U.S. securities market with credit support from Eximbank, the guarantee extends up to 201 million U.S. dollars, according to the First Bank of Chicago. The remaining 60 million dollars will be financed through a syndicated loan arranged by First Chicago Bank and Bank of China and guaranteed by the Bank of China. First Chicago Capital Markets, Inc., a subsidiary of First Chicago Corporation, is the sole underwriter of the AAA\AAA rated certificates, which will be sold to institutional investors. The First National Bank of Chicago is the main bank subsidiary of First Chicago Corporation, which has 53 billion dollars in assets and is the nation's 11th largest bank holding company.
