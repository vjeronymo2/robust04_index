April 12, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: MISSION VIEJO;

COUNCIL PUSHES BACK ITS MEETING TIMES

The City Council has announced changes in its Monday meeting times.

The afternoon session will now begin at 5:30 instead of 3 p.m., and the evening session will start at 7:30 p.m. instead of 7.

The changes were made at the request of Councilman Robert A. Curtis, who said his job as an assistant district attorney for Riverside County made it difficult for him to attend the afternoon session.
