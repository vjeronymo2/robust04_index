940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / Rise reported in building orders The value of construction orders at constant 1990 prices was 10 per cent higher in the first three months of the year compared with the same period last year, figures from the environment department showed yesterday. However, it was 5 per cent lower than in the previous three months. Private housing orders led the way and were 28 per cent higher than during the first three months of last year.
