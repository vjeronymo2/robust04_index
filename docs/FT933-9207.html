930811 FT 11 AUG 93 / Former Wimbledon caterer charged MR Dieter Phillipe Abt, former chairman of Town and County, the catering company which provides strawberries and cream at the Wimbledon tennis championships, appeared before of City of London magistrates yesterday charged with seven offences of theft. He is accused of stealing an E-type Jaguar car and obtaining loans and overdrafts by falsely claiming he owned a New York apartment, 300,000 BP shares and 100,000 British Gas shares. He was granted Pounds 50,000 conditional bail.
