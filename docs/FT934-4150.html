931206 FT 06 DEC 93 / Italian parties fight for control of 129 cities Francesco Rutelli, the leftwing candidate in the election for the major of Rome, casts his vote. He is running against Gianfranco Fini, leader of the neo-fascist MSI party, in the second round of voting. Elections were held in 129 cities across Italy yesterday but attention was focused on Naples and Rome where the MSI emerged as the single biggest party in first round voting. Reuter
