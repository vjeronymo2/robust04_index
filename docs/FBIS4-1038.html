BFN [Text] The minister of state in the office of the president responsible for defense and national service, the Honorable Abdulrahman Kinana, has assured Tanzanians and foreign residents living and working along the Tanzania-Rwanda border that security is under control, and that there is no cause for alarm. Mr. Kinana was speaking during his two-day visit to areas along the borders with Rwanda and Burundi, where emergency measures have been taken to ensure security following the current killings in Rwanda. During the visit, Mr. Kinana was briefed on the prevailing situation in Rwanda, where members of the Tutsi tribe were being killed, and saw hundreds of dead bodies floating in the Kagera River, which separates Tanzania from Rwanda.
