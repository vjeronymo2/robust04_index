April 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

WE NEED DEADLINE FOR GETTING TAX INFORMATION

There should be a deadline for firms mailing you those envelopes containing income tax information, like, let's say, March 1 of each year! It's very inconvenient for taxpayers to have to delay their returns until all of these "missiles" have arrived. Maybe the penalty to the firm mailing same is needed.

EARL S. DRAIMIN

Van Nuys
