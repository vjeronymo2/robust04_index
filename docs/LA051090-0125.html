May 10, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: PASADENA;

HUGHSTON BECOMES MAYOR

Director William Thomson ended his two-year term as mayor Monday and passed the gavel to Director Jess Hughston, a 10-year board veteran.

Director Rick Cole will serve as vice mayor.

The mayor's post is rotated among the seven directors, who elect a mayor each May.
