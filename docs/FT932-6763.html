930527 FT 27 MAY 93 / Gas field contract is announced THE FIRST land-based contract for the Pounds 1.2bn development of Liverpool Bay's gas field was announced yesterday. The project will deliver offshore gas through a terminal at Point of Ayr at the mouth of the River Dee by pipeline to a new power station at Connah's Quay, Clwyd. The Pounds 60m contract to build the terminal has gone to AOC International. David McLean Contractors of Flint, north Wales, will carry out associated civil works.
