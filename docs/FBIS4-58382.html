BFN [Text] Nepal has called for a peaceful resolution to the Kashmir issue. The call was made by Nepalese Law Minister Maheswor Prasad Singh while speaking to newsmen in Islamabad before his departure for Karachi this morning. He said Nepal wants the resolution of all issues, including Kashmir, in accordance with the spirit of the South Asian Association for Regional Cooperation. This is the reason his country is making efforts to politically resolve the refugee problem with Bhutan. He said his country wants to promote ties with Pakistan in all sectors, including trade. The delegation was seen off by Law, Justice, and Parliamentary Affairs Minister Syed Iqbal Haider.
