940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Warrants and Value Net asset value per share at Warrants and Value Investment Trust stood at 34.83p at June 30, a 12.8 per cent decline from 39.97p at end-December. Net losses for the half year were Pounds 24,000, against revenue of Pounds 34,000 and losses per share were to 0.03p (0.04p earnings).
