June 8, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

No Pipes of Peace for McCartney: Paul McCartney didn't let it be when he was called greedy by the manager of U2 just because he had a corporate sponsor -- Visa -- back his recent tour. McCartney responded with an eight-letter curse word. "My advice to Paul McGuinness is to grow up," McCartney said in Rolling Stone. GREG BRAXTON
