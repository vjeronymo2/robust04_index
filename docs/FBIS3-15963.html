Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Vladimir Taranov] [Text] Moscow, 11 Mar -- Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Vitaliy Churkin flew out to London today for further multilateral consultations on settling the Bosnian crisis. The ITAR-TASS correspondent learned this from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. In London, Vitaliy Churkin is expected to meet with Charles Redman, the U.S. envoy at the talks on settling the Bosnian crisis. Then Vitaliy Churkin plans to visit Zagreb, Sarajevo, and Belgrade.
