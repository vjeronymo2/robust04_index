Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Novi Sad, 28 Mar (TANJUG) -- Momcilo Krajisnik, speaker of the assembly of the Srpska republic, has described as strange the behavior and mediation of UN Protection Force members in most places where the Muslim spring offensive has begun. In a statement to Radio Novi Sad, he assessed that UN forces are attempting to calm the situation only in places where the Muslim forces are not having successes. "It is for precisely this reason that we have sent a protest to the United Nations, stressing in particular the examples of the battlefields near Maglaj, Bihac, and some others," Krajisnik added. He stressed that forecasts of a Muslim offensive have turned out to be true and he confirmed that Muslims are obtaining weapons from abroad.
