BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Andrey Palariya and Sergey Ostanin] [Text] Moscow April 20 TASS -- Russia and Turkey signed an agreement on military and technical cooperation on Wednesday. The agreement was signed by Russian Defence Minister Pavel Grachev and his Turkish counterpart Mehmet Golhan who arrived in Moscow on a four-day official visit today. Golhan has already had talks with Grachev, his first deputy Andrey Kokoshin and Chief of the Russian General Staff Mikhail Kolesnikov. During the visit, the Turkish minister will visit the Mil design bureau, the state company Rosbooruzheniye and the Russian State Committee for the Defence Industry.
