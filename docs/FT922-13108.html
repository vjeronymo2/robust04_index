920414 FT 14 APR 92 / International Capital Markets: Moves at HK broker CHINTUNG, the Hong Kong stockbroking arm of Standard Chartered, has appointed Mr Raymond Theodoulou as managing director. He was formerly managing director of United IBV. Mr Edward Yuen was appointed a director of the corporate sales unit of Chintung. He was formerly an investment manager at Crocodile Garments (Canada).
