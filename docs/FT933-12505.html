930723 FT 23 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Sales fall pushes Elbief deeper in red Elbief, the manufacturer of photograph and handbag frames, mirrors, clocks and accessories for leather goods, saw its pre-tax losses widen to Pounds 592,000 over the 12 months to April 30. The increase from the previous deficit of Pounds 451,000 came on turnover down 16 per cent at Pounds 3.74m.
