920221 FT 21 FEB 92 / UK News (Employment): Safety probe THE HEALTH and Safety Executive yesterday announced a national inspection blitz on the roofing industry. The industry accounts for one in five construction deaths. Sir John Cullen, chairman of the Health and Safety Commission, the executive's ruling body, said: 'Roof work is big business and attracts more than its fair share of the 'get-rich-quick' brigade.'
