October 13, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION ROUNDUP;

TRI-VALLEY LEAGUE

Oak Park 28, St. Bonaventure 0 -- Oak Park (3-3, 1-0) scored all of its points in the first half and limited St. Bonaventure (2-4, 0-1) to minus-21 yards rushing in the first two periods at Ventura High.

Oak Park defender Danian Delfino intercepted three passes. David Stern scored Oak Park's final touchdown on a fake field goal on the last play of the first half. MIKE BARTLETT
