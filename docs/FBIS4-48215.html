BFN [Communique from the Niger Media Workers' Union Executive Council; place and date not given] [Text] We have learned with dismay and indignation of the arrest of four fellow members of the Customs Workers' Union [SNAD], including the union's secretary general, who is also the financial secretary for the Federation of Labor Unions of Niger. On behalf of all state media workers, the national executive council denounces this action, which does not foster a social climate conducive to the spirit of partnership. It demands the unconditional release of the SNAD workers and urges the government to show better judgment in the search for a solution to workers' problems.
