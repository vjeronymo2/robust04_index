941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Budget 94 (Government Statements): Operating licences awarded to 12 Tecs Twelve training and enterprise councils, the employer-led bodies that administer government training schemes in England and Wales, are to be awarded three-year operating licences - the first to gain them. The employment department said: 'The new arrangements, which in many respects are similar to the preferred supplier relationships common in industry, are being introduced to provide stability, ensure continuous improvement in the performance of TECs, and to strengthen the framework in which TECs operate as key private sector partners.'
