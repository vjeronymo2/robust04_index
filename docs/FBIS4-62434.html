BFN [Text] The Ukrainian Defense Ministry press service reported that the delegation of the Hungarian Army, led by Miklos Csurgai, its anti-aircraft defense commander, has completed its visit to Ukraine. Together with his colleagues he visited several units and subunits of this service of our state's armed forces. Negotiations were held with the command of the anti-aircraft defense of the Ukrainian Army and a protocol was signed on the results. Agreements were reached on a regular exchange of experience and other trends of cooperation.
