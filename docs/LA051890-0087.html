May 18, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

SERVICES PLANNED IN N.Y. FOR JIM HENSON

A spokesman for Jim Henson's production company said Thursday that those wishing to honor the memory of the creator of the Muppets may contribute to the Henson Foundation at 117 E. 69th St., New York City 10021. The foundation supports emerging artists in the field of puppetry.

Henson was 53 when he died Wednesday in New York, where a memorial service will be held Monday at the Cathedral of St. John the Divine.
