Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 20 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Il-song, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and president of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, on March 18 sent a message of greetings to Sam Nujoma, president of the South West Africa People's Organisation and president of the Republic of Namibia, on the occasion of the fourth anniversary of the independence of Namibia. President Kim Il-song in his message wished the president and people of Namibia greater success in the work to consolidate the national independence and achieve the prosperity of the country and expressed the belief that the friendly relations between the two parties and two peoples would further develop.
