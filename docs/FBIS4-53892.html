BFN [Text] Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet signed a decree dated 1 May 1994 on the establishment of Thanh Hoa City in Thanh Hoa Province. The decree points out: The establishment of the city named Thanh Hoa will be based on the administrative units, area, population, and boundaries of the former Thanh Hoa provincial capital. The Thanh Hoa city party committee, people's council, and people's committee convened a press conference on the afternoon of 20 May in Thanh Hoa to make public this decree of the prime minister of the government. The Thanh Hoa city party organization and people have invested six billion dong to build infrastructure, improve road conditions, and consolidate economic establishments and public utility projects to welcome a ceremony scheduled to take place in October this year to make public the prime minister's decree on the establishment of Thanh Hoa City.
