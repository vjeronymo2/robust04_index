BFN [Text] Brother President Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] received yesterday evening Mr. (Larsen), general coordinator of the Special Committee for Palestinian police who was accompanied by officials in charge of the international presence in Hebron from Norway, Denmark, and Italy. The meetings was also attended by brothers Abu-Mazin [Mahmud 'Abbas], Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, Dr. Nabil Sha'th, and Hebron Mayor Mustafa al- Natshah and the members of its Municipal Council. The practical steps and the mechanisms of the deployment of this international presence in Hebron, in implementation of UN Security Council's Resolution 904, were discussed during the meeting. Brother President Abu-'Ammar stressed the importance of the international role in providing protection to our people and ending the siege and the curfew that has been imposed on the city by the Israeli Army for more than 40 days. He also emphasized the importance of the city's return to normal life. The brother president expressed the PLO's eagerness to cooperate and coordinate with these forces, particularly as the brother mayor is the representative of brother President Abu-'Ammar to the joint committee in Hebron in charge of cooperation with the international presence.
