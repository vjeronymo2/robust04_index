940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / International Company News: Wesfarmers raises bid for Bunnings Wesfarmers, the Australian diversified rural products and services group which last month unveiled a ADollars 535m (USDollars 393.3m) bid for Bunnings made up of cash, shares and options, is adding a full cash alternative to its offer. The new cash alternative is pitched at ADollars 10.40 a share, and values the Perth-based forest products, manufacturing and merchandising company at ADollars 538m. The previous Wesfarmers terms, which remain on the table, are ADollars 22.60 in cash plus two Wesfarmers shares and one option to subscribe for a Wesfarmers at ADollars 12.50 by end-December 1997, in exchange for every four Bunnings shares held.
