940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Sykes-Pickavant accepts bid Sykes-Pickavant, the Lancashire-based hand tool maker, has agreed to a Pounds 7.8m cash bid from Strafor Facom of France. Investors representing 52 per cent of the equity have agreed to the 77p a share offer. The shares closed up 25p at 75p. Strafor Facom, a leading European tool maker and distributor, said the acquisition would extend the range of its Britool UK subsidiary, which it bought in 1990. Sykes reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 305,000 (Pounds 118,000) for the year ended December.
