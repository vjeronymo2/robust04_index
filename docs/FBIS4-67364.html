BFN [Text] The indigenously developed cryogenic payload for measuring ozone depletion was successfully launched from National Scientific Payload Facility in Hyderabad today. Our Bangalore correspondent reports that with this, ISRO [Indian Space Research Organization] has become one among the very few countries in the world to develop and successfully employ this advanced cryogenic technique. Part of ISRO's geosphere program, the cryogenic payload will also measure the greenhouse warming substances in the atmosphere.
