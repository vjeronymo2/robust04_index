September 30, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

DR. EUGENE J. JOERGENSON; SURGEON AND TEACHER

Dr. Eugene J. Joergenson, 84, former chairman of the Los Angeles County Medical Assn. cancer committee.

Born in Stanwood, Wash., Dr. Joergenson was educated at Auburn Academy, Walla Walla College and Loma Linda University. He taught at USC and Loma Linda, and served as president of the Southern California Chapter of the American College of Surgeons. A Glendale surgeon, he was active in the Glendale Adventist Church, Rotary and Chamber of Commerce. On Thursday in Glendale of heart failure.
