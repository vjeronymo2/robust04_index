Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] Khalid Ahmad Kharal, the federal minister for information and broadcasting, said that during the rule of the Islamic Democratic Alliance and its mentor and patron, General Zia al-Haq, local bodies were misused for personal aggrandizement of the ruling alliance leaders. He was commenting on the statement by the opposition leader, in which he had criticized the dissolution of local bodies. He said during the 1988 and 1990 elections, the funds for local bodies were used to run the election campaigns of the Muslim League and its allied parties. On both the occasions, Pakistan Muslim League-Nawaz group used local bodies' funds for personal purposes, which forced the caretaker government to dissolve the local councils before the 1990 elections in order to ensure free and fair elections were conducted. The information and broadcasting minister said that Mian Nawaz Sharif also received substantial assistance from Israel to destabilize the then government. The assistance was provided in the form of steel scraps through Israeli agents either free or at a nominal price. An Israeli ship named (Jamatul) delivered 27,500 tons of scrap worth 240 million rupees.
