Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] Afghanistan welcomes all efforts to bring about a permanent peace. Dr. Najibollah Lefrai, state minister in foreign affairs, told a Bakhtar Information Agency that an Afghan special envoy, who has recently gone to Pakistan, has delivered a message from the Afghan president to Pakistan's president at a meeting. They talked about a permanent peace and explained our principled stand. Dr. Lefrai said the position taken by Afghanistan has not changed. The conspirators should surrender their heavy weapons. The Hezb-e Eslami [Islamic Party of Afghanistan] lead by Golboddin Hekmatyar should give guarantees of no more war against the state.
