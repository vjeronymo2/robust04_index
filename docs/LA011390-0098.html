January 13, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Attorneys for Bob Probert, former Detroit Red Wing but a Canadian citizen, have appealed an immigration judge's order that the former NHL star be deported when he is released from a federal prison.

Probert is scheduled to be released Feb. 7 from the Federal Medical Center in Rochester, Minn., after serving a three-month term for importing cocaine for personal use.
