Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Sofia, January 12 (BTA) -- "Turkey respects Bulgaria's desire to join NATO," said Corps General Teoman Erkan, deputy minister of defence of Turkey, on his arrival at Sofia airport today. "We will do everything possible to support this desire of yours," he went on to say. General Erkan is paying a three-day official visit to Bulgaria at the invitation of the Ministry of Defence. Later in the day, Bulgarian Deputy Defence Minister Boyko Noev conferred with General Teoman Erkan at the Ministry of Defence, whose press centre said the meeting passed in a candid and friendly atmosphere. The sides considered a wide range of questions concerning bilateral relations. [passage omitted]
