BFN [Text] On 29 March, Syrian Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam received in his office in Damascus 'Ali al-Sanjari, member of the Kurdistan Democratic Party political bureau and the party's Arab Affairs official. During their meeting, they discussed the general situation in Iraq, with a particular emphasis on Kurdistan, as well as the recent Iraqi regime's threats. Both sides expressed the desire to strengthen relations between our party and the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party in Syria. Khaddam conveyed greetings from President Hafiz al-Asad to brother struggler Mas'ud Barzani and thanked him for sending a special envoy from the party leadership to take part in a prayer meeting for the soul of deceased Basil al-Asad.
