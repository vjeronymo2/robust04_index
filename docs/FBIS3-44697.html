Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, Jan. 7 (YONHAP) -- National Assembly Speaker Yi Man-sop met with Qiao Shi, chairman of the Standing Committee of the Chinese National People's Congress, Thursday [6 January] evening and called on Beijing to cooperate actively for the reunion of separated family members in South and North Korea. Qiao said Beijing will make efforts at most for the separated families to meet. Yi thanked Qiao for the Chinese Government's active support last year to improve relations between Seoul and Pyongyang, made cool by the North Korean nuclear problem. Reps. Pak Hun-ki and Yi Hwan-ui of the ruling Democratic Liberal Party, Reps. Kim Tae-sik and Cho Chae-sung of the Democratic Party and Rep. Pak Ku-il (independent) are traveling with Yi.
