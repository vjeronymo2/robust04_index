August 6, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

WHEN THE SIVER SCREEN TURNS RED: OPINIONS ON MOVIE VIOLENCE;

'GRATUITOUS VIOLENCE IN MOVIES IS DOING MAJOR DAMAGE...'

Regarding Benson's comment about "unceasing vigilantism": I would remind her that Bernard Goetz did more for the safety of New York City subway riders in 15 seconds than the politically hamstrung and corrupt New York Transit Authority had accomplished in 20 years.

ALAN M. SCHWARTZ

Pasadena
