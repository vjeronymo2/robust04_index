BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 18 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received congratulatory messages on the occasion of his birthday from "Mindan"-lining [as received] compatriots living in Tokyo, Osaka, Kyoto, Hokkaido, Ibaraki and other regions of Japan and South Koreans staying in Japan. Noting that President Kim Il-song has built Korean-style socialism centred on the popular masses in the Northern half of Korea and wisely led the struggle to accelerate the reunification of the country, the messages express the determination to wage a vigorous struggle in response to the 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation for the reunification of the country. The messages sincerely wish President Kim Il-song a long life in good health.
