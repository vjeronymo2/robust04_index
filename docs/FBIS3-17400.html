Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Riga, Mar 24, BNS -- The European Union [EU] has issued an official statement welcoming the agreement on troop withdrawal reached by Latvian and Russian negotiators March 15 in Moscow. [passage omitted] According to the EU statement, the agreement is a considerable step toward the full withdrawal of Russian forces from the Baltic states as stipulated by the CSCE 1992 Helsinki declaration. The accord may also contribute to establishing goodneighborly relations between Latvia and Russia, EU said. The presidents of Latvia and Russia are expected to sign the withdrawal agreement in April.
