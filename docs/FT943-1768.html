940922 FT 22 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Devpt lifts net asset value Kleinwort Development Fund raised net asset value per share by 24 per cent from 354.92p to 439.85p in the 12 months to July 31 1994. After administration expenses of Pounds 456,658 (Pounds 381,525) revenue before tax came to Pounds 1.02m (Pounds 842,241). The dividend for the year has been raised by 5 per cent to 11p (10.5p), including a final of 8.25p. Earnings per share were 12.95p (10.29p).
