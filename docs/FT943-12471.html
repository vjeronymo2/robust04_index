940725 FT 25 JUL 94 / Company News (This Week): Reuters Holdings - Other companies Reuters Holdings: The UK financial information and news group will emphasise its international and high-tech credentials on Wednesday by presenting interim results in New York. The meeting will be transmitted live by satellite to London, where analysts expect pre-tax profits of about Pounds 245m for the six months to June 30, compared with Pounds 215m last time.
