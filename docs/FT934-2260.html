931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / World Trade News: French farmers' union protest against US-European trade deal Hardline French farmers' unions yesterday protested against the US-European Union deal on agricultural trade, David Buchan reports from Paris. Some 300 members of Co-ordination Rurale demonstrated in central Paris and a delegation tried to get to see Mr Edouard Balladur, the prime minister, but were blocked by riot police. In the Marne area east of Paris, some 700 farmers used tractors to block roads yesterday. There were similar protests in the Lille and Calais areas, and in the south of the country.
