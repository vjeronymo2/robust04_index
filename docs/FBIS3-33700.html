Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The House of Representatives today held a session under House Speaker Tahir al-Masri and in the presence of Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali and the ministers. The House discussed a number of its agenda items. Details are in the following report by our correspondent: At the outset of the session and before discussing the agenda, House Speaker Tahir al-Masri announced the death of Senate Member Bahjat al-Talhuni. He also denounced the wicked attack on late martyr Na'ib 'Umran al-Ma'ayitah, counselor at the Jordanian Embassy in Beirut. [passage omitted] The House agreed to issue a statement denouncing this heinous crime. Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali said that the Jordanian Government is in constant contact with the Lebanese Government and will be kept informed about the investigations in this case as Jordan has asked to take part in these investigations. Dr. al-Majali said: [Begin al-Majali recording] In fact, there have been contacts with the Lebanese Government between the minister of interior and his counterpart over there [word indistinct] on the investigations. They said that there are vague leads and we have asked to take part somehow in order to be directly informed about developments. So far there are no fingers pointing to any particular party. However, when such developments take place, we will inform the House, thank you. [end recording] Afterwards, the House began to discuss items on the agenda. The House listened to the government's response to questions posed by a number of deputies dealing with citizens' public affairs.
