Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 16 (XINHUA) -- Luo Gan, Chinese State Councillor and secretary-general of the State Council, met with visiting Thai Minister of Justice Sawai Phatthano and his party here today. Sawai and his entourage arrived here yesterday. This afternoon, an agreement of judicial assistance in civil and commercial matters and cooperation in arbitration between China and Thailand was signed here by Chinese Minister of Justice Xiao Yang and Sawai Phatthano.
