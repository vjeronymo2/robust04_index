BFN [Text] In a communique issued yesterday evening, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs said that the members of the Algerian community living in Yemen are well. The communique said that Algeria's ambassador in Sanaa is in constant contact with the members of the Algerian community there to provide them with the needed help. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs reassures families who have relatives in Yemen that all measures have been taken in coordination with Algeria's embassy in Sanaa to guarantee consular protection for the Algerian nationals to face the current events in Yemen.
