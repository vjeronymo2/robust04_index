940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / N Korea under pressure North Korea could still forestall a move to international sanctions if it kept its promise quickly to allow inspection of nuclear sites, Mr Winston Lord, US assistant secretary of state, told Congress yesterday, Reuter reports from Washington. Talks with North Korea had been cancelled, and US suspension of Team Spirit military exercises with South Korea was being reconsidered. The International Atomic Energy Agency's board of directors is to meet on Monday to decide what to do about Pyongyang's interference with its inspection team. The matter could be referred to the UN Security Council.
