930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Apax/Gabicci Apax Partners, a venture capital company, said yesterday it was in talks about Gabicci, the casual clothing company which agreed last week to an all-share bid from Helene, a clothing distributor. It is believed Apax is considering backing some ex-Gabicci executives in an all cash bid. Helene's offer values Gabicci shares yesterday at 51p against a closing price of 46p.
