June 22, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Douglas L. Bockus has been named president and chief executive of US Benefits Inc., a wholly owned subsidiary of US Facilities Corp., an insurance and reinsurance holding company in Costa Mesa.

He was also appointed a senior vice president of the parent company. Before joining the company, Bockus had his own company, D. L. Bockus & Associates Insurance Marketing Inc., which has been merged into a subsidiary of US Benefits. His previous experience includes serving as a vice president of Professional Insurance Underwriting Corp., a vice president of Manhattan Life Insurance Co., and a regional vice president for the Life Insurance Company of North America.
