940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Little change at Courtyard Leisure Courtyard Leisure, the USM-traded wine bar and restaurant operator, incurred losses of Pounds 197,693 pre-tax for the half year to September 30. The deficit included a loss of Pounds 24,907 on the sale of the Benjamin Stillingfleet wine bar and compared with last time's loss of Pounds 196,050. Turnover declined to Pounds 979,233 (Pounds 1.06m) and losses per share emerged at 1.47p (1.88p).
