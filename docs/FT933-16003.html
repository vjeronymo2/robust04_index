930706 FT 06 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Castle Mill cuts deficit to Pounds 384,000 CASTLE Mill International, the clothing and household textiles company, cut pre-tax losses to Pounds 384,000 in the 1992 year, compared with Pounds 6.93m, restated for the implementation of FRS 3. There was a pre-interest profit of Pounds 209,000 (Pounds 6.3m losses) after taking into account losses on discontinued activities of Pounds 139,000. Interest costs were lower at Pounds 593,000 (Pounds 630,000). Turnover was Pounds 9.65m (Pounds 9.75m) with Pounds 1.34m relating to the discontinued side. Losses per share were 3.1p (51.7p).
