Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] AL-MU'TAMAR newspaper, mouthpiece of the Unified Iraqi National Congress, has reported that the Free Revolutionary Officers Movement has issued a statement saying that the Iraqi regime's defense minister, 'Ali Hasan al-Majid, was the target of an assassination attempt in March. The paper cites the statement as saying that members of the Revolutionary Officers Movement opened fire at al-Majid outside his house in Baghdad, wounding him in the leg and arm and killing his bodyguard Major Salih and two other guards.
