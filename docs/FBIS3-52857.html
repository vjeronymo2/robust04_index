Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] A terrorist attack was foiled today in the local police station in Ramallah. A young Arab woman from Kafr 'Aqab in the Ramallah District entered the station, pulled out a gun, and aimed it at a policeman. When a security officer pointed his gun at her, she let go of her weapon. She was detained for interrogation by the Judaea District police. A booby-trapped car was identified and safely defused today in Jerusalem's French Hill. An aide of the leader of the military wing of Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] in Judaea and Samaria was today sentenced by the Nabulus military court to seven years in prison. He had planned to detonate a car bomb in East Jerusalem.
