Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Eduard Shevardnadze has assessed the performance of Georgia's government led by Otar Patsatsia extremely negatively. As INTERFAX learned from well-informed sources in Tbilisi, he criticized the Cabinet on Monday at a session held by the parliamentary majority behind closed doors. The parliamentary majority had been formed by Shevardnadze's supporters to ensure more coordinated backing for his reforms. Addressing the session, Shevardnadze expressed dissatisfaction with the work of some ministers, and even raised the issue of forming a new government.
