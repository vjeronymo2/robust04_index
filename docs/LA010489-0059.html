January 4, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Unbeaten Michael Nunn of North Hollywood will face Sumbu Kalambay of Italy March 25 at the Las Vegas Hilton Hotel in a middleweight championship bout.

Nunn, 25, has a 32-0 record with 22 knockouts and holds the International Boxing Federation middleweight title. Kalambay, a 32-year-old native of Zaire, is 46-3-1 with 26 knockouts and is the World Boxing Assn. middleweight champion.
