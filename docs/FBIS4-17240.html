BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 20 April (SNARK) -- According to an order of the Georgia's Cabinet of Ministries, now the Government of Abkhazia will sit in Zugdidi city, instead of Tbilisi. The order, which was published in "SVOBODNAYA GRUZIA" newspaper, allows the Georgian Corporation of TV and Radio Broadcast to censor information about Abkhazian-Georgian relations. The Defence Ministry of Georgia has been charged to present a concrete plan of special actions at the zone of Kodor canyon in Western Georgia to the Coordinating Headquarters on Abkhazian problems till 20 April.
