Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondent for TASS] [Text] Kiev, 27 Mar -- Over 67 percent of the electorate took part in today's elections of people's deputies in Ukraine. In accordance with the law, this means that the election is valid, a UKRINFORM correspondent was told at the Central Electoral Commission. The highest turnout was recorded in Ivano-Frankovsk, Lvov, and Poltava Oblasts, where between 84 and 92 percent of those eligible to vote came to the polling booths. In oblasts throughout the country, between 70 and 76 percent of the electorate took part in the voting, while in eight other oblasts the turnout was between 60 and 69 percent. Over 50 percent of the electorate cast their votes for parliamentary candidates in the Republic of Crimea. According to preliminary reports, the elections were valid in 445 out of 450 constituencies.
