Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Bileca, 3 March (TANJUG) -- The information service of the [Bosnian Serb army] Herzegovina corps said this morning that Muslim forces resumed their sporadic combat activities in the upper and central Drina river basin last night. More intense Muslim attacks were in evidence in the wider Gorazde area. The targets were Foca and Cajnice. Serb military sources said that despite their latest agreement, Croats and Muslims had resumed their clashes in the Neretva valley. Fighting went on throughout the night in Mostar's southern zone, and in the area of Jablanica, TANJUG has learned from Serb military sources.
