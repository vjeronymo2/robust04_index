Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Vladimir Gundarov report: "Nuclear Fuel Kept Under Padlock, Whereas Electronic Security Lock Was Needed"] [Text] The more than 1 kilogram of nuclear fuel intended for Northern Fleet submarine reactors whose disappearance from a depot in Murmansk was detected on 27 November has not been located as of today. "The thieves, who are most probably nuclear specialists, knew how to remove the so-called core from the coolant elements at the actual scene of the crime," Major General of Justice Fedor Shepetov, military prosecutor of the Northern Fleet, said. "This is the second similar case in the last six months."
