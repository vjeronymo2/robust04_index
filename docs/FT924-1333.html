921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Tinsley Robor reduces losses Tinsley Robor, the specialist printing and packaging company, reduced its pre-tax losses from Pounds 398,000 to Pounds 132,000 in the six months to September 30. This was struck after a rise in operating profits to Pounds 226,000 (Pounds 30,000), a fall in interest charges to Pounds 358,000 (Pounds 428,000), and a provision for a single bad debt of Pounds 195,500. Mr John Rose, chairman, said that trading conditions had remained 'difficult across the whole group with continued severe pressure on margins'. However, turnover advanced 15 per cent to 12.4m, with most of that growth coming from the music sector. Losses per share shrank to 0.38p (1.37p) and there is no interim dividend (0.75p).
