BFN [Text] In Kashmir Valley, six persons, including four militants, were killed in the last 24 hours. An official spokesman said in Srinagar today that the security forces arrested 39 militants, including five hard-core, and recovered a large quantity of arms and ammunition. Meanwhile, the day curfew was lifted in Bhadarwah town of Doda District today following improvement in the situation. Night curfew from 1900 till 0700 will however continue as a precautionary measure.
