Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Catherine Lalumiere, secretary general of the Council of Europe, has said Turkey's stability is important for all the European countries. She expressed the belief that Turkey will overcome certain difficulties it is experiencing, adding that stability in Turkey means stability in Europe. Lalumiere ended her contacts in Ankara and left Turkey this evening. In a statement to Turkish Radio and Television at Esenboga Airport, she said that while in Turkey she exchanged views on the problems of the countries in the region. I also had the opportunity to receive information on the [word indistinct] problems, she said.
