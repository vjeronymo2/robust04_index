930602 FT 02 JUN 93 / Danish aid truck attacked by artillery fire A British UN soldier drives away a Danish aid truck which was badly damaged when a UN relief column was hit by artillery fire in northern Bosnia yesterday. Two Danish drivers and a local interpreter were killed and four Danes wounded. Meanwhile, the bodies of two Italians - reported to have been shot while delivering food aid in central Bosnia on Saturday - were found yesterday. Report, Page 3
