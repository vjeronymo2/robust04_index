March 15, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: SIERRA MADRE;

A CASE OF COURT CONGESTION

The cost of switching Sierra Madre from the Pasadena Municipal Court District to the Santa Anita Court District will be examined by the city manager and the police department.

The change was proposed by City Councilman George Maurer, who believes the Pasadena system is too congested. Using the Santa Anita Municipal Court in Monrovia will be faster and more convenient for Sierra Madre residents and Sierra Madre police officers who need to appear in court for traffic citations and misdemeanor violations, he said.
