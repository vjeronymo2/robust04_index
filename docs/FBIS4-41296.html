BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 528 ] [Text] Vilnius, June 23, ELTA -- Department for Information and Media at the Lithuanian Foreign Ministry has distributed the following statement. President of Russian Federation Boris Yeltsin June 21 has signed the decree "On Demarcation of Borders Between the Russian Federation and Estonian Republic". On this basis the Russia is going to demarcate the border between the states unilaterally. Foreign Ministry of Lithuania expresses concern about such a position of Russia that contradicts the requirements of the European Security and Cooperation Conference to strive for agreements by means of negotiations, without any pressure. The Ministry encourages Russia to abstain from unilateral actions which could threaten the stability in the region.
