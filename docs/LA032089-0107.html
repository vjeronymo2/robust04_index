March 20, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

5.0 QUAKE JOLTS GREEK ISLANDS

A sharp earthquake, the fourth in two days, jolted a wide region around Athens today but there were no immediate reports of damage or injury, the Athens Geological Institute said. The quake, with a magnitude of 5.0, struck at 12:23 p.m. local time with its epicenter in the sea around the northern Sporades Islands 90 miles north of Athens, it said.
