BFN [Text] Cairo, 20 Jun (MENA) -- Lakhdar Brahimi, the UN secretary general's envoy to Yemen, left Cairo for the French capital today following a three-day visit to Egypt. During his stay Brahimi met with President Husni Mubarak, Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa, Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid, and two delegations from north and south Yemen.
