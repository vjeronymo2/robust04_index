BFN [Text] The decree on the constitutional conference has been signed into law by the head of state, General Sani Abacha. This was announced today in Enugu by the chief of general staff, Lieutenant General Oladipo Diya, as a meeting with traditional leaders and leaders of thought in the eastern part of the country. On the economy, Gen. Diya called on Nigerians to engage more seriously in the productive sector for self-reliance.
