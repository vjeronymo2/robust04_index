December 21, 1989, Thursday, Valley Edition

AUTOPSY FOR CSUN FOOTBALL PLAYER GRAY RESCHEDULED

An autopsy of Tom Gray has been rescheduled for today, spokesman Robert Dambacher of the L. A. County coroner's office said Wednesday.

Gray, 21, the starting nose tackle on the Cal State Northridge football team, died late Friday night of cardiac arrest. The 6-foot-3, 235-pound junior had been visiting with friends near the pool of the University Park Apartments in Northridge before collapsing suddenly.

An autopsy had been scheduled for Wednesday.
