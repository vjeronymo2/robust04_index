930814 FT 14 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Expansion by Select Appoints SELECT Appointments, the USM-quoted recruitment consultancy, is buying Accountants Inc of California and Hughes Castell with offices in London and Hong Kong, for a total maximum of Pounds 5.87m cash. Accountants reported pre-tax profits on continuing activities of Dollars 96,540 (Pounds 64,000) on sales of Dollars 9.5m in the 1992 year. Net assets at the end of the period were Dollars 360,122. Select is paying an initial Dollars 4.9m plus Dollars 1m to Ms Dianne Burr, the company's founder, under a non-competition covenant. There is a profit-related payment to a maximum of Dollars 1.5m. Hughes Castell provides legal recruitment services. In its last financial year it reported profits of Pounds 110,000 on sales of Pounds 750,000. Net assets at the period-end were Pounds 59,000. There is an initial payment of Pounds 481,000 plus a profit-related maximum payment of Pounds 419,000. The initial payments are funded by loans from Waverley (UK).
