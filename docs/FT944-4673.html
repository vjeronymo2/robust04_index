941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Northern Investors Northern Investors, the Newcastle-based venture capital investment trust, reported net asset value per share up 1.3 per cent from 333.6p to 338p over the six months to September 30, compared with a 3.3 per cent fall in the benchmark FT-SE-A All-Share index. Net revenue for the half year was Pounds 118,000 (Pounds 104,000) for earnings per share of 3.2p (2.8p). The interim dividend is unchanged at 1.5p.
