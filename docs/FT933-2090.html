930921 FT 21 SEP 93 / Management (The Growing Business): Hiring a locum personnel officer - In a Nutshell Small companies in south-east London which do not have a full-time personnel officer can now use a new locum service to buy in expertise. The service, launched by Solotec, the South London Training and Enterprise Council, will offer expertise on issues such as employment legislation, staff development, performance appraisal and industrial relations.
