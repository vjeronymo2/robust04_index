941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Protean acquisition Protean, the laboratory equipment and water purification specialist, is to acquire Jenway, the bench-top monitoring and analysis equipment manufacturer, for Pounds 4.15m. Funding will be met with Pounds 2.8m in cash, Pounds 1.2m in loan notes and Pounds 150,000 in Protean ordinary shares. Essex-based Jenway was established in 1973. For the year to July 31, pre-tax profits were Pounds 167,000 after non-recurring expenses, on turnover of Pounds 4.25m.
