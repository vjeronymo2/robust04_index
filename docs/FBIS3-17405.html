Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert ("LRAGIR", "AZG" dailies), 03/24/94 -- The Azeri formations continue the attempts of breaking the Karabakh defense lines in the Fizulinskiy and Agdamskiy districts. The most active attacks were commenced in the south-eastern direction. According to the RMK [Republic of Mountainous Karabakh] Defense Army Headquarters, the Azeri units in the Fizulinskiy district carried out fierce artillery preparations at 6:50am on March 23, followed by an extensive offensive in the direction of the Karabakh defense positions to the east from the Fizulinskiy district center. The Azeri side involved a large quantity of armored equipment in the offensive. Meanwhile the enemy attacked in the direction of the village of Alkhanly, where the Armenian forces captured 4 important heights on March 22. Fierce battles were carried out in both directions, causing significant manpower losses to the both sides. The details are being verified. The battles were still continuing in this direction by 6pm in the evening. The RMK forces have been repulsing the enemy attacks. The Azeri units in the Agdamskiy district commenced an offensive in the direction of the villages of Novruzlu, Yusifjanly and Marzili later in the evening of March 23. The Azeri infantry was supported by 6 tanks and three armored infantry carriers. The Karabakh units drove the offensive back, causing the enemy significant losses in manpower. The Armenian side suffered no deaths, 7 soldiers were wounded. Meanwhile the Azeri Air Force became active again. According to the preliminary information, an Azeri fighter jet approached the RMK air borders twice, apparently pursuing reconnaissance purposes.
