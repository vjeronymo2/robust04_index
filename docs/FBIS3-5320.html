Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 6 -- Prime Minister Goh Chok Tong of Singapore and his party left Ho Hi Minh City this afternoon, ending their official visit to Vietnam. While in Ho Hi Minh City, Prime Minister Goh Chok Tong was received by Mayor Trung Tan Sang, who gave him a brief account of the development of the city and of the investment and business environment in the city. On March 4 the mayor of Ho Chi Mind City gave a banquet in honour of Prime Minister Goh Chok Tong and his party. On March 5 the prime minister and other Singapore guests visited Saigon harbor and attended the ground breaking ceremony for the Ba Son-Keppel shipyard, a joint venture between Vietnam and Singapore. Meanwhile the minister of trade and industry, Yeo Cheow Tong, and the business delegation accompanying Singapore prime minister attended the launching of the Singapore- built handphone system. The Singaporean guests were seen off at Tan Son Nhat Airport by Deputy Chairman of the Ho Chi Minh City People's Committee Phan Chanh Truc and other senior Vietnamese officials.
