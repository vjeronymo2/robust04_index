November 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ELECTION RESULTS

What do you mean the election is over? No more negative ad campaigns? No more back-stabbing, mudslinging "I'm-gonna clean-up-this-state" rhetoric? No more candidates versus the big corporation, greedy land-developing, old-boy network toxic polluters? Oh, what will I do? Same thing I did last election -- not vote out of disgust!

PATRICK CERVANTES

Los Angeles
