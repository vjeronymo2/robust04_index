BFN [Text] Eijiro Hata, Japanese minister of international trade and industry, who met with Deputy Prime Minister Grzegorz Kolodko today, announced the resumption of short-term credit guarantees for Poland. Minister Hata said that prompt servicing of the Polish foreign debt is a condition for the further improvement of financial relations with Japan. During the meeting, Deputy Prime Minister Kolodko and Minister Hata discussed the latest plans by Japanese firms to invest in Poland, the problems of bilateral trade exchange, and Japanese technical aid for Poland.
