940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / Flying bishops Downing Street yesterday named The Venerable John Gaisford, archdeacon of Macclesfield, and The Venerable John Richards, archdeacon of Exeter, to minister to Anglican dioceses opposed to the ordination of women. Archdeacon Gaisford, 59, will be consecrated Bishop of Beverley, and cover northern England, and Archdeacon Richards, 60, will become Bishop of Ebbsfleet and cover southern England.
