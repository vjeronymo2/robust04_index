930227 FT 27 FEB 93 / French jobless close to 3m FRANCE'S struggling Socialist government is entering the final month of the parliamentary election campaign with unemployment just below the psychologically important 3m mark, according to the latest official figures, writes Alice Rawsthorn in Paris. Ms Martine Aubry, employment minister, yesterday announced that the number of unemployed people rose by 0.1 per cent in January to 2.99m, leaving 10.5 per cent of the workforce without jobs. Despite criticism, the Socialists have are clinging to their policy of sustaining a strong currency and low inflation rather than reflate the economy before the elections.
