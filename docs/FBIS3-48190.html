Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Jan. 17 (YONHAP) -- President of Moscow State Technical University (MSTU) Igor Fedorov arrived in Seoul on Saturday [15 January] at the invitation of the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) to discuss academic exchanges between the two organizations, a KAIST spokesman said Monday. Fedorov spoke on Russia's education system and Moscow State Technical University Monday at Taedok industrial complex near Taejon, South Chungchong Province, and will give another address there on Tuesday. He will attend a seminar on radar systems before he returns to Moscow on Thursday.
