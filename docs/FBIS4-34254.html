Peace Commissioner Camacho Solis Resigns PA1706035394 Madrid EFE in Spanish 0319 GMT 17 Jun 94 PA1706035394 Madrid EFE Spanish BFN [Text] Mexico City, 16 Jun (EFE) -- Today, Manuel Camacho Solis, commissioner for peace and reconciliation in Chiapas, resigned his post due to criticisms made by PRI [Institutional Revolutionary Party] Candidate Ernesto Zedillo on his role in the Chiapas conflict. Camacho expressed his disagreement with Zedillo's position, which he said weakened his efforts in the political negotiations with the Zapatist National Liberation Army (EZLN). Camacho's resignation is in a letter to Mexican President Carlos Salinas de Gortari, which Camacho read to journalists at a hotel in Mexico City at 2100 local (0300 GMT Friday).
