BFN [Text] Johannesburg May 10 SAPA -- Pan Africanist Congress [PAC] Secretary General Benny Alexander on Tuesday [10 May] night confirmed he had requested to be relieved of his position to concentrate as the sole PAC provincial assembly representative. "It will not be possible for me to carry the workload as sole PAC PWV [Pretoria, Witwatersrand, Vereeniging] provincial assembly representative and the workload as secretary general. I will, however, remain a member of the National Executive Committee and remain a loyal and disciplined member of the party," he said in a statement. He said the matter had still to be discussed by the National Working Committee. "I must express surprise at reading of my confidential correspondence to the president in the media, especially as the matter has still to be discussed by the National Working Committee," he said.
