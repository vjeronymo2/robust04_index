BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 26 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to President Gnassingbe Eyadema on the 34th anniversary of the independence of Togo. In the message President Kim Il-song wished the president and the people of Togo great success in their work and expressed the belief that the friendly relations between the two countries would continue to develop.
