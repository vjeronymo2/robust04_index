920624 FT 24 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Hambros Inv net asset value lower Hambros Investment Trust, a subsidiary of Hambros, had a net asset value of 46.78p at March 31 compared with 48.6p a year earlier. Net revenue for the 12 months fell from Pounds 1.35m to Pounds 49,065 after a tax credit of Pounds 25,286 (Pounds 325,841 charge). Earnings per share dropped to 0.01p (2.26p).
