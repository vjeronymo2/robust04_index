Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] Border Guard action units are on permanent alert along the southern border in case of possible NATO air strikes in Bosnia, Border Guard Spokesman Attila Krisan said. He added that at the moment there are no signs of border provocations. Two of the 19 Border Guard units are stationed near the Serbian border, but forces can be relocated within a few hours in an emergency situation. The spokesman also said that the Border Guards are prepared to receive refugees.
