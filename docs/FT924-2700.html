921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / Parliament and Politics: Bill to back NHS whistle-blowers A BILL designed to protect National Health Service staff who speak out on patient care and fraud was presented in the Commons yesterday by Mr Derek Fatchett, a Labour frontbench trade and industry spokesman. Mr Fatchett said the private member's bill would 'create a stronger and better health service where staff can speak out on matters of fraud and malpractice without fear of dismissal'.
