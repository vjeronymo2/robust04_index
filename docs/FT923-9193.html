920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / World Trade News: Car giants target Pakistan PAKISTAN'S tiny car market will be supplied with local production from as many as four of Japan's nine motor groups, following Honda's decision to begin manufacturing there, writes Gordon Cramb in Tokyo. Suzuki has a long-established car manufacturing presence in the country, while Toyota has a quarter share in a production venture. The local Bibojee group said in Islamabad yesterday it was near to completing talks on the supply of Sunny cars in knockdown kit form from Nissan.
