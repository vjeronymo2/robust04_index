920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / Little and barge A bulky sea-going barge pushed along the Thames yesterday by its own 'clip-on' tug briefly upstaged HMS Belfast, the historic warship moored near Tower Bridge. The Danish-built Brage, the first sea-going barge to be powered by an attached tug, was making a promotional visit to London.
