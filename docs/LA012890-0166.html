January 28, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: STUDENT SAYS MIAMI QUARTERBACK PUNCHED HIM

University of Miami quarterback Craig Erickson allegedly punched another student four or five times in the face in a fight over a girl, according to a complaint filed with campus police in Coral Gables, Fla.

Jay Dewing, 20, was treated at a hospital for a black eye, a bloody lip and bruises about his forehead after the altercation Thursday at a campus bar. Dewing said he would press charges Monday.

Erickson is 6-2, 196 pounds; Dewing is 5-9, 150.

Dewing, who filed the complaint, said he used to date Erickson's girlfriend and had talked with her about 20 minutes before the altercation.
