940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Surrey seeks Pounds 2.1m as losses reduce Surrey Group, the bookmaker, is seeking Pounds 2.1m to cut gearing and for expansion. The USM-quoted company also announced reduced pre-tax losses for the year to March 31 of Pounds 292,000, compared with Pounds 1.38m. It is issuing 92.7m shares in a 1-for-2 rights issue and placing a further 85.7m shares, both at 1 1/4 p. Its shares were unchanged yesterday at 1 1/2 p. Of the rights, commitments have been received to subscribe for 17.2m shares and the balance underwritten by Ellis & Partners. The reduced loss was achieved on turnover down from Pounds 47.1m to Pounds 35.5m as a result of the sale of 28 betting shops in early 1993.
