May 19, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

THEODORE STRAVINSKY; ARTIST KNOWN FOR CHURCH WINDOWS

Russian-born artist Theodore Stravinsky, 82, the eldest son of composer Igor Stravinsky. Born in 1907 in St. Petersburg, now Leningrad, Stravinsky spent most of his life in Morges, on the shores of Lake Geneva, and made his name painting stained glass windows in Swiss, French and Dutch churches. He wrote several art books and was made a commander of St. Gregori Magni by Pope Paul VI in 1977 for services to the Roman Catholic Church. His father died in 1971. In Geneva on Tuesday.
