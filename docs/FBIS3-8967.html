Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The military delegation headed by Army General Vitaliy Radeskyy, Ukrainian defense minister, arrived in Constanta this morning. The Ukrainian guests were received by the head of the Chief of Staff of the Romanian Navy, Vice Admiral Gheorghe Anghelescu. The Ukrainian delegation's schedule also included visits to the destroyer Marasesti, to the Mircea cel Batran Navy Academy, and to the Romanian sea coast, our correspondent Mircea Lamba reports. The Ukrainian defense minister will be received later today by Romanian President Ion Iliescu and by Foreign Affairs Minister Teodor Melescanu.
