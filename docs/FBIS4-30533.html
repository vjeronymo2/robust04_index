BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 28 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, chairman of the National Defence Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army, received a gift from the friendship visiting group of the Chinese People's Liberation Army [CPLA] on a visit to Korea. The gift was handed to chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army Choe Kwang by the head of the group, General Wang Ke, commander of the Shenyang military area of the CPLA.
