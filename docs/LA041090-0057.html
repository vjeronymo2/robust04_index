April 10, 1990, Tuesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: CAMARILLO;

2 CONDOMINIUMS DAMAGED BY FIRE

A fire swept through two unoccupied units of a Camarillo condominium complex Monday, Ventura County fire officials said.

The fire at 730 Temple Ave., touched off by plumbers using blowtorches, went up a wall and burned two units of the six-unit complex, said dispatcher Paula Aliano.

There were no injuries.
