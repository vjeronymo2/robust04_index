930128 FT 28 JAN 93 / UK Company News: CST Emerging net asset value 53.85p CST Emerging Asia Trust had a net asset value of 53.85p per share at September 30 against 49p a year earlier and 63.3p at the December 31 1992 year end. Net revenue for the six months amounted to Pounds 56,311 (Pounds 21,163 losses) for earnings per share of 0.43p (0.16p losses).
