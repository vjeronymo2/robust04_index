BFN [Text] People are still leaving the country in greater numbers than they are entering it. Figures released by the Central Statistical Services in Pretoria show that more than 1,200 people had emigrated from South Africa in January, compared with 433 in January last year. The number of people immigrating to South Africa dropped from 784 in January last year to 484 in the same month this year. Most of the people leaving South Africa tended to head for England, New Zealand, or Australia.
