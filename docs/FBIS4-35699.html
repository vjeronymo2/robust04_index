BFN [Text] His excellency the president has issued a decree to the following effect: Article 1: Minister of Electricity Engineer Kamil al-Baba shall be relieved of his post. Article 2: This decree shall be published and shall be communicated to those concerned. The president issued another decree to the following effect: Article 1: Engineer Munib As'ad Sa'im al-Dahr shall be named minister of electricity. Article 2: This decree shall be published and shall be communicated to those concerned.
