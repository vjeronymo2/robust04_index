940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Revenue falls at Fleming High Inc Fleming High Income Investment Trust reported lower net revenue of Pounds 1.52m in the year to April 30, against Pounds 1.94m. Earnings per share were 4.7p, against 6.18p, and the fourth interim dividend is cut to 1.1p (1.45p) for a total of 4.4p (5.8p). Net asset value per share at the end of the period was 101p, against 94.1p a year earlier.
