941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Drayton Recovery Drayton Recovery Trust reported net asset value per preferred growth share of 136.6p at October 31, compared with 123.1p a year earlier. The figures per ordinary shares were 105.2p, against 132.8p. Net revenue for the year to the end of October was Pounds 686,000 (Pounds 725,000) for earnings per income share of 8.3p (8.77p). The proposed final dividend is maintained at 4p, making an unchanged total of 8p.
