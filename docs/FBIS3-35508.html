Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] There is currently a tense quiet in Hebron. The security forces acted forcefully to restore order. Our correspondent Gil Litman reports that four Arab residents were apparently killed by IDF [Israel Defense Forces] gunfire. Large crowds of Arabs rioted at the intersection of the road leading from Bethlehem to Bayt Jala. Large Army forces dispersed the rioters with gunshots. Witnesses to the incident told our correspondent Ron Nesi'el that four residents were injured. Two people suffered from gunshot wounds and two others were injured by inhaling tear gas. A general strike is being observed in East Jerusalem. The Fatah organization has declared three days of mourning and a general strike in the Gaza Strike. Arab residents are now throwing stones and rioting throughout Judaea and Samaria. There were serious clashes between local residents and the security forces on the Trans-Judaea highway, near the village of Tarqumiyah. In Hebron, the Border Police shot and wounded a youth who had thrown a firebomb at the policeman. The commander of the Border Police arrived in Hebron in order to closely follow Border Police operations in the area.
