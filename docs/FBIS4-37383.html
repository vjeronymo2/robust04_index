BFN [Excerpt] Kuwait, 19 Jun (KUNA) -- Pilot Staff Colonel Nasir al-Hujayl, commander of Ahmad al-Jabir air base, said today that the joint exercise that the Kuwaiti Air Force is conducting with the French Air Force has great importance in raising the Kuwaiti pilot's competence in dog fighting and ground assault. [passage omitted] He pointed out that six "F-18" combat planes took part in the exercise on the Kuwaiti side, while the French participated with four "Mirage 2000" planes. The commander of the base said that the team which took part in this exercise consisted of 60 people, including pilots, technicians, and administrators. He pointed out that the third phase for rebuilding the Ahmad al- Jabir air base, which was destroyed by the Iraqi forces during the period of occupation, would start during the coming months.
