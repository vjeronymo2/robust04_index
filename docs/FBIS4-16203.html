BFN [Text] Despite the fact that hostages were taken on April 12 on the territory of North Ossetia, the second round of the North Ossetian-Ingushi talks started in Nalchik on Thursday. The delegations are led by Akhsarbek Galazov and Ruslan Aushev. Before the talks the head of the interim authority Vladimir Lozovoy read out a government telegram signed by Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin. It says that the Cabinet worked out and approved measures to carry out the Russian president's decree on the return of refugees. Chernomyrdin expressed hope that both sides will find success in their talks. The delegations started discussing the draft treaty between the two republics "On the Principles, Measures and Terms for the Implementation of the Russian President's Decree for the Return of the Refugees and Displaced People to the North Ossetia and the Ingushi Republic."
