April 1, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ONCE WILD IS WESTCHESTER WINNER

Gary Marano's Once Wild, the odds-on favorite, led all the way in scoring a 1 1/2-length victory Saturday in the $113,000 Westchester Handicap at Aqueduct.

The winner carried high weight of 121 pounds and Angel Cordero Jr. and paid $2.80. Its Acedemic and King's Swan finished in a dead-heat for second.
