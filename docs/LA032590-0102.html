March 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

'DECLARATION' CAUSES OUTPOURING OF DEEPLY DIVIDED OPINIONS

Gosh, your lesbian piece was really on key and wonderfully done.

You also managed to cram into the back part of the article a bunch of good info on us male gays and the lesbian/gay situation in general.

Truly, the "New Gays," as I tend to call the kids in their teens and 20s who are coming out these days (I'm 44), seem to come out more easily and into a space we made for them. As a result, their personalities look more straightforward because they have spent less time hiding from any part of life.

JAMES BAKER

Tecate
