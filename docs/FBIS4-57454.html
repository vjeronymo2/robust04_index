BFN [Excerpt] After arriving in our country to settle the details, Bolivian Vice Foreign Minister Jorge Gumucio announced that Bolivian President Gonzalo Sanchez de Lozada will make an official visit to Peru. According to the Bolivian official, the Bolivian president will arrive in Lima on 5 July. Diplomatic sources indicated that Sanchez de Lozada's visit will be an opportunity to promote mutual cooperation and integration. The confirmation of such an important visit was made by the Bolivian vice foreign minister at a time when there is a lot of interest in Peru to increase trade ties that began with the installation of an industrial free zone in the port of Ilo. The Foreign Ministry has also reported that President Alberto Fujimori has officially invited Colombian President Cesar Gaviria to visit the country. In a letter he sent to Gaviria, Fujimori highlights the close ties that unite the two nations. The invitation was made as a result of the visit Fujimori made to Bogota in February. [passage omitted]
