BFN [Excerpt] Doboj, 3 Jun (TANJUG) -- After several days of fighting on the Ozren-Vozuca front, the fiercest of which took place yesterday, forces of the so-called Bosnia-Herzegovina army have been heavily defeated. Attacking from three directions in the Vosuca area, the Muslims attempted to take control of the Zavidovici-Banovici road, which passes through Vozuca, and in this way link the Zenica and Tuzla regions. Their attacks were repulsed by the defenders of Ozren and Vozuca, and the enemy suffered heavy losses. [passage omitted]
