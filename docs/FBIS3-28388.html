Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Feb. 6 -- A memorandum of understanding on long-term cooperation in the economic, engineering, scientific and related aspects of water, land and environmentul management, development and control between Vietnam and Australia was signed here on Feb. 4. Signatories were Vice Minster of Water Resources Phan Si Ky and Australian Minister of Land and Water Conservation Hon George Souris [name and title as received] who has been here for a working visit to Vietnam. Among those present at the signing ceremony were representatives of the governmental offices, the Ministry for Foreign Affairs and other offices concerned, and Australian counselor proper [name indistinct].
