Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] St.Petersburg, 18 Mar -- The resolution on recommended legislative acts regulating economic relations within the framework of the CIS Economic Union will serve to implement the Treaty on setting up the CIS Economic Union. This is stressed in a communique on the plenary setting of the Interparliamentary Assembly which ended today. The participants in the sitting agreed with the proposal to draw up a model criminal code and a model code of criminal procedure and to speed up the preparation of legislative acts to fight crime in the CIS. Stressing the urgency of maintaining and recreating on new foundations a single educational and culture space within the framework of the CIS, the Assembly recommended that national parliaments adopt measures for the development of cooperation. A recommendation was approved to raise the social status of pupils in the system of education and science, and to join the UNESCO convention "Agreement on the importation of materials of an educational, scientific and cultural nature". It was found expedient to draw up a recommended act for keeping internal waters in CIS ecologically safe. Measures were defined to prepare for the celebration of victory in the 1941-1945 Great Patriotic War.
