July 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: MUSEUM WINS GRANT FOR OUTREACH PROGRAM

The Raymond M. Alf Museum in Claremont has received a $20,000 grant from GTE California Inc. to enhance the museum's Scientific Educational Outreach Program to educate children about the history of life.

The museum is an independent paleontological museum located on the campus of The Webb Schools at 1175 W. Base Line Road.
