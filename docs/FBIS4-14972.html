BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Roman Zadunayskiy] [Text] Moscow, 5 Apr -- A group of Ukrainian military leaders, including Minister of Defense Vitaliy Radetskyy, will arrive in the Crimea today. The ITAR-TASS correspondent learned this from informed sources at the headquarters of the Black Sea Fleet. The purpose of the Ukrainian generals' coming to the peninsula is not known either to the administration of the Crimea or to the command of the Black Sea Fleet. This same source reports that the group also includes Chief of the Intelligence Administration of the Ukrainian Armed Forces Major-General Vehunnikov, commander of the Air Force, Major-General Antonets, chief of staff of the National Guard Mokhov, and Chief of the Second Counterintelligence Administration Major-General Kravchenko. All of the said generals are coming to the peninsula in the course of today separately, by different means of transport, without preliminary agreement with the local authorities, and this has provoked a protest from the leadership of the peninsula. President of the Crimea Yuriy Meshkov is to return to Simferopol after a business visit to Cyprus at 1800 today.
