BFN [Communique issued by ex-King Mihai's Press Bureau in Versoix on 13 April 1994: "King Mihai Has Never Granted an Interview to IZVESTIYA"] [Text] The Press Bureau of His Majesty King Mihai is empowered to issue the following communique: In accordance with the news published by EVENIMENTUL ZILEI on 13 April, we note that His Majesty King Mihai has never held a dialogue with any representative of the Russian daily IZVESTIYA on the issue of the current fate of Romania, the future of the royal family, or on any other issue. Note of the editorial office: Indeed, King Mihai I has not granted an interview to IZVESTIYA. It was a reproduction of an interview granted by His Majesty to the Swiss daily "24 HOURS." The way in which IZVESTYIA presented this interview has caused some confusion.
