Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 14 Jan--Hungarian Foreign Minister Geza Jeszenszky arrived in Warsaw on Friday morning. During his one-day visit to Poland, the Hungarian diplomat will meet President Lech Walesa, Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak, and the deputy Sejm and Senate speakers. He will also hold private talks with Foreign Minister Andrzej Olechowski. The Hungarian delegation, led by Jeszenszky, will conduct talks in the Polish Foreign Ministry. Bilateral relations will be discussed with special emphasis on the lack of progress in economic and trade cooperation.
