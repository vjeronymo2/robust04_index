Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Perkina] [Text] Good evening. The latest from our correspondents: The working meeting between Russian President Boris Yeltsin and Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin, which had lasted six hours, has just ended in the Kremlin. Our agency's correspondent has been told by very well-informed sources that issues connected with finalizing the composition of the government were debated during the meeting, along with other questions. The composition of the government has for the time being not been made public.
