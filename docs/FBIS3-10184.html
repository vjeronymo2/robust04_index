Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] President Itamar Franco has just finished reshuffling his cabinet. A short time ago Planalto Palace announced that General Rubens Bayma Denys, head of the Military Command of the East, will head the Transportation Ministry, replacing Minister Margarita Coimbra. Presidential Press Secretary Francisco Baker has just announced that President Itamar Franco has decided to maintain acting Minister Eleonor Franco as head of the Social Welfare Ministry, and acting Minister Guillermo Morais as head of the Communications Ministry. According to Baker, Israel Vargas will stay at the Science and Technology Ministry; Senator Beni Veras of the PSDB [Brazilian Social Democracy Party] of Ceara is the new planning minister; and Alexis Stepanenko is the new mines and energy minister. Lastly, Francisco Baker said that President Itamar Franco has nominated Deputy Aluizio Alves of the PMDB [Brazilian Democratic Movement Party] of Rio Grande do Norte to head the Ministry of Regional Integration. He had been proposed for the post by the PMDB. Minister Romildo Caim will stay at the Federal Administration Secretariat. The new ministers will be installed next week after President Itamar Franco's return from Venezuela.
