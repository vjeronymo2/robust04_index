Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A Maltese ship laden with large quantities of rice from Thailand bound for Iraq, imported by the Trade Ministry, docked at the Umm Qasr port this afternoon. A ceremony was held at the port, during which the population of the Umm Qasr subdistrict and workers at the port sang of their love for leader President Saddam Husayn and to express their happiness with the arrival of this ship, which is carrying food supplies and contributing to breaking the unjust blockade. It is noteworthy that this is the third ship that has entered the Umm Qasr port since the unjust blockade was imposed on Iraq.
