BFN [Text] The NATIONAL NEWS AGENCY office in al-Nabatiyah has reported that the Israeli occupation forces shelled al-Jabal al- Akhdar, al-Jabal al-Rafi', and al-Luwayzah in Iqlim al-Tuffah at 1030 today while Israeli warplanes flew over the al-Nabatiyah and Iqlim al-Tuffah areas. The resistance retaliated against the Israeli aggressions today by attacking the Sujud position with rockets and machine guns.
