BFN [Text] Two Russian battalions in the Caucuses are fully prepared for peace-keeping operation in the Georgian-Abkhaz conflict zone, Interfax learned Tuesday at the press center of the Russian forces. The center said that the batallions were ready to take control of the area allotted to them within a few hours of receiving orders. The order could come immediately following the Federation Council's approval of the decision of President Yeltsin to send peace-keepers to Georgia.
