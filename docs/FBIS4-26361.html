BFN [Text] Bangkok, June 22 (XINHUA) -- A Thai version of Deng Xiaoping biography written by his daughter, Deng Rong, has been published in Thailand. The book, My Father Deng Xiaoping tells about the life and struggle of the great man and great events and changes of China since early this century. Published by the Managers' Daily, the Thai version of the book will provide a good channel for Thai-speaking readers to get to know more about Deng Xiaoping and contemporary China.
