BFN [Text] Moscow June 16 ITAR-TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin will visit the Greek island of Corfu soon to sign an agreement on partnership and cooperation between Russia and the European Union. A European Union summit is due to take place on the island on June 24 and 25. Greek Foreign Minister Karlos Papoulias on June 3 handed over to the Russian president a message from Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou of Greece, the current president of the European Union, who expressed the hope that the agreement between Russia and the European Union would be signed during the EU summit on Corfu.
