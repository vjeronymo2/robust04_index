Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri affirmed last night that the resistance is Lebanon's peace card. He noted that Lebanon is facing an attempt to undermine it economically and cut it down to size geographically. He said that ending the resistance would be a surrender to Israel. Speaking at a Ramadan dinner banquet held by al-Mabarrat Charitable Society, Birri warned that ending the Arab boycott of Israel without the implementation of UN Security Council Resolution 425 would be a stab in the back for Lebanon's steadfastness and sacrifices.
