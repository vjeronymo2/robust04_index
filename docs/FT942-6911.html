940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Martin Currie Euro above benchmark Martin Currie European Investment Trust reported a 38.1 per cent increase in net asset value during the year to April 30 against a 27.8 per cent gain by the benchmark FT-A Europe (Ex UK) Index. The figures were 127.74p (92.46p) basic or 123.13p (93.72p) fully diluted. Net revenue for the year to end-April was Pounds 105,000 (Pounds 152,000) for earnings per share of 0.46p (0.66p). An unchanged single final dividend of 0.29p is being recommended.
