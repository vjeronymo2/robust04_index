Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report from the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] A press conference on the military-political aspects of the agreement between the Russian, U.S., and Ukrainian presidents was held at the Defense Ministry today. Given this situation, Russian plants are prepared to dismantle all Ukrainian warheads within two years. The ball is now in the Ukrainian leadership's court.
