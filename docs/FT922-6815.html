920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Cupid doubles A full year's contribution from Pronuptia and three months from Youngs helped Cupid, the USM-quoted bridalwear and nurserycare company, to achieve more-than-doubled profits in the year to end-March. From turnover up by Pounds 5.38m to Pounds 14.7m, pre-tax profits came through at Pounds 982,000, against Pounds 411,000 last time. Pronuptia, the bridalwear retailer, was acquired in December 1990 for some Pounds 3m. Youngs Formal Wear was bought for about Pounds 1.65m in December 1991. Earnings improved from 6.5p to 9.1p and the dividend is being increased to 3.25p (3p). An extraordinary charge of Pounds 115,000 (nil) related to professional fees incurred in an aborted acquisition attempt.
