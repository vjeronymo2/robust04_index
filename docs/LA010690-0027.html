January 6, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

BOYS' BASKETBALL ROUNDUP;

CORONA DEL MAR BEATS WOODBRIDGE

John Paulsen scored 22 points and Warren Johnson added 21 to lead Corona del Mar High School to a 68-53 victory over Woodbridge in a nonleague basketball game Friday at Woodbridge.

The Sea Kings (9-6) got off to a slow start and trailed, 16-12, at the end of the first quarter.

However, Corona del Mar outscored Woodbridge, 21-12, in the second quarter to take a 33-28 halftime lead.

Corona del Mar never trailed in the second half.

In other nonleague games:

Brea-Olinda 88, Sonora 45 -- Keith Walker had 30 points and Rodney Stewart 14 to lead Brea-Olinda (11-3). Greg Farlow had 13 for host Sonora.

Fullerton 80, Covina 63 -- Rene Arias had 26 points and Chris Rubal added 12 points, including six free throws in the fourth quarter, and five rebounds for the Indians (3-10) at home. Covina is 4-8.

Savanna 74, Glenn 55 -- Forward Keith Rodriguez scored 26 points and had 13 rebounds to pace Savanna (10-6). Forward Mario Valdez led Glenn (3-8) with 19 points.

Troy 58, Valencia 56 -- Jermaine Butler had a tip-in with one second left for Troy. Mike Abbott had 16 points to lead Troy (4-6).

Western 84, Bellflower 57 -- Sam Sabbara had 37 points, including a school-record seven three-pointers, and eight assists and Robbie Shelton had 17 points and nine rebounds for Western (9-4) at home. Scott Moten had 28 points for Bellflower.

Newport Harbor 80, Costa Mesa 67 -- Guard Paig Parish scored 22 points and had eight assists for host Newport Harbor (11-4). Forward Eddie Martinez added 15 points and guard Huy Nguyen 16 for the Sailors. Xavier Hanna led Costa Mesa with 21 points.
