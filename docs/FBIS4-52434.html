BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 9 (KCNA) -- Delegates of political parties and organizations of different countries, who had attended the international seminar of revolutionary parties and organizations held in Brussels, made public a joint statement on May 4 denouncing the U.S. and its allies' moves to isolate and stifle Korea and supporting the Korean people's cause of national reunification. The statement expressed deep apprehensions of the anti-DPRK campaign launched by the United States and its followers to isolate and stifle socialist Korea under the pretext of fictitious "nuclear suspicion". It favoured the 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation which indicates a shortcut to Korea's reunification. It demanded that the United States and its followers including the International Atomic Energy Agency stop at once their anti-DPRK, anti-socialist campaign going against the present trend of the world towards peace and justice. It urged the South Korean authorities to send Kim In-so and Ham Se-hwan back to the North at once, set free all prisoners of conscience and accept the proposal for a convocation of national conference advanced by the North to achieve Korea's reunification in the 1990s. It called upon political parties, organizations and progressive forces the world over to extend solidarity to the Workers' Party [WPK] and the people of Korea in their efforts for the defence of the socialist gains and for the independent and peaceful reunification of the country and conduct an international campaign to defend socialist Korea. Meanwhile, the chairman of the Danish Communist Party (M-L), the chairman of the Communist Party of Germany, the general secretary of the All-Union Bolshevik Communist Party, the chairman of the Bulgarian Communist Party and the chairman of the Workers World Party of the United States, who attended the seminar, expressed full support to and solidarity with the WPK and the Korean people in their efforts for the building of socialism and the independent and peaceful reunification of the country, when they met the delegation of the Workers' Party of Korea.
