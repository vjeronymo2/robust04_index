Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Khartoum, 10 Feb (SUNA) -- President of the republic General 'Umar al-Bashir has declared Sudan readiness to send troops to Bosnia-Hercegovina if the country is asked to do so and if the U.N. peacekeeping forces decided to pull out from there. Speaking at his regular meeting with the leaderships of the national media, General al-Bashir called on the Islamic nations to send forces beside the U.N. Forces in Bosnia, adding that the threat to withdraw the U.N. troops from Bosnia in the last part in the conspiracy targeting the Bosnian people [sentence as received]. He added that there is no serious reaction to the agression of the Serb forces which have been bombing the civilians in Bosnia- Hercegovina.
