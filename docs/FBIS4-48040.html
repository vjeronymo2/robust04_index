BFN [Text] Johannesburg, al-Ma' [May] 9, JAMAHIRIYAH NEWS AGENCY -- The secretary of the general people's committee for foreign liaison and international cooperation and the accompanying delegation have arrived in Johannesburg to take part in the ceremonies which well be held tomorrow on the occasion of appointing the African freedom-fighter Nelson Mandela as president for South Africa. It is known that Nelson Mandela had been awarded the al-Qathafi international human rights prize.
