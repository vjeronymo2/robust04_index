December 9, 1990, Sunday, South Bay Edition

EL SEGUNDO TOURNAMENT STARTS MONDAY

The El Segundo High/Kiwanis boys' basketball tournament, featuring nine South Bay teams, will begin Monday with four games.

Top local entries include defending champion Bishop Montgomery, Leuzinger, Hawthorne, South Torrance and Mira Costa. In the most attractive first-round matchup, Bishop Montgomery will meet Hawthorne at 4:30 p.m. Tuesday.

The tournament continues through the week, with the championship final at 7:30 p.m. Friday.

The schedule:

First-round games

Monday

Pater Noster vs. Eisenhower, 3 p.m.; South Torrance vs. Mira Costa, 4:30 p.m.; St. John Bosco vs. Chadwick, 6 p.m.; El Segundo vs. North Torrance, 7:30 p.m.

Tuesday

Miraleste vs. Leuzinger, 3 p.m.; Bishop Montgomery vs. Hawthorne, 4:30 p.m.
