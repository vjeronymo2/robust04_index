930224 FT 24 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Henderson Euro Henderson EuroTrust had a net asset value per ordinary share of 97.6p at January 31, a 29 per cent rise on the value at the trust's launch on July 2 1992. The value per zero dividend preference share at January 31 was 21.43p, and per unit of one ordinary and one zero preference, 119.03p. Net revenue for the seven month period amounted to Pounds 367,000 for earnings per share of 2.12p. A maiden interim dividend of 1.5p is declared.
