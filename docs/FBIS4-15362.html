BFN [From 8 April PAEVALEHT, p 3; "News Bulletin" No. 67] [Text] The Russian military held an exercise at the shooting range in Manniku, causing some alarm among the public. The Defence Forces GHQ and the police had not been informed of the training. Defence Ministry press secretary Maige Priim explained that the explosions had been caused by hand grenades, adding that the Russians had not asked for the permission of the Estonian authorities to hold the exercise.
