Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi received Lebanese Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri at 1000 today. Their discussions dealt with the relations of brotherhood, cooperation, and coordination between the two fraternal countries and ways of promoting them in all fields in the service and the interest of the two fraternal countries and peoples. An official session of talks began at 1100 today between the Syrian side under Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi and the Lebanese side under Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri. Dr. Salim Yasin, deputy prime minister for Economic Affairs; Engineer 'Abd-al-Rahman Madani, minister of Irrigation; Engineer Kamil al-Baba, minister of Electricity; Nasir Qaddur, minister of state for Foreign Affairs; Dr. Nadir al-Nabulsi, minister of Oil and Mineral Resources; and directors and advisers attended the session on the Syrian side. On the Lebanese side, it was attended by Ilyas Hubayqah, minister of Water and Electricity, and Marun Asmar, chairman of the Lebanese Electricity Corporation. The two sides discussed economic cooperation between the two countries and the joint ventures that are being studied, particularly in the field of power generation. The two sides also discussed the scopes of joint cooperation in all fields, in light of priorities and needs, to enhance the bonds of brotherhood, cooperation, and coordination between the two countries and to attain the two fraternal people's interests.
