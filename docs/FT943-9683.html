940810 FT 10 AUG 94 / World Trade News: New container port for Bahamas Hutchinson International Port Holdings, a subsidiary of Hutchinson Whampoa of Hong Kong, has created a joint venture with the Grand Bahama Development Company and will invest Dollars 80m to construct a container port at Freeport, Grand Bahama, writes Canute James in Kingston. Construction will begin in 18 months and completion is due by 1998. The project will be managed by the Port of Felixstowe.
