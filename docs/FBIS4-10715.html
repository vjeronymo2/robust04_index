BFN [Text] San Salvador de Jujuy, 15 Apr (NA)--Jujuy Province Governor Jose Carlos Ficoseco, who was on a six-month leave of absence, resigned his post early this morning after Judge Mario Gonzalez issued a writ depriving Ficoseco of his rights. Ficoseco's resignation was submitted early today to Oscar Perassi, parliamentary secretary of the local legislature. Perassi, who assumed the governorship on the night of 13 April, will now remain in his post until 1995, when a new governor will be elected in the general elections.
