940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Strong asset growth at Dartmoor Inv Dartmoor Investment Trust reported a net asset value of 118.45p as at April 30, a rise of more than 49 per cent on the comparable figure of 79.33p. The trust easily outperformed its benchmark, the FT-SE-A All-Share Index, which rose just 13.8 per cent during the same period. Attributable profit for the 12 months to end-April dipped to Pounds 2.78m (Pounds 2.84m), for earnings of 11.57p (12.09p) per share. Nevertheless, the total distribution for the year is maintained at 11.6p via a same-again final dividend of 3.9p.
