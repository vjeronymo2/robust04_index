April 28, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

EARTH DAY FOCUSES ATTENTION ON DETERIORATION OF ENVIRONMENT

On Sunday we celebrated Earth Day. This day was to remind all of us that we must do something to save our planet from destruction, perhaps by recycling cans, plastics, glass.

In general, do not pollute.

On Tuesday morning I went out to get my paper and to my surprise found The Times wrapped in a large plastic bag.

How can we save our world when large corporations, such as The Times, keep right on polluting? How many thousands of these bags will be found in landfills waiting hundreds of years to decompose?

DONNA PRIETTO

Redondo Beach
