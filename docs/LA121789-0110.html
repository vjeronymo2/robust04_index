December 17, 1989, Sunday, South Bay Edition

A $750,000 CHALLENGE FOR LOYOLA MARYMOUNT

The Fritz B. Burns Foundation and alumnus William H. Hannon have joined to present a $750,000 challenge to Loyola Marymount University in honor of LMU's 75th anniversary. The challenge will match dollar-for-dollar, up to $2,500 per donor, all new and increased giving to LMU from last July 1 to next June 30.
