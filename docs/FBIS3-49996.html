Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, January 15 (ATA) -- Officials of the Socialist Party [SP] have told ATA that they intended to provide clarification of their accusation that ATA was blocking news about the opposition parties in Albania. The SP statement was made by the SP vice chairman, Servet Pellumbi, and Deputy Bashkim Zeneli on January 10, who said that a further declaration on the subject would be forthcoming the next day. However, Mr. Zeneli subsequently informed our reporter that the SP had not yet marshalled the data to substantiate their charges. Several days have now passed without further word from the SP.
