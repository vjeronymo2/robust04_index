Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Macao, March 11 (XINHUA) -- The 19th meeting of the Sino-Portuguese Joint Liaison Group is scheduled to be held in Beijing and Wuxi, Jiangsu Province, from March 22 to 25, according to sources from the Chinese team of the group. It is learned that during the meeting the two sides of the joint liaison group will continue their discussions on issues covering localization of law and public servants, officialization of Chinese language, payment for retired public servants in Macao, the involvement of the Bank of China in issuing bank notes in Macao, financial reserve of the Macao Government and other issues in the transitional period. Ambassador Guo Jiading, leader of the Chinese team, Ambassador Fernando Andresen Guimaraes, leader of the Portuguese team, other group members of the two sides and concerned experts will attend the meeting.
