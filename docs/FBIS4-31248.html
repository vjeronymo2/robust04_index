BFN [Text] The main hearing at the trial of Dusko Tomic, secretary-general of the 1st Children's Embassy, will begin on 11 June, Bosnia-Herzegovina Press reports. It is suspected that Tomic has cooperated with the aggressor and he is considered the one most guilty for the inadequate organization of a convoy carrying food from Macedonia [Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], which eventually ended up in the aggressor's hands in Pale.
