BFN [Report by Jose Luis Sarmenta] [Text] Next Thursday, 12 May, a debate will take place between presidential candidates Cuauhtemoc Cardenas, Ernesto Zedillo, and Diego Fernandez de Cevallos. The PRI [Institutional Revolutionary Party], PAN [National Action Party], and PRD [Party of the Democratic Revolution] representatives who agreed to hold the debate on 12 May also decided that it would take place at 2100 to allow most of the Mexican people to watch the debate, which will be broadcast live. At a brief news conference called by PRI Deputy Secretary General Esteban Moctezuma, PRD representative Adolfo Aguilar Zinzer, and PRI presidential candidate Diego Fernandez de Cevallos, it was announced that the site of the debate has not yet been determined. According to presidential candidate Fernandez de Cevallos, the site has not yet been selected because of technicalities and logistics that need to be worked out among the three parties. [Begin recording] [Fernandez Cevallos, in progress] ...that the debate will take place on 12 May at 2100. It will only be possible to reschedule the debate through an agreement among the three of us. This is all of the information we have for you at this time. [Unidentified reporter] Where will the debate be held, sir? [Fernandez Cevallos] The site has not yet been determined. The selection of an appropriate site will depend on logistics. [end recording] PRI representative Esteban Moctezuma noted that, so far, more than 10 possible debate sites are being studied. At any rate, it was explained that the exact site would be announced within the next few days.
