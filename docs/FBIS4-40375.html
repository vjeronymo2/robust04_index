BFN [Russian Federation President's Edict No. 1181 "On Measures To Ensure the Completion of Construction of Unfinished Apartment Blocks"] [Text] With a view to creating conditions for providing housing for Russian Federation citizens, reducing the volumes of expenditure on maintaining uncompleted apartment blocks, and ensuing their completion and commissioning, I decree: 1. That it be established that uncompleted apartment blocks under federal ownership, including those under the full economic management of enterprises and the operational management of organizations and institutions and whose construction was ended or suspended in connection with the absence of the necessary federal budget financing this year are subject to being transferred for the completion of construction or to being sold. The decision to transfer for the completion of construction or to sell uncompleted apartment blocks which were ordered by federal organs of executive power and organizations and institutions financed from the federal budget is made by the Russian Federation State Committee for Questions of Architecture and Construction and the Russian Federation State Committee for the Administration of State Property in agreement with the organs of executive power of the Russian Federation components. The decision to transfer for the completion of construction or to sell uncompleted apartment blocks ordered by federal state enterprises is made upon representation from the client enterprise by the Russian Federation State Committee for the Administration of State property and the Russian Federation State Committee for Questions of Architecture and Construction by agreement with the RUssian Federation components' organs of executive power. 2. That the federal state enterprises which ordered the construction of apartment blocks whose construction has been halted or suspended in connection with the lack of the necessary financing be allowed to transfer to subcontracting organizations for completion or to sell uncompleted apartment blocks. 3. That it be established that the transfer for the completion of construction and the sale of uncompleted apartment blocks is implemented exclusively at investment and commercial competitions with a view to ensuring the speediest completion of apartment blocks and the provision of housing for citizens. 4. That it be established that the attached statute on the procedure for transferring uncompleted apartment blocks and for selling them be approved. 5. That it be recommended to the organs of executive power of the Russian Federation components and the organs of local self-management that they make the decision to transfer for the completion of construction or to sell apartment blocks under, respectively, the state ownership of components of the Russian Federation and municipal ownership. 6. That this edict enters into force from the moment it is published. [signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin, Moscow, Kremlin, 10 June 1994.
