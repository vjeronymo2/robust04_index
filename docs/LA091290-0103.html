September 12, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Carl Karcher Enterprises Inc.: The Anaheim-based operator of Carl's Jr. restaurants reported net income of $5.1 million for its second fiscal quarter, up 7.7% from the second quarter of 1989. Revenue for the quarter was $125 million, up 1.8% from the same period last year, despite the franchising or sale of 40 company-owned restaurants in Arizona during the first and second fiscal quarters. The company said the figures were encouraging in light of heavy competitive discounting in the market.
