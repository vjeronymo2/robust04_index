August 9, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

"Well, so much for the critics," NBC News president Michael Gartner scoffs in a staff memo noting the ratings success of "Yesterday, Today and Tomorrow," which critics roasted because of its news re-creations. "The viewers viewed -- in big numbers," the news chief crows in his communique. He said "YTT" won its time slot with a 12 rating and 23% audience share and held "its audience throughout the hour" for its debut last Wednesday at 10 p.m. He also noted that the debut of ABC's far more publicized "PrimeTime Live" last Thursday "had lesser numbers and was third in its time period." Two more editions of "YTT" will air on Aug. 23 and Sept. 13, after which NBC will decide if the program will go as a series. ALEENE MacMINN
