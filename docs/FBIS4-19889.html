BFN [Text] A strong explosion occurred at 0512 [0212 GMT] today at the UN High Commission offices at 59 Skoufa Street in Kolonaki. The explosion, which happened inside the UN offices, caused extensive damage inside the building and to nearby apartment buildings. Five minutes after the explosion, an unidentified man called FLASH Radio [private station] saying that no one should go near the UN High Commission building because a stronger explosion would follow.
