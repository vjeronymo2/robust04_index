920901 FT 01 SEP 92 / Construction Contracts: Moscow offices BOVIS INTERNATIONAL is shortly to begin work on an office redevelopment scheme in the centre of Moscow. The P&O company has signed a joint venture agreement with the Moscow State Philharmonic Orchestra and the International Non-Government Foundation House for Children-Orphans to redevelop three office buildings controlled by the orchestra. Preparation plans are already under way for the demolition of the interior and back walls of the buildings, to be followed by complete reconstruction, providing 57,000 sq ft of offices.
