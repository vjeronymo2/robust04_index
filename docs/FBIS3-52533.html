Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 6 Jan (MENA)--Dr. Yusuf Ni'matallah, Arab League assistant secretary general for economic affairs, today tendered his resignation to Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid for health reasons. Dr. Ni'matallah said that Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid accepted his resignation as of 1 January. Dr. Ni'matallah noted that he had undergone very serious surgery and that the doctors told him to rest for a long time. He denied that differences are behind his resignation. He noted that Saudi Arabia is expected to name a person to serve the remainder of his term, which is one-and-a-half years. He stressed that, because of his health condition, he will not assume any official position when he returns to Saudi Arabia. Dr. Ni'matallah wished the Arab League success in its difficult tasks so that the Arab nation can find a suitable place on the world map. Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid has instructed 'Ali 'Abd-al-Karim, Arab League assistant secretary general for internal supervision, to temporarily direct the League's Economic Affairs Department until a candidate is named.
