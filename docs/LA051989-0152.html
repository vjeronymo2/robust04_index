May 19, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: NAMES IN THE NEWS

Matt Geiger, a 6-foot-11 center who plans to transfer after averaging 15.9 points and 6.6 rebounds as a sophomore last season at Auburn, will visit UCLA this weekend, he said from his parents' home in Clearwater, Fla. Geiger said he has already visited Georgia Tech, Villanova and Syracuse.
