Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Jan 19 -- Nong Duc Manh, politburo member of the C.C. [Central Committee] of Vietnam Communist Party and chairman of the National Assembly received here today Prof. Dr. I. Kalantari, minister of agriculture of Iran who is on a visit to Vietnam. Chairman Nong Duc Manh and Minister I. Kalantari exchanged views on organization and activities of the two national assemblies of Vietnam and Iran. Minister Kalantari informed Chairman Manh of the results of his visit to Vietnam and conveyed the Iranian Parliament president's invitation to the latter to visit Iran. For his part, Chairman Manh expressed his wish for a new stage of development of the time-honoured traditional relationship between the Vietnamese and Iranian peoples. He accepted the invitation with the pleasure and asked Minister I. Kalantari to convey his thanks to the president of Iranian Parliament.
