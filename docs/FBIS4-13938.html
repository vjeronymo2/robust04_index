BFN [Text] Mashhad (Khorasan), April 22, IRNA -- Friday prayer leader Hojjat ol-Eslam Abaei was the target of an assassination attempt by a terrorist here today. Abaei, who substitutes as Friday prayer leader in this holy city was fired at twice just after delivering the sermon and was immediately rushed to the hospital where his health condition was reported as satisfactory, said a local official to IRNA. The terrorist after his failed attempt tried to kill himself when faced with angry reaction from the worshippers chanting `Death to Munafiq (hypocrite)'. Law enforcement officers whisked away the culprit who had badly injured himself.
