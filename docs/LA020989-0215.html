NATION;

36 KILLED IN INDIA TV STUDIO BLAZE

A fire at a television studio in southern India killed at least 36 people and injured at least 55, including one of India's best-known actors, a news agency reported today.

The fire on Wednesday destroyed the studio near Bangalore, 1,100 miles south of New Delhi, United News of India said. The studio was filled with actors and technicians filming a television show.
