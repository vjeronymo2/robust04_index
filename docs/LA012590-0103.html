January 25, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

GROUP PLANTS GARDENS AT ELYSIAN PARK HOSPICE

The LA/San Gabriel Valley chapter of the California Landscape Contractors Assn. has landscaped the grounds of the Chris Brownlie Hospice in Elysian Park.

Volunteers have planted more than half an acre of gardens at the site.

They will be thanked at a potluck luncheon from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. Saturday at the hospice, 1300 Scott Ave., Los Angeles.
