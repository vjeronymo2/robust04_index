Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Amman -- AL-DUSTUR has learned that several PLO Executive Committee members in Amman continue to hold meetings and consultations to formulate a unified stand on the resumption of Palestinian-Israeli peace negotiations. Some of those holding such contacts are Yasir 'Amr, Sulayman al-Najjab, Muhammad Zuhdi al-Nashashibi, Taysir Khalid, and 'Abd-al-Rahim Malluh.
