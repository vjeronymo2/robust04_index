921203 FT 03 DEC 92 / Gunmen kidnap a UN soldier A Danish soldier with the United Nations Protection Force in Bosnia was kidnapped while jogging yesterday near the Bosnian capital, Sarajevo, a UN official said, Reuter reports from Sarajevo. The soldier was kidnapped by a group of men in Croat-held Kiseljak about 25km north of Sarajevo, Mr Peter Kessler, the spokesman for the UN High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR), said. Since their deployment last March, 20 UN troops have been killed and 300 wounded.
