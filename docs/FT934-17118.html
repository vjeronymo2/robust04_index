931002 FT 02 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Camellia surges Turnover of Camellia, the tea, coffee, edible nuts and citrus fruits producer, expanded from Pounds 73.5m to Pounds 80.5m for the first half of 1993 while pre-tax profits were boosted to Pounds 7.38m compared with Pounds 2.69m. Earnings surged from 18.44p to 69.36p per share and the interim dividend is 13p (12p).
