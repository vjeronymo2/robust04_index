940307 FT 07 MAR 94 / Observer: Howard's way Michael Heseltine may be gaining support from the Tory right as future contender against John Major; but some key king-makers seem not to be in the giving vein. Home secretary Michael Howard - another right-wing pretender to Major's throne - was asked four times by David Frost on BBC breakfast television yesterday whether he forgave Heseltine his part in the usurpation of Baroness Thatcher, when prime minister. Howard evaded the question on each occasion. With endorsements like that, who needs enemies?
