931112 FT 12 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Britannia Group BRITANNIA GROUP has received Pounds 1m from Westbury Group as deposit on an option under which Westbury has the right to acquire all or part of Britannia's site at Green Farm, Quedgeley, Gloucester. The transaction was arranged via subsidiaries of both companies. Britannia has also finalised the purchase from Westbury of 93 housing plots with planning permission situated on three sites at Gloucester, Hereford and Ledbury for Pounds 1m.
