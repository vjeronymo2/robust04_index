940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / Hatfield Airfield to close in April Hatfield Airfield in Hertfordshire is to close in April with the loss of 142 jobs. London Business Aviation, part of British Aerospace, blamed continuing operating losses. The airfield became famous in the 1950s as the home of the Comet, the world's first commercial jet airliner, and was the base of the de Havilland Aircraft Company.
