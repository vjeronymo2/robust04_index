Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Article by Patricia Bravo] [Excerpt] The Center Center Union (UCC) general assembly, with the approval of 80 percent of those present, decided to merge with the National Party (PN). At the end of the event, UCC President Carlos del Campo said: "We hope to materialize a new political party of national interest." Del Campo met with the media accompanied by PN President Enrique Schilling, who attended the assembly in his capacity as a guest. The two politicians agreed on the principles that unite their respective parties, and voiced their pleasure with the request by a group of independent liberals headed by Luis Claro Lagarrigue to join the new group.
