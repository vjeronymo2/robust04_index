Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Armenian troops on Friday morning launched an offensive on Bash-Gervend, a village about 10-15 km north-east of Agdam, Azerbaijan's Defense Ministry said. The Armenians concentrated most of their attacking troops, supported by 20 tanks and armored personnel carriers, in the area between Agdam and Barda. The Azerbaijani army has put up a stiff defense, the ministry said. It said the Armenians, in the past 24 hours, had lost three armored vehicles, two cannon and one Grad multiple-rocket launcher. Fighting was also raging on Friday around the border between Fizuli and Beylagan Districts, where Armenian forces were attacking Azerbaijani positions near the Agburun strategic height.
