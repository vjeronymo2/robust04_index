May 27, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

'HOMELESSNESS IN SUBURBIA' AND SHEEN'S OFFER OF A SANCTUARY

After reading, and re-reading many times Pettepiece's poignant article I came to the realization that most of us are only a few weeks away from joining the ranks of the homeless when unexpected adversity strikes.

Bravo to Pettepiece for sharing his experiences. Also, best wishes for success in his literary career.

M. POLLARD

Los Angeles
