930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Tories clash over hot potato THE GOVERNMENT clashed with its backbenchers yesterday over European Community proposals which call into question the future of the UK's Potato Marketing Board. Both Sir Peter Tapsell, Conservative MP for Lindsey East, and Mr Bill Walker, Tory MP for Tayside North, expressed their constituents' concerns about the possible changes which aim to harmonise the market control mechanisms in different member states.
