930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Derwent Valley DERWENT VALLEY has received acceptances to its open offer in respect of 1.72m new ordinary shares (44.35 per cent). Shareholders had previously undertaken not to accept their entitlements to 472,653 shares (12.18 per cent). These, together with the remaining 1.69m shares not taken up (43.48 per cent), are to be taken up in accordance with arrangements outlined last month.
