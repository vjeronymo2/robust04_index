940819 FT 19 AUG 94 / World News in Brief: Dollar slips below Y100 despite rates rise Despite the 50 basis point rise in US interest rates earlier this week, the dollar yesterday slipped below Y100 again. The catalyst for renewed weakness was the Bundesbank's decision to keep its rates on hold, lending further strength to the D-Mark. A jump in the US's bilateral trade deficit with Japan in June also prompted further dollar selling.
