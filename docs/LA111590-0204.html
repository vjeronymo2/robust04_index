November 15, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

NAME CHANGE: David Rothenberg, burned in a 1983 Buena Park fire set by his father, has made clear he doesn't want to see his dad again, and now he has dropped the name too. . . .David, 14, is now David Jordan Robinson, after taking the names of basketball stars Michael Jordan of the Chicago Bulls and David Robinson of the San Antonio Spurs, both of whom befriended him. Charles Rothenberg (A3) consented to the change but wanted David to ask in a "Dear Dad" letter. David wrote the letter -- to "Dear Charles." Nancy Wride and Herman Wong
