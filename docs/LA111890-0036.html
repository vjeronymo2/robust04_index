November 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

AND NOW, A ROUND OF APPLAUSE FOR THE NEW NC-17 MOVIE RATING

For those who insist on calling attention to what they deem "obscene" in a film or its advertisement, perhaps they should consider a quote from Oscar Wilde: "It is the spectator, and not life, that art really mirrors."

LISA BRECHBIEL

and SUSAN BUCKLEY

Riverside
