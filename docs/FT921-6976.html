920225 FT 25 FEB 92 / Routemasters back on the road Ticket to ride: the first of a series of refurbished Routemasters. London Buses is spending Pounds 10m to extend the life of nearly 500 of the popular open-platform vehicles that were designed in 1954. The company estimates that each has travelled about 1m miles. The programme will extend the Routemasters' lives for a further 10 years.
