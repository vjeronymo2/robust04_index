BFN [Text] At the request of the U.S. Embassy in Ukraine, members of the Ukrainian Supreme Council commission for defense and state security issues met with Robert (Hansen), a high-level official from a research bureau at the U.S. Department of State. He studies [word indistinct] problems of East European countries. He had already visited Russia. As for our country, he is interested in Ukraine's eastern regions. The commission's head Volodymyr Mukhin, who represents Kharkov Oblast in the parliament, answered the guest's questions about this region. Also discussed was the situation in the Crimea, and problems of Ukraine's nuclear disarmament. In the words of (Hansen), the U.S. ambassador to Ukraine, William Miller, will soon meet with the commission for a detailed examination of the above issues. The deputies also met with Simon Hemans, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of Great Britain to Ukraine. They agreed to organize a trip of the British Defense Committee to Ukraine.
