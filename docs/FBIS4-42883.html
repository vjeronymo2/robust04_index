BFN [Excerpt] The National Council decided unanimously at today's three-hour meeting that discussions on the confidence-building measures have been terminated. In a statement after the National Council meeting, Government Spokesman Ioannis Kasoulidhis said the decision was made due to the lack of political will by the Turkish side, which has been ascertained by the UN secretary general. The spokesman also noted that the National Council examined options that the Greek Cypriot side should support, and added that these options will be finalized at a National Council meeting scheduled for 0900 tomorrow.
