BFN [Text] Seoul, April 26 (YONHAP) -- Kim Tae-chung, president of the Peace Foundation for the Asia-Pacific Region, sent his Secretary-General Cho Yong-hwan to the South African Embassy in Seoul Tuesday to help monitor absentee voting here. The International Electoral Commission's Asia branch earlier asked Kim for monitoring assistance through the South African Embassy in Seoul. South Africa's first all-race general election begins on Tuesday and runs through Thursday.
