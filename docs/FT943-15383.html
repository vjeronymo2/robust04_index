940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Demand for pepper in general has bee improving, despite the holiday season, reports Man Producten. White pepper has become very scarce on the Europe spot market with limited fresh supplies coming from producing countries. The new Muntok crop has only just started and arrivals are still small. Prices firmed up towards the end of the week as several shippers tried to cover their July and August commitments. Muntok white faq, spot Rotterdam fetched USDollars 2,950 a tonne, while July/August and August/September shipments were at Dollars 2,850, cif. Black pepper prices remained steady, with spot goods commanding a premium as stocks in Europe are diminishing. Black pepper faq spot Rotterdam was at Dollars 1,700 a tonne, while grade 1 spot fetched Dollars 1,775.
