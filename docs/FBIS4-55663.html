BFN [Text] Croatian President Franjo Tudjman today received international mediators Thorvald Stoltenberg and Lord David Owen. The talks were attended by Croatian Foreign Minister Mate Granic and Defense Minister Gojko Susak. Under discussion was the continuation of talks between the Croatian Government and local Serb representatives on the reintegration of the UN-protected areas into the Croatian constitutional system. After the talks, Thorvald Stoltenberg said he hopes the next round of talks, which is to deal with economic issues, will be called soon. Lord Owen said the mediators acquainted President Tudjman with the results of this morning's talks with Serbian President Milosevic and Bosnian Serb leader Radovan Karadzic in Belgrade.
