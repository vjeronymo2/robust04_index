930927 FT 27 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Robert H Lowe Increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.28m, against Pounds 162,000, were announced by Robert H Lowe, the clothing maker and printing and packaging concern, for the half year to April 30. The group was however, currently trading profitably. Turnover declined to Pounds 12.2m (Pounds 15.5m). Losses per share emerged at 3.27p (0.95p).
