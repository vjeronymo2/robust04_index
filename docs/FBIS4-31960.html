BFN [Text] For over a month now, Serbian soldiers have been entrenching along the border with Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], raiding constantly on the territory of the neighborly country. The Serbian soldiers, who are also deploying mortars along the border, are in the direct vicinity of U.S. Protection Forces soldiers, who have not reacted so far. It is also reported that the Serbian soldiers have entered up to 300 meters into Macedonian territory.
