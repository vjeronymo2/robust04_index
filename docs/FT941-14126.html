940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Scottish Provident alters bonus system Scottish Provident is switching to a two-tier annual bonus system for with-profits endowment policies. Bonuses on the sum assured and on existing bonuses will be awarded at different rates - 4 per cent and 6 per cent respectively this year, compared with a level rate of 5.2 per cent last year.
