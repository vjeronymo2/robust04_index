November 3, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

BLOCKBUSTING

We think you are very cruel. The New Kids on the Block are very talented.

They are more talented than you. We saw the New Kids on the Block in concert. They were great. Even my mom thought they were great. The New Kids on the Block don't make fun of you or other reporters, so why do you make fun of the New Kids on the Block?

From your enemies, JENNIFER & NICOLE, Tustin
