(6) Certificates of compliance shall be valid only through March 1st of the calendar year following the year in which they are issued. Expiration dates shall be indicated on the certificates. (7) Effective January 1, 2000, no piece of nonroad equipment that is equipped with an engine of model year 1998 or earlier, except equipment and associated engines exempted in paragraph (a)(2) of this section, shall be allowed to be registered unless it was included in the 1999 information submittal specified in paragraph (h)(2) of this section. (8) Effective January 1, 1999, no piece of nonroad equipment that is equipped with an engine of model year 1999 or later, except equipment and associated engines exempted in paragraph (a)(2) of this section, shall be allowed to be registered for use in the FIP areas without proof that it meets the enhanced in-use compliance program engine standards of paragraphs (c) and (d) of this section.
