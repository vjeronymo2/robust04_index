BFN [Text] At the Royal Hashemite Court today, His Majesty King Husayn received Yemen's Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah, who communicated a verbal message from President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. The message thanks his majesty for his attitude toward sisterly Yemen, and outlines the latest developments there. His Majesty King Husayn told Basindwah that Jordan wants to see the Yemeni people stay united, within a single entity. Jordan is anxious for the gains achieved by the Yemeni people to be preserved. His majesty told the envoy the flow of Yemeni blood should be stemmed and outside intervention in the country's domestic affairs kept at bay. Jordan, his majesty added, is prepared to extend humanitarian aid to Yemen whenever the need arises. The meeting was attended by Royal Court Chief al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir; his majesty's adviser Khalid al-Karaki; Talal al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs; and the Yemeni ambassador in Amman. The Yemeni minister left Amman this afternoon and was seen off at the airport by Talal al-Hasan.
