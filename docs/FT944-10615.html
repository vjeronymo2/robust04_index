941109 FT 09 NOV 94 / International Company News: Banamex to lift Argentine bank stake Banamex, the Mexican bank subsidiary of Grupo Financiero Banamex-Accival, said it planned to raise its stake in Argentina's Banco del Sud to 43 per cent from 27 per cent, Reuter reports from Mexico City. It said the operation would be carried out via participation in the rights offering of 6.606m Class B Banco del Sud shares that represent 30 per cent of the existing total of Class A and B shares. Depending on the extent to which Banco El Sud minority shareholders exercise their rights, Banamex's extra investment in Banco del Sud will range between Dollars 75m and Dollars 94m.
