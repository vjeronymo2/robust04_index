931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / Rival Somali clans clash Fighting broke out between rival clans in Mogadishu yesterday, scattering crowds marching to a planned peace rally, witnesses said, Reuter reports from Mogadishu. Several Somalis were reported to have been wounded. Fighting broke out when guerrillas loyal to Mohamed Farah Aideed fired at the crowd as it neared the edge of his southern fiefdom. Gunmen from the north returned fire, some hurling grenades.
