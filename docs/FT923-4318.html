920908 FT 08 SEP 92 / World Trade News: Boeing set to sign a contract with Tarom - The Farnborough Air Show BOEING IS set to sign a contract to sell commercial aircraft to Tarom, the Romanian state-owned airline, this week, Daniel Green writes. Boeing has already sold aircraft to a string of other former east European communist countries. The contract is possibly for fewer than five Boeing 737s, the model sold to CSA Czechoslovakian Airlines, Malev of Hungary, Lot of Poland, Balkan Bulgarian Airlines, and JAT of Yugoslavia.
