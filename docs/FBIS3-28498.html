Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 7 (KCNA) -- The chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly, Yang Hyong-sop, sent messages of greetings to Eduardo Gomes Tamayo and Carmelo Lauria upon their election as president of the Congress and president of the Chamber of Deputies of the Republic of Venezuela. He in the messages expressed the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two peoples and the two parliaments would develop for the better with each passing day and wished them greater success in their responsible work.
