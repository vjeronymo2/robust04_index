BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, received at his office yesterday evening Igor [word indistinct], ambassador of the Russian Federation. The ambassador conveyed to the brother chairman a message from the Russian leadership on the current developments in our country, bilateral friendly relations, as well as issues of common concern. During the meeting, the Russian ambassador reiterated the Russian Federation's constant position in support of Yemen's unity and stability.
