940611 FT 11 JUN 94 / International Company News: Balsam files for bankruptcy Balsam, the sports-surfaces company pushed into crisis after the arrest of its entire board this week, filed for bankruptcy on Friday in the second financial scandal to hit Germany in the past two months, Reuter reports from Frankfurt. The crash has also put at risk the future of Procedo, Germany's largest export financing group. Balsam was Procedo's biggest client, and bankers say that up to 75 per cent of invoices it took as collateral on loans to Balsam may be worthless. Procedo owes banks more than DM1.5bn (Dollars 899.6m).
