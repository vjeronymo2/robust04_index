BFN [Text] A responsible military source in the command of the Armed Forces of the Democratic Republic of Yemen has categorically denied in a statement to the Yemeni news agency, SABA', Aden, the reports carried by some foreign news agencies this evening, that the al-'Anad air base is under the control of the forces of the al-Ahmar gang. The source confirmed that the Armed Forces of the Democratic Republic of Yemen are in full control of the al-'Anad zone, including the air base. In its statement, the source pointed out that news agency correspondents visited the air base and confirmed that our forces were in control of the air base, and indicated this in voice and video reports.
