BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Bosnia-Herzegovina Vice President Ejup Ganic and Foreign Affairs Minister Irfan Ljubijankic met with Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin today. In a statement before the meeting, Cetin said that Turkey is pleased with the first step taken by the Bosnians and Croats toward coexistence, the Washington Agreement. [Begin Cetin recording] We hope and wish, like Mr. Ganic said, that the other link of this development is completed soon, that is, that the Serbs also participate in this multiracial, multireligious, and multicultural independent Bosnia-Herzegovina federation. Turkey is continuing to conduct relations both with the officials of Bosnia-Herzegovina and with the Croats. We are also continuing to conduct a dialogue with the Serbs. [end recording] Ganic said: Without the participation of Turkish diplomacy, it is impossible to achieve peace and stability in the Balkans.
