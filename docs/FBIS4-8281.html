BFN [Text] Budapest, 11 April 11 (MTI) -- AWACS surveillance planes flew patrols in Hungarian airspace both on Sunday [10 April] and Monday, Defense Ministry spokesman Lieut.- Col. Lajos Erdelyi told MTI today, stressing they flew solely under a UN Security Council mandate. The spokesman declined to answer whether or not the monitoring aircraft had been in Hungary's airspace during NATO air attacks on Bosnian Serb military positions around Gorazde.
