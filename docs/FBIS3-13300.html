Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Yasir 'Arafat, president of the state of Palestine, this morning received a telephone call from U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher. The call dealt with current efforts to issue a United Nations Security Council resolution on the al-Ibrahimi Mosque massacre. Brother President Yasir 'Arafat stressed that the draft resolution presented to the Security Council, which contained assurances on Jerusalem and for international protection for our people, was a vital issue for the peace process and its continuity in these current circumstances. An agreement was reached for follow-up contacts on that matter.
