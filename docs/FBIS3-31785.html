Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Report by the Information Center of the 7th South Operations Group of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army] [Text] The enemy started tank-infantry attacks in the valleys of the rivers Bosna, Usora, and Spreca. Since this morning Maglaj and the surrounding area have been in the most serious situation. Namely, from several directions the aggressor has been using all available weapons in attacks on defense positions of the Maglaj defenders, the town center, and the surrounding area. This is actually the fiercest chetnik attack on the town since the beginning of the war. The enemy initiated these forceful attacks from Mt. Ozren, Brankovici, and Novi Seher. The enemy has also been attacking free Doboj territories from several directions since this morning. From Mt. Ozren and all other strongholds the enemy has attempted a breakthrough at the river mouth [usce] near Doboj. The chetniks are also attacking free Tesanj territories, where the defense positions in [word indistinct] Kosa have been targeted from the direction of Vidkovac. Members of the 110th Brigade of the Croatian Defense Council in Usora have also been defending their positions bravely this morning, as the chetniks have attempted breakthroughs in the region of Bandera. There is no peace on free Teslic territories, where the defense positions of Teslic defenders and civilian settlements have been attacked.
