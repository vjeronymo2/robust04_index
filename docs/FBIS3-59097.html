Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Geneva, Jan 26 (AFP) -- Croatian Foreign Minister Mate Granic and Zeljko Simic, the deputy prime minister of rump Yugoslavia, will meet here Thursday afternoon [27 January], diplomatic sources from both countries said Wednesday. The meeting will be the first between senior politicians of the two countries since the January 19 accord on normalising bilateral ties. On the agenda will be the mutual exchange of Croatian and Serbian prisoners of war, said a Geneva-based spokesman for the government of rump Yugoslavia, which comprises Serbia and Montenegro.
