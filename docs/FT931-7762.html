930223 FT 23 FEB 93 / Parliament and Politics: Brooke rejects media complaint MR PETER BROOKE, heritage secretary, yesterday rejected Labour demands in the Commons for immediate action to prevent Mr Rupert Murdoch retaining control over his present proportion of the British media. Miss Ann Clwyd, the shadow heritage minister, said Mr Murdoch's ownership of three daily newspapers, two Sunday newspapers and 50 per cent of the BSkyB satellite television company was a 'totally unacceptable concentration of media ownership'.
