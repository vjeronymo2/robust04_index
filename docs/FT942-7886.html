940524 FT 24 MAY 94 / Judicial Independence called for by Chinese Newspaper China's top legal newspaper yesterday published a risky call for judicial independence, saying Communist party officials still tipped the scales of justice, Reuter reports from Beijing. The Legal Daily's front-page editorial said free courts were essential to developing a market economy - and added that China's supposedly independent judiciary was far from free. 'The courts have become administrative tools of the government,' the editorial quoted one unnamed court official as saying, in a direct attack on Beijing's long-standing position that it allows judicial freedom.
