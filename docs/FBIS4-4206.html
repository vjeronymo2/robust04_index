BFN [Text] Beijing, April 26 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing met with Dong-seon An [spelling of name as received], chairman of the Committee on Commerce, Industry and Resources of the National Assembly of the Republic of Korea (ROK), and his party here today. Dong-seon an and his party arrived here April 24 as guests of the Finance and Economic Committee of China's National People Congress (NPC). Prior to their arrival in Beijing, the visitors had toured south China's Hainan Province and Harbin in Heilongjiang Province, northeast China. During the meeting this afternoon, Vice-Premier Li said that the current visit will promote bilateral cooperation in trade and tourism. ROK President Kim Yong-sam's recent China visit has played an important role in pushing forward the friendly relations of cooperation between the two countries, Li said. Dong-seon An expressed belief that bilateral cooperation in trade and tourism will see greater development through the joint efforts of the two governments. Liu Suinian, chairman of the NPC Finance and Economic Committee, took part in the meeting.
