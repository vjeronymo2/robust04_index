Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] The Party for Democracy and Progress [PDP] decided to withdraw from government yesterday afternoon. Among other things, the party disapproves of the Alliance for Democracy in Mali's attitude regarding the underlying principles of the Republican pact, as well as the management of public affairs. The party's decision follows the cabinet reshuffle, which confirmed the withdrawal of the National Committee for Democratic Initiative and the Rally for Democracy and Progress from the government. The PDP representative within the cabinet was Boubakar Karamoko Coulibaly at the Ministry of Youth and Sports.
