930402 FT 02 APR 93 / UK Company News: Leeds Group in Pounds 2.5m purchase Leeds Group has expanded into continental Europe via the acquisition of two Netherlands-based textile companies for Fl 6.78m (Pounds 2.49m). Further consideration, to a maximum Fl 2.8m, is dependent on profitability. Initial consideration is satisfied by the issue of 613,628 new shares, placed with institutions at 405p, apart from 29,727 retained by the vendors. A further 386,372 are being issued to provide Pounds 1.43m net of additional working capital. The two companies, TIM Beheer and Itex Brummen, specialise in the transfer printing of textiles and the merchanting of dyed and printed fabrics. For the 1992 year TIM returned pre-tax profits of Fl 1.16m and had net tangible assets of Fl 4.73m. Itex had net tangible assets of Fl 737,000 and made pre-tax profits of Fl 725,000.
