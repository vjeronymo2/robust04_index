Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The government has decided to actively participate in the Vietnamese Government's five-year economic development plan. On 4 February, relevant ministries, including the Economic Planning Board and the Ministry of Trade, Industry, and Resource, said that the government had decided on formally participating in the Vietnamese State Planning Committee's establishment of a five-year economic development plan via the Korea Development Institute. They added that the government would formulate various plans, including the specific economic planning formula and other development work projects.
