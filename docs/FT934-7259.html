931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Black Arrow falls to Pounds 346,000 REFLECTING difficult market conditions, pre-tax profits of Black Arrow Group plunged to Pounds 346,000 for the half year to September 30, compared with Pounds 1.55m last time which included an exceptional Pounds 668,000 from the sale of a trading property. Turnover of the furniture and leasing group dropped from Pounds 11.73m to Pounds 7.62m. Earnings per share came to 0.84p (3.82p). The interim dividend has been halved to 0.5p (1p).
