November 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

GARDENING Q & A: LEAF CURL ON TOMATO PLANT CAUSED BY COLD

Q: What is causing the lower leaves of my tomato plant to curl? The rest of the plant is fine.

A: Don't worry. This condition is caused by a cold spell early in the plant's growth cycle. It will not affect new growth and the plant should grow normally and produce fruit.

Bill Sidnam
