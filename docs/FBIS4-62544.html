BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 499] [Text] Vilnius, 13 May, ELTA -- An Italian-Lithuanian agreement on the promotion and protection of investments was initialed in Rome on Thursday [12 May], Italian Embassy reported ELTA. Italian side was represented by the Deputy Director of Foreign Ministry Mr. Cavarai and Lithuanian -- by Ambassador Stasys Lozoraitis. The agreement which will be signed in the nearest time, is yet to be completed. The agreement gives to Italian investors in Lithuania a legal framework, which will constitute an impetus to the Italian presence in Lithuania.
