BFN [Report by 'Abdallah Nasir al-Shahri: "Saudi Arabia-Yemen: Sanaa Requests Postponement of Border Talks"] [Excerpts] Riyadh -- The eighth session of the Saudi-Yemeni Experts Joint Borders Committee, scheduled to be held in the Saudi capital today, has been postponed at the request of the Yemeni Government. AL-HAYAH has learned from reliable sources that the Yemeni Foreign Ministry instructed its ambassador in Riyadh to convey to Saudi officials Sanaa's wish to postpone the meeting until a later date "perhaps to be announced after the current pilgrimage season." The sources attribted the Yemeni request to the intensifying differences between Sanaa and Aden, particularly after the failure to implement the reconciliation agreement between the Yemeni president and his deputy. [passage omitted] This is the fourth time Yemen has requested postponement of committee meetings since they started in July 1992. Saudi Arabia still insists on its position, calling for the implementation of al-Ta'if border agreement signed by the two countries in 1934. [passage omitted]
