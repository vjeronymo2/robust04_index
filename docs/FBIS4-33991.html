BFN [Excerpts] Justices Edgar Oblitas and Ernesto Poppe have been removed from office and forbidden from serving in public office for 10 years by the Senate after being found guilty of malfeasance in office. [Begin unidentified senator recording, in progress] ...remove Edgard Oblitas Fernandez as president of the Supreme Court and deprive him from public office for 10 years based on Article 3, Chapter 1, and Article 4, Part 1, of the law of 7 November 1890. To remove Ernesto Poppe as Supreme Court justice and deprive him from public office for 10 years based on Article 3, Chapter 1, and Article 4, Part 1, of the law of 7 November 1890. [end recording] [passage omitted] Senate President Juan Carlos Duran said the Senate decision was based on the old juridical adage of emphasizing the positive and minimizing the negative. As such there is no prison sentence for either of the two justices. [passage omitted]
