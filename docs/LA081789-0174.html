August 17, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: ANAHEIM;

ANAHEIM COUNCIL SEEKS APPLICATIONS FOR BOARD

The Anaheim City Council is reviewing applications from residents to fill a vacancy on its Community Services Board. Members of the 15-member board act as advisers to the council on human services issues such as homelessness and child care.

Members are appointed to a four-year term. Meetings are on the second and fourth Thursday of the month at 7 p.m. in the council chambers.

Those interested should write to: City Clerk's Office, 200 S. Anaheim Blvd., Anaheim, Calif. 92805.
