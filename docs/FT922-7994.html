920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / UK Company News: City of Oxford net asset value falls City of Oxford Investment Trust reported total net assets at the end of the year to March 31 of Pounds 24.9m (Pounds 19.3m) - an effective fall of 16.5 per cent after adjusting for last summer's rights issue. Net asset value per income share was 19.6p, against 40.1p, with assets attributable to each zero dividend preference share rising to 72.4p (64.2p). Directors estimated that net asset value per income share had risen to 29.7p by May 12. Net revenue rose 35 per cent to Pounds 1.28m (Pounds 947,807) and earnings per share were 4.9p (4.74p). As forecast at the time of the rights issue, a proposed final dividend of 1.3p makes a total of 4.9p (4.5p).
