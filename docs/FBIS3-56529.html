Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Valeriy Gromak under the "News" rubric: "Lithuania Asks Sweden for Help"] [Text] Sweden -- Lithuanian National Defense Minister Linas Linkevicius and his deputy Sarunas Vasiliauskas are visiting Sweden. A meeting between the Lithuanian national defense minister and Swedish Defense Minister Anders Bjork took place. The Lithuanian National Defense Ministry Press Service has reported that a "new security structure in Europe and the Baltic countries" and Sweden's and Lithuania's security policy were discussed at it. Deputy Minister Vasiliauskas also held talks with representatives of the Swedish Armed Forces Support Service [sluzhba obespecheniya] on help in controlling Lithuania's state borders.
