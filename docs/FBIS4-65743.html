BFN [Excerpt] President Glavkos Kliridhis met British Deputy Foreign Secretary Tom Richardson in the Presidential Palace this morning. Speaking afterward, Richardson said the current process in the Cyprus issue has reached a decisive point. He expressed the hope that there would be an agreement on the confidence-building measures before the UN secretary general submits his report to the Security Council. After the report is submitted, he said, we can evaluate the direction that should be followed. Richardson said that in a meeting he will have tomorrow with [Turkish Cypriot leader] Rauf Denktas, he will discuss the need to accept the confidence-building measures, which he described as important. The British official also referred to the British Government's continuing interest in reaching an agreement on the confidence-building measures. But added, he is not in a position to know whether Denktas is willing to change his stance to facilitate moves toward an agreement. [passage omitted]
