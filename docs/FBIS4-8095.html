BFN [Text] In Kasindol Street in Sarajevo, the Serbian police stopped a convoy of a French nongovernment humanitarian organization Premiere Urgence because it found several boxes of ammunition in the convoy's vehicles. The headquarters of the army of the Srpska republic has stated that an investigation is underway and that a statement on this will be issued this evening. For the time being we only have pictures of this, which have already been sent to the world. Comments would be unnecessary anyway. [The studio announcer-read report is followed by a 2-minute video of Serbian policemen discovering ammunition in trucks with European Union and Premiere Urgence markings]
