July 14, 1990, Saturday, Southland Edition

PILKENTON SUSPENDED FIVE DAYS

Bruce Pilkenton, the leading rider at Los Alamitos summer quarter horse meeting, was suspended for five days Thursday night by the track stewards.

Citing Pilkenton for "crossing over without sufficient clearance" while riding Streakin Rampage in Wednesday night's 10th race, officials gave Pilkenton the suspension to be served next Tuesday through Saturday.
