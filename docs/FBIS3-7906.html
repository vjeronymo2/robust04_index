Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES 26/2/1994 -- Romania's President Ion Iliescu has sent a telegram of condolence to Yasir `Arafat, president of the state of Palestine, president of the Executive Committee of the PLO, in which he firmly condemns "the criminal and irresponsible act committed in Hebron, Cisjordan." "We express our conviction that the Palestinian people and the PLO leadership would know how to overcome this sad and dramatic moment of hard trial and sufferings, honouring the memory of the Palestinian martyrs in Hebron by new efforts and active demarches to further the process of peace in the Middle East," the telegram reads.
