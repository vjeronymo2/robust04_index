930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Jupiter European net assets decrease Jupiter European Investment Trust reported a fall in net assets per share to 66.2p at the end of October 1992, against 79.9p a year earlier. After-tax revenue, however, increased from Pounds 50,931 to Pounds 82,490, representing earnings per share of 0.69p (0.85p). An interim dividend of 0.5p has already been paid for the current year.
