Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, March 15 (SNARK)--Today the Parliament of Armenia voted down the governmental program of social-economic development of the republic for 1994. The opposition accused the government, headed by Grant Bagratyan, of lack of accurate economic model, principle of management system perfection and also concrete social guarantees. Simultaneously, the parliament ratified the budget of Armenia for 1994.
