BFN [Text] Baghdad, 9 May (INA) -- A delegation from the Jordanian al-Mustaqbal Party, led by the party's Assistant Secretary General Samir Khirfan, left Baghdad this morning at the conclusion of a visit that lasted several days. During the visit, the delegation met with several Iraqi officials in the party and the state and signed a working program with the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party to develop relations of cooperation in the interest of the peoples of the two fraternal countries and the objectives of the glorious Arab nation.
