January 9, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

JUDGMENT: Flamboyant Texas attorney Richard (Racehorse) Haynes (D6), who is defending a woman accused of looting more than $16 million from a now-defunct Santa Ana savings and loan, is not the trial's only newsmaker. . . . The prosecutor, Assistant U.S. Atty. Nancy Wieben Stock may be on the verge of becoming Orange County's next Superior Court judge. . . . Stock has been interviewed by several judicial screening committees, and insiders say she is a leading candidate. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
