DATES: Comments must be received on or before May 16, 1994. 14 CFR Part 71 [Airspace Docket No. 94&hyph;AWP&hyph;2] Relocation of the Kahului (OGG) VORTAC and Realignment of VOR Federal Airways at Kahului, Maui, HI AGENCY: Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), DOT. ACTION: Notice of proposed rulemaking.

SUMMARY: This notice proposes to relocate the Kahului (OGG) VORTAC at Kahului, Maui, Hawaii. The new Kahului VORTAC (OGG) would be constructed and operated approximately 4,600 feet northeast of the existing VORTAC facility. The proposed site is lat. 20 ○ 54 ’ 23.22 ’ ’ N, long. 156 ○ 25 ’ 15.47 ’ ’. The relocation of this NAVAID affects the following airways: V1, V5, V6, V11, V15, V17, V22. These airways shall be realigned coincidental with the new VORTAC location. DATES: Comments must be received on or before May 16, 1994.
