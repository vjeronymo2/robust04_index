BFN [Text] The State Bank reported recently that the Government Loans Market Research Committee has completed three draft legal documents related to establishing a securities market in Vietnam. The draft document on setting up the securities market specified tasks to be carried out from now until the establishment of a liaison office for the market. The draft decree on stocks and securities market is composed of 101 articles dealing with the issuance, posting, selling, and transferring of stocks and shares; foreign investment in securities; and capital and bonds of companies and corporations. The draft decree for a National Securities Committee stipulates that the committee is an independent administrative agency affiliated with the government. These three draft documents have been submitted to the Monetary and Financial Council and have been highly appraised by the government.
