BFN [Text] On Tuesday [10 May], Arthur Paecht, the rapporteur of the military programming law, declared that, "according to a unanimous recommendation of all experts consulted, the resumption of nuclear tests is indispensable." As to the Palen test simulation program, it "would not suffice to ensure the development of new nuclear charges." The deputy also pointed to two other "controversial" issues: "The decision by the president of the Republic to keep active the ballistic missiles on the Albion plateau" and "the five-year delay in the M5 strategic ballistic missile program."
