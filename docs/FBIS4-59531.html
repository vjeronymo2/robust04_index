BFN [Text] Brother 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani, member of the Presidential Council, has received the Arab League delegation led by brother Major General Muhammad Sa'id Bayraqdar, assistant secretary general for military affairs. The Arab League delegation is currently on a visit to our country. The member of the Presidential Council received a message addressed to brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, from Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid on the current developments in our country. The message expresses the Arab League's concern to maintain Yemeni unity as a Yemeni national gain and a pan-Arab achievement from which there must be no retreat. Brother 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani affirmed to the Arab League delegation that the rebellious faction in the [Yemeni] Socialist Party [YSP], which provoked the military situation, despite the exhausting efforts and many concessions made to the YSP to avoid military confrontation. However, the misguided faction continued with its stubbornness and dragged the country into the current situation. He stressed that the Yemeni leadership is keen to end the fighting by adhering to Constitutional legitimacy and its Constitutional and executive institutions as well as by sticking to the results of the parliamentary elections held on 27 April 1993. The member of the Presidential Council also expressed our country's appreciation of the Arab League's concern about what is happening in our country, for which the rebellious factions in the YSP leadership are held responsible. He added that Yemeni unity represents a great gain for our Yemeni people, who will defend and protect themselves from any plotting concocted by the rebellious faction in the YSP leadership, whose grudge against our people and their people made them shell the capital, Sanaa, and other Yemeni cities with Scud missiles. He said: The determination of our people to defend unity and democracy cannot be shaken by these desperate criminal attempts.
