BFN [Text] Italy has offered to supply troops to a UN standing army. More than 15 countries have already placed troops at the UN's disposal, and 54,000 soldiers could be rapidly deployed in peacekeeping operations. The UN plans to have 100,000 blue helmets by the end of the year. They will be used where both sides in a conflict ask for their intervention. The contingent being offered by Italy would largely be made up of medical personnel. Seventy thousand troops are currently deployed by the United Nations.
