930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / International Company News: BIL plans public offering BRIERLEY Investments, (BIL) the New Zealand-based hotels and investments concern, is planning an initial public offering of a company that comprises around 10 per cent of its New Zealand investments, AP-DJ reports from Wellington. The offer will not be made until at least the second half of 1993. The subsidiaries to be included in the new company, will comprise Brierley's small holdings in New Zealand.
