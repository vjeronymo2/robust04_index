Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by S Denny] [Text] Pretoria March 29 SAPA--The Transitional Executive Council [TEC] has proposed the cities and towns where the nine provincial legislatures could convene within seven days of the elections, SABC radio news reported on Tuesday. It said in Pretoria, however, these centres would not necessarily be the provincial capitals. The cities are: Cape Town for the Western Cape; Johannesburg for the PWV [Pretoria, Witwatersrand, Vereeniging] region; Bloemfontein for the [Orange] Free State; Mmabatho for the North West Region; Kimberley for the Northern Cape; Pietermaritzburg for kwaZulu/Natal; and Nelspruit for the Eastern Transvaal. It suggested both King William's Town and Bisho for the Eastern Cape and both Pietersburg and Lebowakgomo for the Northern Transvaal.
