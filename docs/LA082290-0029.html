August 22, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

VAN NUYS RUTH TEAM LOSES 3RD-ROUND GAME

The Jefferson Parish team from New Orleans collected nine hits and eight walks Tuesday against three Van Nuys-Sherman Oaks pitchers en route to a 9-3 victory in a third-round game of the Babe Ruth World Series in Houma, La.

Van Nuys-Sherman Oaks (15-3) will play Olathe, Kansas, tonight at 8 in an elimination game.
