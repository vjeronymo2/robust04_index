BFN [Text] A Philippine delegation led by Mr. Roberto R. Romulo, foreign minister of the Republic of the Philippines, arrived in Yangon [Rangoon] by air at 1245 today at the invitation of U Ohn Gyaw, minister of foreign affairs. The Philippines foreign minister, Mr. Roberto R. Romulo, and delegation called on Foreign Affairs Minister U Ohn Gyaw at the Foreign Ministry's Annex Building at 1600 and held talks on matters of bilateral interest. Foreign Minister U Ohn Gyaw hosted a dinner in honor of the Philippines foreign minister and entourage at the Mya Yeik Nyo Royal Hotel in the evening.
