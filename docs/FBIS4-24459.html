BFN [Text] Beijing, May 30 (CNS) -- According to a survey conducted by the Consumers Committee of the China Quality Management Association and the Beijing Shiqing Market Consultancy to 5,120 households in Beijing, Shanghai and Guangzhou, the three municipalities are now taking a lead in the country in terms of households' possession rate of colour TV sets, hi-fi sets, refrigerators, air conditioners, automatic washing machines, electric fans and water heaters. The possession rate of colour TV sets by households in these cities has reached 97.4 percent, that of refrigerators 95.7 percent, that of electric fans 90.7 percent and that of water heaters 58.7 percent. Such a possession rate of hi-fi sets is 25.3 percent in Beijing, 23.8 percent in Shanghai and 45 percent in Guangzhou. Owing to the regional weather differences, the possession rate of air conditioners is 4.6 percent in Beijing, 14.5 percent in Shanghai and 34.9 percent in Guangzhou, while the average rate of this machine in these three cities is 16.3 percent.
