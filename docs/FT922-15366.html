920401 FT 01 APR 92 / UK Company News: Estates & General dips to Pounds 2.3m Estates & General, the property group, made a 'creditable' pre-tax profit of Pounds 2.3m in 1991. It was struck after provisions of Pounds 900,000 against trading stock and joint venture schemes and writing off all interest. For the previous year the group made a profit of Pounds 2.77m. Gross investment rental income rose by 19 per cent to Pounds 8.6m. The level of voids increased but still represented less than 2.5 per cent of the annual rent roll on the established portfolio. Turnover rose to Pounds 14.4m (Pounds 10.4m) excluding the sale of investment properties. Rent reviews concluded in the year showed an increase of 84 per cent. The investment property portfolio had been professionally valued at Pounds 95.7m, a reduction of 9.1 per cent on a year earlier, after sales and expenditure. Borrowings had risen to 125 per cent of shareholders' funds and to 53 per cent of property assets. Fully diluted earnings per share were 4.59p (4.92p). The proposed final dividend is an unchanged 2.525p to hold the total at 3.75p.
