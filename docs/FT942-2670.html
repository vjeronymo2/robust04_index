940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / International Company News: Peru sells 49% stake in cement producer The Peruvian government has sold its minority holding in Cementos Lima, the cement producer. The auction, held in Lima, was Peru's first over-the-counter primary offering for a privatisation. Proceeds from the sale of the 49 per cent holding - comprising some 309,000 voting shares - topped Dollars 103m. The 39 per cent auctioned fetched Dollars 82m while existing shareholders paid Dollars 21m for another 10 per cent, the equivalent of the final price. Lead managers for the sale were Bankers Trust of New York. Thirteen stockbrokers presented bids which finally secured 41 per cent of the allocation at the clearing price of Dollars 266 a share.
