BFN [Text] In Sikkim, two bombs exploded at the residence of the state power minister and the former chief secretary. The director general of police, Mr. P.S. Bawa, told PTI today that the power minister, Mr. B.B. Subba, was not at his residence when the explosion took place last night. Another bomb was hurled at the residence of the former chief secretary, Mr. P.K. Pradhan, in the early hours yesterday. The explosions, however, did not cause much damage.
