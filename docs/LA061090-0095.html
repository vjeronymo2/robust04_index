June 10, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ROAD NOTES

Caltrans plans the following closures on San Gabriel Valley freeways this week:

Two lanes on the westbound Pomona Freeway (60) in the City of Industry from southbound 605 Freeway to Santa Anita Avenue will be closed today from 6 a.m. to noon.

One lane on the eastbound San Bernardino Freeway (10) in the city of El Monte from Peck Road to the San Gabriel River will be closed Tuesday from 8 a.m. to 2 p.m.

Two lanes on the southbound Orange Freeway (57) in the city of Pomona from Madera Road to Kuehner Drive will be closed Wednesday from 8 a.m. to 3 p.m.
