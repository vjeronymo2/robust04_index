November 23, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: COSTA MESA;

OCC SENDS OUT ITS SPRING SCHEDULE

The 1991 spring class schedule for Orange Coast College is available to the public.

The 120-page booklet has been mailed to households in the Coast Community College District and may also be picked up at the Admissions and Records Building.

The schedule includes a list of all 2,500 classes offered at OCC this spring. It also contains information on financial aid, enrollment procedures and support services.

The spring semester starts Jan. 22. "Late start," 15-week classes begin the week of Feb. 11.

Returning students may register by mail and telephone during November. Registration by appointment begins Dec. 12 and open registration, on a first-come, first-served basis, starts Jan. 17. For more information, call (714) 432-5772.
