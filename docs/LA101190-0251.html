October 11, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

GERRING, RARICK LEAD 1ST ROUND

Cathy Gerring and Cindy Rarick shot 3-under-par 69s today to share the first-round lead in the LPGA World Championship.

One stroke back in the 16-player invitational event was another American, Dottie Mochrie. The top money winner this year on the U.S. women's tour, Beth Daniel, was tied at 71 with European Tour star Marie-Laure de Lorenzi of France.

Gerring had seven birdies over the 6,155-yard Cely course, one bogey, and a triple bogey.
