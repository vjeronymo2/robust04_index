940627 FT 27 JUN 94 / Arts: Montreux - Openings The jazz glitterati meet the elder statesman of rock at the Montreux Jazz Festival, which starts on Friday. The lakeside Stravinski Hall will swing to the diverse sounds of Bob Dylan, Wynton Marsalis, the Attaila Superchoir and the opera orchestra of Marseille. A major highlight this year is the 50th anniversary celebrations of the Verve Label and the appearance of its entire roster on one bill across two nights.
