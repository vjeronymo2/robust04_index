Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The final results have been compiled for the 15 January nationwide referendum in Turkmenistan. Live on the air, here is our Ashgabat correspondent, Merdyen Amanov: [Amanov] I think that Mayak listeners will be interested to hear the results of the nationwide referendum held yesterday on whether to extend the tenure of office of Saparmurad Niyazov for a second five-year term. Literally an hour ago the Central Electoral Commission completed its work and Turkmenistan will not now be holding its next presidential elections. The final results of the referendum are as follows: The number of people who received voting slips was 1,959,637. Turnout was 99.99 percent of the electorate, and 99.99 percent of those who voted were in favor of extending the term in office of President Niyazov. The vote against was 212 people, which comes to 0.01 percent.
