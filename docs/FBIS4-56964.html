BFN [Article by Felipe Farias] [Excerpt] The Bahia State branch of the Brazilian Social Democracy Party [PSDB] has decided to support Workers Party presidential candidate Luis Inacio Lula da Silva. The decision was made during the 8 May convention that officially nominated former Minister Jutahy Magalhaes, Jr., as the party's gubernatorial candidate for Bahia, Deputy Sergio Gaudenzi as vice gubernatorial candidate, and Waldir Pires as candidate for the Senate. The position of the Bahia branch of the PSDB is deemed irreversible. Jutahy Magalhaes, Jr. said: "The decision has been made. Everywhere I go I say that my presidential candidate is Lula. We have already reported our decision to the party and to Fernando Henrique Cardoso." [passage omitted]
