BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Anna Melnikova, Yuriy Sizov] [Text] Moscow, 30 Apr -- An accord has been reached between the Russian and Latvian presidents that Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin is to visit Riga, where a package of bilateral economic agreements will be signed, Russian President Boris Yeltsin told an ITAR-TASS correspondent today after the signing of Russian-Latvian agreements. In his words, the bloc of economic documents comprises about 10 agreements. They will allow the solution of questions of free trade, most-favored-nation status, taxation, tariffs and duties, as well as other topical problems concerning the development of bilateral relations.
