BFN [Text] Paris, May 21, IRNA -- Iran and France reached an agreement on rescheduling of Iranian debts here Friday [20 May]. An Iranian Embassy spokesman in an interview with IRNA here said the financial accord was reached after two days of negotiation between representatives of the Iranian Central Bank and the French Economic Ministry. The spokesman also said the two sides could solve the main points of the financial accord within a broad framework while the remaining details would be worked out in the near future.
