930927 FT 27 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Garton Engineering Garton Engineering, a maker of engineering components and special fasteners, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 37,000 to Pounds 197,000 for the first half of 1993. Turnover edged ahead to Pounds 10.6m (Pounds 10.6m) but some Pounds 275,000 of operating profits of Pounds 304,000 (Pounds 53,000) came from the sale of land stocks. Interest payable was Pounds 107,000 (Pounds 16,000). Earnings per share advanced to 3.41p (0.63p) and the interim dividend is lifted to 1.125p (1p).
