940211 FT 11 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Alpha shares rise to 172p on first day trading Shares in Alpha Airports Group, which provides flight catering to airlines and retail services at airports, rose sharply on their first day of trading yesterday. They closed at 172p, against the flotation price of 140p. Alpha was formerly the airports division of Forte, which has retained a 25 per cent stake in the company. A total of 113.09m shares were offered, of which half were firmly placed with institutions. The remainder were subject to clawback to meet retail demand through intermediaries. The public offer was subscribed 6.1 times. For the year to January 31 1994, Alpha is forecasting a 19 per cent rise in operating profits to Pounds 20.6m on turnover of Pounds 423.1m.
