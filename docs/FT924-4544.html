921202 FT 02 DEC 92 / King Fahd urges UN to allow Bosnia's moslems to arm themselves King Fahd of Saudi Arabia yesterday urged the UN to allow Bosnia's Moslems to arm themselves in their fight against the Serbs, writes Our Foreign Staff. Foreign ministers from the 47 states of the Organisation of the Islamic Conference meeting in Jeddah are expected to call on the UN to use force to stop Serbian attacks on Bosnia's Moslems.
