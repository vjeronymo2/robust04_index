Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Constitutional National Union announced today its participation in the multiparty meeting between a group of parties and the National Dialogue Commission which will be held today and tomorrow at the commission's invitation of the latter. It is known that the Constitutional National Union, which is composed of 12 political parties, announced its official establishment on 24 December at a news conference held by the representatives of these parties at the headquarters of the Popular Association for Unity and Action. It is worth pointing out that the Constitutional National Union is based on 10 principles, the most important of which are: adherence to the constitution, the unity of Algeria, the 1 November Declaration, Islam as the state religion, pluralism, and rotation of power.
