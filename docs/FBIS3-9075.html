Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Mate Granic, Croatian deputy prime minister and foreign minister, had a meeting with Bosnian Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic in Washington today. They discussed the implementation of the agreements signed so far and established with satisfaction that events were developing in a positive direction. For example, prisoners have already been exchanged. They stressed that both the Muslim and Croat sides should prevent all attempts at provocations. Granic and Silajdzic also discussed the strategy of negotiating with the Serb side and some current issues, such as the EU's future assistance to Croatia and Bosnia-Herzegovina.
