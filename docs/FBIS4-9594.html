BFN [Report by Fahrudin Becic from Gorazde] [Text] Not only have they not withdrawn to the distance of 3 km, but the chetniks are also entrenching as close as 1 km to the city center. At about 1300, a small group of them came down to UNPROFOR [UN Protection Force] personnel carriers situated some 350 meters from the hospital. With UNPROFOR approval, the chetniks are continuing settling their civilians inside the 3 km zone. This is what is happening in Zubcici, south of the town. This is what is happening east of the town, in the suburbs of (?Fazagici) and (?Pubjeri). One should also say that all these three villages are villages that were populated exclusively by Muslims before the war. A (?similar) situation is in Kopaci, which is more than 3 km from the city center. The chetniks are also settling people there. Looting on the entire occupied territory is drawing to an end. According to the information of the Army of Bosnia-Herzegovina in Gorazde, artillery weapons have not been removed from the majority of the strongholds between Cajnice and Foca. Of course, in some cases, they were only redeployed to new positions, close to [words indistinct] This refers to (?Nekopi), Zebina Suma, [word indistinct], Djakovici, and many other locations. This is Fahrudin Becic reporting for the Radio and Television of Bosnia-Herzegovina.
