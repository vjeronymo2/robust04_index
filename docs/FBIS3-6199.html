Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, March 15 (YONHAP) -- South Korea's Government Administration Ministry and China's Personnel Ministry signed an agreement Tuesday [15 March] on exchanges and training of public officials. The agreement promotes bilateral visits, business trips and training for public officials and, as a start, Seoul and Beijing will exchange about 15 division director-level officials taking part in a training program this year. In addition, the two countries will exchange administrative information such as government publications and provide conveniences for each other's officials under the reciprocity principle. The agreement was signed during a visit to Beijing by Son Chong, director-general of the Central Officials Training Institute.
