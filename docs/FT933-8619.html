930814 FT 14 AUG 93 / UK Company News: British Thornton placing for Pounds 5.6m British Thornton Holdings, the packaging group, is raising Pounds 5.6m via a placing of 43m shares to help finance the acquisition of Masterform Group, the manufacturer of packaging and display materials. The new shares are being placed at 13p. The group also announced a 39 per cent rise in pre-tax profits, from Pounds 947,000 to Pounds 1.3m, for the year ended April. Sales increased to Pounds 8.7m (Pounds 4.7m). Earnings per share fell to 0.98p (1.04p) and there is a final dividend of 0.25p. The consideration for Masterform is Pounds 6.6m with the initial payment being Pounds 5.83m, satisfied by 17.1m shares and Pounds 3.4m in cash. Further payments, up to a maximum of Pounds 340,000 and Pounds 425,000, will be made in two tranches based on Masterform's profits exceeding Pounds 850,000 and Pounds 1.2m in the years ending April 30 1994 and 1995 respectively. In addition loans of Pounds 247,000 made to Masterform will be paid back on completion of the acquisition.
