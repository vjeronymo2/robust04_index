930211 FT 11 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Elbief deficit up to Pounds 244,000 Shares of Elbief yesterday fell 5p to 21p as the Birmingham-based manufacturer of photograph frames, clocks and mirrors accompanied news of an increased interim deficit with a gloomy trading statement. Losses before tax for the six months to October 31 increased to Pounds 244,000 (Pounds 57,000). Mr Harry Prais, chairman, said: 'Overheads were pruned severely and employee numbers were drastically reduced, but this was negated by lower sales.' Turnover declined 17 per cent to Pounds 1.9m. Mr Prais said he saw little sign of an end to recession and did not anticipate a return to profits in the current year. Losses per share were 0.2p (0.04p).
