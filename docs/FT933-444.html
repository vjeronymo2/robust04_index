930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / The Labour Party Conference: Morris warns on policy 'vacuum' MR BILL MORRIS, leader of the TGWU, last night warned that Labour must address the 'vacuum ' that people perceived in its policies. Speaking at a rally of the leftwing Tribune group, Mr Morris said that voters often liked what Labour said about issues such as health and education, but believed there was a lack of substance. 'We need to make the quantum leap from saying what is wrong to saying what is right,' he said.
