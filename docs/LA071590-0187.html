July 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: ASSOCIATE PROFESSOR AT CALTECH GETS FELLOWSHIP

Yaser S. Abu-Mostafa, associate professor of electrical engineering and computer science at Caltech, has been awarded the Richard P. Feynman-Hughes Fellowship.

The fellowship is awarded annually to a young member of the faculty in the division of engineering and applied science. It provides research support of $30,000 each year and may be renewed annually for up to three years.
