940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / Buyer for Binder NI offices Grant Thornton, the UK's seventh largest accountancy firm, has acquired the Northern Ireland offices of Binder Hamlyn, the accountancy network that has broken up in the wake of a proposed merger with Arthur Andersen. Eleven partners and 115 staff in the Belfast and Enniskillen offices of Binder, generating fee income of Pounds 3.5m, will join Grant Thornton, which is also in discussions with Binder's Birmingham office. The largest four Binder Hamlyn offices are poised to merge with Andersen, the UK's third largest firm.
