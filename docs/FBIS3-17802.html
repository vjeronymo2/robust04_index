Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Grant Bagratyan today received the U.S. Ambassador to Armenia Harry Gilmore, with whom he discussed economic issues. The improvement in the taxation system and American experts' assistance in this direction, the American contribution to spring agricultural work, and other problems were discussed. They also touched upon ways to find a political settlement to the Karabakh conflict. The CSCE Minsk Group and Russia's important efforts in this regard were stressed. The prime minister also referred to the reoperation of the Armenian nuclear plant and to the position of the U.S. official organs. The ambassador noted that those Americans opposing the reopening of the Armenian nuclear plant are not informed about the power situation in Armenia. They are worried that the safety of the nuclear plant is not up to the desired level therefore they will not assist with its reopening. Gilmore handed Bagratyan a letter from the president of the International Organization of Science and Technology inviting Armenia to join the international organization.
