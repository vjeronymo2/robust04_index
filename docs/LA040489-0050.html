April 4, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

TODAY'S CHICAGO RACE

Chicago officials predict that 70% of the 1.56 million voters will cast ballots in today's mayoral election. Whites make up 48% of registered voters, blacks 42% and Latinos 7%. The winner will complete the last two years of the late Harold Washington's second term. A poll released Monday by The Daily Chicago Southtown Economist and WBBM-TV gave Daley 51% to 35% for Evans and 3% for Vrdolyak, with 11% undecided.
