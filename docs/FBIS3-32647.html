Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The foreign ministers of the Mercosur [Common Market of the South] countries will meet in Buenos Aires on 9 March. Uruguayan Foreign Minister Sergio Abreu reported that the foreign ministers of the Mercosur countries will review a Brazilian proposal to form a regional free trade zone and establish a common external tariff. The meeting date was decided during an informal meeting held by the foreign ministers of Argentina, Brazil, Uruguay, and Paraguay.
