940728 FT 28 JUL 94 / Anglo-Saxon woman examined The hand of Michael Halliwell, of the Canterbury Archaeological Trust, yesterday examining that of an Anglo-Saxon woman found on a building site overlooking Dover harbour. The woman has a ring on her modern-day ring finger, which is believed to be a rare find. More than 200 Anglo-Saxon graves have been discovered at the 5th-century hillside cemetery.
