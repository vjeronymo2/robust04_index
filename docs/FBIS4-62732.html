BFN [Text] Borispol, Kiev oblast, 16 May -- Deputy Prime Minister Valeriy Shmarov and Deputy Foreign Minister Borys Tarasyuk gave a news conference at Borispol airport on the results of their visit to the United States. Mr. Shmarov, the head of the delegation, spoke about the main issues which the delegation worked on in Washington, in particular, Ukraine's entry into the regime of control over the proliferation of missile technologies and missile equipment, analysis of the implementation of START I Treaty obligations, cooperation in nuclear power, and issues connected with conversion. The planned work was carried out. When working with their American counterparts, Mr. Shmarov emphasized, our delegation felt their recognition of Ukraine as a partner. An important result of the Ukrainian delegation's work in Washington was the signing of the memorandum on mutual understanding between the governments of Ukraine and the United States concerning the handover of missile equipment and technologies, and also a joint statement which noted that all missiles aimed at Ukraine will be redirected by 30 May. "This visit was an important step toward extending cooperation between the governments of Ukraine and the United States," Ukraine's deputy premier stressed.
