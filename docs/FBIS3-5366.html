Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 5 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il showed deep solicitude to the Pyongyang children's foodstuff factory for the rising generation. He recently provided the factory with new conditions for the improvement of the quality of foodstuffs for children and increase the quantity at the factory. After building the excellent production bases of foodstuffs for children in Pyongyang, he indicated long ago the orientation and way of supplying different kinds of foodstuffs good for the health and growth of children in large quantities, sent a lot of equipment to the factory and took measures for the production, management and operation of the factory.
