BFN [Text] Kuwait, June 21, SPA -- The Kuwaiti First Deputy Premier and Foreign Minister Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad met here on Tuesday [21 June] with the secretary general of the Organization of Islamic Conference Dr Hamid Algabid who is currently visiting Kuwait. During the meeting, they discussed cooperation between Kuwait and the ICO. Shaykh Sabah also conferred today with the parliament speaker of Comoros Islands Mr Fernandez who is visiting Kuwait.
