April 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE TIMES' ALL-INLAND BASKETBALL TEAM

The Times' all-star basketball awards banquet is today at the Anaheim Hilton Hotel. Male and female athletes from 14 zones will be presented awards, with a coach and player of the year being named from each region.
