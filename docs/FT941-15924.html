940112 FT 12 JAN 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Mexican equities had recovered most of Monday's substantial losses by yesterday's close as concern about the recent spate of rebel activity began to abate. The IPC index was quoted 95.45 points, or 3.9 per cent, up at 2,554.56 - this followed a 6.3 per cent drop during the previous session, which was the biggest one-day percentage fall since October 1989. Some 146.6m shares were traded and advances outstripped declines by 84 to 22.
