BFN [Text] A spokesman for the Islamic Movement in Iraqi Kurdistan has announced that his movement carried out military operations yesterday, during which it regained Tappah Kurrah, (Muhammad Abad), (Kumalar), Anab, Bakrawah, Sirah, and Halabjah from the forces of the Patriot Union of Kurdistan [PUK], at the same time killing a number of PUK troops. The spokesman added that the Islamic Movement is continuing its advance and is besieging the city of Sayyid Sadiq. He added that during their occupation of the above-mentioned villages, Jalal Talabani's forces violated people's honor, carried out murders and massacres, and stole livestock. The Islamic Movement spokesman said that the citizens of Shanaderi Khwaru fled to the plains and wadis to escape the PUK forces' assaults.
