940913 FT 13 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Hanson's Scholes offer unconditional Following clearance from the Office of Fair Trading, Hanson, the Anlgo-US conglomerate, has declared its agreed cash offer for Scholes Group, the electrical equipment maker, unconditional in all respects. It has received acceptances in respect of 25.8m shares or 67.08 per cent.
