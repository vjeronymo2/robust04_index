940131 FT 31 JAN 94 / Ex-minister faces legal challenge A telephone quiz due to be launched this week in which Mr Timothy Renton, former arts minister, is involved, could face a legal challenge from the Labour Party which says it would undemine the National Lottery. Mr Renton is chairman of of Interactive Telephone Services, which on Thursday is due to announce the first pounds 250,000 jackpot winner in a telephone quiz game played on premium charge telephone lines. Mr Renton concedes that the quiz could provide competition to the lottery.
