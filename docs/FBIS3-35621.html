Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yuriy Meshkov, elected president of Crimea on 30 January, has spoken in favor of holding a referendum on independence. In an interview with journalists he proposed that the referendum be held on 27 March, Interfax reports. Meanwhile, Nikolay Bagrov, who lost the Crimean presidential election, believes that a referendum would destabilize the situation in the region and aggravate relations between Ukraine and Russia.
