December 16, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

O.C. EARNINGS

The Admar Group Inc.: The Orange-managed health care services company said its third-quarter earnings rose 74% to $47,000 from $27,000 a year earlier.

Revenue for the quarter ended Oct. 31 increased 4% to $2.6 million from $2.5 million. For the past nine months, earnings nearly doubled to $284,000 from $146,000. Revenue increased 3% to $7.7 million from $7.9 million.
