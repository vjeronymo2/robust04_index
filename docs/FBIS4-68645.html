CSO ["Georgian Head of State Order on Chairman of the Board of Tbilisi Samgorskiy Rayon"; signed by E. Shevardnadze] [Text] Givi Maziashvili is appointed Chairman of the Board of Samgorskiy Rayon. Emzar Turdzeladze is removed from the post of Chairman of the Board of Samgorskiy Rayon. [signed] E. Shevardnadze Givi Maziashvili was born in Tbilisi in 1941. He has a higher education. He graduated from the Transportation Faculty of Georgian Polytechnical Institute. At various times he has worked in various positions in the Highways Ministry, as chief of Tbilisi Zavodskiy Rayon's Street Operations Section, first deputy chairman of the Rayispolkom, and chief of the Tbilisi Street Operations Administration. He is married and has two children.
