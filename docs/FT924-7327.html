921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Towles cuts interim loss to Pounds 537,000 Towles, the Loughborough-based textile company, reported lower pre-tax losses of Pounds 537,000 for the six months to August 31, compared with Pounds 644,000. Turnover increased from Pounds 5.75m to Pounds 6.49m. After a tax credit of Pounds 177,000 (Pounds 212,000) losses per share were 15p (18p).
