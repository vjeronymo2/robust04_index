April 3, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

From Broadway to the 'City of Angels'?"City of Angels" may be on its way to the City of the Angels. The producers of the Larry Gelbart/Cy Coleman Broadway musical hope to begin previews Oct. 9 for a Century City production at the Shubert Theatre, said co-producer Roger Berlind. A spokesman for the Shubert Organization confirmed that "the intention is to have the show out here as soon as possible, but there is no booking at the moment." BETH KLEID
