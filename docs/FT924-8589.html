921110 FT 10 NOV 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Landed Assams opened 5 to 15p dearer and closed often up to 20p higher reports the Tea Broker's Association. Bangladesh teas met keen competition and prices advanced 5 to 15p. East Africans also appreciated between 5p and 15p particularly better liquoring types. Offshore good demand at fully firm rates. The highest price realised this week was 208p for an Assam pf. Quotations: Quality 195p, nom good medium 160p, medium 137p, low medium 98p.
