920520 FT 20 MAY 92 / Ugandan aid pledge Western countries pledged Dollars 830m (Pounds 467m) in aid to Uganda yesterday and urged it to improve its human rights record, saying this would affect future aid levels, Reuter reports from Paris. The money, promised after a two-day meeting chaired by the World Bank, would be split into Dollars 500m for projects and Dollars 330m for balance of payments assistance.
