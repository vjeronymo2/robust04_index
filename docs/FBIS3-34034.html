Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam received an EC delegation headed by Eberhardt Rhein yesterday evening. The meeting was attended by Dr. 'Abd-al-Rahim Subay'i, minister of state for planning affairs, and (Allain Rodans), head of the EC mission in Damascus. The talks dealt with the regional situation and Syrian-EC ties.
