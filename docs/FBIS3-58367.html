Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Georgian Head of State Eduard Shevardnadze arrived in Ankara today as the official guest of President Suleyman Demirel. He was received at Esenboga Airport by Refaiddin Sahin, minister of agriculture and village affairs. The guest head of state first visited the Ataturk Mausoleum. The official welcoming ceremony will be held at the Cankaya Mansion this afternoon. After that, Shevardnadze and Demirel will hold a meeting. Official talks will be held at the Cankaya Mansion under the chairmanship of the two heads of state. Shevardnadze will receive Prime Minister Tansu Ciller and Motherland Party leader Mesut Yilmaz this afternoon. This evening, he will attend a dinner to be given in his honor by President Demirel. Shevardnadze will leave Turkey tomorrow.
