Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sofia, February 25 (BTA) -- Macedonia's President Kiro Gligorov has addressed a special message to President Zhelyu Zhelev to thank him for the support the Republic of Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] received from Bulgaria after Greece had imposed a unilateral economic embargo against it. President Gligorov appreciates deeply Bulgaria's readiness to help Macedonia overcome the difficulties it is facing and its support at international level. The message was delivered by Bulgaria's ambassador to Skopje Angel Dimitrov, the Presidential Press Centre reported.
