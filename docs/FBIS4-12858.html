BFN [Text] Government reshuffle decree: After taking cognizance of the Constitution and our decree issued on 16 Rabi' al-Thani 1413 H, corresponding to 2 October 1992 AD, appointing the heir apparent, Sa'd al-'Abdallah al-Salim al-Sabah, as prime minister, and decree number 198 of the year 1992 forming the government, and decree number 2 of the year 1993 AD appointing a minister of state for the cabinet affairs, and decree 150 of the year 1993 amending the decree on the government reshuffle. On the basis of the recommendation of the prime minister, we have decreed the following: Article One: The appointment of Nasir 'Abdallah al-Rawdan [second deputy prime minister] is amended in the following way: Nasir 'Abdallah al-Rawdan [becomes] second deputy prime minister and finance minister. Article Two: -- Ahmad al-Hamud al-Jabir is appointed defense minister. --Ahmad Khalid al-Kulayb, minister of social affairs and labor --Jasim Muhammad al-'Awn, minister of communications, electricity, and water --Habib Jawhar Hayat is appointed minister of public works, and minister of state for housing affairs. --'Abd al-'Aziz Dakhil al-Dakhil, minister of state for cabinet affairs and minister of planning. --Dr 'Abd al-Rahman Salih al-Muhaylan, health minister --Dr 'Abd al-Muhsin Mid'aj al-Mid'aj, oil minister. --'Ali Sabah al-Salim, interior minister. --Dr 'Ali Fahd al-Zumay', minister of religious endowments and Islamic affairs. --Hilal Mishari al-Mutayri, minister of commerce and industry. Article Three: The prime minister is requested to implement this decree and convey it to the national assembly. It is to take effect as of the date of its issuance and will be published in the official gazette. [Signed] Emir of Kuwait Jabir al-Ahmad al-Sabah; Prime Minister Sa'd al-'Abdallah al-Salim al-Sabah. [Dated] Issued on 2nd Dhi al-Qa'dah 1413 H. corresponding to 13 April 1994 AD.
