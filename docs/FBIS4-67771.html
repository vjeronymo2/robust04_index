BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- The Federal Government will provide 3.5 million German marks for the scrapping, dismantling, or conversion of Russian nuclear arms. This was stated by the Foreign Ministry in Bonn on Tuesday [19 April]. After a round of German-Russian talks, agreement was reached on, among other things, technical assistance, including special instruments and the safe dismantling of nuclear arms and their warheads. The Foreign Ministry further stated that a study on the conversion of weapons-grade plutonium would be initiated. In addition, cooperation for the destruction of chemical weapons will continue.
