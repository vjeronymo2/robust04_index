940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Saints boosts net asset value by 24% Scottish American Investment - Saints to the stock market - yesterday reported a 24 per cent expansion in net asset value per share, from 152.2p to 188.3p, over the 1993 year. Net revenue dipped to Pounds 10.3m (Pounds 10.6m), for earnings of 4.61p (4.73p) per share. A recommended final dividend of 1.18p brings the total for the year to 4.63p (4.35p).
