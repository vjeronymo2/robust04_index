940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Henderson Strata Net asset value per share of Henderson Strata Investments increased from 271p to 297.7p during the six months to April 30; the outcome compared with 231.8p at end-April 1993. Available revenue for the six months fell from Pounds 47,000 to Pounds 12,000, equivalent to 0.07p (0.29p) per share. The directors expected to recommend payment of a reduced dividend for the year - last year's single, final payment was 1.4p.
