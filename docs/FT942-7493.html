940525 FT 25 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Andrews Sykes Andrews Sykes, which hires out and distributes engineering, heating and air conditioning equipment, said the 3.5p dividend for the six months to November 30 1993 on its convertible preference shares, which was scheduled for payment in December 1993, would be paid on May 31. A decision on the preference dividend due on June 1 will be taken later in the year.
