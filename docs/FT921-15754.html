920108 FT 08 JAN 92 / Environment law group launched COMPANIES COULD face an increase in damages claims after the launch yesterday of the Environmental Law Foundation (Elf) - an organisation which aims to help people protect their environment. Elf has nearly 100 lawyers ready to give local groups legal advice and to take cases through courts or planning inquiries. Leigh Day, one of Elf's member firms, is handling a claim by 1,500 residents of London's Docklands against the London Docklands Development Corporation and Olympia & York, the builder of Canary Wharf. Elf. It announced yesterday that the residents had won the right to legal aid.
