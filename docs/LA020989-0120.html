February 9, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

SCRIPPS PRESIDENT TO RESIGN IN JUNE

John H. Chandler, president of Scripps College for 13 years, has announced that he will resign June 30 to work on research for a book at Oxford University's Worcester College.

Howard Brooks, provost at Scripps, will serve as acting president until Chandler's successor is chosen. A college spokesman said the board of trustees hopes to have the new president by July 1, 1990.

Chandler headed a recently completed campaign that raised about $41 million for the women's college, which is one of six in the Claremont Colleges group.
