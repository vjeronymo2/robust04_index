931209 FT 09 DEC 93 / UK Company News: S&N sells ophthalmic solutions side Smith & Nephew, the worldwide healthcare group, is to sell its UK ophthalmic solutions business to Laboratoire Chauvin for Pounds 18m cash. The proceeds will go to reduce borrowings. LC is engaged in the manufacture of opthalmic products in France. The sales and profit of S&N's solutions business were Pounds 7m and Pounds 1.5m respectively in 1992 and net assets were Pounds 6m.
