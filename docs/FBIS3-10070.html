Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] President-elect Eduardo Frei has ratified Jose Pablo Arrellano in his post as Finance Ministry budget director, at the request of future Finance Minister Eduardo Aninat. Finance Minister-designate Eduardo Aninat and Budget Director Jose Pablo Arellano, who has been ratified in this post for the next administration, arrived at Frei's office on Hindenburg Street at noon. Arellano said that he will continue with an austerity policy in the fiscal field. [passage omitted]
