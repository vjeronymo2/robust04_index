BFN [Unattributed report: "Ukraine's Military Agency Denies"] [Text] The Ukraine Defense Ministry denied 3 June an ITAR-TASS report to the effect that more than a hundred Ukrainian paratroopers have been redeployed to the Belbek airfield near Sevastopol. TASS claimed their objective was to prepare the airfield for a landing, if necessary, of large Ukrainian military units.
