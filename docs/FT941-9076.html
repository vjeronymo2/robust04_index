940216 FT 16 FEB 94 / International Company News: IBJ Securities leads Nissan placement Nissan Motor, the Japanese carmaker, has appointed IBJ Securities, the securities affiliate of Industrial Bank of Japan, to lead manage the placement of domestic straight bonds worth Y20bn (Dollars 192m), writes Emiko Terazono in Tokyo. The issue, one of three tranches totalling Y60bn, will be the first led by a securities house affiliated to a bank, established last year, when Japan's ministry of finance partially eased barriers between the banking and securities industries.
