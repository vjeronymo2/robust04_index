Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow February 7 TASS -- Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov said the Black Sea Fleet was and is the guarantor of stability on the peninsula, the press service of the fleet told ITAR-TASS on Monday. Meeting today in Sevastopol Fleet Commander Eduard Baltin, the first popularly elected president of the Ukraine's semi-autonomous republic said that the problems of the fleet should be resolved considering opinions of the Crimean public, its supreme bodies and in the interests of the peoples of the Crimea, Ukraine and Russia. Commander of the disputed fleet Baltin expressed confidence that "as the president of all the Crimeans, Meshkov would protect their rights irrespective of nationality and political views, that his activities would be aimed at strengthening peace and international concord in the Crimea." Meshkov and Baltin agreed to meet regularly to exchange information on the most pressing issues of the Crimean political life.
