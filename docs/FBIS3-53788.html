Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Algeria: Kafi Decides To Live in Tunisia"] [Excerpt] Algeria -- AL-HAYAH has learned that Algerian President 'Ali Kafi has decided to live in Tunisia after leaving power on 31 January. Well-informed sources have told AL-HAYAH that secret contacts have been made at the highest level between Tunisia and Algeria to guarantee President Kafi's security and stability after the end of the Higher State Council's term. The president's wife had gone to Switzerland to look for a place for them to live in, but the president prefers to live in an Arab country. A source close to the National Dialogue Commission told AL-HAYAH that the commission yesterday proposed to President Kafi to issue an amnesty for the "Salvation Front leaders" to enable to them to participate in the "National Seminar" scheduled for 25 and 26 January. It is not ruled out that the president may issue an amnesty including Abassi Madani, leader of the Islamic Salvation Front. [passage omitted]
