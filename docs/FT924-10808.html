921029 FT 29 OCT 92 / International Company News: Imasco maintains profits advance IMASCO, the tobacco, financial services and retailing group controlled by BAT Industries, saw profit gains in the third quarter and nine months, but says the fourth quarter may be more challenging. Third-quarter consolidated net profit was CDollars 111.5m (USDollars 90.2m), or 88 cents a share, up from CDollars 99.8m, or 78 cents, a year earlier, on net revenues of CDollars 2.02bn, against CDollars 2.05bn. For the first nine months, it earned CDollars 269.7m, or CDollars 2.09, up 14 per cent from CDollars 237.2m, or CDollars 1.82. Tobacco earnings were up 10 per cent in the quarter and 9 per cent in the nine months. The financial services subsidiary, Canada Trust, suffered a 29 per cent drop in net earnings in the quarter.
