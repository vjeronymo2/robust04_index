BFN [Excerpt] Hannover (DDP/ADN) -- Lower Saxony Interior Minister Gerhard Glogowski has announced a halt to the deportation of Kurds from his federal state to Turkey. Should the heads of the regional governments fail to decide tomorrow on a collective halt to deportation, Lower Saxony will act unilaterally, Glogowski told the Lower Saxony Landtag today. The interior minister assured the Landtag that at the moment no one is being sent from Lower Saxony to face death or torture. In his speech, the text of which was released in advance, Glogowski stressed that no Kurds will be deported from Lower Saxony until the prime ministers' decision. The interior minister criticized the fact that weapons are again being supplied to Turkey, despite its being well-known that the Turkish Army uses them against the Kurds. The minister spoke of a war of extermination against the Kurds in Turkey. He called on Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel to take a firm stand to bring the murder in Turkey to an end. [passage omitted]
