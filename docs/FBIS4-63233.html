BFN [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk has sent a message to Russian President Yeltsin where he expresses hope for the earliest solution of the Black Sea Fleet problem. As it has become known to INTERFAX, in his message the Kravchuk expresses regret over what he describes as the unconstructive stand taken by the Russian delegation at the talks on the Black Sea Fleet which took place in Sebastopol [Sevastopol] in April. Kravchuk said about the need to divide the fleet in line with the Moscow understandings of 15 April (according to the understandings Russia possesses 80 pct of the Fleet and Ukraine -- 20 percent). The Ukrainian President voiced the need to attach a special status to the Marines.
