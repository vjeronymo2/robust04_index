930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Increased deficit at Oliver THE OLIVER Group, the footwear retailer, reported increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 12.6m for 1992, compared with Pounds 10.7m. Turnover fell from Pounds 84.9m to Pounds 82.1m. There was an exceptional charge of Pounds 6.92m (Pounds 5.29m), including reorganisation and revaluation costs. Losses per share came out at 49.27p (39.43p). Action taken to dispose of loss-making shops and to cut costs contributed to achieving break-even trading in the second half with sales improving significantly in the final quarter.
