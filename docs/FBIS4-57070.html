BFN [Report by correspondent Walter Lima from the Federal Police headquarters in Brasilia -- live] [Text] Today at dawn, the Army surrounded the Federal Police [PF] headquarters in Brasilia. The Army surrounded the perimeter of the Federal Police installations at about 0100 today. The Army surrounded the PF headquarters in the Southern Autarky [State autonomous organizations] block of Brasilia, the PF regional office at the southern police block, and the PF office at the Justice Ministry, strategically stationing men armed with rifles and submachine guns and accompanied by German shepherds. The area here in the Southern Autarky sector is isolated. Both drivers and pedestrians must identify themselves if they want to go through any of the isolation cordons. Traffic has been diverted from the area. The situation here is so far apparently calm. There is great expectation over the arrival of the PF members on strike. There are nearly 1,000 soldiers stationed here in the PF headquarters. A short while ago reinforcements arrived in two trucks and a jeep. We will have more information from the Southern Autarky block where Army troops surrounded the perimeter of the PF headquarters in Brasilia.
