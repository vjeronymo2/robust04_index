BFN [Excerpt] President Rauf Denktas has said that the nondocument on the confidence-building measures [CBM's] has been further damaged as a result of the violation of the map during the last round of the talks. Replying to a question by a Turkish Cypriot News Agency correspondent on whether any results were achieved during his visit to Maras [Varosha] yesterday, Denktas said that he did not go to Maras to achieve results but to see what the two maps submitted by the United Nations were about and to determine whether the sections requested in the second map were actually within the barbed wire region. He added that the sections requested in the second map were not remotely connected with the barbed wire region. Commenting on the Greek Cypriot shots fired at Turkish Cypriot positions on the border, Denktas noted that it is clear that provocations are increasing in all fields. He stressed that the side that is wrong and knows it to be so is escalating the provocations. Pointing out that it is the duty of the just to be patient and coolheaded, Denktas added: I, therefore, sincerely congratulate our soldiers and their commanders who have maintained their patience, discipline, and their cool in the face of these provocations.
