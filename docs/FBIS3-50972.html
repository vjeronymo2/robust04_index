Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "This Morning" newscast] [Text] Ramiro Gurdian Ortiz, president of the Higher Council of Private Enterprise [Cosep], has urged the National Opposition Union [UNO] deputies to rejoin the National Assembly as of Monday, when the 10th legislative session of the Nicaraguan parliament begins. During a meeting held this past weekend, the business leader made the request to the 22 deputies who continue with their UNO strategy. Cosep President Gurdian Ortiz pointed out, however, that the businessmen are not taking sides in the strategy by the UNO deputies who are struggling to call for a constituent assembly in Nicaragua.
