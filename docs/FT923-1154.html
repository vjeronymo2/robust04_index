920924 FT 24 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Headlam more than doubled to Pounds 465,000 Headlam, the footwear and fabrics group, yesterday announced more-than-doubled interim profits and an acquisition. Pre-tax profits for the six months to end-June advanced from Pounds 213,000 to Pounds 465,000, and came from turnover up from Pounds 11.5m to Pounds 24.1m. The company being acquired is Minto and Turner, a Manchester-based industrial fabrics group. The total consideration for the deal is Pounds 820,000, payable in cash, loan notes and ordinary shares. The company has made a number of acquisitions in the period as part of its strategy of broadening its base, and Mr Graham Waldron, chairman, said further acquisitions were being sought. The loss-making Faire Bros business has also been sold to management. Earnings per share emerged at 1.79p (1.41p) and the interim dividend is maintained at 0.75p.
