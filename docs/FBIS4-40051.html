BFN [Text] Pavlodar Oblast maslikhat deputies of the standing commission for social policy matters adopted an appeal to the Kazakh Supreme Council. It says the oblast inhabitants standard of living is decreasing, food and goods prices are rising unstably, and payments of wages and pension are not paid on time, especially for workers in education, medicine, and culture. It can be interpreted as a great violation of the Kazakh law on labor and pension, of which Kazakhstan has signed the human right declaration. Therefore, it demands the adoption of a decree by the Supreme Council on payment of wages, pension, and benefits. In case of delay in payment, the Supreme Council should also adopt a law to pay additionally for inflation.
