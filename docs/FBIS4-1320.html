BFN [Text] It's been reported from Pietersburg, Gazankulu, Venda, and Lebowa that voting has been hampered by a lack of ballot papers and equipment. Reports say that people have traveled far and are waiting to vote. The commissioner of the QwaQwa police, Major General Jaap Venter, has made an urgent appeal for ordinary voters not to travel to polling stations for special votes today. Gen. Venter said that thousands of people had arrived at the seven polling stations in the area, thinking that they could vote today.
