BFN [Text] A House of Representatives multi-party delegation under House President Alexis Galanos will go to Israel this evening for a three-day official visit. Speaking at Larnaca Airport before the delegation's departure, Galanos said the visit is of great importance for strengthening relations between Cyprus and Israel. The efforts under way for peace in the Middle East, he added, and the latest agreement signed between Israel and the Palestinians, open great possibilities for Cyprus as well, to promote a solution to its problem.
