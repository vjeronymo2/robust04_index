931217 FT 17 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Park Food in Pounds 9.3m expansion Park Food Group, which specialises in supplying Christmas hampers via a network of commission-paid agents direct to homes, has paid Pounds 9.3m for Handling Solutions, a promotions management company. The consideration is being satisfied by the issue of new Park shares worth about Pounds 800,000 and the balance of Pounds 8.5m through the issue of 10-year variable rate guaranteed redeemable loan notes.
