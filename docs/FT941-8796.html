940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / Russia in telecom share issue Russia's telecommunications monopoly Rostelekom will offer 22 per cent of its shares to the public between March 14 and April 12, privatisation officials yesterday, Reuter reports from Moscow. Rostelekom, which controls some 80 per cent of all telephone lines in Russia, became a joint-stock company last September. The state owns 51 per cent of the company's shares.
