BFN [Text] The justice minister and attorney general, 'Abd-al-Aziz Shiddu, has directed the supreme prosecution attorney in Khartoum State to adopt legal procedures regarding violation of the daily AL-SUDAN AL-DUWALI of Section No. 6 of the penal code related to circulation of false news. He also directed the concerned authorities to conduct preliminary investigations of some of the issues published by the daily. He underlined that those who raised claims on corruption and malpractices at the state government institutions will be required to prove their claim . On application of the emergency law against the daily, Shiddu said that the law is valid in spite of the fact that it has not been applied in many cases in the past period so as to ensure stability of the political system.
