Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] It has been stated by Prime Minister Rabin's office that no telegram was received by the office from the head of the Supreme Muslim Council in Jerusalem and the head of the Waqf in Hebron containing a complaint about Barukh Goldstein, the Hebron murderer. Meretz Knesset member 'Anat Ma'or today presented the telegram in which the two complain that more than four months ago Goldstein attacked the muezzin in the mosque in the Cave of the Patriarchs and tried to damage the carpets in this mosque.
