BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Amangeldy Akhmetalimov and Gennadiy Kulagin] [Text] Alma-Ata [Almaty], 14 Apr -- Kazakhstan's delegation to the CIS summit left for Moscow today. The delegation is led by Vice President Yerik Asanbayev. It has already been officially stated that Nursultan Nazarbayev will not be able to attend the summit for health reasons.
