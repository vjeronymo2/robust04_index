921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Reorganisation lifts Eliza Tinsley Reorganisation at Eliza Tinsley Group, the USM-quoted maker of hardware products, is paying off. Pre-tax profits for the half year ended September 30 showed a 42 per cent increase. The profit came to Pounds 408,000, compared with Pounds 287,000 last time and with Pounds 510,000 for the full previous year. Turnover declined from Pounds 7.71m to Pounds 7.47m, after a first quarter increase. Earnings per share were 3.57p (2.5p) and the interim dividend is again 1.8p.
