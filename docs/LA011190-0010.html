January 11, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

ILLEGAL SIGNS

I see there are now "sign cops" added to the list of enforcers who control our lives.

These include smog cops, street-cleaner cops, dirt cops, water cops and sky cops.

Will this ever stop?

Add the usual assortment of delighted finks and witness new horizons of "save us from ourselves" regulation.

DON REED

Santa Maria
