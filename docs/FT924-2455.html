921212 FT 12 DEC 92 / Bank to issue Pounds 700m of gilts THE Bank of England is to issue Pounds 700m worth of gilts in three tranches, available on Monday. They are Pounds 100m of 8 per cent Treasury stock due 2002/2006, Pounds 250m of 9 per cent Treasury stock due 2008 and Pounds 350m of 9 per cent Treasury stock due 2012. The certified price for the bonds is 95 7/8 , 103 1/16 and 102 1/4 respectively.
