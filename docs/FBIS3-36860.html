Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yuriy Meshkov, the recently elected president of the Ukrainian autonomous republic of Crimea, on Wednesday [9 February] left for a visit to Moscow, a source in his staff said. Meshkov will discuss with the Russian leadership the possibility of closer economic cooperation between Crimea and Russia, the source told Interfax. At the same time, no sources in the Russian parliament, the Russian Foreign Ministry or Ukraine's embassy in Moscow have been able to confirm this information.
