BFN [Decree no. 426 of the Russian Federation Government "On Ratifying the Charter of the INFORMATION TELEGRAPH AGENCY OF RUSSIA," signed by V. Chernomyrdin, chairman of the Russian Federation Government, and dated 4 May 1994] [Text] In implementation of the Russian Federation [RF] president's edict no. 2257 of 22 December 1993 "On the INFORMATION TELEGRAPH AGENCY OF RUSSIA," the RF Government decrees that: 1. The appended Charter of the INFORMATION TELEGRAPH AGENCY OF RUSSIA adopted by the agency's labor collective is to be ratified. 2. The RF State Committee for the Management of State Property is to register in accordance with prescribed procedure the assignation to the INFORMATION TELEGRAPH AGENCY OF RUSSIA of property which is in federal ownership, along with property acquired with federal budget funds and used by the agency to pursue its statutory activity. [Signed] V. Chernomyrdin, chairman of the RF Government [Dated] Moscow, 4 May 1994 Editorial Office note: The Charter of the INFORMATION TELEGRAPH AGENCY OF RUSSIA is not cited.
