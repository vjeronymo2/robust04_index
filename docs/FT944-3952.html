941210 FT 10 DEC 94 / UK Company News: BBB Design loss The cancellation of its contract with ICL to provide software for the Automobile Association left BBB Design Group with a pre-tax loss of Pounds 88,000 in the half year ended October 31, against a profit of Pounds 43,000. The USM-quoted design and computer software company said some shareholders had expressed concern about the litigation between the company, ICL and the AA. The board said it was determined to pursue the matter 'to a just and equitable conclusion'. Turnover was Pounds 633,000 (Pounds 956,000). Losses per share were 1.06p (earnings 0.51p).
