Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Agronomist Roosevelt (Saindic) officially announced his resignation from PANPRA [National Progressive Revolutionary Party of Haiti] leadership after having discontinued participation in their activities as of May 1993. The resignation letter of Roosevelt (Saindic) mentions deep opposition to his management style from several members of the party, especially concerning work methods, strategy, and the party's current political leanings. The resignation of (Saindic) from PANPRA's leadership, follows that of several other members of this political party including Georges Weirleigh, Arnold Antonin, and Claude Elisma.
