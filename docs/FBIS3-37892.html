Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [UKRINFORM report: "A Meeting Between Members of the Ukrainian and Crimean Governments"] [Text] Under the chairmanship of Ukraine's acting prime minister, Yukhym Zvyahilskyy, Ukraine's Cabinet of Ministers held a meeting to discuss the fulfillment of the Ukrainian president's commissions on the results of his talks with the president of the Crimean Republic. Those present included Ukraine's vice premiers Vasyl Yevtukhov, Valentyn Landyk, secretary of the Ukrainian presidential administration Mykola Khomenko, and other members of the government. The working group of the Crimean government was headed at the meeting by acting chairman of the Crimean Republic's Council of Ministers Anatoliy Franchuk. The participants in the conference considered and discussed a whole series of questions, including problems associated with the implementation of the agreement on budget relationships between the government of Ukraine and the government of the Crimean Republic; on the allocation of 110 billion karbovantsi to clear the debt in financing the program for stabilizing the Crimean economy and in financing measures associated with the return of Crimean Tatars, Germans, and other deported peoples; on the division of state property among Ukraine and the Crimean Republic; on the determination of sources for covering the expenditure in hard currency of enterprises situated on the territory of the Crimean Republic; on questions of canceling restrictions for Ukrainian citizens' entering the city of Sevastopol; and on measures for stabilizing the functioning of a number of branches of the Crimean Republic's economic complex.
