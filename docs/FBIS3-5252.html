Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN [Text] On the night of 25 February, the people, who were furious with the Vietnamese communist puppets for shipping troops and ammunition by trains to attack and massacre them and plunder their property, cut off three stretches of railroad with a total length of 25 meters in an area between Moung and Kouk Trom in Battambang Province. They also cut off four other stretches with a total length of 200 meters in an area between Prey Svay and (O Krut) in the same province. The people said only by doing so can the Vietnamese communist puppets be prevented from shipping troops and ammunition to attack and massacre them and plunder their property at will.
