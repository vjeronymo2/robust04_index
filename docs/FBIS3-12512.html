Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The forces of the tyrant Saddam's regime are continuing their indiscriminate bombardment of the residential areas in al-'Amarah [Maysan] Governorate. This cowardly bombardment, which began several days ago, has set fire to and demolished several houses and wounded many citizens. According to reports, the tyrant's regime is seeking to evacuate that area of its residents in an attempt to stem the tide of jihad operations that use the marshlands as a springboard. The reports add that the citizens of the area are facing the assault steadfastly.
