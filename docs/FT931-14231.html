930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / International Company News: Barlow Rand plans stock distribution SHAREHOLDERS in Barlow Rand, South Africa's leading industrial conglomerate, voted unanimously to distribute by dividend a portion of the company's 22.2m shares in Randgold & Exploration, AP-DJ reports from Johannesburg. The distribution will be in the ratio of six ordinary shares in Randgold for every 100 Barlow Rand shares held at the close of business on January 29 1993 and will result in Barlow Rand's interest in Randgold being maintained at approximately 35 per cent. Randgold shares will be listed on the Johannesburg Stock Exchange on February 1.
