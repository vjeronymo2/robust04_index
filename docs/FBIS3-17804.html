Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Armenia's leadership has so far made no comments on an Iranian Foreign Ministry note saying the Iranian C-130 military transport aircraft which crashed in Nagorno-Karabakh on March 17 was shot down by Armenian detachments. Sources in the president's office and Foreign Ministry of Armenia declined to comment on the note, reported by the news media on Monday, since, they said, the Armenian Government had not yet received it.
