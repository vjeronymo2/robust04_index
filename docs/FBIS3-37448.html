Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Byrkin] [Text] Moscow February 17 TASS -- Georgia's separatist republic of Abkhazia fears the Georgian Army is assembling forces to attack across the border, the press service of the Abkhazian mission in Moscow told ITAR-TASS on Thursday. The mission, referring to the Abkhazian military sources, said over 2,000 Georgian soldiers backed by armour and artillery are now concentrated in the western part of Georgia. The press service warned against "an attempt of the Georgian Army to invade the territory of the sovereign republic".
