910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / UK Company News: King & Shaxson falls to Pounds 1.8m The year to April 30 ended with lower net profits at King & Shaxson Holdings, the discount house group. The outcome was Pounds 1.8m after providing for rebate, tax and transfer to contingency reserves, against Pounds 2m previously. Shareholders' funds and minority interests amounted to Pounds 18.89m. The Pounds 1m subordinated unsecured loan stock 1986-91, issued by Smith St Aubyn (Holdings), was redeemed at par on March 31. The final dividend is 7.75p for a same-again total of 10.25p.
