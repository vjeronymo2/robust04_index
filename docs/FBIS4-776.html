BFN [Text] The situation in Bophuthatswana is reported to be on the brink of going out of control. A co-administrator of the homeland, Mr. Job Mokgoro, said after a meeting with the Bophuthatswana police in Mmabatho that law and order was threatened and that this could affect the elections in the territory. Mr. Mokgoro said the TEC [Transitional Executive Council] had been informed of the deteriorating situation in the homeland. Reacting to rumors that certain police members loyal to the deposed government were regrouping to disrupt the election in the homeland. He said the police were investigating the situation.
