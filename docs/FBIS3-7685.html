Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Acts Creating Tension" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, March 30 (KCNA) -- The U.S. imperialists and the South Korean puppets staged a war exercise directed against the North of Korea on March 28, creating a strong electronic jamming in the air above Yongwol, Chunchon, Kongju and Oeyon Islet, according to military sources. It involved overseas-based flying corps including nuclear-capable F-16 fighter-bombers plus fighter-bombers, assault planes and observation planes belonging to the U.S. Airforce in South Korea and the South Korean puppet Airforce, more than 300 in all. An RC-135 strategic reconnaissance plane flew over the military demarcation line (MDL) area that day for photographing and electronic espionage on the whole areas of the North. On the same day, formations of helicopter gunships and transport helicopters of the puppet army staged exercises for supporting ground units in an attack and airlifting in the air above Paju and Yonchon Counties, Kyonggi Province, while 105 mm artillery units fired scores of shells at an area adjacent to the Demilitarized Zone of the MDL. The war moves of the U.S. imperialists and the South Korean puppets against the North are driving the situation of the Korean peninsula to the brink of war.
