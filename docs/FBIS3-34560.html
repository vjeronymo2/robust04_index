Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 9 Feb (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa today met with Shulamit Aloni, Israeli minister of science, culture, and communications, who is currently visiting Cairo. The Israeli minister said that the talks dealt with the relations between the two countries and the means to promote them in the fields of arts and scientific research. She added: We hope that a comprehensive peace will soon be established so that cooperation will be developed in the various domains. The Israeli minister noted that she was briefed by 'Amr Musa on Egypt's efforts to advance the peace process. She expressed her satisfaction with the role Egypt is playing in achieving peace between the Arab countries and Israel.
