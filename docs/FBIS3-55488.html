Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The second round of talks on the peaceful settlement of the Georgian-Abkhaz conflict will be held in Geneva on 11 January. In this connection, Aleksandre Kavsadze, chairman of the Georgian committee for inter-ethnic relations and human rights, has stated that Georgia is opposed to the examination of the future state system of Abkhazia at the Geneva talks. He said that the state system of Abkhazia is Georgia's internal problem and that it is now being examined by the republic's Constitutional Commission. Mr. Kavsadze believes that the subject of the Geneva talks should be the future of the Georgian refugees who left Abkhazia last year.
