Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 30 (KCNA) -- Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto mentioned the friendly relations between Pakistan and Korea when she received DPRK ambassador to her country on Jan. 25. She said the friendly relations between the two countries were established by his excellency the great leader President Kim Il-song and the late Prime Minister Zulfiqar Ali Bhutto and are growing in strength today. The DPRK has wrought amazing miracles in different sectors of the national economy under the wise guidance of his excellency the great leader President Kim Il-song, she said. To have his excellency respected Kim Chong-il as the successor to the revolutionary cause pioneered by his excellency the great leader President Kim Il-song and as the dear leader is a firm guarantee for more solid political stability and eternal prosperity of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, she said. She told the ambassador that the Pakistani Government would strengthen and further develop friendly relations with the DPRK in political, economic, cultural and all other fields.
