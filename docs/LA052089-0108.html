May 20, 1989, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

MISL PLAYOFFS

WINGS 6, BLAST 3 -- Chico Borja's hat trick led Wichita to a 6-3 victory over Baltimore in the fourth game of the semifinals, saving the Wings from elimination.

Borja scored his third goal with 1:36 to play after tapping away the ball from Baltimore sixth-attacker David Byrne near the Blast red line. His 30-footer into the open-net gave the Wings a 5-3 lead.

The Blast lead the best-of-seven series, 3-1.
