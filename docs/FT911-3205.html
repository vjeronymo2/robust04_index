910425 FT 25 APR 91 / UK Company News: Hopkinsons static after restructuring Hopkinsons Group, the valves manufacturer, reported static profits for 1990 -a year which saw its restructuring virtually completed. Sales fell 21 per cent from Pounds 60.34m to Pounds 47.82m, including Pounds 42.37m (Pounds 38.86m) from continuing operations. This turned into operating profits of Pounds 4.7m (Pounds 5.7m), including Pounds 4.19m (Pounds 4.42m) from continuing operations. After interest and other income amounting to Pounds 2.02m (Pounds 1.02m) the taxable result was unchanged at Pounds 6.72m. An unchanged final dividend of 2.5p is recommended, making a total for the year of 3.7p (3.5p) on reduced earnings per share of 8.01p (8.5p).
