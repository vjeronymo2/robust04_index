BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] PRI [Institutional Revolutionary Party] deputies, senators, and assembly members have affirmed their total support for PRI presidential candidate Ernesto Zedillo Ponce de Leon. Thanking them for their support, Zedillo said he favored honest elections and respect for the citizen's vote. [Begin Zedillo recording] As the PRI's presidential candidate, I promise to respect the electoral law reliablely and honestly. Today I confirm, before my party colleagues and the entire nation, that I will assume each and every one of my party's commitments in favor of electoral democracy. [end recording] Zedillo reiterated he will not accept any illicit votes and demanded that there be an external audit of the electoral register. He said he will heed the decisions, reached by consensus, on the presence of foreign visitors at the elections. The PRI candidate said he is prepared to debate other presidential candidates and added that PRI members do not fear democracy. Zedillo observed that lasting economic progress and social improvement can only be achieved in a democratic system. At the meeting with the legislators, candidate Zedillo appointed new PRI Secretary General Ignacio Pichardo Pagaza as the general coordinator of his political campaign. Ernesto Zedillo Ponce de Leon also met with CTM [Confederation of Mexican Workers] leaders and promised to defend the workers' rights. He said he will allow changes to the labor law that will benefit Mexican workers and added that their benefits will not be withdrawn. [Begin Zedillo recording] The time has come for wages to improve in Mexico. We can do this without risking the stability that has cost us so much. We will do it by improving productivity and making sure the benefits that result from this productivity end up in the Mexican workers' pockets. [end recording] As he arrived and when he left the CTM auditorium, the PRI candidate hailed all PRI members gathered there.
