910418 FT 18 APR 91 / International Company News: Blackstone in German link-up BLACKSTONE Group, the US investment banking boutique, yesterday announced that it was linking up with Sal. Oppenheim, an old-established private bank in Germany. Blackstone has already formed similar alliances with Hambro Magan in the UK, with Banque Indosuez in Paris, and with Nikko Securities in Japan. The aim of the latest venture is for the two firms to co-operate on transatlantic mergers and acquisitions business, and to participate jointly in investment opportunities in both Germany and the US as principals.
