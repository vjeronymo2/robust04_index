Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] On the morning of 4 January, the provincial party committee held a meeting in Shijiazhuang to report to veteran cadres of the departments directly under the provincial authorities on the current situation. Cheng Weigao, secretary of the provincial party committee, presided over the meeting. Attending the meeting were provincial leaders Li Bingliang, Chen Yujie, Xu Yongyue, Gao Yongtang, Li Haifeng, and Wang Manqiu, as well as members of the veteran cadre work advisory group under the provincial party committee. At the meeting, Li Bingliang, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee, gave a report on the province's anticorruption situation. Chen Yujie, standing committee member of the provincial party committee and director of the organizational department of the provincial party committee, relayed the guidelines of the third plenary session of the 14th CPC Central Committee and the guidelines of the national economic work conference, reported on the situation of the seventh plenary session of the fourth provincial party committee, reviewed and summarized the province's work in 1993, and arranged for the province's 1994 work. Xu Yongyue, standing committee member of the provincial party committee and secretary of the provincial political and legal commission, reported on the province's public security, procuratorial, and judicial work and the situation on comprehensive improvement of public order since the beginning of last year. Cheng Weigao gave a speech at the conclusion of the meeting. He hoped that veteran comrades would support, as always, the work of the provincial party committee and the provincial government, would continue to set an example in supporting and participating in reform, would continue to exploit their advantages of having rich experience, extensive social contacts, and great political and social influence to offer advice and suggestions for the province's economic construction, would serve as the mediator for opening up and absorbing foreign investment, would help solve contradictions in strengthening spiritual civilization and maintain stability and unity, and would continue to exploit the remaining strength to help invigorate Hebei and build Hebei into an economically strong province. He demanded: Party committees at all levels should pay high attention to the work related to veteran cadres, sincerely and genuinely respect and depend on veteran cadres, sincerely and wholeheartedly show concern for the livelihood of veteran comrades, truly solve veteran comrades' problems in food, clothing, housing, transport, and medical treatment, and strive to create a fine living condition for veteran comrades.
