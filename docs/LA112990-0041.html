November 29, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Hindu-Stone: A priest who conducted the secret wedding of British rocker Mick Jagger and actress-model Jerry Hall on the island of Bali last week said the couple had converted to Hinduism. Antara news agency Wednesday quoted Hindu priest Ida Pedanda Sukawati as saying they had signed a letter stating their commitment to Hinduism during the ceremony a week ago. CLAUDIA PUIG
