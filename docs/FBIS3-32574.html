Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Normalizing relations with the United States regarding migration has always been one of the Cuban Government's objectives. For example, in 1993, Cuba authorized 100,000 people to temporarily leave the country, but only 30,000 were able to travel. The remaining 70,000 were denied a visa by the U.S. Interests Section, located in our capital. Journalist Zenaida Costales has a report for us on what goes on there, day after day.
