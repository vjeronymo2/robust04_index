December 30, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

DODGERS' NEW APPROACH: JUST TEACH 'EM TO HIT

When Tommy Lasorda was confronted with the strikeout record of their new acquisition, Juan Samuel, he said, "We will help him make the necessary adjustments in that, too." If they can do that for Samuel, how come they never made the necessary adjustments for John Shelby?

FREDERICK D. MULLEN

Upland
