940423 FT 23 APR 94 / UK Company News: Joseph Holt advances to Pounds 8m Joseph Holt, the Manchester-based brewer, turned in higher pre-tax profits of Pounds 8m for the 53 weeks ended December 31, against Pounds 7.04m for the previous year. Turnover rose from Pounds 25.7m to Pounds 28m. Earnings per share were 177.75p, against 154.49p, while the recommended final dividend of 37p makes a total of 48p (41p) for the year.
