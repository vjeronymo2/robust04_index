930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa INDUSTRIAL shares were generally firmer in low volume and the index closed 15 higher at 4,573. The overall index rose 3 to 3,418, while golds slipped 5 to 784 on a weaker bullion price. Barlow Rand lost 25 cents to R46 after warning that first-quarter earnings might be below the equivalent 1992 period.
