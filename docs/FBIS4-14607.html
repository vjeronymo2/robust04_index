BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Sitnikov] [Text] United Nations April 1 TASS -- U.N. Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali has appointed Vladimir Petrovskiy general secretary of the U.N. disarmament conference in Geneva, U.N. secretary general's official representative Joe Sills said here today. Announcing this decision, Joe Sills said that after proper consultations with members of the disarmament conference, the U.N. secretary general has decisded to appoint director general of the U.N. branch in Geneva Vladimir Petrovskiy as disarmament conference general secretary, and also as his special representative at the conference. This decision comes into force immediately, Joe Sills said. Joe Sills noted that Petrovskiy had been carrying out these duties on a temporary basis since December 5, 1993. Sills said that Petrovskiy will continue to carry out duties of the U.N. branch director general in Geneva.
