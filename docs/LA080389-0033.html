August 3, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: DANCE

The Children of Crisis Foundation, a newly formed charity, will benefit from tonight's performance of the Bolshoi Ballet Academy at the Pantages Theatre. Classical Artists International, which is sponsoring the academy's six-week tour, and the Nederlander Organization donated 500 tickets, which are being sold at $250 apiece to benefit Armenian children who were orphaned or disabled by last December's devastating earthquake. ALEENE MacMINN
