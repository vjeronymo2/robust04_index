BFN [Text] Tokyo, June 10 KYODO -- A delegation of legislators from the Social Democratic Party (SDP) left Japan on Friday [10 June] to visit north korea via Beijing. The legislators will arrive in Pyongyang on Saturday. They will hold talks with senior officials of the Korean Workers' Party on North Korea's nuclear issue before returning to Tokyo next Tuesday, SDP officials said. The SDP is Japan's second largest party after the Liberal Democratic Party, both in the opposition camp.
