May 13, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

'EYE ON L.A.' AND ITS SEGMENT ON CHILE

My husband and I have traveled in Chile twice in the last three years. Does that mean we are making (as it was put by Erik Nelson, former "Eye on L.A." producer) "a passive endorsement of what, at best, is a murky political situation"? Come on -- give me a break.

LOUISE VAN DELL

Corona del Mar
