Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] President Dzhokhar Dudayev of Russia's separatist republic of Chechnya on Sunday made some major replacements in his civil service. He dismissed Prosecutor General Elza Sheripova (the post still being vacant), Interior Minister Salman Albakov was replaced by Magomed Eldiyev, chief of the Penalty Execution Department, chief of the Criminal Police Department Saipuddi Khasanov was replaced by his deputy Said-Khasan Batayev, while Akhmed Gaduyev, a district prosecutor, was substituted for Idris Albastov as Supreme Court chairman. Observers do not see any connection between these replacements and an attack on Dudayev's residence on Saturday evening.
