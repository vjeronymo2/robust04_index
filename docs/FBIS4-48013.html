BFN [Excerpt] Nairobi, 9 May (AFP) -- Fierce fighting erupted in Kigali on Monday [9 May] between government troops and rebels of the Rwandan Patriotic Front (RPF) forcing a United Nations plane heading to the wartorn country to return to Nairobi. The Hercules C-130 from the Canadian Air Force had left Nairobi at 10:00 A.M. (0700 GMT) but midway through the flight was ordered to return to Nairobi because of the battles in Kigali. Abdul Kabia, spokesman for the UN Assistance Mission in Rwanda (UNAMIR) said the fighting began at 0800 GMT in various parts of the capital including the military camp in Kanombe, near the Kigali airport and the rebel headquarters. He said one mortar fell on the airport runway but did not cause any injuries. [passage omitted]
