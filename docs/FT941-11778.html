940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / SA army restores peace in East Rand The army was ordered to restore peace and community services in South Africa's strife-torn East Rand yesterday, replacing police units many accuse of fomenting unrest, Reuter reports from Pretoria. Mr Mac Maharaj, representing the multi-party Transitional Executive Council, said troops would help to clean streets, repair homes and restore amenities.
