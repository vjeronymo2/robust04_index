Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, December 24 (XINHUA) -- Vice-Foreign Minister Liu Huaqiu will, as special envoy of the Chinese Government, attend the inauguration ceremony of President Frel of the Republic of Chile on March 11, at the invitation of the Chilean Government. Around this time, he will pay a working visit to the Republic of Venezuela and the Republic of Argentina upon the invitation of the foreign ministries of the two countries, a Foreign Ministry spokesman announced here this afternoon.
