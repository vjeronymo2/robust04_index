Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 12 (XINHUA) -- There are now 470,000 state-owned enterprises in China with unemployment insurance schemes, covering some 74 million employees. Some collectively-owned firms have followed suit. In the first ten months of last year more than 700,000 unemployed people received benefit funds from the state, according to the first issue of the newspaper "CHINA REFORM" today. Since China changed its "insurance for people waiting for jobs" to unemployment insurance in 1986, a total of 1.2 million people have received relief funds. Now the majority of the unemployed have found new jobs, according to the paper.
