921126 FT 26 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Chester Waterworks achieves Pounds 1.04m Chester Waterworks Company, the statutory water company which acts as agent for the Welsh Water Authority and the North West Water Authority, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.04m for the six months to September 30. The company has changed its year end, and for the 15-month period ended March 31 profits were Pounds 2.45m. Turnover amounted to Pounds 2.86m (Pounds 6.58m). Mr Joseph Musgrave, the chairman, said the results were being published in anticipation of proposed changes in the company's constitution, including re-registration as a plc. Pro forma earnings per share were 283p (655p) and the dividend 2.1p, the maximum statutory rate. An interim dividend of 75p is forecast, subject to the removal of the statutory restrictions in the new year.
