920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Losses deepen to Pounds 1.7m at de Morgan Pre-tax losses at de Morgan Group, which provides property investment and finance advice, deepened from Pounds 1.13m to Pounds 1.71m in the year to April 30. Mr P Cooper, chairman, said the property market had remained difficult during the year with a resultant decline in turnover. However, the first quarter of the current year showed a small profit, reflecting an improvement in transaction activity. Turnover fell to Pounds 1.38m (Pounds 2.69m). Losses per share worked through at 3.06p (3.03p).
