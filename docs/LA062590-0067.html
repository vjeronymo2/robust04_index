June 25, 1990, Monday, San Diego County Edition

WOMAN ATTACKED AT EL CAJON SCHOOL

A 24-year-old El Cajon woman was sexually assaulted over the weekend on the grounds of an El Cajon school, police said.

The woman was confronted by a man with a knife as she walked by Naranca Elementary at 10:30 p.m. Saturday, said police. The man forced the woman to a remote area of the campus, where he sexually assaulted her with a foreign object, then robbed her of $4 in cash.

The assailant was described as a white or Latino male about 5 feet 4 inches tall, weighing about 165 pounds, with short, dark hair and dark-rimmed glasses.
