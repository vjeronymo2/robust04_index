Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, February 28 (XINHUA) -- Bosnian Muslim leader Alija Izetbegovic today expressed opposition to the deployment of Russian peacekeepers in Sarajevo, terming it as "a provocation that might lead to conflict." "We oppose the dispatch of Russian troops to Bosnia,...the decision should be canceled," he said. The presence of Russian troops in Sarajevo demonstrated Russian support for the Bosnian Serbs, Izetbegovic said in a letter to Yasushi Akashi, UN special envoy in former Yugoslavia. Meanwhile, Bosnian ambassador to the UN presented a similar letter to the Security Council.
