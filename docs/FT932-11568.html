930504 FT 04 MAY 93 / Report says Germany's GDP to fall 1.5 per cent this year Germany's GDP will fall 1.5 per cent this year and unemployment will rise to 3.5m people, according to the country's five leading economic institutes in a report due out today, writes Ariane Genillard. Unemployment in the west would rise by a further half million to 2.3m and in the east by 80,000 to 1.25m.
