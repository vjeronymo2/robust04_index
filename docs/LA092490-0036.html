September 24, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: OPERA

Designing Sendak: Original paintings by artist Maurice Sendak created for the L.A. Music Center Opera's new production of Mozart's "Idomeneo" will go on exhibit Tuesday at Every Picture Tells a Story . . . , the Los Angeles gallery devoted to original art from children's literature. Sendak, who has created and illustrated more than 30 books, including "Higglety Pigglety Pop," "In the Night Kitchen" and "Where the Wild Things Are," said he has had a lifelong ambition to design the Mozart opera, an epic tale of the Trojan War.

The opera will also premiere Tuesday at the Dorothy Chandler Pavilion. Sendak has created sets for other operas, including works based on "Where the Wild Things Are" and "Higglety Pigglety Pop," which also have been presented by the Music Center Opera. GREG BRAXTON
