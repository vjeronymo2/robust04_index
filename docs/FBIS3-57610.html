Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [ITAR-TASS correspondents Grigoriy Arslanov, Georgiy Shmelev] [Text] Beijing, 28 Jan -- "Our visit is taking place on the eve of the most popular holiday in China -- Chunjie -- the holiday of Spring. This is a good omen for the further development of Chinese- Russian relations," PRC President Jiang Zemin stated at the Zhongnanhai party and political residence today during a meeting with Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, who is here on an official visit. The leader of the PRC said that he was glad about the meeting with the Russian minister and cited a Chinese proverb in this connection: "Meetings with old friends are a source of particular joy." Jiang Zemin recalled that during the visit to Beijing by Russian President Boris Yeltsin in December 1992, a conversation rich in content took place between the heads of the two states. Andrey Kozyrev, for his part, noted that the Chinese and Russian new years are starting with confirmation of a course aimed at goodneighborliness and partnership, the foundations for which were laid during the visit to Beijing by Russian President Boris Yeltsin. Kozyrev handed to Jiang Zemin a personal message from Boris Yeltsin containing an invitation to make an official visit to the Russian Federation.
