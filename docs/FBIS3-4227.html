Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 25 (XINHUA) -- The No. 2 Generating Unit at the Daya Bay Nuclear Power Plant began full capacity trial operation from 19:15 [1115 GMT] Beijing time today. Trial operations at 15, 30, 50, 75, 87 and 97 percent of its designed capacity of 900,000 kilowatts have been conducted since February 7, according to the Shenzhen-based Guangdong Nuclear Power Joint Venture Company. The trial operation, conducted under the supervision of the State Nuclear Safety Bureau, has been a success, meeting all technical and safety norms in all stages, it was announced. The No. 2 Generating Unit will start commercial operation after every planned test proves a success during a period of trial operation at full capacity. The No. 1 Generating Unit started commercial production on February 1 this year. The Daya Bay Nuclear Power Plant, with two generating units, is about 45 kilometers from the Shenzhen Special Economic Zone and 55 kilometers from Hong Kong.
