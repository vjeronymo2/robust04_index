BFN [Excerpt] Khartoum, 30 Mar (SUNA) -- Yesterday, Minister of Transport 'Uthman 'Abd-al-Qadir 'Abd-al-Latif met with a delegation of Iraqi experts assigned to the sea ports corporation. The delegation briefed the minister on what steps have been taken in restoring and developing the loading ramp at Port Sudan harbor and the recommissioning of 22 cranes. [passage omitted]
