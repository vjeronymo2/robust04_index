930430 FT 30 APR 93 / BIFU issues writ against Barclays Bank BIFU, the bank staff union, issued a writ yesterday against Barclays Bank claiming damages on behalf of 400 computer staff suspended without pay in March 1991 for refusing to take a loyalty pledge during an overtime ban. Mr Rob MacGregor, Bifu's assistant general secretary, said: 'It has taken a long time to collate all the evidence but we are now confident of success.' The bank would be 'called to account'.
