920414 FT 14 APR 92 / International Company News: Slide at Alcoa ALCOA, the world's largest producer of aluminium, reported a drop in first-quarter earnings to Dollars 76m, or 89 cents a share, from Dollars 97m, or Dollars 1.14 per share, last year, writes Barbara Durr in Chicago. Revenues were Dollars 2.3bn, down 7 per cent, from Dollars 2.4bn. The company's share price rose Dollars 5.75 to Dollars 73.75, near its high for a year. Analysts had estimated earnings at around 20 cents. The results included Dollars 8m from the sale of investments.
