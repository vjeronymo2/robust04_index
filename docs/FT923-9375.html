920805 FT 05 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Radiant Metal reduces loss Radiant Metal Finishing reduced its losses from Pounds 169,002 to Pounds 94,505 pre-tax for the year to February 28. Turnover was static at Pounds 807,571. Tax credits of Pounds 34,976 (Pounds 26) left a retained loss of Pounds 59,529 (Pounds 168,976).
