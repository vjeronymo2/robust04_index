BFN [Text] The U.S. assistant secretary of state for Asian Pacific affairs, Mr. Winston Lord, will visit Vietnam from 1 to 4 July in a trip focussing on American soldiers missing-in-action [MIA] from the Vietnam War. Mr. Lord will be flanked by a delegation that was personally sent by President Bill Clinton in a bid to speed up the resolution of the MIA issue. The two sides will discuss cooperation between Vietnam and the U.S. on efforts to resolve the MIA question as well as other problems with a view to promoting bilateral relations in the new situation.
