BFN [Text] Senior Army sources state that the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] will not transfer any powers to the Palestinians in the Gaza Strip today. Yesterday about 140 Palestinian policemen entered an installation in Dayr al-Balah, where they remain for the time being. Today several dozen more policemen are slated to enter the Gaza Strip. Our correspondent Karmela Menashe reports that over the next few days more Palestinian policemen will prepare in the area for the transfer of power to their hands.
