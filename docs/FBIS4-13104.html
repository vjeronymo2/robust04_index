BFN [Text] Israeli warplanes staged several sorties over the south this morning, specifically over the al-Nabatiyah and the Iqlim al-Tuffah areas. They staged two mock raids at low altitude and broke the sound barrier. The eastern hills and the outskirts of Kafr Rumman came under heavy machine-gun fire at 0800 today from the Israeli forces' positions in al-Dabshah. In the central sector, the outskirts of the town of Yatar came under intensive machine-gun fire at midnight last night while the sky overhead was lit by flares. In the Marj 'Uyun area, an agent Lahd [South Lebanese Army] unit combed with machine-gun fire the main road that links Marj 'Uyun with the Zumarayya crossing point and that passes through the al-Khiyan junction and the Nab' al-Hasbani. According to information received from the occupied strip, the SLA command interrogated a number of its members following the explosion of a device under one of its patrols at the entrance to Marj 'Uyun three days ago. The explosion killed recruit Faris Ilyas Jabbur from Kawkaba and injured another.
