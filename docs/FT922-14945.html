920403 FT 03 APR 92 / Irish bank strike threat Ireland's four main clearing banks face a possible strike by their 13,000 employees next week, after two banks threatened to sack workers taking industrial action, writes Tim Coone in Dublin. Members of the Irish Bank Officers' Association (IBOA) are seeking a 6.5 per cent pay rise. The banks are offering 6 per cent in return for longer hours.
