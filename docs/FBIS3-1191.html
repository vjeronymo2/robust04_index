Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Police Public Relations Chief Leon Mellet has denounced President de Klerk's handling of accusations that senior officers had resorted to dirty tricks and gun running to undermine South Africa's march to democracy. In a rare public criticism of a state president by a spokesman for the police force, General Mellet said the accusations had demoralized the police force. He said he was speaking on behalf of the vast majority of police generals.
