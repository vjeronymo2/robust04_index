BFN [From the "Union Radio News" newscast] [Text] The General Bank Superintendency is preparing a special data bank to ensure that funds in accounts held by shareholders of intervened banks are not payed out with the funds Fogade [Deposit Guarantee and Banking Protection Fund] will provide to help bank depositors. The superintendency has made this announcement in a communique stating they are working at full force on the computer systems of the 51 companies and institutions that were affected by bank intervention measures so as to honor, as soon as possible, the commitments accountable to the depositors.
