920801 FT 01 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Trilion losses double to Pounds 1.5m Trilion, the USM-quoted television production company, reported pre-tax losses almost doubled at Pounds 1.48m in the six months to March 31, compared with Pounds 788,000. The company blamed the tough market faced by its facilities at the Trocadero in London for the deterioration. Turnover declined to Pounds 7.17m (Pounds 8.15m). Interest costs rose from Pounds 55,000 to Pounds 314,000. Losses per share rose from 1.3p to 2.5p.
