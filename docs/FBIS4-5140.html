BFN [Text] A Myanmar [Burmese] delegation led by Brigadier General Abel, national planning and economic development minister, left Yangon [Rangoon] by Myanmar Airways International this evening to attend the GATT organization's ministerial meeting on the final agreement on Uruguay trade negotiations to be held in Marrakech, Morocco. After attending the meeting, the minister, at the invitation of the Vietnamese Government, will attend the ministerial meeting on regional economic cooperation among Cambodia, China, Laos, Myanmar, Thailand, and Vietnam to be held in Hanoi. The meeting is jointly sponsored by the Asian Development Bank and the National Planning Committee.
