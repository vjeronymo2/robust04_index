Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Simferopol, 23 Feb -- The press service of the president of Crimea circulated a statement saying that the fact that Chairman of Crimea's Council of Ministers Borys Samsonov, who is currently running for the Supreme Council of Ukraine, went on holiday without the consent of his superiors was an act of sabotage. In this connection, the staff of the president of Crimea and President Meshkov himself have no offers to Mr. Samsonov regarding his position in the new Crimean government.
