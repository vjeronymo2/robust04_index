BFN [Text] The deputy chairman of the presidential council and secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party Central Committee, Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, late yesterday afternoon made an inspection tour of the wharves of al-Mukalla port, in the Khalf zone. On arrival at the port, he was received by Brother Awad 'Abdallah al-Murshidi, the deputy of the Governorate of Hadramawt, the director generals of the governorate, port officials, shipping corporation officials, and chamber of commerce members. The brother secretary general was briefed during his visit on the activities at the port, shipping movements, and work going on to unload basic foodstuffs. He also heard a detailed briefing on programs to import commodities like wheat, rice, flour, milk products, and sugar, 33 tons of which arrived during the last two days. He was also briefed on the distribution of these commodities in the Governorates of Hadramawt, Shabwah, and al-Mahrah to ensure stable supplies and improve living standards. He was accompanied on his visit by political bureau member Salim Muhammad Burran; Salih Abu-Bakr Bin Husaynun, minister of oil and mineral wealth; and a number of other officials.
