920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Practical Investment asset value ahead Practical Investment Company had a net asset value of 139.51p per share at May 31, a 9 per cent improvement on the 128.35p value a year earlier. Net revenue for the 12 months amounted to Pounds 1.01m (Pounds 936,441) for earnings per share of 5.07p (4.68p). The second interim dividend is raised to 2.2p (1.965p) and again there is a special dividend of 1.01p. The total payment for the year is 4.31p (3.975p).
