940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / International Company News: Soros group in Polish venture Quantum Emerging Growth Partners has invested 304.8bn zlotys (Dollars 13.5m) in Agros Holding, a Polish food trading company. It is the first large investment in central and eastern Europe by the Quantum group which is managed by Mr George Soros, the financier. Quantum bought the shares with 39 other investors. The investments give Agros, soon to be listed on the Warsaw stock exchange, 780bn zlotys in new funds.
