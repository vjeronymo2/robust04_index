April 5, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MISSILE TEST

I sure hope that all the reader's of the Los Angeles Times enjoyed that spectacular front-page photo (March 22) of the fizzled Trident 2 missile doing cartwheels off the coast of Cape Canaveral.

A magnificent sight to behold as our taxes to the tune of $23.7 million went up in smoke. It's no wonder the folks at the IRS find it hard to deal with the natives in this country on April 15th!

ARTHUR J. BREZAE

Chatsworth
