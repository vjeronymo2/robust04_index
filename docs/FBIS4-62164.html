BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Dmitriy Gorokhov and Konstantin Pribytkov] [Text] Geneva, 13 May (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev has arrived in Geneva, where talks are to be held today by foreign ministers from the Russian Federation, the United States, and five countries of the European Union -- Greece, Belgium, Germany, Great Britain, and France -- on problems of a Bosnian settlement. The meeting aims to work out a common position on the Bosnian problem and to pave the way for the resumption of talks between the conflicting sides in Bosnia-Herzegovina.
