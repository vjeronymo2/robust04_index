Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Croatian Prime Minister Nikica Valentic also received Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko. The Croatian prime minister briefed the Ukrainian foreign minister on the situation in the country, stressing that it was dependent on the war in Bosnia- Herzegovina and the general crisis in this region. Laying emphasis on Croatia's desire for peace, Valentic called on Ukraine -- as a respectable European country -- to help [Croatia] in its search for a way out of the crisis.
