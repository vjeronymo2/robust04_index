BFN [Text] The Attorney General's Office authorized special provincial prosecutors for drug cases in Pucallpa, to participate in the joint antinarcotics operations carried out throughout the country. For this purpose, the prosecutors must coordinate their participation with the Armed Forces joint command, the Peruvian National Police Antinarcotics Division, the DEA, and the U.S. Embassy antinarcotics department. If special prosecutors Juan Malca Perez, Roberto Pesantes Creder, Luis Valdivia Susunaga, and Jorge Requifo Rojas find evidence of crimes, they must inform the provincial prosecutor of the respective jurisdiction so he can adopt the measures established by law.
