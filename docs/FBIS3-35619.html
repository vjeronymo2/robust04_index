Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yefim Shvartsman] [Text] Simferopol, 31 January -- Yuriy Meshkov, chairman of the republican movement and of the Republican Party of Crimea, has been elected Crimea's first president. The Electoral Commission's protocol has just been confirmed at the session of the Central Electoral Commission for elections of the president of Crimea, Yuriy Sakharov, deputy chairman of the commission, reported to the ITAR-TASS correspondent. Of the citizens entered on the electoral register, 75.12 percent took part in the voting, or 1,427,419 people; 72.92 percent of the electorate who took part in the voting, or 1,040,888 people, voted for Yuriy Meshkov; 23.35 percent of the citizens of Crimea who took part in the elections, or 333,243 people, voted for Nikolay Bagrov.
