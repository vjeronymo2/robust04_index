Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Gligorov Talks With Demirel on Phone"] [Text] Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] President Kiro Gligorov, during his yesterday telephone conversation with the President of the Republic of Turkey Suleyman Demirel, discussed the newly emerged situation after Greece closed its border. The Turkish president expressed readiness to help Macedonia in coping with problems resulting from the Greek embargo.
