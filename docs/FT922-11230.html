920427 FT 27 APR 92 / Enterprises set targets for number of women hired BRITISH AIRWAYS, British Rail, IBM, Lucas, Littlewoods, and the National Health Service are among UK enterprises which have started to set targets for the number of women being hired and promoted within their organisations. Interviews conducted by the Financial Times with compa-nies taking part in Opportunity 2000, the campaign designed to help working women launched six months ago by Mr John Major, the prime minister, reveal that more than 60 organisations have signed up to the initiative.
