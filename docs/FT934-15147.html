931013 FT 13 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Currency gain lifts Boxmore INCLUDING an exceptional currency gain of Pounds 323,000, profits of Boxmore International, the USM-quoted packaging and printing group, rose from Pounds 2.05m to Pounds 2.72m pre-tax for the half year to June 30. The 33 per cent improvement came from turnover ahead Pounds 1.2m at Pounds 17m. The interim dividend is lifted to 1.25p (1.15p) from earnings of 9.4p (7.3p). Net cash balances at the period end totalled Pounds 2.73m after investment in new plant and buildings of Pounds 4m. Directors anticipated capital investment of over Pounds 5m in the second half.
