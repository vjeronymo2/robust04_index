Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In the Rajya Sabha, the opposition today expressed concern over the reported decision of the Belgian Government to deport the JKLF [Jammu Kashmir Liberation Front] chief, Amanullah Khan, to Pakistan. Raising the issue during zero hour [time when there is nothing on the agenda], Mr. J.P. Mathur, BJP [Bharatiya Janata Party]; Mr. I.J. Gujral, Janata Dal; and others said Amanullah Khan was arrested in Brussels on a warrant issued by India. They asked the government to clarify whether the Indian case was effectively presented in the Brussels court.
