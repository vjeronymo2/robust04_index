Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] An Amal Movement group used machine guns, rockets, and mortars to attack an Israeli position on Tallat al-Suwayda' shortly before midnight. The Movement's operations room said that fierce clashes ensued between the two sides for 20 minutes, adding that the attackers scored direct hits on the position. The Movement said the Israeli forces bombarded the approaches to Habbush and 'Arab Salim as well as (Jisr al-Sitt Zubaydah).
