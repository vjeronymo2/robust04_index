940901 FT 01 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Geest Geest, the fresh produce and prepared foods company, has bought St Martins-Waltham Abbey from Hazlewood Foods for Pounds 6.5m cash. St Martins-Waltham Abbey manufactures a range of dressed salad products, principally under customers' own labels. The acquisition includes Hazlewood's Greek dip operation, which currently forms part of Forrester Foods. The combined businesses had an after-tax profit of Pounds 8,000 from turnover of Pounds 10.6m for the year to end-March, with assets of Pounds 5.6m.
