921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Offer for Simpsons goes unconditional Baldwin's offer for Simpsons of Cornhill, owner of the famous City chop house, has been declared unconditional. The holidays, printing and property company has received acceptances in respect of 2.82m shares or 62.8 per cent and 450,000 'A' shares or 75 per cent. The Baldwin offer is on the basis of 1-for-2 and values Simpsons at Pounds 1.68m. It had been accepted by the Simpson board and stockbrokers Raphael Zorn Hemsley representing 54 per cent of the capital.
