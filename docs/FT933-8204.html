930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Powell Duffryn buys Powell Duffryn, the Berkshire-based specialist engineer, has purchased Pressure Systems, a US supplier of breathing air equipment, for Dollars 2.7m (Pounds 1.8m) cash. Based in North Carolina, Pressure Systems has an annual turnover of some Dollars 7m. It employs a workforce of 50.
