920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / UK Company News: RHM expands in the Netherlands Ranks Hovis McDougall is acquiring Van der Zande Meelfabriek 'De Waal', a family-owned flour milling business based in Beneden-Leeuwen and Oss Following the acquisition, RHM will have three flour mills in the Netherlands. Completion is expected on September 8.
