910420 FT 20 APR 91 / Andreotti wins vote of confidence THE new Italian government, headed by Mr Giulio Andreotti, yesterday won a vote of confidence in parliament, despite the small Republican party voting against, writes Haig Simonian in Rome. The Republicans, part of the coalition ruling Italy since 1983, had decided to withdraw from Mr Andreotti's new government after a clash with the premier over cabinet posts.
