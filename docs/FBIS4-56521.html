BFN [Report by Luis Alberto Mogollon; from "Caracol's 0600-0900" newscast] [Text] Roads remain blocked in Giraldo Municipality, western Antioquia Department, not allowing Army troops to reinforce the police command that is currently fighting with the guerrillas. A Giraldo Municipality official told Caracol the following: [Begin unidentified municipality official recording] We cannot leave our homes. It all began after midnight, at 0030. We cannot see clearly; we cannot step outside the door but we can see through the windows and through the crack in the door that the main square and the streets are full of guerrillas. We are sure that the highway junction, the road between (Manglara) and Uraba is blocked. [Words indistinct] at the command and the police have been unable to leave. The guerrillas are gathered at the entrance into town. [end recording] The Antioquia Department Government will analyze the public order situation at a Security Council meeting this morning.
