930213 FT 13 FEB 93 / Age Concern issues council-tax papers AGE Concern has published briefing papers on the council tax highlighting points of interest to older people. There are three papers to cover the differences in the tax, which is to be introduced in April in England, Scotland and Wales. The papers include information on the disability reduction scheme, discounts for living alone and the transitional reduction scheme, which is intended to cushion large increases in bills because of the change from the community charge. The Council Tax and Older People Briefing, Age Concern England, 1268 London Road, London SW16 4ER. Free on receipt of 9x4 sae.
