Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, Jan. 7--Polish Defence Minister Piotr Kolodziejczyk Friday said he received a promise that the participation in the "Partnership for Peace" programme would in the end give Poland NATO membership. He said he received such a promise while meeting special envoys of U.S. President Bill Clinton, Ambassador Madeleine Albright and General John Shalikashvili, chairman of the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff, who arrived in Warsaw earlier today. Kolodziejczyk said that during the meeting he was convinced to the "Partnership for Peace" programme adding that there is no alternative solution.
