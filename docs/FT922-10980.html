920428 FT 28 APR 92 / Observer: Tar babies The rundown in UK manufacturing has taken its toll on factory moggies but havens still exist. At the Newcastle works of protective coatings producers Jobling Purser the works cat, Slinker, and her five one-week old kittens are in residence on a batch of bagged bitumen earmarked for Ever Ready's County Durham battery plant. Not to worry. Another batch has been made.
