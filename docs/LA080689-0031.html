August 6, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

WHEN THE SIVER SCREEN TURNS RED: OPINIONS ON MOVIE VIOLENCE;

'BATMAN THE KILLER IS NOT A HERO FOR OUR TIMES...'

Benson's article seems to underestimate the film audience. I don't see any face-painted maniac defacing art to impress a woman. Shooting Presidents is the acceptable norm.

Speaking of women, Ms. Benson's point of view toward violence harks back to the days of June Cleaver.

JOHN INGRAM

Los Angeles
