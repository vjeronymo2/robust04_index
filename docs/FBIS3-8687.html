Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Greece Unexpectedly `Slackens' Embargo"] [Text] Unexpectedly and without any previous announcement, four compositions made up of 79 wagon-cisterns carrying crude oil for Macedonia [FYROM--The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia] arrived at the railway crossing Idomeni on the Macedonian-Greek border, near Gevgelija. Unofficial sources say two more compositions with 50 wagon-cisterns loaded with petrol, were expected to arrived in the early morning hours of today. Namely, the compositions and wagon-cisterns in question were loaded before the blockade, and had been waiting to enter Macedonia for 22 days.
