Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] As the meaningful February holiday approaches, our people's heart running toward the revolutionary holy place where General Kim Chong-il, an ever-victorious and iron-willed brilliant commander, was born is all the more palpitating. The number of observers and visitors who come to see the old home of the secret camp of Mt. Paetu with the heart of boundless thanks to and admiration for Supreme Commander Comrade Kim Chong-il, who defends the destiny of the fatherland and nation, leads the chuche-oriented socialist cause of our own style to the brilliant victory, and invariably leads the van of the revolution by going through violent storms, is increasing day by day. According to the data compiled, the number of observers and visitors who toured the old home of the secret camp of Mt. Paetu, the revolutionary holy place, ran up to approximately 1.7 million by the end of last year since its opening. On the occasion of the auspicious February holiday, the number of observers and visitors touring the old home of the secret camp of Mt. Paektu is sharply increasing.
