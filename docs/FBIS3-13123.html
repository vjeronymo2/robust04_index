Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A military source has stated that at about 0900 today, according to a tip by a citizen, Lebanese Army units found an explosive charge containing six kg of explosives packed like a present and placed near the French Cultural Center in the township of Ghadir in Kasrawan. The units cordoned off the area and a military expert defused the charge in coordination with the Internal Security Forces. It should be recalled that certain people earlier protested the decision to close down the French Cultural Center and move it to another area.
