March 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

SHOW FOCUSES ON REMODELING;

ANAHEIM HOME SHOW FOCUSES ON RESTORATION AND REMODELS

The 14th Home Restoration & Remodeling Show, opening Thursday at Anaheim Stadium, is expected to be the biggest gathering of its type on the West Coast.

Delta Burke, a star of the "Designing Women" television series, will make a guest appearance from 4 to 8 p.m. Friday. She plays an interior designer on the show, but said she's interested in home improvement "because I'm a homeowner too."

The show runs through next Sunday indoors at the stadium, under the principal sponsorship of Glendale Federal Savings & Loan. The hours are 4 to 10 p.m. Thursday and Friday, 10 a.m. to 10 p.m. Saturday and 10 a.m. to 8 p.m. Sunday. Admission is $4.75 for adults, $2.50 for seniors and children under 12. Children under 12 accompanying their parents are admitted free.

Exhibits will feature products ranging from carpeting, cabinets, roofing, plumbing fixtures, spas, tiles and appliances. Representatives from custom cabinetmaking firms, interior design concerns and furniture manufacturers will be on hand to answer questions.
