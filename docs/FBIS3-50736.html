Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Buenos Aires, 5 Jan (NA) -- President Carlos Menem today expressed his support for his Mexican colleague, Carlos Salinas de Gortari, "in the name of the Argentine people" in view of the serious incidents carried out in Mexico by the brand-new Zapatista Army for National Liberation (EZLN). This was reported by Media Secretary Raul Burzaco at the end of today's cabinet meeting in which the implementation of educational and social plans throughout the country and their projections were studied. [passage omitted] Burzaco was charged with reporting the details of the first cabinet meeting of 1994, which was "complementary" to the last cabinet meeting of 1993. [passage omitted] Burzaco reported that just after the end of the cabinet meeting, Menem called Salinas de Gortari "to express my support as a friend, as president, and in the name of the Argentine people in view of the subversive situation that is being experienced by your country."
