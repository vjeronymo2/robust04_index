BFN [Report by Mladen Bosnjak] [Text] The session of the Assembly of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina and the Constitutional Assembly of the Federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina opened at 1200. The first part of the assembly session will be held behind closed doors. Today's agenda for the session of the Assembly of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina envisages a debate on the report by the state delegation of Bosnia-Herzegovina on the Geneva peace negotiations, the election of the reshuffled Government of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina, and the examination and, we believe, adoption of the proposal on the law on ministries and other administrative bodies of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina. The session of the Constitutional Assembly of the Federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina will examine the proposal on the rules of procedure for the work of the Federal (?Assembly), ratify the Government of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina as a federal government, and discuss the proposal on the law on ministries and on [word indistinct]. This is all from today's session of the assemblies of the Republic and Federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina. We shall resume reporting as soon as the session is opened to reporters, that is, for a live relay.
