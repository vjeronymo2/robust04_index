BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow April 2 TASS -- Following President Nursultan Nazarbayev of Kazakhstan, who last week suggested creating a Euro-Asian Union which would include CIS countries which would opt to join it, the Party of Russian Unity and Accord, known under the Russian acronym PRES, on Saturday issued a statement suggesting that a confederative union of Euro-Asian states be established. "Not only the peoples but also many political forces in CIS countries are prepared not just to discuss the idea of creating such a union but also to tackle specific matters concerning its functioning," said the statement signed by PRES leader Sergey Shakhray. The PRES suggests setting up an international working group to elaborate the text of a confederative agreement. The party has stated that it "takes upon itself to provide organisational and financial support for such a group's activities".
