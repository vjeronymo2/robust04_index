Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 4 Jan (MENA)--Italian Foreign Minister Beniamino Andreatta arrived in Cairo this evening on a two-day visit to Egypt at the invitation of Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa. Talks between the Egyptian and Italian foreign ministers are expected to begin tomorrow. They will focus on bilateral relations and international and regional issues of common interest, especially the Middle East peace process, the Somali problem, and the situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina.
