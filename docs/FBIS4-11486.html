BFN [Report by Jairo Gomez from the "Seven O'Clock Newscast"] [Text] There was an unexpected meeting between President Cesar Gaviria and Prosecutor General Gustavo de Greiff at Narino Palace this afternoon. This newscast has learned they shared a peace pipe and the president and prosecutor resolved their differences, which have been aired by the national media. Despite their differences, they sat down to discuss the possibility that 2,000- 7,000 prisoners might go free because their legal situation has yet to be resolved. The president and the prosecutor will meet again next week when there will be a meeting among the legislative, executive, and judicial branches.
