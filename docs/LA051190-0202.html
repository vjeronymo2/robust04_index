May 11, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

DOLEMAN NOW TOP-PAID VIKING

Chris Doleman has signed a contract that makes him the best-paid player in Minnesota Vikings' history and one of the NFL's best-paid defensive linemen, according to agents with knowledge of the agreement.

The agents said Doleman's one-year contract is worth about $1.4 million, the Star Tribune reported today.

The deal might be worth as much as $1.6 million including incentive clauses, the agents said. The agreement also includes money from an out-of-court settlement, the agents said.

Doleman, an All-Pro defensive end, made $300,000 in base salary last season.

If the estimates are correct, Doleman's contract would eclipse those of the highest-paid Vikings, quarterback Wade Wilson and running back Herschel Walker, both of whom will make more than $1 million for the 1990 season.
