BFN [Text] Canberra, April 21 (CTK) -- The Czech Republic will support Australia's effort to become a non-permanent member of the U.N. Security Council [UNSC], Foreign Minister Josef Zieleniec told journalists today. The Czech Republic, currently one of the Security Council non-permanent members, pledged to support some countries when it itself was striving for the post, Zieleniec said after he had met Australian Foreign Minister Gareth Evans. Australia wants to achieve the post in 1996.
