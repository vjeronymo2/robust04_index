BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Fedor Ignatov] [Text] Alma-Ata [Almaty] 29 April TASS -- "We shall march together. There is no other alternative," said Kazakh Defence Minister Sagadat Nurmagambetov here on Friday, commenting on the treaty on military cooperation and some other agreements on military affairs, which were recently signed by the Russian and Kazakh presidents. He made this statement, speaking before servicemen of a guards garrison and the Alma-Ata Higher Army School. Reporting this event in its latest issue, the Kazakhstan SARBAZY (KAZAKH SERVICEMAN) newspaper quotes the words by the defence minister on the future of the armed forces of his republic and the Commonwealth of Independent States. "There was the Soviet Union. There is the Commonwealth of Independent States now," he said, adding that not a single state of the former union can exist independently. "Therefore," the minister stressed, "our president has repeatedly advocated political consolidation and economic integration."
