BFN [Text] According to Defense Ministry press service, Armenia's Armed Forces launched a new large scale offensive on the northern and northeastern sectors of the front at 0600 this morning. The Azerbaijani Army inflicted heavy losses on the enemy in Geranboy [Shaumyanovskiy] and Agdamskiy Rayons and forced them to retreat to their initial positions. Supported by armored cars, the infantry units of the Armenian Army launched an attack on Kirmizikend [Karmiravan] in Terterskiy Rayon. Fighting continues in this region. The desertions from the Armenian Army fighting in Karabakh has assumed a massive dimension. Colonel Grigoryan, commander of one of the military units of the Armenian Army fighting in Geranboy, shot and killed three Armenian soldiers from Gyumri for refusing to fight.
