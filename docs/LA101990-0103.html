October 19, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Stallone Fan Restrained: A woman who had been sending Sylvester Stallone hundreds of threatening and "demonic" letters has been ordered by a Santa Monica Superior Court judge to stay away from the actor or face jail time. For the past 20 months, a woman identified as Elfie Wade has allegedly sent or dropped off more than 200 letters and cards to the actor's home. The star of the "Rocky" and "Rambo" movies did not attend the hearing. CLAUDIA PUIG
