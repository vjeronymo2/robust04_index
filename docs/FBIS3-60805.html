Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 27 Dec (MENA)--Eyewitnesses in the area of the incident noted that three people perpetrated the crime, one of them masked. They were carrying machine guns and pistols, which they used to shoot citizens, terrorizing them when they tried to confront and chase them. Witnesses said the criminals laid an ambush for the tourist bus. One of them threw an explosive device at the bus, shattering the front, back, and side windscreens of the vehicle. But the driver did not stop and sped away from the scene. Eyewitnesses also affirmed that the three criminals fled through a side street leading to the Corniche Road.
