October 6, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

READERS HIT BACK WITH COMPLIMENTS

I just wanted to commend both Chris Dufresne and Chris Baker (not necessarily in that order) and The Times sports department for the excellent and often witty writing in covering the Raiders. No one expects you to blindly write obligatory stories; fans just want to read a balanced truth. And I get that from Chris . . . and Chris.

KEVIN CASH, El Segundo
