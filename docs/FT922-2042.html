920619 FT 19 JUN 92 / International Company News: UK TV stations unveil link plan YORKSHIRE Television and Tyne Tees Television, two northern England companies that won independent television franchises last year, yesterday announced terms of a merger, write Richard Gourlay and David Owen. A recommended offer from Yorkshire values the Tyne Tees share capital at Pounds 30.4m (Dollars 56.24m). Lex, Page 16
