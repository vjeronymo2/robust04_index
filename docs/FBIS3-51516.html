Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Santa Cruz Province today signed the federal fiscal pact. The ceremony was chaired by President Carlos Menem, Governor Nestor Kirchner, and other government officials. Santa Cruz has thus become one more provinces that already has joined the government initiative, which initially was rejected by Santa Cruz officials. President Carlos Menem said Catamarca also will sign the fiscal pact within the next few hours. The president said that perhaps this very afternoon Catamarca will sign the pact. He also expressed his satisfaction over the Santa Cruz decision to join the pact proposed by the national government.
