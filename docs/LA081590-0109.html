August 15, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The Castle Pines (Colo.) Golf Club, host of this week's International golf tournament on the PGA Tour, anticipates adding black members in the near future, its founder, Jack Vickers, said.

A planned boycott of this week's LPGA Big Apple tournament in New Rochelle, N.Y., was averted when the local NAACP said it was satisfied that the host Wykagyl Country Club would speed efforts to recruit blacks.
