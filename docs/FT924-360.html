921224 FT 24 DEC 92 / Finance and the Family: C & G rate cut - At a glance CHELTENHAM & Gloucester is reducing the interest paid on its London Deposit postal account from January 2. In October, C & G promised to guarantee the rate for three months. The new rates represent a drop of about 2 percentage points. They will be 7 per cent gross annual interest on balances on Pounds 5,000-Pounds 9,999; 7.5 per cent on Pounds 10,000-Pounds 24,999 and 8 per cent on sums over Pounds 25,000.
