931201 FT 01 DEC 93 / Budget 93: Their Budget - The single mother Suzana Radovic, 43 Job: art teacher. Head of year at a girls' comprehensive. Her son, aged five, attends a state primary school and is looked after at home by his grandmother. Suzana there is not dependent on state benefit Earns: Pounds 20,000 a year. Lives in Worcester Park, Surrey, with a pounds 58,000 mortgage Politics: in the last election voted Labour Verdict: 'The unemployed are going to be hit very badly. I'm lucky I'm employed. I think it's an unfair budget. I'm 43, so I'll be affected by the increase in retirement age for women being lifted to 65. If I had to work until then I'd be dead.'
