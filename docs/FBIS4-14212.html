BFN [Text] The proposed bill to fortify the Golan Law was defeated in the Knesset plenum by a majority of 57 to 47. Our correspondent Hayim Hecht reports that SHAS [Torah Observing Sephardim], Hayim Ramon, Shmu'el Avital, and Amir Peretz [members of Ramon's list to the Histadrut trade union elections] voted with the coalition.
