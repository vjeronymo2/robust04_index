August 23, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WEEKEND DIGEST: SANTA MONICA;

MEETING ON COLLEGE CENTER

Santa Monica College will hold a community meeting tonight to discuss plans to open a center for the humanities and a children's center at the former Madison School this fall.

The meeting begins at 7 p.m. at the Madison School Auditorium, 1310 11th St.

For more information contact Nathaniel Trives, deputy superintendent of government relations, at (213) 452-9208.
