BFN [Excerpts] Banja Luka, 29 Apr (TANJUG) -- The Republic of Croatia is by all accounts intensively preparing for an imminent attack on the Serb positions in Posavina, the press center of the republic of Srpska army's First Krajina Corps said in a statement today, citing well-informed sources. Croatia's army has recently accumulated units and military equipment in Posavina, on the left bank of the River Sava, mainly in the wider areas of Slavonski Brod and Zupanja. According to the same source, large numbers of Croats and Muslims from Posavina are members of these units, and some new units have also been founded. [passage omitted] The press center notes that Croatian media have recently carried an increasing number of "reports" about the Serbian side's preparations for attacks against the Croatian positions in Posavina. This is a typical form of media preparations: an attempt to condemn the Serbian side in advance before strong Croatian-Muslim attacks begin. [passage omitted]
