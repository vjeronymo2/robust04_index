BFN [Text] More than 11,000 basijis [volunteer forces] of Ilam Province have enrolled at basij resistance centers and bases and are ready to be dispatched to Bosnia-Herzegovina. This follows the guidelines issued by the exalted leader of the Islamic revolution on all-round support for the Muslims of Bosnia-Herzegovina. The commander of the Islamic Revolution Guards for Ilam district said today: If the political obstacles to the dispatching of volunteers are removed, and we dispatch the first group of volunteers to Bosnia-Herzegovina, the number of enrolling volunteers is expected to increase manyfold. He added: The enrollment of volunteers in Ilam Province is still continuing in urban and rural areas and in offices.
