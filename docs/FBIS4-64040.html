BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] Ukraine and Crimea have pledged to resolve the dispute about the degree of autonomy enjoyed by the Crimean peninsula through negotiations. However, according to the statement of Ukraine's deputy prime minister Zhulynskyy, President Kravchuk intends to dissolve the Crimean Parliament in the near future and introduce presidential rule on the territory of the peninsula in the event of the Crimean legislators refusing to reject the new constitution. It is still not clear what place Crimean President Meshkov occupies in the political plans of President Kravchuk. One thing is clear -- at any moment the situation may get out of control.
