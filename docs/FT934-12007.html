931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / International Company News: Forever bicycle group woos foreign investors SHARES in the manufacturer of one of the China's most famous products, the 'Forever' bicycle, are being offered to foreign investors, writes Tony Walker in Beijing. Sixty million 'B' shares - those denominated in US dollars - in the Shanghai Yongjiu Bicycle Co. Ltd. were made available yesterday through a local brokerage house and foreign underwriters. The company plans to raise UDollars 20m from the share issue to upgrade its plant to enhance export potential. The 'Forever' bicycle company is the 15th to issue B shares on the Shanghai market. Among foreign underwriters are Goldman Sachs, Wardley, and Credit Lyonnais. Par value of the shares is 35 cents. The Shanghai company has produced 60m bicycles since its founding in 1940. Annual production now exceeds 3m.
