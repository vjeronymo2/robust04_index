931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / Change to Europe time urged PUTTING THE clocks forward one hour in summer and winter, to bring Britain in line with much of continental Europe, would reduce road deaths and save more than Pounds 200m in health service costs, says an independent report published yesterday. The author of the report, Dr Mayer Hillman of the Policy Studies Institute, says business would benefit from the change. Tourism could be Pounds 1bn better off and travel and communications would be improved.
