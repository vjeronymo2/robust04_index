June 29, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

MAN VS. MICROCHIP

The losing streak continued when one half of my exacta bet, the entry of Flirty and Past Remembered ran 1-2 but I was unable to connect the second half and my other exacta horse, Belara, was scratched.

About the only good thing was that due to a typographical error, a $20 exacta came out a $2 exacta, limiting my losses to $12.

In an effort to right my ship tonight I'll bet $20 exactas from Hazlemere to Secret Angel and Atrevida and $10 exactas from Secret Angel and Atrevida to Hazlemere in the third race.
