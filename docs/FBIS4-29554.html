BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 17 (KCNA) -- Secretary of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] Hwang Chang-yop today met and conversed in a friendly atmosphere with a delegation of the Japan Liberal Democratic Party [LDP] headed by Taku Yamasaki, deputy secretary general of the party and member of the house of representatives, at the Mansudae Assembly Hall. Present there was vice department director of the WPK Central Committee Kim Yang-kon.
