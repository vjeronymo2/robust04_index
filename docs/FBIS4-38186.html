BFN [Text] Baghdad, 28 Jun (INA) -- Trade Minister Dr. Muhammad Mahdi Salih has met with Ahmad Shakir, head of the society for promoting Arab-French relations, and his accompanying delegation. During the meeting, the minister reviewed the repercussions of the continued criminal, unfair economic blockade imposed on the Iraqi people in violation of international and humanitarian laws and norms, as well as the Iraqi people's suffering due to the severe shortage of food and medications. The French delegation expressed its appreciation for the construction achieved in Iraq, which was based on its own capabilities despite the blockade imposed on it. The delegation also said that it hoped the blockade will be lifted as soon as possible and that French-Iraqi relations will return to normal in order to serve the interests of both countries.
