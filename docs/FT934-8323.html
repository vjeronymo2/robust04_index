931116 FT 16 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Graystone offer unconditional The recommended offer by Graystone, made via Chemical Investment Bank, for British Syphon Industries, has become unconditional as to acceptances. As at November 11 valid acceptances had been received in respect of a total of 32.07m shares, representing 94.55 per cent. The offer will remain open for further acceptances until 3pm on December 6.
