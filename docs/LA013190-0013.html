January 31, 1990, Wednesday, Southland Edition

WESTERNERS TO ADVISE E. EUROPE EDITORS

East European editors, long used to being told what to write by Communist governments, will get advice on how to break old news habits at a conference with Western media heads in Paris next month.

The U.N. Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization said in a statement Tuesday that it had arranged the Feb. 27-28 meeting at the request of "several international media organizations."

The agency said media executives from 15 Western countries, including the United States, are being invited to the conference.
