921013 FT 13 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Reorganisation hits Slingsby After Pounds 78,000 exceptional reorganisations costs, HC Slingsby, maker of trucks and ladders, has seen its pre-tax profit slide from Pounds 82,000 to Pounds 28,000 in the first half of 1992. Turnover was Pounds 5.16m (Pounds 5.13m). Earnings per share came to 1.4p (4.9p) and the interim dividend is again 2p.
