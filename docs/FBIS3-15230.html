Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 1/3/1994 -- The poll "What People Advise" that will be run on March 6th, is a political event of major importance "for the present and future of the Republic of Moldova", stated today President Snegur at a session with factors responsible for its running, a presidential communique is reporting. The president noted that "the people is the most firm arbiter, able to discern the good and bad, as the results of elections have proved." Snegur asserted that the poll will directly influence the social-political stability in the country, being a solid base for the new Parliament which will discuss the draft Moldovan Constitution, it is said in the release.
