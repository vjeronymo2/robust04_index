931209 FT 09 DEC 93 / World News in Brief: MPs back ITV shake-up MPs early today voted 144 to 109 to approve a government order giving the go-ahead for a shake-up in commercial television announced last week by heritage secretary Peter Brooke. The order will allow two merger proposals to proceed. Carlton Communications, the London broadcaster, has agreed a merger with Central, the Midlands company, and Granada, which covers the north-west, has made a Pounds 600m hostile bid for London Weekend Television.
