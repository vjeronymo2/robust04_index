BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 22 -- The Vietnamese high-ranking military delegation led by Gen. Doan Khue, politburo member of the Communist Party of Vietnam Central Committee (CPVCC) yesterday left Pyongyang concluding a week-long official friendship visit to the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (DPRK). While there, the Vietnamese military delegation was received by President of the DPRK and General Secretary of the Korean Workers' Party Central Committee (KWPCC) Kim Il Sung. It also had talks with a DPRK high-ranking military delegation. The Vietnamese delegation also toured some units and colleges of the People's Armed Forces of the DPRK and a number of socio-economic establishments and scenery in Pyongyang and other localities.
