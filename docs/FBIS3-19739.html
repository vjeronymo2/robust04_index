Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Mar 21 (AA) -- Interior Minister Nahit Mentese said on Monday [21 March] that cooperation between Turkey and Iran against terrorists will continue and improve. "We are sure that cooperation between Turkey and Iran that was set up to struggle against every kind of terrorism will improve," Mentese said. Mentese sent a message and thanked Iranian Interior Minister 'Ali Mohammad Besharati-Jahromi for his country's turning over 14 terrorists from the outlawed separatist terrorist organization PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan]. The terrorists fled to Iran during an operation launched in eastern Agri and Igdir Provinces. Mentese said that this gesture is a concrete result of cooperation and friendship ties between Turkey and Iran. Mentese also said that Turkey thanked Besharati for his individual efforts.
