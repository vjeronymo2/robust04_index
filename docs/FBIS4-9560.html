BFN [Text] The Presidential Council of so-called Herceg-Bosna in Mostar has decided to relieve General Ante Roso of his duties as chief of General Staff of the Croatian Defense Council [HVO]. Roso has been appointed to a military post in the joint command of the HVO and the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army. Major-General Milivoj Petkovic has been appointed chief of the HVO General Staff.
