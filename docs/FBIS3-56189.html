Language: Estonian Article Type:BFN [Text] The parliament foreign commission has proposed to the government to signal readiness for participation in the Partnership for Peace program and to sign a basic document to this end. The parliament foreign commission also recommends that the government should present to NATO Estonian proposals to draw up an Estonian-NATO partnership program while also consulting relevant parliament commissions. The parliament foreign commission took this decision after it had familiarized itself with the documents of the NATO heads of state and government leaders' conference. As is known the meeting took place in Brussels on 10 and 11 January.
