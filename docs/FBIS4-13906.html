BFN [Text] Cairo, 24 Apr (MENA)--Dr. Nabil Sha'th, adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and head of the Palestinian delegation to the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations, today held a closed meeting with General Amnon Shahaq, Israeli deputy chief of staff. The meeting lasted for one and a half hours. Knowledgeable Palestinian sources have stated that the talks dealt with the outcome of the previous rounds of talks. Sha'th and Shahaq reviewed the topics that will be discussed by the Security and Legal Affairs Committees during the current round of talks, the sources said. The sources added that the Sha'th-Shahaq meeting aimed at eliminating any obstacles that might face the negotiations. The meeting also aimed at reaching a final agreement on implementing the Gaza-Jericho accord by the end of this week, the sources added.
