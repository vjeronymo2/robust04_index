Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 10 Jan (TANJUG) -- The information service of the Yugoslav Army's Third Army [as received] has said in a statement that at 1350 today a new border incident took place near the Morina border watch-tower on the border with Albania. The incident was caused by the Albanian side. Yugoslav Army border guards killed an Albanian citizen, who had tried illegally to cross the border and had refused to obey their orders, on FRY territory. Competent investigating officers have been sent to the scene of the killing in order to establish all the circumstances relating to the killing of the person who tried illegally to cross the border, the statement concludes.
