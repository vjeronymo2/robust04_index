930731 FT 31 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Radiant Metal loss deepens to Pounds 112,784 Increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 112,784 were announced by Radiant Metal Finishing, the electroplating, metal finishing and property group, for the year to February 28. Losses last time were Pounds 94,505. Turnover slipped to Pounds 748,299 against Pounds 807,571. Trading losses were Pounds 128,551 (Pounds 94,121) while investment income amounted to Pounds 15,767 (Pounds 384 payable).
