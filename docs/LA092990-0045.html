September 29, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

SUNSET LEAGUE;

KARLINER RUNS, PASSES OCEAN VIEW TO 30-0 VICTORY

Randy Karliner threw two touchdown passes and ran for one to lead Ocean View over Fountain Valley, 30-0, in Friday's Sunset League opener at Sheue Field.

Karliner completed a 26-yard scoring pass to Jeff Bush with 8:45 remaining in the first quarter and a 28-yard touchdown pass to Dennis Eichen in the final four seconds of the second quarter.

He also scored on an eight-yard run outside right tackle in the final 3:25 of the first quarter.

Ocean View Coach Howard Isom said this was Karliner's best game. "He has started to settle down," Isom said. "He is not as pressed as he was earlier in the season."

Karliner completed 19 passes for 188 yards. He also gained 59 of Ocean View's 134 rushing yards on 10 carries.

Ocean View (1-0, 4-0) dominated the game from the opening kickoff. At halftime, the Seahawks had netted 303 yards while their defense held Fountain Valley (0-1, 0-4) to a net total of 95 yards on 14 plays.

"Our defense rose to the occasion. It was our best defensive effort this year," Isom said.

Leading 14-0, Ocean View lined up in punt formation with 9:20 left in the second quarter. Albert Fanoga received the snap and promptly completed a 58-yard scoring pass to Eichen.

After the ensuing kickoff, which went in the end zone, Fountain Valley marched from its 20 to the Ocean View 12 on nine plays. Two costly penalties, the latter called for an illegal forward pass by Marcos Diaz that ended the Barons' possession, stopped the drive. BOB BORSUM
