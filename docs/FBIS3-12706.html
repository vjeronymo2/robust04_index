Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council and secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, met this afternoon with the brother commanders of the military units and security agencies and the commanders of military police in the commercial and economic capital, Aden. He made a speech in which he expressed eagerness to meet with them 11 days after the accord document was finally signed. He expressed thanks and appreciation for the efforts made by the Jordanian and Omani brothers, the Arab League secretary general, and all the friends in the European Community and the United States for helping our country to overcome the challenges that faced the march of unity and democracy and the building of a modern Yemeni state. The vice chairman of the Presidential Council asserted that by signing the document, the Yemeni people have proven they can rely on dialogue and reason. He urged all sincere members of the dialogue committee, as well as those not on the committee, to adhere to the accord document and work toward implementing it.
