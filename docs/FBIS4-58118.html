BFN [Text] Cairo, 30 Apr (MENA)--President Muhammad Husni Mubarak this morning received Justin Mugenzi, Rwanda's minister of trade, industry, and handicrafts and president of the Liberal Party. Minister Mugenzi briefed President Mubarak on developments in his country. President Mubarak, who is the current OAU chairman, is exerting efforts to resolve the current crisis in Rwanda. The Rwandan ambassador to Cairo attended the meeting.
