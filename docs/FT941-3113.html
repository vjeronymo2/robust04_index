940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / UK Company News In Brief: Hewitt Group HEWITT GROUP will receive an investment subsidy of DM550,000 (Pounds 216,000) from Treuhandanstalt, which also agreed to waive accrued interest of DM350,000 on the purchase consideration as part of claims made under a 1991 agreement to buy assets from Eisenberger Ton-und Brennhilfsmittelgesellchaft for DM2.5m.
