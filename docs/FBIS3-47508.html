Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 12 (KCNA) -- On the Advantages of Socialism in Our Country, a collection of works of the great leader President Kim Il-song, was published by the Workers' Party of Korea publishing house. Carried in the book in full text or summary are 18 famous works of President Kim Il-song including his highly important reports and speeches on bringing the advantages of man-centred socialism of Korea into full play, which were published in the period from April 1955 to April 1992. The book reflects profound ideas on the characteristics and essential advantages of socialism in Korea, and the source of the might of the socialist system and its invincibility. Comprehensively consummated in the book are concrete tasks and ways for bringing the advantages of socialism in Korea into full play and outstanding ideas and theories, line and policies giving answers to the theoretical and practical questions.
