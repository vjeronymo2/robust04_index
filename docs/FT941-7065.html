940226 FT 26 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Shake-up for top management at Burton Burton, the fashion retailing group, is shaking up its management by appointing chief executives to oversee the strategic planning of its retail multiples. Mr Stuart Rose, who took over as managing director of Dorothy Perkins last year, has been appointed to the new position of chief executive of Burtons, Dorothy Perkins, Evans and IS. Mr Terry Green, Debenhams chief executive, is additionally becoming chief executive of Top Shop and Top Man. Mr Martin McNamee and Mr Nick Hollingworth remain in their positions.
