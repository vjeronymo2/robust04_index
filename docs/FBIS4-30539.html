BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 28 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received a gift from the delegation of the Czechy-Morava Communist Party on a visit to Korea. The gift was handed to an official concerned by the head of the delegation, Vratislav Novak, secretary of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Czechy-Morava Communist Party.
