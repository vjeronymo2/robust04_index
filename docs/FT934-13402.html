931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / Czech budget deficit to total Dollars 640m THE Czech Republic's budget for 1994 will show a deficit of Kcs18.3bn (Dollars 640m), Mr Tomas Jezek, head of the National Property Fund which oversees privatisations, said yesterday, writes Patrick Blum from Prague. The deficit, which would be caused by debts incurred under the communist regime, would be covered by privatisation revenues, the Czech news agency reported.
