930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / Plans for Pounds 50m quarry approved WESTERN Isles Council yesterday approved plans for a Pounds 50m coastal quarry at Lingarabay, South Harris. Redlands Aggregates wants to quarry 600m tonnes of rock for the construction and road-building industry over 60 years creating one of the world's biggest man-made holes, which will eventually become a sea loch. The plan will now be considered by Mr Ian Lang, the Scottish secretary.
