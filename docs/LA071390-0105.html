July 13, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: DANCE

Bolshoi Announces Casting: Nina Ananiashvili, Aleksei Fadeyechev, Nina Semizorova, Ludmilla Semenyaka, Gediminas Taranda and Aleksandr Vetrov will perform during the Bolshoi Ballet's engagement at the Shrine Auditorium Aug. 7-19, company officials said. Irek Mukhamedov, who was tentatively scheduled to appear with the company during its U.S. tour, will remain in Great Britain and close to his expectant wife, Martha. GREG BRAXTON
