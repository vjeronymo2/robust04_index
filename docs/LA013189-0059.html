January 31, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The World Boxing Council said it would suspend the promoter of an International Boxing Federation title fight involving a South African fighter and also will sever ties between the WBC and West Berlin, where the fight was held.

West German Graciano Rocchigiani defended his IBF super middleweight title against Thulane Malinga of South Africa Saturday in a bout promoted by Wilfred Sauerand of Switzerland.
