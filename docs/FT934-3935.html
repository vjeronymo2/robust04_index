931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / Computer viruses 'cost Pounds 50,000' each The cost of tracking down and eliminating a single computer virus is about Pounds 50,000 for a typical company, the National Computing Centre said. The centre today launches a Pounds 60,000 survey in conjunction with the Department of Trade and Industry and the computer company ICL to reassess the cost of security breaches and identify the causes of incidents.
