CSO [Summary] At the end of 1993, Yunnan Province had 442,300 individual businesses, employing 646,600 people, and funds totaled 1.784 billion yuan, increases of 18.7 percent, 19.8 percent, and 33.5 percent respectively over 1992. There were 1,563 private enterprises, employing 33,900 people, and a registered capital of 359 million yuan, increases of 49.9 percent, 44.7 percent and 119.1 percent respectively over 1992.
