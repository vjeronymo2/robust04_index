Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Summary] Beijing, 24 Dec (XINHUA) -- The 10-volume book Selected Pieces of Mao Zedong's Handwriting [mao ze dong shou shu xuan ji 3029 3419 2639 2087 2579 6693 7162], which contains over 2,000 pieces of Mao Zedong's own handwriting, was published by the Beijing Publishing House to commemorate the centennial of Mao Zedong's birth, and will be distributed in China and abroad. General Secretary Jiang Zemin wrote the book's title and Comrade Bo Yibo congratulated its publication.
