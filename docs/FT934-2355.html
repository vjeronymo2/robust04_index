931214 FT 14 DEC 93 / International Capital Markets: RBC Dominion profits up 39% to CDollars 101.5m RBC Dominion Securities, Canada's biggest securities firm, which is 74 per cent owned by the Royal Bank of Canada, posted a record profit of CDollars 101.5m for the year to September. Profit jumped 39 per cent and revenues 18.6 per cent to CDollars 820m. Return on equity reached 40 per cent and total assets at year-end CDollars 14bn, up from CDollars 8.9bn last year. Trading commissions rose 41 per cent and investment banking revenues 29 per cent. RBC managed or co-managed CDollars 27.6bn of new issues during the year, up from CDollars 20.5bn in 1991-92. The company warns that the favourable environment is not likely to continue. 'Often periods of intense prosperity in financial markets come to an abrupt end for reasons completely unforeseen even by the most experienced observers.'
