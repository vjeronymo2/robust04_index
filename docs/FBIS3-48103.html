Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 17 (KCNA) -- A large number of literary and art works praising the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, supreme commander of the Korean People's Army [KPA], have been created and published by officers and men of the KPA. More than 700 poems, songs and novels were created and published last year alone. The works emphatically sing of the great honor and happiness of the soldiers of the Korean People's Army who are advancing along an ever-victorious road under the leadership of Comrade Kim Chong-il, an iron-willed brilliant commander possessed of extraordinary military intelligence and great commandership, and of their steadfast faith and fiery pledge to remain unfailingly loyal to comrade supreme commander, wholly entrusting their destiny and future to him. A lengthy epic "Our Supreme Commander" tells about the immortal feats performed by Comrade Kim Chong-il last year in dealing consecutive blows at the anti-DPRK, anti-socialist moves of the enemies and defending the destiny of the country, the destiny of socialism by wisely leading the whole party, alw the country and the entire people with his iron will, matchless grit and rare leadership ability. A lyrical epic "The Eternal Sun of Life", a lyric "Orders of Love", a song "We Feel Warm", a short story "Benevolence" and other works show the noble virtues of comrade supreme commander who always takes warm, paternal care of the soldiers of the People's Army. A long poem "We Will Win Loudly Shouting Long Live General Kim Chong-il", a lyric "Under the Leadership of Supreme Commander" and other works reflect the determination of the People's Army soldiers to consolidate rock-firm the singlehearted unity of the whole Army around comrade supreme commander and creditably guarantee the cause of the party, the cause of socialism with arms.
