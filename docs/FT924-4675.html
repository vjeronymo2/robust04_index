921201 FT 01 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Crown Eyeglass up 13% and sees more Crown Eyeglass, the USM-quoted spectacles manufacturer and distributor, advanced 13 per cent to pre-tax profits of Pounds 274,000 in the half year to September 27, against Pounds 242,000 last time. The company said the improvement had continued in the second half. Turnover was lower at Pounds 1.78m (Pounds 1.91m). Earnings per share were 11.6p (10.2p). The interim dividend has been increased from 2p to 2.5p.
