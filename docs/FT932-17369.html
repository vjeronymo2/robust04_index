930401 FT 01 APR 93 / People: Non-executive directors Johnson Group Cleaners, the biggest dry cleaners in the UK and the US, has added a Johnson to its list of non-executive directors. However, Peter Johnson (above), 60, is not a member of one of the firm's founding families, but the retiring chairman of Unilever's Brooke Bond Foods business. Johnson, who was found through a firm of headhunters, is filling a board vacancy that has existed since the death of Arthur Winspear. Peter Johnson started his career as a young grocery salesman with Oxo in the 1950s and was managing director of Brooke Bond Oxo when it was acquired by Unilever in 1984. He retires from Brooke Bond Foods at the end of the month and has already been appointed a non-executive director of Nurdin & Peacock, the wholesale cash and carry firm.
