September 30, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

HEADLINE FAILED MORE THAN THE BRUINS DID

The sports headline, "UCLA Fails," is a disservice to players and coaches of UCLA. I wish to congratulate the team for one of the most exciting games in memory. Perhaps for some only winning counts, but the American tradition of sports is not whether you win or lose but how you played the game.

JACK PARRY, Huntington Beach
