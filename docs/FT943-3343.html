940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / International Company News: GM Hughes Electronics to shed 4,400 jobs General Motors' GM Hughes Electronics unit says the consolidation of its defence-related business will mean the loss of about 4,400 jobs by 1995, and the closure of its facilities in Fullerton, California, writes Louise Kehoe in San Francisco. GM Hughes, which announced in March it would combine its electronics and defence operations in a newly-formed Hughes Aerospace and Electronics subsidiary, said the cuts were essential. 'Today, the Department of Defense and our foreign customers are even more cost-driven and Hughes must change to meet those new priorities,' said Mr Michael Armstrong, chairman and chief executive.
