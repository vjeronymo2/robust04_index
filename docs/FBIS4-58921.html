BFN [Text] Cairo, May 9 (MENA)--Electricity ministers from Egypt, Jordan, Syria and Turkey as well as a representative from the Iraqi electricity minister today signed the executive protocol of the Arab Mashreq Power Grid project. The signing came at the end of ministerial meetings of the five countries, which opened yesterday under the chairmanship of Egyptian Minister of Electricity Mahir Abazah. Under the protocol, the signatories will ask the Arab Fund for Economic and Social Development for funds to finance the project. Abazah said feasibility studies have favored the Arab Mashreq Power Grid. The upcoming session of the electricity ministers will be held in Ankara in April 1995.
