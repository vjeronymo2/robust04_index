940806 FT 06 AUG 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Renewed domestic and foreign demand took Johannesburg higher in response to the more positive trend in bullion. The overall index added 39 to 5,728, industrials gained 51 to 6,529 and golds were 25 ahead at 2,111. Sasol gained R1.90 or 6.5 per cent to R31.25.
