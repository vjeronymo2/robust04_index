August 9, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Woolworth Corp.: The New York-based retailer reported second-quarter net income of $50 million, up 4% from the same period last year, on revenue of $2.19 billion, an increase of 10.5%. The company said improvements in European, domestic Woolworth and domestic Kinney operations offset profit declines from increased promotional activity in some specialty chains and the Canadian and Australian operations.
