December 9, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

KILLING OF HOMELESS MAN

Your caption "Fatal Shooting in Altadena" (Metro, Nov. 14) should have read "Three Deputy Sheriffs Pass Death Penalty on Transient With Crowbar."

Well, maybe that is a little too harsh. How about "Deputy Sheriffs Help Cure Problem of the Homeless."

Is murder really necessary in this situation?

BRENT STEINER

Long Beach
