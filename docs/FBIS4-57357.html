BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 16 May (NA) -- The Economy Ministry press office has reported that Economy Minister Domingo Cavallo announced in the ROK on 16 May that the launch of the Mercosur [Common Market of the South] free trade zone will be postponed for three months. The press office issued a communique to inform the public about Cavallo's agenda in that Asian country. It states that the head of the Economy Ministry "asserted that the Mercosur will function like a free trade zone as of 1 April 1995 and that a customs union will be established later." The free trade zone between Argentina, Brazil, Paraguay, and Uruguay was scheduled to come into effect on 1 January 1995.
