BFN [Text] Washington, 23 Jun (PL) -- The U.S. Federal Government and the Florida State Government will implement a program intended to control the massive flow of refugees to the coasts of this southern U.S. state. U.S. Attorney General Janet Reno made this announcement today. She did not provide the details of this operation, called Short Distance, however. She said the operation is still being prepared. Reno pointed out that details of the operation are being coordinated with Florida Governor Lawton Chiles and that the operation will be implemented promptly with the support of the Pentagon and other government organizations. According to its designers in the Justice Department, the Short Distance operation is intended to help the coastal states, especially Florida, control the entrance of refugees coming mainly from Haiti and Cuba. According to reports, concentration centers will be set up and the Army and other government agencies will provide provisions, while asylum applications are being processed.
