February 27, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Supernumeraries, ballet's equivalent to television's extras, are being sought for American Ballet Theatre's "Romeo and Juliet" at the Orange County Performing Arts Center in March.

ABT is seeking men from 5-feet-8 to 6-feet-2, women from 5-feet-6 to 5-feet-8, and boys, 9 to 12 years old, from 4-feet-6 to 4-feet-8. Auditions will be held Monday at the Center. Reservations must be made: (714) 556-2122, Ext. 558 for women and 557 for men and boys.
