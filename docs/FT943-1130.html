940926 FT 26 SEP 94 / Arts (Openings): Barbican London Riding high on the turbulent seas of London's orchestral life, the London Symphony Orchestra celebrates its 90th birthday in fine fettle this week. A gala concert on Thursday to raise money for its Endowment Fund brings together three conductors who are especially close to the orchestra, Michael Tilson Thomas, Colin Davis and Mtsislav Rostropovich. Its big Mahler festival under Tilson Thomas also continues with the Second Symphony on Tuesday.
