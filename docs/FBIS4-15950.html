BFN [ITAR-TASS report: "Swelling Ranks of Unemployed"] [Text] In 1994 the majority of graduates from grades nine to 11 in general education schools and from higher educational establishments in Russia will swell the ranks of the unemployed. This was stated by Fedoro Prokopov, leader of the Federal Employment Service, addressing the second session of the Russian Academy of Social Sciences. According to forecasts of specialists from the Federal Employment Service and the Russian Federation Ministry of Education, in the fall of 1994, 0.5 million school graduates and about 100,000 young people with higher education will be thrown onto the open labor market. In all, about 700,000 people will be left with no chance of finding work in the sphere of the traditional economy.
