Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Russian Foreign Affairs Minister Andrey Kozyrev has sent a message addressed to Foreign Affairs Minister Velayati expressing regret over the crash of a C-130 aircraft belonging to the Islamic Republic of Iran that caused the death of all on board. He expressed his and his government's sympathy concerning the incident. The message also offers condolences to the relatives of the victims of the crash, which included families of some of the diplomats in the Islamic Republic of Iran's Embassy in Moscow.
