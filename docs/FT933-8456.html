930816 FT 16 AUG 93 / Gunmen kill 16 in India Gunmen killed 16 bus passengers on the Jammu-Kishtwar road in northern India at the weekend, writes Shiraz Sidhva from Srinagar. Witnesses said the gunmen asked Moslems to get off the bus before spraying bullets into the 16 remaining Hindu passengers, killing 14 immediately.
