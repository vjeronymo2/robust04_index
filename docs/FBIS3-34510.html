Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At the Royal Hashemite Court today, His Majesty King Husayn received (Yusashi Awadi), adviser to the Japanese foreign minister, who is currently visiting Jordan. His Highness Crown Prince Hasan attended the meeting. His Majesty King Husayn awarded the Japanese guest the al-Kawkab Medal of the First Class. The meeting was also attended by the Japanese ambassador in Amman.
