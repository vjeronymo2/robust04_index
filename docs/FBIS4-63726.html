BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko, Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev, 25 May -- ITAR-TASS has discovered from reliable sources that the chairman of Ukraine's Supreme Council will be making a working visit to the Republic of Crimea. He plans to hold a series of meetings with leaders of the autonomous republic and with representatives of public organizations and movements with an interest in resolving the current problems and settling the situation on the Crimean peninsula.
