BFN [Text] Santo Domingo, 19 May (NOTIMEX) -- Tonight, Jose Francisco Pena Gomez, presidential candidate for the Dominican Revolutionary Party, PRD, demanded the annulment of the elections held on Monday and the holding of new elections because of alleged election fraud. Pena Gomez made the demand before a group of diplomats and foreign journalists, who arrived in the Dominican Republic to cover the election process. The Social Democratic candidate said the annulment of the elections is the only way to prevent the Dominican masses from trying to seek a solution to the crisis by themselves.
