Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] In Vientiane recently, Mrs. Khampheng Phonsena, deputy chairman of the Lao State Commission for Planning and Cooperation, and Mr. Tran Khai, deputy chairman of the Vietnam's State Commission for Planning, signed a document on communications cooperation between the two countries. According to this document, the two sides will contribute funds for the construction of Road No. 8 and the Nam Thom Bridge on Lao National Route No. 1, and to repair Roads No. 9, 12, 18, 42, and the Road No. 7 that connects Laos with the East Sea. Vietnam continues to create favorable conditions for Laos to use the sea ports of Haiphong, Da Nang, and Cua Lo.
