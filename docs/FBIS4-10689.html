BFN [Text] On 15 April, the Electric Union of the Ministry of Basic Industry announced that the electric power deficit being experienced nationwide will continue on into next week, and that outages will continue to be extensive. The nation's power generation capability is 3,132 megawatts, but the fact that the generator units of certain plants have not received maintenance or have broken down has resulted in a significant drop in the output of energy. Although 1,325 megawatts were provided this week, there is a marked difference between the nation's supply of electricity and its demand. This has resulted in extensive outages between 0500 and 0000. Next week's deficit is estimated at 680 megawatts; therefore, the extensive outages will continue.
