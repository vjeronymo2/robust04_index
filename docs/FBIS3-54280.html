Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 22, IRNA -- Iran's Ambassador to Morocco Ja'far Shamsiyan in a meeting with Moroccan National Education Minister Mohamed Gnidirine called for expansion of ties in all fields. During the meeting held in Rabat on Friday, the two sides also called for promotion of scientific, educational and research cooperation as well as the resumption of Persian language courses in Morocco. They also underlined exchange of university professors and students.
