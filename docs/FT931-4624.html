930310 FT 10 MAR 93 / UK Company News in Brief AMBERLEY GROUP: Mr Mark Pye has sold 965,400 shares, which reduces his holding from 17.97 per cent to 2.98 per cent. He has also resigned as managing director. Purchasers of the shares were, at 27p each, Hemery Nominees (791,325), Mr Brian Meddings (100,000) and Mr Robert Healy (74,075). Hemery Nominees now holds 1.76m shares, or 27.28 per cent of the equity.
