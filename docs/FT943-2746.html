940917 FT 17 SEP 94 / International Company News: Preussag acquisition Preussag, the diversified industrial group, is buying from Metallgesellschaft its 64.88 per cent share in Lehnkering Montan Transport for about DM150m (Dollars 62.5m), Mr Michael Frenzel, management board chairman said, Reuter reports from Hanover. The price was calculated according to the market value.
