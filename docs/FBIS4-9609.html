BFN [Text] Konin, 29 Apr--On 29 April, Zdzislaw Stasiak, spokesman for the National Strike Committee of the Brown Coal Miners, told listeners of local radio station "66" in Konin that at 0620 the committee decided to suspend coal supplies to Belchatow, Konin, Patnow, and Adamow power stations. The spokesman explained that this was the result of prolonged negotiations with the government. According to Stasiak, the suspension of coal supplies also includes the so-called strategic reserves. On Friday, the Turow brown coal mine in Turoszow suspended coal supplies for the local Turow power station. The strike committee at the mine told a PAP journalist that output of its power units would gradually decrease during the day.
