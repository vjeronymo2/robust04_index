930923 FT 23 SEP 93 / Conference to raise aid for Israeli-PLO accord set for October An international conference aimed at raising Dollars 3bn (Pounds 1.97bn) to support the Israeli-PLO peace accord has been set for October 1 at the State Department, US officials said yesterday, Reuter reports from Washington. Foreign and finance ministers from Europe, Japan, Canada, the Nordic countries, Saudi Arabia, Tunisia, Syria, Lebanon and Jordan have been invited, along with the Israelis and Palestinians, State Department spokesman Michael McCurry said.
