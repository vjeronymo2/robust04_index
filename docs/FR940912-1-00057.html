§403.140 Special Accounts or Revolving Funds. Revenues and fees are to credited to special accounts or revolving funds in accordance with the table below.

␣ 1Revenues and fees to be credited 1Account or fund User fees (16 U.S.C. 460l.6a) and concession or franchise fees Recreation Account. Revenues specific to the Central Arizona Project (except recreational user fees) Lower Colorado River Basin Development Fund. Revenues specific to the Boulder Canyon Project Colorado River Dam Fund. Revenues from divisions of the Upper Colorado River Storage Project Upper Colorado River Basin Fund. Revenues from sale, exchange, or lease of Columbia Basin Project lands Columbia Basin Land Development Account.
