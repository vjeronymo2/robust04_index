Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 2 (KCNA) -- Industrial establishments of Korea overfulfilled their February plans and are expanding the achievements. The Anju District coal mining complex topped its February target on all indices by introducing a new coal cutting method. It continues boosting production in March. The Chilli coal mine doubled production in February compared with the same month of last year by introducing a new blasting method. The Tokchon District coal mining complex carried out its February assignments for capital tunnelling at 100.5 nercent, preparatory tunnelling at 101.3 percent, slope pit tunnelling at 101.3 percent, pit concreting at 112.8 percent and coal output at 101.8 percent as of February 27. The Changjagang Power Station fulfilled its February plan three days ahead of schedule and the Chagang provincial combined general bureau of forestry produced over 10,000 more cubic metres of timber in February as against the same period of last year. The Songsan mine surpassed its February goal by 48 percent in terms of output value and by 27 percent in kind. It is carrying out its daily quotas at 130 to 150 percent in march. The mine fulfilled its January plan in a matter of 14 days. February plans were also overfulfilled by the Myongchon District coal mining complex, the General Bureau of Harbour Works, the Pyongyang Municipal General Bureau of Garment Industry and many other units, which are keeping a high rate of production in March.
