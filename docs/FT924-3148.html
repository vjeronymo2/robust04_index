921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Manders sells arm to Croda for Pounds 2.5m Manders (Holdings), the paints, inks and property group, has severed its ties with the industrial paints business with the sale of Mander-Domolac to Croda International for about Pounds 2.5m. Mr Roy Fenney, managing director industrial chemicals at Croda, said that the acquisition would help fill its factory capacity, which is running at between 50 and 60 per cent. Mander-Domolac paints are high performance coatings, specific to particular applications and customers. The business will be transferred to Croda factories in Hull, Birmingham and Sheffield.
