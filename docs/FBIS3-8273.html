Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Republic of Croatia President Franjo Tudjman has passed a decision relieving Brigadier Stjepan Hadanic of his duty as assistant defense minister and appointing him head of the Military Office of the President, it has been announced from the president's office. Franjo Tudjman also relieved Brigadier Mile Cuk of his duty as military aide to the president, and appointed him commander of the 1st Croatian Guards Corps. Franjo Tudjman also relieved Brigadier Kresimir Kaspar of his duty as head of the Military Office of the President, and appointed him military aide to the president.
