940118 FT 18 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Northern Industrial downturn Pre-tax profits of Northern Industrial Improvement Trust, the investment and property company, fell from Pounds 224,800 to Pounds 220,200 in the six months to end-September. Income amounted to Pounds 245,200 against a previous Pounds 251,800 and earnings per share worked through at 13.04p (13.18p). The directors anticipate the interim dividend to be similar to last year's 9p.
