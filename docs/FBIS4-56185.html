BFN [Text] The president of the Chamber of Deputies calls on the Haitian minister of finances and U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher to shed light on the use of Haitian state funds by President-in-exile Jean-Bertrand Aristide. In a letter addressed to Minister Rey and to U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher, Deputy Monde indicates that the Haitian populace has the right to explanations of President Aristide's expenses covered from state funds frozen abroad.
