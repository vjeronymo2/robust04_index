940716 FT 16 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Quadrant makes flight training buy Quadrant Group, the photographic and video concern, is expanding its aircraft training activities. Through Quadrant Systems, its 80 per cent owned offshoot, it is acquiring for Pounds 1.43m from Ceselsa of Spain the flight training business of Aeronautical Systems Designers, based in West Sussex. The company is also setting up a joint marketing activity with Binghamton Simulator of the US involving the sale of the US company's aircraft training systems in the UK, the middle east and east Asia. Quadrant Systems is a new company, 20 per cent of which is owned by its management, which has subscribed Pounds 150,000 for its interest. Quadrant can increase its interest to 100 per cent after five years.
