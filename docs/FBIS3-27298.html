Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hong Kong, February 22 (XINHUA) -- Experts of the two sides of Sino-British Joint Liaison Group held a meeting here today to discuss matters relating to Hong Kong's military sites and navy base. Attending the meeting which is scheduled to last until Friday [25 February] are Luo Jiahuan, representative of the Chinese side, his British counterpart Alan Paul and their assistants. Making comments to reporters prior to the meeting, Luo Jiahuan said that the Chinese side hopes that an agreement can be reached during the meeting. To achieve this, he said, the British side should show adequate flexibility. During the meeting, participants will visit some local military sites.
