BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 10 (KCNA) -- The DPRK Government economic delegation led by Vice-Premier Hong Song-nam returned home on Saturday from a visit to Pakistan. It was met at the airport by Chairman of the State External Economic Affairs Commission Yi Song-tae and Charge d'Affaires ad Interim of the Pakistani Embassy in Pyongyang Shafqat Ali Cheema.
