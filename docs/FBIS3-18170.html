Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Report by correspondent Nikolaos Bellos from Brussels] [Text] Alternate Foreign Minister Theodhoros Pangalos described the Greek legal arguments [on measures taken against The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM] as extremely well founded. Referring to the political aspect of the measures, Pangalos said that Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou's relevant letter is already in the hands of European Commission President Jacques Delors. In light of the submission of the Greek memorandum to the European Commission, Pangalos said that he had a telephone conversation with Greek Commissioner Ioannis Palaiokrasas and agreed to meet him on Monday afternoon [28 February] in Brussels. They will discuss ways of cooperation to deal with the issue further.
