940831 FT 31 AUG 94 / UK Company News: BP's big find in Colombia British Petroleum yesterday announced one of its biggest discoveries in recent years with the disclosure of a major new natural gas field in Colombia. The field, named Volcanera, will more than double Colombia's gas reserves. Although its potential is still being assessed, the company has estimated recoverable reserves of five trillion cubic feet of natural gas and 250m barrels of condensate, a naturally occuring gasoline.
