921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / World Trade News: Texas and Samsung in chip venture Texas Instruments (TI), the US high-technology company, and Samsung, the South Korean electronics giant, yesterday signed a letter of intent for a multi-million-dollar joint manufacturing investment in Oporto, northern Portugal, writes Peter Wise in Lisbon. The agreement is the first joint venture between the two companies and the Samsung Electronics' first overseas investment in a semiconductor manufacturing plant.
