940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Equifax in Pounds 39m purchase Equifax, the Atlanta-based information services group, and UAPT-Infolink, the United Kingdom credit referencing business, last night announced a recommended all-cash offer for UAPT-Infolink. The offer, at Pounds 5 per share, values UAPT-Infolink at approximately Pounds 39m. It represents a premium of 49.3 per cent over the UAPT-Infolink share price of Pounds 3.35 on July 25, 1994. The board of UAPT-Infolink is unanimously recommending the offer. Equifax has received undertakings to accept or indications of support in relation to the offer in respect of 32.1 per cent of UAPT-Infolink's issued ordinary share capital. Mr Travis Halford, chief executive of Equifax Europe, commenting on the offer, said last night: 'We are delighted to have agreed terms with UAPT-Infolink's board. This attractive offer for UAPT-Infolink is made possible by virtue of the efficiencies our combined UK businesses can achieve.'
