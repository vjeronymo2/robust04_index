BFN [Text] A fully charged antitank mine was found on Tuesday morning on the railroad station square in Vilnius. The police received information about the mine at 0645. The fire brigade and police blocked all approaches to the railroad station. The mine was disarmed. It had been wrapped in a polyethylene bag and could explode if touched by a passing vehicle. It took 45 minutes to eliminate the danger. During this time all operations at the Vilnius railroad station were stopped. The police had difficulty shutting trading kiosks as their owners, according to policemen, preferred to lose their lives than leave their property.
