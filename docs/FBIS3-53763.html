Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Official Army sources deny that a soldier belonging to the Finnish contingent of the UN Interim Force in Lebanon [UNIFIL] was hit by an artillery shell fired either by the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] or the SLA [South Lebanese Army]. Our correspondent in the north Yo'av Aviv reports that according to the sources, the IDF and SLA did not fire artillery in the region. It was also reported that UNIFIL withdrew a complaint issued this morning to the IDF's Liaison Unit with the United Nations.
