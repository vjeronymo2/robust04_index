920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / Pound touches low with DM STERLING last night touched a new low against the D-Mark in the European exchange rate mechanism, amid continuing investor nervousness about a possible realignment of weak ERM currencies. In late European trading, the pound hit DM2.7808, a little more than a quarter of a pfennig above its DM2.7780 ERM floor, after a news agency report cited the German Bundesbank as saying the currency was a candidate for devaluation. Sterling gained ground later to DM2.7850, after the Bundesbank denied the content of the report.
