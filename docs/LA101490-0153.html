October 14, 1990, Sunday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: COUNTYWIDE;

OXNARD MAY CHANGE SITE OF FESTIVAL

Relocation of the California Strawberry Festival to a larger site will be discussed Tuesday at the Oxnard City Council meeting. Festival organizers hope to have the May event moved to College Park, a meadow next to Oxnard College.

The fair has been held at Channel Islands Harbor since its beginning seven years ago. More than 70,000 people swarmed through the harbor's seven-acre fairgrounds last May, said Dr. Tsujio Kato, co-chairman of the 1990 event.

This year's fair would spread to 14 acres at College Park to accommodate shorter lines, a larger arts-and-crafts area and more entertainers, Kato said.

Organizers say another advantage to College Park is its on-site parking and easy freeway access.

The fair, which has generated about $500,000 for local nonprofit groups since it began, will be held May 18 and 19. KIM KASH
