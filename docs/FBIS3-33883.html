Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pakistani troops again fired six rockets from across the border in Mendhar Sector of Punch District in Jammu and Kashmir yesterday. No damage is reported. According to official sources, Pakistani troops have been firing rockets on the Indian side without any provocation for the last four days. The president of the All-India Youth Congress-I, Mr. Maninderjit Singh Bitta, has said that Youth Congress-I activists will demonstrate near the Indo-Pakistan border at Atari on the fifth of next month. Addressing a rally in Bhubaneswar, he said this is to rebuff the three-day strike call given by Pakistan Premier Ms. Benazir Bhutto in that country on the Kashmir issue.
