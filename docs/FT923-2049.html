920919 FT 19 SEP 92 / Shipyard jobs go A FURTHER 250 workers at Cammell Laird shipyard on Merseyside are to lose their jobs, it was announced yesterday. That will reduce the workforce to just more than 700. It is the latest stage in a rolling redundancy programme at the yard, which has shed 1,150 jobs since VSEL, the parent company, put the yard up for sale two years ago.
