BFN [Text] A sitting of the heads of the city and rayon administration of Zhezkazgan Oblast was held today. Prime Minister Sergey Tereshchenko who arrived on a visit, introduced Albert Salomatin, the new head of the oblast administration. On his visit to the city, the head of government visited a business center, which is under construction here, the production amalgamation Zhezkazgantsvetmet and a complex of medical establishments built by the Samsung company of South Korea. On the same day Tereshchenko returned to Almaty.
