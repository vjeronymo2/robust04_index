Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the press review] [Text] The lead story in the DAILY GRAPHIC is the smashing of a passports syndicate by the narcotics unit of the police. The syndicate, uncovered at a spot near the (?Crepys) Hotel in Accra, is suspected to be run by drug barons. The GRAPHIC quotes the deputy superintendent of police in charge of the narcotics unit, Mr. Robert Ayalindu, as saying that eight people, including the owner of the spot, have been arrested. Fifty-nine international and diplomatic passports were also seized during the operation. The passports are from the Bahamas, Liberia, the Netherlands, Switzerland, Kenya, and Canada. Some have American visas issued in them while others have visible signs that the original pictures have been taken off with the view to inserting new pictures.
