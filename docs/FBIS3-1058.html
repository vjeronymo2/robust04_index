Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Transitional Executive Council's subcouncil on finance is to set up a control committee to ensure that public funds are properly spent and accounted for. This follows the news that four self-governing territories, Lebowa, Gazankulu, kaNgwane, and kwaNdebele have indicated they will overspend their budgets for 1993-94 by a total of 333 million rands. The subcouncil recommeded approval of central government guaranteed overdraft facilities for a total of 478 million rands.
