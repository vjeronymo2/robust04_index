940106 FT 06 JAN 94 / Official reserves shrink by Dollars 674m The UK's official reserves fell by Dollars 674m in December to stand at Dollars 42.93bn at the year end, the Treasury announced. The main reason for the fall was the Treasury's decision to repay the Ecu5bn (Dollars 5.6bn) revolving bank credit arranged in September 1992 to support the pound.
