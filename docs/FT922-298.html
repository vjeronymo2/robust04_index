920629 FT 29 JUN 92 / Economics: Markets on tenterhooks over Fed's next move INVESTORS and financial markets will be on tenterhooks this week in case the US Federal Reserve decides on a further easing of monetary policy. The Federal Open Market Committee, the Fed's top decision-making body, meets tomorrow amid fears that the US economic recovery may fade. Several important US indicators are scheduled during the week, beginning with consumer confidence tomorrow, the national purchasing managers' report on Wednesday and ending with labour market data on Thursday. Some analysts have suggested that poor labour market figures could prompt the Fed to cut rates after discussion of such a move in the FOMC.
