920207 FT 07 FEB 92 / International Company News: MacMillan Bloedel in red MACMILLAN Bloedel (Macblo), the Canadian west coast forestry group, suffered a record loss of CDollars 93.4m (USDollars 79.8m), or 98 cents a share, in 1991 compared with 1990 earnings of CDollars 50.8m, or 37 cents, writes Bernard Simon in Toronto. Sales dropped to CDollars 2.73bn from CDollars 3bn. Prices for all key products, except lumber, fell last year. Earnings were also hit by the strong Canadian dollar.
