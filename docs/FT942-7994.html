940523 FT 23 MAY 94 / Arts (Openings): Drottningholm One of Europe's most handsomely-preserved festival settings, the Drottninholm Court Theatre, begins its 1994 summer season on Thursday. In her second year as artistic director, Swedish soprano Elisabeth Soderstrom follows up Drottningholm's recent series of Gustavian operas with a revival of Edouard Du Puy's 'Youth and Folly', a 'Singspiel' by the French singer, composer and adventurer responsible for introducing 'Don Giovanni' to Sweden in 1807.
