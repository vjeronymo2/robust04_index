Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Macao, February 7 (XINHUA) -- Macao Governor General Vasco Joaquim Rocha Vieira said that the visit of China's Communications Minister Huang Zhendong to Macao was a great success. He also said that this had again showed the friendly cooperation between China and Portugal. Macao governor made these remarks at the Barrier Gate Check Point in Macao where he said good-bye to Minister Huang Zhendong this morning. The governor said the visit of the minister showed that he was deeply concerned with related projects in Macao. He noted, Macao residents should be confident in Macao's future and in the contributions that both China and Portugal have made to the area. "We'll further our efforts to create conditions for the progress and stability of Macao," he said.
