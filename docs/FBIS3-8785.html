Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Autonomy for Pirin Macedonia"] [Text] The leader of the Macedonian organization OMO Ilenden in Bulgaria Jordan Kostadinov stated during the press conference for the agency BALKAN his organization's demand for a status of an autonomous region of Pirin Macedonia. He claims Bulgaria is forcibly assimilating the Macedonians by not acknowledging their language and by physical and moral violence by the authorities, which is a kind of a genocide. He added claims that Macedonians are in fact Bulgarians is a war propaganda, since 75 percent of the population in Pirin Macedonia have a sense of belonging to the Macedonian nation. Kostadinov demanded introducing the Macedonian language in the schools and Macedonian nation as a category in the passport and diplomas. In addition, he insists on separation of the Nevrokop diocese of a metropolitan from the Bulgarian Church and appointing a Macedonian metropolitan. He also demands the Macedonian autonomous region to have a crowned lion in its symbol and a sun on its flag.
