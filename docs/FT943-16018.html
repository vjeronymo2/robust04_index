940706 FT 06 JUL 94 / International Company News: Steel unit venture Wheeling Pittsburgh's steel unit has signed a letter of intent for a 50-50 joint venture with Ispat Group, part of the Mexico-based steel manufacturer Ispat Mexicana, AP-DJ reports from New York. The venture will purchase steel slabs from Ispat Group. Wheeling-Pittsburgh Steel will convert these into finished hot-rolled products.
