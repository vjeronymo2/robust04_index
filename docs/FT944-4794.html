941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Pernas Precision Engineering and Construction (Perspec) The main terminal building contract at the Kuala Lumpur international airport at Sepang has been awarded to a consortium led by Pernas Precision Engineering and Construction (Perspec). Perspec bid MDollars 1.74bn (USDollars 682m) for the job. The Malaysia-Japanese consortium Perspec-Taisei-Kajima-Shimizu-Hazama outbid eight groups to win the contract to build the passenger terminal.
