940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Jove Investment net asset value at 66.99p Jove Investment Trust reported net asset value per capital share of 66.99p at February 28, compared with 38.38p a year earlier. Net revenue for the year to end-February was Pounds 1.01m (Pounds 841,226) for earnings per income share of 7.13p (5.94p). A second interim dividend of 2.9p (2.8p) is declared, which with a special dividend of 1p makes a total for the year of 6.8p (5.6p).
