BFN [Text] A group of 480 Tajik refugees returned to their homeland from Afghanistan Thursday via a check-point in Nizhny Pyanj. INTERFAX was informed by the Tajik parliament's commission for refugees that this was the first time in the last three weeks that refugees had been sent home. The repatriates are from the Khotlon region (formerly Kurgan-Tyube) in southern Tajikistan. The refugees commission said that at least 25,000 Tajik refugees are still in Afghanistan.
