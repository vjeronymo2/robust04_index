Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Supreme Soviet Interim Commission for Fighting Corruption has adopted a draft resolution that provides for dismissing Prime Minister Vyacheslaw Kebich, National Bank Chairman Stanislaw Bahdankevich, Deputy Prime Ministers Stanislaw Bryl and Mikalay Kostsikaw, Uladzimir Radkevich, chairman of the State Committee for External Economic Relations, and Defense Minister Pavel Kazlowski, "in connection with failing to adopt appropriate measures for protecting the economic interests of the state in relations with commercial organizations, as well as failing to enforce the existing law." The draft resolution was worked out at the 29 March meeting of the commission, and will submitted to the Supreme Soviet for consideration on 30 March, following a speech by Alyaksandr Lukashenka. As BELAPAN has learned, the commission has prepared a list of another 60 people to be dismissed. Among them is also Foreign Minister Petr Krawchanka.
