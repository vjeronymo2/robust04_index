Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [INTERFAX report: "Moldavian President Denies Rumors of the Migration of Russian-Speaking Population"] [Text] Rumors circulating about the migration from Moldavia [Moldova] of the Russian-speaking population have no real basis, Republic President Mircea Snegur stated. He described them as tendentious. Meeting on Monday [28 March] with leaders of Tarakliyskiy Rayon, where there is a concentated population of Bulgarians, M. Snegur also touched on the certification of the population in the knowledge of the state language, scheduled for 2 April. In his opinion, the timetable for this should be finally examined by the new parliament. At the same time, the president believes that the necessary conditions should be created for study of the state language, and that the state is seeing to this. M. Snegur remarked that knowledge of the state language for people occupying leading posts is obligatory.
