Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] On the majority of the battlefields in the former Bosnia-Herzegovina provocative and offensive activities of the Muslim units toward the Serbian side have continued. From the direction of Kalesija two 120-mm projectiles filled with a chlorine-based chemical agent were fired at the Zelina region. The Serbian side has officially notified the command of the UN peace troops for former Bosnia-Herzegovina about this.
