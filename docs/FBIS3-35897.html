Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, 31 Jan (ARMENPRES) -- The Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan received Vladimir Kazimirov, the Russian president's ambassador on special missions, upon the latter's return from Stepanakert. They discussed Russia's revised initiative for the resolution of the Karabakh conflict, and the proposals it has submitted to the sides for a temporary and universal cease-fire and halt in military operations as of 1 February, pending the implementation of the measures envisaged in the initiative. Kazimirov held talks with Armenian Foreign Minister Vahan Papazyan and the Armenian president's ambassador for special missions, Davit Shahnazaryan. The Armenian side has officially given a positive reply to the Russian proposal. Earlier, Kazimirov met in Stepanakert with the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic leadership, who reiterated their desire for a political resolution of the problem and their readiness to enter into talks.
