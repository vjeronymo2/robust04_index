BFN [Text] Following the assassination of two French nationals in the capital Algiers today, Prime Minister Mokdad Sifi sent a cable of condolences to his French counterpart, Edouard Balladur, on behalf of the government and in his personal name, which stated that the Algerian authorities will do their utmost to arrest the perpetrators of this crime. This is no more than a confirmation, the head of government adds, regarding combating terrorism. The interior minister has deplored, in a statement released this evening, this abominable action and offered condolences to the families of the two victims and the whole of the French community regarding this tragic incident. The interior minister stressed that Algeria is known for its tradition of hospitality and its respect of human life and is not and will never be a home for criminals and killers. The perpetrators of this operation will be sought and punished severely, he said. Regarding the terrorist activities, the security services announced today that they had eliminated 15 terrorists and arrested 5 others between Monday and Saturday at various parts of the country.
