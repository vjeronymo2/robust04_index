BFN [Text] Cairo, 5 Jun (MENA)--The northern al-Jizah Prosecution Office today interrogated Muslim Brotherhood General Guide Hamid Abu-al-Nasr over leaflets that were distributed after the 'Id al- Adha prayers. These leaflets, which bore the signature of Hamid Abu-al-Nasr, advocated views and terms opposed to the regime. Public attorney counselor Muhammad 'Arafah confronted Abu-al-Nasr with these leaflets. Abu-al-Nasr stated that they indeed were issued by him. The Prosecution Office decided to complete interrogation with him tomorrow morning, 6 June. The security authorities arrested four youths who were distributing leaflets bearing the signature of the Muslim Brotherhood General Guide Hamid Abu-al-Nasr. They were referred to the Prosecution Office which ordered them put in jail pending further interrogation. The Prosecution Office decided to summon Abu-al-Nasir to hear his testimony.
