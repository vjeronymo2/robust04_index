Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 3 Feb (INA)--Iraq and the UN Special Commission held a second round of talks at the Foreign Ministry this morning. The Iraqi side to the talks was led by Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf. Rolf Ekeus, chairman of the UN Special Commission, led the Commission's side to the talks. The Iraqi side comprised Lieutenant General 'Amir Muhammad Rashid, director of the Military Industrialization Organization; Dr. Riyad al-Qaysi, Foreign Ministry under secretary; and several Foreign Ministry officials. The Special Commission's side comprised Zifferero, deputy chairman of the International Atomic Energy Agency, John Scott, the Commission's legal adviser; and Tim Terevan [name as received], official spokesman for the Commission.
