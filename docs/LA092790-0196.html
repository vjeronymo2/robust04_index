September 27, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: COSTA MESA;

CHILDREN'S CENTER TO MARK ANNIVERSARY

The Children's Center at Orange Coast College will celebrate its 20th anniversary with an open house and fair Oct. 6.

Parents and children who have frequented the center are invited to attend. The fair will include games, arts and crafts, entertainment and a potluck lunch. Admission is free and families are asked to bring food to share.

Activities will be run from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. at the center, on the northeastern edge of campus on Monitor Road behind LeBard Stadium.
