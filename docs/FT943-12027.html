940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / UK Company News: TT increases stake in Dale to 9.5% TT Group, the expanding conglomerate, yesterday announced the purchase of 582,283 shares in its latest takeover target, Dale Electric, the generator manufacturer. The shares, bought at 70 1/4 p apiece, lift TT's stake in Dale to 1.26m shares, or 5.51 per cent of the equity capital. Taking in parties deemed to be acting in concert and irrevocable undertakings from directors, TT now controls 9.54 per cent of Dale.
