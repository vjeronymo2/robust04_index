921212 FT 12 DEC 92 / Finance & The Family: Warburg guide to investment trusts - At a glance SG Warburg has produced a useful guide to investment trusts for the private investor. It gives details of savings schemes and Peps, together with an explanation of the different classes of share. The guide costs Pounds 12, from Roger Adams, SG Warburg, 1 Finsbury Avenue, London EC2M 2PA.
