930105 FT 05 JAN 93 / Chips spiked with rat poison MR MICHAEL Lloyd, former national sales manager for McCain, was yesterday sentenced at St Albans crown court to 100 hours community service with Pounds 275 costs after admitting that he planted rat poison in bags of frozen chips to take revenge on the firm for dismissing him. The court heard he approached staff at Asda, Gateway and Tesco with opened bags of McCain Oven Chips spiked with rat poison.
