930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / Labour plea in Kuwait A Kuwaiti trade union yesterday accused manpower companies of ill-treating foreign workers and demanded greater legal protection, Reuter reports from Kuwait. 'These companies have greatly damaged Kuwait's image abroad through violations of labour laws and their inhuman practices,' the Union of Oil and Petrochemical Industry Workers said. Two thousand foreign employees went on a rampage in a strike earlier this month, overturning cars and smashing up rooms at Kuwait National Petroleum's Mina Abdulla refinery.
