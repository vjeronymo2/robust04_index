930705 FT 05 JUL 93 / International Company News: BankAmerica Corp BANKAMERICA Corp's Bank of America subsidiary is forming an organisation, based on wholesale and retail banking divisions, to serve its Asia markets more effectively, Reuter reports from San Francisco. The organisation will include an Asian wholesale banking division and an Asian retail banking division, both based in Hong Kong. Mr James Hulihan, chief executive officer of Bank of America (Asia), will head the retail division, and Mr Kai Nargolwala, senior credit officer with the Asia division, will lead the wholesale division.
