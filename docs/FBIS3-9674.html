Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Deputies of the Bihac assembly discussed the draft constitution of the Bosnia-Herzegovina federation at a session today. After an introductory speech by Mirsad Delagic, Bosnian government minister for regional coordination for the Bihac-Banja Luka district, and statements by the republic's deputies Adem Ibrahimpasic and Ejub Topic, deputies of the Bihac assembly praised this historic document and fully approved the proposed constitution. They also concluded that -- due to administrative mistakes -- a protest must be lodged with the chairman and the secretary general of the Bosnia-Herzegovina parliament to request that invitations and documents for future sessions be sent to the Bihac deputies.
