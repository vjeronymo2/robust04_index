BFN [Excerpts] Kuwait, 29 May (KUNA) -- Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, envoy of the secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, arrived here today from Abu Dhabi on a two-day visit to Kuwait during which he will give a message to the amir of Kuwait. [passage omitted] In an arrival statement to KUNA, the Yemeni envoy said he is confident that his meetings with the amir of Kuwait and Kuwaiti officials will be successful. He stated that they will discuss the situation in Yemen "as a result of the war launched by the military command in Sanaa in the same spirit and with the same rationale as that with which the perfidious war was launched against Kuwait." Al-'Attas praised the efforts made by Arab states to halt the war and said that these efforts are appreciated by the Yemeni people. [passage omitted]
