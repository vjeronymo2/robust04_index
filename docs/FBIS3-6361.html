Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 17 (KCNA) -- A national seminar was held at the People's Palace of Culture here Wednesday to mark the 30th anniversary of the publication of the famous work of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il "Position and Role of the County in Socialist Construction". Present at the seminar were Politburo member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] and Vice-President Yi Chong-ok, Secretary of the WPK Central Committee Kim Chung-nin, Vice-Premier of the Administration Council Chang Chol, and other officials concerned and personnel of party cadres training organs, men of science, education and the press. At the seminar the speakers said the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il in his work proved the validity and truthfulness of the idea of the great leader President Kim Il-song on the position and role of the county in socialist construction with his lucid logic and persuasive power and further developed it in depth with his profound, unique idea and new scientific and theoretical propositions. The dear leader, they noted, put it forward as a strategic task that should certainly be carried out by a working-class party and state after the establishment of the socialist system to clear away the differences between town and country, between the working class and peasantry by raising the role of the county as a regional base, and made clear the revolutionary essence and importance of the question of raising the role of the county. They said the work that gives a profound, unique scientific explanation of the role of the county as a comprehensive unit of developing the local economy and as a base of economic links between town and country is a highly important one in which the dear leader developed in depth the theory of socialist construction from a new angle with his rare ideological and theoretical wisdom. The speakers pointed out that the dear leader's work is a highly important work that developed the theory of socialist construction because, by giving a scientific exposition of the question of raising the position and role of the county in the socialist rural construction, it provides a firm guideline in leading the socialist rural construction and socialist construction as a whole along a straight road to victory. They said the idea of the regional base expounded in the work indicates the way of bringing earlier the complete victory of socialism and making the popular masses masters of the state and society by raising the role of the county. They explained and proved that to strengthen economic links between town and country as expounded in the work is the mighty way of finally solving the rural question and dynamically accelerating the building of socialism and communism.
