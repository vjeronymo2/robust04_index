Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] This morning, Souban Salitthilat, acting minister of foreign affairs of the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR], met and held talks with Ajit Singh, secretary general of the ASEAN, at the Vang Sadet guest house in Vientiane. Ajit Singh is currently on his first visit to the LPDR. The visit is scheduled from 27 February to 2 March. The meeting and talks proceeded in a very cordial atmosphere. On this occasion, Souban Salitthilat informed the ASEAN secretary general of the (?domestic situation in Laos) and noted the possibility of expanding cooperation between Laos and the ASEAN group [words indistinct]. The two sides also discussed other issues of common interest. They particularly discussed means of effectively promoting and expanding the relations and cooperation between Laos and the ASEAN member countries in the immediate future. ASEAN Secretary General Ajit Singh and his wife arrived in Vientiane at noon on 27 February. They were accorded a warm welcome by Souban Salitthilat, acting minister of foreign affairs, and his wife. They were also welcomed upon their arrival by members of diplomatic corps of the ASEAN countries along with a number of (?high-ranking cadres). [passage omitted] The visit to Laos by the ASEAN secretary general on this occasion is considered a good sign for the friendly relations between Laos and the countries of the ASEAN group. The visit has also contributed to creating an atmosphere of mutual understanding between the countries in the region.
