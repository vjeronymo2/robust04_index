Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Cuban Vice Foreign Minister Isabel Allende today traveled to Las Palmas, Canary Islands, as part of her work visit to Spain, where she has broached such topics as bilateral relations and cooperation. Allende began her work visit to Spain on 11 January. While in Madrid, Allende met with high-ranking Spanish Foreign Ministry officials. Allende also traveled to Barcelona and Seville. During yesterday's visit to the Andalusian capital city, Allende met with Mayor Alejandro Rojas and with Concepcion Gutierrez, adviser to the Andalusian Government Junta. During the meeting, the agreement between the Havana Provincial Assembly and the Andalusian Junta, as well as its contribution to the renovation of Old Havana, were discussed.
