May 11, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

CLU'S COHEN ADVANCES IN SINGLES, DOUBLES IN NAIA DISTRICT 3 FINALS

Cal Lutheran's Tony Cohen advanced to the quarterfinals in both singles and doubles in the NAIA District 3 Tennis championships Thursday at Westmont College. Cohen teamed with Matt Simpson in doubles play.

In the quarterfinals of the women's NCAA Division II individual championships in Rancho Cordova, Jennifer Slattery of Cal Poly Pomona eliminated Cal State Northridge's Cathie Teobaldi, 6-2, 6-2.
