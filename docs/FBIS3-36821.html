Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Russian 14th Army's Military Council has called on Chisinau and Tiraspol to search for peaceful ways to solve the Dniester region problem. The military council's statement notes that an extraordinary situation has taken shape in the region, and tension is being whipped up again. In the military's view, a solution can be found by implementing the Moldovan-Russian agreement on settling the conflict, which was signed in Moscow.
