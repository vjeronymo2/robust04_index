940910 FT 10 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Stavert Zigomala Pre-tax profits at Stavert Zigomala more than doubled for the year ended March 31 from Pounds 52,746 to Pounds 116,078. Turnover at the furniture and carpet wholesaler and investment holding company was up from Pounds 788,607 to Pounds 815,111. Earnings per share were 31.82p (11.91p). The final dividend is again 9.4145p.
