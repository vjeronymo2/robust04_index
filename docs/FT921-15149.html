920111 FT 11 JAN 92 / Council tendering adviser appointed SIR Peter Levene has been appointed part-time adviser to the government on extending contracting-out of public services. He will also be a member of the Citizen's Charter panel of advisers. He is deputy chairman and managing director of Wasserstein Perella, the investment bankers. From 1985 to last year he was chief of defence procurement at the Ministry of Defence.
