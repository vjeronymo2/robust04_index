Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report under the "Provincial Chronicle" rubric: "Omsk"] [Text] President Yeltsin has appointed Boris Vasilyevich Tyulkov his representative in Omsk Oblast. Meanwhile, Valeriy Roshchupkin, chairman of the Coordinating Council for Industrial Policy of the "Siberian Agreement" association, has said that $7 million has been transferred from Moscow to the Omsk-based Baranov Engine Making Association's hard-currency account toward payments for military aircraft engines sold to Iran over a year ago under a $35 million contract. Moscow has just begun to settle accounts with the enterprise, although the customer paid the money long ago.
