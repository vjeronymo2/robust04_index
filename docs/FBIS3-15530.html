Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA/LOGOTIP-KP roundup column report under the general heading "In Brief"] [Text] Armenia -- The republic State Committee for Statistics reports that, according to figures for 1 January 1994, the population of Armenia stands at 3,742,000. There are fewer people living in the republic today, but it is not possible to estimate the number who have left, since most left the republic without deregistering. During 1993 58,363 children were born in the republic, while 28,877 people died, of whom 1,022 were children aged under one year. In the same year 21,646 marriages and 3,045 divorces were registered.
