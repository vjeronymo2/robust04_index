Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday afternoon [24 February], Du Qinglin, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee and chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee, and Wang Xiaofeng, deputy secretary of the provincial party committee and executive vice governor, met at the Hainan Hotel with Fei Xiaotong, vice chairman of the National People's Congress Standing Committee, who was inspecting work in Hainan. Du Qinglin and Wang Xiaofeng briefed Vice Chairman Fei Xiaotong on Hainan's economic growth, economic exploitation, economic structural reform, production, circulation, and other aspects of work over the past few years since Hainan Province was officially established. Vice Chairman Fei Xiaotong spoke highly of each and every tremendous achievement scored by the hardworking Hainan people since Hainan Province was officially established. He also expressed the hope that Hainan will continue to further economic exploitation and construction and will strive to embark on a new stage of development.
