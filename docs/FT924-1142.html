921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Turkey Trust little changed Net asset value per share of the Turkey Trust amounted to 148.06p at October 31, compared with 155.12p six months earlier and 148.97p at the previous year-end. After-tax revenue for the 12 months to end-October was little changed at Pounds 601,000 (Pounds 605,000), equal to earnings of 6.13p (6.19p). The dividend for the year is maintained at 5p, made up as to a same-again 4p special and a 1p ordinary payment.
