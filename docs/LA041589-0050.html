April 15, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

SHIP WRECK FOUND IN MASSACHUSETTS BAY

The wreck of the Portland, a 280-foot side-wheel steamer that claimed more than 160 lives when it sank in a ferocious 1898 storm, has been found in Massachusetts Bay, a maritime explorer said Friday.

John Fish, co-director of Historical Maritime Group of New England, said his crew used sonar equipment this week to confirm that they had found the ship in waters north of Cape Cod.
