Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Maldives President Maumoon Abdul Gayoom visited his highness the amir at 1000 hours this morning at the Bayan Palace, where the official talks between them were held. Shaykh Jabir Mubarak al-Hamad al-Sabah, adviser at the amir's office, said that the talks focused on developments in relations between the two friendly countries and issues of mutual interest. The talks were held in a cordial atmosphere, characterized by mutual understanding and a spirit of friendship.
