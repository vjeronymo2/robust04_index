921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / More out of work in EC THE number of people out of work in the European Community rose to 14.6m in October, up from 13.3m in October last year, the EC statistics office, Eurostat, said yesterday, Reuter reports from Luxembourg. Eurostat's monthly bulletin said the seasonally adjusted average rate of unemployment also edged up to 9.8 per cent from 9.7 in September, and 9 per cent in October 1991. It is now at the highest rate since June 1988. The rise was marked in Britain, Spain and eastern Germany.
