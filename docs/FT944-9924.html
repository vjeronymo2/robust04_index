941112 FT 12 NOV 94 / Increase in National Savings National Savings' contribution to government funding rose to Pounds 287m last month from Pounds 211m in September. A total of Pounds 99m was from net receipts while accrued interest contributed Pounds 188m. The highest contribution came from premium bonds for the fifth month running, at Pounds 123m, followed by savings certificates at Pounds 109m. At the end of October, a total of Pounds 50.8bn was invested in National Savings.
