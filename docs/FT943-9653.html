940810 FT 10 AUG 94 / Shiver me timbers Mary Rose, the warship raised from the seabed in 1982, has now been reassembled in the condition in which it was found with the replacement of a final timber - a bracket supporting screens to protect archers. The ship, on display in Portsmouth, was built between 1509 and 1511 on the orders of Henry VIII and sank in 1545.
