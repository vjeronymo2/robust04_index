Article Type:CSO [Editorial Report] Managua LA PRENSA in Spanish of 7 February 1994 on page 9 reports that the Agency for Economic and Social Development (ADES) will make loans to cooperatives in Leon department for growing rice. ADES director Luis Enrique Morales explains that the program will offer on-site training, seminars, and financing for 332 manzanas. According to the article, ADES has received financial support from the European Union, through the Italian Government. It is also reported that interest rates will be three points lower than the bank rates, repayable in six months.
