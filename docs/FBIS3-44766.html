Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 30 Dec (XINHUA) -- President Jiang Zemin yesterday signed Presidential Decree No. 16 promulgating "Company Law of the People's Republic of China." According to the presidential decree, the law was adopted by the Fifth Session of the Eighth National People's Congress Standing Committee of the People's Republic of China on 29 December 1993, and it will become effective on 1 July 1994. The Company Law has 11 chapters -- general provisions; the establishment and organizations of limited liability company; the establishment and organizations of company limited by shares; share issuance and transfer of company limited by shares; corporate bonds and debentures; corporate finance and accounting; corporate merger and division; corporate bankruptcy, disbandment and liquidation; branch of foreign company; legal liability; and supplementary provisions.
