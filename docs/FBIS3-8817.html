Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Vitaliy Churkin, special envoy of the Russian president, has suddenly arrived in Belgrade as part of the latest peace initiative of the United States and Russia to find a solution to the Bosnian conflict. Immediately on arrival, Churkin met Radovan Karadzic, president of the Srpska republic. It has been unofficially learned that Churkin will also be received by Serbian President Slobodan Milosevic. According to the latest statements by Churkin, the three warring factions in Bosnia could met at a negotiating table as early as next week. This round of negotiations would be held outside the framework of the Geneva Conference and would be mediated by the United States and Russia.
