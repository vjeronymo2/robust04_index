930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / Technology: Software goes on trial - Worth Watching However much market research a company does before investing in computer hardware or software, most have to admit that they have made mistakes. ICL Secure Systems believes it has the solution with its latest security system, which it is offering on a 90-day trial basis. Access Manager is intended to enable computer users on a network to access all the different software packages and files by logging on just once. During the trial ICL will develop a security policy with the company and use Access Manager to design a 20-user pilot site. ICL: UK, 0734 693131.
