Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Seven Arabs were wounded by IDF [Israel Defense Forces] gunfire in Hebron today during disturbances and riots in the al-Shaykh neighborhood. Our correspondent Moti Amir was told by local hospitals that the seven were slightly wounded and are being treated in the hospitals. An explosive charge was thrown at the military government court building in Bethlehem this afternoon, but nobody was hurt in this incident.
