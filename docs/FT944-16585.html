941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Federal Express - Contracts Federal Express of the US has signed an air operating agreement with the Subic Bay Metropolitan Authority in Manila. Fedex is scheduled to start operating from Subic Bay, a former US naval base, by the second quarter of 1995. Fedex will pay Dollars 1.65m annually until 2002 for airport use, landing fees, parking fees, and rental of building and office spaces inside Subic.
