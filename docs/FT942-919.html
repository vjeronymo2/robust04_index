940627 FT 27 JUN 94 / Observer: Own goal The BBC's exhausting, sorry exhaustive, coverage of the football world cup has been interspersed with what the corporation calls 'mood music' which is, it points out, 'entirely unrelated' to the different national teams. That is a relief. For the tune that pipes up when Cameroonian players appear sounds very like 'The Lion', by Senegalese singer Yousso N'Dour, described in the record's sleeve notes as 'a sprightly, rallying tribute to Senegal's national heroes, its soccer team.'
