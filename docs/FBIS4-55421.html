BFN [Text] In connection with the announcement issued by the cabinet of Alija Izetbegovic, president of the Presidency of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina, that aid in the amount of 100 German marks will soon be given to all families of killed Muslim fighters [sehit] in Bosnia-Herzegovina, the Liberal Party of Bosnia-Herzegovina has issued the following statement: We are very pleased with President Izetbegovic's statements and concrete activities concerning the welfare of families of killed fighters and (?killed Muslim fighters), as well as his statement that he will soon devote his endeavors to this problem alone. However, what has been left unclear is whether there was an error in the announcement or whether discrimination against killed fighters, patriots in the defense of the state of Bosnia-Herzegovina, is really being introduced, something that we cannot support. We expect the error, be it in the announcement or intentional, to be immediately corrected.
