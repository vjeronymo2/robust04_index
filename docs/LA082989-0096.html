August 29, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Gone in an Instant: Singer Paul McCartney's Nov. 27-29 concert dates at the Forum were near-instant sellouts Monday, so tickets were put on sale for two additional dates: Nov. 23 (Thanksgiving) and Nov. 24. They immediately sold out, too. A spokeswoman for the promoter, Parc Presentations, said no more dates would be added to the engagement, which will be the tour's only Southern California stop. McCartney's last local appearance was a three-night stand at the Forum in June, 1976. ALEENE MacMINN
