September 14, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

In other water polo matches:

Crespi 7, Santa Monica 5 -- Crespi remained undefeated in seven matches with a nonleague victory at Santa Monica (1-1).

Crespi driver Rusty King had three goals and Vince Greco added two. Crespi goalie Tim Behrens recorded 12 saves.

BOYS' CROSS-COUNTRY

Notre Dame 17, Crespi 43 -- Senior Eddie Diaz timed 17 minutes 23 seconds on the three-mile course at Pierce College as the Knights swept the top four places and ended a five-year drought against the Celts in the Mission League opener for both schools.

GIRLS' VOLLEYBALL

Chaminade d. Louisville, 15-7, 15-5, 15-8 -- Pam Powers recorded 12 kills and Noelle Nonaka 20 assists as the Eagles (2-1) notched their first win over the Royals (0-2) in five years in a nonleague match at Chaminade.

Royal d. Arcadia, 15-3, 15-9, 15-4 -- Stacy Kaufmann and Becky Campbell each had seven kills and Kaufmann added seven digs as the Highlanders improved to 2-0 with a nonleague win at home.

Montclair Prep d. Buckley, 15-9, 15-10, 15-4 -- Maria Cicione (nine kills) and Kendra Dousette (seven) led Montclair Prep (2-0) in a nonleague game at Buckley.

La Reina d. Santa Paula, 15-12, 15-1, 15-7 -- Amy Crotty, a setter, had nine kills to pace host La Reina (2-0) and Shannon Mahan added five kills for the winners in a nonleague match.

Burroughs d. Bell-Jeff, 15-13, 15-17, 15-2, 15-11 -- Jennifer Wagner had 29 kills and 14 blocks for Burroughs (3-0) in a nonleague match at Burroughs.

L.A. Baptist d. Oak Park, 15-7, 5-15, 15-2, 11-15, 15-10 -- Outside hitter Monica Davis had 10 kills and Amy Anderson added 19 assists for L.A. Baptist (2-0) in a nonleague match at Oak Park.

Thousand Oaks d. Notre Dame, 15-12, 10-15, 15-3, 15-5 -- Aimee Stone had 10 kills for Thousand Oaks in a nonleague match at Notre Dame (1-2).

Setter Jennifer Streltzoff had 36 assists for Thousand Oaks (2-0).

MEN'S SOCCER

North Texas 1, The Master's College 0 -- Tommy St. John scored the only goal of the match at the 19-minute mark of the first half in a nonconference match in Denton, Tex.

North Texas, a Division I school, outshot Master's, 18-9. Goalkeeper Allan Bowden recorded five saves for Master's (2-2-1).
