940817 FT 17 AUG 94 / International Company News: Record at Bunnings Bunnings, the Australian timber, hardware and building materials group, yesterday reported a 63 per cent rise in after-tax profits to ADollars 30.8m (USDollars 22.8m) for the year to June, writes Emilia Tagaza in Melbourne. Earnings before interest, tax and abnormals increased by 77 per cent to ADollars 53.4m. The earnings were struck on record sales of ADollars 370m, which were 41 per cent higher than the previous year's ADollars 213m.
