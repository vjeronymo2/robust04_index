920320 FT 20 MAR 92 / UK Company News: RPS falls sharply into the red RPS Group, the environmental consultancy, fell sharply from pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.74m to losses of Pounds 493,000 in the year to December 31. The decline was struck after exceptional charges, relating to reorganisation, of Pounds 928,000. Turnover slipped to Pounds 8.83m (Pounds 9.63m) and the cost of sales grew to Pounds 6.31m (Pounds 5.72m), leaving gross profits down at Pounds 2.52m (Pounds 3.91m). Losses per share emerged at 3.36p (earnings 9.49p) and the final dividend is reduced to 0.6p for a total of 2p (3.2p).
