GARDENING SUPPLY SPENDING COMPARISON

In the third quarter of 1988, the most recent period for which information is available, county residents spent $27.1 million for gardening/farm supplies. That averages out to just more than $12 per resident and also comprised 8% of all such spending throughout the state during the July-September period.
