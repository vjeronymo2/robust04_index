BFN [Text] The Yemeni Socialist Party's [YSP] political bureau has welcomed the efforts of brothers and friends to stop the in-fighting and war ignited by the secessionist military family in Sanaa between the sons of the Yemeni people and their armed forces. It confirmed that the response to these Arab and international efforts and initiatives was prompted by the Yemeni people's interest to preserve their gains and unity, and safeguard their armed forces economic structures and the citizens' souls. It was in this respect that the YSP political bureau responded to the calls by the United States, French, Egyptian, and Algerian Governments, and expressed its hope that these calls and efforts would receive the support of all the sides concerned at a time when the battles are leading towards comprehensive destruction. In keeping with these efforts, the militant brother, 'Ali Salim al-Bid, secretary general of the YSP Central Committee, continued his contacts with a number of Arab presidents, including the Egyptian president, Muhammad Husni Mubarak, the Syrian president, Hafiz al-Asad, and the Libyan president, Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi. He briefed them on developments in Yemen and the bloody battles at the various axes of fighting. He thanked them for their concern for Yemen and confirmed the YSP's compliance with any effort to reflect Yemen and the Yemeni people from the devastation of war and in-fighting among the brothers.
