910420 FT 20 APR 91 / Finance & The Family: Kleinwort focuses on trusts Kleinwort Benson has established a separate organisation to manage its stable of investment trusts. Ben Siddons, managing director of Kleinwort Benson Investment Trust Management (KBITM) said the aim was to develop the trusts and to enhance the perception of the group as IT managers. KBITM has Pounds 800m under management and the largest trust, Merchants, which was established in 1889, has assets of more than Pounds 250m.
