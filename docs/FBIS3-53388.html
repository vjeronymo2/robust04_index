Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad 13 Jan (INA)--Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf received here today Serg Mathew, member of the French Senate, and General La Caze, member of the European Parliament and former French chief of staff, and their delegation. They exchanged views on a number of international issues of mutual concern and means of improving and strengthening bilateral ties. They also reviewed the international efforts and France's role in lifting the unjust blockade imposed on Iraq. The French delegation arrived here last night on a visit to Iraq that will last several days.
