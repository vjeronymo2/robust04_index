940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / Financial adviser jailed for fraud Mr Peter Owen-Jackson of the liquidated financial advisers Kenascot was jailed for 2 1/2 years by Maidstone Crown Court on Friday after admitting charges of false accounting and conducting unauthorised investment business. He traded in his own right and in the names of Financial Planning Services and Southdown Financial Services. In July 1990 the Securities and Investments Board obtained High Court injunctions preventing him dealing in any investment business and he was declared bankrupt.
