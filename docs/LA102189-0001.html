October 21, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

MASON'S STEREOTYPES

As if there wasn't already enough trouble between ethnic groups, between racial groups, between religious groups, etc., along comes Jackie Mason to set whatever progress has been made back at least 100 years.

His vicious stereotyping of his own people in the name of comedy on his television series "Chicken Soup" has taken a new turn. He is now allowing his bigotry toward other groups to become obvious.

The man is no longer just an embarrassment to the Jewish people; he is an embarrassment to the human race.

R.W. WEINSHENKER, DDS, Beverly Hills
