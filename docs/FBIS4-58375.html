BFN [Text] Eighty-three rockets and mortar shells were fired into residential areas of Kabul today by Dostam-Golboddin militia. A responsible source of the General Command of Kabul Garrison told a military correspondent of BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY that today's rockets, which were fired from Golboddin-Dostam controlled areas into the city of Kabul, landed in wards 1, 2, 4, 9, 10, and 11. As a result, one of our innocent compatriots was martyred and nine others were injured. One house was destroyed.
