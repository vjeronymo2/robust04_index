Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, March 22 (YONHAP) -- President Kim Yong-sam called upon the Armed Forces to maintain watertight vigilance against North Korea on Tuesday, two days before his scheduled official visit to Japan. Kim made the call during a luncheon he hosted at Chongwadae [presidential office] for top military leaders. Those invited to the lunch included Defense Minister Yi Pyong-tae, Joint Chiefs of Staff Chairman Yi Yang-ho, Army Chief of Staff Kim Tong-chin, Chief of Naval Operations Kim Hong-yol and Air Force Chief of Staff Kim Hong-nae. The president asked the attendees to prepare fully against any contingency by bolstering defense preparedness and maintaining close cooperative system with allies, a Chongwadae source said.
