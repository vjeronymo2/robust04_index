BFN [Report by Fahrudin Becic for Bosnia-Herzegovina Radio and Television] [Text] After a very short break between 1800 and 1900, explosions can be heard again in Gorazde. Several apartment blocks have been hit by tank shells. Buildings in the immediate vicinity of UNPROFOR [UN Protection Force] were also hit. Several shells completely destroyed three apartments. Again, there are killed and injured. From 0800 up to this moment, according to incomplete statistics, 21 people have been killed in Gorazde and 53 wounded. At about 2100 chetnik forces began using anti-aircraft guns and infantry weapons to fire on housing in the town center and buildings around the hospital. The consequences of these activities are not yet known, while at this moment, at about 2117, howitzer shells are landing on the town. Chetniks today launched fierce attacks from the direction of Pale. Four tanks, several armored vehicles, and strong infantry forces took part in these attacks. Actions from the direction of Rogatica and along the demarcation line northwest of the town were intensifying. There is no precise information on the events in this area.
