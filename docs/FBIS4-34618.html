BFN [Text] The executive branch this morning named Ruben Melgarejo Lanzoni as political affairs secretary general and presidential private secretary. The decree also names Miguel Angel Maidana Zayas as presidential general secretary and civil household chief, and Hugo Saguier Caballero as integration minister. The president, exercising his prerogative, created the new Political Affairs General Secretariat to handle the presidency's relations with political parties and movements. Secretary General Melgarejo Lanzoni is currently Paraguayan ambassador to the OAS in Washington. Integration Minister Saguier Caballero was presidential secretary general and civil household chief while his successor, Maidana Zayas, was the presidential legal adviser. Saguier Caballero replaces Atilio R. Fernandez who resigned recently to run for the presidency of the National Republican Association (ANR-Colorado Party). Saguier Caballero is the fourth integration minister in less than three years. The first to head the newest of all ministries was Juan Carlos Wasmosy. He was replaced by Horacio Galeano Perrone, who was in turn replaced by Atilio R. Fernandez.
