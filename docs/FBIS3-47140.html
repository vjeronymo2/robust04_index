Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 9 (KCNA) -- The South Korean puppets staged on January 6 and 7 provocative ground and air war exercises against the North in the vicinity of the Demilitarized Zone along the Military Demarcation Line [MDL], according to military sources. In the war games in Paju, Yonchon, Inje, Kanghwa and Kimpo Counties ground attack units fired hundreds of large-calibre machinegun and automatic rifle bullets, charging towards the MDL. And puppet Air Force units engaged themselves in a drill of support to ground attack units and intensive attack by missiles on targets in coastline areas of the Northern half of the country and a drill of intensive airlifting at night by large transport planes.
