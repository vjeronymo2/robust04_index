920713 FT 13 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Malvern UK Index asset value ahead Malvern UK Index Trust had a net asset value per share of 111.11p at June 30 compared with 105.58p a year earlier and 108.13p at the December 31 year end. Net revenue for the half-year rose to Pounds 983,000 (Pounds 896,000) for earnings of 2.03p (1.85p) per share. The interim dividend is lifted to 1.7p (1.25p), and directors forecast a 2p final payment.
