BFN [Text] Representatives of the Israeli Knesset and the Paramilitary and Security Committee of our Chamber of Deputies had talks in Prague today on parliamentary control of intelligence services. According to the chairman of the Israeli committee, the Knesset is informed every month about the activity of the Israeli intelligence service Mossad. The guests were also interested in the change of the structure of our Army. Chairman of the Committee Vladimir Suman said about this, among other things, that the reason for the existence of the Czech Army is the uncertain development in the countries of the former USSR, organized crime, and aggressive nationalism, especially on the territory of the former Yugoslavia.
