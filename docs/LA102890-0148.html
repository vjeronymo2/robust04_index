October 28, 1990, Sunday, South Bay Edition

EL SEGUNDO FALLS, 19-7

Junior tailback Jeremiah Aguolu rushed for 120 yards and two touchdowns and quarterback Steve Kezirian connected with Stafford Lee on a 43-yard touchdown pass to lift Harvard to a 19-7 victory over El Segundo on Saturday and give the Saracens a share of the San Fernando League title.

Harvard limited El Segundo to 12 yards in total offense in the first half and finished the day with 130 total yards.
