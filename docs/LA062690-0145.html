June 26, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS: ROBERT STREET IS APPOINTED TO HEAD AMERICAN FIRST FEDERAL CREDIT UNION

Robert Street has been appointed president and chief executive of American First Federal Credit Union in La Habra.

He succeeds Kenneth L. Baugh, who has retired after 25 years with the credit union.

Street was formerly president and chief executive of the Nevada Credit Union.
