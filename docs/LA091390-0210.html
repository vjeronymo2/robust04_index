September 13, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

YOU DRIVE, YOU PAY: Expect a major announcement Friday about privately funded toll roads here. . . . Caltrans won't say which counties will get approval to build the roads, but look for press conferences Friday in Sacramento and Orange County. Insiders say toll lanes on the Riverside Freeway and an all-toll extension of the Orange Freeway are likely to be approved. Jeffrey Perlman and Ann Conway
