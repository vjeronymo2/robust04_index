BFN [Text] Esteemed Professor Borhanoddin Rabbani, president of the Islamic State of Afghanistan, received today Mr. Amir Usman, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan. During the course of this meeting, talks were held and views were exchanged on expansion and consolidation of relations between the two countries and on other topics of mutual interest. Also, according to another report, Esteemed Dr. Najibollah Lafra'i, minister of state for foreign affairs, met this morning in his office with Esteemed Amir Usman, ambassador of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan. During the course of this meeting, both sides discussed various issues, including the reopening of the embassy of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan in Kabul.
