BFN [Text] Brother Abu-Lutf [Faruq Qaddumi], head of the PLO's Political Department and acting chairman of the governors committee, has met with a delegation of the World Bank, led by Ram Shabra. Brother Abu-Lutf said that the atmosphere at the meeting was positive and that it focused on core issues between the World Bank, as the secretariat of the donor countries [as heard], and the PLO. A number of agreements were signed. They will be submitted to the PLO's Executive Committee. These agreements concern the financing of the temporary working budget of the Palestinian Economic Council of Development and Construction to enable it to continue its work and to implement the plans that have been approved by the Palestinian national authority, the donor countries and the World Bank, as assigned by the PLO. Brother Abu-Lutf said that a series of coordination meetings between the presidency of the World Bank would take place within the next few days with a view to expediting the execution of the plans agreed on between by the Palestinian national authority, donor countries and the World Bank, and which concern the reconstruction of the Palestinian national economy.
