Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "A Meeting With an Opposition Leader"] [Text] Ukraine's Ambassador to Israel Yu. Shcherbak met with B. Netaniyahu, leader of the parliamentary opposition in the Knesset. During their conversation, the ambassador told him in detail about the political and economic situation in Ukraine. Yu. Shcherbak laid particular emphasis upon the issues of nuclear disarmament in the context of the ratification by the Supreme Council of START-1 and the Lisbon Protocol. B. Netaniyahu showed understanding of Ukraine's position and stressed the need for a concrete, well-weighed, and differentiated approach to the problem of nuclear disarmament.
