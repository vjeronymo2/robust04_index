Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] In the opinion of Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko, the efforts of the Ukrainian, Russian, and U.S. presidents and diplomats have been successful. Mutual compromises, unconventional approaches, and the consideration of national interests of all three states, including, primarily, Ukraine, have brought to realization the political decision to withdrawal nuclear weapons from Ukrainian territory. Specific mechanisms for the political, economic, technical, and financial promotion of this difficult process by the states of the EEC have already been worked out. Anatoliy Zlenko said that the EEC has to objectively evaluate the desire of Ukraine to acquire nonnuclear status.
