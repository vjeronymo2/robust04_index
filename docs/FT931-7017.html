930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / Observer: Family spirit The sins of the fathers would seem partly responsible for the jailing of a man in Nairobi for grabbing two bottles of whisky from a supermarket shelf, promptly swallowing the contents, and refusing to pay. His name is Charles Wanjohi Hiuhu which, in his native Kikuyu, means: 'Charles of the quick drink.'
