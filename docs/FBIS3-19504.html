Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Burgenland Provincial Councilor Christa Krammer (49) is the new health minister. Shortly after her presentation by Chancellor Vranitzky she was sworn in by President Klestil. Krammer is a lawyer. As provincial councilor she was responsible for, among other things, the field of preventive health care. She told the press conference introducing her that she considers all spheres of her ministry (health, consumer protection, sports) equal. The Nationalrat is currently holding a debate on the government reshuffle.
