Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe February 20 TASS -- An officer from the Panj border-guarding detachment of Russian border troops in Tajikistan fell victim to unidentified gunmen. According to Russian military sources, a 21-year-old Russian lieutenant was riding in a car together with three civilians from the place of his duty to Dushanbe when the car was fired upon from automatic weapons by a group of armed people in a border area on the night of February 17 to 18. The Russian officer and two civilians were shot to death and the third civilian was heavily wounded and rushed to a Dushanbe clinic.
