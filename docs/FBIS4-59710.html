BFN [Text] Labor ministers today decided to pass control of the Histadrut labor federation to Ramon's list in an orderly and dignified way. Contacts on this issue will commence on Tuesday [17 May]. In a meeting of Labor ministers today, Prime Minister Rabin said the transfer of power must be completed quickly in order to prevent a feeling of procrastination. Our party affairs correspondent Shmu'el Tal reports that Minister Liba'i proposed to remove Hayim Haberfeld and Nisim Zvili from the negotiations team. Minister Qeysar reacted angrily to this proposal. Minister Ora Namir also said that Haberfeld should not conduct these negotiations. Minister Ben-Eli'ezer said the elections proved that the age of ideology is over, and the only thing which remains now is the media. Foreign Minister Peres said the elections do not merely constitute a defeat for the Labor Party, but also signal the end of the Histadrut.
