March 2, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

IMMIGRANT AID

In your article you discussed the new wave of Soviet immigrants coming to settle in Los Angeles (Part I, Feb. 19). The nonprofit agency which I direct, United Community and Housing Development Corp., has been actually involved in placing both Soviet Jewish and Armenian immigrants in "on-the-job-training" positions where they can transform their skills, background, and training to the American way of being employed.

We also have offered "English as a second language," classes on a technical level.

Both of these programs are funded through the Los Angeles Community Development Department. Our new location for these services is 5455 Wilshire Blvd., Suite 2100, and the telephone number is (213) 936-6101.

STANLEY TREITEL

Executive Director

Los Angeles
