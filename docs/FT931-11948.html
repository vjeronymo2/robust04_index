930130 FT 30 JAN 93 / Chinese invited to Taiwan Taiwan will invite mainland Chinese trade officials to a meeting next month under the auspices of the Asia Pacific Economic Co-operation (APEC) forum, the Board of Foreign Trade said yesterday, reports Reuter from Taipei. They would be the first Chinese officials to make a formal visit to Taiwan since the end of the Chinese civil war in 1949, said Mr Peter Ho, a section chief at the board. However, he said the three Chinese participants would not hold bilateral talks with Taiwanese officials. They would be invited to attend a training course on the development of small and medium-sized enterprises in Taipei from February 27 to March 2.
