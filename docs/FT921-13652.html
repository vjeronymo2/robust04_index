920121 FT 21 JAN 92 / VTech reduces debt with sale of stock VTech, the Hong Kong-based, Bermuda-registered, electronics, computers and telecommunications group which came to the market in October 1991, has sold 2.24m shares of common stock in CPS, a US software house, for Dollars 19m (Pounds 10.6m) cash. The disposal reduces VTech's holding in CPS from 34.5 per cent to 21.5 per cent. CPS specialises in the development of personal computer software utilities. In the year to end-March 1991 it made a pre-tax profit of Dollars 12.6m from total revenues of Dollars 53.8m and had net assets of Dollars 12.9m. Proceeds of the sale will be used to reduce VTech's debt.
