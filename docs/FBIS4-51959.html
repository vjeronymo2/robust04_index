BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 5 (KCNA) -- Over 2,000 students of 40 universities and colleges in Seoul including Seoul National University, Yonsei University and Korea University had the inaugural ceremony of the seventh-term Seoul District Federation of General Student Councils (Sochongnyon) at Hongik University on May 4 and staged a demonstration calling for an objection to treacherous rice market opening and U.S. troops' withdrawal, according to a radio report from Seoul. They said that the Kim Yong-sam puppet clique yielded to the U.S. pressure to open market so the lives of 10 million peasants and even workers are being threatened. The students charged that the United States continues to threaten the North with a nuclear war and increase the war atmosphere on the Korean peninsula. They demanded that authorities refuse a "ratification of the final act of the Uruguay Round of world trade talks at the National Assembly," object to rice import liberalization and realise the U.S. troops' pullout and denuclearisation and disarmament and signing of peace agreement.
