L. 92&hyph;463, as amended), the National Science Foundation announces the following meeting: Name and Committee Code: Special Emphasis Panel in Geosciences (1756). Date and Time: February 7&hyph;8, 1994; 8:30 a.m. to 5 p.m. Place: Room 370, National Science Foundation, 4201 Wilson Boulevard, Arlington, VA. Type of Meeting: Closed.
