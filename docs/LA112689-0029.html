November 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

SEX AND IQ

The Constitution guarantees "life, liberty and the pursuit of happiness" to all citizens, not just those who are "normal" and thereby declare themselves non-handicapped and in some way superior.

The mind-set that by virtue of a three-digit IQ we are dominant beings elected to oversee selective reproduction is not unlike the reasoning some half-century ago when only the blond and blue-eyed were to be allowed to procreate.

As the writers of "L.A. Law" are so eloquently attempting to illustrate, the mentally retarded can be quite capable of making decisions regarding responsible sexual behavior if they are afforded the simple luxury of an unbiased education.

Happily, some of my favorite people are dark-eyed. And some are mentally retarded.

KATE CAHILL

Palm Springs
