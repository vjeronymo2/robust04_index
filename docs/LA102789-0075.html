October 27, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

BAY AREA QUAKE;

EARTHQUAKE FATALITIES LISTED

As of 4 p.m. Thursday, the following had been listed by coroners' offices in Bay Area counties as killed in the earthquake. Names were released after next of kin were notified. Thirty-nine bodies were recovered in Alameda County, 12 in San Francisco, six in Santa Cruz County, five in Santa Clara County and one in Monterey County.
