Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Mohamed Betchine has been appointed minister adviser to the head of state. Mohamed Betchine was born on 28 November 1934 in Constantine. He was a member of the National Liberation Army and a retired brigadier in the National Popular Army. He is a member of the National Council of Mujahidin. Betchine holds a diploma from the national school of artillery and the multi-weaponry high school in Russia. He pursued graduate research in law and occupied a number of positions in the National Popular Army until his retirement in August 1990. He is married and the father of six children.
