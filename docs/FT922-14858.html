920403 FT 03 APR 92 / International Company News: Sita launches services group AN international air transport consortium is launching a commercial telecommunications services company to provide value-added network services to industries ranging from leisure to financial services, writes Michiyo Nakamoto. The Socie'te' Internationale de Te'le'communications Aeronautiques (Sita), a non-profit-making organisation of 460 companies from the airline, aerospace and related industries, is investing about Dollars 20m over the next four to five years in Scitor, a wholly owned UK-based subsidiary. Scitor, which will provide services, such as reservation systems for hotels, aims for revenues of Dollars 100m by 1998. It has won orders worth Dollars 40m from 250 companies including Hilton International and Budget Rent A Car.
