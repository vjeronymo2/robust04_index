BFN [Text] Wahil Nufal, an 18-year-old resident of Dayr Sharaf, a village near Nabulus, was fatally shot in the chest this afternoon. Villagers told our correspondent Moti Amir that Nufal had been shot by settlers firing at stonethrowers near the village. The security forces are investigating the circumstances of the shooting. Two stonethrowers were shot and wounded by IDF [Israel Defense Forces] gunfire in the Nur al-Shams refugee camp. In Shuwaykha, a youth was also wounded when IDF soldiers shot at stonethrowers.
