Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 3 Mar (MENA)--A small explosive device went off at around 1820 [1620 GMT] this evening at the bottom of the stairs leading to the Egyptian-American Bank on al-Batal Ahmad 'Abd-al-'Aziz Street in al-Muhandisin district [of Cairo]. The explosion caused minor damage to some windows and the entrance stairs. No one was injured. Security men from the al-Jizah Security Department and forensic experts went to the scene of the crime to collect evidence at the site and the prosecution office has started investigations.
