December 7, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Seagram Co.: The Montreal-based beverage company's third-quarter net earnings increased 19% from a year ago to $169.5 million. Sales were up 4% to $1.4 billion, giving the company a nine-month record sales total of $3.9 billion. Seagram officials attributed the higher earnings figures to increased beverage profitability in all markets -- international, United States and Canada. Seagram produces distilled spirits, wines, coolers, juices and soft drinks.
