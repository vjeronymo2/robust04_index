BFN [Text] The negotiations between a Croatian delegation and representatives of the Serb authorities from the UN-protected areas, scheduled to resume tomorrow, will not materialize. Nobody wants to make any guesses about the reasons behind the postponement of the negotiations or about a possible new date when they could take place. Slavko Degoricija, member of the Croatian delegation, said that the Croatian delegation is ready for the next round of talks and stressed that the resumption of the talks depends on the decision of the Serb representatives. Economic and social issues were to be discussed in this round of the negotiations.
