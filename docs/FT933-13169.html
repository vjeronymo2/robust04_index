930721 FT 21 JUL 93 / UN rejects Russia's claim to the Ukrainian port of Sevastopol The United Nations Security Council yesterday rejected the Russian parliament's claim to the Ukrainian port of Sevastopol, saying it was incompatible with the UN charter, Reuter reports from New York. The UN said it recognised statements by President Boris Yeltsin and Russia's foreign ministry that distanced themselves from the parliament.
