BFN [Unattributed report: "CDU Overtakes SPD"] [Text] Bonn -- With 42 percent (last week: 39 percent), the Christian Democratic Union [CDU]/Christian Social Union [CSU] has for the first time overtaken the Social Democratic Party of Germany [SPD] with 39 percent (40 percent). The Free Democratic Party of Germany [FDP] gains 4 percent (6 percent), the Alliance 90/Greens 10 percent (8 percent), the Republikaner 1 percent (3 percent), and the Party of Democratic Socialism [PDS] 2 percent, again. Thirty-five percent (32 percent) want Kohl as chancellor and, as before, 32 percent want SPD candidate Scharping (Forsa poll).
