930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Norbain shares leap on doubled profit Shares in USM-traded Norbain Electronics jumped 19p to 158p yesterday as the security products group announced doubled profits and a 50 per cent lift in dividend for the year to April 30. Sales increased 71 per cent to Pounds 21.8m (Pounds 12.8m); of the rise 42 per cent came from the original business and 29 per cent from acquisitions. Pre-tax profit worked through at Pounds 1.01m (Pounds 502,000) and earnings were 12.99p (5.7p) - of which 3.46p could be attributed to release of tax provision. The dividend is 1.5p (1p).
