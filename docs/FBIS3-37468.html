Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] British Prime Minister John Major arrived in Nizhniy Novgorod, a regional center on the upper Volga, on a one day visit from Moscow. His party was welcomed by Boris Nemtsov, head of the local administration. He informed journalists in the airport that Russia will be visited by Queen Elizabeth II of Britain. Major visited Progress, a car service which was among Russia's first joint stock societies. He will visit the local privatization voucher exchange and the Nizhniy Novgorod Fair joint stock society.
