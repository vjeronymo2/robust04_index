May 17, 1990, Thursday, South Bay Edition

AVIVA CENTER SEEKING POSSIBLE FOSTER PARENTS

AVIVA Center, a licensed nonprofit agency serving troubled children and their families, is seeking foster parents.

An information session will be held at the center, 1701 Camino Palmero, west of La Brea Boulevard and north of Franklin Avenue, Los Angeles, at 7 p.m. May 30. For more information, call 876-0550.

AVIVA Center, formerly known as Hamburger Home, was incorporated in 1915.
