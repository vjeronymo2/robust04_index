BFN [Text] The Knesset Finance Committee this morning approved a special budget of 19 million shekels to pay compensation to the victims of the Cave of the Patriarchs massacre and their relatives. Our correspondent Hayim Hecht reports that 5 million shekels were allocated to pay for the expenses of the state commission of inquiry and to pay some of the expenses of the hospitals that treated the victims. The sums were approved unanimously.
