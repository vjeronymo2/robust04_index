930918 FT 18 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Magnolia looks abroad for growth Magnolia Group, the picture frame and reproductions company, raised pre-tax profits from Pounds 52,000 to Pounds 76,000 in the first half of 1993. Turnover fell from Pounds 9.47m to Pounds 9.05m. The company said the UK remained quiet but exports showed an improvement of 150 per cent. The result was helped by lower interest charges of Pounds 93,000 (Pounds 108,000). Gearing at the period end was 18 per cent. Earnings per share were 0.92p (0.61p). The interim dividend is again being passed.
