Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Simferopol, 21 Jan -- In a statement issued to the press, Yuriy Meshkov, the chairman of the Republican Party of the Crimea [RPK] and Crimean presidential candidate, emphasized in particular that President Kravchuk had his confidence, since he had not had any objections to holding the elections on the peninsula and apparently did not consider holding them to be illegal. Therefore, the RPK leader is hopeful he will be able to come to an agreement with President Kravchuk and develop relations between the Crimea and Ukraine in accordance with current legislation and the norms of international law. In the event of his election as president, Yuriy Meshkov added, one should not fear that interethnic relations would exacerbate.
