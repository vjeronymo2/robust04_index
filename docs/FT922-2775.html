920615 FT 15 JUN 92 / International Company News: Sappi completes equity placing SAPPI, the South African pulp and paper group, has successfully completed its Dollars 268m Euro-equity placing, the largest such offer by a South African company since the lifting of sanctions against the country, writes Philip Gawith in Johannesburg. The proceeds will be used to finance Sappi's purchase of Hannover Papier, the leading German manufacturer of coated paper.
