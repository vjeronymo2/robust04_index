DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE Bureau of the Census [Docket No. 941251&hyph;4351] 1994 Company Organization Survey AGENCY: Bureau of the Census, Commerce. ACTION: Notice of Determination.

SUMMARY: In conformity with Title 13, United States Code, Sections 182, 224, and 225, I have determined that a 1994 Company Organization Survey is needed to update the multiestablishment companies in the Standard Statistical Establishment List. The survey, which has been conducted for many years, is designed to collect information on the number of employees, payrolls, geographic location, current status, and kind of business for the establishments of multiestablishment companies. These data will have significant application to the needs of the public and to governmental agencies and are not publicly available from nongovernmental or governmental sources. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Edward D. Walker on (301) 457&hyph;2617. SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: The data collected in this survey will be within the general scope, type, and character of those that are covered in the economic censuses. The Office of Management and Budget approved the proposed survey November 17, 1994 under Control No. 0607&hyph;0444 in accordance with the Paperwork Reduction Act, Public Law 96&hyph;511, as amended. Report forms will be furnished to organizations included in the survey, and additional copies of the forms are available on request to the Director, Bureau of the Census, Washington, DC 20233&hyph;0101. I have, therefore, directed that a survey be conducted for the purpose of collecting these data. Dated: December 22, 1994. Martha Farnsworth Riche, Director, Bureau of the Census.
