Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow, 3 Mar--Prospects for and the state of military and technical cooperation were discussed today at a meeting between Andrey Kokoshin, Russian first deputy defense minister, and Dao Dinh Luyen, chief of Staff of the Vietnamese People's Army. An ITAR-TASS correspondent was informed at the Directorate for Information and Press of the Defense Department that issues of the maintenance, modernization, and production of military equipment were discussed at the meeting of the military leaders of Russia and Vietnam. It is quite possible that an agreement on bilateral military and technical cooperation will be signed in the very near future. Dao Dinh Luyen arrived in Russia on Wednesday on an official visit.
