941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Budget 94 (Their Budget): Unemployed man Peter Chapman, 39: design engineer made redundant after 23 years at Tyneside shipbuilder Swan Hunter with a pay off of just over Pounds 3,500. Children aged 13 and 14. Earns: Total weekly family income is Pounds 183. Politics: Labour party member. Said yesterday: 'He hasn't done anything to address the issues I'd have liked. He's attacking the benefits on one side, with the change on mortgage interest for people on income support and encouraging a low paid economy on the other; it isn't beneficial for anybody.'
