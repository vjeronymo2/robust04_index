930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / BA extends its no-smoking rule BRITISH AIRWAYS is to ban smoking on flights of 90 minutes or less from September 27. The airline said the policy would affect more than 400 flights a week to and from destinations such as Paris, Amsterdam and Brussels. Smoking has been banned on all internal flights since 1988. The airline has banned smoking on one of its two daily 14-hour services to Hong Kong. Non-smoking trials are to start next month on Los Angeles and San Francisco services.
