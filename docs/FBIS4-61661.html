BFN [Text] Comrat, BASAPRESS, 5/5/1994 -- Yesterday, Ambassador Umut Arik, chairman of the International Cooperation Agency of the Turkish Ministry of Foreign Affairs, and Ender Arat, Turkish general consul to Chisinau, met with Stepan Topal, president of the self-proclaimed Gagauz Republic. Andrei Ibrisim, Gagauz presidential spokesman, stated to BASA that economic issues, in particular the possibility of investments in the region made by Turkish businessmen, were analyzed during the meeting. The possibility of implementing a telephone network in southern Moldova was also discussed. The "Teletas" Turkish firm is willing to grant for this purpose $9,000,000 and another $6,000,000 for creating a telephone station in Chisinau. The possibility of constructing a tile factory in the region was also discussed. The Turkish diplomats also met with representatives of the Comrat Government and Supreme Soviet. Arik also visited Comrat TV staff and promised it technical and financial support, said Ibrisim.
