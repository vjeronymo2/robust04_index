BFN [Text] Cairo, 7 Jun (MENA) -- UN Secretary General Dr. Butrus Butrus-Ghali has expressed his hope that UN envoy to Yemen al-Akhdar Ibrahimi will succeed in convincing the Yemeni parties to implement the Security Council resolution on the Yemeni crisis. In an interview with the Voice of Arabs Radio today, he described the Security Council resolution on the crisis in Yemen as a positive step. He emphasized that if each side insists on its stand, the issue may be put forth to the Security Council again for the necessary decisions.
