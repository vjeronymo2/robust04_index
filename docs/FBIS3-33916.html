Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Khartoum, 1 Feb (SUNA) -- The Transitional National Assembly (TNA), chaired by Muhammad al-Amin Khalifah, Monday approved the bill on the tenth constitutional decree on the states' redivision for 1994 at the second reading stage. The decree, which stipulates increasing the number of states in the Sudan to 26 states, was approved by TNA at the second reading stage in accordance with a report presented to the assembly by the legal affairs and decentralization committees. A number of MPs spoke at the session, expressing their unanimous support for the decree, which they affirm has come to fulfill the desires of the Sudanese masses.
