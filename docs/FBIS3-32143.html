Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] A new Cuban bank is now operating in this capital. The International Trade Bank, Incorporated [Banco Internacional de Comercio, S.A.], was opened to provide services to industrial and commercial clients, as well as to the general public. Jose (Faz), president of the bank, said that the operating permit granted embraces all kinds of operations, although the bank is interested in developing trade in merchandise, services, and currencies. Importance is also given to checking account services and safe-deposit boxes, which are in great demand by the general public.
