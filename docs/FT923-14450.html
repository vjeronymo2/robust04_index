920708 FT 08 JUL 92 / Investment in Romania up DIRECT foreign investment in Romania rose by Dollars 118m (Pounds 62m) in the first half of 1992, bringing the total since January 1990 to Dollars 387m, according to figures released yesterday by the Romanian Development Agency. Foreign investment in the first half of this year more than doubled compared to the corresponding period in 1991. France, Germany and Britain were the top three investors.
