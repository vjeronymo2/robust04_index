BFN [Text] Ichiro Ozawa, Shinseito [Japan Renewal Party] secretary general, said that Japan should work flexibly on measures to break the deadlock in the Japanese-U.S. economic framework talks. He noted that it may be possible for Japan to set import targets as long as these are not linked to U.S. sanctions. In a 4 April news conference at the Diet, Ozawa said, "I think that Japan should take the initiative in improving its market practices so that these are not criticized by the international community."
