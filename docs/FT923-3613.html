920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / World Trade News: Hewlett-Packard in deal with Samsung Hewlett-Packard has expanded its alliance with Samsung Electronics of Korea with a Dollars 50m computer workstation deal, writes Louise Kehoe in San Francisco. Samsung will resell Hewlett-Packard workstations in South Korea under its own and Hewlett's name, as well as using them internally.
