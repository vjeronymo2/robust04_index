April 29, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

A CASE OF TWO PLAYERS IN THE WRONG PLACE WITH THE WRONG SKIN?

I can't believe that Mal Florence and Bob Oates came up with racism as one of the possibilities for Rodney Peete being drafted so late.

In a sport dominated by black players, what kind of sense does that make? It just may be that the people who pay out all those big bucks are better qualified to judge athletic ability than many sports writers.

W.G. PEW

Santa Maria
