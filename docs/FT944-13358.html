941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / International Company News: AIG registers steady growth Earnings at American International Group, the US insurer, advanced by more than a fifth in the third quarter, reflecting its international expansion in both the life and general insurance areas, writes Richard Waters. Excluding accounting adjustments and capital gains, group net income jumped 22 per cent to Dollars 532.3m from a year ago, on revenues up 11 per cent to Dollars 5.7bn. In general insurance, operating income (before capital gains) was up 34 per cent.
