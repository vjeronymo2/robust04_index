BFN [Text] Beijing, April 28 (XINHUA) -- Ending his official visit to Canada on 27 April, Chinese Vice Premier Zou Jiahua made a special trip to California, United States, Yorba Linda City to attend former U.S. President Nixon's funeral on behalf of the Chinese Government. Zou Jiahua presented a wreath at Nixon's coffin with characters written on the attached satin strap that read "We Deeply Mourn Mr. Richard Nixon [shen qie dao nian li cha de ni ke song xian sheng 3234 0434 1902 1819 3810 2686 1795 1441 0344 2646 0341 3932]." Expressing his heartfelt condolences over Nixon's death at the funeral, Zou Jiahua said: Nixon is an old friend of the Chinese people; we will never forget the positive efforts and contributions he made in establishing and developing Sino-American relations. Vice Foreign Minister Liu Huaqiu and Chinese Ambassador to the United States Li Daoyu also attended the funeral.
