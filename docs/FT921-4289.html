920310 FT 10 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Allied-Lyons sells 40 pubs to Greenalls Allied-Lyons, the drinks, food and retailing group, has agreed to sell 40 pubs to Greenalls Group, the north-west pubs and hotels operator, for Pounds 10.15m. Allied's existing agreement to supply ale and lager to the Greenalls estate will be extended to include the additional pubs. Allied will continue to brew Greenalls brands, such as Greenalls bitter, as well as the Davenport and Shipstones brands.
