Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vilnius, Jan 18, BNS -- Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas said he is greatly satisfied with the arrest of Mykolas Burokevicius and Juozas Jarmalavicius, the believed organizers of a failed coup attempt in Lithuania in January 1991. The two were detained in Minsk Saturday [15 January]. Brazauskas expressed gratitude to the Belarussian Government and special services for extraditing the alleged state criminals who are accused of high treason and complicity in aggravated murder. As a result of a military aggression in Vilnius Jan. 13, 1991, 13 civilians were killed and around 700 injured. The Lithuanian president expressed hope that the investigation of the January 13th case will be fast and smooth and that "justice will finally be restored."
