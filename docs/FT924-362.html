921224 FT 24 DEC 92 / Finance and the Family: USM threat - At a glance The Stock Exchange recommended this week that its junior tier, the Unlisted Securities Market, should close at the end of 1995. The USM was founded in 1980 and, as the graph shows, enjoyed mixed fortunes in the early 1980s. Then, in 1987, an economic boom coincided with a period when small company shares were the darlings of the stock market; the USM index more than doubled in months. Since the Crash, the recession and the illiquidity of many small stocks have taken their toll.
