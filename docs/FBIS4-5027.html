BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 7 -- Vietnamese Minister of National Defence General Doan Khue received here yesterday a delegation of the institute of the high-ranking Military Research of France led by General Francois Bresson on a working visit to Vietnam. Also present at the reception were deputy general staff of the Vietnam People's Army Lieut. Nguyen The Bon, and director of the high-ranking Military Institute Senior Lieut. General Nguyen Huu An. French ambassador to Vietnam Jean-Francois Nugarede and military attache Lt. Col Daniel Schaeffer were also present. During the reception, General Doan Khue expressed his hope that the delegation's visit will contribute to the promotion of the mutual understanding and cooperation between the two countries. General Francois Bresson, for his part, expressed his admiration at the achievements recorded by the Vietnamese people and armed forces in their national construction. While in Vietnam the French military delegation was informed of Vietnam's polices on military, foreign and economics and investment affairs.
