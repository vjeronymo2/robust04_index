July 14, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: NAMES IN THE NEWS

Dick Butkus, a Hall of Fame linebacker formerly of the Chicago Bears, and sportscaster Dick Enberg will be honored at the fourth annual Sports Spectacular Dinner, to be held at the Century Plaza Hotel Sunday. Butkus will receive the Lifetime Achievement Award for his contributions to football. Enberg will be honored as the Sports Journalist of the Year. Proceeds from the dinner will benefit the Cedars-Sinai Medical Genetics-Birth Defects Center.
