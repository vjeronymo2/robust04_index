April 25, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BUCHANAN ON MANIFEST DESTINY

Is Buchanan insane, or is he just blind and arrogant? I thought the concept of Manifest Destiny was long dead and buried next to its primary victims -- Native Americans, Hispanics, Hawaiians and Filipinos.

What Buchanan suggests contradicts Canadian popular opinion and would amount to a shotgun wedding. The best way to antagonize otherwise friendly Canadians is to suggest, like an insensitive boob, that "pieces" of Canada ought to be part of the United States.

FRED H. LOBB

El Monte
