Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Bulgarian delegation led by Finance Minister Stoyan Aleksandrov and the creditor banks of the so-called London Club, represented by Deutsche Bank, have just ended their two days of talks in Frankfurt. Here is our colleague Aleksandur Andreev of Deutsche Welle with a brief report on the talks supplied by Deutsche Bank. [Begin Andreev recording] The published joint declaration states that the talks were held on the basis of the interim agreement reached in November 1993 and that progress was achieved in the technical details concerning the rescheduling and repayment of the Bulgarian foreign debt. The contracting parties also reached agreement on the time schedule for finally completing the documentation and concluding the negotiations on the restructuring of the Bulgarian foreign debt, which is expected to be completed before the end of June next year. [end recording]
