Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] By decree of President Zhelev, Colonel Valentin Nikolov Popinski was dismissed today from the post of chief of the Defense Ministry's Department for Supplies and Trade. Col. Popinski was said to have committed gross violations in connection with poor control of the fulfillment of contracts and had signed contracts unilaterally, to the detriment of the Defense Ministry. According to the Defense Ministry announcement, for the same reasons Defense Minister Valentin Aleksandrov has ordered the dismissal from active military service on disciplinary grounds of Colonel Engineer Stoyan Tsvetkov Tsakov, deputy chief of the Department for Supplies and Trade.
