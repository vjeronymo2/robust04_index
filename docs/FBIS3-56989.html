Language: Russian Article Type:BFN ["Own information" report from "From Our Correspondents and News Agencies" column: "Russian Gas Deliveries to East Germany To Be Continued"] [Text] The conflict between "Gazprom" and its German partner "Wintershall" on the one hand and the "Verbundnetz Gaz" joint-stock company on the other, which IZVESTIYA reported in its previous issue, has by all accounts been settled. INTERFAX has reported that "Wintershall" and "Verbundnetz Gaz" reported Thursday [20 January] that they had managed to reach a last-minute compromise on the price of Russian gas and thereby avert the termination of deliveries of it to the FRG's eastern Laender. It was announced that this month the sides will sign a long-term contract for gas deliveries from Russia.
