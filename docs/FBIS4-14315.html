BFN [Text] The House of Representatives held an extraordinary meeting this morning under the chairmanship of Shaykh 'Abdallah Bin-Husayn al-Ahmar, the House speaker. Discussions centered on developments resulting from the country's political crisis, the latest of which are the events that took place in 'Imran. The House told its committee, which includes members of the Defense and Security Committee, as well as a number of deputies, to immediately begin a fact-finding mission in 'Imran and other problem areas, and to present a comprehensive and urgent report to the House. The House heard a number of proposals on these developments and assigned the House speakership and leaders of parliamentary blocs to issue a House statement in light of proposals and ideas that were presented at the meeting. We will broadcast the House statement later this evening. The House of Representatives will continue its meetings next Monday, God willing.
