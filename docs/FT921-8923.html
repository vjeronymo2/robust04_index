920214 FT 14 FEB 92 / Drexel sues ex-employees THE bankrupt US investment company, Drexel Burnham Lambert, yesterday sued two of its former senior executives, jailed for their role in the insider-trading scandal of the 1980s, Reuter reports from New York. Drexel filed suit against Mr Martin Siegel and Mr Dennis Levine, for unspecified damages. Drexel said it had suffered enormous losses because of their illegal activity.
