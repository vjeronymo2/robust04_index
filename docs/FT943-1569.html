940923 FT 23 SEP 94 / Observer: Toothless More whispers that Kenneth Baker, former UK cabinet minister, is poised to return to government. The Beast of Bodmin, a large cat-like creature, is still supposedly roaming the west of England. So the reasoning is that Baker, as home secretary at the time of the Dangerous Dogs Act 1991, is just the man to deal with dangerous beasts - particularly a sheep in wolf's clothing.
