930618 FT 18 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Tighter margins hit Dart TIGHTER MARGINS in avia-tion services and the cost of introducing Electra freighters into European service hit the profit of Dart Group. In the year ended March 31 1993 it lifted turnover from Pounds 37.4m to Pounds 42.8m, but saw pre-tax profit fall from Pounds 1.88m to Pounds 1.66m. The introduction costs would be fully depreciated during the present year, Mr Philip Meeson, chairman, assured shareholders. Earnings per share came to 7.5p (9.4p). The final dividend is 2p, for a maintained 3.3p total. For the current year there were indications of improved performance, and trading so far was encouraging, said Mr Meeson.
