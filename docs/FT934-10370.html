931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / UK Company News: German Smaller net assets rise Undiluted net asset value per ordinary share of the German Smaller Companies Investment Trust stood at 236.8p at end-September. That compared with 213.6p 12 months earlier and with 229.4p at the March 31 year-end. Available revenue for the half year to September 30 declined from Pounds 448,000 to Pounds 303,000, equal to earnings of 1.69p (2.49p). The trust does not pay interim dividends but intends to recommend a final - 2p was paid previously.
