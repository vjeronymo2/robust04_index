October 15, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

GREENBERG WITHDRAWAL

I have developed a serious addiction, and this week was in danger of going into severe withdrawal. Where was Peter Greenberg's column? I so look forward to his wit and insight every Sunday morning, and slink sadly away from my Times Travel Section when he's not there. I hope this was only a rare oversight. But since his column was missing several weeks ago as well, I felt it warranted a protest.

MEG THIDWICK, Los Angeles
