940831 FT 31 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Strong second half leaves Verity at Pounds 1m After a strong second half which consolidated its return to the black at the interim stage, Verity, the manufacturer and distributor of Wharfedale and Mission loudspeakers, reported a pre-tax profit of Pounds 1.01m for the year to end-June. The outcome compared with a deficit of Pounds 4.27m and was achieved on turnover of Pounds 24.1m (Pounds 16.2m). The directors attributed the 'encouraging result' to a turnround in Wharfedale's lossmaking operations and the continuing growth of Mission. The interest charge was reduced to Pounds 379,000 (Pounds 870,000). Earnings per share emerged at 0.4p (5p losses).
