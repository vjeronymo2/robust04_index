940621 FT 21 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Eidos cuts annual loss to Pounds 77,000 Eidos, the USM-quoted developer of video compression and decompression software, has announced that arrangements for its proposed Pounds 582,000 1-for-10 rights issue had been completed. Dealings in the rights nil paid commenced yesterday. The announcement coincided with publication of the company's results for the year to end-December 1993, when pre-tax losses were cut from Pounds 190,449 to Pounds 76,928 on turnover up from Pounds 110,200 to Pounds 130,872. Net interest payable and similar charges totalled Pounds 2,246, against income of Pounds 3,550 last time. Losses per share worked through at 3.38p (8.66p). The directors said the net proceeds of Pounds 517,000 from the rights issue would be used first to repay the company's outstanding borrowings of Pounds 42,500 with the balance being used to fund research and development and marketing plans.
