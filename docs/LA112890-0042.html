November 28, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Vanilli Veracity: Three singers whose voices have already found fame behind the faces of disgraced pop duo Milli Vanilli plan to launch their own group.

At a news conference in Frankfurt on Monday, album producer Frank Farian said singers Brad Howell, John Davis and Gina Mohammed will record a new album together with a fourth group member, Ray Horton. Farian will manage the new group. Howell and Davis were the voices on the hit album, "Girl You Know It's True." Mohammed sang the background vocals. A name for the group had not yet been chosen. BETH KLEID
