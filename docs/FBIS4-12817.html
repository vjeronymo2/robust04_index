BFN [Text] Islamabad, April 13, IRNA -- The visiting Iranian Majlis speaker, 'Ali Akbar Nateq-Nuri, arrived in the city of Lahore in Punjab Province Wednesday and was warmly welcomed by the local Muslim people and ulema. Enthusisastic people chanting 'Death to America' and 'Death to Israel' lined up from the airport to the city to receive the Iranian Majlis speaker. According to an official at Iranian's culture center in Lahore, people from various cities of Punjab had arrived in the city and spent the night in religious places as well as at the culture center to meet Hojjat ol-Eslam Nateq-Nuri. One of the officials in charge of the welcoming ceremonies told IRNA that Lahore was rather spending a holiday today so that people could take part in the ceremonies. An Iranian student in Lahore said that such an unprecedented welcome by the people had been only given to the leader of the Islamic revolution Ayatollah Khamene'i and to President Rafsanjani. Upon his arrival to the city, Nateq-Nuri visited the tomb of Pakistan's great Muslim poet, Iqbal Lahori, where he laid a wreath of flowers. The Majlis speaker arrived in Pakistan Sunday and has so far held talks with high-ranking Pakistani officials including President Faruq Ahmad Leghari, Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto, heads of parliament, Shiite and Sunni Muslim ulema, and Afghan leaders. The Iranian delegation will later leave Lahore for Karachi.
