Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 2 (KCNA) -- Famous works of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il have been disseminated and studied in different countries. A seminar on his work "Abuses of Socialism are Intolerable" was held by the British group for the study of the revolutionary activities and works of Comrade Kim Il-song. Chief of the group Dermot Hudson in his report stressed: This work of Comrade Kim Chong-il is a militant banner for frustrating the anti-socialist strategy of the imperialists and defending and advancing the cause of socialism. He in his work lays bare the falsehood and reactionary nature of the anti-socialist vile propaganda made by the imperialists and reactionaries. Comrade Kim Chong-il has developed and enriched the idea and theory on socialism and brought the essential superiority of socialism to capitalism into full play in Korea, so that mankind may confidently advance along the road of socialism with a beacon of hope. Under his wise leadership the cause of socialism will make a long drive. Seminars on the same work were held by the group for the study of the chuche idea of the Marien Ngouabi University of the Congo, chuche idea study groups of Guyana and Calcutta University of India. Seminars on his work "Our Socialism Centred on the Masses Shall Not Perish" were held by the committee for the study of Kimilsongism of youth in Karachi, Pakistan, the Murewa, Zimbabwe, group for the study of the chuche idea and the group for the study of the chuche idea of the Administration and Development College in Mjumbe, Morogoro, Tanzania and a seminar on his work "The Historical Lesson in Building Socialism and the General Line of our Party" by the Uttar Pradesh State, India, committee for the study of the chuche idea.
