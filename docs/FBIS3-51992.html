Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Guatemala City, 28 Jan (ACAN-EFE) -- A group of unidentified individuals fired at the U.S. Embassy in Guatemala City on Thursday shortly before midnight while almost the entire city was without electricity because three high tension towers were blown up, the Guatemalan police reported today. A spokesman for the security forces told ACAN-EFE that unidentified persons inside a vehicle fired at the diplomatic mission as they drove by. No one was injured, and there was no property damage. Embassy guards did not repel the attack, which took place at around 2330 (0530 GMT Friday), when the city was still without power as a result of three downed high tension towers in different areas of the outskirts of the city. No organization has claimed responsibility for the attack.
