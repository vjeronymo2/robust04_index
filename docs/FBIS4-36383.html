BFN [Text] Under the patronage of President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali and at the invitation of the Tunisian radio and television company, the 17th session of the General Assembly of the Arab Broadcasting Union convened in Tunis today. The meeting was chaired by Foreign Minister Habib Ben Yahia who made a speech in which he conveyed to participants greetings from President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali and his good wishes for the success of the meeting. He stressed that in the Tunisia of the new era, proceeding from awareness of the importance of joint inter-Arab action, we are seeking to host any inter-Arab activity, particularly those aimed at finding ways of fruitful cooperation and entrenching concord between the Arab states. It should be underscored that at its morning meeting the General Assembly of the Arab Broadcasting Union elected Abdelhafidh El Hargem, director-general of the Tunisian radio and television company, as chairman of the Union and of its Executive Bureau.
