940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Feedback declines to Pounds 722,000 A disappointing year at two of its subsidiaries resulted in lower profits at Feedback, the USM-quoted designer and maker of electronic, electrical and computer-based equipment. Sales overall for the year to end-March fell from Pounds 9.84m to Pounds 9.46m and the pre-tax result was Pounds 338,000 down at Pounds 722,000. Earnings per share were 5.74p (7.66p). A final dividend of 1p is recommended, making an unchanged total of 1.5p.
