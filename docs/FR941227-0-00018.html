Sections 1735.3, 1737.3 are amended by adding a sentence at the end of each to read as follows: *␣*␣* The terms ``RUS form'', ``RUS standard form'', and ``RUS specification'' have the same meanings as the terms ``REA form'' ``REA standard form'', and ``REA specification'', respectively, unless otherwise indicated. §1786.51 [Amended] 30.
