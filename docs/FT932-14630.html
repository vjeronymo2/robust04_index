930417 FT 17 APR 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazil SAO PAULO rose another 2.7 per cent at midsession, following a 9 per cent rise in the Bovespa index earlier in the week. Investors welcomed news that the head of the privatisation commission was to stay in his post. Equities have been active ahead of Monday's options settlement as well as on fears that the government might introduce an anti-inflation package. Inflation currently stands at 26 per cent a month.
