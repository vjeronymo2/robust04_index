June 17, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

RECORD RACK: IN BRIEF:;

*** EN VOGUE, "BORN TO SING," ATLANTIC.: ALBUMS ARE RATED FROM FIVE STARS (A CLASSIC) TO ONE STAR (POOR).

Unlike most female groups, these four R&B singers can really sing -- and do some passable rapping too.

Best known for the hit single "Hold on," En Vogue, on its debut album, admirably performs a batch of mostly well-crafted ballads and medium-tempo songs. Grating, dissonant vocal blends do spoil a few otherwise strong songs. The ingeniously arranged "Time Goes On" is the class of this collection. DENNIS HUNT
