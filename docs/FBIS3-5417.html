Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 7 (KCNA) -- Marshal of the Korean People's Army O Chin-u, Minister of People's Armed Forces, sent a message of greetings to Burundian Defence Minister Gedeon Fyiroko on the occasion of the day of the Burundian Army. Expressing the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two peoples and Armies would continue to develop in the future, the message wished him success in his responsible work for strengthening the defence power.
