March 29, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Perrier Switches Ad Agencies: Perrier Group fired its ad firm, then rehired its former agency to create ads for the Perrier Sparkling Water brand. San Francisco-based Hal Riney & Partners lost the estimated $8-million to $10-million annual business to Waring & La Rosa, the same New York agency that lost the business to Riney in 1986. Riney will still handle four other Perrier brands, including Arrowhead and Calistoga.
