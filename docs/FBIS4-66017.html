BFN [Text] Ankara, May 26 (AA) -- Interior Minister Nahit Mentese said on Thursday [26 May] that the emigration of civilians from eastern and southeastern Anatolia to northern Iraq is another tactic of the separatist terrorist organization PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan]. Speaking at parliament's general council Mentese refused the claims that the government was pursuing those emigrating to northern Iraq. `The PKK called these people there to provide international support and to make propaganda but still Turkey calls these people back,' Mentese said. He said the PKK initiated all the incidents in eastern and southeastern Anatolia and massacred children, women and old people there.
