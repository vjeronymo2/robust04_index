920218 FT 18 FEB 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There were 17,827 packages for the day, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. There was a much better demand for both landed and offshore teas. Brighter east Africans met good competition and closed 5-10p up. Medium and lesser sorts were also well supported with prices firm to 4p higher. Brighter Ceylons again advanced. Offshore a strong demand prevailed. East Africans were 4-8p dearer, while central African and Mauritius teas saw much improved enquiry at firm to dearer rates. The highest price realised this week was 165p for a Rwanda pf. 1. Quotations: quality 150p, good medium 110p, medium 90p, low medium 78p.
