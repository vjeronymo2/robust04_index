November 21, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS: VASWANI NAMED VICE PRESIDENT OF ROCKWELL UNIT

John Vaswani has been named vice president, international, for Rockwell International Corp.'s Autonetics Electronics Systems in Anaheim. He was formerly director of strategic management and international.

Fred Sheldon, who had been director of business development and advanced technology for the missile systems division, has been appointed director of strategic planning for Autonetics.

In addition, Richard Janik has been named vice president for sensors and signal processing. Janik joins the company from Hughes Aircraft's Electro-Optics & Data Systems group. Autonetics produces missile systems and submarine navigation systems.
