930721 FT 21 JUL 93 / Parliament and Politics: Labour in human rights move LABOUR yesterday hosted an innovative one-day working conference in a move to draw up a bill for incorporating the European convention on human rights into UK law that is ready to run on 'the first day' of a Labour government. Mr John Smith, the Labour leader, is committed to such a move as the first step to creating a British bill of rights. The party said the conference was designed to move the constitutional debate 'from the esoteric to the practical'.
