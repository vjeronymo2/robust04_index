Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Galina Gridneva and Aleksandr Kharchenko] [Text] Dushanbe, 2 Mar -- Another attempt to invade the territory of Tajikistan by armed fighters from Afghanistan was prevented during the night of 1-2 March on a sector of one of the Pyanj frontier detachment's outposts. There were no losses among the Russian frontier guards. Since the beginning of the year, Russian frontier guards have engaged in battles with armed fighters on the Tajik-Afghan frontier more than 30 times.
