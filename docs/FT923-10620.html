920729 FT 29 JUL 92 / International Company News: Hypo-Bank ahead 12.7% BAYERISCHE Hypotheken-und Wechsel-Bank (Hypo-Bank) yesterday said group operating profits rose nearly 12.7 per cent to DM769m (Dollars 513m) in the first half of this year, writes Andrew Fisher in Frankfurt. Higher lending volume and improved margins had enabled the bank to lift net interest income by 16.4 per cent to DM1.6bn, while net fee income increased 6.6 per cent to DM266m.
