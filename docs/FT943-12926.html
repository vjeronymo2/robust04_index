940722 FT 22 JUL 94 / Tyne and Wear bus deal backed Employee shareholders of Busways, the main urban bus company in Tyne and Wear, yesterday backed a Pounds 27.5m offer for the company by Stagecoach, the bus group. Stagecoach said acceptances representing 99.26 per cent of the Busways issued share capital had been received, making the offer unconditional.
