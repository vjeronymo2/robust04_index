BFN [Report by correspondent Roberto Cavada Barrera] [Excerpt] On 11 April, Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina ratified in Peru an antidrug trafficking agreement and a customs accord between Cuban and Peruvian customs services. Robaina and Peruvian Foreign Minister Efrain Goldenberg signed the agreements at the closing of an official meeting at the Torre Tagle Palace. The meeting between Robaina and Goldenberg, who is also Peru's prime minister, lasted 45 minutes. Goldenberg granted us an exclusive interview for Radio Rebelde's "Exclusive" program: [Begin recording] [Goldenberg] This is just one more step in the long friendship that unites Peru and Cuba. We are ready to engage in other issues and continue to make progress. [Cavada] Are you pleased with Foreign Minister Robaina's visit? [Goldenberg] Of course. [end recording] [passage omitted]
