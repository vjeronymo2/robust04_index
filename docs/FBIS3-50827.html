Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Global TV News" newscast] [Excerpts] President Alberto Fujimori revealed today that Ecuadoran President Sixto Duran-Ballen's upcoming visit to Peru has been postponed until further notice. The head of state also referred to other issues during the various activities he carried out at the settlement of Huaycan. [passage omitted] Shortly before concluding his tour, he briefly inspected the site where the local technological institute is being built. In his statements to the media Fujimori ruled out the possibility that Ecuadoran President Duran-Ballen's visit to our capital will take place on 20 January. [Begin Fujimori recording] The date has not been officially coordinated at the Foreign Ministry level. In any case, it will not occur on 20 January -- but we are waiting for our Foreign Ministry to establish the most appropriate date. We are obviously waiting for this expectantly, to welcome the president of our brother country Ecuador with open arms, as he deserves. [end recording]
