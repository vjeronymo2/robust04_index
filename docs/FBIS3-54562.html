Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A statement by the General Customs Department said that a group of about 14 smugglers of basic commodities yesterday set an ambush for a customs officers' patrol in Timelaouine, close to the Algerian-Malian border, which led to the killing of a customs officer. The statement pointed out that the five-member customs officers' patrol responded to the attack and succeeded in killing four members of the smuggling gang and wounding two others.
