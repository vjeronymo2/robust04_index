Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow January 5 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin today met CIS Executive Secretary Ivan Korotchenya, who stopped over in Moscow en route to China. According to information received from the Presidential Press Service, the sides have discussed prospects for implementing the agreements and documents signed during the CIS summit in Ashgabat. President Yeltsin expressed satisfaction with the creative tendencies appearing within the Commonwealth, with the growing understanding of the need and inevitability of integrational processes. As chairman of the CIS Heads of State Council, Yeltsin stressed the importance of stepping up the formation of a full-blooded and effective economic union and of strengthening collective security. The sides also discussed some organisational measures, intended to consolidate the Commonwealth of Independent States, to enhance its authority on the international arena.
