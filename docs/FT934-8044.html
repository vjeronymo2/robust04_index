931117 FT 17 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Kembrey back in the black THE ELIMINATION of losses from discontinued businesses helped Kembrey, the Sheffield-based electrical connector and accessory manufacturer, report a marked recovery at the interim stage. Mr David Burnet, chairman, said the outcome for the six months to October 2 -pre-tax profits of Pounds 29,000 compared with losses of Pounds 304,000 - also reflected reduced borrowings and lower interest rates. Interest payable dipped from Pounds 382,000 to Pounds 218,000; gearing at was 93 per cent, down from 98 per cent in March.
