930621 FT 21 JUN 93 / International Company News: Digital sells US leasing business DIGITAL Equipment, the computer manufacturer, is to sell its US leasing business to GE Capital, the fast-growing financial services arm of General Electric, writes Martin Dickson. The companies declined to disclose financial terms of the deal but GE will take over servicing Digital's leasing customers and the unit's 40 employees. GE Capital is one of the largest leasing organisations in the US.
