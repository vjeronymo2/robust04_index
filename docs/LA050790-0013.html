May 7, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

ANIMAL RIGHTS MOVEMENT AND MEDICAL EXPERIMENTS

Your editorial placed human life above animal life. I congratulate you on making the only possible moral decision.

However, since human life is more valuable than animal life, it requires that we ask and answer this question: What is the moral justification for animal rights groups spending millions of dollars on saving experimental animals while human beings die of hunger, war and pestilence?

The answer to this question is really an eye-opener!

ROBERT H. JACOBS

Los Angeles
