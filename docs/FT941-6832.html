940228 FT 28 FEB 94 / Observer: Another Waterloo? For much of its history Belgium has been a place where Britain and France conducted battles, so it's perhaps inevitable that one should now be threatening to flare up over the latest Anglo-French foray, the Channel tunnel. Some Belgians are miffed that they are being short-changed. Belgian Railways is a full partner with British Rail and France's SNCF in European Passenger Services, which will run through-trains between London, Paris and Brussels. Yet passenger signs at London's new Waterloo International terminal are in English and French but not Flemish, which is spoken by 60 per cent of Belgians. Come to think about it, there are no signs in Welsh or Gaelic either ..
