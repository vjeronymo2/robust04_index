BFN [Unattributed article: "Brouwer Bids Her Political Farewell -- Rosenmoeller Becomes Party Chairman of Green Left"] [Excerpts] Green Left leader Ina Brouwer is to leave politics with immediate effect. Brouwer announced her decision Wednesday [4 May] in reaction to the disappointing election result achieved by her party. The new leader of Green Left will be Member of the Second Chamber Paul Rosenmoeller, the party's third-ranking representative. Brouwer is the first political victim of the latest elections to the Chamber. [passage omitted] Brouwer will be replaced by Tara Oedaray Singh Varma, former Amsterdam city councillor.
