Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Last night in Aden, Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council, received the members of the Joint Military Committee. Committee members briefed him on the progress of the committee's efforts to stop the deterioration and return military units to their previous positions, in accordance with the powers given to it to normalize the military situation and implement the provisions of the pledge and accord document pertaining to the military aspect. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid welcomed the members of the Joint Military Committee and congratulated them on the end of the blessed month of Ramadan and the advent of the happy 'Id al-Fitr. He wished that the committee's efforts, as well as the positive results it has reached, will succeed in achieving all goals and missions. He stressed the need to speed up efforts to normalize the situation, remove all recently established military sites, and return units to their positions in order to end the tension that might spark sedition and fighting among the sons of the homeland. The vice chairman of the Presidential Council stressed the need to double peaceful and democratic efforts and to resort to the spirit of dialogue, wisdom, rationality, patience, and perseverance to deal with all issues and challenges threatening the unity and efforts to build a modern Yemeni state. He emphasized the need to maintain unity, adhere to democracy, promote security and stability, and protect national and other interests under unity. Concluding, brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid wished the committee every success in overcoming the difficulties and challenges and in preparing appropriate climates to continue its work to entrench the pillars of security and stability for our people and the region's peoples. The meeting was attended by Defense Minister Staff Brigadier General Haytham Qasim Tahir.
