910513 FT 13 MAY 91 / Observer: Hear Hear 'At the beginning of my review, I stated that 1990 had been a difficult year. For me, it was also a rewarding one.' So says Mick Newmarch, chief executive of Prudential Corporation, writing in his annual report for 1990 - a year in which, it may be recalled, the Pru's pre-tax profits rose 9 per cent and his salary rose 43 per cent.
