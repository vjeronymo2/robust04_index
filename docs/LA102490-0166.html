October 24, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

MD-11 SETS RECORD TO KOREA

Douglas Aircraft Co.'s new MD-11 commercial jetliner set a speed record during a test flight from Dallas to the South Korean capital of Seoul, the company said.

The three-engine jet covered 7,266 miles without stopping in 14 hours and 50 minutes on Tuesday, the company said.

The record was set as part of the MD-11's certification flight test program with the Federal Aviation Administration.

Douglas said it had applied to register the flight as a point-to-point speed record with the International Federation of Aeronautics.
