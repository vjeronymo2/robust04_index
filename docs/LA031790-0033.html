March 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

HE SEEMS TO HAVE ALL THE QUALIFICATIONS

POSITION WANTED

Name: George L. Schulman

Age: Younger than Tommy Lasorda, Nolan Ryan and Darrell Evans.

Position sought: Stardom in major league baseball.

Position: Owner's discretion.

Ability and experience: Not applicable.

Salary required: $450,000 (less than average).

If my application is accepted, I agree to perform the following duties even if I have a cold or headache:

1. Play doubleheaders.

2. Play day games after night doubleheaders.

3. Wash my own uniform.

4. Buy my own franks and burgers.

5. Provide for my own retirement from the aforementioned meager sum.

6. Fly coach.

7. Stay at under-20-bucks-a-night motels.

8. Clean up dugout after each game.

If you own a baseball team and would like to employ at least one player who actually enjoys playing the game of baseball, call immediately.

I'm available the first week of April. Even if no one else is.

GEORGE L. SCHULMAN

Newport Beach
