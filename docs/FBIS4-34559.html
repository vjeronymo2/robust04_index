BFN [Text] Port-au-Prince, 21 Jun (AFP) -- Tuesday night, the Ministry of Information, Coordination, and Culture announced that hereafter foreign journalists must "request and obtain" from that Ministry authorization to go to the cities in the provinces. The objective of the measure is "to guarantee the security of foreign journalists in the country," the communique notes. Many foreign journalists, including the crews of the principal U.S. television stations, are in Port-au-Prince just as the new U.S. sanctions are to be enforced.
