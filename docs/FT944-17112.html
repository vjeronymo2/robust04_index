941010 FT 10 OCT 94 / Equity Markets: Frankfurt - Other markets Thursday's Bundesbank meeting and next Sunday's federal election dominate a volatile market. Few expect the Bundesbank to change rates. James Capel says the weakness of the August industrial production and orders data largely reflected seasonal problems. It believes that the German recovery remains robust. UBS notes that the polls suggest the present CDU/CSU-FDP government of Chancellor Kohl holds a slender lead. In the event of his re-election, it expects 'a significant relief factor to be manifested in the markets'.
