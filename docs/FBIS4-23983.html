BFN [Text] The ECOMOG [Economic Community of West African States Cease-Fire Monitoring Group] High Command has denied claims by Mr. Alhaji Koromah that troops of the Nigerian contingent deployed in Tubmanburg are fighting alongside the Krahn faction of the ULIMO [United Liberation Movement for Democracy in Liberia] under the Imam of Tubmanburg Alhaji Mohamed Sheriff, (?war chief). In a release issued yesterday, ECOMOG said no Nigerian or any other troops had ever supported any faction. The release pointed out that Imam Sheriff had taken refuge in (Naibas) territory with other displaced people. ECOMOG is inviting any concerned citizens who visit (Naibas) territory to speak to Imam Sheriff.
