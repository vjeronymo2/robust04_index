930924 FT 24 SEP 93 / World News in Brief: Venturer of the Year award winner Michael Peagram, chief executive of speciality chemicals group Holliday Chemical Holdings, is this year's winner of the Venturer of the Year award sponsored by the Financial Times, Cartier the jewellers and the British Venture Capital Association. Mr Peagram appears in today's FT survey on Venture and Development Capital. The report also lists the eight category winners of this year's awards, announced in London yesterday. Survey, Section 4
