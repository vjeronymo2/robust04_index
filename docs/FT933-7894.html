930819 FT 19 AUG 93 / Unemployment in Finland tops 20% Finland's jobless rate topped 20 per cent of the workforce in July, Reuter reports from Helsinki. The labour ministry said unemployment had increased to 20.4 per cent from 19.5 per cent the previous month and 15.1 per cent a year earlier. The number of unemployed looking for a job rose by 23,100 to 519,300 in July from a month earlier.
