920908 FT 08 SEP 92 / Central African poll date set President Andre Kolingba of the Central African Republic announced yesterday that presidential and parliamentary elections would be held on October 25, Reuter reports from Bangui. He signed a decree setting the date for the double poll as six public sector trade unions began a 10-day strike over unpaid salaries. General Kolingba came to power in a 1981 coup with the blessing of France, Central Africa's former colonial power.
