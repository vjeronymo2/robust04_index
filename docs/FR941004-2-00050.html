American Electric Power Service Corporation [Docket No. ER94&hyph;1670&hyph;000] Take notice that on September 21, 1994, American Electric Power Service Corporation, on behalf of Appalachian Power Company, Columbus Southern Power Company, Indiana Michigan Power Company, Kentucky Power Company and Ohio Power Company, (the AEP Companies) tendered for filing an AEP System Interim Allowance Agreement. The purpose of the Agreement is to establish, on an interim basis, a methodology and transfer price for the transfer of SO 2 emission allowances among the AEP Companies. The AEP Companies request an effective date of January 1, 1995. Copies have been served upon the state regulatory commissions in Indiana, Kentucky, Michigan, Ohio, Tennessee, Virginia and West Virginia.
