February 12, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

VENICE CLINIC BEGINS $2.1-MILLION EXPANSION

Construction is under way on a $2.1-million expansion of the Venice Family clinic, 604 Rose Ave., Venice.

Fern Seizer, executive director, said that while the architect Widom Wein Cohen of Los Angeles donated 80% of its fees, the free medical clinic is still seeking donations of construction materials.
