BFN [Text] Firmino Lukamba, Voice of Resistance of the Black Cockerel correspondent, reports that war planes of the Popular Movement for the Liberation of Angola-Labor once again massacred the civilian population of N'Dalatando yesterday. The air raid resulted in 88 people killed and a number of others wounded. The dead include 80-year old Antonio Coelho, a well-known man in the area, whom the children used to call uncle Capataz.
