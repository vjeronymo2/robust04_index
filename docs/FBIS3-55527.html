Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow January 10 TASS -- The interdepartmental commission of the Russian Security Council on economic security held its first meeting here on Monday. President Boris Yeltsin issued a decree appointing Andrey Zverev, head of the economic division of the Russian Government's office, chairman of the commission. The meeting examined the drafting of a nation-wide economic security doctrine as well as progress in carrying out economic reforms and other issues. The meeting was attended by top executives of ministries and departments represented on the commission.
