920211 FT 11 FEB 92 / Machine tool group sold YUASA-SHOJI, one of Japan's oldest trading houses, has bought Warwick Machine Tools, a UK importer, from Goladetz, a London-based import-export company. Terms were not disclosed for the deal, which will lead to Warwick becoming the Japanese company's European technical centre. Yuasa wants to expand in the European Community, and chose Warwick because it was performing better than its continental counterparts.
