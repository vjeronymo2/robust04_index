September 8, 1989, Friday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

3 ARAB COLLABORATION SUSPECTS SLAIN

Three Palestinians suspected of collaborating with Israeli authorities were found dead today in the occupied territories, and the army said it arrested four Arabs in one of the slayings.

The deaths raised to 110 the number of Palestinians killed by fellow Arabs since the start nearly 21 months ago of the uprising against Israeli occupation. Most have been accused of collaboration. The slayings came despite an appeal in a leaflet distributed by the uprising's leadership calling on Palestinian activists to not be too quick to kill alleged collaborators.
