Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Kuchina] [Text] Riga January 14 TASS -- The command of the Russian North-Western troops ordered Russian military in Latvia to open fire in case of new attacks on military objects, the north-western troops press-centre reported Friday. Officers sent to settle conflict situation are to be accompanied by armed guards. Armed units on duty will protect military objects which do not have permanent guards. In order to avoid misunderstanding commanders of military units are to notify local authorities and residents about these measures. The order was issued in connection with the incident, which took place in Latvia Monday, when four military objects were blockaded and two generals were arrested and delivered in manacles to the state border. These actions "cast doubt on promises of Latvia about its intention to solve all the problems of Russian troops in Latvia in a civilized way, the ability of the Latvian state to control activities of local troops under their command",
