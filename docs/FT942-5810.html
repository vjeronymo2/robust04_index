940602 FT 02 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Johannesburg was firmer as equities were given a boost by a rise in the gold bullion price. The overall index moved forward 24 to 5,420, industrials 14 to 6,563 and golds 31 to 1,948. Anglos, which reported higher full-year earnings, advanced 75 cents to R219.
