940409 FT 09 APR 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Shares closed sharply higher on confidence over progress of the peace summit, although dealers forecast further volatility depending on the outcome. The overall index ended 96 higher at 4,954, industrials put on 116 to 5,734 and the gold index improved 38 to 1,951. De Beers gained R2.50 to R105.25 and Anglos R5 to R202.
