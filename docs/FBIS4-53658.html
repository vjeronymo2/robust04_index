BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 20 (KCNA) -- The Workers' Party of Korea publishing house brought out the volume 10 of "Kim Il-song Selected Works", an encyclopedia of the chuche idea which carries famous works of the great leader President Kim Il-song in chronological order. The volume contains 33 famous works of those published by the great leader from January 1987 to December 1992. It comprehensively consummates the outstanding ideas, theories and policies of the great leader on further accelerating the building of socialism and communism and successfully achieving the independent and peaceful reunification of the country and the cause of global independence, under the uplifted revolutionary banner of the chuche idea to meet the requirements of the developing reality in which the cause of modelling the whole society on the chuche idea has been propelled, and the precious achievements and experiences gained in the struggle for their realisation. Consummated there are the unique ideas, theories and policies giving answers to the theoretical and practical questions arising in different domains of the revolution and construction including the question of effecting a new turn in science, education, public health and socialist culture and the question of further strengthening and developing the People's Army.
