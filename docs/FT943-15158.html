940711 FT 11 JUL 94 / Company News (This Week): Packaged for further material success - Low & Bonar Improved margins in North America and signs of increased volumes in continental Europe are expected to lift Low & Bonar's interim pre-tax profits by about 22 per cent to Pounds 17.5m. The international packaging and materials group, which announces results for the six months to May 31 on Monday, has shed non-core businesses and shored up its mainstream activities with acquisitions such as Cereal Packaging, the former Kellogg company.
