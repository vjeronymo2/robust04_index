910422 FT 22 APR 91 / World News in Brief: Marathon winners Soviet Yakov Tolstikov won the 11th London Marathon in 2 hrs, 9 mins, 17 secs, while Portuguese world champion Rosa Mota was the first woman home (2 hrs, 26 mins, 14 secs). More than 24,000 began the 26.2-mile run, including Sir Jimmy Savile who completed his 200th marathon.
