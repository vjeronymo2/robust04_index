BFN [Excerpts] Dr. Sa'id al-Tall, Jordanian deputy prime minister and higher education minister, and his accompanying delegation arrived in Damascus via Dar'a this evening. [passage omitted] Dr. al-Tall made a statement to a Syrian Arab News Agency correspondent in Dar'a. He expressed happiness at this visit, and said: On my visit to fraternal Syria, I would like first to convey to His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad greetings from His Majesty King Husayn. In my name and on behalf of the delegation, I also convey to His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad profound appreciation, respect, and pride. Dr. al-Tall added: We are emphasizing a consistent fact, in addition to the pan-Arab ties between both fraternal countries, which are two parts of a whole. The two countries are facing the challenges posed to the Arab nation. He also lauded the strong cooperation and sisterly relations between both fraternal countries as well as the constant coordination between President Hafiz al-Asad and King Husayn for pan-Arab interests. Al-Tall said this visit comes at the invitation of the Syrian higher education minister to further enhance cooperation in various higher education fields between both fraternal countries.
