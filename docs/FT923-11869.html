920722 FT 22 JUL 92 / Debt collectors seek legal role DEBT recovery companies are to seek approval to represent their clients in legal cases which are usually handled by solicitors. About 80 debt recovery companies met in Birmingham on Monday to launch the iniative and to form an Institute of Licensed Debt Practioners as a professional organisation supervising debt collection practices. The Lord Chancellor's department said Lord Mackay would soon issue a consultation paper on the way in which civil enforcement agents - bailiffs and sheriffs - go about their business and whether any changes in the law are necessary.
