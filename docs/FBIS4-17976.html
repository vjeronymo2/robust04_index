BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Boris Krivoshey and Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow, 28 Apr -- The visit to Russia by Finnish Foreign Minister Heikki Haavisto planned for 30 April is being postponed, Grigoriy Karasin, the director of the Information and Press Department of the Russian Foreign Ministry, stated at a briefing today. He noted that Haavisto's visit had been postponed at the request of the Russian side and the request was properly agreed with the leadership of the Finnish Ministry of Foreign Affairs. "This postponement is purely technical and has no political overtones. It is simply a matter of foreign minister Andrey Kozyrev's `timetable'," Karasin said.
