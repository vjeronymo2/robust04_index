Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Bai Meiqing, vice minister of the Ministry of Internal Trade and concurrently director of the State Grain Reserve Administration, came to Changsha City on 9 January to inspect how the city controlled grain and oil prices. Accompanied by Vice Governor Zhou Shichang as well as provincial and Changsha City grain bureau leaders, Vice Minister Bai Meiqing went to grain shops in Wuyi Zhonglu, Renminlu, and Taipingjie, where he carefully checked the price tags as well as the grain and oil quality and asked about grain and oil sales. To implement instructions of the State Council, 400.5 million kg of grain has been dumped onto the markets throughout the province to control grain prices. Grain departments have been active in transporting grain and in ensuring variety, quality, quantity, prices, and service. Since the period before the New Year, all grain departments have openly sold their grain with price tags, thus speedily reducing grain and oil prices throughout the province.
