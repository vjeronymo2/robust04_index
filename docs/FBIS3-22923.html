Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The PP [Popular Party] federal deputy for Sao Paulo, Alberto Haddad, escaped a bomb attack in his political office in Pinheiros (western Sao Paulo). This information had been kept secret since 2 February, when the PM [Military Police] bomb squad was called. There are no clues on who planted the bomb. "I have suspicions about my enemies within the party," Haddad said.
