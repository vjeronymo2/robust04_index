BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 5 (KCNA) -- A statement of a spokesman for the Foreign Ministry of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea dated May 19 regarding the recent exposure of the concealment of a large quantity of plutonium at a nuclear fuel plant of Japan was distributed on May 25 as official document of the UN General Assembly No. A/49/165 and official document of the UN Security Council No. S/1994/616.
