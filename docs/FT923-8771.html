920808 FT 08 AUG 92 / The Barcelona Olympics: Tennis title for Capriati Jennifer Capriati of the US defeated defending champion Steffi Graf of Germany 3-6, 6-3, 6-4 to win the women's singles tennis gold. Spain's Aranxtha Sanchez Vicario and American Mary Joe Fernandez shared the bronze. Germans Boris Becker and Michael Stich won the doubles by overcoming South Africans Wayne Ferreira and Piet Norval 7-6, 4-6, 7-6, 6-3, whose silver is the first medal South Africa has won since returning to the Games after 32 years.
