September 7, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

On a happier note, General Motors has agreed to provide exclusive corporate sponsorship for PBS' Emmy-winning series "Live From Lincoln Center" for three years beginning in January, 1990, Lincoln Center officials announced Wednesday. Exxon Corp., which has provided sponsorship for the Lincoln Center programs since 1976, will continue its support through the end of 1989. ALEENE MacMINN
