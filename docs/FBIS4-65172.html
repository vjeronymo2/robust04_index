BFN [Excerpt] Bonn -- The Federal Government wants Bonn to be the headquarters of the new World Trade Organization (WTO) that is to succeed GATT in January 1995. The cabinet adopted Economic Minister Guenter Rexrodt's proposal on Wednesday [11 May]. The offer was transmitted to the GATT Secretariat in Geneva immediately. The deadline for applications is 18 May. If the application is successful, the new institution will be understood as a compensation to Bonn for the government and parliament's move to Berlin.
