Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Petr Altunin report under the "Yesterday" rubric: "Military Pensions Being Recalculated"] [Text] As has already been reported in KRASNAYA ZVEZDA of 31 December 1993, basic pay and pay according to rank is being increased by a factor of 1.9 for servicemen in the Russian Armed Forces from 1 January 1994 in accordance with a Russian Federation Government decree. The Ministry of Defense Main Directorate for the Military Budget and Finance announced: The Russian Federation Law On the Provision of Pensions to Servicemen (Article 49) and the decree of the Russian Federation Council of Ministers/Government of 22 September 1993, No.941 (Article 14) says: If the monetary allowance for servicemen increases, pensions which have been previously fixed for discharged servicemen and members of their families are also subject to review from the same date. The military commissariats have already set about this work. Many people are interested in the current minimum wage which, as we know, is taken into consideration when reviewing military pensions. It stands today at 14,620 rubles.
