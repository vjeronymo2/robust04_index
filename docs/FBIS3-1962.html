Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [From the "Morning News" program] [Text] Mayor Huang Ju met yesterday evening with former U.S. Secretary of State Lawrence Eagleburger and his party at the Jinjiang Hotel. The guests and their host had a warm and friendly conversation. During the meeting, Huang Ju briefed the guests on Shanghai's economic achievements. While speaking about utilizing foreign funds, Huang Ju pointed out that currently the United States was the largest foreign investor in Shanghai. He warmly welcomed U.S. entrepreneurs accompanying Eagleburger to invest in Shanghai and participate in cooperative projects. Eagleburger highly praised the drastic changes in Shanghai's appearance in recent years. He reiterated the importance of U.S.- PRC cooperation for world peace, and expressed his willingness to continue to work to promote cooperation between the two countries. Yesterday Eagleburger visited the Pudong New Area, the Shanghai Stock Exchange, and the city's foreign exchange regulation center.
