941128 FT 28 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Eclipse makes disposal Eclipse Blinds has sold the business and assets of the Apollo Blinds retail franchise to Hunter Douglas (UK) for Pounds 2.4m cash, plus an estimated Pounds 190,000 in respect of the value of certain debtors attributable to the Apollo business. Apollo makes window blinds for franchise retail outlets in the UK and Irish Republic and supplies about 70 outlets which sell window blinds under the Apollo brand name. The sale is in line with Eclipse's policy to develop around the profitable merchandised distribution of textiles and the component parts of window blinds.
