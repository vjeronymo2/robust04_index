BFN [Text] Yangon, [Rangoon] June 22 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese military delegation, led by General Li Jiulong, commander of the Chengdu Military Region of the People's Liberation Army, arrived here today to begin one-week friendly visit to Myanmar [Burma]. The delegation was met by Myanmar Chief of Staff (Army) and Commander of No. 1 Bureau of Special Operations Lieutenant-General, Tin Oo and other high officers, Chinese Ambassador to Myanmar Liang Feng and Chinese military attache Senior Colonel Wu Huachang at Yangon airport. At the invitation of the Myanmar Ministry of Defense, the Chinese delegation came to Myanmar for a friendly visit.
