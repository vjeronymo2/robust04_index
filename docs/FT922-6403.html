920523 FT 23 MAY 92 / Rightist surge feared in Berlin German politicians are braced for further evidence of a rising extreme right-wing protest vote in Berlin local government elections, writes Quentin Peel in Bonn. In the first city-wide elections since 1946, more than 2.5m voters from East and West Berlin tomorrow will elect 23 district councils, and opinion polls suggest the far-right Republicans could win over 10 per cent of the vote.
