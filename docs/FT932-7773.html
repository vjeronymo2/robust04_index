930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / UK Company News: SelecTV Mr Michael Buckley resigned yesterday as a director of SelecTV two months after he was ousted as chairman of the independent television production company. SelecTV said only that he had left to 'devote more time to his other business interests'. He will, however, offer consulting services to the company. In March, Mr Buckley lost a battle against Mr Allan McKeown who heads the Los Angeles office of SelecTV.
