BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Nechayev] [Text] Moscow June 2 TASS -- The Federation Council, the upper chamber of the Russian parliament, approved at its closed-door meeting on Thursday the draft 1994 budget as the basis for further discussions which are to be held in its committees, TASS learned from the press service of the upper chamber. If the discussion goes as scheduled, Chairman of the Budget Committee of the upper chamber Nikolay Gonchar will address the lower house on June 8 with the results of the work. No other details were disclosed.
