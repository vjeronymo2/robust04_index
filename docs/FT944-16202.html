941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / Observer: Sock it to me Germany's leftwing Party of Democratic Socialism - dubbed the 'red socks' on account of its origins in the old East German ruling communist party - has high hopes. It's put up election posters in the prosperous villages nestling outside Frankfurt in the Taunus hills, homes to bankers and businessmen - yet the only constituencies the party is expected to win are in east Germany. Gregor Gysi - the charismatic PDS leader - is not known for his modesty.
