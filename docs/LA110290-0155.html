November 2, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

CHANGING TIMES: In 1980, Disneyland security gave the bum's rush to two men caught dancing together.

This year on Nov. 30, the park hosts the third private party thrown by AIDS Project Los Angeles and the AIDS Service Foundation of Orange County. . . . "About 90% are going to be gay, but that's not the point," says an AIDS Project spokesman. "Disneyland sees it as a fund-raiser for the AIDS cause, not as 'gay night.' If they have any misgivings or apprehensions, they definitely don't show it." Zan Dubin and George Frank
