BFN [Text] The National Dialogue General Congress will hold its first procedural session Saturday evening [25 June] under the chairmanship of Dr. Mustafa Khalil, the congress' moderator general. The opening session will be held on Saturday morning. During the procedural session there will be a discussion about organizing the congress' three committees. They are the Political, Economic, and Social Committees, the establishment of which was recommended by the National Dialogue Preparatory Committee.
