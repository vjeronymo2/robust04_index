BFN [Text] The 59th day of the Serb aggressor's offensive on the Bihac region was marked by fresh attacks on civilian targets, industrial areas, and defense lines. The industrial area in Bihac was hit by a number of artillery shells and one woman was killed. The Serb aggressor shelled defense lines on Mount Grabez and the Grmusa-Srbljani plateau throughout the day. However, the soldiers of our army destroyed yet another aggressor tank in an energetic counterattack. Members of the paramilitary units of the so-called Kladusa Autonomy [Autonomous Province of Western Bosnia -- declared by Fikret Abdic] used mortars to shell the Cazin municipality [area loyal to Izetbegovic]. One person was killed and another one seriously wounded.
