941126 FT 26 NOV 94 / UK Company News: James Latham Nearly doubled pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.14m against Pounds 598,000 were announced by James Latham, timber importer and building materials merchant, for the six months to September 30. Turnover was up 15 per cent to Pounds 40.4m, against Pounds 35.1m which included Pounds 479,000 from discontinued operations. Mr Christopher Latham, the chairman, said it was the first time since the onset of the recession that sales from all the trading activities had been over target. A 2.25p (1.5p) dividend is being paid from earnings of 16.6p (7.91p) per share.
