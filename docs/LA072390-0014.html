July 23, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

PRESIDENT'S SON AND BAILOUTS OF FAILED SAVINGS AND LOANS

The responsibility for raising morally disciplined children now seems to fall entirely on the shoulders of artists, musicians, movie producers, cartoon producers, television producers, and writers of literary works, and not their busy, busy parents, right?

The responsibility of deciding what I have the right to see, read and write has been oh so graciously "accepted" by narrow-minded, emotionally deficient right-wingers, correct?

The responsibility of deciding what is legitimate and "acceptable" freedom of expression rests in the sacred hands of those who believe they have the right to live everyone's life, huh?

But, the responsibility of saving the butts of corrupt presidential sons and other greedy, lying, hypocritical politicians is solely mine.

Hmmmmmm.

I don't know about you, but, this is all starting to scare the hell out of me.

KARON AGHOTTE-RICE

Northridge
