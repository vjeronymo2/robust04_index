Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Foreign Ministry of the Islamic Republic of Iran has sent messages to the political and Jehadi leaders of Afghanistan, calling for an extension of the cease-fire. The cease-fire between the warring sides in Afghanistan came into effect at 0600 this morning in response to a request from the Islamic Republic of Iran. The Islamic Republic of Iran has also called for the continuation of negotiations between the engaged parties toward ending the fighting.
