June 4, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

EIGHTH-INNING UPRISING LIFTS CUBS TO 8-1 WIN

Pat Cheek doubled with the bases loaded and Greg Cantor added a two-run home run to key a seven-run eighth inning in the California Cubs' 8-1 victory over the Woodland Hills Astros in a semipro W. A. Goodman's League game Saturday at Pierce College.

Winning pitcher Al Leiser did not allow a run in two innings of relief. Cub starter Jon Secrist gave up two hits, including a solo home run to Kevin Ogle in the fifth, in five innings.

Anthony Espitia and Steve Savage also doubled for the Cubs (2-0).
