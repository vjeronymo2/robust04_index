Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pakistan and Iran have agreed on closer cooperation to eliminate the menace of narcotics and to launch [a] campaign against trafficking of illicit drugs and psychotropic substances. The agreement came at the conclusion of the three-day session of the Pakistan-Iran Special Committee on Interior and Security in Islamabad today. The Iranian deputy interior minister for political and social affairs, Mr. ('Ali Reza Tabish), and the federal secretary, interior, Mr. Jamshed Barki, signed the agreement. Both sides also discussed in detail matters pertaining to expansion of border trade and improvement of communication.
