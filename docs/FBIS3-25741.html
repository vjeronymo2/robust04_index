Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] President of the Republic Jose Eduardo dos Santos returned from Paris this afternoon after a four-day visit to France. He was welcomed at the 4 February International Airport by the president of the National Assembly and government members. His visit to France dealt mainly with political issues and bilateral cooperation. Observers say the visit went according to plan. The Angolan head of state held talks with Paris Club officials and Elf Aquitaine directors with whom he discussed economic issues. Angolan First Lady Ana Paula dos Santos accompanied the head of state on his return trip.
