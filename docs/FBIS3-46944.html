Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 7 (KCNA) -- An inaugural meeting of the Tanzania-Korea Friendship Association was held in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, on December 29. Joseph Mbwiliza, vice-minister for water, energy and mineral resources and member of the National Executive Committee of the Revolutionary Party of Tanzania, was elected chairman of the Tanzania-Korea Friendship Association. The chairman of the association in his speech said the association would briskly study and disseminate the chuche idea founded by the great leader President Kim Il-song, actively introduce and propagandize policies of the Workers' Party of Korea and the DPRK Government and lift up voices of support and solidarity for the cause of independent, peaceful reunification of Korea, the cherished national desire of the Korean people. Letters to the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il were adopted at the meeting.
