921105 FT 05 NOV 92 / World News in Brief: American Telephone & Telegraph American Telephone & Telegraph is negotiating to buy a 33 per cent stake in McCaw Cellular, the biggest company in the fast-growing US cellular telephone market, for Dollars 3.8bn as part of a broad alliance between the two businesses. The deal includes AT&T buying out the 22 per cent stake BT holds in McCaw.
