920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / UK Company News: LOFS announces change of domicile London & Overseas Freighters, the tanker company controlled by the Kulukundis family, is to be re-established under the same name in Bermuda, but will keep its London listing. The intention is to eliminate the tax disadvantages to international investors of investing in a UK-resident company. The move will be accompanied by the expansion of LOFS through the acquisition of Scoresby, another Kulukundis company. Scoresby was previously owned jointly by LOFS and Iroquois Shipping, another Kulukundis family company. The acquisition will be achieved through the issue of Pounds 7.4m worth of LOFS' shares to Iroquois.
