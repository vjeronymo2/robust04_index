930422 FT 22 APR 93 / World Trade News: S Africans win ECGD backing The UK Export Credits Guarantee Department (ECGD) has announced it is supporting a Dollars 225m line of credit to three South African companies to finance expansion of a stainless steel plant in eastern Transvaal, Reuter reports. It is the largest line of credit the ECGD has supported to South Africa and will help buy capital plant and equipment for Columbus Joint Venture's Middelburg site.
