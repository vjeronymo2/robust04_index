940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Slight downturn at Zetters Zetters Group showed a slight reduction in pre-tax profits from Pounds 427,000 to Pounds 421,000 in the six months to September 30, on reduced turnover of Pounds 10.7m, against Pounds 10.8m. Turnover from football pools slipped to Pounds 10.5m (Pounds 10.6m), while competitions and marketing rose to Pounds 166,000 (Pounds 88,000) and the spotting-the-ball partnership contributed Pounds 122,000 (Pounds 111,000). After lower tax of Pounds 147,000 (Pounds 160,000), earnings per share edged ahead from 4.1p to 4.2p. The interim dividend is again 4p. Since August pools turnover had risen, following a small price increase in stakes and the broadening of the collector service.
