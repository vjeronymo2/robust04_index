921008 FT 08 OCT 92 / Israeli troops fire on demonstrators Israeli soldiers shot and wounded at least 78 Palestinians in the occupied Gaza Strip yesterday during marches in support of a prisoner hunger strike, Reuter reports from Rafah, Gaza Strip. Soldiers fired live ammunition, plastic bullets and tear gas to disperse several thousand demonstrators outside the Red Cross headquarters in Rafah refugee camp. Palestinians threw stones and fire bombs. The clashes were the fiercest in the Strip in five months. The protests were to back an 11-day-old hunger strike by Arab prisoners demanding better treatment.
