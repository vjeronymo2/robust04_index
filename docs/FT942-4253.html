940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Premier Land achieves Pounds 146,000 Premier Land, the property investment group, achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 146,000 for the six months to March 31. For the previous nine months, before the company divested its mining interests, there were losses of Pounds 719,000. Rent for the period amounted to Pounds 1.79m while net property sales were Pounds 650,000. Earnings per share came through at 0.08p (losses of 0.85p for nine months).
