BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anatoliy Yurkin] [Text] Moscow May 4 TASS -- Russian defence officials are holding talks today with a U.S. military delegation which arrived in Moscow on Tuesday. The U.S. delegation to the talks held in the Russian Defence Ministry is led by the U.S. defence secretary's aide for issues of nuclear security, Ashton Carter. Russia is represented by a working group consisting of officers and generals of the general staff and main departments of the Defence Ministry. The Russian delegation is led by Lieutenant-General Gennadiy Ivanov, aide of the Russian defence minister for military politics. The two-day talks are devoted to the discussion of further steps to improve coordination and strengthen confidence between the defence departments of the two countries, sources from the Russian Defence Ministry told ITAR-TASS.
