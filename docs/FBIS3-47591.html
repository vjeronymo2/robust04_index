Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 13 (KCNA) -- Songs of unquestioned trust in and loyalty to the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il have been created and widely disseminated in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Nearly 400 songs including "Kim Chong-il, He Is Our Destiny" and "Comrade Kim Chong-il Is Our Supreme Commander" are edited in collections of songs recently issued by the literature and art press group. "We Will Win Because You Are Leading Us," a poem dedicated by Yi In-mo, the incarnation of faith and will, to dear Comrade Kim Chong- il last year, was composed as a song. In the poem Yi In-mo emphasizes the unshakable ideological conviction that as long as there is the leadership of dear Comrade Kim Chong-il the revolutionary cause of chuche, the cause of socialism, shall not perish. Today the Korean people are filled with revolutionary optimism and conviction that as long as they are under his wise leadership, they are sure to win victory, no matter what ordeals and difficulties may crop up in their way. A song "Single Heart" sings of the indomitable spirit and determination of the Korean people to carry their revolutionary cause through to accomplishment, united around the illustrious leader in one mind and will. "Comrade Kim Chong-il Is Our Supreme Commander," "Let Us Uphold Our Supreme Commander With Arms" and other songs tell about the national pride and honor of holding in high esteem Comrade Kim Chong-il, an outstanding military strategist and ever-victorious iron-willed brilliant commander, as chairman of the National Defence Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, and about the unshakable faith and will of the People's Army and people to resolutely defend him with arms. Many songs including "We Will Hold Comrade Kim Chong-il in High Esteem Forever," "Kim Chong-il, He Is Our Destiny," and "Long Live Comrade Kim Chong-il, Great Brilliant Commander" reflect the unanimous mind and will of the Korean people to follow with loyalty and filial piety the illustrious leader who takes care of and protects the destiny of the nation and its future and hope.
