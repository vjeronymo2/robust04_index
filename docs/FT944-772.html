941223 FT 23 DEC 94 / International Company News: Coca-Cola Amatil in Dollars 240m Polish move Coca-Cola Amatil, the Australian soft drinks company which is 51 per cent owned by Atlanta-based Coca-Cola, said yesterday it would buy its parent company's bottling operations in Poland, plus certain local distribution rights, for about USDollars 240m. The assets being acquired include four bottling plants in Gdynia, Niepolomice, Sroda Slaska and Radzymin. CCA will also acquire the exclusive rights to make, distribute and market certain Cola-Cola products in the Gdynia, Krakow and west Poland territories, and to manufacture trademarked beverages in single-fill PET bottles and cans at Radzymin to supply companies which have distribution rights in the rest of Poland. The purchase prices will be USDollars 237.5m if settlement occurs by end-January. CCA said it would fund the consideration from internal cash resources.
