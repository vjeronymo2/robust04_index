BFN [Text] Kiev [no date as received] -- The prime minister of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam arrived in Ukraine today on an official visit. UNIAN has learned from sources close to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs that the following documents will be signed during the visit: a consular convention, an agreement on cooperation in the sphere of culture, education, and science, and an agreement on protection of investments. A treaty on friendship and cooperation between the two countries will be initialled. Ukraine and the Socialist Republic of Vietnam have so far signed an agreement on a Ukrainian-Vietnamese intergovernmental commission for trade, economic, scientific, and technical cooperation, a protocol on trade and economic cooperation in 1994, an agreement on visa-free entry of diplomatic and service passport holders, and an agreement on air transport. The two countries have a long history of bilateral relations. Ukraine was involved in the development of Vietnam's chemical, petrochemical, and food industries, as well as machine-building. During the visit, Vo Van Kiet will meet President of Ukraine Leonid Kravchuk, Acting Prime Minister Yukhym Zvyahilskyy, and officials at the Vietnamese Embassy in Ukraine.
