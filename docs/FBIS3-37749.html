Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe February 23 TASS -- Despite Dushanbe's call on the Afghan leadership to take steps to avert acts of armed provocation against sovereign Tajikistan and Russian border troops from Afghan territory, the village of Sarigor in Tajikistan's south was again shelled on Tuesday night, according to sources in the information department of Tajikistan's Ministry of Foreign Affairs. According to the same source, the Russian border troops responded by devastating fire which destroyed a mixed group of Tajik opposition militants and Afghan mojahedin near the 12 border post of the Moscow border unit. The Russian border troops are fully in control of the area now. Along with this the source in Tajikistan's Ministry of Foreign Affairs cited the command of the Russian border troops in Tajikistan as saying that more Tajik opposition militants have been observed concentrating near the Afghan city of Talukan, province of Tahor, and moving towards the border. The source did not rule out the possibility of a new act of armed provocation against the Russian border troops in the region.
