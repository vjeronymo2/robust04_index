941019 FT 19 OCT 94 / UK Company News: HK Trust assets up The Hong Kong Investment Trust lifted net asset value per ordinary share by 8 per cent - from 60.6p to 65.64p - during the 12 months to June 30. The Tyndall-managed trust reported net revenue of Pounds 387,740 (Pounds 401,077) for earnings of 1.94p (2.01p) per share. The proposed final is 1.1p for a total of 1.85p (1.75p).
