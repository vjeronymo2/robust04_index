November 1, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

VARIETY ROAD 3-1 FAVORITE FOR CALIFORNIA CUP CLASSIC

Variety Road, a stallion who returns to the races between breeding seasons, has been established as a 3-1 favorite in the $300,000 Classic, the richest stake Saturday at Santa Anita on the inaugural California Cup card.

Variety Road, whose three stake wins this year have come in Northern California, is a 7-year-old who has left training for the breeding shed in February the past two years.

The California Cup is a seven-race, $1-million stakes program restricted to California-bred horses. Two other races on the program -- maiden events -- are also for Cal-breds.

There were 85 horses entered for the stakes. Here's the lineup:
