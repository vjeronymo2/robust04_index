DEPARTMENT OF EDUCATION Office of Special Education and Rehabilitative Services [CFDA No.: 84.133B] The National Institute on Disability and Rehabilitation Research; Notice Inviting Applications for New Awards Under Certain Programs for Fiscal Year 1994 AGENCY: Department of Education. ACTION: Correction notice.

SUMMARY: On December 17, 1993 a notice inviting applications for new awards under certain programs for fiscal year 1994 was published in the Federal Register at 58 FR 66228. This notice corrects the estimated average size of awards for the funding priority on Rehabilitation of Persons with Long-term Mental Illness. On page 66299, the table should read that the estimated average size of awards for the funding priority on Rehabilitation of Persons with Long-term Mental Illness = $650,000.
