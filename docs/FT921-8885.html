920214 FT 14 FEB 92 / Observer: Pride swallowed Yesterday being the 300th anniversary of the Glencoe massacre, how did the Macdonalds end their ceremonies to mark the treacherous slaughter of 37 kinsfolk by troops headed by Archibald Campbell, 10th Duke of Argyll? They lunched at the Glencoe Hotel. What is its manager's name? Lorne Campbell.
