April 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

CONSTRUCTION STARTED ON BROADMOOR PLACE

Ground breaking of Broadmoor Place at 28120 Front Street in Riverside County has been announced by its developer, Broadmoor Homes' Commercial Division.

The $6-million commercial development will consist of two single-story buildings with office and some retail space, designed by Oreman & Associates. Following completion of the project in early 1990, Broadmoor will open a regional office at the location.
