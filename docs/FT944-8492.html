941119 FT 19 NOV 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices The pepper market was somewhat quieter this week, mainly due to holidays in various countries. Black pepper tended a little easier in light business. White, however, especially for delivery to the US, remained steady. Supplies in Malaysia and China are gradually drying up. This week, at a pepper seminar in Kuching, Malaysia pessimistic estimates for Malaysian 1995-96 pepper production were issued. As carry over stocks of previous years have been fully liquidated, when demand re-appears prices are expected to increase further. Muntok white spot was quoted at USDollars 3,650 a tonne and November/December shipment at Dollars 3,625, cif. Spot black pepper faq was at Dollars 2,600 a tonne and November/December shipment at Dollars 2,575, cif.
