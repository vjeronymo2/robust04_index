940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / International Company News: Dwyer Estates back in black with Pounds 0.38m Dwyer Estates, the Irish property investment and trading group, returned to the black in the six months to March 31 with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 378,000, against a loss of Pounds 729,000. The recovery was achieved on turnover down from Pounds 2.82m to Pounds 2.43m and struck after a cut in interest payable from Pounds 2.06m to Pounds 965,000 and a reduction of realised deficit on sale of properties from Pounds 537,000 to Pounds 37,000. Earnings per share emerged at 0.99p (2.69p losses). Directors said that following December's rights issue, which increased net worth by Pounds 12.3m, net assets at end-March were Pounds 26.7m - 52p a share - and net debt stood at Pounds 29.8m.
