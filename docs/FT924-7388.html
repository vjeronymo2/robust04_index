921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Great escape from the clutches of disaster - In a Nutshell A competition for businesses which have faced disaster and then triumphed has been launched by Durham University Business School and consultants CPCR. The Great Escape Award is intended to persuade entrants to examine their business and to learn from their mistakes. The award is open to businesses in the north of England which are run by someone who has undertaken higher education and then gone into business. The Pounds 1,000 prize will be presented in May.
