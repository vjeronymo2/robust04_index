940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / Nigerian oil workers strike Nigeria's main oil workers' union yesterday began a strike in protest against the government's annulment of last year's presidential election, won by Mr Moshood Abiola, Paul Adams writes from Lagos. The military government may bring Mr Abiola before a court in the inland capital Abuja today, his lawyer said yesterday. 'Abiola is very, very likely going to be brought before the high court in Abuja,' Mr Alao Aka-Bashorun, the lawyer, told reporters.
