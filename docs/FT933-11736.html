930728 FT 28 JUL 93 / People: Cable and Wireless Cable and Wireless, the UK telecommunications group, has set up a new North American company to manage its US business, and appointed the head of one of the constituent companies as its first chief executive. Alan Peyser, 59, head of Cable and Wireless Communications, which serves small and medium-sized businesses in the US, is to become chief executive of Cable and Wireless Inc. The new company will comprise C&W Communications and C&W Americas, which serves multinational companies based in the US.
