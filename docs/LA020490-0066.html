February 4, 1990, Sunday, Southland Edition

NATION IN BRIEF;

NEW YORK;

MARCHERS PROTEST YOUTH'S SLAYING

Demonstrators led by the Rev. Al Sharpton marched in a Brooklyn neighborhood after a police officer shot and killed a teen-ager who pointed a gun at him.

Robert Cole, 13, was carrying 35 vials of crack cocaine and the unloaded gun when confronted by the officer. Cole was the third teen-ager shot to death by police in a week in New York City and the eighth civilian killed this year, officials said. Sharpton's demonstration had already been planned to protest the earlier deaths of the other teen-agers.
