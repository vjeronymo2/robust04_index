BFN [Text] Division General Ulises Rosales del Toro, first vice minister of the Revolutionary Armed Forces, has praised the contribution made by the 35th Anniversary of the Triumph of the Revolution unit in the tomato harvest in Pinar del Rio. Rosales pointed out that the harvest this year was larger than last year, but that this is no reason to feel smug because there is still room to improve production. Rosales del Toro added that the experience attained by the Youth Labor Army and several farms of Los Palacios Rice Agroindustrial Complex ought be implemented to achieve higher efficiency with students from the military schools. This year, the 35th Anniversary of the Triumph of the Revolution unit harvested over 193,000 quintals of tomatoes, as well as 50,000 quintals of tubers and vegetables.
