Language: Portuguese Article Type:CSO [Text] Brazilian Army units in the Amazon Region will take a more active role in combating the narcotics traffic. Measures toward that end are being developed by the Army General Staff. Possible actions include the use of Army helicopters to back up the work of the Federal Police. Three aircraft are already operating in Manaus at the Military Command of Amazonia. In the future, a company of those aircraft will be assigned there, as the embryo of a future Helicopter Battalion in that region, Army sources say.
