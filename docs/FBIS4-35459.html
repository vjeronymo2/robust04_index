BFN [Text] New York, 27 May (INA) -- Iraq has once again protested to the United Nations against the continued violation of its airspace by U.S. aircraft on reconnaissance and provocative missions. In a memorandum addressed yesterday to the UN secretary general, Iraq's permanent representative said the U.S. aircraft conducted 698 aggressive sorties over many areas in the country, in addition to other provocative flights and dropped flares during the period 10-20 May.
