BFN [Text] The Ninth Session of the Eighth Provincial People's Congress Standing Committee concluded in Nanchang this afternoon. The closing session was presided over by Mao Zhiyong, secretary of the provincial party committee and chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee. Standing Committee Vice Chairmen Wang Zhaorong, Chen Kuizun, Wang Guoben, Wang Zhongfa, and Hu Dongtai attended the session. Huang Zhiquan, vice governor of the provincial government, attended the session as a nonvoting delegate. Also present at the session as nonvoting delagates were persons in charge of the provincial higher people's court and the provincial people's procuratorate, persons in charge of the various departments of the provincial people's congress, and persons in charge of the standing committees of the people's congresses of the cities under the jurisdiction of the provincial government and liaison offices in various prefectures of the standing committee of the provincial people's congress. Of the 55 persons who were anticipated to attend the session, 48 were present. A quorum was thus achieved. The session adopted the "Regulations of Jiangxi Province Concerning the Management of River Courses," the "Regulations of Jiangxi Province Concerning the Protection of the Citizens' Right to Report Offenders," the "Management Regulations of Jiangxi Province Concerning the Addition of Iodine to Table Salt to Eliminate Iodine Deficiency Hazards," the approval decision on the "Provisional Regulations of Nanchang City Concerning the Management of the Cultural Market," the approval decision on the "Regulations of Nanchang City on the Management of the Labor Market," and the list of personnel appointments and removals. Liu Dali, Wang Jiafu, and Yue Dayou, members of the provincial people's congress, spoke at the session.
