January 22, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

MONEY MATTERS: Costa Mesa financial adviser Victoria Felton-Collins' new book, "Couples and Money," is a Book-of-the-Month Club featured selection for April. . . . She hosts luncheons Tuesday and Wednesday to discuss the book, subtitled "Why money gets in the way of love," at -- where else? -- the posh Center Club in Costa Mesa. For $35 you get lunch and an autographed copy of the $17.95 book. . . . Most of her clients "have a great marriage," she says. "All that stands in their way is money." Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
