July 25, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Former Olympic gold medalist Steve Hegg of Dana Point won the men's individual time trial at the U.S. Senior National Cycling Championships in Gleneville, N.Y. Hegg covered the 25-mile course in 50 minutes 24.80 seconds.

Inga Thompson won the women's 40-kilometer race by 23 seconds in 56:16.76.
