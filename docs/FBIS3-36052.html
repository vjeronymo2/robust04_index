Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan February 1 TASS -- Press secretary of the Armenian President Aram Abramyan has sent in his resignation. The resignation was accepted by the president on Monday night. In the words of Abramyan, the reasons for his resignation are not political. He confirmed his loyalty to President Levon Ter-Petrosyan and noted he will return to scientific activities at the Institute of Arts of the Armenian Academy of Sciences.
