March 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: PCC WILL TAILOR PROGRAMS TO BUSINESSES

Pasadena City College has created an Economic Development Office that will offer customized educational programs to local businesses.

The program will include courses in English as a second language, business and intercultural communications, cutting-edge technology, supervisory training, and stress management. The classes are provided at the request of the business and held on-site for the convenience of the employees.

Businesses may obtain information from Mary Jane Cordon at (818) 578-7098.
