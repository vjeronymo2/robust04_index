BFN [Text] The Habbush Bridge came under intermittent artillery fire by the Israeli occupation posts in al-Suwayda' at 0510 this morning. It received two 82-mm mortar shells and came under bursts of 12.7-mm machine-gun fire. At approximately 2200 last night, several flares and shells fell on the Iqlim al-Tuffah hills, 'Ali al-Tahir, Mlita, and Jabal Safi, which overlook al-Nabatiyah and the neighboring villages, from the occupation troops posts inside the occupied border zone.
