BFN [Text] A Defense Ministry delegation led by Colonel General (Algej) Gutman, head of the Republican Headquarters of the Territorial Defense of the Republic of Slovenia, today attended the opening ceremony of the Coordination Unit of the Partnership for Peace in Mons in Belgium. The meeting was attended by the majority of the prominent NATO representatives, including Silvio Balanzino, deputy NATO secretary general, and George Joulwan. In his opening speech, Balanzino said that the Coordination Unit of the Partnership for Peace will be the main communications link between NATO and the states that are signatories to the Partnership for Peace. It will also enable the assembling of individual states' programs into groups of projects, exercises, and training.
