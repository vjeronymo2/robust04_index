940506 FT 06 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Westminster Scaffolding Poor trading conditions and low margins were cited by Westminster Scaffolding Group as the reasons behind deepening losses for the 14-months to December 31. The pre-tax deficit amounted to Pounds 4.23m, against Pounds 2.46m for the preceding 12 months, and was struck on turnover of Pounds 5.72m (Pounds 6.18m). Losses per share came out at 7.9p (8.6p).
