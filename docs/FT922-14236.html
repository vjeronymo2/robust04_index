920407 FT 07 APR 92 / Australia airport strike near end Australian aircraft refuellers and tanker drivers were to end a 24-hour strike at midnight, their union said, Reuter reports from Sydney. They agreed to return to work after a two-hour meeting with oil industry officials in the Australian Industrial Relations Commission yesterday. The refuellers stopped work over a lack of progress in pay talks. Transport Workers' Union vice-president John Mclean said he hoped negotiations could be completed without further action.
