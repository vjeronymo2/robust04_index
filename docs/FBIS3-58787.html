Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Swiss President Otto Stich has defended the government's decision to invite Mr. 'Arafat for talks at the end of this month. Mr. Stich said in a newspaper interview that he thought Mr. 'Arafat's visit to Bern was a good idea and was in line with Switzerland's promised support for Palestinians in the Israeli-occupied territories. He pointed out that Mr. 'Arafat had now renounced violence, and it was important for Switzerland to be briefed on developments in the Middle East peace process. During his first official visit to Bern, Mr. 'Arafat will meet Mr. Stich and the Swiss Foreign Minister Flavio Cotti.
