Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 Feb (MENA)--An official at the Palestinian Information Office in Cairo has said that the Palestinians have held contacts with the Israelis to fix a date and venue [as received] for the meeting that the two sides had agreed during the Davos talks to hold in Cairo. This meeting is supposed to prepare the way for the upcoming talks in Cairo between Yasir 'Arafat and Shim'on Peres. The official said the Palestinians are still waiting for the Israeli response. Nabil Sha'th said on his arrival in Cairo from Davos yesterday that a meeting will be held in Cairo in the next two days to prepare for the 'Arafat-Peres talks scheduled for Sunday.
