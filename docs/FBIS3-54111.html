Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 22 Jan (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak left Cairo for Syria this morning to offer his condolences to Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad on the death of his eldest son, who was killed in a tragic accident yesterday. Mubarak will take part in the funeral of the deceased in his hometown of al-Qardahah on the Syrian coast. The president is being accompanied by Defense Minister Field Marshal Muhammad Husayn Tantawi, Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa, and Dr. Usamah al-Baz, Foreign Ministry first under secretary and director of the President's Office for Political Affairs.
