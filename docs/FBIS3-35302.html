Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Leader President Saddam Husayn, may God preserve him, has decorated 'Izzat Ibrahim, vice chairman of the Revolution Command Council [RCC], Arab Socialist Ba'th Party National Command members, RCC members, and members of the Iraq Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party with the medal of high merit for their distinguished role in the Mother of Battles. Leader President Saddam Husayn, may God support him, congratulated the honorees and beseeched Almighty God to protect them, along with all our people, and lead their steps on the road of firm principles to serve our beloved homeland. Hatim Hamdan al-'Azzawi, chief of the Presidential Office, attended the awarding ceremony. Following are the texts of the presidential decrees: In the name of God, the merciful, the compassionate. Presidential Decree: In view of RCC Vice Chairman 'Izzat Ibrahim Khalil's stand during the Mother of Battles, and based on the provisions of RCC Decree No. 407, dated 17 November 1991, we decree the following: RCC Vice Chairman 'Izzat Ibrahim Khalil shall be awarded the medal of high merit. The competent ministers shall implement this decree. [Signed] Saddam Husayn, president of the Republic In the name of God, the merciful, the compassionate. Presidential Decree: In view of the stand adopted by the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party National Command members, whose names are listed below, during the Mother of Battles, and based on the provisions of RCC Decree No. 407, dated 17 November 1991, we decree the following: The Arab Socialist Ba'th Party National Command members, whose names are listed below, shall be awarded the medal of high merit: 'Abd-al-Majid al-Tayyib al-Rafi'i Badr-al-Din Muddaththir Sulayman Ilyas Nakhlah Farah The competent ministers shall implement this decree. [Signed] Saddam Husayn, president of the Republic In the name of God, the merciful, the compassionate. Presidential Decree: In view of the stand adopted by the RCC members, whose names are listed below, during the Mother of Battles, and based on the provisions of RCC Decree No. 407, dated 17 November 1991, we decree the following: The RCC members, whose names are listed below, shall be awarded the medal of high merit: Taha Yasin Ramadan Tariq 'Aziz Taha Muhyi-al-Din Ma'ruf 'Ali Hasan al-Majid Muhammad Hamzah al-Zubaydi Mizban Khadr Hadi The competent ministers shall implement this decree. [Signed] Saddam Husayn, president of the Republic In the name of God, the merciful, the compassionate. Presidential Decree: In view of the stand adopted by members of the Iraq Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party, whose names are listed below, during the Mother of Battles, and based on the provisions of RCC Decree No. 407, dated 17 November 1991, we decree the following: Members of the Iraq Command of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party, whose names are listed below, shall be awarded the medal of high merit: Kamil Yasin Rashid Sa'di Mahdi Salih 'Abd-al-Ghani 'Abd-al-Ghafur Muhammad Zimam 'Abd-al-Razzaq Muhammad Yunus al-Ahmad Khadr 'Abd-al-'Aziz Husayn 'Abd-al-Rahman Ahmad 'Abd-al-Rahman Mizhir Matni 'Awwad Radi Hasan Salman 'Aziz Salih al-Nu'man The competent ministers shall implement this decree. [Signed] Saddam Husayn, president of the Republic
