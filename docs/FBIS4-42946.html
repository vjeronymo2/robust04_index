BFN [Text] Istanbul, June 9 (A.A.) -- The Istanbul Trade Chamber and the Kiev Trade and Industry Chamber signed a cooperation agreement here Wednesday [8 June]. An Ukranian delegation led by the deputy president of the country's trade and industry chamber, Victor Yanovsky [title and name as received], visited the Istanbul Trade Chamber (ITO). Deputy president of the board of directors of ITO, Omer Besim Kufrevi, said that mutual visits and mutual participation in fairs were to further the economic relations between Turkey and Ukraine. Under the agreement signed, the officials of the two chambers will exchange information and hold annual regular meetings, to increase trade between the two countries.
