940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Scottish Eastern boosts net asset value 33% to 105.4p The net asset value per share of Scottish Eastern Investment Trust rose by 33 per cent over the year to January 31, from 79.2p to 105.4p. After a Pounds 1m fall in the tax charge to Pounds 3m, net revenue for the 12 months advanced to Pounds 10.5m compared with Pounds 10.2m, producing earnings per share up from 1.59p to 1.65p per share. The proposed final dividend is lifted to 1.06p (1.02p) for an increased total of 1.58p (1.52p).
