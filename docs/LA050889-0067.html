May 8, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

180 ARRESTED IN RAID ON COCKFIGHTING ARENA

Authorities raided a cockfighting arena, arresting about 180 people and confiscating $30,000 in cash, guns, marijuana and cocaine, authorities said. Ten people, including the event organizers, were charged with commercial gambling and cruelty to animals in the raid Saturday. The others, mostly spectators, were charged with cruelty to animals.
