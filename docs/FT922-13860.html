920409 FT 09 APR 92 / Fifty die in Kashmir Indian security forces killed at least 50 people during operations against Moslem militants in the border Baramulla district in Jammu and Kashmir state, local residents said yesterday, Reuter reports from Srinagar. They said the security forces had besieged 40 villages surrounding Lake Wullar last week, killing local people. Mr Amar Kapoor,a senior police officer, said 20 militants were killed in the weekend operations and 22 arrested.
