Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [From the "News" program] [Text] Preparations for convening the Second Session of the Eighth Jiangsu Provincial People's Congress have been completed. A preparatory meeting was held at the Great Hall of the People in Nanjing yesterday afternoon. The meeting was presided over by Zhang Yaohua, deputy chairman of the provincial people's congress Standing Committee. He announced that there were 985 deputies to the provincial people's congress and that 875 deputies attended the meeting yesterday, thus forming a quorum. The preparatory meeting, by adopting the method of counting raised hands, elected the presidium and secretary general of the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress. The presidium is composed of 73 people, with Zhang Yaohua serving as its secretary general. The preparatory meeting adopted the agenda for the second session: hearing and deliberating the work report of the provincial people's government; examining and approving the implementation of Jiangsu's economic and social development plan for 1993 and its economic and social development for 1994; examining and approving the implementation of Jiangsu's budget for 1993 and its 1994 budget; hearing and deliberating the work report of the provincial people's congress standing committee; hearing and deliberating the work report of the provincial people's procuratorate; and electing the chief procurator of the provincial people's procuratorate. Shen Daren, Gao Dezheng, Zhang Yaohua, Ling Qihong, Wu Xijun, and Qu Qinyue were seated at the rostrum.
