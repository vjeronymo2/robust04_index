Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Davos, Switzerland, Jan 29 (CTK) -- Czech Premier Vaclav Klaus and Estonian Premier Mart Laar have agreed that the signing of a free trade zone agreement between the two countries is a necessity. Speaking after a meeting of the World Economic Forum, the two premiers told CTK the agreement would "fundamentally" strengthen the countries' bilateral economic relations, but that the date of signing such an agreement would depend on the progress of talks. Klaus added that he had been invited to visit Estonia, and that he would probably go some time in May.
