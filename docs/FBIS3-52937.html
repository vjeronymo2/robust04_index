Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Yusuf Bin-'Alawi Bin-'Abdallah, minister of state for foreign affairs, left the country today for Riyadh on a short visit to fraternal Saudi Arabia. This visit will be followed by a visit to the fraternal Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan. His excellency will also go to Damascus tomorrow to take part in the meetings of the foreign ministers of the Damascus Declaration, which is comprised of the Gulf Cooperation Council member states, Egypt, and Syria. On Tuesday, his excellency will go to Cairo to hold talks with a number of Egyptian officials. On Wednesday, he will visit fraternal Yemen and hold talks with the Yemeni officials. [passage omitted]
