July 15, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

DOUG COLLINS DESERVED BETTER FROM THE BULLS

Does anyone really understand the firing of Doug Collins as coach of the Chicago Bulls?

Here's a man who helped his organization make quantum leaps toward credibility and respectability in recent years and come within a heartbeat of the NBA finals this year.

But instead of a much-deserved pat on the back and a contract extension, they gave him the air. Collins deserved better.

ROSS PATTERSON

Burbank
