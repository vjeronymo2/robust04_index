BFN [Text] House of Representatives Speaker brother 'Abdallah Bin- Husayn al-Ahmar today received Iranian Ambassador in Sanaa brother Mohammad Jalal Firuz-Nia. They discussed parliamentary relations between the two fraternal countries. The House of Representatives speaker voiced his thanks to and appreciation for the Islamic Republic of Iran for supporting Yemen's unity, wishing relations between the two fraternal countries even greater development and prosperity. The Iranian ambassador invited a Yemeni parliamentary delegation to visit the Islamic Republic of Iran on behalf of the Iranian Majles.
