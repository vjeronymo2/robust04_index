Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Last night, 15 minutes after midnight, a shell fired from a Serbian position hit the PTT Engineering building, the Sarajevo headquarters of UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces], UN Major Manuel Capea told the Bosnia-Herzegovina press. The shell hit the duty-free shop and slightly damaged the news conference hall. Yesterday, throughout the day, the aggressor fired 54 shells at the city. During the night another 93 shells landed on the city. This morning there were bouts of intense sniper fire. According to UNPROFOR, yesterday five Sarajevans were killed and 40 wounded. The people of Sarajevo welcomed 1994 without water and electricity.
