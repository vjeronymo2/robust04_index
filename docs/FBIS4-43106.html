BFN [Text] State of Emergency Governor Unal Erkan has stated that 1,700 terrorists have been killed and 2,000 captured in operations conducted by the security forces in the last five months. Addressing the inauguration ceremony of the security directorate building in Adiyaman's Celikkan District, Erkan said that 4,700 abettors also have been captured during the same period. According to information given by Erkan 300 terrorists, 140 of them leaders, have surrendered to the security forces during that time. Some 2,000 rifles, 5,000 rounds of ammunition, 2,000 rockets, 1,950 hand grenades and 300 tonnes of foodstuff also have been seized. [passage indistinct].
