930603 FT 03 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Europe Energy Europe Energy, the USM-quoted mining group, incurred a loss of Pounds 132,817 pre-tax for the year to March 31 against a deficit of Pounds 163,953 for the preceding 15 months. The loss took account of a Pounds 61,855 (nil) provision for the permanent diminution in value of an oil investment. Losses per share were 1.06p (1.37p).
