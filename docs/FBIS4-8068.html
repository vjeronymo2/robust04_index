BFN [Text] Sarajevo, 10 Apr (TANJUG) -- NATO planes fired four missiles against civilian targets in the southeastern part of the Srpska republic, General Milan Gvero, deputy chief of staff of the Srpska republic's army, has confirmed to SRNA. He stressed that there are some civilian casualties and that the consequences of the air strikes are being investigated, but he did not give the precise locality where the NATO strike took place. The information service of the Herzegovina Corps has confirmed that two U.S. NATO planes carried out an attack in the area of Srbinje, southeast of Gorazde, at about 1820.
