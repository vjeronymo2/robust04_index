Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 31 (XINHUA) -- An 11-person team headed by Chiao Jen-ho, vice-chairman and secretary-general of the Taiwan-based Straits Exchange Foundation (SEF), arrived in Beijing this afternoon to prepare for talks between the SEF chairman and the president of the Association for Relations Across the Taiwan Strait (ARATS) on the mainland. Zou Zhekai, vice-president and secretary-general of ARATS, greeted them at the airport on behalf of ARATS Chairman Wang Daohan and Vice-Chairman Tang Shubei. Under an agreement reached between the two organizations, Tang Shubei and Zou Zhekai will meet with Chiao Jen-ho from February 1-4 in advance of talks between Wang Daohan, ARATS president, and Ku Chen-fu, SEF chairman. They will also exchange views on other issues of mutual or unilateral concern. Zou Zhekai said that he hoped this meeting would achieve good results and have positive bearings on the future talks. He hoped that greater progress would be made in relations between the two organizations. Chiao Jen-ho said at the airport that he hoped his trip would benefit the development of relations across the straits. He hoped that this meeting would improve cooperation, strengthen relations and promote understanding between the two sides. Reflecting on the talks that have been held, Chiao said that it is very important to increase contacts and talks. Every meeting or discussion will bring closer the understanding between the two sides, he said. He is willing to exchange views with Tang Shubei on the issue of mutual visits between top economic and trade personnel of the two sides, Chiao said. ARATS Vice-President Tang Shubei hosted a dinner in honor of Chiao Jen-ho at the Diaoyutai State Guesthouse this evening.
