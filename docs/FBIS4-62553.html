BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Grigory Arslanov, Pavel Spirin] [Text] Beijing May 16 -- Russia's State Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin, who is leading a delegation from the Russian legislature to Beijing, met Chinese Chairman Jiang Zemin on Monday for an exchange of views on bilateral relations. "I am very glad that the State Duma maintains friendly ties with the Chinese People's Assembly," Jiang Zemin said. "This will undoubtedly play an important role in the development of our relations," the Chinese leader said. Ivan Rybkin briefed the Chinese leader on the work of the State Duma, the Lower Chamber of the Russian Federal Assembly. He said the Russian lawmakers had held hearings on Russian-Chinese relations. "Irrespective of political sympathies we decided that our relations with great China are a high priority," he stressed.
