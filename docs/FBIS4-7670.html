BFN [Text] Warsaw, April 1 -- Sejm Speaker Jozef Oleksy said his visit to Moscow, ended Friday night, had a profound political, economic and state sense. Commenting on the visit after returning to Warsaw, Oleksy added: We want to do good business with Russia and our visit has been a contribution to that end. The Sejm speaker said the delegation of Polish MPs he had headed and their Russian interlocutors had searched for a future common European road for Russia and Poland.
