940115 FT 15 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Kode Intl issues profits warning Kode International, the computer group, has warned that profits for the 1993 year will be below market expectations. They are, however, expected to exceed the Pounds 1.03m pre-tax achieved in the previous 12 months. The shares fell 4p to 138p. The company expects to recommend a maintained final of 4p to give an increased full year total of 6p (5.5p).
