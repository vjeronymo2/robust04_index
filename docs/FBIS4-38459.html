BFN [Text] Baku, May 27 (TURAN) -- According to Azerbaijan Defense Ministry, relative calm on the front line was broken yesterday night. Beginning from May 26 at 20.30 p.m. Azeri positions near Akhmedagali (Agdamskiy region) were shelled. During night on May 27 Armenian troops shelled the village Sadarak (Nakhichevan Republic). In both cases Azeri side did not open fire. TURAN was told by the informed sources from Azeri Defence Ministry that shelling of Azeri positions was not by chance; the order came from Yerevan. The same source told that in spite of the fact of reconciliation, Armenian commandment continue to gather its technical and live forces in the North of Karabakh.
