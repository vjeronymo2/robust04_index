BFN [Text] The World Bank will extend a loan of $100 million to Turkey within the framework of the privatization program. According to a statement issued by the Treasury and Foreign Trade Undersecretariat, the World Bank Board of Directors discussed and approved a loan for the privatization project and the social security network technical assistance. The project aims at establishing the necessary infrastructure for the success of the privatization program in Turkey, retraining the work force in coordination with the privatization process, and basing the Public Economic Enterprises on a solid foundation within the economy.
