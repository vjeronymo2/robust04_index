Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] French Defense Minister Francois Leotard arrived here tonight for an official visit. He was received at the airport by Defense Ministry Director General David 'Ivri. Our correspondent Karmela Menashe reports that he will be received with full military honors at the Defense Ministry tomorrow and then visit an Air Force base. During his visit, Leotard will meet with senior Army officers and tour military industries.
