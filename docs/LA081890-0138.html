August 18, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: REAL ESTATE

Keith Cos. Win Contract: The Keith Cos., a Costa Mesa planning and engineering firm, has landed a contract to plan 600 acres for a resort in Taiwan.

The resort, on Mao-ao Bay at the northeastern tip of the island country, will be a waterfront resort with a marina. It is being developed by King Yue Recreation Co. Ltd.

There's no word on how much the contract is worth.
