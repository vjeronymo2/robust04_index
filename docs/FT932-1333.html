930624 FT 24 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Coastal 'no-go' zones rejected LORD DONALDSON, chair-man of the government's inquiry into marine pollution, will not recommend banning oil tankers and ships carrying hazardous cargo from Britain's most environmentally-sensitive coastal waters when he reports later this year. Lord Donaldson said the banning of ships from stretches of the UK coast was 'desirable but not possible'.
