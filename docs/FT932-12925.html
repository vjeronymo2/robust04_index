930426 FT 26 APR 93 / International Company News: British Empire Securities ahead Net asset value at British Empire Securities and General Trust rose 30 per cent over the 12 months ended March 31 1993 to 78.66p, compared with 61.15p. At September 30 1992 it was shown as 60.47p. In the half year ended March 31 1993 gross income came to Pounds 2.05m (Pounds 1.74m) including Pounds 338,000 (Pounds 65,000) gains from dealings. Earnings per share were 0.59p (0.3p) and the interim dividend is unchanged at 0.25p.
