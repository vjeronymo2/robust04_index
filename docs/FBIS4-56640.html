BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities monitored through 5 May: The Paraguayan Government has signed an agreement with the Republic of China (Taiwan) to avoid double taxation and prevent income tax evasion. The document was signed by Foreign Minister Luis Maria Ramirez Boettner and Taiwanese Ambassador to Asuncion Agustin Tingsu Liu. The agreement seeks to promote investments. (Asuncion ABC COLOR in Spanish 29 Apr 94 p 15) On 3 May, the Paraguayan Central Bank reported that the inflation rate during April was 1.1 percent. The report adds that the accrued inflation rate for the year now stands at 9.2 percent while total inflation for the past 12 months totals 21.9 percent. (Asuncion ABC COLOR in Spanish 4 May 94 p 17)
