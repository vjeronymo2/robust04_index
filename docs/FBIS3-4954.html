Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Vientiane, March 3 (KPL) -- Ajit Singh, ASEAN general secretary, and his wife, yesterday returned home ending his four-day official visit to Laos at the invitation of the Lao foreign minister. [passage omitted] Seeing the ASEAN general secretary off at the airport were Souban Salitthilat, acting foreign minister, and ambassadors of Malaysia and Thailand to Laos. [passage omitted] This is the first visit to this non-ASEAN member country ever made by the ASEAN general secretary to consolidate ties of friendship and cooperation between Laos and member countries of the ASEAN, on the one hand, and to pave ways for the acceptance of Laos to ASEAN membership at a proper time, on the other.
