Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, March 9, IRNA -- Finance Minister Morteza Mohammad-Khani and German Ambassador to Tehran Reinhold Schenk here today emphasized the need for expansion of mutual cooperation in the areas of trade, joint ventures and transfer of technology. Mohammad-Khani elaborating on the payment of the Iranian debts to Germany said Iran is interested to use technical assistance of the friendly countries including Germany. He also expressed hope that Germany would purchase a higher volume of the Iranian non-oil commodities. Schenk in reply said his country is also keen to expand its political, economic and cultural ties with Iran. Establishment of a German language training center which would be affiliated to the Irano-German Chamber of Commerce, would pave the way for the expansion of cultural and trade relations between the two countries, he added.
