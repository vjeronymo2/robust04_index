Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The indigenously developed surface-to-surface medium range missile Prithvi [Earth] was successfully launched at 1225 PM [0655 GMT] from the interim test range at Chandipur on sea in Orissa today. Prithvi landed accurately after three minutes on a small island (Tentuli Chada) located in the Bay of Bengal as planned. This was the 12th launch of Prithvi. The speciality of today's launch is that it was from mobile launcher with a 250 kg payload. The scientific adviser to the Defense Ministry, Dr. Abdul Kalam, and the three service chiefs witnessed the test launch.
