Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tallinn, Mar 09, BNS -- Starting from this week, the Estonian Foreign Ministry has three deputy chancellors responsible for different areas of the ministry's activity. Raul Malk, formerly adviser at the ministry, is in charge of the work of the political, information and press departments. Priit Kolbre, who so far headed the ministry's foreign economic policy department, was appointed deputy chancellor for foreign economic policy. The third new appointment is Epp Aruja, former deputy head of the ministry's administrative department, who became deputy chancellor for ministry administration.
