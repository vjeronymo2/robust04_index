BFN [Text] Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri returned to Beirut this afternoon from Damascus following talks with Syrian Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam and other senior Syrian officials. The talks dealt with the affairs of Lebanon and the area in the light of the developments in the peace talks. Information Minister Michel Samahah and Health Minister Marwan Hamadah also met this morning in the Syrian capital with their Syrian counterparts and discussed media and health affairs within the framework of the coordination between the Lebanese and Syrian ministries.
