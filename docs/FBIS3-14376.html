Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, al Rabia [Mar] 27, JAMAHIRIYAH NEWS AGENCY--The Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah condemned the unfair coercive measures imposed on the Great Jamahiriyah by the western countries through the so called Security Council. In a statement to JANA in Cairo today, the Yemeni foreign minister reaffirmed the solidarity of Yemen with the Great Jamahiriyah in countering these measures. He stressed the need for the prompt lifting of the unfair measures which violate all international laws and conventions.
