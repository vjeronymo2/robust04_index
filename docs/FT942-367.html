940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Smith St Aubyn (Hodings) SMITH ST AUBYN (Holdings): Pre-tax loss for year ended April 30 was Pounds 282,000 (restated Pounds 2.37m profit). Losses per share were 1.47p (6.21p earnings). Final dividend nil leaving 6.25p (6.2963p) for the year. Company is discount house subsidiary of King & Shaxson.
