August 14, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

ON THE SIDELINES;

NUNN, BARKLEY WEIGH IN FOR THE TITLE

Michael Nunn weighed in at 159 pounds for his International Boxing Federation BF middleweight title defense tonight against Iran Barkley. Barkley weighed the class limit of 160.

The scheduled 12-round bout was to be held at the Lawlor Events Center and televised by HBO.
