June 24, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: STUDENTS ARE TUTORS IN PEER TRAINING PROJECT

Several local school districts are participating in a California Scholarship Federation-sponsored peer training project.

Freshmen at Rosemead High School will receive instructional assistance in English, math, science and social studies from 15 high-school federation members.

Eighth-graders from Gidley Elementary School in El Monte and Muscatel Intermediate School in Rosemead will also receive the tutoring.
