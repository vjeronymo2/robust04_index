May 14, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

MORE ON AEROBICS

Apropos the furor surrounding Susan Littwin's humorous criticism of men in aerobics classes, "Walk Like a Man" (Feb. 5): Erik Fair's rebuttal "Sex and Exercise" (April 2) was a cute attempt, but his caustic remarks merely prove Littwin's point that men think they set all the standards.

MARGOT DOKKEN

Redondo Beach
