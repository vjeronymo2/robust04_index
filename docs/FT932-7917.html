930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / Settlement fees are reduced THE London Stock Exchange yesterday announced a reduction in its settlement charges to members amounting to about Pounds 7m per year. The current basic charge on the exchange's Talisman settlement system for UK equities will be reduced by 20 per cent (from 50p to 40p per Pounds 1,000 lot) with effect from May 1. Sir Andrew Hugh Smith, the exchange's chairman, described the reduction as 'an important first step in lowering the cost of settlement to the market'. He hoped to announce a further rebate in the coming months.
