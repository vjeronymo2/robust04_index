Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 11 (XINHUA) -- China and the United States have decided to start another round of negotiation Saturday [15 January] over a new agreement for bilateral textile trade, a spokesman for the ministry of foreign trade and economic co- operation announced here today. The three-day negotiation will be the fourth round to be conducted between the two sides on the new textile agreement. The former agreement on bilateral textile trade, reached in 1988, expired by the end of last year.
