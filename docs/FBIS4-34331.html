BFN [From the "Doing Radio" program] [Text] Politburo member Jose Ramon Machado Ventura has conducted a two-day visit to 12 Basic Cooperative Production Units [UBPC's] in Granma Province. During the visit, he observed the work being done in the province to repair damages caused by heavy rains in May. Alfredo Enriquez Pina, member of the Central Committee and first secretary of the provincial committee of the Communist Party of Cuba, accompanied Machado. At the end of the visit, Machado Ventura stressed the importance of strengthening the workers' and relatives' self-sufficiency efforts in the UBPC's. He pointed out that this is an essential endeavor to boost the workers' spirits as well as to improve work attendance and the use of human and material resources. Machado Ventura praised the positive changes in the cattle and sugarcane units visited in Granma, in terms of production and work organization, while criticizing low yields in the production of tubers and vegetables.
