Language: French Article Type:BFN [Summary From Poor Reception] "President Felix Ange-Patasse this morning signed a treaty on the harmonization of business law in Africa." Senegal was represented by "Justice Minister Jacques Baudin, who is Senegalese President Abdou Diouf's special envoy. The ceremony took place at the Renaissance Palace in the presence of Prime Minister Jean-Luc Mandaba, government officials, and the head of state's adviser. The signing of this treaty took place a few hours after the head of state received the Senegalese minister. On this occasion, President Felix Ange-Patasse made a statement in which he touched on the importance of true African integration."
