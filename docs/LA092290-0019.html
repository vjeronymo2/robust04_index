September 22, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

L.A. FANS ARE THRILLED (IN THEIR SPECIAL WAY) THAT RAIDERS WILL STAY

Al Davis has just completed a great grand slam in sports -- Oakland, Irwindale, Sacramento, Oakland and Los Angeles.

In the process, he made some of the officials of those cities look like fools, although perhaps they didn't have that far to go.

Davis is very good at what he does. But so was John Dillinger.

WILLIAM WELLS

Malibu
