Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] INTERFAX has learned that Boris Yeltsin's work schedule for the next few days has undergone some changes. In particular, it is expected that tomorrow the president will have a meeting with Chairman of the Federation Council Vladimir Shumeyko. The head of state will have a meeting with Chairman of the State Duma Ivan Rybkin on 20 January. Next week Boris Yeltsin is to visit St. Petersburg where the president will take part in the celebrations marking the 50th anniversary of the full liberation of the city from the blockade. In connection with this, the Russian president's visit to Kyrgyzstan, scheduled to take place some time between 20 and 29 January has been postponed.
