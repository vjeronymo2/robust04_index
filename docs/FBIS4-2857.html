BFN [Video report by unidentified correspondent; from the "Night News" program] [Text] From a national meeting on coastal defense management that closed in Hangzhou on 16 March, this reporter learned the Ministry of Public Security has decided, beginning 1 April, to organize into a single unit all public security coastal defense boats assigned to ensure offshore security and crack down on smuggling. These boats will use standardized names and serial numbers. When performing antismuggling and coastal patrol tasks, boats should bring with them proof issued by the Ministry of Public Security and which cannot be counterfeited that they are allowed to perform offshore duties; personnel performing offshore duty should wear a standardized duty-performing badge. Domestic and foreign ships may refuse inspection by patrol boats and personnel that do not meet the aforementioned requirements. [video shows marine policemen in action, an offshore duty-performing authorization booklet, and a duty-performing badge on a marine policeman's arm]
