930406 FT 06 APR 93 / Trafalgar House wins Pounds 25m contract TRAFALGAR House Engineering Contractors, part of the Trafalgar House group, has won a Pounds 25m contract for a production platform for the Markham field in the southern North Sea. The contract, awarded by Lasmo, operator of the field, covers the engineering, procurement, construction, installation and commissioning of the platform.
