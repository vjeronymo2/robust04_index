BFN [Text] Cairo, 7 May (MENA)--Field Marshal Muhammad Husayn Tantawi, minister of defense and war production, left Cairo for the United States this morning on a visit of several days. He was leading a large military delegation made up of senior armed forces commanders. During the visit, he is scheduled to have talks with U.S. Secretary of Defense William Perry and other U.S. Administration officials on military cooperation between the two countries and the military aid program for Egypt.
