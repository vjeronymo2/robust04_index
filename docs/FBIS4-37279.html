BFN [Text] On the sidelines of the official visit which the Namibian President Sam Nujoma is currently paying to Tunisia, a ceremony was organized at the foreign affairs ministry headquarters this afternoon during which Tunisian Foreign Affairs Minister Habib Ben Yahya, and his Namibian counterpart, Theo-Ben Gurirab, signed two agreements. The first agreement calls for the creation of a joint committee between the two countries, while the second represents a framework agreement for economic and scientific and technical cooperation between the two states.
