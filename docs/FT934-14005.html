931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Buy-out at Readicut Wool for Pounds 1.3m Readicut International has sold the Readicut Wool Company to Dardalion, a company formed by Mr JJ Bowskill and Mrs J. Jarman, two directors of Readicut Wool. The consideration was Pounds 908,000 cash with a further Pounds 392,000 for the premises. Readicut will use the proceeds to reduce net borrowings. Readicut Wool, which distributes rug kits, knitting yarn and handicraft products, made Pounds 264,000 pre-tax for the year to March 31 1993. Net assets at that date were Pounds 480,000.
