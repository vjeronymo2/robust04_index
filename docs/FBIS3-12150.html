Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Foreign Ministry and official and unofficial circles in Yemen have received with intense pain and indignation the news of the egregious crime perpetrated this morning by a member of the Zionist gangs in the Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron against innocent worshippers, of whom over 60 were killed and 300 wounded. The Foreign Ministry appeals to the international community to act to halt such terrorist acts, and condemns the Zionist authorities for obstructing the transfer of the wounded to hosptials. The Foreign Ministry calls on the community of nations to press for bringing pressure to bear in the interest of Israel's withdrawal from the occupied Arab territories.
