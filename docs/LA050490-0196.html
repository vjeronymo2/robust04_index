May 4, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

PANAMA REPORTING

Column One's "Accuracy a Casualty in Panama" (April 24) stated that the false information spread about the invasion of Panama can cause "an erosion of public confidence in the armed force."

What it actually causes is erosion of confidence in the press! Where are the journalists, correspondents, reporters who should be on hand to see and report the facts? What are you using for information, PR handouts?

BABS MEYER

Rolling Hills Estates
