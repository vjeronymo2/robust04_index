940316 FT 16 MAR 94 / MPs urge controls on Independent bid MPs of all parties demanding tight controls to preserve the independence of The Independent newspaper were joined yesterday by Mr Neil Kinnock, former Labour leader, and Mr John Watts, Tory chairman of the Commons Treasury committee. Mr Michael Heseltine, trade and industry secretary, is expected to announce today whether he is prepared to give the go-ahead to the consortium bidding for Newspaper Publishing, owner of The Independent and its Sunday newspaper. The consortium, which includes Mirror Group Newspapers, owner of the Daily Mirror, yesterday received approval from the European Commission to press ahead with its planned takeover.
