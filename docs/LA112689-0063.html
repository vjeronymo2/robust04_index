November 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

KANSAS CITY SUGGESTION

Although I enjoyed "Kansas City Is a City of Fountains" by Beverly Beyer and Ed Rabey (Nov. 12), I wonder why there was no mention of the Alameda Plaza Hotel. As far as I'm concerned, if I can't stay at the Alameda Plaza I don't want to go to Kansas City. The address is 401 Ward Parkway, Kansas City, Mo. 64112, (816) 756-1500.

LOUISE MILLER

Glendale
