BFN [Text] Residential areas of Kabul city were the target of heavy weapons fired by Golboddin-Dostam militia last night. An official source in the Kabul garrison told a BAKHTAR military correspondent that missiles were fired on Kabul city from the south and southwest, from areas under the control of the shameful militias of Golboddin-Dostam. As a result a number of our compatriots were martyred and injured. The report adds that in addition to the loss of life, four houses located in the 2d and 11th wards of Kabul city were also destroyed in the course of the missile attack.
