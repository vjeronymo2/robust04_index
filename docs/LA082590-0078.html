August 25, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

WORK WILL CLOSE PARTS OF VENTURA FREEWAY

Portions of the Ventura Freeway near the San Diego Freeway interchange will be closed this weekend due to construction, Caltrans announced Friday.

From 11 p.m. Saturday to 11 a.m. Sunday the southbound San Diego Freeway connector road to the eastbound Ventura Freeway, as well as the northbound San Diego Freeway connector road to the eastbound Ventura Freeway, will be closed. Two eastbound lanes between Balboa Boulevard and Kester Avenue and three eastbound on-ramps -- Hayvenhurst Avenue, Balboa Boulevard, Sepulveda Boulevard -- will also be closed.

The closures are part of the Ventura Improvement Project, a $76-million effort to widen and improve the freeway. Motorists may call Caltrans' 24-hour toll-free hot line -- 1-800-CALL VIP for updated information.
