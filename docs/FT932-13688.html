930422 FT 22 APR 93 / Observer: Suicidal Zeneca's pathfinder prospectus revealed just how keen it is on killing things. Herbicides, insecticides and fungicides are its stock in trade. And its products bear suitably militaristic brand names: Fusillade, Ambush and Karate are three. But judging by ICI's recent share price performance, new product names might include retreat, rout and surrender.
