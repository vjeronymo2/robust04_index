BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Twelve members of the illegal Islamic Great Eastern Raiders Front were caught in Ankara. According to a statement issued by the Ankara Security Directorate, security forces also seized 17 handwritten books stolen from the Amasya Sultan Beyazit Library. The income to be generated from the sale of the books, including a Koran written by the Prophet Osman, was to be used to finance the organization.
