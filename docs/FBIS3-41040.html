Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anatoly Gordeyev] [Text] Kiev February 3 TASS -- The Ukrainian parliament approved on Thursday President Kravchuk's actions during the January Russian-U.S.-Ukrainian tripartite summit in Moscow. The parliament stated that Kravchuk acted "in conformity with the parliament resolution of November 18, 1993, `on the ratification of the Soviet-U.S. Strategic Arms Limitation Treaty'." Under the Moscow accords Ukraine is to liquidate its nuclear warheads in exchange for security guarantees and one billion dollar compensation.
