Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Sarajevo, 10 Jan (TANJUG) -- The National Assembly of the Srpska republic resumed in Pale just before 1100 today. Deputies are considering drafts of, and proposals for, legislation affecting the economy. The final item on the agenda is a debate on the peace talks in Geneva and Brussels. Dr. Radovan Karadzic, president of the Srpska republic, will make introductory remarks on this topic.
