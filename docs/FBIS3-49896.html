Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] The Partnership for Peace program was the main subject of talks between Slovak Defense Minister Imrich Andrejcak and Lieutenant General Henning von Ondarza, commander in chief of NATO Allied Forces Central Europe. According to Imrich Andrejcak, Slovakia cares for a dignified presentation of the program in which all NATO member states would be interested. Henning von Ondarza asked for Slovakia's contribution to drafting new legal norms compatible with laws of the Alliance member states.
