920824 FT 24 AUG 92 / GMB to push training at TUC THE right to five days training a year should be included in collective bargaining negotiations, the TUC will hear next month. The GMB general union will urge congress to push training up the collective bargaining agenda by including claims for the right, in line with the European Social Charter. The TUC is circulating advice on how to get training on the bargaining agenda and to negotiate agreements containing targets for training as measured by National Vocational Qualifications.
