BFN [Quotation marks as received] [Text] "I think that we have reached agreement over the program," said Forza Italia Leader Silvio Berlusconi following a four-hour meeting with the National Alliance leaders Gianfranco Fini and Giuseppe Tatarella, and the Northern League's leaders Umberto Bossi and Roberto Maroni. The newly appointed prime minister stressed: "We discussed the program, not ministries. The government is being formed under favorable auspices." "There are no plans" to widen the majority. Ministries will be discussed next week. Berlusconi added: "I do not think that Bossi is going to set conditions concerning the choice of ministers."
