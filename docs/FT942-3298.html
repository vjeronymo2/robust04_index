940615 FT 15 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Sheriff Sheriff Holdings, the USM-quoted plant hire group, reported pre-tax profits for the six months to March 31 more than doubled at Pounds 1.2m, against Pounds 465,000. Mr Richard Dunn, chairman, said: 'The strong performance was a satisfying mix of organic growth and initial contributions from two acquisitions.' The shares advanced 14p to 278p. Turnover was Pounds 8.68m (Pounds 5.72m), including Pounds 1.86m from acquisitions, for operating profits of Pounds 1.25m (Pounds 534,000), including Pounds 122,000 from acquisitions. Earnings per share were 7.4p (3.5p) and the interim dividend is 1.75p (1.25p).
