941119 FT 19 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Worldcatch sale Receivers of Worldcatch, the property company which went into receivership in February with debts of approximately Pounds 39m, have sold the leasehold interest in a London building for Pounds 36m. The building, 2-14 Baker Street, comprises 73,495 sq ft of offices, 16,678 sq ft of retail units and a restaurant. Mr Chris Barlow, joint administrative receiver from Coopers & Lybrand said the sale followed an extensive marketing campaign. 'As a result I will be in a position to pay my appointing bank in full.'
