January 10, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

ARAFAT'S HELP

If it weren't so tragic, it would be funny -- the U.S. seeking PLO leader Yasser Arafat's aid in identifying those responsible for the bombing of Flight 103.

Why should Arafat have better access to that information, unless he is part of the terrorist network himself?

LESLIE FUHRER FRIEDMAN

Los Angeles
