February 15, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

DEST TO LAY OFF 25% OF WORKERS, SEEK BUYER

DEST Corp. said it will reduce its work force by 25% over the next month, and said it is seeking a buyer.

Robert Riopel, vice president of finance, said the cutbacks, which total "22 or 23 employees," will come by way of attrition or layoffs.
