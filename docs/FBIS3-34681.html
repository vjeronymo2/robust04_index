Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A report from Mosul says that hundreds of antiregime leaflets have been distributed by the heroes of the Islamic resistance inside the city and on the roads leading to it. The report says that the leaflets denounced tyrant Saddam's continued control of the ruling process and his execution of the officers who took part or were suspected of taking part in the coup attempt. The leaflets also urged the citizens to boycott the ruling regime, declare disobedience, refuse to carry out orders, and avoid joining the unfair ruling party. They threatened further armed operations against the regime's henchmen, associates, and institutions.
