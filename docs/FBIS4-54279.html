BFN [Text] Wataru Kubo, secretary general of the Social Democratic Party of Japan [SDPJ], explained his view that on the precondition that the Hata cabinet resigns en masse, the SDPJ may return to the coalition government. In this regard, Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata said in the evening of 22 May: "I am sure that (Mr. Kubo) will come to see me or call me to talk about his trip to China. There is no change in our policy of keeping seats for (the SDPJ) and they are welcome to come back at any time. I have to deal with the situation after directly talking to him." Hata made these comments to a group of reporters at his official residence.
