941223 FT 23 DEC 94 / International Company News: Pacific Dunlop forms venture in Sri Lanka Pacific Dunlop, the Australian conglomerate, has formed a new joint venture with the Development Finance Corporation of Ceylon, Sri Lanka's largest listed company, and this in turn has acquired a 75 per cent interest in Kelani Cables, a local cable manufacturing business, writes Nikki Tait. Pacific Dunlop, which has a majority interest in the Lanka Olex joint venture, said about ADollars 23m (USDollars 17.8m) would be invested in Kelani Cables over the next three years, with the aim of making this the first local manufacturer of metallic telecommunications cables in Sri Lanka. A new plant will be built as part of the venture, and this should start operating by early-1996.
