920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / UK Company News: CRT pays Pounds 1.5m for Reach-a-Teacha CRT Group has paid Pounds 1.5m to acquire Reach-a-Teacha, which trades as Wetherby Training Services and provides open learning materials principally in office, secretarial and computer training. Wetherby operates through 160 franchised locations throughout the UK, together with a small number of overseas franchises. For the year to April 30 it made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 263,000 from turnover of Pounds 929,000. The acquisition was made via the Pitman Training Group subsidiary. The consideration was met by the allotment of 659,000 ordinary shares, the issue of Pounds 399,800 bank guaranteed unsecured loan notes, and Pounds 220,000 cash.
