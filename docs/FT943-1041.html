940926 FT 26 SEP 94 / World News in Brief: Adams delivers message of reunification On the second day of his two-week US tour, Sinn Fein president Gerry Adams delivered his message of Irish reunification to the Hartford area's Irish-American Home Society. 'If you ask me why the British don't want Sinn Fein people to come and speak, it's because they fear your power,' Adams said. Meanwhile, prime minister John Major has sent former Northern Ireland minister Michael Mates to the US to counter the Adams publicity.
