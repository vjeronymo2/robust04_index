BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] The Ukrainian Central Electoral Commission is still counting the results of the elections held on Sunday [26 June]. However, officials in Kiev are by now convinced that Leonid Kravchuk is in the lead in the vote count. INTERFAX has learned this from the head of the Ukrainian president's information service, Vladimir Shlyaposhnikov. Unofficial data for 1000 today, as received by INTERFAX, show that the gap between the present head of state and his main rival in the elections, Leonid Kuchma, is about two million votes. The situation is as yet unclear only in Donetsk and Ivano-Frankovsk oblasts.
