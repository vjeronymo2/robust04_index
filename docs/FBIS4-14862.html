BFN [Edict No. 614 of Russian Federation President "Questions of the Protection of State Secrets" signed by Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin and dated Moscow, the Kremlin, 30 March 1994] [Text] In accordance with Article 20 of the Russian Federation Law "On State Secrets" and with a view to coordinating the activity of federal organs of state power in protecting state secrets, I decree: 1. That the Russian Federation President's State Technical Commission is to be temporarily vested with the functions of an interdepartmental commission for the protection of state secrets. 2. That the Russian Federation Government, the Russian Federation Security Council, and the Russian Federation President's Administration are to present within one month in accordance with established procedure proposals for ensuring the activity of the Russian Federation President's State Technical Commission in connection with its being temporarily vested with the functions of an interdepartmental commission for the protection of state secrets. 3. That the Russian Federation Government in conjunction with the Russian Federation Security Council and the Russian Federation President's State Technical Commission is to submit by 15 May 1994 in accordance with the established procedure proposals for further improving the management of the system of safeguarding state secrets in accordance with the Russian Federation Law "On State Secrets." 4. The present decree comes into force the moment it is signed. [Signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin Moscow, the Kremlin, 30 March 1994, No. 614
