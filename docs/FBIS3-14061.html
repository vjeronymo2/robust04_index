Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Following consultations with the PLO leadership in Tunis, Dr. Ahmad al-Tibi, PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat's adviser, said this morning that there was no intention of suspending the Cairo talks. Al-Tibi asserted that the statements made by Yasir 'Abd- Rabbuh, the head of the PLO's Information Department, were misunderstood. Dr. al-Tibi believes that Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh merely sought to highlight the obstacles events in Hebron are placing in the path of the autonomy talks.
