BFN [No video available] [Text] The German Foreign Affairs Ministry has circulated a statement by Foreign Affairs Minister Klaus Kinkel. It stresses that the Federal Government is watching the recent developments in Crimea with concern. Ukraine's territorial integrity and independence that are necessary for stability and peaceful relations in Europe cannot be called into doubt, the statement emphasizes. The German Federal Government hopes that all interested political forces are aware of the importance of principled obligations within the CSCE, and the unconditional need for joint action in accordance with these obligations.
