Through the use of electronic fund transfer mechanisms for these large accounts, the Department will be able to expedite and streamline its fee collection efforts. The TFCS is the computer-to-computer link between the U.S. Department of the Treasury and the Federal Reserve Bank of New York (FRBNY). This system provides the capability for: (1) Automated receipt and processing of funds transfer, and (2) computer-assisted generation of funds transferred between Treasury, Federal Reserve banks, and other banks utilizing the Federal Reserve Communications System (FRCS). The TFCS also integrates these transactions into Treasury's Government-wide Accounting System which accounts for all Federal receipts and outlays. Treasury maintains an account at FRBNY. As a result, banks that maintain an account at a Federal Reserve bank may transfer funds to Treasury through the FRCS for credit to the Account of the U.S. Treasury at FRBNY.
