July 3, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Robert C. Brosius has been named corporate director of quality management for MagneTek, Inc., and general manager of its program to supply electrical products for the national Superconducting Supercollider program.

He was formerly general manger of the Los Angeles-based company's Anaheim facility. Brosius joined Magnetek in 1980 as director of contracts and marketing in Anaheim. Before that, he held marketing and management positions with Aeroject General and Rockwell International.
