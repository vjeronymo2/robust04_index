Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 21 Jan--On 21 January, the Foreign Affairs Ministry issued the following statement to the Polish Press Agency: "It is with great satisfaction and approval that the Foreign Affairs Ministry of the Polish Republic heard of the signing on 14 January in Moscow of a three-sided agreement by the Presidents of the Russian Federation, the United States, and Ukraine. President Leonid Kravchuk's decision regarding the denuclearization of Ukraine will have a beneficial influence on the security and the stability of the region, of Europe, and the world, despite the fact that this process is expected to take seven years. The signing of the Moscow document opens the way to broader possibilities of cooperation between Ukraine and the Western world. The Foreign Affairs Ministry of the Polish Republic expresses the hope that the concluded agreement will be implemented very shortly."
