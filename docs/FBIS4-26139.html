BFN [Text] Seoul, 15 June (XINHUA) -- ROK President Kim Yong-sam met with Wang Zhongyu, Chinese chairman of the PRC-ROK Industrial Cooperation Committee and minister in charge of the State Economic and Trade Commission of China who is currently on a visit here, at the presidential palace this afternoon. Kim Yong-sam expressed admiration for the achievements in China's economic development. He also said: Our two economies are highly complementary; leaders of our two countries have reached a consensus on strengthening our industrial cooperation. Our two countries will carry out cooperation in the automobile industry, civil aviation industrial technology, program-controlled interchange, high- definition television, and other fields. This will have a significant impact on the economic development of both the ROK and China. Wang Zhongyu, who is leading a delegation to the PRC-ROK Industrial Cooperation Committee's first meeting that opened today, arrived here on 12 June. The delegation will leave for home on 17 June.
