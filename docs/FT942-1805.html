940622 FT 22 JUN 94 / International Company News: Former IBM man appointed Kodak treasurer Eastman Kodak, the US photographic products group, has appointed Mr Jesse Greene, former assistant treasurer at International Business Machines, as treasurer, Reuter reports from New York. The company said Mr Greene would report to Mr Harry Kavetas, senior vice-president and chief financial officer of Kodak. Mr Greene joined IBM in 1971 as an engineer and held several management positions before becoming assistant treasurer. He also held positions at IBM Credit.
