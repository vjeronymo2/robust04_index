920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Scottish Inv Tst Scottish Investment Trust's net asset value rose by 0.3 per cent to 206.8p in the first quarter to January 31 1992. Revenue before tax in the quarter was down 21 per cent from Pounds 3.7m to Pounds 2.92m, the decline being attributed to timing differences in income receipts.
