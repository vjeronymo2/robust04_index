Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: Russian President's Finance-Budget Administration in the Making"] [Text] Boris Yeltsin signed an edict yesterday on the formation of a finance-budget administration of the Russian Federation president within the head of state's staff. The new subdivision "is being created for the information and analytical support of the Russian president's activity in the area of statewide finances, budget, insurance, pricing, credit relations, and money circulation, as well as for preparing practical recommendations on conceptual and strategic questions of the finance-budget, taxation, monetary, and hard currency problems." Igor Moskovskiy was appointed its head.
