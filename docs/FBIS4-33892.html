BFN [Text] The following is a communique from the Civil Aeronautics Institute: Arriel Corral Gonzalez, pilot, defected yesterday, 12 June. Corral, who worked for the National Air Services Enterprise, took an AN-2 [as heard] type airplane that was being used for agricultural work in Pinar del Rio. The traitor flew the plane to the Boca Chica Naval Air Station in Key West, United States. Cuba is currently taking steps to have the plane returned.
