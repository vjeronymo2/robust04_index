940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / People: Redland Meanwhile, at Redland, Sir Colin is to be replaced as non-executive chairman by Rudolph Agnew, chairman and chief executive of Consolidated Gold Fields between 1983 and 1989. Agnew has plenty of experience of construction markets and was previously chief executive of ARC which, like Redland, is a large British supplier of aggregates. He is currently chairman of Lasmo and Stena Sealink UK.
