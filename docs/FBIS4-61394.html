BFN [Text] Moscow, 6 May (ITAR-TASS) -- In the Kremlin today, Russian President Boris Yeltsin met with Vladimir Shumeyko, chairman of the Federation Council. First results of the signing of the Treaty on Public Accord were reviewed. The two men also discussed cooperation between the president and the Federation Council. This was reported by the press service of the President of the Russian Federation. Vladimir Shumeyko also informed Boris Yeltsin about the results of the Bishkek talks on settlement of the conflict in Nagorno- Karabakh.
