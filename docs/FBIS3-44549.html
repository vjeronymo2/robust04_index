Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By Xiao Gong (2556 1362): "SHICHANG FAZHI DAOKAN (MARKET LEGISLATION GUIDANCE JOURNAL) To Be Published in January 1994"] [Text] SHICHANG FAZHI DAOKAN, China's only comprehensive journalist monthly specializing in propaganda on socialist market legislation, will be launched in January 1994. This journal is under the jurisdiction of the Ministry of Justice and run by FAZHI RIBAO. It will contain over 20 authoritative, readable, and informative columns that mainly deal with the market and legal system.
