January 15, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

PERU;

DEFENSE MINISTER REPORTEDLY QUITS

Peruvian Defense Minister Julio Velasquez Giacarini submitted his resignation five days after his predecessor was shot to death at a Lima shopping center, official sources said.

President Alan Garcia had not decided whether to accept the resignation, the sources said. Gunmen believed to be leftist guerrillas killed then-Defense Minister Enrique Lopez Albujar on Tuesday. The attack brought fresh criticism of Garcia's handling of the nation's growing leftist uprising.
