BFN [Text] Doboj, 24 Jun (TANJUG) -- This morning Doboj [Serb-held city in northern central Bosnia] was again the target of Muslim artillery attacks from the direction of Gracanica and Tesanj [Muslim-held towns]. A number of destructive shells landed on residential and other buildings, causing considerable material damage. A general alert was sounded at 1030 and the citizens of Doboj went to their shelters.
