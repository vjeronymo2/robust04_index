Language: Thai Article Type:BFN [Text] The Police Department is cooperating with the Commerce Ministry in suppressing intellectual property rights [IPR] violations by setting up a working committee with authority to arrest any offenders. Deputy Commerce Minister Churin Laksanawisit says that the Police Department director general has set up a committee comprising 37 police officers nationwide to suppress intellectual property rights violations. The committee is chaired by Police General Winit Charoensiri, deputy police director general for suppression. The cooperation shows the Thai Government's sincerity in suppressing intellectual property violations.
