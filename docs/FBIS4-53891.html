BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA May 21 -- Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet has signed a decision permitting the establishment of the Vietnam Bankers Association (VBA). VBA will be an NGO [Nongovernmental Organization] grouping on a voluntary basis all Vietnamese banking institutions, state-owned commercial banks, joint-stock banks and financial companies. Apart from the state commercial banks, some 40 joint-stock banks and two financial companies are now operating in the country. The new association will protect their rights within the legal framework. It should also function to promote their modernisation and mediate any disputes between member banks.
