Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Acts of Military Provocation in South Korea Will Not Be Tolerated, Warn Pyongyang Papers" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, March 25 (KCNA) -- The traitor Kim Yong-sam ordered all the puppet Army forces on to "a stand-by alert", instructing the puppet Army bosses to round off the preparations for invading the North through the "cooperation system" with the United States, and the U.S. ambassador in Seoul and the U.S. Army commander present in South Korea, the puppet defence minister and the puppet foreign minister were closeted over "reexamination" of the war preparedness of the U.S. imperialist aggression forces and the puppet Army. Papers here today brand this as unpardonable acts of military provocation and premeditated kickup of military confrontation. An analyst of NODONG SINMUN says: The grave military step taken by the South Korean puppet clique at the instigation of the United States is a very grave provocation going beyond the limit of mere threat to us. We can never tolerate and overlook the military racket of the South Korean puppets encouraged by outside forces. The Korean people and People's Army do not want a war but are never afraid of it. If the South Korean puppets dare ignite a war against us, our people and People's Army will answer it with a hundred-fold retaliation. A MINJU CHOSON analyst declares that if the Kim Yong-sam group provoked a war against the North in league with outside forces, we would take a decisive self-defensive step to defend the socialist motherland and the dignity of the nation.
