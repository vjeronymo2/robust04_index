Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Over the weekend the government sent 1,491 soldiers to assembly areas opened by the United Nations. According to the daily UN Operations in Mozambique [Unomoz] report, in the same period the Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] sent 185 soldiers to assembly areas. The government had by yesterday confined 23,543 soldiers, which represents 38 percent of the expected number of soldiers. Renamo had by yesterday confined 10,021 soldiers, representing 52 percent of the expected number of soldiers. A total of 36,574 soldiers from both sides have already been confined.
