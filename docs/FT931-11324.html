930203 FT 03 FEB 93 / World Commodities Prices: Jute & Cotton JUTE February/March c and f Dundee BTC Dollars 360, BWC Dollars 380, BTD Dollars 325, BWD Dollars 340; c and f Antwerp BTC Dollars 340, BWC Dollars 340, BTD Dollars 315, BWD Dollars 315. COTTON Liverpool- Spot and shipment sales amounted to 129 tonnes for the week ended January 29, compared with 38 tonnes in the previous week. Improved demand brought moderate purchases mainly in central Asian and American descriptions. Spanish and Russian growths made some headway.
