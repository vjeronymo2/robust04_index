Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] A statement issued by the Foreign Minister expresses serious concern regarding the continuing incident with the Bulgarian vessel the Khan Kubrat. Despite the initial promises of assistance made by the responsible Serbian bodies and their assurances that the vessel and its cargo were under control, the fuel cargo is being discharged from the Khan Kubrat barge train. The Foreign Ministry insists that the unloading operation should be stopped immediately and the ship returned to Bulgaria together with its cargo and crew. The commander of a military patrol launch belonging to the Western European Union has reported to the State Navigation Inspectorate in Vidin that at about 2200 hours [2000 GMT] this evening the Khan Kubrat vessel is expected to arrive in the port of Vidin together with its crew. The monitoring bodies have made arrangements for the reception of the vessel.
