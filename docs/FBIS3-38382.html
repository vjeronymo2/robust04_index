Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The French Defense Minister Francois Leotard has held talks in Switzerland with his Swiss counterpart Kaspar Villiger. Mr. Leotard briefed Mr. Villiger on France's United Nations involvement in Bosnia in reference to possible Swiss participation in future UN peacekeeping operations. The French minister also said he was concerned about Bosnia's warring factions mobilizing for an all-out war and reiterated that he favored protective air strikes.
