Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Riyadh, 25 Feb -- The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia has condemned the massacre perpetrated at dawn today by Israeli settlers against Palestinians who were praying in the mosque of Abraham in Hebron. This massacre resulted in the deaths of dozens of people and the wounding of hundreds. In a statement today to SPA, a responsible source expressed his condemnation and censure of this regrettable event, which bore the marks of extremism, fanaticism, and lack of respect for human rights on the part of the Israelis. The source laid responsibility for this massacre on the Israeli Government because of its disregard for the crimes committed by Israeli settlers against unarmed Palestinians. He called on the international community to face up to its responsibility of providing protection for the Palestinians, who are suffering under the Israeli occupation, and of stopping these serious crimes and massacres against them. In conclusion the statement indicates that this abominable crime might have a negative effect on the peace process in the Middle East.
