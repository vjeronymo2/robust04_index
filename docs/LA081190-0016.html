August 11, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CRISIS RESOLUTION

If one has any doubt as to the capability of Los Angeles to solve problems one has only to attend the Hollywood Bowl this summer!

After attending concerts for 30 years there, I experienced the first totally enjoyable evening because the intermission was no longer a race to the women's restroom -- they finally realized that when you have probably 8,000 women at an average concert, you need "facilities" to accommodate them.

GRACE MARGOLES

Malibu
