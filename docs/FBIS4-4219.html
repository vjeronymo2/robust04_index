BFN [By reporter Lin Minzhong (2651 3046 1813)] [Text] Buenos Aires, 24 Apr (XINHUA) -- At 1200 on 24 April, a CPC delegation headed by Hu Jintao, member of the Political Bureau Standing Committee and Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, flew from Buenos Aires to Brazil, to continue their overseas trip, after successfully concluding a four-day official visit to Argentina. Seeing them off at the airport were senior officials of the Argentine government and leaders of the Justicialist Party. Chinese Ambassador to Argentina Tang Yonggui also was present at the airport. Hu Jintao and his party arrived on 20 April to begin their visit to Argentina at the invitation of the ruling Justicialist Party. Accompanying Hu Jintao on his visit were CPC Central Committee Member and Zhejiang Provincial Party Committee Secretary Li Zemin, CPC Central Committee International Liaison Department Director Li Shuzheng, and others. During his visit, Hu Jintao held a meeting with Argentine President Carlos Menem and working talks with Justicialist Party leaders. Hu Jintao also met with Secretary General of the Argentine Presidency Eduardo Bauza, Speaker of the Chamber of Deputies Alberto Pierri, former Argentine President and Radical Civic Union Chairman Raul Alfonsin, and others. In addition, Hu Jintao and his party also visited Mendoza Province. Argentina was the second stop on the delegation's trip to South America; earlier they visited Uruguay.
