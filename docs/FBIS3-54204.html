Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The General Union of Algerian Workers [UGTA] also agreed to participate in the national conference. According to the UGTA national secretary in charge of information, the decision was made unanimously in view of the crisis in which the country is living. On its part, the National Coordinating Committee of the Martyrs' Children announced its participation in the conference in order to contribute all its energies to saving the homeland. The general coordinator of the National Coordinating Committee of the Martyrs' Children, Mr Moussa Touati confirmed the participation to Mesaoud Ramdani: [Begin Touati recording] We met the national council and presented the questions forwarded by the National Dialogue Commission [NDC]. It approved them and we appointed a group from the think-tank to participate and express the views of the National Coordinating Committee to the NDC that we want to participate in this conference and at least attempt to reduce the tension currently present between the children of the Algerian nation. [end recording] [passage omitted] Among the political parties which have so far announced their non-participation in the national consensus conference are: the National Liberation Front [FLN], the Socialist Forces Front, the Rally for Culture and Democracy and the Movement for Democracy in Algeria.
