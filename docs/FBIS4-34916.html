BFN [Text] Mexico City, 26 Jun (EFE) -- Today, Jorge Carpizo decided to stay on as interior secretary after resigning on 24 June. Mexican President Carlos Salinas de Gortari did not accept the resignation. Carpizo, in a letter addressed to the president, said after receiving the support of broad segments of the community "and to prevent anyone from saying I acted irresponsibly and unpatriotically, I will remain in the post of interior secretary." Carpizo's announcement averts a government crisis that many political analysts believe would have had serious consequences, if the resignation had occurred, on the political process Mexico is experiencing as it heads toward the 21 August presidential election,
