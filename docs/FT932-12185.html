930429 FT 29 APR 93 / UK Company News: Hoare Govett Smaller Cos Trust Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index Investment Trust, yesterday announced its first interim results. The trust was set up by Hoare Govett to track its Smaller Companies Index under the management of Broadgate Investment. The offer for subscription and placing was completed on December 14. On that date asset value per share was 95.5p. At March 31 the figure had grown to 108.1p. The board plans to announce an interim dividend in October. Net revenue for the period from October 5 1992 to March 31 1993 amounted to Pounds 162,055, for earnings per share of 0.57p.
