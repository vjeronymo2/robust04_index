931211 FT 11 DEC 93 / UK Company News: John Swan John Swan, the Scottish livestock auctioneer and estate agency, raised pre-tax profits to Pounds 233,200 for the half year to October 31. This compared with Pounds 162,700 last time which was struck after an exceptional charge of Pounds 65,000. Trading in the current six months was considered to be satisfactory, although the outlook for the price of both cattle and sheep remained uncertain. Turnover improved from Pounds 762,600 to Pounds 806,800 and earnings per share were 26p, against 18.2p.
