December 21, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

AUTO FOCUS: Thousands of cars are broken into every year, above, but there are ways to avoid becoming a statistic. Since many car burglars are after stereos, you can protect yourself by using pull-out stereo mounts and removing the player when your car is unoccupied, says Costa Mesa Police Detective Darell Freeman.

Common sense helps too. Park in well-lighted, occupied areas and use audible alarm systems. "Burglars hate to be heard and seen," Freeman says.
