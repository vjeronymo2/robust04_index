Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] On the auspicious birthday of Aba Abdullah al-Husayn, peace be upon him, and the Guards Day, Hojjat ol-Eslam val-Moslemin Seyyed Ahmad Khomeyni met a group of Islamic revolution guards today. Referring to the great role played by Imam Husayn in revival of the genuine Islam, he said: Imam Husayn, peace be upon him, by creating the epic of 'Ashura, changed the course of Islam's history for ever to serve the interests of justice-seeking free people. While offering congratulations to all the Islamic revolution guards on the Guards Day, he added: Our armed forces, by giving up their lives and assets, brought honor and pride to Islam and the country. [passage omitted] He went on: An important Islamic and national duty today is to assist and encourage the government, to create hope, and to give assurance to people and officials.
