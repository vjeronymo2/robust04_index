BFN [Text] The Knesset plenum rejected a proposed bill by Likud Knesset Member [MK] Eliyahu Ben-Elisar that any political agreement that transfers responsibility for water resources from Israel would require the approval of at least 80 MK's. MK Ben-Elisar said that if the water resources are under Palestinian control there is a danger that they will try to dry us up. Our correspondent Hayim Hecht reports that the motion was rejected by a majority of one.
