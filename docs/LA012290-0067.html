January 22, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

IMAGE = FACT: According to "The Lifestyle Market Analyst," Orange County's image as well-to-do and trendy is fully justified. . . . People here are 64% more likely to invest in real estate, 63% more likely to ski frequently and 62% more likely to travel overseas than the average American. . . . We drink fine wines, play tennis, own personal computers, ride bicycles, eat health foods and take vitamins far above the national average. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
