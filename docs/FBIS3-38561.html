Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Army Deployed in Calabria, Attacks on Police Linked"] [Text] Reggio Calabria, 3 Feb (ANSA) -- The first 200 of 1,350 army personnel deployed in the southern region of Calabria arrived here Thursday [3 February] as investigators confirmed that the same weapon was used in three separate attacks against Carabinieri police in which four officers were killed. One hundred soldiers will be posted to the city's main courthouse while the others will replace local police in the provinces of Reggio Calabria, Catanzaro and Cosenza for routine patrol duties in what has been dubbed Operation Riace, aimed at crime-fighting and prevention in the region. Separately, investigators said the same M-12 nine-caliber machine-gun was used in attacks December 2, January 18, and February 1 against Carabinieri police car patrols in the region. No one was injured in the first attack but both members of the two-man patrols were killed in the second and third attacks.
