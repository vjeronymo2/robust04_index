BFN [Text] Kyrgyz President Askar Akayev and Chinese Prime Minister Li Peng are deeply satisfied with the level, prospects, and dynamics of the development of bilateral relations, Imar Rasaliyev, the Kyrgyz president's press secretary, said at a briefing in Bishkek today. He was speaking about the results of talks between the leaders of the two countries. In particular, he noted a certain degree of progress achieved in discussing the issue of disputed territories between Kyrgyzstan and China.
