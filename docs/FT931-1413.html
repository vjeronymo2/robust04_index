930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / International Company News in Brief: DBS Land DBS Land, a Singapore property group, posted a 14 per cent rise in net profits to SDollars 58.2m (USDollars 36.4m) for 1992 from SDollars 50.9m the year before, AP-DJ reports from Singapore. Turnover advanced 17 per cent to SDollars 309.7m from SDollars 265.5m. Earnings per share rose 14 per cent to 9 cents from 7.9 cents and the dividend is maintained at 5 cents a share.
