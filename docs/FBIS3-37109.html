Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Sevindzh Abdullayeva and Viktor Shulman] [Text] Baku, 11 Feb -- Azerbaijan's Defense Ministry has today supported the suggestion of the International Olympics Committee that a cease-fire be introduced for the duration of the 1994 Winter Olympics. A statement circulated today says that "this step can have a positive effect on removing the tension and resolving the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict around Nagorno-Karabakh." The document says that Azerbaijan's Armed Forces are ready to stop fighting along the entire length of the front line if Armenia withdraws its troops from the territories it has seized.
