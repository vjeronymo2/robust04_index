Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] President Jiang Zemin held a special interview for ROK reporters only today. During the interview, he revealed once again that he will visit the ROK in the near future and that China will continue to exert efforts to resolve the North Korean nuclear issue. The following is a report by correspondent Yi Tong-sik: [Begin correspondent Yi Tong-sik recording] In a news conference for ROK reporters only, President Jiang Zemin emphasized once again that China's consistent position regarding the resolution of the North Korean nuclear issue is to ease tension and maintain security on the Korean peninsula, support denuclearization on the Korean peninsula, and holding dialogue between the parties concerned with patience. In connection with resolving the nuclear issue, President Jiang Zemin said that any act that intensifies contradiction will not be helpful in resolving the nuclear issue, and if tension and uneasiness increase, the ones that will suffer loss will be the Korean people themselves. President Jiang Zemin said reunification on the Korean peninsula is basically a matter of the Korean people, and that he hopes that South and North Korea will work to unfold active efforts to improve relations through dialogue so that ultimately they can provide a favorable condition for a peaceful reunification. President Jiang expressed satisfaction over the increase in economic cooperation between the two countries since the establishment of diplomatic relations, and hoped that future cooperation will be through an open way which will be of help for both sides, not in a closed way. In today's news conference, President Jiang Zemin showed faith in economic development and promotion in cooperation between the two countries, and highly assessed the significance of President Kim's visit to China. This has been Yi Tong-sik from Beijing. [end recording]
