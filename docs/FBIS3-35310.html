Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Feb. 22, IRNA -- The visiting Australian Deputy Foreign Minister, Rig Smith, in a meeting here today with Agriculture Minister 'Isa Kalantari discussed bilateral trade as well as cultural and diplomatic cooperation. Kalantari who is also head of the Iran-Australia joint economic commission called for a trade balance between the two countries as well as the expansion of ties in all fields. Heading a delegation, Smith arrived here Monday.
