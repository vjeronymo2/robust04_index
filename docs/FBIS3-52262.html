Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The National Progressive and Patriotic Front [NPPF] has held a meeting under its secretary general, comrade 'Abd-al- Ghani 'Abd-al-Ghafur, in the presence of the NPPF's party representatives. During the meeting, comrade 'Abd-al-Ghani 'Abd-al-Ghafur stressed that leader President Saddam Husayn's historic message in which he congratulated the great people of Iraq on the new year carries great meanings and bodes well for a happy, prosperous new year. He noted that the killing and sabotage committed by the agent cliques in the Autonomous Region proves that these cliques are protected by their colonialist and Zionist masters. He affirmed that our Kurdish people, who take pride in the historic leader struggler, President Saddam Husayn, will step up their struggle against these agent cliques. During the meeting, the participants discussed a plan for the NPPF's General Secretariat for 1994 on the Arab and world levels, as well as political issues.
