November 20, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

MASTER'S TIES WESLYAN IN NAIA SOCCER TOURNAMENT

The Master's College soccer team entered the National Assn. of Intercollegiate Athletics Championships at the College of Boca Raton on Monday looking for a little respect.

After playing West Virginia Weslyan, the No. 1-seeded team in the 12-team tournament, to a 1-1 tie, the 12th-seeded Mustangs accomplished their goal.

Master's (9-10-4) took a 1-0 lead at the 18-minute mark of the second half when Chris Palm scored his 17th goal of the season on an assist from Jorge Barba and Randy Whitfield.

Weslyan (17-1-3) countered with a goal of its own 10 minutes later.

Master's, the NAIA District 3 champion, will play William Carey (Miss.) College (14-3-0) in a pool play match at 8 a.m. today.
