930602 FT 02 JUN 93 / People: Snijders to head Eurotrack Indices The FT-SE Actuaries Share Indices Steering Committee yesterday announced the appointment of Dick Snijders as chairman of the FT-SE Eurotrack Indices Committee. He will also be a member of the steering committee. Snijders is managing director of Philips Pensioenfondsen based in Eindhoven.
