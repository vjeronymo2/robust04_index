Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Article by "(ivh)": "An Air Force Faculty Has Opened in Brno"] [Text] Brno--A new Air Force and Air Defense Faculty was opened yesterday at the Military Academy in Brno. According to the Academy's spokesman, Major Pavel Pazdera, pilots, aircraft technical specialists, and airfield servicing staff will be trained at the faculty. Graduates receive an electronic-based education that can also be applied at civilian airports. The faculty's establishment comes as a result of the reorganization of the Academy and of the requirements for training aircraft specialists for the Czech Army. The only military Air Force college in the former CSFR was in Kosice.
