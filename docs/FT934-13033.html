931023 FT 23 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Bridport-Gundry Bridport-Gundry, maker of netting and twisted woven products, announced a decline in pre-tax profits from Pounds 589,000 to Pounds 162,000 for the year to July 31. Mr David Sebire, chairman, said the results had been affected by costs relating to management changes, discontinued activities and provisions for surplus property. Turnover from continuing operations was Pounds 29.6m (Pounds 27.3m). Earnings were 0.84p (4.59p). The final dividend is cut from 2.6p to 1p, making 2.5p (4.1p).
