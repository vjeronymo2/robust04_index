Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Indictments have been filed in the Jerusalem Magistrate's Court against three Israelis who are accused of publicly supporting the Hebron massacre and expressing solidarity with Barukh Goldstein. The three are Shmu'el Ben-Yishay, Amnon Tadmor, and Arye Ben-Yosef. They are charged with violating the antiterrorism act. Shmu'el Ben-Yishay has already been under administrative detention for a week, and the High Court of Justice yesterday discussed a petition against the move.
