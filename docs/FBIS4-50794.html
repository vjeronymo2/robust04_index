BFN [By reporter Kang Xinwen (1660 2450 2429)] [Text] Brussels, 21 May (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC] National Committee, departed Brussels for home by special plane after successfully concluding a five-day official goodwill visit to Belgium today. He was seen off at the airport by Hughes [name as received], second vice president of the Belgian Senate, and Chinese Ambassador to Belgium Ding Yuanhong. Before boarding the plane, Chairman Li Ruihuan shook hands with and bid farewell to Vice President Hughes, thanking him for the kind invitation and warm reception. The latter congratulated Li Ruihuan on his successful visit. Members of Li Ruihuan's entourage who returned in the same plane were Zhu Xun, secretary general of the CPPCC National Committee; Liu Feng, member of the CPPCC National Committee and chairman of the CPPCC Zhejiang Provincial Committee; Mei Xiangming, Standing Committee member of the CPPCC National Committee and vice chairman of the China Association for Promoting Democracy Central Committee; Jiang Enzhu, vice foreign minister; Wu Guanzhong, Standing Committee member of the CPPCC National Committee; Fang Fan, member of the CPPCC National Committee; and Tang Longbin, member and vice chairman of the Foreign Affairs Committee of the CPPCC National Committee. Belgium was the fifth leg of Chairman Li Ruihuan's European tour. Prior to this, he had visited Finland, Sweden, Norway, and Denmark. During his stay in Belgium, he held talks with the principal leaders of the Belgian Parliament and Government on bilateral relations and international issues of mutual concern. He was also invited to visit Belgium's Chemistry Union, Bell Telephones Company, Antwerp drilling center, and other enterprises. It has been learned that from the special plane, Li Ruihuan cabled a message to Belgian House of Representatives President Nothomb and Senate President Swaelen, expressing his heartfelt gratitude to the Belgian Parliament and Government for the warm hospitality accorded him during his stay in their country. He also asked them to convey appreciation to Prime Minister Dehaene and Vice Prime Minister and concurrently foreign minister Claes.
