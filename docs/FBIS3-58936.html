Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Moscow (DDP/ADN) -- Gerhard Frey, leader of the right-wing extremist German People's Union (DVU), is expected to arrive in Moscow today to hold talks with Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, leader of the right-wing extremist Liberal Democratic Party of Russia, DDP/ADN news agency learned from parliamentary sources. They said Frey and Zhirinovskiy intend to hold a joint news conference tomorrow at the State Duma building. Zhirinovskiy only met Frey at the end of December during a short stopover in Munich. Zhirinovskiy and Frey have had close contacts for years. [passage omitted]
