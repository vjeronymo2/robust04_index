920310 FT 10 MAR 92 / Appointments: New finance director for Mountleigh Kevin Gregory has been promoted to finance director of MOUNTLEIGH on the resignation of Jeff Warren. ***** Michael Foster, formerly md of COURAGE, has been appointed chairman of Courage UK and Europe. Nick Bryan is promoted to take his place as md of Courage UK. Ian McHoul is appointed executive director, strategy and planning. David Clayton-Smith is promoted to group executive director, marketing, and Colin Ions to group executive director, human resources. ***** John Wedgwood has been appointed company secretary of LASMO on the resignation of Edwin Titcombe.
