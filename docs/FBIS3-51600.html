Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Bulletin No. 24 issued by the Mexican National Defense Secretariat in Mexico City on 20 January; from the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] (Isabel Maria Rabel), a Swiss national assigned to the UN High Commissioner for Refugees, and William Calderon Sanchez, a Mexican national, arrived at a military checkpoint in Chancala at 1545 [2145 GMT] aboard a Wagoneer-type vehicle registered as OZFDSRORG INT MEX, belonging to the United Nations. They stated that at 1200 [1800 GMT] 20 armed men carrying rifles and hand grenades held them up near the 200 km marker along the Palenque Benemerito de las Americas Highway. The men wore gray shirts and dark trousers and had their faces covered. They robbed Calderon Sanchez of 1,000 new pesos and his credit and identification cards.
