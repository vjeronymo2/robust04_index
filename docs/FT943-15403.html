940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / UK Company News: EuroDollar More than 1.3m EuroDollar shares were traded in first-day dealings yesterday following the car hire group's placing and public offer. The shares, offered at 220p, dipped during the day to 218p but recovered to close at 222p. The issue last month involved 22.7m shares and valued the group at Pounds 107.9m.
