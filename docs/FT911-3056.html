910426 FT 26 APR 91 / International Company News: Sharp rise at Sun Micro SUN MICROSYSTEMS, the California-based maker of computer workstations, reported a sharp advance in third-quarter profit to Dollars 58.7m after tax from Dollars 36.7m in the year-ago period, writes Nikki Tait. The company, which lifted sales to Dollars 848.3m from Dollars 632.2m, said its operating margin improved by 2 1/2 percentage points from the second quarter, pushing this above 10 per cent. Earnings per share were up to 56 cents from 40 cents.
