941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Vega rises 35% A number of high-profile contract awards in its major market areas helped Vega Group, the software and systems engineering concern, lift first-half pre-tax profits by 35 per cent from Pounds 757,000 to Pounds 1.02m. Turnover moved ahead 27 per cent to Pounds 5.7m, against Pounds 4.5m. Earnings per share were 4.76p (3.54p) and the interim dividend was raised from 1.04p to 1.4p.
