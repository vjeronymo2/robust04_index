November 30, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

CONSEQUENCES OF PORNOGRAPHY

We were delighted to see the article on the social impact of illegal pornography. Just as the community has united on the war against drugs, we must also join together to eradicate illegal pornography which debases our women and children, and contributes to a wide variety of violent crimes.

Because we believe that one of the most urgent causes today is the more stringent enforcement of existing laws against illegal (child and violent) pornography, we implore our local law enforcement agencies and the U.S. attorney's office to prosecute those who have broken these laws.

JOANNE GALLAGHER

JOE GALLAGHER

Sherman Oaks
