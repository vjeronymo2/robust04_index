BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 4 (KCNA) -- The cooperative farms across the country have finished rice-transplanting in all paddy fields except northern highland and cold areas and some rice seedling beds. The agricultural working people have made a breakthrough for reaping another rich crop this year by finishing rice transplanting in the time most opportune for the highest yield. Weeding has already entered a full-scale stage in Paechon, Chaeryong and Anak, South Hwanghae Province, and in Unjon, Chongju and Yomju, North Pyongan Province, and many other areas on the west coast. Cooperative farms are pulling their full weight on manuring and tending crops.
