Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In Afghanistan, the forces loyal to President Borhanoddin Rabbani and Premier Golboddin Hekmatyar have agreed to another four-day cease-fire in Kabul. The cease-fire will come into effect from tomorrow morning. A member of the Islahi Shura [Reformation Council] told AIP [AFGHAN ISLAMIC PRESS] in Jalalabad that during the cease-fire, roads leading to the Afghan capital will be opened for traffic to enable the vehicles carrying foodstuffs and medicines to reach Kabul. The cease-fire has been brokered by the six-member delegation of Rabata al-Alam al-Islami [World Muslim League] and Islahi Shura of Afghanistan. An APP report says both President Rabbani and Premier Hekmatyar have agreed to the cease-fire.
