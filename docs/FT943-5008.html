940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Computer People doubles to Pounds 733,000 Computer People, the computer recruitment service and consultancy, reported pre-tax profits doubled from Pounds 364,000 to Pounds 733,000 for the half year to end-June. The shares rose 10p to 195p. The outcome was struck after an exceptional charge of Pounds 530,000 relating to management reorganisation, and came from revenue up from Pounds 33.5m to Pounds 38m. Earnings per share emerged at 3.42p (1.45p) and the company is restoring the interim dividend with a pay-out of 1p.
