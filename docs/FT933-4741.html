930907 FT 07 SEP 93 / World News in Brief: Tapie to sue over cup soccer ban Olympique Marseille owner Bernard Tapie is to sue the French soccer authorities following the decision of Uefa, the European game's governing body, to ban his club from the European Cup because of allegations of bribery. The suit from the businessman and former Socialist minister is aimed at forcing the French authorities to name Marseille as the country's representative in this season's competition. Uefa has asked for a replacement club to be nominated.
