BFN [Text] Tokyo, June 20 KYODO -- New German Ambassador to Japan Heinrich Dieckmann proposed on Monday [20 June] that Japan and the European Union hold a summit meeting after Germany's general elections in October, Japanese officials said. The officials said Dieckmann made the proposal when he paid a courtesy call on Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa at the ministry. Germany will hold the post of the EU's rotating chair for six months from July.
