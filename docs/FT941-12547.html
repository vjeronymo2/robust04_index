940129 FT 29 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Wholesale Fittings 10% higher Wholesale Fittings, the Essex-based distributor of industrial and commercial electrical products, achieved a 10 per cent increase in profits to Pounds 1.03m pre-tax for the half year ended October 31. Sales improved from Pounds 30.3m to Pounds 34.5m. Basic earnings emerged at 4.96p (4.4p) and the interim dividend is held at 3.23p. Interest income declined from Pounds 137,000 to Pounds 15,000. Second half sales to date were showing an 'even stronger improvement' than for the opening six months.
