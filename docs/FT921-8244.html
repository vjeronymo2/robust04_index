920218 FT 18 FEB 92 / International Company News: Vard suffers NKr115m loss VARD, the Norwegian cruise ship and ferry owner, suffered a NKr115m (Dollars 18.2m) loss in 1991, compared with a NKr191m profit in 1990, writes Karen Fossli in Oslo. The setback was mainly due to Kloster Cruise, which posted a NKr126m operating loss for the year.
