June 19, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

SIDELINES;

INDY CARS TO HEAD DOWN UNDER

An Indy-car race -- the first ever outside North America -- will be staged in Surfer's Paradise in Queensland on March 17, Queensland state government officials announced today.

Queensland Treasurer Keith De Lacy said the 195-mile Gold Coast Grand Prix, which will be run on the 2.8-mile road course in the Gold Coast resort town, will begin CART's 1991 schedule and will receive financial backing from the state.
