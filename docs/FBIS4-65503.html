BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- The Federal Executive Committee of the Christian Democratic Union [CDU] has condemned the right-wing extremist riots in Magdeburg "with utmost severity." "Xenophobic excesses and all other acts of violence must be punished with the utmost severity of the law," CDU Secretary General Peter Hintze said in Bonn on Monday [16 May]. The alleged violent criminals, who were arrested on Ascension Day [12 May], should not have been released again during the same night, Hintze stated. Saxony-Anhalt Minister-President Christoph Bergner (CDU) will investigate why the hooligans were released from police detention so quickly, he announced.
