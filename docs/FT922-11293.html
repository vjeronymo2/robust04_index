920427 FT 27 APR 92 / Construction Contracts: Facelift for east London tower blocks The Southern Construction division of M J GLEESON group has been awarded two London contracts, together worth almost Pounds 10m. At Tower Hamlets, Gleeson is to undertake the Pounds 5.69m internal and external refurbishment of Wapping Neighbourhood of two 14-storey tower blocks built in the 1960s. At Greenford, Gleeson will construct a multi-storey car park for Glaxo Holdings, which is designed and landscaped to be barely visible. It will take 370 cars and cost Pounds 3.1m.
