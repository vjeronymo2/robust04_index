Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [TA SR report: "Another Deputy Expelled"] [Text] Bratislava--The Movement for a Democratic Slovakia [HZDS] Municipal District Presidium at Strecnianska Street in Bratislava 5 decided on 9 March to terminate the HZDS membership of Vladimir Bajan, deputy to the National Council of the Slovak Republic [NRSR]. The Municipal District Presidium expressed full support for the prime minister and the government of the Slovak Republic and for the NRSR chairman.
