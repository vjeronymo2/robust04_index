921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / International Company News: Brilliance China sets issue price BRILLIANCE China Automotive, which owns a majority stake in China's largest mini-bus manufacturer, last night became the first Peking-controlled company to raise equity finance in the US with an Dollars 80m share offering. Its initial public offering of 5m shares was priced at Dollars 16 a share through underwriters led by First Boston. The Bermuda-based company will be listed on the New York Stock Exchange, where it will be classified as a Hong Kong business, since it has offices in the colony.
