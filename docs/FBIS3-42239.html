Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Retribution [Vozmezdiye] terrorist group has demanded that the Yakutsk authorities hand over $500,000 and 500 million rubles at the city's airport on 4 February. Otherwise they are threatening to blow up facilities providing vital services to the capital of Yakutia. According to INTERFAX, a cassette recording with these demands was handed in to the mayor's office by an unidentified person. Employees of the Ministries of Security and Internal Affairs are searching for the criminals.
