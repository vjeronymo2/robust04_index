930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / Finance and the Family: Unit Trust sales enjoy bumper month -At a glance the Pounds 981m of net sales making it the third best month on record. Helped by buoyant world stock markets, funds under management reached a new high of Pounds 84bn. Private investors bought a net Pounds 555m of funds, up 22 per cent on the previous month. Ironically in August 1992, which in retrospect was an ideal time to buy unit trusts, net sales to private investors were just Pounds 14.6m.
