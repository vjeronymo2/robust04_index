930824 FT 24 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Caverdale offer for Trust Parts Caverdale Group, distributor of industrial consumables, is making an offer worth a maximum Pounds 864,000 for Trust Parts, a supplier of engineers' and motor trade accessories. Irrevocable acceptances have been received in respect of 76 per cent of the total 1.06m shares. The initial offer is 73p per share, worth Pounds 774,000 on 100 per cent acceptance, and further cash deferred consideration will be payable to a maximum Pounds 90,000. In the year ended October 31 1992 Trust Parts incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 81,0000, but the following eight months to end-June indicated a return to profitability.
