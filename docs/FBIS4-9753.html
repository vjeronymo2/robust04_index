CSO [Text] The standing committees of the Chamber of Deputies were established definitively yesterday when the speaker of the house, Carmelo Lauria, approved their makeup. The speaker asserted that with the establishment of these committees "Congress will start to work at full capacity and must greatly improve its legislative efforts." The formation of the working committees had been paralyzed by the political controversy that arose when their chairmen were to be appointed. This controversy prompted the first political crisis of the new Congress and created great confusion among the public.
