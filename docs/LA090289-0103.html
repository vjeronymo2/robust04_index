September 2, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Oczy Czarnie, ridden by Corey Black, came from next to last to score an eight-lenghth victory in the $112,800 Pucker Up Stakes at Arlington Racecourse in Arlington Heights, Ill.

Oczy Czarnie, an import from France making her American debut, paid $15.20, running 1 1/8 miles on a soft grass course in the slow time of 1:55. Coolawin, the 7-10 favorite in the nine-horse field of 3-year-old fillies, finished fifth.
