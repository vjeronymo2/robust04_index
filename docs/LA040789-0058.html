April 7, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

VETS BACK FROM VISIT WITH EX-FOES

A group of Vietnam veterans returned from Southeast Asia on Thursday to an airport greeting of confetti and hugs and kisses.

"Boy, we didn't get a greeting like this when we came back from the war," said Army veteran Steve Brooks of Santa Cruz.

While in Vietnam, the veterans helped build a 14-room clinic for their former enemies, using $50,000 in donated money.

With the cooperation of the Vietnamese government, the veterans, along with about 30 Vietnamese, erected a one-story, 2,500-square-foot building at Vung Tau, 60 miles from Ho Chi Minh City, formerly Saigon.
