Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Serbian Court of Prishtina District is expected to begin the trial of 19 Albanians who are charged with allegedly creating the organizations "National Movement for the Republic of Kosova" and "National Movement for the Liberation of Kosova." They are also charged with hostile activity in infringing the territorial integrity. The trial has been postponed four times so far. Such trials, more of which are expected to be trumped-up in the future, aim to frighten Albanians and encourage them to leave Kosova.
