Language: French Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "New GIA Chief"] [Text] Sayah Attia has become the new chief of the Armed Islamic Group (GIA), replacing Mourad Si-Ahmed, who was killed by Algerian security forces in Algiers in February. Sayah Attia, who already was at the helm of several active GIA cells, is described in the local press as "the most violent fundamentalist terrorist." On Monday and Tuesday alone, three Algerian citizens were killed in Islamic attacks.
