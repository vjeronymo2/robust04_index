Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, March 1 (XINHUA) -- The Russian counter intelligence service said today that it had arrested a Russian official working in a military-industrial complex, charging him with spying for the British secret service. It said the spy was arrested on January 15 and faced criminal charges 10 days later, without specifying his name. The charged spy sold information on the latest Russian research in new armaments and their technical characteristics, the financing of military research and development and the state and prospects of Russian military cooperation with foreign countries, according to the service. "On orders of British special services and for monetary remuneration, the Russian citizen collected and passed classified information of military and economic character," the service pointed out. According to the preliminary investigation, the Russian official received instructions on using hiding-places for supplying information to the British Embassy in Moscow.
