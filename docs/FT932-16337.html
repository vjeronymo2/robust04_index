930406 FT 06 APR 93 / UK Company News: Denistron Denistron International, the electronic components group, swung from losses of Pounds 377,000 to profits of Pounds 1.01m pre-tax for 1992. The shares rose 5p to 32p. Earnings of 4p per share compared with losses of 5,5p. A proposed final dividend of 1p makes a 1.4p (nil) total.
