920103 FT 03 JAN 92 / Commodities and Agriculture: Rubber buffer stock manager may buy today MR ALDO Hofmeister, the International Natural Rubber Organisation's buffer stock manager, said yesterday he might resume support buying today and stressed that he had funds to continue his buying programme, reports Reuter from Kuala Lumpur. 'Conceivably, I may step in', he said. 'We can enter the market but we are not going to use the (financial) resources in one day.'
