September 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE WORLD

The body of a Palestinian suspected of collaborating with Israeli authorities and missing since early August was discovered in an abandoned well about 15 miles outside Nablus in the Israeli-occupied West Bank, Arab sources said. Masked Palestinians had reportedly kidnaped Salah Mohammed Hadi, 22. In the occupied Gaza Strip, two Arabs were wounded by army gunfire in clashes in the Nusseirat refugee camp, Arab reports said. The clashes occurred after a camp curfew was imposed and residents were forced to paint over political graffiti. A third Arab was reported wounded in a clash with troops in Nablus.
