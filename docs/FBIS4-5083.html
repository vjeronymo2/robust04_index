BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 6 (KCNA) -- The South Korean Federation of General Student Councils (Hanchongnyon), the vanguard organization of one million students in South Korea, reportedly contended, in a document adopted at a rally, that the United States should find a key to the solution of what it calls "nuclear issue" of the North in the improvement of relations with the North, not in pressure. The document stated that "after it declared its withdrawal from the nuclear Non-Proliferation Treaty the North has totally frustrated with its flexible and steadfast strategy the maneuverings of the United States and other imperialists to isolate it."
