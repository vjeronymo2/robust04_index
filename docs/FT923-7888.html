920814 FT 14 AUG 92 / Australian GDP grows by 0.6% Australia's gross domestic product grew by a modest 0.6 per cent in the three months ending June, confirming that national recovery from recession would be a long slow haul, writes Emilia Tagaza from Canberra. The rise was the largest since recovery began a year ago and brought the 1990-91 annual growth rate to 1.6 per cent. After release of the national account figures yesterday, the currency regained some lost ground. But analysts said the markets would stay edgy until the projected budget deficit size is known next week. Forecasts put it near ADollars 14bn (Pounds 5.4bn).
