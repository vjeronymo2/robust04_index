940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Frank Usher ahead Frank Usher, the USM-quoted designer and maker of dresses and special occasion wear, raised pre-tax profits by 14 per cent from Pounds 1.22m to Pounds 1.38m in the year ended May 31. Turnover grew 12 per cent to Pounds 19.7m, of which exports accounted for Pounds 9.44m or 48 per cent. Earnings per share climbed to 13.1p (11.3p) while a recommended final dividend of 4.5p (3.5p) makes a total of 7p (6p).
