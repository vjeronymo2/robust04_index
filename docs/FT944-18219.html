941005 FT 05 OCT 94 / World News in Brief: Reed Elsevier to pay Dollars 1.5bn for US information group Reed Elsevier, the Anglo-Dutch information and publishing group, said last night it had reached agreement to pay Dollars 1.5bn (Pounds 950m) for the electronic information arm of Mead Corporation, the US paper group. Reed Elsevier is to acquire the assets of Mead Data Central, which operates the on-line Lexis/Nexis legal and business publishing business. Reed Elsevier plans to finance the acquisition from surplus cash and Dollars 1bn in borrowings
