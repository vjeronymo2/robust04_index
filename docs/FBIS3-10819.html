Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Fabio Callejas from the National Civil Registry Office in Santa fe de Bogota--live relay] [Text] Here is the first bulletin of the Liberal Party's [PL] internal primary. These results represent 2 percent of the tables; out of 45,837 tables, 946 have been counted. The potential number of male voters are 8,603,751, for female voters, 8,429,199. The first bulletin of the Liberal Party's internal primary shows the following results: Ernesto Samper Pizano is in first place, with 21,477 votes, representing 39.7 percent; second is Rodolfo Gonzalez with 12,286 votes, or 22.7 percent; third, Humberto de la Calle Lombana, with 6,556 votes, or 12.1 percent; fourth, Carlos Lleras de la Fuente, with 3,262 votes, or 6 percent. The other candidates follow in this order: David Turbay--1,872 votes, or 3.4 percent; Carlos Lemos Simmonds--1,715, or 3.1 percent; Gloria Gaitan--1,204 votes, or 2.2 percent. Total number of blank votes--2,739; null votes--2,958; total number of votes counted--54,059.
