Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "At Daybreak" newscast] [Text] On the night of 11 January, the National Defense Secretariat issued Press Communique No. 13, explaining the missions conducted by military aircraft in the Chiapas conflict. The Defense Secretariat reports that up to now, operations by Air Force airplanes and helicopters in direct support of the ground forces are no closer than 5 km to the towns, so townspeople have not been harmed in any way. The communique adds that the aircraft have been used to transport troops and supplies. They have also been transporting food, medicine, clothing, and other articles to be distributed among residents of towns where military personnel are conducting activities to help civilians.
