BFN [Text] Baku (May 23) [date as received] TURAN--Today a member of Azerbaijan's Milli Majlis, Ibrahim Ibrahimli, was beaten after he exited the parliament building. According to Ibrahimli the police "asked him to come in to talk for about a minute." He refused to go with them, and they forcibly threw him into a car and took him to Baku's Chief Police Division. En route to the station, he was beaten. According to witnesses, fifteen men took part in the beating. On May 21 Ibrahimli was severely beaten when a demonstration was dispersed and incurred serious injuries. According to unofficial sources, the leadership will continue to suppress the opposition.
