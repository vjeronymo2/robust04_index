Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] There is to be tighter security around Messina's Palace of Justice and for some of the judges who are said to be Mafia targets. These were the decisions made by the Committee for Order and Security which met in the Messina Prefect's Office. The meeting follows the news that the mafia has made plans to kill, among others, Judge Antonio Di Pietro. In particular, the bosses are said to have set aside 500 million lire for the project. The operation was revealed to investigators by the supergrass, Luigi Sparacio, whose tesitmony has led to the arrest of dozens of people during the last few months.
