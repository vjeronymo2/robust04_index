Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By Jamal Khashuqji in Jeddah] [Excerpt] Sources of the dissolved Islamic Salvation Front and citizens in Algiers who were contacted by AL-HAYAH over the telephone confirmed that the front began television transmission last Monday [27 December] under the name of Salvation Television, which was monitored in the capital. By this, the front is adding clandestine television transmission to its radio transmission named al-Wafa' Radio, which regularly transmits every Saturday from an unknown place. [passage omitted]
