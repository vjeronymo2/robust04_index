Venture BR1204092294 Paris LA LETTRE DU GIFAS in English 10 Mar 94 BR1204092294 Paris LA LETTRE DU GIFAS English BFN [Unattributed article: "Alenia Takes Shares in Eisys, a Joint Thomson-CSF and EIS Venture"] [Text] Via its Alenia branch, the Italian group FINMECCANICA [Mechanical Engineering Finance Corporation] has taken a stake in the Eisys Co., a joint venture created at Rome in 1992 by Thomson-CSF and Elettronica Ingegneria Sistemi (EIS SpA [joint stock company]). The new equity holdings will be: Thomson-CSF (37.5 percent and EIS SpA (25 percent). For Alenia/FINMECCANICA and for Thomson-CSF, Eisys is a valuable partner for the building of data processing systems for European FSAF (Future Surface-to-Air Family) anti-air systems in which Thomson-CSF and Aerospatiale participate for France and Alenia for Italy under the EUROSAM [expansion unknown] venture.
