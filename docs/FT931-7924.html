930222 FT 22 FEB 93 / Observer: Without tears Kingfisher and Darty, which last week agreed terms of their marriage, still have a lot to work out between them. But Darty's finance director Gerard Basini is clear about one thing. If Darty applies UK accounting principles to its figures its profits look higher than under French rules. So while Darty's numbers will be prepared on a UK basis for Kingfisher's benefit, he says, 'we will use French rules for the taxman'.
