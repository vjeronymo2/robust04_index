921105 FT 05 NOV 92 / Iliescu names Romanian PM ROMANIA'S President Ion Iliescu yesterday named a little-known economist, 49-year-old Mr Nicolae Vacaroiu, as prime minister, Bucharest radio said, Reuter reports from Bucharest. The appointment ended a five-week search for a new premier after inconclusive elections on September 27 produced a hung parliament with Mr Iliescu's party well short of a majority.
