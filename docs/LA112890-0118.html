November 28, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

BASE TREK: Congressmen Dana Rohrabacher and Christopher Cox will fly an East Coast colleague to George Air Force Base near San Bernardino today to lobby for its conversion into a regional airport and a stop for the proposed Anaheim-Las Vegas mag-lev train. . . . The guest is Robert Torricelli, a New Jersey Democrat who chairs a house science and technology subcommittee. "He may be able to help us with federal permits and right-of-way issues," Rohrabacher said. . . . The three will breakfast first in Newport Beach with developer Buck Johns, a mag-lev and airport promoter who owns land near the base. Jerry Hicks and Jeffrey A. Perlman
