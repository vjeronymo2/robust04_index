Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 11, IRNA -- The conference on nature and magnitudes of cultural assault concluded its work Monday [10 January] after discussing the cultural, media, political, social and economic aspects of the issue. Speaking at the closing session, secretary of the conference Mohammad Hoseyn Panahi said that the conference was aimed at reviewing cultural assault and its scientific definition. To identify the loopholes through which the domestic culture would be affected by alien cultures was among other objectives discussed by the participants. He called for revision in cultural policies of the country reasoning that present regulations were vulnerable and defenceless against penetration of foreign standards into the society. Panahi referred to preservation of unity and genuineness of cultural system, along with acceptance of useful elements in foreign cultures, as the main factors which should be included in the country's cultural strategy. Experts, university professors and government officials in charge of cultural affairs took part in the 3-day conference co-sponsored by research department of the Culture and Islamic Guidance Ministry and Social Sciences Faculty of Allameh Tabataba'i University.
