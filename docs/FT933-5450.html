930903 FT 03 SEP 93 / Detectives probe share applications FRAUD SQUAD detectives in Northern Ireland are investigating suspected large-scale multiple applications for shares in privatised public utilities, it was disclosed last night. The Royal Ulster Constabulary was called in by the Director of Public Prosecutions. The share issues being investigated are BT-2, which closed in December 1991; the 12 regional electricity companies, which closed in December 1990; and the two electricity generating companies, which closed in March 1991.
