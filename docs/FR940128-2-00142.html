SUMMARY: The Food and Drug Administration (FDA) announces the renewal of the Advisory Committee on Special Studies Relating to the Possible Long-Term Health Effects of Phenoxy Herbicides and Contaminants by the Secretary of Health and Human Services. This notice is issued under the Federal Advisory Committee Act of October 6, 1972 (Pub. L. 92&hyph;463 (5 U.S.C. app. 2)). DATES: Authority for this committee will expire on December 2, 1995, unless the Secretary of Health and Human Services formally determines that renewal is in the public interest.
