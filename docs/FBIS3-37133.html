Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report under the "NEGA Reports" rubric: "Abkhazia"] [Excerpt] The Republic of Abkhazia Supreme Soviet circulated a statement yesterday contending that "there is no single bilateral document today attesting to the fact that Abkhazia and Georgia are a single state having common territory." It is also stressed in the statement that no international-law agreement concluded by Georgia's leadership has either anything to do with Abkhazia or is valid on its territory. Observers believe such a statement has appeared now because the Georgian leaders have stepped up their foreign policy activities and signed on behalf of Georgia, as a single state, a number of interstate agreements.
