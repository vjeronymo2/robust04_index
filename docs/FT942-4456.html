940609 FT 09 JUN 94 / International Company News: Reynolds Metals in joint deal to build Saudi plant Reynolds Metals, the world's third-largest producer of aluminium cans, is to build an aluminium beverage can plant in Dammam, Saudi Arabia, in a joint venture with four local partners. The plant, which is scheduled to be completed in early 1996, will have an annual capacity of 1.2bn cans. It will supply cans for Coca-Cola, and other soft drink brands and juices produced by the project's Saudi shareholders. Reynolds will provide technology for the project and manage and operate the facility, which is expected to employ 260 people. Reynolds' partners in the venture, in which it holds a 27.5 per cent stake, are the Olayan Group, which owns the Coca-Cola Bottling company of Saudi Arabia; the Olayan Financing Company; and two local soft drink and juice producers.
