930402 FT 02 APR 93 / World Trade News: Germany wins ships order CELEBRITY Cruises of Greece, an affiliate of the Chandris shipping and leisure group, has placed a Dollars 1bn order for three luxury cruise ships with a German shipbuilder, Josef L. Meyer of Papenburg. Celebrity wants to expand its presence in the Caribbean market by offering more year-round cruises. The ships are to be delivered between 1995 and 1997. They will be of 70,000 tons each, will have identical design and are to carry 1,740 passengers apiece.
