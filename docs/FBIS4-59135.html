BFN [Text] Cairo, 11 May (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak received Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah at the Presidential Office this morning. The meeting was attended by Assistant Foreign Minister Badr Hammam. The Yemeni foreign minister arrived in Cairo last night on a brief visit to Egypt to hand President Mubarak a message from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih.
