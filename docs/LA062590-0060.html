June 25, 1990, Monday, Southland Edition

FBI AGENT, TROOPER FOUND IN SMALL PLANE'S WRECKAGE

The bodies of an FBI agent and a Massachusetts state trooper were found Sunday in the wreckage of a small plane atop a mountain ridge in western Maine, a search and rescue official said. The missing Piper Cherokee vanished in rough weather late last Monday, but FBI agent Paul Cavanagh and Trooper Joseph Moynihan were not declared missing until Friday, said a Maine Warden Service spokesman.
