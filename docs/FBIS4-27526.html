BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 31 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] Monday sent a message of greetings to Daniel Ortega Saavedra upon his reelection as secretary general of the Sandinist National Liberation Front of Nicaragua. Expressing the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two parties would continue to develop, the message wished the secretary general big success in his responsible work to implement the resolutions of the front congress.
