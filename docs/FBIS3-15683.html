Language: Kazakh Article Type:BFN [Text] In connection with measures being taken in the republic to balance the budget on the basis of the Kazakhstan law of 10 December 1993 on temporary delegation of additional powers to the president of the republic of Kazakhstan and heads of local administrations, the president of the republic has decreed as follows. To hold as no longer valid the decisions of the Supreme Kenges of the Republic of Kazakhstan regarding the creation of the Atyrau, East Kazakhstan, Karaganda, and Mangistau free economic zones. The Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Kazakhstan should submit a draft resolution of the Supreme Kenges of the Republic of Kazakhstan concerning this issue for examination by the newly elected Supreme Kenges of the Republic of Kazakhstan. This decree has the legal power of a law and is valid until a relevant resolution of the Supreme Kenges is adopted. The decree enters into force on the day of its publishing.
