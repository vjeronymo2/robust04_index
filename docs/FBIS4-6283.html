BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 19 (KCNA) -- Functions were held in different countries in celebration of the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song. Celebration meetings were held at the power designing station of the Ministry of Primary Industry of Cuba and Namuronge Agricultural Research Centre of Uganda. The speakers at the meetings said that President Kim Il-song embarked upon the road of the revolution in his early years and set forth a chuche-based revolutionary line and has wisely led the Korean people, thus converting Korea into a modern socialist country independent in politics, self-sustaining in economy and self-reliant in national defence. Noting that the Korean people owe all their achievements to the wise leadership of President Kim Il-song, the speakers said he has been devoting his whole life to the freedom and happiness of the people. DPRK photo exhibitions and film weeks, film shows and photo explanatory meetings were hosted by the National Association of Writers and Artists of Peru, the Aburime-Changsu Oriental Diagnostic Hospital, Ltd., Benin, the Egyptian National Film Centre, the Maleice Agricultural Cooperative in Chibuto County, Gaza Province, Mozambique, and the Pathet Lao News Agency of Laos.
