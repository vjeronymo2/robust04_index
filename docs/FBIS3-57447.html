Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Bakina] [Text] Moscow January 25 TASS -- Two high officials of the Russian Ministry of Health -- First Deputy Minister Aleksey Moskvichev and Deputy Minister Vladimir Starodubov -- sent in their resignations after the appointment of Eduard Nechayev as health minister had been endorsed. ITAR-TASS learned this from a well-informed source. The source said there is a conflict between deputy ministers and Nechayev, so Moskvichev and Starodubov deemed it impossible to work under his direction. Nechayev has signed the resignations which are now considered by the government. New candidatures to fill the posts have already been found, ITAR-TASS was told.
