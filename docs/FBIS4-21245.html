`Stabilized' LD1404105294 Lisbon RDP Antena 1 Radio Network in Portuguese 0900 GMT 14 Apr 94 LD1404105294 Lisbon RDP Antena 1 Radio Network Portuguese BFN [Excerpt] Mario Mendes, director of the Judiciary Police, told us this morning that drugs trafficking has stabilized in Portugal: [Begin recording] Mendes: According to data we have available, we can notice that trafficking for consumption in Portugal has stabilized, but we have to be alert to any possibility of an increase. Unidentified correspondent: And what about Portugal being used as a route for trafficking into Europe? Mendes: It is still a main route for hashish and cocaine. We can see this through the amounts we have been seizing. Portugal continues to have a main role for the entry of hashish and cocaine. [end recording] [passage omitted]
