BFN [Text] A short time ago, a Cyprus Broadcasting Corporation correspondent reported from Luxembourg that the European Union [EU] Council of Foreign Ministers has approved a joint statement on the Cyprus issue. In their statement the 12 ministers blame the Turkish Cypriot side for the failure of the talks and plan to discuss the Cyprus issue at the EU summit in Corfu. According to our correspondent, during the second day of its session the council asked Serge Abou, the EU observer at the Cyprus talks, to present a new report at the EU summit. The council has also approved the Cyprus-EU Fourth Financial Protocol, which amounts to ECU74 million.
