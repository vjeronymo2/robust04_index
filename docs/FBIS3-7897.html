Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Lany, Central Bohemia Feb 28 (CTK) -- Technical and administrative aspects of the integration of the Czech Republic and Poland into the European Union were discussed on Monday [28 February] by Czech President Vaclav Havel and Speaker of the Polish Sejm (parliament) Jozef Oleksy, Oleksy told journalists. Oleksy said he spoke with Havel about pan-European matters, while he discussed more concrete concerns with other Czech representatives. The talks dealt generally with the European situation and integration, the Polish Constitution which is in preparation, the role of the president and the balance of power in the political system, Havel's spokesman Ladislav Spacek said.
