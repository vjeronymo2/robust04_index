920925 FT 25 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Ecu Trust net asset value at 58.7p Net asset value per share for the Ecu Trust increased from 51.6p at June 30 1991 to 58.2p at August 31 1992. Net revenue for the year to June 30 was Pounds 326,000 (Pounds 506,000 for 11 months to June 30 1991). Earnings per share came out at 1.09p (1.69p) and a single final payment of 1p is proposed. Last time 1.3p was paid including a special dividend of 0.3p.
