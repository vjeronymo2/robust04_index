930515 FT 15 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Euromoney advances to Pounds 5.94m PRE-TAX profits of Euromoney Publications increased by Pounds 1.32m to Pounds 5.94m in the six months to the end of March. Turnover showed a 14 per cent improvement, from Pounds 17.1m to Pounds 19.5m. At the operating level, profits advanced from Pounds 3.71m to Pounds 4.43m and associate undertakings contributed profits of Pounds 469,000 against a deficit of Pounds 16,000 last time. The pre-tax outcome included interest receivable of Pounds 1.04m (Pounds 924,000). The balance sheet is strong, the directors said, with net cash of Pounds 24m against Pounds 22m a year ago. The company, 75 per cent- owned by the Daily Mail and General Trust, publishes 44 magazines worldwide - a number of which had their most successful first halves yet, the directors said, although Euromoney magazine did less well than last year. An interim dividend of 10.5p (8p) is being paid from earnings of 19.5p (14.9p) per share.
