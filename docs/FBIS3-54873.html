Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] Azerbaijan's military units continued their activities through the holidays. They have launched combat operations to penetrate the defensive lines of the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] off and on since 1 January. The NKR State Committee for Defense has disclosed that the defense units taught the enemy a lesson by advancing and obstructing their activities. According to the reports we received as of 1900 yesterday, there has been no significant change in the confrontation.
