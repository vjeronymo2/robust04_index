Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Santa Fe de Bogota, 10 Mar (EFE) -- The explosion of a domestic fuel tank at a restaurant today injured five people and caused alarm in downtown Santa Fe de Bogota, according to the capital city's corps of firemen. The explosion broke windows in several buildings and caused a fire in a six-story building which was promptly extinguished by the firemen. The explosion occurred when a fuel supply vehicle was filling the restaurant's tanks on Jimenez Avenue. The explosion was so strong that early reports asserted that it was an attack on the building where the Office of the Attorney General is located. According to one employee, all of the windows at the Attorney General's Office were shattered and there was confusion for several minutes.
