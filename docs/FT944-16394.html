941013 FT 13 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Sturge disposal Sturge, the Lloyd's underwriting agency, expects to receive an initial consideration of Pounds 500,000 for the sale of its members' agencies business to a management buy-out. The sum will consist of Pounds 100,000 cash and Pounds 400,000 in preference shares in Falcon Agencies, the company taking over the business of RW Sturge and Donner, Sturge's two members' agencies. In addition RW Sturge and Donner will receive a deferred cash consideration dependant on future results of Falcon, subject to a limit of Pounds 5m . The deal requires approval by Sturge shareholders and Lloyd's.
