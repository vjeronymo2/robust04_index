940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Third quarter rise at Citizens Financial Citizens Financial Group, the US subsidiary of Royal Bank of Scotland, reported net income of Dollars 12.3m (Pounds 7.9m) for the third quarter to June 30, compared to Dollars 7.2m for the corresponding period of 1993. Three acquisitions in the last 12 months brought total assets to Dollars 8.1bn at the end of the third quarter, against Dollars 5.3bn. Net interest income for the quarter was Dollars 72.8m (Dollars 43.3m)before provision for credit losses. The provisions amounted to Dollars 5.1m, down 5 per cent on the comparable Dollars 5.4m. Non-interest income for the quarter was Dollars 23.2m (Dollars 17.9m), and non-interest expense was Dollars 70.3m (Dollars 44.2m).
