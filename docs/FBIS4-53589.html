BFN [Text] Implementing the Prime Minister's directive, the Ministry of Interior instructed police in localities to conduct a survey on the number of cars imported illegally into the country, including those imported without complying with the set procedures. According to a recent survey, 28 provinces and municipalities have imported 1,512 cars of various types without meeting the set procedure requirements, mostly in Song Be, Tay Ninh, Dong Nai, Vinh Long, Dong Thap, Binh Thuan, Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, Long An, Lam Dong, and Ninh Thuan Provinces. Quang Ninh and Gia Lai Provinces reported that they did not register any new cars. A recent investigation found, however, that Quang Ninh Province registered four cars with fake customs clearance documents while the Pleiku City police of Gia Lai Province coordinated with the Quang Ngai provincial police to register 180 cars and motorcycles using fake customs documents.
