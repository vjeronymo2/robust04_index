BFN [Text] Pro-communist forces are seeking to remove from the political scene the three men who were the authors of the 1991 Belovezh agreements of 1991 on establishment of the CIS, Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk said today. He added that following this the pro-communist forces would annul the Belovezh agreements and restore the USSR. Commenting on the political situation in Ukraine, Kravchuk stated that the decision that the presidential and local elections take place this June could completely destroy executive power in the country. Kravchuk said he could have decided not to run for presidency. However, he suggested that those who will take part in the presidential race could in two days eliminate everything which had been created, if one of the present candidates is elected president.
