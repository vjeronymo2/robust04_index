BFN [Text] Muhammad Ibrahim, a 29-year-old Syrian citizen, was detained for 15 days on the orders of the 'Akko Magistrate's Court. The young man surrendered himself to Israel Defense Forces forces three days ago. Our correspondent Yo'el Dar reports that the Syrian man is suspected of infiltrating Israel. He told the court that he fled Syria so as to receive medical treatment for scars left on his body by electric treatments he had been given by Syrian doctors. The judge ordered that the man be given a medical examination before a verdict is passed.
