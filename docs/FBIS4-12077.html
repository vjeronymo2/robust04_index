BFN [Text] Brother Presidential Council Chairman Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih received at his place of residence at the Guest House in Doha this evening representatives of the Yemeni Community Council in the fraternal State of Qatar. The representatives of the Yemeni community expressed their happiness over this meeting with the brother president in their second home, Qatar. They briefed him on the situation of the Yemeni community in Qatar, and also on the care and hospitality accorded to them by the Qatari Government under His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of Qatar. They praised the fraternal ties that bind them to the fraternal Qataris -- officials and people. They expressed their confidence that this visit will contribute toward promoting and enhancing the ties between the two fraternal countries and peoples. The representatives of the Yemeni community in Qatar also expressed concern over the current political situation in the homeland. They reiterated their adherence to our people's national constants, foremost among which are unity, democracy, and stability. Brother Presidential Council Chairman Lt. Gen. 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih spoke to them. He expressed his great happiness over his meeting with his compatriots in their second home, Qatar. He appreciated the strong patriotic sentiments they expressed toward their homeland, toward its unity, and toward the problems plaguing the country due to the ongoing crisis. The brother president paid tribute to the positive stands of the Yemeni expatriates in general, and the expatriates in Qatar in particular, especially since their conduct reflects the Yemenis' nobility, as well as their cultural and humane values. He urged them to make greater efforts to promote and enhance their ties with their Qatari brothers, and to do their best to reinforce these fraternal ties to epitomize the ties of kinship between them and their Qatari brothers, given the fact that they are representatives of their people and homeland. The brother president also addressed the current developments in the homeland, and the challenges posed to Yemeni unity and the Yemeni people. He reaffirmed his confidence that our people are, God willing, capable of coping with these challenges, thanks to their cohesion, solidarity, and unshakable faith in Yemeni unity, which is the people's destiny and fate. The brother president added: I am confident that our people, who have managed to cope with the challenges that faced the Yemeni revolution [words indistinct], are capable of coping with the current challenges posed to unity in the same spirit, and with the same zeal and (?faith). The meeting was attended by the members of the delegation accompanying the brother Presidential Council chairman on his current visit to the State of Qatar.
