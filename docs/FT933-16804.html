930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Havelock Europa Havelock Europa, the shopfitting company, reported that the High Court had confirmed the special resolution of the company passed on June 2 to reduce the share premium account by Pounds 2.93m. Havelock said the reduction would enable it to resume dividend payments when appropriate to do so. The High Court also approved a reduction of Cussins Property's share premium account by Pounds 2.18m.
