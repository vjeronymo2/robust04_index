920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / International Capital Markets: GPA in Dollars 500m security programme GPA, the Irish aircraft leasing firm which is heading for its world stock market launch next month, plans to launch a Dollars 500m aircraft and lease-backed security programme before the end of this month, Reuters reports. GPA is effectively selling 14 aircraft and the lease income from the airlines is the first programme of its kind. Most of the issue - some Dollars 375m - will be in Eurobonds.
