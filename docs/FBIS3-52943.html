Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riyadh, Jan 8, SPA -- Information Minister 'Ali al-Sha'ir met here tonight with the French Minister of Industry, Posts, Telephone and Foreign Trade Gerard Longuet and talks dealt with ways of cooperation between the two countries in information sectors. The two ministers also reviewed ways to benefit from the media satellites and coordination between technicians of the two countries in this field. The meeting was attended by Ibrahim al-Qadhi, undersecretary of the Ministry of Information for administrative affairs, Dr Fu'ad al-Farsi, undersecretary of the Ministry for Information Affairs, Prince Turki Bin Sultan Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, assistant undersecretary for planning and studies and many senior officials.
