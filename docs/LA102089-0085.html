October 20, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Cook County Sheriff's deputies in Chicago served Raider receiver Willie Gault with an eviction notice, passed by the owners of a building because Gault owes $32,533.33 in rent on a clothing store.

Stuart Dubin, an attorney for the landlord, said the store opened in January, but Gault had paid only $14,000 of a rental bill that totaled more than $50,000 through August.
