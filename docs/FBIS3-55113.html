Tymoshenko: "Parties Are Standing in Line"] [Text] The Central Electoral Commission has adopted resolutions on registering the Ukrainian Christian Democratic Party, the Democratic Party of Ukraine, Rukh, the Labor Party, the Social Democratic Party, the Ukrainian Party of Solidarity and Social Justice, the Party of Free Peasants of Ukraine, the Peasant Party of Ukraine, the Socialist Party of Ukraine, and the Ukrainian Conservative Republican Party, in connection with their plans to field candidates in the 27 March Supreme Council elections. The parties have received the appropriate certificates.
