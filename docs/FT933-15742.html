930707 FT 07 JUL 93 / International Company News: Energie-Versorgung Niederosterreich Energie-Versorgung Niederosterreich (EVN), a leading Austrian energy utility, said revenues in the nine months to 31 May 1993 were up 4.8 per cent, writes Ian Rodger in Zurich. Mr Rudolph Gruber, the chief executive, said the result provided a solid base for the full year. Revenue from electricity sales was up 3.7 per cent, gas income was up 7.2 per cent and heating sales gained 11.9 per cent. No figures were given. In the first half, pre-tax profits jumped 12.1 per cent to Sch1.2bn (Dollars 105.3m) on revenues up 4.7 per cent to Sch5.9bn.
