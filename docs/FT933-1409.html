930924 FT 24 SEP 93 / Labour's 'entire credibility' is at stake LABOUR'S 'entire credibility' over education is at stake following its last-gasp decision to delay publication of a discussion document which was already in the hands of university vice-chancellors, Mr John Patten, education secretary, said yesterday, David Owen writes. Mr Patten, who distributed the higher education paper to journalists, said Labour was in 'chaos' over education and urged Mrs Ann Taylor, shadow education secretary, to resign.
