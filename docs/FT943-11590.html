940729 FT 29 JUL 94 / People: National Westminster Charles Bryant, 45, has been appointed to the new post at NATIONAL WESTMINSTER of director of UK trade services for the bank's UK branch business sector. His responsibilities extend to drawing together the operations, sales and marketing units of the sector's international trade and banking services division. A former managing director of Midland Montagu, Bryant will be joining NatWest in September.
