Language: French Article Type:BFN [Article signed PV/RF: "The Geneva State Council Meets OPEC Ministers"; as released by Bern ATSA/SDAA database] [Text] Geneva, 25 Mar (ATS) -- Geneva State Counselors Olivier Vodoz and Gerard Ramseier met the ministers of the oil producing countries on Friday [25 March]. Mr. Vodoz described this as a courtesy visit. The government in Geneva has been trying to convince OPEC to return its headquarters to Geneva, which it left in the mid-1960s, from Vienna. Saudi Arabian Petroleum Minister Hisham Nazir, Iranian Petroleum Minister Gholam Reza Aqazadeh-Kho'i, as well as other ministers from Algeria, Gabon, Nigeria, Qatar, the United Arab Emirates, Venezuela, and Indonesia took part in the meeting with Messrs. Vodoz and Ramseier.
