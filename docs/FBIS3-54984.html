Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] A letter from Jiang Zemin, president of the People' Republic of China, reached the address of Geydar Aliyev, president of the Republic of Azerbaijan. In his letter, the president of People's Republic of China, attaching great importance to multi-faceted strengthening and development of friendly and economic relations between Azerbaijan and China, invites Geydar Aliyev, president of the Republic of Azerbaijan, to China for an official visit at a time that is convenient for him.
