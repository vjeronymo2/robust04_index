Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In Paris, Israel and Palestinian negotiators have ended the fifth round of economic talks, and progress has been reported. A Qol Yisra'el reporter says advances were made in the areas of agriculture and taxation; however, the Palestinians presented new requests in the area of banking. The Palestinians continue to demand their own currency and a central bank, and are opposed to Israeli representation at customs at border crossing points. The two sides say the talks are due to resume in 10 days.
