930128 FT 28 JAN 93 / International Company News: Former GM chief to leave board MR Roger Smith, the controversial former chairman of General Motors, is to resign from the board of the ailing car company in April after 19 years as a director, writes Martin Dickson. Mr Smith, who retired as GM's chairman in 1990, is rumoured to have been under pressure from fellow directors to step down, since many of GM's current financial problems originated during his chairmanship.
