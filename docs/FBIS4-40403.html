BFN [Text] Kiev [no date as received]--Ukrainian Defense Minister Vitaliy Radetskyy is continuing his official visit to the United States. During a meeting between the Ukrainian and U.S. defense ministers on 14 June, the U.S. side again emphasized its willingness to mediate between Ukraine and Russia on the division of the Black Sea Fleet, the press service of Ukraine's Ministry of Defense reports. Radetskyy and William Perry also discussed the implementation of the trilateral statement by the presidents of Ukraine, the United States, and Russia. The Ukrainian defense minister noted that the work on the withdrawal of nuclear arms from Ukraine was proceeding in strict accordance with the schedule. The sides also discussed the Partnership for Peace program and joint training between the two states' subdivisions at various levels. On 15 June, Radetskyy and Perry plan to visit a naval base in Norfolk and meet with NATO Supreme Allied Commander Atlantic David Miller.
