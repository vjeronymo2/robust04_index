BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 11 (KCNA) -- A seminar of pressmen took place in Pyongyang on the lapse of 30 years since the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il began to work in the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea. The speakers said the dear leader, with a deep insight into the great role of news media in the revolutionary struggle, has attached particular importance to the publication and information services and led them since he began his work in the party Central Committee. They said the undying feats performed by him in guiding the publication and information services are that he provided a great guideline for the building of revolutionary news media of the working class by putting forward a chuche-based idea and theories on the publication and information services, kindled the flames of revolutionization of newspapers, news services and publications and established a revolutionary work system of the press media. They pointed out that at a time when news media of some countries have been reduced to guides on the frustration of socialism and their return to capitalism, giving in to the bourgeois ideological offensive of the imperialists and reactionaries, the news media of the DPRK are serving as triumphant drumbeats in the noble struggle to resolutely defend the party and the leader and safeguard and glorify the socialist bastion under the banner of the chuche idea. They added that this is entirely results of the wise guidance of the dear leader who put forward a great chuche-based programme of the press and led the struggle to carry it into reality.
