BFN [Text] Brahim Ben Gayou, governor of the Province of Bouira, has had a narrow escape from an assassination attempt. The security services said that he had a narrow escape from an assassination attempt committed this morning by unknown individuals near his home when he was preparing himself to leave for work accompanied by his driver. The same services added that the governor was slightly injured while his driver suffered various injuries after which he was taken to Bouira hospital to have preliminary treatment before the evacuation of the two of them to Ain Naaja military hospital in Algiers.
