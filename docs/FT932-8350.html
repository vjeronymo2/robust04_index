930519 FT 19 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Pittencrieff stake raised in Aberdeen Pittencrieff yesterday said it had acquired further shares in Aberdeen Petroleum to bring its total holding in the company to 25,778,496 shares, approximately 50.4 per cent of Aberdeen Petroleum's issued share capital. The price paid for the additional 766,666 shares was 17p each, Pittencrieff's cash alternative offer for each Aberdeen share.
