910426 FT 26 APR 91 / UK Company News: Majedie net asset value rises to 341p At March 31 the net asset value of Majedie Investments, an investment trust, stood at 341p, 60p higher than the 281p reported at September 30. Net revenue for the six months totalled Pounds 1.19m (Pounds 1.09m) and earnings were 4.51p (4.15p) per share. The interim dividend is unchanged at 2.5p.
