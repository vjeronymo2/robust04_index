BFN [Text] Pedro Luis Pedroso, a Foreign Ministry expert, believes that only cooperation among small island countries and decisive international support will make it possible for this group of nations to achieve sustainable development by the year 2,000. Pedroso pointed out Cuba's efforts in this regard, and ratified Cuba's willingness to help other nations. Pedroso is a member of the Cuban delegation to the First International Small Island Nations Sustainable Development Conference to be held from 25 April to 6 May in Barbados.
