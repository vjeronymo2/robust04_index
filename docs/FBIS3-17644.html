Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Sitnikov] [Excerpt] United Nations, 26 Mar -- [Passage omitted: The UN Security Council has passed a resolution calling for the Georgian-Abkhaz conflict to be resolved peacefully] Speaking at the session, Yuliy Vorontsov, the Russian permanent representative at the United Nations, noted that on 21 March the Government of Georgia had expressed its readiness to: Broaden the political and legal status of Abkhazia and, with the aim of giving this status real content, to delimit the competencies between the bodies of state administration of the Republic of Georgia and Abkhazia, including investing the latter with exclusive powers. This position adopted by Georgia is an important prerequisite for success at the talks, Yu. Vorontsov stated. The Russian representative also went on to point out the importance of both sides' having before them the prospect for the institution of a UN peace-keeping operation, which both the Georgian and the Abkhaz sides urgently requested.
