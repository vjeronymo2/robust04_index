BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 21 (KCNA) -- Yi Yong-pin, chairman of the Society of Overseas Korean Christians for National Reunification in Germany, and his party Wednesday met and had a talk with Yi In-mo, a former war correspondent of the Korean People's Army who is called the incarnation of faith and will, in an atmosphere of compatriotic feelings. Yi Yong-pin said he was deeply touched, seeing Yi In-mo leading a happy life in good health after he was embraced by the motherland thanks to the deep loving care of the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. "There is no such great and kind person in the world as our great leader and dear leader," said Yi Yong-pin. He denounced the South Korean rulers' violation of human rights and said compatriots overseas will vigorously fight for the repatriation of unconverted long-term prisoners in South Korea to the DPRK.
