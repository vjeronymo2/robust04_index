Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Kiev, 28 Jan -- [passage omitted] At a meeting with servicemen of the UN peacekeeping forces in Sarajevo on 27 January, Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko noted in particular that "one of the most important tasks of my trip to the Balkans is the necessity of outlining the extension of specific assistance by Ukraine in settling the situation in Bosnia- Herzegovina." In the opinion of the head of the Ukrainian foreign policy department, only by taking account of all the possibilities and all the interests of the parties concerned can a mutually acceptable way of resolving this problem be elaborated.
