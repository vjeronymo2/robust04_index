February 6, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

William R. Brody has been appointed executive vice president and chief operating officer at Beckham/Eisenman, an Irvine-based interior design and contract furnishings group. Brody joined the company in 1988 as vice president, marketing. Before that, he was director of marketing for Tecton Media Inc. in New York, a management consulting firm.
