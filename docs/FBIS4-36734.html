BFN [Text] Beirut, June 13 (AFP) -- A member of Abu Nidal's radical Palestinian group Fatah Revolutionary Council claimed here on Monday responsibility for bombing a US airliner over Lockerbie, Scotland, in December 1988, which killed 270 people. Yusuf Sha'ban, 29, a suspect in the murder case of a Jordanian diplomat, told a Lebanese court he had placed the bomb that ripped through a Pan Am plane packed with Christmas travellers.
