930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / World Trade News: Lisbon help for exporters PORTUGAL yesterday announced an Es300bn (Pounds 1.4bn) package designed to support exports and help industry through the international recession, Peter Wise reports. The aim is to help exporters penetrate non-traditional markets, to promote Portuguese products in specific markets and to support companies hit by exchange-rate instability and the crisis in the former Soviet Union.
