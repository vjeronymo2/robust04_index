Language: Spanish Article Type:CSO [Text] Senator Claudia Blum de Barbieri said during a trip to Leticia that "the report concerning the circulation of drug money to support political campaigns is quite true." She noted however that to control these sources of financing, the Treasury Inspector has been reviewing the candidates' financial books. She said however: "I don't know how well they will be able to cover up the ill-gotten money." She said further that the issue of the so-called hot money is not a new one since that money has been around for some time, especially in Valle Department.
