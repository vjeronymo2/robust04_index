920114 FT 14 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Merchants Tst lifts third quarter pay-out The Merchants Trust is lifting its third quarter dividend, for the year to January 31 1992, from 2.25p to 2.5p. At the same time, the investment trust announced the appointment to the board of Mr Colin Black, chairman of Kleinwort Benson Investment Management and of Scottish Widows Fund and Life Assurance Society.
