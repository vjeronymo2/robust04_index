June 16, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

LAMB'S CANCELS 'LION, WITCH AND WARDROBE'

Because of last-minute complications over acquiring the rights to "The Lion, The Witch and the Wardrobe," the Lamb's Players Theatre will not present the C.S. Lewis classic June 15-July 22. Instead, the troupe will present a musical version of "The Wind in the Willows" July 6-29.
