Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg Feb 28 SAPA -- Afrikaner Volksfront [AVF, Afrikaner National Front] leaders Tienie Groenewald and Constand Viljoen would meet an African National Congress [ANC] delegation in Standerton on Monday [28 February] (this) morning to discuss a variety of issues affecting the Eastern Transvaal town, the ANC said. ANC spokesman Ronnie Mamoepa confirmed an ANC delegation led by Mathew Phosa would meet the two Boer leaders in Standerton to discuss: - the declaration of Standerton as part of a "volkstaat" [homeland]; - a planned ANC march through the town on Wednesday (March 2) after a previous march in the town ended in confrontation between ANC supporters, rightists and the police; - the consumer boycott affecting the area and other issues. Mr Mamoepa said the meeting was due to get underway at 9am at the Standerton town council offices and could last "some time".
