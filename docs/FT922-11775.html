920423 FT 23 APR 92 / UK Company News: Assets downturn for English National Net asset value per preferred ordinary share of English National Investment fell from 316.7p to 288.1p over the 12 months to March 31. The figure for the deferred ordinaries declined from 241.7p to 213.1p. After-tax revenue worked through at Pounds 510,417 (Pounds 526,089). Earnings per preferred share amounted to 16.2p (16.6p) and per deferred share 11.3p (11.7p). A final dividend of 11.2p per preferred share and of 8.75p per deferred share makes same-again totals of 16.2p and 11.3p respectively.
