BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Hakki Atun, the prime minister of the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus [TRNC], has stated that they are determined to further relations with Turkey. Atun and a delegation of Turkish Cypriot young businessmen participated at a meeting organized by the Aegean Young Businessmen Association in Izmir. At the meeting, Atun said that they are aiming at economic integration between Turkey and the TRNC. Pointing out to the importance of the political dimension in the economic relations between Turkey and the TRNC, Atun indicated that economic strengthening will give them more power at the negotiating table. Answering reporters' questions, Atun said that the Greek Cypriot administration entering the European Union will undermine the efforts toward a Cyprus solution and will create great political drawbacks which will harm the Cyprus issue. Later, the TRNC Young Businessmen Association and the Aegean Young Businessmen Association signed a joint declaration.
