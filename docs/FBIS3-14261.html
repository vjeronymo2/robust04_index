Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn and the Amir of Qatar Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani have held a meeting at the Guest Palace in Doha, which was attended by senior officials from the Jordanian and Qatari sides. The two sides exchanged views on current regional and international issues of common interest, especially unifying the Arab stand. Discussions also highlighted the latest developments in the peace process following the Ibrahimi Mosque massacre and the UN Security Council resolution condemning it. Bilateral relations and means of bolstering them were also discussed. Taking part for the Jordanian side were the chief of the Royal Court; their Royal Highnesses Prince 'Abdallah and Hamzah; in addition to senior advisers and officials accompanying his majesty the king on his current visit to Qatar. On the Qatari side, attending the talks were the crown prince and minister of defense; the minister of foreign affairs; and other high-ranking Qatari officials.
