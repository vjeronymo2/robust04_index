BFN [Text] The National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA] is still bent on waging war throughout the country. Yesterday, it continued the shelling of the martyred city of Cuito, which it intends to take by storm at any price. The city of Malanje has also come UNITA under attack. Clashes took place in that city yesterday. UNITA rebels abandoned 50 dead troops and assorted war materiel in the field.
