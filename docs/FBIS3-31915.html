Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Finance Minister Fernando Enrique Cardoso is irritated because Congress did not approve some measures in the government's economic plan. He said that now part of it is missing. The PPR [Progressive Renewal Party]'s blocking of voting on the provisional measure increasing the income tax on large companies and creating a control tax on the financial system somewhat irritated Finance Minister Fernando Enrique Cardoso. He said he will not accept suggestions in the future to balance the public budget at the expense of low-income groups.
