Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] In the reshuffled government, the Ministry of Health, the Ministry for Protection of the Environment and Natural Resources, and the State Committee for Sanitary Epidemics Supervision will most probably be overseen by not just one vice premier, as was the case before, but by three -- Oleg Soskovets, Aleksandr Zaveryukha, and Yuriy Yarov respectively. ITAR-TASS correspondents learned this from sources in the aforementioned ministries. The closely related problems of health, ecology, and sanitary and epidemiological welfare, which cannot be solved without constant cooperation, will thus be divided into the spheres of influence of the different supervisors.
