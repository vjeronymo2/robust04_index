BFN [Excerpt] A famous commander of Dostam's militia, Brigadier General Mohammad Zarif, was recently killed by his men in Andkhoi District in Jawzjan Province. A report received by the BAKHTAR INFORMATION AGENCY says the general was killed by Hesamoddin, one of his men. He and his six colleagues left Dostam's militia with their weapons and later joined the mojahedin of Lieutenant General Ata Mohammad in Balkh. [passage omitted]
