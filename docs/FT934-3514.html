931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / International Company News: Downturn at Canadian food group Univa, Canada's second biggest food distributor, which is being reorganised, reports net income of CDollars 7.6m (USDollars 5.7m), or 7 cents a share, for the third quarter to November 6, down from CDollars 9m, or 8 cents, a year earlier, on sales 4.3 per cent lower at CDollars 1.43bn, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. After special charges of CDollars 117m, Univa's net loss was CDollars 74.1m, or 88 cents, in the latest quarter. Nine-month operating net income was CDollars 25.6m, or 23 cents, compared with CDollars 17.7m, or 16 cents, on sales little changed at CDollars 4.8bn. After special charges, the nine-month net loss was CDollars 59.1m, or 75 cents.
