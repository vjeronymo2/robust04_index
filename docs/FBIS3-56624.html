Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Barykin] [Text] Moscow January 17 TASS -- The intensive shelling of Nagorno-Karabakh positions began today in the morning by the Azeri National Army at the eastern and south-eastern sections of the Nagorno-Karabakh front. According to the press service of the headquarters of the Nagorno-Karabakh Defence Army, units of the Nagorno-Karabakh Army are fighting defence battles along the whole of the front line. At 10.00 in the morning Azeri assault planes made five attempts to bomb Stepanakert, a spokesman for the Nagorno-Karabakh press and information department told ITAR-TASS by telephone. However, anti-aircraft defence units made them retreat. The population is being warned in time about the coming air raids. According to the press service of the defence army headquarters, the fighting was especially fierce at the eastern and Askeran-Agdam sections. Dozens of Azeri armoured vehicles took part in it. The Nagorno-Karabakh troops destroyed a tank, an armoured personnel carrier and killed 60 enemy soldiers, forcing the Azeri troops to retreat to their initial positions. In the Nagorno-Karabakh units two men were killed and seven wounded.
