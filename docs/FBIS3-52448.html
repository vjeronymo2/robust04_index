Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] Shim'on Peres and Jozef Moravcik, ministers of foreign affairs of Israel and the Slovak Republic, signed a memorandum in Jerusalem about relations between the governments of the two states. The memorandum says that, in the interest of reinforcing mutual relations, Israel and the Slovak Republic will sign, in the near future, a number of agreements on agriculture and aviation, and also agreements on the prevention of double taxation in mutual trade and on the support and protection of investments.
