930401 FT 01 APR 93 / Warning as more Daf jobs go RELUCTANCE by some component manufacturers to keep supplying the Leyland Daf truck plant at Leyland, Lancashire, is again threatening efforts to sell it as a going concern, its administrative receivers warned yesterday. The warning came as a further 349 Leyland Daf workers were made redundant. Most of the redundancies - 273 - were at the Leyland trucks plant, leaving a workforce of 273. The remaining 76 jobs went at the Albion axle plant, Glasgow, leaving a workforce of 342.
