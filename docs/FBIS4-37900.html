BFN [Text] Cairo, 25 Jun (MENA)--The National Dialogue General Congress held its first session this evening. It elected People's Assembly Speaker Dr. Ahmad Fathi Surur as chairman of the Political Committee, Consultative Council member Dr. Hamid al-Sayih as chairman of the Economic Committee, and Consultative Council Speaker Dr. Mustafa Kamal Hilmi as chairman of the Social and Cultural Committee. The National Dialogue General Congress has approved the formation of the three committees and their agendas.
