Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities monitored through 27 January. Source is listed in parentheses following each item: Finance Minister Fernando Illanes signed a loan agreement for 32 million German marks -- $18.6 million -- with the German Government. The funds will be used to supply electricity to Larecaja Province and La Paz Department and potable water to Oruro Department. (La Paz LA RAZON in Spanish 31 Dec 93 p B2) Fedor Perez, deputy director general of the General Customs Directorate, DGA, reported that the DGA collected 946 million bolivianos in 1993, a 17.9 percent increase compared with 1993. But he said this was less than national budget estimates due to international agreements signed with Colombia, Ecuador, Venezuela, Peru, Argentina, and Chile that provide for the reduction or exemption of tariffs. (La Paz LA RAZON in Spanish 11 Jan 94 p B5)
