Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] French Foreign Minister Alain Juppe has arrived on a three-day official visit to Israel. Arriving at Ben-Gurion Airport, he was received by Deputy Foreign Minister Yosi Beilin. Tonight the guest will dine with Foreign Minister Peres, and tomorrow the two will hold a working meeting. Also tomorrow, Alain Juppe will meet with the prime minister, the president, MIA's relatives, and the Likud chairman and visit Yad Vashem [holocaust memorial museum]. He is also slated to tour the Gaza Strip in the course of his visit. Lord MacKay, the British shipping and aviation secretary [title as heard], is also expected in Israel tonight. He is accompanied by representatives of British airlines and shipping companies, who will discuss joint projects with Israeli businessmen.
