BFN [Excerpts] In constituencies where the Social Democratic Party of Hungary [MSZDP] has not put forward its own candidates, it is going to support the Hungarian Socialist Party. Edit Schranz reports: [Schranz] The MSZDP wants to establish a strong left wing in Hungary. Without a strong left wing, serious decisions cannot be made in parliament. Moreover, should the occasion arise, a strong left wing is needed to govern the country, party Chairman Zoltan Kiraly said. [passage omitted] The MSZDP is planning to make a decision on where and with whom to conclude election agreements in the second round of the elections at its presidium meeting to be held on 14 May. In connection with this, Zoltan Kiraly also revealed that, with the exception of left and right extremists, they might conclude election agreements with other parties, including liberal parties. For that matter, the party asks the voters to vote for the Socialists' list in constituencies where the MSZDP does not have a party list in the first round, the chairman said. [passage omitted]
