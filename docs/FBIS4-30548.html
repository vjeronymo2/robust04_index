BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 28 (KCNA) -- A meeting was held here Monday on the occasion of the 34th anniversary of the independence of Zaire under the sponsorship of the Korea-Zaire Friendship Association. Present there were Yi Chol-sin, vice-chairman of the Committee for Cultural Relations With Foreign Countries and vice-chairman of the Korea-Zaire Friendship Association, other officials concerned and working people in the city. A speech was made and a message of greetings to the Zairean president adopted at the meeting.
