940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / World News in Brief: Dutch telecoms shares to go on sale Shares in state-owned Dutch postal and telecommunications company Koninklijke PTT Nederland are to be sold to investors early next month at between Fl 46 and Fl 52 each, valuing the company at Fl 21bn to Fl 24bn (Pounds 7.4bn-Pounds 8.5bn).
