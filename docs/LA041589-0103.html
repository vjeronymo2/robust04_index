April 15, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

WEEKEND HIGHLIGHTS FOR L.A. INTERNATIONAL FILM FESTIVAL

The American Film Institute Los Angeles International Film Festival continues at the Cineplex Odeon Century Plaza Cinemas this weekend. Highlights from today's and Sunday's program follow.

TODAY

'Not Since Casanova'

United States, 1988, 80 minutes

Noon

Discouraging debut feature from Brett Thompson about a shallow young man and his search for the perfect woman. This is student level film-making at its most off-putting and has no business being in a film festival. KEVIN THOMAS
