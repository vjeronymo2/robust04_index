930902 FT 02 SEP 93 / International Company News: Anglo-French packages group acquires Zeller CARNAUDMetalBox, the Anglo-French packaging group, has acquired Zeller Plastik Group, a private German company which manufactures closures and dispensing systems for household and beauty products, writes John Ridding. The acquisition price was not disclosed. But CarnaudMetalBox said that Zeller was profitable and achieved sales of FFr600m (Dollars 102m) last year. CarnaudMetalBox said that Zeller's range of mechanisms to close and dispense household products would complement its packaging activities.
