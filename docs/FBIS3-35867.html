Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk believes that "the Ukrainian Armed Forces will act in the appropriate order and are capable of defending the state in an extreme situation." This was the way he commented on the results of the military command exercise on territorial defense, the Defense Ministry's press service told INTERFAX-Ukraine. The first strategic exercise on territorial defense in Ukraine which took place under the main staff led by the commander in chief, President Leonid Kravchuk, finished on Friday. The purpose of the exercise was to develop interaction between the power ministries, the frontier defense forces, the national guards' army and the civil defense forces. The press service also announced that actions not only in case of an external threat but also in case of a natural calamity had been carried out during the exercise.
