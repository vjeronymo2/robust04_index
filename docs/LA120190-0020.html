December 1, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

WHAT'S SO BAD ABOUT USC BASKETBALL?

What disparity! UCLA beats UC Irvine by 33 points and sets a school record of 134 points and it gets front-page coverage in the sports section. USC beats Chicago State by 41 points and has the third-highest point total in school history and they get Page 11.

Go figure.

KEVIN O'CONNELL

Glendale
