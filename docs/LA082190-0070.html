August 21, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL ROUNDUP: SIEMENS SOLAR PLANS VANCOUVER, WASH., PLANT

Siemens Solar Industries, a Camarillo-based maker of solar-power products, said it plans to build a new production facility in Vancouver, Wash.

The plant will create about 100 jobs in Vancouver and increase the company's manufacturing capacity by 50%, said Siemens Solar, a U.S. unit of Siemens AG of West Germany that employs about 500 people in Camarillo.

The company's products provide power for a variety of industrial uses such as telecommunications, and for remote houses and recreational sites.
