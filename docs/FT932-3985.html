930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / Technology (Worth Watching): Apricot re-enters the PC market Apricot computers has re-entered the market for home and small business PCs with the Xen-PC, a 486-based machine which has been designed so that it can be upgraded to use Intel's latest and most powerful Pentium processor once the chip becomes widely available. The Xen-PC, manufactured in Glenrothes, Scotland, has high resolution graphics and an on-board CD-Rom interface. It will sell for Pounds 799 until July 31st and for Pounds 899 subsequently. Apricot: UK, tel: 021 717 7171.
