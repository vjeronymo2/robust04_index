Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, March, 2, IRNA -- Dr. Abdolrahman, personal envoy of the Afghan President Borhanoddin Rabbani, and Iran's Deputy Foreign Minister for Asia-Pacific Affairs 'Ala'eddin Borujerdi, here Wednesday discussed the recent developments in Afghanistan. Abdolrahman praised Iran's humanitarian efforts to put an end to Afghan clashes and hoped for cessation of hostilities between the two sides. Abdolrahman welcomed the assistance of the Iranian officials to Afghanistan in order to tackle its problems and said, "we trust in good intention of the Iranian officials". Borujerdi referred to Iran's intense efforts to put an end to the Afghan clashes and reiterated that the Afghan war is in no one's benefit. The Iranian official warned Afghan rival factions of the folly of destroying Afghan potentials and called on them not let the country to go on the brink of destruction. He also hoped for a speedy stop for Afghan war.
