April 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THOUGHTS ON LIFE OF DAREDEVIL KNIEVEL

A battered wife -- she didn't leave because she was afraid "he'd hurt me or kill me."

An abused son -- Evel kicked him in the face. "He looks a lot better with the new face. I did it for no charge."

Probable alcoholic -- "knocking back one Canadian Club after another chasing them with a Lite beer."

He is Evil!! Evil!! Evil!!

MARY B. MASCHAL

San Diego
