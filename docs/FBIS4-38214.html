BFN [Text] Tehran, June 29, IRNA -- Iran's foreign Ministry Tuesday [28 June] categorically denied allegations made by the Zionist regime on the presence of an Israeli pilot in Iran and termed it as "sheer lie". Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin and the Zionist regime's Air Force commander recently had claimed that Ron Arad, an Israeli pilot whose plane crashed in 1986 while bombarding south Lebanon, is in Iran. An informed source at the Foreign Ministry in a contact with IRNA categorically rejected the allegations and stressed "the Zionists were following other objectives by repeating such baseless and unfounded claims."
