Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 7 (XINHUA) -- Chinese President Jiang joint communique concerning the basis of Sino-Azerbaijani friendly relations here this evening. Six other documents between China and Azerbaijan were also signed on the occasion. They were the summary of agreements establishing air transportation, agreement on scientific and technological cooperation, agreement on cultural cooperation, agreement on sanitary and medical science cooperation, agreement on tourism exchange and agreement on broadcast, film and television cooperation.
