BFN [Text] The provisional government of attorney Emile Jonassaint has established an official agenda of meetings with the Senate office headed by Senator Bernard Sansaricq. The agenda notes that various provisional government ministers will have to appear before legislators to defend their management policy. The agenda, which lasts until 23 June, plans for an audience every two days at the Senate beginning 31 May. Information and Coordination Minister Jacques Saint-Louis will be the first guest of the Senate office headed by Bernard Sansaricq. Saint-Louis will be received by the Senate on 31 May.
