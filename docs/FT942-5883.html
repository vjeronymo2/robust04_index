940602 FT 02 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Spring Ram buys Rest Assured Stag Furniture Holdings, part of Spring Ram, the lossmaking kitchens and bathrooms group, is acquiring Rest Assured and five properties used by it for Pounds 5m from BTR, the industrial conglomerate. Rest Assured is a branded bedding, upholstery and furniture maker. Stag has purchased net assets of about Pounds 3m in respect of the business and Pounds 1.75m for the properties. Spring Ram said that Rest would break even over the current year, after costs.
