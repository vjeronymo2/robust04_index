September 15, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

Santa Paula 39, St. Bonaventure 6 -- Freddy Torres ran for four touchdowns to lead Santa Paula at home.

Torres, who had 80 yards in 10 carries, scored on runs of two, five, 23 and 21 yards. Santa Paula (1-1) scored on its first two possessions and built a 39-0 lead before St. Bonaventure scored on the final play of the game.

Dustin Magdaleno had 124 yards in 13 carries, scored the game's first touchdown from four yards and caught a 34-yard touchdown pass from Ben Fausset for Santa Paula's final score. Fausset completed nine of 15 for 142 yards. LANCE O'FALLON
