930522 FT 22 MAY 93 / Finance & The Family: Banks shake off dust of ages - At a Glance The banking sector has come out of the doldrums. After 16 years of lagging behind the FT-A All-Share Index, the FT-Actuaries Bank Index this week moved ahead. Throughout the 1980s, bank shares were depressed by bad Third World loans and were hit again by the recession. But the addition of Hong Kong & Shanghai Bank to the sector, and the perception that profits are set to recover, have led to a rally in the sector's relative rating.
