930528 FT 28 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Southnews advances 76% to Pounds 1.58m Southnews, the USM-quoted London regional newspaper publisher, lifted pre-tax profits by 76 per cent in the year to April 3. The advance, from Pounds 899,000 to Pounds 1.58m, was achieved on lower turnover of Pounds 13.9m (Pounds 14.1m) and was after an exceptional Pounds 364,000 gain on the sale of its south coast newspapers. Turnover from continuing operations improved from Pounds 12.4m to Pounds 13.1m. Earnings per share worked through at 6.59p (5.32p) and the dividend is lifted to 2.1p (1.5p) with a proposed final of 1.4p (1p).
