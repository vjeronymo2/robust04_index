BFN [Text] Acting Internal Minister of the Crimea, Valeriy Kuznetsov, said in the morning of May 19 in an address to the session of the Crimean parliament in Simferopol that a coup had been attempted in Crimea last night. He said that Deputy Ukrainian Internal Minister Valentin Nedrihaylo had arrived in Simferopol at night. He was accompanied by a group of 15 special policemen. Nedrigailo read out to the personnel of the Crimean Internal Affairs Ministry the decree issued by Ukrainian President on May 18, 1994 to reorganize the Ministry and create on its basis a department of the internal affairs of the Crimea which would be subordinate to the Ukrainian Interior Ministry. In connection with this Valeriy Kuznetsov demanded from parliament that deputies immediately approve him as the Crimean Minister. An interval was announced at the parliament session. Deputies are waiting for the arrival of the parliament's defense commission which is to clarify these events. Kuznetsov was appointed acting Interior Minister of the Crimea a month ago by the decree of president Yuriy Meshkov.
