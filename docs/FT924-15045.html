921007 FT 07 OCT 92 / UK Company News: QS holds on for 7% interim rise QS HOLDINGS, the specialist retailer of value for money clothing, held its competitive trading advantage to raise turnover 14 per cent to Pounds 23.2m in the half year ended July 31. This fed through to pre-tax profits in a near 7 per cent rise, from Pounds 3.6m to Pounds 3.86m. Earnings per share were 6.57p (6.07p) and the interim dividend is pushed up to 1.56p (1.45p). The group operates from 78 stores in the south of England, predominantly selling women's and children's wear, and a selection of mens wear.
