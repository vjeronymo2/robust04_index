921026 FT 26 OCT 92 / International Company News: Downiebrae decline continues The profit decline at Downiebrae Holdings continued in the first half of 1992 with a pre-tax figure of Pounds 152,000, against Pounds 241,000. The Glasgow-based company said that no immediate improvement was forecast. Turnover for this maker of pipe flanges and cutter of steel profiles increased to Pounds 822,000 (Pounds 808,000). Earnings per share were 0.7p (1.07p).
