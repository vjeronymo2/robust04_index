Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, March 13 (XINHUA) -- The Guangzhou Nanfang Securities Exchange Center has been linked up with the Shanghai Stock Exchange in a network which will be put into operation on March 14. The Shanghai Stock Exchange is currently linked with the securities markets in Fuzhou and Shenyang. It plans to link up with Tianjin and five other cities in the near future. The Nanfang Securities Exchange Center has 64 members, among which 34 have started trading.
