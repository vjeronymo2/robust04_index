Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Feb. 23 -- Chairman of the National Assembly Nong Duc Manh was received in Beijing this morning by Jiang Zemin, general secretary of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee and president of China. President Jiang Zemin highly valued the visit of the Vietnam's National Assembly delegation, considering it as a vivid manifestation of the development of the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries. Chairman Nong Duc Manh, for his part, stressed that the problems left behind by the history and newly emerging problems need the two countries negotiate to solve in the principle of equality, mutual respect for each other's sovereignty and interests. [sentence as received] Chairman Nong Duc Manh [words indistinct] President Le Duc Anh and party General Secretary Do Muoi's invitation to President Jiang Zemin to pay an official visit to Vietnam, President Jiang Zemin accepted the invitation with pleasure. In the previous day, Chairman of the National Assembly Nong Duc Manh was received by Qiao Shi, standing member of the Communist Party of China's Political Bureau and chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China. Speaking to Mr. Qiao Shi, Mr. Nong Duc Manh said: "Vietnam and China are neighbours, their history has gone through ups and downs, but friendship and peace are the main characters, in their national liberation cause, the two peoples shared their last crust, helped each other in each country's revolutionary cause. With the constant tradition, Vietnamese people always bear in mind and is grateful at the previous assistance of the Chinese people. Since the normalisation of the two countries' relations, the two parties and states ties have seen considerable development steps, meeting the deep aspiration of the two peoples and conforming to the fundamental and long-term interests of the two countries was well as the trend of peace and cooperation in the region and on the world. [no end quotation marks as received] On this occasion, Chairman Nong Duc Manh invited a delegation of the National People's Congress of China led by Comrade Qiao Shi to Vietnam for an official friendship visit. Chairman Qiao Shi happily accepted the invitation. Speaking to his guests, Chairman Qiao Shi expressed his belief that the exchange of visits between the two legislative bodies will contribute to further promote the relations between the two parties, states and peoples. Later, Chairman Qiao Shi gave a banquet in honour of Chairman Nong Duc Manh and his party.
