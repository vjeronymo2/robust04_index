BFN [Text] The commerce minister has strongly justified India signing the new GATT deal, saying the opposition stand is based on narrow partisan considerations. Mr. Pranab Mukherji, who returned to New Delhi from Marrakesh this morning, said there is no rationale in the demand for opting out of the global trading system. He pointed out that 20 more countries want to join the new trade agreement in addition to the 125 countries which are signatories to it.
