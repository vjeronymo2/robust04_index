December 23, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

A staff member at Robert Morris College in Pittsburgh twice in 14 years paid apartment rents for athletes who had been kicked out of dormitories, the school has informed the NCAA.

Four athletes were involved, none of whom are currently at the college. One incident lasted five months, the other three months, according to college president Edward Nicholson.

Nicholson did not think the violations were major, but would not discuss them in detail until the NCAA reviewed the report.
