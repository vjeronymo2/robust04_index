BFN [Text] The Kurdistan office of the Supreme Assembly of the Islamic Revolution in Iraq [SAIRI] has stated that the popular resistance forces in the south have ambushed a unit of the 14th brigade currently deployed in al-Basrah marshlands. An intelligence service car was destroyed and its crew, including a lieutenant, have been either killed or wounded. A statement issued by the SAIRI office two days ago said that the popular resistance forces launched a major attack against a commando unit inside the al-'Amarah marshlands. The regime's troops were forced to retreat, leaving behind many dead troops and several destroyed vehicles. The regime's artillery pounded areas in [word indistinct], killing a civilian and injuring four others.
