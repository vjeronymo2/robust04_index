920424 FT 24 APR 92 / Hyundai move dropped The South Korean government yesterday decided to drop plans to punish Hyundai Electronics Industries (HEI) for alleged loan violations, writes John Burton in Seoul. The decision by the Office of Bank Supervision (OBS) is the first sign the government may be trying to defuse its feud with Mr Chung Ju-yung, the Hyundai founder. The OBS alleged HEI used bank loans illegally to finance the Unification National Party, the new anti-government group established by Mr Chung.
