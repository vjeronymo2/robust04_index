940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Wm Ransom A legal settlement of Pounds 325,000 helped William Ransom increase pre-tax profit by 13 per cent, from Pounds 817,000 to Pounds 925,000, despite a fall in turnover from Pounds 7.19m to Pounds 6.83m. The settlement related to an abortive move to Biggleswade for the pharmaceutical products maker. Earnings per share were 4.18p (3.49p). A final dividend of 1.22p gives a total of 1.989p (1.903p).
