Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In Sanaa, Yemeni sources have declared that the Ugandan plane that had been forced to land in Yemen by Yemeni warplanes last Sunday has been allowed to take off for Uganda after the northern Yemeni Armed Forces seized its cargo, which belongs to the southern authorities. Military sources in Sanaa said the cargo includes, specifically, a large number of batteries and 15 telecommunications sets for military purposes. However, the Aden authorities insist the plane's cargo included nothing but equipment for civilian purposes.
