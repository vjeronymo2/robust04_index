940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Restructure cuts losses at Bimec Bimec Industries, the water and waste treatment group, announced yesterday that its disposal and closure programme, initiated in November 1992, was nearing completion. The statement accompanied news that pre-tax losses for the six months to September 30 had been cut to Pounds 1.23m, against Pounds 5.28m last time and Pounds 16.4m in the full year to end-March; comparisons were restated for FRS 3. Turnover from continuing operations dipped to Pounds 9.3m (Pounds 10.3m), for operating losses of Pounds 796,000 (Pounds 1.26m). Mr Roy Barber, chairman, emphasised that the group still faced 'a long and difficult recovery period during which the continued support of our bankers will be essential'. Losses per share were 1.07p, down from 4.73p last time.
