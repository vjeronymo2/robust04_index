BFN [From the "This Morning" newscast] [Text] President Violeta Barrios de Chamorro's 4-7 April official visit to Mexico resulted in the signing of a $10-million credit agreement as well as ensuring cooperation assistance programs in the health, social, welfare, and technical sectors. Nicaragua and Mexico agreed that a free trade agreement between both nations would be signed before year's end. Likewise, the president indicated that both nations agreed that their respective finance ministers would meet shortly to discuss Nicaragua's debt with Mexico which it expects to repay with assets. [as heard] After publicly expressing her gratitude to President Carlos Salinas de Gortari for the attention received during her visit, as well as to all those persons who expressed their concern for her alleged fainting spell, she proceeded to explain all the activities carried out while in Mexico. The president also expressed enormous satisfaction with the results of her trip.
