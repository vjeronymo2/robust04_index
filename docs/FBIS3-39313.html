Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Feb.14 (A.A) -- The Constitutional Court issued its decision for closing the Freedom and Democracy Party (OZDEP) in today's OFFICIAL GAZETTE. The justification of the decision said that, the program of OZDEP was stating that "a Kurdish people and other minorities exist in Turkey, other than the Turkish nation." The decision said, "mentioning of the solidarity and self-determination of peoples in the program, damages the unity concept of the Turkish State. "The objectives of the party is similar to those of terrorists. "There is a single nation in Turkey and that is the Turkish nation.... Citizens of Turkish and Kurdish origin have formed the unity of our nation and efforts aiming at separation, are being supported by terror," it said.
