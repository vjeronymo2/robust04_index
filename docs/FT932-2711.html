930617 FT 17 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Craig & Rose Taking in Pounds 187,000 from the sale of land and investments pushed Craig & Rose back into profit in 1992. Pre-tax the figure came to Pounds 38,000, compared with a loss of Pounds 20,000. Turnover of the group, which makes paint and varnish, was little changed at Pounds 5.68m (Pounds 5.62m). Earnings per share worked through at 8.25p (losses 5p) and the dividend is again 14.25p with a proposed final of 12.5p.
