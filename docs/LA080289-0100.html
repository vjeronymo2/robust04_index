August 2, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Disney Studio Expansion: Heavy crowds and unexpectedly long waiting lines will soon force Walt Disney World to double the size of the entertainment portion of its new Disney-MGM Studios theme park, company Chairman Michael D. Eisner said. Specific plans for the expansion, which would be carried out in three years, will be announced in six weeks, Eisner told the Orlando Sentinel. Because many of the existing attractions are geared toward adults, the studio expansion would add more features for younger children, he said.
