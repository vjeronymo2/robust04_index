920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / Construction Contracts: Scottish hospital work HALL & TAWSE, the const ruction division of Raine Industries, has won hospital development contracts worth Pounds 7.3m. An orthopaedic centre and two operating theatres are being built at Woodend Hospital in Aberdeen by Hall & Tawse Scotland. The contract, worth Pounds 3m, also includes refurbishment of the hospital's theatre block. Hall & Tawse Southern has won a Pounds 1.7m contract to refurbish Southampton General Hospital, carrying out extensive repairs and renewal to the buildings' roofs and windows. The same Hall & Tawse company has also been awarded a contract to construct a private hospital in Reading for Independent British Hospitals. The project at Coley Park, which will take 55 weeks to complete, will provide 69 bed spaces.
