BFN [Text] Moscow June 3 TASS -- Presidents Boris Yeltsin and Bill Clinton had a telephone conversation today, the presidential press service reports. The Russian state head "informed Bill Clinton of Russian ideas on settlement of the North Korean `nuclear problem,' including the idea of a multilateral conference, in development of his conversations with South Korean President Kim Yong-sam on Thursday." The presidents agreed to instruct their experts to analyze these issues and prepare corresponding proposals, said the press service.
