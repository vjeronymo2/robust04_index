BFN [Text] Cairo, 6 Apr (MENA)--Hebron Mayor Mustafa al-Natshah arrived in Cairo from Israel today on a four-day visit. Upon his arrival, al-Natshah said the aim of his visit was to meet President Yasir 'Arafat to brief him on the situation in Hebron and to listen to his instructions for easing the suffering of the Hebron inhabitants, who were under curfew for 30 days after which the city center was closed on the pretext of protecting Jewish families. Al-Natshah explained that this closure inconvenienced 120,000 Palestinians. He said he would ask 'Arafat to explain this tragedy to President Mubarak so that he could try to help in this direction.
