June 20, 1989, Tuesday, Southland Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

'Here's a case where we have a brand new, state-of-the-art facility, and something in the security system was faulty. It certainly raises concern about placing another prison in the downtown area."

-- Gerry Hertzberg, chief legislative deputy to City Councilwoman Gloria Molina, commenting on the escape of five federal prisoners from the Metropolitan Detention Center.
