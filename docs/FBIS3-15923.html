Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Russian President Boris Yeltsin today signed a decree on holding an experiment in Tula Oblast to create organizational, legal, and economic conditions for the development of a regional agro-industrial complex, the presidential press service has reported. It is believed that the experiment will span the period between 1994 and 1998. It will be underpinned by economic self-management, expanded rights of the oblast authorities in implementing budgetary, investment, and credits policies, and also by improved internal production and economic relations.
