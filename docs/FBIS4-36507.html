BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received General Mikhail Petrovich Kolesnikov, Russian Armed Forces chief of staff, at the People's Palace at 1030 today. Lieutenant General Hikmat al-Shihabi, Army and Armed Forces chief of staff, attended. The president was briefed by Gen. Kolesnikov and Lt. Gen. al-Shihabi on the talks the Russian chief of staff has held in Syria. Talks dealt with bilateral cooperation between Syrian and Russia.
