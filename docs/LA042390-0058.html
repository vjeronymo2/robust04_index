April 23, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

ARGENTINA;

WORK SUSPENDED ON CONDOR 2 ROCKET

Argentina has suspended work on its program to build the controversial medium-range Condor 2 rocket, Defense Minister Humberto Romero said. He said that the project, which is being developed jointly with Egypt, "is not going ahead because a political decision was taken and also because we have neither the funds nor the budget to go ahead."
