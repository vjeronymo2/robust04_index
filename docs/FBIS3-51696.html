Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Caracas, 19 Jan (AFP) -- Disturbances broke out at noon today in Caracas to protest the value-added tax (VAT), even though its implementation at the consumer level was suspended until 28 February, reporters at the site of the disturbances reported. The disturbances broke out near the Central University of Venezuela, where hooded students set fire to a soft drink company truck, and in secondary schools and higher education centers in central and southwestern Caracas.
