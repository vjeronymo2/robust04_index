BFN [Text] Official report: On 24 May, according to the Minsk Oblast civil defense procedures plan, planned quarterly training was held for the networks of supervision and laboratory monitoring. Elaborate radiation protection measures were put into operation in Myadelskiy Rayon, Minsk Oblast. According to evidence obtained from the Main Hydrometeorology Service, the State Atomic Supervision and the leadership of the Ignalina nuclear power station, at 1200 today the radiation situation around the Ignalina nuclear power station was within normal limits. Since April, one generating unit at the station itself has been undergoing a scheduled overhaul. The second generating unit has been halted for economic reasons and is not producing electricity.
