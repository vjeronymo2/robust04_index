BFN [Text] Senior General Than Shwe, chairman of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] of the Union of Myanmar [Burma], arrived back in Yangon [Rangoon] by special aircraft at 0915. They were on a goodwill visit to the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR] at the invitation of the Laotian president, Mr. Nouhak Phoumsavan. Sr. Gen. Than Shwe was accompanied by his wife Daw Kyaing Kyaing. They were welcomed at Yangon airport by General Maung Aye, vice chairman of SLORC; Vice Admiral Maung Maung Khin and Lieutenant General Tin Tun, SLORC members and deputy prime ministers; Lt. Gen. Tin U, SLORC secretary-2, and SLORC members; cabinet ministers; deputy foreign minister, senior military officers and their wives; responsible military and civil officials; Dr. Baron Walther von Bieberstein, German ambassador and dean of diplomatic corps; ambassadors, charge d'affairs, and their wives; and Mrs. (Virasak Bunsak), charge d'affaires of LPDR. Sr. Gen. Than Shwe and his wife were also accompanied by Lieutenant General Khin Nyunt, SLORC secretary-1, and his wife, Dr. Khin Win Shwe; Lt. Gen. Myint Aung, minister of agriculture; Brigadier General Abel, minister of national planning and economic development; U Ohn Gyaw, minister of foreign affairs, and wife; Brig. Gen. Myo Thant, minister of information; and responsible personnel from the SLORC office, Defense Ministry, and Foreign Ministry.
