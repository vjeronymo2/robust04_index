930709 FT 09 JUL 93 / Confused recovery picture in north COMPANIES in the north are confused about economic recovery, with no consistent pattern of improvement yet apparent, according to the Yorkshire and Humberside regional council of the Confederation of British Industry. Mr Brian Bigley, CBI regional director, said: 'Order books are better than they were three, six and nine months ago, but it seems half a step back for every step forward.'
