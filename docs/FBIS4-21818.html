BFN [Text] According to an ATHENS NEWS AGENCY dispatch, the Orthodox Patriarch's quarters at Fanari in Istanbul was attacked last night with two Molotov cocktails. According to the dispatch, at 2200 [1900 GMT] last night, unidentified people threw the two Molotov cocktails from the northern wall into the yard between the central building and library. Patriarch employees dealt with the fire before it caused any extensive damage. The fire department did not need to intervene. Turkish police are investigating the case. Similar incidents have occurred at least four times in the past couple of months but no effective safety measures have been taken at the Patriarch's quarters.
