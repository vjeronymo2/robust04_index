940516 FT 16 MAY 94 / Company News (This Week): Kmart - Other companies Today the market will get further evidence of the US discount store group's difficulties when it produces its results for the first quarter to April. The company warned last month that the figures would be even worse than last year's dismal first quarter, so there are unlikely to be any surprises. In the comparable period, it made net profits of Dollars 50m before extraordinary items, and analysts expect the figure to be halved.
