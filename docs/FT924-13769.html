921014 FT 14 OCT 92 / Taiwan growth rate set to fall TAIWAN'S economic growth could drop to 6.3 per cent this year, below the government's projection of 7 per cent, because of the world recession and delays in major public projects, the Council for Economic Planning and Development said yesterday, AP reports from Taipei. Taiwan's economy grew 7.24 per cent last year and 5.29 per cent in 1990. Taiwan's total trade in the first nine months of this year totalled Dollars 113.77bn (Pounds 66.7bn). Its trade surplus was Dollars 7.43bn.
