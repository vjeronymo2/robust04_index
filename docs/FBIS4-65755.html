BFN [Text] In Alain Juppe's opinion, lifting the embargo on arms to the Bosnian Muslims would hasten their destruction. Such a measure would also run the risk of launching a drawn-out war, with its attendant deaths, ruin, refugees, and deep-rooted hatreds, the foreign minister said. Let me remind you that the lifting of the embargo on arms is being called for notably by the U.S. Senate, and in France by Michel Rocard and intellectuals like philosopher Bernard-Henri Levy.
