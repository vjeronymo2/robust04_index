BFN [Unattributed report in the "Provincial Chronicle" column] [Text] Yaroslavl -- Russian Federation Vice Premier Yuriy Yarov, who arrived at the oblast center 27 April on a working visit, went to a number of industrial enterprises and met with the leadership of the oblast administration. During the meetings the employment problem was discussed. Mr. Yarov explained his visit to Yaroslavl by the government's intention to study the situation in the regions and try and "see, in good time, the line beyond which a social explosion could follow."
