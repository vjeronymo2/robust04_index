April 10, 1990, Tuesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

IN THE STARS: ITALY VS. ARGENTINA

After overnight occult rituals in the "very magic moment of the Aries plenilune, (full moon)" Italian clairvoyants today predicted that the World Cup final will pit Italy against defending champion Argentina.

The prediction was made by wizards, astrologers and clairvoyants who are members of the Italian Wizards Assn. Some said they see an Italian victory. The clairvoyants also predicted that Cameroon and United Arab Emirates, both considered underdogs by experts, will do unexpectedly well.

A dissident group, the "Wizards of the Hut," made different predictions. Its members see Brazil as the World Cup winner and Romania coming up with very interesting surprises.
