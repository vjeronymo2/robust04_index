BFN [Text] Iraq has once again protested to the UN secretary general the U.S. aircraft violations of Iraqi airspace for observation, provocation purposes and for dropping flares. Iraq's permanent represenative said in a memorandum addressed to the UN secretary general yesterday that the referent aircraft carried out 561 hostile air sorties and several reconnaissance sorties 21-30 April, in addition to dropping flares. Iraq's permanent representative asked that the memorandum be circulated among the UN Security Council members and to consider it as one of its documents.
