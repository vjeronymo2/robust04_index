BFN [Text] Moscow, 4 April, IRNA -- Turkmenistan is to pump gas to Europe by way of a trunkline that will pass through Iranian territory, according to an agreement between the Turkmen Republic and Iran. Iranian Oil Minister Gholam Reza Aqazadeh said in Ashgabat Monday that the two countries would be the principal sides to the agreement that will pump Turkmen gas to Europe. Turkmen President Saparmurad Niyazov appreciated the value of the project Monday saying cooperation between the two neighbours would be mutually beneficial. The Iranian oil minister also conveyed the greetings of President Hojjat ol-Eslam Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani to the Turkmen president.
