Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Ivanova] [Text] Moscow January 10 TASS -- A total of 67 per cent of 1,223 respondents in a public opinion poll carried out by the Grigory Pashkov Service said Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, leader of the Russian Liberal Democratic Party, will not keep his election pledges. At the same time, one in ten believe he will. Seventy two per cent of those polled said they would not like to see Zhirinovskiy as prime minister, while eight per cent are of the opposite opinion.
