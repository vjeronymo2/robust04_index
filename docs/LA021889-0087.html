February 18, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

UCLA WOMEN GYMNASTS TOP ENTRIES

The UCLA women's gymnastics team, undefeated and ranked second in the country, will play host to the 11th annual UCLA-Los Angeles Times Invitational meet tonight at 7:30 at Pauley Pavilion.

The Bruins, who won the event last year but have never been national champions, have won 23 consecutive dual meets over the last three years. This season, they have a 7-0 overall record and are 1-0 in the Pacific 10 Conference.

Also competing are third-ranked Cal State Fullerton, fifth-ranked Georgia, 10th-ranked Arizona State and Stanford.
