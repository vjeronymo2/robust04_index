Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Feb.1 -- Deputy Prime Minister Nguyen Khanh received here this afternoon an economic delegation of the Romanian government led by Mr. Cristian Ioneseu, minister of trade and chairman of the Romania's Sub-Committee of the Romania-Vietnam Inter Governmental Commission for Economic, Scientific and Technological Cooperation, currently on a visit to Vietnam to attend the 14th session of the commission. Minister Cristian Ioneseu informed Deputy Prime Minister Khanh of the activities of the commission and the results and solutions of the problems put forth at the commission's meeting, thus pushing up the economic, scientific and technological cooperation between Vietnam and Romania. He expressed his hope that possibilities and potentials of each country would be a basis for new development of such relation in the interests of the two peoples. For his party, Deputy P.M. Khanh affirmed that the Vietnamese Government and people always attach great importance to the existing relations between Vietnam and Romania. He expressed his belief that the current meeting of the commission would actively contribute to the further development of the cooperation between the two countries.
