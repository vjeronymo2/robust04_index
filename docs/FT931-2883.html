930318 FT 18 MAR 93 / International Company News in Brief: Landsbanki ICELAND'S government is rescuing Landsbanki, the country's largest bank, through a IKr4.25bn (Dollars 68m) support package in the face of the bank's soaring credit losses, writes Christopher Brown-Humes. Half of the IKr4.25bn injection will be in the form of subordinated debt while the other half will be equity.
