Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 14 (KCNA) -- The 27th plenary meeting of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Unions of Korea held in Pyongyang on January 12 and 13 discussed tasks for the trade union organizations to carry out the tasks set by the great leader President Kim Il-song in his new year address and implement to the letter the resolution of the 21st plenary meeting of the sixth Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and the teachings given recently by the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong- il. The meeting stressed the need for the trade union organizations to energetically organize the working class and other union members for the implementation of the party's revolutionary economic strategy of following the agriculture-first, light industry-first and trade-first policy. Noting that Comrade Kim Chong-il recently said the working people's organizations should push ahead more vigorously with the work of human remoulding and indicated the ways to this end, the meeting emphasized that the trade union officials should conduct ideological education among the unionists well.
