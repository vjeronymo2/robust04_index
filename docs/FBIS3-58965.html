Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The presidential candidates, Elisabeth Rehn and Martti Ahtisaari, are both calling for moderate tones in discussions on the possible return of territory ceded to the Soviet Union at the end of the last war. At a joint appearance in the southeastern city of Lappeenranta, in the Finnish part of Karelia, Ms Rehn said that she has had numerous discussions on the issue with Russians. Mr. Ahtisaari said that time is needed before discussions with Russia on the possible return of the ceded parts of Karelia can begin.
