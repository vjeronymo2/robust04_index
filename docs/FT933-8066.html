930818 FT 18 AUG 93 / Teenager wins harassment case A PIZZA company was yesterday ordered by an industrial tribunal to pay a teenage girl Pounds 6,000 compensation after ruling that she had been sexually harassed by the manager. The tribunal said Big Mama's takeaway pizza company should pay Ms Lesley Jones Pounds 1,000 for loss of wages and Pounds 5,000 for hurt feelings. The tribunal heard that the manager of the branch in Horsforth, Leeds, had molested her.
