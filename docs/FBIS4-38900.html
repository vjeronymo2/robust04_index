BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe, 3 Jun -- Four people, including two staff officers of the Tajik Defense Ministry, fell victim to unknown assailants in Dushanbe yesterday evening. An ITAR-TASS correspondent was told at the Tajik Interior Ministry that the murder occurred in an apartment where Major Aleksandr Zakharov lived. The attackers also fatally wounded Lieutenant Colonel Sergey Khristoforov and two women who were in the room. The motives for the murders are being established by an operational investigation group of the Tajik Interior Ministry.
