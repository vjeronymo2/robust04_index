920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / UK Company News: City Merchants High Yield assets up City Merchants High Yield Trust reported a net asset value of 101.5p at June 30, up from 92.7p at its December year-end. The trust, which came to the market in May last year, achieved net revenue of Pounds 734,646 in the six months to end-June for earnings of 4.897p per share. A second interim dividend of 2.875p brings the total so far this year to 4.875p.
