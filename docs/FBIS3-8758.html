Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Catherine Lalumiere, Council of Europe secretary general, who is on an official visit to Poland, has had a meeting with Sejm Speaker Jozef Oleksy and Senate Speaker Adam Struzik. Both speakers agreed with Madame Lalumiere that the Council of Europe should be extended to the countries of the former Soviet Union. According to Oleksy, Poland's experiences could help Polish neighbors to achieve closer contacts with the Council of Europe.
