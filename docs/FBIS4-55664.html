BFN [Text] Croatian President Franjo Tudjman received a letter from Bosnian Presidency President Alija Izetbegovic, who stayed in Zagreb yesterday en route to Saudi Arabia, the presidential office reports. I want to send you my regards and confirm the commitment of the Presidency and Government of Bosnia-Herzegovina to the spirit and provisions of the Vienna agreement. We shall do our best to implement this agreement as purposefully as possible [break in transmission] Awaiting your forthcoming visit to Sarajevo, I would like to express my kind regards, Alija Izetbegovic writes in his letter to President Tudjman.
