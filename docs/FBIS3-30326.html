Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] On 1 February National Assembly Chairman Aleksandur Yordanov met Iranian Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati, who is paying an official visit to Bulgaria. The Iranian foreign minister agreed with Aleksandur Yordanov's opinion that the issue of the Bulgarian Muslims must be taken off the agenda of the Islamic Conference Organization, because this issue has been settled in Bulgaria in accordance with the law. The possibilities of developing parliamentary relations between Bulgaria and Iran were discussed, as well as the possibilities of establishing friendship societies in the two parliaments. The two countries presented their positions on the Yugoconflict.
