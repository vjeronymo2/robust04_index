Language: Malay Article Type:BFN [Text] The international conference of parliamentarians on Bosnia-Herzegovina ended in Kuala Lumpur today with a call for the convening of a new international conference to replace the Geneva peace talks. All parliamentarians from 33 countries who attended the two-day conference vowed to wage a campaign for a new conference. A resolution issued at the end of the conference today called on all countries to support an initiative to lift the ban on arms exports to Bosnia-Herzegovina. The parliamentarians also voiced their reservations against the Bosnia peace negotiators, who had acted against the UN Charter and the London Conference. They also called on the United Nations to take immediate action to save Sarajevo and other cities from Serbian destruction.
