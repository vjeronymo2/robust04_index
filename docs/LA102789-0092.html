October 27, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Steve Moore, a former starting offensive lineman for the New England Patriots, was shot and killed after apparently being robbed outside a convenience store in Memphis, Tenn., Wednesday night, police said.

Moore, 29, a tackle for the Patriots from 1983 through 1987, bought several items at the store and then was shot as he returned to his car, Lt. Hank Arnold said.

Moore managed to get back inside the store and told the clerk he had been robbed and shot.

There were no witnesses, and police have no suspects.
