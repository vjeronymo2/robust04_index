931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Hadleigh Industries turns in Pounds 332,000 Hadleigh Industries, the USM-quoted storage tank and trailer manufacturer, built on its return to profitability at the year end by turning in pre-tax profits of Pounds 332,000 for the six months to October 1, against losses of Pounds 357,000. Turnover was up from Pounds 13.8m to Pounds 14.7m. Earnings per share came out at 3.1p (3.3p losses). The pre-tax figure was also boosted by a reduction in interest payments to Pounds 173,000 (Pounds 366,000). The company returns to the interim dividend list with a distribution of 0.5p.
