BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan 5 May TASS -- As a result of shelling at the only operating gas pipeline in Armenia from the territory of Azerbaijan, the pipeline was damaged leaving Armenia without gas, Roland Adonts, general director of the "Armgazprom" state enterprise, told ITAR-TASS. The accident occurred on the territory of the frontier Noemberyan district of Armenia where specialists from the "Armgazprom" were sent for repairs. Meanwhile, the gas pipeline damage has immediately affected the electric power supply of Yerevan and other cities and districts of Armenia. Under the transport blockade conditions gas is the only fuel for Armenian thermal power stations. Therefore, residents of Yerevan are supplied with electricity only for one-two hours daily as it was in winter.
