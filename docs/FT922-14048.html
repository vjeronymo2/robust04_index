920408 FT 08 APR 92 / UK Company News: Baillie Gifford Tech Net asset value per share at Baillie Gifford Technology stood at 11.5p on February 29 compared with 40.9p a year earlier. Net revenue for the year amounted to Pounds 39,786 (Pounds 204,012) for earnings of 0.36p (1.85p). The dividend is cut from 1.4p to 0.2p.
