November 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ELECTION RESULTS

On Election Day we voted in the local elementary school down the street from our home in Westwood.

The school auditorium was old, shabby, poorly lit, cluttered with decrepit furniture and grungy.

Our bank in Westwood is beautiful. It's new, made of marble, its floors covered with plush carpeting. It has the latest in lighting, a three-story ceiling and exquisitely styled furniture.

Where are our priorities?

JEFFREY HAYDEN

Los Angeles
