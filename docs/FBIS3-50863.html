Article Type:BFN [From "Caracol's 0600 to 0900" newscast] [Text] Economic Development Minister Luis Alberto Moreno will be replaced next week, as he is joining New Democratic Force leader Andres Pastrana's presidential campaign. Pastrana confirmed to this newscast today that Luis Fernando Ramirez, Labor and Social Security minister, will remain in his post while the social security law is being drafted. [Begin Pastrana recording] It is clear that Economic Development Minister Moreno will join my campaign in mid-January. We have also spoken with Minister Ramirez. President Cesar Gaviria wants Mr. Ramirez to stay in his post not only to advance the regulations but also to put the final touches on the social security law. [end recording] Mr. Pastrana said that upon his return from Chile he will meet with President Gaviria to finalize the details of this issue.
