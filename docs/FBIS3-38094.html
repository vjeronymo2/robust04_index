Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Commenting on a statement signed by 40 people's deputies of the Belarusian Supreme Soviet, in which they refused to participate in the voting on the draft constitution, Vyacheslaw Kuznetsow, first deputy Supreme Soviet chairman, stated: "I think that the behavior of these people's deputies is very illogical, the more so since some of them were members of the Constitutional Committee. They have stated that they are against the form of voting. I, personally, believe that this is just an unwillingness to adopt the basic law," Vyacheslaw Kuznetsow concluded.
