December 17, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: MISSION VIEJO;

CITY EMBARKS ON 'MISSION RECYCLE'

The city's recycling program gets under way today with ceremonies featuring balloons, refreshments and information about the program.

City officials will open the event at 9:45 a.m. in Flamenco Park, at Melinda Road between Tobarra and Anaya. Dewey's Rubbish Service is contractor for the program.

"We want recycling to become a community commitment," said Damian Hopp, assistant city manager. "We want that commitment to be our 'Mission Recycle.' "
