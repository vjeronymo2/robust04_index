940308 FT 08 MAR 94 / Pounds 15,000 fine for pollution incident Pasminco, a smelting company in Avonmouth, Bristol, was fined Pounds 15,000 with Pounds 6,230 costs by Bristol magistrates yesterday for allowing sulphur trioxide to escape on July 27 last year. The cause of the release was 'a malicious act by person or persons unknown', the Department of the Environment said but the company admitted failing to use the best practicable means to prevent the release. Several people suffered breathing difficulties in the incident.
