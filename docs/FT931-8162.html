930220 FT 20 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Fleming High net asset value improves Net asset value per share of the Fleming High IncomeTrust amounted to 94.9p at January 31 compared with 83.3p a year earlier and 88.7p at the trust's year end on April 30 1992. Net revenue for the nine month period fell from Pounds 1.38m to Pounds 1.29m for earnings per share of 4.12p (4.53p). The third interim dividend is an unchanged 1.45p, payable April 1, making 4.35p (same) so far.
