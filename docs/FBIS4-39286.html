BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Khabarovsk, 7 Jun--Today, on his way back to his motherland, Republic of Korea President Kim Yong-sam made a short stop in Khabarovsk. An honor guard was formed at the airport. At the steps to the "Boeing 747", the high-ranking guest was met by Viktor Ishayev, head of the Khabarovsk Kray administration, and other officials. A half-hour conversation took place between the kray's governor and the president of South Korea. Viktor Ishayev reported that an exchange of opinions had taken place on a number of problems relating to mutual relations between the Republic of Korea and the Far Eastern region. In particular, the issue of setting up a free economic zone and ways of further activating economic cooperation were examined. More than 30 joint Russian-Korean enterprises are currently operating in the kray, the leading South Korean firms "Samsung", "Goldstar", and others are represented. The president of South Korea guaranteed assistance in implementing a large-scale project to produce buses in the Amur region with the participation of the Korean side. After an hour-long stay in Khabarovsk, the president of South Korea left by air for Vladivostok.
