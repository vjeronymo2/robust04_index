BFN [Excerpt] AL-SAFIR has learned that the Lebanese State has seized all the Lebanese Forces' movable and immovable assets in Lebanon. It has obtained declarations from financial and administrative officials in the Lebanese Forces that they will not use the title deeds in their possession. The assets have been sold, on paper only, to these officials on direct orders from Samir Ja'ja'. According to available information, official inquiries are being made to track down the Lebanese Forces' assets and property abroad in preparation for requesting their seizure. [passage omitted]
