920326 FT 26 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Estates & Agency advances to Pounds 0.44m Estates & Agency Holdings, the property development group, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 437,000 for the six months to December 31. The outcome - a 25 per cent increase on the comparable Pounds 349,000 - came on gross rental income of Pounds 2.5m, up from Pounds 2.41m. An interim of 2.5p is payable from earnings of 5.59p (4.26p).
