941119 FT 19 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Personal Assets Personal Assets Trust had a net asset value of Pounds 86.47p at October 31, a rise of 1.3 per cent on its April year-end figure of Pounds 85.34p. The independently managed trust's benchmark - the FT-SE-A All-Share Index - declined by 2.8 per cent during the same period. Earnings per share in the six months to end-October were unchanged at 108p. As already announced, a second interim dividend of 100p will bring the total for the year to 200p (195p).
