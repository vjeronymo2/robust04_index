October 13, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

An unidentified man was killed Thursday when the car he was driving crashed into a parked car in East San Diego, a San Diego police spokesman said.

The victim reportedly fell asleep while driving east on Home Avenue about 12:40 a.m., Sgt. Mike Healey said. The car veered across the double-yellow line and smashed into a car parked along the north curb, he said.

The driver was taken to UC San Diego Medical Center, where he was pronounced dead.

Healey said the coroner's office has been unable to determine the victim's identity.
