BFN [Text] Beijing, May 20 (XINHUA) -- Ni Zhifu, vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of China's National People's Congress (NPC), hosted a dinner here today for a delegation from the Lao Federation of Trade Unions. They exchanged opinions on issues of common interest. The four-member delegation, headed by the federation's president, Khampan Philavon, started a five-day goodwill visit to China yesterday at the invitation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions.
