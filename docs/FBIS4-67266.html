Huallaga PY1605230794 Madrid EFE in Spanish 0114 GMT 16 May 94 PY1605230794 Madrid EFE Spanish BFN [Text] Lima, 15 May (EFE) -- Military sources in the Upper Huallaga jungle region have reported that on 15 May Peruvian security forces seized 150 kg of cocaine base after finding a Colombian light plane. The sources added that the plane was found early on Sunday morning. It had been abandoned on a clandestine airfield in San Pablo, San Martin Department. The situation of the plane is being investigated. It is believed to have been abandoned by traffickers after they were discovered by an Army patrol. The site where the Colombian plane was found is considered a "red zone" where coca is grown and macerated into cocaine base. Peruvian cocaine base is taken to laboratories in Colombia where it is processed into pure cocaine.
