BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Helsinki April 4 TASS -- Russia's Vladimir Zhirinovskiy criticised on Monday the idea of his country joining the Council of Europe. "We do not need your Council of Europe. We do not ask for it. On the contrary, it is you who need us", leader of a powerful faction in the russian parliament told reporters on arrival in Helsinki. A delegation of Russian lawmakers is due to take part in a parliamentary seminar here on prospects of the Russian membership of the Council of Europe. Zhirinovskiy stressed that his party (ultra-nationalist party misleadingly called Liberal-Democratic) "has nothing against Finland". He complained that Finnish journalists lack true information, apparently referring to local media reports repeatedly calling him an advocate of Finland's re-joining Russia.
