October 5, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WHO'S HOT

Rudy Ramirez of Bell Gardens rushed for 202 yards and scored four touchdowns in a 28-20 victory over Montebello.

Ivan Wilson rushed for 165 yards and scored four touchdowns as South Gate High defeated L.A. Jordan, 34-0.

Cerritos High won its first game, scoring two touchdowns in 28 seconds to come from behind and upset Lynwood, 21-19.

John Glenn High junior fullback Paseka Teo rushed for 139 yards as the Eagles defeated Duarte, 27-12.

Ron Papazian and Scott Jordan combined for 352 yards and six TDs as Whittier Christian beat Bell-Jeff, 42-0.

Who's Not

Lakewood High has lost its last three games, including a 17-7 defeat last weekend to Marina, which snapped an 18-game losing streak.

Brethren High has been outscored, 101-0, in its last two losses to Montclair Prep and Valley Christian.

The La Serna Lancers are 0-4 and have been outscored, 124-29, under new coach Wayne Manzo. The team was 1-9 last year with Coach Ron Moncrief.

Pioneer High has started the season 0-4 after going 1-9 last year.

Millikan High of Long Beach had three first downs and 72 yards of total offense in a 10-6 loss to Schurr.
