BFN [Text] Seoul, June 17 (YONHAP) -- The Foreign Ministry briefed members of the foreign diplomatic corps here Friday as part of efforts to subside war scares on the Korean peninsula. Assistant Foreign Minister Choe Tong-chin held a special national security briefing at the ministry for some 100 attendees, explaining that South Korea is fully monitoring any movement in North Korea and has not detected any sign of danger. Explaining the latest development on the North Korean nuclear situation, the assistant minister emphasized that South Korea is fully ready to detect and counter any armed provocation.
