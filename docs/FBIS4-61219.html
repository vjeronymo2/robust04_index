BFN [Text] Moscow, 4 May (ITAR-TASS) -- The Russian Federal Counterintelligence Service has detained officials from the law enforcement bodies suspected of corruption. ITAR-TASS has learned from well-informed sources that among those detained are a senior official of the directorate for administering punishments of the Moscow Oblast Main directorate of internal affairs, a senior officer of the investigating committee of the Russian Interior Ministry, and a Moscow lawyer. Being "kept" by Moscow criminal groupings, by using their contacts with corrupt officials from the law enforcement bodies and by using bribes in hard currency, they secured the release of members of criminal groupings who had committed burglaries, robberies, hostage-taking, murders, and other crimes. During the operation, illegal firearms, several hundred thousand U.S. dollars, gold, and precious stones were confiscated from the suspects. The criminal case involving the individuals is being investigated by the Russian General Procuracy.
