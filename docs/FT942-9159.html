940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / International Company News: Telecom NZ ahead 15.4% Telecom New Zealand, controlled by Bell Atlantic and Ameritech of the US, reported a 15.4 per cent rise in net earnings to NZDollars 528. lm (USDollars 308.8m). Mr Roderick Deane, chief executive, said strong progress had been made on all fronts with operating revenue rising by NZDollars 22.7m to NZDollars 2.49bn.
