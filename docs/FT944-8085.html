941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / VG try to gain access to national newspaper supplies VG, the convenience stores group that spearheaded the campaign by independent grocers to get access to national newspaper supplies, yesterday accused News International of flouting a new legally binding code of practice, Raymond Snoddy writes. Mr John Gardner, the VG managing director, has written to Mr Jonathan Evans, minister of corporate affairs at the Department of Trade and Industry, complaining about new News International distribution contracts. His company said yesterday the contracts demonstrated that 'News International is seeking to introduce levels of control for retail outlets over and above the code for newspaper supplies.'
