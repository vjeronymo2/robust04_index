931119 FT 19 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Arthur Shaw in black at midway Arthur Shaw, a maker of builders' hardware and supplier of engineering products, maintained its improvement in the 26 weeks to October 3 with pre-tax profits of Pounds 243,000, against losses of Pounds 124,000 in the corresponding 27 weeks of 1992. Turnover grew 22 per cent to Pounds 9.85m. Earnings per share were 1.83p (1.03p losses) and the interim dividend is restored with a payment of 0.5p - last year there was no dividend and Pounds 99,000 losses were incurred. The shares rose 4p to 50p.
