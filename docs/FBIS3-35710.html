Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Nuclear Subs Dismantling Goes Ahead in Far East"] [Text] A commission of the Pacific Fleet and of the Maritime Kray administration began work in Vladivostok on 26 January. Navy Commander in Chief Feliks Gromov underlined that the main objective of such a union is to compile a comprehensive program for the recycling and exporting of obsolete and sunken Pacific Fleet ships. Observers point out that not only the 35 written-off Pacific Fleet submarines can be used for these purposes, but also the obsolete aircraft-carrying cruisers Minsk and Novorossiysk.
