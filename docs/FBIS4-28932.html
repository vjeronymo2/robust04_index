BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 12 (KCNA) -- The letter sent by the general director of the General Department of the Atomic Energy of the DPRK to the general director of the International Atomic Energy Agency regarding the latter's hurried "conclusion" that the agency could not guarantee the non-diversion of nuclear material, alleging that the technical possibility for later measurement of core fuel rods was lost at the 5-megawatt experimental nuclear power plant of the DPRK, was distributed as official document of the UN Security Council S/1994/677 on June 6.
