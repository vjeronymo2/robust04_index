BFN [Report from roundup of reports by correspondents and ITAR-TASS: "Why Does General Need Bayan?"] [Excerpt] A new commander of the coalition peacekeeping forces has arrived in Dushanbe -- Colonel General Valeriy Patrikeyev. In his 56 years the new commander in chief has managed to graduate from two military academies and General Staff higher academy courses. In serving the fatherland, he has traveled the whole country from West to East. Before being appointed to Tajikistan for the past four years Patrikeyev has commanded the Transcaucasus Military District so that, as he says, he has peacekeeping experience. [passage omitted]
