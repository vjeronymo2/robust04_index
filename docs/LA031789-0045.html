March 17, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

More on Letterman: His uninvited guest -- a woman charged with breaking into his home on several occasions -- has been ordered to undergo further psychiatric exams to determine whether she understands the charges against her. The attorney for Margaret Ray, 36, requested the additional tests after one psychiatrist found her competent to stand trial. Ray faces charges of criminal trespassing, burglary, possession of marijuana and larceny. She was arrested for breaking into Letterman's home in New Canaan, Conn., on Feb. 28. It was the second time in five days that she had been found inside Letterman's unoccupied house, and last spring, she also had been arrested twice for breaking into his home that's about 40 miles north of New York City. Ray will return to court March 28 to learn whether the judge believes she is competent to stand trial. Aleene Macminn
