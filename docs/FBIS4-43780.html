BFN [Text] House of Representatives President Alexis Galanos expressed the hope that Russia will play a leading role in the convening of an international conference on the Cyprus issue. Galanos made the above statement at Larnaca Airport prior to departing with a parliamentary delegation for an official visit to Moscow. Galanos said that the delegation will try to promote the position of the Cyprus Government regarding the Cyprus issue. The delegation will ask for Russia's support on the issue, which, he added, has always stood by our side. The House of Representatives delegation will have talks with Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and officials of the Russian parliament.
