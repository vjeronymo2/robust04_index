February 22, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"I have a feeling that some people who are pushing these laws are thinking more like Communists and liberals."

-- Tony Acuerdo, one of seven members of the National Rifle Assn. from Los Angeles and Stockton who said they would file a lawsuit today asking the California Supreme Court to block ordinances that ban the sale and ownership of assault-type weapons.
