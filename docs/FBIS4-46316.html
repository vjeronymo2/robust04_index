BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Terrorists stopped a vehicle near Karamese village in Sason District in Batman at 1900 last night, killed the four passengers, and set the vehicle on fire. Extensive operations are under way to catch the terrorists who fled after the incident. In Adana, eleven members of an illegal leftist organization were caught. Six of the suspects are women. According to information received from the governor, the suspects were trying to revive the organization and were getting ready for action. The police found one pistol and organizational documents on the suspects and in their homes.
