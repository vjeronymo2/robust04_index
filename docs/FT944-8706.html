941118 FT 18 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Fulcrum Investment Fulcrum Investment Trust reported a fall in net asset value per capital share to 47.09p at October 31, against 63.12p a year earlier and 58.38p at the end of April. Net asset value per income share was 3.68p (2.46p at October 31 1993 and 1.44p at April 30) and 129.94p (117.04p and 123.26p respectively) per zero preference share. After-tax revenue for the six months to the end of October rose from Pounds 497,467 to Pounds 565,353 and earnings per income came through at 5.03p (4.43p). A second interim of 1.4p makes a same-again 2.8p to date.
