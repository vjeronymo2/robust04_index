September 22, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION FOOTBALL;

ROUNDUP

Murphy 21, Village Christian 13 -- Trailing, 14-7, Village Christian (2-1) scored on a four-yard run by Rick Nickels with 8:39 to play, but a two-point conversion pass failed and Murphy scored with 2:30 remaining at St. Bernard High. KEVIN DAVIS
