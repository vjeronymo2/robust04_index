BFN [Text] Tokyo, April 15 KYODO -- New party Sakigake (Harbinger) led by Chief Cabinet Secretary Masayoshi Takemura will not take posts in a new cabinet, but will support the coalition's candidate for premier to succeed outgoing Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa. Shusei Tanaka, acting leader, made the statement. Sakigake has boycotted discussion sessions of secretaries general of the coalition parties to select a candidate for the next prime minister, saying the coalition chiefs should first discuss the matter.
