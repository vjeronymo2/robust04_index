BFN [Text] Hangzhou, June 20 (XINHUA) -- East China's Zhejiang Province has leased five plots of land for foreign investment. With a combined area of 23.7 sq km, the five plots are located in the Ningbo Bonded Zone, Hangzhou's Qianjiang Investment Zone, Zhoushan's Donggang Economic Development Zone, the Jiaxing Economic Development Zone and the Zhapu Economic Development Zone. The plots are being developed jointly by the Zhejiang Real Estate Industrial Development Company and five other companies. Altogether, 300 million yuan has been used in constructing infrastructure facilities, and seven sq km of the areas are ready for foreign investment. An official of the company said that business people from the United States, Canada and Australia have expressed interest in investing in the areas.
