BFN [Text] Sanaa, 21 Apr (INA) -- A prominent Yemeni commander has said that implementing the Yemeni document of pledge and accord and withdrawing the military units from the previous areas of partition is the way to end the current Yemeni crisis. In statements published by the newspaper 26 SEPTEMBER, mouthpiece of the Yemeni Armed Forces, today, Brigadier General 'Abd-al-Malik (al-Sabati), Yemeni chief of staff, revealed that a military plan is being prepared which contains numerous measures to withdraw the military forces to new strategic areas in six Yemeni governorates. He added that this plan aims to remove military tension and keep the military units away from the previous areas of partition.
