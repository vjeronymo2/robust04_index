BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valentina Ivanova] [Text] Cheboksary April 23 TASS -- President of Chuvashia, a republic in the Volga region, Nikolay Fedorov, announced on Saturday his programme of popular reform in the republic. The programme prioritises steady state support for manufacturers and small businesses, effective investment policy and establishment of an economic and social infrastructure. Fedorov believes that the implementation of his programme will hinge on "professional cooperation with the federal authorities of the Russian Federation," Russian constituent republics and CIS states, as well as neighbouring regions and all ethnic communities living inside and outside Chuvashia.
