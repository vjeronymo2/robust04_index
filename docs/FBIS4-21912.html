BFN [Text] A powerful explosion caused by a car bomb occurred at a bus stop near the 'Afula Culture Palace. This has been announced by Commander Ya'aqov Ganot, the commander of the Israel Police Northern District Department. An initial report has four dead and about 10 injured. Our correspondent Yo'av Aviv reports that numerous Magen David Adom ambulances arrived to the site. No other details are available at this time.
