BFN [Text] The information center of the Russian Federal Employment Service has told Interfax that in the first five months of the year the service provided 375,000 people with jobs. Last month the figure was 88,000 and 16 pct of them got work within 10 days after appealing. 68 pct had been unemployed. The service now has 347,000 vacancies: 85 pct for industrial and 15 pct for office workers. However, two thirds of the applicants have a secondary specialized or higher education. Since the beginning of the year the service offered training to 115,000 people in construction, farming, banking as well as in business. 46 pct people were retrained, 18 pct upgraded their skills and 36 pct were given initial training. Last month 70,000 people were given a chance to acquire a new trade. The Federal Employment Service has registered 1,219,000 jobless and 1,483,000 persons entering the labor market.
