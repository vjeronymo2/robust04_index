Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Baku (February 23) TURAN--Yesterday Georgian Premier, Otar Patsatsia met with Azeri Premier, Suret Guseynov in Baku. During their meeting Georgian Premier made a request to Azerbaijan to assist to remove energy crisis in Georgia, reports press-service of Azerbaijani Cabinet. Otar Patsatsia mentioned that last week Georgian Foreign Minister, Alexander Chikvaidze and Iranian leadership concluded the agreement concerning delivery of Iranian gas to Georgia. Georgian Premier wanted Azerbaijan to give a consent to transport gas through its territory. Azeri Prime Minister Guseynov said that the meeting between President Aliyev and President Shevardnadze is due to take place in the nearest future. And presently Azerbaijan works out the package of documents stipulating establishment of Azerbaijani-Georgian cooperation, including providing energy assistance to Georgia. Azeri Premier mentioned that Azerbaijan will deliver energy carriers to Georgia if it guarantees that they not be further sent to Armenia. Georgian Premier Patsatsia agreed to Azerbaijan's control over energy carriers transportation. Suret Guseynov drew attention of Georgian Premier at the situation of Azeri minority in Georgia and asked the Georgian leadership to take measures to secure their civil rights. [passage omitted]
