Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, March 5. (SNARK) -- Situation is still disquiet in the south-eastern part of Karabakh conflict zone. As SNARK was told today in the press-service bureau of Karabakh Defense Army (KDA), in the developed combats Karabakh forces were able to reject all attacks of Azeri troops in Fizulinskiy direction and caused the enemy to retreat to initial positions. Towards morning of March 5, by the same source, combat actions in that sector bore positional character. At the same time, told journalists today the KDA staff operative division chief Aleksandr Mamunts, KDA command regularly informed Russian defense minister of all cease-fire violations by Azerbaijani side, since the truce agreement was reached in mediation of Russian military establishment. Mamunts said that in the evening of Feb. 28, KDA command has ordered its troops to cease all military activity since zero time of March 1.
