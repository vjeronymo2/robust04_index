BFN [From the "990 Morning News" program] [Text] Yesterday, the municipal intermediate court harshly punished three persons who had committed serious violent crimes of murder and robbery. In accordance with the municipal high court's death-sentence order, murderer (Yu Yucai) was dragged to the execution ground for execution. The municipal intermediate court also handed down death sentences on (Zhang Qijiang) and (Lu Huaqing), who, armed with knives, robbed their victims.
