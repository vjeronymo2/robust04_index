Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by 'Uthman Mirghani: "Garang Movement To Hold Its First General Conference in Southern Sudan"] [Excerpt] London -- The Garang movement yesterday confirmed that it will hold its first national conference next Saturday in southern Sudan. Akol Akor told AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT from the movement's office in Nairobi that "the Sudanese People's Liberation Movement [SPLM] conference will be held under the chairmanship of Colonel John Garang, will discuss a number of important issues, and will be attended by 500-600 representatives of various sectors in the region." Akor also confirmed that the conference would be held in one of the areas under Garang's control in southern Sudan. He declined to say exactly where for security reasons. He said that conference would last several days and would discuss over 20 issues. [passage omitted]
