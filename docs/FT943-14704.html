940713 FT 13 JUL 94 / Observer: Pointer As the country hunkers down for round five in the Great British rail strike, a copy of the latest British Rail timetable has landed on Observer's desk. Passengers will not be pleased to find that the chairman of Railtrack is listed as Sir Robert Horton. Surely he's supposed to get his K after he's beaten the striking signal staff into submission, not before?
