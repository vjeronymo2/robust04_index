BFN [Excerpt] Magdeburg (DDP/ADN) -- The Republicans' list of candidates for election to the state parliament of Saxony-Anhalt has now been accepted for the 26 June elections. This follows the Saxony-Anhalt Electoral Committee today revizing its decision adopted last Wednesday [1 June], by one vote. Following detailed discussion, four of the seven members of the committee voted to recognize the list. Only the Christian Democratic Union stuck to its decision not to admit the Republicans. The Social Democratic Party and the Party of Democratic Socialists abstained and Alliance-90/Greens changed their decision. [passage omitted]
