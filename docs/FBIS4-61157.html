BFN [Text] Stepanakert, May 4 (SNARK)--After one-day relative calm, the military actions have restarted in the Karabakh conflict zone. The press centre the Karabakh Defence Army Staff told SNARK that on May 4 dawn Azerbaijani side, using the armoured hardware, tried to break through the Karabakh forces' positions at the eastern (Agdam) [parenthesis as received] sector of the front. According to the data received by this midday, the attacks of Azerbaijani troops were beaten off. The Azerbaijani side has considerable losses in man power. The Karabakh side has no losses. Meanwhile, according to the same source, the intensive accumulation of Azerbaijani troops and hardware is observed in the north of Mardakert region of Karabakh.
