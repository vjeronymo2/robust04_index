930814 FT 14 AUG 93 / International Company News: Refinancing for Reliance RELIANCE Group, the US insurer, has filed offerings of 25m new common shares and Dollars 650m of debt as part of a refinancing, AP-DJ reports. The proceeds will be used to repay the balance under the company's old credit facilities and all outstanding public debt. Reliance is also entering into a new credit facility as part of the plan. The company plans to offer 20m common shares in the US and 5m shares in international markets. Of the debt, Dollars 300m is to be senior notes and Dollars 350m senior subordinated debentures.
