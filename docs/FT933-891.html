930927 FT 27 SEP 93 / Cash for company cars 'falls short' MOST CASH alternatives to company cars being offered by employers fall 'far short' of the actual cost to employees of providing their own cars, according to a study undertaken by Wyatt, the remuneration consultancy and actuarial group. The study adds that 'few employees have a clear idea of how to calculate the true value of a company car in their personal circumstances'. Based on an annual survey of 260 companies operating sizeable car fleets, it concludes that these are two of the main reasons why the number of employees taking up cash offers is low. 1993 Survey of Company Cars and Flexible Benefits Packages, The Wyatt Company (UK), 21 Tothill Street, London SW1H 9LL. Pounds 75.
