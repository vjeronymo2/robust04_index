Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Guatemala, 12 Mar (ACAN-EFE) -- Government sources reported today that Guatemalan security forces have defused at least four explosive devices in the capital over the last few hours. The national Police stated experts found one bomb at the telegraph office. Another bomb was found on a bridge near the Olympic village in Guatemala City. Police found still another in a private office building, and one last device was in a city suburb. Last Sunday, alleged guerrillas blew up a bridge about 20 km outside the capital city along the highway toward the Atlantic coast. No organization or person has claimed responsibility for placing the devices, none of which exploded.
