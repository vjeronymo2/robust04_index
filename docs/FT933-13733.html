930717 FT 17 JUL 93 / UK Company News: MITIE strengthens cleaning operation MITIE Group is to strengthen its Scottish-based cleaning operation with the acquisition of Olscot, a cleaning company based in Glasgow and Edinburgh. The consideration of Pounds 2m is to be satisfied as to Pounds 1.75m in cash and by the issue of 110,000 new ordinary shares. Prior to completion, however, Olscot will declare a dividend of Pounds 900,000 to the vendor shareholders making a total amount of Pounds 2.9m to be received by them. Completion of the transaction is subject to MITIE's auditors being satisfied on or before July 30 that Olscot's projected profits for the year ended September 30 will be not less than Pounds 350,000.
