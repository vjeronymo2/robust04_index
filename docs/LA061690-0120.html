June 16, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

FOX'S TEEN SOAP OPERA 'TRIBES' WILL FADE OUT NEXT MONTH

"Tribes," a 3-month-old TV soap opera aimed at teen-agers and young adults, has been canceled by Fox Television Stations Productions. It will have its final broadcast July 13 on KTTV Channel 11.

The serial, also running on other Fox-owned stations, was developed with the intention of syndicating it nationally.
