Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by N. Patterton] [Excerpt] Pretoria March 11 SAPA -- Rietgat police station was attacked with a petrol-bomb on Friday [11 March] as violence continued in areas of Bophuhatswana north-west of Pretoria. Rietgat, however, is on South African territory between Mabopane and Hammanskraal. Gunfire had been exchanged with the South African police, residents said. Bophuthatswana areas north-west of Pretoria such as Winterveld, Mabopane and Garankuwa were torn by violence, looting and shooting on Thursday night and Friday morning, according to witnesses.
