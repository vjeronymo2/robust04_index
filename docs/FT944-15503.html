941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Shaftesbury buys Shaftesbury is expanding its property interests in London's West End to Carnaby Street. The company is paying Berkeley Hambro Pounds 9.75m cash for a block of 11 freehold shops and restaurants, with offices, at the corner of Carnaby Street and Beak Street in Soho. Covering a total of 47,000 sq ft the block currently produces an annual income of Pounds 785,000.
