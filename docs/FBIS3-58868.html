Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The Portuguese Government has banned -- except with special prior permission from the Bank of Portugal -- the movement of any funds in Portugal belonging to or controlled directly or indirectly by the Libyan Government, public authorities, or companies. This decision is contained in a Finance Ministry release published in the official state gazette. The release also bans the use of any other funds or financial resources in Portugal by the abovementioned bodies. These measures will remain in force as long as the UN Security Council's sanctions are in force.
