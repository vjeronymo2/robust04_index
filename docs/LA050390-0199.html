May 3, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

EURO-TECH: Fed up with your Sony? . . . Britain's Lord Cockfield, architect of the Common Market, says you can expect a wave of West European high-tech products in your home and office by the end of this century (D5). . . . Common Market nations are focusing on their high-tech industry, he says, meaning "they must get their hands on the next big market, which is the United States." Steve Emmons and Cristina Lee
