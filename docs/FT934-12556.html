931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Whitecroft sells office development Whitecroft, the building products, lighting and textiles company, has sold its 25,250 sq ft Eldon Square, Reading, office development, which is jointly owned by a financial institution. The property has been sold for Pounds 4.8m cash. After repayment of loans outstanding on the development, Whitecroft has received Pounds 950,000 cash. The company's share of the property was valued at Pounds 819,000 in the group balance sheet at March 31 1993. The proceeds will be used to reduce borrowings.
