921207 FT 07 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Gartmore Value revenue rises Net asset value per share fell over the six months to October 31 from 27.4p to 16.8p at Gartmore Value Investments. However, the figure per zero dividend preference share improved from 72.1p to 76.7p. Net revenue for the half year to the end of October was Pounds 779,000 (Pounds 754,000) giving earnings per share of 1.57p (1.52p). As already announced, an increased second interim of 0.9525p (0.925p) is being paid.
