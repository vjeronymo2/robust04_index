940602 FT 02 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Hartons acquisition talks terminated Shares of Hartons slipped 2p to 5p after the Sheffield-based plastics distributor said that discussions relating to a 'possible substantial acquisition' had been terminated. The group announced that the talks were under way in early March when it also released annual results for the 1993 year showing reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 4.52m on turnover from continuing operations of Pounds 47m.
