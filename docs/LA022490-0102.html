February 24, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Dana Niguel Bank: The Dana Point bank reported fourth-quarter net income of $16,000, contrasted with $198,000 for the quarter in 1988. Net income for 1989 was listed at $606,000, up 32% from the $459,000 for 1988. Total assets increased 61% to $69.9 million from $43.4 million.
