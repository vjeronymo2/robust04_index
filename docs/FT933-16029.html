930706 FT 06 JUL 93 / Observer: Quality control Does anyone know a high-level executive in a UK clearing bank named David who is married to someone called Nikki? If so could they contact Observer who is anxious to identify the source of the following anonymous extract from a bank's in-house magazine: 'From dawn on Monday to about 8pm Friday, we both know that I'll put 110 per cent into work, but Nikki also knows that at weekends it's the family time', explains David. 'You have to accept in a role like this that quantity of family time is going to suffer, so you just recontract on the basis of quality.'
