STATE_60 System name: Refugee Data Center Processing Records. Security classification: Unclassified. system location: Refugee Data Center, 200 Park Avenue South, Room 801, New York, NY 10003. (The Refugee Data Center is a project of the International Catholic Migration Commission (ICMC) authorized under a cooperative agreement with the Department of State.) categories of individuals covered by the system: Individuals who have been approved for admission to the United States under the U.S. refugee program.
