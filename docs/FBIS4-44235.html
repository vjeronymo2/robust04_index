BFN [Excerpt] A Turkish Cypriot parliamentary delegation left the island for Strasbourg this morning to attend the Council of Europe Parliamentary Assembly meetings. The delegation, which is led by Assembly Speaker Ayhan Halit Acarkan, is comprised of Hakki Atun, Democratic Party leader and prime minister; Ozker Ozgur, Republican Turkish Party leader and state minister and deputy prime minister; Dervis Eroglu, National Unity Party leader; and Mustafa Akinci, Communal Liberation Party leader. The parliamentary delegation will also meet with its Greek Cypriot counterpart in Strasbourg. [passage omitted]
