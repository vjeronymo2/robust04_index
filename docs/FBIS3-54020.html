Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 21 Jan (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak has sent a cable of condolences to Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad upon the death of his son Staff Major Basil al-Asad, who passed away today. The following is the text of the cable: Dear brother Your Excellency Hafiz al-Asad, president of the Syrian Arab Republic: With deep sorrow and extreme grief I have received the news of the death of your son [words indistinct]. While sympathizing with you over this tragic incident, I pray God Almighty to have mercy on the dear deceased, may he rest in Paradise, and grant you and all the members of your family patience and consolation. From God we came and to Him we return.
