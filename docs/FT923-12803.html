920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / World News in Brief: BR privatisation 'bureaucratic', chairman warns Rail privatisation could create Britain's biggest bureaucratic complexity since the introduction of rationing, Sir Bob Reid, BR chairman, warns. Sir Bob warns of possible difficulties arising from charging for the use of railway tracks. Under government plans, train services will be handed over to the private sector, but tracks will remain in the hands of a BR body, Railtrack. Other aspects of the white paper win a cautious welcome from Sir Bob. Franchising out the passenger services, he says, may well produce gains for passengers by unlocking entrepreneurial flair.
