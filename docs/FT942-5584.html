940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Glynwed shares up after AGM Glynwed International's shares closed up 7p at 355p yesterday after Mr Gareth Davies, chairman, told the annual meeting that the company had made a good start to the year. 'Group profits for the first four months of the year are well ahead of the same period last year,' Mr Davies said. The recovery in earnings and dividend cover, together with strong cash generation, were bringing nearer the time when the company would be able to resume dividend growth.
