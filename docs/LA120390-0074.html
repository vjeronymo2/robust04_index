December 3, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

A BIG DEAL: The Angels made the first splash of the winter baseball meetings with a six-player trade (C1) with the Toronto Blue Jays. They sent center fielder Devon White, above, pitcher Willie Fraser and a minor leaguer to be named later to Toronto for outfielder Junior Felix, infielder Luis Sojo and a minor leaguer to be named later. The two clubs had been talking since the World Series, but the deal was completed late Sunday. Jeffrey Perlman and Helene Elliott and Tom McQueeney
