930126 FT 26 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Templeton Emerging net asset value lifted 3.4% TEMPLETON Emerging Markets Investment Trust, the specialist fund investing in markets such as Brazil and the Philippines, increased its fully diluted net asset value by 3.4 per cent in the first half of its financial year. At October 31, its value per share was 214.23p, compared with 207.2p on April 30. The trust was launched in June 1989 and produced the fifth best share price performance of all investment trusts, according to Micropal, over the three years to January 1. The trust had investments in 157 companies operating in 13 markets at the end of October. It hopes to launch a savings scheme for small investors this year. In accordance with policy, the trust is not paying an interim dividend. The shares fell 1p to 222p yesterday.
