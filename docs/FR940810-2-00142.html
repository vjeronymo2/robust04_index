Internal Revenue Service (IRS) OMB Number: 1545&hyph;0935 Form Number: IRS Form 1120&hyph;FSC and Schedule P (Form 1120&hyph;FSC) Type of Review: Revision Title: U.S. Income Tax Return of a Foreign Sales Corporation (1120&hyph;FSC) and Transfer Price or Commission (Schedule P_Form 1120&hyph;FSC) Description: Form 1120&hyph;FSC is filed by foreign corporations that have elected to be foreign sales corporations (FSCs) or small FSCs. The FSC uses Form 1120&hyph;FSC to report income and expenses and to figure its tax liability. IRS uses Form 1120&hyph;FSC and Schedule P (Form 1120&hyph;FSC) to determine whether the FSC has correctly reported its income and expenses and figured its tax liability correctly.
