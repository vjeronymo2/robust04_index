Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In Amman, a diplomatic source has announced that the Lebanese police have arrested two persons suspected of killing Na'ib al-Ma'ayitah, the Jordanian counselor at the Jordanian Embassy in Beirut who was assassinated in 29 January in the Lebanese capital. The same source added that the two arrested persons are Palestinians who belong to a hard-line group which could be Fatah-the Revolutionary Council led by Abu-Nidal.
