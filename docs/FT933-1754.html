930922 FT 22 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Cadbury gets sweet in Argentina Cadbury Schweppes has taken a foothold in the South American confectionery market with the purchase of 80 per cent of Productos Stani, based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Founded in 1912, privately-owned Stani is stated to be that country's leading branded sugar confectionery maker and had sales of USDollars 83m (Pounds 55m) for the year ended September 1992 with net tangible assets at that date of Dollars 25m. The South American confectionery market is estimated at about 700,000 tonnes with Argentina producing 130,000. The consideration has not been disclosed.
