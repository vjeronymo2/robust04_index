920811 FT 11 AUG 92 / International Company News: Prima interest payments start PRIMA Inmobiliaria, a Spanish real estate company controlled by the Kuwait Investment Office (KIO), yesterday began making interest payments under a debt moratorium plan presented to creditors last week, Reuter reports from Madrid. Grupo Torras, KIO's 100 per cent owned industrial holding company which has a 27 per cent direct stake in Prima, said the most urgent payments were being made to banks and to construction companies. Prima held a 15-minute meeting on the plan with representatives of some 60 bank creditors on Friday.
