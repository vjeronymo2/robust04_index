Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, December 22 (KCNA) -- A spokesman of the Foreign Ministry of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea, answering a question put by KCNA about a recent DPRK-USA contact in New York, said today: There was a contact between the DPRK and the USA in New York on December 20 concerning the nuclear problem. At the contact, the DPRK side made a counter proposal to the proposal put forward by the U.S. side on December 10. The DPRK side's counterproposal has reasonable suggestions for smoothly solving the nuclear problem. The U.S. side said it would study the DPRK side's counterproposal and notify the DPRK of its position later. We will watch the attitude of the U.S. side.
