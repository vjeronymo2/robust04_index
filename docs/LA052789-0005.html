May 27, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

WHAT DOES LAKERS' RILEY HAVE TO DO TO WIN AWARD?

I do not know how the NBA coach of the year is selected. I do know that the fact that Pat Riley has never been selected is insulting to any honest fan of the sport.

After the May 14 victory over Seattle, I was sure I had watched not only a great team but an extraordinary coach at work.

More than anything, that victory said for me -- "In your face, Tom Heinsohn" -- and everybody else who refuses to give this team and this coach their due.

MICHAEL FREAS

Sherman Oaks
