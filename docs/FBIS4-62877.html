BFN [Text] The press service of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the republic of Georgia reports that on 18 May, a delegation of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Estonia led by the vice chancellor of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Estonia, Raul Malk, arrived in Georgia on a working visit. Estonian guests will meet leadership of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Georgia with the aim of discussing issues of wide spectrum concerning the development of Georgian-Estonian relations and the general situation in the Transcaucasus. The two sides will discuss the draft governmental agreement between Estonia and Georgia on economic cooperation and the process of reforms in both of the states.
