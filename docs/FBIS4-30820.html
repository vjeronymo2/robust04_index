BFN [Text] Goran Percevic, vice president of the Socialist Party of Serbia [SPS], in a statement for Belgrade Radio maintains that the sanctions against the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia [FRY] that were imposed two years ago had the goal of destroying Serbia economically and thwarting its policy of aid and solidarity with the struggle of the Serbian people in the republic of Srpska and the republic of Serbian Krajina. Two years later, we can say that Serbia and the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia have managed to withstand this terrible pressure -- that is, that the sanctions have not accomplished their goal -- said Percevic, and added that Serbia had had a dignified response to this attempt at economic destruction. I believe that this latest economic program that is being implemented in Serbia represents an economic response to the attempt at economic genocide over our country and people, said SPS Vice President Goran Percevic, in a statement for Radio Belgrade on the second anniversary of the sanctions.
