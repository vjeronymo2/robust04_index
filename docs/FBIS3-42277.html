Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Asyut, 6 Feb (MENA)--Police Brigadier General 'Umar Hasan Mustafa, assistant director to the northern Suhaj security division, was martyred in Asyut this evening. Unidentified gunmen opened fire at Brig. Gen. Mustafa as he was leaving his home near al-Jumhuriyah Street in Asyut. He was killed instantly. The gunmen managed to escape. Security forces cordoned off the area and instituted a search for the criminals. Asyut authorities are investigating the incident.
