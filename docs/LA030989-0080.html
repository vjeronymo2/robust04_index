March 9, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

DIGEST: COMPTON;

VOLUNTEER CREWS TO PAINT OUT GRAFFITI

Block club members, city employees, elected officials and other volunteers will mount an attack Saturday morning against graffiti in the city of Compton.

Cleanup crews will be divided into two shifts with the first one assembling at 7 a.m. at Gonzales, Leuders, Kelly and West parks. The second three-hour shift, which starts at 10:30 a.m., will gather at Gonzales Park. A pep rally will be held at 10 a.m. in Gonzales.
