941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / Bank credit agreed for milk offshoot Dairy Crest, the milk processing arm of the Milk Marketing Board, has negotiated a Pounds 60m three-year credit facility with its bankers to finance its working capital requirements after next week's deregulation of the milk market. The funds are being provided by Barclays and NatWest Markets. Dairy Crest's flotation has been delayed, creating the need for alternative finance.
