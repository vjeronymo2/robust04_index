931222 FT 22 DEC 93 / Pounds 100m telecoms deal confirmed Hutchison Microtel, UK mobile-phone subsidiary of Hong Kong conglomerate Hutchison Whampoa, yesterday confirmed a Pounds 100m contract with Nokia, the Finnish telecommunications equipment supplier. The contract confirms Microtel's determination to open its UK network next year. The network will compete with existing networks operated by Vodafone, Cellnet and Mercury One-2-One. Complaints over British Telecommunications services dropped to 27,000 in the year to the end of September, from 41,000 in the previous 12 months, industry watchdog Oftel said yesterday. Complaints about disputed accounts fell by more than half.
