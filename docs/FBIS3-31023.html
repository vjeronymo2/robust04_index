Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] At today's news conference by the Kosova Democratic Alliance, reporters were told that the meeting of president of the republic of Kosova Dr. Ibrahim Rugova with U.S. President Bill Clinton in Washington testifies that the world knows and accepts the self-identity of Kosova and that the Serbian attempts to present Kosova as its internal affair are futile. This news conference also greeted the NATO ultimatum on the withdrawal from Sarajevo of the heavy artillery of Serbian forces as a precondition to solving the question of Bosnia.
