920227 FT 27 FEB 92 / Kuwaitis celebrate first anniversary of liberation Thousands of jubilant Kuwaitis waved flags and cheered as aircraft from more than a dozen countries staged a flypast over Kuwait City yesterday to celebrate the first anniversary of the emirate's liberation from Iraq. One year after the Gulf war, prosperity has returned to Kuwait and there are few signs of destruction in the capital. The estimated bill for rebuilding the emirate has fallen from Dollars 100bn (Pounds 57bn) to Dollars 20bn. Of the Gulf war allies, the US is winning the battle for defence and oil industry contracts, while US exporters have increased their market share in Kuwait. US leads the charge, Page 4
