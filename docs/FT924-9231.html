921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / UK Company News: TDG pulls out of US with Willig sale Transport Development Group has completed its withdrawal from the US through the sale of Willig Freight Lines for Dollars 17.3m (Pounds 11.2m). It has been bought by a consortium of senior management led by Mr Eric Anderson, who was the principal shareholder in Willig when TDG originally purchased the company. The extraordinary provision of Pounds 15m made in 1991 for losses on TDG's withdrawal from the US covered the deficit on disposal of Willig.
