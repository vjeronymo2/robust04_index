April 26, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The NBA has filed a second lawsuit against the Oregon Lottery, alleging that its new scratch-off ticket game is based on the league's championship series.

The lawsuit, filed in U.S. District Court, claims the "Basketball Championship Pool" game infringes on the NBA's trademarks, creates confusion for its fans and unjustly enriches the Oregon Lottery.
