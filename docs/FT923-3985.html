920909 FT 09 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Somerset Trust net asset value ahead Somerset Trust, formerly the Children's Medical Charity Investment Trust, reported a net asset value of 86.2p per share as at June 30, up from 84.3p 12 months earlier and 82.6p at its December year-end. The trust currently holds the bulk of its funds in short-dated gilts but is seeking to expand its activities. Earnings per share for the six months to the end of June emerged at 2p.
