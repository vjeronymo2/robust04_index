920727 FT 27 JUL 92 / Polish PM seeks rule by decree The two-week old government of Polish Prime Minister Hanna Suchocka is to ask for powers to rule by decree to improve the efficiency of government work, the PAP news agency reported yesterday, AP reports from Warsaw. President Lech Walesa discussed the question with Mr Suchocka after religious ceremonies in the Baltic port of Gdansk. The decrees are expected to allow the government to bypass the lower house of parliament, which is faced with an overwhelming backlog of vital economic legislation.
