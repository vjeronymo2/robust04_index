Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] La Paz, 25 Feb (EFE) -- Bolivian Foreign Minister Antonio Aranibar today said that the violent death of Spanish citizen Manuel Ramon Puchol in Bolivia has nothing to do with the Bolivian Government or people. Aranibar told ANF [Fides News Agency] that his ministry will "investigate the real cause" of the death of Puchol, who died on 10 February in San Matias, Bolivia. The foreign minister said he spoke today with Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana "to whom I expressed our regrets and guaranteed the Bolivian Government's political will to clarify this murder." According to witnesses, Puchol, 33, died on 10 February in San Matias, after being beaten by a group of military men. According to a preliminary Bolivian police report he died as consequence of an intoxication by drugs. The police promised to continue investigating.
