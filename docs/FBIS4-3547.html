BFN [Text] Ashkhabad [Ashgabat], April 22 (XINHUA) -- China and Turkmenistan signed another three documents on bilateral cooperation today, the third or last day of Chinese Premier Li Peng's official visit to this Central Asian country. Premier Li Peng and President S. A. Niyazov attended the signing ceremony, which took place at the presidential palace. The documents signed today include an agreement on China providing loans to Turkmenistan, a protocol on regular consultations between the foreign ministries of the two countries and an exchange of notes on China presenting a batch of materials to Turkmenistan. Meanwhile, the delegation of Chinese entrepreneurs traveling with the premier has so far signed six letters of intent and two agreements with Turkmen businessmen. Under one of the agreements, the China National Construction Engineering Corporation and Turkmenistan's Ministry of Construction will set up a brick-making joint venture here to produce 30 million bricks annually. The other was signed between the China Council for the Promotion of International Trade and Turkmenistan's Chamber of Industry and Commerce for establishing cooperative ties.
