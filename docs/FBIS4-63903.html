BFN [Text] On Tuesday Russian President Boris Yeltsin transformed the control department of the president's administration into a control department of the Russian president. The president's press service announced that this had been done in order to strengthen the control functions exercised by the president in Russia and to ensure efficiency and coordination of such activities. In accordance with the provision on the control department, its main purpose is to exercise presidential control over implementation of the laws and presidential decrees and ordinances by the state federal bodies and bodies of the federation's constituent members on behalf of the president. The department will also exercise control over: implementation of presidential decrees by the executive bodies and officials; over the activities of the executive bodies, enterprises, establishments and organizations on implementation of social and economic policy; and over activities of Russian diplomatic missions, trade offices and other establishments abroad. The department will carry out examinations and consider appeals submitted to it by individuals, enterprises and organizations. The Control Department has the right to demand from the federal executive bodies, enterprises, establishments and organizations, regardless of the form of property, all information required for its examinations . If a violation is revealed during an investigation the department can submit its recommendations to the heads of the executive bodies for consideration within ten days.
