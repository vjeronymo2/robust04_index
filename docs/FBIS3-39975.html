Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over still of President Suleyman Demirel] [Text] It has been reported that Bosnia-Herzegovina has submitted a request to the United Nations asking that Turkish and Pakistani troops be sent to the country. President Suleyman Demirel spoke with Bosnia-Herzegovina President Alija Izetbegovic today by telephone. According to a statement issued by the Press Center of the President's Office, Izetbegovic said that his country submitted a request to the United Nations asking that Turkish and Pakistani troops be sent to his country. Izetbegovic also requested the participation of Turkey in all meetings concerning Bosnia-Herzegovina and thanked Demirel for Turkey's contribution to the recent developments. Izetbegovic also briefed Demirel on the situation in Sarajevo and other regions.
