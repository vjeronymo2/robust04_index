940907 FT 07 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Oriel tops Pounds 2m at midway Oriel Group, the insurance broker, consolidated its return to the black in the first quarter and reported a pre-tax profit of Pounds 2.03m for the six months to June 30. The outcome was achieved on income of Pounds 8.82m and compared with a deficit of Pounds 28,000 from income of Pounds 4.65m in the 1993 first half. Earnings per share came out at 5.7p (1.2p losses) and the interim dividend is maintained at 2p. Since the period end Oriel has completed a Pounds 4m rights issue.
