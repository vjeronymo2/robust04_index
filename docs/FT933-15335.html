930709 FT 09 JUL 93 / London may get EC drug test base THE government's campaign to have the European Medicines Evaluation Agency based in London received a boost yesterday when the Medicines Control Agency said a third of all new applications for drug licences in the European Community were being handled in the UK. Mrs Virginia Bottomley, health secretary, said the strengths of the UK medicines regulatory system - its scientific excellence, speedy and efficient licensing and commitment to protecting public health - were evident.
