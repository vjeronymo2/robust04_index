BFN [Text] Cairo, 19 Jun (MENA) -- The British Embassy in Cairo will hand over equipment valued at over one-half million [Egyptian] pounds to the Palestinian police tomorrow. Designed to be used in Gaza and Jericho, the equipment will be delivered to the Egyptian town of Rafah. A statement by the embassy today said that the equipment is part of the assistance program of 105 million dollars, about 250 million Egyptian pounds, which, during his visit to Gaza early this year, Foreign Secretary Douglas Hurd promised to the Palestinians over a three-year period. The statement said that Britain will also contribute 20 percent of the 50 million Sterling pounds, 250 million Egyptian pounds, earmarked by the European Union for development projects.
