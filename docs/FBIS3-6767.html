Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 21 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] on March 18 sent a message of greetings to the 29th Congress of the Communist Party of Austria. The message expresses the belief that the congress will mark an important occasion in the activities of the Communist Party of Austria to strengthen the party, oppose imperialist domination and subjugation and defend peace, socialism and socio-political and democratic rights of the working masses. It also expresses the conviction that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two parties will continue to grow stronger and develop in the future, and wishes the congress success in its work.
