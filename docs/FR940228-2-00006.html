Specifically, these orders cover BBs, CRBs, and SPBs from France, Germany, and Japan; BBs and CRBs from Italy, Sweden, and the U.K.; and BBs from Singapore and Thailand. On June 28, 1993, in accordance with 19 CFR 353.22(c)(1993), we initiated administrative reviews of those orders for the period May 1, 1992, through April 30, 1993 (58 FR 34563). The Department is now conducting these administrative reviews in accordance with section 751 of the Tariff Act of 1930, as amended (the Tariff Act).
