920827 FT 27 AUG 92 / UK Company News: United Uniform Services edges ahead Profits before tax at United Uniform Services edged ahead to Pounds 1.46m in the six months to June 30. The increase from last time's Pounds 1.42m was struck on sales of Pounds 26.3m (Pounds 24.6m) and after interest charges ahead from Pounds 158,000 to Pounds 170,000. The company, which supplies uniforms and corporate clothing through its US-based subsidiaries, traditionally achieves the bulk of its profits in the second half. Earnings per share emerged at 4.2p (3.9p) and the interim dividend is maintained at 1p.
