Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Byrkin] [Text] Moscow January 11 TASS -- After a relatively calm night, the Azeri National Army resumed offensive operations at many sections of the Karabakh front, sources at the Karabakh defence army headquarters' press center told ITAR-TASS. The fighting was especially fierce in the Karabakh Mardakert region on the line of Madagis-Akop Kamari settlements and on the border of the Agdam region. All the attacks were repulsed by 3 P.M. After a counter-offensive, Karabakh units seized a number of heights in the Mardakert region.
