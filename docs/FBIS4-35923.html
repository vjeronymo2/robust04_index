BFN [Text] The soldiers in the war of liberation against the British, and their brave young people in Radfan Province have sent the following cable to the chairman and members of the Presidential Council and the command of the Armed Forces in the Democratic Republic of Yemen [DRY]: We send you our warmest congratulations on the occasion of the declaration of the DRY, on behalf of our colleagues Labbuzah, Thabit Husayn al-Uqbi, and other strugglers in the war of liberation, and send you a suitable gift contained in what follows: A group of fighters in the war of liberation and their brave youths attacked the invading enemy forces as they arrived at one of the remote villages in Radfan Province. A fierce battle ensued, lasting from 1100 on 3 June until the dawn of the following day. The following losses were inflicted on the enemy: Three military vehicles destroyed, 60 men killed, and a number of others wounded. One of our colleagues was martyred, while six others were lightly wounded. In response, the enemy proceeded to shell villages indiscriminately, martyring a citizen and wounding a number of others. We promise our political leadership in the DRY that we shall deal with these new occupiers just as we dealt with the colonialists, and shall not give up an inch of the territory of the DRY. This is a promise from strong and loyal strugglers: We shall rout the attacking forces, and victory will be our ally, God willing.
