920306 FT 06 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Singapore Para shows rise Singapore Para Rubber Estates, the rubber and palm oil producer, reported an increase from Pounds 243,774 to Pounds 263,889 in pre-tax profits for the year to December 31 on turnover up from Pounds 514,882 to Pounds 586,449. The dividend is partially restored to 1.1p (1p) per 5p stock unit - it was 1.55p for 1989 - from earnings of 1.58p (1.62p).
