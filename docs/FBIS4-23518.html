BFN [Text] The Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] is preventing electoral registration teams from operating in two locations of Malema in Nampula Province. The MOZAMBIQUE INFORMATION AGENCY [AIM] reports that Renamo officials in (Leia) and (Nicuassula) claim they have not yet been instructed by their seniors in Maputo to allow the teams to register voters for the October elections. Aurelio das Neves, chairman of the National Elections Commission in Nampula Province, told AIM that if a solution is not reached locally, he could still contact the Renamo leadership in Maputo today for explanations.
