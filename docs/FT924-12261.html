921021 FT 21 OCT 92 / International Company News: Bloedel deficit cut to CDollars 16.3m MACMILLAN BLOEDEL, the Canadian forest products group 49 per cent held by Noranda, posted higher sales and reduced losses for the third quarter and first nine months. The third-quarter loss was CDollars 16.3m (USDollars 13m), or 15 cents per common share, against CDollars 29.5m, or 29 cents a share, a year earlier. Sales were CDollars 755m, up 13 per cent. The nine-month loss was CDollars 32.3m, or 35 cents per common share, against a loss of CDollars 37m, or 43 cents a share, a year earlier. Sales were CDollars 1.92bn, up 9 per cent.
