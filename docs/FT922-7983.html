920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Precondition met in Barlo bid Barlo Group, the Irish radiator manufacturer, announced yesterday that the precondition for its bid for IRG, a plastics packaging manufacturer, has been met. Irrevocable commitments to accept the offer have been received from shareholders holding a minimum of 15 per cent of IRG's issued equity capital. A formal bid for IRG is expected to be made shortly.
