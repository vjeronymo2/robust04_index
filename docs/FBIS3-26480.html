Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The provincial people's government this morning invited the provincial industry and communications department director, as well as all prefectural and city industry and communications bureaus chiefs from across the province, to a work meeting to analyze the industrial situation in January 1994 and to arrange for industrial and communications production in February 1994. Provincial Vice Governor Zhang Yunchuan presided over and delivered a speech at the meeting. The meeting revealed that the province's total industrial output value realized at and above the township level in January 1994 reached 5.3 billion yuan, up by 48.5 percent over the same period last year, thereby overfulfilling the planned quota by 300 million yuan. [passage omitted] The meeting called on industry and communications departments at all levels across the province to maintain sustained and fast-paced industrial growth in February 1994 by generating a total of 4.7 billion yuan of industrial output value, up by 13 percent over the corresponding period last year; raising daily industrial output value to 188 million yuan, up by 10 percent over January 1994; raising production-marketing ratio to and above 95 percent; and reducing overall losses by 10 percent. To this end, it is imperative to concentrate on the following work aspects: 1. To vigorously increase the production and marketing of products and to maintain sustained and fast-paced industrial growth; 2. To step up technological transformation and scale new heights of production; 3. To strengthen and improve enterprise management by strengthening overall quality control and increasing economic efficiency; 4. To guarantee electric power supplies during the Lunar New Year festival and to avert major mechanical problems; 5. To guarantee smooth railroad transportation during the Lunar New Year festival period, especially transportation of passengers, coal, petroleum, iron, and steel, as well as of essential goods and materials.
