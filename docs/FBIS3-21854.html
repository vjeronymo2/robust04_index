Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] India and Myanmar [Burma] have signed an agreement on checking drug smuggling and insurgency on the border. The six-point agreement was signed at the end of the three-day talks in Imphal yesterday by Mr. H.V. Goswamy, chief secretary of Manipur, and Mr. (Ohn Pyne), director-general Home Affairs, Myanmar. The talks were held as a follow-up of the memorandum of understanding signed by the Government of Myanmar and India in New Delhi in January for the maintenance of peace and tranquility along the border.
