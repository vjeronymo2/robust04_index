940124 FT 24 JAN 94 / International Company News: Zurich bank raises pay-out Bank J Vontobel, the Zurich private bank, reported net profits of SFr33.1m (Dollars 22.80m) for 1993, double the SFr15.9m earned in 1992, thanks to booming profits from securities and higher commissions. The bank, the principal subsidiary of quoted Vontobel Holding, proposes to raise its ordinary dividend by 20 per cent to SFr18m and pay an extraordinary SFr12m dividend as a result of the excellent performance.
