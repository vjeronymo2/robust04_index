931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Beazer unit could raise Dollars 245m for Hanson Hanson, the Anglo-US industrial group, could raise up to Dollars 245m gross from the flotation of Beazer Homes USA, its US house building subsidiary, according to the prospectus filed yesterday with the US Securities and Exchange Commission in Washington. The group plans to offer 6m shares in Beazer Homes USA at between Dollars 17.50 and Dollars 20 a share and Dollars 125m of senior notes due 2004. It had announced with its year-end results last week its intention to float off its Beazer subsidiaries in the US and UK. Sale of the US and UK businesses, acquired in 1991, could raise more than Pounds 400m. Hanson's activities include gold mining, chemicals, material handling, tobacco, forest products and lumber.
