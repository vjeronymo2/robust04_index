BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 23 -- Vietnamese President Le Duc Anh today extended his sympathy to Chinese President Jiang Zemin over human and material [words indistinct] on the recent floods to the people in the Provinces of Guangdong, Guangxi and Hunan. On this occasion, the Vietnam-China Friendship Association also sent a message of sympathy to the China-Vietnam Friendship Society.
