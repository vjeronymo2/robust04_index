BFN [From the "First Thing" program] [Text] Julian Rizo Alvarez, Politburo member and first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba in Camaguey, has called on provincial lawyers to support the Unified Vigilance and Protection System. Rizo made the call at a meeting with lawyers, during which it was stressed that the goal of that united effort is to live under the rule of law and to uphold the workers' peace of mind. Rizo said: I ask Camaguey lawyers to honor the virtuous legacy of the nation's founding fathers and the glory gained from loving and defending the fatherland with passion and valor. The meeting included a briefing on the outstanding and decisive performance of these legal workers in detecting and processing cases of illegal purchases and sales of homes and vehicles in accordance with Decree-Law 149.
