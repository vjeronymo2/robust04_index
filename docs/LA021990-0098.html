February 19, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

WASHINGTON'S BIRTHDAY

Today is Washington's Birthday. The following will be closed in observance of the holiday:

* All county, state, and federal offices.

* All public elementary, junior and high schools, and all colleges and universities, including community colleges.

* County, state and federal courthouses.

* County libraries.

* All city offices, with the exception of Westminster.

* All banks and savings and loans.

Mail: All post offices will be closed and there will be no mail delivery. Mail will be collected from mailboxes according to the posted holiday schedule.

Trash: There will be regular trash pickup by city and county refuse workers.

Buses: Orange County Transit District buses will run on regular schedule. There will be dial-a-ride and car-pool services available on the holiday.

Parks: All city- and county-run parks and recreation facilities will be open during regular hours.
