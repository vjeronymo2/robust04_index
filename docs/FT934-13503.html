931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Save & Prosper Linked cuts first dividend Save & Prosper Linked Investment Trust has declared a first interim payment of 19.6p per income share for the three months to October 7, against 30.25p. Net revenue for the three months was Pounds 995,000 (Pounds 1.59m) resulting in a loss after the dividend payment of Pounds 33,800 (Pounds 319 profit).
