BFN [Text] Over the past 24 hours 131 rockets were fired into residential areas of Kabul. According to a report of the Kabul garrison command, the rockets were fired into the city from the directions of Balahesar and Maranjan Hill. As a result four defenseless residents of the city were martyred and 10 others were injured. According to another report, positions of the Islamic army were attacked yesterday evening west of Kabul. A security source considered the internecine clashes of Hezb-e Wahdat over the appointment of the future party leader as the reason for the attack. The report adds that the adventurist section of Hezb-e Wahdat wants to hide its internal problems from the public by fanning conflict between the Islamic Army and Hezb-e Wahdat. However, Islamic Army units have been emphatically instructed to exercise forbearance before such provocations.
