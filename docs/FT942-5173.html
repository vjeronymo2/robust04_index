940606 FT 06 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Radstone beats forecast with Pounds 1.45m In its first set of results since flotation in February, Radstone Technology, the supplier of open architecture computer subsystems to the defence and industrial sectors, beat a forecast of Pounds 1.35m for the year ended March 31 with pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.45m. This compared with Pounds 1.87m for the previous year and was achieved from turnover up from Pounds 25.9m to Pounds 28.3m. Basic earnings per share amounted to 5.7p, against 11.75p. As stated in the prospectus, the first dividend payment will be an interim distribution for the current year.
