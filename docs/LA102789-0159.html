October 27, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

RANCHO SANTIAGO WILL TRY TO RECOVER AGAINST GOLDEN WEST

Rancho Santiago College is hoping to rebound and Golden West is looking for an upset in a Mission Conference Central Division football game at 7 tonight at Orange Coast College.

Rancho Santiago opened the season with five victories but was defeated by Riverside, 34-24, last Saturday. The Dons are 5-1 overall, 4-1 in conference play and 0-1 in the division.

The Dons were ranked sixth in the national J.C. Grid-Wire poll before the loss, but now are 19th.

Golden West (3-2, 2-2, 0-1) is going for its third Friday night victory of the season. Games are normally played on Saturday, but due to scheduling conflicts created because Golden West shares a field with Orange Coast, the Rustlers already have played -- and won -- twice on Friday this season.
