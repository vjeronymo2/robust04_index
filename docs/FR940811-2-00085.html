Proposal: Performance Funding System Data Collection Forms. Office: Public and Indian Housing. Description Of The Need For The Information And Its Proposed Use: These forms are used by Public Housing Agencies (PHAs) and Indian Housing Authorities (IHAs) to calculate the annual operation subsidy eligibility under the Performance Funding System.
