931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / International Company News: Samsung to cut stake in Kia THE SAMSUNG group yesterday agreed to reduce its shareholding in Kia Motors, ending its possible attempt to take over South Korea's second largest vehicle manufacturer, writes John Burton in Seoul. The decision by Samsung, one of the country's leading conglomerates, was taken after Kia said it would resist any takeover bid and state officials warned that the government might intervene in support of Kia.
