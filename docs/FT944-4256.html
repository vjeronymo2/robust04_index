941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / Insurance staff 'to get 23% pay rise' The MSF technical union yesterday said more than 1,000 insurance workers will be given a pay rise of up to 23 per cent in the new year. It said the Britannia Life staff in Glasgow would receive a 10 per cent bonus and wage increases of between 4 per cent and 13 per cent. The union also said it had negotiated a deal giving part-timers the same terms as full-time staff.
