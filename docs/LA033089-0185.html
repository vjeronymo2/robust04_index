March 30, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

NEWSWIRE

The Rams lost one free agent -- special teams player Tim Tyrrell to the Buffalo Bills -- and signed five others.

The Rams signed former New York Giants defensive tackle Henry Brown, a 6-foot-3, 265-pounder signed by the Giants as a free agent before the 1988 season. He spent the entire season on injured reserve with a groin injury.

Also signed were quarterback Christopher Parker of Cal State Northridge, wide receiver Thomas Henley of Stanford, linebacker Andre Lloyd of Jackson State and linebacker Thor Salonoa of Hawaii.
