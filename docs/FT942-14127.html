940421 FT 21 APR 94 / UK Company News: M&W M&W has exchanged contracts for the purchase of six convenience stores from Kings Quality Food Stores for Pounds 900,000 plus stock at valuation, payable in cash on completion. Also, Beeson Gregory, on behalf of M&W, has placed with institutional investors 560,000 ordinary 10p shares at 150p to raise Pounds 840,000.
