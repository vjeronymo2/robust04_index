940421 FT 21 APR 94 / International Company News: Record term for Dean Witter Dean Witter Discover, the US brokerage firm spun off as an independent company last year by the retailer Sears, Roebuck, reported record first-quarter earnings of Dollars 209.5m, or Dollars 1.23 a share. That was an increase of 54 per cent on last year's first-quarter earnings of Dollars 136.4m, or per share income, on a pro-forma basis, of 80 cents. First-quarter operating revenues were Dollars 1.27bn, up 17 per cent from Dollars 1.08bn. Dean Witter's Discover Card credit card operations outperformed its securities business. The credit card division saw operating revenues rise 33 per cent to Dollars 542.4m, from Dollars 408.7m in last year's first quarter. Net operating revenues for Dean Witter's securities business edged up 8 per cent in the first quarter, to Dollars 727.7m, from Dollars 672.9m a year ago. The company said that the assets it managed in its securities business grew 14 per cent in the quarter, to Dollars 70.9bn, and that managed loans increased 15 per cent, to Dollars 20.5bn. The company added 2.4m new Discover Card accounts in the quarter, bringing the total to 29.6m. In its securities business, Dean Witter added 536 account executives in the first quarter, bringing its total sales force to 7,701 brokers.
