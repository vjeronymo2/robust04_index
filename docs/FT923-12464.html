920718 FT 18 JUL 92 / Markets: At a glance - Power shares reminder Shareholders of regional electricity companies have until next Friday July 24 to sell their Number 4 certificates if they want to avoid the final call for payments for the shares, according to the Share Centre. Making the final call, of 70p per share, is a complicated process which will affect more than 3m shareholders over the next two months. It is not payable until September 15. Meanwhile new Number 5 certificates will be sent to shareholders on August 18, and they will have until September 4 to avoid paying the call.
