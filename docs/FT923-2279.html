920918 FT 18 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Unilever sells salmon producer Unilever, the Anglo-Dutch food and consumer products group, said yesterday that it has found a US buyer for its Marine Harvest International subsidiary, Scotland's largest salmon producer accounting for nearly a quarter of total production. MariFarms, an aquaculture company based in Woodbridge, New Jersey, will pay Dollars 39m (Pounds 22m) cash for Marine. The sale will result in the loss of 160 jobs.
