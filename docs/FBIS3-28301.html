Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] Japan-Russia meeting on defense policy and planning, which was held at the Foreign Ministry for two days, ended on 4 February. It was attended by officials in charge of defense and foreign policy affairs from the two countries. At the meeting, the Japanese side pointed out that Russian forces in the Far East have become an unstable factor to the security of northeast Asian nations. In response, the Russian side stressed that Russia has continued its effort to promote disarmament and reduce its forces. At the meeting, the Russian side said that Russia hopes to join the Asia Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) forum. Shunji Yanai, director general of the Foreign Policy Bureau in the Foreign Ministry, attended the meeting and Agayev, chief of the Analysis and Estimate Department in the Russian Foreign Ministry, and other Russian officials attended the meeting.
