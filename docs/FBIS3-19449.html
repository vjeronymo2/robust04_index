Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara -- It is being reported that Jalal Talabani, leader of the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan [PUK], went to Damascus last week to take part in the 40th day memorial services for Hafiz al-Asad's late son Basil al-Asad. It is being reported that Talabani, who reportedly returned to southern [Iraqi] Kurdistan yesterday, held a meeting with PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] leader Ocalan before his return. It is being said that the foreign ministries of Turkey, United States, and Britain were aware of Talabani's visit. Sources close to Poised Hammer who are carefully following Talabani's visit claimed that Ocalan is planning to announce a cease-fire in the next six to nine months.
