940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Atreus at Pounds 418,000 A second half loss of Pounds 133,000 at Atreus, the shower screen and mirror supplier, left the company which came to the market last year, with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 418,404 for the 12 months to March 31. Turnover was Pounds 6.7m. As forecast at the time of the listing, the dividend is 0.75p with a recommended final of 0.486p. However, the company does not expect to pay an interim for 1994. Earnings per share came to 0.67p.
