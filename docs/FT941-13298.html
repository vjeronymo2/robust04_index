940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Clayhithe cuts loss to Pounds 0.2m The improved second half performance achieved by Clayhithe last year continued into the six months to September 30 with pre-tax losses for this finance and management provider dropping from Pounds 1.09m to Pounds 210,000. Turnover dipped to Pounds 16.3m against Pounds 16.5m. Operating profits were Pounds 162,000 (losses Pounds 580,000). Losses per share were 3.3p basic compared with 9.6p, and 1.1p (4.7p) fully diluted. The interim dividend is maintained at 0.75p.
