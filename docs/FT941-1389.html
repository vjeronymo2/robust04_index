940325 FT 25 MAR 94 / Technology (Worth Watching): A step ahead of the customers A neural network, artificial intelligence software which 'learns' how to analyse data from past examples, is helping retail chain Radio Rentals to retain customers. In trials, the neural network, from Thorn EMI's Central Research Laboratories, has made Radio Rentals 30 per cent more accurate in predicting which customers will terminate rental contracts. This helps the company target its marketing more effectively. CRL: UK, 081 848 9779.
