940222 FT 22 FEB 94 / International Company News: Quaker Oats sees strong second half Quaker Oats, the US foods group, predicted that its second-half earnings per share would be 'up strongly' on good volume growth in the US, AP-DJ reports. 'We expect margins in the US to continue improving, to 13 per cent for the year, while our international margins will be flat,' said Mr Philip Marineau, president and chief operating officer. Mr Marineau said that, despite disappointing fiscal first-quarter earnings, the company was on track to 'achieve our target of 7 per cent real earnings growth for fiscal 1994'.
