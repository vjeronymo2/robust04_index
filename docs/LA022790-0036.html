February 27, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Dov Alexander Scarf, a 38-year-old Israeli conductor, will lead the Capistrano Valley Symphony Sunday at 2 p.m. at the Ritz-Carlton in Dana Point, replacing founding music director Donn Laurence Mills, who died earlier this month.

The program will be announced. The concert is free, but tickets are required. Information: (714) 240-8584.
