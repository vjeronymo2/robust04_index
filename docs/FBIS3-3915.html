Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 23 (XINHUA) -- Chinese President Jiang Zemin said here today that Microsoft Corporation of the United States is welcome to further strengthen mutually beneficial relations of cooperation with China. Jiang made the remark at a meeting with President William Gates of this leading U.S. software manufacturer. He told the visitor that China attaches importance to the development of the information industry. A program is being carried out to build a series of major projects to computerize the main economic indexes, Jiang said. Gates said that his three-day visit has helped him know more about China, and this is useful for Microsoft to expand its cooperation with its Chinese counterpart. Microsoft has a program to increase exchanges with China, including training Chinese software personnel, he said.
