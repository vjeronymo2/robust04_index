BFN [Text] A delegation of the Propaganda and Training Board of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee [LPRP CC], headed by Osakan Thammatheva, member of LPRP CC and acting chief of the LPRP CC Propaganda and Training Board, left Vientiane at noon yesterday to pay an official visit to the PRC. The visit to the PRC by the delegation is aimed at meeting and exchanging experience on ideological work with Chinese counterparts and at strengthening the traditional solidarity between the two parties, states, and peoples of Laos and China. Our delegation will stay in Beijing and Kunming for one week.
