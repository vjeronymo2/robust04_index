941001 FT 01 OCT 94 / 200 Scottish jobs to be created Solectron, the US electronics company, is to invest Pounds 10m in expanding its printed circuit board assembly plant at Dunfermline, Fife. The expansion by the California-based company will create 200 jobs by the end of next year, bringing the total number of workers to 1,000. A new 68,000 sq ft manufacturing unit will be built. Solectron bought the Dunfermline plant from Philips of the Netherlands in October 1993.
