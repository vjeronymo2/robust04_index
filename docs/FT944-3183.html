941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / International Company News: BHP/Dia-Met expand Broken Hill Proprietary, the Australian resources group, and its Canadian partner, Dia-Met Minerals said yesterday that they were expanding the scope of their proposed diamond mine in Canada's Northwest Territories, writes Nikki Tait. The companies said that an updated project development plan, submitted to the Canadian government, had been enlarged to five diamondiferous kimberlite pipes, instead of the three previously suggested.
