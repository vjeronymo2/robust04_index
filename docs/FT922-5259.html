920601 FT 01 JUN 92 / MP hopeful on BCCI disclosures MR KEITH VAZ, Labour MP for Leicester East, said last night after visiting Abu Dhabi that some former senior officials with Bank of Credit and Commerce International had shown willingness to co-operate with Serious Fraud Office investigations into the affairs of the collapsed bank. 'Some of these officials are prepared to come forward and clear their name,' Mr Vaz said. 'There also appears to be a lot more money left in the bank than we at first thought.'
