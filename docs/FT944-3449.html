941213 FT 13 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Hamlet ahead Hamlet Group, the clothing distribution company which came to the market in October 1993, raised pre-tax profits for the half year to September 30 from Pounds 2m to Pounds 2.32m. The increase was struck on turnover ahead by 13 per cent at Pounds 34.6m, compared with Pounds 30.5m. Mr Malcolm Dagul, chairman, said the figures represented expansion in both domestic and European markets, and incorporated the results of Hong Kong-based Citi-March for the first time. Earning per share were ahead to 4.44p (4.41p). The maiden interim dividend is 2.1p.
