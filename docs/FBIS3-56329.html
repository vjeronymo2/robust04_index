Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] A goodwill gathering was held at the DPRK Embassy in Moscow on 12 January. Members of the Central Committee of the Russia-DPRK Friendship Association, Korean diplomats, and reporters were present at the gathering. (Polshchekov), chairman of the Russian Association of Veterans of the Korean Liberation War, summed up the association's work for last year and explained the schedule of main events for 1994. A work plan for the association was approved. The Russian Cultural Association for Friendship With the DPRK is a self-supporting social coalition established on the basis of the Russian people's voluntary intention for humanism, democracy, progress, and friendship and cultural cooperation with the DPRK. The association receives no outside financial assistance and faithful friends of the DPRK and enthusiasts support it. The DPRK ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to Russia spoke at the gathering. He mentioned the decisions made in the 21st plenary session of the Sixth Central Committee of the Workers Party of Korea and introduced important parts of President Kim Il-song's New Year's address. The participants at the gathering adopted a statement supporting the DPRK people's effort to achieve reunification of their fatherland in accordance with the DPRK's peace-loving policy and in order to establish the democratic confederal Republic of Koryo.
