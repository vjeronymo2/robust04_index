930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / Labour attacks brochure costs THE Department for Education has spent more than Pounds 500,000 on 'nearly 6m booklets and glossy folders on opting out', according to answers to parliamentary questions by Mrs Ann Taylor, Labour's education spokeswoman. Mrs Taylor attacked the government for hypocrisy, as this revelation followed an amendment to the Education Bill yesterday which limits the amount local education authorities may spend 'for the purpose of influencing the outcome of ballots'.
