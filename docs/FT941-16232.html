940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / International Company News: Albatronics plans HK listing Albatronics (Far East) Co said it was seeking a listing in Hong Kong to raise HKDollars 89m (USDollars 11m) through the issue of 50m shares, or 25 per cent of the company's enlarged share capital, Reuter reports from Hong Kong. The shares in the electronic components maker are being offered at HKDollars 1.78 each, a fully diluted prospective price/earnings multiple of 8.9 times, it said. The funds will be used to build new plants in China. 'We plan to invest some HKDollars 40m more in the coming few years in China for setting up new joint venture plants,' said managing director Mr Fukumori Nakahara.
