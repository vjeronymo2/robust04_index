921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / Observer: Reticent One Australian who can't be accused of pommie-bashing is Down Under's treasurer John Dawkins, in London for a meeting with Norman Lamont. Despite attempts beforehand to lure Dawkins into discussion of Britain's withdrawal from the exchange rate mechanism, he refused to be drawn. All he'd say was that he was 'a bit surprised there was no Plan B'.
