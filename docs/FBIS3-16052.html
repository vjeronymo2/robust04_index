Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Stepanakert, March 12 (SNARK) -- Today the Press-Service of the Karabakh Foreign Department spread the declaration which says that the Azerbaijani mass media issued reports which cite statements First Deputy of the Russian Foreign Affairs Minister Anatoliy Adamishin did at his meeting with Rasul Kuliyev, speaker of the Azerbaijani Milli Majlis, which taken place in Baku March 7. Mr. Adamishin as if [i.e. allegedly] said Russia speaks for territorial integrity of Azerbaijan and autonomy for Nagorno-Karabakh. Such statement do not contribute to the solution of the conflict because [they] determine the status of Nagorno-Karabakh beforehand. The status of Nagorno-Karabakh could be determined only as a result of the negotiations following all the norms of international law. The declaration also says the Republic of Nagorno-Karabakh hopes Foreign Affairs Department of Russia will give an estimation to such attempts of disinformation, torpedoing the process of negotiations with the Russian intermediary role.
