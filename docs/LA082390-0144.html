August 23, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BEAN NAMED FINANCE OFFICER FOR LIVE ENTERTAINMENT INC.

Theodore E. Bean has been named executive vice president and chief financial officer of Live Entertainment Inc., a Van Nuys video and recorded music distributor and retailer. He will succeed Roger R. Smith, who is resigning to take a position with Carolco Pictures, which owns approximately 48% of Live.

Bean, 48, is chief financial officer at Pace Membership Warehouse. Live's chairman, Wayne H. Patterson, who joined the company in April, was formerly chairman of Pace.

Before Pace, Bean was a partner in the Denver office of Deloitte & Touche and headed the accounting firm's retail and emerging companies practices.

Patterson said Bean would join Live next month and would work with Smith during a transition.

Live also said it named Michael J. White to the newly created position of senior vice president and general counsel. White is Pace's general counsel. PATRICE APODACA
