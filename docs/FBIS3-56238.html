Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Kharkov, 14 Jan -- The interpartite electoral association Spravedlyvist [Justice], uniting the Democratic Party, Republican Party, ChDPU [Christian Democratic Party] and Social Democratic Party in the general elections, has nominated its candidates for people's deputies. [passage omitted] The Liberal Democratic Party of Ukraine [LDPU] has announced its secession from Spravedlyvist. Explaining this decision, party Chairwoman Yevheniya Novikova said that certain changes to the association's pre-election platform were introduced without consulting the LDPU. In particular, the provision on the possibility of a federal structure of Ukraine in the future was struck out.
