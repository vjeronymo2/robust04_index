930113 FT 13 JAN 93 / Motorcycle sales rise by 35.5% MOTORCYCLE sales last December were 35.5 per cent higher than the figure for December 1991 at 1,970 bikes, the Motor Cycle Industry Association said. Total motorcycle sales for all of last year were 43,992. That was down on the 1991 figure of 51,781, but the association said the rate of decline had slowed.
