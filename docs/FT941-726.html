940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / Management (The Growing Business): Directory deadline approaches - In a Nutshell Companies have until the end of April to advertise products and services, and for potential partners, in the 12th edition of Europages, the 1400-page, multi-language business directory. The directory contains analysis of industrial sectors and market trends in 19 countries in western Europe, Scandinavia and eastern Europe. Europages says the directory, available on CD ROM, reaches 600,000 companies. Europages - 081 879 3033
