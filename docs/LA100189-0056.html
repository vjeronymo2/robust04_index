October 1, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

JUST FOLLOWING THE LEADER

In the Sept. 24 View letters, Fred Nathan suggested the possibility of an Ollie North presidency. Besides the fact that North is a lying, amoral, soldier-of-fortune-mentality wacko, he is also a convicted felon, which means that he cannot run for public office. The latter reason you should have mentioned.

PAUL MILLS, Van Nuys
