Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] The Greek Government, which holds the European Union [EU] presidency, condemns the massacre of Palestinian civilians in Hebron. Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos described the massacre as an abhorrent action and expressed the Government's grave concern over the repercussions which it might have in the course of the Arab-Israeli peace talks. The Government spokesman also hoped that the efforts for peace in the Middle East continue and stressed that the Greek Government's statement is made in its capacity as EU president.
