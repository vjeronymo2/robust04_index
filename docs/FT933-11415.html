930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / International Company News: Scott Paper Scott Paper of the US is to pay Dollars 3m to settle a class action lawsuit by shareholders, Reuter reports. Scott said its insurer would also pay the shareholders Dollars 5m. In a lawsuit filed in September 1990, the shareholders alleged the company did not disclose in a timely manner certain material facts about 1990 earnings. Scott said lawyers for the class had conceded that no deliberate or intentional wrongdoing was involved.
