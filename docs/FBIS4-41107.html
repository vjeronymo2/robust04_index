BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondent Anatoliy Yurkin] [Text] Mirny, Arkhangelsk Region, June 22 TASS -- During command post exercises conducted under the supervision of the Russian defence minister with the participation of President Boris Yeltsin, supreme commander-in-chief of the Russian Armed Forces, missile regiments' crews launched a Topol (Poplar) missile on Wednesday from the Russian Strategic Missile Forces' state test site at Mirny to a target area on the Kamchatka peninsula. The crews displyed high standards of training and skill and successfully accomplished the task that was set to them. The missile hit the target.
