931020 FT 20 OCT 93 / Sizewell C 'would give Pounds 285m boost' BUILDING the Sizewell C nuclear power station would give a Pounds 285m boost to the regional economy, Nuclear Electric, the state-owned generator, said yesterday. It has applied for permission to build a Pounds 3.5bn twin-reactor at the Suffolk site where Britain's first pressurised water reactor plant is nearing completion.
