BFN [Text] The Information Ministry has issued the following statement: His Majesty King Hassan II, God make him victorious, received Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres at the royal court in Rabat on the evening of 2 June 1994. During the meeting, Peres briefed the king on the stages of the peace process in the Middle East and his perceptions of the future and development of this process. On his part, the king reaffirmed to his guest Morocco's continued efforts to establish a just peace in the Middle East region. Finally, his majesty the king confirmed to Peres that he has accepted an invitation to chair the international economic summit on the development of Middle Eastern countries which is scheduled to be held in Marrakech at the beginning of November.
