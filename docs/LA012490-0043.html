January 24, 1990, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

CAR HITS, KILLS PEDESTRIAN, 69

A 69-year-old San Diego man was hit by a car and killed crossing a street Tuesday evening, police said.

Rodney Smith was crossing West Mission Bay Drive when he was struck by a westbound car at 5:39 p.m., said Sgt. Jim Clear of the San Diego Police Department. Investigators believe Smith "was at fault -- that he walked into the path of the vehicle," Clear said.

Smith was taken to UC San Diego Medical Center, where he was pronounced dead at 6:16 p.m., said San Diego Deputy Coroner David Lodge. Smith, a resident of the 3600 block of Bayside Way, apparently was taking his customary evening walk, Lodge said.

Police are investigating.
