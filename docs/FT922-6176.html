920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / Construction Contracts: Export orders won by ML Aviation ML AVIATION, a subsidiary of ML Holdings, has recently been awarded an order for defence products. This brings the total won since October to Pounds 16m. Over 80 per cent of the orders are for export customers and were won against international competition. ML Aviation, which incorporates ML Wallop Defence Systems, is a UK manufacturer of infra-red decoys, aircraft weapon carriage and release equipment, and naval decoy systems. The orders included in the list are: over Pounds 4m of airborne infra-red decoys for the Far East and Europe; over Pounds 4m of aircraft bomb racks and missile launchers from McDonnell Douglas for Harrier 2 AV8B for the US Marine Corps and from BAe for Hawk for various export customers; and Pounds 3.5m of naval decoy systems and ammunition destined for Asia and the Middle East. The company is also to supply Pounds 1m of Special Forces equipment for the United Kingdom's Ministry of Defence and the Gulf area.
