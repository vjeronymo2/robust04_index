January 30, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Into the Night With Rick Dees: While still being heard in the early mornings, KIIS-FM morning man Rick Dees will soon be seen on late-night television in an ABC comedy show premiering in July.

"Into the Night" which will air Monday through Friday following "Nightline" from midnight to 1, has been described as a "comedy hour of unpredictable events, supported by a family of regular comedy players and guest stars." Dees, who has been at KIIS since 1982, has had one of the top-rated morning shows for the past six years. He also hosts the syndicated "The Weekly Top 40," which airs on more than 300 radio stations. CLAUDIA PUIG
