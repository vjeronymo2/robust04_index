July 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE RED ROOM

At the risk of appearing ignorant, I have to know if "The Red Room," by Paul Paree (May 21), is a true story. The story starts out like a bit of fiction about paint or decorating, then becomes quite disturbing. Please let me know if these two gentlemen really need help or if they spent too much time in the Red Room.

GARN STEPHENS

Studio City

The story is true. -- The Editors
