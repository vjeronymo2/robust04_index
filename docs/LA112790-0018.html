November 27, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

The Los Angeles-based law firm O'Melveny & Myers has named nine new partners effective Feb. 1. They are: Thomas W. Baxter, Michael A. Curley, Steven L. Edwards, David E. Killough, William Satchell, Steven L. Smith, Drake S. Tempest, Debra A. Valentine and John Welch.
