940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Caldwell advances 50% to Pounds 487,000 Caldwell Investments, the Leeds-based textile company, reported a 50 per cent rise in pre-tax profits for the year to October 31. However, shares of this USM-traded company lost 4p to 57p. On turnover up 18 per cent at Pounds 6.78m (Pounds 5.77m) profits were Pounds 487,000 (Pounds 322,000). From earnings per share of 4.05p (3.15p) net, or 3.76p (2.94p) fully diluted, a final dividend of 0.6125p is proposed for a total of 1p. Mr Stanley Wootliff, chairman, said that current trading was strong with an expanded customer base and new acquisitions and he looked for continuing improvement in the year.
