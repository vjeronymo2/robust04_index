921023 FT 23 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Airflow Streamlines improves to Pounds 0.86m Profits before tax of Airflow Streamlines, the vehicle cab maker and Ford main dealer, rose from a virtual break-even Pounds 5,000 to Pounds 860,000 for the half year to August 31. Turnover improved to Pounds 34.7m (Pounds 31.9m). Earnings per share came to 6.44p (losses 0.27p) and the interim dividend is resumed via a 1p distribution. The shares rose 3p to 93p.
