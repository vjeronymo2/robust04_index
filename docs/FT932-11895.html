930430 FT 30 APR 93 / UK Company News: Majedie Invs Majedie Investments reported net asset value of 204p at March 31, compared with 171p a year earlier. Net revenue for the six months to end-March was Pounds 1.26m (Pounds 1.15m) for earnings of 2.41p (2.19p) per share. The interim dividend is raised from an adjusted 1.25p to 2p to reduce disparity.
