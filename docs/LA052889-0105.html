May 28, 1989, Sunday, Bulldog Edition

SINGAPORE HANGS 2 AS DRUG TRAFFICKERS

Two Singaporean drug traffickers were hanged Friday for trafficking in nearly 45 pounds of drugs in 1982, a prison spokesman said.

Singapore has hanged 24 drug traffickers since 1975 when it introduced a mandatory death penalty for anyone convicted of trafficking in more than half an ounce of heroin.
