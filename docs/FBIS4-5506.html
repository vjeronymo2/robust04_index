BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 8 (KCNA) -- Signature campaigns supporting "10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation for the reunification of the country" were conducted in Lebanon and Mozambique. The signature paper was signed by the chairman of the Lebanese Committee for Supporting Korea's Reunification and secretary general of the Central Committee of the Lebanese Federation of Workers Unions on behalf of the committee and federation, the chairman of the Lebanese Socialist Progressive Party on behalf of the 50,000 party members, the general secretary of the Supreme Council of the Nationalist Socialist Party of Syria in Lebanon on behalf of 80,000 party members and the chairman and the general secretary of the Lebanon-Korea Friendship Association on behalf of the association. The signature paper was also signed by the international secretary of the Central Committee of the workers organisations of Mozambique on behalf of its 600,000 members and by the secretary of the Maputo committee of the Mozambique Liberation Front Party on behalf of the 1,500,000 citizens. A signature campaign is going on in Madagascar. Taking part in it are the party of the congress for the independence, the Monima socialist organisation, the equality militant, the socialist progressive party and the solidarity committee of Madagascar and public organisations under the Malagasy vanguard of revolution. Some 2,300,000 people had signed the signature paper in the country by April 1. Meanwhile, the organizing committee of the signature campaign supporting "10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation for the reunification of the country" was inaugurated in Togo on March 30. The general secretary of the Togolese people's rally, authorized by President Gnassingbe Eyadema, signed the signature paper on March 31.
