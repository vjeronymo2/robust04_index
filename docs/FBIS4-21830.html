Four TA2703093794 Ankara ANATOLIA in English 0915 GMT 27 Mar 94 TA2703093794 Ankara ANATOLIA English BFN [Text] Semdinli, Mar 27 (AA) -- A blast that occurred at the main opposition Motherland Party (ANAP) party office in Semdinli District of southeastern Hakkari Province caused material damage, it was reported on Saturday. AA was told the blast occurred early in the evening and no one has claimed responsibility. An investigation was started, police said. Gaziantep -- Three people were wounded when blasts occurred at the party offices of ANAP and RP (Welfare Party) parties. AA was told the blasts occurred late on Saturday evening and eye-witnesses reported there were at least two people involved in the incident. They escaped after throwing firebombs to the two offices which were facing each other in the same street in Bozoklar district of the town. Samsun -- In the Black Sea province of Samsun an armed attack on the ANAP party office left one critically injured, it was reported on Saturday. AA was told two unidentified people fired at the office early in the evening and escaped after wounding one person. Police said investigations were started to find the assailants.
