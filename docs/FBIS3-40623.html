Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The second Type-21 frigate, PNS [Pakistan Navy Ship] Babar, was inducted into the Pakistan Navy in Karachi today. Addressing the induction ceremony, Defense Minister Aftab Shahan Mirani, said development of the Navy, induction, and replace of ships, submarines, and aircraft is a continuing process. [sentence as heard] Speaking on the occasion, the commander of Pakistan fleet said the induction of PNS Babar to the Pakistan Navy is part of induction of six ships of Type-21 class. This will improve our fighting efficiency.
