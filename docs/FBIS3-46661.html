Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, December 31 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] sent a message of greetings to Indalicio Sayago Herrera upon his reelection as general secretary of the Socialist People's Party of Mexico. The message expressed the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations forged between the two parties in the common struggle for the cause of independence against imperialism and for socialism would grow stronger and develop, and wished the general secretary success in his responsible work to implement the decision of the 16th Congress of the Socialist People's Party of Mexico.
