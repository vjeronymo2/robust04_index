BFN [Text] Ashkhabad [Ashgabat] 21 April ITAR-TASS -- Chinese Premier Li Peng today continues his official visit to Turkmenistan. On the eve of his arrival, the local press published a series of statements on relations between Turkmenistan and China by President Saparmurad Niyazov. Niyazov described these relations as having great perspectives. "China is a great country, and its role in the Asian-Pacific region and the entire world is considerable," Turkmenistan's president said. "We have no intention of copying somebody's model uncritically," Turkmenistan's leader said. "Along with this we are studying carefully and interestedly China's economic reform experience." The head of the Chinese Government is on a 12-day tour of the Central Asian countries, including Turkmenistan, Kyrgyzstan, Kazakhstan and Mongolia. These visits were planned in spring of last year but had to be postponed because of the premier's unexpected illness.
