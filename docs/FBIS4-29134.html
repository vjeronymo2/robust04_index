BFN [Text] The 36 Filipino fishermen earlier detained by the Indonesian Navy for fishing in Indonesian waters have been freed. The release of the fishermen was confirmed by the office of the presidential adviser for Mindanao. The fishermen are expected to arrive at General Santos City anytime now. Meanwhile, Indonesia is ready to host the peace talks between the government and Moro National Liberation Front. Indonesian Ambassador Pieter Damanik said they are ready anytime to host the peace talks, but the negotiators should have something to talk about. Damanik also relayed to President Ramos the message of thanks by Indonesian President Suharto for government's handling of the East Timor crisis.
