October 3, 1990, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

An unidentified man in his mid-20s jumped 200 feet to his death from the Interstate 805 bridge near Interstate 8 on Tuesday, said San Diego police.

The suicide occurred shortly after 6 p.m. when the man parked his car on the southbound shoulder of the freeway just south of Interstate 8. The man jumped over the railing falling to his death in the 2800 block of Camino del Rio North. Identification is the man is pending.
