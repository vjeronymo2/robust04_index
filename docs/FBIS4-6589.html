BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 22 (KCNA) -- Korea is putting big efforts into agricultural production this year, too. According to the state budget for 1994, a huge amount of fund, or up 6 percent from last year, will go to the agricultural sector. This year, Korea will consolidate the achievements of the rural technical revolution which has been completed in the main and bring its might into full play and further expand and develop the successes in mechanization and extensive application of chemicals in keeping with the high level of irrigation and electrification. Many farm machines of various kinds are supplied to the countryside to develop the comprehensive mechanization of agriculture to a higher level. Only recently, 500 tractors and 250 trucks were supplied at a time to Chongdan, Yonan and Paechon Counties. Large quantities of various chemical fertilizers are produced and supplied as well. Last year, the state allocated funds, or up 2.8 percent from the previous year, to agriculture. One of the most important successes registered in the countryside of Korea last year is that an unusual bumper harvest was reaped even under the unfavorable weather conditions.
