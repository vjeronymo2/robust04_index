BFN [Text] Cairo, 16 Jun (MENA)--Field Marshal Husayn Tantawi, armed forces commander in chief and minister of defense and war production, today received General Cao Shuangming, commander of the Chinese Air Force and defenses, who is currently visiting Cairo at the head of a military delegation to discuss developing bilateral military cooperation. They discussed several topics of mutual interest between the two countries' armed forces. China's ambassador in Cairo and several Armed Forces commanders attended the meeting. During his stay, the Chinese official is scheduled to attend some activities of the air and air defense forces.
