BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 20 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- According to the Parliamentary Press Office, the Great State Hural has discussed the demands of the Mongolian Democratic Association (MDA) for resignation of the government which members are being accused of being involved in corruption. Such accusations were also brought against some members of the parliament. The Great State Hural has decided to consider this issue after proper juridical bodies where the case is under investigation would pass their verdict. The Parliamentary Press Office notes that the Great State Hural has set up a working group for elaborating the draft law on legitimate status of the members of the parliament.
