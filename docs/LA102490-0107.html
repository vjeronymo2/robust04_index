October 24, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Motorola Unveils Office Technology: The Schaumburg, Ill.-based electronics company said it has developed a new wireless technology that will allow companies to use radio waves to link computers into networks, eliminating the need for a myriad of wires in office buildings.

The firm plans to introduce the first product based on the technology early in 1991. Company officials expect sales of the system to 100 clients before it is widely available early next year.
