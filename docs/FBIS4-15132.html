BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov has conveyed a letter to Hamid al-Gabid, secretary general of the Islamic Conference Organization [ICO] to draw his attention to the threat posed to Azerbaijan by Armenia's open aggression. Gasanov informed al-Gabid that 17,000 citizens have been killed and 50,000 others have been wounded. Gasanov also informed al-Gabid that many others are either missing or have been captured. The damage caused in Azerbaijan totals more than $1 trillion. More than 1 million people have become refugees. Gasan Gasanov expressed the hope that the ICO, which is an influential international organization, and its members will move to see to it that the forthcoming foreign ministers meeting adopts more serious measures to obstruct the Armenian aggressors.
