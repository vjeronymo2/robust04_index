February 1, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Mark April 13-27 as the dates for the third annual Los Angeles International Film Festival (AFI/L.A. FilmFest). The festival will showcase some 120 feature films, documentaries and shorts from 40 countries. The two-week event will take place at the Cineplex Odeon Century Plaza Cinemas, and the festival program will be released the end of this month. ALEENE MacMINN
