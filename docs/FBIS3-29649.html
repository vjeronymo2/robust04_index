Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The ROK Defense Ministry has announced it is examining a plan to replace various types of existing antiair missiles with new types of missiles. Today, the Defense Ministry said: The ministry is examining a plan to introduce new types of missiles because the Nike Hercules missile our military has is too old and the Hawk missile has limitations for improvement. The ministry also said that it is examining the Patriot missile and the ERINT [extended-range interceptor missile], which the U.S. Defense Department had recently decided to introduce.
