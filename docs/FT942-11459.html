940505 FT 05 MAY 94 / International Company News: Former Cigna man joins AMP Mr George Trumbull from the US insurer Cigna has been named managing director of the Australian Mutual Provident Society, Australia's largest life insurance group and one of the country's biggest institutional investors, writes Nikki Tait in Sydney. Mr Trumbull, 49, was president of the individual financial services division of Philadelphia-based Cigna. He will join the AMP in June.
