October 5, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

PRO FOOTBALL REPORT / WEEKDAY UPDATE;

AROUND THE NFL;

DID RYPIEN TAKE WRONG OPTION?

The knee injury that has sidelined Mark Rypien could be an expensive one for the Washington Redskins' quarterback.

Rypien, who damaged ligaments in his left knee in a game Sept. 23, is playing out his option after turning down the Redskins' contract offer worth $700,000 this season.

Rypien had turned down a three-year, $2.4-million deal that included $600,000 in incentives. He's playing for $275,000 this season.
