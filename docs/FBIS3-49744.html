Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] This morning Romania's President Ion Iliescu received Turkish Health Minister Kazim Dinc. During the first minutes of the meeting, which was also attended by the press, the head of the Romanian state asked his guest to convey his thanks to Turkish President Suleyman Demirel for his letter in connection with [expressing support for] Romania's wish to join NATO structures. President Ion Iliescu also expressed his satisfaction concerning the materialization of certain joint actions that had been agreed with President Suleyman Demirel, which are beneficial for both nations. For his part, the Turkish minister said that he was the bearer of messages conveying regards from the president and the prime minister of his country. [passage omitted]
