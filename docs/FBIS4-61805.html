BFN [Text] Estonian Prime Minister Mart Laar has instructed the Foreign Ministry to demand from the Russian Foreign Ministry clarification concerning Defense Minister Pavel Grachev's 6 May anti-Estonian statement. The prime minister hopes that the Russian defense minister's statement represents his personal stance and not the official standpoint of Russia. The Estonian prime minister expressed hope that the Russian Foreign Ministry will refute Grachev's irresponsible and (?tension-creating) statement. In Mart Laar's opinion, the statement by the Russian minister of defense may increase (?a dangerous threat) to Estonian independence and sovereignty.
