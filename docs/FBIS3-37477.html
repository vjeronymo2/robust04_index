Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Denis Perkin] [Text] Moscow February 16 TASS -- Europarliament Chairman Egon Klepsch met with the leadership of the Russian State Duma to discuss prospects for enhancing bilateral cooperation between the two bodies. The sides also focused on the situation in the European Union, the situation in Bosnia and EU [European Union] technological assistance to CIS coutnries. Speaking at a news conference devoted to the results of his talks in Moscow here today, Klepsch said that he had met with Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin and Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev to discuss the situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina and inform them about measures taken by the Europarliament to settle the conflict. The Europarliament supported the NATO ultimatum urging Bosnian Serbs to withdraw their heavy guns from around Sarajevo. Magdaline Hoff, who is in charged of relations with CIS states and who also attended the news conference, called for holding talks in Bosnia on the basis of partnership and cooperation rather than dictating conditions to one side.
