May 16, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

MALLS POST $41-BILLION RISE IN SALES

America's shopping centers generated $627 billion in retails sales in 1988, a $41-billion increase over the previous year, according to a new industry study.

The glowing report was issued as shopping center owners, developers, builders and retailers gathered in Las Vegas for the annual spring convention of the International Council of Shopping Centers.

The new report says there are 32,560 shopping centers nationwide, 15 times the number in operation 30 years ago. Council figures show that in a typical month, 170 million adults -- 94% of the country's 18-and-older population -- visit a shopping center.
