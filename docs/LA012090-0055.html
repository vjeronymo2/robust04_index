January 20, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

DORNAN COLUMN

The anti-Robert Dornan column (Opinion, Jan. 14) was classic name-calling by a true liberal Washington carpetbagger meddling in Orange County politics.

Christopher Matthews knows all the pejoratives: "John Wayne politics of Orange County," "most hawkish," "peripatetic ex-Los Angeles talk-show host," "arch-conservative" and, of course, "B-1 Bob."

But, for his darling Ron Kovic: "paralyzed war hero," "combat veteran," "someone who speaks in grand phrases" and "Marine sergeant who endured the horror of Vietnam and the inhumanity of a V.A. hospital." Please, give us a break!

Ron Kovic is the perfect choice for a Democratic Party now dominated by every manner of crazy on the left fringe of America: a disturbed Viet vet backed by Hollywood. I can't wait for the inevitable Jane Fonda fund-raisers!

MICHAEL V. STRATTON

Brea
