Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 10 (KCNA) -- The Federation of South Koreans in Japan for Democracy and Unification ("Hantongnyon") finalized its action program for 1994 at the second representative conference held in Tokyo recently. The keynote of the action program is to bring earlier the reunification of the country by strengthening great national unity, repulse foreign interference and realize national independence, peace and denuclearization, t the "National Security Law" of South Korea scrapped and urge atonement for the history of Japanese imperialist aggression and a settlement of the issue of postwar compensation.
