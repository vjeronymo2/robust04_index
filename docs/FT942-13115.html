940426 FT 26 APR 94 / Fear averts coup in Burundi A military coup failed in the central African state of Burundi early yesterday when soldiers refused to take part for fear of triggering a tribal bloodbath like the one raging in neighbouring Rwanda, the Burundi army chief-of-staff, Colonel Jean Bikomagu, said, Reuter reports from Bujumbura. Burundian President Cyprien Ntaryamira was killed with his Rwandan counterpart, Juvenal Habyarimana, on April 6 when a rocket brought down their aircraft at the airport in the Rwandan capital, Kigali.
