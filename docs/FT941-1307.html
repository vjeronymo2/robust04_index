940325 FT 25 MAR 94 / UK Company News: R H Lowe loss rises to Pounds 11.9m Including Pounds 7.83m of goodwill from discontinued operations previously written off, Robert H Lowe, the sportswear, printing and packaging concern, showed pre-tax losses of Pounds 11.88m for the year ended November 5 1993, compared with Pounds 1.98m. Turnover totalled Pounds 26.47m against Pounds 32.24m, which included Pounds 10.46m (Pounds 12.4m) from ongoing businesses. Operating profit was Pounds 1.19m (Pounds 1.3m). Shareholders' funds were Pounds 402,000 (Pounds 4.49m). Losses per share were 26.45p (5.87p).
