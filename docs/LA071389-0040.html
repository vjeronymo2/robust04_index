July 13, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Gannett Co.: The Washington-based diversified news and information company reported second-quarter profit of $112 million, up 15% over a year ago. Revenue totaled $904 million, up 7%. The results reflected a 6% gain in newspaper advertising revenue, while circulation revenue rose 4%. Broadcasting revenue advanced 4%, while outdoor advertising revenue gained 28%.
