BFN [Text] The joint Greek-Cypriot defense planning is proceeding according to the program at a faster pace than was anticipated, and there will be tangible proof of this in the near future. This statement was made today by Defense Minister Konstandinos Iliadhis at Larnaca after his return from Athens where he had been on a working visit. On his contacts in Athens, Mr. Iliadhis said there was a joint assessment of progress in some issues, and they planned the next steps. He expressed his full satisfaction.
