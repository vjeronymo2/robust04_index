940604 FT 04 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Property Partnerships static Property Partnerships, the developer and hotel owner, reported virtually unchanged pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.08m for the year to March 31. Net rental income from investment properties showed a small increase from Pounds 2.09m to Pounds 2.14m, while hotel turnover was lower at Pounds 4.17m (Pounds 4.28m). The hotel subsidiary continued to face tough conditions and profits declined by 11 per cent, with occupancy levels and room rates static. Profit took in an exceptional item in respect of an Ipswich property which was sold for more than Pounds 500,000 in December, Pounds 97,000 over book value. A final dividend of 4.9p lifts the total by 3.5 per cent to 7.5p, more than twice covered by earnings per share of 15.93p (14.63p). A professional revaluation of the investment and hotel properties at the year-end showed valuations of Pounds 26.9m and Pounds 13.7m, representing a surplus of Pounds 5.1m over book value and a deficit of Pounds 540,000 respectively. Net asset value per share was up from 293p to 344p.
