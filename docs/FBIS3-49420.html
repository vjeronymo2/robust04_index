Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Personnel Changes in Defense Ministry"] [Text] By a presidential decree Mr. Mikhail Atanasov Srebrev has been released from the post of deputy minister of defense on military-political issues, because of his appointment as Bulgarian ambassador to Austria. Boyko Nikolov Noev has been appointed to the post of deputy defense minister on military-political issues. Vladimir Trifonov Penchev has been appointed deputy defense minister on financial and foreign currency issues.
