March 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $30 Friday when Dancelittlesister, the first horse in his parlay, ran out of the money in the second race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $934.80.

Bob's bet today is a $30 triple from Akrokerami in the sixth to Stylish King in the seventh to Fit To Scout in the eighth.
