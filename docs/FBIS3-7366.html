Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] On 26 March, secretaries of the Workers Party of Korea [WPK] Central Committee visited quarters of deputies who will attend a conference of cell secretaries of the whole party. The conference will be held amid great expectations and interest of all party members and workers. Comrade Kye Ung-tae, member of the Political Bureau and secretary of the WPK Central Committee; Comrades Kim Chung-nin and So Kwan-hui, secretaries of the WPK Central Committee, and responsible functionaries of the party Central Committee, visited the quarters of deputies in the city, including Ponghwasan Inn and Moranbong Inn, and met with deputies of the conference of cell secretaries of the whole party. The secretaries and responsible functionaries of the party Central Committee said that the party's cell secretaries made active efforts to make the party cells the cells of loyalty endlessly loyal to the party and to the leader and the militant cells that vigorously organized and mobilized the party members and workers into the struggle to tenaciously defend and protect and to further glorify the socialist cause. The secretaries and responsible functionaries of the party Central Committee congratulated them for their attendance in the historic conference as deputies. The secretaries and responsible functionaries of the party Central Committee expressed the firm belief that the party cell secretaries, keenly realizing their missions and duties toward the party and the revolution as responsible figures for our party's lowest basic organizations [maldan kichung chojik], will actively contribute to strengthening and developing the WPK into a chuche- oriented revolutionary party forever. The deputies expressed a firm determination to make the current conference a significant meeting which will open a new turning point in further strengthening and developing our party into an invincible revolutionary party by smashing various manipulations and disturbance maneuvers of the imperialists and reactionaries. They resolved to tenaciously defend, protect and carry to accomplishment the socialist cause--the chuche revolutionary cause--by constantly enhancing the functions and role of the party cells and by deeply cherishing great trust and expectations by the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il who made them attend a conference of cell secretaries of the whole party, the first in its kind in our party's history.
