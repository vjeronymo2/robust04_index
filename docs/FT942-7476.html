940525 FT 25 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Deficit at Baris widens to Pounds 1.4m Losses at Baris Holdings, the USM-traded building services company, widened from Pounds 351,000 to Pounds 1.43m pre-tax for the year to end-February. The costs of early commercial settlements, together with the effects of restructuring and redundancies, accounted for Pounds 900,000 of the loss. Turnover fell to Pounds 14m (Pounds 20.6m) and losses per share worked through at 20.8p (earnings 0.2p).
