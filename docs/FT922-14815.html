920404 FT 04 APR 92 / UK Company News: Substantial fall in Shorco margins Shorco Group Holdings did better in the second half to produce a pre-tax profit of Pounds 293,000 for 1991. However, that compared with Pounds 750,000. Turnover for the year fell from Pounds 7.03m to Pounds 6.76m and margins dropped from 10.7 per cent to 4.3 per cent. The final dividend is 2.8p for an unchanged total of 5.2p.
