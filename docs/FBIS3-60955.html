Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In Amman, Muhammad Nazzal, Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] spokesman in Jordan, has voiced the movement's determination to carry out major operations in the occupied territories. In an interview with the French magazine V.S. DAY [name as heard] published today, Nazzal said that the coming days will witness successive and escalating operations. Nazzal indicated that since the signing of the Oslo agreement between the PLO and Israel, the intifadah has acquired a new dimension represented by the use of automatic weapons and suicide operations. He stressed Hamas's refusal to ceasefire with the Israelis. He said that the movement is committed to continuing struggle against the Israeli occupation.
