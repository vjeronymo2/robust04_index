Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 1 February Morning Edition] [Text] At a news conference on 31 January, Shigeru Hatakeyama, administrative vice-minister for the Defense Agency, commented on a British newspaper report claiming there is a possibility that Japan may acquire nuclear arms in the future. He denied the report unequivocally, saying: "That is quite out of the question; it is quite incomprehensible why there should have been such a report."
