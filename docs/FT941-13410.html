940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / World Trade News: Exports of Swiss watches up Exports of Swiss watches and clocks rose 3 per cent last year to SFr7.59bn (Pounds 3.46bn), as business picked up in the second half of the year, writes Ian Rodger in Zurich. The Federation of the Swiss Watch Industry said the volume of finished watches exported rose 7.8 per cent to 43.2m pieces, while their value increased 3.3 per cent to SFr6.6bn. Exports of watch movements jumped 25 per cent to 72.4m pieces. However, their value fell 3.2 per cent to SFr304.6m. The value of exports of clocks, components and watch cases was SFr671m.
