940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / International Company News: ING seeks Polish insurance licence Internationale Nederlanden Groep (ING), the Amsterdam-based banking and insurance group, has applied for a life insurance licence in Poland, Reuter reports from Warsaw. It planned to start with a share capital of 30bn zlotys (Dollars 1.3bn) and to lift it to more than 200bn zlotys in the third year.
