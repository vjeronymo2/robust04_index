921031 FT 31 OCT 92 / Clifford health plea opposed Mr Clark Clifford, the former US defence secretary who claims on health grounds he should not stand trial on charges stemming from the Bank of Credit and Commerce International affair, was accused in court yesterday of trying to manipulate the US criminal justice system, writes Alan Friedman in New York. Mr Robert Morgenthau, the New York district attorney, opposed Mr Clifford's request that his case be dismissed because of his heart condition.
