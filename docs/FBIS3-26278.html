Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] During an inspection of Yongchang County, Acting Governor Zhang Wule said: Peasants in western Gansu should speed up their move toward moderate prosperity to set a good example for the rest of the province to follow. We should encourage peasants to boldly enter the market economy, to simultaneously develop agriculture, forestry, animal husbandry, sideline production, and fisheries, and to comprehensively develop primary, secondary, and tertiary industries. We should put increasing peasant income in the first place. Two days ago, Acting Governor Zhang Wule visited some peasant households in Shuiyuan Township, Yongchang County, and asked them about their lives in the period leading to the Spring Festival. On behalf of the provincial party committee and government, he expressed his Lunar New Year greetings to the peasants. In home of a peasant named Wang Jiaoying, Zhang Wule asked him about his income from grain sales last year, his purchase of agricultural production materials, and the township's service for agricultural production, supply, and marketing. [passage omitted] When a village party branch secretary told Zhang Wule that the village people ensured grain acreage in the course of planting fruit, Zhang Wule expressed agreement with this and stressed: Gansu is a grain-deficient province, where 6 million people are short of grain, so developing grain production is Gansu's basic principle.
