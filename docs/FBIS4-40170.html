BFN [From the "Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] Russian-German naval exercises ended today in the Baltic. According to a report from the Russian Navy's press center, the maneuvers were held 13-14 June west off the Danish island of Bornholm. The Russian side was represented by the patrol escort Neustrashimyy, and the German side by missile patrol boats [words indistinct]. Questions of communications and tasks of joint antiaircraft defense and maneuvering, as well as conducting rescue operations at sea were exercised.
