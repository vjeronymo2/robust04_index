Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Amman, PETRA--Iraqi National Assembly Speaker Sa'di Mahdi Salih and his delegation arrived in Amman last night on their way to Baghdad after participating in the 91st conference of the Interparliamentary Union held in the French capital, Paris. Salih was received at the airport by Dr. 'Abd-al-Razzaq Tubayshat, first deputy speaker of the House of Representatives, and 'Abd Musa al-Nahar, second deputy speaker of the House of Representatives.
