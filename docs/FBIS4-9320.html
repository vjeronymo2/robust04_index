BFN [Text] Jagodina, 24 Apr (TANJUG) -- Biljana Plavsic, vice president of the republic of Srpska, said today that by withdrawing its forces from around Gorazde the republic of Srpska showed the world that it is in favor of the peaceful resolution of the crisis, even though its army did not carry out any aggression there but was on the defensive. In a Jagodina Radio program called "Weekend" she noted that "some people regret not having seen bombardment last night, which forebodes fresh provocations" and added that for this reason a summit between Russia, America, the European Union, and the United Nations should be held as soon as possible, in order to clarify the issues completely and restore a permanent peace.
