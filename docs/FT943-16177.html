940706 FT 06 JUL 94 / World Cup '94: Leonardo case to be considered by Fifa The possible suspension from the World Cup of Brazilian defender Leonardo for elbowing US mid-fielder Tab Ramos during Monday's game and causing a small fracture of his skull will be discussed today by the disciplinary body of Fifa, soccer's governing body. It is expected that Leonardo will be sidelined at least until after the semi-finals.
