940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / UK Company News: All-round advance helps SEP to Pounds 2m Profits before tax of SEP Industrial Holdings, the Surrey-based general engineer, rose from Pounds 883,000 to Pounds 2m in the six months to March 31 with all main activities contributing to the advance. Included in the figure was an exceptional profit on a property disposal amounting to Pounds 689,000. Turnover expanded from Pounds 16.3m to Pounds 21.3m, which included Pounds 730,000 from acquisitions. Order books increased substantially and new capacity to be introduced in the second half should enable further growth. Earnings pr share emerged at 2.35p (1.2p). The interim dividend is being lifted to 0.45p (0.35p).
