BFN [Text] Vilnius, Apr 08, BNS -- Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas named Lt. Col. Arvydas Pocius to head the staff of the Voluntary State Defense Service. Col. Stasys Knezys, head of the Lithuanian joint chiefs of staff, has been appointed as chief inspector of defense forces. Knezys told the Lietuvos rytas newspaper that his main duty will be to analyze the situation in the system of national defense. The colonel earlier also served as government commissioner for Russian troop withdrawal from Lithuania.
