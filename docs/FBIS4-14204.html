BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' today received Viktor Posuvalyuk, personal representative of the Russian Federation's president and director of the Middle East and North Africa Department. During the meeting, the talks dealt with relations between Syria and Russia and the means of promoting them in all fields. The two sides also exchanged views and opinions on developments in the peace process and Russia's role in it, since it is one of two cosponsors of this process.
