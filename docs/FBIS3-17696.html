Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to the Georgian Defense Ministry, the situation in Kodori Gorge remains tense. Fierce fighting is going on at the village of Zemo-Lata. As before, the Georgian positions are being subjected to fire from artillery and tank weapons and mortars. The Defense Ministry reports that during the night Georgian losses amounted to five killed and four wounded. More than 50 were killed on the Abkhazian side.
