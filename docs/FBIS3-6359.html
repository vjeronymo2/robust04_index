Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The public announcement on the convention of the DPRK Supreme People's Assembly [SPA]: The DPRK SPA Standing committee informs the SPA deputies that it will convene the seventh session of the Ninth DPRK SPA in Pyongyang on 6 April 1994. The registration of the deputies will take place at Mansudae Assembly Hall between 4 and 5 April. [Signed] The DPRK SPA Standing Committee [Dated] 16 March 1994, Pyongyang
