Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Report by Ya'ir Meydan] [Text] Finance Minister Avraham Shohat met with his Hungarian counterpart, Ivan Szabo, yesterday. The two men discussed strengthening commercial ties between the two countries; the activities of Israeli companies in Hungary; Israel's participation in Expo 96, which will take place in Budapest; and the return of property to the relatives of Jewish victims of the Holocaust. Szabo promised on behalf of his government to promote legal and legislative efforts in this regard.
