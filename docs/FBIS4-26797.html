BFN [Text] New delhi, June 24 (XINHUA) -- Indian Prime Minister P.V. Narasimha Rao today expressed distress at the devastating floods that have occurred in southern China recently, according to PRESS TRUST OF INDIA (PTI) . In a message to Chinese Prime Minister Li Peng, Rao said, "We are deeply distressed to hear about the devastating floods. Please convey our heartfelt sympathy to the affected people." He assured China of India's "readiness to assist the friendly Chinese Government and people in whatever way we can in their task of overcoming the effects of this natural disaster."
