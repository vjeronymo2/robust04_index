930827 FT 27 AUG 93 / Observer: Well wisher Queens Moat Houses, the struggling hotels group, has just provided a splendid modern example of the ancient Roman principle of memento mori. At yesterday's annual meeting of Queens Moat, in the resplendent surroundings of the New Connaught Rooms, shareholders were distracted by the sight of a balloon, presumably left over from the debauches of the night before, bobbing against the ornamental ceiling. As the lately appointed managing director Andrew Coppel went through his address, the balloon descended gently to hover behind his head. The message printed on it? 'Enjoy retirement.'
