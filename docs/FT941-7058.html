940226 FT 26 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Margins fall at Frank Usher Frank Usher, the USM-quoted women's clothing group, reported a 26 per-cent fall in interim pre-tax profits from Pounds 843,000 to Pounds 624,000. Turnover in the six months to November 30 was marginally higher, at Pounds 9.28m (Pounds 9.23m). The group attributed the fall in margins to its decision not to raise prices in line with costs. Earnings per share fell to 6p (8.2p), while the interim dividend is unchanged at 2.5p.
