June 24, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ADDENDA TO TERMITES

I'm just a little termite

Trying to eat my meal.

So don't fry, freeze or gas me

And make me squirm and squeal.

I can't help it if my food

Happens to be your home.

Don't blame me but Mother Nature,

So please leave me alone.

KENNETH L. ZIMMERMAN

Cypress
