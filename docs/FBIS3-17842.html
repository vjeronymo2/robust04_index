Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tallinn, Mar 28, BNS -- Estonia is making preparations to start the production of hand grenades, Defense Ministry Deputy Chancellor Tarmo Molder told BNS. Molder said a state-owned enterprise, a company with state participation and a private firm are involved in the project. As next step, Estonia hopes to launch production of shells. Manufacture of domestic cartridges was put off because of the high cost price. Lithuania is at present the only Baltic nation to produce its own ammunition -- cartridges and hand grenades. The country plans soon to begin making 60 mm and 80 mm cannon and shells for them. The Latvian Defense Ministry has invited tenders for the manufacture of cartridges.
