941202 FT 02 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Leeds Group makes 5% improvement Despite a 'fragile' UK market, Leeds Group, the textile dyer and printer, reported a 5 per cent increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 7.23m to Pounds 7.62m for the year to September 30. The group also said it was close to selling its 23.1 per cent stake in the West Yorkshire Insurance Company. Turnover was up 12 per cent to Pounds 52.9m (Pounds 47.1m). Earnings per share edged up to 19.3p (19p). The final dividend is 4.2p making a total of 6.3p (5.7p).
