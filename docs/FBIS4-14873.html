BFN [Text] Viktor Mikhaylov, the Russian minister for atomic energy, has said that a train carrying 25 tonnes of highly-enriched uranium obtained through dismantling nuclear warheads will arrive in Ukraine from Russia in the next few days. He said in an interview with INTERFAX on Monday that it is planned to send another train to Ukraine in April with nuclear power plant fuel. Altogether Russia is to supply 100 tonnes of highly-enriched uranium to Ukraine as a compensation for the removal of nuclear warheads from that republic.
