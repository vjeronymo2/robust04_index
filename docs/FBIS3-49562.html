Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague, 12 Jan -- Bill Clinton will pay a visit to Poland in July. During a meeting today, President Lech Walesa invited the U.S. President and, as PAP journalists heard from well-informed sources, the invitation has been accepted. The visit will probably take place in July during a European trip by the U.S. President, who will take part in a meeting of the leaders of the G7 nations.
