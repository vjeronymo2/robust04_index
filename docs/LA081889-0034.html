August 18, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

Conductor Stanislaw Skrowaczewski will replace Edo de Waart at the Hollywood Bowl Aug. 29 and 31. De Waart canceled because of schedule conflicts. There will be no program changes on Aug. 29; on Aug. 31, Weber's "Euryanthe" Overture will be played instead of John Adams' "The Chairman Dances." CLAUDIA PUIG
