Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 23 Mar (XINHUA) -- The People's Liberation Army [PLA] General Political Department today issued a circular urging the troops of the whole army to conscientiously study the guiding spirit of the Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress [NPC]. The circular said: The Second Session of the Eighth NPC was held at a critical moment of the development stage in China's reform, opening up, and modernization drive. Guided by Deng Xiaoping's theory of building a socialism with Chinese characteristics and the CPC's basic line, the meeting has comprehensively carried out the guiding spirit of the 14th National CPC Congress and the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee [CPCCC]. In the light of the fundamental principle of this year's work for the whole party and country, the meeting has forwarded the goals and missions for the country's work. The meeting served to promote democracy, unity, matter-of-fact attitude, practical spirit, as well as readiness for new explorations and progresses. Conscientiously conveying and studying the guiding spirit of this meeting will bear great significance to educating officials and soldiers of the whole army to have a better understanding of the overall situation; defining their responsibilities; strengthening the army building; and making new contributions to China's reform, opening up, economic development, and social stability. The circular says: The task of conveying and studying the guiding spirit of the meeting should be closely related to the new situation in which China's economic reform enters a critical stage of integrating the overall improvement with the breakthrough of major points. With Premier Li Peng's government work report as well as the important speech made by Jiang Zemin, general secretary of the CPCCC and chairman of the CPCCC Military Commission, at the plenary session of the PLA representatives as a major content, the task aims to guide all personnel to have full understanding of new achievements and the great situation of China's reform and modernization drive, further consolidate their faith, and strengthen their self-awareness of carrying out the party's basic line and various principled policies. It also means to help them correctly understand the importance of "seizing the opportunity, deepening reform, opening up wider, promoting development, and maintaining stability" -- a general principle for the work of the whole party and the country, resolutely safeguard the authority of the CPCCC and the CPCCC Military Commission with Comrade Jiang Zemin as the core, conscientiously act with the overall situation in mind, and maintain a high degree of stability and centralized unity of the troops. The task should define the basic goal and this year's tasks for setting up a socialist market economy; give resolute and active support to a series of important reforming measures adopted by the state for the establishment of a socialist market economy; and become a model for conscientiously subordinating interests of the part to those of the whole and for attaching importance to ideal, discipline, unity, and overall situation. The task should serve to help all personnel to have a profound understanding of historical responsibility and the glorious mission of our army under the new situation; further play the role of the enthusiasm of officers and soldiers of the whole army; and strengthen the overall work of army building, continue to enhance combat readiness, and make contributions to the reform, development, and stability of the state under the guidance of Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building a socialism with Chinese characteristics and his ideology concerning the army building in the new period as well as in the light of the requirement put forward by Chairman Jiang Zemin -- "The PLA must be qualified politically and competent militarily. It must have a fine work style, maintain strict discipline, and be assured of adequate logistical support." The circular urges party committees and political organizations at all levels to make an overall arrangement for integrating the work of conveying and studying the Second Session of the Eighth NPC's guiding spirit with the troops' theoretical and political education. It is necessary to study in a thoroughgoing manner the meeting's guiding spirit by integrating the study of Volume 3 of the Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping with that of the basic viewpoints of the theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics, which is currently conducted at the grass-roots level.
