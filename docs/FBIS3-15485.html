Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Igor Ivantsov] [Text] Moscow, 4 Mar -- Belarus has guaranteed that the transit of export supplies of gas from Russia to foreign countries across its territory will continue, despite the fact that Russia has reduced its supply of gas to Belarus. An ITAR-TASS correspondent learned this today from Vladimir Podmarkov, the chief engineer of the Gasprom Company Central Control Directorate. According to Russia's Gazprom, even after the cut the amount of natural gas being delivered to Belarus is enough to meet all the requirements of the public utilities system.
