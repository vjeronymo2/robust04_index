930415 FT 15 APR 93 / UK Company News: Gowrings gets lift from its leisure division Gowrings, which operates motor dealerships, food services and residential parks, recorded profit of Pounds 100,000 pre-tax for 1992 after taking in exceptional credits of Pounds 75,000. Mr John Fowles, chairman, said there was continued improvement from the two Ford dealerships and a significant recovery from losses of Pounds 236,000 to trading profits of Pounds 318,000 for the leisure division - Burger King restaurants and Rocco's, and Park Homes. Group turnover rose to Pounds 51.9m (Pounds 48m) and trading profit surged to Pounds 743,000 (Pounds 78,000). In the previous year there was a loss of Pounds 953,000 after exceptional charges of Pounds 342,000. The dividend is held at 2p with a final of 1p. Earnings were 0.61p (losses 10.6p).
