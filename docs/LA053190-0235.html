May 31, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

U.S. NAVY HAVEN TO BOAT PEOPLE

Organized refugee smugglers have found a way to smuggle Vietnamese boat people into Thailand by sailing them toward U.S. warships heading for Thai ports, an Interior Ministry spokesman said today.

Spokesman Pathai Wichanpreecha said the ministry has observed that the smugglers take Vietnamese refugees out to sea when U.S. Navy vessels are scheduled to call on Thai ports.

"They seem to know in advance when the American warships planned to visit Thailand so they could bring Vietnamese boat people out to the vessels," the spokesman said. "The sea is so wide, they should not have met the warships so often."

He said five U.S. warships have picked up 286 Vietnamese refugees at sea on their way to Thailand since last June.
