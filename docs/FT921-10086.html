920207 FT 07 FEB 92 / The Lex Column: Perrier In recent weeks French regulators have struck some welcome blows for minority investors. They were at it again yesterday, when the Conseil des Bourses de Valeurs said that Exor, Groupe Saint-Louis and other Agnelli allies with a combined 49 per cent stake must make a full bid for Source Perrier. There will no doubt be frenzied speculation when Exor and Perrier shares return from suspension on Monday. But there are dangers. The real battle is yet to come in the courts, when the country's fledgling takeover rules will be put to their first major test. The Agnellis can also be expected to try to persuade the EC to block the higher Nestle offer.
