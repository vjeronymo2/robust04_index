BFN [Text] Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin had a telephone conversation today on the eve of the expiry date of a deadline set for the withdrawal of Israeli troops from the Gaza Strip and Jericho. The talks focused on a series of facts revolving around the need to give a push to the current negotiations in Cairo to achieve tangible results and to avoid all that could halt the peace process.
