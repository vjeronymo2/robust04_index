Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 30 Jan (MENA)--Ambassador Mayhub al-Mayhubi, director of the Arab League's Arab Department, returned to Cairo today following a one-week visit to Iraq during which he handed a message from Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid to Iraqi Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf. In an arrival statement, al-Mayhubi said that he has a message from the Iraqi foreign minister to Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid. Al-Mayhubi added that he will submit a report to the Arab League secretary general on the outcome of his visit to Iraq. Al-Mayhubi said that in his meetings with Iraqi Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz and Foreign Minister al-Sahhaf, he sensed that they understand the implications of the topics they discussed.
