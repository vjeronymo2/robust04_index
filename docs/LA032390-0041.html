March 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Giuliani for Commentator: Former U.S. Atty. Rudolph Giuliani, who lost his bid last year to be mayor on New York, will be a commentator on the syndicated "Inside Edition" series, a spokeswoman said Wednesday.

Giuliani called the weeknight show "the best daily investigative news program on the air." He will make his debut next Thursday and will return periodically. BETH KLEID
