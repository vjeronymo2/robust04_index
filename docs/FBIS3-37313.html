Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tallinn, Feb 15, BNS -- The government passed today a bill on the administration of Paldiski drafted by the Justice Ministry. Under the bill, the islands of Suur-Pakri and Vaike-Pakri, as well as the Pakri Peninsula, will be transferred to the Padise and the Keila administrative districts, while the town of Paldiski is to be temporarily administered by the town of Keila. Thus, Paldiski may acquire the status of a district of Keila. The bill terminates the authorities of the Paldiski Town Council and the mayor elected in 1989. The government has the right to restrict entrance into the town until hazardous radioactive substances are removed from it. The bill stipulates that the special order of Paldiski's administration becomes invalid immediately after the election of new local government bodies in the town. The government passed the bill to the parliament.
