Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Kosova Information Center reports that at about 1400 [1300 GMT] on Thursday, Serbian police arrested the leadership of the Kosova Parliamentary Party [PPK] in Lipjan and that of the Kosova Democratic Alliance [LDK] branch in this commune. It is reported that the chairman of the LDK branch in Shtimje commune was also arrested. A local leader informed the Kosova Information Center that the PPK Central Presidency and the LDK local leadership were holding meetings in the offices of the LDK branch in Lipjan. The number and names of those arrested are still unknown.
