940524 FT 24 MAY 94 / Bank of Israel to raise interest rates The Bank of Israel, the country's central bank, announced yesterday it would raise interest rates by half a point in the hope this would help the fight against inflation, Reuter reports from Jerusalem. The rate on its daily monetary tender to commercial banks would rise from Thursday to a minimum 10.8 per cent, up from a minimum 10.3 per cent. It was the second such rate rise this month.
