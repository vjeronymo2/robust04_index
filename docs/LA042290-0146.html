April 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $60 Saturday when all three of his horses, Grover's Mill in the fourth race, Where Is She in the fifth race and Forty Niner Days in the simulcast race from Golden Gate, all failed to win. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $811.40.

Bob's bet today is a $6 triple from Proud Irish and T.V. Screen in the sixth race to Summer Matinee in the seventh to Hawkster, Frankly Perfect and El Senor in the eighth.

Saturday's Bankroll $871.40

Saturday's Loss $60
