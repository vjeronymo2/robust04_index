Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Islam Karimov, president of the Republic of Uzbekistan issued a decree on the formation of a special (stockholding) and commercial bank to assist with trading and commercial activities. The document states that with the aim of meeting the requirements of the population in goods and replenishing the home market and also for providing financial support to [word indistinct] and further developing trade in the Republic, a special stockholding and commercial bank called Saudagar [Trader] to promote trade and commerce be formed. Its main activities will be to serve the trading organizations of Uzbekistan, carry out commodity circulation, engage in trading and purchasing operations, and [words indistinct] between trading organizations and enterprises in other fields of the economy. The founders of the Saudagar bank will be the Finance Ministry, the Central Bank, the Uzbeksauda [Uzbek trade] Association, [name indistinct], Uzbekbirlashu [Uzbek Association], and the National Bank for Foreign Economic Activities of the Republic of Uzbekistan. The full text of the this decree will be published in the press.
