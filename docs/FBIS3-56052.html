Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev -- Asked by a UNIAN correspondent on 11 January to comment on the expected meeting between the Ukrainian, Russian, and U.S. Presidents, Oles Shevchenko, the deputy chairman of the Ukrainian Republican Party and people's deputy of Ukraine said: "He who finds opportunities for talks and meetings in any situation is a wise politician." Shevchenko added that he welcomed Leonid Kravchuk's decision to meet William Clinton in Kiev and take part in negotiations in Moscow. "I hope that decisions taken by the Ukrainian Parliament -- and that on the ratification of the START I Treaty in particular -- will be complied with in possible agreements," he noted.
