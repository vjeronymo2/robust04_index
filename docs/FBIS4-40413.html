BFN [Text] The INTERFAX News Agency reported today that Russia has recently ferreted out and expelled five North Koreans who had tried to obtain parts to be used for nuclear bombs. The INTERFAX News Agency reported that a Russian counterespionage agency had ferreted out and arrested North Koreans who had tried to obtain parts for nuclear weapons in Yuzhno Sakhalinsk, the capital city of Sakhalinsk. Without revealing any personal details about them, the Russian counterespionage agency has only disclosed that North Korea's espionage activities in the Russian territory are a matter of special concern of the Russian counterespionage agency.
