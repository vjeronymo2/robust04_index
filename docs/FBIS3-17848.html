Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (March 29) TURAN -- The past days fierce fightings continued in the north sections of Agdamskiy region. According to the military sources, the Armenian units attempting to penetrate to the Azeri territories continue attacks in the northern direction of Agdam. As the same sources announce that the fightings continue on a variable successes. [sentence as received] Many casualties are reported from the both sides. At the same time the bordering settlements of the Kazakh region of Azerbaijan once more suffered from the mass firing from the military bases of the Idzhevanskiy and Berd regions of Armenia. There are destructions and wounded.
