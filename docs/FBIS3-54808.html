Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Ivanova] [Text] Moscow January 3 TASS -- Well-known Russian economist Grigory Yavlinskiy, one of the leaders of a democratic electoral coalition, told ITAR-TASS on Monday that rumors circulating in the mass media that he was offered a job as chairman of the Anti-Monopoly Committee were completely false. Such rumors apparently arose from the fact that Yavlinskiy was asked to propose an appropriate candidate for that post. "We were talking about strengthening the Anti-Monopoly Committee, but there was no question of my appointment," he asserted.
