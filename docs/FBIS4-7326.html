BFN [Declaration No. 3/94 of the State Law and Order Restoration Council; issued 27 April] [Text] Change of portfolio of cabinet minister. The State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] has transferred Education Minister Colonel Pe Thein to the portfolio of minister of prime minister's office. By the authority vested in me, Signed; Khin Nyunt, lieutenant general, secretary-1 of the SLORC.
