BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] Alberto Urdinola Grajales, older brother of confessed drug traffickers Ivan and Julio Fabio Urdinola, was killed by gunshot early this morning in a Cali discotheque. No one has been arrested yet. Early this morning at the Chaparrito Disco, located in southern Cali, Alberto Urdinola Grajales was killed. He was the older brother of Ivan and Julio, who have surrendered to justice based on Article 37 of the new Criminal Code. Official sources reported a brawl had occurred at the crime site. Four young men made a flirtatious remark to a woman who was sitting with Urdinola. Urdinola bit the ear of one of the young men who in turn shot him. [Begin unidentified speaker recording] The body was taken to Remedios Clinic after we went to the site and followed normal procedures. [end recording] Meanwhile, the Prosecutor's Office has said Urdinola did not have a criminal record. The authorities said no one had been arrested for the crime.
