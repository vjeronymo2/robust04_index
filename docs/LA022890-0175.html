February 28, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

FILLY WINS THE STYLES

Why Wont Ya took the lead in the stretch and went on to post a half-length victory in the $81,800 Night Styles Final for fillies and mares at the Meadowlands on Tuesday night. Bill O'Donnell drove the 4-year-old daughter of No Nukes to a 1:57.1 mile over a fast track. Modern Day Lady closed for second.
