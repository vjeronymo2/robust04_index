920414 FT 14 APR 92 / UK Company News: Willis Corroon buys US reinsurer Willis Corroon, insurance and reinsurance broker, is extending its operations in the US with the acquisition of Reinsurance Activities, a re-insurance intermediary specialising in life, accident and health treaty insurance. Consideration is the issue of 5.98m shares, which will be represented by 1.19m American Depositary Shares. On the date of the agreement the ordinary shares were quoted at 253p and the ADSs at Dollars 22.50.
