BFN [Text] The five-day study session for leading cadres of Tianjin Municipal discipline inspection and supervision organs successfully ended yesterday. Gao Dezhan travelled to the Party School of the municipal party committee to visit all attendees yesterday afternoon. He hoped that cadres of the municipal discipline inspection and supervision organs across the municipality will conscientiously study Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics, submit themselves to and serve the overall situation of the whole party's work, further strengthen party style and administrative honesty, and make new contributions to reform, opening up, and modernization. While holding cordial talks with attendees of the study session for discipline inspection and supervision organs, Gao Dezhan first extended regards to comrades of the discipline inspection and supervision departments at all levels across the municipality. He said: In 1993, with the common efforts of the broad masses of cadres of the discipline inspection and supervision departments and all sectors, effective results were achieved in Tianjin's discipline inspection and supervision work. This contributed greatly to Tianjin's reform, opening up, and economic construction and to the strengthening of party style and administrative honesty. The second plenary session of the sixth municipal party committee has worked out plans for carrying out all fields of work of the municipality this year. We must strengthen party style and administrative honesty, deeply conduct anticorruption struggle, and further deepen and develop this struggle on the basis of the expected results we made. We must submit our discipline inspection and supervision work to and serve the overall situation of the whole party's work of seizing the opportunity, deepening reform, expanding opening up, promoting development and maintaining stability, further attend to party style and administrative honesty, and create good conditions for promoting reform, opening up, development, and stability in Tianjin. Gao Dezhan said: It is good to hold this study session for discipline inspection and supervision cadres. The attendees have conscientiously studied Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics and laid an ideological foundation for this year's discipline inspection and supervision work across the municipality. This work has been grasped early and successfully, and the fundamental points have been grasped. It is hoped that comrades on the discipline inspection and supervision front will work hard to create a new situation in discipline inspection and supervision work at their own work posts, realistically strengthen self-development, and make unceasing efforts to achieve new progress. This study session organized 130 discipline inspection and supervision leading cadres to devote a period of time to further studying the guidelines of Volume No.3 of the Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping, the party Central Committee's "decision on several issues concerning the establishment of the socialist market economic structure," and Comrade Jiang Zemin's speech at the Spring Festival tea party for nonparty personages on 4 February, and the guidelines of the second enlarged plenary session of the sixth municipal party committee and of the municipal economic work conference, use these guidelines to arm their mind, unify thinking, and lay a good ideological foundation for pushing this year's discipline inspection and supervision work to a new height. Liu Jinfeng, member of the standing committee of the municipal party committee and secretary of the municipal discipline inspection commission, gave a summing-up speech at the end of yesterday's study session. He said: This study session has some characteristics: The attendees have paid great attention to this session, given prominence to the major points of study, created a good study atmosphere, paid attention to establishing ties with the reality, and achieved great results in the study. In referring to the next step of study of the discipline inspection and supervision departments, he said: This study session is the beginning of theoretical study for the discipline inspection and supervision departments at all levels. Discipline inspection and supervision departments at all levels should regard theoretical study as a major matter and grasp it conscientiously. We must persist in studying theories with unremitting efforts. Leading cadres should take the lead in this aspect, pay attention to combining theory with practice and with all fields of work, promote discipline inspection work through study, raise this work to a new level, and make new contributions to promoting reform, opening up, development, and stability.
