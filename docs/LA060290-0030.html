June 2, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

WHAT COULD BE WRONG WITH FLYING DUTCHMAN WINNING THE INDY 500?

Jim Murray's article about Arie Luyendyk is typical of most of his articles about auto racing.

Arie has paid his dues, and this win was long in coming. The Indy 500 is Americana and, rightly so, is open to drivers regardless of where they were born.

BETTY HENRY, Torrance
