May 8, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

SOUTHLAND;

NIGHT STALKER SUSPECT YELLS AT PRESS

Accused Night Stalker Richard Ramirez, who has made several courtroom outbursts, yelled at reporters today, calling them "sensation-seeking parasites."

Ramirez's outburst occurred as he was led away by bailiffs to a lockup area next to the court where his trial is taking place. As the accused serial killer was led past a section of the court set aside for reporters and camera crews, he turned to them, growled and yelling at the top of his lungs, said: "Media. Sensation-seeking parasites."

He was led away with no further incident.
