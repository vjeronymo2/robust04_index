February 27, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

McEntire, Baby at Home: Country music star Reba McEntire, 34, and Shelby Stephen Blackstock, her new baby boy born over the weekend, are home from the hospital, McEntire's publicist said Monday.

Shelby, the first child of McEntire and her husband-manager Narvel Blackstock, was born Friday at 5:04 a.m. at Westside Hospital in Nashville, said Jennifer Bohler. He weighed 6 pounds, 10 ounces. An Oklahoma native, McEntire last June married Blackstock, 33, some three years after divorcing her first husband, Charlie Battles. SHAUNA SNOW
