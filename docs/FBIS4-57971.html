BFN [Editorial Report] Asuncion ULTIMA HORA in Spanish on 17 May publishes on page 20 an unattributed 300-word article on the Paraguayan foreign debt's current standing. The article says: "According to an official report by the Technical Planning Secretariat, Paraguay's foreign debt totaled $1.2316 billion during the first quarter of 1994, which represents a 1.4 percent reduction compared with the first quarter of 1993."
