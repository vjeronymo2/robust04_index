BFN [Text] Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali received Yemeni Education Minister Dr. Abu-Bakr al-Qirbi at his office in the Prime Ministry today. Dr. Qirbi conveyed a message to the prime minister from the Yemeni prime minister dealing with fields of bilateral cooperation and the latest developments in Yemen concerning the accord document, which was signed recently in Amman. Dr. al-Majali expressed the government's readiness to provide all state capabilities and expertise in the field of educational development on the level of universities and the Ministry of Education to meet Yemeni educational and training requirements, and to exchange expertise in a demonstration of the two countries' distinguished bilateral relations and the great importance His Majesty King Husayn attaches to brotherly cooperation with Arab countries. Dr. al-Qirbi hailed the great efforts made by Jordan, under the leadership of His Majesty King Husayn, to help Yemen resolve its political crisis, and said he hoped that his majesty will continue his efforts to overcome the political difficulties in Yemen. The meeting was attended by Education Minister Dr. Khalid al- 'Umari.
