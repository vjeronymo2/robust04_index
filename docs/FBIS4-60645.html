BFN [Excerpts] The Yemeni civil service minister and member of the General People's Congress in brotherly Yemen affirmed the Yemeni people's appreciation of the brother leader of the revolution's firm and unwavering stance in support of the unity of the Arab nation and the freedom of the Arab people. In an interview with AL-ZAHF AL-AKHDAR, the Yemeni civil service minister said that the leader Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi had made great efforts toward achieving Yemeni unity. [passage omitted] For his part, Yemeni Planning Minister 'Abd-al-Karim al-Iryani announced yesterday that Yemenis living in Saudi Arabia had sent large sums of money to the forces of the splinter group within the Socialist Party in southern Yemen headed by 'Ali Salim al-Bid for the purchase of weapons and military supplies from abroad. In a statement to journalists, he indicated that Saudi Arabia was involved in this, explaining that a military consignment worth 5 million dollars was purchased for the rebels in the south by one of the Yemenis resident in Saudi Arabia. He said that the consignment was now being detained at a Red Sea port in Djibouti. [passage omitted]
