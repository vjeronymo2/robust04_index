BFN [Text] Elbasan, June 21 (ATA) -- The Normalisti Association, an organization of Elbasan retired teachers, recently organized a rally in support of the education in Kosova, against the repression and blackmail of the Serbian regime toward the students, teachers, cultural and educational institutions of Kosova. Retired teachers, students and teachers of the Aleksander Xhuvani University as well as intellectuals of different professions participated to this rally. Speaking at the rally, Kristaq Mullisi, chairman of Normalisti, said that the situation in Kosova is grave and tragical especially in education, culture, and science. He considered the situation in Kosova is an unprecedented case in the history of the nations of Europe. Mr. Mullisi also expressed his solidarity with the teachers, pupils, and all the Kosova intellectuals for their mature and brave approach being always determined in their right and noble work.
