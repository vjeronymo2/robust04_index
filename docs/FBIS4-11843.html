BFN [Text] Tunis, 1 Apr (MENA)--Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat will visit Egypt in the next 48 hours, possibly the day after tomorrow, Sunday [3 April]. He will meet with President Husni Mubarak and Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa to discuss the latest developments in the Palestinian position regarding the negotiations with Israel, which are scheduled to resume in Cairo on the day after tomorrow, Sunday. In a related development, a senior Palestinian source has stated that Israel unofficially informed the PLO that it approves of a 10,000-member Palestinian police force in Gaza and Jericho. Israel formerly insisted that the number not exceed 6,000.
