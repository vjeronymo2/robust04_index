940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Care UK helped by exceptional Care UK, the restructured nursing homes group, swung from losses of Pounds 2.36m to profits of Pounds 6.35m pre-tax for the half year to end-March, thanks mainly to a Pounds 7.05m profit on the sale of its sheltered housing division. Turnover totalled Pounds 7.51m (Pounds 8.35m) of which Pounds 3.51m (Pounds 5.19m) related to discontinued operations. Operating profits of the continuing activities emerged at Pounds 563,000 (losses Pounds 32,000), but interest costs accounted for Pounds 789,000 (Pounds 851,000). Undiluted earnings per share worked through at 3.31p. The USM-traded company was formerly known as Anglia Secure Homes.
