930508 FT 08 MAY 93 / EC call over xenophobia EC ministers of Justice and the Interior at a meeting yesterday called for action by national authorities and international co-operation between European states to combat racism and xenophobia, writes Hilary Barnes in Kolding, Jutland. They also agreed on the need for international co-operation on refugees, where a Danish proposal for the establishment of a European Commission on Refugees is to be pursued.
