BFN [Text] Moscow, 24 May (ITAR-TASS) -- The Progress M-23 automatic freight craft docked with the manned Mir space complex at 1019 Moscow time today. The freight craft docked with the complex at the Kvant module. Fuel for the joint propulsion unit, a reusable ballistic capsule, equipment, scientific apparatus, food, and water was delivered into orbit. According to telemetric data and reports from cosmonauts Viktor Afanasyev, Yuriy Usachev, and Valeriy Polyakov the systems on board the orbital complex are working normally.
