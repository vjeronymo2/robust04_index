921127 FT 27 NOV 92 / Protest halts work at power station WORK on the Pounds 800m Enron project, the world's biggest gas-fired power station, was almost at a halt yesterday because of the third stoppage in seven weeks in protest over safety standards at the Teesside site. The protest followed an incident on Tuesday in which seven men accidentally inhaled gas. The company expects normal working to resume this morning.
