Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Summary] Pak Chae-pil, our ambassador to Kazakhstan, paid a farewell visit to Svoik, cochairman of the Kazakh Socialist Party, on 11 February. During the meeting, the cochairman asked the ambassador to convey his greetings to Kim Il-song and Kim Chong-il. He said that his party cherishes relations with the Workers' Party of Korea and that he supports the DPRK position of resolving the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula through DPRK-U.S. talks.
