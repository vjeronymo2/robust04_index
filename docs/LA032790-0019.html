March 27, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Statutory Records: As Oscar ages, it becomes harder to set new records.

William Wyler's "Ben-Hur" is still the most Oscared film of all time with its 11 awards out of 12 nominations. Katharine Hepburn remains the most Oscared of all performers with her four statuettes, and the late John Ford will still be the most Oscared director with his four awards. So far, Oscar Hammerstein holds the record for the only Oscar to win an Oscar. BETH KLEID
