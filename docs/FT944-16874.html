941011 FT 11 OCT 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports less demand. A few selected best Assams sold well at firm rates but others were 2 to 5 easier following quality, with plainest sorts neglected. Brightest East Africans sold readily at last levels but below best lost 4 to 8 pence. Mediums opened about steady but closed up to 5 down. Bright ceylons sold well at firm rates but plainer sorts were lower. Fair demand at about steady rates although Ugandas were neglected. Quotations: best available 200p/kg., good 140p/kg., good medium 126p/kg., medium 115p/kg., low medium 86p/kg. nom. The highest price realised this week was 230p for an Assam pf.
