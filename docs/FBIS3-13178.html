Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Dezful, Khuzestan Prov., March 9, IRNA -- The Iraqi Army has killed scores of inhabitants of the southern marshlands and destroyed at least 167 houses in the area in fresh attack, sources close to the Iraqi mujahidin said here today. They said the Iraqi Army had also levelled 15 more houses in the area between al-Amara and Basra. Two Iraqi students were killed at a school in the area as a result of the attack, they added. The sources said the Iraqi regime deployed its divisions 18 and 19, armoured division six and mechanized division 25 as well as its elite forces and even the riot police for the attack.
