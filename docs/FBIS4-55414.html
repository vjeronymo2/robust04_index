BFN [Text] On the basis of the agreement on the demilitarization of the three-kilometer exclusion zone east of the River Drina, the command of the eastern Bosnian operational zone of the Bosnia-Herzegovina army in Gorazde has witdrawn its troops from the area of Mala Bisernica. Within the 24-hour deadline, members of our army withdrew while members of the army of the so-called republic of Srpska, who were supposed to withdraw all their forces, failed to do so. Positions in Mala Bisernica are under the control of UN Protection Force [UNPROFOR] troops. Given that the aggressors failed to withdraw their (?police), the Bosnia-Herzegovina army returned to its positions.
