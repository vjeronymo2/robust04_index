930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Verity deficit widens to Pounds 4.3m Losses at Verity Group, a manufacturer and distributor of Wharfedale and Mission loudspeakers, widened from Pounds 3.68m to Pounds 4.27m pre-tax for the year ended June 30. The deficit was scored after taking account of a provision of Pounds 1.7m (Pounds 651,000) for reorganisation and restructuring costs together with closure charges on discontinued operations. Interest costs rose from Pounds 546,000 to Pounds 870,000. Turnover of Pounds 16.23m (Pounds 8.56m) incorporated Pounds 8.7m from the Mission companies for the eight and a half months since their acquisition.
