920222 FT 22 FEB 92 / Lisbon to sell railway lines Portugal is to privatise two railway lines and build one to be run as a private concession, Reuter reports from Lisbon. Transport Minister Joaquim Ferreira do Amaral said the government would invite bids to build a private railway from central Lisbon to suburbs on the southern bank of the Tagus. The lines to be sold are the 18-mile railway from Lisbon to Cascais and the line from Lisbon to the Algarve.
