941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Sanderson Elect Sanderson Electronics, the computer software and hardware company, is set to double its sales with the acquisition of a controlling interest in SGA Pacific from General Automation. It is paying Pounds 3.42m for a 51 per cent stake which will take its total holding to 78 per cent, or 72 per cent fully diluted. The deal will result in Sanderson's turnover rising to Pounds 55m. In the six months to March 31 turnover was Pounds 14.2m. The consideration is being satisfied by Dollars 1m (Pounds 600,000) cash, Dollars 1m loan notes and the transfer of 4.1m of GAI's shares. SGA supplies computer software and hardware in Australia, New Zealand, Singapore and Hong Kong.
