BFN [Text] On the occasion of the 176th anniversary of the birth of Karl Marx -- 5 May 1818 -- NHAN DAN carries an article by Hoang Tung entitled: A Science of Life. The newspaper says that Marxism- Leninism is part of the colossal treasure of wisdom accumulated by human society. Marxist doctrine has extraordinary value in the science of thinking and in the idea of liberating society, human beings, and thinking. Marxist doctrine has a long-lasting vitality. It is an open doctrine that has been constantly supplemented and developed, and has determined their path of national liberation, regained their independence and freedom, and taken the initiative in renovating their socialist thinking. It has increased production toward the goal of building a prosperous people, powerful country, and civilized society. We must firmly grasp the science of thinking and the pattern of national development so as to bring Vietnamese thinking to a new level on the basis of our guiding ideology: Marxism-Leninism and Ho Chi Minh Thought. To study Marxism is to study his scientific method of thinking and struggle to follow his example in dedicating our whole life to our lofty cause.
