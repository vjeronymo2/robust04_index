April 26, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

SOUTHLAND;

NO ONE GETS ALL SIX IN LOTTERY

A $4.76-million jackpot went unclaimed today as no one correctly guessed all six winning numbers picked Wednesday night in the California Lottery's "Lotto 6-49" game, officials said.

The jackpot will roll over to an estimated $10 million for Saturday night's drawing.

Seven players picked five numbers plus the bonus number. Each won $182,859, dividing a prize pool of $1.3 million.

There were 207 tickets with five of six numbers, each winning $3,185, and 12,447 ticket-holders will get $48 for holding four of six numbers.
