BFN [Excerpts] His Excellency Alawi Darwish Kayyal, Saudi minister of post, telephone, and telegraph, arrived in Rabat today on a visit to the Kingdom of Morocco, during which he will participate in the proceedings of the 17th session of the general assembly of ARABSAT, which will start tomorrow, Monday. [passage omitted] The minister said that ARABSAT is about to manufacture the second generation of ARABSAT, the first satellite of which would be launched soon. He pointed out that ARABSAT has now become a firm economic institution.
