Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] It was reported today in the Russian Federation Press Committee that Government Chairman Viktor Chernomyrdin has instructed Vladimir Shumeyko to hold a meeting on 5 January with members of the journalists' strike committee to work out a solution to the problem which has arisen. A final decision will be taken within the next few days at a meeting between Chernomyrdin and the editors in chief of newspapers and magazines.
