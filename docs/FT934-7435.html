931119 FT 19 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Heavy demand for Hozelock Demand has been heavy for shares in Hozelock, the garden equipment manufacturer coming to the market with a valuation of Pounds 60m, writes David Blackwell. Barclays de Zoete Wedd said yesterday it had received valid applications for 11.6m 5p shares at the issue price of 250p, making the offer 2.1 times subscribed. A total of 5.6m shares were available to intermediaries, who have been allocated 47.5 per cent of shares applied for.
