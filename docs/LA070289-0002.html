July 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

MOVIES TO TAPE

Bedlam (AMC Sunday at 1:30 p.m., again at 8 p.m.): Directed by Mark Robson, this Val Lewton-produced chiller stars Boris Karloff as the head of the notorious London madhouse. (1:30)

Breezy (Channel 13 Tuesday at 3:30 a.m.): Clint Eastwood's memorable, heart-tugging May-December romance with Kay Lenz and William Holden. (2:00)

The Old-Fashioned Way (Channel 5 Thursday at 4 a.m.): W. C. Fields finds himself managing a traveling company performing "The Drunkard." This is the one in which Fields meets his match in Baby Leroy. (1:30)

The Band Wagon (Channel 5 Friday at 1:30 a.m.): Classic Vincente Minnelli-directed backstage musical with Fred Astaire, Cyd Charisse, Oscar Levant, Nanette Fabray and Jack Buchanan. (2:00)
