May 30, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

YORBA LINDA CYCLIST HIT, KILLED BY CAR

A Yorba Linda bicyclist was fatally injured Tuesday when he was struck by a car, police said.

Charles Randall Bright, 61, was pronounced dead at the scene after being struck by a car driven by Julie Schaefer, 18, of Yorba Linda, police said.

No citation was issued pending an investigation of the accident, which occurred at 3:50 p.m. at the intersection of Village Center Drive and Via Marwah, police said.
