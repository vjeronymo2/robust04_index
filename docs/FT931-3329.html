930316 FT 16 MAR 93 / International Company News: Brierley sells US motel stake BRIERLEY Investments, the New Zealand-based hotels and investment group, is selling its 18.6 per cent stake in La Quinta Motor Inns, a US motel group, for NZDollars 112.5m (USDollars 59m). Brierley paid NZDollars 82m for the stake. The sale is part of the investment group's strategy to withdraw from the US and reorientate itself on Asia, Australian and New Zealand.
