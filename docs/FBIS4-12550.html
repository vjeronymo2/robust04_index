BFN [Excerpts] Austrian President Thomas Klestil arrived in Damascus at 1200 today on an official visit to the Syrian Arab Republic. President Hafiz al-Asad led those who received him at Damascus International Airport. Also present were Vice Presidents 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam and Dr. Muhammad Zuhayr Mashariqah, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi, Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar', and Austria's Ambassador in Damascus Robert Karas. [passage omitted on reception ceremony] After a short rest at the airport's VIP lounge, President Hafiz al-Asad and his guest left in an official motorcade for Tishrin Palace, the guest president's place of residence. President Klestil is accompanied by a delegation including Alois Mock, the foreign minister, and (Erik Fetman), deputy minister of public economy and transport. President Hafiz al-Asad and President Klestil held a meeting at Tishrin Palace. The meeting was attended on the Austrian side by members of the accompanying delegation and on the Syrian side by Vice Presidents 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam and Dr. Muhammad Zuhayr Mashariqah, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi, and Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar'.
