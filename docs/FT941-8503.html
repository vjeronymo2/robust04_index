940219 FT 19 FEB 94 / GEC pairing wins frigate contract Mr Jonathan Aitken, minister for defence procurement, said yesterday that General Electric Company and British Aerospace had won the contract to be the UK member of the joint venture between France, Britain and Italy to design and build the 'first of class' of the common new generation frigate. The frigate is due for production about 1997. GEC and BAe beat VSEL and Hunting Engineering for the contract.
