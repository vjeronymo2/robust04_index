BFN [Text] Bulgarian Defense Minister Valentin Aleksandrov, who is in Ankara on an official visit, has been continuing his contacts. The guest minister met with State Minister Necmettin Cevheri in the afternoon. Cevheri pointed out to the importance of friendly and neighborly relations between Turkey and Bulgaria in establishing peace and stability in the Balkans. Stressing that Bulgaria has views similar to Turkey's regarding Bosnia-Herzegovina, Cevheri noted that action should be taken in unison with international organizations to eliminate this issue from being a sensitive matter in Europe. Aleksandrov, in turn, stated that they are following the incidents in Bosnia-Herzegovina with great concern. He stressed that Bulgaria opposes actions taking place in the region directed toward ethnic cleansing and territorial acquisition through the use of force. President Suleyman Demirel received the Bulgarian defense minister and accompanying delegation at the Cankaya Mansion. Demirel conferred with Aleksandrov for some time. National Defense Minister Mehmet Golhan also participated at the meeting.
