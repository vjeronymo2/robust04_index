Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] India and Italy have agreed to intensify contacts between businessmen of both the countries and disseminate information about opportunities created by liberalization. This was agreed to when the visiting foreign minister of Italy, Professor Beniamino Andreatta, met the finance minister, Dr. Manmohan Singh, in New Delhi today. Earlier, Mr. Andreatta along with members of his delegation called on the president, Dr. Shankar Dayal Sharma, at Rashtrapati Bhavan [President's House]. During the half an hour meeting they exchanged views on various bilateral matters and international issues. The Italian minister later called on the external affairs minister, Mr. Dinesh Singh. Both the foreign ministers expressed satisfaction at the excellent relations between the two countries. The visiting dignitary will call on the prime minister tomorrow.
