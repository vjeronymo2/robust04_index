930420 FT 20 APR 93 / UK Company News: Rentokil makes Pounds 1.75m acquisition Rentokil has acquired the facilities maintenance division of John Lelliott, the building company which is in receivership, for a maximum Pounds 1.75m cash. In the year to June 30 1992 the business had turnover of Pounds 6.49m and profits of Pounds 440,000.
