BFN [Text] The visiting Vietnamese Prime Minister Mr. Vo Van Kiet, his wife, and entourage, accompanied by Deputy Prime Minister U Nyunt Swe and party, left Yangon [Rangoon] in a special plane yesterday afternoon and arrived in Mandalay at 1614. The visiting prime minister, his wife, and party arrived at the Nanmyo Guest House at 1630 and were warmly welcomed by Major General Kyaw Than, commander of the Central Commander. Later, the visiting prime minister, his wife and entourage toured the Myanan Sankyaw Golden Palace inside the Mandalay Palace Grounds, Golden Palace Museum and Mandalay Hills and viewed Mandalay from the hill. In the evening, Central Command Commander Maj. Gen. Kyaw Than hosted a dinner in honor of the visiting prime minister and his entourage. This morning, the visiting prime minister and his entourage, accompanied by Maj. Gen. Kyaw Than visited Hong Kong Store, Defense Services Department Store, Chanmya Thazi, and a preview of the cooperative trade fair in Mandalay. The entourage lated visited Pyint-oo-lwin by car. Deputy Agriculture Minister U Tin Hlaing presented factory products to the visiting prime minister and his wife at Maymyo Silk Factory and gave the visitors a tour of the factory. The local commander then hosted a luncheon in honor of the visiting prime minister and his entourage at the Military Guest House. The visiting minister and his entourage later visited the Botanical Gardens and the Military Academy. The delegation then left Pyint-oo-lwin by car for Mandalay and left Mandalay Airport at 1435 and arrived back in Yangon at 1610.
