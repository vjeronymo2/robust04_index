(3) Methods to be employed by the owner or operator to limit NO X emissions from emissions sources to within the emissions level required in paragraphs (b)(1)(iii)(B) (1) through (5) of this section. Methods may include but are not limited to: (i) Installation of capture systems and control devices; (ii) Modifications to increase the efficiency of existing capture systems and/or control devices; (iii) Process modifications and/or substitutions; and (iv) Reduction in operating schedules for one or more emissions sources at the facility. For those facilities choosing to comply through use of reduced operating schedules, operating schedules for baseline emissions levels and for emissions levels starting January 1, 2001, continuing for a period not less than 5 years. (4) Projections of annual NO X emissions for each emissions source through the year 2005 after application of the reduction methods described in paragraphs (b)(1)(iii)(B) (1) through (5) of this section.
