Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 11 (XINHUA) -- Chinese State Councillor Song Jian met here this afternoon with a Pakistan science and technology delegation, headed by Parvez Ahmad Butt [name as received], secretary of the Pakistan Ministry of Science and Technology. Song, also minister in charge of the State Science and Technology Commission, had a warm and friendly talk with the Pakistan guests. Both Song and Butt believed that the two-day 12th session on scientific and technical cooperation between China and Pakistan provided extensive opportunities for furthering bilateral cooperation in science and technology. Han Deqian, vice-minister of the State Science and Technology Commission, and Butt signed a protocol of the 12th session on scientific and technical cooperation between China and Pakistan after the meeting. Butt and his party arrived here on Sunday [9 January]. They are also scheduled to visit east China's Shanghai municipality and Fujian Province.
