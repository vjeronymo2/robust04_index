Section 29.35 is amended by revising paragraph (a) to read as follows: §29.35 Changes or modification of approved analyzers and detectors; issuance of modification of certificate of approval. (a)(1) To change any feature of an approved analyzer or detector an applicant may request a modification of the original certificate of approval issued by MSHA by filing an application for such modification in accordance with the provisions of this section.
