February 4, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ELSEWHERE IN THE TIMES

There are no heroes in "Gameface," a play written by John Schalter and former Ram offensive lineman Russ Bollinger, currently at the Flight Theater in Hollywood. However, there are moments in this portrayal of professional football life when you believe what Bollinger and Schalter have presented is true -- or could be. See Gene Wojciechowski's column.
