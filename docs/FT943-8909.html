940815 FT 15 AUG 94 / Cracks found in Concorde wings Hairline cracks have been found in the wings of British Airways' seven Concorde aircraft, the company has confirmed. Two Concordes have already been repaired and the Civil Aviation Authority said it had allowed the ones yet to be repaired to remain in service if they were checked after every 10 flights. It said the cracks did not affect airworthiness.
