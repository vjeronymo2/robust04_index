BFN [Text] Seoul, April 27 (YONHAP) -- The Hyundai Group will manufacture and sell aircraft in a joint venture with Russia's Yak Co., a group spokesman said Wednesday. The group's affiliate, Hyundai Technology and Development Co., will hold 51 percent of the joint venture and Yak the remaining 49 percent. Company Chairman Chong Mong-ku will be the owner, the spokesman said. Hyundai-Yak is to manufacture the Russian aerospace firm's 150-passenger Yak 42h as well as the short-haul Yak 40h. The group's Sosan, Kwangyang and Changwon industrial plants will serve as manufacturing sites. The two companies signed a contract in November last year, with Yak offering the design and manufacturing technology and Hyundai taking care of sales, financing and production. Hyundai will dispatch engineers to oversee the technology transfer and plans to set up a branch in Moscow.
