Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tallinn, Mar 02, BNS -- Estonian Foreign Minister Juri Luik is today on an official visit to Sweden at Swedish Foreign Minister Margaretha af Ugglas' invitation. Juri Arusoo, Foreign Ministry press secretary, told BNS that Luik is scheduled to meet with Ugglas and speak at the Swedish Foreign Policy Institute on Baltic security. Arusoo said Luik will probably meet also with Swedish Prime Minister Carl Bildt.
