March 5, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: ARTS

Ullman, Freeman Star in 'Shrew': A quirky version of Shakespeare's "Taming of the Shrew" is scheduled to open in New York on June 22. Comedian Tracey Ullman will play Katherine, and Morgan Freeman, who was nominated for an Oscar for "Driving Miss Daisy," has been cast as Petruchio. This version of "Shrew" will be set in the Old West and will be directed by A. J. Antoon, who once set "A Midsummer Night's Dream" in Brazil. SHAUNA SNOW
