940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / World News in Brief: Spurs fine rises to Pounds 1.5m A Pounds 600,000 fine imposed on Tottenham Hotspur for alleged irregular payments to players was increased to Pounds 1.5m by a Football Association appeal hearing. The number of points to be deducted from the club next season was halved to six. Everton share issue, Page 23
