May 15, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

L.A. West Magazine Repurchased: Robert Loomis and Jan Loomis, the former owners of L.A. West magazine, have reacquired the publication for an undisclosed price. L.A. West Publisher Robert Loomis and Jan Loomis, editor of the magazine, bought L.A. West from Morristown, N.J.-based Micromedia Affiliates and will retain their positions at the publication. They sold controlling interest in the monthly publication to Micromedia in September.
