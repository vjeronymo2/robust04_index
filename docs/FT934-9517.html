931110 FT 10 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Bermuda Fire & Marine Insurance The Department of Trade and Industry is examining the circumstances surrounding the collapse of a leading Bermuda-based insurance company, Bermuda Fire & Marine Insurance. Bermuda Fire & Marine filed for a winding up petition on the island last week. DTI concern is understood to focus on the company's London branch which operated until the mid-1980s. Mr Gareth Hughes, a partner of Ernst & Young, London, and Mr Tony Joaquin, a partner in Ernst & Young, Bermuda, were appointed provisional liquidators of the Bermuda company last week.
