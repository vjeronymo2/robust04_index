BFN [Text] Nick Bolkus, minister of immigration and ethnic affairs of the Commonwealth of Australia, and his delegation arrived in Vientiane yesterday on an official and friendly visit to the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR] at the invitation of LPDR Interior Minister A-sang Laoli. Nick Bolkus and his delegation were welcomed at Wattai Airport by Deputy Interior Minister Siangsom Kounlavong and a number of high-ranking Interior Ministry cadres. Also on hand at the airport to welcome the delegation was Michael Mann, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to the LPDR from the Commonwealth of Australia. During the visit, Nick Bolkus is scheduled to pay a courtesy call on LPDR Deputy Prime Minister Khamphoui Keoboualapha. He will hold a meeting to exchange experiences with Interior Minister A-sang Laoli and will meet Khamtan Thepbouali, acting chairman of the Lao Front for National Construction. Nick Bolkus and his delegation are also scheduled to visit some historic and cultural sites in Vientaine Municipality and Luang Prabang Province.
