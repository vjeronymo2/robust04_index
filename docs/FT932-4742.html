930608 FT 08 JUN 93 / Management (The Growing Business): The Smart way to gain a grant - In a Nutshell A record 1,450 businesses have applied for 180 UK Small Firms Merit Awards for Research and Technology (Smart Awards), reinforcing calls for more funds to be made available to expand the scheme. The Smart initiative offers grants of up to Pounds 45,000 to help businesses employing fewer than 50 people to develop innovative technology. The winners, who will be announced at the end of July, may then apply for a stage two award of up to Pounds 60,000. Contact Department of Trade and Industry. Tel: 071 215 5000.
