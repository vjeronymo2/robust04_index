920530 FT 30 MAY 92 / International Company News: CGE confirmed as new Potsdam studio owner COMPAGNIE Generale des Eaux (CGE) has been confirmed as the successful bidder for the huge Universum-Film Babelsberg studios at Potsdam, outside Berlin. It beat Bertelsmann, the German media group. The supervisory board of the Treuhand privatisation agency confirmed the sale, but declined to reveal the price. It said, however, it expected DM1bn (Dollars 600m) to be invested by CGE and its partners. The 460,000 sq m site, which launched stars such as Marlene Dietrich and Greta Garbo, will be converted into a media and entertainment centre.
