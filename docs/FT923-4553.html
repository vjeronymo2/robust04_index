920905 FT 05 SEP 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices SPICES Cassia demand is picking up and local stocks are rapidly disappearing, reports Man-producten. Prices are moving higher. Vera a and Korintji a sticks traded at USDollars 2,300 a tonne ex-store in Rotterdam, shipment Dollars 1,985 cif; Korintji b broken Dollars 1,575 ex-store, shipment at Dollars 1,325 cif; Madagascar unavailable; Seychelles Dollars 1,350 cif. Nutmeg prices are weaker due pressure from Indonesian exporters. Ginger - firm in Cochin. New crop estimated at 60 per cent of last year's harvest, due to the heavy monsoon. Shipment Dollars 1,350 cif. Nigerian spot up to Dollars 850 ex-warehouse. Cloves and all-spice decreasing further.
