October 28, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

PACIFIC LEAGUE

Muir 56, Hoover 0 -- Muir's Sam Williford ran the opening kickoff 85 yards for a score and matters only got worse for Hoover (2-6, 0-3) at La Crescenta High. Hoover was held to 10 yards in offense. ANDY MEYERS
