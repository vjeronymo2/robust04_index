November 27, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

GETTING READY: Shelter officials are hoping that the recent cold snap (A3) is not an indication of a rough winter ahead. . . . When nights get cold, armories in Santa Ana and Fullerton open as emergency shelter for the homeless. . . . Last winter, a mild one, forced them to open only 14 times compared to 28 the previous winter. Nonetheless, officials are planning for the worst. "We're just never sure what the weather is going to do," says one. Tony Marcano and Jim Newton
