Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] According to a GLOBE-HEBDO-IFOP poll published on 2 March, 75 percent of the French think that "France should not resume nuclear testing as long as the other countries do not resume theirs." Some 19 percent of the French questioned think that the tests should be resumed, and 6 percent have no opinion. Among the people questioned who identified themselves as "supporters of the Left," 81 percent oppose the resumption of tests; 3 percent are in favor; and 6 percent have no opinion. This GLOBE-HEBDO-IFOP poll was conducted on 25 February on a sample of 957 representative members of the population aged 18 years or more and chosen according to the quota method.
