BFN [Presidential Decree No. 28 issued in Sanaa on 26 May] [Text] The Presidential Council chairman, After taking cognizance of the Republic of Yemen Constitution and Law No. 52 of 1991 concerning the law of local administration, at the recommendation of the acting prime minister, and following the approval of the Presidential Council and the Council of Ministers, decides:

Article One: Brother Ahmad 'Ali Muhsin shall be appointed
governor of Shabwah Governorate.
Article Two: This decree shall go into effect as of the
date of its publication. It shall be published in the official gazette. Issued at the Presidential Office in Sanaa on 17 Dhi al-Hijjah 1414 Hegira, corresponding to 26 May 1994 [Signed] Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman; Dr. Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar, the acting prime minister
