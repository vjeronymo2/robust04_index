Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] At the Information and Press Department of the Russian Defense Ministry it has been said that the SUNDAY TIMES report referring to anonymous intelligence sources on the creation in Russia of a new biological weapon does not correspond to the facts. Lieutenant General Valentin Yevstigneyev, deputy commander of troops for radiation shielding and chemical and biological warfare defense, stated that in accordance with the Russian president's Edict No. 390, dated 11 April 1992, all work running counter to the requirements of the 1972 convention banning biological weapons has been halted.
