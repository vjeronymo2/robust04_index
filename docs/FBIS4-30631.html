BFN [Text] Australia's Prime Minister Paul Keating and Indonesia's President Suharto, have held talks in Jakarta. Although the talks were mainly on trade, Mr. Keating is under pressure to raise the issue of Indonesia's recent media clamp down during his discussions. The Australian Government has already expected disappointment about last week banning of Indonesia's three leading news magazines. Mr. Keating did not commit himself to raising the sensitive issue with President Suharto. Before leaving Canberra, Mr. Keating said that Australia was not about to become an international media policeman.
