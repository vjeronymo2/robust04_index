October 8, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

DAYTIME CAR LIGHTS

Car lights for a daytime drive? You bet! Just visit Scandinavia and see for yourself, as this recent tourist can confirm. In Finland and Sweden, it's the law to drive with lights on both day and night. In fact, new cars contain a device that automatically turns on the lights when the engine starts up.
