931122 FT 22 NOV 93 / International Company News: Crossroads Oil loss USM-quoted Crossroads Oil Group incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.52m for the 15 months to June 30, 1993, against a restated Pounds 297,002 profit for the previous year. Losses per share were 3.54p (0.8p earnings). No dividend is declared for the period, but the board said it intended to declare a distribution in 1994.
