March 25, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: CITRUS COLLEGE STUDENTS TAKE FORENSICS HONORS

Three Citrus College speech students have won top honors in a forensics competition held at Pasadena City College.

Shana Furlow won first place in dramatic interpretation, and Laura Chiara and Ted Torres won second place in duo interpretation.

The competition drew students from 50 colleges and universities. Citrus is located in Glendora.
