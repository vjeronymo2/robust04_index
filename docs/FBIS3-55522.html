Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Kamchatka News" program] [Text] Sergey Levushkin, head of the Koryak Autonomous Okrug, on 4 January approved the decree which sets the date and ratifies the statute for the election of deputies to the okrug Duma. Elections are set for 6 March 1994. The voters of the Koryak Okrug are to elect eight deputies to the okrug Duma, two deputies from each rayon, who will work on a full time basis. The decree of the okrug electoral commission establishes the okrug electoral districts, some of which will be territorial, and others, national. The composition of the rayon electoral commissions is to be submitted for approval before 14 January. The commissions will prepare and conduct the election of deputies to the okrug Duma. It is presumed that the elections will be conducted according to the same procedure as for the Federal Assembly of Russia. So, on this occasion, the Koryak Autonomous Okrug is behaving like a member of the Russian Federation ensuring independent economic and cultural development in the territory.
