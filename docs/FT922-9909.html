920505 FT 05 MAY 92 / Ford to reorganise FORD is to reorganise its research and development engineering activities in Europe to eliminate the duplication of activities between its UK and German R&D centres, John Griffiths writes. The restructuring, which will take advantage of technology advances allowing 'simultaneous engineering' at geographically distant centres, is expected to lead to the closure of Ford's prototype manufacturing and production engineering centre at Aveley, Essex, which employs 900.
