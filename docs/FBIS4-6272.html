BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 18 (KCNA) -- Artistes of foreign countries who have participated in the 12th April Spring Friendship Art Festival visited various places during their stay in Pyongyang. They visited Mangyongdae, the cradle of revolution, where the great leader President Kim Il-song was born. With deep emotion and pleasure of visiting the sacred place of revolution, they enthusiastically sang in front of the old home the songs "Song of General Kim Il-song" and "Let Us Sing of Spring Holiday of April" carrying boundless respect and reverence for President Kim Il-song who is making an undying contribution to accomplishing the cause of global independence. They went to the tower of the chuche idea, the arch of triumph, the three revolutions exhibition and others. Vice-minister of Culture of Egypt Abdul Rahman Abdul Farah, said the tower of the chuche idea was a monumental edifice which has been built so splendidly. He went on: Through the visit, I have come to know well where is the source of the Korean people's fortitude. It is because the Korean people are firmly armed with the chuche idea. I wish greater success to the struggle of the Korean people who are advancing forward under the banner of the chuche idea. The artistes appreciated the mass gymnastic display "Great Victory of Rural Theses" and participated in the evening dancing party of youth and students in Pyongyang and celebrations of April 15.
