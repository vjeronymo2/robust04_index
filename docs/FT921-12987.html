920123 FT 23 JAN 92 / International Company News: Whirlpool chief in Europe quits WHIRLPOOL, the world's largest white goods producer, announced the surprise resignation of Mr Jan Prising as president and chief executive of its European unit, Whirlpool International. Mr Prising said: 'I feel as if I have largely accomplished what I was asked to do. . . I want to spend more time with my family and pursue other business interests in Europe.' He is to be replaced by Mr William Marohn, vice-president of Whirlpool's North American Appliance Group.
