940525 FT 25 MAY 94 / Observer: Arid patch Horror of horrors. Who was that lurking in the flower beds at the Chelsea Flower Show when Graham Hearne's Enterprise Oil hosted its annual dinner for the great and the good on Monday night? Step forward Rudolph Agnew, the new chairman of Lasmo, the struggling oil company which is under attack from Enterprise. Apparently, both chairmen did bump into each other but Observer is assured that the only thing they talked about was that hardy perennial . . . the weather.
