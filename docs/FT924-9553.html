921105 FT 05 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Net assets decline at German Smaller Net asset value at the German Smaller Companies Investment Trust was 213.6p at September 30, down from 226.9p at the March year end and 223.5p at the same stage of 1991. The figure after dilution for exercise of warrants was 195.7p, against 206.9p and 212.4p respectively. At end-September the trust's discount to fully diluted net asset value had widened from 16 per cent to 28 per cent. Net revenue rose to Pounds 448,000 (Pounds 352,000) for earnings of 2.49p (1.96p) per share.
