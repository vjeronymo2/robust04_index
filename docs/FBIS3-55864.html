Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN ["Text" of President Geydar Aliyev's amnesty decree for the Biregadi Battalion on 10 January -- read by announcer] [Text] Considering the fact that the Biregadi Battalion's fighters have admitted their criminal offense and said that they repent what they have done and are ready to try to rectify their mistake by going to the front lines to bravely fight against the enemy, and acting in the light of humanitarian principles and Azerbaijan's Constitution, I have decided: That the Biregadi Battalion's military personnel, who have been convicted of committing a criminal offense, be pardoned and released from prison to be sent to join the fighting army units under the defense minister's orders. [Signed] President Geydar Aliyev [Dated] Baku City, 10 January 1994
