920225 FT 25 FEB 92 / International Company News: IBM to cut top managers' pay INTERNATIONAL Business Machines said the basic pay of Mr John Akers, chairman, and the four other members of its management committee were likely to be cut by at least 40 per cent due to the company's first annual loss ever, in 1991, Reuter reports. The company said Mr Akers could see a decline in the Dollars 2.6m in annual cash incentive, which was tied to business results.
