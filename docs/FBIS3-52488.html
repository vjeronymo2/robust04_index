Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today's meeting of the National Security Council, which was chaired by the interior minister, reviewed the latest political and security situation in the western parts of the country as well as the process of confrontation against criminal elements. Necessary decisions were adopted. The state public prosecutor also submitted a report about his visit to the country's eastern areas and praised the local officials for their careful supervision over the eastern borders and their efforts to prevent the passage of large convoys of narcotics smugglers through these borders.
