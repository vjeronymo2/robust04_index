Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Afghan prime minister, Mr. Golboddin Hekmatyar, has said the Supreme Coordination Council representing the parties opposed to President Rabbani will welcome mediation efforts by any country, including Pakistan for the restoration of peace in Kabul. This was stated by a spokesman of Hezb-e Eslami [Islamic Party of Afghanistan], while talking to Radio Pakistan's representative in Peshawar this morning. He urged President Rabbani to accept cease-fire offer of 45-group of mojahedin commanders to pave the way for a dialogue to end the fighting in Afghanistan. Reports from Afghanistan say Kabul was relatively calm this morning barring a few minor skirmishes.
