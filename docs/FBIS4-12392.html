BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres says that the negotiations will the PLO will not be concluded by the 12 April target date, but Israel is determined to withdraw from Gaza and Jericho. Our correspondent Yo'el Nir reports that speaking at the Labor faction meeting today, the foreign minister further said that Israel was going to propose a change in the negotiations with the PLO. Minister Peres said Israel proposes mapping out all the issues under negotiations: Issues which have been discussed to a successful conclusion would be summed up, and issues which have encountered difficulties would be transferred to the comprehensive negotiations.
