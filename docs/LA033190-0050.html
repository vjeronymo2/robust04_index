March 31, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

JUDGING OSCAR: TELECAST VIEWERS HAVE THEIR SAY

As I watched the Academy Awards presentations last Monday, I learned something new about world geography.

Presenter Arnold Schwarzenegger mentioned that Billy Crystal's ancestors came from Odessa, "Russia." Because I was born in Odessa in 1945, I quickly rechecked my birth certificate to see if some elves had changed the name of the country. What a relief it was to find out that Odessa is still a city in the Ukrainian Republic. Whew!

VICTORIA S. CHAMPLIN, Huntington Beach

The Ukraine is part of the Soviet Union. A nationalist movement is seeking its independence.
