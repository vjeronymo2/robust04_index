August 9, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Swimsuit Maker Names Ad Firm: Body Glove International, the Redondo Beach designer of swimwear that looks like wet suits, has hired its first ad agency. Body Glove selected Davis, Ball & Colombatto, the Los Angeles ad agency best known for creating the original "Mac Tonight" ad campaign for McDonald's. Body Glove, which formerly created its own ads, did not disclose its advertising budget. Brad Ball, ad agency president, said of new client Body Glove: "It quite literally seemed like a perfect fit."
