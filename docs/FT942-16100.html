940412 FT 12 APR 94 / UK Company News: ICI Dollars 10m paint investment Imperial Chemical Industries is spending about Dollars 10m (Pounds 6.8m) expanding its US paints operation. Through Glidden, its paints offshoot based in Cleveland, Ohio, it is buying California-based Decratrend from a subsidiary of Total, the French petrochemical group. The purchase is part of Glidden's plans to expand outside its traditional home of the north-eastern US.
