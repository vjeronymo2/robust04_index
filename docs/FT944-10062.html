941112 FT 12 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Henlys purchase Henlys Group is paying Pounds 4.06m for Motorpoint Vehicle Sales, a motor dealership based in Romford, Essex, with net assets of Pounds 1.56m. The consideration is satisfied by Pounds 850,000 cash and the balance in loan notes. The company also said that demand for Plaxton buses and coaches continued to show encouraging growth with the order book to the end of 1995 showing an increase of Pounds 20m.
