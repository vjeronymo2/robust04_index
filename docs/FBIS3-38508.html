Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn -- According to an agreement between the parties and Bundestag groups, the Bundestag election is to be held on 16 October 1994 in most likelihood. This was learned by DPA on Wednesday [2 February] from a reliable source in Bonn. Politicians of the coalition parties are said to have agreed on this day with the Social Democratic Party of Germany [SPD].
