Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The parliament of the Russian republic of North Ossetia on Friday appointed the republic's recently elected president, Akhsarbek Galazov, head of government. The legislature also appointed deputy premiers and 16 other ministers, and scheduled the next parliamentary election for March 1995, the time when the term of the present parliament expires. Two drafts for a law on the new parliament, one of them proposed by an opposition leader, Yuriy Koniyev, were returned to relevant committees and commissions for amendment.
