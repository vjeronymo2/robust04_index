May 22, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLE WATCH

Ross Porter in DodgerTalk Line-Up: Dodger play-by-play broadcaster Ross Porter has joined Stu Nahan as co-host of KABC-AM's pre-game DodgerTalk show.

Porter will also host Monday-through-Friday post-game DodgerTalk shows following home and away contests. He will also continue as play-by-play announcer. Ed Bieler will continue to host SportTalk, heard Monday through Friday afternoons. GREG BRAXTON
