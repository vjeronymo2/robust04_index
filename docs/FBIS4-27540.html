BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 2 (KCNA) -- NODONG SINMUN today carries an article by Yi Yong-tok, vice-chairman of the Central Committee of the League of Socialist Working Youth of Korea [LSWYK], under the headline "Strengthening Education of Youth and Children is Consistent Policy of Our Party." The article says: The lifeline of the revolution is maintained by the new generation and the destiny of the country is decided by it. The destiny of the party and the revolution largely depends on the education of the youth and children, the new generation. From early days the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] has paid deep attention to firmly training the new generation, attaching importance to the education of the youth and children. The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il indicated the road of the movement of the Korean youth and children conforming to the development of the revolution at the stage where the cause of modeling the whole society on the chuche idea is being realized and powerfully organizations of the WPK, the LSWYK and the childrens' union to follow this road. The question of the education of the youth and children has been successfully solved in Korea under the wise guidance of the party. The 8 million youths and children are demonstrating their stout spirits and noble traits as the dependable successors to the revolution and living and striving with the resolution to lay down their lives as rifles and bombs in case of emergency. This clearly proves the vitality of our party's policy of education of the youth and children. To strengthen ideological education among the youth and children is an urgent demand of our developing revolution and the obtaining [word as received] situation. The imperialists and reactionaries of the world are getting more desperate in their maneuvers to isolate and stifle the DPRK, the socialist fortress. They are trying to disarm the youth and children ideologically in executing their anti-socialist strategy. The historical experiences and lessons of the socialist movement teach us that the destiny of the socialist cause largely depends on the ideological education of the new generation. Only when we better educate the young men and women, the most active and influential unit of the society, and the school children, the future of the motherland, can we firmly defend Korean-style socialism and successfully carry forward the revolutionary cause of chuche to accomplishment. The vice-chairman elaborates on important questions arising in the education of the youth and children in the present times such as education in revolutionary tradition, class education and socialist patriotic education.
