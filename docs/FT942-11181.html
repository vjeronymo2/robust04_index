940506 FT 06 MAY 94 / International Company News: Westinghouse chief optimistic Shares in Westinghouse Electric, the struggling US conglomerate, jumped 6 per cent yesterday, as chief executive Mr Michael Jordan issued an optimistic forecast of earnings growth over the next three years, writes Richard Waters in New York. The shares closed up Dollars 3/4 at Dollars 13. Mr Jordan, who was brought in to head the group last summer, predicted that growth in operating earnings per share would reach between 15 and 20 per cent a year over the next three years. Mr Jordan also indicated that cost-cutting over the next two years would reduce the company's expenses by Dollars 300m.
