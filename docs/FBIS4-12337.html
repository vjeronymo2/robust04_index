BFN [Text] In a statement reported by WAFA, a Palestinian spokesman yesterday expressed regret for the 'Afula incident which led to the killing of a number of Israeli civilians. He pointed out that the series of actions that started with the al-Ibrahimi [Hebron] Mosque massacre and the subsequent massacres, particularly in the Jabaliyah camp in the Gaza Strip where a number of PLO cadres were assassinated by the Israeli forces, and affirmed the urgent need to achieve tangible progress for the sake of implementing the transitional phase agreement. The spokesman added that the PLO hoped that all the concerned sides would work toward living up to their responsibilities to protect the peace process and achieve the aims for which it started.
