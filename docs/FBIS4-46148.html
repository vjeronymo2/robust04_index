BFN [Text] Asyut, 20 May (MENA)--Today, in a security sweep in Asyut, Upper Egypt, security forces caught 17 fugitive terrorists whose cases had been recently tried in various areas of Asyut. The sweep included villages and urban centers in Sadafa and al-Ghana'im and involved over 1,000 soldiers and officers. A total of 25 automatic rifles, 12 semi-automatic rifles, 10 revolvers, 15 local-made pistols, and 876 rounds were seized. Also, during the sweep, 10 previously convicted for felonies, five for embezzlement, four in narcotics cases, and 10 minors were arrested.
