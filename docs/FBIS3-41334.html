Language: Spanish Article Type:CSO [Article by Ignacio Gutierrez A.] [Excerpts] Another spectacular blow was dealt to the drug traffic yesterday by the PGR [Office of the Attorney General of the Republic] when, in antidrug operations conducted in several states of the Federation, it seized 4,164 kg of dried and packed marijuana. A gun battle occurred in one of them, without casualties. During the operations, Federal Judicial Police agents arrested 28 presumed drug traffickers. The value of the confiscated drugs exceeds 40 million new pesos. The federal agents, under orders from Commander Adrian Carrera, also destroyed 90,420 marijuana plants located on five plantations in Sinaloa, Veracruz, and Nayarit States. According to the police reports, the most significant operation took place in Sonora State, where federal agents arrested Roberto Palomera Hernandez, driver of a tractor trailer truck with Federal Public Service plates. Found in the vehicle, which had arrived from Guamuchil, Sinaloa, were 1,000 kg of marijuana, bound for the town of Tijuana, Baja California. [passage omitted] One plantation was destroyed in Sinaloa State, and another in the settlement of Cospita, in the township of Baila, Culiacan. [passage omitted]
