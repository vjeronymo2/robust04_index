November 12, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY: CONSUMER NEWS

Mazda Recalls Somes RX-7s: Mazda voluntarily recalled 2,708 RX-7s after it was told by air-bag maker TRW that there might be three defective bags among the sports cars made between Nov. 1, 1989, and July 2, 1990, a Mazda spokesman said.

Owners are being notified by mail to take their cars to Mazda dealers for inspections. If necessary, air bags will be replaced at no cost. Mazda said it was not aware of any injuries.
