921012 FT 12 OCT 92 / Construction Contracts: Residences for Brunel students NUGENT has been awarded contracts for both the public and private sectors totalling Pounds 10m. These include a Pounds 5m design and build contract for Brunel University to erect 400 student residences and a Pounds 2m contract for Co-operative Retail Services for 50,000 sq ft of car showrooms at Bedford. Other contracts include major refurbishment works for the Victoria and Albert Museum and Imperial College and a Pounds 1m contract for work at Wormwood Scrubs prison
