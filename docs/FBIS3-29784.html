Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] President Le Duc Anh received here this afternoon Mr. Juwana, ambassador of the Republic of Indonesia who paid him a farewell visit at the end of his term of office in Vietnam. Speaking to his guest, President Le Duc Anh highly appreciated the ambassador's contributions to the consolidation and promotion of the friendship and cooperation between Vietnam and Indonesia, and expressed his belief that the friendship and cooperation beween the two countries would be further developed in the interests of the two peoples, for peace, stability and development in the region. President Anh asked Ambassador Juwana to convey his regards to President Suharto and wished the Indonesia people happiness and prosperity. For his part, Mr. Jawana expressed his sincere thanks to the Vietnamese Government and people for creating favourable conditions for him to fulfil his mission in Vietnam.
