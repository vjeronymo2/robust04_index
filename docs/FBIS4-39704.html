BFN [POSTFACTUM report under the "From Our Correspondents and News Agencies" rubric: "Ukraine Will Repay Gas Debts in Apartments"] [Text] Ukraine is obligated to build 145,000 square meters of housing in Russia to pay off its debt to the "Gazprom" Russian joint-stock company. As the "Gazprom" Russian joint-stock company reported, the Ukrainian debt for gas already supplied amounts to $800 million. Ukraine is obligated to build housing for citizens working in the north in the gas-production industry to pay off this debt. Financial repayment of the debt has for the moment been postponed.
