March 22, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ABORTION PILL

To me it doesn't make any difference whether Atty. Gen. John K. Van de Kamp's announcement regarding RU 486 was politically motivated or not; it was still a brilliant idea.

Here we are living in supposedly the most industrialized nation in the world and are stopped by a few religious zealots. Hopefully, this will be something the state can proudly point to as a progressive move not only for women, but for all people suffering breast cancer, prostate cancer and Cushing's syndrome.

MRS. KAY STRATE

Fountain Valley
