Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] We switch to the National Assembly Hall, where the results of the no-confidence vote will be announced. National Assembly Chairman Aleksandur Yordanov is giving the floor to Khristo Markov, who will announce the results: [Markov] Today, 9 February, the National Assembly conducted a secret vote on the proposal to vote on confidence in the cabinet in connection with the failure of the cabinet to implement its duties regarding the citizens' security and the public law and order. The no-confidence vote was proposed on 28 January by the parliamentary faction of the Union of Democratic Forces [SDS]. A commission composed by Khristo Markov, Ruslan Semerdzhiev, Mariela Miteva, and Kiril Zhelev announces the following results of the no-confidence vote: Some 228 National Assembly members took part in the vote. Some 89 ballots were cast in support of the no-confidence vote. Some 135 ballots were cast against the no-confidence proposal. Three envelopes were found empty, and one contained more than one ballot. On the basis of the voting results, and on Article 81, Paragraphs 1 and 2 of the Bulgarian Constitution, and Paragraph 4 of the additional instructions on the activity of the National Assembly, the no-confidence vote in the cabinet has been rejected.
