Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 2 (XINHUA) -- Enhancing the build-up of the Communist Party of China (CPC) is a key to China's further development and to building socialism with Chinese characteristics, a party leader said here today. Hu Jintao, member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party (CPC), made the remarks at a forum to mark the fifth anniversary of the birth of the magazine, "THE STUDY OF PARTY CONSTRUCTION." The magazine is run by the Organization Department of the CPC Central Committee. Hu praised the magazine's progress in the study of the party's construction and publicity work over the past five years and urged the magazine to make new contributions in its field. He said that in the new period, there were many experiences and problems in the construction of the party which call for study with a long-term view and with an overview of the whole international and domestic situation. He stressed the role of the party's leadership in the improvement of the party's construction. Other Chinese leaders -- Jiang Zemin, Li Peng and Qiao Shi -- wrote messages to the magazine.
