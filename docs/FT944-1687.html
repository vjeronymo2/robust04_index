941219 FT 19 DEC 94 / Company News (This Week and Next): Eurotherm - Other companies The industrial process control equipment supplier has performed well in recent years by cutting costs and improving productivity. Now investors are looking for evidence that the management under Mr Claes Hultman, chief executive, can boost sales. Confirmation that order book strength has been translated into higher sales could come when Eurotherm reports its results tomorrow for the 12 months to October 31. These should show pre-tax profits up from Pounds 20.1m to about Pounds 26m.
