930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / Management (The Growing Business): Spanish lessons in 'match-making' - In A Nutshell Businesses seeking contacts in Spain may benefit from Enterprise Galicia '93, a European Commission- sponsored initiative to present this region of north-west Spain to the European business community. Participants will have the opportunity to meet representatives of up to 300 Galician businesses at the two-day event on October 21 and 22 in Santiago de Compostela. Thirty-minute 'match-making' meetings can be arranged in advance. The association of British Chambers of Commerce (BCC) plans to lead to a UK delegation and will hold a briefing for interested businesses in Coventry on October 5. Contact BCC, 4 Westwood House, Westwood Business Park, Coventry CV4 8HS. Tel. 0203 694484.
