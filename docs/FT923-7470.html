920818 FT 18 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Lilleshall sale will cut gearing Lilleshall, a distributor of fasteners and engineers' tools, has exchanged contracts for the sale of the remaining part of its industrial investment property at Tipton for Pounds 1.32m. Following the sale, together with the other part of the Tipton site for Pounds 800,000 in June, Lilleshall's current gearing is anticipated to be under 25 per cent.
