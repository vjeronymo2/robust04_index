September 8, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

KID'S PAPER

Why do you make newspapers so boring for kids? Kids would like a section for themselves.

There's too much junk like fires and accidents on the freeways in the newspaper. Kids would like a page on their own futures. Kids get tired of the stuff grown-ups like. Kids like toys, bikes and skateboards.

Grown-ups like shopping for ugly stuff. Grown-ups like boring business books.

COURTNEY COOPER

Age 8

Altadena
