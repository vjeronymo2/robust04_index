940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Davenport Knitwear Davenport Knitwear, the Birmingham-based maker of knitted fabrics and garments, lifted pre-tax profits by 49 per cent in the year to December 31. On turnover up 12 per cent to Pounds 9.73m (Pounds 8.65m) profits rose from Pounds 1.45m to Pounds 2.15m pre-tax. Earnings per share emerged at 84.5p (56.7p) and the proposed dividend is raised from 8.525p to 9.2p.
