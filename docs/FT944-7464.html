941124 FT 24 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Abtrust High Income Abtrust High Income Trust, which aims to provide quarterly income with potential for capital and income growth, has declared a second interim dividend of 1.4p. The total dividend so far of 2.8p is in line with the forecast in the prospectus. Net revenue for the period from the trust's incorporation in February to September 30 was Pounds 1m. Net assets per share stood at 89.25p, with earnings per share of 3.28p.
