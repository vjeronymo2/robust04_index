940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): Williams Holdings - Other companies Interim profits of about Pounds 90m are expected on Thursday. With a hefty chunk of business dependent of housebuilding, investors will be looking for signs of some pick-up in underlying trading in both the US and UK. After April's Pounds 267m rights issue, observers want to see signs that the more conservative strategy of bolt-on acquisitions is working.
