BFN [Text] Two opposition Knesset factions, the Likud and the National Religious Party [NRP], are due to submit to the parliamentary secretariat the 30 signatures required for a special plenary session. It would take up the issue of the deployment of the Palestinian police in the territories. NRP Knesset Member Hanan Porat told Qol Yisra'el that the urgent session was being called because the house had not been given any details about the makeup and strength of the force, not its equipment. Porat said that facts were being created on the ground without the knowledge and approval of the Knesset.
