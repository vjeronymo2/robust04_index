BFN [Text] The popular resistance in Shu'ayb led by fighters of the liberation war with great courage and heroism repulsed yesterday the attacks of the gang of the al-Ahmar clan, inflicting on the enemy huge human and materiel losses. The popular resistance managed to kill a number of enemy officers and men of the invading forces. Among those killed was staff Colonel Muhammad 'Abdallah Haydar al-Sanhani, a relative of the leader of the gang of the al-Ahmar gang, who was [words indistinct] al-Hamzah and had been charged with leading the attack on Dhamar and the liquidation of al-Dali' and Shu'ayb. The popular resistance found in his possession orders from 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih charging him with the destruction of al-Dali' and Shu'ayb and killing their inhabitants without mercy, looting their possessions, and [word indistinct] their land, all of which is indicative of the savagery of the criminal and inhuman operations being conducted by the gang of the al-Ahmar clan and its (?hungry) retinue.
