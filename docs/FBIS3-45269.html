Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Summary] Shandong Province has succeeded in improving its auto industry to a new level thanks to vigorously readjusting the mix of products, introducing advanced technologies from foreign countries, and setting up technical and economic associations with fraternal provinces. In 1993 the province realized a total output of 7.68 billion yuan, which showed a 38 percent increase over 1992 and realized 740 million yuan of profits and taxes, which showed a 46 percent increase over 1992. The sale rate of products reached more than 95 percent. During the year, the province turned out 176 varieties of new products. Of these varieties, four reached the world standard; 11 filled the national gaps in this regard; and 35 reached the national advanced standards. The rate of output value of these new products reached 31.7 percent which surpasses that of 1992 by 10 percent.
