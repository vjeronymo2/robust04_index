BFN [Text] The second round of Tajikistan's peace talks will commence on 18 June in Tehran. The office of the UN secretary general announced that the talks, which are aimed at achieving national reconciliation in Tajikistan, will be chaired by Ramero Piriz Ballon, an envoy of the secretary general. In addition to Tajik Government authorities and the government opposition groups, the representatives of the Islamic Republic of Iran, Russia, Pakistan, Afghanistan, Kazakhstan, Kyrgystan, Uzbekistan, and CSCE will attend the talks.
