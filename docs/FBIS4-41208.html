BFN [Text] The CONTACT news agency provided us with information from the Defense Ministry press center that work is continuing to reduce the Defense Ministry's structure and improve its efficiency. Several deputy defense minister positions were abolished. A new position of first deputy defense minister of the Republic of Georgia was created, who is to be responsible for rear front construction and troop disposition [zurgis mshenebloba da jarebis gantavseba]. By a decree of the Georgian head of state, Colonel Razhden Bitsadze has been appointed first deputy defense minister of the Republic of Georgia responsible for rear front construction and troop disposition. By a decree of the head of state, Major General Giorgiy Vashakidze has been appointed deputy defense minister of the Republic of Georgia. By a decree of the head of state, Maj. Gen. Boris Ivanov has been appointed head of the Army Service Department [sajariso samsakhuris gankopileba] of the General Staff of the Defense Ministry of the Republic of Georgia.
