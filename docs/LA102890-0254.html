October 28, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ALSO WORTH WATCHING

John Larkin, the actor who was the voice of Perry Mason on the radio series,guests on the TV version of Perry Mason (Monday at noon on KDOC) as a sculptorsuffering from a midlife crisis.

It wouldn't be Halloween without an Alfred Hitchcock Presents Marathon Week(Monday-Saturday 8 p.m-6 a.m. on Nickelodeon).

Jessica Fletcher (Angela Lansbury) leaves Cabot Cove and "Murder, She Wrote" fora trip to Honolulu to help Thomas Magnum nab a hired assassin on Magnum, P.I.(Wednesday at 4 p.m. on KTLA).

John Ford Noonan, John Masius and Tom Fontana received an Emmy for scripting"The Women" episode of St. Elsewhere (Thursday at 7 p.m. in KDOC), about threepatients with different illnesses who share a room at St. Elgius. Veteranactress Eva LaGallienne guests.

Charles Ingalls brings his family to Plum Creek, Minn., on the premiere episodeof Little House on the Prairie (Friday at 11 a.m. on KTLA).

Jack Nicholson drops by Mayberry on the "Opie Finds a Baby" episode of The AndyGriffith Show (Saturday at 2:05 p.m. on TBS).

Bonnie Franklin appears on The Munsters (Saturday at 1:05 p.m. on TBS) whenHerman, put in a trance by Grandpa to get rid of his hiccups, is mistaken for amannequin by two fraternity inductees who take him off to a sorority house.Franklin later went on, after starring in "One Day at a Time," to direct manyepisodes of the syndicated "The New Munsters."
