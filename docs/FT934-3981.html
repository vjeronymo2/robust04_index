931207 FT 07 DEC 93 / Indian bomb blasts Indian police tightened security in several states after a series of bomb blasts on four Delhi-bound express trains and one train from the capital left two killed and 18 injured yesterday, writes Shiraz Sidhva in New Delhi. The campaign coincided with the first anniversary of the destruction by Hindu fanatics of a 400-year-old mosque in Ayodhya, northern India, The Home Ministry has ordered an inquiry into the blasts. Shops and schools in Moslem-dominated areas of Bombay closed yesterday to protest against the destruction of the mosque last year and to mourn the nearly 2,000 who died in ensuing riots.
