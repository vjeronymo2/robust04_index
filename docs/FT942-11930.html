940503 FT 03 MAY 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto prices closed higher, despite a slump among gold stocks. The TSE-300 composite index rose 15.21 points, or 0.36 per cent, to close at 4,282.33 but declining issues outnumbered advances 382 to 346. Volume was a record 109.35m shares, up from Friday's 55.06m. Today's volume,however, was boosted by several large blocks of the new class A non-voting shares of PWA Corp. These accounted for 54m.
