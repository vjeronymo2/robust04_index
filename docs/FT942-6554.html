940530 FT 30 MAY 94 / Motorists urged to leave cars at home Rush-hour motorists will this week be urged to leave their cars at home for at least one journey in five during June. The move is part of a campaign by the Association of Metropolitan Authorities. The campaign, which is backed by the Automobile Association, will be launched tomorrow by Mr Robert Key, the roads minister. The association said: 'In the same amount of space used by a car, 45 times as many people can travel by train, 18 times as many by foot, and seven-and-a-half times as many by bus.'
