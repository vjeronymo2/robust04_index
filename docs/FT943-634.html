940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Herald Inv Trust In its first results since incorporation on December 10 last year, Herald Investment Trust, which specialises in small communications companies, announced a decline in net asset value from 98.7p on February 16 to 95p on June 30. This 3.7 per cent decline compares with a 14.6 per cent fall in the FTSE-Small Cap Index. After tax of Pounds 80,000, profit stood at Pounds 234,000 to give earnings per share of 0.36p.
