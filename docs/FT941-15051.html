940118 FT 18 JAN 94 / Call for cheaper Eurofighter The German and Spanish defence ministers said yesterday they would press for a lower price for the four-nation Eurofighter combat aircraft, Reuter reports from Bonn. Mr Volker Ruhe of Germany and his Spanish counterpart, Mr Julian Garcia Vargas, said that they wanted the European consortium to sink the price for the new fighter aircraft below current estimates of DM102m (Pounds 40m) per unit.
