Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Gligorov's Message to Demirel"] [Text] The President of the Republic of Turkey Suleyman Demirel received in Ankara the Macedonian Ambassador Trajan Petrovski. The latter delivered a message from the Macedonian President Kiro Gligorov and informed Mr. Demirel on Macedonian economic losses due to the latest embargo imposed by a Greek Government decision. President Demirel repeated Turkey's readiness to aid Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] in its efforts to cope with difficulties.
