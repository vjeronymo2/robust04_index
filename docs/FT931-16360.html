930107 FT 07 JAN 93 / Observer: Buba relaxes Proof that the Bundesbank president is human after all? Officially in Oslo yesterday to hold forth again on the subject of German monetary policy, Schlesinger's thoughts had been on higher things. He had arrived secretly a day earlier intent on renewing his acquaintance with childhood hero Birger Ruud, the two-time double Olympic ski-jumping gold medallist. A celebrity in Germany in the 1930s, Ruud had once recounted his tales to a Bavarian audience that included the bright-eyed 12-year-old Helmut. Nearly 60 years later, Europe's monetary Cerberus requested another meeting. So Ruud gave him the grand tour of his ski museum and presented him with a Norwegian winter Olympic gold coin.
