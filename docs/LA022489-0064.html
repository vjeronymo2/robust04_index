February 24, 1989, Friday, Southland Edition

'BOMB' WAS JUST A BAG

The U.S. Embassy in Ottawa received a bomb threat Thursday, but all Canada's Royal Canadian Mounted Police found outside was a plastic grocery bag, which they promptly blasted with a remote-controlled shotgun.

Officers found the remnants of a Bible, other religious books, a pie and a straw crucifix inside the bag.
