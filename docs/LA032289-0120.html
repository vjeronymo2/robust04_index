March 22, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

SOUTH COUNTY;

YWCA WILL HONOR 10 WOMEN OF ACHIEVEMENT

The South Orange County YWCA will honor 10 Women of Achievement at the YWCA's annual Celebration of Excellence and Dinner at the Irvine Hilton and Towers on March 30.

This year the YWCA inaugurates the "Y's Guy" Award, which honors a man who has demonstrated outstanding support for the YWCA's effort to bring continuing awareness of the roles that women play in the county and the resources they provide. For information and reservations, call (714) 542-3577.
