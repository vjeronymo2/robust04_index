930505 FT 05 MAY 93 / Irish American Factfile Over 40m US citizens claim Irish descent - about 10 times the population of Ireland, north and south The US is the single most important source of international investment for Northern Ireland In the last five years US companies have invested over Pounds 850m in Northern Ireland, creating over 4,400 new jobs Some 38 US companies now employ over 8,500 people in the province The US companies employ about 9% of the total manufacturing workforce in Northern Ireland
