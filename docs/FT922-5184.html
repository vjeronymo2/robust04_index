920602 FT 02 JUN 92 / People: Financial moves Andrew Buxton and Harry Littlefair have been appointed directors of the RIVER PLATE AND GENERAL INVESTMENT TRUST. ***** Gerry Moes is moving from Fuji International to become a director of HAMBROS international debt issues division; he replaces Gerard de Nadaillac who is to become md of Hambros France in September.
