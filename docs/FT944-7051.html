941126 FT 26 NOV 94 / Weekend Money (Financial Planning): Evaders beware - Briefcase What is the name of the Inland Revenue department that deals with tax evasion inquiries (corporate and otherwise) and where might it be contacted? The address of the Inland Revenue Inquiry Branch is Room 510, Alexandra House, Kingsway, London WC2B 6TR, tel: 071-438 6875. The special Trades Investigation Unit is in the same building, but its 'phone number is 438 7456. No legal responsibility can be accepted by the Financial Times for the answers given in these columns. All inquiries will be answered by post as soon as possible.
