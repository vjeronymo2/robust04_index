BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 21/4/1994 -- Israel's Minister of Foreign Affairs Shim'on Peres said he would continue Thursday [22 April] in Bucharest the talks with Yasir 'Arafat. A first round of talks took place last night until 0300 in the morning. Shim'on Peres said that during the talks the sides are trying to assess the latest progress made in the Israeli-Palestinian negotiations. We feel we are very close to a conclusion and we want to translate the conclusions of the negotiations into the practical achievement of a new reality in the Middle East, Shim'on Peres said, adding that when the few problems still awaiting negotiation in Cairo are settled, a conclusion of the negotiations would be sought.
