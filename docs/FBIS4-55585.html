BFN [Text] Mariofil Ljubic, chairman of the Constituent Assembly of the Federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina, has convened a session of the Constituent Assembly of Bosnia-Herzegovina for 30 May this year. In accordance with the Constitution of the Federation and the Vienna agreement, the constituent session will adopt several acts from the sphere of competence of the Constituent Assembly. At that session, the deputies will also elect the president and vice president of the Federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina in the transitional period. The Constituent Assembly will begin work at 1100. After that session, on the same day the Assembly of the Republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina will hold its session.
