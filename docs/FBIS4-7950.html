BFN [Staff report: "Neftokhim Concludes $5 Million Deal With Macedonia"] [Text] During their visit to Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM], the chiefs of Neftokhim signed a contract on the sale of car gasoline, propane-butane, diesel, and reactive fuel to the tune of $5 million. After Greece imposed its embargo Neftokhim remains the sole supplier of fuel to Macedonia.
