BFN [Unattributed report: "Stevo Crvenkovski: `Little Can Be Done Under Pressure'"] [Text] After the talks with Matthew Nimetz, Macedonia's [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] Foreign Minister Stevo Crvenkovski stated that Macedonia was satisfied that the United States has its own mediator in the Macedonian-Greek dispute, who was in tight coordination with the UN mediator Cyrus Vance. "This way, President Clinton will have the opportunity to become closer acquainted with the dispute between Macedonia and Greece. We believe that the best place for the continuation of the negotiations is the United Nations. This way we have a process which can eventually result in the continuation of the negotiations. Under the pressure of embargo, little can be done."
