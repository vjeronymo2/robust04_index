BFN [Text] Havana, 14 May (PL) -- A delegation of the Vietnamese Communist Party Central Committee has begun an official visit to Cuba to exchange views and to promote even closer bilateral relations. The delegation arrived on 13 May at the invitation of the Cuban Communist Party. Nguyen Van Hon, who is responsible for the southern provinces, is heading the delegation. The Vietnamese visitors will tour agricultural, biotechnological, and health centers as well as the scientific area on the outskirts of Havana. They will also visit points of interest in Matanzas Province. Reports indicated that the delegation will meet with officials of the Cuban Friendship with the Peoples Institute, among other organizations.
