February 25, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

WOMEN'S BASKETBALL ROUNDUP;

NELSON SCORES 35 TO LEAD TROJANS

Center Cherie Nelson scored 35 points to lead the USC women's basketball team over Washington State, 87-67, Friday night in a Pacific 10 game at USC's North Gym.

Tammy Hammond scored 16 points for the Trojans (10-14 overall and 6-8 in the Pac-10), and Tammy Story had 14 points, six assists and six steals. For the Cougars (12-13, 5-10), Angie Miller had 16 points and nine rebounds.

Washington 64, UCLA 62 -- Despite career highs of 32 points and 18 rebounds by UCLA's Sandra VanEmbricqs, the Bruins (11-13, 7-7) lost to the Huskies (19-9, 12-3) in a Pac-10 game at Pauley Pavilion.
