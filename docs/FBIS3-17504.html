Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Sevindzh Abdullayeva and Viktor Shulman] [Text] Baku March 28 TASS -- Azerbaijani troops rebuffed an Armenian offensive in the direction of Agdam in the disputed enclave of Nagorno-Karabakh on Monday, the Azerbaijani Defence Ministry said. The Armenians had to retreat, sustaining heavy losses, from two villages they had seized earlier -- Shirvanly and Garagashly. Fighting is now continuing ten kilometers northeast of Agdam. The ministry said that the enemy continued shelling villages in northwestern Azerbaijan. In a separate report, the press centre of the Nagorno-Karabakh defence army said earlier today that heavy fighting continued throughout last night and most of the day on Monday after Azerbaijani troops launched an offensive in the direction of Agdam. The press centre said the operation involved tanks and aviation. Reports from Stepanakert, capital of Nagorno-Karabakh, said that Karabakh fighters were repelling the attack, inflicting severe damage on the enemy.
