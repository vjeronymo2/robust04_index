920530 FT 30 MAY 92 / MFI breached consumer law MFI, THE furniture retailer, was yesterday convicted of falsely claiming it was reducing the price of its stock. MFI Furniture Centres denied at Swansea magistrates' court nine breaches of consumer protection legislation, but was convicted on all counts. The company was fined Pounds 2,000 on each count and ordered to pay Pounds 8,970 costs. Mr Neil Palfreeman, MFI marketing director, admitted that at a store at Llansamlet, Swansea, a suite priced at Pounds 1,289.99 was given a 30 per cent discount, but told the court: 'It was never sold at that pre-sale price.'
