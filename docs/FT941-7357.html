940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / House break The Commons will have an unusually long early summer break - from Friday May 27 to Monday June 13 - it was announced. The Lords will rise a day earlier on Thursday May 26 - but return a week earlier than MPs on Monday June 6. The Easter breaks start on Thursday March 31 for both houses. The Commons returns on Tuesday April 12 and the Lords on April 11.
