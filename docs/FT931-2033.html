930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa GOLD shares held onto earlier gains in spite of some profit-taking as the bullion price rose above Dollars 333 an ounce. The index advanced 24 or 2.1 per cent to 1,163. The overall index was 2 better at 3,530 but industrials slipped 17 to 4,487.
