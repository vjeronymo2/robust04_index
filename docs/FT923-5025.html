920903 FT 03 SEP 92 / Observer: Debatable Heads of state are worth 10 times as much as journalists. This little nugget is one of the more interesting statistics to emerge from the Non-Aligned Movement conference in Jakarta. Apparently, the Indonesian government has taken out a life and medical insurance policy which provides hacks attending the conference with maximum cover of Dollars 11,500. For heads of state the figure is Dollars 114,000.
