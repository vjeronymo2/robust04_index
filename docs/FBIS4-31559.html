BFN [Text] The new government will also have members who have already gained experience in the Miklos Nemeth government, said Imre Szekeres, [executive deputy chairman of the Hungarian Socialist Party, MSZP], speaking to the "Napteve" program. At the same time the MSZP executive deputy chairman added that those younger experts and politicians who have only recently become known in the MSZP will also be considered in the course of the distribution of portfolios. The most important qualification in the ministerial appointments will be suitability, Imre Szekeres confirmed.
