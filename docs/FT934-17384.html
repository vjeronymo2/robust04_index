931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / The Labour Party Conference: AEEU comes into the TUC fold THE AEEU engineering and electrical union becomes fully affiliated to the TUC today. Welcoming the move, Mr John Monks, the TUC's general secretary, said that the 'outstanding problems with affiliate unions involving the AEEU's electrical section' would 'continue to be dealt with constructively'.
