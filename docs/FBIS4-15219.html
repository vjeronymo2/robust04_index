BFN [Text] Tbilisi, April 6 (SNARK) -- Georgia takes an active part in the process of ensuring cooperation among the security services of the Black Sea basin countries. This declaration was made yesterday by the Chairman of State Security Service of Georgia (the former Ministry of Security) Igor Giorgadze at the press-conference held in Tbilisi. In his words, the relations with Russia are extremely important here. He said, in particular, that the Service headed by him is going to conclude agreements on cooperation with Russian counterintelligence and reconnaissance services, President's Security and Russian frontier troops in the near future. Georgia counts on Russia's understanding of mutual interests in the cause of ensuring security of the two states, concluded Igor Giorgadze.
