Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Ricardo Alarcon, president of Cuba's National Assembly of the People's Government, headed a delegation that arrived in Brussels today to carry out an extensive work program. Alarcon will be in Belgium until (?next month). During his stay, he will meet with leaders from the Belgian and European Parliaments, leaders of European political parties, and heads of nongovernmental organizations. Alarcon will also participate in a meeting with Belgian businessmen and in activities organized by associations for solidarity with our country.
