BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Roman Zadunaiskiy] [Text] Moscow April 8 TASS -- The command of the Black Sea Navy has asked the Ukrainian Defence Ministry to pay before April 10 all the debts of the Ukrainian military units which consume electric power through the Black Sea Navy, as well as the debts of Ukrainian Defence Ministry customers to the building department of the Black Sea Navy. The telegram, addressed to Ukrainian Defence Minister General Vitaliy Radetskiy, says that on March 28 the debts of Ukrainian units to the Black Sea Navy amounted to 4 billion karbovanets for the consumed electricity, and 16,709 billion karbovanets for the completed construction jobs. The cable says that if the debt was not paid before April 10, "the Black Sea Navy Command will be compelled to consider the question of stopping all building and assembly works, carried out by the Black Sea Navy construction department for the Ukrainian Defence Ministry, and of switching over all the Ukrainian units, consuming electric power through the Black Sea Navy, to their own power sources". "The Black Sea Navy Command holds," the cable says, "that the Ukrainian Defence Ministry has breached the summit agreements on the joint financing of the Black Sea Navy and is trying to solve its own problems at the expense of the Russian Navy".
