BFN [Excerpts] Cairo, 18 May (MENA) -- Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid returned to Cairo this afternoon from Damascus after a five-day visit to Syria during which he chaired the meetings of the 26th round of the Higher Coordination Committee in Damascus. [passage omitted] The Arab League secretary general affirmed that the League is doing all it can to stop the bloodshed in Yemen and that it will not stop its efforts in Yemen because the current situation there is very dangerous. The secretary general said the Arab League delegation was not able to hold contacts with Aden. He noted that he had made some contacts while in Damascus in an attempt to contain the Yemeni crisis.
