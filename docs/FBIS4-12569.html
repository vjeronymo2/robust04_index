BFN [Text] In a report from inside Iraq, our sources say that a group of the Islamic resistance threw three hand grenades at the al-Nasiriyah governor's headquarters during his meeting with security and intelligence officials. The sources say the resulting explosion inflicted material damage on the building, wounded a number of its guards, and prompted the governor and the regime's henchmen to hide in an underground shelter. In another development, the resistance dealt a destructive blow to the district officer's headquarters in al-Nasiriyah on the evening of 18 March. Various types of light and medium weapons and mortars were used. Our sources confirm that eight regime elements were killed and others wounded. Heavy damage was inflicted on the building, which the regime uses as a center for its mercenaries and criminals who monitor the citizens' movements and arrest them.
