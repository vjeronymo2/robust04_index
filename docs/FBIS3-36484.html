Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] In an interview with a ITAR-TASS reporter on Friday [4 February], the DPRK Ambassador to Russia Son Song-pil pointed out that the allegation that Russian nuclear physics scholars participated in Pyongyang's nuclear arms development is groundless and false. Stressing that the DPRK has never implemented any military nuclear program, he said that the DPRK has limited research projects to totally peace-oriented nuclear energy development. The DPRK ambassador pointed out that Japanese press organizations have spread such a false rumor.
