BFN [Text] Cairo, 6 May (MENA)--Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid described the current situation in Yemen as "extremely grave." In a statement to be published tomorrow, Saturday, by AL-WAFD, the mouthpiece of the opposition Wafd Party, Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid said every effort is being exerted to stop the fighting. He said the Egyptian initiative to resolve the Yemeni crisis aims to reach a solution, noting that this depends on acceptance by the Yemeni parties.
