920409 FT 09 APR 92 / UK Company News: EFG Pre-tax losses at EFG, the USM-quoted garden leisure concern, grew from Pounds 84,000 in the year to September 30 1990 to Pounds 3.06m for the 69 weeks to January 26 1992. Turnover shrank to Pounds 36.5m (Pounds 49.9m) and operating losses emerged at Pounds 1.91m (profits Pounds 976,000). An extraordinary charge of Pounds 405,000 related to the withdrawal from the company's forestry businesses. Losses per share were 16.22p (earnings 1.26p) and the final dividend is passed, leaving no payment for the 69 weeks (1.25p).
