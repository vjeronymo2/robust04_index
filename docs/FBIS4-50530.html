BFN [Text] Beijing, May 19 (XINHUA) -- China is willing to jointly explore ways of developing cooperative relations with the Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development (OECD), a Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman said here today. Spokesman Wu Jianmin made the remarks at a weekly news briefing here this afternoon in answer to the question, "it is reported that the OECD wishes to strengthen relations with China. What is China's attitude towards this organization? Are you willing to join this organization?" "We have taken note of this report," Wu said. "We have been enjoying good relations of cooperation with many member states of the OECD and we would also like to explore jointly with this organization ways of developing cooperative relations. "We are confident that it will be beneficial to both sides to develop cooperative relations on the basis of equality and mutual benefit," the spokesman noted.
