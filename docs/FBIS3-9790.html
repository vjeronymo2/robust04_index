Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, March 30 -- Visiting Canadian Minister of Foreign Affairs Andre Ouellet and Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak discussed economic cooperation between Poland and Canada. Prime Minister Pawlak stressed the need to develop direct economic contacts among firms and enterprises of the two countries. Pawlak and Ouellet also touched upon the issue of tourist exchange. They also agreed that a pilot programme on the exchange of Polish and Canadian youth will probably be launched soon. The Canadian side wants to intensify the exchange of youth with the countries of Eastern and Central Europe. Young people from Canada would come to Poland on the same principles as voluntaries [as received] from the U.S. Peace Corps. Polish youth would travel to Canada on the same principles.
