920316 FT 16 MAR 92 / Construction Contracts: Water supplies EC HARRIS has been appointed by the Sydney Water Board, Australia, to review tender documentation and estimates on four water-purification contracts with a total value of AusDollars 600m (Pounds 257m). They will be built and operated under a 'build own operate transfer' style contract with a transfer option at the end of the term. Three of the projects, Avon, MacArthur and Woronara, which are based inland from Sydney, will each supply 200 megalitres of drinking water a day to the city's suburbs. The largest project, Prospect, will have a joint module capacity of 600 megalitres a day to meet Sydney's water demand in 1997. Further modules will upgrade total plant capacity to 3000 megalitres a day to meet the expected demand in the year 2000.
