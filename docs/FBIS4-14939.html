BFN [Text] At today's Ukrainian Cabinet of Ministers session, the Ukrainian-Chinese Intergovernmental Commission for Matters of Trade and Economic Cooperation held its first sitting. Its main task is to lay the new foundations in order for trade and economic relations between the two states to develop. This was stated by Valeriy Shmarov, deputy prime minister and head of the Ukrainian side in the commission. The PRC machinery industry minister, who heads the commission's Chinese side, stressed the importance of his country developing trade and economic relations with Ukraine, which is China's long-standing and reliable partner.
