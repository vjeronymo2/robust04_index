920728 FT 28 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Wardle Storeys increases capacity Wardle Storeys, the plastics and safety equipment group, has acquired machinery from a Spanish PVC-coated fabrics manufacturer which had ceased production. Wardle has paid about Pounds 1m for the plant which will be installed in the Earby factory of its technical products arm. Mr Brian Lees, managing director of the division, said the acquisition would lead to improved production efficiencies and an improved range of products.
