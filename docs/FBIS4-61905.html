BFN [Text] Tskhinvali, 10 [date as received] -- IBERIA news agency was told at the press service of the Georgian Defense Ministry that on 9 May, at 2145, the situation in Samachablo region between the Russian Federation and Ossetia's peacekeeping troops deteriorated. Together with the local population, a battalion of Ossetia's peacekeeping troops carried out an assault on the Russian military servicemen attempting to disarm them. The highest state of alert was declared among the Russian peacekeeping troops. The tension lasted until 2400. The tension has now been removed and the situation is under control.
