940714 FT 14 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Aberforth Split net assets improve The net asset value per unit share of the Aberforth Split Level Trust stood at 271.2p at the 12 months ended June 30, up from 250.3p at the same date last year. Available revenue rose from Pounds 1.27m to Pounds 1.91m. Earnings per income share rose from 8.43p to 8.47p and a fourth interim dividend of 2.45p gives a total for the year of 8.45p (8.4p).
