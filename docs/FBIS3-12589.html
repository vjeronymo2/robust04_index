Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The District Labor Court in Tel Aviv is due to begin considering a request by the Bezeq [Telecommunications Company] management to issue a restraining order against workers who have adopted measures that have caused serious interruptions in telephone communications all over the country. Our correspondent Meron Tzur reports that the workers have deliberately cut telephone communications to hundreds of thousands of subscribers. Dozens of company workers stormed the office of the director general of the Telecommunications Ministry in Megdal Shalom in Tel Aviv at noon today, shouting loudly to prevent him from explaining the nature of the Bezeq Company's new license.
