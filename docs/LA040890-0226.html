April 8, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ANGELS '90;

PROFILING THE NEWCOMERS

RICK SCHU

Age: 28

Position: Infielder

Major league experience: Four years, 162 days

How obtained: Signed as free agent, Feb. 10, 1990.

Pertinent stats: Spent most of last season with Detroit, with 7 home runs and 21 RBIs in 98 games.

Comments: Right-handed hitter who once was tabbed as the replacement for Philadelphia third baseman Mike Schmidt. Can play third base, outfield and has been working out as a catcher. Schu does not hit for power, but is considered a good contact hitter.
