Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Thai navy has released 14 Cambodian sailors held after a skirmish in the Gulf of Thailand last month, a military source said yesterday. "They were released from our detention late on Tuesday," a senior officer based in Trat province said. He said the 14 Cambodians including their commander were handed over to Cambodian Vice Admiral Tea Win but declined to say why they had been freed. [passage omitted]
