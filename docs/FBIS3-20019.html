Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Report by K.P.] [Text] Turkish agents are moving freely in the free areas, monitoring every move of the National Guard. This is the police's evaluation after discovering evidence in Athalassa Forest during the search for the motorcycle used in Theofilos Yeoryiadhis' murder. Specifically, the police found a blue and white cigarette package of the Harman brand, which had the initials of the pseudostate [Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus] inscribed on it. There were 15 cigarettes inside the package, which was found near a National Guard military camp. Police are examining the package, which is believed to belong to Turkish secret service agents who systematically watch every move to and from the military camps.
