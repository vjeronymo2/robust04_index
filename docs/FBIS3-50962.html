Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Good Morning From Mexico" newscast] [Text] The Attorney General's Office has taken legal action against eight rebels arrested in Chiapas. The eight rebels have said they were led by Commander Marcos, a Guatemalan, and (Jesus Meza Yaladaqui), also a Guatemalan. The arrested were remanded under the charges of homicide, infliction of injuries, criminal association, and the use of regulation weapons exclusive to the Army. The First District judge in Tuxtla Gutierrez has remanded another group of 18 rebels.
