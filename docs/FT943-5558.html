940903 FT 03 SEP 94 / World News in Brief: Twist in opera saga The Paris Opera was instructed to pay sacked conductor Myung-Whun Chung FFr50,000 (Pounds 6,016) a day as long as it disobeys a court order to reinstate him. Chung was turned away from work despite a court order that he should have his job back pending a final ruling on his appeal against dismissal.
