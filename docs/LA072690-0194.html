July 26, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

FOR SALE BY OWNER: David A. Brown, convicted of murdering one wife and plotting to kill another, now is offering to sell his story for $200. . . . Why so cheap? Because he needs cash fast to move his "lady friend" into an apartment near County Jail for more convenient visits, Brown explains. . . . It would be a temporary arrangement. He'll be transferred to a state prison sometime in the next few months. Ted Johnson and Vince Sweeney
