Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 21 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received a greeting card and floral basket in the joint name of First Prime Minister Norodom Ranariddh and Second Prime Minister Hun Sen of the government of Cambodia, on his birthday. A member of the Secretariat of the Cambodian Government, authorized by the prime ministers, handed the card and basket to the Korean ambassador to Cambodia on Feb. 15.
