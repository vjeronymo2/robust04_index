February 12, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ANIMALS AS ACTORS

As I have read about the "Project X" controversy, I have become more and more curious about how animals that work in movies are treated.

According to the Los Angeles Times Magazine, Vicki Hearne's story was "The Trainers' Side of the Controversy."

Now, I would like to hear the other side.

WANDA BOWSER

Mission Hills
