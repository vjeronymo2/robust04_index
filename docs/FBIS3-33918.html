Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The president of the republic, Lieutenant General 'Umar al-Bashir, will attend the final session of the first nomads' conference at Atbara River today, in line with his current tour of Eastern State. Yesterday, His Excellency declared open Oseif harbor, on the Red Sea. In a speech to a rally there he said the revolution's interest in the area had been confirmed by the creation of the Governorate of Hala'ib in view of its richness in natural resources, which require the use of energies and capabilities to yield the required development of the area. He added that the area had suffered from neglect during past regimes, which had affected its progress. [passage omitted]
