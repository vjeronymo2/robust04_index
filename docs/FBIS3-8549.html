Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] New York, March 9 -- PAP has learned from British sources that Poland has offered to send an additional 150 soldiers to the former Yugoslavia. The decision was adopted in reply to an appeal of the UN secretary general calling for the strengthening of UNPROFOR [United Nations Protection Force] forces in Bosnia-Herzegovina. PAP has also learned that Polish General Jozef Chmiel will soon assume command of the northern sector of UNPROFOR. The Polish mission at the UN has not officially confirmed Poland's offer to send the additional soldiers.
