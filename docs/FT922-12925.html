920415 FT 15 APR 92 / Observer: Loose ends Rubber masks of British prime minister John Major are now on sale at Hamleys, the London toy shop - knocked down from Pounds 19.99 to just Pounds 10. Meanwhile, spotted among remainders on a book stall at London's Waterloo station - 'John Major: Prime Minister' - knocked down from Pounds 12.99 to Pounds 2.99. Hardly a vote of confidence.
