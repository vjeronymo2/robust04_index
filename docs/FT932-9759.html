930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Pittencrieff Pittencrieff, the Edinburgh-based oil explorer and mobile communications group which is in the midst of a hostile 17p per share bid for Aberdeen Petroleum, has acquired a further 6.62m shares, or 12.8 per cent, in its target. The latest acquisition lifts its stake in Aberdeen to 20.3m shares (39.4 per cent).
