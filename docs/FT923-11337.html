920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Shield reduces loss to Pounds 1.37m The Shield Group, residential property developer and estate agent, announced a reduced pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.37m for the year to March 31. The comparative loss was a restated Pounds 7.33m. Turnover improved to Pounds 10.6m (Pounds 7.1m). Net interest payable was Pounds 302,000 (Pounds 870,000) and there were exceptional costs of Pounds 460,000 (Pounds 3.58m). Losses fell to 20.8p (81p) and the directors said that a dividend was unlikely to be paid 'for some time'.
