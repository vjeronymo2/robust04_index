941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / International Company News: Ammeberg Mining - Correction The price range for the offering of shares in Ammeberg Mining by its parent, Union Miniere, is SKr112 to SKr130, not SKr103 to SKr112 as stated yesterday. This values Ammeberg at between SKr1.26bn and SKr1.46bn (Dollars 170m to Dollars 197m).
