930123 FT 23 JAN 93 / Underwriter dies of gun wound MR ROY BROMLEY, 71, a former underwriter of Lloyd's syndicate 475, has been found dead of a shotgun wound at his home in London. Police are not treating the death as suspicious. Mr Bromley's syndicate specialised in catastrophe reinsurance. Knightstone, the agency now managing the syndicate's affairs, recently estimated losses were at least Pounds 50m, double the syndicate's Pounds 25m capacity or capital base.
