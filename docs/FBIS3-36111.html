Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] A meeting of the Supreme Council Presidium of Tajikistan took place yesterday. Issues related to the new structure of the Cabinet of Ministers of the republic were discussed. By a proposal from Abdujalil Samadov, the head of the government, the Supreme Council Presidium of Tajikistan approved Mahmadsaid Ubaydulloyev, Mayonsho Nazarshoyev, [name indistinct] Shokirov, and Munavarsho Nazriyev to the posts of deputy chairmen of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Tajikistan. Ramazon Mirzoyev was appointed as administrator of the affairs of the Council of Ministers of the republic. The decrees on appointing the above-mentioned officials are signed by Imamali Rakhmanov, the head of state and chairman of the Supreme Council of the Republic of Tajikistan. Similarly, the ministers of the Republic of Tajikistan were appointed yesterday. Rustam Mirzoyev is appointed as the economy and forecasting minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Mahmad Yunusov is appointed as the finance minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Izatullo Hayoyev is appointed as the foreign economic relations minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Shavkat Umarov is appointed as the industry minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Odil Ochilov is appointed as the construction minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. [First name not given] Nazirov is appointed as the grain products minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Fariddun Muhiddinov is appointed as the transport minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Bozgul Dodkhudoyeva is appointed as the education minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Ato Khojayev is appointed as the culture minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Alamkhon Ahmadov is appointed as the health minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Saydullo Khayrulloyev is appointed as the Environmental Protection and Water minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Saidamir Zuhurov is appointed as the security minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Bobokhon Mahmadov is appointed as the press and information minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Shukurjon Zuhurov is appointed as the labor and employment minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Ibrohim Usmonov is appointed as the communication minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Aleksandr Shishlyannikov is appointed as the defense minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Yokub Salimov is appointed as the internal affairs minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Shavkat Ismoilov is appointed as the justice minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Abdusattor Jabborov is appointed as the social security minister of the Republic of Tajikistan. Bahoviddin Zuhurddin is appointed as the chairman of the State Committee for Construction and Architecture of the Republic of Tajikistan. [First name not given] Davlatov is appointed as the chairman of the State Committee for State Property of the Republic of Tajikistan.
