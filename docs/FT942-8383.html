940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Ocean Wilsons dips to Pounds 5.88m Ocean Wilsons Holdings saw pre-tax profits decline by 8 per cent, from Pounds 6.38m to Pounds 5.88m, over the 1993 year. The London-quoted investment holding company, now incorporated in Bermuda following a change of domicile in 1992,lifted net assets per share from 83.63p to 97.51p. Earnings per share emerged at 10.46p (7.99p). A proposed gross final dividend of 3p maintains the total at 4p.
