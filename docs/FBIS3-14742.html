Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday, Ukraine's Foreign Affairs Ministry disseminated a statement resolutely condemning the bloody event, which took place in the town of Hebron when Palestinians were at prayer. The statement by Ukraine's foreign policy department expresses deep sympathy to the families of those who died and the hope that the injured will recover. It emphasizes that any terrorist actions, whatever their aim might be, and whoever they are carried out by, are not justified. Ukraine's Foreign Affairs Ministry has appealed to the Palestinian and Israeli sides with a call not to resort to self-seeking emotions, and expressed the hope for a continuation of the Arab-Israeli dialogue with the aim of achieving a peaceful settlement in the Near East.
