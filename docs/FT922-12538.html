920416 FT 16 APR 92 / Observer: Unruffled Meanwhile one person who wasn't worried by Lloyd's building's narrow escape from the City of London bomb, was the controversial pile's designer Sir Richard Rogers. Winterthur Insurance's UK chief Andrew Fleming-Williams, flying back from skiing to his own wrecked offices, saw Rogers in Geneva and inquired how his master-work had fared. He hadn't even heard about the bomb.
