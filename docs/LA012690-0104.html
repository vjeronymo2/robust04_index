January 26, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

QUAYLE COUNTRY? Orange County adores conservatives but is oddly cool toward Dan Quayle, according to the Quayle Quarterly. . . . That's right: It's a new East Coast newsletter devoted entirely to the vice president.

Issue No. 1 quotes local Republican campaign pros Eileen Padberg and Harvey Englander. "Orange County and Dan Quayle are both white bread. There should be a great fit there, but there isn't," Englander said. Padberg said local Republicans "are loyal, but not stupid. . . . Quayle may very well be qualified to be president in eight years, but he will have to prove it." Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
