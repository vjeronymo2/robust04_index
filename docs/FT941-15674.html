940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / Shevardnadze visits Turkey Improved relations between Georgia and Turkey would help peace and stability in the Caucasus, Mr Eduard Shevardnadze, Georgia's president, said yesterday, Reuter reports from Ankara. Mr Shevardnadze, who arrived in Ankara for a two-day official visit, met President Suleyman Demirel before the two leaders headed their delegations in official talks. 'I am hoping this visit will produce positive consequences for peace in the Caucasus,' Mr Shevardnadze said.
