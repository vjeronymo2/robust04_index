June 10, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

6 HURT IN NEWPORT AS 2 CARS COLLIDE

Six people were hurt Friday night in a two-car collision that caused one vehicle to overturn at Jamboree Road and Coast Highway, police reported Saturday.

The accident happened about 8 p.m. when a Dodge Colt was turning left in the intersection in front of a Chevrolet Nova. Although two victims were airlifted to emergency hospitals, none of the injuries turned out to be life-threatening, police said.

The rest of the injured were taken to Hoag Hospital, where they were treated and released. Police made no arrests.
