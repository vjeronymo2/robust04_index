940719 FT 19 JUL 94 / UK Company News: TDG sells stake in Portuguese offshoot Transport Development Group is selling for Pounds 1.6m its 60 per cent stake in Transportes J Amaral to members of the Amaral family from whom it purchased the holding in 1989. Based in Estarreja, between Lisbon and Oporto, Amaral is engaged in the transport of chemical products in Portugal and general international haulage. In 1993 Amaral's profits before interest and tax attributable to TDG were Pounds 250,000 and attributable assets at the year end were Pounds 1.42m.
