Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Faruq Qaddumi, head of the PLO Political Department, has called on Russia and the United States, the cosponsors of the Middle East process, to pressure Israel to expeditiously withdraw from Gaza and Jericho. In his statement, Qaddumi said that Israeli behavior definitely proves that Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin is trying to avoid withdrawing the Israeli forces under security pretexts that are unacceptable and not in line with reality and facts.
