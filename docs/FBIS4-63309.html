BFN [Text] As we have already informed, a Belarusian military delegation headed by Defense Minister Pavel Kazlowski paid a one-day working visit to Lithuania on 19 May. The delegation held meetings with Seimas [parliament] Chairman C. Jursenas and Defense Minister L. Linkevicius. According to our correspondent in Vilnius, it was resolved to create working groups of both states for preparing documents on cooperation between the Lithuanian and Belarusian Ministries of Defense. During the news conference, held at the Lithuanian Seimas at the end of the visit, L. Linkevicius stated that the meeting with P. Kazlowski was devoted to issues related to the formation of a structure of both states' defense systems and other common problems. "Notwithstanding the fact that Belarus participates in the CIS collective system of security, there were no barriers to a normal dialogue," Linkevicius said.
