Section 4 of the Endangered Species Act (16 U.S.C. 1533) and regulations (50 CFR Part 424) promulgated to implement the Act set forth the procedures for adding species to the Federal endangered and threatened species lists. A species may be determined to be an endangered species due to one or more of the five factors described in section 4(a)(1). The threats facing these three species are summarized in Table 1.
