940718 FT 18 JUL 94 / Brazil wins its fourth World Cup Delighted Brazilian footballers hold aloft the World Cup trophy after the team defeated Italy 3-2 on penalties yesterday in Pasadena, California. It was the first championship final to be decided by a penalty shoot-out, after the match went to extra time with neither side able to score.
