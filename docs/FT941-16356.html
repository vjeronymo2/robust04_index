940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / Arsonist jailed for fraud attempt A businessman who set fire to his hotel in an attempted Pounds 2m insurance fraud was jailed for four years at Bristol crown court yesterday. In December Mr Mohammed Parvaz, of Fishponds, Bristol, was convicted of arson while being reckless as to whether life was endangered at the Waverley Hotel, Redland, Bristol - one of three hotels he owned in Bristol providing bed and breakfast accommodation for people on social security. Investigators found that the fire had been deliberately started with petrol. Mr Parvaz was seen leaving the hotel minutes before the outbreak.
