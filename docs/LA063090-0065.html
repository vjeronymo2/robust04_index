June 30, 1990, Saturday, Southland Edition

LE VOYAGEUR WINS BELMONT FEATURE

Le Voyageur led from start to finish for a 4 3/4-length victory over Any Minute Man in Friday's $31,000 High Gun purse for 3-year-olds and up at Belmont Park.

Poppiano finished a head behind Any Minute Man for show.

Le Voyageur, a four-year-old son of Seattle Slew, covered a mile on the fast track in 1:35 1/5 to earn $18,600 and return $5, $4 and $2.80.
