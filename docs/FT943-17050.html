940701 FT 01 JUL 94 / Roadbuilders get security allowance The Department of Transport is to allow construction companies which bid to build the Pounds 66m Newbury by-pass to include up to Pounds 1m in their proposals to cover the cost of site security. The cost of protecting road construction sites from environmental and transport protest groups has risen sharply in the past year and has provoked growing concern among civil engineering groups.
