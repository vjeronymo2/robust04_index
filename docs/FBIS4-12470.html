BFN [Text] Amman -- AL-DUSTUR has learned that from the premise of its belief in action and the formation of joint committees between it and the fraternal Arab countries, Jordan has completed for its part the formation of the joint coordination committees between it and the PLO. The following are the names of the members of these committees: 1. The Jordanian-PLO higher ministerial coordination committee under Sa'id al-Tall, deputy prime minister and minister of Higher Education, and the following as members: Jawad al-'Anani, minister of state for Cabinet Affairs and information minister; Dr. Ziyad Fariz, minister of planning; Mahdi al-Farhan, minister of Agriculture; Salamah Hammad, Interior Minister; and Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs. 2. The Jerusalem Committee is made up of the following: 'Izz al- Din al-Khatib al-Tamimi, his majesty's adviser and Chief Justice; Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-'Abbadi, minister of Awqaf and Islamic Affairs; and ambassador 'Awn al-Khasawinah, adviser to the crown prince. 3. The economic committee comprises the following: Dr. Safwan Tuqan, secretary general of the Planning Ministry; Dr. Michel Martu, Jordanian Central Bank deputy governor; Musa al-Jaghbir, Customs Department assistant director general, and Safwan al-Batayinah, director of the prime ministry's economic department. The committee on refugees and the displaced is made up of: Marwan Dudin; Khalil Khuraysat, Interior Ministry secretary general; Ambassador Nayif al-Hadid, Foreign Ministry secretary general; and Muhammad Shahinkari, from the Foreign Ministry. The committee on security and borders comprises Dr. 'Abdallah Tuqan, his majesty's adviser on scientific affairs; Major General Tahsin Shurdum, deputy chief of staff; and Major General Muhammad al-Bassul, Public Security Department assistant director. AL-DUSTUR has also learned that that the Palestinian side, as yet, has not communicated to Jordan the names of the Palestinian members on these committees.
