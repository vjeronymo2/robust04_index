921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / UK Company News: J Smart lines up with forecast PRE-TAX profits at J Smart (Contractors), the building and public works contractor, were in line with the minimum Pounds 3.25m forecast made in May. Profits worked through at Pounds 3.29m in the year to July 31, compared with Pounds 4.44m previously which included Pounds 682,000 exceptional surplus on sale of equity investments. Turnover declined 7 per cent to Pounds 13.9m. Earnings per share fell from 29.29p to 22.08p, but the dividend is raised from 7.95p to 8.5p, with a final of 6.2p.
