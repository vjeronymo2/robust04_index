BFN [Text] Moscow, April 8 (AFP) -- Russia wants to link its joining the NATO Partnership for Peace [PFP] to joining the G-7 group of industrial nations, presidency spokesman Viacheslav Kostikov said. "If we are invited into the political and military partnership, it would be logical to make an approach in the economic sphere," he told reporters. "Russia would like to join the G-7. Why not link the two problems," he added.
