BFN [Text] Hanoi [words indistinct] Another domestic air route, Hanoi-Na San (Son La Province) was reopened yesterday. This is the 20th air route of the national flag carrier Vietnam Airlines. To reopen (?the Hanoi)-Na San air service, Vietnam Airlines invested more than one million VND [Vietnamese dong] in building a new terminal and installing a new communication system. Together with the reoperation 10 days ago on the air service between Hanoi and Dien Bien Phu (Lai Chau Province) the Hanoi-Na San air route will meet the (?growing demands) for fast transport to serve tourism and business, thus helping develop socioeconomic activities in northern province.
