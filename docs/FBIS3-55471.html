Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Interfax reports from the zone of the Armenian-Azerbaijan conflict that yesterday and overnight Armenian subunits attempted a large-scale offensive. According to the Azerbaijan Defense Ministry, an offensive began yesterday evening and overnight in the direction of the populated areas of Bagbanlar, Marzili [several km southeast of Agdam], and Papravend [on border with Nagorno-Karabakh, north of Agdam]. At present, defensive battles are continuing there. At the same time, Nagorno-Karabakh's defense army press service reports that the Azerbaijani Army began an offensive this morning in the north and northeastern part of Mardakert Rayon in Nagorno-Karabakh. The Azerbaijani forces are using armored equipment and airplanes. Both sides are suffering casualties as a result of these battles.
