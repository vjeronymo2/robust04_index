930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Inchcape lifts Revasa stake INCHCAPE, the motor and businesses services group, has increased its stake in Revasa, the Italian insurance broker, through Bain Clarkson, its wholly owned insurance broking subsidiary. Bain Clarkson will pay a maximum of Pounds 1.7m, subject to performance, to lift its stake in Revasa from 10 per cent to 49 per cent. Bain Clarkson bought its initial 10 per cent shareholding in January last year.
