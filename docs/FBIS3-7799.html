Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Rome, 28 February (TANJUG) -- It has been confirmed in the American Air Force base in Aviano in northeastern Italy that two American F-16 fighters from this base downed four planes this morning at 0530, 55 miles east of Banja Luka. U.S. fighter planes that returned to the base at around 0600 fired six "air-to-air" missiles at a group of six planes, sources in Aviano said. The identity of the downed planes has not been officially confirmed yet, but it is presumed that they are Galeb planes belonging to the Serb republic air force.
