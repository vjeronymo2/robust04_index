930122 FT 22 JAN 93 / Technology: Projecting latest TV advances - Worth Watching Television addicts are in for a treat. They can now watch the latest TV programmes on a 100-inch screen using projection television technology developed by Sharp in Japan. The XV-710P projector has a short focal length which enables the lens to project a 60-inch picture from just two metres away or a 30-inch picture from just one metre. Videorecorders, laser disc players, camcorders or video games consoles can also be plugged into the back of the projector. It weighs 4.3kg and retails for Pounds 1,799.99. Sharp: Japan, 06 621 1221; UK, 061 205 4255.
