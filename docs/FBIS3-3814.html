Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 22 (CNS) -- Deputy of the NPC and director of the Party Literature Research Centre Mr. Pang Xianzhi said that Deng Xiaoping's Selected Works (1938-1965) and (1975-1982) would be renamed The First and Second Volume of Deng Xiaoping's Selected Works after revision and their second edition would be published. Other important works to be published this year, Mr. Pang said, were "Key and Important Decisions Made at and After the Third Session of the 11th Central Committee of the Communist Party of China", the third volume of Mao Zedong's Selected Works, Mao Zedong's Selected Works on Diplomacy and the eighth and ninth volumes of Selected Important Documents Since the Establishment of the People's Republic of China.
