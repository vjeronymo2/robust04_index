June 29, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: SANTA MONICA;

FREE COUNSELING RESUMES

Free Monday morning counseling by SCORE (Senior Core of Retired Executives) has resumed at the Santa Monica Area Chamber of Commerce now that the chamber's remodeling has been completed.

A SCORE counselor will advise anyone interested in starting a new business or having problems with an existing one. SCORE is funded by the Small Business Administration.

In the fall, seminars on sales promotion and advertising, restaurant operations, catering, and starting a home business will be offered.

The chamber's office is at 1460 4th St. For more information, call the chamber at (213) 393-9825.
