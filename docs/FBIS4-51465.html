BFN [Text] Macao, May 23 (XINHUA) -- The Hong Kong and Shanghai Banking Corporation Macao branch issued today a newly-designed Global Access Credit Card which is available in more than 70 countries worldwide. It is learned that Global Access Credit Card holders can draw money through more than 160,000 automatic teller machines (ATM) scattered over 70 countries in the world. Each time, one holder can draw 6,000 Macao patacas or foreign currencies of the same value at most. Global Access Card users can transfer accounts, check cash surplus, apply for accounts and draw cash in related banks such as the Hong Kong and Shanghai Banking Corporation, Banco Hang Sang, the Express Net of the Philippines and Hing Kong and Shanghai Banking Corporation branch in Canada. The annual charge for global access card stands at 50 Macao patacas (about 6.25 U.S. dollars).
