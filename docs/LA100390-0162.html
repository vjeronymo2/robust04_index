October 3, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

PREP ROUNDUP;

SANTIAGO'S LEAGUE RECORD REMAINS PERFECT IN TENNIS

Led by the doubles team of Cyndie Lam and Van Nguyen, Santiago High School defeated Kennedy, 10-8, in a Garden Grove League girls' tennis match Tuesday at Santiago.

Lam and Nguyen won their sets, 6-2, 6-0, 6-0, as Santiago (9-1, 4-0) remained undefeated in league play.

In Angelus League tennis:

Mater Dei 10, Rosary 8 -- The No. 1 doubles team of Trisha Abe and Kim Conter won its sets, 6-2, 6-0, 6-0, and Erika Asmuss won her singles sets, 6-4, 6-1, 6-2, for host Mater Dei (5-2, 1-0).

In Century League tennis:

Villa Park 10, El Modena 8 -- Tobey Wu won, 6-3, 6-3, 6-1, and Ashley Thurston won, 6-2, 6-0, 6-2, in singles for visiting Villa Park, which is 3-0 in league.
