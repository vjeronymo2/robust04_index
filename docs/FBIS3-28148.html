Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] On 2 February, the government confirmed its diplomatic itinerary, according to which Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa will visit the PRC on 19-21 March and Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata will visit Russia on 19-20 March. It has started working out the details of the planned visits with the governments of the respective nations. A while ago, Prime Minister Hosokawa frankly stated that the pre-World War II Japanese invasion of Asian countries "was a war of aggression." China, which highly acclaimed the prime minister's remarks, has requested he visit the country soon. However, political uncertainties in Japan have delayed coordinating the schedule.
