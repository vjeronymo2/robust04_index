940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / Netherlands ferry route may restart P&O European Ferries yesterday said it may resume ferry freight services - due to close in May - from Sheerness in Kent to Vlissingen in the Netherlands. P&O has acquired two new 33,000-ton ferries from the German Olau line, which is closing its Sheerness passenger and freight operation. P&O's new ships will operate between Portsmouth and Le Havre in northern France.
