921003 FT 03 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Dolphin Packaging falls 45% to Pounds 0.79m Lower selling prices in Dolphin Packaging's markets, which reflected reductions in the costs of polymers and increased competition, led to a 45 per cent drop in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.43m to Pounds 789,000 in the half year to June 30. The company, which produces plastic packaging, primarily for the food industry, saw turnover fall from Pounds 13.9m to Pounds 12.3m. The interim dividend is being held at 1.7p, payable from earnings per share of 2.39p (4.98p).
