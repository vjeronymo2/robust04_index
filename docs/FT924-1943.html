921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / Parliament and Politics: Premier Major meets Dr Tatsuro Toyoda Prime minister John Major yesterday met Dr Tatsuro Toyoda, president of the Japanese carmaker Toyota, who will today open the company's first car plant in Europe at Burnaston, near Derby. The Pounds 700m plant is intended to produce 200,000 cars a year by 1997 with 3,000 workers. The workforce will total 1,700 in the first stage of the project with output rising to 100,000 cars a year by 1995
