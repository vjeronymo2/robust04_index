§266.23 Standards applicable to users of materials that are used in a manner that constitutes disposal. (a) Owners or operators of facilities that use recyclable materials in a manner that constitutes disposal are regulated under all applicable provisions of subparts A through N of parts 124, 264, 265, 268, and 270 of this chapter and the notification requirement under section 3010 of RCRA. (These requirements do not apply to products which contain these recyclable materials under the provisions of §266.20(b) of this chapter.) * * * * * Subpart H_Hazardous Waste Burned in Boilers and Industrial Furnaces 16.
