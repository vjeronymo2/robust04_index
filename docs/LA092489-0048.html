September 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

BALLOON PAYMENT

I was appalled to see the photo in the (Sept. 10) Travel Section of the cruise ship releasing a cloud of balloons over the sea. Someone in the cruise business needs to realize that those deflated balloons look like food to sea birds and animals and, after being swallowed, can block their stomachs. Starvation results.

There have to be other ways to create a celebratory atmosphere which won't endanger the environment in which these creatures live and whose very existence heightens the enjoyment of the cruise goer.

Let's hope those in charge of celebrations on the ships will search for less hazardous ways to send off a cruise.

BERNICE VAN STEENBERGEN

Long Beach
