941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Raychem and Pacific Electric Wire and Cable - Contracts and ventures US-based Raychem and Taiwan's Pacific Electric Wire and Cable have signed contracts jointly to invest TDollars 320m (Dollars 12m) in an electric wire and cable plant. The 50/50 venture will build its plant in the northern Hsinchu Science Park, and begin production in the second half of 1995.
