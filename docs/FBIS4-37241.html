BFN [Text] The Yemeni News Agency, SABA', has asked Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah about reports disseminated by news agencies that the Government of the Republic of Yemen has agreed on the participation of Arab and foreign countries in the committee that will be in charge of consolidating a cease-fire. Brother Muhammad Salim Basindwah said: It should be clear that we have informed brother Lakhdar Ibrahimi, the personal envoy of the UN secretary general, and a number of Arab leaders that the Government of the Republic of Yemen agrees on activating the joint military committee, which includes Yemeni military officials and representatives from Jordan and the Sultanate Oman. He added that the Yemeni Government agreed on adding representatives from Syria and the Kingdom of Morocco, for the Arab side, in addition to the U.S. and French military attaches from the non-Arab side.
