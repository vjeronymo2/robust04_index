930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / GM court plea General Motors has asked for a new trial of a Georgia case in which the parents of a boy who died in a lorry crash were awarded Dollars 105m (Pounds 73.9m), AP-DJ reports from Detroit. The company said new witnesses claimed Shannon Moseley, 17, might have been dead before the lorry caught fire. His family blamed his 1985 death on placement of the fuel tanks.
