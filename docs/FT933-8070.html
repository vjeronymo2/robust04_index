930818 FT 18 AUG 93 / Lucas venture wins Ford deal A joint venture between automotive and aerospace group Lucas Industries and Sumitomo of Japan has won a Dollars 30m-a-year (Pounds 20m) contract to supply disc brakes for two of Ford's main North American car models. Ford's Taurus and Mercury Sable models will use the components produced by Lucas Sumitomo, an equally-owned joint venture based at Lebanon, Ohio.
