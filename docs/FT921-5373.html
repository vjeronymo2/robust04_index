920304 FT 04 MAR 92 / Shake-up urged in pharmacists' role A JOINT working party of civil servants and pharmacists yesterday recommended a shake-up of community chemists' role. It suggested that the country's 20,000 pharmacists should take on a more active role in dispensing health services by recommending treatments and prescriptions after medical diagnosis. The proposals, which would help to ease the pressure on doctors' practices, have received government backing.
