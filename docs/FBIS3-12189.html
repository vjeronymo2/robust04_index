Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Labor Secretary General Nisim Zvili has sent a cable to Yasir 'Arafat stating he is shocked and horrified by the insane murder of innocent Palestinians at the Cave of the Patriarchs. Mr. Zvili emphasized the Israeli authorities are doing everything to prevent an exacerbation of the situation, and that the only way to break out of the circle of violence is to concentrate all efforts in order to facilitate the peace process. Our party affairs correspondent Shmu'el Tal reports opposition leaders will convene this evening to consolidate positions in view of the possibility that the government will take advantage of the situation in order to facilitate the peace process.
