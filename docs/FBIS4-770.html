BFN [Text] The police have released a detailed report on security measures for the coming election. A police spokesman said in releasing the report in Pretoria that measures would be implemented nationally to prevent disturbances at polling stations. No weapons or firearms will be allowed within a 1,100-meter radius of voting stations, and people will be searched before being allowed in to vote. No party tents, political activity, flags, or banners will be allowed within the same radius. The polling stations will be searched for explosives or other undesirable objects before people will be allowed to go in, and only voters will be allowed within a 600-meter radius of polling centers.
