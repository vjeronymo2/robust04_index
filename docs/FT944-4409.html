941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Acquisitions help Amberley rise to Pounds 0.5m Bolstered by acquisitions, Amberley Group, the USM-quoted building services company, reported a healthy advance in pre-tax profits from Pounds 21,000 to Pounds 552,000 for the six months to end-September. The pre-tax outcome was struck after interest receivable of Pounds 21,000 (Pounds 1,000 payable) and came from turnover up from Pounds 2.14m to Pounds 5.21m, of which Pounds 1m came from acquisitions. Lawrence Industries, acquired in September last year, contributed its first full six months' profits, and Metacol, bought in June this year, made a near three-month contribution. Earnings emerged at 1.16p (0.21p) per share and an interim dividend of 0.25p (nil) is declared.
