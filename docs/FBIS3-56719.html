Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Shanayev] [Text] Vladikavkaz January 18 TASS -- 159,793 people (64 per cent of the turnout) voted to elect Akhsarbek Galazov as president of the Russian Caucasian Republic of North Ossetia. 81,386 people (32,6 per cent) voted for his rival Sergey Khetagurov, according to the final results of the election published on Tuesday. Galazov held today a working meeting with lawmakers, members of the government and heads of the city and rural executive committees. He appealed to the members of the government to continue their duties until the new government would be formed. Khetagurov resigned as the prime minister. The inauguration of the president is scheduled for January 28, in accordance with the election law.
