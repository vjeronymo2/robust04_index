920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Woodchester plans to give full service Woodchester Investments, the Dublin-based small-ticket leasing and finance group, has changed its name to Woodchester Credit Lyonnais Bank. The move reflected what Mr Craig McKinney, Woodchester chairman, described as the group's 'ambition to develop a full retail banking service in Ireland'. The group intended to acquire 'around 40 to 50' retail banking outlets in Ireland 'to be in a position to offer services that Credit Lyonnais offers elsewhere in Europe'. Mr Jean Yves Harberet, president of Credit Lyonnais, said his bank's tie-up with Woodchester 'opens a window in Europe for the development of Woodchester's leasing business'.
