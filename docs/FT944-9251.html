941116 FT 16 NOV 94 / UK Company News: John Lusty recovers John Lusty Group, USM-quoted food importer, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 215,000 for the half year to September 30, the first since 1989. Losses last time were Pounds 51,000. The turnround follows the integration of Trustin-Kerwood and The Foodfinders into a new operating entity, Trustin the Foodfinders. Restructuring costs in the period were about Pounds 50,000. Turnover amounted to Pounds 7.17m (Pounds 3.58m). Earnings per share were 0.19p (0.44p losses). The company has applied to reduce its share capital by cancellation of its deferred shares. No dividends can be paid until that is completed.
