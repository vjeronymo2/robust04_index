930920 FT 20 SEP 93 / Construction Contracts: Laing starts RAF job LAING EASTERN has begun work on a Pounds 3.6m contract awarded by the Ministry of Defence. The contract requires the design and construction of a new headquarters building at RAF High Wycombe. The 4,000 sq metre, two and three-storey building will be used as offices and as an auditorium. Work involves constructing a steel frame clad with brick, fitting out the auditorium, all internal finishes and services, parking and the erection of security fencing. The project is being managed by Tilbury Douglas and architects are Jones Stocks. Work for the project is expected to be completed by June next year.
