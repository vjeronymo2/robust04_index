BFN [Text] Tskhinvali, 12 Apr -- IBERIA has been told by General Nodar Tatarashvili from the Tskhinvali headquarters of the Georgian peacekeeping forces in South Ossetia that the second round of elections to the so-called South Ossetian people's nikhas [parliament] ended on 11 April. Its results will be promulgated in the next few days. An IBERIA correspondent reports, citing reliable sources, that the newly-elected parliament of the Autonomous Republic of South Ossetia will declare the republic independent at its first session.
