Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Bulgarian vessel Khan Kubrat has anchored at the 793-km point near Vidin with empty tanks. There were 21 people on board--the crew and a woman, and they have all been detained at the regional Internal Affairs Ministry Directorate. The captain of the vessel is Konstantin Stoyanov, 49, from Ruse. REUTER disseminated a photograph accompanied by a text that recalls the history of the vessel's strange trip. This means that tomorrow Bulgaria's name will be on the front page of every major newspapers in the world. The UN Security Council will also discuss the incident. Meanwhile, the State River Sailing Inspectorate in Vidin has refused to issue a statement for the radio. It was pointed out from the Vidin Inspectorate that they are subordinate to the Lom Inspectorate, and the chairman of the Lom Inspectorate will have a statement for the public tomorrow.
