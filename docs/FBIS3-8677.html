Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Office of the President of the Slovak Republic informed the Press Agency of the Slovak Republic about further steps to be taken in connection with the results of today's session of the National Council [parliament] of the Slovak Republic. As is known, the National Council passed a vote of no-confidence in Vladimir Meciar, the current prime minister. According to Article 116, Paragraph 6 of the Constitution, on the basis of this decision of the National Council, the president of the Republic is to recall the prime minister. The recall of the prime minister means the resignation of the whole government. The president of the Republic is determined to ask those members of the government who were not personally subject to a vote of no-confidence to temporarily continue exercising their duties at the utmost until the naming of the new government. The president of the Republic invited Tibor Cabaj, the chairman of the Movement for a Democratic Slovakia parliamentary group, to negotiate tomorrow morning with the strongest parliamentary group about the possibility of creating a new government.
