BFN [Text] A responsible source at the Interior Ministry has denied press reports today that demonstrations have taken place in Beirut and elsewhere. The source said that what happened is that several popular delegations flocked to the residence of Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri to voice their support. The ministry has issued no permit for demonstrations, in accordance with a Cabinet decision. Interior Minister Bisharah Mirhij was contacted by Chamber Speaker Nabih Birri this morning. They assessed the political situation in light of the critical stage through which the country is passing. Minister Mirhij affirmed to Speaker Birri his commitment to the mainstays of the democratic parliamentary system.
