Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Khartoum, 2 Jan (SUNA) -- Major General Dominic Kassiano, minister of labour and administrative reform, met Sunday [2 January] the Iraqi minister of planning and labour now visiting the country to participate in the celebrations of the 38th anniversary of independence.
