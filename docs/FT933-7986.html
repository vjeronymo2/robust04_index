930818 FT 18 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Burmine seeks Pounds 3.45m via placing Europa Minerals, the UK mining finance house which is in merger talks with Burmine - the Australian gold mining company in which it has a 38.5 per cent stake - has announced that Burmine is to raise Pounds 3.45m with a placement of 1.5m fully paid ordinary 50 cent shares with Australian and UK financial institutions. The placement has been made at a share price of ADollars 2.30 (107p). The money will be used to fund exploration drilling and development at the company's Copperhead, Golden Pig and Fraser mines in Western Australia and to provide additional working capital. As a result of the placement the issued capital will increase from 17.5m to 19m fully paid ordinary 50 cent shares.
