March 31, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

In the "Staying Legal" supplement published March 13, The Times inadvertently omitted several programs from a list of government-approved schools for the amnesty program. They are: Chaffey Community College District, 5885 N. Haven Ave., Rancho Cucamonga 91701 (714-987-1737); English Training Schools Inc., 14907 Vanowen St., Van Nuys, 91405 (818-782-2112); Tustin Unified School District, 300 South C. St., Tustin 92680 (714-730-7396) and Theatre of Arts, 4128 Wilshire Blvd., L.A. 90010 (213-380-0511).

The government has expanded the list of approved programs since the section was published and will continue to do so. Questions about whether an amnesty program has been approved can be directed to the Immigration and Naturalization Service at (800) 448-4994.
