Language: German Article Type:BFN [Report signed "ank": "New Warning Strikes in Metal Industry"] [Text] Frankfurt/Main, 9 Feb -- On Wednesday [9 February] the IG-Metall trade union continued the warning strikes in the western German metal industry with strikes in 228 companies. The focal points of the rallies were in North Rhine-Westphalia, Baden-Wuerttemberg, and Hesse. According to the trade union, about 119,000 metalworkers participated in the actions. This Thursday and Friday the IG-Metall will not be able to keep its warning strikes up to the same extent, since in many regions of western Germany Karneval is being celebrated, IG-Metall Chairman Zwickel said. On the weekend the leaders of the employers and the trade union plan to discuss a solution to the wage conflict.
