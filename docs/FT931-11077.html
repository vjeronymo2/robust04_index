930204 FT 04 FEB 93 / Observer: Daf's death notice Moved by the plight of his compatriot company, a Daf-driving Dutch haulier decided to do his bit by calling on customers to continue buying trucks from Daf. So Peter Stoof, general director of Breda Transport, took out an ad in the Algemeen Dagblad: 'Let your national feelings speak if you are about to invest in new material]' he urged fellow truckers. Unfortunately, the newspaper placed the ad at the bottom of the deaths column.
