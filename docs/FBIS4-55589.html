BFN [Text] Sarajevo, May 17 (TANJUG) -- The Moslem forces on Tuesday [17 May] launched mortar and anti-aircraft attacks on Serb positions and settlements in the Sarajevo front, Bosnian Serb military sources have said. The sources said that in the Ilijas area the Moslems fired 82mm mortars on Serb positions, and opened anti-aircraft fire on the Krcevine region from the Visocica area. In the Ilidza part of the Sarajevo front, the Moslems attacked from the stojcevic region on the Luzani settlement and Serb positions in the area of the forestry school and the Vrucci suburb. Moslem snipers on Tuesday opened fire from the direction of the Vojnicko Polje on Nedjarici, from the direction of Strupsko Brdo on Kasindolska street, from the right bank of the Miljacka river on Grbavica, and from the direction of Sokolje on the Rajlovac barracks, the Bosnian Serb Army sources said.
