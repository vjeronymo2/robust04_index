BFN [From the press review] [Text] The daily ARB'AT'ASHAR 14 OCTOBER newspaper continues to publish reports on the current developments and the war launched by the bloody al-Ahmar gang against the armed forces of the unity and democracy. It published reportages about the damages caused by the shelling of the residential apartments. One of the reportages was written about the calm and tranquil situation in Dar Sa'd. It was compiled by a press and television team which came to Aden to cover the war events. The newspaper said in the reportages that one of the correspondents talked to the soldiers who were guarding the gates of al-Anad camp. The journalist described the stable conditions in this region thanks to the armed forces of the country and democracy which are controlling in the battlefield [sentence as heard]. The journalist wondered about the lying information which falsifies the facts and confuses the people and [words indistinct]. He said: Such media deserve to be described as lying media, because it presents false information about the situation. He said: All information we have heard from the Sanaa media to the effect that 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih's forces had captured al-Dali' is untrue.
