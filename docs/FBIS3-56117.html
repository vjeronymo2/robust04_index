Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Kiev -- On 11 January, the Central Electoral Commission issued registration certificates to the Constitutional Democratic Party, the Party of the Organization of Ukrainian Nationalists in Ukraine, and the Peasant Democratic Party of Ukraine in connection with their intention to nominate candidates for participation in the elections of Ukrainian people's deputies on 27 March 1994. Thus, 23 political parties have been issued their Central Electoral Commission registration certificates by now.
