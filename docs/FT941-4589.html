940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Harrington Kilbride up 30% to Pounds 2.4m Harrington Kilbride, the specialist publisher, achieved a 30 per cent rise in pre-tax profits to Pounds 2.42m for the year to end-December. Turnover advanced from Pounds 15.4m to Pounds 23.8m. Earnings per share at the year end worked through 31 per cent ahead at 15.5p. The dividend is being lifted to 4.9p (4.5p) by a proposed final of 3.2p. The directors described the year as one of significant progress. They said the balance sheet had been strengthened by the Pounds 1m share placing of last October and that at year-end shareholders' funds had increased by 51 per cent to stand at Pounds 6.07m.
