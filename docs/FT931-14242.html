930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / Print union seeks longer holidays GPMU, the print workers union, will be pressing for a sixth week of annual holiday and the inclusion of part-time, casual and temporary workers in the industry national agreement when it meets the British Printing Industries Federation early next month. It will also seek a substantial increase in basic pay. The union has been involved in disputes over temporary and casual workers and will be pressing for equal treatment for them.
