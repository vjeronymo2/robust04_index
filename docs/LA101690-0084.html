October 16, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

PEOPLE

Grossmont Bank has named Debra A. Dvorak, commercial loan officer, Anna Holmes, assistant vice president and operations officer, Alan J. McEachern, vice president and manager, Sharon E. Murray, vice president/business development, Patrick M. Saunders, vice president and manager and Thomas E. Swanson, senior vice president and North County administrator.
