February 9, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CHARITY SCORECARD

The "Carousel Goes Hog Wild" comedy show and dinner Feb. 2 at the Irvine Improvisation in Irvine raised an estimated $12,000 for the Carousel chapter of the Guilds of the Orange County Performing Arts Center. Lu-an Cohen and Carol Mock were chairwomen.

The "Mardi Gras" dinner Feb. 3 at the Westin South Coast Plaza in Costa Mesa raised an estimated $30,000 for the Catholic Charities of Orange County. Doris Cantley was chairwoman.

The "Carnival of Holidays" dinner Feb. 4 at the Disneyland Hotel in Anaheim raised an estimated $15,000 for the Masked Ball chapter of the Guilds of the Orange County Performing Arts Center. Susan Levine was chairwoman.
