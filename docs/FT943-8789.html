940815 FT 15 AUG 94 / Equity Markets: Other Markets - Paris Wednesday is the final day on which Generale des Eaux should decide whether to exercise its pre-emptive rights to acquire a majority stake in Fnac, the French music and book retailer which, otherwise, will see a controlling stake sold by Credit Lyonnais to Mr Francois Pinault, the entrepreneur. Compiled by William Cochrane
