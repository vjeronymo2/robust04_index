930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / Clowes appeals over convictions MR PETER CLOWES, the former financier jailed for 10 years for fraud over Barlow Clowes' 'milking' of more than Pounds 100m of investors' money, yesterday started an appeal against some convictions. He is appealing against 10 convictions for theft. Mr Clowes intends to ask for a reduction in his total sentence if his appeal fails. The appeal is expected to end today.
