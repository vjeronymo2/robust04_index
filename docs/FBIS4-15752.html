BFN [Text] As Konstantin Zatulin, chairman of the parliamentary committee for CIS affairs, said at a press conference, Admiral Feliks Gromov, commander of the Russian Fleet, and Yuriy Dubinin, head of the Russian delegation at the Russian-Ukrainian talks, will leave for Ukraine on a special flight. Zatulin believes that this visit will put an end to the incident and that all detained servicemen will be released.
