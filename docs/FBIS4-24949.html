BFN [Text] Beijing, June 6 (AFP)--A Chinese Northwest Airlines plane carrying 159 people crashed Monday morning near the central tourist city of Xian, a spokesman from the Civil Aviation Administration of China said. Domestic flight WH2303 from Xian in Shaanxi province to Guangzhou in southern Guangdong province took off at 8:12 a.m. (0012 GMT) and lost radio contact with Xian airport eight minutes later, the official said. The plane was carrying 13 crew and 146 passengers, including foreigners, he said. According to aviation sources in Guangzhou, all passengers aboard the Soviet-built plane were killed.
