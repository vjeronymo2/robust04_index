BFN [Text] Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri left his house at 1000 today. Earlier, he received Ministers Marwan Hamadah, 'Abdallah al-Amin, and Jean 'Ubayd. He also received a delegation from Tripoli led by Misbah al-Ahdab, which conveyed its support for al-Hariri's political line and urged him to continue shouldering his responsibilities.
