July 1, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

EVERYBODY'S A CRITIC

Being a native Floridian with Cuban heritage, I almost died the first few months in L.A. Luckily, our neighbors introduced us to El Rincon Criollo in Culver City. It's unpresentable and tacky, but the authenticity is 100%. Even the bread is authentic.

REBECCA RODRIGUEZ

Redondo Beach
