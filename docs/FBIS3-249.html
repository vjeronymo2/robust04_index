Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] We have just heard that for about half an hour this morning about 20 Somalis directed fire at the former Italian Embassy in Mogadishu, in which a unit of the Italian contingent is based. Guards returned fire, as did the crews on two caterpillar vehicles that were returning to the base when they also came under attack. No Italians have been hurt. The Somalis fled shortly afterward and it is not known if any of them were hurt. The motives behind the attack are not yet known.
