940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / Pay rise for British Channel Tunnel train drivers British train drivers who will operate Channel tunnel services are to receive pay rises of around 30 per cent. Average annual salaries will rise to Pounds 25,000 under flexible agreements based on performance-related pay reached by rail union Aslef with Railfreight Distribution and European Passenger Services, currently subsidiaries of British Rail. Aslef sees the deal as a model for agreements with new train operating companies after rail privatisation begins on April 1.
