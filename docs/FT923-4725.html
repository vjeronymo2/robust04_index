920904 FT 04 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Trans World in the black with Pounds 0.4m Trans World Communications, the USM-quoted independent radio station operator, achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 395,000 for the half year to June 30 compared with a loss last time of Pounds 1.09m. Turnover advanced 9 per cent to Pounds 5.69m (Pounds 5.21m). Earnings per share were 1.1p (5.3p losses).
