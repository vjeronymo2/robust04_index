BFN [Text] Rudan (Hormozgan), June 5, (IRNA) -- Unidentified gunmen kidnapped a Chinese national and two Iranians in this southern province on Friday. Fu Weixian, who worked for a mineral factory, was abducted by armed men along with his driver and another Iranian accompanying him. The car in which the Chinese national and his Iranian companions were travelling was found on Saturday around Heights city. Efforts are underway to track down the kidnappers and free the victims.
