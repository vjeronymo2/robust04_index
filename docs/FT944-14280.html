941024 FT 24 OCT 94 / Arts (Openings): New York The Welsh bass-baritone Bryn Terfel makes his New York recital debut tonight at Lincoln Center's Alice Tully Hall, singing a portion of Schumann's 'Eichendorff Liederkreis' and Schubert's 'Schwanengesang' with James Levine at the keyboard. Last Wednesday he made his Metropolitan Opera debut singing the title role in 'The Marriage of Figaro'.
