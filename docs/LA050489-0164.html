DMV OPENING OFFICE AT SIMI CIVIC CENTER

Anew Department of Motor Vehicles office will open in Simi Valley on Monday in the Simi Valley Civic Center complex.

The office, at 3855D Alamo St., will be open from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Monday through Friday, with hours extended to 8 p.m. Thursdays. Test drives will be given by appointment only. Motorists should call (805) 527-7960 for an appointment for all other licensing and registration needs, according to Lillian Williams, office manager.

The formal opening of the office is scheduled for May 16 at 10:30 a.m. Assemblywoman Cathie Wright and Simi Valley Mayor Greg Stratton are expected to attend.
