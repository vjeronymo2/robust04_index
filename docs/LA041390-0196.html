April 13, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

111 YEARS A 'LIFELONG RESIDENT'

The words "lifelong resident of Vicksburg" mean a lot to Bessie W. Putman -- she reckons 111 years.

Putman celebrated her birthday Wednesday at the Vicksburg Convalescent Home. An inscription in her family Bible shows that she was born in 1879 in a small house on the edge of Vicksburg.

The daughter of slaves, Putman said her mother and father died while she was a baby, and an aunt raised her and her brother. Born Bessie Wilson, she married Sam Putman in 1900 and had 12 children, most of whom have died.
