BFN [Text] Ho Chong-man, responsible vice chairman of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan [Chongnyon], said he believes "DPRK (North Korean) President Kim Il-song has delegated full power -- political, economic, and military -- to Workers' Party of Korea Secretary Kim Chong-il." He made the remark in an address at a meeting of the Social Democratic Party of Japan's Japan-Korea Committee held at the First Dietmen's Hall on the morning of 5 April. Vice Chairman Ho said he had talks with Secretary Kim Chong-il for about two hours when he visited North Korea from 15 February to 1 March. Ho denied rumors that Kim had been seriously injured, saying "he was in excellent health."
