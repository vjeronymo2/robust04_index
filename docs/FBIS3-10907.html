Language: Slovene Article Type:BFN [Text] As a part of his official visit in Chile, Foreign Minister Lojze Peterle held several important meetings today. Among other things, he bid farewell to President Aylwin and attended a reception given by Foreign Minister Silva. On that occasion Peterle met with the chiefs of several foreign delegations that are staying in Santiago to attend the inauguration of the new Chilean president. Minister Peterle also held talks with the director general of the international fair Feria Intersonal de Santiago about the possibilities to organize a Slovene-Chilean business conference.
