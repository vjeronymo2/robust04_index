931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / Rail tickets by phone launched A Pounds 2m InterCity national telesales office, enabling rail travellers to order tickets in advance for delivery to their homes, was opened yesterday. Customers can make a freephone call and pay by credit or debit card for rail tickets. There are 50 staff and 20 more are being recruited. From early next year tickets will also be mailed electronically to more than 400 main rail stations for customers to collect them there if they prefer.
