930529 FT 29 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Lasmo sells Dollars 350m of bonds LASMO has sold two tranches of bonds in the US public bond market totalling Dollars 350m (Pounds 230m). The proceeds will be used to repay debt with maturities of less than three years. The two sales were Dollars 150m of 30 year guaranteed notes with a coupon of 8.375 per cent, which can be called at Lasmo's option after 10 years, and Dollars 200m of 10 year guaranteed notes with a coupon of 7.125 per cent.
