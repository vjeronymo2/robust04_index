940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / International Company News: Chevron result worse than expected Chevron, the San Francisco-based energy group, turned in worse than expected results for the second quarter as profit margins at its refining and marketing operations slipped further than those of other big US energy companies. Overall, after-tax profits in the period were Dollars 257m (after a Dollars 5m charge,) against Dollars 50m (after special charges of Dollars 515m) the year before. Earnings per share were 39 cents, up from 8 cents a year ago (or 87 cents before charges). For the first six months, net income was Dollars 645m (after charges of Dollars 41m), compared with Dollars 551m (after charges of Dollars 517m).
