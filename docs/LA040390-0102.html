April 3, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Crazy for You: Madonna fans snatched up the 60,000 tickets for the singer's four Los Angeles shows -- May 11, 12, 13 and 15 -- in 68 minutes.

To meet the Madonna demand, a fifth date has been added for May 16. Tickets go on sale Wednesday at 8 a.m. at Music Plus outlets and through Ticketmaster's charge-by-phone number. Madonna fans across the country were equally voracious: All of the 500,000 tickets for the scheduled Madonna concerts nationwide were bought in just four hours. BETH KLEID
