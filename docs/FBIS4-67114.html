Johannesburg MB0905204294 Johannesburg SAPA in English 1904 GMT 09 May 94 MB0905204294 Johannesburg SAPA English BFN [Text] Johannesburg May 9 SAPA -- A tip-off led Johannesburg narcotics policemen to a cocaine laboratory at an hotel in nearby Randburg on Saturday evening where they arrested an Israeli man and seized 1.5kg of the drug, valued at r[Rand]375,000. An airline ticket to Rio de Janeiro and foreign and local currency with a value of r60,000 were also found in a room at the city lodge, police reported on Monday. Laboratory equipment and chemicals were seized. Mr Gazit Yechaim of Tel Aviv appeared in the Johannesburg magistrate's court on Monday and was remanded to wednesday.
