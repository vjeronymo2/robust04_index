November 16, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY BRIEFLY

Gabelli Raises Stake: New York-based Gabelli Group said that it has raised its stake in Wynn's International to 24.74% of its common shares outstanding. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission, Gabelli said it acquired 43,500 shares of Wynn's from Oct. 16 to Nov. 7 for $22.75 to $24.625 each. The group now holds 950,150 shares of Wynn's, a Fullerton-based holding company with a business in automotive parts and accessories and petrochemical specialties.
