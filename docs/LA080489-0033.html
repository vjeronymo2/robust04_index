August 4, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Liz Kiley, assistant program director and music director at KOST-FM, will become program director at KFAC-FM on Aug. 14, further fueling the widespread speculation that KFAC will change from its longtime classical format to a contemporary hits or rock station. Kiley also has been the on-air host for KOST's "Love Songs on the Coast." ALEENE MacMINN
