Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Budapest, 13 February (MTI) -- The parliamentary system created over the past four years has become viable so it does not need to undergo any sweeping transformation. In the future, emphasis should rather be placed on improving its operation and increasing its efficiency, the draft electoral manifesto of the Hungarian Democratic Forum (MDF) says. The 200-page document, which was distributed among members of the MDF National Board in Budapest this weekend, will be discussed and finalized by the party's three-day national congress next weekend. The three chapters of the draft comprise the main issues of legislation, home affairs, foreign affairs, economy, agriculture, education, culture, health, and social welfare.
