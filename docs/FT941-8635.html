940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / UK Company News: CrestaCare buys nursing homes CrestaCare, the north of England and Northern Ireland nursing home operator, has conditionally agreed to purchase a further two nursing homes for Pounds 2.38m cash. The purpose built homes, Cedars Nursing Home and Cedars Lodge Nursing Home, are situated in Doncaster and comprise 80 beds. The purchase raises CrestaCare's number of clinics to 37 and the number of beds in operation to 2,264.
