October 5, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

SCC TOPS CAL BAPTIST IN MEN'S SOCCER

James Helmuth scored twice to lead the Southern California College men's soccer team to a 3-1 victory over visiting Cal Baptist Wednesday.

Toby Ray scored three minutes into the game to give SCC a 1-0 lead, and Helmuth scored 32 minutes later to make it 2-0. Cal Baptist, which dropped to 4-7 overall, 0-2 in the conference, cut the lead to 2-1 on Terry Cassidy's goal 10 minutes into the second half.

Helmuth scored at the 33:25 mark in the second half to seal the victory for SCC (3-9, 2-0).

In California Collegiate Athletic Assn. men's soccer:

Chapman 1, Cal State Dominguez Hills 1 (OT) -- Mark Merino scored for visiting Dominguez Hills with five minutes left in regulation to tie the game. Brian Wilson scored in the first half for Chapman (5-3-3). Dominguez Hills is 6-3-1.

In nonconference women's soccer:

Cal State Dominguez Hills 2, Chapman 0 -- Goalie Laurel Angell had 17 saves for Chapman (3-5-2).
