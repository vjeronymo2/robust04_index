BFN [From the "Xinjiang News" program] [Text] The first music radio station in China's northwestern region, the Xinjiang People's Radio Broadcasting Network's music station, formally started to broadcast yesterday in Urumqi. Party and government leaders of the autonomous region, including Janabil, Amudun Niyaz, Feng Dazhen, (Paxia Yixia), and other comrades went to the station to extend their congratulations. Janabil, deputy secretary of the regional party committee and chairman of the regional committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference, expressed warm congratulations to the station on behalf of the regional party committee and the regional government at a ceremony for the start of broadcasting by the music station. Janabil said: The music station should publicize the main theme and adhere to the correct media orientation like the people's station and the economic affairs station. It should broadcast more musical works which can represent China's national tradition and spirit and the atmosphere of the times, as well as famous and artistic Chinese and foreign music and songs. The music station and the people's station should take an active attitude toward organizing the creation of more musical works which can encourage people of various nationalities to work hard and make progress and satisfy the increasing spiritual and cultural needs of the people. In conclusion, Janabil expressed his hope that various circles in society would vigorously support the music station. He said that concerned departments should strengthen their leadership over the music station while comrades of the music station should work hard and find ways and means to accomplish the propaganda task entrusted to them by the party and the government.
