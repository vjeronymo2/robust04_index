930621 FT 21 JUN 93 / Two bombs defused in Egypt POLICE in Egypt defused two bombs yesterday, including one in the Cairo suburb where a bomb blast killed seven people last week, news reports said, AP writes from Cairo. The state-run Middle East News Agency said a package containing a bomb was left in front of a shop on a busy street at Aswan, a main tourist town in the south. A resident noticed the package and called police, the agency said. The bomb contained nails, a timer and gunpowder, an interior ministry official told the MENA. In the Cairo suburb of Shubra, police found another bomb, cordoned off the area and defused it, the newspaper al-Ahram reported early today.
