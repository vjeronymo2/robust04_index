BFN [Text] In order to more effectively implement the state policy in the field of utilization of the fuel and energy potential of the country as the most important prerequisite of an accelerated social and economic development of all its regions, President of Kazakhstan Nursultan Nazarbayev decreed the establishment of the Ministry of Oil and Gas Industry of the Republic of Kazakhstan and the Ministry of Power Engineering and Coal of the Republic of Kazakhstan on the basis of the dissolved Ministry of Power Engineering and Fuel Resources of the Republic of Kazakhstan. By the decree of the President of the Republic of Kazakhstan, Syzdyk Dzhumatay-Uly Abishev was appointed as Head of the apparatus of the Cabinet Ministries of the Republic of Kazakhstan. He was released from his post as deputy prime-minister of the Republic of Kazakhstan and minister of foreign economic relations. Abishev keeps his membership in the Cabinet of Ministries. In order to more effectively use the production potential of the Republic and to satisfy the market demand and to [words indistinct], Nazarbayev decreed the establishment of a Ministry of Industry and Trade of the Republic of Kazakhstan on the basis of the dissolved Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations of the Republic of Kazakhstan, the Ministry of Industry and the Ministry of Trade of the Republic of Kazakhstan. In order to cut expenses on the upkeep of the bodies of state management, and to separate economic functions from them, as well as to carry out a common state policy and to regulate the work of all kinds of transport and communications, Nazarbayev decreed the establishment of the Ministry of Transport and Communications of the Republic of Kazakhstan on the basis of the dissolved Ministry of Transport of the Republic of Kazakhstan and the Ministry of Communications of the Republic of Kazakhstan. Zhanibek Salim-Uly Karibzhanov was appointed Minister of Agriculture of the Republic of Kazakhstan. He was released from his post as Deputy Prime Minister of the Republic of Kazakhstan and Chairman of the State Committee for State Property. By decree of the Kazakh president, Yesenberlin Kozy-Korpesh Ilias-uly is appointed chairman of the state committee of the Republic of Kazakhstan on state property. In order to ensure independence of the national bank while implementing monetary and crediting policy, the President of Kazakhstan decreed that governor of the Kazakh national bank Sembayev Daulet Khamit-uly be withdrawn from the membership of the Cabinet of Ministers.
