941110 FT 10 NOV 94 / International Company News: Tiger Oats improves Tiger Oats, South Africa's largest agricultural goods and foods supplier, attributed a 10 per cent gain in pre-tax profit, to R661.8m (Dollars 189.3m) in the year to September 30 from R599.7m in 1993, to restructuring and better cash management, AP-DJ reports from Johannesburg.
