Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Article from the "Radar" page by Ancelmo Gois] [Text] The PFL [Liberal Front Party] has decided it will not put forward a presidential candidate of its own even though it is the country's second largest political party, with nine governors, 15 senators, and 84 federal deputies. It is going to seek an alliance with an anti-Lula [Workers Party President Luis Inacio da Silva] candidate. The very last choice would be [Sao Paulo Mayor Paulo] Maluf.
