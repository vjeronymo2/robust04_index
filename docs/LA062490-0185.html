June 24, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Jerry Tarkanian and Nevada Las Vegas officials met for almost three hours in secret session in Kansas City, Mo., with the NCAA Committee on Infractions.

The committee will decide whether to take further action against the basketball coach whose case has lasted 13 years.

A decision by the six-member panel, chaired by Virginia history professor Alan Williams, is not expected for at least two or three weeks.
