931015 FT 15 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Pounds 1m buy for Tunstall TUNSTALL, the emergency communications and security systems group, is acquiring its Dutch distributor in a Pounds 1m deal. Rotterdam-based Nieuwburg Care and Communications has acted as distributor and installer of Tunstall's UK manufactured products in the Netherlands since 1985. The consideration, payable in cash from existing resources, comprises some Pounds 450,000 for inventory and equipment and Pounds 550,000 for outstanding orders. In the 1992 year, Nieuwburg achieved turnover of Fl 4.38m (Pounds 1.6m) and reported profits at break-even. Turnover for the 12 months to end-October 1993 will not be less than Fl 5m.
