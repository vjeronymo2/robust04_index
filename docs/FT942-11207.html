940506 FT 06 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Highcroft Inv net assets increase 13% Highcroft Investment Trust reported net assets per share up 13 per cent to 314p at the end of 1993, against 277p a year earlier. After-tax profits climbed from Pounds 643,000 to Pounds 748,000 in the year. Earnings were 15.9p (14p) including gains on disposals of assets, or 13.8p (11.8p) excluding the same. The final dividend is 3.3p for a total of 5.2p (4.9p).
