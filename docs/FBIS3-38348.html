Language: French Article Type:BFN [Article signed SVD (ADB): "I Have Nothing To Blame Myself for in the Agusta Affair"; as released by Brussels BELGA Database] [Text] Brussels, 31 Jan (BELGA) -- On Monday morning [31 January], Foreign Minister Claes confirmed to the press that he is to sue the newspaper HET LAATSTE NIEUWS for libel. The HET LAATSTE NIEUWS Saturday issue incriminated Claes over an alleged FrB15 billion bribe deal with the Italian aeronautic company Agusta. HET LAATSTE NIEUWS based its allegations on an "anonymous witness close to the SP [Flemish Socialist Party]." Minister Claes said that he was ready to confront this witness at anytime, adding that "it was the only way to prove my innocence." During a 90-minute news conference, Minister Claes also described in details the role he played as economic affairs minister in negotiating, signing, and controlling the purchase contract of the 46 Agusta helicopters. "I have nothing to reproach myself neither as regards the procedure itself or the content. My administration, my government colleagues and I myself defended the interests of the Belgian State," said Minister Claes, who believes that the Agusta affair remains a case of alleged compensation....
