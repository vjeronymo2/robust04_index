920829 FT 29 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Continuous Stationery Pounds 6.8m buy-out Continuous Stationery, the printer of business forms and stationery, has announced a management buy-out via a share offer valuing it at Pounds 6.8m Managers at the company, which includes the Prontaprint copying chain, have been negotiating an offer with potential financiers for some time. The 'public to private' buy-out follows a 9 per cent decline in profits last year. A new company, Prontaprint 1992, has been formed to oversee the buy-out. The offer is 40p cash per share. The shares of Continuous closed 4p higher at 38p yesterday. Alternatively, investors can opt for securities in Prontaprint 1992.
