Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 2 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to Zhelyu Zhelev, president of the Republic of Bulgaria, on the occasion of the national day of the country. President Kim Il-song in the message wished him and the Bulgarian people success in the work for the prosperity of the country. He expressed the belief that the relations between the two countries and peoples would continue to develop.
