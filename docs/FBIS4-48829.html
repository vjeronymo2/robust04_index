BFN [Text] The cabinet committee investigating the implication of the KwaZulu land issue says it hopes to submit its report and recommendations to the cabinet by the 14th of next month. The minister of land affairs, Mr. Derek Hanekom, said experts had been appointed to assist the committee. He said that over three million hectares of the former homeland had been transferred to the control of the Zulu king. The transfer of the land took place just before the election without any media coverage.
