Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] Professor N.D. Khan, Prime Minister Ms. Benazir Bhutto's special envoy, met Iranian Foreign Minister `Ali Akbar Velayati, in Tehran yesterday. He stressed the need for continuing international pressure on India to stop the atrocities on Kashmiris. He said that Pakistan, through bilateral dialogue, had tried to persuade India to adopt an appropriate posture on the Kashmir issue. Pakistan, however, felt that it is India's strategy to take time to suppress the legitimate demands of the Kashmiri people. He said that Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto has decided to present a resolution at the current session of the Human Right Commission meeting in Geneva. He expressed the hope that the world community will raise its voice against the Indian atrocities in Kashmir. The Iranian foreign minister assured the envoy of continued support to Pakistan's efforts to stop the human rights violations being committed by India and improve the human rights situation in Kashmir.
