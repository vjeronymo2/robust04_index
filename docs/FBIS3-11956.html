Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] On 25 March, the president of the Chamber of Deputies called a meeting of a delegation of Haitian legislators and political leaders at the KINAM Hotel. Their discussion revolved around the crisis resolution plan approved on 2 March by the Chamber of Deputies. In this framework, the participants reiterated their conviction that that plan takes into account the main parties involved in the conflict. The participants also discussed the crisis in the Senate of the Republic. They unanimously agreed to send a public, patriotic, and urgent call to the Senate and its president to meet immediately in order to examine and pass the resolution text approved by the Chamber of Deputies.
