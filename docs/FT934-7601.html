931119 FT 19 NOV 93 / Shrine siege civilians freed Relatives and friends embraced and wept yesterday, as 35 civilians caught in a month-long siege at Kashmir's Hazratbal shrine were reunited with their families, Reuter reports from Srinagar. The 35, all men, were released from police custody not far from the Srinagar mosque where they had huddled for 32 days in a standoff with Indian troops. Thirty others, suspected of being Moslem militants, stayed in detention.
