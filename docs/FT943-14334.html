940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / Murdoch journalists strike Journalists at Mr Rupert Murdoch's Australian newspapers began a nationwide strike yesterday over a dispute involving the introduction of new technology and a wage claim, Reuter reports from Sydney. A total of 1,300 journalists walked off the job until tomorrow, said Mr Chris Warren of the Media, Entertainment and Arts Alliance union. But Mr Warren Beeby, editorial manager of Mr Murdoch's News group, said all the company's newspapers would be produced as usual.
