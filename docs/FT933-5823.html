930901 FT 01 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Robinson Brothers advances strongly Robinson Brothers (Ryders Green), the unquoted West Bromwich-based chemicals group, reported a strong advance in pre-tax profits for the first half of 1993. On turnover ahead 8 per cent at Pounds 14m, against Pounds 12.9m, profits rose from Pounds 736,000 to Pounds 1.9m. However, the company warned that trading conditions in the second half were weaker. Earnings per Pounds 1 share came out at 74p (25p).
