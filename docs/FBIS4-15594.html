BFN [Text] There was a powerful explosion at approximately 0400 hours today, 9 April, at Sadakhlo station in Marneuli, eastern Georgia, in one of the cars of a passenger train travelling from Tbilisi to Yerevan. Our correspondent Nikolay Mamulashvili has reported that according to preliminary figures, as a result of the blast three people were killed, a child was seriously injured, and three cars were burned out. Representatives of the Georgian Interior Ministry and special services have left for the scene of the incident.
