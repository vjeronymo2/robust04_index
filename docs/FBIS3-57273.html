Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tengiz Pachkoriya] [Text] Tbilisi, 24 Jan -- Four forensic experts from Russia, Germany, and Great Britain, who have spent more than a week in Tbilisi in order to participate in a forensic examination of the body of ex-President Zviad Gamsakhurdia, intend to leave Georgia on 25 January. Explaining the reasons for their departure, the experts stated that a decision on conducting the expert examination is being delayed for various reasons, in particular, it was noted, the family of the ex-president first and foremost is against this. At the same time, the experts noted, if the Georgian Ministry of Health again invites them to the republic to take part in an expert examination, they intend to arrive in Tbilisi immediately.
