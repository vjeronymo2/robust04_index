June 12, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

LEONARD WEIGHS 160, HEARNS 162 1/2

Sugar Ray Leonard weighed in at 160 pounds for his World Boxing Council super middleweight title defense tonight against Thomas Hearns, who weighed 162 1/2. The class limit is 168 pounds, but the fighters had an agreement that they could not weigh more than 164 without paying a penalty.
