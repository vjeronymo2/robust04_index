February 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

27TH GOLD NUGGET COMPETITION SET

The Pacific Coast Builders Conference (PCBC) has issued a call for architects, builders, developers and land planners to participate in its 27th annual Gold Nugget -- Best in the West -- Awards competition.

Completed entries must be submitted by April 11.

The competition this year includes more land-use design categories and a "new town" category, according to Joe Pickett Jr., Gold Nugget Awards chairman.

The competition is open to all residential, commercial and industrial developments -- including remodeling and renovation projects -- and those in the site-planning stages in the 14 western states including Texas.

Awards will be presented on June 14 during the PCBC in San Francisco. Entry forms and information are available from: Gold Nugget Awards, PCBC, 605 Market St., Suite 600, San Francisco, Calif. 94105. The telephone number is (415) 543-2600.
