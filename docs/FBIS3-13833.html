Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn will pay a visit to the Sultanate of Oman today. This will be followed by a visit to the State of Qatar on Wednesday [23 March] in order to hold consultations and coordination with the leaders of both fraternal countries. In a statement to AFP, his majesty added that during his visit to Oman he will discuss with His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id the Yemeni accord document signed in Amman on 20 February. His majesty pointed out that there will also be an assessment of the joint efforts regarding what was achieved and the situation in the future; in other words, whether or not to continue [these efforts] or have the kinsfolk and brothers in Yemen tackle their matters and situation [internally]. His majesty regarded his visit to Qatar as heralding the resumption of relations, which were always strong and based on confidence and amity. He pointed out that the visit comes after a long cessation of such fraternal visits.
