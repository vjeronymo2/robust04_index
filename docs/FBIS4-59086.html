BFN [Text] Cairo, 7 May (MENA) -- Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid telephoned Foreign Minister Ahmad Salim Basindwah to inform him of the resolution passed earlier today by an Arab League extraordinary session on Yemen. Tal'at Hamid, press adviser to the Arab League secretary general, has stated that Basindwah pointed out that Yemen welcomes the Arab League's resolution. Basindwah voiced Yemen's readiness to receive a delegation from the Arab League's General Secretariat and to adopt the necessary measures as soon as possible to secure the arrival of the delegation in Sanaa. Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid has formed a high-level delegation from the League's Secretariat led by Major General Sa'id Bayraqdar, assistant secretary general for military affairs, and assigned it to exert efforts to stop the fighting there, but the closure of the Sanaa airport precluded the arrival of the delegation. During the telephone conversation, Dr. 'Abd-al-Majid underscored the interest of all member states to end the fighting and find an appropriate settlement of the Yemeni crisis that could ensure the unity, security, and stability of Yemen.
