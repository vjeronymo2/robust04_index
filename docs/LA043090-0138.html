April 30, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

HOLLYPARK PICK SIX CLIMBS TO 4-YEAR HIGH

The Pick Six carry-over of $320,494.40 on Wednesday at Hollywood Park will be the largest at the track's spring/summer meeting in four years, since $471,528.81 constituted the pool on June 15, 1986.

The pool reached its current level when nobody picked six winners on Saturday or Sunday. The meet opened Wednesday.
