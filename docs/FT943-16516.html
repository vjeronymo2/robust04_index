940704 FT 04 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Storm placing to raise Pounds 2.3m Storm Group, which is involved with intellectual property rights relating to toys and cartoon characters for the children's entertainment market, is raising a net Pounds 2.3m via a placing and open offer. It also announced the disposal of its magazine publishing interests. Storm is issuing 16.97m shares, representing a 21.4 per cent increase in the issued share capital, at 15p per share with a 3-for-14 clawback. USM-traded Storm returned to profitability last year after two years of losses with a 1993 pre-tax profit of Pounds 122,000 (restated Pounds 2.1m loss).
