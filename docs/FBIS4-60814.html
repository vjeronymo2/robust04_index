BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Anna Melnikova and Yuriy Sizov] [Text] Moscow, 30 Apr -- Addressing the talks between the official delegations of the two countries, Boris Yeltsin described as "historic" the first official visit by the Latvian president to Russia. Yeltsin expressed "great satisfaction" with the fact that the first official visit by the Latvian president to Russia had taken place and a large package of documents had been prepared that touches upon the moral and material interests of Russia and Latvia. I would like our relations to cover an even larger spectrum, Yeltsin said. He expressed hope that the visit would create great opportunities for the development of cooperation between the two countries.
