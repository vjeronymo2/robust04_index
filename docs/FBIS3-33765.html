Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Egypt and Italy have signed three protocols on economic cooperation between the two countries worth $670 million. They were signed for Egypt by Dr. Yusuf Butrus Ghali, minister of state for international cooperation, and for Italy by Foreign Minister Beniamino Andreatta. Dr. Ghali had left Paris for Rome two days ago at the end of a conference of the advisory committee of the creditor nations to Egypt, which opened at the World Bank's European headquarters in the French capital on Monday.
