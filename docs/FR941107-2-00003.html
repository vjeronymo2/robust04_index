EFFECTIVE DATE: Upon publication in the Federal Register . DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE Office of the Secretary Members of Performance Review Boards AGENCY: U.S. Department of Agriculture. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: This notice announces the appointment of members of the Performance Review Boards (PRBs) for the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA). The USDA PRBs provide fair and impartial review of Senior Executive Service (SES) performance appraisals and make recommendations to the Secretary of Agriculture, regarding final performance ratings, performance awards, pay adjustments, and Presidential Rank Awards for SES members.
