Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 12 Jan (UNIAR) -- Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, Russian Federation State Duma deputy and leader of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia has made public a statement expressing support for Sergey Shuvaynykov, leader of the Russian Party of the Crimea, in the presidential elections of the Republic of Crimea. Incidentally, the Crimean regional branch of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia, which has not been registered in Ukraine according to the established procedure, belongs to the Russian Party of Crimea as an associate member. Mr. Zhirinovksiy also expresses support for the Russian Party of Crimea during the elections to the Crimean Supreme Council, which will be held on 27 March 1994.
