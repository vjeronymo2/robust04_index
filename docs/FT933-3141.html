930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / UK Company News: EC Commissionclears BT's Dollars 5.3bn link-up with MCI The European Commission has cleared BT's Dollars 5.3bn (Pounds 3.44bn) alliance with MCI, the US long distance carrier, and the Pounds 600m purchase by Alcatel, the French telecommunications supplier, of STC Submarine Cables, the UK-based subsidiary of Northern Telecom of Canada. It said neither of the telecoms deals, announced earlier in the summer, fell within the scope of EC merger regulations. The STC sale is still under consideration by the UK's Office of Fair Trading. A final decision is expected later this month.
