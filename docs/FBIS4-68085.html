BFN [Report from London by Mustafa al-Buhayri in the "Good Day" box] [Text] Millionaire Usamah Bin-Ladin, the primary source of financing for terrorist groups in Egypt and the Arab world, has requested political asylum in Holland. Bin-Ladin, whose Saudi nationality has been withdrawn, manages his terrorist activities from Holland. He left Sudan after bombs were thrown at his home recently. Bin-Ladin currently distributes leaflets opposed to Arab governments and regimes.
