BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 29 (KCNA) -- A national symposium on "For the Ultimate Solution of the Rural Question Under the Banner of the Socialist Rural Theses", a famous work of the great leader President Kim Il-song, was held here on Thursday. The historical letter sent by President Kim Il-song to the national agricultural conference on February 24, 1994, gives a perfect exposition of the goal and tasks for the final solution of the rural question, including the questions of continuously pushing ahead with the three revolutions, ideological, technical and cultural in the countryside to make the farmers revolutionary, assimilate them to the working class and imbue them with the socialist idea and with the chuche idea, and of improving the technological and cultural standards of the agricultural working people to meet the requirements for making all the members of the society intellectually-proficient, and carrying out the five tasks of electrification, water supply and bus service in the rural areas and central heating and gas systems in the rural villages to eleminate the distinctions between the urban and the rural population in their living conditions and complete the industrialisation and modernisation of agruculture on a high level, and of converting cooperative property into all-people property. The speakers at the symposium said the work which raises the final solution of the socialist rural question and the complete victory of socialism as immediate practical questions and comprehensively indicates tasks for their solution is a great programme for the complete victory of socialism. The work is an immortal grand programme which has further developed in depth the theory of the working class on the socialist rural construction and a true textbook of socialist construction which indicates in a unique way the tasks and ways for the ultimate solution of the socialist rural question and the complete victory of socialism, the speakers stressed.
