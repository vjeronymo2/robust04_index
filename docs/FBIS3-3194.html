Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 18 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing met with Jean Gurunlian, executive secretary of World Symposium on Trade Efficiency of the United Nations, and his party here today. The visitors arrived here Wednesday [16 March] as guests of the Chinese Ministry of Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation. The U.N. symposium is scheduled to be held from October 17 to 21 in the United States.
