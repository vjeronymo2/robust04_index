BFN [Text] A meeting has been held at the Military Counterintelligence Administration, during which the participation of this service in events carried out in the Belarusian Armed Forces was discussed. According to the Belarusian Defense Ministry press center, particular attention was attached to qualitatively new conditions for performing counterintelligence functions concerning defense of the Army, detecting agents of foreign special services, protection of military state secrets under conditions of practically unlimited openness, and some other issues.
