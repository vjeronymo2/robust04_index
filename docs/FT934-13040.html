931023 FT 23 OCT 93 / Letters to the Editor: A fair cop if they buy British Sir, Having recently been overtaken and booked by a fast, new and shiny police BMW in Hampshire, I write to inquire as to whether the Sheehy Report identifies how much could be saved by using a centralised car purchasing policy. I plead guilty to driving at 85 mph, but I would rather my fine went towards buying British-made cars and keeping British jobs] R Madelin, 143a Fawe Park Road, Putney, London SW15 2EG
