June 29, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

... BACK BAY CHEERS FROM THE ROBINSONS

John Needham, writer, and Scott Brown, graphic artist, are an incredible team! They produced not only the quintessential article on the back bay, which they were striving to do, but a magnificent work of art as well. On behalf of all the friends of Newport Bay and the bay's varied inhabitants, thank you, The Times, for the very generous, complimentary coverage.

Frank Robinson

Fran Robinson, Newport Beach
