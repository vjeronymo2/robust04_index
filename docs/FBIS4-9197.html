BFN [Text] The French Humanitarian organization (?Premiere) [word indistinct] has reported that Karadzic's Serbs have offered to exchange 11 humanitarian workers from this organization, whom they have held prisoner for two weeks, for 11 Serbian prisoners in Sarajevo. The association received this proposal in a letter from Dragan Kalinic, so-called health minister of the Bosnian Serbs. Kalinic wrote that among the 11 imprisoned Serbs are five doctors and two nurses, and added that the French humanitarians were caught when they tried to deliver non-humanitarian aid to the Muslims. The French Ambassador to Bosnia-Herzegovina informed the organization that the 11 humanitarian volunteers could be tried soon.
