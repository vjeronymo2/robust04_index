May 16, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

SHORT SHOPPERS

Have pity, you supermarkets, giant drug stores and monumental hardware establishments, now all blithely stacking goods to a height of 7 feet.

Please, either set out small stools for customers' use, or instruct your help to take the scowl off their faces when entreated to step an aisle or two over and bring down out-of-reach articles for disadvantaged shorties.

TRUDI BOMBA

Los Angeles
