940808 FT 08 AUG 94 / Explosion injures supected bomber A bomb exploded on a street corner near a Tel Aviv-area cemetery yesterday, injuring the suspected bomber, security sources and witnesses said, Reuter reports from Tel Aviv. They said a youth was carrying a bomb by a cemetery in the suburb of Givatayim when the explosive blew up in his hands. Police said two other bombs were found in the cemetery and defused. They said the motive for the bombing was unclear.
