BFN [Text] The judicial official investigating the bombing of Sayyidat al-Najat Church in Zuq Mikha'il, Magistrate Joseph Furayhah, has issued an indictment against Samir Ja'ja' [leader of the dissolved Lebanese Forces Party]; Antonios Ilyas Ilyas, alias Tony 'Ubayd; Rushdi Ra'd, alias Nabil Munassa; Jean Yusuf Shahin; alias Asad; Jiryis Tawfiq al-Khuri; retired Major Fu'ad Malik; Paul al-Fahl; and Rafiq al-Fahl under the criminal code, which stipulates certain penalties, including the death penalty. Magistrate Furayhah has referred the entire dossier to the Appeals Public Prosecutor, Magistrate Munif 'Uwaydat, for further referral to the Judicial Council so that the suspects may be tried according to the indictment. Three of the suspects are already in custody; Samir Ja'ja', Fu'ad Malik, and Jiryis al-Khuri. Subpoenas in absentia have been issued against the others, who are fugitives.
