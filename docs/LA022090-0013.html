February 20, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

STABBING VICTIM FOUND IN LOGAN HEIGHTS

An unidentified man was found dead, stabbed in the neck, in Logan Heights, San Diego police said Monday.

The victim, believed to be a Latino in his late 20s, carried no identification, according to the coroner's office. His body was found at 6:10 a.m. Sunday in a vacant lot in the 3200 block of National Avenue. He was dressed in a maroon long-sleeve shirt and blue jeans.

Police are investigating.
