Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] President Wasmosy on 21 February ousted Cancio Urbieta, the controversial IBR [Agrarian Reform Institute] chairman, along with three of the five IBR board members. Urbieta and the IBR board members were summoned on 21 February to Government House to tender their resignations. In the afternoon, the executive branch appointed Hugo Halley Merlo as the new IBR chairman. [passage omitted]
