BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 Apr (MENA)--Lebanese Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz has stressed that the bombing of the Sayyidat al-Najat Church was committed after the al-Ibrahimi Mosque massacre to divert international attention away from the massacre. In an interview with the newspaper AL-AHRAM published today, the Lebanese foreign minister said that a number of people have been arrested for the church bombing. These people revealed that they has connections to Israeli officers and had obtained instructions, funds, and equipment that enabled them to assemble the explosive device, Buwayz added. Buwayz maintained that it is not possible to terminate the resistance before obtaining assurances that south Lebanon will return to Lebanon because the resistance is not the reason for the Israeli occupation but a result of it. Responding to a question on the Middle East market, the Lebanese foreign minister said: Any concept of future regional relations must be based on the dimensions of peace.
