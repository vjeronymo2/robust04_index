940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Fleming Mercantile Net asset value at Fleming Mercantile Investment Trust stood at 335.8p per share at July 31, a 9.3 per cent fall on the value at January 31. Net revenue rose from Pounds 4.3m to Pounds 4.7m in the six months. An unchanged second interim dividend of 1.675p is declared.
