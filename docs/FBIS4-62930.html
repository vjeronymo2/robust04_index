BFN [Text] Kiev, 18 May -- The Central Electoral Commission has completed the registration of presidential candidates. Registration certificates were issued to five more contenders: Valeriy Babych, Leonid Kuchma, Volodymyr Lanovyy, Oleksandr Moroz, and Petro Talanchuk. The commission noted that the contenders complied with all the requirements of Article 28 of the electoral law and thus may begin to campaign. The Central Electoral Commission rejected the applications of two contenders who failed to raise the required number of signatures, Ivan Valenya and Mykola Rud. The Central Electoral Commission registered Ivan Plyushch and Leonid Kravchuk earlier.
