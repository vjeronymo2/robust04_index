Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Yesterday evening, His Majesty King Husayn and Her Majesty Queen Nur arrived in Washington from Rochester. Their working visit to the United States is schdeuled to last for several days. During the visit, his majesty will meet with U.S. President Bill Clinton. For the last two days, his majesty has been undergoing planned medical checkups at the Mayo Clinic, a famous medical center. The results of these checkups have proven that there are no signs of the disease [cancer]. His majesty underwent surgery to remove the disease approximately one and a half years ago. His majesty has also undergone other medical checkups; the results of which have been reassuring and good. His majesty enjoys good health, God be praised. [passage omitted]
