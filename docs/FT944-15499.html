941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Audax Properties Audax Properties, a wholly owned subsidiary of Value and Income Trust, doubled pre-tax profits from Pounds 54,000 to Pounds 110,000 for the half year to September 30. There was a realised loss of Pounds 24,000 (Pounds 20,000) on disposal of properties. Earnings per share for the period amounted to 132p (50p).
