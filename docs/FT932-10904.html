930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / International Company News: Deficit widens at German railways DEUTSCHE Bundesbahn, Germany's state-owned national railways, reports net losses for 1992 of DM14.8bn (Dollars 9.25bn), or 54 per cent more than for the previous year, writes Judy Dempsey in Berlin. Turnover for this first quarter of the current year totalled DM6.1bn, or 1.3 per cent lower than the same period last year. Goods transport sales fell by 14 per cent to DM2.5bn.
