February 12, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

LINCOLN'S BIRTHDAY

Monday is Lincoln's Birthday. The following will be closed:

COURTS: Los Angeles superior courts, municipal courts and state appeals courts are closed.

SCHOOLS: Schools in the Los Angeles Unified School District and many other schools are closed.

GOVERNMENT: State offices are closed.

OPEN MONDAY: The Los Angeles Law Library, city offices, county offices, federal courts, federal offices, post offices and the Federal Reserve Bank will be open.
