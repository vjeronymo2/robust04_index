August 12, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

GLACIER TRIPS, BOAT RIDES

Glacier basics: With the exception of the Glacier Bay cruises, you can schedule most glacier trips and boat rides while in Alaska.

Unless you want full-scale cruise ship amenities with loungers on deck, look for companies offering more maneuverable "yacht-sized" boats. This is one situation in which there is no point to looking for off-the-beaten-track companies. Most tours end by Sept. 30.

For more information: Brochures are readily available from travel agents and Alaska tourism centers: Alaska State Division of Tourism, Box E-101, Juneau, Alaska 99811, (907) 465-2010, or the Anchorage Visitor Information Center, 546 W. Fourth Ave., Anchorage, Alaska 99501, (907) 274-3531. Laurie Becklund
