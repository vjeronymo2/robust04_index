BFN [Text] The militia's of Dostam-Golboddin have embarked on an all-round attack on Chardara District, Konduz Province, in order to compensate for their defeats in the capital and in the northern regions during the last few days. The attacks of the murderer militia's, accompanied by heavy air bombardment, on the residential areas of Konduz and Chardara District, caused loss of life and financial damage to people. The militia's also resorted to plundering the people's properties, too. The Islamic Army and Mojahedin embarked on a successful military operation this morning at 0500 in order to foil the attacks by militia's of criminal Golboddin and infidel Dostam. As a result of these operations, the coordinated militia's of Golboddin-Dostam were cleared from the district of Chardara. In the course of these operations more than 100 militia were killed and more than 100 militia were arrested. Among militia that were killed are General Ahmad and General 'Abdol Samad. General Nabi, General Ahmad Ankhoyi, General Saleh, and General 'Abdol Wahed, four other members of the murdering militia's of Golboddin-Dostam were arrested by mojahedin. During these operations more than 300 types of light and heavy weapons were either destroyed or captured.
