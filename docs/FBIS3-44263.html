Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Durban Jan 28 SAPA -- The task group appointed by the Transitional Executive Council [TEC] to inquire into the existence of hit squads in Natal/kwaZulu has begun its investigations in Durban. The TEC said in a statement on Friday the task group would continue to hear evidence and information, conduct interviews and scrutinise documents throughout the weekend and until Wednesday.
