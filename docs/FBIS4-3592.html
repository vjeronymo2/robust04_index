BFN [Text] Haikou, April 21 (XINHUA) -- South China's Hainan Province, the largest special economic zone in the country, will quicken the establishment of a sound property rights market, according to Deputy Governor Wang Xiaofeng. Wang said that the province's property rights transactions market is open to any economically eligible and reliable domestic and overseas investment institution or individual. The property rights can appear in various forms, such as fixed assets of enterprises, management rights, land-use rights and intellectual property rights. The property rights can be transferred by way of agreement or public auction, and investors can buy the assets, take the original debts and conduct barter trade, the deputy governor said. Between April 8 and 13 the province sponsored its first property rights transactions fair, attended by more than 100 domestic enterprises. The entire property rights of three state-owned or public enterprises were bought by other large firms at the fair.
