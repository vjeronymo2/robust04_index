October 21, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

PLEASE DON'T TESTIFY, TOBY

I really don't mind that Roy Schaeffer's cat, Toby, has an appraiser's certificate ("Appraiser Certificate Really the Cat's Meow" by H. W. Moss Oct. 14).

I'm sort of a live-and-let-live guy, and if Toby needs a hobby, appraising real estate seems harmless enough. It's just that I don't want Toby to be testifying as an expert witness in eminent domain valuation cases, which -- judging from the reported court decisions -- he seems to be doing with regularity.

GIDEON KANNER

Los Angeles

Kanner is Editor and Publisher of a monthly periodical on eminent domain.
