940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / International Company News: Burns Philps earnings rise to ADollars 76.3m Burns Philps, the Australian food and hardware retailing company which has been restructuring around its food and food ingredients division, yesterday reported a 40 per cent increase in profits after tax in the six months to end-December, to ADollars 76.3m (USDollars 54.5m) from ADollars 54.5m. The company said it was still preparing to float its hardware retailing division on the stock market, but would consider a trade sale of the business if this was in the interests of shareholders. It said earnings before tax interest and tax from its hardware division were up by 55 per cent at ADollars 27.7m.
