October 4, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: PCC TO SPONSOR INFORMATION NIGHT

Pasadena City College will sponsor college information night in Room 200, Building D on the campus, 1570 E. Colorado Blvd., from 5 to 7 p.m. Tuesday, Oct. 30.

The program gives those who want to earn a bachelor's degree an opportunity to meet with university representatives and receive information on admissions, program requirements, tuition and financial aid.
