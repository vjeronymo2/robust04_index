940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / Tory MP wins BBC libel damages The BBC yesterday agreed to pay Mr Malcolm Moss, Conservative MP for Cambridgeshire North East, substantial damages and legal costs in settlement of a libel action over an allegation that he lied about his earnings while picking onions during private research into agricultural wages. The action concerned a Public Eye programme on BBC2 about how workers were hired on piece rates to harvest or clean fruit and field crops.
