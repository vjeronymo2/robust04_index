920708 FT 08 JUL 92 / International Company News: Time Warner quits venture TIME Warner, the US media group, said it was withdrawing from a consortium, led by Germany's Bertelsmann, which has applied to set up a German television channel, Reuter reports. Time Warner said it had notified the other shareholders that it was exercising its right to withdraw from the consortium, known as the Westschiene Channel (VOX).
