940223 FT 23 FEB 94 / People: Non-executive directors - Wellington Holdings Brian Kent has been appointed non-executive chairman of Wellington Holdings in preparation for its planned flotation next month. Kent, a 62-year-old engineer, recently retired as non-exec-utive chairman of Staveley Industries, where he had formerly been chief executive. He will become president of the Institution of Mechanical Engineers at the end of May. Wellington, which makes high-performance seals and rubber products and has annual turnover of more than Pounds 40m and more than 700 employees, is coming to the London Stock Exchange in mid-March through a placing of shares with institutions.
