Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali received at his office today Dr. Francisco Villiar, Spanish Foreign Ministry under secretary. The prime minister praised the existing relations between the two friendly countries in various areas. He also voiced his thanks and his appreciation for Spain's role and its efforts to bring about a just and comprehensive peace in the Middle East and to prepare for and hold the Madrid peace conference, which took an important step toward bringing about peace in the region. For his part, Villiar conveyed the Spanish prime minister's greetings to the prime minister, as well as the pride he takes in the existing relations between the two friendly countries. Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs, met with Dr. Francisco Villiar at the Foreign Ministry today. They discussed the special Jordanian-Spanish relations, nurtured by His Majesty King Husayn and His Majesty King Juan Carlos. Dr. Villiar conveyed the Spanish foreign minister's greetings to al-Hasan and talked about Spain's role in calling for the Madrid Middle East peace conference. He also referred to Spain's relations with the Arab countries, particularly with Jordan. The two sides praised the Madrid peace conference as a historical step that brought together the disputing parties in the Middle East and turned the conference into a legal term of reference to resolve the Middle East issue.
