920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Aegis poised to switch brokers Aegis, the European media buyer undergoing a controversial refinancing, yesterday announced that it was switching brokers from Rowe & Pitman to Hoare Govett. Aegis said the transfer was amicable and Rowe & Pitman would continue to act as broker until the refinancing was completed, probably next month.
