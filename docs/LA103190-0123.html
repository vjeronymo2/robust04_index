October 31, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

NOTES

McDonnell Douglas Space Systems in Huntington Beach said it launched its 200th Delta rocket from Cape Canaveral, Fla., at 3:16 p.m. on Tuesday.

The space subsidiary of McDonnell Douglas Corp. said a Delta II rocket carried an Inmarsat communications satellite into orbit. Each launch generates an estimated $50 million in revenue for the company.
