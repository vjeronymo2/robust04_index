BFN [Unattributed report: "Statistics Information"] [Text] The State Statistics Institute reports a 8.6 percent productivity decrease in April as compared to the same period last year. The April inflation is 2.4 percent, and living costs rose by 2.6 percent. The number of employed citizens in March was 405,610, with an average wage 7,497 denars. There were 180,644 unemployed, or 0.8 percent more than in the previous month. In the first four months of the year, the total exports fell by 13.1 percent in comparison to last year (the exports totalled $233.7 million), while imports increased by 8.7 percent (total of $261.8 million). Public consumption goods are mostly imported (the import of these goods increased by 85.5 percent).
