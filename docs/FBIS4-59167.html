BFN [Text] Marivan, Kurdistan Prov., May 11, IRNA -- In the attack of the forces of the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan of Iraq [PUK] affiliated to Jalal Talabani against the positions of the Party of Islamic Movement of Kurdistan of Iraq more than 1,400 of the attackers were either killed or injured, according to a member of the Central Council of the Islamic Movement Party. Mamustan Mulla Sadiq told IRNA that the forces of the Islamic Movement of Iraq have prepared themselves for confronting a wide attack from the Patriotic Union in Galuyeh, which could occur at any time. This member of the Central Council stressed that in the course of the clashes during the recent week between the PUK and joint Muslim forces in Northern Iraq the armed guerrillas that are against Turkish Government and also the Komaleh and democrats have cooperated with Jalal Talebani. The region of Galuyeh was under the heavy fire of the PUK since Tuesday morning.
