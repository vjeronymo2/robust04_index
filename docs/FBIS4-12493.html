BFN [Text] Tehran, 10 April, IRNA -- A memorandum of understanding and a protocol were inked here Sunday between Cuban Deputy Foreign Minister Nicolas Rodriguez and his Iranian counterpart Mahmud Va'ezi. The two documents were signed at the second round of talks held between the two officials today. The memorandum of understanding calls for expansion of Tehran-Havana scientific, cultural and technical collaboration and the protocol underlines cooperation between Foreign Ministries of the two countries. The two officials stressed the need for broadening and bolstering of Tehran-Havana ties in all areas.
