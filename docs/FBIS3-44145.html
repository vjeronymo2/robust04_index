Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The government has intensified operations against illegal arms dealers along the Kenya-Sudan border, the Turkana District Commissioner (Simon Mutogwa), has said. He said there would be no compromise on matters of Kenyan security and asked wananchi [citizens] to report suspected leaders to the police. He was reacting to allegations by the Turkana North MP, Mr. Japheth Ekidor, who alleged that people from outside the district were in Lokichoggio with the intention of buying arms from the Sudanese.
