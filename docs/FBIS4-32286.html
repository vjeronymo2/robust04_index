BFN [Text] Tirana, June 21 (ATA) -- Pupils' enrollment in the secondary schools of Kosova began yesterday. In the secondary school Xhevded Doda in Prishtina, the Serb police acted against pupils to be enrolled, arresting four of them. Other persons are reported arrested by the police. The Serb police also acted against enrollment of pupils in the Middle School of Medicine Xheladin Deda and the Middle School Hasan Prishtina in Mitrovice. The police asked the pupils and teachers to leave these centres.
