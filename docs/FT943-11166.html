940801 FT 01 AUG 94 / Observer: Doctor on call Now that France's constitutional council has watered down the dafter bits of the planned legislation to ban the use of Franglais, would it be too much to ask US news organisations, such as CNN, to report the work of a well-known international relief organisation, such as Medecins sans Frontieres, without feeling it necessary to translate its name into 'Doctors without borders'?
