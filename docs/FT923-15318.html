920702 FT 02 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Walker & Staff jumps by 57% Walker & Staff Holdings, the valve and pipeline equipment distributor, reported a 57 per cent improvement in annual profits. On turnover down 2 per cent at Pounds 8.35m, pre-tax profits for the 12 months to March 31 advanced from Pounds 389,000 to Pounds 610,000. The recommended single distribution for the year goes up from 3.3p to 5p, covered 3.7 times by earnings of 18.5p (11p) per share.
