Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 1 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song Friday [31 December] met with a home-visiting group of students of Korean University under the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon) and an art troupe of Korean school children in Japan staying in the socialist homeland. Kim Yong-sun was on hand. The school children in Japan extended New Year's greetings to President Kim Il-song and wished him a long life in good health. President Kim Il-song had a cordial talk and posed for a souvenir picture with them.
