940422 FT 22 APR 94 / UK Company News: United Energy United Energy, the USM-quoted oil and gas production company, reported lower pre- and post-tax profits of Pounds 23,000 for 1993 after increased interest charges. Profits in 1992 were Pounds 58,000. Turnover was 7.5 per cent higher at Pounds 3m (Pounds 2.79m), generating operating profits of Pounds 226,000 (Pounds 63,000). The net interest charge jumped from Pounds 5,000 to Pounds 203,000. Mr John Billington, chairman, said the result had been marred by falling oil prices. Earnings per share slipped to 0.1p (0.2p).
