920311 FT 11 MAR 92 / World News in Brief: Murder charges A 44-year-old former van driver is to be charged with kidnapping and murdering three schoolgirls. Londoner Robert Black is accused of the kidnap and murder of Susan Maxwell in 1982, as well as the murders of Caroline Hogg in 1983, Sarah Harper in 1986 and the kidnap of Teresa Thornhill in 1988.
