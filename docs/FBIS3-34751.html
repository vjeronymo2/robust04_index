Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In a statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', brother Ahmad Jabir 'Afif, rapporteur of the Political Forces Dialogue Committee, explained that the committee last night concluded its discussions and approval of issues pertaining to the constitutional, legal, and procedural guarantees concerning the signing of the accord document in the Jordanian capital, Amman. Brother Ahmad Jabir 'Afif indicated that based on an agreement with the brothers in the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, it has been decided to set 10 Ramadan, corresponding to 20 February, as the date for signing the agreement. At the conclusion of his statement, brother Ahmad Jabir 'Afif extended the Political Forces Dialogue Committee's congratulations to our Yemeni people and all peoples of Arab and Islamic nations on the beginning of the blessed month of Ramadan.
