GRAND OPENING: IN HILLS OF ANAHEIM

Crowne Summit opens today in the hills of Anaheim, featuring 24 two-story attached houses priced from $250,000 to $350,000.

The three- and four-bedroom townhouses by Boulevard Development Inc. are available in three floor plans ranging in size from 1,484 to 1,900 square feet and have tile roofs, up to three bathrooms, rear-yard fencing and front-yard landscaping.

Move-ins are scheduled to begin in late July and the homeowners association fee is $161.18 a month. The project is near Anaheim Hills, but is outside the boundaries of that planned development.

The sales office is open daily from 10 a.m. to 5 p.m. at 803 Shanada Court, reached from the southbound Imperial Highway exit of the Riverside (91) Freeway.
