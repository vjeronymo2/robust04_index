941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Border TV 6% ahead to Pounds 849,000 Border Television, the Carlisle-based independent television company, lifted pre-tax profits by 6 per cent, from Pounds 802,000 to Pounds 849,000, in the half year ended October 31. The result, on turnover up 15 per cent at Pounds 5.59m (Pounds 4.85m), was after a Pounds 46,000 loss from an associated company and a Pounds 297,000 exceptional charge relating to start-up costs of Century Radio and Scot-FM. Mr Melvyn Bragg, chairman, said that radio operations were expected to contribute to profits in mid-1995. Earnings slipped to 6.1p (6.7p) and the interim dividend is raised from 1.6p to 1.9p. The company trades on the USM.
