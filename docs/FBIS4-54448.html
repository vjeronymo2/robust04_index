BFN [Text] Tirana, April 26 (ATA) -- In early hours of April 25, Serbian forces that control the border between Kosova and Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] killed the Albanian Naim Bajrami from Llashtice of Gjilan. Still no reports have arrived about the conditions in which Bajrami, father of two children, was killed. He was killed near the Miratovc village, the commune of Presheve. The sub Council for the Defence of the Human Rights and Freedoms in Gjilan reports that Naim was searched by Serbian police while during the last week police searched his home in Llashtice, under the pretext of looking for arms. He was killed while he was leaving Kosova because of the pressures and threats by the Serbian police. Bajrami is the second Albanian victim of the Serbian terror killed on the Kosova-Macedonia border since the beginning of this year.
