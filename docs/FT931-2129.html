930322 FT 22 MAR 93 / Construction Contracts: Maintaining motorways in Manchester EDMUND NUTTALL has been awarded the Greater Manchester motorways term maintenance contract 4 by the Department of Transport. The Pounds 23m contract extends over a period of four years, beginning in mid-March with completion in April 1997. The works involve the maintenance of the live motorway network in the Greater Manchester area, comprising the M56, M61, M62, M63, M66, M67, M602, A627(M) and A5103. A full range of maintenance activities will be provided for approximately 220 kilometres of highway in the area, including over 800 structures and 12,000 lighting units. Operating out of the Barton administration centre on the M63 and using four satellite depots on the network, Nuttall will undertake routine winter maintenance activities, traffic management, carriageway repairs as well as emergency response operations. In addition, sign and road lighting will be maintained, safety and boundary fences installed and repaired, gullies swept and emptied and structures maintained.
