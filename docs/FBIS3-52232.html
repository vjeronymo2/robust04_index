Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, the prime minister, today received in his office at the Prime Ministry Dr. George Habash, secretary general of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine [PFLP]. The two sides reviewed developments in the Palestinian issue and the peace march in the region. Dr. Habash said he held talks with the prime minister on the peace march during which Dr. al-Majali reaffirmed Jordan's support for the Palestinian issue. He added that Jordan, under the leadership of His Majesty King Husayn, will continue to support the Palestinian issue. Habash said: We seek to achieve our national program and our trends toward repatriation and self-determination, establishing an independent Palestinian state.
