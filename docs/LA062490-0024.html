June 24, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: QUIBBLES & BITS

. . . Will Richard Pryor and Gene Wilder do it again? We're told that there are discussions at Tri-Star about a fourth Pryor-Wilder comedy teaming, titled "Another You," and that Peter Bogdanovich is a possibility to direct. The actors teamed for last year's "See No Evil, Hear No Evil," as well as "Stir Crazy" (1980) and "Silver Streak" (1976).

. . . Ever wonder who would win a battle between the monster from "Alien" and the title nasty from "Predator"? Look for the answer in "Predator II: Body Count" (due at Christmas), which features the predator's trophy case -- filled with the skulls of his human and inhuman victims, including one that's unmistakably that of the Alien.
