BFN [Text] Beijing, June 1 (XINHUA) -- Wang Hanbin, vice-chairman of the Chinese National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, met with Lamine Sidime, president of Guinea's Supreme Court, here today. Wang exchanged views with the Guinean visitors on how to strengthen the friendly exchanges and cooperation between the two countries. Sidime arrived here yesterday as guest of the Chinese Supreme People's Court. It is the first formal contact between the Supreme Courts of the two countries.
