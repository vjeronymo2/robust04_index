940524 FT 24 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Richards loss deepens to Pounds 1.6m Losses at Richards, the Aberdeen-based textile manufacturer, widened from Pounds 326,000 to Pounds 1.62m pre-tax for the six months ended March 31 1994. The figure included provisions totalling Pounds 1.61m for the period. Before these items there was a swing at the operating level from losses of Pounds 248,000 to profits of Pounds 220,000 from continuing activities. Turnover of the ongoing businesses rose to Pounds 38.5m, compared with Pounds 33.3m. Losses per share emerged at 5.56p against a previous 0.63p, while the interim dividend is maintained at 1.07p net per share.
