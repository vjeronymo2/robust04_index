931022 FT 22 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Airflow falls to Pounds 464,000 Profits at Airflow Streamlines, a manufacturer of cabs for industrial tractors and trucks, fell from Pounds 860,000 to Pounds 464,000 pre-tax for the half year to August 31. Turnover was static at Pounds 34.34m (Pounds 34.7m) - the company also has Ford car and commercial vehicle dealerships. Earnings emerged at 3.34p (6.44p) and the interim dividend is a same-again 1p.
