Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A delegation from the All-Polish Trade Unions Agreement [OPZZ] led by its chairman, Mrs. Ewa Spychalska, arrived in Damascus at noon today on a four-day visit to Syria. The delegation was received at Damascus International Airport by comrade 'Izz-al-Din Nasir, head of the General Federation of Trade Unions [GFTU]; the comrade members of the GFTU Executive Bureau; the comrade head and members of the Damascus Trade Union Federation; and several comrade trade unionists. Mrs. Spychalska, made a statement to a SANA correspondent. She said that her visit to Syria is cordial and that she hopes to meet with comrade 'Izz-al-Din Nasir in order to consolidate the relations of cooperation between the Syrian GFTU and the Polish OPZZ and to foster coordination between the two sides in order to cooperate in all fields that serve the interests of both organizations. She added that the second objective of this visit is to hold talks on coordination between the two organizations in order to prepare for the meeting of the World Federation of Trade Unions, which will convene in Syria in the last quarter of this year.
