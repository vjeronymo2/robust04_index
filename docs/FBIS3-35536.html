Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In an initial reaction, the joint command of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine [PFLP] and the Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine [DFLP] has strongly condemned today's ugly crime at Ibrahimi Mosque in which many defenseless Palestinian citizens were killed by the armed gangs of settlers under the very eyes and ears of the occupation forces. In the name of the Red Star and Red Eagle Battalions and on behalf of the militant nationalist forces, the joint command pledged to the martyrs and the Palestinian people in the occupied territores to take revenge on the occupation and the settlers. The two fronts appealed to the international community to act immediately to secure peace and protection for the Palestinian people under occupation, and called on the PLO command to learn from what is taking place in the occupied territories and to suspend the separate negotiations and the destructive partial deals which have catastrophic results on the Palestinian people. The two fronts hold the Israeli Army responsible for this crime.
