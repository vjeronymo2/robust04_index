Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 19 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] sent messages to foreign parties in reply to their messages of greetings on the birthday of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il . The reply messages were addressed to the Central Committee of the Communist Party of India (Marxist), the Central Committee of the Mozambique Liberation Front Party, the Central Executive Committee of the People's National Congress of Guyana, the national leadership of the Central Committee of the Socialist People's Party of Mexico, the Political Committee of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Chile, the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Argentina and the Central Committee of the October 8 Revolutionary Movement of Brazil. The WPK Central Committee in reply messages expressed thanks to the foreign parties for their congratulations on the birthday of Comrade Kim Chong-il and the conviction that the friendly and cooperative relations between the WPK and these parties would continue to develop on good terms.
