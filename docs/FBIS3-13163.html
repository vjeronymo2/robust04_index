Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] SHAS [Torah Observing Sephardim] sources said today that the faction will return to the government within 10 days. According to these sources, the prime minister promised Rabbi 'Ovadya Yosef to fulfill SHAS' demands. [passage omitted] A meeting between Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin and Knesset Members Arye Der'i and Rafi Edri ended a short time ago at the prime minister's bureau. Our party affairs correspondent reports that SHAS' return to the coalition within the next few days, even before the prime minister's departure for the United States, was conclusively agreed on at the meeting.
