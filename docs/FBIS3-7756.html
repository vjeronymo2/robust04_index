Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Report by Ahmed Begicevic from Novi Travnik] [Text] Early this morning the citizens of Novi Travnik and Travnik were awoken by a series of powerful explosions. According to the information that we have received from the Operative Group Bosanska Krajina, this morning at 0645 [0545 GMT] a group of six Galeb fighter planes bearing the insignia of the former Yugoslav People's Army entered the air space of our country despite the UN no-fly resolution. Four planes bombed the Bratstvo Factory in Novi Travnik, causing considerable material damage. Just after this air raid NATO planes appeared in the sky, and the attackers dispersed and tried to escape in various directions. Early morning eye-witnesses in Travnik have confirmed that an armed clash took place in the sky, and the attackers were attacked by missiles from the NATO planes, which were much faster.
