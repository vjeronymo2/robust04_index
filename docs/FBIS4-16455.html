BFN [Text] RIA reports: Russia needs a powerful, modern navy, Russian Federation Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin said at a ceremony to raise the St. Andrew's flag over the new destroyer, Intrepid [Besstrashnyy], to bring it into service. The destroyer handed over to the Russian Navy today is the first naval ship to be launched for two years. Viktor Chernomyrdin noted that the task of constructing a new Russian fleet is being tackled under extremely difficult conditions connected with the loss of a number of military bases in the Baltic, on the Black Sea, and the Caspian, and also with the difficult economic condition and the complicated political situation.
