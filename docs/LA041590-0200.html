April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

Three cheers to KTTV for showing "The Andy Griffith Show" twice a day.

Fans like me tape the shows to keep. Unfortunately, the station must be desperate for commercial time. It edits the shows severely. The worst part is they don't show the end of the program. It's like listening to a wonderful joke with the punch line omitted.

Kenton L. Moody, Los Angeles
