BFN [Text] At a House of Representatives' Budget Committee meeting on 8 June, Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata commented on the DPRK's suspected development of nuclear weapons. He said: "The situation will become serious if nuclear arms development should progress. In some cases, sanctions will be necessary to deal with such a situation. In a conflict of power, I think (imposing sanctions) is effective under certain circumstances." In this way, the prime minister stressed the effectiveness of sanctions by the UN Security Council.
