940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / International Company News: Credit Lyonnais in talks Credit Lyonnais, the French state-owned bank, is in talks with potential partners in the insurance industry, with a view to marketing and distributing non-life products. The bank said talks were being held with a number of insurance companies, but declined to confirm reports that Allianz of Germany was among them. Les Echos, the French financial daily newspaper, reported yesterday that high-level discussions had been held with Allianz. Credit Lyonnais said it was seeking a partnership with a company which could supply non-life products, to be marketed through the bank's retail network. Initially, the partnership would concentrate on the French market.
