Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 26 (XINHUA) -- Premier Li Peng called today for efforts to achieve electrification in 200 counties during the 1991-95 period. In a letter to a national conference on hydro-power-based rural electrification, which closed here today, Li asked various central departments and government bodies at all levels to do everything within their power to assist the electrification drive. "The electrification of 200 counties will play a significant role in helping the rural residents in mountainous areas eliminate poverty, expand the local economy and promote ideological and cultural progress," he said. According to delegates at the conference, hydro-electric power stations with a total generating capacity of 15 million kilowatts will be built in the countryside by the end of the century. The Chinese Government plans to achieve hydro-power-based electrification in 200 counties by 1995 and another 300 counties during the 1996-2000 period.
