BFN [Text] Baku (June 2) (TURAN) -- Azerbaijan's Defense Ministry reports that there has been no fighting on the front over the past day. Nakhchivan's Supreme Soviet reports that Armenian troops continue to consolidate their positions on the strategically important hills in the Sharur region of Nakhichevan, which they captured on May 27. As a result of these operations, Armenian troops managed to move 3-5 kilometres further into the territory of Nakhichevan.
