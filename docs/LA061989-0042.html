June 19, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: FOUNTAIN VALLEY;

WORK ON WARNER AVENUE TO SLOW, DETOUR TRAFFIC

Warner Avenue between Euclid Street and the Santa Ana River is undergoing a major reconstruction that will cause traffic slowdowns and detours in the area through September.

In announcing the $850,000 project, the city of Fountain Valley urged residents and businesses to seek alternate routes.

The project will primarily affect two residential neighborhoods. One is bounded by Euclid Street, Warner Avenue, Newhope Street and the Warner Flood Control Channel. The other is bounded by Newhope, Warner, the Santa Ana River, Harbor Boulevard and Quartz Street.

Those who normally use Warner to enter and exit their neighborhoods will have to use Euclid and Newhope streets.
