BFN [Text] Jia Qinglin, provincial party committee secretary and chairman of the people's congress standing committee, met in Hong Kong yesterday [4 May] with some Hong Kong-based Fujian officials, including the provincial department and bureau officials, as well as prefectural and city officials. Secretary Jia Qinglin called on the Hong Kong-based Fujian institutions to become both a bridge and a springboard linking Fujian with the outside world Earlier, a Fujian delegation led by Jia Qinglin had visited Malaysia. Jia Qinglin and his entourage arrived in Hong Kong after winding up their official visit to Malaysia.
