BFN [Text] Zhuhai, April 21 (XINHUA) -- China has chosen its 100 largest retail shops in 1993, the State Statistics Bureau and the Ministry of Interior Trade jointly announced here recently. The three biggest were the Shanghai First Department Store Corp. Ltd, the Tianjin Lida International Store Corp. Ltd and the Beijing Xidan Store Corp. Ltd. Statistics show that 210 retail outlets in China had achieved more than 120 million yuan each in turnover last year.
