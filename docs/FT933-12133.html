930726 FT 26 JUL 93 / Observer: Time lag Stung by the official prisons inspector's remark that most inmates prefer idleness to the many opportunities to make good use of their time, Wolds private jail in Humberside is giving references to departing clients who stirred themselves. An example, handed to one who worked as a cleaner, testifies that besides being a hard worker, he was 'an excellent timekeeper' - which, leaving aside the question of how he could have been otherwise, is perhaps more than the prison's management could claim. The jail is run by Group 4, whose late deliveries of prisoners to courts has been the subject of acid comments from the judiciary.
