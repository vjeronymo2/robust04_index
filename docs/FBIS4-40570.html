BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Yuriy Kozlov and Boris Krivoshey] Moscow June 16 TASS -- Russia is trying to maintain friendly relations with not only the CIS country-members, but with the Baltic countries as well, according to Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev. Kozyrev, speaking at a foreign policy council meeting on Thursday, said civilised dialogue was being strengthened between Russia and the three Baltic states. The questions of human rights violations and the rights of the Russian-language population and other minorities in the Baltic region are still on the agenda, he said. The Russian foreign minister noted that the complete withdrawal of Russian troops from the Baltic countries would not lead to a decrease in attention paid to those questions.
