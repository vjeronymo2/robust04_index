940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Equities in Sao Paulo closed 2.27 per cent stronger on bargain hunting, and traders expected the market to open higher today due to optimism over the government's plan to introduce a new currency and cut inflation. The Bovespa index of the 56 most active shares gained 710 points at 31,985, after peak-ing at about 32,521 at midday. Volume was on the low side at Cr479.7bn (Dollars 195.7m).
