BFN [Text] Khalid 'Abd-al-Mun'im Rashid al-Janabi, Iraqi President Saddam Husayn's adviser and personal envoy, has arrived to offer condolences to His Majesty King Husayn on the death of Her Majesty Queen Zayn al-Sharaf. He was received at Amman airport by Dr. Khalid al-Karaki, his majesty's adviser, and the Iraqi ambassador in Amman.
