921017 FT 17 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Anglo St James Pounds 289,000 in the red Anglo St James, the commercial property developer, incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 289,000 in the first half of 1992. Last year the loss was Pounds 499,000 but was not directly comparable as St James Estates was an associate at the time. The rental income now exceeded interest payable on an annualised basis and made a substantial contribution towards overheads. Turnover amounted to Pounds 1.35m (Pounds 174,000). Losses per share for the period were 1.8p (4.8p).
