930105 FT 05 JAN 93 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 6.5m workload for WSJ (Contractors) Contracts valued at Pounds 6.5m have been won by WSJ (CONTRACTORS), based in Oswestry, on the Shropshire Welsh border. In Shropshire, contracts totalling Pounds 3.4m have been secured and include the Pounds 2.4m refurbishment of Shrewsbury's swimming centre and three housing renovation schemes amounting to Pounds 1m, for clients Shrewsbury and Atcham Borough Council, Oswestry Borough Council and Wrekin District Council. In Powys, projects include; a new industrial unit in Welshpool for the DBRW, valued at Pounds 1.6m, and the construction of six bungalows for Montgomeryshire District Council. A further three projects in Clwyd, totalling Pounds 1m, are for the following clients; Clwyd County Council, Wrexham Maelor Borough Council and Delyn Borough Council.
