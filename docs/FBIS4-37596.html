BFN [Text] President 'Arafat and his delegation discussed the latest developments in the occupied territories and the need to hasten the provision of all kinds of assistance required for starting the reconstruction process and building of the structures of the Palestinian National Authority in the self-rule territories. The meeting was attended by brother Abu-Lutf [Faruq Qaddumi], brother (Hail Fahhum) and brother Abu-Karsh.
