940521 FT 21 MAY 94 / Finance and the Family: Smaller is bigger - At a glance Smaller companies have produced the best returns for investors in UK shares in 1994. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (Capital Gains tax version) fell 0.3 per cent to 1735.64 over the week to May 20. Since the start of this year it has risen 2.6 per cent, while the FTSE-A All-Share index has fallen 6.5 per cent, and the FTSE 100 index has fallen 8.6 per cent.
