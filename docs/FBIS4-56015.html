BFN [TA SR report: "Reconstruction of the Bohunice Nuclear Power Station"] [Text] Bratislava -- The three stages of the gradual reconstruction of the V-1 nuclear power station at Jaslovske Bohunice will require costs of 11.3 billion korunas [Sk]. The first stage will involve the revalidation of safety-related installations and systems that are to be used in further operation of the unit. The second stage will focus on enhancing the capacity of the emergency systems and the cooling of the active zone for the primary circuit. The aim of the third stage is to enhance the capacity of the system for confining an accident. The reconstruction costs will be divided into individual years according to the cost-intensiveness of needed investments. A sum of Sk315 million will be appropriated in 1994, Sk3.07 billion in 1995, Sk3.25 billion in 1996, Sk3.15 billion in 1997, and Sk1.515 billion in 1998.
