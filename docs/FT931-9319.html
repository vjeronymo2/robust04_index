930215 FT 15 FEB 93 / Construction Contracts: Providing accommodation for students WIMPEY CONSTRUCTION has won two contracts worth Pounds 6.3m to provide three halls of residence which will house a total of more than 460 students. The larger of the two contracts for the Nottingham Trent University has been divided into two stages. The first comprises the construction of a 160 bed accommmodation block. The second phase involves the refurbishment of the Basford Maltings, a listed malt house building, into a five-storey 107 bed accommmodation complex.
