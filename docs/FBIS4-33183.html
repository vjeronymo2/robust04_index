BFN [Excerpts] Iran's Minister of Construction Jihad Gholam Reza Foruzesh arrived in Havana accompanied by an ample delegation, made up of vice ministers and government authorities. Foruzesh stated that relations between Cuba and Iran are very good and the purpose of his visit is to strengthen them even more. His visit will also cover the analysis of new areas of cooperation between both nations primarily in the areas of technology and fishing. Foruzesh will analyze, together with Cuban authorities, the paths for broadening trade and economic relations in a short period of time with Cuba . The delegation will cover a tight agenda during their visit to Cuba, from which new projects could develop. [passage omitted] [Begin Foruzesh recording in Persian with passage by passage translation into Spanish] There are very good relations, especially diplomatic ones, between Cuba and Iran. We have come to analyze new areas of cooperation between both nations, primarily in the areas of technology and fishing, as well as to study new paths for broadening trade and economic relations. I see this future cooperation as a clear and positive. [end recording] Minister of Foreign Trade Ricardo Cabrisas and Jose M. Miyar, both members of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Cuba, greeted the Iranian delegation upon their arrival.
