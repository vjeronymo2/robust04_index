940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Kembrey Kembrey, the electrical connectors and accessories manufacturer, swung from losses of Pounds 655,000 to profits of Pounds 15,000 pre-tax for the year to April 2. Turnover fell from Pounds 22m to Pounds 18.4m. A proposed single dividend of 0.155p (same) is being paid from earnings of 0.11p (losses 1.55p).
