Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt from interview with Anatoliy Chubays, deputy prime minister and chairman of the State Committee for State Property, by unidentified correspondent; place and date not given -- recorded] [Text] [Chubays] It is hard for me to speak about the whole government or forecast its destiny. But I can speak for myself personally, especially as my mind is made up. I am sure I am obliged to bring to completion the job for which I stood for election. Privatization will be brought to conclusion and the commitments that were made will be fulfilled.
