Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Ilyas al-Hirawi has sent a congratulatory cable to Liamine Zeroual on his assumption of power in Algeria, wishing him success. He also wished stability and prosperity for the fraternal Algerian people. The president also cabled King Husayn of Jordan offering condolences on the assassination of al-Ma'ayitah, the charge d'affaires [title as heard] of the Jordanian Embassy in Beirut. He conveyed Lebanon's denunciation of violence and eagerness to reject any attempt to mar relations with Jordan. He noted that the competent security authorities have taken the appropriate measures to investigate the painful incident.
