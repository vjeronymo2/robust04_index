BFN [Text] Guangzhou's foreign-related economy and foreign trade have been developing in a healthy way. By the end of March this year, another group of 699 contracts, using foreign capital valued at $3.38 billion, had been signed throughout the city, an increase 4.6 percent over the same period last year. In addition, foreign capital totalling $489 million actually had been used, 80.8 percent more than in the same period last year. The city's export volume reached $820 million, registering a 23 percent increase over the corresponding period last year. Last year, the development of Guangzhou's foreign-related economy and foreign trade were marked by the following five main features: 1) The actual amount of invested capital increased by a greater margin owing to direct investment by foreign businessmen; 2) The scale of investment was larger and the quality of investment projects was higher; 3) The areas in which foreign businessmen made direct investments achieved diversified development; 4) There was a smooth transition of foreign trade and export to a new system, and a faster increase in exports, changing the previous situation of insufficient exports at the beginning of the year, and excessive increases at the end of the year; 5) New progress was made in opening up a diversified market. During the first quarter, Guangzhou examined and approved the establishment of three enterprises from abroad, and six enterprises were registered.
