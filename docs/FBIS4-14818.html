BFN [Text] The Association of Latvian National Forces has sent a letter to Estonian President Lennart Meri and State Assembly Chairman Ivo Nugis. The letter expresses support for the determination of the Estonian Government and people to achieve complete and unconditional withdrawal of the occupation forces from Estonia within the shortest possible timescale and without any additional conditions being attached by the Russian side. The letter also expresses regret at the Latvian Government's yielding to Russia.
