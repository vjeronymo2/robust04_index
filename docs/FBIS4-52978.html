BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 15 (KCNA) -- Some 800 students affiliated with the South Korean Federation of General Student Councils (Hanchongnyon) held a rally at Hanyang campus on May 14 demanding the investigation into the Kwangju massacre and the punishment of those responsible for it, a Seoul-based radio reported. Noting that the truth behind the massacre has not yet been made clear, the ralliers strongly demanded the punishment of those responsible for it including two former "presidents," Chon Tu-hwan and No Tae-u. After the rally, they went out of the campus demanding the "government"-level investigation and the punishment of the culprits.
