June 18, 1989, Sunday, Southland Edition

BOY SCOUT TROOP RESCUED AFTER NIGHT ON MOUNTAIN

Seventeen Boy Scouts and three troop leaders who got lost during a heavy rainstorm spent a chilly night on a mountain ridge before they were rescued by helicopter Saturday. Members of Troop 199 of the Church of Latter-day Saints in Kahuku had been on a 62-mile hike across the Koolau Mountain range when they became stranded by the weather.
