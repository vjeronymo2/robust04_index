Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- Following the failure of last weekend's Croatian-Bosnian Petersberg conference, Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel has said he seriously doubts the various sides' willingness for peace. During talks with the American UN Ambassador Madeleine Albright late last night, Kinkel said that we have to "seriously ask ourselves to what degree the conflicting parties are currently driven by a genuine desire for peace," the Foreign Ministry announced today. In this context, Kinkel referred to the Muslim offensive against areas to which the Croats are entitled.
