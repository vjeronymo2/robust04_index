MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

Lampoon Picks the Right Comedian: The Harvard Lampoon will dub Steven Wright as its Comedian of the Year at a Friday ceremony.

Wright joins such entertainers as Bill Cosby, Robin Williams, John Cleese, Jay Leno, John Candy and Christopher Guest who all have come to Harvard to receive humor awards from the Lampoon. Wright will be presented with his award in a ceremony following his concert at Harvard's Sanders Theater. The Lampoon is the nation's oldest humor magazine. It also makes the annual Hasty Pudding awards to celebrities. BETH KLEID
