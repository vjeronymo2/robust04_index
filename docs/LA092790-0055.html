September 27, 1990, Thursday, South Bay Edition

WEDDING THEME FOR HOSPITAL DINNER

"The Wedding of the Century" is the theme of a fund-raising dinner dance to be held by the YES support group of Little Company of Mary Hospital at 7 p.m. Saturday at Rolling Hills Country Club, 27000 Palos Verdes Drive East, Rolling Hills Estates.

Those attending are invited to wear wedding attire.

There will be a wedding cake at each table, a five-course meal, and dancing to the music of Billy Haynes and the Fabulous Charmers. Cost is $65 a person. For information, call 543-6903.
