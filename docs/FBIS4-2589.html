BFN [Text] Beijing, April 11 (XINHUA) -- Wang Guangying, vice- chairman of the National People's Congress Standing Committee, met here this evening with a delegation headed by Hoseyn Sobhani-Nia, chairman of the Iranian-Chinese Friendship Group under the Iranian parliament. In the afternoon, Meng Liankun, chairman of the Sino-Iranian friendship group under the National People's Congress, held talks with the Iranian guests.
