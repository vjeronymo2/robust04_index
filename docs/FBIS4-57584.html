BFN [Text] During the assessment meeting held in Alquizar, Agriculture Minister Alfredo Jordan Morales extended recognition to members of the Revolutionary Armed Forces [FAR] and of the Youth Labor Army [EJT] who participated in the potato harvest in Havana Province: [Begin Jordan recording] Our Armed Forces have provided the greatest contribution in collecting 76 percent of the harvest. We often hear positive and encouraging opinions everywhere praising the revolutionary cadres -- whether linked or not linked with agriculture -- because these opinions are in keeping with the opinions of the party cadres and because they convey the opinions of the people in the production fields, stressing the organization, the discipline, and the directness with which officers of the Revolutionary Armed Forces work toward fulfilling this mission. [end recording]
