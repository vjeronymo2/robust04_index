Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sergey Shakhray, the PRUC (Party of Russian Unity and Concord) leader, is engaged in a splitting activity which will increase the confrontational trends even before the State Duma opens, Interfax was told by a senior official in the presidential headquarters who preferred to remain anonymous. He noted that Shakhray called a meeting of Duma members who were elected in single-mandate districts from the republics incorporated in Russia. Following Shakhray's speech, 15 deputies of Tuva, Yakutia, Karachayevo-Cherkessia, Mari-El, Dagestan, Ingushetia, North Ossetia and other autonomies declared the setting up of a group which would nominate Sergey Shakhray for the position of the State Duma speaker. The deputies said that the group will work for implementation of the Federative Treaty. They want this group to be given the standing of a parliamentary party to be able to contribute to the formation of the State Duma bodies.
