January 10, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Larry Hanson and Terry Noonan have been appointed to the newly created position of executive vice president of Furon, a Laguna Niguel-based manufacturer of polymer components formerly known as Fluorocarbon.

Both had previously been group vice presidents. Hanson will be responsible for Furon's fluid-sealing and advanced-polymers groups. Noonan will be responsible for the extrusion-technologies group, the engineered-products group and the CHR division.
