January 23, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

IVORY COAST, ZIMBABWE TIE IN SOCCER QUALIFYING

Ivory Coast and Zimbabwe played to a scoreless tie Sunday in a World Cup qualifying-round soccer match at the National Sports Stadium.

Ivory Coast got a point for the tie and moved to the top of its qualifying section with 3 points from 2 matches. Ivory Coast is a point ahead of Algeria, which has a match in hand.
