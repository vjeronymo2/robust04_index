April 25, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

MOORPARK DISMISSES BOYS' BASKETBALL COACH

Rick Kent, dismissed as boys' basketball coach at Moorpark High, has rejected the school's offer of a lower-level coaching position. Kent was fired last week after five seasons, including a 7-13 mark last season.

No replacement has been named by Moorpark Athletic Director Rick Uphoff. ROCKY PINHEIRO
