BFN [Text] Cajnice, 12 Apr (TANJUG) -- Muslim units resumed their attacks on all Serb positions on the right bank of the Drina river this morning, the command of the Srpska Herzegovina Corps said. According to the same source, Muslims have opened fierce fire from all types of artillery weapons from Gorazde, a UN safe area. At the same time, Muslim infantry units launched attacks on the Jarciste, Gradine, and Majdan areas. According to the command of the Herzegovina Corps all Muslim attacks have been repelled and the front line has not changed today.
