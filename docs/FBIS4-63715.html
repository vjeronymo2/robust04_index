BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow, 25 May -- The State Duma has today instructed the Credentials Commission of the Duma to verify the authenticity of reports in some of the mass media saying that a number of electoral associations that now have factions represented in the Duma used funds for their election campaign, which were received from abroad. In addition, the Credentials Commission of the State Duma has been instructed to elucidate whether there is any truth in reports saying that not enough of the electorate voted for the adoption of the new Russian Constitution. The commission must provide its response to these issues in two weeks.
