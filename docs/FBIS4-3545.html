BFN [Text] Ashgabat, April 21 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Premier Li Peng said here today that China gives priority to developing relations with the neighboring countries. He made the remarks at a welcome banquet hosted by president S.A. Niyazov this evening. He said China and Turkmenistan are both concentrating on economic construction, both in need of a long-term peaceful international environment. "We attach great importance to the consolidation and development of relations with the neighboring countries, including Turkmenistan, and place such ties in a position of priority in foreign relations," Li said. On bilateral ties, Li said that during this visit, China and Turkmenistan reached new agreement and understanding on the expansion of cooperation. "We have every reason to believe that the friendly and mutually beneficial cooperation between the two countries enjoys broad prospects of further growth," he said. Also, Li praised Turkmenistan's domestic policy, its efforts to explore a path of development that suits its national conditions, as well as its handing of relations with the neighboring countries. China supports Turkmenistan in its efforts to safeguard independence and to develop economy, he said. Niyazov said he agreed with Li Peng on a range of issues, including the expansion of bilateral cooperation, ways to develop economy, the safeguarding of peace and other major international issues. He appreciated China's consistent proposition on taking equality and mutual benefit as a principle for handling international relations, which he said is also the basis for Turkmenistan's foreign policy. Premier Li Peng is here on a three-day official visit, the second leg of his five-Asian-nation tour.
