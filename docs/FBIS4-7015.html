BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 26 (KCNA)--The South Korean Federation of General Student Councils (Hanchongnyon) called a press conference on the first anniversary of its inauguration on April 25 and vowed to wage a powerful anti-U.S., anti-Kim Yong-sam struggle, according to a radio report from Seoul. The federation at the press conference noted that the Kim Yong-sam regime made many commitments when it set sail, but has fully revealed its anti-popular nature in the course of the Uruguay Round of the multilateral trade negotiations. It declared that it will launch an anti-U.S., anti-Kim Yong-sam struggle on the occasion of its first founding anniversary. It said there is nothing to expect from the Kim Yong-sam regime which has clearly disclosed its color of a conservative "government".
