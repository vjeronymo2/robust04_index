Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos described the meeting between Greek Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias and European Union [EU] Commission President Jacques Delors as particularly positive. Venizelos stated that Papoulias said he was satisfied with the meeting and hoped it would produce results. Responding to a question on whether there is a new initiative from Delors on the issue of Skopje, the spokesman said that for several days there has been a political initiative from the EU Commission that could take more specific forms. Venizelos said that anything that leads to the formulation of positions for the resumption of the dialogue is positive. The government spokesman also said that Foreign Minister Papoulias, who is presiding over the EU Council of Ministers, will visit Belgrade and Moscow. However, the dates of his visits were not revealed. Referring to the purpose of the visits, the spokesman said that it is obvious: To be informed in person on the positions of the Serb leadership and the Russian initiatives in the Balkans.
