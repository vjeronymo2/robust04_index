N.B. 1: General License GFW is not available for the export of commodities that the exporter knows will be used to: a. Enhance the performance capability of a ``supercomputer'' (see §776.11 for definition of ``supercomputer''). N.B. 2: To determine whether General License GFW may be used to export related equipment controlled under another entry in the CCL, consult the GFW paragraph under the Requirements heading of the appropriate entry. FP controls apply to computers for computerized fingerprint equipment to all destinations except Australia, Japan, New Zealand and members of NATO. 4.
