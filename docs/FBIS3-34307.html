Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 5 Feb (INA)--Iraq and the UN Special Commission concluded talks at the Foreign Ministry this evening. Deputy Prime Minister Tariq 'Aziz headed the Iraqi delegation, and Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf attended. The UN delegation was headed by its Special Commission Chairman Rolf Ekeus. During the final session, the Iraqi delegation consisted of Lieutenant General 'Amir Muhammad Rashid, head of the Military Industrialization Organization; Dr. Riyad al-Qaysi, Foreign Ministry under secretary; and several specialists. The UN Special Commission comprised John Scott, the Commission's legal adviser, and Tim Tereven [name as received], official spokesman for the Commission.
