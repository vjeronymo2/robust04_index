BFN [Text] At 1230 today, President Hafiz al-Asad received Iranian Foreign Minister Dr. 'Ali Akbar Velayati and the delegation accompanying him. Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' and the Iranian ambassador in Damascus attended. Dr. Velayati conveyed to the president a message from Iranian President Hashemi-Rafsanjani concerning developments in the region. The president asked Velayati to convey a reply message. Discussion during the meeting revolved around a number of issues concerning the situation in the region and Israel's escalation of tension in it, as well as issues of mutual concern.
