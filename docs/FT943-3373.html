940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Second Alliance The Second Alliance Trust had a net asset value of Pounds 17.40 per share at July 31 against Pounds 16.40 a year earlier. At the January 31 half year end the value was Pounds 18.95. Net revenue for the year rose to Pounds 8.18m (Pounds 7.66m). Earnings per share amounted to 42.49p (39.74p) and the dividend is raised to 42p (39p) via a recommended final of 29p (26.5p).
