920212 FT 12 FEB 92 / International Company News: Peugeot buoyed by second half PEUGEOT, the French car maker which includes Citroen, last year achieved a virtually unchanged group turnover of FFr160.17bn (Dollars 29.2bn), compared with FFr159.98bn a year earlier, thanks to a strong recovery in the second half, writes Kevin Done. The company said yesterday that sales had risen year-on-year by 8.7 per cent in the final quarter to FFr41.99bn. In the first half, group turnover fell by 6.3 per cent.
