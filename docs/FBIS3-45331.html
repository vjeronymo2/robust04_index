Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] At a news briefing yesterday, a person in charge of the provincial government's Taiwan affairs office indicated that this year Fujian will further widen the scope of opening up and work hard to scale a new height in Fujian-Taiwan economic and trade cooperation. He said that all levels of Taiwan affairs offices in the province will continue to act as bridges and links in encouraging big financial groups and enterprises to invest in Fujian. He welcomed Taiwan businessmen to invest in the construction of highways, power stations, harbors, airports, and other basic facilities. He also encouraged them to invest in Fujian's hinterland areas. He also indicated that in the course of helping Taiwan businessmen run enterprises in Fujian, the provincial Taiwan affairs office will cooperate with the relevant departments in strengthening Taiwan-related local legislation.
