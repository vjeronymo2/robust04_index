August 5, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

THE WEEK AHEAD

CALABASAS CITYHOOD: A Los Angeles County panel meets Wednesday to decide whether to approve Calabasas cityhood.

The incorporation issue would then be put before voters in the proposed city.

LOPEZ CANYON: Hearings are to resume Friday on noxious gas emissions at Lopez Canyon Landfill. Regional air quality officials are investigating alleged violations of state air pollution laws at the city dump.
