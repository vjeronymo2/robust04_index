BFN [Excerpt] Reports are coming in of new instances of arbitrary seizure of Black Sea Fleet property by the Ukrainian military. According to river motorboat crews, a group of eight Ukrainian naval servicemen arrived in Izmail at 1300. They handed to the unit commander an order from the Ukrainian defense minister to the effect that the unit was disbanded and replaced by a third brigade to guard the Ukrainian naval military district. Sources close to the Black Sea Fleet headquarters have learned that on 15 April, near the settlement of Perevalnaya, near Simferopol, one of the battalions of a mechanised division of the Ukrainian armed forces has been placed on full combat alert. Battalion personnel have been issued ammunition. Similar measures have been taken at an artillery unit of the same mechanised brigade. [passage omitted]
