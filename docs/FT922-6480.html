920523 FT 23 MAY 92 / Finance & The Family: GT launches Pep GT has launched a Pep which allows investors to select from seven funds: UK capital; UK special situations; income; smaller companies dividend; global assets, European and Germany. More than one fund can be chosen, subject to a lump sum minimum of Pounds 500 per fund, or Pounds 50 for monthly savings. Investors can switch between funds at a 2 per cent discount. The initial charge is 5.26 per cent; the annual fee varies between 1 and 1.5 per cent.
