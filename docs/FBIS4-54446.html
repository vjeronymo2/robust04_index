BFN [Text] Tirana, April 26 (ATA) -- The tribunal of Skopje had scheduled for April 25 the trial against 10 Albanians arrested several months ago, among who Mitat Emini, former secretary of the Democratic Prosperity Party, Shenasi Rexhepi, former assistant minister of defence in The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia [FYROM] and others. The trial has been postponed for May 9, 1994 due to the strike staged by defence attorneys. The 10 Albanians are accused of the criminal act of "collaboration for hostile activity," the Albanian TV said in an information with a source from Skopje. Further on the information said that Macedonian means of information are manipulating even in the case of this trial, presenting the accused as heads of the Albanian Army in this republic. Their trial is being used for political aims on the verge of multinational elections, presenting the Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia endangered by them. The Albanian political parties in this republic considered the said trial as framed and against the Albanians.
