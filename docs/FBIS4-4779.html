BFN [Text] Ho Chong-man, responsible vice chairman of Chongnyon [General Association of Korean Residents in Japan], today expressed his view that Kim Chong-il, secretary of the North Korean Workers Party, has completely been delegated full power--political, economic, and military--from his father Kim Il-song. Ho Chong-man said this at the Japan Socialist Party's Japan-Korean Committee general meeting held today at the First Lawmakers Hall of the Japanese Diet.
