BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondents Pavel Kuznetsov, Gennadiy Kulbitskiy, and Gennadiy Temnenkov] [Text] Bonn, 16 Jun -- After a meeting with the head of the German state, Ivan Rybkin, chairman of the State Duma, described German President Richard von Weizsaecker as "a major state figure to whose lot it fell to head Germany during the period of the reunification of its eastern and western lands." Replying to a question from an ITAR-TASS correspondent, the speaker of the lower chamber of the Federal Assembly said that during the talks a wide range of political and economic issues connected with relations between the two countries were touched upon. Rybkin said Weizsaecker showed great interest in the coming into being of the State Duma. In addition, they discussed in detail the economic processes that are under way in the Russian Federation and also the situation in the agro-industrial complex.
