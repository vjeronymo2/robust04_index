October 23, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: ANAHEIM;

3 CHANCES TO MEET COUNCIL CANDIDATES

Anaheim residents will have three opportunities this week to meet and question the seven candidates running for City Council.

On Wednesday, there will be a 7 p.m. forum at the Anaheim Plaza Resort Hotel, 1700 S. Harbor Blvd.

Candidates will discuss a broad range of issues, including their opinions on zoning, crime reduction and mobile home rent control. The forum is sponsored by the Anaheim Bulletin and the Anaheim Chamber of Commerce Political Action Committee.

The women's division of the Chamber of Commerce will hold a noon luncheon to meet the candidates Thursday at the Grand Hotel, 7 W. Freedman Way.

Friday evening, MultiVision Cable will host a forum at its studios at 3041 E. Miraloma Ave. The discussion will begin at 6:30 p.m.
