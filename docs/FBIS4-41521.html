BFN [Text] Kazakh Foreign Minister Kanat Saudabayev today met Former French Prime Minister Edith Crisson. They discussed cooperation between Kazakhstan and the European Union. The strengthening of integrational processes in the world as whole was noted. The minister spoke about the main provisions of the Euroasian union formation concept and the future implementation of the idea. Edith Crisson displayed interest in President Nazarbayev's initiative on creation of the Eurasian union of states. She said that she was impressed by the conceptual nucleus of this initiative. The ambassador of France to Kazakhstan participated in the meeting.
