Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Knin, 31 Jan (TANJUG) -- Representatives of the republic of Srpska army [VRS] and the Croatian Army yesterday signed another agreement -- with the mediation of the UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] -- on the extension of the so-called Christmas cease-fire. The extended cease-fire agreement comes into effect at 0000 this evening. The warring sides signed their first cease-fire agreement -- the Christmas cease-fire -- on 17 December last year and on 13 January extended it until midnight on 31 January. The extended truce will be in effect until 28 February this year. The VRS General Staff said that both sides were prepared to resume negotiations on a general cease-fire.
