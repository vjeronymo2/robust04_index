BFN [Text] The bank council of the National Bank of Slovakia discussed and approved today a report on 1993 currency development in Slovakia. The council has authorized Vladimir Masar, the bank's governor, to submit the report to the National Council. The bank council has stated that the priorities of the currency program have been successfully carried out, the price increases were kept at a minimum, and the internal convertibility of the Slovak koruna was maintained. The current account for the balance of payments ended in 1993 with a negative balance of $559 million, while the overall balance ended in a positive balance of $58.5 million.
