Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe March 11 TASS -- Tajikistan's Vice-Premier Mayonsho Nazarshoyev was shot and killed in his apartment in the early hours on Friday. Unidentified assassins were armed with an automatic gun, a source on the information department of the Tajik Ministry of Foreign Affairs told ITAR-TASS. The sources promised more information later.
