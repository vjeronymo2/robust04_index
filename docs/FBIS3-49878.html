Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] [Correspondent] Zdenko Vranjes reporting for Croatian radio. [passage omitted] Mostar is quiet, but sporadic explosions can be heard from the foothills of Mount Velez [east of Mostar]. According to the Serbian media, Serbian forces have launched an artillery attack on Muslim positions on the foothills of Mount Velez. Radio Nevesinje [Serb-held town east of Mostar] reports that Serbian forces have responded to an artillery attack launched by the Muslim army on Cobanovo Polje near Nevesinje. According to this report, two Serbian soldiers were killed in the attack. Serbian military sources have reported that the Muslims are massing their forces on the foothills of Mount Velez.
