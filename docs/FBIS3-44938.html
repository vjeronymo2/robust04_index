Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Kuwait City, January 5 (XINHUA) -- Kuwaiti Crown Prince and Prime Minister Shaykh Sa'd al'-Abdallah al-Salim al-Sabah received new Chinese Ambassador to Kuwait Wang Jingqi here today. The two sides probed during the meeting ways of developing bilateral ties between Kuwait and China. The crown prince praised the Chinese stand of supporting the U.N. Security Council resolutions concerning the Kuwaiti question. The Chinese ambassador reiterated China's support to Kuwait's efforts to safeguard its independence and sovereignty. The two sides expressed their desire to boost bilateral cooperation in various fields, especially in the oil and petrochemical industries.
