BFN ["News on the Meeting of the Roundtable Commission"] [Text] On 15 June 1994 from 0900 [0200 GMT] at the Damnak Chan pavilion in the royal palace, a meeting of the roundtable commission for peace and national reconciliation was held under the chairmanship of Samdech Chakkrei Nhoek Chulong, high adviser of His Majesty the King of the Kingdom of Cambodia. Attending this second roundtable meeting were His Excellency [H.E.] Tol Loah, representative of Samdech Chea Sim, chairman of the National Assembly; H.E. Yu Hokkri, representative of Prince Krompreah Norodom Ranariddh, first prime minister of the Royal Government of Cambodia [RGC]; H.E. Sok An, representative of Samdech Hun Sen, RGC second prime minister; H.E. Chan Youran, representative of H.E. Khieu Samphan, chairman of Democratic Kampuchea. Attending the meeting as observers were Supreme Patriarch Tep Vong of the Mahanikay order and Samdech Preah Khousananda. The agenda of the meeting is as follows: 1. The unconditional, immediate, and permanent cease-fire throughout the territory of the Kingdom of Cambodia -- proposal of the King of Cambodia; 2. The maximum political program -- proposal of the Democratic Kampuchea side.
