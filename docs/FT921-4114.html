920311 FT 11 MAR 92 / Budget Highlights INCOME TAX: New 20p rate on first Pounds 2,000 of taxable income. No change in 25p basic rate or 40p higher rate. Personal allowances increased in line with inflation. Most taxpayers' burden cut by Pounds 2.64 a week. VAT: Rate unchanged. Penalties eased. BUSINESS RATES: No real increase in non-domestic rates in 1992-93. CARS: Car tax halved to 5 per cent today. Vehicle excise duty on cars raised by Pounds 10. COMPANY CARS: Scale charges raised in line with inflation. Lower scale charges for diesels. Capital allowances increased from Pounds 8,000 to Pounds 12,000. PETROL: Unleaded and diesel duty increased by rate of inflation but leaded fuel duty raised 7.5 per cent. DRINKS: Duty raised 4.5 per cent. Beer up 1p a pint, wine 5p a bottle and spirits 28p. Most tobacco duties up 10 per cent - 13p on packet of 20 cigarettes. BES: Scheme to end in December 1993. FILM INDUSTRY: Relief on pre-production costs. INHERITANCE TAX: Threshold raised from Pounds 140,000 to Pounds 150,000. Most family businesses taken out of tax. CAPITAL GAINS: Threshold raised Pounds 300 to Pounds 5,800. PENSIONS: Raised in line with inflation. Income support up Pounds 2 a week for single person, Pounds 3 for couples. FUTURE BUDGETS: Proposal to put tax and expenditure in single Budget each December.
