930310 FT 10 MAR 93 / UK Company News: French group sells Bristol Water stake Lyonnaise des Eaux Dumez, the French water and construction company, has sold its 17.5 per cent stake in Bristol Water Holdings. The company sold more than 1m shares to Cazenove, the stock broker, which placed them with institutional investors. The French group took stakes in a number of British water companies around the time of the industry's privatisation.
