931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / Legal framework urged for rail The contracts between the companies which take over railways from British Rail must be flexible but will need to be set in a legal framework, Mr John Swift, the new rail regulator, said yesterday. Mr Roger Salmon, the railways franchising director, has expressed a wish to establish a 'no-fault' system of agreements, with companies regulating dealings by commercial contracts rather than the law.
