BFN [Text] Khartoum, 18 Apr (SUNA) -- The president of the Republic, Lieutenant General 'Umar al-Bashir, has said in a statement to the AL-MULTAQA magazine that the government had earlier stopped its contacts with the American ambassador because of its indignation over his hostile statements. These came at a time when both sides, the Government of Sudan and the American Government, had agreed to resume their dialogue on the basis of frankness. It is worth, mentioning that some news agencies had quoted the presidents statement imprecisely.
