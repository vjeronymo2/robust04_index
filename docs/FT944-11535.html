941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / International Company News: Rothmans Holdings drops 6.7% to ADollars 59m Rothmans Holdings, the Australian-based cigarette company, has reported a pre-tax profit of ADollars 59.05m (USDollars 44.1m) in the six months to end-September, down 6.7 per cent on the same period of the previous year, writes Nikki Tait. Sales were 2.5 per cent higher at ADollars 752.8m. Profit after tax was static at ADollars 37.7m.
