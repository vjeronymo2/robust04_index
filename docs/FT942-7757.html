940524 FT 24 MAY 94 / UK Company News: S Daniels warns of deficit Shares in S Daniels yesterday fell 10p to 64p as the supplier to the food manufacturing and baking industries warned of a loss for the first half of the current year. The group had returned to profit of Pounds 63,000 in 1993 after incurring a succession of losses. Mr Paul Daniels, chairman and chief executive, said pressure on margins remained intense as competition intensified. Several group development projects were under way but these would not impact on the interim result, he said. As a result of this setback Mr Daniels was reviewing operations and had started looking for a group chief executive.
