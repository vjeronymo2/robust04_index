BFN [Text] Beijing, April 2, IRNA -- Chinese Vice Premier and Minister of Foreign Affairs Qian Qichen in his meeting on Friday the with Iranian ambassador here, Mohammad-Hoseyn Mirfakhar, expressed hope that the developing countries would expand their ties despite the pressures put by the big Western powers. He said Tehran-Beijing relations were "unique" and that the two countries pursued independent policies. Mirfakhar referring to common interests expressed hope that with expansion of bilateral ties a new phase can be opened in Iran-China ties. At the end of the meeting, Qian assessed the outcome of his last "satisfactory".
