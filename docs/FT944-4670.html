941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Newport retail deal Newport Holdings, the property investment company, has bought two retail properties for Pounds 1.63m of which Pounds 1.3m will be paid in cash and the balance by the issue of shares. One property, in Gloucester, the principal tenant of which is Grand Metropolitan Restaurants, produces rent of Pounds 105,000 a year. The other, in Newquay, has 11 tenancies and produces rent of Pounds 55,000 a year.
