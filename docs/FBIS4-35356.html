BFN [Text] On 26 May, the people of Tawr al-Bahah Province succeeded in thwarting an infiltration attempt on the Kahbub front by the forces of the bloody al-Ahmar family. A military source told the YEMENI NEWS AGENCY in Aden that the people of the province laid an ambush for the patrols of the military clique of Sanaa, killing four of their men and taking two prisoners -- one of them a lieutenant colonel. They also seized two military (?vehicles).
