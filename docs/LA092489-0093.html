September 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

' . . . LIBERAL VIEWS THAT CREATE RESENTMENT BETWEEN RACES'

Come on, Feiffer -- your Sept. 17 cartoon is so anti-white and racist it's pathetic.

It's people like you who have the opportunity to express your liberal views that create so much resentment between races.

Your cartoon was out of line to state "any black who's famous we love, any black who ain't we kill."

Does The Times really pay you for this type of trash?

HELEN PENNINGTON

Port Hueneme
