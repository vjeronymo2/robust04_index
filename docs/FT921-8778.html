920215 FT 15 FEB 92 / Finance & The Family (At a Glance): New share service set up Granville Davies has set up a execution-only share dealing service called Transact, with a minimum commission of Pounds 15 for postal dealings and Pounds 17 for telephone transactions. The percentage rate is 0.75 per cent, which will effectively start to apply on deals above Pounds 2,000. Telephone clients will have to pay an annual registration fee of Pounds 10 and sign a customer agreement letter (the number is 071-338-0350). The London-based Granville Davies is a member of the Stock Exchange and the Securities and Futures Authority.
