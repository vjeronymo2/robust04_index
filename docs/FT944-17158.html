941010 FT 10 OCT 94 / Observer: Deaf to reason Earnest US youths working in the House of Commons as research assistants to MPs sometimes encounter local problems. One recently asked a British colleague: 'What's the politically correct expression over here for 'deaf'?' The Brit pondered, then replied: 'Well, I've got a relative who is deaf. We call him 'stone' deaf. That any good?'
