BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 3 (KCNA) -- A delegation of officials of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon) led by Kim Su-chin, a department director of Chongnyon Central Standing Committee, and a home-visiting group of Korean students in Japan and the 211th short-term home-visiting group of Koreans in Japan arrived in Wonsan Monday by the ship "Mangyongbong-92" for a visit to the socialist motherland.
