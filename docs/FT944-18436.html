941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / The Labour Party in Blackpool: Wholesale review of CSA urged The conference yesterday called for a wholesale review of the Child Support Agency to make it more responsive to families' needs. Mr James Taylor of the Society of Labour Lawyers said the agency - set up to ensure absent parents paid child maintenance - had failed those it was designed to help, the children of lone parents. He said they were in many cases worse off because of the inflexibility of the agency's operation.
