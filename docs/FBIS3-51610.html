Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Good Morning From Mexico" newscast] [Text] The federal government announced new measures to contribute to the normalization of the economy in Chiapas and overcome the conflict. President Carlos Salinas de Gortari issued a decree condoning the payment of income taxes for taxpayers in 21 municipalities of Chiapas State between January and June 1994. The decree also exempts taxpayers from paying property tax if their business address is located in the adversely affected region.
