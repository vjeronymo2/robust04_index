November 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

PRO FOOTBALL REPORT / WEEKDAY UPDATE;

RAMS;

ELLARD MAY NOT PLAY AGAINST 49ERS

Ram receiver Henry Ellard was listed as questionable with a sore right hamstring Wednesday, and Coach John Robinson suggested that Ellard isn't expected to play against San Francisco Sunday.

If Ellard can't play, Robinson said that rookie receiver Tony Lomack, who has been on the injured-reserve list all season, will probably be activated. TIM KAWAKAMI
