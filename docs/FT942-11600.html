940505 FT 05 MAY 94 / Trocadero deal The Trocadero Centre, the entertainment centre in London's Piccadilly Circus which went into receivership in January last year, is likely to be sold in the next few weeks. KPMG Peat Marwick, administrative receivers of the 400,000 sq ft development, are in exclusive negotiations with a buyer. Birmingham property, Page 13
