BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 27 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- In accordance with the decree by the President of Mongolia P. Ochirbat the staff composition of the Council on Religious Issues has been reorganized and renewed. The press service of the president has made public the list of ministers, scholars and other persons including in the composition of the council and announced in this respect that the Decree on the Creation of the Council on Religious Issues by the president of the People's Republic of Mongolia of 26 March 1991 is null and void.
