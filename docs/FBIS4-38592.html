BFN ["Ukraine's Supreme Council Decree on the Election of First Deputy Chairman of Ukraine's Supreme Council" issued on 25 May; and biographical information on the newly elected first deputy chairman of Ukraine's Supreme Council] [Text] Ukraine's Supreme Council decrees as follows: To elect [as published] Oleksandr Mykolayovych Tkachenko, Ukraine's people's deputy, as first deputy chairman of Ukraine's Supreme Council. O. Moroz, Chairman of Ukraine's Supreme Council Kiev, 25 May 1994

First Deputy Chairman of Ukraine's Supreme Council Oleksandr
Mykolayovych Tkachenko Oleksandr Mykolayovych Tkachenko was born on 7 March 1939 in the town of Shpola in Cherkasy Oblast. He advanced in his career from metal worker at a machine and tractor station to Ukraine's minister of agriculture. He was elected as secretary of Komsomol [Young Communist League] rayon committee, secretary and first secretary of the [Communist] party rayon committee, and chairman of the executive committee of the Ternopil Oblast Council of People's Deputies. He was first deputy chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Ukrainian SSR. Recently, he headed the agro-industrial association "Land and People." He is a member of Ukraine's Peasant Party and Ukraine's people's deputy of three convocations. O. Tkachenko is married. His wife is an economist. He has a grown-up daughter.
