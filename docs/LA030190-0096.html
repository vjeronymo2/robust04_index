March 1, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: GALLEGLY NAMES LIMON AS FIELD REPRESENTATIVE

U.S. Rep. Elton Gallegly (R-Simi Valley) has announced that Al Limon will serve as his new field representative beginning today.

Limon will provide a link between Gallegly and his constituents in the 21st Congressional District. Limon worked in real estate last year after serving as a field representative for Rep. Robert J. Lagomarsino (R-Ojai).
