Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 26 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- After accomplishing his official visit to India the president of Mongolia P. Ochirbat has granted an interview in the plane to the special correspondents of the MONTSAME news agency and the newspaper "ARDYN ERH". The president announces that he considers the outcome of his visit to India as very successful. He particularly points out to the signing of the treaty on friendly relations and cooperation between Mongolia and India, noting that this treaty defines the basic principles of mutual relations of the two countries under new conditions. As the President P. Ochirbat says, during the visit signed was a number of other documents, defining the principles of cooperation of the two countries in concrete branches and directions. In the president's view, the sides have got an idea about mutual interests in the sphere of economic relations. P. Ochirbat says that the Indian business circles have shown a great attention to the Mongolian businessmen, accompanying the president during his visit. Answering the questions of the correspondents, P. Ochirbat especially points out to the statement of the Prime Minister of India N. Rao about coincidence of the open-door policy of Mongolia with the policy of India. Concerning financial assistance to Mongolia on the part of India, P. Ochirbat points out to readiness of the Indian businessmen to render financial assistance to Mongolia in realization of big projects. Projects will be made concrete during the pending visit of the Indian businessman sent to Mongolia in May.
