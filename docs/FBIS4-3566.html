BFN [Text] Kathmandu, April 21 (XINHUA) -- Nepali National Council Chairman (upper house) Beni Bahadur Karki returned here this evening after completing the good-will visit to China. The chairman said in the Tribhuvan International Airport that this visit to China was fruitful and deep-impressed on China's great change in recently years. He expressed appreciations on China's continued economic support to Nepal and believed that the friendship between the two countries would be strengthened in the future. On his impression on Tibet, the chairman said that great changes have taken place in the autonomous region of China. About Shenzhen, Karki said that the southern city of China has developed like Hong Kong and he was impressed. The National Council chairman left here for China on April 9 at the invitation of chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) Li Ruihuan.
