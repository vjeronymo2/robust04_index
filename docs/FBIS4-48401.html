BFN [Text] Malawian Foreign Minister Dr. Hetherwick Ntaba says there is no proof that the Mozambique National Resistance is harboring Malawi Young Pioneers. Dr. Ntaba added that his government has always been willing to repatriate the probable elements of the military wing of President Banda's party, but that has not yet happened. The Malawian foreign minister was speaking to a group of Southern African Development Community journalists at the end of the parliamentary session which adopted Malawi's new constitution. Dr. Ntaba noted that all members of the armed unit were demobilized soon after the referendum held in 1993 following complaints by opposition parties.
