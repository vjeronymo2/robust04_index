Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Sardar Asif Ahmad Ali, who arrived in Kabul today, had detailed talks with Afghan President Professor Borhanoddin Rabbani on the situation in Afghanistan. Afghan sources told Radio Pakistan's Peshawar representative that they held talks on the ways to establish permanent peace and stability in Kabul. The two leaders also held talks on fresh prospects of permanent cease-fire in Kabul to pave the way for peace talks. Sardar Asif Ahmad Ali gave the assurance that Pakistan will continue to help Afghanistan on humanitarian grounds.
