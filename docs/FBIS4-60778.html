BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 488] [Text] Vilnius, April 29, ELTA -- Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk sent a telegram to his Lithuanian counterpart Algirdas Brazauskas saying he welcomed Lithuanian-Polish friendship and co- operation treaty. Kravchuk said in the telegram: "I am sure the important political act will have positive influence on the further development of Lithuanian-Polish relations and on the strengthening of confidence, security and stability atmosphere in the European continent".
