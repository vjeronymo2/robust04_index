June 14, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

THAI FIGHTER RETAINS HIS CROWN

Thailand's Fahlan Lookmingkwan retained his International Boxing Federation mini-flyweight crown today in a unanimous decision over seventh-ranked contender Joe Constantino of the Philippines.

Lookmingkwan improved his record to 15-1 by charging his opponent at the opening bell and slugging it out until the end of the 12-round bout.
