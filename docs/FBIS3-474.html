Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] South Africa and Russia have signed an agreement providing for closer cooperation in the areas of labor and social relations. Speaking at the ceremony in Pretoria, Manpower Minister Leon Wessels said that South Africa had survived a protracted period of isolationism, however, now that doors were opening to the country, it was becoming clear that it needed to make up lost time. The Russian-South African agreement, he said, would enable the two countries to exchange knowledge and expertise.
