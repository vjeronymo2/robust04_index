Language: English Article Type:BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 459] [Text] Vilnius, March 17, ELTA -- A rally concerning the Lithuanian-Polish agreement was organized by seven parties and movements and held in the Independnce square on Thursday [17 March]. The participants of the rally which gathered several hundred people demanded that the historical fact of the occupation of Vilnius region by Polish Army in 1920 should be included into the agreement. They urged Foreign Minister Povilas Gylys to refuse signing the agreement on March 18.
