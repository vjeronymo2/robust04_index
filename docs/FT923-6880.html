920821 FT 21 AUG 92 / CBI starts family business forum THE Confederation of British Industry is launching a Family Business Forum in September which will meet regularly to discuss issues of importance to family businesses. These will include planning for succession, taxation and family distribution of shareholdings. Mr Richard Brucciani, chairman of the CBI's smaller firms' council, said many family businesses were smaller than they needed to be 'often because the range of obstacles lead them to lower their horizons'.
