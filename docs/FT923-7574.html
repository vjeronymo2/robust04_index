920817 FT 17 AUG 92 / Manila police chief resigns Philippine police chief Cesar Nazareno resigned yesterday in a widening scandal over the alleged involvement of senior officers in illegal gambling, kidnapping and other crimes, agencies report from Manila. Mr Nazareno, head of the country's 100,000-strong police force, was the second high official in a week to leave the government. President Fidel Ramos meanwhile shrugged off reports of bitter infighting in his cabinet. He took his ministers on a cruise around Manila Bay in an apparent bid to quash reports of schisms between power blocs in his seven-week-old government.
