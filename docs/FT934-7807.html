931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Property Partnerships lower For the half year ended September 30, Property Partnerships, the commercial property owner and developer, turned in lower pre-tax profits of Pounds 994,000, compared with Pounds 1.01m. After tax of Pounds 293,000 against Pounds 335,000, earnings per share were 6.5p (6.31p). The interim dividend is increased from 2.55p to 2.6p. Gross rental income and sales fell slightly to Pounds 3.19m (Pounds 3.36m) and operating profits were behind at Pounds 1.08m (Pounds 1.11m).
