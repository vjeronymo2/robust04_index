September 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE JIM BAKKER SAGA

Congratulations to Garry Abrams on his excellent article "Amazing Disgrace."

Who says the theater is dead? Jim Bakker has had many years of success in his ongoing role as a telecharlatan minister's telecharlatan minister. He is an experienced actor who could've given the great Olivier lessons.

I hope Bakker's judge and jury view his apparent "psychological deterioration" with the eye of a skeptical theater critic. With Bakker's "sense of drama and staging," he just may be attempting to give the greatest performance of his life.

BOB CANNING, Los Angeles
