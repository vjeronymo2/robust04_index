BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 5 April Evening Edition] [Text] Vice Foreign Minister Kunihiko Saito appeared on a TV Asahi program on 5 April, during which he commented on the DPRK's development of nuclear weapons. With regard to the possibility of armed conflict on the Korean peninsula in the near future, Saito stated: "We cannot discount such a possibility, and must take an optimistic view of the situation." However, he added: "It will not be good for the North Koreans to feel that Japan's thinking is premised on the possibility of armed conflict." He stressed North Korea should not be provoked.
