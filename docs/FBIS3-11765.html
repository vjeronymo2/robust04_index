Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Hungarian Foreign Minister Geza Jeszenszky has stated that he believes that ties between Hungary and Cuba should be strengthened in all spheres. Jeszenszky meet with Cuban Ambassador to Hungary (Carlos Grego Soso) [name and title as heard] in Budapest. Jeszenszky said that despite the reasons that initially obstructed Hungarian-Cuban relations, during the current period of change the development of bilateral ties should be promoted, taking advantage of mutually beneficial elements. In another meeting, (Janos Marcandi), deputy secretary of state at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, also expressed his interest in reviving relations with Cuba, specifically in the financial arena. (Marcandi) expressed his hope that formulas can be found through which financial collaboration will be possible under the new conditions, even though the distance makes trade more difficult.
