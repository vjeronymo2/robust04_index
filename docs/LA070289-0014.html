July 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

WEATHERMEN

Thank you for the story of the weathermen who dedicate their lives to monitoring the planet's health, or lack of it ("Weathermen of the Apocalypse," by Michael Rogers, May 21). It must be difficult to know the horrid truth and be responsible for telling the nation.

Unfortunately, there are too many people who don't believe that they, as individuals, truly can make a difference. It will take everyone on this planet to help heal the wounds all of us are responsible for inflicting.

JANET COLE

Long Beach
