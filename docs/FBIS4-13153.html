BFN [Text] Vietnam's deputy foreign minister today in a meeting with Dr. Velayati in Tehran called for the expansion of cooperation in international circles and organizations. Nguyen Dy Niem, who brought the Vietnamese president's message to Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani, in the meeting expressed hope that with the Vietnamese president's imminent visit to Iran, ties between the two countries will be further strengthened. The Islamic Republic of Iran's foreign minister, Dr. Velayati, during the meeting pointed to the historical record of the Vietnamese combats against occupiers and said: The Islamic Republic of Iran places great significance on Vietnam. Expressing Iran's readiness for hosting the Vietnamese president, Mr. Velayati said he is hopeful that the visit will have good foreign policy results for expanding the two countries' ties.
