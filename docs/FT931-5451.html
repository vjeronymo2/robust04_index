930305 FT 05 MAR 93 / International Company News: Compass applies for receivership COMPASS Airlines, which brought full competition to the Australian airline industry, was forced yesterday to call in receivers and managers. The decision came after crisis meetings over a ADollars 9.2m (USDollars 6.5m) funding package which the company needs to keep operating. Mr Sam Coats, chief executive, said the receivership was necessary for the airline to keep flying.
