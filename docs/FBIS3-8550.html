Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bratislava, March 9 (CTK) -- The Slovak Foreign Ministry welcomes the proposal to create a Bosnian-Croatian federation as a possible positive impulse for further developments in Bosnia-Herzegovina, a ministry statement handed to CTK on Wednesday [9 March] reads. The statement praises the United States' contribution to attaining the agreement and also the significant role of Russia in the search for a peaceful solution to the crisis surrounding Sarajevo. The ministry favors resolving the crisis in the former Yugoslavia by peaceful means. "We support all processes based on mutual consent, understanding and tolerance. We welcome every initiative which could lead to the ending of the war in Bosnia-Herzegovina," the statement reads.
