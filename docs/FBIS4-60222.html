BFN [Text] Brother Dr. 'Ali Salim al-Bid, chairman of the Presidential Council, today issued Presidential Council Decree no. 3 of 1994. The first article charges brother engineer Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas to form a government of national unity in consultation with the Presidential Council. The other articles provide for the implementation of the decree with effect from the date of its issuance, and for its publication in the official gazette.
