BFN [Text] During the Telecommunications Week, 185 telecommunication centers with a capacity of 195,000 telephone lines were inaugurated throughout the country. Also, 279 rural telecommunication offices were commissioned. In an interview with correspondents today, on the occasion of Telecommunications Week, the post, telegraph, and telephone minister announced the news and said: The most modern technology was used to develop the country's telecommunication network, and 3,000 kms of fiber optic cable were used. He added: With the inauguration of a factory producing fiber optics and its raw materials, our country will soon be among those producing such materials.
