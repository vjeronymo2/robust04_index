920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / International Company News: Amcor net 138% ahead at midterm AMCOR, the Australian paper and packaging group, yesterday reported a 138 per cent rise in net profits to ADollars 190.7m (Dollars 142.8m) for the first half to December from ADollars 80m a year earlier, Reuter reports. Sales rose to ADollars 2.3bn from ADollars 2.26bn. Earnings per share rose to 22.5 cents from 16.7 cents, and the dividend is being raised to 14 cents a share from 13 cents.
