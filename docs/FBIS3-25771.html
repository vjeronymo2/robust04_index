Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Netherlands Prime Minister Ruud Lubbers has arrived in South Africa and says it is important for Europe to show solidarity with the peace process in South Africa. Mr. Lubbers was welcomed on arrival in Cape Town by President F. W. de Klerk, who told reporters he saw the visit as the final reconciliation between The Netherlands and South Africa. Mr. Lubbers said ties between The Netherlands and South Africa were completely restored. He will be holding talks with a number of South African leaders, including African National Congress President Nelson Mandela.
