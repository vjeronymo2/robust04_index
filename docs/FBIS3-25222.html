Language: French Article Type:BFN [Communique issued by the international group of election observers in Lome on 9 February; read by group rapporteur] [Text] The National Electoral Commission met at the Congressional Palace yesterday at 0830 in the presence of representatives of the International Monitoring Committee, a group of international observers representing the Association of African Lawyers, the Burkina Faso Government, the French Ministry of Cooperation, the Cote d'Ivoire Government, a German expert, and Nigerian observers. After an exchange of different points of view, the observers present unanimously observed that the material conditions of the first round of the legislative elections in Togo were satisfactory and that they were conducted in good conditions of transparency.
