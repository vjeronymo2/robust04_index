940521 FT 21 MAY 94 / London Stock Exchange: Equity futures and options trading Stock index futures gave up the early advance as dealers followed the retreat in both UK and US bonds, writes Joel Kibazo. The June contract on the FT-SE 100 closed at 3,121, down 4 on its previous close and at a 4 point discount to cash. Volume remained low reaching 10,306 contracts by the close. Turnover in the traded options jumped to 47,439 lots of which 17,916 was dealt in the FT-SE 100 option and 3,022 in the Euro FT-SE option. BT was the busiest stock option with a total of 4,659 contracts traded.
