Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 24 (KCNA) -- Kang Song-san, premier of the Administration Council, sent a message of greetings to Ibrahim Boubacar Keita upon his appointment as Malian prime minister. The message expressed the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries would continue to develop favourably and wished him success in his responsible work. Meanwhile, Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam sent greetings to Sy Kadiatou Sow upon his appointment as Malian minister of foreign affairs, Malians abroad and African integration.
