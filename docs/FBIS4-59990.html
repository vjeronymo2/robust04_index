BFN [Text] A military source has explained to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA' that in a desperate, hysterical, and cowardly attempt the gang of apostasy and secession in the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] fired a missile on Ta'izz at 2030 yesterday, targetting defenseless citizens. But God's providence ordained that this missile fall on Sadr Mountain. The source said no human losses were sustained, but that children, women, and elderly people in the city and the nearby villages were scared and there were some cracks in the houses of the citizens from the explosion of the missile.
