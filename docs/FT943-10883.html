940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / World News in Brief: Armed robber dies after shoot-out in London suburb An armed robber died and a policeman was wounded when a raid on a jewellers in Putney, south-west London, ended in a high street gun battle. Police believe the man killed himself with his own shotgun after being wounded by a marksman. The shootings came just a day after Metropolitan Police commissioner Sir Paul Condon called for tougher sentences to tackle the growing problem of guns. In a separate incident yesterday, armed robbers escaped with Pounds 1m after spraying a courier and driver with CS gas at Heathrow Airport.
