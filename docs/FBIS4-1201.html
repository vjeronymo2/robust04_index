BFN [Text] The South African Army has become the latest institution to express serious concerns about the capabilities of the National Peace Keeping Force [NPKF]. The chief of the Army, General Hattingh Pretorius, said in Heidelberg in the Transvaal that the Defense Force was lately perceived as being the only organization that could ensure stability and protect the country from anarchy and chaos. He said the image of the SADF [South African Defense Force] was now more positive than that of any other security force.
