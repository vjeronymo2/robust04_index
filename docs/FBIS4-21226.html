Possessing Cocaine MB1504113294 Johannesburg SAPA in English 1042 GMT 15 Apr 94 MB1504113294 Johannesburg SAPA English BFN [Text] Johannesburg April 15 SAPA -- Police arrested a 35-year-old man at Johannesburg's Jan Smuts Airport on Friday after 6 kg of cocaine with street value of R[Rand]2.1 million was discovered in a false compartment of his briefcase. The man, a South African, was returning from Bangkok. A 23-year-old South African on his way back from the United State was arrested as he re-entered the country on Thursday with 1 kg of cocaine hidden in his briefcase. Both men are due to appear in the Kempton Park magistrate's court on Monday.
