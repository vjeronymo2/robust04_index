September 14, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The Athletics Congress announced that Racquel Andrews, a long jumper at Prairie View A&M University, has been suspended from competition for two years after testing positive for a banned substance.

Andrews, who is from Houston, tested positive in June at the U.S. intermediate track and field championships at San Jacinto College in Pasadena, Tex. Officials did not identify the banned substance.
