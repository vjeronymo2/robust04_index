Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In a major breakthrough Delhi Police has busted an extremist network with the arrest of four Punjab militants owing allegiance to a banned organization. One Ak-56 rifle, a Maruti van and 69 kg of explosives, besides 118,000 rupees in cash were recovered from them. Talking to newsmen in New Delhi today, the commissioner of police, Mr. N.B. Kaushal, said the accused intended to recruit youths into the gang from states outside Punjab and motivate them to strike at VIP's and important installations. He said the group was being funded by Pakistan's ISI [Inter-Service Intelligence] to carry out terrorist activities in India.
