BFN [Text] Our military correspondent reports that a terrorist cell opened fire on an Israeli army post in 'Ali al-Tahir area yesterday afternoon. Immediately afterward, the Israeli army carried out a wide-scale combing operation in which helicopters and tanks took part. Meanwhile, South Lebanese Army personnel [SLA] manning a tank positioned in Kafr Rumman discovered the cell as it was entering a red stone villa in the town. The SLA soldiers shelled the cell, killing three elements from the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine [PFLP] and wounding three others.
