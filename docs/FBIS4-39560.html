BFN [Text] At today's sitting, the Russian Government is discussing the state of Russian industry, ITAR-TASS reports. In addition, draft laws on the struggle against organized crime and state support for small businesses in Russia as well as the issues of price formation and the fuel and power engineering and metallurgy complexes are to be examined. The sitting is being chaired by First Deputy Prime Minister Oleg Soskovets. He said Chernomyrdin is likely to return from his leave this evening. Tomorrow morning he is expected to speak at a conference of builders. Boris Yeltsin and almost all members of the Cabinet of Ministers are expected to attend the conference.
