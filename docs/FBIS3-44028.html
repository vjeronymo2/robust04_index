Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] South Africans have been advised not to travel to Maseru or its surrounding areas because of the renewed fighting between rival Lesotho military factions which broke out there this morning. Diplomats say it is difficult to obtain a clear picture of what is happening, but troops from the Makoanyane Barracks on the outskirts of the capital are believed to have launched an attack on the defense force headquarters in Maseru. South African Foreign Minister Pik Botha is being kept well informed on the situation.
