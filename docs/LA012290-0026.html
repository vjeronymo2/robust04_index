January 22, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Webber to Produce Show at Disney World: Composer Andrew Lloyd Webber will move on from cats to all other kinds of animals, as the executive producer of a new show at Disney World in Orlando, Fla., called "Noah's Ark."

Lloyd Webber, the creator of "Cats" and "Phantom of the Opera," will compose an original symphonic score for the pageant, which will be staged on water. "This is the most ambitious night-time spectacle in Disney history," said Michael Eisner, chairman and chief executive officer of the Walt Disney Co. Set to open in 1992, "Noah's Ark" will be part of 29 new shows that Disney plans to add to its Florida park in the 1990s. Claudia Puig
