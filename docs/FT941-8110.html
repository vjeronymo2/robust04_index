940222 FT 22 FEB 94 / International Company News: Indian drug group to offer GDRs Wockhardt, an Indian pharmaceutical and healthcare company, announced it is launching a Dollars 75m offering of global depositary receipts (GDRs), writes Sara Webb. Wockhardt is the first Indian company in the pharmaceutical sector to launch an international equity offering. The proceeds will be used to finance the expansion of various business areas, including the construction of a new bulk drug manufacturing plant and a new pesticides manufacturing plant. BZW is lead manager for the international offering.
