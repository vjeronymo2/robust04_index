Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Hong Kong, 13 Jan (ZHONGGUO XINWEN SHE)--According to a report from Taipei, Taiwan's "Investment Examination Committee of the Ministry of Economy" said yesterday that last year the committee had approved a total of over $3.16 billion of investment in mainland China by Taiwanese businessmen, and the amount ranked first in external investment by Taiwan businessmen. An official concerned of Taiwan's "Investment Examination Committee of the Ministry of Economy" admitted that although Taiwan businessmen increased their investment in Southeast Asia, they had invested more in the mainland. This shows the mainland remains the first choice for Taiwan businessmen investing outside the island. [passage omitted]
