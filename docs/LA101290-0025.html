October 12, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Unchained Movie: The hit movie "Ghost" is scaring people into theaters abroad. The film grossed $2,854,732 on 233 screens during its first three days in release in the United Kingdom and Ireland last weekend, achieving the biggest three-day gross of any film released in the United Kingdom this year. In Japan, the film's second weekend gross at 11 theaters brought a gross of $2,169,316 for a 10-day per-screen average of $197,211, it was announced by Barry London, president of the Paramount Pictures' Motion Picture Group. BETH KLEID
