930811 FT 11 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Fairey in Dollars 12.7m US purchase FAIREY GROUP, the specialist electronics and engineering group, is to buy Luxtron, which manufacturers specialist equipment, for Dollars 12.7m (Pounds 8.4m). In the last financial year Luxtron made pre-tax profits of Dollars 1m (Pounds 600,000) before exceptional restructuring costs of Dollars 500,000. Sales came to Dollars 11.4m.
