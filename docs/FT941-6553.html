940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Gartmore Scotland Net asset value per capital share at The Gartmore Scotland Investment Trust rose 26 per cent from 249p to 314.5p over the six months to January 1. Gross revenue advanced to Pounds 976,000 (Pounds 898,000) in the first half, on the back of Pounds 119,000 (nil) coming from enhanced scrip dividends. Interest on deposits fell to Pounds 28,000 (Pounds 68,000). Administrative expenses rose to Pounds 191,000 (Pounds 145,000) and net revenue to Pounds 644,000 (Pounds 562,000). Earnings were 4.76p (4.15p) per share and the second interim dividend is held at 2.4p for a total at the halfway stage of 4.8p.
