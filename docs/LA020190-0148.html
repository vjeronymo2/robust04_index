February 1, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $30 Wednesday when Ariosa finished second to longshot Hail The Foxybaybe in the fifth race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now down to $1,003.

Bob's bet today is $40 to win on Monsieur Maison in the third.
