Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Dear viewers; please pay attention to a report which has just come in: After the brutal massacre by the Zionists in the Ibrahim Mosque, in a statement issued by the Government of the Islamic Republic of Iran, a general day of mourning has been declared for 26 February, throughout the country. The text of the statement is as follows. In the name of God, the compassionate, the merciful. The cabinet of the Government of the Islamic Republic of Iran strongly condemns the brutal atrocity by the Zionists in the shrine of His Holiness Ebrahim-e Khalil, peace be upon him, which ended in the martyrdom and wounding of hundreds of fasting Palestinian Muslims. It considers the slaughter to be the foreseeable result of the unquestioning support given by the world arrogance and American imperialism to the policies of an illegal and illegitimate regime, which is an agent of tension and crisis in the region and the instigator of the suppression and massacre of the Muslims and free minded individuals who wish to live in their own homeland. The cabinet of the Government of the Islamic Republic of Iran, categorically supports the justice seeking struggles of the Palestinian nation, and in sympathy with those people and the families of the martyrs and injured in that horrific disaster declare a general day of mourning throughout the country on Saturday 7 Esfand [26th February]. [Signed] The cabinet of the Government of the Islamic Republic of Iran
