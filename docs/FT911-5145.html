910416 FT 16 APR 91 / UK Company News: Fuchs has over 30% of Century Oils Fuchs, the family-controlled German lubricants group, said that it held more than 30 per cent of Century Oils, the UK lubricants company, following its increased offer of 145p last Wednesday and had launched a full bid under takeover rules. Before the improved bid from 110p, Fuchs owned 13.4 per cent of Century's shares and bought an additional 7 per cent in the market as soon as it increased its offer. All other conditions of the offer have been waived.
