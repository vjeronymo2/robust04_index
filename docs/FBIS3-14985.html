Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau, BASAPRESS, 28/2/1994 -- The Council of Europe Observation Group is satisfied with the way in which elections in Moldovan Parliament were organized and held, declared the delegation chief Lambert Kelchtermaans at a press-conference today. Though some infringements were detected, they were unlikely to influence the results of first democratic elections based on pluripartisism. The delegation chief mentioned that the observation group visited over 40 voting sections. According to the collected information analyzed by the Council of Europe delegation a final report will be presented to the Bureau of the Assembly of the 32. This report will be the basic element in the adherence of Moldova to the Council of Europe, which Council experts think will last for a longer period. Before the conference Parliament Speaker Petru Lucinschi assured the foreign observers that Moldova will cause no trouble to Europe" and wishes to join Europe with a democratic system and market economy.
