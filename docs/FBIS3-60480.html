Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vilnius, Dec 17, BNS - The reported incidence of the AIDS virus has increased to 20 in Lithuania, the national AIDS Center said. A blood sample from a hospital in the seaport of Klaipeda has tested HIV-positive. The center said the infected person is male, but there is no information about his sexual orientation and occupation. The center also reported that one of the AIDS-infected people registered in Lithuania recently died abroad and one more AIDS carrier was killed at a hospital in 1988 by a mentally disturbed patient.
