931116 FT 16 NOV 93 / World Stock Markets: Mexico MEXICAN shares again set record levels yesterday on expectations of a positive result in a vote by the US congress tomorrow on Nafta. The IPC index ended 14.89 up at 2,123.33 in busy turnover of 1.35bn pesos. Telefonos de Mexico 'L' series lost 4 centavos to 8.72 pesos.
