BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 13 -- Kazakhstan Prime Minister S. Tereshchenko gave a banquet this evening in honour of Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet and his party now on an official friendship visit to Kazakhstan. Among those present at the banquet were the deputy prime minister and minister of science and technology, the foreign minister and the minister of external economy. In his speech, Prime Minister S. Tereshchenko said that Kazakhstan is making its efforts to attract Vietnam's technology and investment and to develop the economic and commercial relations between the two countries. He also said that Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet's visit has ushered in a new stage for the all-round development of the relations between the two countries. In his reply, Mr. Kiet expressed his belief that with its current socio-economic programmes, it's certainly that Kazakhstan will rapidly advance forward on its road of development. He also hailed Kazakhstan's initiatives aimed at contributing to the maintenance of peace, stability, cooperation and development in Asia. The two prime minister and everybody proposed toasts to the further consolidation and development of the friendship and cooperation between Kazakhstan and Vietnam.
