BFN [Text] According to the correspondent of VIETNAM NEWS AGENCY in China, at the invitation of the Senior Lieutenant General Zhang Wannian, chief of general staff of the China People's Liberation Army, Senior Lieutenant General Dao Dinh Luyen, member of the Central Committee of Communist Party of Vietnam and deputy minister of National Defense and chief of general staff of the Vietnam People's Army led a high-ranking military delegation that arrived in Beijing on 9 April for an official visit to China. On the morning of 10 April, after the welcome ceremony, which was held in front of the Military Museum, our delegation held a conference with the Chinese high-ranking military delegation headed by Senior Lieutenant General Zhang Wannian. The two sides informed each other the situation of economic activities of the two armies, discussed and agreed on the continuation of the consolidation and promotion of the traditional and friendly relations between the two peoples and armies. In the afternoon the same day, Comrade Liu Huaqing, standing member of the Communist Party of China's [CPC] Central Committee Political Bureau and vice chairman of the CPC Central Military Commission warmly received the delegation.
