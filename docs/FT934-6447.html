931125 FT 25 NOV 93 / Survey finds more stress at BT A growing number of British Telecommunication's managerial and professional staff are suffering from work-induced stress and are critical of the performance related pay system introduced this year, STE Research, the research arm of the Society of Telecom Executives, said yesterday. Its survey found that 56.6 per cent of staff said they were suffering from work-related stress compared with 47.2 per cent two years earlier.
