Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Petr Kravchanka has met with the heads of diplomatic missions in the Republic of Belarus. The head of the Foreign Ministry informed them of the results of the no-confidence vote in Stanislav Shushkevich. He also emphasized that the course of Belarusian foreign policy remains unchanged and complies with international agreements which were signed by Belarus, including, first of all, the agreements on nuclear disarmament.
