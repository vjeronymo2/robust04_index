Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Article by Danko Peric from Knin] [Text] The Headquarters of the Serbian Army of Krajina has announced that in the past 24 hours, the Croatian Army on several occasions has hit Krajina positions and civilian targets in Lika, shooting from (?Trle), from where it still has not withdrawn. These attacks from infantry and artillery weapons were responded to by shooting at the Croatian Army combat positions and, it is said in the announcement, great losses have been inflicted upon them. When visiting that section of the Lika battlefield, Milan Martic, president of the Republic of Serbian Krajina, and Major General Milan Celeketic, commander of the Serbian Army of Krajina, expressed satisfaction with the development of events. After visiting the Kozjan region, Martic and Celeketic met the local leadership of Teslingrad, the Lika town that was most frequently attacked from Croatian soldiers' positions in this war and to which huge material damage has been caused. The Krajina Army General Staff reported that provocations by the Croatian Army have also been registered in the Plasko region in Lika, Zemunik Gornji, Smiljcic near Benkovac, and Novigrad, as well as Kricke and other towns near Drnis and Velika Glava near Skradin.
