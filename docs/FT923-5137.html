920902 FT 02 SEP 92 / Bulgaria to resume debt payment BULGARIA WILL start paying off overdue interest on its Dollars 12bn foreign debt on September 15, Mr Ivan Kostov, finance minister, said yesterday, Reuter reports from Sofia. 'The first payment will be 25 per cent of Bulgaria's overdue interest,' he said. Bulgaria's last communist government suspended payments of principal on foreign debt in March 1990 and froze interest payments in June of that year. The country owes Dollars 10bn to more than 300 commercial banks and Dollars 2bn to the Paris Club of official creditors. Mr Kostov said Bulgaria would pay Dollars 10m each month but did not specify how the money would be shared between the commercial banks and official creditors.
