920320 FT 20 MAR 92 / Election 1992: Ashdown admits pact 'difficulties' MR Paddy Ashdown, leader of the Liberal Democrats, admitted last night that he could face difficulties in persuading his party to back a minority Conservative government to keep Labour out of office. Interviewed by Sir Robin Day on Thames Television's This Week, he said: 'It may require a very considerable degree of leadership. What will matter to me is what is right for the country and not what is right for the party.'
