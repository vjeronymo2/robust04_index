October 8, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ARTFULLY SUITED

Regarding "Artfully Suited," by Mary Rourke (Aug. 27): My trusty calculator tells me that it cost $19,436 to dress the nine women you chose to highlight women's fall fashions: an average of $2,159.55 per outfit. There is not an outfit in there that cannot be duplicated for less than $50 at the Salvation Army. I guess those ladies will just have to keep the price tags on to truly get their money's worth.

FREDERICK CLEVELAND, Hollywood
