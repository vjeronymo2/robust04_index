BFN [Text] Beijing, June 18 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing met with George Vojta, vice-chairman of the U.S. Bankers Trust Company, and his party here today. On June 15, the company donated 5 million renminbi yuan (some 600,000 U.S. dollars) to the "Hope Project" for the building of primary schools in China's relatively economic backward areas. Li expressed gratitude for the donation. China is willing to further expand its cooperation with the company in financial and investment spheres as well as in training of qualified personnel, he said. Vojta voiced his pleasure with his company's progress in business operation in China, and wished the country still greater achievements in its reform and opening-up process.
