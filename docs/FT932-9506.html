930513 FT 13 MAY 93 / UK Company News: REA down 33% to Pounds 619,000 Pre-tax profits of REA Holdings, a cultivator of plantation crops, merchanting, warehousing and storage, fell from Pounds 883,000 to Pounds 619,000 in 1992. Turnover increased from Pounds 76.9m to Pounds 87.75m and earnings per share emerged at 4.3p (4.1p). An interim dividend of 2p is being paid. The directors intend a second interim of 2p before April 5 1994.
