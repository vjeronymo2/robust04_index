941215 FT 15 DEC 94 / Pounds 1bn boost for Tyne and Wear Nearly Pounds 1bn has been invested along the urban river banks of Tyne and Wear since 1987, figures released yesterday by the Tyne and Wear Development Corporation show. This comprises Pounds 258m of public money, channelled through the corporation, and Pounds 718m from other, mainly private-sector, sources.
