BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 15 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received gifts from the delegations the Liaoning Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China, the October 8 Revolutionary Movement of Brazil, the secretariat of the Society for the Study of the Chuche Idea of Japanese teachers, the Kim Chong-suk Kindergarten in Dalandzadgad City, Omnogov Province, Mongolia, and Maria Lazar, vice president of the Socialist Workers' Party of Romania, and Alexandr Jarikov, secretary general of the World Federation of Trade Unions. The gifts were handed to officials concerned. Gifts were also presented to him by Choe Hong-hui, president of the International Taekwon-do Federation, Yi Yong-pin, chairman of the Association of Korean Christians for National Reunification in Germany, and Rev. Cho Tok-chon, director of the Korea Institute of William Carey University, the U.S., staying in the socialist homeland.
