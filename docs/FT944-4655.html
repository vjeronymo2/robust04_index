941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Wrexham Water Wrexham Water reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.22m for the six months to September 30, against Pounds 1.74m. Turnover was up from Pounds 5.52m to Pounds 5.78m. Earnings per share were 13.67p (12.06p) and the interim dividend is 3.6168p (2.4225p) per ordinary and 3.5834p (2.3875p) per non-voting ordinary.
