August 22, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

QUOTABLE

"While we were cutting this Partridge Family stuff, I was out seeing Jimi Hendrix. I was the only singer-musician in the group. You can only imagine what it was like seeing Danny Bonaduce play bass, while I was doing sessions with monster talents Larry Knechtel, Jim Gordon and Hal Blaine." -- David Cassidy, in an Enigma Records press release
