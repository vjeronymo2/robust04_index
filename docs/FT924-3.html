921231 FT 31 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Receivers appointed at Beverley arm Beverley Group, the engineering concern formerly known as Petrocon, has announced the appointment of receivers at Clydebank Engineering, a wholly-owned subsidiary. The remaining companies in the group continued to trade normally, the directors said. The company is also disposing of its Gorseline subsidiary to management for Pounds 1.47m. The proceeds of the sale would be used to reduce group borrowings, the directors said.
