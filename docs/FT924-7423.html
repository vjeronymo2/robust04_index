921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / Panamanians vote against elimination of army in referendum In a referendum on Sunday an overwhelming majority of Panamanians voted against a proposal to eliminate the army under constitutional reforms backed by President Guillermo Endara, pictured casting his ballot. The special vote which was widely seen as a referendum on the government. was marked by high abstention, with barely 40 per cent of Panama's 1.4m registered voters showing up at the polls. With 92 per cent of results in, 64 per cent of voters were against.
