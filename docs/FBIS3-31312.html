Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] Approximately 300 trade unions affiliated to the Agrostar Trade Union Federation, which represent the interests of workers in the state agricultural sectors, organized a two-hour warning strike this morning. During this time, work came to a complete standstill. This warning strike is the beginning of large-scale labor actions due to a labor conflict between the executive and the Agrostar Free Trade Union Federation, our reporter Marin Eftimie informs us.
