940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / UK Company News: EFT Pounds 2.56m placing EFT plans to raise about Pounds 2.56m net via a placing and open offer of 5.48m new ordinary shares at 49p each. The Glasgow-based asset finance group also said that its principal bankers had agreed to increase available borrowing facilities to Pounds 56.8m - up Pounds 13.5m from the facilities available at December 31 1993. The new shares are being conditionally placed with institutions, subject to a clawback to qualifying shareholders on a 1-for-6 basis. EFT said that revenues for the first five months of the current financial year were more than 15 per cent ahead. The directors forecast the interim dividend would be 0.525p and the final 1.225p, making a total of 1.75p (1.5p).
