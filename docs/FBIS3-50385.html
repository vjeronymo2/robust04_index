Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Bijeljina, 31 Jan (TANJUG) -- Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, chairman of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia, arrived in Bijeljina shortly before 1100. Outside the municipal assembly building he was welcomed by Radovan Karadzic, president of the Srpska republic, and by Prime Minister Vladimir Lukic. Greetings were exchanged during a cordial meeting and on this occasion Zhirinovskiy told Karadzic that wherever he goes he finds "proofs and monuments indicating that the Serb nation is a historic nation." The host and his guest then went to the municipal assembly building where talks are to be held. More than 10,000 people have been waiting at the city square in Bijeljina since early this morning to hear Zhirinovskiy and Karadzic speak.
