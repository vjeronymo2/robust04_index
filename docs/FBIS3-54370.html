Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By special AL-QUDS correspondent] [Text] Gaza -- Fatah yesterday stressed its principled opposition to the interrogations and killings being carried out in the Gaza Strip. The organization states this should only be done through a general and national agreement based on a centralized leadership decision to preserve the Palestinian cause. In a statement distributed in Gaza, Fatah reiterated its demand from all organizational cadres to halt such acts because they harm the people's interests. It called on the Palestinian people, including all their popular organizations, to be alert and aware, with extreme patriotism, of all the conditions around them.
