BFN [Station report by Renqing (0088 7230), Qimei (0366 5019), and Dawa (6671 1216) from the "Regional News Hookup" program; reporters' names shown in caption] [Text] On the afternoon of 27 May, the Tibetan People's Armed Forces Committee, the Tibetan Military District [TMD], and the regional party committee's Propaganda Department jointly held a forum in Lhasa to mark the 10th anniversary of the promulgation of the present Military Service Law. Regional party, government, and military leaders Raidi, Puqung, (Cui Jiguo), Laba Pingcuo, Luosang Toinzhub, Zhou Wenbi, Hu Yongzhu, Gama Cedain, Jiang Chengguang, and concerned leaders from various regional departments and bureaus attended the forum. Regional Vice Chairman Laba Pingcuo addressed the forum. He said: Since the present Military Service Law was promulgated 10 years ago, party and government organizations at all levels across the region have been carrying out the propaganda and implementation of the law as a strategic task for improving the understanding of national defense among the masses of all nationalities in the region and enhancing their awareness of the need for national defense, and have been taking the support for national defense and army building as an important element in stabilizing the situation and developing the economy. They gave full support to military families accompanying the troops, helping them to get jobs and education for their children. Over the past decade, the vast number of cadres and the masses -- after acquainting themselves with the basic guidelines of the Military Service Law -- enhanced their understanding of national defense and of the great significance of strengthening army building and national defense and inspired youngsters' patriotic zeal and enthusiasm in supporting and joining the Army, bringing about a favorable general mood in society to support and show concern for national defense building and to support, cherish, and honor the people's own Army. TMD Commander Zhou Wenbi and leaders from the regional party committee Propaganda Department, the regional justice department, and other relevant units also spoke at the forum.
