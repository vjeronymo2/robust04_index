940112 FT 12 JAN 94 / International Company News: Ericsson merges two Italian units Ericsson, the Swedish telecommunications group, is merging its two Italian subsidiaries to win a greater share of the expanding domestic telecommunications market. The combination of Ericsson Fatme and Ericsson Sielte will create a company with forecast sales of more than L1,600bn (Dollars 974m) this year and over 7,000 employees. Group net profits in Italy amounted to almost L59bn in the first half of last year. The new company, to be called Ericsson Telecommunicazioni, is the Swedish group's largest foreign subsidiary.
