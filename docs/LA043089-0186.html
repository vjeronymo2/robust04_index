April 30, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

ANKLE INJURY TO SIDELINE HART SOFTBALL ACE WEIL

Kym Weil, Hart High's ace softball pitcher, will be sidelined indefinitely after she was struck in the left ankle by an errant throw between games of a doubleheader in the Thousand Oaks tournament Saturday.

Weil was taken to an area hospital where X-rays proved negative.

Weil is 17-1, including Saturday's one-hit, 4-0 win over Camarillo. The Indians beat La Habra, 7-3, in the second game.
