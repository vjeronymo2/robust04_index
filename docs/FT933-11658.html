930728 FT 28 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Temple Bar Trust net assets ahead Net asset value per share of Temple Bar Investment Trust rose from 310.9p to 325.09p net of prior charges at nominal value over the six months ended June 30. Attributable profits for the half year to end-June amounted to Pounds 3.92m (Pounds 4.38m). Earnings emerged at 6.835p (7.642p) and the interim dividend is 4.6p (4.5p).
