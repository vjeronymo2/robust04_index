Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Havana, 26 Mar (PL) -- A PRC Government delegation headed by Hao Jianxiu, vice president of the PRC National Planning Commission, arrived in Cuba today. The mission will participate in the sixth meeting of the Joint PRC-Cuba Commission for Economic and Commercial Relations, which, according to reports, will be held in Havana. During their stay in the country, the visitors will also meet with important Cuban officials and will visit scientific, production, and historic centers. The delegation will remain in Cuba until 1 April. Before leaving, the delegates will sign documents related to bilateral cooperation with their Cuban counterparts.
