July 22, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

NEWSWIRE

Former Raider Rusty Hilger, who last week led the Oklahoma City Twisters to a 13-6 victory over the Fresno Bandits, was signed by the Seattle Seahawks.

Hilger played for Tom Flores, Seahawk president, with the Raiders from 1985-87.

The former Oklahoma State quarterback started nine games for the Detroit Lions in 1988, but was cut in preseason last year.
