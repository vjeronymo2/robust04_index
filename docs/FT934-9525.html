931110 FT 10 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Pittencrieff boosts Canadian interests Pittencrieff, the oil and gas concern, has agreed to acquire for CDollars 6.75m (Pounds 3.32m) an additional 24.8 per cent working interest in the Patricia Gas Field in Alberta, Canada. This is together with a 100 per cent working interest in 5 wells and over 3,500 acres of development lands adjoining the Patricia unit.
