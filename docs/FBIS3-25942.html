Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The provincial army and local cooperation amity delegation headed by Governor Ye Liansong, successfully visited Canada and the United States in mid-January. During visits to Canada and the United States, the delegation extensively established contacts and held talks on a group of the province's key external economic and technological cooperation projects with the personages of the business, banking, scientific and technological, and education circles of the two countries; and signed 26 letters of agreement and intention on cooperation and joint investment in the aspects of highway construction, medical industry, chemical industry, and power industry. On 25 January, Ye Liansong, governor of Hebei Province, and Missouri State Governor Carnahan formally signed a protocol on establishing friendship ties between Hebei Province of China and Missouri State of the United States. Following Iowa State, this is the second city [as heard] in the United States that has established friendship ties with our province. The delegation held symposiums in Canada's Toronto city and the cities of New York, Saint Louis and Jefferson in the United States to invite businessmen. Personages in the business and banking circles of Canada and the United States were extensively interested in more than 100 machinery and electronics projects and technological transformation projects, introduced by the province. Many companies expressed the desire to visit our province in the foreseeable future and to hold further talks on items of interest.
