941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / Letters to the Editor: A nod does not suffice as the law Sir, Surely if Lloyd's is to recover monies receivable by Names arising from successful court cases against member's agents, and so forth, it should not be permitted to do so on the nod of the secretary of state for trade and industry but only following new legislation and proper open debate?
