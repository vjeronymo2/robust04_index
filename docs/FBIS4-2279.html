BFN [From the "National Hookup" program] [Text] French Prime Minister Edouard Balladur arrived in Beijing this morning to begin a four-day official visit to China. Balladur is visiting China at the invitation of Premier Li Peng. During his visit, he will exchange views with Chinese leaders on further developing Sino-French relations, including economic and trade relations. Among those who accompanied Prime Minister Balladur in his visit to China were his wife; the French foreign minister, the French minister of industry, post, telecommunications, and foreign trade; and the vice presidents of the French National Assembly. This afternoon, Premier Li Peng presided over a welcoming ceremony for Prime Minister Balladur and his party on the square outside the eastern gate of the Great Hall of the People. Zhu Lin, Premier Li Peng's wife; Qian Qichen, State Council vice premier and concurrently foreign minister, and his wife, (Zhou Hanqiong); and Shi Dazhen, chairman of the Chinese Government Reception Committee and minister of power industry, attended the welcoming ceremony. After the ceremony, the Chinese and French premiers held talks in the Great Hall of the People. This afternoon, Wu Yi, foreign trade and economic cooperation minister, met with Gerard Longuet, French minister of industry, post, telecommunications, and foreign trade, who accompanied Balladur on his visit to China.
