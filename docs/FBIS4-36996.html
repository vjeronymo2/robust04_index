BFN [Text] Kuwait City, 14 Jun (KUNA)--Shaykh Sabah al-Ahmad al-Jabir, first deputy prime minister and minister for foreign affairs, received a message from U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher. An authoritative source at the Foreign Affairs Ministry stated that the message--which was conveyed yesterday by Ryan Crocker, U.S. ambassador to the State of Kuwait, to Sulayman al-Shahin, under secretary at the Foreign Affairs Ministry, dealt with bilateral relations, issues of common interest, and the latest developments in the region.
