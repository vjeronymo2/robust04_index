940216 FT 16 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Stanelco tumbles Pounds 84,000 into the red Stanelco, the USM-quoted maker of thermal processing equipment, announced pre-tax losses of Pounds 84,000 for the half year to August 31. The result, which was achieved on turnover of Pounds 638,000 (Pounds 791,000), compared with profits of Pounds 14,000 and included exceptional relocation costs of Pounds 15,000. Losses per share came through at 0.01p (nil). There is no interim divi-dend and directors do not expect to declare a full-year payment.
