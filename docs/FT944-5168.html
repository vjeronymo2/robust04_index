941205 FT 05 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Henderson Strata net assets rise 5% Henderson Strata Investments had a net asset value per share of 284p at October 31 a rise of 4.8 per cent on the 271p of a year earlier. Gross revenue for the year increased by Pounds 15,000 to Pounds 841,000, but the after-tax figure dropped to Pounds 167,000 (Pounds 222,000) for earnings per share of 1p (1.36p). A reduced dividend of 0.5p (1.4p) is recommended for the year.
