October 6, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

A 'CAUSE CELEBRE' OVER WEALTHY SOCIAL ACTIVISTS

To paraphrase the late, great Lenny Bruce: Anyone who professes to care about his fellow man's nakedness while possessing two suits is a hypocrite.

An apartment at the Beverly Wilshire for $20,000 a month?

A $6.8-million home?

Matching Mercedes?

And this person expects others to contribute to his campaign for the homeless?

HUGH R. TAYLOR

Los Angeles
