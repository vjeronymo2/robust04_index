BFN [Excerpt] Zvornik, 3 May (TANJUG) -- Transportation of aid through the territory of the republic of Srpska resumed today in accordance with the Serb side's decision fully to normalize humanitarian aid transportation, TANJUG has learned from the command of the republic of Srpska army's Zvornik garrison. Like yesterday, a UNHCR [UN High Commissioner for Refugees] humanitarian aid convoy crossed the Drina River near Zvornik carrying prefabricated homes for the Muslim enclave of Srebrenica. A logistics convoy of 18 vehicles and 40 French UNPROFOR [UN Protection Force] members has left for Sarajevo. [passage omitted]
