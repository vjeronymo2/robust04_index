920918 FT 18 SEP 92 / Observer: Dutch double No doubt the reasons for the merger of Reed International and Dutch publishers Elsevier are just as the companies say they are - synergy, the opportunity of creating the world's most profitable publishing group and suchlike. But what a coincidence that Peter Davis, chairman of Reed and chief executive and deputy chairman of the new company, should have a Dutch mother. Not to mention the fact that Reed's finance director Nigel Stapleton has a Dutch wife.
