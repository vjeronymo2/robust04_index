Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A circular issued by the National Liberation Front [FLN] notes the rejection by the Higher State Council [HSC] and the National Dialogue Commission of the FLN Political Bureau's proposal to convene a meeting between the HSC, the National Dialogue Commission, and a group of six political parties, in order to discuss measures sufficient to make the national conference successful. The FLN circular adds that the party's general secretariat received the rejection in two letters, the first from the HSC chairman, and the second from the chairman of the National Dialogue Commission.
