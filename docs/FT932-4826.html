930608 FT 08 JUN 93 / Successor nominated for presidency of IG Metall MR KLAUS ZWICKEL was yesterday nominated to succeed Mr Franz Steinkuhler as president of IG Metall, the German engineering union, writes Christopher Parkes in Frankfurt. His selection, followed Mr Steinkuhler's resignation two weeks ago after allegations of insider share trading. Mr Zwickel was formerly deputy president.
