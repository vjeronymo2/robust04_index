Description of proposed amendment: Nebraska Public Power District, licensee for the North Platte/Keystone Diversion Dam Project, filed an application to amend article 401 of their license. Article 401 requires the licensee to develop and maintain eight islands as nesting habitat for interior least terns and piping plovers. The proposed amendment would allow the licensee to substitute up to four sand pits for four of the eight islands they are required to develop. The proposed amendment requires a plan on how these sand pits would be developed. A draft plan was included in the licensee's filing. l.
