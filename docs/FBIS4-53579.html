BFN [Text] All foreigners participating in the East Timor Conference will be barred from entering the country. This was the order given by the president to the Departments of Justice and Foreign Affairs. Ramos said that the entry of foreign nationals attending the conference on East Timor is inimical to national interests. The eight foreigners who will be banned from entering the country are: Jose Ramos Horta, Carmel Budjardjo, Jose Gutierrez, Lim-Siou Liong, A. Perera, Maria Alkatiri, Juan Feberer, and Jaoa Carrascalao. They will not be given visas. Visas already issued will be canceled, while those foreigners already in the country may be deported. Only foreign exchange students will be allowed to attend the conference.
