910504 FT 04 MAY 91 / Appointments: Pfizer chairman PFIZER, Sandwich, has appointed Mr Hugh O'Connor as chairman. He was managing director, and succeeds Mr Ian Wilson who has retired. Mr O'Connor joined the company as managing director a year ago. He had previously been managing director of the Pfizer Chemical Corporation at Ringaskiddy in the Republic of Ireland, and has been with the group for over 20 years. Mr Wilson becomes a non-executive director.
