Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] France will provide financial aid to update the Nos. 5 and 6 Power Generating Units at the Kozloduy Nuclear Power Plant. This is stated in the cooperation agreement signed today between Electricite de France, the Bulgarian Power Supply Committee, and the National Electrical Company. On the French side, the document was signed by Mr. Remy Carle [as heard], deputy director general of Electricite de France and chairman of the World Association of Nuclear Operators -- WANO, who is paying an official visit to Bulgaria. As already reported, yesterday Mr. Carle was in Kozloduy, where he familiarized himself with the work completed on restoring the Nos. 1 and 2 Reactor Units at the nuclear plant, which was also carried out with French assistance.
