BFN [Article signed JCH/MTH: "Meeting Between Rwandan Prime Minister and Willy Claes"] [Text] Brussels, 10 May (BELGA) -- According to a press release issued by the Belgian Foreign Ministry on Tuesday evening [10 May], Foreign Minister Willy Claes has met, at his request, with Faustin Twagiramungu, the Rwandan prime minister designated in the 4 August 1993 Arusha Accord. The press release added that, at the meeting, which took place on Tuesday morning, the two men discussed the situation in Rwanda and the steps that should be taken by the international community to halt the massacres, end the suffering of the population, establish a cease-fire, and restart the peace process and dialogue in the spirit of Arusha.
