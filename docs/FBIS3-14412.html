Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 27 Mar (INA)--Yemen Foreign Minister Mr. Muhammad Salim Basindwah described the continuation of sanctions imposed on Iraq as an act against the international law and world relations and does not serve the security in the region. Mr. Basindwah told INA here today that the continuation of the sanctions aimed to prevent the Security Council from adopting a resolution for the sake of Iraq. He urged the council to be more just concerning the Arab issues mainly the question of lifting the sanction imposed on Iraq.
