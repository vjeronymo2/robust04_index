BFN [Excerpts] [Passage omitted] The launch of the next Mir crew has been postponed, as Colonel General Vladimir Ivanov, commander of Russia's Military Space Forces, has confirmed. According to unofficial information, the launch has had to be postponed because of a hold-up in testing the nose fairing on the Soyuz spacecraft: The fairing covers the craft during the launch when the rocket is going through the Earth's atmosphere. TM-19 was initially due to blast off on 20 June; now the possibilities of carrying out a launch on the 29 or 31 June are being examined. [passage omitted]
