Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Datuk Mohamad bin Rahmat, secretary general of Malaysia's United Malays National Organization, received a Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] delegation on 27 January led by Kim Yang-kon, deputy director of the WPK Central Committee. During the meeting the head of the delegation asked the secretary general to convey the greetings of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il to Datuk Seri Dr. Mahathir bin Mohamad, prime minister and president of the United Malays National Organization in Malaysia. Expressing deep gratitude, the secretary general asked the head of the delegation to convey the greetings of his party's President Datuk Seri Dr. Mahathir bin Mohamad to the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. The secretary general spoke of actively developing the relations between the two parties and stressed that he hopes the peaceful reunification of Korea will be achieved as soon as possible.
