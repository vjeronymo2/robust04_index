BFN [Text] United Nations, April 27 (AFP) -- The UN Security Council gave the green light Wednesday for the deployment of an additional 6,500 troops to reinforce the UN Protection Force (UNPROFOR) in the former Yugoslavia. A resolution adopted by unanimity by the 15 council members authorized an increase of up to 6,500 additional troops for UNPROFOR, 150 military observers and 275 civilian police personnel.
