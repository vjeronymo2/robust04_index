941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Sec Endowment Securitised Endowment Contracts, which makes a market in assigned with-profits endowment policies, raised pre-tax profits by 52 per cent from Pounds 745,585 to Pounds 1.13m for the year to September 30. Turnover jumped 78 per cent to Pounds 19.3m. Earnings per share came out at 5.43p (5.66p). A recommended final dividend of 2p makes a total of 3.5p. In line with indications in last year's prospectus, the company intends to move from the USM to a full listing. The company hopes that dealings will begin in the latter half of next month. The company is also implementing a corporate restructuring to facilitate the development of other trading activities, incorporating a change of name to SEC Group.
