930501 FT 01 MAY 93 / Bolsover pit is closed Miners mark the closure of Bolsover pit in Derbyshire, which ceased production yesterday along with four other mines. British Coal is to offer them for sale or lease. The pits, which employed more than 2,000 people, are the first of 20 to be offered to the private sector. The NUM said it would continue to fight the closures
