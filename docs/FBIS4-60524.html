BFN [Text] Cairo, 25 May (MENA) -- Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa has stated that efforts are currently being made to reach a cease-fire in Yemen. He said that Egypt is contacting the parties involved in the Yemeni conflict, and also contacting a number of regional and other interested countries. He made this statement after meeting with Edmund Hull, U.S. charge d'affaires, today for the second time in 24 hours. He met with the U.S. official last night and discussed the situation in Yemen and Somalia.
