940812 FT 12 AUG 94 / International Company News: SBC expands Swiss Bank Corporation has entered into a partnership agreement with four regional banks, in an attempt to strengthen its domestic market position, AP-DJ reports from Basle. The four banks are Bank in Gossau, Sparkassa Berneck, Bank in Niederuzwil, and the Spar und Leihkasse Wartau-Sevelen. Under the agreement, SBC will acquire a 5 per cent stake in each bank. Terms were not disclosed.
