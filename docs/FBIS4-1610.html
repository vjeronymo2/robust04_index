BFN [Text] Lusaka, March 31 (XINHUA) -- China's State Educational Commission today donated a batch of office equipment and stationery to the Evelyn Hone College in Zambia. A hand-over ceremony was held at the college in Lusaka today, which was attended by Zambian Minister of Science, Technical Education and Vocational Training Gabriel Maka and Chinese Ambassador to Zambia Yang Zengye. The donation includes a computer and varieties of stationery worth some 10,000 U.S. dollars. The Chinese ambassador said at the ceremony though the quantity of the equipment and goods is not much, it demonstrates the Chinese government and people is willing to make humble efforts to the development of the Zambian educational sector. There are two Chinese associate professors and one lecturer working in the college.
