930731 FT 31 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Unilever expands German operations Unilever is to expand its bakery raw materials activities in Germany. It has agreed with Diamalt in Munich and BM Backermuhlen in Stuttgart to take over Diamalt's interests in baking products and malt extracts, supplied to bakeries and the bread products industry. The deal, which requires Bundeskartellamt approval, is expected to become effective on October 1. The company said the consideration was not significant in relation to Unilever's assets.
