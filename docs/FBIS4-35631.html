BFN [Text] Sanaa, 1 Jun (INA) -- An official Yemeni source has told INA correspondent in Sanaa that the legitimate Yemeni forces have seized 80 tanks which were sent from Saudi Arabia to the secessionists in Aden. The source said that Saudi Arabia moved these tanks via the al-'Abr border area into Hadhramaut Governotate three days ago. The source added that Yemeni tribes in Shabwah Governorate and Hadhramaut Governorate seized the tanks when those who were transporting them hinted that they were representatives of 'Ali Salim al-Bid. The source explained that as a result, Yemeni President Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih contacted Saudi King Fahd by telephone and informed him that he had received the consignment of tanks sent to the secessionists.
