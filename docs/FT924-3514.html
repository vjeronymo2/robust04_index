921208 FT 08 DEC 92 / World News in Brief: Wildlife hit as oil leaks from tanker Salvage workers are fighting a losing battle against a tide of oil spilling from the Greek tanker Aegean Sea, which ran aground at the entrance to La Coruna harbour last week. Strong winds are spreading the 67,000 tonnes of oil along Spain's north west coast, smothering seabirds and contaminating fishing grounds.
