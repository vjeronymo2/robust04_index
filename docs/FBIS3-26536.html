Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video; from the "National News Hookup" program] [Text] On behalf of the CPC Central Committee and the State Council, Jiang Zemin, general secretary of the CPC Central Committee, and Liu Huaqing and Hu Jintao, members of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau Standing Committee, went to the residences of Comrades Peng Zhen and Wan Li on the afternoon of 7 February to pass on happy New Year and Spring Festival greetings. [video shows close-ups of a smiling Jiang Zemin shaking Peng Zhen's left hand -- his right arm hanging limply down his side -- sitting in a wheelchair and wearing a hearing aid. Jiang Zemin is seen presenting a bouquet of flowers to Peng Zhen; and Liu Huaqing, in military uniform, is seen saluting Peng before shaking his hand. After Liu, Hu Jintao is seen stepping forward to shake Peng's left hand with both hands. Video then cuts to show Jiang Zemin talking loudly to Peng over a small table. Jiang is heard saying to Peng: "I visited your hometown, Luliang, and southern Shanxi." Jiang is also heard saying something about road construction in Shanxi] Jiang Zemin warmly inquired of Peng Zhen and Wan Li about their lives and health and briefed them about the important tasks which the party Central Committee and the State Council accomplished last year, as well as the basic principles guiding the tasks this year. He hoped the veteran comrades will continue to give the central authorities more suggestions about their work, and make greater contributions to the motherland's economic construction and reform and opening endeavors. [video shows close-ups of Jiang Zemin and Wan Li shaking hands and Wan Li receiving a bouquet of flowers from Jiang and posing for a picture with Jiang. Wan Li appears healthy and in good spirits. Video cuts to show close-ups of Jiang Zemin and Wan Li sitting on large sofas having a pleasant chat over a small table] Peng Zhen and Wan Li asked Jiang Zemin, Liu Huaqing, and Hu Jintao to convey their thanks to leading comrades of the CPC Central Committee and the State Council. Peng Zhen and Wan Li also wished the visitors Happy New Year. The two veteran comrades were very happy with the successes China achieved in reform, opening up, and construction. They also expressed their wholehearted wish that, during the new year, China will achieve even greater success in economic construction, reform, and opening up, as well as in maintaining social stability.
