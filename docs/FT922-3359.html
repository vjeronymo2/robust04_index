920611 FT 11 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Danka Business Systems rises 25% Danka Business Systems, which distributes office equipment throughout the eastern US through its operating subsidiary based in Florida, lifted pre-tax profits by 25 per cent, from Pounds 9.35m to Pounds 11.7m, in the year to March 31. Mr Mark Vaughan-Lee, chairman, said Danka's performance had been significantly better than most of its competitors, enabling the group to increase market share. During the second half the group acquired three distribution companies and a fourth was bought in May. All would be fully integrated into its branch network which now totalled 84 outlets. The group is investigating the possibility of acquiring distribution operations in Europe. Turnover amounted to Pounds 115m (Pounds 93.8m), generating operating profits of Pounds 13.2m (Pounds 10.8m). Earnings per share came through at 29.3p (23.1p) and a final dividend of 2.5p is recommended for a 3.75p (3p) total. There was an extraordinary charge of Pounds 922,000 (Pounds 2.23m).
