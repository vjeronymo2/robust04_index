BFN [Text] Tirana April 20 (ATA) -- On April 20 Albanian ambassador in Athens, Hysen Cabej, was called to the Greek Foreign Ministry by the vice director of Balkan political department, Mr.Ralis, who presented the Greek request that the Albanian diplomatic staff be reduced by one more person. The present staff of the Albanian Embassy in Athens is composed of five diplomats. Strangely enough, this fact runs counter to the statements uttered by the Greek government members themselves on finding a way to dialogue and cooperation. As a result, the message from Athens is difficult to be understood, says a statement of the spokesman of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Albania regarding this event.
