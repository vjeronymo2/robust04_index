October 18, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Flutist Emily Lin, 16, of Placentia, bassist Brent Simmons, 17, of Placentia and William Shieh, 17, of Villa Park were picked as finalists from a field of 20 players Tuesday at the Orange County Youth Symphony Orchestra Concerto Competition at Chapman College in Orange. Each will be a featured soloist with the orchestra at Chapman: Lin on Dec. 9, Simmons on March 3 and Shieh on May 4. Violinists Cathy Kim, 16, of Anaheim and Michi Wiancko, 15, of San Clemente were semi-finalists.
