BFN [Text] Here is a report from our Foreign Ministry's press service: To ensure the irreversibility of the peace talks, Azerbaijan, Armenia, and the international organizations, primarily the CSCE, should declare that they will assume the following obligations after the establishment of a cease-fire in the region. 1. The Armenian Republic's leadership should guarantee to pull out its Armed Forces from all the occupied Azerbaijani lands, including Shusha and Lachin. 2. The Azerbaijani Republic's leadership should guarantee to discuss Daghlyk-Karabakh's [Nagorno-Karabakh's] status within the Azerbaijani state structure. 3. The CSCE countries should announce that they will not allow military equipment to be sent to Daghlyk-Karabakh. This proposal was put forward by our republic's foreign minister, Gasan Gasanov, during a meeting with Mathias Mosberg, deputy chairman of Minsk Group.
