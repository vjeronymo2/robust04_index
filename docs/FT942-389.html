940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Colorgen shares suspended at 26p Shares in Colorgen, the US-based colour matching specialist, were suspended at 26p yesterday pending clarification of the company's financial position. In March, Colorgen, which is traded on the USM, reported pre-tax profits of Dollars 12,000 (Pounds 8,000) for the half year to December 31, against Dollars 9,000, on sales up from Dollars 5.91m to Dollars 7.33m. Profits for the year to end June totalled Dollars 407,000 (Dollars 928,000).
