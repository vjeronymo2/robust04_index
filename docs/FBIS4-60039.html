BFN [Text] Dr. Kamal al-Janzuri, deputy prime minister and minister of planning, has affirmed that the investments in the new plan for 1994-1995 amount to 35 billion pounds. From this, 12.8 billion pounds are allocated to the infrastructure. Dr. al-Janzuri, in remarks he made today after the meeting of the Planning and Budget Committee of the People's Assembly, said that the government is allocating the necessary funds to important popular projects, foremost of which are education, health, drinking water, and sewege systems. He added that during the past 10 years the government spent around 14 billion pounds on public services. The deputy prime minister and minister of planning affirmed that the government for many years has been working on allocating government expenditures to various projects.
