930406 FT 06 APR 93 / UK Company News: Home Counties Difficult trading conditions and start-up costs of new publications meant Home Counties Newspapers suffered a 29 per cent fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.06m to Pounds 747,000 in 1992. Turnover edged ahead from Pounds 21.5m to Pounds 22.9m. As forewarned at the interim stage, the final dividend has been cut from 5.5p to 3.25p for a total of 6p (8.25p). The result included exceptional gains of Pounds 512,000 (restated Pounds 189,000) on the sale of investments.
