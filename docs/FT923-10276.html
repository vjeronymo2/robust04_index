920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / International Company News: Omnicom posts 3% increase in pre-tax profits OMNICOM Group, the US-based international advertising group, yesterday reported a 3 per cent rise in pre-tax profits for the first half of 1992 to Dollars 56.8m - allowing for extraordinary items of Dollars 6.7m - against Dollars 54.9m a year ago. Barring extraordinary items, which involved new accounting principles and retirement benefits, pre-tax profits were up by 16 per cent. Net income for the six months increased by 20 per cent, to Dollars 35.1m from Dollars 29.1m. Fully diluted earnings per share increased 14 per cent to Dollars 1.17 from Dollars 1.03.
