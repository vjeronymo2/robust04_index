941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / Observer: Old banger Cabinet ministers enjoy revealing new policies at the Conservative party conference, even when the 'new' is actually just the old, dusted off a little. Yesterday at Bournemouth, transport secretary Brian Mawhinney gave literal extension to that metaphor, by saying that vintage car owners will be consulted over widening the application of vehicle excise duty to their old jalopies. As a vote-catching vehicle it looks a little rusty.
