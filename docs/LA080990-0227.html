August 9, 1990, Thursday, South Bay Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Recall attempt -- A story in the Aug. 2 edition of The Times incorrectly stated that organizers of a recall attempt against two trustees in the Inglewood Unified School District had withdrawn their effort. The story should have said that the organizers of the recall did not submit the required signatures to the county registrar-recorder's office by the July 17 deadline.
