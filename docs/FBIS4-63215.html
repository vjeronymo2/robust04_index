BFN [Text] Col-Gen [Vladimir] Semenov, commander in chief of ground forces, said the recent talks in Moscow on the Karabakh conflict must be regarded as abortive as the Azerbaijanis have failed to sign the final document. A similar assessment was already made by Col-Gen [Yevgeniy] Podkolzin, commander of airborne forces. He also believes airborne forces cannot be dispatched to the region without the consent of Baku authorities. However, according to the SNARK agency, Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev does favor the dispatch of Russian forces to the Karabakh region.
