Language: English Article Type:BFN [By CRIME-PRESS correspondent Yuriy Shcherban for TASS] [Text] Moscow March 11 TASS -- Court investigation of the case of organisers of the 1991 Soviet coup has resumed after the Presidium of the Supreme Court of the Russian Federation considered and satisfied the appeal of the prosecution on Friday, TASS learned from the secretariat of the Presidium. The trial of the 1991 coup organisers was declared over after the Russian State Duma proclaimed an amnesty to the plotters. However, the prosecution appealed against the decision saying that any trial which is already under way is to be completed by passing a guilty or non-guilty verdict. If the verdict is guilty, the accused will then be amnestied.
