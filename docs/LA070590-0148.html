July 5, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: SANTA MONICAS;

PARK SERVICE EVENTS LISTING

National Park Service officials have announced a three-month schedule of outdoor events in and near the Santa Monica Mountains.

Rangers from national and state park services, docents and volunteers will be leading a variety of nature walks, talks, and hikes through September, a park service spokesman said.

The booklet "Summer in the Santa Monica Mountains and Seashore -- 1990," provides a complete listing of events. It is available free. To obtain a copy, call (818) 597-9192, or write to Calendar, Santa Monica Mountains National Recreation Area, 30401 Agoura Road, Suite 100, Agoura Hills, Calif. 91301.
