BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- Chancellor Helmut Kohl praised the reconciliatory role of Moscow's State Duma Chairman Ivan Rybkin in Russian politics. According to government spokesman Dieter Vogel, during talks with Rybkin in Bonn Kohl also stressed Germany's "energetic interest" in the continuation of the democratic and market economic reform process in Russia on the basis of stable framework conditions. The two politicians agreed "that bilateral relations are free from fundamental and insoluble problems." This positive starting point should be used and further developed in the interest of the two countries and all of Europe. The chairman of the Russian State Duma also plans to meet Bundestag President Rita Suessmuth and Federal President Richard von Weizsaecker today.
