(a)(1) General. For each fiscal year for which funds are appropriated to carry out this part, a State that has entered into an agreement with the Secretary under §667.3 shall apply to the Secretary to receive its allotment. The State's application must contain the information required in paragraph (b), (c), or (d) of this section, as appropriate. The Secretary publishes in the Federal Register the date by which a State must submit its application. (2) Established review standards. A State has established review standards if those standards met the requirements in §667.21 and the Secretary did not disapprove those standards under §667.22.
