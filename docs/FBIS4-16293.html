BFN [Text] It was announced today at a news conference by Prime Minister Adolfas Slezevicius that the form of work by the Lithuanian Government is to be changed in the immediate future. All decisions will be made at closed meetings, and then the government press attache will comment on them to journalists and the public. Such practice exists in many countries. This does not mean that government work will be secret. We free journalists from taking part in weary meetings, said the prime minister.
