920505 FT 05 MAY 92 / Government for Sierra Leone The leaders of a coup last week in Sierra Leone have announced a 19-man government which includes two former ministers from the cabinet of ousted President Joseph Momoh, Reuter reports from Freetown. A statement by the National Provisional Ruling Council (NPRC), which seized power from Mr Momoh on Thursday, named 27-year-old Captain Valentine Strasser as head of state, defence minister and NPRC chairman. Among six civilians named are Mr Jim Funa, finance minister under Mr Momoh, and Mr Ahmed Dumbuya, foreign minister.
