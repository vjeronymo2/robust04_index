941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Observer: Small matter Calling all 'resting' actors below a certain height. With the pantomime Snow White proving unusually popular this season - 20 plus productions around the country - there is an awkward little supply and demand imbalance relating to the long-tressed damsel's seven compact companions. Martin Brown, a spokesman for the actors' union Equity, highlights the problem: 'We only have 37 persons of restricted growth on our register. 'That's not necessarily the full list,' he adds brightly, 'because not every performer of restricted growth wants to be pigeon-holed in that way.' What, they'd rather be called a dwarf?
