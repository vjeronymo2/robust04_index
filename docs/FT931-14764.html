930116 FT 16 JAN 93 / Cable TV 'soon to grow by 60%' CABLE television will grow by more than 60 per cent this year, the Cable Television Association predicted yesterday. Mr Richard Woollam, director-general of the association, forecast at a conference in London that at least 800,000 further householders would be given access to cable networks enabling them to subscribe and that there would be 300,000 new subscribers.
