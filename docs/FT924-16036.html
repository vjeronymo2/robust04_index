921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Ramco rises to Pounds 250,000 Ramco Oil Services, the Aberdeen-based oil services company which is seeking opportunities in the former Soviet Union, recorded an 11 per cent rise in interim pre-tax profits to Pounds 250,000, in difficult conditions, writes Wallton Morais. Turnover in the six months to June 30 rose sharply to Pounds 2.87m (Pounds 1.89m). Earnings per share were static at 0.95p (0.94p), and there was again no interim dividend.
