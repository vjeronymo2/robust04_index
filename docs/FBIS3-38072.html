Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Denis Perkin] [Text] Moscow February 25 TASS -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and his Ukrainian counterpart Anatoliy Zlenko agreed during a telephone conversation on Friday that it is necessary to boost political settlement in Bosnia. Kozyrev and Zlenko stressed the importance to ensure safety and most favourable conditions in order to help Russian and Ukrainian peace-keepers to fulfil their mission near Sarajevo and coordinate activities.
