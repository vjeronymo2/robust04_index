December 4, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Keating Bail Rules Modified: Superior Court Judge Lance A. Ito ordered indicted Arizona businessman Charles H. Keating Jr. to follow a stricter weekly check-in schedule after prosecutors complained that the former thrift executive couldn't be found over the Thanksgiving Day weekend. Ito ordered Keating and the Los Angeles County District Attorney's Office to choose a specific day and time for weekly check-ins by telephone. "That way, you don't have to play phone tag with each other," Ito said.
