March 31, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

JUDGING OSCAR: TELECAST VIEWERS HAVE THEIR SAY

A couple of suggestions to Kim Basinger regarding her remarks about "Do the Right Thing" on the Oscar telecast: Consider the possibility that Spike Lee's movie was not one of the five best of 1989. Don't presume that the academy has a problem because it doesn't share your opinion.

STEVE FOSTER, Los Angeles
