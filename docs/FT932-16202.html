930407 FT 07 APR 93 / West Midlands signals upturn COMPANIES in the west Midlands are indicating that the long-awaited economic recovery could be under way. A survey of more than 700 companies by the West Midlands Group of Chambers of Commerce paints the brightest picture for many months, with a marked rise in orders and deliveries from manufacturers and improved trade for service companies. Manufacturers saw a 13 per cent increase in home orders and an 8 per cent rise in export orders in the last quarter compared with the previous three months. Service companies reported home orders rising 9 per cent and export orders increasing 11 per cent. Companies in both sectors expect to see their turnover increase.
