921024 FT 24 OCT 92 / Markets: Cheer for smaller companies - At a glance Small company shares joined in the general euphoria of the equity market this week, as the government emphasised its plans to 'go for growth'. The County Small Companies Index rose 1.8 per cent to 831.03 (815.94) over the week to October 22, while the Hoare Govett Index (capital gains version) rose 2 per cent from 1054.6 to 1075.56 over the same period.
