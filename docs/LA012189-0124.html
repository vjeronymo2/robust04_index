January 21, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

THE COLLEGES;

LOPEZ AND RICH LEAD CHRIST COLLEGE IRVINE

Mary Lopez scored 26 points to lead Christ College Irvine to a 75-59 victory over Southern California College in a Golden State Athletic Conference game Friday at Irvine.

Lauren Rich scored 20 points for Christ College (6-12 overall, 1-4 in conference play).
