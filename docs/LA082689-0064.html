August 26, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

NEWSMAKERS

Thomas L. Burkholder has been appointed treasurer and director of taxes for Gradco Systems Inc., Irvine. His previous positions include assistant treasurer for Coldwell Banker Residential Group and tax manager at the accounting firm of Ernst & Whinney. Gradco supplies paper handling equipment to manufacturers of photocopiers, computer printers and facsimile machines.
