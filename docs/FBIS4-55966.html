BFN ["Pool" item] [Text] Doboj, May 24 (TANJUG) -- Moslems and Croats on Tuesday afternoon [24 May] attacked Serb positions on all sections of the front from Doboj in the north of Bosnia-Herzegovina, through central Bosnia-Herzegovina, to the line of delimitation in the area of Nevesinje in the south of this former Yugoslav republic, Bosnian Serb military sources said on Tuesday evening. Armed forces of Bosnian Croats, the Croatian Defense Council -- HVO, joined the Moslem forces with their tanks and attacked Serb positions in central Bosnia, REUTERS said on Tuesday evening, quoting sources of the United Nations Protection Force (UNPROFOR) in former Yugoslavia. Bosnian Croat forces are actively helping Moslem troops against the local Serbs, REUTERS reported from Sarajevo. Two Croat T-55 tanks and HVO troops attacked Serb positions in a joint action southwest of Tesanj, REUTERS said. Two M-84 tanks also took part in this operation which began on Monday morning, said REUTERS quoting the same source. Moslems forces are attacking serb defense positions and villages in fronts in the wider area of Doboj. In the afternoon hours, Moslems shelled Doboj and Serb villages and positions west of this town. The Serb forces returned strong fire and neutralized the enemy operations. On the front southeast of Teslic (south of Doboj), Moslem units have been trying to penetrate the Serb defense lines for days. Moslems are speedily building up their forces south and southwest of Serb positions near Mt. Ozren (south of Doboj), the First Krajina corps of the Bosnian Serb Army said. United forces of the HVO and regular Croatian Army units on Tuesday fired several shells at Serb positions in the area of Gradine, on the front near Nevesinje (south of Mostar), TANJUG reported from Bileca on Tuesday evening, quoting a statement by the Herzegovina corps of the Bosnian Serb Army. The source also said that Serb fighters answered the attack, thus silencing the Croat guns.
