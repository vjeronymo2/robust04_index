BFN [Text] Commander in Chief Fidel Castro met with Christine Stewart, Canada's minister of state for Latin America and Africa. Sources from Canada's delegation confirmed that Stewart has also met with Carlos Lage, vice president of the council of ministers, Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina, and other high ranking government officials. A spokesmen from Canada's Foreign Ministry informed the media that this is the most important moment in relations between Cuba and Canada. On 20 June, Canada reported the resumption of official economic aid to Cuba. Canada also reported that $370,000 [country of currency not specified] in food products for children will be given to Cuba this year and $720,000 in 1995. The decision was announced simultaneously in Ottawa by Canada's Minister of Foreign Affairs Andre Ouellet and in Havana by Christine Stewart, who concludes a two-day visit to Cuba today.
