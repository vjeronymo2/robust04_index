BFN [Text] First Deputy Defense Minister Andrey Kokoshin announced the recreation of the Russian Academy of Rocket and Artillery Sciences during a visit to the St. Petersburg Artillery Museum on Saturday. He said that a decree on this was signed by Russian President Boris Yeltsin on 5 April. Sixty-nine research institutes and military-industrial firms have become founders of the academy. Professor Vladimir Keyeyev, doctor of technical sciences, has been elected president of the academy. The Russian Information Agency recalls that the artillery academy was originally set up in 1946 and closed down in 1953.
