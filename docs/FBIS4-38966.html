BFN [Text] Kiev -- Ukraine's Supreme Council Presidium held a meeting on 1 June attended by the chairmen of the parliament's commissions and deputy group leaders. President Leonid Kravchuk attended the meeting and proposed candidates for the post of Ukraine's prime minister. According to parliamentary sources, the candidates proposed by the president include Vitaliy Masol, Pavlo Lazarenko, Anatoliy Holubchenko, and Valentyn Symonenko. Other nominees are Acting Prime Minister Yukhym Zvyahilskyy, and Ukraine's Liberal Party leader Ihor Markulov.
