December 4, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

SOVIETS, KINGS MEET IN FORUM

The Kings will play an exhibition game tonight at 7:30 p.m. against one of two teams from the Soviet Union touring National Hockey League cities. The Kings will play Khimik, a team from the Soviet Elite league. Also touring is the Soviet Olympic team.

Every team in the league will play an exhibition game against a Soviet team in a tour planned to raise money for the NHL Players Assn. This will be the first of six games in the United States for Khimik. The Kings will be home against Vancouver Wednesday before starting a nine-day, six-game trip.
