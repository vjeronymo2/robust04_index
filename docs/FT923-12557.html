920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Moorgate lifts net asset value 14% Moorgate Investment Trust, which concentrates its portfolio on UK smaller companies, reported a net asset value of 128.1p as at May 31 - a rise of 14 per cent over the year. Net revenue for the 12 months to end-May dipped to Pounds 1.5m (Pounds 1.56m), equal to earnings of 5.37p (5.59p) per share. A recommended final dividend of 3.8p maintains the total of 5.5p.
