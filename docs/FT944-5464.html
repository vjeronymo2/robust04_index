941203 FT 03 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Browne & Tawse Brown & Tawse, the steel and pipes distributor, reduced pre-tax losses from Pounds 2.03m to Pounds 1.19m for the six months to October 2. After the sale of some subsidiary operations turnover was down from Pounds 53.5m to Pounds 38m. The interest charge fell from Pounds 813,000 to Pounds 573,000, mainly because of lower interest rates and the disposal of subsidiaries. After a nil tax charge (Pounds 1.17m), losses came out at 3.7p (2.7p) per share.
