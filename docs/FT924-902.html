921221 FT 21 DEC 92 / Hunt for Christmas bargains Shoppers approached Christmas with caution yesterday at Lakeside shopping centre in Thurrock, Essex. They came in their thousands but were intent on finding bargains. 'Any high-priced goods have been very slow,' said Lakeside merchants' association chairman Bob Kurring.
