920129 FT 29 JAN 92 / UK News (Employment): Tax workers vote to halt action TAX WORKERS have voted by a narrow majority to end industrial action after concessions from the Inland Revenue on a performance-pay system. They voted by 22,865 to 21,283 to accept the interim package, which includes the winding-up of the staff appraisal system. Their union, the Inland Revenue Staff Federation, recommended acceptance of the interim deal.
