Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Nairobi, 7 Jan (SUNA) -- The countries of IGADD [Intergovernmental Authority on Drought and Development] committee for mediation between the Sudanese Government and the rebel movement has held yesterday a meeting with the Sudan delegation headed by the Minister of Peace and Development 'Abdallah Deng Niyal. This meeting took place at Kenyan Foreign Ministry in Nairobi. The Sudanese minister affirmed during this meeting the government commitment to negotiate on controversial issues with the rebel movement and government engagement to the agreements reached in previous meetings, indicating that the government is ready to negotiate with the rebels as one body or as separate factions.
