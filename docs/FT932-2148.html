930619 FT 19 JUN 93 / Finance and the Family: Pension guide published - At a Glance The Consumers Association this week published The Which? Guide to Pensions which has chapters on pension choices, the basic state pension, Serps and occupational and private schemes. It also examines the pension problems facing women and what to do if you want to retire early or if problems arise with your pension scheme. The Which? Guide to Pensions by Jonquil Lowe, Consumers, Association and Hodder & Stoughton, 359-61 Euston Road, London NW1 3AL, Pounds 9.99 (p&p free).
