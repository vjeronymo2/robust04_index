930217 FT 17 FEB 93 / Sellafield leak rated low LAST WEEK'S leak from the Sellafield nuclear complex has been classed as '1' -the least serious type of incident - on the international scale of nuclear events. The scale, devised by the IAEA, the international nuclear watchdog, ranges from 1 to 7 - the Chernobyl blast was classed as a '7'.
