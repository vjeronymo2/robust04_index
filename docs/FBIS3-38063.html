Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Andrey Polaria] [Text] Moscow February 28 TASS -- "The General Staff of the Russian Armed Forces and its intelligence service have no information on any links between the Ames couple and Soviet or Russian special services," Russia's Chief of Staff Colonel-General Mikhail Kolesnikov told ITAR-TASS on Monday. He stressed that the U.S. mass media misinterpreted his statement on the Ames affair at the press conference on February 26. "Such misinterpretations pursue one unseemly objective -- to get confirmation of the assumption that the CIA officer and his wife cooperated with the Soviet and Russian intelligence services".
