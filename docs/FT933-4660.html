930908 FT 08 SEP 93 / RSI an epidemic, says GMB chief BRITAIN is suffering an epidemic of repetitive strain injury (RSI), which afflicts more than 100,000 people, said Mr Dick Pickering, president of the GMB general union. Mr Pickering, launching a TUC campaign on RSI, said that compensation claims could cost employers Pounds 500m, and that 500,000 working days a year are lost.
