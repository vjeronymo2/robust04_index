930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Law Debenture net assets rise Law Debenture Corporation, the investment trust, increased its net asset value by 11.8 per cent to 574.5p in the six months to June 30. Net profits for the half year rose from Pounds 2.1m to Pounds 2.27m and earnings per share increased to 9.99p (9.26p). The interim dividend is stepped up to 6.5p (6.25p), a rise of 4 per cent. The board said that, subject to unforeseen circumstances, the final dividend would be not less than last year's 12p.
