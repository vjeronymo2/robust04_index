940117 FT 17 JAN 94 / Police reform fears Plans to reform the police service could undermine the present local structure and lead to a national force, the Police Federation warned yesterday. Mr Richard Coyles, federation chairman, warned the plans, contained in the Police and Magistrates Court Bill, due to go to the Lords for its second reading tomorrow, would concentrate power with the home secretary to a greater degree 'than is healthy in a democracy'.
