BFN [From the "National Newscast"] [Text] According to a TIME magazine article, the Clinton administration is concerned because a narcodemocracy could be forming. The article stated Cali Cartel money is available for use in the election campaign. According to the magazine, the situation has reportedly led top Clinton administration officials to meet with presidential candidates Ernesto Samper and Andres Pastrana to warn them about Cali Cartel attempts to infiltrate their campaigns. Pastrana has denied such a meeting was held. [Begin Pastrana recording] That is information which is published in EL TIEMPO today and which was published by TIME magazine, but I have no knowledge of this. I am now sending a letter to the overseer and to the U.S. ambassador because I believe the situation must be clarified. [end recording] Ernesto Samper, meanwhile, admits he did discuss the topic with White House officials. [Begin Samper recording] During several meetings I have held with State Department officials, we discussed various topics. We also talked about campaign financing. [end recording]
