BFN [Report by Yao Bosheng (1202 2672 3932): "Wu Bangguo Meets Russian State Duma Delegation"] [Text] Wu Bangguo, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and secretary of the Shanghai Municipal party committee, received a delegation from the Russian State Duma at the Hengshan Hotel yesterday afternoon. During the meeting, Wu Bangguo warmly welcomed the Russian State Duma delegation, which was headed by Ivan Rybkin and was on its first visit to Shanghai, and briefed his guests on Shanghai's economic development, reform, and opening up. The two sides conducted friendly talks and agreed to further strengthen economic cooperation and bilateral exchanges. Liu Suinian, member of the National People's Congress Standing Committee and chairman of the Sino-Russian Friendship Group, and Hu Chuanzhi, vice chairman of the Shanghai Municipal People's Congress Standing Committee, attended the meeting. The delegation left Shanghai for a visit to Shenzhen on a special plane yesterday afternoon.
