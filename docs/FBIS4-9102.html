BFN [Excerpt] Novi Sad, 20 Apr (TANJUG) -- Industrial production in Vojvodina increased by 39.8 percent in March compared to February this year. A further increase is expected in April, given that loans have been granted to revive production, Todor Balos, member of the Vojvodina Economic Chamber Board told TANJUG today. He said that industrial production in the first three months of this year was at a slightly lower level than in the same period last year, but the March results, which were better than in February, as well as the expected further increase in production, indicated that we are on the path of reviving production in line with the country's stabilization program.
