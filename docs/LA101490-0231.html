October 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SLEEK CHIC

To whom are you addressing this article? I myself am a physician and a working woman, but your choice of clothes was insulting to the majority of the working women out there.

Come on now, more than $1,000 for a jacket? It boggles the mind. Those prices would be appropriate for a down payment for a car, not for working clothes.

Get with it. Most of us dress well, and we still have money to buy the groceries.

DEBORAH R. ISHIDA

Beverly Hills
