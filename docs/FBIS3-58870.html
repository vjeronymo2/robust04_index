Language: Finnish Article Type:BFN [Text] Finland and Russia have reached a preliminary agreement that the threshold for relaying information about nuclear accidents will be lowered. Finland will thus receive information from Russia about less serious accidents on the other side of the eastern border. In addition, the countries agreed this week that exchanges of information about nuclear plants will be increased. Within the next few weeks a Finnish group of experts will visit the Chelyabinsk nuclear waste reprocessing plant.
