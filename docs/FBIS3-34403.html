Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Washington Succeeds in Persuading Ukraine To Abandon Plan To Arm Iran"] [Excerpt] Tehran, London, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- High-level diplomatic sources yesterday revealed the success of U.S. attempts to "persuade" Ukraine to withdraw an offer to sell Iran $1.2 billion worth of modern weapons. Ukraine made this offer to Iran in November within the framework of a world Ukranian effort to find new oil supplies to replace what it used to import from its neighbor, Russia. The sources pointed out that the question was raised during the meeting held last month in Kiev between U.S. President Bill Clinton and Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk. [passage omitted]
