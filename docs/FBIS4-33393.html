BFN [Text] President Alberto Fujimori has left for Tokyo, where he will arrive on 5 June. During his stay, Fujimori will meet with Emperor Akihito, the Japanese prime minister, foreign minister, and other Japanese government officials. Fujimori will stay in Tokyo until Wednesday, 8 June, when he will be awarded an honorary degree. From Tokyo, he will depart for an official visit to Beijing. During his stay in China, Fujimori he will seek a fishing agreement, considering Beijing is the main fish meal importer.
