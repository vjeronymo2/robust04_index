BFN [Text] Tbilisi 8 Apr -- Iberia news agency received a statement from the political council of the Georgian Social-Democratic Party. The statement says: "The political council of the Georgian Social-Democratic Party welcomes and supports Kazakhstani President Nursultan Nazarbayev's proposition that a Euro-Asian union be established, an idea which has long been under consideration within political circles of the states of the former Soviet Union."
