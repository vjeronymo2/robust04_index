February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

A group led by Minneapolis investor Irwin A. Jacobs said it raised its stake in Shaklee Corp. to 8.46% of the company's outstanding stock, from 6.8%. In a Securities and Exchange Commission filing, the group said it bought 219,000 additional shares of Shaklee between Jan. 27 and Feb. 3 at prices ranging from $25.125 to $32.817 a share, increasing its holding to 1.1 million shares. In November, Jacobs disclosed an initial stake of more than 5% in the company and said he might attempt to seek control. Shaklee is a San Francisco-based food supplies, household goods and personal-care products distributor.
