BFN [Text] Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev, who is in Sevastopol for talks on dividing up the Black Sea Fleet, has had a meeting with personnel of the fleet. He said at the meeting that the Russian delegation will insist on separate bases for the Russian Black Sea Fleet and the Ukrainian Navy, and that the Russian Navy's base will remain in Sevastopol. The Russian minister called on the Black Sea sailors not to give in to provocations, but to show restraint and calm. Our correspondent Yuriy Novikov reports that this afternoon there will be talks between the Russian and Ukrainian delegations on the fate of the Black Sea Fleet. It has been learned that the Ukrainian sidle is ready to hand over to Russia only some of the moorings and bases of the fleet in Sevastopol. The Russian delegation does not agree with this proposal and will insist on Russia being handed over all the fleet's bases in Sevastopol.
