BFN [Excerpt] Brother Ambassador Badr Hammam, assistant Egyptian foreign minister and the Egyptian president's envoy, left Sanaa today after a visit to our country lasting several days. During the visit, he conveyed two messages to brothers Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, and 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council, from brother Egyptian President Muhammad Husni Mubarak. The messages dealt with relations between both fraternal countries and developments in the political crisis that our country is experiencing. The Egyptian official also met with several officials in our country. [passage omitted]
