930125 FT 25 JAN 93 / Deal on Euphrates near TURKEY and Syria have agreed to find a settlement this year to the long dispute over the allocation of water from the Euphrates river, according to the joint statement issued following last week's meeting in Damascus, writes John Murray Brown from Ankara. Resolution of this riparian dispute could open the way for vital financial support from hitherto wary international donors for Turkey's Dollars 23bn (Pounds 15.1bn) Gap development project which makes exhaustive use of the Euphrates for irrigation.
