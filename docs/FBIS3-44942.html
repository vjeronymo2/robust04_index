Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] This morning provincial party Secretary Xie Fei hosted a provincial meeting on exchanging experiences in conducting the month of courtesy, safety, health, and service. [passage omitted] After listening to feedback on progress in this activity, Xie Fei made important remarks. He said: The the month of courtesy, safety, health, and service has practical and far-reaching significance. Do not think that this activity is an expedient measure. This activity serves as an important measure for carrying out modernization and the two civilizations. The purpose of this activity is to improve our cultural quality, civilization quality, and management over society. Comrade Xie Fei stressed: The month of courtesy, safety, health, and service first creates a fine environment of work, study, and life for the masses and is where the masses' interests lie. It enjoys the masses' support. It is necessary to further promote Guangdong's economic development by conducting the month of courtesy, safety, health, and service. This will help create a good environment for reform and opening up. Comrade Xie Fei pointed out: The month of courtesy, safety, health, and service should be combined with professional jobs in all trades and undertakings. [passage omitted]
