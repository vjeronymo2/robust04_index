920925 FT 25 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Lower copper prices affect Antofagasta Antofagasta Holdings, which has interests in mining, banking, transportation and water supply in Chile, announced a 7 per cent fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 9.6m to Pounds 8.9m in the half year to June 30. The results were affected by lower copper prices, the company said, although prices in the second half had improved. The group's other activities maintained their contribution. Turnover rose to Pounds 31.8m (Pounds 27.9m). Earnings came through at 22.1p (25p) per share and the interim dividend is unchanged at 6p. Its ultimate holding company is Dolberg Finance Corporation, incorporated in Liechtenstein.
