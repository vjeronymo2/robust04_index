Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Electric Union of the Ministry of Basic Industry has announced that summer time will go into effect throughout Cuba on 2 April at midnight, at which time clocks will be set forward one hour. The main objective of this traditional measure is to take maximum advantage of the light of the sun. Official time will be reestablished on 9 October, at 0100, at which time clocks will be set back 60 minutes.
