940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / Arafat warns on peace deal Mr Yassir Arafat, chairman of the Palestine Liberation Organisation, warned yesterday that further delay in reaching agreement over Israeli troop withdrawals from the Gaza Strip and Jericho could threatenthe peace process, writes Frances Williams in Geneva. Mr Arafat said he had been ready to sign an accord in Davos, Switzerland, where he met Mr Shimon Peres, Israel's foreign minister, at the weekend. However, the Israeli government had asked for a week to consider the draft pact before a formal signing in Cairo, provisionally set for Sunday. Israeli officials have since said an agreement could be some weeks away.
