May 4, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

SPOTLIGHT / COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE: A WEEKLY LOOK AT OFFICE AND INDUSTRIAL REAL ESTATE IN THE COUNTY.;

FOCUS: 4100 NEWPORT PLACE

Construction has been completed on 4100 Newport Place, a nine-story office tower featuring a 60-foot atrium and an exterior of granite and dark reflective glass. The building, developed by McLachlan Investment Co. and built by Snyder-Langston Builders, is currently 30% leased.

On-site amenities include an athletic club, a restaurant, a travel agency, a hair salon and a Bank of America branch.
