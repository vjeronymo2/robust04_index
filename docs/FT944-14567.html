941022 FT 22 OCT 94 / Ecuador minister impeached Ecuador's parliament has impeached energy minister Francisco Acosta, a key minister in the cabinet of President Sixto Duran Ballen. With 53 out of 72 parliamentarians in favour, Mr Acosta was voted out of office on charges of negligence, mismanagement and illegal conduct. The opposition's accusations centred on the proposed construction of a Dollars 600m oil pipeline.
