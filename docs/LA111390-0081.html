November 13, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Saturn May Add More Models: General Motors Corp.'s highly touted subsidiary will expand from three to 10 models by 1994, an automotive journal reported.

Automotive News said Saturn also may increase its plant capacity. The Spring Hill, Tenn., company will add a luxury sedan and coupe, a coupe convertible, two station wagons and two two-door hatchbacks in the next three years, the News said. The cars appear to be selling well. In fact, some dealers say they have a backlog of orders.
