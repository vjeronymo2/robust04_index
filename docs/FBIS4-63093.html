BFN [Text] The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] Defense Army Headquarters has disclosed that the situation at the front line is tense. However, no military operations were carried out today. The Armenian side has refrained from retaliating against the gunfire from the Azerbaijani military units at the defense positions from time to time. The situation on NKR's front line is relatively peaceful. The areas along the border between Armenia and Azerbaijan have been fired on from Azerbaijan and the Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic from time to time during the past two days. The firing has caused damage and [words indistinct]. According to the Armenian Ministry of Defense Press Service, the situation on the border was relatively peaceful today. The sides heeded the cease-fire agreement in the region. We hope that peace and security will be gradually established on our common border.
