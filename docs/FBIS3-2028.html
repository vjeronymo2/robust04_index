Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Washington, March 3 (XINHUA) -- A senior U.S. official said here today that the United States hopes to build a more constructive relationship with China. Assistant Secretary of State Winston Lord told reporters at a news briefing that "the China-U.S. relationship is extremely important, and as we look ahead over the coming decades, increasingly important." "We wish to have constructive relations with a stable and prosperous China," Lord noted, "that remains the clear intent and hope of the President." Lord made the statement one week before U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher pays a visit to Beijing.
