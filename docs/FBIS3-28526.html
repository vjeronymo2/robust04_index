Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vietnam has handed over to the U.S. the remains of 12 U.S. servicemen reported missing in the Indochina war. Head of the Hanoi Task Force for MIA Colonel John Cray said the lifting of the embargo created more favorable conditions for his job in Vietnam. He also believed that Vietnam would now more actively cooperate with the U.S. to solve the MIA issue.
