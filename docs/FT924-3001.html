921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / Canada rates cut Canada's chartered banks have cut their prime lending rates to corporate customers by 1/4 point to 8.75 per cent as the Canadian dollar stabilised at about 78.5 US cents in foreign exchange markets, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. Late last month the banks raised their prime rate to 9.75 per cent when the dollar came under pressure. The central bank has since cut rates by more than 1 point.
