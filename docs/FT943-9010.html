940813 FT 13 AUG 94 / Finance and the Family: Cheer for smaller companies - At a glance The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (Capital Gains version) rose one per cent over the week to Thursday 11 August. This move leaves it very close to its level at the beginning of the year, whereas the FT-SE-A All Share index is still over 6 per cent down, and the FT-SE 100 over 8 per cent down.
