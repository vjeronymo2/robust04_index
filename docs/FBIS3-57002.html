Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] A large-scale military exercise by the Ukrainian Armed Forces is planned in the Crimea for the end of January. In connection with this, representatives of a number of Crimean organizations are pointing out that these maneuvers are being carried out just before the second round of presidential elections in the Crimea, scheduled for 30 January. Meanwhile, the Ukrainian Defense Ministry press service has reported that the exercises set for January are planned and represent training for territorial defense.
