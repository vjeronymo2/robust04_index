Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Caracas, 7 Feb (PL) -- Today, Cuban Vice President Carlos Lage arrived in Caracas for a short visit in which he will meet with Rafael Caldera, the new Venezuelan head of state. Tomorrow, Lage will also meet with Foreign Minister Miguel Angel Burelli Rivas, Venezuelan Investment Fund President Abdon Vivas Teran, representatives of the Venezuelan Petroleum Corporation, and local businessmen among others. Lage just concluded a visit to Brazil. Upon his arrival in Caracas, Lage described his visit to Brazil as very satisfactory. PRENSA LATINA confirmed that Lage will travel to Colombia at the invitation of the Colombian Government in the early hours of 9 February.
