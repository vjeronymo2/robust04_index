August 15, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

SIDELINES;

JACKSON ON REDS DISABLED LIST

The Cincinnati Reds placed left-handed starter Danny Jackson on the 15-day disabled list today, retroactive to Tuesday, because of recurring stiffness in his left shoulder.

It is the second time in recent weeks that Jackson, 28, has been sidelined with shoulder ailments. He is 4-2 with a 2.95 earned run average in 14 games this season, 13 of them starts.

The Reds replaced Jackson on the roster by calling up left-hander Chris Hammond from the Class AAA Nashville Sounds, where Hammond is 14-1 with a 2.17 ERA. Hammond is to join the Reds in time for Friday night's twilight double-header in Cincinnati against the Pittsburgh Pirates.
