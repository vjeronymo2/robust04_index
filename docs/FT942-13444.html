940425 FT 25 APR 94 / Company news this week: Tarmac - Other Companies The UK's biggest construction and building materials group reports its 1993 results tomorrow, with the market expecting underlying trading profits of between Pounds 50m to Pounds 53m, against about Pounds 21m. After goodwill write-backs on disposals, the group is expected to report pre-tax losses of Pounds 40m-Pounds 45m, compared with 1992's record losses of Pounds 350.3m.
