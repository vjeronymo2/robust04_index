BFN [Text] Vientiane, May 3 (KPL) -- Foreign Minister Somsavat Lengsavat yesterday received visiting German State Secetary and Deputy Miister for Economic Cooperation and Develoqment, Wighard Hardte. The Lao foreign minister, talking with the German economic cooperation delegation, has contributed towards developing the good relations of friendship and cooperation between Laos and Germany. He highly appreciated the growing cooperation between the two countries as well as the German assistance to Laos. In response, Wighard Hardte thanked Somsavath Lengsavat for extending him a warm welcome during his visit to the country. He confirmed that the German Government has agreed to give assistance to Laos in such areas as education and human resources development. In the morning of the same day, the German deputy minister met with Acting Minister of Finance Bounlit Khenavong.
