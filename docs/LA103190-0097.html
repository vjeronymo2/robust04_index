October 31, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: DANCE

Georgians Cancel Tour: The Georgian State Dance Company, now performing in New York though Sunday, has canceled its nine-city tour -- including a seven-performance run at the Pantages which was to begin next Wednesday -- because of disappointing ticket sales. Anne Abrams, the Los Angeles spokeswoman, said ticket sales were "not even moderate." CLAUDIA PUIG
