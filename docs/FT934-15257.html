931013 FT 13 OCT 93 / 'Rao payment' probe dropped INDIAN police have dropped an inquiry into claims by Bombay stockbroker Harshad Mehta, the central figure in a huge financial scandal, that he paid Rs10m (Pounds 212,000) to an aide of Prime Minister P V Narasimha Rao, Reuter reports from Delhi. Mr Rao, who survived a no-confidence motion in parliament over the accusation, has denied receiving the money.
