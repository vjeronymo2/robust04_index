920602 FT 02 JUN 92 / London crime rises CRIME in London has risen by 11 per cent with the biggest increases being recorded in street crime and domestic violence, according to figures released yesterday. Street thefts increased by 36 per cent with 7,600 reported incidents in the year to the end of March. Only 6 per cent of muggers were caught. Reports of domestic violence increased by 46 per cent to almost 8,700.
