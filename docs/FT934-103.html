931231 FT 31 DEC 93 / Threat to S Wales police stations More than 50 police stations are set to close across south Wales and about 200 police and civilian jobs could be axed after the region's police authority yesterday failed to resolve a Pounds 14m budget shortfall. Leaders of three Labour-controlled county councils in Glamorgan, which partly finance the authority, blamed the government for failing to ensure a proper police service.
