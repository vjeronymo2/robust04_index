940325 FT 25 MAR 94 / Commercial property gets pounds 1bn boost Institutional investors spent just over Pounds 1bn on commercial property in the last quarter of last year, according to the Central Statistical Office, Vanessa Houlder writes. The figures underline the sharp improvement in sentiment towards commercial property, which began last year. Over 1993, annual net investment by pension funds, insurance companies, investment trusts and unit trusts increased to Pounds 791m.
