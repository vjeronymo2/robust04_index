BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] It has been announced that the public is well aware of and appreciates the fact that the Turkish Armed Forces are not the authority or organ for establishing governments. A statement to Anatolia Agency by the General Staff Press and Public Relations Department recalled that today certain newspapers carried false reports regarding political quests and new structures based on assumptions that do not correspond with the actual task of the Armed Forces. Pointing out that the public is well aware of the fact that the Armed Forces are not the authority or organ for establishing governments, as suggested by these reports, the statement remarked that the terms in office of the chief of staff and the force commanders are determined in line with the need for service within the framework of the law. The statement also asked the public not to heed any concocted reports other than announcements made by authorized bodies.
