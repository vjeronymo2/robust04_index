Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 13 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- As a result of numerous working meetings between the United States and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea the sides have reached some agreements in New York on the eve of a new year. The agreements concluded include the following as the United States agree to stop carrying out the "Team Spirit" military exercises while the North Korea to permit that control to be enforced by the UNO [UN organization] observers over its nuclear objects and start a dialogue with magate [as received] in this respect. This was said in the statement released there today by a press officer of the Ministry of External Relations of Mongolia. We are highly appreciating and welcoming the statement runs the fact that the both sides have arrived at an agreement on arranging by June, July of 1994 the third stage of negotiations at high level in order to conduct a comprehensive discussion of the nuclear problems of the Korean peninsula and consider the issue on normalizing the relationships between them. The press officer noted the significance of urgent implementing of the above mentioned agreements for the sake of regulation in the spirit of consent the nuclear problems of the Korean peninsula and complicated military and political situation and wished a success in this cause.
