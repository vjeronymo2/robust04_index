BFN [Report by Sezai Sengun: "Joint Action by PKK and ASALA" -- place names as published] [Text] Ankara--Acting on information that the Workers Party of Kurdistan [PKK] terrorists in Armenia are preparing to infiltrate into Turkey, officials have placed the security forces in Kars, Ardahan, and Igdir Provinces on a state of alert. Meanwhile, the 3d Army units have intensified Turkey's security measures along the Armenian border. According to an intelligence report submitted to the National Security Council, the PKK terrorists and the ASALA [Armenian Secret Army for the Liberation of Armenia] militants are cooperating in carrying out joint activities against Turkey. The report outlined the locations of the PKK camps in Armenia as follows: 1. Mirak, Elegiz, Reya Taze, Shengavit, Derik, Suphan, Cumusvan, Amri Taze, and Senger in Aragaz District. 2. Sorik, Hako, Tellik, and Baysiz in Talin District. 3. Ferik in Echmiadzin District. 4. Samirin in Ashtarak District. The report said the PKK terrorists train in those villages and organize armed exercises from time to time. It also said that they are strongly supported by the local people. The report stressed: "It is believed that the PKK terrorists and ASALA members will organize joint attacks against Turkey. The Kars, Ardahan, and Igdir Provinces, which have a common border with Armenia, are cautioned to take the necessary measures and maintain a state of vigilance." The report said that PKK leader Abdullah Ocalan is personally maintaining his organization's relations with Armenia and that the Armenian officials have been informed of and support the PKK activities.
