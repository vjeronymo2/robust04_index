BLACKSTONE RIVER VALLEY NATIONAL HERITAGE CORRIDOR Notice of Public Meeting The Blackstone River Valley National Heritage Corridor Commission will HOLD A PUBLIC MEETING TO solicit comments on the proposed Boundary expansion of the Blackstone River Valley National Heritage Corridor. Notice is hereby given in accordance with Section 552b of Title 5, United States Code, that this meeting will be held. The Commission was established pursuant to Public Law 99&hyph;647, in order to establish a management framework to protect, enhance and interpret the nationally significant resources in the Blackstone Valley. The meeting will be held at Smithfield Town Hall, Smithfield, RI. It will be at 7:00 PM on Thursday, July 21, 1994. The Public is invited to discuss the boundary expansion at the meeting and/or provide written comments at that time. James R. Pepper, Executive Director, Blackstone River Valley National Heritage Corridor. [FR Doc. 94&hyph;16828 Filed 7&hyph;7&hyph;94; 2:00 pm] BILLING CODE 4310&hyph;70&hyph;P
