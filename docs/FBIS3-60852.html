Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS Sharip Asuyev] [Text] Groznyy, North Caucasus December 24 -- The Chechen Republic has announced it is ready to receive the helicopter with four terrorists and hostages on board and guarantee their complete safety, if the kidnappers agree to exchange the children taken hostages in Rostov on Thursday are exchanged for any Chechen representatives, ITAR-TASS was told by first deputy minister of internal affairs of the Chechen Republic Saipudi Khasanov. He said the Chechen Republic was prepared to provide the terrorists with a fully fueled aircraft to fly to any destination they may choose. Saipudi Khasanov stressed that he was ready to go on board himself to free the children. Russian authorities negotiating with the terrorists have been notified about this proposal of the Groznyy authorities prompted by their concern about the lives of the Rostov schoolchildren.
