930826 FT 26 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Polish factory for Cadbury Schweppes Cadbury Schweppes, the confectionery and soft drinks group, is to build a chocolate and sugar confectionery factory at Wroclaw, south-west Poland, for Pounds 20m. The company said the site chosen had good access to population centres. Construction was expected to begin in 1993 with production starting in 1995. The Polish confectionery market is estimated at about 180,000 tonnes.
