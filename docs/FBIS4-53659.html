BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 21 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il recently bestowed again a benevolent solicitude upon the Pyongyang Maternity Hospital. He sent large quantities of baby's clothes of various styles for boys and girls, precious pharmaceuticals needed for the manufacture of medicines and articles to be used in ensuring medical services on a higher level. Always paying deep attention to the work of the Pyongyang Maternity Hospital, he had sent more than seven tons of wild honey and over ten tons of unshelled pine nuts on three occasions this year alone. The hospital completed in March, 1980, is a comprehensive medical service base appointed with latest equipment, which was built for women and children on the initiative and according to a plan of Comrade Kim Chong-il. The total floor space of the hospital is over 60,000 square metres. The hospital has a total of 1,500 beds, of which 600 beds are for the obstetrical section, 400 for the gynaecological section and 500 for just-born babies.
