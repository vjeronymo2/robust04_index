July 13, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Mead Corp.: The Dayton, Ohio, paper products firm said second-quarter profit dipped 15% to $62.2 million from a year ago. Revenue rose to a record $1.21 billion, up 4%. Although earnings from operations climbed 10.6% in the period, the firm said net income eased because of its restructuring program.
