Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, January 9 (ATA) -- Macedonian police confiscated some 350 copies of the Albanian review JEHONA in the border crossing between Presheve and Kumanove. JEHONA is published by NOVA MAKEDONIA Printing Press in Skopje and is distributed in Kosova and other ethnically Albanian territories. The Macedonian police had confiscated some 350 copies of JEHONA three months ago.
