Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Aleksandr Krylovich and Vladimir Taranov] [Text] Moscow January 12 TASS -- Russia welcomes the renewal of negotiations in Egypt between delegations from Israel and the Palestinian Liberation Organization. Russia is hoping for the successful outcome of the talks, which concern an agreement on the Gaza Strip and Jericho, according to Grigoriy Karasin, the director of the information and press department of the Russian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, speaking in the ministry's press centre. Karasin expressed his regret about several interruptions in the Israeli-PLO talks on specific measures to implement their declaration on principles, signed in September of 1993. "In our contacts with both Israeli and Palestinian representatives we always point out to them the necessity of concentrating maximum constructive effort and good will on overcoming differences and following the spirit and the letter of the September agreements", the Russian representative stated.
