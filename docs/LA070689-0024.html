July 6, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

PEOPLE: L.A. COUNTY

Arthur Andersen & Co. Southern California, Los Angeles, has named the following people to partnership: Ronald W. Fulks, William H. Hranchak, Brian M. Kadison and Michael J. Puntoriero. Also named principals are Masako H. Carpenter, Hwai-Tang Chen, David I. Nolte and Kyle J. Owens.
