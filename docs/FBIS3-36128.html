Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Armenian foreign minister sent letters today to Ronaldo Mota Sardenberg, the president of the UN Security Council, and Jan Eliasson, the chairman of the CSCE Minsk Group. In noting the repeated Azerbaijani rejection of the peace plans of Russia and the CSCE, the shelling of and attacks against the territories of Nagorno-Karabakh and Armenia, and the internationalization of the Karabakh conflict, the foreign minister asked the CSCE Minsk Group to condemn the political and military actions of Azerbaijan and the UN Security Council to consider and implement necessary measures to force UN members to submit to UN resolutions.
