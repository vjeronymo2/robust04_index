BFN [Excerpts] His Majesty King Husayn of Jordan arrived in Damascus at 1230 today. [passage omitted] After arriving at the People's Palace, President Hafiz al-Asad and His Majesty King Husayn held a meeting, which was attended on the Syrian side by Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam, Vice President Muhammad Zuhayr Mashariqah, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi, Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar', and Presidential Affairs Minister Wahib Fadil. On the Jordanian side, the meeting was attended by his majesty's delegation. This meeting was followed by a closed meeting between President Hafiz al-Asad and His Majesty King Husayn, interrupted by a dinner banquet given by President Hafiz al-Asad. It was attended by the two sides' delegations, as well as Rashid Akhtarini, deputy prime minister for services affairs, Dr. Muhammad Harbah, minister of interior affairs, and Dr. Muhammad Salman, the minister of information. Following the luncheon, the president and his majesty the king held a second closed meeting. Talks centered on the situation in the Arab arena, particularly in Yemen. The two sides voiced deep regret over the fighting raging there and expressed hope that the Yemeni brothers would solve problems through dialogue rather than fighting. The talks also touched on the peace process, current contacts connected with this process, and the Israeli obstacles to prevent the attainment of the goal of this process, namely, a just and comprehensive peace based on the principle of land for peace and the implementation of UN resolutions. They agreed to continue contacts and coordination among the Arab sides in the peace process to reach a comprehensive peace. They also discussed issues of common interest. His Majesty King Husayn and his delegation left Damascus at 1815 today. He was seen off at the airport by President Hafiz al-Asad.
