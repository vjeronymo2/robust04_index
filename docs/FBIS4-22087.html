BFN [Text] A republican decree was issued yesterday, referring case No. 574 for the year 1993 to the military judiciary. Al-Jihad organization members involved in the case are accused of the attempted assassination of Interior Minister Hasan al-Alfi, the assassination of a car showroom owner in Shibin al-Qanatir, and of detonating a bomb in the al-Jazirah youth center. Judicial sources have said that the case files will be handed to the military prosecution in the next few hours to begin interrogating the apprehended defendants, mainly Muhammad 'Abd-al- 'Alim Khalifah and Muhammad Rashad. The defendants will face charges for murder, attempted murder, and possession of weapons, bombs and ammunition. They face a punishment that can be anything from death, life with hard labor, to imprisonment.
