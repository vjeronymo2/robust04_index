BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow, 27 Apr -- Ivan Rybkin, chairman of the Duma, will sign tomorrow the Treaty on Public Accord on behalf of the State Duma. Only 106 deputies of the chamber voted today for the decision not to recommend the chairman of the Duma to do so. Accordingly, the majority of 449 deputies of the lower chamber of the Federation Council confirmed the power of Ivan Rybkin to sign the treaty on behalf of the Duma.
