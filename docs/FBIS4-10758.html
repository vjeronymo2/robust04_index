BFN [Bulletin issued by the Mexican National Defense Secretariat on 15 April; place not given; from the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] At approximately 0530 today, the military checkpoint established near Tuxtla Gutierrez on the road to Chicoasen was attacked with gunfire by a small group of unidentified civilians. The shots were fired from a distance of approximately 200 meters, wounding a sergeant of the signal corps. He was evacuated to the regional military hospital in Tuxtla Gutierrez where he later died. The armed attack lasted until 0620. It was repulsed by the military personnel who forced the attackers to flee. It is not known whether the attackers sustained casualties. The civilian population was not harmed.
