920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Kitty Little tumbles Pounds 0.31m into the red A pre-tax loss of Pounds 306,000 was announced by Kitty Little Group, the USM-quoted maker of fragrant gifts, air fresheners and self-selection reading glasses, for the 11 months to December 31 1991. For the 12 months to end-January 1991 there was a profit of Pounds 167,000. Mr Grahan Webster, chairman, said that on a pro rata basis sales in the fragrance division fell by 37 per cent and losses there were Pounds 285,000 (Pounds 514,000 profit). Shardan, the toilet bag manufacturer, incurred losses of Pounds 108,000 (Pounds 106,000) after provisions for slow moving stock and bad debts. However, following the acquisition of the minority interest in Magnivision - now KL Eyecare - and the stock and goodwill of Ready Readers, both companies had reported profits. Turnover amounted to Pounds 4.23m (Pounds 6.8m). Net interest payable fell to Pounds 224,000 (Pounds 369,000) and the company is concentrating on reducing bank borrowings further by keeping a close control of working capital requirements. Losses per share amounted to 1.96p (0.19p earnings) and there is no dividend (0.575p).
