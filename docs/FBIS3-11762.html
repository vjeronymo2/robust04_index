Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Jaime Guerrero; from the "Good Morning from Mexico" newscast] [Text] A footnote on this day of national mourning: President Salinas de Gortari received [Canadian] Prime Minister Chretien. The day is very hot, and at a closed-door meeting Chretien and Salinas talked about matters related to NAFTA. They highlighted the benefits it represents for Mexico and Canada. It is a meeting where they discussed matters of bilateral interest. The Mexican president and the Canadian prime minister talked about common goals for the benefit of their nations. The president's activities continued. Shortly after noon, Salinas met with former British Prime Minister Thatcher in private. Mrs. Thatcher waved at reporters following one and a half hours of talks.
