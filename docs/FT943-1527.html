940923 FT 23 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Cluff Resources losses at Pounds 0.58m Cluff Resources, the minerals and oil and gas exploration group, reported increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 583,000 for the first half of 1994. Losses last time were Pounds 343,000. Increased turnover of Pounds 12.2m (Pounds 6.67m) reflected the higher gold production and gold price together with the acquisition of Aberfoyle group in the second half of 1993. Losses per share amounted to 0.91p (1p).
