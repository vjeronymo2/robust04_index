921006 FT 06 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Cooper Clarke lower at Pounds 125,000 Cooper Clarke Group, the Bolton-based builders' merchant, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 125,000 for the first six months of 1992 compared with Pounds 203,000 in the half year to October 31 1991. With no sign of an improvement in the construction sector, the USM-quoted company has again passed its interim dividend. Turnover increased to Pounds 11.3m (Pounds 10.5m) but pressure on margins continued. The company said profits were struck after bad debts of Pounds 129,000. Earnings per share were 2.2p (3.7p).
