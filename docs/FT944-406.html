941229 FT 29 DEC 94 / Scottish marble carves a niche Marble from Ledmore in Sutherland is to be exported to a company in Carrara in Italy, the area where sculptors such as Michelangelo and Canova found their raw material. Anglo Pacific Resources, owner of the Ledmore quarry, said a marble polishing company from Carrara would take 22-tonne blocks of pre-Cambrian blue-grey marble and prepare it for sale on international markets.
