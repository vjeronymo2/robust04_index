BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 15 (KCNA) -- O Chin-u, member of the presidium of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea and minister of People's Armed Forces, met and had a friendly conversation with a Vietnamese high-level military delegation led by General Doan Khue, Politburo member of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Vietnam and minister of National Defence, which paid a courtesy call on him Tuesday at the Mansudae Assembly Hall. Present were Vice Marshal of the Korean People's Army Kim Kwang- chin, Col. Gen. Pak Chae-kyong and Vietnamese Ambassador to Korea Duong Chinh Thuc.
