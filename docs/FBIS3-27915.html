Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Feb. 1 (YONHAP) -- Some Army troops and armored personnel carriers will pass through downtown Seoul on Wednesday and Saturday for a field training, a Defense Ministry spokesman said Tuesday. The route will be Namhyondong-isu crossroad-Songsu Great Bridge- Kunja Bridge-Wollung Bridge-Saenmal and the time will be from 5 a.m. to 6 a.m. Wednesday and from 1 a.m. to 2 a.m. Saturday. The spokesman called on nearby residents not to be frightened by the Army's movements at night.
