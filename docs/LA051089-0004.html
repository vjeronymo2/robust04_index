May 10, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"Bravo for L.A. It's a stellar group with a breathtaking level of achievement by each artist. . . . MOCA and the L.A. basin are very, very lucky to receive them."

-- Jack Cowart, curator of 20th Century Art at the National Gallery of Art, on the Museum of Contemporary Art's receipt of at least $60 million worth of donated art works.
