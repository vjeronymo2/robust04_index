Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Ministry of Youth and Sports and all the administrators of the national movement for youth and sports announced today with great sorrow and deep regret the assassination this morning of Mr. Yahia Bouguetaya, deputy chairman of the Algerian Judo Federation and chairman of Mascara sports club. The Ministry calls on all officials and organizers of sports events throughout the national territory to mark one minute of silence on Thursday and Friday. The Ettahaddi party announced today that a party member was killed in Constantine. The same source added that Mr Touali Mohamed who was 32-years old was kidnapped from his home last night before he was found assassinated with a sharp weapon.
