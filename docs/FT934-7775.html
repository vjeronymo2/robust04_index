931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / International Company News: Newbridge in fourfold leap NEWBRIDGE Networks, the Ottawa-based specialised telecommunications equipment maker, has reported a fourfold profits increase for the first half ended October 30, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. First-half profit was CDollars 69.1m (USDollars 52.30m), or 87 cents a share, up from CDollars 16.8m, or 24 cents, a share a year earlier, on revenues of CDollars 243m against CDollars 123.5m. Second-quarter profit was CDollars 37.1m or 47 cents a share against CDollars 10.6m or 15 cents a share on revenues of CDollars 128m against CDollars 67.4m.
