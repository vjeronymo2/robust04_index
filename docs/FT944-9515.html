941115 FT 15 NOV 94 / International Company News: Options at Warburg Liffe has announced that SG Warburg has been approved to make a market in equity options, bringing to 11 the total number of market makers at the exchange, writes Richard Lapper. Warburg will today begin making a market in 36 equity options. It will operate as an 'assigned market maker', in which it is committed to a continual presence in the pit for the purpose of making and updating two-way prices in 16 equity options.
