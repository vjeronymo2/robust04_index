931018 FT 18 OCT 93 / People: Spring Ram John Procter, formerly a regional md at McCarthy & Stone and before that group md of G-Plan, has been appointed chief executive of Stag Furniture Holdings, part of SPRING RAM, on the retirement of George Ella, who will become a non-executive director of Stag and of Regency Doors, another Spring Ram subsidiary. Roger Blaney, Stag's marketing director, has resigned.
