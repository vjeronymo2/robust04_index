BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anatoliy Yurkin] [Text] Moscow, 8 Apr -- The Air Defense Troops will continue to fulfill the tasks before them, remaining an arm of Russia's Armed Forces, a spokesmen at the Russian Ministry of Defense told the ITAR-TASS correspondent, denying press reports about the disbanding of the Air Defense Troops. It emerges from the explanation that structural reorganization is nearing completion in the Air Defense Troops. The number of personnel has been cut here, as in other arms of the Russian Armed Forces.
