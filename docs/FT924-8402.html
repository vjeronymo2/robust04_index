921111 FT 11 NOV 92 / Observer: Spin-doctored President George Bush's lips may not be worth reading any more, but one well-worn Bushism has just arrived in the UK. His inimitable way of denying any knowledge of the Irangate arms-for-hostages deal has seeped into the patter of the British Ministry of Defence. A civil servant, when asked whether there would be a defence review after Thursday's Autumn Statement, replied: 'I'm sorry, but I'm out of the loop on that one.'
