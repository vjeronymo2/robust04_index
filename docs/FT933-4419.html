930909 FT 09 SEP 93 / Two killed as Haiti mayor returns for reinstatement TWO people were killed and several wounded when a ceremony to return the capital's pro-democracy mayor to his post sparked shooting and other violence yesterday, independent radio stations said, Reuter reports from Port-au-Prince. There was no immediate confirmation of the reports. Six days ago a new government took over with the aim of bringing exiled President Jean-Bertrand Aristide back to power next month. Scores of City Hall employees opposed to President Aristide used rocks and staves to attack departing cars crammed with government ministers and foreign diplomats at the end of a ceremony formally reinstating Mayor Evans Paul.
