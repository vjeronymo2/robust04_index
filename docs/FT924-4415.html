921202 FT 02 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Millwall losses reduced by Pounds 1m Millwall Holdings, which runs the First Division football club and other activities, cut its loss from Pounds 2.67m to Pounds 1.65m in the year ended May 31 1992. Of the loss, Pounds 2.2m related to Tavern Leisure, a subsidiary disposed of during the year for a net consideration of Pounds 2.24m. An extraordinary loss of Pounds 1.99m arose on the disposal after deducting goodwill previously written off. Turnover in 1991-92 came to Pounds 3.38m (Pounds 8.77m) and led to an operating loss of Pounds 1.95m (Pounds 2.17m). Losses per share were 1.46p (2.36p).
