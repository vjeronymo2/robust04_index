920327 FT 27 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Arnotts moves ahead to IPounds 4.22m Arnotts, the Dublin-based department store operator, reported pre-tax profits up 10 per cent from IPounds 3.84m to IPounds 4.22m (Pounds 3.9m) for the year to January 31. Turnover was IPounds 44.1m, against IPounds 41.6m. Earnings per share were 14.1p (12.2p) and the final dividend is raised to 5.25p for a total of 7.5p (7p).
