Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Veysel Atasoy, energy and natural resources minister, has stated that natural gas usage will be restored without any restrictions as of this evening. Concluding his contacts in Bulgaria and Ukraine regarding natural gas usage, Atasoy returned to Turkey yesterday. Atasoy explained that the shortages in the natural gas delivery to Turkey have been eliminated after the two-day conflict between Russia and Ukraine was resolved following discussions regarding this issue. Atasoy further stated that agreement was reached during his meeting with the chairman of the Bulgarian Committee on Power Supplies that Turkey will receive half of the natural gas delivered to Bulgaria. During his contacts in Kiev, Atasoy explained, he was assured of a continuous natural gas delivery as of 1600 today after the conflict between Russia and Ukraine were resolved.
