Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] At least 12 people were killed during a war between two gangs for the control of drug trafficking at the Acari and Jorge Turco shantytowns before daybreak yesterday. Two pregnant women and two minors were among the dead. A morning of terror began in Acari. Some 30 traffickers headed by (Parazao), armed with machine guns and grenades, invaded the Acari shantytown and assassinated four individuals at point blank range. (Parazao's) men then moved to the Jorge Turco shantytown, in Rocha Miranda. Eight more were killed during the ensuing clash.
