930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Templeton Emerging Templeton Emerging Markets Investment Trust is hoping to raise Pounds 85m via a placing of shares with warrants attached. The trust specialises in small stock markets in areas such as south-east Asia and Latin America. The trust has frequently traded at a premium to net assets, an indication that demand for shares exceeds supply. Existing shareholders have the right to subscribe for new shares on the basis of one offer unit for every three existing shares. The offer units, priced at 100p, will be converted into ordinary shares using a formula based on net asset value.
