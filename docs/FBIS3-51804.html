Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Evening Information Review" newscast] [Text] Cuban Vice President Carlos Lage will visit Brazil from 2 to 7 February to strengthen relations between Cuba and the South American country. Lage, who will visit Brasilia, Rio de Janeiro, and Sao Paulo, will be received by President Itamar Franco and will hold meetings with the Brazilian ministers of health, foreign policy, and finance. He will also meet with Brazilian businessmen, including the head of the Souza-Cruz Commission, to analyze the possibility of establishing joint investments in the tobacco sector.
