931111 FT 11 NOV 93 / Observer: Lingua obscura Hard to believe that Unigate's chief executive, Ross Buckland, hails from Australia, supposedly the home of blunt, no-nonsense sorts. Talking about his company's half-year results yesterday, he admitted that one activity had 'negatively impacted profitability'. Did this mean that it had made a loss, he was asked. 'Yes,' said Buckland, adding that he thought he 'had worded it rather well . . .'.
