930127 FT 27 JAN 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa GOLD shares rallied sharply towards the close as the price of bullion rose above Dollars 330 an ounce. The gold index put on 30, or 3.7 per cent, at 839, while industrials recovered some earlier losses to close a net 10 down at 4,508. The overall index gained 6 at 3,405. Vaal Reefs added R6 at R149 and Kloof rose R1.25 to R27.
