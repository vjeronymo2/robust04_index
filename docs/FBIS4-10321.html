BFN [Excerpt] (Jorge Tenas Rueda), Dominican Republic under secretary of education, fine arts, and public worship, arrived today in the country. Cuban Minister of Higher Education Fernando Vecino Alegret welcomed the Dominican delegation, which also includes Dr. Roberto Reyes, rector of the Pedro Enriquez Urena University, and Dominican writer Manuel Rueda Gonzalez, national literary award winner. They will attend the ceremonies Cuba has organized to commemorate the centennial of the birthday of Dominican-Cuban writer and researcher Camila Enriquez Urena. [passage omitted]
