October 14, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

'DEVELOPMENT PERILS WILDLIFE, STUDY SAYS'

In "Development Perils Wildlife, Study Says" (Oct. 7), Tony Moiso of the Mission Viejo Co. says "this is not Yellowstone" to justify his opposition to a 500-foot setback from oak-studded Arroyo Trabuco.

However, deterioration in an urban forest within 500 feet of development can be observed less than a year after a project is completed, says arborist Alden Kelley of the Orange County Tree Society.

For a resource of Yellowstone's stature, setbacks are measured in miles, not feet.

CONSTANCE SPENGER

Fullerton
