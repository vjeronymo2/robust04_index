September 7, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

ONTARIO POLICE ARREST 2 PAROLEES IN MURDER, KIDNAPINGS, RAPES

Police arrested two parolees Thursday in connection with the rape and murder last weekend of a 15-year-old girl and the abduction, rapes and stabbings of two other women.

Alfredo Rolando Prieto, 24, was arrested about 7 p.m. in an apartment building on the 1300 block of West "E" Street in Ontario, said Ontario Police Detective Mike Cormican.

Police arrested Ricardo Estrada, 19, at the 400 block of South Campus Avenue, about six blocks from the site where Yvette Woodruff, 15, was killed early Sunday, Cormican said.

Woodruff and the two other women, a mother and her daughter, were abducted from their Ontario home about 2:15 a.m. Sunday by two suspects who looted the residence, police said.

They were abandoned in an industrial area of Ontario, where the teen-ager was shot to death and the other two stabbed. All three were raped.

Both men were held on suspicion of one count of murder, three of rape, three of kidnaping and two of attempted murder. They are being held without bail at San Bernardino County Jail.
