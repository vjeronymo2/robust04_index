BFN [Unattributed report: "Belarusian-Iranian Trade Agreement Was Signed"] [Text] As BELAPAN has been told at the Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations, Uladzimir Radkevich, minister of foreign economic relations, signed a trade agreement between the Governments of Belarus and Iran. The agreement outlines the introduction of most favored nation status for both countries in their trade relations. The document, in particular, postulates the development of mutual investments, setting up of joint ventures, exchange of delegations of businessmen and entrepreneurs, and an exchange of information.
