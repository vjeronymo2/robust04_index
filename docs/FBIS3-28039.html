Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 3 (KCNA) -- Chairman of the Workers' Party of Turkey Dogu Ferincek, when he received the chief of the Korean general mission in France on January 28, said that the Workers' Party [WPK] of Korea was victoriously advancing socialist construction, pushing aside all machinations of the imperialists because it has achieved the singlehearted unity of the leader, the party and the masses under the wise guidance of the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. "Loyalty to the socialist cause and resolute anti-imperialist position maintained by the WPK gives a great encouragement to the communists of the world who are struggling for socialism," he stressed, and went on: "The Workers' Party of Turkey [WPT] deems it great happiness to have friendly relations with the powerful WPK, greatest in the world. It will actively strive to learn from the idea and theory of Comrade Kim Il-song and Comrade Kim Chong-il on revolutionary party building." He said he regards it as the revolutionary duty of the WPT to support the struggle of the Korean people for the independent and peaceful reunification of the country. He declared his party would conduct a powerful solidarity movement for supporting the struggle of the Korean people to achieve the reunification of the country in the 1990s in accordance with the ten-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation for the country's reunification advanced by the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song.
