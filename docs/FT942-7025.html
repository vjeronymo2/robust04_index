940527 FT 27 MAY 94 / Collapsed group's customers win aid Customers of the former investment advisory company Pentre-Vere Limited will be entitled to apply for help from the Investors Compensation Scheme, the scheme's board said yesterday. The scheme acts as a safety net for customers of investment companies which cannot pay money or return investments owed to clients. The maximum compensation payment is Pounds 48,000. In the next few weeks, the scheme will be contacting all those customers of the company - which is now in liquidation - who had previously asked whether compensation is available. The compensation scheme is also inviting any other former customers of the company to get in touch. The scheme can be contacted on 071 628 8820.
