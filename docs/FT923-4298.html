920908 FT 08 SEP 92 / Zoo shelves closure plans LONDON ZOO is shelving its plan to close at the end of this month. The zoo said it was set to break even on running costs in the current financial year, allowing the council of the Zoological Society of London to drop its decision to close. A significant increase in visitors had generated an extra Pounds 500,000 in revenue since the society's announcement in June that the zoo would have to close by the end of this month, the zoo said.
