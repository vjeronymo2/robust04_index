BFN [Text] Tskhinvali 20 [dateline as received] -- Peacekeeping forces stationed in the Tskhinvali region have undergone changes. While up to 1,000 Russian airborne assault troops were stationed in the region in 1992, only 523 soldiers of a motor rifle battalion of the Russian Armed Forces are involved in the peacekeeping operations there at the moment. The commanders of the Russian peacekeeping forces have said that they cannot stay in the region permanently. Therefore, Ossetians and Georgians should find a common language promptly to ensure a political settlement the current conflict.
