930910 FT 10 SEP 93 / International Company News: Record earnings for Campbell Soup CAMPBELL Soup, the US food products group, said yesterday it had achieved record earnings and sales in the fourth quarter, buoyed by a strong contribution from overseas markets. Earnings and revenues for the full year also reached record levels for the fourth consecutive time. The results highlight Campbell's recent efforts to reorganise its global operations, as well as its renewed emphasis on its core soup, biscuits and bakery businesses. Net earnings in the quarter to August 1 were 11 per cent higher at Dollars 122m, or 48 cents a share, up from Dollars 109m, or 44 cents. Revenues rose 7 per cent from Dollars 1.4bn to Dollars 1.5bn. Before the cumulative effect of accounting changes, earnings in the 1993 quarter were 15 per cent up. For the full year, net earnings before special charges rose by almost 17 per cent to Dollars 574m, or Dollars 2.28 a share.
