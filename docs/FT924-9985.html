921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Embassy Property reduces deficit Embassy Property Group reported pre-tax losses for the year to March 31 of Pounds 7.8m compared with Pounds 9.49m, after a fall from Pounds 6.64m to Pounds 3.99m in exceptional items. Turnover dipped from Pounds 15.5m to Pounds 13.4m and losses per share were down from 121.9p to 90.6p. The company said the reduction of group debt by the Pounds 2.78m raised in September's placing would improve the balance sheet.
