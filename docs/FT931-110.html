930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / UK Company News: EW Fact Profits of EW Fact, a USM-quoted provider of classes and publication of texts for students preparing for accountancy courses, slipped from Pounds 1.01m to Pounds 947,000 pre-tax for the year to end-December. Turnover of Pounds 4.67m compared with Pounds 4.59m. Earnings amounted to 8.77p (10.53p) and a second interim dividend of 2.71p in lieu of a final, to beat Budget changes on ACT, makes a same-again 4.31p total.
