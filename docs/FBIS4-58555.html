BFN [Text] Pakistan has said that India is vitiating the mutual atmosphere by harping upon the baseless and malicious allegations of Pakistan's involvement in Kashmir and some other parts of India. A Foreign Office spokesman commenting on the recent propaganda campaign against Pakistan launched by Indian Government said that Indian Government is trying to divert attention from its own failures and brutalities in held Kashmir. The Muslim ambassadors based in New Delhi after visiting the occupied Kashmir have observed that gross violation of human rights are taking place in the valley and the Kashmiris hate India. One of the members of the delegation -- the Iranian ambassador, Mr. ('Ali Raza Shaykh Ata) -- in a statement has said that the program presented to the delegation in New Delhi was never implemented. He said the members of the delegation were not allowed to visit Hazratbal shrine, Sopor and Bijbiara. He said the meeting with the leaders of Hurriet Conference was not arranged and we were not allowed even to meet and talk with the common people in the valley.
