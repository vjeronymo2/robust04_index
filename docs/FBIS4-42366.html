BFN [Excerpt] General Jean Cot, the commander of the blue helmets in the former Yugoslavia between July 1993 and March 1994, has decided to leave the Army, where his grade of Army general (five stars), the highest military rank, did not give him much more headroom. The departure of the 60-year-old General Cot, who still had one year of service to complete before reaching the age limit, was confirmed at yesterday's cabinet meeting. [passage omitted]
