930212 FT 12 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Asset value ahead at Finsbury Smaller Finsbury Smaller Companies Trust had a net asset value per share of 116.7p at December 31, an 11.4 per cent increase on the 104.8p of a year earlier. At January 31 1993 it was 131.8p. Earnings were 3p (2.9p) per share. The proposed final dividend is 2p for a total of 3p (2.9p).
