930429 FT 29 APR 93 / UK Company News: Schroder Korea pays first dividend Schroder Korea Fund, a London-listed investment trust, had a net asset value of Dollars 8.75 (568p) at December 31. The trust, formed ahead of the opening of the Korean stock market to foreign investors on January 3 1992, had attributable revenue of Dollars 119,000 for the period from October 10 1991 - the date of incorporation - to end-December. Earnings emerged at 2.38 cents and a maiden dividend of 2 cents is recommended.
