August 18, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CONTROVERSY IS ONE THING, BUT GIVE FANS SOME GOLF

Trying to watch golf on ABC during the PGA was futile.

The most important aspect of this tournament to ABC was obviously the controversy about blacks not belonging to country clubs. I have never been so sick of a subject. They beat it to death Friday night instead of showing the tournament; they beat it to death Saturday and Sunday. It would be nice to be able to watch some of the tournament.

JAMES ASHBY

Palos Verdes Estates
