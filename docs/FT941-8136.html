940222 FT 22 FEB 94 / UK Company News: City Merchants High Yield up 38% City Merchants High Yield Trust saw its net asset value per share rise by 38 per cent - from 105.7p to 146.2p - over the 12 months to end-December. Net revenue improved from Pounds 1.46m to Pounds 1.55m. Earnings per share, on capital increased by last summer's successful placing, dipped to 9.746p (9.02p). A recommended final dividend of 2.4p brings the total distribution for the year to 8.4p.
