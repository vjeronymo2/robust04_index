920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Reflex Investments warns of deficit Reflex Investments, the Dublin-based computer leasing group, warned that it would report a loss for the year to end-April. The company said trading conditions in the second half of the year had remained difficult. It said it was taking all steps available to return the group to profitability. In the 1990-91 financial year Reflex made a pre-tax profit of IPounds 2.51m.
