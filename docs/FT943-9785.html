940809 FT 09 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Equifax seeks recommendation Equifax, the US credit information business, said yesterday that it continued to seek the recommendation for its increased cash offer of 600p per share for UAPT-Infolink, the UK credit reference company. In yesterday's Financial Times an announcement published spelling out the cash offer made by Barings on behalf of Equifax said that it was recommended. UAPT-Infolink said yesterday that shareholders should be aware that this offer has not been recommended by the UAPT board.
