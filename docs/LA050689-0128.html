May 6, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

BANK ROBBER WITH NOTE GETS CASH

A robber who presented a note demanding money to a teller at a Tustin bank made off with an unknown amount of cash Friday, police said.

The robbery was reported at 2:50 p.m. at the El Camino Bank at 300 El Camino Real.

Police said a man described as white, about 30 years old, 5 foot 6, 145 pounds and wearing a plaid shirt entered the bank and gave a teller a demand note. He displayed no weapon.

Police said that after the teller handed over the cash, the robber fled the bank. FBI officials and Tustin police are investigating.
