BFN [Excerpt] Eighty paracommandos are landing at Melsbroek military airport outside Brussels this morning. They left from Nairobi in Kenya last night, following the evacuation operation of Belgians and other Westerners from Rwanda nearly three weeks ago. Several hundreds of Belgian troops are still stationed at the military camp at the Nairobi airport. The Defense Ministry in Brussels says they will all be repatriated. Earlier it was expected that the troops would stay in Nairobi to intervene if necessary in Burundi, south of Rwanda where ethnic violence has claimed thousands of lives in the past few months.
