930528 FT 28 MAY 93 / Technology: High-tech buyers move into focus - Worth Watching Marketing teams, eager to direct accurately their high-technology product to the right group, are being helped by New York consultancy Backer Spielvogel Bates, which has drawn up a profile of the 'techthusiast' - technology enthusiasts who are the first to buy new products. According to the researchers there are 37m techthusiasts in the US alone, with an average age of 38.2 years. They read more newspapers and magazines, are prosperous, with a median household income of Dollars 56,500 (Pounds 36,700), and are spread throughout the US, although many congregate around leading universities and technology companies. The analysis is part of BSB Projections 2002, issued by the Media Research & Technology Department of BSB. BSB: US, 212 297 7000.
