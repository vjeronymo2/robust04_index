Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Tallinn, Mar 10, BNS -- Estonian parliament on Wednesday [9 March] adopted the law on service in the defense forces. The law regulates registration and call-up of citizens, deferment of call-up, exemption from conscription, beginning and end of service, service on a contractual basis, service in the reserve, alternative service, and the rights and obligations of conscripts.
