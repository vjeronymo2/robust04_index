Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Supported by tanks and other armored vehicles, the Azerbaijani troops launched an attack towards Fizuli in the southeastern sector of Karabakh war front at 0515 this morning. They managed to advance to some distance. The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] Defense Army press service reported that the fierce fighting was still continuing at this front at noon. The NKR Defense Army command has ordered its troops to take necessary measures to neutralize the enemy's attacks and retake their former positions. The NKR Foreign Ministry has stated that all the responsibility for the consequences of such actions fully rests with the Azerbaijani side, which is methodically violating the cease-fire agreement achieved through the mediatory efforts of the Russian Defense Ministry. According to information received this evening, thanks to operations undertaken by the NKR Army, the Karabakh subunits first managed to halt the enemy's advance, and then launched a counterattack in the afternoon and threw the enemy back to his initial positions. The NKR Defense Army headquarters reported that both sides suffered loses during the battles. According to preliminary reports, four Karabakh defenders were killed and 15 injured. The Azerbaijani side lost 70 soldiers and officers killed. Three enemy armored vehicles were destroyed. The same source reported at 1800 today that the military operations are still continuing at that sector of the war front. The Armenian Defense Ministry press service reported that the situation on the Armenian borders have remained calm since last night.
