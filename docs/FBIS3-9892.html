Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Commander in Chief Fidel Castro has met with representatives of the Cypriot Chamber of Commerce headed by its president, Alexis Galanos, who is also president of the Cypriot Congress' Cyprus-Cuba Friendship Group. The Cypriot delegation that is visiting our country at the invitation of the National Assembly of the People's Government [ANPP] held a lengthy meeting with the commander in chief, exchanging opinions and information on various international issues as well as on the respective situations in Cuba and Cyprus. Galanos took advantage of this opportunity to say that to the extent that his country has always enjoyed the firm support of Cuba during the most difficult times, the people of Cyprus now wish to express their solidarity with the Cuban struggle to defend its revolution, through their presence in the country. In addition to the Cypriot deputies, (Georgios Paraguiotides), Cypriot consul to Cuba, and ANPP President Ricardo Alarcon de Quesada were also present.
