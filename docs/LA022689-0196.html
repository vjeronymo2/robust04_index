February 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Sigrid Wolf of Austria won a World Cup super-G race at Steamboat, Colo., where 21 skiers failed to finish, most of them unable to negotiate a treacherous jump near the top of the course.

Wolf was timed in 1:18.97, beating teammate Anita Wachter by .24 of a second. Michela Figini of Switzerland was third in 1:19.26.
