921215 FT 15 DEC 92 / International Company News: KLM expecting no lift next year KLM, the Dutch airline, faces a difficult 1993, on top of the loss already forecast for this fiscal year, according to Mr Pieter Bouw, chairman, writing in a staff magazine, Reuter reports from Amsterdam. He blamed fare wars on important routes, the strength of the guilder and the impact of recession on traffic growth for his bleak forecast.
