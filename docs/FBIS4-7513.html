BFN [Text] Dr. Franjo Tudjman, president of the Republic of Croatia, today received in the Presidential Palace [in Zagreb] General John Shalikashvili, chairman of the joint chiefs of staff of the United States, and Madeleine Albright, U.S. permanent representative at the UN. The talks were also attended on the U.S. side by the U.S. Ambassador in Croatia Peter Galbraith, and Charles Redman, the special envoy of the U.S. President. The Croatian delegation comprises Dr. Mate Granic, Dr. Jure Radic, Gojko Susak [defense minister], Hrvoje Sarinic [the Croatian president's national security adviser], Janko Bobetko, and Zvonimir (Crnjenko). Before his meeting with President Tudjman, Gen. Shalikashvili had a meeting at the UN base in Pleso [near Zagreb airport] with UNPROFOR [UN Protection Force] Commander in Chief Gen. Bertrand de Lapresle, and he also visited the U.S. military hospital.
