Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [INTERFAX report: "Gamsakhurdia's Body Is in a Village Controlled by the Zviadists"] [Text] The body of Georgian ex-president Zviad Gamsakhurdia is in the village of Dzhikhashkari in Tsalendzhikhskiy Rayon in Western Georgia. This was stated at the Georgian Internal Affairs Ministry press center. According to a staffer of the press center, the village is under the control of armed Zviadists who are demanding that international experts come. Z. Gamsakhurdia died on the night of 31 December-1 January in a settlement in Western Georgia. It is believed that he committed suicide.
