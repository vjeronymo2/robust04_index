BFN [Text] Chisinau, BASAPRESS, 5/5/1994 -- President Mircea Snegur received today, 5 May, the accreditation letters from the Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary Ambassador of the Islamic Republic of Pakistan in Moldova Nawabzada Mahbob Ali Khan [name as received], states a Presidential Press Service communique. During the meeting Snegur said that Moldova is ready to collaborate with Pakistan in pursuit of long lasting world peace. Both agreed that an efficient collaboration between the two states within international organizations would be welcome and recalled that Pakistan was one of the first governments to recognize Moldova. "Our aim is to integrate within the European structures as quickly as possible, but Asia is not without importance for Moldova," said Snegur. Nawabzada Mahbob Ali Khan began activity in 1973 within the Pakistan Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA). He performed a number of diplomatic functions at home and afterwards at Pakistan's embassies in Ankara (1979-80), London (1984-87), and Dhaka (1987-90). He then worked as chief of MFA. Now he is Pakistani Ambassador in Romania.
