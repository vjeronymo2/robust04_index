940323 FT 23 MAR 94 / UK Company News: UK Safety at Pounds 1.6m UK Safety Group, the protective footwear maker which obtained a listing in May last year following a reverse takeover of Television South West Holdings, returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.6m for the year to end-December. The results include the trading activities of UK Safety for the eight months following the reverse takeover. The directors said profits of Pounds 3.65m shown for 1992 refer to TSW and valid comparisons cannot be made. Turnover of continuing activities totalled Pounds 21.12m. Earnings emerged at 5.7p and a final dividend of 1.56p makes a 2.6p total.
