BFN [Text] The body of a woman who appeared to be a foreigner and was accused of stealing children was found yesterday along the banks of the Cabus River in Malacatan Municipality, San Marcos Department. The body showed signs of bruises and machete wounds. The body of the woman, who was described as being about 40 years old and 1.7 meters tall, was found by passersby who immediately notified police. The regional judge and National Police arrived at the scene and determined that the woman had been murdered several days earlier. Because the body was already decomposing, they ordered an immediate burial. Her identity remains unknown. Police report that an investigation has begun and that four individuals have confessed to killing the woman, who apparently was a foreigner. Those arrested were identified as Hector Perez Vicente, 21; Valentin Santos, 27; Jesus Martin, 20; and Roberto Perez. They confessed to committing the crime because the woman was allegedly involved in the theft of children at El Silencio ranch, San Marcos Department.
