Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The sixth meeting of the eighth provincial people's congress standing committee ended in Kunming this morning. Provincial people's congress Chairman Yin Jun presided over the session. The meeting ratified the draft namelist for members of the presidium, as well as the secretary general for the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress, and the draft agenda for this second session, to be submitted to the preparatory meeting for the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress for approval. In principle, the meeting ratified the draft work report of the Yunnan Provincial People's Congress Standing Committee; authorized Bao Yongkang, vice chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee and its secretary general, to report to the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress; ratified the report on deputies' qualifications submitted by the Credentials Committee of the Eighth Yunnan Provincial People's Congress Standing Committee; decided on the namelist of participants who will attend the second session of the eighth provincial people's congress without the right to vote; ratified the decision of the Yunnan Provincial People's Congress Standing Committee on abolishing Xuanwei County and changing it to a city; and decided on the appointment of (Feng Xiankun) and Liu Ping as deputy secretaries general of the provincial people's congress standing committee. Vice chairmen of the provincial people's congress standing committee -- including Bao Yongkang, Yang Yitang, Yang Ming, Dao Guodong, Li Shuji, Bai Zuoguang, and Mai Ciqiu -- attended the meeting. Vice Governor Yang Jianqiang, and Wu Guangwen, deputy chief procurator of the provincial people's procuratorate, also were present at the meeting.
