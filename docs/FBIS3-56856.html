Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] We have just received the following urgent report from ITAR-TASS: The Russian President Boris Yeltsin, has signed an edict on government changes. This was announced at a news conference today by Viktor Chernomyrdin, chairman of the Council of Ministers. Oleg Soskovets has been appointed first deputy prime minister. Anatoliy Chubays, Aleksandr Zaveryukha, and Yuriy Yarov have been appointed deputy prime ministers. Aleksandr Shokhin becomes minister of economics. Viktor Khlystun will be minister of agriculture and food. Sergey Shakhray is to be minister of nationalities affairs and regional policy. Boris Fedorov was asked to head the Finance Ministry, but so far he has not replied, Chernomyrdin added. That was an ITAR-TASS report on a news conference at which Chernomyrdin announced the Russian Government reshuffle.
