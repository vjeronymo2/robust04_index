930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Market falls cut IFG Upheavals in currency and interest rate markets contributed to 1992 pre-tax profits falling by 39 per cent from IPounds 1.09m to IPounds 663,000 (Pounds 656,000) at IFG Group, the Dublin-based financial services company. The company estimated that the resulting loss of business, extra interest charges and currency translation losses cut profits by more than IPounds 300,000. Turnover was IPounds 4.96m (IPounds 4.85m). The pre-tax figure was struck after exceptional charges of IPounds 124,000 (IPounds 237,000). Earnings were 1.47p (3p) and a nominal single final dividend of 0.25p (1.05p) is proposed.
