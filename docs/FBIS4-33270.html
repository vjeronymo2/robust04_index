CSO [Editorial Report] Santa Cruz EL MUNDO in Spanish on 2 May 94 on page 3 reports that Cuba is strengthening scientific and cultural ties with Bolivia through university programs and tourism. Dr. Jesus Castro, international relations director of the Jose Antonio Echeverria Politechnic Institute, said the Ideal Tours Agency of La Paz is opening an agency in Santa Cruz which will promote tours to Havana. He also announced that professors from universities in Havana will be teaching summer courses in humanities, technical sciences, and medicine at Bolivian universities.
