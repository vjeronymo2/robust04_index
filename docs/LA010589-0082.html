January 5, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: SAN MARINO;

SCHOOL BOARD PRESIDENT

David Destino, a lawyer, has been elected president of the San Marino Board of Education. Other officers who were elected at a special meeting of the board in December are Debby Bowes, vice president, and Mary Snaer, clerk. Destino succeeds Selma Sax, who was board president for two years.
