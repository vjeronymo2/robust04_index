920813 FT 13 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Buyer found for two Rank hotels Rank Organisation has found a buyer for two of the 22 hotels it put up for sale last April. The two, both in Scotland, are among the cheapest of the hotels being offered for sale. Morton Properties of Dundee is to buy the Golf View Hotel in Nairn and the Royal Golf Hotel in Dornoch. The selling price of the hotels was not disclosed. Last May, however, Rank asked for offers in excess of Pounds 950,000 for the Nairn hotel and Pounds 700,000 for the Dornoch property.
