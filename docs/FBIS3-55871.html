Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Zaur Kadymbekov report: "Will PRAVDA Reach Baku?"] [Text] Baku -- Confusion over mutual transactions, the lack of a sufficient number of Russian rubles in Azerbaijan, and the impossibility of money transfers to Russia have introduced complete chaos into financial relations between the two countries -- which has not been slow to affect subscriptions to Russian publications, their delivery, and their printing in the republic. Meanwhile, according to the information of the local Ministry of Communications, subscriptions to them have been taken out in Azerbaijan to the value of 150 million rubles. Citing the same old reasons, the leadership of the national bank has proposed returning the money to the subscribers. But Nazim Novruzov, chief of the Azerbaijani Press Distribution Association, has declared that possibilities have nonetheless been found to preserve four Russian newspapers for the readers: PRAVDA, IZVESTIYA, KOMSOMOLSKAYA PRAVDA, and ARGUMENTY I FAKTY.
