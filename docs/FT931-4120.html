930312 FT 12 MAR 93 / People: Insurance moves Roger Lawson has been appointed md of the employee benefits division, Ralph Bevis md of the personal financial planning division, and David Emptage md of the international financial services operation, at BAIN CLARKSON Financial Services. William Gloyn, formerly vice-president of insurance at Olympia & York Canary Wharf, has been appointed md of Bain Clarkson's commercial property services division.
