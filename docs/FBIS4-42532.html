BFN [Excerpt] According to the latest extensive poll from Statistics Sweden, the Social Democrats still have a big lead in front of the government parties. The Social Democrats will get 50.1 percent, the Party of the Left 3.9 percent, the Moderates 21.8 percent, the Liberal Party 6.7 percent, the Center 7.9 percent, Christian Democratic Party 4.4 percent, New Democracy 2.3 percent, and the Greens 2.6 percent. The parties that have increased their support compared to November last year are the Moderates and the Center, while New Democracy has gone down by 3 percent. [passage omitted]
