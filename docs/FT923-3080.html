920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Guernseygas advances to Pounds 2.7m Guernseygas Group, which includes Guernsey Gas Light Company and has interests in property, motor vehicles, energy and fuel, reported pre-tax profits up from Pounds 2.5m to Pounds 2.7m in the first half of 1992. Turnover fell to Pounds 40m (Pounds 62m) because of technical changes in the method of buying and selling cars, according to Mr John Morris, chairman. Earnings were 3.72p (3.45p) per share and the interim dividend is maintained at 0.9p.
