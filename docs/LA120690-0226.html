December 6, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

HOME DECORATION JUDGING SCHEDULED

The city's Arts and Culture Committee will sponsor its second Holiday Home Decorating Contest.

Homes and front yards will be judged, and awards will be presented for the most original, most beautiful, most unusual and best religious display.

Residents who want to compete need to register at the Recreation and Community Services Department office, 6767 Passons Blvd., by Dec. 14. Judging will take place Dec. 17 and 18 beginning 6:30 p.m. For more information, contact committee liaison Maureen Hammond at 942-2000, Ext. 260.
