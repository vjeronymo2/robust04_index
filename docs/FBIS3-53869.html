Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister brother Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas has received (Jean Martel), the French ambassador to our country; [name indistinct], the German charge d'affaires in our country; and EC representatives. They extended the congratulations of their countries and the EC states on the results achieved by the political forces' dialogue committee to circumvent the recent crisis in our country. They also stressed their eagerness to support the march of accord, unity, and democracy, and expressed their countries' readiness to take part in whatever would secure the necessary circumstances and guarantees to implement this agreement. This agreement will help Yemen take great steps forward. Brother al-'Attas voiced his thanks and appreciation for the EC ambassadors for the interest they showed in the Yemeni crisis and for helping circumvent and end it. He affirmed that the pledge and accord document requires great efforts and potentials to implement its content. The brother prime minister also expressed confidence that the EC states and all friends will extend every possible aid to implement this agreement. Brother Muhammad Salih (?al-Qatays), the acting under secretary at the Foreign Ministry's branch in Aden, attended the meeting.
