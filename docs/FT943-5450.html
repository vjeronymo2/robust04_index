940903 FT 03 SEP 94 / Finance and the Family: Tied or untied? - At a glance Life insurance advice is still driven by commission and can be 'wholly inappropriate' according to the Consumer's Association. Its monthly publication, Which?, sent researchers to 30 financial advisers. Some were independent, some tied - only able to recommend the products of one company -and others were based at banks or building societies. 'Too many advisers recommended commission-generating products at the expense of appropriate advice. IFAs were no better than tied agents,' concluded Which?
