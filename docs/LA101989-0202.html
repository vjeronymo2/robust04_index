October 19, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

EARNINGS

BoMed Medical Manufacturing Ltd., an Irvine manufacturer of heart-monitoring equipment, posted third quarter net earnings of $39,000, up from $9,000 in the same quarter of fiscal 1988. Net revenue for the third quarter ended Aug. 31 was $797,000, up from $740,000 for the same period of fiscal 1988. Nine-month earnings for fiscal 1989 were $121,000, compared to $67,000 last year. For the first nine months of fiscal 1989, the company posted $2.3 million in revenue, compared to $2.1 million for the same period a year ago.
