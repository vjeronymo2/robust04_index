BFN [Text] Tripoli, June 25 (XINHUA) -- Chinese State Councillor and Secretary General of the State Council Luo Gan arrived here this afternoon for a four-day friendly visit to Libya at the invitation of the General People's Committee (cabinet). Upon his arrival, Luo Gan was received by 'Umar Mustafa al-Muntasir, secretary for foreign liaison and international cooperation (foreign minister) of Libya's General People's Committee. In a written statement issued on his arrival in Tripoli, Luo Gan said that China and Libya are two countries linked together by friendly ties, and that the ties of friendship and cooperation between them have developed satisfactorily since they established diplomatic relations in 1978. He said that during his current visit to Libya he would meet with Libyan leaders and have talks with the 'Umar Mustafa al-Muntasir on bilateral ties between the two countries as well as international and regional issues of mutual interest. Luo expressed confidence that his current visit to Libya would contribute to consolidating friendship and understanding between the Chinese and Libyan peoples and would promote the existing cooperation between the two countries. The Chinese official arrived here by road from Tunis after visiting Tunisia.
