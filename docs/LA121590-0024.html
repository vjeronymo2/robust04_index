December 15, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

TAKING SIDES OVER ABORTION AND TBS

Bravo! I salute Howard Rosenberg's column in speaking up for equal time for the other side of a controversy that is dividing the nation. I am myself pro-choice on the abortion issue, but as someone concerned with media and with the future of civilized dialogue in this country I feel the defense of balance and of respect for difference is vital for our democratic future.

In the past, I have felt that he has only spoken up for balance when he felt that points of view on the liberal-left side of the divide did not get an airing. This column would seem to indicate that I did him an injustice. Please take this as my apology.

DAVID HOROWITZ, Chairman, Committee on Media Integrity, Los Angeles
