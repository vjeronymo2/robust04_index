Language: Latvian Article Type:BFN [Text] As the Foreign Affairs Ministry press center reports, Latvian Foreign Minister Georgs Andrejevs has received a letter from U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher. The letter once again expresses U.S. Government support for the Russian Army withdrawal from Latvian territory by 31 August 1994, and shows an understanding of the Latvian people's problems. The Skrunda issue is also mentioned and it is stated that the U.S. Government is considering possible financial support in the issue of dismantling the Skrunda [facility]. Christopher also expressed understanding of the tackling of the citizenship issue in Latvia.
