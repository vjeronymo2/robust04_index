BFN [Text] The Mozambican Government had demobilized 10,615 soldiers by 8 April. The Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] has only demobilized 365 men since the troop demobilization process began on 10 March. According to a UN Operations in Mozambique report, the Renamo troops are due to start demobilizing in central Mozambique tomorrow. An as yet unspecified number of Renamo troops are due to be demobilized in Mabunde assembly area, in Sofala Province's Chibabava District.
