BFN [YONHAP report from UN Headquarters] [Text] ROK Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu, who is visiting New York, held consecutive individual contacts with representatives of the permanent member states of the UN Security Council [SC] on 6 June to discuss cooperation on sanction measures on North Korea. Minister Han explained the ROK Government's position that sanctions on North Korea are inevitable and exchanged views with representatives of the UN Security Council permanent member states regarding the objective, timing, and methods of sanctions. Particularly, Minister Han reportedly asked for cooperation in implementing sanctions on North Korea during his meetings with Representatives Li Zhaoxing of the PRC and Vorontsov of Russia. A high-ranking official of the ROK mission to the United Nations said that the UN Security Council is likely to adopt a sanction resolution rather than a warning resolution and that the United States will lead discussions on sanction measures sometime this week.
