930821 FT 21 AUG 93 / Ten hurt in Ulster mortar attack TEN PEOPLE, including a 10-year-old boy, suffered minor injuries yesterday in an IRA mortar bomb attack on the courthouse at Newry, Co Down. Three missiles were fired from a lorry abandoned at traffic lights 100 yards from the court building. One exploded in trees causing minor damage to the wall outside the building. Windows were shattered in nearby premises and a number of cars damaged, police said.
