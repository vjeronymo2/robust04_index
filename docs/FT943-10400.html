940805 FT 05 AUG 94 / Holiday discounts climb to 15% The two largest travel retailers yesterday increased discounts on summer 1995 holidays to 15 per cent. Lunn Poly sparked the price-cutting earlier this week with initial discounts of 11 per cent. Going Places, the second biggest retail chain, increased its top discount from 13 per cent to 15 per cent. It is offering other summer 1995 holidays at discounts of 5 per cent to 8 per cent.
