Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The India-China expert group has agreed on several issues, including its mandate and the methodology to be followed in its future meetings. During its two-day meeting in New Delhi, the group expressed satisfaction at the confidence building measures which are already in place. Matters relating to clarification of the line of actual control, reduction of armed forces along the line, and prior information about military exercises and prevention of air intrusion came up for discussion during the talks. The Indian side, led by Mr. Shiva Shankara Menon, joint secretary in the External Affairs Ministry, included representatives from the Defense and Home Ministries and the Survey of India. The Chinese delegation was headed by Mr. Fu Xuejhang.
