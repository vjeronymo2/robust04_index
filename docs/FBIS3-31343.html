Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Croatian President Dr. Franjo Tudjman received Italian Senator Francesco Cossiga, who was paying a private unofficial visit, in the Presidential Palace this evening. During the course of their friendly and open talks, President Tudjman and Senator Cossiga discussed the political situation in Croatia and developments in Bosnia-Herzegovina. President Tudjman informed his guest about efforts being invested by the Croatian state leadership aimed at ending the crisis in Bosnia-Herzegovina through political negotiations, i.e., in a peaceful way, to the benefit of all three nations living in that republic.
