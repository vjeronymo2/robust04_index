940112 FT 12 JAN 94 / Commodities and Agriculture: Russian grain crop down Russia's 1993 grain harvest fell by about 4m tonnes to 102m but the result could have been worse, Agriculture Minister Viktor Khlystun said, reports Reuter from Moscow. He told Russian television 120m tonnes could have been brought in but for bad weather during harvesting. 'This situation is extremely grave,' he said.
