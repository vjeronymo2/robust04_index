930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Net assets ahead at Witan Investment The net asset value per ordinary share of Witan Investment Company stood at 212.8p at the December 31 year end. That compared with 181.3p at the June 30 interim stage and with 178.8p a year ago. The net asset value per warrant increased from 102.3p to 136.3p over the 12 months. Revenue after tax increased from Pounds 19.5m to Pounds 19.6m for earnings per share of 5.71p (5.67p). A final dividend of 2.9p raises the total from 5.3p to 5.6p.
