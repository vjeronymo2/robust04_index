930505 FT 05 MAY 93 / Boost for Welsh agency budget THE GOVERNMENT is to increase the budget of the Welsh Development Agency by Pounds 9m this year to Pounds 30m to support job creation and other projects in urban areas, Mr David Hunt, Welsh secretary, announced yesterday. Mr Hunt said the increase 'represents a comprehensive and concerted programme of action' to tackle problems associated with urban change. In addition to this urban development programme, the agency is to receive Pounds 31m for land reclamation schemes to clear derelict industrial sites and a further Pounds 5m for environmental improvement schemes.
