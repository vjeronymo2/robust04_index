931002 FT 02 OCT 93 / Finance and the Family: Dollar waxes against D-Mark - At a Glance THE US dollar strengthened against the D-Mark this week, after being lifted by positive US economic data on Thursday, but it fell back a little on Friday after further statistics which were not as good as expected. Worries over the political situation in Moscow had depressed the D-Mark, although in the middle of the week economic concerns took over.
