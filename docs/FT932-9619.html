930513 FT 13 MAY 93 / First geared unit trust launched JOHN GOVETT, the UK fund management group, plans to launch the first geared futures and options unit trust aimed at UK retail investors on Monday, two years after UK regulators first opened the doors to the creation of these funds. The Govett Geared UK Index Fund aims to double every capital movement in the FT-SE 100 index of UK stocks, by using futures and options to provide gearing.
