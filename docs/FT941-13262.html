940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / International Company News: Martin Marietta rises to Dollars 118m Martin Marietta yesterday reported fourth-quarter net income of Dollars 118.7m, excluding a Dollars 17.4m charge for accounting changes, from Dollars 75.7m, Richard Tomkins writes. The US defence contractor saw sales rise to Dollars 3.19bn from Dollars 1.45bn. Earnings per share rose to Dollars 1.08 from 81 cents before accounting changes and 90 cents after the changes. Last year Martin Marietta bought General Electric's aerospace division for Dollars 3.05bn and General Dynamics' space launch business for Dollars 208.5m. Full-year net income rose to Dollars 450.3m from Dollars 345.4m before accounting changes, but slumped to Dollars 20.9m after the changes. Turnover was up to Dollars 9.44bn from Dollars 5.95bn. Earnings per share rose to Dollars 4.25 from Dollars 3.60 before accounting changes, but turned into losses of 26 cents a share after the changes.
