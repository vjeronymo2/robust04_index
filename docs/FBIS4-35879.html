BFN [Text] President Sardar Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari returned to Islamabad this morning after a 12-day private visit to the United States. During his visit, the president held talks with American leaders and congressmen. He also addressed prominent U.S. businessmen, financial institutions, and Pakistanis living in the United States on prospects of investing in Pakistan. The president also met with the French president in Paris while returning home.
