BFN [Text] At a news conference in Legnica, Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak has assessed the strike situation in the Polish steelworking industry. He pointed out its international implications. According to the head of the government, Western manufacturers of steel have large surpluses of that raw material. We are losing at our own volition, the prime minister said, because strikes bring our own steelworks to a standstill and foreign competitors can enter our markets.
