BFN [Text] From June 6 through 10 in the southern part of the Baltic Sea the Baltops-94 joint exercises will take place. Participants include vessels from Russia, the USA, Belgium, Denmark, Germany, Finland, Latvia, Lithuania, the Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Sweden and Estonia. Russia will be represented by the patrol boat Neustrashimy, the press service of the Russian navy told Interfax. During the exercises firing and rescue operations will be practiced. For the first time the exercises will include U.S. parachutists from the Orion naval aircraft making a landing on water to be taken aboard by the Russian ship Neustrashimy. During the exercises the ships are due to call in at Kiel (Germany) and Gdynia (Poland).
