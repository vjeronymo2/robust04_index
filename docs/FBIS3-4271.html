Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, March 28 (XINHUA) -- The Russian Defense Ministry today refuted the latest batch of media reports alleging that it is continuing a program of creating biological weapons, including a new "super-plague" to which the West has no antidote. A spokesman for the ministry said that some foreign and Russian media have quoted an anonymous intelligence source as saying the program was under the Russian Defense Ministry. The spokesman said it is astonishing that the story, circulated by Western media in search of sensation, was thoughtlessly repeated by some Russian mass media organs. "Russia is neither developing nor producing biological weapons," the spokesman stated.
