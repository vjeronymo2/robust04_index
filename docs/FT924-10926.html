921028 FT 28 OCT 92 / International Capital Markets: Hitachi slips 53% at midway - Japanese Electronics Company Results HITACHI yesterday reported a 53 per cent plunge in parent company pre-tax profits to Y37.5bn (Dollars 307.3m) in the half-year to September, on a 6 per cent drop in sales to Y1,895.6bn. Hitachi's sales of information systems and electronic equipment were off 7.4 per cent to Y910.9bn. Sales of heavy electrical equipment rose 8.2 per cent to Y428.8bn. The recession has taken its biggest toll on Hitachi's consumer products, where sales dropped 22 per cent to Y204.9bn. Net income fell 46 per cent to Y25bn.
