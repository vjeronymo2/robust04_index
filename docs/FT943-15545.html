940708 FT 08 JUL 94 / UK Company News: St David's Trust net asset value rises St David's Investment Trust reported net asset value per capital share up from 237p to 251p during the year to May 31. Net revenue for the six months to the end of May was Pounds 460,000 (Pounds 450,000) for earnings per income share of 5.61p (5.4p). A third interim dividend of 3p is declared, making an unchanged total so far of 9p.
