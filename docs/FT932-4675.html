930608 FT 08 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Further distribution likely at Manakin Manakin Holdings, the investment trust which went into voluntary liquidation in 1991, reported a net asset value of 68p at April 15, against 100p six months earlier. During the period two distributions totalling 42p were made. It is expected that a further repayment will be made before the end of 1993. There was a net loss for the six months to April 15 of Pounds 188,000, against profits of Pounds 257,000. Losses per share were 0.96p (earnings 1.25p).
