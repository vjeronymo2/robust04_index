Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hong Kong, March 14 (XINHUA) -- A six-day China metallurgical export products trade fair opened here today in a bid to achieve overseas investment and cooperation. On display are various metallurgical products manufactured by the China Metallurgical Import and Export Corporation and its 31 subsidiaries. It is learned that a large-scale metallurgical exhibition will open in Beijing on April 12. It would be the largest of its kind China would ever give.
