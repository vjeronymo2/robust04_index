July 4, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

The director of "The Revenger's Tragedy" at the Callboard Theatre and the performance schedule were listed incorrectly in Monday's Calendar. The play is directed by Stuart Wood and runs Thursday and Friday at 8 p.m. in repertory with "3 for 1!," which plays Saturday at 8 p.m. and Sunday at 7 p.m.
