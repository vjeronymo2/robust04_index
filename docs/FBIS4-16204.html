BFN [Unattributed NEGA report in the "Northern Ossetia" column] [Text] Ali Aliyev, chairman of the Confederation of Caucasus Peoples' parliament, who arrived in Vladikavkaz on a peacemaking mission, has visited the Supreme Soviet of Northern Ossetia. A. Aliyev told about the Confederation's activity to restore peace and accord in Russia's southern republics. Elaborating on problems of resolving the Ossetian-Ingush conflict, Aliyev stressed that to a very large extent it "has been provoked" by the Russian Federation law On Rehabilitation of Repressed Peoples, which does not envision a mechanism for territorial rehabilitation, and also by the Russian Federation law On the Formation of the Ingush Republic, which does not designate its administrative boundaries.
