920811 FT 11 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Caspen makes two acquisitions Caspen Oil has expanded its engineering and oil producing interests for Pounds 217,000 and Dollars 285,000 (Pounds 149,000) respectively. It has acquired the specialist engineering business and trade names of Villiers, which is expected to double turnover of the engineering division. The US subsidiary has consolidated its position in the Somerset Field, Texas, by purchasing a small group of oil producing properties and associated equipment. Current production is running at an annual rate of 15,000 barrels net to Caspen, and there are attributable proven reserves of over 100,000 barrels.
