BFN [Editorial Report] Moscow Radio Moscow in Russian to Tajikistan at 1700 GMT on 10 May, in a broadcast from the "Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry, reports that weapons which the USSR used to supply to Yemen are now being used in the current fighting. The unidentified announcer says: "In Yemen there are now over 1,000 of our tanks. These are T-62, T-55, and T-54 models. In Yemen at present there are about 800 Soviet-made artillery combat vehicles and armored transporters. There are tens of our aircraft -- Su-22, MiG-21, and MiG-23, and even surface-to-surface missiles, Scud, and SS-21 missiles. The Yemenis also have some of our Mi-8 and Mi-24 combat helicopters and our combat vessels." "Slavyanka" will report on how these weapons perform.
