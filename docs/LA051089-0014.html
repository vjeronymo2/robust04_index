May 10, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Her-r-r-r-e's Jesse?: The Chicago Tribune reported Tuesday that Jesse Jackson may host a weekly TV talk show this fall that would be produced by King World Productions, the company that syndicates such hits as "Wheel of Fortune" and "The Oprah Winfrey Show." However, Frank Watkins, a Jackson spokesman, told Reuters, "We have no comment at this time" on the report. ALEENE MacMINN
