Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 19 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice Premier Li Lanqing met with a group of Russian journalists here this morning. The journalists, as guests of the China Association for International Friendly Contact, are here to acquaint themselves with China's reforms, opening drive and the situation of the county's economic construction. In addition to Beijing, they have also visited Shenzhen and Hangzhou.
