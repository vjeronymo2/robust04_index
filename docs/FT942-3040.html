940616 FT 16 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Drayton Recovery Drayton Recovery Trust reported a net asset value of 139.2p per ordinary income share as at April 30, against 132.8p at the trust's October year-end and 110p 12 months earlier. Attributable revenue for the six months to end-April was flat at Pounds 331,000 for earnings of 4p (4.02p) per share. The interim dividend is maintained at 4p.
