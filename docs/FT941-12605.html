940129 FT 29 JAN 94 / Belarus chooses a conservative The Belarus parliament yesterday elected as head of state a conservative determined to align his country closer to Russia, Reuter reports from Minsk. Mr Mechislav Grib, 55, succeeded as chairman of parliament ousted liberal Mr Stanislav Shushkevich, who for two years pressed for Belarus's neutrality and rapid economic reforms against opposition from conservatives in parliament and government.
