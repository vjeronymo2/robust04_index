BFN [Excerpt] Foreign Minister Alekos Mikhailidhis returned a short while ago from visits to the United Nations, Washington, and London where he discussed the situation unfolding in the Cyprus issue following the latest report by UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus- Ghali and the new contacts between the UN and [Turkish Cypriot Leader] Rauf Denktas. Foreign Minister Mikhailidhis presented a document to the UN secretary general in which the Cyprus Government calls on the permanent members of the UN Security Council to hold deliberations for a new beginning in the Cyprus issue through the investigation of new options.
