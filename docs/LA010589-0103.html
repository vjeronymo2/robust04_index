January 5, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

LIBYA AT A GLANCE

Official Name -- Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya, or "state of the masses."

Population -- 3.9 million.

Official Language -- Arabic.

Ethnic Divisions -- 97% Arab and Berber.

Religion -- 97% Sunni Muslim.

Government -- Officially, a republic governed by a General People's Congress, drawn from popularly elected local councils; in fact, a military dictatorship.

Leader -- Col. Moammar Kadafi, military strongman who holds no governmental title.

Political Parties -- None.

Political Background -- Under Ottoman Turkish control for four centuries, then an Italian colony from 1912 until Allied victory in World War II; an independent, generally pro-Western monarchy from 1949 until radical officers under Kadafi took power and set up a revolutionary, military-governed regime in 1969.

Economy -- Largely based on oil, which dominates exports and brought in $4.5 billion in1986, roughly half the country's total revenue. Other industries: agriculture, fishing, food processing.

Capital -- Tripoli.

Area -- 679,358 square miles, somewhat larger than Alaska.

Climate -- Mediterranean along coast, desert in interior.

Sources: The World Factbook, 1988; Political Handbook of the World, 1988
