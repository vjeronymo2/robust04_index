940115 FT 15 JAN 94 / Capital issues hit record Pounds 35.2bn Capital-raising issues worth a record Pounds 35.2bn were announced by the UK corporate sector last year, Bank of England figures show. The total in 1992 was Pounds 17.3bn. Almost Pounds 17bn of ordinary share issues were announced last year, compared with Pounds 6.5bn in 1992. Financial institutions announced Pounds 15.8bn of capital-raising issues, higher than 1991 and 1992 combined. The Bank figures show that, after redemptions, net issues by UK borrowers last year were Pounds 30bn.
