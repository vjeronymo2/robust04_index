Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 19 (KNS-KCNA) [dateline as received] -- A central symposium of the General Association of Korean Residents in Japan (Chongnyon) was held at the Korea Hall in Tokyo on March 18 to learn from the greatness of the dear leader Marshal Kim Chong-il. It was attended by vice-chairmen and department directors of the Central Standing Committee of Chongnyon, senior officials of organisations and enterprises and officials of propaganda, social science, the press, education, culture and art under Chongnyon. Many people took the floor. The speakers said the dear leader is a great thinker, theoretician, statesman and military strategist with literary and military accomplishments, a subject intensely loyal and filial to the great leader and a people's leader infinitely faithful to the country and the people. In his address, Yi Chin-kyu, first vice-chairman of the Central Standing Committee of Chongnyon, stressed the need to strengthen Chongnyon to be a patriotic organisation faithful to the idea and leadership of Comrade Kim Chong-il, and expressed the belief that the participants would take the lead in making this year a year of education in his greatness. A letter to Marshal Kim Chong-il was adopted at the symposium.
