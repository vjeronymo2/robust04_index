Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Contrary to what had been scheduled, Afonso Dhlakama, the Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] leader, who is on a visit to South Africa, did not meet Inkatha Freedom Party leader Mangosuthu Buthelezi in Ulundi yesterday. Portugal's LUSA news agency quotes Renamo Secretary General Vicente Ululu as saying that the program of Dhlakama's visit to South Africa has suffered other alterations. His meeting with Thabo Mbeki, national chairman of the African National Congress, has been canceled. Dhlakama plans to meet President F. W. de Klerk and representatives of the Portuguese community in South Africa.
