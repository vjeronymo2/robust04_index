November 2, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

FANN, BURT TO GUEST ON RADIO SHOW

Albert Fann, Cal State Northridge tailback, and Matador Coach Bob Burt will appear on radio station KABC 790 tonight at 6 on the Superfan sports talk show.

Fann will lead Northridge (7-1, 4-0 in Western Football Conference play) against Cal Poly San Luis Obispo (7-1, 3-1) on Saturday. A victory would give Northridge its first WFC championship and its first berth in the NCAA Division II playoffs.
