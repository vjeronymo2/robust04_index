BFN [Text] For the first time, Palestinian forces in the autonomous zones have arrested four murder suspects and turned them over to Israel Police. The four, arrested by police in Jericho, are suspected of the brutal murder of a young Palestinian woman, Fatima 'Abd-al-Hamid, from the village of Baytuniya, near Ramallah. The suspects, from the al-Jalazun refugee camp, escaped to Jericho and, at the request of Israel Police, were extradited.
