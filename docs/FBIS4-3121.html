BFN [Text] Shijiazhuang, April 16 (XINHUA) -- The upgrading work of the Beijing-Zhengzhou railway electrification project is now in its crucial stage. Most of the earth work of the project has been completed and the first electric line has been laid on this section of the Beijing- Guangzhou railway. The upgrading project of the electrification section, which is about 283 km long, started in March 1993. It is expected to open for traffic by the end of 1995. It is one of the ten key projects of China's railway construction during the Eighth Five-Year Plan period (1991-1995). All the electric wiring and major parts for the project were imported and full use is being made of new- and high-technology.
