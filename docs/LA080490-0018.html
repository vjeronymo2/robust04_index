August 4, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MAYBE IT'S A CASE OF SPLIT PERSONALITY

Please, just one more viewpoint regarding the Pete Rose story.

One of your readers wrote: "To me, he will always be a great player.

Nothing more, nothing less. The one and only Charlie Hustle."

Well, maybe "Charlie Hustle" should be elected to the Hall of Fame. Certainly not "Pete Rose."

BEN MACK

Studio City
