September 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

GUNS

As a member of the NRA, I think there ought to be a referee to whom I could complain that the magazine published a picture of a NRA member clowning in a Darth Vader-ish helmet and then showed gun-controller Sen. Dennis DeConcini smiling under a ceiling light that gave the effect of a halo.

CHARLES F. WETHERBEE, Pasadena
