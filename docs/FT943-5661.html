940902 FT 02 SEP 94 / UK Company News: CRP Leisure CRP Leisure plans to raise Pounds 900,000 through a placing and open offer to repay debt and fund acquisitions. The USM-quoted company, whose only active subsidiary makes theatre scenery, is issuing 20m shares at 5p with a 5-for-4 clawback. The shares yesterday closed down 1p at 6p. Shareholders, including the 14.2 per cent holding of RA Colman (North Wales), intend to take up their entitlements to a total of 5.84m shares. A further 4.23m shares have been conditionally placed with Colman, which could leave it with a holding of 25.7 per cent.
