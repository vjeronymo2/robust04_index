930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / World Trade News in Brief: Volvo to make buses in China Volvo, the Swedish car and commercial vehicle maker, has formed a joint venture with the Xian Aircraft company to manufacture buses in China, writes Kevin Done. Volvo said investment in the joint venture, to be called the Xian Silver Bus Corporation, would total Dollars 15m (Pounds 9.7m). The initial target would be to produce 1,000 buses a year, rising to 3,000. The venture will produce tourist and intercity buses, based on Volvo chassis and components and with aluminium alloy bodies, for both Chinese and export markets.
