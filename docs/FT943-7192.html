940824 FT 24 AUG 94 / International Company News: Helsinki-listed companies expected to see profit this year The aggregate profit after financial items of companies listed on the Helsinki bourse is expected to total FM11.86bn (Dollars 2.37bn) this year, compared with a loss of FM981m in 1993, according to the investment research department of Kansallis-Osake-Pankki, the Finnish bank, Reuter reports from Helsinki. In 1995, the aggregate profit is expected to rise to FM20.07bn compared with the current record of FM14.7bn in 1988, KOP said.
