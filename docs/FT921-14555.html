920115 FT 15 JAN 92 / BSA chief denies mortage lenders and housing associations 'at loggerheads' MR Mark Boleat, Building Societies Association director-general, denied yesterday that mortgage lenders and housing associations were 'at loggerheads' over schemes to ease the repossessions crisis. He told an Institute of Economic Affairs conference: 'What is happening is negotiation. It will take some weeks. But there is no question of the initiative faltering.'
