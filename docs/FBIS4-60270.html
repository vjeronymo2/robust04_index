BFN [Text] Dr. Ahmad al-Tibi, Yasir 'Arafat's adviser, said tonight that the next Palestine National Council [PNC] convention will revoke Palestinian Covenant clauses contravening the Oslo agreement, including those calling for Israel's annihilation. He announced that the PNC will convene soon, and for the first time in six years, the convention will be held in the Gaza Strip. Dr. al-Tibi stressed that his remarks were made with PLO chief Yasir 'Arafat's knowledge.
