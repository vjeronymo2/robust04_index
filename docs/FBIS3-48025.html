Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bank Negara's gold and foreign exchange and other reserves, including special drawing rights rose to RM [Malaysian ringgit] 64,930.89 million as at Dec 15, 1993, from RM 60,265.32 million a forthnight earlier. A statement from the central bank said its total assets and liabilities also increased to RM 93,788.00 million from RM 92,727.63 million previously. Loans and advances rose to RM 2,366.83 million from RM 2,351.96 million while holdings of Malaysian Government papers, declined to RM 377.83 million from RM 515.78 million. Holdings of bills discounted fell to RM 3,644.97 million against RM 3,695.66 million previously. Currency in circulation also dropped to RM 14,032.54 million from the previous figure of RM 14,099.11 million. Deposits of commercial banks, finance companies and merchant banks totalled RM 49,200.81 million as at Dec 15 against RM 47,780.51 million. Deposits of Federal Government declined to RM 5,457.06 million from RM 5,978.51 million while State Government deposits increased to RM 610.50 million from RM 504.69 million a forthnight ago.
