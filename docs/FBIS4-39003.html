BFN [Text] Russia is not short of oil and is ready to export more, the Ministry of Fuel and Energy said in a report prepared by its experts. By the beginning of last month the country had more than 6.5 million barrels of crude oil unsold. This is more than average reserves. Among the factors restricting the sale of oil are disproportionately high government taxes and prices. As a result, the experts say, even oil exports have become ineffective.
