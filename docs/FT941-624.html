940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Reed Elsevier Reed Elsevier France has acquired the OIP Group, a leading French exhibition organiser, which organises 13 trade and public exhibitions in Paris. OIP, which comprises five companies - FIEF, OIP, CLC, PRK and Sorespex, made an operating profit of FFr32.5m (Pounds 3.8m) on turnover of FFr143.8m in 1993. Mr Peter Davis, chairman of Reed International, the joint owner of Reed Elsevier, received Pounds 456,592 in salary and other emoluments plus Pounds 180,000 in a performance bonus in 1993, against Pounds 342,106 and Pounds 150,527 a year earlier, according to the annual report released yesterday. He also received a pension contribution of Pounds 22,500 (Pounds 16,800). In total, the directors of Reed International enjoyed a 27.3 per cent rise in their combined salaries with the bill rising from Pounds 2.54m toPounds 3.25m in the year ending December 1993.
