921208 FT 08 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Total Systems Total Systems, the USM-quoted computer software and hardware company, reported pre-tax profits up 21 per cent in the six months to September 30. Turnover rose 6 per cent to Pounds 1.36m (Pounds 1.29m) for profits of Pounds 425,000 (Pounds 351,000). Earnings per share came out at 2.85p (2.38p) and the interim dividend is doubled to 1.5p.
