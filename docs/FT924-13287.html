921016 FT 16 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Capital & Regional Capital & Regional Properties, property investment and management group, yesterday reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 136,805 in the six months to June 24, in the absence of any profit on investment property sales. Turnover rose 4 per cent to Pounds 2.96m (Pounds 2.84m). But this translated into an operating loss of Pounds 136,805 (Pounds 202,965 loss). The previous first half benefited from a Pounds 450,992 contribution from property sales and made pre-tax profits of Pounds 248,027. The interim dividend has been maintained at 0.3p. Losses per share were 1.48p (1.12p earnings).
