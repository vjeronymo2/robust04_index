Corruption PA2705175594 Mexico City XEW Television Network in Spanish 0430 GMT 27 May 94 PA2705175594 Mexico City XEW Television Network Spanish BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] Attorney General Humberto Benitez Trevino today said the Federal Judicial Police [PJF] has only one command, and this will prevent corruption. Mr. Benitez Trevino made this statement as he replied to charges by some Catholic Church leaders who claim that agents in the PGR [Attorney General's Office] are involved in drug trafficking. [Begin Benitez recording] Beginning yesterday, there is only one command in the PJF. This is the start of a reorganization of the police. [end recording] Interviewed at the end of the first Ibero-American Congress on Copyrights, he said the entry of government employees into the PGR has been limited. [Begin Benitez recording] The only way of obtaining employment in the PJF, professional police service, or the PGR will solely and exclusively be through the Professional Training Institute. [end recording] He reiterated that in the fight against corruption, no one is above the law. Benitez also admitted that crime has increased 5 percent in the Federal District over the past four months.
