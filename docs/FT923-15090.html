920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / German property fund purchase DEGI, the property fund offshoot of Germany's Dresdner Bank has bought an office building in Westminster for Pounds 57m from Norwich Union. The purchase is its third London acquisition in a year and brings its investment in London office property to nearly Pounds 148m. The Scottish Life Assurance Company has bought a 34,000 sq ft building in Fleet Street from Hammerson, the quoted property company, for Pounds 12m.
