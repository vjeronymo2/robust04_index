940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / IT vacancies 'difficult to fill' Thousands of highly-paid jobs in the information technology sector are proving difficult to fill following a surge in IT investment by UK companies, says Visit, a leading recruitment agency. Visit, which is organising a recruitment event at Olympia, west London, says exhibitors are seeking to fill more than 1,500 vacancies for jobs paying between Pounds 15,000 and Pounds 50,000. It says that the recruitment position has changed quite suddenly in the past three months and is radically different from last year.
