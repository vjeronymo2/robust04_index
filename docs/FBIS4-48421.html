BFN [Text] Khartoum, 17 May (SUNA) -- The four-party Intergovernmental Authority on Drought and Development commission, charged with supervising peace talks between the Sudanese Government and rebel factions of southern Sudan, told the Sudanese Government delegation, which is currently in Nairobi, that John Garang's faction would arrive in the Kenyan capital later on 17 May to take part in the second session of peace negotiations. This was announced by Mr. Mathiang Malwal, director of the Peace Department at the Sudanese Foreign Ministry. The Sudanese official, who spoke on the telephone with Dr. 'Ali al-Hajj Muhammad, spokesman for the Sudanese Government delegation, added that the other faction led by Riak Machar was expected to arrive in Nairobi on 18 May from Cyprus, where his delegation took part in a symposium on minorities.
