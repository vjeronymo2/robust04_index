Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A senior Angolan military adviser says the Angolan Armed Forces have temporarily suspended military operations throughout Angola. The special adviser to the Angolan Military High Command, Mr. Kundi Paiama, said however that the army would respond to any UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] attack against government held areas. The UNITA movement had earlier warned the government that it would not return to peace talks this week if the military continued large scale attacks against its bases.
