BFN [Text] In the territories today, two Israeli civilians were wounded when their car was pelted with stones while passing through Bethlehem. One of them, lightly hurt, was treated by IDF [Israel Defense Forces] medics on the spot; the other was evacuated to Hadassah Hospital where he is reported to be in moderate condition. Meanwhile, Palestinian sources say up to 24 local residents were wounded in clashes with IDF troops today. The major flash points were reported to be in Hebron's old city, and in the town of Dayr al-Balah in the Gaza Strip.
