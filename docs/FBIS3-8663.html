Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN ["Announcement by Defense Ministry Press Center"] [Text] On 1 and 2 March the Defense Ministry leadership held working meetings with commanders and officers of the 1st, 2d, and 3d Armies. These meetings discussed a wide range of problems stemming from the need to continue the reform in the Armed Forces following the adoption of the budget. In a special session, the leaderships of the Defense Ministry and the General Staff made a comprehensive analysis of the issues raised by servicemen during the above meetings. The most severe problems are finding rented living accommodation for officers on the free market, and the prospects as regards pay and housing policy. This issue of improved everyday conditions for the soldiers also awaits a solution. Particular attention was paid to the need to speed up structural reform in the Armed Forces. The Defense Ministry leadership expressed the conviction that the defining of clear prospects for the Army in the future is of decisive importance as regards motivating the personnel and increasing the combat readiness of the Armed Forces. Notwithstanding the limited budget possibilities, the minister of defense has ordered proposals to be prepared on solving the most pressing problems. The session also examined offenses committed by certain servicemen against the military statutes and army discipline.
