May 15, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL EARNINGS: CHARTER PACIFIC BANK INCOME UP 72%

Charter Pacific Bank in Agoura Hills said its net income for the first quarter ended March 31 soared 72% to $430,222, from $250,498 a year earlier. The three-branch bank had assets of $71.7 million on March 31, a 41% jump from $50.8 a year before.
