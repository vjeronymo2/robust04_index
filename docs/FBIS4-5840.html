BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 13 -- Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet received here this afternoon a delegation of the Russian Federation's Sub-Commission of the Russia-Vietnam Intergovernmental Commission for Economic, Commercial and Scientific, Technological Cooperation who is here for the third session of the commssion. On this occasion, the Russian head delegate, Deputy Prime Minister Yuriy Yarov affirmed that the Russian Federation will try not to lose its position in various branches in Vietnam that in the past used to cooperate with, and at the same time to seek for new cooperation fields in order to have a fair competition with other foreign countries being present in Vietnam. The Government of the Russian Federation also encourages the expansion of cooperative relations between branches and localities of the two countries for the prosperity of each country and conforming to the traditional Russia-Vietnam relations. Speaking to his guests, Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet expressed his profound thanks to the former Soviet Union previously and the Russian Federation at present for their great assistance to Vietnam in its national construction and defence, the best symbols of which, he said, are Hoa Binh hydro-electric power construction and Vietsovpetro. He also affirmed that the Vietnamese Government respects the consolidation and promotion of the friendly and cooperative relations with the Russian Federation. This is the inheritance of the traditional relations with a fine past between the two peoples and countries in many fields, scales and with great efficiency. Vietnam will continue that cooperation path, Mr. Kiet concluded.
