May 20, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Digital Freezes Salaries: Digital Equipment Corp., facing a decline in earnings, said it will delay all salary increases for its 73,500 employees in the United States. The salary freeze will go into effect July 2, the beginning of Digital's fiscal year, and will be re-evaluated in late September or October, the company said. The Maynard, Mass.-based computer maker reported earnings in the quarter ending April 1 of $256.4 million, down from $305.1 million a year earlier.
