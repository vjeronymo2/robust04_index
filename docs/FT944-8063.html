941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / EU leaves whisky out of new rules The European Commission gave assurances last night that Scotch whisky would not be affected by new rules on the transport of inflammable goods. The Commission has exempted drink 'containing more than 24 per cent but not more than 70 per cent alcohol by volume' carried in wooden casks from a directive on the classification, packaging and labelling of dangerous goods.
