930624 FT 24 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Bristol Water rises by 30% Bristol Water Holdings lifted pre-tax profits 30 per cent, from Pounds 5.24m to Pounds 6.8m, in the year to March 31, maintaining the advance shown at the interim stage. Turnover increased 9 per cent to Pounds 52.1m, mainly reflecting a 7.3 per cent price rise permitted for the core water supply business by OFWAT. A proposed final dividend of 20.7p lifts the total to 31p (28p), covered 2.6 times by basic earnings of 82p (68.9p).
