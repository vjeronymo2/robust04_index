Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] In Hanoi on 27 January, Deputy Prime Minister Phan Van Khai received the Japanese Oversea Economic Cooperation Fund delegation led by its Vice President Hiroshi Kikumoto currently on a working visit to Vietnam. Mr. Hiroshi Kikumoto thanked Prime Minister Phan Van Khai for his hospitality and briefed him on the purpose of the delegation's visit, which included the signing of a diplomatic note and an agreement on the Japanese Government's official development aid to Vietnam in fiscal 1993-94. Deputy Prime Minister Phan Van Khai expressed the Vietnamese Government and people's gratitude to the Japanese Government and people for their goodwill and valuable assistance in helping with the renovation.
