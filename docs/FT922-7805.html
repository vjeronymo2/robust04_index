920516 FT 16 MAY 92 / US returns bases to Japan US Vice President Dan Quayle left Japan yesterday after promising to return three small military bases to Japanese control, and having extracted promises for Dollars 300m (Pounds 170m) in Japanese loans for private sector development in eastern Europe, writes Robert Thomson in Tokyo. Mr Quayle came to Tokyo to commemorate the 20th anniversary of the transfer to Japanese control of the Okinawan islands, in the south, which had been under the authority of the US military after the Pacific war ended.
