BFN [Text] The first part of this year's meeting of the Western European Union [WEU] opened in Paris today. A Slovene parliamentary delegation has also been invited to attend the meeting as an observer. Our delegation consists of Borut Pahor, head of the Slovene delegation to the European Council Parliamentary Conference [as heard], and Jozef Kupse, chairman of the Parliamentary Committee for Defense. What is above all important for Slovenia, is to successfully present in Paris our country's interest in acquiring the status of WEU associate member.
