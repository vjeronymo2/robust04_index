September 23, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

CSUN TO PLAY IN SOCCER TOURNAMENT AT UNLV

Cal State Northridge will play the University of San Diego in the first round of the Nevada Las Vegas soccer tournament at 3 this afternoon.

The Matadors are scheduled to play the host Runnin' Rebels on Sunday at 1 p.m.

Scott Piri and Jim Hofferber will lead Northridge (3-0-2), runner-up in the past two NCAA Division II championships.

Piri and Hofferber have each scored a team-high three goals this season.

Hofferber had a goal and an assist in Northridge's 3-3 overtime tie with Westmont on Wednesday.
