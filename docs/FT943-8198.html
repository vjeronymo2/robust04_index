940818 FT 18 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Dudley Jenkins to seek listing Dudley Jenkins, the supplier of direct mail services, is seeking to move from the USM to the Official List. Dealings are expected to begin on Monday. Mr Tylan Bahcheli, chairman, announced the company's intention when reporting preliminary results in July. He then reported pre-tax profits for the year to April 16 per cent higher at Pounds 903,000 (Pounds 779,000) on turnover 19 per cent ahead at Pounds 12.6m.
