Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Ambassadors Ja'far Mu'awiyah, Samir Mubarak, and Jihad Murtada, members of the Lebanese delegation to the Washington Arab-Israeli bilateral peace talks, returned to Beirut from Washington via Paris this afternoon, following the suspension of the 12th round of talks. The delegation's head, Ambassador Suhayl Shammas, is still in the United States, but on a private visit.
