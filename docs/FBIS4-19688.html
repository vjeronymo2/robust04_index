BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] Thirty-five terrorists were killed in clashes that erupted between security forces and terrorists in Dugumdagi in Sirnak, Uludere District and near Diyarbakir, Kulp District. A first lieutenant and five security personnel were martyred in the clash in Dugumdagi. One terrorist was caught in Tunceli, and one terrorist surrendered to the security forces in Sirnak.
