940714 FT 14 JUL 94 / City bond dealer charged with theft A City bond dealer appeared in court yesterday charged with the theft of nearly Pounds 2m from his employers. Mr Gregory Stranger, 26, of west London, who worked for the Swiss Bank Corporation, is accused of taking the SFr3.821m between May 16 and 27 this year. Magistrates adjourned the case for three months.
