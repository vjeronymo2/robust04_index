940402 FT 02 APR 94 / International Company News: Saint Louis slips Groupe Saint Louis, the French industrial group with interests in sugar and paper, saw net profits slip to FFr717m last year, from FFr774m in 1992, writes John Ridding. The company showed that the relative stability of its results demonstrated its resilience in the face of a deterioration in the European economy last year and the effects of strong price competition.
