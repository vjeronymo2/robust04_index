June 8, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

WOODBRIDGE'S BASEBALL COACH RESIGNS

Tim O'Donohue, Woodbridge High School's varsity baseball coach, resigned Monday and will be replaced by assistant Johnny Ceballos, Athletic Director Dave Cowen said.

O'Donohue, who had a 4-20 record in his only season at Woodbridge, informed Cowen of his decision last Friday and cited a problem of teaching in Tustin and coaching at Woodbridge as being one of the factors in his decision, Cowen said.
