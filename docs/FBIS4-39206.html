BFN [Text] Tbilisi, June 6 (SNARK)--Rezo Adamia, secretary of the Georgian Parliament Commission on Defence and National Security told SNARK today Georgia insists on joint command over Russian military bases, stationed in Georgia. In his opinion, the command over the Russian bases should be implemented on coordination with Commander- in-Chief of Georgian Army. Besides, we suggest that in the Russian troops, should also serve Georgians, but the Russian side is very suspicious in this question. Among the serious discords at the negotiations is also the problem of the terms of the troops' staying. Russia insists on the term of 25 years, but Rezo Adamia considers that the agreement should be signed for 5 years. It will allow to revise it and to move necessary amendments every 5 years. "We should follow the example of the American bases in Philippines," said the MP.
