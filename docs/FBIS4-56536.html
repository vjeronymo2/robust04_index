CSO [Editorial Report] Managua LA PRENSA in Spanish of 19 April 1994 on page 4 reports that 800,000 pounds of chile tabasco peppers have been exported to the United States since the end of 1993. The peppers, which are grown in Nueva Guinea, El Tuma, and farms in the western zone are sold at 30 cents per pound. Growers receive financing from the Nicaraguan Investment Fund and the National Development Bank. Exporter David Robleto explains that the peppers require no special handling for export.
