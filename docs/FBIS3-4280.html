Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Guangzhou, March 28 (XINHUA) -- A four sq km electronics industrial park was inaugurated today in Guangzhou, capital of south China's Guangdong Province. It is aimed to develop advanced electronics and telecommunication products and to grow into an international electronics and telecommunication research and development center. The park is supported by the State Science and Technology Commission, the Commission of Science, Technology and Industry for National Defense, the Ministry of Electronics Industry and the Guangzhou Municipal Committee of Science and Technology. It is intended that program-controlled telephone exchanges and digital transmission equipment should soon be produced in the new park. Cooperation with world-renowned electronics and telecommunications companies is also planned.
