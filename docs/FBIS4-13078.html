BFN [Report by Salih Qallab: "Camps Reopened To Train `Afghan Arabs'"] [Excerpt] Islamabad-London, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- [Passage omitted] The leader of a major Afghan faction, who asked to remain anonymous, has said that the majority of Arab volunteers in the Afghan jihad, known as the Afghan Arabs, are in Afghanistan, and that camps have been opened to retrain and rehabilitate them in order to send them to their original countries under a plan supervised by some fundamentalist parties and forces in the Arab region. Asked about the faction sponsoring, training, and rehabilitating these Afghan Arabs, the Afghan leader said that all the major Afghan factions have such camps, and that the Arab region will witness new waves of violence unless conditions in Afghanistan calm down and one central authority is established.
