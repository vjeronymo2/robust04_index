August 27, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

STALIN'S GRANDSON TO PORTRAY HIM

The grandson of Josef Stalin will portray the late dictator in a new Soviet film about World War II, the Communist Party newspaper Pravda reported today.

It said Yevgeny Djugashvili, a professor at a military academy, has agreed to take the part in "War Is War for Everyone" after long refusing to do so.

The film covers the life of Stalin's son Yakov -- Djugashvili's father -- who was captured by the Nazis and offered in exchange for high-ranking German officers.

Stalin, whose real name was Josef Vissarionovich Djugashvili, refused the offer, saying: "We do not exchange generals for soldiers."

Yakov is assumed to have died in a German prison camp.

The new film will star Zaza Kolelishvili, a well-known Georgian actor, as Yakov.
