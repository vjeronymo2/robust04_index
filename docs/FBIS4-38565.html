BFN [Unattributed report] [Text] In the evening of 23 May, Supreme Council Chairman Oleksandr Moroz received Israel's Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary in Ukraine, Tzvi Magen, at the latter's request. In the course of the conversation, which proceeded in a businesslike and relaxed atmosphere, an exchange of opinions on the present socioeconomic and political situation in Ukraine took place, as well as on Ukrainian-Israeli relations and ways of developing them. Particular attention was given to questions of mutually advantageous cooperation between the two states and to prospects and directions for intensifying them.
