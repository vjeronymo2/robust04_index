940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / International Company News: Strong advance at Magna Int'l Magna International, the Canadian-based car parts group, lifted fourth-quarter sales 60 per cent to CDollars 1.04bn (USDollars 750m) and net profit 64 per cent to CDollars 65.2m, or CDollars 1.08 a share. Magna attributed the strong gains to acquisitions in Europe, expanded North American vehicle output and higher productivity in its plants. Full-year net profit was CDollars 234.4m, or CDollars 4.18 a share, up from CDollars 140.4m, or CDollars 3.09.
