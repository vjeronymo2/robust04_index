BFN [Text] Kuala Lumpur, June 7, RIA -- Russia regards Malaysia as its prime partner helpful in establishing broader relations with the entire ASEAN, First Vice-Premier Oleg Soskovets announced at the talks with his Malaysian opposite number Anwar Ibragim today. Oleg Soskovets also said that Russia has already produced 75 business offers for Malaysia. The two countries may, in particular, conclude an agreement on industrial, scientific and technological cooperation. Russia may help Malaysia with power production and shipbuilding and jointly manufacture satellites with a Malaysian private firm. The chairman of the Defence Industry Committee, Viktor Glukhikh, said the two countries may cooperate in environmental protection, use of natural resources, medicine, aerospace industry, machine- building, shipbuilding, radioelectronics, computer industry, communications and dual application technologies.
