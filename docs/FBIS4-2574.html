BFN [Text] Beijing, April 11 (XINHUA) -- The Chinese and Turkish trade officials met here today for the 10th session of the Sino- Turkish Joint Commission of Economy and Trade. The session was co-chaired by Yang Wensheng, Chinese assistant minister of foreign trade and economic cooperation, and Osman Dogangun, Turkish under-secretary for treasury and foreign trade. During the session, the two sides exchanged views on development of bilateral trade and economic cooperation.
