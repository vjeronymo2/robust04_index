BFN [Text] Bratislava, June 21 (CTK) -- Wage growth limits in 1994 will be 10 percent for loss-making companies and 12 percent for profit-making companies, according to a resolution approved by the Slovak Government today. During the first quarter, wages in loss-earning companies in Slovakia grew by 15.1 percent in comparison with the first quarter in 1993. As many as 2,900 firms with 25 or more employees had combined losses of ten billion crowns in the first quarter. By holding down wages in profit-making firms the government is attempting to prevent wage increases in the financial, banking and insurance sphere.
