BFN [Text] Abkhazian Svaneti, 9 May -- The military and operational situation in the Dali gorge has improved considerably. Georgian positions were reinforced and more reinforcements are due to arrive later. Fighters from all parts of Georgia joined the local population in defense of the area. Akaki Gasviani, deputy chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Autonomous Republic of Abkhazia, has been visiting Abkhazian Svaneti for several days.
