The Department of Treasury has submitted the following public information collection requirement(s) to OMB for review and clearance under the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1980, Public Law 96&hyph;511. Internal Revenue Service (IRS) OMB Number: 1545&hyph;0228 Form Number: IRS Form 6252 Type of Review: Extension Title: Installment Sale Income Description: Information is needed to figure and report an installment sale for a casual or incidental sale of personal property, and a sale of real property by someone not in the business of selling real estate. Data is used to determine whether the installment sale has been properly reported and the correct amount of profit included in income on the taxpayer's return.
