Language: Persian Article Type:CSO [Text] By decree, the grand leader of the revolution appointed Hojjat ol-Eslam Habibollah Mohammadian-Neyshaburi as his representative in the province of Sistan and Baluchestan and the Friday imam of Zahedan. Engineer Mahluji, the minister of mines and metals, merged eight deputy positions of this ministry into three positions and, in separate decrees, selected Behruz Bushehri and 'Ali Kolahduz as logistics and parliamentary deputies for mines and materials of this ministry.
