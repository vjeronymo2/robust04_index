August 31, 1990, Friday, Valley Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Civil lawsuit -- An article Aug. 12 incorrectly reported that The Wellington Group had been barred from conducting business or occupying its Torrance offices as a result of a civil lawsuit brought against the company by the state Department of Corporations. In fact, Wellington has remained open, though it was barred by the court from selling securities.

The Times also incorrectly reported that The Wellington Group is part of Pentagon Investment Group, a real estate consortium.

Also, the story said the corporation was co-owner of Toluca Court apartments, a 48-unit building taken over by the state as part of the court action. Roger L. Merwin, a Wellington Group vice president, said Wellington never owned a share of the building. Mark Harman, an attorney for the state, said state investigators believe that Wellington was a co-owner.
