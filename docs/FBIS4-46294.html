BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sharip Ausuyev] [Excerpt] Groznyy, 28 May -- Chechen President Dzhokhar Dudayev described yesterday's terrorist act in the republic as carefully organized sabotage directed against independent Chechnya. He said on local television today that forces opposed to the slight thaw in Russo-Chechen relations were behind this act. Some details about the terrorist act emerged today. There was a powerful explosion at about 2000 local time on 27 May on the road between the Chechen settlements of Zakan-Lirk and Alkha-Kala just as the president's motorcade was driving past a Zhiguli model nine parked at the edge of the road.
