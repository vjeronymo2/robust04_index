920615 FT 15 JUN 92 / Construction Contracts: Preparing for the Channel Tunnel link London Underground has awarded SIR ROBERT McALPINE & SONS a multi-million construction contract to extend Waterloo Underground Station in readiness for the Channel link and to relieve congestion. The project will provide the interchange between the international terminal and the underground station and comprises the construction of a 7.5 metres internal diameter escalator shaft with a bank of three escalators leading down from the ticket hall to a new lower concourse, the provision of interchange passageways varying in size from 3.85 to 8.25 metres in diameter from the lower concourse to the Northern Line platforms, the fitting out of the new ticket hall and the fitting out of the new London Underground ticket office within the international terminal area. Three overbridges will be built over the Northern Line running tunnels. The shaft and passageways will all be hand mined and lined with cast iron tunnel segments. The contract also includes the instrumentation of existing structures and provision for compensation grouting to counteract any settlement.
