Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Amman--SAWT AL-SHA'B has learned that the PLO has survived the financial crisis that has plagued it over the past three years as a result of the position it took on the second Gulf war. The PLO has received $29 million over the past few days. The following is a breakdown of the amounts received: Greece provided $15 million, Turkey $2 million, Malaysia $5 million, Indonesia $3 million, Algeria $2 million, not to mention the 8 million Saudi riyals transferred by the Saudi Popular Committee.
