BFN ["wor." report: "War Atrocities: Serb Arrested"] [Text] Salzburg -- A Bosnian Serb for whom an international arrest warrant had been issued and who is accused of serious war crimes was arrested in Salzburg last Friday [21 May] and taken into custody the following day pending trial. Dusko Cvjetkovic, 26, is said to have directed a "paramilitary operation" as the leader of a unit of Bosnian Serbs in the Bosnian village of Kucice on 14 July 1992, a spokesman for the Salzburg Provincial Court stated on Tuesday. Preliminary investigations for genocide, murder, and arson are being made against Cvjetkovic. If these accusations are confirmed in the further investigations, charges within the framework of substitute jurisdiction may be brought against the Serb in Austria.
