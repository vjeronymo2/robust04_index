August 30, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

ESCONDIDO DMV MOVES TO LARGER BUILDING

The state Department of Motor Vehicles has relocated its Escondido branch, moving to a new building at 725 N. Escondido Blvd.

The new building is more than twice as large as the previous one, in the 1600 block of South Escondido Boulevard.

People with DMV business can either telephone 741-2811 to make an appointment, or visit the office between 8 a.m. and 6 p.m. on Mondays, Tuesdays, Wednesdays and Fridays, or between 8 a.m. and 6:30 p.m. on Thursdays.
