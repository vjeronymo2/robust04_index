920104 FT 04 JAN 92 / Lubbers to visit South Africa Mr Ruud Lubbers, the Dutch prime minister and Mr Hans van den Broek, his foreign minister, will visit South Africa next month, Reuter reports from Amsterdam. It will be the first official visit to South Africa by a Dutch prime minister since the 1950s. The two are expected to meet African National Congress leader Mr Nelson Mandela. Despite cultural and religious ties between the two countries dating from the 17th century, The Netherlands has long been one of the fiercest critics of apartheid.
