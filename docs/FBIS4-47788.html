BFN [Text] The head of state, General Sani Abacha, said in Abuja today that the planned demarcation of the maritime borders between Nigeria and Equatorial Guinea will benefit both countries economically and socially. Gen. Abacha was speaking to Mr. Juan Olo Mba, Equatorial Guinea's petroleum resources minister, who brought a special message from President Nguema Obiang Mbasogo. The head of state affirmed Nigeria's commitment to maintain the warm and brotherly relations with Equatorial Guinea's president. The visiting envoy spoke of Equatorial Guinea's wish to strengthen its relations with Nigeria. He said his country desires the patronage of Nigerian businessmen in its timber industry. Mr. Mba disclosed that the Equatorial Guinean president wants to visit to Nigeria.
