921202 FT 02 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Sterling Publishing recovery continues Recovery at Sterling Publishing Group, publisher of annuals and magazines, continued in the six months to September 30 with pre-tax profits up from Pounds 571,000 to Pounds 1.04m. The outcome was achieved on turnover from continuing activities up from Pounds 13.6m to Pounds 14.78m. Interest charges were sharply down at Pounds 654,000 (Pounds 1.01m). The interim dividend is increased from 0.5p to 0.6p, payable from fully-diluted earnings of 1.5p (1.1p). If results for the full year match expectations, the board expects a comparable increase in the final dividend.
