February 21, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

SCHLUMBERGER EYES SALE OF DIVISION

Schlumberger Ltd. said today that it has begun exploratory talks with several interested parties for the sale of its graphics division.

The division, which designs and manufacture plotters for the computer graphics market, had 1988 revenues of $125 million and employs 850. It has plants in Guerande and Creteil, France, and Mountain View, Calif.
