April 23, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

DEBBIE GIBSON ON EARTH '90 CAST

Singer-songwriter Debbie Gibson is the latest performer to join the cast of international stars who will appear on the June 2 global telecast "Earth '90: Children and the Environment."

Gibson will host the broadcast from its U.S. site, the Brooklyn Academy of Music, and will perform a song she has written for the occasion. Gibson joins a musical lineup that includes Canada's Jeff Healey Band, Brazilian musician Gilberto Gil and Japanese new age composer Kitaro. John Denver will host the Tokyo-based segment of the program, and Olivia Newton-John will host in Paris. Planners hope the three-hour syndicated program will be watched or heard by more than 1 billion people in 100 countries.
