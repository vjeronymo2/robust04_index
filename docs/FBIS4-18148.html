BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] 'Umar al-Muntasir, Libyan secretary of the General People's Bureau for External Liaison and International Cooperation, arrived on an official visit in Ankara tonight. At Esenboga Airport the Libyan minister was received by Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin. In an airport statement, Cetin said that there are deep rooted relations based on friendship and brotherhood between the two countries. He added that measures needed to further develop relations in all fields will be discussed at the talks. Cetin said that Libya provides business opportunities to the Turkish private sector, adding that Turkish contractors have made significant contributions to Libya's development. Al-Muntasir said that his visit will be beneficial in developing bilateral friendship and cooperation. Pointing out that his country attaches great importance to relations with Turkey, al-Muntasir added that bilateral and international problems will be discussed during his visit. The visit of al-Muntasir and the delegation accompanying him will last for three days.
