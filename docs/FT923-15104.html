920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / World Trade News: ABB in Philippines power deal A consortium led by ABB Asea Brown Boveri, the Swedish-Swiss group, has won a Dollars 300m (Pounds 162m) contract from the Philippine National Power Corporation to build a 300MW combined cycle power station near Manila, writes Ian Rodger in Zurich. ABB said its portion of the Japanese financed contract was worth over Dollars 110m. It will supply the plant's control system plus three gas turbines and one steam turbine and has signed a 15-year contract for operation and maintenance of the plant. Its partners, Marubeni and Kawasaki Heavy Industries, will look after civil engineering and other aspects.
