930716 FT 16 JUL 93 / Technology: Conference call via the PC - Worth Watching Personal computers are about to be turned into video-conferencing terminals. PictureTel, the US video-conferencing company, has developed a plug-in kit that will bring video-conferencing to the PC for Pounds 4,995. The Live PCS 100 gives full-colour, full-motion video, which shares the screen with computer data. Video calls can be made via the public ISDN network to and from any other industry-standard video-conferencing terminal. PictureTel: US, 508 762 5000; UK, 0753 673000.
