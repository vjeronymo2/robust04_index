Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] After meeting Joe Clark and Gustave Feissel, the UN secretary general's special representatives in Cyprus, Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias said he hopes the outcome of the procedure on the Cyprus issue will be positive. He added that efforts are being made within the framework of UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali's ideas. Clark described the Greek Government's public support as significant and helpful to the UN secretary general's offices. He added that to achieve progress he will need the assistance of the Turkish Government and the two sides in Cyprus. He and Feissel will go to Ankara tomorrow.
