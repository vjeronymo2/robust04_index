Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] An Iranian diplomat has been expelled by the Norwegian Foreign Ministry for so-called activities unsuited to his diplomatic status. Iran's ambassador was informed of the decision on Friday 25 March. Press spokesman Invard Havnen, in the Foreign Ministry, said that the deportation has nothing to do with the assassination attempt on William Nygaard, Norwegian publisher of Salman Rushdie's book, Satanic Verses. The Iranian diplomat has 10 days in which to leave Norway.
