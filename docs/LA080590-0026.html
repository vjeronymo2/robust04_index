August 5, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

LINKING AID TO POPULATION GROWTH

Beilenson persists in the utopian vision of Americans telling the rest of the world how many babies they should be making. Apparently it is not enough that we intervene in their wars and meddle in their internal politics.

Does Beilenson really think it should be the business of the U.S. government to attempt to alter the religious and cultural traditions of societies that have been in existence for thousands of years?

JOHN VERNON

Van Nuys
