BFN [Report by TIA KHOVAR correspondent Abdugaffor Kurbonov for TASS] [Text] Dushanbe 13 May TASS -- "It can now be said with certainty that Tajikistan will rejoin the ruble zone -- the country's welfare depends on this first of all. We will build a single economic space with Russia," Tajikistan's leader Emomali Rakhmonov said at a meeting with local activists in the mountainous region of Tajikabad on 12 May. "We discussed these questions with Russian President Boris Yeltsin and government chairman Viktor Chernomyrdin," Rakhmonov said. At present the heads of government of the two countries are busy drafting relevant bilateral agreements to cover all economic aspects of the merger -- banking, customs, and taxation.
