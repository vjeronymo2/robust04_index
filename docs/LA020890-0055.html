February 8, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS: PDA ENGINEERING'S SOFTWARE DIVISION NAMES VP

Thomas C. Curry has been named vice president and general manager for the software products division of PDA Engineering, Costa Mesa.

He will be responsible for the division's worldwide operations, including product planning, development, manufacturing, marketing, sales, education and customer support.

Curry was previously vice president of marketing for McDonnell Douglas Corp.'s Manufacturing & Engineering Systems Co. He also has held management positions with Auto-Trol Technology Corp. and Air Products & Chemicals Inc.
