BFN [Excerpts] A meeting of the union of Kazakh consumer cooperative societies was held in Almaty on 17 June. [passage omitted] The union is the largest in the Central Asian system, which ensures reliable provision to the rural population of food, industrial goods, and various services. It is a powerful organization that purchases agricultural raw materials and provides quality prosessing of it in a most beneficial way. It is a full-fledged member of the International Cooperative Alliance. Its Kazkoopvneshtorg branch maintains direct trade contacts with companies of 35 foreign countries. Deputy Prime Minister Sergey Kulagin read out a greeting from President Nazarbayev. The union of consumer societies, it says, have come a long way; its history has been inseparable from that of our state. At each stage of the country's development, cooperatives made a significant contribution in the improvement of the rural population's living standard and strengthening of ties between the city and the village. The president suggested that the members of the Republic's cooperatives, using their long experience of organization of market relations, should quickly restructure their work in conformity with the demands of the times.
