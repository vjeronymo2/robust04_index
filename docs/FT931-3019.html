930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / The Budget (Government Statements): Detailed changes in VAT regime THE annual registration limit for VAT was increased from Pounds 36,600 to Pounds 37,600 from midnight last night. The limit which applies to acquisitions from other EC countries will also be increased to Pounds 37,600. The limit for those considering cancellation of their registration will be increased from Pounds 35,100 to Pounds 36,000 from May 1.
