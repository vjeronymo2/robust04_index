930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / 3.5m pay-out for Maxwell and Mirror Group Newspapers A group of insurance companies has paid Pounds 3.5m to administrators of Maxwell companies and Mirror Group Newspapers in an out-of-court settlement over a policy on the life of the late Robert Maxwell. It is the uncontested part of a Pounds 6.5m settlement sought by the administrators and MGN.
