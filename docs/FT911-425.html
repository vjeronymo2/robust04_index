910511 FT 11 MAY 91 / Govan shipyard workers start strike WORKERS at the Norwegian-owned Kvaerner Govan shipyard in Glasgow yesterday began an indefinite strike against a pay and conditions offer which their union leaders had advised them to accept. Industrial workers at the yard, which employs 2,200 people, recently rejected by 932 votes to 647 a 21-month offer which involved changes in shift patterns and improvements in productivity.
