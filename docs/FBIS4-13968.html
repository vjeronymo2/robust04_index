BFN [Text] Tehran, April 25, IRNA -- Turkmen Vice-President Boris Shikhmuradov arrived here this morning and was welcomed by Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati. He is carrying a message from Turkmen President Saparmurad Niyazov for his Iranian counterpart Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani. Upon arrival at Mehrabad Airport, Shikhmuradov told IRNA that the objective of his trip was to discuss different issues, including matters of mutual interest, with high-ranking Iranian officials. He added that following the visit of the two presidents, mutual ties have been expanded and fundamental political and economic changes have been made in bilateral relations. Shikhmuradov noted that another objective of his visit here was to make preparations for Niyazov's forthcoming visit to Iran. Meanwhile, the Turkmen official and Velayati discussed the passing of Turkmenistan's gas pipelines to Europe through Iran. The two sides also studied implementation of agreements reached between the two countries during Niyazov's last trip to Iran.
