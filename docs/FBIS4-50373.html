BFN [Text] Beijing, May 15 (XINHUA) -- General Cao Shuangming, commander of Air Force of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA), met with and hosted a dinner in honor of Air Marshal Barry Gration, chief of the Australian air staff, and his party here this evening. Gration and his party arrived here yesterday as guests of Cao. Besides Beijing they are scheduled to visit Xian, Hangzhou and Shanghai.
