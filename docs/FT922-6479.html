920523 FT 23 MAY 92 / Finance & The Family: Dublin bond fund offered Morgan Grenfell has launched a Dublin-based Reserve Assets Bond Fund. The open-ended fund will invest in government debt and AAA rated bonds and will aim for a 10.5 per cent initial gross yield. Dividends can be paid gross because of the fund's offshore status. The fund will have a single pricing structure - the equivalent of the conventional unit trust bid-offer spread is the initial charge of 5.25 per cent. The annual charge is 1 per cent; minimum investment Pounds 1,000.
