Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The vice premier and foreign minister of China, Mr. Qian Qichen, called on President Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari at the Aiwan-i-Sadr [President House] today and exchanged views with him on various bilateral and regional issues. They expressed satisfaction at the depth and direction of bilateral relations between Pakistan and China. Reviewing various aspects of bilateral cooperation, they shared the desire to further expand it to mutual benefit. During the meeting, regional situation with particular reference to situation in occupied Kashmir and Afghanistan was considered in depth. Both the leaders underlined the need to immediately stop human rights violations in occupied Kashmir and resolve the issue peacefully and in accordance with the wishes of its people and international law. On Afghanistan, the need to restore peace was stressed from both the sides. It was felt that the continuing bloodshed was depriving the people of Afghanistan of the fruits of their victory in their struggle against foreign occupation.
