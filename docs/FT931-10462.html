930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / UK Company News: US Smaller Cos net assets ahead 43% Net asset value per share of the US Smaller Companies Investment Trust at December 31 was 137.8p, compared with 96.2p a year earlier. Over the 17 months since the trust commenced trading the net asset value in sterling has risen some 43 per cent. Much of the appreciation was due to a rise of 26 per cent in the dollar over this period. Pre-tax revenue amounted to Pounds 41,000 against Pounds 115,000, while earnings per share emerged at 0.09p compared with 0.41p; there is no dividend payment.
