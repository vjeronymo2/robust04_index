Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Al-Basrah--President leader Saddam Husayn, may God protect him, has ordered that 49 million dinars be allocated to Saddamiyat al-Qurnah and al-Faw, the city of sacrifice and the gate of the great victory, accordiang to al-Basrah Governor Latif Mahal Humud. He said that this generous act on the part of the leader is in line with his excellency's previous good deeds for the city of cities [al-Basrah]. It is noteworthy that the share of Saddamiyat al-Qurnah from this allocation is 35 million dinars, whereas the share of al-Faw totals 14 million dinars. In the name of the residents of the valiant al-Basrah, the al-Basrah governor renewed the pledge of love for our militant leader Saddam Husayn, may God protect him.
