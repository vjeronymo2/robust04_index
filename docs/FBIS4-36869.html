BFN [Report by 'Abd-al-Ra'uf al-Arna'ut in Jerusalem] [Excerpt] A responsible source in the Palestinian National Authority in the Jericho area has stated that strikes in Palestinian sovereign areas will be strictly prohibited. In an exclusive statement to AL-NAHAR, the source said: Strikes express the rejection of a certain policy. However, under a national authority which represents the people, there is no reason to hold strikes. He added: Strikes are allowed in other parts of the West Bank because they are a legitimate activity to remove the occupation. [passage omitted]
