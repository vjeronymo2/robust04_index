BFN [Text] Russia's Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev will meet with representatives of the south and north of Yemen in Moscow tonight to discuss the situation in this Arab country. Kozyrev will first receive the foreign minister of the united Yemen, Muhammad Basindwah, and then hold talks with Salim [Salih] Muhammad, the emissary of south Yemen. INTERFAX has learned this at the Russian Foreign Ministry. Observers note that each of the sides in the conflict would like to obtain Russia's support prior to a Yemeni discussion at the UN Security Council.
