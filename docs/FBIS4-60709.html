BFN [Text] Following today's meeting between the presidents of the Islamic Republic of Iran and Turkmenistan, a joint communique was issued this evening. Part of the communique says: The presidents of the two countries held negotiations in a climate full of friendship, fraternity, and understanding, which is indicative of the good neighborly relations between the two countries. They exchanged views on political, economic, and cultural issues of mutual interest. Both sides have expressed their satisfaction at the agreements reached. Cooperation in the oil and gas sector was high on the agenda of these talks. Both sides emphasized their support for the implementation of the project to transport gas from Turkmenistan to Europe via the Islamic Republic of Iran. Both sides regarded cooperation in the field of transport as the main axis for the expansion of economic and trade relations between the countries. Expressing happiness at the move toward implementation of the Mashhad-Sarakhs railway line and the roads linking Quchan-Imamqoli, Dar-e Gaz-Imamqoli-Bajgiran, and Artiq-Lotfabad, the two sides called for the speeding up and completion of these projects. Both sides also stressed the need to activate banking relations, establish agency [kargozary] relations, and to inaugurate a special account [hesab-e makhsus]. Mr. Niyazov further thanked the government and people of the Islamic Republic of Iran for the warm welcome accorded to him. He invited Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani to visit Turkmenistan. The invitation was warmly accepted.
