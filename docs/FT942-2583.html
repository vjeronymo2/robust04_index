940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Storehouse Mr Keith Edelman, chief executive of the Storehouse retailing group since last August, earned the equivalent of an annualised Pounds 606,175 last year, according to the annual report. His actual pay up to the April year-end was less than that of Ms Ann Iverson, chief executive of Mothercare who is leaving to join Melville, the US retailer. She was the highest-paid director with Pounds 495,986, including a Pounds 171,500 bonus. Both figures are well below the Pounds 3.37m - including a Pounds 2.7m bonus -earned by Mr David Dworkin, the former group chief, in the previous year.
