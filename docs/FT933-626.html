930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Deficit deepens at How CONTINUED recession in the construction industry resulted in deepening losses at How Group, the building services contractor. The company turned in a pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.45m for the six months to June 30, against Pounds 96,000 last time. Turnover was down from Pounds 75.2m to Pounds 70.5m. The group, which is ungeared, has declared an interim dividend of 0.375p (0.75p). Losses per share were 2.64p (0.35p).
