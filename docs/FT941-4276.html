940311 FT 11 MAR 94 / International Company News: Wrightson ahead of predictions Wrightson, New Zealand's biggest rural supply company, reported after-tax profit of NZDollars 8m (USDollars 3m), 10 per cent higher than predicted at its flotation in December, writes Terry Hall in Wellington. Wrightson was formerly a subsidiary of Fletcher Challenge. It was sold to Fletcher Challenge shareholders as part of the parent company's asset reduction programme.
