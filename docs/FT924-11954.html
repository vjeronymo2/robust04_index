921023 FT 23 OCT 92 / Billionaire PM Lebanon appointed Mr Rafik al-Hariri, 48, a billionaire entrepreneur, as its prime minister yesterday, hoping he will use his money-making skills to stop the economic and social rot caused by 15 years of civil war, Reuter reports. He was named to his first job in politics after winning the backing of Syria, Saudi Arabia and most members of parliament.
