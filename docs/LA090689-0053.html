September 6, 1989, Wednesday, Southland Edition

CARRIER AMERICA GOING THROUGH SUEZ CANAL

The aircraft carrier America and four accompanying warships entered the Suez Canal on Tuesday on their way from the Persian Gulf to join the U.S. 6th Fleet in the Mediterranean, a canal official said.

The official identified the ships accompanying the America as the Mobile Bay, a guided missile cruiser; the Lawrence, a guided missile destroyer; the fast combat support ship Seattle, and the Joseph Hewes, a frigate.
