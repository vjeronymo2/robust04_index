BFN [Text] The Russian State Duma Committee on Economic Policy and the administration of the Sakhalin Region have agreed to draft a law on Sakhalin providing for the establishment of a special economic zone on a part of the region's territory, Chairman of the State Duma Committee on Economic Policy Sergey Glazyev said in Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk when meeting with representatives of the local executive and legislative bodies. Glazyev said that the administration could be given the right to set quotas for fishing independently immediately without waiting for the law to be adopted. However, Glazyev stressed that enlargement of rights must be followed by greater responsibility of the local authorities for the utilization of natural resources and for investments into the social infrastructure.
