940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / UK Company News: F&C Ventures in Zeneca deal Foreign & Colonial Ventures has backed D George Harris and Associates, the chemical industry specialists, in a buy-out of a Zeneca subsidiary. The deal, the second DGHA buy-out backed by F&C within two years, involved the simultaneous purchase of Thoro System Products from Zeneca and PCR Inc from its private owners. The merged companies will be known as Harris Speciality Chemicals and will have combined worldwide sales of more than Dollars 100m (Pounds 68m). The venture will be headed by Mr D George Harris as chairman and Mr Arthur Wotiz, the former head of PCR, as president.
