Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prishtina, March 26 (ATA) -- The Prishtina Serbian Court has sentenced the chairman of the Republican Party of Kosova, Reshat Nurboja, to one month imprisonment. Last year as well Mr. Nurboja was imprisoned because he had organized a congress of the Republican Party in Prishtina. The Serbian police did not allow the congress to continue its proceedings.
