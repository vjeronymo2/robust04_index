Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riyadh, Jan 31, SPA -- The joint Saudi-Canadian Commission on Economic and Technical Cooperation concluded here today its meetings under the co-chairmanship of Saudi Acting Foreign Under Secretary for Economic and Cultural Affairs Mohammad Mamoun Kurdi and the Canadian Foreign Under Secretary for International Trade Allen Kilpatrick. Addressing the final session, Kurdi expressed thanks and appreciation for the spirit of cordiality and cooperation which prevailed during the commission's deliberations. He stressed that the joint goal of the Saudi and Canadian sides is to boost bilateral cooperation and expressed hope that the outcome of the meetings would contribute to realizing more progress for the two countries. Kilpatrick praised Saudi-Canadian trade relations and pointed to the increase of joint ventures and investments.
