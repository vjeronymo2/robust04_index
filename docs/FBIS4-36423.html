BFN [Text] The Amir of Jihad Barasin has been killed in battle in Hadramawt. An authoritative source at the military and administrative joint command of the coast of Hadramawt that the so-called Amir of Jihad in the Republic, Ahmad Barasin, alias Abu Mahdi, was killed on 7 June in the battle which took place between our heroic forces and the enemy group which tried to infiltrate the Hadramawt Province. The source added that the group, which was led by Barasin, was routed. It is noteworthy to recall that Barasin was one of the leaders of the so-called al-Jihad Organization in Yemen. He was accused of the terrorist explosions which took place in the provinces of the republic in previous years.
