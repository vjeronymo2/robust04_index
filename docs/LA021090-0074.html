February 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

AT&T'S TOLL-FREE 800 NUMBERS BREAK DOWN

As many as 200 AT&T customers with toll-free 800 numbers, including the Internal Revenue Service, were left without service for nearly 1 1/2 hours Friday, the second time in a month that the phone company has experienced a major service problem.

American Telephone & Telegraph Co. blamed the latest problem on a maintenance error.
