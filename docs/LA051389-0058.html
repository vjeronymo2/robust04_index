May 13, 1989, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: CHULA VISTA

A 37-year-old Tijuana man was electrocuted Friday when he touched an exposed transformer box in Chula Vista where workers were razing a building, authorities said.

Eduardo Vargas Vargas touched an electronic control panel, for an unknown reason, about 1 p.m. on a lot in the 100 block of Mace Street, Deputy Coroner Leard Hillbrand said.

The workman died about two hours later at Chula Vista Community Hospital, Hillbrand said.
