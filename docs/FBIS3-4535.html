Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 29 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-President Rong Yiren met here today with a delegation from the Assembly of the Council of Europe, led by its president, Miguel Angel Martinez. Extending his welcome to the guests, Rong described the relationship between the Assembly of the European Council and the National People's Congress of China (NPC) as a "good cooperative one." Rong noted that China is a developing country whose people hope that it can be built under a peaceful international environment, which is essential to the development of Europe and Asia as well. The vice-president added that Europe has achieved great progress in the economy, science and technology and is complementary economically to China. China hopes that its friendly relations and cooperation with Europe in the fields of economics and trade, science and technology and other fields will be strengthened further, Rong said. Martinez said that meetings with and frank exchange of views with Chinese leaders during the past two days will be conducive to the development of the friendly cooperative relations. Martinez expressed his conviction that without cooperation between Europe and China, reconstruction of the world order cannot be carried out harmoniously. He said that the purpose of his current visit is to enhance mutual understanding and make joint efforts toward the establishment of a new world order. Ni Zhifu, vice-chairman of the NPC Standing Committee, was present at the meeting. Earlier today, Mayor of Beijing Li Qiyan also met with Martinez and his party. The delegation was scheduled to visit Shanghai and Xiamen and wind up its China visit Saturday [2 April].
