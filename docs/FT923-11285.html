920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / Finance & The Family: Smaller companies gloom deepens Small companies had another terrible week. Share prices in smaller companies have now been falling consistently for two months. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies index (capital gains version) fell 3.69 per cent to 1092.23 over the seven days to Thursday, July 23, while the County NatWest index fell by 3.49 per cent to 869.47.
