October 6, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

SUN VALLEY HOUSE FIRE DOES $130,000 DAMAGE

A fire caused $130,000 in damage to a Sun Valley house Thursday, the Los Angeles City Fire Department reported.

The fire in the 9700 block of Sunland Boulevard began about 5:40 p.m. and was extinguished in 22 minutes, Fire Department spokesman Greg Acevedo said. There were no injuries, and the cause of the fire was being investigated, he said.
