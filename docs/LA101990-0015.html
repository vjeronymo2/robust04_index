October 19, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Chris-Craft Industries Inc.: Excluding a one-time, $454-million gain credited to the third quarter of 1989, the television broadcasting conglomerate's profit for the third period of 1990 was up 109%, to $18 million. Revenue was $70.5 million, 11% higher than last year. The New York-based company said "surprisingly strong revenue growth" was responsible.
