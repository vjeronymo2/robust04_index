930706 FT 06 JUL 93 / Observer: Dead cert Investors in business expansion schemes are often lured with promises of guaranteed returns. Now the Enlightened Tobacco Company is extending its refreshingly honest approach to marketing - its Death brand of cigarettes has a skull and crossbones logo and unambiguous health warnings - to financial services. The only guarantee attached to its BES launch, which closes on Friday August 13, is that successful applicants will receive a Death certificate.
