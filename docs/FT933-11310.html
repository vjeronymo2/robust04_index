930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / Lloyd's claims US court victory LLOYD'S of London, the insurance market, has claimed victory in a US pollution case. The New Jersey Supreme Court reaffirmed two lower court decisions in favour of insurers in a case involving Morton International, which sued for recovery of contamination clean-up costs at Berry's Creek, New Jersey. Costs of the clean-up were estimated at Dollars 100m (Pounds 67.1m).
