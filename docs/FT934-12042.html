931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Delaney cuts loss to Pounds 187,000 A FALL in losses, from Pounds 495,000 to Pounds 187,000 before and after tax, was announced by Delaney Group, the building products and shopfitting concern, for the half year to June 30. Turnover of continuing operations advanced to Pounds 7.52m (Pounds 7.07m). Mr Nathu Puri, chairman, said there appeared to be a slight improvement in the commercial property market and one of the surplus investment properties had been let on a short-term basis with a purchase/sale option. Christies Panel Products had continued to increase sales and improve market share. Current demand for its products remained strong and Mr Puri reiterated his optimism for a positive outcome for the year. Losses per share were reduced to 0.3p (0.9p).
