Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Article by "r": "Correction"] [Text] In the article "Miklos Duray and Bela Bugar Keep Writing....," published in yesterday's issue of REPUBLIKA (pages 1 and 2), it should have been correctly stated that, in their letter addressed to the Council of Europe, Miklos Duray and Bela Bugar demand that "towns be given their historical Hungarian names" and not that "Hungarian towns be given their historical names," as incorrectly stated.
