940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Baillie Giff Japan Baillie Gifford Japan Trust saw its net asset value fall by 3.3 per cent from 792.8p to 767.4p over the year to August 31. Net losses for the 12 months to the end of August were Pounds 388,886 (Pounds 168,892) for losses per share of 3.54p (1.54p).
