BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Leonid Vinogradov] [Text] Vladivostok, 14 May -- As a result of today's explosion of ammunition depots belonging to the Pacific Fleet's air wing, there are casualties. According to preliminary data obtained by ITAR-TASS, all windows in the settlement of Novonezhino, located near the accident site, were blown out by the shock wave [of the blast]. The evacuation of the settlement's 3,000 inhabitants has begun. They are being taken to the towns of Bolshoy Kamen and Partizansk, where they are being accommodated in palaces of culture and sport, hotels, and hostels. The evacuation operation is being directed by Yevgeniy Nazdratenko, head of administration of Primorskiy Kray. According to a report from the hospital in Bolshoy Kamen, the first casualties, with slight injuries, have arrived there from the vicinity of the explosion. In order to avoid further mishaps, the military and the militia have cordoned off the accident site within a radius of 30 km. Traffic on the Vladivostok-Nakhodka road, which passes through Novonezhino, has been stopped.
