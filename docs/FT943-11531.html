940729 FT 29 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Sphere Inv Tst Net asset value per income and residual capital share at Sphere Investment Trust rose 20 per cent, from 36.74p to 44.19p, over the 12 months to the end of June. The board said it was still looking to extend the life of the trust, managed by Marathon Asset Management, beyond its wind-up date in October 1995. Net revenue fell from Pounds 2.83m to Pounds 2.12m, while earnings per income and residual capital share moved from 2.31p to 1.73p. As already announced, the second interim dividend is held at 0.75p, making an unchanged 1.5p to date.
