BFN [Article signed RAY/JDT: "Rwandan Patriotic Front To Support Departure of Belgian UN Troops"] [Text] Brussels, 18 Apr (BELGA) -- RPF [Rwandan Patriotic Front] troops will do all they can to help the Belgian blue helmets remaining at Kigali airport depart, RPF spokesman Jean-Baptiste Nadhumba told BELGA. Nadhumba said that he would have preferred the Belgians to continue their participation in the UN peacekeeping mission in Rwanda, but he expressed understanding for their departure. According to the spokesman, the RPF has captured Byumba, 70 km north of where a battalion of 4,000 Rwandan Armed Forces is located. The RPF reportedly also defeated government troops in Gabiro and Kagitumba.
