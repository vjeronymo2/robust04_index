941107 FT 07 NOV 94 / Company News (this Week): Sharp recovery for insurance sector Siemens - Other Companies Preliminary 1993/94 results from Siemens on Wednesday are expected to reflect the lagging recovery, lower financial earnings and ferocious competition afflicting the electrical and electronics sector. There has been no change in official forecasts of a 10 to 15 per cent fall in net earnings in the year to end September.
