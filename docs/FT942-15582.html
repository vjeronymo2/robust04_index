940414 FT 14 APR 94 / UK Company News: A&C Black declines to Pounds 507,000 Pre-tax profits of A&C Black, the publishing group, fell by Pounds 28,000 to Pounds 507,000 in 1993. Turnover dipped 10 per cent from Pounds 6.78m to Pounds 6.1m. The outcome was struck after a fall in net interest payable from Pounds 59,000 to Pounds 18,000. Earnings per share emerged at 23.5p (24.4p). The final dividend is again 9.25p, maintaining the total at 13.5p.
