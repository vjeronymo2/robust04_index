May 22, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

RAMS: Four months after losing to the 49ers in the playoffs, some of the Rams were back on the field Monday (C2). . . . This year the problem is finding a field. After a two-week rookie camp at Rams Park in Anaheim, summer camp begins July 25 in Irvine instead of the Rams' usual Cal State Fullerton site, closed by construction. . . . Then it's off to Berlin for an Aug. 11 exhibition game against Kansas City. David Reyes and Jeffrey A. Perlman
