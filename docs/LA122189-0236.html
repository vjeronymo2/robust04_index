December 21, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

SPICY CATALOGUE'S NUMBER

In the Food Briefs column in the Sunday Food Section the telephone number was incorrectly listed for Mo Hotta Mo Betta, a mail order catalogue for spicy foods. The correct number is (805) 544-4051. The brochure also can be ordered from P.O. Box 4136, San Luis Obispo, Calif. 92403.
