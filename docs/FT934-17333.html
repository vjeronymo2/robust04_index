931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / Observer: Know thyself An eagle-eyed company secretary has stumbled on one reason for the alarming weight gain in the Stock Exchange Yellow book. The new set of rules governing listings tips the scales at 2.075kg, over twice the weight of its predecessor. Chapter 16, which details directors' obligations, sets out, a trifle superfluously, how 'A company is not required to notify to the Company Announcements Office information which, notwithstanding compliance by it with this paragraph, it does not have.'
