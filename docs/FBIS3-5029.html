Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 3 -- Mongolian President P. Ochirbat's visit to Vietnam is an important event which will contribute to further strenthening the relations of friendship and cooperation between Vietnam and Mongolia, says NHAN DAN in an editorial to welcome the Mongolian leader who is expected here today. The editorial continues: Vietnam and Mongolia have had traditional cooperation and friendship for more than four decades now. The Mongolian Government and people gave wholehearted support to the Vietnamese people in their struggle for national liberation in the past as well as in national construction and defence at present. Mongolia has highly appreciated the result recorded by the Vietnamese people in the current renovation process, and wished to develop its multisided cooperation with vietnam. During President Ochirbat's visit the Vietnamese and Mongolian delegations will exchange views on measures, forms and possibilities to promote bilateral relations.' `As two developing countries where economic reforms are in process, Vietnam and Mongolia will contribute to speeding up the trend for peace and stability in the East Asian region and the world,' the editorial concludes.
