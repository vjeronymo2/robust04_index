August 10, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Bumper Crops Expected: The first reliable estimate of the size of this year's harvest, to be released today by the Agriculture Department, is expected to project a bumper corn, soybean and wheat crop. Officials expect a big jump in output from last year's drought-hit levels because of better weather in key Midwestern states. The report is expected to project soybean production at 1.90 billion to 2.01 billion bushels, corn at 7.29 billion to 7.76 billion bushels and wheat no lower than 1.95 billion bushels.
