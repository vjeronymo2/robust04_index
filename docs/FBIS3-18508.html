Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "European Spark Ignited: 56 Percent for Entry; Exclusive Poll for KURIER on Results of EU Negotiations in Brussels"] [Text] The grand coalition's euphoria over the conclusion of the negotiations in Brussels looks like it has spilled over to the population. Twelve hours after the end of negotiations in Brussels, 56 percent of interviewees said in a flash poll they would vote for Austria's entry into the European Union if the referendum were held next Sunday. Only 25 percent would oppose it; 59 percent believe they would benefit from the result of the negotiations.
