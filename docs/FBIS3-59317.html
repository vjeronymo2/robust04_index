Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Summary] As of 5 December, Sichuan Province had procured 760,000 dan of cotton, fulfilling 34.8 percent of the task, and a 64.3 percent decline from the same period in 1992. Sichuan consumes over 3 million dan of cotton a year, in addition to state transfers of 1.1 million dan, Sichuan itself must produce over 2 million dan. It is estimated that cotton procurement for 1993 will total 900,000 dan, a decline of 1.4 million dan from 1992.
