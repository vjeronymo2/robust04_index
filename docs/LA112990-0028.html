"Fandango" (1985), directed by Kevin Reynolds, 91 minutes. Rated PG. Critics panned this, one of Kevin Costner's first movies, because they said it went nowhere.

But who says a movie has to go anywhere? This tale of five graduating Texas college students contemplating their futures is poignant and adventurous. GUILLERMO TORRES
