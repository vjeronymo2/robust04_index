Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bangkok, January 4 (XINHUA) -- Thai Princess Maha Chakkri Sirinthon left here this morning on a 15-day official visit to China. While in China, she will visit Beijing, where she would meet Chinese government leaders, and make a trip to northeast China to study education and cultural development projects. The princess is interested in the Chinese language, culture, traditional painting and customs of the minorities.
