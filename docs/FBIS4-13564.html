BFN [Text] The cabinet today approved the appointment of Commander Asaf Hefetz as Israel Police Commissioner, to replace Commissioner Rafi Peled. He will assume duty on 1 May and serve a three-year term. Commander Yehuda Wilk, commander of the Jerusalem District Police, announced that he is resigning the force. Speaking to our correspondent earlier today, Commander Wilk said he cannot cooperate with the new police commissioner because their methods of operation are different. Police Minister Shahal told reporters that a very small police officers reshuffle is now expected.
