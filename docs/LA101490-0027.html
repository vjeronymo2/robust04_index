October 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

GOVERNMENT-RUN BROTHEL PROMISED NICE TURNABOUT

Regarding "Mustang Ranch Seized," (Sept. 20), I was very excited when the federal government took over operation of the infamous Mustang Ranch (a 105-room brothel in Nevada now being liquidated by the IRS).

I was equally disturbed when they changed their mind. It would have been the first time in history that the people could do to the government what the government has been doing to them all these years.

DARRYL O. DICKEY

Reseda
