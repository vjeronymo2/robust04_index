Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Reacting to yesterday's attack against the Shin Bet men near Ramallah, Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres said this morning that Israel will take all the steps possible from the military and legal viewpoints to prevent attacks against Israeli civilians and security personnel without any relation to the peace talks and, at the same time, will pursue the political negotiations. Mr. Peres made these remarks to our political correspondent Shlomo Raz. Minister Peres met with visiting French Foreign Minister Alain Juppe this morning. Following the meeting, the guest said that beginning next month, Israelis visiting France for short periods will not require visas. The two ministers also agreed on bilateral space research cooperation. It was also reported that Israel and France will tighten their political and military relations, especially in the research sphere.
