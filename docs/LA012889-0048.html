January 28, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

COLUMNIST'S VIEWPOINT IS WAY OFF THE MARK

Taxpayers do not want their schools to be farm teams for college and pro sports teams.

Coaches know full well that there are very few spots at the top where the big sports money is.

What happens to those students' 2.0 grade-point average when there is no room at the top?

ROBERT GARRICK

Pasadena
