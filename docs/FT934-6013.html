931126 FT 26 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Rise at Leveraged Opportunity Trust Leveraged Opportunity Trust lifted net asset value per share by 28 per cent, from 99.3p to 127.4p, over the 12 months to September 30. The company, managed by JO Hambro, reported a net deficit of Pounds 77,000 (profits of Pounds 10,000), resulting in losses of 1.02p (earnings of 0.13p) per share.
