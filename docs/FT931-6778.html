930227 FT 27 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Dividend reduced at Merlin Intl Green Net asset value at Merlin International Green Investment Trust advanced from 90.48p to 106.55p over the 1992 year. Available revenue fell from Pounds 830,374 to Pounds 593,648, equivalent to earnings of 2.36p (3.3p) per share. A final dividend of 1.1p is recommended for a reduced total of 2.1p (3.15p).
