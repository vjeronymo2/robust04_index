BFN [Unattributed report on the results of an opinion poll: "The Population's Sympathies Lie With the Opposition"] [Text] At the beginning of May, a sociological survey was carried out with the aim of establishing the popularity rating of political parties. The following political organizations topped the poll: The National-Democratic Party, the Republican Party, and the Popular Front. It is significant that the Union of [Georgia's] Citizens that was widely publicized by the authorities and that is led by the present head of state was only in ninth place. As can be seen, the population's sympathies basically lean toward the opposition. This is also shown by the fact that eight of the 10 leading political organizations belong to the opposition.
