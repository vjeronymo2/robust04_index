921201 FT 01 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Flagstone losses sharply lower Pre-tax losses at Flagstone Holdings, which operates in the fields of marine, leisure and property, were reduced from Pounds 756,000 to Pounds 161,000 for the half-year to July 31. Turnover of Pounds 388,000 compared with Pounds 338,000. Losses amounted to 0.12p (0.44p) per share.
