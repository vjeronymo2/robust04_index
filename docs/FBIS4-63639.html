BFN [By Alla Baula, AZERTAC correspondent for TASS] [Text] Baku, 23 May (ITAR-TASS) -- "The Armenian armed forces are perpetrating barbaric acts in the occupied Azeri territories, destroying intact residential buildings, communications, health care, economic, and other facilities." This statement was made today by Azeri State Adviser Vafa Gulizade, who was citing reliable sources. He said this information was also confirmed by a deserter from the Armenian army. Vafa Gulizade informed journalists that the republic's president, Geydar Aliyev, appealed to Jan Eliasson, chairman of the CSCE Minsk group, and to permanent members of the CSCE, asking them to put pressure on Armenia to end acts of vandalism by occupying Armenian troops. In the current situation the Azeri president has also written to UN Secretary General Butrus-Ghali, asking him to inform members of the UN Security Council of these developments.
