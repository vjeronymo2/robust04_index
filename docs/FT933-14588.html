930714 FT 14 JUL 93 / Salvatore Ligresti freed following questioning on corruption charges Mr Salvatore Ligresti, one of Italy's richest businessmen, was freed yesterday after questioning on corruption charges. Mr Ligresti now awaits trial over L13bn (Pounds 5.58m) of bribes his insurance company SAI SpA is alleged to have paid to secure a contract from state energy company Eni.
