BFN [Decree issued by PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat in Tunis on 20 May 1994] [Text] Decree: The chairman of the Palestinian National Authority, the chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, in accordance with the PLO Executive Committee decision, and in accordance with the powers invested in him, decrees the following: 1. The laws, regulations, and orders that were operational before 5 June 1967 in the Palestinian territories [the West Bank and Gaza Strip] will remain in force until they are standardized. 2. The various regular and religious courts will continue to do their work in accordance with the existing laws and orders. 3. Regular and religious judges and public prosecutors will continue to exercise their work each in his own field of jurisdiction in accordance with the laws. 4. This decree will take effect from the date it is issued, will be conveyed to those who will implement it, and will be published in the official gazette. Tunis, 9 Dhu al-Hijjah 1414 Hegira, corresponding to 20 May 1994. Yasir 'Arafat, chairman of the PLO Executive Committee chairman of the Palestinian National Authority
