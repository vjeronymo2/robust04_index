Foreign Investments Law NC0806204994 Yerevan SNARK in English 1630 GMT 8 Jun 94 NC0806204994 Yerevan SNARK English BFN [Text] Yerevan, June 8 (SNARK)--Today Armenian Parliament accepted the Foreign Investments Law. Speaking to the Parliament of Armenia Tigran Sargisyan, chairman of the finance and budget commission, said that the foreign investors are supplied not only with the same modes as the citizens of the republic but also with the certain privileges to stimulate the in-flow of the foreign capital. "We can't achieve certain success in privatization and other spheres without foreign investments", said Tigran Sargisyan. According to the law, the foreign investors can 100 per cent own the enterprises distributed on the territory of Armenia. Meanwhile, they have the right to acquire securities and create joint ventures in Armenia. Foreign investments in Armenia are out of the nationalization.
