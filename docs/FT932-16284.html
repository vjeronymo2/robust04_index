930406 FT 06 APR 93 / London Stock Exchange: Mirror improves The market liked the confident statement from Mirror Group enough to discount the company's 1992 pre-tax loss of Pounds 84m. The newspaper group's shares rose 11 to 115p on heavy turnover of 10m. Analysts had anticipated the heavy exceptional charge and turned their attention to the rise in operational profits. They were also heartened by improvement in advertising revenues. Ms Lorna Tilbian of SG Warburg increased her 1993 forecast by 50 per cent to Pounds 60.9m and said: 'Despite the recent rise in the share price, the fundamentals have improved sufficiently to enable us to maintain a buy recommendation.'
