BFN [Text] Aden, Yemen, June 26 (AFP) -- Northern Yemeni forces killed 16 civilians, including four children, and wounded 45 in heavy shelling of the southern port city of Aden Sunday [26 June], hospital workers said here. The four children died when Katyusha rockets slammed into the al-Tawahi district near the port. Twenty civilians were wounded in the same attack, workers at the Basuhayb military hospital said. One of the rockets damaged a house and another exploded near a school sheltering displaced people. Earlier workers at the Al-Jumhuriyah hospital said 12 dead civilians and 25 wounded had been admitted during the bombardment Sunday. Fierce fighting resumed at dawn, shattering a ceasefire announced by the Sanaa authorities, and northern troops advanced into the western suburbs, seizing the Hiswah power station and Radio Aden buildings on the coast road.
