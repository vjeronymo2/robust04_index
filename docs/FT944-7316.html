941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / Pounds 450m Teesside plan approved Samsung, the Korean industrial group, has been granted outline planning permission for its proposed Pounds 450m electronics complex on a 420-acre green-field site at Wynyard, Teesside. Stockton council's development control committee has given the plans, which would create 3,000 jobs, a 'cautious green light'. However, Mr John Scott, the chairman, expressed disappointment that so little time had been given for consideration.
