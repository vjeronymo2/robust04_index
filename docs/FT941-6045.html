940303 FT 03 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Fleming trust gains 27% Net asset value per share at Fleming Mercantile Investment Trust rose 27 per cent, from 291.2p to 370.1p, over the 12 months to January 31. After tax and preference dividends, available revenue improved to Pounds 9.21m (Pounds 7.85m) for earnings of 6.38p (5.43p) per share. The fourth and final quarterly dividend is again 1.675p, maintaining the total at 6.7p.
