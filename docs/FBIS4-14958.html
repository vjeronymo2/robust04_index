BFN [Text] Yerevan, April 5 (SNARK)--A three-month assembly (the second one in this year) started in Armenia. It is supposed to involve the medium-rank staff of the Ministries and State Departments. This is the assertion of the Armenian State Minister for Defence Vazgen Sargisyan. Speaking on television he declared that such assemblies will be carried out constantly from now on, and all the Armenian citizens liable for call-up will take part in them. The State Minister confirmed that after a short military training the participants of the assembly are usually sent to the Armenian- Azerbaijani frontier. However, they serve not at the front line, but in rearguard.
