Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The director general of the National Drug Law Enforcement Agency [NDLEA], Mr. Mohamed Bappa Jama'are, has been ordered to suspend his overseas tour and appear before the currency and miscellaneous offenses tribunal. The chairman of the tribunal, Mrs. Justice Olufunmilayo Oni-Okpaku, gave the order yesterday when 11 officials of the agency appeared before her in connection with the missing caches of heroin. She also ordered another official of the agency, [words indistinct], to appear before the tribunal on the 15th of this month. Those who appeared yesterday included the acting director general, Dr. Yusuf Dibal; the director of personnel, Alhaji Y. Saleh; and the legal adviser, Mr. Hyeladzie Nganjiwa. Four of the 11 were discharged on the ground that they were not involved in the seizure and security of the said drug but only accompanied it to get destroyed.
