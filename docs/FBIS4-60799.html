BFN [Unattributed report in the "Provincial Chronicle" column] [Text] Groznyy -- Chechnya Vice President Zelimkhan Yandarbiyev announced a possible meeting and signing of a treaty between the Russian Federation leadership and official Groznyy representatives. Mr. Yandarbiyev noted that "Elections in the Chechen state are possible only after a decision has been made by the leadership of the Ichkeria Republic, within the time frame established by the Constitution of Chechnya." The head of the Chechnya security department has charged Russia's law enforcement bodies with criminals action aimed at undermining the republic's economy. In an official letter sent to the leadership of Russian Federation power structures, Sultan Geliskhanov reports that stable criminal groups, which for a long time have engaged in oil smuggling, are taking refuge in Russia. The Crimean Republic has expressed a wish for comprehensive cooperation with the Chechen Republic. This is stated in a letter received by President Dudayev from the Crimean administration. The reaction by the Chechen leadership is so far unknown.
