920622 FT 22 JUN 92 / Construction Contracts: Cornish orders E THOMAS CONSTRUCTION has won four contracts in Cornwall worth more than Pounds 4m. They include a Pounds 3m project at Treloggan Road, Newquay, for construction of a superstore shell for Safeway. It involves building a generally single-storey superstore of about 46,700 sq ft and a petrol filling station.
