Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN ["PRC State Council Decree 144 of 31 January 1994"] [Text] Beijing, 5 Feb (XINHUA) -- The "Regulations on the Management of Foreigners' Religious Activities Within PRC Territory" are hereby promulgated. These regulations go into effect as of the date of promulgation. [Signed] Premier Li Peng [Dated] 31 January 1994
