BFN [Text] Prague, April 14 (CTK) -- In future, the Czech Army will only be purchasing weapons and equipment from Czech suppliers, NOVA Television said today, citing deputy Defense Minister Miroslav Kalousek. Kalousek said NOVA said [as received] that the army is considering placing a commission for a modernized version of the T- 72 tank with the Czech weapons producers. Kalousek was taking part in a meeting in Jihlava, South Moravia, of representatives of Czech weapon factories, the Defense and Interior ministries, and other producers which looked at the possibility of the Czech Army, police, and national security tapping the potential of Czech weapon companies. The meeting followed a plenary meeting of the weapons consortium RDP Group. The RDP Group Praha joint venture was founded last year to negotiate with the ministries and government. Forty shareholders participate in it.
