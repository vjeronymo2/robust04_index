BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 31 March (SNARK) -- Clashes between Georgian and Abkhazian detachments are going on at the Kodor ravine and at the Kvemo Lata village (Abkhazian Svanetia), press-service of the Georgian Defense Department told SNARK. Last night positions of Georgian volunteers and frontier guards were under the shelling of artillery and military hardware. Georgian Defense Department informs of the Abkhazian side concentrating the man power and military hardware. 2000 Abkhazian soldiers are attacking the ravine. The authorities are evacuating civil population. Commenting on the situation existing in Abkhazia Georgian Vice- Prime Tamaz Nadareishvili told SNARK "the Kodor ravine is the strategically important region. Georgians should prevent Abkhazian capturing of the ravine. If the ravine is lost, Abkhazians will have the direct access to the border with the Northern Caucasus. No need in Russian-Georgian border at the Psou River." Mr. Vice-Prime also mentioned from without help -- man power and military hardware -- the Abkhazian side is getting.
