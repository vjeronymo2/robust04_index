BFN [Text] A spokesman for our country's Foreign Ministry rejected the claim that Tehran supports one side of the conflict in Yemen and is dispatching military assistance to that country. He stated: From the start of the conflict the Islamic Republic of Iran clearly expressed its regret over the outbreak of the war and expressed its concern about the possible separation of the Muslim brothers in Yemen. He added: By adopting a neutral stance Iran has announced its readiness to mediate. The Foreign Ministry spokesman stressed: Iran is in favor of Yemen's unity and resolving the existing disputes through peaceful means, bearing in mind the national interests of the people of Yemen.
