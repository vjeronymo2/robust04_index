BFN [Text] The Oran special judicial council this morning handed down its final verdict in the case of the 10 people implicated in the establishment of an armed group. Here is a report on the matter from our Oran studio. [Unidentified reporter] The sentences were as follows: 20 years in jail for one person, 15 years in jail for one person, 20 years in jail for another person, three years in jail for three people, one year in jail for one person, and two people acquitted. Last Saturday the Oran special judicial council handed down several sentences varying from death to jail sentences against an armed group implicated in several assassination operations. It also condemned to death in absentia four persons implicated in the assassination of a policeman in Telagh, Sidi Bel Abbes Province. In another development, the Algiers special judicial council yesterday handed down six death sentences against the Emir Harik Noureddine group which consists of 13 persons -- five of whom are on the run -- while the rest of these defendants were sentenced to 10 and 8 years in jail. For its part, the Constantine special judicial court handed down sentences varying from death to acquittal sentences in the case of two armed groups consisting of 37 people implicated in several assassinations, possession of, and trading in firearms.
