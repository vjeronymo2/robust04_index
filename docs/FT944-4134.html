941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Abtrust New Dawn Abtrust New Dawn Investment Trust's net asset value per share rose to 258.76p at the end of the six months to October 31, compared with 255.48p at the year-end and 250.11p last time. The trust, which aims for high capital growth through investment in emerging markets, had net revenue of Pounds 189,299 (Pounds 185,056) for earnings per share of 0.53p (0.62p).
