Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By TIA correspondent Negmat Ochilov for TASS] [Text] Dushanbe, 12 Jan -- The Supreme Council Presidium of the Republic of Tajikistan has extended its decree of 6 January 1993 "On the introduction of a state of emergency in Dushanbe, the town of Tursunzade and its rayon, Shakhrinavskiy, Gissarskiy, Leninskiy, and Varzobskiy rayons, as well as the town of Kofarnikhon and its rayon for six months, until 8 June 1994." This measure was taken in order to stabilize the situation, establish the rule of law, and ensure the security of citizens, on the basis of Article 108 of the Constitution of Tajikistan.
