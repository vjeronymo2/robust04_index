BFN [Text] Paris, 14 Apr (INA)--The Iraqi parliamentary delegation arrived here last night from Strasbourg last night to participate in the meetings of the Euro-Arab parliamentary dialogue conference due to begin here tomorrow [15 April]. The Iraqi parliamentary delegation, led by 'Abd-al-Karim Jihad, member of the Culture and Information Committee at the National Assembly, took part in the Arab delegation's meetings with the general secretariat of the Euro-Arab parliamentary dialogue conference yesterday. 'Abd-al-Karim Jihad told an IRAQI NEWS AGENCY correspondent in Paris that he made a statement before the Arab delegations in which he explained the serious effects of the embargo on Iraqi children, women, and the elderly, providing figures, and the fatalities and new diseases that infect children and old people due to the shortage of medicines. 'Abd-al-Karim Jihad, head of the Iraqi parliamentary delegation to the meetings of the Euro-Arab parliamentary conference, held bilateral meetings with heads of the Sudanese, Moroccan, Tunisian, Algerian, Lebanese, Palestinian, and Jordanian Parliamentary delegations, who expressed their support for the Iraqi people, and stressed the need to lift the blockade imposed on them.
