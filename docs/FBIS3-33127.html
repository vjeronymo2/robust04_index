Article Type:CSO [Editorial Report] Managua LA PRENSA in Spanish of 23 January 1994 on page 3 reports that leaders of the Independent Liberal Party for National Unity (PLIUN), the Central American Unionist Party (PUCA), and the National Justice Party (PJN) held a press conference on 18 January in order to announce that they are "joining forces." The parties' communique stated that they "...seek alternative viable solutions with a view toward Nicaraguans working together to implement these solutions." Party leaders invited Nicaraguans to participate in a project called "Unity for Change" which offers a "credible and trustworthy" program to create "a new political culture." Party leaders explained that the country suffers from "a lack of moral values, discredited political leaders, and the absence of concrete programs to solve its problems."
