930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Unigate withdraws from UK exhibitions Unigate is selling Marler Haley ExpoSystems, the exhibition and display equipment company, to its management for an undisclosed sum. Marler had sales of Pounds 11m in the year to March 31 1993. The sale, which completes Unigate's withdrawal from the UK exhibition market, is part of the move to focus on its core food and distribution businesses.
