BFN [Text] Asked by the Jordanian News Agency about statements attributed to him regarding the occupied Syrian Golan, published by the London-based newspaper AL-HAYAH yesterday, Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali has asserted that the statement attributed to him was untrue and distorted. Dr. al-Majali added: We reject any attempts to distort our strategic relations with sisterly Syria. We deal with Syria with full confidence in the unity of both our objective and fate, and also out of the desire to reach a comprehensive peace that will safeguard the rights of all of us and secure the implementation of Security Council Resolutions 242 and 338, on which the peace process is based. Dr. al-Majali said: We look forward to discussing all issues of mutual interest during the meetings of the Joint Jordanian-Syrian Higher Committee, which will be held in Amman at the beginning of next week in order to achieve the policies of the two leaders; namely, His Majesty King Husayn and his brother His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad, in a way that will achieve the utmost degree of cooperation and coordination between the two fraternal countries.
