BFN [Text] Baku, 20 April (TURAN)--"Today the Cabinet of Azerbaijan passed a resolution dealing with all aspects of gas purchase from Turkmenistan by through clearing," said Namig Muradov, head of the clearing department for "Azerigas" state concern. It is known that today, on 20 April, the deadline to solve the debt problem for gas, which was proposed by Turkmenistan, has come to an end. The debt totals $60 million. "In order to pay off the debt, Turkmenistan demanded that Azerigas deliver credits for oil equipment and production of the next export to Turkmenistan," said Muradov. According to him Annageldi Gandimov, the deputy head of the State Supply of Turkmenistan arrived in Baku to deal with the matter.
