930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / International Capital Markets: S&P upgrades Foster's FOSTER'S Brewing yesterday claimed an upgrading of its credit rating showed it was 'moving in the right direction'. S&P Australian Ratings, the Australian arm of Standard & Poor's, the US credit rating agency, said it had upgraded the Australian group's debt to BB plus from BB. S&P said it regarded as 'positive' the emergence of Broken Hill Proprietary, Australia's biggest company, as a 37 per cent shareholder in place of Harlin Holdings, which is in receivership.
