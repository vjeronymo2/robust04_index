940323 FT 23 MAR 94 / International Company News: Reflex Group Reflex Group, the computer services and software company based in the Irish Republic, returned to profit in the year ended December 31. The pre-tax figure came out at IPounds 619,000 (Pounds 596,000) against a loss of IPounds 4.53m for the eight months to December 31 1992, when the figure was hit by IPounds 2.18m written off to reserves. The 1993 pre-tax profit was also boosted by a write-back of IPounds 294,000 of provisions for losses on discontinued operations against a write-down of IPounds 486,000. Turnover was ahead at IPounds 8.1m (IPounds 5.58m). Earnings per share were 4.25p (26.85p losses).
