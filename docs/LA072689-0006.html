July 26, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

'TIME TO GET YOUR NICKEL'S WORTH'

Hooray for writing a terrific editorial on July 13 in support of state Sen. Gary Hart's (D-Santa Barbara) bill, SB 1221, which will raise the redemption rate on bottles and cans. Because of your editorial, I have written to my assemblyman and state senator asking them to support Hart's bill.

I am trying to do my part to help clean up litter.

JENNIFER ROSE OHANIAN

Glendale
