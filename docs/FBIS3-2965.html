Language: English Article Type:BFN [By Debbie Kuo] [Excerpt] Taipei, March 14 (CNA) -- Mikhail Gorbachev, former president of the now defunct Soviet Union, is scheduled to arrive in Taipei March 20 for a five-day visit at the invitation of the UNITED DAILY NEWS. Gorbachev will be accompanied by his wife and an eight-member entourage including his spokesman and several specialists in Southeast Asian affairs. During their stay, Gorbachev and his party will meet with President Li Teng-hui, Premier Lien Chan and other government officials and parliamentarians. Gorbachev will also make several speeches and meet with local scholars and students. UNITED DAILY NEWS Chairman Wang Pi-cheng invited Gorbachev to Taipei last February while on a visit to Moscow. Gorbachev responded to the invitation by saying, "it will be my pleasure to see the rich Chinese culture and to meet the industrious and intelligent people in Taiwan." [passage omitted]
