July 14, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

IN SEARCH OF FIRST WIN, DUKES MEET BREAKERS

The Newport Beach Dukes try for their first victory when they host the Miami Beach Breakers in a Team Tennis match at 7:30 tonight at the John Wayne Tennis Club.

The Dukes lost their opener, 27-23, to the Raleigh Edge Thursday. Miami Beach was leading Charlotte, 24-14, when the match was suspended due to rain Thursday in Miami.

No current Team Tennis player has played more seasons than Miami Beach's Sandy Collins, an eight-year veteran of the league.

Collins, who mostly plays doubles on the pro tour, is joined on the Breakers by Cammy MacGregor, Richard Schmidt and Greg Van Emburgh.
