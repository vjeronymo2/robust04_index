May 17, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

CAL THOMAS ON ABORTION

I cannot believe what I have just read. The article by Thomas was very upsetting.

Is there no law requiring proper disposal of all tissue removed from humans? I had miscarriages followed by D&Cs, and I was assured all material was disposed of properly and respectfully. The material was supplied to the young girl by her uncle who was a pathologist. Is that proper and respectful disposal?

DORTHA KAUFMAN

Glendale
