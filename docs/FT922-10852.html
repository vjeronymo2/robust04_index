920429 FT 29 APR 92 / UK Company News: Explaura deficit rises to Pounds 2.11m Explaura Holdings, the USM-quoted producer of limestone aggregate, reported increased pre-tax losses for 1991 of Pounds 2.11m, against Pounds 1m. The second half loss was reduced from Pounds 958,000 to Pounds 764,000 reflecting increasing production and sales. Turnover advanced from Pounds 266,000 to Pounds 1.92m. Losses per share were 1.66p (0.87p).
