Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Report by correspondent Mina Asimakopoulou from Ellinikon Airport] [Excerpt] Jacques Delores, European Union [EU] Commission president, and EU commissioners arrived at Ellinikon Airport a short time ago and, without making any statements, left immediately for the Grande Bretagne Hotel. Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou will host a dinner in Delors' honor. Alternate Foreign Minister Theodhoros Pangalos welcomed the EU commissioners. Delors and Papandreou will meet tomorrow to discuss the general guidelines for implementing the white paper on development, employment, competition, and foreign policy issues. [passage omitted]
