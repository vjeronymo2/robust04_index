BFN [Item from roundup of ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA, ITAR-TASS reports under the "Yesterday, Today, Tomorrow" rubric: "Far Easterners Discuss Incentives"] [Text] A draft presidential edict "On Special Measures in Managing the Socioeconomic Development of the Far East and Transbaykal in 1994-1995" was discussed yesterday at a session of the Interregional Association for Economic Collaboration Between Federation Components in the Far East and Transbaykal. Territory leaders believe that it is essential to set uniform electricity and heat tariffs for Siberia and the Far East, to recompense from the budget 50 percent of the expenditure incurred both on importing fuel and foodstuffs into the region and on exporting produce made here, and to repay the debt owed by the federal budget to state sector enterprises. The draft also provides for restrictions on tax deductions from profits, and customs and tax incentives.
