ONLY 944Q0176A Moscow DIPLOMATICHESKIY VESTNIK Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Article: "Statement of the Council of Ministers and Government of the Russian Federation, 22 November"] [Text] While in the zone of the Karabakh conflict with a peacemaking mission, on 20 November of this year V. N. Kazimirov, a special envoy of the president of the Russian Federation and ambassador for special commissions, and persons accompanying him were subjected to intensive fire from the Armenian side while crossing the Azeri-Armenian border in the vicinity of the city of Kazakh on the basis of an agreement with the administration of the president of Armenia. One vehicle was hit and burned. Two Azeri servicemen providing security to the Russian envoy were wounded. The government of the Russian Federation decisively condemns this unprecedented action grossly violating the rules of international law, and demands and immediate public apology from the supreme leadership of Armenia and a guarantee of unwavering provision of security in the future to Russian envoys on official missions. The government of the Russian Federation firmly expects that the culprits in this malicious act will be tried, and reserves the right to take the most decisive steps in order to preclude repetition of such incidents.
