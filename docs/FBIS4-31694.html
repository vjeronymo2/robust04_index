BFN [Text] An ethnic purge is fully under way in the cultural and economic fields and in the executive power in the Kurdzhali region; this is a deliberate line being followed by the Rights and Freedoms Movement [DPS]. Stefan Stefanov, National Assembly deputy for the Bulgarian Socialist Party [BSP], made this statement this evening at a news conference in Kurdzhali. He cited dozens of examples of leading Bulgarian officials being replaced by DPS activists lacking the necessary professional qualities. According to the BTA report, Stefan Stefanov tomorrow will brief the BSP parliamentary group on the activities of the DPS in the region and will demand a meeting with Interior Minister Viktor Mikhaylov.
