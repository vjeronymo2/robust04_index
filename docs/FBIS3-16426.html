Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Andrey Sitov] [Text] Moscow March 16 TASS -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev returned to Moscow on Friday night upon the completion of his trip to Vladivostok and Dushanbe. When in the Far East, the minister met U.S. State Secretary Warren Christopher to discuss a broad range of international issues and bilateral relations, and when in Tajikistan he met foreign ministers from Central Asia and Kazakhstan to discuss prospects for inter-Tajik political settlement. Andrey Kozyrev also visited a warship of the Pacific Fleet and a border post at the Tajik-Afghan border.
