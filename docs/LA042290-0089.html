April 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

PERKS PANNED

Regarding Peter Greenberg's article, "Some Perks Make Visitors Feel Right at Home," on March 18.

I don't need cookies (ugh) or flowers on my pillow in my hotel room. I like a clean, quiet, tastefully furnished room and decent towels. I want to pay for the room and the service, not the unneeded amenities.

LYNN SPEAR MERLES

Costa Mesa
