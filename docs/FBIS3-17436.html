Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tunis, March 21, RIA -- "Russia is ready to assist in the implementation of the UN Security Council resolution no. 904, including measures to protect Palestine civilians," says a special message sent by Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev to PLO leader Yasir `Arafat. The message was handed in yesterday night by Russian President's special envoy Viktor Posuvalyuk. During the "substantial and friendly" talk which was held on this occasion both sides stressed the thought that it was "important to restore the peace process and the Palestine-Israeli dialogue as soon as possible."
