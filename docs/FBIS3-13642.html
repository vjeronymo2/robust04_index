Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres says Israel knows that negotiations with Syria are a top priority now, and it is ready for them. Speaking to foreign correspondents in Jerusalem, Peres said Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin would investigate ways of advancing peace talks with Damascus when he meets in Washington this evening with U.S. President Bill Clinton. On talks with the PLO, Peres said Israel could not agree to Palestinian demands for the removal of Jewish settlers from Hebron. But, he added, Israel would take additional steps in Hebron to meet Palestinian security concerns. He did not elaborate.
