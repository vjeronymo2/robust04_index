Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 1 (KCNA) -- Famous works of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il were reported in different countries. Comrade Kim Chong-il's work "Abuses of Socialism are Intolerable" was carried by the Romanian papers VREMEA, NATIUNEA and DIMINEATA, the Angolan paper JOURNAL DE ANGOLA and the Guinean paper HOROYA. The work was published in pamphlet by the Warsaw Lomianki printing shop of Poland and the Afzal publishing house of Pakistan, and the Nigerian paper INDEPENDENT carried an article explaining the work. The Nigerian papers OBSERVER and NEW NIGERIAN carried the gist of his work "On the Fundamentals of Revolutionary Party Building" and an article explaining it. A detailed summary of his work "The Historical Lesson in Building Socialism and the General Line of our Party" was carried by the Jordanian paper AL CHAAB and the first part of his work "Our Socialism Centred on the Masses Shall Not Perish" by the Egyptian paper AL SIYASSI.
