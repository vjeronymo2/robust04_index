920402 FT 02 APR 92 / UK Company News: Servomex 6% ahead at Pounds 2m Servomex, a maker of gas analysers and instrumentation systems, saw profits advance 6 per cent, from Pounds 1.94m to Pounds 2.06m, in 1991. Turnover was 10 per cent higher at Pounds 18.3m, against Pounds 16.7m. Operating margins narrowed slightly to 13 per cent mainly because of the weakening of the dollar against sterling. Earnings per share were 13.8p (12.7p) from which a final dividend of 3.9p is proposed for a total of 5.7p (5.4p).
