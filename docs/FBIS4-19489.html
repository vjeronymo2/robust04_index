BFN [Unattributed article from Brussels EUROPE: "EU: Commission Prepares Communication on Clandestine Imports of Nuclear Material From East"] [Text] Brussels, 13 Apr 94 (AGENCE EUROPE) -- The Council of the European Union [EU] gave the following response to written question No. E-3697/93 by Mr A. Smith: "The Council does not have information on contraband trade in nuclear material, but it shares the concerns of the honourable member of the European Parliament regarding the consequences of fraudulent traffic into the European Union of nuclear and/or radioactive material from the Central and East European countries and the former Soviet Union. In this context, the Commission is currently preparing a communication to the Council which should make it possible to get a better idea of the scope of this problem as well as action which could be taken at Community level in terms of information."
