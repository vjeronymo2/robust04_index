April 19, 1990, Thursday, Ventura County Edition

LASORDA TO BE RAZZED WITH BERRIES

Los Angeles Dodgers Manager Tommy Lasorda has upset the Oxnard Convention and Visitors Bureau so much that it is, well, sending him a flat of strawberries.

Apparently, Lasorda asked a reporter from an Oxnard newspaper recently: "Oxnard, where the hell is that?"

The visitors bureau is not going to take a comment such as that lying down. "We are planning on shipping him a flat of strawberries," said Robert Varley, bureau executive director.

It's not much of a reprisal for making fun of the city, but Varley said that, if nothing else, the strawberries will make Lasorda remember the comment.

"And, since he's on a diet, maybe we should send him a strawberry shortcake," Varley said.
