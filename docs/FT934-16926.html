931004 FT 04 OCT 93 / People: Christies' board acquires a French style Nicholas Clive Worms has been appointed a non-executive director of Christies International and chairman of Christie's France. He is senior partner of Worms & Cie, one of France's more venerable financial services and industrial companies, having been established by his great-great grandfather in the turbulent year of 1848. Worms has long taken a personal interest in the art market. He has a swathe of directorships in French companies, including the consumer goods producer BSN and the television station TF1. Sir Anthony Tennant, chairman of Christies International, said that he would be 'a very valuable addition' to the board.
