920907 FT 07 SEP 92 / Construction Contracts: Hospital plan R. DURTNELL & SONS has been awarded new orders worth Pounds 4.3m. In Haywards Heath, the company is working for Prodescon on a fast-track contract for a Pounds 1m 60-bed hospital for Independant Health. A Pounds 600,000 contract has been received for the refurbishment of Hounslow Manor School in the London Borough of Hounslow. In Kent, a Pounds 600,000 three-storey complex of sheltered housing units will be built for the Shaftesbury Housing Association.
