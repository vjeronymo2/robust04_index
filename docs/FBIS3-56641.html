Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Georgian authorities know where former Georgian President Zviad Gamsakhurdia, who committed suicide in a village in Western Georgia in the early hours of 1 January, is buried. INTERFAX reports this was stated by Igor Giorgadze, the Georgian minister of security, at a news conference in Tbilisi yesterday evening. He said that a group of specialists from Russia, Germany, and the United States was due to depart for Western Georgia today to exhume and inspect Gamsakhurdia's body. Only a visual inspection will be carried out, in keeping with what was requested by the former President's family.
