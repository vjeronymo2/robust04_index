BFN ["Guangdong's Lianxian County Upgraded to Lianzhou City"-- ZHONGGUO XINWEN SHE headline] [Text] Guangzhou, 30 Apr (ZHONGGUO XINWEN SHE)--Recently, the State Council has approved the cancellation of Lianxian County and the establishment of Lianzhou City (county level) in Guangdong. The original administrative division under Lianxian County will become the administrative division under Lianzhou City. The Guangdong Provincial Government has decided to entrust Qingyuan City Government with the duty of administering Lianzhou City on its behalf. The seat of the Lianzhou City Government will be located in the original seat of the county government.
