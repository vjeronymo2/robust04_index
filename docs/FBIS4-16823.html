BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow April 19 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin received visiting Palestinian president and PLO leader Yasir 'Arafat here today to discuss the mideastern situation, the state and prospects for the Arab-Israeli settlement and ways to develop the Russian-Palestinian cooperation. Comprehensive and just peace in the Middle East remains a Russian strategic priority in the region vitally important for Russia, said the president. Boris Yeltsin is satisfied with the on-going Israeli-Palestinian negotiations to overcome confrontation and build a solid peace infrastructure despite all the difficulties. He reaffirmed the Russian firm intention as a co-sponsor of the mideastern peace process to render comprehensive assistance to further progress at Arab-Israeli negotiations. In his turn, Yasir 'Arafat praised Russian contribution to the mideastern peace process and achievement of positive results in the Arab-Israeli dialogue. In his opinion, Russian diplomatic efforts has played a considerable role in settlement of difficulties resulting from the February tragedy in Hebron, as well as in the implementation of measures to consolidate Palestinian security in fulfilment of the U.N. Security Council's Resolution 904. The PLO leader thanked Russia for its intention to help Palestinians in the organization of self-government in the Gaza Strip and the Jericho area. The sides stressed their striving to consolidate historical relations between Russia and the Arab people of Palestine, the presidential press service reports.
