921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Jove Investment Trust net asset rise Net asset value per share of Jove Investment Trust, a split level capital trust which invests primarily in smaller UK companies, stood at 52.19p per income share at the half year ended August 31. This compares with 51.5p at the February year end and with 51.31p a year ago. Net asset value per capital share was 22.57p against 29.04p six months ago and 26.62p a year ago. Earnings per income share came out at 3.39p (3.05p) and the interim dividend is being increased to 2.8p (2.7p). The directors anticipate that the total pay-out for the year would be not less than 5.6p (5.4p)
