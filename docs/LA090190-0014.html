September 1, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

TROJANS SEND MESSAGE BY REINSTATING PLAYERS

The news of the assault on a woman graduate student at USC was indeed shocking to me.

However, I was outraged when I read that the students (allegedly) involved continue to practice with the football team.

I am well aware that the legal aspects of this case have yet to be resolved. It seems to me that the issue here is not one of legality but of morality.

The message sent by allowing these students continued participation in the USC athletic program is clear: violence and sexual abuse of women is permitted at USC.

SISTER JO ANN DeQUATTRO

Los Angeles
