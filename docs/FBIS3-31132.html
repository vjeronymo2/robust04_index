Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Text] On no account will Hungary participate in a possible NATO military action on the territory of the former Yugoslavia. This is contained in the memorandum that was handed over today in Belgrade by a Hungarian Embassy official. Andras Vince also said that U.S. AWACS reconnaissance planes could not stay in Hungarian airspace in the case of a possible air strike. The embassy official said that Hungary's Embassy in Belgrade was not going to be evacuated for the time being. Belgrade radio and television have also reported the Hungarian Government's position.
