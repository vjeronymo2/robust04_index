Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Summary] Qinghai's public security apparatus has broken four major drug cases, seized 1.6 kg of heroin, and captured six drug criminals. On 30 October 1993, the Xining public security bureau's counternarcotics team caught drug trafficker Han Defang [7281 1795 5364] and seized .527 kg of heroin. During interrogation, Han admitted that two of his partners had gone to Yunnan, met with one Zhou Kaiqi [0719 7030 2475], and brought back over six kg of drugs, which they sold in Lanzhou and Xining. Public security cadres investigated and caught Zhou, Han Haode [7281 1170 4104], and Han Yisi [7281 0044 0934], seizing .5 kg of drugs and 28,000 yuan in drug profits. In January 1994, the counternarcotics team investigated drug trafficking in Xining by Ma Zhanzhong [7456 0594 1813]. On 4 January, they caught him and seized .5 kg of heroin. Interrogation of Ma led to the 5 January capture of Zhao Zilu [6392 1311 4389]. Officials seized .137 kg of heroin and drug processing equipment and additives.
