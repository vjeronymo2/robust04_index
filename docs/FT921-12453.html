920127 FT 27 JAN 92 / Arts: Jazz at the Palladium Tenor-saxophonist Sonny Rollins returns to London on Sunday April 26 for a one-night engagement at the London Palladium. He will be accompanied by the band he has led for the past decade - Clifton Anderson (trombone), Al Foster (drums), Mark Soskin (keyboards), Jerome Harris (guitar) and Bob Cranshaw (electric bass). Booking information from (071) 494 5038.
