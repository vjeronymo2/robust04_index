941119 FT 19 NOV 94 / UK Company News: S Staffs Water rises 22% South Staffordshire Water Holdings raised pre-tax profits by 22 per cent from Pounds 5.8m to Pounds 7.1m in the six months to September 30, on turnover 8 per cent higher at Pounds 30m, against Pounds 27.8m. Mr Lindsay Bury, chairman, said operating costs had been well contained as a result of initiatives on performance-related pay and cost control. Operating margins had improved from 23.4 per cent to 25 per cent. Net interest charges fell to Pounds 400,000 (Pounds 700,000), as a result of continued improvements in debtor control procedures. After tax of Pounds 2.1m (Pounds 1.2m) earnings per share came to 84p (80.8p). The interim dividend is stepped up from 16.5p to 19p.
