Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riga, Mar 24, BNS -- The Latvian Consulate in Vitebsk, Belarus, opened Thursday [24 March] as the first foreign representation outside the capital, Minsk. Armands Kruze is appointed Latvia's vice consul to Vitebsk. The opening ceremony was attended by Latvian Ambassador to Belarus Janis Lovniks, Vitebsk Mayor Valeri Neusev and other officials.
