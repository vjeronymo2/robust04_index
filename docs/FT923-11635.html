920723 FT 23 JUL 92 / UK Company News: BWD Securities falls to Pounds 1.22m BWD Securities, the USM-quoted financial services group, reported a 10 per cent fall in pre-tax profits, from Pounds 1.36m to Pounds 1.22m, in the six months to May 31. Turnover rose to Pounds 5.7m (Pounds 5.2m) but administrative expenses increased and operating profits declined to Pounds 903,000 (Pounds 1.03m). Interest receivable was Pounds 320,000 (Pounds 333,000). The interim dividend is maintained at 1.3p, payable from earnings of 4.5p (5.2p).
