BFN [Unattributed article from Brussels EUROPE: "Sir Leon Brittan Holds Talks in Moscow"] [Text] Sir Leon Brittan returned to Brussels after talks on Monday [2 May] in Moscow with Mr. Shokhin. Community circles announced that there had been progress in the development of plans for an exchange of letters between Russia and the European Union to regulate trade in nuclear materials in the framework of the partnership and cooperation agreement. However, it was also noted that the negotiations are not over and must be pursued. The Commission services will undoubtedly contact the delegations of the Member States regarding the wording under consideration. Pending a compromise on the nuclear dossier (in particular the conditions for the import of Russian uranium by the EU), two other aspects remain open: the regime of access to the Russian market for EU banks and shipping.
