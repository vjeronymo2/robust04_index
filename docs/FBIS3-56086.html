Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridnevadushanbe] [Text] January 12 TASS -- An address by Tajik leader Emomali Rakhmonov to his compatriots outside Tajikistan was released here on Wednesday. The address says, in particular, that "we hope that you, our dear compatriots abroad, will take an active part in Tajikistan's future political and economic life. The Tajik leadership is ready to create all necessary conditions for you to return". The document notes that at present the republic was taking efforts to stabilize the internal situation, and every Tajik citizen should contribute to the flourishing of this ancient land. Fruitful negotiations with the republic's different political forces have been under way for several months already. Now it is necessary to discuss comprehensively Tajikistan's new constitution and put it to a nationwide popular vote.
