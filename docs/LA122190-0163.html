December 21, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

PRICES INFLATE JAPANESE ASSETS

Paced by soaring land and stock prices, Japan's national assets jumped 14.4% in 1989 to $52.7 trillion, far exceeding the nation's gross national product, the Economic Planning Agency reported today.

Japan's GNP grew 7.2% to $3.12 trillion in fiscal 1989 ended last March 31.

The agency said Japan's gross domestic product at the end of calendar 1989 stood at $22.1 billion, ranking second after the United States among member countries of the Organization for Economic Cooperation and Development.

Japan's per capita gross domestic product stood at $23,296, ranking second after Switzerland with $26,104.

Finland was third with $23,196, followed by Sweden with $22,443, Norway with $21,334 and the United States with $20,768, the agency said.
