September 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

EASTERN EUROPE AND GERMANY

Following your excellent coverage of the events taking place in Eastern Europe, especially the escape of the hundreds or thousands of young East Germans from Hungary to the West, I am reliving the moments of joy and euphoria that I felt when I escaped from Hungary to Austria with my brother during the night of Nov. 24, 1956. It is an indescribable feeling that can only be compared to giving birth to a child.

I am very proud of Hungary and would like to thank Austria for helping the refugees of Eastern Europe all these years.

MARIANNE MARTIN

Altadena
