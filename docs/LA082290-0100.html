August 22, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

HOME COURT: Tennis whiz Michael Chang, fresh from his $155,000 victory at the Canadian Open and recently picked for the U.S. Davis Cup team, is moving from Placentia to Coto de Caza. Chang has bought a 6,500-square-foot home on 8 acres for $1.25 million. Jeffrey A. Perlman and Tom Hamilton
