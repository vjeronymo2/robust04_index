Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Azerbaijani Defense Ministry announced that hostilities in western Karabakh region had continued for the last two days. A number of Azerbaijani populated centers were subjected to artillery fire on Friday evening and Saturday morning. There have been civilian victims reported. The Armenian units tried to undertake an attack on Terter direction towards the northeastern region from the Sargang storage pool on Friday night. The Azerbaijani units have offered a hard resistance. The loss suffered by the Armenian army was put at 18 people killed and 70 wounded. The Armenian troops also lost a tank and an armored vehicle. The Armenian units also started an attack towards the railway station of Goradiz (the front's southern region) on Friday. However, they had to retreat to the primary positions after the Azerbaijani army had lost up to 50 people killed and wounded and an armored vehicle. The situation in the zone of the Karabakh region remains difficult, military sources in Stepanakert reported. The Karabakh units resisted several simultaneous attacks of the Azerbaijani army. Moreover, during the successful counter-attacks the Karabakh units took strategically important positions in the northern regions of Kelbajar and Mardakert.
