940817 FT 17 AUG 94 / Card spending up 16% on year Consumers in the UK spent Pounds 14.53bn using credit and debit cards in the three months to the end of July - a 16 per cent increase from the same period last year, according to figures from the Credit Card Research Group, which is funded by UK card issuers. There was a sharper rise, but from a lower base, in spending through debit cards, which increased to Pounds 5.46bn from Pounds 4.41bn. The value of credit card expenditure was Pounds 9.07bn, 12 per cent up from last year.
