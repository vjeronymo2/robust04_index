April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: ARROYO HIGH INSTALLS NEW MESSAGE BOARD

Arroyo High School in El Monte has installed a new message board west of the district field facing Santa Anita Avenue.

The sign announces club meetings, athletic endeavors, and school events and reunions.

The old board was destroyed during a severe windstorm two years ago. The new board can be programmed in the office of Nick Salerno, assistant principal for activities.
