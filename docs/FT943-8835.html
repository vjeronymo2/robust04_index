940815 FT 15 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): Kalon - Other Companies The paints group is expected to report increased first-half profits of Pounds 10m (Pounds 8.73m) tomorrow following improved contributions from Novodec - the French paint maker acquired for Pounds 22m last year - and Vallance, the specialist products business which cost Pounds 6.2m. It has also enjoyed some organic growth in the trade paints business, and is likely to recommend a 1.5p (1.3p) interim dividend.
