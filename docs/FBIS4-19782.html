BFN [Text] Ankara, April 20 (AA) -- The Foreign Ministry on Wednesday [20 April] said that relations between Turkey and Germany have very strong foundations, adding neither country should allow third countries or circles to hurt them. Speaking at his weekly briefing on Wednesday, he said that German Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel has personally confirmed the fact that German tanks were not used by Turkey in its operations carried out against terrorists. He said that from time to time misunderstandings can emerge between two countries, which in turn lead to certain difficulties. "What matters is that both countries should be determined to overcome them," Ataman said. "Both countries have the necessary political will for this. Turkey hopes the strengthening of existing mutual trust will help to overcome these misunderstandings."
