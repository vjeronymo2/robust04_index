November 8, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

STAR BILLING: TV mogul Ted Turner is coming to UC Irvine tonight, but that's not what has the social circuit crackling. It's word that he's bringing a guest. . . . When Turner's in California, his companion is usually actress Jane Fonda, who would be a newcomer to the local social scene. . . . Turner is here to accept the Eisenhower Award from the local chapter of the anti-nuclear group Physicians for Social Responsibility. John O'Dell and Ann Conway
