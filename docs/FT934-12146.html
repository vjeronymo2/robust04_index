931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / World Trade News: India and The Netherlands to sign trade agreement The Netherlands and India are to sign an investment promotion and protection agreement, Mr Ruud Lubbers, the Dutch prime minister, said in India yesterday, Stefan Wagstyl writes from New Delhi. Mr Lubbers is leading a business delegation seeking to expand commerce with India in the wake of the 1991 economic reforms. Their trip follows visits this year by delegations from other countries. He welcomed India's economic liberalisation but warned Delhi against moving too slowly. 'Too much caution in liberalising the economy risks eroding the confidence of foreign investors in India's willingness and capability to carry out reforms,' he said.
