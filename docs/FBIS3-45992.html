Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, 11 Jan (XINHUA) -- The Ministry of Finance will expand at certain places the experiment concerning first-class government bond dealers. After submitting an application to and obtaining approval from the authorities, nonpolicy-nature banks, securities companies, trust and investment companies, and other nonbank monetary organizations may serve as first-class government bond dealers of the People's Republic of China, join the 1994 national group of contract dealers of government bonds, and directly sign a contract with the Ministry of Finance to deal in government bonds. First-class government bond dealers, as an important medium of the government bond market, shoulder certain obligations in committing themselves to marketing government bonds and promoting the development of the government bond market. At the same time, they enjoy the right to and precedence in dealing with the central bank in the open government bond market. A responsible person concerned at the Ministry of Finance has pointed out: To expand the experiment involving first-class government bond dealers at selected places is an important measure for reforming the government bond market. To promote the work actively and properly, the Ministry of Finance, working together with the People's Bank of China and the China Securities Supervision and Administration Committee, has formulated the "Procedures for Managing First-Class Government Bond Dealers of the People's Republic of China" and the "Procedures for Examining and Affirming First-Class Government Bond Dealers." They will jointly examine and approve the applications of first-class government bond dealers. Finance organizations may, as of today, submit their applications and their intention to deal in the first issuing of 1994 government bonds to the State Debts Administration of the Ministry of Finance.
