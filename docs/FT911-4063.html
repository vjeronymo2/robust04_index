910422 FT 22 APR 91 / Venezuelans pick telecom contenders VENEZUELA has picked eight international companies to tender for the privatisation of the state-owned telephone company, CANTV, writes Joe Mann. They are: Ameritech, Bell Atlantic, Bell Canada, France Telecom, GTE, Nippon Telephone & Telegraph, Southwestern Bell and US West. The government wants an international company to take over CANTV operations and install around 300,000 new telephone lines a year. This would require annual investments estimated at Dollars 800m.
