October 19, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CHINGADEROS WINS AT LOS ALAMITOS

Chingaderos extended his winning streak to three races Wednesday night, holding off a rally by Murgies Kip to win the $12,500 featured allowance race at Los Alamitos Race Course.

Chingaderos, the 1988 Champion Aged Gelding, has won four races in seven starts this season, including three stakes races. Chingaderos ran the 400 yards in 19.86 seconds. Golden Rustler finished third.
