931022 FT 22 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Fleming European lifts asset value Fleming European Fledgeling Investment Trust reported a net assets of 88.3p per share as at September 30, up from 77.6p at the March year-end and 69.3p 12 months earlier. Available revenue for the six months to end-September was Pounds 240,000 (Pounds 264,000) for earnings of 0.06p (0.66p) per share.
