BFN [Text] On Tuesday coordinators of the peace-keeping operation in the Georgian-Abkhaz conflict zone Russian generals Aleksandr Sokolov, Valeriy Kuznetsov and Vasiliy Yakushev arrived in Zugdidi (West Georgia). This was reported to Interfax at the press center of the Georgian Interior Ministry force. The generals met the commander of the force Vladimir Chikovani and the local administration and discussed the details of the coming operation. Also on Tuesday the Council of the Federation, the upper house of the Russian parliament, approved the sending of Russian peace-keepers to the conflict zone. According to well-informed sources in Tbilisi, the restoration of the bridge across the Inguri river has not yet started. Russian troops on both sides are building temporary premises for future checkpoints.
