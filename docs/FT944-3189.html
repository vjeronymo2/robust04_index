941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / International Company News: Hardie buys windows division of Kalamazoo James Hardie, the Australian building products group, is acquiring the windows division of Kalamazoo Holdings, another listed building products company for ADollars 55m (USDollars 42m), writes Nikki Tait. The business has annual sales of about ADollars 80m at present, and Hardie said it would provide an entry in the ADollars 800m-a-year Australian windows market.
