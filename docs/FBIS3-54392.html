Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] A cabinet meeting was chaired this morning by esteemed Mawlawi Arsalan Rahmani, the deputy prime minister [name and titled as received]. The press department of the prime minister's office told a Bakhtar Information Agency correspondent that various political, social, and economic topics were discussed in today's meeting and necessary measures were taken. Also, an appointed cabinet delegation, including Hojjat ol-Eslam Seyyed Mohammad 'Ali Javid, the minister of planning, esteemed engineer Bashir, the acting minister of foodstuffs and light industries [name and title as received], esteemed Seyyed Mohammad Ayub, the minister of water and power [name and titled as received], esteemed Abdol Hay Elahi, the minister advisor to the Islamic State, and Gholam Sakhi Mesbah, the deputy minister of information and culture [name and title as received] -- that had been appointed to bring about reconciliation, peace, and understanding between Jihadi formations -- presented their work report to the Cabinet meeting. After assessing the report, the cabinet approved the following points: 1. The present problems between Jihadi formations should be solved thorough talks. 2. All sides should try [words indistinct] toward the election of the future leadership in [words indistinct] six months. 3. The Cabinet delegation is ready to declare a cease-fire between the state and Hezb-e Eslami [Islamic Party of Afghanistan] very soon through delegations of goodwill.
