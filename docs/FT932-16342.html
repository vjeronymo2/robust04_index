930406 FT 06 APR 93 / UK Company News: Wescol Wescol, the USM-quoted constructional steelwork and steel products group, returned profits of Pounds 13,000 pre-tax for the six months to January 31 compared with previous losses of Pounds 379,000. Turnover on continuing operations amounted to Pounds 8.15m (Pounds 7.77m). Earnings worked through at 0.1p (losses of 3.6p).
