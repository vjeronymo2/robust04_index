Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 23 (KCNA) -- Reminiscences of the great leader President Kim Il-song was carried by foreign press. The Peruvian paper INDEPENDENCIA reported the publication of the fourth volume of Part I the anti-Japanese revolution of the reminiscences "With the Century" and introduced its content with a photo of President Kim Il-song responding to the cheers of the masses. The Russian paper VETERAN carried the detailed gist of "The Demonstration of Unity" from the first volume of Part I the anti-Japanese revolution of the reminiscences "With the Century", the Nepalese paper MAJADUR KISSAN the full text of the title "The Kalun Meeting" from the second volume of Part I of the reminiscences and Paekpong library published by the International Institute of the Chuche Idea the full text of the fourth volume of the reminiscences. Meetings for expressing impressions of the reminiscences were held by the Zairean Youth Group for the Study of the Chuche Idea and the Group for the Study of the Chuche Idea of the Dar-es-Salaam Friendship Textile Mill.
