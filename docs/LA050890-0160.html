May 8, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ALL-STAR GAME COACHES NAME ASSISTANTS

Coach Dan DeLeon of El Toro High School has named La Quinta's Dave Demarest and Fountain Valley's Ron LaRuffa as his assistants for the South team in the Orange County All-Star baseball game June 12 at Glover Stadium in Anaheim.

North Coach Chris Pascal of Kennedy selected Anaheim's Dave Torres and Sunny Hills' Doug Elliot as his assistants. Pascal and Elliot were teammates on the North team in 1976.
