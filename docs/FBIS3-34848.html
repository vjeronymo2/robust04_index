Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] A four-day cease-fire between forces of the Islamic state forces and Hezb-e Eslami [Islamic Party] came into force beginning at 0500 on 15 February. A high authority of the Islamic State of Afghanistan told BAKHTAR that, as demanded by peace councils, for the purpose of opening supply routes and in respect for the sacred month of Ramadan, the Islamic State of Afghanistan has agreed to implement a cease-fire with the forces of Hezb-e Eslami in Kabul alone. During this period, peace delegations will observe the opening of highways to Kabul and the free flow of transport along these highways. The source said the jehad declared against the Dostam looter militia by the president of our country is continuing. Dostam is in no way a party to the current cease-fire. The Islamic state will observe a complete cease-fire during these four days, provided that the opposition side also observes it.
