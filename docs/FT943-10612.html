940804 FT 04 AUG 94 / Dominican result The US and the Organisation of American States yesterday criticised the belated decision, by electoral officials in the Dominican Republic, to declare Mr Joaqun Balaguer, 87, the winner of the presidential election held on May 16, Reuter reports from Washington. The authorities on Tuesday proclaimed Mr Balaguer, the incumbent seeking his seventh term, the winner with 42.4 per cent, against 41.4 per cent for Mr Jose Francisco Pena Gomez of the Dominican Revolutionary party, who alleges widespread electoral fraud.
