940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / Bustamante quits as Peruvian PM Mr Alfonso Bustamante, Peru's premier and trade and industry minister, has resigned following last week's move by parliament and the executive against the autonomy of the Supreme Court, writes Sally Bowen in Lima. Mr Efrain Goldenberg, a businessman who has headed the foreign ministry for rather less than six months, replaces Mr Bustamante as premier. Mr Goldenberg joined the cabinet last September with no previous political background and no experience of diplomacy.
