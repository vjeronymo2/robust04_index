BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow 13 June TASS -- The situation on the Tajik-Afghan border remained tense this weekend. An armed band made an attempt to cross into Tajikistan from Afghanistan on the third sector outpost of the Pyandzh frontier guard detachment on Sunday evening, Russian frontier guard press bureau informed ITAR-TASS today. Meeting a decisive rebuff, the violators disappeared without an armed confrontation with frontier guards. One day ago the third outpost of the Khorg frontier guard detachment came under automatic fire from Tajik territory. Russian frontier guards returned the fire. No casualties were reported among frontier guards.
