BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 11 April Evening Edition] [Text] The government convened the second meeting of the Administrative Reform Headquarters' (headed by Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa) "Working Panel on Import Promotion, Improvement of Market Access, and Distribution" at the Prime Minister's Official Residence on the morning of 11 April. At the meeting, Tsutomu Hata, deputy prime minister and concurrently foreign minister, said: "Deregulation is a very important issue, since its implementation is beneficial from the standpoint of people's livelihood. For example, it will help reduce the gap of commodity prices at home and abroad. Despite the current political situation, it is desired that further efforts will be made toward this end."
