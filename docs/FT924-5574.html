921126 FT 26 NOV 92 / Non-stop flights to Kiev launched AIR UKRAINE International yesterday launched the first non-stop scheduled flights from the UK to Kiev. It will operate from London's Gatwick airport three times a week, Paul Betts writes. The airline, set up last month, is the first western-style airline to operator from out of the former Soviet Union using new Boeing aircraft. Mr Valery Nazarenko, the airline's president, said it hoped to launch services to New York, Toronto and Chicago next summer.
