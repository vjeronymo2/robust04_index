May 15, 1990, Tuesday, Southland Edition

18TH DRUNKEN DRIVING ARREST NETS PRISON TERM

A woman who has been charged with drunken driving 18 times was sentenced Monday to a maximum five years in prison.

Dollie Louise Fazekas is to begin serving the sentence after completing a one-year term imposed last month in another county for violating probation on a previous DUI charge. Last month, her driver's license was revoked for life.
