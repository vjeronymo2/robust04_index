Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Vietnamese Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam emphasized that the visit by Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina marks a new stage in consolidation of bilateral ties between the two countries. Minutes before Robaina's visit, Nguyen Manh Cam told PRENSA LATINA that the traditional ties of friendship between Cuba and Vietnam have received a significant boost in the last 18 months. Robaina said that his trip to Vietnam was not only an unavoidable duty, but an enormous satisfaction because of the existing friendship between the two peoples and the desire to improve on cooperative trade and all other possible areas. Tomorrow, the Cuban delegation will hold a series of meetings with Dau Ngoc Xuan, chairman of the state Cooperation and Investment Committee, and the ministers of agriculture, the food industry, and commerce and tourism.
