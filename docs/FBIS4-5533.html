BFN [Text] Vice Foreign Minister Kim Chang-kyu, the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song's special envoy, returned home on 7 April by train after winding up his visit to Malaysia and Indonesia. The great leader's special envoy departed Jakarta on 4 April after winding up his visit to Indonesia. He was seen off at the airport by (Nana Sutri), the Indonesian president's adviser on (?movement) affairs, other functionaries concerned, the DPRK ambassador to Indonesia, and embassy members. During his visit to Indonesia, the special envoy met with Indonesian Foreign Minister Ali Alatas. Prior to this, Vice Foreign Minister Kim Chang-kyu visited Thailand, India, and Singapore as the head of a Foreign Ministry delegation.
