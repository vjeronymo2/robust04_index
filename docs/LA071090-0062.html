July 10, 1990, Tuesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: SIMI VALLEY;

2 CARS DAMAGED IN DRIVE-BY SHOOTING

Guests at an outdoor party Sunday night were startled by gunshots fired from a passing car, Simi Valley police said.

No injuries were reported.

Two cars in a driveway in the 2300 block of Fig Street were damaged by the shots, which guests said were fired from a 1960 Chevrolet Impala about 11:30 p.m., Lt. Neal Rein said.
