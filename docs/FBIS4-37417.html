BFN [Text] Mashhad, June 20, IRNA -- The toll of the dead has risen to 70 and the injured to 114 in the aftermath of this afternoon's bomb blast in the mausoleum of Imam Reza (a.s.) [peace be upon him]. A caller to IRNA who called himself the spokesman for the terrorist Mojahedeen Khalq Organization (MKO) said that MKO has taken the full responsiblity for the blast. The caller said that the bombing was meant to avenge the death of "martyrs of June 20th uprising". He said that MKO was planning more acts of subversion in "the following days." The caller then finished his conversation, which lasted for 90 seconds, by yelling obscenities at the officials of the Islamic Republic of Iran and hung up. On June 20, 1981 MKO operatives began their campaign of terror against the then nascent Islamic Republic of Iran.
