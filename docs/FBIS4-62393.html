BFN [Report by Nikolai Burbyga: "Development of `Intellectual' Weapons One of the Main Military-Technical Priorities"] [Text] The first session of the presidium of the Russian Academy of Missile and Artillery Science, recently resurrected by an edict of the Russian president, has been held in Moscow. First Deputy Defense Minister Andrey Kokoshin stressed that this academy is designed to be an important organ uniting leading scientists of various specialities engaged in developing promising technologies for arms systems using artillery and missile weapons. Among the military-technical projects which are being implemented first place goes to the creation of "intellectual" weapons systems capable of striking primarily the "nerve centers" of the other side's armed forces and taking out the enemy's complex equipment.
