930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Erith Erith, the builders' merchant, staged a recovery in the six months to June 30, reporting a pre-tax profit of Pounds 523,000. That compared with a deficit of Pounds 229,000 last time and with losses of Pounds 557,000 for the 1992 year. Turnover for the period was static at Pounds 33.5m. Earnings per share worked through at 0.75p (losses 0.34p). The company is resuming payment of dividends (last year's final was passed) with an interim of 0.35p (same).
