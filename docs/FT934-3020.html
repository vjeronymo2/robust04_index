931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Turkey Trust net asset value surges Turkey Trust more than doubled net assets to 324.16p per share in the 12 months to October 31 1993, against 148.06p a year earlier. After-tax revenue dropped to Pounds 356,000 (Pounds 601,000), giving earnings per share of 3.64p (6.13p). The recommended dividend is 3p (5p, including special 4p).
