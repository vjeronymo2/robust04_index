BFN [UPI, YONHAP from Macao] [Text] Relevant officials said on 30 March that North Korea had closed its trade office, which has so far issued visas in Macao. This office was one of the North Korean overseas offices dealing with the visa business throughout the world. Since the mid-1970's, North Korea has operated its trade office in Macao. The office has received applications for visas since 1991. However, it has not disclosed the reason why it closed the office.
