March 11, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

BOBBY SCHWARTZ WINS SPRING SPEEDWAY SERIES

Bobby Schwartz of Costa Mesa won the main event Friday to win the the Spring Classic speedway series in front of 6,240 at the Orange County Fairgrounds.

Schwartz moved past Sam Ermolenko of Cypress, who was first after last week's race at Long Beach. Schwartz finished the two-race series with 36 points. Ermolenko, who failed to make the main event, had 32 points.
