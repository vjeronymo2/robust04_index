941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / Economic Watch: West German producers prices rose 0.2% West German producer prices rose 0.2 per cent in October from September and by 1 per cent from October 1993, the federal statistics office reported. The October figures are just higher than the 0.1 per cent month-on-month rise and 0.8 per cent rise on the year that analysts had expected.
