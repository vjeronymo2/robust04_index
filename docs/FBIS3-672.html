Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg March 11 SAPA -- The situation in Ciskei is back to normal, with the military again in control of key government buildings and installations on Friday [11 March], reports SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news. On Thursday, the soldiers withdrew to their barracks demanding assurances from homeland leader Brig Oupa Gqozo about their future in a new dispensation. Brig Gqozo held talks with one Ciskei battalion on the outskirts of Bisho. Ciskei minister of foreign affairs Mr Mickey webb said reports of an attempted coup were malicious rumours. He said Ciskeian soldiers had reaffirmed their support for Brig Gqozo at Thursday's meeting.
