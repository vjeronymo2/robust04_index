July 16, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MEDIA BIAS ON ABORTION ISSUE

Can I believe my eyes? Just yesterday you finished an excellent, in-depth series by Shaw on the extent of blatant pro-abortion bias in the media. Today, as I open my paper (July 5, Page B2), I see that the listing of Los Angeles area hot lines includes (of course) an abortion referral service but does not include any of the pro-life agencies which exist to render medical, financial and emotional support to those courageous young women who refuse to abort their developing children. It kind of makes you wonder, doesn't it?

ANN K. GRIVICH

West Covina
