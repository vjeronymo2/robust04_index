BFN [Text] Petrovac, 17 June (TANJUG) -- The offensive launched by pro-Izetbegovic forces against positions held by Fikret Abdic's units in Cazin Krajina in northern part of the Bihac region has continued for the seventh day in a row, local Muslim media reports. According to the same sources, last night remnants of the 5th Corps of the so-called Bosnia-Herzegovina army launched an infantry and mortar attack against positions and residential areas controlled by the army of the Autonomous Province of Western Bosnia [APWB] from the direction of Djogici and Pajezitovici. The Abdic proautonomy forces repelled the attack without suffering any losses. Alija Izetbegovic's forces resumed the attacks this morning from the direction of Zeljkovici, Porcici, Memici, and Liskovac. Later in the day fighting also took place in the Beganovici, Todorovo, and Pecigrad areas. Local media in the Bihac region, loyal to Alija Izetbegovic, report that 5th Corps forces have been inflicting heavy losses on the army of the APWB and that both sides have suffered casualties.
