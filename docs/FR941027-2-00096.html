Special Emphasis Panel in Human Resource Development; Notice of Meeting In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92&hyph;463, as amended), the National Science Foundation announces the following meeting: Name: Special Emphasis Panel in Human Resources Development ( # 1199). Date and Time: November 17 & 18, 1994_8:30 am&hyph;5:00 pm. Place: National Science Foundation, 4201 Wilson Blvd, Arlington, VA. Type of Meeting: Closed. Contact Person: Lawrence Scadden & Mary Kohlerman, National Science Foundation, 4201 Wilson Blvd., Arlington, VA 22230. Telephone: (703) 306&hyph;1636.
