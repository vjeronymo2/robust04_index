920808 FT 08 AUG 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Availability of Indonesian cassia is limited on the European spot market, reports Man producten. Korintji a and Vera a sticks quoted at USDollars 1,900 a tonne cif for shipment. Seychelles cinnamom at Dollars 1,250 and Madagascar Dollars 1,150 cif. Indonesian nutmegs for shipment - abcd Dollars 1,650, shrivels Dollars 1,375, bwp Dollars 875 and mace broken 2 at Dollars 1,700, all cif europe. Madagascar cloves unchanged at Dollars 800 cif for shipment. Jamaica pimento Dollars 2,390 fob Jamaica, spot euorope quoted Dollars 2,550. Indian cochin ginger limited stocks in Europe available at Dollars 1,150. New crop position Dec/Jan firmer. Nigerian ginger Dollars 300-Dollars 700, depending on quality.
