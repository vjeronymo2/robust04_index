November 11, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

IN ANOTHER SAN FERNANDO VALLEY LEAGUE GAME:

Harvard 54, St. Genevieve 0 -- Harvard scored early and often at Birmingham High, opening a 40-0 halftime lead en route to clinching third place and a Division VII playoff berth. Kent Dahn led the Saracens (2-8, 2-2), rushing for 128 yards in just four carries, including touchdown runs of 36 and 10 yards.

Quarterback Adam Rowins completed four of 11 for 111 yards, including a 61-yard touchdown pass to Iheanyi Uwaezuake. Rowins also scored on a one-yard run.

Both coaches agreed to play the final half on running time. GERRY GITTELSON
