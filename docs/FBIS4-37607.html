BFN [Text] Rabat, June 22 (MAP) -- King Hassan II of Morocco Tuesday [21 June] afternoon received at the Royal Palace of Skhirat, 20 km south of here, the delegation of the Democratic Republic of Yemen. The delegation is composed of Salim Muhammad Salih, member of the presidency council and assistant secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, Muhsin Abu-Bakr Bin-Farid, vice president of the ministerial council, planning minister and secretary general of the League of the Sons of Yemen and 'Abd-al-'Aziz Dali, president of the political department of external relations of the Yemeni Socialist Party. The audience was attended by Crown Prince Sidi Mohamed, Prince Moulay Rachid, Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs and Cooperation Abdellatif Filali and Minister of State Moulay Ahmed Alaoui.
