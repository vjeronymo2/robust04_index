940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / Letters to the Editor: Use your Euro vote Sir, Much has been written about the question that needs to be asked in any referendum concerning the UK's future within the European Union. But the disenfranchised electorate now has the chance to take part in an unofficial referendum with no questions asked. To pencils, citizens] Vote for your MEP of choice or spoil the ballot paper in protest] Martin Roper, 23 Horniman Drive, Forest Hill, London, SE23 3BJ
