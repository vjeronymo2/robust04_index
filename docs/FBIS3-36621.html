Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladas Burbulis] [Text] Vilnius, 8 Feb -- Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk arrived today on a one-day official visit at the invitation of the Lithuanian president. The Ukrainian delegation consists of temporary acting Prime Minister Yukhym Zuyahilskyy, Nationalities and Migration Minister Vladimir Yemets [name and title as received], Ukrainian National Bank Chairman Viktor Yushchenko and other officials. At the Vilnius airport, decorated with the two countries' national flags, the Ukrainian leader was greeted by President of Lithuania Algirdas Brazauskas, Prime Minister Adolfas Slezevicius, Foreign-Affairs Minister Povilas Gylys and others. After the ceremonial welcome, Leonid Kravchuk departed for the Antakalnskoye cemetery to lay a wreath at the memorial to the defenders of freedom. Kravchuk and Brazauskas will meet one-to-one during the first part of the day, after which the Ukrainian president will address a plenary session of the Seimas and the two heads of state will sign a treaty of friendship and cooperation between the Ukraine and Lithuania.
