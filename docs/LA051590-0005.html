May 15, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BUSH AND TAXES

According to Rep. Jon Kyl(R-Ariz.), "The President feels a lot about taxes the way he feels about broccoli.

You can put it on the table in front of him, but he's not gonna eat it" (Part A, May 10).

Perhaps if Barbara makes him eat all the green stuff like a good boy, the deficit won't appear nearly so red. After all, a Bush is at its best when it is green.

MONROE RUBINGER

Beverly Hills
