BFN [Text] Cape Town April 3 SAPA -- Detectives of the Narcotics Branch in the Southern Cape have confiscated the largest haul of LSD ever recovered in the area, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reports. Acting on information received, the team, led by Capt Gordon Lamstra, raided a house in Long Ships Road, Plettenberg Bay, where the LSD and dagga [marijuana] valued at more than R[Rand]3,000 was found. A Johannesburg man, Mr Derek John Nel, was arrested and charged with dealing in drugs. He was released on R1,000 bail.
