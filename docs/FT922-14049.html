920408 FT 08 APR 92 / UK Company News: FBD Profits before tax of FBD Holdings, the Dublin-based insurance company which is quoted on the USM, rose by 15 per cent to IPounds 9.33m (Pounds 8.7m) for 1991. The improvement was struck on an 18 per cent increase in turnover to IPounds 74.4m. Earnings emerged at 13.02p (11p) and a final dividend of 2p makes a 3.6p (3p) total.
