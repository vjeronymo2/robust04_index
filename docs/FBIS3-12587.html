Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres said this evening that Israel will agree to discuss new PLO requests, provided they do not contradict the Declaration of Principles [DOP]. Speaking at a conference on Jewish communications in Jerusalem, Peres noted that the deputy Russian foreign minister and the Greek foreign minister, arrived in Israel today from Tunis. They brought a message from the PLO leadership stating it is very important that Israel realize these are indeed conditions for the resumption of the negotiations. Minister Peres clarified that Israel had accepted some of the conditions, including the release of prisoners. Israel agrees that the UN Security Council resolution on the Hebron massacre include a temporary placement of foreign presence in Jericho and Gaza, in accordance with the DOP. We are also willing to discuss the Palestinian request that the autonomy police force be larger, Peres said. Our correspondent Amy Rhodes filed this report.
