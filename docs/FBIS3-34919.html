Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report entitled "Beware of the U.S. Ambassador"] [Text] U.S. Ambassador Mark Hambley has been holding weekly meetings with Lebanese journalists at the U.S. Embassy in 'Awkar. At these meetings, Hambley leaks false reports to the journalists on U.S. policy in Lebanon and the region so that these reports will be published in the Lebanese press and lead to confusion in the Lebanese political club [al-nadi al-siyasi al-lubnani], which is at a "crossroads" with the new ambassador.
