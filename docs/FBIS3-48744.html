Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Text] Malaysia has reacted positively to Indonesia's desire to settle the dispute over Sipadan and Ligitan more quickly. Izhar Ibrahim, Indonesia's chief delegate to the Kuala Lumpur talks on the dispute, said there seemed to be progress in the talks because Malaysia had reacted positively to Indonesia's hope for a speedier settlement. During the talks, which started on Wednesday, Malaysia also responded to Indonesia's statement on the ownership of the islands made during the earlier talks in July 1992.
