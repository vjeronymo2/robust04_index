Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vilnius, Jan 12, BNS - Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas pursued only one aim by asking for NATO membership -- he wanted to take away a trump card from the rightist opposition, which sharply criticized the administration for delaying an official request, the LIETUVOS RYTAS newspaper said in its editorial Wednesday [12 January]. The newspaper said key issues in foreign policy are used only as a "tool for internal struggle" and a lacks a clear attitude toward this or that problem. LIETUVOS RYTAS noted, however, that the international community heard Lithuania's name once again after a considerable break. "A majority of politicians consider the step premature, but they realized that Lithuania's aspiration is sincere -- to be safe on a dangerous crossing in Eastern Europe."
