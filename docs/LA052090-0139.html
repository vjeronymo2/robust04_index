May 20, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

Police, continuing a crackdown in the San Diego Sports Arena area, arrested 33 men late Friday and early Saturday morning on suspicion of soliciting for prostitution.

Police Department spokesman Bill Robinson said the operation brings to 211 the number of men arrested there in recent weeks.

In addition, three women were arrested, he said.

Among the men cited were college students, businessmen and a NASA engineer, Robinson said.
