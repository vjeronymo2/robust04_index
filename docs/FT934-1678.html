931217 FT 17 DEC 93 / Observer: Home truths Well done Diane Abbott, MP for Hackney North and Stoke Newington, for saying something that needed saying. 'Economics is a happy hunting ground for charlatans. This is partly because it is dominated by men - famously the less practical sex,' she informed the Treasury and Civil Service committee examining the role of the Bank of England. 'The aridity of the subject (economics), the opacity of the concepts, and the unintelligibility of the jargon all combine to cow otherwise healthily sceptical observers.' What a sensible person.
