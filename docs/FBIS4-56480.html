BFN [Excerpt] San Cristobal de las Casas (Mexico), 3 May (EFE) -- Today, Peace and Reconciliation Commissioner Manuel Camacho Solis said the site and date for a new round of talks with the Zapatist National Liberation Front (EZLN) will be announced tomorrow. Camacho Solis came here from the state capital, and he told journalists he will announce the resumption of negotiations with the EZLN tomorrow. The media reported he later met privately with Bishop Samuel Ruiz Garcia and members of the diocese to discuss the mechanism for the resumption of talks. The announcement of the date and site for the resumption talks between the commissioner and the EZLN will come one month after the conclusion of the first round of talks. [passage omitted]
