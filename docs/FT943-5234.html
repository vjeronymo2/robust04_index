940905 FT 05 SEP 94 / Emerging Markets: News round-up - Poland Investors oversubscribed the public offering of the Polish treasury's 48 per cent stake in tyre maker Debica, the privatisation ministry said. The initial results of the offer did not reveal the extent of the oversubscription, or the final issue price for the shares which were offered at a minimum 160,000 zlotys apiece.
