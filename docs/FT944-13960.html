941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports improved general demand. Good liquoring Assams sold well at fully firm rates, while plainer sorts lost several pence. Bright East Africans were strong and often advanced 3 to 8p. Coloury mediums were fully firm but plainer sorts tended lower. Ceylons lost 3 to 5p. Offshore cif/fob fair demand. Kenyas were lower but Ugandas and Assams were steady. Quotations: best available 200p/kg. nom, good 140p/kg., good medium 124p/kg., medium 112p/kg., low medium 84p/kg., The highest price realised this week was 205p for an Assam pf.
