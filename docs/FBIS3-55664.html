Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 10 Jan -- The first session of the inter-departmental commission of the Russian Security Council for economic security took place today. By decree of the Russian president, Andrey Zverev, head of the economic department of the Russian Federation governmental apparatus, has been appointed the commission's chairman. Questions connected with working out a state doctrine of economic security, the progress of the economic reforms in the country, and other matters were examined. Heads of ministries and departments reepresented in the commission took part in the session.
