BFN [Text] A ceremony for signing agreements between the Governments of the Union of Myanmar [Burma] and the Socialist Republic of Vietnam was held at 1715 today at Hall D of the Seinle Kantha Guest House. U Myo Min, director general of the Directorate of Hotel and Tourism Industry and Mr. Do Quang Trung, chairman of the Vietnam State Tourism General Department signed the agreement on cooperation in tourism between the governments of the Union of Myanmar and the SRV and exchanged the signed documents. U Aung Thaung, deputy trade minister and the SRV deputy minister of trade signed the agreement on trade between the governments of the Union of Myanmar and the SRV and exchanged the signed documents. Foreign Minister U Ohn Gyaw and SRV Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam signed the agreement to establish a joint commission on cooperation between the governments of the Union of Myanmar and the SRV and exchanged the signed documents. The signing ceremony ended after the two foreign ministers raised a toast to lasting bilateral friendship.
