930417 FT 17 APR 93 / Finance and the Family: New Barclays mortgage - At a glance Barclays bank is replacing its 7.99 per cent fixed rate mortgage, fixed until January 1998, with a mortgage fixed at the same rate of 7.99 per cent (8.4 APR) but for a shorter term - until the end of April 1997. There is a booking fee of Pounds 100 and an arrangement fee of Pounds 150, which is waived if an endowment, pension or Barclays Lifestages policy is bought through the bank.
