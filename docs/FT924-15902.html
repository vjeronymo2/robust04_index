921002 FT 02 OCT 92 / 3i Group to close regional offices 3i GROUP, the venture capital company which is preparing for a stock market flotation next year, is to close nearly a quarter of its regional offices and make 100 staff redundant. It will shut five of its 22 offices outside London - Brighton, Guildford, Milton Keynes, Hull and Sheffield - in response to changing economic patterns and improved motorway communications, Mr Ewen Macpherson, chief executive, said.
