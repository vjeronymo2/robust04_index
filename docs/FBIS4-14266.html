BFN [Text] The ambassadors of 11 countries currently on a four-day visit to Kashmir were yesterday briefed in Srinagar by senior Army officers about the Pak-sponsored militant activities in the valley. The ambassadors were told that the militants indulged in killing of innocent people, burned government and private property, and also misbehaved with women. Ambassadors of Peru, Egypt, Morocco, Tunisia, Kazakhstan, Kuwait, Iran, Argentina, Brazil, Jordan, and Oman were shown the arms and ammunition seized by the security forces during their anti-militant operations. The ambassadors also met some foreign mercenaries who admitted that they were misled by false propaganda that a lot of atrocities were being committed on the Muslim population of the valley of Kashmir but when they came there they found that nothing of this sort was happening there. The militants said they were disappointed now. The ambassadors saw some wounded militants being treated by Army doctors. The visiting ambassadors today called on the Jammu and Kashmir governor, Mr. K.V. Krishna Rao, at the Raj Bhavan.
