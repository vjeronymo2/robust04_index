Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto arrived today in Muscat on an official three-day visit to Oman. During the visit she will meet with His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id and senior government officials for talks on bolstering cooperation between the two countries in all fields. Leading those who were on hand to receive the Pakistani prime minister was Thuwayni Bin-Shihab Al Sa'id, his majesty the sultan's personal representative. [passage omitted]
