Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Decree of the Supreme Soviet of the Ukraine On Putting Into Effect the ``Fundamentals of Health Care Legislation of the Ukraine,'' signed by I. Plyushch, chairman of the Ukrainian Supreme Soviet, on 19 Nov 92] [Text]The Supreme Soviet of the Ukraine hereby decrees: 1. To put into effect the Fundamentals of Health Care Legislation of the Ukraine as of the day of their publication. 2. To establish that existing legislative acts of the Ukraine apply insofar as they are not in contradiction to these Fundamentals until Ukrainian legislation is brought into line with the Fundamentals of health care legislation of the Ukraine. 3. The Ukrainian Cabinet of Ministers is to: --submit to the Supreme Soviet of the Ukraine suggestions on putting Ukrainian legislative acts into line with the Fundamentals of Health care legislation of the Ukraine before 1 February 1993; --adopt enforceable enactments on application of the Fundamentals of health care legislation of the Ukraine, referred by said Fundamentals to the purview of the Ukrainian Cabinet of Ministers, before 1 November 1993. --bring other decisions of the Ukrainian Government into line with the Fundamentals of Health Care Legislation of the Ukraine and implement addition of appropriate amendments to enforceable enactments of ministries, departments and other State administrative central agencies of the Ukraine before 1 November 1994; --elaborate and adopt scientifically validated norms for funding health care. 4. The Commission for Public Health Affairs of the Ukrainian Supreme Soviet is to implement monitoring of execution of this Decree.
