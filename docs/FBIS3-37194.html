Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] On 14 February, the Belarusian Defense Ministry issued a declaration regarding the commentary on Defense Minister Kozlovskiy's visit to a military unit in Maryina Gorka. The commentary appeared on the "Step" program and was broadcast on Belarusian television on 12 February. According to the program, the defense minister's planned meeting with personnel of the unit was aimed at instructing soldiers on how to fight strikers. The declaration stresses that the Defense Ministry is not going to change the existing training schedules so that they comply with the current political situation, adding that nobody can order soldiers out of their barracks to take part in political adventures.
