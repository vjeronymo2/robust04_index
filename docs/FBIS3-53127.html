Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] A group of Palestinian entrepreneurs calling itself Falastiyah announced in Jerusalem today a huge building plan to set up 13 towns in the West Bank and the Gaza Strip at a cost of $30 million. The entrepreneurs told a Jerusalem news conference that they intend to start work soon. The project is to build 10 towns on the West Bank, with 50,000 residents each, and another three in the Gaza Strip, with 250,000 inhabitants each. Our economic affairs correspondent Menahem Friedman reports that the investors plan to convene an international conference in Jerusalem in April to be attended by official representatives from the United States and Russia, as well as Yasir 'Arafat, and Israel's prime minister and cabinet ministers, in order to disclose the sources which will fund the project and to recruit investors and donors.
