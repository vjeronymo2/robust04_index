BFN [Text] The president of Malawi, Hastings Banda, facing challenges from three presidential contenders in multiparty elections on Tuesday, says that his work as the country's ruler is not yet finished. Speaking on state radio, he said that Malawi needed an experienced leader and politicians of ability, honor and good reputation. Accused of being responsible for serious human rights abuses during his three decades in power, he said that he would respect human rights and uphold good and accountable government.
