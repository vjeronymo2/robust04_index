Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanaa, 29 Mar (INA)--A source in the Yemeni Reform Grouping, a party to the Yemeni coalition, has denied the reports that 22 of its cadres were killed in the governorate of Shabwah in eastern Yemen in recent clashes with forces loyal to the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP]. In statements to the INA correspondent in Sanaa reported today, the source asserted that these reports are untrue because no clashes erupted between the Reform Grouping and any other party in this governorate.
