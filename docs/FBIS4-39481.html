BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko and Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev 8 June TASS -- Prime Minister of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam Vo Van Kiet arrived here for an official visit today. The "Borispol" airport was decked out with Vietnamese and Ukrainian flags in honour of the distinguished guest. A guard of honour was lined up and the anthems of the two countries were played. The head of the Vietnamese Government will be received by Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk, First Vice-Speaker of the Ukrainian Supreme Soviet Aleksandr Tkachenko, and Acting Prime Minister Yefim Zvyagilskiy. The sides are expected to sign some Ukrainian-Vietnamese documents and to expand mutually advantageous cooperation. The Vietnamese premier will fly to St. Petersburg on 10 June.
