940905 FT 05 SEP 94 / Observer: Lumbered They will soon be humming 'I'm a lumberjack and I'm OK' in the forests of Arkhangelsk in northern Russia. The Yarensky logging company has found an unorthodox way round paying its hardworking lumberjacks. After a year without salaries, the Itar-Tass news agency reports that the brawny loggers will be paid in what is, for Russians, a rare commodity - tampons.
