BFN [Text] The cabinet headed by Prime Minister Edem Kodjo held a meeting yesterday afternoon at the Entente Palace, seat of the government. No statement was issued at the end of the meeting. In addition to the prime minister, 18 ministers and one secretary of state make up the Kodjo cabinet. After the composition of the cabinet was announced, the French foreign minister stated yesterday that he acknowledges Togo now has a cabinet. France expressed the hope that this cabinet, formed one month after the appointment of the prime minister, will urgently take measures to revive Togo's development in a spirit of union called for by the present circumstances.
