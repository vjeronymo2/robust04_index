940824 FT 24 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Eaglet Inv net asset value at 110.72p Eaglet Investment Trust, launched in July last year, reported net asset value per share of 110.72p at June 30. The diluted figure was 109.32p. The trust, which invests in smaller companies, said that since December 31, when it was substantially invested, its increase in net asset value had exceeded the Extended HGSC Index by 7.7 per cent. Net revenue for the 51 weeks to end-June was Pounds 374,000 for earnings per share of 1.68p. A single final dividend of 1.4p is recommended.
