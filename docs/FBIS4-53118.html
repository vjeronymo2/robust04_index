BFN [Text] The government of Yemen has agreed to waive the issuance of exit visas for Filipino contract workers who will be repatriated from Sanaa. Foreign Affairs Secretary Roberto Romulo said this is with the understanding that the respective employers will certify their allowing their workers to leave. In these countries, as a matter policy, require foreign employees will get out of their territories to secure exit visas. [sentence as heard] The government is now faced with funding problems with the evacuation and repatriation of Filipino workers in Yemen. Foreign Affairs Secretary Roberto Romulo says that they are now looking for other resources to fund the repatriation of Filipino workers.
