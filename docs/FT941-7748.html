940223 FT 23 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Edinburgh New Tiger Trust Applications for the offer for subscription of 280m shares in Edinburgh New Tiger Trust were received in respect of 290.2m ordinary, with warrants attached. The result included applications for some 210m shares from those who had given irrevocable undertakings. As a consequence the balance of the offer was oversubscribed by 14.5 per cent. The basis of allocation was: For the first 20,000 shares - 100 per cent of those applied for; for the next 30,000 - 80 per cent; for the next 150,000 - 60 per cent; for additional shares - 53.4 per cent. The issue will raise some Pounds 134.5m net of expenses.
