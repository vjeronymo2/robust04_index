September 13, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Judge Denies Neil Bush's Request: Administrative Law Judge Daniel J. Davidson has denied Neil Bush's request to dismiss the government's case against him for alleged conflict of interest in his role as a former outside director of Denver-based Silverado Banking, Savings & Loan Assn. The judge rejected Bush's argument in his one-page order, saying it is not supported by existing regulations and case law.
