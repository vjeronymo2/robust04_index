931129 FT 29 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Fulcrum Inv Trust Net asset value per capital share of Fulcrum Investment Trust stood at 63.12p at the half year ended October 31. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 497,467 for earnings per income share of 4.43p. A second interim dividend of 1.4p makes a total for the year to date of 2.8p.
