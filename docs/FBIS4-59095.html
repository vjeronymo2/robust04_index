BFN [Text] The House of Representatives met in an ordinary session this morning under brother Muhammad al-Khadim al-Wajih, member of the House's Speakership Council. The meeting was held in the context of the third round of the second phase of the council's first term. At the outset of the session, the deputies reviewed and ratified the minutes of the previous meeting. They then discussed oil issues in terms of finance, administration, accounting, and consumption. The House referred the matter to its Financial Affairs and Economic Subcommittees for scrutiny and to collect data and information before submitting a detailed report. Today's session was attended by brother 'Adil Khurshid, undersecretary at the Ministry of Oil and Mineral Resources, and 'Askar 'Ali al-Dahiri, chairman of the Technical and Consultative Office at the ministry.
