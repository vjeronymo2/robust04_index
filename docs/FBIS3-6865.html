Language: Thai Article Type:BFN [Text] His Royal Signature, given on 19 March 1994, being the 49th year under his reign, His Majesty King Phumiphon Adunyadet has commanded that it be announced that for the interests of the state it is deemed necessary and appropriate to convene an extraordinary session of the Parliament. Therefore, empowered by Articles 130, 132, and 175 of the Constitution of the Kingdom of Thailand, he has graciously issued a decree to convene the extraordinary parliamentary session from 31 March 1994. Countersigned by Prime Minister Chuan Likphai
