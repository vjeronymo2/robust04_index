Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Hanoi, Jan 11 (AFP) -- Vietnam is prepared to hold talks with the United States about "certain problems" concerning human rights, a Vietnamese foreign ministry spokesman said Tuesday. "Vietnam has told the United States that it is prepared to hold talks with the United States and other countries on problems of mutual concern, including human rights, on a basis of equality, mutual respect and non-interference in the internal affairs of each country," he said.
