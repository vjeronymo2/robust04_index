Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 4 (XINHUA) -- Myanmar [Burma] Ambassador to China, U Set and his wife hosted a reception here at noon today to mark the 46th anniversary of the independence day of the union of Myanmar. Vice-Chairman of the Standing Committee of the Chinese National People's Congress Bu He, President of the Chinese People's Association for Friendship With Foreign Countries Han Xu, and Vice- Foreign Minister Tang Jiaxuan were among some 100 people who attended the reception.
