BFN [From 30 May RAHVA HAAL, p.1; "News Bulletin" No. 106/94 ] [Text] Estonian Prime Minister Mart Laar and Slovenian Prime Minister Janez Drnovshek signed a declaration to begin free trade negotiations. Estonia has already started such talks with Poland, Hungary and Czech Republic. According to Laar Slovenia may become a "gate to former Yugoslavia, also to Italy". Laar remarked on the similarity of security problems in Estonia and Slovenia and mentions the possibility of defence cooperation. Estonia has appointed Sven Jurgenson for its charge d'affaires in Slovenia. Slovenian Prime Minister had a positive attitude to Estonia's suggestion to enact visa-free travel.
