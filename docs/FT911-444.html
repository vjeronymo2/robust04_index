910511 FT 11 MAY 91 / Three-year Oslo growth plan Norway's minority Labour government unveiled yesterday a three year NKr10bn (Pounds 870m) infrastructure plan to revive economic growth in the country's mainland private industries as part of its revised budget, Robert Taylor writes from Stockholm. Higher priority is to be given to the creation of jobs, improved social benefits for children and young people, and cost-effective measures to clean up the environment.
