940714 FT 14 JUL 94 / World Cup '94: Goals bring joy to weary people In Brazil as elsewhere, the weary people need a glimpse of happiness. Goals give them that. Recent research has shown that factory workers are much more productive the day after a Brazilian victory. Should Brazil win the World Cup, the country will close for at least one day of celebration. If it loses, experts say, there will be an increase in suicides. Brazilian players do not consider this a burden. They are protected by their cockiness - so long as they do not lose. After Brazil's 3-2 quarter-final win against Holland, Romario told reporters: 'You witnessed art today,' while Brazil manager Carlos Alberto Parreira said: 'The last 34 minutes provided some of the most exciting, dramatic moments of the World Cup.'
