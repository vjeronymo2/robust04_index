Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 14 (XINHUA) -- Wei Jianxing, member of the Political Bureau of the Chinese Communist Party (CPC) Central Committee and member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, met with a delegation from Malian National Congress of the Initiative Democracy here today. The Malian guests arrived here last Saturday [12 March] at the invitation of the International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Committee.
