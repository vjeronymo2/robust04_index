July 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CHINA PROPAGANDA?

The blood hasn't dried on Tian An Men Square and you already beat the drum for renewed tourist trade for China ("In Wake of Turmoil, China Still Holds Surprises," July 16). Elizabeth Mehren's thinly disguised propaganda is not far from disgusting. I know Guangzhou, which is, to put it mildly, an unremarkable city. What's the hurry to invite Americans and their dollars back to visit that or any other part of China?

FRANK DESSAYER

Glendale
