930527 FT 27 MAY 93 / International Company News: Robert Fleming teams up with SA broker MARTIN & Co, South Africa's top stockbroking firm, is teaming up with Robert Fleming, the UK merchant bank, in a partnership aimed at developing their international mining brokerage business. Robert Fleming will buy from McIntosh Securities, an Australian broker, its half-stake in McIntosh Martin, a venture with Martin & Co. When the deal is completed, McIntosh Martin, which trades in South African securities held by non-residents, will be renamed Copthall Martin. Robert Fleming will support Martin & Co in widening the scope of the joint venture.
