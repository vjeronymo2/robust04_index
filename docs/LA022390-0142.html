February 23, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

POLL REFEREES: Rep. Robert K. Dornan, a veteran monitor of seven foreign elections, says independent observers can make or break public confidence in election results. . . . In Panama and in the Philippines, U.S. observers' reports of widespread fraud led to governments' being overturned, he noted. Now awaiting permission to enter Nicaragua for Sunday's elections, Dornan says: "The only thing more enjoyable than voting yourself is to watch other people vote when it gives them freedom for the first time."A16 Dana Parsons and Kristina Lindgren
