Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] A representative of Georgia's Defense Ministry called reports that forces are being concentrated in military camps around Tbilisi blatant disinformation designed to discredit the Defense Ministry. The Security Ministry also denied reports that the main bulk of its forces were being concentrated in Tbilisi. The Security Ministry is not ready to comment on the incidents in Tbilisi on 24 and 25 December before the state commission investigating them, headed by Prime Minister Patsatsia, has finished its work.
