BFN [Text] Luxor, 6 Apr (MENA)--Mahjub Husayn Muhammad, the hijacker of the Sudanese airliner, has stated that he chose to seek political asylum in Egypt because it is a safe and dear country. He said that the political, economic, and social situation in Sudan is deteriorating. The hijacked Sudanese airliner landed at Luxor airport this morning and the hijacker surrendered to the Egyptian authorities.
