Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Kurbanova] [Text] Ashgabat February 16 TASS -- The interior ministers of the 12 CIS member-states and Latvia met today in the Turkmen capital Ashgabat to discuss further cooperation and coordination of activities in the sphere of law and order enforcement. Participants in the meeting are to discuss the structure, staff and budget of the coordinating bureau for fighting organized crime, cooperation in escorting valuable and special cargoes on the territory of CIS states, and a procedure for a transit and hand-over of criminals taken into custody. During their two-day meeting, the interior ministers are also expected to sign bilateral interdepartmental agreements on cooperation.
