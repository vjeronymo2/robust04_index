September 8, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: SAN CLEMENTE;

AGENCY URGES PUBLIC TO ATTEND MEETING

Citizens interested in learning about the proposed Foothill Transportation Corridor that could run through the city can attend a public meeting Monday.

The Transportation Corridor Agencies meeting will be held at 7 p.m. in the Community Center, 100 N. Calle Seville.

"This meeting is an opportunity for residents to learn more about the corridor's proposed alignments through San Clemente," said Councilwoman Holly Veale, who is the council's representative on the Transportation Corridor Agencies. "This meeting is an opportunity for the citizens of San Clemente to be part of the planning process."

By voicing opinions, the public can help the agency "reach an informed decision," she said.

Further information can be obtained by contacting Laura Coley Eisenberg, associate environmental analyst for the Transportation Corridor Agencies, at (714) 557-3298, Ext. 326. FRANK MESSINA
