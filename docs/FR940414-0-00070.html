ACTION: Technical corrections to FAC 90&hyph;20.

SUMMARY: The Civilian Agency Acquisition Council and the Defense Acquisition Regulations Council are correcting a notice and several interim and final rules as part of Federal Acquisition Circular 90&hyph;20. The corrected documents were published on March 10, 1994, at 59 FR 11368, 11371, 11374, 11375, 11376, 11378, 11380, 11386 and 11387. The corrections read as follows: 1.
