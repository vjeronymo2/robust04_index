920214 FT 14 FEB 92 / International Company News: Northrop to cut 2,000 jobs NORTHROP, the US defence contractor, is to cut 2,000 jobs from its aircraft division this year, reducing the total number of employees to below 10,000, writes Karen Zagor. THe California-based company said about half of the jobs would be lost through normal attrition and retirements. Northrop, has been heavily dependent on the B-2 Stealth bomber programme, which is to be scaled back.
