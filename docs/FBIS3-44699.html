Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bangkok, January 7 (XINHUA) -- Gen Liu Huaqing, vice chairman of the Chinese Central Military Commission, reiterated here today that China follows a good neighborly policy, never seeking hegemony and being opposed to power politics. During a meeting with Thai Deputy Defense Minister Maj Gen Sombat Rotphonthong, Liu said that China stands for settlement of disputes through peaceful means and dialogue. Welcoming Liu, who is on a six-day visit to Thailand, Sombat said his visit has great significance in furthering the ties between the peoples and armed forces of the two countries. On a separate occasion today, Liu met with Thai supreme commander air chief Admiral Woranat Aphichari. Woranat said the armed forces of the two countries would develop their traditional friendship, increase exchange and learn from each other to the benefit of both sides.
