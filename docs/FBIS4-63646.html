BFN [Text] Ukrainian Parliament leader Oleksandr Moroz received Simon Hemans, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of Great Britain. The ambassador informed the Supreme Council chairman of tomorrow's visit to Ukraine by Douglas Hurd, the British secretary of state for foreign affairs, and expressed his request in respect of certain aspects concerning Hurd's meeting with the Ukrainian parliament leader. Touching upon the Crimean issue in the conversation, Hemans stressed that Great Britain's position on the issue remains unchanged. It recognizes Ukraine as a single integral state and supports the inviolability of its borders.
