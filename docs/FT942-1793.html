940622 FT 22 JUN 94 / International Company News: Malaysian car group declines Perusahaan Otomobil Nasional, manufacturer of Malaysia's Proton car, has announced pre-tax profits of MDollars 282m (USDollars 108m) for the year ended March, a 9.3 per cent drop on the previous year. While group revenues rose 35 per cent to MDollars 3.09bn, net earnings declined by 6.8 per cent to MDollars 247m. Earnings per share fell to 48.8 Malaysian cents. The Proton is manufactured in co-operation with Mitsubishi Motors of Japan. Proton said that the decline in pre-tax profit was mainly due to a drop in investment income and the rise of the Japanese yen.
