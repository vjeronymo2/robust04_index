930205 FT 05 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Burlington net assets higher at 18.13p Burlington Group, the investment company, reported net assets of 18.13p per share at December 31, against 16.87p a year earlier. Net revenue for 1992 was Pounds 150,000 (Pounds 90,000) for earnings per share of 1.01p (0.61p). Directors are proposing an unchanged single dividend of 0.5p.
