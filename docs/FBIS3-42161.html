Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 25 Jan (MENA) -- An official in the Supreme State Security Prosecution has stated that the security agencies succeeded in capturing a number of automatic rifles and explosives in an arms cache in the Hulwan district on information provided by terrorist Muhammad Ahmad Farhan, who was arrested during the al-Ma'sarah incidents last week. The captured material was sent to the criminology laboratory for examination and to prepare a report on its condition and date of manufacture. The prosecution ordered the cache sealed after a thorough search by the security agencies. Meanwhile, the Supreme State Security Court continues its interrogation of Farhan in the Qasr al-'Ayni hospital for the sixth consecutive day.
