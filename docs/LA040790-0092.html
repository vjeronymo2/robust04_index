April 7, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

JAMES E. PERKINS; EPIDEMIOLOGIST AND TB ASSN. OFFICIAL

James E. Perkins, 84, former managing director of the National Tuberculosis Assn., now the American Lung Assn.

The epidemiologist was part of the first delegation to the World Health Organization formed after World War II and worked for the New York State Health Department as commissioner and director of communicable diseases before joining the tuberculosis association. During World War II he was a senior surgeon with the Public Health Service Reserve. In Laguna Hills, Calif., on March 25.
