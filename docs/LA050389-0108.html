May 3, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: SANTA ANA;

WEEKEND SHOOTING TAKES SECOND LIFE

A second man has died as a result of a weekend shooting that started as an argument over drugs, police said Tuesday.

Jose Rodriguez Nambo, 20, of Santa Ana died Tuesday morning at the United Western Medical Center-Anaheim from wounds suffered when he and another man where shot about 3 a.m. Saturday during an argument over drugs, Maureen Thomas, a Santa Ana police spokeswoman said Tuesday.

Nambo and Marciano Garcia, 21, were shot in the 1200 block of W. Highland St. Garcia, shot twice was pronounced dead at the scene. He lived in Santa Ana.

Police said they still have no suspects in the shooting.
