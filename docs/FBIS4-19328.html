BFN [Unattributed report: "Rwanda: Evacuation of Italians Concluded"] [Text] (ANSA) Rome, April 15 -- The military operation to evacuate almost 200 Italians from war-torn Rwanda concluded Friday [15 April], when 133 Italian troops left the capital Kigali and returned to Nairobi, Foreign Ministry sources said. For the past three days the Italians, along with Belgian troops, have controlled Kigali airport as part of the operation to get European and U.S. nationals out of Rwanda.
