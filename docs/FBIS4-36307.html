BFN [Text] Leader President Saddam Husayn, may God watch over him, has ordered an increase in the ration quota of vegetable oil listed on the ration card, as well as the ration quota of vegetable oil supplied to the bearers of central market papers. Trade Minister Dr. Muhammad Mahdi Salih told INA that leader President Saddam Husayn, may God protect him, has ordered the ration quota of vegetable oil listed on the ration card increased to 750 grams per month per person, instead of 625 grams. The president also ordered an increase in the family quota listed in the central market papers to 2 kg of vegetable oil per month instead of 1.5 kg. The order of the leader president, may God watch over him, shall be implemented as of today, 1 June 1994.
