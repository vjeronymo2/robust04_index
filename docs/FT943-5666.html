940902 FT 02 SEP 94 / International Company News: Silvermines Following the acquisition in March of Molynx, the closed-circuit television company, Silvermines, the Dublin-based electrical services and property group, trebled pre-tax profits from IPounds 251,000 to IPounds 761,000 (Pounds 750,000) in the six months to June 30. Turnover jumped from IPounds 12.3m to IPounds 20.2m. Earnings emerged at 1.23p (0.5p) and a 0.25p dividend is declared.
