BFN [Text] Pristina, 11 May (TANJUG) -- Federal Defense Minister Pavle Bulatovic and his aides today spoke in Pristina with the governors of the Kosovo and Metohija districts and with representatives of the military authorities about issues relating to the implementation of the policy and system of defense in Kosovo and Metohija, the Federal Defense Ministry has announced. All issues of relevance to the security system were comprehensively discussed. The governors stressed that there is very wide-ranging and successful cooperation between state authorities and units and command bodies of the Yugoslav Army. Bulatovic said that the political, security, and economic situation in Kosovo and Metohija demands the permanent involvement of the republican and federal governments. The successful cooperation of civilian authorities and units of the Yugoslav Army and organs of the Interior Ministry is of extreme importance, because for the time being this is the only reliable way of keeping the peace in Kosovo and Metohija, the federal defense minister stressed. Minister Bulatovic today observed exercises carried out by units and command bodies of the Third Army and some units of the Serbian Interior Ministry.
