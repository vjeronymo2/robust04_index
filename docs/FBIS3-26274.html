Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The Jinzhou city intermediate people's court respectively held public trial meetings in Jinzhou city, Yixian County, Beizhen, Heishan, and (Linhai) city on the morning of 3 February. Seventeen criminals, including (Tong Xiangli) and (Wu Chunli), were sentenced to death and deprived of their political rights for life. After the meetings, these criminals who are guilty of the most heinous crimes were escorted to the execution grounds and executed by shooting. Another 25 criminals were sentenced to death with a stay of execution, life imprisonment, or to set terms of imprisonment. A major gang of hooligans with (Tong Xiangli) and (Wu Chunli) as prime culprits ran wild for 12 years in Yixian, (Linhai), Jinzhou, and Pancheng areas. They killed persons with weapons, harmed the innocent, engaged themselves in looting, sought hegemony, and raped women. Individually, or in a group, they committed 211 cases of crimes and brought damages to over 200 cadres and people.
