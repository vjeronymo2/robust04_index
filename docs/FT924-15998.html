921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / Romanian president warns of social and economic crisis President Ion Iliescu warned yesterday that Romania faced an economic and social crisis. He called on the country's divided political parties to form a broad-based government of 'national reconciliation'. He said he was prepared to accept any coalition which found consensus within the new parliament following inconclusive results from Sunday's general election. Mr Iliescu looks set for re-election in a presidential run-off on October 11.
