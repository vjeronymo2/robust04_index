Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 29 (KCNA) -- The Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] on January 27 sent a message of greetings to Jorge Arturo Reina, general secretary of the Central Executive Council of the Liberal Party of Honduras, on the victory of his party in the presidential election. Expressing the belief that the friendly relations between the two parties would develop favorably in conformity with the idea of independence, peace and friendship, the message wished the Liberal Party of Honduras success in the work for social justice and consolidation of democracy in the country and the general secretary good health.
