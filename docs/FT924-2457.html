921212 FT 12 DEC 92 / Junior education minister resigns MR Nigel Forman has resigned as a junior education minister for 'personal reasons', it was announced last night. Downing Street and Conservative Central Office refused to elaborate on the reasons. Mr Forman was appointed higher education minister in April. He has been replaced with Mr Timothy Boswell, a senior whip. Before joining the Whips Office, Mr Boswell was an active member of the Conservative academic liaison forum, responsible for fostering links between the party and higher education.
