921110 FT 10 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Smith & Nephew sells SoloPak Smith & Nephew has sold SoloPak, which makes pharmaceuticals for the US market, for Dollars 13.5m (Pounds 8.7m) to SoloPak Pharmaceuticals, a management buy-in vehicle created by Welsh Carson Anderson & Stowe, the New York investment company. Smith announced that it had sold the loss-making company to Ivax Corporation for Dollars 19m in March. However, Mr John Robinson, S&N chief executive, said that the deal fell through when Ivax pulled out.
