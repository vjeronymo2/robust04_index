BFN [Text] Kiev, 28 Jun -- A Cuban delegation led by Vice President of the Council of Ministers Lionel Soto was received by Supreme Council of Ukraine Chairman Oleksandr Moroz. He informed the delegation about the process of forming Ukraine's supreme legislative body and the decisions made to curb the economic crisis and to encourage nation-building in Ukraine. The meeting was attended by Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko, and by Diogenes Hernandez Astorga, the Cuban ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to Ukraine. The Cuban guests were acquainted with the work of the Antonov aircraft production association, where they met with personnel of the machine building military-industrial complex, Conversion Minister Dmytro Chernenko, and the association's designer-general, Petro Balabuyev, and leading specialists.
