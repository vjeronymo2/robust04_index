Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Liberal Democratic Party Chairman Vladimir Zhirinovskiy has called on the Crimean residents to cast their ballots at the coming presidential elections on January 16 in favor of the leader of the Russian Party of the Crimea, Sergey Shuvaynikov. Zhirinovskiy believes that the Russian Party of the Crimea is the only party in the peninsula capable of "sincerely defending the interests of the Crimeans, the interests of the Russian people." "Remember that the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia will stand up for your rights together with the Russian Party of the Crimea. We will protect Russian people wherever they are," Zhirinovskiy said.
