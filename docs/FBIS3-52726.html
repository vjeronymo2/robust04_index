Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres has disclosed that Israel will participate in an international economic conference slated to be held in Amman in the spring. Representatives from regional states and from large U.S. and European economic organizations are to take part in this conference. He added that the conference's aim will to be mobilize capital in a bid to invest it in the region with the advent of the era of peace. Mr. Peres said that Israel has begun preparing detailed programs relating to water desalinization and the construction of roads and harbors to be presented at the conference.
