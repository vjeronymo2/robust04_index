940119 FT 19 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Freeman Shares of Freeman Group jumped 27p to 232p yesterday on news that directors were in discussions which may lead to an offer for the company. The USM-traded group is an insulation, distribution and fabrication specialist. For the half year to June 30 it suffered a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 550,000 to Pounds 357,000 after taking account of exceptional income of Pounds 147,000.
