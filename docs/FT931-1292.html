930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / Observer: No charge A mobile phone racket which has already confused the airwaves over Australia and Sweden has hit London. High tech criminals who have pinched mobiles have found a way of altering the in-built electronic security number so that the bill goes to another user. Salesmen in long coats lined with phones are now touting stolen state-of-the-art equipment around offices in London for Pounds 260 apiece with the promise that they will be good for a year, no charge. Gives Freefone numbers a whole new meaning.
