BFN [SEGODNYA report: "Russian-U.S. Military Exercises at Totsk Training Site Postponed"] [Text] The Russian Federation Defense Ministry has informed the State Duma that the ministry is in receipt of its presidentially endorsed decree on the joint Russian-U.S. exercises at the Totsk training ground. In line with the decree, the Defense Ministry has postponed the exercises and will now conduct talks with the U.S. side on possibly moving back the opening date for the exercises. The Defense Ministry assures that no preparations are being made for the exercises at the Totsk training ground.
