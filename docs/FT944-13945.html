941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto stocks suffered in late trading, closing with broad-based losses as investor fears over interest rates again crept to the fore. The TSE 300 composite index fell 19.11 points to close at 4,266.02 in extremely modest turnover of 39.15m worth CDollars 496.49m. Of 14 sub-indices, only two stayed afloat at the close. Metals and mining dropped 61.33, or 1.44 per cent, to 4,192.94. Alcan Aluminium dropped CDollars 1 to CDollars 36 1/2 in busy trade. Nickel heavyweight Inco fell CDollars 1/2 to CDollars 40 3/8.
