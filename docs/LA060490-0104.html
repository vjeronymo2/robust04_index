June 4, 1990, Monday, Ventura County Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Santa Paula shooting -- An item on Sunday's Week in Review page incorrectly stated that Delfino Enricos Lopez, 43, of Santa Paula was accused in the shooting of a neighbor May 28. Lopez had been arrested on suspicion of murder after the shooting, but he was released Friday after prosecutors said they had insufficient evidence to prove he had committed a crime.
