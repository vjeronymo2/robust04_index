Language: Hungarian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] [Announcer] Prime Minister Peter Boross has ended his visit to Germany end started on his return journey. Gyorgy Bernat reports from Bonn. [Begin Bernat recording] Hungary is to submit its request for admission to the European Union [EU] in April, Peter Boross told Hungarian journalists today in Bonn. This was also discussed during the talks between the Hungarian prime minister and Chancellor Kohl. Later, the head of the German Government said publicly that this was not just a symbolic but a very wise decision indeed. [passage omitted] Peter Boross is to meet Helmut Kohl again soon who will visit Budapest on 15 April at the invitation of the prime minister. [end recording]
