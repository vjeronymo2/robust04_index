BFN [Text] Following the conclusion of his three-day visit to Germany, Foreign Minister Velayati stressed that the Islamic Republic of Iran will continue its efforts toward strengthening regional peace and stability as well as increasing regional and international cooperation to end existing crises in the world. In interviews with SPIEGEL and FRANKFURTER ALLGEMEINE, Velayati said that a new chapter has opened in relations between Iran and Germany. Our country's foreign minister ended his visit to Germany on Wednesday evening and set off for Switzerland to take part in the international seminar of the World Forum Foundation.
