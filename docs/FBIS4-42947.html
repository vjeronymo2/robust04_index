BFN [Text] Trabzon (AA) -- Georgian Minister of Foreign Affairs Aleksandr Chikvaidze has said that there are historic ties of friendship between Turkey and his country and has added: Our relations will develop speedily. Chikvaidze, who arrived in Turkey through the Sarp border crossing this morning to attend the NATO foreign ministers meetings to be held in Istanbul, called on Trabzon Governor Alaaddin Yuksel. In a statement at the meeting, Chikvaidze drew attention to the extensive dimensions of the bilateral social and economic relations and added that talks should continue to further advance them. Yuksel pointed out that Georgia has a significant place in Turkish trade and especially in the trade of the eastern Black Sea region, adding that an extensive business delegation will leave for Georgia soon. Chikvaidze later left for Istanbul by plane.
