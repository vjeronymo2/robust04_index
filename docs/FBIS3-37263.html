Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, 12/02/94 ("RESPUBLICA ARMENIA" daily--NOYAN TAPAN)--During the press conference on February 8 the Ambassador of the USA in Armenia Harry Gilmore was questioned whether the presence of 23 Turkish builders in the U.S. Embassy is appropriate to the current conditions. The Ambassador replied: "According to the decision of the U.S. State Department the reconstruction of all U.S. embassies in the territory of the former USSR is done by the engineering department of the U.S. Army. The main office of this agency in the region is in Ankara. This office is collaborating with a number of private Turkish companies and when the reconstruction of the American Embassy in Yerevan started, the American side invited the experienced Turkish building specialists. Currently about 20 Turkish builders are working at the embassy. I personally made quite a lot of efforts for the Turkish specialists to come to Armenia since I thought this would be a sort of Armenian-Turkish collaboration on the individual level". The ambassador also reported that he is trying to use the Armenian builders as much as possible in the reconstruction, and to use Armenian materials when possible. "I would like to especially stress that Armenian builders are very experienced and I saw myself the Turkish-Armenian collaboration", Harry Gilmore added.
