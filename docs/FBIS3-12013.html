Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Juan Manuel Pereyra from the "First Thing" newscast] [Excerpt] The Permanent Committee of the National Congress has authorized Miguel Montes Garcia to conduct the investigation into the murder of Luis Donaldo Colosio, after granting him leave to step down from his post of magistrate of the Supreme Court of Justice. The PRD [Party of the Democratic Revolution] was skeptical of the magistrate's work and his being able to clarify the murder of the Institutional Revolutionary Party presidential candidate. The PRD has argued Montes is a friend of President Salinas and that does not guarantee the success of his mission. [passage omitted]
