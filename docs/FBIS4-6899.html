BFN [Text] Malaysia has proposed that Brunei sign an avoidance of double taxation agreement and an investement guarantee accord as soon as possible to further enhance bilateral trade. The proposal was made by its foreign minister, Datuk Abdullah Ahmad Badawi, in his speech at the first meeting of the Malaysia-Brunei Joint Commission in Langkawi. He (?noted) ample potential in stepping up bilateral trade between the two countries. He said Malaysia is ever ready to encourage its private sector to share its manpower and financial resources to ensure the success of development projects and joint ventures between the two nations.
