Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] The deadline of the ultimatum to the Bosnian Serbs, fixed for tonight at 0100 [0000 GMT], will not be postponed, a NATO spokesman said during a summit of the defense ministers of France, the United States, the United Kingdom, the Netherlands, and Italy, which is under way at the Aviano military base. The ultimatum still stands. NATO's position has not changed and we have nothing to add to what we already said in the past few days, the spokesman said.
