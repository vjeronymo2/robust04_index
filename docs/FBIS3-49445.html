Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Bileca, 11 Jan (TANJUG) -- The information service of the [Serb] Herzegovina Corps announced this morning that Serb fighters last night repelled a strong attack by the Muslim Army from the direction of Gorazde on the Nekopi area in the municipality of Srbinje (formerly Foca). The same source says that, in addition to light weapons, the Muslims used antiaircraft guns and machine guns, as well as mortars. The Herzegovina Corps headquarters claims that the "green berets" sent a sabotage-terrorist group into the Cajnice region yesterday, which was spotted and destroyed in time. On other parts of the Herzegovina battlefield, only occasional provocations from light weapons were registered yesterday and last night.
