Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Provincial party committee Secretary Yan Haiwang yesterday went to see comrades who had attended the seventh Standing Committee meeting of the Eighth Gansu Provincial People's Congress, which concluded in Lanzhou yesterday morning, including both the provincial people's congress standing committee members and those who attended the meeting as guests. Secretary Yan Haiwang made a speech in which he emphatically called on people's congresses at all levels across the province to seize each and every excellent opportunity to deepen reform, expand opening up, promote development, maintain stability, and successfully further all types of work. Yan Haiwang stated: Since the First Plenary Session of the Eighth Gansu Provincial People's Congress, people's congresses and deputies have successfully armed themselves with the theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics, conscientiously implemented the spirit of the 14th CPC National Congress and the spirit of the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee, and accomplished a lot in respect of stepping up provincewide socialist democracy and legal system building, material and spiritual civilization building, reform, opening up, and economic construction. Yan Haiwang expressed the hope that people's congresses across the province will continually and closely concentrate on improving services for reform, opening up, and economic construction, bring into full play each and every power and function belonging to people's congresses as provided in the constitution, formulate laws and regulations at a faster pace, and improve supervision of both work and major decisions of the provincial people's government, higher people's court, and people's procuratorate. Yan Haiwang also urged people's congress at all levels across the province to improve work and strengthen supervision in accordance with the law. In the end, Yan Haiwang called on party committees at all levels across the province to attach great importance to and strengthen leadership over the people's congress work, support people's congresses in exercising powers in accordance with the law, and give full play to people's congresses' role as legislative organs.
