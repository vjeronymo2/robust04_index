Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Nuclear Safety: EC Agreement With EBRD on Multilateral Fund for East Europe"; as released by Brussels Euroscope EIS Database] [Text] European Commissioners approved proposals for an Agreement with the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development (EBRD) concerning the European Community's contribution to the multilateral Nuclear Safety Account, when they met on October 20. The EC has committed itself to contributing 20 million Ecus to the Nuclear Safety Account, which is managed by the EBRD and which aims to improve nuclear safety in the countries of Central and Eastern Europe, including the former Soviet Union. The draft Council decision is scheduled to appear on the agenda of the Council of Ministers and the European Parliament in December.
