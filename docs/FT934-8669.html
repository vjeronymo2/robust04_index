931113 FT 13 NOV 93 / Haiti petrol warning A lawyer for Haiti's petrol distributors said yesterday his clients would use force if necessary to secure the release of fuel reserves frozen under a UN embargo, Reuters reports from Port au Prince. Shell Co (WI) Ltd and Exxon Corp said they would not comply with orders from a military-backed court to distribute gasoline reserves because that would violate a UN oil embargo imposed on Haiti three weeks ago.
