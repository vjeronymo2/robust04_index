940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Cassidy stages recovery As foreshadowed in January's interim statement, Cassidy Brothers, the USM-traded toymaker and nursery goods manufacturer, staged a strong recovery over the full year to April 30. On turnover ahead 8 per cent to Pounds 6.77m, pre-tax profits jumped 36 per cent, from a depressed Pounds 457,217 to Pounds 620,110. Mr Tom Cassidy, chairman, said orders were currently 14 per cent ahead on a year-on-year basis. 'Nursery products continued to support the performance of traditional toys and valuable export business is being won, particularly in Germany,' he said. The final dividend goes up to 2p, bringing the total for the year to 2.75p (2.4p), covered 2.8 times by earnings of 7.8p (6.11p) per share.
