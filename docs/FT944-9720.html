941114 FT 14 NOV 94 / Company News (This Week): Sony expected to pull out of economic downturn Sony, the consumer electronics maker, is expected to indicate that it is pulling out of the economic downturn when it reports its mid-term results on Thursday. The company has been working to increase its overseas manufacturing to shield itself from the impact of the high yen. Sales growth in Asia, outside of Japan, and the benefits of new products such as wide-screen television are forecast to have a positive impact on profits.
