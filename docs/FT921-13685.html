920120 FT 20 JAN 92 / OFT investigates beer price rises Bass, Courage, Allied-Lyons, Whitbread and Scottish & Newcastle - to explain why beer prices have been rising faster than inflation. Brewers claim the rises were largely due to increases in duty and VAT, which added up to 7p to the price of a pint, and to the costs of complying with government orders to restructure their pub estates.
