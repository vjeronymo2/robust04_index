920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / BNF compensates DEPENDANTS of three British Nuclear Fuels employees who died of cancer have received compensation payments from the company. An award of Pounds 15,243 was made for a Sellafield employee based on a 25 per cent probability that his work contributed to his lung cancer. A second man was awarded a 75 per cent probability payment of Pounds 19,047.
