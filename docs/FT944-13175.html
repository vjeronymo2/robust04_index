941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / International Company News: McInerney A strong performance on the Irish housebuilding side helped McInerney Properties cut first-half losses from IPounds 1.77m to IPounds 465,000 (Pounds 459,486) pre-tax, and the directors expect to announce a return to the black at the year-end. The Dublin-listed housebuilder and property developer reported group turnover 19 per cent ahead at IPounds 15.3m (IPounds 12.9m) for the six months to June 30, with a trading profit of IPounds 143,000, compared with a IPounds 998,000 loss. Losses per share were reduced to 0.7p (2.9p).
