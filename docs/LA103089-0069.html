October 30, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

FRANCE;

MUSLIM TENSION IN TOWN IS REKINDLED

A makeshift Muslim prayer hall in a small southeastern French town has been attacked and strewn with pieces of pork, a meat forbidden by Islam, just two months after the demolition of the local mosque outraged France's 3 million Muslims. The mosque in Charvieu-Chavagneux was bulldozed in mid-August during morning prayers, a move that brought accusations of racism against right-wing Mayor Gerard Dezempte. Dezempte said at the time that the mosque, a condemned building, was accidentally destroyed.
