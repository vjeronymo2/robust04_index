CSO [Text] Patna, Feb. 18 -- AIDS is threatening to assume a menacing proportion in Bihar with 61 HIV positive cases being recorded till last December. Out of the 61 HIV positive cases, 13 have been declared full blown cases of AIDS after the Western Blot test. A senior official of the health department, preparing anonymity, said the maximum number of Elisa as well as Western Blot positive cases have been reported from Patna where 41 HIV and eight Western Blot positive cases had been detected so far.
