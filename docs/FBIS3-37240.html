Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, February 14 (SNARK)--Today the Armenian President Levon Ter-Petrosyan and other official persons: two state ministers, transport minister and head of the National Security Department left for Tbilisi to discuss problems in Georgia-Armenian relations, Press-Service of the Armenian president told SNARK. These days the Armenian Government did strongly-word statement to mention there are forces in Georgia who are interested in solving Georgian economic problems at the expense of neighboring states. Non-official sources told SNARK President Levon Ter-Petrosyan is on the intentions to raise a question of no obstacles goods delivery to Armenia and the safety for the gas pipe-line.
