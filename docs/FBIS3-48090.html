Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 16 (KCNA) -- The president of the National Assembly of People's Power of Cuba, Ricardo Alarcon de Quesada, voiced full support for the policies for national reunification clarified by the great leader President Kim Il-song in his New Year address. In a solidarity message to chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Yang Hyong-sop, he said the Korean people would be always fully supported by the Communist Party, Government and people of Cuba. He expressed the belief that friendship and solidarity between the Cuban and Korean peoples united closely with each other in the common struggle against the imperialists' aggressive moves and for socialist construction would further develop with each passing day.
