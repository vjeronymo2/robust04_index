BFN [Text] Tokyo, June 23 KYODO -- Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa informed U.S. Trade Representative Mickey Kantor by phone on Thursday [23 June] night that he has canceled his planned visit to the United States this weekend for bilateral "framework" trade talks, Foreign Ministry officials said. Kakizawa and Kantor agreed to hold a working-group meeting on the framework talks in the middle of next week in Washington, the officials said. The cancellation was decided because the main opposition Liberal Democratic Party (LDP) submitted a no-confidence motion earlier in the day to the House of Representatives against the cabinet of Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata, they said.
