Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] At the start of the news, I will repeat once again the announcement from the Council of Ministers Press Center: At 2 AM on 8 March, Prime Minister Lyuben Berov suffered a heart attack, following which he was admitted to the Lozenets Hospital of the Military Medical Academy in Sofia. After the doctors had held a consultation, it was decided that it was necessary to admit him for in-patient treatment. It was announced later that Deputy Prime Minister Evgeni Matinchev will fulfill the duties of prime minister while Professor Berov is undergoing medical treatment.
