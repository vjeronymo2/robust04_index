BFN [Text] Budapest, 31 March (MTI) -- Prime Minister of the Russian Federation Viktor Chernomyrdin, arriving Thursday [31 March] morning for a two-day official visit, was received with military honors in front of the Parliament building. Following the ceremony, the Russian prime minister held an hour-long private meeting with his host, Prime Minister Peter Boross. After the meeting, Hungarian and Russian negotiating delegations opened talks under the chairmanship of the two prime ministers. Hungarian Minister of International Economic Relations Bela Kadar and his Russian counterpart, Oleg Davidov, chaired a session of the Hungarian-Russian inter-governmental committee on trade and economic cooperation. Kadar said the main topic discussed was the debt Russia owes Hungary and possible forms of repayment, adding that an agreement has been reached in principle on the means of repayment and possible Hungarian participation in Russian privatization.
