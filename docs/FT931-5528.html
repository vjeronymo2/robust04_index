930305 FT 05 MAR 93 / Observer: For valour The Rugged Cross, Observer's award for bravery, goes to Julian Robins, banking analyst at Barclays' BZW. When all his pundit peers were saying Barclays would hold its dividend, Robins forecast back in November that his employer would cut same. Moreover, unlike some others, he stuck to his guns. If Barclays chairman Andrew Buxton is a charitable sort, he should make sure young Robins gets a bonus for customer service beyond the call of duty.
