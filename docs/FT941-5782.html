940304 FT 04 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Paramount edges ahead to Pounds 273,000 Paramount, the USM-quoted licensed premises operator, announced pre-tax profits just ahead from Pounds 261,000 to Pounds 273,000 for the six months to November 30. The outcome was helped by increased profit from associated undertakings and reduced interest payable. Turnover improved to Pounds 13.6m (Pounds 12m). Earnings per share worked through at 0.41p (0.33p) basic and 0.32p (0.25p) fully diluted.
