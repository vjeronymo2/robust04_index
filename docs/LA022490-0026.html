February 24, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

JACKIE ROBINSON INSPIRED EVERYONE

Let me correct Dick Balos (Feb. 12) on something. Jackie Robinson did not show the way for "his race."

He showed the way for the human race. To limit Jackie Robinson's accomplishments to blacks denigrates the stature of one of the nation's all-time great athletes.

EARL OFARI HUTCHINSON, Inglewood
