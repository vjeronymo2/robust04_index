920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / UK Company News: AB Ports Associated British Ports is buying a 49 per cent stake in Tilbury container terminal for Pounds 5.5m from P&O Containers, which will retain 51 per cent. The companies have also agreed to develop further their Southampton terminal, owned in the same proportions. The terminal will be expanded by 15 acres and three new quay cranes are being installed.
