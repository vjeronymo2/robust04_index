920211 FT 11 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Lloyds trust falls short of Pounds 30m target Lloyds Bank has raised about Pounds 24.5m for its smaller companies investment trust. The amount raised falls short of the maximum Pounds 75m which the trust allowed for, and of the Pounds 30m which was mentioned as a likely target during the trust's marketing. Applications for 24.5m packaged units, at 100p each, were received.
