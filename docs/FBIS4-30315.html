BFN [Text] A delegation of the upper house of the Kingdom of Thailand, led by Speaker Michai Ruchuphan on a visit to our country Korea, laid wreaths at the Taesongsan Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery on 22 June. Yang Hyong-sop, chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly and Yu Ho-chun, member of the Standing Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly, were present. On 22 June, the delegation of the upper house of the Kingdom of Thailand visited Mangyongdae, and inspected the Three Revolutions Memorial Hall and Mansudae Assembly Hall. On 22 June, the guests also watched general acrobatic performances at the Pyongyang Acrobatic Theater.
