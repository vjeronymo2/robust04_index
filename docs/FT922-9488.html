920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / The Queen's Speech: Other measures - Main Points The non-domestic rating bill will allow implementation of non-domestic rate transitional arrangements; the boundary commission bill will set new deadlines for the completion of reviews; the social security bill aims to modernise the social security system; a Welsh language bill will promote the use of Welsh; the Scottish bankruptcy bill will close legislative loopholes; and a Scottish prisoners and criminal proceedings bill is intended to make legal practice conform with that in England and Wales.
