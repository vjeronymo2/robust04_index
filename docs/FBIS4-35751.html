BFN [Text] The minister of state for home, Mr. Rajesh Pilot, says there are distinct signs of improvement in the law and order situation in the Kashmir Valley. Addressing a meeting at Gurez near Kashmir, Mr. Pilot reiterated that the center is ready to hold talks with anyone if it aims to bring back normalcy to Jammu and Kashmir, but he warned that the government will never give up its efforts to crush militant activities and their designs to undermine the nation's unity and integrity.
