940712 FT 12 JUL 94 / Virgin joins Channel link bid Mr Richard Branson's Virgin Group has become a shareholder in London & Continental Railways, one of the four consortia bidding to build the 68-mile high-speed link between London and the Channel tunnel. The winning consortium will run the British end of a joint venture with the French railway SNCF and the Belgian railway SNCB. The venture will operate Eurostar trains through the tunnel between London, Paris and Brussels. The other members of the London & Continental group are consulting engineers Ove Arup and Sir William Halcrow; Bechtel, the US construction group; Sofrerail, French consultants; National Express, the coach company; and cement group Blue Circle.
