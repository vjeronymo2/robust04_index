Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] President Lech Walesa has said that he would bring legal action against the granting of the national television license to PolSat. Before leaving for India this morning, Lech Walesa said that one of the reasons behind the dismissal of Marek Markiewicz from the post of chairman of the National Radio and Television Broadcasting Council was the public signing of the license for PolSat. The president said that he would appoint a new chairman of the Council after his return from India next week. Lech Walesa added that, despite the dismissal of Markiewicz, the Council should carry on working as usual.
