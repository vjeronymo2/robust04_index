940919 FT 19 SEP 94 / UK Company News: OIS decline in line with warning In line with a warning in July OIS International Inspection reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 60,000 for the first half of 1994, against Pounds 1.44m. Mr James Mayne, chairman, said action had been taken to cut costs. The shares lost 1/2 p to 25 1/2 p, compared with a fall of 18p to 36p following the warning and a price of 50p at flotation at the end of 1992. Turnover for the testing company fell from Pounds 24.2m to Pounds 19.6m. Earnings per share came out at 0.2p (3.4p) and the interim dividend is 0.5p (0.7p).
