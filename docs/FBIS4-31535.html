BFN [Text] Mate Granic, Croatian deputy prime minister and foreign minister, has received in Zagreb Ejup Ganic, a member of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Presidency and vice president of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Federation. Ejup Ganic informed Minister Granic about the Geneva peace talks, which resulted in a one-month cease-fire in Bosnia-Herzegovina. He stressed that representatives of the Bosniak and Croat nations in the Federation's delegation coordinated their activities during the talks. Ganic expressed thanks for the great assistance of the Croatian mission at the UN in Geneva. Minister Granic told his guest about the steps Croatia will undertake in order to peacefully reintegrate its occupied areas into the constitutional and legal system of the Republic of Croatia.
