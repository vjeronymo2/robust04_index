940107 FT 07 JAN 94 / International Company News: Malaysia publisher steady The New Straits Times Press, Malaysia's largest newspaper and publishing group, announced pre-tax profits for the year ended August 31 1993 of MDollars 92.2m (Dollars 35.5m), a marginal increase on the previous year's MDollars 92m, writes Kieran Cooke in Kuala Lumpur. Group turnover improved 11 per cent to MDollars 422.6m. The company said higher taxation costs and depreciation on capital expenditure from new printing facilities kept the increase small.
