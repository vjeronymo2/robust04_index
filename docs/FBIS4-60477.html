BFN [Text] Mashhad, Khorasan Prov., May 22, IRNA -- The government was paying some 6,000 billion rials in subsidies on essential goods in the current year, Interior Minister 'Ali Mohammed Besharati announced here Sunday. Speaking at a country wide gathering of political and security deputy governors general, Besharati added that some 2,000 billion rials of subsidies has been allocated to bread and some 600 billion rials for pharmaceutics. The two day seminar is to deal with security along the borders and drug trafficking. In related news, the public relations office of the plan and budget organization announced today that the government was paying over 20,000 billion rials in subsidies on energy.
