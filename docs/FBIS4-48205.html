BFN [Excerpt] The Ghana Exports Promotion Council [GEPC] and the Burkinabe National External Trade Board [ONCEB] have signed a draft cooperation agreement. From now on, the institutional relations between the ONCEB and the GEPC will be governed by the terms of this draft agreement according to which the two countries have agreed to consult each other at least once a year to establish and strengthen increased trade contacts between them and to facilitate the participation of their businessmen in trade fairs, exhibitions, and all other commercial activities organized in each of the two countries.
