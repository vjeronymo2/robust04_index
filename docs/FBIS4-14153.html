BFN [Text] A statement by the security services today announced that Moulay Tahar, a 45-year-old doctor working in the capital, was the victim of a terrorist attack on 26 April at about 1115 hours. The source said that Moulay was wounded after two armed people stormed his office located in a clinic at Makaria in the municipality of Hussein Dey. A 16-year-old student at Sadadou Mourad secondary school was also the subject of a terrorist attack yesterday at Meftah, in the province of Blida, south of the capital. The security services' statement said that the victim, who was attacked by a group of terrorists armed with automatic pistols and sawn-off shot guns, died of his wounds on the spot.
