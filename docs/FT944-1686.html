941219 FT 19 DEC 94 / Company News (This Week and Next): Wessex Water - Other companies One of the smallest of the 10 big privatised water and sewerage groups in England and Wales, should tomorrow report a profits rise of more than 10 per cent to Pounds 58m-Pounds 60m (Pounds 53.4m) pre-tax, while the dividend is forecast to go up by a similar margin to 4.5p (4p). Investors will be waiting for a progress report on the company's ambitious waste management joint venture with the UK arm of WMX, the US-based world leader in waste.
