§413.202 [Amended] 21. In newly redesignated section 413.202, the following changes are made: (a) The heading is revised to read as follows: 413.202␣Organ procurement organizations' (OPOs') or transplant centers' costs for organ sent to foreign countries or transplanted in patients other than Medicare beneficiaries. (b) The term ``organs'' is substituted for ``kidneys'' in the text wherever the term appears. (c) The phrase ``military transplant hospital'' is substituted for ``military renal transplant hospital''. C. 42 CFR part 414 is amended as follows: PART 414_PAYMENT FOR PART B MEDICAL AND OTHER HEALTH SERVICES 1.
