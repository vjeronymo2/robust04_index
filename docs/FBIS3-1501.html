Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pretoria March 29 SAPA -- The UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] rebel movement executed an Executive Outcome employee who had been captured while providing training and security in Angola, the company said in a statement on Tuesday. The company gave the man's name as Steyn Marais. Pretoria-based Executive Outcome hires "security guards" to protect Angolan Government installations. Through its attorneys, the company said information from Angolan sources said two other Executive Outcome employees had been wounded and UNITA was denying them outside medical attention. The men were captured last Thursday [24 March]. An attorney said their names and details of where they had been captured and were being held would not be released at this stage.
