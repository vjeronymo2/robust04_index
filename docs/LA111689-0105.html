November 16, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

COMMUNITY DIGEST: WHITTIER;

RIO HONDO STUDENTS START EARTHQUAKE RELIEF FUND

An earthquake relief fund for victims of the Oct. 17 Bay Area temblor has been created by the Rio Hondo College Associated Student Body.

To kick off the donation drive, the student organization donated $100 of its own funds to the American Red Cross.

Contributors may take canned food or clothing items to any of the following three campus locations: the counseling office, the Campus Inn or the bookstore. Financial contributions should be taken to the Office of Student Activities in Room 200 of Putnam Center.

All donations will be submitted to the American Red Cross for earthquake recovery assistance.
