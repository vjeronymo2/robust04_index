September 30, 1990, Sunday, South Bay Edition

COMMUNITY COLLEGE ROUNDUP

Pierce 27, Harbor 12 -- Harbor failed in its attempt for its first 3-0 start in 25 years when it lost a Western State Conference game at Pierce.

Pierce (1-2, 1-1) used big plays to earn the victory. Running back Arie McQuaig scored on a 68-yard run and LeShante Parker scored on a 56-yard run.
