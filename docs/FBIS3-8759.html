Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] On the second day of her visit to Poland, Madame Catherine Lalumiere, secretary general of the Council of Europe, was received by President Lech Walesa. Earlier she had a meeting with the chairmen of the Sejm and Senate. The need to broaden Poland's cooperation with the Council of Europe was discussed in the course of the conversation at the Belweder. Our country can also mediate in contacts between Russia, Ukraine, and this organization. [passage omitted] According to Madame Lalumiere, human rights are not being violated in Poland. In the near future, Poland is to ratify a Council of Europe convention banning torture and inhuman treatment.
