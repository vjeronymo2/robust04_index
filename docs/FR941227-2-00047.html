DATES: Nominations must be submitted to the EPA on or Before October 27, 1995. [FRL&hyph;5129&hyph;1] Nominations of Estuaries to the National Estuary Program AGENCY: Environmental Protection Agency. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) is announcing its call for nominations of estuaries to the National Estuary Program (NEP). The Agency intends to streamline the operation of this program and is specifically seeking nominations which further this approach. The Governor of any State or trust territory may nominate an estuary located wholly or partly within the State to be included in the NEP. After evaluating the nominations received, the EPA will select estuaries to be included in the NEP in Fiscal Year 1995. DATES: Nominations must be submitted to the EPA on or Before October 27, 1995.
ADDRESSES: Governors' nominations should be addressed to The Administrator, U.S. Environmental Protection Agency, 401 M Street SW., Washington, D.C. 20460. A copy of the nomination should also be sent to Darrell Brown, Oceans and Coastal Protection Division (4504F), U.S. Environmental Protection Agency, Washington, D.C. 20460, and to the appropriate regional representative listed below.
