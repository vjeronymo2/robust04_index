BFN [Excerpts] We begin with a meeting of the Council of Ministers at the presidential offices today. Mr. Liamine Zeroual, the head of state, chaired the meeting. In accordance with the agenda, at the beginning of its proceedings the council listened to a report by Prime Minister Mokdad Sifi on all the activities of his government since it was appointed. In light of the report presented by the minister delegate for the budget, the Council of Ministers then studied and approved the Supplementary Budget Act 1994. The Supplementary Budget Act aims to implement austerity measures in public expenditure in order to reduce the total deficit in the state's finances; to provide additional financial resources to restructure and clean up public enterprises; to restructure banks; to limit the effects of the structural reform program on deprived social groups; to establish new tax measures aimed at promoting investment in conformity with and in transition to a market economy, on one hand, and at achieving economic revival so that national development can take off again, on the other. [passage omitted] In his contribution to the Council of Ministers' discussion, the head of state insisted on the need to formulate a program for social protection, as well as undertaking work aimed at achieving economic effectiveness, because, as he explained, we must succeed not only on the economic level but also on the social level. [passage omitted] In reviewing Algeria's foreign activities in the economic field, the head of state gave directives on the setting up of a joint ministerial committee to be headed by the prime minister. This committee will follow up the implementation of the agreement signed with the IMF. [passage omitted]
