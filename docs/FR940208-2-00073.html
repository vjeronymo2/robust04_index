NATIONAL LABOR RELATIONS BOARD Appointments of Individuals To Serve as Members of Performance Review Boards 5 U.S.C. 4314(c)(4) requires that the appointments of individuals to serve as members of performance review boards be published in the Federal Register. Therefore, in compliance with this requirement, notice is hereby given that the individuals whose names and position titles appear below have been appointed to serve as members of performance review boards in the National Labor Relations Board for the rating year beginning October 1, 1992 and ending September 30, 1993.
