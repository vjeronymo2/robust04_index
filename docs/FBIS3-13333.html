Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, and His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, president of the United Arab Emirates [UAE], have held a closed session at al-Musharraf Palace in Abu Dhabi. The Yemeni News Agency, SABA, has learned that the two presidents discussed issues and developments of interest to the relations between the two brotherly countries and ways of enhancing them. They also discussed developments in the Arab arena and the efforts of the two fraternal countries to restore Arab solidarity. During the meeting, the brother president briefed his brother, His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan, on developments in Yemen in light of the signing of the accord document and the efforts under way to contain the political crisis in our country. He also briefed him on the successes by the joint military committee in stopping the regrettable military developments. He further expressed his appreciation for the brothers, including the brothers in the UAE, for their support for our people ini overcoming the challenges imposed by the political crisis and their support for Yemeni unity. Brother Lt. Gen. 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih arrived in Abu Dhabi at 2100 Sanaa local time today leading a high-level delegation on a brotherly visit to the fraternal UAE. He is to meet with his brother, His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, president of the UAE, for talks on fraternal relations and ways of enhancing them. They will also discuss developments in the regional, Arab, and international arenas, as well as issues of interest to our Arab nation, primarily ways to clear the Arab atmosphere and restore Arab solidarity, and issues of common interest. The brother president will also brief his brother, His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan, on the developments in our country and the efforts being made to contain the current political crisis and implement the accord document. The purpose of this is to end the crisis, safeguard unity and democracy, and bolster stability in our country. His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, president of the UAE, led those who received the brother chairman of the Presidential Council at Abu Dhabi Airport. He gave the brother chairman a warm brotherly hug and welcomed him. These distinguished relations are testified to by the projects that have been carried out by the UAE in Yemen, projects that prove the deep relations of amity among the peoples of the Arabian Peninsula and the Gulf. Our Yemeni people will always highly appreciate the stand of Shaykh Zayid and the sons of his generous people in the UAE and their contribution to our development process and support for the unity of Yemen.
