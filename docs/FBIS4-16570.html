CSO ["Election Results in Novohrad-Volynskyy"] [Text] On 10 April, in the last electoral district of Zhytomyr Oblast, the Novohrad-Volynskyy E.D. No. 158, where the second round of voting did not take place on 3 April, P. Horbatyuk, a collective farm chairman, was elected people's deputy of Ukraine, defeating V. Veselovskyy, a physician with no party affiliation.
