BFN [Vladimir Urban, KRASNAYA ZVEZDA correspondent, report from Tallinn under the "In Brief" rubric: "Estonian Defense Forces Main Staff Chief To Resign"] [Text] This was announced by the Main Staff's press service. Colonel Ants Laaneots has occupied this post since the end of 1991; earlier he served in the Soviet Army and was a city military commissar in Tartu. No reasons for Laaneots' resignation have been reported. It has only transpired that his term as Main Staff chief will expire on 18 April and that he has submitted the separation request voluntarily. Laaneots has already turned his business over to his deputy, Colonel Arvo Sirel, chief of the operational department, who is also a former Soviet Army officer.
