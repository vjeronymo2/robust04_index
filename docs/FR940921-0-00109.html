67. A new section 86.401&hyph;97 is added to subpart E, to read as follows: §86.401&hyph;97 General applicability. (a) This subpart applies to 1978 and later model year, new, gasoline-fueled motorcycles built after 31 December, 1977, and to 1990 and later model year, new, methanol-fueled motorcycles built after 31 December, 1989 and to 1997 and later model year, new, natural gas-fueled and liquefied petroleum gas-fueled motorcycles built after 31 December, 1996. (b) Motorcycles with engine displacements less than 50 cc (3.1 cu in) are excluded from the requirements of this subpart. (c) Motorcycles are excluded from the requirements of this subpart, if with an 80 kg (176 lb) driver, it cannot: (1) Start from a dead stop using only the engine; or (2) Exceed a maximum speed of 40 km/h (25 mph) on level paved surfaces. 68.
