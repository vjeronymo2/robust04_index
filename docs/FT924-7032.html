921118 FT 18 NOV 92 / World Commodities Prices: Jute & Cotton JUTE November/December c and f Dundee BTC Dollars 355, BWC Dollars 370, BTD Dollars 320, BWD Dollars 330; c and f Antwerp BTC Dollars 335, BWC Dollars 335, BTD Dollars 310, BWD Dollars 310. COTTON Liverpool- Spot and shipment sales for the week ended November 13 amounted to 409 tonnes against 160 tonnes in the previous week. Improved demand brought moderate purchases mainly in American and Russian descriptions. South American growths made some headway.
