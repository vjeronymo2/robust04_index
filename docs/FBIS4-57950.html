BFN [Text] Mexico City, 26 May (EFE) -- A source at the Mexico City Attorney General's Office told EFE today that Jose Luis Fortuna, commander of the Judicial Federal Police, was murdered by a gunshot to his forehead and left in his own car on the highway to Ajusco on the outskirts of Mexico City. The source said the Metropolitan Police found the commander's body in the car at approximately 1330 (1730 GMT) on the border between Mexico City and the State of Mexico. The body, the head of which was covered with a sack, received a gunshot wound to the forehead. A spent 9-mm cartridge was found on the car's floor, the official reported. The commander was identified by his electoral voting card, which has a picture, and the badge of the Attorney General's Office, which identified him as commander.
