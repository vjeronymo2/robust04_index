940707 FT 07 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Colorgen's bank forecloses loan Colorgen's bank, with the agreement of the directors, has foreclosed its loan to the company and is seeking purchasers for the assets. Shares in the US-based colour matching specialist, were suspended at 26p on June 27 pending clarification of its financial position. The USM-traded company said that in May and June, the last two months of its financial year, trading had been very poor.
