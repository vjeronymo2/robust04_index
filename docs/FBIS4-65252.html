BFN [Text] Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa held a meeting this afternoon with the new Italian foreign minister in Brussels. Musa arrived there today from Geneva. The two ministers discussed relations between Egypt and the European Union before the convening tomorrow of the Egyptian-European Cooperation Council, which will discuss the new economic agreement between the two sides. 'Amr Musa and the Italian foreign minister discussed developments in the Middle East peace process in light of efforts to find a comprehensive solution to the crisis. The two sides also discussed ways to bolster cooperation between the countries around the Mediterranean Sea.
