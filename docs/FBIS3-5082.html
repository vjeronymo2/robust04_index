Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 3 Mar Morning Edition] [Text] A two-day study session held at the National Institude for Defense Studies Research in Tokyo by Japanese and Russian defense researchers closed on 2 March. The session was part of the program for the third Japan-Russia defense study exchange of researchers of Japan's Defense Agency and the Russian Defense Ministry and Federation Armed Forces. In the meeting, the Russian officials presented their view on the issue of nuclear arms development by the DPRK (North Korea) and said economic sanctions against that country would have no effect.
