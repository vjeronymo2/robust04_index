940503 FT 03 MAY 94 / Bank staff plan 2 1/2-hour strike About 100 staff of Barclays Bank in Birmingham are planning a 2 1/2 -hour strike today. The staff, members of the finance union Bifu who work at Barclays Centralised Securities Office, are protesting at the withdrawal of protection for their salaries following a reorganisation at Barclays. The union claims the reorganisation has left many staff working in lower paid grades.
