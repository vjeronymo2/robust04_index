Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] It was learned on 4 February that Colonel General Andrey Nikolayev, commander of the Russian Border Security Force, in an unofficial visit to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs [MOFA], asked the Japanese Government to take appropriration actions against Japanese boats engaging in "illegal fishing operations" around the northern territorial islands. According to an official concerned, Commander Nikolayev visited the MOFA on 3 February to meet with officials of the MOFA and the Maritime Safety Agency. At the meeting, he warned that Russia will take measures to ban Japanese boats from fishing in Russian waters in the future, saying: "Such acts cannot be tolerated because it is a violation of Russian borders." The MOFA insisted there was no problem because these northern territories are Japan's territories. But the Russian commander said emphatically: "No peace treaty has been concluded with Japan yet, and Russia's borders with Japan are existing facts."
