940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto finished a sleepy post-holiday session stronger as several inter-listed shares played catch-up with gains made in the US on Friday, when Canadian markets were shut for Canada Day. The TSE 300 index rose 28.3 to 4,053.6 but advances outpaced declines by just 299 to 289 after extremely thin volume of 19.96m shares, well below last Thursday's 59.4m. Trading value was CDollars 131.9m. All but one of the 14 stock groups rose, mining and banking leading with increases of 1.90 and 1.37 per cent respectively. Industrial products gained 1.02 per cent, while energy and golds were flat. Wall Street was closed for the Independence Day holiday
