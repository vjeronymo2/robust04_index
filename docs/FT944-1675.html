941219 FT 19 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Chemex Intl Chemex International, the USM-traded chemical analysis company, continued its recovery into the second half, ending the year with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 135,534, against a deficit of Pounds 12,961 last time. Turnover for the 12 months to September 30 grew to Pounds 1.59m (Pounds 1.33m) and the pre-tax outcome was struck after net interest payable of Pounds 3,141 compared with income of Pounds 2,337. Earnings emerged at 0.33p (0.03p losses) and the company is introducing dividend payments with a 0.1p distribution for the year. It also said it intended to apply for a full listing.
