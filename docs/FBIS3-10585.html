Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Michael Kozac, the assistant to the special representative of President Clinton in the Haitian crisis, arrived in Port-au-Prince on the afternoon of 8 March. During his stay, Mr. Kozac is to meet with Haitian businessmen and parliamentarians, such as members of the Senate office presided over by Firmin Jean-Louis. This is the second visit by Kozac in less than five days.
