Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Minister of State for Prime Ministry Affairs and Minister of Information Dr. Jawad al-'Anani has received Director of the Middle East Affairs Department at the Japanese Foreign Ministry (Shiguro Andu). Discussions tackled bilateral relations. Dr. al-'Anani reaffirmed Jordan's constant stance towards the Middle East conflict and the necessity to achieve a just and comprehensive settlement as well as resolving all issues on all negotiating tracks between the Arab states and Israel. The Japanese official, whose country presides over the environment working group at the multilateral talks, praised Jordan's role in the Middle East peace negotiations and its interest in the environment. He underlined the importance of bolstering the existing bilateral ties, particularly in the economic and trade fields.
