Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN ["Decree issued by King Norodom Sihanouk dated 25 December"] [Text] I, Norodom Sihanouk Varaman, king of Cambodia, pursuant to the recommendation of Samdech Chea Sim, chairman of the National Assembly of the Kingdom of Cambodia, appoints Their Excellencies Say Phuthang, Chea Soth, Bou Thang, and Mat Li as supreme personal advisers to the king of Cambodia. [Signed] Norodom Sihanouk
