BFN [Statement by Liberal Party presidential candidate Ernesto Samper at his campaign headquarters at the Orquidea Real Hotel in Santa Fe de Bogota on 29 May -- recorded] [Text] This is a message of optimism. I am very grateful to all the people who accompanied me today. Of course, we would have liked this election to be decided today, but that was not possible. I won the first round. [applause] I hope I will be able to continue relying on you and on your happiness, support, and enthusiasm because we are going to win the runoff elections. Thanks to the people of Bogota, my dear city, for their exemplary behavior [applause, cheers] and to all the people throughout the country who supported me. Thank you very much. I have come here with vice presidential running mate Dr. Humberto De la Calle, not only to celebrate the events of the day, as we are doing tonight with you, but also to celebrate the two to one victory we just had over the Milan soccer team. [applause] Thanks for your company and now, let the party continue.
