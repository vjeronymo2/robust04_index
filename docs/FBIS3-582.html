Matlala] [Text] Pretoria March 10 SAPA -- More than 7,000 Bophuthatswana public and private sector workers converged on the Union Buildings in Pretoria on Thursday [10 March ]morning to demand intervention by the South African Government in the homeland crisis. Marchers, mostly from the Odi/Moretele regions, also demanded the pay-out of pension contributions before the April elections and the immediate re-incorporation of Bophuthatswana into South Africa. They are expected to present a memorandum to the state president's office, stating their demands.
