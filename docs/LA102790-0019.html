October 27, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

LET GIBSON LEAVE AND GET STRAWBERRY

What must we faithful Dodger fans do to get Darryl Strawberry dressed in blue? I read he wants to come to the Dodgers and then I heard it from his own mouth on television. Surely the Dodger front office can come up with a solution that will make Strawberry, the Dodgers and the fans extremely happy in 1991.

LYNDA M. GALLUP

Ventura
