Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The great leader Kim Il-song's special envoy Choe Ki-yong, Education Committee chairman, was received by Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad on 27 March. During the meeting, the special envoy delivered a personal letter from the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song to President Hafiz al-Asad. The special envoy also conveyed greetings from the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il to President Hafiz al-Asad. The president expressed his deep gratitude to this and asked the special envoy to convey his sincere and warm greetings to the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. The president stressed that His Excellency President Kim Il-song is a great man respected by the people of the world and the most outstanding leader. He said he was well aware of the greatness of the dear leader of the Korean people His Excellency Kim Chong-il. He said he agreed that the nuclear issue of the Korean peninsula should be resolved through DPRK-U.S. talks. He said that since long ago, Syria has taken a position against anything that blocks the reunificaiton of Korea. He said that Syria cherishes friendship with the DPRK and promised that Syrian people will remain close friends with DPRK people. The DPRK ambassador to Syria was present on the occasion.
