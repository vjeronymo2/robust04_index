BFN ["D.H."-initialed report: "April Inflation 2.1 Percent"] [Text] Ljubljana -- According to a report by the Slovene Statistical Institute, retail prices in April have increased by 2.1 percent, therefore, this is how high the April inflation is. In the month just ending, the prices of vital necessities increased by 2.4 percent. In the first four months of this year, retail prices increased by 6.1 percent and the prices of vital necessities by 7.2 percent. In one year, retail prices increased by 20.8 percent and the cost of living by 21.8 percent.
