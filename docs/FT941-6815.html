940228 FT 28 FEB 94 / Company News (This Week): Ladbroke - Other Companies The hotels, betting and do-it-yourself group also reports full-year results on Thursday, with estimates of pre-tax profits before exceptional items ranging from Pounds 125m to Pounds 140m, compared with Pounds 186.8m. More closely watched will be the manner of the new management under Mr John Jackson, chairman, and Mr Peter George, chief executive, who took over at the beginning of the year. The City will be asking for details of future property disposals, hotel trading and a resolution of problems at the Texas Homecare chain.
