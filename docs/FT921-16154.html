920106 FT 06 JAN 92 / International Company News: Merchant bank wins bid battle FINANTIA-Sociedade de Investimentos, the Portuguese merchant banking and investment company, has finally won control of Sofinloc-Sociedade Financeira de Locacao, the country's leading leasing company, writes Patrick Blum. After months of bids and counter bids by Finantia and Banco Comercial de Macau (BCM), the Macao-based bank controlled by Banco Portugues do Atlantico (BPA), Finantia secured 76.28 per cent of Sofinloc, paying about Esc10.5bn (Dollars 78m) for over 1.5m shares, at Esc6,933 per share.
