BFN [Text] Reports from reliable security sources say that last Saturday [4 June], a huge explosion took place in the Huzori Bagh, a five-story building in Mazar-e Sharif, in which at the present the so-called Central Office of Jenbish [National Islamic Movement of North, led by Dostam] is located. As a result of this explosion a number of militiamen were killed and the building was damaged. Eye-witnesses say that the number of such explosion has recently increased, which indicates chaos in the militias security systems.
