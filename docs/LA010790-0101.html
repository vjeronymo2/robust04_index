January 7, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: SUPERINTENDENT NAMED TO U.S. EDUCATION PANEL

West Covina Unified School District Supt. Jane Gawronski has been appointed to a three-year term on the U.S. Education Department's Program Effectiveness Panel.

The panel reviews project proposals submitted for funding to the Department of Education. A third of the 60-member panel are federal employees; the rest are appointed.
