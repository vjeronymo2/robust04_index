Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The United States and South Korea have agreed to suspend the 1994 Team Spirit joint military exercise. As has already been reported, the DPRK-U.S. agreement reached in New York prescribes that the United States will announce that South Korea agrees to suspend the 1994 Team Spirit joint military exercise. [sentence as heard] According to reports, the United States and South Korea have officially announced that the two sides, in conformity with the agreement, have agreed on the suspension of the Team Spirit 1994 joint military exercise. The South Korean Defense Ministry announced on 3 March that it will discontinue the Team Spirit 1994 joint military exercise. On the same day, a spokesman for the U.S. State Department also announced that the United States has agreed with South Korea's decision to cancel the 1994 Team Spirit joint military exercise.
