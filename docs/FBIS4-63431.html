BFN [Text] The opposing sides in the Nagorno-Karabakh front line have refrained from launching intensive combat operations. However, the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] Defense Army Press Service has disclosed that over the past few days Azerbaijan has violated the relatively peaceful situation. That republic's military forces have been firing on the NKR defense positions. For example, this morning Azerbaijani units fired on the NKR positions in the northeastern part of the confrontation lines with mortars. The Armenian side remains committed to the cease-fire agreement and has held back from returning the fire. Meanwhile, the Armenian Ministry of Defense Press Service has disclosed that the relatively peaceful situation in the northeastern part of Armenia was disrupted again yesterday. The defense positions around Vagashen in Idzhevanskiy Rayon were fired on with infantry combat vehicles at 1405. No one was hurt. The same source also noted that no other incidents occurred on Armenia's border last night and this morning.
