BFN [Text] Novi Sad, May 21 (TANJUG) -- Slobodan Milosevic, president of the Republic of Serbia, is the most popular personality in the Federal Republic of Yugoslavia (F.R.Y.), say the results of a poll published here on Saturday. The poll was conducted by the `Mark-Plan' marketing centre in 27 muncipalities throughout the F.R.Y. on the basis of a representative sample of 1,045 persons of the different social and demographic profile. The questioned were asked to state five most popular public figures in the different fields, like politics, show business, sports, culture or the like. The largest percentage (16.7 per cent) of the vote went to Slobodan Milosevic, followed by National Bank Governor Dragoslav Avramovic (7.3 per cent) who eliminated hyperinflation, and two folk music singers Dragana Mirkovic (6.9 per cent) and Lepa Brena (4.3 per cent). As for other questions, 60 per cent of the answers said they would vote again for the political party they voted at last December's elections, while 48.6 and 29 per cent respectively believed and disbelieved in Dragoslav Avramovic's economic recovery program.
