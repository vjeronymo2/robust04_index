BFN [Text] Knesset Member [MK] Ari'el Sharon continues to fund raise abroad for activities in the Jewish settlements in Judaea, Samaria, and the Gaza Strip. The Council for Jewish Settlements in Judaea, Samaria, and the Gaza Strip says that MK Sharon recently raised over $7 million abroad and that he intends to go abroad next week to continue the activity. Sharon told our correspondent Hayim Hecht that the funds he raises are meant for activities in the territories that he charged the government has ignored, including linking up houses to electricity and water systems.
