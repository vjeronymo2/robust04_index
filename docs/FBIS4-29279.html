CSO [Editorial Report] Seoul TONG-A ILBO on 23 May reported on page 2 that with the local election date set for 27 June 1995, the government is planning to transfer over 500 responsibilities to local governments. The government believes this will accelerate local autonomy. These tasks concern regional matters, including issues such as public welfare, revitalization of the economy, local transportation and roads, promotion of agriculture, and boosting local customs and culture. The government is sending two information-gathering teams to 15 cities in May and a final decision will be announced in December.
