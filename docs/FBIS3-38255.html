Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Salzgitter (DPA) -- In Salzgitter this evening, at least two police officers were injured in a protest involving over 1,000 person demonstrating against an election campaign meeting by the right-wing extremist Republikaner. Several windows of a school assembly hall were also smashed. Eggs and firecrackers were also thrown. Police had monitored all cars on the roads leading to Salzgitter beforehand. The IG Metall trade union and the German Trade Union Federation, among other organizations, had called the demonstration.
