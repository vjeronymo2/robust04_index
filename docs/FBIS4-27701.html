BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 3 June Morning Edition] [Text] On 2 June, the government decided to send Shunji Yanai, director general of the Foreign Policy Bureau, Ministry of Foreign Affairs; and Haruhisa Takeuchi, councilor at the Asian Affairs Bureau, to participate in the meeting of Japan, the United States, and the ROK on the DPRK (North Korean) nuclear issue to be held in Washington from 3 June.
