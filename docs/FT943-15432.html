940709 FT 09 JUL 94 / Plans for hostel licensing system The government is to hold consultations on proposals for a licensing system for houses in multiple occupation, Sir George Young, housing minister, announced yesterday. In answer to a question from Mr Roger Gale, MP for Thanet North, he said: 'The government is aware of the continuing concern about the number of hotels in resort areas which are operating as benefit hostels and of the wider concern about safety standards in all houses in multiple occupation.'
