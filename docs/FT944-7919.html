941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There was good general demand this week reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Landed coloury Assams were well supported at firm rates, while plain North Indians often gained several pence. Brightest East Africans were strong and fully firm to dearer. Coloury mediums were steady but plainer sorts were 3 to 5p lower. Plain Burundis were a weak feature. Ceylons met fairly wide enquiry but at lower levels. Offshore met less demand at easier rates. Quotations: best available 175p/kg; nom good 145p/kg; good medium 120p/kg; medium 110p/kg; low medium 85p/kg; the highest price realised this week was 177p/kg for an assam pd.
