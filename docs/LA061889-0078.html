June 18, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

HOSPITAL'S DECISION TO TURN AWAY PREGNANT WOMEN

After reading Vance Gardner's "Taking Responsibility for Medical Care" (June 11), I want to state that I have no problem whatsoever seeing to it that my tax dollars go to aid the health of indigent or elderly people who are in need.

But I resent having to subsidize indigent Hispanic women who seem to think that their mission in life is to bear more and more children. Not only are these women unable to support themselves but they cannot support those kids.

We are no longer living at a time when infant mortality was high and many children were needed to see if any survive to take care of aging parents. Enough is enough!

JOE GAGE

Villa Park
