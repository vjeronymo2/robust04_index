February 23, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

LOCAL;

LOTTO PRIZE MISSED; NOW $12 MILLION

No player picked the six winning numbers in the mid-week Lotto 6-49 drawing, leaving the $5.7-million jackpot unclaimed, state lottery officials said today. The jackpot for Saturday's drawing was estimated at $12 million.

The numbers drawn Wednesday were 23, 31, 32, 33, 46 and 48. The bonus number was 29. In the five-of-six plus bonus category, two players won prizes of $745,343 apiece, said spokeswoman Kathy Manzer. They will be identified after submitting claims to lottery offices.
