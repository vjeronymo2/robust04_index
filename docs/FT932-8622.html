930518 FT 18 MAY 93 / UK Company News: F&C Eurotrust net assets rise Foreign & Colonial Eurotrust net assets were 192.6p at March 31, compared with 167.5p a year earlier. At September 30 the figure had been 161.5p. As usual net revenue for the six months to the end of March showed a loss of Pounds 371,000 (Pounds 184,000). Most European companies pay their dividends in the summer. Losses per share were 0.63p (0.31p).
