BFN [Text] Tirana, April 27 (ATA) -- Three tank trucks destined for Montenegro have been intercepted and the vehicles together with 67 thousand litres of fuel confiscated. This is the latest bag of the Shkoder financial police, who arrested the three drivers in a blitz operation as they were attempting to move their contraband cargo across the Buna river. According to the financial police, the vehicles had Tirana, Lac and Fier plates. The tank trucks and the fuel are now in the possession of the Shkoder financial police. The drivers will face prosecution. At the beginning of the year, the financial police broke up another smuggling operation destined for Montenegro involving three tractors each with 1,000 litres of fuel and 15 barrels of oil. The police also shut down a trading station near Velipoje, which was furnishing kerosene to smugglers.
