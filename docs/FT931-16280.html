930107 FT 07 JAN 93 / EC emigrants 'prefer' France Thirty-five per cent of EC residents are prepared to move to another country within the Community and most would pick France as their adoptive home, according to an opinion poll published yesterday, Reuter reports from Paris. The survey in the daily Le Parisien newspaper said the highest number of would-be emigrants, 53 per cent, were Dutch, followed by Germans, Britons and Belgians.
