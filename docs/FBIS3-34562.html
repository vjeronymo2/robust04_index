Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Egypt's permanent UN envoy Dr. Nabil al-'Arabi has told our radio correspondent that Egypt, in its capacity as chairman of the Arab group at the United Nations for this month, will intensify its efforts and that of the other Arab and Islamic countries in order to find a settlement for the crisis in Bosnia. He said that the Arab and Islamic countries have decided to intensify their efforts and do all they can to have the embargo on the sale of weapons to Bosnia lifted following the barbaric crimes committed by the Serbs in Bosnia. He added that UN Secretary General Dr. Butrus-Ghali is trying to persuade NATO to stage air strikes against the Serbs' positions in Bosnia.
