November 20, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

SAUDIS SECRET STAKE IN MARINA

So, a Marina developer donates $10,000 to Supervisor Deane Dana and he approves a secret deal and overlooks a host of irregularities costing taxpayers a fortune.

Not mentioned was Dana's unstinting support for an ill-conceived Marina bypass which by law needs completion before further Marina del Rey development can take place.

Will we ever get pro-consumer instead of pro-developer county officials?

JOHN KEADY

Marina del Rey
