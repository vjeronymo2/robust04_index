931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / UK Company News: JO Walker back in black with Pounds 0.16m JO Walker, the timber importer, reported a return to profits in the six months to June 30. Helped by an exceptional gain of Pounds 227,000 on the realisation of an investment, pre-tax profits were Pounds 162,000, against losses of Pounds 162,000. Turnover advanced 13 per cent to Pounds 7.17m (Pounds 6.33m) for operating profits of Pounds 15,000, compared with losses of Pounds 78,000. Earnings per share for the six months jcame out at 20.2p (losses 19.2p). The company said profit margins were under intense pressure resulting from higher cost of raw materials because of sterling's devaluation.
