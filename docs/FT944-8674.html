941118 FT 18 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Millwall curbs loss Millwall Holdings, the USM-quoted football club currently struggling near the bottom of the Endsleigh First Division, yesterday reported a sharp fall in pre-tax losses from a restated Pounds 1.13m to Pounds 119,000 in the year to May 31. Sales for the year reached Pounds 5.61m (Pounds 2.67m). Net interest charge was Pounds 375,000 (Pounds 115,000) and losses per share on a FRS 3 basis were 0.04p (0.7p).
