Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 4. (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Today afternoon at the State Palace of Vietnam the Prime Minister of the SRV Vo Van Kiet and his spouse paid a visit to the President of Mongolia P. Ochirbat and his spouse. During the meeting the President P. Ochirbat pointed out to a great success of the Vietnamese people achieved in transition of their country's economy to market relations, noting that experience of the Vietnamese friends would be of great importance for Mongolia. Mongolia has a respect for traditional relations and cooperation of many years established with the SRV and follows the line on enrichment of them by new content and forms on the principles of equality and mutual benefit under new conditions. P. Ochirbat expressed thanks to the SRV for rendering gratis assistance to Mongolia worth 200 thousand U.S. dollars in the difficult for the country's economy period. As the Mongolian president noted, parallel with activation of political dialogue at all levels with the SRV Mongolia is interested in development of bilateral trade economic cooperation. In the sphere of external policy Mongolia is also interested in development of cooperation with Vietnam. P. Ochirbat made a suggestion to the Vietnamese side to cooperate on the issues of the Asian and Pacific region. The Prime Minister of the SRV Vo van Kiet pointed out to the importance of development of bilateral relations and noted that for this purpose it is necessary to look for new forms and ways. That is why, first of all, it is necessary to revive activity of the inter-government commission, said the Vietnamese prime minister.
