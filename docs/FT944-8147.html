941121 FT 21 NOV 94 / Company News (This Week): ANZ - Other companies The reporting season for Australia's big banks closes on Wednesday when ANZ unveils profits for the year to end-September. After the sharp recovery in Westpac's fortunes and the record result from National Australia Bank, analysts say that ANZ should manage a near-tripled profits figure of ADollars 680m-ADollars 690m (USDollars 519m).
