940627 FT 27 JUN 94 / Company News (This Week): Delicate moment for Ferruzzi-Montedison - Other companies The annual shareholders meeting of Montedison, the Italian industrial group, is scheduled for tomorrow, and the AGM of Ferruzzi Finanziaria, Montedison's holding company, for Wednesday, although both meetings could slip by 24 hours. Shareholders will want to hear a progress report about the delicate restructuring of Ferruzzi-Montedison, a year after the near-collapse of the heavily indebted group, and the suicide of its chairman, Mr Raul Gardini.
