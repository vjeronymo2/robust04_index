Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Munich, January 20 (XINHUA) -- Qiao Shi, chairman of China's National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, arrived here this evening from Bonn via Stuttgart, capital of the Baden-Wuerttemberg State. Upon their arrival here, Qiao and his wife Yu Wen and their party were greeted by Wihelm Vorndran, president of the Bavaria State's Parliament. During his short stay in Stuttgart, Qiao visited a well-known German auto company -- Mercedes-Benz. This morning, Qiao and his party attended a meeting in Bonn held by the German Federal Assembly. Extending her warm welcome to Qiao Shi, Rita Suessmuth, president of the Federal Assembly, wished China peace, prosperity and greater successes in reform. Qiao is paying an official goodwill visit to Germany at the invitation of Suessmuth.
