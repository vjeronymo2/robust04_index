BFN [Text] The prime minister has called for specific follow-up action with a time frame to increase bilateral trade with the United States. Talking to a delegation of the Confederation of Indian Industries which called on him in New Delhi today, Mr. Narasimha Rao said that an overall change has taken place in the Indo-U.S. business climate following his recent visit to Washington. He said the Indo-U.S. subcommission on trade should be reactivated to achieve this end. Mr. Rao pointed out that the subcommmission will provide a common forum for business planning, interaction, and progress.
