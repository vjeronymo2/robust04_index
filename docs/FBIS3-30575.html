Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] A delegation of the Slovene Government, headed by Slovene Prime Minister Janez Drnovsek, has arrived on a one-day official visit to the Republic of Croatia. The Slovene delegation was welcomed and greeted by Croatian Prime Minister Nikica Valentic in front of Banski Dvori [offices of Croatian Government in Zagreb] with all state honors. The two prime ministers and their delegations have begun talks behind closed doors in Banski Dvori. Prime Ministers Drnovsek and Valentic are expected to sign several agreements: on employment, on cooperation in education and culture, on reciprocal implementation of court decisions in criminal law, on legal aid in civic and criminal issues, on sea fishing, and on economic and trade relations and cooperation between Croatia and Slovenia. Slovene Prime Minister Janez Drnovsek and Foreign Minister Lojze Peterle are also expected to be received by Croatian President Franjo Tudjman.
