930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / UK Company News: LGW LGW, the USM-quoted marketing services and luxury goods group, continued the progress seen in the first six months to report 1992 pre-tax profits of Pounds 832,000, against Pounds 77,000. The company said that it had made a promising start to the present year. Turnover improved slightly to Pounds 13.5m (Pounds 13.3m). Earnings per share were 10.2p (0.8p). A final dividend of 1.875p is recommended for a total of 3p.
