August 15, 1990, Wednesday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Milwaukee Buck guard Alvin Robertson was charged with misdemeanor assault and jailed in the reported beating of his wife in San Antonio.

Robertson's wife, Jacquelin, told Bexar County sheriff's investigators that she refused to let her husband in their house after he returned home from a trip with another woman. Robertson went to the back of the house and smashed a window to get in, police said. He then followed his wife upstairs and later to the garage, where he grabbed her hand "and struck it against the concrete floor," according to police.
