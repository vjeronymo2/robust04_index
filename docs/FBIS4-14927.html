BFN [Unattributed report: "Yuriy Karasyk Signed an Agreement With the United States Which Is Unfavorable for Ukraine"] [Text] According to People's Deputy Vitaliy Polyakh, chief agricultural adviser to the Ukrainian president, Agriculture and Food Minister Yuriy Karasyk signed an "unfavorable for Ukraine contract for $20 million" during his visit to the United States. According to this deal, Ukraine will receive 70,000 tonnes of soya at a considerably higher price than in Europe. Vitaliy Polyakh believes that Ukraine will have to repay $20 million over some seven years. "Instead of buying technology and equipment, we are following the path of banana republics," said Vitaliy Polyakh.
