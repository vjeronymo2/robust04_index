921223 FT 23 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Brake Bros placing to raise Pounds 8.7m BRAKE BROS, a supplier of frozen foods to the catering trade, is placing 2.29m new ordinary 10p shares at 380p each to raise Pounds 8.7m net. The funds will be used for the continuing development and further expansion of the business. The new ordinary shares - representing a 5 per cent increase in Brake Bros' equity - have been placed by UBS Phillips & Drew. Dealings are expected to commence on December 30.
