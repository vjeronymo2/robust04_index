930612 FT 12 JUN 93 / Government plan creates 200 homes MORE THAN 200 homes have been created by turning vacant property above shops into comfortable flats, Sir George Young, housing minister, said yesterday. He said that of the Pounds 5m allocated to the Flats over Shops scheme in its first year of operation (1992-93) the indications are that almost all of it, Pounds 4.7m, had been spent.
