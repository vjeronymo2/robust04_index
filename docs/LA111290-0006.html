November 12, 1990, Monday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

A 28-year-old woman was in custody Sunday after allegedly stabbing a pregnant woman outside a Logan Heights bar, police said.

According to police, both the victim and the suspect were inside the Aztec Bar at 2950 National Ave. Patricia Camargo-Beltran, 27, who is three months pregnant, left the bar at 1:59 a.m. Sunday and Martha Barron followed her. An argument ensued, and Barron stabbed Camargo-Beltran in the stomach, police said.

Camargo-Beltran was in good condition late Sunday at UC San Diego Medical Center.
