920225 FT 25 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Berkeley Govett advances 16% Berkeley Govett, the Jersey-based international fund management group, announced a 16 per cent increase in pre-tax profits for 1991 to Dollars 53.8m (Pounds 30.7m), largely driven by the success of its US-based life insurance division. Despite difficult conditions in the US insurance market, net profits in London Pacific Life and Annuity Company rose by 54 per cent to Dollars 15.6m. Profits are measured on an embedded value basis which takes account of future profits. Earnings per share increased by 14 per cent to 53.1 cents. An increased final dividend of 13.5 cents is recommended, making a total of 22 cents (19 cents).
