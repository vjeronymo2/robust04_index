February 21, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

VOORHEES TO PLAY GOLF AT USC

Heidi Voorhees, a senior at Notre Dame High, has orally committed to play golf next season for the women's team at USC.

Voorhees, 17, a four-year starter on the golf team at Notre Dame and winner of numerous girls' junior tournaments, said that she will sign a letter of intent in April.

Regarded as the best female senior in the area, Voorhees received a full scholarship.
