930813 FT 13 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Brandon Hire trims deficit Losses at Brandon Hire, the USM-quoted hirer of power tools, catering equipment and furniture, were reduced from Pounds 291,000 to Pounds 14,000 pre-tax for the 12 months ended April 30. Turnover slipped to Pounds 3.87m (Pounds 3.98m). Earnings emerged at 0.44p (losses 1.72p). The dividend for the year is being omitted - last time only an interim of 0.1p was paid.
