920808 FT 08 AUG 92 / The Barcelona Olympics: Unified Team in gold rush Svetlana Kriveleva of the Unified Team won the women's shot putt gold from Huang Zhihong of China. Kathrin Neimke of Germany took the bronze. In the men's pole vault, Maxim Tarasov took the gold with a winning height of 5.80m from Igor Trandenkov, who achieved the same height but with more misses. Javier Garcia of Spain was third on 5.75m. Russian Andrei Perlov conquered heat, humidity and the steep climb to the Olympic stadium to take the men's 50km road walk gold. Perlov, second in last year's world championships, took an early lead and held on to win in an unofficial time of 3 hours 50.13 seconds. Italian Carlos Mercenario was second (3:52.09) and Germany's Ronald Weigel took bronze (3:53.45).
