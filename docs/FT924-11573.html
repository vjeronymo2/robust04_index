921024 FT 24 OCT 92 / Markets: Hammerson to cut dividend - At a glance Interest rates may be falling, but the troubles of the property sector are far from over. This week, Hammerson, one of the biggest companies in the sector announced plans to cut the final dividend from 17p to 6.5p, although the interim dividend is being maintained. Sidney Mason, chairman, said the recession was 'both deeper and longer than any in living memory.'
