BFN [Text] Colonel Dr. Milan Petrov, chief of the Military Medical Academy, informed us a few minutes ago that this morning Prime Minister Lyuben Berov was released from the Lozenets Hospital. It is up to the prime minister whether he will make use of the 15-20 day home rest, which is customary after a cardiovascular operation, or whether he will return immediately to regular activity, of which, according to the medical team, he is sufficiently able, Col. Dr. Milan Petrov said.
