940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Ladbroke Pounds 8.7m hotel purchase Hilton National, part of Hilton International, a division of Ladbroke, the hotels, betting and home improvement group, has paid Pounds 8.68m for the 149-bedroom Milton Keynes Hotel and conference centre. The purchase will bring the number of Hilton National hotels in the UK to 24.
