940129 FT 29 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Suter Suter, the industrial conglomerate, is to acquire the remaining 51 per cent of Refrigeration Investment Company (Proprietary), a holding company for a group involved in the wholesaling and distribution of refrigeration and air conditioning products in South Africa. The price is R31.11m (Pounds 5.4m) and could rise to R36.26m.
