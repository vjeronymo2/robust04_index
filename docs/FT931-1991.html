930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / UK Company News: BET to redeem AMPS BET, the business services group, is to redeem its remaining Dollars 120m (Pounds 80.5m) auction market preference shares in April from its own resources. In July 1992, BET raised Pounds 200m in a 1-for-4 rights issue to redeem Dollars 380m of its Dollars 500m AMPS, and said at the time it was its intention to redeem the remainder at an appropriate time.
