941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / International Company News: Santander sells stake Banco Santander said it sold 3 per cent of Banco Espanol de Credito (Banesto) to Mexican beer group Grupo Modelo for Pta850 a share, and continues to look at Banesto group divestments, Reuter reports from Madrid. The deal is valued at Pta15.6bn (Dollars 118m).
