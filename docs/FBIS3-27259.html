Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, February 21 (XINHUA) -- Shanghai city will construct its second subway line, which is to run east-west under the Huangpu River. The 27.3-kilometer subway will run from Hongqiao International Airport to the Pudong Railway Station. According to China International Engineering Consulting Corporation, the No. 2 Subway will have 17 stops. The No. 2 Subway will consist of two track tunnels, each 5.5 meters in inner diameter. It will intersect the No. 1 Subway at People's Park in the city center. The first phase of the No. 2 Subway project, 13.38 kilometers in length, will run from Jinganshi to Pudong district. It is estimated that during rush hours, the subway will carry about 70,000 passengers per hour.
