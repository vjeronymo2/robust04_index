Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- Defense Minister Volker Ruehe (Christian Democratic Union) on Wednesday afternoon [2 March] received his counterpart from Thailand, General Vijit Sookmark, with military honors in the Defense Ministry. Sookmark has come to Germany for a four-day visit. Immediately after the ceremony, the two ministers discussed bilateral relations, the development of the international security policy situation, and the role of the FRG in the United Nations. Ruehe also briefed Sookmark on the experiences of the German Blue Helmet soldiers during their mission in Somalia. Another subject was the situation in the former Yugoslavia. During his stay in Germany, the Thai defense minister will visit the Navy's submarine fleet in Kiel, as well as defense technology companies in Kassel and Munich. From Berlin, Sookmark will go on to Moscow.
