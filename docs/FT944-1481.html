941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / Letters to the Editor: Story is for real Sir, Reviewing Princess Caraboo (Arts, December 15), Nigel Andrews is, wrongly, sceptical of the claim that the film is based on a real story. In her English Eccentrics, Edith Sitwell relates the story at some length and includes references to contemporary journals and letters mentioning the visit of the 'princess' to England. Richard M W Bullock, 12 Peterborough Villas, London SW6 2AT
