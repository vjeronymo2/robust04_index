Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 20 (KCNA) -- Marshal O Chin-u, minister of people's armed forces, sent a message of greetings to Laotian National Defence Minister Choummali Sai-ngason on the occasion of the 45th anniversary of the founding of the Laotian People's Army. Noting with joy that the friendly and cooperative relations between the peoples and armies of the two countries continue making favorable development, the message wished the latter great success in his responsible work.
