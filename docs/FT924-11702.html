921024 FT 24 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Scapa may buy German assets Scapa Group, the Blackburn-based company which supplies technical products and services to the paper industry, said it was in talks with the receivers of a German company, F. Oberdorfer, which might lead to Scapa acquiring the business and assets of its German, Austrian and US operations. Scapa said Oberdorfer, which is based in Heidenheim, was a leading manufacturer of forming fabrics for the paper industry, with an annual turnover of about Pounds 12m.
