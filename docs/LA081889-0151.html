August 18, 1989, Friday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

BRITAIN, ARGENTINA TO OPEN TALKS

Britain and Argentina agreed today to hold talks in Madrid in October on restoring diplomatic relations and renewing economic, cultural and sports ties ruptured since they went to war over the Falkland islands in 1982.

Representatives of the two countries announced the breakthrough in London, Buenos Aires and the United Nations after several days of negotiations in New York.
