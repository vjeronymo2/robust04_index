October 27, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NOVAK ON AMERICANS MOVING UP

In response to "House OKs Hike in Taxes on Rich" (Part A, Oct. 17):

I have read this article carefully and perhaps I am naive but should there not be some mention concerning cuts in spending? We already know what legislators do with more tax money; they spend it! Will someone please explain to us hard-working taxpayers how raising taxes helps balance the budget?

JEAN M. BALDWIN

El Segundo
