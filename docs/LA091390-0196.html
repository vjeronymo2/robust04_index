September 13, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Firm Honors Preservationist: Redmond, Wash.-based outdoor clothing manufacturer Eddie Bauer has chosen an Oregon rancher, nature book author and founder of the Institute of Range and the American Mustang for its first Heroes of the Earth award.

Dayton O. (Hawk) Hyde, 65, founded the nonprofit institute, aimed at helping save wild horses, in 1987. Its first two refuges, at the institute's headquarters in Hot Springs, S.D., total more than 50,000 acres. Hyde is also founder of Operation Stronghold, a nonprofit corporation that has helped landowners establish wildlife habitats on more than 4 million acres of private property.
