July 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

DOES PETE WILSON HAVE A HEART?

I can tell that Pete Wilson does have a heart ("RoboPol," by Ronald Brownstein, May 13). I doubt I'd vote for him, because any Democrat is bound to address my concerns -- education, medical care for the needy, controlled growth, et cetera -- better than any Republican. Still, I admire him for the seemingly incorruptible stubborn conscience he votes with.

MARILYN SEFF

San Diego
