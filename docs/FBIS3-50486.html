Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] High-ranking military sources on 29 December stressed that there some high-ranking officers who are annoyed because they were not given an audience with the president and commander in chief of the Armed Forces, Juan Carlos Wasmosy. The complaints highlight the fact that when he visits his offices at the Armed Forces headquarters, the chief of state prefers to meet with [Army Commander in Chief] Division General Lino Cesar Oviedo Silva, with whom, according to the reports, he meets for two or three hours. The reports indicate that the high-ranking military officers are also annoyed because on 29 December Wasmosy abruptly suspended the scheduled audiences because of a commitment at Government House. Some of the officers expressed their annoyance because they had to come from the interior of the country, where their units are located, to talk with the commander in chief and to receive their instructions. According to the reports, this type of situation has become repetitive and fewer and fewer audiences are being requested.
