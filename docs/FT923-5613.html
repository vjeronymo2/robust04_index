920829 FT 29 AUG 92 / Ballet orchestra votes for strike LONDON City Ballet's orchestra has voted by more than three-to-one in favour of strike action over the dismissal of three members. The Musicians' Union has instructed the 19-strong orchestra not to report for rehearsals for the new season which is due to start on September 14. London City Ballet said the season would start as planned. It would use another orchestra or taped music if necessary.
