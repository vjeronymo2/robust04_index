BFN [Text] An official source in the Democratic Republic of Yemen [DRY] welcomed the American stand concerning the necessity of Sanaa's obligations to the UN Security Council's resolution on cease-fire and not to impose unity by force. In a statement to the Yemeni news agency, Aden, the source also praised the communique of the Arab Gulf countries, members of the Cooperation Council, and the communique of the ARE [Arab Republic of Egypt] expressing thanks to the fraternals in the Gulf and Egypt for their positive stand which shows eagerness about Yemen's land and people. The source added: We announce our thanks to the Arab community and the world community, which rejected the law of force, destruction and bloodshed, and we would like to confirm our obligation to the Security Council Resolution No. 924 and that Sanaa is trying to deceive the world just as it did in the night of the blessed 'Id al-Adha when it announced a cease-fire and gave its orders to its forces to resume fighting. The source said: We are picking up signals of orders by the head of the Sanaa regime to these forces in the fighting fronts to regather and mobilize themselves in an offensive position on our country. And, as for us, we will commit ourselves to the resolution of the world community and we will not do any military action except in defense against the awaited attack.
