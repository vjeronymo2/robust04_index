July 27, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Chrysler to Unveil Plan: Chrysler Corp. is expected to unveil a plan to help cut up to $1 billion from the No. 3 auto maker's annual budget. Chrysler Chairman Lee A. Iacocca will present the plan at a regularly scheduled meeting of the corporation's top managers at company headquarters in Highland Park this afternoon. The proposal is expected to include reductions in white-collar jobs. A Chrysler spokesman said the plan to be presented to the managers didn't involve any plant closings or a restructuring of the company.
