940530 FT 30 MAY 94 / Opposition hits at health press office The Department of Health spends enough on public relations to pay for a small healthcare centre, Labour said yesterday. Ms Dawn Primarolo, the shadow health minister, said the press office at the department's Whitehall headquarters employed 14 people at a cost of Pounds 396,108, according to the government's own figures. She said: 'Despite the fact that the press office is notoriously unhelpful to journalists it churns out press releases like confetti.' Mr Tom Sackville, health minister, called on Ms Primarolo to withdraw her 'disgraceful personal attack on the integrity of civil servants. 'This department has one of the smallest press offices of all the major Whitehall departments, and one of the busiest.'
