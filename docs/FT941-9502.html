940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / World Trade News: Canada air deal Air Canada has signed a contract to buy six long-range Airbus 340-300s, with deliveries in 1996-97, and has taken options on three more for 1998-99 delivery, reports Robert Gibbens in Montreal. The aircraft will take the place of Boeing 747s on some Atlantic and Pacific medium-density routes. The value of the order exceeds USDollars 1bn (Pounds 682m). Air Canada will be the first North American airline to fly the A340.
