920312 FT 12 MAR 92 / Asean urged to act over Burma The Association of South East Asian Nations (Asean) should take a unified stand against Burma for mistreating its Moslem minority, Mr Najib Razak, Malaysia's defence minister, said yesterday, Reuter reports from Kuala Lumpur. About 170,000 Burmese Moslems known as Rohingyas have fled to Bangladesh since December, accusing Burmese troops of burning their homes, stealing their belongings, raping women and killing those who protested.
