930922 FT 22 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Herring Baker drops Pounds 1.08m into red HERRING Baker Harris Group, the chartered surveyors, fell Pounds 1.08m into the red in the six months to July 31, against a Pounds 1.63m pre-tax profit last time. The result was after charging Pounds 840,000 arising from the company's decision to withdraw from the US following substantial losses there. The company is winding down its New York operation and disposing of its and Denver office to local management. Turnover from continuing operations fell to Pounds 7.86m (Pounds 9.43m). Losses per share were 8.95p (6.24p earnings) and the interim dividend has been cut to 0.5p (3.25p).
