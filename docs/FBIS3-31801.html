Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Albanian villages in Vushtrri commune have long been placed under strict police control. Reports confirm that the people in these villages live under conditions of complete siege, and that in every moment they are subject to checks and maltreatment by the Serbian police that this time do not hesitate to beat, raid, and arrest Albanians in the streets and everywhere they are. Reports from Vushtrri inform that during the last two months, the Serbian police raided and harassed more than 60 Albanian families in Shtanoc in Poshtem village. Raids and massive harassments have been conducted in Zhilivol village. Last week, under the pretext of searching for arms, the Serbian police raided 13 families in Kacibek village in Podujeve. The police terror continues to escalate.
