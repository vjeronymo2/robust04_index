BFN [Text] Sarajevo, 24 June (TANJUG) -- On the occasion of the Day of the Patron Saint of the army of the republic of Srpska and the state holiday of St. Vitus' Day, Radovan Karadzic, the president of the republic of Srpska, has promoted 12 officers of the republic of Srpska army, the republic of Srpska president's office announced in Sarajevo today. Lieutenant Colonel General Ratko Mladic has been promoted to the rank of colonel general. Major Generals Manojlo Milovanovic, Milan Gvero, and Djordje Djukic have been promoted to the rank of lieutenant colonel general, and Colonels Mico Grubor, Zdravko Tolmar, Jovan Martic, Stevan Tomic, Mico Vlaisavljevic, Budimir Gavric, Milutin Skocajic, and Bozo Novak to the rank of major general. The officers were promoted in the recognition of their contributions and successes in organization, managing, and commanding the republic of Srpska army.
