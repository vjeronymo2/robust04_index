December 4, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: HOLIDAY TREAT: HELPING OTHERS;

ORANGE;

POLICE OFFICERS BUY GIFTS FOR FAMILIES

This Christmas, instead of drawing names and buying gifts for colleagues, 15 employees of the Orange Police Department will pool their money to purchase gifts for a few families over the holiday.

The 15 administration officers asked the community resource officers to select the families and to determine their needs.

The department also plans to buy groceries, a Christmas tree and toys for the children.
