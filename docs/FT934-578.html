931224 FT 24 DEC 93 / World News in Brief: Russian children taken hostage Four heavily-armed men seized 12 Russian teenagers and their teacher and flew them out of the southern city of Rostov-on-Don in a commandeered helicopter. The kidnappers landed at Krasnodar, 180 miles to the south, but took off again early today with their captives, heading south east. They originally said they wanted to go to Iran.
