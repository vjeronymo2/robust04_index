February 1, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Coppola Bankruptcy: In an unusual exchange, Orion Pictures said Francis Ford Coppola's Zoetrope Productions was incorrect in listing Orion as a $6.2-million creditor in its recent Chapter 11 bankruptcy filing. Attorneys for Coppola and Orion couldn't be reached. Zoetrope Productions is a unit of Coppola's San Francisco-based Zoetrope Corp.
