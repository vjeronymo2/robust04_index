January 3, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

TWO PHYSICIANS STABBED TO DEATH IN N. Y. HOME

A man and his wife were found stabbed to death in the master bedroom of their home here, police said.

Shanta Chervu, 51, and her husband, Lakshmamrao, 58, both physicians, were found late Sunday by a relative. No motive for the slayings had been determined, police said.
