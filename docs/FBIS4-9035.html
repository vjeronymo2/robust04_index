BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 20/4/1994 -- A Romanian-Armenian consular convention was signed on Wednesday, 20 April, in Bucharest by the two countries' foreign ministers, Teodor Melescanu and Vahan Papazyan. The signing of the convention was, according to the two foreign ministers, "a step ahead" in a long process. "The visit paid by the Armenian foreign minister is a clear sign of the two governments' wish to expand closer ties," Teodor Melescanu said. The formal talks between the two foreign ministers concluded on Wednesday, 20 April. According to the Romanian Foreign Ministry's spokesman Mircea Geoana, the Romanian and Armenian foreign ministers approached during their interview issues related to bilateral and economic ties. Also stressed was the decision to speed up the process of concluding a basic political treaty that would be signed by the presidents of the two states by next fall.
