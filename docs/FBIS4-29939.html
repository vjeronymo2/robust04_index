BFN [Text] Commenting on negotiations on the automobile and auto parts sector under the Japan-U.S. economic framework talks, a senior official at the Ministry of International Trade and Industry [MITI] stated on 20 June: "We cannot make any concessions on the point on which we cannot compromise in principle." Thus, the official stressed Japan's stand that it will not make easy concessions in final consultations on the sector, which will start on 24 June to prepare for the Japan-U.S. summit talks prior to the July Naples summit (meeting of leaders of the seven industrialized nations). It is believed that he reiterated Japan's stance of not accepting anything like numerical targets.
