BFN [Text] Moscow April 18 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin met with chairman of the deputy faction "New Regional Policy" Vladimir Medvedev in the Kremlin on Monday to discuss regional problems. The presidential press service said that Yeltsin and Medvedev stressed the need for signing an agreement on civil peace and called for taking practical measures to rescue the country out of the current crisis. Medvedev informed the president about the goals and tasks of his faction and expressed concern about the non-payment crisis. Yeltsin reiterated his intention to continue discussions with deputy factions and groups, including those representing the interests of Russian regions.
