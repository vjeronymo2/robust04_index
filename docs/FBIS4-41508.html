BFN [Report by Aleksandr Veklich and Vasiliy Fatigarov under "KRASNAYA ZVEZDA and News Agency Correspondents Report" rubric: "Garrisons Looking Ahead to Winter"] [Text] Novosibirsk -- A group of Russian Federation Defense Ministry generals and officers headed by Deputy Defense Minister Colonel General Vladimir Toporov has completed a working visit here. Problems of preparing the garrisons located in the Far North, Siberia, and Far East areas for winter were considered during the visit. "Despite the complexity of the situation it can now be said that the freight deliveries necessary to maintain the vital activity of troops in remote garrisons are unlikely to be disrupted," Toporov stated.
