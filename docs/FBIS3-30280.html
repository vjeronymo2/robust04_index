Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] According to Croatian Defense Force [HVO] reports, Muslim forces in central Bosnia attacked parts of the Zepce front this morning. On the southern section of this front large deployments of Muslim troops were observed. On the Uskoplje-Rama front Muslim forces were also active, firing all along HVO lines. On the Mostar front Muslim forces have increased their attacks, firing with artillery and smallarms on the city, while several mortars hit the city center. Serb forces were active in the Orasje-Samac area, using small arms and heavy machineguns.
