August 12, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

POP EYE

AND FINALLY: Our Band Name of the Week (baseball division) goes to the New York-based group Honus Wagner (named after the great Pittsburgh Pirates shortstop), who are in town Monday at Al's Bar. . . . And if you're if looking for rap on the razor's edge, get a copy of "HollyWord," the new spoken-word collection from Rhino/WordBeat Records. Masterminded by Harvey Kubernik and due out this week, it features provocative patter from such rocksters as Jane's Addiction's Perry Farrell, Fishbone's Angelo Moore and Paul Kantner (plus raps from such unlikely TV-types as Gavin MacLeod, Katey Sagal and Patti D'Arbanville).
