December 9, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

STEPHENS SCORES 31 TO LEAD UCLA WOMEN

Rehema Stephens scored a career-high 31 points to lead UCLA women's basketball team to a 84-75 victory over Cal State Fullerton Friday night at UCLA.

Sandra Van Embricqs had 22 points and 10 rebounds for the Bruins (4-1). Genia Miller had 34 points and 18 rebounds for the Titans (3-2).
