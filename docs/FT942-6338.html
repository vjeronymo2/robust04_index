940531 FT 31 MAY 94 / Observer: Dear Bill's cavil Lord Deedes, the Daily Telegraph's former editor, will not have been happy to see Red Cross matron Edith Cavell, the first world war martyr, misidentified as 'Edith Carroll' in an article bearing his joint byline yesterday. Deedes, 81 tomorrow, is one of the few journalists still active in Britain who was alive when Cavell met her fate.
