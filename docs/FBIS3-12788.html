Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Tunis, 3 Mar (MENA)--Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat has decided to set up an investigative committee made up of some prominent figures in the city of Hebron in the occupied West Bank to investigate the circumstances of the massacre that took place in al-Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron last Friday. The Palestinian president's decision stipulates that this committee will cooperate with a committee of Arab lawyers in Jerusalem; the Research Center of the University Graduates' Association; Hebron University; and national, Arab, and international personalities, institutions, and associations. The decision also stipulates that the committee begins work and submits its report to the PLO Executive Committee as soon as possible.
