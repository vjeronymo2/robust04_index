December 30, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NO LAUGHING MATTER

As the 28-year-old father of twin boys, I am outraged at the promotion trailer for Chevy Chase's "Christmas Vacation." What is wrong with critics who by praising the film seem to be condoning a family pet being electrocuted?

Where was the SPCA when such a disgusting scene was even being considered for filming? Probably taking another coffee break.

GRANT McCULLOGH, Woodland Hills
