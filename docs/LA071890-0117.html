July 18, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

COMING HOME: Actress Kelly McGillis, who will star in a July 30 benefit for the Garden Grove Shakespeare Festival, says she'll move back to Orange County as soon as she can sell her home in Marina del Rey. . . . McGillis, who grew up here, says she needs more room now that she has a 7-week-old daughter, Kelsey Lauren. . . . Just where will they settle? "We haven't really looked," she says. Jeffrey A. Perlman and Jan Herman
