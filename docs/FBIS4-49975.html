BFN ["A decision of the National People's Congress [NPC] Standing Committee adopted on 12 May 1994"] [Text] Beijing, 12 May (XINHUA) -- The Seventh Meeting of the Eighth NPC Standing Committee has decided to appoint another 13 members to the Preliminary Work Committee of the Preparatory Committee of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region. They are: Wang Yingwei (3769 5391 0251), Ulanmulun (3527 5695 2606 0243), Liu Hanquan (0491 3352 6898), Li Zuze (2621 4371 3419), Wu Jiawei (0702 1367 3837), Wu Qinghui (0702 3237 6540), Zhang Liangdong (1728 5328 2767), Zheng Mingxun (6774 2494 6064), Jia Shiya (6328 2457 7161), Xu Ze (1776 3419), Weng Xinqiao (5040 1800 2890), Guo Fengmin (6753 0023 3046), and Huang Yihong (7806 1355 1738).
