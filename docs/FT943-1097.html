940926 FT 26 SEP 94 / Company News (This Week): Tarmac - Other Companies Half-time pre-tax profits from the housing and construction group, are expected to fall within the range of Pounds 19m to Pounds 25m tomorrow. Last time, profits were Pounds 2.5m. It is thought that the dividend will be maintained at 3p. The housing division and aggregates market are forecast to produce improved performances.
