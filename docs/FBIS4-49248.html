BFN [Report: "ARATS Confirms Secretary General Zou Zhekai Has Left Office"] [Text] Beijing, 3 May (ZHONGGUO XINWEN SHE)--In a dispatch sent to Taiwan's Straits Exchange Foundation [SEF] today, the Association for Relations Across the Strait [ARATS] confirmed that ARATS Secretary General Zou Zhekai has already left office. In the dispatch to the SEF, ARATS stated: Mr. Zhekai Zou is no longer secretary general of ARATS. He now works in XINHUA Hong Kong Branch. Zou Zhekai asked us, ARATS, to relay his gratitude to all the gentlemen of the SEF for their support for his work during his term of office as ARATS secretary general.
