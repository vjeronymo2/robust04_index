940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / Algeria reduces oil revenue hopes Algeria has revised down the revenue it expects from its petroleum exports this year to Dollars 8bn from Dollars 9bn, a spokesman for the state-owned Sonatrach agency said yesterday, Reuter reports from Rabat. The estimate is 'based on an average price of Dollars 15 a barrel for Algerian crude. Our forecast of oil revenues for 1994 will be around Dollars 8bn,' the spokesman for Sonatrach's communications department said.
