March 11, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

HOT LINE KEEPS TRACK OF WILDFLOWERS IN BLOOM

Wildflowers are starting to bloom earlier this year thanks to winter rains and recent high temperatures, according to the Theodore Payne Foundation, a nonprofit organization that perpetuates native California flora. The foundation's hot line -- (818) 768-3533 -- informs which flowers are blooming and where they may be seen. The message is updated each Thursday through May 31.
