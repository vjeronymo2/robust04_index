930918 FT 18 SEP 93 / World News in Brief: Inflation pushes Footsie down The FT-SE 100 Index fell nearly 32 points this week on concerns over inflation and disappointing company trading reports. Over the London stock market's two-week account, which closed last night, the Footsie has shed 51.18, but it has fallen 109 points, or 3.5 per cent, since the trading peak of 3,115 reached on August 31. It closed last night 1.6 up on the day at 3,005.5. London stocks, Page 13; Editorial Comment, Page 6; Lex, Page 22; Markets, Weekend Page II
