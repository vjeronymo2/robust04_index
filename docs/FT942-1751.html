940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / Outsider chosen to lead ICI Imperial Chemical Industries, the bulk chemicals company demerged from the old ICI a year ago, has broken with tradition by appointing outsider Charles Miller Smith of Unilever as its new chief executive. Miller Smith, a former Unilever finance director who now runs its food operations in southern Europe, will take over next April when the present chief executive Ronnie Hampel will succeed Sir Denys Henderson as chairman.
