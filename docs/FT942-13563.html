940425 FT 25 APR 94 / World News in Brief: Mirren wins third Bafta award Helen Mirren won her third Bafta award in London last night. She was chosen as Best Television Actress for the series Prime Suspect 3. Steven Spielberg won Best Film award and the David Lean Award for Best Direction for Schindler's List, while Sir Anthony Hopkins took the film award for Best Leading Actor for The Remains of the Day.
