BFN [From the "Provincial News Hookup" program] [Excerpt] On the afternoon of 26 April, colorful flags fluttered over the Jiashan Shopping Center, which won the glorious national title of 100-strong market for 1993. Inside, the atmosphere was warm. Inspecting the shopping center, Zhu Rongji, member of the Standing Committee of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and vice premier of the State Council, praised it saying it was beautifully built. The Jiashan Shopping Center is situated in a location where Zhejiang, Jiangsu, and Shanghai meet. Being close to the Shanghai-Hangzhou railway and National Highway 320, it has become an important commodity distributing center in northern Zhejiang. The shopping center cost 15.93 million to build.
