Language: Lithuanian Article Type:BFN [Text] A Czech Defense Ministry delegation, headed by Defense Minister Antonin Baudys, has arrived in Vilnius for a three-day visit. The guests have already met National Defense Minister Linas Linkevicius. This afternoon the delegation from the Czech Defense Ministry will be received by Algirdas Brazauskas, the president of the Republic of Lithuania, and Seimas Chairman Ceslovas Jursenas. Prior to this, the guests will meet Jonas Andriuskevicius, the commander in chief of the Lithuanian Army. During the three-day visit, the Czech Defense Ministry delegation members will visit the Seimas and hold meetings with the members of the government. They will also visit the Gelezinis Vilkas, a Lithuanian field troops unit, and Trakai [historic place near Vilnius]. At the end of the visit, on 19 January, a news conference is to be held.
