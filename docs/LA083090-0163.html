August 30, 1990, Thursday, Ventura County Edition

BEST BETS: SINGER/SONGWRITER OFFERS FAMILY FARE AT PERFORMANCE IN VENTURA

If you are seeking family entertainment tonight, you may want to consider a performance by children's recording artist Dan Crow aP. Foster School in Ventura.

"Dan's been around for years, entertaining countrywide with his humorous, all original music," Foster Principal Greg Campf said.

Crow may be familiar to Disney Channel viewers as a singer/songwriter for the Disney productions "Welcome to Pooh Corner" and "Dumbo's Circus."

Strumming an acoustic guitar and playing the kazoo, Crow will sing numbers from his eight albums.

"He's really funny, and it's good, wholesome family entertainment," Campf said. "The children really love him."

The school is at 20 Pleasant Place, and the concert will be at 6:30 p.m. in the cafeteria. Tickets are $5.50, $3.50 for children. After the concert, sundaes will be served. For information, call 653-5372.
