940825 FT 25 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Newmarket Venture distribution soon The joint liquidators of Newmarket Venture Capital intend to make a first distribution of about 36p a share in about six weeks time. Losses for the 7 months to August 1 were Pounds 80,000 and net assets at that date totalled Pounds 20.8m or 58p a share.
