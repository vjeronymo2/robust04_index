Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bratislava, March 31 (CTK) -- Dusan Rozbora was introduced to the post of secretary of state to the Foreign Ministry by Foreign Minister Eduard Kukan today. Rozbora, 51, is a career diplomat with no party affiliation. He worked in the Czechoslovak Foreign Ministry, held diplomatic posts in Finland and Mongolia, and most recently in Slovak Foreign Ministry.
