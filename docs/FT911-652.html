910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / Wallace Smith head remanded on bail MR Duncan Smith, chairman of Wallace Smith Trust, the collapsed London banking and finance group, was yesterday remanded on Pounds 250,000 bail until July 11. He was freed at Guildhall magistrates' court after having been in custody since his arrest last week, when he was charged with fraudulent trading. The court required two sureties, each of Pounds 100,000, and a security deposit of Pounds 50,000. Other bail conditions were that he surrenders his passport, reports to the police three times a week and does not contact witnesses or visit the offices of Wallace Smith Trust.
