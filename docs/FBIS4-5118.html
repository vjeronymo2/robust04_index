BFN [Text] Seoul, April 6 (YONHAP) -- President Kim Yong-sam accepted on Wednesday the resignation of Pak Tae-kwon, governor of South Chungchong Province, who recently created a public controversy over his alleged pre-election campaigning. President Kim was briefed on the details of the controversy by his Senior Secretary for Governmental Affairs, Yi Ui-kun, and accepted the resignation, an official at Chongwadae [presidential office] said. Kim is expected to appoint a replacement for Pak soon.
