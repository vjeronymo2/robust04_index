Article Type:BFN [Text] Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana is meeting Sir Leon Brittan, European commissioner for foreign economic affairs and commercial policy, in Madrid. In Community circles, Brittan is considered to be a candidate for Jacques Delors' replacement as the president of the European Commission. During the last few hours [as heard] Leon Brittan met Spanish Prime Minister Felipe Gonzalez to explain the aims of his candidacy to the presidency of the European Commission. Later the British conservative politician met a group of Spanish businessmen to whom he explained the need to make the European labor market more flexible. Last night in Brussels the Spanish foreign minister met Jacques Delors, current president of the European Commission, with whom he discussed, among other items, relations between the Community and East Europe and the future enlargement of the European Union and Europe's role in the world.
