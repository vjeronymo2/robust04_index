921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Write-off ups loss at CH Bailey Losses at CH Bailey, the engineering, ship repairing and leisure group, increased from Pounds 664,081 to Pounds 1.09m pre-tax over the 12 months to March 27. The figure was struck after a write-off of Pounds 639,191 in respect of the group's investment in Clarke Foods, the ice-cream maker which called in administrative receivers in October. Turnover amounted to Pounds 3.54m, down from Pounds 5m. Losses per share were 1.8p (1.1p).
