920709 FT 09 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Quiligotti loss exceeds Pounds 1.8m Quiligotti, the USM-quoted maker and installer of terrazzo floors, incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.83m in the year ended March 31, in line with June's forecast. The outcome compared with a profit of Pounds 905,000, and included Pounds 280,000 for rationalisation and reorganisation measures taken in the UK businesses. Losses per share were 6.17p (earnings 1.31p) and there is no dividend (1.53p). The profit forecast was made with the announcement of a rights issue to raise Pounds 3.2m which would reduce debt, and the sale of the lossmaking US subsidiary for Pounds 333,000. Turnover fell to Pounds 19.7m (Pounds 23.4m).
