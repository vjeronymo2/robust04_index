940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / Black Country meets job targets The Black Country Development Corporation yesterday said that it had met all the government targets for the regeneration of its area three years before the end of its 10-year life. The corporation was set up by the government in 1987 with the task of reviving 10 sq miles of rundown industrial land north-west of Birmingham. In its latest annual report it says that the government's target of creating 21,000 jobs in its area had been met.
