940127 FT 27 JAN 94 / UK Company News: TDG in talks to sell engineering offshoot Transport Development Group is negotiating to sell Beck & Pollitzer Engineering to its management. With annual sales of more than Pounds 20m, B&P Engineering is an international contractor engaged mainly in industrial removal and installation contracts. Net assets were Pounds 7.3m at December 31 1992. Mr Michael Cox, managing director of TDG's hire division, said the company was outside TDG's main activities and a sale to management would allow the group to develop further its core activities.
