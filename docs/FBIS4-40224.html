BFN [Text] This year, under various pretexts, over 60 industrial enterprises in Semipalatinsk Oblast have come to a standstill. Over 20,000 workers have lost their jobs. In addition, small businesses, with a total work force of over 3,500 workers, have also discontinued work. Over 20 other enterprises of the oblast, which employ a total of about 6,500 people, are working half days. The oblast's industrial output has declined by 30 percent, as compared with last year.
