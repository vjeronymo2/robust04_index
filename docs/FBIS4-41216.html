BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] Our television agency received interesting information today from the public relations center of the Ministry of Internal Affairs, which we give almost in full. On 21 June, operatives from the Moscow city directorate of internal affairs--with the support of 20,000 men of the Dzerzhinskiy Motor Rifle Division of interior troops--carried out a citywide preventive operation code-named Hurricane. In 24 hours, the special service visited 689 sites. Of these, 14 hotels, the offices of 232 commercial structures and banks, and four markets were inspected. At 27 parking lots and 43 main vehicle inspectorate checkpoints, 1,053 motor vehicles were inspected. A total of 2,251 people were detained for investigation. Administrative proceedings have been instituted against 759 of them. The operations of 193 commercial structures have been suspended. A total of 292 crimes were registered, and 219 were solved. Twenty-three previously stolen vehicles were found.
