930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / World Trade News: Earth-mover joint venture Terex Equipment of the UK has signed a Dollars 5.5m joint venture agreement in China to manufacture earth-moving equipment, writes Tony Walker. It is Terex's second such venture in China. Weili Terex, a venture with the Linyi Machinery Construction Factory, will produce Terex's five-model range of wheeled loaders and articulated dump trucks. Terex's other venture, in Inner Mongolia, produces 45-tonne trucks from UK kits.
