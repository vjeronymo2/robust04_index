December 25, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

The Rhythm Is Gonna Get Her: Gloria Estefan is back. The singer is returning to full speed after spending most of last year recovering from serious back injuries suffered in a bus accident.

Her first album since the crash, "Into the Light," will be released Jan. 29. The single from the album, "Coming Out of the Dark," is a song inspired by Estefan's ordeal. The ballad with gospel influence is due out Jan. 14. Estefan will perform at the American Music Awards on Jan. 28, and this spring she'll tour Europe.
