BFN [Text] Probably thanks to Mr. Akashi's visit, the chetniks have reduced the number of cease-fire violations in Sarajevo today. Still, their snipers have managed to wound one civilian today near the Simon Bolivar primary school in Dobrinja. They also fired with an antiaircraft machine gun from Azici on the city's free territory. Throughout the day they have been carrying out tactical regrouping of men and equipment in various directions, and grouping of their army on certain positions around Sarajevo. There are indications that their sniper fire and other military activities could intensify, so the citizens are reminded to take seriously all the caution warnings. This is reported by the [Bosnia-Herzegovina Army] 1st Corps Information Center.
