Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow February 8 TASS -- "Russia has military production which is competitive enough to find its buyer. This will make it possible to earn money for conversion and preserve highly qualified staff of the domestic (military) industry", Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin said in an address to readers published in the first issue of a new journal, "The Military Parade", which was presented at a news conference here Tuesday. The new edition, which is available both in Russian and English, is aimed at providing Russian and foreign readers with updated information about the Russian defence complex development, new sample weapons and military equipment. The first issue specifically describes the new combat helicopter KA-50 "Black Shark", 152-mm self-propelled howitzer "MSTA-S", the air defence system "Arrow-10MZ", the state-of-art aircraft complexes, and the modern submarine "Piranha". "This is the first step towards the implementation of the large-scale program to support defence industries in Russia and extend the export of Russian weapons and technologies of binary usage", the news conference participants said. The journal has been founded by the League of Defence Industries, the association of investors in the Russian defence complex conversion, and the U.S. company "Zigzag Venture Group". Authors will include Russian top military officials, developers and producers of weapons and military materiel, scholars, experts and all those whose activities are related to this field. Such a publication has been long required by Russian military producers and their foreign customers, the journal's chief editor Yevgeniy Shashkov finalized.
