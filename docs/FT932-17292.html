930401 FT 01 APR 93 / UK Company News: Broadcastle Broadcastle, the financial services company, swung from losses of Pounds 400,000 to pre-tax profits of Pounds 276,000 for 1992. Turnover of Pounds 2.68m compared with Pounds 2.75m. Substantial provisions were again necessary against receivables and it was expected that further arrears would occur.
