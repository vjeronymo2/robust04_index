941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Philip Harris jumps to Pounds 0.95m Philip Harris, the laboratory equipment maker and pharmaceutical supplier, announced a jump in pre-tax profits from Pounds 378,000 to Pounds 947,000 for the six months to the end of September. There was a turnround in the education and scientific division from losses of Pounds 148,000 to profits of Pounds 344,000 with improved sales in all the division's areas. Group turnover rose by 13 per cent to Pounds 50.2m (Pounds 44.3m). Earnings per share came through at 5.77p against 2.42p and the interim dividend is maintained at 2.2p. Mr Bob Jordan chairman, said the general improvement in all areas of activity pointed to an improved outcome for the full year.
