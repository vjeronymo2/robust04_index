Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 13 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song sent a wreath to the bier of the late people's actor Han Chin-sop of the state theatrical company, expressing deep sorrow over his death. Han Chin-sop died of illness at the age of 79 on March 12. He dedicated his all to the work for the development of literature and art, upholding the literary and art idea and line of the Workers' Party of Korea.
