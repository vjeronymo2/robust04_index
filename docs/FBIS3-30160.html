Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 26 (KCNA) -- Yang Hyong-sop, chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly, sent a message of greetings to Riitta Uosukainen upon his election as president of the parliament of the Republic of Finland. Expressing the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two parliaments would grow stronger and develop, the message wished him success in his responsible work.
