940823 FT 23 AUG 94 / International Company News: Securitas lifts earnings 21% Securitas, Europe's largest security group, lifted pre-tax profits by 21 per cent to SKr220m (Dollars 28.52m) in the six months to June 30. The figure includes a SKr48m contribution from the group's recently demerged lock operations. Sales grew 16 per cent to SKr3.86bn, reflecting the group's continuing acquisition programme. The most recent purchases were in Portugal and Spain. Securitas and Finland's Metra group agreed to merge their lock businesses to form ASSA Abloy in June. Full-year profits for the new entity are expected to be SKr390m.
