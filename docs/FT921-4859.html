920306 FT 06 MAR 92 / UK Company News: MTL rises sharply to Pounds 4.61m PROFITS of MTL Instruments Group rose from Pounds 3.77m to Pounds 4.61m pre-tax for 1991. The 22 per cent improvement was achieved on the back of a Pounds 4m rise in turnover to Pounds 18m. The USM-quoted company, a maker of electrical instruments and control systems, is raising the year's dividend by 21 per cent to 3.4p with a proposed 1.9p final. Earnings amounted to 16.6p (13.69p) per share. An extraordinary credit of Pounds 552,000 represented a pension scheme surplus in respect of a pension fund established for one of the founder directors.
