941201 FT 01 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Evans Leeds rises 15% Evans of Leeds, the property investment company, reported a 15 per cent increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 4.06m to Pounds 4.67m for the half year to September 30. Gross rental income rose from Pounds 10.9m to Pounds 12m over the period, while the sale of stock properties contributed Pounds 298,000 (Pounds 236,000) to turnover of Pounds 12.3m (Pounds 11.1m). Earnings came out at 2.65p (2.38p) per share and the interim dividend is increased to 0.87p (0.79p).
