BFN [Report by Iqbal 'Ali 'Abdallah: "Yasin Nu'man Tells AL-HAYAH: There Are Military Movements Aimed at Eliminating All Southern Units in the North"] [Excerpts] Aden -- In the first statement by a leading Yemeni Socialist party [YSP] official on the serious military situation in some Yemeni areas, Dr. Yasin Sa'id Nu'man, chairman of the YSP Central Committee Secretariat, has told AL-HAYAH that there is an "attempt to eliminate the southern military units present in northern governorates, beginning with the 5th Brigade and the 3rd Tank Brigade in 'Imran. There are continued provocations aimed against the other units, especially the Basahib Brigade in Dhamar." [passage omitted] He stressed that, "despite the continued attempts to lift the siege on the southern brigades in the north and the remaining units of the 3rd Brigade in 'Imran, these efforts have been obstructed by a rejectionist position from the other side and the military command belonging to the General People's Congress [GPC] led by Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council." [passage omitted] He added: "We hold the GPC leadership fully responsible for all the dangers and seditions that may afflict the homeland as a result of these actions." The chairman of the YSP Central Committee Secretariat urged "political forces and the Dialogue Committee to condemn these ugly actions in order to prevent a repetition of the tragedies on which this leadership has embarked."
