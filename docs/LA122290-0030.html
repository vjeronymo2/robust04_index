December 22, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

THE TEST OF TIME

Re David J. Fox's article on the mixed reaction to "Godfather III" at its first industry screening (Dec. 14):

I for one can't wait to see the film.

I'm sure not everyone remembers this, but the reactions to three of the greatest films of the 1960s -- "Bonnie and Clyde," "2001" and "The Wild Bunch" -- were decidedly mixed. People either hated them or loved them; there was no middle ground. But with the passage of time all three are now considered classics.

STEVE BARR

Culver City
