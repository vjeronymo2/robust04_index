March 22, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

SOUTHLAND;

LOTTO PLAYER WINS $4.9 MILLION

A player who bought a ticket in Novato picked all six numbers to win the $4.9-million jackpot in the Lotto 6/49 game, the California Lottery said today.

The numbers drawn Wednesday night were 6, 11, 15, 28, 45 and 48 with the bonus number 43. Sales were $12.6 million.

In the 5 of 6 plus bonus category, five players each won $269,786. They purchased their lucky tickets in Alameda, Fresno, Lakewood and at two locations in Anaheim.

In the 5 of 6 category, 164 winners won $4,237.
