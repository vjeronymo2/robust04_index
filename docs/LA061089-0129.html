June 10, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ALTERNATIVE THEATRE

I am writing to commend Jan Herman on his coverage of the financial difficulties that the Alternative Repertory Theatre of Santa Ana is having.

Having seen every production that ART has mounted, I know what a cultural resource this theater company is becoming. Orange County needs this company's survival. It is gratifying to see The Times be so supportive of ART's endeavors.

I would encourage all of your readers to come and see what is happening at this intimate theater in Santa Ana. It is well worth the time and effort.

LORETTA BEAUMONT

Anaheim
