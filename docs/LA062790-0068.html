June 27, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The Anaheim city council approved final agreements with a New York developer for construction of a 19,500-seat indoor sports arena.

The proposed $100-million arena, tentatively scheduled to be completed by September of 1992, is the second major indoor sports facility planned for Orange County, following the Santa Ana city council's previous approval of a privately owned and operated 20,000-seat facility.
