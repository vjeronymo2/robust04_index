July 14, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

GETTING SOME NOTES ON READERS' SCORECARDS

Not a week goes by that Allan Malamud does not mention Tommy Lasorda in his column. And every other week Lasorda's mug is plastered on top of Opinions on a Scorecard . . . I mean Notes on a Scorecard.

Is Malamud being a reporter or a paid advertisement?

MARC BERNSTEIN

Los Angeles
