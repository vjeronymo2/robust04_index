910427 FT 27 APR 91 / Insurance warning LOSSES from US pollution and asbestosis claims may have a serious effect on the results of the whole of the Lloyd's marine insurance market, Mr John Prentice, chairman of the Wellington Underwriting Group, warned yesterday. Wellington said earlier this week it was asking Names (Lloyd's members) to provide more than Pounds 100m to cover losses incurred by two of the nine syndicates it manages.
