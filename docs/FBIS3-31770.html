Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Foreign Ministry Demand"] [Text] The Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] Foreign Ministry categorically refuted the France Press news on an alleged statement by Minister Crvenkovski during February 21 talks with the Netherlands counterpart Kooijmans, on Macedonia being ready to seek a compromise on the name issue, regardless of the latest trading embargo enforced by Athens. It is obvious that the word "not" is omitted after the word "Macedonia," without which the sentence makes no logical sense. Crvenkovski had explicitly stated in The Hague that Macedonia is ready to continue the negotiations with Greece, regardless of the Greek trading blockade imposed on Macedonia, the Foreign Ministry underlined.
