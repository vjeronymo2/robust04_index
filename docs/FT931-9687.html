930212 FT 12 FEB 93 / Ravenscraig plant buyer named THE INDONESIAN company negotiating to buy plant and equipment from British Steel's closed Ravenscraig steelworks in Lanarkshire was named yesterday as PT Gunawan Dianjaya, a family-run steel producer. According to the Glagow Herald newspaper a team of experts from Gunawan visited Ravenscraig recently, and a report is being studied at the company's headquarters in Surabaya, eastern Indonesia. British Steel yesterday confirmed it was holding talks with Gunawan but stressed that any agreement could be months away.
