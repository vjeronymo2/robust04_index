September 4, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

PEOPLE

William P. Taylor Jr. has been promoted to senior vice president/manager of corporate banking at Bank of Southern California. Sali D. Tice has been promoted to vice president/administration of branch operations at the bank. Stephanie A. Toomey has been promoted to senior vice president/branch manager and Fred Cutler has been promoted to senior vice president/manager of credit administration.
