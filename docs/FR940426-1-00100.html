To the extent that a Zero Emission Vehicle sales requirement must be a component of a LEV program adopted under section 177, such a requirement shall apply. Further, if such a Zero Emission Vehicle sales requirement is not a required component of programs adopted under section 177, individual States within the OTC may at their option include such a requirement and/or economic incentives designed to increase the sales of ZEVs in the programs they adopt.
