941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / The Labour Party in Blackpool: Blunkett calls on delegates to unite Mr David Blunkett, the party chairman and shadow health secretary, called on delegates to unite behind policies designed to achieve social justice and economic efficiency, Ivor Owen writes. The message sent out by the conference, he said, should confirm that Labour under the leadership of Mr Tony Blair was 'in tune with the British people'. Mr Blunkett looked forward to full employment and a national minimum wage 'going hand-in-hand together'. He said: 'Equity and the narrowing of the gap between rich and poor must be our watchword.'
