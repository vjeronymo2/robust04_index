BFN [Announcer-read report over still of regional map] [Text] A slight tremor was felt in Istanbul in the afternoon. There was no loss of life or damage to property. According to the information received from the Bogazici University Kandilli Observatory Directorate, the epicenter of the tremor was around Beykoz. It occurred at 1640 [1340 GMT] and registered 3.0 on the Richter scale.
