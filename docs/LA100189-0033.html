October 1, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

LAST DAYS

The litany about the loss of KFAC as a classical music station has offered little in the way of a practical stratagem to restore this asset. I believe the following has merit: Listen to the new KFAC long enough to identify the sponsors. Write a letter to them (with a copy to KFAC) saying you will not buy their goods or service as long as they support KFAC as a rock broadcaster. It is possible that without donations, without foundation grants and with the voice of those who love classical music, the Philistines may yet be beaten at their own money game.

LAWRENCE A. SHAPIRO

Corona del Mar
