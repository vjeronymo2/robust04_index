November 10, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

WALK OF STARS: Campaigners against development in Laguna Canyon are drawing on the city's sizable show-biz faction. . . . So far, Robert Englund (a.k.a. Freddie Krueger and the Phantom of the Opera, above) and Bette Midler have declared their support and say they will try to attend Saturday morning's canyon "Walk-In" (B3). Actress Julie Harris has posed for photos wearing a Save-the-Canyon sweat shirt. She said she loves Laguna from having visited friends there. Dana Parsons
