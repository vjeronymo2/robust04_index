January 5, 1990, Friday, South Bay Edition

SOUTH BAY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: ROUTE 103 CLOSES TONIGHT FOR FILMING

All northbound and southbound lanes of the Terminal Island Freeway (Route 103) between Willow Street in Long Beach and Henry Ford Avenue approaching Terminal Island will be closed from 8 p.m. tonight to midnight Sunday to allow a film to be shot on the roadway.

In addition, the freeway on- and off-ramps at Anaheim Street and Pacific Coast Highway will be closed during the same 28-hour period.
