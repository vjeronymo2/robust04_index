November 29, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: ARCADIA;

A GIANT GREETING FOR TROOPS

A 4-by-8-foot holiday card for soldiers in Saudi Arabia will be at Santa Anita Fashion Park through Sunday.

After it is signed by the public, the USO will deliver it to the Desert Shield troops.

Hours for card-signing are 10 a.m. to 9 p.m. today through Saturday, and 11 a.m. to 7 p.m. Sunday. The mall is at 400 S. Baldwin Ave.
