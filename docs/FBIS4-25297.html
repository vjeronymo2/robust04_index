BFN [Text] Moscow, June 6 (XINHUA) -- Inter-parliamentary cooperation was the main topic discussed here today by leaders of the Russian State Duma (lower house) and China's National People's Congress (NPC). Valentin Kovalev, deputy chairman of the Duma, met with the vice chairman of the NPC National Committee, Wang Guangying, head of the Chinese NPC delegation which had stopped over in Moscow on its way home from Malta. The two also discussed political and economic matters. Kovalev said that the Russian State Duma was satisfied with a recent visit to China by its Chairman Ivan Rybkin. Briefing the NPC delegation on Russian current affairs, he added that the Chinese and Russian parliaments should now develop a friendly and business-like relaionship.
