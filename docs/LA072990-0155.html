July 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

FIGHTING WORDS

U.S. English exists to see that the English language gains official status in government -- that no other languages become official.

We are a nation of immigrants with little in common -- not race, religion, culture, history. The only thing we have in common is the English language. That has been the mortar in the mosaic.

ANGIE PAPADAKIS

Rancho Palos Verdes
