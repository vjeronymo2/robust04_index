February 24, 1989, Friday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Marjo Matikainen became the first four-time medalist as Finland kept its home-track winning streak alive in the World Nordic Ski Championships at Lahti with a victory in the women's 20-kilometer relay.

Finland now has won all four events in the last three days and has six gold, three silver and three bronze medals.

The United States placed 11th out of 14 finishers in 59:23.1. Nancy Fiddler of Crowley Lake skied the fifth-fastest second leg.
