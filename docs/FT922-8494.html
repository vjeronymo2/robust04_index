920513 FT 13 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Contra-Cyclical meets forecast At March 31 1992, net asset values at Contra-Cyclical Investment Trust were 34.75p for the capital, 3.69p for the income, and 55.8p for the zero rated preferred shares. The trust was formed on February 26 1991 and started trading on March 28. For the initial period to March 31 1992, gross income totalled Pounds 1.68m and net revenue came to Pounds 1.12m. Earnings per income share were 13.94p and the final dividend is 4.5p for the forecast total of 11.25p.
