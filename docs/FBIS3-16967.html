Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Unemployment Growing in Ukraine"] [Text] As of the end of 1993, around 93,000 people were registered in Ukraine as unemployed. According to the Ukrainian Labor Ministry's forecast, the figure is expected to rise to 1 million by the end of 1994. Meanwhile, Ukrainian Deputy Prime Minister Valentin Landik has said that the number of people out of work will rise even higher following mass bankruptcies of enterprises. By year's end, up to 3.4 million of Ukraine's able-bodied citizens, that is, every ninth resident of the republic, will be fully or partially unemployed.
