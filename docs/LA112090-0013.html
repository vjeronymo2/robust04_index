November 20, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

HOWLETT'S 28 KILLS PROPEL FULLERTON

Becky Howlett had 28 kills to lead Cal State Fullerton to a four-game victory over Colorado State in the Titans' final women's volleyball match of the season Monday at Fullerton.

Angela Lightfoot had 13 digs as the Titans (8-30) won the nonconference match, 15-12, 15-6, 6-15, 15-13.
