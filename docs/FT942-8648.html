940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Siam Selective lifts net asset value 74% Siam Selective Growth Trust reported a 74 per cent rise in net asset value per share - from 150p to 261p - over the 12 months to end-March 1994. The Stock Exchange of Thailand Index rose 43 per cent over the same period. The deficit per share widened to 1.44p (1.34p) and the single distribution for the year is cut from 1p to 0.6p.
