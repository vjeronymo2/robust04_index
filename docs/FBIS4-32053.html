BFN [Text] Katowice, 18 Jun--The strike at Katowice Steelworks, which has been in effect since 1 June, was suspended this morning. The suspension will be in force until all demands are met and a new pay agreement coordinated, Mariusz Kwasniowski, of the steelworks' information office, reports. He added that as agreed between the strike committee and the management, each worker will get a pay raise of 1.5 million zlotys gross per month, with effect from 1 May.
