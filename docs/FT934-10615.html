931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Roxboro 12.9 times subscribed THE PLACING and offer for sale of shares in Roxboro, the Newmarket-based manufacturer of specialist electronic components, was completed yesterday with the offer 12.9 times subscribed. Under the placing 14.1m shares at 230p each were placed firm with institutional and other investors by Samuel Montagu. Valid applications totalling 98m shares were received for the balance of 7.61m shares offered for sale to the public and the basis of the allocation is: between 200 and 2,000 shares, weighted ballot for 200 shares; 2,500 to 10,000 shares, 8 per cent of application; 15,000 shares, 800 shares; 20,000 to 400,000, 5 per cent of application; more than 400,000, nil.
