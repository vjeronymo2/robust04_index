940615 FT 15 JUN 94 / UK Company News: River Plate General River Plate and General Investment Trust reported net asset value of 156.2p per capital share at April 30. The figure compared with values of 166.7p at the October year end and 124.7p 12 months earlier. Gross revenue for the six months was ahead at Pounds 3.41m (Pounds 3.15m); net revenue was Pounds 2.39m (Pounds 2.16m) leaving earnings per income share up from 4.01p to 4.44p. The interim dividend is held at 3p.
