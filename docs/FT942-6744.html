940528 FT 28 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Dunedin Worldwide Net asset value per ordinary share of Dunedin Worldwide Investment Trust was 880.5p at April 30 against 843.5p at the October 31 year-end and 702.5p 12 months earlier. Available revenue for the half year improved from Pounds 1.65m to Pounds 1.72m, for earnings per share of 5.05p (4.84p). The dividend is held at 2.4p.
