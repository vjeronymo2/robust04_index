940608 FT 08 JUN 94 / Call to exploit TV superhighway The UK is uniquely placed to take a global stake in 'the biggest commercial play of the next century' - the development of the information superhighway, Professor David Youlton, chairman of broadcasting technology group Snell and Wilcox, told the Royal Television Society last night. Prof Youlton, the visiting professor of business studies at Kingston University in London, said the UK could not claim pre-eminence in either telecommunications or computers. However it had 'the world's largest and most sophisticated knowledge base in the art of the moving image in real time combined with a deep understanding of all that happens between the transmitter and the receiver in the home.'
