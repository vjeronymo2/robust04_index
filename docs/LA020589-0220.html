February 5, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFING: EVENTS IN THE NEWS THIS WEEK.

Trial is set to begin Monday for six members of a Vietnamese gang charged with murder in a 1985 shoot-out at a Garden Grove restaurant that left two people dead and four others injured. Hung Ly, Houn Cheng Chou, Ninh Xuan Nguyen, Thanh Van Nguyen, Dat Tien Pham and Khan Quang Nguyen were charged in connection with the Nov. 7, 1985, shoot-out at My Nguyen Restaurant. The defendants have remained in jail since their arrests.
