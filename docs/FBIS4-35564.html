BFN [Text] The prime minister has dismissed Yasir 'Arafat's remarks that Jerusalem is the capital of Palestine. Mr. Rabin reacted to 'Arafat's remarks today, saying: He can talk. In a meeting of leaders of the Jewish National Fund at the Hyatt Hotel in Jerusalem Rabin stated: I heard that somebody in Tunis said something; I would like to declare here that united Jerusalem is the capital of the Jews and so it will remain forever. Our correspondent Esti Peres reported the prime minister's remarks.
