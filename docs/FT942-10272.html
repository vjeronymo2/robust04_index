940511 FT 11 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Hogg Robinson acquisition Hogg Robinson, the business services group, has acquired International Transportbedrijf G Snel of the Netherlands for an aggregate Fl 8m (Pounds 2.85m) in cash. The directors said the purchase, carried out by Hogg Robinson Transport, would complement the group's existing operations in the Netherlands, the UK and Belgium.
