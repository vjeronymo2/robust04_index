920909 FT 09 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Lasmo raises Dollars 85m from sale of Ultramar US offshoot LASMO, the independent oil company, has sold its last principal US upstream assets as part of a programme of disposals following last year's Pounds 1.1bn takeover of Ultramar. It is selling Ultramar Oil & Gas of Houston to Cody Resources, a private company, for about Dollars 85m (Pounds 42.5m). This should be completed in November and includes assets in Louisiana and Texas producing 3,500 barrels of oil and 45m cu ft of gas a day. Lasmo has also agreed to sell its share of the Bright Star gas gathering system in Texas for Dollars 15m, and various other properties in Texas and Louisiana are expected to bring in another Dollars 4m. The company has now raised Dollars 1.4bn of its target of Dollars 1.8bn from disposals for 1992 and 1993. They are designed to reduce net borrowings, which stood at Pounds 914m in July.
