DATES: May 31, 1994. FCC Reestablishes Charter for Small Business Advisory Committee AGENCY: Federal Communications Commission. ACTION: Notice of Advisory Committee Renewal.

SUMMARY: In accordance with the GSA Final Rule on Federal Advisory Committee Management, 41 CFR §§101&hyph;106, the Federal Communications Commission is giving official notice of the reestablishment of the charter for the Small Business Advisory Committee. The term of the current charter is for a two-year period and expires on May 31, 1996. DATES: May 31, 1994.
ADDRESSES: Federal Communications Commission, 1919 M Street, NW., Washington, DC 20554. FOR ADDITIONAL INFORMATION CONTACT: John Winston, Director, Office of Small Business Activities, Federal Communications Commission, at (202) 632&hyph;1571.
SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: The Small Business Advisory Committee advises the FCC on small business issues, including minority and female participation issues, with regard to reviewing existing policies, recommending changes to policies as appropriate, and promoting new telecommunications services.
