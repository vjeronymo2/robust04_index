930316 FT 16 MAR 93 / Large letting deal in West End THE largest letting deal in London's West End for more than two years has been agreed by McKinsey & Company, the management consultancy. It is to move its UK headquarters to Number One, Jermyn Street, part of the Criterion development at Piccadilly Circus. McKinsey, which will move out of St James's Street, has signed a 25-year lease on the 120,000 sq ft building at a rent of almost Pounds 40 per sq ft.
