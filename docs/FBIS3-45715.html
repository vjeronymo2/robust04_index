Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Munich, January 22 (XINHUA) -- Qiao shi, chairman of China's National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, today continued his visit to Munich, Germany. Qiao and his party, with great interest, visited the Deutsches Museum, one of the largest natural science and technology museum in the world. Later, Qiao visited the Munich Olympic stadium which was founded in 1906. This evening, Qiao will watch an opera "Clown".
