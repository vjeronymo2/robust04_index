930401 FT 01 APR 93 / International Company News: Loss at Von Roll steel group widens VON ROLL, the Swiss steel and engineering group, said its loss widened in 1992 to SFr55m (Dollars 36.7m) from SFr30m in 1991, and forecast a further loss this year, writes Ian Rodger in Zurich. Von Roll, in which Proventus of Sweden bought a 20 per cent stake in December 1991, said almost all of its business activities and important markets suffered last year.
