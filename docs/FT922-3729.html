920610 FT 10 JUN 92 / Names face Pounds 21m cash call MORE THAN 4,000 Lloyd's Names face a cash call for some Pounds 21m after an upsurge in claims at the Merrett syndicate 418, Richard Lapper writes. Total losses from business underwritten by the syndicate in the 1985 year now amount to Pounds 136.3m. Even after the current cash call of Pounds 21m, Names - individuals whose wealth backs the market - will be asked to pay a further Pounds 22.4m. The losses relate to asbestos and pollution-related claims.
