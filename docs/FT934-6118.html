931126 FT 26 NOV 93 / DVLA censured for licence 'errors' Hundreds of professional drivers lost their licences or had applications refused because of 'serious errors' by the Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency, says a report yesterday from Mr William Reid, the parliamentary ombudsman. One lorry driver whose licence was unjustly revoked is to be compensated, and the DVLA has agreed that a further 230 drivers can submit compensation claims.
