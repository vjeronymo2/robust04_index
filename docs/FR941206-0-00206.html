(a) This section applies to owners and operators of tanks electing to comply with §265.1085(b)(2) or (b)(3) of this subpart. (1) The owner or operator electing to comply with §265.1085(b)(2) of this subpart shall design, install, operate, and maintain a fixed roof and internal floating roof that meet the following requirements. (i) The fixed roof shall comply with the requirements of §265.1085(d)(1) of this subpart. The internal floating roof shall rest or float on the waste surface (but not necessarily in complete contact with it) inside a tank that has a fixed roof. The internal floating roof shall be floating on the waste surface at all times, except during initial fill and during those intervals when the tank is completely emptied or subsequently emptied and refilled. When the roof is resting on the leg supports, the process of filling, emptying, or refilling shall be continuous and shall be accomplished as rapidly as possible.
