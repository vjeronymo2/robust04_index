930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / London Stock Exchange: RTZ active RTZ shares dipped 3 to 637p, with turnover reported by Seaq reaching a hefty 9.8m shares, the heaviest for many months. Dealers noted a block trade of 5.5m shares transacted in the morning period at 582 1/2 p and a substantial number of following trades at 625p a share. RTZ is one of the six leading companies to have offered an enhanced scrip dividend to its shareholders since the changes incorporated in the budget earlier this year. The company was said to have issued almost 30 shares to holders as part of the scrip issue scheme.
