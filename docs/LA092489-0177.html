September 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: FERRY SCORES 25, LEADS ITALIAN TEAM TO WIN

Danny Ferry scored 25 points, and former Boston Celtic Brian Shaw added 19 as Messaggero Roma beat Vismara Cantu, 90-86, in an Italian Basketball League opener Saturday at Cantu, Italy.

Ferry, the No. 2 pick -- by the Clippers -- in the 1989 NBA draft, scored three of his points after Messaggero fell behind, 86-85, with less than two minutes left -- after leading by 19 points earlier.

Shaw added two points in the final seconds as a partisan crowd of 3,300 booed and whistled at the U.S. stars.
