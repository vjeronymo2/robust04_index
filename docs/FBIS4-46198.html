BFN [Text] Police yesterday issued arrest warrants for two Iranians allegedly involved in a foiled truck-bomb plot believed to have been aimed at the Israeli Embassy in March, Bangkok Metropolitan Police Chief Pol Lt Gen [Police Lieutenant General] Chaisit Kanchanakit said. However, Chaisit admitted that it was not possible that the men were still in Thailand. Mohammad Lotfollah and Hossein Shahriarifar, both about 40 years old, have been identified by police in connection with the plot. Police said they had asked Interpol to help. "We still don't know where they are living but we are certain that they left the country through the southern border of Thailand," Chaisit said. [background passage omitted]
