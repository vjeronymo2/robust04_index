July 29, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

'PHILOSOPHICAL BAGGAGE' OF EVOLUTION

According to Christian orthodoxy, faith is a gift given by grace, by God. That which can be proven does not require faith, which is the cornerstone of Christianity. One is "saved" by grace -- not by the state.

Fundamentalist Christians indulge in idolatry by demanding that the state perform a function which doctrinally belongs only to their God.

If fundamentalist children have their faith torn down "by talking about evolution as fact," their parents should address the problem to their God rather than to state curriculum commissions.

LAURIE HALL

Whittier
