920409 FT 09 APR 92 / UK Company News: Melville Group Melville Group, the exhibition services and interior fitters, announced pre-tax profits of Pounds 840,000 for the six months to December 31, compared with Pounds 1.76m. Turnover fell from Pounds 46.6m to Pounds 30.4m. Earnings per share were 1.08p (2.92p) and there is no dividend (1.6p). The company said it was withdrawing entirely from commercial property development by realising its remaining assets. It is recapitalising by raising Pounds 8.4m through a share subscription and placing for cash. The 45m new ordinary shares at 20p each will represent 50.3 per cent of the enlarged capital.
