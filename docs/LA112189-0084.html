November 21, 1989, Tuesday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE

Miami may be without its leading receiver and punt returner Saturday night when it plays No.1-ranked Notre Dame.

Wesley Carroll, who has 53 receptions for 770 yards and 32 punt returns for 381 yards, separated a shoulder last Saturday in the Hurricanes' 42-6 win over San Diego State and is listed as doubtful for Saturday's game at Miami.
