BFN [Report by Muhammad Daraghmah] [Excerpt] Nabulus -- Shaykh Hamid al-Baytawi, chairman of the League of Palestinian Ulema and one of the most prominent Islamic figures in Nabulus, has said that the Islamic Movement in Nabulus has no objection to participating in a temporary municipal council, headed by Attorney Bassam al-Shak'ah, to run the municipality's affairs and implement stalled vital projects. Shaykh Baytawi disclosed that various national and Islamic forces and institutions in the city have been holding consultations on forming a municipal council. He noted that the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine was the only front to reject such a council and adhere to the option of elections. [passage omitted]
