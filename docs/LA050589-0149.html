May 5, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: CALIFORNIA REAL ESTATE MAGAZINE AIMS EXCLUSIVELY AT THE JAPANESE MARKET;

REAL ESTATE

Japanese real estate investors can let their fingers do the walking. A new magazine pitched to them, California Real Estate Guide, will come off the presses in May.

Based in Costa Mesa, the guide will feature ads in Japanese on California commercial buildings and residences.

The magazine says it will have a mailing list of 2,500 real estate companies, banks, trading firms and individuals in Japan.
