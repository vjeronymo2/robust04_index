930403 FT 03 APR 93 / Norway's new foreign minister Mr Johan Joergen Holst was chosen yesterday as Norway's new foreign minister in succession to Mr Thorvald Stoltenberg who is taking over from Mr Cyrus Vance as one of the two international peace negotiators on the former Yugoslavia, Christopher Brown-Humes writes. Mr Holst will be succeeded as defence minister by Mr Joergen Kosmo, head of parliament's justice committee.
