920808 FT 08 AUG 92 / International Company News: Citicorp in Dollars 650m Percs issue CITICORP yesterday filed plans with the Securities and Exchange Commission to issue 32.5m depositary shares of a new series of preferred equity redemption cumulative stock (Percs). The Dollars 650m the banking group expects to raise from the issue will go towards boosting Citicorp's capital base.
