931202 FT 02 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Cantab Pharm Cantab Pharmaceuticals, which came to the market in October, reported higher third quarter pre-tax losses of Pounds 762,000, against Pounds 359,000. Turnover fell from Pounds 280,000 to Pounds 219,000. The figures for the nine months to September 30 showed losses of Pounds 2m (Pounds 825,000) on turnover of Pounds 837,000 (Pounds 1.45m). Three months losses per share were 14p (8p) for nine month figures of 37p (20p).
