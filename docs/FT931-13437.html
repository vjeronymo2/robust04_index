930123 FT 23 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Contra-Cyclical net asset value at 35.6p Contra-Cyclical Investment Trust saw its net asset value per capital share decline from 38.8p to 35.6p over the 12 months to December 31. Per income share the figure increased from 1p to 3.7p and for the zero dividend preferred share nav improved to 60.7p, against 54.4p. Net revenue for the nine months to the end of December fell to Pounds 677,000, compared with Pounds 788,000 for the period from incorporation in February 1991 to December 31 1991. Earnings per income share came out at 8.46p (9.85p). The third interim dividend is maintained at 2.25p.
