Although he talked about some forthcoming "changes" what he will bring about is nothing but national disaster such as deepening of dependence on the United States and Japan and nuclear scourge. Patriotism and treachery are incompatible. Although Kim Yong-sam tries to lull the growing complaints of the South Korean people against the "civilian" rule with the mask of "patriotism" it is a mistake. The South Korean people will never pardon the Kim Yong-sam clique which is engaged in treacheries while indiscreetly insulting the patriotic spirit of the forerunners.
