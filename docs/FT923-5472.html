920901 FT 01 SEP 92 / Russia-Belarus debt deal Russia has agreed to pay Belarus's portion of the former Soviet Union's foreign debt in return for its neighbour's share of former Soviet assets, Itar-Tass news agency said yesterday, Reuter reports from Moscow It said Moscow had agreed to pay Belarus's share of Dollars 3.5bn (Pounds 1.8bn) out of the total outstanding foreign debt of around Dollars 70bn. In return, Russia will receive assets including part of the Belarussian entitlement to former Soviet property abroad, under an agreement published by the Belarussian government.
