920302 FT 02 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Waterman Prtnrshp Pounds 235,000 in red Waterman Partnership, consulting civil and structural engineer, incurred a loss of Pounds 235,000 in the half year ended December 31 1991, compared with a profit of Pounds 306,000. Work done fell to Pounds 3.84m (Pounds 6.22m) and there were further reorganisation costs and provisions, explained managing director Mr Bob Campbell. However, the group remained cash positive and an interim dividend of 0.5p is declared - half the previous payment. Losses per share were 0.8p, against earnings of 1p.
