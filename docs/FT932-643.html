930628 FT 28 JUN 93 / Company failures fall by 20% THE NUMBER of company failures fell by 20 per cent in the second quarter, says Austintel, a Worthing-based company which specialises in providing information about insolvencies. Corporate liquidations and receiverships fell to 5,430 in the second quarter from 6,837 in the first quarter. Of the second-quarter failures, 633 were receiverships. The figure was well down on the 1,040 registered in the second quarter of last year. In the second quarter, 2,880 companies went into voluntary liquidation compared with 3,070 in the corresponding period last year. Between the first and second quarters of this year personal bankruptcies fell 18 per cent to 7,417.
