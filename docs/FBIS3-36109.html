Language: Kazakh Article Type:BFN [Text] Today Vice President Yerik Asanbayev received the Vietnamese Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam, who is on a visit to Almaty. Touching upon the Kazakh domestic situation, Asanbayev spoke about the preparation for the forthcoming elections to a permanent parliament. Elections will be carried out openly. Nguen Manh Cam supported the idea of the gradual development of relations between Kazakhstan and Vietnam. Talks were held between the foreign ministers. Nguen Manh Cam highly evaluated an initiative by President Nursultan Nazarbayev on convening a conference of confidence building measures in Asia. The foreign ministers signed an inter-governmental agreement on trade and economic cooperation and a protocol on consultations between the two countries' Foreign Ministries. Nguen Manh Cam left for Uzbekistan in the evening.
