Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] While participating in the ceremony marking the fourth anniversary of the Cuban-Chinese friendship cooperative for agriculture and livestock production in the municipality of Cifuentes in Villa Clara, Chinese Ambassador to Cuba Xu Yicong said that bilateral relations with Cuba are developing satisfactorily for both countries. We have many reasons to seek the strengthening of Cuban-Chinese relations of friendship and cooperation, because the two countries have the same principles and ideals uniting us closely, Xu Yicong said. The Chinese ambassador added that political relations between the two countries are also growing closer every day, trade is increasing, and economic cooperation is developing quickly. He said that the Joint Commission for Cuban-Chinese Cooperation will meet in March in Havana to study trade prospects between the two nations. In Santa Clara, Xu Yicong visited the installation of what will be the largest Cuban bicycle factory with Chinese technology. This factory will be able to produce 150,000 units annually.
