Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A responsible source at the Algerian airlines [Air-Algerie] company today confirmed that an Air-Algerie Boeing 727 airliner was hijacked today to Alicante airport, Spain. The plane was on a scheduled Oran-Annaba flight with 122 passengers on board, including a baby, and nine crew members. According to the Spanish press agency, the number of hijackers is thought to be three, and it is believed that they are asking for political asylum in Spain. According to Spanish television, strict security measures were taken in Alicante airport, while the authorities started contacts with the plane's captain.
