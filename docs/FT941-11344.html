940204 FT 04 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Hong Kong Inv Tst net assets leap The Hong Kong Investment Trust reported a net asset value of 109.3p per share as at December 31. The figure represented a substantial advance on values of 60.6p at the trust's year-end in June and 44.4p at the end of 1992. Net revenue was flat at Pounds 178,996 (Pounds 179,406) for earnings of 0.89p (0.9p) per share. The interim dividend is maintained at 0.75p.
