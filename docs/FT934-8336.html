931116 FT 16 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Spirax-Sarco Engineering SPIRAX-SARCO Engineering has announced that the reference price in respect of the enhanced scrip dividend alternative is 348.44p. Accordingly the UBS cash offer price is 341.47p. Elections were received for the enhanced scrip in respect of 72.9m shares (97 per cent), out of which acceptances of the UBS cash offer were received for 9m shares.
