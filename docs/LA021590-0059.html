February 15, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Touchstone Announces Another Baby: The baby may be bigger, but the three men will be the same in the upcoming sequel to the successful 1987 comedy "Three Men and a Baby."

Touchstone Pictures announced this week that sequel "Three Men and a Little Lady" will star Tom Selleck, Steve Guttenberg and Ted Danson as the bachelor dads. The baby has aged to 5. The director will be Emile Ardolino, known for "Dirty Dancing." The movie should be out the end of the year. BETH KLEID
