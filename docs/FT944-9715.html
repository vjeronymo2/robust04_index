941114 FT 14 NOV 94 / Company News (This Week): National Australia Bank - Other companies The strongest of Australia's 'big four' banking groups is expected to announce after-tax profits of about ADollars 1.7bn (Dollars 1.25bn) on Thursday for the year to end-September. In 1992-93, the bank made Dollars 1.13bn after tax including a ADollars 59m abnormal gain. The strong economic recovery in Australia is benefiting the banks, as strong figures from Westpac demonstrated last week. NAB has already expressed confidence about repeating the first half's ADollars 805m (before abnormals) in the second six months.
