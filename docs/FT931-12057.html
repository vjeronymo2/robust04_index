930130 FT 30 JAN 93 / Announcement of Saudi defence deal greeted by Westland as 'very good news' John Major's announcement on Thursday of the Pounds 4bn-Pounds 5bn Saudi defence deal, including 48 Tornados, was greeted by Westland as 'very good news'. British officials are optimistic that other orders will now follow. But apart from Saudi Arabia, the Tornado has found no clients outside the three manufacturing countries.
