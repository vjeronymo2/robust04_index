BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 4 (KCNA) -- The idea, theory and policies set forth by the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il in his historical letter "On Further Improving the Work of the Trade Unions" have displayed their great vitality in practice and their validity and attraction have been fully proven, says NODONG SINMUN today in a signed article devoted to the tenth anniversary of the publication of the letter. In his work Comrade Kim Chong-il set forth the highly important tasks to build, strengthen and develop the trade unions including the tasks to push ahead with the three revolutions, ideological, technical and cultural, further enhance the role of trade union organizations in the socialist construction, firmly build up the trade union ranks and enhance the militant function of the trade union organizations. Referring to the validity and vitality of the work, the article says: They are evidently manifested in the fact that continued innovations and upswing are being effected in the socialist construction because, with the three revolutions successfully carried out, the Korean working class has been well prepared as faithful ranks which hold in esteem the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il in one mind of loyalty and filial piety, and the leading and vanguard role of the working class has been enhanced. Technical and cultural revolutions, along with the ideological revolution, have been pushed ahead among the working class and trade union members. As a result, their technical and cultural standard is constantly increasing and their sense of duty and role in carrying out the technical revolution is growing more and more. With the promotion of creative cooperation between workers and scientists and technicians, scientific and technical problems arising in putting the national economy on a chuche-oriented, modernized and scientific basis have been satisfactorily solved. Thus, the chuche character and independence of our industry have been consistently strengthened and production is going up at a fast speed. Through the efforts to implement the idea and theory and policies set forth in the work, the party's economic policy propaganda and economic agitation have been activated and various forms of mass movements and socialist emulation drives have been dynamically conducted by the trade union organizations to powerfully encourage the working class and union members to the socialist economic construction. In the flame of the socialist emulation drives our working class and trade union members made big innovative achievements in the fulfilment of the third seven-year plan and successfully carried out large-scale projects for the everlasting prosperity of the country. The validity and vitality are also expressed in that the trade union ranks have been firmly built up and their militant function and role enhanced considerably, the article says.
