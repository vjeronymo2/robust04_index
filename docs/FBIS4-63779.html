BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 23/5/1994 -- Today, 23 May, President Mircea Snegur and Speaker Petru Lucinschi conferred with leaders of the districts densely inhabited by Gagauzians, states a presidential press service communique. They discussed the draft law on special juridical status for these districts. "The sides examined in detail all difficulties taking into consideration the analysis representatives. They discussed especially the controversial issues and proposed compromise solutions that could be accepted by the vast majority of the Moldovan population. The solutions are founded on political methods and experience of the international organizations," says the government and Parliament, says the communique.
