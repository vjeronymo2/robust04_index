940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Newman Tonks Dollars 6.8m acquisition Newman Tonks Group, the building products group, has acquired Hartmann-Sanders, a maker of architectural timber columns based in Georgia for Dollars 6.8m (Pounds 4.65m) cash. Hartmann reported 1993 operating profits of Dollars 900,000 on sales of Dollars 5.1m. Net tangible assets were Dollars 1.5m.
