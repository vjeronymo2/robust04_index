BFN [Text] Algeria is prepared to enter into partnership with any foreign bank that wishes to work in Algeria, on the basis of competition in the banking system which will come into force soon. This was stated by Finance Minister Ahmed Benbitour in a televised interview on the recent agreement between Algeria and the IMF. The minister said that Algerian banking, as a vital element in the stage of economic stability, is now subject to restructuring much the same as other institutions.
