Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Feb. 3 (YONHAP) -- Ku Pyong-hoe, chairman of Lucky-Goldstar International Group, was elected chairman of the Korea Foreign Trade Association (KFTA) Thursday in a general meeting of member companies held at the Korea Exhibition Center. More than 500 representatives of the companies also elected Pak Yong-hak, former chairman of KFTA, as honorary chairman. Ku, the 22nd chairman of KFTA, said in his inaugural address that in view of the current trend of internationalization and economic opening, South Korea faces a critical moment on the road to becoming an advanced country. "KFTA will strengthen information and other services for member companies as well as maintaining close cooperation with the government, thus playing a leading role in realizing the internationalization of South Korean businesses," he said.
