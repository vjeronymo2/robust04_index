931127 FT 27 NOV 93 / BT price cut British Telecommunications announced yesterday it plans to enlarge its local call areas for many rural communities. At cheap-rate times, some rates now classed as long distance will be cut to 5p for three minutes. The changes, to come into effect on January 12, will mean cheaper calls for parts of the Highlands and Islands, Wales, the Lake District, the Pennines, south-west England, the Scilly Isles and Northern Ireland.
