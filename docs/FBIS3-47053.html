Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 6 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The Minister of Construction and Town-Building Ts. Damiran in an interview with the newspaper "ARDIYN ERH" correspondent announces that under conditions of the country's transiting to market relations also a system of settlement, population service and supply should be renewed. In T. Damiran's view, regioning of the country's territory, optimum location of the main centres of regions with corresponding infrastructure acquires now special importance. He notes that a priority development of the basic centers of regions will enable to organize properly the population service in different spheres of economy and culture, reduce a tendency for overcentralization of citizens in the capital. T. Damiran considers that an advantage of regional development is more wide opportunities for cooperation of aymags in use of raw materials, natural wealth and labour resources, in use of pastures, in creation of conditions for the population for free living in different regions. As T. Damiran notes, foundation of the main regional centers will enable to combine rationally nomadic and settled way of life of the population, ensure a balanced development of regions, rationally organize the ture [as received] and environment protection, develop tourism.
