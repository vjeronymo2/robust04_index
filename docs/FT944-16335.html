941013 FT 13 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Intracom - Contracts Intracom, the Greek telecommunications and information company, yesterday signed a contract to modernise telephone communications at the Kremlin. The one-year turn-key project involves installation of an AXE-10 Digital Exchange. Intracom manufactures the AXE-10 under licence from Ericsson of Sweden. Foreign Staff, London
