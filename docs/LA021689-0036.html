February 16, 1989, Thursday, South Bay Edition

SOUTH BAY DIGEST: LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

FUNDS FOR PALOS VERDES DRIVE WORK

Rolling Hills will allocate $7,200 of the federal funds it will receive this fiscal year to the city of Rolling Hills Estates for reconstruction of Palos Verdes Drive North from Crenshaw Boulevard to Sunnyfield Drive.

Rolling Hills, a private and gated community, cannot use the money since the funds may only be spent on public streets. Palos Verdes Drive North in Rolling Hills Estates is a principal access road to Rolling Hills.

Plans for reconstruction of the 1 1/4-mile stretch will be put before the Rolling Hills Estates City Council Feb. 28. The cost of the project has not been set.
