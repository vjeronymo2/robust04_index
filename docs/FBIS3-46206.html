Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Last year Jiangxi registered a major increase in foreign trade. Its total imports and exports topped $1 billion for the first time and now stand at $1.13 billion, a net increase of $212 million, up 23.48 percent over the previous year, which is 5.28 percent higher than the country's average. Foreign-invested enterprises' exports continued to grow by a wide margin and registered an increase of 71.42 percent over the previous year, 26 percent higher than the growth rate of foreign-invested enterprises' exports throughout the country. Their proportion to the province's total exports rose from 5.5 percent in the previous year to 8.28 percent.
