BFN [Text] At an official ceremony at the Boyana residence on 29 June, Finance Minister Stoyan Aleksandrov signed the documents on settling the country's foreign debt to the London Club creditors. Peter Tils, Deutsche Bank executive director, and Dr. Hans-Dirk Kleckerer of the Deutsche Bank signed the basic agreements that regulate the replacement of our present debts to the banks by state securities, or reduced payments. Lee Warner, director of settlements for Citibank in New York, signed an agreement on the fiscal aspects of the settlement, the reduced initial interest payments, and the postponed interest payments. Representatives of 12 banks, which are our basic creditors from the London Club, signed an annex to the agreement that settles our direct debts to them. The ceremony was attended by Prime Minister Lyuben Berov.
