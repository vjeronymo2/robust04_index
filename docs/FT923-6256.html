920826 FT 26 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Porth loss at Pounds 1.84m in unseasonal half Porth Group, the USM-quoted Christmas decorations manufacturer, reported a deficit of Pounds 1.84m pre-tax in its seasonally unfavourable half year to June 30. The outcome, which compared with losses of Pounds 1.82m at the same stage of 1991, was struck after exceptional charges of Pounds 292,000 (Pounds 105,000) to cover restructuring costs. Turnover amounted to Pounds 2.76m (Pounds 2.47m). Interest payable, reflecting the proceeds of last year's rights issue, fell to Pounds 180,000 (Pounds 423,000). Losses per share were reduced to 9.6p (20.76p).
