920109 FT 09 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Hadleigh Industries moves into loss A 20 per cent decrease in sales and exceptional charges of Pounds 214,000 hit Hadleigh Industries, the industrial group connected with transport. It ran into a pre-tax loss of Pounds 283,000 in the half year to September 30, compared to a profit of Pounds 1.1m. Exceptional charges covered plant closure and redundancies. The directors declared an interim dividend of 1.25p, a reduction of 0.95p. Gearing rose from 44 per cent to 84 per cent mainly because of the purchase of Truckline. It should fall in the current six months. Sales were Pounds 14m (Pounds 17.5m) and operating profit came to Pounds 227,000 (Pounds 1.17m). Interest charges increased to Pounds 296,000 (Pounds 67,000). Losses per share were 2.5p (earnings 10.4p).
