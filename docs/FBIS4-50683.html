BFN [By reporter Wang Bixue (3769 3024 1331): "Ministry of Public Security Trains Over 2,700 County, City Public Security Bureau Chiefs, Commissars in Past Three Years"] [Text] Beijing, 23 Apr (RENMIN RIBAO)--This reporter learned from the Ministry of Public Security today that in order to improve the quality of the cadres who supervise grass-roots public security organs, and to meet the demand of public security work under the new situation of reform and opening up, since 1991, the Ministry of Public Security has offered training to chiefs and political commissars from county and city public security bureaus throughout the country, training 2,700 persons in three years. Over the past few years, the Ministry of Public Security party committee has attached great importance to the training of chiefs and political commissars from county and city public security bureaus and has adopted a serious of effective measures: In 1992 and 1993, it organized seminars for chiefs of public security departments and bureaus, inspection tours for senior police officers, and study classes on learning from volume three of the Selected Works of Deng Xiaoping. Over the past three years, various public security departments and bureaus have also trained over 6,000 deputy chiefs and political commissars of county and city public security bureaus, and nearly 100,000 policemen of various arms of the police force have obtained on-the-job training certificates. Wang Zikang, director of the training and education section in the political department of the Ministry of Public Security, revealed that from now on, for every five-year period, the Ministry of Public Security will arrange training with different contents and duration according to changes in the situation and duties.
