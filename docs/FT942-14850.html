940419 FT 19 APR 94 / Calls grow for Haiti invasion A second US congressman has raised the issue of an American military invasion of Haiti, Reuter reports from Washington. Senator John Kerry, a Massachusetts Democrat, said on Sunday: 'I don't think it is the first step, but it is certainly high on my list of options, depending on what the scenario afterwards would be.' Last week, Representative David Obey said US forces should invade Haiti to remove its military government.
