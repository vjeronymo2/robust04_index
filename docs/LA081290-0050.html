August 12, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SCORES OF CRUISE CHOICES

Cruises: Holland America offers about 70 Alaska cruises during the May-to-September season.

The Westerdam, Rotterdam, Nieuw Amsterdam and Noordam are the backbone of the fleet. Prices per week start at about $1,000 per person, double occupancy. Get booking information from travel agents or Holland America Line, 300 Elliott Ave. W., Seattle, Wash. 98119, (800) 426-0327.

Alaska Sightseeing Tours runs the Sheltered Seas on a May-to-September, three- and five-day cruise schedule. The yacht can carry about 100 passengers.

For more information: Contact travel agents or TravAlaska, 4th and Battery Building, Suite 700, Seattle, Wash. 98121, (800) 426 7702. Arturo Gonzalez
