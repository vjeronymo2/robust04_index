940205 FT 05 FEB 94 / Gatt praises Australia Australia's programme of economic and trade reform yesterday wonpraise from trading partners as a model for others to follow, Frances Williams reports from Geneva. A Gatt secretariat report on the country's trade policies and practices, says Australia has pressed ahead with far-reaching reforms despite recession and big balance of payments deficits. However, Gatt chides Australia for high tariffs on items such as cars (up to 30 per cent) and clothing (up to 47 per cent).
