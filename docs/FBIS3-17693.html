Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Georgian armed formation that stayed in the mountains in Gulripsha region, Abkhazia "has been routed and no longer threatens the Abkhazian capital Sukhumi." The statement was made in a Monday interview with Interfax by the Abkhazian Defence Minister Sultan Sosnaliev. At the Abkhazian Defence Ministry Interfax was told that Georgian troops are besieged in Azhara community in the mountains. Abkhazian forces also control the Kodor canyon.
