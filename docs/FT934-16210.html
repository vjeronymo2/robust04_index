931007 FT 07 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Sale profit boosts Ex-Lands to Pounds 0.91m Profit on the sale of a subsidiary enabled Ex-Lands to report increased pre-tax profits of Pounds 914,000 for the year to June 30, against Pounds 65,000. Turnover was little changed at Pounds 1.27m (Pounds 1.25m) for operating losses of Pounds 1.3m (Pounds 1.22m). The sale profit came to Pounds 1.4m making up for lower interest income of Pounds 807,000 (Pounds 1.28m). Earnings per share were 1.07p (0.1p) and the company is returning to the dividend list with a proposed final of 0.35p.
