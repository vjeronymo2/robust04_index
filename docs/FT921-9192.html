920213 FT 13 FEB 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG closed generally firmer in thin trading. The JSE index rose 23 to 3,690 while the all-gold index put on 26 to 1,314. Industrials gained 23 to 4,514. Vaal Reefs put on R4.50 to R228.50 as world gold prices firmed to Dollars 357 an ounce.
