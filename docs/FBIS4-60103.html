BFN [Excerpts] The unifying Friday prayers were held throughout the country simultaneously today. In Tehran, the prayers were led by Hojjat ol-Eslam val Moslemin Hashemi-Rafsanjani. [passage omitted] In his second sermon, the president of the Islamic Republic of Iran pointed to the restrictions placed by the Saudi government on the holding of the disavowal of infidels ceremony. He said: The Saudis could have acted more sensibly and not caused differences between the two important countries of the region. Today, Islamic unity is a principle as important for us as the disavowal of infidels principle. He added: Considering the crisis points in various parts of the world, and with the wise leadership of the honorable leader of the Islamic revolution [Khamene'i], another crisis was averted over this matter. The president advised the Saudis to think harder and to take on more correct methods in future years. He said: We hope that in the future, a program on the hajj acceptable to both countries will be formulated which will take into account the views of both sides. [passage omitted]
