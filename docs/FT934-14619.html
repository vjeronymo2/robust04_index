931015 FT 15 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Maunders declines to Pounds 3.84m JOHN MAUNDERS, the resid-ential property developer, suffered a profits fall from a restated Pounds 4.23m to Pounds 3.84m pre-tax for the year to end-June. Turnover improved to Pounds 59.8m (Pounds 52m) while legal completions rose from 775 to 904. Interest charges were reduced to Pounds 1.54m (Pounds 1.83m). The 'introduction of a new product range, an excellent portfolio of land, modest gearing and a satisfactory forward sales position', prompted directors to recommend an increased final dividend of 2.85p, making a 5.15p (4.95p) total. Earnings per share emerged at 10.36p (11.52p).
