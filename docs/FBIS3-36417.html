Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Ivan Novikov] [Text] Moscow February 4 TASS -- On Friday, the Russian State Duma adopted a resolution, sponsored by Duma chairman Ivan Rybkin, determining the chamber's structure and staff. The Duma's activity will be supported by 677 full-time staffers, not including technicians. In addition, faction and deputy group apparatuses are to be manned by 132 people, along with the 386 support personnel approved for the Duma's committees and commissions. The Duma's apparatus is to have the status of a legal entity. It was suggested that the Duma chairman continue working on the formation of the apparatus in accordance with the chamber's structure. It was resolved that workers in the parliament's lower chamber will be considered equal to comparable workers in the apparatus of the Russian Federation Government in terms of salary, and social, material, and medical support. Finally, the Duma resolved that its leadership shall request the government to take the appropriate steps towards implementing this resolution.
