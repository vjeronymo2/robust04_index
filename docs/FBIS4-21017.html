Operation LD2603151394 Paris France-2 Teletext in French 1339 GMT 26 Mar 94 LD2603151394 Paris France-2 Teletext French BFN [Text] The Dutch, Belgian, and French police forces carried out a joint anti-drug operation on Friday night [25 March]. Over 1,000 vehicles were stopped for controls on Dutch, Belgian, and French highways in an operation involving 700 police and customs officers. Seventy dealers and nine drug users were arrested in the course of the operation. The Dutch police described the joint operation as a success and said they did not rule out similar cooperation in the future.
