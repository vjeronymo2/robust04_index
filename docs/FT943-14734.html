940713 FT 13 JUL 94 / People: Washington moves to Mansfield The Coal Authority, the new agency which will regulate the privatised coal industry, took a further step towards reality yesterday with the appointment of a chief executive designate. He is Neville Washington, currently director of human resources and operations at the Victoria Infirmary, an NHS trust hospital in Glasgow. Before he took that job in 1992 he was a lieutenant colonel in the army. He is 46. The Coal Authority already has a chairman, Sir David White, and will be based in Mansfield, Nottinghamshire.
