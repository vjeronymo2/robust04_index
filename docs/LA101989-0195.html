October 19, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

BARRED JOBS

Re: "Ominous Message for Women" (Oct. 11):

"Fetal protection policy?" Barring all women of child-bearing age from certain jobs? This outrageous decision was upheld by the U.S. 7th Circuit Court of Appeals?

I must be hallucinating back to the turn of the century. I am outraged, and rapidly becoming militantly feminist. We women must defend ourselves against such medieval policies!

KATHRYN S. TARBELL

Huntington Beach
