BFN [Text] The postponement of the pope's visit to Lebanon was the focus of a meeting yesterday between President Ilyas al-Hirawi and His Eminence Shaykh Muhammad Mahdi Shams-al-Din, chairman of the Higher Islamic Shiite Council. Shaykh Shams-al-Din said: We expressed our regret over the postponement of the pope's visit, which all Lebanese welcomed and prepared for to ensure its success. He said: We have already expressed our regret and repeat it now. He added: We pray to God almighty that the pope's circumstances will allow him to visit. He said: From this forum, the home of all Lebanese, from the Presidential Palace, I repeat my wish that his holiness will visit Lebanon as soon as possible because the Lebanese people strongly desire to see this visit take place. Also, the Lebanese Government wants the pope to visit. President al- Hirawi has just expressed the same desire and urges the pope to visit Lebanon as soon as possible. We hope the competent departments in the Vatican will continue to prepare for the visit and reconsider the decision postponing the visit. He called on those who question Lebanon's unity to stop putting this unity to the test, stressing that Lebanon's unity is safe and has passed every test. He emphasized that Lebanon is too secure for any attempt to fragment and divide it.
