Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] Pakistan has proposed hosting a regional peace conference on Afghanistan. Foreign Minister Sardar Asif Ahmad Ali told newsmen in Islamabad this evening that he has apprised the visiting Afghan delegation led by President Rabbani's special emissary, Engineer Ahmad Shah Amedzai, about this proposal. The foreign minister said it is the first time that all countries in the region have urged Pakistan to take initiative to end fighting in Afghanistan and restore peace there. When asked about the latest situation in Kabul, the foreign minister described it as bad. He said no one will emerge victorious in these hostilities and he felt that all factions are suffering losses.
