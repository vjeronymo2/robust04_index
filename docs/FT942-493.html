940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / World Trade News: Siemens in Singapore deal Siemens and Singapore's Mass Rapid Transit Corp (MRTC) have signed a memorandum of understanding to work jointly in transportation projects in the Asia-Pacific region, Reuter reports from Singapore. The agreement between MRTC, which owns the island's underground rail system, and Siemens Transportation Systems Group is part of a Siemens plan to join forces with Singapore companies for regional projects. The MRTC said it may wish to involve Singapore companies in its projects with Siemens. It said this may include, but not be limited to, equity funding.
