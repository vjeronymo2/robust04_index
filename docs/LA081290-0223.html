August 12, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

REAL LIFE

I just wanted to say how I enjoy all the "true life" informative programs now on TV -- most of all "Rescue 911" -- and the rest, i.e., "Cops," "Unsolved Mysteries," "PrimeTime Live," "48 Hours," "America's Most Wanted," "20/20."

I loathe the myriad of moronic sitcoms and unfortunately realize I'm in the minority since the networks base popularity on the inaccurate A.C. Nielsen ratings, which is wrong!

Anyway, I'm afraid "911" will be canceled since it seems to me the repeats began very early.

Annette Mason, Inglewood

Not to worry. CBS rescued "911," which will return this fall, Tuesdays at 8 p.m.
