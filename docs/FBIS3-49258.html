Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 7 Jan -- At 1000 this morning a delegation of U.S. politicians and military personnel arrived in Warsaw. The delegation is led by Ambassador Ms. Madeleine Albright, President Bill Clinton's special envoy. General John Shalikashvili, head of the U.S. Joint Chiefs of Staff, is among the members of the delegation. The guests will be received by the president and the prime minister and by the foreign and defense ministers. Talks with defense ministers of the Visegrad Group, who are meeting in Warsaw, are also expected.
