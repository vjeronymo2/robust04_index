Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Feb 3 (A.A) -- The 12th term Turkey-Iran joint economic commission meetings are scheduled to take place in Teheran between Feb 5-10 the Trade and Treasury Under Secretariat said in a statement on Thursday [3 February]. The statement also said that State Minister Cemil Erhan will head the Turkish delegation at the meetings which will be conducted on the technical level in the form of `trade and banking', `economic, industrial and technical cooperation,' and `communication,' committees. A forth committee will regulate coordination between the other three and will be responsible for writing documents that will be signed by the ministers. During the meetings, the increase of export to Iran, border trade, trade relations on oil with Iran, contractor services, exchanges of electrical energy, and precaution to prevent delays in payment as well as double taxation will be discussed. A memorandum of understanding is expected to be signed at the end of the meetings, the statement said. Erhan and accompanying delegation will return to Turkey on Feb 10.
