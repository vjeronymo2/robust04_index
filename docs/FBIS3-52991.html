Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali has received a written message from Turkish President Suleyman Demirel, which was delivered to his excellency at Carthage Palace this morning by Turkish Defense Minister Mehmet Golhan, in the presence of Minister of National Defense Abdelaziz Ben Dhia. [passage omitted] The Turkish defense minister pointed out that the meeting enabled them to review cooperation between the two fraternal countries, as well as the situation in the countries of the former Soviet Union and former Yugoslavia and said: We agreed to further bolster cooperation between Tunisia and Turkey, thanks to the similarities between them as they are both Islamic countries and could achieve constructive progress thanks to their success in reconciling Islamic values with modernity values. [passage omitted] The Turkish minister pointed to the close bonds of rapprochement between the Turkish and Tunisian peoples, and hoped that bilateral cooperation would rise to the level of those ties. He said Tunisia and Turkey represent an element of peace and stability and are capable, due to the efforts they made in this respect, to further bolster their roles in the future as citadels of peace and stability. In conclusion, he voiced hope that 1994 would be a year of peace in the world.
