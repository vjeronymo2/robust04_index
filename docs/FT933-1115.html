930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / Interest in ethics codes increases A THIRD of large UK companies have codes of ethics or are developing them, says a survey released yesterday by the Institute of Business Ethics. The proportion of companies that have published codes is 28 per cent, the same level as two years ago. But the survey detected a further 5 per cent of companies working on codes. The codes typically cover topics such as environmental responsibilities, conflicts of interest, 'whistle blowing', and receiving entertainment and gifts from suppliers. Code of Business Ethics. Institute of Business Ethics, 12 Palace Street, London SW1. Pounds 10.
