BFN [Text] Over 200 Russian public organizations have expressed their readiness on 5 May to join the treaty on social accord, which was signed 28 April by the country's main political forces. This was announced by Sergey Filatov, head of the Russian president's administration. The treaty envisions rejection of violent actions to achieve political goals and the government has undertaken a number of obligations with a view to overcoming the socioeconomic crisis.
