921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / Hitachi and GE in lighting venture General Electric of the US and Hitachi, two of the world's biggest electrical and industrial combines, yesterday announced a lighting equipment joint-venture to be based in Tokyo, writes Charles Leadbeater in Tokyo. The two, which already co-operate in manufacturing heavy-duty power generation equipment, are to set up Hitachi GE Lighting. It will give GE access to Hitachi's extensive Japanese distribution network, while Hitachi will gain access to GE's advanced lighting technology.
