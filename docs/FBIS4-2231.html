BFN [Text] Beijing, April 8 (XINHUA) -- Lei Jieqiong, vice-chairman of China's National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, met with a delegation from Kazakhstan led by Procurator-General Tuyakbaev here this afternoon. Lei said China and Kazakhstan have enjoyed very good relations and contacts between high-level officials increased since the two countries established diplomatic ties in 1992. Strengthening mutual understanding and friendship between the judicial departments will be beneficial to the growth of bilateral ties, she said. Tuyakbaev said the Kazakhstan Government and people are looking forward to Chinese Premier Li Peng's forthcoming visit to their country, believing that this visit will give an impetus to the existing good bilateral ties and trade. During the meeting Lei briefed the visitors on the NPC's composition and functions.
