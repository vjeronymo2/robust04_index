Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The president of the Republic, Lieutenant General 'Umar al-Bashir, has confirmed the revolution's adherence to the cultural course accepted by the Sudanese people of making god's law their arbiter. He said that the dogfight of the enemies and those lying in wait for us would only make us more determined to move forward to establish a good society, a society of values and decent morals. Lieutenant General al-Bashir made it clear in his speech before the masses during the holding of god's table [ma'idat al-rahman ayt -- breaking the fast for the poor] at Umm Baddah that the conspiracy of the West will not frighten us, because we know that power belongs to god alone. The shortage of funds, men and fruits [koranic quote] to which we have been subjected is no more than a trial from god to test the extent of our adherence to his course and the extent of our turning towards him. He stated: We once again declare our rejection of all resolutions and charters of the security council and the United Nations which contradict god's law.
