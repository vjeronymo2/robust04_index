940407 FT 07 APR 94 / International Company News: Korea Telecom bids to open on April 18 The South Korean finance ministry said its previously-announced sale of a further 5 per cent stake in state-owned Korea Telecom (KT) would be made through open bidding on April 18 and 19. The government hopes to raise about Won420bn (Dollars 520m) through the sale as it moves towards reducing its holding in KT to 51 per cent.
