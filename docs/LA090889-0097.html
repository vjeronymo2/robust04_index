September 8, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: NAMES IN THE NEWS

An arrest warrant was issued for former Tennessee quarterback Tony Robinson after he failed to appear at a probation revocation hearing in Knoxville, Tenn. Robinson, 25, was arrested in January 1986 and charged with a variety of felony cocaine-related offenses. He pleaded no contest to a lesser charge and was given probation after serving six months at the Knox County Penal Farm.
