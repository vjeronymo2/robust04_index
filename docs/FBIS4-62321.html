BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Dmitriy Gorokhov] [Text] Geneva, 14 May -- A meeting between Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher has started at the Russian permanent mission at the United Nations' Geneva office. It was learned from Russian diplomatic circles that the sides will discuss a range of questions on bilateral relations and continue to exchange opinions on the settlement in the former Yugoslavia. The Yugoslav crisis was the main topic during the meeting between the foreign ministers of Russia, the United States, and five of the European Union countries, which took place on Friday night [13 May]. The participants in the meeting have reached agreements on the first steps to be taken toward resolving the situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina.
