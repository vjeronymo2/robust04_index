BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondents Boris Krivoshey and Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow May 17 TASS -- The Russian Foreign Ministry welcomes free democratic elections in Panama at which Ernesto Peres Balladares won, ministry spokesman Grigoriy Karasin told a briefing on Tuesday. He stressed that "among political, public circles and businessmen of Panama there is a broad consensus regarding further development of Russian-Panamanian relations." Karasin expressed hope that under the government of Balladares the relations between the two countries "will considerably progress" in various directions which were reflected in the bilateral declaration signed during a recent visit to Moscow by Panamanian Foreign Minister Jose Raul Mulino.
