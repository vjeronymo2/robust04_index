Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 24 (KCNA) -- A national contest of workers art circles was held in Pyongyang from March 14 to 23 to significantly celebrate the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song. Put on the stage of the contest were more than 650 pieces of colorful numbers, including instrumental music and song, dance and story-telling, which had been created by working people at factories and enterprises across the country through their brisk art activities, upholding the policy of the Workers' Party of Korea on popularizing art activity. Among them were a poem and song tale "The Love and Benevolence Will Be Conveyed Forever", a choral poem recitation and story-telling "Great Might of Korea", a female vocal solo and mixed vocal homophony "The Whole World Envies", a choral poem recitation and story-telling "Let Us Carry Through Party's Revolutionary Economic Strategy". They well showed the greatness, wise leadership and noble communist virtues of the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, the great pride and honor of the working class of chuche Korea and the happy life and worthwhile labour of the Korean working people.
