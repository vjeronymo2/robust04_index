941026 FT 26 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Korean Air Lines - Contracts Korean Air Lines said it has signed a contract with Airbus Industrie to supply fuselage panels for 400 Airbus A330/A340 aircraft from next year to 2002. The deal was valued at Dollars 13m. KAL also won an Airbus contract in 1988 to supply panels for 600 aircraft, a company spokesman said.
