940408 FT 08 APR 94 / International Company News: Foreign BPA holders chosen One Brazilian and three French companies have been chosen as the foreign investors allotted stakes in the privatisation of Banco Portugues do Atlantico (BPA), Portugal's largest retail bank, writes Peter Wise in Lisbon. Caisse National de Prevoyance and Mutuelles du Mans, the French insurers, will hold 3 per cent and 1 per cent respectively, the Brazilian Banco Economico 2.5 per cent and French bank Credit Commercial de France 1 per cent.
