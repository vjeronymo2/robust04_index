Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A spokesman for the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine-General Command [PFLP-GC] has stated the following: Since our heroes' operation in the al-Rayhan-al-'Ayshiyah area in southern Lebanon last week, an operation which resulted in the killing and injury of many agents, the Zionist enemy has been trying to lift the morale of its troops and agents through lies and fabrication. And it seems that what the enemy has broadcast about the killing of two PFLP-GC elements and capturing two others is part of the same series of lies and deception. We, the PFLP-GC, are interested in exposing the Zionist enemy's lies. We have not lost a single martyr in the aforementioned operation and none of our strugglers has been captured. [Signed] The PFLP-GC
