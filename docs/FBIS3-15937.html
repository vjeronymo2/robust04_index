Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] The Defense Ministry Press Service has reported that there is no end to the attempts by Armenia's Armed Forces to break through the defensive line of the Azerbaijani Army. The enemy has been attacking our positions in Fizulinskiy Rayon since yesterday. The Azerbaijani Armed Forces' units have succeeded in repulsing all these attacks. Having suffered heavy losses, the enemy was forced to withdraw to its initial positions. The shelling of our cities in the northwest of the Republic from Armenian territory is continuing.
