930526 FT 26 MAY 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Cowie COWIE said there was a 78.9 per cent take-up of the open offer to shareholders to apply for up to 13.97m new ordinary shares. A total of 1.01m ordinary shares were placed firm. The 2.73m subject to recall but not taken up have been placed with institutional clients by De Zoete & Bevan.
