Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Simferopol, [no dateline as received] -- A UNIAN correspondent learned on 9 March that at the initiative of the Crimean Republican Human Rights Committee and also the Sevastopol and Yalta branches of this human rights organization, a branch of the general Ukrainian nonparty Committee of Electors has been created in the Crimean Republic. This committee considers that its main task is to contribute to the democratic elections to Ukraine's Supreme Council and the Supreme Council of Crimea, to check adherence to legislation in the process voting by citizens' and not to allow falsifications of the results of the elections. The Committee of Electors stresses its nonparty affiliation, its impartiality with regard to parliamentary candidates and its independence of state and commercial structures. Members of the Crimean branch of the committee intend to observe the organization and holding of the elections and to take part in preparing and training voluntary observers at polling stations. The creation of international observer stations on the basis of the committee's regional representations is also part of the organization's sphere of activity.
