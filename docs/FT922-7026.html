920520 FT 20 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Stratton Inv Trust net asset value rises The net asset value per share of Stratton Investment Trust stood at 183p at the year ended March 31 1992 against 178p a year earlier. Total revenue, including bank interest and underwriting commissions, grew from Pounds 749,000 to Pounds 792,000. Earnings per share came out at 2.28p (2.6p), as a consequence of which the dividend is reduced to 1.7p (1.86p).
