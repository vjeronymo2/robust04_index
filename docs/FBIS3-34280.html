Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Just in from the newsdesk. We have a report about the security situation in the south. Dani Nur-al-Din, our correspondent in al-Nabatiyah, has just told us that Israeli warplanes are raiding positions in the al-Zahrani River basin and the periphery of Jabal al-Rayhan. The raiding planes have made four sorties. Our correspondent also says that Lebanese Army antiaircraft guns in al-Nabatiyah are confronting the raiding Israeli planes. Over the telephone, we can hear the sound of heavy gunfire and the explosions of shells dropped by the Israeli aircraft.
