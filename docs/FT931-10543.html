930208 FT 08 FEB 93 / Homeworkers' wages 'too low' A GROWING army of 'invisible' homeworkers, including an increasing number of men, should receive the same pay and conditions as on-site workers, a report urges today. 'Homeworkers represent a small but growing proportion of Britain's workforce, yet they remain invisible and marginalised,' said the report's author, Ms Michelle Reeves of the Greater Manchester Low Pay Unit. Estimates suggest there could be as many as 30,000 such workers in the region.
