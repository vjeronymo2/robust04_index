BFN [Summary from poor reception] While attacking diplomatic quarters in Kabul city today the Dostam-Karmal-Golboddin militia fired rockets at the embassies of the Federal Republic of Germany and Libya. A spokesman of the Kabul garrison command told a BIA correspondent that the militiamen, who have no principles or Islamic standards, fired rockets today against Kabul, resulting in one of our innocent compatriots being martyred and 12 others injured. A number of houses were demolished.
