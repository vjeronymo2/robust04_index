Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Phnom Penh 11 Jan (AKP) -- His Majesty Norodom Sihanouk, king of Cambodia, has recently promoted His Royal Highness [HRH] Norodom Yuveakneat [Sihanouk's son] to the rank of Prince Kromluong. The promotion decree dated 31 December 1993 is seen in Beijing where the king is receiving medical treatment. The king of Cambodia has also nominated HRH Norodom Yuveakneat as his senior privy councillor with the ceremonial rank of a deputy prime minister. The prince is the 10th senior privy councillor to the king of Cambodia. The nine others are: His Excellency [H.E.] Samdech Nhoek Chulong, H.E. Samdech Son Sann, H.E. Samdech Ngor Hu, H.E. Chau Senkosal alias Chhum, H.E. Heng Samrin, H.E. Say Phuthang, H.E. Chea Soth, H.E. Bou Thang, and H.E. Mat Ly.
