940214 FT 14 FEB 94 / Red Cross warns of Kabul aid 'disaster' A senior Red Cross official in Afghanistan yesterday said Kabul would face a disaster if food did not reach people who had been displaced by factional fighting, Reuter reports from Kabul. Forces loyal to Afghanistan President Burhanuddin Rabbani and his opponents held their fire for the third consecutive day but it was not clear whether there would be a formal ceasefire during the fasting month of Ramadan, which began on Friday.
