941006 FT 06 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Sims completes buy Sims Food Group has bought the outstanding 40 per cent of shares in General Provisions, the supplier of sliced and packed bacon, for Pounds 2.53m. The acquisition has been financed by the issue of 3.54m new Sims shares at 5p each. Sims acquired 60 per cent of the share capital of General Provisions in January 1992 for Pounds 1.37m in cash. General Provisions' pre-tax profits for the year ended March 1994 were Pounds 584,000 on turnover of Pounds 13.5m.
