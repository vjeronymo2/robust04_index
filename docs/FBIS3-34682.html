Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] As part of the campaign to liquidate the officers who took part in the coup attempt in Tikrit in late July, 20 senior officers in the Republican Guards were recently expelled and arrested. Some of these were even executed by the guards' intelligence organ, our sources in Baghdad said. The families of the executed officers were notified by the authorities. However, the bodies were not handed over to them. Meanwhile, the authorities warned the families against publicizing the issue or holding memorial ceremonies for the deceased. Panic prevailed among the Republican Guards as a result of this campaign. Many officers and soldiers escaped for fear of liquidation.
