July 27, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: CULVER CITY;

16 PARKING PERMITS GRANTED

A limited number of parking permits will be granted to residents of Higuera Street in Culver City who must leave their cars on cross streets when parking on their street is prohibited.

The City Council on Monday voted unanimously to grant a maximum of 16 permits to residents of Higuera Street who live from Lucerne Avenue to southeast of Schaefer Street.

Several residents have requested permits to park on cross streets, which have one- and two-hour limits for those without permits, according to a staff report. Parking on Higuera Street is prohibited during the morning and afternoon rush hours.
