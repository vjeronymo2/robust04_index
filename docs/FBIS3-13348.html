Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The security services today announced that 79 of the prisoners who escaped from Tazoult prison last Thursday evening have been recaptured, while 24 others have been killed by the security forces which have retrieved several weapons. A statement by the security services adds that the search and surrounding operations are continuing. On the other hand, the Ministry of Interior and Local Authorities announces to the citizens residing in the privinces of Algiers, Blida, Boumerdes, Medea, Tipaza, Ain Defla, Bouira, Djelfa, Msila, and Chelef that from tomorrow night the curfew hours will be from 2330 to 0400.
