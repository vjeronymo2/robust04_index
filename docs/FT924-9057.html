921107 FT 07 NOV 92 / International Company News: Cornfeld joins bid for MGM MR BERNIE CORNFELD, the controversial former mutual funds promoter, joined yesterday in a Dollars 500m cash bid for MGM, the troubled Hollywood studio owned by Credit Lyonnais, the French state bank, writes Alan Friedman in New York. The bid was submitted to Credit Lyonnais by Mr Giovanni Di Stefano, a former business associate of Mr Giancarlo Parretti, the Italian financier who was forced out of MGM last year by the French bank. Mr Di Stefano and Mr Cornfeld have joined forces in the bid. Credit Lyonnais is expected to reject the bid, since it is locked in a series of legal battles with Mr Di Stefano.
