BFN [Text] In Tel Aviv today, Yitzhaq Rabin, Israel's prime minister and minister of defense, and Czech Defense Minister Antonin Baudys signed an agreement on military cooperation. This was announced by the Israeli Defense Ministry's spokesman. Rabin made use of the presence of the Czech Government representative in Israel to thank the former Czechoslovakia for supplying arms to the young Jewish state when it was fighting for its independence in 1948.
