BFN [Text] Sonatrach company announced the signing of an agreement in Washington this week with a value of $200 million allocated to finish the Algerian part of the Arab Magreb gas pipeline Europe. This is a loan agreement between the American Eximbank and Sonatrach and a loan agreement between Sonatrach and American Citibank. The completion of the gas pipeline also benefits from loans from European investment banks and French loans guaranteed by the groups Coface.
