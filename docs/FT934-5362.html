931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Sep hits Pounds 2m and seeks Pounds 5m Sep Industrial Holdings, the Surrey-based general engineer, yesterday reported a rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 830,000 to Pounds 2.08m for the year to end-September. The company also announced a placing and open offer to raise Pounds 5.05m net to enhance the balance sheet and help fund recent acquisitions. Gearing at year-end of 54 per cent was expected to fall to about 16 per cent. The placing and open offer is of 17.86m new ordinary shares on a 1-for-4 basis. The shares have been placed with institutional and other investors at 30p per share, subject to a clawback by existing shareholders. For the past year acquisitions added Pounds 890,000 to profits and Pounds 16m to total turnover of Pounds 37.72m (Pounds 24.04m). Earnings worked through at 2.8p (1.42p) and a final dividend of 0.45p makes a 0.8p (0.65p) total. At the interim stage a total of not less than 0.75p was forecast. The shares closed 2p higher at 33p.
