Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by Hamdi al-Husayni] [Text] Egypt has asked all the countries situated on the Nile River to discontinue cooperation with Israel in agricultural projects and the building of dams. Egypt warned Kenya and Ethiopia against using Israeli experts in implementing irrigation projects. Egypt had obtained information that the two countries recently employed a number of Israeli experts to assist them in building dams on the Nile and setting up agricultural projects.
