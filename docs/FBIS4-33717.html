BFN [Article by Carlos Franco] [Excerpts] The weapons and ammunition industry increased significantly last year, recovering its $1.5 million loss in 1992, attaining a net profit of $8.4 million in 1993. Sales, especially those of the Brazilian Cartridge Company [Companhia Brasileira de Cartuchos] (CBC) jumped from $98.8 million in 1992 to $143.2 million in 1993, that is, a 44.9 percent increase. [passage omitted] The oil and gas industries, which include Petrobras [Brazilian Petroleum Corporation] distributors, closed 1993 with net sales of $22.6 billion and net profits of $858.9 million. Profits obtained in 1993 were 194 percent up on the $292.4 million profit obtained in 1992.
