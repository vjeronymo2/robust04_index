May 20, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

U.S. ATTEMPTS TO OUST GEN. NORIEGA

In all of my 25 years of religiously reading your magnificent paper, never has my stomach turned as much as when I saw the photo on the front page on May 11 showing the Panamanian opposition vice presidential candidate Guillermo Ford being beaten with a steel pipe by someone who clearly could be seen by his facial expression to be enjoying hurting Ford.

Never has a photo so reviled and saddened me, showing a soldier passively standing by, allowing this violence to occur. I was and remain stunned by your photo, so stunned and shocked that I do not know how to respond to this sight.

DAN ROBBINS

Ventura
