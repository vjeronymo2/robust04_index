BFN [Text] Colonel Mu'ammar al-Qadhdhafi this afternoon received Hamid Yusuf Hammadi, President Saddam Husayn's envoy and minister of culture and information. The leader president's envoy conveyed a message from his excellency on Iraq's view of the situation at the UN Security Council, relations with the UN Social Commission, which is headed by Rolf Ekeus, and Iraq's implementation of the provisions of Part C of UN Security Council Resolution No. 687. The message stresses the importance of coordination among sisterly and friendly states to work to lift the unjust blockade imposed on Iraq. Colonel al-Qadhdhafi expressed Libya's solidarity with Iraq and his support for the recent Iraqi move. The meeting was attended by Jum'ah al-Fazzani, secretary of the General People's Committee for Unity, and the Iraqi ambassador in Libya.
