September 20, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MCDONNELL DOUGLAS EMPLOYEES GIVE TO SCHOOL OF THE ARTS

The McDonnell Douglas Employees' Community Fund donated $5,000 to the Orange County High School of the Arts Foundation.

Brian McEvoy, an employee of McDonnell Douglas, presented the check at a recent meeting of the foundation's board of directors.

The grant will directly benefit students at the Orange County High School of the Arts through the artist-teacher program. The school is beginning its fourth year this month as Orange County's only "Fame" high school, offering a comprehensive performing and visual arts education in a four-year program for talented students.

The Orange County High School of the Arts is on the campus of Los Alamitos High School, 3591 Cerritos Ave, Los Alamitos. For more information, call 596-1435.
