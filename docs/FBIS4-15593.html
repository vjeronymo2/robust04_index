BFN [Text] Tbilisi 9 Apr -- According to the `7dghe' [Seven Days] newspaper, on his resignation [Former Tbilisi Police Chief] Davit Zeikidze demanded that he should be appointed a manager of a tobacco factory or a head of a customs check-point. His request has been turned down. At present, Davit Zeikidze, together with his family, is in the village of Mukhrani and lives in his own house.
