July 14, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

ON THE RECORD

"The problem is that while the Silicon Valley is burning, the Bush Administration is playing its fiddle." -- Dan Hutchinson, president of VLSI Research of San Jose, on a National Advisory Committee on Semiconductors report that recommends tax incentives for U.S. chip makers but omits the need for more funds for Sematech, the U.S. chip consortium.
