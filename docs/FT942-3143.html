940616 FT 16 JUN 94 / Virgin in Channel rail link move Virgin Atlantic Airways is expected to join London & Continental Group, one of the four consortia which has been shortlisted to build the Channel tunnel fast rail link. The winner of the bidding to build and operate the fast link will acquire ownership of European Passenger Services, the company which will run Eurostar trains through the tunnel. Virgin had said earlier that it was talking with several of the bid consortia. London & Continental includes Ove Arup, the engineering consultants, coach operator National Express and cement group Blue Circle.
