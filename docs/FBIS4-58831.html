BFN [Text] Dubai, United Arab Emirates, May 8 (AFP) -- Troops loyal to President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih on Sunday captured the south's main military airport, 50 kilometres from the port city of Aden, Western diplomatic sources said. The sources in the region said Al-'Anad was the only military airport around the southern stronghold of Aden, although the south has a second base in the Hadhramaut, 700 kilometres (420 miles) to the east. The northern forces have the port city trapped in a "pincer movement," one diplomat said.
