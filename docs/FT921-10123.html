920207 FT 07 FEB 92 / Observer: Mixed-up Bizarre though psychologists' minds may be, they are clearly not of the one-track variety, to judge by the agenda for the British Psychological Society's annual conference at Scarborough in April. Topics up for discussion include, among numerous others: athletes and labour pain, nine-year-olds and body shape, sexual abuse of clients by therapists, and . . . the future of the Royal Air Force?
