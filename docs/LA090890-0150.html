September 8, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

HOLMES REPLACES SCHAAF AT SANTA MARGARITA

Richard Schaaf has resigned as boys' basketball coach at Santa Margarita High School.

He will be replaced by Roger Holmes, his assistant last season.

Schaaf, who guided the Eagles to a 14-13 record last season, will remain as the school's athletic director. Holmes has named Todd Dixon as assistant coach.
