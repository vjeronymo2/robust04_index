910501 FT 01 MAY 91 / Coal price cut BRITISH COAL is cutting the price of the coal sold to domestic consumers by up to Pounds 20 per tonne, the company announced yesterday. This is its traditional method of encouraging sales in the summer. Between May 1 and July 31, prices to merchants who sell to domestic customers will be Pounds 20 per tonne for Welsh anthracite, a reduction of around 20 per cent, and Pounds 10 per tonne for housecoal, a cut of around 14 per cent.
