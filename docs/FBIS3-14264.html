Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, today received Badr Hammam, envoy of Egyptian President Husani Mubarak's and assistant to the Egyptian foreign minister, who conveyed to the brother president a message from his brother President Mubarak. The message deals with close brotherly relations between the two fraternal countries and efforts by the brothers in Egypt to bring viewpoints closer in order to overcome the current political crisis and its regrettable repercussions. The message also deals with issues and developments of interest to our Arab nation. During the meeting, the two sides discussed aspects of brotherly bilateral relations and means to reinforce them as well as issues of common interest.
