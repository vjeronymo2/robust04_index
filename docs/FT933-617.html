930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Rise in net asset value at ECU Trust The ECU Trust saw net asset value increase from 58.7p per share to 71.3p over the year to June 30. Total revenue was almost static at Pounds 777,000 (Pounds 784,000) and interest payments increased to Pounds 87,000 from Pounds 2,000. Revenue after tax was down at Pounds 230,000 (Pounds 326,000) for earnings of 0.76p (1.09p) per share. A reduced final dividend of 0.5p is proposed, compared with 1p.
