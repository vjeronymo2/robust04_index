BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 4 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to Henri Konan Bedie, president of the Republic of the Cote d'Ivoire, on the occasion of his 60th birthday. President Kim Il-song in the message wished him success in his work for the prosperity of the country as well as good health and happiness.
