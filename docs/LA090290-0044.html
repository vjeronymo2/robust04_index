September 2, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

UMBERG'S OCCUPATION AND PRINGLE SUPPORTERS' LAWSUIT

Curt Pringle should change his ballot title to "thug" or "ringleader," since his henchmen hired "guards" to intimidate Hispanic voters in his district.
