BFN [Text] According to the opinion poll conducted by the Independent Institute of Socioeconomic and Political Research, assisted by the U.S. International Center for Support of Private Entrepreneurship, 72.4 percent of those polled consider it admissble to introduce private land ownership in Belarus, while 26.6 percent oppose this. Some 20.3 percent believe that land can be owned by foreigners; 78.2 percent are opposed.
