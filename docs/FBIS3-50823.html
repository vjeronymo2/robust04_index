Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] We would like to reiterate that Blas N. Riquelme resigned today as president of the Colorado Party. The controversy between Riquelme and President Juan Carlos Wasmosy came to a conclusion today when the controversial politician and brewery owner resigned. Riquelme's resignation was reported at the headquarters of the Colorado Party by Eugenio Sanabria Cantero, first vice president of the Colorado Party Executive Board, to whom Riquelme conveyed his decision to resign after a telephone conversation they held this morning. According to the statutes of the Colorado Party, Sanabria Cantero automatically becomes the acting president of the Colorado Party. The party's executive board is holding a closed-door meeting to evaluate Riquelme's situation regarding his controversy with President Wasmosy. [passage omitted]
