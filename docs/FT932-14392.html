930419 FT 19 APR 93 / International Company News: First Boston and Merrill Lynch win YPF mandate FIRST BOSTON and Merrill Lynch, the US investment banks, have won the mandate to act as global co-ordinators in the privatisation of YPF, Argentina's national oil company. The sell-off will be Argentina's largest privatisation. Mr Daniel Marx, Argentina's top financial negotiator, said no decision had been taken on the price or amount of shares to be offered. The offering is planned for June or July. YPF has an estimated net worth of Dollars 8bn and the government will sell 70 per cent of the company in stages.
