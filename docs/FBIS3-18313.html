Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] General Romolo Mangani has been arrested by officers from the Division for General Investigations for Special Operations who were implementing an arrest warrant issued by Deputy Prosecutors Galasso and Piro as part of the investigation into a plot, among other things, to the storm RAI [Italian Broadcasting Corporation]'s offices at Saxa Rubra, near Rome. The charges on the warrant are those of political conspiracy. General Mangani was the commander of the III ROC [NATO regional operation center] based at Martinafranca. His name also came up as part of the inquiry in the Ustica disaster.
