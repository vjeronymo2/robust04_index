Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report under "How We Will Enter the Field" rubric: "Fuel in Exchange for Grain"] [Text] Stavropol -- In exchange for 200,000 tonnes of grain, Stavropol Kray inhabitants will receive from Bashkortostan diesel fuel, gasoline, and the oils necessary for spring sowing. This decision was adopted during a working trip to Stavropol Kray by Aleksandr Rykov, Bashkortostan minister of material resources and interregional ties. The people of Stavropol Kray have also concluded an accord on an exchange of grain for petroleum products with Azerbaijan's oil refiners.
