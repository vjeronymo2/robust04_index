Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Blagoevgrad (southwestern Bulgaria), February 11 (BTA) -- Customs officers at the Kulata checkpoint (on the border with Greece) seized 6.650 kg of hashish yesterday. The narcotic was found in a handbag in a bus taking Bulgarian tourists to Greece. An investigation was launched and all passengers were questioned. A 27-year-old man admitted owning the handbag. Inquiry is under way.
