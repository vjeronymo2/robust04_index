941205 FT 05 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Board changes at Tomkins Tomkins, the industrial components and food group, is expected to announce a boardroom reshuffle this week which will see the retirement of non-executive chairman Mr Michael Moore, after 14 years as a director, writes Peggy Hollinger. Mr Moore, 58, will be replaced by Mr Greg Hutchings, who as chief executive has transformed the group from a small engineering company to a Pounds 2.5bn conglomerate. Mr Hutchings will become executive chairman. Several other directors are expected to be promoted in the reshuffle, including Mr Ian Duncan, who will become deputy chairman.
