Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [From the "News and Press Review" program] [Text] Listeners. As a result of continued progress in broadcasting, recording, and media technology, effective 1 March, the Central People's Radio Network will air casts under Program One, that have been originally broadcast via frequency 106.1 MHz, in stereophonic instead of monophonic sound system. While being broadcast in stereophonic sound system, casts -- especially literary casts -- in the Central People's Radio Network Program One will have characteristics such as treble and bass notes, a strong sense of reality, and a pleasant timbre to provide listeners with more melodious entertainment.
