BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Glazovskaya] [Text] Ashgabat, 11 April -- Turkmenistan has partially resumed the supply of gas to Azerbaijan in response to assurances from the Azerbaijani side that supplies of petroleum products, equipment for oilfields, and engineering products to Turkmenistan - envisaged under an agreement between the two countries - will be resumed within a ten-day period. To date, Azerbaijan owes Turkmenistan 74 million dollars for gas supplies and is now being forced to take urgent steps to avoid the economic sanctions stipulated in the inter-governmental agreement.
