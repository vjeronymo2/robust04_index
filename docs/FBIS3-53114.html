Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Husni Mubarak today received Yusuf Bin-'Alawi, Omani minister of state for foreign affairs, who conveyed to him a message from Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa, who said that the discussion dealt with the future moves in the Middle East and the Gulf region and the results of the recent conference of the Damascus Declaration foreign ministers.
