930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / Observer: Aitch-oo British Gas's directors were feeling the chill yesterday as they presented their annual results - which bore the scars of heavy regulation and last year's unhelpfully clement weather. The atmosphere was hardly improved when the letter 'h' fell off the British Gas sign which was hanging behind their heads and clanged on to the floor. 'Times are hard and the squeeze is on,' muttered the deadpan chief executive, Cedric Brown, through gritted teeth.
