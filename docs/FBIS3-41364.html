Language: Persian Article Type:CSO [Text] News Service. With the round-the-clock efforts of the officials of the antinarcotics office of the Central Region of Greater Tehran, a narcotics production and distribution gang operating throughout Tehran was destroyed. The gang was active in large-scale production and distribution inside the country and abroad. The officials of this region, after infiltrating the gang, seized 62 kg of opium and about 200 liters of alcoholic beverages and arrested nine persons.
