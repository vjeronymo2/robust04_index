Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Amnestied Opposition Members Invited to State Duma"] [Text] Andrey Dunayev, Russia's former internal affairs minister, was invited on Monday [28 February] to parliament by the lower chamber's speaker Ivan Rybkin. Dunayev briefed parliamentary journalists on this upon arriving at the State Duma building. As was explained by Viktor Ilyukhin, head of the parliamentary committee for security, the Monday session of the State Duma discussed inviting to parliament the former opposition leaders subject to amnesty, to discuss with them the situation and their actions in the future.
