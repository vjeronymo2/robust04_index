Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] Fasi Iqbal, chairman of the Senate Standing Committee on Information and Broadcasting, hosted a reception in honor of the visiting Indian members of parliament. Speaking on the occasion, he said such visits contribute toward promoting bilateral relations and could pave the way for the resolution of the outstanding matters. Indian members of Parliament, Mahinder Prasad and Kishore Singh said that they are impressed with the sentiments of sincerity and goodwill expressed by the Pakistani senators.
