Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 31, IRNA -- The country's overall oil revenue in the next year's budget bill was set at rls. 17,651,988,000,000 or dlrs. 10,721,300,000 by the deputies in the Majlis open session Sunday. Deliberations on 1373 (starting March 21) budget bill began Saturday and its outline was approved yesterday. Discussion on other sources of the government revenue still continues in the Majlis.
