March 29, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County P.M. Final

LOCAL;

CRASH CLAIMS MISSION VIEJO STUDENT

A third Westmont College student on a missionary trip in Mexico died from injuries suffered in a head-on car crash with a car driven by a suspected drunken driver in Ensenada, a nursing supervisor in San Diego said today.

Lisa Bebout, 24, a senior physical education major from Mission Viejo, died overnight at Sharp Memorial Hospital after suffering multiple injuries, said nursing supervisor Dorothy Price.

Five students from the Santa Barbara college who were doing missionary work over the Easter holiday were in the car when it collided with the suspected drunken driver's car Monday morning, authorities said.
