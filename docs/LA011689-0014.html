January 16, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

CHARGES DROPPED AGAINST NORTH

Is North still to be America's perception of a national hero now that he and his cunning attorney are succeeding in blackmailing the justice system of this country and threatening to expose the national security of the United States to save the skin of this Rambo hero?

The same proud Marine who defiantly asserted before the world that this same security was the reason he appointed himself to be its guardian. Thus began the Iran-Contra scandal.

GLORIA GIARDINA

Anaheim
