941010 FT 10 OCT 94 / Company News This Week: Lucas provisions to temper revival - Other Companies George Simpson's first set of results as chief executive of Lucas Industries is likely to include provisions of up to Pounds 100m for restructuring. The charges may outweigh annual pre-tax profits at the automotive and aerospace components supplier, which are forecast at Pounds 60m to Pounds 80m, up from Pounds 50.3m. The revival of automotive business will have helped the underlying performance and the dividend is expected to be maintained at 7p.
