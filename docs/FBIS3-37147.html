Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] A relative calm has been prevailing on Armenia's borders for the past three days. The Armenian Defense Ministry press service reports that the only incident occurred on the evening of [date indistinct] February. An exchange of fire took place between 2120 and 2140 at the border near Diran in Idzhevanskiy Rayon. The enemy used howitzers, machine guns and automatic weapons. There were no casualties. According to the Karabakh Defense Army press service, the Karabakh side managed to occupy another two strategically important heights in Kelbadzharskiy Rayon on the evening of 11 February. In the afternoon, the enemy air force dropped bombs on Martuninskiy Rayon. After a relative lull on Saturday, active combat operations resumed along most of the Karabakh front on Sunday. The main fighting took place in northern Kelbadzharskiy Rayon. The Karabakh formations launched a counterattack and managed to regain control of a number of heights near the Omar mountain pass. On the same day, the Karabakh formations managed to halt a number of attacks by the Azerbaijani troops on the southeastern sector of the battlefront. After destroying one enemy tank and killing 30 soldiers, the Armenian fighters threw the Azerbaijanis back from the eastern borders of Martuninskiy Rayon. A relative calm has again been prevailing along the entire war front since this morning.
