BFN [Text] Rabat, 23 Apr (INA) -- Barzan al-Tikriti, personal envoy of leader President Saddam Husayn, has relayed a message from his excellency the president to his brother King Hassan II of Morocco on the fraternal bilateral ties between Iraq and Morocco and the relationship between Iraq and the UN Security Council after Iraq honored its commitments to the relevant UN Security Council resolutions. The message underscores that this requires the UN Security Council to fulfill its commitments by lifting the embargo clamped on Iraq. The president's message was communicated to the Moroccan monarch during a meeting he held with the Iraqi envoy yesterday. During the meeting, King Hassan II expressed his thanks to His Excellency President Saddam Husayn for keeping him informed of the Iraqi position, and also of the recent developments in the relationship with the UN Security Council. He reiterated Morocco's support for the Iraqi Government and people. The Moroccan monarch asserted that Iraq has done all it is required to do on both the Arab and international scenes, and that there is no longer any justification for maintaining the current state of affairs. King Hassan II of Morocco asked Barzan al-Tikriti to relay his greetings to His Excellency President Saddam Husayn. The meeting was attended by Abdellatif Filali, Moroccan minister of state for foreign affairs and cooperation; Abdelhadi Boutaleb [name as received], adviser to King Hassan II; and the Iraqi ambassador in Rabat.
