921229 FT 29 DEC 92 / Mogadishu peace accord LEADERS of Mogadishu's two warring factions united in peace moves yesterday, Reuter reports from Mogadishu. More than 10,000 people celebrated the dismantling of the 'green line' war boundary which has divided the Somali capital for more than a year. 'We have had enough lessons from the civil war,' said a resident as warlords Gen Mohammed Farah Aideed and Ali Mahdi Mohammed shook hands. Meanwhile, US and Canadian troops yesterday secured the town of Belet Huen.
