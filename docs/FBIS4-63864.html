BFN [Valeriy Gromak report: "Officers Seem To Have Chance to Study in Sweden"] [Text] Kaliningrad -- Swedish Foreign Minister Baroness Margaretha af Ugglas is here on a familiarization visit. The baroness met with Yuriy Matochkin, head of the Kaliningrad Oblast Administration, visited the Kaliningrad International Business School, toured the city, laid wreaths at the tomb of the German philosopher Kant and the Pushkin Memorial, and met with journalists. The Swedish foreign minister believes that Kaliningrad Oblast has too many military personnel, and Sweden is prepared to help retrain officers being discharged into the reserve.
