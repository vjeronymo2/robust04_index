BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- A "hot line" will be installed between the Chancellor's Office in Bonn and the Kremlin in Moscow. News of this official agreement broke on Tuesday [10 May], prior to the start of Russian President Boris Yeltsin's three-day visit on 11 May. According to the agenda for Yeltsin's visit, Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel and Andrey Kozyrev, his Russian counterpart, will sign the agreement on the hot line between the two government headquarters.
