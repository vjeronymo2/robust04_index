941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / World News in Brief: German terrorists 'to be released' German justice minister Sabine Leutheusser-Schnarrenberger said she expected several former leftwing Red Army Faction guerrillas to be released from jail next year. This follows the release this month of Irmgard Moeller, who spent 22 years in jail for the murder of three American soldiers.
