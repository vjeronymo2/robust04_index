Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 22 (KCNA) -- A detailed summary of "For the Ultimate Solution of the Rural Question Under the Banner of the Socialist Rural Theses," the letter sent by the great leader President Kim Il-song to the national agricultural conference, was reported by the Burundian paper "UBUMWE", the Togolese paper "ATOPANI" and radio and television of Equatorial Guinea. A seminar and a reading session on the letter were held by the Zimbabwean youth centre for the study of Kimilsongism and the Zambia-DPRK friendship class named after Kim Chong-il at the Mulali Senior Middle School in Lusaka, Zambia.
