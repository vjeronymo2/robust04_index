Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Former U.S. state secretary James Baker arrived in Tbilisi on Friday. "I am pleased to enjoy the hospitality of my friend Eduard Shevardnadze once again," he said in the Tbilisi airport. Georgian leader Eduard Shevardnadze and former U.S. state secretary Baker had a conversation on Friday afternoon. After the conversation former U.S. state secretary Baker told journalists that he had come to Tbilisi to learn more about Georgia's predicament, to discuss economic aid to Georgia, issues concerning the country's interests along with those of the former Soviet republics and of Europe. Georgian leader Shevardnadze and former U.S. state secretary Baker were present at the opening of the First Congress of Georgian Citizens which took place on the same day.
