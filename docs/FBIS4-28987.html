BFN [Text] Australia's employment minister, Simon Crean, arrives in Vietnam shortly on the first leg of a trip to Asia aimed at strengthening trade links with the region. In Hanoi, Mr. Crean will sign a memorandum of understanding with the government as the first step in Australian business setting up cooperative projects providing high quality education and training. And in Indonesia, Mr. Crean will be part of Australia's largest ever trade and cultural promotion in the region. During his Malaysian stopover, Mr. Crean will meet senior officials to discuss priority areas for cooperation in vocational education and training.
