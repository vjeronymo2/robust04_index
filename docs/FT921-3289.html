920314 FT 14 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Unilever expands Turkish interests Unilever is to acquire the stakes held by the Turkish bank, Turkiye Is Bankasi and its pension fund, in Unilever's foods, detergents and household cleansers businesses in Turkey. As a result of the transactions, Unilever will raise its holding from 65 to 100 per cent in the Unilever-Is margarine, oils, fats and ice cream business and from 51 to 91 per cent in the Lever-Is detergents and cleansers operation.
