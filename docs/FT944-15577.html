941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / National Savings contribution falls National Savings contributed Pounds 211m to government funding in September, down from Pounds 265m in August. Of this, Pounds 116m was accrued interest. Net receipts amounted to Pounds 95m while total gross sales were Pounds 719m. The contribution made by premium bonds was almost unchanged, and, at Pounds 127m, was the highest-selling product. National Savings is expected to contribute Pounds 3.5bn to government funding in the 1994-95 financial year and at the end of September had already raised Pounds 2.17bn. The total invested in National Savings at the end of September was Pounds 50.5bn.
