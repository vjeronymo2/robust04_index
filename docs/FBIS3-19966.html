Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Berlin -- British Prime Minister John Major wants to attend the 8 September farewell ceremony in Berlin for the Western Allies, the UK Consulate in Berlin said in a note on Wednesday [23 March]. The prime minister and the UK foreign and defense secretaries "are pleased to accept Chancellor Kohl's invitation." They look forward to attending the program. The note says the ceremony will pay tribute to the contribution British, French, and U.S. troops have made toward the freedom of Berlin since the end of World War II. Invitations have also gone to U.S. President Bill Clinton and French President Francois Mitterrand.
