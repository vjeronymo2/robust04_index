930305 FT 05 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Unidare advances to IPounds 5.4m UNIDARE, the Dublin-based supplier of heating, electrical and welding goods, reported pre-tax profits ahead 7 per cent for 1992. On turnover up 12 per cent at IPounds 113.9m (IPounds 101.4m), pre-tax profits were up from IPounds 5.09m to IPounds 5.43m (Pounds 5.53m). Earnings per share were 33.04p (28.33p) and a final dividend of 11.4p is proposed to make a total of 15.7p (15p).
