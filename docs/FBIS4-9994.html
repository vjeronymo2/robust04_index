BFN [Text] Today, Industry Minister Liliana Canale said Peru will gradually resume its obligations with the Andean Pact. She added that Peru has not returned to the subregional group, which is also made up of Venezuela, Bolivia, Colombia, and Ecuador, because Peru never left that organization. She indicated that Peru is only resuming its obligations with the Andean Pact based on certain conditions. Among these, she mentioned the fulfillment of the proposal made by Peru in the meeting being held in Caracas. Our country requested the elimination of any type of export subsidies as well as of the distortions in external tariffs.
