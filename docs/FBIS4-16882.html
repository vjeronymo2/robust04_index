BFN [Text] Tskhinvali, 18 Apr -- The headquarters of the peacekeeping forces deployed in Tskhinvali told an Iberia correspondent that, today a session of the so-called South Ossetian Supreme Council was held. Following the elections, communists obtained 24 seats out of a total of 34, the National Unity Party got 6 seats, and 4 seats went to representatives of various public organizations. Ludwig Chibirov was today elected chairman to the state Nikhas [South Ossetia's parliament].
