Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 25 Feb (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa has stated that there are Egyptian-Yemeni contacts currently taking place in an attempt to contain the stand and stop the fighting in Yemen. In a statement he made today commenting on the current fighting in Yemen, Musa called on both sides of the conflict in Yemen to stop the tension between them and calm the situation down.
