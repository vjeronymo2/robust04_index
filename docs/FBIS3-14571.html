Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Islamic Republic of Iran's Foreign Ministry has said that, according to the report prepared by the fact-finding team, the reason why our country's C-130 aircraft crashed in the Karabakh region was the forced alteration of its flight path, the lack of air traffic assistance and guidance from the region's control towers, and the impact of a missile fired by the Armenian forces stationed in the Karabakh region. The Foreign Ministry, while reserving the right for the Islamic Republic of Iran to pursue the downing of the aircraft through legal and judicial means and to seek compensation for the victims, has called on the Armenian Government to identify and punish the perpetrators of this painful incident.
