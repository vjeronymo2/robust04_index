BFN [Text] Katowice, June 1 -- Deputy Industry and Trade Minister Herbert Gabrys and Deputy Minister of the Coal Industry of The People's Republic of China Zhang Baoming on Wednesday signed an agreement on the resumption of the activity of the permanent interministerial working team for the coal industry. In the "Staszic" colliery, head of the coal company in Pingdingshan, Liang Youping, head of the "Katowice Coal Holding SA" Tadeusz Demel and colliery director Miroslaw Major signed an agreement on cooperation. The Chinese deputy minister said a reform is underway in the Chinese coal mining industry, which aims at adjusting this sector to the market requirements in management, progress in science and technology and modern know-how. The Staszic director said the Chinese coal industry delegation toured the colliery and watched the work of the Polish-manufactured machines which allow to increase tenfold hard coal extraction from one longwall. The Chinese guests were also interested in the organisational structure of the colliery, its financial results, the winding gear and the work of the mine rescue service station.
