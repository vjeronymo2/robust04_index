921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Hunter Saphir falls to Pounds 1m SHARES OF Hunter Saphir, the fresh produce, herbs and spice group, yesterday fell 7p to 30p on news of a downturn in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.82m to Pounds 1m over the 28 weeks ended September 12. The previous interim figure, however, included a consequential loss claim credit of Pounds 1.6m. Sales from continuing activities rose to Pounds 96m (Pounds 93.2m). Basic losses emerged at 1.46p (earnings 1.6p); fully diluted there were earnings of 1.3p (2.56p). The interim dividend is halved to 0.5p. There was an extraordinary credit of Pounds 1.99m before goodwill, but after taking account of a Pounds 4.71m debit for goodwill previously written off the attributable loss worked through at Pounds 2.05m (profits Pounds 1.22m).
