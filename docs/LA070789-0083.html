July 7, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Missouri basketball Coach Norm Stewart, who had to step down after midseason cancer surgery during one of the Tigers' finest seasons, will return in the fall, Athletic Director Dick Tamburo said. Stewart has declined to talk to the media and has not said what his plans are.

Tamburo also said the school is investigating allegations that NCAA rules were broken in the basketball program. Any NCAA penalties probably would not be announced until the fall, Tamburo said.
