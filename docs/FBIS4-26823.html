BFN [Text] Brussels, June 24 (XINHUA) -- The Supreme Allied Commander, Europe (Saceur), General George A. Joulwan, will visit Russia on June 26-28 at the invitation of Russia's first deputy defense minister, General Mikhail Petrovich Kolesnikov. A NATO spokesman said that the visit demonstrates the great value that both Russia and NATO attach to an intensification of military contacts. During his visit, General Joulwan will meet the Russian Minister of Defense General Pavel Grachev, and General Kolesnikov, to exchange views on issues of mutual interest. He will also lay a wreath at the Tomb of the Unknown Soldier in Moscow and visit an operational military unit, the 27th Motorized Rifle Brigade. On Wednesday [22 June] Russia signed a framework document for the "partnership for peace" program with NATO.
