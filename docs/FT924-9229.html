921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Receiver completes Mowat disposal Touche Ross, receiver of Mowat Group, has completed the sale of the Potter Heigham Boatyard and Fleet to Broad Tours. The sale was originally announced to have been at Pounds 3.87m, but Touche Ross could not confirm the completion price. The receiver is in talks for the sale of Mowat's holiday camps and is 'hoping to conclude a deal shortly'. Concerning Mowat's properties, Touche Ross is 'looking at options and methods' to achieve the best return possible.
