September 23, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

2ND U.S. HOSPITAL SHIP DUE IN GULF THIS WEEK

The hospital ship Mercy is in the Arabian Sea and by Wednesday will join its sister ship Comfort in the Persian Gulf, a U.S. Navy spokesman said Saturday.

The 69,000-ton ships each have 1,000 beds, 12 operating theaters and up to 850 medical staff, the spokesman said.
