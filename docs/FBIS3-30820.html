Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] The Slovak Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a statement today to a news agency on the criminal attack on the Sarajevo market. The statement notes: The Ministry resolutely condemns such deliberate attacks, whichever of the warring countries in Bosnia-Herzegovina might be the culprit. The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Slovak Republic at the same time appeals to the UN secretary general and the Security Council to adopt more radical measures in cooperation with other international organizations and regional groupings for a speedy solution of the politically unbearable and inhuman situation on the territory of Bosnia-Herzegovina.
