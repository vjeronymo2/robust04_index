Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Ukrainian and Polish foreign ministers had a telephone conversation on Friday during which it was decided to postpone the visit of Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko to Warsaw from February 28 to late March. According to sources close to the Ukrainian Foreign Ministry, the visit was put off due to the legal proceedings against an officer of the Ukrainian Security Service (the so-called case of Lysenko) continuing in Warsaw from January 19. Anatoliy Lysenko and his family left for Poland in August, 1993, on a private invitation and was arrested immediately after he crossed the border. Lysenko has been accused of spying for Ukraine. The Ukrainian Security Service refutes the accusations.
