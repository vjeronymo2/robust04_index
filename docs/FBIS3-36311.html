Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Abkhazian Defense Ministry reported on continuing acts of sabotage committed by the Georgian formations in the Abkhazian region of Ghali. The ministry's statement published on Thursday says that as many as 3 Abkhazian servicemen were killed during an act of sabotage near the populated center of Sida on February 2. The document underlined that this displayed the Georgian formations' intention to destabilize the situation in the region of Ghali and to block the Abkhazian troops defending the Abkhazian- Georgian border on the Inguri River. The Abkhazian Defense Ministry announced that it could "not ignore all these facts and would undertake the appropriate measures."
