BFN [Report by Ahmad al-Yamani] [Text] Al-'Aqabah -- While preparations were under way to celebrate the anniversary of the Great Arab Revolt, led by al-Sharif Husayn I of Mecca, a large number of U.S. rapid intervention forces of aggression were present in the port city of al-'Aqabah and the al-Jafr area for their usual annual exercises and training on fighting us in the desert, sea, and air. These forces had earlier participated in the 30-state aggression against Iraq and attacked our trucks and oil tankers, burning them and killing their occupants--our sons--while driving along the road in Jordan. Allowing the U.S. forces of aggression to pursue training on fighting us or resting and recreating in Jordan requires all the national forces and their representatives in the House of Representatives to firmly prevent this provocation and stop these exercises, which are due to last one month.
