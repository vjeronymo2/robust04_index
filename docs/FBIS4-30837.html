BFN [Excerpt] We begin this program with a report from a session of the people's assembly of the [Bosnian] republic of Srpska which opened in Pale today. Ljilja Kovacevic reports: [Kovacevic] At the two-day session of the people's assembly of the republic of Srpska, the deputies will review a number of laws and legal documents on internal policy. These are primarily laws and legal documents on the functioning of state bodies and a number of legal documents from the spheres of legislation, agriculture, health, and information. These will be discussed today, at the open part of the session. A proposal for the economic policy of the republic of Srpska for this year, a report on the work of the defense ministry, the problem of reorganizing the republic of Srpska army, as well as a report on the financial transaction and audit service will be discussed behind closed doors, most probably tomorrow.
