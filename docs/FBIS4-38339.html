BFN [V.K.-signed report under the "In Brief" rubric: "Moscow"] [Text] Yakutia President Mikhail Nikolayev has met with Russian President Boris Yeltsin in the Kremlin. Mr. Nikolayev described in detail the critical situation that has come about in the Republic of Yakutia owing to the failed efforts to bring freight and goods into so vast a region. He pointed out that the situation is becoming more than an economic problem and taking on an acute political nature. President Yeltsin voiced concern for the requests made by Mr. Nikolayev and issued instructions on all problems that were outlined. The question of granting an appropriate loan to the Republic of Sakha (Yakutia) was also resolved.
