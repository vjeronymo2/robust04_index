July 30, 1989, Sunday, Bulldog Edition

BAHRAIN FOREIGN MINISTER LEAVES FOR U.S. TALKS

Bahrain's Foreign Minister Sheik Mohammed ibn Mubarak Khalifa left Friday for the United States for talks on regional issues.

The Gulf news agency, received in Nicosia, said during his four-day visit Mohammed will deliver a message to President Bush from Bahrain's Emir Sheik Isa ibn Salman al Khalifa.
