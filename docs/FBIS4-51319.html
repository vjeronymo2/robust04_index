BFN [Text] After successfully winding up an official visit to Germany and Denmark, Xie Fei, CPC Central Committee Political Bureau member and Guangdong Provincial CPC Committee secretary, returned to Guangzhou yesterday evening. In Germany, Xie Fei and his entourage held extensive talks with friends in German industrial, commercial, and banking circles and briefed German friends on Guangdong's economic growth, investment environment, and projects open to foreign investment. The two sides also held conscientious discussions on ways and means of expanding bilateral cooperation and exchanges. In order to acquire a better understanding of the German economic, cultural, and environmental protection situations, Xie Fei and his entourage inspected Frankfort Airport, which is currently undergoing extension; travelled on a high-speed railroad in a high-speed special train made by Germany's Siemens Company; and visited a farm in Bavaria, where they also called on the farm owner. During their two-day stay in Denmark, the Guangdong provincial economic investigation delegation led by Xie Fei held separate talks with Denmark's ministers of agriculture and fisheries and foreign affairs and conscientiously conducted investigations into Denmark's agricultural production, farm products processing, and agricultural management structure. In their talks with people of all walks of life in Germany and Denmark, Xie Fei and his entourage fully publicized the excellent opportunities in and good prospects for Guangdong-Germany and Guangdong-Denmark economic and technological cooperation; expressed the hope that both sides would make continued efforts to further bilateral cooperation and exchanges; and discussed and agreed on with Germany and Denmark specific forms of bilateral cooperation as well as a number of joint-venture projects.
