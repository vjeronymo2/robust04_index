June 16, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

STAR'S SENTENCE DISPUTED

After reading about Kelsey Grammer ("Cheers" star) being given 30 days for violating probation -- I wanted to throw up.

If he was a poor man, he couldn't afford a good attorney. He would be thrown in jail for a year or perhaps more.

And we talk about "Justice for All."

DOROTHY DUNN

Sherman Oaks
