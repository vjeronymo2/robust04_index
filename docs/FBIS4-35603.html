BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 Jun (MENA)--The Nasirite Arab Democratic Party announced a freeze on its participation in the committee in charge of preparation for national dialogue. In a statement today, the party announced a freeze on its participation in the dialogue until the approach to this dialogue, its topics, and the parties involved in it are reviewed. The statement said that the committee in charge of preparing for the dialogue was formed in full violation of the agreement reached among the political parties. According to the statement, the committee was supposed to be formed after consultations among political parties and not by a National Democratic Party decision.
