Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 27 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent thanks to the employees of the Institute of Unity Management Science of the State Academy of Sciences and the robot and computer assembling plant under the branch Academy of Electronic and Automation Science. The researchers, workers and technicians of the unit are producing electronic and automation elements and devices needed for the development of the electronic industry of Korea onto a higher stage and various electronic goods. They invented and mass-produced new items conducive to the promotion of the people's health.
