BFN [Text] Mr. Ahmad Sa'id al-Suraymah, the governor of Shabwah Governorate and the commander of the Shabwah Governorate military axis, refuted the declaration by the Sanaa regime that its forces are in control of 90 percent of Yemeni land. Al-Suraymah commented that this applies to the Yemen Arab Republic land. He told the YEMENI NEWS AGENCY, Aden, that the Democratic Republic of Yemen's forces are controlling 90 percent of the Shabwah Governorate, which the Sanaa military regime had wanted to occupy. He affirmed that our Armed Forces and the hero people of Shabwah had turned the aggressors away, after inflicting on them heavy casualties.
