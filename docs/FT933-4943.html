930907 FT 07 SEP 93 / Yeltsin follows suspension with lock out President Boris Yeltsin has followed suspension of his vice president by locking him out of his office, Itar-Tass news agency said yesterday, Leyla Boulton reports. The offices of Mr Alexander Rutskoi and of Mr Vladimir Shumeiko, suspended first deputy prime minister, have been put 'under the protection' of the president's security service to prevent 'manipulation' of documents which would hinder corruption investigations.
