920901 FT 01 SEP 92 / Letter: Insulting by numbers Sir, Pakistanis' dislike of the number 420 (Observer, August 24) is based not on any code describing swindlers but on Pakistan's criminal code, where the relevant clause is, indeed, number 420. About the worst you can say of a businessman is that he is a char saw bees aadmi - a 420 man. Perhaps, in view of the difficulties on the UK financial scene in recent years, one might be able to refer to relevant clauses from the Financial Services Act or other laws to describe dubious individuals one encounters] Khalid Aziz, chairman, Aziz Corporation, West Stratton, Winchester SO21 3DR
