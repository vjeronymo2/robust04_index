940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Union Square Pounds 73,000 in the black Union Square, the property investment and development group, turned in a pre-tax profit of Pounds 73,000 in the six months to end-September, against a deficit of Pounds 9,000 last time. A nil tax charge left earnings per share of 0.04p (nil). Directors said the past six months had represented a period of consolidation for the group, which was now looking to rebuild the strength of its balance sheet and improve its level of profitability.
