May 20, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

E. CONTINI'S DEATH

I was deeply saddened to learn of Edgardo Contini's death. He was far more than an architect.

He was a colleague from the UCLA campus. In a development community characterized by glitz and slickness, he personified charm and class. It was always a delight to interact with him, even when he was on the other side of the bargaining table. He brought intelligence, dignity, patience, wit and a quiet strength to the process of development. He will be sorely missed.

LAURA LAKE

Los Angeles

Editor's Note: Edgardo Contini died April 28 at age 76. His obituary was published in Section A of The Times on May 1.
