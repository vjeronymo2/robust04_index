BFN [Text] Ibrahim Ghawshah, spokesman for the Islamic Resistance Movement, Hamas, reiterated in Damascus today his objection to the agreements signed recently between Israel and the Palestinians and between Israel and Jordan. He said: We reject the defeatist policy that began in Madrid, Oslo, and Cairo and ended now in Amman, because these policies aim at relinquishing the rights and lands of the Palestinian people. Ghawshah made this statement before the meeting of the 10 Palestinian factions that oppose the self-rule agreement. Palestinian sources said the conferees will discuss in this meeting the situation in the self-rule territories and in all territories still under occupation.
