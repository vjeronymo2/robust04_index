920822 FT 22 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Sale Tilney agreement Sale Tilney, which has food, industrial and financial services interests, has reached agreement with its Isle of Man-based insurance underwriting subsidiary, Monument Marine & General, on proposals for two schemes of arrangement involving Monument and its creditors, and Sale Tilney and certain Monument policyholders. If the schemes become effective Sale Tilney's beneficial interest in Monument will be transferred to a trustee for the benefit of scheme creditors and any further group liability in respect of Monument's losses will be eliminated.
