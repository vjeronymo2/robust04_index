930216 FT 16 FEB 93 / UK Company News: MY Holdings acquisition MY Holdings, the packaging company, is to acquire Kohler Packaging from Tawneydown for between Pounds 1.33m and Pounds 1.45m, satisfied by the issue of loan stock in up to four tranches. Tawneydown is a wholly owned subsidiary of Malbak, a South African industrial company with extensive packaging interests. Tawneydown holds about 86 per cent of MY's equity. Kohler, based in Hampshire, makes printed folding cartons for the health care industry. It began trading in March 1991 and made pre-tax profits of Pounds 13,000 on turnover of Pounds 2.02m in its first full year ended August 29 1992.
