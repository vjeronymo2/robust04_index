November 2, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

THE BOTTOM LINE

The Indiana Pacers placed veteran forward Calvin Natt on the injured list, bringing the team to the NBA's 12-man limit. Natt, 32, has been bothered with a series of physical problems since training camp began and is currently battling a sore knee and thigh muscles. The 6-foot-6 Natt, signed as a free agent this summer after he was waived by San Antonio in February.
