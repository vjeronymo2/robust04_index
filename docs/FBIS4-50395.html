BFN [Text] Beijing, May 16 (XINHUA) -- The third international TV show opened here today, drawing over 100 television experts and industrialists from some 20 countries and regions. The show is co-sponsored by the Beijing Municipal Broadcasting and Television Bureau and the Beijing Television Station. Experts will exchange TV programs and display the latest acoustics and television facilities. Representatives from the United States, Germany, Britain, Japan, Italy, Hong Kong and Taiwan are among those attending the week-long show.
