November 2, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: EL TORO;

HOLIDAY FOOD DRIVE TO BENEFIT FAMILIES

A Thanksgiving food drive to benefit needy families will be held in El Toro Nov. 18.

Donors are asked to bring non-perishable foods from 9 a.m. to 4 p.m. to Serrins Chiropractic, 29941 Aventura Lane. Those bringing food can receive free chiropractic services and are eligible for door prizes.
