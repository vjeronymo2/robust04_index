July 8, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

DIGEST: LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

ROOFER DIES IN FALL FROM HOME BEING BUILT

A 23-year-old roofer fell from a home under construction in Quartz Hill and died Friday, a Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department spokesman said.

Jose Manuel Gutierrez, 23, suffered a broken neck after falling from a roof in a housing tract at 50th Street West and Avenue M-8 at 7:30 a.m., the spokesman said. Gutierrez died at the scene.

The death is being investigated as an industrial accident, the spokesman said.
