941221 FT 21 DEC 94 / Observer: Waiting room Three regional electricity companies have set tongues wagging in the industry by sending out the same corporate Christmas card, in aid of the hospice movement. They are: Northern, which is the subject of a hostile bid from Trafalgar House; Seeboard, widely thought to be the next most likely candidate for a takeover; and Southern which last week indicated it would be interested in mergers. More than just a coincidence?
