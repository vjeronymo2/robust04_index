Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother President Abu 'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] received last night their excellencies the European Union ambassadors delegation, which was led by the Greek ambassador. The delegation included the Belgian ambassador, the German charge d' affaires, and the European Commission ambassador. The brother President Abu 'Ammar briefed them on the latest development in the peace process and the UN Security Council Resolution 904 on the al-Ibrahami Mosque massacre. The brother president called upon the ambassadors' prime ministers to swiftly implement the UN Security Council 904 on the protection of our Palestinian people in the Palestinian occupied territories, asserting the need for the European countries' participation in the international forces to be sent to the Palestinian occupied territories to protect our Palestinian people.
