Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] A Belarusian official delegation has concluded its visit to London. The main purpose of the visit was to sign intergovernment agreements on economic cooperation and the encouragement and protection of investments. Chairman of the Belarusian Foreign Trade Committee Radkevich and Minister of Trade and Industry Heseltine signed the documents on behalf of the Belarusian and British Governments, respectively. The Belarusian delegation members visited the Trade and Industry Department and met with representatives of British major banks and companies that came up with a range of cooperation initiatives.
