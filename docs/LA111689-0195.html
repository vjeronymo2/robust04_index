November 16, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

LOOSE ENDS: Joseph Angelo Grosso has been convicted (A1), but two investigations related to the mob-tainted demise of Santa Ana's Mustang Club topless bar are stalled. . . . The investigation of the 1987 murder in Buena Park of club operator Jimmy Lee Casino is "in kind of a limbo," police say. . . . And in Brea, where Horace Joseph (Big Mac) McKenna was killed in March, the investigation "is winding down rapidly," investigators said. Dana Parsons and Steve Emmons
