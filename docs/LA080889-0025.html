August 8, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Look for "Ghostbusters II" to be available in the home video market by Christmas. The word Monday from RCA/Columbia at the eighth annual Video Software Dealers convention in Las Vegas is that the hit film will hit the market in the fourth quarter of this year, possibly by Thanksgiving. The expected price tag: around $90. "See No Evil, Hear No Evil" is also headed for the video shelves about the same time. Convention delegates now are awaiting word from Warner Bros. as to when "Batman" might be available in video form. An announcement could be made Wednesday at a Warner-hosted luncheon. ALEENE MacMINN
