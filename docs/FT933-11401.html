930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / International Company News: Anheuser Busch increases dividend ANHEUSER-BUSCH, the biggest US brewer, yesterday posted essentially flat second-quarter net income on sales which edged 1.3 per cent higher and said it would raise its quarterly dividend by 12.5 per cent. For the 1993 second quarter, the company had net income of Dollars 308.6m on sales of Dollars 3.43bn, compared with earnings of Dollars 308.4m on sales of Dollars 3.38bn a year earlier. Fully diluted earnings per share rose 4.7 per cent to Dollars 1.11 from Dollars 1.06. There were fewer shares outstanding in the latest quarter. The company also said it would increase its quarterly dividend on common stock to 36 cents a share from 32 cents. For the first half, the company had net income of Dollars 502.7m, or Dollars 1.80 on sales of Dollars 6.3bn. A year earlier, Anheuser-Busch earned Dollars 446.8m, or Dollars 1.54, in the first six months on sales of Dollars 6.41bn. Last year's earnings included charges of Dollars 76.7m for accounting changes. During the second quarter, the company's market share rose to 41.9 per cent compared with 41.6 per cent a year ago.
