910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / Arts: Ronnie Scott to open in Birmingham Ronnie Scott and his partner Pete King are to open a jazz club in Birmingham, opposite the International Convention Centre and the New Festival Hall in Broad Street. Like their Soho venue, it will be open six nights a week and will book top US names as well as British. A corporate membership scheme is available. Details from Ronnie Scott's Club, 258 Broad Street, Birmingham B12 H5. Tel: 021 643 4525.
