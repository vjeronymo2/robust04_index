940319 FT 19 MAR 94 / Northern growth continues, says CBI Businesses in north east England and Cumbria are continuing to see steady growth in trade, although margins remain very tight, Mr David Cranston, the Confederation of British Industry's, new northern regional chairman said yesterday. Mr Cranston, chief executive of Northumbrian Water, said the northern economy was continuing its steady recovery, and a few companies were finding market conditions positively buoyant.
