Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- German Chancellor Helmut Kohl has stressed that he wishes to continue the Bonn Government coalition between the Union parties and the FDP [Free Democratic Party] following the Bundestag elections on 16 October this year. He would "fight" for this, Kohl said today during an interview with the private television station SAT1. He intended to conduct and win an "intensive and highly committed election campaign." Kohl added he was "not particularly impressed" by poor opinion poll results. "The election will be decided on polling day," he said.
