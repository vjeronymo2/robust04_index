940826 FT 26 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Delaney turns in Pounds 232,000 Delaney, the direct sales fitted furniture company, consolidated its recovery in 1993 and reported a pre-tax profit of Pounds 232,000 for the half year to June 30. The outcome compared with a loss of Pounds 187,000 last time and came from turnover up from Pounds 7.52m to Pounds 8.97m. Earnings per share emerged at 0.2p (0.2p losses). No interim dividend is declared, but Court approval for a share capital reconstruction was granted on July 20, leaving the way open for the company to distribute profits.
