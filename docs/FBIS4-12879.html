BFN [Text] The Lebanese Foreign Ministry today officially asked the Iraqi Embassy in Beirut to lift the immunity of the two diplomats accused of killing Shaykh Talib al-Suhayl. The Iraqi Embassy meanwhile demanded that the two diplomats be released because they enjoy diplomatic immunity. Iraqi Charge D'affaires 'Awad Fakhri has been summoned by the Foreign Ministry to meet Foreign Ministry Secretary General Ambassador Zafir al-Hasan. The Iraqi diplomat has already arrived at the Foreign Ministry.
