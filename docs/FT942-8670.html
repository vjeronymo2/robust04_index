940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Porter Chadburn disposal Porter Chadburn, the packaging group, is completing its exit from the consumer products business with the sale of its Leeda fishing tackle group. The buyer is a company in which the executive directors of Leeda have an interest. Proceeds of the sale are expected to be about Pounds 4m, representing book value plus a premium of Pounds 400,000. In addition, a further Pounds 1.5m is expected to be raised from assets being retained. The sale follows the recent disposals of Sportline and TPL Marketing and Adam Leisure's withdrawal from its toy and baby products business. Proceeds from these transactions are expected to amount to Pounds 11m, which will be used to reduce group borrowings.
