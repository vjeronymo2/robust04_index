BFN [Text] Budapest, 9 May (MTI) -- After counting 99.9 percent of the votes, the unofficial computerized report states that candidates of the Hungarian Socialist Party (MSZP) lead in 160 of the 176 individual constituencies, and have already secured two parliamentary seats. In addition, MSZP candidates finished in second place in 15 of the remaining constituencies and in third place in one. In other words, the Socialist Party's candidates are in the running for the second round in all individual constituencies. Candidates of the Alliance of Free Democrats finished first in 12 constituencies, second in 125, and third in 25. The Hungarian Democratic Forum has one candidate who awaits the second round in first place, 18 in second, and 81 in third. The Independent Smallholders Party came in second in five constituencies, third in 42. The Christian Democratic People's Party won in one constituency, was second in seven, and third in 10. The Federation of Young Democrats secured one second place and 11 third places in the individual races. The Workers' Party has one second and two third places. The Agrarian Federation has one first and two second places. Independent candidates finished second in one constituency and third in two. The Republic Party managed to come in second in one constituency.
