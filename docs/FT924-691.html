921223 FT 23 DEC 92 / World News in Brief: Win for council dustmen A decision by Michael Heseltine, taken when he was environment secretary, to close the London borough of Haringey's own refuse collection service was overturned by the High Court. The closure move came after allegations of 'anti-competitive behaviour' by the Labour controlled council in awarding itself the contract. Heseltine rebuffs pit restart calls, Page 4
