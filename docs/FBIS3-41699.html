Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Unattributed article: ``Diptheria Outbreak in Zapolyarye''] [Text] Diptheria, a disease already forgotten by physicians in Zapolyarye, has reminded them of itself menacingly. In the Murmansk Oblast alone, 45 cases of the disease diptheria have been recorded in the first 7 months of this year. One case had a lethal outcome. Efforts are being made to keep the disease from spreading.
