940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea A good demand prevailed report the Tea Broker Association. Best liquoring and good medium East Africans opened firm to dearer but tended easier by the close. Mediums ruled firm with coloury types often dearer while Central Africans sold readily but at lower rates. There were less withdrawals than of late. Quotations: best available 280p/kg, nom good 160p/kg, good medium 150p/kg, medium 125p/kg,low medium 92p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 290p for a rwanda pf.
