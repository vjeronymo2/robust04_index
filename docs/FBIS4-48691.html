BFN [Text] President Joaquim Chissano said today that public opinion in the provinces of Cabo Delgado, Nampula, Sofala, Tete, Manica, and Gaza encourages him to say that the forthcoming multiparty elections will be won by the Mozambique Liberation Front [Frelimo] Party and its presidential candidate. The Mozambican head of state was addressing about 15,000 people at a mass rally in Pemba to mark the end of his working visit to Cabo Delgado Province. Chissano requested Cabo Delgado residents to vote with confidence and responsibility. President Chissano also said that demobilized and war-disabled troops, as well as Mozambicans who returned from the former GDR and other countries, should demonstrate their initiative so that the Mozambican state and the international community can grant them assistance. The head of state said this in reply to the demands by these people's associations that the government should give them jobs and funds for their wellbeing. President Chissano returns to Maputo today.
