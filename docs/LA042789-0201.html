April 27, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

LOCAL;

LOTTO JACKPOT GROWS TO $12 MILLION

No one picked all six winning numbers in Wednesday night's $5.77 million "Lotto 6-49" game, and the jackpot will grow to an estimated $12 million, lottery officials said today.

Seven players, picking five of the numbers, will receive $222,591 each. The numbers were 38, 11, 49, 17, 36, 44 and the bonus number, 3.
