March 26, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

TEXANS WALK TALL ON TV SERIES

Former Mayor Henry Cisneros will be host of a new syndicated series of TV specials featuring interviews with Texas celebrities.

"Texans," scheduled to debut in April, will be shown in Texas, New Mexico and Oklahoma.

The first show will feature former Dallas Cowboys Coach Tom Landry, country-Western singer Janie Fricke and Dr. Red Duke, a trauma surgeon in Houston, executive producer Bob Phillips said.
