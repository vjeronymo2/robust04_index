920523 FT 23 MAY 92 / UK Company News: New Throgmorton net assets decline New Throgmorton Trust (1983) reported a sharp decline in net asset value to 128.2p at the end of March 1992, against 197.1p a year earlier. Pre-tax profits for the year were unchanged at Pounds 4.1m, while earnings per share slipped from 8.02p to 7.14p. A recommended final dividend of 2.25p makes a total for the year of 6.75p (8p).
