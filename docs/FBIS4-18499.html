BFN [Text] The leader of the National Alliance, Gianfranco Fini, has said that he is satisfied with the decision of Silvio Berlusconi to break off talks with the Northern League: "Other than a four-sided agreement which comprises the Forza Italia, the Northern League, the National Alliance and the Christian Democrat Center party, said Fini, there is room for nothing but an election. The only other possibility is a government made up of the League, the Party of the Democratic Left and the Italian Popular Party. If we returned to the polls, not a single representative of the League would be elected to parliament". Berlusconi is right when he says that he cannot take anymore, continued Fini. " How can one carry on negotiations with someone who goes back on what they said the day before".
