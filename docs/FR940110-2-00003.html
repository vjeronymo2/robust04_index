Forest Service Grand Island Advisory Commission; Meeting AGENCY: Forest Service, USDA. ACTION: Grand Island Advisory Commission meeting.

SUMMARY: The Grand Island Advisory Commission will meet on January 21, 1994 at 8 a.m. at the Comfort Inn on M&hyph;28 East in Munising, Michigan. An agenda for the one day meeting will consist of a final planning team update, a review of the final changes to the alternatives and a discussion by the Advisory Commission on the recommendation to the Secretary Interested members of the public are encouraged to attend.
