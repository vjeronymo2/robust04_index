BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondent] [Text] Peking, 27 May -- A meeting between Viktor Chernomyrdin, Chairman of the Russian Federation Government, who is on an official visit to China, and Jiang Zemin, Chairman of the PRC, took place today in the state residence of Zhongnanhai. "I believe that cooperation between the two countries is developing very successfully", Jiang Zemin said, opening the meeting. He expressed confidence that the visit of the head of the Russian Government would give a new boost to economic cooperation. Viktor Chernomyrdin conveyed to PRC Chairman Jiang Zemin greetings from Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin and his invitation to visit Russia in early September this year. During the course of the meeting Viktor Chernomyrdin also pointed out that Russo-Chinese relations are flourishing and are not dependent on changes in the political scene. The level of mutual relations and cooperation which has been achieved between Russia and China gives hopes for Rusian-Chinese relations to be switched to a higher stage -- a stage of economic cooperation.
