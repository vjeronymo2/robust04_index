921204 FT 04 DEC 92 / Parliament and Politics: Labour has 23-point lead in Gallup poll LABOUR holds a 23-point lead over the Conservatives in a Gallup poll taken for today's Daily Telegraph. For support in a general election if one were held tomorrow, Labour is on 52 per cent (up 2 per cent from the previous poll last month), the Conservatives on 29 per cent (down 3 1/2 ) the Liberal Democrats 14 per cent (up 1) and others 5 per cent (up 1/2 ). The figures are based on a sample of 1,026 electors in 100 districts interviewed between November 25 and 30.
