BFN [Text] Tokyo, June 1 [date as received] (KNS-KCNA) -- A seminar on the chuche idea was held recently in Sanda, Hyogo prefecture, under the co- sponsorship of the societies for the study of Kim Chong-il's works in Kansai district, Japan. Lectures on the subjects "On ideological and theoretical achievements of secretary Kim Chong-il" and "Chuche-based view of life" were given at the seminar. Masaji Ie, chief advisor to the Japan Liaison Council of Societies for the Study of Kim Chong-il's Works and professor at the Kobe University of Foreign Studies, elaborated on the great ideological and theoretical achievements of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il in developing in depth the immortal chuche idea founded by the respected leader President Kim Il-song.
