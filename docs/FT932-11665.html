930501 FT 01 MAY 93 / Finance & The Family: Broker launches new product - At a glance John Charcol, mortgage brokers, has launched a fixed-rate mortgage which allows the holder to make capital repayments without facing early redemption penalties. The rate is fixed at 7.99 per cent (8.7 APR) until January 1 1997. It is only available on a repayment basis and there are no requirements to buy insurance-related products. The fee on completion is Pounds 295 with a Pounds 35 reservation payment. The mortgage has been designed to give borrowers the option of increasing their monthly repayments. The 'accelerator mortgage' is also available on a variable rate of 7.99 per cent.
