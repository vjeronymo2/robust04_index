921113 FT 13 NOV 92 / Survey of Japan and the European Community (6): Cumulative Japanese direct investment abroad Cumulative amount, all countries, 1980: Dollars 36.49bn; rising to Dollars 352,392m in 1990, showing an average annual rate of growth from 1985-90 of 33.3 per cent.
