930222 FT 22 FEB 93 / International Company News: Sherwood Computer in two deals Sherwood Computer Services has paid Pounds 300,000 in cash to acquire THS (Guernsey), in a move which strengthens its position in the insurance companies market. THS (Guernsey) comprises the majority of the assets and business of the company which traded as THS Limited, a subsidiary of English & American Group. At the date of acquisition, THS (Guernsey) had net assets of Pounds 1.1m. The deal will give Sherwood the rights to a number of proprietary IBM software products. In a separate move, Sherwood announced a joint venture with Beta Computers (Europe) to form a new company, Sherwood International. The company is 51 per cent owned by Sherwood, which acquired its equity by way of a subscription for shares for Pounds 255,000 cash. The new company has been formed to acquire the rights to PALACE, an advanced life and pensions administration system with international potential.
