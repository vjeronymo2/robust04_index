August 14, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

TACOMA LOSES PREKI, TO ST. LOUIS STORM

The St. Louis Storm on Monday signed Preki, a forward who scored 219 goals in 247 games for the Tacoma Stars of the Major Soccer League during the past five seasons.

Preki, 27, ranked fifth in the league with 33 goals and 39 assists last year.
