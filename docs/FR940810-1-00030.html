Section 352.33 is revised to read as follows: §352.33 What is the project period of a Rehabilitation Research and Training Center? Awards are made under this program for a period of five years except that awards may be made for a lesser period if_ (a) The award is made to a new recipient; or (b) The award supports a new research area or an innovative approach to a research area. (Authority: Sec. 204(b)(2)(L); 29 U.S.C. 762(b)(2)(L)) 20. A new Section 352.34 is added to read as follows: §352.34 What other factors does the Secretary consider in making an award under this program? In making an award under this program, the Secretary takes into consideration the location of any proposed Center and the appropriate geographic and regional allocation of all Centers.
