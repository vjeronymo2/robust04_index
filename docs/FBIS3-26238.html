Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Chengdu, 31 Jan (ZHONGGUO TONGXUN SHE) -- The national conference of price inspection office directors, concluded here the other day, reported that from January through November 1993, a total of 220,000 cases of price-related illegal practices and cases were handled nationwide. According to the information, the illicit gain reaped through unlawful price increases discovered last year totaled 353 million yuan, of which 87 million yuan was refunded. The conference emphasized that the prices of people's daily necessities and charges for various kinds of services will become the main objects of inspection in the coming year. At present, inspection of the prices of grain, oil, meat, eggs, and vegetables should be well conducted and such irregularities as jacking up prices, seeking exorbitant profits, and disrupting market prices must be firmly handled. Unauthorized price increases and charges for railway, highway, and water transportation and forcing customers to buy insurance when selling or handling various kinds of tickets should also be dealt with according to law.
