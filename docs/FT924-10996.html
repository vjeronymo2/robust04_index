921028 FT 28 OCT 92 / Army contract goes to Saab A Pounds 40M CONTRACT to provide the British Army with laser equipment to simulate firing has been awarded to the Saab group of Sweden. Saab was chosen in preference to Loral Solartron, a subsidiary of the US Loral group, which provides similar equipment for US forces. The equipment is designed to reduce the amount of live firing carried out by troops in training. It involves laser projectors attached to a range of weapons including tanks, anti-tank missiles and guns.
