Language: Amharic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Addis Ababa police today reported that unidentified gunmen fired three times at an American Embassy staff member while the diplomat was returning towards town from the Bole US AID offices. The police added that the embassy staff member who was attacked escaped unhurt, and that the criminals have not yet been apprehended. The police also noted that the shooting came from a Toyota Land Cruiser which was identical in make and color to the one belonging to the American Embassy staff member, had a diplomatic corps registration number of 04 Addis Ababa 60, and approached the left-hand side of the embassy staff member's car. Two of the three bullets fired penetrated the driver's door, and the two vehicles collided as a result. [passage omitted]
