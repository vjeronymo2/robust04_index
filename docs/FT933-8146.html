930817 FT 17 AUG 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Positive reaction to changes in Brazil's Central Bank command and strong selective buying due to today's options settlement boosted shares by 6.2 per cent in heavy trade on the Sao Paulo exchange. The Bovespa index ended at 77,278, up 4,538 from Friday's close. Volume hit a record of Cr46.3bn. The options settlements accounted for 50.3 per cent of the total turnover.
