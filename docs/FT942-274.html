940629 FT 29 JUN 94 / International Company News: Poland tobacco privatisation Poland is likely to announce privatisation of its large but derelict tobacco industry this week and foreign bidders have already shown interest, a privatisation ministry official said, Reuter reports from Warsaw. 'I expect a strategic decision on the industry's privatisation will be have been taken by June 30,' Mr Andrzej Piechocki, adviser to Mr Wieslaw Kaczmarek, the privatisation minister, said. Privatisation was the only way to help the industry modernise and stand up to western competition, he added.
