BFN [Excerpt] London, QUDS PRESS -- British sources have confirmed that Britain has agreed to construct a power station in the occupied territories as part of a $300 million trade deal. The sources said that the agreement was reached during recent talks in Tunis between British diplomats and PLO officials. The implementation of this agreement was "linked" to the attainment of self-rule authority by the Palestinians in Gaza and Jericho. [passage omitted]
