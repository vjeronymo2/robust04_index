BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 19 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Completing his official visit to Mongolia the Governor General of Australia Bill Hayden has spoken at the session of the Mongolian parliament. My current visit to Mongolia which is the first one is a manifestation of developing recently close contacts between the two countries. In this respect I would like to say addressing the members of the Mongolian parliament that Australia has been and is supporting the democratic renovations in Mongolia. We are appreciating the fact that Mongolia is striving to expand her relations with the countries of the Asian and Pacific region. We Australians are greeting the peaceful transition of the country to democracy, said in his speech Bill Hayden. Touching upon the bilateral cooperation the governor general notes that the representatives of the two parliaments have mutually paid visits and it is a hope that a number of spheres of cooperation might become a real support for the Mongolian people such as training of Mongolian specialists, cooperation in the agricultural sphere especially in keeping animal husbandry in semideserted natural conditions, mining cultural and humanitarian spheres, etc. The governor general has expressed his gratitude for a warm welcome and hospitality during the days of a visit of his.
