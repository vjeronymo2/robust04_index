BFN [Announcer-read report over still of Finance and Customs Minister Ismet Atilla] [Text] Finance and Customs Minister Ismet Atilla has repudiated claims that a net wealth or capital tax will be included in the stabilization program which is being prepared by the government. In a written statement, Atilla stated that including a net wealth or capital tax in the stabilization program prepared by the government is unthinkable.
