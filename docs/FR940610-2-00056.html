Norwest Corporation; Acquisition of Company Engaged in Permissible Nonbanking Activities; Correction This notice corrects a notice (FR Doc. 94-13153) published on page 28105 of the issue for Tuesday, May 31, 1994. Under the Federal Reserve Bank of Minneapolis heading, the entry for Norwest Corporation is corrected to read as follows: Norwest Corporation, Minneapolis, Minnesota; to acquire through Norwest Mortgage, Inc., Minneapolis, Minnesota, the mortgage business of American National Bank of Silver City, Silver City, New Mexico, and thereby engage in originating and servicing mortgages pursuant to 225.25(b)(1) of the Board's Regulation Y. These activities will be conducted in Silver City, New Mexico. Comments on this application must be received by June 24, 1994. Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, June 6, 1994. Jennifer J. Johnson, Associate Secretary of the Board. [FR Doc. 94-14160 Filed 6-9-94; 8:45 am] BILLING CODE 6210-01-F
