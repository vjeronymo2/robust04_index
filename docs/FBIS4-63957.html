BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko and Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev, 25 May -- "Personnel of the Security Service's Main Directorate in Crimea are continuing to carry out their duties of protecting the security of the state in a precise and organized manner, in spite of the difficult political situation that has arisen around the regional branch of the special service." Thus, reads a release for the mass media circulated today by the press center of the Ukrainian Security Service. The main task for staff of the Ukrainian Security Service's Main Directorate is vigorously to campaign against corruption and organized crime, to protect the region's economy from criminal violations, and to maintain peace and calm in the Republic of Crimea, the document stresses. Furthermore, the Security Service is closely collaborating with the internal affairs and procuracy bodies. The requirement to provide the president, the leaders of the parliament, and the Supreme Soviet of Crimea, and local authorities with full and objective information in the interests of resolving the autonomous republic's vital issues also remains in force.
