July 13, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: FULLERTON MAN MISSING FROM LOW-SECURITY JAIL

A 26-year-old Fullerton man was missing from the Theo Lacy minimum-security jail facility during an early morning bed check, a Sheriff's Department spokesman said.

Manuel S. Orosco, 26, was absent after a bed check revealed only his jail clothing, Sheriff's Sgt. Richard Paddock said.

Orosco, who was awaiting trial on a drug charge, is not considered dangerous, he said.

An investigation is being conducted to determine how Orosco escaped, Paddock said.
