March 9, 1990, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: DEL MAR

Bettie Kujawa of Coronado, Gloria Parry of Del Mar and E. Allan Royster of Poway were reappointed Thursday to the 22nd District Agricultural Assn. by Gov. George Deukmejian.

Kujawa, 66, the owner and manager of Viva Burgers -- a San Diego fast-food restaurant -- has served on the board since 1986. She is also a member of the San Diego County Hazardous Waste Management Advisory Committee.

Parry, 67, served as the assistant livestock superintendent at the Del Mar Fair for 10 years until retiring in 1986. Since then, she has served on the board.

Royster, 52, president of Royster Construction Co., has also been on the board since 1986.

The three appointees' new terms expire Jan. 15, 1994.
