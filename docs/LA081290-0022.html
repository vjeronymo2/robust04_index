August 12, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

GAY-BASHING IN THE RECORD INDUSTRY

Judging by the public image of many gay-bashing rock musicians, their struggle for masculine security continues.

What else explains their tendency toward portraying women as phallic slaves and looking more confused in their gender identities than do most avowed homsexuals?

Without whole men to call our youths out of an increasingly long and confused adolescence, the gap between homophobes (and their troubadours) and homosexuals will only widen.

Gay activists swat at symptoms in attempting to silence gay-bashing rockers.

ANDY COMISKEY

Los Angeles
