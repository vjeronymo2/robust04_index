Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN ["Dispatch" by staff reporter: "KAIFANG RIBAO Starts Publication; Wang Qianghua Appointed Chief Editor"] [Text] Shenzhen, 31 Dec--It is reported here that KAIFANG RIBAO [Open Daily], which is sponsored by the Special Economic Zone's Office of the State Council, is to start publication in Shenzhen on New Year's Day. Wang Qianghua, former deputy director of the China Press and Publications Administration, is its chief editor. The daily will carry an important 40,000-odd-character article entitled "China's Policy Decision and Practice of Opening Up to the Outside World." It is learned that an official in charge of the Special Economic Zone's Office of the State Council has spent quite some time writing this article.
