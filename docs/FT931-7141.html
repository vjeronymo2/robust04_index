930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / World News in Brief: Date set for Clinton-Yeltsin summit US president Bill Clinton (right) and his Russian counterpart Boris Yeltsin are to hold their first summit meeting on April 4 at a venue yet to be decided. Mr Clinton had hoped for a meeting in March, but Russian foreign minister Andrei Kozyrev said the agenda would probably require longer preparation. Summit, Page 8
