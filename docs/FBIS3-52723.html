Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] This morning 101 Palestinian prisoners were released from prison installations in southern Israel. They are now on their way home. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan reports that the prisoners, most of whom were released from Qezi'ot prison, are now being driven home by bus. Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin decided to release the prisoners as a goodwill gesture prior to the resumption of the Taba talks. The prisoners belong to organizations that support the political process. None of them were convicted of either murder or manslaughter.
