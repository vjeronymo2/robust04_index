Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By Yan Liqiang (0917 0500 1730): "Exert Strenuous Efforts to Firmly Grasp the Party's Ideological and Theoretical Construction and the Building of Leading Bodies To Guarantee a Long Period of Peace and Security in China"] [Excerpts] The municipal organizational work conference was held yesterday. Chen Xitong, secretary of the municipal party committee, stressed in his speech: We must uphold the party's leadership and strengthen party building in the course of accelerating reform and development. In this year's organizational work, the task of prime importance is to continuously exert strenuous efforts to firmly grasp the party's ideological and theoretical construction and the building of leading bodies and to guarantee the implementation of the central authorities' major reform policy decisions and the capital's stability and unity. First of all, Chen Xitong fully affirmed the prominent achievements in Tianjin's organizational work, and at the same time, he pointed out: This is a crucial year for comprehensively implementing the guidelines of the third plenary session of the 14th CPC Central Committee. Making all-out efforts to safeguard the capital's political and social stability and guaranteeing the smooth implementation of the central authorities' major reform policy decisions is a major matter of prime importance in the work of Beijing Municipality and in organizational work. The organizational work should serve this overall situation, and strenuous efforts should be made to grasp two basic construction. First, we should pay attention to ideological and theoretical construction, strengthen study of Marxism, Mao Zedong Thought, and Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics, strive to raise the ideological and theoretical level of party-member cadres, formulate plans, and provide evaluations. Second, we should pay attention to the building of leading bodies, and after selecting a number of outstanding young cadres as the new leading bodies during the district and county end-of-term elections, be determined to select a number of outstanding young cadres for the leading bodies of the municipal party committee and of various departments, committees, offices, and bureaus of the municipal government, and install the echelon well. Meanwhile, it is necessary to strengthen the building of grass-roots party organizations, with an emphasis in consolidating the backward party branches and strengthening the building of party branches in three types of foreign-funded enterprises, people-run enterprises, high-tech enterprises, and various new economic organizations. Chen Xitong stressed: Strengthening party building is a fundamental task in consolidating the party's leadership and guaranteeing a long period of peace and stability in the country and we must grasp it firmly and successfully. Chen Guangwen, deputy secretary of the municipal party committee, delivered a report at the conference. He summarized last year's work and put forth this year's tasks. In 1993, in carrying out the municipal organizational work, we persisted in emancipating the mind, seeking truth from facts, conscientiously implementing all tasks set forth by the 14th party congress and the seventh municipal party congress, realized the municipal party committee's demand of comprehensively overpassing the work of last year, and achieved a series of breakthroughs. Leading cadres enhanced awareness in the study of the theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics and achieved noticeable results in this aspect; the selection of young cadres was strengthened and the structure of leading bodies greatly improved; the contingent of reserve cadres was expanded and their management strengthened; and the exchange of cadres increased, the channels rose in number, and the scope expanded. We summarized a number of typical examples in building the grass-roots party organizations and consolidated a batch of backward party branches; selected "10 outstanding" communist party members and cultivated an image of communist party members of the new period; and began to conduct organizational reform in party and government organs, and achieved progress in experimenting with the public service system. The ideas for the municipal organizational work in 1994 are to take Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics and the party's basic line as the guidance, further emancipate the mind, act in connection with the party's central task, strengthen and improve party building, strive to make new breakthroughs in strengthening the building of leading bodies and grass-roots party organizations and expanding strength in reform of the cadre personnel system, and provide an organizational guarantee for acccelerating the development of the socialist market economy, fulfilling all reform and construction tasks of this year, and realizing the objective of becoming well-off three years ahead of schedule. [passage omitted] Wang Tong, Ouyang Wenan, Yang Chaoshi, and Qiang Wei, member of the standing committee of the municipal party committee, and Li Yongan, secretary of the municipal Discipline Inspection Commission, attended the conference.
