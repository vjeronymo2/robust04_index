940702 FT 02 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Fitch A recovery in its US business helped Fitch, the design services group, cut losses from Pounds 3.98m to Pounds 3.18m pre-tax in the 1993 year despite continuing problems in the UK. At the operating level there was a Pounds 294,000 loss, against Pounds 3.08m after Pounds 2.43m exceptionals. There was an operating profit of Pounds 790,000 in the US but a Pounds 1.08m loss in the UK. The pre-tax figure was after a provision of Pounds 2.41m against an expected loss on the sale of its central London property. Turnover fell 25 per cent to Pounds 12m (Pounds 15.9m) with a 9.6 per cent decrease on continuing operations. Losses per share came out at 9.6p (31.9p).
