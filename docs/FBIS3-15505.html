Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Grigoriy Arslanov] [Text] Beijing, 5 Mar -- Viktor Gerashchenko, the chairman of Russia's Central Bank, arrived in Beijing today at the invitation of the People's Bank of China. The ITAR-TASS correspondent learned from informed sources that during Viktor Gerashchenko's talks with the heads of the People's Bank and the Bank of China, and also with the Chinese State Administration for Exchange Control, there will be discussion of issues relating to further improving currency, finance, and accounting relations between the Russian Federation and the PRC. During his sojourn in Beijing, and during a trip to Shanghai and the Shenzhen special economic zone, the Russian Central Bank chairman will be briefed on the progress of reforms in the money and credit sphere in China.
