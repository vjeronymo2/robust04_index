940610 FT 10 JUN 94 / UK Company News in Brief: David Lloyd Leisure DAVID LLOYD Leisure has acquired a 12-acre site at Long Ashton, Bristol, with planning permission for a tennis and fess club. Facilities will include 10 indoor and six outdoor courts and a gymnasium, at a cost of about Pounds 6m. Under planning conditions a park-and-ride facility will also be built at a cost of Pounds 1.5m.
