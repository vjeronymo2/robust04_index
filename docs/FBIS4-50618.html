BFN [By GUANGMING RIBAO reporter Zhang Yufu (1728 7183 4395) and XINHUA reporter Zhu Dongju (2612 0392 5468)] [Excerpts] Beijing, 6 May (XINHUA) -- The 1994 national congress of China Vocational Education Service was held in Beijing today. On behalf of the CPC Central Committee and the State Council, Li Lanqing, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and vice premier of the State Council, attended the congress to offer congratulations and deliver a speech. He said: Since its inception, China Vocational Education Service has devoted itself to researching and promoting vocational education, has set up various vocational schools, and has disseminated new ideas aimed at linking education with labor, such as those promoting "respect for labor," "manual skills," and the "application of both physical and mental power." This has helped nurture large numbers of specialized personnel who are knowledgeable and technically skilled. [passage omitted] Li Lanqing noted: Educational development will determine whether we can build a socialist market economic system, and whether we can realize modernization; so will the improvement of national quality and the training of various specialized personnel. Our country is not an economically developed nation but it has to undertake the world's most extensive educational service; this is the reality of our education situation. By the end of this century, therefore, we should vigorously expand vocational education and train large numbers of people with practical skills needed for developing the socialist market economy, while basically universalizing the nine-year compulsory educational system, and basically eradicating illiteracy among youth. This provides the means by which we can achieve greater, faster, better, and more economical results in solving our country's educational problems; it can be described as a fundamental solution. The development of vocational education is a basic task of strategic importance for improving workers ideologically, morally, scientifically, and culturally, and for realizing socialist modernization; it is also of special importance to further consolidating the socialist system lead by the working class and based on the worker-peasant alliance. [passage omitted] Attending today's opening ceremonies were Ni Zhifu, vice chairman of the National People's Congress Standing Committee; Ngapoi Ngawang Jigme and Wan Guoquan, vice chairmen of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference; and responsible persons from various democratic parties, the All-China Federation of Industry and Commerce, and relevant departments.
