931211 FT 11 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Quiligotti Exceptional costs of Pounds 1.13m left Quiligotti, the USM-traded tiles and flooring group, Pounds 1.27m in the red for the six months to September 30. There were losses of Pounds 228,000 last time after an exceptional charge of Pounds 80,000. Turnover fell to Pounds 7.01m (Pounds 8.45m) and there was a trading loss of Pounds 138,000 (Pounds 148,000). The exceptional charge comprised further provisions against old contract debtors and problem contracts, reorganisation costs, and management changes relating to the placing and open offer and the acquisition of Chelsea Group. The company said its strategy was to concentrate on the terrazzo floor tiles business, and accordingly it was ending its activities in the ceramics area. Losses per share deepened from 0.33p to 1.74p.
