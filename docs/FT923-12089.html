920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / Bahamas poll next month By Reuter in Nassau THE Bahamian parliament was dissolved yesterday and a general election set for August 19, when Sir Lynden Pindling, prime minister, will seek his seventh consecutive term, government officials said, Reuter reports from Nassau. Mr Hubert Ingraham, opposition leader, criticised the ruling party for delays in calling the election. Sir Lynden predicted his Progressive Liberal party will win 38 of the 49 seats in the legislative House of Assembly.
