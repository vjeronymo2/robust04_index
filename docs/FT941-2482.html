940321 FT 21 MAR 94 / Lack of success for staff scheme Less than half of the employees who participated in the Investors in People scheme reported they had become more productive, a survey by the Institute of Manpower Studies found. The institute found that 48 per cent of employees who had taken part in the scheme had become more satisfied with their job but less than 40 per cent said they had become more productive. Two thirds of employers said the scheme had contributed to a change in business performance.
