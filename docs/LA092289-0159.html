September 22, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

SCR SNAGS $50,000 NEW PLAY GRANT

South Coast Repertory is one of seven theaters -- and one of only two outside of New York -- to snag a chunk of $300,000 being provided for the production of six new plays as part of an AT&T "New Plays for the '90s" project.

The Costa Mesa theater will receive $50,000 for its staging of Howard Korder's "Search and Destroy," scheduled to open Jan. 12. The winning entries were selected from proposals submitted by 35 theaters.
