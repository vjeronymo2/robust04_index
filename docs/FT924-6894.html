921119 FT 19 NOV 92 / Semi-finished stocks reduced MANUFACTURERS' stocks of semi-finished products fell in the three months to the end of September by a provisional Pounds 154m at 1985 prices, after falls of Pounds 1m in the second quarter and Pounds 550m in the first quarter, the Central Statistical Office said yesterday. Stocks of finished products in factories increased by Pounds 306m. Among wholesalers, stocks were down by Pounds 207m, compared with a fall of Pounds 237m in the previous quarter. In the retailing sector, stocks rose by Pounds 67m, after a decrease of Pounds 197m in the previous quarter.
