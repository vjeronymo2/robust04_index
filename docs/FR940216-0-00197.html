(a) Sulfur. Sulfur content shall be determined using American Society for Testing and Materials (ASTM) standard method D&hyph;2622&hyph;92, entitled ``Standard Test Method for Sulfur in Petroleum Products by X-Ray Spectrometry.'' (b) Olefins. Olefin content shall be determined using ASTM standard method D&hyph;1319&hyph;93, entitled ``Standard Test Method for Hydrocarbon Types in Liquid Petroleum Products by Fluorescent Indicator Adsorption.''
