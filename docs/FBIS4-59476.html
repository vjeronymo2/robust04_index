BFN [Text] Rafah, 15 May (MENA) -- A new 314-strong Palestinian police force commanded by Colonel 'Abd-al-Majid Fadl Salih crossed at the Rafah border point on the way to Gaza today. Col. Salih said the unit comes from the 'Ayn Jalut forces in Egypt together with some personnel from the forces stationed in Libya and Sudan. Salih said the deployment of the Palestinian police forces continues in Gaza and Jericho. He said Badr forces have been deployed in Jericho and Rafah and that the northern, central, and southern sectors of the Gaza Strip have also been covered through deployment in Khan Yunus, Dayr al-Balah, Jabaliyh, and al- Shaja'iyah. It remains for the Palestinian force to deploy in Gaza City and the Eretz checkpoint on the border between Palestine and Israel, he said. He said Palestinian police forces are now working to maintain security and order and protect vital installations, such as electricity and water stations, custom departments, ports, and airports. Col. Salih praised the facilities provided by Egypt and the efforts made by the Egyptian Administration at the Customs Office, Passport Department, and border security to organize the entry of Palestinian forces.
