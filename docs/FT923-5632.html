920829 FT 29 AUG 92 / Letter: A Lament on the Wreck of the Britannia Sir, Mr P B Swain's discovery of 'Lamont' among the 'new lows' listed in your newspaper (Letters, August 26) reminds me of Longfellow's 'The Wreck of the Hesperus'. The last two lines of the poem read: Christ save us all from a death like this On the reef of Norman's Woe] Michael Foreman, 6 The Glade, Sevenoaks, Kent TN13 3HD
