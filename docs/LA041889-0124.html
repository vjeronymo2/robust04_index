April 18, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: SCIENCE/TECHNOLOGY

Active Memory Technology Inc., an Irvine computer company, has promoted Charles A. von Urff to president and chief operating officer.

Von Urff, formerly executive vice president, will be responsible for day-to-day management of the company's U.S. operations.

Active Memory is a manufacturer of super-fast computers used for scientific, research and military applications.
