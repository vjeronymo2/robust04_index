930208 FT 08 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Vaux hotels hit by the recession Vaux Group's Swallow hotels continued to suffer in the recession and trading profits were falling short of the interest costs on some recent investments, the annual meeting was told. Hotel occupancies in the October-January period were 5 per cent higher than a year ago but achieved room rates were 5 per cent down. However, the group's breweries were performing well.
