930424 FT 24 APR 93 / International Company News: China Light improves 11% CHINA LIGHT and Power, the Hong Kong utility controlled by the Kadoorie family, yesterday announced net profits of HKDollars 1.73bn (USDollars 225m) for the six months to March 1993, an 11 per cent increase, writes Simon Davies in Hong Kong. Domestic sales of electricity increased by 3.1 per cent, but sales to China increased 53 per cent, which helped to lift turnover by 14 per cent to HKDollars 6.36bn.
