Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Irina Vladykina report from Yekaterinburg: "Resignation of Eduard Rossel's Cabinet"] [Text] The Cabinet of the Sverdlovsk Oblast government, which was formed by Eduard Rossel and worked for a few years under him, has resigned in full strength. It was pointed out at a press conference that the decision was not related to the executive branch representatives' unwillingness to cooperate with the new chief of administration, nor was it a protest against his appointment. The government did so proceeding from generally applied standards: Once a chief is gone, his cabinet goes, too. The new governor has asked all members of the oblast administration to stay on until February, when a new Sverdlovsk Oblast government will be formed.
