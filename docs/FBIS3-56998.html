Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Foreign Affairs Ministry of Ukraine has made public a joint communique on the results of the official visit to Ukraine by President of the Republic of Kazakhstan Nursultan Nazarbayev. It says: During the negotiations between the presidents of the two friendly states, there was a profound and constructive exchange of opinions on a wide range of issues of bilateral cooperation and international issues of mutual interest. The sides stated the importance of the Kazakh-Ukrainian summit within the context of the further consolidation of the potential of bilateral cooperation and interaction on the international scene. The presidents of Kazakhstan and Ukraine expressed mutual understanding of the necessity of effective efforts aimed at consolidating the mechanism of the implementation of the agreements in various spheres of bilateral relations. The leaders of both states pointed out the urgency of diversifying the forms and trends of the trade and economic relations. In particular, issues were considered and a relevant agreement was signed. Leonid Kravchuk and Nursultan Nazarbayev paid special attention to an in-depth examination of the tendencies in the present-day international situation and within the Commonwealth of Independent States. On the results of an exchange of opinions on these issues the presidents signed a Declaration Contemporary World: Challenges and Dangers. While discussing the nuclear disarmament issues the president of Kazakhstan welcomed the signing of the trilateral statement by the presidents of Ukraine, Russia, and the United States of America on 14 January this year. A Treaty on Friendship and Cooperation, a Consular Treaty, a protocol on consultations between the foreign ministries and an number of other important agreements were signed in the course of the Kazakh-Ukrainian negotiations. The sides expressed their certainty that the signed documents would become a reliable contractual and legal basis of a mutually advantageous and equal cooperation between Kazakhstan and Ukraine. The leaders of both countries commissioned experts to continue work with the aim of signing a treaty on legal relations and legal support in civil and criminal cases and also agreements which would regulate the issue on the rights of national minorities and the problems of deported peoples. President Nursultan Nazarbayev invited President Leonid Kravchuk to pay an official visit to Kazakhstan. The invitation was accepted with gratitude.
