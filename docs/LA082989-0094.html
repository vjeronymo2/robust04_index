August 29, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Carolco Pictures Inc. has sold a package of feature films to Sky Television, a satellite channel in the United Kingdom owned by Rupert Murdoch's News Corp. The first two Carolco titles to premiere on the Sky Movie Channel will be "Rambo: First Blood II," starring Sylvester Stallone, and "Angel Heart," directed by Alan Parker and starring Mickey Rourke and Robert De Niro. ALEENE MacMINN
