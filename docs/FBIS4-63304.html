BFN [BELINFORM-TASS report: "Will the Ruble Come to Belorussia?"] [Text] Whether the Treaty on Monetary Union with Russia goes ahead or not now depends on the results of Belorussia's [Belarus'] presidential election, Vyacheslav Kebich, chairman of the Republic's Council of Ministers, told a public meeting in Grodno Oblast. He also stressed that "this is not so much monetary union as economic union, and it is being signed not for two or three months but for centuries to come. I am confident that this union will go ahead, just as I am confident that Ukraine will also join it." According to the prime minister, in the near future Tajikistan will also state that it is joining the economic union.
