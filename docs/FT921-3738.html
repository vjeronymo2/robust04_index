920312 FT 12 MAR 92 / International Capital Markets: Wella launches CP programme WELLA, the German personal care products manufacturer, has established a DM300m (Dollars 180.7m) commercial paper programme. It has been arranged by Deutsche Bank, with Dresdner Bank, Westdeutsche Landesbank Girozentrale and Deutsche Bank as placing agents, AP-DJ reports. Commercial paper programmes run for between seven days and two years, less one day.
