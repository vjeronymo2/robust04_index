940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / Oil director's trial to start this week The trial of Mr Mohammed Baqi, a director of Attock Oil International charged with fraud over his alleged involvement with the collapsed Bank of Credit and Commerce International, is set to start later this week. Mr Baqi faces 16 charges concerning the alleged furnishing of false information about the finances of BCCI. The Serious Fraud Office, which has brought the prosecution, alleges he conspired with members of the bank's management to furnish documents which falsely indicated that the bank was owed a total of Dollars 123m (Pounds 83m) by Attock Oil. Mr Baqi denies all the charges.
