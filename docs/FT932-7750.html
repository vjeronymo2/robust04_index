930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / International Company News in Brief: Toppan Printing Toppan Printing, Japan's second-largest printing company, yesterday reported a 20 per cent fall in unconsolidated pre-tax profits for the year to March 31 from the previous fiscal year, AP-DJ reports from Tokyo. Pre-tax profits were Y51.6bn (Dollars 464.9m) against Y64.6bn, while net profits fell to Y27.3bn from Y34bn. Sales were down just under 6 per cent to Y842.6bn. The dividend is unchanged at Y10.
