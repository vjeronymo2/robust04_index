BFN [Text] Cairo, June 1 (MENA)--Sudanese security authorities have launched a large-scale arrest campaign against leaderships and activists of the opposition Ummah Party. The arrests, carried out on May 19, included a number of former ministers and key officials as well as university professors who are affiliated with the Ummah Party, according to a statement faxed to MENA today. Families of the detainees were denied access to information on their whereabouts, the statement added.
