BFN [Text] Iraq has protested to the Arab League that Saudi and Kuwaiti rulers continue to make base facilities available for American, British, and French aircraft violating the Iraqi airspace. A letter from Iraq's permanent delegate to the Arab League pointed out the serious threat the violations carry for the security, sovereignty, and national unity of Iraq. The American, British, and French aircraft mounted 277 sorties over Iraq between 1 and 7 April, having taken off from bases in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait. Attached to the letter was a detailed list of the flights for the attention of the league members.
