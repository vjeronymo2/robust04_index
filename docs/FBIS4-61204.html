BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Vladimir Shumeyko, chairman of the CIS Interparliamentary Assembly, told a POSTFAKTUM agency correspondent of the need to create a contingent of peacekeeping forces from CIS countries. In his opinion, the internal problems of the CIS members should be settled with the aid of this contingent. The Federation Council speaker is certain that it will be noted in the minutes of the Bishkek meeting that CIS parliamentarians are in favor of Commonwealth peacekeeping forces with a defined status and uniform.
