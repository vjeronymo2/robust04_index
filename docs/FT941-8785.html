940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / Arab-Israeli clashes result in more fatalities In a spate of Israeli-Arab clashes yesterday, an Israeli soldier shot dead a Palestinian man at an army checkpoint near Jenin in the West Bank, and an Israeli guard at a building site in the southern port town of Ashdod was stabbed to death, apparently by a Palestinian, writes David Horovitz in Jerusalem. Another Israeli, stabbed by Palestinian militants near Hebron six weeks ago, died of his wounds yesterday. And at least four Palestinians were shot and wounded in clashes with the army in the West Bank town of Halhoul.
