940328 FT 28 MAR 94 / Company News (This Week): BMW - Other Companies By common consent, the domestic German car market is unlikely to make much headway this year, but strong revving noises are expected from BMW tomorrow. Encouraged by the US recovery, the launch of a cut-off version of its 3-Series and an upcoming new-look 7-Series, it is likely to paint a bullish picture of 1994 prospects - not least to dress the window for its oncoming rights issue.
