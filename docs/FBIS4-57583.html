BFN [Text] Army General Raul Castro Ruz, minister of the FAR, met with officers of the Arc Gloria, flagship of the Colombian Navy, who are visiting our country. Raul, who is also second secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba [PCC], spoke of his visit on board the Arc Gloria in 1976 and thanked the officers for stopping in Cuba for the second time. Raul was interested in learning of their activities during their few days in Cuba and saluted the recently appointed military-naval delegate from Colombia. Colombian Ambassador Ricardo Santamaria, and the ship's Captain Eduardo Otero Erazo, as well as other officers and diplomats of the Colombian Navy, were present at the meeting. On the Cuban side, Division General Ulises Rosales del Toro, first vice minister of the Minfar [Ministry of the Revolutionary Armed Forces] and head of the General Staff, as well as other Minfar officials were also present at the meeting.
