BFN [Text] Solidarity's protest action is continuing. At 0900, Solidarity employees at the Czestochowa Steelworks [central-southern Poland] staged a two-hour strike. The Solidarity regional board did not receive notification of strike participation by other factories in Czestochowa Province. In the central-eastern region of Poland, the work-force of the Communications Equipment Plant in Swidnik, the scales factory in Lublin, and the rolling bearings factory in Krasnik joined the strike today.
