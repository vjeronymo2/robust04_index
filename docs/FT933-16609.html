930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Partridge Fine Arts Partridge Fine Arts, the New Bond Street-based dealer in antique furniture and objets d'art, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 987,000 to Pounds 1.05m in the year to April 30. Turnover improved from Pounds 4.88m to Pounds 5.85m. Operating profits edged ahead to Pounds 1.04m (Pounds 1.01m). Earnings per share rose to 3.22p (2.94p) and the interim dividend is 1.2p (1p).
