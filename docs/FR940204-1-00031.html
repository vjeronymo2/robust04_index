Fully discuss any material aspects of financial condition, changes in financial condition, and results of operations of System institutions, on a combined basis, during the last 3 fiscal years or such other time periods specified in the following paragraphs of this section. At a minimum, the discussion shall include the following: (1) Loan portfolio. (i) Describe the loan portfolio of the System by major loan purpose category, indicating the amount and approximate percentage of the total dollar portfolio represented by each major category. (ii) Disclose the amount of loans outstanding that were used to finance the purchases of stock or other equities of System institutions, if any. (iii) Risk exposure. (A) Describe and analyze all high risk assets and other property owned, including an analysis of the nature and extent of significant credit risks and potential credit risk within the loan portfolio and of other information that could adversely affect the loan portfolio and other property owned.
