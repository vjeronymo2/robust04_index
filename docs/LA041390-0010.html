April 13, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

SARAH VAUGHAN

I remember one magical night in Agoura High School Gymnasium about 13 years ago.

It was a school variety show and Sarah Vaughan sang for us. We were an audience of students, parents and teachers -- not a very large audience. Vaughan brought with her that night her own back-up musicians.

It was a night my family and I will never forget. Thank you, Sarah.

LILLIAN SAROIAN

Woodland Hills
