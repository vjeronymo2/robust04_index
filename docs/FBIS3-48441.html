Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 25 (KCNA) -- An agreement on the abolition of the visa system of diplomatic passport carriers was signed here Monday between the governments of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the Islamic Republic of Iran. Present at the signing ceremony on the Korean side were Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Cho Kyu-il and officials concerned, and on the opposite side were the members of the Iranian Foreign Ministry delegation led by its Deputy Minister Manuchehr Mottaki and the charge d'affaires ad interim of the Iranian Embassy here.
