Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By RENMIN RIBAO reporter Wang Xuexiao (3769 1331 1321) and XINHUA reporter Zhang Shuying (1728 3219 5391)] [Text] Tianjin, 4 Feb (XINHUA) -- A lecture series aimed at publicizing reform among the masses and enlisting their participation in the ongoing profound economic structural reform has kicked off in Tianjin; it will be broadcast throughout the nation starting from 6 February. Sponsored by the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee and the General Office of the State Council, the lectures are designed to enable leading comrades and people across the country to have a good grasp of the guidelines of the "Decision of the CPC Central Committee on Some Issues Concerning the Establishment of a Socialist Market Economic Structure" and to familiarize themselves and understand the basic contents of the profound reforms in the systems of finance and taxation, banking, investment, foreign trade, enterprises, and so forth. The nine lectures in the televised series are given by leading officials of relevant central government departments. The speakers and contents of their lectures are as follow: Vice Minister of the State Planning Commission Zeng Peiyan on the great significance and main contents of the "decision;" Vice Minister of Finance Xiang Huaicheng on reform of the financial system; Deputy Director Yang Chongchun of the State Administration of Taxation on reform of the system of taxation; Vice Governor Zhou Zhengqing of the People's Bank of China on reform of the banking system; Zhu Xiaohua, vice governor of the People's Bank of China and director of the State Foreign Exchange Administration, on reform of the foreign exchange system; Vice Minister of the State Planning Commission Guo Shuyan on reform of the investment structure; Vice Minister of the State Planning Commission Luo Zhiling on price-forming mechanisms; Vice Minister of Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation Shi Guangsheng on reform of the foreign trade system; and Vice Minister of State Economics and Trade Commission Chen Qingtai on modern enterprise systems. The nine lectures cover the major issues involved in the ongoing efforts to establish a socialist market economic system. Gao Dezhan, secretary of the Tianjin Municipal CPC Committee, presided over the lectures. Tianjin Mayor Zhang Lichang and leading officials at all levels, over 1,000 in all, attended the lectures. Also present at the lectures were leading comrades of the relevant departments and commissions including Xu Weicheng, executive deputy director of the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee; Li Shizhong, deputy secretary general of the State Council; Yang Changji, vice Minister of the State Economics and Trade Commission; Lu Baifu, deputy director of the Development Research Center [jing jin fa zhan zhong xin 4842 3444 4099 1455 0022 1800] of the State Council. Xu Weicheng noted: China is now in a crucial period of establishing a socialist market economic system. It is a task of top priority to disseminate, among the vast ranks of cadres, new ideas, new knowledge, and new approaches in regard to developing socialist market economy. In conscientiously studying Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics, we must organize people to study the basic ideas and theory of socialist market economy. This televised lecture series represents a concrete step in providing education and training to leading cadres at all levels.
