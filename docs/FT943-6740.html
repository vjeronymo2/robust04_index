940827 FT 27 AUG 94 / UN arrears to be paid The US, after years as the United Nations' chief debtor, intends to pay more than Dollars 900m (Pounds 580.6m) towards UN peacekeeping costs by the end of the year, according to Ms Madeleine Albright, US ambassador to the world body, agencies report from New York. President Bill Clinton plans to sign a bill in the next few days authorising Dollars 1.2bn for peacekeeping. The funds will cover arrears until the end of 1994 and allow some revenues for 1995.
