930927 FT 27 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Culver In spite of the 'patchy recovery' in the UK economy, pre-tax profits of Culver Holdings, car dealers, advanced by 50 per cent from Pounds 122,000 to Pounds 183,000 in the half year to June 30. Turnover, boosted by a Pounds 1.3m contribution from new acquisitions, rose from Pounds 10.4m to Pounds 11.1m. Earnings per share improved to 0.3p (0.21p) and the interim dividend is raised to 0.11p (0.1p).
