941228 FT 28 DEC 94 / Government announces fund cuts for student support scheme The government has announced plans to cut funds for its support scheme for postgraduate students by 10 per cent from next year, David Owen writes. About 750 students received funding from the support scheme for the year 1994-95. Mr Tim Boswell, education minister, said in a Commons written answer that the maximum level of fees payable through the scheme would be Pounds 2,430 for 1995-96. He said the decision had been made 'in the light of other demands and priorities'.
