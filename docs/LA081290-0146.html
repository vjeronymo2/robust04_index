August 12, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CURRENTS: GALLERIES;

BEADS FROM ABROAD

WHEN TZERL SELTZER opened her ethnic-bead gallery and boutique specializing in African, Middle Eastern and Asian articles, she filled it with ostrich-eggshell and snake-vertebrae beads, amber, Austrian crystal and African trading beads. Soon customers were clamoring to learn how to create their own such objets d'art, and a class was born.

Bead Werk, 10895 Pico Blvd., West Los Angeles; (213) 470-1515.JOAN DRAPER
