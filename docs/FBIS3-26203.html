Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, Feb 4 (AFP)--China's justice ministry confirmed Friday that three dissidents jailed after the crushing of the 1989 pro-democracy movement had been released. A ministry spokesman confirmed that Xiao Bin, a worker, Liao Yiwu, a leading poet from Sichuan province, and Ding Junze, a former assistant professor of philosophy at Shanxi University had been released. News of the releases was first announced by John Kamm, a US businessman and human-rights campaigner.
