BFN [Text] If Israel prevents working meetings by French diplomats at East Jerusalem's Orient House, France will, in reaction, restrict the meetings of its representatives with Israeli representatives in Jerusalem. This was made clear by a senior French Foreign Ministry official in a meeting held at Israel's Embassy in Paris. Our political correspondent 'Imanu'el Rosen reports that Jerusalem is concerned by the increasing Palestinian pressure, exerted through foreign countries, for the achievement of a political status in Jerusalem.
