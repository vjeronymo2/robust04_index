920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / International Company News: Saks appoints new president SAKS FIFTH AVENUE, the upmarket US retailer, yesterday named Ms Rose Marie Bravo its president, AP-DJ reports from New York. The department store chain said Ms Bravo was formerly chairman and chief executive of I Magnin, a unit of RH Macy. She has also been named a director of Saks. Macy's has named Mr Joseph Cicio as chairman and chief executive of I Magnin, replacing Ms Bravo in those posts. Mr Cicio had been RH Macy's senior vice-president, with responsibility for fashion direction and creative resources.
