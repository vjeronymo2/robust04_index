940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Isle of Man Steam rises to Pounds 3.5m Isle of Man Steam Packet Company, the operator of ferry services, lifted pre-tax profits by 6 per cent, from Pounds 3.29m to Pounds 3.48m, in the year to December 31. The result, achieved on improved turnover of Pounds 25.5m (Pounds 24.5m), was after an exceptional Pounds 1.06m write-down in the book value of Lady of Mann. Operating profits advanced from Pounds 3.28m to Pounds 4.43m. Earnings per share emerged at 20.6p against 19.5p and the proposed final dividend of 7.5p (7p) lifts the total by 1p to 11p.
