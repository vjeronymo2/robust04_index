Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (January 3) TURAN--On December 31 in his televised address to the nation, Azerbaijani President Aliyev said that in 1994 all the territories occupied by Armenian Army would be liberated. He stressed that this goal should be achieved in spite of all losses. The President Aliyev said that there have been positive changes in Azeri Army recently. For the last month in the course of firings Armenian Army could not break the Azeri defense. According to the president, these are the consequences of the measures taken by the government in the field of military building. Azerbaijan comes out for peaceful means of settlement of the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict and it will continue to adhere to this policy in the nearest future, the President Aliyev said.
