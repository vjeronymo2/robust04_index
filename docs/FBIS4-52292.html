BFN [Text] UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali has praised the Indonesian Government for giving UN officials, human rights activists, officials of humanitarian organizations, and journalists access to East Timor. His views were disclosed by his spokesperson Theresa Christophe during the fourth round of Indonesian-Portuguese talks in Geneva, Switzerland. Speaking at a news conference after the talks, Foreign Minister Ali Alatas said Indonesia and Portugal have made much progress in their efforts to create mutual trust. He said Indonesia has done many things to accelerate the settlement of the East Timor issue; for example, East Timorese have been allowed to see relatives in Portugal and Portuguese activists have been permitted to visit the former colony.
