Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Two Azerbaijani warplanes, an Su-24 and an Su-25, entered Vardenisskiy Rayon at 1500 today. The first started to carry out a reconnaissance flight over Sodk [formerly Zod] and (Kurts). Overflying (Kurts), the second plane swerved toward the rayon center but had no time to bomb the city. It broke into pieces and fell in Vardenisskiy Rayon after being hit by a missile fired by the Armenian Republic's Air Defense Forces. The other plane quickly flew off. The Armenian Defense Ministry Press Service reported that the two pilots of the downed plane were captured. While the neighboring republic expresses readiness to stop the military operations during the Olympic games, it also sends military airplanes to bomb our Republic's peaceful border settlements. The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic's [NKR] Air Defense Forces shot down another Azerbaijani Air Force plane today over the north of the Karabakh war front. Details of the case are not yet known. According to reports received from the NKR Defense Army headquarters at 1700, the Karabakh units repulsed the attack launched by the Azerbaijani side against Agburun in Martuninskiy Rayon this morning. There was no report of casualty figures.
