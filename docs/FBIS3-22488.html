Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Summary] The southwest China's first biological-safety laboratory for AIDS research has been established in Kunming by the Logistics Department of Chengdu Military Region's Institute of Military Medical Sciences. The laboratory passed the assessment recently and will be put in operation soon. Since 1988, the laboratory has been monitoring AIDS situation in the area, researching HIV and HIV pathogenesis, and developing pharmaceutical products for AIDS treatment. A monkey model for AIDS research has also been established.
