940115 FT 15 JAN 94 / Yeo wins backing from local party Mr Tim Yeo, who resigned as environment minister after revelations that he had fathered a child in an extra-marital affair, last night received 'overwhelming' backing at an extraordinary meeting of his constituency association. Mrs Patricia FitzPatrick, chairman of the South Suffolk Conservative Association, speaking after the 1 1/2 -hour meeting, said an overwhelming majority had felt Mr Yeo had been a conscientious and effective MP. She added that there was a strong wish to continue working with him. Mr Yeo said he was enormously grateful for what he took to be a clear vote of confidence.
