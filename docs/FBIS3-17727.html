Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Despite low participation -- about 40 percent of voters took part in the elections -- preliminary results indicate the elections are valid. The Central Electoral Commission also has information that in two regions, Samara and Magadan oblasts, the necessary 25 percent threshold has not been reached. In other regions, local bodies of legislative power have already been formed to two-thirds or even completely, which enables them to start working immediately. Representatives of the president's apparatus, have analyzed the results of the elections and are saying the level of conservatism in the elected representative bodies of power will be considerable. According to the same source, rural voters, who gave their votes to socialist candidates, were the most active.
