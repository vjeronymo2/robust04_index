Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow March 9 TASS -- Former U.S. President Richard Nixon will continue his unofficial visit to Moscow, Dimitri Simes, political scientist accompanying the ex-president, told reporters on Wednesday [9 February]. Thus he rejected media speculations that Nixon decided to cut short his visit after President Boris Yeltsin and some other top officials refused to meet him. The refusal came after Nixon's extensive contacts with opposition leaders including former Vice President Aleksandr Rutskoy amnestied last week.
