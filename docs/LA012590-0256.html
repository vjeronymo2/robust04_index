January 25, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

13-1 LONG SHOT WINS GULFSTREAM HANDICAP

Sez Fourty, a 13-1 shot, outpaced the heavily favored Classic Value Wednesday to win the $45,940 First Lady Breeders' Cup Handicap for fillies and mares Wednesday at Gulfstream Park.

Ridden by Mike Gonzalez, Sez Fourty was timed in 1:11 15 going six furlongs, and paid $28.40, $7.20 and $6.20. Classic Value paid $3.20 and $3. Fit For A Queen paid $5.80.
