920716 FT 16 JUL 92 / UK Company News: S&P Return of Assets Trust Net asset value per share of Save & Prosper Return of Assets Investment Trust stood at 278.5p at May 31 compared with 273p a year earlier. After treating warrants as exercised the figure amounted to 250.2p against 244.1p. Pre-tax profits for the year amounted to Pounds 4.44m (Pounds 4.39m) and earnings per share 10.65p (11.36p).
