BFN [Presidential Decree No. 24, issued by the Presidency in Sanaa on 18 May] [Text] The chairman of the Presidential Council, Taking cognizance of the Republic of Yemen Constitution and law No. 2 of 1991 on the diplomatic and consular corps, and upon the recommendation of the foreign minister and after the approval of the Council of Ministers and the Presidential Council, decrees the following:

Article I
Brother Ahmad Muhammad Luqman, will be appointed ambassador and a permanent representative of the Republic of Yemen at the Arab League.
Article II
This decree will be valid as of date of issue and will be published in the Official Gazette. Issued at the Presidency in Sanaa on 7 Dhu al-Hijjah, 1414 of the hegira, corresponding to 18 May 1994. [Signed] 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar, the acting prime minister, Muhammad Salim Basindwah, the foreign minister
