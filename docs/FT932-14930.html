930416 FT 16 APR 93 / Better services demanded by Child Poverty Action Group Better services for claimants and efforts to increase the take-up of benefits are demanded today by the Child Poverty Action Group. It says Pounds 1.5bn a year in means-tested benefits is unclaimed, and that claimants had experienced 'appalling delays' in getting new disability benefits in spite of undertakings in the Benefits Agency's customer charter.
