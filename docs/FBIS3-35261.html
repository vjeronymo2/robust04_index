Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Four radical members of the PLO Executive Committee have pledged to prevent the implementation of the Palestinian-Israeli Cairo agreement, saying it is a catastrophe for the Palestinians and involves very serious concessions. The four Palestinian officials are: 'Abd-al-Rahim Malluh, member of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine; Taysir Khalid, member of the Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine; Mahmud Isma'il, member of the Arab Liberation Front; and 'Abdallah Hurani, an independent Executive Committee member.
