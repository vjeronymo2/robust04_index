May 7, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BATTLING THE MEDFLY

The state Department of Food and Agriculture will conduct aerial spraying of malathion beginning at 9 p.m., weather permitting.

Area: 27 square miles in two separate areas encompassing parts of parts of Arleta, Burbank, Granada Hills, North Hollywood, Panorma City, Sepulveda, Sun Valley, Van Nuys, Hancock Park and parts of Koreatown, Country Club Park and Mid-City.

Precautions: Stay indoors; keep animals indoors; wash animal dishes and toys left outside; cover cars; keep doors and windows closed.

Information: Toll-free numbers for the Agricultural Commissioner: (800) 356-2894; (800) 225-1346.
