April 28, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Five Wyoming football players charged with taking indecent, immoral or immodest liberties with a minor, were released from the Albany County Jail in Laramie after posting $10,000 bonds.

They were charged in connection with an incident involving an 18-year-old female student that allegedly occurred April 7 in a university dormitory. Those under 19 are considered minors under Wyoming law.
