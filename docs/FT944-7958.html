941122 FT 22 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Ferraris up at Pounds 1.1m Ferraris Group, which designs and produces instruments for the medical and precision engineering industries, saw a 56 per cent rise in pre-tax profits for the year ended August 31, up from Pounds 681,000 to Pounds 1.06m. Acquisitions contributed Pounds 87,500 to an operating profit ahead at Pounds 1.07m (Pounds 907,000) and the result also included a writeback of Pounds 125,000. Turnover was Pounds 11.3m (Pounds 10.6m), of which Pounds 692,000 came from acquisitions. Earnings per share came out ahead at 7p (6.1p). The recommended final dividend of 1.4p (1.25p) makes a total of 2.25p (2p) for the year.
