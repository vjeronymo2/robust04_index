921016 FT 16 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Resignation of Harmony chairman Sir Stanley Grinstead, chair-man of Harmony Leisure, resigned yesterday as the loss-making pubs and restaurants group prepared to release preliminary results showing a 70 per cent increase in pre-tax losses, writes Tim Burt. Harmony said Sir Stanley, 68, was resigning in order to pursue personal commitments. Mr Robert Dawson, a non-executive director, has been appointed acting chairman. News of the resignation followed calls for board changes made by a group of dissident shareholders led by Mr Andrew Martyr, a former hotel executive.
