February 27, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Kansas reclaimed the No. 1 ranking in the Associated Press college basketball poll from Missouri, beaten Sunday by Oklahoma, which rose from No. 11 to No. 5.

The Jayhawks (27-2), who have lost only to Missouri (25-3), are No. 1 for the third time this season. The Tigers slipped to No. 3.

Nevada Las Vegas (24-4) moved from fourth to second, and Connecticut (24-4) moved from sixth to fourth. Duke (23-5) fell into a tie for fifth with Oklahoma after losing to North Carolina State.
