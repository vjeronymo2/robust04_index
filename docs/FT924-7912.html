921113 FT 13 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Two disposals by Cray Electronics Two disposals by Cray Electronics Cray Electronics Holdings has agreed to sell Cray Systems Integration for Pounds 4.5m, together with RFL Electronics for Dollars 10m (Pounds 6.6m). The existing managements of CSI and RFL have formed companies to make the acquisitions. The effect of the disposals will reduce net borrowings of the parent by some Pounds 8.5m.
