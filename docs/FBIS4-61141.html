BFN [Text] At the order by the head of the Georgian state [Eduard Shevardnadze], a service of the state envoy and the representative extraordinary and plenipotentiary [as heard] to the head of the Georgian state has been set up in the region of Shida Kartli [South Ossetia]. The key function of the service is to reinforce the republic's territorial integrity, law and order and the defense capability on the relevant territory, as well as stabilize the political and social situation, carry out restoration works, secure the return of the forcibly removed population, and secure the population's social interests and their human rights.
