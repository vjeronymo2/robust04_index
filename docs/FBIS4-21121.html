BFN [Text] As the Ukrainian Border Troops press service has reported today, the last day on the Ukrainian-Moldovan border was quite tense. Soldiers of the Kotiv Border Control Squad prevented an attempted contraband of 10 tonnes of diesel fuel to Moldova, and at [words indistinct] Border Troops soldiers detained a truck with 20 tonnes of coal which Moldovan citizens attempted to transport to Moldova. Attempts to smuggle firearms and drugs through our borders also continue. For example, a Nagan gun and ammunition for it were found with a Kazakhstani citizen, and in Sumy Oblast, while searching the Kiev-Moscow train, Border Troops soldiers uncovered almost 3 kg of heroin. All the cases are currently under investigation.
