Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Report by Crina Nedelcu: "Ioan Gavra Admitted That the New Justice Minister Is a Sympathizer of the PUNR"] [Text] During a National Romanian Unity Party [PUNR] news conference held yesterday, [Deputy Party Chairman] Ioan Gavra admitted that on 1 March the PUNR leaders examined the personalities of the experts proposed [by the government party] to take over the leadership of the Justice Ministry and the Transportation Ministry, and they supported the persons who were appointed as ministers on Sunday [6 March]. Ioan Gavra stated that Iosif Gavril Chiuzbaian was proposed as a candidate for the Ministry of Justice by Pavel Suian, brother of Valer Suian, first deputy chairman of the party [PUNR]. Horia Pop [Brasov PUNR deputy] added: "It cannot be excluded that he will join the PUNR in the future." Talking about the second stage of the government reshuffle, Gavra asserted that the new ministers will also be technocrats: "In the end, the government will consists of more experts than Social Democracy Party of Romania members, because we need experts to pull the country out of the current crisis."
