BFN [Text] Damascus, 27 Apr (SANA)--Comrade Dr. Sulayman Qaddah, assistant regional secretary of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party, received today Aleksandr Dzasokhov, deputy chairman of the parliamentary group of the Russian Federation. The comrade assistant regional secretary of the Ba'th Party and Dzasokhov reviewed the friendly relations between the two countries and ways of promoting them for the benefit of the two friendly countries and peoples.
