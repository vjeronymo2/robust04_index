March 29, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SUITE: The Newport Beach Four Seasons Hotel picked up the tab for Nancy Reagan's one-night stay in the Presidential Suite before her visit here Wednesday (B2). . . . The last First Family member to stay there? President-elect George Bush in November, 1988. For the rest of us, the suite goes for $1,700 a night. . . . Nancy got a complimentary arrangement of white tuber roses from the management but no mini bar or mint on the pillow. And check out time is "always flexible," says the hotel's Cathy Weiss. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
