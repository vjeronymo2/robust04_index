BFN [Text] Tripoli, al Maa [May] 2, JAMAHIRIYAH NEWS AGENCY -- The leader of the revolution yesterday received Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid, the secretary general of the League of Arab States, who is currently visiting the Great Jamahiriyah. The meeting discussed the role of the League of Arab States in meeting the dangerous challenges threatening the Arab nation so as to safeguard national sovereignty. Also new developments at Arab and international levels were discussed. Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid was present as the leader of the great al fateh revolution was welcomed by the popular masses of Tarhunah.
