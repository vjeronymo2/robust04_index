BFN [Text] Yesterday, Peruvian Air Force [FAP] Commander General Enrique Astete Baca installed Lieutenant General Cesar Urday Begazo as the new FAP General Staff Chief. The ceremony took place at the Las Palmas Air Base parade square. Present at the ceremony were members of the Air Force top command, officers, subordinates, technical personnel, and noncommissioned officers. Moreover, at the commander general's office Lt. Gen. Fernando Moy Romarioni was installed as inspector general of the FAP, replacing Lt. Gen. Ernesto Burga.
