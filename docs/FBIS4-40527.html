BFN [Text] Tallinn, June 16, BNS -- Estonian Ambassador to Iceland Arvo Jurgen Alas and Iceland's Foreign Minister Jon Baldvin Hannibalsson on Thursday signed in Reykjavik an Estonian-Icelandic agreement on avoiding double taxation, the Foreign Ministry press service told BNS. After the signing Alas and Hannibalsson discussed Estonian- Russian relations, including the problems related to the withdrawal of Russian troops from Estonia, and prospects for developing economic cooperation between the two countries. The meeting also touched on Iceland's bid to join the Council of Baltic Sea States, the Foreign Ministry press service informs.
