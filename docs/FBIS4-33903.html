BFN [From the "Doing Radio" newscast] [Text] Over 10,000 efficiency assemblies have been held in the country, it was stated by CTC [Cuban Workers Federation] Secretary General Pedro Ross in a meeting with a delegation of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions that is currently visiting Cuba. Ross, who is also a member of the Communist Party of Cuba [PCC] politburo, explained to the labor union leaders of the friendly nation that the assembly process is a result of the Cuban workers' decision that labor unions become directly involved in the solution of problems at their enterprises in particular and the country in general. Ross' statement was given at the Medical Biophysics Research Center, a leading institution in Santiago de Cuba's scientific sector that is in charge of manufacturing equipment for medical diagnoses and research on diseases through the use of nuclear magnetic resonance. Xiao Zhenbang, a member of the PRC delegation, congratulated the workers for their achievments, and he urged them to continue their vital research for the development of the island's pharmaceutical and medical industry.
