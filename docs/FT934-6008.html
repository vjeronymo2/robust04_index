931126 FT 26 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Casket buys German cycle maker Casket is expanding its European presence with the acquisition of Heidemann Fahrrad, a German maker of bicycles. To fund the acquisition and provide additional working capital for Heidemann and the group, Casket is raising about Pounds 4.5m net. This will be by an issue of up to 13.89m new ordinary shares, conditionally placed with institutional investors at 36p per share. In addition, Casket shareholders can apply for an offer of new ordinary shares at the placing price, on the basis of one new share for every 5.761075 held.
