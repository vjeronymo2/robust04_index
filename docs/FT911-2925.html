910426 FT 26 APR 91 / International Company News: BCE FIRST-quarter earnings at BCE, the Canadian telecommunications holding company, were CDollars 293m (USDollars 252.5m), or 88 cents a share, up 11 per cent from CDollars 265m, or 82 cents, a year earlier. Revenues were CDollars 4.7bn, against CDollars 4.4bn, writes Robert Gibbens.
