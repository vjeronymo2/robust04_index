BFN [Text] This evening, Mr. Yusuf Bin-'Alawi Bin-'Abdallah, Oman's minister of state for foreign affairs, arrived in Amman on a two-day visit to Jordan. He will convey a message from His Majesty Sultan Qabus to His Majesty King Husayn. He will also have talks with Jordanian officials dealing with bilateral ties and Arab and world issues. On hand to welcome him were Talal Sat'an al-Hasan, minister of state for foreign affairs; the secretary general of the Foreign Ministry; and the Omani ambassador in Amman.
