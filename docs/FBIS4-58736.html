BFN [Text] Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati will head for Bosnia-Herzegovina on May 7 to meet and confer with the Bosnian officials and open the embassy of the Islamic Republic of Iran in Sarajevo. Latest developments as well as mutual relations will be discussed in the trip. President of the Iranian Red Crescent Society and a number of Islamic Consultative Assembly are accompanying Dr. Velayati on his trip to Bosnia.
