940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / Explosion rips Srinagar camp An explosion ripped through an Indian army camp in Kashmir state yesterday, killing 13 soldiers and injuring seven, Reuter reports from Srinagar. Domestic news agencies quoted an army spokesman as saying the blast at Badamibagh base, in Srinagar, occurred while officers were inspecting weapons captured from Moslem separatists. Two pro-Pakistan militant groups, the Jamiat-ul-Mujahideen and the Ikhwan-ul-Muslimeen, claimed responsibility.
