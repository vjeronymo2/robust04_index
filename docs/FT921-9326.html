920212 FT 12 FEB 92 / International Company News: Oryx plans Dollars 500m offering ORYX ENERGY, the independent oil and gas company, said it plans to raise Dollars 500m in debt securities in order to pay off existing debt, writes Alan Friedman. In a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission the company said it would also use some of the proceeds for general corporate purposes. The underwriters of the planned issue were not named. The company's share price closed at Dollars 24 3/8 yesterday, up Dollars 3/8.
