Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Islamabad, Jan. 15, IRNA -- Iran's special envoy for Afghan affairs, Mohammad Ibrahim Taherian, and the United Nations Secretary General's envoy for Afghanistan Mosorees here Saturday emphasized their mutual feelings on the need for returning peace to Afghanistan. The UN envoy lauded Iran's efforts, and added that the United Nations is willing and ready to do whatsoever that is necessary for returning peace to Afghanistan. The past two weeks have seen the bloodiest ever fighting between forces of Afghan interim President Burhanoddin Rabbani and Uzbek militia chief 'Abdol Rashid Dostam.
