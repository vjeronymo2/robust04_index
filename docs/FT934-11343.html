931101 FT 01 NOV 93 / Construction Contracts: UK group rebuild in Kuwait 6m contract to rebuild an ammunitions facility in Kuwait has been won by the KUWAITI ANGLO DEFENCE COMPANY (KADCOL), a Taylor Woodrow / Wimpey joint operation. KADCOL will repair and refurbish air-conditioned storage facilities for munitions, maintenance and testing facilities. It will also build a new administration block, and install a telemetry and security system.
