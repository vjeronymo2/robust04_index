Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] "We expect the Israeli Government to adopt all necessary measures to prevent further bloodshed." This was stated by the Foreign Ministry, which added that "increased vigilance and a heightened sense of responsibility" are necessary following the Hebron massacre. A statement from the Foreign Ministry stresses that "we must make sure extremist fanaticism does not jeopardize the progress made toward peace." The statement further says that acts of "extremists on all sides" against normally defenseless citizens are aimed at dashing the hopes for peace.
