920501 FT 01 MAY 92 / International Company News: Snecma expands US interests SNECMA, the French state-owned aircraft engine company, is expanding its US interests by taking a majority stake in Speco, which is based in Springfield, Ohio and is one of the world's oldest aircraft-parts makers, writes Alice Rawsthorn in Paris. The French company is expected to bring in at least one other investor as a partner.
