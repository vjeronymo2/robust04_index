November 19, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: WOUNDED MAN FOUND IN CAR DIES AT HOSPITAL

A 27-year-old man died of multiple gunshot wounds in the chest early Saturday shortly after he was found wounded in the front seat of a car parked in Sun Valley, Los Angeles police said.

The victim, whose identity was withheld until relatives could be notified, was found slouched in the front seat of a 1982 Chevrolet Camaro about 4:40 a.m. at the intersection of Sherman Way and Lankershim Boulevard, Detective Mike Coffey said.

Officers found the man after neighbors reported hearing gunshots.
