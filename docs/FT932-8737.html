930518 FT 18 MAY 93 / Afghan jets bomb guerrillas Afghan government jets streaked over Kabul yesterday to bomb the Hezb-i-Islami party headquarters of prime minister-designate Gulbuddin Hekmatyar as his rockets pounded the city, Reuter reports from Kabul. The Hezb rocket barrage on Sunday night and yesterday killed 23 people and wounded 119, according to official Kabul Radio. Yesterday government troops tried to regain ground lost to the guerrillas in battles that have paralysed the capital for five days and killed hundreds of people.
