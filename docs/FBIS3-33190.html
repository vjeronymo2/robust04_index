Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] In Matanzas, over 8,000 people are self-employed under Decree Law No. 141; 61 percent of these are new requests. The occupations most requested to date in Matanzas Province are wagon worker and preparer of light foods -- the latter of which has been temporarily suspended. Independently of the services and needs of the Matanzas market that the province's residents might meet through self-employment, the state budget also benefits, receiving income of over 0.25 million pesos for new registrations and almost 5 million pesos in taxes.
