December 23, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

HERE'S A FUND WAY TO SUPPLEMENT NFL DRAFT

Regarding the issue of NFL teams drafting underclassmen and the potential damage to marginal players, how is this for a solution:

Any NFL team signing a player with eligibility remaining should be required to fund a $100,000 trust fund for the player. Such fund could be used only for the athlete's continuing college education.

The effect is to protect the athlete's opportunity to get a college education and also to raise the stakes for teams that draft underclassmen.

JEFF YOUNG, Los Angeles
