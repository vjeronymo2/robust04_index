BFN [Text] Moscow June 20 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin signed several decrees on Monday appointing Aleksey Kazakov head of the Department for Work with Territories of the Russian Presidential Administration and Vladimir Varov head of the Federal Labour Inspectorate of the Russian Ministry of Labour, Chief Labour Inspector. Yeltsin also appointed Vladimir Zaitsev first deputy head of the Russian President's Auditing Department and put him in charge of the department, the presidential press service said.
