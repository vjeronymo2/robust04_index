921111 FT 11 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Elan shows strong interim growth Net income at Elan Corporation, the Dublin-based specialised health care group, amounted to IPounds 4.01m (Pounds 4.39m) for the second quarter to September 30, for a first half total of IPounds 8.28m, compared with IPounds 5.5m. Earnings per share amounted to 0.26p, against 1.9p.
