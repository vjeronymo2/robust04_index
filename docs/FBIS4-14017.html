BFN [Text] Cairo, 25 Apr (MENA) -- Terrorist Tal'at Yasin Hammam, leader of the military wing of the so-called Islamic Group [IG], was killed in his hideout in Hada'iq al-Qubbah, Cairo, at dawn today during a shootout with the State Security detectives, who had to shoot back at him. His death is considered as one of the successful strikes at local terrorist leaders and their failing attempts to shake Egyptian security and stability.
