BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] RIA-Novosti agency reports that a Russian diplomat, Igor Makeyev, has disappeared in mysterious circumstances in Thailand. He has allegedly disappeared with a portable computer containing secret information. The Thai police believe Makeyev has either secretly left the country or is no longer alive. The Russian Embassy has so far refrained from commenting, but it has noted that any information in the possession of an employee of the consular department could only be concerned with that department.
