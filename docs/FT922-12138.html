920422 FT 22 APR 92 / Pounds 20m property company set up TWO venture capital groups and a private property company have set up a Pounds 20m company to invest in high-yielding government properties. The company will buy provincial offices, let to the government for a period of at least 15 years, on yields of 11 per cent. Midland Montagu Ventures provided Pounds 14m, 3i provided Pounds 5m and Fiscal Properties, a private investor, provided Pounds 1m.
