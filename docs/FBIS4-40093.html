BFN [Text] The Ostankino television company has reduced broadcasting to Armenia to just a few hours in the evenings. ITAR- TASS reports that this has been caused by demands for additional payments for daytime broadcasts. Tigran Akopyan, chairman of Armenia's State Television and Radio, explained that the restrictions will be in force until Yerevan pays Moscow for the additional expenses of communications enterprises. The technical service of Armenian television estimates the cost of the services at 600 million Russian rubles.
