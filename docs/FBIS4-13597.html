BFN [Text] Cairo, 20 Apr (MENA)--Nine out of a group of 17 Palestinian deportees arrived in Cairo from Algeria today. They were scheduled to return to the occupied territories last week. These deportees will be joined by eight others who will arrive tomorrow ahead of their return to the occupied territories. Deportee Muhammad Abu-Shanab said this group was deported in 1989 for involvement in the intifadah. He said that despite his companions' joy over their return, they will be even happier when all the deportees return and all the detainees in Israeli jails are released.
