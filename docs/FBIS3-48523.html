Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Text] Talks were held at the Vang Sadet residence in Vientiane capital this morning between a Foreign Relations Committee delegation of the Lao People's Democratic Republic [LPDR] and a SRV Foreign Relations Committee delegation. The LPDR delegation was headed by Somsavat Lengsavat, deputy chief of the Foreign Relations Committee, while the Vietnamese delegation was headed by Do Van Tai, deputy chief of the Foreign Relations Committee. The SRV delegation is currently on a friendship visit to the LPDR. In the talks, the two sides informed one another of their achievements in various fields, particularly in fulfilling the agricultural production plan and in carrying out economic work and the export and import trade in 1993. They also informed each other of the gradual developments of various services, for example the development of the marketing mechanism under state management. The two sides have agreed to further strengthen the special fraternal solidarity and to further enhance and consolidate the already normalized relations of friendship between the two countries. They have also unanimously agreed to improve and broaden cooperation with other friendly countries of the world. The Vietnamese delegation arrived in Vientiane at noon on 23 January for a week's visit to Laos. During its stay, the delegation is scheduled to visit some economic and cultural establishments in Vientiane Municipality, Vientiane Province, and Luang Prabang Province.
