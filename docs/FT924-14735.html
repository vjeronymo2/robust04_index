921008 FT 08 OCT 92 / UK Company News: BCE moves back into the black BCE HOLDINGS, the USM-quoted distributor of snooker, billiards and pool products, moved back into the black for the first time since 1988, with taxable profits of Pounds 34,000 for the year to March. This compared with losses of Pounds 622,000 previously. Turnover totalled Pounds 5.2m (Pounds 5.47m). Interest charges fell to Pounds 276,000 (Pounds 586,000) and exceptional income increased to Pounds 57,000 (Pounds 7,000). Earnings per share of 0.2p compared with losses of 1.65p.
