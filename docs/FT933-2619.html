930917 FT 17 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Expansion of net assets at Pantheon For the year ended June 30 1993 net asset value per share of Pantheon International Participations expanded to 195.5p, compared with 139.7p, assuming full conversion of warrants. Undiluted the figure amounted to 211.1p against 146.2p. Available revenue of this investment trust was lower at Pounds 169,000 (Pounds 438,000) for earnings per share of 1.16p (3.01p). The dividend has been cut from 2.5p to 0.5p.
