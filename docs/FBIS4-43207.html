BFN [Excerpt] A bomb was placed on a city bus in Istanbul. The ticket collector aboard the bus noticed the package and threw it off. It exploded on the road. Nobody was hurt by the explosion. According to information received from Istanbul Security Directorate officials, the incident happened at around 0800 on a double-decker city bus traveling from Taksim to Bostanci. Two suspicious-looking people, who got on the bus at Taksim, left the bomb on the upper level after the bus crossed the Strait Bridge. The ticket collector became suspicious and threw the package off the bus. It exploded on the highway. No damage occurred and no one was hurt by the explosion. [passage omitted]
