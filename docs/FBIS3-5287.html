Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Fukuoka, March 4 KYODO -- Chinese Vice Premier Zhu Rongji ended a 10-day visit to Japan on Friday [4 March], leaving for home from Fukuoka in Kyushu. During his stay, Zhu met Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa and other Japanese leaders. He also visited Osaka and Fukuoka and urged business leaders to strengthen economic cooperation with China.
