March 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

GENERAL MEDICAL CORP. LEASES SPACE IN CARSON

General Medical Corp., a distributor of disposable medical supplies, has leased 82,500 square feet of office and warehouse space, or about half of the new building, at 970 E. 236th St., Carson.

The five-year-lease, valued at about $2.5 million, was for space at Watson Industrial Center South, a development of Watson Land Co. Occupancy is scheduled in June.
