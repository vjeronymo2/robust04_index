Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Deputy Foreign Minister 'Abd-al-Ghani 'Abd-al-Qadir and the delegation accompanying him met with PRC Vice Foreign Minister Tian Zengpei in Beijing today. During the meeting, which is part of the ongoing and regular consultations between the two countries' foreign ministries, the two sides reviewed Yemeni-PRC ties and the means of promoting and enhancing them in the interest of the two friendly countries and peoples. They also exchanged views on regional and international issues of common interest.
