940203 FT 03 FEB 94 / Child support rules changed Changes in the methods used by the child support agency to secure maintenance payments from absent parents were demanded from both sides of the Commons last night. Around 20 Labour backbenchers ignored the official party line to abstain and joined with Liberal Democrat MPs in voting against regulations embodying modifications announced in December on the grounds that they did not go far enough. The regulations were approved by a government majority of 218 (364-46).
