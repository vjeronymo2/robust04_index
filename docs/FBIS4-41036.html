BFN [Text] Kiev [no dateline as received]--The press service of the Ukraina democratic association reports that the association has written to presidential candidates Valeriy Babych and Petro Talanchuk, urging them to withdraw from the presidential race for the benefit of Leonid Kravchuk. The statement points out that, "in view of the threat to our state's independence, the Ukraina democratic association has resolved that the situation can be improved only if the current president, Leonid Kravchuk, who has proved himself to be a consistent champion of Ukrainian statehood and the country's democratic development, wins the 26 June election." The Ukraina democratic association has prepared documents in support of Kravchuk, signed by the leaders of most of the association's parties and organizations. Support groups campaigning for the current president will soon leave for Ukraine's eastern and southern regions. The press service of the Ukraina democratic association told UNIAN that the appeal to the presidential candidates was sent on 15 June, but no reply has been received thus far.
