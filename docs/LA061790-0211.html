June 17, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Sprinter Chris Nelloms of Dayton, Ohio, set two meet records as 250 athletes from around the world competed in the Keebler International Prep Track and Field Invitational at Elmhurst, Ill.

Nelloms set meet records of 45.36 seconds in the men's 400 meters and 20.67 seconds in the 200 meters at the 19th annual invitational.

Shelly Choppa of Glens Falls, N.Y., set a meet record in the high jump of 6 feet 3/4 inches.
