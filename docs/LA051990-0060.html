May 19, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION SOFTBALL ROUNDUP;

WEIL PITCHES NO-HITTER TO LEAD HART TO 7-1 WIN

Senior right-hander Kym Weil lost her perfect game and shutout in the seventh inning but still threw a no-hitter to lead Hart High to a 7-1 win over Rio Mesa Friday in a first-round Southern Section 4-A Division softball playoff game at Hart.

Rio Mesa (13-10) scored in the seventh when a runner reached base on a fielding error and scored on a throwing error.

Weil (25-2) struck out 12 and walked none.

Heather Medearis and Stephanie Keeler each had two hits for Hart (26-3), the top-seeded team in the division. Hart will play Torrance, a 3-0 winner over Long Beach Poly on Friday, on Tuesday.

In other 4-A games:

Crescenta Valley 2, Burroughs 1 -- Both Crescenta Valley runs scored on errors in the second inning at Montrose Field.

Becki Mixon (24-2), a sophomore, struck out eight and scattered five hits for the Falcons.

Buena 4, Hoover 0 -- Freshman Nancy Evans struck out 10 and allowed six hits, but Hoover (17-6) stranded 10 runners and fell in the first round to host Buena (19-6).

1-A DIVISION

Paraclete 11, Oak Park 0 -- Laura Staats held Oak Park (16-8) to three hits and struck out 13. Kelli Rommey had two Oak Park hits.

La Reina 9, San Gabriel Mission 3 -- Sheri Sisco struck out 17, and Valerie Serpico hit a home run, the first time a ball has cleared the fence at La Reina in more than five years.

Serpico's two-run home run cleared a fence 190 feet away in right-center field. Sisco (7-2), a senior right-hander, struck out eight in a row at one point for La Reina (21-5).

Santa Paula 12, Village Christian 0 -- Santa Paula senior left-hander Julie Coert (4-4) struck out six, allowed only three hits, walked none and helped her own cause with a run-scoring double.

Santa Paula (13-13) will play La Reina in a second-round game Wednesday.

Agoura 11, L.A. Baptist 0 -- Junior right-hander Melissa Thatcher (20-5) threw a one-hitter and hit a two-run double against visiting L. A. Baptist.

Jill Hartwig was two for four with a three-run triple for Agoura (21-5).

SMALL SCHOOLS DIVISION

Crossroads 19, Highland Hall 11 -- Crossroads' Melony Berkey was three for four with two doubles and two RBIs against visiting Highland Hall.

Stephanie Reisberg had three hits, and Rebecca Bloom (11-5) earned the win for Crossroads (12-6).

Ribet Academy 7, Oakwood 3 -- Ribet Academy's Kate Stoll, a converted catcher, pitched a no-hitter but walked 14 at Ribet Academy.
