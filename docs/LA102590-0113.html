October 25, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: GLENDORA;

ADULT DAY-CARE CENTER OKD

The City Council on Tuesday granted permission for an adult day-care center for the developmentally disabled to operate at 1614-1616 S. Grand Ave.

The center will operate from 7 a.m. to 7 p.m. Monday through Friday.

It will teach basic skills, such as grooming, ordering a meal at a restaurant, buying items at a market and crossing streets. Some job skills will also be taught.
