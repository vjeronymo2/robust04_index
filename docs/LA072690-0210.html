July 26, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

AFTER A SUCCESSFUL TRIP, DUKES ARE HOME TONIGHT

After five matches on a trip through the East, the Newport Beach Dukes will play host to the Sacramento Capitals at 7:30 tonight at the John Wayne Tennis Club.

In Team Tennis' West Division, the Capitals (6-2) are half a match ahead of the Dukes (5-2) and half a match behind the first-place Los Angeles Strings. The Dukes won four of their five road matches.

The Dukes' Amy Frazier, who has a 41-27 record, is the top-ranked women's singles player in Team Tennis after seven matches.
