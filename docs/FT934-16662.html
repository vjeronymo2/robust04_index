931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Sherriff Holdings SHERRIFF HOLDINGS, the plant and tool hire group, has purchased Robson (Power Tools) for up to Pounds 110,000. Leeds-based Robson has seven depots in Yorkshire and the north-east. In the year ended March 31, Robson's turnover was Pounds 2.68m and operating losses Pounds 51,000.
