BFN [Text] Tirana, June 3 (ATA) -- Fadil Berisha, chairman of the assembly of the Pristina university, Destan Halimi, secretary, and Sali Maqesonci, of the rectorate, have been put under arrest for two days now. The Serb police interrupted the proceedings of the assembly of the Pristina University on May 31, and besides the above three persons, it also arrested Prof. Dr. Ejup Statovci, rector of the university, Selman Brymen and other leading officials, who were released later, according to the information centre of Kosova.
