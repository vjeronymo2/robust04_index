BFN [Text] Baku (April 9) TURAN -- Yesterday approximately one hundred highway police picketed in front of the Presidential Palace. The policemen demanded the release of their chief, Tofik Guseynov, head of the Republican Highway Police. Tofik Guseynov, as was reported earlier, was arrested on April 7 after having been accused of stealing the sputnik antennae from "Amoco" oil company in 1992.
