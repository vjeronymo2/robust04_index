BFN [Text] Mexico City, 9 Apr (DPA) -- A leader of the Zapatist National Liberation Army (EZLN) was killed yesterday by a rancher in Altamirano municipality, Chiapas State, the EZLN Clandestine Revolutionary Committee reported today. In a statement published by the EXCELSIOR News Agency, guerrilla spokesman Subcommander Marcos said this action contradicts the alleged climate of peace to which Manuel Camacho Solis, commissioner for peace and reconciliation in Chiapas, has referred. Nevertheless, the cease-fire will be maintained. According to the statement, the EZLN leader went with a companion to see a parcel of land to farm. When they arrived at the site, they were met with shots fired by the ranch owner's son. The leader's companion was wounded.
