BFN [Text] About 14,300 crore rupees of direct foreign investment has been approved since August 1991. Over 80 percent of the investment went to priority industries with fuel and oil refinery sector accounting for a maximum of 2,395 crore rupees, followed by the power sector, food processing, metallurgical industries and electrical equipment and electronics. The actual inflow of foreign investment including NRI [nonresident Indian] investment amounted to 3,406 crore rupees. The maximum investment worth 650 crore rupees was from the United States followed by Britain and Germany.
