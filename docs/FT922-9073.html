920509 FT 09 MAY 92 / Finance & The Family: Directors' Transactions DIRECTORS' dealing activity has now calmed down to a more reasoned level where the overall picture is no longer distorted by one-off influences. Clinton Cards retails greeting cards and gifts throughout England. Directors have been buying shares during April. Most recently Clinton Lewin, the managing director, and Don Lewin, the chairman, both bought 25,000 shares at 76p. That is 16p higher than the price at which three directors bought two weeks earlier. Selling at Renishaw, the metrology and inspection equipment company, has been a feature since the end of last year. Most recently Allan Roberts, the finance director, sold 24,000 shares at 272p. John Hall, the deputy chairman of Hall Engineering, and Robert McAlpine, a non-executive director, both purchased shares in this steel processing group. Hall bought 14,000 shares at 152p in an off-market deal on April 29, while McAlpine paid 148.5p for 15,000 shares from the market on April 23.
