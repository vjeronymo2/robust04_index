940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / International Capital Markets: ANI in ADollars 191m tube maker bid Australian National Industries, the Sydney-based engineer which owns the Aurora group in the UK, yesterday announced a ADollars 191m (USDollars 140.4m) bid for Palmer Tube Mills, an Australian company which makes steel tube and pipe sections, writes Nikki Tait in Sydney. Shareholders are being offered 72 ANI shares for every 100 Palmer shares held, or 64 ANI shares and ADollars 13 cash for every 100 shares. Palmer's board said it would consider the offer.
