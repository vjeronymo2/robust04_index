920121 FT 21 JAN 92 / Signs of life seen in property market THE PROPERTY market may be starting to splutter back to life outside London and south-east England, according to a poll of estate agents. The straw poll organised by the National Association of Estate Agents showed agents in other regions becoming more bullish. 'The market has been virtually dead for two years and estate agents were thinking it would continue like that well into this year,' said the association. 'But we are now thinking we were wrong.' It added that the cut of 1/2 a percentage point in some mortgage rates which was announced last week by five lenders - including the four biggest home loans groups - would assist a recovery.
