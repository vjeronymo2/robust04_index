BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 15 (KCNA) -- The National Alliance for Democracy and Reunification (Chongukyonhap) of South Korea released a statement concerning the recent discovery of 70 kilograms of unregistered plutonium at a nuclear fuel factory of Japan, the Seoul-based MBC (Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation) reported on May 13. The statement urged Japan to promptly give up the moves to revive militarism. Chongukyonhap criticized the South Korean authorities' humiliating dependence on Japan and urged them to take a tough attitude toward it.
