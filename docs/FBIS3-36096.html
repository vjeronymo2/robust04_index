Language: English Article Type:BFN [By BELINFORM for TASS] [Text] Minsk February 2 TASS -- Belarusian Parliament Chairman Myecheslaw Hryb instructed the Republican KGB to probe the decision of the National Strike Committee which, according to him, contains appeals to topple authorities. The Strike Committee decided on Wednesday to call an indefinite political strike on February 15. The major demand is the resignation of the government and early parliamentary elections. "If there are criminal aspects in the issue, criminal proceedings should be instituted jointly with the prosecutor's office and an investigation carried out", Hryb instructed Acting KGB Chairman Gennadiy Lavitskiy. Hryb informed parliamentarians about his order during the evening session on Wednesday.
