BFN [Excerpt] UN special envoy Iqbal Riza, a Pakistani, is continuing his mission in Rwanda. After holding talks on 23 May with Rwandan Patriotic Front [RPF] political and military officials, including Chairman Colonel Alexis Kanyarengwe, he held talks yesterday with the government side, in a meeting with the Government Army and Gendarmerie chiefs of staff. The UN special envoy will hold talks today with political officials of the Gitarama government. [passage omitted]
