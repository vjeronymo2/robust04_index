BFN [Text] Asyut, 20 Apr (MENA) -- Brigadier General Shirin 'Ali Fahmi, commander of the security directorate in Asyut, was shot dead in the city center this morning. The brigadier general was leaving his home for work when unidentified men opened fire at him. Conscript Ahmad 'Ali al-Fuli was also killed in the shooting. One of the terrorists was seriously injured during the shoot-out with the guards. He was transferred to the hospital. Conscript 'Amir Ahmad Husayn was also injured in the attack. Commanders of the Asyut security directorate and criminal laboratory officials have started investigations into the attack. The search for the criminals has already begun.
