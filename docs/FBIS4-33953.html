BFN [Text] Economy Minister Jorge Camet said today in London that Peru is a secure and stable country for investments, and that its people have regained their faith. Minister Camet is attending a conference on Latin America organized by the Inter-American Development Bank and the HERALD TRIBUNE in London. Camet added that in the next 18 months all state companies, including Petroperu [State Petroleum Agency], Solgas [Compania Peruana de Gas S.A.--Peruvian Natural Gas Company Inc.], Petromar [Petroleos del Mar S.A.--Off-shore Oil Drilling Inc.], Electroperu [Peruvian State Electric Power Company, Inc.], and Sedapal [National Potable Water and Sewage Service] will be privatized.
