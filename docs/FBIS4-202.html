BFN [Excerpt] The governor of the Central Bank of West African States [BCEAO], Ivoirian Charles Konan Banny, was sworn in today in Dakar. He was appointed as governor of the BCEAO by the Conference of the heads of state of the West African Monetary Union. The ceremony took place in the presence of several officials and was presided over by Senegalese Prime Minister Habib Thiam. [passage omitted]
