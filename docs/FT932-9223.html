930514 FT 14 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Sharp net asset rise at Abtrust New Thai A STRONG second half performance in net asset value, which rose by 94 per cent, enabled Abtrust New Thai Investment Trust to finish the year to February 28 with the figure 76.6 per cent higher at 133.02p, against 75.34p a year earlier. Gross revenue was Pounds 514,000 (Pounds 522,000) the result of lower interest receivable. Net revenue was Pounds 183,925 (Pounds 216,594) and earnings per share came to 1.23p (1.44p). An unchanged single final dividend of 1p is being recommended.
