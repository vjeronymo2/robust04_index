BFN [Text] Here is a report just in. A Jewish woman was stabbed a short while ago while strolling on the promenade in East Jerusalem's Armon Hanetziv neighborhood. Our correspondent Uri Kohen-Aharonov was told that the assailant, who fled, was an Arab. A Magen David Adom ambulance evacuated the young woman, who was wounded in the stomach, to Hadassah-'En Kerem Hospital.
