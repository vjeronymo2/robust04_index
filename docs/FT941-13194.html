940127 FT 27 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: Student charged over pistol attack on Prince Charles Student David Kang appeared in court in Sydney early today on six charges following an attack on Prince Charles yesterday in which two blank shots were fired with a starting pistol. The Prince, pictured being protected by his bodyguard, Colin Trimming (left), had stayed calm and delivered his address at the Australia Day ceremony. Police said Kang, 23, had written to the Prince protesting about the treatment of Cambodian boat people in detention camps in Australia.
