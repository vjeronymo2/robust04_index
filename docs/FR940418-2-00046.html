On December 5, 1990, Medina, in Docket No. QF91&hyph;040&hyph;000, filed with the Commission a notice of self-certification as a QF pursuant to 18 CFR 292.207(a)(2). Niagara Mohawk is the purchaser of electric power generated by Medina's QF. According to Niagara Mohawk, Medina's QF does not currently and has not had in the past, a thermal output customer. Niagara Mohawk states that during a plant visit to Medina's QF in 1992, Medina represented that a greenhouse would be the facility's thermal output customer, but that Medina subsequently discontinued its plans to construct a greenhouse. Thus, Niagara Mohawk contends that Medina's QF is not in compliance with §292.205(a) of the Commission's regulations.
