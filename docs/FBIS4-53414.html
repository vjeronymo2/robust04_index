BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 18 (KCNA) -- Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam sent a message of condolence to acting minister of external relations of the Republic of Zaire Mukulumanya Wa N'gate Zenda. Kim Yong-nam expressed deep condolences to the bereaved family upon receiving the sad news that minister of external relations Mpinga Kasenda died in a plane crash.
