Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Gursel Balci video report from Agri Mountain region] [Text] In operations launched five days ago, war planes bombed terrorist targets in the northern section of Agri Mountain. Yesterday, security forces engaged a group of 150 terrorists in a clash in the Korugan-(?Kislagi) region. The area harboring the terrorists was encircled by two battalions and subjected to intense artillery and mortar fire. Cobra helicopters were also used. The terrorists, who sustained heavy casualties, are taking advantage of the 2-meter high snow and mountainous terrain and carrying their dead and wounded to Kupgolu area. Today, security forces began their search operations in the area of the fighting. During the operations, acting on information that a group of terrorists descended from Agri Mountain into the surrounding villages, security forces searched the villages located between the mountain and Armenia. A total of 68 persons -- eight organization members, 28 abettors, and 32 wanted suspects -- were captured during the searches. With these people, security forces seized three Kalashnikov rifles and large quantities of unlicensed weapons and rounds of ammunition.
