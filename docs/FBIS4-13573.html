BFN [Text] The Jammu and Kashmir governor, General K.V. Krishna Rao, said the security situation in the Kashmir valley has considerably improved. Addressing a meeting of senior-level officials in Jammu, he said the security forces have achieved notable successes in their drive against the militants with the increased cooperation from the people. Meanwhile, the former chief minister of the state, Dr. Farooq Abdullah, has said the solution to the Kashmir problem lies not in violence but in political discussions. He told PTI in Jammu yesterday that as the path of violence has failed in Punjab so it will in Kashmir.
