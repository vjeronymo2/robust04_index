930803 FT 03 AUG 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa IN SPITE of a strong start as bullion held firm above Dollars 400 an ounce, gold shares showed a downward trend through much of the day, the index slipping 28 to 2,070. Industrials moved forward 12 to 4,610 and the overall index shed 14 to 4,163. De Beers ended 75 cents up at R86.75, while associate Anglos shed R3 to R150 after recent strength. Gold division Amgold eased R1 to R369. Vaal Reefs retreated R5 to R396 after hitting R410.50 early in the day. Kloof firmed R1 to R402. Doornfontein came off the day's low of R4.50 to finish a net 35 cents down at R4.75. Dealers said news that it had lost a court action on its dismissal of about 6,000 workers in 1991 would not have helped general negative market sentiment.
