(g) Temporary nonuse and conservation use may be approved by the authorized officer if such use is determined to be in conformance with the applicable land use plan, AMP or other activity plan, and standards and guidelines as follows: (1) Conservation use may be approved for periods of up to 10 years when, in the determination of the authorized officer, the proposed nonuse will promote rangeland resource protection or enhancement of resource values or uses, including more rapid progress toward resource condition objectives; or (2) Temporary nonuse for reasons including but not limited to financial conditions or annual fluctuations of livestock, may be approved on an annual basis for no more than 3 consecutive years.
