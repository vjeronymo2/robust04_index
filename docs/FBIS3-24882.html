Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Bissau, 1 Feb (AFP) -- Teachers in Guinea-Bissau today began a five-day strike called by their trade union, the National Union of Teachers [SYNAPROF], to demand better working conditions and salary increases. The minister of national education condemned this strike, adding that he did not rule out the use of force to call the teachers back to work. In October 1993, the SYNAPROF had sent a notice to the authorities for a strike slated for December.
