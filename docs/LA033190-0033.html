March 31, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

YOU CAN'T PUT A PRICE ON HIGH SCHOOL SPIRIT

I read where the L.A. City school governing body is thinking of a realignment of the City football playoff system.

I am of the school that believes that the more champions you have, the more happy student-athletes, proud communities and families you have.

If anything, I urge a return to the three-division alignment, lest we see first-round matchups like Banning vs. Marshall.

PETE ARBOGAST

Los Angeles
