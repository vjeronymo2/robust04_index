BFN [Text] Ankara, April 4 (A.A.) -- President Suleyman Demirel said on Monday [4 April] that Turkey was willing to improve relations with Albania in every field. Demirel, speaking after Albanian President Sali Berisha's arrival ceremony at the Cankaya Palace said the visit will contribute to Turkish-Albanian relations. Demirel said with this visit cultural and historical relations will be enhanced. "Turkish and Albanian people share the same standard of judgment and these two people feel sincere love and respect for each other," Demirel said. Demirel said the visit takes place at a time when important changes are taking place in the region, the Balkans and the Black Sea region. "Regional and international problems will be discussed during the visit," he added. Demirel said Turkey is doing its best to support Albania's transition to a free market economy. Berisha recalled that Demirel supported the improvement of democracy in Albania. Berisha also expressed his pleasure with the positive improvements in political, economic and military relations between the two countries.
