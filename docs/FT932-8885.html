930517 FT 17 MAY 93 / Lending and leasing increases by 7% FIGURES from the Finance and Leasing Association show that new lending and leasing to the business sector rose by 7 per cent in the first quarter compared with the same period a year ago. Association members said the rise in lending to consumers came from a 24 per cent increase in unsecured personal loans and a 12 per cent rise in retail store credit products for the first quarter, against a year ago.
