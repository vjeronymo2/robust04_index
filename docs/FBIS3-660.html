Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The South African and Bophuthatswana presidents were in brief telephonic contact this morning, and the minister of state affairs of Bophuthatswana, Mr. Rowan Cronje, held talks at the Union Buildings for talks with President F.W. de Klerk. [sentence as heard] It was not known whether President Lucas Mangope was involved. A government spokesman said Mr. de Klerk had also had a telephonic conversation with ANC [African National Congress] leader, Nelson Mandela, this morning concerning the situation in Bophuthatswana.
