Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Acapulco, 8 Jan (NOTIMEX) -- Windows and doors at the Federal Palace, which houses public offices at the Acapulco resort in southern Mexico, were shattered today by an explosion that yielded no victims, according to government sources. The sources stated that they have no immediate details concerning the explosion in Acapulco, which is 300 km south of Mexico City.
