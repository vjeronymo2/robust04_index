940324 FT 24 MAR 94 / World News in Brief: Mexico's leading presidential candidate shot Mexico's ruling party presidential candidate, Mr Luis Donaldo Colosio, was shot and seriously injured last night in the northern city of Tijuana. Mr Colosio, 44, was taken to hospital in the city. Witnesses said he had been hit in the head and chest. Mr Colosio had just finished giving an outdoor speech to his Institutional Revolutionary party supporters when he was hit. One man was reported to have been detained for the assassination attempt. Mr Colosio has been overwhelming favourite to win the country's election this August, to take over from President Carlos Salinas.
