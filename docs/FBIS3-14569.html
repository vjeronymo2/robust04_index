Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Islamabad, March 28, IRNA -- A prominent member of Afghan Premier Golboddin Hekmatyar's Hezb-e Eslami [Islamic Party of Afghanistan], Qazi Amin Waqqad, today called for cessation of the ongoing civil war in Afghanistan. In his statement, Waqqad referred to the civil war as a factor marring the image of Islam and responsible for the destruction of Afghanistan. He added that he has called on the belligerent parties to bury the hatchet and initiate peace efforts. The statment reads that Afghan people are in need of a healthy system, peace and security, and those in authority should not resort to arms which were used for the sacred defence of Afghanistan (against Soviet occupation), to mar the image of Islam and destroy the country. The statement issued in response to the press release of Hezb-e Eslami, criticized Hekmatyar for his recent claims. In a statement which appeared in press on Sunday [27 March] Hekmatyar had claimed that his party had not nomiated Waqqad to any post nor does the post of deputy leader exists in his party. Waqqad in his statement named himself as one of the founders of the Hezb-e Eslami and said Hekmatyar's recent claims were to please his new comrades.
