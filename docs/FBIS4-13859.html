BFN [Text] Cairo, 24 Apr (MENA)--AL-AHRAM states that President Husni Mubarak's visit to China was succesful. The paper says Egypt's political gains from the visit are no less important than the economic gains. These gains, AL-AHRAM explains, are represented by the two countries' complete agreement on regional issues in Asia and the Middle East in particular, and international issues in general, especially the future of the world in light of the new world order, which is now being formed. In its editorial today the paper says that Egyptian-Chinese relations have been strong and firm for years. They have never cooled and are becoming stronger. The paper adds that Egypt's policy under President Mubarak has always been balanced and eager to support and promote relations with all nations.
