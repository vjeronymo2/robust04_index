January 7, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

MILITARY TO PRESENT REAGAN WITH MEDALS

The nation's armed forces are preparing a military farewell for President Reagan at Andrews Air Force Base next week, White House spokesman Marlin Fitzwater announced Friday.

Fitzwater said that Defense Secretary Frank C. Carlucci is staging the ceremony, planned for Thursday, in which the President will be presented with medals from all of the services, and his wife, Nancy, will receive a special certificate.

Fitzwater said that some of the new weapons systems and equipment produced during Reagan's eight-year military buildup will be on display at the Maryland air base just southeast of Washington. About 1,500 people have been invited to the event.
