February 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The USC men's swim team will play host to UCLA today at 1 p.m. at the McDonald's Swim Stadium. The Trojans, 10-0 overall and 4-0 in the Pacific 10 Conference, are ranked second nationally and have won the last four meetings between the two teams. UCLA (9-3, 3-1) is ranked sixth.
