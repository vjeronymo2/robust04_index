920522 FT 22 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Shires Investment asset value lower Net asset value per share of Shires Investment stood at 225.84p on March 31 compared with 260.39p a year earlier. Fully diluted the value was 219.6p against 242.5p. Net revenue for the 12 months amounted to Pounds 4.39m (Pounds 4.02m) for earnings per share of 19p (19.03p). A final dividend of 5.9p (5.8p) is proposed for a total of 18.4p (17.8p).
