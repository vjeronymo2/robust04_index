November 10, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL FOOTBALL;

NONLEAGUE

Westlake 28, Cerritos 13 -- Warriors' quarterback Todd Preston completed 16 of 29 passes for 272 yards and four touchdowns at Newbury Park High.

Westlake (4-5-1) scored on its first possession when Preston connected with Dave Monheim on a 12-yard touchdown pass, capping a 55-yard drive.

Cerritos (2-8) took a 7-6 first-quarter lead on a five-yard pass from Jon Neilsen to Keith Harris.

Westlake took the lead for good in the second quarter when Preston hit Seamus Gibbons with a 25-yard touchdown pass.

Westlake's Luke Crawford caught a game-high five passes for 92 yards, including a 39-yard, third-quarter touchdown pass. Monheim made four catches for 81 yards. LANCE O'FALLON
