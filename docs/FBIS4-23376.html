BFN [Excerpts] The Rwandan Patriotic Front [RPF] chairman, Colonel Alexis Kanyarengwe, held a press conference yesterday at Mulindi RPF headquarters in northern Rwanda. The press conference was attended by foreign journalists from Italy and Australia. [passage omitted] The RPF chairman condemned the Rwandan leaders for promoting the killings through their preaches, newspapers and government-controlled radios. He estimated the people who have been killed so far in a period of only two months to be close to 1 million.
