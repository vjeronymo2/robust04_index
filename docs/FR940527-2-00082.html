Foreign Quarantine (43 CFR part 71)_0910&hyph;0134 (Extension, no change)_Foreign quarantine regulations implement the provisions of the Public Health Service Act in preventing the introduction, transmission, or spread of communicable diseases from foreign countries into the United States. Inspections and control measures are undertaken with respect to conveyances, persons, and shipments of animals and etiologic agents. Respondents: Individuals or households, Businesses or other for-profit.
