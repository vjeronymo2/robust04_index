BFN [Text] Stepanakert ("HAYASTANI HANRAPETUTYUN" daily - Armenpress), 06/04/94 -- RMK Supreme Council Session of June 3 promoted the Commander of the RMK [Republic of Mountainous Karabakh] defense army Colonel Samvel Babayan to the rank of General-Major. S. Babayan was promoted to this high rank for the great input in forming and enforcing the RMK defense army, for supporting the interests and ensuring the security of the republic, for the great skills expressed in this field. Karabakh land historically gave life to numerous famous commanders, generals and marshals. 29 year old S. Babayan became the first general of the Republic of Mountainous Karabakh, which declared its independence in December of 1992.
