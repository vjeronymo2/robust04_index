921110 FT 10 NOV 92 / French blast kills at least five Explosions rocked a big oil refinery near the southern French port of Marseille early yesterday, killing five people and seriously injuring one, the refinery's owners said, Reuter reports from Marseille. Eight people were on duty at the time of the blast and one was still missing, the French oil company, Total, said in a statement. The eighth person was safe. The explosions were believed to have been caused by a gas leak. More than 250 firefighters took more than three hours to bring the fire under control.
