930427 FT 27 APR 93 / International Company News: Goodyear exceeds forecast with Dollars 87m GOODYEAR Tire & Rubber, the last US-owned tyre group, posted better-than-expected first-quarter earnings of Dollars 87.1m, or 60 cents a share, against a forecast Dollars 83m to Dollars 86m, writes Karen Zagor. A year earlier, underlying income was Dollars 66.1m, or 47 cents, excluding the cumulative effect of accounting changes. Including the changes, it had a net loss of Dollars 944.5m, or Dollars 6.67. Earnings per share have been restated to reflect a two-for-one stock split effective May 4.
