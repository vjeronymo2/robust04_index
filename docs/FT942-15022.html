940418 FT 18 APR 94 / High-technology skills shortage Severe skills shortages are emerging in high-technology industries as demand picks up. Hoskyns, one of the UK's largest computer services companies, has warned that a recruitment drive to meet increased demand for its services might be hampered by skills shortages. GPT, the UK-based telecommunications group, says it has for the past year experienced problems recruiting graduate engineers for its telecommunications systems.
