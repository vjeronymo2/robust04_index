Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Three Arab citizens were arrested 24 hours after an attack on a New York bus carrying a number of Jewish Americans. One was killed and three others were injured in the attack. One of the arrested Arabs is a Lebanese named Asad Baz who was charged with attempted murder. The other two, Malay Muhammad and Bassam Riyati, both Jordanians, are accused of possessing weapons and obstructing justice. The New York police chief said that detectives found what he described as a veritable arsenal in the home of the Lebanese suspect. When asked if there was a link between the attack on the Jewish Americans in New York and the Hebron massacre, the police chief said that he did not rule out any possibility.
