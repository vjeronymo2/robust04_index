May 7, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

OUTTAKES: UNINVITED

"Pieces of Silver," the latest film from Oscar-winning compilist Chuck Workman ("Precious Images"), has been pulled from the Cannes Film Festival. "Pieces" -- 310 sequences celebrating the 100th anniversary of Edison's purchase of Eastman Kodak film for moving pictures, running 7 1/2 minutes -- hit clearance problems.

In addition to scenes from American and foreign movies, "Pieces" -- which premiered at the recent AFI Fest -- includes anim-ation, newsreels, commercials, documentaries, experimental films and home movies.
