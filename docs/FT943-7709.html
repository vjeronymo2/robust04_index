940822 FT 22 AUG 94 / ICI may generate own electricity Imperial Chemical Industries and other large electricity users are considering building or buying power stations to cut the price they pay for electricity. ICI is investigating building power stations next to its chemical works. One such plant is operated by Enron at ICI's Wilton Teesside site. The company is also exploring, with other large energy users, the possibility of buying power stations from National Power and PowerGen.
