941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / Carers' charity awarded BS 5750 The Princess Royal Trust for carers was yesterday awarded British Standard registration, after giving an assurance that no more than 15 per cent of donations would be spent on administration. It is thought to be the first charity to gain BS 5750 registration, given in recognition of an organisation's competence and efficiency in achieving its goals. The charity was set up to provide recognition and support for the estimated 6.8m carers in Britain.
