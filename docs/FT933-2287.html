930920 FT 20 SEP 93 / Study highlights smoking policies THE MAJORITY of employers have a formal policy on smoking, and many have implemented a ban in the workplace a study by Incomes Date Services, the research organisation, shows. IDS, which drew its conclusions from a sample of companies ranging from the European Bank for Reconstruction and Development to Help the Aged, said that the past few years had seen a change in attitudes to smoking at work.
