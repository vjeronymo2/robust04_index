BFN [Text] The Council of Ministers met under Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi at 1100 today and reviewed the work of the services, economic, and cultural committees. During this meeting, the Council of Ministers also discussed current political developments in the Arab and international arenas. Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' briefed the council on the latest developments and on the results of the contacts and visits that have recently taken place. The foreign minister stressed Syria's rejection of Israeli attempts to drain the peace process of its true essence and to evade commitments to a just and comprehensive peace. The Council of Ministers also discussed a draft law on endorsing the protocol on scientific and technical cooperation in the field of geology between the Egyptian general authority of geological survey and mining stores, and the general authority for geology and mineral resources in Syria. The Council of Ministers discussed the topics on its agenda and the issues submitted to it and made the appropriate decisions.
