April 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

WALK LIKE A MAN

We instructors in the aerobics profession have been trying very hard to entice men into the aerobics room. It is a great workout for anyone, any age, any sex. It is not a woman's domain. Men are already hesitant to try for fear of being confronted by women like Littwin. I hope none of my class members read her article, or I'll have to start back at square one! One more man in aerobics equals one less couch potato!

JUNE SCHNEIDER

Redondo Beach
