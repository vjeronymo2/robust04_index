Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Mashhad, Khorasan Prov., Feb 6, IRNA -- A group of Friday prayer leaders and ulema of Sunni muslims of the cities of Khauf [placename as received] and Torbat-e Jam, east of this northeastern border province, in message have condemned the last week assassination attempt on President Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani's life. Mowlavi Ghowseddin Ahrari, Mowlavi Nik Kerdar and Hajj Qazi, Friday prayer leaders of khauf, Sangan And torbat-e Jam as well as Mowlavi Mottahari and Sheikh Assadollah Tavasoli, heads of the theological schools of Khauf and Torbat-e Jam sent separate messages to the president. [spelling of all personal names as received] In their statements, the Sunni Muslim leaders wished for the longitude of President Hashemi-Rafsanjani and stressed that failure of the attempt was a sign of God's favor.
