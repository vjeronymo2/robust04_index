940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / Health chairman is sacked Mr Brynn Davies, for 16 years chairman of Mid Glamorgan Health Authority, will not have his contract renewed this summer after a report highlighting serious management failures. The Commons committee on the parliamentary commissioner for administration (the ombudsman) was told yesterday of his repeated failure to act on complaints. The decision was made by Mr John Redwood, Welsh secretary. Mr John Wyn Owen, a member of the National Health Service management executive for Wales, told the committee action was being taken to improve matters. In January Mr Davies apologised to the committee for the treatment of the relatives of patients who tried for 18 months in vain to see a consultant.
