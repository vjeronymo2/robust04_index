920321 FT 21 MAR 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Most spice prices remained steady except for cloves, which came down rapidly after export duties were abolished in Madagascar, reports Man-producten. Early this week Madagascar cloves were offered at between Dollars 1,325 and Dollars 1,350 a tonne cif, but by the end of the week sellers were looking for bids at Dollars 1,125 to Dollars 1,150. Cassia: some demand from factories which are starting to cover their autum needs. Pimento: steady, Dollars 2,450 ex-warehouse Rotterdam. Another official price increase expected. Small lots Mexico pimento are offered at Dollars 2,350 cif for immediate delivery. Market for chillies remains hot.
