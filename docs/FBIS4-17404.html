BFN [Report from the ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA, ITAR-TASS column: "Moscow Extends Hand"] [Text] An agreement on cooperation in the economic, scientific, technical, and sociocultural spheres has been concluded between the governments of Dagestan and Moscow. The agreement was signed during the course of a visit here by a delegation from the Russian capital headed by Mayor Yuriy Luzhkov. The delegation visited a number of cities in the republic, familiarized itself with the economy, toured construction projects, and visited the Caspian "Dagdizel" Plant, where a discussion was held on the enterprise's potential, the problems of conversion, and opportunities for cooperation with Moscow plants.
