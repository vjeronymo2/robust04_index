931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / International Company News: USG earnings rise to Dollars 65m in third quarter USG, the US gypsum products group, said that its third-quarter earnings before interest, taxes, depreciation, depletion, amortisation and other non-cash charges totalled Dollars 65m, compared with Dollars 54m last year, Reuter reports. The company said that it was reporting earnings on this basis due to a bankruptcy filing. USG emerged from bankruptcy on May 6 1993. Net sales for the three months rose to Dollars 514m from Dollars 474m in the corresponding period a year ago. On the same basis, earnings for the full nine months rose to Dollars 165m from Dollars 132m a year ago while net sales increased to Dollars 1.42bn from Dollars 1.34bn.
