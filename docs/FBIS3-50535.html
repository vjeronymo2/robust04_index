Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Over the last few days, 1 January 1959 has become a constant subject in Vietnam, where numerous ceremonies and congratulations for the 35th anniversary of the triumph of the Cuban Revolution are being reported by Hanoi's media. Du Muoi, secretary general of the Vietnam Communist Party, Vietnam's President Le Duc Anh, and Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet sent a letter of congratulations to Commander in Chief Fidel Castro on the occasion of such a highly significant date.
