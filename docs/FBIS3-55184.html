Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Party groups in the State Duma who are continuing their confidential consultations on candidatures to the posts of speaker and his deputies have not yet reached agreement. INTERFAX has learned from a well-informed source that Russia's Choice, the Communists, the Agrarians, and the Liberal Democrats are ready to agree to the election of a speaker from one of the smaller groups -- Yavlinskiy's bloc or Women of Russia -- so long as the four deputies are elected from these four largest groups.
