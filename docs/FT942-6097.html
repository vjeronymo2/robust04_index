940601 FT 01 JUN 94 / UK Company News: CIA in Pounds 3m purchase of Dutch group CIA Group has acquired MediaMend, a Dutch media independent, for up to Pounds 3.1m, including the issue of 660,000 new ordinary shares. The acquisition takes in 75 per cent of MediaMend's Belgian sister company. MediaMend has projected billings in Benelux of more than Pounds 30m.
