BFN [Text] On behalf of the organization committee of the Alfa Cartel National Trade Union Confederation, this evening we received the following communique: On the pretext of the uncertain nature of the dialogue, considering that the government team is including fewer people than the trade union team, the executive rejects a meeting with the trade union members who have been designated by the assembly of representatives who are holding a rally in Victoria Square. The representatives of the government want the number of trade union members who are included in the team not to exceed 15 people, while the trade unions wish to participate in the dialogue with a team that includes 40 people. A psychological war has started in front of the government headquarters. The first demand voiced at Victoria Palace [government headquarters] is that the outside yard in front of the government building should be cleared, or the government will be compelled to resort to shield bearers. We protest such methods of intimidation, the more so since the bilateral dialogue has not even started yet, the communique we received from the organization committee of the Alfa Cartel National Trade Union Confederation says.
