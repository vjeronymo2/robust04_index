Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Zhang Huanli (1728 3562 0448)] [Text] Fukuoka, 1 Mar (XINHUA) -- Japan's Fukuoka Prefectural government, the Fukuoka Prefectural Sino-Japanese Friendship Association, and the Kyushu Yamaguchi Economic Association held a grand banquet at the New Otani Hotel in Hakata this evening to warmly welcome Chinese Vice Premier Zhu Rongji and his party on their arrival in Fukuoka. Fukuoka Prefecture Governor Hachiji Okuda gave a welcome speech at the banquet. He said: Fukuoka is an important port in the 2,000-year history of exchanges between Japan and China. It has established friendly relations with China's Jiangsu Province. In addition, four cities in the prefecture have established friendship ties with Chinese cities. The Fukuoka people are ready to take further steps to help develop friendship and cooperation between Japan and China. In his speech, Zhu Rongji praised Fukuoka Prefecture for its relentless efforts to develop Sino-Japanese friendship not only in the past but also at the present. Zhu Rongji said: Sino-Japanese economic and trade relations have developed rapidly in recent years. However, Fukuoka still has great potential for developing economic relations with China. I hope the two sides will work hard to continue to expand cooperation between Fukuoka and China. Before concluding their visit to Osaka, Zhu Rongji and his party also visited Okayama Prefecture, where they visited the Seto Ohashi Bridge. Zhu Rongji praised the ocean bridge for its magnificent construction and attractive layout. He also inquired about the economic benefits obtained from the bridge's construction.
