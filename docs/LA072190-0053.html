July 21, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SHE MIGHT BE A COURT, BUT SOME READERS SAY SHE SHOULD NOT JUDGE

Match point to Mike Downey for his searing, brilliant shot at Margaret Court. I don't know if Martina is 100 times the athlete Jack Nicklaus is, but she is certainly 100 times the human being Court is.

MIKE WERB

Los Angeles
