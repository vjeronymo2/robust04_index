931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / London to Dublin air service planned London City airport yesterday announced the start of its first domestic service. In January VLM, a Belgian airline, will start a twice-daily link to Liverpool using a Fokker 50. The Liverpool service is the first example of a foreign carrier operating a UK domestic service since the liberalisation of the European air transport industry. The Belgian carrier operates services between London City and Antwerp.
