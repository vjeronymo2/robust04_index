BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 9 (KCNA) -- A delegation of the Iran-Korea friendship parliamentary group headed by member of parliament Khodakaram Jalali, head of the Agricultural Commission of the Islamic Consultative Assembly and chairman of the group, arrived here today. It was met at the airport by Chairman of the Korea-Iran friendship parliamentary group and Vice-Chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly Paek In-chun, Secretary General of the group and Deputy to the Supreme People's Assembly Yu Kyu-tong and the charge d'affaires ad interim of the Iranian Embassy in Pyongyang. A delegation of the Tanzanian Youth Union headed by its General Secretary Sukuwa S. Sukuwa arrived here today.
