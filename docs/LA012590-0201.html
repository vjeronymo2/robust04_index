January 25, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

GARDEN GROVE LEAGUE ROUNDUP

In other league games:

Garden Grove 40, Santiago 33 -- Mark Perry scored 15 points for host Garden Grove (7-10, 4-4).

Mike Corrasco had nine points for Santiago.

Bolsa Grande 65, Los Amigos 56 -- John Miller had 17 points and Andre Robinson added 15 in a losing effort for Los Amigos (1-14, 0-8). Bolsa Grande improved to 6-9 and 4-4.

Pacifica 81, La Quinta 40 -- Forward Scott Lesser led Pacifica (8-8, 6-2) with 21 points. Center Jeff Sogoian led La Quinta (0-16, 0-8) with 10 points.
