920128 FT 28 JAN 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG closed narrowly mixed in dull trading. The all-gold index closed up 15 at 1,273, as world bullion prices held steady: Southvaal gained R1.50 to R72.50. The industrial index shed 8 to 4,428, while the all-share index eased 3 to 3,623.
