Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the BTA press review] [Text] Sofia, February 1 (BTA) -- DEMOKRATSIYA and OTECHESTVEN VESTNIK carry front-page reports on the detention of a Bulgarian ship carrying oil by an embargo patrol of the Western European Union [WEU] near Calafat, Romania. The tugboat Khan Krum and three barges carried 5,000 tonnes of diesel fuel, the dailies say. The convoy tried to pass by the port of Ruse with its lights off, obviously attempting to reach the Serbian port upstream, DEMOKRATSIYA, the daily of the Union of the Democratic Forces (SDS), reports. According to the daily, the boat was hired by Multigroup. This is not the first time that this financial grouping is named in connection with a sanctions-busting attempt, the two dailies write.
