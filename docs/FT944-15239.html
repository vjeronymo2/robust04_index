941019 FT 19 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Ldn & Strathclyde London & Strathclyde Trust raised net asset value by 8p to 266.8p per share over the year to August 31. After-tax revenue increased from Pounds 816,000 to Pounds 881,000 in the 12 months and earnings per share came to 6.03p (5.58p). The proposed final dividend is held at 4.25p for a same-again total of 5.75p.
