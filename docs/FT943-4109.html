940910 FT 10 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Towles/London City Towles, the Loughborough-based textile maker, has been acquired by London City Equities, the Australian company which was already Towles' largest shareholder. The purchase became effective following a shareholders meeting of London City in Australia and waiver of final conditions. This brings to an end a three month takeover bid which was initially opposed but ultimately recommended by the Towles board.
