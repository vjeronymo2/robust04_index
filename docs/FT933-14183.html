930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Aberforth Split Level assets up 22% Aberforth Split Level Trust, which invests in small UK quoted companies, had a net asset value of 239.7p per capital share at June 30, up almost 22 per cent over the year. Available revenue amounted to Pounds 1.27m (Pounds 1.43m) for earnings of 8.43p (9.56p) per income share. A lower final dividend of 2.4p reduces the total for the year to 8.4p (9p).
