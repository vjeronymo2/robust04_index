BFN [Report attributed to RIA] [Text] For the first time within the CIS framework, the drafting of a general concept has started, providing for the protection of the external borders of the CIS using the means and resources of the entire commonwealth. This has been told to an RIA correspondent by General Colonel Andrey Nikolayev, Russian Federation Border Troops commander in chief. According to him, the concept envisions a system of mutual commitments of the [CIS] states in providing security of both their own national borders and the borders of other countries. Protection of the borders will be maintained in a coordinated manner.
