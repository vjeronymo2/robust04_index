November 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

American President Lines, Oakland, has named Peter Young, formerly senior vice president-North America, as senior vice president-sales and marketing.

Keith A. Mackie, formerly vice president-sales and marketing-North America, has been named vice president-Western region. Michael Morris, formerly vice president-logistics and operations-North America, has been named vice president-logistics. Eugene K. Pentimonti, formerly vice president-engineering and technical services, has been named vice president-processes and systems.
