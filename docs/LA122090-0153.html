December 20, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

YOUTHS TO PERFORM IN 'LAS POSADAS'

The Escuela de Nuevo Canto and the Long Beach Council of the League of United Latin American Citizens will present "Las Posadas Navidenas" from 4 to 7 p.m. on Saturdayat Drake Park, 951 Maine Ave., Long Beach.

"Las Posadas" will include music, songs and a theatrical presentation performed by Latino youths. The singers are from the Escuela de Nuevo Canto, a group of families interested in developing the cultural, linguistic, musical and performing talents of their children. The theater performers are from the city of Long Beach Gang Prevention Program.

The celebration will include refreshments and pinatas. For more information, call Irma Archuleta at 492-6585 or Tonia Reyes Uranga at 492-6478.
