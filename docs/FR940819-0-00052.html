§135.249 Use of prohibited drugs. * * * * * (c) No certificate holder or operator shall knowingly use any person to perform, nor shall any person perform for a certificate holder or operator, either directly or by contract, any safety-sensitive function if the person has a verified positive drug test result on or has refused to submit to a drug test required by appendix I to part 121 of this chapter and the person has not met the requirements of appendix I to part 121 of this chapter for returning to the performance of safety-sensitive duties. Issued in Washington, DC, on August 12, 1994. David R. Hinson, Administrator. Note: These exhibits will not appear in the Code of Federal Regulations.
