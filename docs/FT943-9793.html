940809 FT 09 AUG 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Radius RADIUS is acquiring Avalon Systems, a Leicester-based computer software company. The total consideration of Pounds 848,000 is to be satisfied by the issue of 547,945 Radius shares at 36.5p each, loan notes to the value of Pounds 250,000 and the balance in cash out of Radius's resources.
