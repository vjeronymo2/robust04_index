941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Rubicon expands with Pounds 2m buy Rubicon Group, the precision engineer, has agreed to acquire 82 per cent of Strathclyde, a manufacturer of precision metalwork and assemblies for the electronics industry. The Pounds 2m consideration is to be satisfied with Pounds 446,038 in cash and the issue of 1.16m ordinary shares. The cash element will be raised by the issue of 330,400 shares. Rubicon has an option to acquire the outstanding 18 per cent next May for Pounds 435,733 cash. Strathclyde's pre-tax profits for the year to April 30 were Pounds 363,000 on turnover of Pounds 8.82m.
