BFN [Text] Police of Ba Ria-Vung Tau recently started prosecution of a smuggling case concerning a ship, the Hoang Giang. The ship belongs to the Vietsovpetro joint venture enterprise, and was on its way back from a regular repair trip in Singapore to Vung Tau. During an administrative inspection, the customs and police forces of the province discovered a large amount of merchandise that had not been declared to customs and on which taxes had not been paid. The lot contains 22 types of items worth over 1 billion dong. This smuggling case discovered at the sea port of Vung Tau is a major one.
