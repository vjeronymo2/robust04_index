BFN [Text] David Mishli, a 30-year-old resident of Tel Aviv, was killed, apparently with an iron bar, in his launderette in the Shapira neighborhood today. Police say their main suspect is Sa'idi Khalil Jabr, a Palestinian who cooperated with Israeli authorities and was given permission to live in the south Tel Aviv neighborhood. They say he had announced he would do something to return to the ranks of the Fatah organization.
