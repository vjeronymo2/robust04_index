921106 FT 06 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Finsbury Growth net assets lower Finsbury Growth Trust, which concentrates its portfolio in larger UK companies, saw its net asset value decline from 98.7p to 85.6p over the 12 months to September 30. The trust, formerly Scottish Cities Investment Trust, acquired Anglo-Scandinavian Investment Trust during the year and subsequently restructured its capital. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 1.41m (Pounds 948,000) for earnings of 3.14p (restated 2.79p) per share. A proposed final dividend of 2p brings the total for the year to 2.9p.
