Language: Tajik Article Type:BFN [Text] Imamali Rakhmonov, head of state and chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tajikistan, yesterday met Ramiro Piriz-Baloon, UN secretary general's special envoy in the Republic of Tajikistan. Ramiro Piriz-Baloon handed over to Imamali Rakhmonov a letter from UN Secretary General Butrus-Ghali. The letter supports the measures taken by the leadership of Tajikistan to consolidate peace and promote national reconciliation. Issues related to further stabilizing the situation in the country were discussed in the course of the meeting. Rashid Olimov, minister of foreign affairs of the republic, and Karimjon Yuldoshev, state adviser to the head of state, took part in the meeting.
