940621 FT 21 JUN 94 / Collapsed bank in further payout Depositors and creditors of Edington, the Manchester merchant bank which closed in 1991, are to get a further payout of Pounds 3.4m, 12p in the pound. This brings the total so far to Pounds 26.7m, 97p in the pound. Mr Philip Ramsbottom of KPMG Peat Marwick, one of the administrators, said there was a possibility of a final distribution.
