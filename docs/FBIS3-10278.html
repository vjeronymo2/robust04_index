Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "This Morning" newscast] [Text] Troops from the rebel 3-80 Northern Front began demobilizing 2 March. An initial group of 100 men will become the guardians of public order in the municipalities of Quilali, Wiwili, Murra, and Jicaro, following an intensive training process that will begin 7 March at the Manauga Police Academy. The demobilization of a second group of the 3-80 Northern Front is scheduled for 16 March. At that time, another 220 men will demobilize, which, together with the initial group, will add up to 50 percent of the rebel troops. The last contingent will demobilize on 16 April, at which time the rebel leader, Jose Angel Talavera, alias El Chacal, will also disarm.
