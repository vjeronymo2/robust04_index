December 17, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: GARDEN GROVE;

NEGOTIATIONS BEGIN ON HOUSING PLAN

The City Council has taken the first step toward the construction of a three-story senior citizen housing project.

In a unanimous vote, council members last week approved a six-month negotiating agreement with Axiom Development & Investments Inc. of Van Nuys for a proposed 195-unit building with an adjacent 70,000-square-foot medical office building.

The project would be on six acres on the east side of Palm Street, between Aspenwood Lane and Garden Grove Boulevard.

The council's vote begins a negotiating process between the developer and the city toward a binding development agreement.

The council also has named Mayor W.E. (Walt) Donovan chairman of the redevelopment agency, and named newly elected Councilman Mark Leyes vice chairman.
