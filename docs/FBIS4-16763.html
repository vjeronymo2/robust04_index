BFN [by ITAR-TASS] [Text] Moscow April 19 TASS -- "We, Cossacks, a people of Russia, have always served for the interests of the Russian state, protected the inviolability of its borders and been a guarantor of internal stability of society. "A meeting of atamans of the Union of Troops of Russia and abroad held on April 16 in the historical capital of Cossack troops - the town of Novocherkassk, approved the draft agreement on achievement of civic accord in Russia," says a statement of the Union of Russian and Foreign Cossack Troops sent to Russian President Boris Yeltsin. The Union of Cossack Troops of Russia and abroad is ready to sign the agreement on "the basis of mutual respect to different outlooks, political convictions, ethnic and cultural traditions and human rights, and carry its share of responsibility for the implementation of the agreement," says the document. The statement was reported by the Russian president staff's information center.
