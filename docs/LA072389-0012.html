July 23, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE GODDESS THEORY

Regardless of the academic validity of Marija Gimbutas' studies, a case can be made for the benefits of a female ruler of a culture ("The Goddess Theory," by Jacques Leslie, June 11). The female, as carrier of future generations, would be more concerned with promoting a peaceful environment. Perhaps this kind of thinking may lead some brave women to become presidential candidates. It's about time.

LINDA UMSTEAD

Long Beach
