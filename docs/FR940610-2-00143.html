NATIONAL ENDOWMENT FOR THE ARTS Expansion Arts Advisory Panel, Panel Meeting Cancellation; Amended Notice of Meeting ACTION: Notice of correction to Federal Register Notice regarding the Overview Section of the Expansion Arts Advisory Panel to the National Council on the Arts.

SUMMARY: This notice to announce the cancellation of the above panel meeting previously scheduled to meet on June 16, 1994 and published in Federal Register 59 FR 28567 on June 2, 1994. EXPLANATION: Current new guidelines cover a two-year period, therefore there will be no opportunity for field input this session. Additionally, the Expansion Arts Program has no significant field data, new initiatives, or personnel action to report. Cancellation is deemed both prudent and cost-saving at this time.
