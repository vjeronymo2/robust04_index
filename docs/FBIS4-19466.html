BFN [Excerpt] The Nation Party [NP], established in 1992, joined the National Unity Party [NUP] today. NP deputy leader Esref Dusenkalkan informed the NUP officials of the decision reached by the NP Central Administrative Council on 15 April, 1994. Addressing a meeting on the occasion, NUP leader Dervis Eroglu said that he is proud to be uniting with the NP which shares his party's view that the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus is an indivisible unity together with its people and its territory.
