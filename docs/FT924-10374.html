921031 FT 31 OCT 92 / Markets: Fillip for smaller companies - At a glance Smaller company shares have continued to benefit from the more cheerful tone of the markets, in the wake of recent interest rate cuts. The Hoare Govett Index (capital gains version) rose 0.9 per cent over the week to October 29 from 1075.56 to 1085.44. Meanwhile the County Index also rose 0.9 per cent from 831.03 to to 838.71 over the same period.
