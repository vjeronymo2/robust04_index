BFN [Text] The Israeli occupation forces brought 175-mm and 203-mm artillery pieces via Metulla to the eastern sector of the occupied border strip today. The Israeli forces installed two 175-mm guns in the al-Majidiyah area and two 203-mm guns in the Sahl al-Miri area. A National News Agency correspondent said today that the occupation forces have constructed the biggest military base ever built inside the strip in the Mash'arun area at the southern entrance of Tallusah in the Marj 'Uyun District. The occupation forces destroyed 150 dunums of plantation land and erected earth barriers and concrete fortifications there.
