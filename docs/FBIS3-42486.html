Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [ITAR-TASS Article: "Fire on a Northern Fleet Nuclear Submarine"] [Text] A fire occurred on a Northern Fleet nuclear submarine during the evening last Tuesday on the territory of a military ship repair plant in Murmansk Oblast. An ITAR-TASS correspondent has learned that the fire broke out during welding work on the nuclear submarine that was being prepared to be written off as obsolete. The fire was extinguished, there were no injuries, and the radiation situation was not violated.
