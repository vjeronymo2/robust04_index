December 20, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

GIRLS' BASKETBALL ROUNDUP;

UNBEATEN ESTANCIA WINS BOLSA GRANDE TOURNAMENT

The Estancia High School girls' basketball team ran its unbeaten streak to nine games, defeating Trabuco Hills, 73-48, in the finals of the Bolsa Grande Tournament Wednesday.

The Eagles took a 36-19 halftime lead on the shooting of Melody Earle, the tournament's Most Valuable Player, and forwards Patrice Lumpkin and Monique Sweet.

Estancia led, 56-29, late in the third quarter before Trabuco Hills made a 10-0 run, but the Eagles went on their own 14-0 run early in the fourth quarter.

Lumpkin has 21 points, Earle 20 and Sweet 18. Estancia shot 44.7% from the field and its man-to-man defense held Trabuco Hills (5-3) to 39.2%.

In other Bolsa Grande tournament games:

Capistrano Valley 49, Bolsa Grande 39 -- Angie White had 18 points for Capistrano Valley (6-2) in the third-place game. Judy Valenzuela had 15 for Bolsa Grande (4-4).

Newport Harbor 77, Laguna Hills 38 -- Center Maureen McLaren had 20 points for Newport Harbor (4-5) in a consolation game. FERNANDO DOMINGUEZ
