920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / UK Company News: IBC returns to black after restructuring International Business Communications (Holdings), the publishing and conferences company, returned to profits in the first half of 1992, the first figures to reflect the effects of restructuring in January. On turnover slightly lower at Pounds 27.5m (Pounds 27.9m) pre-tax profits were Pounds 572,000, against losses of Pounds 2.44m. Operating profits rose 51 per cent to Pounds 3.28m (Pounds 2.18m) and interest charges fell to Pounds 1.28m (Pounds 3.57m) as a result of the restructuring. Earnings were 0.2p (losses 1.9p). No dividend can be paid because of accumulated losses.
