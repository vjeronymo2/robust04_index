940108 FT 08 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Glynwed buys RTZ Metal Stockholders Glynwed, the Birmingham-based engineering group, is extending its European metals distribution business with the purchase of RTZ Metal Stockholders from RTZ, the minerals group. It would not disclose the purchase price but said that RTZ Metal Stockholders had a net asset value of Pounds 5.3m. Glynwed recently sold its metals distribution business in the US.
