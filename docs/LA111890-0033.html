November 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

A TICKET TO BOOGIE

Re Lisa Wolfenbarger's Oct. 21 letter (which complained about people blocking the view at rock concerts): You're damn right any ticket I buy to a rock concert entitles me to boogie till the cows come home. Real rock 'n' roll is a passionate form of music that rarely calls for sitting on your keister.

If you're too old (in your heart) to rock 'n' roll, please stay home! Or go see Barry Manilow next time.

PEG POLLARD

Rancho Bernardo
