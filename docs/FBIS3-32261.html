Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Guatemala City PRENSA LIBRE in Spanish on 27 January 94, page 105 reports that official sources disclosed that on 26 January the Inter-American Development Bank (IDB) granted $132 million in credit to the Guatemalan Government to improve solvency of the financial system and increase access to credit. Edmond Mulet, Guatemalan ambassador to the United States, and IDB President Enrique Iglesias signed the agreement.
