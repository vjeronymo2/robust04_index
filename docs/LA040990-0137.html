April 9, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BASEBALL '90 PREVIEW;

DODGER HOME GUIDE

Home park: Dodger Stadium

-- Location: 1000 Elysian Park Ave., Los Angeles, 90012

-- Phone number: 213-224-1500

-- Seating capacity: 56,000

-- Year completed: 1962

-- 1989 attendance: 2,944,653

-- Number of 1989 home dates: 81

-- 1989 Per game Average: 36,354

-- Largest crowd, 1989: 48,493 (April 23 vs. San Francisco)

-- Record crowd, regular season: 55,185 (July 28, 1973 vs.

San Francisco)

-- Home run fences: Left and right fields 330 feet; left- and right-center 385; center 395. Height of fences is 8 feet.

-- Ticket office hours: 8:30 a.m.-5:30 p.m. Monday through Friday at the stadium and at the Advance ticket office: 1750 Stadium Way, adjacent to Naval-Marine Center.

-- Telephone orders: Call Dodger ticket office at (213) 224-1400. Teletron at (213) 410-1062, (818) 763-9101 or (714) 634-1300. Tickets can be charged on Visa or Mastercard. Tickets ordered 10 days before a game will be mailed to customer. Tickets ordered after that time will be available on game day at the Will Call window.

-- Mail orders: Orders must be received at least seven days before the game. Include $3 per order for postage and handling. Mail orders to Los Angeles Dodgers, PO Box 51100, Los Angeles, CA 90051.

-- Ticketron: Tickets can be purchased at Tower Record stores and several other locations. Call 213-642-4242 for information.

-- Ticket prices:

Box seats $9.00

Reserved $7.00

General Admission $5.00

Children* $3.00

* Children's general admission tickets (12 and under) available two hours before games. Club level seating, shown in the stadium diagram, is not available for general purchase.
