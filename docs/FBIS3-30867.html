Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, Feb 7 -- Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak on Monday received Russian Interior Minister Viktor Yerin. The meeting was attended by Interior Minister Andrzej Milczanowski, the government press office announced. The talks focused on cooperation between Polish and Russian Interior Ministries which had resulted in some improvement in effectiveness of fighting crime. The two sides stressed the need to share experiences in the field of tactics and techniques used by police in Poland and Russia, the communique of the press office reads.
