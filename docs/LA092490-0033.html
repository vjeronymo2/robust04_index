September 24, 1990, Monday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLE WATCH

Mercouri Undaunted: Melina Mercouri remains undeterred in her quest to return the Olympics to Greece.

The movie star-turned-politician, who's running for mayor of Athens, sounded a rallying cry for Greeks despondent over losing the 1996 games to Atlanta. "If I am mayor, I will seek Athens' candidacy to host the Olympics in the year 2000," she said over the weekend. "I have the feeling that the whole of Europe will support us." GREG BRAXTON
