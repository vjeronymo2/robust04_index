BFN [Text] Following a meeting with Forza Italia leader Silvio Berlusconi and National Alliance leader Gianfranco Fini, Northern League leader Umberto Bossi was asked if he would reiterate his demands for the Interior Ministry. Bossi answered: "Of course, even if we are going to discuss this next week. Today we discussed programs." He said that agreement has been reached over the program, and that problems might arise when we get round to filling the ministerial posts. Bossi left the meeting certain that Berlusconi and Fini have accepted his proposals concerning federalism.
