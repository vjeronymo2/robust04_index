930407 FT 07 APR 93 / UK Company News: Epwin advances by 6.5% to Pounds 3.1m Epwin Group, a maker of PVCu windows and doors, increased pre-tax profits by 6.5 per cent for 1992, from Pounds 2.91m to Pounds 3.1m. In value terms the market shrank by about 15 per cent over the year but the group's turnover improved to Pounds 43m (Pounds 40.3m), said Mr Jim Rawson, chairman. Earnings were 11.8p (12.3p) reflecting the increased capital resulting from the rights issue in March 1992. A proposed final dividend of 4.7p makes a total of 6.8p (6.6p).
