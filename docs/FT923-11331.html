920725 FT 25 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Pendragon expands dealerships Pendragon, a distributor of luxury and executive cars, has increased its Jaguar franchises in the UK to five and its Land Rover franchises to four through the acquisition for Pounds 2.8m cash of Stratstone, the central London dealer. Stratstone has showrooms in Mayfair and the City; its annual turnover is some Pounds 21m. Pendragon has also agreed with Mercedes-Benz UK to set up a new greenfield dealership in Wakefield, starting operations in October. It will then trade from four Mercedes-Benz passenger car dealerships.
