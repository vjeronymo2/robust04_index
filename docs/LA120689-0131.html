December 6, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ANGELS WILL OPEN APRIL 3 IN SEATTLE

The Angels will open the 1990 season at Seattle on Tuesday night, April 3, and entertain the Mariners in their home opener six days later, it was announced Tuesday.

The Angels will play three games at Seattle and three at Oakland against the defending World Series champion Athletics before playing the Mariners in their first home game on Monday, April 9, at 6 p.m.

The Angels' first homestand will have three games against the Mariners, four against the Minnesota Twins and two against the Athletics.
