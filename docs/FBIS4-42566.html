BFN [Text] Turkish President Suleyman Demirel has sent a letter to President Konstandinos Karamanlis. Government Spokesman Evangelos Venizelos confirmed that the letter has been sent, but he is not yet aware of its contents. The letter reportedly refers to issues of the Workers Party of Kurdistan. Referring to Turkish claims that Kurdish guerrillas are being trained in Greece, the spokesman explicitly reiterated that such claims are slanderous and unfounded. He added that the Greek position is well-known, and mentioned the case of the Kurd who was arrested in Turkey and, after being tortured, supposedly confessed about the training of Kurds in Greece.
