940324 FT 24 MAR 94 / Health service told to switch funds Mr John Redwood has asked the National Health Service in Wales to switch Pounds 12.5m a year from management and administrative costs to patient care. The Welsh secretary, who has previously criticised the number of 'grey suits' in the NHS, said yesterday: 'I will do what I can to cut the paperwork and divert money and manpower from administration to health care.'
