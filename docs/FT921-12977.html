920123 FT 23 JAN 92 / International Company News: Profit doubts at ICI Australia ICI Australia, the chemicals and pharmaceuticals company, cannot confidently predict whether the current year's profit will be a significant improvement on the 1991 result, Mr Milton Bridgland, chairman, said yesterday. Sales for the three months to December were 3 per cent down on the same period last year, Reuter reports. He said the immediate outlook was dominated by the uncertainty of both overseas and home economies.
