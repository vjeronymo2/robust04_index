Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over still photograph of Foreign Minister Cetin] [Text] Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin telephoned Bosnia-Herzegovina Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic to extend condolences to him. Cetin asked for information about the incident and exchanged views on the things that can be done against the Serbian attacks. Cetin said they agree with Silajdzic that NATO must definitely intervene.
