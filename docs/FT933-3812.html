930911 FT 11 SEP 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Black pepper prices advanced further, reports Man Producten, as replacement levels continued to climb. And there was more demand for decorticating black into white. White pepper prices stabilised at high levels. European stocks have dwindled as demand from the US has intensified. White: Muntok/Sarawak faq for September/October delivery was quoted at Dollars 3,900 a tonne, with spot supplies at Dollars 4,000. Black: Sarawak black label spot was Dollars 1,950 a tonne and for September/October Dollars 1,925. Yellow label spot was Dollars 2,075a tonne, and September/October Dollars 2,000. Brazilian grade 1 spot was offered at Dollars 1,950 a tonne.
