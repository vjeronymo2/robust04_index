931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Glenchewton deficit deepens to Pounds 463,000 at midway LOSSES at Glenchewton, the toys, housewares and basketware group, widened from Pounds 242,000 to Pounds 463,000 pre-tax for the six months to end-June. However, the previous deficit, which was adjusted to comply with FRS 3, included litigation costs recovered amounting to Pounds 247,000. Turnover of continuing operations rose from Pounds 6.42m to Pounds 7.3m. And with sales weighted to the second half directors said they were confident they would be able to report a 'healthy operating profit' for the second six months. Net interest payable declined to Pounds 49,000 (Pounds 174,000). Losses per share increased to 2.09p (1.37p). Sir Victor Garland, chairman, said the initial focus would be to improve and optimise the efficiency and growth of the group's current operations.
