BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 1 (KCNA) -- Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam sent a message of greetings to Mohamed Salah Dembri upon his reappointment as foreign minister of the Algerian Democratic and People's Republic. The message expresses the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries will develop on good terms, and wishes the Algerian foreign minister success in his responsible work.
