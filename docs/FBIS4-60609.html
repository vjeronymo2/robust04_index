BFN [Text] Yasir 'Arafat is to arrive on the 12th of next month for a three-day visit to Jericho and Gaza. A message to this effect has been relayed by 'Arafat's advisers to the Israeli Government. Our correspondent notes that 'Arafat postponed his visit by one week due to Israel's demand that he give at least two weeks advance notice of his arrival. According to Palestinian sources, Israel is not allowing 'Arafat to visit East Jerusalem and the Temple Mount. During his visit in Jericho 'Arafat will meet U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher, who is scheduled to return to our region.
