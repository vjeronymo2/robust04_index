930318 FT 18 MAR 93 / Thorn's 17p deferred payment to Thames Thorn-EMI, the parent company of Thames Television, is to pay an additional 17p per share to Thames' shareholders who accepted the leisure group's 1991 offer for the company which then held the London weekday franchise. It is second of two contingent payments and represents 1p for each Pounds 1m by which the net UK television advertising revenue of Thames, and its subsidiaries, exceeded Pounds 240m, for the year ended December 31 1991.
