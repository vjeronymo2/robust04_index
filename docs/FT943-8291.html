940818 FT 18 AUG 94 / Scots exports up Sales overseas of manufactured goods from Scotland rose 11 per cent in value in the second quarter of 1994 compared with the previous quarter, the SCDI/Scottish Enterprise index of Scottish exports shows. The index, based at 100 in the first quarter of 1993, rose from 114.33 in the first quarter to 127.4. Scotland's leading manufactured exports are computers and whisky.
