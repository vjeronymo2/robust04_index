BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Spirin] [Text] Beijing, 13 June -- Estonian President Lennart Meri and a delegation of Estonian businessmen have arrived in Beijing on an official visit at the invitation of the Chinese President Jiang Zemin. ITAR-TASS learned today from well-informed sources that at the forthcoming talks with Chinese leaders, L. Meri will discuss the prospects for bilateral cooperation, primarily in trade and sea transport, and international problems, including China's relations with Estonia and the CIS. A meeting of the heads of state is also expected today. L. Meri will spend seven days in China.
