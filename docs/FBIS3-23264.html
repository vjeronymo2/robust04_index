Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Alexandria, 9 Mar (MENA)--The Alexandria security authorities have arrested fugitive terrorist Sabir 'Abbas 'Abd-al- Rahman, alias 'Isam, a construction worker, in his hideout in the al-Dakhilah al-Jabal area west of Alexandria. The terrorist escaped from Asyut 20 days ago. He is charged in connection with several terrorism cases.
