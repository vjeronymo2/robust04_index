930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / Parliament and Politics: Campaigning Newbury Tory candidate Tory candidate Julian Davidson and his party chairman Sir Norman Fowler taking a break during campaigning in Newbury yesterday. Mr David Rendel, the Liberal Democrat candidate, appears to have gained support in some traditionally staunch Tory areas. Both the Conservatives and the Liberal Democrats are predicting a close result in today's by-election. Labour meanwhile is confining itself to expressing an expectation that the Tories will lose, insisting that such an outcome would not herald the end of the two-party system
