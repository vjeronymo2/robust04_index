Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Simferopol, 30 Jan -- Yuriy Meshkov, the chairman of the Republican Party of Crimea, who won today's presidential election, held a news conference this afternoon at the Russian-speaking Movement of Crimea headquarters. He said that a referendum in favor of Crimea's independence within a union with other states will mean that the Republic of Crimea will return to the Constitution of 6 May 1992, according to Article Nine of which the Republic of Crimea remains a part of Ukraine on a confederation basis.
