Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Unscheduled announcement] [Text] In the name of God, the merciful, the compassionate. To the righteous soul will be said: O thou soul, in complete rest and satisfaction! Come back thou to Thy Lord, well-pleased thyself, and well-pleasing unto Him. Enter thou, then, among my devotees! Yea, enter thou my heaven! [Koranic verses] With resignation to God's will, and with immense sorrow and sadness, President Hafiz al-Asad announces to the people the death of his lost one, which is a loss to the dear homeland, Staff Major and Engineer Paratrooper Basil al-Asad, who passed away on the morning of Friday, 10 Sha'ban 1414 Hegira, corresponding to 21 January 1994, as a result of a tragic accident. His pure body will be laid to rest in al-Qardahah after the noon prayers on Saturday 11 Sha'ban 1414 Hegira, corresponding to 22 January 1994. From God we come and to Him we return.
