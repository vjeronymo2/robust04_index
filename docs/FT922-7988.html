920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Hartlepools Water advances to Pounds 1.24m Hartlepools Water Company yesterday reported a 29 per cent rise in annual pre-tax profits, from Pounds 960,000 to Pounds 1.24m. Directors plan to raise the dividend for the year to March 31 from 43p to 50p via a final of 29p. The company commented that although metered demand had been falling it was expected to stabilise at the present level. Turnover rose 7 per cent to Pounds 5.09m (Pounds 4.77m); earnings per stock unit increased to 117p (85p).
