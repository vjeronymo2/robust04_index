Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The major robbery case relating to a gang of hooligans, that had once caused a sensation throughout the province, was publicly tried by the Benxi city intermediate people's court on 26 February. The 18 criminal offenders relating to this case were respectively sentenced to death and set terms of imprisonment. To the neglect of the state law, criminals, including (Zhang Hengju), hurt the people with tools, robbed the people of their property and materials, and raped women. They earned the bitter hatred of the people. The Benxi city people's court sentenced (Zhang Hengju), the principal culprit, to death and deprived him of his lifelong political rights. (Hu Guangfa) and (Fan Pingjun), both repeat offenders, were sentenced to imprisonment for life and deprived of their lifelong political rights. (Li Yucheng), (Ying Yaoming), (Li Yan), and (Li Xue), also repeat offenders, were sentenced to imprisonment for 18 years or less. After the trial, principal culprit (Zhang Hengju) was escorted to the execution ground and executed by shooting.
