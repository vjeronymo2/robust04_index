December 10, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

GRAND OPENING: CHATELAINE HOMES IN FONTANA

Chatelaine at Southridge will celebrate its grand opening this weekend in Fontana.

Twenty-four of 94 detached homes, all with wood-burning fireplaces, are available. The homes feature formal entries and ceramic-tiled kitchens fitted with oak cabinets and breakfast nooks.

The master suites have luxury bathrooms with oval tubs and dual sinks.

All homes have central air conditioning and rear and side yard fencing. Lawns have sprinkler systems and many of the two-car garages have a storage or hobby area.

The homes have 2 to 4 bedrooms and 2 to 2 1/2 baths. They range in size from 882 to 1,843 square feet and in price from $123,990 to $170,990. They will be available for May move-ins.

The sales office at 15475 Castle Road is open Wednesday through Sunday from 9 a.m. to 9 p.m. and Mondays and Tuesdays from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
