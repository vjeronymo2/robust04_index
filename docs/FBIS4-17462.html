BFN [Text] The press service of the Ukrainian Defense Ministry today denied Moscow's ITAR-TASS report that the position of the Ukrainian side at the negotiations in Sevastopol allegedly did not correspond with the spirit of constructiveness characteristic of the approach of the Russian side to the negotiations, and also that representatives of the Ukrainian Defense Ministry and Navy were allegedly trying to disavow the Moscow agreement of 15 April. The Defense ministry press service also stated that the ITAR-TASS report does not conform to reality and that some of the mass media distorted the undeniable fact that the Black Sea Fleet belongs to two states, rather than to Russia alone. As for the reference to the fact that the position of the Ukrainian side with regard to a Ukrainian Navy base in Sevastopol contravenes military theory, the Defense Ministry press service is authorized to state that Ukraine has its own military doctrine and follows its requirements aimed at ensuring state independence.
