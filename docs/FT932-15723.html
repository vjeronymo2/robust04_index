930410 FT 10 APR 93 / UK Company News: MEPC sells office building for Pounds 52.5m MEPC, the UK's second largest property group, has sold an office building opposite London's Victoria Station to an overseas investor for Pounds 52.5m, a net yield of about 10 per cent. The 167,258 sq ft building at 76 Buckingham Palace Road, is let to BP Chemicals on a 35 year lease, which started in 1978.
