940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Brazilian stocks forged ahead 7.4 per cent in heavy trade as investors returned to the market and bought state blue chip issues on renewed confidence that the government will have its economic plan approved by congress. The Bovespa index ended at 74,056, up 5,134 points. Volume was Cr99bn (USDollars 215.7m).
