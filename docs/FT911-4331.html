910419 FT 19 APR 91 / Record photo finish CHRISTIE'S record auction price for a photograph of Dollars 132,000 (Pounds 74,074) lasted just 24 hours. On Wednesday in New York Sotheby's set a new high of Dollars 165,000. It was paid by a Californian private collector for a platinum print of 1925, Roses, Mexico, taken by Tina Modotti.
