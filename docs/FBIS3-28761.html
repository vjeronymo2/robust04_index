Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 9 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received a letter on February 5 from the participants in the inaugural meeting of the Liaison Council of Japanese Societies for the Study of the Works of Kim Chong-il. The letter informs him that the Liaison Council of Japanese Societies for the Study of the Works of Kim Chong-il was formed following the inauguration of the first society for the study of works named after the august name of Comrade Kim Chong-il in Tokyo in March last year. Noting that mankind was, at last, able to vigorously advance the era of independence thanks to the chuche idea founded by President Kim Il-song, the letter says: "Your excellency enjoys warm support from the world people as a leader guiding the people's cause of independence to victory by carrying forward and further developing the revolutionary cause of President Kim Il-song. "Convinced that to follow the ideology and theory advanced by your excellency is to open the history of shaping a new era, confidently looking ahead to the 21st century, we declared here today the inauguration of the Liaison Council of Japanese Societies for the Study of the Works of Kim Chong-il. "The process of inauguration of the Liaison Council of Japanese Societies for the Study of the Works of Kim Chong-il was a process in which the relations of the popular masses cooperating with each other and linked by love and trust were expanding throughout the society. "We once again extend heartfelt thanks to Your Excellency Kim Chong-il for providing an ideological and theoretical guideline for the inauguration of the Liaison Council of Japanese Societies for the Study of the Works of Kim Chong-il." Noting with great pleasure that the inauguration of the Liaison Council of Japanese Societies for the Study of the Works of Kim Chong-il has become a priceless present on the occasion of the 52nd birthday of Comrade Kim Chong-il, the letter sincerely wishes him a long life in good health.
