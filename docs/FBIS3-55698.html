Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [by ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Rzhevskiy] [Text] Bratislava, 10 Jan -- Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Vitaliy Churkin met Slovak Republic Foreign Minister Jozef Moravcik here today. Questions relating to the development of security structures for Europe were looked at in depth, including NATO, along with the main trends in political and economic relations between European states. After expressing, on the whole, mutual satisfaction with the first year in which links have been developed between Russia and Slovakia, the two spoke in favor of developing them in all ways possible. European integration was discussed during the meeting between V. Churkin and Slovak Deputy Prime Minister Jozef Prokes.
