921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / IMF revokes Yugoslav membership THE International Monetary Fund has revoked Yugoslavia's membership and laid down the conditions for its successor republics to become members, writes George Graham in Washington. After weeks of deliberations on a legal formula, the IMF concluded that Yugoslavia had ceased to exist and had therefore ceased to be a member. The decision lays down the quota of each successor republic, which will also be the share each must accept of the former Yugoslavia's liabilities to the Fund, which amount to about Dollars 217m (Pounds 143m).
