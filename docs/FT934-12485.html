931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / London Stock Exchange: Scotia strong Another stock launching into the market yesterday was Scotia Holdings, the research-based pharmaceuticals group that has financed its growth from products based on evening primrose oil. Strong institutional demand for the shares pushed turnover up to 5.5m. The issue was placed at 290p and immediately jumped to a strong premium at 323p. Some profit-taking then set in and took the shine off, with the shares closing at 316p. Dealers said bullish sentiment surrounds the stock and its strong start is not expected to peter out. One analyst said: 'It has been an excellent debut, with institutions waiting to mop up the shares.'
