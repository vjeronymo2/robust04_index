940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Cussins tops Pounds 1m Cussins Property Group, the residential property developer, reported a 68 per cent advance in pre-tax profits from Pounds 667,000 to Pounds 1.12m for the six months to June 30. Turnover grew to Pounds 11.8m (Pounds 9.46m). Earnings came out at 6.3p (4.2p) per share and the interim dividend is raised to 1.4p (1p).
