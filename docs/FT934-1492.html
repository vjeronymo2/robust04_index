931218 FT 18 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Abtrust Preferred assets improve Abtrust Preferred Income Investment Trust raised net asset value per zero dividend preference share to 132.09p at November 30, against 124.94p six months earlier, and from 99.77p to 121.16p per ordinary income share. After-tax revenue for the half year to end-November more than doubled from Pounds 684,000 last time to Pounds 1.49m and earnings per share grew from 7.59p to 7.86p. A second interim dividend of 2.90625p (same) makes an unchanged 5.8125p to date.
