940331 FT 31 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Roskel Roskel, the suspended ceilings contractor, partitioning and ceilings distribution group, reduced its pre-tax loss to Pounds 699,000 in 1993, against a Pounds 1.2m deficit previously, restated under FRS 3. Sales fell by 3 per cent to Pounds 46.3m (Pounds 47.5m) but gross profit increased to Pounds 11.3m (Pounds 11m). Share of the loss of associated companies increased to Pounds 177,000 (Pounds 54,000). There was a Pounds 1.71m provision related to the sale in October of Access Rental, the lossmaking plant hire arm. An unchanged final dividend of 3p makes a same-again total of 4.3p. On a per share basis the deficit was 6.36p (5.46p).
