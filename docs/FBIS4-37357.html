BFN [Text] Yasir 'Amr, who will be in charge of the education portfolio in the autonomy authority, crossed into Jericho via the Allenby Bridge this morning. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that he is the second member of the autonomy authority to arrive in Jericho in the last two days. 'Amr was preceded by Muhammad al-Nashashibi, who will be in charge of economic affairs. Yasir 'Amr met with General Isma'il al-Hajj, the military commander of the area, and later left for the offices of Sa'ib 'Urayqat who is also a member of the autonomy authority.
