BFN [Text] An additional Katyusha volley was fired at the western Galilee a short time ago. Menahem Horowitz, our correspondent in the north, updates us: [Horowitz] Good evening, about half an hour ago, explosions were heard once again in the western Galilee. Katyushas were fired from Lebanon at the Galilee and the security zone in south Lebanon. In response, IDF [Israel Defense Forces] and SLA [South Lebanese Army] artillery fired at terrorist positions in south Lebanon. IDF guns have shelled terrorist targets there intermittently throughout the day. Many northern border inhabitants are spending the night in shelters and are anticipating a very tense night.
