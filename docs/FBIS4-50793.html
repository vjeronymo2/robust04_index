BFN [By reporter Kang Xinwen (1660 2450 2429)] [Text] Brussels, 21 May (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee, met with staff members of the Chinese Embassy and other organizations in Belgium today. He extended warm greetings to them. Chairman Li Ruihuan briefed the staff members about his current five-nation European tour, as well as new circumstances of reform and opening up at home. He said his visits to Finland, Sweden, Norway, Denmark, and Belgium had been a great success and had attained the objective of enhancing understanding, expanding exchanges, and strengthening cooperation. On the latest situation in the deepening of reform and economic construction at home, he said rapid progress and fruitful achievements had been made in the domestic economic construction. On the same day, Li Ruihuan had a cordial meeting at the Chinese Embassy with representatives of Chinese nationals and students in Belgium.
