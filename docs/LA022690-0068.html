February 26, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

EASY RIDERS: Caltrans urges commuters to share rides, but do its workers? . . . Only 8.2% of its employees here use ride-share programs. Not bad, but local firms with such programs average 10 to 15% participation, says the Orange County Transit District. . . . The head of the county Chamber of Commerce's Transportation Council does practice what he preaches: Jerome C. Premo lives near a bus stop and often takes a bus to work. Dana Parsons and Kristina Lindgren
