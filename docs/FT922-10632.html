920430 FT 30 APR 92 / UK Company News: Bowthorpe has 83% of Penny & Giles Bowthorpe Holdings, the electrical and electronic components maker, has won acceptances from shareholders representing 83.3 per cent of Penny & Giles, the instrument manufacturer. Bowthorpe is offering 136 new shares for every 100 Penny & Giles shares or 1 new share and 84p cash.
