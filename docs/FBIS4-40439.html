BFN [Report by Grigoriy Nesmyanovich: "Ukrainian Officers Union Has Admitted There Will Be Spectacles in Addition to Exercises in the Army"] [Text] Kiev -- The Sixth Congress of the Ukrainian Officers Union has been held here. An appeal to the Ukrainian Supreme Council was prepared at the congress calling for preservation of Ukraine's nuclear status. A number of complaints at the congress were directed at the Ukrainian president and especially at the defense minister. Army General Vitaliy Radetskyy was reproached for the decline of combat readiness and discipline, the increase in the number of military crimes, and for the unprecedented increase in the number of generals and "showcase" exercises, all of which had no parallels in the Soviet period.
