BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] Oleksandr Moroz, chairman of the Ukrainian Supreme Council, has called for the establishment of a joint command of the Ukrainian and Russian Fleets on the Black Sea. Speaking in Donetsk and Simferopol, he also said he opposed a final division of the Black Sea Fleet. To remind you, at the talks on the Black Sea Fleet between Ukraine and Russia, agreement has so far been reached on the division of vessels, and discussions continue on division of the infrastructure of the fleet's bases and on the status of the town of Sevastopol.
