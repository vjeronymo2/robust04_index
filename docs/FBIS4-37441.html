BFN [Text] Baghdad, 20 Jun (INA) -- Official talks between the Iraqi National Assembly and the Yemeni House of Representatives began here today. The Iraqi side to the talks was led by Safa' Salih al-'Umar, chairman of the Arab and International Relations Committee, whereas the Yemeni side was led by House Member Muhammad 'Abdallah al-Kibsi. The heads of the two sides reviewed Arab and international developments, particularly the situation in fraternal Yemen in light of the continued imperialist-Zionist conspiracy that is targeting Yemen's unity. Talks also dealt with ways to coordinate cooperation between the Iraqi National Assembly and the Yemeni House of Representatives in the parliamentary and political fields in a way that will serve both fraternal peoples and benefit the Arab nation in various fields.
