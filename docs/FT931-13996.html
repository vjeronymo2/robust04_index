930120 FT 20 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Receivers continue Lilley sales RECEIVERS to Lilley have announced further progress in selling the businesses and contract assets of companies within the Glasgow-based contracting and construction group. The rights to the contracts of both Henry Jones Construction and Eden Construction have been sold to the Kier Group, while negotiations for the sale of MDW, the Glasgow-based subsidiary, are far advanced and it is anticipated that a deal will be concluded today. Earlier this week, Lilley Construction (Scotland) was sold to Sunley Turriff Holdings in a deal which was described by Sunley, a subsidiary of Lonrho, as a strategic move into civil engineering and into Scotland.
