Language: Kyrgyz Article Type:BFN [Text] On 17 March, the president of Kyrgyzstan, Askar Akayev, arrived on the evening flight in Bishkek. He had been staying near Moscow on leave, and for treatment. At Manas airport he was met by Abbas Dzhumagulov, the prime minister of the Republic; Bakayev, the chairman of the Committee for National Security; Defense Minister Subanov; Interior Minister Sutalinov; and other officials.
