931009 FT 09 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Harrington Kilbride Harrington Kilbride, the publishing group, is raising about Pounds 1.03m net through the issue of 475,000 ordinary 5p shares at 219p. The company, floated in December 1991, will use the proceeds to cut gearing. It said the expanded capital base would enable it to continue investment in both its own titles and its contract publishing division.
