920620 FT 20 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Jessups acquires Ford main dealer Jessups, the Essex-based motor dealer, is acquiring all the issued share capital of Clarkes, a Ford main dealership based in Bristol. Consideration will be satisfied by an issue of 767,532 new ordinary Jessup shares of which 761,839 will be placed on the vendors' behalf with institutional investors at 77p each in return for Pounds 586,000 in cash. For 1991 Clarkes' pre-tax loss was Pounds 215,000 on turnover of Pounds 13.3m. Its net assets at December 31, including associated and other indebtedness which will be repaid to Clarkes by the principal vendors, amounted to Pounds 1.05m.
