920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / The Barcelona Olympics: Popov wins second freestyle title Alexander Popov of the CIS, who toppled Matt Biondi in the 100 metres freestyle on Tuesday, grabbed a second Olympic swimming title from the big American in the men's 50m freestyle. Popov never hurried his long stroke but Biondi could not catch him in the one-length thrash, shortest race in the swimming programme. Popov touched home in 21.91 seconds, with Biondi taking the silver in 22.09 and American world champion Tom Jager the bronze in 22.30. Jager was silver medallist behind Biondi in Seoul. Victory gave the Unified Team golds in all the men's individual freestyle events from 50m to 400m, with Popov victor in the 50m and 100m and Evgeny Sadovyi champion in the 200m and 400m. They also won the 4 x 200m freestyle relay in world record time.
