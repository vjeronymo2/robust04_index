February 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: LIONS PLAN SCHOLARSHIPS FOR FLAG SPEECH WINNERS

Walnut students interested in entering the annual California-Nevada Lions International Student Speakers Contest must submit applications by Feb. 15.

Students will vie for up to $12,000 in scholarships by giving speeches on this year's subject: "What the American Flag Means to Me."

The student speaker contest, hosted by the Walnut Lions Club, will begin at the Walnut City Community Center Feb. 22 at 7 p.m.

Students from Walnut, Del Paso and Nogales high schools should contact Eric Harmon at (714) 598-5065 or Ray Watson at (714) 598-2091. Other high school students should contact Lions District Student Speaker Chairman Rod Wallace at (714) 535-0645 or (714) 738-1496.
