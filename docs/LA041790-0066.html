April 17, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Nearly four months after the Big Ten announced tentative plans to add Penn State as an 11th school, the Chicago Tribune reported that the proposal is encountering opposition from some athletic directors and coaches, especially Rick Bay of Minnesota and Bob Knight of Indiana.

"I've been to Penn State," Knight said recently. "And Penn State's a camping trip. There's nothing for about 100 miles."
