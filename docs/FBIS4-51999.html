BFN [Text] Today Foreign Minister Squadron Leader Prasong Sunsiri received Rupert Von Arney, visiting representative of the UN High Commissioner for Refugees [UNHCR]. They discussed the refugee situation in Thailand. The UNHCR representative expressed satisfaction with the Thai Government's care of the refugees. At the meeting, the foreign minister suggested that the UN establish an area in Cambodia to receive refugees fleeing from the fighting. The UNHCR representative accepted the suggestion for consideration. [Begin recording] [Suwit Simasakun, Foreign Ministry Information Department director, in progress] ...replied that it may be possible to implement the idea. However, it depends on the political situation in Cambodia. This is because neither Khmer side would want the other to have access to its population. In any event, the UNHCR representative accepted the idea for consideration. [end recording]
