Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Report by "D.S.": "Arkan Is Calling Volunteers"] [Text] On the occasion of the NATO ultimatum and the threats of air strikes, Zeljko Raznatovic Arkan, chairman of the Party of Serbian Unity [SSJ], has called on Serbian volunteers "to be prepared to again gather under the Serbian flag." If a single bomb falls on the Serbian positions in Bosnia, it is said in yesterday's announcement, the SSJ will activate the military agreement with Zhirinovskiy's Liberal-Democratic Party and invite Russian volunteers. The SSJ warns that in case the war escalates, it will use the Russian secret weapon [the Elipton], obtained from Zhirinovskiy, against the aggressor's manpower and enemy aircraft.
