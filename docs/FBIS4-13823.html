BFN [Text] This morning, terrorists entered the settlement of Neve Deqalim in the Qatif Bloc and stabbed one of the settlers. She was flown to the Soroka Hospital in Beersheba, where her condition was determined to be moderate and stable. Our correspondent Amir Rapoport reports that residents of the settlement fired at the terrorists, killing one and injuring another. Israel Television correspondent Shlomi Eldar reports that the security forces are currently carrying out an intensive search for additional terrorists who may have taken part in the attack.
