May 5, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

PCC Group Inc.: The Brea-based distributor of computer products and systems reported net income of $215,714 on sales of $19.9 million for the first quarter of fiscal 1990 ended Dec. 31. The comparable figures for fiscal 1989 were not available because the company was reorganizing under Chapter 11 bankruptcy proceedings.
