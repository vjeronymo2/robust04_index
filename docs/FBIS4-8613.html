BFN [Text] On the occasion of the Day of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army, Army General Rasim Delic, supreme commander of the General Staff, has sent Bosnia-Herzegovina Prime Minister a note of congratulations, which, among other things, says: We are convinced that this year's marking of the Day of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army -- when we recall all the difficulties and casualties but also the results we have achieved -- will be a great motivation to all of us to continue the war of liberation in an even more organized and effective way, with dignity and courage to persist in the establishment of a just peace. In that respect, we are aware of the moral obligation we have toward the casualties among the population, the killed fighters, our sehids [Muslim fighters killed in battle], our invalids, and [word indistinct]. I would like to use this opportunity to express my satisfaction and gratitude to you -- your personal stature and results, particularly in the diplomatic sense -- for being a significant force in preserving and strengthening the moral and combat effectiveness of the units and commands of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army. This is said in the note of congratulations from Army General Rasim Delic to Prime Minister Haris Silajdzic on the occasion of 15 April, the day of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army.
