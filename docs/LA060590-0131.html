June 5, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

MONORAIL MYSTIQUE: One "M" with a future might be monorails. . . . Several cities have expressed interest in Irvine's, which will get $125 million if Proposition 116 wins today. . . . The monorail mystique has even made it to the Valley, where backers of a measure to elevate an extension of the L.A. Metrorail have a radio ad that features a commuter dreaming of an imaginary monorail ride. Mark I. Pinsky and Steve Emmons
