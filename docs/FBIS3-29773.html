Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] Concerning the issue of opening up the rice market, Chief Cabinet Secretary Masayoshi Takemura said on 19 February: "I have been pondering whether it is truly desirable to impose tariffs on all agricultural products without exception to protect the global environment." In this way, he disclosed his critical approach toward the government decision on the rice market opening. It has been observed that Takemura ventured to mention his view to indicate his consideration of the Social Democratic Party of Japan, which was negative about the rice market opening.
