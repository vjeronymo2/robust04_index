January 8, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

LENNON LIVES

If your year-end wrap up of major record label activity, Patrick Goldstein and Paul Grein referred to the sound track to "John Lennon: Imagine" as a "stiff" (Pop Eye, Dec. 25).

In actuality, the album has done quite well (recently placing 2 in Tower Records chainwide CD chart) and has just passed the mark for gold record status well after the film has faded away.

More stiffs, please!

TIM DEVINE

Capitol Records

Hollywood

Devine assembled the Lennon sound track.
