BFN [Text] Foreign Minister brother Muhammad Salim Basindwah has denied radio and news agency reports that an Arab League delegation will arrive in Sanaa to mediate a cease-fire. The brother foreign minister objected to the Arab League meeting to discuss an internal Yemeni affair, viewing such a meeting as a serious precedent that could extend to other Arab countries in the future. The brother foreign minister emphasized that what is taking place in Yemen is just a confrontation by the legitimate authorities of a rebel group that has deviated from constitutional legitimacy.
