BFN [Excerpts] Sofia, June 24 (BTA) -- The units on duty of Bulgaria's air force registered a violation of Bulgarian air space by 21 light transport aircraft at 10.40 a.m. local time [0740 GMT] on June 23, the Defence Ministry's Press Office reported. [passage omitted] In its newscast tonight national television aired a report on the incident from Sofia Airport. Three French and German sports planes landed at Sofia Airport yesterday [23 June] noon on their way back from an exposition in Turkey, it said. The traffic controller on duty would not be videotaped. The TV reporter learnt from another controller the planes had asked permission but the request had arrived late for technical reasons. An investigation is underway; this is the reason behind traffic controllers' unwillingness to be shown on TV.
