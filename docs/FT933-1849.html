930922 FT 22 SEP 93 / Labour attacks bancassurance THE LABOUR party has called on the government to investigate the growth of bancassurance groups which span the banking and insurance sectors. Mr Alistair Darling, Labour's City spokesman, is writing today to Mr Kenneth Clarke, the chancellor, asking him to urgently examine the implications of the sector. In a speech in Glasgow yesterday Mr Darling said there was a substantial risk that bancassurance groups would create financial conglomerates that would ruthlessly exploit their selling power.
