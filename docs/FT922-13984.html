920409 FT 09 APR 92 / World Trade News: Bell wins CDollars 1bn helicopter order Canada's federal government is ordering 100 Bell 412 helicopters from Textron Canada for about CDollars 1bn (Pounds 485m), writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. They will replace a mixed national defence fleet averaging about 20 years old. CAE Electronics, of Montreal, has won a CDollars 18m contract from Delta Airlines to build the world's first McDonnell Douglas MD90 flight simulator with three flight training devices.
