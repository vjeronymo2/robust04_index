BFN [Excerpt] Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev is to meet with defense ministers of the Baltic states today [in Brussels]. However, Estonia has two acting defense ministers at present, claims the HOMMIKULEHT with reference to its conversation yesterday with the government's press secretary, Ain Saarna. At home the defense minister's duties are carried out by Roads and Communications Minister Andi Meister but Interior Minister Heiki Arike will be travelling to Brussels for the meeting with Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev.
