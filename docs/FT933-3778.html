930911 FT 11 SEP 93 / Markets: Deadline for BES investors - At a glance Business Expansion Scheme investors who did not use up their full Pounds 40,000 allowance in the 1992-93 tax year have only until October 5 to carry back tax relief. The maximum that can be carried back to offset against last year's liability, is 50 per cent of their BES investment in the current tax year to a maximum of Pounds 5,000. If you have invested only Pounds 8,000 before October 5, Pounds 4,000 can be carried back but if you have invested Pounds 30,000, you can carry back only Pounds 5,000. More BES, page VII.
