BFN [Text] Kiev, 15 Jun -- Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk received Israel's Foreign Minister Shimon Peres during the minister's visit to Ukraine. "Ukraine attaches great importance to the development of friendly bilateral relations with Israel," the Ukrainian president stressed. "This visit opens a new stage in Ukraine's relations with Israel." Leonid Kravchuk spoke warmly about his visit to Israel in January last year when he signed a memorandum on mutual understanding and fundamental principles of cooperation between the two countries. At the same time, Ukraine's and Israel's foreign ministries signed a protocol on consultations. The two countries also signed intergovernmental agreements on cooperation in education, culture, science and technology, agriculture, and air transportation. "To us, Israel is and has been a sovereign state with which we are keen to develop relations," the Ukrainian president said. "We are neighbors, and many of our countrymen are now citizens of Israel. They live there and they wish Ukraine to be happy. "We have worked out a considerable legislative basis for the development of our relations. The main thing is that the people of Ukraine want to have friendly relations with Israel, and there are no problems that we cannot solve."
