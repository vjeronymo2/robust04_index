BFN [Text] A royal decree has been issued appointing Major General Humaydi al-Fayiz military adviser and chief escort of His Majesty King Husayn as of 29 March 1994. Maj. Gen. Humaydi al-Fayiz has occupied several leading positions in the Jordanian Armed Forces, among which are the commander of the Royal Guard and commander of the al-Husayn Bin-'Ali Brigade.
