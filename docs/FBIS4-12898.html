BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn saw off His Excellency Austrian President Thomas Klestil and his delegation when they departed Amman this afternoon, concluding an official three-day visit to the Kingdom. During the visit, the Austrian president held talks with His Majesty King Husayn and senior officials. The talks focused on several regional and international issues of common interest, developments in the Middle East peace process, and the distinguished relations between the two friendly countries. His excellency also visited several cultural landmarks in Jordan. The Austrian president was also seen off by His Highness Crown Prince Hasan, His Highness Prince 'Abdallah Bin-al-Husayn, Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, and a number of senior officials.
