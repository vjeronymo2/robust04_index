BFN [Text] Four Abkhazian servicemen have been killed and the same number wounded by Georgian saboteurs in the Gali District of Abkhazia in the morning of April 29, Interfax was told by Abkhazian Defense Minister Sultan Sosnaliyev. He said that this was another act which flagrantly violated the documents on settlement in Abkhazia signed in Moscow on April 4 by representatives of the Abkhazian and Georgian sides with the mediation of the United Nations, the CSCE and Russia. Sosnaliyev said that Abkhazian Army will have to retaliate to curb banditry of armed detachments sent by the Georgian side.
