January 12, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Kaufman & Broad Home Corp.: The home builder and commercial real estate developer reported record revenue and earnings for the fiscal year ended Nov. 30, its seventh-straight year of record results. Net income rose to $81.4 million, up 69% from 1988, on revenue of $1.26 billion, a 31% gain over the previous year. The Los Angeles-based company cited a successful expansion in its primary home-building markets and growth in commercial development.
