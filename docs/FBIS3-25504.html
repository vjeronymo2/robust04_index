Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Observers in Angola say heavy fighting between UNITA [National Union for the Total Independence of Angola] and government forces is continuing in the southern Huila Province. They say many government tanks and armored vehicles with heavy artillery are moving towards UNITA positions in the province. Air support is being provided to the tanks and armored vehicles by fighter aircraft and bombers from the Angolan Air Force. The latest fighting is centered in an area between the towns of Cacula and (Negola).
