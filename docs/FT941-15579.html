940114 FT 14 JAN 94 / Airport park plan outlined A Pounds 400m plan to create a forest park around Glasgow airport incorporating commercial, airport-related, tourism and leisure developments, was outlined to MPs in London yesterday. The scheme, a partnership between seven public and private-sector groups, was devised by Renfrewshire Enterprise. Promoters say it could create up to 16,000 jobs. Mr John Ashworth, chairman of Renfrewshire Enterprise, said final plans for the 15-year development were being drawn up. Outline planning approval may be sought before next summer.
