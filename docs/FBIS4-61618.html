BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Igor Belskiy] [Text] Moscow, 9 May -- A ceremony to consecrate a cross in memory of those who died on 3-4 October 1993 was held today not far from the former House of Soviets [White House] in Moscow. The cross was consecrated by Bishop Arseniy of Istra, vicar of the patriarch of Moscow and all Russia. About 100 people took part in the ceremony. Aleksandr Rutskoy, the former deputy president of the Russian Federation, who attended the ceremony, said he is confident that the Russian opposition will soon come to power, and without bloodshed.
