940704 FT 04 JUL 94 / Company News (This Week): MFI - Other Companies The furniture group is expected today to announce a big increase in full-year, pre-tax profits from Pounds 40.2m to about Pounds 70m. Analysts raised their forecasts from about Pounds 60m after MFI's interim results in January, when it disclosed a much stronger performance in the January sales than in 1993. They are keen to hear from MFI whether the improvement has been sustained.
