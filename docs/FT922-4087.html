920608 FT 08 JUN 92 / Construction Contracts: Supermarket plan CLUGSTON CONSTRUCTION has been awarded a Pounds 6.7m contract to construct a supermarket with a retail sales area of 32,000 sq ft for Morrisons at Wakefield, West Yorkshire. The 50-week contract, which is being handled by Clugston's Leeds office, is for a building of 72,000 sq ft which includes offices plus extensive car parking facilities and landscaping. The location is on Dewsbury Road on the outskirts of Wakefield on the site of the former Westgate Brickworks.
