January 12, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: NEWPORT BEACH;

RED CROSS OFFERS DISASTER TRAINING

A training course for volunteers in disaster services will be offered by the Red Cross on Jan. 27 at Newport Shores Clubhouse.

The training session will be from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. at 511 Canal St. For reservations, send $5 check or money order, payable to Red Cross, to Mary Williams, P.O. Box 5700, Newport Beach, Calif. 92662. Include your phone number.

For information, call (714) 722-3574.
