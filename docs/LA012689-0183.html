January 26, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

PREP SOCCER

In another Century League match:

El Modena 2, Villa Park 0 -- Mike Glaspey and Ronnie Murrietta scored goals for El Modena (6-5-2, 2-3) at Villa Park.

In Garden Grove League boys' soccer:

La Quinta 4, Rancho Alamitos 2 -- La Quinta's Steve Kalajian scored 2 goals to give the Aztecs a 2-1 halftime lead. Chris Doherty and Jon Korzon finished off the scoring for La Quinta (8-6-2, 4-2-1). Hung Nguyen and Shawn Boddy scored for Rancho Alamitos.

Pacifica 3, Santiago 3 -- At Santiago, Daniel Barber scored 2 goals and Tim Stevenson 1 for the Mariners (10-0-2, 8-0-1). Manny Manuel scored 2 goals and Carlos Gonzejo 1 for Santiago.

In the Orange League:

Brea-Olinda 5, Magnolia 1 -- Senior midfielder J.R. Ochs scored 4 goals and assisted his brother Jasyn on the other at Magnolia. Brea (10-3-1, 4-0) is the only undefeated team in the league. Magnolia is 7-8-7, 1-2-1.

In the Pacific Coast League:

Laguna Hills 0, Orange 0 -- Goalie Jeff Calvert made 5 saves for Laguna Hills (5-3-2 in league play) at Orange. Tres Martin made 3 saves for Orange (6-8-3, 4-4-2).

In the Sea View League:

Estancia 1, University 0 -- Marco Vasquez scored the only goal on a penalty kick 90 seconds into the game to help Estancia (7-5-3, 2-1-2) at home. University is 1-4 in league play.

Saddleback 3, Newport Harbor 0 -- George Elias, Jose Esqudero and Braulo Mendez scored goals for host Saddleback (11-4-4, 4-0-1). Josh Klein made 10 saves for Newport Harbor (5-4-4, 2-2-1).

In Angelus League girls' soccer:

Rosary 2, Bishop Amat 1 -- Amy Polnazeka and Lana McHue scored goals for host Rosary (4-2-2, 6-2-3).
