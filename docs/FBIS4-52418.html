BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 10 (KCNA) -- The Ministry of People's Armed Forces gave a reception at the Mansudae Assembly Hall Monday in honor of the visiting high-level delegation of the Ministry of National Defence of Laos headed by Choummali Sai-ngason, Politburo member of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Lao People's Revolutionary Party and defence minister. Addressing the reception, Choe Kwang, Politburo member of the C.C., the Workers' Party of Korea and chief of the general staff of the Korean People's Army, said the Laotian delegation's visit would mark an important occasion in developing the friendly and cooperative relations between the two armies of Korea and Laos. The present complex international situation, in which anti-socialist moves of the imperialists and the reactionaries are getting all the more wanton, requires to further strengthen and develop cooperation between the peoples and armies of the two countries, Choe Kwang noted, adding: We will as ever set store by the friendly and cooperative relations with the Laotian People's Army and work hard to constantly develop the relations. Defence Minister Choummali Sai-ngasone said in his speech that the Laotian Army and people were rejoiced over the great victory the fraternal Korean people have won in the cause of socialist construction under the banner of chuche, true to the leadership of the Workers' Party of Korea headed by the great Comrade Kim Il-song and dear Comrade Kim Chong-il. This gives a powerful encouragement to us, he noted. The army and people of Laos believe that the traditional relations of friendship and cooperation between Korea and Laos, established by the tireless efforts of the parties, governments and peoples of the two countries, will consistently strengthen and develop in keeping with the present new situation, he said. He stressed he would make all efforts to this end. On the same day, Choe Kwang met and had a friendly talk with the delegation which paid a courtesy call on him.
