920923 FT 23 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Development costs leave Comac lower Pre-tax profits at Comac Group, the Hertfordshire-based computer consultancy, fell from Pounds 186,500 to Pounds 44,000 in the first half of 1992 despite a halving of the net interest costs to Pounds 22,000, against Pounds 56,000. The USM-quoted company blamed the Pounds 50,000 costs of an aborted acquisition, taken as an exceptional charge, and the costs of strengthening the overseas activities, from which benefits did not show in the period. Turnover was Pounds 4.91m (Pounds 5.27m). Earnings per share were 0.44p (2.01p). Again there is no interim dividend but directors were confident of being able to recommend a final.
