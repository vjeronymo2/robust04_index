920131 FT 31 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Sale Tilney in Pounds 6.8m disposal Sale Tilney has disposed of certain businesses and assets of Sale Tilney Foods, which comprise Peabody Foods, its UK food division, to Stevens & Brotherton. STF's dried pasta distribution business is excluded from the deal and the board will shortly determine the future of this profitable business - in the 12 months to November 29 there was an operating profit of Pounds 200,000 on turnover of Pounds 1.4m. The aggregate net proceeds of the Peabody disposal and the collection of trade debts are estimated to be Pounds 6.8m.
