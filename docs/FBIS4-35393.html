BFN [Text] Deputy Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Qadir Bajammal has said that the forces of legitimacy and unity have completed the purging of the remaining pockets which were controlled by the forces of apostasy and secession in the Lahij Governorate. He said that they have completed the purging of the Tawr al-Bahah District. He stressed that the important targets in the Aden Governorate are now within the range of the guns of the forces of legitimacy and unity. He noted that the forces of legitimacy in the governorates of Shabwah and Hadhramaut have managed to purge the al-'Abr District in the Hadhramaut Governorate. This came at the news conference Deputy Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Qadir Bajammal held at the Prime Ministry today. The conference was attended by the representatives and correspondents of local, Arab, and foreign news media. Brothers 'Abd-al-Rahman Zaydan, director of the Council of Ministers' office and [name indistinct], the Information Ministry under secretary, were also present. The deputy prime minister spoke about various national issues, particularly the current situation in Yemen.
