BFN ["Opinion Poll for SAKARTVELOS RESPUBLIKA"] [Text] What governed your view that Georgia should join the CIS? Documents signed with CIS countries -- 12.1 percent; The experience of relations between Georgia and the CIS -- 17.7 percent; Georgia's experience of being part of the USSR -- 24.4 percent; I could not see any other alternative -- 33.2 percent; Difficult to say -- 12.6 percent. The poll was conducted for SAKARTVELOS RESPUBLIKA by the "Inform Service" on 22-24 April.
