Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Knin, 29 Mar (TANJUG) -- Boris Mikelic, the prime minister-designate of the republic of Serb Krajina, told TANJUG this evening that Milan Babic had accepted in principle his offer to become the deputy prime minister of the government. Mikelic said that this position was offered to the leader of the Serb Democratic Party of Krajina, Milan Babic, after consultations with party leaders. According to Mikelic, Babic accepted the offer in principle. [passage omitted]
