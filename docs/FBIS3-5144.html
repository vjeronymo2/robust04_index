Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] On 3 March, Chinese Ambassador to the ROK Zhang Tingyan visited the Foreign Ministry and met with Vice Foreign Minister Hong Sun-yong. Ambassador Zhang asked Vice Foreign Minister Hong to support the Chinese side's position when the UN Human Rights Committee in Geneva puts to a vote the "resolution for calling for the promotion of human rights in China" slated for 8 March. Vice Foreign Minister Hong stressed that "human rights are a dignified value which should not be infringed upon under any pretext," and revealed a position "to prudently review the issue by taking into account special relations between the ROK and the PRC."
