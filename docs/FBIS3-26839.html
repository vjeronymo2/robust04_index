Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Yesterday evening [8 February], officers and soldiers of a Guizhou-stationed armed forces unit under the Commmission for National Defense Science, Technology, and Industry satisfactorily completed a tracking test on China's No. 4 scientific experimental satellite. To fulfill this test, three months ago this armed forces unit already made the necessary arrangements, assigned technological personnel to their posts, given them training, and examined and repaired the equipment.
