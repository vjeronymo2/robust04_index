941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / International Company News: CSM advances 11% to Fl 164m CSM, the Dutch food and ingredients group, said net profit rose by 11.4 per cent to Fl 164.1m (Dollars 94m) in the year to September 30, on turnover up 4.6 per cent at Fl 2.64bn, writes Ronald van de Krol in Amsterdam. The company, traditionally the first Dutch group to report annual results, said it would announce the level of its 1993-1994 dividend on January 3.
