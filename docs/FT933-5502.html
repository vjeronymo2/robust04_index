930903 FT 03 SEP 93 / Labour still ahead in Norway Norway's ruling minority Labour party has slipped slightly in the polls 10 days before the general election, but still has a clear lead over the main opposition Conservative party. According to a poll in Aftenposten, Norway's leading daily, support for Labour has fallen by 1.7 percentage points to 33.4 per cent, while backing for the pro-EC Conservative party slipped 1.6 points to 20 per cent. Support for the small anti-EC Centre party fell 0.9 points to 10.6 per cent. The Socialist Left party, also opposed to EC membership, rose 2.7 points to 12.8 per cent.
