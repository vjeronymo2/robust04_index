July 3, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

An unidentified man was struck and killed in the northbound lanes of Interstate 805 just south of Imperial Avenue in San Diego early Monday morning, police said.

According to Joe Wolf, a California Highway Patrol officer, a northbound driver who saw the man walking west across the freeway about 6 a.m. swerved, but hit him anyway.

The victim, estimated to be 35 years old, was dead at the scene.
