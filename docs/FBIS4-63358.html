BFN [From the "Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] More than 20 combat ships, support vessels, and submarines have left the main base of the Black Sea Fleet. Until 22 May, the Black Sea Fleet is holding the scheduled exercises of the fleet's various forces, including firing practice and the targeting and elimination submarines. The exercises are kind summation of the results of a training period, preparations for the summer campaign, and at the same time, a competition to win the navy prizes.
