910513 FT 13 MAY 91 / Appointments: Managing director at Chubb Fire Security CHUBB FIRE SECURITY, the fire protection specialist, has appointed Mr Richard Marshall as managing director. Mr Marshall, a physicist, has extensive marketing and general management experience in the UK, Europe and North America. Until recently he was international operations manager of the American-based company Varian Associates Inc where he was responsible for the operation of the US and 20 overseas subsidiary companies. ***** Mr Alec Lafone, distribution manager of Glaxo Pharmaceuticals UK, has been elected president of the FREIGHT TRANSPORT ASSOCIATION. Appointed vice chairmen are Mr Angus Clark, distribution director, J. Sainsbury, and Mr Bill Houseman, national transport manager, J. Bibby Agriculture.
