Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Abu-Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, last night received the Russian ambassador in Tunis who conveyed to him a message from the Russian leadership. Brother President Abu-Ammar asked the ambassador to convey a message to the Russian leadership on the Israeli-Palestinian talks in Davos and Israel's attempts to delay implementation of the Declaration of Principles. The meeting was also an opportunity to review the various stages of the peace process and the worsening situation in the occupied territories as a consequence of the occupation and Israel's violation of Palestinians' human rights.
