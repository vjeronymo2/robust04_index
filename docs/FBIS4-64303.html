BFN [Text] Unal Erkan, state of emergency region governor, has announced that 500 terrorists were killed in operations conducted by security forces in the region and across the border in the past 20 days. In statements issued in the Savur District and Kabala subdistrict of Mardin, Erkan reported that 500 weapons, eight antiaircraft guns, 10 mortars, 74 rocket launchers, and numerous rounds of ammunition were seized during the operations. Explaining that the terrorists are attacking defenseless citizens, Erkan stated that 70 citizens were killed by terrorists in the past month. Erkan stressed that the state is determined to completely neutralize the terrorists this year. Pointing out that the terrorist organization is in the process of collapsing, the regional governor remarked that this is leading to internal quarrels and executions. Erkan asked the citizens to be careful with regard to terrorist incidents and to assist the state even more.
