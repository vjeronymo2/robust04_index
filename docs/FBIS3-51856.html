Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] A delegation of deputies headed by [Democratic Socialism Alternative Deputy] Jerjes Justiniano, [Patriotic Accord Deputy] Guido Nayar, and prosecutor Freddy Flores, has confirmed the existence of a DEA center for intercepting telephone and radio communications of [Santa Cruz] Department officials. The delegation visited a property, allegedly owned by the DEA, where the existence of the center had been reported, at kilometer 11 of the highway to Cotoca.
