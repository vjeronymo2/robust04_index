920710 FT 10 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Symonds Symonds Engineering reported sharply reduced profits for the 12 months to March 31. The Hertfordshire-based general and precision engineer saw the pre-tax line fall 29 per cent to Pounds 155,807, down from Pounds 220,435 last time. Earnings per share fell to 1.102p (1.717p); a same-again final dividend of 0.7p maintains the total for the year at 1p.
