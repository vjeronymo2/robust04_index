930927 FT 27 SEP 93 / World Bank/IMF Meeting: Russia suggests creditors write off part of its foreign debt Russia suggested yesterday that international creditors write off part of its Dollars 80bn (Pounds 51.9bn) foreign debt but Germany rejected the request, Mr Theo Waigel, German finance minister, said, Reuter reports from Washington. Mr Waigel told reporters that the issue had been raised by Mr Alexander Shokhin, Russian deputy prime minister. 'But I said we were against this. It would not find our favour,' Mr Waigel said.
