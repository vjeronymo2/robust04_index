DEPARTMENT OF DEFENSE Office of the Secretary Department of Defense Wage Committee; Closing Meetings Pursuant to the provisions of section 10 of Public Law 92&hyph;463, the Federal Advisory Committee Act, notice is hereby given that a closed meeting of the Department of Defense Wage Committee will be held on September 6, 1994; September 13, 1994; September 20, 1994; and September 27, 1994, at 10:00 a.m. in Room 800, Hoffman Building # 1, Alexandria, Virginia. Under the provision of section 10(d) of Public Law 92&hyph;463. the Department of Defense has determined that the meetings meet the criteria to close the meeting to the public because the matters considered are related to internal rules and practices of the Department of Defense and the detailed wage data considered were obtained from officials of private establishments with a guarantee that the data will be held in confidence. However, members of the public who may wish to do so are invited to submit material in writing to the chairman concerning matters believed to be deserving of the Committee's attention. Additional information concerning the meetings may be obtained by writing to the Chairman, Department of Defense Wage Committee, 4000 Defense Pentagon, Washington, DC 20301&hyph;4000. Dated: August 17, 1994. Patricia L. Toppings, Alternate OSD Federal Register Liaison Officer, Department of Defense.
