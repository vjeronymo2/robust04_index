RSPA has determined that master meter operators will not be affected by this notice of proposed rulemaking because the master meter operator generally owns the complete gas distribution system. Thus, the master meter operator is responsible for the pipeline from the point of purchase to the ultimate customer. A draft regulatory evaluation has been prepared to determine the economic impact of this proposed rule on public, private, and municipal gas distribution systems. Based on the facts available, I certify that this proposal will not, if promulgated, have a significant economic impact on a substantial number of small entities. This certification is subject to modification as a result of a review of comments received in response to this proposal.
