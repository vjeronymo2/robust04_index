920430 FT 30 APR 92 / UK Company News: Sotheby's incurs Dollars 5m deficit Sotheby's Holdings, the auction house which is controlled by the Detroit-based Taubman family but still quoted in London and New York, made a Dollars 5.2m (Pounds 2.93m) loss in the first quarter of 1992. Auction sales in the first three months of 1992 were Dollars 141.4m, slightly higher than the Dollars 123.4m seen in the previous year. The company claimed that the increase was 'consistent with an improving art market", and that pre-tax earnings were up by over 35 per cent.
