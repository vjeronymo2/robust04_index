July 29, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BOYCOTTING TV SHOWS

I've found the most obscene TV shows have been produced by the likes of Jim and Tammy Faye Bakker, Oral Roberts and Jimmy Swaggart. "Knots Landing" can't even compare to the avarice and greed, stories of illicit sex and homosexuality, air-conditioned doghouses, fraud, deception, tearful confessions on TV that these hypocrites have engaged in.

At least when I watch "Midnight Caller" I know I won't be harangued by someone who wants me to send them my money.

RICHARD A. KILGORE

Hollywood
