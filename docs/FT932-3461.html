930614 FT 14 JUN 93 / International Company News: RCO expands 6% to Pounds 2.41m RCO Holdings, a supplier of integrated site support services, announced a 6 per cent rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 2.27m to Pounds 2.41m for the 27 weeks to April 4. The interim dividend is increased to 4.95p (4.62p) on earnings per share of 14.71p (13.87p).
