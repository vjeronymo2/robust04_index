Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "The Crimea Has Its Trade Representative in Moscow"] [Text] [Crimean] President Yu. Meshkov has appointed M. Nikolayev as Crimea's first trade representative in Russia. He is 40, a graduate of Kuybyshev Institute of Culture and of the Californian Business and Marketing College. The government in Moscow has allocated the premises of the former Krasnaya Presnya Rayon Council of People's Deputies for the Crimean Trade Mission in Russia. In a month's time, an exhibition of Crimean goods will be held there.
