BFN [Text] A communique by the Armed Forces of the DRY [Democratic Republic of Yemen] has announced that the Sanaa military regime has continued its aggression on the peaceful villages and residential headquarters and the vital economic establishments. Last night, surface-to-surface missiles were launched on some residential quarters in Aden, and their shrapnel fell and injured several innocent persons. Fierce fighting also took place between our forces and the aggressive forces at all battlefronts. Our forces stopped the advances of the enemy and inflicted on them heavy losses.
