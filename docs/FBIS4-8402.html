BFN [Text] Cajnice, 12 Apr (TANJUG) -- NATO aircraft overflew Serb positions around Gorazde several times late this afternoon and this evening, dropping to only 1,000 meters, but did not engage in combat actions, the command of the Herzegovina Corps has announced. The Muslims again tried to take advantage of the low-flying U.S. aircraft, launching a fierce attack from the direction of Gorazde on the positions of the Herzegovina corps in the region of Jardiste and Majdan. Their infantry attack was supported by artillery fire. As the command of the Herzegovina corps announced, the attack has been repelled while the Muslim units, having suffered huge losses, were returned to their starting positions. One Serb fighter was killed and two others wounded in the attack.
