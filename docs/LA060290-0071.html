June 2, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SPORTS ON WEEKEND TV

With local basketball and hockey long done, baseball highlights the sports choices on TV this weekend.

Crucial is usually not an appropriate label to a series scheduled for the first week of June.

But that designation does apply to the Dodgers-Cincinnati Reds matchup as the locals desperately seek to remain a credible pennant contender. SportsChannel will televise tonight's game at 7.

The Angels, no longer underachievers but overachievers, meet the Texas Rangers at 5:30 p.m. today on KTLA Channel 5.

Finally beginning to meet their lofty preseason expectations, the San Diego Padres play host to the Atlanta Braves tonight at 7 (TBS and San Diego Cable Sports) and 1 p.m. Sunday on TBS.

Two area college baseball teams will also make television appearances today. Cal State Fullerton faces Oklahoma State at 5 p.m. on ESPN. Cal State Northridge goes for the Division II championship against Jacksonville (Ala.) State. Prime Ticket will have delayed coverage beginning at 5:30 p.m. STEVEN HERBERT
