BFN [Text] A military source has issued a statement in which it said a number of unionist officers and soldiers in units under the command of the rebel secessionist elements in the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] are continuing to join the ranks of the army of unity and constitutional legitimacy. Yesterday, all the personnel of the 1st Artillery Brigade in the Yarim region and the Khurafi camp that includes the personnel of the special guards of al-Bid and the personnel of the military police battalion joined their brothers in the units of the Armed Forces, which are protecting unity and democracy, after they discovered the hellish plan of the secessionist clique that wants to disrupt the homeland. The military source pointed out in its statement that our Air Force defenses confronted all the treacherous, hostile air raids on the capital, Sanaa, and the cities of Ta'izz, al-Hudaydah, and Ma'rib--raids that targeted vital and strategic installations in these cities in order to terrorize peaceful citizens. The source also clarified that Colonel Jawad Muthanna, commander of the Ba Suhayb Brigade, and the brigade's staff were arrested yesterday after they sparked sedition in the city of Sanaa.
