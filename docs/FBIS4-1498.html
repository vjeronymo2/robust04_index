BFN [Text] East London April 29 SAPA -- A suspected member of African National Congress military wing, Umkhonto we Sizwe [Spear of the Nation], has been arrested after being found carrying a hand grenade at a polling station in Duncan Village, reports SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news. East London police confirmed that six men claiming to be ANC election monitors had tried to gain access to a guarded building housing sealed ballot boxes. When they were confronted by the security forces, one of the men fled, dropping a machine pistol. The grenade was found on another man.
