Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Czech Republic: Mr. Havel Says His Country Is Waiting To Be `Ripe' for Membership"; as released by Brussels TEXTLINE database] [Text] The speed with which the Czech Republic can become a member of the European Union [EU] will depend partly on how quickly changes take place in the country, but also on the Union's strategy for future enlargement. President Havel made this comment at a press conference, adding that conditions for membership have become more stringent following the Community's "metamorphosis" into a Union. "We cannot apply for membership tomorrow or the next day.... We are waiting to be ripe," he added. Mr. Havel did not express a view on the advisability of "partial" membership (to the second and third pillars, for example) but underscored his country's will to participate in political dialogue with the EU.
