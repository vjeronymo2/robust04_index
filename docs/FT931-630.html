930329 FT 29 MAR 93 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 28m work for Mowlem MOWLEM NORTHERN has won more than Pounds 28m worth of new work in the north of England. Mowlem's Manchester office has won the first phase of a Pounds 13.5m project to extend and upgrade Manchester Crown Courts for The Lord Chancellor's Office, a Pounds 1.1m contract to build Coppice Junior School, Oldham, for Oldham Metropolitan Borough Council and a Pounds 582,000 contract to build a Co-op store at Cheadle, south Manchester. The North West Regional Health Authority has awarded a contract worth nearly Pounds 11m for new teaching and medical blocks at Hope Hospital, Salford. The project consists of two linked, three-storey medical and patient accommodation blocks and a two-storey university teaching building.
