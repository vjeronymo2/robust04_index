BFN ["Defense Ministry Press Service report" -- read by announcer] [Text] Having reinforced its troops in northern Karabakh and with the support of a number of tanks, the Armenian Armed Forces launched attacks towards Terter on the afternoon of 17 April. Armenian units resumed their tank attack last night and in the early hours of 18 April; infantry troops also joined the fight. Bloody battles continued until 0800. The enemy's attacks were repelled, and they were driven back to their initial positions. Five tanks and one infantry fighting vehicle were destroyed and nearly 90 Armenian troops were killed.
