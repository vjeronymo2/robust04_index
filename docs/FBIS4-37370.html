BFN [Text] This morning, the chairman of the Jordanian state bank board of directors attended the opening ceremony of the bank's Nabulus branch, which came in the footsteps of the first branch opened in Ramallah some time ago. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the Bank of Jordan's [preceding three words in English] Nabulus branch employs 40 people, and it will trade in shekels, dinars, and foreign currency, under Bank of Israel supervision for the time being.
