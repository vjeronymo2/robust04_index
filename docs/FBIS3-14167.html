Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Viktor Lashtsover, the Israeli guard shot in a terrorist attack near the Damascus Gate entry to the Old City of Jerusalem yesterday, has died of his wounds in Hadassah Hospital. The 40-year-old Lashtsover, a resident of Pisgat Ze'ev, immigrated to Israel three years ago from Russia. The Popular Front group of George Habash claimed responsibility for the attack, and police are continuing their search for the assailant.
