November 8, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

HOT, HIP & HAPPENING;

PRESS CLUB, BOY SCOUTS IN MEDIA VENTURE

The Orange County Press Club is undertaking a venture with the Boy Scouts of America to sponsor a group for teen-agers interested in journalism and related fields.

Under the plan, Press Club members would serve as advisers for the Explorer Post, which would meet two or three times a month.

Activities would include tours of local newspapers, cable television stations and radio stations, and probably a production of a newsletter, according to Robin Kruger, exploring executive.

Potential members -- girls and boys between 14 and 20 -- are welcome to attend the post's first meeting tonight at the Days Inn Santa Ana, 1600 E. 1st Street, Santa Ana. The post has an annual fee of $12, payable to the Boy Scouts of America for insurance and administrative costs.

The Orange County Press Club is a nonprofit organization with more than 500 members.

For more information, contact press club president Jonathan Volzke at (714) 454-7338.
