BFN [Text] Cuban Commander in Chief Fidel Castro arrived on 4 May in Bridgetown, Barbados, to attend the First International Small Island Nations Conference on Sustainable Development. Castro will participate in the high-level portion of the conference on 5-6 May. President Castro traveled to Barbados in the company of Ricardo Alarcon de Quesada, president of the National Assembly of the People's Government; Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina; and Environment Minister Rosa Elena Simeon, among other high-level officials.
