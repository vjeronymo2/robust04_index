930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Allied Radio losses jump ALLIED Radio is considering a reconstruction of the balance sheet including the conversion of loan stock and eliminating the negative balance on the profit and loss account. The company, which provides local services in Surrey, Sussex and Hampshire, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 1.17m (Pounds 585,000) for the six months to March 31. Turnover was Pounds 2.25m (Pounds 2.17m) of which Pounds 745,000 (Pounds 650,000) related to discontinued activities. The operating loss for continuing activities increased to Pounds 671,000 (Pounds 217,000) but the company said that the figure included non-recurring costs. Losses per share were 2.52p (1.39p).
