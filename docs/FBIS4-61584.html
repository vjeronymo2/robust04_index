BFN [Text] The first 113 Russian nationals evacuated from Yemen have returned home. A transport aircraft from the Russian Ministry of Civil Defense, Emergencies, and Natural Disasters, which flew them out of the neighboring state of Djibuti alongside two Slovak nationals [as heard], landed today at 0440 at Ramenskoye airport outside Moscow. All of the new arrivals are feeling good. They were welcomed by representatives of the Foreign Ministry, the Defense Ministry, and other organizations, and were all driven to their homes. The second aircraft with the next group of evacuees is expected to fly to Russia later today.
