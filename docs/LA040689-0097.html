April 6, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: SANTA MONICA;

DISTRICT THANKS BUSINESSES

The school district will honor Partners in Business who support the public schools at a breakfast today at Grant Elementary School.

Lee Canter and Associates will be honored for adopting Will Rogers Elementary School; Lebowitz Accountancy Corp. for providing accounting for the Santa Monica Education Foundation and for helping the district pass Proposition TT (the parcel tax), and Coldwell Banker Residential Real Estate for providing phone banks for the Proposition TT campaign.

The breakfast is sponsored by the school district, the Santa-Monica Malibu Assn. of School Administrators and the Masonic Lodge.
