940511 FT 11 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Heath buys more of Bermudan offshoot CE Heath, the insurance broker, is paying Dollars 4.41m (Pounds 3.02m) in accordance with an earnout arrangement to acquire 2,401 further ordinary shares in Heath Fielding (Latin America), its Bermuda-based subsidiary. The purchase raises Heath's stake in the company from 80.4 per cent to 90.2 per cent. Consideration will be met by the allotment of 796,029 ordinary shares, which are being placed through SG Warburg Securities.
