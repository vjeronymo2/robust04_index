April 29, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

TRIO PRESENTED WITH HUMANITARIAN AWARDS

The National Conference of Christians and Jews recently honored John W. Hancock, John A. Martin and James A. Thomas at its construction industries humanitarian awards dinner in Beverly Hills.

Hancock is executive vice president of Security National Bank Real Estate Industries Group; Martin is chairman and chief executive officer of John A. Martin & Associates, and Thomas is a managing partner at Maguire Thomas Partners. Architect Herb Nadel of the Nadel Partnership Inc. was general dinner chairman.
