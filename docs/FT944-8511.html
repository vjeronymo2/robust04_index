941119 FT 19 NOV 94 / UK Company News: M&G Income Inv M&G Income Investment Trust suffered a fall in net asset value per capital share from 78.47p to 70.3p over the year to October 31. After-tax revenue for the nine months to end-October grew from Pounds 9.34m to Pounds 10.7m and earnings per income share came to 4.32p (3.79p). The third interim dividend is held at 1p for an unchanged 3p in the nine months.
