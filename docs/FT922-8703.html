920512 FT 12 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Brewers in trading link Marston, Thompson & Evershed and Wolverhampton & Dudley, the Midlands regional brewers, have signed a reciprocal trading agreement designed to strengthen their competitive position against the national brewers. Under the agreement, W&D's Banks's Mild ale will be distributed in nominated pubs in Marston's estate, and Marston's Pedigree bitter will be distributed in selected W&D outlets.
