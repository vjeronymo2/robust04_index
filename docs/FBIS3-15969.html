Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The official visit to Germany of Army General Pavel Grachev, the Russian defense minister, is continuing. In Bonn Grachev had talks with Volker Ruehe, head of the German military department, during which they discussed various matters regarding defense cooperation. At the end of their meeting the defense ministers signed a joint statement. The document notes that the development of cooperation between the two countries, including in the military sphere, is an important factor in strengthening security in Europe. They expressed the shared view that it is necessary to maintain stable contacts between their military departments after the withdrawal of Russian troops has been completed. In particular, it is envisaged that Russian officers will be trained in Germany and German officers in Russia. Agreement has been reached to hold joint military exercises. As early as this summer naval maneuvers are to be held in the Baltic, and in 1995 and 1996 exercises involving land force units are to be held in Russia and in Germany.
