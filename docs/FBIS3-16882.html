Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today, the final round of negotiations between the Belarusian Government and the IMF mission was held with the participation of Belarusian Prime Minister Vyacheslav Kebich. The discussion focused on the issues of carrying out economic reforms in Belarus and the role of the IMF in their implementation. The strategic goals of supporting the continuous, effective improvement of the Belarusian economy, and increasing people's living standards were specified in the signed memorandum of mutual understanding between the government and the IMF. The strategy of the program is mainly based on the renewal of macroeconomic stability, economy, the strengthening of market tendencies including a decrease in inflation, the recovery of balance of payments, and protection of the weakest groups of the population during the transition period. At the meeting, an agreement was reached to open a permanent IMF mission and nominate its chairman. The opening of this mission is planned for July 1994.
