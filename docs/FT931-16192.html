930108 FT 08 JAN 93 / Dulwich gallery in Pounds 200,000 plea DULWICH Picture Gallery, south London, is seeking Pounds 200,000 a year of government aid to ensure its survival. The gallery was founded in 1811 with the ambition to be the national art gallery. It survives on a grant from the governors of Alleyn's College, Dulwich, and from admission charges. Costs exceed income by more than Pounds 100,000 a year.
