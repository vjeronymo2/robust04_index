November 19, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

A 'THANKS' FOR DOUG

Driving down Laguna Canyon Road recently, my car ran out of gas in the middle of nowhere. I was there all alone. After sitting in my car for what seemed like forever, I decided to walk before it got too late. I heard something rolling over the pebbles behind me, and there was a man sitting on a bike. He rode to a phone, and in 30 minutes my family was there to get me.

All I know about the man is that his name is Doug. I would like to say thank you.

METER JONES

Orange
