BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Vlasov] [Text] Pskov May 11 TASS -- A delegation of French military officials arrived by plane here on Tuesday evening to familiarize themselves with the way of life of servicemen of the Pskov Airborne Infantry Division. They will visit the Division's quarters on Wednesday. Lieutenant Colonel Viktor Sorokin, chief of the Russian airborne infantry forces, is accompanying the French delegation. Sorokin and Major General Ivan Babichev, commander of the Pskov division in question, will do parachute jumps from an IL-76 assault aircraft on May 12. In June, 90 French paratroopers from an airborne infantry regiment stationed near the town of Castres are to come here to participate in a joint exercise with the Russian counterparts. They will stay here for ten days. Simultaneously, 90 Russian paratroopers from the Pskov Airborne Infantry Division will take part in a joint exercise in France.
