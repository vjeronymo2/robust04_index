BFN [Text] "The Northern League is the leading political force in the country, and especially in the Chamber of Deputies. So we must act as if we already had an objectively effective mandate from the president, who must give the mandate to form a government to whoever is chosen by the Right Honorable Umberto Bossi who, in any event, has already indicated the Right Honorable Roberto Maroni for some time." This was contained in a note read out by Simonetta Faverio, Bossi's new spokesperson. It also said that tomorrow the Northern League would start consultations with the various political forces, starting with their electoral allies.
