BFN [Text] A resistance group attacked an Israeli position near al-Ahmadiyah in al-Biqa' this morning. The attack was followed by violent clashes between the resistance fighters and the occupation forces, which shelled the area with artillery. At 0930 today, another resistance group attacked a patrol of agent Lahd militia [South Lebanese Army] on the Kawkaba-Dallafah road inside the occupied zone. The two sides then traded artillery fire.
