921029 FT 29 OCT 92 / People: Non-executive directors Scotia Holdings, an unlisted pharmaceuticals group, has acquired another heavyweight non-executive director for its board. Following the appointment two months ago of Sir James McKinnon, director general at the Office of Gas Supply, William Glynn-Williams, Glaxo's deputy managing director, has also joined the board. Yesterday the company also announced it was considering the possibility of a Stock Exchange listing. The group, which had a turnover of Pounds 16m last year and pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.1m, changed its name from Efamol Holdings in September. It has developed treatments for eczema, breast pain and seborrhoeic dermatitis.
