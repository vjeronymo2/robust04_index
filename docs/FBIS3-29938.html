Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 23 (KCNA) -- The Pyongyang declaration "Let Us Defend and Advance the Cause of Socialism" was recently signed by the Socialist Progressive Party, the militant party for equality and struggle of Madagascar; the Renovation Movement; the Unionist Democratic Union and the Progressive Socialist Rally, of Tunisia; and the Communist Party of Canada (Marxist-Leninist). The declaration was signed by 207 political parties as of February 15. In April, 1992, many delegates of communist and workers' parties and other political parties aspiring after socialism from 70 countries adopted the historical Pyongyang declaration reflecting their steadfast will to defend and advance the cause of socialism. The declaration, elucidating the secret of defending and advancing the cause of socialism, stresses the need for each party to firmly maintain independence.
