920123 FT 23 JAN 92 / S Africa draft review South Africa said yesterday it had suspended prosecution of draft dodgers while it weighed possible changes to whites-only conscription, Reuter reports from Johannesburg. Mr Wynand Breytenbach, the deputy defence minister, said prosecutions of young men who refused to serve the compulsory 12 months' duty would be frozen while the government reviewed a report on possible defence changes by Gen Ian Gleeson.
