August 14, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Tickets for UC Irvine-sponsored events at the Irvine Barclay Theatre are on sale now even though the theater is still under construction and will not open until October.

More than 60 events, including an Opera a la Carte production of Gilbert and Sullivan's "H.M.S. Pinafore" and the Royal New Zealand Ballet's "Coppelia," have been scheluded at the 750-seat theater. Tickets will range from $12 to $30. For information, call the Arts and Lectures office at UC Irvine, (714) 856-6379.
