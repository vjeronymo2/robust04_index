940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / Deadline set for Clydesdale offers Receivers for Clydesdale Group, the Glasgow-based electrical retail chain that went into receivership in January, have set a deadline of next Monday for submission of formal offers for the group's assets. Grant Thornton, the receivers, said they expected to receive more than six formal offers. Clydesdale Group has 135 branches throughout Scotland and England.
