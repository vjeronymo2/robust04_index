920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Kingston Oil & Gas slides to Pounds 142,644 Net profits of Kingston Oil & Gas, tumbled from Pounds 593,842 to Pounds 142,644 in the six months to end-June as turnover fell by 18 per cent to Pounds 2.94m. However, operating and administration costs of the oil and gas producer were only slightly down at Pounds 2.72m (Pounds 2.86m), leaving operating profits of just Pounds 220,662 (Pounds 724,706). An interest turnround to payable of Pounds 11,189 (Pounds 36,629 receivable) left pre-tax profits at Pounds 209,473 (Pounds 761,335). Earnings came out at 1.08p (4.48p) per share but the interim dividend is maintained at 1p. Mr Ray Chambers, chairman, said the reduction in profits was principally accounted for by a fall in the contribution from the US operations.
