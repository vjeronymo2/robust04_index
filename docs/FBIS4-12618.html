BFN [Text] Damascus, 8 Apr (MENA)--Yemeni Presidential Council Member Salim Salih Muhammad has said that he requested Syrian participation in the military committee or in any political activity to resolve Yemen's political crisis. The Yemeni official was speaking to a MENA correspondent in Damascus after his meeting with Syrian Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam this evening. He said Syrian officials are now studying the Yemeni request. Muhammad described his meeting with the Syrian vice president, which was attended by Syrian Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar', as positive. The Yemeni official is visiting Syria as part of an Arab tour.
