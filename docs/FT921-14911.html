920114 FT 14 JAN 92 / Mexico trade deficit likely to top Dollars 10bn MEXICO'S trade deficit climbed to Dollars 5.09bn (Pounds 2.8bn) in the first 10 months of last year, Damian Fraser reports from Mexico City. Excluding revenues from maquiladoras, or in-bond plants, the deficit reached Dollars 8.48bn, or Dollars 1.14bn more than in September. The deficit, excluding maquiladoras, is likely to top Dollars 10bn for 1991, against Dollars 4.14bn the year before.
