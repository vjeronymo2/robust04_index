Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Customs officials in Beirut Airport foiled an attempt to smuggle narcotics into Lebanon yesterday. Jihad 'Afifi Ziyadah, from al-Mansurah, western al-Biqa', was arrested as he arrived from Venezuela. Four kg of cocaine, wrapped in nylon and carbon sheets in order to confound electronic surveillance cameras, were found in his luggage. Ziyadah was referred to the Counternarcotics Office for interrogation.
