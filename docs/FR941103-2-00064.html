A line item (which should be included in the Other Expense line) which appears on the detailed report was coded incorrectly so that it does not appear on the summarized reports, but is included in the totals of both reports. Thus, the totals are $40,000 higher in the Work Program than the total of the numbers shown and $23,921 higher than the total of the numbers shown in the FY 1995 Congressional Budget Submission. The $40,000 budgeted Other Expense item was later reduced to $23,921, which matches the $1,141,775 figure shown in the FY 1995 Congressional Budget Submission.
