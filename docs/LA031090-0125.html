March 10, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

SHOOTING SUSPECT JAILED

A San Diego man was arrested early Friday morning at a La Mesa nightclub in connection with the shooting of a Camp Pendleton-based Marine at the club, La Mesa police reported.

Christopher Avalon Miller, 23, is being held without bail in County Jail on suspicion of attempted murder.

Miller allegedly shot Steven Christian in the arm and leg during an argument Feb. 4 in the parking lot of Cafe Vid in the 7300 block of El Cajon Boulevard, Sgt. Charles Peightal said.

Peightal said Miller was identified by bouncers at the club and was arrested at 1 a.m. Friday as he left.
