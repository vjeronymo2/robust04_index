920204 FT 04 FEB 92 / Libya opens up N-facilities Libya has told the International Atomic Energy Agency that all its nuclear facilities are open to inspection to prove it has no secret bomb-making project, Reuter reports. The IAEA said, after a visit to Libya by Mr Hans Blix, its director general, that the country had expressed willingness to co-operate fully with the agency in implementing safeguards against diversion of nuclear technology to military uses. Col Muammer Gadaffi, the Libyan leader, told Mr Blix there was no foundation to western claims that Libya was trying to produce an atom bomb, the IAEA said.
