940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / UK Company News: N American Gas Net asset value per share of the North American Gas Investment Trust expanded from 80.5p to 94.8p over the 12 months ended January 31. For the six months to end-January available revenue fell to Pounds 33,000 (Pounds 256,000 adjusted), equal to earnings of 0.09p (0.73p).
