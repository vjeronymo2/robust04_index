930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / Parliament and Politics: Poll gives Labour 12-point lead A MORI survey published in today's issue of The Times gives Labour a 12-point lead over the government, putting the party on 46 per cent, the Conservatives on 34 per cent and the Liberal Democrats on 16 per cent. Mr John Major's personal rating has taken a severe knock with 64 per cent of the public dissatisfied with the way he is doing his job, including 30 per cent of Conservative supporters.
