940225 FT 25 FEB 94 / World Trade News: BMW Rolls-Royce wins engines order BMW Rolls-Royce, the joint venture between the German car maker and the UK aero-engine group, is to supply McDonnell Douglas of the US with aero-engines for a potential market of 1,000 aircraft, Rachel Johnson reports from London. Its 20,000lb thrust BR715 engine was selected ahead of a rival engine from a consortium led by Deutsche Aerospace's engine subsidiary MTU.
