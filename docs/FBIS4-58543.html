BFN [Text] The Islamic Republic of Iran's Foreign Ministry issued a statement today expressing satisfaction at the dismantling of apartheid in South Africa and the victory of the African National Congress in that country's elections. The statement announced: Our country is ready to do all it can to expand bilateral relations in all fields. The statement refers to the Islamic Republic of Iran's support for the struggle of the people of South Africa to eliminate racism and for cooperation with black struggling groups and adds: The Islamic Republic of Iran has always supported the establishment of a popular government in South Africa through a general election in which all races participate, and which is based on the principle of one person, one vote. It is in that respect that Iran has sent four diplomats, through the United Nations, as observers to the elections in South Africa.
