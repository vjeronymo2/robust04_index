920530 FT 30 MAY 92 / Markets: At a Glance - New trust from Lloyds Lloyds Investment Managers has launched a new investment trust savings scheme linked into four trusts: First Spanish; German; German Smaller Companies; and Lloyds Smaller Companies. Monthly savings start from a minimum of Pounds 50 and lump sums from Pounds 500 on initial purchase (Pounds 250 subsequently). There will be a transaction charge of 0.2 per cent on all purchases, and a Pounds 10 selling fee. Meanwhile, Save & Prosper is launching a new unit trust on June 13 which will invest in a selection of 20 blue-chip shares. Capital Portfolio requires a minimum investment of Pounds 5,000. The initial charge is 5.5 per cent with an annual management fee of 1.25 per cent. There is a 1 per cent discount on the offer price of units from June 13 to July 3 and the trust can be held in a Pep at no additional cost.
