921107 FT 07 NOV 92 / Finance and the Family: At a glance - Small companies grow bigger 'The ingredients for a resumption of smaller company sector outperformance are now being assembled', according to John Houlihan of Hoare Govett in his latest weekly round-up. It was certainly a good week for small company shares; the Hoare Govett Index (capital gains version) rose 1.4 per cent from 1085.44 to 1100.12 over the seven days to November 5, while the County smaller companies index increased 1.2 per cent from 838.71 to 849.18 over the same period.
