BFN [Text] Lima, 12 May (EFE) -- The Defense Ministry today reported the arrest of nine members of the Maoist Shining Path [SL]. They were captured during an antisubversive Army operation in the northern Peruvian jungle from 6 to 9 May. Among those arrested are three women and the governor of Punchao village who tried to bribe Army members to free two of the arrested subversives. The Defense Ministry communique points out that an SL group occupied Cachicoto settlement on 7 May, murdered a "narco- terrorist," and seized 150 kg of cocaine base and $90,000. These events occurred in Huanuco and San Martin Departments in the northern Peruvian jungle.
