BFN [Text] Brother Lakhdar Ibrahimi, envoy of the UN secretary general, arrived in Sanaa this afternoon on a working visit to our country, during which he will meet with the brother officials in order to be closely acquainted with the current developments in the Republic of Yemen. In an arrival statement to the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', the UN envoy expressed his great appreciation for the responsibility entrusted to him in order to carry out this historic mission. He added: I take pride in the UN secretary general's assignment of this mission to me and by the unanimous support this nomination was met with in the Security Council, in addition to the Yemeni brothers' welcome of my appointment to his mission. Ibrahimi added: I am looking forward to the help and assistance of my brothers in this country to perform this mission. He went on to say: Dialogue should be restored among the brothers and the Yemeni people's love for their country is well known. We are looking forward to their cooperation with each other and with us in order to return Yemen to its previous known march. Concluding, Ibrahimi said: I will not answer questions at this stage until we meet with the brother officials and agree with them on the way to work together. He was received at the airport by brother Ahmad Dayfallah al-'Uzayb, Foreign Ministry under secretary for political affairs; Ambassador 'Abdallah al-Uhadi, chief of protocol at the Foreign Ministry; a representative of the Presidential Council protocol; Dr. 'Awni al-'Ani, UN representative in our country; Chadli Benhabib, Algerian ambassador in Sanaa; and (?some officials).
