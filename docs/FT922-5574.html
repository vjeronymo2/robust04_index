920529 FT 29 MAY 92 / International Company News: Good quarter for Magna HIGHER North American car production and lower interest rates helped Magna International, Canada's biggest independent car and parts maker, to achieve a fourfold jump in third-quarter profits. The surge came on a 27.5 per cent gain in sales. Net profit was CDollars 30.5m (USDollars 25.40m), or 89 cents a share, for the April 30 quarter, up from CDollars 4.4m, or 16 cents, a year earlier. Sales were CDollars 632m against CDollars 495m. Nine months' earnings amounted to CDollars 66.7m, or CDollars 2.13 a share, against CDollars 4.9m, or 18 cents a year earlier, on sales of CDollars 1.7bn against CDollars 1.5bn.
