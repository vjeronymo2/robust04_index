July 18, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Mark and Brian and the Diceman: Andrew Dice Clay took to the airwaves Tuesday morning as a guest on KLOS 95.5 FM's Mark and Brian Show.

The show's producer, Nicole Sandler, said that Clay took unscreened listener calls from 8 to 10 a.m. in his only local radio appearance. "It's the first time he's ever taken phone calls like that," said Sandler. "He really dropped the Diceman act and talked to people." She said he explained that what he does is only an act. "Some people were hostile at the beginning, but to my surprise, there wasn't one person who went away angry," said Sandler. Clay fielded 25 calls. BETH KLEID
