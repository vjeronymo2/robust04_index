August 3, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Former tennis star Bjorn Borg has "personally promised" to meet the debts of his fashion company, the president of the firm, Stig Sjoblom, said in a statement on behalf of the Bjorn Borg Design Group.

Borg, 33, announced last week that he will close the Scandinavian division of the clothing empire.
