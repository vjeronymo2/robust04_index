Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Talks between Libya and Chad on bolstering bilateral cooperation relations started today. The opening meeting of the talks was attended by the brother secretary of the General People's Committee for Justice and Public Security and the brother secretary of the General People's Committee for Unity. On the Chadian side the meeting was attended by Interior Minister Abderahmane Izzo, and brother Mamadou Mohammad, the minister delegate to the presidency in charge of national defense, war veterans, and war victims in Chad. The meeting was also attended by technical experts from both delegations.
