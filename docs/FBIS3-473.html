Language: Afrikaans Article Type:BFN [Text] The Portuguese foreign minister, Dr. Jose Manuel Barroso, has warned that there is not much time left for an all-inclusive political settlement in South Africa and that there's potential for civil conflict if this fails. He was addressing a news conference in Johannesburg after meeting CP [Conservative Party] leader, Dr. Ferdi Hartzenberg. Dr. Barroso said that he had urged Dr. Hartzenberg to be flexible and to consider participation in the election. Dr. Barroso, who is on a visit to South Africa, is to meet Afrikaner Volksfront [Afrikaner National Front] leader Constand Viljoen later today.
