BFN [Text] Javier Solana will be one of the 10 foreign ministers at the signing ceremony in Cairo. He will have a special place, just as his Norwegian colleague who mediated the talks. This afternoon, Solana highlighted how Spain will support Palestinian self-rule. [Begin Solana recording] In a few weeks we will provide 14 vehicles for the Palestinian police and telecommunications equipment. We will also provide a credit for civilian equipment for this same police force. In short, we will contribute to the most important element, that is to the issue of security of the occupied territories. [end recording]
