December 22, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NATION IN BRIEF;

WASHINGTON, D.C.;

BOY, 6, WOUNDED IN DRIVE-BY SHOOTING

Three teen-age boys were charged with the drive-by shooting of four adolescents and a 6-year-old boy as District of Columbia police continued their search for two other juvenile suspects.

Meanwhile, all the victims were listed in good to fair condition at Children's Hospital. The three teen-age suspects were scheduled to be arraigned on five counts of assault with a dangerous weapon. Police said the occupants of a car fired into a group of students on a residential street.
