BFN [Text] Ambassador Lansana Kouyate, the UN secretary general's acting special envoy to Somalia, returned this afternoon to Mogadishu after holding talks in the Kenyan capital, Nairobi, with Somali political leaders there. This morning he met Somali Salvation Alliance representatives, with whom he discussed the course of national reconciliation, the conference for the reconciliation of the Jubbada Hoose region, re-establishment of the police force, the judiciary, and regional organizations, such as the regional councils. Ambassador Lansana Kouyate held talks yesterday with General Omar Haji Mohamed, the chairman of the Somali National Front.
