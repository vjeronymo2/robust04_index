BFN [Editorial Report] Asuncion ULTIMA HORA in Spanish on 28 June publishes, on page 8, a 300-word unattributed report on the publication of the newspaper LA UNION. The report says the newspaper is published by the National Republican Association, Colorado Party. It replaces PATRIA, which was closed a few months ago. The report further says the director is writer Mario Halley Mora and that the newspaper rehired 96 of the 300 employees who worked for PATRIA.
