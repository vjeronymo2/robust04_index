BFN [Unattributed report: "Algeria: National Security and Foreign Intelligence Chiefs Replaced in Military Reshuffle"] [Except] Algiers, AL-HAYAH -- Three days before the march to be staged in support of the dialogue in Algeria, President Major General Liamine Zeroual yesterday made changes among commanders of the military zones and some senior Army posts. No information has leaked about the new appointments and changes among commanders of the military zones. The names of the commanders who have been pensioned off are surrounded by complete secrecy. However, a high-level source has revealed to AL-HAYAH that Fadil Saidi, director of foreign intelligence, has been moved to another post and that Gen. Tawfik, chief of the intelligence organs, is still in his post. The ALGERIAN NEWS AGENCY announced in the afternoon that Mohamed Douadah had been appointed director of national security, replacing Mohamed Tolba, who has been in charge of national security since January 1992.
