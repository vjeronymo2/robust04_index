BFN [By ITAR-TASS parliamentary correspondent Lyudmila Yermakova] [Text] Moscow June 24 TASS -- "I have said nothing about prolongation of powers of the Russian president," Federation Council Speaker Vladimir Shumeyko told ITAR-TASS today, commenting on reaction to his pronouncement. After looking through many newspapers, Shumeyko noted that "those who do not want political stability were extreme opponents of prolonged powers of deputies and misinterpreted everything".
