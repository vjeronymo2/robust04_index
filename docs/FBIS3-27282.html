Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanaa, February 21 (XINHUA) -- The three Chinese experts, who were kidnapped while working on a road construction project, were set free today without any condition, thanks to the efforts of Yemeni security organs. The experts belong to a technical team carrying out the project of building the Jahanna-Maareb highway in eastern Yemen. The three Chinese, the head of the technical team, the chief engineer and the assistant manager of the project, who were kidnapped by gunmen on February 12, were in good health upon their release.
