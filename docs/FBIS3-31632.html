Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Polish Ministry of Foreign Affairs [MSZ] fully supports the position of the NATO states with regard to military intervention in the conflict in Bosnia-Herzegovina. According to Jerzy Chmielewski, of the MSZ European Department, the current situation, which has arisen after the expiry of the NATO ultimatum to the Bosnian Serbs, should be used to consolidate the ceasefire and resume peace talks between the parties involved. Chmielewski's opinion is that as a transition stage, new areas in Bosnia-Herzegovina could be incorporated into the security zones.
