Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The Jerusalem Magistrate's Court has meted out a six month prison sentence to Tiran Pollak, a member of Kakh's Committee for Security on the Roads. Pollak was convicted of assault, disturbing a policeman, and rioting. Our police affairs correspondent Aviv Bushinsky reports the judge acquiesced to Pollak's request to incarcerate him only after the Passover holiday.
