May 23, 1990, Wednesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: OXNARD;

FORUM ON ALCOHOL, DRUGS SCHEDULED

County Supervisor John K. Flynn will hold a public forum about alcohol and drug-related problems at 7 tonight in Oxnard.

"I wanted to contact the community through this forum and see what the needs are," Flynn said. "I think too often we start off on these programs without contacting the community first."

The county Department of Alcohol and Drug Programs, which is sponsoring the forum, will use the feedback about its services to improve them, Flynn said.

The forum will be at the South Oxnard Community Center, 200 E. Bard Road. PEGGY Y. LEE
