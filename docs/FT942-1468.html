940624 FT 24 JUN 94 / World Cup '94: Colombia coach to quit after next game Colombia's coach Francisco Maturana, whose team are effectively out of the World Cup after two disastrous games, will quit after their final first round match, a team spokesman said yesterday. Maturana, 45, tendered his resignation after the United States beat Colombia 2-1 in a major upset at the Rose Bowl in Pasadena on Wednesday. They play their last group A game, against Switzerland, on Sunday.
