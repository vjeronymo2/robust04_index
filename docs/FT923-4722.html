920904 FT 04 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Nestor-BNA falls 12% to Pounds 2.11m Against a background of continuing strong market pressure, Nestor-BNA, the international healthcare and specialist personnel group, reported a 12 per cent fall in pre-tax profits in the first half to June 12. The decline from Pounds 2.39m to Pounds 2.11m was on sales down 5 per cent at Pounds 44.7m (Pounds 46.9m). The company said that BNA, the UK nursing agency side, had benefited from the restructuring in 1991 and its operating profits rose 40 per cent to Pounds 571,000 (Pounds 408,000). The interim dividend is maintained at 1.15p on earnings per share of 2.26p (2.58p).
