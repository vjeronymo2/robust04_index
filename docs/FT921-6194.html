920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / UK News (Employment): Truck staff accept 25% cut in pay NEARLY 700 staff at AWD, the small independent truckmaker based at Dunstable, Bedfordshire, have accepted a 25 per cent pay cut and 10 days' enforced leave without pay, the company said. It added that the measures were necessary in the short term to reduce operating costs before starting work on several overseas orders.
