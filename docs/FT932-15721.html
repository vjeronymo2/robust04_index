930410 FT 10 APR 93 / UK Company News: Sale of MCC offshoot raises Pounds 5m Price Waterhouse, the administrators of Maxwell Communication Corporation, has sold International Learning Systems (Japan), a wholly owned subsidiary of MCC, to Falcon Press Japan. The administrators said about Pounds 5m had been realised through the sale and debt repayment. A significant proportion of ILS's turnover relates to the sale of BBC English language courses, for which it is the sole agent in Japan.
