920630 FT 30 JUN 92 / Governor's greeting Mr Robin Leigh-Pemberton, governor of the Bank of England, yesterday welcomed the chairmen and senior officials from central banks of the former Soviet Union republics to a two-week seminar at the Bank's Centre for Central Banking Studies in London. The seminar has attracted representatives from 13 of the 15 newly created central banks and will cover such issues as the creation of separate currencies.
