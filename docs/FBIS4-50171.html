BFN [Text] Beijing, May 13 (XINHUA) -- The Chinese mainland is to dispatch a 160-member delegation to Hong Kong to hold a trade and investment fair on the 23rd of this month. The members of the delegation headed by Zheng Silin, deputy minister [ministry] of foreign trade and economic cooperation (MOFTEC), will be from 24 provinces, autonomous regions and municipalities, as well as four ministries. This will be the first time for the mainland to sponsor such comprehensive negotiations on trade and investment in Hong Kong. A MOFTEC official told a press conference held here today that the delegation would bring 760 projects covering transportation, energy, light industry, electricity, raw material and advanced technology. The delegation is expected to be co-sponsored by MOFTEC and the Hong Kong Trade Development Council. Chinese officials will also speak at a seminar on foreign investment in China, to be held during the fair.
