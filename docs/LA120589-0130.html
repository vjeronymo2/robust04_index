December 5, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

FREE ELECTIONS

The clamor in Eastern Europe for free elections ought to be echoed in the U.S. -- Free elections instead of expensive elections! Stop the sale of our representatives to the highest bidder!

And now that our "isms" are all becoming "wasms," how about new terms for left and right? Forward and backward? Up and down?

PAUL JARRICO

Santa Monica
