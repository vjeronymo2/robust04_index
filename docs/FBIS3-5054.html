Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 1 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song today had a picture taken with the participants in the National Agricultural Conference. When President Kim Il-song appeared in the picture-taking hall, the participants enthusiastically welcomed him, raising stormy cheers of "hurrah!" He was presented with a basket of flowers carrying boundless reverence and loyalty of the entire agricultural working people. Present there were O Chin-u, member of the presidium of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] and minister of People's Armed Forces; Kang Song-san, member of the Political Bureau of the WPK Central Committee and premier of the Administration Council; Yi Chong-ok, Pak Song-chol and Kim Yong-chu, members of the Political Bureau of the WPK Central Committee and vice-presidents; and other cadres of the party and the state. President Kim Il-song highly estimated the great feats in labour performed by the participants in the National Agricultural Conference in the fulfilment of the tasks set by the socialist rural theses over the past three decades, holding the leadership of the party with loyalty, and posed for a photograph with them.
