920718 FT 18 JUL 92 / Letter: Testament to old dilemma Sir, The dilemma of economic forecasters discussed by Peter Beck (Letters, July 10) is not new: see Jonah 4 for the reaction of a forecaster of doom denied the satisfaction of being proved right - the people did listen, they did repent, and Nineveh was spared. N C Sloan, Kervesridge, Kerves Lane, Horsham, W Sussex
