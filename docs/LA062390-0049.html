June 23, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BELLA LEWITZKY AND THE NEA

What sanctimonious drivel coming from the lips of Lewitzky! I for one (but I am certainly not alone in this) say to her: "Go ahead and keep your pious hands off public tax monies!"

What people like Lewitzky and Michael Hudson of the (misnamed) People of the American Way fail to realize is that the proposed NEA restrictions now being considered by Congress in no way trample on anyone's First Amendment rights!

RANDALL J. PALTZA

Harbor City
