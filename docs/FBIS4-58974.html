BFN [Text] According to eye witness reports, many headquarters of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] in the governorates of Ta'izz, Ibb, Sanaa, Dhamar, Hajjah, Sa'dah, Mahwit, and al-Hudaydah have been taken over and converted to centers for detaining YSP members, other patriotic and democratic parties supporting it, and members of the gang of the al-Ahmar clan who support the pledge and accord document. These reports further state that ambushes are being laid for YSP members who are heading for their party centers. They are deceived into entering the centers and are then detained. Reports by people coming from the northern governorates say that this same method is being used on members of other parties participating in the dialogue committee, outside the [word indistinct].
