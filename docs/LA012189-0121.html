January 21, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

AREA ROUNDUP;

SOFTBALL CLINIC FOR COACHES STARTS TODAY AT FULLERTON

Cal State Fullerton will host its 15th annual softball clinic for coaches beginning at 8:45 a.m. today.

The clinic, which continues Sunday, will feature instruction in slap-hitting, an analysis of hitting, fundamental skills and tips on helping athletes cope with recruiting.

For more information, call 773-3495 or 773-2783.

Signups are under way for the Super Bowl softball tournament, which will be held Jan. 28 at Hart Park in Orange.

The tournament is open to C and D teams, and the entry fee is $160 per team. All teams are guaranteed at least three games.

For more information, call (213) 866-8685.

The Newport Harbor Lawn Bowling Club will hold its annual Meter Stock Open Triples tournament Jan. 31.

For more information, call 644-4138.
