January 8, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ROUNDUP

In other nonleague boys' basketball:

Anaheim 51, San Clemente 49 -- Victor Zambrano hit 3 3-point shots in the fourth quarter and Anaheim overcame a 9-point deficit by outscoring San Clemente, 22-11, in the final period.

Cypress 61, Westminster 39 -- Mark Johnson had 16 points and 18 rebounds to lead host Cypress. Mike Flowers added 13 points and Andy Babbitt had 11 points and 16 rebounds for Cypress (6-9), which led, 32-22, at halftime.

Estancia 83, Palm Springs 61 -- Visiting Estancia outscored Palm Springs, 13-2, in the last 2:30 of the second quarter to break the game open. Estancia had a 42-29 halftime lead. Mike Curtis had 26 points and Augustine Heredia added 20 for Estancia (13-1).

Magnolia 69, Santa Ana Valley 66 -- Jason Nester's 19 points and Jerry Jones' 16 led visiting Magnolia (6-6). Court Bickmore had 17 points and Bryan Singleton 15 for Santa Ana Valley (9-5). Santa Ana Valley shot just 38% from the field for the game.

Trabuco Hills 79, Mission Viejo 66 -- Rick Swanwick scored 23 points and Tim Manning 20 to lead host Trabuco Hills. Trabuco Hills led, 13-10, after the first quarter and then outscored Mission Viejo, 23-14, in the second quarter.

In Olympic League basketball:

Whittier Christian 77, Capistrano Valley Christian 48 -- Chris Wright scored 15 points to lead host Whittier Christian (9-3, 2-0). Bret Bigert had 15 for Capistrano Valley Christian (1-1 in league play).

Orange Lutheran 64, Valley Christian 58 -- Jason Neben had 21 points and Pat Bagatourian 17 for visiting Orange Lutheran (10-3, 3-0). Mike Blackstad had 16 for Valley Christian (10-3, 1-2).

In Arrowhead League basketball:

Southern California Christian 74, La Sierra Academy 40 -- Shawn Wiltshire and Kenny Cross each scored24 points to lead host Southern California Christian.

In Olympic League girls' basketball:

Valley Christian 47, Orange Lutheran 27 -- Rolynn Rienstra had 14 points to lead host Valley Christian (10-3, 3-0). Devon Koster had a game-high 16 points for Orange Lutheran (5-4, 1-2).

In Angelus League boys' soccer:

Mater Dei 3, Bishop Amat 0 -- Matt Tanner had 2 goals and Marty Williams added a third for Mater Dei (6-5).

In Olympic League girls' soccer:

Valley Christian 2, Orange Lutheran 1 -- Christina Riedmann had the only goal for visiting Orange Lutheran.
