930116 FT 16 JAN 93 / Markets: Pause for smaller companies - At a glance Smaller company shares paused for breath after their recent rally. The County index rose 0.5 per cent over the week to January 14 from 968.43 to 973.25; the Hoare Govett index (capital gains version) fell 0.1 per cent from 1252.69 to 1251.67 over the same period.
