921130 FT 30 NOV 92 / CSO turns to environment THE Central Statistical Office is considering new ways of evaluating the harmful effects of human activities on the environment in response to a United Nations proposal that all member states should establish integrated economic and environmental national accounts. In an article in the latest edition of Economic Trends, the CSO examines how environmental statistics might be presented to clarify the interaction of the economy and natural resources. Environmental Issues and the National Accounts, Economic Trends, CSO, Great George Street, London SW1P 3AQ. Pounds 2.
