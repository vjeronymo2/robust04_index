BFN [Report by ``special correspondent'' Kang You-feng in San Jose from the "Hookup" program] [Text] At a joint news conference held by Republic of China [ROC] President Li and Costa Rican President-elect Figueres, President Li said he has a dream of transferring state power to another person in a peaceful manner during his lifetime. He said this would be his happiest wish. Meanwhile, President Li also called on Communist China to establish the system of the peaceful transfer of state power based on the concept that sovereign authority rests with the people. Especially to express high respect and courtesy, Costa Rican President-elect Figueres held a joint news conference attended by the heads of state of both ROC and Costa Rica. Owing to the fact that the transfer of state power from the old president to the new president in Costa Rica will take place soon, reporters from various countries who are going to cover the presidential inauguration ceremony all seized the opportunity to attend the news conference. The site of the news conference was crowded with people. When a reporter asked President Li about his views on the democratic politics in Costa Rica and the enlightenment of Costa Rica's democratic experience, President Li pointed out that in recent years, Costa Rica has been a very democratic country because all systems of the country are based on the concept that sovereign authority rests with the people. In particular, the transfer of state power from the old president to the new president is being carried out peacefully. People will not feel uneasy because of the transfer of state power. At that time, when President Li noticed that there were XINHUA reporters present, he switched the conversation to another subject and pointed out: The biggest problem in mainland China is how to find a successor. We can understand, after examining democratic politics in several countries, that we must establish a system to ensure a long period of political stability. Establishing a good system is the basic way to ensure a long period of political stability. President Li said: [Begin Li recording] The sovereign authority rests with the people. This sentence is not new. In fact, however, it is also a new concept. Are representatives of mainland China here, too? Think about it! The biggest problem in mainland China is how to transfer state power to a successor. Please see that only people elected by the people can represent the will of the people. Our current goal is to improve democratic construction, which is precisely to establish such a system. [words indistinct] [end recording] At this point, President Li emotionally said he has a dream, which is not to transfer state power after his death. If possible, he will transfer state power peacefully to another person during his lifetime. He said that this is his happiest wish. [Begin Li recording] I have a dream. That is, if there is such an opportunity, I shall transfer state power to another person. This will be my happiest wish. In China, our ROC, no president has ever transferred power to another person during his lifetime. I hope that our society [words indistinct] will not have such problem. This is why I said that sovereign authority rests with the people. Meanwhile, our constitional reform is precisely for this purpose. Only in this way can the state achieve stability. To develop the country, we must have a stable foundation. [end recording] After President Li spoke about sovereign authority resting with the people under democratic politics and the establishment of a political system to ensure a long period of political stability, reporters from the Xinhua News Agency also expressed their views to ROC reporters that President Li's remarks are quite right and that China needs a scientific system and must not continue the past way of a certain person designating a successor. Su Chih-cheng, head of the secretariat of our presidential office, who accompanied President Li on his visit, also told the XINHUA reporters that President Li's remarks all were words from the bottom of his heart and he expressed his hope that they could reflect and report fully President Li's remarks.
