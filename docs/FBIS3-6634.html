Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 18 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- As is known from reliable sources, it is expected that the leadership of the Mongolian Social Democratic Party would be changed and renewed substantially. The leaders of the party seemed to decide to give up their places to new persons. The issue on this point would be settled by more than hundred of delegates of the forthcoming congress of the Social Democratic Party to be held from 10 to 10 March [dates as received].
