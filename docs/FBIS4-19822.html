BFN [Unattributed report: "`PARIS MATCH -- BVA Opinion Poll: The Fall of Prime Minister Balladur (Down 9 Percent)"] [Text] The latest "PARIS MATCH -- BVA" opinion poll, conducted after the withdrawal of the CIP [youth job training contract] but before the suicide of [President's Mitterrand's adviser] Francois de Grossouvre, shows a real drop in Edouard Balladur's popularity. He has lost 9 percent, posting his worst score since he took office. His popularity rating is negative for the first time and is now below that of Francois Mitterrand. It should be noted that the prime minister's rating has dropped considerably (down 15 percent), compared with people with more centrist views. The government as a whole has been affected by this fall from grace since, also for the first time, the French people -- if they were deputies -- would not support the government in a confidence vote (49 percent no's as opposed to 39 percent yes-votes). As a result of this situation, the Left has gained 3 percent in legislative election voting intentions. The Left-Right balance of power is now 34-44.
