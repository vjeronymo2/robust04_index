April 30, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

NAMES IN THE NEWS;

HUGH WASN'T IN DELIVERY ROOM

Hugh Hefner was relegated to the waiting room while the Playboy founder's wife, Kimberly Conrad, was giving birth to their first child.

"Kimberly didn't want me in the delivery room, and I didn't really want to be there," Hefner, 64, said in the May 7 issue of People magazine. "She just felt it was a time of embarrassment. She's a very private person."

Their son, Marston Glenn, was born April 9.
