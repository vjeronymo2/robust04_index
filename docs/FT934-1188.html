931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / Job seekers 'still face age barrier' Employers are failing to recognise a skills gap at the lower end of the jobs market which could be plugged by the use of older workers, a survey of personnel officers by recruitment agency Brook Street says. The survey showed that while employers were becoming slightly more receptive to the recruitment of the over-35s there remained a deeply entrenched tendency to recruit from people aged 18 to 35.
