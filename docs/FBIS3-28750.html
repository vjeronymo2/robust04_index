Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The states of the Nonaligned Movement opened their consultative meeting in Jakarta this morning. Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' participated in this meeting. In his comments, al-Shar' called for activating the movement's stands and decisions in order to take part in formulating international resolutions and resolving regional and international problems in the current international situation. Within this framework, the foreign minister denounced the barbaric massacre against Muslim Bosnians. He demanded that the world community not accept just an expression of regret and concern but take a measure to end this continued tragedy. [passage omitted]
