Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Yaqub Khan, special envoy of the UN secretary general, left the capital this afternoon at the end of his 24-hour visit to our country. [passage omitted] Yaqub Khan was received at noon today by President Maaouiya Ould Sid'Ahmed Taya. [passage omitted] After the meeting, the UN official made a statement to our colleague Dada Abdallah in which he said that he was honored to meet the president to whom he conveyed the greetings of the UN secretary general. He added that the aim of his visit to our country was to give impetus to the efforts being made to solve the Western Saharan problem which has now reached a critical stage, and these efforts require continuous consultation with the states concerned. At the same time he praised the support which our country continues to give to the United Nations in finding a just and peaceful solution to this problem. [passage omitted]
