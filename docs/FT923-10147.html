920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Sharp recovery at Freeman Group Pre-tax profits of Freeman Group, the USM-traded supplier of insulation material to the construction and process industries, recovered sharply to Pounds 550,000 during the six months to June 30. The outcome compared with a loss of Pounds 62,000 last time. An interim dividend of 2p (3p) is declared - the company failed to pay a final last year. Earnings per share of 4.9p compared with losses of 0.9p.
