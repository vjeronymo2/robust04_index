July 16, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

MODEL-TO-BE COMANECI IN SEOUL

Romanian gymnastics star Nadia Comaneci arrived today to tour Olympic sites and for talks on her modeling in South Korea.

Comaneci, the 1976 Olympic gold medalist in Montreal, fled from Romania last December and sought refuge in the United States.

She was scheduled to visit Sports Minister Chung Dong Sung. Korean fashion promoters have been interested in her modeling.
