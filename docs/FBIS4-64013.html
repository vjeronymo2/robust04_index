BFN [By an ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow May 26 TASS -- The majority of deputies of the State Duma, the lower house of the Russian parliament, believes that a sensible pragmatism should dominate in the Russian foreign policy, chairman of State Duma Ivan Rybkin said, receiving Iraqi ambassador in Russia Hasan Fahni Jum'ah on his request today. "I want to tell you frankly that we would like to establish cordial and friendly relations between Russia and Iraq," the State Duma chairman said. He noted that with all changes taking place in Russia, the state leaders should not forget their old friends while making new friends. Hasan Fahni Jum'ah, who has been performing his duties over the past five months only, emphasised Russia's influence at the international arena. "Taking into consideration a great potential of the Russian people, one can say that Russia will soon regain its past status in the world and restore the balance, which was completely destroyed during the recent years," the ambassador said. Hasan Fahni Jum'ah and Ivan Rybkin also discussed the strengthening of bilateral, interparliamentary cooperation between Russia and Iraq, the State Duma press service informed ITAR-TASS.
