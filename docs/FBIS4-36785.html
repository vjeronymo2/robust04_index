BFN [Text] Geneva, 13 Jun (MENA)--President Mubarak arrived in Geneva tonight. He was welcomed at the airport by senior officials of the Geneva canton; heads of the permanent missions to the United Nations; Dr. Mahir Mahran, state minister for population and family affairs; Mahir al-Sadiq, Egyptian ambassador to Bern; and Munir Zahran, head of Egypt's permanent delegation to the United Nations in Geneva. Accompanying President Mubarak on his trip are Information Minister Safwat al-Sharif and Dr. Zakariya 'Azmi, director of the Presidential Office.
