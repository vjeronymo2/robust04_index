November 29, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

London Voted Europe's Top Business City: American and European executives say London is the top European city for business, according to a new study. The survey, titled the European Business Real Estate Monitor, was commissioned by Cushman & Wakefield and Healey & Baker, two commercial real estate companies. Senior managers from more than 900 American and European corporations were polled on doing business in Europe.
