Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 23 (KCNA) -- Political parties and organizations of Guyana and Brazil denounced the U.S. insincere attitude toward the talks between the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and the United States. Fourteen political parties and organizations of Guyana including the People's Democratic Movement, the Amerindian Action Movement, the Agricultural and General Workers Union and the Guyana-Korea Friendship Association released a statement on March 7 saying that no problem could be solved in such a manner as putting pressure on one side or setting unreasonable preconditions. It stressed that the United States must come out to the talks without any precondition and fully discharge its responsibility and obligation in the solution to the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula. The October 8 Revolutionary Movement of Brazil issued a statement on March 11 strongly urging the United States to immediately rescind the unreasonable preconditions and satisfactorily implement all the steps of simultaneous action according to the DPRK-U.S. agreed conclusions. It also demanded that Japan and the South Korean authorities stop balking the DPRK-U.S. talks and promptly give up their nuclear arms development jeopardizing peace not only on the Korean peninsula but in Asia and the rest of the world.
