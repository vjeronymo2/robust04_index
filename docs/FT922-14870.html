920403 FT 03 APR 92 / UK Company News: North British Canadian assets up The net asset value of North British Canadian Investment was 112.3p at February 28, a rise of 23 per cent over the year. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 914,829 (Pounds 837,284) for earnings of 3.39p (3.1p) per share. A final dividend of 2.35p makes a total of 3.25p (adjusted 3.0625p) for the year.
