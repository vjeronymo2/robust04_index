Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, January 14 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The president of Mongolia P. Ochirbat answered questions of a correspondent of the newspaper ARDIYN ERH on the occasion of the second anniversary of adoption of the new Constitution of Mongolia. As the president announced, the new Constitution has legally consolidated achievements in democratization and renewal of the country, has become a legal basis for ensuring the state sovereignty, the human rights and freedoms, formation of the new state system, transition of the country to market economic relations. The Constitution, stressed P. Ochirbat, ensures national consent in solution of difficult tasks at the first stage of foundation in the country of a humane, democratic civil society. The president notes that in the past two years a big work was done on realization of provisions of the Constitution, including on legal consolidation of a legal basis for organization and activity of the state organs. The president calls the State Great Hural, the government and other organs to work actively for further intensification of the renewal processes in accordance with ideas of the Constitution. In the president's view, for the present it is early to speak about changing of certain provisions of the Constitution, about which talks are going on. The main thing, stressed the president, is a complete realization and observation of the basic law. In this connection President P. Ochirbat pointed out to the necessity of combining efforts of scientists for interpretation of provisions of the Constitution. The president does not deny a possibility of perfection in future of certain provisions of the Constitution, and expresses a confidence that its basic ideas will remain unchanged.
