BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 16 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to Hafiz al-Asad, president of the Syrian Arab Republic, on the occasion of the 48th anniversary of the independence of Syria. President Kim Il-song in the message said that after the independence the Syrian Arab people have made great strides in the struggle to defend the sovereignty of the country and national dignity and build a new prosperous society. He noted that the Korean people rejoice as over their own over the success made by the Syrian Arab people and always express firm solidarity with the efforts of the president and the Syrian Arab people for the independent development of the country and a fair and comprehensive solution of the Middle-East issue. He expressed the belief that the excellent relations of friendship and cooperation existing between the two countries would grow stronger and develop day by day.
