BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Excerpt] Moscow, 6 Apr -- Yuriy Meshkov, president of the Republic of Crimea, returned to Simferopol from a business trip to Cyrpus nearly eight hours late. An ITAR-TASS correspondent has been told by a member of staff on duty at Yuriy Meshkov's office that the delay occurred when Turkey, over whose territory the Crimean president's plane was due to fly, unexpectedly closed its airspace. As a result of the incident, which the member of staff believes to have been caused by a "misunderstanding," the plane landed in Simferopol at about 0200. [passage omitted]
