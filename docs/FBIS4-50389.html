BFN [From the "Morning News" program] [Text] An 11-member Vietnamese delegation will be in Shanghai on a visit from 13 to 15 May at the invitation of the CPC Central Commission for Discipline Inspection. The delegation is led by Do Quang Thang, member of the Political Bureau, secretary of the Secretariat, and chairman of the Inspection Committee of the CPV Central Committee. Wang Guang, member of the standing committee of the CPC Central Commission for Discipline Inspection, is accompanying the Vietnamese visitors on the tour. The delegation held a work discussion with Zhang Huixin, member of the standing committee of the Shanghai Municipal CPC Committee and secretary of the Municipal Commission for Discipline Inspection. Wang Liping, deputy secretary of the Shanghai Municipal CPC Committee, met the delegation.
