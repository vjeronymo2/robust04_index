Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Redha Malek has decided to form an inquiry commission into an incident that involved an armed attack on an Algerian prison yesterday during which more than 400 [as heard] prisoners escaped. Some members of the country's armed Islamic movements, who faced the death penalty, were among the escapees. The Algerian security forces are continuing to comb the area surrounding the prison to search for the escaped convicts and the gunmen who carried out the attack. Four prison guards were killed in the attack.
