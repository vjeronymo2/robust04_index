930511 FT 11 MAY 93 / Asian group fails to acquire bank MEGHRAJ BANK, the private banking arm of Meghraj Group, has failed to reach an agreement to buy the assets of Equatorial Bank, the bank with 3,000 Asian small business customers that was put into administration in March. Meghraj said yesterday it had not been possible to establish a basis for offering to acquire the shares or assets of Equatorial.
