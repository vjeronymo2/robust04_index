Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel has spoken out in favor of a political dialogue with the Russian nationalist leader Zhirinovskiy. In an N-TV interview, Kinkel explained the conditions that might lead to an entry visa being issued for the right-wing extremist: [Begin Kinkel recording] I would not be worried about getting involved in a discussion with him, not be concerned that we could not be a match for him and for his extremist theories and arguments. I did not say, however, that I was prepared to invite him to Germany tomorrow for a discussion or to take part in a discussion. However, if he does get invited, for example, to take part in a specific discussion by a political foundation or is invited by someone here in Germany at some point, if we have the opportunity to tell him quite clearly what we think of his theories, and if the right people would debate him, then I would say we could and should think about it at least. [end recording]
