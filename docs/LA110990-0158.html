November 9, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

JOGIS MAKES IT TO QUARTERFINALS IN BADMINTON

Chris Jogis of Manhattan Beach advanced to the quarterfinals of the U.S. Open Badminton Championships Thursday, defeating Iain Sydie of Canada, 7-15, 15-11, 18-17, at UC Irvine's Bren Center.

Jogis was one of three U.S. players to advance in men's singles. Tom Reidy of Tempe, Ariz., defeated En Horng Liu of Taipei, 17-14, 15-11, and Tariq Wadood of Redondo Beach won by default.

In second-round play in men's doubles, the U.S. teams of Ximing Yao/Guy Chadwick, Karl Knudsen/Peter Baum, Ignatius Rusli/Arilin Wiguna, Benny Lee/Jogis, John Britton/Reidy and Wadood/Martin French each advanced.

Play in the $15,000 tournament resumes at noon today.
