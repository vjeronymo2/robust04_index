930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Platon in talks with third parties Platon International, the USM-quoted instrumentation group which has rejected an offer from Wills Group, confirmed that it has received approaches from third parties. Last Friday, Wills, an industrial, electronic and automotive products company, announced that it had acquired 30,000 Platon ordinary shares (0.03 per cent) at 26 1/2 p each. Wills has received irrevocable undertakings to accept the offer from shareholders holding a further 15.7 per cent.
