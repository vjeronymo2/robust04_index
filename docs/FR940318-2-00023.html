Type of Review: Extension. Title: Report of Services for Children with Deaf-Blindness Program. Frequency: Annually. Affected Public: State or local governments; Non-profit institutions. Reporting Burden: Responses: 65. Burden Hours: 260. Recordkeeping Burden: Recordkeepers: 65. Burden Hours: 325. Abstract: Form OMB No. 1820&hyph;0532 submitted by programs supported by the Services for Children with Deaf-Blindness program, is the sole source of data on (a) Number of deaf-blind children served by age, severity, sex and nature of deaf-blindness; (b) Number of service providers trained/counseled; and types of services provided.
