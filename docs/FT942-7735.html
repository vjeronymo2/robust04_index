940524 FT 24 MAY 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Tees and Hartlepool Port Authority TEES AND HARTLEPOOL Port Authority has acquired R Durham, a Cleveland-based road haulage and warehousing company, for an undisclosed sum. The company operates a fleet of more than 140 vehicles and has annual turnover of Pounds 10m. Teesside Holdings, a consortium formed by Humberside Holdings, 3i and Powell Duffryn, acquired the Tees and Hartlepool Port Authority for Pounds 180m in 1992.
