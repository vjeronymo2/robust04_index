Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 29 (YONHAP) -- Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu will visit Russia in April to discuss the North Korean nuclear issue and President Kim Yong-sam's planned trip to Moscow. President Kim, who is currently in China, told the press over lunch Tuesday, "diplomacy with the four neighboring countries -- the United States, Japan, China and Russia -- is important in solving the North Korean nuclear problem, so I will send Foreign Minister Han to Russia in April."
