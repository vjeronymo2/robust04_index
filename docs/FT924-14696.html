921008 FT 08 OCT 92 / Commodities and Agriculture: Taiwan gold imports plunge 40% TAIWAN'S gold imports plunged almost 40 per cent in September from August because of falling demand from industry and individual investors, the Finance Ministry said, Reuter reports. September imports were 9.31 tonnes worth Dollars 102m (Pounds 57.30m) compared with 15.45 tonnes worth Dollars 174m in August. Higher prices cut demand from the jewellery and electronics industries but imports are expected to rise before the Chinese New Year.
