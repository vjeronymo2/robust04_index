CSO [Unattributed article: "A. Ster Proposed"] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] Andrej Ster was born on 12 June 1958. He graduated from the Ljubljana Law School in 1982. From 1983 to 1990 he served as a deputy in the Slovene Assembly. At the same time he was employed as the secretary of internal affairs and a member of the Executive Council of the Kranj Community. In the present government he has held the post of the state secretary in the Ministry of Internal Affairs.
