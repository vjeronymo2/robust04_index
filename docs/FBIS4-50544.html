BFN [Text] Singapore, May 18 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice Premier Li Lanqing arrived here today to attend the ongoing international conference on Southeast Asia, Vietnam and China, and the inaugural meeting of the joint steering council on the Suzhou industrial township project. Li will deliver a keynote address at the international conference, known as "Waves of the Future - ASEAN, Vietnam and China: Opportunities for World Business" on Thursday [19 May]. The conference, started on Tuesday, is being attended by some 800 delegates from 21 countries and regions around the world. Singapore Prime Minister Goh Chok Tong, New Zealand Prime Minister James Bolger, Vietnamese Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet, Singapore Senior Minister Lee Kuan Yew are among the speakers at the conference. During his four-day stay, Li will also chair jointly with his Singapore counterpart Lee Hsien Loong the inaugural joint steering council meeting on the Suzhou industrial township project on Friday.
