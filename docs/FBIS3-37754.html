Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Vitaliy Denisov report under "In Brief" rubric: "Does Gas Supply to Armenia Depend on Russian Military Too?"] [Text] Tbilisi -- Yet again the gas pipeline supplying gas to Armenia has been blown up on Georgian territory. The Armenian Government has delivered a statement to the Georgian Government protesting that, despite repeated assurances, the Georgian authorities are not taking resolute measures to prevent such incidents. Many people in Armenia are linking the future security of the only gas main via which fuel is delivered to this country with a mediator's role for Russian troops.
