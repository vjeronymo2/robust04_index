BFN [Report by Diego Andres de Gatica] [Excerpt] Epaminondas Gonzalez Dubon, president of the Constitutionality Court and one of the country's most oustanding men in recent times, was killed last night. He was gunned down while going to his house on Seventh Ave., No. 750, Zone 2. Gonzalez Dubon, 64, was in a 1990 Mitsubishi white sedan with the license plate number 259-899 when two unidentified gunmen driving by in a red car shot him in the chest. Gonzalez Dubon was rushed the Latinoamericano Hospital. He was admitted at approximately 2255, but died at 2310. Today, his body was taken to the Mancia Funeral Parlor in downtown Guatemala City, where he will be laid out and later buried. [passage omitted]
