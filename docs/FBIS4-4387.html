BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 31 -- French Foreign Minister Alain Juppe arrived at Narita Airport Thursday morning [31 March] for a four-day visit to Japan for talks with Japanese leaders. Juppe was invited by his Japanese counterpart Tsutomu Hata, Foreign Ministry officials said. Juppe is scheduled to have talks with Hata Thursday night over North Korea's suspected nuclear development program, Japan-France relations and the situation in Europe and Asia, the officials said. During his stay in Japan, the French foreign minister is also scheduled to meet Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa, Defense Agency head Kazuo Aichi, Finance Minister Hirohisa Fujii and International Trade and Industry Minister Hiroshi Kumagai.
