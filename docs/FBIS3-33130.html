Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Uruguay through 17 February: The Central Bank reported that the state registered a $654.2 million deficit in the January-November 1993 period. This was the result of the public sector's nonfinancial deficit of $194.5 million and the parafiscal deficit of $459.7 million. (Montevideo LA MANANA in Spanish 11 Feb 94 p 38) The European-Argentine group Miniphone will invest $21 million in the next 24 months on the installation of a mobile cellular telephone system nationwide. This service will benefit 80 percent of the urban population and 70 percent of the rural. (Montevideo LA MANANA in Spanish 11 Feb 94 p 12) The public sector's deficit increased $69 million in November 1993, raising the cumulative deficit in the past 11 months to $152 million, more than 1 percent of the GDP. (Montevideo EL OBSERVADOR ECONOMICO in Spanish 11 Feb 94 p 10)
