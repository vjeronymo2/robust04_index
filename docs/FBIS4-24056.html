BFN [Text] A civilian adviser from the British Ministry of Defense, Mr. Phil Jones, has assumed his duties in Maseru in secondment to the Lesotho Government to assist with the establishment of a Ministry of Defense. According to a press statement released by the British High Commission in Maseru, Mr. Jones' duties will include guidance on procurement, the production of annual plans, advice on financial central systems, the establishment of appropriate office procedures and the provision of hands-on training support. Mr. Jones is expected to return to the United Kingdom at the end of March 1995 on completion of his assignment.
