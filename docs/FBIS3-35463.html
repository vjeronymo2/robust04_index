Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] By firing 20 rockets, the infamous militia of Karmal-Dostam-Golboddin murdered and injured a number of our compatriots last night. A source of the General Command of Kabul Garrison told a military correspondent of BIA that Karmal-Golboddin-Dostam's militia made last night the targeted 20 Saqar rockets at ward 11 of the city of Kabul. As a result of this savage act, six of our oppressed and innocent compatriots were martyred and 10 others were injured. The injured were quickly taken to hospitals and treated. In addition to the loss of life, much damage has been inflicted on the residents of Kabul. The rockets were fired from an eastern direction, which is controlled by Golboddin's men.
