930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / Hopes for end to recession in Europe EC CONSUMERS are starting to see faint light at the end of the long recessionary tunnel, according to a survey published yesterday by the European Commission, Reuter reports from Brussels. Although consumer confidence remained depressed in April, there was a distinct sign of improvement for the coming 12 months, the survey said. In spite of predictions this week that unemployment would rise from April's 10.4 per cent to 12 per cent next year, the survey said consumers had become less worried about joblessness.
