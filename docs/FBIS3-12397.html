Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 28 Feb (MENA)--The Muslim Brotherhood group, whose activities are banned in Egypt, has issued a statement denouncing the bombing of a church in Kasrawan, Lebanon. The statement also denounces the criminal hands which terrorize the public and stab Muslims in their backs as they kneel down to pray. The statement says that enemies are hatching conspiracies to fan the flames of sedition among our people. We should foil these conspiracies through the unity of our ranks and the mobilization of our potential, the statement adds.
