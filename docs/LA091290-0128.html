September 12, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: COUNTYWIDE;

EL TORO'S MARINES PLAN RUNWAYS RUN

El Toro Marine Corps Air Station is accepting applications for its Sept. 30 Run of the Runways.

The third annual event will have races of 5 and 10 kilometers, plus a half-marathon.

The first event will start at 7:30 a.m.

Awards will be presented in each age division for first through third place, plus a wheelchair division.

The registration fee is $12 until Sept. 21 and $15 after that. Registration will be held on race day from 6 to 7 a.m. Proceeds will go to station's morale, welfare and recreation division to benefit Marines and sailors.

Entrants should make checks payable to MWR 0230. For entry forms or information, call the MWR office at (714) 726-2571. Information and applications can also be obtained in writing: MWR Dept. Bldg. 75, MCAS El Toro, Santa Ana, Calif. 92709.
