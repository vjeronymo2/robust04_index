920530 FT 30 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Greyfriars net asset value reduced Greyfriars Investment Company had a net asset value of 44.44p at March 31 against 65.26p a year earlier. After tax of Pounds 59,637 (Pounds 86,119) net revenue for the half year amounted to Pounds 218,543 (Pounds 225,055) for earnings of 3.12p (3.22p). An unchanged interim dividend of 2p is declared. Total income fell from Pounds 442,208 to Pounds 374,916 including a subsidiary's loss on its dealing account of Pounds 16,470 (Pounds 40,709 profit).
