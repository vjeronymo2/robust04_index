BFN ["Statement" issued by the National Security Council] [Text] In its meeting today, the National Security Council [NSC] reviewed the security and law and order situation throughout the country as well as the internal and external developments that affect this situation. The NSC appraised the results of the struggle waged against the domestic and foreign activities of the separatist terrorist organization and its supporters aimed at disrupting the indivisible unity of our state and the tranquillity and security of our people. The NSC was pleased to observe the successful results of this struggle. The Council stressed the need to utilize all means available to the state and continue the support accorded to the struggle waged by our security forces in a bid to totally neutralize such separatist activities and establish security and tranquillity in all parts of the country. Furthermore, the NSC underlined that, in accordance with the importance Turkey attaches to peace, stability, and security in the regional and international spheres, diplomatic efforts for the solution of existing problems should continue with determination.
