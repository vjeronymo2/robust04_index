March 25, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

A SUGGESTION FOR THOSE GOOD FOLKS IN LINDSAY

To solve the dilemma of the embarrassed educators who operate Steve Garvey Junior High School in Lindsay, Calif., the name of the institution should immediately be updated to read:

"Father Garvey Junior High School."

It would still be nondenominational, of course.

FRANK FINCH

Temple City
