930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Titon edges ahead as sales pick up Titon Holdings, the USM-quoted manufacturer of ventilation products, reported profits of Pounds 1.05m pre-tax for the six months to March 31. The outcome, achieved on turnover of Pounds 5.69m (Pounds 5.38m), compared with Pounds 942,000 last time and Pounds 1.94m for the full-year to end-September. Directors said the last two months of the first half had shown a 'marked' increase in sales. The interim dividend is lifted to 1.3p (1.13p), payable from earnings of 6.49p (5.82p) per share.
