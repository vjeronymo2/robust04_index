Language: English Article Type:BFN ["(Urgent) China Successfully Issues Global Bonds" -- XINHUA headline] [Text] New York, February 3 (XINHUA) -- China has successfully issued global bonds worth one billion U.S. dollars. According to Jin Renqing, Chinese vice minister of finance, the proceeds of this bond issue will be used to finance projects in sectors of energy, transportation and communications. Merrill Lynch and other international security firms have helped to form a strong syndicate group for this offering.
