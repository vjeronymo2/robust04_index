941110 FT 10 NOV 94 / Commodities and Agriculture: LME agrees to warehouses for US copper The London Metal Exchange board agreed yesterday to open warehouses for copper in the US. The locations, available from April 1, 1995, 'subject to there being suitable approved warehouse companies operating there by this date', would be New York/New Jersey, Chicago, St Louis and Los Angeles/Long Beach, the exchange said.
