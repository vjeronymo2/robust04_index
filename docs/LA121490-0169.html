December 14, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

LINDSEY LEADS BAYLOR TO VICTORY

Dennis Lindsey scored 23 points to lead unbeaten Baylor to an 86-62 victory over the University of Idaho today in the Daiwa Ball, an NCAA-sanctioned tournament.

Led by a pair of 3-point shots by Melvin Hunt and another by Willie Sublett, Baylor jumped to a 16-4 lead in the first five minutes and never trailed in the first half.

Idaho led for the first time, 42-41, three minutes into the second half on Calvin Ward's 3-pointer, but could not keep up with Baylor's shooting after a seesaw five minutes.
