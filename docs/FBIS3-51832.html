Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Communist Party of Chile [PPCh] has asked President Patricio Aylwin to normalize diplomatic relations with Cuba before concluding his term in office on 11 March. The PPCh directorate, headed by its President Volodia Teitelboim, Secretary General Gladys Marin, and Political Commission members Jorge Insunza and (Juan Andres Dago), met with the Chilean president for approximately 45 minutes. The PPCh, the Socialist Party of Chile directorate, and Party for Democracy leader Victor Manuel Rebolledo in the past few days have expressed support for resumption of diplomatic ties between Chile and Cuba.
