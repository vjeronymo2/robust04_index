930921 FT 21 SEP 93 / UK Company News: DCC raises Flogas stake to 60.1% DCC, the Dublin-based private industrial holding company, has succeeded in its aim to raise its stake in Flogas, the quoted Irish supplier of liquefied petroleum gas, from 29.9 per cent to 60.1 per cent. At Friday's close of the 325p-a-share offer acceptances had been received in respect of 14.6m shares against the 7.37m shares sought. The excess was eliminated by scaling down the excess acceptances from each shareholder.
