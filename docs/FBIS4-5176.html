BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 8 (KCNA) -- A national meeting marking the 20th anniversary of the publication of "On Further Developing Health Care for the People", a work of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, on April 9, 1974 was held here Thursday. The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il in the work expounded on a scientific basis upon the essence, character and main duty of socialist medicine which meets the demand of the chuche idea, stressing that hygienic propaganda, hygienic culture and hygienic and preventive work must be energetically conducted for the implementation of the party's policy of prophylactic medicine. Saying medical service for the people is not a mere technical work but an important political work, he elucidated detailed questions arising in the development of health care such as waging a vigorous movement for kind service, widely introducing Koryo therapy, consolidating the material and technical foundations of hopitals and improving health care by upgrading the level of specialization. Vice-Premier of the Administration Council Chang Chol delivered a report at the meeting. He said the work of the dear leader serves as a highly important guideline for developing health service in conformity with the demand of the developing revolution when the modelling of the whole society on the chuche idea has surfaced to the fore and thus defending and exalting the health care system of anthropocentric Korean-style socialism and is an immortal classic work that gives flawless answers to all theoretical and practical questions arising in the ultimate solution of the health care problem. Reviewing the achievements registered in health service over the past 20 years since the publication of the work, he stated the people's right to medical treatment is ensured on a high level as the people enjoy ever greater benefits of the communistic health care policy of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK]. The meeting was attended by secretary of the WPK Central Committee Kim Chung-nin, officials concerned and health workers.
