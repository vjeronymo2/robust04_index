940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / Housing market recovery boosted The recovery in the housing market strengthened last month with some housebuilders reporting a 'dramatic' increase in activity, a survey said yesterday. Eight out of 10 housebuilders saw an increase in the number of people visiting homes for sale and 70 per cent recorded a rise in reservations, the House Builders Federation found. The survey of 452 groups found that 79 per cent of housebuilders expect an increase in sales this year.
