BFN [Announcer-read report over still of regional map] [Text] The Turkish Air Force today launched an air raid on the Zhala camp in northern Iraq belonging to the PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan]. It is reported that all targets were completely destroyed in the operation. The Chief of Staff's Press and Public Relations Office issued the following statement on the issue: Intelligence information has been received that the bandits are regrouping in the Zhala camp in northern Iraq, which was rendered ineffective as a result of an air raid launched by the Turkish Air Force on 28 January 1994, and that they are preparing to engage in various activities. Between 1200 and 1230 today, the Turkish Armed Forces used 19 planes, eight of which have air to ground assault capability, to launch an air operation against the bandit settlement called the Zhala camp. All intended targets have been destroyed. The planes completed their mission and returned to their base.
