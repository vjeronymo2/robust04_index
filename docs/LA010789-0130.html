January 7, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

EXUDE WINS MTV'S VIDEO COMPETITION

Exude, a rock band from Anaheim, won MTV's monthly "Basement Tapes" contest Thursday for its video "Safe With You," an AIDS-awareness song. Exude's victory over three other groups means that MTV will play the video six times over the next month.

"It's going to help a lot," said Exude's singer, Frank Rogala. "It's going to be great exposure."

It won't be the first time the band has been in the national spotlight: Exude won Musician Magazine's "Best Unsigned Band in America" contest in 1987 and was included with nine other groups on a record produced by Warner Bros. and circulated among the recording industry.

"Somebody better sign us because we're not letting these other bands have a chance to win these contests," Rogala said, laughing.

Actually, "the music business seems a little cautious when it comes to us," Rogala said, adding that he hopes the MTV award might finally earn the group a recording contract.
