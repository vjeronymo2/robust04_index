941217 FT 17 DEC 94 / Weekend Money (Companies): New issues Research Machines, the educational computer group, this week managed to avert the attentions of computing services group Misys and unveiled a placing price of 175p a share for its forthcoming flotation. The price valued the group at Pounds 29.5m - some Pounds 10m below the valuation put on by Misys' 235p per share approach made the previous week. After being rebuffed by the RM board, Misys made a second proposal to acquire a 29.9 per cent stake at a price of at least 210p a share. This was also rejected by RM. The float will raise just Pounds 200,000 net for the company. The listing is being instigated ostensibly to enhance the company's profile.
