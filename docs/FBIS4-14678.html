BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anatoliy Yurkin] [Text] Moscow, 2 Apr -- Three border detachments and the Pytalovo and Pechory border checkpoints have been reinforced in order to guard the Russian-Estonian border, an ITAR-TASS correspondent was informed at the press office of the Russian border troops. This is related to the incident that occurred there on Friday 1 April when two unidentified persons fired a pistol at a border detail. The Russian border troops' press office reports that customs checks are being carried out according to special procedures. Together with Ministry of Internal Affairs bodies, reserve forces of the border troops are searching for the criminals. The search staff, which was set up immediately after the tragic events, is being led by Lieutenant General Vladimir Vyunov, commander of the North-West Border District. An ITAR-TASS correspondent has learned from the headquarters of Russia's border troops that the Estonian side has expressed its readiness to assist in the search for the criminals, who went to ground on the territory of the republic after committing their crime.
