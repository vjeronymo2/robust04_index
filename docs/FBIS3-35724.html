Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Minsk, 28 Jan (BELINFORM-TASS) -- Following the reports that Stanislav Shushkevich has responded positively to the proposal of Vladimir Shumeyko, chairman of the Federation Council of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly, that Shushkevich should be given the post of chairman of the CIS Interparliamentary Assembly, Ivan Korotchenya, executive secretary of the Commonwealth of Independent States, told a BELINFORM correspondent today that only a head of parliament can occupy this post. Therefore, after Stanislav Shushkevich's resignation his appointment to this post is out of the question.
