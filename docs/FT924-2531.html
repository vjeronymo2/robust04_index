921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / World Trade News: Taiwan pledge Taiwan's China Steel, the island's largest steel maker, said yesterday it would invest TDollars 67.35bn (Pounds 1.75bn) to boost output capacity 43 per cent over the next five years, Reuter reports from Taipei. The company expects steel demand in Taiwan to rise by 1m tonnes a year over the next few years.
