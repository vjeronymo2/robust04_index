January 20, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: REAL ESTATE;

HUNTINGTON BEACH CO. BUYS RANCH WITH WINERY, VINEYARDS IN SONOMA

The Huntington Beach Co., a subsidiary of Chevron Corp., said it has purchased the 5,600-acre Gauer Ranch in Sonoma County.

The seller is Edward H. Gauer, former chairman of the Roos-Atkins clothing store chain.

No price was disclosed.

The purchase includes 400 acres of vineyards and a winery called Vinwood Cellars that produces 350,000 cases a year. The oil company unit said it would continue to operate the winery.

Chevron acquired majority interest in the Huntington Beach Co. in 1922. The real estate investment firm became a wholly owned subsidiary in 1987.
