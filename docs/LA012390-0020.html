January 23, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Cam Data Systems, Inc.: The Fountain Valley-based company posted a net loss of $169,814 for fiscal 1989, contrasted with a loss of $700,560 last year. Revenue for the fiscal year ended Sept. 30 rose 71%, to $5.7 million from $3.3 million. Cam Data designs and assembles cash register and inventory control systems.
