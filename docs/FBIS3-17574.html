Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 26 [Mar] -- In the opinion of Georgian Security Minister Igor Giorgadze, the Georgian people have already made their choice regarding the CIS. In an interview granted to the ZAKAVKAZSKIYE VEDOMOSTI newspaper, Igor Giorgadze said that according to the information obtained by the Security Ministry, 98 to 99 percent of Tbilisi population and some 95 percent of the total population of Georgia supported Georgia's joining the CIS.
