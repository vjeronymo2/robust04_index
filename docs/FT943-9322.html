940812 FT 12 AUG 94 / World News in Brief: Horror film actor Peter Cushing dies Actor Peter Cushing, star of 22 Hammer horror films, died yesterday, aged 81. Cushing, pictured left as Sherlock Holmes in The Hound of the Baskervilles, was best known for films such as The Curse of Frankenstein, The Mummy, and Dracula, in which he was often teamed with Christopher Lee. He made many other films, worked on the stage and for television, and was awarded an OBE in 1989.
