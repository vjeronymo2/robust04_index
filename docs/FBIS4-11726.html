BFN [Text] In the second phase of his visit to African countries, our foreign minister met the president and other officials of Burkina Faso yesterday. The Burkina Faso president described their talks as positive and called for increased political contacts and agricultural and development cooperation as well as for exhibitions of Iranian products in his country. Velayati expressed Iran's readiness to train people from Burkina Faso in the diplomatic, insurance, and banking fields. Our foreign minister described his meeting with Burkina Faso officials as significant; it led to agreements on forming joint economic commissions, offering Iranian goods to Burkina Faso, and on cooperation in the field of commerce. Velayati also met the prime minister of Burkina Faso yesterday. The two sides expressed hopes that a joint bilateral economic commission and exhibitions of Iranian goods in Burkina Faso would further expand bilateral relations. Velayati and his delegation left Burkina Faso for Namibia last night.
