May 13, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $54 Saturday when his two horses in the seventh race of his $9 triple, Swing Shift and T.V. Screen, failed to win. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is at $546.

Bob's bet today is a $25 win parlay from A Shade Faster in the fifth to Patapsco in the ninth.

Saturday's Bankroll: $600

Saturday's Loss: $54

Current Bankroll: $546
