February 7, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

TROUBLED KIDS: Juan Cardenas, 12, assigned to Juvenile Hall for accidentally shooting and killing a friend in the Mall of Orange last December, doesn't belong in the California Youth Authority with hardened offenders, county juvenile authorities said. . . . Only 2 to 3% of the county's youthful offenders go to a CYA institution. . . . The county's traditionally low commitment rate to CYA, Deputy D.A. John Conley said, is in part due to the variety of optional facilities not available in some other counties. Dana Parsons and Kristina Lindgren
