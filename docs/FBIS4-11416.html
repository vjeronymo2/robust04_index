BFN [Text] The Jose Marti Order has been presented to Zambian President Frederick Chiluba. The high award was pinned on the distinguished guest's chest by our Commander in Chief Fidel Castro. The ceremony is being held at this very moment at the Palace of the Revolution. Chiluba has been in Havana since yesterday on an official visit to our country. This afternoon, the African leader visited the center for genetic engineering and biotechnology, and one of the vaccine plants of the Finlay Institute. He also placed a floral wreath at the Jose Marti Monument in the Plaza of the Revolution in Havana.
