BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 8 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- The Association of Free Journalists of Mongolia (AFJM) has put forward a proposal to change the current status and jurisdiction of the national radio and television. The association is considering that it is necessary to reorganize the radio and television functioning currently as the state mass media means and create on their basis parallel operating stations. In reference to the character and property ownership there should be functioning state, public and private radio and television stations, the association notes. The availability of powerful mass media means belonging to the government makes it impossible to develop free journalistic notes the statement.
