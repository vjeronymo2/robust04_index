BFN [Report English by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe 18 May TASS -- Armed provocations on the Tajik-Afghan border continue. Several attempts by a group of supporters of the Islamic Revival of Tajikistan movement to force entry from Afghanistan through a number of sectors of Pyanj and Moscow frontier guard detachments into the republic's territory were thwarted by Russian frontier guards on the night of May 18. The same night the Tajik opposition militants shelled a number of Russian border check points in the Shurobad direction from the rear. The enemy weapon emplacements were neutralized by the Russian frontier guard detachment shelling. There were no casualties reported among the Russian servicemen. ITAR-TASS was informed of these facts by military sources in Dushanbe today. According to the sources, a large-scale operation to uncover and neutralize militant groups of the Tajik opposition, which penetrated the Tajik border territory during the past few weeks, is underway in the Shurobad direction in the zone guarded by the Moscow frontier guard detachment. Tajikistan's Ministry of Defence, Ministry of Security and Ministry of the Interior detachments are taking active part in the operation together with the Russian frontier guards.
