940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / UK Company News: DCS jumps to Pounds 0.4m Shares of DCS increased by 13 per cent to 68p after the computer software supplier reported an 84 per cent advance in profits and a return to dividends. Turnover in the 12 months to June 30 rose to Pounds 6.38m (Pounds 5.55m) including Pounds 562,000 from two acquisitions made in the final three months. Pre-tax profits jumped to Pounds 426,144 (Pounds 231,406) and the company returned to the dividend list after an absence of five years with a recommended payment of 0.5p. Earnings per share were 4.32p (2.69p).
