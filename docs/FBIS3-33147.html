Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Paraguay through 17 February: The River Plate Basin Development Fund granted Paraguay a $50 million loan, of which $30 million will be used to pave the San Ignacio-Pilar highway and $20 million to create an industrial development fund to finance industrial activities. (Asuncion HOY in Spanish 5 Feb 94 p 22) The Paraguayan trade balance registered a $524 million deficit in 1993, with exports at $748 million and imports at $1.272 billion. This represents a drop of 10 percent compared with the 1992 deficit of $580 million. (Asuncion ABC COLOR in Spanish 13 Feb 94 p 25) The Central Bank's net international reserves totaled $698.4 million in 1993, an increase of 14.4 percent compared with $610.5 million in 1992. (Asuncion ABC COLOR in Spanish 15 Feb 94 p 13)
