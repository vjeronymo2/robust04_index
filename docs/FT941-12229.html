940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / Angolan police reform agreed The Angolan government and Unita rebels yesterday agreed on a reorganisation of the national police force, a breakthrough in finding a settlement to their long war, agencies report from Lusaka. Composition of the force was a main stumbling block to finding a settlement to end 18 years of civil war. The talks started in Lusaka in November. The two sides had previously agreed on a ceasefire, which has not come into effect.
