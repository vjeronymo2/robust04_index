920526 FT 26 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Southern Electric in Pounds 11.8m deal Southern Electric has paid Pounds 11.8m in cash for 70 per cent of Rightmain, the parent company of MP Burke, a utility contractor for the major English and Scottish gas and water utility companies. Southern has put call options for the remaining 30 per cent. Consolidated net assets of Rightmain and Burke at March 31 1991 were Pounds 2.36m.
