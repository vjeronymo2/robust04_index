BFN [Text] Here is a report from the news desk: Press reports a short while ago said that PLO Chairman Yasir 'Arafat is suffering from a serious deterioration in his health condition that might require moving him from Tunis. He is expected to be taken to the royal hospital [King Husayn's medical center in Amman] this evening or tomorrow. Reports circulating among Palestinian leaders this morning indicated that 'Arafat's health has entered a serious stage. Denying this, Mrs. 'Arafat said that 'Arafat was suffering from bronchitis that forced him to stay in bed.
