May 14, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ON THE COVER

Call it a "Ties" breaker.

After seven successful years, NBC and the cast of "Family Ties" will part company as the final episode of the Emmy-winning series airs Sunday at 8 p.m. on Channels 4, 36 and 39. The show debuted Sept. 22, 1982; a photo from the early days is shown on the cover, along with a picture from later in the run.

In the finale, titled "Alex Doesn't Live Here Anymore," Wall Street-bound Alex (Michael J. Fox) accepts a lucrative job with an investment banking firm, a decision that packs an emotional wallop for the rest of the Keatons.

Originally scheduled for one hour, the final episode will be extended an extra 10 minutes by NBC to include seven additional minutes of dialogue and three minutes of spontaneous farewells by cast (Meredith Baxter Birney, Michael Gross, et al.) and crew.
