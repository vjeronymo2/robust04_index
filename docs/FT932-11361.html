930505 FT 05 MAY 93 / India tax and duties cut Mr Manmohan Singh, Indian finance minister, yesterday announced tax and import duty cuts worth Rs4.29bn (Pounds 86m) in his final proposals for the annual budget for the year which began in April, Reuter reports from New Delhi. Import duties were cut for items including car components, caustic soda, powdered industrial diamonds, silicon wafers, telecommunication components and life-saving drugs. Fresh Hindu-Moslem clashes erupted yesterday in Manipur, a north-east Indian state previously immune from sectarian strife, taking the death toll in two days of violence to past 100.
