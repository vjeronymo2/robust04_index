Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] A republic seminar-conference of chairmen of executive committees of the local soviets of people's deputies continues its work in Dushanbe. Its participants met the members of the presidium of the Supreme Council of the Republic of Tajikistan, yesterday. The issues relating to the population's social security during the period of transition to a market economy, financial and economic activities of the executive committees, and the strengthening of material and technical bases of the local soviets were discussed. Emomali Rakhmonov, the head of state and chairman of the Supreme Council of Tajikistan met the participants of the seminar-conference yesterday afternoon. He pointed out that the specific tasks of the local soviets were determined in the republican meeting that took place in Dushanbe on 25 February 1994. That is why, he pointed out, everyone has to resolve issues relating to a way out of the economic crisis and the successful implementation of the spring sowing. Similarly, issues relating to the repatriation of refugees, creation of new working seats, provision of food for the population, and other vital problems were examined during the course of the meeting. Rakhmonov said that the working commission has completed the work on the draft of the new Tajik constitution and asks the participants of the seminar-conference to express their opinion on ways of adopting the new constitution, holding the referendum on determining the form of state administration, and election of the new parliament. The head of state answered numerous questions from the chairmen of town and rayon executive committees.
