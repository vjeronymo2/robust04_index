920901 FT 01 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Stanelco makes Pounds 80,000 profit Over the 14 months ended February 28 1992, Stanelco made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 80,000. That included seven months from the subsidiary Stanelco Products, maker of induction heating and welding equipment. The group formerly traded as Merchant Manufactory Estate Company, and was reorganised at the end of last year. In 1991 the old group incurred an operating loss of Pounds 1.58m, to which was added exceptional charges of Pounds 11.6m. Earnings per share were 0.02p (46.85p).
