BFN [By Y. C. Tsai] [Text] Taipei, April 28 (CNA) -- Taiwan's efforts to promote industrial upgrading have paid off as heavy industry exports reached a record high in the first quarter, the Ministry of Economic Affairs reported Thursday [28 April]. Ministry tallies show that Taiwan exported US$108.30 billion worth of heavy industry products in the first three months of 1994, accounting for 53.8 percent of its aggregate exports, which totaled US$201.3 billion. The ratio of heavy industry exports to Taiwan's total outbound shipments represented a 2.5 percent growth from the same period of last year, while total exports increased only 0.4 percent, officials said.
