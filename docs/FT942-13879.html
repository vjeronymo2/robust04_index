940422 FT 22 APR 94 / UK Company News: Benchmark recovery The recovery seen in the second half of the previous year at Benchmark Group continued in the six months to December 31. Pre-tax profits for the property investment company were Pounds 1.24m, against losses of Pounds 3.08m, restated for FRS 3. The property division reported a turnround from pre-interest losses of Pounds 577,000 to profits of Pounds 415,400. The overseas division, which carries out portfolio investment, improved from Pounds 240,500 to Pounds 1.17m. The pre-tax result was helped by a write-back of provisions of Pounds 258,300, against a charge of Pounds 1.69m, and lower interest costs of Pounds 433,800 (Pounds 684,000).
