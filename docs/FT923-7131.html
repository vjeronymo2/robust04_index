920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / International Company News: Compaq suffers parts shortage COMPAQ Computer, the US personal computer maker, said in a filing with the US Securities and Exchange Commission it expects its third-quarter revenues and profits to be hurt by parts shortages for some of its products, Reuter reports. Compaq introduced several notebook, desktop and server products in June and said the initial response has been 'excellent.' Compaq said the lower prices for the products will substantially lower the company's average unit selling price.
