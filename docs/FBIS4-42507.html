BFN [Excerpts] Northeim (DDP/ADN) -- Approximately 3,000 people attended a demonstration against the right-wing Free German Workers' Party (FAP) in Northeim/Lower Saxony today. Clashes between demonstrators and police, in which several people were injured, occurred late this afternoon at the police cordon outside the home of Thorsten Heise, the chairman of the Lower Saxony and Saxony-Anhalt regional association, who was arrested on Thursday [2 June]. [passage omitted] Twenty-four-year-old Heise has previous convictions and is considered the brains behind many violent actions carried out by right-wing extremists. [passage omitted]
