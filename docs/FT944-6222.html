941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Budget 94 (Their Budget): The entrepreneur Bertie Grotian, 35: Former army officer, running a marquee-hiring company employing five people full time, and 10 casuals in the summer. Married with one small child. Earns: Pounds 21,000 Politics: Not sure. Verdict: 'It was encouraging in that it said the things I wanted to hear, like cuts in public spending. But the net effect will be a slight increase in taxes and less disposable income, which is not good for someone in business for themselves. The one thing that did please me was that I can now do employees' national insurance and tax quarterly instead of monthly. These changes lift a great and costly administrative burden.'
