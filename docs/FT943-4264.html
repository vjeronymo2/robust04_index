940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Porvair Porvair, the maker of microporous synthetic materials, reported pre-tax profits up by 12 per cent from Pounds 849,000 to Pounds 954,000 for the six months to May 31. Turnover for the period amounted to Pounds 10.2m, down slightly from Pounds 10.3m in the 1993 first half. The interim dividend is increased to 1.6p (1.4p), payable from earnings of 4.5p (same).
