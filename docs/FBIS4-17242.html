BFN [Unattributed report: "Peacekeeping Troops in Georgia May Be Russian Only"] [Text] It has become known that yesterday's consultations in Moscow between representatives of Georgia and Russia looked at the composition of international peacekeeping forces in Georgia. In the view of one of the participants in the consultations, who wished to remain anonymous, the peacekeeping forces will be fully manned by Russian military men, but will be under quadripartite control of the United Nations, CSCE, Georgia, and Abkhazia.
