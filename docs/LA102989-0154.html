October 29, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE: LAGUNA BEACH SPA HOTEL ADDED BY COAST CHAIN

Jett Mora -- owner of Orange County's Dana Point Inn and Santa Monica's Sovereign Hotel -- recently added the 66-room Laguna Beach Spa Hotel to his portfolio for $7.7 million. The Los Angeles investor reports that he is looking for more California coast hotels to add to the collection.
