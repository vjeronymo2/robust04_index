940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Middlesex advances Middlesex Holdings turned round an Pounds 81,000 loss into a pre-tax profit of Pounds 1.57m in the first half of 1994 on sales up sharply from Pounds 558,000 to Pounds 12m. The company said considerable progress had been made in providing support services to natural resource industries in the Commonwealth of Independent States. Earnings per share for the period came to 0.23p (0.04p loss).
