BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres has left for an extended visit to the United States and South America. During his stay in Washington, he will meet with U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher and Vice President Al Gore. He will also reportedly try to encourage the U.S. and other nations to quickly speed up the funding of the Palestinian police force in the Gaza Strip and Jericho. Peres will later travel to Mexico, Colombia, and Chile. He is due to return to Israel on June 1st.
