Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Report by Sefi Handler] [Text] Egyptian President Husni Mubarak telephoned Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin about 15 minutes ago. Rabin conveyed his appreciation for the efforts exerted in Cairo and his delight in seeing the end of the current stage of the negotiations with the Palestinians. Rabin voiced his appreciation for Mubarak and the other Egyptian figures who played a role in the mediation which culminated in a signed agreement. Husni Mubarak invited the prime minister to Cairo. Rabin said he will gladly come, but he will wait for the formulation of the entire agreement to be completed. There is still much work to be done, the prime minister told Husni Mubarak. When the entire agreement is formulated and its implementation is launched, I will come to Cairo, he said.
