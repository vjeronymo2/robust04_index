April 8, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

WATER WASTE

Did anyone notice the two photos on Page B3 (April 4) of water gushing from hoses, which were captioned "Stadium Gussied Up for Home Opener?" And on Page B6 was an editorial "Why Gamble With Our Water," in which we were wisely advised to make some minor sacrifices to conserve the water supply.

I laughed so hard, I went out and "gussied up" my petunias.

JUNE HUFF

Carlsbad
