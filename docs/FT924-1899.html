921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / Reforms on course, says Yeltsin RUSSIAN president Boris Yeltsin yesterday attempted to reassure the west that his country's reforms remained on course. Mr Yeltsin, pictured with Chancellor Helmut Kohl of Germany, who is visiting Moscow, said he had succeeded in protecting the reforms despite opposition from the Congress of People's Deputies.
