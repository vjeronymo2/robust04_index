Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Comrade 'Abdallah al-Ahmar, assistant secretary general of the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party, today received a Pakistani parliamentary delegation, led by Maulana Fazlur Rahman, chairman of the Pakistani National Assembly Standing Committee on Foreign Affairs. Attending the meeting were Shakir Is'ayyid, head of the Arab and Foreign Affairs Committee of the Syrian People's Assembly; Hasan Sibahi, People's Assembly member; and Naimul Hasan, Pakistani ambassador in Damascus. During the meeting, the talks dealt with relations between the Arab Socialist Ba'th Party and the Pakistani parties represented in the Pakistani National Assembly in particular, and between the two friendly countries, Syria and Pakistan, in general, in the interest of their two peoples. The talks also dealt with several issues of interest to both countries, particularly the peace issue in the region, and the issue of Kashmir. At 1000 today, Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam also received Maulana Fazlur Rahman, chairman of the Pakistani National Assembly Standing Committee on Foreign Affairs, and his delegation. Shakir Is'ayyid and the Pakistani ambassador in Damascus attended the meeting. The talks dealt with the situation in the Middle East and in Kashmir, and with relations between the two countries.
