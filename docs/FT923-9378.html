920805 FT 05 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Dudley Jenkins maintains recovery Dudley Jenkins Group, the USM-quoted supplier of direct mail services, continued its recovery in the year to April 30 with full-year pre-tax profits of Pounds 466,000. Mr Tylan Bahcheli, chairman, said the results had been achieved 'through flexibility, cost control and simply working harder'. The profit was a 37 per cent increase on last year's Pounds 340,000, which included a net compensation for fire losses of Pounds 135,000. Turnover rose slightly from Pounds 8.8m to Pounds 9.01m. Since the year end the acquisition of Market Location has been completed and Pounds 2.4m has been raised through a rights issue. Earnings worked through at 4.51p (3.63p). A proposed final dividend of 2.65p makes an unchanged total of 4p.
