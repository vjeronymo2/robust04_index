December 20, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: SANTA ANA;

COMMUNITY COLLEGE ENROLLMENT TO BEGIN

Enrollment for spring semester classes at Rancho Santiago College is scheduled for Jan. 7-18 at the college's three campuses.

New students who want to attend spring classes, which begin Jan. 22, should fill out an application as soon as possible.

Rancho Santiago College campuses are in Orange, Santa Ana and Garden Grove. Classes are also held at the Rancho Santiago College Centennial Education Center in Santa Ana, and at the Rancho Santiago Orange Adult Learning Center in Orange. Call (714) 564-6000 for information.
