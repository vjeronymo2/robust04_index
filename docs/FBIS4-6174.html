BFN [Text] Kuala Lumpur, April 19 (OANA-BERNAMA) -- Czech Foreign Affairs Minister Josef Zieleniec arrived here Tuesday for a two-day official visit to Malaysia. Zieleniec, accompanied by his wife Pavla Zieleniecova, and an entourage of senior government officials, economic experts, businessmen and journalists, was met upon arrival at the Kuala Lumpur International Airport by Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs Dr Abdullah Fadzil Che Wan. Zieleniec told reporters his visit here was to foster closer bilateral relationship. He also said that he would discuss ways to enhance trade cooperation and government-to-government relationship between his country and Malaysia. Zieleniec and his entourage will leave for Australia Wednesday.
