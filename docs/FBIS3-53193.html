Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] An IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldier was killed and another was wounded this morning in the Qezi'ot detention facility when a several rounds were mistakenly fired by an IDF officer. Our correspondent Yariv Ben-Yehuda reports: The incident took place during the morning head count at the Qezi'ot detention facility. One of the inmates suddenly charged the IDF soldiers there. The officer in charge who attempted to prevent the prisoner from escaping from the prison fired his personal weapon, hitting the two soldiers. Sergeant Ehud Sabitzky was killed and a reserve soldier was injured and taken to hospital for treatment. The families of the two were notified. The Military Police have started an investigation into the incident.
