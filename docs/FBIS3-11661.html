Language: French Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] On Friday, 18 March, Pope John Paul II renewed the call for dialogue with all the components of Haitian society that he had issued on 15 January. It is up to the Haitians, he stated in a speech to the Haitian bishops who came to Rome on their quinquennial visit, to build their future based on the principles about which you have reminded them recently. The pope mentioned in his speech Haiti's numerous problems to which are added, he said, the obsessive problems created by the political instability and the economic embargo and most of all the shattering show of a nation in distress which is struggling day after day in order to survive.
