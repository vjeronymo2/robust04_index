September 23, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

WHY ISN'T LASORDA GIVING SOME YOUNG PROSPECTS A CHANCE?

The failure of the Dodgers this year can be summed up in four categories: Kirk Gibson's leg, Mike Marshall's back, Eddie Murray's bat, and the fact that all of Tom Lasorda's lost weight went to his head.

EDWARD J. MONTAGNE, Agoura Hills
