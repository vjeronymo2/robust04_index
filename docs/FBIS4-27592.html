BFN [Text] Beijing, May 30 (YONHAP) -- '94 South Korean commodity fair opened at the Dalian International Exhibition Center in Dalian of China's Liaoning Province for a five-day run on Monday. About 3,000 pieces of commodities are put on display by 32 major and medium South Korean firms in the fair sponsored by the Korea Trade Promotion Corp. (KOTRA). The participating industries include the Daewoo, Sunkyung, Ssangyong and Kolon business groups and the Pohang Iron and Steel Co. This is the third South Korean commodity fair to take place in Chinese cities. The first Korean fair was held in Beijing in 1992 and the second fair in Shanghai in 1993. A total of 184 South Korean firms were doing business in Dalian as of the end of last year. Their investments totaled 140 million dollars, about 11 percent of South Korea's total investments in China.
