940810 FT 10 AUG 94 / UK Company News: CU Environment net assets improve CU Environment Trust raised net assets per share by 1 per cent to 120p in the six months to June 30 and by 8.2 per cent over the last 12 months. After-tax revenue dropped from Pounds 28,849 to Pounds 387 and there were nil earnings per share (0.16p). No interim dividend will be paid.
