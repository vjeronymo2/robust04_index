BFN [Text] Jibril al-Rujub and Muhammad Dahlan, who will be in charge of internal security in the West Bank and the Gaza Strip on behalf of the PLO, will arrive in the autonomous area on Tuesday [17 May]. Jibril al-Rujub told our correspondent Karmela Menashe he will arrive to the Allenby Bridge with several of his aides Tuesday morning, from where he will continue to Hebron. Al-Rujub expressed satisfaction with the rate of IDF [Israel Defense Forces] withdrawal from the autonomy areas. He said it feels very good to return after six years and five months in the diaspora, and 17 years in prison. Al-Rujub says the two nations have no alternative to coexistence. He defined this a national and religious duty.
