931223 FT 23 DEC 93 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton and Jute COTTON Liverpool- Spot and shipment sales amounted to 240 tonnes for the week ended 17 December, against 77 tonnes in the previous week. Improved demand brought moderate purchases, mainly in Cameroon descriptions. Mali Growths made some headway. JUTE December/January C and F Dundee: BTC Dollars 475, BWC Dollars 500, BTD Dollars 450, BWD Dollars 465. December/January C and F Antwerp: BTC Dollars 455, BWC Dollars 455, BTD Dollars 425, BWD Dollars 425.
