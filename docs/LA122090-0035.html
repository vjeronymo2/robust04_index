December 20, 1990, Thursday, Ventura County Edition

ATTITUDES ANTHONY SMITH

Age: 26

Residence: Camarillo

Occupation: Former electric guitarist with a struggling rock band called Decrepit Resurrection.

Recently got a sales position with a Thousand Oaks art gallery.

Outfit: Black pleated pants with a dark purple paisley shirt.

Fashion attitude: "I grew up in rock concert T-shirts and basically lived in Birkenstocks and shorts. Then I got this job, and so my girlfriend took me shopping. I wanted stuff that was fashionable but still comfortable."
