Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Chisinau, BASAPRESS 1/3/1994 -- Today, Ambassador von Moltke, deputy secretary general of NATO, who arrived in Moldova with a group of NATO experts concerning the "Partnership For Peace" program, had a meeting with Moldovan Minister of Defence Pavel Creanga, as BASAPRESS learned from Ion Mastic, chief of the Ministry Press Service. The sides examined military aspects of Moldova's participation in the program, which assumes joint military applications for peacekeeping, planning of the Armed Forces structures, control over conventional armament and the process of disarmament, exchange of information in the military field, mutual control on the air defense and other aspects. It was decided that Moldovan Ministry of Defence will appoint in NATO structures an officer for coordination of the republic's actions within the "Partnership For Peace" program.
