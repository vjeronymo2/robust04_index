June 3, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

HOME TRUTHS ABOUT LIFE IN TWO BIG CITIES

Re "The Big Move" (May 23): As an ex-Californian now living in New York, every day I'm asked why someone like me would be crazy enough to move from Los Angeles to New York (after all, most people move from East to West). I probably am crazy, but if you're not crazy, you don't belong here.

SUSAN MOSS

New York, N.Y.
