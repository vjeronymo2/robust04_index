BFN [Excerpt] His highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, the president, received Engineer Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, envoy of President 'Ali Salim al-Bid, this morning. The meeting was attended by his highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Zayid Al Nuhayyan, Abu Dhabi heir apparent and deputy commander in chief of the Armed Forces. During the meeting the two sides reviewed the recent developments in the Yemeni arena and the efforts to settle the Yemeni crisis and achieve peace and stability for the fraternal Yemeni people. Eng. al-'Attas said he values the role his highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan has assumed to restore peace to Yemen and his concern over the Yemeni people's interests, security, and stability.
