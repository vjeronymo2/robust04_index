BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko and Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev 23 May TASS -- Acting Ukrainian Prime Minister Yukhym Zvyahilskiy left for Moscow on Monday [23 May] to meet his Russian counterpart Viktor Chernomyrdin and discuss the Crimean crisis and Russian-Ukrainian relations. It became known to tass that it is planned to draft a package of agreements to be later signed by presidents of Russia and Ukraine. The meeting between the prime ministers was agreed upon in a telephone conversation of Boris Yeltsin and Leonid Kravchuk on 19 May.
