Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Russian Federation Defense Ministry Information and Press Directorate report under the ``Yesterday'' rubric: ``Session of the Russian Federation Defense Ministry Collegium''] [Text] Questions regarding the nuclear safety levels of nuclear munitions and nuclear weapons in the Russian Federation Armed Forces, and progress in fulfilling the Russian Federation Government-Council of Ministers directive and the Russian defense minister's order on carrying out primary tasks to fulfill international commitments in the sphere of chemical disarmament were examined at a Russian Federation Defense Ministry Collegium session 17 December 1993.
