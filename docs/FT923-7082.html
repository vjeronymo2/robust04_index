920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / Observer: Late arrival A lawyer, who had recently passed away, is met at the pearly gates by thousands of angels. 'Is this the normal size of the reception committee?' the lawyer asks St Peter. 'No,' replies St Peter, 'but we thought it would be nice to make a special fuss of you, because you are the oldest person ever to make it to heaven.' Puzzled, the lawyer asks why the reception committee is so sure. 'It's simple,' replies St Peter. 'We checked your time sheets and just added up the hours.'
