931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Hicking Pentecost up to Pounds 2m Hicking Pentecost, the textiles and industrial products group, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.65m to Pounds 1.98m in the six months to September 30. Turnover was Pounds 18.2m, against Pounds 13.6m. Earnings per share, after an increased tax charge, were 8.9p (7.73p) while the interim dividend is 1.55p (1.35p). Mr John Lister, chairman, said that all businesses within the textiles and industrial products divisions were trading profitably and the group was benefiting from strong order books.
