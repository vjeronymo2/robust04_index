Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] General Constand Viljoen's Freedom Front has submitted a list of candidates, including some Conservative Party [CP] members for the April election. The list was submitted only minutes before the deadline for the submission of election lists by the Conservative Party MP for Stilfontein, Mr. Peter Groenewald. Mr. Groenewald declined to say how many CP members were on the list. The Inkatha Freedom Party failed to submit a list of candidates before the election.
