920623 FT 23 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Investment Company assets improve Net asset value per 25p share of the Investment Company rose from 32.8p to 36.5p over the 12 months ended March 31. At September 30 1991 the figure stood at 36.65p. Earnings for the year slipped to 2.75p (3.14p). A final dividend of 1p makes a 1.375p (1.15p) total.
