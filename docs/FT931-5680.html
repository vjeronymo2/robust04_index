930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa CONCERN that the government is planning tough measures for its budget on March 17 weighed on a Johannesburg market already unsettled by worries about De Beers' year-end results, due next week. The overall index shed 30 to 3,367 and industrials ended 37 lower at 4,429. Golds gave up an early, small advance to finish unchanged at 947. De Beers was R2.75 down at R64.25, but off the day's low of R64.10, with analysts sharply divided in their views on the company's performance.
