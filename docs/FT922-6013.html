920527 FT 27 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Fulcrum cash offers closed Fulcrum Investment Trust has announced that its cash offers for its own shares have closed. The investment trust was due to be wound-up between 1991 and 1994 but has been seeking to extend its life via a rights issue and placing. The cash offers of 10.75p per capital share and 40p per income share were an alternative to the restructuring proposals. They have been taken up by 17 per cent of capital shareholders and 0.64 per cent of income shareholders.
