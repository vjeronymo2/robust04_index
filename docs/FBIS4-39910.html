BFN [Text] The head of the Armenian parliament, Babken Ararktsyan, who is visiting Moscow, said he supported Russia's plan for the settlement of the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict. In Ararktsyan's words, the position taken by Armenia fully coincides with the Russian position. Its main points are a cease-fire in the conflict zone, the disengagement of the warring sides and the introduction of the CIS peacekeeping forces into the buffer zone. These measures are designed to create conditions for resolving the status of Nagorno-Karabakh.
