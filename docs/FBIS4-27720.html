BFN [Text] Kuala Lumpur, Jun 6 (OANA-BERNAMA) -- Cable News Network (CNN) of the United States has agreed to carry a denial of its report that Malaysia supplied arms to Bosnian Muslims, Information Minister Mohamed Rahmat said Sunday. The television station would also contact the Malaysian Embassy in Washington to rectify the error, he told reporters in southern Johor state. CNN had recently carried a report saying that Malaysia had supplied light weapons to the Muslim army in war-torn Bosnia-Herzegovina. Mohamed said the government did not want to pick a quarrel with the CNN over the report but hoped that the western media including CNN would be fair and accurate in their reporting, particularly on Malaysia. We only wanted a clarification from CNN... But they said they will carry a denial, which is very good as we want fair reporting, he said, adding that CNN had written to him a few days ago.
