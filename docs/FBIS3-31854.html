Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Grounded Goods -- Unforeseeable Consequences"] [Text] Macedonia's [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] Minister of Economy Petrus Stefanov informed the head of the CSCE monitoring mission in Macedonia, Norman Anderson, that vast quantities of goods of vital interest for the Macedonian economy were currently held at the Salonika port and other routes throughout Greece. During their meeting they also discussed that large quantities of goods which are expected to arrive via Greece in the upcoming period, and concluded that if this situation continued, it would create unforeseeable consequences on the Macedonian economy.
