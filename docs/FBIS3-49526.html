Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Belgrade, 11 Jan (TANJUG) -- Serbian Patriarch Pavle has never said that the Macedonians have the right to their own church at any of the meetings between delegations of the Serbian Orthodox Church and representatives of the self-proclaimed Macedonian Orthodox Church, Patriarch Pavle's office said in a statement today. This was the reaction of the Belgrade Patriarchate to the claim by Archbishop Mihail, newly-elected head of the Macedonian Orthodox Church, which have recently been published in the media, that Patriarch Pavle said that "Macedonians are a people, they have their state, and they have the right to have their own church."
