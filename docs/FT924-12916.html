921017 FT 17 OCT 92 / Markets: Looking to Latin America - At a Glance Edinburgh Fund Managers is launching a Latin American unit trust which aims to take advantage of the resurgence of economies in that area. The initial charge is 5.25 per cent and the annual charge 1.5 per cent. There is a bonus offer of 1 per cent of units purchased during the launch period, which lasts until October 23. The minimum investment is Pounds 500. Perpetual is now offering its global bond unit trust to private investors, having made it previously available only to institutional holders. The minimum investment is Pounds 1,000; the initial charge is 5 per cent and the annual charge 1 per cent.
