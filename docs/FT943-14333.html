940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / Hutu moderate leader flies in Mr Faustin Twagiramungu, a Hutu moderate named by the Tutsi-dominated Rwanda Patriotic Front to head a national unity government, arrived in the capital Kigali on a UN aircraft from Uganda, saying his first priority was to reassure Rwandans fleeing a rebel advance, Reuter reports from Kigali.
