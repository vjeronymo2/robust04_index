BFN [Text] Cairo, 21 Apr (MENA)--The Palestinian and Israeli negotiators ended a round of negotiations in Cairo on the implementation of the Gaza-Jericho agreement with a meeting by the Civilian Affairs Committee this morning. Dr. Nabil Sha'th, adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and head of the Palestinian negotiating delegation, said the negotiations with the Israelis will resumed on 24 April to discuss the remaining outstanding issues. He said that only five clauses remain to be discussed in the general agreement between the two sides. Sha'th explained that the first air route to be established after an international airport is established in Gaza will be between Gaza and Cairo.
