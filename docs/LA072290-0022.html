July 22, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

ROCK AS IT NEARS 40: RUNNING ON EMPTY?

Doesn't Hilburn (after all, it is his job) listen to musicians such as Eric Johnson, Jeff Healy, the Pretenders, Neil Young or Jeff Beck? There is great, exciting rock 'n' roll out there if one doesn't limit him/herself to popular FM air play.

JAMES J. COX

Ontario
