921201 FT 01 DEC 92 / UK Company News: East Surrey slight increase to Pounds 2.92m East Surrey Holdings, a supplier of drinking water, reported a marginal increase from Pounds 2.86m to Pounds 2.92m in pre-tax profits for the six months ended September 30 in spite of a 16 per cent increase to Pounds 12.04m in sales. The interim dividend is being raised from 3.56p to 4.1p per share out of unchanged earnings of 19.9p.
