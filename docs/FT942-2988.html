940616 FT 16 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: Industrial sector is highlight in Johannesburg Industrial shares provided one of the main features of the day in a thinly traded session, the index climbing 33 to 6,757. The overall index receded 16 to 5,746 and the golds index shed 31 to 2,090. De Beers retreated R2.25 to R116, while Anglos lost R6 at R241. Gencor eased 30 cents to R11.70 and, in golds, Kloof declined R1.25 to R64.50. Argus Newspapers saw its first trade since its listing on Monday and jumped R4 to R15. The Johannesburg stock exchange, which faces a threat from the Merchant Bankers Association to form a rival exchange in South Africa, said yesterday that it had embarked on proposals for a so-called 'evolutionary' change in its practices. Critics of this approach had called for full deregulation.
