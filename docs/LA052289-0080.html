May 22, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: SANTA ANA;

SHOT FROM CAR WOUNDS MAN AT CENTENNIAL PARK

A shot from a passing car wounded a 21-year-old man as he walked through Centennial Regional Park in Santa Ana on Saturday.

Officers said Manuel Santos of Paramount was treated at UCI Medical Center for a groin wound and was released.

Santa Ana police said they did not believe the shooting was related to gangs.

The shooting occurred shortly before 2 p.m.
