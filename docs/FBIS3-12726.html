Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres says he has not received any official communication from the United States regarding the need to adopt further measures against the settlers. He said the government has decided to adopt measures against persons who are likely to constitute a threat. These measures include administrative detentions, transferring people from one place to another, and disarming people. Our correspondent Gil Litman reports that Minister Peres was speaking at the conclusion of a meeting with the EC representatives, the Greek foreign minister, and the commissioner for foreign relations.
