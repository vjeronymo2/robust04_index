940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / Friendly society woos life company Family Assurance friendly society plans to buy Templeton Life Assurance, part of the Templeton Marketing Group, for Pounds 1.75m. This is the first time a friendly society has tried to acquire a life assurance company. Templeton Life, which has Pounds 230m under management, markets and underwrites life assurance and pension policies. The acquisition on July 1 depends on Family Assurance members' agreement and approval by the Friendly Societies' Commission.
