DATES: This notice is effective July 27, 1994. Geological Survey American Barrick Resources Corp.; Contribution Acceptance AGENCY: U.S. Geological Survey, Interior. ACTION: Notice.

SUMMARY: Notice is hereby given that the U.S. Geological Survey has accepted from American Barrick Resources Corporation a contribution of $10,500 to support geological studies of the occurrence of gold mineralization to northern Nevada that are being conducted by scientists in the Branches of Central Mineral Resources and Western Mineral Resources. DATES: This notice is effective July 27, 1994.
ADDRESS: Information on the work is available to the public upon request at the following location: U.S. Geological Survey, Branch of Central Mineral Resources, Denver Federal Center, MS&hyph;935, P.O. Box 25046, Denver, Colorado 80225&hyph;0046.
