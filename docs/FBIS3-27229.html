Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Reporter Gai Yumin 5556 3768 3787] [Text] Beijing, 17 Feb (XINHUA) -- The People's Liberation Army's General Staff Department held a meeting today to commend 301 outstanding trainers of "four military skills" for militia units and 49 outstanding trainers of "four military skills" for reservist units. China's militia units and the Army's reservist units have been stepping up training of a contingent of trainersin recent years, thus bringing about a rise in training quality and the emergence of a large number of trainers of "four military skills." The commended outstanding "four military skills" trainers are the advanced representatives from the nation's militia and reservist units. They have a strong sense of devotion to the revolutioanry cause and to building up a reserve force for the national defense. They have a good ideological and workstyle; they study hard to improve their professional and teaching skills; and they have made positive contributions to do a good job in training militiamen and reservists.
