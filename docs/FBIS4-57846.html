BFN [Statement by Boris Moreno, private secretary for Panamanian President-elect Ernesto Perez Balladares, in Santa Fe de Bogota on 23 May; from the "RPC Television News" newscast -- recorded] [Text] President-elect Perez Balladares arrived at Eldorado Airport at 0900 today. He was accompanied on his approximately six-hour visit by his official delegation, which included future Foreign Minister Gabriel Lewis Galindo and presidential adviser Jose Luis Sosa. The president-elect was received at Eldorado Airport by Colombian Foreign Minister Noemi Sanin de Rubio and other officials. The president-elect made a courtesy visit to EL TIEMPO newspaper, where he met with the editorial staff for 10 minutes. He then went to EL ESPECTADOR, another important Colombian newspaper, where he spent approximately 20 minutes. At 1120, he was received by Foreign Minister Sanin at the Foreign Ministry, from which they will travel to the Presidential Palace to meet President Gaviria. They will discuss bilateral integration and a ferry service that will begin operations in October or November. The ferry will link Panama to Colombia and the rest of South America. They will also discuss drug trafficking and the struggle the two countries are waging against it.
