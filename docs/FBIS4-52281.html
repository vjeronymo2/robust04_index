BFN [Text] Seoul, May 7 (YONHAP) -- Foreigners have done more buying than selling in the Korean stock market since last week, securities sources said Saturday. Foreigners bought shares worth 176.3 billion won and sold 127 billion won in stocks for a net purchase of 49.3 billion won between April 24 and Wednesday. During the 10 days, they recorded net sales only on April 26 at 1.3 billion won and on May 2 at 6.3 billion won. In March, foreigners' net sales totaled 184.2 billion won and in the first 23 days of April, the figure came to 56.9 billion won. Foreigners appear to be buying stocks again in response to reports of conspicuous signs of economic recovery and the perception that prices in the Seoul bourse have bottomed out.
