940514 FT 14 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Personal Assets Net asset value per share of Personal Assets Trust rose from Pounds 75.18 to Pounds 85.34 over the 12 months to April 30, an improvement of 13.5 per cent. Available revenue for the period amounted to Pounds 322,000 (Pounds 378,000), equal to earnings of 212p (252p). A final dividend of 100p makes a 195p (180p) total. For the years 1994-95 and 1995-96 the board intends to declare dividends totalling 200p.
