August 19, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

CRITIC'S PICKS

'INTO THE NIGHT'

John Landis' terrific 1985 comic thriller takes us on a roller-coaster ride through contemporary L.A. when a harried aerospace engineer (Jeff Golblum) crosses paths with a beautiful woman (Michelle Pfeiffer, above) who's being pursued by four killers, one of them Michael Zand (above) A vast supporting cast includes Irene Papas, David Bowie and a raft of directors -- including Paul Mazursky, Roger Vadim -- in cameos.

(WGN Wednesday at 5 p.m.) Kevin Thomas
