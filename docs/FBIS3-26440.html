Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 7 (XINHUA) -- People and officials of all nationalities of China attended a get-together in Beijing to celebrate the spring festival. Li Ruihuan, chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), and Buhe, vice-chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress, attended the get-together. Wang Zhaoguo, vice-chairman of the CPPCC National Committee and head of the United Front Work Department of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee, said in a speech at the get-together that China is a united country with multiple nationalities. The current situation on nationalities work is good, which shows that the nationality policy of the party is correct. Wang said that over the past decades, more and more officials from minority nationalities had been encouraged and fostered and they had played an important role in safeguarding the unification of the motherland and enhancing the unity of nationalities. He said the establishment of the socialist market economic structure will bring opportunities for the economic development in the regions of China's minority nationalities. He called for seizing the opportunity so as to accelerate development and promote prosperity in these regions. His speech was followed by an excellent performance of songs and dances.
