BFN [Text] On Wednesday the North Ossetian parliament extended the state of emergency in the zone of the Ossetian-Ingush conflict till July 31. Under the parliamentary ruling the state of emergency will be in force in parts of Mozdok, Prigorodny and Pravoberezhny regions and several communities near the capital Vladikavkaz. Together with the provisional administration of the conflict zone the North Ossetian government should do everything to prevent the operation of terrorist groups, attacks on industrial facilities, army patrols and militia posts in the republic. Under the federal constitution the ruling of the North Ossetian parliament should be approved by President Yeltsin and the Council of the Federation. At a Wednesday meeting with the press in Moscow the speaker of the Council of the Federation Vladimir Shumeyko spoke for prolonging the state of emergency. He said the upper house of parliament would discuss the matter at its next session on May 31.
