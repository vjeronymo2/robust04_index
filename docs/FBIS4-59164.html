BFN [Text] Over 2,000 people from the two sides have been killed in the recent clashes between the Kurdish Democratic Party [KDP] and the Patriotic Union of Kurdistan [PUK]. Citing informed sources, IRNA has reported that the PUK's artillery attacks on most of the towns controlled by the KDP and the Islamic movement has wrecked large parts of residential areas, especially in Halabja, Khurmal, and Chamchamal. The recent clashes in northern Iraq between the KDP and PUK began last week in the town of Qal'ah Diza.
