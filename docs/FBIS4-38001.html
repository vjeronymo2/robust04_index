BFN [Excerpt] Kuwait City, 27 Jun (KUNA) -- Gulf diplomatic sources informed KUNA today that the ordinary meeting of the Damascus Declaration states will convene in Kuwait 5-6 July. The sources stated that the meeting, which was due to take place today, was postponed while awaiting the outcome of the ongoing political efforts to contain the Yemeni crisis, and the latest developments in the region.
