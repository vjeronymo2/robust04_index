BFN [Text] Large Serbian Army forces, supplied with armored cars, tanks, and other military equipment, have been stationed in Hani i Elezit. These Serbian Army forces have also been deployed in Paldenice village near the Ramuka quarter. Transporters and other accompanying armaments have been placed in the trenches and are supplied with antiaircraft machine guns. The Kosova Information Center reports that the Serbian soldiers and officers are performing military training and provoking the citizens of the area by raiding them.
