Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Stuttgart (DDP/ADN) -- Germany's Free Democrats are beginning the mammoth election year of 1994 without any definite coalition plans. "We will decide before each election whether, when, and which coalition intentions we have," FDP [Free Democratic Party] chairman Klaus Kinkel said at the Liberals' Epiphany meeting in Stuttgart. The FDP wants to be involved in determining the shape of the government after the Bundestag elections, Kinkel stressed. The FDP chairman said his party is aiming for "a double-figure" share of the vote. [passage omitted]
