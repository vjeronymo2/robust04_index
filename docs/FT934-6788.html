931123 FT 23 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Towles Towles, the Leicestershire-based hosiery and knitwear group, saw its pre-tax losses widen to Pounds 694,000 in the six months to August 31, against Pounds 537,000. Turnover dipped to Pounds 6.19m (Pounds 6.49m). Losses per 10p shares emerged at 18.9p, against 15p last time.
