July 6, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

CINCINNATI BELL BUYS TELEMARKETING FIRM

Cincinnati Bell Inc. said Wednesday that it has acquired the telemarketing company Automated Phone Exchange Inc. of Salt Lake City.

Apex, founded in 1979, is a privately held company offering inbound telemarketing services using toll-free 800 lines, as well as outbound services.

The Apex operation will be merged with the NICE Corp. of Ogden, Utah, to form a new entity, NICE-Apex.

The new company will be headed by the president of NICE, Seldon O. Young, and will continue to operate as a division of Matrixx Marketing Inc., a Cincinnati Bell subsidiary. Apex management will join NICE management to lead the new company.
