930115 FT 15 JAN 93 / Steel output falls by 3.5% STEEL OUTPUT in the UK fell by 3.5 per cent last year to 16.2m tonnes - 14.3 per cent below the 18.95m tonnes recorded in 1988. According to figures yesterday from British Steel and the British Independent Steel Producers' Association, output in the last quarter of 1992 averaged 277,800 tons a week, down 13.8 per cent on the corresponding quarter of 1991.
