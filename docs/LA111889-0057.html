November 18, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

CITY SECTION FOOTBALL ROUNDUP;

IN ANOTHER NORTH VALLEY LEAGUE GAME:

Granada Hills 20, Chatsworth 6 -- Quarterback Bryan Martin completed 15 of 35 for 178 yards and three touchdowns to lead Granada Hills (10-0, 7-0) over Chatsworth (3-5-1, 2-5), which was eliminated from playoff consideration.

Martin threw scoring passes of 10, four and four yards. Brett Washington led Granada Hills with 49 yards in 15 carries. Washington caught a 10-yard touchdown pass in the first quarter and a four-yard touchdown pass in the fourth quarter.

Chatsworth scored its only touchdown when linebacker Scott Lawrence forced a Martin fumble, and Montay Hardison returned it 43 yards for the score.

Granada Hills limited Chatsworth to eight yards passing and 86 yards rushing. The Granada Hills offense rolled up 236 yards but had only 58 on the ground. TODD JACOBSON
