920909 FT 09 SEP 92 / Poll indicates 'poor' training MORE than two thirds of workers believe their employers do not provide them with sufficient training, a poll conducted by NOP for the TUC shows. Almost seven out of 10 workers, of both sexes and all ages and classes, would like more training, the poll of nearly 1,000 people found. More than 80 per cent of people believe trade unions should bargain for better trade unions.
