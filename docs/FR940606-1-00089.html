47 CFR Part 73 [MM Docket No. 93&hyph;280, RM&hyph;8369] Radio Broadcasting Services; Lund, NV AGENCY: Federal Communications Commission. ACTION: Proposed rule; dismissal of.

SUMMARY: The Commission dismisses the petition filed by Keith E. Lamonica requesting the allotment of Channel 264A to Lund, Nevada, as the community's first local and aural transmission service. See 58 FR 62320, November 26, 1993. Neither the petitioner nor any other party filed comments reiterating an intention to apply for the channel, if allotted.
