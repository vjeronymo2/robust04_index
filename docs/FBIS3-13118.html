Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The capital city of Afghanistan, Kabul, was today the arena of heavy fire exchange among the warring factions. The fire exchange killed or wounded dozens of people. Several rockets landed near a mosque in a north-western district of Kabul, where the food consignment dispatched by the UN was being distributed, killing at least six people and wounding 30 others. At dawn this morning, the exchange of fire between the supporters of Prime Minister Golboddin Hekmatyar and President Borhanoddin Rabbani, in the eastern and southern suburbs of Kabul, left several casualties.
