930911 FT 11 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Reduced losses at Dragon Oil Dragon Oil, the Dublin-based oil and gas explorer formed through the takeover of Kirkland by Oliver Resources in April, has reduced its net loss for the six months ended April 30 from IPounds 100,000 to IPounds 95,000 (Pounds 89,000). Revenue from oil and gas sales was IPounds 62,000 (nil). Losses per share declined to 0.04p (0.1p). As the result of a series of farm-out arrangements Dragon is now the largest foreign owner of Philippine exploration acreage and will open an office in Hong Kong in October. The company is also seeking a listing for its shares on the Hong Kong Stock Exchange.
