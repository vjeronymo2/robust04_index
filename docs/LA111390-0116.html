November 13, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

GET READY: Laguna wasn't crowded enough? Now novelist MacDonald Harris (the pseudonym of UCI English professor Donald Heiney) has touted Laguna as his "cherished spot" to all 1.2 million readers of Travel & Leisure magazine. . . . "You can easily see why people choose to live here, and why they don't want to attract too many others," he writes. . . . No telling how many of Harris' readers will drive by next summer, says a chamber of commerce official, "but I walk to work, and I'm glad of it." Steve Emmons
