BFN [Text] The president, Mr. Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari, conferred Nishan-i-Imtiaz, military, on Commander of Turkish land forces, General Ismail Hakki Karadayi, in Islamabad today. Gen. Ismail Hakki called on the president and discussed matters of bilateral interest. Issues regarding regional situation also came under discussion. Later, in an impressive investiture ceremony, the president decorated Gen. Ismail Hakki Karadayi with Nishan-i-Imtiaz, military. The award was conferred on the Turkish general for his meritorious services in enhancement of professional cooperation between the land forces of the two brotherly Muslim countries. The ceremony was attended among others by chief of the Army Staff, General Abdul Waheed; Chief of General Staff, Lieutenant Gen. Farrukh Khan; Corps Commander, Lieutenant Gen. Ghulam Mohammad Malik; and other senior civil and military officers.
