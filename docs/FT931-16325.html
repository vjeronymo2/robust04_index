930107 FT 07 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Irish Continental advances to IPounds 4.13m Irish Continental, the Dublin-based shipping company which last year acquired the B&I Ferries operation, reported a pre-tax profit of IPounds 4.13m (Pounds 4.33m) for the year to end-October. That compared with IPounds 3.21m and came from turnover up from IPounds 30.9m to IPounds 98.2m. Earnings were 21.8p (14.8p) and the dividend 2.42p (2.2p).
