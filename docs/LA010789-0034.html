January 7, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

TROJANS' SWAN SONG IN ROSE BOWL HAD A FAMILIAR REFRAIN

Got an idea: Reserve the "bragging rights of L.A." until Jan. 2.

Got an even better idea: Minimize the Pac-10's embarrassment by reinstating the no-repeat rule, which kept the same team from choking in the Rose Bowl in successive years.
