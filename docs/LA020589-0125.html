February 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

A SPECIAL KINDNESS

Anyone who could read "An Act of Kindness in 1933 Is Still Remembered" (Jan. 22), by Bob O'Sullivan, and not be moved would be hard-hearted, indeed. That one act on the part of the manager of the Harvey House restaurant should have earned him a special visa through the pearly gates.

HAROLD G. CONNOR

La Habra
