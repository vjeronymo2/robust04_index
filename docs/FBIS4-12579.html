BFN [Text] The Iraqi prime minister, Mr. Ahmad Husayn Khudayyir, has said that his country will continue to support the Indian stand on Kashmir. He said that the relations between Iraq and India are based on friendship and cooperation. The Iraqi prime minister was talking to a visiting Indian delegation led by Mr. John Joseph, member of the National Commission for Minorities, in Baghdad yesterday. He reiterated that the Iraqi response will continue to be the same in the years to come.
