BFN [Text] Abu Dhabi, 23 Apr (WAKH) -- Egyptian President Husni Mubarak arrived in Abu Dhabi today from Beijing on a two-day visit to the United Arab Emirates [UAE]. Upon his arrival, he was received by UAE President, His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, several ministers, and senior civic and military officials. During his visit, President Mubarak will hold talks with the UAE president on international and regional issues of mutual interest, as well as ways to bolster bilateral relations in all fields.
