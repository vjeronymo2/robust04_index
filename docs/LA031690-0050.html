March 16, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Williams Back on NBC: Mary Alice Williams, now the mother of Alice Ann, born Jan. 6, returns to the NBC airwaves this weekend as co-anchor of "NBC Nightly News," "Meet the Press" and "Sunday Today" with Garrick Utley, who is on assignment in East Germany. Another new NBC mother, Maria Shriver, is set to return to her anchoring duties on "Sunday Today" April 1. BETH KLEID
