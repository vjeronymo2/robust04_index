BFN [Text] Vilnius, 14 Apr, BNS -- Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas on 8 April named Lieutenant Colonel Audronius Beisys, Army vehicles engineer, to be aide-de-camp to the president. A President's Office official said Brazauskas had no aide-de-camp until now because "we could not afford one." The principal function of an aide-de-camp is to assist the president in fulfilling his duties as the supreme commander in chief of the Armed Forces. Until his present appointment, Beisys, 41, served as acting commander of the Gelezinis Vilkas (Iron Wolf) infantry brigade. He is a graduate of a Soviet military academy in Leningrad and until 1988 served in a paratrooper brigade of the Transcaucasian command. Beisys began service in the Lithuanian system of defense in 1991. He is divorced and has two children.
