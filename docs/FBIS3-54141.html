Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Ilyas al-Hirawi telephoned Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad to convey the sadness felt by the Lebanese when they heard about the painful martyrdom of Staff Major Basil al-Asad. The two presidents cried as they exchanged sympathies. President al- Hirawi will attend the funeral in al-Qardahah tomorrow. The General Directorate of the Lebanese Presidency has issued the following statement: Joining his brother His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad and the fraternal Syrian Arab people in their painful tragedy that surprised us all, President Ilyas al-Hirawi is canceling his commitments for Friday, Saturday, and Sunday. May God bestow mercy on the deceased, Staff Major Basil al-Asad, and grant patience and solace to President Hafiz al-Asad and his family.
