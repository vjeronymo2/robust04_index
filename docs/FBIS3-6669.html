Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 19 (KCNA) -- The Asian Regional Committee for Supporting Korean Reunification on March 14 called on the governments, social organizations and peaceloving peoples of all countries to launch a signature campaign supporting "10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation for the reunification of the country" put forward by the great leader President Kim Il-song. The appeal said that Korea could be reunified only when the 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation based on the three principles of national reunification -- independence, peaceful reunification and great national unity -- was implemented and the Democratic Confederal Republic of Koryo founded. It demanded that the U.S. troops immediately withdraw from South Korea with their nuclear weapons, the United States stop interfering in the internal affairs of the Korean nation and the U.S.-South Korea joint military exercises be discontinued for good and all. It expressed full support of the DPRK's stand for a peaceful solution to the nuclear issue and urged the United States to promptly give up its attempt to destroy the socialist system in the northern half of Korea under the cloak of the nuclear inspection.
