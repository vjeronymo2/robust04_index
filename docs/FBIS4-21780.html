BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] The body of a person suspected of collaboration with Israel, Muhammad 'Ali Abu-'Ata, 28, a resident of the Rimal neighborhood, was found near a garbage dump in Gaza. Local residents told our correspondent Moti Amir that Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] 'Izz-al-Din al-Qassam members murdered him since he participated in several IDF actions against wanted Hamas people. According to them, more than six months ago Hamas members murdered Abu-'Ata's brother for similar reasons.
