930114 FT 14 JAN 93 / BNFL says Thorp report cautious BRITISH Nuclear Fuels said the financial assumptions in its unpublished report to the government on its Thorp plant at Sellafield, disclosed in yesterday's Financial Times, 'was careful to err on the side of caution'. It added: 'Our calculation that the UK will lose Pounds 900m if Thorp does not go ahead assumes that the UK does not have to pay back Pounds 2bn of early payments from customers and that alternative disposal can be found soon for used nuclear fuel.'
