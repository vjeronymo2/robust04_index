940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / UK Company News: EFT Group EFT Group, the asset finance company, reported record pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.03m for 1993, up 40 per cent on the comparable Pounds 1.45m. Revenue was up from Pounds 7.4m to Pounds 7.65m, of which Pounds 312,000 was from acquisitions. Mr Ted Black, managing director, said the company had experienced an excellent start to the present year with strong demand. Earnings per share rose to 4.01p (3.01p), the third successive year growth had been above 30 per cent. A final dividend of 1.04p is recommended for a 1.5p (1.3p) total.
