December 23, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

MEXICO;

48 DIE WHEN BUS PLUNGES OVER CLIFF

A bus overshot a turn and plunged down a cliff on a mountain road in the central state of Guanajuato, killing at least 48 people and injuring five, police said. The El Universal newspaper said that 60 people were on the Green Arrow bus Friday afternoon. Police said all of those who died were believed to be Mexican citizens.
