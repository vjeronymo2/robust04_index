940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / International Company News: ASB Bank New Zealand's ASB Bank, 75 per cent owned by Commonwealth Bank of Australia, posted an unaudited after-tax profit of NZDollars 23.06m (USDollars 13.09m) in the six months to December 31, a 15.5 per cent increase over the year-ago period, Reuter reports from Wellington.
