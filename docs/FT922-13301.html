920413 FT 13 APR 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 37m Irish packaging plant COURTAULDS ENGINEERING has won a contract to project manage the construction of what is believed to be one of the largest civil engineering contracts of its kind in Eire. The tabletting and packaging plant to be built for Wyeth Medica Ireland at Newbridge, Co. Kildare represents an investment of over Pounds 37m. Work on the 300,000 sq ft plant has already commenced and the total project will be completed within 13 months.
