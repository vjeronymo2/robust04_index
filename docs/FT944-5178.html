941205 FT 05 DEC 94 / Company News (This Week): Carlton Communications - Other companies The television and television services group is expected to announce a significant jump in pre-tax profit on Wednesday when it publishes its results for the year to the end of September. Analysts' forecasts range from Pounds 169m to Pounds 180m compared with Pounds 126.1m last time. It is likely that the company, which has been showing strong growth in its main businesses - video duplication, film processing and television - will come in at the top end of the range.
