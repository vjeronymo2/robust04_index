920709 FT 09 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Loss rises to Pounds 0.29m at depressed Torex Torex Hire saw turnover fall from Pounds 2.09m to Pounds 1.92m and losses increase from Pounds 228,000 to Pounds 290,000 in the half year to April 30. Mr Benjamin Longrigg, chairman, said turnover in the core business of tool and equipment hire, which was dependent on the construction industry, had fallen 30 per cent since 1990. Turnover in catering hire and other activities had risen 22 per cent, but the activities were not large enough to compensate for the fall in the main business. Losses per share amounted to 2.06p (1.57p).
