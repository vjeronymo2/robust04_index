BFN [Text] The validity of vouchers will not be prolonged after June 30 and President Yeltsin may sign a decree on post-voucher privatization soon, Vice-Premier, Chairman of the RF State Property Committee Anatoliy Chubays said. Interviewed by the Financial Information Agency on Wednesday, Chubays said that the program for post-voucher privatization approved by the government was to be submitted to President Yeltsin for signature no later than June 25. Chubays said that the bulk of funds (51 percent) raised by the post-voucher privatization beginning on July 1 would be left to the enterprises. About the mechanism of post-voucher privatization Vice-Premier said that simultaneously with the presidential decree effecting it the program would be presented to the State Duma for consideration. Chubays said that discounts to staffs were one of the key features of monetary privatization. Two types of discounts are planned. Under one option the employees will receive no more than 25 percent of privileged shares giving no right to vote free. Under the other the employees will be able to buy out 10 percent of simple (voting) actions at a 30 percent discount. The starting price of shares is expected to grow 600 to 700 percent, because the assets will be evaluated in January 1, 1994 prices.
