931012 FT 12 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Green Property in shopping centre deal Green Property, the Dublin-based property investment company, has issued 795,937 shares, or 5 per cent of its equity, to PDFM as general partner for PDFM Property Partnership at a price of 130p per share. In addition, Green Property (UK) and PDFM Property Partnership have jointly acquired the Broadway Shopping Centre in Devon for some Pounds 10m.
