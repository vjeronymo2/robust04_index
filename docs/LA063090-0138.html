June 30, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

MAGAZINE HONORS EX-CORONA DEL MAR VOLLEYBALL STANDOUT

Jason Perkins of USC, a former standout at Corona del Mar High School, has been named to Volleyball Monthly magazine's all-freshman team.

Perkins, a 6-foot-4 middle blocker, started on the Trojans' NCAA championship team last season. His USC teammate, Bryan Ivie, was named player of the year.
