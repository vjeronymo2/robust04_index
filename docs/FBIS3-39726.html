Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] "Positive signals are emerging": These optimistic comments were made by Prime Minister Ciampi about developments in Bosnia, following the Russian initiative (which Yeltsin had informed him about in advance). Defense Minister Fabio Fabbri has also expressed optimism: "These are encouraging developments. Moscow has played a positive role in the peace process." But we must "wait for things to become certain before deactivating the NATO intervention plan." In other words NATO aircraft will remain on the runway at Aviano.
