Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, Jan 3 (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa returned home this evening after a few hours' visit to Amman during which he handed King Husayn of Jordan a message from President Husni Mubarak and held talks with the Jordanian premier and foreign minister. Musa said the Jordanian monarch entrusted him with a reply message to President Husni Mubarak. The Egyptian top diplomat said his talks with King Husayn and Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali covered developments of the Mideast peace process and bilateral ties. The foreign minister voiced hope that the Palestinian and Israeli delegations would resume their talks shortly. He will present a report to President Mubarak on the upshot of his talks in Amman. Musa will hold talks tomorrow with chief of the PLO Political Department Faruq Qaddumi, who is due in Cairo later today.
