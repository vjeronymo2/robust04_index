April 28, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: PLACENTIA ;

SCHOOL TRUSTEE RESIGNS; APPLICANTS BEING SOUGHT

Stacy Holland, a trustee of the Placentia Unified School District, announced that she is resigning her position effective May 23 because her husband is being transferred to Illinois.

Holland, a resident of Placentia for 9 years, was elected to her second term in November.

Supt. Jim Fleming said the school board decided at its last meeting to make a provisional appointment to fill the vacancy instead of holding a special election. Both are legal options.

Applications will be accepted until May 12, and a final decision on the new board member may be announced as early as May 23, Fleming said.
