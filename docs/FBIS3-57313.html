Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sergey Ostanin] [Text] Moscow, 25 Jan -- Russian Defense Minister General Pavel Grachev today met with Belgian Defense Minister Leo Delcroix, who has arrived in Moscow on a two-day working visit. The Press and Information Directorate of Russia's military department told the ITAR-TASS correspondent that the prospects of bilateral cooperation were discussed during the meeting. The participation by battalions of both countries in the peacekeeping mission in the former Yugoslavia is given here as an example of the fruitful development of Russian-Belgian relations.
