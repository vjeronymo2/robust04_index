920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There was good demand with 12,726 packages for the day reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Landed brightest liquoring East Africans remained about steady. Better mediums sold at fully firm rates and lesser descriptions were often dearer. Price levels for Central African teas showed little change. Offshore good demand for the brighter cif offerings at fully firm to dearer rates: plainer sorts tended lower where sold. Fob teas from Mauritius sold at last levels. The highest price realised this week was 200p for rwanda pf. 1. Quotations: quality 165p, nom good medium 135p, medium 112p, low medium 83p.
