931108 FT 08 NOV 93 / People: Finance moves Michael Martin, formerly chairman of Kleinwort Benson North America, has been appointed president-directeur general of KLEINWORT BENSON France on the move of Denis Gautier-Sauvagnac to become delegue general of the Union des Industries Metallurgiques et Minieres, though he remains a non-executive director of KLEINWORT BENSON Ltd.
