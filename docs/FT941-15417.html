940115 FT 15 JAN 94 / Politicians to face bribes probe A Belgian Senate committee ruled yesterday that two politicians can be questioned about bribery allegations over the purchase of army helicopters from the Italian firm Agusta, Belgian radio said, Reuter reports from Brussels. BRTN radio said the justice committee had agreed to partially lift immunity of Socialists Guy Spitaels, head of the francophone regional Walloon government, and Guy Mathot, a minister in the Walloon government.
