September 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

MOTHER LOVE'S IMAGE

In her Sept. 3 letter on "Hot Radio" (Aug. 20), Claudia Anderson criticizes KFI talk-show personality Mother Love and urges that Calendar instead "focus on someone who provides (women) with a positive image."

In recent weeks Mother Love has called for increased tolerance and respect for the gay population; appealed to rap singers to tone down profanity and use their music to positively influence youths; urged full-figured people to have more self-esteem and others to respect them more.

Could it be that Anderson has trouble transcending Mother Love's race or her full figure?

ALVA MOORE-STEVENSON

Los Angeles
