930121 FT 21 JAN 93 / International Company News: Republic rises 13.9% to Dollars 258m in final quarter NET profits at Republic New York Corporation, the holding company of Republic National Bank and The Manhattan Savings Bank, rose by 13.9 per cent last year to Dollars 258.9m. That translates into Dollars 4.42 per common share, against Dollars 3.95 per share in 1991. Republic, controlled by Mr Edmond Safra, achieved these results in spite of nearly-doubled bad debt provision of Dollars 120m for 1992. Net income in the fourth quarter of last year was 14.8 per cent higher at Dollars 66.8m (Dollars 1.14 per share). Bad-debt provision was Dollars 35m, against Dollars 14m in the last quarter of 1991.
