BFN [Text] Shanghai, April 14 (XINHUA) -- Visiting Portuguese Prime Minister Anibal Cavaco Silva, accompanied by Liu Xiliang, chairman of Chinese Government Reception Committee and vice-minister of radio, film and television, toured Minhang Economic and Technological Development Zone and a village in the western part of Shanghai this morning. Silva and his party arrived here from Beijing by special plane yesterday. Vice-Mayor Xu Kuangdi of Shanghai, on behalf of Mayor Huang Ju, met with Silva last night. Silva said during the meeting that his country would like to contribute to the economic development of shanghai. Portuguese entrepreneurs accompanying Silva on his China tour held talks with their counterparts from Shanghai this afternoon. Silva and his party left Shanghai by special plane for Hangzhou, capital of East China's Zhejiang Province.
