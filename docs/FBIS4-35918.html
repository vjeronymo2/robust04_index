BFN [Text] His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad yesterday received a telephone call from Yemeni Presidential Council Chairman 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. During the telephone conversation, the two presidents addressed the developments of the situation in Yemen. His excellency the president expressed Syria's regrets over the bloody course of events in Yemen, and reiterated the call to end the bloodshed in Yemen and reinstitute dialogue to resolve outstanding disputes in Yemen to safeguard unity. His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad today received a telephone call from 'Ali Salim al-Bid, secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party. During the telephone conversation, al-Bid informed his excellency the president of the developments in Yemen. His excellency the president expressed the sadness Syria feels because of the bloodshed and infighting in Yemen. He reiterated, on Syria's behalf, his call for an end to the fighting, and for a return to dialogue to resolve outstanding disputes within the framework of Yemeni unity.
