BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, June 12 (A.A.) -- Thirty-seven terrorists from the separatist terrorist organization were killed and nine others surrendered to security forces in southeastern provinces, it was reported on Sunday [12 June]. Emergency Regional Governor's Office told AA [ANATOLIA] that 22 terrorists were killed in Eylul Mountain in Kulp township in Diyarbakir Province, seven were killed in Kuyulu village in Derik township in Mardin Province, six terrorists were killed in Balkaya region in Semdinli township in Hakkari Province and two others were killed in Dogantepe region in Eruh township in Siirt Province. Meanwhile, nine terrorists surrendered to security force in Diyarbakir, Adiyaman, Bingol, Hakkari, Siirt and Tunceli Provinces.
