920501 FT 01 MAY 92 / Rise in unit trust investment seen UNIT TRUST investments picked up in March with gross sales for the month reaching Pounds 956.2m, the highest figure since April last year. Sales were boosted by institutional investment ahead of last month's general election and by private investors placing funds in personal equity plans before the end of the tax year. Net sales for the month (gross sales minus repurchases) were Pounds 234.9m, and the value of funds invested at the end of the month was Pounds 56.2bn. Lex, Page 16
