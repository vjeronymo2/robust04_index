Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The first flight abroad from Abadan's international airport took off this morning. After 13 years of interruption the airport resumed its international flights as the first plane left Abadan for Dubayy. There are two weekly return flights by the Aseman [Sky] airways to Dubayy on Mondays and Fridays, carrying 80 passengers in each flight. Two return flights from Tehran to Abadan are also added to the [Aseman] domestic flights. These flights are also scheduled for Mondays and Fridays so as to assist the passengers in Abadan. This brings the total number of Aseman airways return flights from Tehran to Abadan to three flights per week. [passage omitted]
