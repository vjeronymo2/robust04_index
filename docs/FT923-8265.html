920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / International Company News: Dismay at store appointment THE appointment of Mr Arthur Martinez to head the troubled retail division of Sears, Roebuck, has left noses badly out of joint at P. A. Bergner, the bankrupt Milwaukee-based retail group, writes Nikki Tait. The company said yesterday it had been negotiating for several months to secure Mr Martinez's services as chief executive - and had filed a motion with the bankruptcy court to approve an employment contract. Yesterday, Mr Bertrand Maus, Bergner's chairman was quoted as saying he was 'personally shocked and angry that with virtually no notice to us, he (Mr Martinez) has chosen to join Sears, Roebuck'. Mr Martinez, previously a senior executive with Saks Fifth Avenue, is due to join Sears on September 1.
