940813 FT 13 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Increased losses at Birkdale Increased pre-tax losses of Pounds 4.53m, after exceptional costs of Pounds 1.75m, were announced by Birkdale Group, the advertising and marketing consultancy, for the year to March 31. Last time there were losses of Pounds 3.59m, restated for FRS 3. Turnover improved from Pounds 20.2m to Pounds 22.7m including Pounds 7.35m (Pounds 7.33m) from discontinued operations and Pounds 1.24m from acquisitions. The exceptional costs comprised of Pounds 715,000 for reorganisation including severance and redundancy costs and provisions of Pounds 1.04m for future holding costs of leasehold properties. Following the rights issue last year, losses per share were cut to 7.7p (12.4p). The shares closed 1p lower at 7 1/2 p.
