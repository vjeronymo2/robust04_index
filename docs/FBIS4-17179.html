BFN [Unattributed report: "Changes at the Border"] [Text] Following Brest Oblast, a border zone has also been established on the Belarusian-Polish border in Grodno Oblast. The oblast executive committee has approved a list of village, settlement, and hamlet soviets, which crisscross the border zone. The Border Troops have vacated plots of land located within 800 meters of the state border to their engineering facilities, including land on which barriers and other facilities were previously located. The oblast executive committee has also established a border zone ranging from between 200 and 500 meters along the Lithuanian border, including plots of land on which checkpoints have been erected. These regulations have been introduced according to the Belarusian Cabinet of Ministers resolution "On Measures for Implementing the Belarusian Republic Legislation on the State Border of the Republic of Belarus." Rayon executive committees and commanders of military units have been requested to explain these measures to the general population.
