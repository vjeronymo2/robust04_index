Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] Outside the Labor Bureau meeting, Housing Minister Binyamin Ben-Eli'ezer, who is close to Rabin, said if al-Asad would do what the late Egyptian President al-Sadat did, 16 years ago, he might discover that Israel is willing to withdraw completely from the Golan Heights. [Begin Ben-Eli'ezer recording] With all the problems that we had with Egypt, once President al-Sadat came to the parliament of Israel and made his declaration -- once he stated: No more war. -- I think he did 70 percent out of the problem [as heard]. He had convinced the Israeli people. If President al-Asad will do the same, maybe. He might be surprised and get maybe the same thing. [end recording] [passage omitted]
