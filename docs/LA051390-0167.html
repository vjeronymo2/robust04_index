May 13, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NO EASY ANSWERS TO ENVIRONMENTAL ISSUES

It is refreshing to read a discussion of technology versus ecological consciousness/correctness that acknowledges the give-and-take nature of humanity's relationship with the Earth ("Cyberpunk and the Future of Technology," April 19).

I'm tired of the knee-jerk extremists in both technology and ecology who refuse to find a level on which to relate and successfully carry out their plans.

I hope indeed that all of the hype surrounding Earth Day does not simply vaporize. It would be symptomatic of what Michael Schrage calls the "disgusting attitude" so prevalent in the United States regarding environmental issues.

JEANNE G. WYSHAK

Beverly Hills
