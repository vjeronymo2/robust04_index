December 13, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: SAN DIMAS;

TRASH PICKUP FEES RISE

Beginning Jan. 1, residents will pay more for trash pickup and curbside recycling.

The City Council approved a request by San Dimas Disposal Co. for a hike in monthly rates from $10.30 to $11.14 per household.

In a letter to City Manager Robert L. Poff, the company cited cost-of-living increases, higher fuel prices, increased landfill costs, the addition of plastic to the recycling program and a decline in glass and newspaper prices as justifying the higher rates.
