June 29, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Mexican boxer Gilberto Roman, who lost his World Boxing Council super flyweight title to South Korean Sung-kil Moon three weeks ago, died in a car accident Wednesday night. Roman, 29, was going to Acapulco when the vehicle in which he was riding struck a cargo truck near Chilpancingo, killing Roman and three others.

Roman (54-6-1) won the WBC title three times.
