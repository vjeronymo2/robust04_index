931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / Leisure group trial sent to crown court THE prosecution of six people following the Serious Fraud Office investigation into European Leisure, the entertainment and nightclub group, was yesterday transferred to the crown court by Bow Street magistrates. The SFO investigation, carried out with the Metropolitan police, centred on European Leisure's hostile takeover of Midsummer Leisure, the pubs and discos group, in May 1990. The six include Mr Michael Ward, former chairman and chief executive of European Leisure and two former directors, Mr Jeremy Howarth and Mr George Hendry. They are charged with conspiracy to defraud, conspiring to create a false market in the company's shares and theft. All six were remanded on bail.
