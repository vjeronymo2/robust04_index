BFN [Text] Beijing, May 18 (XINHUA) -- A top Chinese party leader said that the party must center its work around the country's economic construction and strengthen itself while furthering reforms, promoting development and maintaining social stability. Hu Jintao, member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, made the remarks during a recent tour of central China's Henan Province. Hu, who is a member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee, visited rural enterprises and villages in the east and north of the province during his five-day inspection. The party leader encouraged party members to carry on the fine tradition of hard work and thrift even now they are better off. Hu urged grassroots party organizations to take the lead in promoting the local economy, especially the collective sector, in order to achieve common prosperity through hard work. Hu stressed the necessity to strengthen and improve the leaderships of rural grassroots party organizations.
