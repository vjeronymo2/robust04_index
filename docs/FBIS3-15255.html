Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova] [Text] Moscow, 3 Mar -- Islam Karimov, the president of Uzbekistan, has completed his two-day official visit to Russia. This morning Boris Yeltsin, the Russian president, took leave of Uzbekistan President Islam Karimov at St George's Hall of the Grand Kremlin palace. Both countries' state delegations attended the ceremony.
