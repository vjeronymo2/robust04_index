941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Bornion - Contracts Malaysia has awarded a MDollars 700m (Dollars 273m) contract to build a hydroelectric facility to a joint venture company which includes a consortium from China. The 160MW plant will be built in Sabah state. A Sabah-based company, Bornion, along with the Chinese, will carry out the bulk of the work.
