Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 9 (XINHUA) -- Geydar Aliyev, president of Azerbaijan, and his party left here for Shanghai by special plane today. The Azerbaijan guests were accompanied by Yin Dakui, chairman of the Chinese Government Reception Committee and vice minister of public health. In a speech delivered this morning at the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences (CASS), Aliyev briefed his listeners on the domestic situation and foreign policies of Azerbaijan. Aliyev was warmly welcomed by CASS President Hu Sheng. President Aliyev held a press conference here yesterday evening.
