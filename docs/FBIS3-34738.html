Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Feb. 13, IRNA -- Leader of the Islamic Revolution Ayatollah 'Ali Khamene'i appointed 'Ali Larijani to the post of director general of the Islamic Republic of Iran Broadcasting (IRIB) here Sunday. The appointment was made for a period of five years. Larijani was also appointed as the member of the High Council of the Cultural Revolution. Larijani who, prior to his new assingment served as the minister of culture and islamic guidance, replaces Mohammad Hashemi who run IRIB for 13 years.
