BFN [Text] There was a terrorist attack in north Jerusalem a few minutes ago. As far as we know, three Israelis were wounded when a terrorist struck at them with an ax. Our police affairs correspondent Tali Katzenelson reports: We have only initial reports about the incident. Police forces arrived at the scene a few minutes ago. According to what we know, an Arab terrorist from East Jerusalem was traveling on a No. 25 bus near the entrance to the Neve Ya'aqov neighborhood in north Jerusalem when he pulled out an ax and began to strike at the passengers. The terrorist wounded three people before a soldier from a nearby military base shot him in the back, killing him. The wounded were taken to a nearby Army base.
