940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / World News in Brief: New York post for British editor Andrew Neil, editor of UK newspaper The Sunday Times, which is owned by Rupert Murdoch, is being seconded to New York for seven months to launch a current affairs programme for Murdoch's US television network. If the show, which is designed to rival CBS's 60 Minutes, is a success, Mr Neil is likely to sign a two or three year multi-million dollar contract.
