Fight Narcotics, Terrorism TA0705181994 Ankara TRT Television Network in Turkish 1700 GMT 7 May 94 TA0705181994 Ankara TRT Television Network Turkish BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Turkey and Jordan will cooperate in the struggle against narcotics smuggling and terrorism. An agreement was signed in Ankara today between Turkey and Jordan for the cooperation in the struggle against narcotics and barbiturates smuggling, international terrorism, and organized crime. During the signing ceremony, Interior Minister Nahit Mentese said that the cooperation envisaged in the agreement will contribute to the development of relations in other fields as well. [Begin Mentese recording] This agreement will result in effective and comprehensive cooperation in the struggle against international narcotics smuggling, international terrorism, and other organized crime. It has also been decided to establish a joint committee to be comprised of the representatives of the two sides with the aim of supervising the implementation of this cooperation and eliminating its shortcomings. Moreover, under the agreement, our countries will exchange experts as well as information and documents. [end recording] In turn, Jordanian Interior Minister Salamah Hammad said that the agreement will further increase bilateral relations.
