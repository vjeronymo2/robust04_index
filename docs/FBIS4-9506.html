BFN [Text] The Alliance of Democrats of the Slovak Republic and the Alternative of Political Realism have merged into a new political body called the Democratic Union of Slovakia [Demokraticka Unia Slovenska--DEUS] at a meeting this morning in the Charlie Center in Bratislava. Anna Samelova and Ivan Samel are on the line to report more in detail about the assembly: [Samel] [passage omitted] This morning the delegates acquainted themselves with reports about the assemblies of the Alliance and the Alternative, and with statements expressing their willingness to merge. They discussed and approved the statutes of the new party. Following a lengthy discussion in the afternoon, they also approved the DEUS's program. [Samelova] This afternoon, the assembly delegates elected senior DEUS officials. The result of the vote is as follows: Jozef Moravcik is the chairman, Milan Knazko is the first deputy chairman, Viliam Vaskovic is the deputy chairman for economy, and Roman Kovac is the deputy chairman for social affairs and culture.
