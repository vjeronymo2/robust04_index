BFN [Text] Minister Shim'on Shetrit will propose that the government suspend the negotiations with the PLO until Yasir 'Arafat condemns the terrorist attack in 'Afula. Shetrit expressed the belief that all the cabinet ministers will support his proposal. The minister said that not only has 'Arafat failed to condemn the terrorist attack of his own volition but also avoided replying to a question on the matter yesterday in Cairo. Shetrit explained that by his actions, 'Arafat is giving cause to doubt the sincerity of his intention to fulfill his commitments to Israel. Shetrit said that Prime Minister Rabin and Foreign Minister Peres will find the appropriate time and place to comment on 'Arafat's behavior.
