October 17, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

REUNIFICATION OF GERMANY

As a Vietnam-era veteran, I can appreciate the bad times Rummel and his family suffered due to Germany's initiation of World War II. But the experiences he shared in his commentary are words of a man whose country lost a war. I wonder what Rummel would have written on Oct. 3, 1990, if Germany had claimed victory in 1945! I say let Germany earn the trust of our democracy.

ART GOMEZ

San Gabriel
