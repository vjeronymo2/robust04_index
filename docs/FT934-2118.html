931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Ewart recovery continues Ewart, the Belfast-based property company, continued its recovery programme during the half year to October 31 and revealed pre-tax profits of Pounds 351,000. Last time there were losses of Pounds 260,000 but a second-half pick up left the year's figure at Pounds 156,000. The group is also returning to the dividend list with a 0.4p interim. Turnover for the six months advanced from Pounds 1.94m to Pounds 3.32m.
