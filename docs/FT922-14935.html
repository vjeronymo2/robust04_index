920403 FT 03 APR 92 / Bank chief quits in Venezuela THE president of Venezuela's central bank, Mr Miguel Rodriguez, has resigned in an effort to ease political pressure on the beleagured government of President Carlos Andres Perez, reports Joe Mann in Caracas. The president named, late on Wednesday, Ms Ruth de Krivoy, a respected economist and the first woman to hold the job, to head the central bank. Mr Rodriguez, who only took the post in late February, was the main architect of the highly unpopular economic reform programme the Perez government initiated three years ago.
