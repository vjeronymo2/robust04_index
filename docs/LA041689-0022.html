April 16, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TAXES

The great thing about democracy is that we pretty much deserve what we get. Ronald Reagan and his string-pullers didn't make any secret about the fact that they believed in lower taxes for the rich. Now is not the time for those of us in the middle who have to make up the shortfall to scream and rend our clothing. After all, we are the very ones who put him there -- not once but twice -- and then elected his veep for yet another four years!

Have no pity, George -- sock it to us.

FRANK LAROCHE

Ladera Heights
