BFN [Report by Sergey Voziyanov; from the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Talks between Russian and Ukrainian military delegations on a stage-by-stage settlement of Black Sea Fleet problems begin in Sevastopol today. Pavel Grachev, the head of the Russian delegation and Russian defense minister, said the Russian military delegation would not grant any favors or concessions going beyond the framework of the agreement and also the Massandra protocol and other documents concerning the fleet. Pavel Grachev stressed the Russian delegation would approach the destiny of the Black Sea Fleet in a constructive and equitable manner. According to INTERFAX, the defense minister's view is that Russia has enough strength and resources to purchase the whole of the Black Sea Fleet altogether.
