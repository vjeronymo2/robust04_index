940108 FT 08 JAN 94 / UK Company News: FKI Subsidiary FKI subsidiary, Bristol Babcock, has received an order exceeding Dollars 5m (Pounds 3.3m) from El Paso Natural Gas, one of the largest gas pipeline companies in the US. The order is for the supply and installation of a radio-based SCADA system to automate the measurement and control of 1,300 gas wells primarily in the Farmington, New Mexico, region.
