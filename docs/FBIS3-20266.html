Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Geneva, March 30 (AFP) -- GATT negotiators were unable to agree on a draft final ministerial statement on the Uruguay Round on Wednesday [30 March] owing to disagreement between the United States and developing countries on inclusion of a clause on social conditions for workers, GATT officials said. The negotiators, who have approved all the non-controversial documents which are to be presented to the GATT ministerial conference in Marrakesh from April 12 to 15, also set aside a document approving the creation of a committee to prepare for the setting up of the World Trade Organisation which is to replace the GATT in 1995.
