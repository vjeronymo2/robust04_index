BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondents Sergey Zhirnikhin and Ivan Ivanov] [Text] Budapest April 1 TASS -- The official visit of Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin to Hungary has resulted in signing six bilateral economic agreements. The documents signed in Budapest on Friday include an agreement on the settlement of the problem of mutual undebtedness, a convention on evading double taxation, protocol on exchange of goods supplies in 1994, proceedings of a session of the joint commission on trade and economic cooperation, and a memorandum on cooperation in the field of postal services and electric communication. The two sides also signed a protocol which supplements the 1996 protocol on joing efforts in building a nuclear power plant in Hungary.
