One respondent urged that state and local governments and U.S. universities and non-profit research institutes working independently should be eligible to receive individual ATP awards outside of joint ventures. No change has been made to the regulation based upon this comment because eligibility for individual awards is expressly limited to United States companies by section 278n(b)(2) of title 15 of the United States Code. Effective Date of the Final Rule This final rule relating to grants, benefits, and contracts is exempt from the delayed effective date requirement, and accordingly, under section 553(a)(2) of the Administrative Procedure Act (5 U.S.C. 553), is therefore being made effective immediately without a 30 day delay in effective date.
