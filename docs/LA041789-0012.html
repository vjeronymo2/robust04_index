April 17, 1989, Monday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST

Police have arrested two suspects in the fatal shooting Friday night of a teen-ager at Martin Luther King Park in Southeast San Diego.

Police Sgt. Nate Caplan said Sunday that Robert Lazaga, 19, of the 6500 block of Broadrick Place and an unidentified juvenile were booked on suspicion of murder.

The victim, John Castro, who was shot in the head and shoulder, died after being taken to Mercy Hospital by a Life Flight helicopter.
