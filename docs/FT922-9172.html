920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Company News in Brief BRITANNIA GROUP has acquired Construction Exports (NI), a Northern Ireland-based aggregates marketing company, for Pounds 80,000 cash and 60,000 shares. Conexpo incurred a loss of Pounds 4,700 on turnover of Pounds 362,000 in 1991. Assets at December 31 were Pounds 45,000. HEADLAM has disposed of its Faire Brothers subsidiary for Pounds 510,000. The consideration is on a staged basis comprising Pounds 255,000 cash on completion and the issue to Headlam of 255,000 12 per cent cumulative redeemable preference shares of Pounds 1 each redeemable as to Pounds 150,000 on April 30 1993 and Pounds 105,000 on January 28 1994. ISA INTERNATIONAL has acquired the business assets and certain liabilities of Oslo-based Datarekvisita for about Pounds 1.32m cash. Datarekvisita, a distributor of computer consumable supplies, made pre-tax profits of some Pounds 174,600 in 1991 and had net assets at the year-end of approximately Pounds 260,000. JLI GROUP: Company's recent rights issue accepted in respect of some 13.3m new ordinary shares, representing approximately 96.1 per cent of the offer. MACFARLANE GROUP (Clansman) has acquired Star Products Packaging for Pounds 510,000, payable in cash and shares. Star is based in Sudbury, Suffolk. NEW LONDON, through its International Drilling Fluids subsidiary, has acquired the oil and gas services division of Aberlan Environmental Services for about Pounds 1.65m. Of the total consideration Pounds 750,000 was paid in cash, plus the allocation of 1.56m ordinary shares. Deferred payment of about Pounds 650,000 is due next year.
