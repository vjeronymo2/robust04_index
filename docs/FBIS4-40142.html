BFN [Excerpts] The leaders of the People's Front, Charter-91, and the Republican Party signed an agreement today uniting the three parties into a single political organization. The new organization will be called the United Republican Party of Georgia. [passage omitted] The text of the agreement was presented to journalists and participants at the ceremony by chairman of the People's Front, Nodar Natadze. The United Republican Party's manifesto was presented by the leader of Charter-91, Tedo Paatashvili. The leader of the Republican Party, Vakhtang Gabiradze, read out the list of members of the new party's provisional ruling body, the Political Council. [passage omitted] The United Republican Party will work as a single faction in the Georgian Parliament. [passage omitted]
