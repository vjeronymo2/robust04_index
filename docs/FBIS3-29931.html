Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 23 (KCNA) -- The Filipino Foreign Minister, Roberto Romulo, stated on February 21 that his country would not support the United Nations' "economic sanctions" against North Korea, KYODO reported. Earlier, on February 15, he said a coercive measure against North Korea would adversely affect the stability of the Asia-Pacific Region. In a memorandum presented to the president, he said the Philippines should check the moves to internationally isolate North Korea
