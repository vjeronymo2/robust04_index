930726 FT 26 JUL 93 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 50m work awarded to Wiltshier The WILTSHIER CONSTRUCTION GROUP has made new contract gains totalling almost Pounds 50m since April. The largest project is the refurbishment of an office block for Unisys at Uxbridge in Middlesex, valued at Pounds 4m and due for completion in the autumn. Other major contracts include a Pounds 3m steel-framed warehouse with car parking for Nurdin & Peacock in Croydon, a Pounds 2.1m leisure centre in Canterbury, a Pounds 2.8m student accommodation unit in Newcastle-upon-Tyne and a series of nine individual store fit-out contracts for Boots the Chemist totalling Pounds 2.2m.
