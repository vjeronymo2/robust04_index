Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [LReport by Aleksandr Sokolov: "A National Revival Movement Has Been Created. It Will Assist Implementation of the `10 Years of Prosperity' Program"] [Text] A few days ago the founding congress of the Galkynysh National Revival Movement took place in Ashgabat. The movement includes all public and political organizations in the republic as well as religious groups. The congress formed the movement's council, headed by President Saparmurat Niyazov. Addressing the congress, Mr. Niyazov stated that "the movement as a public and political organization will thoroughly assist the spiritual and cultural revitalization of the Turkmen people and raise the citizens' well-being." The movement is also to contribute to the implementation of the main government program, "10 Years of Prosperity." According to the president, the immediate economic tasks include mass privatization of public utility and retail trade organizations. S. Niyazov promised that in the first half of this year all money losing and small enterprises will be privatized.
