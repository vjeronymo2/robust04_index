May 6, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

'HOMO MASSMARKETUS'

I couldn't agree more with Mark C. Taylor's Endpapers article, "Love of Learning and the Celebration of the Learned" (Book Review, April 15).

The norm in our society is to hail and exalt our athletes and movie stars while totally ignoring or berating our scholars and intellectuals. Whoever makes the most bucks receives the greatest adulation in our sick society.

I suggest that we make National Library Week a national holiday so everyone will go to the library at least once a year.

BENNY WASSERMAN

LA PALMA
