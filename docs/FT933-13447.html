930719 FT 19 JUL 93 / International Company News: Evans & Price Industrial Metal Products Corporation, a Michigan-based machinery group, has taken an 80 per cent stake in Evans & Price, the Tamworth-based specialist machine tool builder, for an undisclosed amount. Evans & Price has marketed micro-finishing machinery built under license from Impco for the past 10 years, and sees the new relationship as a way to accelerate penetration of the European market.
