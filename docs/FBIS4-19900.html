BFN [Text] National Defense Minister Mehmet Golhan has returned home after concluding his visit to the Russian Federation. In a statement at Ankara's Esenboga Airport, Golhan said that the defense industry cooperation agreement signed in Moscow envisages an initial purchase of 42 armored vehicles and urgently required ammunition. Golhan noted that experts are working on the purchase of two helicopters, which will serve a mobile hospital Stating that Turkey attaches great importance to good-neighborly relations and cooperation, Golhan stated that as the free market economy in Russia develops, its relations with Turkey will be strengthened in all areas. Pointing out that the clashes in the Caucasus were emphasized during meetings with Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev, Golhan added that finding solutions to regional issues under the umbrella of the United Nations was also assessed. Stating that during his talks, the idea of converting the Black Sea into a sea of friendship was enhanced, Golhan expressed the hope that this spirit of friendship and cooperation will spread to other neighbors and enable all regional countries to live in peace and tranquillity.
