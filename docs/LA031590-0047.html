March 15, 1990, Thursday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: COUNTYWIDE;

6 FILE TO RUN FOR 4TH DISTRICT SUPERVISOR

Six candidates have filed to run for a Ventura County Board of Supervisors seat being vacated by Supervisor James R. Dougherty, who will not seek re-election when his term expires in November.

Filing for the 4th District post by Wednesday's deadline were Robert Pegg, 65, foreman of the Ventura County Grand Jury; Harold Lawrence Fick, 52, a General Motors employee; former county supervisor Glen Schmidt, 60; Simi Valley City Council members Vicky Howard, 56, and Bill Davis, 62, and Ventura County Community College District Trustee Tom Ely, 54.

The district includes Simi Valley, Mo- orpark, Somis and Santa Rosa Valley.
