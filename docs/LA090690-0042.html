September 6, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

O.C. NEWSMAKERS

* Judy Johnson has been named vice president of Thomas Wilck Associates, an Irvine public relations firm. Before joining the company, she was executive assistant to then-Orange County Supervisor Phil Anthony. Johnson was also a staff aide to former President Richard M. Nixon's press secretary and was an aide to Henry A. Kissinger when he was national security adviser.
