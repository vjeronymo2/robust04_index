930512 FT 12 MAY 93 / BR chief backs London plan THE Pounds 2bn London CrossRail east-west link must be built if Britain is to exploit the advantages of railways in meeting the transport needs of the 21st century, BR chairman Sir Bob Reid said last night. Sir Bob also called for other projects such as the high-speed link to the Channel tunnel and Jubilee Line extension to Docklands to get the go-ahead. 'The forces of inertia can be damagingly powerful and if you delay long enough there will always be some change in circumstances that can be used to warrant more paper studies,' he warned.
