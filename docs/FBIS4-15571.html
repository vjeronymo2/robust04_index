BFN [Text] Yerevan-Stepanakert, April 9 (SNARK) -- The delegations of Armenia and Nagorno-Karabakh will participate in the non-official consultations of the CSCE Minsk group. The consultations will devote to the regulation of the conflict. The consultations start in Prague April 11. Arkadi Gukasyan, the Nagorno-Karabakh Minister for Foreign Affairs, and Masis Mailyan, Head of the International Organizations Department of the Nagorno-Karabakh Ministry of Foreign Affairs, left for Prague to participate in the consultations. Press-Center of the Armenian Foreign Affairs Department told SNARK the Armenian delegation at the Prague consultations will be headed by the deputy Foreign Affairs Minister Gerard Libaridyan.
