(24) Certification regarding Lobbying. Section 319 of the Department of the Interior Appropriations Act, Public Law 101&hyph;121, approved October 23, 1989 (31 U.S.C. 1352) (the ``Byrd Amendment'') generally prohibits recipients of Federal contracts, grants, and loans from using appropriated funds for lobbying the Executive or Legislative Branches of the Federal Government in connection with a specific contract, grant or loan. The Department's regulations on these restrictions on lobbying are codified at 24 CFR part 87. To comply with 24 CFR 87.110, any PHA submitting an application under this announcement for more than $100,000 of budget authority must submit a certification and, if applicable, a Disclosure of Lobbying Activities (SF-LLL form).
