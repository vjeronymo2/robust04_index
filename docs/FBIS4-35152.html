BFN [Text] Former Peruvian President Fernando Belaunde Terry has confirmed that he will not run for president in 1995. He said he would be an active participant in the presidential campaign, but not a presidential candidate. The Popular Action, AP, leader said that he does not believe in polls, and added that his party plans to participate in the presidential elections with its own presidential candidate. Belaunde Terry said that AP does not need to improve any policy, but rather project a path into the future.
