930922 FT 22 SEP 93 / Collapse of two holiday companies TWO London-based holiday tour operators, Number 1 Airtours and Air Style Aviation have collapsed, the Civil Aviation Authority said last night. The companies are covered by the CAA's bond and the holidays of about 4,500 people now abroad will not be affected. People who have holidays booked will be able to claim refunds. Number 1 Airtours is not connected with Airtours, the second-biggest tour operator.
