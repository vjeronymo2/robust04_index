BFN [Anatoliy Ladin report in the "From Alma-Ata" column: "The New Commander Is Meeting With Ministers"] [Text] Col.-Gen. Valeriy Patrikeyev, commander of the Collective Peacekeeping Forces in Tajikistan, has visited Tashkent, Alma-Ata [Almaty], and Bishkek. In a conversation with KRASNAYA ZVEZDA's correspondent Col.-Gen. Patrikeyev, who has been commander of the Collective Peacekeeping Forces for several weeks (he replaced Col.-Gen. Boris Pyankov in this post), noted that during his trip he has met with defense ministers of the states whose units, jointly with Russia's military formations, are performing a peacekeeping mission in Tajikistan. Main attention was given to questions of joint financial support for the Collective Peacekeeping Forces.
