BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 26 -- State President Le Duc Anh and his wife left here today for an official friendship visit to Indonesia at the invitation of President Suharto. The Vietnamese president is accompanied by Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam, Presidentual Office Director Nguyen Viet Dung, Trade Minister Le Van Triet, Agriculture and Food Industry Minister Nguyen Cong Tan, Tourism General Department Director Do Quang Trung and Vietnamese Ambassador to Indonesia Nguyen Trung Hieu. During his stay, President Le Duc Anh and his party are expected to have talks with Indonesian President Suharto, sign agreements for cooperation on education, culture, and tourism between the two countries and visit Bandung.
