940216 FT 16 FEB 94 / Hunt denies plans to leave ILO Mr David Hunt, employment secretary, denied yesterday that the government intended to pull Britain out of the International Labour Organisation in protest at its criticisms of alleged breaches of labour standards. But observers believe some action against the ILO cannot be ruled out if the Geneva organisation condemns Britain in June for its outlawing of trade unionism at the government communications headquarters in Cheltenham 10 years ago.
