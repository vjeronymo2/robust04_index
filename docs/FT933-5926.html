930901 FT 01 SEP 93 / Oil pipe coating plant to close BRITISH Pipe Coaters is shutting its 77-acre site at Invergordon, Ross and Cromarty. The plant put corrosion-proof coatings on pipelines for the offshore oil and gas industry. The company is 51 per cent owned by British Gas and 49 per cent by American oilfield contractor Dresser Industries. The Invergordon plant, which once had a 700-strong labour force, employed 150 people last year but only eight remain.
