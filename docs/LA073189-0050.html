July 31, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Julian Jackson knocked out Terry Norris in the second round to retain his World Boxing Assn. junior-middleweight title at Atlantic City, N.J.

Jackson (38-1) stopped Norris (21-3) with a hard right to the chin midway through the round and finished him with a second right before referee Joe Cortez signaled that the bout was over.
