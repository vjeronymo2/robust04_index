BFN [Text] Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri has stated that the Lebanese Government insists on the need to hand over the two Iraqi diplomats accused of assassinating Shaykh Talib al-Suhayl to the judicial authorities to complete their investigation. The Council of Ministers said there is no going back on the ban on the General Labor Federation staging a demonstration on 26 April, because this measure serves the interests of the Lebanese people and Lebanon.
