Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Direct involvement of Pakistani nationals and Afghan mojahedin into Kashmir militancy and taking over command of terrorist leadership to step up subversive activities in Kashmir Valley have been established with the arrest of several foreign mercenaries and nationals of Pakistan. This came to light recently when an advertisement in a local Urdu daily of Srinagar gave the list of foreign mercenaries belonging to its outfit killed in Kashmir in encounters with the security forces. According to the newspaper, of the list of 35 such mercenaries, at least 27 were Pakistanis, three from Afghanistan, two from Saudi Arabia, and one each from Bahrain and Algeria. The Border Security Force [BSF] today found 10 bags of foreign currency worth crores of rupees from the Regal area in Samba Sector of Jammu region. A BSF spokesman told PTI the currency notes of countries like Pakistan, United Kingdom, United States, Iran, Saudi Arabia, and India were sealed in bags and left at different places in the area. The spokesman said this is one of the major successes of the BSF in its drive against international smugglers operating between India and Pakistan via Jammu border.
