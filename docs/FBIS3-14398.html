Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Asyut, 27 Mar (MENA) -- Three terrorists were killed in al-Ghana'im Town in Asyut Governorate tonight and two others were injured during an exchange of fire with security forces. The incident happened when a security force ambush asked a Peugeot taxi to stop. When it did, the taxi's passengers suddenly opened fire with automatic weapons on the police. Captain 'Umar Mustafa al-Jundi, 1st Lieutenant Basyuni Ahmad 'Abd-al-Nabi, 1st Lt. Hisham Ahmad al-Sayyid, and Sergeant Ra'fat 'Ali Muhammad were slightly wounded. The other members of the police force succeeded in chasing the taxi and fired at its tires to stop it. Three terrorists were killed and two others were injured as a result. The injured policemen and terrorists were taken to hospitals for treatment. The Asyut security commands and the criminal laboratory experts went to the site of the incident to investigate. The identities of the terrorists are being checked. The Prosecution Office has been notified to start its investigations.
