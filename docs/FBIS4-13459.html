BFN [Text] Kuwait, 21 Apr (KUNA) -- The Kuwaiti Foreign Ministry categorically denied here today the truth of the report broadcast by Radio Israel that Kuwait has agreed to host the next round of the water resources committee talks within the multilateral negotiations. Commenting on this claim, a Foreign Ministry spokesman stressed to KUNA that Kuwait has not agreed to host this committee or any other in Kuwait. Yosi Sarid, Israeli minister of environmental affairs, claimed yesterday in an interview with Radio Israel that Kuwait had agreed to host the second round of the water resources committee talks which began in the Sultanate of Oman last Sunday and lasted for three days. It is to be noted that the State of Kuwait and 43 other states are participating in the multilateral committees which are convening in various world capitals.
