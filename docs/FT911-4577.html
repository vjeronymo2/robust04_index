910418 FT 18 APR 91 / Parliament and Politics: National sovereignty FURTHER surrenders of national sovereignity by the 12 member states of the European Community were envisaged by Baron Hermann von Richthofen, the German Ambassador to London, yesterday. Responding to questions at a Parliamentary press gallery luncheon he argued that the pooling of sovereignity which had already occured through the creation of Nato and the United Nations was likely to continue as the EC progressed towards economic and monetary union. Baron von Richthofen saw a further pooling of sovereignity as the inevitable consequence of the creation of the single European single market.
