Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Czech president, Dr. Vaclav Havel, left for Varanasi this morning after a three-day stay in New Delhi. He was given a ceremonial send-off at the Rashtrapati Bhavan [President House]. Speaking on the occasion, President Dr. Shankar Dayal Sharma said that Dr. Havel's visit has provided India with an opportunity to come in contact with the great personality like him. He said such contacts will result in establishing better relations between the two countries. Talking to newspersons, Dr. Havel described his visit to India as successful.
