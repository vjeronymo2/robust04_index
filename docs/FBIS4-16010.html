BFN [Text] Azerbaijan air force planes again bombed residential areas in Stepanakert, capital of Nagorno-Karabakh, at 0630 today. According to preliminary figures, an INTERFAX correspondent reports, five people were killed and more than ten wounded. The dead and injured consist mainly of women and children. Several buildings were destroyed. In connection with this the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic's government accused Azerbaijan of continuing the war against the civilian population of Karabakh. According to a statement issued today, Azerbaijani planes have twice in the last three days bombed Stepanakert, killing women and children who are tens of kilometers away from the area of military actions. The ruling circles of Azerbaijan have again demonstrated to the whole world the true worth of their false peace-loving assurances, the statement stresses.
