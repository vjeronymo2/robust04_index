BFN [Excerpt] The Islamic Arab Rally today announced the assassination of one of its founders, lawyer Laid Grine, who was kidnapped from his home near Rouiba and was horribly and savagely murdered. Following this painful incident, the Islamic Arab Rally and all its party activists present their most sincere condolences to his wife, children, siblings, and relatives and express to them their sympathy. Mr. Laid Grine started in the profession of lawyer on 28 June 1981. According to information obtained from the Union of Lawyers, the victim was kidnapped by armed individuals from his home on Friday evening at 2300 hours. [passage omitted]
