BFN [From the "RPC Television News" newscast] [Text] This morning a ceremony installing the government transition team was held. The team is formed by representatives of President Guillermo Endara and President-elect Ernesto Perez Balladares. The so-called transition team is composed of the following people: Representing President Endara: Eduardo Linares, deputy minister of planning and economy policy; and Joaquin Franco, vice president of the Authentic Liberal Party. The president-elect's representatives are Francisco Sanchez Cardenas and Jose Luis Sosa. At the installation ceremony, Ernesto Perez Balladares praised the demonstrated goodwill as well as the work done in recent days by President Endara and his government to make an exemplary and democratic transition possible. [Begin Perez Balladares recording] The next few months will be months of coordination and transition and will attest to our respect and determination that Guillermo Endara continue as president of the country until 1 September 1994, until an hour yet to be established. We hope that in the spirit of cooperation we may do whatever is feasible and possible to see that the transition is carried out in the least disruptive way possible, with the utmost respect for all the people of the Endara administration and all public officials involved in this transition, who I am sure understand that their jobs are not at stake. [end recording] Presdent Endara expressed his satisfaction with the normalcy that prevailed during the 1994 elections. [Begin Endara recording] It is a fact that the outgoing president wants the president-elect and his team to be prepared to assume the government on 1 September by knowing whatever they want to know. We will gladly oblige. [end recording] From now on, the government's transition commission, which began work immediately, will have to prepare full reports for the members of the new government that will be sworn in on 1 September.
