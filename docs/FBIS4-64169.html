BFN [ITAR-TASS report: "Turkmenistan Is Establishing Good Relations With Iran"] [Text] Turkmenistan and Iran intend to strengthen bilateral relations in all spheres. An accord to this effect was secured during a meeting in Mashhad Thursday [26 May] between Iranian President 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani and Turkmen President Saparmurad Niyazov, who is here on a visit. During the talks particular attention was paid to stepping up work on the project to lay a pipeline to export Turkmen gas via Iran and Turkey to Europe and to completing the work of building a railroad between the two countries.
