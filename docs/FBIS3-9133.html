Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] In a brief interview for the "Around the World and at Home" program on Bulgarian National Television, which will be transmitted in an hour's time, President Zhelyu Zhelev said that he has visited Prime Minister Berov and that the prime minister's health continues to improve. This was announced in a release from the press center of the Presidency. According to the release, the president said that early elections should be held, probably in the fall. In the interview the president supported the demand made by the chief editors of 15 central newspapers that press publications should not be subject to value-added tax. He stressed the responsibility of the media for checking the authenticity of the information they disseminated. The president specifically mentioned certain insinuations [passage indistinct].
