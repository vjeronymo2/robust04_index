920415 FT 15 APR 92 / UK Company News: Tight control helps Shani lift margins London Page 23 Tight control of overheads and production costs enabled Shani Group to produce a pre-tax profit of Pounds 770,000 for the half year to January 31 1992, compared with Pounds 764,000. This USM-traded maker of women's and children's clothes saw its margins increase as turnover fell from Pounds 5.92m to Pounds 5.09m. Liquidity during the period remained healthy. Earnings per share improved to 3.6p (3.5p) and the interim dividend is again 1.6p.
