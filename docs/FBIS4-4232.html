BFN [Text] Beijing, April 27 (CNS) -- According to recent statistics, by the end of March this year, China had approved 186,062 foreign-funded items with the contracted foreign capital being U.S.$241.5 billion and actual utilization of U.S.$67.2 billion. In the first quarter of this year, a good developing trend was seen in China's absorption of foreign capital with a new increase in foreign-funded items of 11,800 and actual utilization of foreign capital of U.S.$5.4 billion. With the development of ten or more years, the three type foreign-funded enterprises in China constitute an important part of China's economy. The important roles they play in China's economy include the following: They have speeded up the development of China's economy with foreign capital being an important source of money collection for construction and an important factor promoting the economic development in the country. They have promoted industrial progress and development with the main part of foreign capital introduced being used for the export of advanced technology and equipment to fill some technological blanks and to bring many Chinese products closer to world standard. Foreign-funded enterprises have become an important force in China's foreign trade with their total import and export value making up 34 percent of the country's total. The establishment of foreign-funded enterprises have brought into China the market-based idea, new forms and ways of operation of enterprises, competitive mechanism and a scientific management system. They have also trained a large number of talented people in operations and management, setting examples for the transfer of the operational mechanism of state-owned enterprises and the establishment of modern enterprises.
