BFN [Text] [No dateline as received] -- CIS forces will be deployed in the zone of the Abkhaz-Georgian conflict 10-15 June 1994. The military contingent will number from 2,500 to 3,000 troops to be stationed along the Enguri River, which serves as a line separating the two sides. Military experts from the CIS countries have been arriving in Abkhazia to prepare for the deployment of peacekeeping forces.
