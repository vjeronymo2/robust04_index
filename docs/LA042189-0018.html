April 21, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: CAMARILLO MOTORCYCLIST FOUND DEAD IN HILLS

A Camarillo man who failed to return home from a motorcycle outing was found dead Thursday in the hills north of Moorpark, where he apparently had an accident while practicing jumps, authorities said.

Kirk Puener, 22, was found dead about 6 a.m. in an off-road area north of Happy Camp Road, said Ventura County Sheriff's Deputy Jody Keller. Authorities began a search of off-road motorcycle riding areas after the man's mother reported early Thursday that Puener had failed to return home from the Wednesday outing, Keller said.
