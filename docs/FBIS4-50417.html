BFN [Feature by XINHUA reporters Xu Furui (6079 4395 3843) and Wu Ping (0702 1627): "Chairman Li Ruihuan Visits Danish Farmhouse as a Guest"] [Excerpts] Copenhagen, 15 May (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference National Committee who is visiting Denmark, stayed as a guest at a Danish Farmhouse some 50 km west of Copenhagen. The host Moden and his wife Tova [names as received] warmly greeted Chinese visitors at the door. Moden's parents made a special trip to their son's house to meet the distinguished guest from China. Moden's house is a typical Danish farmhouse, a white-fence, red- roof two-story house surrounded by a vast lawn and garden, with a piggery and machine room at the side. [passage omitted] Led by Moden, Chairman Li and his entourage first visited the piggery. [passage omitted] After Chairman Li completed the tour of the farmhouse, the host brought out refreshments and shared them with the guest on the lawn. Li Ruihuan presented an embroidered tablecloth set to the Danish couple and toy pandas to their two children. The hostess shook hands with Chairman Li and thanked him for the gifts, while the two kids were totally immersed with their new toys. Before leaving the house, Li Ruihuan had a picture taken with the Danish family. The whole farmhouse was filled with profound sentiments of friendship between the Chinese and Danish peoples.
