BFN [Text] A meeting took place in the Council of Ministers of the Republic, under the chairmanship of Abdulmadjid Samadov today. The issue relating to revising the concept of development of agriculture of the Republic was discussed in the meeting. The heads of the relevant ministries and departments and prominent scientists of the Tajik Academy of Sciences attended the meeting. It was pointed out in the meeting that the present concept of development of agriculture should be revised in the conditions of the present-day economic crisis of Tajikistan. The absence of foreign loans makes the farmers rely only on the use of home resources. [words indistinct] With the purpose of revising the concept of development of agriculture of the Republic, a working group has been established out of the scientists of the country's Academy of Sciences and prominent agricultural experts.
