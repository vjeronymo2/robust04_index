940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / UK Company News: CapCo in Pounds 162m acquisition Capital & Counties, the property company owned by TransAtlantic Holdings, has paid Sun Alliance Pounds 162m for part of the Harlequin shopping centre in Watford. The purchase, which is due to be completed on February 28, will mark the end of a legal dispute between the two companies. The dispute centred on different interpretations of the agreement governing how the centre's rental income is split between the two companies.
