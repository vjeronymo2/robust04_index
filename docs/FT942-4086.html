940611 FT 11 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Somic improves to Pounds 254,000 Somic, the maker of yarns and woven fabrics, continued to progress through the second half, and for the full year to March 31 achieved a rise in profits from Pounds 81,249 to Pounds 254,077 pre-tax. Higher turnover of Pounds 4.06m (Pounds 3.59m) reflected increased activity by regular customers together with new business. Earnings rose to 9.57p (3.86p) and a final dividend of 1.5p makes a 2.5p (1.5p) total.
