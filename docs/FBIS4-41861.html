BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Gondusov] [Text] Moscow June 28 TASS -- The Russian Armed Forces are efficient, controllable, ready to protect the state from any encroachments and ensure national security, including in peace-making, Russian Defence Minister General of the Army Pavel Grachev told graduates of military academies here today. Today's Russia has no enemies, said the defence minister. "This is confirmed by major provisions of the Russian military doctrine and known by the whole world. However, Russia same as any other state has its interests of the military-political and geo-strategic position. We have to remember about this and proceed from national interests in military building," said Pavel Grachev. The minister recalled names of renowned Russian military leaders and said they valued the soldier's confidence most. "Be more attentive to your subordinates. Remember that an army where an soldier believes in his officer has such an advantage that cannot be gained with strength, materiel and any other thing," said Grachev. The armed forces are interested in social stability and civic peace. "Unity and accord are necessary for prosperity of the state and successful development of the military sphere," said the minister.
