940617 FT 17 JUN 94 / World Trade News: Boeings destined for Maersk Air Boeing, the US commercial aircraft manufacturer, has received an order for six Boeing 737-700 aircraft from Maersk Air of Denmark, it announced yesterday, AP-DJ reports from Seattle. Boeing said six more 737-700s are included in the agreement but are subject to reconfirmation by Maersk. Boeing said Maersk Air has also ordered two additional 737-300s. Boeing will deliver both 737-300s in October 1995, with deliveries of the 737-700s scheduled to begin in the latter part of 1997.
