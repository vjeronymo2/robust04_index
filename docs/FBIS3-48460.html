Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 24 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent thanks to the war veterans in Nangnang district of Pyongyang, teachers and students of the Pyongyang financial cadres school, employees of the Haeju City Commercial Administration Office in South Hwanghae Province, Chong Son-yo, secretary of the Primary Party Committee of the Potonggang district people's hospital of Pyongyang, and Yim Sin-ok, worker of Hamhung Restaurant, who set examples in assisting the People's Army. The speakers at the meetings held to convey the thanks said that it is the sacred duty of citizens to love and assist the People's Army soldiers. But, we have the honor and happiness of receiving the thanks from the supreme commander, they stressed. They evinced their determination to give more assistance to the People's Army and bring the traditional traits of army-people unity into fuller play.
