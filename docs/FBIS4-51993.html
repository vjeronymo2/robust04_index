BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 5 (KCNA) -- A meeting was held and a photo exhibition opened in Pyongyang Wednesday to celebrate the 75th anniversary of the founding of the League of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies under the sponsorship of the Red Cross Society of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Yi Song-ho, acting chairman of the Central Committee of the Red Cross Society of the DPRK, made a report at the meeting. The reporter outlined the significance of the founding of the league and reviewed the achievements in its activities. He elaborated on major tasks facing the Red Cross Society of the DPRK at the present juncture and on the work the society must carry out in accordance with the strategy of Red Cross development in the 1990s and the 1994-1995 working plan of the league. Then, central and local Red Cross officials and voluntary servants took the floor. On display at the exhibition were pictures and materials showing the relief and community health and public service activities of the league and first-aid and other primary medical service and prevention of natural disasters, environmental protection and other activities of the Red Cross Society of the DPRK. The participants saw the exhibits. They planted memorial trees on the anniversary. The Red Cross Society of the DPRK issued postage stamps commemorating the anniversary.
