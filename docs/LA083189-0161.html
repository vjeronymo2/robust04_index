August 31, 1989, Thursday, Southland Edition

2 THAI BUSES COLLIDE WITH TRUCKS; 14 KILLED

Two buses racing to pick up passengers in southern Thailand slammed head-on into two trucks, killing at least 14 people, including a Buddhist monk, highway police said Wednesday.

The accident 75 miles south of Bangkok occurred Tuesday when one bus trying to pass the other collided head-on with a six-wheel truck.
