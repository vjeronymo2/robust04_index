November 13, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Nelson's Goods on the Road: Willie Nelson's real estate holdings, including his golf course and recording studio, were padlocked Monday after the Internal Revenue Service seized the country singer's properties in six states to satisfy $6.5 million in back taxes. The actions were taken less than two months after the IRS placed $16.7 million in liens on Nelson's real estate holdings and personal property. The order applies to Nelson's property in Texas, Alabama, California, Colorado, Hawaii and Washington. CLAUDIA PUIG
