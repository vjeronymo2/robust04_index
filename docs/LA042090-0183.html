April 20, 1990, Friday, P.M. Final

INJURED LA. JOCKEY REMAINS IN A COMA

Ronnie Daigrepont, a jockey injured in a spill at Jefferson Downs, remained in critical condition in a coma today at St. Jude Hospital.

Daigrepont, 21, of Kenner was headed into the homestretch Wednesday night aboard Tudor Gold when his horse and another apparently clipped hoofs.

Tudor Gold stumbled and threw Daigrepont, said Brian Krantz, general manager of the track.
