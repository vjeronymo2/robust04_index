BFN [Text] Chisinau, BASAPRESS, 5/5/1994 -- Today, President Mircea Snegur had a meeting with the President of the Belarusian Supreme Soviet Mechislav Grib and Premier Vyacheslav Kebich, who currently conduct an official visit to Moldova, states a Presidential Press Service communique. Both sides stressed at the meeting the importance of deepening and extending the bilateral relations on multiple levels between the two countries. Snegur mentioned that "relations between Moldova and Belarus can serve as a model of prosperous cooperation within the CIS economic zone." Referring to the political aspect of cooperation, Snegur underlined the necessity of mutual support in preventing military conflicts of various sorts. "After the 27 February elections and the opinion poll `La sfat cu poporul' [What People Advise] favourable conditions were created for the resolution of the problems in the eastern and Gagauz regions," mentioned Mircea Snegur. The president also noted that if reasonable compromises will be accepted, the situation could be improved in the nearest future. In their turn, the guests referred mainly to the presidential elections campaign in Belarus. They also thanked the Moldovan people for their support of Belarusians who suffered from the Chernobyl disaster, says the Presidential Press Service communique.
