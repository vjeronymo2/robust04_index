Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Madrid 24 Mar (JANA) -- The brother Secretary of the General People's Committee for External Liaison and International Cooperation ['Umar Mustafa al-Muntasir] met with Spanish Foreign Minister Javier [Madariaga] Solana, in Madrid yesterday. Bilateral relations, ways to develop and promote them, and issues of common interest were discussed and reviewed during the meeting. Foreign Minister Solana stressed his country's support for the positive initiatives taken by the great Jamahiriyah to find a peaceful settlement for the crisis between it and Western countries. The minister said that the persistence of this crisis directly affects security and stability in the region. The Spanish foreign minister said his country is interested in finding a solution to this crisis in accordance with international law.
