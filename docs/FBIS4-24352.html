BFN [Text] French Defense Minister Francois Leotard today visited a camp housing 8,000 Tutsi refugees in Nyarushishi, in the southwest of Rwanda. The camp has been under the protection of the French special forces for a week. Francois Leotard arrived in Goma, Zaire, this morning. He was accompanied by Lucette Michaux-Chevry, the minister delegate for humanitarian action and human rights, for a one-day visit to Operation Turquoise.
