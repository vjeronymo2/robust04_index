Language: English Article Type:BFN ["News Release"] [Text] Free trade agreement between the three Baltic countries will take effect on April 1, Priit Kolbre, the head of the Estonian Foreign Ministry's economic policy department, told ETA on Friday [11 February]. All three Baltic countries have already ratified the agreement. The original of the document will remain in Estonia and the country will also act as a coordinator of the agreement, Kolbre said. Baltic free trade agreement involves practically every industrial product with clauses providing for some restrictions based on individual interests of the countries.
