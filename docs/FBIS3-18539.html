Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Helsinki, March 3 (AFP) -- The Finnish Ministry of Justice has made a preliminary proposal for a referendum on September 18 on Finland's entry into the European Union (EU), the ministry announced Thursday [3 March]. The government parties have yet to take a final decision on the date. Several months are needed to spread information about the EU and to allow parliament sufficient time to deal with the membership agreement reached March 1, the ministry said.
