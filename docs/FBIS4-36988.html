BFN [Text] India will support China's reentry into the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade, GATT, as this will ensure enhanced growth in trade. The commerce minister, Mr. Pranab Mukherji, said this while addressing the India-China Joint Group meeting on economic relations, trade, and science and technology in New Delhi today. Mr. Mukherji said New Delhi shares the Chinese view that improvement in trade position should benefit the economy of other Asian countries.
