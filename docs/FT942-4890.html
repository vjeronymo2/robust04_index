940608 FT 08 JUN 94 / World News in Brief: Chief of French group Schneider freed in Brussels Didier Pineau-Valencienne, chairman of French industrial group Schneider, was released from prison in Brussels, where he had been held since May 27. Mr Pineau-Valencienne faces charges of fraud and swindling in a case which arose from complaints filed by minority shareholders in two subsidiaries of Schneider in Belgium. The company has denied all allegations against him.
