930807 FT 07 AUG 93 / Finance and the Family: S & P launches smaller Asian trust - At a glance SAVE & PROSPER is launching an Asian Smaller Companies unit trust to invest for capital growth in companies with market capitalisations of less than Dollars 500m. Initial investments will be mostly in Hong Kong, Japan, Malaysia, Australasia and Thailand. The fund will be managed by Jardine Fleming, S & P's sister company. The minimum investment is Pounds 1,000, or Pounds 35 a month in a regular savings scheme. Investment through a Pep is limited to Pounds 1,500. Lump sum investments made before August 27 will receive a 2 per cent discount on the fixed launch offer price per unit of 50p. The initial charge is 5.5 per cent and the annual charge is 1.5 per cent.
