May 12, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: ONE OF MOST-WANTED SUSPECTS ARRESTED

One of the city's 12 most-wanted bank robbers was arrested Thursday, one day after the Los Angeles Police Department released a list of the suspected felons.

Archie Michael Wolf, 35, also known as "Red Eye" because of a red spot between his left eye and nose, was arrested at 12:15 p.m. at his Sylmar apartment at 14040 Foothill Road, detective Joe Chandler said.

Wolf is believed to be responsible for 17 bank robberies in Los Angeles and 10 others in the county, Chandler said.

"We got lots of calls from people after we released the 12 most-wanted list," Chandler said. "One citizen who saw Wolf's picture on a TV news broadcast was able to tell us where he lived."
