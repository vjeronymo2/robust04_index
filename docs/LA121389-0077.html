December 13, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

DIFFERENT TRACKS: A monorail linking a proposed Irvine commercial-residential development and the terminal at John Wayne Airport was envisioned as a possible first step in a bigger system. . . . But airport officials now say the monorail is in limbo because its developer -- the McDonnell Douglas Realty Co. -- hasn't filed updated plans. "We're not going to put projects on hold while they make decisions," said an airport official. McDonnell Douglas replies that the monorail is a "full go." Dana Parsons
