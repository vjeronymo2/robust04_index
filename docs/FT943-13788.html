940718 FT 18 JUL 94 / Company News (This Week): Waste Management International - Other companies The environmental services group today reports second-quarter figures, which are expected to show continued growth despite the impact of the recession in continental Europe. Analysts are looking for pre-tax profits to rise from Pounds 37m in the same period last year to between Pounds 40m and Pounds 44m. There should be a good contribution from Wessex Waste Management, the joint venture with Wessex Water.
