930305 FT 05 MAR 93 / Hatton acted in a 'dishonest way' MR Derek Hatton and two other former Liverpool Labour councillors acted in a 'dishonest way' when they helped a businessman clinch two car-park deals, Mold Crown Court was told yesterday. Mr Alan Rawley QC, prosecuting, was starting his closing speech in the trial in which Mr Hatton and his personal tailor Mr John Monk deny two charges of conspiring to defraud Liverpool City Council. The two other former councillors, Mr John Nelson and Ms Hannah Folan, both deny one conspiracy charge.
