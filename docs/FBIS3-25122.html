Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Afonso Dhlakama, leader of the Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo], today begins two days of political contacts in South Africa, meeting Inkatha leader Mangosuthu Buthelezi in Ulundi. Afonso Dhlakama arrived in South Africa on a private visit on 4 February. A source with the Renamo delegation in South Africa told the Portuguese news agency, LUSA, that Dhlakama's visit is basically designed to gather financial and political support for Renamo's electoral campaign. The source declined to give further details of Dhlakama's visit to South Africa.
