BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Demidetskiy] [Text] Kishinev [Chisinau] May 26 TASS--Leaders of the Dniestr [Dniester] region regarded as slander the statements made by commander of the 14th Russian Army Aleksandr Lebed who accused Tiraspol leaders of illegal possession of weapons and asked the Russian leadership to deprive them of Russian citizenship which they were abusing. According to Vladimir Ivanov, aid to the president of the Dniestr Republic, Tiraspol leaders really have firearms in personal possession but he stressed that these weapons were handed to them in a legal way. He also confirmed that some Dniestr leaders were granted Russian citizenship but denied the statements of the command of the 14th Army accusing them of abusing this citizenship for illegal trade in weapons. A spokesman for the Interior Ministry of Moldavia [Moldova] told ITAR-TASS that the police also had information about misappropriation, illegal possession and re-sale of weapons by Tiraspol leaders and mafioso structures in the Dniestr Republic patronized by them.
