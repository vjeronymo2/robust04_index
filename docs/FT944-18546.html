941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / Emerging Markets: News round-up - Morocco Morocco is soon to sell off its stakes in five entreprises with a total turnover of 3.85bn dirhams (Dollars 438.4m), the Privatisation Ministry said. The state has between 35 and 99 per cent of the five groups - Banque Marocaine du Commerce Exterieur (BMCE), the Simef diesel and electric motor maker, the Sonasid steel mill, the Somas oil company and Sochepresse publication distributor. Since January, 12 state-owned enterprises have been sold off, officials said. Emerging markets coverage appears daily on the World Stock Markets page
