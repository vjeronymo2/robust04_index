BFN [Text] A communique by the government of republic of Kosova states that an international summit on Bosnia, proposed by Russian President Yeltsin and agreed upon by U.S. President Clinton, must include the Kosova question. The communique further states that peace can never be achieved in the Balkans as long as the Serbian repression against 2 million Albanians of Kosova is not stopped and the independence of the republic of Kosova is not ensured. The government of the republic of Kosova asks the United Nations, the United States, the European Union, and Russia to earnestly include the question of Kosova in the peace process.
