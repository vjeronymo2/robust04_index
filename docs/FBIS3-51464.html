Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The U.S. National Association of Broadcasters has demanded that TV Marti, the anti-Cuban television station, be closed. The association has described the station as inefficient and illegal. In statements to a special U.S. Congressional body, created to study the future of TV Marti and Radio Marti, (William Haratuntam), adviser to the National Association of Broadcasters, said that TV Marti violates the international agreements that limit the use of radio and television signals for domestic use only. The so-called TV Marti and Radio Marti have been on the air since May 1985 and March 1990, respectively.
