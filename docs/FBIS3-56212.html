Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Up to 23 deputies of the State Duma are to set up a group named the Union of December 12, the Economic Freedom Party's Secretary-General Irina Khakamada told Interfax. Khakamada was elected this group's co-chairperson on Thursday. The other co-chairpersons are member of the Moscow government Aleksandr Braginskiy and an entrepreneur from Nizhniy Novgorod Yevgeniy Bushmin. To quote Khakamada, the Union of December 12 consists of lawyers, entrepreneurs and economists. It will not join any of the political factions. She expressed hope that during the next three weeks the group will have 35 members which is sufficient for being registered.
