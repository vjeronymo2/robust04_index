BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Byrkin] [Text] Moscow June 17 TASS -- Russian Defence Minister General Pavel Grachev was not able to appear before the State Duma on Friday due to illness, the lower house of the parliament was informed during its "government hour". First deputy chief-of-staff Colonel-General Vitaliy Zhurbenko will give the Duma a report on the sending of Russian peacekeeping troops to Abkhazia. Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev has already begun his speech, which is devoted to the situation in North Korea, the Crimea, and the Russian peacekeeping mission in the Trans-Caucasus.
