December 22, 1990, Saturday, San Diego County Edition

HEAVEN WON'T TEAMS THAT HURT THEMSELVES

A longtime sports fan has been lost to the greed of sponsors, management and "athletes."

I worked my Norwegian butt off for 35 years and made about $600,000 in that time, working 11 months a year. . . . You now are paying jerks more money in six months of questionable labor.

The only way I can express my disgust with this stupidity is to not buy any product of the dumb corporations that help proliferate this garbage.

CHET LARSON

Solana Beach
