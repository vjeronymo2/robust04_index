July 15, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BREAST-FEEDING IN RESTAURANTS

While students in China are facing machine guns in the name of liberty and the Supreme Court in Washington is deciding questions of, literally, life, death and the American flag, our own defender of the Constitution, Gloria Allred, is prosecuting a civil rights case on behalf of a woman who did not want to be told by a restaurant that she would have to breast-feed her child discreetly or in the restroom.

After fighting this case for five years, and losing, Allred naturally held a press conference to announce another appeal. Perhaps if the press stopped covering her nonsense she would go away.

I. NELSON ROSE

Los Angeles
