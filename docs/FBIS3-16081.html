Language: English Article Type:BFN [By KAZTAG correspondent ivan Zakharchenko] [Text] Almaty March 11 TASS -- President Geydar Aliyev of Azerbaijan met President Nursultan Nazarbayev of Kazakhstan for one-to-one talks during a brief stopover in Almaty on his way back home from China last night. The presidents told journalists after the meeting that they had discussed prospects for the development of bilateral relations between the two countries. "The peoples of Kazakhstan and Azerbaijan have been bound by ties of friendship and fraternity for a long time, and the question now is how they should better restore former economic, political and other links as two independent countries, and create conditions for mutual beneficial cooperation," the Kazakh leader said. Geydar Aliyev invited the Kazakh president to pay a visit to Azerbaijan.
