November 3, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

PLAYERS NOT GUILTY, BUT USC BAND IS

I would like to respond to the letter of Oct. 27 from Mr. Butler concerning the USC band.

Come on. "Tribute to Troy"? "School songs"? "A band the university can be proud of"? Don't you mean "Tribute to repetition," "School song" and "a band the university should do something about"?

Let's face it. "The Song" is no longer inspiring. The results of the football team will attest to this. How about a "Tribute to Mercy"?

STEVE ACOSTA

Monterey Park
