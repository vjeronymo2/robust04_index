BFN [Text] Jilin has achieved good results in the anticorruption struggle for the present stage. The provincial procuratorate today held a news briefing to make public the previous work to investigate and handle major and serious cases. According to statistics, Jilin placed 2,100 economic offenses and law breaches on file for investigation from August 1993 to March 1994. Of the total, 537 were cases on embezzlement, 193 were cases on bribery, and 412 were cases on misuse of public funds. Cases involving 50,000 to 500,000 yuan totaled 229, those involving 500,000 to 1 million yuan totaled 12, and those involving more than 1 million yuan totaled nine. Cases involving cadres at and above the county or section level totaled 51. After investigation and handling of these cases, more than 43 million yuan of economic losses were recovered, and a number of major and serious cases that had created bad influence and repercussions were rapidly cracked. When handling the cases, procuratorial organs throughout the province handled a number of cases committed by functionaries of party and government organs, especially leading cadres. One hundred and forty-five functionaries of party and government organs were implicated in the cases cracked by the procuratorial organs across the province from August 1993 to March 1994. (Li Cai), former deputy director of the Meihekou city public security bureau, (Chi Haijiang), director of Taonan city tax bureau, and other lawless persons who had violated laws while enforcing laws were dealt with. The procuratorial organs also investigated and handled 189 cases on embezzlement, bribery, and misuse of public funds committed by banks and other economic management departments, captured a number of escaping economic criminals, verified cheated state tax refund on exported goods totaling 6.84 million yuan, and recovered 3.3 million yuan of it.
