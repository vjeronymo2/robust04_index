921006 FT 06 OCT 92 / Management (The Growing Business): Family problems will get an airing - In a Nutshell The problems facing family-owned businesses are the subject of a conference in Leuven, Belgium on November 12-14. The conference, organised by the Family Business Network, based at the Swiss business school, IMD, will consider issues such as the role of non-family managers, finance, and the value of non-family board members. Contact Small Business Research Institute, UFSAL University, Vrijheidslaan 17, 1080 Brussels, Belgium. Tel 32 2 412 4228.
