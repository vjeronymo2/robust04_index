940712 FT 12 JUL 94 / Thai government faces vote The Thai government is due to face a no-confidence debate in parliament at the same time as it plays host to the annual meeting of the Association of South-East Asian Nations, AP reports from Bangkok. Opposition parties have targeted four cabinet ministers in the no-confidence motion to be debated on July 27-28. Mr Prasong Soonsiri, foreign minister, is among those in the firing line.
