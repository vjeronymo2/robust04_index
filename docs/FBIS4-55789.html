BFN [Text] Tirana, May 15 (ATA) -- Albanian soldier Shaban Bajrami has been killed by two bullets fired into his mouth under charges of having killed two Serbian solders in the Mladenovc garrison in Serbia. Serbian military authorities said that Bajrami has been liquidated because he fled and did not accept to surrender. Information Council of the branch of the Party for Democratic Action in Medvegje reported these days about the killing of Shaban Bajrami from the village Kapit of Medvegje and described the grave situation of the Albanian population prior and after Bajrami was killed. Reports from the council throw light on some facts which show that Shaban Bajrami has not killed the two Serbian soldiers. Reports also confirm that Shaban Bajrami has not been executed in the village Ratare of Smederevska Pallanka, as Serbian military authorities has reported. On the basis of testimonies by Bajrami's family members, the council reports that Shaban Bajrami was shot with two bullets in the mouth. His father confirmed this after he saw his son's corpse when asked to carry it from Belgrade.
