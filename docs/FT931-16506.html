930106 FT 06 JAN 93 / International Company News: Upjohn to expand French plant UPJOHN is expanding its pharmaceutical chemical plant in Val de Reuil, northern France, for a total investment estimated to be Dollars 35m, Reuter reports from Kalamazoo. The US group said the expansion, which is expected to become operational in 1995, would integrate the most advanced technologies in quality control, productivity and environmental protection. Mr William Parfet, president, said the investment would enable it to meet increased demand for non-US clinical supplies.
