BFN ["sda"-attributed report: "Large Amount of Heroin Seized in Weinfelden] [Text] Frauenfeld, 7 June -- On Monday, two Turkish citizens carried out a drugs deal involving 3 kg of heroin a few meters away from the police station in Weinfeld. They were observed by the police and arrested. The Thurgau Investigations Office and police said on Tuesday that they had received information from Germany and Austria that the two arrested persons were dealers in weapons and drugs. The Thurgau authorities undertook investigations together with the Criminal Office of the land of Baden-Wuerttemberg. When it transpired that drugs were going to be sold, the criminal and security police entered a shop owned by one of the two Turks and arrested both of them. They are suspected of organized crime. One of them was recently convicted by the District Court of Bischofszell of possessing a few hundred grams of heroin. He has lodged an appeal.
