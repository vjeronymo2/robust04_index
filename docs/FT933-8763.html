930813 FT 13 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Turnround for Sleepy Kids A turnround from losses of Pounds 109,000 to pre-tax profits of Pounds 3,000 was announced by Sleepy Kids, the USM-quoted children's animation and merchandising group, in the half year to April 30. Mr Martin Powell, chairman, said the results reflected the progress the group continued to make not only with Budgie The Little Helicopter, the character created by the Duchess of York, but also with its other properties. Turnover amounted to Pounds 127,000 (Pounds 80,000).
