Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Fuzhou, December 30 (XINHUA) -- Jia Qinglin has been appointed as the new secretary of the Fujian Provincial Committee of the Communist Party of China (CPC). Zhang Quanjing, executive deputy head of the Organization Department of the CPC Central Committee, announced the appointment today at a meeting attended by local party, government and army leaders in this capital of Fujian Province, east China. Jia succeeds Chen Guangyi who was appointed director of the Civil Aviation Administration of China recently.
