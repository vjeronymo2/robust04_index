BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 10 Jun (NA) -- President Carlos Menem today issued precise instructions to Defense Minister Oscar Camilion to "quickly eliminate the compulsory recruitment service and implement an entirely professional Armed Forces." This was reported by the head of state, who unexpectedly asked reporters waiting outside the Olivos residence to enter the residence's main chalet, where the president was meeting with his ministers. Menem interrupted the meeting to release the news, and then boarded a helicopter to travel to the northern dock of the metropolitan port to preside over a farewell ceremony for the training ship Fragata Libertad. "We will report future steps in time. The Defense Ministry has instructions to take the appropriate measures, together with the military commanders and authorities, to fulfill what the president said," Menem added.
