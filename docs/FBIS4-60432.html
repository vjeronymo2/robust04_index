BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, chairman of the Presidential Council, held telephone conversations today with His Excellency Egyptian President Husni Mubarak, Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad, His Majesty King Fahd Bin 'Abd-al-Aziz, custodian of the two holy places, and His Majesty Shaykh Zayid bin Sultan al-Nahayyan, the president of the UAE. During these conversations, the brother chairman of the Presidential Council dwelt on political developments in Yemen and the country's military situation.
