BFN [Text] Brother President Yasir 'Arafat, president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, yesterday evening [25 June] received Yahya al-'Arashi, the Yemeni minister for civil service and administrative reform, the personal envoy of Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. The minister briefed President 'Arafat on the current developments in Yemen at all levels, especially the moves being made by the Yemeni leadership on the political and diplomatic levels to halt the fighting on the basis of the maintenance of Yemeni unity. Brother President 'Arafat stressed to the Yemeni minister that the Palestinian people and leadership supported the Yemeni people and their leadership, and that on our part we were fully prepared to exert every effort to halt the fighting and to support everything that will benefit the interests of Yemen. The meeting was attended by brothers 'Abbas Zaki and Sakhr Habash, members of the Fatah Central Committee, and Muhammad Ahmad Haydar, the Yemeni ambassador in Tunis [name and title as received].
