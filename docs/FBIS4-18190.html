BFN [Excerpts] Brussels, March 31 (AFP) -- NATO Thursday [31 March] called on Russia to discuss what it said were misunderstandings over the functioning of a joint partnership program after reports that Moscow was reconsidering plans to join the deal. "Reports hint at a misunderstanding over the reason for the existence of the Partnership, its objectives and functioning, said a NATO official who refused to be identified. We are ready to begin a dialogue with the Russians on the issue." [passage omitted] "NATO has understood that Russia wants to effectively join the Partnership and (Russians) actually indicated possible dates" for joining, the NATO official added. [passage omitted]
