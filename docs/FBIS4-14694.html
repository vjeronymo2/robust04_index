BFN [Text] Baku (March 31) TURAN -- Today Musavat Party made a statement. It says that on March 29 when the session of Milli Majlis [National Assembly] came to an end, the People's Deputy and a member of Musavat Party, Adalyat Ragimli, was beaten unmercifully by the Adviser of President's State Adviser, Neymat Panakhli, in the building of the Parliament. The essential fact is that Adalyat Ragimli criticized the policy of the current Azeri government and their attempts to shift the blame for the events of January 1990 onto the Popular [People's] Front. The Deputy was beaten in the presence of the employees of Supreme Soviet, Minister of Internal Affairs and Procurator General of the republic. The statement of Musavat Party demands to take necessary measures to punish Neymat Panakhli. "Otherwise reconciliation between different political forces in the republic will be impossible".
