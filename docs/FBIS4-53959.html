BFN [Text] Osaka, May 23 KYODO -- The Kansai Economic Federation (Kankeiren) on Monday [23 May] appointed Tetsuro Kawakami as its chairman to succeed Osamu Uno, who is retiring. The selection of Kawakami, chairman of Sumitomo Electric Industries Ltd., was adopted at a board meeting. The federation is a business lobby representing Kansai-based firms. Uno told a press conference after the meeting that the last days of his seven-year term were focused on pulling the economy out of the recession. Kawakami chose as one of his priority projects during his term a grandiose scheme to build a commercial and residential town near the Kansai International Airport, slated to open in early September, as well as other projects designed to boost the status of Osaka as a key hub of Asia.
