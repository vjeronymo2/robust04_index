Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 5 -- A delegation of the Cambodia's National Assembly led by its Chairman Samdech Chea Sim, left Hanoi today, ending its four-day official friendship visit to Vietnam. Prominent among, the farewell party were National Assembly Chairman Nong Duc Manh and Cambodian Charge d'Affaires to Vietnam i.e. Chhong Toeun. While here, the delegation visited the Thang Long Export Garment Company, the Mekong Car Corporation, the Hoa Binh hydro-electric power plant and Hoa Binh Province.
