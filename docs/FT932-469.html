930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / World Trade News: Taipei seeks Gatt deals TAIWAN said early today it would need a transitional period after joining the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade to conform with the world trade body's rules, Reuter reports from Geneva. It also announced it would not adhere to either Gatt's existing civil aviation code or the Gatt government procurement code until its industries became internationally competitive. Ke-Sheng Sheu, head of the delegation of the separate customs territory of Taiwan, Penghu, Kinmen and Matsu, made the statements to a special working party studying its application to Gatt.
