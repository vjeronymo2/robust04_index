Uzbekistan Increasing LD2305195494 Moscow INTERFAX in English 1811 GMT 23 May 94 LD2305195494 Moscow INTERFAX English BFN [Text] According to the International Narcotics Control Committee, the production of opium in Tajikistan has grown by more than 1.5 times to reach 650 tons a year since the beginning of the war in Afghanistan in December 1979. In addition, well-informed sources in Tashkent told Interfax that the quantity of narcotics confiscated during smuggling or transit through Uzbekistan in 1992-1993 has increased greatly. More than 16 tons of hashish, marijuana, opium and synthetic narcotics have been registered in criminal files over this period. Since the beginning of this year, ten criminal cases have been launched following attempts to smuggle narcotics to Uzbekistan.
