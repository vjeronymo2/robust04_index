920121 FT 21 JAN 92 / International Capital Markets: Generale Bank optimistic over earnings GENERALE Bank NV, Belgium's largest bank, yesterday gave an upbeat forecast of its 1991 profits, Reuter reports from Belgium. 'Our whole year results will largely confirm the extremely encouraging interim figures,' said Mr Ferdinand Chaffart, chairman of the bank's executive committee. In 1990, Generale Bank's group net profit amounted to BFr8.04bn (Dollars 248m) and the bank's first-half 1991 group net profit was BFr4.61bn.
