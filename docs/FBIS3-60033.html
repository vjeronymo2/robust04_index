Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo Airport narcotics agents today arrested a Nigerian passenger, a 54-year-old merchant, in possession of one kg of heroin hidden in his intestines with the intention of selling it in Egypt during the New Year celebrations. The Nigerian passenger arrived today aboard an Egyptian plane from Lagos in a state of a severe fatigue. Being suspicious, the security authorities searched him and found 75 capsules full of heroin on him.
