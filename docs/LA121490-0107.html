December 14, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

GRIDIRON-LOCK: Monday is going to be a nightmare for those commuters who use the Santa Ana, Garden Grove and Orange freeways. The Rams-49ers game will be drawing its sellout crowd to Anaheim Stadium right at the going-home commute hours. . . . "Kickoff is at 6, and fans don't have time to go home first so they drive one-to-a-car, and it's the '49ers game. You couldn't ask for a worse combination for traffic," said a city spokeswoman. . . . Her advice: If you're not going to the game, stay far away. Dallas M. Jackson and Lily Eng
