941128 FT 28 NOV 94 / Company News (This Week): Royal Bank of Scotland - Other Companies Forecasts for pre-tax profits for the full-year centre on Pounds 508m to Pounds 528m (against Pounds 258m last time) when the bank reports on Thursday. This takes account of the payment announced last year to Mr Peter Wood, chief executive and founder of Direct Line, the group's motor insurer, of Pounds 21m, and the extra Pounds 50m from RBS' disposal of its stake in 3i. Forecasts for the full-year dividend range from 12.5p to 13.8p, against 11p.
