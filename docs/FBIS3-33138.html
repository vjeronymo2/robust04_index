Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Report by Claudia Moreira from Brasilia] [Text] Finance Minister Fernando Henrique Cardoso again said that he is not a candidate for president of the Republic in the 1994 elections. He made this remark after President Itamar Franco said he will support Cardoso if he decided to become a candidate: [Begin Cardoso recording] The president is very generous, polite, and noble, but this does not mean the launching of any candidacy at all. [end recording] Minister Cardoso is now totally dedicated to the approval, in a second round of voting, of the social emergency fund bill that will be voted on in the plenary hall on 23 February.
