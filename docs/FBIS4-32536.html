BFN [Text] At about 0500 this morning, a Serbian Army convoy made up of 28 tanks and seven transportation trucks, as well as other military equipment, moved from Prishtina in the direction of Podujeve on the Miloshev-Leban road. A characteristic of the Serb Army movements this time is that many Serb flags were seen placed above the tanks. It is worthy of mention that in this region today, there were frequent flights by Serbian aircraft, and many military vehicles were observed on the streets of Prishtina, reports the Kosova Information Center.
