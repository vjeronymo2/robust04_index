February 24, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: EXIT LINES

Comedian George Burns, who recently celebrated his 93rd birthday, insists that he will play the London Palladium on his 100th. "I'm booked in for two weeks," Burns told Parade magazine. "If you're on the ball, you'll order your tickets now." Burns was born on Jan. 20, 1896. SHAUNA SNOW
