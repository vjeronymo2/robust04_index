BFN [Text] Bileca, 5 May (TANJUG)--Muslim forces today opened fire in the Osanica area, about 10 km southwest of Gorazde, on a group of UNPROFOR [UN Protection Force] officers who were on their way to a meeting with representatives of the republic of Srpska army, the command of the Herzegovina Corps announced this evening. Republic of Srpska army representatives were informed about the incident by UNPROFOR officers.
