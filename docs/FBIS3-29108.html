Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 14 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il was presented with a floral basket by the diplomatic corps here on his birthday. The floral basket was handed today to Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, by Syrian Ambassador Yasir Farah, doyen of the diplomatic corps. The doyen said that the Korean people are registering great achievements in the socialist construction because they have Comrade Kim Chong-il as their leader. Noting that the Korean-style socialism centred on the popular masses where the leader, the party and the masses are united in one mind is making a long drive under his wise leadership, he said that the Korean people, guided by the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il, are invincible.
