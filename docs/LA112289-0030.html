November 22, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP / ROCK

Old Blue Eyes in Red Square: Frank Sinatra may become the first singer in modern times to perform in Moscow's Red Square. The concert is reportedly scheduled for July 19 and will feature Sinatra singing the Russian folk song "Kalinka" with the Red Army Choir and performing with two dancers from the Bolshoi Ballet. He is also to sing a special version of "My Way," with new lyrics promoting peace and friendship. DANIEL CERONE
