Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] At a news conference on 14 January after a cabinet meeting, Tsutomu Hata, deputy prime minister and foreign minister, commented on a 20 January time limit for U.S. sanctions in the construction field and said: "Transparency of bidding will be achieved and competition will be promoted (by the action plan for revision of bidding procedures which will be officially announced on 18 January). I am sure the United States will appreciate the plan." Thus, he expects sanctions can be avoided.
