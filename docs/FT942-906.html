940627 FT 27 JUN 94 / Company News (This Week): Vendome - Other Companies On Friday, the luxury goods group, which was spun off from the restructuring of Rothmans last October, is expected to report profits of at least Pounds 205m for the year to March - ahead of a pro forma Pounds 187.8m. The group will also be reporting exceptional charges of more than Pounds 20m.
