940820 FT 20 AUG 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Shares ended mixed in unenthusiastic trade ahead of the weekend. De Beers came under renewed pressure from US selling to close down R2.50 at R107. The overall index shed 15 to 5,799, while industrials lost 27 to 6,589 and the gold index rose 15 to 2,196.
