BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council and secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, tonight in Aden received brother Ambassador Badr Hammam, Egyptian assistant foreign minister and envoy of President Muhammad Husni Mubarak, who conveyed to him a message from his brother President Mubarak dealing with current developments, and the Egyptian leadership's concern about the escalation of these developments, and efforts to contain the crisis. The meeting was attended by brothers Muhammad Salih Qutaysh, acting undersecretary at the foreign ministry--Aden branch, and 'Ata Harun, the ambassador of the Arab Republic of Egypt in our country.
