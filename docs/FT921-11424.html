920131 FT 31 JAN 92 / World Trade News: Nokia secures Japan foothold Nokia, the Finnish maker of mobile telephones, has become the first European manufacturer to secure access to the Japanese cellular telephone market, it announced yesterday, writes Robert Taylor in Stockholm. It has concluded a pact with Nippon Idou Tsushin, a cellular network operator in the Tokyo and Nagoya areas with an estimated 25 per cent market share.
