BFN [Text] Pakistani Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto received our country's government economic delegation led by Hong Song-nam, vice premier, on 6 April. During the meeting, the head of the delegation conveyed the greetings from the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il to Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto. The prime minister expressed her deep gratitude to this and asked the head of the delegation to convey her sincere greetings to the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. The prime minister mentioned her impressions on the visit to our country and said that she feels it is her responsibility to develop the traditional friendship between the two countries and talked about how to further develop the friendship and cooperation in various aspects. The governor of Pakistan's Sindh Province, the Sindh Province responsible officer, other functionaries concerned, and our country's ambassador to Pakistan were present on the occasion.
