940923 FT 23 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Russian bank lifts Middlesex stake The Russian joint stock commercial bank, Vozrozhdeniye, is to buy another 50.7m shares at 5p each in Middlesex Holdings, the metal mining, recycling and trading company. The bank currently holds 4.7 per cent of share capital in the USM-traded company. Purchase of the second tranche of shares, for Pounds 2.54m, would bring its holding to 12.3 per cent. Middlesex also intends to enter into a joint venture with the bank to provide trade finance for companies worldwide.
