October 3, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

NAIA OKS 2 BASKETBALL DIVISIONS

Schools will be separated into two divisions each in men's and women's basketball under a resolution adopted today at the 50th annual NAIA convention.

The proposal, which has been considered at the three previous National Association of Intercollegiate Athletics conventions, was approved 257-143. Three delegates abstained.

The divisional setup, similar to that for the small colleges that compete in NAIA football, will begin for a two-year trial period in 1991-92. Schools will determine what division they play in -- with financial-aid capabilities and enrollments among the determining factors.

The NAIA said separate championships will be held in the two divisions for both men's and women's basketball.
