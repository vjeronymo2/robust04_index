920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit and vegetables Conference pears are the best fruit buy this week at 34-45p a lb (35-45p), along with English Discovery apples at 25-30p a lb (29-35p) and French Golden Delicious at 29-38p a lb (30-45p), reports FFVIB. British cauliflower continue in plentiful supply, priced between 30-59p each (35-45p), depending on size. British potatoes at 8-12p a lb (10-15p), English primo cabbage at 18-25p a lb (20-25p), and English savoy cabbage, 20-25p a lb (20-25p) complete the vegetable prices this week. There are still plenty of English tomatoes to enjoy at 30-49p a lb (30-45p) as well as Chinese leaves at 50-65p each (50-65p).
