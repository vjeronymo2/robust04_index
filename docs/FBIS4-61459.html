BFN [Text] A state delegation from Tajikistan headed by Emomali Rakhmonov, head of state and chairman of the Tajikistan Supreme Soviet, left for Moscow today on a working visit at the invitation of Boris Yeltsin, president of Russian Federation. The delegation comprises Abdujalil Samadov, chairman of the republic's Council of Ministers; Kayum Kavmiddinov, chairman of the National Bank; Rashid Olimov, minister of foreign Affairs; Rustam Mirzoyev, minister of economics and forecasting; (Yunusov), minister of finance; and a group of experts. In Moscow Emomali Rakhmonov, head of state and chairman of the Tajikistan Supreme Soviet, will meet with Boris Yeltsin, president of the Russian Federation, and other state officials and will sign documents regulating mutual beneficial relations. The state delegation was seen off at Dushanbe airport by the vice chairmen of the Supreme Soviet and of the Council of Ministers and other officials.
