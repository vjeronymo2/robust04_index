930731 FT 31 JUL 93 / Finance & the Family: Peps lead unit trust surge - At a Glance UNIT trusts had a bumper second quarter, with net sales reaching nearly Pounds 2.5bn, higher than in any period since the third quarter of 1987. Private investors have returned to the sector, buying a net Pounds 1.98bn of trusts in the first half of 1993. Peps were particularly popular, with net sales of nearly Pounds 1.6bn in the first half. Total funds managed by the industry have reached a record Pounds 76.4bn.
