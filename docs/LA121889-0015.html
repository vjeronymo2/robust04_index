December 18, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Pasadena-based Jacobs Engineering Group Inc. said its common stock is being listed on the New York Stock Exchange under the symbol JEC . . . Cineplex Odeon Corp. said it completed the sale of its Pantages Theater and live theater division in Toronto to former Chairman Garth H. Drabinsky and a colleague for $56 million in cash and the assumption of $19 million in liabilities . . . CVB Financial Corp., the Ontario-based parent of Chino Valley Bank, announced that it has begun trading on the American Stock Exchange under the ticker symbol CVB . . . The Bank of New England said it is boosting its loan-loss reserves by $900 million, is exploring the sale of the bank or its assets, and expects a substantial loss for the year.
