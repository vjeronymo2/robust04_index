September 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COMING CLEAN

"Coming Clean" was the most profound no-nonsense description of curing the drug and alcoholic abuses that I've ever read.

Hernandez is to be commended for the time and effort spent at Impact House, one of the three largest on the West Coast. As a past president and presently a member of the board of directors, I and my vast number of friends are thrilled at the true picture portrayed about a dedicated staff at Impact House.

MORT TOOREDMAN

Van Nuys
