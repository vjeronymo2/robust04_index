Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 18 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Five representatives of the oppositional parties of the country have decided to resume their participation in the activities of the Great State Hural if proposals forwarded by the president of the country at the opening ceremony of the autumn session of the Parliament would be accepted by the Parliament. Such a statement was made at the press conference by a member of the Great State Hural R. Gonchigdorj. He noted that the opposition in the Great State Hural are fighting not for power but in the interests of particular strata of the Mongolian public. According to him, the opposition parties would not abandon their democratic principles standing for the creation of a state structure to be acting in the interests of the whole people of Mongolia.
