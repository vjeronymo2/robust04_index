940112 FT 12 JAN 94 / People: Mobil in the UK John Banfield, a career executive with Mobil Oil, is to take over as chairman of the UK arm of the US-based company. He will succeed Geoffrey Cardinal, who moves on to Mobil Egypt. A Cambridge graduate, Banfield has spent most of his career in marketing. Overseas stints have included postings in New York, Cyprus, the Netherlands and Germany, where he is a manager at the marketing and refining division in Hamburg.
