BFN [Text] Another incident between the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] and the Palestinian police occurred near the Erez checkpoint yesterday. Our military correspondent Alon Ben-David reports that an argument erupted between an IDF patrol and the Palestinian police, during which the Palestinian policemen cocked their weapons and aimed them at an Israeli officer. The Palestinians claim that the IDF soldiers cocked their weapons first, while the IDF soldiers claim the Palestinians threatened them without cause. The joint liaison and coordination committee launched an investigation into the incident.
