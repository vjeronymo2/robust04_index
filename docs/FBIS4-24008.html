BFN [Excerpt] More than 900,000 who had sought refuge in neighboring countries have already returned to Mozambique. Alfredo del Rio, representative of the UN High Commissioner for Refugees in Mozambique, said this at a news conference in Maputo this morning on the occasion of Africa Refugee Day marked today. A message from Mrs. Ogata, UN high commissioner for refugees, was also read on the occasion. [passage omitted]
