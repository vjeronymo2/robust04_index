941209 FT 09 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Trident steps up its Lloyd's involvement Trident, a Bermuda-based company investing in insurance and reinsurance, has increased its involvement in Lloyd's of London by providing funds for a new company investing in syndicates run by Venton managing agencies. Trident was formed a year ago by Marsh McLennan, the world's largest insurance broker, J P Morgan, Mid Ocean, a Bermuda reinsurance company, and Byrne Sons, a small investment bank. Venton has raised a total of Pounds 18.5m in corporate capital to invest in three insurance syndicates via two vehicles, one capitalised exclusively by Trident. Venton has not disclosed how much Trident, which is also connected with the Hiscox managing agencies, has invested.
