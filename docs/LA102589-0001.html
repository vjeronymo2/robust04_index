October 25, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

ON THE RECORD

"It's time for you to stop it and begin acting like the kind of lady you so repeatedly say you are."

-- Beverly Hills Municipal Judge Charles G. Rubin after sentencing Zsa Zsa Gabor to three days in jail, fines and retribution totaling $12,937 and 120 hours of community service for slapping a Beverly Hills police officer and related charges.
