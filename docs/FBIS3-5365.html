Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 7 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Chong-il, chairman of the National Defence Commission of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea and supreme commander of the Korean People's Army [KPA], on March 5 saw a music and dance performance of the artistes of the song and dance ensemble of the Korean People's Army. Appreciating the performance with him were Kye Ung-tae, member of the Political Bureau and secretary of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK], Kim Chung-nin, Kim Ki-nam, Kim Kuk-tae and Kim Yong-sun, secretaries of the C.C., the WPK, and senior officials of the party Central Committee and of the press. The orchestral music and chorus "The Sound of Horse's Hooves in Mt. Paektu" was the curtain-raiser of the colorful program which included the dances "The Kitchen Cajt Runs" and "The Final Decisive Battle," the mixed duet "Affection of Officer for his Men in a March," the men's group singing "We Will Keep the Red Party Card at Our Heart," and the solo and group singing to the accompaniment of Kayagum "The Paradise of Hope Focusing World's Attention." After seeing the performance, Comrade Kim Chong-il noted with deep satisfaction that the creative workers and artistes of the ensemble created and performed revolutionary and militant stage works high in ideological and artistic plane and highly estimated their success and extended them thanks of the party central military commission and the Supreme Commander of the Korean People's Army. He said that all the numbers of the program were very good works suited to the character of the Army and the demand of the times and that the performances of the ensemble gave deep confidence and courage to our people and People's Army soldiers for their militancy and strong appeal. Noting that the songs, dances and all other works well showed the courage and resourcefulness and the revolutionary mettle of our People's Army, the Army of the party and the revolution, which upholds the party and the leader with arms, defends the socialist system with arms and is carrying the cause of socialism to completion with arms, he expressed satisfaction at the fact that the song and dance ensemble of the Korean People's Army was fulfilling with credit its basic duty to educate the People's Army soldiers to devotedly serve the party and the leader, the motherland and the people, conscious of its being the defender of the party and the guard of the revolution, by creating many revolutionary and militant stage works. Saying that the KPA song and dance ensemble, the militant art unit of our revolutionary Armed Forces, must continue energetic art activities to educate soldiers in revolutionary and militant way, he indicated a concrete orientation of creation which would serve as the guideline in art creation. He stressed that it is important for the ensemble to represent well in its stage works the great political and ideological features and invincible might of the People's Army soldiers who are firmly guaranteeing the revolutionary cause of our party with arms, united rock-firm behind the party and the leader. Noting that it is a very important matter in creating works to vividly represent the life of soldiers as shown in the performance, he said that the ensemble must depict well the loyalty of soldiers to the party and the leader, and their spirit of devoted service for the motherland and people, indomitable revolutionary spirit and revolutionary comradeship and other noble mentality and combat features. The ensemble must maintain its inherent style, socialist, revolutionary, militant, dignified and grandiose, not only in content but also in form to suit its mission and duty as an art organisation educating the People's Army soldiers and serving them, he said. Saying that all the stage works created by the ensemble must be flawless ideologically and artistically as they contribute to educating the invincible revolutionary ranks, a-match-for-a-hundred revolutionary soldiers, he stressed that a new orchestral music, chuche-based and national in style, conforming to the character of the Army must be created. Comrade Kim Chong-il expressed the belief that the creative workers and artistes of the People's Army song and dance ensemble would actively contribute to the accomplishment of the revolutionary cause of our party with its energetic creative activities conducive to the education of soldiers.
