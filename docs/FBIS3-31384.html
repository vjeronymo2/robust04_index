Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sofia, February 15 (BTA) -- The purpose of my visit here is to discuss bilateral military cooperation issues of mutual interest with Army General Lyuben Petrov, chief of the General Staff of the Bulgarian Army, said Admiral Khristos Limberis, chief of the General Staff of the National Defence of Greece, upon [name as received] his arrival here today. This is Admiral Limberis' first visit to Bulgaria in implementation of the Military Cooperation Plan between the two countries. According to General Petrov, apart from military cooperation issues, the two sides will also discuss the Partnership for Peace initiative and Bulgaria's accession to it, as well as some aspects of the situation in the Balkans. Today the guest will be received by Defence Minister Valentin Aleksandrov and later by the Chief of the Parliamentary National Security Committee Nikolay Slatinski.
