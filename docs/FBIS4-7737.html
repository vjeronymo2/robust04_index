BFN [Text] Mikhail Ukleba, first deputy minister of foreign affairs for Georgia, met with Yordan Shkolagerski, deputy chairman of the National Assembly, and Foreign Minister Stanislav Daskalov on 4 April. After talks with Deputy Foreign Minister Dimitur Ikonomov at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, a cooperation protocol will be signed. Experts are also working on a specific document which will regulate the trade and economic relations between the two countries. One of the goals of Ukleba's visit is to discuss issues related to the work of the Black Sea Economic Cooperation Zone. Ukleba conveyed a message from Georgian President Eduard Shevardnadze to Bulgarian President Zhelyu Zhelev which points out that we must strengthen and expand our relations in all directions.
