December 19, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

SEVERAL FORD MODELS RECALLED DUE TO EMISSIONS PROBLEMS

Ford Motor Co. on Monday recalled 64,115 cars and light trucks because of emission system problems.

The company said it was recalling 64,000 1986 model Lincoln Town Cars, Continentals and Mark VIIs; Ford Crown Victoria, Thunderbird and Mustang; Mercury Grand Marquis, Cougar and Capri, and some 1987 Lincoln Mark VII cars with 5-liter or 5-liter, high-output engines.
