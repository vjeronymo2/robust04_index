BFN [Text] The Iraqi Drilling Compnay, which belongs to the Oil Ministry, has scored higher percentages in drilling water wells for the General Irrigation and Land Reclamation Establishment in different Iraqi governorates. A spokesman for the company said a contract was signed with the Establishment to drill 200 wells in al-Najaf at depths ranging from 100 to 200 meters at a total cost of 16 million dinars. The spokesman added that company teams are implementing this project using their own resources and capabilities despite geological hardships.
