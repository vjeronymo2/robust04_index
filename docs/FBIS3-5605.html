Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 8.-- A high-level military delegation of Laos, led by Defence Minister Sr. Lt. Gen. Choummali Sai-gnason, left here this afternoon, winding up a 4-day official visit to Vietnam. While here, the Lao delegation held talks with a high level military delegation of Vietnam led by Defense Minister Gen. Doan Khue. The two sides informed each other of the situation in their respective countries. They noted with satisfaction that despite sweeping changes in the world and the region in recent years, the relationship between the two countries and armies kept on developing, thereby making active contributions to building their countries, and to peace, cooperation and stability in the region. Both sides compared notes on the building of the army, the all-people national defence and on issues of common concern. The two sides reaffirmed their determination to foster the special friendship and solidarity between the peoples and armies of Vietnam and Laos. A protocol on the friendly cooperation between Lao and Vietnamese armies was signed today by Sr. Lt. Gen. Siphon Phalikhan, vice defence minister of Laos, and Lt. Gen. Nguyen Thoi Bung, vice defence minister of Vietnam. Vietnamese and Lao defence Ministers Doan Khue and Choummali Sai-gnason witnessed the signing. Earlier, the Lao delegation paid a floral tribute to late President Ho Chi Minh at his mausoleum. It was received by Party General Secretary Do Muoi.
