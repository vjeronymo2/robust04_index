Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Amman -- Well-informed Palestinian sources have stated that several Fatah leaders and cadres are holding consultations among themselves to convene Fatah' Revolutionary Council to discuss the situation in the Palestinian arena in light of the recent developments in the occupied territories and the bloody massacre committed by the Rabin government against worshipers in the Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron. A Fatah source told AL-DUSTUR that there are undeclared real divisions in the Fatah Movement ranks, the most acute of which is that the Fatah command outside the territories has lost control over the Fatah strike forces in the occupied territories which call for suspending the negotiations with Israel. The sources added that several Fatah military officials might announce stands completely different from Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat's stands if the Revolutionary Council does not convene.
