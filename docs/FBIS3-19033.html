Language: Estonian Article Type:BFN [Text] Estonian Interior Minister Heiki Arike and [Eduard] Lintner, the parliamentary state secretary at the German Ministry of Interior, have signed an intergovernmental agreement in Bonn, on cooperation in fighting organized crime, terrorism, and other serious crimes. According to the Estonian Interior Ministry's press service, Estonia will now have access to German databases and German experts for consultation and help in fighting such crimes as money laundering, drug business, forgeries of bank checks and credit cards, and terrorism, not customary in Estonia. The German Ministry of Interior has also promised to provide help to Estonia with staff training and equipment, as reported by the Interior Ministry's press service.
