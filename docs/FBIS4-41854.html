BFN [Excerpts] Boris Yeltsin, who is touring Moscow region, today visited the Soyuz Research and Production Association in Lyubertsy. During a brief conversation with journalists, the president said the political situation has calmed down and is gradually returning to normal. The Treaty on Social Accord has started to work. Boris Yeltsin thinks it is necessary to focus on implementing a targeted program for eliminating poverty. The president believes the gap between the minimum wage of 20,000 rubles and the average wage of 500,000 rubles a month is unacceptable. Boris Yeltsin also said the economic situation in Russia will stabilize this year. [passage omitted] Answering a question by an INTERFAX correspondent, Boris Yeltsin acknowledged that budgetary means for developing the defense industry are insufficient. Therefore, nonbudgetary means will have to be used.
