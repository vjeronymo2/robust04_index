July 21, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SHE MIGHT BE A COURT, BUT SOME READERS SAY SHE SHOULD NOT JUDGE

I was amazed that you dignified Margaret Court's homophobic remarks about Martina Navratilova by printing them as a news story. Would you publish such a story without editorial rebuttal if Court were commenting on race, ethnicity or religion? Martina has gained the respect of players and fans because she is so very talented, intense and disciplined. The fact that she is at ease in her personal life and is in a loving relationship is far from a "bad example for younger players" or anyone else.

RITA SCHERREI

Studio City
