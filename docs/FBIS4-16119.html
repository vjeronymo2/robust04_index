BFN ["Defense Ministry press service report"--read by announcer] [Text] The Armenian Army's offensive against Geranboyskiy [Shaumyanovskiy], Terterskiy, and Agdamskiy Rayons have been continuing for the last four days. Our units fighting in Geranboyskiy Rayon last night and in the early morning of 13 April succeeded in inflicting heavy blows on the enemy, in ousting the enemy from Gulistan in Geranboyskiy Rayon and from Talish in Terterskiy Rayon, and in recovering their former positions. After an artillery barrage at 0600 today, the enemy launched a new offensive against Terterskiy Rayon. The city of Terter is still being shelled by Grad launchers. Zangishaly, Mekhrizli, and Sarydzhaly in Agdamskiy Rayon have been coming under artillery shelling since 0630 this morning. The bloody battles are continuing uninterrupted in the Terterskiy and Agdamskiy sectors of the front. A large number of enemy troops were killed during the fighting.
