Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shchegoleva] [Text] Sochi, 7 March -- The Abkhazian Defense Ministry regards the actions of the Georgian military command as preparations for a new aggression. It issued a statement today which notes that, in the period from 24 February to 6 March, the Mkhedrioni and White Eagle military units, as well as armored vehicles and other weapons of the Georgian Army, were transferred from central Georgia to Zugdidskiy rayon. The statement says that the Georgian special services are continuing to send subversive and terrorist groups to the territory of the Republic of Abkhazia. The Abkhaz side stresses that it will take the necessary measures appropriate to the actions of the enemy. All responsibility for the consequences rests with the Georgian side.
