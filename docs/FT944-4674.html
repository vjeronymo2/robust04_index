941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: E Surrey at Pounds 3.42m Interim pre-tax profits at East Surrey Holdings, the water supply, property and building supplies group, were 10 per cent ahead at Pounds 3.42m, compared with Pounds 3.11m last time. Turnover for the half-year to September 30 rose 4 per cent from Pounds 12.6m to Pounds 13.1m. Earnings per share came out at 21.9p (21p) and the interim dividend rises to 5p (4.43p).
