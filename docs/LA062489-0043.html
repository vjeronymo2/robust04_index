June 24, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

CHINA, 1989: THE SHATTERED DREAM

Beijing's bloody suppression of student protesters June 3-4 and harsh crackdown on dissent sent a shock wave of revulsion around the world.

In Sunday's editions of The Times, a special 12-page report, "The Shattered Dream, China/1989," examines the roots of the dramatic events, the crackdown and prospects for the future, including vivid details and new photos of the massacre.
