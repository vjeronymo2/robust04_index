921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / Parliament and Politics: PM's seat faces cut in electorate THE Boundary Commission yesterday published its latest proposals for the redrawing of the parliamentary constituency map, on this occasion including the prime minister's Huntingdon constituency in Cambridgeshire. Commissioners propose cutting Huntingdon's electorate of more than 90,000 to just over 70,000. The commission has to report its plans for the whole country by the end of 1994.
