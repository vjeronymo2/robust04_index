930617 FT 17 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Cook calls on Heseltine to bring charges against Nadir Labour's Robin Cook yesterday called on Michael Heseltine, trade and industry secretary, to bring charges against Asil Nadir and 'any other donor' who failed to declare donations to the Tory party. It emerged yesterday that the DTI is preparing actions to disqualify former directors of Polly Peck International but is not contemplating action against Nadir. Mr Cook alleged that indecisiveness by the DTI could lead to Nadir becoming 'eligible to hold company office' when his bankruptcy was discharged
