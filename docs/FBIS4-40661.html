BFN [Text] In order for the presidential edict on further improvement of land relations to be carried out, the Cabinet of Ministers endorsed the procedures of purchase and sale by legal entities and citizens of the Republic of Kazakhstan of the right to the lifelong inheritance and possession, use, or rent of land plots. The state committee of land relations and land use is charged with the task of clarifying, if and when necessary, the basic rates of guidance prices for these rights on the basis of the state statistical data on the general inflation rate. The full text of the document will be published in the press.
