March 12, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

"The Measure of a Man," the Feb. 19 episode of "Star Trek: The Next Generation," was wonderful. Fascinating . . . intriguing . . . superb . . . beautiful -- it was all these and much, much more. Not since "City on the Edge of Forever" has "Star Trek" produced such a heart-rending and heartwarming show.

Rivka Gila Simon, Los Angeles
