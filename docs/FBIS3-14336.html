Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Text] The opening ceremony of the Group of 15 (G-15) summit was held today in New Delhi, India. President Suharto addressed the ceremony on behalf of Asian member countries in the group. On this occasion, President Suharto said the time has come for countries from the North or South to make a new collaborative effort for development and take the initiative to promote a democratic spirit for a productive partnership to seek a global solution to the existing problem. He said to date, efforts to launch the North-South dialogue has shown some positive results, presented our determination, and indicated our common views on the best way to create a new world order. On the G-15, President Suharto said that within three years, the G-15 had embarked on forming 18 cooperation projects covering information systems, finance, agriculture, population, science and technology, energy, as well as the environment. President Suhato expressed his gladness over the increase in positive responses from the business sector in taking advantage of the opportunities available. The fourth G-15 summit was attended by eight countries. It is expected to end on 30 March.
