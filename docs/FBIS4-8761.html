BFN [Text] Sarajevo, April 17 (AFP) -- Serb tanks were continuing to fire on the Moslem town of Gorazde, a UN "safe haven," a ham operator said Sunday, despite the declaration earlier of an immediate ceasefire there by Bosnian Serb leader Radovan Karadzic. Shells from Serb tanks, which rolled into the town Sunday afternoon, were still raining down on the town said the radio operator who contacted Sarajevo around 7:45 p.m. (1745 GMT).
