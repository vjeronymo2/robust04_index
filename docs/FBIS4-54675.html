BFN [Relayed by Warsaw TV Polonia Network] [Text] Sejm Speaker Jozef Oleksy said at a meeting with workers at the Warsaw FSO [Automobile Factory] today that, by organizing strikes, Solidarity is not fighting in the interest of employees but is seeking to achieve the upper hand over other trade unions. According to the speaker, Solidarity has been deliberately avoiding negotiations with the government. It has announced the escalation of strike action because the aim of the union is to overthrow Waldemar Pawlak's Cabinet. Jozef Oleksy said that trade unions should primarily fight to resolve problems of employees and not play political games for their own benefit.
