930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / International Company News: Sparebanken boosts profits SPAREBANKEN Nor, Norway's biggest savings bank, boosted first-quarter pre-tax profit by NKr380m to NK384m, due to substantial gains on shares and bonds and improvements in ordinary operations, writes Karen Fossli. It forecast a profit for 1993 and said it may even be in a position to pay a dividend.
