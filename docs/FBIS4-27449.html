BFN [Text] A five-member Myanmar [Burmese] delegation led by Construction Minister U Khin Maung Yin, which paid a goodwill visit to the People's Republic of China [PRC] at the invitation of PRC's minister of railways, this afternoon returned to Yangon [Rangoon] by Myanmar airways International flight via Singapore. The minister was welcomed at Yangon airport by Brigadier General Maung Maung, minister of livestock breeding and fisheries; Colonel Pe Thein, minister of prime minister's office; Col. Aung San, deputy minister of construction; Mr. Liang Feng, ambassador of the PRC; responsible personnel, friends, and family members.
