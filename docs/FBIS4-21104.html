BFN [Text] Three members of a syndicate recruiting youngsters to export hard drugs have been arrested in Lagos by the National Drug Law Enforcement Agency. The chairman of the task force on hard drugs, Brigadier General Musa Baaniyi, made this known in a statement. He said the syndicate had recruited a 17-year-old student, who was arrested at Murtala Mohamed Airport, to export hard drugs to the United States. Gen. Baaniyi appealed to parents, guardians, and school principals to monitor the activities of their students at all times. He advised those with children or wards possessing international passports to ensure the safekeeping of the document.
