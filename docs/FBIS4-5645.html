BFN [Text] Tokyo, April 11 KYODO -- The Bank of Japan (BOJ) appointed Yasuhiro Harada on Monday [11 April] as general manager in the Americas, the bank's top post in the United States, BOJ officials said. Harada, 48, replaces Masahiro Sugita, who will move to the personnel department, the officials said. Harada previously served as deputy director of the international department. He joined the central bank in 1968.
