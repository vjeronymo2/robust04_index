940324 FT 24 MAR 94 / UK Company News: BB&EA declines to Pounds 902,000 Profits of British Building & Engineering Appliances, the specialist building products and services group, fell from Pounds 1.41m to Pounds 902,000 pre-tax for the half year to December 31. The figure was in line with expectations and was struck from a higher turnover of Pounds 24.9m (Pounds 21m). Interest took Pounds 51,000 more at Pounds 234,000 but tax fell from Pounds 464,000 to Pounds 298,000. Earnings per share slipped by 3p to 5.4p but the interim dividend is being maintained at 2.5p.
