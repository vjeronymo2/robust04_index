940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / US-Japan talks on Thursday US and Japanese negotiators will meet on Thursday in Washington to seek a way to restart trade talks stalled since February, US Trade Representative Mickey Kantor said yesterday, AP reports from Washington. Mr Kantor told reporters the discussions would follow preliminary talks he held with then-Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata last month in Morocco. He gave no indication who would lead the Japanese delegation. Mr Hata, now prime minister, has let it be known he wants to resume the negotiations, known as 'framework talks', aimed at opening Japan's markets in areas ranging from cars to medical equipment.
