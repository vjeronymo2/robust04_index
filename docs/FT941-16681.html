940108 FT 08 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Serif SERIF: of the 49.5m new shares offered in the recent rights issue some 28.8m shares, representing the renounced rights of John Pryke and certain members of his family and Ray Deeks, have been placed with institutional and other investors. Of the balance acceptances for 17.9m shares (86.6 per cent) were received.
