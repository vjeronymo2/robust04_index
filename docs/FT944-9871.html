941114 FT 14 NOV 94 / Costs row halts Midlands project A dispute about future costs has stopped building work at the site of the new headquarters of the Birmingham Midshires Building Society near Wolverhampton, west Midlands, and led to the withdrawal of the contractor, John Mowlem, from the project. Mowlem has apologised to its sub-contractors and professional consultants, all of whom had expected to see the project to completion. But Midshires said a natural break had been reached in the project with the completion of the shell-and-core work - the basic construction of the building. 'We always had it mind to retain flexibility to bring the fitting-out phase in-house,' it said.
