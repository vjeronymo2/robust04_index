December 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

I was appalled to turn on my favorite late night news program "Nightline" (ABC, Dec. 3) and find Madonna and her video the evening's topic.

I called KABC to complain and was further shocked when I was told my opinion was "a refreshing change" from the other calls the station was receiving.

My complaint was simple. I objected to Channel 7's use of precious airtime of Madonna and her hardly news-worthy video. I was told most calls were complaints about morality.

Why don't the news programs just keep us up to date on current crises and leave the trash to trash TV?

Leslie Myrick, Los Angeles
