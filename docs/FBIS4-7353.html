BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 26 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today sent a message of greetings to Valentine Esegragbo Melvin Strasser, head of state and chairman of the National Provisional Ruling Council of the Republic of Sierra Leone, on the 33rd anniversary of the independence of Sierra Leone. In the message he wished the chairman and the people of Sierra Leone great achievements in their work for the stability and democratic development of the country and expressed the belief that the friendly relations between the two countries would continue to develop.
