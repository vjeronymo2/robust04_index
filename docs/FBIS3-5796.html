Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Australian Government has opened tenders for the rebuilding of up to 16 medium span bridges in Cambodia in an aid project worth $8.5 million [Australian dollars]. The project followed the construction of six bridges along Cambodia's national Route-5 from Phnom Penh to the border with Thailand. The federal minister for development cooperation, Gordon Bilney, said the latest bridge project matches Australian engineering expertise with Cambodia's development priority. The bridge rebuilding is part of a nearly $17 million worth of development projects Australia is funding in Cambodia this year. Mr. Bilney also announced the $600,000 grant for credit scheme in Cambodia aimed at helping people at the village level start small businesses and agricultural schemes.
