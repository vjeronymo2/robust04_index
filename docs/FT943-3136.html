940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Try jumps to Pounds 0.25m Try Group, the construction and housebuilding company, reported a jump in pre-tax profits from Pounds 68,000 to Pounds 252,000 in the half year to June 30. For 1993 there were losses of Pounds 2.17m. Turnover of continuing operations slipped to Pounds 60.1m (Pounds 61.9m) but operating profits rose from Pounds 338,000 to Pounds 486,000. Earnings per share came through at 0.34p (0.08p) basic and 0.37p (0.11p) fully diluted. The interim dividend is held at 0.5p.
