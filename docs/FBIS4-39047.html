BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Valeriy Shanayev] [Text] Vladikavkaz, 6 Jun (ITAR-TASS) -- During his trip to the North Caucasus, Nikolay Yegorov, the Russian minister for affairs of nationalities and regional policy, aims to familiarize himself on the spot with the latest situation in the state of emergency zone and decide how to implement the presidential decrees and the resolutions of the Russian Government. On Monday, he arrived in Vladikavkaz, where he met with North Ossetia President Akhsarbek Galazov. This afternoon Nikolay Yegorov, Vladimir Lozovoy, head of the interim administration, and a group of representatives from federal ministries and departments, flew out to Nazran where Yegorov is to meet with Ruslan Aushev, president of Ingushetia.
