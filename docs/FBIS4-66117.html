BFN [Unattributed article: "Germany/Italy: Scalfaro Congratulates Herzog"] [Text] (ANSA) Rome, May 23 -- Italian Head of State Oscar Luigi Scalfaro this evening sent a message of congratulations to the new president of the German Federal Republic, Roman Herzog, in which he stressed the ties of friendship between Italy and Germany and the tasks they must face together in the future. "At the moment you have been called to fill the State's highest office" Scalfaro wrote in his message, "I wish to send you my most sincere and warm best wishes for the happy and profitable execution of the high duties which the Federal parliament has assigned you." "I am sure that, under your guidance" the note continued, "the deep ties between our countries and between our peoples, linked by centuries of friendship and by common European and Atlantic ideals, will find new causes for enrichment and development." The Italian president then added "Italy and Germany are, in fact, called on to give, together with the other members of the European Union, a fundamental contribution to the new Europe and a more free and just world." "In the hope of soon having the occasion to meet with you" Scalfaro concluded, "I send a word of friendship along with the best personal wishes for you and your family."
