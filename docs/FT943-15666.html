940708 FT 08 JUL 94 / Australian jobless up again Australia's unemployment rate moved back into double digits last month, although the apparent setback was dismissed as a technical distortion, writes Nikki Tait in Sydney. Seasonally adjusted, June saw unemployment at 10 per cent, against 9.8 per cent in May. But some analysts pointed to a net 30,400 full-time jobs created during the month, with full-time employment moving back above 6m for the first time since February 1991.
