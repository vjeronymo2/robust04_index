BFN [Text] Four Iraqi citizens who over a month ago infiltrated from Jordan to Israel and asked for political asylum, were sent off by IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldiers into Lebanese territory this afternoon. UNIFIL [UN Interim Force in Lebanon] Spokesman Timor Goksel told our correspondent in the north Yo'av Aviv that the IDF soldiers brought the four Iraqis, whose hands were tied, up to the border, untied them, and the four marched northward to a UNIFIL post. They have been held in prison since they crossed the border into Israel, and have now been handed over to Lebanon.
