BFN [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk and Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko have received telegrams from Russian President Boris Yeltsin and Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev. In the telegrams Boris Yeltsin and Andrey Kozyrev express their gratitude for the congratulations and good wishes in connection with Russia's national holiday--the Adoption of the Declaration of the Russian Federation's Sovereignty Day. In his telegram, President Yeltsin confirmed Russia's desire to develop friendly all-round relations with fraternal Ukraine unwaveringly in the name of the well-being of the peoples of our two countries. Minister Kozyrev expressed his confidence that the development of mutually advantageous interstate relations between Russia and Ukraine will further promote consolidation of friendship and mutual understanding between the nations of the two countries, contribute to achieving security and stability in Europe and the world over.
