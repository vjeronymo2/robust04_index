October 11, 1989, Wednesday, Southland Edition

QUAKE JOLTS TURKEY

An earthquake with a magnitude of 4.9 shook the central Anatolian province of Kayseri on Tuesday, causing damage but no casualties, the semiofficial Anatolia News Agency reported.

The dispatch said four houses and the minaret of a mosque collapsed in the villages of Sarayli and Doruklu. Many houses had cracks in their walls, the agency said.
