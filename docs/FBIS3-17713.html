Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The NOVAYA YEZHEDNEVNAYA GAZETA reports that the Russian Defense Ministry has confirmed the existence of a secret order by Yeltsin on the withdrawal of Russian forces from the Kuril Islands. According to the newspaper, in the four South Kuril islands there is at present a machine-gun and artillery division with tanks dug into the earth dating from the Second World War. It is this division that comes under the presidential redeployment order. Air Force subunits have already virtually been withdrawn from the islands.
