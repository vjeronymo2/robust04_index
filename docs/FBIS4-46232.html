BFN [Press communique issued by the Colombian Presidency in Santa Fe de Bogota on 28 May; from the "Quarter of an Hour Caracol" news segment] [Text] Given various news reports that elections have been suspended in some corregimientos, the government hereby informs the public that elections have not been suspended anywhere in the territory. Election preparations are under way throughout the country. [Issued in] Santa Fe de Bogota, Capital District [Dated] 28 May 1994
