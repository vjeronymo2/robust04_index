Notice of Consolidation of Electronic Bulletin Boards Working Groups April 19, 1994. At the informal conference 1 on electronic bulletin boards (EBBs) on April 12, 1994, it was decided to consolidate the five EBB working groups into a single group. The group will be headed by an Interim Steering Committee consisting of representatives of five industry segments. The Interim Committee will serve until the first meeting of the consolidated working group presently scheduled for April 28, 1994, in Houston, Texas at the facilities of Transcontinental Gas Pipeline Company. At that meeting, the group will consider permanent leadership and other issues. 1 See Notice of Proposed Rulemaking in this docket (58 FR 41647, Aug. 5, 1993).
