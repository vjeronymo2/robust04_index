940311 FT 11 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Lynx Holdings Pounds 2m purchase Lynx Holdings, the computer services and leisure products concern, is acquiring Computer Service Technology Group for Pounds 2m in cash and shares. Lynx also intends to seek approval from shareholders to adopt an unapproved share option scheme so as to be able to meet a commitment to Mr Peter Galliott, managing director of Guernsey-based subsidiary, Financial Systems. Computer Service made Pounds 322,000 pre-tax in the year to September 30 on Pounds 6.1m sales. Net assets were Pounds 400,000.
