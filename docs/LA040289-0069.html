April 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE NEIGHBORHOOD

The article "Living Downtown" (by Myrna Oliver, March 22) really caught my attention. But I am angry that the term Latinos was used in such a way as to classify them as "undesirables" for the upper-middle and upper classes to interact with. I'm offended, and I believe an apology to the many Latinos whom you have offended is in order.

And don't be too surprised if some of those residents of that beautiful skyscraper land are Latinos.

VALERIE RAYAS

Gardena
