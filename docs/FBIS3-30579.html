Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Wroclaw, 6 Feb--Subinspector Zbigniew Jarosz, police deputy commandant in Wroclaw Province, told a PAP journalist that President Lech Walesa, who was on vacation in Duszniki Zdroj (Walbrzych province) in the Jodla mountain troops training center, met the Russian Federation Minister of Internal Affairs, General Viktor Fedorovich Yerin, on 6 February. Along with the Russian delegation were Minister of Internal Affairs Andrzej Milczanowski and Chief Inspector Zenon Smolarek, the chief of police. The meeting took place in the parish administration office in Duszniki Zdroj. Minister Yerin, who leads a delegation from the Russian Ministry of Internal Affairs, arrived in Poland on 5 February on an official, working visit.
