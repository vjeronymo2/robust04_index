940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Asda buys site from Tiphook and Storey Tiphook, the trailer leasing company, and Thos Storey (Engineers) have sold the Tiviot Works site in Stockport, Cheshire, to Asda Stores which plans to develop it as a retail scheme. The contract is conditional on the necessary planning consents being granted and completion of the deal is not expected until next year. Tiphook's share of the net consideration will be about Pounds 5.2m.
