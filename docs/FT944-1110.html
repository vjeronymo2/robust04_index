941222 FT 22 DEC 94 / London Life fined Pounds 50,000 by Imro London Life Association, part of London Life Group, has been fined Pounds 50,000 by Imro, the self-regulating body for the fund management industry, for rule breaches that put client monies at risk, Norma Cohen writes. Imro said no losses had occurred to clients of the company as a result. London Life had, among other breaches, wrongly informed the independent custodian for client assets that it was the beneficial owner of securities that were owned by clients.
