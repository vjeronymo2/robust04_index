BFN [Text] At present Pavel Grachev, Russian defense minister, does not see prospects for setting up joint armed forces for the Commonwealth republics. According to the minister, such forces can be set up only if CIS states form a closer political and economic union. In addition, Defense Minister Pavel Grachev had a meeting with Butrus Butrus-Ghali, UN secretary-general, today and said after the meeting that Russian peace-keeping forces would be able to carry out their operations in the CIS under the UN aegis. According to the minister, Butrus-Ghali supported this position wholeheartedly.
