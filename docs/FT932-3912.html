930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Martin Shelton shares up on 35% advance Shares in Martin Shelton Group yesterday rose 17p to 53p after the USM-quoted company reported a 35 per cent rise in pre-tax profits from Pounds 302,000 to Pounds 407,000. The company, which specialises in business calendars, diaries and gifts, increased turnover to Pounds 5.02m (Pounds 4.85m). Mr Paul Martin, chairman, said record turnover in betting office supplies had made a significant contribution to group profits. The final dividend is lifted 50 per cent to 1.5p, making a total of 2.25p (1.75p) on earnings per share of 5.73p (4.34p).
