941123 FT 23 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Shires Investment Shires Investment's net asset value per share slipped to 260.4p at September 30 against 292.2p at the March 31 year-end. Fully diluted, the fall was from 284.4p to 256p. Net attributable revenue for the six months improved to Pounds 2.3m (Pounds 2.24m) for earnings per share of 8.42p (8.81p). A second interim dividend of 4.2p makes an unchanged 8.3p so far.
