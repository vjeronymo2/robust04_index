BFN [Text] Mr. He Zhiqiang, governor of Yunnan Province, People's Republic of China, and delegation called on Lieutenant General Thein Win, minister of transport, at the latter's office on Merchant Street at 0900 this morning. At the meeting cordial and frank talks were held on improving bilateral economic cooperation and transportation matters such as Bhamo jetty development, the integrated use of Irrawaddy River, upgrading of ports in Yangon [Rangoon], construction of Kyaukpyu deep sea port, construction of international airports, upgrading of domestic airports, improving bilateral air links, and promotion of tourism. Similarly, the party called on Lt. Gen. Maung Thint, minister of progress of border areas and national races and development affairs; Colonel Aung San, deputy minister of construction; Lt. Gen. Sein Aung, minister of industry-1; and U Khin Maung Thein, minister of energy, at their respective offices at 1030, 1130, 1430, and 1530 respectively. Present at the meetings were responsible personnel from the departments and enterprises of the various ministries concerned. They held talks on improving bilateral economic cooperation. Members of the economic cooperation mission accompanying the Yunnan provincial governor and delegation, also held talks with officials at the Trade Ministry and the Public Works in the morning and the Myanmar [Burma] Heavy Industries at 1400 respectively. In the evening, Lt. Gen. Thein Win hosted a dinner in honor of the visiting delegation at Oasis Restaurant at 1900. The dinner was attended by Lt. Gen. Khin Nyunt, secretary-1 of the State Law and Order Restoration Council; Brigadier General Abel, minister of national planning and economic development; Brig. Gen. Myo Thant, minister of information; U San Wai, deputy minister of transport; and directors general and managing directors from departments and enterprises under the ministry of transport.
