BFN [Text] Beijing, April 7 (XINHUA) -- Premier Li Peng told visiting Mongolian Minister of External Relations Tserenpiliyn Gombosuren that he is looking forward to his upcoming official visit to the State of Mongolia in late April. Gombosuren, who is here to make preparations for Li's visit, described Li's Mongolian tour as "an important event" in Mongolia- China relations and believed that the visit will be a success. Li, expressing satisfaction at the smooth growth of Sino- Mongolian relations in the past few years, reaffirmed that the Chinese Government attaches importance to promoting good-neighborly Sino-Mongolian ties and hoped that the common border between China and Mongolia remains a border of peace and friendship. Li described the changes that have taken place in Mongolia in recent years as resulting from the Mongolian people's choice and stressed that the Chinese Government respects such choice and hopes that Mongolia enjoys economic development and its people happiness. Gombosuren assured Li that to consolidate and develop Mongolia- China ties is an important part of Mongolia's foreign policy. He expressed admiration at the successes China has made in its reform and opening drive, saying that a stable and rapidly- developing China is very important to Mongolia.
