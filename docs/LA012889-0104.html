January 28, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

THE COLLEGES;

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

DEVRIES LEADS PACIFIC CHRISTIAN TO 131-64 VICTORY

Mike DeVries scored 27 points and made 5 of 6 3-pointers to lead Pacific Christian College to a 131-64 victory over Simpson College Friday in a California Costal Conference men's basketball game at Fullerton.

Brian Keiser and Steve Heard each added 19 and Doug Wood 18 for PCC (17-8, 5-2).

In nonconference wrestling:

Cal State Fullerton 33, UC Davis 12 -- The Titans won 7 of 10 matches. In the 118-pound bout, Fullerton's Erin Millsap pinned Doug Koinman at 2 minutes 3 seconds.

In BYU women's tennis tournament:

Colorado 6, UC Irvine 3 -- Colorado won 3 of 4 doubles sets. Courtney Weichsel defeated Karen Myers, 7-6, 6-3, in singles for Irvine (1-2).
