BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Apr (MENA)--Mahmud 'Abbas, alias Abu-Mazin, a PLO Executive Committee member, arrived in Cairo this morning. He met General Amnon Shahaq, Israeli deputy chief of staff and head of the Israeli delegation to the Palestinian-Israeli negotiations. Palestinian sources said the meeting aims to bridge the gap between the two sides' views concerning various points that are still the subject of dispute, particularly the legal jurisdiction in Gaza and Jericho and the control of the crossings. Abu-Mazin earlier met members of the Palestinian negotiating team. The talks during the meeting dealt with progress and the efforts required to coordinate the Palestinian stances on the proposals to be presented during this round of negotiations.
