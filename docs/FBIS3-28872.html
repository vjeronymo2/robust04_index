Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 11 (KCNA) -- The January 31, 1994, statement of the DPRK Foreign Ministry spokesman denouncing the United States for recently reversing the points agreed upon between the DPRK and the United States over a peaceful solution of the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula was distributed as official document of the UN Security Council S/1994/105 on February 1.
