Language: Russian Article Type:BFN ["Edict No. 238 of the Russian Federation President on Additional Guarantees of the Rights of Russian Federation Citizens To Participate in the Administration of State Affairs," signed by Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin in the Kremlim in Moscow on 4 February 1994] [Text] With the aim of creating additional guarantees for representative people's power and fuller realization of the rights of Russian citizens to participate in the administration of state affairs through elections to the organs of Russian Federation state power, I decree: 1. That the words "for a period of no more than 12 weeks" are to be deleted from Article 20 Point 7 of the Statute on Elections of Deputies to the Russian Federation Federal Assembly Federation Council in 1993, ratified by edict No. 1626 of the Russian Federation president of 11 October 1993 "On Elections to the Russian Federation Federal Assembly Federation Council" (Collected Acts of the Russian Federation President and Government 1993 No. 42, page 3994; No. 45, page 4333). 2. That the words "for 12 weeks" are to be deleted from Article 24 point 6 of the Statute on the Election of State Duma Deputies in 1993, ratified by edict No. 1557 of the Russian Federation president of 1 October 1993 "On the Ratification of the Edited Version of the Statute on the Election of State Duma Deputies in 1993 and on Amendments and Additions to the Statute on the Federal Organs of Power in the Transitional Period" (Collected Acts of the Russian Federation President and Government 1993, No. 41, page 3907; No. 45, page 4333). 3. In the event of new or additional elections being held in individual electoral districts for election to the Soviet Federation or to the Federal Assembly State Duma the date of the elections is to be set independently by the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission. [Signed] Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin [Dated] 4 February 1994 Moscow, the Kremlin No. 238
