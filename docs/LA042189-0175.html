April 21, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

EARNINGS

National Heath Care Systems of Irvine posted a modest profit for 1988 after a loss the year before. Net income was $57,277, contrasted with a loss of $212,367 for 1987. The company's revenue rose 28% to $5.1 million from $4 million. "The company's dental plan membership has approximately doubled from the prior fiscal year to 156,000 members throughout the state of California," said Dr. Carl E. Bozzo, chairman and chief executive. The company operates a prepaid dental plan called DentiCare that has about 156,000 members throughout California.
