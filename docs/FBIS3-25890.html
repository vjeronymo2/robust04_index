Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 13 (XINHUA) -- On behalf of the Chinese Supreme People's Court President Ren Jianxin, Gao Changli, vice-president of the court, met with and hosted a banquet here this evening in honor of Dr. Jorge Angel Marabotto Lugaro, president of the Supreme Court of Uruguay. At the invitation of the Chinese Supreme People's Court, the four-member Uruguayan delegation arrived here January 12. They are also scheduled to visit Nanjing and Shanghai.
