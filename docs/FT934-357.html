931229 FT 29 DEC 93 / Seoul cool on North Korea bomb reports South Korean President Kim Young-sam yesterday played down reports that North Korea had probably developed nuclear bombs, Reuter reports from Seoul. 'North Korea has strong intentions to develop nuclear weapons. But (I) cannot say North Korea possesses any nuclear arms at the moment,' Yonhap news agency quoted Mr Kim as saying. On Sunday, the New York Times said the US Central Intelligence Agency had advised President Bill Clinton that North Korea had probably built one or two nuclear bombs.
