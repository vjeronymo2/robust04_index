Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 30 (XINHUA) -- Qiao Shi, chairman of China's National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, met with C.H. Llewellyn Smith, director of the European Organization of Nuclear Research (EONR), and Professor C.C. Ting, Nobel prize winner, here today. The NPC chairman told them that he hoped that the research work of EONR will continue to make great achievements. Llewellyn Smith briefed Qiao on EONR's research and training work. He expressed appreciation of the cooperation between EONR and Chinese scientists, hoping that such cooperation will be further enhanced. Llewellyn Smith and his party arrived here on March 27 as guests of Song Jian, state councillor and minister in charge of the State Science and Technology Commission, and Zhou Guangzhao, president of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. During their visit, Llewellyn Smith and his party will inspect some research institutions, and hold talks with their Chinese counterparts on future exchanges and cooperation in high-energy physics.
