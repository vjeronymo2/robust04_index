November 27, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Stop the Garbage: Rock singer Henry Lee Summer is declaring war against the mayor of his hometown of Brazil, Ind. Summer says that after visiting area landfills he decided to join the fight against out-of-state trash being dumped in Indiana. Summer says he has written Gov. Evan Bayh volunteering his help "to stop New York's garbage from becoming Indiana's biggest cash crop." CLAUDIA PUIG
