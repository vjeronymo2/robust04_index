September 20, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

BH JAMBABY AFTER HER SIXTH WIN

The Black Hills Ranch's B H Jambaby will be looking for her fifth career win Friday night at Los Alamitos as she tackles Colross Racing Inc. and Michael Schwartz's Kitty Spirit and seven others in the $25,000 California Platinum Series Final for pacing fillies and mares.

In post position order, the field for the $25,000 Friday night main event, which is carded as the eighth race: Cheechita Princess, Joe Anderson in the race bike; Cappuccine, Robert Sleeth; Black On Black, Robert Rosen; Kitty Spirit, Ross Croghan; La Boulangere, Todd Ratchford; Bread To Shine, Rick Plano; Equus Ronni, Ed Hensley; B H Jambaby, Rick Kuebler, and Miss Boom Boom.

Also on the Friday night card is a $20,000 distaff pacing Free For All. The Gusty Stable's Doodlin will be gunning for her third straight victory.
