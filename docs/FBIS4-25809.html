BFN [Text] During the last day of his stay in Beijing, President Alberto Fujimori firmly defended respect for human rights but expressed his opposition to the interference of great powers in domestic affairs. Fujimori described as fruitful his meetings with his Chinese colleague Jiang Zemin, and Chinese Premier Li Peng. Fujimori noted the progress made in the fight against terrorism, a scourge which in the past 13 years has killed at least 22,000 Peruvians.
