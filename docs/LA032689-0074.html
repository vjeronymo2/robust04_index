March 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

EDUCATIONAL TOURING

Every year about this time the Mature Traveler column tells us what community tours offers for the summer in the way of overseas professor-led tours. So far I have been on four. They are wonderful tours and I plan on my fifth this summer -- the one to the Soviet Union. We have great socials and lectures before we leave and those reunions after the tours are really a lot of fun. I have made many friends from all parts of the United States through these trips and I thank you for the information.
