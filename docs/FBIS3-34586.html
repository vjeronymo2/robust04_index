Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Political Forces Dialogue Committee held a meeting in Sanaa today. The representatives of the parties to the dialogue committee took part in the meeting. The conferees resumed discussions and deliberations on one of the topics of agenda related to the implementation guarantees. A number of the committee members presented their opinions, proposals, and views that would produce the appropriate formula to achieve national consensus and to define the real bases of the guarantees of implementing the accord document following its signing in the Jordanian capital, Amman. Following a length discussion, the committee members agreed to refer the various views and formulas presented by the representatives of the parties to the political dialogue to a mini-committee. This committee will review them as a first step in preparing a unified formula to (?endorse it) within the required guarantees in order to implement the document and to present it to the expanded meeting which the committee will hold on 10 February in order to endorse it and to discuss the rest of the topics on the committee's agenda.
