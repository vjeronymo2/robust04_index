April 16, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

WRONG PUBLISHER

I am very pleased with John Allyn's positive review of my anthology, "The Best Japanese Science Fiction Stories" (Book Review, March 26); however, I found two errors worth mentioning: First, the book was not published by W. W. Norton. It was published by Dembner Books, a small New York company, and distributed by Norton. Second, a quote in the final sentence contains a typo that garbles the meaning of the sentence. The quote should read: "These seem like things scarcely to be believed, but they all happened." Not "These men like things. . . ."

JOHN L. APOSTOLOU

LOS ANGELES
