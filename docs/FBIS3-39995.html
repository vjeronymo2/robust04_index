Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The National Human Rights Commission has assured the international community that it will further strengthen and propagate the culture of human rights in the country and function with maximum transparency. Addressing the UN Human Rights Commission in Geneva, one of its members, Mr. Justice Virendra Dayal, said that the Commission is enjoying statutory autonomy and its functions cover all violations of human rights mentioned in the Vienna Document. He told the delegates and the nongovernment organizations that the Commission will make extensive visits to various states beginning with Jammu and Kashmir, Punjab, Assam, and other northeastern states to assess the human rights condition. Mr. Dayal said the Commission is in touch with the armed forces and police in matters relating to protection and promotion of human rights and training of their personnel.
