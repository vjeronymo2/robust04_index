931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / UK Company News: JLI expands with herb purchase JLI Group, the food processor, has expanded its ingredients division with the acquisition of Frozen Herbs. Consideration comprises an initial Pounds 1.75m cash and a further profit-related amount, also in cash, to a maximum total of Pounds 3m. Suffolk-based Frozen Herbs supplies leading UK food manufacturers with a range of fresh frozen herbs. In the 12 months to September 30 1992 it made pre-tax profits, before exceptional management charges, of Pounds 459,000 on turnover of Pounds 1.84m. Net assets at that date amounted to Pounds 193,000.
