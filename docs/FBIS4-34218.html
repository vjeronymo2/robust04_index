BFN [Report on interview with Dominican Vice President Carlos Morales Troncoso by unidentified reporter in Cartagena de Indias on 16 June -- recorded] [Text] [Reporter] Amid complications, restrictions, and endless competition with the local media, we were able to approach Dominican Vice President Carlos Morales, and ask him about the Cuban [as heard] situation. [Morales] What is not being said is that we, Dominicans, are the hardest hit by the Haitian situation. As a result of the continuing Haitian problem, our country has over 1 million illegal Haitian residents, which constitutes an extremely heavy burden. We support a prompt, but democratic solution.
