941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Lowland Investment The net asset value per share of Lowland Investment Company was virtually unchanged over the 12 months to September 30 at 282.3p, against 282.4p a year earlier. Net revenue was also static at Pounds 2.21m (Pounds 2.22m). Earnings came out at 9.4p (9.45p) and the proposed final dividend of 6p (5.8p) makes 9.3p (9p) for the year. The primary objective of the trust, which is managed by Henderson Touche Remnant, is to give shareholders a higher than average income return of both income and capital over the medium to long term.
