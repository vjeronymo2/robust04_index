BFN [Text] Belgrade, April 13 (AFP) -- Bosnian Serbs have agreed to a ceasefire around the Moslem enclave of Gorazde, Russian special envoy Vitaliy Churkin said Wednesday [13 April]. According to the Bosnian Serb SRNA news agency, Churkin said after a meeting with Bosnian Serb leader Radovan Karadzic at his Pale headquarters that the "accord should help lead to a general ceasefire between Moslems and Serbs."
