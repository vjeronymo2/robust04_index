May 15, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Salomon Bros. said it was selected by the Gannett Foundation to handle the proposed sale of the 10% stake it owns in Gannett Co. . . . The number of working U.S. oil and natural gas rigs rose for the second straight week, increasing by 11 to total 941, Baker Hughes Inc. reported. A year ago, the rig count was 771.
