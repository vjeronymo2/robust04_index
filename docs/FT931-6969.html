930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Smaller Cos Trust assets edge ahead to 84.07p Net asset value of the Smaller Companies Investment Trust edged ahead from 82.86p to 84.07p over the 12 months to December 31. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 478,000 (Pounds 405,000) for earnings of 3.19p (2.7p) per share. A recommended final dividend of 1.4p brings the total for the year to 2.6p (2.5p).
