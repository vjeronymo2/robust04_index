941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / International Company News: Klockner back in profit with DM5m for year Klockner Werke, the German industrial group which shed its steel interests last year, yesterday said it was back in profit. The group, now centred on its car parts division and bottling plants, made a DM5m (Dollars 3m) profit following a DM196m loss last year, according to preliminary figures for the year to September. Final results will be available in February. Operating profit rose to DM106m, compared with a loss of DM324m last year, while turnover fell to DM4.1bn from DM6.1bn following the sale of the group's steel works. The company said it would make 'further significant improvements' next year.
