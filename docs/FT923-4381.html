920907 FT 07 SEP 92 / International Company News: Whitbread in cash disposal Whitbread has sold its Pizza Hut business in Belgium and the Netherlands to Pizza Hut International, its joint venture partner, for a cash consideration. Included in the sale were 21 outlets which were currently trading and two sites in the Netherlands which were near to completion. The value of the net assets disposed of was less than 5 per cent of Whitbread's net assets at February 29.
