Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Text] Hwang Chang-yop, Secretariat member of the Workers Party of Korea [WPK], and his delegation left Vientiane for home at noon on 13 March after ending a four-day visit to Laos. Hwang Chang-yop and the delegation were seen off at Wattai Airport by Thongsing Thammavong, Political Bureau member and head of the Organizing Committee of the Lao People's Revolutionary Party [LPRP] Central Committee. Also on hand to see the delegation off at the airport was Chang Yong-chun, ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary of the DPRK to Laos, along with many high-ranking cadres. During the visit to Laos, the WPK delegation led by Hwang Chang-yop, Secretariat member of the WPK Central Committee, paid a courtesy call on Khamtai Siphandon, chairman of the LPRP Central Committee and prime minister, and met with Thongsing Thammavong, Political Bureau member and head of the Organizing Committee of the party Central Committee. It also visited the high-level party and state political training school, the construction site of the Lao-Thai friendship bridge, and the party unit of the Nam Ngeum hydroelectric dam.
