June 19, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

CARDINAL'S POWER REPLACES MCGEE

The St. Louis Cardinals today reinstated right-handed pitcher Ted Power to replace outfielder Willie McGee, who was retroactively placed on the 21-day disabled list with a sprained left wrist.

The Cardinals also announced that team doctor Stan London put McGee's wrist in a cast Tuesday. London said the cast would help immobilize the wrist.

McGee last appeared in a game June 6. His placement on the disabled list is retroactive to June 7.

Power, 34, comes off the 21-day disabled list. He suffered a pulled left rib-cage muscle May 17 at Houston.
