920630 FT 30 JUN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Fair general demand, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Landed bright East Africans were again well supported but the better mediums generally met a more selective enquiry and tended easier. Plain varieties also sold at lower rates. Good liquoring Central African teas remained fully firm but others proved irregularly easier. Offshore selective demand with brighter descriptions about firm, but the balance mostly easier where sold. The highest price realised this week was 220p for a kemnya pd. Quoations: quality 165p, nom good medium 135p, medium 106p, and low medium 80p.
