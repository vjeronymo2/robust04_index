940118 FT 18 JAN 94 / Thorp licence in operation The licence for the Pounds 2.8bn Thorp nuclear reprocessing plant at Sellafield, Cumbria, became effective yesterday. The plant, owned by British Nuclear Fuels, is not scheduled to become fully radioactive until the High Court has heard a challenge to it next month by Greenpeace, the environment pressure group, and Lancashire County Council.
