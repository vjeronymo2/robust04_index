July 27, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

OPENING OF LIBRARY-MUSEUM RETURNS NIXON TO LIMELIGHT

Worse than burning the flag -- worse than parading Willie Horton -- worse than the Iran-Contra guilt.

Worse than all these is the desecration of all the ideals our country is supposed to stand for was the obscene spectacle of three ex-Presidents honoring a man who escaped impeachment by resigning.

At this celebration, where were all the former jailbirds from his co-conspirators who were found guilty of crimes lesser than his?

What greater shame can our ex-presidents Bush, Reagan and Ford bring upon American history? This was a day that exceeds all in infamy.

GLENN L. ANDERSON

Topanga
