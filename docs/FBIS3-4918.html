Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Gunfiring at Frontline" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, March 2 (KCNA) -- The South Korean puppets on February 28 committed a provocative gunfiring in an area near the Demilitarized Zone (DMZ), the frontline area, getting on the nerves of the North, according to military sources. They fired rifle bullets and shells into areas adjacent to the DMZ from Chorwon county, South Korean Kangwon Province and Paju and Yonchon counties, Kyonggi Province. In another development, they staged ground strike, ground attack support and airlifting exercises against the northern half of Korea in the skies above Kosong County, South Korean Kangwon Province, and Yonchon, Paju and Hwachon of Kyonggi Province with the mobilisation of formations of fighter planes, helicopter gunships and transport helicopters.
