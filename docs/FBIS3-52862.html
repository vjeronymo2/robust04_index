Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A U.S. Congressional delegation gathering information on Israeli soldiers who went missing in Lebanon 12 years ago arrived in al-Biqa' today. Senior Lebanese Army intelligence officers and a high-ranking U.S. diplomat received the delegation at al-Masna' on the Lebanese-Syrian border. The delegation began its mission with a visit to 'Ayta al-Fakhkhar, where it met the mayor and the parish priest. The delegation visited the town's cemetery and took a sample of bones. At 1315, the delegation left for al-Sultan Ya'qub for a meeting with some of the town's inhabitants. The delegation seeks to gather information about the fate of missing Israeli soldiers in Lebanon. Hizballah's Consultative Council member Nabil Qawuq today held a news conference in which he called for the release of all the Lebanese, Palestinian, and Arab detainees in Israel in return for the release of an Israeli soldier held by Hizballah. He reiterated Hizballah's refusal to negotiate with the U.S. Congressional delegation.
