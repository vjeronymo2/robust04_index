Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council, received in Aden this evening visiting Indonesian Foreign Minister Ali Alatas. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid welcomed his brother the Indonesian foreign minister and his delegation, wishing them a pleasant stay in their second homeland, Yemen. He underlined the longstanding historical and cultural ties between the two countries, affirming our country's eagerness to promote cooperation with Indonesia. [passage omitted] Also, Prime Minister Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas received this evening in the commercial and economic capital, Aden, the visiting Indonesian Foreign Minister Ali Alatas.
