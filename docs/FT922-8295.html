920514 FT 14 MAY 92 / World News in Brief: Heseltine doctrine Michael Heseltine (left) signalled that his move to the Department of Trade and Industry would not lead to any immediate change in the government's approach to monopolies and mergers. In a parliamentary written answer, he reaffirmed the so-called Tebbit doctrine which establishes the impact on competition as the main criterion on which proposed mergers should be referred to the monopolies commission.
