920422 FT 22 APR 92 / International Company News: BellSouth rises to Dollars 460.9m BELLSOUTH, one of the seven US 'baby bell' telecommunications operators, has registered a first-quarter rise in net income, Reuter reports. BellSouth earned Dollars 460.9m, or Dollars 0.94 a share, on revenues of Dollars 3.74bn, compared with net income of Dollars 366.9m, or Dollars 0.76, in the first quarter of 1991. Revenues in the 1991 quarter were Dollars 3.54bn.
