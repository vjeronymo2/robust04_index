940926 FT 26 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Pantheon Investment doubles net assets Pantheon International Participations reported an increased diluted net asset value per share of 239.6p at the end of 12 months to June 30, up from 195.5p. Net revenue fell 17 per cent from Pounds 169,000 to Pounds 140,000. Earnings per share were down from 1.16p to 0.88p and the single final dividend is maintained at 0.5p.
