930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / Institutions invest less in property INSTITUTIONAL investment in UK commercial property totalled Pounds 937m last year, less than half the previous year's Pounds 1.95bn, according to the Central Statistical Office. In the last quarter of the year insurance companies and pension funds were Pounds 77m net sellers of property. That compares with net investment of more than Pounds 300m in the previous three quarters.
