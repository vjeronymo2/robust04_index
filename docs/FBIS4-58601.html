BFN [Text] Sanaa, 4 May (MENA) -- A military source at the Ministry of Defense in Sanaa has stated that President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih has issued orders to the Defense Ministry and the General Staff to stop the fighting that broke out earlier in Dhamar, south of Sanaa. He also asked the commander of the Republican Guard Battalion, northern, to halt the fighting unilaterally. The source accused the Basahib Camp forces, southern, of starting the fighting this evening and charged that influential forces in the Yemeni Socialist Party are embarked on a plan to divide Yemen. The source accused the same parties of attacking the Central Security personnel in Aden this evening and encircling and shelling their camps with tank artillery.
