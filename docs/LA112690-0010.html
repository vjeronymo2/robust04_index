November 26, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MAYOR BRADLEY'S APPOINTEES AND FUND-RAISING

Once again, there is a scandal brewing in Mayor Bradley's neighborhood.

What will the good mayor do this time? Will there be another person who has to resign his position because he was unduly influenced by the good mayor and should have known better?

Will there be another new "ethics" law introduced for the city by the good mayor?

Will he show us that politics is really a sleazy profession and ask for the public's understanding?

The truth is that Bradley can ask for anything but my vote. That is one thing that he will never get again.

HARVEY J. GLASS, Sherman Oaks
