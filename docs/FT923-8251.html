920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / French dockers step up action The threat of a summer of discontent looms in France with dockers calling a nation-wide dock strike today and disgruntled farmers renewing protests against EC agricultural reforms, Alice Rawsthorn reports from Paris. The dock strike will be the second national stoppage at France's ports this week. Farmers yesterday occupied the Agriculture Ministry's office at Auch in the Gers region seeking the right to put their case to Mr Pierre Beregovoy, French prime minister.
