Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The meeting that had been scheduled for 20 March in Formosa, Argentina, among Paraguayan President Juan Carlos Wasmosy, Argentine President Carlos Menem, and Bolivian President Gonzalo Sanchez de Lozada has been postponed. The decision was prompted by the fact that Sanchez de Lozada could not attend the meeting, because he is scheduled to meet in La Paz with U.S. Vice President Al Gore on that day. The three presidents had been scheduled to meet on 20 March to discuss the Pilcomayo River issue. Yet the above problem has forced a postponement of the "minisummit" to a later date. During Sanchez de Lozada's recent visit to Asuncion, Bolivian Foreign Minister Antonio Aranibar Quiroga noted that the Formosa presidential summit might be postponed. It was confirmed yesterday at Lopez Palace that the meeting has in fact been postponed.
