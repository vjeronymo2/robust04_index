910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / Observer: Polished off Meanwhile the name Mitsui is to disappear from Japanese banking, 308 years after the Mitsui family opened its first exchange house. Mitsui Taiyo Kobe Bank, formed last year from a merger of the Mitsui and Taiyo Kobe institutions, is to retitle itself Sakura - or Cherry Blossom - Bank.
