BFN [Excerpt] Vienna, April 30 (AFP) -- North Korea has rejected conditions set by the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA] for new inspections of its nuclear sites, the IAEA said Friday [29 April]. "The new reply from Pyongyang to our demands, which arrived in Vienna Friday afternoon, is unsatisfactory," said IAEA spokesman Hans-Friedrich Meyer, adding that inspectors would not therefore travel to North Korea this weekend. "We will discuss the situation again with the member states at the start of next week," he said. [passage omitted]
