BFN [Report by 'Abdallah Hammudah and Lutfi Shatarah: "Sanaa Appoints New Ambassador to Poland To Facilitate Conclusion of Arms Deals"] [Excerpts] London -- The new Yemeni ambassador, Sharaf al-Sa'idi, who was an under secretary at the Foreign Ministry, Aden branch, arrived in the Polish capital, Warsaw yesterday to assume his duties there. Charge D'Affaires Muhammad Sharaf has been withdrawn because of his objection to an arms deal the Sanaa leadership had intended to conclude with the two companies Senzen and Sezbex [as transliterated] one month before the war broke out in Yemen. [passage omitted] Well-informed sources say that the most important task assigned to him [the new ambassador] is to facilitate the Military Economic Establishment's task of concluding arms deals with Warsaw.
