May 18, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

SPOTLIGHT / COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE: FOCUS: STATE OF THE ART HEADQUARTERS;

STATE OF THE ART INC., AN ACCOUNTING SOFTWARE COMPANY, IS MOVING ITS CORPORATE HEADQUARTERS FROM A 23,000-SQUARE-FOOT FACILITY IN COSTA MESA TO A 71,000-SQUARE-FOOT BUILDING IN THE IRVINE SPECTRUM. THE COMPANY, WHICH HAS BEEN IN BUSINESS SINCE 1981 AND HAS 135 EMPLOYEES, EXPECTS TO HAVE THE MOVE COMPLETED BY JUNE 4.
