941123 FT 23 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Queensborough Queensborough Holdings, the leisure and hotels group, returned to the black with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 50,000 for the six months to July 31. This compared with an interim loss of Pounds 278,000 last time and a Pounds 424,000 loss at the year-end. The USM-traded company, which changed its name from Flagstone Holdings earlier this month, had turnover of Pounds 1.26m (Pounds 250,000). Mr Kevin Leech, who was appointed executive chairman in August, said the company was applying to return to a full listing, and that its 'primary objective' was to declare a dividend as soon as circumstances permitted. Earnings per share were 0.01p (0.2p losses).
