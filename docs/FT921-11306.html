920201 FT 01 FEB 92 / UK Company News: 52% setback for Wholesale Fittings In the half year ended October 25 1991 Wholesale Fittings limited its reduction in sales to 7 per cent. But pre-tax profit declined by 52 per cent. Turnover was Pounds 29.9m (Pounds 32.4m) and profit Pounds 1.21m (Pounds 2.5m). Earnings per share halved to 5.6p (11.5p) but the interim is again 3.23p. In the opening months of the current year turnover showed a slight increase over last year. The group trades from 50 locations.
