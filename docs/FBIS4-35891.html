BFN [Text] Leader President Saddam Husayn has received a cable of thanks from his brother King Husayn Bin-Talal of Jordan in reply to the cable of congratulations that his excellency sent on Jordan's Independence Day. Following is the text: His Excellency brother Saddam Husayn, Republic of Iraq President and Revolution Command Council chairman, may God protect you: On my own behalf, and on behalf of the Jordanian people and Government, I thank you very much for your kind greetings and good wishes that you sent to us on Independence Day. Wishing you continued good health and happiness and your fraternal people all goodness and progress. [Signed] Your brother, Husayn
