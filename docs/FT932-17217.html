930401 FT 01 APR 93 / London Stock Exchange: Drug stocks firm Pharmaceuticals stocks received a much needed lift after a US newspaper reported that one of President Clinton's senior health aides had 'expressed distaste' at price curbs on drugs in the US. However, some marketmakers suggested there might also have been some 'window dressing' to lift prices on the last day of the first quarter. Hard-hit Wellcome recovered 23 to 762p, while Glaxo was lifted 18 to 606p. SmithKline Beecham, which has been talking to analysts in Philadelphia, gained 15 at 412p in the 'A's and 11 at 359p in the Units. The group announced that it expected to acquire North American marketing and distribution rights to a treatment for testosterone deficiencies.
