BFN [Text] Cairo, 29 May (MENA)--President Muhammad Husni Mubarak has issued a decision forming the National Dialogue Drafting Committee as follows: Mustafa Khalil shall be the committee's general rapporteur. The National Democratic Party [NDP] shall be represented by Dr. Yusuf Wali, deputy prime minister and minister of agriculture and land reclamation and NDP secretary general; Muhammad Safwat al-Sharif, minister of information; and Kamal al-Shadhili, minister of state for People's Assembly and Consultative Council affairs. The following parties shall be represented by: The Wafd Party by Fu'ad Siraj al-Din; the National Progressive Unionist Grouping by Khalid Muhyi al-Din; the Labor Party by Engineer Ibrahim Shukri; the Liberal Party by Mustafa Kamil Murad; the Nasirite Arab Democratic Party by Diya' al-Din Dawud; Egypt's Arab Socialist Party by Jamal Rabi'i; the Social Justice Party by Dr. Muhammad 'Abd-al-'Al; the Ummah Party by Ahmad al-Sabbahi; the Greens Party by Kamal Kirah; and the Democratic Union Party by Ibrahim 'Abd-al-Mun'im Turk. The following people shall also be members of the Drafting Committee: Dr. 'Abd-al-Qadir Hatim, Fikri Makram, Dr. Ahmad Muharram, Kamal Henri Abadir, Dr. Hamid al-Sa'ih, Sayyid Rashid, Tharwat Abazah, Hilmi Nammar, Sayyid Zaki 'Abd-al-Hadi, Dr. Muhammad Raja'i al-Tahhawi, Dr. Ramzi al-Sha'ir, Dr. Mufid Shihab, Ibrahim Nafi', Dr. Hamdi al-Sayyid, Engineer Faruq 'Afifi, Ahmad al- Khawajah, Dr. 'Abd-al-'Azim Ramadan, Dr. Farkhundah Hasan, Dr. Fawziyah 'Abd-al-Sattar, Dr. Aminah al-Jindi, Dr. Nabih al-'Alqamani, Counselor 'Abd-al-Rahman Faraj Muhsin, Muhammad Idris, Abu-Salih al-Alfi, Muhammad Farid Khamis, and Dr. Ibrahim Kamil Abu- al-'Aynayn. Dr. Mustafa Khalil has invited the members for a meeting on Wednesday morning.
