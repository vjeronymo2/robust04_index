940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / International Company News: Nova seeks to raise CDollars 500m Nova, the western Canada energy and petrochemicals group, is raising CDollars 500m (USDollars 381.7m) in new equity to finance domestic pipeline expansion and its international gas service business, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. The new equity issue is priced at CDollars 9.55 a share, against a price of CDollars 10.25. Nova shares closed in the market on Wednesday at CDollars 10.25. The company is spending Dollars 600m this year on its Alberta gas transmission system.
