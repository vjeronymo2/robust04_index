Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Robert Lamb, the U.S. State Department's special coordinator on Cyprus, had a new meeting with President Glavkos Kliridhis this afternoon. The meeting was attended by Finance Minister Faidhros Oikonomidhis, who was acting foreign minister, and U.S. Ambassador Richard Boucher. Speaking afterward, Oikonomidhis said that Lamb briefed the president on his meetings in the occupied areas with Turkish Cypriot leader Rauf Denktas. Lamb described his meeting with President Kliridhis as constructive but refrained from responding to correspondents' questions. Lamb will have another meeting with Denktas. [passage omitted]
