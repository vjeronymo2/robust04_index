BFN [Text] The right honorable prime minister, Dr. Ntsu Mokhehle, gave a briefing to members of the diplomatic corps on the situation in the country at the prime minister's offices this morning. The diplomats present were heads of diplomatic missions of the United Kingdom, Ireland, United State, South Africa and Germany. As spokesman for the prime minister's office said this morning that five cabinet ministers were arrested by the army. The deputy prime minister is under house arrest at his home at (Ha Matoela) and four other ministers arrested have been held at the Makoanyane Barracks.
