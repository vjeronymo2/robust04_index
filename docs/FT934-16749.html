931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / Business and the Law: Loans for trainees - Legal briefs National Westminster Bank has agreed to make up to Pounds 100m available over the next five years for a special loan scheme to help trainee solicitors through their vocational course. The deal allows any student with a job waiting following completion of the course to borrow up to Pounds 10,000, repayable within five years of qualification.
