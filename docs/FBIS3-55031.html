Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Valeriy Vinogradov ; from the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] We have just received a statement of the Foreign Ministry of the Russian Federation, which states: The leadership of the Russian Federation has received with profound grief and indignation the report of the brutal murders carried out in recent days in Tajikistan of Russian-speaking citizens -- a deacon of the Orthodox Church, a whole family including five children, and a young woman resident of Dushanbe. We are firmly relying on the fact that the Tajikistan authorities will take the most energetic measures in investigating what has happened and in speedily identifying and suitably punishing the murderers. We also expect that the Government of Tajikistan will do everything necessary to ensure the security and the protection of the rights and dignity of the Russian-speaking citizens of the republic.
