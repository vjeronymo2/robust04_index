Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 25 (XINHUA) -- Chinese President Jiang Zemin met with Lars Ramqvist, president and chief executive officer of the LM Ericsson International AB, [Sweden] and his party here this afternoon. Jiang expressed his appreciation of Ericsson's long-time friendly cooperation with China in the fields of electronics and telecommunications. He voiced the hope that Ericsson would continue to expand its cooperation with Chinese partners in electronics, telecommunications, high-tech and other fields. Wu Jichuan, Chinese minister of posts and telecommunications, was present at the meeting.
