920713 FT 13 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Exceptional profit boosts Peter Cox Helped by an exceptional profit of Pounds 477,000, Peter Cox, the building preservation company, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 904,000 on turnover of Pounds 30.5m in 1991. Mr John Wilson, chairman, said the result was creditable in the economic gloom. The previous period was for 14 months following the purchase by its management from John Mowlem in which pre-tax profits were Pounds 933,000 on turnover of Pounds 34.3m.
