BFN [Text] The next round of Russian-Belarusian talks on the issues of military cooperation has ended in Minsk. According to Veniyamin Popov, head of the Russian delegation, Russia will be given the possibility to use the radio station in Vileyka even after the completion of strategic forces' withdrawal from Belarus. In addition, the construction of a missile warning radar system near Baranovichi will be completed. This facility will also remain on Belarusian territory after the year of 2000 and will operate in the interests of all the countries which are the members to the treaty on collective security.
