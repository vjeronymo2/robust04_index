Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] An official representative of the Russian Foreign Ministry, Grigory Karasin, said that an agreement between the PLO and Israel on continuation of the Mideast peace talks and a final agreement on the Gaza Strip and Jericho had been shaped during the contacts the Russian president envoy Viktor Posuvaluk had with the PLO leadership, the Israeli delegation and U.S. and Norwegian high-ranking diplomats in Tunis. According to Karasin, Posuvaluk had transferred a personal message from Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev to PLO leader Yasir 'Arafat. The message calls for renewal of the talks and confirms the intention of the Russian co-sponsor to contribute to implementation of the UN Security Council's resolution 904. Russia believes that the adoption of this resolution, in particular providing for international participation in ensuring safety for Palestinians living on the occupied territories, creates the necessary atmosphere for a move forward in the Arab-Israeli dialogue, the Russian Foreign Ministry's representative Karasin said.
