930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / Observer: Hogg wash Having bequeathed the chairmanship of Courtaulds Textiles to chief executive Martin Taylor, Sir Christopher Hogg predictably spent yesterday morning on the telephone to his four biggest shareholders arguing that he was not cocking a snook at the Cadbury recommendations. 'Three said fine; one thought it a pity. But that was a Scottish institution, so they are more remote.' Aha.
