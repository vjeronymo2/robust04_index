BFN [Text] The North side's chief of the North-South Liaison Office sent a telephone message to the chief of the South side today [11 April]. The telephone message said that the North side will send two liaison officials to the conference room of the Neutral Nations Supervisory Commission in Panmunjom at 1000 [0100 GMT] on 13 April to hand over to the South side the letter to the South Korean authorities, political parties, organizations, individual figures, and overseas compatriots which was adopted at a joint meeting of the DPRK Government, political parties, and social organizations.
