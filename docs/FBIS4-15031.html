BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sergey Ostanin] [Text] Moscow April 5 TASS -- Black Sea Fleet Commander Admiral Eduard Baltin has been taken to hospital due to Sunday's wound in his right leg's tissue. The wound was caused by "a malfunction of his gun," head of the Black Sea Fleet press center Captain 1st Class Andrey Grachev told ITAR-TASS today over the telephone. In the words of Grachev, "the wound is not dangerous, the commander feels well and will return to his duties in the near future". While Baltin is at hospital to undergo wound treatment and annual medical examination, his first deputy Vice-Admiral Vitaliy Larionov is temporary in charge of the Black Sea Fleet.
