930407 FT 07 APR 93 / UK Company News: P&O sells retail park for Pounds 9.3m P&O Properties has sold a retail park in Aberdeen to Legal & General Property, the life fund, for Pounds 9.3m. The 133,754 sq ft Kittybrewster Retail Park, built in 1990, is let to retailers including Halfords, Dixons, Comet and Great Mills. The net initial purchasing yield is 9.94 per cent, while the equivalent yield is 10.6 per cent. Legal & General said the deal was in line with its policy of increasing the retail warehousing element of its life fund.
