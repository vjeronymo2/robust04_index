BFN [Text] The Brest Oblast Council of people's deputies has adopted a resolution on the restoration of a border zone along the boundary with Poland. In Brest, Kamenets, and Pruzhany Rayons adjacent to the border, special rules of entry into the zone, as well as of residence and temporary visits, have been introduced. Permanent residents of these rayons should have a special notation in their passports, while newcomers -- a permit to enter a specific populated area. This resolution was provoked by the need to halt smuggling and illegal border crossings.
