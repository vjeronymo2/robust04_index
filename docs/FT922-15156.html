920402 FT 02 APR 92 / Youth Training A SURVEY showing that more than 50,000 young people were waiting for Youth Training places at the end of 1991 has been 'shelved' by the government, according to the Unemployment Unit, a research group that campaigns for the unemployed. The Unemployment Unit said the survey, conducted by the Local Authority Careers Services for the Department of Employment, undermined government pledges on training for school leavers.
