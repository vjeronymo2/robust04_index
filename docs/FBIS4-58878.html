BFN [Text] Cairo, 8 May (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak received a message from Tunisian President Zine el-Abidine Ben Ali during an audience today with Habib Ben Yahya, Tunisian foreign minister and the Tunisian president's personal envoy, who is currently in Cairo. The Tunisian envoy said after the meeting that the message is part of the continuous consultation and cooperation between Egypt and Tunisia. He said the meeting was productive and that they had reviewed various African issues because the date of the forthcoming African summit in Tunis is approaching. He added that Tunisia will be honored by the participation of President Mubarak, current chairman of the OAU, in this summit so they can benefit from his experience in the African arena. He said the meeting also dealt with ways to consolidate and develop bilateral relations and expressed his country's desire to arrange more meetings between the two countries' officials to enhance bilateral cooperation.
