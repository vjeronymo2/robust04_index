BFN [Text] The Lithuanian parliamentary delegation, led by Parliament Speaker Ceslovas Jursenas, left Damascus this morning at the end of a visit to Syria that lasted for several days. He was seen off at Damascus International Airport by People's Assembly Speaker 'Abd-al-Qadir Qaddurah, and a number of ranking people's Assembly officials. Before departure, Jursenas made a statement to a SANA correspondent in which he expressed pleasure at meeting with President Hafiz al-Asad. He added: As we leave Damascus, we take with us a good impression. We understand the positions and nature of Syria's policy more than before, especially those dealing with the peace process. We support Resolution 242, and stress that Israel should withdraw from the occupied Arab territories.
