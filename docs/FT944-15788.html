941017 FT 17 OCT 94 / Business Travel (Update): Flight interference A computer scientist who tried to open an aircraft's door at high altitude has been charged with trying to interfere with an airline flight. The 34-year-old Canadian citizen, was arrested when an American Airlines MD-11 made an unscheduled stop at Seattle-Tacoma airport on a flight from Dallas to Tokyo with 168 passengers and 19 crew members.
