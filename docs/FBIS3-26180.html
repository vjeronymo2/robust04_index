Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 2 (XINHUA) -- President Jiang Zemin today appointed new Chinese ambassadors to Norway, Venezuela and Micronesia in accordance with a decision of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress. Zhu Yinglu succeeds Wang Gixin as ambassaor to the Kingdom of Norway, Sun Yanheng replaces Hung Zhiling as ambassador to the Republic of Venezuela and Chen Yogcheng succeeds Li Qinping as ambassador to the Federated States of Micronesia.
