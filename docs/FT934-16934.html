931004 FT 04 OCT 93 / Hanson rejects mediation call LORD HANSON has rejected a call from the Trades Union Congress to help resolve the long strike over union recognition at Peabody, the largest coal company in the US and part of his conglomerate. A meeting of the four unions in Hanson's UK companies - including Imperial Tobacco and SCN Chemicals - agreed to put pressure on them over the Peabody dispute.
