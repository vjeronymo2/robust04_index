BFN [Text] According to a decision by the government's Office for Displaced Persons and Refugees, on 1 April 1994 refugees from Mostar lost their refugee status in the Republic of Croatia and will have to leave hotels and holiday facilities in the areas of Ploce and the Makarska riviera, the Ploce regional Office for Displaced Persons and Refugees has announced. The majority of the 2,000 refugees from the Mostar area have been accommodated in hotels and other holiday facilities. Because of the Easter vacation, many learned about this decision too late to leave. Many whose homes have been destroyed have expressed concern about the decision because they have no home to return to.
