940511 FT 11 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Cardiff Property set to expand Cardiff Property yesterday announced it was making a share offer for First Choice Estates, which values the residential property developer at Pounds 2.5m. The purchase, which is being made on Cardiff's behalf by Brown Shipley, will be satisfied by the issue of 781,557 new shares at 20p apiece. Cardiff is offering 13 new shares for every 125 ordinary Pounds 1 shares in First Choice and 23 new shares for every 16 Pounds 1 A ordinary shares. The underlying net asset value of First Choice at September 30 1993 was Pounds 2.27m, and the offer values each share at 33.3p - a 10 per cent premium over the net asset value - and 460p for each First Choice A ordinary -a premium of 9.6 per cent. In addition, Cardiff has placed 200,000 new shares at 315p apiece to raise Pounds 630,000. The placing and offer are not interconditional.
