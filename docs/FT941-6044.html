940303 FT 03 MAR 94 / UK Company News: SPLIT net assets improve Save and Prosper Linked Investment Trust had a net asset value of Pounds 14.32 per capital share at December 31, an 18 per cent improvement over the Pounds 12.10 value at the June 30 year-end. For the six months to end-December, net revenue fell from Pounds 1.59m to Pounds 998,920 for earnings per income share of 19.03p (30.33p). A first interim dividend of 19.6p (30.25p) is being paid.
