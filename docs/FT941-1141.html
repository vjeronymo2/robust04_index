940326 FT 26 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Piper European Smaller for market Piper European Smaller Companies Trust is coming to the stock market via a placing and offer for subscription. It is offering up to 30m ordinary shares - with warrants attached on a 1-for-5 basis - at 100p each. The minimum investment is Pounds 1,000. Piper will invest mainly in quoted European smaller companies with market capitalisations of up to Pounds 250m and its objective will be long-term capital growth. Charterhouse Tilney has underwritten 10m ordinary shares, of which 9.7m have been placed with institutional and other investors. Dealings are expected to start on April 11.
