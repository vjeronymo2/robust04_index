Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Sitnikov] [Text] United Nations Organisation February 3 TASS -- Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Sergey Lavrov had a meeting here on Wednesday with U.N. Secretary-General Butrus Butrus-Ghali. Lavrov handed over to the U.N. secretary-general a message of Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev dealing with cooperation between the Russian Federation And the United Nations Organisation, including the activisation of efforts, aimed at ensuring a peaceful political settlement in Bosnia-Herzegovina, as well as the promotion of peace-keeping activities on the territory of the new Commonwealth, specifically in Abkhazia and Tajikistan. Lavrov also handed over to Butrus-Ghali the request of Kozyrev, as chairman of the CIS Council of Ministers, to grant the new Commonwealth the status of an observer at the U.N. General Assembly in accordance with a resolution of the CIS summit in Ashgabat. Lavrov and Butrus-Ghali discussed issues dealing with the preparation of the visit of the U.N. secretary-general to Moscow, whose date will be coordinated in the near future.
