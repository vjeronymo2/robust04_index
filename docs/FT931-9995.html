930211 FT 11 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Fleming Enterprise asset value ahead Fleming Enterprise Investment Trust had a net asset of 170.7p at December 31 1992. This compared with 159.6p a year earlier. Net revenue for the half year was sharply lower at Pounds 734,000 (Pounds 1.12m). Earnings per share dipped from 2.79p to 1.83p. The interim dividend is maintained at 1.3p.
