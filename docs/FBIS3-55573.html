Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Space Watch Continues"] [Text] Vasiliy Tsybliyev's and Aleksandr Serebrov's space watch aboard the "Mir" orbital complex is continuing. Over the last two days the scientific part of the crew's flight program mainly comprised geophysical studies. The cosmonauts took several series of shots of the earth's surface and carried out an experiment to assess the state of the atmosphere's ozone layer over various regions of our planet. Preparations have begun to hand over to the crew of the 15th main expedition, scheduled for liftoff 8 January. The flight of the "Mir" manned complex is going normally. The cosmonauts are healthy and feel fine.
