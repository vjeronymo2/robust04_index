BFN [Text] A rally and a parade in connection with anniversary of the victory in the Great Patriotic War are being staged in the capital by representatives of the opposition parties and movements. The National Salvation Front, the Russian Popular Alliance, the Agrarian Party, and the Russian Communist Party are among the initiators of these mass actions. The route and the procedure of the rallies were agreed with the Mosow mayor's office. The organisers of the rallies have accepted responsibility for the behavior of the demonstrators. The Union of Officers led by Stanislav Terekhov will also take part in the actions.
