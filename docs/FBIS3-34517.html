Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At 1300 today, His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad received Mr. Mohamed (Saidi), chief of the Algerian presidential office and envoy of the president, the Algerian ambassador in Damascus, and the Algerian Embassy staff. (Saidi) gave the president a message from Algerian President Liamine Zeroual, in which he extends his personal and the Algerian people's condolences over the death of Staff Major Basil al-Asad. They talked about Arab and world issues, as well as Algerian developments. Mr. (Saidi) conveyed President Zeroual's appreciation for President al-Asad's efforts to advance Arab causes and supreme Arab interests. He expressed Algeria's interest in more advanced ties with Syria in all fields. His excellency the president thanked the Algerian president for his sympathies and his appreciation for Syria's pan-Arab stands. President al-Asad told him Syria wished to see relations with brotherly Algeria grow further.
