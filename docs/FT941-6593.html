940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: SWP expansion SWP Group, the USM-quoted maker of specialist components for the construction industry, more than doubled pre-tax profits in the half year to December 31, from Pounds 42,000 to Pounds 105,000. Turnover improved from Pounds 3.71m to Pounds 4.43m generating operating profits of Pounds 167,000 (Pounds 126,000). Earnings worked through at 0.3p (0.1p).
