Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Mission Control Centre, February 18 (TASS) -- Cosmonauts Viktor Afanaysev, Yuriy Usachev and Valeriy Polyakov are continung their flight on board the "Mir" scientific research complex. They continued astrophysical, geophysical and technological experiments this week. Several series of experiments were carried out in accordance with the "Roentgen" international astrophysical research programme. The telescopes of the "Kvant" module were trained on one of the pulsars in the crab nebula. Today, the crew is regulating the equipment of the basic block. They are to replace one of the storage batteries in the station's power-supply system with a new one, delivered to the orbit by the automatic "Progress M-21" cargo vehicle. Medical examinations of the crew, carried out during the past few days, show that they are all in good health.
