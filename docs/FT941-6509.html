940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / International Company News: Compaq plans Dollars 20m expansion in Texas Compaq Computer plans to spend Dollars 20m expanding its manufacturing in Houston, Texas, increasing production capacity by 41 per cent. The move follows news of a Dollars 10.5m expansion in Erskine, Scotland. Last year Compaq also opened a new plant in Shenzhen, China, and is shortly expected to announce expansion in Singapore.
