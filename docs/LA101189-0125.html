October 11, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

PREP ROUNDUP;

EL TORO BREEZES PAST MISSION VIEJO IN SOUTH COAST LEAGUE TENNIS, 15-3

Sweeps from two singles players and two doubles teams paced host El Toro High School to a 15-3 girls' tennis victory over Mission Viejo in a South Coast League match Tuesday.

Corie Anderson won, 6-3, 6-1, 6-0, and Denise Kenyon won, 6-3, 7-6, 6-1. The doubles teams of Michelle Micone and Jenny Sher won, 6-3, 7-6, 6-1, and Michelle Pillsbury and Carrie Anderson won, 6-3, 7-6, 6-4.

El Toro is 6-5 overall, 2-1 in league play. Mission Viejo is 1-2 in the league.

In Angelus League girls' tennis:
