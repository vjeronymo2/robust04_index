930903 FT 03 SEP 93 / Bonn's rubbish row deepens Germany's federal Environment Ministry will hold emergency talks today with representatives of trade and industrial associations in a last-ditch attempt to save the increasingly controversial national recycling scheme from going bankrupt, writes Ariane Genillard in Bonn. The talks take place as waste management companies across the country are threatening to stop picking up household rubbish from Monday if no financial solution is found to rescue the scheme called Duales System Deutschland (DSD).
