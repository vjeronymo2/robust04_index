930410 FT 10 APR 93 / Sydney Burfoot: Obituary SYDNEY BURFOOT, for many years a member of the Financial Times' printing staff, has died at the age of 66. Mr Burfoot, a popular figure with his colleagues, worked as a printer at the FT for 26 years, latterly as an overseer. His career at the paper spanned the eras of hot-metal typesetting and computer output. Mr Burfoot retired to the Isle of Wight, where he is survived by his wife Joyce.
