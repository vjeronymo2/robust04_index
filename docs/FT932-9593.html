930513 FT 13 MAY 93 / Parliament and Politics: Hurd seeks to defuse Bosnia row MR DOUGLAS Hurd, the foreign secretary, yesterday sought to calm differences between the US and members of the European Community over Bosnia. In an apparent reference to an attack on EC governments by Senator Joseph Biden, Mr Hurd said: 'I do not think some of those remarks would have been made if those concerned had actually seen the exchanges which have taken place between the allies in recent days, or if they had understood the nature of the effort and the risk some of us are actually making in Bosnia at the present time.'
