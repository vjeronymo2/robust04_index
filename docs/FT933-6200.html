930828 FT 28 AUG 93 / Chrysler to end 15-year truck agreement with Mitsubishi Chrysler is to stop buying pick-up trucks from Mitsubishi, ending a 15-year arrangement, writes Michiyo Nakamoto in Tokyo. The US company's move reflects the growing pressure the strong yen is putting on the competitiveness of Japanese motor exports. Mitsubishi had been expected to raise its prices soon.
