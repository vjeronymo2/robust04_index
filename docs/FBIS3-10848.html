Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] On 8 March Paraguayan President Juan Carlos Wasmosy appointed Mario Lujan Melgarejo as acting president of the National Development Bank [BNF] replacing Rolando Eligio Bogado Martinez who was thanked for his services as head of the BNF. The appointment of Mario Lujan Melgarejo, a economist by profession, is the second time President Wasmosy has replaced a key official in his administration since the peasant crisis began. The peasants criticized the Agrarian Reform Institute, the BNF, and the Agricultural Development Fund for not serving the interests of farmers. [passage omitted]
