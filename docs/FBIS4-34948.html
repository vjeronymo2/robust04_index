BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on developments related to the scheduled 3 October general elections. PDT-PP Alliance in Minas Gerais -- The Minas Gerais office of the Democratic Labor Party, PDT, decided on 6 June to support the Popular Party, PP, candidate for the state's governorship, Helio Costa. In exchange Costa will allow PDT presidential candidate Leonel Brizola to take part in his rallies. According to Brizola, this alliance "will open up new possibilities not only in Minas Gerais, but also in the rest of Brazil." It has also been decided that Senator Darcy Ribeiro, vice presidential candidate on Brizola's ticket, will concentrate his electoral campaign in Sao Paulo, Minas Gerais, and Rio de Janeiro. (Rio de Janeiro JORNAL DO BRASIL in Portuguese 7 Jun 94 p 6) Paraiba PMDB Not To Support Lula -- The decision of the PT gubernatorial candidate in Paraiba State, Avenzoar Arruda, not to give up his candidacy has caused former Paraiba Governor Ronaldo Cunha Lima, who is the local Brazilian Democratic Movement Party leader and Senate candidate on Antonio Mariz' ticket, to withdraw his support for the presidential candidacy of the PT's Luiz Inacio Lula da Silva. (Rio de Janeiro JORNAL DO BRASIL in Portuguese 12 Jun 94 p 2)
