BFN [Text] Moscow, 25 May (Business-TASS) -- The State Duma committee on economic policy has spoken in favor of increasing Russia's military expenditure in 1994 to up to 55 trillion rubles, thus supporting the Duma's defense committee on this. If the State Duma were to approve this initiative during the debates of the budget in its second reading, the overall increase for national defense would exceed the original sum by 17.9 trillion rubles. The proposal to increase Russia's military expenditure to up to 55 trillion rubles was supported by Russian President Boris Yeltsin.
