Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By UKRINFORM correspondent Lev Ryabchikov for TASS] [Text] Simferopol, 21 Mar -- President Yuriy Meshkov's administration has issued a communique on the results of the meeting between the delegations of Tatarstan and the Crimea, which was held in Kazan on 18-19 March. At the meeting, questions of the fulfillment of the agreement on the principles of trade and economic cooperation between the republics were discussed, and their mutual interest in the further development of economic, scientific-technical, cultural, and humanitarian ties was expressed. The presidents of Tatarstan and the Crimea agreed to hold consultations on questions of bilateral relations and to promote the development of contacts between state establishments and public, national, and other organizations of the republics. An agreement was reached on the opening of trade-and-economic and cultural centers in Kazan and Simferopol.
