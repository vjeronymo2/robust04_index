BFN ["Message of His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk to His Excellency Khieu Samphan, Chairman of the Party of Democratic Kampuchea"; dated 22 April -- read by announcer] [Text] Your highly esteemed Excellency: Today, I am extremely pleased to received a fax giving the total support of the Party of Democratic Kampuchea [PDK] headed by youself for my 22 April 1994 statement concerning a round-table meeting for peace and national reconciliation. I would like to profoundly and warmly thank you and the PDK for the above demonstration of responsibility, which is imbued with fine and sublime patriotism. Please, Your Excellency and the entire PDK, accept my warmest and deepest affectionate regards. [Signed] Norodom Sihanouk. [Dated] Phnom Penh, 22 April 1994.
