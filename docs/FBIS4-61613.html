BFN [From the "Business SNARK" file: "Transcaucasia Economic News"] [Text] Tbilisi [Dateline as received] -- Georgian Parliament confirmed the appointment of Nodar Djavakhishvili as the Chairman of Georgian National Bank on 5 May. In his interview to SNARK Djavakhishvili observed that inflation rate would not exceed 10 percent monthly if the government made -- by administrative measures -- the coupon a sole payment means.
