July 19, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

BUSH'S VISIT TO POLAND

I didn't vote for President Bush, but as a third generation Polish-American, I can't help but love him! First we get a Polish Pope, now we get a President who likes us. Maybe praying does work, after all.

Did anyone notice the photo on Page 8, Part I, July 12? Overseeing the dinner with the President and Polish Solidarity leader Lech Walesa was Our Lady of Czestchowa, the black Madonna who performs miracles in Poland. We should give her a visa to perform some miracles here.

MARY JACOBS

Westlake Village
