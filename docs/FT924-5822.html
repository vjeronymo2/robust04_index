921125 FT 25 NOV 92 / Chinese air crash kills 141 A Chinese Boeing 737 flying from Guangzhou to Guilin in south-west China crashed yesterday, and all 133 passengers and eight crew are believed to have died, Reuter reports from Beijing. The official Xinhua news agency said among the passengers were a Canadian, two Spaniards, nine Taiwanese and one person from Portuguese-run Macao.
