Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 19 (KCNA) -- Jun Sik, chairman of the Human Rights Committee of the National Alliance for Democracy and Reunification (Chongukyonhap) of South Korea, at a press conference urged the authorities to stop the violation of human rights, the South Korean newspaper HANGYORE SHINMUN reported. Noting that such human rights abuses as in the years of military fascist dictatorship continued under the Kim Yong-sam regime, he said: "All manner of harsh treatment such as torture and severe penalties must be totally checked."
