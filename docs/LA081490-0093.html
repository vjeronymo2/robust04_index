August 14, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL PEOPLE

Lisa M. Hansen has been named president of CineTel Pictures in Burbank, a movie production operation that plans to produce up to eight motion pictures a year.

CineTel Pictures is a newly formed affiliate of CineTel Films, which will distribute CineTel Pictures' movies in international markets. New Line Cinema will distribute the films domestically. Hansen is also executive vice president of CineTel Films.
