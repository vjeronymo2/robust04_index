930824 FT 24 AUG 93 / International Company News: S China Morning Post slips 7% SOUTH China Morning Post, the News Corporation subsidiary which publishes Hong Kong's leading English language newspaper, reports a net profit of HKDollars 494m (USDollars 64m) for the year ended June, down 7 per cent. The fall was primarily the result of the full utilisation of tax losses during 1992. The figures were also adversely affected by the recent purchase of Wah Kiu Yat Po, a Chinese language newspaper.
