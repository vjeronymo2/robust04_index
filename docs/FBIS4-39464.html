BFN [Unattributed report: "YuzhNIIGiprogaz Intends To Affiliate With RAO Gazprom] [Text] The Donetsk-based YuzhNIIGiprogaz has moved away from the jurisdiction of the Ukraine State Oil and Gas Committee and intends to become an affiliated enterprise of RAO Gazprom. This was announced by the Russian Fuel and Energy Ministry. In the event that YuzhNIIGiprogaz becomes part of the RAO, a precedent will be set for corporatizing Ukraine's gas industry enterprises as part of Gazprom.
