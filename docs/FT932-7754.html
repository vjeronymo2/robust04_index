930521 FT 21 MAY 93 / International Company News: BAT Indian affiliate bucks profit trend ITC, India's largest tobacco company and an affiliate of BAT Industries of the UK, yesterday reported a rise in net profits to Rs1.55bn (Dollars 50m) for the 12 months to March, up from Rs1.15bn the year before, bucking the country's general trend of falling profits. Sales rose by a quarter, to Rs38.09bn, mostly due to strong growth in the tobacco business. After provisions, net profits rose by more than a third to Rs1.55bn. Earnings per share rose to Rs13.30 from Rs9.87, reversing the fall in the previous year.
