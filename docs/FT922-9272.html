920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit and Vegetables Top quality pineapples from the Ivory Coast are plentiful this week and are priced between 90p-Pounds 1.40 (80p-Pounds 1.50), reports FFVIB. Different varieties of apples remain good buys including Cape and Chilean Granny Smith at 50-60p a lb (50-65p). Majorcan new potatoes are superb at 25-30p a lb (40-45p), along with English spring greens at 30-35p a lb (30-35p) and English and French Hispi cabbage at 30-35p a lb (30-35p). New season French Iceberg at 60-75p each (60-65p) are plentiful along with Spania imports at 55-65p each (55-65p). British red and green peppers are good at Pounds 1.50-1.70 a lb (Pounds 1.50-1.70).
