May 2, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Directors of Chatsworth-based Syncor International Corp. authorized the repurchase of 500,000 shares, or 5%, of the company's common stock, worth about $3.25 million at current prices . . . Digital Equipment Corp. and Apple Computer Inc. introduced software products to link their computers together. The products enable users of Apple Macintosh and DEC computers to exchange and share data files, jointly author documents and share Apple and Digital computers.
