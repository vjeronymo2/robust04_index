Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow March 10 TASS -- A regular session of the Interdepartmental Commission for the Security Council of the Russian Federation on Information Security was held in Moscow on Thursday. The participants in the session discussed issues related to the realisation of the law "On the State Secret" adopted in 1993 and creation of an up-to-date mechanism to protect state secrets. The session approved recomendations with a view to speed up work on a state programme of the complex modernisation of the state secret protection system. Representatives of the Russian Government, leaders of ministries, departments and the Russian Academy of Sciences participated in the session.
