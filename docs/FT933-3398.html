930914 FT 14 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Berry Starquest net assets advance Berry Starquest had a net asset value per share of 200.1p on July 31 compared with 157.1p a year earlier and 170.7p at the January 31 year end. Net revenue for the six months improved to Pounds 48,000 against Pounds 40,000 for earnings per share up from 0.8p to 0.9p.
