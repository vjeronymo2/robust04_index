940308 FT 08 MAR 94 / London Stock Exchange: Other big moves An announcement by Northern Leisure that its accounts are to receive an Pounds 8.5m boost, together with positive remarks on current trading, pushed the shares 10 ahead to 51p. USM-quoted Cupid, the manufacturer of bridal wear, lost favour in the marketplace after the board issued a profits warning and the shares plunged 6 to a new low of 16p. Cupid said it would be 'substantially loss-making' in the current financial period.
