June 17, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

MAYBE DODGERS SHOULD WORK A LITTLE HARDER

The more I think about it, the more I believe that last year's Dodger championship was pure luck.

This year's team has a better pitching staff, with the addition of Mike Morgan, and a better right side of the infield.

But a winner is not someone who rests on his laurels after one little championship. Just look at the Lakers.

No one is suggesting that the Dodgers win four consecutive championships, just that they look as if they want to win four consecutive championships.

GARY ROBERT FENTON

Beverly Hills
