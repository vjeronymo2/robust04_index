October 28, 1989, Saturday, Southland Edition

BURGLARY PROBE TURNS UP 100 POUNDS OF COCAINE

A police officer investigating a report of a burglary in a Queens house stumbled on 100 pounds of cocaine, drug paraphernalia and $15,900 in cash, police said Friday.

The owner of the house, who had rented it to a couple, called police Thursday after finding that the locks had been changed and spotting a strange man in the window, police said. One man was arrested.
