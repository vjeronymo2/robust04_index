Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Zhang Chuanxi (1728 0278 3556): "Guangdong Provincial People's Congress Standing Committee Adopts Six Local Laws and Regulations"] [Text] Guangzhou, 18 Jan (ZHONGGUO XINWEN SHE)--The Eighth Guangdong Provincial People's Congress Standing Committee met today and adopted six local laws and ordinances, including the "Properties Auction Regulations of Guangdong Province," the first of its kind ever introduced in the province. The other five laws and ordinances adopted at the meeting are: "Regulations of Guangdong Province Governing Operation of Non-Government-Run Science and Technology Enterprises," "Regulations of Guangdong Province Governing Operation of Singing Lounges, Ballrooms, Cabarets, and Other Entertainment Establishments," "Ordinance of Guangdong Province for Protection of Enterprise Employees' Rights and Interests," "Regulations of Guangdong Province Governing Assignment of Real Properties in Urban Areas," and "Regulations of Guangdong Province on Job Arrangements for Personnel Discharged From Prisons and Reform-Through-Labor Camps." The "Regulations of Guangdong Province Governing Operation of Non-Government-Run Science and Technology Enterprises" expressly provide that intellectual property rights can be converted into registered capital at a price based on legal assessment, but the proportion of capital invested as such shall not be higher than 30 percent of the total registered capital. The "Ordinance of Guangdong Province for Protection of Enterprise Employees' Rigths and Interests" reiterates the rule that no enterprise shall force workers to work overtime beyond the limit of working hours.
