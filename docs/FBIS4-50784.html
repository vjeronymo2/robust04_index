BFN [Text] Beijing, May 21 (XINHUA) -- Wu Xueqian, vice-chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), met here this evening with Thai Crown Sister, Princess Galyani Vadhana and her party in the Great Hall of the People. The Thai Crown Sister and her party arrived in Beijing this afternoon for a two-week long visit to China at the invitation of the CPPCC National Committee.
