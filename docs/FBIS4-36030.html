BFN [Text] Deputy Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Wahhab al-Anisi this morning received (Chan Chichan), the charge d'affaires at the embassy of the People's Republic of China in our country. They discussed developments in the Yemeni arena and ties between the two friendly countries. During the meeting, the Chinese charge d'affaires underlined his country's concern for Yemeni unity since it is a factor of stability in the region. For his part, the brother deputy prime minister voiced appreciation for China's position on the events and developments in our region. He also expressed the hope that the ties of cooperation between the two countries will improve for the benefit of the two friendly peoples' mutual interests. The meeting also touched on the discussions that will be held at the UN Security Council and our country's position, since these discussions run counter to the UN charter and constitute a precedent that will affect not only Yemen, but also other countries with worse internal situations than those in Yemen.
