August 25, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

METRO DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF : MAN FATALLY STABBED IN ROOM AT HILTON HOTEL

The body of a 27-year-old man who had been fatally stabbed was found in his 15th-floor room at the downtown Los Angeles Hilton hotel Friday afternoon, police said.

The victim was identified as Kenneth Wayne Laws of Los Angeles. Hotel officials declined to say how long he had been staying at the hotel.
