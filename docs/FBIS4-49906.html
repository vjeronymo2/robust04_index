BFN [Text] Oslo, May 11 (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the national committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), held talks here today with Norwegian Acting Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Bjorn Tore Godal on joint efforts to promote cooperation between China and Norway. Li Ruihuan briefed Godal on China's current economic construction and the democratic politics. He said democracy marks the advance of a society, it is also the inevitable trend of history. The Chinese government maintains that the building of democratic politics actively and steadily must be on the basis of social stability. Otherwise, he said, it will hurt society and even create social chaos. Godal said that Norway gives great importance to relations with China which has world influence. He said he is pleased to see the rapid development of Sino-Norwegian cooperation in trade, science and other fields.
