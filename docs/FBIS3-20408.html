Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Lille, France, March 31 (AFP) -- A contingent of 200 French troops left the northern French city of Lille for Zagreb Thursday [31 March] to relieve the 403d Logistical Support Detachment whose term of service in the former Yugoslavia is due to expire. The contingent, the first of three totalling 600 troops to leave for Zagreb over a three-day period, is headed by Lieutenant-Colonel Patrick Rose. France has a total of more than 6,000 men in the former Yugoslavia.
