930909 FT 09 SEP 93 / Unions plan Tupe legal cases THE TUC said yesterday that unions were 'trawling through potential cases' to bring against the government retrospectively over the so-called Tupe regulations. Unions say the government, by introducing the Transfer of Undertakings (Protection of Employment) regulations, has failed fully to implement a European Community directive on the transfer of workers when businesses are taken over.
