931125 FT 25 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Platignum advances to Pounds 398,000 In spite of markets in which it operates remaining weak, Platignum, the manufacturer of pens, stationery, furniture and housewares, returned sharply higher profits for the six months to end-September. At the pre-tax level they improved from Pounds 69,000 to Pounds 398,000 from turnover, boosted by acquisitions, ahead 32 per cent at Pounds 10.7m. Profits took account of higher interest charges of Pounds 146,000 (Pounds 78,000). However, there were no exceptional provisions this time compared with Pounds 260,000 previously. The interim dividend is lifted to 0.28p (0.25p) from earnings of 1.74p (0.29p). Gluetek and Housley International, both acquired earlier this year, made a 'positive' impact on the results.
