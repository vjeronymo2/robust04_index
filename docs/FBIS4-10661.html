BFN [Text] The final protocol of the conversations held this week by the joint commissions of Colombia and Cuba was signed on 14 April at the headquarters of the State Committee for Economic Cooperation [CECE]. The agreement includes areas such as education, science and technology, culture, and sports, among others. The agreement was signed by Colombian Vice Foreign Minister Andelfo Garcia [name and title as heard] and by Raul Taladrid, vice minister of the CECE. Taladrid stressed the importance of this agreement, which opens wide the doors for mutual cooperation. Garcia thanked the Cuban delegation for the treatment he received and praised the efforts made by both delegations. [Begin Garcia recording] First, we were trying to establish institutional channels through which our relationship with Cuba could be handled. Our aspiration is to broaden our relationship with Cuba. We are building a relationship unhurriedly but unceasingly. [end recording]
