941001 FT 01 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Pounds 3m plant hire purchase by Allen Allen, the engineering, building and property group, is expanding its plant hire activities with the Pounds 3m acquisition of Kendrick Hire, the UK's largest privately owned tool hire company. The consideration will be satisfied by the issue of 1.35m shares and Pounds 600,000 cash. Kendrick reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 105,000 on turnover of Pounds 7.1m for the March 31.
