940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / HK newspaper to print in Beijing A Hong Kong newspaper publisher has won permission to print its English-language daily, the Hongkong Standard, in Beijing, the company said yesterday, Reuter reports from Hong Kong. 'We are the first and only foreign-language newspaper to be granted permission to be printed in the People's Republic of China,' Mr Jim Marett, the group's general manager said. Publishers Sing Tao Holdings, founded by philanthropist Mr Aw Boon Haw who made his fortune by inventing Tiger Balm ointment, said the China print run would begin today
