931111 FT 11 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: Seatbelt call after 10 die in coach crash MPs, the RAC and the Consumers' Association last night demanded the compulsory fitting of seatbelts in coaches. The call came after 10 people died and many others were injured when a coach (above) carrying 44 US and Canadian tourists from London to Canterbury skidded off the M2 motorway in heavy rain near Faversham, Kent. In south-west France 13 motorists were killed in a multiple crash after a tanker truck caught fire on the Bordeaux-Paris motorway.
