940608 FT 08 JUN 94 / International Company News: Minproc halts funding plan Minproc, the Western Australian mining and engineering company, has suspended plans to raise ADollars 352m (USDollars 258m) after Kerr-McGee, the US group, said it wanted a bigger interest in their Tiwest joint venture. Minproc added that it planned to resume the fund-raising effort, once the Tiwest matter was settled.
