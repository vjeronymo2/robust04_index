Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [TA SR report: "Ivan Stadtrucker in Office; New Slovak Television Director"] [Text] Bratislava -- Ivan Gasparovic, chairman of the National Council of the Slovak Republic, introduced Ivan Stadtrucker into the office of central director of Slovak Television yesterday. He paid tribute to his practical and theoretical experience and expressed the wish that television should have mainly the viewer in mind and should not act as a political truncheon. Jozef Darmo, chairman of the Slovak Television Council, voiced his conviction that under the management of Ivan Stadtrucker, television broadcasts will meet the tasks arising from the law and will be constructive, politically tolerant, and of a high cultural standard.
