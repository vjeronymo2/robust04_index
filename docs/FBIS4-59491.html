BFN [Excerpt] The Central Command of the anti-'Arafat coalition of the 10 factions has decided to transfer its operations to the occupied Palestinian territories to defeat the Oslo accord and its offshoots. The 10 factions plan to achieve this through mobilizing the masses in the territories to defeat, expose, and isolate the accord. This was decided at a meeting held by the Central Command last Wednesday [11 May]. The five-hour meeting addressed the latest developments, particularly the Cairo agreement, ways to respond to the recent developments, activating confrontation with the enemy, and the situation of the Palestinians in Lebanon.
