BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 22 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Yesterday's signing of a political consensus between the leaders of the three parliamentary parties and the decision of the Great State Hural in support of the political will of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party, the Mongolian National Democratic Party, the Mongolian Social Democratic Party [MSDP] have not giving a decisive effect on the settling down of the matter around the hunger strike. The hunger strike by the activists of the above mentioned parties is being continued for 10 days. Another one meeting has been held today in support of the demands of hunger strikers. The participants of the meeting once more were picketing the state residence and scanning the slogans for resignation of government. Today a member of the parliament, leader of the MSDP, having been the first vice president in the history of Mongolia R. Gonchigdorj was taken to the hospital after carried out for several times examinations of his health state by physicians who has joined four days ago the hunger strikers out of humanism. According to physicians, his condition is grave. The another hunger strike of supporters of the government is going on as well.
