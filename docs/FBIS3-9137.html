Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] The parties of the new government coalition should agree on the final filling of all government and ministerial posts, including the posts of state secretaries, at their talks in Bratislava today. This was stated by Prime Minister Jozef Moravcik on yesterday's "Kroky" TV program. Deputy Prime Minister Roman Kovac added that the heads of government bureaus would also be named within a short period of time so that the government could start working fully. Roman Kovac described as a new feature the fact that the coalition agreement to be signed today takes into account cooperation with the partners from [ethnic] Hungarian parties.
