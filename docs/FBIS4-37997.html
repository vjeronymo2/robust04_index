BFN [Text] Aden, 25 Jun (KUNA) -- The southern Yemeni air defenses downed a northern Yemeni plane above the Shaykh 'Uthman area in the outskirts of Aden at midday today after its attempt to raid the area. A KUNA correspondent in Aden saw the northern Yemeni MiG-21 aircraft burning in flight over the central prison in the Shaykh 'Uthman area. However, an official communique issued by the Defense Ministry in Aden affirmed that the Southern air defenses downed a northern Yemeni plane above Aden.
