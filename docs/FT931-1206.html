930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables Oranges are the best fruit buy this week priced between 10-25p reports the FFVIB. English Bramley apples 30-35p a lb, French Granny Smith 35-40p a lb and British Columbian Red Delicious 45-50p a lb. Jaffa and Cyprus white grapefruit 15-25p each and kiwifruit from Italy are 8-12p each. Top vegetable buy is Brittany Prince cauliflower at 50-65p each, English onions at 18-20p a lb and potatoes at 10-14p a lb. English and Dutch cucumbers are increasing in supply as the salad season gets underway, 60-70p each, and celery has come down in price to 45-50p per head and watercress is 35-40p a bunch.
