910507 FT 07 MAY 91 / Construction Contracts: London road maintenance programme BALFOUR BEATTY CIVIL ENGINEERING has secured a major highway maintenance contract. Awarded by the Department of Transport, the west London term maintenance contract 1991 to 1993, valued in excess of Pounds 7m, is for work on a network of motorway and trunk roads located within the London Boroughs of Hounslow, Hammersmith & Fulham, Kensington & Chelsea and the City of Westminster. These include, among other highways, sections of the M4, A40 (M) and M1. Maintenance of some 60 kilometres of motorway and trunk roads, 270 structures, 3,000 lighting units, winter maintenance work, assistance to emergency services and temporary traffic management will be carried out from two depots located at Heston and White City. Minimal disruption to the general public, safety, and assistance to the emergency services, are an integral part of the contract which is being managed by Frank Graham, consulting engineers.
