Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Article by Engineer V. Konyakhin] [Text] The machine is designed for drilling boreholes in rock and wells in frozen and unfrozen ground when doing engineer work. It consists of a KamAZ-4310 base chassis and operating equipment, which includes a distribution box with an oil pump and compressor, a traversing platform on which rifle equipment is installed, and the drilling mechanism with the working instrument. The machine used the design of a modern impact-rotary method of breaking up rock. The impact frequency is 83-94 Hz. The impact drilling machine can be successfully used in fortification of troop positions by the explosive method in conditions of frozen ground and in rock.
