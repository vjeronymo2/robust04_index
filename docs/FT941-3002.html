940318 FT 18 MAR 94 / DuPont opens in Vietnam US chemicals company DuPont opened an office in Ho Chi Minh City yesterday and said Vietnam would be a focal point for its efforts to quadruple sales in the region by the year 2000, Reuter reports from Hanoi. DuPont is one of the first US companies to open up in Vietnam since President Bill Clinton lifted a 30-year economic embargo on February 3. Mr David Pang, vice-president Asia Pacific, said DuPont saw the region as its biggest growth area. It was looking to boost sales there to Dollars 10bn (Pounds 6.7bn) by the year 2000.
