920629 FT 29 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Electric & General Net asset value of Electric & General Investment Co stood at 152.6p per share at May 31 against 137.1p a year earlier. Net revenue for the year fell to Pounds 2.84m (Pounds 2.97m) for earnings per share of 3.14p (3.28p). A proposed final dividend of 1.6p (1.5p) makes a total for the year of 3.1p (3p).
