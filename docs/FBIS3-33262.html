Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "This Morning" newscast] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] Today, the government begins the first serious negotiations for a definitive cease-fire and the disarmament of the Northern Front 3-80's rebel troops, led by Jose Angel Talavera, a.k.a. El Chacal, in an effort to eventually pacify the country through demobilization, which will be the key to national stability and production. Today a Nicaraguan Government commission will present a proposal to the rebels which includes the granting of lands and the legalization of ownership for the disarmed, creation of jobs in the community, and the implementation of educational and health programs.
