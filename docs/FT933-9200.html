930811 FT 11 AUG 93 / Tory party tops unpopularity poll A TOTAL of 48 per cent of voters do not want a Tory victory, at the next election, a Guardian/ICM poll says. This compares with 30 per cent of electors who do not want a Labour win and 5 per cent a Liberal Democrat win. The poll puts Labour as the most popular party at 40 per cent with the Tories and Liberal Democrats both on 28 per cent. Since last month Labour has fallen two points, the Tories are the same and the Liberal Democrats have climbed five points.
