Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, Feb. 14--Foreign Minister of Moldova Ion Botnaru who starts an official two-day visit to Poland on Tuesday, arrived in Warsaw on Monday. Minister Botnaru will meet with Polish Foreign Minister Andrzej Olechowski. The two officials will sign a consular convention and a protocol on mutual consultations between their ministries. Olechowski and Botnaru will also discuss preparations for economic agreements between Poland and Moldova. The Moldovan foreign minister will be received by the Polish president, prime minister and deputy speakers of the Sejm and Senate. He will also meet with the managment of the Polish Chamber of Commerce.
