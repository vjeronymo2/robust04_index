930731 FT 31 JUL 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Indonesian cassia prices remain firm, reports Man Producten, with a rising trend in European currency terms. Industrial buyers, however, are holding back from covering their needs for autumn grinding. Ka/va sticks for spot delivery are fetching Dollars 2,325 a tonne, with shipment offers at Dollars 2,215. Madagascar and Seychelles cassia prices are unchanged at Dollars 1,100 and Dollars 1,275 a tonne cif respectively. Chinese broken cassia is on offer at between Dollars 1,300 and Dollars 1,350 a tonne, and for whole at Dollars 1,500 to Dollars 1,550. Nutmeg and mace prices are unchanged with S/A bwp at Dollars 700 a tonne, S/A shrivels at Dollars 940, S/A abcd at Dollars 1,100.
