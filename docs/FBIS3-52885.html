Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Mr. Wen Jiabao, an alternate member of the Politburo of the Chinese Communist Party, has said Beijing attaches utmost importance to develop friendly ties with India. Mr. Jiabao is heading an eight-member high-level delegation of the Chinese Communist Party. The delegation called on the vice president, Mr. K.R. Narayanan, in New Delhi today. Mr. Narayanan expressed satisfaction on the progress achieved in bilateral cooperation between the two countries. The Chinese delegation also held discussions with Congress-I leaders. The delegation exchanged views with Congress-I leaders on strengthening cultural, economic, and political relationship between the two countries. Mr. V.N. Gadgil, Mr. R.L. Bhatia, and Mr. Shushil Kumar Shinde took part in the discussions.
