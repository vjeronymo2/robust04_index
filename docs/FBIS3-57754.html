Language: English Article Type:BFN [Received via Internet BALT-L] [Text] January 24 -- The first session of the Parliamentary Assembly of the Council of Europe [CE] met and elected the office holders for the year. The posts of President and of fourteen vice- presidents were filled. Deputy Speaker to the Estonian parliament and leader of the Delegation to the Council of Europe, Mr. Tunne Kelam, was elected to one of the posts of vice president.
