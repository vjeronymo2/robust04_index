930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / Major to meet Ulster leaders NORTHERN Ireland's Social Democratic and Labour party is understood to be pressing prime minister John Major to keep alive the prospect of round-table talks on the province's political future, even without the Rev Ian Paisley's Democratic Unionist party. SDLP leader Mr John Hume is to meet Mr Major tomorrow for talks, a day before Mr Paisley is scheduled to meet the prime minister to present new proposals for progress.
