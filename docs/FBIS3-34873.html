Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Khartoum, 15 Feb (SUNA) -- Meetings of the official talks between Sudan and Iraq in the field of transport were started here on Tuesday under the co-chairmanship of Iraqi Transport Minister Mr. Ahmad Murtada Khalil and Sudanese Transport Minister Uthman 'Abd-al-Qadir ['Abd-al-Latif]. 'Abd-al-Qadir hailed the cooperation between the two countries and the Iraqi support to Sudan despite its critical circumstances. The Iraqi minister said his visit came as a confirmation of the continuous cooperation between the two countries in the field of transport and condemned the embargo being committed against Sudan and Iraq. He underlined that Iraq realized great achievements following the Gulf war in the field of rehabilitation and installations. Iraq was looking for more cooperation in all fields, he said. The two ministers visited today Khartoum airport and the Sudanese Airlines Corporation, where they got acquainted with the efforts being made to promote the corporation and the cooperation fields. The Iraqi transport minister expressed readiness to remove all difficulties facing the corporation.
