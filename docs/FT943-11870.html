940728 FT 28 JUL 94 / Tyne and Wear investment report The Tyne and Wear Development Corporation attracted Pounds 116m of private-sector investment in the 1993-94 financial year, the corporation's annual report says. The report, published yesterday, says private-sector investment in the area has totalled Pounds 603m since the corporation started operating in 1987. Since then, the corporation has ploughed Pounds 265m of public-sector funds -including Pounds 53.8m in 1993-94 - into the 27 miles of land beside the Tyne and the Wear for which it is responsible.
