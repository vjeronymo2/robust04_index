941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / Company News (This Week): Acatos & Hutcheson - Other Companies The maker of own-brand edible oils and fats for UK supermarkets is expected on Thursday to announce a rise in pre-tax profits to about Pounds 12m for the year to the end of September. This compares with Pounds 10.2m for 53 weeks previously. The dividend is expected to rise from 8p to 9p.
