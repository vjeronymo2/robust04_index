Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, January 26, RIA -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin today had a working meeting in the Kremlin with Viktor Stepanov, the chairman of the Supreme Soviet of Karelia, the presidential press service reported. In the conversation held the Russian president was informed of the progress of reforms in Karelia in the bodies of state power and local self-government and of the adoption by the Supreme Soviet of a number of laws aimed at increasing the social and legal protection of citizens. Much attention during the conversation was paid to the rational utilisation of Karelia's forest resources and also to the construction of Russia's first commercial railway line Ledmozero- Vacha-Kochkoma, which will make it possible to increase Russia's foreign trade in real terms.
