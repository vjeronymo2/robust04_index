Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] On 12 January Evgeni Matinchev, deputy chairman of the Council of Ministers and chairman of the Interdepartmental Council on Issues of the Military-Industrial Complex and Mobilization Readiness, met Anatoliy Semyonovich Gavrikov, Russian trade representative in Bulgaria. Issues related to the state of bilateral cooperation in the sphere of special production and the prospect of developing it in the future were discussed. The information was provided by the Press Center of the Council of ministers.
