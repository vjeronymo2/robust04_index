941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / UK Company News: BAe says GEC breached Panel rules British Aerospace yesterday argued to the Takeover Panel that GEC, its rival in the bidding war for shipbuilder VSEL, breached Panel rules by circulating a critique of BAe to selected MP's. The Panel refused to comment yesterday but is understood to feel that lobbying of MPs is permissible under the Takeover Code.
