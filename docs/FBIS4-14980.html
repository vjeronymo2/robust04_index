BFN [Text] Azerbaijani President Geydar Aliyev issued a decree on Liberalization of Foreign Trade in the Azerbaijan Republic in order to achieve a turning point in the development of the national economy on the basis of the broad application of market mechanisms, expand mutually advantageous trade and economic ties with foreign countries and firms, ensure the further deepening of economic reforms, increase productivity in foreign trade and economic ties, improve the export mechanism, and liberalize the activities of firms engaged in exports and stimulate their interest in accruing foreign currency reserves for the republic and boosting exports.
