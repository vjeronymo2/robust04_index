Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, Feb 1 (MAP) -- Egyptian President Husni Mubarak Monday received a verbal message from King Hassan II. The message was conveyed by Mohamed Aouad, adviser to the Moroccan king, who was accompanied by Moroccan Culture Minister Mohamed Allal Si Nasser. Si Nasser at the end of the meeting told the press the Egyptian president voiced feelings of fraternity towards the Moroccan sovereign with whom he shares identical views on issues concerning the Arab world. Talks covered the broadlines of the ongoing conference on Moroccan-Egyptian relations, Si Nasser said adding that President Mubarak who surveyed the development of the Middle East peace talks explained that problems accumulated for decades cannot be resolved in one or two phases of negotiations. Aouad and Si Nasser arrived here Sunday to partake in the conference on Moroccan-Egyptian relations.
