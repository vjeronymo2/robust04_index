BFN [Text] In a solemn ceremony, Commander of the Revolution Juan Almeida Bosque, vice president of the Council of State, received His Excellency (Augustus Kuesi Yankey), who presented his credentials as Ghana's extraordinary and plenipotentiary ambassador to Cuba. Foreign Vice Minister Isabel Allende was also in attendance. The Ghanaian ambassador was accompanied by advisers (Francis A. Segat), and (Paul Wenton Keshirt).
