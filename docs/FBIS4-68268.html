CSO [Rosvooruzheniye State Company Press Service article under the "Novosti" rubric: "Russian Arms Exhibition"] [Text] An exhibition (to be held from 24 to 28 May) will include about 30 enterprises of the Russian VPK [military-industrial complex], which will display small arms, rocket and artillery weapons, PVO [air defense] items, and engineer equipment. The exhibition is being organized by the Golden Gates of Russia Vladimir-Suzdal International Fair AO [joint-stock company]. Visitors will be able to speak with representatives of the Rosvooruzheniye State Company and discuss purchasing of weapons and military equipment.
