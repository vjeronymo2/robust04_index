Language: French Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Keenly concerned about the crisis, the labor union sector is trying to facilitate a negotiated solution. Meetings among the sectors involved in the crisis are increasing. The labor union sector plans to meet with President Jean-Bertrand Aristide, the diplomats accredited to Haiti, and the Army High Command, which has had a conversation with the unionists. In an initial press release, the union organizations assert that all sectors believe it can last no longer, that the bleeding of Haitian society must stop. The princicpal labor unions met at the premises of the Federation of Workers Trade Union for a roundup on the state of the discussions, but nothing has leaked out of those consultations.
