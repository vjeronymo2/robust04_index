Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pretoria March 3 SAPA -- The Transitional Executive Council [TEC] and members of its Sub-Council on Defence meet on Friday [4 March] in a bid to solve the pay grievances which sparked a strike by 3,100 members of the National Peacekeeping Force [NPKF]. NPKF spokesman Capt Johan Loots said on Thursday members of the TEC Management Council would visit the De Brug training camp, near Bloemfontein, on Monday to present the force's commander Maj-Gen Gabriel Ramushwana with a solution to the problem. Capt Loots said the majority -- 98 per cent -- of the trainees who went on strike on Monday had returned to work, following a visit to the camp by TEC representatives.
