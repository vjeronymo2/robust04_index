940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Rowlinson advances to Pounds 3.13m Pre-tax profits at Rowlinson Securities, the Cheshire-based property group, jumped from Pounds 722,000 to Pounds 3.13m in the year to March 31 on sales ahead 14 per cent to Pounds 10.2m. The company attributed the profit outcome to asset disposals, the absence of significant write-downs and lower interest rates. A final dividend of 1.56p makes a total of 1.8p (1.5p) on earnings of 15.7p (4.64p).
