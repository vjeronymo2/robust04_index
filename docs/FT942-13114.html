940426 FT 26 APR 94 / Egyptians kill key militant A man described as the most wanted Islamic militant in Egypt was killed along with 10 other suspected fundamentalists in two shootouts yesterday, security officials said, Reuter reports from Cairo. Taalat Yassin Hammam and six other militants were shot dead in a flat in the east Cairo suburb of Kobri el-Kobba when police went to arrest them early yesterday morning, the officials said. Hammam was sentenced to death in absentia in 1992.
