Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Radio Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] Pavel Grachev, Army general and Russian defense minister, recently summed up the results of our servicemen's peacekeeping operations. [Begin Grachev recording] We act as peacekeepers in Moldova, in the conflict between Moldova and the Dniester region; and we act as peacekeepers in Transcaucasus and Tajikistan. The total number of Russian peacekeepers is 16,000 men, more than all other countries' peacekeepers combined. We are active peacekeepers and we would like to settle conflicts in various regions of the former USSR, not just by ourselves but by joining forces with others. We are particularly interested in three hot spots: Azerbaijan, Armenia, and Nagorno-Karabakh; Georgia, Abkhazia and, South Ossetia, and the problem of Georgia's integrity and its political future; and Tajikistan. [end recording]
