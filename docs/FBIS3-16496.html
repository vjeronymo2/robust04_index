Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Coordinating Council of the Association of Democratic Forces `Spring-94' Issues a Statement"] [Text] The Coordinating Council of the Association of Democratic Forces "Spring-94" has issued a statement denouncing the stance of the Belarusian Government regarding the activities of the Belarusian mass media. "The prevailing majority of mass media are, as before, in the hands of the executive bodies," the statement reads. "Leaders of the government overtly declare that media which do not support the government will not be granted subsidies." The authors of the statement demand that space in newspapers and even broadcasting time be issued by the government to various parties and political associations to allow them the opportunity to clarify their platforms during the elections.
