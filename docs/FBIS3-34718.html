Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Islamic Conference Organization [ICO] secretary general, Dr. Hamid al-Ghabid, called on President Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari at the Aiwan-i-Sadr [President House] today and discussed with him various problems facing the Muslim world. The secretary general briefed the president about the various efforts made by the ICO to put an end to hostilities in Afghanistan. He also informed the president about his current mission to affect a cease-fire in the strife torn country which will pave the way for a dialogue between the warring factions. The president briefed Dr. al-Ghabid about the appeals made by Pakistan and other steps taken by it to restore peace in Afghanistan. He wished him all success in his efforts and assured him of all possible cooperation by Pakistan. The Kashmir problem and situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina also came up under discussion during the meeting. Dr. Hamid conveyed to the president the ICO's deep concern and anguish over the situation in the Indian-held Kashmir and reiterated support to Pakistan's stand.
