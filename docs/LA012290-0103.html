January 22, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

ANNE DENIES DIVORCE REPORTS

Britain's Princess Anne denied banner-headline reports today that she was planning to divorce her estranged husband, Mark Phillips.

"I haven't seen a paper today," Queen Elizabeth's only daughter told Independent Television News. "Divorce? Never been mentioned by anybody."

The 39-year-old Princess Royal, looking puzzled, was speaking from behind the wheel of a farm vehicle on her country estate in western England.

Princess Anne and Phillips announced their separation last August, saying there were no plans for divorce proceedings.

In a report headlined "Anne: I Want a Quickie Divorce," the Daily Express said the princess was pushing to end the 15-year-old marriage despite Phillips' wish to let the separation run on indefinitely.
