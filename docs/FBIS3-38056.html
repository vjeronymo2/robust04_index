Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Igor Borisenko] [Excerpts] Washington, 26 Feb -- The U.S. State Department declared Aleksandr Iosifovich Lysenko, counsellor in the Russian Embassy in the United States, "persona no grata." [passage omitted] ITAR-TASS has learned that Aleksandr Lysenko arrived in Washington in July 1993. He is 56 years old. The Russian Federation Embassy stated that "they had no comment on the State Department's statement." Immediately after the announcement by the State Department, a large group of American TV reporters and media photographers gathered at the Russian Federation Embassy, practically blocking the entrance to the building situated a few blocs away from the White House. Policemen who are permanently stationed at the Embassy are watching the developments with indifference.
