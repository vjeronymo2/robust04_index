Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Vladimir Taranov] [Text] Moscow, 2 March -- A program of cooperation between the Russian and Uzbek Foreign Ministries for 1994 envisages that bilateral consultations at the level of deputy foreign ministers, working meetings, and consultations on specific subjects will be held in Moscow and Tashkent. On the basis of this document and in accordance with the mutual agreement there will be an exchange of information between the foreign ministries of the two countries. The program was signed today by the leaders of the Foreign Ministries, Andrey Kozyrev and Saidmukhtar Saidkasymov.
