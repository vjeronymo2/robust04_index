BFN [Text] Iran and Ukraine will form a joint economic and trade cooperation commission. This was announced this morning during a meeting between a Ukrainian economic and diplomatic delegation headed by Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko and our country's oil minister, [Gholamreza] Aqazadeh. The two sides expressed hope that the first session of the commission will review existing problems in the implementation of agreements which have already been signed between the two countries. During the meeting Mr. Zlenko also announced his country's readiness to cooperate with the Islamic Republic of Iran in the fields of oil, energy, metals, transportation, and tourism.
