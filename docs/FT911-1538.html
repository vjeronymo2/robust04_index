910504 FT 04 MAY 91 / Appointments: Restructure at Inchcape INCHCAPE is re-organising its worldwide operations on a business stream basis. The following board changes will be made, effective from January 1, 1992. Mr Alan Marsh, chairman of Toyota (GB), will be appointed a main board director and will manage all Toyota businesses worldwide. Mr Derek Whittaker, director in charge of the group's European motors operations, will become a main board director and responsible for all worldwide motor operations other than Toyota. Mr David John, a main board director, will become responsible for the worldwide marketing and distribution business stream. Mr Jean-Paul Parayre has been appointed a non-executive director. He is vice chairman and executive vice president of Group Lyonnaise des Eaux-Dumez, and chairman of Trans-Manche Construction. He is on the boards of Alcatel Alsthom, and of Alfred McAlpine, and on the supervisory board of Peugeot. ***** PERSIMMON, York, has appointed Mr Michael Walker as construction director of Persimmon Homes (East Midlands). Mr Steve Feneley has been appointed managing director of Persimmon Homes (North Midlands). ***** IVERNIA WEST has appointed to the board Mr Michael Gordon, vice president, corporate finance, Minorco, and Mr Christian Schaffalitzky, managing director of Crowe Schaffalitzky and Associates. Mr Andrew Woollett has resigned from the board to pursue other mining interests. ***** MIDLANDS ELECTRICITY has appointed Mr Clive Parsons as human resources director from June 1. He is personnel director of British Gypsum. ***** COOPERS & LYBRAND DELOITTE has appointed Mr David Anton as partner-in-charge, national litigation support unit. ***** Mr Peter Kempster has been appointed head of the tax and trusts department at NABARRO NATHANSON, taking over from Mr Ron Gulliver who is retiring but remains a consultant. ***** TYNE TEES TELEVISION, independent television contractor for north east England, has appointed Mr Adrian Metcalfe as its director of programmes and Mr James Lancaster as director of resources. Mr Metcalfe joins Tyne Tees after a career at Channel 4 and Sky TV, and Mr Lancaster joins from ITN. ***** STREETS COMMUNICATIONS has appointed Mr Alex Glover to the board. He was with Valin Pollen. ***** CAMERON MARKBY HEWITT, City solicitors, has appointed aviation specialist Mr Tim Brymer as a partner. He joins from Brymer Marland & Co. Ms Julia Onslow-Cole, Ms Fiona O'Neill and Mr Stephen Foster have been promoted to partners. ***** Mr J. Stanley Mowatt has been appointed a non-executive director of the SETON HEALTHCARE GROUP. He was chairman and chief executive of Ultra Laboratories, recently acquired by Seton. He is chairman and majority shareholder of the Radwinter Group, and will continue as a director of Ultra. ***** Mr Brian Waghorn has been elected senior partner of SCRUTTON BLAND, Ipswich accountants, and Mr David Foster has been elected to the new post of managing partner. Mr Waghorn succeeds Mr Geoffrey Lockhart who retires but becomes a part-time consultant. Mr Andrew Strickland has joined as a partner in the Colchester office. He was with Grant Thornton. Mr David Verney has become a partner at Ipswich.
