930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Capital Gearing Tst net assets up 29% Capital Gearing Trust lifted net asset value per share by 29 per cent - from 343.3p to 443.5p - in the 12 months to April 5. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 11,000 (Pounds 30,000) for earnings of 0.49p (1.33p) per share. The single dividend for the year is raised to 0.43p. The previous year's distribution of 0.4p was accompanied by a special of 0.8p.
