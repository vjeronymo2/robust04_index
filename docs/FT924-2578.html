921211 FT 11 DEC 92 / International Company News: Inco takes Dollars 16m charge for cuts INCO, the world's second-biggest nickel producer, will take a USDollars 16m pre-tax charge in the fourth quarter in respect of 400 job cuts, writes Kenneth Gooding. The Toronto-based group estimated the move would reduce employment costs by an annual Dollars 22m. Inco previously took an Dollars 18.8m charge in the third quarter for an early-retirement programme.
