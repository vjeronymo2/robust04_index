Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The 27th Plenary Session of the Central Committee of the General Federation of Trade Union of Korea [GFTUK] was held in Pyongyang on 12 and 13 January. The plenary session was attended by members and candidate members of the GFTUK Central Committee and chairmen of the trade unions of major plants and enterprises in provinces and cities. The plenary discussed the task elucidated by the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song in this New Year's address, the trade union organizations' task to thoroughly implement the decision of the 21st plenary session of the Sixth party Central Committee, and the trade union organizations' task to thoroughly implement the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il's recent remarks. Chu Song-il, chairman of the GFTUK Central Committee, made a report on the first agenda item. Following this, discussions were held. The reporter and those who participated in the discussions stressed: In this New Year's address and in the 21st plenary session of the Sixth party Central Committee, the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song summed up the brilliant victory won by our people in socialist construction by firmly uniting around the party as one mind to repel all kinds of imperialists' challenges and, thus, safeguarding and defending socialism of our own style. They noted the fact that in the Third Seven-Year Plan period the might of our working class' and working people's loyalty to the party and the leader [suryong], that is, the might of the singlehearted unity of the leader, the party, and the masses was more clearly proven and that thanks to the promotion of the work of making the national economy chuche-oriented, modernized, and scientific the economic and material foundations of our own style socialism have been firmly deepened and the vitality of our self- reliant national economy has been demonstrated. Noting that in this period the GFTUK outstandingly performed the honorable mission and duty as the reliable supporter and defender of the party, the reporter and those who participated in the discussions mentioned the successes registered in the GFTUK's work under the wise leadership of the party. They stressed that today the trade union organizations are faced with the honorable work of contributing to safeguarding and defending socialism of our own style and to further adding luster to it. The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song has taught: We should consolidate and develop the successes already registered in socialist construction and achieve a new greater victory so that we cannot only add luster to the popular masses-centered socialism of our country, but also fly higher the banner of socialism of chuche. The reporter and those who participated in the discussions stressed that the trade union organizations at all levels should vigorously struggle to implement the party's strategies of pursuing the agriculture-first, light industry-first, and trade-first policies in socialist economic construction while designating two to three years to come as an readjusting period. Saying that the ideological revolution should be strengthened among trade union members, the reporter and those who participated in the discussions presented detailed measures to wage ideological indoctrination work, including indoctrination in the principles of the chuche idea, indoctrination in the greatness of the idea, indoctrination in the revolutionary traditions, and indoctrination in the class character, through various forms and methods and in a substantial manner. They then stressed that all trade union members should deeply master the greatness of the respected and beloved leader's and the leader comrade leader's ideologies and theories, the greatness of their leadership, and greatness of their features and should be loyal to the party and the leader [suryong] under all circumstances. They also stressed the need for trade union organizations to vigorously organize and mobilize working class and trade union members into the implementation of the party's revolutionary economic strategies. They noted that members of the trade unions at all levels should vigorously wage economic propaganda activities and carry out mass movements, including the socialist emulation movement, in a substantial manner, and, thus, should see to it that trade union members can vigorously advance in the spirit of adding the speed of the battle of Chollima so that all plants and enterprises can overfulfill this year's national economic plans. The reporter and those who participated in the discussions stressed the need for trade union members to contribute to further strengthening the singlehearted unity of the leader [suryong], the party, and the masses by enhancing the function and role of their organizations and to effect a new upsurge in socialist construction. A report and discussions were made on the second agenda items at the plenary session. The reporter and those who participated in the discussions noted that the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il has recently elucidated the task that public organizations should further grasp human remolding work and the methods for its implementation. They stressed that functionaries of all trade union organizations should properly carry out ideological indoctrination work among trade union members in conformity with specific circumstances. Saying that indoctrination to influence people by positive example is a mighty indoctrination method in line with the essence of the socialist society, the reporter and those who participated in the discussions stressed the need to strengthen indoctrination work through the examples set by forerunners in the laudable communist virtues. They also stressed that [words indistinct] should become a work with trade union members, the guidance of the organizational and ideological life of trade union members should be further strengthened, primary organizations of the GFTUK should be firmly built, and the movement to create model primary GFTUK organizations should be strengthened so that the ranks of trade unions can be further strengthened. The reporter and those who participated in the discussions noted that by cherishing boundless loyalty to the party and the leader [suryong] and more properly carrying out human remolding work in conformity with the demands of the developing realities, all trade union functionaries should inspire revolutionary zeal and creative positiveness among the masses so as to achieve high successes in the implementation of the decision of the 21st plenary session of the Sixth party Central Committee. The session adopted a relevant decision.
