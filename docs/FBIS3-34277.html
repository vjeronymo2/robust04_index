Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] An agent force [South Lebanese Army] raked both sides of the road between the al-Khiyam junction and al-Hasbani Bridge. Israeli forces stationed in the 'Ayn Qinya hills opened heavy machine-gun fire on Mimis Bridge and the surrounding woods for 20 minutes at dawn today. They fired flares over the area, causing fires in groves nearby. The Israeli artillery bombarded the al- Rayhan woods in the Jazzin area this morning and columns of smoke could be seen billowing in the area. Israeli military aircraft overflew the south, concentrating on al-Nabatiyah and Iqlim al-Tuffah, before midnight.
