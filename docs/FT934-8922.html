931112 FT 12 NOV 93 / International Capital Markets: Mexican group plans global offer GRUPO Financiero Serfin, the Mexican finance house, filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for a global offering of 6.5m American depository shares and 9m series L limited voting shares in Mexico, AP reports from Mexico. The company said it would offer the ADSs, each representing four series L shares, through an underwriting syndicate managed by CS First Boston, Morgan Stanley and Serfin Securities. The company said it would make the 9m L share offering through a Mexican syndicate in Mexico, and the Mexican government would sell 5m of the L shares. The offering price for the ADSs would be determined by the price for its series B shares on the Mexican stock exchange.
