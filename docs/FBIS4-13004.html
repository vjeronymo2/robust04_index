BFN [Text] The first vice president has called for a regional development organization in Khuzestan. Habibi, who attended the Khuzestan Province Administrative Council session last night, said that in view of the favorable circumstances in Khuzestan, this organization can accelerate the area's growth and progress. Various provincial officials briefed the session on the developments and problems of Khuzestan. The ministers of agriculture and of culture and Islamic guidance attended last night's session.
