Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] Warsaw, 25 Feb--The official visit to Poland by Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko, scheduled for 28 February-1 March, has been deferred and will take place at another time, the Foreign Ministry Press and Information Department reported today. The Foreign Ministry comunique adds that the visit has been deferred at the request of Minister Zlenko. A new date has not yet been decided upon.
