January 16, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

2ND CREDIT BRANCH OPENED BY TOYOTA

Citing a steady increase of business in the important Southern California marketplace, Toyota Motor Credit Corp. opened its second Orange County branch office here Monday.

The new office, at 140 S. State College Blvd., is the 29th TM Credit Corp. location in the United States. It will serve north Orange County, Riverside and San Bernardino counties and the southern and eastern portions of Los Angeles County.

About 155,200 of the 935,000 Toyotas sold in the United States in 1988 were sold by Southern California dealers, a company spokeswoman said.
