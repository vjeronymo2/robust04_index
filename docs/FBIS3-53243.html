Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Afghan prime minister, Mr. Golboddin Hekmatyar Khan, is no longer a member of the Borhanoddin Rabbani government. In a statement, the minister of state for foreign affairs, Dr. Najibollah Lafra'i, said by attempting the coup, Mr. Hekmatyar has brought about a situation in which it will be self-contradictory and incompatible to further consider him as being part of the government. The minister's statement was released by the Afghan Embassy in New Delhi.
