940530 FT 30 MAY 94 / Company News (This Week): Volkswagen - Other companies Bullish noises are expected from VW at its annual meeting on Wednesday in Hamburg. The group is bidding to break even this year (again). Much depends on the state of play with the Spanish subsidiary Seat, blamed for last year's DM1.8bn (Dollars 1.07bn) loss, and which is still restructuring.
