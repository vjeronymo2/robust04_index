BFN [Text] Romanian President Ion Iliescu has expressed satisfaction with his contacts with Greece's state and political leadership. In an exclusive statement to Athens radio, Iliescu stressed that Romania will reinforce and develop bilateral relations. On the issue of Skopje [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM], the Romanian president again expressed support for Greek positions. On the presence of Turkish peacekeepers in Bosnia-Herzegovina, Iliescu said that he does not judge the decisions of other governments and that his country has decided to respect the UN principle under which neighboring countries do not participate in such operations.
