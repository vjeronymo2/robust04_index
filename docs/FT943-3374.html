940914 FT 14 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Mid-States ahead Second quarter pre-tax profits at Mid-States, the US automotive parts distributor with a USM quote, increased by 16.8 per cent from Pounds 1.7m to Pounds 2m. Turnover for the three months to June 30 grew to Pounds 20.7m (Pounds 16.3m); earnings fell to 2.9p (3.1p) per share. The pre-tax outcome for the half year showed a 25 per cent advance to Pounds 3.27m (Pounds 2.61m). Turnover for the period totalled Pounds 36.8m (Pounds 30.9m). Earnings emerged at 4.8p (4.5p). There is no interim dividend this time against 0.75p in 1993.
