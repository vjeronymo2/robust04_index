BFN [Text] The first session of the new parliament has opened with procedures to elect speakers for the Chamber of Deputies and the Senate. The majority parties' candidates are Carlo Scognamiglio (Forza Italia) for the Senate, and Irene Pivetti (Northern League) for the Chamber of Deputies. Pivetti will be challenged by Anna Finocchiaro [Democratic Party of the Left], and Scognamiglio will be challenged by Spadolini, who is the opposition choice and officially became a candidate this morning. For the first two ballots in the Senate 164 votes are required. In the Chamber of Deputies two thirds of MP's -- ie. 420 -- must vote in the first three ballots.
