931007 FT 07 OCT 93 / International Company News: Coca-Cola unit to buy control of bottling group COCA-COLA Amatil, the stock market listed Australian snacks and beverage group in which Atlanta-based Coca-Cola holds a majority stake, plans to acquire control of an Indonesian bottling company, PT Djaya Beverage Bottling. It is paying about USDollars 46m, writes Nikki Tait in Sydney. DBBC is the Coca-Cola franchise bottler for Jakarta and adjacent parts of the Jakarta metropolitan area, one of the most heavily populated and prosperous centres in the region, and has about 30 per cent of Coca-Cola system sales in Indonesia. The deal is subject to regulatory approvals.
