930607 FT 07 JUN 93 / People: Procession of chief executives continues at Prestwick Wayne Osman has resigned as chief executive of Prestwick Holdings, the quoted Scottish printed circuit board maker. John Gilhooly, a senior figure in the electronics industry and deputy chairman of Albacom, becomes acting chief executive and deputy chairman. Osman, who has run Prestwick since 1990, joins the long list of former chief executives of Prestwick, a company which has only fleetingly seen a premium on its shares since it was listed in 1985. Osman, a former professional cricketer with long experience of the electronics industry, was once seen as the man to end a succession of false dawns and poor performances. Although turnover has increased recently profits have been poor.
