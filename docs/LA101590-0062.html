October 15, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

The Grove Shakespeare Festival raised $7,200 Saturday with two fund-raising events. A 16K "Walk for William" through Garden Grove drew 50 walkers and brought $4,900 in registration fees and pledges, and a sale at the Festival Amphitheatre of costumes, masks and props from past productions brought another $2,300.
