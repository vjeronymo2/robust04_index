920811 FT 11 AUG 92 / UK Company News: New Ireland static at IPounds 2.63m New Ireland Holdings, the Dublin-based composite insurance company ultimately controlled by UAP, a French insurance group, reported a static first six months with pre-tax profits of IPounds 2.63m (Pounds 2.47m), against IPounds 2.65m. Earnings per share were 9.6p (10.4p). An unchanged interim dividend of 3.3p is declared. The pre-tax profit included IPounds 1.53m (IPounds 1.32m) from general insurance but a lower IPounds 884,000 (IPounds 1.13m) return on investments.
