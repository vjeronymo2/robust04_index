BFN [Text] Cairo, 3 Apr (MENA)--Interior Minister Hasan al-Alfi has affirmed that the security agencies are keeping up their irreversible course in fighting terrorism without hesitation. He added that the Egyptian mass media have exposed what these groups are doing behind the scenes through the confessions of some of their members who came to their senses and returned to society's ranks. Speaking at a celebration at the police academy today to mark the day of learning, al-Alfi said that these groups have plenty of secret undertakings, which will soon be publicized to expose the truth about this misguided bunch. Al-Alfi added that the security agencies will not rest until they have eradicated the remnants of these terrorist elements who have turned terrorizing society into a profitable trade and way of life. He emphasized that the security agencies are finished with the policy of action and reaction and will take the initiative to eradicate these elements in their hideouts to protect society. During the celebration, excelling leaders and learners at the Interior Ministry were rewarded.
