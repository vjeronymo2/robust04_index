June 18, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

SUNDSTRAND DATA LEASES IN IRVINE SPECTRUM

Sundstrand Data Control Inc. has signed a 10-year, $6.5-million lease for 70,025 square feet in the O'Donnell Business Complex, 40 Parker Ave. in the Irvine Spectrum.

O'Donnell, Armstrong & Partners is developer of the complex. Sundstrand leased the space for its Trans Com Systems division.
