BFN [Text] Baku (May 16) TURAN: "We are on the threshold of a very important stage in the settlement of the Armenian-Azerbaijani conflict," said President Aliyev during yesterday's reception for a delegation from the US Department of State headed by the Coordinator for the Newly Independent States (NIS), James Collins. President Aliyev mentioned that Azerbaijan stands for a settlement of the conflict on the basis of the principles of the CSCE. He said that the representative of the CSCE Minsk Group must take part in Moscow's meeting of Defense Ministers between Azerbaijan, Armenia and Nagorno-Karabakh, which is scheduled for today. As far as the status of Nagorno-Karabakh is concerned, President Aliyev said that this matter must be discussed in the context of territorial integrity and inviolability of Azerbaijan's state borders. Touching upon the matter of the oil contracts, President Aliyev said that "the talks concerning the joint development of Azeri oil-fields have entered their final stage." According to several sources, an oil contract with the consortium of Western firms will be signed by the end of December. According to TURAN, the consortium of western companies is not ready for signing the contract in the near future.
