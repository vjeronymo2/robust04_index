January 21, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

JAPANESE LOBBY

If anyone had ever predicted that I'd concur with Buchanan on any issue, I would have shaken my head with a smile of disbelief.

I do, however, totally share his anger with the U.S. firms, universities, think tanks and ex-U.S. officials now serving as public relations representatives/lobbyists for Japanese interests in the United States.

Buchanan calls William Eberle, Charles Manatt, William Brock III, Robert Strauss and other kowtowers to the Rising Sun "mercenaries." I call them traitors.

MICHELE MOONEY

Los Angeles
