August 27, 1990, Monday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: SIMI VALLEY;

EARLY SIGN-UP URGED FOR KINDERGARTNERS

To avoid a last-minute rush, Simi Valley Unified School District officials are encouraging parents whose children will be entering kindergarten during the 1990-91 school year to pre-register.

Parents can pre-register their children by appointment at each of the district's 19 elementary schools. Children eligible for kindergarten must be 5 years old on or before Dec. 2, 1990. Parents should bring their child's birth certificate and proof of immunization to the school the youngster will be attending, she said.

Parents can call 526-0200 to make appointments. School begins Sept. 5. CARLOS V. LOZANO
