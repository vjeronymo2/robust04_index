Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The minister of state for home, Mr. P.M. Sayeed, has said that the Pakistani prime minister, Ms. Benazir Bhutto, is accusing India only to divert the people's attention from domestic problems. Speaking to newsmen at Thiruvananthapuram today, he said that Kashmir is an integral part of India and the government will initiate political process in the valley as soon as the law and order situation returns to normal. Meanwhile, the minister of state for external affairs, Mr. Salman Khurshid, has criticized the Pakistani prime minister's propaganda offensive against India.
