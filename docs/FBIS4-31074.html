BFN [Text] The Albanian authorities have arrested 25 citizens of the FRY charged with organized smuggling of oil across Skadar Lake. Those arrested belong to a group of Montenegrins from Zeta who were shipping oil to Montenegro. The Albanian authorities arrested them after supervision of cross-border traffic was stepped up in line with UN observers' demands. As of Friday Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] had also stepped up customs control measures, especially concerning fuel.
