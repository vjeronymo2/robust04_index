BFN [Unattributed report: "The Commander in Chief Calls a Conference"] [Text] A two-day operational conference with the leadership of Ukraine's executive power structures was held under the chairmanship of supreme commander in chief of Ukraine's Armed Forces Ukraine's President Leonid Kravchuk. The main task of the conference was to deepen the knowledge of representatives of the state administration in questions of the military building and strengthening of the country's defense capability. As reported by the Press Service of the Ministry of Defense, the Ukrainian president pointed out in his speech that every leader must find and clearly comprehend his place and role in the system of state security. Ukraine's Armed Forces and the state apparatus must be constantly ready to defend Ukraine. its sovereignty, and territorial integrity -- this is the demand of our time. At the same time, Leonid Kravchuk stressed that political passions, which presently rage in Ukraine, must remain outside the Army, whose main task is to maintain the state's combat readiness and inviolability of its borders. The Ukrainian president drew the audience's particular attention to the need to make every effort in order to raise the authority of the military service and to elaborate a package of legislative acts, which would speed up the army's transition to the contractual basis of recruitment. Ukraine's minister of defense Army General Vitaliy Radetskyy delivered a speech on the concept of the building and development of Ukraine's Armed Forces for the period of up to the year 2010. On the second day of the conference, its participants visited the military training center "Desna," where they examined new samples of technology and weapons.
