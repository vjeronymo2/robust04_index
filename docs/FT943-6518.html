940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / Arts (Openings): Wigmore Hall The 1994/5 season opens with a starry quartet of singers coming together for a light-hearted evening, featuring Brahms's favourite 'Liebeslieder Walzer'. Barbara Bonney leads the quartet, which includes Anne Sofie von Otter, Kurt Streit and Olaf Bar. Among the other singers appearing during September are three eminent Americans - Dawn Upshaw, Jennifer Larmore and June Anderson.
