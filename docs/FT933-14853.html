930712 FT 12 JUL 93 / International Company News: Bank of Bermuda Group Bank of Bermuda Group has agreed to acquire the institutional trust business of Standard Chartered Bank's Equitor Group in Hong Kong and Singapore, increasing its Asia Pacific assets to about USDollars 7bn, Reuter reports. Standard Chartered Equitor has about USDollars 3bn of institutional assets, and Bank of Bermuda has USDollars 4bn in assets in the area, the bank said. The acquisition raises total funds under administration to more than USDollars 28bn from USDollars 25bn, the bank said.
