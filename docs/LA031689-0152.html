March 16, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: BRENTWOOD;

ACTRESS TO LEAD PARADE

Brentwood's honorary mayor, actress Hope Lange, will lead a parade and festival in the community May 28.

"A Salute to Youth" is the theme of the annual Brentwood Memorial Day Parade and Festival, featuring veterans and military units, bands and drill teams, fraternal organizations, school youth groups and entertainment and media celebrities.

The two-hour parade will travel along San Vicente Boulevard from 26th Street to the festival area near Barrington.

Additional information is available through the Brentwood Chamber of Commerce, (213) 552-0220.
