941126 FT 26 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Stoddard Sekers more than trebled Interim pre-tax profits at Stoddard Sekers International, the carpet and furnishing fabric manufacturer, more than tripled from Pounds 167,000 to Pounds 565,000. Operating profits increased from Pounds 583,000 to Pounds 963,000 in the six months to September 30, on turnover 6 per cent ahead at Pounds 29.2m (Pounds 27.4m). Mr Hugh Laughland, chairman, said the group's opportunities in the contract and export carpet markets had shown a 'gratifying improvement'. Earnings per share emerged at 0.7p (0.1p) and the interim dividend is held at 0.75p.
