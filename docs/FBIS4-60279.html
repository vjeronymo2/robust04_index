BFN [Text] Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat has appointed Mrs. Fatimah Barnawi, a most veteran member of Fatah, as commander of the Palestinian policewomen force, which will be established soon. He also asked her to supervise the formation of the Palestinian policewomen force. Israel had sentenced Barnawi to 42 years in prison, and then had her deported in 1977 after serving 10 years of her sentence.
