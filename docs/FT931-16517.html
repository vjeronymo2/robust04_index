930106 FT 06 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Stonehill shares suspended Shares in Stonehill Holdings, the furniture maker and property investment group, were temporarily suspended at 11p yesterday. The company said it was in discussions which could lead to a material transaction. Stonehill has incurred pre-tax losses for the past three years. In the year to March 31 1992 the deficit was cut from Pounds 1.13m to Pounds 387,000 on turnover of Pounds 2.37m (Pounds 1.6m).
