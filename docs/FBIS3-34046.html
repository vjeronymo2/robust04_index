Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Busek Inaugurates Professorship"] [Text] Jerusalem -- On Wednesday [2 February], during his visit to Israel, Vice Chancellor Erhard Busek met with Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin for a talk and for the signing of a scientific-technological cooperation agreement. As Vice Chancellor Busek stressed, during the meeting Rabin pointed out the "international responsibility for peace in the Middle East." The international community, which has called for peace for years, must now also participate in the reconstruction of the Palestinian territories. In the afternoon Busek inaugurated the "Franz-Cardinal-Koenig Professorship for Austrian Studies" at the Hebrew University of Jerusalem. Previously Busek was the first Austrian politician to lay a wreath at the grave of Theodor Herzl, the father of political Zionism, and visited the Yad Vashem memorial for the 6 million victims of the Holocaust.
