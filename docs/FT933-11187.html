930730 FT 30 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Celtic International Celtic International, a small Irish general insurer, is to become part of Eureko, an alliance of four European companies. AVCB of the Netherlands (32 per cent), Friends Provident (31 per cent), Topdanmark of Denmark (14 per cent) and WASA of Sweden (23 per cent), formed the alliance in 1992. Eureko is currently represented in 10 European countries. Eureko has acquired the entire share capital of Galway-based Celtic. Friends Provident's Irish subsidiary will also be transferred to Eureko. Celtic reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.1m in 1992.
