August 21, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

ON THE SIDELINES;

MCMAHON TO START AGAINST 49ERS

Newly acquired quarterback Jim McMahon will start San Diego's exhibition game Wednesday night at San Francisco, Chargers Coach Dan Henning said.

San Diego acquired the eight-year veteran last Friday from the Chicago Bears for a conditional 1990 draft choice. McMahon took the first four snaps of the second half against his former teammates on Saturday in a game the Chargers won 24-7 at Chicago.

While the rest of the Chargers relaxed before Sunday afternoon's practice, McMahon spent four hours with quarterback Coach Ted Tollner preparing for the San Francisco game.
