BFN [Text] Sanaa, May 5 (AFP) -- The Yemeni parliament decided to dismiss Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid and other southern leaders, blaming them for the outbreak of civil war on Thursday [5 May], the official news agency SABA said. The parliament, meeting in Sanaa in the absence of southern deputies, ruled that "any action or decision taken by 'Ali Salim al- Bid or his supporters is from now on considered illegal and not binding."
