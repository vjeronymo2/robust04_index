January 31, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

PREP ROUNDUP;

NEWPORT CHRISTIAN CAN'T STAY WITH ST. MARGARET'S

For a little more than a quarter, Newport Christian High School matched baskets with St. Margaret's, the third-ranked team in the Southern Section Small Schools' poll.

But reality hit with a thud.

After a slow start, St. Margaret's defeated Newport Christian, 71-33, Tuesday in an Academy League game at the 15th Street Gym in Newport Beach. It was the 11th consecutive victory for St. Margaret's (14-4, 7-0 in league).

Newport Christian (5-9, 2-5) kept pace until one minute into the second quarter. Led by center Ryan Westendorf, St. Margaret's went on a 24-7 run to close the quarter.

Westendorf, a 6-foot-7 junior, scored 21 of his game-high 25 points in the first half. He made 10 of 14 shots in the first half.

Westendorf was 12 of 23 for the game and also had 21 rebounds. CHRIS FOSTER
