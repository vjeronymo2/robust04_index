Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Radio dispatch from Geneva] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] At 2200 yesterday, Damascus time, [Syrian] Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' held a meeting with U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher. The meeting was attended on the Syrian side by Muwaffaq al-'Allaf, head of the Syrian side to the peace talks, and Walid al-Mu'allim, Syrian ambassador in Washington. The talks during the meeting dealt with the peace process and the upcoming meeting between President Hafiz al-Asad and U.S. President Bill Clinton. The president of the Swiss Confederation held a news conference at the Press Center yesterday in which he spoke about his meeting with President Hafiz al-Asad. He expressed his country's happiness at hosting the meeting between the Syrian and U.S. presidents. Swiss President Stich expressed hope that the al-Asad-Clinton summit today will produce results and achieve progress in the Middle East peace process. President Stich said: We had the opportunity to listen to President Hafiz al-Asad's view regarding many issues in the region. [passage omitted]
