May 15, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

Jeanine Kabrich has joined Orange County Newswatch on KDOC-TV Channel 56 as a reporter and fill-in anchor. A former reporter for the Armed Services Network in West Germany and a graduate of Emerson College in Boston, she joins anchors Michelle Merker and Pat Matthews, reporter-anchor Christoper Heinbaugh and reporter Beth Bingham on the staff of the half-hour program shown weekdays at 6 p.m.
