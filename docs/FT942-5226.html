940606 FT 06 JUN 94 / Business Travel: Canadian switch Canadian Airlines International has now switched its London- Toronto daily service from Gatwick to Heathrow, writes Bernard Simon in Toronto. Canadian, in which American Airlines recently bought a 33 per cent stake, wanted the Heathrow slot largely to cater for travellers with connecting flights to and from eastern Europe, Africa and Asia. Its flights to Vancouver and Calgary will continue to operate from Gatwick for now.
