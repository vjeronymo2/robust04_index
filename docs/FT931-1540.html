930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / World News in Brief: Austria refuses to accept any more Bosnian refugees Austria, currently host to 60,000 refugees from Bosnia, will no longer accept asylum-seekers from the region, interior minister Franz Loeschnak said. 'I cannot perceive Austria accepting any more refugees in the near future,' he added. 'One part of Europe acts as if the consequences of the war in the Balkans only affected the directly bordering states.'
