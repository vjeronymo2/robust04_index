BFN [Text] Five deputy ministers and other Colombian officials from various sectors will begin talks today with their Cuban counterparts as part of the preliminary meeting of the binational mixed commission that will be in progress until 15 April. Representing Colombia will be the deputy ministers of education, health, foreign affairs, and communications, in addition to the directors of the sports, culture, civil aviation, and training service institutions. The Colombian delegation is also made up of the National University rector, the director of the Tourism Corporation, and other high-ranking officials from the Ministries of Finance, Foreign Trade, Environment, Foreign Affairs, and the Planning Institute.
