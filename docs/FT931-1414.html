930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / International Company News in Brief: JVC to sell stake in video unit to UK group VICTOR Company of Japan (JVC), the Japanese consumer electronics group, has agreed to sell half of its 50 per cent stake in CIC Victor Video to CIC-Video of the UK in April, Reuter reports from Tokyo. The sale gives CIC-Video 75 per cent of CIC-Victor Video, a joint venture of MCA and Paramount Communications, which markets video tapes in Japan of films made by the two US film studios.
