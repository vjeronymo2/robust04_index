BFN [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk has issued a decree rescinding the edicts by the president of Crimea on the acting chairman of the Crimea Republic Security Service, and on terminating the plenary powers of the acting chairman of the Crimea Republic Security Service as such that do not comply with the Constitution of Ukraine and the laws of our state in this sphere. The decree has been issued in connection with the appointment by the president of Crimea of Volodymyr Lepikhov to the post of acting chairman of the Crimea Republic Security Service and the termination of the plenary powers of Ivan Kolomyytsev, acting chairman of the Crimea Security Service. The document emphasizes that the edicts by the president of Crimea violate the relevant laws of Ukraine, according to which the chairman of the Security Service of Crimea is appointed and dismissed.
