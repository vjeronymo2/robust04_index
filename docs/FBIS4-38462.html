BFN [From the "Business SNARK file] [Text] Baku [dateline as received]-- The bill on Azerbaijan Republic state budget for 1994 has been approved by Milli Majlis on May 26. According to the document, the income part of budget is planned at a level of 138 billion manats, while the expenditure will be at amount of 181 billion manats. Thus the deficit will make nearly 43 billion manats, which is 7 percent of GNP. The expected inflation level by the end of the year may reach 360 percent. The following allocations are planned in the budget: 13 percent--for economy purposes; 5.38 percent--for industrial engineering; 1.2 percent--for science; 9.6 percent--for control bodies; 7.3 percent--for servicing of international debts and credits; 10 percent--for needs of power and law enforcement structures.
