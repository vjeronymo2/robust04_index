921016 FT 16 OCT 92 / International Company News: Cimpor lifts its holding in CN to 97.7% CIMENTOS de Portugal (Cimpor), the state-owned Portuguese cement producer facing privatisation, has lifted its holding in Corporacion Noroeste (CN), a Spanish cement group, to 97.7 per cent. The three-stage acquisition cost Esc40bn (Dollars 307m). Last month Cimpor bought a 24.9 per cent stake. It later reached agreement for the purchase of a further 25.7 per cent. Cimpor has also undertaken to buy 10.4 per cent of Cementos Cosmos, another Spanish cement company, in which CN has a major shareholding.
