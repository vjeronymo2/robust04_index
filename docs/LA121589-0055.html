December 15, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

Sallinen Premiere Expected in L.A.: Peter Hemmings, general director of Los Angeles Music Center Opera, is expected to announce the world premiere today of Aulis Sallinen's fourth opera, "Kullervo," at the Music Center in early 1992. Hemmings will make the announcement in Helsinki, Finland, jointly with Ilkka Kuusisto, general manager of Finnish National Opera, which will produce the work at the Dorothy Chandler Pavilion, Feb. 22-March 1, 1992, in association with LAMCO. The Finnish contingent will furnish the leading singers -- led by baritone Jorma Hynninen, who is artistic director of the Finnish company -- the chorus and conductor Ulf Soderblom. The composer has fashioned his own libretto for "Kullervo," loosely basing it on the play "Kullervo" by the 19th-Century Finnish playwright Aleksis Kivi. SHAUNA SNOW
