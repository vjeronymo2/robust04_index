BFN [Text] Yerevan ("YERKIR" daily - Haylour), 06/10/94 -- Official delegation of the Russian Defense Ministry, headed by the Chairman of the Operative Department General-Colonel V. Varennikov visited Gyumri on June 9. During a short interview in the airport Varennikov said, the goal of the visit is to resolve several organizational problems related with stationing of Russian units. General-Colonel noted that on the very first day of the visit P. Grachev discussed the Russian project on peaceful resolution of the Karabakh problem with the President Levon Ter-Petrosyan, who positively evaluated the plan. "In fact, L. Ter-Petrosyan accepted all our proposals", he stressed. The delegation returned to Yerevan on the same day.
