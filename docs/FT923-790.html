920926 FT 26 SEP 92 / IMF gets bevy of members THE IMF took a step towards its goal of global membership this week as the republics of the former Soviet Union lined up, George Graham writes from Washington. But the IMF's 173rd and newest member is a republic of much older vintage: the tiny mountain enclave of San Marino. San Marino had something in common with Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan and Azerbaijan, which just beat it to the tape. From 1978 to 1986 it boasted the only communist government in western Europe.
