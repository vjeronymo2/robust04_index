940913 FT 13 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Trans Union offer for UAPT lapses Trans Union's offer for UAPT-Infolink, the British credit reference company, has lapsed. Although originally recommended by the UAPT board, the Trans Union offer was superseded by a higher Pounds 51m offer from Equifax, a US credit data group, which has had 31 per cent acceptance so far.
