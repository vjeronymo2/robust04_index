July 29, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS: ALPHA MICROSYSTEMS NAMES V.P. FOR FINANCE

David A. Young has been named vice president for finance and chief financial officer of Alpha Microsystems.

Before joining the Santa Ana computer products company, he was corporate controller of California Industrial Products.

Young's previous positions include vice president for finance and administration, chief financial officer and secretary and treasurer of San/Bar Corp., and vice president of finance and administration, chief financial officer and secretary of MSI Data Corp.
