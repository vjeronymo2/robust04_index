BFN [Text] A responsible military source at the command of the armed forces of the Democratic Republic of Yemen has said that the forces of the adventurous al-Ahmar gang not only failed to adhere to the cease-fire announced on evening of 20 May, but had in fact escalated the fighting on all fronts. It unleashed large forces in all directions. More serious than this, it used rockets in its criminal acts. Last night, the inhabitants of the quiet and peaceful city of Aden, which was celebrating the declaration of the Democratic Republic of Yemen and the blessed 'Id al-Adha, were surprised by a rocket which fell in a heavily populated area killing and wounding peaceful women and children. Meanwhile, the air defense forces of the Democratic Republic of Yemen succeeded in shooting down a similar rocket in the air. The responsible source confirmed that this was a criminal act and was part of the series of the terrible crimes of the family of al-Ahmar, under the leadership of war general 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. The armed forces of the Democratic Republic of Yemen reserve for themselves the right to retaliate forcefully against these stupid and crazy acts of the bloody al-Ahmar gang.
