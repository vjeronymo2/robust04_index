BFN [Text] Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat met with Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres this morning on the sidelines of the conference of countries extending aid to the Palestinians which is being held in Bucharest. Peres said after the meeting that Chairman 'Arafat was concerned during the meeting about a number of issues pertaining to the implementation of Palestinian self-rule in Gaza and Jericho. 'Arafat also met in Bucharest with the Romanian president and discussed the obstacles facing the peace process in the Middle East. 'Arafat held talks in Moscow yesterday with Russian President Boris Yeltsin on the relations between Russia and the PLO and the developments of the peace process in the Middle East.
