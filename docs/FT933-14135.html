930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / International Company News: LA Gear worse than expected LA GEAR, the US footwear and clothing maker in the red since the end of 1990, yesterday posted a bigger-than-expected second quarter loss. For the three months to May 31, the company had a net deficit of Dollars 13.2m, or 57 cents a share, against a deficit of Dollars 24.4m, or Dollars 1.19, a year earlier. The 1992 results included an after-tax charge of Dollars 13.9m to settle litigation.
