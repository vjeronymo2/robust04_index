930607 FT 07 JUN 93 / China rules out big devaluation China has ruled out a big devaluation of the official yuan exchange rate and denied rumours of plans to abolish foreign exchange certificates for foreigners, Reuter reports from Beijing. An official with the State Administration of Exchange Control said last week's move to lift price ceilings at forex swap centres - which led to a sharp drop of the yuan against the dollar on those markets - did not mean Beijing was planning further large currency adjustments.
