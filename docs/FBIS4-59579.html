BFN [Text] The Imam Khomeyni Relief Committee and the Zagreb Merhamet [Charity] Foundation will be cooperating in giving aid to Muslims in Bosnia-Herzegovina. In a meeting with the Imam Khomeyni Relief Committee, the head and other officials of the Merhamet Foundation, praising the Islamic Republic of Iran's extensive assistance given to the people of Bosnia, called for cooperation in the fields of agriculture, livestock rearing, and setting up of carpet-weaving workshops for women in Bosnia-Herzegovina. The Imam Khomeyni Relief Committee supervisor declared readiness for all-round assistance for Bosnian Muslims and the exchange of experiences gained in relief work done for the deprived in that country.
