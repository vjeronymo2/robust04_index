Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Feb 17 (A.A) -- Iranian Embassy in Ankara denied on Thursday [17 February] that one of the outlawed separatist terrorist PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] organization leaders, Osman Ocalan, was in Iran. Several Turkish newspapers earlier said Osman Ocalan was in Iran and Turkey and Iran was bargaining on his return. The embassy in a written statement said several secret forces aimed at spoiling the two countries' relations.
