October 7, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

HIGH SCHOOL FOOTBALL

Avocado League

Oceanside 17, El Camino 16 -- Cesar Garcia kicked a 30-yard field goal with 36 seconds remaining to lift Oceanside (2-3, 1-1).

Garcia, Oceanside's quarterback, also scored a touchdown on a one-yard run. Marlon Jenkins rushed for 167 yards on 20 carries and had a 79-yard scoring run. Von Robinson scored twice for El Camino.

Desert-Coastal League

Army-Navy 14, Mountain Empire 7 -- Ron Allen rushed 18 times for 100 yards and a 28-yard touchdown to lift Army-Navy (4-0, 1-0). Mountain Empire is 1-4, 0-1.
