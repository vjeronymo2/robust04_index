920615 FT 15 JUN 92 / International Company News: FT-SE Eurotrack 100 Index The FT-SE Eurotrack steering committee has agreed to make the following changes to the FT-SE Eurotrack 100 Index constituent list with effect from June 22 1992: Additions: Asea A (Sweden); Investor B (Sweden); Pryca (Spain); SKF B (Sweden); Michelin B (France). Deletions: Thomson-CSF (France); Pernod-Ricard (France); Banco di Roma (Italy); Ciba-Geigy Bearer (Switzerland); Euro Disney (France). The indicative reserve list now comprises: Bancario San Paolo (Italy); Thyssen (Germany); Nestle participation certificates (Switzerland); Euro Disney (France); Elsevier (Netherlands). With effect from today opening hours for the FT-SE Eurotrack 100 and 200 indices will move from 9:45 to 10am, in line with the change in opening hours for the Spanish sector.
