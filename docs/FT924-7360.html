921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / Observer: Punters Cambridge University's recently-launched glossy 'Cam' magazine is no doubt an attempt to stoke up US-style loyalty to the alma mater so that it can milk its richer alumni for contributions. The question is whether some of the more distinguished potential recipients from the worlds of business and finance will have much money to spare. An advertisement at the back of the latest issue plugs an invaluable tome for that same audience: 'How to survive an Inland Revenue investigation'.
