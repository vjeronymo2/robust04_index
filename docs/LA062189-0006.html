June 21, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

FANG LIZHI

We have ample precedent for sheltering Fang Lizhi and his wife, Li Shuxian, in the U.S. Embassy at Beijing. For 15 years -- from 1956 to 1971 -- the U.S. gave refuge to Hungarian Cardinal Jozsef Mindszenty in the U.S. Embassy at Budapest.

Don't give up Fang and Li!

LEO E. PERSELLIN

Rancho Palos Verdes
