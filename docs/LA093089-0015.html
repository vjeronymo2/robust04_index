September 30, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

A LOGICAL EXPLANATION OF WHY A PITCHER LOSES

If Fred Claire of the Dodgers was named baseball's executive of the year in 1988 for trading Pedro Guerrero for John Tudor, what does that make the general manager of the St. Louis Cardinals?

After all, any fool can see they got the best end of the deal.

SERVANDO BANUELOS, Pasadena
