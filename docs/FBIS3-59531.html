Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Belgium: SNECMA To Recapitalise Belgian Subsidiary"; as released by Brussels Textline database] [Text] Brussels, 17/12/1993 (AGENCE EUROPE) -- The French public group SNECMA has been authorized to participate in the recapitalisation of its Belgian subsidiary Techspace Aero (formerly FN [National Factory] Moteurs) to the tune of 510 million Belgian francs. SNECMA will thus retain its 51% share in its Belgian subsidiary. The latter will reduce its capital to cover losses, before proceeding with a capital increase.
