March 11, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

ARCHBISHOP'S JET HELICOPTER

While we are fully aware of the good works, and donations, made by Richard Riordan and his contemporaries, we feel compelled to make a comment on the gift of a helicopter to Archbishop Mahony.

Assuming that the archbishop will use it to "avoid traffic congestion," we find it ironic that just last month the parents of students in one of the poorest parish schools in Los Angeles were told that their monthly tuition needs to be raised by $15 a month next school year. The parents, many of whom work two and three jobs now, were told that we all need to make sacrifices for the sake of education. Where is the sacrifice on the part of the church leadership?

SISTER CAROL BURCHARDS, S.M.

SISTER CRISTINA CABALLERO, S.M.

Burbank
