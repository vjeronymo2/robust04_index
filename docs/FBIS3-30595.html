Language: Slovene Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Croatia will have paid off 14 million German marks of its debt to the Krsko nuclear power station by 5 February. The remainder of the debt will be settled by 1 August. If the neighboring country keeps to this agreement, then the extraordinary circumstances surrounding the supply of electricity to Croatia should have ended by the end of this week. Economics Ministers Maks Tajnikar and Nada Vidosevic agreed this at a meeting this morning in Otocec. [passage omitted]
