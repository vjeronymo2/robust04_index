Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] A short while ago we received via fax a copy of the following open letter addressed today by the Social Democracy Party of Romania [PDSR] to the chairmen of the PNTCD [National Peasant Christian-Democratic Party], PD-FSN [Democratic Party-National Salvation Front], UDMR [Democratic Union of Hungarians in Romania], PL '93 [Liberal Party '93], PAC [Civic Alliance Party], and PSDR [Romanian Social-Democratic Party]: Being aware of the virtues of dialogue, I reiterate the readiness of the Social Democracy Party of Romania, which I also expressed in a letter I sent to you at the beginning of last year, to initiate general discussions among our parties. Hoping that you also agree to that idea, we are expecting your suggestions regarding the date, agenda, and the representation level of the delegations that would engage in those discussions. Please accept my high esteem. The letter is signed by PDSR Chairman Oliviu Gherman.
