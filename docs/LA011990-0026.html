January 19, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

SCEcorp: The parent firm of Southern California Edison Co. posted 1989 profit of $778 million, about the same as the previous year. Revenue totaled $6.9 billion, up 10%. The Rosemead firm attributed results in part to an increase in earnings at its non-utility subsidiaries, which more than offset a slight decline in utility earnings.
