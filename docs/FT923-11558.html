920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / New oilfield identified AMERADA Hess, the oil company, said yesterday a test well it had drilled on behalf of three other licence partners on North Sea block 15/21 had penetrated a thick column of oil. The well flowed at a rate of 5,880 barrels a day of light crude. The partnership, which includes Deminex UK, Kerr-McGee Oil and Pict Petroleum, plans to call the field Perth.
