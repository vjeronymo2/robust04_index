June 8, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: BEVERLY HILLS;

CHAMBER PRESIDENT NAMED

Charles Reilly has been elected president of the Beverly Hills Chamber of Commerce and Civic Assn.

Reilly, 46, is managing general partner of Shamrock Investments in Century City. He and newly elected members of the chamber's executive committee and of the board of directors will be installed on June 15 at the Annual Past Presidents Dinner at the Beverly Hills Hotel.
