Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] President Lucas Mangope of Bophuthatswana is to recommend at an extraordinary meeting of Parliament in Mmabatho that the homeland participates in the election. [passage omitted] State President F. W. de Klerk has welcomed the decision by President Mangope, saying this meant that Bophuthatswana could now give its full support to the electoral process. Addressing a news conference in Pretoria, President de Klerk said that the decision removed the main reason for the current unrest in Bophuthatswana.
