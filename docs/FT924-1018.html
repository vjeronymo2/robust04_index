921221 FT 21 DEC 92 / World News in Brief: European Monetary System The French franc remained at the bottom of the European exchange rate mechanism despite the fundamental strength of the French economy. Some selling last week was countered by intervention from the French central bank which, by the end of the week, appeared to have headed off the threat of devaluation. However, most economists are convinced that speculators will return in the new year to test the government's commitment to the currency. Meanwhile the Irish punt and the Danish krone remained under pressure, though the krone received support from the Belgian and Dutch central banks.
