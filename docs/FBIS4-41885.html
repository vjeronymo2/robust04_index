BFN [Editorial Report] Moscow Ostankino Television First Channel Network in Russian at 1100 GMT on 28 June carries a report on an interview with Indian Prime Minister Narasimha Rao by correspondent Georgiy Kirichatyy in New Delhi. Regarding Indian-Russian relations, Kirichatyy quotes Prime Minister Rao as saying, "Relations between Russia and India have always been good, but especially since President Yeltsin's visit to our country last year. We managed to resolve a number of the most difficult questions which arose as a result of the changes taking place in Russia. We have now reached the stage where we have the opportunity to make faster progress. I hope my visit and the various agreements to be signed will give new impetus to the development of Russo-Indian ties. Russia and India need each other." "They need to work together."
