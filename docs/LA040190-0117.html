April 1, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

WESTLAKE'S EBY TO ATTEND UCLA

Westlake High's Mike Eby gave an oral commitment to UCLA on Friday after limiting Channel Islands to one hit in the Warriors' 1-0 Marmonte League victory.

Eby, a 6-foot-2, 190-pound senior left-hander, has a career record of 16-7 at Westlake and holds five school records. Eby is 4-0 this season with an earned-run average of 0.77. He narrowed his choices to Cal State Northridge and UCLA.
