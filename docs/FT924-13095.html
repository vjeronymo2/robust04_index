921017 FT 17 OCT 92 / Swiss doubts on trade bloc Nearly a quarter of Swiss voters are uncertain how to vote in a referendum in December on whether Switzerland should join the European Economic Area (EEA) trade bloc, according to a poll published yesterday, Reuter reports from Zurich. The survey, in the Berner Zeitung, showed 42 per cent would vote in favour, with 36 per cent against and 22 per cent undecided.
