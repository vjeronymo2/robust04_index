BFN [Summary] Kiev, 20 Apr -- Ukraine's deputy minister of the economy, Yuriy Yekhanurov, informed journalists at a meeting of the Market Reform Press Club about the results of Ukraine's economic activity in the first quarter of this year. He said the decline in production amounts to 38.4 percent and expenditure from the state budget exceeds income by 5 trillion karbovantsi. There are currently 93,100 unemployed persons in Ukraine, while so-called hidden unemployment is about 4 million persons. He also said that small businesses manufactured 1.4-times more products in the first quarter of 1994 than in the corresponding period of last year.
