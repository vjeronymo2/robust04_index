940421 FT 21 APR 94 / UK Company News: Allied-Lyons ALLIED-LYONS has had a 92.9 per cent take-up of its rights issue, with acceptances received in respect of 127m of the 137m stock units offered. The remaining 9.73m stock units have been subscribed for at 320p per unit. The net proceeds of approximately 74.3p per unit will be distributed to shareholders.
