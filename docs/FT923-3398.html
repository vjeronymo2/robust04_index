920912 FT 12 SEP 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG was supported by news that the ANC would hold talks with the government following violence in Ciskei earlier this week. The overall index put on 16 to 3,064 and the industrial index added 27 to 4,018. Gold finished 4 higher at 861 in spite of a weaker bullion price. De Beers lost 50 cents to R53.50.
