920609 FT 09 JUN 92 / International Company News: H-P unveils small disk drive HEWLETT-Packard, the computer and electronics group, unveiled the smallest hard disk drive yet available, targeted at portable electronic products, AP-DJ reports. H-P said it expected the 1.3-inch drive to be used initially in hand-held computers. The new drive was developed through a joint effort with the AT&T Microelectronics, a unit of American Telephone & Telegraph and Citizen Watch of Japan, which will manufacture the product.
