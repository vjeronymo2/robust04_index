930313 FT 13 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Thomas Walker Pounds 71,400 in loss Thomas Walker, the Birmingham-based maker of metal smallwares, incurred a loss of Pounds 71,400 pre-tax for the six months ended December 31. That compared with previous profits of Pounds 2,600 and was scored from a turnover of Pounds 1.7m (Pounds 1.73m). Losses per share emerged at 1.1591p (0.0535p) and the interim dividend is the same at 0.18p.
