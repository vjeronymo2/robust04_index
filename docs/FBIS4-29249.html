BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 15 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il extended thanks to an official and different units that had distinguished themselves in displaying the beautiful tradition of army-civilian unity. His thanks went to the Korea joint-venture bank, Yanggang Hotel, Pyongyang University of Railways, the national training centre of the State Commission of Physical Culture and Sports, the soy and edible oil direct sales store in Pyongyang, the Mangyongdae District fuel supply base and Pak Kyong-mi, a reporter with the Inmin Pogon Office. In the Democratic People's Republic of Korea today the drive for aiding the People's Army is going on briskly as a social movement and, through it, the Army and civilians form an integrated whole, hardening their determination to resolutely defend the party and the leader and safeguard the Korean-style socialist system.
