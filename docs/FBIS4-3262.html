BFN [Announcer-read report from the "National News Hookup" program] [Text] A nationwide check on the enforcement of the environmental protection law for 1994 will formally begin in May. Wang Bingqian and Song Jian will head the general inspection group. Major inspection work for this year will be carried out in Hebei, Inner Mongolia, Jilin, Zhejiang, Fujian, Henan, Hunan, Guangxi, Sichuan, and Guizhou. Inspection teams will mainly carry out inspections in those provinces on the implementation of the environmental protection law, atmospheric pollution prevention law, water pollution law, wildlife animal protection law, ecology-related laws and regulations, and laws and regulations formulated by the State Council.
