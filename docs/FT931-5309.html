930306 FT 06 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Murray European net assets rise Murray European Investment Trust, managed in Glasgow by Murray Johnstone, raised net asset value by 10.3 per cent to 46.3p at the end of 1992, against 42p a year earlier. Net revenue in 1992 fell from Pounds 96,862 to Pounds 68,749, after an exceptional charge this time of Pounds 77,492 for professional fees incurred in respect of a change of investment manager during the year. Earnings per share were 0.23p (0.32p) and the final dividend is increased to 0.16p (0.15p).
