BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 13 (KCNA) -- Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, Sunday met and had a friendly talk with the delegation of the Japan-Korea Friendship Exchange Association of Agricultural Farmers headed by Masao Yoshida, former Social-Democratic member of the house of representatives of Japan and chairman of the association. The head of the delegation said that during his stay in Korea he witnessed the achievements made by the Korean people in socialist construction under the banner of the chuche idea, closely rallied around the great leader His Excellency President Kim Il-song and the dear leader His Excellency Kim Chong-il. Noting that Korea has made great achievements in agriculture by implementing the rural theses, he said he saw the peaceful looks of farmers of the northern half of Korea engaged in farming even in areas near the Military Demarcation Line. He said he got a good knowledge of the progress of socialist construction in Korea, seeing the splendid apartment houses of Tongil street built under the wise guidance of the dear leader His Excellency Kim Chong-il.
