Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] All parties must now focus on searching for the final way of settlement of the Balkan conflict by peaceful means, the Ukrainian Foreign Ministry's statement which was made on Monday due to the information on the 4 planes shot down in Bosnia, says. The planes must belong to Bosnian Serbs. It was also pointed out that all possible provocations must be prevented and the resolutions adopted by the UN Security Council must be unconditionally carried out. The situation in Bosnia is still under discussion, the press service told INTERFAX-UKRAINE.
