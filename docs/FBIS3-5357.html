Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 5 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il received gifts on his birthday from the Portuguese Central Committee for the Study of Kimilsongism, director of the Universal Logistix Comany of Peru Alberto Arrospide, professor of sociology of the Inca Garcilaso de la Vega University of Peru Roberto Tara, chairman of the International Study Center for Chuche-Oriented Mass Media in Nepal Purna Chandra Sharma, the general director of the Polish "Intermont Group" of the International Construction Company and the head of the Polish "Intermont Group" branch in Germany and the general secretary of the All India Indo-Korean Friendship Association. The gifts were handed to the DPRK ambassadors to the relevant countries and an official concerned.
