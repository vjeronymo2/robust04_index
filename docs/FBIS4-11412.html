BFN [Text] Starting her official visit to Peru, Colombian Foreign Minister Noemi Sanin de Rubio today met Foreign Minister Efrain Goldenberg. She later met President Fujimori at Government House. [Begin recording] [First unidentified reporter] During the first day of her official visit to Peru, Foreign Minister Noemi Sanin de Rubio met at the Foreign Ministry building with her Peruvian colleague, Efrain Goldenberg. From the Foreign Ministry building, Rubio walked to San Pedro church, located a few meters from the Foreign Ministry building. The Colombian foreign minister toured the church and closely observed each of the tableaux and paintings dating from Spanish colonial times that are meticulously kept in the church. Afterward, Sanin de Rubio went to Government House, where she held a protocol meeting with President Fujimori with the objective of strengthening bilateral ties. Before leaving Government House, she observed the traditional changing of the guard conducted by the Junin guard detachment at the Honor Patio. [Second unidentified reporter] In the afternoon, Colombian Foreign Minister Noemi Sanin de Rubio left the Foreign Ministry building to place a wreath at the Simon Bolivar monument at Congress Plaza. [Noemi Sanin de Rubio] We will install the neighborhood committees [comisiones de vencidad]. We will hold meetings and meet with the media. With President Fujimori, we will open a new school. We strive to achieve integration not only at the government level but also at all social levels. [end recording]
