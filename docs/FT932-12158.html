930429 FT 29 APR 93 / International Company News: CanPac Forest Products cuts losses CANADIAN Pacific Forest Products narrowed its losses in the first quarter due to strong construction timber markets, larger newsprint and white paper shipments, lower costs and a lower Canadian dollar. The net loss was CDollars 64.8m (USDollars 51.8m), or Dollars 1.23 a share, against a loss of Dollars 71.1m, or Dollars 1.47, a year earlier. Sales were Dollars 450m, against Dollars 430m. US newsprint prices east of the Rockies have gone up 10 per cent, but a similar increase in the west has been postponed until midsummer. White paper prices are strengthening again in the second quarter but pulp demand and prices remain depressed.
