BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondents Yuriy Sizov, Vladimir Malyshev, and Yuriy Malinin] [Text] Kerkira (Corfu), 24 Jun -- A full-scale document comprising over 80 pages and translated into the 12 [as received] languages of the EU [European Union] member-countries has not yet been circulated in the press center in Kerkira. However, ITAR-TASS correspondents have learned from circles close to the Russian delegation that the main aim of the agreement is to facilitate trade, investments, and harmonious economic relations and to strengthen political and economic freedoms. It follows from the agreement that a permanent political dialogue is being established between Russia and the EU, which both countries intend to develop and make more active. The document also envisages meetings between the Russian president and the chairmen of the EU Council and Commission. A political dialogue at parliamentary level is envisaged to be held twice a year. The agreement plans to set up a cooperation council, which will monitor the implementation of this historic document. It will comprise members of the Russian Federation Government and leaders of the EU working bodies. A Parliamentary cooperation committee is also being set up. The 10 sections of the agreement pay special attention to economic cooperation, including, in particular, articles on trade in nuclear materials and financial and cultural cooperation.
