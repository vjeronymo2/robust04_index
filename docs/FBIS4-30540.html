BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 26 (KCNA) -- The France-Korea Friendship Association issued a bulletin on June 15. The bulletin carries articles under the titles "Who threatens peace in Asia," "Human rights situation in South Korea," "Arms dealing and swindler," etc. The bulletin explains the justness of the principled position of the DPRK toward the nuclear issue and stresses that the aggravation of tensions and disturbance of peace on the Korean peninsula are mainly attributable to the hostile policy of the United States and its followers intended to stifle the socialist system of the DPRK under the pretext of the "nuclear issue."
