May 25, 1990, Friday, San Diego County Edition

CRAWFORD NAMES OLIVERO AS NEW BASKETBALL COACH

Crawford High School has named Jeff Olivero as the boys' basketball coach for the 1990-91 season, Roger Engle, Crawford's athletic director, announced.

John King, the former coach, resigned to spend more time with his family.

Olivero coached Crawford's girls' basketball team from 1986 to 1988 and has been the defensive coordinator for the Colts varsity football team for eight years. It is Olivero's first coaching job for a boys' basketball team.
