920813 FT 13 AUG 92 / UK Company News: GPA at Dollars 49.1m in first three months GPA Group, the aircraft leasing company which recently aborted its global stock market flotation, reported first quarter profits of Dollars 49.1m (Pounds 25.7m) after tax, compared with Dollars 61.3m in the same period of last year. The outcome excluded the cost of aborting the flotation, estimated to be about Dollars 20m, which would be charged in the accounts for the year. NEWS DIGEST
