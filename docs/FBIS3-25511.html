Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The Mozambican Government has sent more than 2,500 soldiers to the assembly areas over the last six days; 43 percent of its soldiers have now been confined. Meanwhile, 540 Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] soldiers turned up at their assembly areas over the same period; 55 percent of Renamo's soldiers have now arrived in the assembly areas. According to the UN Operations in Mozambique, Renamo has sent its first 71 men to [name indistinct], in Maputo Province. Those men came from Salamanga.
