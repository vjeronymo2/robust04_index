BFN [Text] Industry and Minerals Minister Staff Gen Husayn Kamil Hasan has announced the arrival on 11 May 1994 of a ship carrying 236 harvesters and a number of agricultural tractors. In a statement to the Iraqi News Agency, the minister said a special committee will distribute the harvesters and tractors to farmers in all governorates so that they can be immediately used in the current cultivation and harvest campaign. The minister added; The Ministry of Industry and Minerals and the industrial sector are foremost supporters of the agricultural sector by supplying it with equipment and other essentials such as fetilizers and pesticides in order to develop agriculture and increase and improve the quality of production. This came in implementation of the directives of leader President Saddam Husayn, may God prserve and protect him, who is always concerned about developing the agricultural sector and enhancing its resources and capabilities in order to ensure food for our brave Iraqi people and to defeat the unjust blockade on our struggler country. May God grant us success in serving our people under the builder of Iraq's glory leader President Saddam Husayn, may God preserve and protect him. It is noteworthy that a consignment of harvesters and tractors had arrived earlier this year and was distributed to farmers. Other consignments of agricultural harvesters and tractors will arrive forthwith.
