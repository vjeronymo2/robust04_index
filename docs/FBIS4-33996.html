BFN [Excerpt] Montevideo, 10 Jun (EFE) -- Argentine President Carlos Menem and Paraguayan President Juan Carlos Wasmosy returned tonight to their respective countries after participating here in Montevideo in a ceremony to open bids for the dredging of the Martin Garcia Channel on the River Plate. The chiefs of state remained for a few hours in the Uruguayan capital, where they met with Uruguayan President Luis Alberto Lacalle and participated in the ceremony during which Uruguayan Foreign Minister Sergio Abreu and Argentine Foreign Minister Guido Di Tella signed and exchanged the respective documents.
