BFN [Text] Baku, June 17 (SNARK)--The Azerbaijani Defence Minister Mamadrafi Mamedov denied the mass media reports on the fact that there, in Moscow, are going on the negotiations between the Russian, Armenian and Azerbaijani military experts. In the interview to "Radio Svoboda" he reported, that these days the representatives of these countries' foreign policy departments meet in Moscow. Touching upon the Russian plan of the Karabakh conflict settlement the minister mentioned that Moscow wants to be useful as a mediator but there are certain points Azerbaijan can't accept. Mamedov again confirmed the traditional demands of the official Baku - to set the occupied territories free. Answering the question about the situation at the front, Mamedov was more than brief: "At the front everything is all right".
