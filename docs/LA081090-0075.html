August 10, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

ARAB MAN WOUNDED IN ISRAELI ATTACK DIES

A Palestinian, attacked by enraged Israelis after the grisly killing of two Jewish teen-agers, has died in a hospital Thursday, police said.

Izat Halahla, 42, died of wounds received after Tuesday's funeral for the teen-agers, whose bodies were found gagged, bound and stabbed on the outskirts of Jerusalem.
