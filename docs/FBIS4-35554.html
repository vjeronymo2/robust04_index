BFN [Text] Cairo, June 1, IRNA -- Iran's Foreign Minister 'Ali-Akbar Velayati and Foreign Minister of Bahrain Shaykh Muhammad Bin-Mubarak al Khalifah here Wednesday [1 June] conferred on topics of mutual interest and on how to broaden relations between the two countries. Al Khalifah said his government would exert every effort to further develop the relations between the two Persian Gulf countries. He said Iran and Bahrain have identical views on many important issues including those brought up at the current 11th assembly of foreign ministers of the non-aligned nations. Velayati, too, said his talks with his Bahraini ounterpart were very friendly. He added that the two countries have common positions towards many regional and international issues including those of Yemen and Bosnia-Herzegovina.
