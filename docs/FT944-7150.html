941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto responded to bargain hunting by Canadian investors and ended solidly higher, helped by stronger earnings from Canadian banks and firm gold stocks. The TSE 300 index gained 36.96 at 4,085.56 and rises led falls by 410 to 277 after volume of 38.2m shares. Toronto Dominion Bank firmed CDollars 5/8 to CDollars 20 3/4 on good fourth-quarter results.
