921110 FT 10 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Fleming Japanese net assets decline Net assets for the Fleming Japanese Investment Trust fell from 180.76p to 140.64p per share over the year to September 30. Net revenue was halved at Pounds 426,000 (Pounds 858,000) and earnings came out at 0.36p (0.73p). The single final dividend is cut from 0.67p to 0.35p.
