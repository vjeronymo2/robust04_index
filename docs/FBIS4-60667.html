BFN [Text] The closure on the Gaza Strip was lifted this morning, and Palestinians are being allowed to enter Israel under the conditions enforced last week. The closure was imposed last Friday, following the terror attack near the Erez checkpoint in which reserves soldiers Moshe Bukra and Erez Ben-Barukh were killed. Since the attack the Palestinian police have set up roadblocks on the approach roads to entrance from the Strip and are stopping and examining local vehicles.
