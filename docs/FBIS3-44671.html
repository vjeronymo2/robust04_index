Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 5 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese Communist Party delegation headed by Wen Jiabao, alternate member of the Political Bureau and member of the Secretariat of the Central Committee of the Chinese Communist Party (CPC), left here today on an official goodwill visit to India and Bangladesh. The delegation has been invited by the Indian National Congress (Indira Gandhi) and the Bangladesh Nationalist Party. On its way to India and Bangladesh, the delegation will also visit Singapore at the invitation of its government. Among those who saw the delegation off at the airport were Li Shuzheng, alternate member of the CPC Central Committee and head of the International Liaison Department of the CPC Central Committee, and the diplomatic envoys of the three countries in China.
