941115 FT 15 NOV 94 / UK Company News: James Smith Ests Sharply improved gross rental income and a reduced interest burden helped James Smith Estates, the provincial property investment company, report a 72 per cent interim profits expansion. Mr Stephen Mulliner, chairman, said the company had made purchases totalling Pounds 3.3m since the end of the half-year and was seeking further portfolios and individual properties 'which meet our investment criteria'. The pre-tax line for the six months to September 24 jumped to Pounds 1.34m (Pounds 775,000) reflecting a 42 per cent growth to Pounds 1.65m (Pounds 1.16m) in rental income and interest payable of just Pounds 93,000 (Pounds 237,000) following the Pounds 12.3m placing and open offer in March. The interim dividend goes up to 1.7p (1.55p), payable from earnings of 4.1p (3.37p) per share.
