940512 FT 12 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Dollars 3m first quarter loss at SeaCon Sea Containers, the Bermuda-based leisure, ferry and container leasing group, incurred net losses of Dollars 3.17m (Pounds 2.17m) in the three months to March 31, against profits of Dollars 7.47m last time. The company pointed out that the previous figure had been boosted by a Dollars 10.4m exceptional gain from foreign exchange and the sale of a factory. It added that underlying earnings from container leasing had grown by Dollars 1.5m. The ferries and ports division incurred increased losses of Dollars 7.25m (Dollars 6.18m). Finance costs rose by Dollars 9.66m to Dollars 16.2m, and losses per share came through at 71 cents (earnings of 34 cents).
