January 16, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

MATER DEI HOLDS ITS NO. 1 POSITION IN BASKETBALL POLL

Mater Dei High School, coming off a 62-59 victory over Artesia, Los Angeles County's No. 1 team, is the top-ranked team for the fifth consecutive week in the Orange County Sportswriters' Assn. prep basketball poll.

El Toro, ranked second last week, dropped to fourth after a 69-52 loss to unranked Dana Hills.

Edison, ranked second two weeks ago, moved to the No. 2 spot. Corona del Mar makes its first appearance in the poll at No. 9.

In the featured game of the week, Edison plays host to 10th-ranked Marina at 7:30 p.m. Wednesday.
