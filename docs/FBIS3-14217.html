Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Lebanese Army and the Internal Security forces have intensified security measures in accordance with the Lebanese Government decision. They have deployed mobile and foot patrols and set up fixed and mobile checkpoints in Beirut's southern suburb. The army and Internal Security Forces are continuing the security measures taken in the Kasrawan area. A NATIONAL NEWS AGENCY correspondent reports that the security forces on the al-Biqa' plain, helped by Syrian Arab forces units, yesterday evening began implementing similar security measures in the area, especially in Ba'labakk and al-Hirmil.
