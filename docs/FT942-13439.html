940425 FT 25 APR 94 / Company news this week: Net earnings dip at Norsk Hydro - Other Companies Norsk Hydro, Norway's energy and fertilisers group, today produces first-quarter profits. The group, which announced a NKr5bn (Pounds 450m) rights issue last month and is 51 per cent state-owned, saw 1993 net earnings dip from NKr1.8bn to NKr1.2bn.
