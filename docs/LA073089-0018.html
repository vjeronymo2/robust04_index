July 30, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TELLING IT LIKE IT IS ABOUT TV

Thank goodness for people like Arthur Temple ("Former Director of Time Still Hits Warner Merger," July 13) with his strong feelings of how the television and entertainment industries are befouling our national moral culture with their endless repetitious showings of violence, murder, guns, vulgarity, profanity and sex.

Thanks for telling it like it is.

GEORGE E. STACY

Van Nuys
