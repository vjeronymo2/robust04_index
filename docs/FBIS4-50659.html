BFN [By reporter Zhang Jiaxiang (1728 0502 4382)] [Text] Hanoi, 17 May (XINHUA) -- This afternoon, Pham The Duyet, Politburo member of the Communist Party of Vietnam [CPV] Central Committee, held a meeting here with a delegation of CPC cadres, led by Yin Kesheng, member of the CPC Central Committee and secretary of the Qinghai provincial party committee. They held cordial and friendly talks. Pham The Duyet said: Since Vietnam and China normalized relations between their parties and countries, relations in all areas have expanded greatly. It is hoped that the two parties, countries, and peoples will constantly consolidate and develop their traditional friendship. Pham The Duyet spoke highly of China's achievements, and briefed the Chinese visitors on Vietnam's progress and achievements in reform and opening up. The delegation of CPC cadres arrived in Hanoi on 14 May for the visit. Over the past few days, the delegation has held meetings and talks with responsible persons from the CPV Central Committee's Ideology and Culture Department, the State Planning Commission, and the Hanoi City CPV Committee. Yin Kesheng and his entourage will leave Hanoi tomorrow for Ho Chi Minh City where they will continue their visit. They will leave Vietnam on 22 May upon concluding their trip.
