BFN [Text] The Russian Foreign Ministry has informed the British Foreign Office that the 6 May statement by Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev amounts to a misunderstanding. This is what Estonian Government Press Secretary Ain Saarna told journalists today. According to INTERFAX, Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev stated at a press conference on 6 May that 2,500 Russian servicemen will stay in Estonia so long as the issue of the Russian military pensioners is not resolved. As is known, both President Lennart Meri and Prime Minister Mart Laar have reacted to this statement.
