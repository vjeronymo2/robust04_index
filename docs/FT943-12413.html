940725 FT 25 JUL 94 / Company News (This Week): Digital set for a fourth year of woe - Other companies Digital Equipment is expected to report its fourth consecutive year of heavy losses tomorrow. The troubled US computer company has said that it will take a Dollars 1.2bn restructuring charge to axe 20,000 jobs over the next 12 months plus asset write-offs of Dollars 350m-Dollars 400m. Analysts also predict operating losses for the latest quarter of Dollars 100m or more. Digital may also detail further divestiture plans following last week's agreement to sell most of its data storage business to Quantum, a Silicon Valley disk-drive manufacturer.
