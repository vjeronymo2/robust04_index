June 28, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

TOPS IN L.A. TOO: Orange County's two "Donalds" are so powerful that L.A. Magazine's list of that city's "30 Most Powerful People" (E8) wouldn't be complete without them, says Editor Lew Harris. . . . The Irvine Co.'s Donald L. Bren is No. 10. "He's got money, he's got power. . . . He's a big shot," Harris says. No. 30? Developer Don Koll: "He's been shaping the Southern California skyline for three decades." Jeffrey A. Perlman and Dave Lesher
