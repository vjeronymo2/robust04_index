BFN [Text] Cape Town April 9 SAPA -- The Police Prisons and Civil Rights Union [Popcru] has threatened to embark on mass action if its dismissed and suspended members are not reinstated, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reported on Saturday [9 April]. This follows the alleged failure of the Transitional Executive Council (TEC) and the Department of Correctional Services and Justice to address the union's grievances. Speaking in Cape Town, the union's regional chairman, Mr Nathan Ramalane, condemned the TEC for not acceding to Popcru's demands.
