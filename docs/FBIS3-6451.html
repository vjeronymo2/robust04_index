Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Ulaanbaatar, March 16 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Official newspaper "ARDYN ERH" informs about opening yesterday of the spring session of the State Great Hural (SGH). At the opening of the session present were the President of the country P. Ochirbat, the Prime Minister P. Jasray, heads of departments and leaders of some parties, having seats in the parliament, the representatives of press. Opening the session, the Chairman of the State Great Hural N. Bagabandi noted that Mongolia is constantly following the path of democratic reforms guaranteed by the new Constitution, noted that in state policy constitutional provisions on respect of human rights and freedoms are being realized. In his speech N. Bagabandi announced that consideration and adoption of draft laws included in the agenda of the spring session will promote formation of legal basis of further renewal of state system, development of market economy, consolidation of judicial power, reforming of life of the society. The newspaper publishes the text of the speech of the President P. Ochirbat at the opening of the session. He informed about the outcome of his recent official visit to four countries of Asia, pointing out to importance of immediate realization of agreements reached with them. In his speech the president paid a great attention to improvement of legal knowledge of citizens, training of highly qualified specialists. He noted that withdrawal by the Mongolian National Democratic and the Mongolian Social Democratic Parties of their representatives from the State Great Hural does not meet the national interests. In connection with this, P. Ochirbat noted that he had consultations with leadership of the parliament, government and the above mentioned parties, on the basis of which he made a proposal on making amendments and additions in the law on State Great Hural and the law on elections. The newspaper publishes an interview with some businessmen, who accompanied the President P. Ochirbat during his official visit to four countries of Asia. The Chairman of the Chamber of Commerce and Industry D. Hurts, the heads of a number of companies of the country point out to the agreements on cooperation with many firms and companies of India, Laos, Thailand, Viet-nam, in particular, of foundation of the Indian-Mongolian Chamber of Commerce.
