931216 FT 16 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Ensor On turnover from continuing operations of Pounds 6.93m, Ensor reported losses before tax in the six months to September 30 of Pounds 32,000, against Pounds 2.1m restated for FRS 3. Turnover was Pounds 26.6m, including Pounds 19.6m from discontinued operations. Losses per share emerged at 0.1p (18.1p).
