920925 FT 25 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Orbicom agreement with creditors Orbicom, the private Anglo-Soviet trading group yesterday worked out a company voluntary arrangement with UK creditors owed Pounds 1.3m. Creditors have agreed a business plan which, if successful, would mean they would be paid off by cashflow obtained from engineering projects in Siberia and a Moscow contract for a waste recycling plant. The arrangement runs for a year.
