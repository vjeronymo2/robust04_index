BFN ["BS-Press" report under rubric "According to Reports From Correspondents and Information Agencies": "The Tbilisi Police Garrison Is in Need of Help"] [Text] Addressing a meeting of heads of law enforcement bodies in Georgia, Tbilisi Police Chief Demur Mikadze stated that 600 policemen had not been paid at all for eight months and [these] people "were being forced to take up arms and rob." Since the beginning of this year, 28 police employees had been dismissed from the internal affairs bodies for various offenses and criminal proceedings had been initiated in eight cases. He pointed out other problems affecting the Tbilisi police garrison: the lack of a material and technical base, modern equipment, and essential means to fight crime.
