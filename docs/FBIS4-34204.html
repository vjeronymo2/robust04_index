BFN [Text] Brasilia, 15 Jun (AE) -- Presidential spokesman Fernando Costa announced that President Itamar Franco has been deeply shaken by the death of his nephew Ariosto Franco in Cartagena, Colombia. The president was informed early this morning that his nephew had suffered a heart attack. Franco and the members of his entourage have canceled every appointment scheduled for today, and are preparing to return to Brazil as soon as Ariosto Franco's body is released. Franco's trip to Ecuador, which was scheduled to start on 16 June, has thus been canceled. A medical bulletin will soon be released on the death of the president's nephew. [At 1438 GMT AGENCIA ESTADO adds that "in addition to being President Franco's private secretary," the 36-year-old Ariosto Carlos Franco "had been living with the president at Alvorada Palace."]
