Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 7 -- A delegation of the Workers' Party of Korea led by Mr. Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Party Central Committee in charge of the external relations, arrived here today for an official visit to Vietnam. This afternoon, a delegation of the Communist Party of Vietnam led by Mr. Hong Ha, secretary of the Party Central Committee and head of its External Relations Commission, held talks with the Korean delegation. The two sides exchanged views on the situation in the region and on the world of mutual concern and discussed measures to promote the relations between the two parties and peoples. The Korean head delegate expressed his wish to strengthen the traditional friendship between the two parties and peoples, which had been fostered by President Ho Chi Minh and President Kim Il-song. Mr. Hong Ha gave a banquet this evening in honour of the Korean delegation.
