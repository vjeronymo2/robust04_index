BFN [Text] Hefei, April 4 (XINHUA) -- Anhui, one of China's key energy-producing provinces, is boosting local coal industry. Provided with convenient transport facilities, Anhui began to develop local coal mines in the early 1980s in addition to the expansion of state coal mines. It spent about 200 million yuan constructing eight mines last year. The local coal mines still under construction will have a combined production capacity of 4.92 million tons upon completion. With rich coal reserves, Anhui's local mines turned out 10.09 million tons of coal last year.
