941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / International Company News: Philippine media group gains 63% in third quarter ABS-CBN Broadcasting, the Philippines television and radio operator, said its unaudited third-quarter net income rose 63.1 per cent to 735.2m pesos (Dollars 28.9m) from 450.8m pesos in the same period last year, Reuter reports from Manila. Gross revenues grew 25.1 per cent to 2,169bn pesos from last year's 1,734.6bn pesos, and earnings per share rose to 2.83 pesos from 1.73 pesos. Analysts noted the company's audience share continued to grow, and advertising revenues were expected to jump around the Christmas period.
