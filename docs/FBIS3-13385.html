Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 12 Mar (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa received an urgent message today from Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat. Sa'id Kamal, Palestinian ambassador in Cairo, conveyed the message to Musa. Speaking after the meeting, Kamal said that the message deals with the results of the talks held between president 'Arafat and Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev in Tunis yesterday. In his message, the Palestinian leader stressed the need for the Palestinians in the occupied territories to obtain guarantees, particularly from the cosponsors of the peace conference, securing their safety and protection. Ambassador Kamal added that Dennis Ross, U.S. coordinator of the Middle East peace talks, will arrive in Tunis on Tuesday to hold talks with the Palestinian leadership to give impetus to the peace process and overcome the difficulties facing it.
