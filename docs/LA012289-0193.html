January 22, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFING: EVENTS IN THE NEWS THIS WEEK.

County Supervisor Gaddi H. Vasquez is scheduled to discuss a new pilot program for helping sexually abused children in the county at the annual luncheon Friday of the Child Sexual Abuse Network of Orange County. Dr. Michael Durfee, medical coordinator for the Los Angeles Department of Health's Child Abuse Program, will be the featured speaker at the luncheon in Orange.
