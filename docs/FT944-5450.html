941203 FT 03 DEC 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices The market was quiet this week, reports Man Producten. Prices for black and white were under pressure, as buying interest vanished temporarily. The black pepper price decrease was mainly caused by selling pressure from Brazil and India. Brazilian exporters were forced to sell some material to pay interest on loans. Indian traders felt able to sell off remaining stocks as their new crop will become available in January. This new crop is expected to be the same or slightly better than the last one. Muntok white was quoted at USDollars 3,600 a tonne, spot, and for December/January shipment at Dollars 3,500, cif. Spot black pepper faq was at Dollars 2,450 and for December/January shipment at Dollars 2,400, cif.
