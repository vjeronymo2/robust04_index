Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At noon today at the presidency President Liamine Zeroual received Mr. Redha Malek, who offered him the resignation of his government. President Liamine Zeroual has renewed his trust in Mr. Redha Malek and asked him to continue as head of the government. Mr. Redha Malek accepted the offer and thanked the head of state for his trust in him.
