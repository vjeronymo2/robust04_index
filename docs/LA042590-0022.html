April 25, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

BUCHANAN ON MANIFEST DESTINY

After reading Buchanan's little schoolboy fantasy concerning the possibility of our absorbing and democratizing (!) dissatisfied Canadian provinces (yeah, I'll bet those Canadians are just longing to escape the Mountie's oppressive jackboot), I can't help but be in awe of the mileage Buchanan has gotten out of a 39-cent box of crayons!

MARK JONES

Sun Valley
