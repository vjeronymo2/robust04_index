930617 FT 17 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Bromsgrove buys Bromsgrove Industries, the West Midlands-based engineer, is paying Pounds 1.48m for Gainsborough, a precision machinist which concentrates on the aerospace sector. The consideration comprises Pounds 575,000 cash, Pounds 650,000 of nil interest loan notes payable next June and 241,546 new shares - Pounds 50,000 in shares is deferred until next June.
