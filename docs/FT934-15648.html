931011 FT 11 OCT 93 / Builders blamed for gale damage BAD workmanship was to blame for some of the damage to houses in the gales of January and February 1990, a report from the Building Research Establishment says. Repairing the estimated 1.16m homes damaged in the gales - in which 27 people died because of building failures and a further 51 from other causes -cost Pounds 188m.
