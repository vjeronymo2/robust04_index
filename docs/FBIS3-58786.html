Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The president of China's parliament, Qiao Shi, has held talks with the Swiss Foreign Minister Flavio Cotti at the start of his four-day visit to Switzerland. The Swiss Foreign Ministry spokesman said Mr. Cotti expressed his concern to Mr. Qiao over the human rights situation in China, particularly in Tibet. Mr. Cotti pointed out that China still had not ratified the 1966 Human Rights Convention. During the meeting about 30 Tibetans demonstrated outside the parliament building in Bern.
