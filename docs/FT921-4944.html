920306 FT 06 MAR 92 / World News in Brief: England win again All-rounder Ian Botham took four wickets and scored 53 to be named man of the match as England defeated Australia by eight wickets to score their third win in the cricket World Cup. Scores: Australia 171 all out, England 173 for 2; South Africa 200 for 8, West Indies 136 all out.
