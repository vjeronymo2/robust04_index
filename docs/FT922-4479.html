920605 FT 05 JUN 92 / UK Company News: OFT to probe card protection deal The sale of Cardwise, a small credit card protection company formerly owned by the Access banks, is to be probed by the Office of Fair Trading. The prospective buyer is believed to be Credit Card Sentinel, the largest player in the UK card protection market. Cardwise is owned by First Data Resources, the credit card processing arm of American Express. Sentinel is the European offshoot of Datacard Corporation of Minneapolis.
