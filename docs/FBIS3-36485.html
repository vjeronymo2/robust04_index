Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Georgiy Shmelev] [Text] Moscow February 7 TASS -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, who is due to fly to Uzbekistan later today, met Vietnamese Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam on Monday morning [7 February] for talks on bilateral Russian-Vietnamese relations and the situation in the Asian-Pacific region. The Vietnamese minister, who arrived in Moscow on his way back home from a visit to the commonwealth Central Asian republics, told Kozyrev about his impressions from the visit and confirmed his invitation to the Russian minister to visit Vietnam. "We are progressing fairly well in our relations," Kozyrev said before the talks. "We meet three-four times a year, and working meetings also take place on other levels. In particular, the development of economic cooperation between Russia and Vietnam are being discussed on a wide scale". Minister Kozyrev called for speeding up joint work on drafting a bilateral treaty between Vietnam and Russia.
