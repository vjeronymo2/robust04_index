BFN [From the "National Radio News"] [Text] On the morning of 28 June, Foreign Affairs Minister Roberto Robaina departed for Venezuela. Robaina will participate in the foreign ministers' preliminary technical meeting, in preparation for the Barbados conference, to create the Association of Caribbean States. A large group of foreign ministers and experts in foreign relations from the Caribbean and Latin America will finalize issues in Caracas for the forthcoming Association of Caribbean States convention and the Caricom summit for the Anglo-Caribbean community, which is to take place in Barbados in July.
