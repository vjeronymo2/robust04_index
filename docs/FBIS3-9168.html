Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Report by Mirudin Aldobasic] [Text] In spite of major defeats and withdrawal from the occupied territories in Novi Seher, Perkovic, and Vobanice, the aggressor continues to engage in provocations and combat activities. Throughout the night as well as early this morning the enemy has been attacking the Tesanj-Novi Seher road and defense lines of the units of the 7th South Operative Group, as well as the settlements of (Mekis), (Karadaglije), and (Radusa) from its stronghold in Crni Vrh. The chetniks also attempted several tank and infantry attacks on the Doboj front. All these attacks were successfully resisted, primarily thanks to the soldiers of the 203d Famous Motorized Brigade Doboj-Bosnia, and the 110th Famous Brigade of the Croatian Defense Council Usora. In addition, new military hardware and manpower from Novi Seher have been observed arriving in this area. The chetniks were also provoking the defense lines with small arms at of the [word indistinct] 207th Famous Hill Brigade, but naturally without any results.
