930528 FT 28 MAY 93 / Energy minister says government will provide support for pits buy-outs MR TIM EGGAR, energy minister, said the government would provide support for management buy-outs at pits at half the cost of employing consultants. Support in preparing bids for any one pit would not exceed Pounds 80,000. Mr Eggar also said the government was contributing Pounds 6m to clean coal technology projects worth nearly Pounds 33m.
