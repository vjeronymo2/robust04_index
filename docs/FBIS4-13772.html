BFN [Text] In occupied Kashmir, mojahedin during their stepped up retaliatory attacks on Indian troops killed 14 soldiers. Mojahedin attacked military targets in Anantnag-Islamabad and its adjacent areas. Six soldiers were killed in a fierce clash with mojahedin near Anantnag-Islamabad. Mojahedin also killed two Indian soldiers when they tried to besiege the Jamia Masjid [mosque] of Doro. Three soldiers died in an attack by mojahedin on a military target. A number of troops were killed and wounded when mojahedin attacked a military camp in Pahalgam. Mojahedin also carried out rocket attack on a battalion headquarters of the Gurkha Regiment at Ghamir camp in Rajouri sector. Four soldiers were killed and six injured. The camp was badly damaged in the attack. Indian soldiers gunned down two civilians during a crackdown and house-to-house search operation in Dayalgaon in Anantnag-Islamabad. Indian soldiers martyred and wounded a number of civilians in indiscriminate firing during siege and search operation at Batmalu in Srinagar.
