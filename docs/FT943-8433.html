940817 FT 17 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Skipton Skipton Building Society reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 7.04m for the six months to end-June, a 69 per cent increase on last time's Pounds 4.17m. The rise was partly attributable to a fall from Pounds 17.1m to Pounds 15.5m in provisions for bad and doubtful debts. Assets dipped slightly from Pounds 2.91bn to Pounds 2.85bn.
