Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Britain's state radio says that French justice officials have once again accused Iran of involvement in Bakhtiar's assassination. London radio reports that officials of the French public prosecutor's office have said that Iran placed the necessary facilities at the disposal of Bakhtiar's assassins. Recently France released two Iranians from French prisons. Following this, Paris became the target of harsh criticism by the United States, Britain, and some other Western countries.
