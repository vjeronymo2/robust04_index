930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / UK Company News: James Finlay Profits before tax of James Finlay, the overseas trading and financial services group, improved from Pounds 3.6m to Pounds 3.8m for the half year ended June 30. Earnings slipped to 1p (1.8p) per share but the interim dividend is being held at 2p. Turnover advanced to Pounds 76.3m (Pounds 70.7m) and operating profits emerged at Pounds 4.69m (Pounds 4.51m) of which the UK accounted for Pounds 2.3m (Pounds 1.28m).
