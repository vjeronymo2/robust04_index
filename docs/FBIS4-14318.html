BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, last night received brother Badr Hammam, Egyptian assistant foreign minister and the envoy of Egyptian President Muhammad Husni Mubarak. Hammam conveyed to the brother president a message from his brother President Muhammad Husni Mubarak. The message deals with developments in our region and the efforts exerted by the brothers in Egypt to contain the political crisis and to end its regrettable repercussions, especially on the media and military levels. The message also expresses the Egyptian president's concern over the regrettable incidents that took place at the 'Imran camp. The message stresses Egypt's concern about security and stability and about supporting the march of unity and democracy in our country and exerting all efforts to achieve this. The brother president asked the Egyptian envoy to convey a reply to his brother Egyptian President Muhammad Husni Mubarak reassuring him about the situation in our country. In his message, the brother president expresses his eagerness to exert every effort required to guarantee the safety of the Yemeni homeland and to foil all schemes aimed at threatening the march of security, stability, unity, and democracy in our country. The message also expresses the brother president's deep appreciation for the efforts of the brothers in Egypt, especially his brother President Muhammad Husni Mubarak, to prevent the danger of conflicts and violence in our country and to support the march of Yemeni unity since it is a national and pan-Arab gain that cannot be relinquished at any price. The meeting was attended by 'Atta' Hardun, the Egyptian ambassador to our country.
