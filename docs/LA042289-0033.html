April 22, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE: NAMES IN THE NEWS

Doctors treating injured motorcycle racer Bubba Shobert at San Jose Medical Center said he remains critical but stable, and that they have been able to reduce medication required to keep him sedated. Shobert suffered head injuries in an accident after the main event of last Sunday's U.S. International Grand Prix at Laguna Seca Raceway.
