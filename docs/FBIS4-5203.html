BFN [Text] Prime Minister Chuan Likphai hosted a banquet in honor of visiting Australian Prime Minister Paul Keating, his wife, and his delegation at Government House yesterday evening. The Australian prime minister and his entourage are currently on an official visit to Thailand as guests of the Thai Government. In a welcome speech delivered at the banquet, Prime Minister Chuan said that the meeting between the Thai and Australian delegations during the visit will provide a good opportunity for both sides to develop their existing friendship, create better understanding, and expand cooperation between the two countries in the exploitation of their energy and potentials. The meeting will also enable both sides to find ways to enhance their roles in increasing the regional dynamic and collaboration. In addition, the prime minister expressed his admiration for Australia's constructive role in the Southeast Asian and Asia-Pacific regions. Australian participation in the opening of the Thai-Lao Friendship Bridge is an historic event that reflects a great achievement resulting from international cooperation. Australia is thereby considered to have a role in the process of establishing peace and progress in Southeast Asia.
