BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities monitored through 26 May. The Economy Ministry has approved 9.5 million pesos in petty cash for the Presidential Secretariat, the Natural Resources Secretariat, the Drug Abuse Prevention Secretariat, the Science and Technology Secretariat, the Social Development Secretariat, and the Public Function Secretariat. (Buenos Aires CLARIN in Spanish 23 May 94 pp 16, 17) On 19 May, business sources reported that Germany has implemented trade sanctions against Argentina for its delay in paying $7 billion in interest to the Paris Club. Argentine-German Chamber of Commerce sources indicated that the sanctions include the suspension of German loans, which are worth $300 million per year, to finance bilateral trade exports. The overdue interest involves loans contracted with German banks within the Paris Club to finance the Yacyreta hydroelectric dam project and the Atucha nuclear power plant project. (Madrid EFE in Spanish 1331 GMT 19 May 94)
