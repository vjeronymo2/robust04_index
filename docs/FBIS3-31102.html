Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Skopje, 14 Feb (TANJUG) -- Vitaliy Churkin, the Russian president's special envoy for the former Yugoslavia, arrived in Skopje from Belgrade this morning. Churkin will hold talks in the capital of the Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia with President Kiro Gligorov and Foreign Minister Stevo Crvenkovski.
