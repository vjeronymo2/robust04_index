May 22, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Bieber Outlines Bargaining Plan: United Auto Workers President Owen Bieber outlined a five-point bargaining plan for this summer's Big Three contract negotiations in the opening speech at the UAW's three-day bargaining convention in Kansas City, Mo.

Bargaining with General Motors Corp., Ford Motor Co. and Chrysler Corp. begins in July. Bieber ticked off a list of bargaining priorities for when the union sits down with the Big Three shortly after Independence Day. Topping the list, as expected, were job and income security, followed by wage and benefit increases and maintenance of health-care benefits.
