930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Hunters Armley Hunters Armley, the web offset commercial printing group that went public in December, expanded pre-tax profits from Pounds 716,000 to Pounds 973,000 in the six months to March 31. Turnover rose 12 per cent to Pounds 16.3m. Earnings were 3.15p (2.71p) and the dividend is 1.25p.
