BFN [Text] Tirana, June 10 (ATA) -- The commander of Serb unit in Gjakove, Major Slovan Stanisic, in a recent interview with the Serbian TV in Prishtina, acknowledged the fact that the border between Albania and Kosova is turned into a shooting-range for the Serbian forces against the Albanians. He said that since 1991 and up to now, the Serbian border guards have killed 40 Albanians and have wounded 15 others, reports from Prishtina say. Making a detailed analysis of the tense situation created on the Albanian border due to the systematic killings of the Albanians, the Serbian major implied that the military tension will be escalated. He did not mention the responsible persons for the tense situation, but he said that only during the last five months 30 incidents have occurred on the border between Albania and Kosova.
