930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / Monitoring change for factors urged INDEPENDENT factoring companies, which provide clients with cash against their invoices and other credit management services, should be more closely regulated, the larger of the industry's two professional bodies said yesterday. The statement follows the collapse last month of London and Provincial Factors, which was not a member of the Association of British Factors and Discounters, the body which represents the large factors.
