BFN [Text] Beijing, May 16 (XINHUA) -- Chinese President Jiang Zemin said here today that China highly treasures its good-neighborly relations with Russia and wishes to make joint efforts with Russia to push such relations up to a new level. He made the remark when meeting with a delegation of the State Duma from the Russian Federation headed by Chairman Ivan Rybkin. Extending a warm welcome to Rybkin and his party on their visit to China, Jiang said that he expects the chairman's current visit to play a positive role in developing bilateral good-neighborliness and promoting mutually-beneficial cooperation between the two countries. Jiang expressed his happiness with the fresh development of the friendship and cooperation between China and Russia over recent years and said that both China and Russia, sharing a boundary line of over 4,000 kilometers, need a peaceful external environment for developing their economies. He said that sustained and stable good-neighborly relations between the two countries are in line with the common wishes and fundamental interests of the two peoples and also beneficial to the peace and stability of the Asia-Pacific region and the rest of the world. The leaders and statesmen of the two countries are duty-bound to fulfill the wishes of the people, Jiang said. The president said he hoped both China and Russia would handle bilateral relations from a long-term point of view so as to bring into the present good-neighborliness an enduring vitality. Rybkin said that the Russian people wish to maintain their relations of close cooperation with the Chinese people and the Russian State Duma is willing to conduct earnest and constructive cooperation and exchanges with the Chinese National People's Congress. At noon, Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing also met with the delegation. The delegation, which arrived in Beijing last Saturday [14 May] at the invitation of the NPC Standing Committee, will also visit Shanghai and Shenzhen during their 5-day stay in China.
