930306 FT 06 MAR 93 / London Stock Exchange: Cookson rights Confirmation of a rights issue from industrial materials company Cookson Group nevertheless surprised many market watchers and sent the shares into retreat on an otherwise strong day in the market. The company, which also announced figures in line with market expectations, is to make a one-for-four rights issue at 170p a share to raise Pounds 185.6m to fund growth and reduce acquisitions. This is the second time within the last two years that the company has called on the market for cash. The shares gave up 13 to 199p and Mr Andrew Mitchell at Smith New Court said: 'The shares are unlikely to go anywhere until the potential disposals and acquisitions are seen.'
