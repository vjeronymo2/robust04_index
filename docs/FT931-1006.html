930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / Two bailed over 'bogus' insurance MORE THAN 50 motorists have been sold 'bogus' insurance policies through Sunrise Insurance, a company based in Swansea not licensed as an insurer. Two men have been arrested and released on police bail following a raid on the Sunrise offices by West Glamorgan Trading Standards Office. Most of the customers affected responded to newspaper advertisements and were drivers who would find it difficult to obtain cover.
