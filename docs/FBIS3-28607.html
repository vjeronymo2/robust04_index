Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 5 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Lately in the mass media there is a great deal of talks about involvement of high officials of the Mongolian parliament and the government in corruption, bribery and abuse of power, proceeding from the statement made by the former chief of counterintelligence of the State Security Central Department, Colonel L. Sanjaasuren at the press conference organized by him. Apropos of this the government press service organized today a press conference to give an opportunity to the officials involved in the case to speak on the question and to give corresponding explanations themselves. The chairman of the Supreme Court Dembereltseren, the chief of the Central Customs Department Seseer, the Prime Minister's Adviser Bayarbaatar, the Vice-President of Mongolbank Chuluunbat, the Department Chiefs of the Ministry of Trade and Industry Altantulga, Shashnaabazar, the director of the company "Mongol Mark" /"Mongolian Stamp"/ Radnaabazar gave explanations and answered questions of journalists. The above mentioned persons refuted the statement of Colonel L. Sanjaasuren about involvement of the prime minister and the parliament chairman and vice-chairman and other officials in corruption, bribery and abuse of power.
