Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Groz'ny February 13 TASS -- Overnight, explosions shattered several oil and chemical plants in the Chechen Republic of Ichkeria. According to the news agency CHECHENPRESS, shortly after midnight unidentified persons blew up three 2,000-cubic-meter tanks on the territory of the Goit-Yurt oil park on the Rostov-Baku highway between the villages of Mesker-Yurt and Belkata. Around the same time, another explosion damaged an oil pipeline on the territory of the Bufer oil park near the village of Tolstoy-Yurt. The fires caused by these explosions have been extinguished, except for one at an oil well outside the village of Vinogradnoye, 20 km of the regional capital Grozny, senior officer of the republic's fire department, Isa Khasimilov, reported.
