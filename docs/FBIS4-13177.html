BFN [Text] Various Muslim organizations held a demonstration in the capital today to draw the world community's attention toward human rights violations in Pakistan. They also protested against the victimization of Urdu speaking muhajirs [Indian immigrants] by the Army in the Sind Province. Later, a delegation led by the Falah-i-Millat [Islamic Community Welfare] Action Committee's general secretary, Dr. Mohammad Furkan, submitted a memorandum to the Pakistani high commissioner urging upon Islamabad to stop atrocities against muhajirs and release the thousands of workers of the Muhajir Qaumi Movement languishing in jails for several years.
