940110 FT 10 JAN 94 / Company News (This Week): JP Morgan - Other companies The US bank should enjoy a temporary respite from the furore over its entanglement with the struggling Spanish bank Banesto when it reports fourth-quarter results on Thursday. The figures are expected to cap a strong 1993, with market projections putting earnings per share for the year at Dollars 8.20-Dollars 8.50, compared with Dollars 5.63. The earnings momentum in the last quarter is likely to have slowed, however. The question for 1994 is: will a tail-off in trading income lead to a slump in earnings?
