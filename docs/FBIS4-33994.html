BFN [Text] The plenum of the Peruvian Supreme Court today named Ricardo Nunez as its representative to the National Election Board. According to the law, Nunez will be the president of the National Election Board. Ricardo Nunez, former president of the Supreme Court and former rector of the San Martin de Porres University, was the senior justice and member of the executive council of the judicial branch until 20 April 1992 when he resigned following the rupture of the constitutional order. He is currently a labor law professor and emeritus rector of the San Martin de Porres University, emeritus professor of the San Marcos National University, and honorary professor of the National Universities of Trujillo, San Antonio, and San Luis Gonzaga. The Supreme Court members also named Alvaro Chocano Marina, former president of the Supreme Court, as the alternate representative. The official announcement of these appointments will be made during a news conference by Supreme Court President Luis Serpa Segura in the next few minutes.
