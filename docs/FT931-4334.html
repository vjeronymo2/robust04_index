930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Smaller assets edge ahead Net asset value of Kleinwort Smaller Companies Investment Trust edged ahead from 113.7p to 114.6p per share over the 12 months to January 31. Attributable revenue amounted to Pounds 544,479, down from Pounds 669,847 last time and equivalent to earnings of 4.12p (5.07p) per share. A recommended final dividend of 2p reduces the total for the year to 4p (4.6p).
