920620 FT 20 JUN 92 / Markets: Smaller companies slump again - At A Glance Small companies endured another bad week. The Hoare Govett index (capital gains version) fell 3.27 per cent from 1272.1 to 1231.81 over the seven days to June 18. County NatWest's smaller companies index fell 3.1 per cent to 981.13 over the same period. Hoare Govett pointed out that its index peaked on May 26 and added: 'Patience among institutional investors is wearing understandably thin. Too much should not be made of this fact, but it is not the stuff of which bull markets are made.'
