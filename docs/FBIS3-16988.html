Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, March 21 (AFP) -- Japanese Foreign Minister Tsutomu Hata demanded Monday that North Korea let international experts carry out a full inspection of its nuclear facilities and criticized Pyonyang's renewed threat to pull out of the Nuclear non- Proliferation Treaty (NPT). "Japan is worried by the fact that we could not obtain confirmation of the non-use by North Korea of nuclear materials for military purposes," Hata said at a news conference.
