940215 FT 15 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Second Market Inv Second Market Investment Company raised diluted net asset value by 55 per cent from 304.9p to 473.2p in 1993, or from 310p to 482.1p undiluted. Pre-tax profits for the year fell from Pounds 968,474 to Pounds 906,881, after an exchange loss of Pounds 21,477 (gain Pounds 70,282). Earnings per share came to 6.17p (6.13p) and the single dividend is 4.3p (same) which again includes a special 2.3p.
