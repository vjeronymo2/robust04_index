BFN [Text] Beijing, May 2 (XINHUA) -- Wei Jianxing, president of the All-China Federation of Trade Unions, met with a delegation from the Federation of Trade Unions of Uzbekistan led by its council Chairman Hulkar Djamalov here this evening. Wei, also member of the Political Bureau of the Chinese Communist Party Central Committee, said that Chinese Premier Li Peng's visit to Uzbekistan not long ago was a great success and brought bilateral ties to a new stage of development. He said with certainty that the delegation's visit will help deepen mutual understanding and friendship between the two trade union federations. During the meeting Wei also briefed the visitors on China's economic growth, measures that China has taken in curbing inflation and tasks that the Chinese trade unions are confronted with under the new situation. After the meeting, Wei hosted a dinner in honor of the delegation, which arrived here on April 30.
