BFN [YONHAP report from Kiev] [Text] North Korean Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam threatened that South Korea will be devastated if a war between North and South Korea breaks out. Prior to his departure after winding up his visit to Ukraine, Kim Yong-nam asserted that South Korea will face a calamity if a war breaks out because South Korea blindly follows the U.S. policy to enforce sanctions. Kim Yong-nam said North Korea has the will and sufficient capability to defend itself from sanctions. Kim Yong-nam also stressed that North Korea will continue to pursue missile launching tests.
