Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 29 [date as received] (KCNA) -- The international communist movement made further advance in 1993 through powerful political activities and struggle to tighten the bonds of solidarity, declared international secretary of the Brazilian Communist Party Jose Reinaldo Carvalho in an article titled "Advance of Communist Movement" published in the party organ A CLASE OPERARIA. He said the anti-imperialist and democratic progressive forces got united in one idea at various meetings and took the initiative with a great influence worldwide. The declaration "Let us defend and advance the cause of socialism" adopted in Pyongyang in April 1992 has been signed by more than 200 communist and workers' parties and other revolutionary and progressive parties, he said. "Our fighting task is to strengthen the solidarity of communist and workers' parties and the anti-imperialist, progressive forces worldwide, actively support socialist countries and unite with the peoples of the nations fighting for democracy and national independence," he stressed.
