BFN [Text] The president of the Republic has appointed new ministers in the government of Prime Minister Esko Aho. Mikko Pesaelae, deputy speaker of the Diet, will become the new agriculture and forestry minister, replacing Martti Pura, who will become municipal manager of Sodankylae. Diet Deputy Anneli Jaeaetteenmaeki will become the new justice minister to succeed Hannele Pokka, who will become the provincial governor of the province of Lapland. However, Jaeaetteenmaeki will only take up her post at the beginning of next month when Pokka's maternity leave from the ministry ends.
