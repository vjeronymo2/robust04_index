Title: National Fire Academy Long-Term Course Evaluation Forms. Abstract: The National Fire Academy's long-term evaluation forms_one for students and one for the student's supervisor_will obtain course specific feedback regarding impact of course content on job performance. This information is needed to improve instruction and content. Demographic data are needed to identify differentials in course impact. Type of Respondents: Individuals and households. Estimate of Total Annual Reporting and Recordkeeping Burden: 1,634 hours. Number of Respondents: 7,672. Estimated Average Burden Time per Response: Student evaluation form 15 minutes; Supervisor evaluation form 10 minutes. Frequency of Response: Other_three months after completion of a specific course.
