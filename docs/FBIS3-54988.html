Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] Sheikh-ul-Islam Hadji Allahshukyur Pasha-zade, the chairman of the Supreme Religious Council of the Caucasian Muslims, today received the Israeli Charge d'Affaires ad Interim in Azerbaijan (Yulvozr Yotbat) at (Yotbat's) request. Sheikh-ul-Islam Hadji Allahshukyur Pasha-zade congratulated the Israeli people on the new year and discussed the recent broadening of friendship ties between Azerbaijan and Israel. He hoped the Israeli Government would spare no effort to inform the international community about the events in Azerbaijan and the truth concerning the Armenian aggression against our land. The Israeli charge expressed his thanks for the reception he was afforded and welcomed the sheikh's call on people everywhere to support Azerbaijan. The guest said Azerbaijan is facing a refugee problem and the Israel Government will continue to provide humanitarian assistance.
