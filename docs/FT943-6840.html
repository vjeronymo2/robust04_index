940826 FT 26 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Oxford Molecular makes Pounds 5m US buy Oxford Molecular Group, which came to the market in April, is acquiring IntelliGenetics, a US-based company which develops DNA and protein sequence analysis software, for Pounds 5.2m. The consideration consists of Dollars 625,000 (Pounds 403,000) in cash, Pounds 850,000 in loan notes and the issue to Amoco Technology, the vendor, of 6.51m new ordinary shares. Oxford, which develops computer software for use in drug design, also announced pre-tax losses almost doubled, from Pounds 488,000 to Pounds 933,000, for the half year ended June 30. Mr Roderick Hall, chairman, said the deficit reflected significant investment in personnel and products to develop the business. Turnover advanced by 84 per cent to Pounds 1.09m (Pounds 591,000), including Pounds 590,000 of hardware sales. Software turnover increased by 4 per cent. Losses per share on an increased capital emerged at 3.5p (4.4p).
