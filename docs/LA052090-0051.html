May 20, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

'PEAKS' AND VALLEYS

"Twin Peaks" is the only TV program I will put aside the crocheting or crossword puzzle for and watch from beginning to end. One has to watch every move, look and stare; listen to every word, sigh, cry and sound. Miss one second, and the plot is shot.

Bette A. Du Puis, Los Osos
