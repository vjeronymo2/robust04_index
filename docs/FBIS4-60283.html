BFN [Text] The Cairo agreement on the implementation of self- government in Gaza and Jericho will be translated into Arabic in order to make even the junior Palestinian officers in Gaza and Jericho familiar with it. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that it has emerged that the Palestinian leaders have failed to explicate the written details of the agreement to the Palestinian population and security forces. A Palestinian source told our correspondent that the Palestinian leaders often prefer to explain events on the ground in their own way and contrary to the agreement, perhaps in order to win the Palestinian public's support.
