930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / International Company News: Telebras back in the black TELEBRAS, Brazil's state-controlled telecommunications group, recovered to post consolidated profits of Dollars 304.1m for 1992, against a loss of Dollars 154.5m the previous year. The preliminary results also show the parent holding company recorded Dollars 652.9m profit, up from Dollars 121.8m in 1991. Telebras, with 28 operating companies, owns 94 per cent of all public exchanges in Brazil and 91 per cent of the nationwide network local lines.
