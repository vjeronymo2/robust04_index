June 24, 1989, Saturday, Southland Edition

REDS DEFEAT MORGAN AND DODGERS, 3-1

Rookie Lenny Harris scored one run and drove in another Friday night as the Cincinnati Reds handed Mike Morgan some more hard luck, holding off the Dodgers, 3-1.

Morgan (5-6) fell below .500 despite leading the major leagues with a 1.64 earned-run average. He gave up two runs on eight hits in six innings.

Jose Rijo (7-4) allowed one run on seven hits before leaving with back spasms. Norm Charlton relieved in the sixth and pitched three hitless innings.
