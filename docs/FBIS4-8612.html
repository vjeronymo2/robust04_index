BFN [Text] Sarajevo, April 14 (AFP) -- Three children were killed and three injured when Bosnian Serbs shelled Gorazde Thursday [14 April], a spokesman for the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees said. Kris Janovski said two children were killed on the spot and a third died in hospital after the shell hit a usually quiet part of the city around midday. Radio Sarajevo said two women were also killed in Serb attacks on other parts of the Moslem enclave.
