Bureau of Alcohol, Tobacco and Firearms (BATF) OMB Number: 1512&hyph;0474 Form Number: ATF REC 5130/5 Type of Review: Extension Title: Principal Place of Business on Beer Labels Description: ATF regulations permit domestic brewers who operate more than one brewery to show as their address on labels and kegs of beer, their ``principal place of business.'' This option is in lieu of showing the actual place of production, or a listing of all the company's breweries on the label.
