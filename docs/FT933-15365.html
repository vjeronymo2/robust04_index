930709 FT 09 JUL 93 / Mandelson urges ITV costs review MR Peter Mandelson, Labour MP for Hartlepool, last night urged the government to conduct a review of the Broadcasting Act 1990 in tandem with the debate over the renewal of the BBC's royal charter. Mr Mandelson said the review should look at the possibility of reducing the cash burdens imposed on ITV by the franchise auction.
