930511 FT 11 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Cons Venture buys 14.56% of Aberdeen Consolidated Venture, an investment trust specialising in smaller listed companies on both sides of the Atlantic, has acquired a 14.56 per cent stake in Aberdeen Petroleum. Aberdeen, which is fighting a hostile 17p per share bid by Pittencrieff, the Edinburgh-based oil explorer and mobile communications company, notified the Stock Exchange yesterday that the trust had acquired 7.5m of its shares on Friday. The price was not disclosed.
