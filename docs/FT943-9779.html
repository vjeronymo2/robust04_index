940809 FT 09 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Johnson Fry Second Utilities Trust Johnson Fry Second Utilities Trust had a net asset value of 75.7p per ordinary income share at June 30. Total shareholder's funds at that date amounted to Pounds 36.3m, down 11.28 per cent on the Pounds 40.9m at the trust's inception in November; The FT-SE-A Utilities Index fell by 11.55 per cent during the same period. Revenue after tax for the period amounted to Pounds 1.22m for earnings of 4.22p per share. A second interim dividend of 1.5p brings the total to date to 3p.
