BFN [Text] Liberia's new justice minister, Councillor Laveli Supuwood, has called on fighters to turn in their arms and work toward enhancing the peace process. Minister Supuwood was speaking yesterday at the Executive Mansion following the commissioning of he and three other NPFL [National Patriotic Front of Liberia] nominees confirmed yesterday by the Transitional Assembly. He said Liberians should not be deprived of their peace and freedom of movement only to satisfy a handful of people. Minister Supuwood also called on the international community, friendly governments, and the business community to recognize the Liberian National Transitional Government, saying it is the legitimate government of the people of Liberia.
