December 9, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

YOU DON'T HAVE TO BE A COACH TO BASH ONE

Regarding Alma Kenyon's challenge to all of Terry Donahue's detractors to take up coaching and see if they could do better, I quote Samuel Johnson on criticism: "You may scold a carpenter who has made you a bad table, though you cannot make a table, it is not your trade to make tables."

It doesn't take an Al Davis to realize that Donahue has the motivational skills of a piece of toast (dry).

And if Terry needs some laughs, I taped the Arizona game.

SCOTT ANSELL, Los Angeles
