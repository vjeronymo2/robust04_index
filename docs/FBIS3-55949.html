Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Aleksandr Golyayev : "Trilateral Commission Begins..."] [Text] The Russian Trilateral Commission on Regulating Social and Labor Relations is resuming its work. Next Wednesday representatives of the government, Russian trade unions, and the country's entrepreneurs will gather at the Russian Federation Ministry of Labor in order to discuss a candidate nominated for the post of coordinator. It is proposed that the draft of a future general agreement also be scrutinized. This question will be on the agenda of the conference of leaders of regional and sector committees of trade unions belonging to the Federation of Independent Trade Unions of Russia taking place at the end of the week in the Moscow area.
