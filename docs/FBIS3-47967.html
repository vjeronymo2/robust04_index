Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] This morning the Mid-Term National Conference of the Communist Party of Vietnam [CPV] was solemnly opened at the Ba Dinh Conference Hall in Hanoi. In attendance were Central Committee advisers Pham Van Dong, Nguyen Van Linh, and Vo Chi Cong; 10 former CPV Central Committee Political Bureau members, and 647 delegates [number of delegates as heard]. Prior to the conference, delegates paid a tribute to President Ho Chi Minh at his mausoleum. The opening session began at 0900. After the band played the national anthem and the Internationale, Comrade Vo Van Kiet, member of the CPV Central Committee Political Bureau invited General Secretary Do Muoi to open the conference and to deliver the Seventh Party Central Committee's political report.
