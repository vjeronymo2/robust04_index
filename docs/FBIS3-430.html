Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA] has once again attacked Portugal. The UNITA commander in northern Angola today accused Portuguese marines of fighting alongside Angolan government troops in the struggle to retake positions in the oil-rich areas. In Portugal, the navy has denied this. According to Commander Camilo Alves there are no serving marines fighting on Angolan territory. [Begin Alves recording] First, we are not aware of the statements you have just referred to. However, despite not being aware of them, I can tell you that the navy denies any involvement of serving Portuguese marines in combat action in Angola. [end recording] [passage omitted]
