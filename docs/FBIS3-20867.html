Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:CSO [Article by nma: "HSS Without Executive Committee"] [Text] Zagreb -- According to a public announcement made by Dr. Josip Stojanovic, the following members of the HSS [Croatian Peasant Party] Executive Committee -- Prof. Dr. Ivan Basic, Prof. Ivan Medved, Stanislav Tomcik, Prof. Rudolf Zamuda, and Zeljko Pasaric -- have resigned because of "dissatisfaction with the leadership of Drago Stipac and the decision of the Main Committee to suspend Mirko Madjor and Dr. Josip Stojanovic." According to the announcement, "the HSS is left without an Executive Committee because the majority of its members have resigned." The suspended members of the Party Presidency, Mirko Madjor and Josip Stojanovic, will address some public meetings organized by the HSS field branches in Slavonia.
