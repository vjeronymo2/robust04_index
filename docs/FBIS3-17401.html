Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov has signed an instruction according to which the citizens of the Republic will perform their military service only on the territory of the peninsula. At the same time, Aleksandr Volkov, the military commandant of Crimea, said in an interview to INTERFAX that this document has been adopted without his approval and he intends to meet the president in the near future in order to discuss this issue. The matter is that the Military Commissariat of Crimea comes under the command of the Ukrainian Defense Ministry. In this connection, Aleksandr Volkov said that he is not going to fulfill this instruction and will act in accordance with orders from the Ukrainian president. He added that the Spring conscription in Crimea will be held in accordance with orders that were established earlier.
