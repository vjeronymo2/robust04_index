920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / UK Company News: RIT Capital asset value rises to 140.7p RIT Capital Partners achieved a 7 per cent rise in net asset value in the year to March 31, ending at 140.7p, against 131.7p previously. Investment income fell 19 per cent to Pounds 11.5m (Pounds 14.2m) reflecting the change in policy towards investing in equities against fixed interest securities. Interest payable jumped to Pounds 3.09m (Pounds 1.87m) and after tax of Pounds 2.38m (Pounds 3.07m) net profit declined 48 per cent to Pounds 3.7m (Pounds 7.17m). A dividend of 1.15p (2.44p) is proposed for the year on earnings per share of 1.69p (3.53p) undiluted and 1.61p (2.89p) diluted.
