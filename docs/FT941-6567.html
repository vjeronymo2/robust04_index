940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Braime Higher interest charges and lower income from investments led to pre-tax profits at TF&JH Braime (Holdings), the finished metal products, forging, pressing and stamping group, slipping from Pounds 582,606 to Pounds 544,412 in the year to December 31. Turnover, however, was up at Pounds 6.85m (Pounds 6.51m). Trading profits were Pounds 2,000 down at Pounds 482,877, while investment income declined to Pounds 111,640 (Pounds 125,314). Interest took Pounds 50,105 (Pounds 27,582). Unchanged final dividends of 6.5p are proposed for both the ordinary and A ordinary shares for same-again 8.75p totals. Earnings per share emerged at 23.29p (26.69p).
