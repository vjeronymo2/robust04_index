941201 FT 01 DEC 94 / World Trade News: Peugeot to assemble cars in Pakistan Automobiles Peugeot of France yesterday said it planned to assemble diesel cars in Pakistan from 1996 with Pakistani carmaker Worldwide Motors, AFX reports from Karachi. Mr Yves Barbe, Peugeot senior executive vice-president, said talks were at an 'advanced stage'. Peugeot intends to manufacture 10,000 cars there each year.
