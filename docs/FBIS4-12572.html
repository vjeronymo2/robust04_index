BFN [Text] AL-RA'Y AL-'AMM has learned from exclusive sources that a ranking Iraqi officer who was very close to Saddam Husayn has defected to the United States through Turkey after doubts were cast on his role in the attempt to topple the regime that was discovered two months ago, following which a number of ranking military commanders were executed. These sources said the officer had played a central role in preparing for the invasion of Kuwait, and was directly responsible for all cash payments and gifts that were given to top officials and a large number of journalists in some Arab countries in order to extend material and moral support for the Iraqi adventure against Kuwait. AL-RA'Y AL-'AMM also learned that the Iraqi officer will hold a news conference soon to reveal in figures and names the huge sums of money the Iraqi regime had given to some political and military officials and journalists shortly before the treacherous invasion.
