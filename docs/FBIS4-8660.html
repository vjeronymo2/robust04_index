BFN [Text] Prague, April 11 (CTK) -- Colombian Foreign Minister Nohemi Sanin Posada believes that Czech President Vaclav Havel, along with Pope John Paul II and former Soviet President Michail Gorbachev, are three people who have ended the process of the European totalitarianism, Havel's spokesman Ladislav Spacek told journalists after meeting between Posada and Havel today. He said that Posada had voiced her admiration at the restoration of political and economic freedoms in the Czech Republic. Havel and Posada agreed on the need to deepen cooperation between the Czech Republic and Columbia, especially in the economic, technical and scientific fields, Spacek said. Havel acquainted Posada with the basic transformation principles of the Czech Republic and primarily with the privatization process. Posada conveyed to Havel an invitation from Colombian President Cesar Gaviria Trujill to visit Columbia, Spacek said, adding that in his turn Havel invited the Colombian president to visit the Czech Republic.
