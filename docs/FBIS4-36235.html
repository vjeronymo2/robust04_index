BFN [Excerpt] [Announcer] The appointment of the Algerian diplomat Lakhdar Brahimi at the head of the UN fact-finding commission to Yemen has been received with great Yemeni and international satisfaction. Mr. Brahimi welcomes this mission to solve this fratricidal conflict. Over to you Abdelkader Djeridi. [Djeridi] Mr. Lakhdar Brahimi, who was appointed the day before yesterday at the head of a UN mission to Yemen, said to MBC that he would begin the implementation of this mission immediately in accordance with UN resolution 924 issued last Thursday. Mr. Brahimi, who has expressed his satisfaction with this mission and its acceptance by the parties in conflict, said that his mission was limited to fact-finding, getting acquainted with the situation existing in Yemen, and presenting a report on it to UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali.
