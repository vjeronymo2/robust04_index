BFN [Text] Beijing, May 4 (XINHUA) -- Wu Xueqian, vice-chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC) and president of the Chinese Association for International Understanding, met here yesterday with a Japanese molding industry delegation. The delegation, headed by Councillor of the Japanese Molding Industry Association Kiyoshi Hosokawa, arrived here on May 1 at the invitation of the Chinese Association for International Understanding. Hosokawa, an old friend of the Chinese people, started efforts in the 1950s to promote Sino-Japanese friendly exchanges, especially molding industry exchanges and cooperation. The delegation is also scheduled to visit Wuhan, Shanghai and Suzhou.
