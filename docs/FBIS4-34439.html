BFN [From the "Early Morning" newscast] [Text] PAN [National Action Party] Mexican presidential candidate Diego Fernandez de Cevallos commented briefly on Manuel Camacho Solis' resignation from the post of commissioner for peace and reconciliation in Chiapas. Fernandez de Cevallos characterized the decision as very respectable, adding that the text of the resignation is serious and dignified. Meanwhile, the Party of the Democratic Revolution in the Federal District said the resignation proves that there is a crisis within the PRI [Institutional Revolutionary Party]. Deputy Rene Bejarano said Camacho's resignation proves that he is in disagreement with those who want to reduce democratic demands to the simplest expression. He warned that Camacho Solis' resignation increases the likelihood of renewed hostilities in Chiapas at any time.
