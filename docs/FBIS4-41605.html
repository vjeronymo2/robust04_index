BFN [Text] A meeting of the Georgian Defense Ministry's newly appointed collegium was held on 23 June. The meeting was conducted by Lieutenant Genereal Vardiko Nadibaidze, the Georgian defense minister. Personnel problems, the provision of foodstuffs and material, and technical resources were the subjects of the straight talk held at the meeting. The defense minister sharply criticized some senior officials of the ministry for a lack of responsibility and negligence in their work that has caused a just resentment among the personnel in some of the military units. It has been indicated that a detailed program for the improvement of the current situation is to be worked up. Members of the military collegium of the Defense Ministry, as well as Armed Forces commanders have to be actively involved in the implementation of this program. Urgent assistance has been requested from the government and the authorities of the republic. It is necessary to secure maximum efforts, a major sense of responsibility, and emergency operational conditions in order for Georgia to have a single and strong Georgian Army. Special attention has been attached to problems revealed during the inspection of the 11th brigade. It has been indicated that the leadership of the republic demonstrates deep concern over the establishment of the country's regular army. Special attention toward military units will be increased. This information was supplied by the Defense Ministry press service.
