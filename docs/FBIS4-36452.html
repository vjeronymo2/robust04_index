BFN [Text] The cabinet held its ordinary weekly session yesterday under Prime Minister Ahmad Husayn Khudayyir. The prime minister began the meeting by extending in his name and on behalf of the ministers 'Id greetings to leader President Saddam Husayn, may God preserve and protect him, wishing his excellency continued good health and happiness and the Iraqis more pride and progress under his leadership. The cabinet then discussed the items on its agenda. The most important item was a study prepared by the Ministry of Agriculture on the situation and production methods of winter grain in Iraq. The report included average yield, production, marketed quantities of wheat and barley, and proposals. The cabinet adopted the necessary decisions and recommendations in this regard.
