BFN [From the "News" program] [Text] ROC [Republic of China] representative to Singapore Chen Yu-chu is scheduled to depart the city state on 26 April, at which time he will head to Denmark to assume his new diplomatic posting. Meanwhile, the newly appointed ROC representative to Singapore, Chiu Chin-yi, will fly to Singapore on 29 April to take up his new position. Chen is a senior diplomat in the ROC foreign service. He has served in his post in Singapore since May 1990. Over the past four years, many changes have transpired in relations between the ROC on Taiwan and Singapore. The most important change has been that Singapore established formal diplomatic relations with Communist China in August 1990. During his tenure, Chen has worked to maintain substantive ties between the two countries, exhibited in the increase in private exchanges between the two sides and strengthened trade relations. Government officials from both countries often commonly made calls to the other.
