Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report published under the rubric "Fact"] [Text] In an interview for the foreign press, the commander of Ukraine's Naval Forces, Volodymyr Bezkorovaynyy, said that our state does not consider the possibility of transferring its part of the Black Sea Fleet ships to Russia to repay the debt for oil and gas deliveries. The commander of Ukraine's Naval Forces said that the protocol signed in Massandra [in the Crimea] was a protocol on intentions. It was never regarded as a way out of the situation. According to Volodymyr Bezkorovaynyy, that question was studied and rejected.
