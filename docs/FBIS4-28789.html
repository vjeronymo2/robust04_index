BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA June 13 -- President of Kazakhstan N. Nazarbayev received in Almaty this morning Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet who arrived there yesterday for an official friendship visit. President Nazarbayev expressed his wish that Vietnam would share her experience in the economic renovation and inflation restriction with Kazakhstan. He said that in recent years, the relations of cooperation between Vietnam and Kazakhstan was slowly developed, the two-way trade value was at low level due to historic circumstance. At present, he said, in new conditions and with goodwill developing, their traditional cooperative and friendly relations should have a new step of development in such relations. The president highly valued Vietnam's achievements and experience in its renovation process, especially in the economic field. He affirmed that such achievements have been a great encouragement source to the Kazakhstan people. President Nazarbayev also expressed his belief that Vo Van Kiet's current visit would usher in a new era in the relations of friendship and cooperation between the two states and peoples. He asked P.M. Kiet to convey his best regards to President Le Duc Anh. For his part P.M. Kiet said that Vietnam has always followed with keen interest the Kazakhstan's situation, and attached great importance to the consolidation and enhancement of the friendship and cooperation with Kazakhstan and that the delegation's first visit to Kazakhstan would be a foundation for the promotion of friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries on the basis of equality and mutual benefit.
