940816 FT 16 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Manders expands in Australasia Manders, the coatings and printing inks group, is to acquire the printing inks operations of Skellerup Group, based in New Zealand and Australia, mainly trading as Morrison Printing Inks. Its turnover for the year to June 30 was NZDollars 46m (Pounds 17.9m) and profits before interest and tax NZDollars 3.9m.
