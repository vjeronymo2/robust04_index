BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] A three-year program for Turkmenistan's socioeconomic development was proposed at today's meeting of the republican parliament in Ashgabat. Our correspondent says it was drafted by President Niyazov. The program is entitled "10 Years of Prosperity" and contains specific presidential proposals. These include, for example, the idea that there should be a change in the ratio between the proportion of wages devoted to living expenses and the proportion set aside as savings. The leaders of contemporary Turkmenistan claim that citizens should be able to save 30-40 percent of their income and that all the necessary conditions have been created in the Republic for this.
