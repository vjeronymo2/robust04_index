February 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MCMARTIN 'RETRIAL' ON AFTERNOON TALK SHOWS

It was great to read that someone in the media could see through Geraldo Rivera's continuing witch hunt regarding the McMartin trial. Could you sense where the show was headed when Rivera started off with: "What went wrong with the McMartin trial?"

Nothing went wrong with the McMartin trial, except for the judge not demanding that the jurors keep their mouths shut afterward.

KENNETH TRITES, San Pedro
