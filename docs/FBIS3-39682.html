Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Hamburg (DPA) -- For Chancellor Helmut Kohl, the Serbs' withdrawal from the circle around Sarajevo is a "new chance" for peace in the former Yugoslavia. It is now possible "that the terrible murders will come to an end. First in this place and then in the others," Kohl said at the Hamburg Christian Democratic Union [CDU] Congress on Monday [21 February]. In the next few weeks, all one's power must be used to achieve an overall plan. Kohl advocated an international conference with "high-ranking" participants. The Germans must not hide in a corner "when storms are blowing. Nobody will buy that," Kohl said. Such an attitude is a "shame for our country."
