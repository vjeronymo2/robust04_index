BFN [Text] In a statement to REUTER today, Husayn al-Musawi, a Hizballah leader, confirmed the report on the abduction of al-Haj Mustafa al-Dirani. He said that this Israeli operation is part of the ongoing fighting between Hizballah and Israel. Al-Musawi added that Israel wants to use al-Dirani as a bargaining chip to insure the release of navigator Ron Arad. But this Israeli attempt will not succeed, he added.
