931008 FT 08 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Abbeycrest Abbeycrest, the designer and manufacturer of gold and silver jewellery, reported a reduced deficit in its seasonally unfavourable first half. On turnover ahead to Pounds 16.5m (Pounds 14.5m), pre-tax losses for the six months to June 30 were Pounds 345,000, down from Pounds 739,000, struck after net interest charges of Pounds 210,000 (Pounds 109,000). Losses per share worked through at 1p (2.1p); the interim dividend is maintained at 1.2p.
