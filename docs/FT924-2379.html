921212 FT 12 DEC 92 / Finance & The Family: Stewart Ivory launches unit trust - At a glance Stewart Ivory is launching a unit trust in the increasingly fashionable field of emerging markets. The trust will invest in areas such as south-east Asia and Latin America and will aim for capital growth. There is an initial charge of five per cent and an annual charge of 1.25 per cent; the minimum investment is Pounds 5,000. There is a one per cent discount on the 100p launch price between December 14 and January 10.
