Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, 24 Jan (ARMENPRES)--The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] Defense Army's Air Defense Forces shot down one of two Azerbaijani warplanes trying to bomb Stepanakert not far from the NKR's capital on 22 January. The pilot's fate is not known. In recent days, the enemy has actively used air force all along the war front. Yesterday, NKR Defense Army units halted Azerbaijani offensives in the direction of Mardakert, Agdam-Askeran, and Gadrut-Fizuli. Two Karabakh fighters died and six were wounded. The enemy lost 33 men. Karabakh positions have been coming under artillery and rocket shelling on the borders of Agdamskiy and Mardakertskiy Rayons since morning.
