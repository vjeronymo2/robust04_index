940427 FT 27 APR 94 / UK Company News: Emerald calls for Pounds 660,000 Emerald Energy, the Isle of Man-based energy producer which obtained a USM quote last November, is calling for Pounds 660,000 net via a placing of 25m shares at 2 3/4 p. The money raised will be used to purchase and develop further gasfields in the Appalachian Basin, West Virginia, close to its producing New Martinsville and Mannington fields. Following some refurbishment, the acquisition is expected to add about Pounds 300,000 to annual group profits.
