BFN [Excerpt] Sofia, May 26 (BTA) -- [passage omitted] The cabinet also heard a report on the preparation for the first session of an intergovernmental Bulgarian-Korean Commission for Economic, Scientific and Technical Cooperation due in late May in Sofia. The session will survey the economic and business relations between Bulgaria and the People's Democratic Republic of Korea over the 1991-1993 period and consider measures for to promote bilateral ties. It will also discuss the settlement of the reciprocal liabilities in transferable rubles and some draft accords. [passage omitted]
