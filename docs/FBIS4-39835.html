BFN [Text] Chisinau BASAPRESS, 9/6/1994 -- Yesterday, a certain Valeriy Arkan, who was previously sentenced to imprisonment made some statements broadcast on the ASKET television station. Arkan stated that he had been recruited by Major Yuriy Grushevskiy of the Dniester Security Ministry to spy on the Russian 14th Army command. He pointed out that his assignment was to take secret documents. Arkan mentioned that he was to find some documents proving the complicity of Colonel [Mikhail] Bergman [the commander of the Dniester garrison of the 14th Russian Army] and General Aleksandr Lebed [commander of the 14th Russian Army] in the assassination attempt against Dniester prosecutor Boris Luchik.
