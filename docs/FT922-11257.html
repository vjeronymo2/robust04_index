920427 FT 27 APR 92 / UK Company News: Usborne in red Usborne, the agricultural services, property and motor products group, fell into pre-tax losses of Pounds 4.12m in the 1991 year against profits of Pounds 1.63m. Continuing businesses were profitable but the results were hit by a Pounds 4.1m share of an associates loss and losses connected with discontinued businesses of Pounds 1.17m. The company said the continuing businesses made profits in the early months of the present year. Turnover was Pounds 210m (Pounds 193.9m). After a tax credit of Pounds 167,000 (Pounds 531,000 charge) and an extraordinary charge of Pounds 163,000 (Pounds 648,000) there was a loss for the year of Pounds 4.05m (Pounds 529,000). Losses per share were 6.03p (1.68p earnings). A reduced final dividend of 0.3p is proposed making 0.4p (1.5p) for the year.
