921229 FT 29 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Fairbriar restructures and seeks resumption of dealings FAIRBRIAR, the troubled property group which has been in administrative receivership since September last year, has applied for the suspension of its shares to be lifted with the announcement of a capital restructuring. The group proposes an offer of 7.67m new ordinary shares of 1p each, and the re-organisation of its existing share capital. The Royal Bank of Scotland will convert about Pounds 12m in borrowings into 4.13m new ordinary shares and 8.82m convertible preference shares. It will also convert some debt into a maximum of Pounds 10.6m in loan notes, in addition to providing further credit facilities. Pre-tax losses fell from Pounds 7.43m to Pounds 4m in the half year to September 30. Turnover amounted to Pounds 4.49m (Pounds 6.86m).
