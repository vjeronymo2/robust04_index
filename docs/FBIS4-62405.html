BFN [Text] The Azerbaijan Ministry of Defense Press Service has issued the following communique: The Armenian military forces maintained combat operations in Terterskiy and Agdamskiy Rayons today. The enemy shelled two towns. Azerbaijan's military units inflicted heavy casualties on the enemy forces and destroyed a considerable amount of military equipment. They have also liberated a town from the enemy. Our Army's officers and men have displayed heroism in the clashes. Violent clashes continued in Kelbadzharskiy, Terterskiy, and Agdamskiy Rayons today. Our troops have captured [words indistinct] in Seidlyar.
