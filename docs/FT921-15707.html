920108 FT 08 JAN 92 / International Company News: McDonnell stake deal delayed MCDONNELL Douglas said its agreement to sell a stake in its commercial aircraft business to a Taiwan consortium is now expected to close 'a few months' later than the end of January date originally anticipated, Reuter reports. 'Due diligence is taking longer than expected,' a spokesman said. In mid-November the company signed a memorandum of understanding to sell part of its beleaguered commercial aircraft business to Taiwan Aerospace for Dollars 2bn.
