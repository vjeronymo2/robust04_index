Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From "The News" newscast] [Text] Labor Minister Luis Horacio Vivas Pena announced the opening of the National Occupational Welfare, Health, and Safety Institute [Instituto Nacional de Prevision, Salud, y Seguridad Laboral] at Miraflores Palace today. This organization is entrusted with the responsibility of watching over the workers' physical and mental environments. [Begin Vivas recording] To further this objective, the law includes the establishment of two large organizations. One is the National Council and the other is the National Occupational Welfare, Health, and Safety Institute. The latter is the one that was established today. We inaugurated all its members, and they will begin advising the Executive Branch and union and business organizations on how to handle safety problems in the work place. [end recording]
