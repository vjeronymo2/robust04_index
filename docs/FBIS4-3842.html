BFN [Text] Brussels, April 22 (XINHUA) -- The North Atlantic Treaty Organization (NATO) issued an ultimatum here today to the Bosnian Serbs to stop their shelling of the besieged Muslim enclave of Gorazde or face air strikes. A NATO statement said that the Serbs must agree to an immediate cease-fire and withdraw their forces three kilometers from the center of the city of Gorazde or risk allied air raids as early as 0001 GMT Sunday.
