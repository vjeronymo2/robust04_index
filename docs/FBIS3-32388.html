Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Attorney General Luis Emilio Escobar Faella has asked the San Pedro de Ycuamandiyu trial judge to investigate a group of peasants from Tava'i Borda who are allegedly involved in guerrilla activities in the area. The attorney general has lodged a formal protest before the court against the peasants on charges of illegal association for criminal purposes, and for perpetrating crimes against persons and the security of the state. [passage omitted] The report adds that in the Tava'i Borda II area, groups of peasants are allegedly armed with guns, including submachine guns. The alleged leaders of the guerrilla group have been identified as Elvio Benitez and Venancio Zeballos. They reportedly persecute those who disagree with them and indoctrinating the rest to engage in unlawful practices in the area, such as stealing cattle and the illegal lumber trade. [passage omitted]
