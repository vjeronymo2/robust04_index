930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / People: British Alcan Aluminium BRITISH ALCAN ALUMINIUM, the subsidiary of Alcan Aluminium of Montreal, has appointed John Bridgeman as managing director. Bridgeman, 48, previously director of corporate planning at Alcan Aluminium, succeeds Doug Ritchie, British Alcan's chairman and chief executive, who died of cancer in Montreal, aged 52. He has held several senior posts at both British Alcan and at the parent company, which last week underwent a management shake-up in order to cut costs further and improve profitability; he will be responsible for most of the UK's aluminium production. Since 1990 Bridgeman has been a member of the Monopolies and Mergers Council; he is also a member of the Defence Science Advisory Council and a visiting professor of management at Keele University.
