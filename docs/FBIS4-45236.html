Circulation NC2405194594 Athens ATHENS NEWS in English 24 May 94 p 1 NC2405194594 Athens ATHENS NEWS English BFN [Text] Super-strong heroin, up to 55 percent pure, is circulating in Greece. Among confiscated samples sent for inspection to the state laboratory was the same heroin that killed Vasilis Tsaklof, containing 24 percent of the drug and 54 percent of lactose. That combination in itself is not necessarily fatal but the victim had combined it with Hypnostedon pills. Attica police headquarters said that heroin in the Greek market used to be only ten to 15 percent pure. The state lab inspected ten different samples of the drug, confiscated in Athens, and discovered that their heroin content ranged from 22 to 55 percent. The number of victims this month in Athens from "instant death" heroin has now risen to 15 whilst heroin addict Dhimos Tsagalidhis, 27, is in a coma at a hospital in Serres, northern Greece.
