BFN [Text] Concerning Western Sahara, we note this statement by the Algerian foreign minister, as reported by the Moroccan daily L'OPINION. According to L'OPINION, Mr. Mohammed Salah Dembri said that a political solution that could bypass a referendum in Western Sahara is possible. Asked to be more precise, the Algerian foreign minister stressed that such a political solution would be a structure which would accommodate the Western Saharan status within a Moroccan entity.
