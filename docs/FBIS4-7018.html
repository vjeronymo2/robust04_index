BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 22 (KCNA) -- Kim Hyon-chun, chairman of the South Korean Federation of General Student Councils (Hanchongnyon), at a recent press conference expressed the resolve of his federation to win in its confrontation with the pro-U.S. traitorous "government," a South Korean newspaper reported. Noting that "Hanchongnyon at its congress of second-term representatives early this month branded the Kim Yong-sam regime as a pro-U.S. traitorous regime," Kim Hyon-chun said: "We will unite all people including the one million students under the slogan of independence, democracy and reunification and lay a foothold for victory in our confrontation with the Kim Yong-sam regime."
