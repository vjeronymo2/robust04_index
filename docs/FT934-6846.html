931123 FT 23 NOV 93 / Letters to the Editor: A familiar comment on the welfare state Sir, 'Joe Rogaly is right to call for a new Beveridge Report,' writes Frank Field. 'The overall effect of our present welfare state is unsatisfactory not only because many benefits are paid at a relatively low level but because its provisions trap into poverty an increasing number of people . . .'
