930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Regulation task force heads named MR MICHAEL Heseltine, trade and industry secretary, yesterday announced the businessmen who will head seven private-sector task forces to advise ministers on where regulation should be simplified or repealed. They are: Mr Duncan Bluck, chairman of John Swire & Sons (communications and transport); Mr Chris Spackman, managing director of Bovis Construction (construction); Mr Malcolm Bates, deputy managing director of GEC (engineering industries); Mr John Robb, chief executive of Wellcome (chemicals and pharmaceuticals); Mr Nigel Whittaker, corporate affairs director of Kingfisher (other services); Mr Michael Heron, chairman of the Post Office (food, drink and agriculture).
