BFN [Text] Islamabad, May 10 (XINHUA) -- All Pak-China Friendship Association hosted a reception here tonight to celebrate the 40th anniversary of the founding of Chinese People's Association for Friendship With Foreign Countries (CPAFFC). Addressing the function, Chinese Ambassador to Pakistan Zhou Gang, president of All Pak-China Friendship Association Iqbal M. Shafi and former Pakistan Ambassador to China M. A. Bhatty highly praised Pak-China friendship and the role of CPAFFC in promoting the friendship and mutual understanding between the Chinese people and other peoples in the world. "The Chinese people will forever remain the trustworthy friend of Pakistan," said Ambassador Zhou Gang. "it is the steadfast policy of the Chinese Government to further cement and develop friendship and cooperation with Pakistan," he added. CPAFFC, as a non-governmental organization founded in 1954, has established contacts and relations of friendship and cooperation with more than 100 organizations in over 90 countries.
