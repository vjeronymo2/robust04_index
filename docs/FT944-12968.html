941029 FT 29 OCT 94 / Finance and the Family: Bonds stir the markets - At a Glance Wall Street had been expecting it for some time, but when the yield on the 30-year US government bond closed above 8 per cent for the first time in 2 1/2 years on Monday night, it sent a shudder through financial markets. Bond prices have been falling - and correspondingly, yields rising - since late last year.
