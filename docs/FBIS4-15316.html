BFN [Text] [No dateline as received] Russia will face new ordeals in forthcoming months, announced the Russian president adviser and politologist Andranik Migranyan, appearing at a meeting with professorial staff of the Yerevan University. As reports the "HAYASTAN" newspaper today, Migranyan has grounded such a perspective on a fact that Russia experiences now an economic instability, so that important and cardinal decisions are expected in the nearest two months to lead the country out of crisis. He told that emergency measures introduction in economy and industry is being discussed already for some time in the government circles. Migranyan estimated as nearly 40 percent the democrats' votes in Russian Duma, while there is no yet unity among the rights, he said. The politologist negatively characterized the Nazarbayev's idea of Eurasian Union, calling this initiative a "political bluff". Speaking of the Karabakh problem, Migranyan expressed an opinion that the consistent policy of Russia will ultimately result in a settlement of conflict. However to reach this objective the involved parties shall not assume delirious ideas as their starting point, but show instead the readiness for compromise.
