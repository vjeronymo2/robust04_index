930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Wrexham Water Wrexham and East Denbighshire Water Company lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.31m to Pounds 2.6m over the 12 months to March 31. Turnover amounted to Pounds 10.3m, against Pounds 9.16m. Adjusted earnings worked through at 345p (175p). A recommended final dividend of 92.45p brings the total per 4.9 per cent participating ordinary to 134.9p (66.12p).
