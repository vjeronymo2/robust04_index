941128 FT 28 NOV 94 / Arts (Openings): Washington The internationally-acclaimed Oslo Philharmonic Orchestra is touring North America this week and next with its chief conductor Mariss Jansons. Its first major stop is the Kennedy Center on Wednesday, followed by Boston, Toronto, New York and Chicago. Tour programmes include works by Bartok, Berlioz, Beethoven and Shostakovich, with soloists Truls Mork and Yefim Bronfman.
