920115 FT 15 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Exeter Preferred target increased Greig Middleton, the broker, has increased its target for the Exeter Preferred Capital Investment Trust from Pounds 50m to Pounds 60m, having raised Pounds 40m in pre-placing. The prospective redemption yield on the ordinary shares, which will accrue largely in terms of capital growth, has risen from 13.5 to 13.6 per cent. The application list opens today.
