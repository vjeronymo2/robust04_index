BFN [Text] The radio of the Israeli enemy army has reported that rebel John Garang paid a secret visit to Israel and met the prime minister of the Israeli enemy, Yitzhaq Rabin, and discussed with him the possibility of providing assistance to the forces of the rebel movement. The Israeli radio said rebel Garang had also met Mordechai Gur, the Israeli enemy's minister of war [name and title as heard], and heads of security organs in Israel.
