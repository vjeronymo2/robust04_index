BFN [Text] Ceremonial meetings have been held at a monument in Tbilisi to the Georgian language being the mother tongue every year on 14 April. But today, the public garden and the monument were controlled by Tbilisi police forces. Zviadists planned to hold a rally there in the customary way but the police did not permit them to gather. A column of people then moved toward the Russian Embassy to hold a protest action but the police also blocked it. Several people have been detained. At the moment the police control the area adjacent to the Marjanishvili metro station.
