Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 26 Dec (MENA)--Security men this week arrested a number of elements engaged in unlawful acts, including a gang specializing in manufacturing weapons and selling them at exorbitant prices. Major General Mahmud Wajdi, director of the Cairo Investigation Administration, has stated that the elements were arrested as part of the campaigns ordered by Interior Minister Hasan al-Alfi to root out any activity emanating from the slums to disrupt public order. He added that during these campaigns, two workshops for manufacturing weapons in the al-Sharrabiyah and Shubra al-Khaymah Districts of Cairo had been uncovered where 19 firearms and 150 types of weapons--including knives, metal bars, and daggers--were found.
