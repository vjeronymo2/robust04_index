920522 FT 22 MAY 92 / ITV censured over Eurodisney THE Independent Television Commission yesterday censured ITV for a breach of its code of practice on sponsorship over of its coverage of the opening of the Eurodisney resort in France. The ITC told London Weekend Television, the ITV company that produced the programme, that it considered the extent and degree of promotional material in the programme excessive. A substantial portion of the coverage, the ITC noted, had been provided by Buena Vista Productions, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Disney.
