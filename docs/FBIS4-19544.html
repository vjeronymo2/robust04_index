BFN [Text] On Friday, [15 April], the Triomphant, the first of France's second-generation missile-launching nuclear submarines, made its maiden voyage from the naval port of Cherbourg. It will undergo a period of testing before entering into active service in 1997. During the next few days, the 13,000-tonne vessel will make its first static submersion off Cherbourg, and it is scheduled to arrive in Brest within two weeks. According to its manufacturers, it is "the most silent submarine in the world."
