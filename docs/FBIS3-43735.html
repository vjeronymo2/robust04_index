Language: Tigrinya Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] President Isayas Afewerki yesterday afternoon met and held talks with the Chinese delegation led by Mr. Qian Qichen, the PRC deputy prime minister and foreign minister. During the talks, President Isayas Afewerki and Mr. Qian Qichen extensively discussed bilateral relations between Eritrea and China and ways and means of resolving the conflicts in the Horn of Africa. The two sides noted that the solutions to the current economic problems facing Africa are dependent on regional and continental cooperation. Meanwhile Mr. Qian Qichen extended Chinese President Jiang Zemin's invitation to President Isayas Afewerki to undertake an official visit to China. [passage omitted]
