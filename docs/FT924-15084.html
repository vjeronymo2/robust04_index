921007 FT 07 OCT 92 / Company News In Brief CAPITA GROUP has acquired Bristol-based Revenue Collection Services, a provider of debt collection services primarily for local authorities, for a total of Pounds 1.24m in shares. ***** GESTETNER HOLDINGS is to acquire the operations of Ricoh Canada. Following the purchase Gestetner will have total Canadian revenues approaching CDollars 150m (Pounds 67.5m) annually. ***** SEET has disposed of Kenneth Mackenzie Holdings, a producer and exporter of Harris Tweed, to Kenneth Macleod (Shawbost) for Pounds 1.75m in cash. Mackenzie incurred pre-tax losses of Pounds 780,000 and Pounds 259,000 respectively for the years ending April 30 1991 and 1992.
