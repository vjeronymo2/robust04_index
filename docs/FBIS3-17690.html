Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Cabinet of Ministers press service told the CONTACT news agency that fierce battles were under way for the fourth day in the Gali Rayon of the Kodori Gorge. Deputy Prime Minister Avtandil Margiani and MP Jaba Ioseliani, the chairman of the Provisional Committee for Emergency Situations, are in the war zone. The Abkhazian separatists have opened artillery fire on the civilian population of the villages of Sakeni, Azhara, Omarshara, and Gvandra. Despite the complex meteorological conditions, it became possible to evacuate the wounded and women and children. Every measure has been taken to supply the region with medicine and foodstuffs. Today, according to the information received by 1700, positional battles continued in the village of Zemo Lata. Separatists are getting additional manpower and combat equipment. Everything is being done to repel the separatists' attacks.
