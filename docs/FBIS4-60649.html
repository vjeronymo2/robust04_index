BFN [Text] Regarding the Arab stand vis-a-vis the Yemeni southerners' secession declaration and the proclamation of an independent state, 'Abd-al-'Aziz 'Abd-al-Ghani, member of the Yemeni Presidential Council, told told our radio correspondent Ahmad Birru: [Begin 'Abd-al-Ghani recording] In fact, we do not believe that the United Arab Emirates [UAE] has recognized the secessionist state or the secession declaration announced by 'Ali Salim al-Bid. The UAE has always been with unity, particularly Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan, who supported Yemen unity. We do not believe any Arab or foreign state will recognize this secession declaration announced by 'Ali Salim al-Bid, because it is unconstitutional. We do not want to impose unity by force, but (?through peaceful means). The whole Yemeni people in the southern, eastern, northern, and western governorates support unity. Those who are imposing state authority in the southern governorates are government forces. These forces are not from the northern governorates, but from northern and southern governorates. The northern governorates are not imposing unity on the southern governorates. There is readiness for unity. As it was achieved peacefully, it will continue so. All the Yemeni people support unity. [end recording]
