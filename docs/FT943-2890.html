940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Sharp midway increase for Cala to Pounds 3.8m Improved profitability in its Scottish market helped Cala, the Edinburgh-based housebuilder and property developer, to report a sharp increase in profits in the year to June 30. The pre-tax figure jumped from Pounds 226,000 to Pounds 3.76m on sales little changed at Pounds 77.7m. The dividend for the year has been increased by 22 per cent to 2.8p (2.3). This included a proposed final pay-out of 1.9p - 0.1p higher than forecast at the time of its rights issue in March. Earnings per share were 7.25p (1.98p). Net borrowings at the year end were Pounds 4m (Pounds 11.1m).
