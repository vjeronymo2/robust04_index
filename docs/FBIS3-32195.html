Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "NTC News" newscast] [Text] The DAS [Administrative Department of Security] captured the third in command of the EPL [Popular Liberation Army] this morning in Cartagena. The man, identified as Elviro Meneses, is charged with being chief recruiter for the people's militia and possible involvement in the massacre of 35 people in Apartado. He will be transferred to Santa Fe de Bogota in a matter of hours.
