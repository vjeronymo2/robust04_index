BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 Jun (MENA) -- MENA has learned that the issue of choosing a host country for the Nonaligned Movement's next summit has not been decided yet, despite some rumors that Iran, Morocco, and Egypt may submit requests. In a statement to MENA, Iranian Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati denied that Iran will submit a request if any of the Latin American group countries does not ask to host the summit, according to the geographical order under which the movement operates. Egyptian diplomatic sources said that if there is no candidate from the Latin American countries, one of the Asian group countries may be nominated to host the next summit.
