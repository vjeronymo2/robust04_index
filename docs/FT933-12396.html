930724 FT 24 JUL 93 / OFT warns over timeshare activity SIR Bryan Carsberg, director-general of fair trading, yesterday warned a Manchester-based company that it would face legal action if it con-tinued trying to persuade timeshare owners to sell property at a fraction of its value. He said he had received complaints that the company, CR Properties, had telephoned Club Riviera timeshare owners telling them their resorts were going to be demolished or used to house homeless Spanish people.
