941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / UK Company News: City of London PR rises 22% to Pounds 394,000 at interim stage City of London PR Group, the USM-traded specialist investor relations and market research company, yesterday announced a 22 per cent rise in interim pre-tax profits and said the second half should 'at least match' the Pounds 394,000 just announced. On sales of Pounds 1.36m (Pounds 1.29m) in the six months to September 30, operating profit was 39 per cent up at Pounds 172,000 (Pounds 124,000), despite some softness in the market research business. Profit on disposal of investments came to Pounds 50,000 (Pounds 23,000). The interim is increased to 1.4p (1.27p) on earnings per share of 3.71p (3.17p).
