August 21, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL ROUNDUP: BUGLE BOY ADDS MEXICAN LICENSEE

Bugle Boy Industries, a Simi Valley apparel concern, said it licensed Distribuidora De Pantalones in Mexico City to make and market Bugle Boy-labeled clothing in Mexico.

The Mexican company is Bugle Boy's fifth foreign licensee. The others are in Canada, Japan, Australia and Panama.

Bugle Boy specializes in young men's and boys' pants and other casual apparel.
