BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- CDU [Christian Democratic Union] chairman, Federal Chancellor Helmut Kohl, says he is "very happy" with his party's performance at the Saxony-Anhalt parliamentary election. Looking ahead to the [October] general elections, I see reason for optimism, Kohl said after a CDU Presidium session in Bonn today. However, the CDU will have to continue to fight for every vote.
