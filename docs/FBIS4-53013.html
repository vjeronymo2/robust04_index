BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 15 (KCNA) -- A general meeting of the Wiener-Neustadt branch of the society for the promotion of the relations between Austria and the Democratic People's Republic of Korea was held on May 5. At the meeting the chairman of the branch reviewed the achievements and experience the branch has made in its activities over the past 15 years. Referring to the vicious anti-DPRK campaign launched by the United States and its followers with a row over nuclear suspicion to stifle the DPRK, a socialist state, he said they always stand on the side of the Korean people. "We will fully support the Korean people in the just struggle for national reunification and strive to further expand and develop the many-sided friendly and cooperative relations between the peoples of Austria and Korea," he declared.
