930408 FT 08 APR 93 / UK Company News: Severfield-Reeve Severfield-Reeve, the USM-quoted steel group, plans to raise about Pounds 1.55m through a 1-for-2 rights issue at 32p. The issue, of 5.22m new ordinary, has been fully underwritten by Beeson Gregory. The proceeds will be used to reduce borrowings and expand the capital base. The cash call accompanied a Pounds 987,000 pre-tax loss for 1992 on turnover of Pounds 15.6m. Last time there were profits of Pounds 530,000 on turnover of Pounds 16.9m. Losses per share amounted to 6.08p (4.18p earnings) and there is no dividend for the year (1p). Directors expect to resume payments at a modest level in the current year.
