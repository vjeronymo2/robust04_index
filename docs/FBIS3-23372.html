Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Algiers, March 19 (AFP) -- A passenger train linking Algiers to Bejaia in eastern Algeria was attacked by an armed group and set ablaze Friday evening, the second such attack in nine days, security services said here Saturday. Some 300 passengers were forced off the train before its 11 carriages were set on fire near Amal tunnel, sources added. There was no word on whether the passengers had been mistreated, nor on the number of attackers. On March 10, a 12-carriage train linking Algiers to Chlef in the west was destroyed by fire after being attacked by an armed group.
