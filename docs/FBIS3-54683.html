Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN ["Text" of President Aliyev's decree calling up recruits; date not given] [Text] Azerbaijan's citizens who were born between 1967 and 1975 and who became 18 years old prior to the call-up date are called upon to register for active military service from 3 to 23 January. The call up applies to citizens who are not legally exempt from serving in the Azerbaijan Armed Forces and the other legal military units. According to the law, citizens who have attained call-up age but who have not enlisted for active military duty have a responsibility to work in alternative services. The Azerbaijan Council of Ministers takes the necessary measures within the framework of the law to have this decree put into effect. [Signed] President Geydar Aliyev
