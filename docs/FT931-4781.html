930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / Licensed trade federation formed THE NATIONAL Licensed Victuallers Association, which represented self-employed pub licensees until it folded in December, was relaunched yesterday as the National Federation of Licensed Retailers. The new organisation has affiliated to the Federation of Small Businesses, which has 58,000 members. When the association closed it had 4,000 members and the federation expects that between 2,500 and 3,000 members will join the new organisation.
