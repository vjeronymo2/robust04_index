BFN [Text] Colombian Foreign Minister Noemi Sanin will arrive for an official visit to Cuba on 3 May. This visit reconfirms bilateral ties between Bogota and Havana since diplomatic relations were reestablished on 28 October 1993. Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina officially visited Colombia on December 1993. Following Robaina's visit, the executive secretary of the Council of Ministers visited Colombia and at the time, several commercial agreements were signed.
