BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on criminal activity monitored from Havana radio sources in Spanish between 26 May and 5 June. The Havana police have arrested a group of thieves, who were prepared to steal parabolic antennas and automobile batteries in Diez de Octubre Municipality, Havana City Province. Batteries and antennas were repossessed from the people who purchased them from the group to manufacture generators. Seven suspects are currently in jail awaiting trial. (Radio Progreso Network in Spanish 1100 GMT 26 May) "A total of 415 violators of the self-employment law have been fined in Matanzas Province to date. A total of 200 of the fined citizens in Varadero are from other provinces." (Radio Rebelde Network in Spanish 0900 GMT 3 June)
