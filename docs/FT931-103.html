930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Unidare rights to fund US buy Unidare, the Dublin-based industrial holding group, announced a 1-for-2 rights issue to part finance the acquisition of Nasco, a US wholesale distributor of welding equipment. The issue will raise IPounds 16.2m from 6.4m new ordinary shares at 265p apiece, fully underwritten by Riada Corporate Finance. An initial consideration of IPounds 28.8m will be paid for Nasco. If profits before interest and tax exceed Dollars 7m in the year ending June 30 1995, an additional consideration of six times the excess, up to a maximum of Dollars 18m will be paid. In the year to June 1992, Nasco made a pre-tax profit of Dollars 5.8m on turnover of Dollars 81.2m. Unidare's pre-tax profits were IPounds 5.4m on turnover of Pounds 113.9m for the 1992 year.
