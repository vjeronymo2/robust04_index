920305 FT 05 MAR 92 / International Capital Markets: Nomura arm in Taiwan venture NOMURA Securities Invest-ment Trust Management, a subsidiary of the big Japanese brokerage, has applied to set up an investment trust company in Taiwan, together with foreign and local partners, AP-DJ reports from Tokyo. Nomura and Sanyo Investment Trust of Japan, SG Warburg Securities and Shinkong Group of Taiwan have applied to set up a joint venture investment trust unit, capitalised at TDollars 300m.
