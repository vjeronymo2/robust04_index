940516 FT 16 MAY 94 / Council leader to fight by-election Mr Gerry Sutcliffe, the leader of Bradford City Council, was yesterday selected as Labour candidate for the by-election in Bradford South. The vacancy was caused by the death in a road accident last month of the sitting Labour MP, Bob Cryer. Mr Sutcliffe will be defending a Labour majority of 4,902 over the Conservatives at the last general election.
