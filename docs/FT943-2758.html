940917 FT 17 SEP 94 / UK Company News: CH Bailey in red CH Bailey, whose principal activities are engineering, ship repairing, leisure and business and financial management, incurred pre-tax losses of Pounds 189,710 for the year ended March 25 1994, compared with profits of Pounds 446,379. Turnover was down 27 per cent from Pounds 3.71m to Pounds 2.7m. Losses per share were 0.32p (0.74p earnings).
