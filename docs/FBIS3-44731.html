Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] On 27 December, Governor Tian Chengping and Vice Governor Liu Guanghe, together with leaders from the provincial government office and the provincial industrial and commercial bureau, inspected Huangyuan County. After listening to briefings from some of the county leaders on 1993's economic work, they went to visit a Chinese-foreign joint-venture company. [passage omitted] During this inspection, he talked about future rural work. He said: While the planned economy is transforming into a market economy, our future rural work should focus on increasing peasants' income. The peasants should be encouraged to make every possible effort to increase their incomes. There is a need to continue to adjust the production structure. In the course of grain production, the acreage of crops should be expanded. Efforts should be focused on comprehensive agricultural development to improve agricultural production capacity. In rural areas, township and town enterprises should be developed and development projects should be selected in line with local conditions. [passage omitted] On how to expedite the establishment of the socialist market economic structure, Governor Tian Chengping said: We should concentrate our efforts on studying the actual situation and seek the main factors favorable to the formation of markets. [passage omitted] He also hoped that all local leaders and relevant departments would do their best to ensure commodity supplies during the New Year and the Spring Festival so that the masses could spend the holidays happily and harmoniously.
