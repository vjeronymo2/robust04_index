BFN [RIA report: "Russian AES's Plan To Increase Power Generation" -- from page I of FINANSOVYYE IZVESTIYA section] [Text] According to Russian Atomic Energy Minister Viktor Mikhaylov, by the year 2010 it is planned to increase the amount of power generated at Russia's AES's [nuclear electric power stations] by a factor of 1.5. The country's AES's currently generate 22,000 megawatts, around 12 percent of all the power produced in the country. The proportion of atomic energy is expected to rise over the next 15 years to 20 percent. In the near future the Ministry of Atomic Energy plans to bring on stream the Kalinin, Kursk, and Novovoronezh AES's.
