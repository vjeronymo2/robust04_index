Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Following today's attack by the Zionists on fasting worshippers in the Tomb of His Holiness Abraham -- may peace be upon him -- in the occupied town of Hebron, martyring tens and wounding hundreds of Palestinian Muslims, the spokesman of our country's Foreign Ministry condemned this dastardly act, and said that the attack is another sign that the the policy of oppression and massacre is still in progress in the occupied territories. According to the Central News Unit's report, the spokesman referred to this as a horrific massacre that was doubtless carried out with the backing of the military forces of the Zionist regime, and he called on human rights organizations and world public opinion to react seriously to this horrifying crime. He said: Silence and indifference of the countries supporting the Zionist regime will encourage that regime to continue its policies. The Foreign Ministry spokesman expressed the sympathy of the government of the Islamic Republic of Iran with the families of those martyred by this horrific crime.
