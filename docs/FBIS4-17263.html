BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondent for TASS] [Text] Kiev 22 April, TASS--Extra Ukrainian peacekeepers flew off on Thursday night for the Bosnian capital of Sarajevo. On the eve of its departure, the Ukrainian battalion was visited by a group of the Republic's Defence Ministry high-ranking officers headed by Lieutenant-General Anatoliy Kobzar. According to him, Ukrainian peacekeeping troops successfully fulfil their duties in Sarajevo and Bosnia on the whole, while the commanders of the Ukrainian battalion are doing their utmost to avoid casualties among the republic's peacekeepers in the composition of U.N. forces in former Yugoslavia.
