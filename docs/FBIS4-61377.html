BFN [Text] Yerevan ("LRAGIR", "AZG" dailies), 05/04/94 -- The Azerbaijani mass media spread information on April 30, telling that Lezghin soldiers are fighting in the Karabakh Army. The RMK [Republic of Mountainous Karabakh] defense army headquarters confirmed this information, noting that indeed, 23 Lezghin volunteers have recently arrived to Stepanakert from Khachmach and Khudat districts at the north of Azerbaijan, and expressed a desire to fight against the Azerbaijani army. The commander of the Lezghin group Hajibek said, not only the fortune of Armenians but also Lezghins, Talyshs and other national minorities, whose historical lands were joined to Azerbaijan by Stalin's order, is now being solved in Karabakh.
