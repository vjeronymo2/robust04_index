BFN [Text] Cairo, 28 Apr (MENA)--Security men today arrested the killer of Captain Khalid Mustafa of the al-Hammam police force in Marsa Matruh, who was killed by a bullet last night. A security source in the Interior Ministry stated that defendant Fayiz Miftah Muhammad Sulayman admitted committing the crime and led the police to the weapon he used, an unregistered double-barreled hunting rifle. Capt. Khalid was killed and a police detective was injured in the shoot-out after they carried out a job arresting a defendant in the al-Hammam area in Marsa Matruh. The defendant was hunting in the area. When he saw the police force approaching, he tried to escape and during the chase he fired at the police force, killing Capt. Khalid and injuring a soldier.
