930129 FT 29 JAN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & vegetables Navel oranges are this week's best buy at 10-30p each depending on size reports the FFVIB. White and pink fleshed grapefruit continue to be a good buy at 12-18p each for white and 25-30p each for pink. Kiwifruit are an excellent buy at 8-12p each, Lemons are at 15-25p each and apples remain at a stable price of 35-45p a lb for Cox. Dutch and English leeks are very good quality this week at 35-55p a lb, Broccoli at 45-60p per 8oz pre-pack, Savoy Cabbage at 25-35p a lb and potatoes are 10-14p a lb. Red, green and yellow peppers have come down in price this week and are at 70-90p a lb.
