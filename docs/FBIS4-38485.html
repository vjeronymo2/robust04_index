BFN [Unattributed report under the "Hold The Front Page!" rubric: "CIS Troops Brought Into Moscow"] [Text] It has come to MOSKOVSKIY KOMSOMOLETS's attention that the Main Staff of the CIS Joint Forces has finally ceased to exist. It has been transformed into a staff for coordination in the area of military cooperation. Out of the 480 officers, 360 have retired. The remaining 120 Russian staff officers will be supplemented by 120 officers from the CIS-states' armed forces. Russian military men who have gone through the General Staff school are not forming a high opinion about the newly arriving officers. At the moment the staff for coordination of military cooperation has begun to fulfill its first mission -- to form the military part of a concept of military security of... Russia.
