September 23, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

JACK NICHOLSON

Ah, how comforting to know that Hollywood celebrities are human like the rest of us. Witness the pimple in the crease of Jack Nicholson's nose.

Why, I suspect that even these pampered, highly overpaid prima donnas put their pants on, one leg at a time, like Joe and Joanie Ordinary.

IRMA E. BAKER

La Canada Flintridge
