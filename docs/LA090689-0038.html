September 6, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

St. Louis Cardinal relief pitcher Todd Worrell will be examined today by Dr. Frank Jobe and may require surgery to repair an injured elbow.

Worrell, 3-5 with a 2.96 earned-run average, would probably miss the remainder of the season if he sustained a torn ligament, team spokesman Kip Ingle said. If there is only muscular damage, the right-hander would be placed on a rehabilitation program and could return in a few weeks, Ingle said.
