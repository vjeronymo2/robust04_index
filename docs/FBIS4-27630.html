BFN [Excerpt] Prime Minister Datuk Sri Dr. Mahathir Mohamed bade farewell to Turkmenistan President Saparmurad Niyazov at Carcosa Seri Negara [State Guest House] in Kuala Lumpur today. Niyazov was on a three-day official visit with a 45-member delegation, including 5 deputy prime ministers, and six provincial governors. During the visit agreements were signed between Malaysian and Turkmenistan officials to enhance bilateral cooperation between the two countries. [passage omitted]
