Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Article by "kch"-signed: "Speculations Damaged the Military Academy"] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] Vladimir Vetchy, chairman of the trade union organization at the Brno Military Academy, specified that experts of a productive age have been leaving the school, influenced by distorted information about its coming closure. An association was even organized to find immediate employment for them in the civilian sector under advantageous financial conditions. The representatives of the Military Academy also denied reports yesterday about the students at the newly opened Air Force and Air Defense Faculty using Brno airport. The training of Brno pilots is to take place in Pardubice. The Infantry Military School in Vyskov will specialize in the preparation of lower level commanders, and the third military academy in the Republic is the Military Medical School in Hradec Kralove.
