941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Cairn Energy Onshore CAIRN ENERGY Onshore, the wholly owned subsidiary of Cairn Energy, is acquiring Monument Petroleum Mitre's 25 per cent interests in UK onshore production licence PL182 and onshore exploration licence EXL287 for Pounds 1.1m, bringing Cairn's interest in them to 100 per cent.
