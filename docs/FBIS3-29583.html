Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] A goodwill delegation led by Mr. Ali Alatas, foreign affairs minister of the Republic of Indonesia, called on Vice Admiral Maung Maung Khin, member of the State Law and Order Restoration Council [SLORC] and deputy prime minister, at the latter's office at 1100 this morning. At the meeting, cordial and frank talks were held on bilateral economic cooperation matters and bilateral relations.
