FeCoZr Alloys [.sub]90-x[/][.sub]x[/][.sub]10[/] 40100057F Beijing JINSHU XUEBAO [ACTA METALLURGICA SINICA] in Chinese Vol 30 No 3, Mar 94 pp B126-B129 40100057F Beijing JINSHU XUEBAO [ACTA METALLURGICA SINICA] in Chinese Vol 30 No 3, Mar 94 pp B126-B129 Chinese CSO [English abstract of article by Lu Zhichao and Xianyu Ze of Northeastern University, Shenyang, Li Jizhou, Ye Chuntang, Li Zhuqi, and Kang Jian of the Institute of Atomic Energy, Beijing, Shen Baogen of the Institute of Physics Research, CAS, Beijing, and Lu Mangqi of the Institute of Metals Research, CAS, Shenyang; MS received 26 Feb 93, revised 10 Aug 93] [Text] The magneto-volume effect and the generalized phonon densities of states for amorphous Fe[.sub]90-x[/]Co[.sub]x[/]Zr[.sub]10[/] alloys have been studied. The average number of valence electrons N[.sub]eff[/] dependences of the spontaneous magnetostriction _o_[.sub]S[/] and the average magnetic moment [.sub]FeCo[/] have been obtained, and it has been found that the _o_[.sub]S[/] takes a maximum at N[.sub]eff[/] = 8.1-8.2 where the [.sub]FeCo[/] begins to decrease drastically. The generalized phonon densities of states (PDOS) were deduced from inelastic neutron scattering experiments, and the phonon softening phenomenon in amorphous Invar alloy was observed from phonon spectra.
