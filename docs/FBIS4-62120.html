BFN [Text] Our radio station has learned that the issue of releasing Sergey Shakhray from his duties as minister for federation affairs, while at the same time retaining him as vice premier, has almost been settled. By all accounts the new minister for federation affairs will be Nikolay Yegorov, currently Krasnodar kray head of administration and a member of the Federation Council . Sergey Shakhray has refused to confirm or deny this information.
