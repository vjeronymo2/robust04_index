920212 FT 12 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Fleming Overseas net assets at 222.5p The net asset value of The Fleming Overseas Investment Trust stood at 222.5p per share as at December 31 1991. The figure represented a gain of 19 per cent on the same stage of 1990, but a decline of some 7 per cent on the trust's year-end figure of 239.6p. Distributable revenue for the six months to end-December amounted to Pounds 2.37m (Pounds 2.48m) for earnings of 1.78p (1.86p) per share. The interim dividend is maintained at 1.5p.
