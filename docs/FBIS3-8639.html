Language: Romanian Article Type:BFN [Text] At a meeting of the Democratic Party [PD] Steering Collegium, Chairman Petre Roman outlined the party's position regarding the current political issues. In his speech, Roman said that the only way to solve the current crisis situation is to conclude a national pact among the PD, the Democratic Convention of Romania [CDR], and the Social Democracy Party of Romania [PDSR]. The PD chairman also briefed the meeting's attendants about the PD's [separate] negotiations that are currently under way with the CDR and the PDSR and presented the party's stance regarding the [6 March] government reshuffle and the possibility of early elections. Referring to the latter issue, Roman said that in the PD's view, early elections would lead to a disaster, since the electorate, which was betrayed during the last electoral campaign, has lost confidence in everyone and everything.
