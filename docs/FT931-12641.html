930128 FT 28 JAN 93 / UK Company News: City and Comm net assets rise City and Commercial Investment Trust, reported total net assets up from Pounds 61.1m to Pounds 62.3m in the year ended January 26 1993. This is equal to Pounds 16.08 (Pounds 15.75) per capital share. Revenue before tax amounted to Pounds 3.24m, compared with Pounds 3.16m A special interim dividend, in lieu of a final, of 6.4p is being paid raising the total from 9.91p to 11.09p for the period. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 2.62m (Pounds 2.34m).
