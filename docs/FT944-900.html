941223 FT 23 DEC 94 / Orange County cash plan A US court yesterday approved a plan that will allow dozens of cities and agencies to withdraw up to Dollars 1bn from the investment pool run by Orange County, the southern California local authority which declared bankruptcy last month after losing Dollars 2bn in the US bond market. Under the terms of the new cash distribution plan, which will also allow Orange County to withdraw its own funds, participants in the pool can request to take out up to 30 per cent of their total investment.
