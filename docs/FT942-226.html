940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / World Trade News: Japanese loans to Sri Lanka Japan will provide concessionary credit for six Sri Lankan projects that will cost over Dollars 330m (Pounds 217m), reports Mervyn de Silva from Colombo. The largest loan, for Dollars 190m, will be used by the Ceylon Electricity Board to construct a hydro-power plant and a transmission line. Other loans have been allocated for environmental impact and power-system integration studies connected with the coal-fired power project in Sri Lanka's west coast and for development of the Colombo north pier.
