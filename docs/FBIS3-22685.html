Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [ITAR-TASS report: "Viral Hepatitis Attacks Sakhalin"] [Text] A viral hepatitis infection has caused 233 Sakhalin inhabitants to be hospitalized since the beginning of this year. Fortunately, no fatal cases have been recorded yet. The cause of the latest misfortune to befall the island's inhabitants was the extremely unsatisfactory quality of drinking water and a general lack of units to purify it and render it harmless.
