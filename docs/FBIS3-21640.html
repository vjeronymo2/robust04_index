Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Hector Aguilar; from the "Good Morning From Mexico" newscast] [Text] Representatives of the Attorney General's Office in north Quintana Roo have reported that in a joint operation with the Mexican Navy the authorities seized almost 1.5 tons of pure Colombian cocaine base packed in 61 packages contained in 24 bundles. This seizure, which can be described as the most important so far this year, took place approximately 80 km from Cancun, on the beaches of Acumal, where the bundles were dropped into the sea from an aircraft. Apparently, the bundles were to be illegally transshipped in a 30-meter-long fiberglass boat that had a radio with a marine band and other frequencies. The boat and the cocaine are under Navy guard in Cozumel. The Federal Judicial Police are investigating the origin of the cocaine.
