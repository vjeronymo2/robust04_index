July 18, 1989, Tuesday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

TYRONE BRAXTON SIGNS WITH BRONCOS

The Broncos have agreed to terms with veteran free agent Tyrone Braxton, who said he will be in training camp early to try to make the difficult conversion from safety to cornerback.

Braxton today signed a series of one-year contracts, with terms not disclosed, the Broncos said.

The Broncos also announced the signing of Darrell Hamilton, a 6-foot-5 298-pound offensive tackle from North Carolina. Hamilton was the Broncos third-round draft choice in 1989.
