Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 4 -- David Parsons, director general of the Pacific Economic Cooperation Council (PECC), left Hanoi this morning, concluding his two-day visit to Vietnam. During his stay here, he paid a courtesy visit to Vice Prime Minister Tran Duc Luong, had working sessions with Deputy Foreign Minister Le Mai and Deputy Head of the Party Commission for External Relations Pham Van Chuong. David Parsons gave a brief of the Pacific Economic Cooperation Council (PECC), saying that it is an observer of the Asia-Pacific Economic Cooperation (APEC) and also a bridge linking this international body with countries prior to their participation in the APEC. His visit was made at the invitation of the Foreign Ministry.
