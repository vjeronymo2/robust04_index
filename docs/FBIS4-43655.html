BFN [Unattributed article: "Martino Meets Pangalos"] [Text] (ANSA) Rome, June 20 -- Next weekend's European Council summit meeting in Corfu was at the centre of talks here Monday evening [20 June] between Italy's Foreign Minister Antonio Martino and Greece's Minister for European Affairs Theodhoros Pangalos. A communique issued by the foreign ministry described the talks as "cordial" and said that all the main themes to be discussed at the weekend's summit were dealt with. Pangalos is currently touring all 12 EU [European Union] capitals to put finishing touches to preparations for the Corfu summit. Martino thanked the Greek minister for his country's commitment to EU affairs during its six-month duty presidency, which comes to an end with the summit, and voiced particular appreciation for the way in which talks to bring Austria, Finland, Norway and Sweden into the Union were conducted.
