940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / Tallest ride opens at Blackpool The first official car on Blackpool Pleasure Beach's new ride - the world's tallest rollercoaster - taking its first 'drop' yesterday. The 235ft-high ride, which cost Pounds 12m and is called 'The Pepsi Max Big One', takes the drop at an angle of 65 degrees and a speed of 85mph
