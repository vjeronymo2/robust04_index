BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on epidemics and diseases monitored through 3 March.

Brazil
Leptospirosis -- The summer rains have caused a leptospirosis outbreak which has already killed three people in Sao Paulo State, where 78 cases had been registered as of 25 February. (Rio de Janeiro JORNAL DO BRASIL in Portuguese 26 Feb 94 p 6) Meningitis -- The Ceara Health Secretariat has reported that a type-B meningitis outbreak has killed seven people in Fortaleza and Sobral, where 45 cases were registered in 45 days. (Rio de Janeiro JORNAL DO BRASIL in Portuguese 26 Feb 94 p 6) AIDS -- Health authorities from Sao Jose do Rio Preto, 451 km from Sao Paulo, are concerned because this city of 290,000 inhabitants has the third highest rate of AIDS in the country after Santos and Sao Paulo. According to the regional health office, every day an average of over one person contracts the HIV virus. (Rio de Janeiro JORNAL DO BRASIL in Portuguese 2 Mar 94 p 12)
