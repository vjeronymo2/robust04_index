Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "Good Morning From Mexico" newscast] [Text] The Mexican Stock Market registered its largest drop in the last four years. The main indicator pertaining to stock exchange transactions dropped 165.92 points, 6.3 percent, to close the day at 2459.52. Stock market analysts have said the atmosphere of uncertainty in our country as a result of the conflict in Chiapas has caused the drop in the stock market. They have warned about the possibility of a flight of capital and investments if the conflict prolongs or extends to other regions of the country.
