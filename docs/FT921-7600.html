920221 FT 21 FEB 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Avocados at 35-55p each (35-60p) are this week's best fruit buy, reports FFVIB. Cape grapes are superb with Dan Ben Hannah and Thompson varieties at 90p-Pounds 1.20p a lb (90p-Pounds 1.20). Mushrooms are a must with many reasonably priced types in the shops. Choose from large flat varieties at 60-70p a 1/2 lb (50-70p), cups at 50-70p a 1/2 lb (60-90p) and button mushrooms at 60-80p a 1/2 lb (75p-Pounds 1.10). Top quality Chinese Leaves continue to be a great salad choice at 60-70p a lb (60-70p). Tomatoes at 50-75p a lb (45-75p), Spring onions at 45-55p a bunch (45-55p) and celery at 50-60p a head (50-60p) are all good buys.
