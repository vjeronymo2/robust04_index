BFN [Excerpt] For the second time, Greece has blocked European Union [EU] aid to Albania. National Economy Minister Ioannis Papandoniou refused to give a positive vote to the demand for economic support worth ECU 35 million, which the EU Commission again brought to ECOFIN [EU Council of Finance Ministers]. In Athens, the Foreign Ministry spokesman repeated that Albania's relations with the EU depend on respect for human rights. [passage omitted]
