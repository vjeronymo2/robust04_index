October 20, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

EQUITY

Does your publisher read your editorials? "Edison's Great Leap for Equity" (Oct. 9) is the one I'm referring to. It contains more of your hypocritical preaching on equal employment opportunities. As I peruse the masthead immediately above this editorial, I fail to see a Mary, Jose, Kim, Wong, Tanaka, Jesse Jackson III, or any other indication of minorities in The Times' hierarchy. Why don't you practice what you continually preach?

EDWARD DALTON

Westminster
