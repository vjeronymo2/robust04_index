920929 FT 29 SEP 92 / Construction Contracts: Offices development in Chester HEYWOOD WILLIAMS, a part of the Heywood Williams Group, has been awarded a contract worth almost Pounds 500,000 to design, manufacture and supply a variety of high specification windows for a new office development (pictured) in Chester. Designed by GCW Architects for NWS Bank, the six-storey building in Charterhall Drive will provide 5,500 sq metres of office accommodation. Completion is scheduled for April 1993.
