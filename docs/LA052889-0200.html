May 28, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BOGGS-ADAMS AFFAIR

Come on, L.A. Times! An article on Wade Boggs-Margo Adams on the front page as actual news? He's just another boy in the fraternity of sports players, politicians and clergymen caught cheating on his wife. She's just another opportunist using sex as a springboard to plastic surgery, a pictorial in Penthouse or Playboy, and the talk show circuit. Wise up. Leave their sleazy affairs to papers who thrive on their news -- the supermarket tabloids.

SUSAN KNIGHT

Orange
