921230 FT 30 DEC 92 / UK foreign secretary issues Britain's toughest warning yet to Serbs Mr Douglas Hurd, UK foreign secretary, has issued Britain's toughest warning yet to the Serbs of possible military action to end their 'bloody-minded cruelty'. The change of tone, in an interview in today's Daily Telegraph, represents a shift in stance. Mr Hurd said he still distrusted military action as a way to force a settlement but the Serbs had brought even those with that view 'to the point where we can imagine armed action against them to prevent a general Balkan war'.
