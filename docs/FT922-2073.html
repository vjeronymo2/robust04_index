920618 FT 18 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Stockbroking side boosts Oceana A strong contribution from the stockbroking side enabled Oceana Consolidated Company to return a pre-tax profit of Pounds 72,000 for the year ended March 31 1992. That compared with a loss of Pounds 1.29m previously, after exceptional charges of Pounds 1.63m relating to an insurance claim and the writing down of the value of an unquoted investment. Turnover of this financial services group increased by Pounds 1m to Pounds 9m. Earnings per share were 2.31p against losses of 16.74p and the dividend is raised to 1p (0.75p).
