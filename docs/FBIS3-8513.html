Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Zagreb, March 10 (AFP)--Bosnian Croats said Thursday [10 March] they would not accept a federation with Moslems in Bosnia-Herzegovina which was not linked to a confederation with Croatia proper. A statement said: ``The presidential council has decided that setting up a federation is conditional on the establishment of a confederation between the Republic of Croatia and the federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina. "That means that a federation cannot be established without the setting up of a confederation."
