Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The CPI [Communist Party of India] has criticized the United States for conducting joint armed exercises with Pakistan. In a press statement in New Delhi today, the party national executive said that the Clinton administration is only supporting militarist elements in Islamabad by this action. The party said that the U.S. by having exercises with Pakistan is lending direct support to the terrorists in the Kashmir valley. The party also criticized an anti-India letter to a Kashmiri separatist outfit by the American president.
