BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Konstantin Zhukovskiy] [Excerpts] Moscow, 16 May (ITAR-TASS) -- Talks began at noon today in the Russian Ministry of Foreign Affairs between the foreign ministers of Russia and Argentina, Andrey Kozyrev and Guido Di Tella, who is here on an official visit. [passage omitted] Welcoming the Argentinian guest, A. Kozyrev stressed that the visit is a most important element in the development of bilateral cooperation. He also noted that relations between Russia and Argentina have entered a new stage now -- the stage of close cooperation. Our relations, the Russian minister pointed out, are an example of cooperation within the framework of the United Nations and other international forums. According to A. Kozyrev, relations with Argentina are an important element of the development of the new Russia's multilateral ties with the whole of Latin America, taking account of the weight and influence which this country has in the region.
