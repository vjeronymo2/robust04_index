DATES: Illinois' certification is extended to December 19, 1994. [Ex Parte No. 388 (Sub-No. 7)] Intrastate Rail Rate Authority_Illinois AGENCY: Interstate Commerce Commission. ACTION: Extension of certification.

SUMMARY: By decision served August 22, 1989, the State of Illinois, through the Illinois Commerce Commission (Illinois), was certified to regulate intrastate rail rates, classifications, rules, and practices for a five-year period ending on September 20, 1994. Pursuant to a request from Illinois, the certification will be extended for 90 days so that Illinois can make a final assessment of whether to continue its railroad regulatory program and prepare an application for recertification.
