Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Glazovskaya] [Text] Ashgabat 26 Mar, TASS -- Turkmenistan will realise its military doctrine with assistance from the Russian Army, President Saparmurad Niyazov said here on Saturday. Speaking at a meeting of the Turkmen Defence Council, he corroborated his idea with "concurrence of the strategic interests of Turkmenistan and Russia." The president also spoke of his intention to speed up the process of integrating Turkmenistan into the international community by joining NATO's "Partnership for Peace" programme.
