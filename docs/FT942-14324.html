940421 FT 21 APR 94 / IMF to lend Moscow Dollars 1.5bn The IMF approved a Dollars 1.5bn loan for Russia yesterday in a long-awaited deal that should help pave the way for billions of dollars of additional aid from the west to its former communist foe. The move by the International Monetary Fund's board comes after months of difficult negotiations and represents a vote of confidence in President Boris Yeltsin's government.
