930115 FT 15 JAN 93 / Home loans case judgment reserved THE High Court reserved judgment yesterday on whether people who lost money in the collapse of home income plans that offered to release cash from their properties are entitled to improved compensation. Solicitors acting for about 450 elderly people challenged as 'unreasonable or unlawful' methods used by the Investors Compensation Scheme in assessing how much it should pay to victims whose advisers had become insolvent.
