941008 FT 08 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto stocks staged an afternoon rally and closed higher, but many analysts remain cautious about the outlook for next week. The TSE 300 composite index 14.48 points to close at 4,290.80 in modest volume of 49.63m shares. Mr Ron Meisels, president of P and C Holdings, said that bargain-hunters and a rash of traders were covering their short positions so that they can go into the long Thanksgiving weekend in a neutral position and were the main drivers behind the rise.
