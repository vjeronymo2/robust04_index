Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riga, Mar 17, BNS -- The former Soviet Latvia's KGB chief, Alfons Noviks, was arrested Tuesday [15 March] on genocide charges. During his active service in 1940s, Noviks conducted mass murders, persecutions and deportation of those Latvian residents who were unwelcome to the Soviet regime. If convicted, Noviks faces three to 15 years in prison or life imprisonment. Noviks was born in 1908.
