BFN [Text] Prishtina, May 26 (ATA) -- The chairwoman of the Human Rights Fund, with its seat in Belgrade, Natasha Kandic, was received in Prishtina by the members of the presidency of DLK [Kosova Democratic Alliance], Chairman Dr. Fehmi Agani, deputy chairman, Edita Tahiri, and other members, who spoke about the grave situation in Kosova, as a result of Serb state organized oppression. The presidency of DLK presented to her the attitude, already known for the solution of Kosova issue, by peaceful means and through a referendum. The chairman of the fund said that the organization is closely following the developments in Kosova, and added that she is compiling a special report for the situation in Kosova. She expressed her respect for the Albanians, who with their peaceful policy, are trying to solve Kosova issue. In the face of the continuous repression, Albanian people showed maturity and did not react to the provocations conducted by the Serb's regime, avoiding so the war.
