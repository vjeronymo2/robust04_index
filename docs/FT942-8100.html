940521 FT 21 MAY 94 / Malawi's new president discusses coalition Malawi's new president, Mr Bakili Muluzi, conferred yesterday with a northern-based party on forming a coalition government, Reuter reports from Lilongwe. Official poll results showed his United Democratic Front could be five seats short of an overall majority in an enlarged 177-seat national assembly. Mr Muluzi has ruled out co-operation with former President Banda's outgoing Malawi Congress Party.
