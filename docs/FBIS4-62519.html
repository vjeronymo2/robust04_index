BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS special correspondents Vadim Byrkin, Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan 17 May TASS -- Azeri troops resumed shelling of Karabakh positions on Tuesday [17 May] morning, using mortars and fire arms in the north of the Mardakert district of Nagorno-Karabakh and the Agdam-Askeran direction, the Karabakh Army press service told ITAR-TASS. One Karabakh soldier has been injured. The situation on other flanks of the front was quiet on Monday night and Tuesday morning, Stepanakert sources told ITAR-TASS.
