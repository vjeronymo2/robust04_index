Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Kuala Lumpur, March 4 (OANA-BERNAMA) -- Vietnamese Communist Party's Secretary-General Do Muoi will make a four-day official visit to Malaysia from March 24, acccording to the Foreign Ministry here. It said in a statement issued here Thursday that the visit is at the invitation of Prime Minister Dr. Mahathir Mohamed. While here, he will hold talks with Dr. Mahathir on bilateral relations and current regional and international issues. Do Muoi will make a visit to the southern state of Melaka where he will call on the governor, Syed Ahmad Shahabuddin.
