BFN [Text] A Communist Party of Vietnam delegation led by Central Committee member Nguyen Van Hon visited Cuba from 30 May to 3 June. The delegation had visited a number of other Latin American countries earlier. Comrade Juan Almeida, member of the Cuban Communist Party Central Committee Political Bureau in charge of ideological work and foreign relations, received and held cordial talks with the Vietnamese delegation. Comrades Almeida and Nguyen Van Hon asserted the Cuban and Vietnamese parties and governments' aspiration to strengthen and develop the relations of friendship and cooperation between the two countries.
