BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 14 (KCNA) -- The government delegation of the Republic of Zimbabwe led by Minister of Home Affairs Dumiso Dabengwa left here today after a five-day visit to Korea. During its stay the delegation visited Mangyongdae, and saw the Tower of the Chuche Idea, the Arch of Triumph, the West Sea barrage, the victorious fatherland liberation war museum, the Kang Kon Military Academy and other places.
