920612 FT 12 JUN 92 / S African minister criticises ANC Mr Derek Keys, the South African minister of finance and trade and industry, has strongly criticised African National Congress plans for mass action, saying they show the ANC is not yet ready to govern, Philip Gawith reports from Johannesburg. Mr Keys's comments, delivered at the end of his budget debate in parliament, contrast sharply with his assessment last week of ANC long-term economic policy as pragmatic and the basis for agreement.
