BFN [Text] Cairo, June 16 (XINHUA) -- Egyptian Defense Minister Muhammad Husayn Tantawi met with Chinese Air Force commander Cao Shuang Ming here today. During the meeting, the Egyptian minister praised the friendly relations and cooperation between Egypt and China. The Chinese Air Force commander said his current visit to Egypt is aimed at increasing exchanges between the armed forces of the two countries. Cao and his accompanying delegation arrived here Wednesday [15 June] on a one-week visit to Egypt. They are scheduled to view some projects of the Egyptian Air Force and air defense.
