941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / International Company News: Norsk Hydro lifts Njord stake Norsk Hydro, Norway's biggest stock-listed company, is to increase its shareholding in the 200m-barrel North Sea Njord oil field to 30 per cent by buying Norsk Agip's stake in the field. Terms of the deal were not disclosed. Agip, which is restructuring it Norwegian oil field portfolio, put its 10 per cent Njord stake up for sale in the autumn. Hydro has operational responsibility for Njord and is planning its development.
