BFN [Text] Cairo, 29 Jun (MENA) -- Tonight south Yemeni television showed the wreck of northern Yemeni warplanes and the corpse of one of the pilots. This came via a fax message that MENA received from Aden tonight. The southern television station referred in its Arab and English newscasts to a communique by a southern military source, which said that five northern warplanes were downed this morning, including two helicopters, during a raid on some vital and economic installations, including the oil refineries in Aden.
