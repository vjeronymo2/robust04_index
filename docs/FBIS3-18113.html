Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- A report in DER SPIEGEL news magazine saying that Bonn is planning to send soldiers and civilian helpers to Bosnia under a peace settlement has not been confirmed by the German Government. A Bonn government spokesman said today that DER SPIEGEL's information was "pure speculation." The plans cited by the magazine were not known to the Bonn Chancellery. DER SPIEGEL had reported that a German administrator was to rebuild the administration in the Croat-besieged Muslim stronghold of Mostar on behalf of the European Union. German police and administrators were to assist the "emergency mayor." Under the Bonn plans, Bundeswehr medical officers were to run the city's hospital and Bundeswehr engineers were to repair bridges and roads.
