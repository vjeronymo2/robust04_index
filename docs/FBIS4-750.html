BFN [Text] Vice chairman of the Liberian National Transitional Government, Mr. Isaac Musa, today paid a courtesy call on the field commander of the multiafrican peacekeeping force in Liberia, Major General John Inienger, at his free zone office in Monrovia. During the visit, which lasted for an hour, Vice Chairman Musa reiterated the NPFL [National Patriotic Front of Liberia]'s commitment to peace and the Cotonou accord. He however expressed surprise at recent harassment and intimidation being meted against citizens plying the road from Gbarnga to Monrovia by the Nigerian contingent and ULIMO [United Liberation Movement for Democracy in Liberia].
