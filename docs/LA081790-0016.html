August 17, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

ELECTION ISSUES: S&LS, ABORTION

So Wilson is being challenged by Feinstein to vote against the confirmation of Souter if Souter isn't a firm proponent of abortion rights.

If justices are to be placed on the court according to their stance on this one issue, can we say Feinstein would fully support Souter if he was a proponent of abortion rights, yet legally inept? Would she have a pro-choice court, even if the justices couldn't tie their own shoelaces?

PETER LEFEVRE

Huntington Beach
