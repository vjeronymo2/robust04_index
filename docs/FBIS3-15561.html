Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Washington March 8 TASS -- Russian State Duma chairman Ivan Rybkin held a brief talk with Georgian head of state Eduard Shevardnadze, who is on an official visit in Washington, here on Monday night. After the meeting Ivan Rybkin said in an exclusive interview with ITAR-TASS that he discussed with Shevardnadze a wide range of bilateral problems. We display keen interest in establishing peace throughout the Transcaucasus, the Russian parliamentarian emphasised. Recently the Georgian parliament voted for the conuntry's joining the Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS). Debates on this issue and voting were very difficult, and I congratulated Shevardnadze with such a positive outcome, Rybkin said. We have agreed to maintain close, virtually daily contacts both at the level of the two countries' parliaments and personal level, he said.
