BFN [Text] Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] has assumed responsibility for the 'Afula attack, claiming that its men perpetrated the attack. In a leaflet distributed in Janin, Hamas said that the name of the suicide terrorist is Ra'id Zakarnah, 19, of Qabatiyah. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that a short while ago, the security forces arrived in Qabatiyah, and took Zakarnah's father to the Ha'emeq Hospital to identify the body.
