BFN [Text] Tirana, May 16 (ATA) -- Chairman of the Democratic Party of Albania Eduard Selami met yesterday with the chairman of the Party for Democratic Prosperity, Arben Djaferi [title as received]. They evaluated the relations and cooperation existing between the two parties they represent. Mr. Djaferi acquainted Mr. Selami with the efforts of this party for the extension and guaranteeing of the national and human rights of the Albanians in Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM]. Mr. Selami stressed that the constructive and resolute stand of the Party for Democratic Prosperity towards the stability of Macedonia and towards the extension and realisation of the rights of the Albanians in Macedonia, as a condition for the stability, have a particular importance. He considered as significant the cooperation and coordination of the actions of all Albanian political forces in Macedonia for a joint program with regard to the realisation of the Albanians' demands and the independence and stability of the Macedonian state. Concerning the census, Mr. Selami said it is an important act which should be conducted according to the criteria defined by the experts' commission of the Council of Europe.
