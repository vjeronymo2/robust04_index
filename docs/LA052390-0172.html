May 23, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

STALLONE'S ROCKY TO HANG 'EM UP

Sylvester Stallone says this summer's "Rocky V" will be the last in the series because there is nothing more to say about the not-so-talkative palooka.

"I have no more to say about the character, which is my alter ego, I can't deny that," Stallone said Tuesday.

So what's ahead for Stallone?

"Right now," he said, "we're doing something ecological, which is the theme of the 1990s. Then I'd like to do a comedy."

The movie's premiere is tentatively set for Nov. 15.
