941214 FT 14 DEC 94 / UK Company News: BTR in Pounds 35.5m sell-off Manufacturing group BTR Nylex, the Australian arm of UK-based BTR, said early today its Rockware subsidiary had sold its 100 per cent interest in two metal pressing companies for Pounds 35.5m cash, Reuter reports from Melbourne. It sold the companies - Presspart Manufacturing and its US subsidiary, and Decorpart - to the existing management team led by the companies' chief executive, Mr Ray Maddison. The buy-out team was backed by 3i, BTR Nylex said.
