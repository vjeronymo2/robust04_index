March 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

A Texas district judge awarded $1.6 million and a condominium to the common-law wife of New York Yankee outfielder Dave Winfield, who also was ordered to pay $3,500 monthly for child support.

In June, a jury found that Winfield's relationship with flight attendant Sandra Renfro from 1982 to 1985 constituted a common-law marriage. Winfield said he never intended to marry Renfro and will appeal.

Winfield married Tonya Turner in a legal ceremony two years ago. Renfro sought about $2.5 million from Winfield in child support for their 7-year-old daughter, Lauren Shanel.
