Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Forecast Deficit at 142 Trillion"] [Text] Rome, 30 Dec (ANSA) -- The 1994 Budget and Finance Bill, approved by parliament last week and published Thursday [30 December] in the Official Gazette, nails the deficit for next year at 141.97 trillion lire (83.51 billion dollars). The state will not be allowed to obtain more than 294.7 trillion lire (173.3 billion dollars) through the financial markets, according to the bill.
