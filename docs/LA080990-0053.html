August 9, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

William R. Brody has been named executive vice-president, chief operating officer at Raygal Design Associates, a restaurant and hotel design firm in Irvine. He is responsible for day-to-day operations, including strategic planning, marketing, sales, contract administration and production. Brody was previously executive vice-president of Beckham/Eisenman in Irvine.
