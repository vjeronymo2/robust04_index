BFN [Text] Cairo, 28 Jun (MENA) -- President Muhammad Husni Mubarak received a telephone call this afternoon from Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih. They reviewed the latest developments of the Yemeni crisis. During the telephone conversation, President Mubarak affirmed the importance of stopping the bloodshed to provide an opportunity for resolving the crisis and preserving the supreme interests of the Yemeni people.
