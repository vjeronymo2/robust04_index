Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 4 (XINHUA) -- Thai Princess Maha Chakkri Sirinthon arrived here this afternoon for a two-week visit to China at the invitation of the Chinese Government. It is the Thai princess' 5th China visit. Song Jian, state councillor and minister in charge of the State Science and Technology Commission, gave a feast in honor of the princess this evening in the Diaoyutai state guesthouse.
