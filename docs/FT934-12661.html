931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / Exchange risk scheme scrapped THE government is to abandon the Exchange Risk Guarantee Scheme, aimed at protecting companies from fluctuating money rates, Mr Tim Sainsbury, trade and industry minister said yesterday. Loans worth Pounds 123.2m had been made under the scheme to 2,000 companies since 1978. The Department of Trade and Industry said one reason for scrapping it was the recent stabilisation of exchange rates.
