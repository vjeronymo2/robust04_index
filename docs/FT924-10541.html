921030 FT 30 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Aberfoyle creditors reject plan PROBLEMS AT Aberfoyle Holdings, the agriculture and security products group placed in administration last August, deepened after creditors rejected restructuring plans put forward by Touche Ross, the administrator. They voted by some 60 to 40 per cent in favour of a plan said to include an injection into the company thought to be in the region of Pounds 500,000 but the vote fell short of the necessary 75 per cent. Touche Ross said it will go back to the court for direction. Shares in the company, which has most of its operations in Zimbabwe, were suspended in early August at 2 1/2 p.
