BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin will summon additional deliberations on how to curtail PLO efforts to create political facts in Jerusalem. Today's meeting on the issue lasted two hours and was attended by the prime minister, the foreign minister, the police minister, the police chief, the attorney general, the Foreign Ministry's legal adviser, and the Shin Bet head. Our political correspondent Shlomo Raz reports that there is no plan to initiate a comprehensive legislation on the issue, but to deal with each case independently. Among steps considered are moves to prevent problematic visits to Jerusalem.
