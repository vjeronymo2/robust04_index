BFN [Text] Vilnius, 13 May, BNS -- Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas on Friday [13 May] in a no surprise move signed several decrees on rearrangements in the country's diplomatic corps. The president decided to dismiss Osvaldas Balakauskas, ambassador to France, and Dainius Junevicius, ambassador to Poland, who were both appointed by the previous administration of Vytautas Landsbergis. Aurimas Taurantas, Lithuania's charge d' affaires to the Council of Europe, and Vytautas Einoris, charge d' affaires to Kazakhstan, were both promoted to the rank of extraordinary and plenipotentiary ambassador.
