BFN [Text] In a meeting of the extended secretariat of the Cuban Workers Federation [CTC], chaired by CTC Secretary General Pedro Ross Leal, it was announced that the Assemblies for Economic Efficiency will be held in all work collectives beginning on 15 May. A recent survey conducted to obtain reaction on the first assemblies of this type indicated that the assemblies constitute an important form of participation so that the collectives can measure their progress in production and services. During the meeting, Ross insisted on the CTC's role in supporting the changes and transformations that are necessary and said that the Assemblies for Economic Efficiency preserve the spirit of the parliaments in the workplace.
