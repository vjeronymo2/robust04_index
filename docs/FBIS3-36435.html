Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] President of Uzbekistan Islam Karimov yesterday received Vietnamese Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam who is in our country on an official visit. Greeting the guest from Vietnam, the president noted that mutual relations with Vietnam have always been of the best kind, and this visit will make it possible to deepen them and take them to a new level corresponding with the interests of our states. The conversation touched upon questions of Uzbekistan's foreign and domestic policy. The guest was told in detail about the progress of economic reforms, privatization and denationalization of industrial, trading, and service sector enterprises, and also the principles of social direction of the whole program for switching to the market. Vietnamese Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam thanked the president of the republic for the friendly reception and conveyed best wishes to the people of Uzbekistan from the government of his country. The minister expressed profound interest in expanding contacts between the two states. [passage omitted]
