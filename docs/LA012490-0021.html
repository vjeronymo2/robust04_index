January 24, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

Shearson Lehman Hutton Holdings Inc.: The New York brokerage said 1989 profit totaled $110 million, up 15% from 1988.

Revenue rose 19% to $12.5 billion. Fourth-quarter profit was $3.8 million, contrasted with a $13-million loss in the year-ago period. The company, which is 62% owned by American Express, credited higher commissions, net interest income and revenue.
