930721 FT 21 JUL 93 / Parliament and Politics: Major stresses domestic agenda MR John Major told Tory peers yesterday that the government would concentrate on law and order and deregulation in the coming months. His emphasis on developing areas of the domestic agenda intended to appeal specifically to traditional Tory supporters is set to be repeated when he addresses Tory MPs tomorrow. At the annual end-of-term private meeting with the Association of Conservative Peers, Mr Major left no doubt that next session's legislative programme would be heavier than had been expected.
