930920 FT 20 SEP 93 / Close poll expected in Poland MR Alexander Kwasniewski, above, Democratic Left Alliance leader, with his wife Jolanta, faces the media after casting his vote in Warsaw in Poland's parliamentary elections. His party, with its roots in the communist past, was expected to take 15 per cent of the vote and to emerge as one of the strongest in what appeared to be a close-run race. The mainstream Democratic Union party of Ms Hanna Suchocka, the outgoing prime minister, might be relegated to third place. Rural voters lured, Page 2
