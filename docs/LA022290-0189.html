February 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Four University of Florida football players suspended after having been accused of betting on college and pro games will not be prosecuted and will be eligible to play next season, according to a newspaper report.

Starting quarterback Kyle Morris, backup quarterback Shane Matthews and walk-ons George Mangus and Brady Ackerman have been ruled eligible by the NCAA's Eligibility Committee, the Tampa Tribune reported.
