930819 FT 19 AUG 93 / Sudan on 'terrorist' list The US yesterday formally added Sudan to a list of nations it accuses of sponsoring terrorism, Reuter reports from Washington. The list already includes Iran, Iraq, Syria, Libya, Cuba and North Korea. It makes Khartoum ineligible for non-humanitarian US aid or for the commerical sales of US arms or technology that could equip terrorists, and it requires Washington to veto World Bank loans to Sudan.
