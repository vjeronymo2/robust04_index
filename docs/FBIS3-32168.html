Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Caracas, 2 Feb (EFE) -- Today, Venezuelan President Rafael Caldera announced the soldiers who participated in the two Venezuelan 1992 coup attempts will be freed. Caldera made this announcement after assuming his post as eighth constitutional president of Venezuela in a special ceremony held at the Venezuelan Congress. The veteran politician, who is 78 years old, said the coupist soldiers who are still under arrest, 124 of them, will be released in a legal way that will not create tensions in the Armed Forces.
