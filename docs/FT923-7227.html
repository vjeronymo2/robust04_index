920819 FT 19 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Fleming Mercantile asset value declines In the six months to July 31, net asset value of Fleming Mercantile Investment Trust fell by 8.8 per cent to 246p, compared to a decline of 11.7 per cent in the FT-A World Index. The managers said it was a difficult period for emerging growth companies in the UK and overseas. Gross revenue for the half year fell from Pounds 9.13m to Pounds 7.33m and earnings per share declined from 3.85p to 2.58p. The second quarterly dividend is 1.675p to make 3.35p (3.2p) to date; the year's total is forecast to stay at 6.7p.
