930807 FT 07 AUG 93 / Bonn sets air talks deadline Germany, which has threatened to cancel its civil aviation agreement with the US, has set a September 15 deadline for talks on a new bilateral accord, Reuter reports from Bonn. The German Transport Ministry has also proposed a seven-year interim pact, it said. This would restrict US airline activity in Germany ahead of a final negotiated treaty to be implemented in the year 2000.
