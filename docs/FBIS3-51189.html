Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities in Brazil monitored between 7 and 13 January. Fuel prices increased 17 percent as of 0000 on 11 January. This is the first increase of the year authorized by the Finance Ministry. Now fuel prices will not have any real readjustments, but will be readjusted according to the monthly inflation rate. National airline tickets were increased 31 percent. (Brasilia Radio Nacional da Amazonia Network in Portuguese 0900 GMT 11 Jan 94) The Trade Technical Department reported that Brazilian wood exports were $800 million in 1993 compared with $562 million in 1992. (Sao Paulo GAZETA MERCANTIL in Portuguese 11 Jan 94 p 13)
