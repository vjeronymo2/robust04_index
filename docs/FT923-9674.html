920804 FT 04 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Thornton Asian net assets improve The net asset value per ordinary share of Thornton Asian Emerging Markets Investment Trust stood at 74.1p at the half year ended June 30. This compares with 68.9p at the December 31 year end and with 81.6p a year previously. Gross income rose from Pounds 1.19m to Pounds 1.67m. Earnings per share improved from 0.36p to 0.66p.
