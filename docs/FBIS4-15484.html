BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Solntsev] [Text] Moscow April 8 ITAR-TASS -- The resignation of the Japanese Prime Minister Morihiro Hosokawa is Japan's internal affair. The Moscow policy of developing comprehensive relations with Japan remains unchanged, a ranking official of the Russian Ministry of Foreign Affairs told ITAR-TASS today. He stressed that these relations will continue to develop on the basis of the Tokyo declaration signed after the Russo-Japanese summit last October. The Tokyo declaration outlined the strategic perspective for the development of all-round ties between the two countries.
