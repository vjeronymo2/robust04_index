920122 FT 22 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Celltech cuts losses Celltech Group, the independent biotechnology company, halved pre-tax losses and intends to seek a listing for its shares by 1994. A loss of Pounds 6.58m (Pounds 12.5m) was incurred for the year to September 30, on turnover of Pounds 17m (Pounds 19.9m). The company has completed a restructuring programme and its research is now focused on projects aimed at large markets such as cancer therapy and transplant technology.
