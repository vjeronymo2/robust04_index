March 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

SERVICE FOR HARMON SET FOR MONDAY

A Mass for Tom Harmon, who died of a heart attack Thursday, will be celebrated at St. Paul the Apostle Catholic Church, 10750 Ohio Ave., Monday at 4 p.m.

Harmon, who won the 1940 Heisman Trophy while at Michigan, was 70.

The family is requesting that, in lieu of flowers, donations be made to the Tom Harmon Memorial Scholarship Fund, 2236 Encinitas Blvd., Suite A, Encinitas, Calif., 92024.
