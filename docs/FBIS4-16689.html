BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Helsinki, 18 Apr -- Ivan Rybkin, chairman of the State Duma who is on an official visit to Finland at the head of a Russian parliamentary delegation, has today met with Finnish President Martti Ahtisaari. Their one-to-one conversation took place at the president's residence. According to well-informed sources within the Russian delegation, questions concerning bilateral relations between Russia and Finland, as well as measures for improving stability and security in Europe were discussed at the meeting.
