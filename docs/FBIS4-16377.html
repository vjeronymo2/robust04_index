BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Andrey Kirillov] [Text] Moscow April 14 TASS -- The relations with South Korea, a country which made rapid progress due to radical economic reforms, are important for Russia, Vladimir Shumeyko, head of the Federation Council, the upper chamber of the Russian parliament, said at the meeting with visiting South Korean Foreign Minister Han Sung-chu. Shumeyko briefed the minister on the activities of the Federation Council. Han Sung-chu said that Russia and South Korea have no disagreements on international issues, the positions of both states are very close. Russia is an Asian state as well as European and Russia's role in Asia is very important, he noted.
