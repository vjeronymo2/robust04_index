BFN [Text] Shaykh 'Abd-al-Majid al-Zindani, Presidential Council member, today received brother Mohammad Jalal Firuz-nia, ambassador of the Islamic Republic of Iran in Sanaa. During the meeting, discussions dealt with the relations between the two sisterly countries. The Iranian ambassador stressed that Iran supports Yemeni unity and national sovereignty, and believes the crisis should be resolved within the Yemeni framework.
