Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "24 Hours Newscast"] [Text] With a clear conscience of a job well done, Julio Cesar Turbay Ayala, former president of the Republic, retired today from the Liberal Party [PL] leadership. According to party members, Turbay Ayala fulfilled his two goals: primary elections and a single candidate. With the Liberal tradition of having been the president and the party leader, former President Julio Cesar Turbay Ayala is retiring with the recognition of his fellow countrymen because he united them and promoted clean primary elections for choosing the candidate for president of the Republic. [Begin Turbay Ayala recording] With the change in leadership of the party we complete the cycle of a generational renewal within the PL. The change is beneficial. It has its own dynamics, and any efforts to halt the process will be to no avail. [end recording] Next week PL presidential candidate Ernesto Samper Pizano will form the new groups for his electoral campaign.
