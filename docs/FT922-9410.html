920507 FT 07 MAY 92 / UK Company News: UniChem expands into Portugal UniChem, the pharmaceuticals wholesaler which in March withdrew from the battle for Macarthy, the retailer, has acquired two wholesalers in Portugal and has negotiated an option to buy a third. The consideration for the two acquired wholesalers, both based in Oporto, is Es1.2bn (Pounds 4.95m) cash. In the 1991 year, they had combined sales of some Es9.71bn, combined pre-tax profits of Es119m, and combined net assets of about Es371m. The exercise for the option on the third wholesaler, in Aviero, will involve a maximum Es1.05bn.
