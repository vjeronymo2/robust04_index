Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, 18 Mar (AZERTAC)--An Iranian Airlines Company transport plane flying from Moscow to Tehran was involved in an accident over Khankendi [Stepanakert] Rayon at about midnight (2330 Baku time) [1930 GMT] on 17 March. According to the preliminary information given to an AZERTAC correspondent by the "Azerbaijani Airlines" state concern, the plane left Georgian airspace and entered Armenian airspace. It deviated from its established path and reached the Dakhlyk [Mountainous] Karabakh zone of Azerbaijan. It has been ascertained that the transport plane crashed 3 km from Khankendi.
