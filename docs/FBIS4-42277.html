BFN [Text] Thirteen members of an illegal leftist organization and four members of a radical religious organization were caught in Ankara. In a news conference, Ankara Security Director Orhan Tasanlar said that the suspects belonging to Dev-Sol [Revolutionary Left] posted placards, distributed pamphlets, and threw Molotov cocktails on various dates. Tasanlar reported that the suspects belonging to the illegal Islamic Great Vanguards Front [Islami Buyuk Akincilar Cephesi] threw bombs at various places in Ankara. One pistol, large quantities of material used in the manufacturing of time bombs, and organizational documents were also seized.
