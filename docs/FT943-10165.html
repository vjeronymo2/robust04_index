940806 FT 06 AUG 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto stock prices fell in moderate trading as unexpectedly strong US employment figures provoked fears of rising interest rates. All sectors closed lower, led by conglomerates, mining and consumer products. Canadian Pacific, which had risen for much of the week on the strength of solid second-quarter earnings, fell CDollars 1/2 to CDollars 22. The 300 composite index fell 33.55 points to close at 4168.54 in volume of 54m shares.
