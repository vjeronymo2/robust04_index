Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By unidentified ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 5 Feb -- Reports by some mass media that President Mintimer Shaymiyev of Tatarstan has arrived in Moscow and is to have a meeting today with President Boris Yeltsin of the Russian Federation are factually incorrect, an ITAR-TASS correspondent learned at Tatarstan's permanent mission in Moscow. Such a meeting was indeed planned, the diplomat on duty at the mission pointed out, but for a number of reasons it has been postponed until a new date can be set.
