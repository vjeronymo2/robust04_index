920323 FT 23 MAR 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 28m orders awarded to Mansell MANSELL has been awarded contracts totalling Pounds 28m covering new build, design and build, refurbishment and fitting out. In the leisure sector Mansell is working for the Hyatt Carlton Tower Hotel on a fast track 12-week refurbishment contract at its Knightsbridge hotel, value Pounds 1m; for Ladbroke Hotels the company is undertaking refurbishment work at its Hilton International, Regent's Park; and two further contracts, worth a total of Pounds 3m, are for Forte Hotels for construction work at Crest Hotel, Heathrow and the Cumberland Hotel at Marble Arch. Other awards include work for various London boroughs, including the formation of a day care centre at Lewisham; two contracts worth Pounds 1.3m at Whitwell Road Estate, N13 and College Point, E15; the provision of a block of flats in Wood Green for disabled tenants at a cost of Pounds 300,000; a combination of new build flats and refurbishment to two 12-storey tower blocks in Croydon worth Pounds 2.25m; and the construction of a social club in Millwall for the London Docklands Development Corporation. For Runnymede Borough Council, a design and build contract is for a day care centre in New Haw, Surrey, value Pounds 730,000. Eleven two-storey houses costing Pounds 630,000 are to be built in Hackney, E9 for Sanctuary Housing and the British Railway Board has placed contracts for work at Moorgate, Old Street, Finsbury Park/Highbury and Islington stations. Major extension works will be undertaken at the Central School of Speech and Drama's Embassy Theatre and a programme of refurbishment at the Indian study room at the Victoria and Albert Museum.
