BFN [Text] Concerning yesterday's passage by two chetnik tanks on the Pale-Mt. Trebevic-Trnovo direction with the permission of and escorted by the UN Protection Force, Brigadier General Vahid Karavelic, commander of the 1st Corps, has issued the following statement. It reads as follows: At the news conference General Rose said that this had been agreed between Akashi and war criminal Karadzic, and did not represent a violation of the cease-fire. The chetniks said that our offensive activities were in preparation on the Mt. Igman and Mt. Bjelasnica fronts and were therefore asking for permission to transfer the tanks. [word indistinct] says that the chetniks could engage on these fronts forces of the strength of one division in order to divert the attention of the international public from the concentration of manpower and equipment around Brcko and Olovo. Today at 1530 the chetniks issued orders to all the units in Trnovo to be at full combat readiness. This is said in the statement by Brigadier General Vahid Karavelic.
