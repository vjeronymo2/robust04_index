BFN [Text] The Nigerian chief of general staff, Lieutenant General Oladipo Diya, yesterday issued a severe warning regarding the Bakassi Peninsula saying: No one should try to put our determination to the test. Our claim of sovereignty over the Bakassi Peninsula is based on law and justice, he added. Yesterday, Cameroonian External Relations Minister Ferdinand Oyono described the situation on the Bakassi Peninsula as potentially explosive. Troops from both countries have been deployed on the peninsula.
