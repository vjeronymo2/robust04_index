941129 FT 29 NOV 94 / Four unions vote for political funds Members of four unions have voted by big majorities in postal ballots to retain political funds. The GMB general union, the second biggest union affiliated to Labour, voted by a majority of 85 per cent to maintain its fund. TSSA, the white-collar rail union; Aslef, the train drivers' union; and the Communication Managers Association also voted to keep political funds by majorities of 74 per cent to 93 per cent.
