BFN [Text] The Diqing military subdistrict has deployed 10,000 Army officers, soldiers, and militiamen in an emergency disaster relief operation, rescuing people and livestock afflicted by the greatest snowstorm of the past 40 years, thus effectively alleviating the aftermath of the disaster. An extremely strong snowstorm in the Diqing Zang Nationality Autonomous Prefecture hit the prefecture in late March. To fight the snowstorm, the Diqing military subdistrict promptly organized soldiers and militiamen and set up first-aid detachments to plunge into an emergency operation. (He Zhiguang), commander of the military subdistrict, acted as the general commander of the operation. Under his command, fighters and militiamen protected with their own bodies the great dam of the Chongjiang He hydropower plant in Zhongdian against the snow, rescued more than 100 teachers and pupils of the Sanba Primary School in Weixi County who were trapped by flooding, and made arrangements for pupils to continue their studies. So far the soldiers and militiamen in the Diqing military subdistrict have rescued more than 1,700 people, took good care of more than 1,300 people, and saved economic losses worth more than 12 million yuan.
