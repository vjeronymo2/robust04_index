BFN [By reporter Wang Sen (3769 2773)] [Text] Belgrade, 10 Jun (XINHUA) -- Simic, vice premier of Yugoslavia, emphatically pointed out on 10 June that China's role was and remains of great importance for Yugoslavia and the future of the world. Vice Premier Simic made this comment in a meeting with a delegation of the Chinese Association for International Understanding [CAIU] in Belgrade. The Chinese delegation headed by Wu Xingtang, secretary general of the CAIU, arrived here on 6 June for a goodwill visit of Yugoslavia at the invitation of the "Karidzic Brothers" University. During the meeting, Simic expressed gratitude to the Chinese Government and people for their consistent support to the Yugoslav people and their efforts to preserve peace. The Yugoslav Government will continue to do its utmost for the Sino-Yugoslav relations to develop even more multilaterally, he added.
