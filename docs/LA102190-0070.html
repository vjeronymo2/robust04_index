October 21, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

FLARE'S MISSION

Not all children have a morbid interest in the obscene -- in fact, I have found the ones who have just such an obsession are children from homes where censorship ranks No. 1 on the list of "values" to be learned.

Values, respect for oneself and others, religious and political preferences, begin at home. (And not necessarily just a good Christian home.)

When Loeffel educates my children by paying for their school, books, food, clothing and shelter and taxes, I'll let her move in and live with us!

SITA HOYT

Studio City
