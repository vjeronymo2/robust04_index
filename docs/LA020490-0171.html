February 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

THIS WEEK

JOB OPENINGS: Hundreds of applicants are expected to show up this week to seek 150 jobs at the new Sheraton Cerritos, which is scheduled to open April 1.

Interviews are by appointment only, beginning Monday at the Cerritos Public Library, 18025 S. Bloomfield Ave. Information: 404-4150.

SENATE ELECTION: A special election will be held Tuesday to fill the 31st State Senate District seat vacated by Sen. William Campbell. The district includes Whittier, La Mirada and La Habra Heights.
