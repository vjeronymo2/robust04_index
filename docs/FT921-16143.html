920106 FT 06 JAN 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 11.6m workload for the Ward Group The WARD GROUP has been awarded contracts worth around Pounds 11.6m. Heading the list is a Pounds 5.8m order from Olympia & York for 7,900 tonnes of steel and floor decking at B3, B4 and B5 at Canary Wharf. At the Metro Centre, Gateshead, the company is providing a complete building package, worth Pounds 1.3m, including steel work, roof and wall cladding, curtain walling, floor decking, roof ventilators, signage and doors for an IKEA retail furnishing store. In Coventry a Pounds 1.7m contract comprises structural steelwork for a fully automated warehouse and office for Toys 'R' Us, while provision of steelwork, roof and wall cladding worth Pounds 1.8m is being undertaken for an office, warehouse and distribution centre owned by Addidas in Stockport. Other projects include structural steelwork worth Pounds 800,000 for the Seasalter Viaduct, Kent, and structural steelwork worth Pounds 200,000 for the Prebend Street Bridge, Bedfordshire.
