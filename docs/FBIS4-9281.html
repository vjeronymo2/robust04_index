BFN [Text] Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu met with FRY Deputy Prime Minister Nicola Sainovicat at Victoria Palace today. According to information released by the Romanian executive, the guest noted that the holding of the Crans Montana Forum in Bucharest is not only an important event for Romania but for all the countries in the Balkans. The participants reiterated the positions of the two countries regarding the crisis in the former Yugoslavia and noted that the only way to solve the conflict in Bosnia-Herzegovina is through dialogue, which should be attended by all interested sides. The two sides assessed that bilateral relations could once again become as intensive and ample, if not stronger, as they were before the embargo and that this is why they have to be prepared in advance.
