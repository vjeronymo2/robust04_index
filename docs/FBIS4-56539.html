CSO [Editorial Report] Managua LA PRENSA in Spanish of 19 April 1994 on page 4 reports that the Integral Rural Development Program sponsored by the Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock (MAG) will convert farms and parcels of lands in Leon and Chinandega to more profitable units. The program, which will receive German Government financing, will introduce new crops, provide technical training and new technology, and will benefit 1,950 families in 10 municipalities. MAG Minister Rondon noted that the program aims to reduce malnutrition, poverty, and unemployment in the region. Nicaragua will reportedly contribute $2 million for the first phase of the program, planned for 1995, while the German Technical Cooperation agency will provide $3 million.
