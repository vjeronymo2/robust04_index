October 21, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

MASSIVE EARTHQUAKE JOLTS BAY AREA

The vice president circled the stricken city in a helicopter. Later, for television cameras, he described what he saw as "heart-rending." He didn't feel any useful purpose would be served by calling on Mayor Art Agnos!

Sending Dan Quayle to a disaster is a redundancy.

ELLIOTT REID

Los Angeles
