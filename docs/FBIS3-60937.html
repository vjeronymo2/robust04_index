Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Damascus, 6 Jan (MENA)--The 10 Palestinian factions based in Damascus, which oppose the PLO, have declared the formation of the Alliance of the Palestinian Forces [tahaluf al-quwa al-filastinyah] with the aim of confronting the Gaza-Jericho agreement. The Palestinian oppositionists also declared that they are preparing a popular conference to marshal capabilities to undermine this agreement. The leaders of the 10 factions held a news conference in the Syrian capital today.
