940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Prudential Corporation PRUDENTIAL CORPORATION: First quarter results for its US arm, Jackson National Life Insurance Company and its subsidiary Jackson National Life Insurance Company of Michigan were: Dollars 43.9m (Pounds 29.2m) against Dollars 92.9m pre-tax and Dollars 6.9m (Dollars 11.1m) - excluding capital gains - pre-tax respectively.
