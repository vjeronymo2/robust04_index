November 7, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: VAN NUYS MAN HELD IN FATAL SHOOTING OF WIFE

A Van Nuys man was arrested on suspicion of shooting his wife to death during a quarrel in their apartment Tuesday afternoon, Los Angeles police said.

Joseph Holden, 48, was arrested after police found the body of 47-year-old Luz Holden in their apartment in the 8000 block of Langdon Avenue. She had been shot once in the chest with a pistol and was pronounced dead at the scene, Detective Jim Vojtecky said.

Holden was being held without bail at the Van Nuys station.
