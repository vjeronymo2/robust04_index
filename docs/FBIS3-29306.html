Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 17 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il extended thanks to the officials and employees of the Central Zoo. They have contributed to the development of science and education by building up the Central Zoo as a cultural pleasure ground and an educational centre for the people, raising the scientific and technical level of the rearing of animals and sending many animal stuffings and immersion samples to several dozen schools in different parts of the country. Comrade Kim Chong-il also sent thanks to the Hoeyong combined foodstuff company for its innovations in carrying out the revolutionary economic strategy advanced by the Workers' Party of Korea.
