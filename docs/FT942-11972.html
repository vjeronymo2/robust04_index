940503 FT 03 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Cap and Regional Capital and Regional Properties has acquired a 24.9 per cent stake in Lanham, a specialist retail warehouse and food superstore devel-oper. As part of the deal Capital has subscribed to a convertible loan stock and has provided a working capital facility, making a total investment of Pounds 400,000. Capital has also agreed to provide Lanham with a mezzanine loan facility of up to Pounds 3m in order that Lanham may retain a number of developments as investments.
