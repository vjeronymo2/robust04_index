BFN [Text] The Azerbaijani Army again intensified its aggressive operations along the northeastern border of Armenia on the morning of 3 May. The Armenian Defense Ministry press service reported that the enemy subjected the border settlements and defensive positions in Noyemberyanskiy, Idzhevanskiy, Tavushskiy, and Berd rayons to heavy artillery shelling as of 1800. All types of weapons were used. In an attempt to fulfill a recently planned military operation by the Azerbaijani Army command, a large armed group in Kazakhskiy Rayon launched an attack at 2100 with the support of tanks against Berdavan in Noyemberyanskiy Rayon. The enemy achieved some success against our border guards and approached the village. However, after 40 minutes, the Armenian Army fighters regrouped their forces and threw the enemy back and recovered their initial positions. The positional battles continued until 2400. Both sides suffered losses. The Azerbaijani formations fiercely shelled Vazashen and Diran in Idzhevanskiy Rayon, Aygepar and Paravakar in Tavushskiy Rayon, and Vahan in Krasnoselskiy Rayon last night. Casualty figures are being determined. Our border guards took countermeasures and silenced the enemy at dawn. According to the press service, a relative calm prevailed on Armenia's northeastern border during the day today.
