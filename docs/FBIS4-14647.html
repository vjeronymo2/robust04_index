BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 470] [Text] Vilnius, April 1, ELTA -- Lithuanian Foreign Ministry received a note from Russian embassy that visa regulations will be introduced for Lithuanian citizens on entering the territory of Russia from April 28. It was informed in the note that the citizens of the former USSR who permanently live in Lithuania, but do not have the citizenship of Lithuania, Russia or any other country will be able to enter Russia without a visa until February 6, 1995.
