Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] New Zealand's prime minister, Jim Bolger, has welcomed news that the United States has decided to upgrade its contacts with New Zealand after seven years of restriction because of New Zealand's antinuclear policy. The announcement was made in a message from the American secretary of state, Warren Christopher, to New Zealand's foreign minister, Don McKinnon. Mr. Bolger said the upgrading applied to both governmental and military contacts. However, REUTERS news agency says the message emphasized that while the American Government had decided to resume senior-level contacts, this did not signify a restoration of United States' defense obligations to New Zealand.
