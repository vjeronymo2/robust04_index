BFN [Text] The Moscow Duma member Nikolay Moskovchenko was heavily wounded at the entrance to his house Wednesday night. As Interfax has learned from Moscow's law enforcement bodies, he was struck by three bullets. No other details are known yet, except that Moskovchenko is in an intensive care unit in critical condition but is conscious. The Prosecutor General's Office has initiated legal proceedings, and an investigation is under way. The State Duma member Andrey Aizderdzis was shot dead in the town of Khimki near Moscow at the end of April. Police believe his killer was hired.
