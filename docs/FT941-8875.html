940217 FT 17 FEB 94 / Fleming Fledgeling raises net asset value by 36% Fleming Fledgeling Investment Trust reported a 36 per cent rise in net asset value, from 288.4p to 392.6p, for the year to December 31. The trust said the main movement in its portfolio had comprised a reduction in US weighting together with a build-up of bank overdraft, enabling increases to be made in Japan, Europe and east Asia. Available revenue declined to Pounds 244,000 (Pounds 471,000) and eanings per share were 1.94p (3.74p). The total dividend for the year is unchanged at 3p via a final of 2p.
