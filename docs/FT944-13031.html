941029 FT 29 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Benchmark Benchmark Group, the property and portfolio investment company, continued its recovery during the 12 months to June 30 with pre-tax profits of Pounds 2.69m. The outcome, struck after interest charges reduced from Pounds 1.28m to Pounds 760,000, compared with a modest profit of Pounds 77,000 last time and a deficit of Pounds 12.2m for 1991-92. The property division returned operating profits of Pounds 818,000 (losses of Pounds 15,000) on turnover of Pounds 998,000 (Pounds 3.16m). Portfolio investment saw turnover fall to Pounds 9.42m (Pounds 13.1m) for profits of Pounds 2.69m (Pounds 2.86m). Earnings per share were 17.14p (0.48p adjusted).
