BFN [Text] The Federation Council approved Friday changes and amendments of the Russian Criminal and Criminal Proceedings Codes. Nearly 40 articles of the former and nine of the latter were amended. At the same time 19 Criminal Code articles were annulled and 15 new ones included. The Council's Constitutional Legislation and Legality Committee issued a statement saying that among important new articles were 17 on crimes committed by a conspiring group of people or by an organized group and 213 (3) on terrorism that were aimed at combatting organized crime. Article 13 on necessary defense was revised. The notions of socialist and personal properties were removed. The committee said that some changes and amendments of the Criminal Code were fundamental in that they had a bearing on the main institutions of criminal law and would enhance the suppression of crime. Also Friday the Federation Council passed without debate a bill adopted by the State Duma on foreign borrowings and credits granted by the Russian Federations.
