Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 8 Mar (INA)--Veselin Koshev, leader of the Bulgarian Liberal Democratic Party [BLDP], and his accompanying delegation concluded an official visit of several days to Iraq today. During the visit, the Bulgarian party delegation held talks with several Arab Socialist Ba'th Party and state officials dealing with the promotion of the cooperation and friendship ties between the Ba'th Party and the BLDP, and between both friendly countries in the service of their peoples' interest.
