BFN [Excerpts] Buenos Aires, 4 May (NA) -- The family of Army Commander Lieutenant General Martin Balza has received death threats in an anonymous message that demands that the death of conscript Omar Carrasco not be investigated, as "the Neuquen case is closed." Pablo Balza, 23, son of the general, today found on the windshield of his car a typewritten note in which his father is threatened with death and which contained details about the family life of the general's children. [passage omitted] Military sources state that this is not the first time that Gen. Balza and his family have received death threats.
