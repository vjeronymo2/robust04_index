BFN [Text] Antonio Mota Graca, aka Curica, who was responsible for the transportation of the 7 metric tons of cocaine seized in Tocantins State, is the most important man from the Colombian cartels in Brazil. Although 13 drug traffickers have been arrested, Curica again escaped Federal Police. In 1991, he was freed from inside a Military Police patrol car when he was leaving jail to testify before the court in Itapecirica da Serra, Sao Paulo State.
