920310 FT 10 MAR 92 / UK Company News: USDC net asset value improves Net asset value per ordinary share of USDC Investment Trust, managed by GT Management, rose from 169.7p to 192.1p over the 12 months ended December 31. Available revenue rose to Pounds 1.95m (Pounds 1.82m), equal to earnings of 5.33p (4.98p). A proposed final dividend of 3.25p makes a same-again 4.25p total.
