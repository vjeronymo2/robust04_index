January 4, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

'BUSINESS REPORT' PACT EXTENDED

Reuters Holdings PLC and WPBT2/Miami said today they have extended by three years their "Nightly Business Report" television show association, effective Feb. 1.

The agreement continues the original two-year relationship begun in February, 1988, under which Reuters provides worldwide information services and editorial and financial support to the "Nightly Business Report."

The "Nightly Business Report" television program airs on more than 270 public television stations in the United States. The show is produced in Miami at WPBT2/Miami.
