941104 FT 04 NOV 94 / Observer: Good grief Gremlins entered the Foreign Office's machinery this week. Replying to a written question on wine purchases, Alastair Goodlad, the normally lugubrious foreign office minister, referred to the hitherto obscure Government Hospitality Fun Advisory Committee. Not another government quango? No; for 'Fun' read 'Fund'. Not much difference, when you come to think of it.
