Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] Tonight terrorists hurled a package with explosives at the Banco de Credito branch located in Caqueta Avenue. The explosion caused minor injuries to three passersby and property damage to the bank. Other explosions were also reported tonight in Lima. This is the fourth time subversive units have attacked the Banco de Credito located one block from Parque del Trabajo, between the districts of Rimac and San Martin de Porras. The incident took place at approximately at 2000 when a package with explosives was thrown by two terrorists traveling in a white Volkswagen. The blast damaged the bank's neon sign and also ruptured the potable water pipes. The police also reported similar attacks to local banks located in Peru and Mexico Avenues in La Victora. Only minor property damages and no casualties have been reported.
