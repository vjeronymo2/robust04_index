April 18, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

An editing error in Monday's Calendar distorted Martin Bernheimer's review of "Asia," as performed by the Feld Ballet in San Diego. As published, the passage read: "There is a lot of arm in too many directions, both stylistic and emotive, for aesthetic comfort."

The passage should have read: "There is a lot of arm undulating, a lot of mock-Oriental posing, a lot of twisting, churning and limb-tangling among the lunging ladies. The communal silliness rambles in too many directions, both stylistic and emotive, for aesthetic comfort."
