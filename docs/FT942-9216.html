940517 FT 17 MAY 94 / UK Company News: British Gas in power generation move British Gas yesterday moved to strengthen its position in the fast-growing international power generation sector by buying a 51 per cent stake in US-based Gas Ventures Advisers. GVA, which was set up in 1991 with financial help from British Gas, specialises in the development of private sector, gas-fired power generation projects, mainly in Malaysia and Indonesia.
