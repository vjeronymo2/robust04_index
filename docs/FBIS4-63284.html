BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Aleksandrova] [Text] Moscow May 20 TASS -- A meeting between Russian President Boris Yeltsin and French Minister of Foreign Affairs Alain Juppe has begun in the Kremlin. Journalists are not admitted to the talks, but it is known that the Russian president is probing France's stance on the forthcoming summit of the Group of Seven leading industrial countries in Naples, in which the Russian president will take part, and Russia's relations with the European Union and the North Atlantic Treaty Organisation.
