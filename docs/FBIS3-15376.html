Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] Azeri military police units on Thursday [3 March] blocked the approaches to the village of Keleki in Nakhichevan where ex-president Abulfaz Elchibey is staying. Former state secretary [Ali] Karimov told Interfax that armed units of the New Azerbaijan party, which as we know is headed by the current leader President Heydar Aliyev, were also involved in the operation. Karimov said "a punitive operation designed as a reprisal against Abulfaz Elchibey could start any moment now".
