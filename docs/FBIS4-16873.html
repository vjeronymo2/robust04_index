BFN [Text] There was heavy fighting between Armenia and Azerbaijan in the Azerbaijani parts of Nagorno-Karabakh in the north and northeast over the past twenty four hours, the press center for the enclave told Interfax. Karabakh units on Monday beat off the Azerbaijani advance to the east of the administrative center Mardakert regaining strategic points outside Lenivan. The center put Azerbaijani losses at more than 80 troops, one tank and one APC [armored personnel carrier]. Five Armenians were also killed and 17 others injured. One Armenian APC was destroyed. There was no fighting overnight with positional battles being reported by 11:00 on Tuesday. For its part, the defence ministry in Baku reports about a further Armenian advance in Ter-Ter and Geranboy regions of Azerbaijan north of the disputed territory. Armenian forces lost two tanks and one APC after last night's fighting there, according to Baku. One Armenian tank and one APC were also hit during battles in Agdam region. The ministry said that dozens of Armenian soldiers were killed or wounded.
