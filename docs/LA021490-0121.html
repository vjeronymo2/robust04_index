February 14, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

4-A BOYS;

AFTER 23 YEARS, BUENA PARK ADVANCES

Donyelle Collins scored 23 points, including 16 in the first half, to lead Buena Park High School to a 61-56 victory over Garden Grove in a 4-A Southern Section boys' basketball playoff game Tuesday.

It was the first playoff victory for Buena Park since 1967.

Ray Lanier scored 15 points for Buena Park, including a layup with one second left in the first half to give Buena Park a 30-29 lead. The Coyotes (12-12) never trailed again.

Buena Park outscored Garden Grove, 17-10, to take a 47-39 lead. Garden Grove cut the lead to 58-55 with about a minute left.

Mark Perry scored 26 points for Garden Grove (11-13). DALE RUHE
