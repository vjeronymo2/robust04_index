Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 5, IRNA -- President Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani paid a visit to the ailing Ayatollah Abdolkarim Musavi Ardebili in Khatam-ol-Anbiya hospital Tuesday [4 January] night. The president prayed to the Almighty God for his immediate recovery. He was also briefed on the efforts carried out by the doctors and medical team treating the ayatollah. Ayatollah Ardebili, a Tehran substitute Friday prayer leader and former judiciary chief, was taken to hospital last week after suffering an apoplectic stroke.
