920124 FT 24 JAN 92 / S Africa-Angola pact South African foreign minister Pik Botha arrived in the Angolan capital, Luanda, yesterday to sign an agreement that will lead to the two former enemies exchanging diplomatic missions, the Portuguese news agency, Lusa, said, Reuter reports from Lisbon. Government sources in Cape Town said earlier this week that trade links were on the horizon, including sales of Angolan oil.
