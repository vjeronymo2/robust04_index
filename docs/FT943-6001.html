940901 FT 01 SEP 94 / SFA bans and fines ex-banker Mr Ronald Thurlow, a former executive at the Norwegian Den Norske Bank in London, was yesterday banned from stockbroking by the Securities and Futures Authority. He was found guilty of recording fictitious transactions, fined Pounds 25,000 and ordered to pay Pounds 2,700 costs. The SFA declared him 'no longer fit and proper' to be accepted for employment in the City. His junior trader and assistant Mr Steven Royce was reprimanded by the SFA and fined Pounds 10,000 with Pounds 1,975 costs.
