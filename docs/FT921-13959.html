920118 FT 18 JAN 92 / UK Company News: LPA Inds almost halved to Pounds 476,000 'The squeeze on margins' across the sector were shown in the almost halved pre-tax profits at LPA Industries in the year to September 30. Mr Arthur Rusch, chairman of this USM-quoted industrial electrical accessories group, said that the results 'graphically display the severity of the current recession'. Profits fell to Pounds 476,000 (Pounds 904,000) pre-tax and they were struck on turnover down at Pounds 6.49m (Pounds 7.07m). Earnings fell to 3.69p (6.69p) and the final dividend is held at 1.87p for an unchanged total of 3.52p.
