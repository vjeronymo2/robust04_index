May 5, 1989, Friday, Southland Edition

RARE RED WOLF GIVES BIRTH TO THREE PUPPIES IN TEXAS

A red wolf, the second-rarest mammal in the United States, gave birth to three puppies at a wildlife center.

If the puppies born Wednesday survive, they will be the first Texas-born red wolves since the mid-1970s, raising the total population to 92, said officials at Fossil Rim Wildlife Center.

The black-footed ferret is the only mammal that is rarer in the United States.
