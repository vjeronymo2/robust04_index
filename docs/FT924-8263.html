921112 FT 12 NOV 92 / International Company News: Loblaw revenues up 6% to CDollars 2.8bn LOBLAW, Canada's biggest food distributor and controlled by the Weston family, faced intense third-quarter competition and tightening margins in the depressed Ontario market. Revenues were up 6 per cent to CDollars 2.8bn, (Dollars 2.2bn) but earnings dipped 25 per cent to CDollars 23.7m or 26 cents a share from CDollars 31.5m.
