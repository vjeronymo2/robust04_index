930205 FT 05 FEB 93 / UK Company News: DC Cook Pounds 56,000 in the red DC COOK Holdings, the USM-quoted motor dealer and property company, swung from profits of Pounds 107,000 to losses of Pounds 56,000 pre-tax for the half year ended October 31. The interim dividend is omitted - 0.2p was paid previously. Consideration of a final (0.3p) will be made when results for the full year are known. Losses per share on a fully diluted basis emerged at 0.12p (earnings 0.33p). Total sales were Pounds 52.3m compared with Pounds 56.9m. Sales of continuing operations were Pounds 51.2m for pre-tax profits of Pounds 68,000.
