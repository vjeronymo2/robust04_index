920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Chamberlin & Hill slips to Pounds 1.43m CHAMBERLIN & HILL yes-terday reported a 10 per cent fall in annual profits but pointed to an improvement in the second half. It backed this up with a 0.25p increase in the final dividend to 4.25p. Shares in the iron castings, electrical conduit and switchgear company rose 33p to 130p. Sales in the year to March 31 fell to Pounds 19m (Pounds 19.5m), interest payable rose to Pounds 124,000 (Pounds 79,000) and pre-tax profits amounted to Pounds 1.43m (Pounds 1.59m). Earnings came through at 15.28p (16.64p) and the total dividend is 6p (5.75p).
