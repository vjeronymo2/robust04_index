930921 FT 21 SEP 93 / International Company News: BIL's Cedenco move BRIERLEY Investments will take a 25 per cent stake in the initial public offering by Cedenco Foods, the New Zealand foods group, AP-DJ reports from Wellington. Announcing the share offer, Cedenco said it plans to raise NZDollars 25.3m (USDollars 13.9m). Funds raised will be used primarily to finance an expansion programme, said the company.
