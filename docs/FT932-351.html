930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Camellia up to Pounds 11.5m CAMELLIA, an investment company, staged a sharp recovery in the second half of 1992 with pre-tax profits for the year advancing from Pounds 7.86m to Pounds 11.5m. The accounts - prepared under FRS 3 - shown turnover ahead from Pounds 164.2m to Pounds 171.9m. Operating profit from continuing operations was Pounds 13.5m (Pounds 14.4m). The final dividend is 17p making 29p (27p). Earnings were 114.24p (41p).
