BFN [Text] Rafah, 29 May (MENA) -- Six trucks loaded with weapons, ammunition, other supplies, foodstuffs, tents, and equipment, and a number of light vehicles for the Palestinian police force, crossed Rafah border point this afternoon to reinforce the Palestinian security forces in the self-rule regions. Major General 'Abd-al-Razzaq al-Mahayidah, commander of the Palestinian forces in Rafah, said 250 Palestinian policemen coming from Algeria are due to enter Rafah tomorrow, Monday. He said negotiations will be held with Israel on the Rafah and Jericho crossing points with a view to completing technical preparations there before handing them over to the Palestinian National Authority.
