September 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CHEECH DEFENDED

Noffsinger's blaming the movies of Cheech and Chong for his drug problem is not going to help him recover.

Until he accepts that we are responsible for our own actions, he still has some crawling to do.

ANNE BEN-EFRAIM

Los Angeles

Noffsinger wrote: ". . . How many people began a descent into hell while using 'harmless drugs'? I did, and it's taken me over 13 years to crawl back."
