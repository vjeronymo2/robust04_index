Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, Feb. 24 (YONHAP) -- The Woosung business group, a leading business conglomerate of South Korea, will build and operate an international school here in Beijing. Woosung will set up an international school of elementary and secondary school courses in a joint venture with the Beijing Joint Agriculture, Industry and Commerce Corp., a public firm controlled by the Beijing municipality. A basic agreement on the school project was signed by the two partners in a brief ceremony here on Thursday. Ground is expected to be broken within the year for the construction of school facilities. Under the agreement, the two sides will equally invest 30 million dollars in the scheme. The school, capable of accommodating about 1,000 students, will be located at a 16,500-square-meter land in the Chaoyang Ward District in downtown Beijing. Choe Song-chin, vice group chairman, said here the two sides also agreed to build apartments, arcades and tennis courts around the school.
