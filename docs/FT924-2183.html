921214 FT 14 DEC 92 / US and Russia fail to agree on slashing their arsenals The US and Russia failed yesterday to agree on slashing their arsenals of long-range nuclear missiles, Reuter reports from Stockholm. However, Russian foreign minister Andrei Kozyrev and US secretary of state Lawrence Eagleburger said they had made some progress on Start II and it was possible there could be agreement before the Bush administration leaves office.
