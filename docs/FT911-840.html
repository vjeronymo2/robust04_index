910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Administrators called in at West Inds offshoots SHARES IN West Industries fell from 3 1/2 p to 2p yesterday after the engineering and construction group announced that administrative receivers had been appointed at certain of its recently acquired subsidiaries. Arthur Andersen has been appointed receivers to Audit & General and its following subsidiaries - H&M Machinery, Audio Installation and Maintenance Services, Marlpen, H&T Components and Humberside Estates. West Industries recently acquired Audit and General, the industrial, property and nursing home company, with a recommended all-paper offer worth Pounds 1.73m. The company said Svenska Handelsbanken, the Swedish bank, had appointed the receivers. West said it continued to enjoy the support of its remaining banks, who were currently looking at proposals to refinance the group. West Industries' shares have fallen steadily from the beginning of last year, when they were 25p. At yesterday's close, the group had a market value of Pounds 800,000.
