Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Algeria/Italy: Italian Nationals Urged To Leave"] [Text] (ANSA) Algiers, March 23 -- The Italian Foreign Ministry has asked the several hundred Italian nationals in Algeria to leave the country unless their presence there is "indispensable," diplomatic sources said here today. The sources said the "suggestion" was similar to the ones offered by France and other European Union countries through their diplomatic missions here.
