930420 FT 20 APR 93 / International Company News: Operating loss deepens at Austrian Airlines AUSTRIAN Airlines, the Austrian state-controlled carrier, saw its operating loss jump last year to Sch437.1m (Dollars 39m) from Sch156.9m in 1991. No dividend will be paid for last year. The airline is expecting a similar loss this year and plans no dividend for 1993. Austrian, which is one of four European airlines studying combined operations, said its revenues rose 12.1 per cent to Sch11.1bn, but operating expenses advanced 12.9 per cent to Sch11.6bn to produce a loss of Sch524.7m from flights and associated services. The loss was reduced slightly by a Sch87.6m financial result, down 63 per cent from the previous year. Cash-flow was off 3.9 per cent to Sch1.04bn.
