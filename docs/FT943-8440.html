940817 FT 17 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Dixons Mr Stanley Kalms, chairman of Dixons, the electrical retailer, has waived his bonus payment for the second successive year, according to the annual report. Mr Kalms, who waived a payment of Pounds 35,000 due under his service contract in 1992-93, refused a similar payment of Pounds 55,000 for the year to April 1994. Mr Mark Souhami, deputy chairman, also waived a Pounds 37,000 bonus. The chairman's total remuneration, however, still rose by 6 per cent from Pounds 563,000 to Pounds 598,000. Mr Souhami's was virtually unchanged at Pounds 305,000.
