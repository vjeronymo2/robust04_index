BFN [Text] African National Congress Secretary General Cyril Ramaphosa has announced a wide range of measures to adapt the ANC to its new role as the senior partner in the South African Government. The measures include retrenchments of ANC head office staff, the opening of a Cape Town headquarters, and restructuring the organization to prepare it to play a role in implementing the Reconstruction and Development Program. Mr. Ramaphosa said the ANC's finances were also under scrutiny and that levies were being imposed on its paid public representatives to help finance the organization.
