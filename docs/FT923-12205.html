920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / French industrial production falls 1.6 per cent French industrial production fell by 1.6 per cent in May, a sharp turnround from the 1.5 per cent increase in April, said Insee, the state statistics body. It attributed the fall to a 5.5 per cent decline in energy consumption, due to warm weather in May. Manufacturing industry production was roughly stable over the period, a more accurate indication of the real trend.
