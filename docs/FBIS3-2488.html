Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, March 8 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Premier Li Peng today met with visiting Azerbaijani President Geydar Aliyev, urging common efforts to step up bilateral economic cooperation and trade. Reviewing the bilateral trade and cooperation, Li Peng said that the bilateral trade is still at a comparatively low level due to problems in transportation, trade form and areas of cooperation. "The two sides should make concerted efforts to expand areas of cooperation and promote the development of economic cooperation and trade," the Chinese premier said. Aliyev appreciated China's successes in its economic reform over the past decade, saying that it is wise to work out principles and policies in light of the actual conditions and characteristics. Aliyev promised to further its cooperation with China in every field. Li Tieying, State Councillor and minister in charge of the State Commission for Restructuring the Economy, called at Aliyev at the state guesthouse here this afternoon and exchanged views with him on economic reforms. Aliyev spoke highly of tremendous achievements China has made in its reform and opening to the outside world. Azerbaijan is ready to study China's successful experience in reform, he said.
