March 4, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

DUAL PERFORMANCE

James Brosnahan, representing Richard T. Silberman, enjoys a fine reputation as a defense attorney from San Francisco.

Mr. Silberman's guilt or innocence will be determined in court. I look forward to Brosnahan's performance there as an attorney, once he finishes playing as a public relations agent.

PHIL HART

San Diego
