940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Home Counties leaps to Pounds 976,000 Pre-tax profits at Home Counties Newspaper Holdings leapt from Pounds 155,000 to Pounds 976,000 in the six months to June 30. Turnover was up slightly from Pounds 12.1m to Pounds 13.2m. The result was helped by an exceptional gain from the sale of the share in Mid Anglia Radio to GWR for Pounds 316,000. The interim dividend is 2.5p (2p). Earnings per share were 6.54p (1.24p).
