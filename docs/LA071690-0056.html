July 16, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

IN AND OUT: William D. Davis of Villa Park is the new commissioner of the state Department of Savings and Loan, but he may have a short tenure (D6). . . . Not only is a new governor in store next year, but the state Legislature only reluctantly gave Davis' agency a full-year budget. . . . When Gov. Deukmejian's term expires, "I'll have to go out and get a real job," Davis said. James S. Granelli and Mark I. Pinsky
