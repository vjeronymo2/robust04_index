920924 FT 24 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Depressed house sales hit Dencora Dencora, the property investment and development and housebuilding group, saw profits drop to just Pounds 20,000 pre-tax in the six months to June 30. The outcome, which reflected a reduced rent roll and depressed house sales, compared with Pounds 210,000 last time. Net interest charges amounted to Pounds 3.39m (Pounds 2.66m), none of which was capitalised. In the last full year, interest totalled Pounds 9.5m, with Pounds 3.3m capitalised and Pounds 6.2m charged to the profit and loss account. Borrowings at the period end were Pounds 59m, down from Pounds 74m in December. Fully diluted earnings per share dipped to 0.5p (0.9p); the company does not pay interim dividends.
