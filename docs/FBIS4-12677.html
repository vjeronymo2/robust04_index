BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn received at the Royal Court today Salim Salih Muhammad, member of the Yemeni Presidential Council and assistant secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], and Dr. 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Dali, member of the YSP Political Bureau, who are currently on a tour of a number of Arab countries to brief their leaders on the present situation in Yemen. His Majesty King Husayn stressed the need to adhere to the agreements that have been reached and to implement the provisions of the Yemeni accord document, which was signed in Amman and approved by the Yemeni political forces. His majesty also reaffirmed Jordan's concern about fraternal Yemen's march of unity, democracy, and development. He also stressed the need to consolidate the efforts of all Yemeni political forces in order to overcome this crisis through dialogue and adherence to what these forces agree on. For his part, Salim Salih Muhammad, member of the Yemeni Presidential Council, expressed the Yemeni leadership's appreciation for His Majesty King Husayn's concern about the unity of the Yemeni ranks and the sincere efforts his majesty is exerting so that the brothers in Yemen can overcome this crisis. He stressed that Jordanian mediation efforts to end the differences among the Yemeni political forces are appreciated and welcomed by everyone. Within the next few days, a ranking Jordanian delegation will leave for Yemen to meet with the Yemeni leadership and to follow up Jordanian efforts aimed at enabling the brothers in Yemen to overcome this crisis and maintain their march of unity, democracy, and development. The meeting was attended by al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, chief of the Royal Court; Marwan al-Qasim, his majesty's political adviser; Dr. Khalid al-Karaki, his majesty's adviser; and the Yemeni ambassador in Amman.
