BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Mikhail Karlov] [Text] Moscow May 21 TASS -- Former Russian Parliament Chairman Ruslan Khasbulatov has confirmed his intention to quit politics and fully devote himself to science. In his recent interview with ITAR-TASS in Groznyy where he moved after his release from prison in February 1993, Khasbulatov said that he will return to his academic work at the Russian Academy of Economics. One of the academy leaders said that Khasbulatov has been in charge of the Department of Foreign Economic Relations for two weeks.
