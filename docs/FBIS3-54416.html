Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Kuwait, 26 Jan (KUNA) -- Maldives President Maumoon Abdul Gayoom left Kuwait today at the end of a three-day official visit. He was seen off by Kuwaiti Amir Shaykh Jabir al-Ahmad al- Jabir al-Sabah; Prime Minister Shaykh Sa'd al-'Abdallah al-Salim al- Sabah, the heir apparent; and a number of senior Kuwaiti officials. During his visit President Abdul Gayoom conducted talks with the amir and senior officials which centered on the latest developments in bilateral relations and issues of common interest.
