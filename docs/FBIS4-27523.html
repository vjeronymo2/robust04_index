BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 1 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea Tuesday sent a message of condolence to Margot Honecker, the widow of Erich Honecker, upon the death of her husband. President Kim Il-song expressed deep condolences to Mrs. Honecker and her family and relatives. Recalling that Erich Honecker was invariably faithful to the revolutionary cause of the working class for socialism and peace against imperialism and fascism and actively supported the Korean people in the cause of socialist construction and national reunification, President Kim Il-song said the exploits he performed in his lifetime would remain for ever.
