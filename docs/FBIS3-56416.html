Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Russian President Boris Yeltsin began his meeting at midday Tuesday [18 January] with Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin. Expected to also take part is first Vice Premier Oleg Soskovets. They are to make a final decision on the government's new composition. Government sources say that Soskovets, as the first deputy prime minister, will continue to be responsible for industry, transport, the military-industrial complex, and the fuel and energy complex. The meeting of the government commission responsible for solving urgent problems, which was planned for Tuesday at 3 p.m., has been postponed for an indefinite period. The meeting, which was to discuss measures on how to stabilize the situation in the aluminum industry, investment funds in the power industry, and export quotas for 1994, is likely to take place next week.
