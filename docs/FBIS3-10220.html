Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] San Cristobal de las Casa (Mexico), 2 Mar (EFE) -- Bishop Samuel Ruiz has announced the Zapatist National Liberation Army (EZLN) and Manuel Camacho Solis, commissioner for peace and reconciliation in Chiapas, have reached an initial agreement for ending the armed conflict in this southeastern Mexico state. For his part, Camacho Solis has said there is a possibility the Mexican Congress will be summoned for special sessions to discuss the reforms that will guarantee impartial presidential elections in August, which is one of the EZLN demands.
