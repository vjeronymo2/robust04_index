BFN [Article by Marco Antonio Moreira] [Text] Belo Horizonte, 30 May (AE) -- Although President Itamar Franco has assured that his government will not get involved in the electoral campaign, Telerj [Rio de Janeiro Telephone Company] President Jose de Castro today announced that he will coordinate the presidential campaign of Brazilian Social Democracy Party candidate Fernando Henrique Cardoso. Telerj is a subsidiary of the state-owned company Telebras [Brazilian Telecommunications Inc.]. De Castro is a member of the "Juiz de Fora group," which comprises President Franco's closest friends. He said he will hold an executive position in Cardoso's campaign and that he will serve as permanent liaison between Franco and Cardoso.
