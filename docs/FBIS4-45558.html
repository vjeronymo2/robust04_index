BFN [Excerpt] Lima, 24 Jun (EFE) -- A law establishing prison sentences of eight to 15 years for people who grow heroin-producing poppies went into effect in Peru today. The law was approved by Congress on 22 June. It confirms the sentences included in the original bill. The Peruvian penal code now includes the "crime of growing, promoting, assisting, or financing" the planting of poppy plantations used for making opium or heroin. [passage omitted]
