March 18, 1989, Saturday, Southland Edition

LAZERS TO TAKE ON WINGS TONIGHT IN WICHITA

The Lazers, who ended a four-game losing streak with a victory Thursday night at the Forum, will try for two in a row tonight at Wichita, Kan., where they play the Wings in the first of three road games.

The Lazers, after a 10-day layoff, beat the first-place Baltimore Blast, 3-2.

Hector Marinaro scored three goals -- giving him 40 this season -- to become the Major Indoor Soccer League's leader in hat tricks with six, while the Lazer defense held Baltimore's usually productive offense in check.
