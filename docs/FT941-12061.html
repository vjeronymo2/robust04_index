940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Illingworth Morris Illingworth Morris, part of the private textiles group controlled by Mr Alan Lewis, reported an 82 per cent jump in pre-tax profits from Pounds 437,000 to Pounds 797,000 in the six months to September 30. Profits on continuing operations rose to Pounds 1.13m (Pounds 1.01m), while Pounds 273,000 (Pounds 68,000) related to Jermain & Son which was sold to a fellow group undertaking last August. Turnover grew 5 per cent to Pounds 27.1m (Pounds 25.8m), of which continuing operations accounted for Pounds 24.2m (Pounds 25.2m). Earnings per share came out at 1.1p (0.3p).
