921119 FT 19 NOV 92 / International Company News: Cascades returns to the black CASCADES, a Canadian-based paper and packaging group with plants in Europe, earned CDollars 13.6m (USDollars 10.6m), or 25 cents a share, in the first nine months, against a loss of CDollars 4.9m, or 9 cents a share, a year earlier. Sales were CDollars 643m, up 4 per cent. Third quarter profit was CDollars 1.3m, or 2 cents a share, against a loss of CDollars 8.3m, or 16 cents, a share a year earlier on sales of CDollars 217m, compared with Dollars 195m. Both 1991 periods included write-downs for a pulp mill closure. Cascades expects to make an initial public offering of Cascades Paperboard International this year. This new subsidiary includes nearly all its international packaging interests.
