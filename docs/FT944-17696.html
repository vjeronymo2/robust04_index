941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / Private buyers deserting new car market Private buyers appear to be deserting the new car market in growing numbers, although buoyant fleet business is keeping it above last year's levels, according to figures released yesterday Registration of new cars in September totalled 144,998, a 6.54 per cent increase on the same month a year ago, said the Society of Motor Manufacturers and Traders. But registrations to private buyers were down by 4.6 per cent. Page 12
