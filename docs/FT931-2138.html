930322 FT 22 MAR 93 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 35m orders awarded to Try Group TRY CONSTRUCTION and TRY BUILD, members of the Try Group, have been awarded contracts totalling Pounds 35m. More than 60 per cent of the group's workload has been secured by negotiation or has arisen as novations from the receivers of failed contractors. The recent acquisitions of AF Budge Building and CBM Construction in Yorkshire alone have given Try Pounds 20m of work. Try Construction has secured contracts totalling Pounds 19m which include the negotiated fit out of offices for a major petrochemical company in Sunbury worth Pounds 3.82m. Try Build has contracts worth Pounds 15.6m which include a Pounds 1.1m community centre at Slough for Berkshire County Council and an extension to Bishop Challenor School (Pounds 630,000) for the Diocese of Westminster Trustees.
