941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / International Company News: Scott jumps by 75% as restructure continues Scott Paper, the international tissue paper manufacturer that has undergone a restructuring, yesterday reported a near 75 per cent jump in third-quarter net income to Dollars 60.6m, or 80 cents a share, writes Laurie Morse in Chicago. This compares with Dollars 35.1m, or 48 cents, in the comparable quarter last year. Scott last week announced the sale of its S. D. Warren printing paper subsidiary to the South African company Sappi, as part of its strategy to focus on tissue-based products. The profits advance was made in spite of flat sales, at Dollars 1.1bn.
