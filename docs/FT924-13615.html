921014 FT 14 OCT 92 / Venezuela plans to accelerate its privatisation programme The Venezuelan government plans to accelerate its privatisation programme with the sale by the end of 1993 of more than 100 enterprises and minority shareholdings, writes Stephen Fidler. Mr Carlos Hernandez Delfino, president of the Venezuelan Investment Fund responsible for privatisation, said the programme would include the sale of three electricity generation and distribution companies - Enelbar, Enelven and Enelco. The sale of the Planta Centro thermal power generation plant is also planned.
