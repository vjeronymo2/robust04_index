941006 FT 06 OCT 94 / Growth in Channel tunnel traffic launch of Channel tunnel services more than 110 trains now pass through the tunnel each day, Sir Alastair Morton, co-chairman of Eurotunnel, said yesterday. There had been more than 100 Eurostar Express journeys since full-scale tests began last month with most achieving the scheduled times of three hours, London-Paris, and 3 1/4 hours, London-Brussels, he said.
