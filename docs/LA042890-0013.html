April 28, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

L.A. FIESTA BROADWAY:

The stage is set for L.A. Fiesta Broadway, the county's largest Cinco de Mayo celebration, which takes place at noon Sunday. Entertainers will perform on seven stages from First Street to Olympic Boulevard. Braodway will be blocked off to traffic from Temple Street on the north to Olympic Boulevard on the south from 10 a.m. to 10 p.m., George Carey / Los Angeles Times
