BFN [Text] Tehran, June 29, IRNA -- Azeri President Geydar Aliyev arrived here Wednesday [29 June] heading a ranking political, economic, social and cultural delegation. President Aliyev and his entourage were welcomed at international Mehrabad Airport by President 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani. The Azeri delegation will pay a four-day visit to Iran. President Aliyev is being accompanied by ministers of foreign affairs, economy, national security, health, deputy prime minister, a number of presidential aides, head of the clerical bureaux of Caucasia, several parliamentarians, head of the Supreme Council and Nakhichevan's premier.
