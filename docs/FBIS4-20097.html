BFN [Text] Bonn (DDP/ADN) -- Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel said he is confident that a peaceful solution may be possible in Bosnia-Herzegovina after all, due to the most recent UN decisions. Priority at the moment is to remove the Serbs' "terrible stranglehold" around Gorazde, Kinkel said in Bonn today. He stressed that the agreed cease-fire is basically being adhered to. There is at present no reason for NATO to intervene.
