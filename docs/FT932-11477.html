930504 FT 04 MAY 93 / Observer: Going for broke So chancellor Norman Lamont was right all along - those green shoots were stirring even in the depths of recession. The latest retailer profitability rankings, drawn up by market research firm Corporate Intelligence, show Harvey & Thompson, Britain's 581st largest retailer - since last year trading as Lightship - firmly at the head of the league table achieving an extravagant 46.4 per cent pre-tax margin on sales in 1990-91. Its business? A pawnbroker.
