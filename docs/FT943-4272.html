940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Gowrings The recovery seen by Gowrings in the second half of the previous year continued in the six months to June 30 with pre-tax profits of Pounds 179,000, against losses of Pounds 137,000. Turnover improved 11 per cent to Pounds 28.4m (Pounds 25.7m) including Pounds 492,000 (Pounds 1.12m) from discontinued activities. Earnings per share came out at 1.68p (1.84p losses) and the interim dividend is maintained at 1p.
