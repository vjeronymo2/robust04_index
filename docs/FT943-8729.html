940816 FT 16 AUG 94 / House property market 'lethargic' Fewer people are searching for a new home during the holiday season but prices remain stable, the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors said yesterday. The residential property market was 'lethargic', it said, with 54 per cent of estate agents saying levels of activity in the three months to the end of July had fallen compared with the three months to the end of June, 35 per cent saying it remained the same and 10 per cent reporting an increase in activity.
