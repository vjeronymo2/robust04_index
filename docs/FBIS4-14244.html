BFN [Text] Cairo, 27 Apr (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa has stated that Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat will arrive in Cairo at midnight, Israeli Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres will arrive tomorrow morning, and U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher will arrive this evening. Musa said a session of talks with the U.S. secretary of state will be held tomorrow morning. The talks will focus on the Middle East peace process and international issues of mutual interest. He added that President Mubarak will receive the three visitors in separate meetings tomorrow after the speech that he will deliver on workers' day. Musa said Palestinian officials have informed him that the delegations to the Palestinian-Israeli talks have reached an agreement on most issues. He indicated that the Syrian track in the peace talks will be discussed tomorrow. Musa said contacts are under way to arrange a meeting between Christopher, 'Arafat, and Peres.
