910415 FT 15 APR 91 / International Company News: PepsiCo to invest Dollars 1.5bn in expansion PEPSICO, the US food and beverage conglomerate, said yesterday it plans to invest Dollars 1.5bn in expansion during 1991 with the bulk of spending going on its three restaurant chains, writes Our Financial Staff. PepsiCo, whose 19,500 fast-food outlets make it the world's biggest restaurant operator, said about Dollars 600m is being earmarked this year to open new outlets of Kentucky Fried Chicken, Pizza Hut and Taco Bell. PepsiCo said the balance of 1991 capital spending will be divided almost evenly between the Pepsi-Cola soft drink business and snack foods, in which Frito-Lay is the biggest subsidiary. Chairman Mr Wayne Calloway said his company would post 'double-digit' growth in sales and profits this year.
