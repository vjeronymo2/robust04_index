October 25, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: PASADENA ELKS DONATE $500 FOR DRUG BANNERS

Pasadena Elks Lodge No. 672 has donated $500 to buy 20 anti-drug banners for the Pasadena Unified School District in honor of Red Ribbon Week.

Each elementary school in the district will get an 8-by-4-foot banner with the slogan, "Line up to sign up for a drug free decade." Posters will be provided where pupils, teachers, parents and staff can sign their names under the banner.

Red Ribbon Week is a nationwide anti-drug campaign to urge students to lead healthy, drug-free lives.
