Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Kabul, Jan. 5, IRNA -- Iranian Embassy in Kabul has launched serious efforts to broker a cease-fire among the Afghan rival factions, Iran's ambassador to Kabul, Gholamreza Haddadi, told reporters here Wednesday. Haddadi stated that as far as negotiations with different Afghan rival factions are concerned, they seem to be ready to accept mediation for establishment of a cease-fire. "Afghan rival factions believe that none of the country's political parties should be excluded from the future government of Afghanistan," Haddadi added.
