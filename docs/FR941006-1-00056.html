DATES: Comments must be filed on or before November 25, 1994, and reply comments on or before December 12, 1994. 47 CFR Part 73 [MM Docket No. 94&hyph;114, RM&hyph;8515] Radio Broadcasting Services; Ettrick, VA AGENCY: Federal Communications Commission. ACTION: Proposed rule.

SUMMARY: The Commission requests comments on a petition filed by Hoffman Communications, Inc., proposing the allotment of Channel 226A to Ettrick, Virginia, as the community's first local aural transmission service. Channel 226A can be allotted to Ettrick in compliance with the Commission's minimum distance separation requirements with a site restriction of 12.3 kilometers (7.6 miles) northwest in order to avoid a short-spacing conflict with Station WFOG(FM), Channel 225B, Suffolk, Virginia. The coordinates for Channel 226A at Ettrick are 37&hyph;17&hyph;53 and 77&hyph;32&hyph;53. DATES: Comments must be filed on or before November 25, 1994, and reply comments on or before December 12, 1994.
