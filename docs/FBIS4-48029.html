BFN [Text] Violent clashes were reported inside N'dalatando yesterday with the MPLA-PT [Popular Movement for the Liberation of Angola-Labor Party] air force dropping napalm, toxic, and highly explosive bombs throughout the day. Entire buildings have been destroyed. Yesterday afternoon the city was covered with a huge cloud of smoke. Corpses of civilians have been lying on the streets since the past five days. Our correspondent says the combat readiness of National Union for the Total Independence of Angola [UNITA] forces remains high.
