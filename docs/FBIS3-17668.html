Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Daugavpils, Latvia, Mar 28, BNS -- The Russian Armed Forces will hand over the Daugavpils fortress to the local authorities in mid-April, the city's deputy mayor, Grigori Merkuryev, told reporters. The fortress housed the High School of Aviation Engineers, which was sent Russia in August, 1993. The compound, which includes living houses, store houses and fuel depots, is still guarded by a 200-men unit. The both parties have already coordinated the terms of handover, said Merkuryev. Under the draft agreement, the Russian Defense Ministry will temporary continue the ownership of the living houses.
