930709 FT 09 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Dewhurst rises but warns of slowdown Dewhurst, maker of electrical products and control equipment, increased turnover 23 per cent and pre-tax profit 30 per cent in the half year ended March 28 1993. However, the output of rail products was declining because of the delay of investment projects. Traditional markets remained depressed but a modest improvement was expected in full year profit over the previous Pounds 850,000. Turnover for the period was Pounds 5.17m (Pounds 4.21m) and profit Pounds 404,000 (Pounds 310,000). Earnings came to 2.36p (1.85p) and the interim dividend is 0.66p (0.63p).
