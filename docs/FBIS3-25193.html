Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The leader of the ANC [African National Congress], Mr. Nelson Mandela, has accused the National Party [NP] of using elements in the security forces to sow chaos in the country. Speaking at the annual congress of the National Union of Mineworkers in Pretoria, Mr. Mandela said the NP had used the security forces to ensure that SWAPO [South-West African People's Organization] did not get a two-thirds majority in the Namibian election. He said the two batallions used then were now being used to create chaos in South Africa.
