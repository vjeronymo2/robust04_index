920805 FT 05 AUG 92 / Decline in number of failures THE NUMBER of receivership and administration appointments in July fell for the sixth successive month, compared with the same month last year, according to figures released yesterday by Touche Ross, the accountancy firm. The statistics, based on an analysis of notices in the official London and Edinburgh Gazettes, show appointments at 474, compared with 549 in July last year. That is the highest number of appointments since March, and brings the total this year to 3,192, compared with 3,464 in the first seven months of last year.
