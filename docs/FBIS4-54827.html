BFN [Text] Brcko, 5 May (TANJUG) -- Talks began in Brcko this afternoon on the deployment of UN observers in the region. The UN Protection Forces [UNPROFOR] delegation to the talks is headed by Sergio de Melo, an assistant to Yasushi Akashi, and the Serb delegation by Dr. Nikola Koljevic, deputy president of the Serb Republic, and Major General Novica Simic, commander of the East Bosnia corps. The talks are also being attended by Miodrag Pajic, president of the Brcko municipal assembly, and by Milenko Vojinovic, deputy in the assembly of the republic of Srpska. Following the talks, the UNPROFOR delergation is expected to tour the confrontation lines and sectors of the frontlines. It is planned that the UNPROFOR delegation will visit the hospital in Brcko, the Interplet enterprise, and some parts of the town.
