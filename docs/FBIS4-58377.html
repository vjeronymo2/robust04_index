BFN [Text] According to a press note issued by the Karachi Central District magistrate last night, one person was killed and 11 injured in various violent incidents in the district yesterday. The press note says that after four days of continuous violent incidents the Army has been deployed in Karachi Central District to assist the civil administration in bringing the law and order problem under control and to prevent the situation from deteriorating further. Workers belonging to Muhajir Qaumi Movement-Altaf group and miscreants disturbed the law and order situation in Liaqatabad, Gulbahar, Nazimabad, (Nazam Chowringhee), and New Karachi. Twenty persons have been arrested. According to a press note issued by the district magistrate in Hyderabad last night, several miscreants tried to set ablaze a vehicle on Lajpat Road and another in (Faqirkappur) yesterday. However, the police controlled the situation in both places. There are no reports of any deaths or injuries and the overall situation in Hyderabad is under control.
