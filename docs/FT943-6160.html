940831 FT 31 AUG 94 / Observer: Dressing down Australian prime minister Paul Keating's attempts to see more women in important places looks in danger of being misinterpreted. Previously awarded high marks for efforts on behalf of women, not least through the Office of the Status of Women, he is hosting a reception next week marking the OSW's 20th anniversary. Dress code: 'Lounge suit, short dress.'
