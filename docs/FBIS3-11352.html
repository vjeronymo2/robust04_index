Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Samuel Prieto Rodriguez from the "Early Morning" newscast] [Text] Electoral authorities have approved the registration of the nine presidential candidates. Meanwhile, Government Secretary Jorge Carpizo said no final agreements have been reached in the talks on the special congressional sessions, because he has only exchanged views with three political parties. He added that he has met with the other political parties. [Begin Carpizo recording] There is a dialogue. There is respect. Views are being exchanged, and this will continue. [end recording] Carpizo was referring to the talks on the special congressional sessions. The IFE [Federal Electoral Institute] held two sessions today. There was a special session to vote on the official presidential candidacies, all of which were unanimously approved. [Begin recording of unidentified IFE official] All topics on the agenda for this special session with regard to the registration of candidates for the Presidency of the United Mexican States have been discussed. [end recording] There also was a regular meeting to discuss a report on training programs for citizens selected as members of the board of directors of the polling tables, which will be chaired by citizens born in the months of November and December. [Begin IFE official recording] It has been unanimously approved, Mr. President. [end recording] One resignation was announced: that of Counselor-Magistrate German Perez Fernandez del Castillo.
