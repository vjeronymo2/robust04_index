930901 FT 01 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Community Hospitals buys Community Hospitals has contracted to acquire a further two nursing home developments comprising 115 beds. In January the healthcare group reported the acquisition of three purpose-built homes consisting of 140 beds in Derbyshire and Leicestershire for Pounds 4.63m plus deferred consideration of Pounds 210,000. As indicated at that time, Community has now reached agreement to acquire for Pounds 3.8m sites at Little Aston, Birmingham, and Leicester Forest East. The two homes are due to open next year.
