BFN [Text] The outskirts of the towns of Kafra and Yatar have been coming under shelling since 1530 today from the occupation center in Jabal Humayyid. A center belonging to the Nepalese contingent in Yatar was hit by machine-gun fire coming from the Lahd agents' post in the occupied strip opposite the town. The Martyr Husayn Nasir Mahdi group of the Islamic Resistance laid an ambush for a Lahdist security convoy on the Bayt Lif road. The strugglers showered the convoy's cars with a hail of fire from machine guns and artillery. Several artillery shells hit the car transporting the security officials. It was burnt out immediately and its occupants were either killed or injured.
