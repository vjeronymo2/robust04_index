940621 FT 21 JUN 94 / MPs probe Mersey Tall Ships losses Merseyside Development Corporation yesterday appeared before the Commons public accounts committee to defend its involvement in the lossmaking 1992 Tall Ships regatta on the Mersey, and the associated operatic concert. Mr Chris Farrow, corporation chief executive, said the corporation had not known the extent of the losses until the events themselves. In spite of ticket revenue between Pounds 470,000 and Pounds 500,000, there was a final shortfall of Pounds 375,000.
