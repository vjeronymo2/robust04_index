September 22, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

PREP ROUNDUP;

VILLA PARK WINS IN WATER POLO

Kip Parsons scored six goals to lead Villa Park High School to a 14-8 victory over San Clemente in the Villa Park water polo tournament.

San Clemente led, 3-2, in the first quarter, but by early in the fourth quarter, Villa Park (6-2) moved out to a 12-7 lead. Parsons scored three third-quarter goals in that run.

In nonleague water polo:

Cypress 13, Downey 8 -- John Lynn had eight goals to lead visiting Cypress (3-1). Bob Norum had two goals for Cypress. Robert Walshe had seven saves for Cypress.

In nonleague girls' tennis:

University 11, San Marcos 7 -- Jannette Fylpaa swept, 6-2, 6-0, 6-0, and Christina Pham swept, 6-1, 6-3, 6-2, to lead host University (3-3).
