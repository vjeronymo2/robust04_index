930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: MMC to probe waterways board THE BRITISH Waterways Board is to have its efficiency probed by the Monopolies and and Mergers Commission, Mr Neil Hamilton, the corporate affairs minister, announced last night. He said in a Commons written reply the move was part of the commission's regular programme of efficiency studies of publicly bodies. It will investigate the way the board, which looks after Britain's canals, carries out its functions. It will see if the board can increase efficiency, reduce costs and improve quality of service.
