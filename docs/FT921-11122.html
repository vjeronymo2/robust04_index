920203 FT 03 FEB 92 / Turkish avalanche death toll rises to 142 RESCUERS yesterday found more bodies in Kurdish mountain villages swallowed by avalanches, raising to 142 the death toll over the weekend, Reuter reports from Ankara. Some 150 other villagers have been missing after the series of avalanches which have hit south-eastern Turkey since Saturday. A European Community commissioner, Mr Abel Matutes, sent a message to the Turkish government yesterday offering emergency aid for victims of avalanches.
