Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Sanaa--[passage omitted] A responsible source at the Yemeni Ministry of Interior said last night that Colonel Yahya al-Jubi, director of political security in the Maharrah Governorate and member of the General People's Congress [GPC], was the target of an assassination attempt by unidentified gunmen who shot and wounded him near his house before escaping. Al-Jubi sustained several wounds in the attack, which took place the day before yesterday [11 February], according to the source. The source indicated that Col. al-Jubi is currently in the hospital intensive care unit, and that the security agencies are conducting extensive investigations to apprehend the assailants.
