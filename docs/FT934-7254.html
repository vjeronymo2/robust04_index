931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Henderson Strata net assets advance Net asset value per share of Henderson Strata Investments rose from 186.3p to 271p over the 12 months to October 31. Net revenue was static at Pounds 222,000 (Pounds 221,000). Earnings were 1.36p (1.45p) and the single dividend is held at 1.4p.
