940510 FT 10 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Chartwell Pounds 22m property swap Chartwell Land, the property arm of Kingfisher, the retail group, has swapped Pounds 22m of property with Shell Pensions Trust. Shell Pensions Trust has bought six industrial estates from Chartwell Land. In part exchange, Chartwell Land has bought Brislington Retail Park in Bristol and a retail warehouse in Fareham. It has also received a cash payment of about Pounds 4m.
