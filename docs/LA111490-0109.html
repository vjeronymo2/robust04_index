November 14, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

COLLEGE FOOTBALL REPORT;

WEEKDAY UPDATE;

AROUND THE NATION;

WASHINGTON STATE SUSPENDS QUARTERBACK

Washington State backup quarterback Aaron Garcia has been suspended from the team for underage drinking.

Coach Mike Price said Garcia will not play in Saturday's regular-season finale against Washington.

Garcia, 20, from Sacramento, was arrested Saturday in Moscow, Idaho, just across the border from Pullman, where the drinking age is 21.
