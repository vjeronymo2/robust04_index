BFN [Text] Doha, 11 Jun (WAKH)--His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of the State of Qatar, received Iranain Minister of Petroleum Gholam Reza Aqazadeh. The QATARI NEWS AGENCY said discussions at the meeting dealt with bilateral cooperation and the situation in the international oil markets.
