Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Excellency President Hafiz al-Asad received Lebanese Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz at 1245 today. Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' attended the meeting. Al-Shar' received Faris Buwayz this morning in the presence of Yusuf Shakkur, assistant foreign minister. The discussion during the meeting dealt with the peace process and its developments, and the tasks of the heads of the Syrian and Lebanese delegations who had left for Washington for consultations with the U.S. side on the forthcoming stages of the peace process. The two sides also reviewed the latest developments in the region.
