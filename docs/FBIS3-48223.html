Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, Jan. 21 (YONHAP) -- Samsung Co. has set its 1994 export goal at 10 billion U.S. dollars. The company had exports of 9.72 billion dollars last year. The seven other general trading companies are Hyundai Corp., with a target of 9.6 billion dollars, Daewoo Corp., 7.6 billion dollars, Lucky-Goldstar International Corp., 4.2 billion dollars, Sunkyong Ltd., 3 billion dollars, Ssangyong Corp., 2.8 billion dollars, Hyosung Corp., 2.5 billion dollars, and Lotte International Co., goal unknown. When Lotte reveals its goal, the total for the eight general trading companies is expected to be 39.97 billion dollars, up 10.7 percent from 36.16 billion dollars last year. Their share of total exports is projected to rise from 43.8 percent to 44.7 percent. Korea Trading International Inc., an export agent for small- and medium-sized companies, has an export target of 220 million dollars.
