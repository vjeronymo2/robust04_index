BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, chairman of the Presidential Council, met this morning brothers Haydar Abu-Bakr al-'Attas, the chairman of the Council of Ministers; 'Abdallah 'Abd-al-Majid al- Asnaj, deputy premier and foreign minister; Muhsin Abu-Bakr Bin Farid, deputy premier and minister of planning and development; and Salih Ubayd Ahmad, minister of transport and communications. The prime minister briefed his brother the chairman of the Presidential Council on the outcome of the tours made by him and his deputies to Saudi Arabia, Egypt, the United Arab Emirates [UAE] and Kuwait as well as on his meetings with the leaders of those countries and their briefings on the latest developments in the Yemeni crisis and the war imposed on our Yemeni people by the gang of the bloody al-Ahmar family. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid welcomed the patriotic personality of al-Ustadh 'Abdallah 'Abd-al-Majid al-Asnaj who returned to the homeland to participate with his brothers in building the Democratic Republic of Yemen, a new modern state, a state of law and order. The brother chairman of the Presidential Council expressed his satisfaction about the outcome of the tours made and praised the stand of those countries and their leaders on the side of the cause of our Yemeni people as well as their efforts at the Arab and international levels to stop this destructive war which causes concern for the security and stability of Yemen and the region. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid reiterated the unilateral and strict commitment of the Democratic Republic of Yemen to the decision of the UN Security Council Resolution No. 924 on the cease-fire. He stressed that all efforts must be concerted to implement the Security Council resolution and to compel the Sanaa military regime, which is challenging international legitimacy and is violating the council's resolution, to abide by the cease-fire resolution, stop the war and resort to peaceful dialogue to tackle the political situation.
