921104 FT 04 NOV 92 / BR sells consultancy BRITISH RAIL last night announced that Transmark, its consultancy subsidiary, is to be sold as a further step in the privatisation of its non-core businesses. Transmark, which has worked on more than 200 rail projects in 30 countries, made an operating surplus of Pounds 1.1m on turnover of Pounds 10m in the past financial year.
