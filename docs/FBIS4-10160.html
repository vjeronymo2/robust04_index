BFN [From the "Teleprensa" newscast] [Text] The National Congress has declared itself in permanent session to discuss the actions against members of this institutions, including the attack against Deputy Obdulio Chinchilla Vega, the attempted kidnapping of Deputy Rene Barrios Samayoa, and threats against Deputy Jose Lobo Dubon.
