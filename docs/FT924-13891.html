921013 FT 13 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Recovery at Mid-States First-half 1992 pre-tax profits from Mid-States, the London-quoted distributor of car parts in the US, were 17 per cent down on the corresponding figure, but 37 per cent ahead of the second half of 1991 largely as a result of cost reductions. Pre-tax profit this time for the company which is based in Nashville, Tennessee, came to Pounds 1.92m, against Pounds 2.33m and Pounds 1.4m in the second half, generated from turnovers of Pounds 24.9m, Pounds 23.8m and Pounds 25.1m respectively. Earnings per share worked through at 3.2p (4.1p) and the interim dividend is unchanged at 0.75p.
