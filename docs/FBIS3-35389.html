Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by 'Ali Dawud from al-Nabatiyah] [Text] Tension and anticipation still prevail in the liberated villages in the south in the wake of continued Israeli aggression, which was renewed today against villages in the central sector. The resistance retaliated with an operation against a joint patrol along the al-Qantarah-al-Tayyibah road this afternoon. The Israeli occupation troops bombarded the hills of Qabrikhah, Tulin, al-Tamiriyah, Shaqra, and Wadi al-Saluqi with 155-mm tank artillery and 120-mm mortars this afternoon. Citizen Hasan 'Ali 'Awali was wounded in Tulin and was taken to the Tibnin government hospital for treatment. At 1500 today, agent Lahd [South Lebanese Army] positions in 'Alman and al-Shumariyah bombarded the area surrounding the positions with 82-mm mortar fire. this was accompanied by machine-gun fire against adjacent forests. According to reports from the occupied border strip, the occupation troops have arrested Salim 'Awadah, member of the al- Naqurah municipal council; his sons Hasan, Ibrahim, and 'Isam; and the latter's wife and took them to the al-Khiyam prison. The Amal Movement and the Islamic Resistance announced in a joint statement that their forces attacked a joint patrol along the al- Qantarah-al-Tayyibah road at 1345 today. The statement said a vehicle was destroyed and its crew members were killed or wounded. Lahd's radio later reported the operation but kept silent on its losses.
