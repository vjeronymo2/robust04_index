BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah at al-Rawdah Palace at 1230 today. Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' attended the audience. Basindwah conveyed to the president a message from President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Yemeni Presidential Council. Talks dealt with Yemeni developments. The president voiced Syria's concern over the bloody events in fraternal Yemen and renewed Syria's appeal for the Yemeni brothers to renounce fighting and resort to resolving differences by brotherly dialogue, out of concern for Yemeni unity and the safety and security of the Yemeni people. The Yemeni foreign minister left Damascus at 1630 today. He was seen off at Damascus International Airport by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar', a number of Foreign Ministry officials, and the Yemeni embassy staff in Damascus. In a statement to the SANA correspondent, Basindwah said: The aim of my visit to Damascus was to convey a message from President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih to his brother President Hafiz al-Asad. Today I was honored to meet with the president. I conveyed the message and briefed him on the latest Yemeni developments. I found the president fully understanding and concerned about Yemeni security and independence. Basindwah added: I found President Hafiz al-Asad extremely concerned about Yemeni unity. No wonder, President Hafiz al-Asad is one of the pioneers of Arab unity. Yemeni unity is a national achievement and a pan-Arab gain.
