Language: English Article Type:BFN [EC Document No DOC/PESC/94/5: "Press Statement by the Presidency on Behalf of the European Union on the Crisis Situation in Yemen"] [Text] The European Union having repeatedly shown the interest of its member states toward the establishment of an everlasting stability in Yemen based on the principles of democracy and the respect of human rights, welcomes the signing on 18 January, of an agreement by all political parties, represented in the committee for national dialogue in the Republic of Yemen. This is an important step toward solving the serious political crisis in Yemen. The European Union hopes firmly that the agreement will be signed soon by Yemen's political leaders and implemented quickly thereby fostering a peaceful solution of political problems, and strengthening unity and democracy in the best interest of all of the Yemeny people.
