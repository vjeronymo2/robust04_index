921005 FT 05 OCT 92 / World News in Brief: El Al cargo flight explodes over Dutch capital An El Al 747 cargo aircraft exploded in mid-air last night over the Amsterdam suburb of Weesp. A block of flats was ablaze after being hit by falling wreckage which was said to be scattered over a wide area. The Tel Aviv-bound Boeing had just taken off from Amsterdam's Schiphol airport and tried to turn back before it exploded. The aircraft's crew of four were reported killed.
