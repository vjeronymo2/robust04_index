920929 FT 29 SEP 92 / International Company News: SCS Holding hit by currency trading SCS Holding, a Danish group trading in steel and building materials, suspended payments yesterday after its finance unit sustained a DKr700m (Dollars 122m) loss on currency trading. Two of the group's biggest creditors are Unibank, which said its total exposure was DKr300m to DKr500m, and Danske Bank, with DKr150m to DKr175m at stake. The two banks and SCS said the steel trading group has been reorganised, with the banks converting claims on the company into equity capital.
