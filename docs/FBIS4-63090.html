BFN [Text] Yerevan ("LRAGIR," "HAYASTANI HANRAPETUTYUN", "YERKIR" dailies), 05/19/94 -- The situation at the Armenian-Azeri borders has aggravated again. "PAST" news agency reports, the Azeri army opened mortar fire in the direction of the village of Yeraskhavan in the Araratskiy district. Firing started at 2030 from the territory of Nakhichevan, and lasted till 0100. The Armenian frontier forces returned the fire. According to a "PAST" correspondent in Araratskiy district, military operations at the Nakhichevan borders have been continuing in the afternoon. "HAYASTANI HANRAPETUTYUN" reports, Nakhichevan units opened fire again at 0730 am on 18 May. Several casualties are reported in Yeraskhavan. The Armenian frontier forces refrained from returning the fire this time. According to "YERKIR," quoting RA [Republic of Armenia] Defense Ministry press service, significant concentration of the Azeri army is reported at the Kapanskiy borders. The Azeri units opened machine-gun fire in the direction of the village of Koti in the Noyemberyanskiy district. No casualties or significant property damage are reported.
