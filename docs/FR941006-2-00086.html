L. 92&hyph;463, as amended), the National Science Foundation (NSF) announces the following meeting. Name: Advisory Panel for Neuroscience (1158). Date and Time: October 17&hyph;19, 1994; 9:00 a.m.&hyph;5:00 p.m. Place: National Science Foundation, room 370, 4201 Wilson Blvd., Arlington, VA. Type of Meeting: Part-Open. Contact Person: Dr. L. R.
