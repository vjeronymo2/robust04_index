BFN [Excerpts] Rome, June 28 (AFP) -- German Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel said here Tuesday [28 June] that he hoped the dispute over a successor to Jacques Delors as European Commission president would be resolved before an emergency EU summit on July 15. Kinkel, speaking after talks with Italian Foreign Minister Antonio Martino, said: "Clearly our goal is to find a solution before July 15." [passage omitted] Kinkel said he and Martino had discussed other candidates but he refused to say which ones. Kinkel said he would meet Wednesday in London with Major and Foreign Secretary Douglas Hurd. "After that, we will see," he said.
