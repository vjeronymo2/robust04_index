January 11, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski, lost his $30 win parlay when Farma Way ran last in the eighth after his front horse, Stephen's Sooner, won the seventh at $4.80 for $2. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $1,027.

Bob's bet today is a $40 win parlay from Tis Juliet in the sixth to Curio in the eighth.

Wednesday's Bankroll $1,057

Wednesday's Loss $30

Current Bankroll $1,027
