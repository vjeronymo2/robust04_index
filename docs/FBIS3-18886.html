Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Italy will have to select and appoint ombudsmen (mediators) for Bosnia in its capacity as the current president of the Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe. The decision was made by the executive committee of the CSCE. The mediators will be entrusted with the task of promoting respect for human and minority rights. Their mandate envisages, among other things, the reestablishment of CSCE observation missions in the regions of Kosovo, Vojvodina, and Sandzak.
