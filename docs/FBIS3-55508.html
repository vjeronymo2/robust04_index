Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Yelistratov] [Text] Cairo, 8 Jan -- Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad today received a letter from Boris Yeltsin, president of the Russian Federation. The MENA agency reports that the personal message from the Russian President to the leader of the Syrian Arab Republic "concerns the development of the peace process in the Middle East and questions of bilateral relations between Syria and Russia." The letter from Boris Yeltsin was handed over by the Russian Federation ambassador in Syria to Syrian Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar'. The Russian Federation, together with the United States, is one of the sponsors of the process for a peaceful settlemnt of the Middle East conflict between Israel and the Arab states. The Arab-Israeli talks began over two years ago at the Madrid conference, of which Russia was one of the main organizers.
