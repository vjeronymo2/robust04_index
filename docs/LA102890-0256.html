October 28, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

A PERSONAL CHECK

Because I do rely upon your movie-rating system, I was confused and disappointed by your having given a single check ("Desperation Time") to the worthy film "The Seventh Sign" (Oct. 3, Channel 5). Aside from a miscast Jesus (Christ) and a penchant for some obviousness, I found the movie to meaningfully bring together biblical prophecy with current events, and to portray what a personal act of faith would be, in a rather inspiring way.

Barry Klein, Los Angeles
