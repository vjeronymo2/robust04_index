940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / UK Company News: TBI sells factory unit for Pounds 4.1m TBI, the property investment and development company, has sold a 122,000 sq ft factory unit at Bridgend, Mid Glamorgan, for Pounds 4.1m cash to Royal Life Insurance. The asset value of the unit, which produces a rental income of Pounds 334,000 a year, was Pounds 2.7m at March 1 according to listing particulars.
