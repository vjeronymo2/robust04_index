BFN [Text] Tbilisi, April 5 (SNARK)--"The quadripartite agreement, signed in Moscow between Georgia and Abkhazia with the participation of the UN and Russia, promises swift entrance of the peacemaking forces into the conflict's zone and refuges' return to their places". Lorik Marshania, First Vice Prime [Minister] of Abkhazia informed of it SNARK today. He said peace in Abkhazia will set in only provided with unreserved execution of the reached agreement by the sides. Georgian Vice Prime [Minister] Tamaz Nadareishvili is of the same opinion. He told SNARK the agreement gives a good opportunity for 250 thousand refuges to return to their places. Meanwhile, a radical Georgian deputy Boris Kukubava consider the signed agreement to be an adroit using of the international authority of "capitulator" Shevardnadze to acknowledge de-facto Abkhazian state.
