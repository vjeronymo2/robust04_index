941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / Montreal crime ring cracked Montreal police have broken up a crime ring which has stolen hundreds of top-of-the-range Chrysler Cherokee four-wheel drive vehicles for shipment to Russia's black market. They recovered vehicles worth nearly CDollars 1m (Pounds 470,000) from a Montreal factory and rail yard and two more in Halifax, Nova Scotia. Five men were arrested. The ring moved the vehicles in containers, using false papers, through Germany and Finland to Russia.
