October 18, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

MEDICARE SURTAX

I am ashamed of the senior citizens who will cause the repeal of the new Medicare act. More importantly, I am ashamed of the Democrats who caved in to them. May they never need the 150 days per year in a nursing home at $89 per day now covered by Medicare. Try to buy private insurance that will cover that for $800 a year.

The poor in California will be covered by Medi-Cal. It will be you, the more affluent, whose savings will be decimated by these costs.

CARYL WADSWORTH

La Mirada
