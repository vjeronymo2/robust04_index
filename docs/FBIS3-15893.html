Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, March 10, IRNA -- In a meeting in Tashkent, capital of Uzbekistan today, Iran's special envoy Taherian and Iranian Ambassador Mohammad Musa Hashemi-Golpayegani met with Uzbek Deputy Foreign Minister Shahabuddin Siyamov and emphasized on settlement of the Afghan clashes and safeguarding of that country's territorial integrity. They also reviewed the latest developments in Afghanistan and the stances of the two countries towards the issue. Taherian in continuation of Tehran's efforts to settle the Afghanistan crisis is to leave Tashkent for Kabul to meet with the Afghan leaders. He is carrying a message from Iranian Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati for the Afghan leaders.
