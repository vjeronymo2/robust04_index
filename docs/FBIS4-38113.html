BFN [Excerpts] An extended round of talks was held today between the brother leader of the revolution [al-Qadhdhafi] and Egyptian President Husni Mubarak. The meeting was attended by brothers secretary for the General People's Committee, the secretary for the People's Bureau for Foreign Liaison and International Cooperation, and the ministerial delegation accompanying President Mubarak. The talks dealt with bilateral cooperation between the two brotherly countries, and Arab and international issues of mutual interest. [passage omitted] Egyptian President Husni Mubarak later left Surt today at the end of this visit to the Great Jamahiriyah. The brother leader of the revolution was at the head of those saying farewell to President Husni Mubarak at Khalij Surt's civilian airport. [passage omitted] President Mubarak made a statement to JANA's correspondent before departure. He said: My visits to the Great Jamahiriyah are continuous and have a positive effect on the Arab people in Egypt and Libya. Our meeting as brothers gives us the opportunity to exchange views in Arab, international, and bilateral issues. He indicated that all these topics were discussed during the meeting. Concerning the Great Jamahiriyah's absence from a meeting of Mediterranean basin countries due to be held in Cairo next week, President Husni Mubarak said that a restricted preparatory committee meeting will be held, and not all Mediterranean basin countries will participate. It will prepare a conference, draw up an agenda, and specify topics for discussion and when and who will take part.
