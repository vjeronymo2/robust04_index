BFN [Text] Cairo, 2 May (MENA)-- President Muhammad Husni Mubarak received a message from South Korean President Kim Yong-sam. Dr. Han Wan-sang, personal envoy of the South Korean president [name and title as received], gave the message to Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa during their meeting today. Dr. Han Wan-sang said he and Minister Musa discussed developments in the Middle East and strenthening bilateral cooperation. He hopes current efforts will help bring peace to the region. He remarked that it is a great honor for South Korea to attend the signing ceremony of the Palestinian-Israeli agreement, which is the beginning of a solid peace, not only for the region, but for the entire world. He added that it is high time to raise the level of diplomatic representation between South Korea and Egypt.
