920424 FT 24 APR 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Top quality bananas are this week's best fruit buy at 49-60p a lb (48-60p) reports the FFVIB. Other good buys include Cape Golden Delicious apples at 55-60p a lb (55-60p). Also look out for Spania strawberries at 60-75p a 1/2 lb (75-90p). Crisp, sweet tasting Dutch carrots are superb this week at 30-40p a lb (35-40p). Also good are Egyptian new potatoes at 25-28p a lb (25-30p). English and Jersey cauliflowers remain an excellent choice at 38-45p each (45-60p). Best homegrown salad buys this week are English Round lettuce at 20-25p a head (20-30p) and English tomatoes at 70-75p a lb (60-75p).
