BFN [Text] Moscow, April 12, IRNA -- The First Deputy of the Russian Duma (lower house of the state), Mikhail Mityukov, in his meeting with Iranian Ambassador Ne'matollah Izadi here Tuesday, emphasized the peaceful role of Iran and said the Tehran-Moscow interests in Caucasia and Central Asia, require the coopertaion of the two countries on ways of peace in the region and the world. The Iranian ambassador reviewed the necessity of cooperation between the parliaments of the two countries including the setting up of parliamentary friendship groups. The Russian official appreciated the role of Iran for peace and stability in the region. He said the parliament of Iran with its special characteristics, is taking an independent course. Izadi stressed expansion of relations with the Russian Federation and the importance of cooperation among Caspian Sea littoral states.
