May 27, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

FLAG BURNING

I just opened The Times to Page 2 and saw the flag-burning photograph from Portland, Ore., where President Bush paid a visit for a political fund raiser (May 22). I was angry and ashamed to see a young lady holding the flag of our United States while watching it burn.

No wonder our country is filled with crime and corruption when we allow this to happen. We are losing the fight for the respect of our wonderful United States by allowing flag burning.

How can anyone in his right mind, be it a U.S. citizen, alien or a visitor, look inthe mirror after commiting this despicable act?

Those who deliberately burn, mutilate or walk on our "red, white and blue" should be punished by law. I commend our government to take action now and stop this unlawful act.

DORIS B. ELLER

Los Angeles
