Russia PY1406032294 Sao Paulo O ESTADO DE SAO PAULO in Portuguese 9 Jun 94 p B12 PY1406032294 Sao Paulo O ESTADO DE SAO PAULO Portuguese BFN [Column by Alberto Tamer: "A Nuclear Plant for a Submarine"] [Text] No one has yet proposed it, but the possibility of exchanging the Angra-II nuclear power plant -- which Brazil purchased from Germany and which remains fully packed -- for a Russian nuclear submarine, is already being considered. Brazil is striving to enlarge its submarine fleet and is building a nuclear submarine, while Russia badly needs nuclear plants to replace its old reactors that run the risk of causing a tragedy at any time. They do not need submarines, which they have in excess and which are expensive to maintain. We do not need the plant, which we bought without needing it and without having the money to install it.
