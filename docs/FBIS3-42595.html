Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Decree of the Council of Ministers-Government of the Russian Federation of 23 December 1993, No. 1362, Moscow: "On Approval of the Statute on the Procedure for Exercising State Control Over the Utilization and Protection of Land in the Russian Federation"] [Text] In execution of the Edicts of the President of the Russian Federation of 27 October 1993, No. 1767, "On Regulation of Land Relations and the Development of Agrarian Reform in Russia," and of 16 December 1993, No. 2162, "On Strengthening State Control Over the Utilization and Protection of Land While Conducting Land Reform," the Council of Ministers-Government of the Russian Federation decrees: 1. The appended Statute on the procedure for exercising state control over the utilization and protection of land in the Russian Federation shall be approved. 2. The Decree of the President of the Russian Federation of 17 August 1992, No. 594, "On Approval of the Statute on the Procedure for Exercising State Control Over the Utilization and Protection of Land" (Collection of Documents of the President and Government of the Russian Federation, 1992, No. 8, p. 510) shall be declared invalid. [Signed] Chairman of the Council of Ministers-Government of the Russian Federation V. Chernomyrdin
