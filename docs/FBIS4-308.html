BFN [Statement issued by the NPRC public relations office; place and date not given] [Text] Here is a press release from the public relations office of the National Provisional Ruling Council [NPRC]. The recent rebel disturbances in the provinces have been contained and measures put in place to prevent any further disturbance. The public is now requested to be calm and to report any unusual activity in their areas. People are strongly advised to go about their normal activities and not to flee their homes. The NPRC government is fully aware of its responsibility to ensure the safety and security of citizens in the country.
