BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on economic activities monitored through 28 April. The Andean Development Corporation has agreed to a $38 million loan for Bolivia designed to co-finance an investment program with the Inter-American Development Bank and World Bank. (La Paz LA RAZON in Spanish 16 Apr 94 Economic Section p B3) Andres Saldias, La Paz chamber of exporters chairman, reported the trade balance with Argentina registered a deficit of $79 million in 1991, and $89 million in 1992. (La Paz HOY in Spanish 14 Apr 94 Political Section p 13) On 14 April, Bolivian Foreign Minister Antonio Aranibar Quiroga and Japanese Ambassador to Bolivia Shizuya Kato signed exchanges note in which the Japanese Government donated $10.2 million to Bolivia to implement urban projects in La Paz. (La Paz PRESENCIA in Spanish 15 Apr 94 p 7)
