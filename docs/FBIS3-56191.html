Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The foreign relations commission of Estonian parliament on Thursday presented the government a package of proposals on relations with NATO after looking through the documents of the Brussels meeting of the heads of government and state of NATO countries that was held on January 10-11. The commission proposed to express willingness to participate in "Partnership for Peace" programme, to sign the initial documents, to present Estonian partnership proposals to NATO and put partnership programme into force by diplomatic procedures, the package signed by chairman of the commission, Vello Saatpalu, said.
