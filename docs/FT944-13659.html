941026 FT 26 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Bell Cablemedia plans listing Cable operator Bell Cablemedia plans to list its shares in London in the first quarter of 1995, according to chief financial officer Mr William Anderson, Reuter reports. 'We have a very strong corporate commitment (to a UK listing),' Mr Anderson said. In July, Bell Cablemedia, which groups the UK cable interests of Bell Canada International, Cable and Wireless of the UK and Jones Intercable of the US, raised Dollars 479m (Pounds 303m) via a flotation on the Nasdaq stock exchange in the US. TeleWest, the largest UK cable operator, last week said it planned a flotation this year, which is expected to raise between Pounds 300m and Pounds 370m.
