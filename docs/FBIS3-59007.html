Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Eureka Supports Closer Cooperation With Israel"; as released by Brussels TEXTLINE database] [Text] The Norwegian Presidency of Eureka [European Research Initiative] has announced that closer cooperation with Israel (which has not asked to become a member) was decided during a meeting of the top-level group in December.
