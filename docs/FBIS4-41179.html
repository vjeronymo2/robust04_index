BFN [RIA report: "Primakov Has Met With `Arafat"] [Text] Late on Tuesday evening [21 June] Yevgeniy Primakov, chief of Russia's Foreign Intelligence Service, was received by Yasir `Arafat, chairman of the PLO Executive Committee. The Palestinian news agency WAFA, which is preparing an official announcement on the meeting, reported this yesterday. The Russian intelligence chief arrived in Tunis from Morocco, where he was received by King Hassan II.
