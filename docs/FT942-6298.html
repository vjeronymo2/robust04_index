940531 FT 31 MAY 94 / International Company News: Canadian TV group expands CanWest Global Communications, the Canadian TV broadcaster, has made its first foray outside the English-speaking world by buying 50 per cent of La Red, a Chilean TV network. CanWest will initially invest USDollars 8.4m and will operate the network. Its partner is Consorclo Periodistico de Chile SA (Copesa), a leading newspaper and magazine publisher. La Red, which is based in Santiago, began operations in 1991, but has yet to post profits.
