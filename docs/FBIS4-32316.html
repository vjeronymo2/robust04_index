BFN [Report by Semso (Luka) from Zenica] [Excerpt] Every new road is of strategic interest these days, both from the military and from the civilian point of view. That is why the members of the Zenica Engineering Battalion had cause for pride today, as they officially announced that a new road has been marked out, and it will be used for bringing in supplies more quickly and safely to our defense lines, for taking care of the wounded, and also for linking up the existing [word indistinct] between important points in Zenica and the neighboring municipalities. Midhat Ibrahimagic, commander of the Engineering Battalion, stressed today in front of his soldiers-builders that the marking out of this road -- 3,600 meters long, passing through inaccessible terrain and under the worst of weather conditions, in only 10 days -- was a real achievement. The order from Army General Staff has thus been carried out, and the next task on surfacing and [words indistinct] until the road is completed will most certainly be done within the deadline.
