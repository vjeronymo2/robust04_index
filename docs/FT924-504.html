921224 FT 24 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Guardian maintains recovery trend The USM-quoted Guardian Group, formerly Associated Energy Services, has continued its recovery with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 47,000 in the three months to September 30. First quarter turnover was Pounds 817,000 and earnings came to 0.216p. The company intends to declare a dividend at the earliest commercially prudent time.
