BFN [From the "First News" section] [Text] Official sources with first-hand access to security matters say that the bomb planted in Sayyidat al-Najat Church was brought from Israel and assembled at a Lebanese Forces' center in Lebanon. [A related item from the same "First News" section adds: "Ministerial quarters have expressed puzzlement over the insistence of U.S. Ambassador Mark Hambley in his private discussions that Israel had nothing to do with the bombing in Sayyidat al-Najat Church in Zuq Mikha'il, a position that conflicts with the evidence accumulated by the security organs and judiciary."]
