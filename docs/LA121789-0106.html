December 17, 1989, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

BODY OF MAN FOUND IN MISSION BEACH

An unidentified man was found shot to death in north Mission Beach Saturday morning, said a San Diego deputy coroner.

The man, in his thirties, was found on Santa Clara Point at 2 a.m. Saturday, said Deputy Coroner Cal Vine. There was no identification on his body, according to the coroner's office.

He was described as a 6-foot Caucasian, weighing 182 pounds, with razor-cut red-blonde hair, a mustache and short beard. He was wearing a herringbone jacket, a navy blue and maroon striped rugby shirt, black denim pants and blue running shoes. Officials are investigating.
