940930 FT 30 SEP 94 / World Trade News: Italian power job in Lebanon Italy's Ansaldo company has won a contract worth Dollars 720m to build two 450MW combined cycle power stations in Lebanon. The Italian government will finance the project as part of a soft loan to Lebanon. Five international consortia had submitted bids to build the two plants, one in the northern Baddawi region and the second at Zahrani in south Lebanon.
