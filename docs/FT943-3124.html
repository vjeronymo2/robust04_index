940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Castle Comms Castle Communications, the USM-quoted record and video publisher which last month was acquired by Alliance Entertainment of the US, reported pre-tax profits up from Pounds 1.53m to Pounds 2.13m for the year to June on turnover up from Pounds 33.4m to Pounds 34m. Earnings emerged at 19.8p (15.3p) per share. This year's final dividend is passed leaving the total for the year at 4.5p, against 8.5p.
