940402 FT 02 APR 94 / UK Company News: Fire surplus keeps Gaskell in the black Gaskell, the carpet manufacturer, reported a pre-tax profit of Pounds 403,000 for the 12 months to the end of December, against Pounds 131,000. Turnover was up 8 per cent at Pounds 30.5m (Pounds 28.3m) but there was an operating loss of Pounds 174,000 (Pounds 619,000 profit). This was offset by an exceptional credit of Pounds 913,000 representing profit realised on machinery destroyed by fire. The dividend for the year is maintained at 4.25p, with a final of 2.75p. Earnings per share came through at 5.3p (1.6p).
