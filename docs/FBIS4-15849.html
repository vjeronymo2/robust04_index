BFN [Text] The government of the self-styled Republic of Transdniestria [Dniester] supports the idea of setting up a Russian military base on the basis of the Russian 14th Army, Transdniestrian state secretary Valeriy Litskay told INTERFAX on Monday. However, there has been no official proposal from Moscow yet, he said. Litskay stressed that the base question and the future of Transdniestria could be discussed in parallel at bilateral talks with Moldova. The latest round of talks on 9 April gives the chance for settling the dispute, Litskay said. And he added that the emergence of a Russian Army base in Transdniestria could help resolve many controversial issues.
