October 14, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

HIGH SCHOOL FOOTBALL ROUNDUP

Moorpark 28, Bishop Diego 17 -- The Musketeers (4-2) scored three second-half touchdowns on fourth-down plays to overcome an early 14-0 deficit in a Tri-Valley League opener.

Moorpark quarterback Chuong Ho tossed a 17-yard scoring pass in the third quarter to Steve Duron to tie the score, 14-14, and ran for two fourth-quarter touchdowns. The Musketeers' first score came 8:49 into the second quarter on a six-yard run by Chris Peltonen. NORM WEBER
