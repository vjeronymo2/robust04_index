Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 18 (KCNA) -- Cement production is on the constant increase in Korea. The Sunchon cement complex produced 20,000 more tons of cement and the Haeju cement factory 5,600 more tons in the first 17 days of January than in the same days last year. The Sangwon cement complex is topping its daily assignments by 20 percent by keeping all the kilns at full-capacity operation. The February 8 cement complex, the Chonnaeri cement factory and other cement producers are honoring their daily assignments by organizing production efficiently. Production has been lifted to a high rate at more than 200 minor cement factories in different parts of the country. The Songsan, Sindok, Madong and other limestone mines, the Sunchon and Kangdong District coal mining complexes and other coal mines are supplying limestone and coal to cement factories in time.
