CSO [Editorial Report] Managua LA PRENSA in Spanish of 23 March 1994 on page 12 reports that the National Police is installing specialized computer equipment which will enable it to retrieve information from Interpol files. The equipment was made available through the good offices of Interpol liaison for Latin America Juan Carlos Antoniassi. Ministry of Government Deputy Minister Frank Cesar explained that the computers will enable authorities to review more efficiently the legal status of nationalized citizens.
