Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The European Justice Council has made no progress on drawing up common rules on extradition because most EU [European Union] states are reluctant to surrender their own nationals to another state. Discussion also stumbled over whether political offences should be a motive for refusing extradition, a view that was not shared by Greece. "All (EU) member states have a democratic regime and live in a democratic atmosphere...it's not normal, therefore, that you should invoke political reasons," Greek Justice Minister Yeoryios Kouvelakis told reporters in Brussels.
