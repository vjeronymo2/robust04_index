February 12, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

'POLICE OFFICER NOT CHARGED IN SHOOTING'

Re "Newport Beach Police Officer Not Charged in Shooting":

I, too, was saddened over the news of the Sept. 4 shooting of Sundaga Bryant on the Balboa Beach by Police Officer Derek Duncan. I remember that hot, emotionally charged night and I prayed for Sundaga after that. I am grateful to God that his wounds were not greater and that he will recover.

I feel, however, that I must protest his statement, "What gave this man the motive to come out there with a shotgun and blow me away," because I know that Officer Duncan did not shoot him intentionally. Having met and known Derek personally through the Neighborhood Watch Program, I can tell you that he is sensitive, shy and very committed to doing his job and helping people. So I pray for him also, that he will recover from the mental anguish of an accidental shooting.

And, I hope that both these young men will get on with their lives, wiser for the sad experience, but better equipped to help others with their growth because of it.

DAYNA PETTIT

Newport Beach
