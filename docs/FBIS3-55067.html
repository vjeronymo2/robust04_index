Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, January 5 (SNARK)--The rate of Armenian dram relating to USD was dropped down 1/3 times [as received]. So, today, "Armimpexbank" (the Foreign Economic Bank) purchased USD for 100 drams and sold for 105 drams. In the specialists' opinion the Armenian president's [word indistinct] about indexation of salaries, pensions and the resolution of the government to increase the prices for bread, transport, communal services influenced upon the rate. The official rate, set by Central Bank of Armenia, equals 75 drams for 1 USD.
