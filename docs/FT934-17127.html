931002 FT 02 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Losses deepen at Beauford PRE-TAX losses at Beauford, the maker of plant, equipment, machine tools and ceramic products for industry, rose from Pounds 2.07m to Pounds 3.06m in the first half of 1993. The result was struck after a Pounds 1.9m provision for losses on discontinued operations. Turnover fell to Pounds 16.2m (Pounds 21.3m) with turnover of continuing operations at Pounds 13.4m (Pounds 14.5m). Sir Trevor Holdsworth, chairman, said trading remained difficult with no evidence of any recovery in demand. Losses per share deepened to 3.75p (2.54p).
