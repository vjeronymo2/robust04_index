930719 FT 19 JUL 93 / Construction Contracts: Holiday village Center Parcs - Scottish & Newcastle's specialist leisure company - has awarded the major building contract for its new holiday village at Longleat to ALFRED McALPINE CONSTRUCTION. The contract, worth Pounds 22.2m, relates to the major part of the construction programme for the new village, which is on schedule to open in the summer of next year and will cost in total around Pounds 80m.
