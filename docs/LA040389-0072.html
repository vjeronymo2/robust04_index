April 3, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Some Mortgage Rates Dip: Fixed-rate home mortgages are averaging 11.19%, according to a national survey released by the Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corp. That was down from a rate of 11.22% last week. On one-year adjustable rate mortgages, lenders were asking an average initial rate of 9.31% this week, up from 9.3% the week before, Freddie Mac said.
