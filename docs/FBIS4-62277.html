BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS corespondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan 12 May TASS -- The Russian and CSCE-brokered cease-fire, which was to come into force in Nagorno-Karabakh last midnight was violated by Azerbaijani troops on Thursday [12 May] morning when they tried to break through Karabakh positions on the Adgam frontline. According to the Karabakh Army headquarters, the Azerbaijani forces were repelled and had to retreat to previous positions. The press department of the enclave reported, quoting military sources, that Azerbaijan in concentrating forces to the north of the mardakert region of Karabakh. However, it was quiet on the Armenian border overnight and in the morning. No incidents were registered there, TASS was told by the Armenian Defence Ministry.
