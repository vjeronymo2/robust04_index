BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 3 (KCNA) -- Yi Ki-taek, president of the South Korean opposition Democratic Party, in a statement April 1 denounced a reckless war blast of U.S. Secretary of Defense William Perry, according to a radio report from Seoul. Crying about fictitious "development of nuclear weapons" by North Korea, the U.S. secretary of Defense said they were ready for a war to check it. Yi condemned this as a blast designed to heighten the crisis on the Korean peninsula.
