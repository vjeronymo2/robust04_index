October 13, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

HATE COVERAGE, MASCOT, BUT LOVE BOB MILLER

The consistent superstar for the Kings over the years has been play-by-play man Bob Miller. He is the equal of Vin Scully in baseball and Chick Hearn in basketball, but now radio listeners are deprived of his voice.

Now that the Kings finally have a single radio station that can be heard throughout Southern California, they need to return their greatest asset to the listeners.

MARK S. KORNFELD

Crestline
