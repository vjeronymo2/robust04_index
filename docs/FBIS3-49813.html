Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Today, the Muslim army stepped up artillery and infantry attack on Novi Travnik and Busovaca in central Bosnia. The Croat Defense Council [HVO] has heavily defeated the Muslim army. Zdenko Vranjes reporting for Croatian Radio from Medjugorje. In the course of heavy fighting in the Sofe area near Vitez, HVO formations have managed to recapture trenches lost in recent fighting. As the HVO Vitez brigade reports, a large number of dead Muslim soldiers have been left behind in this area following three days of fierce fighting. The HVO will not let UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] enter this area and inspect the site unless the Muslim side enables the peace force to launch an inquiry in Krizancevo Selo and Buhine Kuce, the Vitez brigade has announced. The Muslim army today continued attacking other villages, too, near Vitez-Lazine and Buhine Kuce in particular. The Muslim army's advance has been checked in this area, too. [passage omitted]
