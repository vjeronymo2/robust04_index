August 25, 1989, Friday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPORTS ET CETERA: SOCCER

Tom Crane made the University of San Diego's only goal as the Toreros defeated visiting Brigham Young, 1-0. Midfielder Crane headed the ball into the net on an assist by Andrew Kummer in the first half.

SAN DIEGO STATE

The men's soccer and women's volleyball teams will play against alumni teams Saturday. The soccer game will be at 6 p.m. at the Aztec Bowl, the volleyball game at 7:30 p.m. at Peterson Gym.
