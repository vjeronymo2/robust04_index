941024 FT 24 OCT 94 / Company News This Week: Accor - Other Markets Accor: The French hotels and travel services group, is due to report a loss in its first-half results on Wednesday. The result will reflect the lack of exceptional gains from asset disposals. Analysts will be looking for detail about Accor's strategy for its Sofitel hotels following its loss to Forte for control of Air France's Meridien chain.
