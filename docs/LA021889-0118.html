February 18, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

CAMPAIGN FINANCES

The candidates who sought office in the November general election to represent Orange County in the state Senate and Assembly shared a common bond -- the pursuit of contributions to finance their campaigns. The chart below shows the distinct advantage incumbents have over their challengers in raising money. The figures represent money raised and spent in 1988 by the candidates for their campaigns. The candidate who won is listed first.
