940823 FT 23 AUG 94 / UK Company News: John Waddington The Pounds 42m rights issue by John Waddington, the packaging, printing and games company, has been taken up in respect of 21.7m shares, or 94.2 per cent. The 2-for-7 issue was made to fund the Fl 113.3m (Pounds 42.3m) purchase of Imca Beheer, the privately-owned Dutch cartons business.
