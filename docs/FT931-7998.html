930222 FT 22 FEB 93 / South Korean premier named South Korean President-elect Kim Young-sam yesterday named Mr Hwang In-sung, a top party official, to become prime minister when the new administration takes over on Thursday, Reuter reports from Seoul. Mr Hwang, 67, a former army general, has been the chief policy maker of the ruling Democratic Liberal party since early last year. He served as minister of transport and agriculture under President Chun Doo Hwanin in the 1980s.
