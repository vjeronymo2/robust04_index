BFN [Statement issued by "a responsible source in the Democratic Republic of Yemen"; place and date not given] [Excerpts] At a time when the DRY [Democratic Republic of Yemen] announces its agreement to arrangements for an urgent meeting between the two sides, the southern and northern sides, in the Egyptian capital, Cairo, in order that an agreement may be reached on a machinery to supervise the task of the cease-fire, and to establish it in accordance with the UN Security Council Resolution No. 924 observing any violation of it; and at a time when the DRY has informed Mr. Lakhdar Ibrahimi, the envoy of the UN secretary general, on the evening of 16 July about this, [passage omitted] the ruling military regime in Sanaa continued its flagrant challenge to the will of the Arab and international community. It embarked, since early this morning, on random and barbarous attacks with rockets and heavy, long-distance artillery against the peaceful residential areas in the suburbs of the city of Aden as well as against vital economic installations. This has resulted in the martyrdom of more than 20 persons, most of them children, women and the elderly, as well as a number of injuries. This was accompanied by attempts to advance on all fronts towards the capital, Aden, a matter which compelled our defensive Armed Forces to repulse decisively this new aggression of the forces belonging to the ruling military regime in Sanaa. They stopped them from advancing. Battles were still continuing while this communique was being prepared. [passage omitted]
