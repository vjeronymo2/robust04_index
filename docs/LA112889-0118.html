November 28, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

MAN ANSWERS DOOR, FATALLY SHOT IN HEAD

A man was fatally shot in the head Monday after he answered a knock on his apartment door, police said. Investigators have no suspects and have not established a motive.

Martin Renteria, 26, was pronounced dead at United Western Medical Center-Santa Ana about 20 minutes after the shooting, police spokeswoman Chris Schwartz said.

The shooting occurred about 4:55 p.m. at the Tustin Arms Apartments in the 15000 block of Tustin Village Way, where Renteria rented a two-bedroom apartment.

"One shot was fired," she said. "We believe it was from a handgun."
