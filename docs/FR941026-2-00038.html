The record will remain open for comments until December 6, 1994. All comments will be fully considered in reaching a decision to either withdraw the proposed determination or forward to EPA Headquarters a recommended determination to prohibit or restrict the use of the wetlands as a disposal site for construction of Nashua-Hudson Circumferential Highway.
