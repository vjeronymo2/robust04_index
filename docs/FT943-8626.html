940816 FT 16 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Horace Small 71% ahead to Pounds 0.95m Horace Small Apparel, the maker of fitted uniforms and corporate clothing formerly known as United Uniform Services, lifted pre-tax profits by 71 per cent from Pounds 553,000 to Pounds 945,000 in the half year to June 30. Turnover improved by 7 per cent to Pounds 35.7m (Pounds 33.5m). Earnings per share advanced to 3.7p (1.3p). However the company is not paying an interim dividend (1p).
