BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 26 -- President Le Duc Anh's first visit to Indonesia which begins on April 26 is of significant importance, marking a new milestone in the friendly and cooperative relations between Vietnam and Indonesia, and making active contributions to building Southeast Asia into a region of peace, stability, friendship and cooperation, says QUAN DOI NHAN DAN paper in a editorial today. The army paper expresses pleasure at the time- honoured friendship and cooperation between Vietnam and Indonesia. After lauding the all-sided achievements of the Indonesian people in national construction and defence, the paper says as chairman of the Non-Aligned Movement Indonesia is actively working for the unity and growth of Vietnam following the exchange of visit between delegations of their government and armies, especially those by President Suharto in November 1990, the first Vietnam visit by an Indonesia president in many decades, and Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet's visit to Indonesia in November 1991, President Le Duc Anh's visit to Indonesia this time will open a new page in the fine relationship between the two countries, the paper concludes.
