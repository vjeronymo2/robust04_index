January 20, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

49ER FANS GET LAST LAUGH AT COLUMNISTS

Now that the Rams have been eliminated, I anticipate that the fickle among the Los Angeles "faithful" will be raising a clamor that the Rams, Jim Everett and John Robinson are (A) losers and (B) chokers.

I would respond that the basic purpose of professional sports is to entertain. The Rams delivered the goods in terms of entertainment. The elation that I felt as a result of their regular-season victories and their two playoff wins surely outweighs the final disappointment. The moral? Sometimes it's just as good to win a lot of battles as it is to win the war. Thank you, Rams, for a memorable season.

CHRISTOPHER CHASE, Los Angeles
