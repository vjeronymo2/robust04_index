BFN [Unattributed article: "Fokker Lays Off 1,100"] [Excerpt] Yesterday the agonizing uncertainty over the layoffs announced at Christmas ended for Fokker production workers at Schiphol, Papendrecht, Ypenburg, and Woensdrecht. Today office and support staff will hear who will stay on. A total of 1,100 people will be laid off, with 350 forming part of a labor pool. Yesterday, the managers and workers at the different departments provided support to those who will be laid off. The company medical service, the trade unions, and work councils were ready to support the workers involved. [passage omitted]
