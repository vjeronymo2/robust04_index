930813 FT 13 AUG 93 / UK Company News: Ayrshire Metal shares jump Shares of Ayrshire Metal Products rose 33 per cent to 68p yesterday after the producer of cold rolled steel profiles announced a jump in pre-tax profits from Pounds 115,000 to Pounds 572,000 for the half year to June 30. The directors said the company had continued to monitor tightly its cost base and funding requirements. Turnover increased to Pounds 16.5m (Pounds 14.4m). Earnings were 3.8p (0.8p) per share. There is again no interim but directors hope to recommend a dividend for the year.
