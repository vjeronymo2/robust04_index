BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received 'Abdallah Ghanim, minister of justice in the Republic of Yemen, at al-Rawdah Palace at 1800 today. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar', Justice Minister Dr. 'Abdallah Tulbah, and Yemeni Charge d'Affaires 'Abdallah al-Iryani. Ghanim delivered a message to President al-Asad from President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council in the Republic of Yemen, on the situation in Yemen. Talks dealt with the regrettable developments in the Yemeni arena. The president reiterated Syria's previous and repeated call on the Yemeni brothers to stop fighting and solve their differences through dialogue.
