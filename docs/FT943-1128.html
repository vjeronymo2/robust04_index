940926 FT 26 SEP 94 / Arts (Openings): Paris The main autumn exhibition is devoted to Poussin, marking the 400th anniversary of his birth. It brings together 140 drawings and 100 paintings, including the two sets of Seven Sacraments and some of Poussin's finest paintings on classical and biblical themes. The show opens at the Grand Palais on Saturday, and will move to the Royal Academy in London early next year.
