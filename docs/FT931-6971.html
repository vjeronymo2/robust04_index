930226 FT 26 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Frank Usher advances 44% The conversion of record orders from the autumn/winter 1992 collections into sales helped Frank Usher, the USM-quoted women's clothing group, report interim pre-tax profits up 44 per cent on sales ahead 21 per cent. However, with the second half usually less profitable and the costs of the move to a new showroom falling due in the period, directors did not expect the results to match those of the first. Profits for the six months to November 30 were Pounds 843,000 (Pounds 585,000) on turnover of Pounds 9.23m (Pounds 7.61m). Earnings per share were 8.2p (5.7p). The interim dividend is 2.5p (2p).
