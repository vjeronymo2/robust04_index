BFN [Text] Kiev [No date as received] -- The Ukrainian Defense Ministry's press service reports that Army General Vitaliy Radetskyy, minister of defense, is on a working visit to the Odessa military district. In particular he learned about the state of affairs in the Bolgrad air division. Ukraine's defense minister familiarized himself with the training of personnel for the summer education period which is to begin 1 July. He paid considerable attention to the preparation of premises and the entire housing stock for winter. On the same day the Ukrainian defense minister visited the Donetsk military school where the final, 25th graduation will soon take place. The military academy which was formed on the basis of the military school will this year receive young men who dream of military service.
