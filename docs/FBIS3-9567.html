Language: Albanian Article Type:BFN [Text] The trial of the defendants Fatos Nano, Sokrat Plaka, Robert Gjini, and Hasan Hallkja continued today. At the end of the summation the jury decided to declare defendant Fatos Nano guilty and sentence him to 14 years imprisonment; to declare defendant Sokrat Plaka guilty and sentence him to five years imprisonment; to declare defendant Robert Gjini guilty and sentence him to four years imprisonment; to declare defendant Hasan Hallkja guilty and sentence him to two years imprisonment. The defendants also have the civil obligation to return to the Bank of Albania the amounts of money they are charged with taking. After withdrawing for the decision, the judicial body set 30 March 1994 at 0900 as a sufficient time for the four defendants to prepare their final statements and defense.
