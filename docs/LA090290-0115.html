September 2, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

RABBI HIER

I and many other survivors have worked closely with the Simon Wiesenthal Center since it opened in 1977, and I want to correct the statement by Rabbi Seidler-Feller that the center functions without survivors.

As a matter of fact, our organization, the American Congress of Jewish Survivors, last year honored Rabbi Hier and Rabbi Cooper with the emblem of our group for their work with survivors.

HENRY APPEL

Beverly Hills
