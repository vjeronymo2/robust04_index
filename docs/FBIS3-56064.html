Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to information from the Center for Public Relations of the Ukrainian Ministry of Internal Affairs, today in the Ukrainian Ministry of Internal Affairs, the Ukrainian minister of internal affairs and Israeli minister of police, energy, and infrastructure initialed an agreement on cooperation in the area of crime prevention and law breaking between the two countries. A news conference for journalists was then held. Answering journalists' questions, Israeli Minister Moshe Shahal stated that Israeli police pay special attention to crime prevention, which is a task for the entire state, not just the police. The use of firearms by criminals, as well as drug smuggling were also discussed.
