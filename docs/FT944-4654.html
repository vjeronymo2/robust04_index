941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Drew Scientific Drew Scientific, the medical technology company, incurred a pre-tax deficit of Pounds 515,000 for the six months to September 30, against a loss of Pounds 811,000 in the comparable period. Last year's losses - which amounted to Pounds 1.49m for the year - resulted from problems encountered with the Glycomat testing equipment which led to the product being temporarily suspended. Sales for the latest six months amounted to Pounds 431,000 (Pounds 420,000).
