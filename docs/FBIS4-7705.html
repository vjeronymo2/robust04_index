BFN [Excerpt] Throughout the day yesterday, Serb-Montenegrin aggressors continued their heavy offensive on the free territory of the Gorazde district. According to the press center of the Gorazde district, during the seventh day of the offensive on the town yesterday, the aggressor used over 350 types of the most destructive weapons and about 100 tanks and armored vehicles. Units of the Herzegovina, Sarajevo-Romanija, Drina, and Uzice Corps--as well as a large number of mercenaries from Romania, Russia, and neighboring municipalities--are taking part in the bloody onslaught on about 60,000 people, including refugees. The aggressor's fiercest attacks were directed against the southern section of the front, on the right bank of the Drina River, where--despite the defenders' fierce and heroic struggle--the chetniks succeeded in breaching our defense lines.
