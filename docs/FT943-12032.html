940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / UK Company News: European Assets Net asset value at European Assets Trust, which invests in small and medium-sized companies in continental Europe, fell 6 per cent in sterling terms from 351.2p to 329.2p per share during the six months to June 30. In guilder terms it fell 10 per cent, from Fl 10.07 to Fl 9.07. Net income for the half rose from Fl 2.83m to Fl 3.1m. The interim dividend is maintained at Fl 0.08.
