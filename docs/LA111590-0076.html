November 15, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

NEIGHBORHOOD EATERY: MORE EATERIES:

Salerno, 220 Beach St., Laguna Beach. Open 11:30 a.m. to 10 p.m. Wednesdays through Mondays. (714) 497-2600.

Owner Tony Manzi's accent is nearly as thick as his meat sauce, and his menu is just as hearty. Try the gnocchi, baked mostaccoli with Italian sausage or, if you happen to be dining with understanding friends, the fettuccine with garlic and oil. APRIL JACKSON
