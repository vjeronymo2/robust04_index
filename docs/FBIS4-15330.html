BFN [Text] A delegation of the International Immigration Organization, which is on a visit to our Republic, has visited the Border Troops General Department within the Cabinet of Ministers. In the course of negotiations, the two sides discussed the issue of granting technical assistance, as well as help in training Border Troops soldiers. It was agreed that the organization's experts, who are to arrive as early as May, will examine the state of affairs within the Belarusian Border Troops and determine the amount of aid needed.
