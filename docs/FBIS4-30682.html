BFN [Text] The Lang Son provincial electric power service recently started the construction of three 35-kilovolt power lines: the first one spans from Quoc Khanh town to Trang Dinh district, the second from Cao Loc town to the border district of Ba Son, and the third for new economic zones in the province. Lang Son has, thus far, supplied electricity to 11 districts and towns through the national grid. The three new power lines is aimed at supplying electricity for the development of rural area and zones inhabited by ethnic minorities.
