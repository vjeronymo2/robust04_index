Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "Presidential Bulletin" No. 11 feature] [Text] Monday evening President Boris Yeltsin had a telephone conversation with Georgian leader Eduard Shevardnadze, Shevardnadze's press secretary, Zaza Kandelaki, told Interfax. According to him, the two leaders confirmed the need for holding a meeting in Tbilisi in early February (Interfax's note: about the 3rd or 4th of February).
