930528 FT 28 MAY 93 / 420 at Swan Hunter lose jobs SOME 420 Swan Hunter employees, many of whom spent their entire working lives at the Tyneside shipbuilder, were made compulsorily redundant yesterday and left the yard without any immediate pay-off. Because the company is in receivership, they must apply to the Department of Employment for their pay-offs under the state redundancy fund, which pays a maximum Pounds 6,150.
