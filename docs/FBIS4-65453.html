BFN [Text] The 17 November organization hit the IBM offices eight minutes after midnight with two 2.6-inch-caliber antitank rockets. The offices are on the corner of Filellinon and Dhedhalos Streets near Sintagma Square in the capital's center. The explosions caused only material damage. A homemade launcher, consisting of a tripod and a metallic pipe, was found in a building across from the offices. A few minutes before the attack, an unidentified person telephoned SKY radio warning about the strike and claiming responsibility in the name of 17 November.
