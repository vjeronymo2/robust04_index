931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Fleming Income lifts net asset value Fleming Income & Capital Investment Trust had a net asset value of 85.9p per income share at September 30, up from 77.5p at the March year-end and 64p at end-September 1992. Available profits for the trust's first half emerged at Pounds 2.53m (Pounds 3.13m) for earnings of 2.74p (3.4p) per share. As already announced, a second interim dividend of 1p makes a total to date of 2p.
