Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow January 31 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin has issued on Monday [31 January] a decree appointing Boris Kuznetsov as his representative in the region of Voronezh. Yeltsin also released Aleksandr Minzhurenko from the position of his representative in the region of Omsk in association with Minzhurenko's election to the State Duma, according to the presidential press-service.
