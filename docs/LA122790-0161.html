December 27, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

DOWNEY Y SEEKS BASKETBALL COACHES

The Downey Family YMCA is accepting applications for volunteers to work as coaches and officials for its 1991 youth basketball season.

The season will run from Jan. 5 to March 16.

Training is available and experience is not necessary, but preferred. Applicants should enjoy working with children ages 5 to 12. Teams practice one night per week and games are on Saturdays.

For more information or to apply, contact the Downey Family YMCA at 11531 Downey Ave., or call 862-4201.
