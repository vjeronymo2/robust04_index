921109 FT 09 NOV 92 / UK Company News: S Korea approves Daimler holding SOUTH Korea's Ministry of Finance has approved the purchase by Daimler-Benz of a 5 per cent shareholding in Ssangyong Motor for Dollars 33.9m, writes John Burton in Seoul. Daimler-Benz is the first European vehicle producer to purchase a stake in a South Korean motor company. Ssangyong is South Korea's fifth largest motor company and a producer of trucks, buses and jeeps.
