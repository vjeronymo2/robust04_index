BFN [Text] Baku (May 25) TURAN--Yesterday the employees of the Ministry of Internal Affairs arrested the former Commandant of Internal forces, Fakhmin Gadzhiyev, in Baku. Since June 1993 and until now Fakhmin Gadzhiyev had been hiding in Turkey. He was accused according to Article 225 of the Azerbaijani Criminal Code for using his duties for mercenary motives and military crimes. The Procurator's Office of the republic has begun investigation into this case.
