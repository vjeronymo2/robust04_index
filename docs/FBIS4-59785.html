BFN [Text] Muscat, 16 May (WAKH) -- Muhammad Salim Basindwah, Yemeni foreign minister and President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih's envoy, arrived in Muscat this evening [16 May]. Basindwah said in an arrival statement he will convey a message to Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id of Oman from President Salih on the situation in Yemen.
