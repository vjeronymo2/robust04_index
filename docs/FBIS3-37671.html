Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev agreed with Russian President Boris Yeltsin on the necessity for political settlement of the Bosnian conflict which might be worked out during a special summit between the leaders of Russia, the U.S., France, Great Britain and Germany. Pavel Grachev held a press conference after his telephone conversation with U.S. Secretary of Defense, William Perry, on Monday. According to diplomatic sources, the heads of the diplomatic missions of the U.S., France, Great Britain, Germany and some other states were discussing the possible summit on settlement of the crisis in Bosnia. This summit might take place in a European capital in the nearest future, the same sources said.
