October 30, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: COMING ATTRACTIONS;

NICKELODEON IN GLENDALE:

Nick at Nite's traveling TV Land Mall Tour will set up shop at the Glendale Galleria the weekend of Nov. 10-12. The Tour features memorabilia from the old-time TV shows (such as "Green Acres," "Mr. Ed," "Laugh-In," "Car 54 Where Are You?") that air at night on Nickelodeon cable. There also will be celebrity appearances, interactive displays to challenge TV trivia buffs and sing-a-longs to TV theme songs. SHAUNA SNOW and CLAUDIA PUIG and ALEENE MacMINN
