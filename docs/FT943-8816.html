940815 FT 15 AUG 94 / Emerging Markets: News round-up - Kuala Lumpur Thirty-five Japanese companies operating in Malaysia want to make public offers of their shares, according to a survey by the Japanese Chamber of Trade and Industry in Malaysia. The survey of 150 companies conducted in March and April this year found that two companies were making preparations for a public offer, eight said that they had scheduled an offering, and 25 want to go public in the future.
