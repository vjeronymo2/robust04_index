Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Prague, Jan. 12 -- Polish and Czech Foreign Ministers Andrzej Olechowski and Josef Zieleniec stated at their meeting in Prague on Wednesday that the Visegrad Group continues to exist and that certain "misunderstandings" which occurred recently over the relations with NATO should have no effect on the Polish-Czech relations. Minister Josef Zieleniec stressed that the strategic interest of the Czech Republic is to see Poland within the Western European structures alongside with the Czech Republic itself and other countries of the region. Director of the Information Department in the Foreign Ministry Wladyslaw Klaczynski said that Polish-Czech consultations on building a European security structure would start in the near future.
