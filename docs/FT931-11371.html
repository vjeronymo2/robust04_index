930203 FT 03 FEB 93 / Rise in gold and currency reserves THE UK gold and foreign currency reserves rose an underlying Dollars 38m last month. The Treasury said the overall level of official reserves rose by Dollars 902m, taking reserves at the end of the month to Dollars 42.556bn compared with Dollars 41.654bn at the end of December. The dollar value of the UK's Ecu-denominated revolving credit facility, agreed shortly before the pound left the exchange rate mechanism, rose by Dollars 2m because of exchange rate changes.
