Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over still of President Demirel] [Text] President Suleyman Demirel has called Palestinian Head of State Yasir 'Arafat on the telephone to express his grief over the massacre and to extend his condolences. According to a statement issued by the Presidential Press Center, President Demirel expressed the grief of the Turkish nation that such a massacre should take place in the holy month of Ramadan.
