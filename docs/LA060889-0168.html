June 8, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: SANTA ANA;

'CERO DOLARES LA HORA' TO END ITS RUN SUNDAY

Teatro Cometa, a community-based theater group from Fullerton, will give its last performance of a play about labor exploitation Sunday at 4:30 p.m. at Carr Intermediate School.

The short play, entitled "Cero Dolares La Hora: Ya Basta" (Zero Dollars an Hour: Enough Already), was commissioned by the Orange County Human Relations Commission. Pacific Bell is sponsoring 10 performances to English-as-a-second-language classes in the county.

Sunday's performance will benefit members of the Hermandad Mexicana Nacional, an immigrant-rights and services group. Carr Intermediate School is at 2120 W. Edinger Ave.
