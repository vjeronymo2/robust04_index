BFN [Text] Cairo, 8 Jun (MENA) -- Dr. Nabil Sha'th, political adviser to Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat and minister of planning and international cooperation in the Palestinian National Authority Council, left Cairo for Paris this morning. While in Paris, Dr. Sha'th will take part in the meeting of donor states which will discuss assistance for the Palestinian police and self-government authority. Ahmad Quray', alias Abu-'Ala', head of the PLO Economic Department, is leading the Palestinian delegation to the meeting.
