940901 FT 01 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Ryan losses deepen to Pounds 7.35m Ryan Group, one of the companies expected to bid for British Coal assets later this month, incurred pre-tax losses of Pounds 7.35m in the year to December 31, compared with a deficit of Pounds 3.31m in 1992, writes Michael Smith. Operating profits were Pounds 3.17m (Pounds 3.86m) on turnover of Pounds 113m (Pounds 111m). Net interest payments fell from Pounds 5.9m to Pounds 5.06m, but losses on closure of discontinued operations amounted to Pounds 7.07m (Pounds 366,000).
