Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN ["In the Republic of Georgia Cabinet of Ministers"] [Text] On the Establishment of a Republic of Georgia Trade and Commercial Representation in Bulgaria In order to extend trade and economic relations between the Republic of Georgia and Bulgaria, by a Republic of Georgia Cabinet of Ministers decree, a Republic of Georgia trade and commercial representation has been established in Bulgaria (Sofia).

On the Establishment of a Republic of Georgia Trade and Commercial Representation in the Republic of Turkey

In order to extend trade and commercial relations between the Republic of Georgia and the Republic of Turkey, by a decree of the Republic of Georgia Cabinet of Ministers, a Republic of Georgia trade and commercial representation is to be established in the Republic of Turkey.
