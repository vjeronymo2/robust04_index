January 16, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

HOT STOVE LEAGUE: The Rams (C1) are history and the weather (A1) is lousy, so let's talk baseball. . . . With only 32 days left until spring training, some Angels were working out Monday at Anaheim Stadium, but more were practicing their swings at Western Hills Country Club in Chino, site of pitcher Bert Blyleven's celebrity tournament. "The Rams had kind of the same season we had with Oakland," Blyleven said, recalling the Angels' bid to beat the A's. "But I think when you get that close, when you get a taste of it, it can carry you all the way the next year." Steve Emmons and Mark I. Pinsky
