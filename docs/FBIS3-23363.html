Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The security services have issued a statement, saying that the security forces have been able to kill 9 terrorists and arrest another 14 between Tuesday and Wednesday. The statement by the security services indicates that a quantity of weapons was also recovered. This operation took place in Blida, Jijel, Boumerdes, and Tipaza. The same source also states that Zaouiet Sheikh Rabah in Benhara municipality, Jelfa wilaya, was the target of criminal arson perpetrated by a terrorist group.
