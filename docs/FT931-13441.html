930123 FT 23 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Shield back in black with Pounds 0.05m SHIELD GROUP, estate agency and property company, has returned to profit in the six months ended September 30 1992 having wound down its property development and investment activities. Pre-tax profit came to Pounds 54,000, but this includes Pounds 45,000 of unrealised foreign currency gains. The loss for the comparative period was Pounds 1.34m. Turnover was Pounds 948,000 (Pounds 5.81m). Losses per share came to 2.7p (17.2p). The directors said that Stickley & Kent (Risk Management Unit) was trading profitably and it was intended to expand this activity this year. Shield has acquired 1 per cent of the capital of National Home Loans at 1.25p per share and intends to increase its investment should further opportunities arise.
