BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] The Council of State decree on the composition the new Council of Ministers points out that two Council of Ministers vice presidents have been released from their posts. They are Ramiro Valdez Menendez and Joel Domenech Benitez. The remaining ministries [and institutes] will retain all attributions and functions lawfully assigned to them. These ministries are: Sugar Industry, Agriculture, Foreign Trade, Domestic Trade, Communications, Culture, Education, Higher Education, Revolutionary Armed Forces, Food Industry, Construction Materials Industry, Fish Industry, Interior, Justice, Foreign Relations, Public Health, and Transportation, as well as the Cuban Civil Aeronautics Institute; Cuban Radio and Television Institute; the Cuban Sports, Physical Education, and Recreation Institute; the Cuban Hydraulic Resources Institute; and the Cuban State Reserves Institute.
