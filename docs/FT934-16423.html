931006 FT 06 OCT 93 / International Company News: BMW sales up by 19% in US BMW, the German car manufacturer which has announced that it is to build a manufacturing plant in South Carolina, reported that US sales advanced by 19 per cent in September, compared with a year ago, to 7,018 units from 5,883, Reuter reports from Frankfurt. In the first nine months of this year, sales climbed to 57,763 vehicles from 49,203 in the year-earlier period.
