941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / Left-wingers demonstrate at celebrations to mark anniversary of German reunification Extreme left-wing demonstrators failed to disrupt celebrations yesterday of the fourth anniversary of German unification, writes Quentin Peel. Police said 250 were arrested and widespread damage caused to shops in the centre of Bremen, where the main festivities took place. Inside the city's congress centre, political leaders heard President Roman Herzog praise the east Germans for 'the first successful democratic revolution in our history.'
