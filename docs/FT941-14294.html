940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: Sir Matt Busby dies at 84 Sir Matt Busby, president of Manchester United football club and manager and general manager for nearly 26 years, died, aged 84. In 1958, he was injured in the Munich air crash which killed eight of his players but his team went on to become, in 1968, the first English side to win the European Cup. He also led United to five League Championship and two FA Cup wins.
