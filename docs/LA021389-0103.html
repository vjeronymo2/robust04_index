February 13, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST: LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

COUNTYWIDE;

COMMUNICATION CLASS OFFERED FOR COUPLES

A communication workshop for couples will be held Wednesday at Golden West College.

The program will include advice on increasing fulfillment and resolving issues harmoniously. It is open to couples who are dating, engaged or married. The presenters are a married couple, Bob and Joyce Simpson, who are also professional marriage counselors.

The fee is $50 per person. Additional information can be obtained by calling the Community Services Office at Golden West College, 891-3991.
