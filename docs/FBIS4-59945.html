BFN [Text] Two missile frigates have been repaired and today joined the naval fleet of the Islamic Republic of Iran. The repair work on the frigates Shamshir [sword] and Zubin [javelin] was carried out by experts at factories belonging to the First Naval Zone of Iran. Also today, a reconditioned hovercraft joined Iran's First Naval Zone Armed Forces. To recondition a hovercraft, with less than 75 percent of the parts needing repair or replacement, would cost 4 million pounds [as heard] or its equivalent of hard currency in a Western country such as Britain.
