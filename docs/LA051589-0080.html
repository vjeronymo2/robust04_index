May 15, 1989, Monday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: ART

An exchange exhibit of works by emerging Los Angeles and Czech artists has been organized as a way to bridge cultural barriers. Works by 16 Los Angeles artists will be shown this August in Prague, Czechoslovakia, while works by 10 Czech artists will be displayed at the Otis Parsons Art Institute here next year. The idea of the exhibit is "to promote art and the exchange of ideas and culture between our two countries and around the world," explains participating artist and exhibit organizer Barbara Benish.
