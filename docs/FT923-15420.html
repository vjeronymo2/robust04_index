920702 FT 02 JUL 92 / World News in Brief: Tennis aces knocked out Pete Sampras of the US defeated defending champion Michael Stich of Germany at the Wimbledon tennis championships. In another quarter-final, Stefan Edberg of Sweden was beaten in five sets by Goran Ivanisevic of Croatia. In the women's singles quarter-final, Gabriela Sabatini of Argentina beat Jennifer Capriati of the US. Rain halted the day's other matches.
