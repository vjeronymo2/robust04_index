April 2, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

SITE OF EMERY AIRFREIGHT SELLS FOR $4 MILLION-PLUS

A 40,488-square-foot industrial facility that houses Emery Airfreight Corp. at 2345 Alaska Ave. in El Segundo has been sold to investor Ronald A. Simms by Alaska Properties Inc.

The transaction is valued at more than $4 million.
