921017 FT 17 OCT 92 / Markets: Bad week for smaller companies - At a Glance It was another bad week for small company shares. The Hoare Govett Index (capital gains version) fell 0.5 per cent from 1059.73 to 1054.60 over the seven days to October 15, while the County Small Companies Index fell 0.6 per cent from 820.77 to 815.94 over the same period.
