941119 FT 19 NOV 94 / World Stock Markets: South Africa Industrial shares extended early gains to close firmer while golds posted declines as sentiment worsened ahead of the weekend on a fall in the price of bullion and a firmer financial rand. The overall index lost 16.3 to 5,925.3, industrials rose 38.6 to 6,950.4 and golds fell 51 to 2,138.3.
