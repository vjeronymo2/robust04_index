September 22, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

QUIKSILVER SWITCHES TO DELOITTE & TOUCHE

Quiksilver Inc., the Costa Mesa-based sportswear manufacturer, said in a filing with the Securities and Exchange Commission that it has dismissed Kenneth Leventhal & Co. as its certified public accountant and hired Deloitte & Touche.

The company said the reason for the change is Deloitte & Touche's offer of worldwide retail consulting services.
