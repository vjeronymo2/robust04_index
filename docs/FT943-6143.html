940831 FT 31 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Pounds 15.4m Dutch buy for Bowater Bowater is set to acquire Van Gelder Coatings, a subsidiary of Gelderse Papiergroep of the Netherlands, for Fl 41.5m (Pounds 15.4m). Mr Michael Hartnall, finance director, said Van Gelder, which makes specialist paper or film for high value release liners and heat seal products, would be earnings enhancing immediately. In the last accounting period its turnover was about Fl 40m. Mr Hartnall said the market for release liners - discardable protectors for adhesive surfaces - was worth about Pounds 1bn in the US and Europe.
