941110 FT 10 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Villiers cuts loss to Pounds 1.54m Villiers Group, the USM-traded specialist engineering company, reported pre-tax losses reduced from Pounds 2.83m to Pounds 1.54m for the year to July 31. Turnover was 45 per cent ahead at Pounds 2.72m, compared with a restated Pounds 1.87m. Acquisitions contributed Pounds 1.21m to sales. Operating profits totalled Pounds 11,000 (Pounds 338,000 loss). The company, which is US-based, said its continuing operations had been profitable since January. Following the disposal in June of its Starks Field oil operations for Dollars 2.5m (Pounds 1.52m), its operating accounts consisted entirely of engineering business. The second half had made a positive overall contribution, reducing the Pounds 1.86m loss reported at interim stage. Losses per share improved from an adjusted 5.48p to 1.49p.
