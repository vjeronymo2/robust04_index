940628 FT 28 JUN 94 / Leisure worker awarded Pounds 700,000 A Center Parcs worker crippled for life when he dived into a wave pool and hit his head on the bottom is to receive Pounds 700,000 damages. Mr Edward Rayner, 24, was taking a break from his job as a barman in the Pleasure Dome at the company's Elvedon Forest Holiday Village near Thetford, Norfolk, when the accident happened in 1990. Center Parcs had denied liability, but the case was settled on payment of the damages plus costs.
