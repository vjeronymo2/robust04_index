BFN [Text] Geneva, May 20 (AFP) -- Hundreds of thousands of people are fleeing central Rwanda as heavy fighting in the capital Kigali and elsewhere in the central African nation causes a new exodus of refugees, an International Committee of the Red Cross [ICRC] spokesman said Friday. The ICRC appealed for UN peacekeepers to be sent in as quickly as possible and for the establishment of humanitarian corridors through the country. Up to half a million Rwandans are believed to have died in ethnic and political killings between members of the Hutu and Tutsi people that broke out in early April.
