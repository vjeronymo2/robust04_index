Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] In the provincial Scientific and Technological Activity Center on the afternoon of 5 February, the provincial science and technology association, the provincial labor department, and the provincial planning commission joinly held a tea party for people from scientific and technological circles in Xining. Provincial party and government leaders -- including Yin Kesheng, Yao Xiangcheng, Yang Maojia, La Bingli, and Bainma Dandzin -- attended. [passage omitted] On behalf of the provincial science and technology association, the provincial labor department, and the provincial science and technology commission, Yin Yongzhang, chairman of the provincial science and technology association, expressed Lunar New Year greetings to the participants, saying: Last year, the province proceeded with scientific and technological work along the strategy of economic development, deepened scientific and technological structural reform, and steadily invigorated Qinghai by means of science and technology, thus stimulating the province's economic construction. The province unfolded the 3341 Scientific and Technological Experimental Project, the Torch Program, the Spark Program, the Good Harvest Program, and the Prairie Fire Program -- all of which directly benefited economic construction, played a positive role in popularizing the use of scientific and technological achievements, and made significant contributions to the province's scientific and technological development. Yin Yongzhang sincerely thanked scientific and technological personnel for their creative efforts and great contributions. Provincial party Secretary Yin Kesheng also made a speech. On behalf of the provincial party committee and government, he expressed Lunar New Year greetings to scientific and technological personnel throughout the province, saying: Reviewing the past and looking to the future, we feel deeply the importance of scientific and technological work. Scientific and technological personnel in Qinghai have made great contributions to economic construction and social development. Our economy needs to be developed, and our science and technology need to be invigorated. As Qinghai is economically and culturally backward, the role of science and technology is particularly important. To narrow the gap between technologically advanced regions and ourselves, we must, in many aspects, rely on advanced science and technology, develop and expand the ranks of scientific and technological personnel, and improve the quality of the entire laborers. In the new situation of establishing the socialist market economic structure, science and technology also face a test. [passage omitted]
