Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [From the press review] [Text] Page four of QUAN DOI NHAN DAN carries an interview with Thai Foreign Minister Prasong Sunsiri. Regarding the assessment of Vietnam's role and position in the region, the Thai foreign minister said: Vietnam is a large country in the region, and therefore the fine relations between Vietnam and the ASEAN member countries in general -- and with each member-country of ASEAN in particular -- will be a positive element in maintaining security for the region and the world alike. We highly value the role and position of Vietnam.
