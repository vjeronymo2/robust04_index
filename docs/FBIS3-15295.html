Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow March 4 TASS -- There was a leak of a heat-transfer agent in the first circuit of the reactor set of power unit No. 2 of the Kola nuclear power station at 20.52 [1752 GMT] on March 3. The unit had been shut down for repairs. The leak was rectified at 21.20 [1820 GMT] , the press centre of the Rosenergoatom Company reported. The radiological safety setting at the station and on the surrounding territory is normal. According to the Ines international scale, the incident was rated at zero level.
