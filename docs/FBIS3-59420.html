Language: Slovenian Article Type:CSO [Article by D.H.: "December Inflation Rate 1.5 Percent, Yearly Inflation Rate 22.9 Percent"] [Text] Ljubljana -- The Statistical Institute reported yesterday the latest data on inflation and retail price growth. For the month of December, retail prices and inflation rose 1.5 percent, with inflation reaching 22.9 percent for 1993. In December, the cost of living increased 1.8 percent, resulting in an increase of 22.8 percent for 1993.
