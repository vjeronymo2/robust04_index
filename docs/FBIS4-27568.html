BFN [Text] Yun Ho-chin, counselor of the North Korean embassy in Vienna and chief delegate to the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA], said in an interview with REUTER on 2 June that the IAEA is making an unreasonable demand for inspections and, as a result, North Korea will definitely be compelled to withdraw [taltoerul kangyobatchi annulsu opta] from the Nuclear Nonproliferation Treaty [NPT]. Stressing that North Korea's intent is revealing its nuclear transparency, Counselor Yun said that he is scheduled to have a discussion with IAEA officials this afternoon to defuse the nuclear crisis.
