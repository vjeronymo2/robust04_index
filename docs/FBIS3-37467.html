Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Sergey Sidorov: "Serbs Urge Moscow To Bring Investigation Into Explosion in Sarajevo to a Conclusion"] [Text] Belgrade -- Unless the truth is established on this occasion, the Muslim side will commit further crimes, deceiving the world community and contributing to the escalation of war in the region. So said Radovan Karadzic, president of the Serbian Republic (Bosnia-Herzegovina), in a written appeal to Vitaliy Churkin, the Russian president's representative at the talks on Yugoslavia. The leader of the Bosnian Serbs again urged the Russian leadership to ensure the establishment of an international independent commission of experts to investigate the explosion in Sarajevo which cost the lives of dozens of innocent people. Karadzic stressed that in the search for the truth it is necessary to carry out the political analysis Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin mentioned. Such an analysis will show which of the warring sides in Bosnia has derived political benefit from the crime.
