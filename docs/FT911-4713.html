910418 FT 18 APR 91 / UK Company News: Storm Group on target Storm Group, owner of the Shoe People cartoon characters, returned profits of Pounds 369,000 pre-tax for the period October 17 1989 to December 31 1990. Although that was down on the Pounds 400,000 forecast at the time of the company's USM flotation in December 1989, the figures included losses of businesses which were acquired and started up in the final quarter of 1990 which did not form part of the forecast. Excluding the two companies, profits before tax for the year to December 31 amounted to Pounds 402,000. Earnings per share of 0.68p compared with a 0.62p forecast. Turnover totalled Pounds 985,000 and profits included interest income of Pounds 253,000. At the year-end Storm had net cash of Pounds 1.4m.
