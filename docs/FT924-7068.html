921118 FT 18 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Whitegate Leisure The hot weather in May and June was blamed for increased losses at Whitegate Leisure, the USM-quoted ten pin bowling, discotheques and nursing homes group. In the 26 weeks to June 28 pre-tax losses were Pounds 1.41m, against profits of Pounds 54,000. Turnover was Pounds 10m (Pounds 12.2m). Operating profit was lower at Pounds 669,000 (Pounds 2.15m) and interest charges were slightly lower at Pounds 1.99m (Pounds 2.1m). Losses per share came out at 4.8p (0.1p earnings).
