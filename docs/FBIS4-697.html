BFN [Text] Pretoria April 13 SAPA -- Finance and Trade and Industry Minister Derek Keys departed Wednesday [13 April] evening for Marrakech, Morocco to formally sign the Uruguay Round of the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade. Mr Keys is accompanied by African National Congress National Executive Committee member Professor Kadar Asmal, of the organisation's international affairs wing, and officials from the Trade and Industry Department. A Finance Ministry statement said Mr Keys was expected to meet with senior officials and government ministers from other GATT member countries. He would sign GATT's Uruguay Round treaty on Friday at the formal ceremony.
