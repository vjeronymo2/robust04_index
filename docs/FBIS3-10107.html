Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Santo Domingo, 1 Mar (EFE) -- Today, authorities said three persons died and another person suffered serious burns following a fire at a house that operated as a gasoline warehouse in Dajabon City, bordering Haiti. The deceased, Dominican brothers Ramon and Ovidio Taveras, and Haitian Leonel Solis, 25, were burned to ashes while Osiris Hernandez, 27, was admitted to the regional hospital Jose Maria Cabral y Baez in Santiago City with serious burns. The fire occurred at one of the many family homes that have been converted to store fuel which is eventually sold in Haiti. Many residents in the border city of Dajabon earn their living selling gasoline by taking advantage of the current embargo against neighboring Haiti.
