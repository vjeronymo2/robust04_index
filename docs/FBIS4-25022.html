BFN [Text] Beijing, June 6 (XINHUA) -- A China Northwest Airlines passenger plane crashed early today near Xian, capital city of Shaanxi Province. An official from the Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC) revealed that the Tu-154 passenger plane, on flight WH-2303 from Xian to Guangzhou, took off from Xianyang Airport at 8:12 a.m. [0012 GMT] and crashed at 8:20 a.m. [0020 GMT] near Mingdu township of Chang'an county in the same province. The official said that 146 passengers and 14 crew members were on board, including nine foreigners and one Hong Kong resident. Vice-Premier Zou Jiahua and leading officials of CAAC have flown to the accident site. The crash is now under investigation.
