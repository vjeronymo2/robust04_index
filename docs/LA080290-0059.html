August 2, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Magic Johnson helped Michigan State win an NCAA basketball title 11 years ago.

Now, he would like to help run the university.

Michigan Democratic Party officials suggested Johnson run for a seat on the board of trustees for the East Lansing school.

"I would love (to) be part of it, yes, I would be interested," the Laker player said. "I would be very interested."
