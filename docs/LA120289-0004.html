December 2, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

WELLMAN'S TOUCHDOWN: SHADOW OF DOUBT

Regarding the photo in The Times on Nov. 25 showing Gary Wellman's knee supposedly touching the end zone inbounds, I find it interesting that there's a shadow under Wellman's knee. How could his knee be touching the ground and still have a shadow under it?

Moreover, if his knee was as inbounds as the photo supposedly shows, it would not correlate with the TV picture.

Let's face it: The TD should not have been allowed.

JOSEPH GOETZ, Santa Monica
