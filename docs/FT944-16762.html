941012 FT 12 OCT 94 / New tax 'keeps premiums high' The cost of car and house insurance policies would have fallen in the past three months if the government had not imposed a 2.5 per cent tax on premiums from this month, AA Insurance said yesterday. During 1992 and last year both car and home and house-contents insurance premiums increased sharply but that trend appears to have been reversed as the market has returned to profitability, the AA said.
