January 20, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

GIRLS' BASKETBALL ROUNDUP;

LA QUINTA ROLLS TO 47TH STRAIGHT LEAGUE VICTORY

Center Amy Jalewalia scored 32 points, had nine rebounds and six assists to lead La Quinta High School to a 82-39 victory over host Garden Grove Friday night in a Garden Grove League girls' basketball game.

La Quinta, which improved to 16-2 overall and 7-0 in league play, recorded its 47th consecutive league victory.

Tonya Krill added 19 points and Heather Brannan had 12 for La Quinta.

Melanie Dear scored 18 for Garden Grove (8-7, 4-3).

In other Garden Grove League games:

Los Amigos 55, Santiago 41 -- Carrie Elsaesser had 16 points to lead host Los Amigos (7-9, 2-5). May Ly had 12 points and Uyen Nguyen had 11 for Los Amigos. Becky Elcock had 17 points for Santiago.

Kennedy 69, Pacifica 31 -- Valerie Ruiz scored a game-high 33 points, including 23 in the first half, to lead visiting Kennedy (9-8, 5-2). Ruiz, a junior point guard, also had a season-high 12 steals. Michelle Harris added 12 points for Kennedy.
