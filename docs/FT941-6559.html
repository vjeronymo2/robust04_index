940301 FT 01 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Quayle Munro Quayle Munro Holdings, the Edinburgh-based financial services group which came to the main market last June, reported profits of Pounds 323,000 pre-tax for the six months to December 31. The outcome, achieved on income of Pounds 846,000, was struck after an exceptional provision of Pounds 45,000 relating to service charges on a London office occupied by a subsidiary. The group's net asset value expanded 24 per cent to Pounds 7.9m over the period. An interim dividend of 3p is payable from earnings of 5.5p per share.
