Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] Chief of Army Staff General Abdul Waheed has departed for China on a nine-day official visit. He is leading a high-powered delegation which will hold in-depth discussions with PRC National Defense Minister Chi Haotian, People's Liberation Army Chief Zhang Wannian, and other senior Chinese officials. On his arrival in Beijing, the Pakistani army chief said his visit is a part of the continuing consultations and contacts between the senior leaders of the two countries.
