920522 FT 22 MAY 92 / World Trade News: Total in Russian oil agreement TOTAL, the French oil group, has signed a production-sharing agreement with Komineft, the Russian oil company, for the Khariaga field. The deal will involve investment of Dollars 400m (Pounds 218m) over two and a half years to bring the field on stream, with total investment likely to be Dollars 1bn over the life of the field. Total will be the operator in the development of several geological layers of the Khariaga oilfield, located in the Timan Pechora basin, west of the Urals near the Arctic circle. Reserves are estimated at approximately 250m barrels, with production likely to be around 35,000 barrels a day. An agreement was signed in the presence of the governor of the Nenets territory, where the Khariaga field is situated, but is subject to Russian Federation government approval. Total has signed two previous deals with Russian partners to bring western oil drilling technology to boost yields from fields not yet producing.
