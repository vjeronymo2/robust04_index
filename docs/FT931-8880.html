930217 FT 17 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Trust of Property net asset value falls Net asset value of Trust of Property Shares fell from 78.43p to 57.14p over the 12 months to December 31. However, by February 10 it had recovered to 65.44p. Net revenue for 1992 improved 9 per cent to Pounds 101,000 (Pounds 95,000) for earnings per share of 1.529p (1.443p). The proposed single dividend is increased from 1.432p to 1.503p.
