Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] General Mustafa Talas, Syrian deputy prime minister and defense minister, arrived at B'abda Palace at 1305 today at the head of a high-ranking delegation. The Syrian delegation consists of General 'Ali Duba, head of the security and intelligence department of the Syrian Armed Forces; Major General Ibrahim Safi, commander of the Syrian Arab forces operating in Lebanon; and Brigadier General Ghazi Kan'an, head of Syrian security and intelligence in the Syrian Arab forces operating in Lebanon.
