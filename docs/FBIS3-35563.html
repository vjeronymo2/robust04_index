Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Following yesterday's Zionist crimes in the occupied Palestine, a number of the Islamic Republic of Iran's organizations, foundations, and personalities have issued separate statements expressing sympathy with the families of the victims of the tragic massacre of Muslims at the shrine of Abraham. They also offered their condolences to His Eminence Ayatollah Khamene'i, the guardian of the cause of the Muslims, and to all world Muslims, particularly Palestine Muslims, and condemned the perpetrators of that discriminate crime. The statement issued by Ayatollah Mohammad Yazdi, the head of the judicial power, condemns that horrific crime and offers condolences to his eminence the leader, world Muslims, and the families of victims of the Hebron incident and stresses: The black Friday of Palestine is among crimes that should definitely be taken to the International Penal Court [as heard -- Dadgah-e Keifari-ye Beinolmelali] in order to bring the Israeli perpetrators, commanders, and usurpers to court and punish them for their offenses. [passage omitted] Also, the representative of the Iranian Jewish community at the Islamic Consultative Majles condemned the criminal tragedy of the massacre of the fasting worshipers in the city of Hebron. The statement issued in that respect stresses: The Iranian Jewish community expresses disgust at crimes committed at the shrines of His Holiness Abraham, His Holiness Isaac, and His Holiness Jacob which are among the holiest of shrines and common places of worship for Muslims and Jews.
