March 16, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

SOUTHERN CAL COLLEGE ELIMINATED IN NAIA

Southern California College was eliminated in the National Assn. of Intercollegiate Athletics basketball tournament Thursday, losing its second-round game to second-seeded Wisconsin Eau Claire, 92-59.

Center Duane Bushman led Wisconsin Eau Claire (28-3) with 26 points, hitting nine of 10 shots from the field and all eight of his free throws.

Forward Terry Scruggs had 15 points for Southern California College. The Vanguards (26-9) shot 30% from the field.
