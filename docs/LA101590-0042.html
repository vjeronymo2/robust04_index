October 15, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

THE GIPPER: County Republicans are working to bring Ronald Reagan here to rally the GOP faithful just before the Nov. 6 election. . . . Reagan's appeal would be aimed at all party volunteers but particularly those in Assemblyman Curt Pringle's district. . . . "We're very hopeful that we'll get the president to help motivate the troops," says local GOP exec Greg Haskin. Jerry Hicks and Dave Lesher
