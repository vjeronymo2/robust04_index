Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother leader Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] met U.S. Ambassador in Tunis John McCarthy yesterday evening. During the meeting, they reviewed the latest political developments and the dimensions and repercussions on the peace process and the current negotiations of the massacre at the holy Ibrahimi mosque perpetrated by Zionist settlers. They also reviewed the deliberations of the UN Security Council regarding a resolution on the massacre perpetrated by the settlers, the need to guarantee international protection for our Palestinian people, and the disarming of the Zionist settlers so that such a massacre would not be repeated. They also reviewed the Palestinian-U.S. meeting chaired by Dr. Nabil Sha'th [as heard], which is currently being held with U.S. officials in Washington so that the Security Council can come up with an effective resolution. The meeting was attended on the Palestinian side by brother Abu-Lutf [Faruq Qaddumi], the head of the PLO Political Department, Brother Hayil al-Fahum, the head of the European section in the political department, and Dr. Sami Musallam, head of the president's office.
