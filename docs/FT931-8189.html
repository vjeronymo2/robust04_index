930220 FT 20 FEB 93 / Airport bids for chess match MANCHESTER Airport yesterday lodged a bid to sponsor the World Chess Championship in August between Nigel Short, the British challenger, and Gary Kasparov, the champion, Ian Hamilton Fazey writes. The match would be staged in Manchester's Royal Exchange Theatre, built on the disused trading floor in the city centre. The sealed bid to the world governing body of chess is believed to be in the region of Pounds 2m.
