920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / Leaders of divided engineering unions arrive at Blackpool Leaders of the combined Amalgamated Engineering and Electrical Union arriving at the TUC in Blackpool yesterday: from left, Gavin Laird, general secretary of the Amalgamated Engineering Union section of the merged union; Paul Gallagher, general secretary of the EETPU electricians' section; Bill Jordan, president of the merged union. At the moment the two sections of the AEEU are being run virtually as separate unions while the AEU is affiliated to the Trades Union Congrerss and the EETPU remains expelled from it
