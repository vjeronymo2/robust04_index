BFN [Text] People's Assembly Speaker 'Abd-al-Qadir Qaddurah today received the Omani Consultative Council delegation led by Shaykh 'Abdallah Bin-'Ali al-Qatabi. People's Assembly Secretary Dr. Ibrahim Za'rur and the Omani ambassador in Damascus attended the meeting. At the outset of the meeting, Qaddurah gave a briefing on the democratic process and the work of the People's Assembly and its committees under the economic and political stability in Syria. Shaykh al-Qatabi also spoke about the Consultative Council, its work, committees, and tasks. The talks dealt with relations between the two councils, relations between the two fraternal peoples, and the role these relations and meetings can play in confronting challenges to the Arab nation. The talks also dealt with Arab solidarity and the peace process.
