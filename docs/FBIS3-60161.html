Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Nestor Bravo, the leader of the five coca growers' federations in Chapare, has said that the government is not fulfilling the agreement signed at the end of 1993 during the Chapare coca growers' march on La Paz. Bravo stated that the government should have issued a written response to the peasants' demands to revise Law 1008, industrialize coca, and participate in the planning of alternative development programs.
