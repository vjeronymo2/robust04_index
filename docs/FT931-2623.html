930319 FT 19 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Melville cuts losses to Pounds 0.9m Losses at Melville Group, which has interests in exhibition services and interior fitting, were cut from Pounds 10.9m to Pounds 893,000 pre-tax for the half year ended December 31. Turnover from continuing activities totalled Pounds 31.8m (Pounds 30.4m). Interest payable rose from Pounds 537,000 to Pounds 873,000 and losses emerged at 0.84p (25.1p). Comparable figures were after provisions of Pounds 11.8m.
