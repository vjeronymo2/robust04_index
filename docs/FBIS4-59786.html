BFN [Text] His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id has received a message from His Excellency 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council in the sisterly Republic of Yemen. The message deals with recent developments in Yemen. His Highness Fahd Bin-Mahmud Al Sa'id, deputy prime minister for cabinet affairs, received the message during his meeting today with His Excellency Muhammad Salim Basindwah, Yemeni foreign minister and the Yemeni president's envoy, who is currently on a visit to the country. The meeting was attended by His Excellency Yusuf Bin-'Alawi Bin-'Abdallah, minister of state for foreign affairs, and His Excellency Muhammad 'Umar Ba Sa'd, Yemeni ambassador to the sultanate. The talks dealt with the developments in Yemen. The two sides also reviewed the intensive efforts and continuous good offices extended to resolve these developments. His excellency the Yemeni foreign minister praised the sultanate's leading role since the beginning of the crisis and the special concern demonstrated by His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id for achieving peace and stability in Yemen. His excellency noted with appreciation the honest call made by the Government of Oman to end the Yemeni crisis to safeguard the achievements and gains of the Yemeni people.
