June 1, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLE WATCH

Sinbad Sails Away From Festival: Officials of the Great Lumberton Music Festival in Michigan are going to court to make Sinbad Adkins of "A Different World," appear as scheduled. Promoters say Sinbad signed up to appear at the June 30 show, but backed out when Dionne Warwick asked him to be on her HBO cable network special. GREG BRAXTON
