940423 FT 23 APR 94 / Finance and the Family: LIG to restructure - At a glance London International Group, the Durex condoms and Marigold gloves company, announced plans to restructure and refinance. The shares fell further this week when it revealed that its net assets would be virtually eliminated by 'very substantial' restructuring charges. The sale of its ColourCare photoprocessing business is crucial to the revamp, although it is unlikely to bring in much money.
