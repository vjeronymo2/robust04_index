Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 1 Jan (INA)--President Saddam Husayn has sent a congratulatory cable to Fidel Castro, chairman of Cuba's Council of State and president of the Council of Ministers, on the national day of his country. The cable reads: On the 35th anniversary of the victory of the Cuban revolution, the national day of the Republic of Cuba, I am pleased to extend to Your Excellency, on behalf of the people of Iraq and on my own behalf, our heartfelt greetings, wishing you good health and happiness and wishing the Cuban people greater progress and prosperity. I also hope that our friendly bilateral relations will witness continuous development and growth for the interest of our friendly peoples.
