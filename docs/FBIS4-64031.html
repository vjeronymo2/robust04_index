BFN [Report by Captain 2nd Rank Vladimir Gundarov in the "News From the Regions -- From Our Correspondents" column: "Perestroyka on the Border"] [Text] Murmansk -- Protection of Russia's state border in the arctic region will be restructured taking into account the development of links with Norway and Finland and also the opening of the Northern sea route to foreign vessels with calls at Russian ports. This requires the creation of a Northern Border District whose area of responsibility will stretch from the Kola Peninsula to Chukotka. This was announced by Colonel Anatoliy Koretskiy, chief of the Murmansk border guard operations department.
