August 30, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

European basketball champions Jugoplastika filed a multimillion-dollar lawsuit against the Boston Celtics for signing Dino Radja, one of the stars of the Yugoslav team.

In the suit filed in a federal court, Jugoplastika accused the Celtics of inducing Radja, 21, to break a four-year contract with the Yugoslav club.
