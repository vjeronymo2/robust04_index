930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Batleys declines 22% to Pounds 6.53m Pre-tax profits of Batleys, the private Huddersfield-based chain of cash and carry wholesalers, fell 22 per cent from Pounds 8.33m to Pounds 6.53m in the year to May 1. The outcome was struck on turnover of Pounds 477.9m, against Pounds 454.9m for the previous 53 weeks. Interest charges were Pounds 178,000 lower at Pounds 523,000 and earnings per share were 28.06p (35.54p).
