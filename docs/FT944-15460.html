941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / International Company News: Enso-Gutzeit in talks on taking Veitsiluoto stake Enso-Gutzeit, the Finnish forestry group, said yesterday it had been in talks with the Ministry of Trade and Industry over the possible acquisition of a 35 per cent stake in state-owned Veitsiluoto, agencies report from Helsinki. Veitsiluoto produces paper and pulp and operates saw mills. Its turnover in 1993 was FM4.82bn (Dollars 1bn), resulting in a loss after financial items of FM150m. The Finnish state owns 33.7 per cent of the shares and 52.1 per cent of the voting rights of Enso-Gutzeit, and 91.1 per cent of the shares and voting rights of Veitsiluoto.
