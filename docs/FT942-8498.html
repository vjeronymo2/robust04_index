940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / Shell chairman to meet TGWU Mr Chris Fay, chairman of Shell UK, yesterday agreed to meet officials of the TGWU general union on the long-running dispute over union de-recognition in parts of the oil company's operations. This followed representations made by the TGWU at Shell's shareholders meeting. The dispute started in June when Shell de-recognised the union at its Stanford-le-Hope refinery in Essex after doing the same to its tanker drivers.
