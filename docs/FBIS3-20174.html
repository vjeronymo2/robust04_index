Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Three Dutchmen have been kidnapped in Yemen, the Dutch Foreign Ministry has confirmed. The three worked on a drinking water project and were seized last Friday [25 March] close to the capital, Sanaa. Nothing has been heard from them since. The kidnapping is related probably to conflicts between local tribes and the government. Yemeni tribes regularly resort to kidnapping to add force to their grievances against the government. It is not known which tribe is responsible for the latest kidnapping.
