931014 FT 14 OCT 93 / International Company News: Sara Lee buys Finnish concern SARA LEE, the US food and consumer products group, is to to buy Finland's Viking Coffee Company, Reuter reports. Sara Lee said it expected to finalise the deal, of which no financial details were given, by the end of this year. Viking Coffee produces roasted coffee in Finland and Sweden. It has annual sales of around Dollars 45m.
