940712 FT 12 JUL 94 / Hyundai jail term suspended A Seoul high court yesterday suspended a three-year jail term for embezzlement and election fraud imposed on Mr Chung Ju-yung, founder of South Korea's Hyundai group, Reuter reports from Seoul. Mr Chung, 79, was convicted last November of diverting more than Won50bn (Pounds 40.3m) from the group he founded and funnelling the funds to his political party to underwrite his 1992 presidential bid. The jail sentence imposed at the time was for three years with no suspension.
