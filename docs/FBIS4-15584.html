BFN [Text] Baku (April 9) TURAN -- It was announced yesterday in the Milli Majlis that in 1993 the State's budget deficit totalled 25.1 billion manats, despite the fact that it was not supposed to reach 11.7 billion manats. Fikret Yusifov, Deputy Minister of Finance, stated that the reason for the high deficit is due to the negative development in the economy, the fall in productivity and heightened financial crisis. The deputies from the PFA [People's Front of Azerbaijan], the Musavat party and the National Independence Party urged that the government accelerate activities in order to curb the inflation and balance prices in the country with the low salaries. Discussions about the deficit are to continue next week.
