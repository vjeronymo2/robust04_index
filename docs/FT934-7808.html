931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Atreus 21% ahead at Pounds 551,000 Atreus, a shower screen and mirror supplier, achieved a 21 per cent increase in pre-tax profits from Pounds 456,000 to Pounds 551,000 for the half year ended September 30. Turnover improved by 17 per cent to Pounds 3.78m. The company came to market in March following a reverse takeover of URS International. It is paying a maiden interim dividend of 0.264p from earnings of 1.02p (0.85p) per share.
