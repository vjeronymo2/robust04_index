April 2, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $60 Sunday when Saudi Desert, his "single" in the seventh race, ran second. Pro For Sure and Ruhlmann, two of his other picks in the $10 triple, won the sixth and eighth races, respectively, but all for naught. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $1,020.40.
