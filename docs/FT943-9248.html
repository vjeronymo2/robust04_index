940812 FT 12 AUG 94 / Savings growth for investment trusts Private investors put Pounds 122.1m into investment trust savings schemes in the first half of the year, an increase of 20 per cent on the same period last year. More than 79,000 regular savers invested Pounds 37.6m in the six months, a 41 per cent increase from the same period last year. Lump-sum investment rose to Pounds 81.68m from Pounds 73.97m. Total assets of the investment trust industry stood at Pounds 39.68bn at the end of June. The amount raised by new issues so far this year (excluding Lloyd's of London investment trusts) is Pounds 2.86bn compared with Pounds 1.5bn for the whole of last year.
