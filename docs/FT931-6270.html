930302 FT 02 MAR 93 / International Company News: RH Macy to close five stores R. H. MACY, the bankrupt New York-based department store group, says it is to close five of its underperforming department stores and six of its I. Magnin outlets, Nikki Tait reports. The five department stores employed about 850 people and the annual revenues of the outlets was about Dollars 108m.
