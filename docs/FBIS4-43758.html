BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, June 18 (AA) -- A bomb that was thrown at a mosque in southeastern Diyarbakir Province killed one and wounded another, it was reported on Saturday [18 June]. AA learned that the incident happend late at night when two people threw handbombs at the Feritkosku Mosque in Fiskaya district. Police said that an investigation was started to find the responsibles.
