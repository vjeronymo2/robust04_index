Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Korea-Gabon Diplomatic Ties 20 Years Old" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, January 29 (KCNA) -- Papers here today observe the 20th anniversary of the opening of diplomatic relations between Korea and Gabon. The friendly and cooperative relations between the Korean and Gabonese peoples have developed on good terms over the last 20 years, says NODONG SINMUN in a by-lined article, and goes on: The Gabonese people are striving to clear away the consequences of the colonial rule and build a new society. The Korean people actively support the Gabonese people in their efforts to achieve the prosperity of the country and wish them success in their work. The Korean people will as ever make efforts to develop the friendly and cooperative relations with the Gabonese people in conformity with the idea of independence, peace and friendship. A signed article of MINJU CHOSON says that the Korean people wish the Gabonese people greater advance in their work to achieve national unity and build a new prosperous Gabon.
