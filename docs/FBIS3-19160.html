Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Following instructions by the Ministry of Finance, 78 railway cars loaded with oil crossed yesterday at noon through the Idhomeni border station into Skopje [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM]. The cars arrived at the Thessaloniki port one day before the embargo measures were adopted but were blocked because they were there when the embargo was adopted. However, customs officials were wondering why permission was given yesterday.
