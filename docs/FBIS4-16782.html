BFN [ITAR-TASS report: "New Excise Rate Per Tonne of Oil Sold"] [Text] As of 1 May this year the Russian Government is establishing an excise rate for extracting enterprises of, on average, 14,750 rubles per tonne of oil, including gas condensate. This amount will change monthly, depending on the change in the rate of the ruble against the U.S. dollar. There will be different rates for individual oil and gas extracting enterprises.
