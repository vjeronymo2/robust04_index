Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 1 Feb (TANJUG) -- Dragan Tomic, candidate for the Socialist Party of Serbia, was elected the new Serbian Assembly speaker by a majority of votes this evening. Out of 247 deputies who attended the session, 125 voted for him, while Serbian Radical Party candidate Jorgovanka Tabakovic won 113 votes. Two ballot papers were invalid, while five deputies of the Democratic Community of Vojvodina Hungarians abstained.
