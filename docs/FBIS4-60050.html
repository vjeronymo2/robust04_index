BFN [Text] The Council of Ministers resumed its meetings today under Acting Prime Minister Dr. Muhammad Sa'id al-'Attar. The Council resumed discussion of the detailed working plan of all sectors of work and production in the various economic, social, administrative, financial, security, and media fields. The plan also deals with all the ministries' responsibilities of following up all the facilities and institutions supervised by them. The Council discussed means to reactivate these ministries' work and to tackle all the disorder caused by the gang of secession, rebellion, and apostasy in the leadership of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP] as part of its policy to sabotage unity, democracy, and constitutional legitimacy in the country. The Council endorsed all the proposals and tasks assigned to all the government quarters in this regard. The Council also decreed that the government shall shoulder the additional flight insurance in order to secure the return of citizens from abroad to the country, particularly the sick, students, and those who left the country for work purposes. The Council listened to the foreign minister's report on his recent tour of a number of fraternal Arab countries.
