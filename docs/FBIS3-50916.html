Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Guatemala City, 10 Jan (ACAN-EFE) -- The Guatemalan Government today condemned the attack carried out by unidentified individuals on the Mexican Consulate in Guatemala City and offered security to the personnel and the installations. Guatemalan Government Minister Arnoldo Ortiz Moscoso told the media that the government rejects the use of terrorism as a political weapon and warned that those responsible will be tracked down and "severely" punished. "Terrorism is a crime that is punished by Guatemalan laws and the international treaties to which Guatemala is signatory to," Ortiz said. [passage omitted]
