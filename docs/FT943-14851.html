940712 FT 12 JUL 94 / International Company News: Shopping-centre deal Hammerson Canada, a unit of Hammerson Property Investment of the UK, is paying CDollars 160m (USDollars 115.1m) for three Ontario shopping centres with combined retail space of 1.2m square feet, writes Robert Gibbens. The seller is Sifton Properties, a family-controlled property holding company. Hammerson is one of the largest commercial property companies in eastern Canada.
