BFN [Text] Brother Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat], president of the state of Palestine received a message from the Russian leadership concerning the current contacts and negotiations to implement the Palestinian-Israeli Declaration of Principles. Brother president Abu-'Ammar received the message yesterday evening when he received Mr. Boris Shchiborin, Russian ambassador to Tunisia. During the meeting, brother president Abu-'Ammar informed the Russian ambassador of the latest developments concerning the Palestinian issue.
