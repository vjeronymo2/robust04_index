BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Glazovskaya] [Text] Ashkhabad, 21 April (TASS) -- The first round of Turkmen-Chinese summit talks has revealed the identity of their stands on the main aspects of cooperation. This was stressed at a briefing for journalists, organised by the Chinese side. It was stated that Li Peng and Saparmurat Niyazov had expressed satisfaction over the level of the development of bilateral relations after the establishment of diplomatic contacts in 1992. Both leaders pointed to mutual understanding and confidence, which are typical of the relations between their countries. They stressed that there existed a potential for their further deepening. It was stated at the briefing that the talks had revealed the main spheres of possible economic cooperation, of which the transportation of Turkmen gas to China is the most important one. Participants in the talks, held in Ashkhabad, pointed out that trade turnover between China and Turkenistan is not too great so far, but there are chances to make it more effective. The Chinese side expressed a lively interest in purchasing Turkmen cotton. Four agreements are expected to be signed at the talks, which will be continued on Friday. They will become more specific after a meeting of Chiness business people with heads of Turkmen ministries and institutions, scheduled for Friday.
