Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over still of regional map] [Text] A medium-strength tremor was felt in Manisa and the surrounding area. There were shattered windows and cracked walls in several houses following the tremor. According to information received from Kandilli Observatory at Bogazici University, the tremor occurred at 1745 [1545 GMT] and registered 5.0 on the Richter scale. The tremor was also felt in Izmir, Aydin, Balikesir, and Denizli.
