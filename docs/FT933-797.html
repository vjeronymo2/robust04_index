930927 FT 27 SEP 93 / UN sanctions against Unita THE United Nations banned the sale of fuel and weapons to the Angolan rebel movement Unita yesterday in an attempt to force a negotiated end to the country's bloody civil war, AP reports from Luanda. The sanctions were an attempt to slow the rebel army which has captured almost three quarters of Angolan territory. A UN official said: 'We certainly hope no stronger measures will be necessary to convince Unita that the time has come for negotiations.'
