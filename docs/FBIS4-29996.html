BFN [Text] Comrade Lieutenant General Saman Vi-gnaket, Political Bureau member of the party Central Committee, chairman of the National Assembly, and former chief of the Army's General Political Department, held a meeting with representatives of all Army units from throughout the country on 18 June 1994 in the meeting room of the National Defense Ministry. First, Comrade Lt. Gen. Saman Vi-gnaket hailed every service of the armed forces for being steadfastly loyal and faithful to the nation and the party, consistently enhancing the fine traditions of the Lao nation and the Army, resolutely persisting in overcoming all difficulties and fulfilling national defense and public security maintenance tasks, and striving to consolidate themselves to become progressive in all fields. The comrade also noted developments in the current international and regional situations; the enemy's sinister designs against our country; and the party's current policies spelled out in the various resolutions adopted by the party, which must be profoundly appreciated and strictly implemented by the armed forces with a high sense of responsibility. In conclusion, Comrade Lt. Gen. Saman Vi-gnaket called on our Army to enhance its fine traditions actively; to fulfill the three-duty and four-relations tasks [not further specified] exceptionally; and to act as a progressive model in all four areas -- firmly grasping the party's policy, consistently maintaining the revolutionary line, pursuing correct behavioral patterns, and maintaining a clean and honest lifestyle.
