930811 FT 11 AUG 93 / Pounds 3,500 award in race-bias case A COMPUTER operator who was refused a job interview because he was Asian has been awarded Pounds 3,500 in damages by an industrial tribunal. The award was made against Finserv of Town Street, Armley, West Yorkshire, and Mr A. Dobson, the proprietor. The tribunal heard that Mr Dobson advertised in a Jobcentre for a computer operator/bookkeeper. When officials told him Mr Eqbal Bhogal was a suitable candidate, Mr Dobson had replied: 'I'm sorry but I'm completely prejudiced and there would be no point in him coming along.'
