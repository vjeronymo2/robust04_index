April 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TRAVEL NEWS AND BRIEFS

A slide-discussion on Russia and Scandinavia will be held by the Bon Voyage Travel Club at 7:45 p.m. on April 21 at the Community Room, 9696 Wilshire Blvd., Beverly Hills. Admission $5. For more information, call (213) 476-4869.

-- -- --

Mort Loveman, travel photographer and lecturer will offer a free slide lecture on Kenya and Tanzania safari at 1 p.m. Wednesday at the Roxbury Park Club House, 471 S. Roxbury Drive, Beverly Hills. For more information, call (213) 550-4761.

-- -- --

European travel on a budget will be the topic of an American Youth Hostel (AYH) seminar at 1 p.m. Saturday at the AYH Travel Centre, 335 W. 7th St, San Pedro. Admission $3. For more information, call (213) 831-8846.

-- -- --

More than 400 power and sailboats plus marine accessory and service exhibits will be part of the 16th Annual Newport Boat Show Wednesday through April 16 at Lido Marina Village, Newport Beach. Show hours are 11 a.m. to 6 p.m. weekdays and 10 a.m. to 6 p.m. weekends. Free parking will be available off Tustin and Avon Avenues in Newport Beach with complimentary shuttle to Lido Marina Village every 15 minutes. Admission $5 for adults, $2 for children 6 to 12 and children under 6 free. For additional information, call (714) 673-9360.

-- -- --

The feasibility of trekking on your own in Nepal will be discussed in a three-hour seminar April 15 at 10 a.m. at the Physical Education Building, Room 148, Santa Monica College, 1900 Pico Blvd., Santa Monica. Admission $20, plus $2 fee for materials. Reservations are required. For more information, call (213) 452-9214.
