Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Mate Boban, president of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna, today convened the first session of the presidential council of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna. The council discussed to date the course of peace negotiations and the current military and political situation in the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna and Bosnia-Herzegovina. The Presidential Council gave its full support to the new initiative launched by Franjo Tudjman, the president of the Republic of Croatia, in Bonn as a contractual agreement on establishing lasting and comprehensive peace between Croat and Bosniak-Muslim nations in Bosnia-Herzegovina. They also gave full support to President Tudjman and the negotiating team, and upheld the constructive efforts and proposals to end the conflict in Bosnia-Herzegovina during the upcoming peace talks in Geneva.
