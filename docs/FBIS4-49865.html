BFN [Text] Beijing, May 10 (CNS) -- The State Bureau of Technological Supervision has found the quality of products of large-sized enterprises improved but those from medium- and small-scale and township enterprises were still producing counterfeit and low quality products. Some 954 of medium and small-sized enterprises were examined, making up 82.5 percent of the total enterprises looked at. Of these, only 63.7 percent of the products were found to meet the standards and only 62.7 percent of township enterprise products were found to meet the standards, and at the same time, the production of counterfeit and low quality products remained serious.
