920912 FT 12 SEP 92 / Solicitor jailed for stealing A SOLICITOR was jailed for 3 1/2 years at the Old Bailey yesterday after admitting stealing Pounds 400,000 of clients' funds. Mr John Bridge, a former partner in the City firm of Taylor, Joynson and Garrett, admitted five charges of theft. Mr Bridge gave Pounds 150,000 of his pension to the solicitor's indemnity fund which had covered the losses of clients including the London Hospital Special Trustees, the court heard.
