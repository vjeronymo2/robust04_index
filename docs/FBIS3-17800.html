Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report from "In Brief" column by PRAVDA correspondents and ITAR-TASS: "Registration Refused for the Time Being"] [Text] The Republic's Justice Ministry has refused to register the party of Communists of Moldova. However, the Republic Supreme Court, to which the communists' initiative group appealed, deemed the Justice Ministry's decision unlawful. After some amendments to the founding documents, Moldavia's [Moldova's] communists will make a new attempt at registration.
