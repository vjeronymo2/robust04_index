BFN [Text] The Presidium of the Supreme Council has approved by decree [words indistinct] the military prosecutor's offices of the Republic. The general public prosecutor and the defense minister are to set up the structure and status of the military prosecutor's offices. The Council of Ministers has been instructed to allocate resources for the Ministry of Defense to maintain the military prosecutor's offices. The decree is signed by Emomali Rakhmonov, chairman of the Supreme Council of the Republic of Tajikistan.
