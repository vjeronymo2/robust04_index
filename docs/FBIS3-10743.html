Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Today, in Santiago de Chile, Joao Baena Soares, secretary general of the Organization of American States, called for Cuba's reinstatement in the regional organization. Baena Soares stated that the nineties are not the sixties. Upon his arrival in Chile, where he will participate in the presidential succession ceremony on 11 March, Baena Soares said: Many things have changed and it is necessary to consider the issue of Cuba's reinsertion into the organization. Baena went on to say that he could not predict the decision of OAS members on the matter and stated that the subject has not been included in the agenda of the general assembly or permanent commission.
