920519 FT 19 MAY 92 / Ulster search for stolen gun SECURITY forces in Ulster yesterday continued to search for a heavy-calibre machine gun stolen during confrontations in Coalisland, Tyrone, which culminated in the shooting of three civilians by members of the Parachute Regiment. During the violence the machine gun, capable of firing 1,000 rounds a minute, and a rifle, were taken from soldiers. The rifle was later recovered. Security sources said the Parachute Regiment had been withdrawn from Coalisland but would retain overall responsibility for East Tyrone.
