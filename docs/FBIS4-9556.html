BFN [Text] At a meeting in Split, groups of experts of the governments of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna and Bosnia-Herzegovina have coordinated a draft law on the government of the federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina and a law on the federation ministries. The future government of the federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina is to have 12 ministries, at least five of which will be headed by representatives of the Croat nation. In addition, two ministers will be without portfolio -- the government will have 15 members, prime minister included, it is laid down in the draft law on the government of the federation of Bosnia-Herzegovina. Zaran Buntic, the chairman of the Judicial Council [Sudbeno Vijece] of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna, said that the second round of expert negotiations on the draft laws on the official gazette of the federation and on the stamp of the federation would be held soon. The draft law on the delineation of cantons is also being prepared.
