November 5, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Bingo! It's Newton: Entertainer Wayne Newton's organization has signed a two-year contract to manage a $1.4-million Indian bingo hall that's been closed for six months.

Wayne Newton Gaming Inc. has agreed to manage a bingo hall operated by the Seneca-Cayuga tribe in northeastern Oklahoma, said Sandra Hoy, Newton's director of marketing. The games will begin Nov. 16, but a grand opening ceremony, at which Newton will appear, is scheduled for January. Newton already operates a bingo hall in Santa Ynez. CLAUDIA PUIG
