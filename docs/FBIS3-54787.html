Language: Kyrgyz Article Type:BFN [Text] The presidium of the government has held a regular session. Under examination were two issues: first, the fulfillment by the Kyrgyztamakash state concern of the government decree on improving the activity of that state concern in providing the population of the republic with food; and, second, on measures aimed at preserving state property and observing tax and customs legislature by the Dzhalabad state regional administration. The chairman of the state concern spoke on the first issue. He reported on the work of enterprises which are a part of this concern. Prime Minister Dzhumagulov noted that it is too conservative in its work and demanded that the chairman and other senior officials of the concern strengthened the leadership of their departments, raise the responsibility for the job done on the part of the staff, and exercise control over their activities. The session went on to note that Dzhalabad Oblast has violated laws which regulate the preservation of state property. Regarding the second issue, the prime minister went on note that as a result of these violations 427,000 soms worth of damage have been inflicted on the state. Only 117,000 somes of this sum have been paid back. The head of the government demanded they take all the necessary steps to recompensate for the damage done to the state.
