Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz received the certificate of Ukraine's honorary consulate in Lebanon from Mr. Samir Tawilah after the Lebanese authorities approved his appointment as Ukraine's honorary consul here. Buwayz then received a delegation from the Lebanese-Ukrainian Friendship Society in the presence of Consul Tawilah. Tawilah said after the meeting that a protocol for cooperation between the Chambers of Industry and Commerce in Beirut and Ukraine was signed during his recent visit to Ukraine.
