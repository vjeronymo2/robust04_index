940923 FT 23 SEP 94 / Former M16 officer resigns from Kroll Associates Mr Michael Oatley, a former M16 officer who played a key role in the early stages of the Northern Ireland peace process, has resigned as a senior partner from Kroll Associates, the New York-based corporate investigation agency, Jimmy Burns writes. Mr Oatley last night said he wanted to be more independent and intended to form his own investigative consultancy together with Kroll's former chairman Mr Patrick Grayson, who resigned this summer. According to industry sources his resignation is expected to be followed by others in the coming weeks because of growing dissatisfaction with the management of the company. Mr Oatley, who used the alias 'Mountain Climber', initiated contacts with senior republicans as part of an effort to bring about a ceasefire.
