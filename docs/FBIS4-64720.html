BFN [Text] Foreign Ministry spokesman Konstandinos Bikas said that Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias made no promises--as Tirana claims he did--in connection with Albanian claims that radio stations and other organizations are being obstructed from operating in northern Greece. Bikas said Papoulias never promised not to obstruct operations. Bikas was commenting on reports that the claim was made during the Zurich meeting between Papoulias and Albanian Foreign Minister Alfred Serreqi, and that the Greek side supposedly accepted it.
