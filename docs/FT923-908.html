920925 FT 25 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Throgmorton Dual halves final dividend Throgmorton Dual Trust saw its net asset value per capital share fall from 652.7p to 581.28p over the year to the end of July. The income share net asset figure was 26.83p (30.4p). In the 12 months to July 31 net revenue was Pounds 1.61m (Pounds 1.94m) for earnings per share of 7.02p (8.41p). A halved final dividend of 1.75p is recommended for a total of 7p (8.75p).
