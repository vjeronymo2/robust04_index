October 24, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

O.C. EARNINGS

Gateway Communications Inc.: The Irvine-based maker of communications products for the IBM PC market reported net income for the three months ended Sept. 30 of $331,889 on revenues of $6.9 million, contrasted with earnings of $177,939 on sales of $4.5 million in the third quarter of 1988. Net income for the first nine months was $755,793 on revenues of $19.7 million, contrasted with earnings of $514,725 on sales of $11.7 million for the same period a year ago.
