BFN [Text] Yerevan, 16 MAY (SNARK)--CSCE Minsk group Chairman Ian Eliasson and the Russian president's special envoy Vladimir Kazimirov have issued today a joint statement, following their mission to Transcacasus. In it they particularly expressed their satisfaction by the fact that truce as a whole is observed in the Karabakh conflict zone. "The truce is a result of agreement reached on May 12 this year between the defense ministers of Armenia and Azerbaijan, as well as defense forces commander of Nagorno-Karabakh, under intermediary activity of CSCE and Russia", is said in the document. It is also noted in the statement that presently a draft of agreement is being discussed, the purpose of which is "to back up the truce by disposition of international observers".
