Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] His Royal Highness Crown Prince Hasan, the Regent, Monday called for the formation of a committee to investigate charges raised by Health Minister 'Abd al-Rahim Malhas, of violations of specifications and standards by pharmascists and food merchants. Prince Hasan thanked Dr. Malhas for raising the issue. He also noted that Jordanian pharmaceutical products enjoy good reputation abroad. The Regent made the comments during a visit to al-Balqa' Governorate.
