920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / International Company News: PFC advances POWER Financial (PFC), Mr Paul Desmarais, the Montreal financier's financial services group, said earnings rosein the second quarter and first half, writes Robert Gibbens. Second-quarter profit was CDollars 90.7m (USDollars 76.8m) or CDollars 1.05 a share, up from CDollars 55.3m or 62 cents a year earlier and in the first half CDollars 135.7m or Dollars 1.56 a share, against CDollars 91.9m or CDollars 1.02 a share.
