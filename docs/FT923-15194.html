920703 FT 03 JUL 92 / UK Company News: 7% pay increase for Pilkington chairman Sir Antony Pilkington, chairman of Pilkington, the glass group, had a pay rise of 7.3 per cent to Pounds 335,000 last year. Pilkington's earnings per share, before exceptional charges, fell from 8.6p to 5.5p in 1991 and the dividend was reduced from 10.5p to 6p.
