BFN [Text] The Agrarian Party of Russia will sign the agreement on public accord only after an agreement is concluded between the government and representatives of the agroindustrial complex. This agreement ought to guarantee state purchases of agricultural produce. This decision was made on 23 April at a plenum of the Central Council of the Agrarian Party of Russia. It was attended by Chairman of the State Duma Ivan Rybkin, Deputy Prime Minister Aleksandr Zaveryukha, and Agriculture Minister Viktor Khlystun.
