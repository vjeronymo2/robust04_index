September 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

GREETING GORILLAS;

INN STILL IN

It's been approximately 10 years since I first saw your feature article about the Old Monterey Inn. In case you haven't visited 500 Martin Street lately, Anne and Gene Swett continue to provide the best aspects of every B&B they've ever visited, along with their own amenities. They serve a spectacular full breakfast, different each morning. Throughout the day the huge Tudor house and gardens are open for reading, lounging or partaking of always-available hot and cold beverages. There are teatime cookies, followed by a sherry hour with memorable canapes. And the Inn's accommodations are the finest anywhere. Rates are from $140 to $195. Thanks for the introduction.

WILLIAM B. LENZ

San Clemente
