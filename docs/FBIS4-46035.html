BFN [Unattributed report under the "What's New In Russia" rubric: "Settling Scores Before Nuclear Reactor"] [Text] The fire-fighting crew presented an ultimatum to the chiefs of the Obninsk Nuclear Research Center: Either they pay the two months' arrears in wages or the fire-fighters decline responsibility for protecting the nuclear reactors of the physics and power engineering institute. Specialists think an unscheduled shutdown of the reactors could entail unpredictable consequences. The "Obninskgorgaz," to which the nuclear research center is also in debt, has threatened to stop gas supplies.
