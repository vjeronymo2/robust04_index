931218 FT 18 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Bankers Inv Trust asset value up 37% Bankers Investment Trust increased net asset value by 37 per cent to 183.5p per share over the 12 months to October 31. Net revenue improved by 14.8 per cent representing earnings per share of 4.23p (3.68p). The increase was largely attributable to the higher sterling value of unfranked income, up from Pounds 3.67m to Pounds 4.97m. The total dividend is raised by 7 per cent to 3.68p via a fourth interim of 0.98p. For the current year a minimum of 3.88p is forecast, an increase of 5.4 per cent.
