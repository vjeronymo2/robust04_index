BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 19 (KCNA) -- A Pyongyang-visiting group of former heads of state and government and politicians of different countries led by ex-President of Costa Rica Rodrigo Carazo Odio, staying in Korea, visited the Mansudae Art Studio on April 18. They saw wonderful porcelains, embroidery articles, engravings and Korean paintings created under the wise guidance of the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. At the end of the visit, the head of the group wrote in the visitor's book that the studio is a comprehensive creative base showing how excellent the Korean art and fine arts are. Former Prime Minister of Egypt Aziz Sedky said all the works of the studio fully show the Korean art which is very excellent and charming. The group visited the Pyongyang metro. After the visit Mark P. Barry, director of Research of the Summit Council for World Peace, said this metro is the most beautiful, excellent and clean in the world. Noting that the world knows little about this, he said he was pleased to hear that the metro would be extended in the future. He expressed the belief that it would be not a long time before the whole world come to know what a developed country Korea is. Earlier, the guests visited the Tower of the Chuche Idea and the Korean Central History Museum.
