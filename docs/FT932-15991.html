930408 FT 08 APR 93 / Tour operator's licence called in THE CIVIL Aviation Authority has called in the bond of tour operator Alan Dickson and Company of Bearsden, Glasgow, which traded as Alan Dickson Flights, Alan Dickson Travel and Commodore Flights and specialised in flight-only operations to Spain. About 80 passengers are abroad and will be able to fly home as planned. The CAA said it would arrange a full refund for 1,100 passengers with advance bookings.
