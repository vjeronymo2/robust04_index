April 13, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

WORLD;

CANTON RALLIES MARK 28 EXECUTIONS

The southern China city of Canton held its biggest anti-crime rallies in years to publicize 28 executions, the official Legal News reported today.

Seventy-four other criminals were given jail sentences at the rallies, which a city official said drew over 10,000 people in nine areas of Canton on Wednesday.
