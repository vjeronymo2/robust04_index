Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The University of Abuja has been closed down. That followed a violent demonstration by the students during which the vice chancellor's office and two vehicles belonging to the institution were set ablaze. Radio Nigeria correspondents who visited the university campus report that a detachment of security agents is being stationed there to maintain law and order. The students had embarked on demonstrations to press their demand for a clarification of the legal (?status of the institution).
