931221 FT 21 DEC 93 / UK Company News: BBB Design recovering BBB Design, the USM-quoted marketing and computer services group, continued its recovery in the six months to October 31. It reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 43,000, against losses of Pounds 165,000, following the return to profits in the previous second half. Mr Philip O'Donnell, chairman and chief executive, said the design, marketing and publishing division had been budgeted for a small loss but it broke even. He added that the computer services side continued to benefit from the strong product base of BBB Communications. Turnover advanced to Pounds 956,000 (Pounds 874,000). Earnings per share came out at 0.52p (losses 1.99p).
