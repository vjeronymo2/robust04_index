940823 FT 23 AUG 94 / World Stock Markets: S Africa sees solid gains Equities made solid gains in spite of weakness on European bourses and a sell-off in the domestic bond market. Dealers said that industrial stocks remained dull as investors searched for new direction, but gold and mining related shares showed strength. De Beers bounced back after a decline on Friday, adding R3 to R110. Anglos were firm, adding R4 to R262. The overall index added 48 to 5,847, industrials 4 to 6,593 and gold 50 to 2,246. Housewares, the retail debutante, came in at a premium to its 200 cents issue price, opening at 250 cents and rising to a session high of 280 cents, before closing at 265 cents, in turnover of R4m.
