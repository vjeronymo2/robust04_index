941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / Observer: Wart-torn Erstwhile Oxford don and now Argentine foreign minister Guido Di Tella knows how to put out the red carpet. Saying that Buenos Aires might give political asylum to the freshly booted-out Haitian military leader, Raoul Cedras, Di Tella said: 'It would be like swallowing a toad.' But he added: 'If nobody is ready to swallow that toad, then there is no solution and the whole process will get stuck.'
