921229 FT 29 DEC 92 / BA and Virgin set for libel trial BRITISH Airways yesterday said it would meet Virgin Atlantic in court over a libel action launched by its smaller rival, in spite of speculation about an out-of-court settlement. Mr Richard Branson, chairman of the Virgin group, initiated the libel action after BA denied his allegations of 'dirty tricks' and unfair competition practices. The action is due to start on January 11. British Airways is also suing Virgin over the allegations. BA said: 'We expect to be in court when the case starts and the whole matter is in the hands of our solicitors.'
