930824 FT 24 AUG 93 / Thames Fast Ferries seek talks with Robson Rhodes Thames Fast Ferries, which operates between London and Gravesend, Kent, yesterday sought talks with Robson Rhodes, liquidator of the capital's collapsed RiverBus company, in the hope of restoring the service. The RiverBus boats remain tied up at Trinity Pier, east London, as Robson Rhodes seeks a buyer for the business
