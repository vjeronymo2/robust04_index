Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] In a telephone conversation with His Excellency Lebanese President Ilyas al-Hirawi today, His Majesty King Husayn denounced the criminal incident that occurred in Sayyidat al-Najat church in Lebanon this morning, killing innocent worshipers. On behalf of himself and the people and Government of Jordan, his majesty offered to his excellency the Lebanese president his sincerest condolences for the victims of the outrageous incident.
