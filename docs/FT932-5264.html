930604 FT 04 JUN 93 / UK Company News: T French improves A good contribution from its curtain tape business, which was acquired a year ago, together with the disposal of lossmaking Kaz Krafts were behind the interim advance at Thomas French & Sons. In the six months to April 3 pre-tax profits rose from Pounds 299,000 to Pounds 725,000 on sales 22 per cent higher at Pounds 7.52m. Interest payments increased to Pounds 133,000 (Pounds 40,000) partly due to acquisition costs. The interim dividend is maintained at 1.45p on earnings per share of 3.99p (1.47p).
