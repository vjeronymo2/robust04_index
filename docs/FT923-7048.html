920820 FT 20 AUG 92 / International Company News: Liberty Life lifts payout LIBERTY Life, South Africa's largest listed insurance company, has sharply increased its interim dividend and will pay a special 35th anniversary dividend. The interim dividend, for the six months to June, has been raised 25.6 per cent to 54 cents per share from 43 cents in 1991, while an anniversary dividend amounts R1 per share. Mr Donald Gordon, chairman, said new annualised premiums rose during the reporting period to R278.2m (Dollars 100.70m) from R203.3m, reflecting the success of the new Medical Lifestyle product launched in September 1991.
