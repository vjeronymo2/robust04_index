February 28, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

A Princely Dinner Date: Union Rescue Mission could receive up to $10,000 from the sale of two pictures of Prince and Kim Basinger dining together. Photojournalist Kip Rano is selling 100 prints of each picture for $500 apiece, and he will donate part of the proceeds to the mission. The photos were taken as the two stars were eating at Chaya Brasserie in Beverly Hills this month. Beth Kleid
