BFN [Text] A demonstration in the Afar District of Arhiba was violently repressed by police this morning, who used tear gas and real bullets. According to medical sources, two people are believed to have been killed, and several are reported to have been wounded. The demonstrators are protesting against the police, who for several days now have been destroying wooden houses sheltering people displaced by war. About 10 days ago, similar clashes led to the deaths of two people.
