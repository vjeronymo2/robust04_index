BFN ["IAEA Negotiating Delegation Off"--KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, May 28 (KCNA) -- The negotiating delegation of the International Atomic Energy Agency headed by Demetrius Perricos, director of the division of operation of the safeguards department of the agency, which had come to the DPRK according to an agreement between Korea and the IAEA left here today by plane, wrapping up its five-day visit.
