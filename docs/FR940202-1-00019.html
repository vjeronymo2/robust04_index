EFFECTIVE DATE: The withdrawal of this notice of proposed rulemaking is effective February 2, 1994. 26 CFR Part 1 [IL&hyph;21&hyph;91] RIN 1545&hyph;AQ45 Imposition of Accuracy-Related Penalty AGENCY: Internal Revenue Service (IRS), Treasury. ACTION: Withdrawal of notice of proposed rulemaking.

SUMMARY: This document withdraws the proposed regulations relating to the imposition of accuracy-related penalty under Internal Revenue Code sections 6662(e) and (h) and section 6664(c) for transactions between persons described in Internal Revenue Code section 482 and net section 482 transfer price adjustments that was published in the Federal Register on January 21, 1993. EFFECTIVE DATE: The withdrawal of this notice of proposed rulemaking is effective February 2, 1994. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Thomas L. Ralph at (202) 622&hyph;3880 (not a toll-free number). SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: Background On January 21, 1993, the IRS published a notice of proposed rulemaking (IL&hyph;21&hyph;91) in the Federal Register (58 FR 5304) that proposed amendments to the Income Tax Regulations under sections 6662(e) and (h) and section 6664(c) of the Internal Revenue Code of 1986. The proposed regulations implemented section 11312 of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act of 1990, Public Law 101&hyph;508, 104 Stat. 1388. In view of the enactment of the Omnibus Budget Reconciliation Act of 1993, Public Law 103&hyph;66, section 13236, 107 Stat. 312 (codified at I.R.C. 6662(e) and (h)), which further amended those sections, proposed regulations §1.6662&hyph;5 (e) and (j) and §1.6664&hyph;4(d) are hereby withdrawn. Partial Withdrawal of Notice of Proposed Rulemaking Accordingly, under the authority of 26 U.S.C. 7805, proposed regulations §1.6662&hyph;5(e) and (j) and §1.6664&hyph;4(d) published in the Federal Register on January 21, 1993 (58 FR 5304), are withdrawn.
