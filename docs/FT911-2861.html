910427 FT 27 APR 91 / International Company News: Poor first quarter for Shell Oil SHELL OIL, a US unit of Shell Transport and Trading of the UK and Royal Dutch Petroleum, saw first-quarter net income tumble to Dollars 125m from Dollars 203m for the same period last year. Revenues totalled Dollars 5.67bn, compared with Dollars 5.75bn last time, writes our Financial Staff. The company said natural gas prices declined to their lowest first-quarter level since 1980.
