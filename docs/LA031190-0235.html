March 11, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

KELSEY BREAKS THE "LAW"

For me, "L.A. Law" is indisputably the high point of the viewing week.

It rivals anything on PBS, which I usually watch. However, two recent programs focused on Ann Kelsey. She is a very jealous, hateful and spiteful person. I know that such people do exist; in fact, I had such a "boss lady" myself once. Some of the other workers referred to her as a "witch on wheels." I realize that the series always tries to get in some conflict by showing all kinds of people. However, it is just too painful to watch this greedy and spiteful character.

Bernadette McCrumb, Lynwood
