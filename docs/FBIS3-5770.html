Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, march 10 (YONHAP) -- South Korea has officially notified the United Nations of its intention to seek non-permanent membership in the U.N. Security Council, a government official said Thursday. He said the government officially conveyed its intent last month to the Asia group, comprising 46 countries, to contest an election for non-permanent members of the Security Council next year at the U.N. General Assembly.
