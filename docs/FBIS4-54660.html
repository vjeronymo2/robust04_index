BFN [Excerpts] The economic ministers decided today to pay off part of the debt to the IMF. Editor Vladislav Kulhavy reports from the government office: [Kulhavy] It is a sum to the value of $430 million. According to Dr. (Holman), adviser to the Czech Government, the state can afford this extraordinary repayment for 1995 in view of the high foreign currency reserves of the Czech National Bank. This extraordinary repayment of part of the debt will, at the same time, mean considerable savings. [passage omitted] The ministers also agreed to establish two new independent customs zones, in Decin and Temelin. Apart from that, the meeting accepted a decision to privatize Kablo Velke Mezirici in the form of a public competition and agreed with the principles for further talks on creating a free trade zone with Romania. The ministers' meeting referred the materials on apprentice training for further revision.
