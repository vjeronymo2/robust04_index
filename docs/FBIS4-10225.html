BFN [Text] Osmany Cienfuegos, vice president of the Council of Ministers, has announced that the process of making structural changes in the sphere of tourism was set in March. There will henceforth be a Ministry of Tourism and a series of provincial enterprises and chains. Three of these chains will specialize in hotel exploitation. [Later in the same newscast, in a report on items appearing in GRANMA on 9 April, correspondent Roger Ricardo Luis says: "Among GRANMA's main stories today is a report on the new structure being imposed on the tourism sector in Cuba on the basis of the changes in Intur, the National Institute of Tourism. Osmany Cienfuegos, vice president of the Council of Ministers, has announced that a Ministry of Tourism will be formed soon. The new structure aims to obtain greater efficiency in this vital economic domain."]
