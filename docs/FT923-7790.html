920815 FT 15 AUG 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG drifted lower as De Beers remained under pressure, off R1.65 at R60. Its shares have lost R15 on the week. The overall index closed 15 lower at 3,140 while industrials shed 24 to 3,973. Gold dropped 1 to 936, down 7 per cent on the week.
