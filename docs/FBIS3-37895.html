Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova] [Text] Moscow February 24 TASS -- "The main thing in the Russian president's message to the Russian parliament is an attempt at formulating a national idea", Sergey Shakhray, minister for nationalities and regional policy, said in an interview with ITAR-TASS on Thursday. According to the minister, the message does not just outlines the political and economic situation in the country, it "formulates the task of strengthening a national idea". The formulation of a national idea, he said, is very important now for all peoples in the Russian Federation.
