Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [See Selected Video Reports for Cuban TV report on bombing] [Text] Cairo, 19 Mar (MENA)-- A primitive locally-made explosive device went off this evening in front of the Egyptian American International Bank, near the hunting club in al-Duqqi. The explosion caused no casualties, but it smashed the bank's front windows. Leading cadres in al-Jizah Security Directorate, detective agencies, and laboratory experts headed for the site of the explosion to examine it.
