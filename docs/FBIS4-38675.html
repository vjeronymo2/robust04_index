BFN [Text] The Armenian Republic President Levon Ter-Petrosyan signed a decree today to abolish the Armenian Government's Permanent Mission in the Russian Federation and instructed the Armenian Government to take action to implement this decree. The Armenian president also signed decrees appointing Yuriy Mkrtumyan Armenia's ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary to Russia and bestowing upon him the diplomatic title of ambassador extraordinary and plenipotentiary.
