Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Byrkin] [Text] Moscow February 7 TASS -- Fighting has been going since Monday morning in the north of the Kelbadzhar region in the area of the Azeri-Karabakh conflict, ITAR-TASS learned by telephone from the Nagorno-Karabakh press and information department with reference to the Nagorno-Karabakh army headquarters. A relative lull was reported at other areas of the front, where no active military operations were held, except occasional skirmishes. The Nagorno-Karabakh army headquarters reported that three pieces of the Azeri military hardware were destroyed on Sunday. Azeri bombers continue air-raids. On Monday, air-raid warnings were made twice in Stepanakert.
