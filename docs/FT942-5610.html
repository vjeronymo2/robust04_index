940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Usborne Pounds 8m in red at midway Shares in Usborne resumed trading yesterday and shed 6p to 13 1/2 p after the grain merchant and pig producer reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 8m for the six months to December 31, against profits of Pounds 887,000. Although the grain merchanting side produced trading profits of Pounds 947,000 (Pounds 1.1m), there were losses of Pounds 6m (Pounds 96,000 profits) from pig production and merchanting. The pre-tax result was after a Pounds 3.3m write-down in the value of the pig herds and a Pounds 2m provision for restructuring the division. Turnover from continuing operations was Pounds 93.7m (Pounds 107.6m). Losses per share amounted to 9.94p (0.83p earnings). There is no dividend (0.2p).
