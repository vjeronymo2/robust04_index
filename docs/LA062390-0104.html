June 23, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Pact Reached on VDT Safety at Fresno Bee: The Fresno Bee has reached an agreement with the California Division of Occupational Safety and Health on a long-term safety plan to protect workers using computer terminals.

The plan will require a $750,000 investment. The Newspaper Guild filed a complaint last year after numerous Bee editorial staffers suffered wrist, arm and shoulder injuries resulting from their work at video display terminals. The Guild said as many as one-third of the editorial staff experienced problems.
