Language: English Article Type:BFN [Quotation marks as received] [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 28/2/1994 -- In an interview with daily COTIDIANUL, Romanian Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu said with reference to the previous statements on the possibility of making up a new government by March 1, 1994, that "the political dialogue continues. The final decision on a possible government reshuffle will depend also on these demarches that result from the political dialogue but, first, it will depend on the decision to make or not make a reshuffle and on establishing the persons to replace the present dignitaries. As long as I am the prime minister I am not starting from the idea of a reshuffle for the sake of reshuffle." Referring to the government's reform program, he said that "the strategy we have articulated, including the program of measures and actions for 1994 has an anti-crisis character. It has nothing new, actually stemming from the government's strategy. "Sure," he added, "it is a program of the (ruling) party of Social Democracy of Romania, on which all ministries worked, especially that most ministers are members of this party. Besides, the problem of a dialogue was posed and this seems very fair to me, with the parties that backed the government in 1993, concerning a string of objectives included in this program. The outlook differs in some respects. However, there is a point that unites us and on which we can agree, I mean especially the social protection."
