BFN [Text] Beijing, April 8 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman said here today that Japanese Prime Minister Morohiro Hosokawa's resignation is the internal affair of Japan. When asked to make a reaction to Hosokawa's resignation, the spokesman said: "We've taken note of this news. This is the internal affair of Japan. "Prime Minister Hosokawa has made precious efforts for promoting the development of the friendly relations between China and Japan. We hope that the Sino-Japanese relations will continue to develop steadily and soundly," he added.
