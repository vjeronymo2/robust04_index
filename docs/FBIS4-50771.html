BFN [By reporter Wan Chengcai (8001 2052 2088)] [Text] Vladivostok, 20 May (XINHUA) -- A formation of Chinese Navy ships led by Vice Admiral Wang Jiying, commander of China's North Sea Fleet, satisfactorily concluded a four-day goodwill visit to Russia. It left Vladivostok for home on the morning of 20 May. During the formation's visit, Admiral Kasatonov, first deputy commander in chief of the Russian Navy, (Hermeilinov), acting commander in chief of the Pacific Ocean Fleet, and persons in charge of the administrations of Russia's coastal and border areas and Vladivostok met with Wang Jiying and the key personnel of his party. Wang Jiying and the key personnel of his party also visited the Pacific Ocean Fleet's ships and the Pacific Ocean Naval Academy. When Wang Jiying and (Hermeilinov) addressed a get-together held for Chinese and Russian naval officers and men aboard the "Changxingdao," command ship of the formation of ships of the Chinese Navy, they pointed out the visit improved the understanding and friendship between Chinese and Russian naval officers and men.
