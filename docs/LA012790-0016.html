January 27, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MOYNIHAN'S PLAN TO CUT PAYROLL TAX

Coming through the revolving door of deception now is President Bush's deja-voodoo economics.

He supports the shifting of surplus payroll tax revenue from the Social Security fund where it belongs, to the federal budget, where it doesn't -- a transparent fraud against low- and middle-income employees.

Let's have no more accusing elderly Social Security recipients of robbing younger workers.

Moynihan's "pay-as-you-go" proposal is the right and honest way to go.

PAULINE ROSS

Sherman Oaks
