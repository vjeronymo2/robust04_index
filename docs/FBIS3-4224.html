Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Nanjing, March 26 (XINHUA) -- The development zone in Yangzhou city, Jiangsu Province, has become a hot place for overseas investment. Business people from the United States, France, Brazil, Hong Kong and Taiwan are streaming into the city to set up enterprises and develop land. According to Qian Yurong, chairman of the Yangzhou Economic and Technological Development Zone, the zone has accommodated 56 foreign-funded enterprises over the past year since its founding. These enterprises are involved in the fields of metallurgy, light and textile industries, machinery, commerce and trade and infrastructure facilities. Ten of them already have gone into production. Located in the Chang Jiang River valley, the development zone is developing 9.8 square kilometers of areas in the first construction phase. So far infrastructure construction on one half of the area has been completed. Enterprises in the zone generated output value totalling 310 million yuan and 70 million yuan in profits and taxes last year.
