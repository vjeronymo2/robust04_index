BFN [Text] The Armenian Defense Ministry Press Service reports no incidents on the border since last night. But tension has increased in the southeast of the Karabakh war front. According to the information available to the press service of the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic Defense Army Headquarters this morning, following a fierce artillery barrage, the Azerbaijani Army launched an attack toward Fizuli. Many armored cars and infantry were thrown into the battle. The Karabakh forces stopped the enemy's attacks at 1100 today, inflicting considerable losses on it. Relative calm prevailed in the north of Mardakertskiy Rayon at noon today. Fierce battles have been going on for the past few days.
