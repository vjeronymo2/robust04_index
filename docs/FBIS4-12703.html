BFN [Text] Tehran, April 12, IRNA -- Iran's ambassador to Beirut Homayun 'Alizadeh and speaker of Lebanon's Supreme Shiite Council Shaykh Muhammad Mahdi Shams al-Din Monday discussed latest developments in the region, especially in Lebanon. The ambassador also submitted a message from Iran's Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati to the Lebanese parliamentarian. In his message Velayati congratulated Shams al-Din on his appointment as the speaker of the Council. After the meeting, the ambassador told the Lebanese television that Iran's stance on Arab-Israeli talks was inalterable and would not change. He added that the trend of regional developments had further proved the righteousness of Iran's stance towards the Middle East peace process.
