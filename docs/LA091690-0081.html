September 16, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

SOUTH COUNTY OR NORTH COUNTY FOR JAIL?

I noted from the responses to your editorial ("Time to Deal in Jail Realities," Aug. 22) that those most supportive of putting a jail on that site are from central and southern Orange County.

Those people are right; it is time for NIMBY to stop. Let's build the jail in South County. There is more unused land there than in the northern section.

Laughably, one South County resident asserted that people living near the jail will benefit from it. If this is the case, then he would surely be an advocate of placing the jail in his neighborhood (it could even be built on one of the greenbelts).

J.S. FISHER

Anaheim
