October 19, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: YORBA LINDA;

CURBSIDE RECYCLING GAINS COUNCIL OK

The City Council this week approved a plan to have citywide, curbside recycling by next spring.

Under a proposal by city staff and Yorba Linda Disposal, residents would be given a 110-gallon green bin for glass, paper, aluminum and plastic recyclables, to be collected biweekly, city officials said. The materials would be taken to a central location.
