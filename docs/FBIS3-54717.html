Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Leonid Kravchuk, the supreme commander of the Armed Forces and president of Ukraine, has sent New Year's greetings to the servicemen of the independent special battalion of Ukraine in the Sarajevo sector. The state attributes great significance to your peace-making mission in the troubled region enveloped in the flames of war, says the message of greetings. The whole of Ukraine is following the events in former Yugoslavia with anxiety and unremitting attention. It is proud of the courage and restraint with which the soldiers of the Ukrainian battalion perform their humane duty. Excellent actions by the Ukrainian battalion aimed at achieving peace on the long-suffering land, the message further reads, help establish our state on the world scene and promote the solution of many problems facing Ukraine in international relations. The supreme commander of the Armed Forces of Ukraine wished the officers and soldiers of the battalion happiness, health, balance, and courage in their difficult and responsible service. A New Year's message addressed to the same battalion has been sent by Army General Vitaliy Radetskyy, the Ukrainian defense minister.
