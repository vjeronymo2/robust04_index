Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] A drug-trafficking network led by a Colombian man was broken over the past few days. According to the head of the Information and Joint Coordination Center [CICC], Colonel Antoine Atouriste, the head of the drug network, Juan Alberto Ramirez (Azurio), reportedly linked to the Cali cartel, was arrested on 11 February at his home in (Pegguy-Ville). Five kg of cocaine, weapons, ammunition, and $100,000 were seized in Ramirez (Azurio)'s home. Two other men from Colombia, two from The Bahamas, and one from the Dominican Republic, linked to this network, which is considered the most active in recent months in Haiti, were also apprehended in the north of the country and in Port-au-Prince, according to the head of the CICC.
