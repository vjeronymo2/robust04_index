Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] On the eve of his visit to China, Russian Minister of Foreign Affairs Andrey Kozyrev has said that normalization of relations with China is among the main priorities of Russian foreign policy. PRENSA LATINA cited Kozyrev as stating that the development of bilateral relations with Beijing is based on the two countries' interests and that it strengthens the efforts in the quest of international stability. Kosyrev said: Ties between Moscow and Beijing must be based on the principles of friendship and cooperation between two world powers. According to ITAR-TASS, Kozyrev is making a working visit to the Kyrgyz capital before leaving for China.
