940423 FT 23 APR 94 / UK Company News: Welpac cuts loss and calls for Pounds 2.7m Welpac, a hardware and gardening products maker, yesterday announced a reduction in losses for the year ended January 31 1994 and announced a Pounds 2.7m cash raising plan to expand. Losses before interest on continuing activities were reduced from Pounds 2.11m to Pounds 0.27m and at the pre-tax level the deficit was Pounds 0.98m compared with Pounds 2.98m. The losses were caused by problems at Harwood Hardware and at Anderson and Firmin. Turnover rose by 18.7 per cent to Pounds 19.7m reflecting the core business and Harwood acquired in March 1992. Gross margin on continuing operations rose from 25.4 per cent to 30.6 per cent The placing and open offer is at 25p per share. Holders registered on April 13 may subscribe for shares on the basis of 17 new shares for 40 held. There is again no dividend. The loss per share was 3.5p.
