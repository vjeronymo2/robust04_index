Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Nazran February 7 TASS -- Ruslan Aushev, president of Ingushetia, a tiny Caucasian republic of Russia, said at a press conference on Monday [7 February] that he would not run for local president at the upcoming elections scheduled for February 27. Monday is the last day for registration of candidates for the post of the president and vice-president of Ingushetia. Aushev was elected a member of the upper chamber of the Russian parliament, the Federation Council, last December 12. His republic is in an ethnic and territorial conflict with neighbouring North Ossetia.
