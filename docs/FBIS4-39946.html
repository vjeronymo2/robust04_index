BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gadzhi Shamov] [Text] Makhachkala June 11 TASS -- The parliament of Dagestan, a republic in southern Russia, adopted a new Constitution at an emergency session on Saturday. The new Constitution provides for a 120-member one-house legislature, a State Council -- supreme body of executive power -- consisting of 14 people, one from each of 14 ethnic groups inhabiting the republic, a government accountable to the State Council, and a Constitutional Assembly, half of which will consist of parliament deputies and the other half of representatives of local councils. The Constitutional Assembly will have the right to elect members of the State Council and adopt amendments to the Constitution.
