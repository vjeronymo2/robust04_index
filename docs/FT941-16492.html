940110 FT 10 JAN 94 / Letters to the Editor: Smoky tunnel Sir, Lex ('Eurotunnel', January 5) wonders whether Eurotunnel will be able to generate new cross-channel traffic that no-one had predicted. I imagine that its London-to-Paris trains will attract lots of new business from smokers such as me who have been told by British Airways that they are no longer welcome on board. Daniel Moylan, Egan Associates, 7 Kensington High Street, London W8
