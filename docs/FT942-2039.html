940621 FT 21 JUN 94 / International Company News: Kymmene posts turnround Kymmene, the Finnish forestry group, returned a FM176m (Dollars 32.5m) profit after financial items in the four months to the end of April, a sharp turnround from a FM164m loss (restated) made in the same period last year, writes Hugh Carnegie. Sales moved up to FM5.94bn from FM5.64bn, while group operating profit reached FM604m, compared with FM491m last time. Net financial expenses fell to FM460m from FM657m.
