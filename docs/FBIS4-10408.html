BFN [Editorial Report] San Jose LA REPUBLICA in Spanish on 24 March on page 12A reports that public sector participation in the loan market dropped from 43 percent to 20 percent during 1993, leaving the other 80 percent to the private sector. Jorge Corrales, president of the Central Bank of Costa Rica, reported that services received the larger portion of the funds, thus showing a decrease in primary and secondary activities. He explained that this behavior is consistent with current economic trends, where services tend to show the greatest movement.
