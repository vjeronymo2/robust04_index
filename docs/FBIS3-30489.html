Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Skopje, 3 Feb (TANJUG) -- The Czech Republic informed the former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia today that the government in Prague has reached a decision on the establishment of full diplomatic relations with Macedonia at ambassadorial level, it was announced in Skopje today. Commenting on the news disclosed in Moscow on the establishment of diplomatic relations at the level of embassies between the Russian Federation and Macedonia, Russian envoy in Skopje Yuriy Petrovich said in a statement for Skopje Radio that Russia would mediate in the Macedonian-Greek dispute if asked to do so.
