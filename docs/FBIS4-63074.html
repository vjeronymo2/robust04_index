BFN [Report by Valeriy Kovalev under the "News" rubric: "Talks on Future of Radar Station"] [Text] A meeting between Russian and Belorussian [Belarusian] Defense Ministry delegations opened in Minsk on 19 May. One of the main questions being discussed is the future of the early warning radar station for detecting missile attacks near the city of Baranovichi. The Russian side is interested in using it even after the Russian troops pull out from Belorussia.
