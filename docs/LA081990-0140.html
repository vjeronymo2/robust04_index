August 19, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

FREEWAY REPAIRS;

ON THE ROAD;

CALTRANS ISSUES ADVISORY ON THIS WEEK'S REPAIR SITES SO MOTORISTS CAN STEER CLEAR

Caltrans advises motorists that it plans the following work on freeways and major streets in the Southeast and Long Beach area this week. Repair locations listed below are keyed to the nearest city or cities and are spotted on the map by number.

ARTESIA FREEWAY (91)

1. Bellflower: Eastbound No. 1, 2 and 3 lanes; at Lakewood Boulevard; today, 7 to 11 a.m.; bridge repair.

HARBOR FREEWAY (110)

2. Carson: Southbound No. 1 lane; 223rd Street to Pacific Coast Highway; Monday, 9:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.; raised markers.

3. Carson: Southbound No. 1 lane; Torrance Boulevard to 223rd Street; Tuesday, 9:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.; raised markers.

4. Carson: Northbound No. 1 lane; Sepulveda Boulevard to Torrance Boulevard; Wednesday, 9:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.; raised markers.

5. Carson: Northbound No. 1 lane; Pacific Coast Highway to Sepulveda Boulevard; Thursday, 9:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m.; raised markers.

LAKEWOOD BOULEVARD (19)

6. Bellflower/Lakewood: Northbound and southbound alternating lanes; Compton Boulevard to Artesia Boulevard; today, 5:30 a.m. to noon; stenciling.

SAN GABRIEL RIVER FREEWAY (605)

8. Pico Rivera: Southbound No. 1 lane; Peck Road to Beverly Boulevard; Tuesday, 8:30 a.m. to 1 p.m.; crack seal repair.

9. Pico Rivera: Southbound No. 1 lane; Rose Hills Road to Whittier Boulevard; Wednesday, 8:30 a.m. to 1 p.m.; crack seal repair.

12. Santa Fe Springs: Connectors from the southbound San Gabriel River Freeway (605) to the northbound and southbound Santa Ana Freeway (5); today, 5:30 a.m. to noon; mudjacking.

13. Various Cities: Northbound No. 1 and 2 lanes; Telegraph Road to Peck Road; today, 6 a.m. to noon; raised markers.

14. Downey: Connector from the northbound Santa Ana Freeway (5) to the southbound San Gabriel River Freeway (605); today, 5:30 a.m. to noon; mudjacking.
