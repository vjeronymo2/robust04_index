BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Meetings to be attended by the foreign ministers of NATO as well as the North Atlantic Cooperation Council -- members of the former East Bloc -- will begin in Istanbul on 9 June. NATO Deputy Secretary General Sergio Balanzino, who will attend the meeting, arrived in Istanbul today. Balanzino will chair the meetings on behalf of NATO Secretary General Manfred Woerner, who is unable to arrive in Turkey because of illness. The alliance's relations with the former East Bloc countries, especially with Russia, constitutes the main item on the agenda of the NATO foreign ministers meeting to be held on 9 June. The meeting will also focus on the war in Bosnia-Herzegovina and other regional and international issues. The foreign ministers of NATO and former East Bloc countries will meet on 10 June. The foreign ministers of some 40 countries are expected to attend this meeting.
