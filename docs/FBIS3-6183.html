Language: Korean Article Type:BFN ["AP, YONHAP report from Beijing"] [Text] The North Korean side admitted on 15 March that there were some disputes regarding the International Atomic Energy Agency's [IAEA] method of inspecting its nuclear facilities, but revealed that such friction can be resolved through dialogue. (Choe Han-chun), councillor of the DPRK Embassy in Beijing, said that the inspection team demanded that it take samples from one facility, but the North Korean authorities rejected this move, calling it an unjust demand. Councillor Choe said that there was a slight dispute in the process of inspection, but that it is not serious. He also claimed that this can be resolved through dialogue between North Korea and the IAEA.
