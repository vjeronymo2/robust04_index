BFN [Text] A goodwill delegation led by Mr. Sawit Phothiwihok, minister in the Thai Prime Minister's Office, paid a courtesy call on Vice Admiral Maung Maung Khin, member of the State Law and Order Restoration Council and deputy prime minister, at the deputy prime minister's guest hall in the Secretariat Building at 1100 this morning. During the meeting, they held cordial and frank discussions on matters concerning bilateral economic cooperation, energy, and improving and strengthening our relationship.
