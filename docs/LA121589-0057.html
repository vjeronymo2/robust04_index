December 15, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Petition for 'Homeroom': Parents and teachers in Watts have vowed to petition ABC to save "Homeroom," a canceled half-hour comedy series they say helps keep their kids in school. "All we're saying is give the show a chance," said Jim Goins, who counsels school dropouts in the Watts area. "There's not that many shows of color that deal with educational issues, and we're in a dilemma right now as far as having black male role models," Goins said. "Homeroom" stars Darryl Sivad as a man who gave up a lucrative job in advertising to teach at an inner-city school. SHAUNA SNOW
