931203 FT 03 DEC 93 / Silverdale miners accept closure Miners at the Silverdale colliery in Staffordshire have voted to accept closure and take redundancy payments of up to Pounds 44,000. Members of the National Union of Mineworkers voted not to fight the planned closure. Miners at Staffordshire's other pit, Littleton, voted yesterday on whether to accept its planned closure. The result is likely today.
