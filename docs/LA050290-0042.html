May 2, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

NIBBLES AND BITS

More than 100 works by Orange County artists will be auctioned off Saturday evening when the Arts Associates of Huntington Beach sponsor a fund-raiser, "Portfolio '90," at the Huntington Beach Civic Center, 2000 Main St., from 6 to 10:30 p.m. There will be live entertainment, a Champagne buffet supper donated by local restaurants, and a ceremony at which "visual artist of the year," "performing artist of the year" and "volunteer service to the arts" awards will be presented by Mayor Tom Mays. Donor tickets are $25 per person and Patron tickets are $125 per couple.

Proceeds will benefit numerous arts programs in the city including a scholarship program, children's workshops, annual juried exhibitions, Arts on the Green and the Family Concert Series. Reservations: Caroline Browning at (714) 846-2855. Information: The Arts Associates at (714) 842-9185.
