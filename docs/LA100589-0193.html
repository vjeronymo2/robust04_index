October 5, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: ORANGE;

MAN, 76, DIES AFTER BEING STRUCK BY CAR

A 76-year-old man was struck and killed Wednesday morning while crossing Hardy Street.

Dave Hill, a Police Department spokesman, said Fred Miller of Orange was walking eastbound across Hardy Street when he was struck by a white 1986 Toyota Celica.

Miller, who suffered head and internal injuries, was rushed to United Western Medical Center-Santa Ana, where he was pronounced dead about 3 p.m.

"The impact with the vehicle caused him to go through the windshield," Hill said.

Hill said police questioned the driver of the car, but no charges have been filed.
