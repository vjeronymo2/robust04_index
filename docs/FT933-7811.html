930819 FT 19 AUG 93 / Slow German economic growth predicted GERMAN economic recovery is likely to be slow and hesitant, with growth of 1.4 per cent in all-German GDP next year, compared with a decline of 1.9 per cent in the current year, says the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development. For the west German economy alone, the organisation expects a year-on-year real GDP decline of 2.5 per cent this year, followed by an increase of just 1.0 per cent in 1994. Report, Page 2
