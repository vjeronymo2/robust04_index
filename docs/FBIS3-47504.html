Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 13 (KCNA) -- The spokesman of the General Federation of National Peasants Associations of South Korea (Chonnong) at a recent press conference denounced traitor Kim Yong- sam's policy of killing farmers, according to a report of a South Korean newspaper. He said Kim Yong-sam's promise in his election "commitments" to adopt a policy for the farmers was a lie to fool them. Noting that Kim Yong-sam, disclosing his true color, has taken today the road of totally sacrificing agriculture and farmers with the measure of rice market opening, he declared that Chonnong would resolutely turn out in the struggle to defend "our rice, our agriculture".
