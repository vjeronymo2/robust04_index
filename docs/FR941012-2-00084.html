National Institute on Alcohol Abuse and Alcoholism; Amended Notice of Meeting Notice is hereby given of a change in the meeting of the Immunology and AIDS Subcommittee of the Alcohol Biomedical Research Review Committee, National Institute on Alcohol Abuse and Alcoholism, November 9&hyph;10, 1994, Holiday Inn Bethesda, 8120 Wisconsin Avenue, Bethesda, MD 20814, which was published in the Federal Register on September 26, 1994, (59 RF 49079).
