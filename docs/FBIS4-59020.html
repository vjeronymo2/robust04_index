BFN [Text] Morocco's King Hassan has urged Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin to pursue the peace process and expressed his belief that Israelis and Palestinians can coexist and create a new Middle East. These remarks are included in a message sent by the king to the prime minister via Knesset Member Rafi Edri, who visited Morocco last weekend. Our correspondent Yo'el Nir reports that Moroccan and Israeli representatives are currently discussing the establishment of economic representations in Jerusalem and Rabat, the opening of direct aviation routes, and the establishment of telephone and postal services between the two countries.
