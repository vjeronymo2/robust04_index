931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / International Company News: Koito lists dimmer pre-tax results KOITO, a leading Japanese maker of automobile lighting equipment, reported interim parent pre-tax profit 6.1 per cent lower at Y1.94bn (Dollars 18.13m), compared with Y2.06bn a year ago, AP-DJ reports from Tokyo. Net profit was 5.5 per cent lower at Y896m.
