921128 FT 28 NOV 92 / Worries among small businesses A STUDY of small businesses' relationships with banks by the Mintel research group indicated yesterday that most do not think bank charges unreasonable, but that arrangement fees and fees for minor administrative services caused much irritation. It said some businesses felt the small-business centres established by several banks to handle loans had meant a downgrading of their relationship with the bank. Small Business Finance. Mintel International, 18-19 Long Lane, London EC1A 9HE.
