Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] A political forum took place at the Visa Lodge on 11 March with the participation of political parties and organizations opposed to the return to Haiti of President Aristide. These organizations revealed their intention to form a political front to fill the current power vacuum. Discussions were held on the formation of an ad hoc government or a government of public safety. Following this meeting on 11 March, a nine-member committee in charge of preparing a resolution was established.
