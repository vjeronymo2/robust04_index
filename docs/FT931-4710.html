930310 FT 10 MAR 93 / Claim struck out MERCHANT bankers Henry Ansbacher and Co yesterday succeeded in striking out a Pounds 25m High Court damages claim arising from the Guinness scandal. Dr Mohammed Marwan, an investment client, had alleged breach of trust against the bank for buying 1.9m Guinness shares for him as part of the share support scheme to aid the 1986 takeover of Distillers.
