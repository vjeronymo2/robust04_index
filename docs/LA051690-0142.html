May 16, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

TUSTIN'S CARE ENTERPRISES TAKES A LOSS

Care Enterprises Inc., the Tustin-based company which owns and leases nursing homes, posted a loss of $686,000 for the first quarter ended March 31, contrasted with earnings of $273,000 for the first quarter of 1989.

Revenue fell 9% to $60.4 million from $66.4 million. The company attributed the revenue decline to changes in the Medicare program that have reduced the number of Medicare patients at the company's facilities and also to a decline in the number of facilities operated by the company.

Care Enterprises operates 79 facilities with 8,046 beds during the latest quarter, compared with 94 facilities and 9,707 beds a year ago.
