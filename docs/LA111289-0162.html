November 12, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

PREP FOOTBALL ROUNDUP

In another Olympic League game:

Orange Lutheran 26, Brethren 10 -- Phil Nemeth scored 19 points to lead host Orange Lutheran (3-7 overall, 1-5 in league play). Nemeth, filling in at tailback for injured Gil Green, had rushing touchdowns of one and two yards to go with two field goals (38 and 37 yards) and an extra point.
