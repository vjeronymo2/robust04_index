September 30, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

THIS WEEK

ONE NIGHT IN BANGKOK: The Long Beach Civic Light Opera will open its 1990-91 season with a new version of Tim Rice's international hit musical "Chess," beginning Thursday and running through Oct. 21 at the Terrace Theatre in Long Beach.

SECURITY SHOW: Watergate figure G. Gordon Liddy will be a special guest at the Security Show to be held Wednesday through Friday at the Long Beach Arena.
