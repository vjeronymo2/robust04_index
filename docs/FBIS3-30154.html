Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 26 (KCNA) -- The international department of the Central Committee [C.C.] of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] arranged a friendly gathering at Mongnan House on February 25 with the head of the International Liaison Department of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China [CPC] and her companions visiting Korea. Present on invitation were head of the International Liaison Department of the C.C., the CPC Li Shuzheng and her companions, Chinese Ambassador to Korea Qiao Zonghuai and embassy officials. Present were Secretary of the WPK Central Committee Hwang Chang-yop, DPRK Ambassador to China Chu Chang-chun, vice-directors of a department of the WPK Central Committee Kim Yang-kon and Chi Chae-yong and officials concerned. Artistes in Pyongyang gave a performance at the gathering. The performance was acclaimed by the audience for its high ideological and artistic level. It was preceded by a party. Hwang Chang-yop and Li Shuzheng made speeches at the party.
