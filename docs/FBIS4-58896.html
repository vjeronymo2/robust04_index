BFN [Text] Today, President Hashemi-Rafsanjani in a telephone conversation with German Chancellor Helmut Kohl talked to him about the latest situation in Bosnia-Herzegovina and ways of expanding political and economic relations between Iran and Germany. In this telephone conversation, the president expressed his anxiety about the repetition of the tragedy of Gorazde by the Serbs and stressed the need for a decisive and serious approach by the countries of the world and international organizations toward the Serb aggressors. Mr. Hashemi-Rafsanjani also expressed his hope that the two countries will have a more active participation in the second economic and political development plan of Iran. Mr. Kohl also said that the exchange of political and economic delegations between the two countries to study the issue of Bosnia-Herzegovina and find new ways for the expansion of cooperation between the two countries has always enjoyed the support and attention of the German Government.
