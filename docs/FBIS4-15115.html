It has become common for the UN to use a dual approach toward resolving international conflicts, the letter reads. The UN is becoming a conductor of wishes of a narrow group of states with the United States at the top. The Slavic Assembly demanded that violent interference in the life of the peoples of Yugoslavia be halted.
