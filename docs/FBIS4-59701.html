BFN [Text] The security forces are investigating the incident in which 19 Arab residents and three border policemen were injured in Hebron this afternoon. The incident started when Jewish residents were walking through Policeman's Square in town and came under a hail of rocks. They shot at the stone throwers, wounding nine Arabs. IDF [Israel Defense Forces] patrols that arrived at the site were also pelted with rocks. They were forced to shoot in an attempt to disperse the stone throwers, and apparently wounded 10 residents. The IDF detained two yeshiva students from the region for questioning about the shooting. The city of Hebron was declared closed military zone.
