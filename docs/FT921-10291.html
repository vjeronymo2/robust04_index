920207 FT 07 FEB 92 / 'Desert Rats' present their 'Gulf babies' Parents on parade: Soldiers of the 7th British Armoured Division, the 'Desert Rats', present their 'Gulf babies' nine months after returning from war. Normally their base at Fallingbostel, Germany, sees 12 births a month. But in December there were 60, in January 77 and another 40 are expected this month.
