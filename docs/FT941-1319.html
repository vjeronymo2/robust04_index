940325 FT 25 MAR 94 / UK Company News: BNB Resources Turnover of BNB Resources, the recruitment and consulting concern, rose from Pounds 62.95m to Pounds 70.12m for 1993, while pre-tax profits surged to Pounds 2.59m, against Pounds 712,000. Earnings per share were 8.7p (3p), but the dividend is unchanged at 4.9p with a same-again 3.3p final.
