BFN [Report by 'Abd-al-Nabi 'Abd-al-Sattar] [Text] Sudan has asked the Arab League to intervene immediately to ease the current tension in Egyptian-Sudanese relations. Khartoum has urged Arab countries, particularly Libya, Syria, and the PLO, to work toward achieving reconciliation between Cairo and Khartoum. Sudan has also sent a memorandum to the Egyptian Foreign Ministry on the Hala'ib triangle.
