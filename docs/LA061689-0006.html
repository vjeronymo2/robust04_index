June 16, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

AID TO ISRAEL

How atrocious, how gruesome, how heartbreaking it was to watch Chinese people being murdered for the "crime" of wanting more independence, more democracy. Our President, expressing the outrage of the people of this country, has declared military sanctions against the government that has committed this atrocity.

Why, then, does the U.S. continue to send military aid to a government that ruthlessly and relentlessly murders a people who are also guilty of the "crime" of wanting their independence? That ruthless country is Israel and the victims are the Palestinians.

PHYLLIS SOLOMON

Long Beach
