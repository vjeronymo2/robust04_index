BFN [Text] The prime minister, Ms. Benazir Bhutto, has expressed concern over the missile program launched by India. Talking to newsmen in the Parliament House today, she said Pakistan is watching the situation closely and everything necessary will be done. Replying to a question, Ms. Benazir Bhutto, said the character assassination campaign launched by the opposition against the government is a conspiracy hatched by capitalists. She said we are ready for negotiations but blackmail cannot be allowed. Referring to allegations leveled by the opposition against the president, the prime minister said this is pure forgery and the government is considering taking strict action against those responsible. The prime minister said the government will expose the antipeople elements who are undermining the democratic system.
