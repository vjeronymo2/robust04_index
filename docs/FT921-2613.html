920318 FT 18 MAR 92 / UK Company News: B Elliott agrees bank facility B Elliott, the machine tool and engineering company, has reached agreement with its bankers for a revised credit facility, writes Richard Gourlay. As part of the facility, the group has agreed to suspend all preference and convertible dividend payments. Sir Norman Fowler, the former cabinet minister who joined Elliott as non-executive director last July, resigned on March 13. The group, which has watched its shares fall from 80p to less than 10p in the past year, has recently been the subject of bid speculation.
