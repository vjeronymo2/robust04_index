Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tallinn, Jan 26, BNS -- The Estonian Economics Ministry in its activity abides by a government ruling from Oct. 25, 1993, which says the country's two major distilleries are to remain in state ownership, Economics Minister Toivo Jurgenson told BNS. The minister was commenting on reports published by the ARIPAEV weekly newspaper, which said the Liviko company and the Moe distillery could be included in the list of companies for privatization. He said the ministry was not going to bring up the issue at current discussions.
