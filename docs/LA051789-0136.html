May 17, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

ROGER REES TEMPORARILY OUT OF 'HAPGOOD' ROLE

Roger Rees, in the cast of "Hapgood" at the Doolittle Theatre, has returned to his home in England after a death in his family. He will resume his role "hopefully by next week," said a spokesman for the production.

Understudy Bryan Torfeh will play the part in Rees' absence. Tickets may be exchanged for a later date at the point of purchase.
