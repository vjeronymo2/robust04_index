Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Seoul, March 5 (YONHAP) -- The supreme public prosecutor summoned the chairman of the National Agricultural Cooperatives Federation (NACF), Han Ho-son, Friday night and questioned him overnight about allegedly embezzling part of a 300 million won slush fund he had set aside from the budget for an NACF branch. The prosecutor's office is expected to seek an arrest warrant for Han, 58, Saturday after further questioning him on the alleged misappropriation of the slush fund, its exact amount and its use. Officials at the prosecutor's office said Han built up the slush fund by misappropriating NACF budgets set for local branches between April 1991 and October 1992. The officials said their investigation showed that the fund amounted to 300 million won, of which Han allegedly embezzled 180 million won.
