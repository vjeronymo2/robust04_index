BFN [Text] Rabat, June 23 (MAP) -- Iran's Embassy here denied Thursday involvement in the arms seized lately by Moroccan police and which were destined to the Algerian banned Islamic Salvation Front (FIS). The Government of Iran has nothing to do with the arms case, the Iranian Embassy said noting that Iran in line with its foreign policy principles does not interfere in domestic affairs of other countries. An eight-member ring (including six Moroccans and two Moroccans) [as received] were dismantled lately by Moroccan police while attempting to smuggle arms purchased in Europe to FIS via Morocco. The ring members were sentenced to imprisonment terms varying from 5 to 20 years.
