940205 FT 05 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Fleming American shows improvement The fully-diluted net asset value per share of The Fleming American Investment Trust stood at 326.4p at the December 31 year end, against 279.3p a year earlier. Gross revenue increased from Pounds 4.12m to Pounds 6.06m. Available revenue grew from Pounds 514,000 to Pounds 1.28m. Earnings per share improved to 1.89p (0.73p) and the final dividend is being raised to 0.65p (0.35p) making a total for the year of 1.5p (0.7p).
