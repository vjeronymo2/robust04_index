941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / International Company News: Alcan back to the black in third quarter Strong US and European demand for fabricated products, better pricing and more rationalisation moved Alcan Aluminium, the Canadian aluminium group, firmly into the black for the third quarter and first nine months. Third-quarter net profit was USDollars 66m, or 27 cents a common share, against a loss of Dollars 13m, or 8 cents, a year earlier. The quarter included a special after-tax gain of Dollars 27m mainly on the sale of Alcan Australia. Revenues were Dollars 2.2bn, up 21 per cent. Nine months' net profit was Dollars 48m, or 15 cents, against a loss of Dollars 68m, or 37 cents, a year earlier. Revenues were Dollars 6.1bn, up 12 per cent.
