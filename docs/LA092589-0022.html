September 25, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: RADIO

Talk radio station KABC-AM will make some changes in its lineup next week, once the baseball season is over and there are no more Dodger games to broadcast. Bill Press and Bill Pearl will take calls from listeners and comment on the day's events on the new show, "The Dueling Bills," Monday through Friday from 3-4 p.m. The double Bills replace Wink Martindale and Bill Smith, starting next Monday. As previously announced, Superfan (Ed Bieler) will host a daily sports call-in show from 4-8 p.m. Veteran radio personality Dick Whittington will take on the 11 a.m.-2 p.m. Sunday hours, with former KLSX morning deejay Peter Tilden anchoring from 2-4 p.m. Whittington and Tilden will be paired Saturdays from 1-5 p.m. Behind the scenes, Wally Sherwin, program director of KABC Talkradio for 8 1/2 years, returned to the station this week as executive producer of on-air programming. Claudia Puig
