Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (March 7) TURAN -- Yesterday there was the meeting of the representatives of leading opposition parties and movements of the republic, such as Musavat, the Popular [People's] Front, Democratic Party, People's Party, Ana Torpag and others. The meeting took place in the headquarter of Azerbaijan Democratic Party. Press service of Azerbaijan Democratic Party told TURAN that the participants of the meeting discussed the situation in Azerbaijan and decided to establish cooperation among the parties and to consolidate all democratic forces of the country. The same source reports that the resolution concerning formation of "Democratic Congress" has been adopted. The Congress will unite all democratic organizations of the country.
