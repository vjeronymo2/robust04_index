Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sofia, February 16 (BTA) -- Neither U.S. nor NATO aircraft are being refuelled in Bulgaria, the Bulgarian Air Force Command stated through the Press Office of the Ministry of Defence in response to reports in today's press. The independent KONTINENT daily wrote today that "in the dead of the night, between 2 and 4 AM, NATO aircraft are refuelled at Sofia Airport." According to the report, inhabitants of residential districts close to the airport said that "in the past two weeks the roar of turboprop engines has become unbearable," thus confirming the information of an unnamed source.
