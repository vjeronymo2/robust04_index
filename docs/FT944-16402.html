941013 FT 13 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Brandon expands Brandon Hire, the Bristol-based hirer of power tools, catering equipment and marquees, has acquired Beechwood, a supplier of tools for hire and sale. Consideration is Pounds 1.82m, of which Pounds 500,000 will be satisfied in shares, with the balance in cash. The vendor has undertaken to retain the shares for a minimum of 12 months. The Beechwood business, established in 1974, operates from seven depots in south Wales and one in Bristol. It made profits before interest, tax and directors' remuneration of Pounds 166,000 on sales of Pounds 2.53m in the year to end-March. The value of the assets being acquired is Pounds 632,000.
