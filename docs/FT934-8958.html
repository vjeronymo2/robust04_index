931112 FT 12 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Channel Holdings at Pounds 808,000 CHANNEL Holdings achieved pre-tax profits of Pounds 808,000 for the six months to September 30. That compared with Pounds 191,000 for the half year to December 31 1992, while for the 15 months to March 31 1993 there were profits of Pounds 308,000. The results include a full contribution from Carflow Products (UK) a supplier of mechanical vehicle security products, and a maiden contribution from CQR Security Products, a manufacturer of components for intruder alarms. The acquisitions added Pounds 5.94m to total turnover of Pounds 7.66m (Pounds 1.03m) and Pounds 511,000 to operating profits of Pounds 805,000 (Pounds 182,000). The directors said both companies performed well, achieving 'good growth in the UK and export markets.' They expected cash generation to remain strong for the rest of the financial year. Earnings per share worked through at 1.7p (1p) adjusted and a maiden interim dividend of 0.5p is being paid - 1p was paid for the preceding 15 months.
