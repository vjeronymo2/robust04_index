Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] RIGA, March 11 (LETA) -- The cabinet passed regulations on additional powers of the state police and the national volunteer guards, allowing them to carry out checks on vehicles drivers and passengers on grounded suspicion. The legal commissions of the Saeima [parliament] at the plenary session Thursday [10 March] recommended to annul the regulations as not conforming to the norms of the code of criminal procedure and administrative offense code. The deputies decided the regulations by the cabinet are no longer valid.
