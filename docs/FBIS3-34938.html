Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Baghdad, 15 Feb (INA) -- The Arab Socialist Ba'th Party and the Yemeni Revolutionary Democratic Party today signed a program of action and cooperation to enhance and cement relations of brotherliness, steadfastness, and friendship between the two friendly parties and peoples. The program was signed for the Ba'th Party by 'Abd-al-Razzaq al-Hashimi, chief of the Ba'th Party's Foreign Relations Bureau, and for the Yemeni party by Muhammad 'Abdallah al-Dughaysh, the party's secretary general. The program urges coordinating efforts at Arab and international forums and conferences and educating Arab and world public opinion about the serious, tragic effects of the crime of the unjust embargo against the Iraqi people and using all means to lift it. The program provides for exchanging visits and expertise to ensure continued communication between the two parties in the interest of common pan-Arab struggle and the Arab nation's objectives of liberation and progress. The program provides for supporting the Arab Popular Forces Conference and contributing to its activities and decisions.
