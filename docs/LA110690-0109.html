November 6, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

ON THE RIGHT COURSE

Los Angeles City Councilman Ernani Bernardi's support of uninterrupted play on the three Sepulveda golf courses is applauded by members of the Sepulveda Men's Golf Club.

Our 800-member club has for a long time urged the use of Tillman reclaimed water to irrigate these courses in an effort to alleviate a four-year drought. Tearing up the courses to lay water mains to carry Tillman water can be self-defeating. Reduced fees for shortened courses could cost the city about as much as routing those pipes around them.

Keep up the good fight.

SAM BENSUSSEN, Sherman Oaks. Bensussen is secretary of the Sepulveda Men's Golf Club.
