November 3, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

LOVE'S CHRONOLOGY

In his Oct. 25 piece, Sean Mitchell described a romance between a 20-year-old man and a "35ish" woman in a Peter Falk film as a "May/December" affair. Would he have used that terminology if the woman were 20 and the man 35?

VIRGINIA M. DONOHUE, Los Angeles
