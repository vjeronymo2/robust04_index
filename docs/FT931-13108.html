930126 FT 26 JAN 93 / International Company News: Hanson and Santa Fe agree asset swap SANTA FE PACIFIC, the US railroad and minerals group, and Hanson, the UK-based conglomerate, last night formally signed an agreement swapping major mineral assets, writes Nikki Tait in New York. The deal was first revealed two weeks ago and involves assets worth around Dollars 500m on each side. Under the agreement, Hanson is acquiring virtually all of Santa Fe's coal assets, including the Lee Ranch Coal Mine and undeveloped coal reserves located mainly in New Mexico. Santa Fe is taking over certain gold assets belonging to Hanson's Gold Fields Mining Company.
