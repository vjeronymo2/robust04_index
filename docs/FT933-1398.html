930924 FT 24 SEP 93 / Leading vineyard put on market ONE of the UK's largest vineyards, responsible for a 10th of the country's wine production, has been offered for sale at a price of about Pounds 1.5m. Lamberhurst Vineyards, near Tunbridge Wells, Kent, is being sold by Mr Kenneth McAlpine, a director of the Sir Robert McAlpine construction company.
