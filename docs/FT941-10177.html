940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / International Company News: Saudi bank advances Saudi British Bank, 40 per cent owned by HSBC group, saw 1993 net profits rise 50 per cent to SR402.6m (Dollars 107m) from SR268.1m in 1992, writes Robin Allen in Kuwait. Total assets at end-1993 were SR22.8bn 10 per cent higher than a year earlier. Deposits were up 12.2 per cent at SR16.7bn, while the loan portfolio grew by SR1.2bn to SR8.1bn. Provisions have remained constant at SR46m for the last three years. Earnings per share were SR41.65 compared with an adjusted figure of SR33.52 in 1992, and the board is recommending a dividend of SR30 per share.
