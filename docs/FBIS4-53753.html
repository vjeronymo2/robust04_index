BFN [Text] Seri Begawan, May 22, IRNA -- Kamal Najjari, the first Iranian ambassador to Seri Begawan, Brunei, here Sunday submitted a message from the Foreign Minister, 'Ali Akbar Velayati, to the Foreign Minister of Brunei, Mohamed Bolkiah. The foreign minister of Brunei expressed pleasure with the opening of the Iranian embassy in Seri Begawan and observed that Brunei looks forward to broadening of relations with Iran. He also commented that Brunei wanted to benefit from Iran's experience in her first five-year national development plan (1989-94) as well as participate in the economic and development projects for the second national development plan.
