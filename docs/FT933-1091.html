930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Bilston & Battersea Because of increased investment in sales and marketing, Bilston & Battersea Enamels, a maker of hand decorated enamel boxes, suffered higher pre-tax losses of Pounds 186,000 for the first six months of 1993, compared with Pounds 28,000. Turnover of this USM-quoted concern rose slightly to Pounds 1.78m (Pounds 1.76m). Losses per share were 2.7p (0.8p).
