Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [By reporter Zhou Xierong (0719 6043 5554)] [Text] Shanghai, 26 Mar (XINHUA) -- ROK President Kim Yong-sam arrived in Shanghai this afternoon by a special plane from Tokyo to begin a five-day official visit to China at the invitation of President Jiang Zemin. Wu Jichuan, chairman of the Chinese Government reception committee and minister in charge of the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, made a special trip to Shanghai to greet Kim Yong-sam on behalf of President Jiang Zemin. This evening, Shanghai Mayor Huang Ju met with Kim Yong-sam and his party and hosted a banquet in their honor at the New Jinjiang Hotel. This is Kim Yong-sam's first visit to China after he assumed the presidency. During the meeting, Kim Yong-sam said he is glad that, as China's largest economic center, Shanghai is the first leg of his visit to China. Kim Yong-sam said: Though the ROK and China just established diplomatic relations more than a year ago, their friendly cooperation is enjoying further development in various fields. Last year, the two countries' bilateral trade volume exceeded $10 billion. A bigger growth in bilateral trade volume is predicted for this year. Kim Yong-sam also said he believed Shanghai can play a bigger role in its economic exchanges with the ROK. This afternoon, the ROK guests visited the site of the former provisional government of the ROK immediately after their arrival in Shanghai.
