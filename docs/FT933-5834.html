930901 FT 01 SEP 93 / UK Company News in Brief: In Shops IN SHOPS, the Birmingham-based property group, has received acceptances in respect of 7.69m shares or 85.7 per cent of the total amount subject to the open offer. Of the total under the offer 2.91m shares, representing the entitlement of the directors and DW Newman, were placed firm with institutions with the remaining 8.98m placed subject to recall.
