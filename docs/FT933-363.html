930929 FT 29 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Brammer placing to raise Pounds 5.7m Brammer, the industrial services group, is placing 2.1m new ordinary shares at 275p each to raise Pounds 5.7m. The proceeds will be used to assist in the funding of European investment opportunities and initially to reduce indebtedness. The new shares will represent about 4.7 per cent of the enlarged issued share capital. James Capel is broker to the issue and has underwritten it.
