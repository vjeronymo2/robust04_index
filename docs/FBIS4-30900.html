BFN [Text] Prague May 31 (CTK) -- The arms trading company Omnipol has opened negotiations with the Chilean military delegation headed by the former dictator Augusto Pinochet, currently chief of Chilean ground forces, Antonin Tomecek from the Omnipol Secretariat told CTK today. Pinochet left the Forum hotel, where he is staying, this morning shortly before 8:00 a.m. [0600 GMT]. The negotiations at which leading Omnipol representatives are present are taking place outside Prague, Tomecek said. Chilean Ambassador to Prague Rolando Byrgin Stein said he and Pinochet met when Pinochet arrived in Prague yesterday. Stein said it was unlikely that they would meet again before Pinochet leaves Prague, adding that he had no information about Pinochet's schedule.
