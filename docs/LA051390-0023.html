May 13, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

CURRENTS: A GUIDE TO THE BEST OF SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA: RITUALS;

CAFE AU LAIT BREAK

COFFEE IS for the morning, gulped quickly to wake us up.

Tea is best for two with talk and tiny sandwiches. Cafe au lait, steaming and strong in a thick white bowl that must be nurtured with both hands is the perfect solitary pleasure. With it, have the final indulgence -- orange-flavored biscotti, one side drenched in deep bittersweet chocolate.

Old Town Bakery, 166 W. Colorado Blvd., Pasadena; (818) 792-7943.
