Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] U.S. officials described the Greek positions on Skopje [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia--FYROM] as reasonable. Deputy Foreign Minister Yeoryios Papandreou made this statement upon his return from the United States early this afternoon. He had gone there to prepare for the prime minister's visit. Papandreou was visibly satisfied with his contacts and stressed that he saw that the United States is very willing to see the issue move toward a solution. The deputy foreign minister said that the Greek measures against Skopje operate as a catalyst and create some activity around the issue.
