940207 FT 07 FEB 94 / Company News (This Week): Unidanmark - Other companies The second largest Danish banking group is expected to move modestly back into the black when it publishes its 1993 figures today. In 1992 it made a whacking DKr4.66bn (Dollars 700m) loss, but last year's soaring bond and share prices should ensure a return to profit.
