July 3, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

'Twin Peaks' News: Fans of the quirky soap opera "Twin Peaks" can now do more than watch the show -- they can read the newsletter over cherry pie and coffee.

Steve Ryan of Fullerton has started Twin Peaks Gazette, a quarterly publication of the Twin Peaks Appreciation Society. He said the newsletter will be "devoted to news and information on the series and the work of David Lynch," as well as serve as a "forum for the exchange of information among subscribers." Subscriptions are $8 a year and available by writing Ryan at 1557 Avenida Selva, Fullerton, Calif. 92633. GREG BRAXTON
