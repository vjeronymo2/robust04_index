BFN [Excerpt] Aden, 26 June -- Northern Yemeni troops resumed their artillery shelling of Aden in the south this morning, thus breaching the six-hour long cease-fire declared last night. The Yemeni news agency Aden quoted a responsible source at the Defense Ministry in the self-declared Democratic Republic of Yemen as saying: Northern troops resumed their artillery shelling of vital economic targets in Aden at 0615 local time.
