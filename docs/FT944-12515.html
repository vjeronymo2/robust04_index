941101 FT 01 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Abtrust New Thai Abtrust New Thai Investment Trust had a net asset value per share of 210.12p at August 31 against 126.35p a year earlier and 179.08p at the February year-end. Fully diluted, the values were 192.99p, 122.5p and 165.9p respectively. Net revenue for the six months amounted to Pounds 219,887 (Pounds 98,857) for earnings of 1.46p (0.66p) per share.
