BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] A bomb exploded in front of a bakery in a square in Istanbul's Karagumruk quarter where Democratic Leftist Party leader Bulent Ecevit was supposed to addressed an open air meeting today. According to initial reports, five persons were injured in the explosion. A Turkish Radio and Television correspondent was informed by various eyewitnesses that the bomb, which was left in front of a bakery in Karagumruk's Pazarici Square, exploded around 1700 injuring five persons. The windows of various building were also shattered in the explosion. Ecevit was expected to address an open air meeting in the square around 1700. He had not yet arrived when the explosion occurred.
