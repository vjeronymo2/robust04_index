920625 FT 25 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Merchanting decline hits James Latham Heavy losses in its merchanting activities resulted in an increased deficit of Pounds 612,000 at James Latham, the timber and builders merchants, in the year to March 31. Sales fell to Pounds 59.6m (Pounds 66m). Profit on property sales contributed Pounds 318,000 (Pounds 186,000). In the comparable 12 months the pre-tax loss was Pounds 383,000. The final dividend has been cut to 2.25p (4.5p) for a reduced total of 3.75p (6.5p). Losses per share were 8.7p (7.1p).
