BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on Colombian guerrilla activities monitored through 19 May. Faustino Tovar Trujillo, alias "Commander Nicolas," ideological leader of the southern bloc of the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Colombia, FARC, faked his own death nine years ago thus deceiving the military; however, after long intelligence investigations, the Army arrested Tovar in Neiva, Huila Department last week. (Santa Fe de Bogota EL TIEMPO in Spanish 16 May 94 p 12B) The police reported that National Liberation Army guerrillas kidnapped and murdered Oswaldo Pajaro Garcia, chairman of the Aguachica Municipal Council, Cesar Department on 17 May. Aguachica Alderman Elibardo Galvis was also kidnapped. (Madrid EFE in Spanish 1922 GMT 17 May 94)
