March 25, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

HILL'S CHARITY EFFORTS DRAW WIDESPREAD PRAISE

Jim Hill has supported the Challengers Boys and Girls Club on various special occasions.

He was the chairman of our annual sports day in July. He attended our 20th anniversary celebration banquet in August, and he has never refused an opportunity to make guest appearances at special events held at the club.

Needless to say, our community is well aware of Jim's commitment to them and we appreciate all he has done to make our job here at the club a little easier.

L.E. DANTZLER,

Founder/Executive Director

Challengers Boys and Girls Club

Los Angeles

Editor's note: The preceding three letters were received by The Times after having been sent to Jim Hill's attorney, Ed Hookstratten.
