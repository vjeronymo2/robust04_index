BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 16 May -- The Abkhazian Liberation Union brought an action at the Georgian Supreme Court for instituting criminal proceedings against Eduard Shevardnadze, head of the Georgian state, on the basis of Clause 65 of the Criminal Law of the Republic of Georgia. The clause deals with the betrayal of the country manifested in the fight against the country's territorial integrity and independence. The above-mentioned action was caused by the Georgian delegation's signing of the agreement reached at the Georgian-Abkhaz talks in Moscow.
