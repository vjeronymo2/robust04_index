920320 FT 20 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Loss at Select Appointments Select Appointments (Holdings), which operates miscellaneous business services including permanent and temporary staff placements, went sharply into reverse in the six months to October 5 ending with a pre-tax loss of Pounds 508,000 compared with a profit of Pounds 711,000. Sales of this USM-quoted company totalled Pounds 10.1m (Pounds 31.3m) but the current figure related only to ongoing operations in the UK, Australia and New Zealand, while the previous year's included Pounds 15.3m sales of the discontinued businesses of Morgan and Banks and Select USA. Disposal of these companies was reflected in an extraordinary charge this time of Pounds 21.1m.
