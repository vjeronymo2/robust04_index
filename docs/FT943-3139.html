940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Lambert Howarth Shares in Lambert Howarth Group fell 7p to 143p yesterday after pre-tax profits tumbled from Pounds 784,000 to Pounds 442,000 in the first half of 1994. Turnover was Pounds 2.7m higher at Pounds 31.4m, with the greater part attributable to acquisitions on safety footwear. Earnings per share fell to 3.1p (6.9p) but the interim dividend was held at 2.25p.
