920620 FT 20 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Grainger falls and warns on outlook Profits before tax of Grainger Trust, the property investor and trader, fell from Pounds 1.23m to Pounds 733,000 for the six months ended March 31. The directors said they did not anticipate a better than break-even result in the second half and warned that full year profits were unlikely to exceed those of the opening six months - pre-tax profits for the 1990-91 year totalled Pounds 1.37m. First half turnover slipped to Pounds 16.97m (Pounds 20.07m). Interest took Pounds 1.27m less at Pounds 6.9m and earnings emerged at 2.5p (4.1p) per share. The interim dividend is a same-again 1.2p.
