BFN [Text] Iraqi opposition sources in Salah al-Din in northern Iraq have said that the head of the ruling regime in Baghdad has dismissed Sabir 'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Duri, the director of his intelligence services, after the exposure of the culprits in the assassination of the Iraqi dissident Shaykh Talib Suhayl al-Tamimi in Beirut, and the involvement of the Iraqi regime's intelligence services in the killing of German journalist Lissy Schmidt in northern Iraq early last month.
