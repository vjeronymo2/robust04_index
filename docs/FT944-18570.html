941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Waterman ahead Waterman Partnership, the consulting engineer, benefited from increased activity in the property and construction sectors, reporting a 61 per cent increase in pre-tax profit from Pounds 124,000 to Pounds 200,000 for the year to June 30. Turnover improved 12 per cent from Pounds 7.35m to Pounds 8.22m. Earnings per share were 0.3p (0.1p) but the proposed final dividend is maintained at 0.5p for an unchanged total of 1p.
