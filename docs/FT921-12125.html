920128 FT 28 JAN 92 / International Company News: Boeing announces further job losses BOEING, which is expected to complete the sale of its de Havilland division in about a month's time, is to lay off about 317 workers at the Canadian aircraft factory indefinitely, starting February 14, the company said yesterday, Reuter reports. Boeing laid off 140 workers before Christmas. The number of workers has shrunk from around 6,000 to fewer than 3,500 in the past two years as recession has cut orders for its Dash-8 aircraft.
