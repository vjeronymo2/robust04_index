941025 FT 25 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Sao Paulo share prices fell in weak trading as the Bovespa index fell 2,179 points, or 4.8 per cent, to 43,440 points. Rio de Janeiro's BVRJ index lost 688 points, or 4.0 per cent, to 16,318 points. Stock prices have declined 20.8 per cent since Mr Fernando Henrique Cardoso won the presidential election on October 3.
