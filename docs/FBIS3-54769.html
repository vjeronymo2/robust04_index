Language: Russian Article Type:BFN ["At the Center of Attention" news column report] [Text] There will be no introduction of import tariffs on goods from 1 January 1994, Yegor Gaydar, first vice premier of the Russian Federation stated. The corresponding government decree has been deferred for further processing, and it is planned to introduce import tariffs from 1 March. At the same time Ye. Gaydar stressed that the bulk of goods and agricultural produce comes onto the Russian market from the CIS countries, with the majority of which free trade agreements exist. No duties will be introduced on these goods and products.
