930406 FT 06 APR 93 / Daiwa acts over Y20bn losses Daiwa Securities fired a branch manager and filed a complaint with the police accusing him of using customers' funds to cover up investment losses of over Y20bn (Pounds 117m) a Daiwa spokesman said yesterday, Reuter reports from Tokyo. Daiwa filed the complaint against the former branch manager on charges of fraud late last week, he said. The daily Sankei Shimbun said the Kunitachi branch in suburban Tokyo incurred the losses in stock options trading.
