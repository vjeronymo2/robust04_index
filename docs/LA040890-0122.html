April 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

HERITAGE OF COMMERCE

I am currently researching "City of Commerce: An Enterprising Heritage," a 240-page illustrated history of the City of Commerce, planned for publication by Hacienda Press in January, 1991.

I would like to hear from anyone with historic photographs, maps, manuscripts or relevant first-hand experiences in the city.
