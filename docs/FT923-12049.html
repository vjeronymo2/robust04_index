920721 FT 21 JUL 92 / Observer: Learned Hello How much further downmarket will Oxford University go in its fund-raising? A Columbus Quincentenary Dinner is to be held in October at London's Guildhall in aid of the Bodleian Library's Pounds 10m appeal. Among the patrons assembled for the event are Lord Jenkins of Hillhead (the University's Chancellor), the Duke of Norfolk, Paul Mellon, Lord Carrington, Tony O'Reilly, Lord Rees-Mogg, and a clutch of ambassadors. Since all proceeds will benefit the Bodleian's Hispanic, Caribbean and American collections, presumably this means that the 404-year-old library will soon be able to afford to add Hola], the Spanish celebrity glossy, to its foreign periodicals list. After all, it is sponsoring the event with its English sister, Hello]
