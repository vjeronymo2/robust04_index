BFN [Text] Sao Paulo, 24 May (AE) -- A luncheon hosted by Sao Paulo State Federation of Industries [FIESP] leaders for U.S. Treasury Assistant Secretary Lawrence Summers and Finance Minister Rubens Ricupero has just ended. The business people voiced satisfaction with the support shown by Summers for the Brazilian stabilization program. Businessman Luiz Fernando Burlan of the Sadia Company said: "Summers came here as a U.S. Government representative and it is our impression that it is truly committed to Brazil. I for one was surprised by the way in which he conveyed the U.S. support for the plan." Businessman Antonio Ermirio de Moraes said: "Summers has demonstrated that he has confidence in the plan." Most of the business people who attended the luncheon ruled out the possibility of an outburst of consumption in the short term.
