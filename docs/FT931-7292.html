930225 FT 25 FEB 93 / Technology: Company Snapshot - L & P COMPANY SNAPSHOT: Linklaters & Paines is an international commercial law firm, with headquarters in the City of London and offices in Brussels, Paris, Frankfurt, New York, Hong Kong, Singapore and Tokyo. NATURE OF BUSINESS: Commercial law. The firm's main areas of practice are corporate and commercial, international finance and banking, commercial property, litigation, tax and intellectual property. EMPLOYEES: 1,600 staff, of whom 700 are lawyers. Key personnel: Barry Dean, head of Know-How systems; Andrew Taylor, head of IT; Jeremy Parr, Solicitor. TECHNOLOGY FILE SOFTWARE: Kinesis Total Recall software is the basis of the Cormaks system and two others delivering information on international finance and the Financial Services Act. Total Recall supports a combination of text and data to diverse and disparate information, linked by subject area. It does this by integrating the BRS/Search text retrieval package (from BRS Software Products) into the Ingres relational database. Total Recall works with all the big relational databases and free-text products. Kinesis of Swindon developed the Total Recall information delivery software, and installed the first Know-How system, Cormaks, two years ago. Consultant was Peter Attwood. OTHER SYSTEM: Currently running on a Wang 2200 network are several other information banks, soon to be converted to Kinesis software: Cartel ofr competition law. Perks for employee benefits, Landmark for property, Planet for environmental law, Clicks for litigation. HARDWARE: Kinesis uses a PC front-end to a sun SPARCserver. VALUE OF SOFTWARE: The original development contract with Kinesis was in the region of Pounds 250,000, including software and consultancy, but excluding hardware, and licences for BRS/Search and Ingres.
