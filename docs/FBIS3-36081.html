Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A spokesman for the Black Sea Navy Command has called on Ukrainian naval officials to refrain from "dangerous assessments running counter to the opinion of the Russian and Ukrainian presidents and to the spirit of the Russian-Ukrainian naval accords. [no closing quotation marks as received] This statement came in reply to a cable addressed to the Naval Headquarters Chief, Vice-Admiral Svyatashov, by his Ukrainian counterpart, Rear-Admiral Ryzhenko, who has described the Black Sea Marine Brigade as "a foreign unit on Ukraine's territory." The spokesman emphasized that in line with the Yalta agreement, the brigade, like the entire fleet, is subordinate to the Ukrainian president. Thus, Ryzhenko's declaration contradicts the document signed by the president as the Supreme Commander of the Ukrainian Armed Forces.
