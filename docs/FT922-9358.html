920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / World Trade News: Airport contract A UK-Canada consortium, led by British Aerospace and the Ontario-based construction group Armbro Enterprises, has won a Dollars 170m contract to build and operate a new terminal at Prague international airport, writes Bernard Simon in Toronto. The other bidder was a consortium led by Bouygues, the French construction group, and Lockheed, the US-based airport operator.
