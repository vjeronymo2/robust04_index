Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, February 15 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Mongolia and UNESCO have decided to cooperate in the sphere of culture, elaborate a number of programmes in this direction. First of all, within the framework of cooperation with this big international organization a work will be carried out on protection, popularization in abroad of historical and cultural monuments of Mongolia. It is planned to shoot documentaries and TV films, organize tourist trips. Besides that, UNESCO will render assistance to Mongolia in strengthening material facilities of the Mongolian National University of Culture and Art, in elaboration of the national policy in the sphere of culture.
