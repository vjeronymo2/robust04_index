BFN [Article by "bor": "The Confederation of Trade Unions Will Not Go Into the Elections With the Movement for a Democratic Slovakia"] [Text] Alojz Englis, president of the Confederation of Trade Unions [KOZ], Vladimir Stevko, vice president of KOZ, and J. Blahak yesterday denied reports that the KOZ leadership would go into the elections "in the colors of the Movement for a Democratic Slovakia [HZDS]." Even if they received such an offer, they would not consider it, they said.
