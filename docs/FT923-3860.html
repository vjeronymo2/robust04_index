920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / Gigha sold for Pounds 2m GIGHA, a small islandoff Argyll, Scotland, has been sold for Pounds 2m to Mr Derek Holt and his wife June, ending months of uncertainty over its future. The 3,400-acre island off the Mull of Kintyre was bought by Mr Malcolm Potier, a property developer, for more than Pounds 5m in 1989, but was repossessed earlier this year by a Swiss bank. The Holts' business interests include a marina at Inverkip, Ayrshire. Their daughter Mrs Sandra Howden and her husband William plan to move tothe island with their baby son Alastair.
