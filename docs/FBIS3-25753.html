Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] PRC Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs Tian Zengpei said his country hopes a lasting peace will be established in Mozambique through the fulfillment of the General Peace accord signed in Rome. During his stay in Maputo the PRC official will hold talks with Mozambican Government officials and representatives of political parties. Tian Zengpei arrived in Maputo yesterday for a two-day visit at the invitation of Mozambican authorities.
