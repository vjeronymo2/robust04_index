BFN [Text] Beijing, May 23 (XINHUA) -- Li Ruihuan, chairman of the National Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference (CPPCC), met here this morning with Thai crown sister, Princess Galyani Vadhana [spelling of name as received] and her party in the Great Hall of the People. Li said during the meeting that the Chinese and Thai peoples have enjoyed traditional friendship and long-term and close cooperation, and the constant exchange of visits between high-ranking officials between the two countries has promoted mutual understanding and bilateral friendship. Extending a welcome to the crown sister on her fifth visit to China, Li said that her current visit, during which she had exchanged views with Chinese leaders on issues of common interest, would be helpful to furthering bilateral relations. The Thai crown sister and her party arrived in Beijing May 21 for a two-week visit to China as guests of the CPPCC National Committee. She expressed her thanks to Li for meeting her right after his European trip. The crown sister invited Li to visit Thailand at an early date and Li accepted her invitation. The Thai visitors will also tour China's coastal cities.
