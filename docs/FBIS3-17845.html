Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (March 28) TURAN -- Last night the Armenian army launched an attack towards the Agdamskiy region of Azerbaijan, reports the press centre of Azerbaijan Defense Ministry. The Armenian army has 10 units of military hardware and 200 infantry-men. Earlier the Armenian army shelled positions belonging to the Azeri Army. The same source reports that the Azeri Army managed to thwart the attack and were able to liberate the villages of Garagashly and Shirvanly in the Agdamskiy region. The Armenian army lost military technique [equipment] as well as lives. This morning the Armenian army launched an attack towards the village of Geytepe which is 10 km to the north-east of Agdam. The Azeri army destroyed one Armenian tank. Fighting in this direction continues. Positions of the Azeri Army at the southern section of the front have been shelled as well. At the same time villages in the Tauzskiy region of Azerbaijan have been shelled from Armenian's military bases located on the territory of Armenia's Berdskiy region. Damages have been reported.
