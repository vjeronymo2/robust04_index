August 4, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

A MAJOR PROBLEM WITH DEVELOPMENT

Can we remind the fickle, naive and fair-weather Dodger fan that the major leagues are not the place to "develop" players.

Talent develops in the streets, schoolyards, sandlots and other fields of dreams. You do not teach or learn baseball in the big leagues. Check out rookies DiMaggio, Williams, Musial, Ruth, etc.

JOHNNY MAGNUS

Hollywood
