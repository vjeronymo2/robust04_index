940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Dart Improved trading in the distribution division and aircraft parts business enabled Dart Group to increase pre-tax profits by 41 per cent, from Pounds 1.66m to Pounds 2.34m, during the year to end-March. Turnover for the distribution and aviation services group improved to Pounds 52.2m (Pounds 42.8m). The pre-tax figure was helped by a surplus on the sale of fixed assets of Pounds 130,000 (Pounds 16,000) and lower interest charges of Pounds 26,000 (Pounds 216,000). Earnings per share were 10.6p (7.5p). A final dividend of 2.4p is recommended, taking the total for the year to 3.7p (3.3p).
