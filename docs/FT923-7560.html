920817 FT 17 AUG 92 / Job scheme places 50,000 people many of them long-term unemployed - have got jobs through the Job Interview Guarantee. Jig, provided nationally by the Employment Service since 1991 after being piloted in 20 inner-city areas, provides unemployed people with job interviews by offering employers free services, such as customised training to give an unemployed person the specific skills the employer needs.
