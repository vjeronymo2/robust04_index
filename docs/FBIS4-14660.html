BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Ivanova] [Text] Moscow April 1 TASS -- A number of State Duma factions opposed today Thursday's suspension of the SOVETSKAYA ROSSIYA newspaper's release by the Pressa Publishing House for systematic non-payments for printing services and paper. The Communist, Agrarian and "Russian Way" Factions described the printers' demand for payment as "unlawful persecution and publishers' racket".
