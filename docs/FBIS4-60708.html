BFN [Text] Mashhad (Khorasan) May 26, IRNA -- The presidents of Iran and Turkmenistan inaugurated the Turkmen consulate here Thursday. The visiting Turkmen president, Saparmurad Niyazov, lauded the development saying that due to President 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani's personal efforts, relations between Iran and his country have seen a steady progress. Hojjat ol-Eslam Rafsanjani, too, was also pleased about the inauguration of the Turkmen consulate here and said both countries place an emphasis on their mutual relations. A group of cabinet ministers of both countries were present at the inaugural ceremonies. The Islamic Republic opened its consulate in Marv of Turkmenistan earlier this year.
