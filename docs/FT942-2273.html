940620 FT 20 JUN 94 / Equity Markets: Helsinki - Other Markets The results season continues with figures today for the first four months of the year from Repola, Finland's largest industrial group, which rebounded into the black last year after much improved performances from its main forestry and engineering activities. Meanwhile, parliament votes today on the supplementary budget, which includes government plans to cut state ownership in Finnair to as little as 50.1 per cent from its current 72 per cent. Nokia holds an extraordinary shareholders meeting on Thursday to approve the issue of 6m preferred shares.
