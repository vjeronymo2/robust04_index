940711 FT 11 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Ashtead makes Pounds 3.35m purchase Ashtead Group, the equipment rental company, has bought GKN Powered Access UK for Pounds 3.35m cash. GKN Powered was established in early 1990 and rents out an extensive range of powered access platforms. Turnover in 1993 was Pounds 3.76m with operating losses of Pounds 85,000. Ashtead, however, expects the acquisition to be profitable and earnings enhancing in the current year.
