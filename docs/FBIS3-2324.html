Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] PRC media monitored by Okinawa Bureau carried the following reports on PRC leadership activities from 23 February to 3 March 1994. The source is noted in parentheses after each report. Zou Jiahua Meets Sino-Foreign Petrochemical Project Partners -- Zou Jiahua, vice premier of the State Council, recently met with several directors of a foreign company which is China's partner in a Sino-foreign joint-venture petrochemical industrial project in the South China Sea. The total investments of the project will be about $5 billion. The plant is in Guangdong's Huizhou and expected to begin operations in 1998. (Beijing Central Television Program One Network in Mandarin 1400 GMT 23 Feb 94) Jiang Zemin, Others Inscribe for Chinese Academy of Social Sciences -- The Chinese Academy of Social Sciences [CASS] closed its conference on work for 1994 on 25 February. Jiang Zemin and Li Peng have written inscriptions for CASS, encouraging it to continue reform. Jiang Zemin's inscription reads: "Intensify study, sum up experiences, and persist in integrating theory with reality to build the Chinese Academy of Social Sciences into a staunch position of Marxism." Li Peng's message reads: "Make the study of theory on socialism with Chinese characteristics your lofty mission." Ding Guangen called CASS and said: "I hope CASS will firmly take Comrade Deng Xiaoping's theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics and the party's basic line as guidance, comprehensively follow the guidelines of the 14th CPC Congress and the Third Plenary Session of the 14th CPC Central Committee, emphasize the important task of `arming the people with scientific theory,' emancipate their minds, seek truth from facts, take the actual situation into consideration, make the focal points stand out, and intensify study in the theory of building socialism with Chinese characteristics as well as in important issues in the field of social sciences to better serve reform, opening up, and the modernization drive." Li Tieying said in his letter: "It is clear that China is now making greater efforts in reforming its economic and political systems and is stepping up the pace of modernization. Under the new situation, our tasks are very arduous and we are bound to meet a great deal of new problems. There are no existing experiences and answers ready for resolving these problems; we need to study and search them. The CPC, the state, and the people have placed great hopes on social sciences workers in this regard. We should firmly seize this historic opportunity; uphold Marxism as our guidance; better serve reform, opening up, and the construction of spiritual and material civilization; and serve as a good adviser and assistant to the CPC Central Committee and the State Council." Zhu Rongji also wrote to CASS. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0830 GMT 25 Feb 94) Qiao Shi Meets Painter Cui Zifan -- On the morning of 25 February, Qiao Shi, member of the Standing Committee of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and chairman of the National People's Congress Standing Committee, met with Beijing painter Cui Zifan, who donated 122 works to the China Art Gallery. Qiao Shi praised him, saying: "Your deeds have displayed and set a good example of the spirit of selflessness and dedication." (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1110 GMT 25 Feb 94) Zou Jiahua Attends Sino-German Agreement-signing Ceremony -- Zou Jiahua, vice premier of the State Council, attended an agreement- signing ceremony on 25 February held by the China National Technical Import and Export Corporation and a German company. Two boilers worth some $140 million will be imported from Germany for the Tianjin Huaneng Yang Liuqing Electric Power Plant. (Beijing Central Television Program One Network in Mandarin 1100 GMT 26 Feb 94) Wu Bangguo Inscribes Name of TV Station-- A spring festival theatrical performance marked the inauguration of the Shanghai Educational Television Station on 27 February. Wu Bangguo, member of the CPC Central Committee Political Bureau and secretary of the Shanghai Municipal CPC Committee, inscribed the name of the station. Zhu Kaixuan, minister of the State Education Commission, delivered televised congratulatory message. Chen Zhili, deputy secretary of the municipal party committee, spoke at the opening ceremony. (Shanghai People's Radio Network In Mandarin 2300 GMT 27 Feb 94) Liu Huaqing, Zou Jiahua Greet Opening of National Electronics Industry Work Conference -- Liu Huaqing, vice chairman of the Central Military Commission and Vice Premier Zou Jiahua sent greetings and congratulatory messages to the opening of the national electronics industry work conference on 28 February. "In his message, Liu Huaqing called on the electronics industry to strengthen scientific research and development, properly organize experimental systems and production, strive to achieve advanced international standards, and continue to provide advanced technological equipment to the various sectors of the national economy and the People's Liberation Army." In his congratulatory letter, Zou Jiahua urged the electronics industry to popularize electronics technology, reform traditional industry, constantly develop new products, pay attention to establishing various economic information systems, and strive to promote national economic development. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0751 GMT 28 February 94) Lu Rongjing, Fu Xishou Meet Sanyo Delegation -- Lu Rongjing, secretary of the Anhui Provincial CPC Committee, and Governor Fu Xishou separately met a delegation from the Sanyo Electric Company Limited led by President Yasuaki Takano on 27 February. Lu Rongji praised the far-sightedness shown by the Sanyo Electric Company in entering a joint venture with the Hefei Washing Machine General Company. Fu Xishou predicted broad prospects in store for the joint venture and wished it every success. (Hefei Anhui People's Radio Network in Mandarin 1100 GMT 28 Feb 94) Chen Xitong Thanks Retired Cadre for Waste-Cutting Suggestions -- Chen Xitong, secretary of the Beijing Municipal CPC Committee, recently expressed his appreciation to retired military cadre Lu Lian for writing a letter to him, putting forward several good suggestions for cutting waste while receiving retired cadres and for avoiding extravagance while conducting inaugurations and similiar ceremonies. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 1319 GMT 28 February 94) Li Peng, Liu Huaqing Inscribe for Dictionary -- A "Dictionary on Chinese Heroes and Martyrs" was jointly published by the Ministry of Civil Affairs and the Heilongjiang People's Publishing House recently. Premier Li Peng and Liu Huaqing, vice chairman of the Central Military Commission, wrote inscriptions for the dictionary, affirming the ideological and social significance and the practical value of the dictionary. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service 0809 GMT 1 Mar 93) Chen Muhua, Luo Gan Present Awards to Outstanding Women -- The central government organs held a meeting to commend 10 outstanding women, 100 women pacesetters, and 10 advanced units at Zhongnanhai on 1 March. These individuals and units were nominated by 95 departments under various state organs. Chen Muhua, vice chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress and president of the All-China Women's Federation, and Luo Gan, secretary of the Work Committee for Central Government Organs, attended the meeting and presented prizes and certificates to the advanced women and units. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0821 GMT 1 Mar 94) Chen Junsheng Addresses Commendation Meeting on Greening -- On 1 March, the Beijing Municipality held a meeting at the Great Hall of the People to commend 606 advanced units and 1,048 advanced individuals in Beijing for their greening efforts. Chen Junsheng, state councillor and chairman of the National Greening Committee, spoke at the meeting. He highly appraised the greening and beautification work in the capital and called for efforts to meet several new demands so as to do a better job in greening. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0257 GMT 2 Mar 94) Ren Jianxing Watches Theatrical Performance -- On the evening of 1 March, the Central Committee for Comprehensive Management of Public Security and the Propaganda Department of the CPC Central Committee held a large-scale theatrical performance at the State Theatre to mark the third anniversary of the promulgation of the "Decision on Strengthening the Central Management of Public Order." Ren Jianxing and Zhang Siqing, chairman and vice chairman, respectively, of the Central Committee for Comprehensive Management of Public Security watched the performance. (Beijing XINHUA Domestic Service in Chinese 0829 GMT 2 mAR 94) Chen Xitong Interviewed on Efforts to Promote Education -- A station reporter recently interviewed Beijing Municipal Party Secretary Cheng Xitong on the municpality's efforts to promote education. Chen Xitong talked about the his experience in implementing educational policy. Recollecting his previous visit to a city school some years ago, he described two things there which had spurred him to immediate action -- the words he saw on a blackboard asking who would pity a teacher's hard life and that school committee members pointed an accusing figure at him at a meeting for not paying adequate attention to education. Thereupon he resolved to vigorously promote education, and proposed "every leader, every party standing committee member, every mayor and vice mayor and every district party secretary must make it a point to keep in touch with schools, including all universities, middle schools and primary schools, and do practical work for them." Over the past eight years, 10 solid tasks have been performed annually, and this has been found to be very effective. (Beijing Central Television Network Program One in Mandarin 1100 GMT 1 Mar 94) Wu Bangguo, Huang Ju Plant Trees -- Shanghai Municipal Party Committee and government leaders were among the first batch of the tree planting volunteers in the municipality this year. Wu Bangguo, Huang Ju, Chen Zhili, Wang Liping, Xu Kuangdi, Xie Lijuan, Xia Keqiang, Jiang Yiren, Sha Lin and other municipal leaders, as well as cadres from municipal government organs took part in a tree- planting drive early in the morning of 3 March. Wu Bangguo spoke on the importance of collective efforts to plant trees. (Shanghai People's Radio Network in Mandarin 1000 GMT 3 Mar 94)
