BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow May 13 TASS -- The press service of the Russian Government confirmed that Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin had a 20-minute meeting with nationalist leader Vladimir Zhirinovskiy on Thursday, but refused to give any comments. "Government services refrain from any comments on the matter so far", a spokesman of the press service told TASS. "One can judge about the essence of the talk only from what the LDPR leader said", he added. Zhirinovskiy told reporters after the meeting with Chernomyrdin that he had proposed to the prime minister to replace some of the ministers with members of his "shadow cabinet". Zhirinovskiy is mostly against Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev and Privatization Minister Anatoliy Chubays.
