February 25, 1990, Sunday, South Bay Edition

HARBOR BEATS COLLEGE OF THE DESERT, 88-87

A basket by Roneil Powell with 11 seconds left gave host Harbor College a wild 88-87 win Saturday over College of the Desert in the first round of the state community college playoffs.

Harbor (23-12), seeded 14th, will visit No. 3-seeded Rancho Santiago, which had a bye, on Wednesday.

College of the Desert (21-12) led most of the second half before Harbor, paced by Eric Carpenter's 25 points and Terry Nelson's 21 points and 11 rebounds, closed the gap. A Desert shot to win with seven seconds left was disallowed because of a charging foul, Harbor spokesman Gary Wood said.
