Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The president, Flight Lieutenant J.J. Rawlings, left Accra today for Namibia on a three-day official visit. He was accompanied by his wife, Nana Konadu Agyeman Rawlings; the minister of information, Mr. Totobi Quakyi; the Navy commander, Rear Admiral Tom Annan; and officials of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. President Rawlings will attend the official handing over of Walvis Bay and the offshore islands to Namibia. It will be recalled that at the time of Namibia's independence, South Africa refused to hand over two islands to her, claiming that they were part of her territory.
