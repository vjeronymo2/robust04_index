931220 FT 20 DEC 93 / Observer: Beyond Arafat The Christmas spirit has seeped into the oddest places, including august publishing houses. A colleague of Observer's was last week told by his publishers to expect copies of the new edition of his jointly penned biography of Yassir Arafat. The package duly arrived, containing six copies of a tome entitled 'Beyond Ararat: Travels in Eastern Turkey'.
