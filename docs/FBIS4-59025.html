BFN [Text] This afternoon, Israel Air Force [IAF] jets attacked terrorist targets belonging to Ahmad Jibril's organization in al-Na'imah, north of Sidon. All the planes returned safely to base. The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] spokesman noted that the pilots reported accurate hits against their targets. The area attacked is under the complete control of Ahmad Jibril's organization and serves as a training base and staging point for attacks in the security zone.
