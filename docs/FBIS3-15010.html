Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (March 1) TURAN--As was reported earlier, local police blockaded the building of Baku-based TURAN NEWS AGENCY. Due to this fact, our agency is unable to function. The building is searched without the sanctions of the Procurator. The police does not explain the reasons of their activities. The Ministry of Internal Affairs of Azerbaijan, Procurator's Office and President's staff of the Republic give no details about present situation and refuse to answer the question of the journalists. Presently we use several additional sources to inform you about the situation.
