930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Burndene Invs shows advance to Pounds 2.29m Burndene Investments, the caravans and mobile homes group which also has interests in hosiery and property development, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 2.07m to Pounds 2.29m in the half year to April 3. The caravan making and site operating side rose to Pounds 1.68m (Pounds 1.41m) pre-tax, and property, finance and administration contributed a higher Pounds 233,000 (Pounds 131,000). The hosiery division, however, fell to Pounds 375,000 (Pounds 530,000). Turnover improved from Pounds 26.8m to Pounds 27.7m. Earnings per share came through at 1.45p (1.3p adjusted) and an interim dividend of 0.5p (0.273p) is declared.
