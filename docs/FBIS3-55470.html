Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Sevindzh Abdullayeva and Viktor Shulman] [Text] Baku, 8 Jan -- The large Goradiz railway station in the southwest of Azerbaijan was completely cleared of Armenian armed units today. According to the press service of Azerbaijan's Defense Ministry, subunits of the Azerbaijani Army have taken under control the highway linking Goradiz with Fizuli.
