940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / International Company News: Seapower may spin off business Seapower International Holdings, the cold storage company with interests in finance, trading investments and property, might spin-off its financial services businesses into a separately-listed company, AP-DJ reports from Hong Kong. In a statement to the Hong Kong Stock Exchange yesterday, the group said directors were considering a reorganisation proposal whereby a newly-established company would carry out the financial services businesses now conducted by various subsidiaries. The new company would list on the stock exchange and remain under Seapower International's control. 'The terms of the reorganisation proposal are currently under discussion,' Seapower said.
