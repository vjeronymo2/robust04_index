October 21, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SPARE THE DUMPS

I'll give real estate agents one hint: Don't make a future seller angry by sending tons of junk mail.

My family has lived in the same house on the Westside since the 1930s. When we get so many solicitations to sell from one firm, we make a note in our date book never to offer the house through that firm.

Garbage is garbage is garbage, particularly in Southern California where the dumps are already full.

MARGARET W. ROMANI

Los Angeles
