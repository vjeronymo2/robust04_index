Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Report by Hila Tov] [Excerpt] Some 50 percent of Israelis who contract AIDS belong to the 15-24 age group; 1,084 AIDS carriers are registered in Israel, but it is assessed that their real number is anywhere between 2,000 to 2,500. The number of those suffering from the disease is 255. So far 188 have died from AIDS. These figures were revealed yesterday during the Knesset's Education and Culture Committee session, marking International AIDS Day. Israel is defined by the WHO as a low risk country. Switzerland, Spain, and Italy lead Europe in AIDS frequency. [passage omitted]
