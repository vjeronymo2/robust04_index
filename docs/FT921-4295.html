920310 FT 10 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Baltic declines to Pounds 9.1m BALTIC, the leasing and property group, suffered a Pounds 2.9m profits fall to Pounds 9.1m pre-tax for the year ended December 31 after taking account of exceptional items. Asset finance made profits of Pounds 24.7m (Pounds 27m) which Mr Harry Hyman, finance director, described as a good result in difficult circumstances. The group is now concentrating on its core-activity of leasing. Interest charges declined from Pounds 17.5m to Pounds 14.1m, reflecting falling interest rates and a drop in borrowings from Pounds 144m to Pounds 122m. Net assets increased to Pounds 70m (Pounds 67m). Property incurred a loss of Pounds 1.46m compared to a profit of Pounds 1.38m, which included an exceptional provision of Pounds 842,000 relating to the loss on Spanish property financing. Turnover fell to Pounds 38.46m (Pounds 52.77m). Fully-diluted earnings emerged at 14.5p (21.5p). A final dividend of 2.5p makes a 4.33p (4.13p) total.
