BFN [Text] On 29 June Lionel Soto, vice president of the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Cuba, who is on an official visit to Ukraine, and Ukraine's Transport Minister Orest Klympush signed a merchant shipping agreement. There has long been a need for this document, Lionel Soto said. I am convinced that the agreement initiates a new stage in our relations. As of today we can carry on trade right across the sea, because Cuba is an island state and this is the only way to communicate with the outside world.
