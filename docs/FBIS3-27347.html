Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Xiamen, February 21 (XINHUA) -- Xiamen, one of China's five special economic zones in Fujian Province, is striving to develop into a center of art to attract more tourists. Hong Yongshi, mayor of the city, pointed out that Xiamen is a port and one of China's major tourist attractions. With 46 special beauty-spots and tourist sites, Xiamen is also noted for its developed education. The mayor said that the city government plans to use its superiority of having close relations with Taiwan and Southeast Asian countries to develop the city into a leading cultural center in Asia.
