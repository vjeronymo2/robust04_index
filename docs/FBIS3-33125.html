Article Type:CSO [Editorial Report] Managua EL NUEVO DIARIO in Spanish of 29 January 1994 on page 6 reports that the first development center in Valle de Sebaco has been created in order to facilitate the marketing of the area's products. The article reports that small and medium producers will be able to rent machinery at low cost from the Ministry of Agriculture and Livestock (MAG) and receive training from the Development Foundation (Funde). Producers will eventually be able to form cooperatives and obtain "unconventional" loans. MAG Deputy Minister Julio Rivera explains that there are currently 44 development centers nationwide serving approximately 50,000 peasant families.
