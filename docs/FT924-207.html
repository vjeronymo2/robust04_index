921230 FT 30 DEC 92 / Crackdown on Somali gunmen US forces yesterday began a crackdown on gunmen in the Somali capital seizing weapons, missiles and battlewagons 48 hours before a visit by President George Bush, Reuter reports from Mogadishu. Meanwhile diplomats confirmed that a clan in the port of Kismayu killed over 100 from another clan on the eve of the US landing in Mogadishu.
