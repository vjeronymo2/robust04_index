Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] This morning's CITIZEN newspaper reports a plot to assassinate Afrikaner Volksfront [National Front] leader Constand Viljoen if he continues his efforts to take the front into the election. The plot allegedly has its origin in militant groups within the Volksfront who are violently opposed to the election. The shock revelations come after a weekend of drama which saw General Viljoen registering the front without the go ahead of its executive. The registration was later withdrawn.
