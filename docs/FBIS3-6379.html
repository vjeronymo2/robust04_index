Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Soldiers from Indonesia and Australia are to take part in military exercises over the next four weeks. A team of 25 Australian soldiers from the Special Air Service will take part in Exercise Night Komodo in Indonesia. At the same time, a contingent from Indonesia's special forces will undergo training in Australia's Exercise Kookaburra. A statement from the Australian Defense Department said the training will concentrate on basic military skills relevant to special forces, including weapons practice, parachuting, navigation, and communications. The department said Indonesian troops would use Australian facilities in Perth to train to retake an aircraft after a hijack.
