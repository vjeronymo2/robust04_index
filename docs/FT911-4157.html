910420 FT 20 APR 91 / World News in Brief: Boating disasters At least 72 people, mostly Nigerians, died when a ship capsized off Equatorial Guinea. Meanwhile a wooden boat packed with illegal immigrants from the Dominican Republic capsized off Puerto Rico, killing at least five people. The death toll in an Indonesian boating disaster rose to 63. Rescue workers spent two days recovering corpses from an artificial lake in central Java.
