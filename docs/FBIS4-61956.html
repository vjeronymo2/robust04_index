BFN [Excerpt] Yesterday, Uzbek President Islam Karimov received Japanese newspaper correspondents at their request. Islam Karimov expressed satisfaction with the fact that talks with journalists from a flourishing and a leading country are being held on the eve of his official visit to Japan. He spoke to the guests on Uzbekistan's own path to a market economy and the progress of the reforms in the Republic. He also talked about the development of relations between Uzbekistan and CIS countries and the international ties of our republic, including the prospects for the development of economic ties with Japan. The Japanese expressed gratitude for the warm reception and the interesting conversation and wished Islam Karimov a fruitful and good trip to Japan. [passage omitted]
