Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Portugal supports Algeria. The Portuguese and Algerian foreign ministers met today. At the end of the meeting, Portuguese Foreign Minister Durao Barroso expressed the support of the Portuguese Government for the political and economic reforms in Algeria. [Begin Barroso recording, in progress] ...and that we support the process of political and economic reforms underway in Algeria. We also advocate a more flexible position by the IMF regarding Algeria, a specific case. At the same time, we call on the Algerian Government to make a serious effort towards national reconciliation and towards the implementation of its political and economic reforms. [end recording]
