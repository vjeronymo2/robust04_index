February 11, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

DIGEST: LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF;

COUNCIL ASKED TO MEET IN SUNLAND-TUJUNGA

The Los Angeles City Council may go on the road again -- this time to Sunland-Tujunga for the first time.

Councilman Joel Wachs on Friday asked his colleagues to hold their March 1 meeting at the Sunland-Tujunga Municipal Building in his district. The council has met outside of City Hall twice: five years ago in Canoga Park and last year in San Pedro.

His request is expected to be voted on next week.
