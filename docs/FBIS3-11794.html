Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Mexican Congress factions have unanimously approved the creation of a special multiparty commission to investigate Luis Donaldo Colosio's assassination. The Congress' Permanent Commission signed an agreement declaring 25 March a day of national mourning for Colosio's death. The agreement was signed by the 10 parliamentary factions represented in the legislative body. The multiparty commission also agreed unanimously to conduct the investigation and keep the Legislative Branch informed. This commission is made up of Senators Ernesto Lucre of PRI [Institutional Revolutionary Party] and Hector Teran of PAN [National Action Party], as well as Deputies Gustavo Carvajal of PRI, Alejandro Alcidas of PRD [Party of the Democratic Revolution], Alfredo Castaneda of PARM [Authentic Party of the Mexican Revolution], Juan de Dios Castro of PAN, and Cuauthemoc Amezcua of PPS [Popular Socialist Party].
