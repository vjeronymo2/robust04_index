July 30, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

109-BED HEALTH FACILITY COMPLETED IN SAN DIEGO

The new San Diego Mental Health Facility, a $16-million psychiatric and ancillary center with 109 beds, has been completed.

San Francisco-based Kaplan/McLaughlin/Diaz Architects designed the 55,000-square-foot hospital and renovation of the adjacent, 116,000-square-foot health-services building. The facility is in the city's metropolitan Mission/Hillcrest area.
