Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Afrikaner Volksfront [National Front] coleader, General Constand Viljoen, said he had information that members of the ANC's [African National Congress] military wing, Umkhonto we Sizwe [MK -- Spear of the Nation], were infiltrating into Bophuthatswana. He said it was high time for the Transitional Executive Council to intervene, and urged the ANC to stop its activities and to allow Bophuthatswana's cabinet to decide on reincorporation on Tuesday. Meanwhile, ANC spokesman Carl Niehaus has denied in the strongest terms that MK soldiers were busy moving into Bophuthatswana.
