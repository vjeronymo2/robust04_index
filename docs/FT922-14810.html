920404 FT 04 APR 92 / UK Company News: NMW Computers falls to Pounds 0.2m loss NMW Computers, the stock processing bureau, fell into the red in the year to December 31, as a result of recession, low levels of stock market activity and continued losses from Charterhouse Group, acquired in May. The dividend is passed: last year 0.5p was paid. At the pre-tax level, losses came to Pounds 207,000 (profits Pounds 413,000), even though turnover advanced to Pounds 10.6m (Pounds 8.73m). But the losses at Charterhouse were much reduced, and with its acquisition NMW had now diversified away from the securities industry. The financial services division had, however, traded profitably and was now dependent on stock market volumes for only half its revenue. There was an extraordinary charge of Pounds 111,000 (nil). Losses per share emerged at 0.9p (earnings 1.3p).
