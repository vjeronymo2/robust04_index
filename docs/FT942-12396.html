940429 FT 29 APR 94 / UK Company News: Hopkinsons falls by 69% Hopkinsons Group, the industrial abrasives, engineering equipment and plastic products company, yesterday reported a 69% decline in full-year profits - down from Pounds 2.06m to Pounds 642,000. Turnover slipped to Pounds 99m (Pounds 102.5m). A final dividend of 0.8p (0.4p) is recommended, making an unchanged total of 1.3p. The shares closed 2p down at 57p.
