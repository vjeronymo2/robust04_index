930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / International Company News in Brief: German DIY group plans flotation HORNBACH Holding, the German holding company controlling a group of home improvement stores, said it planned to float DM15m (Dollars 9.4m) worth of ordinary shares in its Hornbach-Baumarkt unit, Reuter reports from Mannheim. Mr Otmar Hornbach, the chief executive, said the issue price would be based on the current market price of preferred shares in the holding company. Existing shareholders can buy three new shares for every eight held.
