930422 FT 22 APR 93 / UK Company News: Regina in black with Pounds 0.14m Improved margins and reduced operating and overhead costs helped Regina, the USM-quoted Royal Jelly products group, return to profits in the half year to February 28. On turnover of Pounds 1.19m (Pounds 1.17m) pre-tax profits amounted to Pounds 143,000 (Pounds 457,000 losses) and included a Pounds 58,000 contribution from Regina Haw Par, the Singapore-based joint venture with Tiger Medicals. Mr Shiraz Malik-Noor, chairman, said that considerable progress had been made towards launching two new products. Earnings per share came through at 0.09p (0.34p losses).
