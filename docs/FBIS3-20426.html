Language: Chinese Article Type:CSO [Summary] To improve grain circulation in Guangxi, Yunnan, and Guizhou, China will use World Bank loans to set up a grain transport corridor. Guangxi is constructing a port grain depot. Guangxi alone has used World Bank loans of $25,690,000. In addition to internal funds, the total investment is 322 million yuan. When the corridor is completed, the area will have the ability to receive 1.6 million tons of grain annually.
