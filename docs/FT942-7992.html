940523 FT 23 MAY 94 / Arts (Openings): War Artists The Imperial War Museum opens two new displays on Wednesday, 'The Slade School' display shows the work of commissioned war artists in the later years of the First World War: Lewis, Nash, Bomberg, Nevinson, Spencer, Wadsworth and others. 'Europe Regained' follows artists including Ardizzone, Bawden, Gross and Weight through campaigns in Italy, France and Germany, after 1943.
