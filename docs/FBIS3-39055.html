Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ankara, Feb 8 (AA) -- Mahir Barutcu, Energy and Natural Resources Ministry under secretary, resigned from his post, it was reported on Tuesday [8 February]. Barutcu speaking to AA reporter on his resignation said he presented his resignation to the minister of energy and natural resources. He said "I resigned because I did not approve certain appointments taking parts in the ministry." [as received] Barutcu was appointed to the post on Dec 31, 1991.
