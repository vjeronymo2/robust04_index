Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Aleksandr Rutskoy intends to stand for the post of head of state in future presidential elections. This was stated at a news conference in Novosibirsk by Vasiliy Lipitskiy, Duma deputy and chairman of the board of the Free Russia national party. He referred to a talk with Rutskoy after the latter was released from prison. This news was reported by ITAR-TASS.
