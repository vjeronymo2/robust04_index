930508 FT 08 MAY 93 / Builder donated Pounds 124,500 to Tories TAYLOR Woodrow, the construction and property group, donated Pounds 124,500 to the Conservative party and paid five former directors Pounds 1.4m in 1992, according to its annual accounts published yesterday. It made a pre-tax loss of a Pounds 66.1m. It said yesterday that the payments to the directors were 'in accordance with company's contractual obligations'. The decision to increase its donation from Pounds 91,000 to Pounds 124,500 is understood to have been taken before Mr Colin Parsons took over as chairman from Mr Peter Drew, who received a payment.
