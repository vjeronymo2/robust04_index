Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] Paek In-chun, chairman of North Korea's National Alliance for the Country's Reunification [Pomminnyon], sent a telephone message to Deputy Prime Minister and Unification Minister Yi Yong-tok today. He revealed in the telephone message that North Korea would send a condolence team on the death of Rev. Mun Ik-hwan and proposed that a working-level contact be held tomorrow to discuss the issue of sending a condolence team to Seoul. Referring to the mounting affection and sympathy of the North Korean people for Rev. Mun Ik-hwan who died without seeing the realization of reunification for which he worked throughout his life, Paek In-chun said that North Korea would send a condolence team to Seoul. Paek In-chun then proposed that a working-level contact be held in Panmunjom tomorrow morning to discuss this issue.
