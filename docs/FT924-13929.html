921013 FT 13 OCT 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There were 17,888 packages for the day, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Demand was good. Landed medium amd better quality Assams were well supported at fully firm to dearer rates, but plainer sorts came to an an easier market with some withdrawals. Brighter liquoring East Africans sold well at firm to dearer rates. The remainder were barely steady. Offshore: fair demand with brightest descriptions dearer. The highest price realised this week was 210p for three Assam pfs. Quotations quality 170p, good medium 138p, medium 124p, low medium 83p.
