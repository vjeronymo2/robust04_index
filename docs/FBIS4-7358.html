BFN [Text] Chong Yun-ho, ambassador of our country to Mongolia, on 20 April paid a farewell call on Budragchaagyn Dash-Yondon, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Mongolian People's Revolutionary Party [MPRP]. At the meeting, the ambassador conveyed greetings from the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il to General Secretary Budragchaagyn Dash-Yondon. Expressing deep thanks for this, the general secretary asked the ambassador to convey his sincere greetings to the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song and dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. Touching on the issue of developing the friendly relations between the MPRP and the Workers Party of Korea, the general secretary expressed support for the Korean people's struggle for the independent and peaceful reunification of the nation.
