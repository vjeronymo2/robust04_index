BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 28 (KCNA) -- Kumsong tideland reclamation, one more creation of eternal value, has been successfully completed in Korea by People's Army [KPA] soldiers mobilized in peaceful construction and builders. A great nature-remaking project for damming up a wide sea extending scores of ri along the West Coast from Kwangnyang Bay to Kumsong-ri in Onchon County, South Pyongan Province, has been completed to obtain 3,300 hectares of arable land. Hundreds of hectares of paddy fields have already been adjusted as a standardized field like a checker board, irrigation and drainage waterways to prevent damages from drought, flood and brine forever and roads extending tens of kilometres have been built. Paddy fields have been divided into fields of 25 hectares each. This year will witness the first farming in these fields. A ceremony for the completion of the Kumsong tideland reclamation took place on the spot on April 27. Vice Marshal Choe Kwang, Politburo member of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] and chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, conveyed a message of thanks of the C.C. [Central Committee], the WPK at the ceremony. The message highly estimated the KPA soldiers and builders for their indomitable spirit and shining feats performed in the large- scale nature-remaking project with the unshakable faith that they have nothing impossible under the wise leadership of the party.
