931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / UK Company News: All-round upturn at Martin Shelton An upturn in business across the product range enabled Martin Shelton, the USM-quoted diaries, calendars, gifts and betting office equipment supplier, to swing from losses of Pounds 144,000 to profits of Pounds 90,000 pre-tax for the half year ended September 30. The shares responded via a 12p rise to 80p. Sales improved from Pounds 1.68m to Pounds 2.1m. Earnings worked through at 1.18p (losses 1.88p) and the interim dividend is lifted to 1.25p (0.75p).
