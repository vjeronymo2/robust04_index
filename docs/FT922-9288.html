920508 FT 08 MAY 92 / International Company News: MGM control to Credit Lyonnais CREDIT Lyonnais - the French state bank with a combined loan exposure of more than Dollars 1.2bn in borrowings by Mr Giancarlo Parretti, the Italian financier, and MGM, the troubled Hollywood studio - yesterday acquired control of 98.5 per cent of MGM stock at a foreclosure auction held in Delaware, writes Alan Friedman in New York It paid a non-cash price of Dollars 483.5m by bidding an equivalent amount of secured debt of Pathe Communications Corporation (PCC) and its affiliates. The MGM stock was owned by Mr Parretti's PCC.
