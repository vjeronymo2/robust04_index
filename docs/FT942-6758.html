940528 FT 28 MAY 94 / UK Company News: BEP rises Pounds 1m to Pounds 5.1m Turnover at Bristol Evening Post, the publisher, printer and distributor of newspapers, dipped from Pounds 60.62m to Pounds 59.48m for the year ended March 31 1994, but pre-tax profits improved by Pounds 1m to Pounds 5.1m. Earnings per share were 12.98p, compared with 10.96p, while the dividend is stepped up to 12.25p (11.75p) with a final payment of 8p. The group also announced the purchase, through its Bristol United Press subsidiary, of a Goss offset press from Rockwell Graphic Systems for Pounds 13m.
