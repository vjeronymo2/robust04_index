930111 FT 11 JAN 93 / S African right to join talks South Africa's political parties took a step at the weekend toward resuming multi-party negotiations on a post-apartheid constitution when right-wing groups agreed to participate in planning a new multi-party forum, Patti Waldmeir reports. The white supremacist Conservative party, the mainly Zulu Inkatha Freedom party and two black homeland governments pledged to start planning a new forum, which could meet as early as March.
