BFN [Text] Shanghai, June 18 (XINHUA) -- China's Huaneng International Power Development Corporation will be granted ADR (American Drawing Rights) to list stocks on the New York Stock Exchange from June 23. According to the Shanghai Stock Exchange, this will be the first of China's second batch of five state-owned enterprises to be listed worldwide. The other four are China Eastern Airlines, China Southern Airlines, the Shandong Huaneng Enterprise Group and the Tianjin Steel Tube Plant. "This indicates that China's reform of its joint stock system is bringing this sector into line with the international standards and that its state-owned enterprises are arousing greater interest of business people overseas," an exchange official said. The first batch of three Chinese state-owned enterprises listed on the New York Stock Exchange are operating smoothly.
