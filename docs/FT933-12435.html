930723 FT 23 JUL 93 / International Company News: Safra Republic net rises 20% to Dollars 27.7m SAFRA Republic Holdings, the Geneva-based private banking group controlled by Mr Edmond Safra, said its net income in the second quarter jumped 20 per cent to Dollars 27.7m, or Dollars 1.56 per share. Net income in the first half was up 20 per cent to Dollars 54.9m. Net interest income soared 150 per cent to Dollars 42.4m in the period, mainly due to margin improvements. Foreign exchange and other trading revenues advanced 69 per cent to Dollars 7.1m. Commission income jumped 58 per cent to Dollars 12.8m, thanks to the growth in revenues from off-balance sheet portfolio accounts. Total assets reached Dollars 10.7bn at June 30, up 11.5 per cent year on year, while shareholders' equity was Dollars 1.13bn or Dollars 63.82 compared with Dollars 1.11bn a year earlier.
