September 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CRITIC'S VIEW OF KFAC

Bernheimer's article was an unusual farewell. The first half was oh so usual Bernheimer -- a little wit, clever short sentences, everything intended to trap us into a positive response. And the second half of the critique? He owns the word pompous.

It would have been classy of him to have said good-by to KFAC with a bit of sentiment and a kindlier attitude. I know critics must criticize, but after all, there is a time for more understanding of a sad situation.

SELMA COMSKY

Pacific Palisades
