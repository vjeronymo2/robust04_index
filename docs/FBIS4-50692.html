BFN [Text] Beijing, May 19 (CNS) -- According to a research report recently published by the state Statistical Bureau of China, with the deepening of China's reform, failing to meet demand of market by Chinese enterprises has become the main factor conditioning their low economic efficiency. The report said with the economic system reform got underway in an overall way and the implementation of those regulations concerned in recent years, adjustment of market force had played more and more important role. An investigation made by relevant departments in 1991 into loss-making 50 enterprises from Beijing, Shenyang and Tianjin had attributed the reason for loss of these enterprises to wrong policies, unfavourable circumstances and bad management, which made up 19.4 percent, 47.8 percent and 32.8 percent of the total reason respectively. However, according to another investigation made in last year into more than 2500 loss-making enterprises from eight provinces and cities such as Shanghai, Shandong and Jilin, the ratios of the reason of wrong policies and unfavourable circumstances had dropped to 9 percent or so respectively and the ratio of the reason of bad management in these enterprises risen to about 82 percent, indicating bad management and failure in meeting demand of market had become the main factor conditioning their economic efficiency.
