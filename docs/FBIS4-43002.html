BFN [Text] Bonn (DPA) -- Hans Stercken (Christian Democratic Union), chairman of the Bundestag Foreign Policy Committee, has departed for Iraq for political talks that will take about one week. As his parliamentary office confirmed on Monday [6 June], he wants to improve the prerequisites for the normalization of relations between Germany and Iraq. Stercken said on West German Radio that a meeting with Iraqi President Saddam Husayn is also planned.
