940628 FT 28 JUN 94 / International Company News: DTB to revise product margins Deutsche Terminborse, the German futures and options exchange, plans to alter the margin-parameters on certain important derivative products because of continued high volatility, Reuter reports from Frankfurt. The additional-margin on the bund future will be raised to DM5,000 (200 ticks) from DM4,000 (160 ticks) per contract, and that for the Bobl future will be increased to DM2,500 (100 ticks) from DM2,000 (80 ticks), the DTB said. The margin calculation interval on some stock options would be changed, the exchange said. The interval on Allianz options would be raised to 7 per cent from 6 per cent, the DTB said: for BMW options it would be lowered to 6 per cent from 9 per cent: for Dresdner Bank it would be raised to 8 per cent from 7 per cent: and for VW it would drop to 7 per cent from 8 per cent.
