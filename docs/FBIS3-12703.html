Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, the deputy chairman of the Presidential Council, last night had a telephone conversation with his brother, King al-Husayn Bin-Talal, king of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan, on the intimate fraternal relations between the two fraternal countries and the latest developments in the national arena since the signing of the document of pledge and accord in addition to developments affecting Arab affairs. The brother deputy chairman of the Presidential Council reaffirmed the importance of adhering to the document of pledge and accord and accelerating its implementation. He expressed thanks and appreciation to His Majesty King al-Husayn and to all the brothers in Jordan for their commendable efforts to help our country emerge from its current predicament. He also expressed thanks for efforts made to create conditions conducive to ending the military and security deterioration and for starting to (?implement) the document of pledge and accord.
