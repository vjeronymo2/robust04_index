930126 FT 26 JAN 93 / International Company News: W Australia banks to merge THE STATE government of Western Australia has proposed a merger of state-owned R&I Bank of Western Australia with Challenge Bank, a small commercial bank based in the state, AP-DJ reports from Perth. R&I Bank said the proposal had 'attractions' but it would prefer to wait until late 1994, after it expected to improve its financial performance. R&I Bank has total assets of ADollars 9.9bn (USDollars 6.8bn.) Assets at Challenge total ADollars 3.4bn.
