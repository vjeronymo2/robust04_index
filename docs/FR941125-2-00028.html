TIME AND DATES: Meeting will convene at 8:30 a.m., December 13, and adjourn at 2:15 p.m., December 14, 1994. PLACE: The Key Bridge Marriott, 1401 Lee Highway, Arlington, VA. STATUS: As required by section 10(a)(2) of the Federal Advisory Committee Act, 5 U.S.C. App. (1982), notice is hereby given of a meeting of the Marine Fisheries Advisory Committee (MAFAC). MAFAC was established by the Secretary of Commerce (Secretary) on February 17, 1971, to advise the Secretary on all living marine resource matters that are the responsibility of the Department of Commerce. This Committee ensures that the living marine resource policies and programs of this Nation are adequate to meet the needs of commercial and recreational fishermen, and environmental, state, consumer, academic, and other national interests. MATTERS TO BE CONSIDERED: December 13, 1994, 8:45 a.m. - 5:00 p.m., (1) Progress report: Administration's Fisheries Initiatives, (2) Status of World Fisheries, (3) 1995 NMFS Budget, (4) Amendments to the Magnuson Act, (5) World Bycatch Issues, and (6) NMFS Restructuring. December 14, 1994, 9:00 a.m. - 3:30 p.m., (1) MAFAC Subcommittee Activities, (2) Amendments to the Marine Mammal Protection Act, and (3) Report on U.N.
