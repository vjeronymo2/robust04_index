February 9, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

SAN GABRIEL VALLEY DIGEST: ARCADIA;

SCHOOL BOARD RACE OPENS

Residents interested in running for the school board in the April 18 election are invited to submit declarations of candidacy to the board.

The race will fill two of five board positions. Board member Mary E. Dougherty has filed for a second four-year term, but Dr. William Spuck said that because of professional commitments, he will not seek reelection. Their terms end June 30.

Interested people can obtain declaration of candidacy forms at the Arcadia Unified School District office or by calling the superintendent's office at (818) 446-0131. Completed forms are due on Feb. 22 by 4:30 p.m.
