Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 25 February (TANJUG) -- The UN secretary-general's special envoy, Yasushi Akashi, and the president of the Republic of Serb Krajina, Milan Martic, reached an agreement in Belgrade today on the bridge between Gradiska and Stara Gradiska. They agreed that the situation on the bridge is to be restored to what it was on 18 February when the police of the Republic of Serb Krajina took control of the bridge from the UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces]. After one hour of talks, Akashi and Martic said that it was agreed that the UN peace forces are to withdraw armored personnel carriers deployed around the bridge over the past few days and that, at the same time, members of the ministry of internal affairs of the republic of Serb Krajina are to withdraw as well.
