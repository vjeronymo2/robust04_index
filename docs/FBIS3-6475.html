Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 17 (KCNA) -- A delegation of the Workers Party of Korea [WPK] headed by Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the Central Committee of the party, came back home today from its visit to Vietnam and Laos. The delegation was met at Pyongyang Railway Station by Kim Yang-kon, vice-director of a Department of the Central Committee of the WPK, Vietnamese Ambassador to Korea Duong Chinh Thuc, and Chinese Ambassador to Korea Qiao Zonghuai.
