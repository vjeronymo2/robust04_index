Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song met with a U.S. religious leader and his party. On 29 January, the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song met with U.S. religious leader Rev. Billy Graham and his party who are visiting our country. Kim Yong-nam, vice premier and foreign minister; Kang Yong-sop, chairman of the Central Committee of the Korean Christian Federation; and Chong Chae-chol, chairman of the Central Committee of the Korea Catholics Association were present. During the meeting the U.S. religious leader conveyed U.S. President Bill Clinton's verbal message to the great leader Comrade Kim Il-song. The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song conversed with the guests in a warm and friendly atmosphere. The great leader Comrade Kim Il-song hosted a luncheon for the guests.
