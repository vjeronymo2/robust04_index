May 12, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

U.S. MILITARY ASSISTANCE TO EL SALVADOR

The Bush Administration's double standard has never been more odious and obvious than by its criticism of El Salvador's leaders for their cover-up and failure to prosecute those responsible for the killing of the six Jesuit priests.

In the meantime, this same Administration is doing a "soft shoe" on the arrest of a Mexican citizen, apparently by the DEA, in Mexico and his removal to the United States, all illegal and in violation of Mexican sovereignty.

In short, how can we "read my lips" when one talks out of both sides of the mouth?

WILLIAM T. KRAUSE

Palm Springs
