920115 FT 15 JAN 92 / World Trade News: BAA suspends CDollars 800m Toronto airport venture BAA, the UK-based airport operator, is pulling out of an CDollars 800m (Pounds 384.6m) bid to modernise two terminals at Toronto's international airport, writes Bernard Simon in Toronto. The company confirmed yesterday it had grown impatient with protracted delays by the Canadian government in calling for tenders for the project. Mr Chris Barlow, BAA's project director for Canada, said the company and its partners had suspended their venture 'and no one is putting in more money'. Prestwick bid, Page 9
