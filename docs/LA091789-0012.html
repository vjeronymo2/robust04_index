September 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ALLA BREVE

Schubert: Sonatas, D. 821, 384, & 408; Rondo, D. 895.Joseph Swensen, violin; Jeffrey Kahane, piano. RCA 7962-2-RC (compact disc). These performances are emotionally direct and remarkably sweet, yet without any hint of cloying sentimentality. The feeling is unaffected, the techniques sure and evenly yoked, and the results easily hold the ear. JOHN HENKEN
