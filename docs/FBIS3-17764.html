Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Excerpt] Rabat, 29 Mar -- The Algerian security forces have killed four armed men suspected of killing Konstantin Kukukshin, a driver employed at the Russian Federation Embassy. According to diplomatic sources, these men were also involved in the killing of high-ranking Algerian diplomat Belkacem Touati. The details of the killing of the Russian representative are as yet unknown. The competent Algerian bodies are continuing their investigation of the circumstances of this act of terrorism. Local newspapers report that the Russian Embassy in Algiers has already received a number of threats from local Islamists belonging, it is suggested, to the underground "Armed Islamic Group."
