930925 FT 25 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Highcroft Highcroft Investment Trust, a financial trust, reported profits before tax of Pounds 663,000 for the six months to June 30. The outcome compared with profits of Pounds 684,000 last time and came despite net rental income improving 14 per cent to Pounds 492,000 (Pounds 432,000). Realised capital profits dipped to Pounds 123,000 (Pounds 220,000). Earnings per share, including realised capital profits, were 8.8p (9.2p); excluding them the figure was 6.9p (5.8p). The interim dividend goes up to 1.9p (1.8p).
