BFN [Excerpt] A seminar of the Ministry of People's Armed Forces was held on 15 June to mark the 30th anniversary of the start of work by Comrade Kim Chong-il, the supreme commander of the Korean People's Army [KPA], at the Central Committee of the Workers Party of Korea [WPK]. Present at the seminar were Comrade Choe Kwang, member of the Political Burau of the WPK Central Committee and chief of the General Staff of the KPA, Comrade Kim Pong-yol, vice marshal of the KPA, responsible functionaries of the Ministry of People's Armed Forces, and generals and officers of the KPA.
