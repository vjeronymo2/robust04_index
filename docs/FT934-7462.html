931119 FT 19 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Thomas Locker Pounds 329,000 in red Thomas Locker (Holdings), the engineering equipment maker, fell into losses of Pounds 329,000 for the six months to the end of September, against profits of Pounds 188,000. Turnover was down from Pounds 18.7m to Pounds 17.3m. The company blamed a decline in orders for companies serving the European capital goods markets. Losses per share were 0.67p (earnings 0.4p). The interim dividend is 0.2p (0.35p).
