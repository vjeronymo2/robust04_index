Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Text] Terrorists have killed eight people near the Mardin District of the Diyarbakir-Elazig highway. According to information issued by Unal Erkan, state of emergency region governor, terrorists stopped two buses, which were on their way from Diyarbakir to Elazig, at 4 km from Mardin around 2100. Terrorists abducted eight persons from the buses, brought them to a rural area, and killed them. Security forces' operations are under way in the region.
