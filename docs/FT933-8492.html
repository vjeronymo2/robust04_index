930816 FT 16 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: Sarajevo airlift goes ahead amid heavy criticism A group of seriously wounded Bosnians were airlifted out of Sarajevo to Britain and Sweden yesterday amid claims that the operation had become a publicity exercise by western governments. Dr Patrick Peillod, the head of the United Nations medical evacuation committee, criticised the motives behind the airlifts. Amid widespread criticism of insufficient co-ordination between the different aid groups, the British government said it would be 'reassessing' the situation before it pressed ahead with its offer to send a second evacuation aircraft.
