940608 FT 08 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Turkey Trust net assets nearly halved Net asset value per share of Turkey Trust, the investment trust dealing mainly on the Istanbul Stock Exchange, dropped to 181.35p, as at April 30 1994, compared with 347.41p a year previous. For the six months to that date the available figure was a Pounds 132,000 loss, against Pounds 60,000, equal to 1.34p (0.61p) per share.
