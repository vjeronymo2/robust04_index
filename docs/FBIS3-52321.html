Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By Nabil Ghazzawi] [Excerpts] Minister of State for Foreign Affairs Talal Sat'an al-Hasan has emphasized that Jordan, under His Majesty King Husayn's leadership, highly evaluates the Jordanian-Qatari relations, and said this evaluation stems from both the heart and mind. Answering a question posed by AL-DUSTUR on the official two-day visit to Jordan today by His Highness Shaykh Hamad bin-Jasim Al Thani, foreign minister of sisterly Qatar, al-Hasan said we view bilateral relations with respect and sincere desire to develop them. [passage omitted] Al-Hasan said the State of Qatar has informed the Gulf Cooperation Council [GCC] states of the progress of Jordanian-Qatari relations, which have begun to positively and substantially improve, and that the GCC states highly valued this development. The minister said the two countries agree that moves should be made to foster Arab cohesion, and that this can develop through bilateral or trilateral cooperation and through joint cooperation. He said this cooperation could be elevated to reach a comprehensive and full reconciliation. [passage omitted]
