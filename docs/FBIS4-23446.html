BFN [Text] The Government of Chad has reported the merger of 450 rebels belonging to Moise Kete's National Awakening Committee for Peace and Democracy in Chad [CSNPD] and the National Recovery Council [CNR], which is based in the east of the country. President Deby, who announced this at a cabinet meeting on 2 June, directed the government to deal with these mergers properly. Moise Kete's CSNPD southern rebels this morning denied the report which, according to them, is intended to undermine morale in the ranks of the CSNPD fighters, the movement's spokesman declared.
