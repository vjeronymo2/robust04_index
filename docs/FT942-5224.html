940606 FT 06 JUN 94 / Business Travel: Taiwan's earthquake Visitors to remote parts of Taiwan this week should contact its embassies if they are concerned about any repercussions of the strong earthquake, measuring 6.0 on the Richter scale, which shook the island yesterday, writes Lisa Wood. The earthquake killed at least one person, injured two, and triggered a landslide in the northeast of the island and a fire in Taipei. The quake, centred near the coast about 34km south of the northeastern county of Ilan, shook buildings in Taipei and other cities.
