940601 FT 01 JUN 94 / Manila trade deficit up 30% The Philippines posted a trade deficit of Dollars 1.66bn (Pounds 1.1bn) in the first quarter, up 30 per cent on the year before, as total imports continued to grow faster than exports, writes Jose Galang in Manila. The central bank said yesterday that total imports rose 22 per cent to Dollars 4.58bn while exports increased 18 per cent to Dollars 2.92bn.
