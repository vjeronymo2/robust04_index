BFN [Text] According to a well-informed source in Kiev, the Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk and the Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin had a telephone conversation on Monday to discuss the attack of the Ukrainian special forces on the Odessa division of Black Sea Fleet mothballed ships and the arrest of three naval officers. The office of the Russian head of government confirmed the fact of the conversation, but refused to disclose the details. According to sources in the Russian Cabinet, on Monday the Russian government intends to issue an official statement on the attack in Odessa.
