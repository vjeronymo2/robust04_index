BFN [Report by E van Wyk] [Text] Pretoria April 22 SAPA -- Finland's newly elected President Mr Martti Ahtisaari plans to attend the inauguration of South Africa's new president in Pretoria on May 10, the Finland Embassy said on Friday. Mr Ahtisaari, a former career diplomat and a former director-general of the Finnish Foreign Affairs Ministry, was for years closely connected with southern Africa. "His role in the developments leading to Namibia's independence was a particularly difficult one," the embassy statement said. Mr Ahtisaari was at the time the special representative of the United Nations secretary-general for Namibia.
