BFN [Leonid Pozdeyev report: "Urals `Blue Helmets' Bring Peace"] [Text] Colonel General Yuriy Grekov, commander of the Urals Military District troops, has conducted an inspection of the Russian battalion of peacekeeping forces formed on the basis of the Urals Military District. The battalion is staffed exclusively by volunteers. Lieutenant Colonel Aleksandr Terekhin is commander of the Urals "blue helmets." Under an interstate agreement reached under the aegis of the United Nations, the Urals battalion, together with other Russian peacekeeping subunits, will be located along the border between Georgia and Abkhazia.
