Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Commenting on a recent statement by the Turkish Foreign Ministry alternate spokesman accusing our country of alleged violation of the Muslim minority's human rights, Foreign Ministry spokesman Kostandinos Bikas said that the government condemns Turkey's attempt to intervene in Greek decisionmaking. The spokesman said: Greece is a just state and fully respects human rights. Turkey is neither concerned nor interested in alleged violations of the Muslim minority's human rights in Greece. It is merely seeking excuses, encouraging nationalistic extremism, distorting situations and facts, and unhesitatingly using the minority issue, obviously realizing the damage done to Greek-Turkish relations and stability in the region.
