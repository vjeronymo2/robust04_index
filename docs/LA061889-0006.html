June 18, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

I'm grateful that I saw Howard Rosenberg's enthusiastic review and took on the 12-week commitment of "Game, Set and Match" on "Mystery!" Ian Holm's performance was so masterful that after one of his brilliant scenes, I found myself applauding the screen for the first time in 35 years of avid TV viewing.

Meredith Brucker, San Marino
