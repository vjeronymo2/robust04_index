March 24, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MAYBE RAIDERS SIMPLY HAD A FEW TOO MANY PRESSING DIFFICULTIES

Something doesn't add up. California taxpayers, through a temporary increase in sales taxes, are forced to assist Oakland residents who suffered losses as a result of the October earthquake. Yet, the city of Oakland somehow came up with $600 million for Al Davis to move the Raiders there.

LEON SMITH

Anaheim Hills
