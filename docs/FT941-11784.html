940202 FT 02 FEB 94 / International Company News: Strong start to second big Italian sell-off Italy's second big public privatisation was closed ahead of schedule last night after heavy oversubscription for shares in Istituto Mobiliare Italiano, writes Haig Simonian in Milan. According to the Rome-based financial services group, about 350,000 applications were received from Italian investors on Monday and Tuesday. The demand covered about 350m shares - almost five times the amount of stock offered to domestic retail investors.
