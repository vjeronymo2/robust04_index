Language: English Article Type:BFN [From the "News At Ten"] [Text] Palestine President Yasir 'Arafat once again accused Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin of prevaricating on Palestinian autonomy. Speaking to reporters upon arrival in Amman today, President 'Arafat criticized Rabin for suggesting a delay in the April 13th deadline for Israel's withdrawal from Gaza and Jericho. He said he was astonished by Rabin's insistence not to implement the PLO-Israeli accord accurately and honestly. [Begin recording] ['Arafat] I am completely astonished why he is insisting not to follow up and not to implement accurately and honestly what had been agreed upon. [Unidentified reporter] When do you expect to sign the agreement on the self-rule? ['Arafat] What he is saying is that he is trying to bypass what had been agreed upon. [Reporter] When do you expect to be back in Jericho? ['Arafat] We have to wait and see. [end recording]
