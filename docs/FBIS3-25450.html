Language: French Article Type:BFN [Communique issued by the prefect of Dakar on 17 February] [Text] The prefect announces that the proposed march by the And-Jef/African Party for Democracy and Socialism slated for tomorrow, 18 February, has been banned for the following reason: threat to public order. Any violations of the provisions of the present order will be punished in accordance with the laws and regulations in force.
