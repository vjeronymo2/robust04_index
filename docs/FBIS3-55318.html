Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] By decree of the head of state of the Republic of Georgia, a bureau for coordinating international aid rendered to Georgia is to be established at the cabinet of ministers of the republic of Georgia and under the supervision of the deputy prime minister. The bureau is to coordinate humanitarian aid to Georgia received from foreign countries, governmental and non-governmental structures, international organizations, and private individuals; find possible sources for rendering aid, planning measures for their realization, and securing their implementation. The bureau council is to include the following persons: 1. foreign minister of the republic of Georgia 2. minister of finance of the republic of Georgia 3. minister of economy of the republic of Georgia 4. chairman of the committee for foreign economic relations of the republic of Georgia 5. chairman of the committee for science and technology of the republic of Georgia 6. president of the national bank of the republic of Georgia The deputy prime minister of the republic of Georgia, Irakli Menagharishvili is to be appointed chairman of the bureau. The cabinet of ministers of the republic of Georgia is to adopt regulations of the bureau, discuss, and resolve the issue of creating appropriate material and technical conditions with the aim of using rationally the international aid rendered to Georgia.
