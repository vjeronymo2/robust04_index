920330 FT 30 MAR 92 / Construction Contracts: Pounds 22m work for Hall & Tawse Contracts totalling over Pounds 22m have been awarded to the HALL & TAWSE GROUP, the construction division of Raine Industries. Hall & Tawse Southern in Southampton has won a contract to design and build a Sainsbury's Homebase store at Hove. The contract, worth Pounds 2.2m, also includes the construction of an ambulance station on the same site. Hall & Tawse Scotland's latest orders total Pounds 10m, including a housing development contract for over Pounds 3m with Kincardine & District Council. Other work includes a Pounds 1.7m refurbishment at the premises of the Porthlethen meat factory and a Pounds 1m refurbishmement contract for Levi Strauss in Dundee. Hall & Tawse Partnership has been awarded a Pounds 3.5m project by West Dorset District Council to provide more than 100 dwellings at Bridport.
