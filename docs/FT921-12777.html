920124 FT 24 JAN 92 / World Trade News: GEC Alsthom wins order GEC Alsthom, the Anglo-French engineering group, extended its recent run of big power plant orders yesterday by announcing a contract from Electricite de France (EdF) for its 1500MW 'Arabelle' turbine generator, writes Andrew Baxter. The generator, the world's largest single shaft model, will be supplied by GEC Alsthom's electromechanical division for the first unit of the Civaux nuclear power station, near Poitiers. Terms were not disclosed, but the order brings to Pounds 1bn the power plant business won by GEC Alsthom in 10 days.
