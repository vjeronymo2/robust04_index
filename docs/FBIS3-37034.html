Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow February 11 TASS--"Payment of debts to regional radio and television centres for work done in the 4th quarter of 1993 was started today. This will be done according to a schedule, approved by the Russian Finance Ministry," head of the Russian Government's press service Valentin Sergeyev stated here today on behalf of Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin. The statement notes that the schedule envisages the complete payment of all the debts in the first quarter of 1994. The demands of the communication workers union will be carefully studied and fulfilled, proceeding from existing possibilities. Chernomyrdin's meeting with heads of the Communication Workers Union is expected to be held within the next few days. The chairman of the Russian Government reminded the heads and personnel of regional radio-television broadcasting centres about the difficulty of the current situation and asked them to resume their work in accordance with the usual broadcasting schedules.
