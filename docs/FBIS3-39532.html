Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos said the Council of Ministers unanimously accepted the prime minister's proposal for measures against Skopje, and that the vast majority of the Greek people agree with this decision. He said: The Greek consulate in Skopje has already suspended its operations. UN Secretary General Butrus Butrus-Ghali will be briefed today with a letter from Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias. The spokesman stressed that the next Council of Ministers meeting will discuss the great development plans.
