Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, December 31 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing met here today with U.S. Foremost Group's Chairman and Chief Executive Officer James Si-cheng Chao and his wife Ruth Mu-lan Chao. U.S. Senator Mitchell McConnell and his wife Elaine L. Chao, president of the United Way of America, were also present at the meeting. The guests arrived here Wednesday [29 December] at the invitation of the China State Shipbuilding Corporation.
