940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / International Company News: Laurentian Bank Laurentian Bank, part of the Desjardins financial services group, posted second-quarter net profit of CDollars 9.3m, or 44 cents a share, ahead from CDollars 8.3m, or 38 cents, a year earlier. First-half profit was CDollars 18.7m, or 88 cents a share, against CDollars 15.8m, or 74 cents.
