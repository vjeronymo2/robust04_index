921016 FT 16 OCT 92 / BNL links inquiry urged The US House Judiciary Committee has renewed its formal request for the appointment of an independent counsel 'to investigate possible criminal activities of high-ranking Department of Justice and Executive Branch officials' involved in the Banca Nazionale del Lavoro prosecution in Atlanta. Attorney General William Barr refused a similar request in August, but Mr Jack Brooks, chairman of the Judiciary Committee, yesterday said new evidence showed the situation 'cries out for an objective and impartial investigation - outside the Justice Department.'
