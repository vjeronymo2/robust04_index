930304 FT 04 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Radius sharply higher at Pounds 1.25m Continued progress through the second six months enabled Radius, the USM-quoted computer systems and maintenance company, to lift profits from Pounds 366,000 to Pounds 1.23m for the year to November 30. Rationalisation was behind a decline in turnover to Pounds 24m (Pounds 28m). Earnings improved to 2.9p (0.8p) and a final dividend of 1.8p holds the total at 2.7p.
