May 3, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Warner Bros. Gala: David L. Wolper, Jack Haley Jr. and Steven Spielberg will join forces June 2 to produce "A Celebration of Tradition," a black-tie-only gala to commemerorate the re-dedication of historic Warner Bros. Studios. The producers plan for the event to feature hundreds of singers and dancers, atmospheric sets, musical numbers and appearences by many major stars. BETH KLEID
