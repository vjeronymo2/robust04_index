Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Feb. 23 -- As of February 15 this year foreign embassies in Vietnam are able to issue directly visas for Vietnamese people who go abroad to do official business. The Consulate Department under the Ministry for Foreign Affairs will continue to be responsible for arranging visas for high-level party, National Assembly and government delegations which go abroad to do official business. For other delegations and individuals after the issue of passports the Foreign Ministry will grant notes and introduction papers for specialized cadres of the offices and institutions in charge or representative of the delegation going abroad so that they come to foreign embassies to apply for a visa.
