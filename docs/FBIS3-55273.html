Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Sources in presidential circles reported to Interfax that a new structure to be in charge of ties with constituent members is supposed to be created in the government currently under reorganization. Vice Premier Yuriy Yarov is expected to head it. The new structural body is to be created on the basis of the State committee on the Federation and nationalities and the unification of several other structures. The sources indicated that the present State committee on the Federation would be withdrawn from the influence of the Party of Russian Unity and Concord [PRUC] headed by the vice premier and leader of the state committee Sergey Shakhray. Ramazan Abdulatipov, another prominent functionary of the PRUC, works as deputy chairman of the State committee on the Federation. PRUC member Aleksandr Kotenkov heads one of the major structural branches of the committee.
