Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tokyo, March 24 KYODO -- The first regular air service between the northern Japan port city of Hakodate and Yuzhno-Sakhalinsk, capital of Sakhalin in the Russian Far East, will start April 4, Transport Ministry officials said Thursday [24 March]. The date was set when the ministry gave the go-ahead Thursday for the Russian airline Aeroflot to operate two flights a week on the route on Mondays and Thursdays, they said. It will use a 32-seat plane.
