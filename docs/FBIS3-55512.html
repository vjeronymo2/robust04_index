Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Glazovskaya] [Text] Ashgabat January 10 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin and Turkmen leader Saparmurad Niyazov discussed via telephone on Monday [10 January] the implementation of Russian-Turkmen agreements. The two leaders confirmed the importance of the documents signed by Russia and Turkmenistan on the eve of the new year as the basis for mutually advantageous and equal partnership. They stressed that the agreements help promote mutual understanding and friendship between the two nations. Niyazov voiced support for Russian reform carried out by Yeltsin and his government. The two presidents also discussed prospects for cooperation in using marine resources of the Caspian Sea and the possibility of holding a summit meeting of the Caspian Sea states in 1994.
