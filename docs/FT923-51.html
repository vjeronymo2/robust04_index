920930 FT 30 SEP 92 / World Stock Markets: Brazil SAO PAULO saw an 8.3 per cent gain in heavy trading by midsession yesterday, writes Bill Hinchberger, the Bovespa index hitting 45,574 in turnover of Dollars 69m for the morning session against Dollars 70m for the whole of Monday. The impeachment vote session against the Brazilian president, Mr Fernando Collor was scheduled to begin at about 1400 local time.
