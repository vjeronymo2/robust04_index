931013 FT 13 OCT 93 / UK Company News: GPG offshoot bids for Power Brewing Guinness Peat Group, the UK investment vehicle of Sir Ron Brierley, the New Zealand entrepreneur, said its wholly owned Australian subsidiary, GPG Pty, would make an offer for Power Brewing Company of Australia. The offer would be 44 cents a share for 50 per cent of each member's fully paid ordinary 50 cent share in PBC. Total consideration would be about ADollars 17.32m (Pounds 7.46m). PBC, which is listed on the Australian stock exchange, is mainly involved in marketing Power's beer through its joint venture with Carlton and United Breweries. For the nine months to June 30 it reported operating profits after tax of ADollars 2.13m (Pounds 920,000). GPG is currently entitled to 8.3 per cent of its issued capital.
