BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Melnikova; Moscow ITAR-TASS in English at 1450 GMT on 21 April transmits a service message killing this item] [Text] Moscow, April 21 (TASS) -- "We do not find it acceptable to remove from the prepared package of documents the agreement on social security for military personnel during the troop withdrawal" Russian presidential spokesman Vyacheslav Kostikov told ITAR-TASS on Thursday, commenting on the results of yesterday's session of the Latvian Sejm (Parliament). According to the spokesman, Russia intends to insist that all of the four prepared documents be signed at the same time. Kostikov noted that President Boris Yeltsin, in his scheduled telephone conversation today with the Latvian president, might touch upon that subject. The Russian spokesman said the Latvian Sejm had been "overwhelmed by emotions". He emphasized that Russia is interested in continuing the negotiating process with Latvia, expressing the hope that "time would solve" such problems.
