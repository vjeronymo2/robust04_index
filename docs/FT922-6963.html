920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Grosvenor Inns joins USM with an Pounds 8.1m tag GROSVENOR INNS was yesterday launched on the USM, with 6m ordinary shares being placed by Rea Brothers at 105p. Dealings in the shares will begin on May 27. With the net proceeds of Pounds 5.7m, Grosvenor is to expand its portfolio of pubs by acquiring two London pub retailing operations - Slug & Lettuce, a group of 13 pubs, seven of which trade under the Slug & Lettuce name, for Pounds 4.46m cash, and the Hutchison Houses, two City pubs, for Pounds 640,000 cash. At the placing price Grosvenor is valued at Pounds 8.1m and the shares being placed represent about 78 per cent of its enlarged capital. Broker to the issue is Panmure Gordon. The acquired 15 pubs will boost Grosvenor's total estate to 30. On a pro forma basis, the net assets of the enlarged group will be about Pounds 7m. Mr Tim Thwaites, non-executive chairman, previously worked with the four executive directors at Grand Metropolitan. Mr Thwaites was managing director of Chef & Brewer and then chairman of Whitbread's retail division. Mr Peter Whitehead and Mr Gary Pettet are joint managing directors. The former left GrandMet in 1985 to establish Cromwell Taverns with Mr Michael Mills, Grosvenor finance director. The latter became a Cromwell director in 1988.
