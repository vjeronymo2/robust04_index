BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 19 May (SNARK) -- Expressing their disagreement to the 14 May Georgian-Abkhazian agreement signed in Moscow the faction of Respublicans left today's session just after the Parliament's non-acceptance of the suggestion expressed by the faction -- to put into agenda two questions: to confirm the authorities of those who signed the agreement and on the agreement's disavowal. One of the faction's leaders Ivlian Khaindrava told SNARK the process of negotiations follows the Russian-Abkhazian scenario. The Respublican Faction will participate in the work of commissions. They will be present at the Parliament's sessions if only Georgian Parliament discusses the problem of conflict in Abkhazia or the economic problems.
