921031 FT 31 OCT 92 / DVLA staff hold one-day strike UNIONS yesterday said that about 2,000 out of 3,500 civil servants took part in a one-day strike at the Driver and Vehicle Licensing Agency in Swansea, south Wales, over a threat to hive off 3,000 jobs to private companies. Regional offices, including Liverpool, Manchester and Sheffield, were also affected. More than 1,300 National Savings clerical staff in Glasgow and Durham held a half-day strike over moves to put work out to tender.
