940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Euromoney sets placing price Euromoney Publications, the publisher and training and conference organiser, has set a price of Pounds 18.75p in its placing of 1.25m new ordinary shares. Gross proceeds of Pounds 23.4m are intended to pay for two acquisitions, refinance another and provide finance for further purchases. Euromoney has entered into a placing agreement with SG Warburg Securities and Merrill Lynch International. The shares added 75p to Pounds 19.25p yesterday.
