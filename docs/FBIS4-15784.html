BFN [Text] Stepakanert, April 11 (SNARK)--An independent Russian-language paper "NAGORNY KARABAKH" began publishing in Karabakh. Its first issue cited the declaration of Arkadiy Gukasyan, Karabakh foreign minister: "Karabakh is now independent de-facto and nobody can deny this fact". Marsel Petrosyan, founder and commercial director of the paper, told SNARK the main readers of the paper will be those Armenians in the CIS, who would like to be aware of the real events in their mother land. The circulation of the paper is 5,000 copies. It is planned to publish "NAGORNY KARABAKH" in English too.
