December 14, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: MISSION VIEJO;

COLLEGE BOARD ELECTS BIOLOGIST PRESIDENT

Marcia Milchiker has been elected president of the Saddleback Community College District Board of Trustees, replacing Joan J. Hueter.

Milchiker, a Laguna Hills research biologist, was elected this week during a secret ballot at the board's annual organizational meeting.

She was vice president during the past year.

It is the first time that Milchiker, who has been a trustee since 1985, has been president of the eight-member board. The president conducts the board's meetings, appoints committees and represents the board at various on- and off-campus functions.

Milchiker said a focus of her term will be the search for a third campus site in the district, in addition to Saddleback and Irvine Valley colleges.

Trustee Robert L. Moore was elected vice president, and Hueter was elected clerk.
