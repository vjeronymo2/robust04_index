BFN [Excerpt] Abu Dhabi, 22 Apr (MENA)-- The newspaper AL-ITTIHAD of Abu Dhabi today reports that Palestinian President Yasir 'Arafat will pay an urgent visit to Jordan next week to meet for the first time with the leaders of "Hamas" [Islamic Resistance Movement], which recently offered a truce with Israel in return for the latter's withdrawal from the occupied territories and its dismantling of settlements. The paper welcomes the visit, noting that it is the first time 'Arafat has met with Hamas leaders, particularly Muhammad Nazzal and Ibrahim Ghawshah, to prepare for full reconciliation between the PLO and the Movement.
