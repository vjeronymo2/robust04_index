941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / Company News (This Week): Suez - Other Companies the French financial services and investment group is to announce its half-year results for the year to June 30 on Wednesday, with some analysts predicting profits of FFr800m (Pounds 96m) compared with FFr516m at last year's interim. One significant influence will be the way it handles the FFr1.3bn loss on disposal of its stake in Victoire, the insurance group, which was bought by Commercial Union earlier this year.
