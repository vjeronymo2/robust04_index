BFN [Text] The Hague -- The environment committee of the multilateral Middle East peace negotiations met for one day of talks in the Hague today, Thursday. Informed Foreign Ministry sources said Jordan did not send an official delegation to the session because we are still awaiting Washington's decision on the Port of al-'Aqabah blockade, anticipated this week. Jordan, sources told AL-RA'Y, sent its ambassador in Rome Hasan Abu-Na'imah to the Hague meeting as an observer rather than participant.
