941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / Tajik deputy premier killed Tajik Deputy Premier Munavarsho Nazriev was killed yesterday when a land mine planted on a highway exploded under his car, Itar-Tass news agency said. Mr Nazriev, 55, was driving from the capital, Dushanbe, to the Garm region near the Pamir Mountains when his car hit the mine. He and a bodyguard were killed outright, and two aides badly injured. Tajikistan, poorest of the former Soviet republics, has been devastated by a two-year civil war. Yesterday was the first day of a ceasefire between the Russian-backed government and Moslem fundamentalists.
