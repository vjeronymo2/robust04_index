BFN [Text] Romanian President Ion Iliescu said today that the results of his visit to Paris were positive. He was speaking after having lunch with French Prime Minister Edouard Balladur. "In France, we always find friends and partners with whom we have emotional ties and ties of common interest in all spheres," he said. The two men spoke of the Partnership for Peace Initiative, Romania's French-backed bid for association with the European Union, and bilateral economic relations.
