BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan June 2 TASS -- An attempt was staged early on Thursday on the life of Vladimir Grigoryan, the head of the investigation department of the Armenian prosecutor's office. A gunman opened submachine gun fire at him when he came out of his home heading for office. Grigoryan was not wounded; however, the driver of his car got a bullet wound. The gunman escaped and the site of the attack has been barricaded by police. No official statements on the incident have been made yet.
