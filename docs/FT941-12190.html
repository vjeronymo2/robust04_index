940201 FT 01 FEB 94 / New rules for USM companies Companies whose shares are quoted on the Unlisted Securities Market will be required to meet certain corporate governance requirements, the Stock Exchange said yesterday. They will have to disclose to shareholders the extent to which they comply with the Cadbury committee's code of best practice for boards of directors and will also have to state in their annual reports the unexpired term-of-service contracts for any director who is due for re-election.
