Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Several people suspected of involvement in shelling the residence of President Jokhar Dudayev were detained in Groznyy, the Chechen capital, Shamsuddi Vakhidov, head of the anti-terrorist squad in the republic's Interior Ministry, told Interfax. He added that a criminal case had been launched by the investigation branch of the State Security Department. A guided missile was fired from a nine-storey house under construction one kilometer away from the presidential palace late Saturday. No significant damage was done. Officials in the presidential headquarters did not confirm that the shelling had taken place.
