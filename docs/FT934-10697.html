931104 FT 04 NOV 93 / Rubble rouser Part of Rover's former complex at Cowley, Oxfordshire, is being redeveloped as part of the carmaker's Pounds 200m project to consolidate its sprawling plant on to one site. The original plant, including the Cowley clock tower which is being retained in the new development, was built on the site of an old military academy where the father of pioneer car manufacturer William Morris, who produced the Cowley in 1915, went to school.
