BFN [Text] Alojz Englis, the president of the Confederation of Trade Unions, has sent a letter to Finance Minister Rudolf Filkus, drawing his attention to the violation of trade union freedoms as outlined in the accord of the International Labor Organization on the freedom of association and the protection of trade unions' right of association. In his letter, Alojz Englis stressed that the Finance Ministry fundamentally violated trade union rights by a decision calling on the trade unions that are members of the confederation to present their account books outlining their assets to appropriate tax bodies by 31 March. Englis pointed out that he will inform the international trade union headquarters about this, and, if the Finance Ministry does not change its stance, he will forward an official complaint to the International Labor Organization about the violation of trade union rights in Slovakia.
