920428 FT 28 APR 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Demand continued to be strong and active reports the Tea Broker's Association. Landed prices for bright liquoring and good medium East Africans showed further advances of between 5 and 20p. The balance sold readily at fully firm rates. Central Africans were also well supported and dearer. The small selection of Ceylon teas on offer were much dearer with quality. Offshore. Bright liquoring descriptions often gained 5 to 10p while others generally tended dearer. The highest price realised this week was 221p for a Rwanda pf. 1. Quotations: quality 180p. nom good medium 135p. medium 118p. low medium 92p
