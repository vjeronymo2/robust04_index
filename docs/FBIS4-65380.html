BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Alekos Mikhailidhis met today with the German parliamentary delegation that is visiting Cyprus at the invitation of the Cypriot House of Representatives. The foreign minister briefed the German deputies on the latest developments in the Cyprus issue. They exchanged views on Cyprus' progress in joining the European Union [EU] and discussed Cypriot-German relations. Earlier, the delegation met acting President Alexis Galanos. In a statement before the meeting at the Presidential Palace, the head of the German delegation offered assurances that their interest in a Cyprus solution will continue. In another statement, he said he is heading a German Bundestag initiative to put pressure on the German Government to influence Ankara so that it will take a conciliatory stance on the Cyprus issue. The initiative also advocates speeding up the procedure for Cyprus joining the EU.
