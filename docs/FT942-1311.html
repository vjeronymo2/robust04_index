940624 FT 24 JUN 94 / International Company News: Danish broker stops trading Benzon Bankier, the Danish private banking company which filed for bankruptcy on Tuesday, suspended payments yesterday by Benzon & Benzon, its stockbroking arm. Benzon & Benzon is one of Copenhagen's small but respected independent brokers, with extensive international connections. Last year it made a profit of DKr9.7m (Dollars 1.5m) and had assets of DKr107m.
