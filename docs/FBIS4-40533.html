BFN [Text] Moscow June 16 TASS -- Russia has abolished air communications with its break-away southern republic of Chechnya. The Russian Ministry of Transport has sent a message to Groznyy that it suspends all flights from Russian airports to Chechnya as well as those from the Chechen capital to any airport of Russia, Shirvani Pashayev, an aide of the Chechen information minister, told ITAR- TASS by telephone on Thursday. Pashayev said that the flights were suspended under a pretext that Chechnya gives no guarantees of safety in the air space over Groznyy.
