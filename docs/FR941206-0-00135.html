Compliance Dates The final rule promulgated today establishes additional air standards for TSDF owners and operators subject to 40 CFR part 264 or 40 CFR part 265. In addition, this rule amends the conditions for hazardous waste generators accumulating waste on-site in RCRA permit-exempt tanks and containers pursuant to 40 CFR 262.34(a) to include air emission control requirements. All of these rule requirements are effective as of June 5, 1995. All hazardous waste placed in the affected tanks, surface impoundments, containers, and other affected units on and after this date must be managed in accordance with the requirements of the final rule. This includes implementing the required air emission controls on an affected unit or performing the required waste determinations and recordkeeping to indicate that the affected unit is exempted from these air emission control requirements. Under circumstances when the air emission control equipment required to comply with the rule cannot be operational at an existing TSDF by June 5, 1995, an implementation schedule for installation of the equipment must be developed and placed in the facility operating records no later than June 5, 1995. In such cases, the facility owner or operator must have all air emission controls required by the final rule in operation no later than December 8, 1997.
