920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Kleen-E-Ze buys mail order company Kleen-E-Ze Holdings is acquiring Direct Choice UK for a maximum Pounds 1m through its Innovations (Mail Order) arm. The consideration will be satisfied by up to 100,000 shares and cash. If the profit-related price is less than Pounds 175,000 Kleen-E-Ze has the option to terminate the agreement. Tangible fixed assets being acquired are valued at Pounds 65,000 and customer lists at Pounds 150,000.
