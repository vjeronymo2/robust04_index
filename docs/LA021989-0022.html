February 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THE BIG MIX

We recognize the complaints against cultural institutions, which sometimes frustrate rather than encourage working artists ("A Theater of Cultural Diversity?" by Victor Valle). But our experience with Plaza de la Raza has been positive. As an emerging comedy troupe, we asked for Plaza's support; it found merit in our work and gave us access to their fine rehearsal facilities.

LUISA LESCHIN, ARMANDO MOLINA, RICK NAJERA, DIANE RODRIGUEZ

Latins Anonymous

Los Angeles
