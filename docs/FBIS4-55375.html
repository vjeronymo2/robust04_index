BFN [Text] Warsaw, May 12 -- President Lech Walesa received the Chief of Staff of the Chinese Army General Zhang Wannian on Thursday and said "It would be beautiful for Poland" if there was a Polish-Chinese alliance. "I am very pleased that I will become acquainted with the point of view of a great state, a great army on what is happening in Europe," Walesa said. The chief of staff of the Polish army, General Tadeusz Wilecki attended the meeting. "After these great changes, attempted changes, we are in a quite strange and unclear situation," Walesa said, commenting on the feeling of insecurity among the countries of central and eastern Europe. "It is true that the Soviet Union no longer exists but there are other countries which have nuclear weapons at their disposal. We are searching for security. We are searching for an alliance which could guarantee this security," Walesa explained adding that it would be beautiful if there were such an alliance with China.
