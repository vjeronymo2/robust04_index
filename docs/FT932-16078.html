930407 FT 07 APR 93 / International Company News: Mystery buyer of Fairfax stake ORD Minnett Securities, an Australian stock brokerage firm, yesterday bought slightly more than 5 per cent of John Fairfax, Australia's second-biggest newspaper publisher, AP-DJ reports from Sydney. The transaction cost about ADollars 75m (USDollars 54m). Ord Minnett would not disclose for whom it bought the 36m shares, nor comment on speculation that the buyer was either Mr Kerry Packer, the Australian businessman, or Mr Conrad Black, the Canadian publisher.
