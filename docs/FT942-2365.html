940620 FT 20 JUN 94 / Business Travel: Jamaican investment Jamaican hotelier Gordon 'Butch' Stewart has agreed to replace Canadian investors who backed out of a deal to take over Air Jamaica two weeks ago, according to Reuters. Stewart is expected to fill the 25 per cent equity gap created when Cochrane Investments Corp of Toronto, Canada, withdrew its pledge made last month to invest up to Dollars 14m in the airline.
