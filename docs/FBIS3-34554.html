Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] In Uttar Pradesh, all educational institutions in Varanasi have been closed down until tomorrow. Police and contingents of the Rapid Action Force are keeping a vigil in the city which witnessed violence on Monday. The chief minister, Mr. Mulayam Singh Yadav, has announced ex-gratia payment of 1 lakh rupees each to the dependents of four persons who lost their lives in the violence. The incident followed the killing of a BSP [Bahujan Samaj Party] leader on Friday last. The Congress-I president, Mr. P.V. Narasimha Rao, has deputed a team of senior Congress-I leaders to visit Varanasi for an on-the- spot study of the situation there. The team will meet the cross section of the people and inform the party president about the details of the incidents. According to a Congress-I spokesman, the team consists of the union minister of state for home affairs, Mr. P.M. Sayeed; the former Uttar Pradesh chief minister, Mr. Ram Naresh Yadav; Mr. Ramnath Shankar Shastri, MP; Mr. Harish Rawat, and Mr. Jain Mishra.
