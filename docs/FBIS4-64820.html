BFN [Text] Murat Karayalcin, state minister and deputy prime minister, has said: We disapprove of the intensive discussions on the extension of the chief of staff's term in office. Stating that only the government has the right to extend the chief of staff's term, Karayalcin added that his term will be extended if it is deemed necessary; if not, it will not be extended. According to information obtained by TRT correspondent Mustafa Cevik, Karayalcin was answering correspondents questions on board a plane on his way to Sanliurfa. In answer to a question on whether the chief of staff's term will be extended, Karayalcin said: [Begin Karayalcin recording] When an issue of this kind is discussed, it should be brief and to the point. In the letter the chief of the staff sent to me concerning these discussions, he clearly states that he has no requests regarding his term in office. I think that these discussions should be ended. [end recording] In answer to another question, Karayalcin said the democratization package has not yet been approved. Referring to speculations regarding this issue, Karayalcin said: You cannot hold discussions on a package that is not ready. How can I or Prime Minister Ciller discuss the democratization package with other people if it is not ready yet?
