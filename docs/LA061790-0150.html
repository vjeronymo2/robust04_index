June 17, 1990, Sunday, Valley Edition

GYMNAST LEONARD WINS VAULT

Angie Leonard of Sepulveda finished first in the junior division vault in the United States Assn. of Independent Gymnastic Clubs meet in Greensboro, N.C., Saturday.

But Leonard could not hold the lead in the all-around competition. Leonard, who had been suffering from the flu during the meet, held the lead in both the all-around and vaulting events after the preliminaries
