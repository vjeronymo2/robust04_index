Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Crvenkovski in London"] [Text] Macedonian Foreign Minister Stevo Crvenkovski visited London yesterday. He met and talked with Roger Roan, undersecretary of the British Foreign Office. They discussed several issues concerning the situation in the Balkans, and focused on the bilateral relations. The British party reaffirmed its interest for stabilizing the situation in the region and pointed out Macedonia's contribution to that. During his short visit, Minister Crvenkovski also met and talked with the Macedonians living and working in London. He briefed them on Macedonia's efforts to gain international affirmation and its struggle to introduce stable economy in the country.
