940616 FT 16 JUN 94 / UK Company News: BAe in deal with Lufthansa Lufthansa Cityline is buying three British Aerospace RJ85 Avroliners and take options on three further aircraft in a contract worth about Dollars 150m. The 80-seat aircraft. which can operate from short runways and make steep approaches before touch-down, will enable Lufthansa to operate services from Munich and into airports like London City and Berlin Tempelhof as well larger air terminals. The order follows the recent announcement that SAM, the Colombian airline, is to buy eight RJ85 aircraft. Avro International, a British Aerospace division, has also won orders this year for the aircraft from Turkish airlines and Air Malta.
