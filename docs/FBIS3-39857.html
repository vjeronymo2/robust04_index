Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Russia has declared that it fully supports Turkey's territorial integrity and unity, and condemns the actions perpetrated by the PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] terrorist organization. According to a statement issued by Foreign Ministry Deputy Spokesman Ferhat Ataman, Russian Ambassador in Ankara Albert Sergeyevich Chernyshev was invited to the Foreign Ministry following the appearance of press items regarding a PKK meeting to be held in Moscow. Foreign Ministry Under Secretary Ambassador Ozdem Sanberk received Chernyshev and inquired as to the truth of the items that appeared in the press. Replying that he will immediately ask Moscow to send information regarding this issue, Chernyshev stated that there was no question of a change in Russia's policy concerning Turkey. Noting that Russia fully supports Turkey's territorial integrity and unity, Chernyshev stated that his country has absolutely no connections with the PKK terrorist organization, and added that they condemn the terrorism perpetrated by the PKK.
