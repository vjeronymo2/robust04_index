CSO ["Election Results in Bukovyna"] [Text] In the elections held on 10 April, in Chernivtsi's Leninskyy e.d. No. 431, Professor Taras Kyyak, head of the oblast organization of the DemPU, defeated Volodymyr Dovheshko, first secretary of the oblast committee of the CPU; in Hlyboka e.d. No. 434, Ivan Popesku, member of the Rumanian Language and Culture Society, defeated Korneliy Nikitovych, deputy head of the oblast administration of education; in Zastavna e.d. No. 435, Heorhiy Manchulenko, head of the regional organization of Rukh, defeated Mykola Ivanovych, chief of the Khotyn Rayon Department of Internal Affairs; in Kelmentsi e.d. No. 436, Hryhoriy Dovhanchyk, head of the peasants' union, defeated Vasyl Burlaka, deputy head of the Sokyryany Rayon State Administration representing the democratic bloc Bukovynska zlahoda [Bukovyna Accord]; in Novoselytsya e.d. No. 438, Yuriy Buzduhan, an official in the Anti-Monopoly Committee of Ukraine, member of the Social Democratic Party of Ukraine [SDPU] and New Ukraine [Nova Ukrayina], defeated D. Rata. Runoff elections will be held in Pershotravnevyy e.d. No. 432 and Vyzhnytsya e.d. No. 433.
