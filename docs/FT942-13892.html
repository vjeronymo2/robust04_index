940422 FT 22 APR 94 / UK Company News: Fleming Japanese Fleming Japanese Investment Trust had a net asset value of 258.2p at March 31 compared with 198.7p a year earlier and 244.7p at the September 30 year-end. There were net losses of Pounds 250,000 for the six months, against profits of Pounds 166,000. Losses per share amounted to 0.15p (0.14p earnings).
