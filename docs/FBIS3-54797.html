Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, December 29 (SNARK) -- The Central Committee of the Communist Party of Armenia has called on people to unite the efforts of the normal layers of society to restore the new political, military and economic unit with Russia. In its New Year's statement communists called upon to the creation of the coalition government of the national agreement, demanded to stop the privatization's process and purchase and sale of land. By spring, as Armenian communists consider, it is necessary to undertake pre-scheduled presidency and parliament's elections. Vladimir Darbinyan, the member of the board of Central Committee of Communist Party of Armenia, said to SNARK that these requirements is for national interests of Armenian people, the future of which impossible to imagine out of the new union of republics, recreated voluntary. Meanwhile, during his recent interview to SNARK Levon Ter-Petrosyan excluded the possibility to recreate the Union.
