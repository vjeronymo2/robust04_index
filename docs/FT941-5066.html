940308 FT 08 MAR 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There was good but irregular demand, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Brightest and good medium east Africans were 5 to 15p easier except Pekoe Dusts which moved higher. Mediums opened fully firm but tended easier towards the close. Plainer descriptions sold well and often advanced. Ceylons met good competition but prices were generally lower. Offshore: good general demand at fully firm to dearer rates. Quality 190p/kg, good medium 140p/kg, medium 123p/kg, low medium 90p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 221p/kg for a Rwanda pf. 1.
