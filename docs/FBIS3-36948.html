Language: Tajik Article Type:BFN [Text] The Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tajikistan released a decree on operative supervision over the State Committee of the Republic of Tajikistan on Radio and TV. The decree notes: Taking into account the need of inhabitants of the Republic of Tajikistan in objective and unbiased information on important political and social events in the country and abroad, and in order to improve the quality of the work of the State Committee of the Republic of Tajikistan on Radio and TV, and taking into account the great importance of radio and television in the life of the republic, as well as in order to stabilize the social and political situation as soon as possible, and in accordance with Article 109 of the Basic Law of the Republic of Tajikistan, the Presidium of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tajikistan decrees: 1. To entrust temporarily the chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tajikistan with the task of operative supervision over the State Committee of the Republic of Tajikistan on Radio and TV. 2. To put this decree forward for approval to the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tajikistan. [Signed] Imamali Rakhmanov, chairman of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tajikistan
