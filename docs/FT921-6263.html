920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / International Company News: Kloeckner to pass dividend KLOECKNER-Werke, the steel and engineering group, will not pay a dividend for the year to September 1991 following a sharp drop in earnings. Kloeckner paid DM5 a share for 1989-90, its first payout in 17 years. The company expects to report a distributable profit of DM1.5m (Dollars 937m) for last year.
