Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] In a statement this afternoon before leaving for Athens, Joe Clark, the UN secretary general's special representative in Cyprus, said Turkish Cypriot leader Rauf Denktas did not give the United Nations a satisfactory answer on whether he accepts the confidence-building measures in principle. However, he appeared optimistic, saying that in his opinion, the negotiations are very close to reaching an agreement. Clark said: There is only one very significant step left.
