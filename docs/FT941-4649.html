940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / Observer: Soccer score The Japanese know how to exact subtle revenge. Honda may have taken a spanking from BMW over Rover, but Mitsubishi has struck back at the heart of German patriotism - by using Franz Beckenbauer, the national soccer coach, in a big advertising campaign on behalf of the company's Sigma saloon. 'As a coach I want everything to run as I wish,' says the football hero - his choice no doubt driven by commercial good sense, rather than silly matters like nationalism.
