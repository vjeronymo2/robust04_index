October 28, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Troy Dayak scored the only goal of the match midway through the first half to lift the U.S. National Team to a 1-0 soccer victory over Spartak Moscow at Blaine, Minn.

Dayak, of Livermore, Calif., split three Soviet defenders at the seven-meter line and beat goaltender Viktor Derbunov.
