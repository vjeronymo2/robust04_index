Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tunis, Jan 15 (AFP) -- A three-man delegation from the Palestine Liberation Organisation (PLO) is to hold talks with Roman Catholic leaders at the Vatican on Sunday [16 January], an informed source here said Friday. The object and duration of the talks was not disclosed. The Vatican signed an historic agreement with Israel last month in a bid to bury centuries of enmity between Jews and Christians.
