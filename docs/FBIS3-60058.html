Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of fileworthy reports on antinarcotics operations in countries covered by FBIS London Bureau from 24 December - 4 January. Sources are given at the end of each report

Spain
The Civil Guard seized 500 kg of hashish resin at the port of Algeciras in southern Spain. It was hidden inside a refrigerator truck that had been on a ferry traveling between Algeciras and Tangier, Morocco. (Madrid RNE-1 Radio Network in Spanish 1600 GMT 1 Jan 94)
Iran
Seventy tons of narcotics were seized during the current calendar year, beginning 21 March, an increase of 5 tons over the corresponding period last year. (Tehran IRNA in English 1543 GMT 1 Jan 94) Approximately 1,000 kg of various types of narcotic drugs have been seized from smugglers in eastern Iran in the past two weeks. The drugs include morphine, heroin, opium, opium residue, and hashish. (Tehran IRNA in English 0930 GMT 28 Dec 93)
Italy
The Naples police drug squad seized 13 kg of heroin from an organization operating between South America and Naples. (Rome RAI Televideo Teletext in Italian 1057 GMT 31 Dec 93)
Russia
Two tons of drugs were confiscated in Chechnya by Interior Ministry criminal investigation officers from September to December 1993. In addition, 6.4 hectares of narcotics plants have been cut and destroyed. (Moscow INTERFAX in English 1528 GMT 30 Dec 93)
France
Two Rwandans are being held for importing 1.3 kg of heroin hidden in bottles of shampoo. The drugs, estimated to have a resale value of 1,000,000 francs, were placed in a false ceiling in their hotel room. The drugs had come from Pakistan via central Africa. (Paris France-Inter Radio Network in French 1300 GMT 27 Dec 93)
