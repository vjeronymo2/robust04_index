BFN [Unattributed report under the "NEGA Reports" rubric] [Text] A poll held by Georgia's sociological services indicates that 44.9 percent think that the bringing of peacekeeping troops to Abkhazia will help restore Georgia's territorial integrity; 38.7 percent think the bringing of peacekeeping forces will have no influence on the restoration of Georgia's territorial integrity, and 16 percent found the question hard to answer.
