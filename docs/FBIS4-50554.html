BFN [Text] Beijing, May 18 (XINHUA) -- General Chi Haotian, China's State Councillor and minister of national defense, met with Seif Bakari, deputy minister of defence and national service of Tanzania [name and title as received], and his party here today. Bakari and his party arrived here on May 16. Apart from Beijing, they are also scheduled to visit the cities of Shanghai, Haikou and Shenzhen.
