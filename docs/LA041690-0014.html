April 16, 1990, Monday, Valley Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: 2-YEAR-OLD MISSING IN SUNLAND PARK

Search teams were scouring Sunland Park Sunday night for a toddler who wandered away from an Easter carnival, police said.

The 2-year-old boy, whose name was not released, disappeared around 5 p.m. and was still missing late Sunday night, said Los Angeles Police Sgt. Sol Polen.

Polen said 16 police officers and several civilians were searching the park for the child.
