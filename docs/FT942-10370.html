940511 FT 11 MAY 94 / Lack of demand closes driving test centres A fall in the number of learner drivers has closed 22 of the UK's 484 driving test centres. Until recently 2m people a year took a driving test, but the figure is now 1.6m. 'Changes in the birth rate means there are fewer 17 to 18-year-olds around and this is the main reason for the fall in test-taking numbers,' said the Driving Standards Agency.
