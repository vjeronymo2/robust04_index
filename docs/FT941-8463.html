940219 FT 19 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Asphaltic buys 50% of Dominion Asphaltic, the roofing materials distributor, has bought a 50 per cent stake in Dominion Alliance Holdings for an undisclosed amount. Dominion, based in St Ives, Cambridgeshire, is a maker and supplier of metal fabrications, fire protection and roofing products. In 1993 it had turnover of more than Pounds 16m.
