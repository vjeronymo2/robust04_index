Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, February 2 (ATA) -- The Albanian minister of foreign affairs, Alfred Serreqi met on February 1, in Tirana a high ranking NATO delegation headed by the assistant secretary general Mr. Moltke, who arrived in Albania on the same day. Mr. Serreqi expressed the Albanian readiness to participate in the initiative Partnership for Peace and stressed that Albania will play an active role and will be a serious partner in it. Mr. Motke conveyed to Mr. Serreqi the greetings of the NATO secretary general, Mr. Manfred Woerner and concentrated on some aspects of the cooperation Partnership for Peace.
