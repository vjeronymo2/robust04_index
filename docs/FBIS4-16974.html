BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Glazovskaya] [Text] Ashkhabad [Ashgabat], 21 Apr -- Turkmen-Chinese summit talks started in Ashkhabad today. Turkmen President Saparmurad Niyazov and Li Peng, premier of the PRC State Council, have started discussing specific issues relating to the development of economic cooperation. Journalists were allowed to be present only for the first few minutes of the meeting. However, these first few minutes did reveal that the meeting was exclusively about business. An understanding has virtually been reached on the purchase of Turkmen cotton by China. Alongside the official talks, discussion is under way of draft intergovernmental agreements. These center on matters relating to projects for sending Turkmen gas to China and a number of other commercial and economic cooperation issues.
