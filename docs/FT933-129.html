930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Fitch Reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 381,000 were announced by Fitch, the design services group, for the half year to June 30. Losses last time were Pounds 1.8m. Mr Rodney Fitch, chairman, said that following the financial restructuring in late 1992 the management team spent the first half of the year concentrating on rebuilding the London business while further developing its worldwide market presence. The group had refocused on its core activities and continued to contain costs. Turnover fell to Pounds 6.3m (Pounds 8.9m). Losses per share came through at 2p (31.9p).
