940804 FT 04 AUG 94 / International Company News: Go-ahead for DTB-Matif co-operation The French government yesterday granted formal recognition to Germany's DTB futures exchange, clearing the way for the implementation of a co-operation deal with Matif, its French counterpart. France's economics ministry said in a statement that the first of two phases of the July 1993 deal between the DTB and Matif would involve the French futures exchange supervising the installation and operation of DTB trading screens in the offices of Matif members. This phase will be implemented after France's summer holidays. The second phase of the agreement, which allows for the entry of Matif products on the DTB exchange, is ex-pected to take place during 1995.
