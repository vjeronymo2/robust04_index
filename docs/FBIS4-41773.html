BFN [Text] A new political party, All-Belarusian Unity and Accord, with numerous businessmen and industrialists among its leaders, was set up as runoff presidential elections are a near certainty. The party intends to hold urgent talk with Aleksandr Lukashenko who gained far more votes than his rivals in the first round of voting. A party spokesman said that if their goals in carrying out economic reforms coincide, the party would support Lukashenko in the runoff elections which are expected to be held in the first half of July.
