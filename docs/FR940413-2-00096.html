[Docket No. AB&hyph;6 (Sub-No. 359X)] Burlington Northern Railroad Co._Abandonment Exemption_In Mobile County, AL AGENCY: Interstate Commerce Commission. ACTION: Notice of exemption.

SUMMARY: Pursuant to 49 U.S.C. 10505, the Commission exempts Burlington Northern Railroad Company (BN) from the prior approval requirements of 49 U.S.C. 10903&hyph;10904 to permit BN to abandon its car barge service between Mobile and Blakely Island, AL, and the Blakely Island track between engineering station (ES) 0.00N to ES 58 + 98N and ES 0 + 00S to ES 103 + 53S, a total of 3.08 miles. The exemption will be subject to standard labor protective conditions. DATES: Provided no formal expression of intent to file an offer of financial assistance has been received, this exemption will be effective on May 13, 1994. Formal expressions of intent to file an offer 1 of financial assistance under 49 CFR 1152.27(c)(2) must be filed by April 25, 1994. Petitions to stay must be filed by April 28, 1994. Requests for a public use condition in conformity with 49 CFR 1152.28(a)(2) must be filed by May 3, 1994. Petitions to reopen must be filed by May 9, 1994 . 1 See Exempt. of Rail Line Abandonment_Offers of Finan. Assist., 4 I.C.C.2d 164 (1987).
