931110 FT 10 NOV 93 / Consett's Pounds 100m project begins GROUND reclamation work costing Pounds 2m begins today for a proposed Pounds 100m scheme to transform the former Consett steelworks site in County Durham. The Project Genesis scheme is designed to be an environmentally friendly development combining industrial, commercial and leisure facilities in a landscaped setting. The first phase, covering 60 acres of the 700-acre area, involves building an industrial park on the former platemill's site. The scheme is being undertaken by Dysart Developments and Derwentside District Council. Consett steelworks closed in 1980.
