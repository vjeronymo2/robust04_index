November 15, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Motions Filed: Attorneys for British heavy metal singer Ozzy Osbourne filed a motion Wednesday for summary judgment in a federal court in Macon, Ga., requesting that a multimillion-dollar product liability lawsuit against the rock star be dismissed. Osbourne is being sued by the parents of Michael Waller, a 16-year-old who took his life after allegedly hearing pro-suicide subliminal messages hidden in Osbourne's music. The motion states that expert witnesses for the plaintiff deposed in Macon last month failed to provide evidence of any subliminal content. CLAUDIA PUIG
