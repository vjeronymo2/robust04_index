931013 FT 13 OCT 93 / International Company News: Result of BNP share-sale due THE RESULTS of the French government's sale of shares in Banque Nationale de Paris (BNP), which ends tonight, will be announced on Friday, the economics ministry said, AP-DJ reports from Paris. The ministry also will announce whether the government has decided to reduce the maximum number of shares that an individual investor can purchase, from the original limit of 40.
