BFN [Text] Hong Kong, April 12 (XINHUA) -- Experts from both sides of the Sino-British Joint Liaison Group (JLG) met here today to discuss issues concerning the Hong Kong residents after 1997. Chen Zuoer, resident representative of the Chinese team of the group, said that experts from both sides exchanged views on matters such as right of abode, visa abolition, and the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region travel documents. He said that the Chinese side attaches great importance to the above issues as they constitute the major contents of the transfer of power in 1997, adding that he hoped a legitimate and reasonable solution towards the issues could be worked out between the two sides.
