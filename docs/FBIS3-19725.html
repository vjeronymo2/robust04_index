Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] Joe Clark, the UN secretary general's special representative in Cyprus, arrived to Cyprus tonight. In a statement at Larnaca Airport, Clark said that during his presence in Cyprus he looks forward to constructive talks. He added that it is important that everyone knows that time is passing and that with this set of events we want to conclude an agreement on the confidence-building measures [CBM's] as soon as possible. And if this is not possible, we must know it as soon as possible. Clark said that as a practical measure we want a clear indication by the end of the week when the UN secretary general will submit his report to the Security Council.
