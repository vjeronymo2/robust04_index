Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shchegoleva] [Text] Sochi March 14 TASS -- Georgian and Abkhazian representatives met at a border-guard post at the Psou River on Monday to discuss exchange of prisoners of war and search for missing persons. The Georgian party handed over lists of 870 persons missing in military clashes in Abkhazia. The Abkhazian representatives promised to assist in searching for them. Both the parties exchanged specific data, addresses, surnames of missing persons and promises to inquire into each particular case. The parties agreed to go ahead with cooperation in the field. The Georgian delegation was led by Paat Zakareishvili, deputy chairman of the State Committee for Protection of Human Rights, the Abkhazian one - by Rezo Zantariya, deputy chairman of the State Commission on Exchange of Prisoners of War. The Sochi-based Staff for Civil Defence and Emergencies contributed to organization of the meeting.
