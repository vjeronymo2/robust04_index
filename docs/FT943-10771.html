940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / Observer: People's choice Karl Habsburg, the grandson of the last ruler of the Austro-Hungarian empire, is to stand as a candidate in Austria's general election on October 9. Habsburg, 33, who used to host a games show on Austrian television called 'Who is Who?', will stand as an independent candidate on the conservative People's Party ticket. Habsburg is also planning to stand as a candidate in Austria's European elections, expected in May or June next year. Austria will join the European Union in January. If successful, he will join his father, Otto von Habsburg, who is a German member of the European parliament.
