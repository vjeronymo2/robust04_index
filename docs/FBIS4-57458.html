BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Sergio Abreu and German Ambassador to Uruguay Ludger Buerstedde yesterday exchanged notes on the "implementation of a dual labor training" project. The project's goal involves professional dual training through theoretical classes, the training of human resources both in our country and in Germany, and visits by German advisers and experts. The German Government's contribution includes the above-mentioned experts and the required supplementary equipment for the existing workshops and laboratories, teaching material, two vehicles, and office equipment, totaling 540,000 German marks. The National Public Education Administration will be the Uruguayan counterpart for the project's implementation.
