Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Report by Adolfo Leon Mejia; from the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] Indignation prevails among the residents of Popayan over the disappearance and kidnapping of Julio Cesar Payan de la Roche -- doctor, journalist, and political leader. The first reports indicate the president of Popayan's municipal council was intercepted near his farm by several men who forced him into a Jeep and took him to an unknown destination. [Begin Popayan Mayor Luis Fernando Velasco recording] I hope the kidnappers understand they have not attacked Payan the doctor. They have actually attacked a whole city, which renounces this action and demands his immediate release. [end recording] Hundreds of demonstrators from the M-19 Democratic Alliance and other movements in the department are currently gathered at Caldas Park to protest his kidnapping and to demand that the outstanding public leader promptly be released. Dr. Payan was president of the municipal council. He was also a journalist who participated in a talk show at Radio Super, a local station. He attended to his patients at a general clinic located north of this department capital. A complete mystery surrounds the disappearance of Dr. Julio Cesar Payan in the city of Popayan.
