940623 FT 23 JUN 94 / World News in Brief: Second shock defeat at Wimbledon: In another upset at Wimbledon, No. 2 seed Michael Stich was beaten by Bryan Shelton in straight sets. Stich, men's singles champion in 1991, joined fellow German Steffi Graf, the women's top seed, who was also beaten in the first round by an unseeded American.
