930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Mediterranean Fund capital restructuring Mediterranean Fund said yesterday that all supporting papers had now been filed with the Royal Court of Guernsey and that the restructuring of the share capital was expected to be confirmed today. If that is received, dealings in sterling shares will begin tomorrow and the listing of dollar shares, IDRs and depositary warrants will be cancelled. Dealings in warrants will be unaffected.
