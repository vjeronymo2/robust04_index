Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] India and Brazil are to cooperate in the fields of forestry, pollution control and waste management. This was announced after talks between the environment minister, Mr. Kamalnath, and his Brazilian counterpart, Mr. Rubens Ricupero, in New Delhi today. Mr. Ricupero offered Brazilian know-how for using alcohol as a substitute for petrol. He sought Indian assistance in environment audit.
