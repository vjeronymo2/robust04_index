940905 FT 05 SEP 94 / Company News (This Week): Bowater - Other companies The packaging, printing and coated products group is expected to announce profits tomorrow for the half-year to June broadly similar to last year's Pounds 102.6m. Consensus forecasts are just over Pounds 100m, with a range of Pounds 99m to Pounds 105m. Last year's figure was boosted by an exceptional gain on disposals of Pounds 7.9m, so underlying profits growth is expected to be about 8 per cent.
