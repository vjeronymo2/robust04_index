April 8, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ALLEGED SPEED TRAP

I read with great disgust your article (March 31) regarding the attorney, Ernest J. Franceshi Jr., wanting to sue my city because of an alleged speed trap.

Poor guy! Eighty-five m.p.h. in a 55-m.p.h. zone; 75 m.p.h. in a 55-m.p.h. zone; and 70 m.p.h. in a 50-m.p.h. zone. Next thing he'll want is that if he is the cause of an accident going that fast, he'll be suing for not having an officer there to stop him. It's a shame he doesn't use his legal talents for something more productive.

GARY ARNESON

Huntington Beach
