921212 FT 12 DEC 92 / UK Company News: 25% growth pushes Albion to Pounds 774,000 A near 25 per cent increase saw Albion produce a pre-tax profit of Pounds 774,000 in the year ended September 30 1992, against Pounds 620,000. Turnover of the group, which manufactures tailored mens wear and is based in Northern Ireland, rose 15 per cent, from Pounds 13.6m to Pounds 15.6m. Earnings per share came through at 15.5p (9.8p) and the final dividend is 1.8p to lift the total to 3p (2.5p).
