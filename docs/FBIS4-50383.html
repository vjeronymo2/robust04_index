BFN [Text] Phnom Penh, May 18 (XINHUA) -- King Norodom Sihanouk of Cambodia left here this morning for Beijing, China, to resume treatment for cancer. The king was accompanied by his wife Queen Monique. They were seen off at the airport by first Prime Minister Norodom Ranariddh, Second Prime Minister Hun Sen, parliament speaker Chea Sim and Chinese Ambassador to Cambodia Xie Yue'e [name as received]. The 72-year-old king made a surprise announcement last week that he would leave for China on May 18 to resume chemotherapy treatment for cancer. Originally, he had planned to stay in his country until June 2 and then pay his first official visit to China after becoming king. King Sihanouk returned to Cambodia on April 8 after six months of treatment for cancer in Beijing. While in Cambodia, he launched a number of proposals for peace in order to hasten the national reconciliation process.
