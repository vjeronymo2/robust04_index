920616 FT 16 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Allied London raises further Pounds 25m Allied London Properties has placed a further Pounds 25m of its 10 3/4 per cent first mortgage debenture stock 2025. In total there is now Pounds 75m of the stock in issue. The gross redemption yield was set at 10.946 per cent, representing 1.9 percentage points above the gross redemption yield of 9 per cent Treasury Loan 2008. Proceeds will be used to refinance debt and in the group's business.
