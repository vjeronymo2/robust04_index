931023 FT 23 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Value and Income Value and Income Trust had a net asset value of 101.1p per share at September 30, up from 92.5p at the March year-end and 74.7p at September 1992. Net revenue dipped to Pounds 664,000 (Pounds 790,000) in the six month period, equivalent to earnings of 1.56p (1.87p). The interim dividend is 2p (1.8p) and directors intend to recommend a 2p final.
