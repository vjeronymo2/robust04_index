Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, Feb.15 (A.A) -- Russian neo-fascist politician Vladimir Zhirinovskiy is not able to visit Turkey for the time being, it was reported today. The Russian politician was reportedly supposed visit Turkey as the guest of a daily. Sources near to the Liberal Democratic Party led by Zhirinovskiy told AA that the Russian politician who had reservations at the Turkish Airlines for Monday with a 15-strong delegation, could not make the trip because the tickets bought by the daily could not reach the Moscow office of the Turkish Airlines in due time. However, an official of the daily told AA that the Foreign Ministry had not approved of the visit of the Russian politician and therefore they have canceled the invitation.
