October 12, 1989, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

TO OUR READERS

The Los Angeles Times San Diego County Edition has changed several aspects of its format. The changes are aimed at presenting a better organized newspaper, giving easier access to The Times' comprehensive coverage. Among the differences:

Highlights, on the front of each daily section except main news, summarizes what is in the section and spotlights stories of specific interest to San Diego County.

The Top of Today's News on A2 summarizes important news.

Headlines, in many cases, include overview subheads that provide further explanation.

The Times invites readers to comment by calling 1-800-678-0105. The line will operate from 7 a.m. to 8 p.m. Monday through Friday. Or write to: "Format Changes," Times San Diego County Edition, 701 B St., Suite 501, San Diego 92101.
