October 23, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

REAL ESTATE: Building permits for single-family homes in the county are at their lowest level since 1986, but builders aren't panicking. . . . Permits are down 28% from 1988 and 14% from 1987, but last year's figures may have been boosted by concern that a slow-growth initiative would pass, a building industry spokesman says. Lanie Jones and Rick VanderKyff
