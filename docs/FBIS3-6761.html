Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 20 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea on March 17 sent a message of greetings to President of the Islamic Republic of Iran Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani on the occasion of the Islamic new year. President Kim Il-song in the message wished the president and people of Iran greater success in the new year in their work for building an independent and prosperous new Iran.
