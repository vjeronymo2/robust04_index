BFN [Text] Khartoum, 12 Jun (SUNA) -- The Council of Ministers was briefed Sunday [12th June] by the president of the Republic, General 'Umar al-Bashir, on the details of recapturing of the strategic Kajo Kaji town by the Armed Forces. Al-Bashir has lauded zeal and struggle of the Armed Forces' troops and the People's Defense Forces' troops in the battle for restoring Kajo, explaining that the troops did not confront significant resistance from the outlaws who fled before the Army vanguards. The president also pointed to the vast agricultural and economic potentialities of Kajo Kaji town. Adding that the government of Bahr al-Jabal State has finalized preparations for the return of Kajo Kaji citizens to their area. Al-Bashir added that Bahr al-Jabal State is now totally free of outlaws, affirming that efforts to implement comprehensive development and to provide stability all over Bahr al-Jabal State will soon be started. He added that the federal government will extend every possible support to the services' utilities in Bahr al-Jabal State.
