BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 13 Jun -- The law enforcement agencies of the Chechen Republic this morning carried out an operation to localize and disarm the illegal gangs of bandits headed by Labazanov, Suleymanov, and Gantemirov. The press service of Chechnya's Ministry of Internal Affairs told ITAR-TASS that the operation carried out by Interior Ministry forces, the Department of State Security and spetsnaz [special assignment] personnel of the republic's armed forces on the outskirts of Groznyy, ended at 1200 hours. According to the latest information, between 15 and 20 people were injured on both sides. The commander of a Chechen spetsnaz subunit was killed. Suleymanov, one of the leaders of the bandit gangs, was arrested and taken to the hospital in a serious condition. The fate of Labazanov is not yet known. A search is continuing for others who fled the scene of the clash. Chechnya's Ministry of Internal Affairs told ITAR-TASS that allegations by certain section of the media about armed clashes that are supposed to have taken place in Groznyy involving armor and civilian casualties are not true.
