930507 FT 07 MAY 93 / Minister predicts gathering speed in housing market SIR GEORGE Young, housing minister, last night predicted that the recovery in the housing market would gather pace during the summer. He said at the International Building Press Annual Dinner in London: 'It does now seem that those who have been hesitating on the brink are going house-hunting in increasing numbers.' The National Association of Estate Agents reported increases across the market for the fourth consecutive month in inquiries, viewings, offers and contract completions, offering further evidence of the long-awaited recovery.
