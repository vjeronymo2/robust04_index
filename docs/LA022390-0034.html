February 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NATION IN BRIEF;

MASSACHUSETTS;

LIMITS ON CLINIC PROTESTERS REINSTATED

Massachusetts' highest court reinstated an order barring demonstrators from blocking abortion clinics, but the group Operation Rescue said it will obey a "higher law" and continue to protest. The Supreme Judicial Court ruled 4 to 1 that the injunction places only minimal limitations on protesters and does not threaten their First Amendment rights. A spokeswoman for Operation Rescue, which has staged several protests at clinics, said her anti-abortion group will continue its activities.
