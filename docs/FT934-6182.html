931126 FT 26 NOV 93 / Strike disrupts French airline Pilots and stewards at the French domestic airline Air Inter launched a surprise strike yesterday, union and company officials said, Reuter reports from Paris. The strike, which began at 2pm was expected to continue until midnight, delaying flights. It was the latest protest against the government's plans to open up the airline, a subsidiary of state-owned carrier Air France, to foreign competition. A two-week strike last month cost Air France about FFr1bn (Pounds 114.28m).
