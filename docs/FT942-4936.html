940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / International Company News: Cameco proceeds with Kumtor project Cameco, the big Canadian uranium producer, said it is proceeding with the development of the USDollars 300m Kumtor gold mining project in the former Soviet republic of Kyrgyzistan in central Asia, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. Cameco is an operator with a one-third interest. A two-thirds interest is held by state-owned Kyrgyzaltan. Start-up is due in 1997.
