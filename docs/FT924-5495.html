921126 FT 26 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Appld Holographics Pounds 622,000 in the red Applied Holographics, the USM-quoted maker of hot stamping foils and holograms, reported a pre-tax deficit of Pounds 622,383 for the six months to end-September. This compared with losses of Pounds 681,394 last time and was struck on turnover down slightly from Pounds 2.46m to Pounds 2.37m. Losses per share worked through at 3.1p (4.5p).
