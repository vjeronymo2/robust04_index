Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] Mahmud Va'ezi, deputy foreign minister of the Islamic Republic of Iran, met the Portuguese foreign minister in Lisbon yesterday. In this meeting the Portuguese foreign minister, stressing the importance of the Islamic Republic of Iran and the its political influence in our region, said: Portugal is anxious to expand its relations with the Islamic Republic of Iran within the context of bilateral relations, as well as within the EC context.
