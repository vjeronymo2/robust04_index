930407 FT 07 APR 93 / Observer: Odd selection With hits such as Birds of a Feather and Lovejoy to their credit, the creative spirits behind SelecTV, clearly know a thing or two about TV programme-making. But are they equally tuned in to the responsibilities involved in running a public company? Not only has the recent muddle over the company's failure to keep minutes of decisions raised some doubts, but a senior official commented yesterday: 'Until now, I thought the Cadbury rules had something to do with a chocolate monopoly.'
