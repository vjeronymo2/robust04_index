Language: Urdu Article Type:CSO [News Report: "Foreign Travel by Major Drug Smugglers Banned To Curb Drug Smuggling"] [Text] Islamabad (Bureau Report)--After thorough investigation of assets owned at home and abroad by the big fish involved in drug smuggling, the federal government has imposed restrictions on their leaving the country. According to very reliable sources, Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto has given instructions to the Pakistan Narcotics Control Board to collect information about property and assets owned here and abroad by the major drug smugglers and has to send recommendations to the home minister. The Narcotics Control Board after a thorough investigation has sent its final report to the home minister. The home minister has provided final lists of persons involved in the drug trade to all related agencies and intelligence agencies at all airports in order to take strict action against these persons and to make sure they are not able to flee the country. These drug smugglers will be fully punished. The sources also revealed that in addition to some ministers, bureaucrats are also involved in this ugly and detestable trade. Prime Minister Benazir Bhutto has given strict instructions to the home ministry to rise above political and personal relations and to take severe action against these persons who have been playing with the lives of our youth.
