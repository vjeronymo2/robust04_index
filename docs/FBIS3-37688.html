Language: Czech Article Type:BFN [Text] Premier Vaclav Klaus and Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev had a brief meeting with journalists after their two-hour long talks. Czech radio correspondent David Stahlavsky reports: [Stahlavsky] Journalists' questions naturally concerned the situation in Bosnia and the ultimatum to Bosnian Serbs due to expire at midnight today. Kozyrev explained the visit of the special Russian envoy to Sarajevo by saying that, according to the agreement with Bosnian Serbs, 400 Russian soldiers will be transferred from Croatia to the Bosnian capital. Asked whether the envoy will stay there even after the ultimatum expires, Kozyrev said that Russia does not recognize this ultimatum.
