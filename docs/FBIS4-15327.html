BFN [GIA report under the rubric "According to Reports From Correspondents and Information Agencies": "In the Struggle Against the Narcotics Business"] [Text] On 29 March, a meeting of the recently created coordinating headquarters of the law enforcement bodies in the Kartli region was held in Gori. The heads of the region's law enforcement structures participated in its work. On account of the worsening situation concerning the narcotics business, methods of resolving this problem were outlined at the meeting and a plan of action was adopted. In this regard, mixed groups made up of representatives of the Ministry of Security, the military police, and the Ministry of Internal Affairs were created. These forces will control all points of entry into and out of Tskhinvali [administrative center of south Ossetia] in order to prevent narcotic substances from coming into the republic from the northern Caucasus.
