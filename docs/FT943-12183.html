940726 FT 26 JUL 94 / International Company News: Turnround at Cominco Cominco, one of Canada's biggest mining and metals groups, posted a final net profit for the June quarter of CDollars 45.3m (USDollars 32.8m), or 56 cents a share, against a loss of CDollars 16.3m, or 22 cents, a year earlier. First-half net profit was CDollars 47.6m, or 58 cents, against a loss of CDollars 53m, or 69 cents.
