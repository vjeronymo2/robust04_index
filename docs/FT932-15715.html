930410 FT 10 APR 93 / UK Company News: F&C Pacific net assets rise to 251.2p Net assets per share at the Foreign & Colonial Pacific Investment Trust rose from 223.2p to 251.2p basic over the year to end-January. Adjusted for warrants exercised the rise was from 211.9p to 237.8p. At the July 31 interim stage the basic figure stood at 190.5p while the adjusted figure was 181.8p. Available revenue for the year fell from Pounds 2.25m to Pounds 2.19m, equal to earnings of 2.05p (2.11p). A final dividend of 1.2p (1p) makes a 1.95p (1.75p) total.
