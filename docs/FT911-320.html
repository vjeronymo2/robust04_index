910513 FT 13 MAY 91 / UK Company News: 'Unique opportunity' for Tootal holders Coats Viyella, which is bidding Pounds 252m for the Tootal textile group, yesterday urged its target's shareholders to accept a 'unique opportunity' to realise a premium value for their shares and to participate in the benefits of the merger. In a letter to Tootal shareholders, Coats said that it only needed a further 12.7 per cent acceptances by the closing date on Friday for the offer to become unconditional.
