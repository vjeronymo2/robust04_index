March 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Quarterback Jim Kelly is about to sign a contract extension with the Buffalo Bills, raising his annual pay to $3 million and making him the highest-paid NFL player, according to the Buffalo News.

Philadelphia Eagle quarterback Randall Cunningham reportedly earns $2.6 million a year. The San Francisco 49ers' Joe Montana makes $1.99 million but is negotiating a new contract.
