BFN [Text] At last the international community has accepted its involvement in UNAMIR [UN Assistance Mission in Rwanda], the UN force in Rwanda, which is being rebuilt. More details from French Foreign Minister Alain Juppe: [Begin Juppe recording] Our initiative is already reaping very substantial diplomatic benefits, since it is accelerating the setting up of UNAMIR. Canada has just announced its readiness to offer UNAMIR 350 soldiers and a communication unit. Tunisia has offered 500 soldiers, Pakistan has stressed its readiness to transfer some of its contingents from UNOSOM [UN Operation in Somalia] to Rwanda, and, at last, the United States has already transported equipment for UNAMIR to Uganda. We suggested that the UN secretary general coordinate the operations to ease the arrival of UNAMIR. More precisely, we suggested setting up a contact group of the main contributing countries under the aegis of the Secretariat General. [end recording]
