910426 FT 26 APR 91 / International Company News: Sara Lee SARA LEE, the Illinois-based food group, saw an 11 per cent increase in net profits during its third quarter, to end-March. It made Dollars 120.8m after tax, compared with Dollars 108.9m in the same period a year ago, leaving it with profits of Dollars 393.9m after the first nine months, against Dollars 353m last time, writes Nikki Tait.
