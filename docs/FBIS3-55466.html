Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yerevan, January 7 (SNARK)--Not one cubic meter of gas has arrives to Armenia for a week after New Year. SNARK was informed about it by Press-Center of Armenia's Government. Meanwhile, Vladimir Manoyan, the chief of the Press-Center, said Roland Adonts, the chief of "Armgasprom" (Armenian Gas Industry) state company, is in Marneulinskiy Region of Georgia, where the gas main was damaged. It has been reported already about the meeting of vice-premiers of the three Transcaucasian republics at the Azerbaijani-Georgian border. However, it didn't lead to the opening of gas main, supplying Armenia with gas.
