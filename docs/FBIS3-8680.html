Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Text] Not only parliament was busy today. It was also busy in front of parliament. Several dozen people, who gathered there at midday, were joined in the late afternoon by another several hundred. According to the latest information, street lights have been switched off in front of the National Council and in the area between parliament and the Slovak National Uprising Square. The crowd that has gathered here is calling on the president, among other things, to resign.
