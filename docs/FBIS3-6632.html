Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, March 18 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Today on the square "Erh Choloo" ("Freedom") of the Mongolian capital a mass rally was organized by the Mongolian Democratic Union (MDU). At the meeting present were the leader of the Mongolian Social Democratic Party (MSDP) B. Batbayar, the General Secretary of the Mongolian National Democratic Party E. Bat-uul, one of the leaders of the Mongolian Democratic Union T. Enhtubshin, the Chairman of the Union of Mongolian Students B. Erdenetsogt, the representatives of the MDU in province. Thousands of people gathered at the meeting. In their hands there were posters "democracy is not anarchy", "down with the government", "mafia of functionaries is dividing the country". They were blaming the government, the Parliament and its leaders for corruption, abuse of power, blockading freedom of press, the issuing of laws not meeting the requirements of the people and were demanding the present government to resign. As the General Secretary of the Mongolian National Democratic Party E. Bat-uul announced, if the government and the Parliament do not meet the demands of the opposition and the president, so it will recall its members from the Parliament, and will continue to struggle in the most resolute way.
