BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Viktor Dmitriyev] [Text] Caracas May 31 TASS -- Venezuela is known as "South- American Kuwait", it is a leading oil power in the Western Hemisphere. A delegation of Russian lawmakers arrived here on Monday [30 May]. Vladimir Shumeyko, chairman of the Federation Council, is at the head of the delegation. The delegation is making a tour of several Latin-American countries. It has already visited Chili, Argentina and Brazil. When answering to journalists' questions at the airport, Shumeyko emphasized the necessity "to go ahead with developing Russo- Venezuelian relations as fast as we can". The programme for the visit includes a meeting between Shumeyko and President Rafael Caldera Rodriguez of Venezuela, during which the former is to hand in a personal message on the part of Russian President Boris Yeltsin. The delegation is also to meet with leaders of Venezuelian parliament, Foreign Minister Miguel Angel Burelli. Shumeyko is to address a plenary session of the council of the National Congress of Venezuela. The Russian lawmakers will also meet with Caracas Mayor Asdrubal Agiar.
