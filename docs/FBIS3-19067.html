Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] President Glavkos Kliridhis said in the most explicit manner that during the talks on the confidence-building measures [CBM's], the UN secretary general's representatives never directly or indirectly, orally or in writing, suggested that the Greek Cypriot side would get Famagusta's fenced-off area [Varosha] in exchange for recognizing the so-called Turkish Cypriot passports. In a written statement on press reports on safe access to and from Famagusta, Kliridhis added that they have no connection to the UN suggestions. The president said that these press reports give the United Nations the impression that we are trying to create an excuse to reject the CBM's.
