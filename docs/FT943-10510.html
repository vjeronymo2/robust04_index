940804 FT 04 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Fairway ahead 30% to Pounds 1.39m Fairway Group, the USM-quoted provider of print facilities and distributor of educational supplies, lifted pre-tax profits by 30 per cent in the half year to June 30, from Pounds 1.07m to Pounds 1.39m. The advance was achieved on mixed results from its activities. Hickeys Trade Binery and Spectrum Marketing Services reporting a 28 per cent and 50 per cent fall in pre-tax profits respectively. Mr Gordon Waddell, chairman, said remedial action had already been taken at Hickeys. At SMS an 'urgent reorganisation and computerisation of the business' together with the installation of a new press had caused disruption and pressure on margins. Group turnover improved to Pounds 24.5m (Pounds 20.1m). The interim dividend is lifted from 1.1p to 1.2p, payable from earnings per share of 2.92p (2.3p).
