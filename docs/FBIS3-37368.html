Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Oleg Velichko] [Text] Moscow, 15 Feb -- Russian Government Chairman Viktor Chernomyrdin today signed a decision on the distribution of responsibilities between the various deputy premiers. First Deputy Prime Minister Oleg Soskovets will coordinate, among other things, investment policy and defense industry. He will also head the government's commission on operational issues and coordinate the activities of 15 ministries, state committees, and departments. Aleksandr Zaveryukha will concentrate mainly on agriculture and hydrometeorology, while Anatoliy Chubays will continue to handle the privatization program and anti-monopoly policy. Responsibilities for social policy and cooperation between the government and representative bodies, political parties and public organizations have been transferred to Yuriy Yarov. Russia's economics minister, Aleksandr Shokhin -- who is a member of the government presidium -- will coordinate the economic reform and the work of the Russian Federation Ministry for Cooperation with CIS States.
