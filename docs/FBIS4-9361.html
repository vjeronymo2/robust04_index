BFN [Text] At the invitation of Mr. Vigil Magureanu, director of the Romanian Intelligence Service [SRI], Academician Yevgeniy Primakov, director of Russia's Foreign Intelligence Service, arrived in Bucharest. On this occasion, the two sides exchanged views on fighting international terrorism and organized crime as well as ways and means to stabilize the situation in various crisis spots. Mr. Primakov was also received by Mr. Oliviu Gherman, president of the Senate, and by Prime Minister Nicolae Vacaroiu. He also had a talk with Mr. Vasile Vacaru, chairman of the joint standing commission of the two Parliament chambers for the exercise of parliamentary control over SRI activities -- a communique of the SRI Press Bureau reads.
