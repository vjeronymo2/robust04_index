941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Overseas side helps Faber Prest rise 32% A strong performance from its industrial services division, particularly overseas, helped Faber Prest to announce a 32 per cent increase in pre-tax profits. The rise in profits at the industrial and distribution services group, from Pounds 5.03m to Pounds 6.63m for the year ended September 30, was achieved on turnover up 15 per cent at Pounds 79.5m (Pounds 69.5m). Underlying growth in turnover on continuing activities was 13 per cent to Pounds 74.7m (Pounds 66.1m) giving operating profits of Pounds 5.78m (Pounds 5.35m), an increase of 8 per cent. There were exceptional profits of Pounds 595,000, compared with net losses of Pounds 42,000. Expenditure on acquisitions was Pounds 5.4m (Pounds 500,000) and capital expenditure doubled to Pounds 7.4m (Pounds 3.5m). The group raised Pounds 8.5m in a 1-for-4 rights issue in July. Mr Roger Feaviour, chief executive, said that this pace of expansion would continue. Operating profits from industrial services, principally slag recovery and utilisation, increased 31 per cent to Pounds 4.33m (Pounds 3.31m). The distribution division, which provides logistics services, increased profits by 5 per cent to Pounds 1.84m (Pounds 1.75m) on sales of Pounds 24.6m (Pounds 21.3m). Earnings per share rose 32 per cent to 43.7p (33.3p). The proposed final dividend is 10p (9p) making a total of 15.5p (14p).
