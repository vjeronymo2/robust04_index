November 12, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Light winds raised the prospect of a long, slow event as 28 boats sailed into Mexican waters in the Los Angeles Yacht Club's 1,100-mile race to Cabo San Lucas, around Guadalupe Island.

Brack Duker's Santa Cruz 70 Evolution led the ULDB 70-raters that started Saturday, but its average speed was only 5.5 knots.
