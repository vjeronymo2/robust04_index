930323 FT 23 MAR 93 / UK Company News: EFM Income Trust net assets at 46.1p The net asset value of EFM Income Trust was 46.1p per share at January 31, up from 43.9p a year earlier. The value of the zero dividend preference shares was ahead from 38.1p to 42.6p per share over the same period. Net revenue for the nine-month period to end-January amounted to Pounds 470,000 (Pounds 650,000 for 11 months), for earnings of 3.1p (4.3p) per share. A third interim dividend of 1.2p maintains the total so far at 3.6p.
