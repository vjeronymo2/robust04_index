Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz met U.S. Ambassador in Lebanon Mark Hambley this afternoon. They reviewed bilateral relations and the ongoing Washington talks. Ambassador Hambley handed Minister Buwayz a written message from his U.S. counterpart Warren Christopher, the contents of which were not disclosed. The U.S. ambassador denied any knowledge of an imminent trip by Christopher to the region before the resumption of talks. He said that the approval of the appointment of Dr. Riyad Tabbarah as Lebanon's ambassador in Washington is being discussed by the two governments and that once the approval is given it will be made public at the proper time. Minister Buwayz met today with Ambassador Tabbarah, who said that he will leave for Washington when he receives the U.S. Administration's approval.
