BFN [Text] President Hafiz al-Asad received PRC Vice Foreign Minister Tian Zengpei and the delegation accompanying him at the al-Rawdah Palace at 1300 today. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' and PRC Ambassador in Damascus Li Qingyu. Zengpei conveyed to the president a message from PRC President Jiang Zemin dealing with bilateral relations. The president asked him to convey his greetings and best wishes to President Jiang Zemin, the Chinese leadership, and the Chinese people. Talks dealt with the Middle East situation and bilateral relations. Zengpei expressed satisfaction with the talks he held in Syria and with the development of bilateral relations and cooperation.
