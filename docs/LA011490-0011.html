January 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

What could Cinemax cable programmers have been thinking of when they scheduled "The Terror of the Tongs"? It is one of the most racially offensive films I have ever seen. I'm not even a member of the Chinese community and I found the film (which dealt with a captain seeking the killers of his daughter) unbearable. It needs to be canned and put in the film archives next to "Birth of a Nation"!

Nicole Meggerson, Culver City
