BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Yuriy Grachev] [Text] Vladivostok June 7 TASS -- South Korean President Kim Yong-sam, who arrived in the Russian Far East, visited the big submarine chaser "Admiral Vinogradov" of the Russian Pacific Fleet on Tuesday and presented the navymen with three video recorders which, according to the fleet command, will be distributed among the best crews. Kim was shown the command post of the battleship and firing strongholds. "The ship is in proper order, the navymen have a fine stature and good appearance", he told TASS.
