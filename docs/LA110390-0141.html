November 3, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Brokerage Fee Case to Go to Jury: Arguments in a suit over a disputed multimillion-dollar brokerage fee ended in Orange County Superior Court in Santa Ana, and the jury is expected to begin deliberations Monday. Real estate broker Lee & Associates alleges that it was cheated out of a $9-million commission when Fluor Corp. sold its Irvine headquarters to Trammell Crow Co. in 1985. Lee is seeking $64 million in restitution and damages against Fluor, the giant engineering and construction company, and Trammell Crow, one of the nation's top commercial developers.
