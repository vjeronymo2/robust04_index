March 14, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

EARNINGS

Bridgford Foods Corp. posted net income of $694,882 for its first quarter ended Jan. 27, a 45% increase from the $478,659 reported for the year-earlier quarter. Sales for the recent quarter rose 20%, to $17.5 million from $14.6 million. Bridgford Foods, based in Anaheim, is a producer of frozen bread dough, microwave hamburgers, processed meats and other convenience foods.
