October 25, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

STATE;

LOTTO PRIZE NEARING $20 MILLION

No one won the $14.9-million jackpot in the midweek Lotto 6/53 drawing, but four players won $152,794 each by picking five of the six numbers and the bonus number, state lottery officials said today.

The winners in the five-of-six-plus-bonus category bought their tickets in Meadow Vista, Westminster, Palm Desert and Chino, officials said.

Ticket sales totaled $11.1 million. Saturday's jackpot was estimated at $20 million.
