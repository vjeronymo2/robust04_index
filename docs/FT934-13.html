931231 FT 31 DEC 93 / World News in Brief: Ex-Salomon executive to pay Dollars 300,000 Former Salomon Brothers managing director and head trader Thomas Murphy was ordered to pay Dollars 300,000 and barred from taking part in US Treasury auction bids, the US Securities and Exchange Commission said. The SEC took action against Mr Murphy over a Treasury bidding scandal, alleging he had submitted false customer bids in three auctions of Treasury securities and caused Salomon to create false books and records.
