BFN [Text] Malaysia is a gateway to ASEAN with the 350 million people in its dynamic region. The country is also a gateway to the whole of the East Asia region. The minister of international trade and industry, Datuk Sri Rafidah Aziz said in view of this there should be a greater network between the private sectors of both Malaysia and Czech. She noted that Malaysia is very interested to receive foreign direct investments in a broad range of activities. She said this at a meeting with the Foreign Minister of Czech Josef Zieleniec and his 35-member trade delegation at her ministry in Kuala Lumpur today. Datuk Sri Rafidah said bilateral trade of the two countries was only U.S.$20 million last year. She pointed out that the volume is very small and there is potential to expand it. Mr. Josef Zieleniec and the delegation are on a two-day visit to Malaysia. He called on the minister of foreign affairs, Datuk Abdullah Ahmad Badawi, at the Foreign Ministry in Kuala Lumpur this afternoon.
