December 9, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: PLACENTIA;

CITY SEEKS $300,000 FOR WIDENING STREET

The city is requesting almost $300,000 in county funds to widen and resurface Crowther Avenue under the Orange Freeway.

The proposal includes reconstructing and resurfacing Crowther from Placentia Avenue to the eastern city limits west of Kraemer Boulevard, and widening the street as it crosses under the freeway.
