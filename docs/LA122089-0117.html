December 20, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

FAILURES: Lincoln Savings & Loan of Irvine (A3, D5), now target of a state criminal investigation, has hardly been the only Orange County thrift to go under. . . . In the last four years, 13 other S&Ls based here have failed -- more than in any other California county. Ten county banks failed this decade, also a high in the state. Dana Parsons and Mark I. Pinsky
