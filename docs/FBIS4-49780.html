BFN [Text] Beijing, May 10 (XINHUA) -- Malaysian Prime Minister Mohamed Mahathir arrived here tonight, starting his two-day working visit to China. This is Mahathir's third trip to China since he became Malaysian prime minister in 1981. During the visit, Mahathir is expected to attend the 1994 China summit meeting, a high-profile economic forum scheduled to open here tomorrow. Chinese Vice-Foreign Minister Tang Jiaxuan were among those greeting Mahathir and his party at the airport.
