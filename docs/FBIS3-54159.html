Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ahvaz, Khuzestan Prov., Jan. 22, IRNA -- `Fath-3' military exercise launched in the northern tip of the Persian Gulf Sunday morning. The exercise is jointly conducted by the Islamic Revolution Guards Corps (IRGC) and the Army. Units from naval, air and Ground Forces take part in the 6-day exercise which is aimed at promoting the combat power of the forces.
