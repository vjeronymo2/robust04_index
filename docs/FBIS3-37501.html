Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 17 Feb (ITAR-TASS) -- Ways of achieving a peaceful resolution of the Bosnian crisis were today the subject of a telephone conversation between Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev and U.S. Defense Secretary William Perry. The conversation, according to the Russian Defense Ministry Information and Press Directorate, took place via the recently established direct telephone line linking the two countries' military departments. The Directorate gave no details about the conversation.
