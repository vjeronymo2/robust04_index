March 19, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

SAUDI, 100, TAKES BRIDE FOR 'TALK'

A 100-year-old Saudi Arabian has remarried because he missed conversation after his wife died three years ago, the newspaper Al Yom reported.

"Now I have someone to talk to," Abdullah Mouzaal told the Dammam-based daily after settling down with his 80-year-old bride.
