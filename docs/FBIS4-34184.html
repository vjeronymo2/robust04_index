BFN [Text] Santiago, 15 Jun (EFE) -- Sources at the Chilean presidential palace of La Moneda have reported that the group of Cubans who today entered the Chilean Consulate General in Havana consists of seven young men, one woman, and a child. According to the same sources, who did not provide any further details, a full report on this event will be released within the next few hours. The Chilean Foreign Ministry had already confirmed the entry of the group of Cubans into the Chilean Consulate, and added that President Eduardo Frei and Foreign Minister Carlos Figueroa have already been informed about the event. Frei and his foreign minister are participating in the Fourth Ibero-American Summit in Cartagena de Indias, Colombia. This is the third time Cubans have rushed into an embassy in Havana over the past 20 days. They had done so before at the Belgian and German embassies.
