September 19, 1990, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

PRO FOOTBALL REPORT;

WEEKDAY UPDATE;

CHARGERS;

BEATHARD MIGHT SIGN HENDRICKSON

General Manager Bobby Beathard said Tuesday the Chargers will add former San Francisco linebacker Steve Hendrickson to the roster if Billy Ray Smith is placed on injured reserve today.

"It's not done yet, but I think that's what is going to happen," Beathard said.

Team doctors have recommended at least four weeks of rest for Smith, who suffers from strained stomach muscles.

Outside linebacker Henry Rolling will take Smith's place in the starting lineup, and will be backed up by inside linebacker Cedric Figaro. If Hendrickson joins the club, he will assume Rolling's special teams duties.

The Chargers took a look at wide receiver/punt returner and former Lincoln High School player Lew Barnes Tuesday.

Barnes was released earlier this year by Buffalo.
