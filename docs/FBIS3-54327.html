Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Mr. Muwaffaq al-'Allaf, Syria's chief delegate to the Washington talks, has reiterated the parameters of the Syrian position underscored by President Hafiz al-Asa at the Geneva talks with President Bill Clinton. Mr. al-'Allaf underlined the need for a settlement embracing all Arab fronts and Syria's rejection of any proposals that do not provide for the entire Golan Heights to be handed back to Syria. At last night's session with his Israeli counterpart Itamar Rabinovich, al-'Allaf outlined the Geneva summit proceedings and developments since.
