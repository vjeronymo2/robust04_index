931018 FT 18 OCT 93 / Construction Contracts: Gyle Shopping Centre Bovis Construction (Scotland) and Wimpey Construction (Scotland) handed over the Pounds 68m, 300,000 sq ft Gyle Shopping Centre (above) in Edinburgh on schedule, ready for its October opening. Almost 100 per cent let, the complex provides 63 multiple occupancy units on the ground floor with anchor stores Marks and Spencer and Safeway. The 420-seat food court and bistro with creche facilities are located on the upper level above the main entrance.
