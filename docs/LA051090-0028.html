May 10, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC/STAGE

Wiltern Seeks Major Artists: George Cree, the new executive director of the Wiltern Theatre, said he is determined to broaden the range of entertainment at the theater, and is negotiating to attract major soloists, orchestras, dance companies, operas and stage productions. Cree made the announcement on the fifth anniversary of the Wilshire Boulevard theater's restoration and reopening. A Mozart festival by the Los Angeles Chamber Orchestra and a performance by the American Indian Dance Theatre will be among the Wiltern highlights this year. GREG BRAXTON
