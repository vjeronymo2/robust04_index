BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres has called on the members of the UN Security Council [UNSC] to immediately send funds to the Palestinians. He noted that Israel continues paying the salaries of the Civil Administration workers in order to prevent a crisis, though they are not under Israeli jurisdiction. The foreign minister asked the Japanese ambassador to immediately provide the Palestinians with $30 million of the $200 million his country pledged to give them. Minister Peres met in New York with the ambassadors of the members of the UNSC. Our correspondent Arye Golan reports that the meeting was attended by the PLO's UN observer, and the ambassadors of Morocco and Djibouti.
