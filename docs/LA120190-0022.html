December 1, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BEATING 49ERS NOT ENOUGH FOR RAM FANS

In a recent story, John Shaw stated that "management would take some major actions" if the Rams' record does not improve.

Instead of fueling speculation regarding the future of John Robinson, Shaw's statement gave me hope that Georgia Frontiere will finally sell the team.

Maybe Georgia can buy a chain of beauty shops with the profits she makes from the sale of the Rams. We'll never have another Super Bowl team until we have a Super Bowl-type owner and management.

KENT McCLISH

San Juan Capistrano
