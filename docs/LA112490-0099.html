November 24, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

NORTHERN IRELAND;

IRA'S BIGGEST BOMB FAILS TO EXPLODE

The Irish Republican Army forced a kidnaped driver to deliver the biggest bomb it has ever used against British troops in Northern Ireland, police said. But the "human bomb" attack against a security checkpoint near Rosslea failed when the driver of a van packed with 3,300 pounds of explosives warned security forces, and only the detonator exploded. The driver and soldiers were unhurt.
