921105 FT 05 NOV 92 / Maxwell's Death - The First Anniversary: Evidence highlights fees EVIDENCE submitted to the Commons social security committee disclose just part of the total professional fees charged so far by accountants and lawyers in unravelling the Maxwell empire, Andrew Jack writes. They include: Pounds 8m in legal fees and Pounds 7m from Price Waterhouse, administrators to Maxwell Communication Corporation. Pounds 5m in legal fees and Pounds 6m from Arthur Andersen, administrators to MCC. Pounds 2.1m in legal fees and Pounds 1.6m from Robson Rhodes, liquidators to Bishopsgate Investment Management, manager and trustee of the Maxwell pension funds. Pounds 443,000 from Buchler Phillips, receivers to the estate of Robert Maxwell.
