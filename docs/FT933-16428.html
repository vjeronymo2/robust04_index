930703 FT 03 JUL 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Indian pepper exporters are asking for an extension of shipping period by one month for the June contracts to New York, reports Man-producten. Unfavourable weather conditions have causing drying problems. Malaysia and Indonesia are both reluctantly offering at higher levels. White: Muntok spot USDollars 1,950 a tonne, Jun-Jul Dollars 1,830. Black: Sarawak black label spot Dollars 1,215, June-Jul Dollars 1,150; Sarawak yellow label spot Dollars 1,265, Jun-Jul Dollars 1,200. Brazil grade 1 Jun-Jul Dollars 1,150, India mg1 spot Dollars 1,245, Jul Dollars 1,175. Vietnam spot Dollars 1,125, Jun-Jul Dollars 1,100. Thailand faq spot Dollars 1,200, Jul-Aug Dollars 1,115.
