BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 6 (KCNA) -- Some 1,000 students in Jonju, South Korea, had a starting ceremony at the Jonbuk National University Thursday and vowed a vigorous fight to achieve national reunification and democracy, a radio report from Seoul said. The starting ceremony was sponsored by the North Cholla Provincial Federation of General Student Councils consisting of general student councils of 15 universities in the province. After the ceremony the students took to the streets and staged a protest, distributing to citizens copies of a literature calling for "Refusal of National Assembly endorsement of the final act of the Uruguay Round of world trade talks".
