940519 FT 19 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Sharpe & Fisher property disposal Sharpe & Fisher is to sell its freehold investment property at Newbury, Berkshire, to the Ocean Accident and Guarantee Corporation for Pounds 4m cash. The property attracts an annual rental income of Pounds 287,500. It was held in the last audited accounts of December 31 1993 at Pounds 3.4m. Completion is due to take place on June 24 and the cash proceeds arising from the sale will be used to reduce debt.
