BFN [Text] According to information in the RABOCHAYA TRIBUNA a criminal group which tried to sell a container of radioactive substances exported from Russia for $20,000 has been detained by the police in Moldova. An expert investigation showed that the factory-made package, which was confiscated, contained gamma-cobalt-60. In spite of the fact that it had been hermetically sealed, the experts discovered a small leak of the radioactive substance.
