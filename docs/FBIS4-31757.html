BFN [Text] Budapest, 13 Jun (MTI) -- President Arpad Goncz, in the course of successive meetings with party leaders, today met Ivan Peto, chairman of the Alliance of Free Democrats (SZDSZ). They discussed the Saturday explosion damaging a Parliament door and agreed that although such incidents are worrying, they occasionally occur in almost every democratic society. There is no nead for general anxiety, Goncz said. Peto outlined the present stage of the SZDSZ's coalition talks with the Hungarian Socialist Party, although not in detail. Goncz had ealier said he would not get involved in the negotiations. Peto saw the talks on joint Socialist-liberal rule as encouraging. A good atmosphere ensures effective work, he said. "Socialists and Free Democrats feel it is vital to improve government-opposition relations in the future," Peto said.
