July 14, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

SPOTLIGHT / COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE: A WEEKLY LOOK AT OFFICE AND INDUSTRIAL REAL ESTATE IN THE COUNTY.;

FOCUS: CLAUSET CENTRE

Located in the South Coast Metro area, Clauset Centre contains 183,000 square feet in one five-story and one three-story building. The business park features plazas, underground parking and an on-site restaurant. Completed in 1982, it is 85% occupied.

FACTS AND FIGURES

Address: 3100 and 3130 S. Harbor Blvd., Santa Ana

Developer: Wachovia Bank & Trust Co.

Architect: Ware & Malcomb Architects Inc.

Primary Contractor: Trammell Crow Co.

Broker: Coldwell Banker

Use: Office

Date completed: 1982

Percentage of building leased: 85%

Parking capacity: 650 spaces
