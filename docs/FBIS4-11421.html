BFN [Text] On 26 April, Cuban Minister Rosa Elena Simeon has been designated vice president of the main committee of the First International Conference of Small Island Nations on Sustainable Development, which is being held in Bridgetown. Simeon told PRENSA LATINA that the appointment is an acknowledgment of Cuba's efforts in this field. The conference will reach its peak on 5-6 May, when it will hold meetings by state and government leaders of the alliance of island nations.
