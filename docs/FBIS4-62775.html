BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Ivanova] [Text] Moscow, 17 May (ITAR-TASS) -- This is not the best time to relieve Shakhray of his post. This was the opinion expressed today in an interview with an ITAR-TASS correspondent by Konstantin Zatulin, chairman of the State Duma committee for CIS affairs. Commenting on yesterday's report on "Russia" TV channel that Shakhray, relieved of the post of minister of affairs of nationalities and regional policy, could resign today from the post of vice-premier, Konstantin Zatulin said that according to his information Sergey Shakhray had to have a series of talks and meetings on this subject. Konstantin Zatulin believes that, in any case, it is the soveriegn right of Sergey Shakhray himself to make such a decision.
