940923 FT 23 SEP 94 / World Stock Markets: S Africa sees quieter session The lack of follow through in the bullion price after it tested the Dollars 396 level left gold shares quieter with no major selling. Industrials regained some stability after Wednesday's fall as buying crept back in at the new lower levels. The overall index shed 28 to 5,754 and the industrial index also lost 28 to 6,340 while the gold index dipped 18 to 2,454. Both De Beers and Anglos shed R1 to R105 and R242 respectively. Among gold stocks, Vaal Reefs shed R5 to R483, Oryx added 25 cents to R6.75 and Western Areas was down R1 at R70. Gencor was 10 cents lower at R14.50 and Iscor lost 6 cents to R4.42. Among the day's other movers SAB lost R1 to R82.50, Barlows fell R1 to R28.50 and Pick n Pay fell 35 cents to R11.25.
