921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Culver at pounds 120,000 and pays dividend Culver Holdings, which was formed to make a recommended offer for Wyndham Group in July 1991, announced a pre-tax profit of Pounds 122,000 for the half year to June 30. For the six months to December 1991 profits were Pounds 130,000. The group sells new and used cars through Honda and BMW franchises. Mr Richard Read, the chairman, said the two periods had different trading profiles as the second half of the year included August, the most significant month for car sales. In spite of that, turnover was slightly ahead at Pounds 10.4m (Pounds 10.3m). Earnings per share were 0.21p (0.29p) and an interim dividend of 0.1p is declared.
