National Institute on Deafness and Other Communication Disorders; Amended Notice of Meeting Notice is hereby given of a change in the March 1, 1994, meeting of the National Institute on Deafness and Other Communication Disorders Special Emphasis Panel, which was published in the Federal Register on January 31, 1994, 59 FR 4289. This Special Emphasis Panel was to have convened at 8 a.m. on March 1, 1994, at the Chevy Chase Holiday Inn, but has been changed to 8 a.m., March 11, 1994, at the Bethesda Marriott, 5151 Pooks Hills Road, Bethesda MD. The meeting will be closed to the public.
