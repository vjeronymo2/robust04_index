Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Police today destroyed exhibits in the form of drugs--mandrax tablets and dagga [marijuana]--at the Usuthu Pulp blast furnace. The police public relations officer, Inspector Azariah Ndzimandze, said the drugs had been in the possession of police from 1990 to 1993. The total number of tablets were 18,006,064 with a street value of over 3 million emalangeni, and there were 195 bags of dagga with a street value of 199,500 emalangeni. Inspector Ndzimandze added that the drugs were not destroyed earlier because some of the exhibits were pending cases with the courts.
