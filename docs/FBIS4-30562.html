BFN [Text] A preliminary contact for top-level talks between the North and the South has begun. A preliminary contact for top-level talks between the North and the South has begun in the South side area at Panmunjom at 1000 [0100 GMT] on 28 June. Kim Yong-sun, chairman of the Reunification Policy Committee of the Supreme People's Assembly and head of our delegation; An Pyong- su, vice chairman of the Committee for the Peaceful Reunification of the Fatherland; Paek Nam-chun, chief councilor in the Administration Council; and the delegates from our side, attended the contact. The contact continues.
