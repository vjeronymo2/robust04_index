Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Armenian Defense Ministry press service reported at 1500 that a relative calm still prevails at the Armenian-Azerbaijani border. The situation remains tense on Karabakh's southeastern war front. Nagorno-Karabakh Republic Defense Army press service reports that following a brief lull overnight, active military operations directed toward Fizuli resumed at 0600 this morning. The Azerbaijani troops opened intensive rocket and artillery fire on the Karabakh positions. The enemy is again trying to break through the defenses of the Karabakh forces. The enemy's attacks were still being checked at 0900. Casualty figures are being determined. A relative calm prevails on the other sectors of the front since last night. At present, we have not received any additional information.
