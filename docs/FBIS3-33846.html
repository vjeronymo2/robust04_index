Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The district magistrate, Karachi East, has issued a press note this evening. It says that after the murder of MQM [Muhajir Qaumi Movement]-Haqiqi Group activist, Zafar Abbas, yesterday in Korangi area, some incidents of violence erupted in the jurisdiction of Landhi, Korangi, and the Shah Faisal Colony and Malir police stations. Miscreants this morning opened fire in Korangi on a van carrying five persons. Two of them received fatal injuries and afterwards died in hospital. Two vans were set on fire by unknown persons in Shah Faisal and Korangi areas. The situation in these areas is peaceful and under control.
