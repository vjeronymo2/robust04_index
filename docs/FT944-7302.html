941125 FT 25 NOV 94 / Tunnel link bill published A bill to authorise the building of the 67-mile, Pounds 2.7bn Channel tunnel high-speed rail link was published yesterday. The bill may take up to two years to complete its passage through parliament, and the project is unlikely to be completed before 2002. The bill also includes the widening of 11 miles of Kent's A2 trunk road at Cobham and the M2 on the outskirts of the Medway Towns.
