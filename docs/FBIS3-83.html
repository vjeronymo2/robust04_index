Language: Afrikaans Article Type:BFN [Text] Inkatha Freedom party leader Mangosuthu Buthelezi said millions of King Zwelithini's followers will not take part in the elections unless demands for a Zulu kingdom are met. Dr. Buthelezi and King Goodwill today held talks in Ulundi with Minister of Home Affairs Danie Schutte. [Begin recording] [Schutte, in English] From our side I can just say that the government is committed to an honorable recognition for the position of the Zulu king and kingdom in the new constitutional dispensation. There are restrictions and limitations but we are committed to that, and we will do everything in our power to try and bring that about. [Buthelezi, in English] We don't think that it's a matter that's impossible to resolve as such because kingdoms exist in Africa. There was a kingdom in Uganda -- I think there are four kingdoms in Uganda, including the most (?northern) part of Uganda. Kingdoms have been accommodated in the colonialization period in Africa. [end recording]
