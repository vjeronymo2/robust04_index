Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] Last year, our country's oil and gas sector exploited 6.3 million tons of crude oil, performed a 5,815-meter deep exploratory drill, completed the drilling of 17 oil wells, and started building a gas pipeline from the Bach Ho Oil Well to the hinterland.
