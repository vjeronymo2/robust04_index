Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 2 (KCNA) -- Official commendations and gifts were awarded to participants in the National Agricultural Conference with a due ceremony here on March 1. Present at the ceremony were Premier Kang Song-san and Vice- President Yi Chong-ok who are members of the Political Bureau of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, and other party and state cadres. Honorary titles were awarded to the units which had made palpable successes in the socialist rural construction and grain and other agricultural production through their vigorous efforts to implement the "Theses on the Socialist Rural Question in Our Country", and letters of commendation of the great leader President Kim Il-song to 27 participants in the conference, the commemoration order of the 30th anniversary of publication of the rural theses to 9,993 and order of national flag second class to 139. And commendations of the great leader President Kim Il-song were awarded to 340 people and gifts to the entire participants.
