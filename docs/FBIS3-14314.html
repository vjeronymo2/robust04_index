Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The NATIONAL NEWS AGENCY correspondent in al-Nabatiyah has reported that the Israeli aggression against Iqlim al-Tuffah intensified a short time ago. All the villages of the region are under heavy bombardment by 175-mm artillery. He also reported that two women were martyred in Jba' as a result of the Israeli shelling of the town. Citing a report from the internal security forces, the correspondent reports that five shells of unknown caliber fell on the occupied town of Marj 'Uyun after 0830. The shells were fired from an unknown position.
