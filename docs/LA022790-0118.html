February 27, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

FULLERTON MAN DIES OF GUNSHOT WOUND

A Fullerton man died Monday from at least one gunshot wound to the head, police said.

Bennie Lee Sewell, 39, was found wounded in an alley in the 1300 block of South Euclid Street about 5:55 a.m., police said. He was taken to Western Medical Center-Santa Ana where he died about 3 p.m.

The weapon has not been found, and police have no suspects.
