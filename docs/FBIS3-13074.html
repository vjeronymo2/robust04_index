Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Islamic Group has announced that it detonated two bombs in front of the Nile Tower in al-Jizah, which houses the Egyptian-American Business Council, the International Bank of Commerce, and the Transmed Travel Agency. The group said the detonations were in retaliation for the criminal act committed in the al-Ibrahimi Mosque in Hebron. A statement by the Islamic Group said the two operations were the beginning of what it termed revenge for the al-Ibrahimi Mosque martyrs and noted that revenge will continue in the end of the month of Ramadan [sentence as published].
