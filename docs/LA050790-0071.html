May 7, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

LIMELIGHT: The Rev. Louis Sheldon, Anaheim's "traditional values" crusader, is being shadowed by a CBS News crew this week as he lobbies in Sacramento. . . . He'll be included in a "48 Hours" program in June examining gay rights and its opponents. . . . "48 Hours" is a step up from the clamor of his recent "Donahue" appearance, Sheldon says. This time, he promises, "you'll see the real Lou Sheldon." Kris Lindgren and Dean Takahashi
