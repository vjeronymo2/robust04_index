Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Rome, March 21 (AFP) -- A Sicilian was holding 160 people hostage aboard a plane that landed at Fiumicino airport here at 11:30 a.m. (1030 GMT) from Palermo, airport officials said. The hostages were 154 passengers and six crew. The hijacker, who has not been identified further, has asked to speak with the airport director. The hijacker was said to be from Trapani in western Sicily, with a criminal record and psychological problems. The plane was from the domestic Italian company Meridiana and has been isolated on a runway.
