BFN [By reporter Yu Wenjiang (0060 2429 3068): "Songyuan Establishes Military Subdistrict"] [Text] On the morning of 13 April, Jilin's Songyuan Military Subdistrict of the Chinese people's Liberation Army officially declared its establishment. By that time, all the eight cities and one autonomous prefecture in Jilin Province had established military subdistricts. Qi Lianyun, deputy commander of the Shenyang Military Region, and Wang Jinshan, deputy secretary of the Jilin Provincial party committee, attended and addressed the inaugural meeting. According to the order of the Central Military Commission, Yang Baoya [2254 0202 0068) was appointed commander of the Songyuan Military Subdistrict, and Wu Jun [0702 6874] was appointed political commissar. Also attending the inaugural meeting were Zhou Zaikang, commander, and Shi Zhaoping, political commissar of the Jilin Provincial Military District; Liu Shuying [0491 3219 3853], vice governor of Jilin; and party and government leading comrades of Songyuan city.
