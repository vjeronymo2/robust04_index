931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Creston deficit cut to Pounds 642,000 In a year of 'considerable change' Creston, the construction components group, achieved a reduction in losses from Pounds 1.09m to Pounds 642,000 pre-tax. Turnover for the 12 months to end-June amounted to Pounds 6.25m (Pounds 8.89m) and losses per share worked through at 3.2p (5.7p). During the period Co-ordinated Land and Estates was acquired for 31m shares and the disposal of 5m shares in Ex-Lands was completed. Since June, Creston's original businesses have been reorganised and it is proposed that the name of the company be changed to Creston Land & Estates. Mr John Ansdell, formerly finance director of Trafalgar House, has been appointed a non-executive director.
