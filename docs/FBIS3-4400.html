Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The third plenary session of the discipline inspection commission of the Heilongjiang Provincial CPC Committee ended in Harbin today after a three day session. The plenary session adopted the resolution and proposed efforts be made to conscientiously implement the guidelines of the third plenary session of the central discipline inspection commission and the plan of the provincial party committee on anticorruption struggle, act in line with the overall situation of seizing the opportunity, deepening reform, expanding opening up, promoting development, and maintaining stability, and unswervingly conduct anticorruption struggle. The plenary session discussed and adopted a work report given by Li Qinglin, member of the standing committee of the provincial party committee and secretary of the provincial discipline inspection commission, on behalf of the standing committee of the provincial discipline inspection commission and stressed the necessity of making further efforts to strengthen leadership over anticorruption struggle and implement responsibility, to expand the degree of work, to further enhance the quality of leading cadres in keeping themselves clean and self-disciplined and in conducting self- investigation and making self-correction, to combine self-investigation and self-correction with mass supervision, screening, and evaluation of one's honesty, to further strengthen the investigation and handling of cases, to resolutely check a number of major and appalling cases, to further attend to checking unhealthy trends, to stress both temporary solutions and radical measures, to expand the degree of work in this aspect, to study supporting measures for preventing passive corrupt phenomena in line with the implementation of major reform measures, to strengthen mass media propaganda on anticorruption struggle, and to persist in correct media guidance. The plenary session called on discipline inspection and supervision cadres at all levels to conscientiously study, strive to enhance their own political and professional level, earnestly sum up the work experience of the discipline inspection and supervision organs after their amalgamation, give better play to the functions of discipline inspection and supervision, and make new contributions to promoting the province's economic development, reform, and opening up, and maintaining social stability. At the end of the session, Li Qinglin explained the relevant issues on policy limits for anticorruption work.
