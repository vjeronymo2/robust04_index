Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Rukh believes that holding presidential elections without determining the presidential status and authority by the [words indistinct] Supreme Council is illogical. This is the essence of a statement by the Rukh Small Council, which was publicized today. According to Council members, the people should know whom they are going to elect -- only a head of state, or a head of executive authority. The Small Council also resolved to immediately begin preparations for local elections by all the Rukh structures.
