BFN [Text] At a news conference in Zagreb, the Croat National Council of Bosnia-Herzegovina [HNV] spoke of an initiative for the founding of a commission for monitoring the implementation of the Washington and Vienna agreements between the Croats and Muslims. The HNV also appealed to the Serb people in Bosnia-Herzegovina to join this agreement on Bosnia-Herzegovina as a state belonging to three equal nations, refrain from violence and ethnic cleansing, stop the war, and return territories taken by force.
