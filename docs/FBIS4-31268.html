BFN [Excerpt] Geneva, 7 Jun (TANJUG) -- Late this evening Srpska republic president Radovan Karadzic said after yet another round of talks with special envoy Yasushi Akashi that the Muslim side wanted war and not peace. "It is also obvious that someone is supporting them from the outside," he pointed out. Before leaving the UN headquarters, Karadzic said that the Serb delegation would remain in Geneva until tomorrow to see if the Muslim-Croat delegation would soften its stand according to which it would accept only a four-week cease-fire.
