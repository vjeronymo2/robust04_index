Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 25 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent messages of greetings to Mohamed Abdelaziz, president of the Saharan Arab Democratic Republic and secretary general of the Polisario [Popular Front for the Liberation of Saquia El Hambra and Rio de Oro] Front, Muhammad Husni Mubarak, president of the Arab Republic of Egypt, and Blaise Compaore, president, head of state and chairman of the Council of Ministers of Burkina Faso on the new year 1994. Comrade Kim Chong-il in the messages wished them greater success in their work for the prosperity of their countries in the new year.
