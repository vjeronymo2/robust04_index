BFN [Text] Istanbul, April 2 (A.A) -- A Spanish tourist died of his injuries in hospital on Saturday [2 April] following the explosion of a time bomb planted in front of Kapalicarsi (Covered Bazaar) earlier in the day, officials said. A Tunisian woman had also died in the same incident and twelve people are still under treatment in various hospitals around the city. A time bomb that was set off at 10.50 A.M. exploded in front of Kapalicarsi killing one and injuring thirteen people. No one has so far claimed responsibility for the blast. On March 24 a similar incident in Kapalicarsi's Yaglikcilar Street had left four wounded, including two Romanians, when a time bomb exploded in the ladies' toilets.
