941004 FT 04 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Capita in Pounds 4.5m buy Capita Group, the provider of outsourcing, advisory and property services to the public sector, is to acquire Beard Dove for a maximum Pounds 4.5m. The initial consideration of Pounds 2.7m will be satisfied by the issue of 656,416 Capita shares at 169.1p apiece along with Pounds 1.64m in cash. An additional profits-related payment of up to Pounds 1.8m may become payable, satisfied by the issue of either ordinary shares or loan notes, or by a combination of the two.
