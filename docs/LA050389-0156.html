May 3, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

IN BRIEF;

MUNICH TO HOST DAVIS CUP SEMIFINAL

The Davis Cup semifinal between West Germany and the United States will be played at the Olympiahalle in Munich July 21 through 23.

The West German Tennis Federation said today that Munich was chosen over Dortmund because the Bavarian capital has more hotel space available.

The Olympic hall was the site of the Davis Cup final in 1985, when Sweden beat West Germany.
