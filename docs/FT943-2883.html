940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Folkes advances Increased demand for engineering products and services and the absence of losses from Evertidy Furniture, following its disposal last autumn, resulted in a turnround at Folkes Group in the first half of 1994. On sales of Pounds 19.2m, against Pounds 20.1m including Pounds 2.2m from discontinued activities, pre-tax profits were Pounds 1.3m (Pounds 200,000). In 1993 the engineering and property concern suffered a pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.72m. The shares jumped 8p to 48p. The interim dividend is increased to 0.592p (0.575p) on earnings per share of 2.58p (0.29p).
