910427 FT 27 APR 91 / UK Company News: Jones Group up 7% and lifts dividend The Jones Group, a Dublin-based engineering company, reported taxable profits of IPounds 5.51m (Pounds 4.97m) for 1990, up 7 per cent from IPounds 5.47m last time. The recommended final dividend is raised by 1p to 9p, making a total of 12p (11p). The sale of the two environmental companies towards the end of the year for a maximum of IPounds 13.8m helped increase shareholders' funds by 50 per cent to IPounds 33m.
