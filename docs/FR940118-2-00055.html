F. ``Diamond drill bits'' means natural diamond drill bits and polycrystalline diamond compact drill bits. Diamond drill bits do not include coring bits. G. ``Drilling fluid'' means a mixture of natural and synthetic chemical compounds used at petrocarbon drilling sites to cool and lubricate the drill bit, clean the hole bottom, carry cuttings to the surface, seal porous well formations, control downhole pressures, and improve the function of the drilling string and tools in the hole.
