BFN [Text] Creation of the Ukrainian Civil Council of the Crimea, UHRK, has been announced today in Simferopol at an expanded meeting of the Congress of Crimean Ukrainians with the participation of representatives of democratic parties, public organizations, and movements that operate on the peninsula. The Ukrainian Civil Council of the Crimea is a nationally-oriented parliament-type of representative body of the national and democratic forces. It was noted at the meeting that the establishment of this body was prompted by the Crimean crisis which is continuing to grow and is assuming a dangerous form.
