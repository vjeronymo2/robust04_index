Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Interview with unidentified captain first rank by unidentified correspondent; place and date not given -- recorded; from the "Aty-Baty" program] [Text] In accordance with the adopted decision, the following armed forces journals will cease publication: VESTNIK PROTIVOVOZDUSHNOY OBORONY [THE AIR DEFENSE HERALD]; VOYENNYY VESTNIK [THE MILITARY HERALD]; AVIATSIYA I KOSMONAVTIKA [AVIATION AND SPACE]; VOYENNO-EKONOMICHESKIY ZHURNAL [THE MILITARY AND ECONOMIC JOURNAL], which until recently was called TYL VOORUZHENNYKH SIL [THE ARMED FORCES REAR]; and TEKHNIKA I VOORUZHENIYE [EQUIPMENT AND ARMS]. From 1 July of this year, we will be publishing the ARMEYSKIY SBORNIK [THE ARMY COLLECTION], which will absorb the topics of and replace the five journals ceasing publication. It has already been included in the subscription catalog, and one will be able to become a subscriber. The three educational and trends journals, ARMIYA [ARMY], SOBESEDNIK VOYNA [THE SOLDIER'S INTERLOCUTOR], and ARGUMENT [ARGUMENT], which were published by the armed forces until recently, will be replaced by a journal called ORIYENTIR [THE ORIENTATOR], the first issue of which we expect will be issued after 1 July.
