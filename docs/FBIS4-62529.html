BFN [Text] The Georgian Defense Ministry press center said Abkhazian separatist detachments on Sunday [15 May] had subjected to heavy fire Georgian positions at Khurcha village in Zugdidi District. The Georgians did not return fire, the center said. According to the Georgian high command, the attack, which lasted from 1900 till midnight, was an attempt by Georgia's separatist region to wreck the Moscow peace agreements and unleash wide-scale hostilities.
