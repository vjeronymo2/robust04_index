BFN [From the "RPC Television News" newscast] [Text] President Guillermo Endara has announced that on 18 April he will disclose a decision he has made, a decision which will generate controversy and prompt criticism against him. The president has warned the statements he is planning to make on a television network broadcast on 18 April will shock the Panamanian community. He has said the mere thought of it makes him ill. [Begin Endara recording] The only reason I am ill is due to the worry this decision has caused me. This decision is that I.... [pauses] I have made a very difficult decision, and it may be very controversial. I will speak on a national network on 18 April at 1900. At that time you will know why I am ill. You will have to wait until 18 April to know. [end recording] The president made the announcement during inauguration of a bridge that links the so-called old section of the Free Zone with the France Field warehouses in Colon Province. [The announcement is] prompting speculation of a possible resignation, because the president said repeatedly over the past few days that his feet are itching to leave the Palace of the Herons. [Begin recording] [Endara] In reality it has nothing to do with that other foot disease. It has nothing to do with the feet. This is a disease of the head and heart. [Unidentified reporter] Is it a political decision, Mr. President? [Endara] It is definitely political, but it is my duty. It will be very controversial, and I will be widely criticized, but it is my duty. [end recording] Despite reporters' persistent questions, Endara declined to provide any details regarding the statements he will make on 18 April at 1900, and he appealed for patience until then.
