BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES, 12/04/1994 -- The leaders of the Liberal Party '93 (PL '93) have made their intention known to the press by issuing an "Appeal to All Romanians for Conciliation and Concord," which also runs as an "invitation" extended to the former sovereign of Romania, Mihai I. The appeal is signed by 84 members of parliament representing PL '93, the Peasant Party (PNTCD), the Civic Alliance Party (PAC), the Hungarians' Union (UDMR), the Social Democrats (PSDR), the Democrats (PD-FSN), and two independent parliamentarians. It shows that on the religious holiday of Easter (Orthodox, 1 May) "King Mihai of Romania must be together with all Romanians, in the ancestral land, as a citizen of this country, observant of its constitutional order." The former sovereign expressed his wish to celebrate Easter in Romania, at Timisoara.
