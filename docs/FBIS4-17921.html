BFN [Text] The government formed a high-level commission today to monitor and regulate the removal of Russian nuclear reactors from the Paldiski naval base near Tallinn. The commission will be led by Energy Minister Arvo Niitenberg. Other members include Foreign Minister Juri Luik, Interior Minister Heiki Arike, and Environment Minister Andres Tarand. Premier Mart Laar said that the ministers appointed as members will also be able appropriate representatives and specialists from any relevant department as needed. (?Estonia) has stated repeatedly that it is ready for the nuclear fuel removal and the dismantlement of the nuclear reactors. Estonia will impose a special regime on Pakri peninsula for the time of the nuclear fuel removal, as the Estonian side must ensure safety of this procedure. According to preliminary information the removal of nuclear fuel will begin in early May.
