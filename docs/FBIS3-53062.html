Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] His majesty the king received Omani Minister of State for Foreign Affairs Yusuf Bin-'Alawi, who arrived in Amman this evening on a two-day visit to the kingdom. The Omani envoy delivered a verbal message from Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id on bilateral ties. Tonight's audience, which is still in progress, dealt with regional, Arab, and international developments, the Middle East peace process, and means to restore Arab solidarity. The Omani envoy arrived in Amman this evening on a two-day visit to Jordan. In an arrival statement, Mr. Bin-'Alawi said his country supports any endeavor towards achieving peace in the region. He described the signing of a Jordanian-PLO agreement yesterday as a step in the right direction. The Omani minister also said that Oman supports efforts to achieve Arab solidarity. Minister of State for Foreign Affairs Talal al-Hasan, who welcomed the Omani guest at the airport, said the visit would enhance and strengthen the already strong ties between Jordan and Oman.
