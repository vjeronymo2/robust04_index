940122 FT 22 JAN 94 / UK Company News: St Andrew Trust net assets rise 30% Strong returns from UK smaller companies, coupled with good performances from its continental European and Pacific Rim portfolios, helped St Andrew Trust raise net asset value per share by 30 per cent in 1993. The advance - from 249.5p to 324.8p - compared with an improvement of 23.3 per cent in the FT-SE-A All-Share Index. The trust, 58 per cent-owned by the Ecclesiastical Insurance Office and managed by Martin Currie, reported net revenue of Pounds 2.71m, up from Pounds 2.58m, for earnings of 7.81p (7.42p) per share. A recommended final dividend of 5.1p lifts the total to 7.8p (7.55p).
