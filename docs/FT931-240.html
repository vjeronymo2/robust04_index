930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / Vauxhall exempts most Cavaliers models from price increase VAUXHALL has exempted most Cavalier models from an average 2.9 per cent price increase yesterday on its cars. The move was presented as a first response to the new company car taxation structure. The carmaker also said it was eliminating all dealer incentives on Cavaliers, worth Pounds 300-Pounds 400 per car, adding that the measures were another step in cutting dealer margins.
