BFN [Text] [No dateline as received] A foreign delegation consisting of IAEA [International Atomic Energy Agency] inspectors and representatives of the donor-countries such as Sweden, Japan, the United States, and Great Britain are on a visit to Almaty. The aim of their visit is to examine issues of technical support, accounting and control, and physical protection of nuclear materials in Kazakhstan. During their 10-day visit, foreign specialists will hold a seminar for those employed in the industry and visit relevant enterprises in Mangistau and East Kazakhstan Oblast.
