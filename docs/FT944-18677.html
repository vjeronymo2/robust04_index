941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / Childcare payments to be launched A childcare allowance worth up to Pounds 38 a week to benefit claimants will be launched today by Mr Peter Lilley, social security secretary. The cost of the allowance announced by Mr Kenneth Clarke, the chancellor, in the last Budget, is expected to reach Pounds 60m by 1996-97. Mr Clarke said it should 'help tens of thousands of mothers to get back into work and off income support'. The social security department estimates that 150,000 families will benefit.
