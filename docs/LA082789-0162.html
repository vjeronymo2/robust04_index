August 27, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

MAGNOLIA ADVANCES IN GIRLS' VOLLEYBALL

The Magnolia High School girls' volleyball team advanced Saturday to the finals of the West Coast Prep Volleyball summer tournament at Cal State Dominguez Hills after finishing 6-2 in pool play.

Magnolia was led by senior outside hitter Jill Schneider, who averaged nearly 10 kills per game. Finals start at 8 a.m. today.
