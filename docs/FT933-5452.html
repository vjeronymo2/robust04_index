930903 FT 03 SEP 93 / BR will cut some fares by 25% SOME off-peak InterCity rail fares are to be cut by 25 per cent from the beginning of next month. In an effort to attract more northbound passengers from the south of England, InterCity is making the cuts on Apex tickets between 190 south-east stations and West Yorkshire from October 3. In addition, the number of seats available to Apex ticketholders travelling from King's Cross to West Yorkshire will be increased by 750 to 3,000 a week.
