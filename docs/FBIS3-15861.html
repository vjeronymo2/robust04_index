Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Volodymyr Brun, a candidate for the Ukrainian Supreme Soviet in the 419th constituency, has recently had a telephone conversation with Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, leader of the Liberal Democratic Party of Russia. The latter, according to Volodymyr Brun, has accepted his invitation to come to Cherkassy as an international observer in the 27 March elections and said that he will try to find time for him. As Volodymyr Brun has already stated, he is going to visit Moscow to also invite [former Russian Vice President] Aleksandr Rutskoy, Albert Makashov, Viktor Varennikov [perpetrators of the 1991 coup in Russia], and [former Defense Minister] Dmitriy Yazov to act as observers.
