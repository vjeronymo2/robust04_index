921216 FT 16 DEC 92 / Engineers are 'more gloomy on prospects' SCOTTISH engineering companies have become more gloomy about their prospects in the past three months, the latest survey by Scottish Engineering says. Eighteen per cent of companies said they were more optimistic than three months earlier while 38 per cent said they were less optimistic, with 44 per cent unchanged. The survey shows that orders for electronics companies have improved and companies in all sectors, except for small ones, have achieved a rise in export orders. Capital investment is generally decreasing except in the electronics and heavy engineering industries. Employment is falling across the industry, except for electronics, where it rising slightly.
