Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] In an interview to ITAR-TASS, Sergey Samoylov, deputy head of the presidential administration directorate for cooperation with territories, said that elections to bodies of representative power are under threat in many regions of Russia. Samoylov said that because of this, Boris Yeltsin has given the Central Electoral Commission permission to allow the regions to relax strict electoral rules and to hold the elections in accordance with local circumstances.
