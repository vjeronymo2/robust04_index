December 27, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: 1990 ORANGE COUNTY. CHALLENGE FOR THE 1990S;

COMMUNITY LEADERS WERE ASKED WHAT WILL BE THE BIGGEST SINGLE CHALLENGE FACING ORANGE COUNTY IN THE NEXT DECADE AND ALSO THEIR TOP FIVE ISSUES.

"Transportation:

"The reality is that we do not have enough land to build the roads to move the people in the autos who want to live here. The next generation of transportation corridors should utilize development which will encourage persons to live and work within walking distance and avoid the use of autos. These new transportation corridors should be above-ground transit systems. One such should connect to an airport off the coast which could be built on floating cement sections.."-Rep. William E. Dannemyer (R-Fullerton)"

Top 5 Issues

Transportation

Lack of affordable housing.

Availability of health care for indigents.

Education of ethnic minorities.

Quality of life in an urban setting.
