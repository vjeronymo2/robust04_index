931012 FT 12 OCT 93 / PLO ratifies peace deal THE Palestine Liberation Organisation central council yesterday ratified the peace deal with Israel, officials said, Reuter reports from Tunis. The accord signed last month provides for Palestinian self-rule first in the Gaza Strip and Jericho. It has been ratified by the Israeli parliament and takes effect tomorrow. The accord was approved by 63 to eight, with 11 members abstaining or absent. Two radical factions boycotted the meeting of the council.
