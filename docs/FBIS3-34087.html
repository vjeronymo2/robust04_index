Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] The first issue of the publication "JOURNAL OF EVENTS" [DAFTAR-E RU'YDADHA] on art, literature, and culture published by the Cultural Review Committee of IRNA has been disseminated. In the first edition of this monthly magazine a variety of articles of cultural interest have been included, such as: the Farsi language, books, publications, the theater, music, sculpture, the fine arts, Iranians abroad, and cultural events in the Islamic and Western world as reported by the IRNA representative offices abroad and from the mass media that conform to the tastes of those interested in the arts, culture, and literature.
