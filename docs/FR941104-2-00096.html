No. 33&hyph;00069) located in Paulding County, Ohio. Due to the 69 inch clearance from the concrete to the shell; the 63 inch clearance from the concrete floor to the rib on the mill; the 61 inch clearance to the door; and given the weight of the steel balls and magnitude of the task, the petitioner requests a modification of the mandatory safety standard to allow the use of a 310 Bobcat that has been modified so that the height of the machine is 66.5 inches that would perform the necessary task of screening the mill safely and efficiently.
