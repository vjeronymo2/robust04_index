BFN [By reporter Chen (7115 8301) and correspondent Fan Liangsheng (2868 5328 3932)] [Text] Nanjing, 17 May (XINHUA) -- China's first provincial tax law -- the "Jiangsu Procedures for Implementing the `PRC Law for Tax Collection and Control'" -- has officially become effective. Ever since their becoming effective, China's tax law and the detailed rules for implementating this law have been playing an important role in expediting the development of market economy in Jiangsu, in intensifying tax collection, in promoting tax management according to law, in tightening tax supervision, and in increasing revenues. Jiangsu's business taxes in 1993 increased 54.4 percent over that of 1992, reaching 21.273 billion yuan, accounting for more than 96 percent of the province's revenues in 1993. Taxes have become the province's main source of income. Basing on the PRC tax law, the procedures drawn up for implementation in light of Jiangsu's actual situation have also assimiliated certain measures which Jiangsu has found effective for tax collection and control.
