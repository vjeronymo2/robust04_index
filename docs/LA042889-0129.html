April 28, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

EARNINGS

Technology Marketing Inc. reported a profit of $1.2 million for the fiscal year ended Feb. 28, contrasted with a loss of $190,018 for the previous year. Revenue rose 21% to $11.1 million from $9.2 million. For the fourth quarter, the Irvine-based company posted net income of $268,281, a 26% gain from the $212,349 recorded for the like period last year. Revenue declined 5% to $2.4 million from $2.5 million. Technology Marketing develops computer and electronics products.
