931231 FT 31 DEC 93 / Bank mortgage lending falls 2.6% Mortgage lending by the main British banking groups was Pounds 1.579bn gross in November, a drop of 2.6 per cent on October. The British Bankers Association said the fall was likely to be seasonal. Gross approvals fell 9 per cent from 29,741 to 27,050 - a 13 per cent fall by value from Pounds 1.446bn to Pounds 1.262bn. The average value of loans approved fell nearly 4 per cent to Pounds 46,700 - but 4 per cent above that for the fourth quarter of 1992.
