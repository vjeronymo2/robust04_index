930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Raglan pays Pounds 7m for 18 shops Raglan Property Trust has contracted to acquire a portfolio of 18 freehold high street shops for some Pounds 7.3m. Vendors are Dunn & Co and Phillips Pension Fund. Current rental income is Pounds 700,000 per year, equating to an initial yield of 9.3 per cent. Raglan will finance part of the purchase by a Pounds 6.3m five-year loan facility from NM Rothschild.
