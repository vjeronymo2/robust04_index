941217 FT 17 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Baris chairman leaves after less than a month Mr Andrew Morton has resigned as chairman of Baris Holdings, the drylining and fire protection systems group, after less than a month. Mr John Foley has become chairman and chief executive. Mr Morton's resignation, due to the 'increasing time requirements' of the other companies of which he is chairman, came as Baris reported an interim pre-tax loss of Pounds 1.29m compared with a profit of Pounds 143,000 last time. Sales for the six months to September 30 were 33 per cent down at Pounds 5.43m (Pounds 8.14m). This half's deficit included a Pounds 1.1m exceptional item relating to costs and write-offs as a result of withdrawal from the German market. Losses per share came out at 20.5p (0.1p earnings).
