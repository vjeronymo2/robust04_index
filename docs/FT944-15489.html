941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Geared Income Geared Income Investment Trust net asset value was 98.89p per share at September 30, compared with 97.71p a year earlier. Net revenue for the six month period slipped to Pounds 778,519 (Pounds 901,497) for earnings per share of 3.42p (4.1p). An unchanged second quarterly dividend of 1.75p is declared.
