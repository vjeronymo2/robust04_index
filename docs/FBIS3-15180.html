Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Mikhail Shevtsov] [Text] Moscow, 2 Mar -- Russian "blue berets" are operating and will continue to operate in the Balkans in strict conformity with the UN Security Council resolutions. This was stated today by Mikhail Kolesnikov, chief of the General Staff of the Russian Armed Forces, at a meeting with Bosnia Serb Leader Radovan Karadzic, who is in Moscow. An ITAR-TASS correspondent was told at the Information and Press Directorate of the Russian Ministry of Defense that during the course of the meeting Radovan Karadzic, on behalf of his people, expressed great gratitude and thanks to the Russian servicemen who are fulfilling their peacekeeping mission in the Balkans.
