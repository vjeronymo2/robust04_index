BFN [Text] Russian First Deputy Prime Minister Oleg Soskovets and the delegation accompanying him arrived in Damascus at noon today on a three-day official visit to Syria. He was received at Damascus International Airport by Dr. Salim Yasin, deputy prime minister for economic affairs; Dr. Muhammad al-'Imadi, minister of economy and foreign trade; several Foreign Ministry officials; Aleksandr Zotov, the Russian ambassador in Damascus; and the Embassy staff. In a statement to the SYRIAN ARAB NEWS AGENCY, SANA, correspondent, Soskovets expressed his happiness over his visit to Syria, and said: My visit to Syria is linked to President Yeltsin's decision to continue and promote economic and political relations with Syria. He added: Our country has long-standing relations with Syria, and now that it has become an independent and sovereign state, we want to promote and expand previous relations and cooperation between the two countries.
