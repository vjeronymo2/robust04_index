Under the Rehab Plan, each ELIC contract holder was permitted to elect between (1) Opting in to the Rehab Plan, in which case Aurora would assume the ELIC contract, or (2) opting out of the Rehab Plan, in which case a cash settlement would be paid in exchange for the ELIC contract.
