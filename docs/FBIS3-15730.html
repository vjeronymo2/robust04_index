Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Sergey Trofimov] [Text] Ulan-Ude March 9 TASS -- Russia's East Siberian republic of Buryatia is from now on "a sovereign democratic law-governed state within the Russian Federation," according to its new Constitution, which came in force on Wednesday to replace the previous Fundamental Law adopted in 1978. The republic's Constitution says relations with the federal centre must be built on the basis of agreements between federal authorities and the local government. The republic is to have its own president at the head of the executive power and the popular Hural (parliament) as the main body of representative authority. The first presidential and parliamentary elections will be held in March 1994.
