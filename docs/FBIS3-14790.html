Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS Correspondent Valery Shanyev] [Text] Vladikavkaz February 27 TASS -- "The implementation of the message by Russian President Boris Yeltsin aiming to strengthen power at all levels is designed to speed up economic reforms in Russia," North Ossetian President Askharbek Galazov told ITAR-TASS upon returning from Moscow. "The most important thing now is to develop a mechanism for implementing this message," Galazov stressed. "The president counts on the Russian Constitution. Boris Yeltsin has confirmed that the state's unity and integrity can be preserved on the basis of Russia's federated arrangement," he said. The president shared gave his assessment to the activities of the parliamentary commission in the state of emergency zone in North Ossetia and Ingushetia. The North Ossetian and Ingush presidents are capable of settling their differences without any mediation, he emphasised. They can solve all complicated issues by themselves. "A negotiating process between the two republics has got underway, it should be continued," Galazov said. Commenting on the results of the Zheleznovodsk meeting of North Caucasian leader with the participation of the chief of the Russian president's staff, Galazov described it as "another attempt to settle relations between North Ossetia and Ingushetia by peaceful means." According to him, "North Ossetia is ready to work consistently for establishing normal relations with all its neighbours. I insist that the problem of refugees cannot be solved by use of force. The final word is to be said by reconciliation commissions," he emphasised.
