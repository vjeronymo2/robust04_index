940912 FT 12 SEP 94 / Observer: Musings Scores of readers have been impressing Observer with their erudition in response to the challenge of naming the Three Graces - Aglaia, Euphrosyne and Thalia. The latter is also the Muse of comedy, so she at least should derive some amusement from the shenanigans connected with the Canova piece. His request for the Pounds 64 garden version of the statue notwithstanding, Jeff Wagland of High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire, who was drawn as the winner, receives instead a small libation.
