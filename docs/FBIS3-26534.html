Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, Feburary 9 (XINHUA) -- Deng Xiaoping appeared at a gathering here this afternoon, exchanging Spring Festival greetings with local officials and retirees. This is the seventh successive year Deng spends the traditional Spring Festival in Shanghai. Deng, who is now 89 and in good health, inspected Shanghai's Pudong New Area and ascended the newly built Yangpu Bridge on December 13 last year.
