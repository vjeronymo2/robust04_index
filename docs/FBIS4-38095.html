BFN [Text] The country's Supreme National Security Council met this evening and stressed the importance of law enforcement officials continuing their activities toward achieving the quickest possible identification and capture of the criminals behind the heart-rending incident at the blessed shrine of Imam Reza -- peace be upon Him -- in keeping with the guidance issued by the eminent leader. The session was attended by President Hashemi-Rafsanjani and by members and high-ranking military commanders. The various dimensions of the recent horrific crime committed by the Mojahedin-e Khalq Organization at the blessed shrine of the eighth imam -- peace be upon Him -- were examined, and law enforcement and security officials briefed the council members in this regard. The council praised the efforts made by security officials and stressed the importance of pursuing and intensifying these efforts.
