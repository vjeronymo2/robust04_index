BFN [Text] Philippine National Police [PNP] Chief Ricaredo Sarmiento has confirmed the presence of Abu Sayyaf members in Metro Manila. He said the PNP suspects the terrorists are recruiting new members from Muslim communities in the metropolis. Sarmiento said security measures have been taken by the police, who fear that besides their recruitment mission the Abu Sayyaf men may carry out sabotage and other terroristic acts as they have been doing in Mindanao. PNP intelligence agents have reported sighting the extremists in the Muslim center in Quiapo and other Muslim communities in Taguig and Tandang Sora in Quezon City.
