930311 FT 11 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Campbell's buys Fray Bentos Campbell's UK, part of the Campbell Soup Company, the international food corporation, is to acquire Fray Bentos, the canned meat business, part of Brooke Bond Foods. The acquisition will mean the closure of the Hackney, east London, factory of Fray Bentos, and the loss of some 270 jobs. Under the sale agreement Campbell's will buy the Fray Bentos brand and transfer production to its existing King's Lynn factory.
