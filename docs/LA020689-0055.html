February 6, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Darrin Van Horn became the new International Boxing Federation junior middleweight champion, overpowering defending title holder Robert Hines to earn a 12-round unanimous decision at Atlantic City, N.J.

Van Horn, a 20-year-old junior at the University of Kentucky, used an effective defense and sharp counterpunching to beat Hines, 27, a Philadelphia fighter.

Van Horn, 153 1/4, is now 39-0 with 24 knockouts. Hines is 24-2-2 with 16 knockouts. his first
