910427 FT 27 APR 91 / UK Company News: Losses deepen to Pounds 0.2m at Ifico Ifico, the insurance broker, saw its pre-tax losses deepen from Pounds 51,000 to Pounds 244,000 in the six months to December 31. Losses per share rose from 0.02p to 0.09p and again there is no interim dividend. The company said that the severe downturn in the motor industry and the extreme winter conditions affected the performance of MBIA, the extended motor warranty division. A management buy-out offer has been received for FMW Group.
