BFN [By UKRINFORM correspondent Mikhail Stestyuk for TASS] [Text] Sevastopol June 20 TASS -- Ships of the Black Sea Fleet have successfully completed a peace-keeping mission in western Georgia. In this connection, the press centre of the Black Sea Fleet reported that Georgian leader Eduard Shevardnadze had gone aboard the "Tsezar Kunikov" paratrooper ship to watch show landing exercises performed by Black Sea Fleet marines jointly with Russian and Georgian servicemen. In a telegram addressed to the commander of the Black Sea Fleet Eduard Baltin Shevardnadze thanked naval officers for their aid in the transportation of civil cargoes and assistance rendered to Georgian troops in the defence of Georgian state borders.
