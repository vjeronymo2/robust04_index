January 14, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

TRACING THE DIRECTIONS OF GAY THEATER

Ray Loynd's Jan. 7 article asks where does gay theater go from here. The answer is: In all directions, because of its diversity.

Gay theater is liberating for all involved. It creates a supportive community for talented gay and lesbian playwrights, performers and theater folk and straights who share our dreams.

Gay theater is where a 50-year-old lesbian can see two women embrace on stage for the first exciting time. It is survival, anger, spirit, gentleness, magic and love.

It will be necessary until my lover and I can walk arm-in-arm down any street in Los Angeles without fear.

BILL KAISER, The Purple Circuit Hotline, Los Angeles

The Purple Circuit Hotline is a public-information service for gay and lesbian plays.
