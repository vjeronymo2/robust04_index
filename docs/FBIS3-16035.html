Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk has issued a decree on strengthening the economic principles of cities' self-government. The president has decreed that property that secures the population's general municipal and socioeconomic needs should be included in the cities' municipal ownership, and has defined the sectors where the property is located. The Cabinet of Ministers has been instructed to compile, within a two month period, a list of facilities that are in general state ownership and are subject to being transferred to the ownership of cities.
