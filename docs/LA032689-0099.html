March 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

TALBOT THEOLOGY SCHOOL RECEIVES $25,000 GRANT

Talbot School of Theology at Biola University has received a $25,000 grant from Lilly Endowment Inc., a private charitable foundation, to be used for faculty scholastic development.

Most of the money will be spent on faculty research grants, graduate student assistantships, increased secretarial help and computer training, said W. Bingham Hunter, seminary dean. He said this is the first such grant the school has received.
