940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Druck expands with Fl 8.2m acquisition Druck Holdings has acquired Unomat Instrumenten, based in Nieuwegein, Netherlands, and Unomat Inc, of Delaware, US, for a total consideration of about Fl 8.2m (Pounds 3m cash). Combined net assets of the two companies is about Fl 3.1m, including Fl 1.2m cash.
