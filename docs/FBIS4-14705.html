BFN [Text] The Defense Ministry press service reports that Abkhaz separatists have shelled Koki, a village on the left bank of the Enguri river, from a tank, and also launched an attack on Georgian positions near Khurcha. The attack, which was preceded by artillery shelling, was carried out by a large enemy unit using a tank and three APC's. The enemy was repelled by Defense Ministry units. There are no victims on our side. A dam at the Enguri hydroelectric power station was damaged yesterday, Electricity supplies to Zugdidi rayon have now been restored, however.
