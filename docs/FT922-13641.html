920410 FT 10 APR 92 / International Company News: Profits setback at Power Corp POWER CORP of Canada, the holding company of financier Mr Paul Desmairais, reported net profit of CDollars 136.3m (USDollars 114.5m) or CDollars 1.02 a share for 1991, against 1990's CDollars 186.3m or CDollars 1.40 a share. Power Corp attributed the drop to lower income from short-term investments and a decrease in earnings of subsidiaries and affiliates. Fourth-quarter profit equalled 25 cents a share, against 26 cents.
