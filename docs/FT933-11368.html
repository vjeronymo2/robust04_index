930729 FT 29 JUL 93 / Danish unemployment at record The Danish Bureau of Statistics yesterday published figures showing unemployment reaching a record level, writes Hilary Barnes in Copenhagen. Seasonally-adjusted unemployment reached 12.3 per cent in June, up from 12.2 per cent in May and 11 per cent in June last year. High short-term interest rates, imposed to counter speculative attacks on the krone, have stifled economic recovery and depressed industrial output, exports and imports.
