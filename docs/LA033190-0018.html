March 31, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

CRISIS OVER LITHUANIAN INDEPENDENCE

Headlines read: "Gorbachev Demands Lithuanians Surrender Personal Guns." Why would he do such a thing? Maybe he doesn't trust honest, law-abiding citizens to be free.

And in the United States, certain politicians demand Americans surrender private guns (a little at a time: handguns, "assault" guns, semi-automatics, etc). Don't they trust honest, law-abiding citizens either? Wake up, America. If you don't protect each of your freedoms, you could lose them all.

PAUL MYERBERG

Burbank
