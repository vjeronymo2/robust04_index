August 22, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Saddam's Life Story: Coming soon to your neighborhood video store, the Saddam Hussein story. On Sept. 14, Strand VCI Entertainment will release "Saddam Hussein -- Defying the World." The 30-minute documentary about the rise of the Iraqi leader sells for $9.98, and includes home-video footage of the Iraqi invasion into Kuwait as an added feature. BETH KLEID
