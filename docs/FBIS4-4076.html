BFN [Text] Guangzhou, April 27 (CNS)--Guangzhou, capital of Guangdong Province, made sound achievements in fighting against fake and inferior-quality commodities in the first quarter this year with 203 cases of this kind being investigated and 136 of them being dealt with fines totalling RMB 1.11 million [renminbi]. Relevant departments in the municipality have strengthened their efforts through their concerted coordination in cracking down on dens producing and selling such commodities. Some 3 000 policemen and personnel from supervision and management departments have been involved in the campaign with a variety of goods with a face value of RMB 1.36 million including foodstuffs, cigarettes, wine, garments, household electrical appliances and compact discs being confiscated while on eight occasions destruction of such commodities have been held publicly and 18 exhibitions of such commodities have been staged. In addition, nearly 30 000 leaflets have been printed and distributed, greatly promoting the crack down.
