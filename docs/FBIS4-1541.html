BFN [From the "News" program] [Text] To support efforts to carry out the anticorruption struggle in depth, the China (Fang Zheng) Publishing House has published the Handbook on Anticorruption Policy, Laws, and Regulations. The book was compiled by the Office of Law and Regulations of the Central Discipline Inspection Commission; relevant anticorruption policies, laws, and regulations in six categories are included in the book. Cao Qingze, deputy secretary of the Central Discipline Inspection Commission and minister of supervision, wrote the book's foreword.
