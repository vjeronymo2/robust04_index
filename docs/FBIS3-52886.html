Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A top leader of the Communist Party of China, Mr. Wen Jiabao, has said that Beijing attaches utmost importance to develop friendly ties with India on the basis of principles of pancasila [five principles of peaceful coexistence]. During his meeting with the vice president, Mr. K.R. Narayanan, in New Delhi today, Mr. Wen said China will continue on the path of socialist market economic system and concentrate on economic development. The visiting dignitary expressed the desire that India and China should maintain long-term, durable, and friendly ties. Mr. Narayanan expressed satisfaction at the good progress achieved in the cooperation program in scientific, cultural, and other fields. He, however, underlined the need to import [as heard] a greater economic content to bilateral relations for further strengthening the very ancient and close relations.
