Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] We now have more details on the downing of the Serbian fighter planes. The NATO fighter planes that downed the Serbian planes took off from Aviano. The two F-16s took off at 0530 and returned to the Italian base at 0600. According to information we have, six missiles were fired against the downed planes. One of the NATO F-16 planes downed three planes and the other downed the fourth one.
