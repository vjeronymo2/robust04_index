National Institutes of Health National Cancer Institute; Meeting of the Subcommittee To Evaluate the National Cancer Program, National Cancer Advisory Board Pursuant to Public Law 92&hyph;463, notice is hereby given of the meeting of the Subcommittee to Evaluate the National Cancer Program, National Cancer Advisory Board, National Cancer Institute, National Institutes of Health on April 27&hyph;29, 1994 at the Washington Duke Inn, 3001 Cameron Boulevard, Durham, NC 27706.
