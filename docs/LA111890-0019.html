IN CELEBRATION OF BACKBONE TRAIL MONTH

COMPLETION OF the 65-mile-long Backbone Trail along the spine of the Santa Monica Mountains is a longtime dream of local hikers.

About two-thirds of the trail -- which will ultimately link Pacific Palisades to Pt. Mugu -- is finished, and November has been named Backbone Trail month. Celebrations include guided hikes along the nature paths, scenic overlooks and trails leading through the diverse ecosystems. JOHN MCKINNEY
