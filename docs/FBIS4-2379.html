BFN [Text] Beijing, April 9 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing met with Kon Tsu Koon [spelling of name as received], chief minister of Malaysian Penang State, and his party here this afternoon. Kon and his party are here on a visit to China as guests of Chinese Ministry of Foreign Trade and Economic Cooperation (MOFTEC). Vice-Minister Li Guohua of MOFTEC was present on the occasion.
