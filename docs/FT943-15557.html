940708 FT 08 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Allied-Lyons plans sales Allied-Lyons, the drinks and retailing group, said yesterday that further disposals of its food manufacturing businesses were being actively explored. The group, which in March paid Pounds 739m for control of Pedro Domecq Group, the Spanish drinks producer, wants to focus on its drinks and retailing businesses. The food manufacturing division, which last year had sales of about Pounds 1bn, makes Tetley teabags and also includes a European bakery business and an ingredients supplier in North America. Yesterday's annual meeting received shareholders' approval for a name change to Allied Domecq.
