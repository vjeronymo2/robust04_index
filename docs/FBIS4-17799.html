BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsov] [Text] Moscow, 27 Apr -- After a State Duma delegation came back from the site of the killing of Duma deputy Andrey Ayzderdzis, chairman of the lower chamber Ivan Rybkin announced a closed sitting of the Duma. At the start of the sitting deputies voted to ask Russian Government chairman Viktor Chernomyrdin and leaders of the country's law enforcement bodies to attend.
