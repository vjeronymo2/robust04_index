BFN [Excerpt] Paris, April 25 (AFP) -- France welcomed as a "positive step" Monday [25 April] the release of Chinese dissident Wang Juntao who was allowed to travel to New York over the weekend to seek medical treatment for severe hepatitis. Foreign ministry spokesman Richard Duque said "we consider this release as a positive step and hope it will be followed by others." Wang, 35, a journalist, was sentenced to 13 years in prison in 1991 for his role in China's pro-democracy movement. [passage omitted]
