BFN [Text] Macao, May 17 (XINHUA) -- The Macao Government has decided to set up a cultural fund to advance cultural activities in the territory, according the latest government bulletin. Concerned regulations will become effective as from today. The bulletin says that the cultural fund is aimed at supporting all cultural activities sponsored and organized by the cultural institution of the Macao Government. The cultural fund will be operated by an administrative management committee, which consists of president, vice-president, administrative assistant and cultural training officials of the cultural institution of the Macao Government, as well as an official from the local government finance department, the bulletin says. The bulletin stipulates that the cultural fund will be used in organizing Macao International Music Festival, Macao Arts Festival, publications of Macao cultural institution, and in protecting Macao's cultural relics and commemorative properties.
