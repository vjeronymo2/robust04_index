930211 FT 11 FEB 93 / International Company News: TRW registers improvement TRW, the US high technology group, yesterday reported fourth-quarter earnings of Dollars 59m, or 94 cents a share, against a Dollars 231m loss or Dollars 3.76 last year, when it took a restructuring charge. Excluding charges, the 1991 quarter's earnings were Dollars 25m. Full-year earnings totalled Dollars 194m, or Dollars 3.09 a share, before accounting changes. They totalled Dollars 116m, or Dollars 1.88 a share in 1991, excluding restructuring charges.
