BFN [Text] The deployment of military trucks, Serb police punitive expeditions, and raids and harassments are still being carried out in various parts of Kosova. In the Kodra e Trimave Quarter in Prishtina at approximately 1600 [1400 GMT] yesterday, a convoy of military vehicles from Podujeve paraded along the main street of this quarter and then headed toward the center of Prishtina. The convoy was made up of ambulances and armored military cars. Meanwhile, a police expedition with four [word indistinct], two armored cars, seven police cars, two Land Rovers, and one excavator entered the Korish village in Prizren and reeked havoc among the peasants. This police expedition raided several homes and harassed Ajet Mucaj and his mother Elmaze. They also opened holes [vrima] in Mucaj's yard under the pretext of searching for weapons. The Kosova Information Center reports that raids and maltreatments have been reported in Podujeve, Mitrovice, Skenderaj, Kline, and Lipjan.
