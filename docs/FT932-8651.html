930518 FT 18 MAY 93 / Observer: Mealy-mouthed Will Sir Terence Conran's upmarket eaterie, Quaglino's, be knocked out of its highly successful orbit by the upstart Planet Hollywood? Robert Earl, the 41-year-old burger-master behind the star-studded new arrival, cites Quaglino's estimated Pounds 12m annual turnover as a target he plans to beat by at least Pounds 3m. But 61-year-old Sir Terence remains relaxed: 'Judging by this week's bookings, the stars who have come to open Planet Hollywood will come to sit down and eat at Quaglino's' . . . if they can get a table that is.
