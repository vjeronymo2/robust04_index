930407 FT 07 APR 93 / UK Company News: Buckingham bid goes unconditional Purlieus, a Jersey-based company controlled by Jemma Trust and Naaz Holdings, holds or has valid acceptances for 64.1m ordinary shares in Buckingham International, representing 53.1 per cent of the ordinary capital of the hotels and nursing homes group. The offer has been declared unconditional in all respects and, along with the loan stock offer, for which no acceptances have been received, has been extended to next Tuesday.
