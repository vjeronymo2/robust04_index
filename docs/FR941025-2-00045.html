Capozzi Bros. Fuel Co. Bridgeport, CT, Lee&hyph;0143, Reporting Requirements Capozzi Bros. Fuel Co. filed an Application for Exception from the Energy Information Administration (EIA) requirement that it file Form EIA&hyph;782B, the ``Resellers'/Retailers' Monthly Petroleum Product Sales Report.'' In considering this request, the DOE found that the firm was not suffering a gross inequity, a serious hardship or an unfair distribution of burdens. Accordingly, on August 16, 1994, the DOE issued a Proposed Decision and Order determining that the exception request should be denied.
