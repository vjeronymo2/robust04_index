Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The National Resistance Council [NRC] has adjourned indefinitely after failing to form a quorum. At 2:30 P.M. this afternoon, the vice chairman of the NRC, Alhaji Moses Kigongo, called for an adjournment of 15 minutes to allow for more NRC members to come and form a quorum, as there were only 50 members. After 25 minutes of waiting, the house was called to order, only to find that there was still no quorum. The house was set to deliberate on the government white paper on education and the public accounts committee report.
