BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpt] The Turkish Grand National Assembly approved the motion submitted by the Prime Minister's Office on the extension of the state of emergency in 10 provinces for another four months. The Assembly, which convened under the chairmanship of Mustafa Kalemli, voted to extend the state of emergency, which is currently in force in Batman, Bingol, Bitlis, Diyarbakir, Hakkari, Mardin, Siirt, Sirnak, Tunceli, and Van, for another four months as of 19 July.
