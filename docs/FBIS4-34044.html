BFN [Text] St. Lucia, like all the other countries in the region, continues to fight off the drug menace. As is customary, large quantities of drugs confiscated are set to the torch, hoping that this and the fines and imprisonment terms can act as a deterrent for both perpetrators and would-be felons. Last month, members of the Royal St. Lucia Police Force, accompanied by prison inmates, took a large quantity of confiscated drugs to the dump site and set it alight. The stash, comprising marijuana, cocaine, and crack, was estimated at over ECU250,000.
