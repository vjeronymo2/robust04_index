920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / Inner-city project wins new funds THE NATIONAL Trust's only inner-city project, aimed at involving people in a deprived area of Newcastle upon Tyne with the trust and developing interest in the countryside, has received new funding for the next three years. The initiative has been supported since 1987 by the Rank Organisation, the leisure group. Mr Angus Stirling, National Trust director-general, will today announce that the Baring Foundation, the Sir James Knott Trust and the Barbour Trust have agreed to provide a total of Pounds 75,000.
