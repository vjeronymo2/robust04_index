National Institutes of Health National Institute on Aging; Cancellation of Meeting Notice is hereby given of the cancellation of the meeting of the Biological and Clinical Aging Review Subcommittee A, National Institute on Aging, October 3, 1994, from 8:30 a.m. to adjournment, at the Holiday Inn Crowne Plaza, 1750 Rockville Pike, Rockville, Maryland, which was published in the Federal Register on September 21, 1994, Vol. 59, page 48441. The meeting was cancelled because the majority of the members were not available to meet to conduct the committee's business. (Catalog of Federal Domestic Assistance Program No. 93.866, Aging Research, National Institutes of Health.) Dated: September 28, 1994. Susan K. Feldman, Committee Management Officer, NIH.
