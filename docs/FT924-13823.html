921013 FT 13 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Paramount rises 14% WITH 165 licensed houses under its management, principally in the west midlands, north Wales and north west of England, Paramount escaped the worst of the recession to produce pre-tax profits up 14 per cent to Pounds 403,000 in the year to May 31, compared with Pounds 355,000. Earnings per share for this USM-quoted company, however, declined from 0.72p to 0.56p, the equity having been increased by shares issued for acquisitions. The company is proposing to pay an initial dividend of 0.1p. Turnover increased from Pounds 5.54m to Pounds 7.64m.
