940802 FT 02 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Hiscox Select Insurance Fund Hiscox Select Insurance Fund, an investment trust supporting underwriting at Lloyd's, reported a net asset value of 92.3p per share at July 26. Profits for the interim period, from October 11 1993 to June 30 1994, stood at Pounds 808,000 pre-tax and Pounds 543,000 after tax, giving earnings per share of 1.81p. There is an interim dividend of 1.5p.
