930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / International Company News: Marriott Corporation Marriott Corporation, the US hotels group, said yesterday a Delaware chancery court judge had denied a request by a group of its preferred shareholders for an injunction to stop the company's annual meeting, scheduled for July 23, Agencies report. The investors, who include UBS Securities and Harvard College, had opposed Marriott's revised plan to split the company into two separately-quoted companies - one containing the bulk of its property assets and much of the group's debts, and the other comprising the financially-healthy hotel management operations.
