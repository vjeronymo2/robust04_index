BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan, 29 Apr (ITAR-TASS) -- From 1700 on Thursday until late at night, Azerbaijani units periodically shelled the Armenian border villages of Vagan in Krasnoselskiy rayon, Chinari in Taushskiy rayon, and Sevkar and Berkaber in Idzhevanskiy rayon using all types of weapons. The ITAR-TASS correspondent learned this today from the Armenian defense ministry's press center. According to the same source, at the same time, on Thursday evening, the border village of Shagat in Sisianskiy rayon in the southeast of the republic came under howitzer and automatic fire from the territory of the Nakhichevan Autonomous Republic. In all cases, the Armenian side did not return fire and there were no casualties, the Armenian Ministry of Defense stressed.
