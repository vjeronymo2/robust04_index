Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Mikhail Kolesnichenko] [Text] New York February 9 TASS -- Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Vitaliy Churkin called on the president of Serbia to promote an urgent lifting of the blockade of Sarajevo. In a CNN interview, Churkin said he had a serious talk with Slobodan Milosevic, telling him that "we cannot put up with such a situation. The Sarajevo blockade should be lifted. And this must be done now". At the same time CNN pointed out that the Russian deputy foreign minister said air strikes on Serbian troop positions in Bosnia-Herzegovina might "cast a shadow on Russia's relations with western countries."
