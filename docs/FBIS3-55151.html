Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] In the first days of the new year, Andrey Nikolayev, commander of the Russian Federation's border troops, met President of Azerbaijan Geydar Aliyev in Baku and visited the Council of Ministers of the Republic of Dagestan. The trip is linked to the very difficult issues of guarding CIS state borders and of the security of internal borders and those with non-CIS states. [Begin recording] [Andrey Nikolayev interviewed aboard plane, in flight] For two years there have been no serious, substantial contacts with Azerbaijan, with our colleagues in Azerbaijan, on the border issue. Much has been lost, but luckily not everything and an important result of the meeting in Azerbaijan was the fact that our colleagues in Azerbaijan and the president of Azerbaijan showed thorough understanding of the problems outlined by the Russian side -- Azerbaijan's entry into the CIS and joining the collective security treaty. Russia's goodwill and wish to provide for the national interests of Azerbaijan and Russia, and to protect the interests of other CIS states where Azerbaijan and Russia are concerned, create the conditions for settling the questions we are faced with. [end recording]
