930501 FT 01 MAY 93 / Yucatan election to go ahead Mexico's governing party has agreed to allow scheduled gubernatorial elections in the state of Yucatan to go ahead in November, writes Damian Fraser in Mexico City. Last week the Yucatan Congress, controlled by Mexico's ruling Institutional Revolutionary party, created a furore by changing the constitution to postpone elections for two years, which would have allowed it to pick an interim governor.
