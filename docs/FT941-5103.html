940308 FT 08 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Perpetual Japanese Investment Trust PERPETUAL JAPANESE Investment Trust improved its net asset from 96.25p at launch in July 1993 to 101.3p at January 31, an increase of 5.25 per cent, compared with a sterling adjusted rise of 3.06 per cent in the FT-A World Japan Index. Net revenue for the period came to Pounds 97,000 for earnings per share of 0.03p.
