920814 FT 14 AUG 92 / World News in Brief: London explosives haul Police confirmed they had seized a 'very large quantity' of explosives, bomb-making equipment and arms in their continuing operation against IRA suspects in London. Several people were detained under the Prevention of Terrorism Act after raids over the past few days. The police operation followed a tip-off from a man who sold a van to a cash buyer he thought was acting suspiciously.
