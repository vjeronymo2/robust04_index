Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council and secretary general of the Central Committee of the Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], received in Aden today brother Taysir Qubba'ah, deputy speaker of the Palestine National Council [PNC] and member of the Political Bureau of the Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine [PFLP], who is currently visiting our country. During the meeting, they discussed the brotherly relations between the Yemeni and Palestinian revolutions and exchanged views on developments in both the Yemeni and Palestinian arenas. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council, reaffirmed our country's support for the Palestinian Arab people in their struggle to achieve their full legitimate rights. He also touched on developments in the national arena, referring to efforts to overcome current difficulties and complications. He stressed that the YSP is determined to search for peaceful solutions, to adopt a serious and responsible dialogue to resolve all differences, and to overcome the challenges that face the march of unity and democracy and the establishment of a modern Yemeni state. For his part, brother Taysir Qubba'ah conveyed the greetings of PFLP Secretary General George Habash to his brother YSP Secretary General 'Ali Salim al-Bid. Qubba'ah also conveyed congratulations on the results of the Political Forces Dialogue Committee, particularly the accord document, which, he said, is considered a great national achievement aimed at finding practical solutions to the current crisis in Yemen. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid then asked Qubba'ah to convey his greetings to his brother struggler George Habash, to wish him good health and happiness, and to express the hope that the current close relations between the Yemeni and Palestinian revolutions will witness further growth.
