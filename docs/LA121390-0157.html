December 13, 1990, Thursday, Southland Edition

NEWSWIRE: MISCELLANY

Two-time Olympian Terry Schroeder, who had been retired for two years, will lead the U.S. men's national water polo team into its third and final practice game against Cuba tonight at 7 at the Belmont Plaza Olympic Pool in Long Beach. The United States, silver medalists in the 1984 and 1988 Olympics, is preparing for the World Championships Jan. 3-13 in Perth, Australia.
