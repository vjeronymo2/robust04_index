921020 FT 20 OCT 92 / World News In Brief: Koskotas on trial Jailed banker George Koskotas, 40, central figure in a scandal that helped bring down Greece's socialist government in 1989, went on trial in Athens on charges concerning a Dollars 200m (Pounds 116m) embezzlement at his former private Bank of Crete. He is serving five years for forgery.
