940211 FT 11 FEB 94 / Clinton doubles aid to Ukraine President Clinton yesterday promised to virtually double the Dollars 175m in aid it has provided so far to help Ukraine dismantle its nuclear weapons, Reuter reports from Washington. In a meeting with Ukrainian-American leaders, Mr Clinton said he would make the new aid available when Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk visits Washington next month.
