Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] New Delhi, March 29 (OANA-BERNAMA) -- President Suharto of Indonesia supports the concept of the East Asia Growth Area (EAGA) encompassing Indonesia, the Philippines, Brunei and Malaysia Malaysian Prime Minister Dr. Mahathir Mohamed said Tuesday [29 March]. Mahathir said he explained the concept to Suharto during bilateral talks here Monday. Both are here to attend the three-day fourth summit of the Group of 15 developing countries which began Monday. Dr. Mahathir said he informed Suharto that Philippine President Fidel Ramos had also agreed to the concept, initiated by Malaysia for the mutual benefit of the countries involved. Dr. Mahathir and Suharto also discussed the need to reduce airport landing fees to promote cross border travel between the two countries. Also discussed was the question of greater air links among South countries to promote better contacts between their peoples.
