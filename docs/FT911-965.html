910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Net assets slip at Portugal Fund Portugal Fund reported a drop in net asset value to Dollars 6.08 per share at February 28 1991, against Dollars 6.73 last time. Total revenue for the six months to end-February dropped from Pounds 174,041 to Pounds 98,480, and after administrative expenses of Pounds 251,962 (Pounds 289,018), net losses came out at Pounds 153,482, against Pounds 114,917. Directors said the outlook for the Portuguese equity market had improved as a result of the more favourable environment for equities worldwide. Also, they expected the continued privatisation programme and the forthcoming capital markets reforms to give an added boost to market performance.
