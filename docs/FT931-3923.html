930313 FT 13 MAR 93 / Japan-Brazil accord signed An accord was signed yesterday for Japan's first financing of environmental projects in Brazil, for a total of Dollars 840m, through its Overseas Economic Co-operation Fund, Christina Lamb reports from Rio de Janeiro. The money is destined for a co-financing project with the Interamerican Development Bank for cleaning up the Rio bay, as well as the depollution of the Tiete river, which runs through Sao Paulo, and the construction of a recycling unit in Sao Paulo.
