Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] According to the daily newspaper, LE NOUVELISTE, changes have been made within the Haitian Armed Forces. The newspaper notes that several transfers were recently effected within the Haitian Armed Forces, including changes at the heads of two units: the Aviation Corps, in which Colonel Wilson Eustache was replaced by Lieutenant-Colonel Serge Bourdeau, who has been promoted to the rank of colonel. Second, the Corps of Engineers where Lt.-Col. Rodrigue Creve-Coeur, promoted to colonel, succeeds Col. Guy Duchemin, who has been pensioned off. Col. Wilson Eustache has been allegedly called to the personal general staff of the Army commander in chief.
