May 16, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Republican Party Chairman Lee Atwater will put on his blues act June 3 with B.B. King at the annual three-day Medgar Evers Mississippi Homecoming in Flora, Miss. "It will be the battle of the guitars with B.B. and Lee," says Fayette Mayor Charles Evers, brother of the slain civil rights leader. Atwater and King have jammed together before in Washington. ALEENE MacMINN
