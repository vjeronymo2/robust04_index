930901 FT 01 SEP 93 / UK Company News: German purchase for Filofax Filofax Group, the USM-quoted personal organiser company, has completed the DM2m (Pounds 800,000) acquisition of the Filofax distribution business of Waltraud Bethge, its former German distributor. In 1992 the business achieved pre-tax profits of DM640,000 on sales of DM2.4m.
