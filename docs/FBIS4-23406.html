BFN [Text] A communique from the World Food Program [WFP] received at our news desk today reveals that eight WFP workers were evacuated from Cuito this morning because of intense clashes there. Also evacuated from Cuito were workers from the Doctors Without Borders organization of Belgium and Oxfam humanitarian organization. The communique also says that the United Nations continues to negotiate with the government and the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola with a view to resuming humanitarian flights soon to Huambo, Cuito, Malanje, and Uige.
