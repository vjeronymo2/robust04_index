Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Petrovac, Srbobran, 6 Feb (TANJUG) -- A 120mm mortar attack was launched on the Srbobran [Donji Vakuf] town center this afternoon from Muslim strongholds north of Bugojno. A general alert was sounded in the town and people were in their shelters and basements. [passage omitted] Members of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army 5th Corps today used all available weapons to fiercely target the republic of Srpska army position's along the Bihac front from their strongholds in the Una River valley, Serbian military sources said. According to [Serbian] military sources, two soldiers of the 2nd [Bosnian] Krajina Corps of the republic of Srpska army were killed and three civilians were lightly wounded in the attacks. The Serbian side was forced to respond and it repelled the Muslim attack successfully. Serbian soldiers have captured Barakovac, the most important strategic point on the Bihac front.
