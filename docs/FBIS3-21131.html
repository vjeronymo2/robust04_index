Language: Japanese Article Type:CSO [Text] Sumitomo Electric Industries Ltd. (Noritaka Kurauchi, president) held a ceremony opening its Haruma Laboratory built at the Haruma Science City in western Hyogo Prefecture. The laboratory, with its superconducting, small-scale ``NIJI-III'' SOR (synchrotron orbit radiation) device, will conduct R&D on precision machining for micromachines as well as such topics as new materials and funtionally gradient materials. The laboratory is built on a two-hectacre industrial site (in Kamigori, Hyogo Prefecture) obtained from Hyogo Prefecture. The total cost of building the lab came to ¥5 billion.
