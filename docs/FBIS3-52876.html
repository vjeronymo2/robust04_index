Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Our correspondent in al-Nabatiyah has reported that the Israelis, who are deployed in Qal'at al-Shaqif, have been raking Mazra'at al-Hamra', Zawtar al-Sharqiyah, and Zawtar al-Gharbiyah since 1300 today with large-caliber machine guns. The Israelis also bombarded Mlita, al-Luwayzah, and Jabal Safi at 1030 today. Meanwhile, Israeli troops raked the areas around Sujud. Israeli warplanes broke the sound barrier over Beirut at 1130. The Islamic Resistance has declared that its fighters have carried out an operation against an Israeli patrol on 'Arqah road. Several members of the patrol were injured. Furthermore, the Amal Movement said that one of its units attacked the Rshaf position, noting that its fighters clashed with the soldiers manning the positions, inflicting losses on them.
