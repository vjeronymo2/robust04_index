BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe May 30 TASS -- Executive Secretary of the Commonwealth of Independent States Ivan Korotchnya has conducted a three-day visit to the Tajik capital of Dushanbe to discuss the expansion of economic, political and military cooperation between the CIS countries and to examine the situation on the border between Tajikistan and Afghanistan. Korotchnya met with Tajikistan leader Emomali Rakhmonov, Prime Minister Abduzhalil Samadov, and other officials of the republic. Tajik Parliamentary Chairman Rakhmalov and Korotchnya visited the 12th post of the Moscow border guard on Sunday. There they laid a wreath of flowers at the obelisk erected in memory of the 22 Russian border guards who were killed in an unequal battle with Tajik opposition forces and mujahideen fighters from Afghanistan on July 12, 1993. The CIS executive secretary departed for Moscow Monday morning.
