BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Kondrashov] [Text] Belgrade, 21 Apr--A parliamentary delegation from the Russian State Duma arrived today in Belgrade. Its aim is to study the present situation in the republics of the former Yugoslavia and inform all the deputies about the results of the study with the subsequent aim of discussing the issue in the parliament. The delegation, which includes representatives of most of the factions, is led by Nikolay Bezborodov, deputy chairman of the State Duma Committee for Defense and Security.
