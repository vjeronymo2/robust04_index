940614 FT 14 JUN 94 / State of the parties: How the nation voted Party line-up with all 87 UK seats declared: Labour 62 (+13), Conservative 18 (-16), Lib Dems 2 (+2), Scottish Nationalists 2 (+1), Others 3 (same). Average turnout: 36.43 per cent. Scotland: the Scottish Nationalists won nearly 33 per cent of the country's vote. The Liberal Democrats are considering an appeal to the electoral court after a Literal Democrat candidate won 10,200 votes in the Devon and East Plymouth constituency. The Lib Dem candidate failed to defeat the defending Tory by 700 votes. Lib Dem leaders believe that thousands voted for the Literal Democrat by mistake
