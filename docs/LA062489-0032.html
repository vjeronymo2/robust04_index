June 24, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

COWBELL CONCERT?

Bill Kohlhaase's June 19 review of the Playboy Jazz Festival was lacking in its coverage. Every artist was mentioned except guitarist Larry Carlton.

I would rather read about Carlton (who gave one of the day's strongest performance) than Bill Cosby's interest in playing the cowbell.

JOHN L. JOANEM

Malibu
