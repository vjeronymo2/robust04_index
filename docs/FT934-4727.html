931202 FT 02 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Mid Kent Mid Kent Holdings, the water supply company, announced an 11 per cent reduction in pre-tax profits from Pounds 4.28m to Pounds 3.8m for the half year to September 30. Turnover rose 5 per cent to Pounds 16.4m (Pounds 15.6m) but the pre-tax result was after a net interest charge of Pounds 644,000 against Pounds 298,000 received. That represented the costs of financing the continuing investment programme. The interim dividend is lifted to 5p (4.75p), payable from earnings per share of 16.7p (19.3p).
