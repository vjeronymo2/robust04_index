BFN [Text] Tokyo, June 17 KYODO -- The Diet will complete enactment of the 73.08 trillion yen fiscal 1994 budget on June 23 when the House of Councillors votes on the legislation, political sources said Friday [17 June] night. The sources said upper house Budget Committee members of both ruling and opposition parties had reached agreement on the schedule for the budget vote. The House of Representatives passed the budget legislation on June 8.
