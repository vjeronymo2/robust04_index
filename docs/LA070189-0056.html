July 1, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

CSUN SIGNS WOMAN SPRINTER

Michelle Barga of Sunnyvale Fremont High has signed a letter of intent to attend Cal State Northridge in the fall on a partial track scholarship.

Barga has a personal best of 56.45 seconds in the 400 meters. She competed in the state championships at Cerritos College in June but did not qualify for the final.
