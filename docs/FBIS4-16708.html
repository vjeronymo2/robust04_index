BFN [Report by Natalya Gorodetskaya under the "Caucasus" rubric: "Ingushetia Urges Introduction of Federal Rule in State of Emergency Zone"] [Excerpt] [Passage omitted] Ingushetia's parliament yesterday discussed the situation in the state of emergency zone and passed a decree "On the Situation in the Zone Affected by the State of Emergency on North Ossetia and Ingushetia Territories and Measures to Uphold Citizens' Rights." The decree says that while Russian Federation laws do not work in the state of emergency zone, the rights of Russian citizens are being trampled, and the Provisional Administration has shown that it is completely incapable of stabilizing the situation. Ingushetia's parliament sees the suspension of local bodies of power of North Ossetia in Prigorodnyy Rayon and the introduction of direct federal rule on this territory as the only way out of the crisis. Ingushetia's parliament has forwarded to the Russian Federation president a special statement to this effect. [passage omitted]
