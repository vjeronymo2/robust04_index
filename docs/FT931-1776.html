930324 FT 24 MAR 93 / Observer: Supply and demand The heritage lobby howled when London's Royal Holloway College sold its famous Turner to California's overly endowed Getty Museum for Pounds 11m. However, the college's entrepreneurial flair in negotiating the highest price paid for a British work of art at auction has clearly impressed one section of the community. Enquiries from prospective students have doubled since the sale a month ago.
