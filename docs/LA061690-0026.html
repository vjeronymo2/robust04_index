June 16, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

WHAT KIND OF ROLE MODEL IS THIS FOR THE CHILDREN?

Nolan Ryan's sixth no-hitter . . . big deal. If he were a real baseball player, he'd have several illegitimate children, a drug/alcohol/gambling problem or a criminal record.

Much has been written about his bionic pitching arm. But what about a man that characterizes what a superstar figure should be -- a polite family man who knows how to stay out of trouble.

LARRY GELFOND

Beverly Hills
