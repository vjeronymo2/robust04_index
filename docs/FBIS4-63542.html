BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Moscow May 21 TASS -- A new Russian negotiator to the talks with the Chechen Republic will be appointed in the near future, Russian Presidential Administration Chief of Staff Sergey Filatov said on Saturday. Filatov made it clear that the removal of Vice-Prime Minister Sergey Shakhray from the post of nationalities minister is connected with the need to find ways to enhance relations between Russia and the Chechen Republic. Shakhray had a firm position at the talks, insisting on early elections in the Chechen Republic and participation of the Chechen opposition in the talks as a pre-condition. Filatov confirmed Russian President Boris Yeltsin's intention to meet with Chechen President Dzhokhar Dudayev in the near future.
