BFN [Excerpt] Ankara, June 21 (A.A.) -- Former Democracy Party (DEP) deputies Sedat Yurtdas and Selim Sadak left parliament premises at 2:05 pm., it was reported on Tuesday [21 June]. Yurtdas and Sadak stayed at parliament premises as Chief Prosecutor Nusret Demiral ordered their detention on Monday. Later the two deputies said they will leave parliament after Acting Parliament Speaker Kamer Genc said that the parliament memberships of Sedat Yurtdas and Selim Sadak will continue until the Constitution Court decision is published in the OFFICIAL GAZETTE [passage omitted].
