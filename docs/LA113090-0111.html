November 30, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

WISH LIST: What do the troops in the Persian Gulf really want from home? . . . According to the county's Navy League, the most-requested items are wet wipes, lip balm, toothbrushes, toothpaste, hand lotion, sunscreen, soap, deodorant, eye drops, foot powder, baby powder, insect repellent, pens, stationery, small games, playing cards, crosswords, comics, magazines, paperbacks, socks, gum, beef jerky, pretzels, licorice, Pop-Tarts, Pringle's chips and Kool-Aid. . . . Call the Navy League at (714) 282-0252 for the nearest drop-off station. Helene Elliott and Danny Sullivan
