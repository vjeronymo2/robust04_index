BFN [Interview with FRG Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel by unidentified correspondent in Vienna on 29 Mar -- recorded] [Excerpts] [Correspondent] The news of the British agreement [to the European Union -- EU -- voting compromise] reached German Foreign Minister Kinkel in Vienna.
