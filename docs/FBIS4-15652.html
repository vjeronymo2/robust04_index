BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vasiliy Belousov] [Text] Murmansk, 9 Apr -- An ITAR-TASS correspondent has just been informed at the Northern Fleet's press center about an incident that is terrible in terms of its cruelty and pointlessness. A serviceman who is serving in a military counter-intelligence subunit of the Federal Counterintelligence Service, killed four Northern Fleet sailors and wounded three last night. The criminal has taken four crew members of a vessel hostage and is now on board the vessel. Representatives of the Fleet's command and of the Federal Counter-Intelligence Service are negotiating with him.
