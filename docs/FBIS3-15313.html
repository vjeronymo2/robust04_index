Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [Passage omitted] After the addresses in the miscellaneous section on the Supreme Soviet agenda, the people's deputies registered themselves. There were 267 deputies at the morning session. Following a short debate, they began to discuss certain articles of the Constitution as well as amendments and supplements. Deputies' questions were answered by republic's Attorney General Vasil Shaladonaw. After a short break, the parliament resumed its work. However, it turned out that the number of people's deputies significantly dropped after the break. Due to this, the session was adjourned until 15 March.
