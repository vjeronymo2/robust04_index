May 27, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

HOLIDAY CLOSINGS

Monday is Memorial Day, a day set aside to remember all of those who have died in wars. The following will be closed in observance of the holiday:

All county, state and federal offices.

All schools, including colleges and universities.

County, state and federal courthouses.

City and county libraries.

Banks and savings and loans.

All city offices.

Mail: All post offices will be closed Monday, and mail deposited in mailboxes on that day will be picked up according to the posted holiday schedule. There will be no mail delivery on Monday.

Trash: There will be no trash pickup by city or county refuse workers on Monday. Trash pickups will be a day late in all areas, with the exception of Fullerton and Orange. Residents in those cities are asked to put out their trash on their regularly scheduled day, except those with Monday pickups, who should put out their trash on Tuesday.

Buses: Orange County Transit District buses will run on the holiday/Sunday schedule on Monday. There will be no dial-a-ride or car-pool services available that day.

Parks: All city- and county-run parks and recreation facilities will be open during regular hours on Monday.
