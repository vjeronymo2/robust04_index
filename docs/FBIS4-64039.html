BFN [Text] A Swiss delegation, headed by Rolf (Hessler), deputy secretary for foreign economic issues, envoy of the Federal Council for Control Over Exports, is visiting Ukraine at the invitation of the Ukrainian Foreign Ministry. The delegation was received by First Deputy Foreign Minister Mykola (Mytarenko). The guests held a number of meetings, in particular with Volodymyr Horbulin, general manager of the National Technical Agency. The sides discussed the issues concerning Ukraine's future access to the system of control over missile technologies [SCMT]. The system currently includes 25 states, whose governments have agreed to adhere to jointly adopted principles and regulations while international transfer of [word indistinct] equipment and technologies, in order to prevent the proliferation of weapons of mass destruction delivery systems. Mr. (Hessler), currently the SCMT head, highly assessed the steps taken by the Ukrainian Government to establish efficient control over the exports of appropriate products and technologies.
