Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, March 25 (KPL) -- Mr. Khamtai Siphandon, prime minister of Laos, yesterday afternoon, met here the visiting minister to the Prime Minister's Office of Thailand, Dr. Sawit Phothiwihok. The Lao prime minister talked to his guest about the (?development of the bilateral) relations of friendship and cooperation. The discussion specifically centered on the implementation of the memorandum of understanding on hydro-electric power generation signed between Laos and Thailand in Vientiane on June 6, 1993. The Lao prime minister expressed his hope that cooperation in various fields between the two countries, such as in energy generation, tourism, and investment, would further be expanded for mutual benefits of both peoples. Accompanying the Thai minister to meet Mr. Khamtai Siphandon was Mr. Phao Bounnaphon, minister to the Prime Minister's Office. Prior to his meeting with the Lao prime minister, Dr. Sawit Phothiwihok called on Mr. Khamphoui Keoboualapha, vice prime minister and president of the Committee for Planning and Cooperation. Dr. Sawit Phothiwihok also had discussions with Mr. Soulivong Dalavong and Mr. Khammon Phonkeo, respectively minister and deputy minister of industry and handicrafts. [passage indistinct]
