Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] French Foreign Minister Alain Juppe met today with Palestinian representatives at Orient House in East Jerusalem. Following the meeting, Juppe told reporters that France would continue to support the peace process. In particular, the foreign minister said, France would contribute funds to the Palestinians, in addition to the 500 million ECU's already pledged by the European Committee. [Begin recording] [Juppe] Yes, we have decided to implement a new protocol of 60 million francs in 1994 for the occupied territories. [end recording] Palestinian leader Faysal al-Husayni, who attended the meeting, told reporters he believed the recent spate of terror attacks could be better handled if the Palestinians controlled the areas. Al-Husayni agreed that support among Palestinians for the peace process had dwindled, but was convinced that things could change. [Al-Husayni] There is less support because nothing is implemented on the ground. In the moment that there will be implementation on the ground, I believe that the Palestinian people will support. The Palestinian people stopped to believe words and sentences; they would like to see something on the ground. [end recording]
