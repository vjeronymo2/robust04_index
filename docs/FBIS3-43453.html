Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] COSATU [Congress of South African Trade Unions] has given a new government five years to implement a reconstruction and development program. COSATU says political freedom for workers in 1994 will mean a vote for democracy, a vote for jobs, a vote for peace and a vote for improved living conditions. However, these objectives will be achieved only through a concrete program which sets out clear priorities, targets and time frames.
