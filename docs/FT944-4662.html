941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Caffyns margins under pressure A difficult market for new cars in the south east, with margins under pressure, led to a fall in interim pre-tax profits of Caffyns, the motor dealer, from Pounds 630,000 to Pounds 336,000. Turnover rose 9 per cent to Pounds 81.4m (Pounds 74.7m) for the half year to September 30. Earnings per share fell from 15.8p to 7.2p, but the interim dividend has been held at 5p.
