BFN [Unattributed report: "Serbian Troops Breach"] [Text] NOVA MAKEDONIJA cites anonymous Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] and police sources on confirmation of reports on an army camp put up by 15 Serbian soldiers near Kriva Palanka, who occupied surrounding peak elevation and strategic points for observation of the Macedonian territory. The same sources also report that the Macedonian troops are enforced on this side of the border and that this "breach" of Serbian soldiers, made into Macedonian territory, is surrounded by Macedonian Army troops, who have an order only to observe for the time being.
