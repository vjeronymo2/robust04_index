BFN [Text] Tirana, April 23 (ATA) -- "The recent detention by the Ministry of Public Order are not and has not been done on basis of nationality but according to their citizenship," the spokesman of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs said yesterday, commenting on the detention for keeping arms without permission, drugs and criminal anticonstitutional activity. The spokesman said that the Ministry of Public Order possesses incontestable proofs and will make them available for any international investigating commission interested in them. "Each Albanian citizen is equal before the law and the intervention of any state for protecting them is unacceptable. Such acts violate the sovereignty of the Albanian state," the spokesman concludes. Some 11 persons have been detained for keeping arms without permission and many criminal anti-constitutional documentations during a control made recently by the Ministry of Public Order. The documentations proves the Greek expansionist intentions against Albania and the Greek flagrant endeavour to Hellenize the Albania's southern territories.
