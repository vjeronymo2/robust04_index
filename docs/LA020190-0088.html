February 1, 1990, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO SPORTS ET CETERA: USD WOMEN GET COACH FROM AZTEC VOLLEYBALL

Susan Hegerle, who for the past four years has been an assistant volleyball coach at San Diego State, will take over the University of San Diego women's program, the school announced Wednesday.

Hegerle will replace John Martin, who had been the only coach in the 15-year history of the USD women's program.

He will now work as USD's director of recreation and facilities.

While at SDSU, Hegerle and Coach Rudy Suwara guided the Aztecs to three NCAA Western Regionals in four years.

Hegerle graduated from SDSU in 1984. In her four years of varsity volleyball there, the Aztecs had a 150-41 record. She was a member of the U.S. Volleyball Assn. national championship team last year.

BASKETBALL

San Diego State announced that Tori Baker, a 5-foot-9 sophomore guard, will transfer from the University of Oregon. Baker played in just two games this season for Oregon, averaging three points and one rebound. Last season, she started 18 of 30 games and averaged 9.3 points a game after red-shirting her freshman year while recovering from an ankle injury. She will be eligible to play at SDSU after the 1990 fall semester.
