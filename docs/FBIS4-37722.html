BFN [Excerpt] Prior to leaving Brussels, the head of government, Mokdad Sifi, confirmed that he has received firm assurances that Algeria would get a European loan of 200 million ECUs before the end of the year. In a news conference before leaving Brussels for Paris, Mokdad Sifi said that he had sensed a firm determination in the European capital, Brussels, to support Algeria in its ongoing economic and political reforms. Mokdad Sifi had a meeting today with President of the European Commission Jacques Delors, after his meeting with his Belgian counterpart, Jean-Luc Dehaene, and the Belgian foreign minister, Willy Claes, who had assured him that the Algerian issue would be discussed at the European summit in Corfu in a few days.
