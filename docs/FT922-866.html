920625 FT 25 JUN 92 / Training spending 'behind rest of EC' SMALLER UK companies spend less on training than other EC countries, according to a survey of 10,000 EC companies by 3i, the venture-capital group, and Cranfield School of Management. The survey said Britain's small and medium-sized enterprises spent 1.29 per cent of turnover on average on training compared with 1.76 per cent in France.
