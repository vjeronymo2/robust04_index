BFN [Text] Santiago de Chile April 22 (CTK) -- At the end of his three-day visit to Chile, Premier Vaclav Klaus signed a Czech-Chilean cultural agreement today. On behalf of Chile, the agreement was signed by Deputy Foreign Minister Jose Miguel Insulza. The agreement provides for development of relations in culture, education and science, cooperation of media and exchanges of youth and sportsmen. It also envisages mutual presentation of works of art, exchange of books, dissemination of literature, direct cooperation of universities and scientific institutes and exchange trips of scholars and students. In the afternoon, Klaus met leading parliamentary representatives. Late in the afternoon he is leaving for to Brazil, the last stop on his ten-day trip to South America.
