940614 FT 14 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Umeco in black Umeco, the USM-traded aircraft refueller manufacturer and seals and sealants concern, ended the year to March 31 with pre-tax profits of Pounds 511,000. Last time saw losses, FRS 3-adjusted, of Pounds 144,000. Directors said the performance reflected a lower cost base and the introduction of new products and services. Turnover rose from Pounds 11m to Pounds 13.4m, including Pounds 1m from acquisitions. Earnings per share were 3.53p (3.4p losses) and an increased final dividend of 1.25p lifts the total from 1.75p to 2p.
