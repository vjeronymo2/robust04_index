March 31, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

FOR AL DAVIS, HOME IS WHERE RAIDERS ARE

I had a dream. Los Angeles and Oakland both turned down Al Davis' request for a billion-dollar franchise fee, so he decided that his would be the first team in outer space, namely the moon.

However, upon applying for a license to take his team there, NASA rejected his request. Their reason? "There is enough debris in space already."

AARON M. SHAYNE

and SHIRLEY E. SHAININ

Beverly Hills
