BFN [Text] Bonn, April 12 (XINHUA) -- German Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel told Huang Ju, the mayor of China's biggest city of Shanghai, here today that more German enterprises are being encouraged to go to Shanghai to develop economic cooperation with China. The German minister praised China's economic achievements and opening and reform policies and those of Shanghai in particular. Huang Ju briefed Kinkel on the fruitful cooperation that already exists between Shanghai and several German enterprises. The Chinese mayor met with German Minister of Economic Cooperation Carl-Dieter Spranger later today and they exchanged views on technical, training and economic cooperation. Huang Ju, who arrived in Germany on Sunday [10 April], is due to call on Chancellor Helmut Kohl on Wednesday.
