930312 FT 12 MAR 93 / Parliament and Politics: Crackdown plan on terror funds THE government is planning to widen current laws governing money laundering as part of an attempted crackdown on IRA fundraising. The proposals - expected to be incorporated before the summer into the Criminal Justice Bill - would create new offences, including a failure to report knowledge or suspicion of the existence of laundered terrorist funds, which will carry a maximum prison sentence of five years.
