920324 FT 24 MAR 92 / Election 1992: Heal likely to hold Mid Staffs MRS SYLVIA HEAL, Labour MP for Mid Staffordshire, should hold the seat if voting intentions shown in a poll published by the Birmingham Post yesterday hold firm. She won the seat in a 1990 by-election. The poll of 887 voters, conducted on Tuesday and Wednesday by Quality Field Work, showed Labour on 54 per cent, the Conservatives on 35 per cent and the Liberal Democrats on 9.5 per cent.
