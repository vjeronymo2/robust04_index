November 21, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: BONITA

A chiropractor pleaded no contest Monday to setting a fire July 21 at a Bonita market as part of a personal dispute with the former manager of the store whom he thought had dated his ex-wife.

Douglas Wofford, 42, of Carlsbad entered his felony plea before San Diego Superior Court Judge Jesus Rodriguez, who set sentencing for Feb. 20.
