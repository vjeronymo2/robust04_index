920303 FT 03 MAR 92 / The Lex Column: Corporate finance It has been many months since the UK market saw three flotations and the same number of management buy-outs announced on the same day, let alone a rights issue and double acquisition as well as a healthy clutch of less valuable deals. But the notion that old times have returned for London's work-starved corporate financiers should be treated with caution. Although yesterday stood out, it was in stark contrast to the recent dearth of deal-making. Most companies are delaying investment decisions ahead of the election. That is contributing to the stubborn slowness of recovery, which in turn is compounding political doubts. And with the two main parties running neck and neck in the polls, the election may not resolve this uncertainty.
