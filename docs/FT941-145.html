940331 FT 31 MAR 94 / People: North West Water Meanwhile, North West Water sought to put the seal on a new era with the announcement that Alan Dean, business development director, would be stepping down from the board. He will remain with the company although he planned to increase 'his other interests outside', the company said. Dean was a key member of the acquisitions team built up by Bob Thian, the chief executive who departed abruptly last year.
