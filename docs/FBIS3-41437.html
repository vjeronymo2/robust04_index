Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Mustafizur Rahman has said Bangladesh is facing serious environmental problems caused by external harnessing of shared international water resources. Briefing the heads of foreign missions in Dhaka yesterday, he said the serious environmental problems being faced by Bangladesh call for urgent international attention. Some of these problems are within the capacity of Bangladesh to solve, but much of them are caused by external harnessing of shared international water resources. He suggested that by visiting the areas in April and May, the envoys could themselves see the impact on the environment of Bangladesh by these external harnessing of waters.
