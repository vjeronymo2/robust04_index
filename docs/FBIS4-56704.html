BFN ["Text" of the official announcement closing the Panamanian 1994 presidential election from the ATLAPA Convention Center Election Media Center in Panama City; read by announcer--live relay] [Text] Ladies and gentlemen: In accordance with the provisions established by the Electoral Tribunal related to the 1994 general elections, today, Sunday, 8 May, at 4 PM [2100 GMT] in the afternoon, we hereby arrive at the conclusion of the voting process.
