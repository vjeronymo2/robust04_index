940411 FT 11 APR 94 / Company News (This Week): Michelin - Other Companies The world's largest tyre manufacturer announces its 1993 results on Wednesday and is expected to report substantial losses. The French group suffered a net loss of FFr3.19bn (Dollars 540m) in the first six months, resulting from the downturn in the European automobile industry and exceptional restructuring charges. At the time of a capital issue earlier this year, however, the group said it was seeing signs of recovery in the international tyre market.
