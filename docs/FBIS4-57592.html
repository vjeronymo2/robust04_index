BFN [Text] On 19 May, PRC Ambassador Xu Yicong visited the Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology Center in Camaguey City. Center director Armando Moran informed Xu Yicong of the achievements in the production of veterinary medicines and in genetic and plant modifications. During his visit, Xu was accompanied by Julian Rizo, first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba in Camaguey, and by (Edislaf Gonzalez), president of the provincial People's Government. Ambassador Xu expressed his thanks for the scientific briefing during his visit to Camaguey and stressed the importance of the cooperation agreement between Cuba and the PRC, which is currently going well and expected to increase.
