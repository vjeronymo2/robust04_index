BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 9/6/1994 -- Romanian President Ion Iliescu received Thursday, 9 June, the Chinese minister of the machine-building industry He Guangyuan, head of his country's delegation to the 12th session of the Romanian-Chinese Joint Governmental Committee on Economic and Technical Cooperation, held these days in Bucharest. At the close of the interview, the guest was appreciative of the progress made by the Romanian-Chinese relations, and quoted President Iliescu as saying that the Romanian side hoped the economic cooperation with China would open better trade opportunities to Romanian and Chinese firms. Minister Guangyuan anticipated that the visit of Premier Li Peng next month to Romania will give a greater impetus to the relations between the two countries.
