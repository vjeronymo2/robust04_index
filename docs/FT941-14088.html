940121 FT 21 JAN 94 / International Company News: Microsoft rises 22% to Dollars 289m in second term Microsoft, the world's largest personal computer software supplier, reported a 22 per cent increase in second-quarter net income, to Dollars 289m from Dollars 236m. The figures translated into earnings per share of 95 cents, up from 78 cents, and were struck on revenues 20 per cent higher at Dollars 1.22bn. The results were in line with expectations. Microsoft said several factors contributed to the improvement, including an enthusiastic response to the latest editions of Microsoft Office in the US, together with the 'ongoing adoption of the Microsoft Windows operating system'. For the six months, it reported net income of Dollars 528m, or Dollars 1.74 a share, up from Dollars 445m, or Dollars 1.48, in the same period of last year.
