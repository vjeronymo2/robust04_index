940727 FT 27 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Geared Income Geared Income Investment Trust reported a net asset value of 99.25p per share as at June 30, up from 86.1p at the same stage of 1993. Net revenue for the three month period amounted to Pounds 273,357 (Pounds 358,732), equivalent to earnings of 1.21p (1.63p) per share. The first interim dividend is maintained at 1.5p.
