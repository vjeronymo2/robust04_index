920905 FT 05 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Hartwell gains 51.6% of Trimoco HARTWELL has won control of fellow motor dealer Trimoco after purchasing a further 8.41m shares. The purchase brings the total number of shares owned by Hartwell and those acting in concert with it, including conversion shares, to 76.3m or 51.6 per cent of the fully diluted share capital. Trimoco has recommended that shareholders accept Hart-well's increased and final offers which value the company at Pounds 29.6m.
