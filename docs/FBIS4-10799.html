BFN [Text] Army General Raul Castro Ruz, minister of the Revolutionary Armed Forces, chaired the national ceremony marking the 33rd anniversary of the proclamation of the socialist character of the Revolution and combatants day. More than 6,000 people participated in the popular gathering where a military review was held. The review was brightened with patriotic poems and songs as well as typical elements of the national culture. The Mariana Grajales Plaza of the Revolution in Guantanamo City also served as the site for the promotion ceremony of reservists and combatants and the decoration of combatants that served important missions along the perimeter that borders the Yankee naval base. The occasion also served to award outstanding people and institutions, with a replica of Maximo Gomez's machete for their excellent work in the cultural field. The ceremony was held outside of La Fortaleza de la Cabana for the fist time.
