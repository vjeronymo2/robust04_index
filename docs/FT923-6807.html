920821 FT 21 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Bad debts behind fall at T Clarke T Clarke, the south London-based electrical services group, suffered a 44 per cent contraction in profits for the six months to June 30. Directors blamed the fall - from Pounds 1.2m to Pounds 672,207 pre-tax - on reduced interest receivable and increased bad debt provisions of Pounds 120,776 (Pounds 16,582). Turnover dipped to Pounds 30.1m (Pounds 33m) and is expected to fall slightly in the second half. Earnings per share emerged at 3.404p, down from 6.135p, but the interim dividend is maintained at 1.26p.
