940809 FT 09 AUG 94 / People: London & Continental Railways Sir Derek Hornby (below), former chairman of Rank Xerox (UK) and a member of British Rail's board for five years, has been appointed chairman of London & Continental Railways. The company is bidding to acquire European Passenger Services and Union Railways, to provide international and local passenger services and to build and operate the fast link from London to the Channel tunnel.
