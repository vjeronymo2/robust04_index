Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] A national meeting on exchanges with Taiwan was held in Haikou from 25 to 28 January. Ruan Chongwu, provincial party secretary and governor, and Vice Governor Liu Mingqi attended the meeting. Tang Shubei, executive vice chairman of the Association for Relations Across the Taiwan Strait, briefed the participants on civilian exchanges between both sides of the strait.
