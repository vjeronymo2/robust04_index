Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vietnamese Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam and Uzbek Foreign Minister Saidmukhtar Said Asymov signed a protocol on cooperation between the two ministries in Tashkent on Wednesday [2 February]. The negotiations in the Uzbek Ministry of Foreign Economic Relations with the participation of the Vietnamese Foreign Minister also took place on Wednesday. The parties discussed and welcomed the draft intergovernmental agreement on trade/economic cooperation. It was also decided to start working on draft agreements on mutual protection and attraction of investments, on prevention of double taxation and on an inter-bank agreement. Uzbek President Islam Karimov will receive the Vietnamese foreign minister on Thursday. Thereafter, the Vietnamese delegation will visit Uzbek leading enterprises and go sightseeing in Tashkent. The visit of the Vietnamese minister will end on February 5.
