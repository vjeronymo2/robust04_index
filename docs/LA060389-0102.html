June 3, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

As of Monday, Paramount Communications will be the new corporate name of entertainment and publishing giant Gulf & Western . . . Santa Monica-based Wickes Cos. announced that its 1989 annual meeting will be held at 9 a.m., Wednesday, June 14 at the Helmsley Palace Hotel in New York . . . The Investment Company Institute reported that the assets of the nation's 467 money market mutual funds increased $599.3 million to $312.68 billion for the week ended May 31.
