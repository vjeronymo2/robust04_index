921204 FT 04 DEC 92 / Technology (Worth Watching): Season's greetings from festive PCs Print your own Christmas card with a festive font pack designed for those using the latest Windows software. The Winter Holiday font pack produces snow-capped letters for the verse, not to mention pictures of jolly Santas, holly, snowflakes and even a turkey. To ensure the Pounds 14.95 package is not just a one-week wonder, developers Bitstream of the US have also incorporated a pumpkin for Halloween and hearts for Valentine's day. Bitstream: US 617 497 6222. Fontware: UK, 0329 822857.
