Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow 26 Mar, TASS -- Automatic rifle fire near strategic missiles reported on 10 March at the Barnaul missile regiment posed no threat to nuclear arms, the press center of the Russian strategic missile troops told ITAR-TASS today. When on duty near missile launching grounds ethnic-Dagestan Private F. Ashurbekov opened fire at the guard, shot two servicemen and wounded two others. A special group headed by regiment Commander Colonel A. Donets localized and rendered harmless the criminal. A statement of the press center said "there was no threat to the missile launching grounds, the situation was under complete of the command [sentence as received]; the service system and control over nuclear missiles in strategic missile troops guarantees nuclear safety under regular conditions and in various extreme situations." "Missile complexes of the Russian strategic missile troops are reliably protected against unauthorized actions of personnel. In case of a threat of capture or shelling of nuclear arms corresponding measures would be taken. "An order of the strategic missile troops' commander in chief was issued on the incident and criminal proceedings have been instituted. Preliminary information received by the investigation shows that Private Ashurbekov has mental disorder," says the statement.
