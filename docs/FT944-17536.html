941007 FT 07 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Radamec advances Radamec Group, the electronics and precision mechanical engineering group, raised pre-tax profits by 26 per cent from Pounds 335,000 to Pounds 421,000 for the first half of 1994. Turnover dropped from Pounds 6m to Pounds 5.19m. Mr Len Whittaker, chairman, said the group's defence and TV studio businesses were expected to trade strongly in the second half, and continued improvement at other subsidiaries was anticipated. Earnings per share grew by 0.3p to 2.1p and the interim dividend has been raised to 0.7p (0.5p).
