930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / International Company News in Brief: British Airways BRITISH AIRWAYS yesterday announced it was to issue a Visa credit card for its US customers in partnership with Chase Manhattan Bank, writes John Gapper. The card is to be marketed to Chase customers and members of BA's Executive Club US scheme. The card, which will not be available in the UK, will offer bonuses in the form of credits for flights. Customers will be able to redeem air miles on BA, USAir, American Airlines and Alaska Airlines flights.
