BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 25 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il extended thanks to different units, officials and working people including Sadong District, Pyongyang Municipality, Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea, the Potonggang District direct sales shop in Pyongyang, the Pyongyang printing combine, a department director Kim Yong-son and instructor Kim Hye-kyong of the Combined General Bureau of Marine Transport who had set examples in highly displaying the traditional beautiful traits of Army-people unity. They had contributed to the consolidation of the relations between the Army and the people by actively aiding the soldiers of the People's Army.
