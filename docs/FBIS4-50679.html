BFN [Text] Guangzhou, May 20 (XINHUA) -- Surinamese President Ronald R. Venetiaan and his party left here today for Hong Kong, ending his visit here. Guangdong Vice Governor Ou Guangyuan and Vice Minister of Forestry Wang Zhibao, chairman of the Chinese Government Reception Committee, were among those to see the Surinamese guests off at the airport. President Venetiaan arrived here yesterday from Shanghai. Vice-Governor Ou met with and feted Venetiaan and his party. Both sides expressed the wish to further the understanding and economic cooperation between Guangdong Province and Suriname. During his stay here, Venetiaan visited a tinned food factory and a hospital.
