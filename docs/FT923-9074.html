920807 FT 07 AUG 92 / UK Company News: TR City of London net asset growth TR City of London Trust saw its net asset value rise from 110.5p to 112.8p per share over the 12 months ended June 30 1992. Gross revenue for the year improved from Pounds 15m to Pounds 15.7m, while earnings per share increased from 4.65p to 4.76p. The fourth quarterly dividend is 1.19p for a total of 4.76p, compared with 4.56p. Since the year-end the trust has maintained liquidity and will be looking for buying opportunities ahead of 'a likely decline in interest rates and a slow economic recovery in 1993'.
