940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Roskel edges ahead Roskel, the specialist suspended ceilings contractor and partitioning and ceilings distribution group, reported pre-tax profits marginally ahead from Pounds 528,000 to Pounds 532,000 for the six months to end-June. Group turnover for the period was up at Pounds 23.7m, against Pounds 21.7m which included Pounds 1.45m from discontinued activities. Earnings were 2.34p (2.23p) per share and the interim dividend is maintained with a 1.3p distribution.
