940128 FT 28 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Reuters expands in US Reuters, the business information and news group, has paid Dollars 13.8m (Pounds 9.2m) to acquire a 79 per cent stake in Reality Technologies, a private US company which develops personal investment technology. The investment includes the injection of new capital for Reality, which is based in Pennsylvania. The acquisition was made from an investor group led by Venrock Associates. Current management will remain in place and report to Reuters NewMedia in New York. Reality markets its investment software and on-line services jointly with Time's Money magazine.
