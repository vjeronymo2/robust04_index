Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Riyadh, 19 Jan -- His Excellency Shaykh Fahim Bin-Sultan al-Qasimi, the secretary general of the Gulf Cooperation Council [GCC], this morning at the council headquarters met His Excellency Dr. Guenter Rexrodt, the German economics minister. During the meeting, economic and trade relations between the two sides were discussed. Relations between the GCC states and the European Union, within the framework of the economic agreement concluded between them in 1988, and the ongoing talks between the GCC and the European Union on a free trade agreement, were also reviewed. The German side heard briefing by his excellency the secretary general on the achievements of collective work within the framework of the GCC. Dr. Abdullah al-Quwayz, the assistant secretary general for economic affairs, and a number of officials in the General Secretariat took part in the meeting.
