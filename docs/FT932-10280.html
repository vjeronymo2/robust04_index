930510 FT 10 MAY 93 / Italian stake in The Independent OFFICIA MECCANICA Cerruti, the Italian manufacturer of printing equipment, has bought a 2.3 per cent stake in Newspaper Publishing, owner of The Independent and the Independent on Sunday. The group bought the stake at 190p a share from Mr John Talbot of Arthur Andersen, the administrator of Robert Maxwell's private interests. Mr Talbot still holds another 3.5 per cent stake in Newspaper Publishing that was once owned by Robert Maxwell.
