January 14, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

BUS STATION

Pursuant to the article indicating the siting of a new Orange County Transit District bus station at Fashion Island, I write to express my strongest objections.

As a commuter traveling the San Joaquin Hills Road/MacArthur (Boulevard) junction daily, I find it congested. To place 12 buses in and out of that junction on a daily basis is absurd traffic planning.

This represents a safety hazard that will clog MacArthur, and the bus turning will increase pedestrian and vehicular accidents at that junction. I speak from experience.

My wife was nearly killed in a severe auto accident three years ago at the MacArthur/San Joaquin junction.

CHRISTOPHER LYON

Corona del Mar
