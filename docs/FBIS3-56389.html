Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] On Friday in Moscow, Russia's First Deputy Defense Minister Andrey Kokoshin and Chief of Russia's General Staff Colonel General Mikhail Kolesnikov met with the Ukrainian Defense Minister Vitaliy Radetskyy. The Russian Defense Ministry's Information and Press Department told INTERFAX that in the course of the conversation the parties discussed ways to effectively implement agreements reached between leaders of the two countries' Defense Departments in Moscow and aimed at further development of military cooperation between Russia and Ukraine and tripartite agreements between Russia, United States, and Ukraine on liquidation of nuclear weapons on Ukraine's territory. The discussion was being held in a constructive mood.
