Federal Aviation Administration Advisory Circular 21&hyph;20A, Supplier Surveillance Procedures AGENCY: Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), DOT. ACTION: Notice of availability.

SUMMARY: This notice announces the availability of Advisory Circular 21&hyph;20A, Supplier Surveillance Procedures. Advisory Circular 21&hyph;20A provides information and guidance concerning an acceptable means, but not the only means, of demonstrating compliance with the requirements of Federal Aviation Regulations Part 21, Certification Procedures for Products and Parts, regarding Supplier Surveillance Procedures.
ADDRESSES: Copies of AC 21&hyph;20A can be obtained from the following: Department of Transportation, Utilization and Storage Section, M443.2, 400 Seventh Street SW., Washington, DC 20590. Issued in Washington, DC, on August 4, 1994.
