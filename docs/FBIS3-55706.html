Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] According to POWs from CIS countries captured in Karabakh, Azerbaijan pays each of the mercenaries fighting on its side 500,000 Russian rubles a month. For carrying out especially important military assignments, a soldier of fortune receives additionally up to 10,000 US dollars. Member of parliament of the Nagornyy Karabakh Republic writer (Zuriy Balayan) has given this information to SNARK agency. Apart from this, nearly 1,500 Afghan modjaheddin are also fighing on the Azeri side, which is not even being denied by Kabul. Balayan expressed his surprise by the total absence of any reaction from the Russian leadership to the recent television report on a group of mercenaries recruited in Ivanovo that was departing for Azerbaijan. This was an IMA-press report.
