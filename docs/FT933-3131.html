930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Ecclesiastical Ins Ecclesiastical Insurance Office, the ethical insurance company owned by Allchurches Trust and responsible for insuring Anglican churches, turned in a Pounds 4.76m profit for the half year to June 30, up from Pounds 3.65m last time. Premium income was up from Pounds 73.5m to Pounds 86.6m, with general premium turnover up to Pounds 76.2m (Pounds 64.3m) and life business contributing Pounds 10.3m (Pounds 9.16m). Investment income was ahead at Pounds 4.88m (Pounds 3.87m). Minorities required Pounds 457,000 (nil). Ecclesiastical obtained a controlling stake in the St Andrew Trust in January as a vehicle for investing in smaller companies.
