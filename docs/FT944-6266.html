941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Budget 94 (Their Budget): Music promoter Rob Cameron, 26: partner in Shindig, a business promoting underground music events. Married with no children. Earns: Shindig has annual profit of around Pounds 100,000 on Pounds 250,000 turnover, split between four partners including Cameron, a student at Northumbria University. Politics: Labour. Wanted: the VATman takes around Pounds 700 per event. 'Any increase in VAT would annoy me.' Increased duty on alcohol would be bad news, as would any rise in air travel costs - most of the specialist DJs used are flown in from the US. Said yesterday: 'It was all a bit boring. It's gathering the money now to make the cuts later, for popularity. I have a Pounds 45,000 mortgage so it would have made a difference if Miras had changed.'
