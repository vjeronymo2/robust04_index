921223 FT 23 DEC 92 / Former BLMC executive dies SIR George Turnbull, chairman and chief executive of Inchcape, the business services group, died yesterday aged 66. Sir George had a long and varied career in the motor industry, both in the UK and overseas, being a former managing director of British Leyland Motor Corporation, vice-president of Hyundai Motors and chairman of Talbot UK. He had also been chairman of the Industrial Society's council between 1987 and 1990 and a past president of the Society of Motor Manufacturers and Traders.
