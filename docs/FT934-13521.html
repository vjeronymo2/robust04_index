931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / Observer: Glad hand Having toppled off a pair of Vivienne Westwood's shoes in Paris, British supermodel Naomi Campbell was taking no chances with her platform appearance at the British Fashion Awards on Tuesday night. This time she strode on to the catwalk hand-in-hand with Brian Pitman, chief executive of Lloyds Bank, which sponsors the awards. Pitman told envious colleagues afterwards that Campbell grasped his hand to steady herself backstage, and then kept hold. And they say banking is dull.
