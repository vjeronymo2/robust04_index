May 13, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

SOUTH ALL-STARS ADD GASKINS AND MAZZOTTA

Free safety Barry Gaskins of University High School and wide receiver Casey Mazzotta of Tustin have been added to the South roster for the Orange County All-Star football game June 30 at Orange Coast College's LeBard Stadium.

Gaskins replaces Ty Price of Corona del Mar and Mazzotta replaces Sean Drinkwater of El Toro, who declined to play in the charity game. Practice for the South team begins June 17.
