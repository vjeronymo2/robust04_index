920416 FT 16 APR 92 / UK Company News: Lasmo completes shipowning pull-out As part of its downstream divestment programme, Lasmo is selling three Suezmax 148,000 tonne tankers currently under construction. With the previously announced sale of two bulk ore carriers, net cash proceeds after meeting the 1992 construction costs were estimated at Dollars 125m (Pounds 70.8m). Two of the Suezmax vessels have been sold to Essar Shipping of Bombay and the other is going to a European owner.
