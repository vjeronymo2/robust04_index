940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / International Company News: Genting pushes ahead 38% to MDollars 959m Genting, the diversified Malaysian gaming, plantations and rubber products group, has announced pre-tax profits for calendar 1993 of MDollars 959m (USDollars 352m), a 38 per cent rise on the previous year. The bulk of Genting's profits came from its listed Resorts World group which announced pre-tax profits of MDollars 673m, a 42 per cent rise on the previous year's figure. Resorts runs the gaming and leisure side of Genting, centred on the giant casino and entertainment complex located in highlands outside Kuala Lumpur. Asiatic Development, the listed company which groups Genting's plantations interests, recorded a pre-tax profit of MDollars 38m, a 3 per cent drop on the 1992 figure.
