Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] In the afternoon of 17 March, Deputy Prime Minister Phan Van Khai cordially received in the Government Office the economic delegation of the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg led by National Economy Minister Robert Goebbels. Minister Robert Goebbels thanked the Vietnamese Government for creating the conditions for his delegation to visit and work with related sectors on studying investment opportunities and developing cooperation with Vietnam. The minister also introduced Luxembourg's potentials and strong points in metallurgy, mechanics, and telecommunications, especially in medium and small industries. Deputy Prime Minister Phan Van Khai welcomed the delegation's visit to Vietnam. He considered the visit to be a positive activity that would strengthen and develop the friendly cooperation between the two countries. The deputy prime minister introduced the delegation to some areas in which Vietnam is calling for foreign investment and in which Luxembourg has special advantages, such as its steel industry. He hoped that after this visit, the economic cooperation relation between Vietnam and Luxembourg will be broadened and developed.
