Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Croat delegation to the Sarajevo talks numbers 44 members in all, 28 of whom are deputies in the assembly of Herceg-Bosna. They are being led by Kresimir Zubak, president of the Presidential Council of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna, Ivan Bender, president of the Chamber of Deputies, Prime Minister Jadranko Prlic, and General Ante Roso. Prior to their departure, Kresimir Zubak told us: [Begin Zubak recording] I am convinced that we will reach agreement in Sarajevo regarding the procedures of the session itself and that we will this evening in Sarajevo also approve the draft [constitution]. It is envisaged that when the constitution comes into effect, if it is approved this evening, then it should come into effect tonight at 2400. The next day there should be elections for the interim president and vice president of the federation, but these elections must be preceded by a political agreement. [end recording] [passage omitted]
