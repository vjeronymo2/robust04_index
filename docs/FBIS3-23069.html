Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] An incident has occurred in Sidon that might be linked to the Hebron massacre and the PLO's stand on it. Security sources in Sidon said that unidentified gunmen have assassinated Sidqi Farhud, brother of 'Ali Farhud, the Fatah intelligence service chief in the Sidon area. According to the latest information, gunmen opened fire from a vehicle at Sidqi, 50 years old, and his 22-year-old son while they were driving in the Sidon port area. Sidqi died immediately but his son, who was slightly injured, managed to drive the vehicle to a nearby hospital.
