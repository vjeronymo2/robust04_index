941210 FT 10 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Turkey Trust Net asset value per share of the Turkey Trust declined by 37 per cent over the year to October 31, from 347.41p to 219.85p. After tax of Pounds 63,000 (Pounds 153,000) net revenue slipped to Pounds 188,000 (Pounds 356,000) for earnings per share of 1.91p (3.64p). The proposed final dividend is cut to 2p (3p). At the year-end 87.2 per cent of the company's assets were invested in Turkish equities.
