BFN [Unscheduled announcement] [Text] Dear listeners, we have received the following: The House of Representatives held a session this morning under its Speaker Shaykh 'Abdallah Bin-Husayn al-Ahmar to discuss the latest military developments in the country. The House called on all the masses of the Yemeni people to stand by Constitutional legitimacy and rally around the legitimate political leadership under brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council and supreme commander of the Armed Forces. The House also called on all officers, noncommissioned officers, and personnel of the Armed and Security Forces in the Republic of Yemen to rally around the Supreme Command of the Armed Forces and not to obey orders unless they are issued by the supreme commander of the Armed Forces. The House also decided to withdraw confidence in the secessionist elements in the leadership of the [Yemeni] Socialist Party [YSP] for their persistent wrongdoing which reached the extent of raiding the capital Sanaa and the two cities of Ta'izz and al-Hudaydah with aircraft and frightening the inhabitants and disturbing sleeping children. The House of Representatives also ratified the declaration of a state of emergency and authorized the political leadership to issue the necessary decisions to deal with the situation through which the country is passing. The people's deputies also decided to keep the House in open session. The House has earlier reviewed and ratified the minutes of its previous session.
