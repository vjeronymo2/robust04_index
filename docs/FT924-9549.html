921105 FT 05 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Booker to buy GrandMet offshoot Buckingham Foods, part of the Prepared Foods Group of Booker, is buying the fixed assets and goodwill of Eatfresh, a sandwich and chilled snacks business from Grand Metropolitan. Book value of the fixed assets acquired is Pounds 1.69m and the current assets will be purchased at valuation.
