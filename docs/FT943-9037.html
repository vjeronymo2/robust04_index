940813 FT 13 AUG 94 / London Stock Exchange: Equity futures and options trading A squeeze in stock index futures sent the near-month contract sharply ahead which helped lead the cash market higher, writes Joel Kibazo. The September contract on the FT-SE 100 started trading at 3,136 and during the session saw a low of 3,112 and a high of 3,169 before settling at 3,159 by the close. In traded options, total volume was 36,099 of which 15,048 was dealt in the FT-SE 100 option and 3,268 in the Euro FT-SE option. Amstrad was the busiest stock option with a total of 3,268 contracts. Royal Insurance and Asda Group were also busy.
