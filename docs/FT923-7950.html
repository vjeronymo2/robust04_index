920814 FT 14 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Specialist Computer achieves Pounds 5.03m Specialist Computer Holdings, a private computer sales and services company, improved turnover by some 7 per cent to Pounds 83.2m in the year to March 31. Pre-tax profits, however, fell from Pounds 5.3m to Pounds 5.03m. Mr Peter Rigby, the chairman, thought that sales would improve in the current year although he expected the price of personal computers to fall by up to a further 20 per cent. Cash reserves of about Pounds 5m were available for strategic acquisitions.
