910415 FT 15 APR 91 / Romanian tennis coach opens bank Mr Ion Tiriac, the former international tennis coach, will today open Romania's first private bank in Bucharest after raising Lei 3bn (Dollars 50m) in equity, Ariane Genillard reports from Bucharest. Mr Tiriac, who represents Mercedes, Siemens and United Distilleries in Romania, will own 25 per cent of the bank's capital.
