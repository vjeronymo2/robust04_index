940218 FT 18 FEB 94 / Banco Latino may be refloated The Venezuelan government says it will attempt to refloat Banco Latino, the country's second largest bank, which failed last month, writes Joseph Mann in Caracas. Mr Julio Sosa, minister of finance, said on Wednesday that Latino would be restored to financial health and reduced in size. The recovery plan includes new public and private sector capital, appointing an administrative board and 'improving communications' between the bank and its account holders.
