Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by Basim Abu-Sumayyah from the occupied territory] [Excerpt] Dr. Sa'ib 'Urayqat, head of the Palestinian delegation to the Washington talks, has said that the U.S. hinting that it will veto the UN Security Council resolution on the al-Ibrahimi Mosque if the PLO does not resume the talks with Israel is a reiteration that the United States cannot play a role other than that of the adversary. He accused U.S. decisionmakers of being more Israeli than the Israelis themselves. He said: The U.S. Administration has lost its credibility and cannot be a fair broker or a sponsor of the peace process. He added: There might be a need to reassess the overall peace process by the Palestinian party in particular and the Arab parties in general. [passage omitted]
