December 5, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY ROUNDUP: STOCKHOLDERS TO VOTE ON SIERRACIN BUYOUT JAN. 24

Sierracin Corp. has scheduled its annual meeting for Jan. 24, at which stockholders will vote on a proposed $30-million buyout of the Sylmar aerospace company.

The company said stockholders of record as of this Friday would be entitled to vote at the meeting. Sierracin's directors have approved the buyout bid from a management-led investor group, which offered $15 a share for the 52% of Sierracin it does not already own.
