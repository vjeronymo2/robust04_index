BFN [Text] Shanghai, May 6 (XINHUA) -- The training warship "Makutarajakumarn" from Thailand arrived here today on a three-day goodwill visit to China. Deputy chief of staff of the Shanghai base of the Chinese Navy Shen Hongzhang met the Thai guests at the pier. Vice-Mayor of Shanghai Meng Jianzhu and commander of the Shanghai base of the Chinese Navy Yan Yuchun met here today with head of the delegation Captain Nara and captain of the warship Krienjrai Anantasan. The Thai guests will visit the Chinese Navy fleet, shipyards and parks.
