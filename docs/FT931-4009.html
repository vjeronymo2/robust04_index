930312 FT 12 MAR 93 / International Company News: BHP tightens grip on Foster's BROKEN Hill Proprietary (BHP), the Australian resources group, yesterday moved to tighten its management grip on Foster's Brewing, the Australian beermaker which it controls, with a management restructuring, writes Bruce Jacques. Mr Tony Larkin, former general manager for finance at BHP Minerals, has been appointed finance director at Foster's. Mr Peter Jonson, the former finance chief at Foster's, has resigned. Foster's board, which already includes four BHP nominees, said the group head office would be divided into three units: corporate affairs, corporate finance and corporate strategy.
