920526 FT 26 MAY 92 / Construction Contracts: Munster base TROLLOPE & COLLS JOINERY, a member of the construction division of Trafalgar House, has won its first overseas contract with a successful bid for joinery in northern Germany. Work has started on a Pounds 140,000 fitting-out contract to provide woodwork at offices being built in Munsterstrasse, Munster. When complete, the project will provide a German headquarters for the Hong Kong-based company Hutchinson Mobilfunk. The contract was placed by the Berlin office of Stanhope Interior (Germany), the main contractor.
