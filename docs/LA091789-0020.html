September 17, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CRITIC'S VIEW OF KFAC

The word that stands out in Bernheimer's article is embarrassment, which he uses to describe the fact that "there will be no commercial (classical-music) radio station in Los Angeles." Let's face it, L.A. is not a sophisticated city. It's a beach town with all the name entails. Angelenos' idea of culture is eating designer pizza and getting tickets for "Les Miz" or "Phantom."

YOLANDA KRELL

Los Angeles
