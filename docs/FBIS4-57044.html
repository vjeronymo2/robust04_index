BFN [Report by Luiz Augusto Mendonca from Planalto Palace] [Text] President Itamar Franco has ordered presidential spokesman Fernando Costa to release the following statement to the press: First, that the president of the Republic is closely following all of the actions taken by the Army Ministry aimed at preserving public order and the Constitution; Second, that President Franco remains in close contact with the presidents of the Chamber of Deputies and of the federal Senate, who have lent him their full support and solidarity for the measures he has taken; Third, that the National Defense Committee of the Chamber of Deputies has requested that Justice Minister Alexandre Dupeyrat brief its members on the measures taken by the government regarding the Federal Police strike. Costa said the president immediately responded to the request, and ordered Dupeyrat to appear before the committee to answer all of their questions. Dupeyrat is now at Planalto Palace reporting to President Franco about his briefing before the National Defense Committee. Finally, Costa reported that President Franco is aware of all the actions planned to preserve order and the Constitution. Commenting on this afternoon's helicopter airlift of armed Army military personnel to Tres Poderes Square, Costa said this action was planned during yesterday's meeting between the president of the Republic with the military ministers, and that this is a combat training exercise by the Army Ministry.
