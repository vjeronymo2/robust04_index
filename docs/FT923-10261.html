920731 FT 31 JUL 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Seedless grapes are abundant at 90-Pounds 1.20 a lb (90-Pounds 1.50), along with Spania plums at 45-55p a lb (40-60p). New season English Discovery apples are in the shops at 60-65p a lb (40-50p). Top quality cabbage is this week's best vegetable buy, with English Primo and Savoy cabbages at 20-25p a lb (30-35p each). Potatoes remain good at 10-15p a lb (10-15p). Homegrown celery at 40-45p a head (50-55p) is in good supply. Other good salad buys include tomatoes at 40-45p a lb (40-50p), peppers at 80p-Pounds 1.00 a lb (70p-Pounds 1.00) and round lettuce at 20p-25p each (25-30p). (Last weeks prices shown in brackets).
