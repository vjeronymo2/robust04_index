BFN [Text] Shrine Abu Dhabi (UAE), June 23, IRNA -- The Islamic Republic of Iran on Thursday [23 June] urged the Organization of the Islamic Conference (OIC) [ICO] to condemn Monday's [20 June] bomb blast at the holy shrine of Imam Reza (as) [expansion unknown] in Mashhad and take serious measures against the terrorist Mojahedin-E Khalq group. Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati, whose official noted that the Mojahedin-E Khalq outfit (MKO) was behind the crime. [sentence as received] The noted [as received], handed to the OIC secretariate in Jeddah, said the terrorist MKO was responsible for the bomb blast at the Imam Reza (as) shrine on Ashura (martyrdom anniversary of Imam Hussein) which killed tens of pilgrims and wounded scores of others. Calling the OIC to step up campaign against terrorism through coordinated measures by its members, Velayati said the organization should take a firm position against terrorism. His note stressed that the OIC members should not let the sanctity of the holy places in Bosnia and Palestine to be desecrated. Monday's bomb explosion has claimed the lives of at least 26 mourners of Imam Hussein and injured 170 others besides damaging the centuries old art embellishment at the shrine.
