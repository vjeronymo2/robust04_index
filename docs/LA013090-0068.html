January 30, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Diet Coke Taps Agency: All Diet Coca-Cola ads will soon be created by a single advertising agency.

Coca-Cola Co. said it awarded its international advertising account for its Diet Coke brand to Lintas International. The account is valued at about $50 million. The agency currently handles Diet Coke ads in the United States and Canada. Losing the business was McCann-Erickson.
