940115 FT 15 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: Fund managers take hold of market The UK fund managers took hold of the London stock market and, with the US sellers content to back away, share prices moved ahead strongly. The FT-SE 100 Share Index gained 40.6 to close at 3,400.6, while the FT-SE Mid 250 Index put on 33.6 for a new closing peak of 3,915.7. In New York the US stock market bounced back from three losing sessions into record territory. The Dow Jones Industrial Average closed up 24.77 at 3,867.20, past its previous record close of 3,865.51, set on Monday.
