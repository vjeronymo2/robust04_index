Language: Russian Article Type:BFN ["Edict of the Russian Federation President on the Plenipotentiary Representative of the Russian Federation President," signed by Russian Federation President B. Yeltsin and dated Moscow, the Kremlin, 18 January 1994, No. 165] [Text] Russian President Boris Yeltsin issued a decree today entitled "On the Plenipotentiary Representative of the President of the Russian Federation, which says in part: With a view to ensure coordination between the president of the Russian Federation and the Federal Assembly I hereby decree: 1. To introduce as part of the administration of the Russian Federation president the post of the Russian Federation president's plenipotentiary representive in the Federal Assembly 2. To entrust the leader of the Russian Federation President's Administration with the task of: -- Submitting within a week draft statute on the plenipotentiary representative of the president of the Russian Federation in the Federal Assembly, as well proposals on the candidacy of the plenipotentiary representative of the president of the Russian Federation in the Federal Assembly, -- Resolving the issue of ensuring the work of the plenipotentiary representative of the president of the Russian Federation in the Federal Assembly. [Signed] President Boris Yeltsin [Dated] Moscow, The Kremlin, 18 January 1994 No. 165
