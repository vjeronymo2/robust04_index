940510 FT 10 MAY 94 / Receivers sell gateau maker Livera Foods, the Stockton-on-Tees gateau maker, has been sold by receivers Arthur Andersen to Sweet 'n' Savoury Frozen Foods of Hartlepool for an undisclosed sum. Sweet 'n' Savoury, which employs 650 people making frozen desserts, will use the Stockton plant for expansion. It plans to employ 350 people there within three years. Livera Foods, which employed 300 people, went into receivership in February, less than a year after the Pounds 7m project started production.
