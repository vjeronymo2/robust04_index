BFN [Text] Alfredo Jordan Morales, minister of agriculture and a Politburo member, has said in Bayamo that Basic Cooperative Production Unit [UBPC] workers are capable of developing many more initiatives to solve problems. Jordan, who participated in the initial phase of the Miscellaneous Crops National Council held in Granma, pointed out that the UBPCs have to be helped and supported; in this connection, he stressed the importance of housing construction. Jordan announced that the agriculture sector built 7,000 materials-efficient homes in 1993, and 1,400 in the first quarter of 1994. Granma Province is the leader in this effort. Jordan pointed out the need to turn performances in miscellaneous crops production around, nationwide, by using good seeds, preparing the soil properly, planting on schedule, and weeding consistently.
