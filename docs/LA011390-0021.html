January 13, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

THE DECLINE OF DAN RATHER'S RATINGS AFTER THE BUSH INCIDENT

Rick Du Brow's Jan. 6 article on the decline of Dan Rather's ratings after his 1988 interview with then-candidate George Bush didn't quite tell it all: Rather and his team of Bush haters such as Lesley Stahl and Bob Schieffer have carried on the vendetta.

When Gen. Manuel Noriega was captured, Rather spent the last 10 minutes of his show in a memorial to the dead in Panama, American and local, and a discussion of all the bad things that occurred in Panama with not one statement of the good things that came out of the U.S. action. This was the most biased, dishonest type of reporting, and Rather was not criticized in the print medium about doing it.

GEOFFREY POST, Santa Maria
