BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Mineyev] [Text] Brussels, 18 June -- Vitaliy Churkin, Russian deputy foreign minister, who arrived in Brussels on 17 June, is holding talks in NATO Headquarters with Gebhardt von Moltke, the alliance's deputy secretary general, an ITAR-TASS correspondent has been told at the Russian Embassy. NATO sources said yesterday that the North Atlantic Council had entrusted Moltke to meet Russian representatives before Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev arrived in Brussels to discuss the details of the visit and to finalize the content and title of the document that Russia and NATO plan to sign on 22 June, when Russia joins the Partnership for Peace program.
