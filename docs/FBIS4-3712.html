BFN [Text] Beijing, April 21 (XINHUA) -- Luo Gan, Chinese State Councillor and secretary-general of the State Council, met with a delegation from Germany's Baden-Weuttemberg State here today. The two sides exchanged views on the enhancement of bilateral economic cooperation. The delegation, headed by the state's Minister-President Erwin Teufel, is here as guest of the Chinese People's Institute of Foreign Affairs. It is accompanied by more than 100 entrepreneurs from Baden-Weuttemberg.
