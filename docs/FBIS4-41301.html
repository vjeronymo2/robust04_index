BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Yuriy Sizov, Vladimir Malyshev, and Yuriy Malinov] [Text] Corfou (Kerkyra) June 24 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin will spend a few days in Greece as a guest after completion of his visit to this country on June 24. During the visit an agreement on partnership and cooperation between Russia and the European Union will be signed, ITAR-TASS was informed of this by some sources in the Russian delegation.
