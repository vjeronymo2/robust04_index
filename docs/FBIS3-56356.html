Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] During the meeting in Tbilisi between Mamedov and Sarkisyan, the ministers of defense of Azerbaijan and Armenia, the Azerbaijani side proposed an immediate cease-fire right along the Armenian-Azerbaijani frontline and the opening of a political dialogue by both sides including at presidential level. Interfax reported that the Armenian side was not prepared to take these decisions. The sides agreed to continue consultations between the heads of military departments.
