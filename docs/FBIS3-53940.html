Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] This afternoon, Israel Air Force jets attacked a terrorist target in al-Na'imah in south Lebanon. The target belonged to Ahmad Jibril's Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine-General Command [PFLP-GC]. An IDF [Israel Defense Forces] spokesman said that the pilots reported accurate hits against the target. All our planes returned safely to base. The target that was hit served as a training base for the PFLP--GC.
