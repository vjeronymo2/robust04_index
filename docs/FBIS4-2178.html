BFN [Text] Beijing, April 6 (XINHUA) -- An economic and trade discussion between Beijing and Berlin, Germany, was held here today. Beijing Vice-Mayor Lu Yucheng and Berlin Mayor Eberhard Diepgen briefly introduced the two cities' investment environments and related economic information. Representatives from more than 70 local enterprises and over 30 German enterprises discussed more than 200 projects, covering automobiles, electronics, light industry, construction materials, food, machinery, communications, environmental protection and energy. Over the past few years, exchanges and cooperation between Beijing and Germany have been on the rise. To date, Beijing has an import and export volume of 170 million U.S. dollars with Germany. Beijing imports electronic parts, light industrial machinery, polyester and internal-combustion engine parts from Germany and exports clothing and textile products. German businessmen have established nearly 60 enterprises in Beijing, involving investments of more than 400 million U.S. dollars. Beijing has opened four enterprises in Germany. At the discussion today, German businessmen were satisfied with the investment environment in Beijing and showed great interest in some projects, sources said. Yesterday, Beijing and Berlin signed a memorandum for becoming sister cities. Under a related agreement, the first economic delegation, including siemens and benz, and headed by Berlin Mayor Diepgen, has started an inspection tour in Beijing. It will also go to Shanghai and Shenzhen.
