930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / Three-year program to protect the Black Sea Countries bordering the Black Sea countries yesterday agreed on a three-year programme to protect the sea, which scientists say is doomed unless pollution is tackled, Reuter reports. Officials said a meeting at the Bulgarian port of Varna agreed to establish centres for protecting the sea - rimmed by Bulgaria, Russia, Ukraine, Georgia, Romania and Turkey - and set up a donor fund of some Dollars 32m. The money will be used for projects approved by a donor commission.
