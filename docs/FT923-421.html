920929 FT 29 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Exceptionals lift Aberdeen Petroleum Exceptional profits of Pounds 133,000 from the sale of royalty in Australia, coupled with a cost-cutting programme lifted Aberdeen Petroleum, the USM-quoted oil and gas company, to a net profit of Pounds 157,000 for the six months to June 30, against Pounds 7,000 last time. Turnover fell from Pounds 1.37m to Pounds 1.16m. Earnings per share were 0.3p (0.01p).
