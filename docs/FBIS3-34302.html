Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Damascus, 6 Feb (SANA)--His Majesty King Husayn Bin-Talal of the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan and his accompanying delegation left Damascus at 1800 today. President Hafiz al-Asad led those who saw him off at the airport. Also present were Vice Presidents 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam and Dr. Muhammad Zuhayr Mashariqah, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi, the ministers, Jordan's ambassador in Damascus, and the embassy staff. [Damascus SANA in Arabic at 1730 GMT on 6 Feburary adds that al-Asad held a luncheon banquet in honor of King Husayn attended by senior Jordanian and Syrian officials].
