940430 FT 30 APR 94 / Maxwell pensions sue Goldman: International News Digest Trustees for two Maxwell Communications pension schemes last night filed lawsuits in New York against Goldman Sachs, charging that the US investment bank was negligent in allowing Mr Kevin Maxwell in 1991 to transfer millions of pounds from the pension funds to companies controlled by the Maxwell family. The lawsuits, filed by MCP Pension Trustees and MGN Pension Trustees, seek punitive damages against Goldman and compensation of Dollars 47m (Pounds 31m), the amount the trustees claim Mr Maxwell appropriated from the funds with Goldman's aid. Goldman said the claims against it were 'without merit' and represented an 'expensive and time-consuming distraction.'
