940702 FT 02 JUL 94 / Piccadilly site sold to Paul Raymond Part of the landmark 'island site' in London's Piccadilly has been sold to Mr Paul Raymond, the Soho property tycoon, who has paid about Pounds 15m for the Rialto Cinema corner site to receivers KPMG Peat Marwick. The site comprises three buildings incorporating the Rialto, the Cafe de Paris and shops and offices on Rupert Street and Coventry Street.
