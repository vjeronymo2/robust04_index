941011 FT 11 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Bourne End Pounds 5.4m buy Bourne End Properties has conditionally agreed to pay Allied Dunbar Assurance Pounds 5.35m for Sword House, a refurbished 1970s long leasehold office building of 46,000 sq ft in High Wycombe, Bucks. The property is currently let for the 105 year remainder of the term to Wilkinson Sword at a gross current annual rent of Pounds 398,000, with upward only rent reviews every seven years. The consideration is payable in cash from Bourne End's existing resources and a new 15 year fixed rate loan.
