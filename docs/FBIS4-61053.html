BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondent Dmitriy Gorokhov, ITAR- TASS correspondent Georgiy Shelenkov] [Text] Cairo May 3 TASS -- Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev arrived here today to attend the signing ceremony of the PLO-Israeli agreement on principles of the implementation of the "Gaza-Jericho first" treaty on Wednesday. The document will be signed by PLO leader Yasir 'Arafat, Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhak Rabin, Russian and U.S. foreign ministers who are co-sponsors of the Mideastern peace process and the Egyptian foreign minister. The latter welcomed Andrey kozyrev at the airport today. Andrey Kozyrev is also expected to meet U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher.
