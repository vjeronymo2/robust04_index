940112 FT 12 JAN 94 / M&S warns over teddy bears Marks and Spencer yesterday issued a warning that the long-hair 'Shaggy Teddy Bears' it sold throughout the autumn were not suitable toys for very young children to play with. It said: 'Babies and toddlers are inclined to put objects in their mouths and since these particular toys have a tendency to shed small quantities of fluff, it is recommended that children under 18 months should not play with them.'
