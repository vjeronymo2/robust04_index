October 29, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

THE WEEK AHEAD

SHARING POWER -- Los Angeles School Board member Roberta Weintraub on Wednesday will host East Valley parents in a discussion of their role in the district's new councils in which parents and teachers share some decision-making powers with administrators.

BIBLE READING -- A judge is to hear a request for a restraining order Friday that would bar the Salvation Army from conducting public Bible readings outside a business plaza.
