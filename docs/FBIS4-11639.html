BFN [Text] President Ilyas al-Hirawi received Phalangist Party leader Dr. George Sa'adah yesterday. Sa'adah said al-Hirawi briefed him on the reasons prompting the government to postpone granting permission to radio stations to broadcast news and explained the recent suspension of newscasts. He said: I was convinced of some reasons and now I am convinced of the others. No one can bargain over the issue of freedom in Lebanon, he said. He voiced his hope that the Council of Ministers will settle this issue quickly. Dr. Sa'adah spoke of the possibility of links between the bombing of the Sayyidat al-Najat Church in Zuq Mikha'il and the Phalangist Party headquarters, expressing his confidence in the Lebanese judiciary and urging patience.
