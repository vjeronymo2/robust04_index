Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Algeria: FIS Detainees To Be Released Before End of Ramadan"] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] Reliable sources have confirmed to AL-HAYAH that Cheik Ali Djeddi and Cheik Abdelkader Boukhamkham, who were released three days ago, did, in the first move since their release, visit Cheik Ahmad Sahnun, leader of the Islamic Da'wah [call] League who is considered by many to be a scholar and jurist who can stop the acts of violence to a large extent if he appeals to the Armed Islamic Groups to that effect. Initial information indicates that Cheik Sahnun received a message about the dialogue between the Army and the FIS and ways of participating in the transitional phase. In that context, sources have told AL-HAYAH that the FIS leaders who have not been released yet, namely Abdelkader Omar, Noureddine Cheghara, and Abdelkader Hachani, have been moved to the military hospital in Ain El Neja [as transliterated] "for important medical tests before their release." They added that the release would be completed before 'Id al-Fitr. [passage omitted]
