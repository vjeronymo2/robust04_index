Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Byrkin] [Text] Moscow February 17 TASS -- Abkhazian leader Vladislav Ardzinba called the attack on the first Russian border post of the Sukhumi frontier detachment on the night between February 10 and 11 a "provocation". The Abkhazian parliament speaker sent a telegramme to the commander of Russian border troops, Colonel-General Andrey Nikolayev, saying that the incident at the first border post was a provocation against "border guards courageously fulfilling their duty and enjoying respect of the entire Abkhazian people". Nobody was injured during the attack. ITAR-TASS learnt at the press service of Russian frontier troops on Thursday that Ardzinba promised in the telegramme that "adequate measures will be taken to track down and detain the provocateur".
