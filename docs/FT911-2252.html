910501 FT 01 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Atlantic Resources cuts losses Losses at Atlantic Resources, a USM-quoted extractor of mineral oil and natural gas, fell from IPounds 3m to IPounds 609,000 (Pounds 553,000) pre-tax for the 1990 year. The comparable figure was struck after taking account of exceptional provisions of IPounds 2.2m. Turnover rose to IPounds 1.27m (IPounds 404,00) following a return to full production at the Claymore field following the Piper Alpha disaster.
