BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 13 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song was presented with a floral basket and a congratulatory letter today by the military attaches corps here on the occasion of his birthday. The floral basket and congratulatory letter were handed to Minister of People's Armed Forces O Chin-u by military attache of the Russian Embassy Georgiy Mariyasov who is the doyen of the Military Attaches Corps. A floral basket was presented to President Kim Il-song by the delegation of the Chinese People's Volunteers of the Korean-Chinese side to the Military Armistice Commission on the occasion of his birthday. Head of the delegation Zheng Daogen handed the floral basket to Minister of People's Armed Forces O Chin-u.
