BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe 28 June TASS -- A border patrol in one of the sectors of the Moskovskiy border guarding detachments was fired upon from automatic weapons from Afghan territory on Monday night, sources at the department of Russian border troops in Tajikistan told ITAR-TASS on Tuesday [28 June]. Fire was intensive enough, but, luckily, none of border guards was hurt. The border guards did not return the fire, the sources said. The situation in the other sectors of the Tajik-Afghan border remains rather complex but is controlled by Russian border guards.
