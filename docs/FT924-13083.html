921017 FT 17 OCT 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Pepper prices are considerably ahead of the extremely low levels of a few months ago, reports Man-producten. Large scale short covering in the main origin countries has pushed domestic prices much higher, but this has not resulted in very active trading in consuming markets - most buyers prefer to wait and see. There were hardly any offers available from Brazil although it is the peak season. White pepper was offered at USDollars 1,550 a tonne spot, Dollars 1,500 cif for Nov/Dec shipment. Black pepper prices improved to Dollars 1,250 spot, Dollars 1,175-Dollars 1,200 cif, Nov/Dec shipment.
