Language: Swedish Article Type:BFN [Text] The Lidkoeping town court today sentenced five people who were accused of entering the F-7 Wing in Saatenaes and destroying the nose section of a Viggen fighter aircraft. The man and the woman who carried out the actual deed were sentenced to a year and two months, and three months imprisonment, respectively, for gross sabotage, Radio Skaraborg reports. Two others involved received sentences of one year each for assisting gross sabotage. The fifth accused received a suspended sentence.
