April 18, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Singer Liza Minnelli, already in the doghouse with Swedish authorities for taking her dog into the country last week without proper authority, ran into more problems over the weekend. On Sunday, she was grounded by a severe ear infection and had to pull out of a concert with Frank Sinatra and Sammy Davis Jr. in Helsinki, Finland. Doctors told her she could not fly for 10 days. Sinatra and Davis went ahead with the show. ALEENE MacMINN
