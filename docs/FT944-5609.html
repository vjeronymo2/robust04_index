941202 FT 02 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Sutcliffe Speakman A strong second quarter at Sutcliffe Speakman meant the high technology carbon and environment protection systems group turned in pre-tax profits of Pounds 306,000 for the half year to September 30. This compared with Pounds 361,000 last time, though this included an exceptional credit of Pounds 576,000. Turnover rose by 11 per cent to Pounds 11.4m (Pounds 10.3m). Earnings per share were 0.04p (0.38p) and an interim dividend of 0.25p has been declared.
