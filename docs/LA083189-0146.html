August 31, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Roger Kingdom won the gold medal in the 110-meter hurdles at the World University Games in Duisburg, West Germany, but cold weather and stiffness prevented the two-time Olympic champion from challenging his own world record.

Kingdom's time of 13.26 was well outside his mark of 12.92.

Americans also won three gold medals in relays on the final day of the Games, tying them with the Soviet Union for the lead with nine.
