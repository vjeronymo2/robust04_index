941119 FT 19 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Brackenbridge Brackenbridge, the USM-traded bridal and formal wear group, returned to the black at the interim stage with pre-tax profits of Pounds 32,000. The outcome for the six months to September 30, which compared with losses of Pounds 239,000 last time and Pounds 6.39m for the full year to end-March, came on turnover of Pounds 5.56m (Pounds 7.49m, including Pounds 1.39m from discontinued operations). After a nil tax charge, earnings per share were 0.04p (losses of 0.29p).
