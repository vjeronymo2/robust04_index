First Bancshares of Texas, Inc.; Change in Bank Control Notice Acquisition of Shares of Banks or Bank Holding Companies The notificant listed below has applied under the Change in Bank Control Act (12 U.S.C. 1817(j)) and §225.41 of the Board's Regulation Y (12 CFR 225.41) to acquire a bank or bank holding company. The factors that are considered in acting on notices are set forth in paragraph 7 of the Act (12 U.S.C. 1817(j)(7)). The notice is available for immediate inspection at the Federal Reserve Bank indicated. Once the notice has been accepted for processing, it will also be available for inspection at the offices of the Board of Governors. Interested persons may express their views in writing to the Reserve Bank indicated for the notice or to the offices of the Board of Governors. Comments must be received not later than June 2, 1994. A. Federal Reserve Bank of Dallas (Genie D. Short, Vice President) 2200 North Pearl Street, Dallas, Texas 75201-2272: 1. First Bancshares of Texas, Inc., Employee Stock Ownership Plan , Tomball, Texas; to acquire 0.33 percent for a total of 19.57 percent of the voting shares of The First National Bank, Altanta, Texas, and thereby indirectly acquire The Hamilton National Bank, Hamilton, Texas, and Bank of Tyler, Tyler, Texas.
