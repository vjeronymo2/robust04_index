940815 FT 15 AUG 94 / World News in Brief: European Monetary System The EMS had a fairly turbulent week, prompted by higher interest rates from two countries outside the grid, Sweden and Italy. The result was a large increase in the position of the strongest currency against the weakest, generalised D-Mark strength, and the Irish punt being displaced at the top of the grid by the Dutch guilder. The D-Mark also climbed above the Belgian franc. Currencies, Page 29
