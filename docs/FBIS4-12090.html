BFN [Text] Cairo, 4 Apr (MENA)--Today Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid received Iraqi Labor Minister Latif Nusayyif Jasim, who is leading his country's delegation to the Arab Labor Conference in Cairo. The Iraqi minister said that during his meeting with the secretary general they discussed Iraq's position regarding the UN Security Council's decision to extend the sanctions imposed on it. He pointed out that some Security Council members, including Russia and China, are inclined to lift these sanctions. Jasim told reporters that the embargo on Iraq has deprived the citizens of basic commodities and services including some foodstuffs, medicines, electric power, and sewage systems.
