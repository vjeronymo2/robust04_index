Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Crimea's President Yuriy Meshkov issued a decree today on organizing and holding a poll among Crimea's citizens on 27 March of this year, INTERFAX reported. Yuriy Meshkov approved the form for the poll. The document prescribes that Crimea's population will be polled on the following issues: -- On their attitude to dual citizenship; -- On the Crimeans' positions regarding the interrelation between Kiev and Simferopol on the basis of agreements and treaties; and also -- On granting Crimea's president the right to issue decrees that have force of law on issues that are not regulated by Crimean legislation.
