Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The situation on all fronts of the Karabakh conflict remains extremely tense. A very serious situation has developed in the southern sector where the Azeri Army has concentrated large forces, including armored vehicles, and is carrying out attacks on positions occupied by the Karabakh self-defense forces. By the evening of 7 January, they had succeeded in breaking through the Karabakh lines of defense and had taken up a number of positions along the Iranian border. Fighting continues with variable success in the north of the Mardakert rayon and in the eastern Martuni rayon where sub-units of the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic [NKR] defense army are holding out against enemy advances, and is inflicting substantial losses of personnel and equipment. The Azeri Army losses suffered in the fighting on 7 January were more than 80 men and officers, two tanks, and one armored vehicle. There are also losses on the Karabakh side. During the night and in the morning of 8 January the situation was relatively calm on all fronts. According to information from the NKR defense army headquarters, the Azeri command is drawing up new forces in order to resume its advance on the whole front. This was a Russian Information Agency report.
