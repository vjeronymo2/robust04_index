September 10, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLEWATCH

So Sly: Sylvester Stallone will have a premiere tonight, but not of a new film. Stallone's first show as an artist opens at the Hanson Gallery on Rodeo Drive in Beverly Hills. The exhibit includes more than 20 works by Stallone; part of the proceeds will benefit the "Big Green" initiative. BETH KLEID
