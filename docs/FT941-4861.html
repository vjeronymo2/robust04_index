940309 FT 09 MAR 94 / UK Company News: HTR Jap Smaller HTR Japanese Smaller Companies Trust, the Henderson Touche Remnant investment trust which came to the market last autumn, reported net assets per share of 100p on January 31 against 96.1p on the October 22 launch date. Earnings per share were 0.2p. Total net assets stood at Pounds 100.2m and retained revenue at Pounds 203,000.
