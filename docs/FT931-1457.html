930325 FT 25 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Umeco makes Pounds 1m rights UMECO, the USM-quoted manufacturer of aerospace equipment, yesterday signalled its intention to push for growth by announcing a Pounds 1m rights issue and the appointment of a high-profile new backer to the board. Its shares jumped 8p to 36p on the announcement that Mr Osman Abdullah, who runs the Starmin quarrying and engineering group, is to buy 2.68m shares in Umeco and will join the board as a non-executive director. Mr Abdullah and his brother Ahmed have agreed not to make a general offer for Umeco or increase the shareholding to 30 per cent or more for at least the next two years. Existing shareholders are being offered up to 1.04m new shares at 33p per share by way of the non-underwritten one for six rights issue.
