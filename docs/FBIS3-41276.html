Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES, 8/2/1994 -- The ruling in the trial of a dozen people charged with narcotics trafficking was postponed for February 22, current year. The group formed by Romanian and foreign citizens, including Alexandru Sava Dogmanov thought to be the head of the gang, was arrested last summer with the uncovering at the Otopeni Airport in Bucharest of about 100 kilos of cocaine hidden in two statuettes coming from Venezuela. Police said it identified and arrested the whole gang of drug smugglers.
