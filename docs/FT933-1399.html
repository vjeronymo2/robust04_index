930924 FT 24 SEP 93 / EC laws 'hamper competitiveness' EUROPEAN directives are hampering companies' competitiveness and the UK government has compounded the problem by its zealousness in interpreting EC law, Sir Bryan Nicholson, deputy president of the Confederation of British Industry, said last night. The EC has become a slow growth area, yet instead of concentrating on competitiveness, the European machine has gone on 'pumping out directives', he said.
