931005 FT 05 OCT 93 / Observer: Backlog Light at last on one of the mysteries of British Rail's operations - why, whenever it alters its running schedules, the new timetables are never available to travellers until after the changes have been put into force. Asking the question at a suburban ticket office, a reader was told: 'It's the timetables department; they work to a 25-hour clock.'
