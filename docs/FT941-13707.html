940124 FT 24 JAN 94 / World News in Brief: Actors die Jean-Louis Barrault, one of France's greatest 20th century actor-directors, died in Paris, aged 83. Telly Savalas, the actor best known as the wise-cracking, lollipop-sucking detective in the 1970s television series Kojak, died of prostate cancer in Los Angeles. He was 70.
