930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / International Company News: Saga Petroleum, Wintershall TALKS between Saga Petroleum of Norway and Germany's Wintershall on Saga taking a stake in Wintershall's gas pipelines have included issues such as merging the two firms, Reuter reports from Oslo. According to a source in the international petroleum industry who declined to be identified, the talks also had touched upon Saga and Wintershall possibly taking a stake in each other.
