920228 FT 28 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Bellwinch Sharply reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 385,000 were announced by Bellwinch, the Wembley-based builder, for the six months to December 31. The loss last time was Pounds 13.6m. Turnover was lower at Pounds 6.01m (Pounds 8.82m) but, at the operating level, the company made profits of Pounds 115,000 (Pounds 333,000 losses). Interest took Pounds 500,000 (Pounds 1.77m) and there were provisions of Pounds 11.5m last time for future development losses. Losses per share fell to 1.2p (38.3p).
