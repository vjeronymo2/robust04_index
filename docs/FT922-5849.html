920528 FT 28 MAY 92 / Liquidator for investment firm THE Securities and Investments Board has appointed a provisional liquidator of RCI Portfolio Managers, a member of the Financial Intermediaries and Brokers Regulatory Authority. RCI, run by Mr Andrew Clive, had about 50 clients and investments of about Pounds 2.5m. According to SIB, the firm appears to be insolvent. In March, SIB directed RCI, which operated from Putney, London, not to carry on further investment business.
