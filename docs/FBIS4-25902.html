BFN [Text] Beijing, June 16 (XINHUA) -- A delegation of the Supreme Soviet of the Republic of Tajikistan headed by its First Vice-Chairman Dosjiyev [spelling of name as received] will pay an official goodwill visit to China from June 21 to 28, as guests of China's National People's Congress Standing Committee. A spokesman for the Chinese Foreign Ministry made this announcement at the weekly press conference here today.
