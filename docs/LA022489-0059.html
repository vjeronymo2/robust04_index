February 24, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

CSUN TO PLAY HOST TO USC IN VOLLEYBALL

Cal State Northridge, still seeking its first victory in the Western Intercollegiate Volleyball Assn., will play host to defending national champion USC tonight and UC Irvine on Saturday in men's volleyball matches. Both matches begin at 7:30.

The Matadors (3-6 overall, 0-6 in conference play) lost last weekend at UC Santa Barbara and Stanford.
