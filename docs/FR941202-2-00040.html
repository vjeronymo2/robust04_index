SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: The Nevada Solar Enterprise Zone Task Force to the Advisory Committee on Demonstration and Commercial Application of Renewable Energy and Energy Efficiency Technologies advises the Department of Energy on making the best use of the solar resource in southern Nevada. The Task Force will seek public input and focus attention on significant issues, develop solutions to problems, organize public and private support for implementing a solar enterprise zone, and incorporate appropriate Task Force findings and results into a final report and recommendations for implementing a solar enterprise zone.
