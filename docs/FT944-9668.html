941115 FT 15 NOV 94 / World Trade News: Qatar National Cement - Contracts and Ventures Publicly traded Qatar National Cement has signed a letter of intent with FCB, a subsidiary of France's Fives-lille, to build a 456m riyal (Dollars 126.6m) cement factory to boost production. The factory, with a daily capacity of 2,000 tonnes, would be completed in 28 months.
