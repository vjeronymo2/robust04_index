April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

The telecast of "Why, Charlie Brown, Why?" was certainly television at its finest.

Instead of the usual frivolous and silly things for children, here was a half-hour of sensitivity and information done with the ever-popular Peanuts gang. Any family with a seriously ill child must be very grateful for this wonderful way to teach children, and all of us, not to shut out the sick. Congratulations to Charles M. Schulz, the CBS network, the sponsors and the medical advisers. We are all better for their caring efforts.

Pat Locher, Chatsworth
