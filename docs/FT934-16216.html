931007 FT 07 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Brasway's Pounds 0.8m expansion Brasway has purchased Vanox, which blends and distributes industrial and automotive lubricants and trades as British Benzol Lubricants, for a maximum Pounds 770,000 in cash. In the 1992 year, Vanox, based in Barnsley, incurred a deficit of Pounds 85,000 before tax, group charges and exceptional items, on turnover of Pounds 4.7m.
