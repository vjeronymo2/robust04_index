April 27, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

ANIMAL SYMPATHIZERS 'BITE BACK'

What right has E. Edward Kavanaugh, president of the Cosmetic, Toiletry & Fragrance Assn., to speak of animal rights compared to a child's life?

His association's members use animals in experiments for perfumes, cosmetics and soap -- horrific experiments where the animals suffer dreadfully; experiments that have nothing to do with saving a child's life.

Let the CTFA use the million dollars put aside to fight animal rights on finding more appropriate humane research techniques.

PAMELA W. ELLIS

Woodland Hills
