941206 FT 06 DEC 94 / People: Finance moves Takumi Shibata, formerly md of investment banking division of NOMURA, based in London, has been appointed executive vice-president of Nomura Project Finance International in Hong Kong. He is succeeded by Hiromi Yamaji. Kozo Yamazoe has been promoted to md of corporate finance within the investment banking division and Sam Berwick and Michael Bloomfield promoted to mds in the debt markets division.
