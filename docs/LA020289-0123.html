February 2, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

49ER WOMEN CAGERS TO PLAY HOST TO SAN JOSE STATE TONIGHT

The Cal State Long Beach women's basketball team will play San Jose State at 7:30 tonight in the university gym.

Coming off an 85-81 victory at Nevada-Las Vegas on Monday night, the 49ers have won seven games in a row. They are 14-4 overall and 8-0 in the Big West Conference.

Penny Toler leads the 49ers with an 18.2-point average, followed by Traci Waites, averaging 17.7, and Shameil Coleman, averaging 11.4.

The 49ers are averaging 95 points a game in the league.

San Jose is 3-14 overall, 0-8 in the Big West and has lost seven straight.

Long Beach will play another home game Saturday night against Fresno State.
