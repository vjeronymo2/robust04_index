BFN [Text] The suspension of central heating in our homes has not yet accelerated the clearance of our debt to Russia's Gazprom. As we were told by the Belarusian State Company for Gas Transportation and Delivery Beltranshaz, over the first three and a half months of this year, we amassed 165 billion rubles [R] in debt, while our total [word indistinct] now stands at R331 billion. We will likely be able to service our debts with gas suppliers only after unification with the Russian monetary system. For a while, our republic receives energy resources in full volume, though, in line with the agreement, we should clear our debts by 1 June because, beginning 1 July, gas will be delivered to us only according to advance payments.
