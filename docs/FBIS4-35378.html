BFN [Reports by 'Ali Salih in London and AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT bureau in Tunis: "'Arafat Releases His Wife's Uncle"] [Excerpt] Palestinian sources within the PLO have confirmed that the night before last 'Arafat released George Hawwa, the uncle of his wife Suha whom he had ordered arrested five days ago for interrogation in connection with a corruption case. The sources said that George Hawwa, the businessman who lives in London, was accused of complicity in a bribery case involving foreign companies he tried to conclude contracts with in the name of the PLO. Other sources in Tunis say that he had sent messages to the trade attaches at foreign embassies in Tunis informing them of the formation of an investment company called "Parly" [as transliterated] and that he was ready to make deals with them for projects in areas in the autonomy region and in the occupied territory.
