BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 13 (KCNA) -- Vice-premier Chang Chol today met and conversed in a friendly atmosphere with Ssyassi Sereyvuth Pannara, deputy secretary of state for Ministry of Art of the Kingdom of Cambodia, at the Mansudae Assembly Hall. Noting the intimate relations established between the great leader President Kim Il-song and his Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk of Cambodia will be everlasting, the deputy secretary expressed firm solidarity with the Korean people's cause of national reunification. Present there were Vice-minister of Culture and Art Yun Kyong-nam and Cambodian Ambassador to Korea Oum Mannorine.
