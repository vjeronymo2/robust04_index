Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] [Announcer] Over the last 24 hours fierce fighting has been going on nonstop on the southeast front in Nagorno-Karabakh, in the direction of Fizuli and Agdam. [Unidentified correspondent] According to the press service of the army of the Nagorno-Karabakh republic, yet another attempt has been made to break through a 60-kilometer section of the front in the direction of Agdam. After fierce fighting the people of Karabakh managed to push the enemy back to their original positions. At present the Karabakh positions along the whole line of the southeast front are under concentrated rocket and artillery shelling from the Azerbaijani artillery. The accumulation of the enemy's manpower and combat equipment is under way. [Announcer] Meanwhile, Azerbaijani sources report that the offensive is developing successfully. According to the information of the republic's Defense Ministry, the government forces are 10 kilometers away from the rayon center of Agdam.
