BFN [Text] Commander of the Ukrainian Navy Volodymyr Bezkorovaynyy accused the Black Sea Fleet commanders of removing property from the garrisons, which are to belong to Ukraine. He told INTERFAX-Ukraine on Wednesday [15 June] that the Black Sea Fleet commanders hoped to make the property of the Black Sea Fleet Russia's property. "The removal of the property will inflict direct damage on the economic interests of Ukraine," said Bezkorovaynyy. In his opinion, this question must be discussed during the next round of the Ukrainian-Russian talks on the division of the Black Sea Fleet. He also announced that the headquarters of the Ukrainian Navy were considering the parameters of the Russian fleet's temporary stay in Sevastopol. He said that the discussion of the division of the logistics property continued.
