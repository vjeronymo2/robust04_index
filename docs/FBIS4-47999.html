BFN [Report by Micel Schnehage] [Text] Johannesburg May 6 SAPA -- Police have uncovered an alleged rightwing plot to assassinate African National Congress PWV [Pretoria, Witwatersrand, Vereeniging] Premier-Designate Tokyo Sexwale. Spokesman Lt-Col Eugene Opperman confirmed on Friday [6 May] police had been informed of a plan to assassinate Mr Sexwale, but he would not divulge further details. Col Opperman said police had informed the ANC of the situation and "certain steps have been taken". The plot apparently involved murdering Mr Sexwale on Friday night or on Saturday. The PWV Provincial Legislature will sit for the first time on Saturday to elect the region's premier. Mr Sexwale is widely tipped to fill the post. ANC sources close to Mr Sexwale said the preier-designate was taking the threat "very seriously" and that the necessary security measures had been taken. ANC spokesman Ronnie Mamoepa told SAPA the ANC was working closely with police to ensure the safety of Mr Sexwale.
