BFN [From the "Union Radio News" newscast] [Text] According to Foreign Minister Miguel Angel Burelli Rivas, the election of a new Colombian president will not change relations between Venezuela and Colombia. Burelli has said Venezuela is quite pleased with the results of the Colombian election in which Liberal Party candidate Ernesto Samper was elected president. The foreign minister said the fact that the marine and submarine area delimitation issue in the Gulf of Venezuela had not been discussed as part of the election campaign is a very encouraging sign for bilateral relations. The new Colombian president, Ernesto Samper, will take office on 7 August.
