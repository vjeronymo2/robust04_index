941031 FT 31 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Daks Simpson rises to Pounds 1.95m Daks Simpson Group, the clothing maker acquired by Sankyo Seiko in 1991, reported improved pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.95m on turnover of Pounds 36.1m for the six months to July 31. Profits last time amounted to Pounds 1.21m on sales of Pounds 30.3m. Earnings per share rose from 9.82p to 14.59p. The production capacity of the two Scottish factories has been increased with the creation of 250 jobs.
