930305 FT 05 MAR 93 / Commodities and Agriculture: Cocoa prices fall again London cocoa prices again fell sharply yesterday on market pessimism over the Geneva talks, writes David Blackwell. The London May contract, which shed Pounds 21 on Wednesday, fell a further Pounds 22 to close at the day's low of Pounds 705 a tonne. 'It was mainly disillusion with the ICCO talks,' said Mr Tony Chadwick of Prudential Bache. 'You would have thought a Pounds 40 drop in the market might have encouraged producers to be more realistic and set their sights a little bit lower.'
