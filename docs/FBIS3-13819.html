Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The Likud Knesset faction has requested an urgent plenum debate, during the Pesah recess, in order to discuss what it described as the government's far-reaching concessions, which endanger the status of Jerusalem and disregard the security needs of the Jewish settlement in Hebron. Our correspondent Yo'el Nir reports that Knesset Member David Mena' has handed the Knesset speaker a list with the 30 signatures required in order to convene the plenum during the recess. The NRP [National Religious Party] faction has announced that in reaction to Prime Minister Rabin's proposals, it will hold its next meeting tomorrow in Hebron.
