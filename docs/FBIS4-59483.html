BFN [Report by Basil Abu-Sumayah from the occupied territories] [Excerpt] U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher will be the first Western official at the foreign minister level to visit the Palestinian self-rule area in Jericho. Christopher is scheduled to arrive in the city on Tuesday [17 May] to meet, in the headquarters of the Palestinian National Security Forces, with the commander of these forces, Brigadier Hajj Isma'il; senior Palestinian officers; and leading Palestinian figures, including Dr. Sa'ib 'Urayqat, a member of the Palestinian authority. The U.S. secretary will also meet with Palestinian figures in East Jerusalem. In a related development, Dr. Hanan 'Ashrawi, former spokeswoman of the Palestinian delegation has stressed that she will not participate in the Palestinian authority that is charged with the running of the Gaza sector and the Jericho area. She told our radio that she will give priority to the Independent Palestinian Commission for Citizens Rights. 'Ashrawi is the secretary general of that commission. 'Ashrawi urged Palestinians, including the opposition, to join in building Palestinian society, both in the national authority, the administrative agencies, and the institutions. She said members of the authority must have competence and credibility and be able to show commitment and will power so they can win the approval of all segments of Palestinian society. She said: There must be an integrated plan for reconstitution with the participation of all. No one can escape their responsibilities and the future challenges. [passage omitted]
