BFN [Text] At 0925 on 25 May, Samdech Chea Sim, acting head of state and chairman of the Kingdom of Cambodia's National Assembly, left Phnom Penh for Pyongyang, DPRK, to attend a roundtable meeting to be held at the royal palace Chhanghsu-on. Seeing the samdech off at Pochentong Airport were several excellencies and mesdames who are members of the National Assembly and the Royal Government of Cambodia. Also present were the ambassadors of North Korea and the PRC and their wives.
