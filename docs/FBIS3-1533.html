Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Members of the international observer group who have been in different parts of Uganda to monitor the CA [Constituent Assembly] elections have expressed satisfaction with the way the exercise was conducted. The group, comprising women members from Canada, South Africa, Mozambique, Zambia and Zimbabwe made their observation today when they paid a courtesy call on President Yoweri Museveni at his home in (Rwakitura) Mbarara District.
