920124 FT 24 JAN 92 / World Commodities Prices: Fruit & Vegetables Cape Sultana grapes at Pounds 1.30-1.40 a lb and Cape Thompson grapes are this weeks best buy reports the FFVIB. Also good are bananas at 45-70p a lb, clementines at 40-55p a lb and Seville oranges at 35-45p a lb. English pears have had a good season and are excellent this week with Conference at 40-55p a lb and Comice at 45-60p a lb. Spania broccoli is good at 50-60 per 8oz pre-pack together with carrots at 10-20p a lb.
