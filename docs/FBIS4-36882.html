BFN [Text] His Excellency Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, the president, received Lakhdar Brahimi, the UN secretary general's envoy, at the al-Jirf Palace this morning. Brahimi briefed his excellency on the results of his efforts to end the Yemeni crisis and implement the UN Security Council resolution calling for an immediate cease-fire between the two northern and southern parties in Yemen, and for the resumption of dialogue between them.
