920910 FT 10 SEP 92 / Observer: Bottomed out Let's hope the currency upheavals don't consign the commemorative banknote Scotland is issuing for December's EC summit in Edinburgh to the fate of Romania's fast depreciating lei notes. In a mood of deep contemplation the other morning, a Bucharest student examined a piece of the toilet roll he'd recently bought. It had obviously been recycled from 100-lei notes, the largest denomination produced until the Communist regime's collapse in 1989.
