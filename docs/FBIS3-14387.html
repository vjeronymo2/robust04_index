Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Mr. Salim Saadi, minister of interior and local authorities, emphasized yesterday in Constantine that the state had mobilized resources for putting an end to the phenomenon of armed violence against citizens, security men, and state institutions. While chairing a regional meeting, which included 15 governors and a number of local officials, he said that there would be no return to the course of elections as long as absolute security and the final elimination of sabotage had not been established.
