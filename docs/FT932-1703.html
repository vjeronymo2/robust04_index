930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Kaufman attacks Labour 'meddling' FORMER Labour front bencher Mr Gerald Kaufman yesterday issued a blunt message to party leader Mr John Smith to end the 'constant meddling' with Labour's constitution. In an open letter to Mr Smith in the Evening Standard, Mr Kaufman declared: 'Since Labour started tampering with its constitution, every general election has been lost.'
