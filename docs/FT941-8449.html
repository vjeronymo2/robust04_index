940219 FT 19 FEB 94 / Commodities and Agriculture: Spices White pepper supplies in producing countries are set to remain tight at least until August. Reports Man Producten. Meanwhile, in a week of limited acivity becasue of the Chinese new year festivities and the Latin American carnaval the spot price in Rotterdam was quoted at USDollars 2,825 a tonne, nearby shipment at Dollars 2,700, cif, and new crop at Dollars 2,575. Black pepper remained steady. Prices in India, which has hardly any carry-over stock and is suffering from small, delayed arrivals of new crop, remained firm. Indonesia could offer only limited quantities. Brazil's local market remained firm in the absence of export activities. India MG-1 was quited at Dollars 1,625 a tonne, cif, for March/April shipment; faq spot was at Dollars 1,525, while nearby shipment was fetching Dollars 1,450, cif.
