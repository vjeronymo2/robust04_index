July 27, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

PBS has lined up President and Mrs. Bush for their first extended TV interview since he took office. "The President and Mrs. Bush Talking With David Frost: A Unique Perspective From Kennebunkport" will be aired Sept. 5, PBS announced Wednesday. The program will include a review of Bush's term so far. Frost in the past has interviewed former Presidents Kennedy and Nixon, among other political leaders. ALEENE MacMINN
