May 31, 1989, Wednesday, P.M. Final

LOCAL;

ELVIS' DAUGHTER HAS BABY GIRL

Elvis Presley's daughter gave birth to the late king of rock 'n' roll's grandchild -- a 7-pound, 2-ounce girl -- at St. John's Hospital in Santa Monica on Monday, a spokesman for the couple said today.

With musician-husband Danny Keough at her side, Lisa Marie Presley delivered the girl by natural childbirth at 8 a.m. Monday, said publicist Paul Bloch. The child hasn't been named, he said.

"Both mother and child are in excellent health," Bloch said, adding that actress Priscilla Beaulieu Presley, now a grandmother, is thrilled. "Everyone is very happy."

Lisa Presley, 21, married Keough, 23, last Oct. 3 during a small, private service at the huge Hollywood headquarters of the Church of Scientology.

Elvis died in his Memphis home in 1977 of a heart attack and apparent drug overdose. Lisa is due to inherit his estate, estimated at over $50 million, when she is 30.
