BFN [Text] Tehran, May 25, IRNA -- The grand `Ra'd 7' (Thunder) military exercise ended in Darengon region of Shiraz, in southern Fars Province, Wednesday [25 May] morning in the presence of senior military officials. The military exercise was launched on May 22, marking the 12th anniversary of the liberation of the port city of Khorramshahr from Iraqi occupation. Acting Commander of the Ground Forces General Karim Ebadat while inspecting the maneuver told IRNA that the military exercise was aimed at increasing the combat power of the forces. Various infantry and artillery units of the army, equipped with light and heavy weapons, participated in the military exercise.
