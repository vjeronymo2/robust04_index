940730 FT 30 JUL 94 / Finance and the Family: Smaller companies cheer - At a glance Smaller company shares had another more positive week. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) climbed 0.8 per cent to 1,652.76 over the week to July 28. It is still down 2.3 per cent since the beginning of the year, but has suffered less than the FT-SE-A All-share index, which is down 7.8 per cent.
