December 6, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

SITE OF WHITTIER CHRISTIAN GAME MOVED

The Southern Section Division IX football playoff game between Whittier Christian and Tehachapi will be played at 7:30 p.m. Saturday at Bakersfield College.

The game had been scheduled at Tehachapi High School.

Whittier Christian (12-0) won the Olympic League title with a 9-0 record. Tehachapi (12-1) was the Desert Inyo League champion at 9-1.
