BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondent] [Text] Beijing, May 27 (TASS) -- An agreement on the regime of the Russo-Chinese state border was signed here on Friday by Viktor Chernomyrdin, the prime minister of the Russian Federation, and Li Peng, premier of China's Council of State, at the close of Russo- Chinese talks held by the official delegations of the two countries. The two sides also signed another six intergovernmental documents: an agreement on the prevention of double taxation and income tax evasion, a protocol of the second session of the Russo- Chinese intergovernmental commission on technical, economic, scientific and technical cooperation, agreements on economic, scientific and technical cooperation in the agro-industrial centre, on cooperation in sea shipping, environmental protection, and on protection, regulation and reproduction of the living resources in the border sections of the Amur and Ussuri rivers.
