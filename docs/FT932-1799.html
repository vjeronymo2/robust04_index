930622 FT 22 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Siam Selective Interest charges up from Pounds 54,487 to Pounds 351,413 left Siam Selective Growth Trust with an after-tax loss of Pounds 201,117 for the year ended March 31, against Pounds 23,259 previously. Gross revenue rose from Pounds 536,622 to Pounds 697,442. After tax of Pounds 83,435 (Pounds 56,939), deficit per share grew from 0.16p to 1.34p. The dividend, however, is lifted from 0.25p to 1p.
