Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] The KINH DOANH VA PHAP LUAT (Business and Law) journal has recently reported that goods smuggling has been increasing since early this year. Concerned agencies recently detected two out of a group of eight foreign cargo ships carrying smuggled goods. Drug trafficking has also been on the increase in recent days. In the first two months of this year, the authorities uncovered 17 cases of trafficking and confiscated 450 kg of opium and 0.72 kg of heroin. The most serious was the smuggling of 129 kg of opium by cadres of the Dien Bien goldsmith shop in Lai Chau Province. It is noteworthy that the manufacturing and trading of fake goods done in meticulous ways are also widespread. Police nationwide have so far detected and dealt with 2,700 cases. This is a new challenge for responsible agencies in the struggle against smuggling.
