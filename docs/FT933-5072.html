930906 FT 06 SEP 93 / Committee absences at 50% NON-ATTENDANCE at many of the TUC's key committees is running at well over 50 per cent and reaching as high as 80 per cent for the education and training committee. That is the finding of an informal survey by Mr Roger Lyons of the MSF white-collar union. Mr Lyons, whose survey was based on three meetings of each committee in the past year, said that the figures proved the need for a radical reform of the committee structure.
