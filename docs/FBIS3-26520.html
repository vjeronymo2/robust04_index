Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] At a recent provincial meeting on foreign affairs, Governor He Zhiqiang, on behalf of the provincial party committee and government, demanded the enhancement of foreign affairs work so as to reach a new level, and the creation of a favorable external environment for Yunnan's modernization, reform, and opening up. Governor He Zhiqiang pointed out: The international situation, and particularly the situation in Southeast Asia, now is quite favorable to Yunnan's economic construction, reform, and opening up. Yunnan is facing the best development opportunity in history. He Zhiqiang asked foreign affairs cadres to uphold the party's basic line and to focus on economic construction in their work. He pointed out, in particular: Foreign affairs and foreign economic relations must be combined organically and made to promote each other. On carrying out friendly cooperation with neighboring countries, He Zhiqiang proposed fulfilling the following six tasks: The first is to further develop good-neighborly relations and strengthen high-level friendly exchanges with neighboring countries. The second is to carry out cooperative projects with neighboring countries and to promote mutually beneficial cooperation. The third is to speed up highway construction. The fourth is to invite economic and trade delegations from neighboring countries and from other Southeast Asian countries to visit Yunnan to seek opportunities for common development. The fifth is to sponsor the Second Kunming Commodity Fair. The sixth is to take effective measures to encourage more Yunnan enterprises and companies to invest in neighboring countries.
