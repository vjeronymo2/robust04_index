BFN [Text] Beijing, May 28 (XINHUA) -- General Chi Haotian, Chinese state councillor and minister of national defense, met with V. T. Penchev, deputy minister of national defense of Bulgaria, and his party here today. Chi briefed the guests on the construction of China's economy and its armed forces. Penchev spoke highly of the achievements China has made since its reform and opening to the outside. He also expressed the hope that the two countries and the two armed forces further their exchanges and cooperation. Penchev ended his goodwill visit and left by air for home this afternoon.
