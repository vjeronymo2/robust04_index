920521 FT 21 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Ferguson Intl declines 43% to Pounds 6.63m Adversely affected by trading conditions world-wide, but particularly in the UK, Ferguson International Holdings saw pre-tax profits fall 43 per cent from Pounds 11.6m to Pounds 6.63m in the year to February 29. Group trading profits on continuing activities were down 20 per cent to Pounds 9.7m, an improvement on the 28 per cent reduction at halfway. Boosted by two acquisitions - Interstate Cable Enterprises of the US and Globus Druck of Germany - turnover on continuing activities rose 6 per cent to Pounds 114.9m. Interest payable was cut by 56 per cent to Pounds 1.1m (Pounds 2.54m) and there were exceptional costs of Pounds 1.6m relating mainly to the closure of Harkwell Labels & Tags. Extraordinary charges of Pounds 1.14m derived from the disposal of non-core businesses. Earnings emerged at 13.5p (22.7p) per share and the directors have proposed holding the final dividend at 8.25p for a maintained 12.5p total.
