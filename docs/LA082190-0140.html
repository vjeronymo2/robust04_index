August 21, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

OptimumCare Corp.: The Laguna Niguel operator of psychiatric care and chemical dependency programs reported a net loss of $231,402 for the second quarter, compared to a year-earlier loss of $71,091.

Revenue declined 25% to $288,989 from $383,088. For the first half of 1990, the company lost $288,978, compared to a loss of $27,545 for the corresponding period in 1989. Revenue fell 26% to $646,501 from $873,948.
