BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Yekaterinburg, 12 Apr -- An unidentified device exploded early this morning inside the building of the Verkh-Isetskiy Rayon courthouse in Yekaterinburg. There are no dead or injured. A wall of the building, two courtrooms and a guardroom were destroyed. No details have been given. An investigation is in progress.
