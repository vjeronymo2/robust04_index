930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There was good general demand reports the Tea Broker's Association. Bright liquoring East Africans and good medium descriptions were 5 to 10p easier with quality. Plainer sorts and selected mediums remained firm and sometimes dearer. Dusts proved a weak feature throughout. Offshore good demand but brighter Kenyas tended easier. Central African teas were well supported at fully firm rates. The highest price realised this week was 175p for a Rwanda pf. 1 and pd. Quotations: quality 160p/kg, nom good medium 145p/kg, medium 135p/kg, low medium 110p/kg.
