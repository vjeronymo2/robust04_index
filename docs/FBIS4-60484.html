BFN [Text] The finance minister, Dr. Manmohan Singh, has said that the successful outcome of the Rao-Clinton summit has vindicated that the prime minister's visit to the United States was in the national interest. Speaking to reporters in London, he said both the U.S. Government and captains of American trade and industry have welcomed India's economic reforms program and the strategy integrating its economy with the global economy. Dr. Singh said there is a lot of enthusiasm in the United States about India's prospects as a large and emerging market offering great opportunities for American investments.
