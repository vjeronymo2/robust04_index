Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Cyprus Committee of Solidarity With Kurdistan revealed that in the past Theofilos Yeoryiadhis received many telephone calls from people speaking Turkish who threatened to kill him. It was also revealed that, according to information at the committee's disposal, one of the next possible targets of Turkish secret agents is former National Guard Commander Lieutenant General Dhimitrios Matafias, who is considered to play a leading role in Greek activities in support of the Kurds. The committee has briefed the Greek police about this information. These revelations were made by members of the Committee of Solidarity With Kurdistan at a news conference this afternoon on the abominable assassination of Theofilos Yeoryiadhis. The news conference was given in the House of Representatives building in the presence of representatives from all the parliamentary parties, which condemned the assassination and praised the personality of the fighter for human rights, Theofilos Yeoryiadhis.
