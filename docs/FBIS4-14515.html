BFN [Text] Kiev [no dateline as received] -- On 31 March, Canada's Minister of Foreign Affairs Andre Ouellet had a one-hour meeting with Ukraine's President Leonid Kravchuk during which they discussed issues pertaining to deepening friendly relations between the two countries. After the meeting, Andre Ouellet told journalists that the Canadian Government allocated $2.5 million for medicine and education in Ukraine, $4 million for humanitarian aid, and $3 million for the Red Cross Society. Other payments will also be made. Asked to comment on the results of the elections in Ukraine, Andre Ouellet pointed out that in the opinion of Canadian observers, the elections were democratic enough and without gross violations.
