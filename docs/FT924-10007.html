921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / Premier Major holds series of private meetings PRIME MINISTER John Major continued the government's charm offensive, holding the latest in a series of private meetings with groups of a dozen or more Tory MPs. Although the majority of yesterday's group were young MPs with serious doubts about the Maastricht process, there were also more experienced ones with few such qualms. Mr Major used the hour-long meeting to allow backbenchers ask about aspects of Maastricht that particularly concerned them. One newly elected MP who will vote with the government on Wednesday described the prime minister's performance as 'very cogent'.
