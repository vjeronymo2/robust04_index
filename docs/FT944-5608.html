941202 FT 02 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Mid Kent Holdings Pre-tax profits at Mid Kent Holdings were static in the first half to September 30, due to difficult trading conditions in its subsidiary. Turnover at the group, which supplies water to Kent and Sussex and provides consultancy services to the water supply industry, rose 11 per cent to Pounds 18.2m (Pounds 16.4m). However, despite progress in the water company's performance, difficult trading conditions experienced by Cyfas-Restbury, acquired last year, held group pre-tax profits to Pounds 3.83m (Pounds 3.8m). The interim dividend has been raised to 5.3p (5p) from earnings per share of 16.3p (16.7p).
