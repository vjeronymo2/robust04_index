BFN [Excerpt] San Salvador de Jujuy, 5 Apr (NA) -- This provincial capital today again experienced a chaotic situation after new clashes between the police and public servants were reported. The clashes left a toll of at least a dozen injured people and about 20 detainees, including Carlos "Dog" Santillan, the Trade Union of Municipal Employees and Workers [Sindicato de Empleados y Obreros Municipales] (SEOM) leader. Trade union leader Santillan, who is among those conducting the mobilizations being carried out by public servants to receive salary hikes, was arrested at noon today, and his whereabouts remained unknown for several hours.
