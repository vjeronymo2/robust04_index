BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondents Yuriy Sizov and Vladimir Malyshev] [Text] Kerkira (Corfu), 24 Jun -- "We benefit from this agreement," Russian President Boris Yeltsin said on the Greek island of Kerkira where he signed today an important document -- the agreement on partnership and cooperation between Russia and the European Union. Speaking to journalists immediately after the signing ceremony, he said that we are enter free trade in Europe as equals without any discrimination which existed in the past and which continues to exist in some other countries outside Europe. Therefore, it is a wise and strong move by the European community, by all its leaders and heads of state. The Russian president stressed: I think that a historic step has been made today which will not permit an economic cold war.
