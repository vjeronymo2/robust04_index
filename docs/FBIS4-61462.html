BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Anna Kurbanova] [Text] Ashgabat 5 May TASS -- President Saparmurad Niyazov of Turkmenistan and Kazakh President Nursultan Nazarbayev had a telephone conversation on Wednesday [4 May] night, the "TURKMENSKAYA ISKRA" daily writes on Thursday. The two leaders agreed to hold as soon as possible a meeting between heads of the Caspian countries to discuss various regional issues. President Nazarbayev confirmed that Kazakhstan intended to participate in an international consortium, which would build gas and oil pipelines from Turkmenistan to the countries of Persian Gulf. The two presidents also discussed ways of developing bilateral interstate relations on the basis of mutual benefit.
