November 30, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: REAL ESTATE;

NOTES

* Amplicon Inc.: The Santa Ana firm's board of directors has authorized the purchase of up to 300,000 of its approximately 6 million common shares outstanding. Between June 30, 1988, and March 7, 1990, Amplicon repurchased about 600,000 shares of its common stock. Amplicon leases and sells high-tech equipment.
