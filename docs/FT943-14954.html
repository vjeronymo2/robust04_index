940712 FT 12 JUL 94 / UK Company News: Shield incurs Pounds 188,900 loss Shield Diagnostics Group, the biotechnology group which came to market last September, reported a pre-tax loss of Pounds 188,900 in the period from its incorporation in June 23 to March 31. Turnover was Pounds 1.67m. Losses per share came out at 1.41p. Shield Diagnostics Ltd, acquired in July, announced a pre-tax loss of Pounds 326,000 (Pounds 653,000) for the year to end March. Turnover was Pounds 2.2m (Pounds 1.68m), with Pounds 131,000 contributed by the medical division of Cortecs Diagnostics, acquired last November.
