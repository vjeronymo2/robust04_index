930401 FT 01 APR 93 / UK Company News: Arcon Intl Pre-tax losses at Arcon International Resources, the Irish natural resources company, rose from IPounds 702,000 to IPounds 1.21m for the year to August 31. Turnover improved to IPounds 863,000 (IPounds 21,000) and losses per share widened to 2.34p (1.68p).
