January 24, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MAN CHARGED IN BEATING DEATH OF N.Y. TRANSIENT

City officials searched Tuesday for the identity of a homeless man who police said was beaten to death on a subway platform in an altercation with a man returning home from a movie with his 3-year-old son.

Rodney Sumter, a 39-year-old plumber, was charged with manslaughter and released without bail. Police said that on Sunday night the homeless man spat toward the Sumters, then took a swing at the elder Sumter and missed. A fight started and police said Sumter began pummeling the other man.
