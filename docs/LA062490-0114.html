June 24, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: $1,000 SCHOLARSHIPS GO TO 2 FOR MEDICAL STUDIES

The San Gabriel Valley Medical Center Auxiliary has awarded $1,000 scholarships to Mario Flores of San Gabriel High School, who will attend Cal State Long Beach, and Thai Duong, a graduate of Temple City High who now attends UC Riverside.

Flores plans to become a physical therapist and Duong a physician.
