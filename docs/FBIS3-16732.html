Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] President of Kazakhstan Nursultan Nazarbayev met U.S. Secretary of Defense William Perry in Alma-Ata [Almaty] today. The head of the U.S. military department told journalists after the meeting that President Bill Clinton had charged him with paying particular attention in Alma-Ata to the implementation of programmes agreed during the recent visit by President Nazarbayev to the United States. This concerns primarily the deepening reinforcement of military cooperation between the two countries, assistance to Kazakhstan on conversion, research into the Semipalatinsk testing ground to establish the extent of the damage, and providing for medical assistance to the inhabitants of the Semipalatinsk region.
