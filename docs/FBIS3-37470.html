Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Evald Kessariyskiy and Vladimir Taranov] [Text] Nizhniy Novgorod February 16 TASS -- British Prime Minister John Major put forward a new initiative at a meeting with local entrepreneurs at the joint stock society "Nizhnegorodksya Yarmarka" on Wednesday [16 February] to open a business centre in Nizhniy Novgorod which will render technical assistance to businessmen. The British prime minister said that the move will promote people's personal interest in carrying out reforms. He pointed out that the best way for cooperation between Russia and Great Britain is the interaction between private sectors of the two countries. Later in the day major visited a voucher auction centre and attended an auction held there. On Wednesday evening the British prime minister left Nizhniy Novgorod for London.
