BFN [Report by Eli Bohadana] [Excerpt] A few hours ago, terrorists opened fire at the hothouses of Kfar Darom in the Gaza Strip, but nobody was hurt. An IDF [Israel Defense Forces] unit arrived on the scene and opened fire at the terrorists, who fled in two Peugeot 404's. Last night, the IDF set out in hot pursuit of a Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] terrorist squad into the area of the autonomy. The three terrorists who opened fire at an IDF patrol fled in the direction of Rafah. Our forces did not sustain any casualties. During the pursuit, hundreds of local inhabitants gathered and threw stones at the soldiers in a bid to prevent them from catching the perpetrators. According to Palestinian sources, five local Palestinians were injured by IDF gunfire during this pursuit. [passage omitted]
