921231 FT 31 DEC 92 / Public finance policy head named MR Robert Culpin was named yesterday as the new head of the Treasury's public finance command. He will take charge of fiscal and monetary policy in succession to Mr Andrew Turnbull, who becomes Treasury second permanent secretary in charge of public expenditure. Mr Culpin will be promoted to grade 2, or deputy secretary level in the Treasury. He has headed the fiscal policy group since 1987.
