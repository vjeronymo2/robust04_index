Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS Valeriy Shanayev] [Text] Vladikavkaz, 17 Jan -- Akhsarbek Galazov, chairman of the republic's Supreme Soviet, has become the first president of North Ossetia. The ITAR-TASS correspondent has just learned this from reliable sources. Approximately 55 percent of the total number of the electorate took part in the elections that were held on 16 January. More than 60 percent of the voters entered in the lists for the secret ballot voted for Galazov.
