BFN [Text] Lieutenant General Varden Nadibaidze, the Georgian defense minister, has ordered new appointments among the ministry's senior personnel. Major General Paata Datuashvili was released from the post of first deputy defense minister. The new appointment to this post is Major General Guram Nikolaishvili, who was up until now the chief of general staff. Major General Nodar Tatarashvili, who until recently was the commander of Georgian peacekeeping forces in Samachablo [South Ossetia], was appointed as chief of general staff.
