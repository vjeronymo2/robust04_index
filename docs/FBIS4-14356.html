BFN [Text] In the presence of His Highness Crown Prince Hasan, His Majesty King Husayn received at the Royal Hashemite Court this afternoon Khalid 'Abd-al-Mun'im Rashid al-Janabi, His Excellency Iraqi President Saddam Husayn's adviser and envoy. The Iraqi envoy conveyed to his Majesty King Husayn and the esteemed Hashemite family the condolences of His Excellency Iraqi President Saddam Husayn and the fraternal Iraqi people on the death of the late Queen Mother Zayn al-Sharaf. Al-Janabi prayed to God to bestow his mercy on the deceased, to permit her into his heavens, and to give his majesty and the esteemed Hashemite family patience and solace.
