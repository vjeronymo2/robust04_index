BFN [Text] Havana, 5 Jun (AFP) -- By Saturday night, Cuban authorities have still not confirmed reports from the United States that 61 Cuban refugees, fleeing Cuba by boat, were attacked by Cuban naval vessels, which resulted in the injury of seven people. The official Cuban press has still not released any information on this new incident, which took place at the same time 124 Cubans occupy the Belgian ambassador's residence in Havana. According to the passengers rescued south of Florida on Saturday by the U.S. Coast Guard, their boat was chased by Cuban military vessels following their departure from Mariel (40 km west of Havana), and they were fired upon. A coast guard spokesman had not confirmed by Saturday night the origin of the shots, stating only that 61 Cubans had been rescued from sea and that seven were wounded, four by gunshots.
