930329 FT 29 MAR 93 / Call for more training in Scotland JOB TRAINING in Scotland lags behind the rest of the UK, according to the Labour party. The Department of Employment's figures show that in Scotland only 9.3 per cent of employed people aged from 16 to 64 are in training compared with 11.4 per cent in the UK as a whole. Shadow Scottish affairs spokesman Mr Henry McLeish said Scottish Office ministers had shown 'sneering complacency' by their failure to meet their own youth training guarantee to more than 8,000 young Scots.
