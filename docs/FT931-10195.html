930210 FT 10 FEB 93 / Cambodia UN office attacked Prince Norodom Sihanouk, Cambodia's head of state, returned home yesterday as UN peacekeepers investigated an overnight attack on a UN office in the north-west of the country that left five Cambodians dead and 16 injured, AP reports from Phnom Penh. Forty unidentified troops launched a three-hour attack late Monday night in Bakan district, about 185km north-west of Phnom Penh.
