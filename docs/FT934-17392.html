931001 FT 01 OCT 93 / Former councillor jailed for fraud THE FORMER deputy leader of Derbyshire County Council was found guilty at Birmingham Crown Court yesterday of fraudulently claiming council expenses. Mr Sean Stafford and Mr Gerald Sexton, a solicitor, were both jailed for 18 months. Mr Sexton was said to have helped Mr Stafford draw up a false contract of employment enabling the former councillor to claim Pounds 13,000 expenses for wages lost while on council business. They had denied the charges.
