Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Israel's Military Industries is now providing weapons to Romania as part of a deal worth more than $90 million. Among the items, Israel is supplying tank and artillery shells said to be four times more effective than those manufactured in Eastern Europe. In an interview with a local ex-servicemen's magazine, Dan Shomron, chairman of the Military Industries, also revealed that a new detachable fuel tank had been developed locally for F-16 warplanes, increasing the flight range by a fifth.
