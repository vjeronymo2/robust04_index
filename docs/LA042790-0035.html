April 27, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

CLOTHESLINES

Q: I will be attending several formal events this spring and need a new evening gown. What's new, and right for a 5-foot-6 38-year-old? I wear a 38-C bra so don't suggest a strapless dress or one with a bustier as they only accentuate my big bosom.

-- P.A.W.

A: But that's what you're supposed to do this season. The most up-to-the-minute look for evening is the hourglass figure. After years of exile, curves are once more fashionable. It's a look right out of "Dangerous Liaisons" and one given a big push -- and push-up -- by Paris designer Yves Saint Laurent (illustrated here) in his latest haute couture collection. It could be the first fashion trend ever inspired by plastic surgery.

If you don't feel comfortable in a bustier or strapless gown, look for one with a scooped-out neckline. MARYLOU LUTHER
