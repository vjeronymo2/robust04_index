Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The Belarusian parliament constitutionally confirmed the additional powers granted to the government. The parliament's session on Tuesday adopted a decision under which the prime minister was granted the authority to alter the composition of the government, to appoint and discharge the government's members coordinating the decisions with the Supreme Soviet's presidium and the appropriate parliamentary commissions. However, the Supreme Council preserved the powers to appoint and to discharge the leaders of the power structures and the foreign minister, an Interfax correspondent in Minsk reported.
