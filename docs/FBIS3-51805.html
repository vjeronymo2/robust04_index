Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Ricardo Alarcon, president of the Cuban parliament, is on a visit to Brussels, capital of Belgium, with the objective of developing Cuba's relations with the members of the European and Belgian parliaments. During his first day, Alarcon today met with the chairman of the Foreign Relations Committee of the European Parliament, the president of the European people's party [del partido popular europeo], and the president of the Belgian Senate. The main subject discussed was the reincorporation of Cuba into the world economy, particularly with regard to relations with Europe.
