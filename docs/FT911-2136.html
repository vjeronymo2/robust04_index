910501 FT 01 MAY 91 / UK News (Employment): Pollution inspectors THE Pollution Inspectorate may face a dispute over a pay award, IPMS, the managers' and specialists' union, warned yesterday. New recruits to the post of pollution inspector have received pay rises of 18 per cent but existing principal inspectors have received only 4 per cent, says IPMS. The union is balloting members on the award. The Department of the Environment disputed the union's allegations.
