Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ukrainian Deputy Prime Minister Valeriy Shmarov has stated that the tripartite declaration on the elimination of nuclear weapons on Ukrainian territory and the accompanying appendix, which was just signed in Moscow, is an historic document which has laid the foundation for the actual process of ending Ukraine's economic and political isolation. In addition, the package of documents on denuclearization will enable Ukraine to receive international financial aid, including much-needed investment to help resolve conversion issues, overcome the economic crisis, and provide for the social protection of our people. Scientific studies on the dismantling of nuclear warheads and their secure withdrawal from Ukrainian territory have already been performed. This process will be completed in several stages and controlled by appropriate monitoring commissions.
