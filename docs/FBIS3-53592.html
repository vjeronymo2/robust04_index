Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] U.S. Secretary of Commerce Ronald Brown has arrived in Amman on the first visit by a U.S. trade chief to Jordan. Mr. Brown is scheduled to meet His Royal Highness Crown Prince Hasan, the regent, and Prime Minister 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali. Discussions will focus on exploring possibilities of enhanced economic cooperation between the U.S. and Jordan. Mr. Brown, who arrived from Saudi Arabia, is accompanied by a trade delegation, including administration officials and representatives of American firms. The commerce secretary has agreed that there had to be a better equilibrium in trade between the U.S. and Jordan. The Kingdom bought more than $300 million in American products in 1993, while exporting less than $5 million.
