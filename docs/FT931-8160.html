930220 FT 20 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Wessex Water wins Pounds 9.8m contract Wessex Water subsidiary, Wessex Waste Management, has, with Avon County Council, won a Pounds 9.8m per annum contract to dispose of the county of Avon's domestic waste. A new joint venture company, Avon Waste Management, has been formed which is 80 per cent owned by Wessex Waste Management. The company won all six contracts that were put out to competitive tender.
