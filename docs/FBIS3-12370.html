Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] India has strongly condemned yesterday's massacre of nearly 50 Palestinian worshipers in the West Bank town of Hebron by an Israeli gunman. New Delhi also conveyed its condolences to the members of the bereaved families. An External Affairs Ministry spokesman said today that India calls upon Israel to take immediate and effective steps to ensure that such incidents are not repeated. Israel has also been urged to bring the culprits to book and protect the lives and properties of innocent people in the occupied territory. New Delhi expressed the hope that all concerned will adopt a path of restraint in bringing down tension and will not vitiate the climate of peace and tranquillity to make the West Asia peace process a success.
