BFN [Unattributed report: "Iraqi Air Force Commander on Mystery Visit to Jordan"] [Text] Amman, AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT -- Informed Jordanian sources have revealed that Iraqi Air Force Commander Staff Lieutenant General Muzahim Sa'b al-Hasan al-Tikriti is now in Amman. The sources declined to give the date of the Iraqi military commander's arrival in Amman or the nature of his mission there. The sources told AL-SHARQ AL-AWSAT that the Iraqi military commander was staying at the Forte Grand Hotel. There was a fire on one floor of that hotel yesterday morning in which two Italian tourists were killed and 12 persons, including four Iraqis, were burned or suffered from smoke inhalation.
