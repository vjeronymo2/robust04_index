DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE Forest Service Westslope Timber Sale, Plumas National Forest; Environmental Impact Statement Cancellation The Plumas National Forest is no longer involved in the preparation of an Environmental Impact Statement for the Westslope Timber Sale. The notice of Intent, published in the Federal Register on March 5, 1991, is hereby rescinded (56 FR 9194). FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: R.C.
