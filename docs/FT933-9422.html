930810 FT 10 AUG 93 / UK Company News: 29% asset growth for Mid Wynd Intl Over the 12 months ended June 30 net asset value of Mid Wynd International Investment Trust rose from 280.9p to 362.3p per share. This increase of 29 per cent was the best for ten years, the managers reported. The Dollars 4.5m (Pounds 3m) loan was repaid in April, the bonds which it financed having been sold. Since 1990 that exercise had produced a capital profit of Pounds 800,000 - 16p per share - as well as gains to revenue. For the year gross investment income came to Pounds 769,000 (Pounds 760,000) and earnings per share to 7.07p (6.34p). The dividend is again 6p with an unchanged final of 3.6p.
