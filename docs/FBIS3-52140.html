Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, the chairman of the Presidential Council, today received brother Shaykh Hamad Bin-Jasim Bin-Jabr Al Thani, Qatar's foreign minister, who relayed a message from His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, the amir of the brotherly State of Qatar, on the close bilateral relations and issues of shared interest. During the meeting with Shaykh Hamad, the brother president praised the close relations between the two countries and valued Qatar's efforts in supporting Yemeni unity. He asked him to convey his greetings to his brother Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani and his best wishes for the Qatari people. The meeting was attended by Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim Basindwah, brother Muhammad 'Ali al- Ansari, Qatar's ambassador to our country, and the Qatari minister's delegation. He has since left Sanaa.
