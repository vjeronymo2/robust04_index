BFN [Unattributed report: "Central Balkan Republic of Macedonia"] [Text] Despite statements by [U.S. Special Envoy] Nimetz on bringing no proposal on the name, A1 Television cites unofficial circles on speculations with an alleged proposal by the Greek Foreign Minister Papoulias that Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] be referred to as The Central Balkan State of Macedonia. Sources suggest Papoulias has sent this proposal to the Macedonian authorities through the envoy Nimetz, and go on claiming that PASOK [Panhellenic Socialist Movement] is all ready to accept the name of Macedonia, and the Greek Government is more inclined to the latest proposal than to the prefix "North" or "New" Macedonia.
