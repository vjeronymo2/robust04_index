BFN [Text] Riga, May 31, RIA -- In accordance with the bilateral agreements signed on April 30 in Moscow, tomorrow, on June 1, the three last Russian naval ships will leave Liepaja [Latvia]. As the spokesman of the Latvian Defence Ministry has declared, the Latvian side, in taking over the facilities of the Russian navy, is encountering certain difficulties. In particular, they are connected with their further utilisation, as well as the need to free the water area of the former Liepaja military port and other former Russian naval bases from the several dozen ships and submarines sunk there.
