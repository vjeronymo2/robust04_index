931118 FT 18 NOV 93 / Observer: Common touch If a number of their Lordships are looking a little green at today's state opening of parliament, do not adjust your set. The explanation is that they have probably been enjoying the latest vintage to be introduced to the House of Lords cellars specially for the occasion - a lively little English Vin Nouveau from that well-known negociant, the Co-operative Wholesale Society.
