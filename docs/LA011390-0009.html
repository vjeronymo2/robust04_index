January 13, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

REBUILDING THE COLISEUM FOR THE L.A. RAIDERS

I root for the Raiders. George Blanda and Dave Casper, and then Jim Plunkett and Coach Tom Flores and Marcus Allen taught me to root for the Raiders.

But when it comes to a test between Al Davis and the Los Angeles Coliseum, I forget all about the Raiders. The Los Angeles Coliseum is the next greatest thing in California to the Golden Gate Bridge.

I rarely advocate lynching; it is quite uncivilized. Tearing down the Coliseum would be as uncivilized as lynching. May the people considering it get the point.

GILBERT S. BAHN

Thousand Oaks
