BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 471] [Text] Vilnius, April 5, ELTA -- A coordination group for the prevention of organized crime was formed by a decree issued by president. The group includes Seimas vice-chairman, justice and interior ministers, prosecutor general, director general of security service, a state inspector, heads of Seimas committees, representatives of customs department, president's office and other institutions, 14 officials all in all.
