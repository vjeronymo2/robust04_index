March 18, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

THIS 'SOUTH' WON'T RISE AGAIN

One of the biggest disappointments for so many of us was to discover that the miniseries "North and South" was not being continued. The potential for an immensely romantic and historically significant series created around the Civil War era and the nation's recovery would have advertisers beating down the network's doors!

Kathleen Fitzgerald, Thousand Oaks
