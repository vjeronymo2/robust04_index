BFN [Text] President Ramos has signed into law the measure liberalizing the entry of foreign banks into the country. In his speech during the signing, the president said the new law will trigger more efficient operation for local banks as well as facilitate new business and investments. Republic Act 7721 was also lauded by members of the banking community. Rafael Buenavantura, president of the Bankers' Association of the Philippines, said the new law is a balance between national interest and the economy's globalization requirement.
