April 16, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMMERCIAL SCENE;

UC SAN DIEGO SIGNS LEASE FOR WAREHOUSE

UC San Diego has signed a $3,656,440, 10-year lease for a 55,155-square-foot warehouse in the Miramar District Center, 7835 Trade St., San Diego. John Burnham & Co. represented the university, while Coldwell Banker represented the lessor, Sudberry Properties Inc., La Jolla.
