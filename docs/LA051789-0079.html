May 17, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

SOUTHERN SECTION PLAYOFFS;

TENNIS ROUNDUP;

LEACH LEADS LAGUNA BEACH PAST ESPERANZA

Jon Leach won, 6-1, 6-0, 6-0, and Graham Gilles won, 6-0, 6-0, 6-0, to lead Laguna Beach High School to a 17-1 victory over Esperanza Tuesday in a first-round Southern Section 3-A tennis match at Laguna Beach.

Leach is 48-0. Lee Kirner and Aaron Schmidt won, 6-2, 6-0, 6-1, in doubles for fourth-seeded Laguna Beach (19-2).

In other 3-A matches:

La Quinta 15, Palm Desert 3 -- Cong Tran won, 6-1, 6-0, 6-0, at No. 1 singles and Peter Vu and Phu Truong won, 6-1, 6-0, 6-0, at No. 1 doubles for host La Quinta (19-2), which has won 12 matches in a row. Vu and Truong are 60-3 this season.

Woodbridge 16, Claremont 2 -- Top-seeded Woodbridge won all nine singles sets at home. Randy Ivey won, 6-4, 6-0, 6-0, Alex Stienhardt won, 6-4, 6-1, 7-5, and Cris Emery won, 6-1, 6-0, 6-0, for Woodbridge (16-4).

Sunny Hills 14, Rubidoux 4 -- Glenn Henderson swept, 6-0, 6-1, 6-1, and all three doubles teams swept for second-seeded Sunny Hills (19-1).

In the 4-A first-round matches:

Corona del Mar 18, Nogales 0 -- Jamie Katzman won, 6-0, 6-0, 6-0, to lead host Corona del Mar (18-3), the third-seeded team. Nogales ends the season 17-5.

El Toro 12, Canyon 6 -- Chris Brown won, 6-1, 6-2, 6-0, and Brian Delbenes won, 6-3, 6-2, 6-1, for host El Toro (20-3).

Mater Dei 13, Baldwin Park 5 -- Dax Peterson won, 6-0, 6-0, 6-1, for host Mater Dei (20-1). Jason Harnett and Jeff Guiltnan won, 6-3, 6-0, 6-1, in doubles for Mater Dei.

Fullerton 9, Gahr 9 (Fullerton wins on games, 77-73) -- The No. 1 doubles team of Brian Brennan and James Fox scored the only sweep for Fullerton (18-2), with a 6-1, 6-4, 6-2 victory.

In 2-A first-round matches:

Western 14, Gladstone 4 -- Ed Peregrino won, 6-4, 6-0, 6-0, and Steve Her won, 6-1, 6-1, 6-0, for host Western (17-4).

Valencia 12, Bonita 6 -- Valencia's No. 2 singles player Paul Rygalski won his match, 6-2, 6-0, 6-1.
