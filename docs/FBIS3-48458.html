Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 26 (KCNA) -- The Peru-Korea Institute of Culture and Friendship and the Peruvian Committee for Support to the Independent and Peaceful Reunification of Korea held a joint annual meeting on Jan. 14. President of the institute Angel Castro Labarello noted in his report that last year the Korean people under the wise guidance of the great leader Generalissimo Kim Il-song and the dear leader Marshal Kim Chong-il smashed the provocations of the imperialists, defended the sovereignty of the country and the dignity of the nation and demonstrated to the world the superiority of man-centred socialism, the validity of the revolutionary line of independence and the might of the single-hearted unity of the leader, the party and the masses. This year the institute will expand its ranks and strengthen diverse political and cultural activities and relations with political parties and public organisations, he declared. In his report, secretary of the Peruvian Committee for Support to the Independent and Peaceful Reunification of Korea Felix N. Fernandez Torres vowed to conduct brisk solidarity activities this year, denouncing the acts of aggression and division on the part of the United States and the South Korean authorities and supporting the reunification of Korea on the three principles of national reunification and in the form of a confederation. Action programmes of the institute and the committee for this year were adopted at the meeting.
