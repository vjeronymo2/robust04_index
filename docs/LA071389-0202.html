July 13, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

AUTOCLAVE WITHDRAWS TYLAN OFFER

Autoclave Engineers Inc. said today it has withdrawn its tender offer for Torrance-based Tylan Corp. after the Federal Trade Commission's decision to block the acquisition on antitrust grounds.

"The process of proving our case in court . . . would be time-consuming and disruptive to our employees and would create uncertainty for our customers and suppliers," said Thomas Guelch, Autoclave's chief financial officer.
