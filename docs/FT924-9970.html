921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Charnos optimism over full year Charnos, the maker of tights, stockings and lingerie, reported an increase in its seasonal loss for the first half of 1992, but said it had no reason to expect the year's profit to compare unfavourably with the Pounds 3.61m of 1991. Turnover was little changed at Pounds 19.4m (Pounds 19.8m), but the pre-tax loss rose from Pounds 1.03m to Pounds 1.89m. Losses on the privately-owned ordinary shares worked through at 64.02p (34.97p).
