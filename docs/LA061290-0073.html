June 12, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

TERRORISTS BLAST U.S. FIRM IN ATHENS

Terrorists have attacked the office of a U.S. company, apparently using a stolen anti-tank missile and a small World War II bazooka, Greek officials said.

The office of Procter & Gamble Co. was damaged in the assault late Sunday, but police said no one was injured.

No group claimed responsibility, but authorities said Monday that they suspect the November 17 terrorist group, which has claimed to have stolen both types of weapons.
