BFN [Text] Judge Munir Hunayn, the judicial investigator looking into the case of the assassination of Engineer Dany Sham'un and members of his family, questioned Samir Ja'ja' in the presence of his lawyer As'ad Abu-Ra'd at the Justice Palace at 1000 today. The interrogation lasted until 2000 this evening. The judicial investigator decided to issue an arrest warrant for Ja'ja' and referred the warrant to the public prosecutor at the court of cassation for implementation.
