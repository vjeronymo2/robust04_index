May 31, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

GIRL, 13, TESTIFIES TO MOLESTATION BY BUCKEY AT PRESCHOOL

A girl who attended the McMartin Pre-School testified today that former teacher Ray Buckey molested her, threatened to harm her parents if she told them and slaughtered a rabbit in front of her.

The girl, now 13, is the third and final child to testify at Buckey's retrial that he molested them at the now-defunct nursery in Manhattan Beach.

Prosecutors allege that the girl was molested between November, 1979, and June, 1981. Buckey, 32, has steadfastly maintained his innocence.

Under questioning from Deputy Dist. Atty. Pam Ferrero, the girl testified that Buckey molested her while playing a naked sex game called "Horsey."

On several occasions during her testimony, the girl replied, "I don't remember," when asked for details of the molestations.
