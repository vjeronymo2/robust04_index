Airport 94WD0322D Bombay THE SUNDAY TIMES OF INDIA in English 6 Mar 94 p 10 94WD0322D Bombay THE SUNDAY TIMES OF INDIA English CSO [Text] Bombay, March 5: A Ugandan woman, allegedly carrying 3.5 kg of heroin concealed in the false top and bottom of her suitcase, was caught at Sahar airport on Thursday. Margaret Wasswa was on her way to Addis Ababa by an Ethiopian airlines flight when she was stopped by officials at the airport on suspicion. It was found that she had smeared the heroin with chili powder to mask the odour of the drug, officials said. Wasswa was asked details of the hotel in which she had stayed in Bombay but, officials say, she could not give any. "She however stated that the heroin was to be delivered to her associate abroad and that she got the suitcase containing the drug in the Colaba area near a five-star hotel," officials said. She has been remanded to custody upto March 17.
