930819 FT 19 AUG 93 / World Stock Markets: Brazil SAO PAULO resumed forward progress after an earlier suspension of trading in Telebras by Brazil's Securities and Exchange Commission was lifted. But the market faltered later, the Bovespa index losing 0.8 per cent on the day, after Tuesday's 3.8 per cent rise. The suspension of Telebras, which accounts for some 60 per cent of the market's daily average volume, was requested by the authorities to clarify comments in the media by the group's president which referred to the launch of a new ADR later this year.
