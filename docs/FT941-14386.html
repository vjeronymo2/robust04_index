940120 FT 20 JAN 94 / International Company News: Li Ka-shing buys into San Miguel Hong Kong property and telecommunications magnate Mr Li Ka-shing has bought 10 per cent of a Hong Kong affiliate of San Miguel, the Philippines' beer-and-food giant. Mr Li's Cheung Kong Holdings bought 37.4m shares in San Miguel Brewery for HKDollars 280.2m (USDollars 36.2m). The purchase was made through subsidiary Conroy Assets.
