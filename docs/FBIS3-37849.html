Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tamara Zamyatina] [Text] Moscow February 23 TASS -- Participating in the Sarajevo settlement, Russia as a member of the world community has upgraded its own status, Marshal of the Russian Air Force Yevgeniy Shaposhnikov said in an interview with TASS on Wednesday [23 February]. He highly praised the efforts taken by the Russian president and diplomats for averting NATO threatened air strikes in Bosnia, noting that Russia "demonstrated its capability of moderating by peaceful means armed conflicts exhausting both big and small nations". Shaposhnikov expressed sincere support of Russian soldiers serving now in the U.N. peacekeeping forces in former Yugoslavia, assessing their mission as a historic one. On day of Fatherland's defenders, the Marshal wished Russian servicemen, wherever they are staying at the moment -- in Serbia, Bosnia-Herzegovina or on Afghan border -- to be steadfast, self-controlled and to fulfil their military duty in the way Russians have always done, from the time of the battle of Kulikovo to the Great Patriotic War.
