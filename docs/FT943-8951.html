940815 FT 15 AUG 94 / S Korean students rally over US troops South Korean students armed with iron pipes prepare to confront riot police sent to break up a protest rally at Seoul National University yesterday. The outlawed rally called for the withdrawal of US troops from the country and unification with North Korea. Sporadic clashes erupted between the police and students outside the gate to the campus but there were no reports of injuries or arrests. Korean nuclear accord is only a first step, Page 4
