Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku (January 7) TURAN--The conductors of the passenger train number 93 Moscow-Baku refused to work at the Minirealniye Vodi-Derbent sector of the rout because of the attacks of the gangsters at the train in Chechnia. Train number 93 is attached to the Moscow Railroad Department. Besides the train N93 there is also train N 5 Baku-Moscow attached to Azerbaijan Railroad Department . As the deputy-chief of Azerbaijan Railroad Department Musa Panakhov said the train N 93 will be definitely canceled. He also said that during October-November 1993 1000 Azerbaijani carriages loaded with food products were plundered at the territory of Chechnia.
