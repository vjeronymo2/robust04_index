For the reasons set out in the preamble, Title 30, Chapter VII, Subchapter T of the Code of Federal Regulations is amended as set forth below: PART 944_UTAH 1. The authority citation for Part 944, continues to read as follows: Authority: 30 U.S.C. 1201 et seq . 2. Section 944.15 is amended by adding paragraph (bb) to read as follows: §944.15 Approval of amendments to State regulatory program. * * * * * (bb) With the exceptions of UCA 40&hyph;10&hyph;14(6), concerning appeals to district court and deletion of UCA 40&hyph;10&hyph;20(3), concerning contest of the violation or amount of the penalty, the revisions to the following sections of the Utah Code Annotated 1953, Title 40, as submitted to OSM on March 7, 1994, and as supplemented with explanatory information on July 5, 1994, are approved effective September 27, 1994: 40&hyph;10&hyph;14(3), Hearings. 40&hyph;10&hyph;20(1), Civil Penalty for Violation of Chapter. 40&hyph;10&hyph;20(2), Informal Conferences. 40&hyph;10&hyph;20(3), Public Hearings. 40&hyph;10&hyph;20 (5) and (6), Criminal Penalties. 40&hyph;10&hyph;20(8), Civil Penalty for Failure to Correct Violation. 3. Section 944.16 is amended by adding paragraph (b) to read as follows: §944.16 Required program amendments. * * * * * (b) By March 26, 1995, Utah shall submit a proposed amendment for UCA 40&hyph;10&hyph;14(6) to eliminate the discrepancy in appellate procedures that govern Board of Oil, Gas and Mining decisions on permit applications in a manner no less stringent than section 526(e) of SMCRA. 4.
