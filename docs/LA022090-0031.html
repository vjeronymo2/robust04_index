February 20, 1990, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO EARNINGS

DH Technology: The San Diego-based computer products firm reported its seventh consecutive quarter of record net income and revenue.

Net income rose 52% to $1.7 million for the fourth quarter ended Dec. 31, up from $1 million a year earlier. Revenue rose 22% to $11.3 million, up from $9.3 million.

Net income for the year rose 88% to $5.9 million, up from $3.1 million during the previous year. Revenue rose 43% to $42 million, up from $29.3 million during 1988.
