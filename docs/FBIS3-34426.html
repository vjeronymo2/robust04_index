Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Sanaa, 7 Feb (INA)--The Yemeni Socialist Party [YSP], led by Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid, has welcomed the decision by the Yemeni parties dialogue committee that set the Jordanian capital, Amman, as the venue for signing the accord document in the near future. In press statements tonight, Salim Salih Muhammad, member of the Yemeni Presidential Council and YSP assistant secretary general, said his party reaffirms its full support for all sincere efforts exerted, and resolutions taken, by the political parties dialogue committee to set a date for signing the document in the near future. Salim Salih Muhammad denied that his party has set any conditions or reservations that prevent the implementation of the committee's resolutions.
