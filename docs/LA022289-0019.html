February 22, 1989, Wednesday, Valley Edition

ALEMANY'S ROSSELLI BREAKS HAND IN A FALL

Alemany High left-hander Joey Rosselli suffered a broken bone in his pitching hand in a fall at his home Monday night and will be sidelined at least six weeks.

Rosselli, Alemany's starting quarterback this season, said that the hand will be placed in a cast for three to four weeks after he undergoes surgery Thursday.

Rosselli compiled a 2-3 record as a sophomore last season with a 3.65 earned-run average. He also batted .357 in 28 at-bats.
