BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] Addressing a True Path Party [TPP] meeting today, Prime Minister Tansu Ciller, leader of the TPP, pointed out that the Turkish economy is being negatively affected by the economic embargo against Iraq. [Begin Ciller recording] The oil pipeline system, which constitutes a valuable investment for both Turkey and Iraq, has been left idle for three years and faces the danger of damage and rotting. An agreement has been reached to carry out a sort of rescue operation in order to eliminate this danger. In this way, when the oil embargo is lifted in the future, the system will be able to function uninterruptedly. [end recording] Ciller explained that oil will be pumped to the pipeline and that food, medicine, and certain basic necessities will be sent to Iraq in return. The prime minister denied reports that the United States gave Turkey a note in connection with this matter. She stressed that the allies realize that this situation arose because of a technical necessity.
