930216 FT 16 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Olim assets increase 19% over 1992 Net asset value of Olim Convertible Trust rose 19 per cent, from 60.57p to 72.09p, over the 12 months to end-January. Net income was slightly lower at Pounds 1.38m, against Pounds 1.5m, for earnings of 9.16p (9.98p) per share. As already announced, the total dividend for 1992 was raised from 8.5p to 8.8p.
