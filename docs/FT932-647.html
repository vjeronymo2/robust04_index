930628 FT 28 JUN 93 / NHS down Pounds 70m a year due to Unemployment UNEMPLOYMENT is costing the National Health Service more than Pounds 70m a year, says a report published today by the Office Of Health Economics, a research body funded by the pharmaceutical industry. The cost to general-practitioner and pharmaceutical services is put at Pounds 40.1m and the loss in revenue to the NHS in prescription charges at Pounds 30.6m. The report says unemployment leads to illness through a combination of stress, poverty and unhealthy living.
