920609 FT 09 JUN 92 / Thorn EMI wins Pounds 20m RAF order THORN EMI has won a Pounds 20m contract in collaboration with Marshall of Cambridge to upgrade the RAF's ageing fleet of specialised aircraft for training combat jet navigators. Updating of the RAF's 11 Dominie aircraft - a variant of the BAe 125 commuter jet - is due to be completed in early 1996. The aircraft first entered service more than 20 years ago.
