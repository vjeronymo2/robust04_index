BFN [Text] A source in the Interior Ministry of the Ukrainian autonomous republic of Crimea told INTERFAX that Ukraine had in the last few days increased its National Guard contingent in the Crimean capital Simferopol. But the ministry does not know exactly how many National Guards are currently stationed in the city, the source said. However, other sources in Simferopol said there are more than 2,500 National Guards in the city, which is being patrolled by details of four or five National Guards with truncheons, an officer and several armed police.
