BFN [text] The Cabinet of Ministers Presidium session today discussed issues which are important for the reform of our country's economy. In particular, the course of implementation of the governments measures to promote economic policy this year, the concept and program of measures aimed at demonopolizing the national economy were discussed. A heated exchange of opinions took place on what is hindering the resolution of these problems. The lack of flexibility among heads of ministries and departments as well as of the government apparatus was criticized. It was stressed that in view of the serious state of the country's economic and social sphere it is no longer possible to tolerate such low executive discipline. Draft resolutions on the issues under discussion were returned for elaboration. Deadlines were fixed for this elaboration. The authors were told to display the highest level of professionalism during the preparation of the documents and to eliminate mistakes made in the drafts. The Cabinet of Ministers Presidium also examined the implementation of intergovernmental agreements on transport and communications with neighboring southern countries. The sitting was chaired by Prime Minister Sergey Tereshchenko.
