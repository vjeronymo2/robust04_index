August 2, 1990, Thursday, South Bay Edition

HEAT-SPONSORED PROGRAMS RESULT IN INCREASED AWARENESS OF SOCCER

I have followed the Heat for two years and over the past year, have seen tremendous changes take place.

I have seen the crowds more than double in size, have seen a family atmosphere develop as well as an increase in enthusiasm for the team.

I think these developments can be attributed to General Manager Jill Fracisco and her staff. A lack of dedication certainly would not result in such a positive change.

Militello pointed out some valid methods of how to increase attendance and interest in the club, but the negative manner in which he expressed his ideas does anything but spark interest in the club or the sport of soccer.

ANGELA M. MONA

Playa del Rey
