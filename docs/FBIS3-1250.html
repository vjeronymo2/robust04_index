Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bisho, Ciskei March 22 SAPA -- The Ciskei government has officially requested South African assistance to maintain control of the homeland. South African ambassador to Ciskei, Mr Piet Goosen, said on Tuesday [22 March] night that a request had been dispatched to Pretoria, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reported. A number of Ciskei policemen on Tuesday embarked on a strike and took at least five officers who they alleged were involved in corruption hostage. They also demanded the arrest of the homeland's commissioner of police.
