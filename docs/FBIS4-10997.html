BFN [Text] On 19 April, Peruvian Public Health Minister Jaime Freundt-Thurne announced that the Peruvian Government is studying the possibility of broadening and strengthening commercial ties with Cuba by purchasing Cuban-made medicines. Freundt-Thurne stated that both the Cuban health system and pharmaceutical industry were excellent despite the difficulties Cuba is currently experiencing. Freundt-Thurne, who is visiting Cuba by invitation of Cuban Public Health Minister Julio Teja, told Teja that Peru wants to broaden ties with Cuba.
