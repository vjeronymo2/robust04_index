920518 FT 18 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Eidos full-time loss soars to Pounds 385,000 Pre-tax losses at Eidos, which produces computer, video editing and allied equipment, rose from Pounds 60,600 to Pounds 385,000 in 1991. As foreshadowed, the group did not achieve any sales but overheads were contained at a lower level than budgeted. Losses per share increased to 17.5p (2.76p).
