BFN [By special ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Bonn, 11 May -- Russia's President Boris Yeltsin and German Chancellor Helmut Kohl have begun their talks at the FRG chancellor's department in Bonn. The talks are being held behind closed doors. They will subsequently be continued with other delegation members taking part. It is thought that the issue of finalizing the withdrawal of Russian troops will be one of the main topics on the agenda of the consultatons between B. Yeltsin and H. Kohl. Considerable attention will also be focused on the problems of economic cooperation between the FRG and Russia, and topical international political problems. B. Yeltsin and H. Kohl will today hold a joint news conference on the results of the talks in Bonn.
