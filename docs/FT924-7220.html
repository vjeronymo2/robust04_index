921117 FT 17 NOV 92 / International Company News: Wacoal tumbles despite sales rise WACOAL, a Japanese underwear maker, yesterday reported a sharp fall in profits despite a rise in sales, due to lower margins and the cost of redeeming warrant bonds. Taxable profits fell 13.5 per cent to Y6.3bn (Dollars 50.8m) on a 4.3 per cent rise in sales to Y66.2bn. Net profits fell 9.3 per cent to Y3bn. Wacoal said its spring and autumn sale campaigns boosted sales of woman's underwear by 6.2 per cent.
