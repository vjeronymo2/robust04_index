Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, January 12 (XINHUA) -- Chinese President Jiang Zemin received credentials presented by three foreign ambassadors to China at the Great Hall of the People here this morning. These ambassadors are Azerbaijan's First Ambassador to China Tamerlan Garayev, Spain's new ambassador to China Juan Lena and Bangladesh new ambassador to China Mustafizur Rahman [spelling of names as received].
