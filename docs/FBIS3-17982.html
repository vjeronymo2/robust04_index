Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Simferopol, 29 Mar--Crimean President Yuriy Meshkov has issued a decree on a mechanism to implement the law on national minorities. In accordance with the decree, a commission has been set up that is to prepare materials on implementing the rights of citizens to have their national surnames, first names, and patronymics, and to restore their nationality in documents. Anife Seytomer-Kyzy, adviser to the president of Crimea on international relations, is head of the commission.
