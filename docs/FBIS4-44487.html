CSO [Summary] In 1994 the grain contract purchase amount is 3.51 billion kilograms, and for rapeseeds 550 million kilograms; in addition Sichuan will purchase 2.5 billion kilograms of grain and 275 million kilograms of rapeseeds from the market. Sichuan will also concentrate on setting up grain reserves at the provincial, prefecture and county levels. In 1994 provincial reserves should total 750 million kilograms, and 250 million kilograms at the prefecture and county levels.
