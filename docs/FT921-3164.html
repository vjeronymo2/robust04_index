920316 FT 16 MAR 92 / Victory for Catalonia moderates MR JORDI PUJOL was set for a fourth term as president of Catalonia's regional government as his moderate nationalist Convergencia i Unio (CIU) gained an absolute majority in elections yesterday, AP reports from Barcelona. With 67.47 per cent of the votes counted, CIU was given 45.9 per cent of the vote and 71 of the parliament's 135 seats, two more than it won in the last elections in 1988. Mr Pujol has been president of the Catalan region since 1980. In second place was the Socialist Party of Catalonia which took 27.64 per cent of the vote earning it 39 seats, three less than in 1988. Voter turnout was put at 54.84 per cent.
