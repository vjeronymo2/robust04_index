Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] President Sardar Farooq Ahmad Khan Leghari and the Saudi King, Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, have made a passionate appeal to the Afghan leaders to establish and maintain peace in their country. In a joint appeal during a meeting in Riyadh last night, the two leaders urged the Afghan factions to end the conflict in true of spirit of Islamic teachings. The two sides held wide-ranging talks covering regional and world issues of mutual interest and bilateral cooperation. The president flew into Jeddah this morning.
