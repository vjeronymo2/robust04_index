Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] A Vietnamese delegation led by Ambassador Le Van Ban and a U.S. delegation led by the deputy assistant secretary of state for humanitarian and human rights issues Nancy Ely-Raphel held in New York yesterday a dialogue on the human rights issue and other matters of mutual concern. The meeting took place in a constructive atmosphere of mutual understanding. Both sides expressed their hope that the dialogue will contribute to spurring the process of improving relations between the two countries.
