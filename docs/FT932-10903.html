930506 FT 06 MAY 93 / International Company News: Bank of Muscat falls 3.7% to Dollars 2.61m THE Bank of Muscat, which is about to complete a merger with Al Bank Al Ahli Al Omani, reports a decline of 3.7 per cent in net profit to Dollars 2.61m for 1992, AP-DJ reports. The bank's operating profit rose strongly to Dollars 7.85m, up 38 per cent, but the bank increased bad-debt provisions by 77 per cent to Dollars 5.24m.
