BFN [By reporter Bata Ercang (1572 1044 1422 0221)] [Text] Ulaabaatar, 29 Apr (XINHUA) -- After holding talks at the State Palace, visiting Chinese Premier Li Peng and Mongolian Prime Minister P. Jasray told the press on the morning of 29 April that they highly appraised the results of the talks and the Sino- Mongolian Treaty of Friendly Relations and Cooperation to be signed soon. The two premiers agreed that the talks proceeded in a friendly and cordial atmosphere. Both sides exchanged views on bilateral relations and on international issues of common interest. When the late Premier Zhou Enlai visited Mongolia 34 years ago, he concluded the Sino-Mongolian Friendship and Mutual Aid Treaty. The new Sino- Mongolian Treaty of Friendly Relations and Cooperation to be signed soon will lay the legal foundation for bilateral relations. Both sides will also sign a series of bilateral documents on specific cooperation issues. The talks were fruitful.
