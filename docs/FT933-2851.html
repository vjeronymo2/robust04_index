930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / International Company News: Honeywell settles dispute with Fuji HONEYWELL has settled its autofocus camera patent infringement dispute with Fuji Photo Film and its US unit, Fuji Photo Film USA, heading off a civil trial scheduled for October, AP-DJ reports. Honeywell said the settlement called for Fuji to pay an undisclosed amount, which will be included in Honeywell's financial results for the third quarter ending September 30. The company said it has agreed to drop its lawsuit, filed in US District Court in New Jersey, alleging that Fuji used Honeywell's automatic focus camera technology without permission.
