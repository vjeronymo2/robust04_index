BFN [Text] Sa'ib 'Urayqat, who is a member of the Palestinian Autonomy Council, said that within a short time committees would be appointed to manage the Arab towns and villages inside the autonomous areas. These committees would make preparations to hold municipal elections within several months. Sa'ib 'Urayqat confirmed to our correspondent Arye Gus that he had accepted the local government portfolio in the Autonomy Council. 'Urayqat, who is a Jericho resident, added that he was happy to see the Palestinian flag flying over the city. 'Urayqat nevertheless noted that the Palestinians still had a long way to go and many challenges to face.
