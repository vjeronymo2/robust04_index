940719 FT 19 JUL 94 / Palau to end UN trust status Palau will end its status as the last United Nations trust territory in the Pacific on October 1 and will be formally linked to the US, said Palau President Kuniwo Nakamura, Reuter reports from Guam. Under an agreement with the US approved by Palau's voters, the islands will become self-governing, with Washington retaining responsibility for external defence.
