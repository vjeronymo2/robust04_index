May 3, 1989, Wednesday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

A 19-year-old man was arrested Monday night in connection with the slaying of Robert Ashley Moon, who was found suffering from a gunshot wound at 8 a.m. April 15 in an alley in the 4100 block of 36th Street, said police spokesman Bill Robinson.

Moon died that afternoon in a hospital.

Arrested in connection with the killing was Clarence Marshall Spurlock, 19. He and Moon lived in a garage behind a home in the 4100 block of 36th Street.

Spurlock was booked into County Jail, where he is being held without bail.
