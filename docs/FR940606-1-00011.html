DATES: This withdrawal is effective June 6, 1994. 14 CFR Part 71 [Airspace Docket No. 94&hyph;AGL&hyph;2] Proposed Class E Airspace Establishment; Savanna, IL AGENCY: Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), DOT. ACTION: Notice of proposed rulemaking; withdrawal.

SUMMARY: This action withdraws the Notice of Proposed Rulemaking (NPRM), which proposed to establish Class E airspace near Savanna, IL, to accommodate a new Very High Frequency Omnidirectional Range/Distance Measuring Equipment (VOR/DME&hyph;A) instrument approach procedure to Tri-Township Airport, Savanna, IL. The NPRM is being withdrawn since it is a duplicate of another regulatory airspace action that was published in the Federal Register on April 6, 1994. DATES: This withdrawal is effective June 6, 1994. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Robert J. Woodford, Air Traffic Division, System Management Branch, AGL&hyph;530, Federal Aviation Administration, 2300 East Devon Avenue, Des Plaines, Illinois 60018, telephone (708) 294&hyph;7568. SUPPLEMENTARY INFORMATION: The Proposed Rule On March 29, 1994, a Notice of Proposed Rulemaking was published in the Federal Register to establish Class E airspace near Savanna, IL, to accommodate a new VOR/DME&hyph;A instrument approach procedure to Tri-Township Airport, Savanna, IL (59 FR 14578). No comments were received. However, the NPRM is being withdrawn since it is a duplicate of another regulatory airspace action that was published in the Federal Register on April 6, 1994. Withdrawal of Proposed Rule Accordingly, pursuant to the authority delegated to me, Airspace Docket No. 94&hyph;AGL&hyph;2, as published in the Federal Register on March 29, 1994 (59 FR 14578), is hereby withdrawn.
