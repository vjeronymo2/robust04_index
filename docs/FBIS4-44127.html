BFN [Text] The joint Cyprus-Greek military maneuvers will be held in October within the framework of the unified defense doctrine. According to CyBC's [Cyprus Broadcasting Corporation] exclusive information, the Greek Air Force and Navy will participate in the exercise. The decision to hold the exercise was made during the recent visit and talks in Athens by Defense Minister Konstandinos Iliadhis. It is possible that this exercise will be combined with the National Guard annual maneuvers codenamed Nikiforos. A group of staffers are working on the exercise plans, which are expected to be ready in August and everything will proceed on the basis of what has been agreed upon in Athens. Military sources have told CyBC that the communications sector is considered particularly important for the success of the exercise as well as for the unified defense doctrine in general. According to the same sources, direct communications with parallel safeguards for confidentiality are also being studied. According to CyBC information, all the diplomatic missions both in Nicosia and in Athens have demonstrated particular interest in the exercise.
