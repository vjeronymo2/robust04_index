Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanover (DDP/ADN) -- An overwhelming majority of 92.9 percent have voted for a strike in the ballot on industrial action in the Lower Saxony engineering industry. This was announced to journalists in Hanover this afternoon. According to IG Metall, the voter turnout was 97.2 percent. A total of 41,310 trade union members were entitled to vote, and 40,172 took part in the ballot. A total of 38,098 voted in favor of a strike, and 2,014 against.
