June 24, 1990, Sunday, South Bay Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Water Pollution -- A June 15 story on the discovery of trace amounts of an industrial solvent in a drinking water well in Hawthorne incorrectly identified the well as a municipal water source. The well, operated by the Southern California Water Co., is not part of the Hawthorne Municipal Water system. However, it helps supply drinking water for Southern California Water Co. customers in Hawthorne, Lennox and Inglewood.
