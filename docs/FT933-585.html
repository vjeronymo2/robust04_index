930928 FT 28 SEP 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea Landed teas met strong general demand, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Selected best and good medium north Indians ruled fully firm, but plainer sorts were again easier. Bright east Africans remained firm while coloury mediums advanced 3-5p. Plainer types were unchanged. Ceylons met good demand and prices moved 4-8p. Offshore: strong competition at dearer rates. Quotations quality 200p/kg, good medium 127p/kg, medium 114p/kg, low medium 95p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 216p/kg for an Assam pf.
