BFN [Text] Spain and Sweden have called on other countries to contribute more UN troops to Bosnia. The call came during the visit of Spanish Foreign Minister Javier Solana Madariaga to Sweden. The two countries have sent around 1,200 troops each to the UN protection forces in Bosnia. Solana Madariaga and Swedish Foreign Minister Margaretha af Ugglas say a sizeable UN presence is necessary in the former Yugoslavia to avoid a spread of hostilities.
