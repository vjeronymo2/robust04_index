940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / Nuclear safety convention backed Over 80 countries yesterday approved a nuclear safety convention which, they said, could force unsafe reactors such as Chernobyl - in Ukraine, one of the signatories - to shut down. But critics said the document was toothless and could not be enforced. The Nuclear Safety Convention was initialled after a three-day conference organised by the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA) in Vienna.
