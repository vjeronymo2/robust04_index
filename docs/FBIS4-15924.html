BFN [Text] The speaker of the State Duma Ivan Rybkin, leader of the faction Russia's Choice, Egor Gaydar and head of the Communist party faction Gennadiy Zyuganov in March exerted the greatest influence on decision-making in parliament. Such are the results of an opinion poll conducted by the Independent Center of Russian Political Culture Studies. Experts of the center, among them parliamentary journalists and political experts, determined the rating of Duma deputies' political influence in March on a 10-point scale. The top twenty after Rybkin (8.2), Gaydar (6.3) and Zyuganov (6.3) include Vladimir Zhirinovskiy (5.4), Sergey Shakhray (5.3), Sergey Glazyev (4.9), Mikhail Lapshin (4.8), Konstantin Zatulin (4.6), Vladimir Lukin (4.5). Anatoliy Lukyanov (4.4), Viktor Ilyukhin (4.3), Vladimir Isakov (4.2), Aleksandr Vengerovskiy (4.1), Sergey Baburin (4.0), Vassiliy Lipitskiy (3.8), Mikhail Zadornov (3.7), Girgoriy Yavlinskiy (3.6), Aleksandr Pochinok (3.6), Aleksandr Nazarchuk (3.5) and Gennadiy Burbulis (3.5). The commentary on results of the poll will be published in the Megapolis-Continent newspaper on Thursday.
