December 4, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL EARNINGS: CAMARILLO COMMUNITY POSTS 30% RISE IN EARNINGS

Camarillo Community Bank in Camarillo reported a 30% increase in earnings to $190,550 for the third quarter that ended Sept. 30, up from $145,821 a year earlier.

Earnings for the first nine months of 1990 were up 6%, to $559,002, compared to $529,395 for the same period last year. The bank's total assets as of Sept. 30 increased 16% to $62.9 million from $54.0 million over the same nine-month period last year.
