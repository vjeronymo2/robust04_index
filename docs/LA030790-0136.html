March 7, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Craig A. Jones has been named a development partner at SDC Development, a Newport Beach-based commercial real estate development and investment firm. In his new position, he will research development opportunities for neighborhood shopping centers and other retail projects and have management responsibilities for site selection, financing, construction and marketing. Before joining the company, Jones was senior sales consultant for Coldwell Banker.
