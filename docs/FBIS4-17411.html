BFN [Unattributed POSTFACTUM report: "People's Assembly `Has Canceled' the Russian Federation President's Edict"] [Text] Yesterday a session of the Tatarstan People's Assembly Presidium promulgated a statement adopted by parliament which rules null and void Boris Yeltsin's edict On Regulating Land Relations and Promoting the Agrarian Reform in Russia, because, according to the People's Assembly, this edict "is effectively tantamount to occupation and enslavement of peoples."
