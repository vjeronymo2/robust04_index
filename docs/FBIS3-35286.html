Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At 1145 today, President Hafiz al-Asad received Lebanese President Ilyas al-Hirawi. Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam attended. This was followed by a closed meeting between the two presidents. Discussion revolved around the conditions and developments in the region, the peace process, and issues of mutual concern. President al-Hirawi joined the president at a fast-breaking banquet. Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi, and Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' were invited to the banquet. President Hafiz al-Asad and President Ilyas al-Hirawi resumed their talks at 1830 today in a meeting they held at the Presidential Palace. The meeting was attended by Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam, Prime Minister Mahmud al-Zu'bi, and Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar'.
