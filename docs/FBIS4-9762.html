BFN [Text] More than 1 million quintals of potatoes have been harvested in Havana Province from 50 percent of the land planted in potatoes. This was announced at a special session of the Provincial Assembly of the People's Government of Havana Province. Alberto Beltran, president of the provincial agriculture and food committee, stated that there are 735,000 quintals in the cold-storage facilities and that measures are being taken to complete the harvest successfully and avoid illegal diversions of products. The participants in the meeting described as positive the progress of the harvest, the efforts being made in education during the current school year, and the preparations for the next school year as positive. Education Minister Luis Ignacio Gomez stressed that the province's workers are seriously working to increase the quality of the educational process, and added his ministry's praises in this regard.
