BFN [Text] Interior Minister Manuel Antonio has just told Radio Mozambique that there will be no general strike within the Mozambican Police force. He was reacting to a letter sent to him by members of the police force. In a letter published by the weekly SAVANA newspaper, police agents said they would be going on general strike as of 1 May and presented demands such as salary increases and promotions. Interior Minister Manuel Antonio said the letter had been written by an opportunistic group, including some officers, that have infiltrated the police force. [passage indistinct]
