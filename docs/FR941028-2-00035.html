Take notice that the following filings have been made with the Commission: 1. Pennsylvania Power & Light Company [Docket No. ER95&hyph;28&hyph;000] Take notice that on October 11, 1994, Pennsylvania Power & Light Company (PP&L), tendered for filing with the Federal Energy Regulatory Commission an Electrical Output Sales Agreement (Agreement) between PP&L and Allegheny Electric Cooperative, Inc. (Allegheny) dated September 23, 1994. The Agreement provides for the sale by PP&L to Allegheny of electrical output solely for Allegheny's use in wholesale bulk power transactions. PP&L has requested an effective date of October 12, 1994, for the Agreement. In accordance with 18 CFR 35.11, PP&L has requested waiver of the sixty day notice period in 18 CFR 35.2(e). PP&L states that a copy of its filing was provided to Allegheny and to the Pennsylvania Public Utility Commission.
