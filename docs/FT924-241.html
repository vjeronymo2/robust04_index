921229 FT 29 DEC 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa GAINS in industrial and financial shares lifted Johannesburg as the all-share index rose 10 to 3,256. The industrial index added 18 to 4,356 while the gold index shed 1 to 816. Last Thursday, industrials rose 10 to 4,338 and the all-share index was 5 up at 3,246. Golds were steady at 817.
