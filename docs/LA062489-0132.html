June 24, 1989, Saturday, Orange County Edition

TRAFFIC TOLL

Fewer people were killed on Orange County's city streets, roads and highways in 1988 than any year since 1984. The 270 fatalities during '88 marked an 11% decrease from 1987. Although the overall trend was down, only tiny La Palma recorded no fatalities during 1988. Indeed, La Palma has had no fatalities during any of the last five years -- the only city where this is true.

Not only was Orange County's traffic toll the lowest since 1984, but it was also lower than any of the surrounding counties last year -- both in absolute terms and when measured by the rate-per-thousand drivers.
