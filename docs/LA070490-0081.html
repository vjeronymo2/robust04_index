July 4, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Alex Stewart, who is scheduled to fight Tyson in September, knocked down Jaime Howe in the fifth round, then stopped him 32 seconds into the seventh round of a scheduled 10-round heavyweight fight in Monticello, N.Y.

Stewart, 26-1 with 26 knockouts, bloodied Howe's nose in the fourth, then dropped him 1:07 into the fifth with a straight right. Howe is 15-12-1.

Stewart, ranked No. 4 by the World Boxing Assn. and No. 5 by the International Boxing Federation, suffered his only loss last year against Holyfield.
