Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The press service of Georgia's Defense Ministry reports that Abkhazian armed formations have continued their mass attacks on Georgian villages in the administrative territory of Abkhazia. A number of settlements in the Lata Gorge have been subjected to intensive shelling for the last two days, causing casualties among the civilian population. Meanwhile, the press service of the Abkhazian mission in Moscow has announced that an operation to render harmless a number of Georgian terrorist groups in the Kodori Gorge has been successfully completed.
