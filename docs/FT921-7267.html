920224 FT 24 FEB 92 / Mitterrand loses further support FRENCH President Francois Mitterrand's electoral popularity has resumed its decline in the wake of the brief admission to the country of Mr George Habash, the radical Palestinian, and ahead of regional elections next month, Alice Rawsthorn reports from Paris. The proportion of the French electorate claiming to be 'satisfied' with the president's performance has fallen by 2 percentage points over the past month to 24 per cent, according to the latest IFOP poll in Journal du Dimanche. Similarly Mrs Edith Cresson, prime minister, saw her satisfaction rating fall from 23 per cent in January to 21 per cent.
