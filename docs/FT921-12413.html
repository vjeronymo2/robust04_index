920127 FT 27 JAN 92 / World News in Brief: Labour set to back voting changes for Scotland A Labour party manifesto commitment to include the voting system for the House of Commons in a post-election national debate on electoral reform was foreshadowed as the party's leadership prepared to endorse proportional voting for a Scottish Assembly. The further shift towards voting system changes came as Labour responded to the third consecutive opinion poll showing a slight Conservative lead with a new campaign to counter attacks on its tax and spending policies.
