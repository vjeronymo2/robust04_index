BFN [Excerpt] The northwestern Zambian city of Solwezi has more than 27,000 refugees. Most of them are Angolans, but there are also people from Burundi, Rwanda, and Zaire. Last weekend 316 Angolans arrived in Mwinilinga and another 55 in Solwezi. They are believed to have come from Moxico Province and they are fleeing the war caused by the National Union for the Total Independence of Angola. [passage omitted]
