Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] During a reception given in Hanoi to the 32-member U.S. Senate delegation led by Senator Johnston, Vietnamese party General Secretary Do Muoi wished that the U.S. make a further effort to improve the relations between the two countries. Meanwhile, Senator Johnston said that the relations between the two countries have reached an important point and nowadays the U.S. should choose either normalize of relations or maintaining its sanction against Vietnam. He said the normalization of the U.S.-Vietnamese relations in economic and cultural fields will not only be in the interests of the two peoples but also influence world and regional peace and stability. A press conference was held in Hanoi on Sunday afternoon by U.S. Senator Johnston and his party. Senator Johnston commented that the Vietnamese government and people have been cooperative in most of the ways, and the way of the world cooperation is not by punishment and the punishment ought to be stopped and start a new chapter [sentence as heard]. He said there is a tremendous vitality and energy in Vietnam. He hoped that his party and himself will make further efforts to consolidate and develop the cooperative relations between the two countries.
