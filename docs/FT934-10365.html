931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Crest Packaging shares start trading The first day of trading in shares of Crest Packaging, the flexible packaging and folding carton group, ended with the shares at 140p, a premium to the placing price of 135p. The shares touched a high of 145p. Trading volume in the shares was 3.8m shares. The group's existing shareholders - directors and their families - sold 10.6m shares, 25.9 per cent of the total, in the institutional placing, and kept the rest.
