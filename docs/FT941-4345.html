940311 FT 11 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Cussins in the black with Pounds 1.42m After a return to the black at midway, Cussins Property Group, the residential property developer, produced pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.42m for 1993. Comparative figures, which have been restated, reveal losses of Pounds 3.26m for 1992, incurred after a Pounds 3.12m debit on the disposal of discontinued operations. Turnover rose from Pounds 16.34m to Pounds 20.04m. Earnings per share were 8.9p, compared with 14.2p losses. The directors stated that the positive results coupled with the group's 'sound prospects for 1994' enabled them to recommend a final dividend of 2p making a 3p (nil) total. The total sales value of properties reserved to March 6 was Pounds 8.2m (Pounds 3.5m).
