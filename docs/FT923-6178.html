920826 FT 26 AUG 92 / Ex-Bank worker sent for trial A FORMER Bank of England worker accused of stealing more than Pounds 165,000 from its incineration depot was yesterday sent for trial at Southwark Crown Court. Mr Kevin Winwright, of Chelmsford, Essex, was committed on unconditional bail by Bow Street magistrates on seven specimen charges of theft from the Bank's printing works in Debden, Essex, between January 1 last year and May 14 this year.
