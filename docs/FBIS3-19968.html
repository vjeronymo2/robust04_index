Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Wiesbaden -- A leak in the primary circuit of the Biblis A nuclear power plant (Hesse) occurred on Wednesday [23 March]. According to Hesse government spokesman Klaus-Peter Schmidt-Deguelle, "highly contaminated water" leaked out. It has, however, remained in the inner security area of the reactor block. Operation of the reactor will be stopped, Schmidt-Deguelle said.
