930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / Mitac plans Pounds 4m computer plant MITAC International, one of Taiwan's largest computer manufacturers, is planning to build a personal computer factory at Telford, Shropshire, to expand its manufacturing and distribution throughout Europe. The company said it would invest more than Pounds 4m to build the plant and corporate campus, creating at least 200 jobs over the next three to four years.
