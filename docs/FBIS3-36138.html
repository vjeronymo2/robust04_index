Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS special correspondent Yuriy Sizov] [Text] Tbilisi, 3 Feb -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin arrived in Tbilisi today. Among the Russian delegation accompanying the president were First Deputy Prime Minister Oleg Soskovets, Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev, and St. Petersburg Mayor Anatoliy Sobchak. Defense Minister Pavel Grachev arrived in Tbilisi yesterday. The honored guest, the Russian head of state, was welcomed by the chairman of the Georgian parliament and head of state, Eduard Shevardnadze, Prime Minister Otar Patsatsia, members of the cabinet of ministers, the speaker and members of the Georgian parliament and officials of the head of state's staff. The Russian president's official visit to Georgia has begun. A treaty on friendship, good-neighborliness and cooperation between the two states will be signed today.
