BFN [From the "NTC News" newscast] [Text] The investigation into the suicide of Private Leonardo Castro has led to the removal of Colonel Carlos Pinzon, commandant of the Presidential Guard Battalion. [Begin General Norberto Adrada, Army 13th Brigade commander, recording] We have decided to relieve the colonel to allow a smoother investigation, without any kind of obstacle. Lieutenant Lozano was also moved, primarily to make the investigation process a little easier. [end recording] For the same reasons, Pinzon was not present for today's change of command ceremonies. His future in the army is being questioned. [Begin Adrada recording] That is a decision the commander of the Army must make. It is something we are evaluating, as you know, because the investigations are continuing. The Army commander will make the final decision. [end recording] Regarding Ricardo Lozano, the 13th Brigade commander simply said he had been transferred to a position similar to the one he held when the tragic events occurred. The new commander of the Presidential Guard is Colonel Jose Domingo Rubio, former commander of the Military School.
