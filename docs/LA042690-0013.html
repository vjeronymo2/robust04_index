April 26, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

SOVIET, EAST GERMAN APOLOGIES

It's remarkable and long overdue that East Germany openly acknowledges and apologizes for the horrors it perpetrated against Jews and others during the Nazi era.

I wonder if it will ever be possible for our own country to officially acknowledge and apologize for the horrors we visited on the blacks brought as slaves from Africa, the destruction of the Indians, and the conquest of northern Mexico.

We have begun to make restitution to the Japanese unjustly interned during World War II -- also along delayed and still blocked. Can we now go a step farther and do the same for the other victims of American history?

LORNE H. WARD

Garden Grove
