940524 FT 24 MAY 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea The Tea Broker's Association reports, strong general demand. East Africans sold well and prices often advanced 4 to 8 pence and at times more for selected lines. Central Africans were fully firm to dearer. Brighter Zimbabwes and Ceylons appreciated up to 10 pence. Others remained steady. Best available, 240p/kg. nom, good 160p/kg., good medium 135p/kg., medium 118p/kg., low medium 85p/kg. The highest price realised this week was 295p for a Rwanda pf. 1.
