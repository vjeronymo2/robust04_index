June 10, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

LES MORMONS UNDER FIRE

Thanks, but I don't need Daniels and York to show me how I as a woman am "suppressed." Let's compare notes in 10 years and see who has had the happier, more fulfilling, more contributing life -- they, the cross-dressed iconoclasts, or me, whose mother was "taught to make sure (I went) to Home Ec."

TAMRA TENNEY

San Dimas
