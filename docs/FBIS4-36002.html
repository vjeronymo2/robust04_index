BFN [Excerpt] Riyad, 31 May -- At the instructions of His Royal Highness Prince Salman Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz, governor of the Riyad region and chairman of the popular committee for helping the strugglers of Palestine, the sum of 5,088,230 Saudi riyals was paid out today from the revenue of the popular committee for helping the strugglers of Palestine in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The director-general of the popular committee for helping the strugglers of Palestine in the kingdom, 'Abd al-Rahman Jamus, said that the sum was the sixth installment which has been transferred from the committee's revenue to the Palestine Liberation Organization during this hijri year of 1414.
