Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Three senior Fatah activists in the Hebron region have submitted their resignations from their organizational posts in the movement. Our correspondent 'Abd-al-Wahhab Zuhdah reports that the three are Jibril al-Bakri, Dhiyab al-Sharabati, and Ihsan al-Nazir. According to them, they resigned in protest of the bad internal situation. Our correspondent adds that the three activists held prominent positions in Fatah.
