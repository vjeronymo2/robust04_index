920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / Olympic News in Brief: Volleyball win for Dutch The Netherlands overcame world champions Italy, the team who had dominated the game in the last four years and won the 1990 World Championship. The Italians recovered in the tiebreaker, saved three match points but finally fell to the big Dutch attack by 15-9, 12-15, 8-15, 15-2, 17-16.
