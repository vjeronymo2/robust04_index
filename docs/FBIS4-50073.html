BFN [From "News at One" program] [Text] China has criticized the American Assistant Secretary of State Winston Lord for planning to meet a released Chinese dissident. Mr. Lord has agreed to have talks in Washington later today with the dissident, Wang Juntao, who was sentenced to 13 years in jail but was allowed to leave China last month for medical treatment. A Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman said his government opposed anyone carrying out political activity against China by making use of Wang Juntao.
