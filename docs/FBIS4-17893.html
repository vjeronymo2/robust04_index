BFN [Unattributed report: "Russia Will Sell to Syria `Only Defense Weapons'"] [Text] Boris Kolokolov, Russian Federation deputy foreign minister, has stated that Russia will not sell offensive weapons to Syria. "The question now is only the selling of spare parts for weapons delivered earlier. We can only speak about the deliveries to Syria of spare parts and `defense weapons,'" he said. He also pointed out that Russia would give Tel Aviv "whatever help it can" in looking for Israeli soldiers taken prisoner or missing in action: "Oleg Soskovets, who is on a visit to Damascus, has already been instructed correspondingly," Mr. Kolokolov said.
