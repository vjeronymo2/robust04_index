Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] The Shenyang city anti-embezzlement bureau sponsored a press briefing on 31 January to inform journalist circles on the work results scored by the two city levels of procuratorial organs in investigating and handling economic crime in 1993. These procuratorial organs in Shenyang accepted 452 cases of embezzlement and bribes and placed 366 cases on file for investigation and prosecution. They investigated and handled 36 economic cases involving party and government organs, judicial departments, administrative departments, and law enforcement departments. (Ma Shiheng), former leader of the armed police brigade under the Shenyang city public security bureau, was arrested according to law on charges of embezzlement for accepting the largest amount of money ever in the processing of administrative accounts during his tenure. On file for investigation and prosecution were cases involving (Chen Shijie), director of the drug policy and administration section under the provincial public health department, and (Liu Shangqian), cadre at the section level of the provincial public health department, for taking advantage of their power on numerous occasions to approve new drugs and new factories in exchange for huge bribes by pharmaceutical factories and sale centers. Both were arrested.
