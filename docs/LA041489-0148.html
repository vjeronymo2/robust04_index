April 14, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

MUMAW SIGNS TO ATTEND CHRISTIAN HERITAGE

Stefan Mumaw of University High School has signed a letter of intent to play basketball at Christian Heritage College in El Cajon.

Mumaw, a 6-foot-9 center, was a two-year starter for the Trojans. He averaged 10 points and six rebounds per game last season. He is also considered one of the top volleyball players in Orange County.
