940129 FT 29 JAN 94 / National Trust man charged with theft A former senior National Trust executive is to appear in court charged with the theft of Pounds 57,994 from National Trust Enterprise, the charity's business arm. Mr Simon Jones, of Batcombe, Somerset, was charged with theft yesterday at Trowbridge, Wiltshire. He was released on bail to appear before West Wiltshire magistrates on April 14. Mr Jones was arrested in December last year.
