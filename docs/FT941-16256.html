940111 FT 11 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Molyneux Pounds 3.8m building buy Molyneux Estates has acquired the freehold office building, Wentworth House, in Eastern Avenue, Gants Hill, Essex, for Pounds 3.8m plus costs. The building is a 55,000 sq ft office block currently producing an annual rental income of some Pounds 480,000, and is let principally to the government which occupies some 38,000 sq ft on 15 and 20 year leases. Following the acquisition, Molyneux anticipates that its contracted net rent receivable will rise to Pounds 5.5m per annum from May.
