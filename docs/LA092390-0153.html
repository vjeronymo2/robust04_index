September 23, 1990, Sunday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSMAKERS

Robert Messina was named director of marketing for Toshiba Copier Products, a division of Irvine-based Toshiba America Information Systems Inc. He was formerly a regional vice president for Monroe Systems for Business in Irvine. Before that, Messina was vice president, sales and marketing, for Los Angeles Cellular Telephone Co. and vice president, sales and marketing, for American Datacom.
