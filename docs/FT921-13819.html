920118 FT 18 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Arjo sale fails Arjo Wiggins Appleton, the paper group, has failed for the second time in as many years to sell its 42.8 per cent stake in Soporcel, the Portugese pulp and paper maker. Yesterday it broke off negotiations with a consortium led by South Africa-based Mondi Paper, part of the Anglo American group.
