BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 15 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- According to the public opinion poll conducted by the Movement for Renewing the Union of Mongolian Students (UMS) with embracing some 650 students from six largest universities and colleges of the country, the majority of them are accusing the UMS of over-politicization regarding its activities which does not correspond to its rules. The UMS is actively participating in the political struggle supporting the MDA [Mongolian Democratic Association] and other oppositional parties who is blaming the government for corruption and incompetence and stands for its resignation. The political situation in the country is becoming heated and lasting for three daylong is the hunger strike of a group of representatives of the opposition at the main square of the capital. The most of respondents are in favour of renovation of activities, structure and composition of the UMS considering the leadership of the organization fails to express and protect the fundamental interests of the student youth.
