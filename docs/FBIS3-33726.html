Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Orumiyeh, West Azarbaijan Prov., Jan 31, IRNA -- A Turkish Foreign Ministry official here Monday expressed Ankara's deep regret over the bombardment of two villages in Sar Dasht by Turkish jet fighters on Friday. Ogur Dugan, director general at the Turkish Foreign Ministry, who arrived here heading a military delegation to inspect bombed regions and visit the wounded, said his speedy despatch indicated Turkey's deep regret over the incident. The team, who arrived here aboard a military plane, is to visit some of the wounded people transferred from Sar Dasht to Orumiyeh for treatment. Friday's air raid by Turkish jet fighters left nine villagers dead and 19 others wounded.
