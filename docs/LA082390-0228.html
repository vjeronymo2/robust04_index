August 23, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

STATE;

NO WINNERS FOR LOTTO JACKPOT

No one had all six winning numbers for the California Lottery's "Lotto 6-53" game Wednesday night.

The jackpot will roll over to an estimated $10 million for Saturday night's drawing, lottery officials said.

There were no tickets with five numbers plus the bonus number, but there were 93 tickets with five of six numbers, each winning $5,489. Another 6,753 tickets had four of six numbers and win $68 each. And there were 137,222 tickets with three of six numbers, each worth an automatic $5.
