BFN [Text] Israeli POW Ron Arad was until 1989 held prisoner in west Beirut under Syrian auspices. This, according to a report by German television which is to be aired tonight. According to the documentary, the information is based on East German and Russian intelligence documents. The prime minister's media adviser, 'Oded Ben-'Ami, has reacted to the report saying that the State of Israel continues to hold Iran and the organizations it supports responsible for the well-being of Ron Arad.
