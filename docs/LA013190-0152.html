January 31, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

P.M. BRIEFING;

OAKLAND-BURBANK FLIGHTS SLATED

Southwest Airlines announced today that it will start service April 16 between Burbank and Oakland, with fares as low as $29.

The airline said it will offer 10 round-trip flights a day starting about 7 a.m. from both Burbank and Oakland with the last flight from both airports leaving about 7 p.m.

The walk-up fare will be $59 each way, the airline said. Advance ticket fares will be as low as $29.

Southwest said it will begin taking reservations for its new Burbank air service today.
