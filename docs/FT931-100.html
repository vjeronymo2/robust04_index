930331 FT 31 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Land Securities in Pounds 29.5m deal Land Securities, the UK's largest property company, has bought two warehouses near Heathrow for Pounds 29.5m. The two buildings, which were bought on an initial yield of 10.25 per cent total 500,000 sq ft. OIL Property Investment, a UK property investment arm of German Hypo Bank, has bought a 145,000 sq ft office in Reading from Kumagai Gumi, the Japanese construction firm, for some Pounds 45m.
