BFN [Text] Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid, chairman of the Presidential Council, has issued Republican Decree No. 14 on the promotion of the two brothers, the defense minister and the chief of general staff. The decree provides for the promotion of brother Brigadier General Haytham Qasim Tahir, defense minister, to the rank of major general; and brother Colonel 'Umar 'Ali al-'Attas, the chief of general staff, to the rank of brigadier general. The decree comes into effect from the date of its issuance and its publication in the official gazette. Brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid also issued Republican Decree No. 13. It states: After taking cognizance of the document declaring the existence of the Democratic Republic of Yemen, and of the Constitution, and on the basis of the proposal of the prime minister and the defense minister, the Presidential Council decrees the following: 1) The appointment of Colonel 'Ali Qasim Salih as commander of the Navy; 2) the appointment of Colonel 'Abdallah Fadl Muqbil as commander of the Air Force and; 3) that this decree takes effect from the date of its issuance and publication in the official gazette.
