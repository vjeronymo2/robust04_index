BFN [Text] Peasants and farmers in Dhi Qar Governorate have carried out 36 percent of the manual harvest and 25 percent of mechanized harvest of wheat and barley projected for this season. The peasants and farmers are continuing to market their crops in the marketing centers of al-Nasiriyah, al-Sudaynawiyah, al-Shatrah, al- Fajr, al-Rifa'i, al-Dawwayah, al-Islah, and al-Batha' areas, where the committees supervising those centers pay the prices of the crops directly to the peasants and farmers.
