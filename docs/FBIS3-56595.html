Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Report by the DEMOKRATYCHNA UKRAYINA News Service: "Zaporizhzhya: An Accident at the Atomic Electric Power Station"] [Text] As reported by the Information Center of the Zaporizhzhya Atomic Electric Power Station [AES], on 12 January at 1000 [local time], [radioactive] contamination was revealed in a compressed-air pipeline in one of the auxiliary technological buildings. No contamination of the plant's territory, irradiation of the personnel, or discharges into the environment took place. There is no danger either for the population or for people working at the plant. It has been tentatively found that radioactive water entered the compressed-air pipeline from the fourth power unit due to the lack of tightness in one of the locking valves in the compressed-air line, which is used for processing the ion-exchange resins in filters of the special water-purification installation. Work on decontaminating the compressed-air pipeline has already been completed. Therefore, the incident was local in character and had no effect upon the plant's operation. A commission is investigating the causes of the accident.
