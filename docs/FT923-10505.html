920730 FT 30 JUL 92 / International Company News: Eastman Kodak climbs in quarter STRONG June sales were behind a small improvement in second-quarter profits at Eastman Kodak, the US photographic products group, agencies report from Rochester. Net income was Dollars 361m, or Dollars 1.11 a share, against Dollars 357m, or Dollars 1.10, in the same period last year. This brought net income for the first half to Dollars 506m, or Dollars 1.56 a share, against Dollars 535m, or Dollars 1.65, a year earlier. Fully diluted earnings per share were Dollars 1.51 against Dollars 1.64 for the half-year and Dollars 1.06 against Dollars 1.09 for the latest quarter. First-half sales tot-alled Dollars 9.75bn against Dollars 9.52bn last year with the second quarter generating Dollars 5.27bn, compared with Dollars 5.03bn. Mr Kay Whitmore, chairman, said he was confident that sales and earnings would be higher for the full year.
