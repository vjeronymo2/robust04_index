Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Fierce clashes continue to take place in the area of Dange-ia-Menha, in Cuanza Norte Province. This is the priority target for Luanda's Futungo de Belas Palace, which wants to eventually reach diamond-rich Lunda Norte and Lunda Sul Provinces. In the latest fighting, the Popular Movement for the Liberation of Angola-Labor Party, MPLA-Labor Party, forces lost one BM-21 rocket launcher and one TMM mobile bridge, suffered 101 soldiers confirmed killed on the ground, 160 soldiers who surrendered, and 30 soldiers who were captured. The fighting is continuing with the Armed Forces for the Liberation of Angola having the upper hand in terms of operational initiative, thereby frustrating this warmongering adventure by the warlords in Luanda.
