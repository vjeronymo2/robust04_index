930108 FT 08 JAN 93 / World Trade News: Environmental group lobbies Salinas on Nafta Grupo de los Cien, one of Mexico's leading environmental organisations, has asked President Carlos Salinas to include in the Nafta an environmental treaty that would protect marine animals, and thus prepare the way for the lifting of the US ban on Mexican tuna fish, writes Damian Fraser in Mexico City. It also wants further regulations on the movement of toxic wastes, and to establish a trilateral commission on trade and the environment.
