941111 FT 11 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Somic advances Cost controls enabled Somic, the maker of yarns and woven fabrics, to raise pre-tax profits from Pounds 133,453 to Pounds 155,788 in the half year to September 30, on turnover of Pounds 2.28m, against Pounds 2.24m. Earnings per share were 5.22p (4.47p) and the interim dividend is 1.25p (1p).
