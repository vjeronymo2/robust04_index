September 24, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

STATE;

4 DIE IN SAN LUIS OBISPO CRASH

A private jet crashed today as the pilot attempted to land in heavy morning fog at the municipal airport, killing at least four of the five people reported aboard, authorities said.

The twin-engine Cessna Citation, flying out of Van Nuys Airport, went down at 7:03 a.m. in a field about three miles northwest of the San Luis Obispo Airport, Federal Aviation Administration spokesman Fred O'Donnell said.
