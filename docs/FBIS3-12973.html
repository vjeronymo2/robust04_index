Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A responsible source at the public prosecution has told the Yemeni News Agency, SABA', that brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, has ordered the release of 1,400 male and female prisoners in the capital and the governorates of Aden, Ta'izz, Hadramaut, al-Hudaydah, Ibb, Dhamar, Hajjah, al-Mahwit, and al-Bayda', who have served more than half of their prison terms for crimes they committed and whose good conduct has been confirmed. This step is taken on the occasion of the blessed month of Ramadan. The brother chairman of the Presidential Council, chairman of the higher judiciary council, has also instructed the competent quarters to pay those who could not pay the blood money or fines decided by court against them and to overlook the terms to which they were sentenced in administrative and other lawsuits.
