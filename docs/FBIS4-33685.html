BFN [From the "Exclusive" program] [Text] Mozambican National Assembly President Marcelino Dos Santos has met Ramiro Remigio Ruiz Vergara, interim secretary of the Afro-Asian-Latin American People's Solidarity Organization [OSPAAAL], at the offices of that intercontinental solidarity organization in Havana City. The meeting was held in a warm and fraternal atmosphere, during which they exchanged ideas and information on OSPAAAL's current tasks and missions and on the situation in Africa.
