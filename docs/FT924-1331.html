921218 FT 18 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Optometrics falls to Dollars 29,000 Optometrics Corporation, the USM-quoted optical systems specialist based in Massachusetts, saw pre-tax profits for the six months to September 30 fall from Dollars 77,000 to Dollars 29,000 (Pounds 19,000). The company also announced the acquisition of Intercon, a US distributor of scientific instruments. No price was disclosed. Turnover for the half year was Dollars 1.76m (Dollars 1.56m) and earnings per share came out at 0.05 cents (0.6 cents). The company has called an extraordinary meeting to increase the authorised share capital from Dollars 120,000 to Dollars 200,000 and allow the company to buy up to 10 per cent of its capital in any fiscal year.
