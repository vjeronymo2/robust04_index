BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Turkey has announced that certain circles want to disrupt Turkey's bilateral relations with Germany and that one should refrain from indirectly encouraging these circles. Foreign Ministry spokesman Ferhat Ataman, replying to a question at his weekly news conference, said that Turkey and Germany are two friendly allies that have extensive, comprehensive and intense relations in all fields. Pointing out that one should not allow these relations to be adversely affected, Ataman said that one should also refrain from acts that might indirectly encourage such circles. He added that it is primarily the duty and responsibility of Germany to exert efforts to achieve this end.
