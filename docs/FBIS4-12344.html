BFN [Text] Brother President Abu-'Ammar [Yasir 'Arafat] received his excellency the PRC ambassador in Cairo this afternoon. The brother president entrusted the PRC ambassador with a verbal message for the Chinese president in connection with the recent developments in the Palestinian arena and the talks currently under way on the implementation of the declaration of principles agreement, the agreements signed in Cairo, and the Israeli attempts to disrupt their implementation, a matter which is threatening the peace process in the Middle East.
