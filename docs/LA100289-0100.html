October 2, 1989, Monday, Orange County P.M. Final

LOCAL;

MAN KILLED IN SANTA ANA SHOOTING

A 24-year-old man was shot to death as he stood with friends on a sidewalk in Santa Ana in what police today described as a gang-related drive-by shooting.

Santa Ana Police Sgt. Doyle Smith said today that Gerardo Veloz of Santa Ana was fatally wounded by shots fired Sunday night from a passing car while he was standing on a sidewalk in the 500 block of South Spruce Street.

Veloz was pronounced dead about 9:30 p.m. No other people were injured, and no arrests have been made. Smith said police have only sketchy information about the vehicle involved in the shooting.
