BFN [Text] According to the press service of the People's Movement of Belarus, the Slavic Assembly Belaya Rus is going to picket the U.S. Embassy in Minsk today. The aim of the picket is to protest the bombing of civilian sites by NATO forces in Yugoslavia. A session of the Belarusian Committee of Solidarity with Serbs and Montenegrins will be held today at the Presidium of Belarusian Academy of Sciences.
