BFN [Excerpts] [passage omitted] The provisional Palestinian Authority held its first official meeting in Tunis. The work plan for the coming period and the appointment of the remaining members of the first governmental team -- which is as old as the self-rule -- are on the agenda. [passage omitted] [Unidentified correspondent] Far away from the self-rule territory, 19 of the 24 members of the provisional Palestinian authority -- including Hanan Ashrawi, Yasir 'Abd-Rabbuh, and Samir Ghawshah -- will hold the first official meeting in Tunis today under the chairmanship of President Yasir 'Arafat. The transitional government for the formation of the self-rule will discuss its work plan for the coming period and will be sworn in at an official ceremony immediately after the appointment of the remaining members and the distribution of the portfolios.
