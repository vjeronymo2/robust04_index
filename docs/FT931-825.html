930327 FT 27 MAR 93 / Finance and the Family: Smaller companies index slides - At a Glance Small company shares fell back in the wake of the Budget. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) fell 0.5 per cent from 1383.21 to 1376.54 over the week to March 25. The Nat West Smaller Companies index also dropped 0.5 per cent from 1092.54 to 1087.19.
