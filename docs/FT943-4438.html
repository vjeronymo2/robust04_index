940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / World News in Brief: BR says it ran half its trains British Rail said it ran about half its passenger service during the latest 24-hour strike by signal workers, but strikers' leader Jimmy Knapp put the proportion at one-third. A train and two cars were in near-collision at a crossing in Long Eaton, Derbyshire, and a train was derailed at Bickley, Kent. No one was hurt. Picture, Page 9
