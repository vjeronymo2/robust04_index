Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Irkutsk, February 9, RIA -- Irkutsk saw the establishment of an organising committee of the Russian presidential party of reformers on the initiative of the local branch of the Christian Democratic Union. The committee brought together local politicians, entrepreneurs and intellectuals. The committee made a public statement today, which stresses that Boris Yeltsin is the only guarantor of stability and abidance by the constitution.
