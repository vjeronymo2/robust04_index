July 11, 1989, Tuesday, San Diego County Edition

MORNING REPORT: FACES/PLACES

For the first time in a decade, Phyllis George will co-host the Miss America Pageant when this year's edition takes place in September in Atlantic City. George, who won the Miss America crown in 1970, will be working with Gary Collins, who has hosted the event for the last seven years. ALEENE MacMINN
