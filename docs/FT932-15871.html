930408 FT 08 APR 93 / UK Company News: OIS Intl OIS International Inspection, the non-destructive testing specialist which came to the market via a placing in December, produced pre-tax profits of Pounds 1.74m for 1992, an increase of 10 per cent over prospectus estimates. Turnover was Pounds 46.8m. Earnings per share amounted to 5.9p against a forecast 5.5p but as indicated there is no final dividend.
