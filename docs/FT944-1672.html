941219 FT 19 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Oceonics in the red as finance director leaves Oceonics Group, the lossmaking marine survey company, announced the departure of its finance director and cuts in executive salaries after sliding into the red with losses of Pounds 1.69m in the half year to September 30. It said Mr Mike Hutchison would be leaving the group at the end of the current financial year, in which it has cut 131 jobs - 20 per cent of the workforce - amid weak demand and over-capacity in the offshore oil industry. The company said Mr David Arnold, the newly appointed group commercial director, would take over as finance director from Mr Hutchison. The costs associated with Mr Arnold's appointment were being matched with voluntary cuts in director compensation. The loss, against pre-tax profits of Pounds 222,000 last time, was on turnover down from Pounds 13.2m to Pounds 8.19m. Losses per share were 1.2p (0.2p).
