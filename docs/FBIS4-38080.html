BFN [Text] Approximately 100 people were killed or injured yesterday during an air attack against Herat city. A Bakhtar Information Agency correspondent reports from Herat city that two jets belonging to Dostam's militia bombed the crowded parts of Herat city yesterday. As a result, eight civilians were killed and around 90 people--including women and children--were injured. The report adds that this savage attack occurred on the eve of a meeting of the country's governors in Herat and was an attempt to undermine this meeting. It was reported that the meeting is scheduled to be held in two days--on 28 June. In addition to the country's governors, a number of social and scientific personalities from in and out of Afghanistan will attend it. It is expected that a number of leaders of the jihadi groups will also participate.
