Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] Karlsruhe (DPA) -- The investigating judge of the Federal Court of Justice (BGH) has issued an arrest warrant for a 51-year-old businessman from the Munich area on suspicion of espionage activity. The Federal Prosecutor's Office announced in Karlsruhe today that the man, who is alleged to have spied for the former GDR for at least 11 years, has been in investigative custody since 14 January. The accused is said to have supplied the former GDR Ministry of State Security (MfS) with computer hardware and software as well as files on military technology. The "betrayed extensive and valuable material" is said to have been handed over during at least 80 meetings on the highway to Berlin. According to the Karlsruhe authority the accused was in regular secret service contact with the science and technology section of the MfS Main Administration from 1978 to November 1989. The Federal Prosecutor's Office has commissioned the Federal Criminal Investigations Office (BKA) with the investigations.
