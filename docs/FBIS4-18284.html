BFN [Text] The Corsican National Liberation Front-Historic Branch [FLNC-Canal Historique] today released a statement claiming responsibility for the commando action at Sperone (Southern Corsica) during which 14 people were arrested. According to the statement there is "no question of giving up the armed struggle." As far as the FLNC is concerned the Sperone tourist complex is "one of the symbols of the despoilment of Corsican land by a speculators' mafia."
