BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 21 Jun--Eduard Shevardnadze, chairman of the Georgian parliament and head of state, announced at today's session of parliament that he had a telephone conversation with Russian Federation Defense Minister Pavel Grachev. The conversation was held at the initiative of the Russian defense minister. Grachev informed Shevardnadze that Russia's Federation Council met today. The Council examined the Russian president's decree authorizing the deployment of peacekeeping troops in Abkhazia. Grachev said that the two-hour meeting had been tense, with a great number of questions having been asked. There was a vote, during which President Yeltsin's decree was approved by an absolute majority of votes. Therefore, the introduction of Russian Federation peacekeeping troops to the region of Abkhazia will begin 22 June.
