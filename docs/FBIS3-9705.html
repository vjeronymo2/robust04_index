Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Warsaw, 28 March--In a poll carried out by CBOS [Public Opinion Research Center], 36 percent expressed the belief that the president has too much power. The opposite view was held by 22 percent, while 29 percent said he has as much power as he should. The CBOS poll was carried out between 10 and 14 March on a representative sample of 1,228 of Poland's adult population. The majority (60 percent) of the respondents expressed the belief that the president's actions weakened the state, and only 17 percent had the opposite view; 23 percent of the respondents had no view on the issue.
