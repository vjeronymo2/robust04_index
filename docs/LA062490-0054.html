June 24, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

TURNING DOWN TAP

The photo of the fountain outside the San Diego County Administration Center, published in The Times June 19, apparently was taken before the Board of Supervisors directed that ornamental fountains at all county government buildings be shut down. As California enters its fourth year of drought, the county is turning down the tap on water use at all of its facilities.

ROBERT A. LERNER

Public Affairs Manager

County of San Diego
