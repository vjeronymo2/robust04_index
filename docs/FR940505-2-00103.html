L. 92&hyph;463, as amended), the National Science Foundation announces the following meeting: Name: Special Emphasis Panel in Electrical & Communication Systems. Date & Time: May 23, 1994, 8 a.m&hyph;5 p.m. Place: National Science Foundation, 4201 Wilson Blvd., room 530, Arlington, Virginia 22230.
