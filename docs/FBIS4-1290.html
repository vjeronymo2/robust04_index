BFN [Text] Postmansberg April 26 SAPA -- A bomb exploded at a shop in Boichoko township, Postmasburg in the Northern Cape on Tuesday [26 April] causing damage to goods and property worth about R10,000 [rand], SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reports. A police spokesman said the Tuesday morning explosion shattered the shop's windows and damages roof tiles. No-one was injured. Police in the Northern Cape said they had reacted to 10 false bomb threats in the area.
