BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] Education Secretary Jose Angel Pescador Osuna inaugurated the Fourth National Conference of Industrial and Technological School Directors. Pescador indicated that technological training is as fundamental as basic education. [Begin Pescador recording] Aside from Mexico's fundamental objective of advancing its basic education through a national program, our hopes for the future might best be met by promoting technological training and research. [end recording] Raul Gonzalez, director general for industrial and technological training, announced the guidelines for the school period beginning in September. Raul Panan, deputy secretary for technological training and research, announced that Mexico and Japan will sign a cooperation agreement to create the national teachers' training center in July. The Japanese Government will supply machinery and high-tech equipment. The General Directorate of Industrial and Technological Education includes 424 schools and 403,000 students throughout the country.
