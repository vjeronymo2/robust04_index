BFN [Text] Rome, April 28 (AFP) -- A U.S. F-18 fighter-bomber crashed at sea Thursday [28 April] shortly after taking off for a surveillance flight over Bosnia from the U.S. aircraft carrier Saratoga, the NATO Southern Command in Naples said. The plane plunged into the sea shortly after take-off. No details were immediately available on the cause of the accident in which the pilot was killed, NATO said. The pilot was on his way to Bosnia to help enforce a no-fly zone over the territory.
