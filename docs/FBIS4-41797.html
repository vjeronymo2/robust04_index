BFN [Text] Kiev -- On 27 June UNIAN learned preliminary data about the voting in various oblasts for candidates in the presidential election. This data was received from oblast electoral commissions for the presidential election and from oblast councils of people's deputies. UNIAN also received preliminary data from the Central Electoral Commission for the election of a president of Ukraine. In most cases the data does not include several rayons in each oblast. Leonid Kravchuk received more votes than the other candidates for president in the following oblasts: Vinnitsa, Volyn, Zhitomir, Ivano-Frankovsk, Rovno, Kiev, Kirovograd, Lvov, Sumy, Ternopol, Khmelnitskiy, Cherkassy, Chernovtsy, and Transcarpathia Oblasts and the city of Kiev. Leonid Kuchma received more votes than the other candidates in the following oblasts: Dnepropetrovsk, Donetsk, Zaporozhye, Lugansk, Nikolayev, Odessa, Chernigov oblasts, the Republic of Crimea, and the city of Sevastopol. Information from Kharkov Oblast is being finalized. UNIAN has learned from reliable sources that the picture there is ambiguous; in some rayons Oleksandr Moroz is well in the lead, but in some rayons either Leonid Kravchuk or Leonid Kuchma is slightly in the lead. In the town of Izyum, Volodymyr Lanovyy received 23 percent. Information on Kherson and Poltava Oblasts is being finalized.
