June 18, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Denver Woos Mexicana Airlines: Denver has joined several other cities in the quest to wrest Mexicana Airlines' North American headquarters from Los Angeles, which newly privatized Mexicana says is too costly.

Denver Mayor Federico Pena and Colorado Gov. Roy Romer have sent letters to Mexicana, saying Denver has low-cost office space. The headquarters will create 210 jobs wherever it relocates. Other cities reportedly in the running for the facility include Dallas, San Antonio and Tampa.
