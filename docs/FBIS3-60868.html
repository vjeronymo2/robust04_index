Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Makhachkala, 27 Dec -- All four terrorists who kidnapped hostages in Rostov-on-Don on 23 December have been identified. The leader of the gang is Arslan Kasimov, born in 1945, a native of Azerbaijan, who has a criminal record. Abdulgafar Abduzhalimov, a native of Tashkent, born in 1972, is a fifth-year student at the faculty of applied mechanics of Tashkent University. Anatoliy Mikheyev, born in 1949, a native of Sverdlovsk, Russia, is a navigator by profession and was discharged from the Air Force for drunkenness; recently he has been unemployed. The fourth thug is Marat Kauzbekov, born in 1974, native of Tashkent, a Kazakh and second-year student at Tashkent Institute of Architecture. Nearly all of the $10 million handed over to the terrorists has been recovered, except for a small sum which, according to gang leader Arslan Kasimov, was thrown out of the helicopter over Chechen territory.
