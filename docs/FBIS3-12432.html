Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] In a massive riot in the al-Zaytun neighborhood of Gaza, a 17-year-old Palestinian was killed. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that he was shot by soldiers, who also injured 20 other Palestinians. They were taken for treatment in local hospitals. In the Jordan Rift Valley, the hunt continues for the terrorist who this morning entered a hothouse in Gitit and fired at one of the workers, but caused no damage. In Rafah, a youth was badly injured by soldiers after he threw rocks at them. In the Bedouin village of Tel Sheva' in the Negev, 10 people were injured in a violent clash between the police and demonstrators protesting the Hebron killings. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan reports that the police detained six of the demonstrators. The policemen were pelted with rocks and had to use tear gas to disperse the demonstrating Bedouin. Riots also took place in the villages of Kusayfah, 'Aro'er, and Laggiyah. In al-Tirah in the triangle, a police officer was stabbed in the back a short while ago. Our correspondent Yafa Glick reports that he was taken to the Me'ir Hospital. [passage omitted]
