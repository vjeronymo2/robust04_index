Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] A Foreign Office spokesman has said in Islamabad today that Jammu and Kashmir is a disputed territory and a final settlement of this dispute through a fair and impartial plebiscite under the UN auspices is pending. Referring to Indian claims on Kashmir, the spokesman said that such unilateral claims by India is tantamount to depriving Kashmiris of their inalienable right to self-determination. The spokesman called upon the Indian Government to settle this very important issue by ending the oppressive activities being carried out by its security forces in occupied Kashmir.
