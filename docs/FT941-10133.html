940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / International Company News: Mr Kirk Kinnear - Correction We are happy to make it clear that Mr Kirk Kinnear was not recruited to run the growing swaps business at MG Corp, as stated on January 10. He joined the company shortly before the end of its fiscal year, ended September, 1993 as vice-president, crude arbitrage. He was not involved with the company's trading in the derivatives markets.
