910508 FT 08 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Delyn rise checked by French losses Losses at Plastella, the French subsidiary, checked taxable profits at Delyn Group, a manufacturer of consumer packaging, for the year ended February 3. The figure was Pounds 614,000 compared with Pounds 610,000 and was struck on turnover ahead at Pounds 13.47m (Pounds 11.76m). Earnings declined to 4.47p (5.01p) per share. An unchanged final dividend of 1p makes a same-again 1.45p total.
