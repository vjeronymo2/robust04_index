BFN [Untitled "Own Information" report] [Text] NOVAYA YEZHEDNEVNAYA GAZETA has learned from unofficial sources that Federal Counterintelligence Service [FCS] chief S. Stepashin is aware of plans to merge the Main Protection Directorate, which is accountable to the president, and the FCS.
