930429 FT 29 APR 93 / UK Company News: Critchley acquires BT Label Centre Critchley Group, a manufacturer of electrical cable accessories and identification products, has paid Pounds 1.5m for the trade and assets of the BT Label Centre from British Telecommunications. BT Label Centre produces specialist plastic labels. In the year to March 31 1993 it turned in sales of Pounds 3.4m and a profit before interest and tax of some Pounds 200,000, after a non-recurring charge of Pounds 50,000. Assets being purchased have a value of not less than Pounds 1m, including fixed of Pounds 465,000.
