940114 FT 14 JAN 94 / Carey launches Sudan appeal Dr George Carey, the archbishop of Canterbury, yesterday launched an appeal for Sudan, following his recent trip to the country torn by civil war. The appeal - Help the People of Sudan - is being set up by an ecumenical group to supply humanitarian aid throughout the country regardless of race or religion. Funds will be administered by the Roman Catholic aid agency Cafod, Christian Aid and the Church Missionary Society through their partners in Sudan.
