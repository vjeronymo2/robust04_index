BFN [Text] Beijing, April 8 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman expressed profound condolences here today on the death of President Cyprian Ntaryamira of Burundi and President Juvenal Habyarimana of Rwanda. Deep sympathy was also expressed for their families. China hopes that this event will not adversely affect stability in their countries nor the on-going peace process in Burundi and Rwanda, the spokesman said.
