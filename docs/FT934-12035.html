931028 FT 28 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Allied-Lyons opens Russian ice-cream plant Allied-Lyons, the drinks, food and retailing group, has celebrated the topping out of a Dollars 30m (Pounds 19.8m) Baskin-Robbins ice-cream manufacturing plant in Moscow. The plant, when fully operational, will produce up to 8m gallons of ice-cream a year for Baskin-Robbins' expanding chain of shops and kiosks in Russia's main cities. Since 1988, 12 stores and 50 kiosks have been opened in Moscow, St Petersburg and Novgorod. Ice-cream supplied from the US is also being sold in 10 hard currency stores in Moscow. The ice-cream plant will employ 170 Russian staff.
