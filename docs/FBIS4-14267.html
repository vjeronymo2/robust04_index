BFN [Text] In the Kashmir valley, eight persons, including two militants and two political workers, were killed in the militancy-related incidents during the last 24 hours. Ghulam Qadir Janbaz, a National Conference worker, was shot dead by the militants at Vanabal and Mr. Abdus Salam Salar of Jamaat-i-Islami was killed at Nurbagh by the militants. Security forces arrested seven militants during search operation and recovered two AK rifles, three pistols, five grenades, one rocket launched, and a large quantity of ammunition. Meanwhile, in Jammu and Kashmir, a vital suspension bridge over the Chenab near Loda was burned by the militants today. This has cut off a vital link to about 40 villages of Doda Tehsil near Jodhpur village. The bridge has been constructed about 25 years ago.
