930429 FT 29 APR 93 / People: Moves in finance Takeshi Kirito has been appointed chief executive of The NIKKO BANK (UK); he succeeds Takeshi Kato who returns to Japan. Masashi Kaneko has been appointed chairman of NIKKO EUROPE and chief operating officer Europe Group; he replaces Masao Inagaki, who returns to Tokyo. Taijiro Goto has been appointed md of the European Administration Division, replacing Koji Mitsumori who also returns to Tokyo. Hideo Abe, deputy md, has been appointed md of Nikko Europe's corporate finance division. Charles Bromley, Peter Clarke and Malcolm Markovitch have been appointed directors of NIKKO EUROPE.
