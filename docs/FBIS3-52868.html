Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri has stated that his meeting with Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad dealt with the internal situation in Lebanon, issues of common interest, the regional situation, the scheduled summit meeting between U.S. President Bill Clinton and President al-Asad, and the insistence of Lebanon and Syria on total Israeli withdrawal from the occupied Lebanese and Syrian territory. Regarding the agreement to establish a joint Lebanese-Syrian power station, Prime Minister al-Hariri said it is a practical measure to implement the Brotherhood, Cooperation, and Coordination Agreement between the two countries. Prime Minister al-Hariri added that the government's efforts for development and reconstruction are manifested daily through the projects which are being implemented. He noted that the political situation is cohesive as a result of consultations and understanding among the constitutional institutions.
