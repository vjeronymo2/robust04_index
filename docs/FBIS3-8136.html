Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Bratislava, March 3 (CTK) -- The Slovak Finance Ministry has officially announced the introduction of a 10-percent import surcharge on consumer goods to be applied as from Thursday [3 March]. The surcharge is to help improve the country's payments and trade balance. It is in harmony with regulations of GATT and the Czech-Slovak Customs Union Council, the announcement said.
