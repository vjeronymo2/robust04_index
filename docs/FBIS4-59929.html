BFN [Report by security affairs correspondent Sharon Shade] [Excerpts] Estonian Defense Minister Indrek Kannik arrived in Israel yesterday for a four-day visit as the guest of Yitzhaq Rabin, prime minister and defense minister. The visiting minister will visit Israel Aircraft Industries [IAI] factories and will be briefed on the IAI's activity in the sphere of overhauling planes and drones. Mr. Kannik will meet with the prime minister and senior defense establishment officials today. He will also visit the Israel Military Industries [IMI] and Elbit Computers. [passage omitted] Czech Defense Minister Antonin Baudys will arrive in Israel next Thursday for a five-day official visit. Baudys will meet with the prime minister and senior defense establishment officials and will visit various defense industries. Next month, Israel will participate for the first time in a weapons and military technology exhibition in the Czech Republic. Israel's exhibit, which is being organized by the Defense Ministry's Defense Sales Department, will include IMI and REFA'EL [Armament Development Authority] booths.
