921202 FT 02 DEC 92 / Fiery protest Thousands of farmers from across Europe gather around a bonfire in the Place de Bordeaux, Strasbourg, for a rally against the EC-US trade deal and farm reforms. About 50,000 farmers took part in the demonstration yesterday, which began in a festive atmosphere but was marred by sporadic violence later as fireworks were thrown and police foiled an attempt to storm the European Parliament.
