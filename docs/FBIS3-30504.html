Language: Bulgarian Article Type:BFN [Text] An Ukrainian cargo ship refused to stop at Ruse port, as we have just learned from our local correspondent Natasha Dimitrova. The ship is called Khabarovsk and is transporting six barges of iron ore for the port of Linz. Around 2300 local time (2100 GMT] on 1 February, the vessel's captain, in a telephone conversation, refused to obey the order of the officials on duty from the Bulgarian State Navigation Inspectorate to stop and await the embargo investigation. According to the latest instructions, such an investigation should take place during the day. The captain explained that he was in a hurry for the obligatory investigation that was awaiting him in Calafat. The investigation teams in Calafat, where the ship is expected to arrive tomorrow morning, were briefed on the case.
