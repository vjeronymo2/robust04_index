June 18, 1989, Sunday, Bulldog Edition

WARRIORS RAISE THEIR TICKET PRICES

The Golden State Warriors announced Tuesday that ticket prices for the 1989-90 season will increase by an average of 21 percent.

The increases will range from $1.50 for the least expensive seats to $5 for the highest-priced seats at the 15,025-seat Oakland Coliseum, the club said.

Ticket prices will now range from $7.50 to $28.
