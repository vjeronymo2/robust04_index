940926 FT 26 SEP 94 / Emerging Markets: News round-up - Bombay Turnover on India's 23 bourses is forecast to triple over the next three years, with capitalisation doubling to Dollars 300bn, says Barclays de Zoete Wedd (Asia), Reuter reports. India has been one of this year's best performing emerging markets, up 24 per cent in dollar terms so far.
