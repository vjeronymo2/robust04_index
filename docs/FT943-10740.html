940803 FT 03 AUG 94 / UK Company News: RPC pays Pounds 1.2m for assets RPC, the rigid packaging group, has bought assets for Pounds 1.2m from Lawson Mardon Packaging, a division of Alusuisse Lonza of Zurich. The assets are at Lawson Mardon Plastic's industrial containers division in Sutton-in-Ashfield, Nottinghamshire.
