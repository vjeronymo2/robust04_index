Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A bilateral agreement on mutual delegating of authorities will be signed by Russia and Tatarstan on March 13, i.e. before the second round of elections to the Russian Federation's Federal Assembly, Tatarstan's president Mintimer Shaymiyev said speaking in Kazan on Tuesday. Last December the elections to the Russian Federation's Federal Assembly did not take place in Tatarstan because of low turnout.
