May 19, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

TANSLEY WILL ATTEND CAL STATE FULLERTON

Mike Tansley, a former Dana Hills High School track and cross-country athlete who attends Long Beach City College, has signed a national letter of intent to attend Cal State Fullerton.

Tansley, who will have three years of eligibility to compete for the Titans' cross-country and track teams, finished fifth in the 1988 California state cross-country championships in his senior year at Dana Hills.
