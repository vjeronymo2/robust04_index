940128 FT 28 JAN 94 / Spanish strike sparks clashes Spanish riot police clashed with pickets in Madrid yesterday as trade unions staged a 24-hour nationwide strike in protest at the government's plans to reform the labour market. There were similar incidents in other cities, with dozens of people reported injured. The unions claimed that 8m workers took part in the stoppage but employers said 70 per cent of businesses operated normally. Report, Page 2
