920408 FT 08 APR 92 / UK Company News: Wescol Wescol Group, the Halifax-based steel fabrication company, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 379,000 for the six months to January 31. The outcome compared with a deficit of Pounds 180,000 in the corresponding period and losses of Pounds 1.21m for this USM-quoted company's last full year. Turnover dived 35 per cent to Pounds 8.67m. Losses per share emerged at 3.6p (1.1p).
