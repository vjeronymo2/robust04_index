February 24, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Viejo Bancorp: The holding company for Mission Viejo National Bank reported record net earnings of $2.3 million for 1989, 42% more than the $1.6 million for 1988. For the fourth quarter, net income rose 25%, to $636,000 from $507,000. Total assets reached $131.4 million, 62% more than the $80.9 million reported at the end of 1988.
