930816 FT 16 AUG 93 / World News in Brief: European monetary system Currencies in the grid continued to draw apart, two weeks after the reform of the exchange rate mechanism. The strongest currency, the Dutch guilder, is now separated from the weakest, the Danish krone, by 8.43 percentage points, more than half the permitted divergence of 15 per cent.
