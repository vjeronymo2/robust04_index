May 20, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The field of candidates to become coach of the Atlanta Hawks has been narrowed to two -- Orlando Magic assistant Bob Weiss and Willis Reed, vice president of the New Jersey Nets.

Said Stan Kasten, Hawk president: "It's down to those two and, frankly, we're having a difficult time choosing between them." The final selection will not be announced until at least Wednesday, Kasten said.
