920318 FT 18 MAR 92 / International Company News: Elektrowatt up 7% to SFr209m ELEKTROWATT, the diversified electricity generating and industrial group, reported a 7.2 per cent rise in consolidated net income to SFr209m (Dollars 138m) in the year to the end of September 1991, writes Ian Rodger in Zurich. The group's first ever consolidated financial statements showed that sales rose 6.1 per cent to SFr4.5bn. Group equity and reserves totalled SFr2.1bn at the end of the year, and SFr1.6bn of them were attributable to the parent company, Elektrowatt AG, in which CS Holding has a 45.3 per cent voting stake. Elektrowatt AG had record net income of SFr87.2m in its year to January 31 1992.
