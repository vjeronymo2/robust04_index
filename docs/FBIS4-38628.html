BFN [From the "Morning Information Review" newscast] [Text] Oleg Soskovets, first deputy chairman of the Russian Government, received in Moscow Cuban Ambassador Rogelio Montenegro, with whom he discussed aspects of economic commercial relations, including the fulfillment of the agreement on supplying fuel to the island and equipment for power plants in exchange for sugar. Both parties noted that scheduled fuel deliveries are proceeding normally. The first part of the contract will conclude this coming June.
