940525 FT 25 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Rathbone Rathbone Brothers, the asset management and private banking group, has acquired Evensound, a London-based business comprising the introduction of private client investment accounts and the provision of executive services. Consideration is via the issue of 185,387 new ordinary shares, valuing the acquisition at Pounds 515,000. The purchase will boost funds under the management of Rathbone by Pounds 35m.
