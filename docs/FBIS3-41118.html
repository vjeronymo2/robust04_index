Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Cape Town Jan 25 SAPA -- Police seized 50,000 mandrax tablets with a street value of almost R[rand]1 million on Tuesday evening during a raid on a Grassy Park, Cape Town house, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reports. Seven people, including two women and a Johannesburg man, were arrested during the operation by members of the Wynberg Narcotics Bureau.
