910510 FT 10 MAY 91 / International Company News: Motorola plans Indian offshoot MOTOROLA, the US electronics group, is seeking New Delhi approval to set up an export-oriented company with a string of manufacturing units. The proposed Indian subsidiary will make semiconductors, portable and mobile telecom systems, cellular mobile radio equipment and radio paging systems. Motorola, which intends to expand its presence in India, will be setting up a software export centre at Bangalore which will largely cater for the requirements of Motorola group. It is also participating in several joint ventures in India to make a data communication equipment. The Indian partners in the first two ventures will be Blue Star and Meltron.
