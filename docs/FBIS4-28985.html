BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 10 (KCNA) -- Salt production is increasing in Korea. At salt works of the country the salt production in four months of this year increased more than 20 percent compared with the same period of last year. The Yonbaek Salt Works which began the production of salt one week earlier than last year has overfulfilled its monthly quotas by 47 percent on an average by actively introducing advanced production methods. The Kwangyangman salt works has produced several thousand more tons of salt in this period. Kuisong and Namyang salt works which fulfilled their may plans respectively at 102 and 105 percent are carrying out their daily salt production this month at more than 130 percent. The salt works across the country are exerting all their efforts to attain the goal of increasing production for this month 50 percent as against last month.
