940813 FT 13 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Engl & Caledonian The Gartmore-managed English & Caledonian Investment lifted net asset value by 21 per cent - from 191.2p to 231.6p per share - during the 12 months to June 30. Net revenue edged ahead to Pounds 200,000 (Pounds 192,000) for earnings of 4.46p (4.28p) per share. A proposed final dividend of 2.75p maintains the total at 4p.
