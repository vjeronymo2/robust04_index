Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] [passage omitted] The security services have announced also that a 41-old Russian national, Konstantin Kokoshin, was murdered in Saoula in Tipaza province. The security services have pointed out the body of the Russian national was discovered around 1100 hours today inside a Toyota car, which belonged to the Russian embassy, parked at a bus station at Saoula. The security services have added that their investigation following the discovery of the murder have enabled them to eliminate four armed elements and to recover three automatic revolvers and a sawn-off shotgun. The initial investigation have established, however, that those killed were involved in the murder of the Russian national and the foreign ministry official, who was murdered at Bourdj el Kiffan this morning.
