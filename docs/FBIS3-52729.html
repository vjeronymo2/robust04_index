Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The confrontation between settlers and the security forces at the Tzofim junction on the Qalqilyah-Nabulus road ended a short while ago. Our correspondent Gil Litman reports that about 10 settlers were detained and some settlers were injured in the course of the confrontation. The head of the Samaria Local Council acted to soothe the flaring tempers. Army and police troops are still in the area, which was declared a closed military zone. The settlers were protesting the deteriorating state of security.
