921104 FT 04 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Fairway expands in marketing FAIRWAY GROUP, the stationery and educational supplies distributor, is buying Spectrum Marketing Services for a maximum Pounds 5.2m. Fairway also warned that with the economic climate deteriorating the trading performance for the 1992 year would be worse than the Pounds 1.77m achieved in 1991. However, the total dividend would be maintained at 3.15p. Spectrum offers a wide range of marketing services. Turnover has grown from Pounds 1.8m in 1990 to Pounds 5.5m in 1992. The consideration is being satisfied by an initial payment of Pounds 2.7m of which Pounds 2.2m will be cash and the balance in shares. Further profit-related payments to a maximum of Pounds 2.5m will be satisfied 40 per cent in shares and the rest in 7 per cent unsecured loan stock 1993-95.
