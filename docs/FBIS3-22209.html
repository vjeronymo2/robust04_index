Language: English Article Type:CSO [Text] On December 29 the President of Russia signed a decree on the resignation of Alexei Yablokov, adviser for ecology. ``I have resigned at my own will,'' Alexei Yablokov told an MN correspondent. ``I have not seen the President in over six months. Besides, there is much talk about the abolition of the office of advisers. Why should I wait? I shall work at the Security Council. Meanwhile, the ecological situation in Russia, as most experts admit, is now close to disastrous.''
