Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Radio Pretoria will terminate its broadcasts from Thursday [3 February] morning, the station's management committee chairman Dr Mossie van den Berg announced on Wednesday night. In a statement, the station said the decision was in "anticipation" of an announcement by Home Affairs Minister Danie Schutte on Thursday. "The announcement follows discussions between the management committee of Radio Pretoria and representatives of all private braodcasting stations." Talks were held between the radio's management commitee and Mr Schutte on Wednesday. The Radio Pretoria statement was handed to a SAPA correspondent.
