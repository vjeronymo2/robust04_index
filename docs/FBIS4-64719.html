BFN [Excerpt] Government spokesman Evangelos Venizelos commented on an Albanian television report that said that the issue of Tsamidhes [Muslims living in Greece who went to Albania after World War II] was discussed at the recent meeting between Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias and Albanian Foreign Minister Alfred Serreqi in Zurich. Venizelos said the statements are inaccurate. Venizelos added that even if the Albanian side had raised such an issue, Greece would not have agreed to discuss it. [passage omitted]
