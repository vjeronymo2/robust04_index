September 26, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

NOTES

Allergan Inc. has obtained the rights to market two products from Burroughs Wellcome Co., while Burroughs obtained the rights to certain sunscreen technology developed by Allergan's skin-care unit. Terms of the agreements were not undisclosed. Under the agreement, Irvine-based Allergan will market a treatment for scabies and an ophthalmic anti-infective drug.
