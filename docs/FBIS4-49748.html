BFN [Text] Beijing, May 6 (XINHUA) -- General Fu Quanyou, member of the Central Military Commission, met with Lieutenant-General Zbigniew Zalewski, deputy chief of the general staff and logistic general of the Polish Armed Forces, and his party here today. Fu, also director of the general logistics department of the Chinese People's Liberation Army (PLA), said that the visit will deepen the mutual understanding and friendship between the two armed forces. Zalewski said the relations between the two armed forces are developing. He expressed the belief that the visit would enhance exchanges between the logistic departments. After the meeting, Fu hosted a banquet for the visitors. The polish guests are here on a friendly visit at the invitation of the PLA's General Logistics Department.
