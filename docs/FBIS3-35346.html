Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Asyut, 23 Feb (MENA)--Security troops in Asyut this morning mounted a search campaign in the city of al-Badari. Various police departments from the Asyut Governorate took part in the operation. While the security forces were performing their routine work, terrorist elements fired on them, wounding Major Majdi Sulayman Musa, First Lieutenant Yasir Kamal Ibrahim, and Lieutenant 'Abd-al- Muhsin Husayn. Two terrorists, one of them known to be dangerous, were also wounded. The clashes between the terrorists and police also wounded three Central Security conscripts and two passers by, Fahd Muhammad Mahmud, a student, and Su'ad 'Ali Ahmad, a farmer. The al-Badari prosecutor's office is investigating the incident. The search operations in the area for the remaining terrorist elements and outlaws are continuing.
