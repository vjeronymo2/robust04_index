941208 FT 08 DEC 94 / International Company News: Reliance merger gains approval Shareholders in Reliance Industries, the Indian petrochemical and textiles company, have approved a plan to merge two group units, Reliance Polypropylene and Reliance Polyethylene, Reuter reports from Bombay. India's financial institutions, which own 16 per cent of Reliance Industries, and Japan's Itochu Corp, a minority shareholder in the other two companies, approved the merger, the company said. Shareholders gave assent on Tuesday.
