BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 19 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- In protest against the political strike announced at the Suhbaatar Square a meeting has been held to support those being on a counter strike at the Freedom Square. The meeting was organized by the "281" Association for Protection of Independence of Mongolia. Speaking at the meeting Z. Dorj, a writer said that the political crisis being deepening in Mongolia might be leading to the edge of a great confrontation and expressed the support by the supporters of the association to the Government of Mongolia and the Prime Minister P. Jasray's activities. Members of the parliament T. Nyamhainyambuu M. Zenee and a writer B. Dogmid in their speeches condemned the actions of the opposition and expressed their full support of the activities of the government as well. Also chief of a department of the General Police Board Garamjil addressing the meeting said that the police are not to serve any of the two opposing political forces announced a hunger strike and can declare with a full responsibility for their words that they would do their best in order to protect Mongolian citizens from any harm, acting within the frameworks of the law.
