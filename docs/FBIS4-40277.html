BFN [By ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Andrey Kirillov] [Text] Moscow June 15 TASS -- Vietnamese Prime Minister Vo Van Kiet's current visit to Moscow is a landmark in the development of Russian-Vietnamese relations, Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev said on Wednesday. Kozyrev met with his Vietnamese counterpart Nguyen Manh Cam who is accompanying Vo Van Kiet on his visit to Russia. Kozyrev stressed that Russia "builds relations with its old friends on a new basis." He noted that Russia and Vietnam have big mutual interests, including in the political sphere. He described relations between the two countries as "very concrete, good, strong and friendly." Nguyen Manh Cam pointed out that Vietnam pays much attention to the strengthening of relations with the countries with which it has traditional ties, primarily Russia. "We value our friendship very much," he said.
