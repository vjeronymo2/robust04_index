921001 FT 01 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Unigroup dives to Pounds 104,000 Pre-tax profits dived to Pounds 104,000 at Unigroup in the year to June 30. The decline from a previous profit of Pounds 1.29m was on sales of Pounds 22.6m (Pounds 23m) and was after increased interest charges of Pounds 615,000 (Pounds 286,000). The profit was also reduced by losses of Pounds 214,000 from discontinued activities. The timber products division suffered from the recession and unfavourable exchange rates. Profits fell to Pounds 450,000 (Pounds 1.35m) on sales of Pounds 10.7m (Pounds 10.3m). Building products' contributed Pounds 42,000 (Pounds 378,000) on sales of Pounds 9.31m (Pounds 8.99m). An extraordinary item of Pounds 898,000 reflected the closure costs of the clothing division.
