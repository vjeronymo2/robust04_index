941026 FT 26 OCT 94 / BBC's auditor will have to compete Coopers & Lybrand, the BBC's auditors for more than 25 years, will have to compete to retain the corporation's business. As part of its process of market testing the BBC is putting out to tender a five-year contract for the auditing of the entire corporation and its commercial subsidiaries. Coopers will be responsible for the 1994-95 audit but then will have to compete with four other firms for the contact for the five years to March 31 in the year 2000.
