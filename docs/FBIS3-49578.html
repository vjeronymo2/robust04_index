Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Bihac, 11 Jan (TANJUG) -- Fierce inter-Muslim clashes resumed today in the Cazin Krajina along the entire demarcation line, local Muslim media reports. According to the same sources, Fikret Abdic's forces have captured the villages of Trzac and Trzacke Rastele and members of the 5th Corps of the Bosnia-Herzegovina army have captured the village of Skokovi and a part of Pecigrad. According to the Western Bosnian News Agency and Radio Velkaton, both based in Velika Kladusa, sporadic clashes are taking place near Cumluk and Todorovo and new movements of troops loyal to Alija Izetbegovic have been registered near Buzim and Podic.
