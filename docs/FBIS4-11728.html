BFN [Text] A Foreign Ministry spokesman has expressed regret over the recent clashes in Afghanistan, particularly in Kabul, which caused loss of life and property to the Muslim people of that country. He said: The Islamic Republic of Iran calls on all the warring sides to end the military clashes as soon as possible and to resolve the disputes through talks in order to prevent further bloodshed. The spokesman added: As ever, the Islamic Republic of Iran is prepared to continue to assist in the process of peace-seeking efforts.
