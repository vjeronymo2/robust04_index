920226 FT 26 FEB 92 / UK Company News: River & Mercantile Smaller Companies River & Mercantile Smaller Companies Trust reported net asset value of 107.7p per share as at January 31, ahead from 94.39p at the same stage of 1990 and from 105.53p at the trust's year end in July. Net revenue for the six months to end-January was sharply lower at Pounds 254,000 (Pounds 759,000). Earnings per share emerged at 1.01p, against 3.04p, but the interim dividend is maintained at 1.25p.
