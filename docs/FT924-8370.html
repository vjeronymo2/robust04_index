921111 FT 11 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Hartlepools Water falls to Pounds 670,000 Pre-tax profits of Hartlepools Water Company slipped by 3 per cent, from Pounds 690,000 to Pounds 670,000, in the six months to end-September. The company said the recession continued to depress metered demand by industrial customers. Turnover rose slightly, from Pounds 2.59m to Pounds 2.61m, and earnings per stock unit increased to 67p (66p). The interim dividend is raised to 22p (21p).
