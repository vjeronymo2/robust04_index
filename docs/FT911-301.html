910513 FT 13 MAY 91 / World News in Brief: Kurds shun western plan Iraqi Kurds have shunned Operation Gallant Provider, a western plan to ferry them down from the mountains on the Turkish border to their homes in the provincial capital of Dahuk, where troops loyal to President Saddam Hussein are deployed, Page 4 Prime minister John Major attended a charity pop concert at Wembley Arena which is aiming to raise Pounds 20m for Kurdish refugees. A worldwide television audience of 50m tuned in to the event.
