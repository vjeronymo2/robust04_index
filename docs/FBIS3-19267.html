Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Carlo Azegio Ciampi, speaking at the Institute of International Affairs, touched upon the topics of the G-7 and the crisis in Europe. He said that Italy is working to ensure that Russia will participate more fully at the next meeting of G-7, which will be held in Naples. The prime minister added that the crisis in Yugoslavia is proof of a lack of a common European defense and security policy. Ciampi also warned that political integration must make progress in order to overcome recent setbacks. He said that without political structures, Europe is putting its future evolution at risk.
