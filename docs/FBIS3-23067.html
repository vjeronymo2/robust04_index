Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Ayman al-Safadi report: "Abu-Nidal Group Urges King Husayn To Give It Opportunity To Clarify Its Position"] [Text] Amman -- Fatah-the Revolutionary Council (Abu-Nidal Organization) yesterday appealed to King Husayn to give it the opportunity to clarify its position on what it termed "allegations and lies" and "campaigns against it" launched since the beginning of the year. In a statement distributed through the "Amal" group the official spokesman for the movement urged "His Majesty to choose the means that enable us to convey our view, its details, and the (case) file to His Majesty in order to acquaint him with the scale of our calamity, or -- alternatively -- this can be done via an Arab state or through an open letter." The movement reiterated its denial of any involvement in the assassination of the Jordanian diplomat, Na'ib al-Ma'ayitah, in Beirut on 29 January. It pointed out that it was surprised that "the al-Ma'ayitah case was thrown at our movement."
