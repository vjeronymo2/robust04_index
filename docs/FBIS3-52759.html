Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Following the opening of Brunei's embassy in Tehran, (Haj Suhini Bin Haj Muhammad Hassan), that country's first charge d'affaires in the Islamic Republic of Iran, yesterday met with the Iranian foreign minister, Mr. Velayati and presented his credentials. In the meeting, the two officials discussed the expansion and the strengthening of the two countries' relations, and the coming visit of the sultan of Brunei, Haj Hassan Bolkiah, to Iran. The Bruneian charge d'affaires considered the Islamic Republic of Iran as a second home, and stressed the willingness of his country's officials in strengthening relations with the Islamic Republic of Iran. Pointing to the results of their previous meetings and discussions of the two countries' officials, and the positive steps which have been taken in line with the two countries' ties, Mr. Velayati considered the Bruneian ruler's coming visit to Iran as a significant step for expansion of relations.
