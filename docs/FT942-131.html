940630 FT 30 JUN 94 / Observer: Snubbed again Fresh evidence of the declining influence of Britain's trade unions: the Transport and General Workers have not been invited to name a representative to the government's consultative panel on badgers and bovine tuberculosis this year. Agriculture minister Gillian Shephard says that only those organisations with a clear and direct concern in the problem need to be represented; she's obviously forgotten that farm workers are members of the TGWU.
