921202 FT 02 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Surge to Pounds 712,000 at Gibbon Lyons Reorganisation, improved trading at the screen ink company, and sale of the loss making Dutch subsidiary enabled Gibbon Lyons to lift its interim pre-tax profit by 47 per cent. The group's principal activities are the manufacture of printing inks and related products. Turnover was little changed at Pounds 11.55m (Pounds 11.45m). However, the pre-tax profit worked through at Pounds 712,000 (Pounds 483,000) after reorganisation costs of Pounds 104,000. Earnings per share improved to 4.4p (3.1p) and the interim dividend is again 2p.
