940726 FT 26 JUL 94 / International Company News: Mitsui expands into Indonesia Mitsui OSK Lines, a leading Japanese shipping company, is to create a subsidiary in Jakarta, Indonesia, to conduct marine transportation business, AP-DJ reports from Tokyo. The new company, PT OSK Lines Indonesia, will be 60 per cent owned by Mitsui OSK, with Mitsui's existing agent Gesuri Lloyd PT owning the remainder.
