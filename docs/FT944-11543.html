941105 FT 05 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Banner Homes declines Increased costs arising from its expansion led Banner Homes, the housebuilder, to turn in lower pre-tax profits for the six months to end-September, down from Pounds 557,000 to Pounds 504,000. Turnover increased from Pounds 3.9m to Pounds 5.2m. The company sold 43 units in the first half against 28 last time. The increased overheads and costs of funding a larger working capital requirement have resulted in higher interest payments of Pounds 438,000 (Pounds 390,000). After a tax charge of Pounds 166,000 (nil) earnings per share were 2.1p (4.1p). There is an interim of 0.7p (nil).
