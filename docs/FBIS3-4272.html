Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, March 28 (XINHUA) -- Russia and Kazakhstan will sign a number of documents including treaties on military and economic cooperation, the ITAR-TASS NEWS AGENCY said. Kazakh Nazarbayev arrived in Moscow on Sunday [27 March] for his first official visit to Russia. Russian President Boris Yeltsin held talks with Nazarbayev today on bilateral and international issues. The two leaders will continue their talks with the participation of their prime ministers Viktor Chernomyrdin and Sergey Tereshchenko, ITAR-TASS NEWS AGENCY reported.
