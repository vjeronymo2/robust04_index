920425 FT 25 APR 92 / Peruvian minister stays on Peruvian economy minister Carlos Bolona, whose resignation earlier this week was seen as a severe blow to President Alberto Fujimori, will now stay on in his post, the president's office said yesterday, Reuter reports from Lima. Two other ministers, foreign minister Augusto Blacker Miller and industry minister Victor Joy Way, who had clashed with Mr Bolona, would resign. Their portfolios will be taken by prime minister Oscar de la Puente and Mr Jorge Camet, a businessman,a spokesman said.
