930601 FT 01 JUN 93 / International Company News: Scottish Inv net assets up 10.8% Net asset value per ordinary stock unit of the Scottish Investment Trust stood at 240.7p at April 30, a 10.8 per cent improvement on the 217.3p reported six months earlier at its October 31 year end. Gross income for the half year amounted to Pounds 12m (Pounds 10.7m) and available revenue was Pounds 5.6m (Pounds 4.64m). Earnings per ordinary unit came out at 2.2p (1.83p) and the interim dividend is being increased to 1.7p (1.6p).
