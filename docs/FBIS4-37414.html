BFN [Text] Khartoum, 17 Jun (SUNA) -- The actual exportation revenues for the fiscal year 1993-94 have reached 431.5 million dollars. The earnings of the exportation contracts have reached 535 million dollars. The balance sheet of the foreign currency for the fiscal year 1994-95, shows that certain commodities have generated profitable revenues. The foreign currency balance sheet also showed that oil seeds earnings have reached 90 million dollars higher than the estimated revenues.
