930604 FT 04 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Pilkington in joint bid for Italian glass maker PILKINGTON, the UK glassmaker, together with Techint Finanziaria, have made a joint bid to acquire Societa Italiana Vetro from the Italian authorities. SIV, a leading flat and safety glass manufacturer, is one of Italy's first state-owned companies to be privatised. In 1992 sales were L670bn (Pounds 298m) and net assets are about L355bn. It is likely to be some time before the outcome of the bidding is known. Acceptance is subject to approval of the Italian government and European competition authorities.
