920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / International Company News: Lockheed moves ahead LOCKHEED, the US defence group, yesterday reported a 10 per cent increase in second-quarter net earnings, from Dollars 70m to Dollars 77m. Mr Daniel Tellep, chairman, attributed the gain mainly to higher profits from programmes in the aeronautical and electronic sectors. The earnings figure worked through at Dollars 1.24 a share, compared with Dollars 1.11 last time, on sales which rose from Dollars 2.43bn to Dollars 2.48bn. For the six months, Lockheed reported net earnings of Dollars 143m, or Dollars 2.30 a share, compared with Dollars 124m, or Dollars 1.96.
