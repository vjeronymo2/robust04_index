Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] Rio de Janeiro -- For the first time in 45 years the War College (ESG) opened its doors to a hero of the Cuban revolution. About 60 professors, and officers and civilians were in the college auditorium to listen to a lecture by Cuban Higher Education Minister Fernando Vecino Alegret, who fought next to Fidel Castro and was vice minister of the Cuban Revolutionary Armed Forces. "Times change," said the former officer, who faced in a natural way the fact that the school was a center for anti-communist preaching during the military regime. ESG Commander Brigadier Sergio Ferolla said: "The doors of the school are open to all without restrictions."
