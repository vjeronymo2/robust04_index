930616 FT 16 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Channel pays first dividend 112 years after the group was founded as the Submarine Continental Railway Company to investigate possibilities of a rail tunnel under the English Channel. The group, as Channel Tunnel Investments, became a virtual shell many years ago before being transformed last August into an industrial concern concentrating on security products. The announcement of a dividend of 1p accompanied results for the 15 month period to March 31. Pre-tax profits amounted to Pounds 307,821, including a final contribution from CTI to August 10 and a maiden contribution from Carflow Products from that date. Turnover totalled Pounds 1.62m. Earnings per share emerged at 3p basic and 2.3p fully diluted.
