920122 FT 22 JAN 92 / UK News (Employment): Apex tries to delay insolvency APEX TRUST, the charity that provides training for ex-offenders, will today apply for an administrative court order in an attempt to avoid insolvency. Coopers & Lybrand Deloitte, Apex's administrator, will ask for a three-week delay to assess and promote the trust's more viable areas. At a board meeting last night members decided against the alternative of immediate liquidation. Mr Bill Mather, Apex chief executive, said: 'I am pleased that we have got time to press our case for government support and to give time for the wealth of goodwill from the private sector to be turned into pledges of financial support.'
