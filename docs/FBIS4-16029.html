BFN [Text] At the State Duma sitting on 13 April, Ivan Rybkin, chairman of the parliament's lower chamber, read his statement regarding the NATO air strikes against Serb positions near the Bosnian town of Gorazde. Rybkin said that the State Duma was not going to remain an indifferent observer. He demanded that NATO should immediately stop its unsanctioned and unilateral use of military force and called upon the parliaments of all countries to actively participate in developing the process of peaceful political settlement of the Yugoslav crisis. The speaker's statement was not enough for the State Duma's deputies and they decided to discuss the issue at the plenary meeting on 15 April. A relevant proposal was submitted by the LDPR [Liberal Democratic Party of Russia], PRES [Party of Russian Unity and Accord], and Agrarian Party Duma groups.
