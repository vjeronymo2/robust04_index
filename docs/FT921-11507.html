920131 FT 31 JAN 92 / International Company News: Haarmann & Reimer buys Hercules unit HAARMANN & Reimer, a fully-owned speciality chemicals subsidiary of Bayer, the German group, has acquired the worldwide fragrance business of Hercules, the Delaware-based chemical and aerospace conglomerate. Terms were not announced, but the business has a turnover of about Dollars 55m.
