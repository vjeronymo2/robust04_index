930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Denmans Electrical Pre-tax profits at USM-quoted Denmans Electrical improved to Pounds 1.08m in the six months to March 31, against Pounds 823,000 at the same stage of 1992. Turnover amounted to Pounds 19.8m (Pounds 18.9m). Earnings per share improved to 16.53p (12.73p) and the interim dividend goes up to 1.9p (1.8p).
