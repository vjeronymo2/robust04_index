BFN [Text] Beijing, June 21 (XINHUA) -- Tian Jiyun, vice-chairman of China's National People's Congress (NPC) Standing Committee, met here today with a delegation from the Turkish Grand National Assembly headed by Vefa Tanir, its deputy president [name and title as received]. Tian hoped that the two countries would increase high-level exchanges between the parliaments and governments, as an effort to enhance understanding and promote friendly cooperative relations and two-way trade. Tanir said that the two countries shared the objectives of opposing expansionism, developing the domestic economy and safeguarding world peace. The Turkish delegation arrived here yesterday at the invitation of the Education, Science, Culture and Public Health Committee of the NPC.
