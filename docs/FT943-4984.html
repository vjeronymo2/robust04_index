940906 FT 06 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Plasmec recovers to Pounds 352,000 Pre-tax profits at Plasmec, the electronic and electrical equipment manufacturer, made a sharp recovery for the six months to June 30 to Pounds 352,000 against a loss of Pounds 237,000. Turnover was up from Pounds 5.96m to Pounds 7.23m, boosting operating profits to Pounds 642,000. The USM-quoted company doubled the interim dividend to 1.5p. Earnings per share were 4.8p (3.6p losses).
