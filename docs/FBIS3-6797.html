Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] It has been learned on 21 March that North Korea recently sent a letter to the U.S. Government through channels in New York warning that if the United States does not withdraw its demands for additional International Atomic Energy Agency inspections and the exchange of North and South Korean special envoys as the precondition of the third round of the North Korea- U.S. talks, North Korea could stop operating various surveillance equipment installed in Yongbyon's nuclear facilities.
