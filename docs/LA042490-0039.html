April 24, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Landmark Bancorp: The La Habra-based parent company of Landmark Bank posted first-quarter earnings of $468,000, up 8% from $434,000 for the corresponding period in 1989. Revenue increased 16% to $4.9 million from $4.2 million. Total assets rose 14% to $176.6 million from $154.9 million.
