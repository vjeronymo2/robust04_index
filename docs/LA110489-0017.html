November 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

THESE GIANTS FAIL TO DO WHAT THOSE GIANTS DID

Too bad "our" Giants couldn't do what "their" Giants did ("Yomiuri Giants Win the Japan Series," Oct. 30). And they did it the impossible way: They lost three in a row, then won four in a row.

If our Giants feel bad, they should phone the Kintetsu Buffaloes and hear their story. I'll bet it's a classic, surely a dynasty in the rough.

DUKE RUSSELL, Hollywood
