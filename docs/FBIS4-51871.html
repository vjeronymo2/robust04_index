BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 1 (KCNA) -- The great leader President Kim Il-song received messages of greetings from foreign party leaders on the occasion of his birthday. The messages came from James Stewart, general secretary, and Eoin O'Murku, international secretary, of the National Executive Committee of the Communist Party of Ireland, Roberto Gabriele, general secretary of the Italian Movement for Peace and Socialism, Juan Ramos, general secretary of the Communist Party of the Peoples of Spain, Michael Hicks, general secretary of the Britannic Communist Party, Rolf Hagel, chairman of the Workers' Party- Communists of Sweden, Ludo Martens, chairman of the Central Committee of the Belgian Labour Party, and Dilip Barua, general secretary of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Bangladesh (Marxist-Leninist). President Kim Il-song also received a message of greetings from the Central Committee of the Norwegian Communist Party on the same occasion. The messages extended warmest congratulations to President Kim Il-song and wished him good health and a long life. They expressed firm solidarity for the Korean people's struggle to defend the sovereignty and peace of their country and achieve national reunification in a peaceful way.
