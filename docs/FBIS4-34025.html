BFN [Report by Orestes Eujelles at Cuban Workers Federation Headquarters from the "Exclusive" program] [Text] The National Federation of French State Workers, made up of civilian employees of the French Armed Forces, will issue a medal of solidarity with Cuba. The medal will carry the image of Commander Ernesto Che Guevara and bear his historical phrase: Until victory always! (Henri Ferry), secretary general of that French organization, made the announcement during a meeting in the capital with high-ranking leaders of the Revolutionary Armed Forces [FAR] civilian workers labor union today. (Ferry) emphasized that the National Federation of French State Workers will develop a strong movement of material and political support to counter the U.S. blockade against Cuba. He added: We will support the Cuban people until they fully achieve their prosperity. Jose Antonio Roque, secretary general of the FAR Civilian Workers' National Union, told the visitors about the organization's priority tasks, including preparations for their sixth congress scheduled for June 1995.
