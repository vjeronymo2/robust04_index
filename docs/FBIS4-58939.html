BFN [Excerpts] Al-Qalyubiyah detectives yesterday seized a weapons factory in Kafr Shukr in a surprise raid on the Shubra al-Khaymah and Kafr Shukr areas. The raid also resulted in the arrest of six dangerous drug traffickers and 25 people suspected of involvement in criminal cases. [passage omitted] Large forces from the Central Security Agency, led by Deputy Director of al-Qalyubiyah Security Major General Sabri Shindi, Detective Department Director Brigadier General Taha al-Zahid, and Detective Chief Colonel Muhammad Rahim stormed the weapons factory in a basement of a house in Marakz Kafr Shukr owned by a dangerous person with a police record. Some 15 weapons, five live ammunition magazines, and equipment for manufacturing various types of weapons for sale to criminals and drug traffickers in the area were seized. [passage omitted]
