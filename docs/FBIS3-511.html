Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by L. Elias] [Text] Mmabatho March 9 SAPA -- Clouds of teargas filled the air and gunfire could be heard in Mafikeng, adjacent to Bophuthatswana capital Mmabatho, on Wednesday [9 March] afternoon. Streets near the station erupted as Bophuthatswana police in Hippo armoured vehicles started firing teargas and live rounds. Reports that one pedestrian had been shot in a leg were being investigated.
