BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Schegoleva] [Text] Sochi April 8 TASS -- President Dzhokhar Dudayev of the rebel Russian Republic of Chechnya said on Friday he will not reject a Moscow offer to find a political solution to the crisis in relation between Russia and Chechnya which had proclaimed sovereignty. "We have never turned down meetings with the Russian leadership, political solutions", he told reporters while visiting a fair in Sochi on the Black Sea. He added that he had arrived to Sochi because of purely economic interests and stressed that his republic is and will be buying grain and foodstuffs from southern Russian regions. Dudayev said it is necessary to resume joint use of the oil pipeline from Chechnya and restore the normal work of railways.
