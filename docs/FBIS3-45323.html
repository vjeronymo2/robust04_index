Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] Wang Ke, commander of the Shenyang Military Region; and Li Xinliang, political commissar of the Shenyang Military Region, who came to our province to inspect the army units and the militia work, went to the provincial military district a few days ago to meet with the commanders and fighters of the organs under the provincial military district. After hearing the work reports made by Zhou Zaikang, commander of the provincial military district, and Shi Zhaonian, political commissar of the provincial military district, Commander Wang Ke and Political Commissar Li Xinliang gave important instructions to the party organizations at various levels in the provincial military district in regard to how to implement the theory on building socialism with Chinese characteristics and Comrade Deng Xiaoping's ideology governing the army building in the new situation, to comprehensively strengthen the building of militia and reserve duty forces, and to enhance the border defense work. Simultaneously, they fully affirmed and praised the provincial military district's achievements in mobilizing the militia and reserve duty forces to help the local governments fulfill the extremely difficult, dangerous, and heavy tasks, and positively engage themselves in economic construction. Commander Wang Ke and Political Commissar Li Xinliang also urged commanders and soldiers of the provincial military district and all militiamen across the province to ceaselessly keep forging ahead in the course of reform and to make new contributions to realizing the target of building a developed, border, near-the-sea province, and building the national defense reserve forces.
