BFN [Text] Yerevan, April 11 (SNARK) -- Today Vazgen Sargisyan, the Armenian State Minister on Defense, speaking in the parliament session-hall called two deputies Ashot Bleyan and David Vardanyan traitors. These deputies come out against forced ways of drafting reservists for three-month assembly. Mr. Sargisyan said after the session's end "these traitors must be first to be drafted", while some deputies demanded to stop arbitrariness and violence of rights in the course of organizing military assemblies. Speaking at the parliament session, Mr.Sargisyan said that all who deviate from the draft will be strictly punished. He said "We arrest and will arrest them either in the streets or in the markets or in the parliament".
