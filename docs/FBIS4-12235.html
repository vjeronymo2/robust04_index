BFN [Text] This evening, in his office, Prime Minister Mohamed Karim Lambrani received Dr. 'Abd-al-Rahman Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz al-Shaykh, the Saudi minister of agriculture and water resources, in the presence of Finance Minister Mohamed Sagou, Abdelaziz Meziane Belfkih, minister of agriculture and agrarian reform, and Mohamed Seqat, governor of the Bank of Morocco. The discussion centered on the special relations existing between the two sister countries that are based on the close fraternal ties between the two monarchs, His Majesty King Fahd Bin-'Abd-al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, custodian of the two holy mosques, and His Majesty King Hassan II. The prime minister and the Saudi minister of agriculture and water resources also reviewed bilateral cooperation in the field of agriculture and ways to bolster it, particularly in the projects linked to the national irrigation program in Morocco. The prime minister also highlighted the importance the Kingdom of Morocco attached to the agricultural sector, which is one of the priorities in the country's development policy in view of its vital role in the preservation of the country's economic and social balance.
