941228 FT 28 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Midlands Elect Midlands Electricity has acquired a 15 per cent stake in Teesside Power Holdings, the company through which Enron Europe holds its 50 per cent stake in Teesside Power, for an undisclosed amount. Central Power, Midlands' wholly-owned subsidiary which has bought the stake from Enron, previously had a 19.2 per cent interest in Teesside Power, which has now been, in effect, increased to 26.7 per cent.
