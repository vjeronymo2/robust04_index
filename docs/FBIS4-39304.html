BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Nikita Yermakov] [Text] Paris, 4 Jun -- An agreement on deferring seven billion dollars of Russia's foreign debt repayments this year was reached here today with representatives of the Paris Club which unites 19 creditor states. Russia's total foreign debt, inherited from the former Soviet Union, is approximately 80 billion dollars. Forty-five billion of this is owed to states belonging to the Paris Club.
