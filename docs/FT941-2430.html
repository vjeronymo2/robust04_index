940321 FT 21 MAR 94 / Observer: Socks pulled up Ever since the Los Angeles Times flagellated itself for publishing a 'staged' picture of a weary firefighter cooling himself by a swimming pool, the earnest US press has struggled to assert its photographic correctness. Hence, an agency picture of Socks, the US presidential cat, commanding the podium in the White House briefing room has had to be accompanied by a note to editors explaining that the person taking Socks for a walk 'was unprompted to place cat on podium'. Don't tell me it was Socks' idea.
