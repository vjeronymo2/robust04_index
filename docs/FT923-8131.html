920813 FT 13 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Moorfield Estates deficit deepens Moorfield Estates, which recently underwent a restructure via the appointment of a new board, an acquisition and the disposal of certain group assets, returned a loss of Pounds 774,000 pre-tax for the half year ended April 30. The deficit compares with a restated loss of Pounds 578,000 for the opening half last time. Losses per share worked through at 9.01p (6.7p).
