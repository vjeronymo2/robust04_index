July 31, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

NOVACEK WINS HILVERSUM TOURNEY

Karel Novacek of Czechoslovakia defeated Emilio Sanchez of Spain 6-2, 6-4 today to capture the Hilversum tennis tournament.

The eighth-seeded Novacek needed one hour, 10 minutes to oust Sanchez, who was seeded second, in a match that had been scheduled for Sunday but was postponed because of persistent rain at the Melkhuisje tennis complex.

Novacek, ranked 88th on the world computer listing, collected $14,000 for the victory.
