Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 1 -- One hundred Vietnamese workers left here yesterday for Libya under a construction contract signed recently between Vinaconex, a Vietnamese overseas construction company, and South Korea's Tong A Consortium. They are the first batch of some 2,000 workers and overseers to be sent to work in a man-dug river project in Libya in 1994. In 1993, 1,500 Vietnamese workers were sent to Libya under contracts signed between Vinaconex and Tong A Consortium.
