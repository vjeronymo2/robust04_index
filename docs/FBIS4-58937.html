BFN [Text] The Muslim Brotherhood has appealed to the parties to the conflict in Yemen to safeguard the public interest and settle their differences within the framework of a constructive dialogue based on God's shari'ah and to place Yemeni unity above any other consideration. In a statement released by the Muslim Brotherhood, it called on the Arab and Muslim rulers and all Arab and Muslim organizations to play the required role to close the Yemeni ranks and stop the bloodshed. The Muslim Brotherhood expressed its worry about the deterioration of the situation in Yemen and the threat to Yemeni unity. It noted that the fraternal Yemeni people's property and resources are threatened. It stressed the need to pool efforts and resources to heal the rift in Yemen and bring the parties together in the interest of that country and its unity and its people's security, freedom, lives, and generation's resources.
