BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 29 (XINHUA) -- Visiting Chinese Premier Li Peng met with chairman Natsagiyn Bagabandi of Mongolia's Great Hural (parliament) here today. Li conveyed Chinese parliament leader Qiao Shi's wish to strengthen cooperation between China's National People Congress (NPC) and the Mongolian Great Hural. Bagabandi said Li's historic visit has turned a new page in Mongolia-China relations and is of great significance to pushing bilateral ties into a new era. The Mongolian parliament approves the two governments' policies of developing bilateral friendly cooperation, he said. He spoke highly of the signing of the treaty on Mongolia-China friendly relations of cooperation, which is scheduled for this afternoon, calling it a good legal basis for further expansion of bilateral ties. The exchanges between the two parliaments, which have been fully normalized, are very important to the development of relations between the two countries, he said. A delegation of the Mongolian Great Hural will pay an official visit to China in coming August in the hope to learn about China's reform and opening as well as the work of the NPC. Li Peng voiced satisfaction with the "fruitful results" of his visit to Mongolia, saying the signing of the new treaty will turn a new page for, and promote the advancement of, bilateral ties. "The Chinese Government will continue working towards that goal," he said. Also, Li said the parliaments of the two countries shoulder an important responsibility for furthering their friendship and expanding their cooperation.
