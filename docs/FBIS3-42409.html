Language: Russian Article Type:CSO [Unattributed article, under the rubric: "Engineer Munitions and Equipment Systems": "PZM-2"] [Text] The PZM-2 Regimental Earth-Mover is designed for engineer preparation of terrain. It consists of a wheeled prime mover, a bucket-less earthmoving tool and a winch.
