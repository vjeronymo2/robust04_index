930721 FT 21 JUL 93 / UK Company News: BZW Securities auctions shares BZW Securities is auctioning some 1.7m Johnson Matthey shares which it agreed to buy as a result of its cash offer for the precious metals group's enhanced scrip dividend scheme. The shares are being sold through a strike price auction, which will be open to member firms of the London Stock Exchange and to institu-tional clients of BZW Securities. Bids must be received by 5.15pm today.
