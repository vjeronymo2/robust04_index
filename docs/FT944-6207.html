941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Budget 94 (Government Statements): Fund to benefit sport and the arts Sport and the arts will continue to benefit from the funds provided by the Foundation for Sport and the Arts, which in the last year received around Pounds 68m to give to sports and arts groups. In his Budget Statement, the chancellor confirmed that the 2.5 per cent reduction in Pool Betting Duty would be continued for a further five years, provided the football pools companies continue their level of contributions. Pool Betting Duty is at 37.5 per cent, following an initial 2.5 per cent reduction in the 1990 Finance Act and a further 2.5 per cent reduction in the 1991 Finance Act.
