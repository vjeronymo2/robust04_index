940609 FT 09 JUN 94 / People: Bodies politic The Accounting Standards Board yesterday announced the names of three new members and said it would be adding an extra person to bring the total up to ten. Huw Jones, a director of Prudential Portfolio Managers, Ken Wild, a partner with accountants Touche Ross, and Geoffrey Whittington, professor of accounting at Cambridge University, will join the board from August. In addition, the board is seeking an additional member from a multinational company, in a move that will tip the balance more towards preparers of accounts.
