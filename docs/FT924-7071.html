921118 FT 18 NOV 92 / UK Company News: European Motor The expanded European Motor group returned to profits and dividend for the half year to September 30. On sales ahead from Pounds 3.7m to Pounds 50.3m, the group made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 1.52m, compared with losses of Pounds 735,000. Earnings per share were 3.5p (losses 18.3p) and dividend payments are resumed with an interim distribution of 1.5p.
