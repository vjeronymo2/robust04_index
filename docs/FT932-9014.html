930515 FT 15 MAY 93 / Finance and the Family: Schroders share service - At a Glance Schroders has launched a dealing service for those who want to buy and sell shares in its split fund, and might not have their own stockbroker. Dealing charges are 1 per cent on both purchases and sales. There is a daily dealing facility for those who invest the minimum of Pounds 1,000. The split fund was launched in March and has income, zero and capital shares.
