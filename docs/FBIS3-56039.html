Language: Russian Article Type:BFN ["Own Information", ITAR-TASS report under the "Yesterday" rubric: "President Personally Upheld Journalists' Interests"] [Text] Russian Federation President Boris Yeltsin has given orders that the State Kremlin Palace be made available for the traditional journalists' ball 15 January, at which the annual awards ceremony of the Union of Journalists of Russia will take place. As has already been reported, the previous day the journalists were denied the opportunity to hold the ball in this building as it it an "official facility." It took a personal intervention by the head of state for the journalists' request to be granted.
