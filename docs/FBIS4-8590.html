BFN [Text] According to the BALKAN NEWS AGENCY the Bulgarian Socialist Party [BSP] could demand the resignation of the cabinet if it allows the United Nations to use a corridor through Bulgarian territory. During a break in the National Assembly session today, the BSP parliamentary group discussed this issue but did not reach a specific decision. There were equal numbers of votes for and against. The parliamentary faction of the Union of Democratic Forces is postponing its decision on the same matter, because it is not clear whether the United Nations really requested a military corridor through Bulgarian territory.
