COOKING BY THE BOOK

"I BRING THE COOKBOOK page to life by re-creating the look and taste of recipes as their authors intended," says professional chef Ruth Hedges. Hedges, well aware of the great number of us who read cookbooks like novels -- for pure pleasure, with absolutely no intention of actually preparing a recipe -- has created Cookbook Fantasies.

"This service is unique, because unlike when dealing with caterers, you can control the entire menu," Hedges says. "You get to serve a fantasy meal to friends without any effort." So the next time you're planning a party -- whether an intimate gathering or a large affair -- -- why not plan on pasta by Wolfgang Puck, or blackened snapper by Paul Prudhomme, and a Martha Stewart tart for dessert.
