920129 FT 29 JAN 92 / World Stock Markets: South Africa JOHANNESBURG drifted in search of fresh direction. The all-gold index shed 15 to 1,258: Vaal Reefs declined R6 to R218 and Kloof dropped 50 cents to R32.75 in the absence of any stimulus. The industrial index eased 2 to 4,426, while the all-share index fell 3 to 3,620.
