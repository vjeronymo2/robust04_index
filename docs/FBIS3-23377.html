Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Asyut, 21 Mar (MENA) -- In the security campaign that has been going on since morning in Abu Tij, the security forces have arrested six terrorists wanted in connection with many terrorist cases and attacks against the police. The six terrorists hail from the village of al-Balaydi in the Abu Tij district of Asyut Governorate. They are 'Ali Jabir Ahmad Salim, Salim Jabir Ahmad Salim, 'Abd-al-'Aziz Mahmud 'Abd-al-Hafiz, Haridi Siddiq Ahmad Muhammad Haridi, Sa'id Siddiq Ahmad Muhammad Haridi, and Ahmad Siddiq Ahmad Muhammad Haridi.
