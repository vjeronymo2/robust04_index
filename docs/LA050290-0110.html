May 2, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

MEDI-CAN'T: An estimated 400,000 Orange County residents cannot afford medical insurance (A1), although half of them are employed, says the United Way. . . . The situation is so serious it will be included in a PBS documentary that will follow the case of an uninsured Laguna Beach real estate agent who delayed costly breast exams and now suffers from widespread cancer. Barbie Ludovise and Lanie Jones
