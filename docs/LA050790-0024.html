May 7, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV

Partying With Arsenio, Peeples: Arsenio Hall will produce a new show to follow his hit television talk show.

The nightly half-hour variety program, called "The Party Machine With Nia Peeples," is set to debut next January. Peeples, an actress and singer, appeared on the TV series "Fame" and has recently released the album "Nothin' but Trouble." "The Party Machine" will showcase live musical performances, comedians, music videos and in-studio dancing. The program will be syndicated by Paramount. BETH KLEID
