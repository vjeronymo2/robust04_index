BFN [Excerpt] Damascus--Arab League Secretary General Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid has praised President Hafiz al-Asad's patriotic and pan-Arab stands toward Arab causes, his defense of Arab legitimate rights, and his adherence to the just and comprehensive peace march. In a statement to SANA upon his departure to Cairo today, accompanied by the Arab League delegation, he said: I have had the honor to meet with President Hafiz al-Asad during my visit to Damascus to attend the 26th round of the Joint Arab Action Higher Coordination Committee. I briefed the president on the positive results of the committee's meetings in terms of strengthening cooperation and joint Arab action. 'Abd-al-Majid said: It was a good opportunity to hear the president's directives and opinions on the various Arab issues, particularly the Yemeni situation. The meeting was also a good opportunity to hear the president's views in this regard and to express my appreciation for the president's intensive efforts and endeavors in defending legitimate Arab rights and adhering to the just and comprehensive peace march in accordance with international legitimacy, the UN Security Council resolutions, and the land-for- peace principle. Alluding to the results of the Arab League's Higher Coordination Committee meetings over which he presided, 'Abd-al-Majid said: They were fruitful. Recommendations and resolutions were reached connected with all issues on the agenda. The results were positive and effective in terms of strengthening the march of joint Arab action in all domains at the present stage. [passage omitted].
