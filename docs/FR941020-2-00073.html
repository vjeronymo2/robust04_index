Proposal: Report on Occupancy for Public Housing. Office: Public and Indian Housing. Description of the Need for the Information and Its Proposed Use: The information collected will be used to measure and evaluate the utilization of Public and Indian Housing units by low-income families as well as assure that all persons have an equal opportunity to participate in and receive the benefits of the housing assistance offered. Occupancy and tenant characteristic information is required for monitoring and compliance activities. Form Number: HUD&hyph;51234. Respondents: State or Local Governments and non-profit institutions.
