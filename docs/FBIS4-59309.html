BFN [Excerpt] Riyadh, 11 May -- His Excellency Shaykh 'Abdullah bin Husayn al-Ahmar, speaker of the Yemeni parliament, and His Excellency 'Abd al-Karim al-Iryani, Yemeni minister of planning and development, and the accompanying delegation left Riyadh this evening [11 May] following a visit to the kingdom during which they delivered a message to King Fahd bin 'Abd al-'Aziz Al Sa'ud, custodian of the two holy mosques, from His Excellency Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council in the Republic of Yemen.
