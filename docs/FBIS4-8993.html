BFN [Text] Belgrade, April 19 (TANJUG) -- Yugoslavia (Serbia/Montenegro) and Ukraine have established diplomatic relations, which has been made official by the foreign ministries exchanging notes, the Yugoslav ministry said on Tuesday [19 April]. In line with this, Yugoslavia has already opened its embassy in Kiev. The establishment of diplomatic ties continues long years of all-round cooperation and friendship between the two nations and affirms their commitment to deepening and consolidation friendly relations and extensive cooperation, the ministry said.
