BFN [Text] A senior Japanese Foreign Ministry official disclosed on 28 May that the DPRK [Democratic People's Republic of Korea] plans to carry out missile test launches over the Sea of Japan in a few days. This information is the result of the analysis of data provided by the United States. He said, "Preparations for the experiment are under way. We have concluded that it will be carried out in the immediate future." The Japanese Government was able to confirm the test launches of the DPRK's "Nodong-1" ballistic missile in May 1993. It believes that the missile to be tested this time will be slightly bigger and that it will have a greater range.
