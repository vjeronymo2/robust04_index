BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] On 11 June a water leak was discovered at the Chernobyl nuclear power station in Ukraine. The water was leaking from the holding reservoir of the power generating unit No. 2. The reservoir is used to store cassettes with worked-out fuel. A shift leader at the Chernobyl nuclear power station, Aleksandr Yelchishchev, said the leak had not led to changes in background radiation levels in the nearby premises. Let me remind you that in accordance with the existing schedule, power generating unit No. 2 is currently undergoing decommissioning.
