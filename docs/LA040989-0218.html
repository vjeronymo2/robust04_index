April 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

SUMMER VACATIONS;

A GUIDE TO OUR GRAND NATIONAL PARKS, FORESTS

Since Yellowstone National Park opened in 1872, the national park system has grown to a collection of scenic and historic areas that includes about 50 national parks and 156 national forests.

The parks and forests peppered across the nation make excellent vacation destinations.

Many of the parks have accommodations as varied as the parks themselves, from modest campsites to handsome hotels and lodges. Contact the individual parks for more information on peak times to visit, the need for reservations, and costs.
