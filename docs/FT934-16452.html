931006 FT 06 OCT 93 / UK Company News: A Beckman reduced to Pounds 790,000 Higher interest charges left A Beckman, the textile and property group, with a drop in pre-tax profits from Pounds 903,000 to Pounds 790,000 for the year to June 30. Turnover advanced to Pounds 14.4m (Pounds 12.3m). Net interest payable rose to Pounds 516,000 (Pounds 429,000). The proposed final dividend is cut to 2.38p (3.15p) making 3.58p (4.78p) for the year. Earnings per share were 4.7p (5.2p).
