BFN [TA SR report: "Slovak National Bank Renewed Refinancing"] [Text] In the 22d week, after a period of approximately three months, the Slovak National Bank [NBS] again started to refinance commercial banks by means of auction credits. Even earlier, the NBS renewed the rediscount of exchange bills for agriculture. According to information from the NBS, the above measures can be regarded as an indication of good marcoeconomic results, such as, for example, the favorable development of the inflation rate, which reached only three percent in the first four months of this year.
