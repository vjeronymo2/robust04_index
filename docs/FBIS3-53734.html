Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At 1145 today, President Hafiz al-Asad received Lebanese President Ilyas al-Hirawi, Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri, Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri, Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz, and Chief Lebanese Negotiator Suhayl Shammas. Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam, Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar', and Nasir Qaddur, the minister of state for foreign affairs, also attended the meeting.
