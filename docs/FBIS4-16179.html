BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow April 13 TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin returned to Moscow on Wednesday after his official visit to Spain. Russian President Boris Yeltsin said the major result of his three-day visit to Spain is its "fruitfulness." Yeltsin said the visit and the agreements signed, including the treaty of friendship and cooperation, have laid "the foundation for vigorous bilateral cooperation" between the two countries.
