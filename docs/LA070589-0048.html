July 5, 1989, Wednesday, Valley Edition

CSUN SIGNS SOUTHERN SECTION 800 CHAMPION

Erick McBride of Palmdale High has signed a letter of intent to attend Cal State Northridge on a partial track scholarship.

McBride, the Southern Section 4-A Division champion in the 800 meters, chose CSUN over Cal State Bakersfield and Azusa Pacific.

McBride, who will enroll at CSUN as a business major, has a personal best of 1 minute, 51.52 seconds in the 800.
