Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Mirza Sadikovic report from the 5th Corps of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army] [Excerpt] The unsuccessful offensive by the members of the so-called [Velika] Kladusa autonomy and the Chetniks from the SAO [Serbian autonomous region of] Krajina on the free territory of Bihac, that is, parts of the Cazin commune, has continued today. Mindlessly attacking the area around Buzim, the separatists have suffered a fresh defeat. In a strong counterattack, members of the 5th Corps of the Army of Bosnia-Herzegovina captured two companies of separatists, about 200 of them, together with a large amount of equipment, ammunition, and weapons. Not only have they suffered a fresh defeat in this region, but Fikret [Abdic]'s separatists have simply being routed in the region of (?Trzac). A significant amount of ammunition and equipment was captured in the fighting there. They could not do anything else but resort to artillery. Thus, the areas of Pecigrad, Skokovi, and Vitkovac are coming under constant fire, where further material damage has been caused to residential property. Some civilians have also been wounded. The reports from this region on the consolidation of the situation are encouraging. The population are slowly retuning to their homes. [passage omitted]
