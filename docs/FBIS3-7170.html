Language: Cambodian Article Type:BFN [Message from His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk Varaman dated 23 March -- read by announcer] [Text] I am honored to inform all of my beloved compatriots of the following: 1. In order to revive an excellent tradition of Sangkum Reas Niyum [Sihanouk's former ruling party] of the 1950's and 1960's, I will receive once a week the representatives of my respected and beloved little people who wish to communicate to me any serious problems or difficulties they experience in their livelihood. 2. On 12 April 1994, from 0900 to 1200, at the Phoucheakni Pavilion in the royal palace I will be happy to welcome the representatives of my respected and beloved little people who wish to relate to me five of their most serious problems. 3. I also understand and know that all my respected and beloved little people are experiencing not only five problems in their lives. We, however, are forced to screen and select only five of these problems to facilitate their solution. This screening and selection shall be undertaken by the samdech and excellencies the private advisers of the king of Cambodia. In order to make the screening and selection of these five most serious problems submitted by the representatives of the respected and beloved little people for the royal audience on 12 April, these private advisers of the king will meet soon at the Damnak Chan Pavilion in the royal palace. All my respected and beloved little people who experience problems and difficulties are invited to send their representatives to meet with the private advisers of the king in order to submit their complaints in time for screening and selection. Please accept the most profound affection and greetings as well as the five Buddhist blessings from me and my wife. [Signed] Norodom Sihanouk
