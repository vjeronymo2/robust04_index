Language: Slovene Article Type:BFN [Text] The Slovene Government has today done everything with a view to settling relations between our state and the neighboring Croatia. Prime Minister Janez Drnovsek has assembled all ministers in order to coordinate views on some issues regarding relations between the neighbors -- ranging from the Krsko nuclear power plant to the joint border. The general secretary of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs announced a high level meeting between the two countries. At that meeting, the (?prime ministers) will coordinate mutual relations. In this connection, let us add that shortly before 1530 the gradual connection of the nuclear power plant to the surroundings has begun.
