Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] President Geydar Aliyev received Mr. Russell (Cheetham), director of the World Bank's Europe and Asia Department, who is on an official visit to Azerbaijan. The guest will hold talks with the republic's leaders and representatives of several organizations during his visit. The objective of his visit is to familiarize himself with the present situation in Azerbaijan and discuss the World Bank's draft proposals on aid to the Republic. President Aliyev warmly greeted his guest and informed him that he welcomes his visit. Aliyev stressed that he highly appreciates the World Bank's effort to cooperate with Azerbaijan and the steps it has taken in that direction. Mr. Russell (Cheetham) said that the World Bank plans to invest in many economic areas in Azerbaijan. President Aliyev informed the guest that the problems he mentioned are vitally important. He asserted: Of course, Azerbaijan also has other problems. Under the existing conditions, we greatly need the World Bank's aid. The World Bank may rest assured that capital investments in Azerbaijan will be [word indistinct] for the future. Mr. Russell (Cheetham) recalled that the World Bank has established relations with the former USSR republics during the past two or three years and stressed that it can also share its experience with Azerbaijan.
