BFN [Article signed BN/AZ: "A Hundred Swiss Observers in South Africa -- Good Offices Mission for the First Multiracial Elections"] [Excerpts] Bern, 22 Apr (ATS) -- The dispatch of 100 observers to monitor the election process in South Africa is part of Switzerland's good offices policy. This will be the 27th mission of this kind since 1989, involving the largest number of participants and being the most expensive of all. [passage omitted] With 100 observers, the Swiss team is the biggest national contingent within the UN observation mission in South Africa. The cost of the operation is estimated at 1.5 million Swiss francs.
