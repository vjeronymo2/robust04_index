920915 FT 15 SEP 92 / World Commodities Prices: Tea TEA Landed good general demand, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. New seasons Assams sold readily although plain descriptions again showed an easier tendency. Bright liquoring East Africans remained fully firm while mediums came to an active market at irregularly dearer rates. Plainer teas met limited enquiry and were easier. Offshore, fair demand with little change in price levels. The highest price realised this week was 156p for a Rwanda pf 1. Quotations :quality 160p, good medium 132p, medium 122p and low medium 78p.
