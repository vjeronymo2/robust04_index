BFN [Text] Tehran, April 19, IRNA -- A bomb planted in a car parked under an overpass at Tehran's Sa'di Avenue went off around noon today, the law enforcement forces told IRNA. The explosion damaged a number of other cars parked near the scene of the incident and smashed a large number of window panes of nearby shops and homes. Eyewitnesses said that a number of bypassers, including two women holding their children in their arms, were injured who were rushed to a Tehran hospital. So far no group has assumed responsibility for the explosion.
