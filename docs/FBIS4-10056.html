BFN [From the "24 Hours" newscast] [Text] President Carlos Salinas de Gortari has announced a reduction of bean and corn prices as of 1 May. Salinas headed the national meeting of Rural Community Supply Councils. In the Lopez Mateos Room at Los Pinos presidential residence, the president said bean prices will be reduced 15 percent and corn prices will be reduced from 80 to 65 centavos per kilogram at rural stores, a move that will benefit over 20 million Mexicans. The president thus addressed almost 300 community supply representatives. [Begin Salinas recording] The implementation of Program of Direct Support to the Countryside means that the government's subsidy will go directly to the peasants, without the intervention of middlemen. [end recording] For the first time 2 million peasants will receive direct support for their land parcels. The president listened to the problems outlined by three Community Supply Council representatives. He responded to each of their requests, assuring them that kernel corn will not be replaced and that flour will only be complementary. He promised that the corn supply will be sufficient and of good quality. The president presented 10 registered deeds for civilian partnerships.
