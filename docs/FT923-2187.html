920918 FT 18 SEP 92 / People: Departures Sir Jeremy Morse has announced that he will retire as chairman of LLOYDS BANK on February 5 next year. ***** John Beedle has retired from CARLTON COMMUNICATIONS. ***** Norman Corlett has retired as chairman of the ISLE OF MAN STEAM PACKET COMPANY Ltd. ***** Florian Walewski has resigned from TRANSPORT DEVELOPMENT GROUP. ***** Keith Simpson, chairman of the Leeds Business Venture, is retiring as assistant general manager, retail banking, of YORKSHIRE BANK after 44 years. ***** Daniel Laughlan has retired from HALIFAX BUILDING SOCIETY.
