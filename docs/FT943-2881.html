940916 FT 16 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Comp Fin Solutions Computerised Financial Solutions, the computer software company which joined the USM in February, reported pre-tax losses of Pounds 36,000 for the half year to June 30, against profits of Pounds 77,000. Turnover was Pounds 1.4m (Pounds 1.64m). Losses per share were 1.4p (earnings 1.5p).
