940805 FT 05 AUG 94 / International Company News: Hong Leong in casino deal Units of the Hong Leong group, the Malaysian conglomerate, plan to take a 60 per cent share in a Dollars 250m casino and entertainment project in the Philippines. Guoman Holdings, of Hong Leong's property division, is to take a 40 per cent share in the project. A unit of Guoco, a Hong Kong-listed company which controls most of Hong Leong's operations outside Malaysia, will hold 20 per cent. Hong Leong, which has 10 companies listed in Kuala Lumpur, is controlled by Mr Quek Leng Chan, a Malaysian Chinese entrepreneur.
