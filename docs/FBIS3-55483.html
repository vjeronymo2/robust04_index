Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Groznyy, 8 Jan -- An aide to Chechnya's president yesterday told an IBERIA correspondent in a telephone conversation that Zviad Gamsakhurdia's wife, Manana Archvadze appealed to the Chechen government to act as a mediator with the Georgian government in clarifying the matters connected with the death of Georgia's former president. Dzhokhar Dudayev's aide also said that Chechnya's president had agreed that independent experts are participating in the clarification of the above-mentioned matter which Zviad Gamsakhurdia's wife, Manana Archvadze, assesses negatively. It was noted during the conversation that the Chechen government had consented to Manana Archvadze's wish that the former president be buried in Chechnya. The representatives of the Georgian procurator's office are currently visiting Chechnya where they met Dudayev in connection with the above-mentioned matter.
