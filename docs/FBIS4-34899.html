BFN [Report by Azucena Lievano from the "24 Hours Newcast"; passages within quotation marks are recorded] [Text] Minutes ago, here at the Narino Palace, President-elect Ernesto Samper Pizano officially announced the first five ministers of his cabinet. [Samper] "Justice Minister Dr. Nestor Humberto Martinez." He was bank superintendent, and he is currently abroad. [Samper] "Government Minister Dr. Horacio Serpa Uribe." He will occupy this post for a second time. He is the ideal person to handle the peace negotiations with the guerrillas. [Samper] "Defense Minister Dr. Fernando Botero Zea." He will be the second civilian to run the Defense Ministry--a big question mark for the military. [Samper] "Finance Minister Dr. Guillermo Perry Rubio." He has been adviser to the president-elect for many years. He has mastered public treasury matters. [Samper] "Foreign Relations Minister Dr. Rodrigo Pardo." He is a political expert who knows how foreign policy works.
