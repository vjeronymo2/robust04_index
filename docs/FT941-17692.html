940101 FT 01 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Danka expands in US Danka Business Systems, the acquisitive office equipment supplier based in the US and quoted in London, has expanded its operations in the Chicago, Illinois, area via the acquisition of American Office Equipment Company for a maximum Dollars 9.3m (Pounds 6.28m). AOE, an authorised copier dealer for Sharp, Ricoh and Mita, has 11 branches throughout the Chicago metropolitan area and in northern Indiana, and represented, said Mr Daniel Doyle, Danka chief executive officer, his company's entry into the copier market in that region. He added that the acquisition would serve as a new base of operations in the US Midwest. In its most recent financial year, AOE had sales of Dollars 28m, operating profits of Dollars 1m and net assets of Dollars 2m. The purchase price comprises Dollars 8.3m cash and a contingent consideration of up to Dollars 1m dependent on future earnings. In the six months to September 30, pre-tax profits at Danka soared 91 per cent to Pounds 13.3m on the back of exchange rates and a series of acquisitions. During the half, it spent some Pounds 36m on 13 companies, including its first in the UK. In October Danka made an Dollars 86m share issue and refinanced its long-term borrowings.
