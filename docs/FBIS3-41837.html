Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, RIA -- Russia's President Boris Yeltsin has signed a decree on the State Committee for Sanitary and Epidemiological Control of Russia, qualifying it as a central agency of the federal executive branch. The committee will establish sanitary and hygienic standards and update regulatory acts in terms of the population's health protection against the adverse impact of environment and habitat. The committee is also due to define priorities in research into sanitary and epidemiological welfare of the population.
