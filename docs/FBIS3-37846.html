Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Aleksandr Krylovich] [Text] Moscow February 23 TASS -- The creation of a "political coalition" to solve the crisis in Bosnia is the major result of the Bonn meeting of representatives of Foreign Ministries of Russia and the West, according to Russian envoy to former Yugoslavia Vitaliy Churkin who arrived from Bonn on Wednesday [23 February]. "The meeting demonstrated a high level of coincidence in approaches and assessments regarding what is to be done in Bosnia-Herzegovina", he told reporters. The political essence of the Bonn meeting will determine practical steps in the near future, according to Churkin who said that "now we have passed the crisis situation and are moving to intensive political contacts". He stressed that it is important to avoid new crises in Bosnia-Herzegovina.
