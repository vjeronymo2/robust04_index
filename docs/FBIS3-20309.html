Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed article: "Andreatta on Lithuania-Poland Friendship Accord"] [Text] (ANSA) Rome, March 29 -- Italian Foreign Minister Beniamino Andreatta, in his role as duty chairman of the Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe, has expressed his pleasure over the recent inking of a friendship and cooperation agreement between Lithuania and Poland, according to a Farnesina [Foreign Ministry] communique today. Such an agreement is an example of how misunderstandings of the past can be overcome in a framework of cooperation and in a European spirit so as to give a new positive dimension to bilateral relations among neighboring countries, according to Andreatta. The accord can be seen as fully ensconced within the framework of fundamental CSCE principles and constitutes an important contribution to security and stability in the entire region, the communique added.
