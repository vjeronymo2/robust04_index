Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [Text] Concerning the recent accident involving aircraft belonging to the U.S. Marine Corps, the prefectural government asked an office of the four military branches coordinator of the U.S. Forces on Okinawa on 22 December to investigate the cause of the accident and to prevent similar accidents from recurring. The prefectural government pointed out that although the accident occurred in training airspace, concerns of local residents who live near Kadena Air Base [KAB] are growing because the aircraft took off from KAB. Accordingly, the office was asked to conduct a through safety review.
