January 15, 1989, Sunday, Bulldog Edition

KEATING SAYS HE WON'T BE NO. 3 IN JUSTICE DEPT.

Associate Atty. Gen. Francis A. Keating II said he will not be reappointed to the Justice Department's No. 3 position in the new Administration.

Keating said he was informed Thursday of the decision, which had been made jointly by Atty. Gen. Dick Thornburgh and the transition team for President-elect Bush.
