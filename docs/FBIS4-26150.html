BFN [From the "Provincial News Hookup" program] [Text] The seventh standing committee meeting of the Seventh Zhejiang Provincial Committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference [CPPCC] was held in Hangzhou today. Liu Feng and Chen Fawen, respectively chairman and vice chairman of the provincial CPPCC committee, presided over the morning plenary session. Participants adopted the meeting's schedule and agenda. According to the agenda, the meeting will discuss further strengthening Zhejiang's socialist spiritual civilization, offer relevant opinions and suggestions, and invite leaders from the departments concerned to give briefings on Zhejiang's progress in promoting socialist spiritual civilization, in implementing nine-year compulsory education, and in launching an anticorruption campaign to foster honesty, as well as on the status of public order in Zhejiang. It will also discuss and approve proposals to be forwarded to the provincial government, as well as personnel matters. In the morning, (Shen Hui), deputy director of the provincial party committee's propaganda department; (Zhu Yaozu), deputy secretary of the provincial discipline inspection commission; and (Cai Yangmeng), deputy director of the provincial public security department, reported to the standing committee members on Zhejiang's progress in promoting socialist spiritual civilization and in launching an anticorruption campaign to foster honesty, as well as on the status of public order in Zhejiang. Sun Jiaxian, Wu Renyuan, Zhan Shaowen, Ding Deyun, Xue Yanzhuang, Que Duanlin, Zhang Kejian, and Wang Xixuan, vice chairmen of the provincial CPPCC committee, attended the meeting, as did (Li Qing), secretary general of the provincial CPPCC committee. Wang Qidong, vice chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee, attended the meeting by invitation. Present at the meeting in observer status were veteran comrades (Mao Jiehua) and Shang Jingcai; Sun Yannian, member of the national CPPCC committee standing committee; and some national CPPCC committee members in Hangzhou.
