BFN [Text] Cairo, 4 Apr (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak held a round of talks with Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad this afternoon at the presidential palace in al-Qubbah. The talks were attended on the Egyptian side by Prime Minister Dr. 'Atif Sidqi, Defense Minister Field Marshal Muhammad Husayn Tantawi, Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa, Information Minister Safwat al- Sharif, and Egypt's Ambassador to Syria Mustafa 'Abd-al-'Aziz. On the Syrian side, the talks were attended by Syrian Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddamm, Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar', and Syria's Ambassador in Egypt 'Isa Darwish.
