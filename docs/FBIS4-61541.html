BFN [Untitled report from ITAR-TASS/INTERFAX "News in Brief" roundup] [Text] The "Gazprom" Russian Joint-Stock Company has warned the leaders of a number of Russian Federation Republics and oblasts that deliveries of natural gas for power industry facilities and industrial enterprises may be cut in the light of their indebtedness to "Gazprom." Warnings that gas deliveries will cease for various regions 4 through 9 May have been sent to Karachayevo-Cherkessia, Ingushetia, Kabardino-Balkaria, North Ossetia, Bashkiria, and Dagestan, as well as Perm, Novosibirsk, Astrakhan, Kemerovo, Rostov, and Voronezh Oblasts, and Stavropol Kray.
