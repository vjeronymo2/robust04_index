941115 FT 15 NOV 94 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There was strong general demand this week, reports the Tea Brokers' Association. Landed coloury Assams were again well supported at fully firm rates. Plainer mediums met more enquiry and sometimes advanced but plainest North Indians lost 2 to 3p. Bright and coloury medium East Africans were generally fully firm although prices eased 2 to 3p by the close. Ceylon bop's lost ground but the remainder were about steady with more enquiry for pf's. Offshore good demand but at slightly easier rates. Quotations: best available; 185p/kg, nom good; 142p/kg, good medium; 122p/kg, medium; 112p/kg low medium; 86p, the highest price realised this week was 190p for an assam pd.
