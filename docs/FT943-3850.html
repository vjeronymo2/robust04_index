940912 FT 12 SEP 94 / International Company News: South African fruit merger Two of South Africa's biggest fruit exporters, Unifroco and Outspan, are to merge their European operations into a new company called Capespan, writes Mark Suzman in Johannesburg. Last year the combined export sales of the two companies exceeded R2.5bn (Dollars 554m). The two will have an equal share of Capespan, which will be based in the UK. Their subsidiary offices throughout Europe will be merged and put under Capespan's control. The new company plans to operate a combined marketing season of 50 weeks, compared with Unifroco's 35 weeks and Outspan's 25 weeks.
