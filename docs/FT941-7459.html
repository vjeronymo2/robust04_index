940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / International Company News: Black & Decker margins to rise Black & Decker, the US power tools group, expects higher margins during 1994, due to lower costs and new product introductions, but said the gains would be confined to the US, Reuter reports. 'We do not anticipate significant improvement in European consumer or industrial markets during 1994,' said Mr Nolan Archibald, chairman and chief execu-tive.
