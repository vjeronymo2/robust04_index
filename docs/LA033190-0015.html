March 31, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

PRESIDENT BUSH BANS BROCCOLI

All the publicity about President Bush's distaste for broccoli is just so much "corn" and will only encourage tomato throwing.

If the President will read my lips, he will discover that broccoli is good for a kinder, gentler nation. It certainly is a better diet than chili or lima beans. Don't give up, Barbara. The White House may yet become the Green House.

MONROE RUBINGER

Beverly Hills
