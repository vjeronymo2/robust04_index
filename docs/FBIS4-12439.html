BFN [Text] Cairo, 9 Apr (MENA)--Information Minister Safwat al-Sharif and Rashid Bin-'Abdallah al-Na'imi, United Arab Emirates [UAE] foreign minister, left Cairo for Sanaa today as envoys of President Husni Mubarak and UAE President Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan. They are scheduled to meet with Yemeni President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih this evening and Vice President 'Ali Salim al-Bid tomorrow morning. The visit is part of the efforts made by President Mubarak and Shaykh Zayid to contain the Yemeni crisis. Assistant Foreign Minister Badr Hammam is accompanying al-Sharif and al-Na'imi on their visit. Earlier today, President Mubarak received a message from Shaykh Zayid during his meeting with UAE Foreign Minister al-Na'imi. Al-Sharif stated that the message deals with the joint efforts by President Mubarak and Shaykh Zayid to find a solution for the Yemeni crisis by bringing the views of the Yemeni political leaders closer together.
