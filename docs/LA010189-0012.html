January 1, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

MOVIES TO TAPE

Gone with the Wind (TNT Sunday at 4 p.m.): The greatest popular movie of them all. (4:45)

The Day of the Jackal (Channel 11 Monday at 1:30 a.m.): An epic-scale 1973 suspense thriller centering on a plot to assassinate De Gaulle. Fred Zinnemann directed this adaptation of the Frederick Forsyth best seller.

Que Viva Mexico! (Bravo Monday at 5 p.m.): A fascinating assemblage of Sergei Eisenstein's aborted 1932 poetic epic of Mexican peasantry. (1:30)

The Ladies' Man (Channel 11 Saturday at 4 p.m.): One of Jerry Lewis' funniest, in which he stars as a handyman at a girls' school. Noted for its imaginative use of its huge single set. (2:00)
