920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / Parliament and Politics: Remand centre working party A WORKING group has been set up to help improve the regime at a remand centre where four young inmates committed suicide last year, Mr Peter Lloyd, home office minister, said in a written reply yesterday. A report by Judge Stephen Tumim, chief inspector of prisons, in May found evidence of widespread bullying at Feltham Remand Centre, west London. Mr Lloyd told the Commons that he had decided to set up a working group, in addition to the steps already being undertaken to tackle the issues raised by Judge Tumim's report.
