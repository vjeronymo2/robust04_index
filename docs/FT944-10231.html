941111 FT 11 NOV 94 / UK Company News: Honeysuckle growth Honeysuckle Group, the ladies wear designer, lifted full-year pre-tax profits 56 per cent to Pounds 1.09m for the year to May 31. The rise, from Pounds 693,852, was struck on turnover 42 per cent ahead from Pounds 15.8m to Pounds 22.4m. Mr David Stern, chairman, said the present season was ahead of budget. Earnings per share improved to 9.1p (5.8p). A recommended final dividend of 2p gives a total 3p (2.25p) for the year. The shares firmed 6p to 75p.
