Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Idil, Dec.10 (AA) -- The priest of a church in southeastern Turkey has been kidnapped. Melki Tok, the priest of the Ogunduk Church in Sirnak's Idil township, was kidnapped by unidentified gunmen on Sunday [9 January] when he was on his way to the church by a minibus. The gunmen dragged the priest, the spiritual leader of the region's Syriacs, to the rural area. Operations are underway to find the priest.
