October 6, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BAD THINGS BREWING AT RAIDER GAMES

Is it only anti-Raider fans who write to the L.A. Times or does The Times choose to print only letters from such morons? People in L.A., wake up! Just try openly flaunting your support of an opposing team in New York, Chicago, Pittsburgh or any other NFL city (except maybe Anaheim) and security will remove you quicker than a breakaway run by Bo Jackson.

JEFF FINK, Los Angeles
