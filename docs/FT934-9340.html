931111 FT 11 NOV 93 / Levitt trial to start today THE TRIAL of Mr Roger Levitt, former chairman of the collapsed Levitt Group, starts today at London's Old Bailey. Mr Levitt and three other former executives of the financial services company, Mr Mark Reed, Mr Robert Price and Mr Alan McNamara, are charged with a single count of fraudulent trading contrary to section 458 of the Companies Act 1985. All four deny the charge. The trial is expected to last at least three months.
