March 27, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE

Frank W. Considine has resigned as chairman of American National Can Co., Chicago. As previously announced, Jean Gandois, chairman of Pechiney S.A., the France-based parent company, has become ANC's chairman. Considine had planned to step down in June, 1989, but agreed to remain chairman during the transition after the election of Jean-Pierre Ergas to chief executive.
