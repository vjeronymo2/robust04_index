Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report from ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA, ITAR-TASS, and LOGOTIP-KP column under the general heading: "One-Liners"] [Text] Kyrgyzstan -- The republic's government has adopted a decree which requires manufacturing enterprises to dispatch output, raw materials, or semimanufactures only after prepayment by their customers. At the same time maximum timeframes for processing payments -- from three to seven banking days -- have been set. A fine of double the rate of interest for every day's delay will henceforth be exacted for any holdup.
