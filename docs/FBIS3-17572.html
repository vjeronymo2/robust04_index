Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] At 0430 this morning an explosion occurred in Tbilisi on the roof of a six-story building that houses the Iberviziya independent television company. At the Georgian interior minister's press center it was said that as a result, a satellite aerial was rendered inoperative and was blown from the roof onto the street. Window panes in the building were broken. There are no casualties. The explosion is being investigated.
