920305 FT 05 MAR 92 / Support for Conservative party in Scotland falls Support for the Conservative party in Scotland has fallen, according to an opinion poll which shows strong backing for the Scottish National party but a decline in support for Scottish independence. The poll, by Market Research Scotland for Grampian Television and the Aberdeen Press & Journal, put support for the Conservative party at 18 per cent, compared with 21 per cent earlier in the month. Labour was down two points at 39 per cent, the SNP up one point at 30 per cent and the Liberal Democrats down four points at 8 per cent.
