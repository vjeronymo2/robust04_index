941130 FT 30 NOV 94 / Budget 94 (Their Budget): Brewery executive Paul Handley, 48: Finance director of Mansfield Brewery. Married. One of his children is at university and another is doing A-levels. Politics: Not committed, but feels Kenneth Clarke has done a good job as chancellor. Said yesterday: 'There were encouraging remarks regarding interest rates, dispelling some of the pessimism in the City. He has recognised that our industry has a major problem on excise duty differential resulting in illegal bootlegging and lost jobs but to expect the European Community to work together to harmonise duty and resolve a peculiarly British problem is pretty naive.'
