BFN [Excerpt] The House of Representatives held an ordinary session this morning under Brother Dr. 'Abd-al-Wahhab Mahmud, member of the House's Speakership Office. At the outset of the session, the people's deputies reviewed and ratified the minutes of the previous session after urging and appealing to the government agencies to transport and distribute the basic food commodities available in large quantities at ports, mills, warehouses, and silos. These commodities, the House stressed, must be distributed to citizens in all governorates of the Republic of Yemen, and merchants and the public and private food suppliers must sell the commodities hoarded in their houses to the citizens at the official prices. The agencies charged with monitoring prices must fulfill their tasks in this and other fields, continuously and without showing any leniency. [passage omitted]
