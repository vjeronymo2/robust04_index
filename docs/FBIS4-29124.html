BFN ["Pool" Item] [Text] Hanoi, 14 Jun (UNA-ECOPOOL) -- The mixed Vietnamese-Cuban VIC Construction Company has been constituted here. Cuban Deputy Construction Minister Alfonso Llamas will chair the administrative council and Vietnamese Dr. Nguyen Truong Tien will be the company director. VIC's total capital is estimated at $6.6 million, out of which $2.2 million are legal funds provided by the two parties in equal shares. The VIC Construction Company inauguration, which is the first joint venture [previous two words in English] between the two countries, was attended by Vietnamese and Cuban Ministers Nguyen Cong Tan and Arturo Guzman, who are co-presidents of the Vietnamese-Cuban Committee for Economic and Science and Technology Cooperation. The company will be based in Hanoi and will have branches in Haiphong and Ho Chi Minh City. It's activities include bids for construction projects, advisory services, production and commerce of construction materials, among others.
