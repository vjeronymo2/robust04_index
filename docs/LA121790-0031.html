December 17, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

Molly's Back: New episodes of the on-again, off-again series "The Days and Nights of Molly Dodd" return to the Lifetime cable network Jan. 19.

The final 13 episodes of the series, which ran for two seasons on NBC and has now been on Lifetime for two years, will air Saturdays at 10:30 p.m. When we last saw Molly, played by Blair Brown, she was about to reveal who fathered her child. Lifetime had previously announced that it would not produce any new episodes of the series. "Two years is about all any network can stand us," Brown said. CLAUDIA PUIG
