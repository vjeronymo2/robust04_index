930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Gartmore Scotland Investment Trust GARTMORE SCOTLAND Investment Trust: Net asset value 232.6p (174.6p) per capital share at April 30. Net revenue for nine months to end-April amounted to Pounds 961,000 (Pounds 1.21m) for earnings of 7.1p (9p) per income share. Third interim of 2.4p makes 7.2p (8.4p) to date.
