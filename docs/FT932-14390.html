930419 FT 19 APR 93 / International Company News: Brokers granted seats on Shanghai stock exchange THREE foreign brokerages have been granted seats on the Shanghai stock exchange trading floor, marking another step towards the internationalisation of China's leading bourse, Reuter reports. Jardine Fleming, Standard Chartered Securities and Baring Brothers will take up their seats this month, trading special B-shares for foreign investors, the English-language China Daily reported yesterday. Analysts said the move would boost the B-share market, which has been in the doldrums in the past several weeks, depressed by earnings reports from some of the 10 listed B-shares.
