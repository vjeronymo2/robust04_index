October 30, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

3 KILLED IN SANTA ANA

Three men were shot to death and two others were seriously wounded late Monday night outside an apartment complex here, authorities said.

A man believed to be the lone gunman was spotted by witnesses running north on Sullivan Street shortly after the 10 p.m. shooting, Police Sgt. Joseph Esther said.

The victims were not immediately identified, but were described as being in their middle to late 20s.
