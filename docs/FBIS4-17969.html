BFN [ITAR-TASS diplomatic correspondent Anatoliy Krasnov] [Text] Moscow, 29 Apr (ITAR-TASS) -- Relations between Russia and South Korea are definitely improving, Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev said at a meeting today with South Korean National Defense Minister Yi Pyong-tae, here on an official visit. I am pleased that the military component in these relations has a worthy place, the head of Russian diplomacy said. Not only do we no longer look at each other along the barrel of a gun, we are ready to cooperate in setting up these relations for the joint security of friendly states and to have full-scale military cooperation, A. Kozyrev stressed. During the meeting, the ministers intend to discuss the situation on the Korean peninsula along with other international problems and questions of mutual relations.
