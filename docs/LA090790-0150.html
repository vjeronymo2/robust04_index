September 7, 1990, Friday, South Bay Edition

HEAT AND BLACKHAWKS BATTLE FOR APSL WEST TITLE

The resurgent Los Angeles Heat, winner of seven of nine games, plays host to the San Francisco Bay Blackhawks at 7:30 p.m. Saturday at Veterans Stadium in Long Beach in the first game of the American Professional Soccer League West Championship.

The two-game home-and-home playoff series resumes Wednesday at Spartan Stadium in San Jose. If the series ends in a tie, a 30-minute mini-game -- with penalty kicks, if necessary -- will decide the championship. The winner plays for the APSL title in Boston on Sept. 22 against the East Champion Maryland Bays.

The Blackhawks opened the year with a league-record seven consecutive victories. They are in the APSL West final for the second consecutive season. PAUL McLEOD
