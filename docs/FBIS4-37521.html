BFN [Report by Mustafa 'Abduh in Bani Suwayf] [Text] The security organs in Bani Suwayf have arrested 'Ali Yahya Ahmad Abu-al-Hana, one of the prominent members of al-Jihad organization. Reports have come in to Major General Muhammad Fahmi 'Azab, deputy of the Security Department, that the accused prepared a number of leaflets stirring the public to move. The leaflets were to be distributed to the residents of Ihnasiyah. Brigadier General al-Siba'i Abu-al-Layl arrested the defendant carrying a locally-made gun and 17 bullets. He was referred to the prosecution and Prosecution Chief 'Imad Sami ordered that he be jailed for 15 days pending interrogation.
