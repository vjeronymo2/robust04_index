Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Army General Raul Castro, second secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba and minister of the Revolutionary Armed Forces [FAR], has congratulated the Eastern Army for its accomplishments in 1993, when it upheld the status of the best command of the FAR. This was stated during the ceremony marking the beginning of the Eastern Army 1994 defense training year held at military unit No. 2034 in Las Tunas Province. This was due to the fact that this unit and its members performed in an outstanding manner in the province and earned the title of best for 1993. Division General Ramon Espinoza Martin, member of the Central Committee and chief of the Eastern Army, stated that this command, in addition to staying at the national vanguard in defense tasks, attained first place in the Eighth Science and Technology Forum.
