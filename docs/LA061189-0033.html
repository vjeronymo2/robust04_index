June 11, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ERASING BORDERS

Barbara Bush for President!

In the meantime, that other Bush should look homeward when calling for walls to be torn down and borders erased.

Europe can't be all that locked into a fortress if a teen-ager from West Germany can land a single-engine plane in Red Square.

Drive from Moscow to Munich? Jolly good! How about from Vera Cruz to Santa Cruz without armed military guards at the border, the ditch, the inverted wall, the migra moat?

MARK NICHOLS

Beverly Hills
