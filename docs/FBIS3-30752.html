Language: Polish Article Type:BFN [Text] President Lech Walesa is treating the Borowski-Pawlak dispute as an internal matter of the coalition and the government, Andrzej Zakrzewski, head of the president's political service team, has told RZECZPOSPOLITA. The president considers it inappropriate to adopt a position in the present situation. There also are not sufficient grounds for the president to break his holiday in the mountains, the spokesman said.
