April 26, 1989, Wednesday, Valley Edition

UMPIRES, WEATHER COMBINE TO HAMPER BASEBALL SCHEDULE

A Northwest Valley Conference baseball game between Taft and Granada Hills highs, postponed Tuesday when umpires failed to show up, has been rescheduled for this afternoon at 3 at Granada Hills.

Canyons and Saugus, rained out Tuesday, will play their Golden League game this afternoon at 3 at Saugus.

In another makeup game, Monroe will play at Sylmar at 3 p.m. today in a Valley Pac-8 Conference game.
