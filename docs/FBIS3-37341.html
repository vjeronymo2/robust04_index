Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The nationwide indefinite political strike declared for 15 February ended early. Not a single worker in Minsk went out onto Independence Square in support of the demands of the strike committees. There was a rally on Independence Square. Among the speakers, there were only representatives of the strike committees and the BPF [Belarus People's Front]. They sharply criticized the government and the Supreme Soviet. From Independence Square, the people in the rally proceeded in a column to the Supreme Soviet building, where they passed on their demands to Chairman of Supreme Soviet Mechyslaw Hryb. The demands include the resignation of the government and elections to the Supreme Soviet in March. In the opinion of the ralliers, the speaker should give his reply not later than 22 February, the day the Supreme Soviet resumes work. The latest rally on Independence Square is set for precisely that time. The members of the strike committee are continuing to carry out organizational work, calling on the workers to strike.
