941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / Observer: Monkey puzzle John Major's reliance on the imperfect prognostications of his spin doctors must continue awhile - Madama Rosina the Palmist is unavailable. On Bournemouth pier the sign says Madame Rosina, palmist, is closed due to unforseen (sic) circumstances. Maybe she's celebrating cornering the Bournemouth market in cut-price John and Norma Major jigsaw puzzles, which have sold out - after they were reduced from Pounds 2 to Pounds 1.
