BFN [Text] The Amal Movement's operations room in the south has issued a statement saying that one of its units detonated an explosive device near an Israeli enemy and agent [South Lebanese Army] mechanized patrol on the Bra'shit-Bayt Yahun road at 1250 today. A half-track vehicle was completely destroyed and three personnel inside it were killed. Another unit attacked the same patrol with rockets and machine guns. The clash with the patrol lasted 15 minutes.
