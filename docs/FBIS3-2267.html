Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] President Geydar Aliyev has begun his official visit to the PRC. The visit will give an opportunity for the improvement of the political, economic, and cultural relations between the two countries. President Aliyev received Ambassador Xia Shuyuan before leaving for the PRC and discussed matters related to the improvement of the relations between the two countries. Aliyev said that his visit to the PRC will be a very important event in Azerbaijan's history. He described the PRC as one of the largest countries in the world, which, as one of the permanent members of the UN Security Council, has an important role in world politics. Special attention was focused on cooperation between Azerbaijan and the PRC in the economic, cultural, scientific, and technological fields. For his part, Ambassador Xia Shuyuan said that his country's people and leaders have highly assessed President Geydar Aliyev's visit and noted that it will be of historic importance to Azerbaijan.
