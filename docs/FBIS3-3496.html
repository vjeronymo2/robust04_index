Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Xiamen, March 9 (XINHUA) -- Xiamen Special Economic Zone has accelerated economic development to mark the 10th anniversary of an inspection made by Deng Xiaoping. Among the objectives are pushing ahead with the development of Haicang, a district set aside for Taiwan investors, implementing some special policies for free ports and increasing industrial output. As a pilot reform zone, Xiamen has developed an overseas-oriented economy, with manufacturing industries as mainstay. According to the blueprint, Haichang will be constructed into a multi-functional area with urban districts, petrochemical plants and harbor. Technology is being updated in engineering machinery, rubber and pharmaceutical industries. Motorcycles, audio components, containers and glasses, all newly-launched projects, will go into production within the year.
