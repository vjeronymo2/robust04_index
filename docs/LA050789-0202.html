May 7, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

ZOO TEAM OMISSION

Sheryl Kornman's article (April 30) on the new Children's Zoo was both accurate and informative. Throughout the article she developed the theme of a well-coordinated team consisting of the owner, architects and specialty contractors. Unfortunately one key member of the team was not mentioned, specifically the general contractor, Ray Wilson Co.

We are very proud of the accomplishments of the group assembled by Greater Los Angeles Zoo Assn., and we are very proud of our contribution to the Adventure Island project.

CHRISTOPHER J. DURKEE

Pasadena

Durkee is vice president of marketing of the Ray Wilson Co.
