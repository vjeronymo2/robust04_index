940714 FT 14 JUL 94 / Lloyd's to probe Sturge losses Lloyd's of London is to launch an investigation into losses suffered by two syndicates of the Sturge agency. The investigation, to be supervised by the insurance market's regulatory board, will concern losses registered by syndicates 210 and 428 in the 1990 underwriting year. Such investigations, known in the market as 'loss reviews', are triggered when losses exceed more than 100 per cent of stamp capacity, the amount of premium income a syndicate is allowed to accept.
