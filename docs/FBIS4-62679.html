BFN [Text] Press-Secretary of the Armenian Foreign Ministry Aram Safaryan dismissed a statement by the Azeri side that talks on the settlement of the Karabakh problem with participation of defense ministers of Armenia, Azerbaijan and Commander of the Nagorno-Karabakh Army mediated by Russian Defense Minister Pavel Grachev were disrupted by Yerevan's fault. Earlier on Tuesday [17 May] a source in the Azeri Embassy in Moscow claimed that Armenia insisted on considering the status of Nagorno-Karabakh along with the withdrawal of troops from seized Azeri territories. Press-secretary of the Armenian president Levon Zurabyan declined to comment on the situation around the talks until the return of the Armenian delegation from Moscow.
