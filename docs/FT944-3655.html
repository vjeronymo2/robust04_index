941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / Emerging Markets: Argentina Telecom Argentina said it has established a sponsored American Depositary Receipt programme with Morgan Guaranty Trust Co, a unit of JP Morgan & Co Inc. The company said that each American Depositary Share will represent 10 Class B ordinary shares of Telecom Argentina and will begin trading on the New York Stock Exchange today. Edited by John Pitt.
