August 7, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: TV & VIDEO

An 'Inside' Job: A show that will reveal the workings of the White House is being produced by the Washington Post Group and the Ventura Entertainment Group.

"Inside the White House" will use newly available material from the presidential libraries to give viewers an "incisive, behind-the-scenes look at presidents from Franklin D. Roosevelt through Ronald Reagan," producers said. In addition, dramatizations with actual historical dialogue will be combined with existing footage. The producers are shopping the show around to several networks. GREG BRAXTON
