BFN [Text] A delegation from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Russian Federation led by (Aleksey Pakin), ambassador and deputy director of the African Department of the Russian Ministry of Foreign Affairs, has been visiting Mali since 14 May. The delegation has held several discussions with our authorities. It held a working session with a Malian delegation at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Koulouba District. This political consultation enabled the two delegations to hold broad discussions on international issues. A similarity in the views of the two governments was noted. During the meeting, the two sides signed a draft agreement on consultations between our two countries.
