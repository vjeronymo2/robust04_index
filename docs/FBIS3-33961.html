Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The workers of the Punjab unit of the Congress-I staged a demonstration before the U.S. Embassy in New Delhi today. They were protesting against the recent statement of the U.S. president, Mr. Bill Clinton, on Punjab. A number of legislators and MP's from Punjab participated in the demonstration. The police told our correspondent that tear gas shells were fired and water cannons used to disperse the demonstrators. Later, the demonstrators submitted a memorandum to the embassy officials.
