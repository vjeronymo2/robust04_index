BFN [Text] An anonymous caller has telephoned a private radio station claiming that 17 November was responsible for explosions that took place shortly after midnight at the offices of the Alico and Nationale Nederlanden insurance companies. The explosion at Alico's office on 119 Kifisias Avenue was caused by a 3.5-inch rocket launched from a construction site across the road. The explosion at Nationale Nederlanden's office on 131 Kifisias Avenue was caused by a timed explosive mechanism. The caller who made the claim on behalf of the 17 November organization also said that a few days ago, a British aircraft carrier was selected as a target by the organization but the launching mechanism failed. Around the same time as the explosions in Kifisias Avenue, a blast occurred by a parked car at 3 Kanaris Street, Stamata, Attiki. The investigating authorities believe this explosion is not related with the two bomb attacks on Kifisias Avenue.
