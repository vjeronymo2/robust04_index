920523 FT 23 MAY 92 / Kurdish poll evenly balanced Jubilant crowds celebrated as election results for Kurdish areas of northern Iraq last night produced a parliament evenly balanced between the two main parties, KDP (Kurdistan Democratic Party) and PUK (Patriotic Union of Kurdistan), Gareth Smyth writes from Arbil, northern Iraq. Each will have 50 seats, with five others reserved for Assyrian and Christian minorities. In the poll for leader of the Kurdistan Liberation Movement, a run-off in two months will be contested by the two front-runners from the first round, Mr Massoud Barzani (KDP) and Mr Jalal Talabani (PUK).
