BFN [Article signed PVB: "Libyan Embassy Can Resume Payments Through Banks"] [Excerpt] Following a decision by the Belgian Government, the Libyan Embassy in Brussels can now freely resume regular payments of its current expenses through banks. This was made possible after negotiations between the Belgian Foreign and Finance Ministries and after it was decided at the 27 May cabinet meeting to issue a ministerial order exempting the embassy from complicated procedures to pay for its transactions.
