BFN [Text] Kigali, 7 Apr (AFP) -- Three Rwandan ministers and their three United Nations guards were taken captive Thursday [7 April] by members of President Juvenal Habyarimana's Presidential Guard, a spokesman for the UN mission in Rwanda said. "We do not know whether these elements of the Presidential Guard were acting on their own initiative or following orders," the spokesman said. The three members of the cabinet, all members of the opposition, were named as Faustin Rucogoza, Frederic Nzamurambaho and Landouald Ndasingwa, the Information, Agriculture and Labour Ministers. They were taken from their homes, some with relatives, the spokesman added.
