930608 FT 08 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Greenway disposal approved AT yesterday's EGM, shareholders of Greenway Holdings approved the Dollars 4.15m (Pounds 2.69m) disposal of its US-based Kingston Oil subsidiary to a consortium including the present management team. The deal marks the end of Greenway's involvement in oil and gas exploration and production. The group intends to concentrate on waste oil recycling.
