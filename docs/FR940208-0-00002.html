DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE Food and Nutrition Service 7 CFR Parts 271, 272, and 273 [Amendment No. 347] Food Stamp Program; Technical Amendments Concerning Disabled in Group Homes and Income Exclusion for Plans for Achieving Self-Support AGENCY: Food and Nutrition Service, USDA. ACTION: Final rule.

SUMMARY: This rule finalizes provisions of the proposed rule published on December 21, 1992 concerning certain provisions of the Food, Agriculture, Conservation, and Trade Act Amendments of 1991 that dealt with disabled persons in group homes and income exclusions for Plans for Achieving Self-Support. This final rule expands the food stamp eligibility of certain blind and disabled individuals residing in group homes and excludes income of an SSI recipient necessary for the fulfillment of a Plan for Achieving Self Support (PASS). DATES: The amendment to 7 CFR 273.9(c)(17) was effective October 1, 1990 and is applicable on the earlier of December 13, 1991 (the date of enactment of Pub. L. 102&hyph;237), October 1, 1990 (for food stamp households for which the State agency knew, or had notice, that a household member had a PASS), or beginning on the date that a fair hearing was requested.
