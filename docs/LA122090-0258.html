December 20, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

THE SIDELINES;

U.S. FLAG TO GRACE NFL HELMETS

NFL players will wear American flag decals on their helmets, starting in this weekend's games.

"This presentation of the American flag is a very appropriate method for the NFL clubs and players to affirm their support for the men and women of our armed forces on duty in Saudi Arabia and the Persian Gulf," Commissioner Paul Tagliabue said today.
