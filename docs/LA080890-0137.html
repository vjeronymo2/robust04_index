August 8, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

7 HURT, 1 CRITICALLY, AS 3 CARS COLLIDE

Seven people, including two young children, were injured Tuesday night in a three-vehicle collision at a busy intersection, police said.

One critically injured adult was airlifted by Lifeflight helicopter to Mission Hospital Regional Medical Center in Mission Viejo after the 8:30 p.m. accident at the intersection of Orangethorpe and Placentia avenues on the border with Fullerton, Orange County fire officials said.
