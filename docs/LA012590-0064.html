January 25, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

THE SOUL OF 'WRITERS ROW' SHINES THROUGH

I really liked your article about homeless writers on Skid Row.

I'm 14, and usually I only look at the paper long enough to read Dear Abby, the comics and the horoscopes. But the article caught my eye. I think it showed that not all homeless people have given up on themselves, and lots of people out there, such as Dino and Jackie, who are trying to improve life not only for themselves, but for others as well. It made me grateful for everything I've got.

MARTA MONTORO

Glendale
