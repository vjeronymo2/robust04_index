Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [From Tokyo NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 25 March Morning Edition] [Text] On 24 March, Kazuo Aichi, director general of the Defense Agency [DA], met with Yi Yang-ho, chairman of the South Korea Joint Chiefs of Staff who is accompanying President Kim Yong-sam on his state visit to Japan. Discussing the situation on the Korean peninsula, Yi said: "Tensions are heightening between North and South Korea. South Korea is trying to prevent North Korea from taking any provocative action against the South by introducing `Patriot' ground-to-air missiles as part of South Korea-U.S. military cooperation."
