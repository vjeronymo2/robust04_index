940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / World Stock Markets: Canada Toronto closed modestly weaker but above its intraday low after quiet trading. The TSE 300 composite index dipped 12.1 to 4,269.0 and declines led advances by 393 to 310. Volume was 45.2m shares valued at CDollars 520m. Ten of the 14 sub-indices were lower, led by the golds group, down 1.8 per cent, as the bullion price declined slightly and the Canadian dollar rallied against its US counterpart. Among gold issues, American Barrick receded CDollars 3/4 to CDollars 31 7/8.
