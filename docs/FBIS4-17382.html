BFN [Unattributed report under the "In Brief" rubric] [Text] Khabarovsk -- "There are no plans at the moment to build any underground or surface nuclear electric-power stations on the territory of Khabarovsk Kray," Nikolay Balyukin, general director of the "Khabarovskenergo" joint-stock company said, commenting on reports of the supposed construction together with Japan of an underground nuclear electric-power station in the area of Komsomolsk-na-Amure. In his words, the discussions conducted in past years about the possible construction of a nuclear station in the area of Lake Emoron ended in the decision that a return to the question is possible only after serious scientific studies and the holding of a referendum among the population.
