Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 20 Jan (MENA)--President Husni Mubarak today received Guinean Foreign Minister Ibrahima Sylla, who delivered a message from Guinean President Lansana Conte. The meeting was also attended by Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa and Guinean Charge d'Affaires in Cairo (Ibrahima Diallo).
