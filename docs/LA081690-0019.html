August 16, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: STAGE

Broadway Bound: Neil Simon is off to Broadway again. His new show, "Lost in Yonkers," is slated to open in New York later this season.

Gene Saks will direct the play; Emanuel Azenberg will produce. "Yonkers" is said to be about a family struggling to stay together during the Depression. BETH KLEID
