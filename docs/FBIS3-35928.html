Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] We have just received a press release from the press service of the Abkhazian Supreme Soviet, stating that a wave of rallies has started in Abkhazia in defense of the republic's independence. The multi-ethnic people of Abkhazia believe that the upcoming Russo-Georgian negotiations, the summit meeting, and the signing of an agreement, starting without a full settlement of the Abkhaz-Georgian conflict, could lead to another escalation of military action.
