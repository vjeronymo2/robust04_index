920509 FT 09 MAY 92 / Markets (At a glance): Smaller companies rally Small company shares continued their recent rally this week. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies Index (capital gains version) rose by 2.2 per cent to 1276.32 over the seven days to May 7, while the County Small Companies Index also rose 2.2 per cent to 1006.1 over the same period. This week, Perpetual is launching a smaller companies unit trust which has an initial charge of 5.25 per cent and an annual fee of 1.25 per cent. Minimum initial investment is Pounds 1,000 and there is also a savings scheme option of Pounds 100 a month.
