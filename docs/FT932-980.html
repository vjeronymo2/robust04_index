930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Dundee & London Dundee & London Investment Trust's net asset value at end-April was 270p, compared with 221p at October 31 and with 262p a year earlier. Total income for the six months fell to Pounds 1.04m (Pounds 1.21m) and earnings worked through at 3.85p (4.75p). The interim dividend is cut to 3p and a final of not less than 6p is expected.
