940624 FT 24 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Optometrics Improved efficiency within its business and improved margins helped Optometrics, the USM-quoted optical systems group, to raise pre-tax profits by 67 per cent from Dollars 103,000 to Dollars 172,000(Pounds 113,160) in the year to March 31. This was after a Dollars 63,000 charge connected to its investment in Euro Biosystems. Overall sales came to Dollars 3.73m (Dollars 3.58m). Gross profit was Dollars 1.39m (Dollars 1.31m). Earnings per share worked through at 93 cents (70 cents).
