Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Talks were held in the Karlovac suburb of Turanj today. The Croatian side was represented by General Petar Stipetic, commander of the Zagreb operational zone, Pavle Miljevac, commander of the Karlovac operational zone, Captain Davor Domazet, and Slavko Degoricija, chairman of the state commission for talks with rebel Serbs. The Serbian side was headed by Colonel Kosta Novakovic, while General (Met Guiness) attended the talks on behalf of the UN Protection Forces [UNPROFOR]. A statement was issued after three hours of talks saying that the [Christmas] cease-fire [between Serbs and Croats in Croatia] was honored and that it has been extended until 31 January. The talks will be resumed at state commission level. It was concluded that expert military groups should be formed to prepare an agreement on a lasting truce.
