Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] President Ilyas al-Hirawi this evening received a telephone call from Syrian President Hafiz al-Asad, who condemned the crime at the Sayyidat al-Najat Church. President al-Asad conveyed his condolences to the victims and wished a speedy recovery for the wounded. He said that what afflicted Lebanon afflicted Syria. He stressed that the Syrian security services, in cooperation with the Lebanese services, will make every effort to apprehend the culprits.
