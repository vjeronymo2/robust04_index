Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Our colleague Zeljko Raic, who is covering the peace negotiations between General Ante Roso, commander of the Croatian Defense Council, and General Rasim Delic, commander of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army, has just told us that a cease-fire agreement has been signed between the Croat and the Muslim sides in Bosnia-Herzegovina. The agreement takes effect on 25 February.
