930625 FT 25 JUN 93 / Timorese quit embassies Seven East Timorese who occupied the Swedish and Finnish embassies in Jakarta began leaving yesterday after the countries declined to grant them diplomatic asylum, write William Keeling in Jakarta and Peter Wise in Lisbon. The men, who had claimed political persecution by the Indonesian government, were later quoted as saying they had been given assurances they would not be arrested.
