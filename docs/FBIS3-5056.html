Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 3 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent birthday tables including 60th birthday tables to participants in the national agricultural conference. The benevolent birthday tables were conveyed on March 2 to 339 people, including farmers, veteran party workers, management officials and young men and women from different rural communities who had performed feats in their efforts to implement the socialist rural theses with unfailing loyalty to the party and the leader. Upon receiving the birthday tables, they expressed their resolve to honorably fulfil their obligations as agricultural working people in charge of the granary of the country and staunchly struggle for the ultimate solution of the socialist rural question and for the accomplishment of the revolutionary cause of chuche.
