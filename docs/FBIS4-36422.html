BFN [Text] The Ministries of Defense and Interior and Security have praised the efforts of citizens in the various governorates of the Democratic Republic of Yemen [DRY], who are discharging their national duties with a high degree of responsibility, bringing to realization the noble national unity for our people in the DRY, supporting the DRY by supporting the Armed Forces and the security forces. The two ministries call on all the citizens to report any destructive activities on the part of the remnants of the mercenaries of the al-Ahmar gang in any area, particularly in those areas currently being liberated and mopped up to get rid of these remnants.
