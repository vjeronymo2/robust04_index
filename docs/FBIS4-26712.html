BFN [Text] Headed by First Deputy Prime Minister Dostiyev, a 16-member Supreme Soviet delegation from the Republic of Tajikstan arrived in Harbin from Beijing by plane on the evening of 23 June to begin its two-day visit in our province. Qi Guiyuan, vice chairman of the provincial people's congress standing committee, and Secretary General Liu Tongnian, welcomed the delegation at the airport. First Vice Chairman Dostiyev and his entourage was invited by the standing committee of the National People's Congress [NPC] to visit China on 21 June. This is the Tajik legislative Assembly's first visit to China. During its stopover in our province, the delegation will pay an official visit to the provincial people's congress standing committee and visit enterprises and plants in Harbin city. Li Yongtai, member of the NPC standing committee, who accompanied the delegation during the visit, also arrived in Harbin in the same plane.
