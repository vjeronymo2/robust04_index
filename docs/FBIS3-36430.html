Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Anatoliy Ladin report under the "News" rubric: "Kazakhstan and Tajikistan Security Forces Find Common Language"] [Text] Almaty -- According to a report from the public relations department of the Kazakhstan National Security Committee, an agreement on cooperation and collaboration has been signed between the Kazakhstan National Security Committee and the Tajikistan State Committee for National Security. The sides agreed on joint measures in the fight against contraband, corruption, organized crime, international terrorism, and the unlawful movement of narcotics, psychotropic substances, and weapons. Tajikistan's national security organs will be given assistance by their Kazakhstan colleagues in the training of cadres.
