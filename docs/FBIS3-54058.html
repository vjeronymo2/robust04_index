Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Joint Italian-Kuwaiti naval maneuvers have begun in Kuwaiti waters in the Gulf, northeast of the island of Kubbar. They are aimed at training the Navy of the emirate in the defense of the shores and, in particular, of the oil plants. The exercise will last two days; 600 sailors of both countries will participate. The frigate "Sagittario", the corvette "Urania" and 330 men--sailors and officers--will represent Italy. Firing practice with real shells is scheduled.
