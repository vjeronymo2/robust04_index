BFN ["Presidential Council Decision No. 8", issued by the Presidential Office in Sanaa 5 May] [Text] The Presidential Council today made the following decision: Presidential Council Decision No. 8 of 1994 regarding the dismissal of Brigadier General Defense Minister Haytham Qasim Tahir from his post and turning him over for questioning on the charge of committing a number of [word indistinct]. The chairman of the Presidential Council, after referring to the Constitution of the Republic of Yemen, and seeing that Brig. Gen. Haytham Qasim Tahir committed war crimes associated with his position, and with reference to the public interest, [word indistinct] the Presidential Council decided: Article 1. Defense Minister Haytham Qasim Tahir shall be dismissed from his post, questioned, and put on trial in accordance with Article 111 of the Republic of Yemen's Constitution for the crimes he committed while carrying out his duties. Article 2. This decision takes effect on the date of its release and publication in the official gazette. Released at the Presidential office in Sanaa on 25 Dhil Qi'dah, 1414 Hejira, corresponding to 5 May, 1994. [Signed] Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council
