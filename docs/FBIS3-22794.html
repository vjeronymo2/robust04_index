Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] In order to conduct a systematic analysis of world experience in the development of nuclear power engineering and the utilization of nuclear power, the Ukrainian Cabinet of Ministers has resolved to create the Interdepartmental Council for Cooperation with the International Atomic Energy Agency [IAEA]. The Interdepartmental Council will be a consultative body operating on a public voluntary basis. It is headed by Umanets, chairman of the State Committee for Nuclear Energy [Derzhkomatom].
