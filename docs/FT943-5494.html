940903 FT 03 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Courtyard Leisure cuts loss to Pounds 0.3m Courtyard Leisure, the USM-quoted wine bar and restaurant operator, reported reduced pre-tax losses of Pounds 296,386 for the the year to end-March, against a deficit of Pounds 577,765 last time. The outcome was struck on turnover down from Pounds 2.22m to Pounds 1.9m. Losses per share were reduced to 2.1p (5.4p). There was a net loss on disposals amounting to Pounds 24,907 (Pounds 195,000) arising from the sale of the Benjamin Stillingfleet wine bar. A further Pounds 32,330 was written off fixed assets investments. Net interest payable fell from Pounds 58,246 to Pounds 38,999.
