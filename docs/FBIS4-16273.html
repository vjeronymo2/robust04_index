BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Sergey Ostanin] [Text] Moscow 14 April, TASS -- A Russian delegation headed by ambassador at large Yuriy Dubinin failed to visit the Ukrainian port of Odessa on Thursday after the Ukrainian Foreign and Defence Ministries denied it a permission to enter the city, a spokesman for Russian naval forces told TASS today. In Sevastopol, the main base of the Black Sea Fleet, the Russian delegation met with the fleet command and the command of the research vessel Cheleken, the spokesman reported. Officers of Ukraine's Naval Command and Defence Ministry were not supposed to take part in these meetings.
