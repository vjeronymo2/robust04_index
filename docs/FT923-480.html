920928 FT 28 SEP 92 / Russians seized in Tajikistan Rival factions in Tajikistan's growing civil war have seized members of a Russian missile unit and threatened to kill them, a spokesman for the Russian defence ministry said yesterday, Reuter reports. The spokesman said an unknown number of soldiers, including a unit commander, were taken hostage from their base in the village of Lomonosovo, amid fierce fighting in the Kurgan-Tyube region.
