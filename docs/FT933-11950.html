930727 FT 27 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Motor side boosts DC Cook A SUBSTANTIAL upturn in second half results of the motor division enabled DC Cook, the Rotherham-based motor retail and property group, to report pre-tax profits of Pounds 550,000 for the year to April 30, against Pounds 121,000. At the interim stage the USM-quoted company recorded a loss of Pounds 56,000. The pre-tax result was achieved after exceptional charges of Pounds 281,000 (Pounds 70,000) and lower interest costs of Pounds 1.48m (Pounds 2.09m). Turnover was Pounds 108m (Pounds 107.6m), including Pounds 1.38m (Pounds 14.1m) from discontinued activities. Fully diluted earnings were 2.02p (1.11p). After passing the interim dividend a final of 0.6p is recommended, against last year's total of 0.5p.
