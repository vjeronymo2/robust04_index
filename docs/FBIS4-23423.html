BFN [Text] The secretary general of the ANC, Mr. Cyril Ramaphosa, has announced that the ANC will abandon attempts to seek a court ruling on alleged election irregularities in KwaZulu-Natal. [Begin Ramaphosa recording, in English] This is being done to enhance the spirit of reconciliation, to make sure that all parties in Natal work together, accept the results as they are, and make sure that we truly have a government of provincial unity in this province. [end recording]
