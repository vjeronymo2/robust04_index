Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Two cars parked near the U.S. ambassador's residence in this capital caused concern among the police forces in charge of the security of U.S. Vice President Al Gore. The police bomb squad verified that both cases were false alarms. The bomb squad acted a little after 0830 today when the police considered suspicious the presence of a Renault 18 pickup on Libertador Avenue with a flat tire. After a thorough check the police verified it was only a false alarm. A police tow-truck removed the car from near the ambassador's residence. Soon afterward, the bomb squad returned to investigate a white Renault 12 parked on Libertador Avenue, 80 meters from the ambassador's residence and which had also called the attention of the police. After inspecting the car, the police verified that this was also a false alarm.
