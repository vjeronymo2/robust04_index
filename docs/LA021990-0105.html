February 19, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

PAIRINGS FOR OCC, FULLERTON, TOP-SEEDED GOLDEN WEST SET

Golden West is the top-seeded team in the women's Southern California community college basketball playoffs, it was announced Sunday.

Orange Coast and Fullerton join Orange Empire Conference champion Golden West in the 16-team field.

Golden West (32-1) plays host to 16th-seeded Antelope Valley (16-10) in the first round at 7:30 p.m. Wednesday. OCC (26-5), the sixth-seeded team, plays host to No. 11 Ventura (23-8) and No. 12 Fullerton (16-13) plays at fifth-seeded College of the Canyons (22-6).

The winners advance to the regional finals that will be played either Friday or Saturday.

The final four meet in the state tournament March 1-3 at College of the Sequoias in Visalia.

Pairings for the men's tournament will be announced on Tuesday. STEVE KRESAL
