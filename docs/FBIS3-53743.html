Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] The Faithful [Amal Movement] Resistance has issued this statement: The Martyrs Hamzah Nasr-al-Din and 'Ali al-Tarabulusi Group planted an explosive device on the Dayr Siryan-'Alman road at 1030 today; and it exploded under a joint patrol of the enemy and its agents. Two of the enemy died and several others were wounded. In the wake of the explosion the occupation forces bombarded Zawtar al-Sharqiyah, Zawtar al-Gharbiyah, Mazra'at al-Hamra', and the al-Litani river basin with 82-mm and 120-mm mortars.
