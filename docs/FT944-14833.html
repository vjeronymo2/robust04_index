941021 FT 21 OCT 94 / World Trade News: Samsung Electronics - Contracts and ventures Samsung Electronics, a unit of the South Korean Samsung Group, will invest Dollars 30m in a white goods factory in Thailand. The factory will have an annual capacity of 300,000 units, producing washing machines, air conditioners and refrigerators. Samsung Electronics already has a plant in Thailand making 500,000 colour TVs and 100,000 VCRs a year.
