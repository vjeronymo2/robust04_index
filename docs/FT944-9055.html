941117 FT 17 NOV 94 / Receivers cut jobs at private hospital Receivers at Health Care International, the private hospital at Clydebank near Glasgow, yesterday made 50 administrative staff redundant, including the chief executive and the executive director. The hospital, which went into receivership last week, is continuing to treat patients as the receivers seek a purchaser to take it over as a going concern. A number of expressions of interest have been received from potential purchasers.
