BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 28 (KCNA) -- Comrade Kim Chong-il, chairman of the National Defence Commission of the DPRK and supreme commander of the Korean People's Army, received a gift from H.E. Prince Chea Sim, acting head of state and president of the National Assembly, H.R.H. Prince Norodom Ranariddh, first prime minister, and H.E. Prince Hun Sen, second prime minister, and the suite from Cambodia. The gift was handed to an official concerned today.
