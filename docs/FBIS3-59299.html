Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tianjin, December 18 (XINHUA) -- The first customs post set up in one of China's bonded zones officially opened today in this north China port city. Among the country's officially-approved 14 bonded zones, the one in Tianjin was established in May 1991, and went into commercial operation a year later. So far, more than 1,600 foreign enterprises have registered at the customs office, which handled 17,000 declaration forms and goods valued at nearly 950 million U.S. dollars in its trial operation.
