Language: English Article Type:BFN [Following item transmitted via KYODO] [Text] Tajik Minister for Public Health Alamkhon Akhmedov told journalists that cholera had spread from the Pyanj district to the Moskovsky, Kolkhozabad and Kumsangir regions of the republic but its further spread had been curbed. Quarantine had been lifted in the Kumsangir district. Necessary steps are being taken in the capital Dushanbe. The laboratories of the countries epidemics service are working round the clock, the chlorination of potable water is under strict control. Thanks to the humanitarian aid there is no shortage of medicines.
