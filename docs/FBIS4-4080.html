BFN [Summary from poor reception] Ruan Chongwu expressed a welcome to Philippine banker (Chen Yung-cai) for his coming to Hainan on a fact-finding mission. Ruan briefed his guest on Hainan's investment environment and preferential policies. Both sides went on to express their intentions to develop projects in Hainan. Also present at the meeting were Mao Zhijun, director of the provincial economic cooperation department, as well as the director of the Hainan Provincial Civil Aviation Company. (Chen Yung-cai) and his party came to Hainan to study and discuss investment projects at the invitation of the Hainan Provincial Civil Aviation Company. On 18 April, Ruan Chongwu, secretary of the Hainan Provincial CPC Committee and governor, met Kwok Chih-yi, general manager and member of the Board of Directors of Hong Kong's Wing On Department Store, and his party. The department store is an old establishment, with a long history in Hong Kong's general merchandise business. At the meeting, Governor Ruan Chongwu welcomed the department store's getting involved in Hainan's general retail merchandise business, and he briefed the party on Hainan's investment environment.
