931105 FT 05 NOV 93 / World News in Brief: Court decides on Sevso silver A New York jury ruled that the Sevso collection of Roman silver, valued at up to Pounds 66m, belonged to the Marquess of Northampton and not to Croatia or Hungary, which disputed ownership. Lord Northampton bought the silver for about Dollars 14m (Pounds 9.2m) from a Lebanese seller.
