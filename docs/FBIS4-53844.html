BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 23 (KCNA) -- Foreign party and state leaders expressed full support and solidarity for the Korean people's cause of national reunification when they recently met the DPRK ambassadors to their countries. Secretary General of the Central Committee of the Algerian National Liberation Front Abdel Hamid Mehri said his party would always stand firmly on the side of the Korean people and actively support the Workers' Party and people of Korea in their struggle to repulse the imperialists' pressure and interference. Micheslav Ivanovich Griv, chairman of the Supreme Soviet of Belarus, said the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula must be settled in a peaceful way and by means of negotiations. "This is the invariable stand of the Supreme Soviet and Government of Belarus," he declared. He supported the Korean people's cause of national reunification. Chairman of the People's Front of Workers, Peasants and Students of Peru Genaro Ledesma Izquieta said no moves of the Japanese reactionaries could frighten the Korean people guided by the great leader Comrade President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il. "We will extend invariable support and solidarity to the cause of socialism of the Workers' Party of Korea and the cause of reunification of the Korean people," he stressed.
