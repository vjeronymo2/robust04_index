October 10, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

CHINA;

FUGITIVE EMBEZZLER BLAMED IN HIJACKING

Officials said a fugitive embezzler hijacked a jetliner and caused its crash last week in Canton, killing 128.

Canton's aviation bureau and public security bureau identified the hijacker as Jiang Xiaofeng, 21, a purchasing agent for a county government office in Changsha. It said he was wanted by Chinese police for embezzling $3,600 in July. Jiang boarded a Boeing 737 in Xiamen and demanded to be taken to Taiwan, the officials said. Low on fuel, the plane landed instead in Canton, striking two planes on the ground.
