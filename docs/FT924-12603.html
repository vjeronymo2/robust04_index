921020 FT 20 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Tharsis boosted by rentals income In the first half of 1992 Tharsis made a pre-tax profit of Pounds 150,000, compared with Pounds 16,000. Most of the income came from rentals of offices and land. The interim dividend is cut from 3p to 2p, costing Pounds 52,000 (Pounds 78,000). That followed omission of the previous final.
