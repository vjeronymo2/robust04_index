940520 FT 20 MAY 94 / International Company News: HK Telecom improves 18 per cent Hongkong Telecommunications yesterday exceeded analysts' expectations with an 18 per cent rise in 1994 net earnings to HKDollars 7.5bn (USDollars 974m), in 1993. Turnover was up 12 per cent to HKDollars 24.3bn, while operating costs were held to a 9 per cent rise. The total dividend is to be HK cents 51 a share - up 18 per cent on 1992. International telephone services - which account for 63 per cent of the company's turnover - grew by 12 per cent. China business grew by 31 per cent, less than 1993's growth of 35 per cent, reflecting a cooling of economic growth on the mainland.
