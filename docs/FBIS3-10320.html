Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Panama City ACAN in Spanish at 1715 GMT on 4 March reported that the consumer price index grew by 1.2 percent in February. The accumulated 1994 inflation rate is 2.07 percent. The Economy Ministry has reported registration fees at private and state universities increased by 22 percent, private medical fees by 17 percent, bread by 10 percent, and electricity rates by 4 percent. Economy Minister Roberto Rojas has said this price increase is in keeping with the goal of a 10-percent annual inflation rate.
