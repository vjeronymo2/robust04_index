Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] The Bosnian-Serb military leadership has announced that it will take foreigners in Bosnia hostage in case NATO attacks Serb artillery round Sarajevo. The Serbs would also attack all Bosnian enclaves where UN troops are stationed, Serb General Milovanovic said in an interview with a Belgrade newspaper. This would be a kind of international massacre, the general said.
