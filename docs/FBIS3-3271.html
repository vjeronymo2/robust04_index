Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Tianjin Municipal Federation of Returned Students which aims at uniting students who studied abroad, extensively connecting domestic and overseas students, colleagues, relatives, and friends, strengthening friendship, exchanging learning, providing information and service for Tianjin to attract students studying abroad, and promoting the development of Tianjin's economic construction, science and technology, education and public health, culture and sports, and other social undertakings, was established yesterday. Gao Dezhan, secretary of the municipal party committee, attended and addressed the inaugural meeting. Li Jianguo, deputy secretary of the municipal party committee, gave a speech on behalf of the municipal party committee and government. Municipal leading persons, including Wang Hongjiang, Wang Chenghuai, Qian Qiao, Yu Guocong, and Wang Jitao, attended the inaugural meeting. Prior to the inaugural meeting, the Tianjin Municipal Federation of Returned Students held a meeting of the board of directors to elect Yu Guocong, vice chairman of the municipal committee of the Chinese People's Political Consultative Conference and member of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, as chairman of the federation. It also elected the hnorary chairman, advisers, vice chairmen, and secretary general. This federation is an organization to unite Tianjin's returned students from all circles. [passage omitted] In his speech, Gao Dezhan said: Returning students have made great contributions to Tianjin's reform, opening up, and economic construction with great patriotic zeal, intelligence, and wisdom. They are worthy of emulation. He ardently hoped that the broad masses of returned students will give further play to their own distinctive advantages, extensively contact and attract more overseas students, colleagues, relatives, and friends to return to China and Tianjin to work, and to introduce trained personnel, funds, and information to promote Tianjin's modernization. He said: The broad masses of intellectuals, including the returned students, and all sectors across the municipality will surely be able to invigorate Tianjin as long as they rally around the party Central Committee with Comrade Jiang Zemin as the nucleus, act closely in connection with the party's overall situation of "seizing the opportunity, deepening reform, expanding opening up, promoting development, and maintaining stability," work with one heart and one mind, struggle in unity, and immerse in hard work. The municipal party committee and the government will continue to create an external environment in which returned students can display their intelligence and wisdom and make contributions, will continue to earnestly ask for advice from them, and make joint efforts to manage Tianjin's work well. In his speech, Li Jianguo extended warm congratulations to the federation of returned students on its establishment on behalf of the municipal party committee and government. He said: Since the founding of China nearly 45 years ago, batches of students studying abroad have returned to the motherland and Tianjin to participate in the development and played a very important role in Tianjin's economy, science and technology, education, public health, and various undertakings. Their pure heart of cherishing and serving the motherland and their lofty spirit of dedicating themselves to science and invigorating China have been praised by the party and the people. Li Jianguo said: Tianjin has several hundred thousand specialized technical personnel and a group of specialists and scholars who have great influence at home and abroad. This backbone contingent is Tianjin's precious wealth and great advantage and a symbol of Tianjin's status. We must grasp the limited time before the end of this century, step up efforts to train a new generation of academic and technical leaders and a batch of young top personnel of domestic first-grade level and international advanced level, and enable Tianjin to continuously maintain the advantage of having trained personnel at home by the early period of the next century. Li Jianguo said in conclusion: To push the economy forward, we must rely on science, technology, and education and give full play to the enthusiasm and creativity of the broad masses of intellectuals. We must do a good job in handling work regarding returned students. It is hoped that the establishment of the Tianjin Municipal Federation of Returned Students will serve as a bridge for establishing ties and strengthening friendship with the broad masses of returned students, exchanging learning, and promoting Tianjin's economic and social development.
