921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Group Development asset value improves Group Development Capital Trust had a net asset value of 41.1p per share at September 30, against 39.4p at the interim stage and 39.5p a year earlier. Net revenue for the year amounted to Pounds 118,485 (Pounds 101,618) for earnings per share ahead to 0.34p (0.23p). The single final dividend is lifted to 0.3p (0.24p).
