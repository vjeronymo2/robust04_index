Language: Tagalog Article Type:BFN [Text] The officials of the Autonomous Region for Muslim Mindanao [ARMM] will not be dismissed from their posts in connection with the ongoing peace talks. This assurance was given by Nur Misuari, chairman of the Moro National Liberation Front [MNLF], to Senator Santanina Rasul, chair of Senate Committee on Mindanao Affairs. Misuari said that the MNLF will respect the terms of office of the elected ARMM officials that will end in 1996. He explained that this will not be affected by his proposal for the establishment of a provisional government in Muslim Mindanao. Misuari also expressed hope for the success of the peace talks.
