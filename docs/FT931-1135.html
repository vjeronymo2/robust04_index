930326 FT 26 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Cornhill Insurance Cornhill Insurance, the UK offshoot of Allianz, returned to the black in 1992 reflecting reduced underwriting losses. Pre-tax profits amounted to Pounds 3.1m, against an 'unprecedented' loss last time of Pounds 39.5m. The solvency ratio, on a market value basis, rose to 46.1 per cent (39.2 per cent). Premium income from general business jumped to Pounds 619.9m (Pounds 595.2m) with underwriting losses down from Pounds 102.1m to Pounds 66.2m. Overall, general business recorded profits Pounds 200,000 against losses of Pounds 37.9m.
