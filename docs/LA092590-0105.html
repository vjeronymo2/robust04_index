September 25, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: IRVINE;

NEW LAW EXPECTED TO HELP COMBAT FRAUD

A new weapon to combat fraudulent telephone-sales and investment schemes may be coming to the city.

The City Council tonight is expected to pass a law requiring anyone who wants to operate an investment or telephone-sales company in Irvine to fill out an extra page of information about their business and themselves to receive a business license.

"The form will give us more information on these companies, and it will make them jump through the hoops, so to speak, so we can determine if they are legitimate," Police Sgt. David Freedland said.
