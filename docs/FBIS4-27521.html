BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 2 (KCNA) -- His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk of Cambodia who had come to preside over a roundtable conference for peace and national reconciliation of Cambodia left here today by special plane. Leaving were also Her Majesty the queen and entourage of the king. They were seen off at the airport by Vice-president Yi Chong-ok and officials concerned. Officials of the Royal Embassy of Cambodia here and Qiao Zonghuai, Chinese Ambassador E.P. to Korea, were present at the airport.
