940912 FT 12 SEP 94 / EU Ministers back Paye EU ministers lined up behind Mr Jean-Claude Paye to stay on as secretary-general of the Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development writes Lionel Barber. But Washington has already signalled that it does not want Mr Paye, a Frenchman, to serve another term. It is standing by Mr Donald Johnston, a former Canadian minister. EU support for Mr Paye is linked to a feeling that France has lost out in recent nominations for top international jobs.
