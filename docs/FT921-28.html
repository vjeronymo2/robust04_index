920331 FT 31 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Sentry Farming advances to Pounds 0.27m Sentry Farming Group, formerly Associated Farmers, which moved up from the Third Market to the USM following its merger with Sentry Farm Management, returned to the black in the 1991 year. On turnover increased to Pounds 4.4m (Pounds 3.66m), the pre-tax result swung from losses of Pounds 319,000 to profits of Pounds 267,000. Net interest payable declined to Pounds 215,000 (Pounds 259,000). Retained profits emerged at Pounds 149,000 (losses Pounds 446,000). A dividend of 1p (nil) is recommended. Earnings were 5.1p (losses 10.1p) per share.
