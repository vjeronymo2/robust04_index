Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Harebrained Act"--KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, March 25 (KCNA) -- MINJU CHOSON today brands Japan as a crime-filled country which has neither qualification nor face to become a permanent member of the United Nations Security Council. The news analyst says: Whenever an opportunity presents itself, high-ranking Japanese officials do not forget to vociferate about "international contributions" of Japan, revealing their intention to make Japan a permanent member of the UN Security Council. The UN Security Council might be polluted with dirt if such a country as Japan with a history of abominable crimes becomes a permanent member of the security council. Japan has jumped to the second topmost rung of the world's ladder in military expenditures and is proceeding with the modernisation of navy and air force of the "Self-Defense Forces" [SDF] and legalization of overseas military activities of the "SDF". It must not be overlooked that the present Japanese authorities are trying to lay obstacles to a solution to the nuclear issue on the Korean peninsula by crying over the "nuclear issue" of the North and make it a pretext for Japan's nuclear armament. It is the height of sarcasm for Japan to attempt to become a permanent member of the UN Security Council the basic mission of which is to ensure world peace and security. If Japan wants to win international confidence, it must clearly liquidate its past crimes and stop its moves to become a military power and arm itself with nuclear weapons.
