Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, and 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Presidential Council, have blessed and approved the Document of Pledge and Accord. They gave the committee full authority to set a date and venue for the final endorsement of the document. This took place when the brother chairman and vice chairman received the committee. This was stated by brother Ahmad Salim 'Afif, the rapporteur of the committee entrusted by the Political Forces Dialogue Committee to follow up the implementation of the seventh paragraph of the Document of Pledge and Accord. Brother Ahmad Jabir 'Afif added that the committee will hold its next meeting tomorrow morning to draft a program. Then it will show it to the brother chairman and vice chairman.
