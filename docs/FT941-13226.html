940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / World Stock Markets: Mexico Mexican stocks reacted nervously to the decline of Telmex, the telephone utility, in New York on news that Grupo Financiero Banacci and MCI Communications had agreed to form a company which might offer long-distance services in Mexico when the Telmex monopoly is broken up in late 1996. The IPC index was off 33.81, or 1.26 per cent, at 2,659.90 at the close. Telmex ADRs declined Dollars 1 to Dollars 68 3/4.
