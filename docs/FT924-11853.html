921023 FT 23 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Sharp cut at Ferry Pickering FERRY PICKERING, the Leicestershire-based packaging and printing group, announced a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 2.26m to Pounds 674,000 in the year to August 31. The result was struck after exceptional costs of Pounds 783,000 (Pounds 52,000) relating to redundancy and restructuring and bad debts. Turnover amounted to Pounds 27.8m (Pounds 29.1m). Earnings per share came through at 4.41p (12.68p) and a second interim dividend of 3.4p is declared, making 5.5p (same). The company expects that a final dividend in respect of the 16-month period to December will be recommended.
