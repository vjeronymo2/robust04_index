Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tengiz Pachkoria] [Text] Tbilisi January 7 TASS -- "So far we have not received any concrete facts confirming or denying reports of Gamsakhurdia's death and burial in one of the districts of western Georgia", Head of the Zugdidi District Administration Edisher Chargazia told ITAR- TASS on Friday. Chargazia confirmed that an investigation is underway in the Zugdidi district and outlying areas. The investigation is being directed by the republic's Deputy Procurator-General Anzor Baluashvili. Deputy Chairman of the Provisional Committee for Emergencies in Western Georgia Vakhtang Kvaratskhelia told ITAR-TASS that the investigative committee left Zugdidi today for the neighboring Tsalendzhikhi district, where it will visit two villages, Nakipu and Dzhagira. It was in this district that the rumor started of Gamsakhurdia's death and burial in one of the above-mentioned villages. Kvaratskheliya himself proposes that "Zviad Gamsakhurdia's death did not occur in one of the districts of western Georgia, but beyond the republic's borders".
