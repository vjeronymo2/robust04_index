November 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

WAITING FOR THE QUAKE;

AREA BUILDINGS NEEDING UPGRADE

There are more than 13,000 unreinforced masonry buildings in Los Angeles and Ventura counties. And possibly thousands more have portions constructed of unreinforced masonry. Here are some notable buildings that according to county and local officials contain unreinforced masonry and have not been upgraded.
