[ER&hyph;FRL&hyph;4717&hyph;7] Environmental Impact Statements and Regulations; Availability of EPA Comments Availability of EPA comments prepared November 7, 1994 through November 11, 1994 pursuant to the Environmental Review Process (ERP), under Section 309 of the Clean Air Act and Section 102(2)(C) of the National Environmental Policy Act as amended. Requests for copies of EPA comments can be directed to the Office of Federal Activities at (202) 260&hyph;5076. An explanation of the ratings assigned to draft environmental impact statements (EISs) was published in FR dated April 8, 1994 (59 FR 16807).
