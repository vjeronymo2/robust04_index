930202 FT 02 FEB 93 / Parliament and Politics: Cabinet committee details disclosed THE prime minister last night announced the membership and terms of reference of a further two cabinet committees as part of his commitment to more open government. The disclosure of cabinet committees on public spending and on refugees from the former Yugoslavia follows publication of a list of ministerial committees last May. The public expenditure committee, known as EDX, is chaired by the chancellor. The committee on Yugoslavian refugees, known in Whitehall as GEN24, is chaired by the home secretary.
