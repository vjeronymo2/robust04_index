930428 FT 28 APR 93 / UK Company News: Bournemouth and West Hants Water Bournemouth Water and West Hampshire Water, both owned by Biwater, the water engineering group, reported increased profits for 1992. Bournemouth raised pre-tax profits 39 per cent to Pounds 4.75m (Pounds 3.42m) on turnover of Pounds 12.1m (Pounds 10.5m). Fully diluted earnings per share were 320p (250p); the dividend for the year is 86p. Pre-tax profits at West Hampshire edged up to Pounds 1.29m (Pounds 1.22m) on turnover of Pounds 8.8m (Pounds 7.9m). Earnings per share came to 152p (149p) fully diluted and a 36p dividend is proposed.
