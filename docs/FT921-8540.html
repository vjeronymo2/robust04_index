920217 FT 17 FEB 92 / Survey of Portable Computers (18): Design for new Tokyo network Intelligent Networks, the London-based computer systems integrator, is using Amstrad colour laptops for its design and implementation of the new Tokyo Sugar Exchange network. Peter Drake, chairman of Intelligent Networks, pictured above, chose the Amstrads for their high resolution colour screen and the ability to take a wide variety of network design cards.
