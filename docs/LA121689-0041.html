December 16, 1989, Saturday, Southland Edition

HEARING SET IN USAIR JETLINER CRASH IN N.Y.

The National Transportation Safety Board said Friday that it will conduct a hearing focusing on Boeing 737 rudder trim design and cockpit performance in the crash of a USAir jetliner at New York's La Guardia Airport.

The four-day hearing is to begin Feb. 13 at the Penta Hotel in New York.
