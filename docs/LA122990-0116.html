December 29, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Canada Sells Second Nuclear Plant to S. Korea: Canada has sold a second nuclear generating station to South Korea, ending a nine-year sales drought at Atomic Energy of Canada Ltd. The Candu station will be built next to an existing reactor at Wolsong on South Korea's southeast coast, Energy Minister Jake Epp said. The project is expected to provide 7,000 jobs.
