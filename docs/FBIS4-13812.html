BFN [Text] Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin left for an official four-day visit to Moscow several minutes ago. Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres, who together with the rest of the cabinet ministers accompanied Rabin to his flight, said that very intensive negotiations with the Palestinians were expected this week. If everything went well, Peres said, the negotiations may be concluded this week. If this does not turn out to be the case, the foreign minister added, we will have to find ways to accelerate the talks. This was reported by our correspondent Yariv Ben-Yehuda from Ben-Gurion airport.
