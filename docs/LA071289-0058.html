July 12, 1989, Wednesday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"He just ate and ate and ate, and when he was done he said, 'You wanna go to bed? You wanna go to bed?' He was so tired. I'm very happy now."

-- Magda Wilson, 80, speaking about the return of her best friend, Baby, a yellow-naped Amazon parrot she had for nearly 40 years before it was apparently stolen last week outside her Santa Monica apartment.
