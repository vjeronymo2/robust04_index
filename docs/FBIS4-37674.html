BFN [Text] The security forces today announced that they had killed 23 terrorists on 20 June at a place called Tagarmout, situated between Tazoult and Ain Touta in the province of Batna. The statement said that this operation was carried out by the security forces following information supplied by one of the members who had surrendered. During the operation, 18 weapons were recovered. In addition, the security forces reported that they had killed three other terrorists on the same day, during an operation at a place called Haouche el Ghir in the province of Oran. The security forces also announced that they had arrested 13 people charged with carrying out several assassinations in the provinces of Algiers and Boumerdes, including the killers of two lawyers--Grine Laid and Belghoul Saad--at Haouche el Makhfi in Reghaia.
