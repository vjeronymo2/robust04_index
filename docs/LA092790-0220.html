September 27, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: NOTES

National Education Corp. said Wednesday that its Steck-Vaughn subsidiary was chosen as the exclusive distributor of official practice tests for the GED or high school equivalency exam.

The Austin, Tex.-based subsidiary will publish and sell the tests for the American Council on Education until Aug. 31, 1993. Terms of the agreement were not disclosed. NEC is an Irvine-based educational products and training company.
