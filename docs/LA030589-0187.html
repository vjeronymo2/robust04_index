March 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

'INDOOR AIR POLLUTION' TO BE DISCUSSED

The sixth annual Lungs and the Environment Conference, "Indoor Air Pollution," will be held April 1 from 8 a.m. to 4:30 p.m.. under the sponsorship of the American Lung Assn. of Los Angeles County.

The one-day, continuing education conference at the University Hilton, 3540 S. Figueroa St., is aimed at medical professionals, real estate agents, architects and health and safety professionals. Conference topics include: Where to go for help, symptoms, chemicals as indoor air pollutants, radon, pesticides and fungal contaminants and polices and legislation.

The fee is $45 advance or $50 at the door (students, $25 and $30). Information: Marianna Buntich 213/935-5864 or 818/797-5864, Ext. 237.
