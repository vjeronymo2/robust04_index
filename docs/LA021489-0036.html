February 14, 1989, Tuesday, Valley Edition

VALLEY COLLEGE ROUNDUP: VICTORY GIVES VALLEY WOMEN SHARE OF CONFERENCE CROWN

Valley College took a 23-point first half lead and breezed to an 85-55 victory over Canyons on Monday to clinch at least a share of the Western State Conference title.

Sandrine Rocher and Ruth Aguilar each scored 18 points for Valley (25-3, 10-1 in conference play), which led, 40-17, at halftime. Valley holds a two-game lead over second-place Santa Monica with two conference games remaining.

BASEBALL

Glendale 7, Mission 3 -- Catcher Robert Bradford, who delivered a two-run single in the seventh inning, was the lone bright spot for Mission (0-3) in a nonconference game at Glendale.

MEN'S GOLF

Led by Tom Hogarth's two-round total of 157, Cal State Northridge is in second place with 18 holes to play in the 54-hole UC Davis Invitational at Silverado Country Club in Napa, Calif.

Hogarth had rounds of 79 and 78 on Monday, one shot better than teammate Roger Metz (80-78 -- 158). Hogarth trails Joe McCormick and Mark Hutchinson of Cal State Stanislaus, both of whom have two-round totals of 151.
