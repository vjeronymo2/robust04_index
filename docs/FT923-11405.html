920724 FT 24 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Losses deepen to Pounds 0.45m at Elbief Elbief, the maker of photograph and handbag frames, clocks and mirrors, fell deeper into the red in the year to April 30 and reported a pre-tax loss of Pounds 451,000, against Pounds 29,000. The difficult trading conditions of the first half had worsened, directors said, and they had introduced 'more drastic cost cutting measures'. Turnover was static at Pounds 4.44m. Losses per share amounted to 3p (0.19p). There is no dividend (0.5p).
