March 18, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

UPROAR OVER TWO FLAG EXHIBITS

The response of offended Muslims to the book "The Satanic Verses" is not unlike the response of Christians to the movie "The Last Temptation of Christ." The difference is in degree: violence or peaceful demonstration. But the basic issue of each is the violation of conscience and the fear of misinterpretation of truth in their religion.

Now, consciences are being violated as the American flag is walked on in the name of art. The consciences of citizens hurt as the symbol of unity is defiled for the artist's individual freedom.

Is there no end to the discounting of others' beliefs? To be effective, freedom always has to have limits (such as obedience to the law); but a far greater limit is that of not doing anything that would hurt my brother.

MARY SMITH

Canyon Country
