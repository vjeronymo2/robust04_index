July 21, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Marine National Bank: The Santa Ana bank reported second-quarter net income of $184,156, up slightly from $183,334 in the year-earlier period.

Revenue rose 5% to $2.2 million from $2.1 million. For the six-month period, net income declined 14% to $323,418 from $376,058. The bank attributed the decline to higher income and franchise taxes. Total assets at the end of June totaled $90.8 million, up 6% from the $85.4 million reported at the same time last year.
