930209 FT 09 FEB 93 / International Company News: Anheuser-Busch net income slips ANHEUSER-BUSCH, the largest US brewer, lifted fourth-quarter net income to Dollars 161.6m or 58 cents a share from Dollars 159.2m or 55 cents on sales of Dollars 2.73bn against Dollars 2.67bn, agencies report from St Louis. Full-year income, however, slipped to Dollars 917.5m or Dollars 3.22 a share from Dollars 939.8m or Dollars 3.26 after a Dollars 76.7m charge for changes in accounting practices.
