Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Vesti" newscast] [Text] Intersept agency reports that members of the Russian State Duma have unanimously spoken against the president's forthcoming trip to Georgia. They believe that it is the wrong moment in time to pay attention to this unstable Transcaucasian state. Experts believe that signing a treaty between Russia and Georgia in the situation when problems of the North Caucasus have not been resolved may provoke a conflict between the executive and the lawmakers in Russia. Most deputies of the lower chamber will vote down the ratification of such a treaty.
