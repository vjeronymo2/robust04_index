940621 FT 21 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Towles advises no action on offer Mr Bill Moss, chairman of Towles, the lossmaking clothing maker, said there was nothing new in the offer document from London City, the Australian investment group which has made a Pounds 4.22m cash bid for the company. London City would have to offer an adequate price, he said, and advised shareholders to take no action. He also announced the sale of the Derby Road factory and premises, in Loughborough, Leicestershire, for Pounds 550,000.
