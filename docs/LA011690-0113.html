January 16, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

FOR THE RECORD

Business Week Case: The last paragraph of a story in Wednesday's Business Section inadvertently substituted the last name of a Securities and Exchange Commission lawyer for the name of one of the defendants in an insider trading suit stemming from leaks of advance copies of Business Week magazine. As stated elsewhere in the article, the defendants in the suit, filed last Tuesday, are Brian J. Callahan and William N. Jackson.
