July 6, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

GATT Meeting Postponed: A GATT meeting to consider a bid by China to join the 96-nation trade forum has been postponed "because of difficulties in having meaningful discussions in present circumstances," a GATT spokesman said. A special working party that has been studying China's application since its submission three years ago was scheduled to meet next week to discuss the terms of a draft document for China's possible accession to the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade. The meeting was put off with China's agreement, and a date for the meeting will be announced later.
