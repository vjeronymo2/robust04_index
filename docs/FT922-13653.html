920410 FT 10 APR 92 / UK Company News: Norish achieves strong recovery Norish, the Irish-based food services group, reported a recovery in the 1991 year with pre-tax profits up from IPounds 1.47m to IPounds 2.2m (Pounds 2.06m), equal to profit earned for 1989. The disposal of the trading assets of the distribution business during 1990 meant a stantial reduction in turnover - down from IPounds 21.1m to IPounds 13.6m - and in working capital. Earnings per share rose from 12.3p to 17.14p and the final dividend is maintained at 7p to hold the total at 11.47p.
