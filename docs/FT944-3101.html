941215 FT 15 DEC 94 / Soros pledges Dollars 55m to S Africa Mr George Soros, the financier, yesterday pledged Dollars 5m (Pounds 3.2m) start-up capital and a guarantee facility for a further Dollars 50m to provide housing for the poor in South Africa. With matching funds from the government, his Soros Foundations are to establish a National Urban Reconstruction and Housing Agency (Nurcha) which 'will promote delivery in the short term and will take financial risks which can unlock private capital.'
