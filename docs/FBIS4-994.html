BFN [Text] Political parties have welcomed the IFP [Inkatha Freedom Party] decision to take part in the elections. The PAC [Pan-Africanist Congress] said it had believed from the beginning that the process should be all-inclusive. The Democratic Party expressed the hope that the decision would result in a reduction in the levels of violence. The Freedom Front said that it had the hope that there was now a move toward accommodating those who wanted self-determination.
