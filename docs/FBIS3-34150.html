Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The union minister of state for home, Mr. Rajesh Pilot, has urged the security forces along the country's border with Bangladesh to be more alert. Addressing the personnel of the Assam Rifles in Shillong, he said insurgent activities of the region are on the increase during the past few months. Later, he met the Meghalaya chief minister, Mr. S.C. Marak, and senior state government officials. Mr. Pilot, who is now on a visit to the northeastern region, told our correspondent that he will hold discussions with the state governments on matters relating to security and law and order. He said Pakistan is trying to make inroads in the region by aiding and abetting some insurgent groups through Bangladesh.
