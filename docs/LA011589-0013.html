January 15, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CRITICS' CHOICE: WHAT MAD PURSUIT: A PERSONAL VIEW OF SCIENTIFIC DISCOVERY BY FRANCIS CRICK (BASIC BOOKS: $16.95; 182 PP.)

"What Mad Pursuit" is "Crick's version of the crucial events" in the discovery of DNA. "An elegant exposition that concentrates almost entirely on ideas. It is about how science is done." Lee Dembart
