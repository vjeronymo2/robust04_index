Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, January 15 (ATA) -- The commission of socialist intelligentsia sponsored a meeting January 14 at which reporters from the Radio-TV, the ALBANIAN TELEGRAPHIC AGENCY [ATA] and other representatives of the mass media exchanged opinions on professional and ethical aspects of journalism. The discussion aimed at avoiding banalities and politicization in the media. Ilir Zhilla, director of ATA, stressed that ATA is a great national asset for the mass media and called on all press organs to use information available from ATA (some 3,000 items of world news a day) and information provided by ATA's district correspondents. Mr. Zhilla asked his colleagues to respect each other and the organizations for which they work. He said that reporters should avoid hostile polemics and declared that the Albanian news media should maintain constructive policies in order to encourage the people and the political leadership in overcoming the difficulties of the transition period. Mr. Zhilla also sternly criticized irresponsibility and damage to the national interest on the part of the press.
