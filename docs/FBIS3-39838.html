Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Text] President Glavkos Kliridhis has warned that if [Turkish Cypriot leader] Rauf Denktas uses delaying tactics in the talks on the confidence-building measures claiming political instability in Turkey, he will find himself against the UN Security Council and the secretary general. In a statement following successive meetings with leaders or representatives of the political parties represented in the House of Representatives, President Kliridhis also noted that the United Nations has submitted no new proposals on ways to implement the confidence-building measures. He added that at the end of February, the secretary general will submit a preliminary report on the results of the proximity talks and a new report at the end of March.
