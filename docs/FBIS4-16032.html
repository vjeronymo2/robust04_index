BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Pavel Kuznetsowv] [Text] Moscow April 12 TASS -- "The central executive committee of the Russian Communist Party strongly protests against NATO aggressive actions in Bosnia," says a statement of the communist party's central executive committee circulated here today. The document calls on factions of the State Duma and the Federation Council to denounce the policy of the United States and NATO in Bosnia, which does not help to achieve peace in the region. It says that peace in former Yugoslavia can be achieved with exclusively political means.
