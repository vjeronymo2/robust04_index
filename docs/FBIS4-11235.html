BFN [Text] Asuncion, 23 Apr (EFE) -- The National Police reported that a Paraguayan department deputy belonging to the ruling National Republican Association (Colorado Party) was shot to death today, Saturday. The incident took place in Ciudad del Este, 320 km from Asuncion. The victim was identified as Julio Cesar Riquelme, national deputy for Alto Parana Department, who died of several gunshot wounds. Two other persons were wounded in the incident and were taken to a hospital in Ciudad del Este, capital of Alto Parana Department on the Brazilian border. Police arrested Tomas Lezcano, 39 years old, as the alleged killer but gave no further details on the case.
