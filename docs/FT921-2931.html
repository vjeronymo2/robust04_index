920317 FT 17 MAR 92 / EC, US air subsidy talks resume The European Community and the US were locked in crucial talks yesterday to solve a five-year dispute over aircraft subsidies, officials said, Reuters reports from Brussels. The two days of talks could determine whether Washington lodges a long-threatened complaint with the General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (Gatt) over EC government support to Airbus Industrie. Washington has accused the consortium of using subsidies to undercut US manufacturers Boeing and McDonnell Douglas.
