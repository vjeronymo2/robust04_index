Language: Tajik Article Type:BFN [Text] A regular session of the presidium of the government of the Republic was held yesterday under the chairmanship of Abdujalil Samadov, chairman of the Council of Ministers. The draft laws of the Republic of Tajikistan on refugees, on forced migrants and those people who have suffered damages as a result of the armed clashes were discussed and approved at the meeting. The above mentioned draft laws will be submitted to the Supreme Soviet of the Republic. Similarly, the issues related to provision of the population and the medical establishments of the Republic with medicines, development of pharmaceutical industry for the period of 1994-1996, reducing business trips expenditures and others were discussed at the meeting. The specific decisions were adopted on these issues.
