BFN [Excerpts] [Speaker Oleksandr Moroz] The floor is given to the chairman of the counting commission, Mykhaylo Dmytrovych Syrota. [Syrota] Esteemed people's deputies! Allow me to announce the protocol of the counting commission. This is protocol number eight of the Supreme Council of Ukraine counting commission of 16 June 1994. [passage omitted] On the results of the secret ballot on the appointment of prime minister of Ukraine -- candidate Vitaliy Andriyovych Masol was entered on the ballot-paper on the appointment of Ukraine's prime minister. On the basis of the results of the secret ballot, the counting commission has established: The overall number of people's deputies of Ukraine elected to date: 337. The number of people's deputies whose powers were recognized by the Supreme Council of Ukraine: 335, as you know, Kudyukin and Hrynyov have not been confirmed. The number of people's deputies of Ukraine who received ballot-papers for the secret ballot: 302. The number of people's deputies of Ukraine who took part in the vote: 224. The number of votes cast in favor of the candidate: 199. [applause] The number of votes cast against the candidate: 24. The number of ballot papers recognized as invalid: one. [passage omitted] In accordance with Article 97 of the Constitution of Ukraine and clause 22.6 of the Temporary Procedures of Supreme Council Sittings, the counting commission has ruled to recognize Vitaliy Andriyovych Masol as being confirmed for the post of prime minister of Ukraine. [applause]
