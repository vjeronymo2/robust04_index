DEPARTMENT OF ENERGY Federal Energy Regulatory Commission 18 CFR Part 284 [Docket No. RM93&hyph;4&hyph;002] Standards for Electronic Bulletin Boards Required Under Part 284 of the Commission's Regulations March 30, 1994. AGENCY: Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, Energy. ACTION: Notice of filings and opportunity to file comments.

SUMMARY: The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission has received a filing from Working Group 5, on March 24, 1994, submitting a consensus proposal for company common codes in capacity release data sets and from Working Group 3, on March 25, 1994, submitting a consensus agreement on the implementation of standard standards for non-capacity release business transactions. The Commission is affording interested persons an opportunity to file comments on these filings. DATES: Comments due by April 6, 1994.
ADDRESSES: Comments should be filed at: Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, 825 North Capitol Street, NE., Washington, DC 20426.
