Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Harare, 18 Mar (MENA)--An official Egyptian source stated that Egypt continues its contacts with the United States on the adoption of a Security Council resolution condemning "the al-Ibrahimi Mosque massacre" on 15 Ramadan, with the aim of resuming the peace negotiations between the Israelis and Palestinians, and on the other tracks. In a statement to MENA in Harare today, the source said the issuance of such a resolution by the Security Council would reassure the PLO about the protection and security of the Palestinian people in the occupied Arab territories. He noted that a U.S. veto of such a resolution would be an obstacle to the peace process.
