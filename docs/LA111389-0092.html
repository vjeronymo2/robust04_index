November 13, 1989, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: LOS ALAMITOS;

APPLICANTS SOUGHT FOR FINE ARTS PANEL

The City Council is seeking applications from residents interested in serving on the Fine Arts and Cultural Affairs Commission.

The seven-member commission meets the third Wednesday of every month at 7 p.m. in the council chambers and plans annual events that promote the fine arts.

Applicants should send letters to the City Clerk, P.O. Box 3147, Los Alamitos, Calif. 90720.
