Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 2, IRNA -- The Islamic Republic of Iran welcomes talks between Pakistan and India, a Foreign Ministry spokesman said here today. He said the talks being held in Islamabad, can help remove the existing tension in the Islamabad-New Delhi ties and lead to restoration of peace and stability in the region. He also expressed hope that the talks on mutual ties between the two countries as well as on the Kashmir issue would have "fruitful" results.
