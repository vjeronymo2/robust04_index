931209 FT 09 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Sterling Publishing 26% up at Pounds 1.3m Profits before tax of Sterling Publishing Group, the USM-traded publisher of advertising-financed journals, rose to Pounds 1.31m for the half year ended September 30. That was an improvement of 26 per cent over last time's Pounds 1.04m and came from turnover ahead 16 per cent at Pounds 17.1m. Interest charges were cut to Pounds 561,000 (Pounds 654,000). The group's publishing cycle remains concentrated in the second half and the directors said first half profits should not be taken as a guide to the full year results. As an indication of their expectations for the full 12 months they are lifting the interim dividend from 0.6p to 0.7p. Fully diluted earnings per share amounted to 1.9p (1.5p).
