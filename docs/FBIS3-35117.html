Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] This morning, the People's Assembly held session a under Speaker 'Abd-al-Qadir Qaddurah. At the beginning of the session, a summary of deputies' written queries was read out, and then referred to competent authorities through the Prime Ministry. The assembly then listened to the government's responses to deputies' written queries to the Ministry of Economy and Foreign Trade on the government's measures concerning the dollar exchange rate. The ministry explained that the government has adopted a number of measures, including the creation of a neighboring market [al-suq al-mujawirah], referring many commercial and noncommercial transactions to this new market, and encouraging exports and permitting [exporters] to keep part of export revenue. The ministry also explained measures to combat smuggling and to effect a financial balance through increasing revenues and rationalizing expenditure, which would include a reconsideration of subsidies for some commodities and institutions. The ministry also explained the reasons behind the slight rise that occurred in the foreign currency exchange rates. Dr. Muhammad al-'Imadi, minister economy and foreign trade, explained some points concerning a number of questions. The session was adjourned and will reconvene at 1200 Saturday, 26 February.
