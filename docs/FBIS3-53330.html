Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The prime minister and the foreign minister have sent their condolences to the family of Norwegian Foreign Minister Johan Jorgen Holst, who died of a stroke today. Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin said that the State of Israel will remember Minister Holst as the man who was successful in reducing the distance between two adversaries, in helping them to overcome a agelong enmity, and in building the foundation of peace in the Middle East. His contribution to the struggle for peace will serve us as a beacon in our future effort to end all wars. Foreign Minister Shim'on Peres said that Minister Holst's compelling contribution to the peace process in our region is indisputable.
