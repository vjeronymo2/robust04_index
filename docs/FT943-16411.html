940705 FT 05 JUL 94 / MPs launch probe into horticulture The cross-party Commons agriculture committee is to inquire into the horticulture industry. It will look at the effect of the Gatt trade agreement on fruit and vegetable producers, biotechnological developments and consumer issues. The inquiry was welcomed by the National Farmers Union, which has warned that the livelihoods of many salad crop growers are threatened by cheap imports from Europe.
