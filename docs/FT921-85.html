920331 FT 31 MAR 92 / Election 1992: Adams warning MR GERRY ADAMS, president of Sinn Fein, the IRA's political wing, launched the party's manifesto yesterday. He said that if Sinn Fein gained good support at the election and was still excluded from the talks on Northern Ireland's political future, 'the answer is self-evident. Who can blame young people who see the only way forward as armed struggle?'
