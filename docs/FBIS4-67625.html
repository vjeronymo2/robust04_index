BFN [SDA report: "3,511 AIDS Cases in Switzerland"] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] So far, 3,511 AIDS cases have been registered in Switzerland and 2,418 of the infected persons have died of the AIDS virus. Last year alone, 634 new cases were registered. Drug addicts and homosexual and bisexual men are still affected most. Regarding the spread in the cantons, the largest number of sick persons is to be found in Zurich with 1,268 cases, followed by Geneva with 521 cases, and Waadt with 421 cases.
