BFN [Excerpt] The date for Spain's official membership of the European Army is now known; it will be 5 May. Defense Minister Garcia Vargas announced this today to the congress defense committee. The government will also study the possibility of increasing by one brigade to a division -- some 12,000 soldiers -- Spain's contribution to what has been called the Euroarmy. [Correspondent R. Martinez Durban] Spain is the fourth country, following France, Germany, and Belgium, to form part of the armed wing of the WEU [Western European Union]. It already has some 50,000 men. Spain will initially join with the 3,400 soldiers of the Cerro Muriano mechanized brigade in Cordoba. Garcia Vargas also revealed that a low-powered ground-to-air SAM-7 missile from the Serb sector hit the fuselage of a Spanish Aviocar on 8 March as it was flying in Croatian air space.
