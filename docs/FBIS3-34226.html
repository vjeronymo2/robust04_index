Language: Pashto Article Type:BFN [Text] Airplanes of the Islamic State of Afghanistan Air Force bombarded civilian and military airfields of Dostam's militia in the area surrounding Mazar-e Sharif. A Ministry of National Defense source told BIA's military correspondent that fighter planes of the country's air force inflicted crushing blows at civilian and military airfields and installations in the area surrounding Mazar-e Sharif today. As a result of those successful operations, the runways of the airfields have been completely destroyed, and planes and installations in the fields have been heavily damaged. During the successful operation, dozens of militia, stationed in the airfields, have been killed, and one fuel store of the militia was set ablaze.
