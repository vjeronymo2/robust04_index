Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] New York, 9 Jan (INA)--Iraq has again protested to the United Nations over U.S. aircraft's violations of the Iraqi airspace for purposes of surveillance, provocation, and the dropping of flares. In a letter to the UN secretary general yesterday, the permanent Iraqi UN representative said that from 21-31 December U.S. aircraft carried out 767 aggressive air sorties over the northern and southern regions of Iraq. In addition, a hostile formation dropped a flare south of al-Basrah. In his letter, the Iraqi envoy asked that the letter be distributed among the member states and be considered one of the UN Security Council documents.
