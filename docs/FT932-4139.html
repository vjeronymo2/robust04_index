930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / International Company News: EMS-Chemie in shares purchase EMS-CHEMIE, the Swiss speciality chemicals group, has received 174,646 bearer shares in response to its offer last month to buy up to 250,000 shares at SFr2,200 (Dollars 1,510) for cancellation. The aim of the company was to distribute excess liquidity. Assuming that an outstanding free warrant for 38,292 bearer shares will be exercised next month, the group's capital would then consist of 515,000 SFr100 bearer shares and 780,000 SFr20 registered shares.
