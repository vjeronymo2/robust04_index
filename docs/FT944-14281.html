941024 FT 24 OCT 94 / Arts (Openings): Vienna To help compensate for the three-month closure of the State Opera for technical alterations, a production of Mozart's 'Cosi fan tutte' is being mounted at the Theater an der Wien. Riccardo Muti will conduct a young international cast including Cecilia Bartoli and Boje Skovhus. First night is Sunday, and there are performances every second day till November 11.
