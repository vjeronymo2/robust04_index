931203 FT 03 DEC 93 / Observer: Fee fi fo fum On Wednesday night one of the more prestigious head-hunting firms, Heidrick and Struggles, gave a slap-up do at the Royal College of Art, to which all its esteemed clients were invited. Mingling among the champagne-quaffers, Observer was amused to hear one senior chief executive delicately talk business - and make a job offer to a fellow guest. 'But don't tell this lot,' said the offeror, 'or they'll be after their fee.' A little hard on said head-hunters - who may well consider charging at the door next year.
