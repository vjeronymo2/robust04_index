Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] AKHIR KHABAR has learned that the security organs yesterday arrested an Islamist in connection with the recent explosions. Some sources said he belongs to the Islamic Action Front [IAF], but IAF sources denied that. According to informed sources, the new arrest raises the number of detainees to over 50. The competent authorities, however, are expected to release about 13 other detainees who were arrested for interrogation but were found not guilty. The prime minister had earlier told the House deputies that the investigation will take its course and that all who had nothing to do with the recent explosions will be released.
