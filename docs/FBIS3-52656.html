Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Lieutenant General Umar Hasan Ahmad al-Bashir, president of the republic, has described the celebrations of independence day as something that should continue and should be strengthened. In his meeting this morning with the media leaders, he said that the revolution's serious work to implement the principles of independence was what instigated others to adopt a hostile attitude in their dealings with Sudan, a matter which instigated us to be even more committed to our independence. In this context he pointed out that opening al-Tahadi [defiance] road was a confirmation of independence. Lieutenant General al-Bashir stressed the need for numerous similar great projects in order to get Sudan out of the state of inherited poverty. He stressed the importance of constructing al-Hamdab dam, which will provide 1.250 million mw of electricity in addition to irrigation water. [passage omitted]
