920618 FT 18 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Foreign & Colonial Smaller Cos higher Foreign & Colonial Smaller Companies reported net asset value of 107.3p at April 30 compared with 104,3p a year earlier. However it was lower than the 109p at October 31. Net revenue increased slightly to Pounds 1.78m (Pounds 1.77m) for earnings per share of 1.97p (1.96p). An increased final dividend of 1.1p is recommended making a total of 1.75p (1.65p).
