BFN [Excerpts] At the Carthage Palace this morning President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali received Luo Gan, state councillor and secretary-general of the PRC State Council, in the presence of Foreign Minister Habib Ben Yahia and An Huihou, the PRC ambassador to Tunis. Luo Gan said that his talks with the president were held in an atmosphere exemplifying the sentiments of Tunisian-Chinese friendship and cordiality. He added that relations between Tunisia and China have always been good and that the exchange of visits between high level officials and bilateral cooperation in various fields continues. [passage omitted] The Chinese state councillor pointed out that Tunisia and China are currently carrying out a process of reform and transformation which would provide extensive fields from which the two countries can benefit. At the end of his statement he said: We hope that the friendly Tunisian people will achieve further great successes under the leadership of President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali.
