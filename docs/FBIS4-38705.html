BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Gennadiy Yezhov] [Text] Moscow June 1 TASS -- Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin's visit to Latvia, initially scheduled for the end of May, was put off until the end of the premier's two-week leave, which begins today, ITAR-TASS was told at the press service of the Russian Government. It is planned to sign documents on economic cooperation between the two countries during Chernomyrdin's visit to Latvia, agreement on which was reached during Latvian President Guntis Ulmanis' visit to Moscow at the end of April, 1994.
