BFN [By reporter Lin Minzhong (2651 3046 1813)] [Text] Buenos Aires, 23 Apr (XINHUA) -- A CPC delegation led by Hu Jintao, member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau and member of the Secretariat of the CPC Central Committee, visited (Jinhehuan) Farm in Argentina on 23 April. Hu Jintao and his party arrived at the farm, located some 80 km west of Buenos Aires, by automobile at 1200. Cowboys on horseback, in Gaucho herdsman's costumes, lined up to welcome the distinguished Chinese guests. The hospitable host briefed Hu Jintao and his party on the situation of the farm. The main crops of the 1,400-hectare farm are wheat, oats, and forage grass. On the farm, Hu Jintao and members of the delegation watched the Gaucho rodeo and folk dance performances with great interest; they also had some delicious roast beef. In the evening, the delegation watched Argentina's unique Tango dance performance.
