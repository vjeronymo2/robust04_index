Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] A communique has just reached us. The minister of the Rally for Democracy and Progress [RDP] party -- the party led by Almamy Sylla -- has reportedly resigned from the government formed yesterday by the new prime minister, Ibrahim Boubacar Keita. We are giving you this information pending confirmation from the general secretariat of the presidency. It is worth noting that Ousmane Omarou Sidibe, minister of employment, civil service, and labor, is the only RDP member of the new government.
