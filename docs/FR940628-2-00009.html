Economic Research Service National Agricultural Cost of Production Standards Review Board; Meeting The National Agricultural Cost of Production Standards review Board will meet on July 11&hyph;12, 1994, in Waugh Auditorium in the Economic Research Service Building, 1301 New York Avenue, NW., Washington, DC. The purpose of this meeting is to discuss general issues related to USDA's estimation of enterprise costs of production. The first session of the meeting will be 8 a.m.&hyph;12 a.m. on July 11, 1994. Subsequent sessions will be held from 1:30 p.m.&hyph;5 p.m. on July 11, and 8 a.m.&hyph;12 noon on July 12. All sessions will be open to members of the public who wish to observe. Written comments may be submitted before or after the meeting to Richard Long, Acting Director, ARED&hyph;ERS&hyph;USDA, room 314, 1301 New York Avenue NW., Washington, DC 20005&hyph;4888. This meeting is authorized by 7 U.S.C. 4104, as amended. For further information, contact Jim Ryan at (202) 219&hyph;0798. Kenneth L. Deavers, Acting Administrator.
