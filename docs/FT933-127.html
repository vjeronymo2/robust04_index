930930 FT 30 SEP 93 / UK Company News: Dencora Dencora, the property and housebuilding group, swung back into the black in the six months to June 30, with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 370,000, against a deficit of Pounds 28,000. Rental income improved to Pounds 4.6m (Pounds 4.3m) while trading turnover grew from Pounds 3.9m to Pounds 5.8m, reflecting, directors said, an 'encouraging improvement in the number of houses sold, albeit at lower margins'. Interest charges were reduced to Pounds 3.05m (Pounds 3.47m). Borrowings were Pounds 57m, against Pounds 59m at December. Basic earnings per share were 0.6p (1.2p losses). Fully diluted earnings worked through at 1.6p (0.2p).
