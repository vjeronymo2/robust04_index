April 8, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

OSCAR MEMORIES

The clips from the Akira Kurosawa movies showed a bunch of people being shot with arrows and some nice young women being cruelly killed off.

For me as a viewer, and I'm sure for other viewers, these scenes were painful and just plain pointless.

The whole attitude involved -- that by showing man's inhumanity to man you make us all better -- is really boneheaded. All it does is give people nightmares.

Let's quit acting like Kurosawa is teaching people something they don't already know. Grossing people out to make a humanitarian point is like punching someone in the stomach to try to explain that being hit is painful.

JOHN SIMONS

Long Beach
