920903 FT 03 SEP 92 / Church files suit against FDA The Church of Scientology yesterday filed a lawsuit against the Food and Drug Administration (FDA) claiming the government agency ignored proper scientific procedures in approving Prozac, Eli Lilley's anti-depressant drug, writes Patrick Harverson from New York. The suit has been filed by the Citizens Commission on Human Rights, a group controlled by the Scientologists, who claim the best-selling drug makes people suicidal.
