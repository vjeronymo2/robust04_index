September 15, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MEAN TO MACLAINE

I have never liked the term mean-spirited, mostly because I did not quite understand it.

However, thanks to Don Shirley's Aug. 30 review of Shirley MacLaine's "Out There Tonight," I do now.

Most of his criticism is leveled at the fact that a great deal of the show is repetitive of previous shows. Since when is this a sin? Why not repeat proven material?

Also, what about those of us who have not seen MacLaine previously? Are we to be discouraged because Don Shirley has?

RICHARD GENOVESE

Toluca Lake
