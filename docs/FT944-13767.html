941026 FT 26 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Stakis buys London hotel Stakis, the Glasgow-based hotel group, has bought Renthotel (UK) for Pounds 6.1m cash. Renthotel's only asset is the 92 bedroom Harewood Hotel, opposite Marylebone Station in central London. Some Pounds 5m of the consideration is repayment of debt. A further Pounds 200,000 is payable in respect of working capital. The hotel, to be known as the Stakis London Harewood, brings the group's total to 37 hotels and 22 casinos throughout the UK. The group announced a Pounds 10.4m pre-tax profit at the beginning of the year, turning round from losses of Pounds 47.7m after two years of restructuring and disposing of Ashbourne Holdings, its nursing home operations.
