Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] According to information received by RIA from the Russian State Committee for Defense Industry, the number of privatized enterprises in the defense sector in 1993 increased by over three times and amounted to 642 enterprises. In July, the State Committee for Defense Industry is going over to a qualitatively new stage of privatization -- investment auctions. Their aim is to attract funds from this country and foreign investors for technological renovation of the plant undergoing conversion.
