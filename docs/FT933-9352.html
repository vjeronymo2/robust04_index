930810 FT 10 AUG 93 / UK Company News: BM banking facilities extended BM Group, the engineering concern, said yesterday that its principal bankers had extended the company's banking facilities for a further period to September 6 while discussions continued regarding suitable longer-term finance. The company, which has debts of over Pounds 100m, is on schedule with its survival plan, which is expected to cost about Pounds 42m in redundancies, closures, disposals and write-offs.
