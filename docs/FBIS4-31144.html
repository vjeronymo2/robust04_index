BFN [Text] Knin, 3 Jun (TANJUG) -- Colonel Lazo Babic, commander of the Western Slavonia Corps of the Serb Krajina Army, sent a protest letter to General Sharmi, the UNPROFOR [UN Protection Force] commander for Sector West, over an incident that happened at a meeting of Sector West's commissions in Lipik yesterday. According to a statement by the Serb Krajina Army's Main Headquarters, Croatian General Dzanko threatened to arrest one of the official Krajina representatives at the talks, and a more serious incident was prevented only through UNPROFOR General Vergar's intervention. Voicing serious concern over the success of the upcoming talks and the safety of the Krajina negotiators, Col. Babic demands the Croatian general's apology over the incident that could have unforeseeable consequences. He also demands an increased level of security by UNPROFOR at future talks' venues.
