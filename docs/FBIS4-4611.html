BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 4 (KCNA) -- His Majesty King Norodom Sihanouk of Cambodia arrived here today by special plane for an official goodwill visit to Korea at the invitation of President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. Their majesties the king and queen are accompanied by the Deputy Prime Minister in charge of the Royal Palace and Minister of Agriculture Kong Sam-Ol, Cambodian Ambassador E.P. [extraordinary and plenipotentiary] to Korea Oum Mannorine, the minister of state, Secretary General of the King's Cabinet Chhorn Hay and others. The guests were met at the airport by Vice-president Yi Chong-ok, Vice-premier and Foreign Minister Kim Yong-nam and officials concerned. They were met at the airport by Chinese Ambassador to Korea Qiao Zonghuai.
