910425 FT 25 APR 91 / UK Company News: Ensign Trust completes Argosy sale Ensign Trust has completed the disposal of Argosy Asset Management, its wholly-owned investment management subsidiary which is being wound down after losing the management in March of the main Merchant Navy Officers Pension Fund valued at Pounds 1.5bn. Ensign, 78 per cent owned by the MNOPF, is selling Argosy's remaining interests to Aberdeen Trust for Pounds 900,000 cash. Earlier this month it announced the sale of the bulk of Argosy's investment management interests to Ivory and Sime for Pounds 1.75m. Ensign also owns 39 per cent of Aberdeen's ordinary share capital. The interests to be sold include those in Argosy's fund management subsidiaries in Australia, Luxembourg, Scotland and Finland.
