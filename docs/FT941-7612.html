940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / N Sea oil output rises by 20% UK North Sea oil output in the three months to January was more than 20 per cent above the level in the same period a year ago, Royal Bank of Scotland Oil and Gas Index reported yesterday. Oil production last month rose to 2.4m barrels a day, worth nearly Pounds 23m, while gas production was up for the 18th successive month and was worth Pounds 16.8m a day.
