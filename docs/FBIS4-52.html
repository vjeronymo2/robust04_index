The Second General Assembly of the Mongolian Youth Association (MYA) held elections to its Presidium and Control Council in December 1993. The names of persons elected to the two bodies, their ages (ranging from 23 to 37) and their affiliated organizations or professions were reported in the youth organization newspaper MONGOLYN DZALUUCHUUD.
