931026 FT 26 OCT 93 / Tour operators agree sales deal OWNERS ABROAD Group has reached agreement with the Pickfords and Hogg Robinson travel agency chains, owned by rival Airtours, for the distribution and display of its principal holiday brands. Pickfords and Hogg Robinson, which have a combined share of 11 per cent of the travel agency market, will sell Owners' holidays under the Enterprise, Falcon, Martyn, Sovereign, Sunmed and Twentys labels.
