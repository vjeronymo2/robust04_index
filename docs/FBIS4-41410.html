BFN [Text] At today's meeting of the cabinet, Oleg Soskovets, Russian first deputy prime minister, voiced his dissatisfaction with the Ministry of Fuel and Power Engineering's position while discussing the issue of how enterprises and organizations were preparing for the fall and winter period. According to him, the panic-stricken pronouncements by the ministry's representative to the effect that we will not survive this winter can mean only one thing: Such people should be found who will be able to organize work in a new way.
