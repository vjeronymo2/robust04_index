June 6, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

EARNINGS

R. H. Macy: The New York-based retailer said it lost $63.1 million in the third quarter, about the same as last year's $64.1 million. Revenue of $1.49 billion was essentially flat compared to last year's $1.48 billion. The company said it continues to experience only slight sales growth and is trying to restructure its debt.
