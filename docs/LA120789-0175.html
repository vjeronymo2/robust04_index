December 7, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEW $100-MILLION PLANT IS PLANNED BY PHILIPS

Philips Display Components Co. has announced plans to build a $100-million plant to produce color television picture tubes and future products for high-definition television applications.

Initially, more than 300 new jobs will be created by the plant's construction, said Philips Display, a division of North American Philips Corp.

Although a specific plant location has not yet been selected, Philips is currently considering several sites in Michigan, Ohio, Tennessee and Kentucky.
