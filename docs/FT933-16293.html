930705 FT 05 JUL 93 / Pledge on S Africa PRESIDENT Bill Clinton pledged last night to work within the Group of Seven leading industrial nations at their Tokyo summit this week 'to include a new and democratic South Africa in the world economy', Reuter reports from Philadelphia. Mr Clinton spoke at a ceremony at which South African President FW de Klerk and Mr Nelson Mandela, leader of the African National Congress, received the Philadelphia Liberty Medal.
