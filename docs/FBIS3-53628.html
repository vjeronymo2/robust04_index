Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] With the agreement of the Commander in Chief Khamene'i, the time limit for requesting the permit for permanent exemption from military service in peace time for those who are eligible and avoiding military service, or those who are absent, has been extended until the end of 1373 [year ending 20 March 1995], on the condition of paying a fine. Brig Rahimi, the head of the Supreme Office of National Military Service, in an interview with correspondents today, announced the news and added: Those eligible for military service who have received two- or three-year temporary exemptions can, before the end of the term of their exemption and during the time limit stipulated by this regulation, call at the Supreme Office of National Military Service to readjust the financial undertaking they had given to the new approved amount to receive permanent exemption. [passage omitted] In conclusion, the head of the Supreme Office of National Military Service said: Those eligible for military service and residing abroad who wish to apply to receive permanent exemption from military service during peace time can pay a fine at the floating exchange rate of one dollar to 1,435 rials, the exchange rate at the time of approval of this regulation, and benefit from permanent exemption from military service during peace time.
