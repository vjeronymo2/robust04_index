931208 FT 08 DEC 93 / Equality chief quits early Valerie Amos, chief executive of the Equal Opportunity Commission is leaving her job a year before the end of her contract to pursue other interests. Ms Amos, who came to the job four years ago from local government, put the commission on a more structured footing and made it more accessible to grassroots organisations and ethnic minorities. She is understood to have had difficulty developing a strong personal relationship with Ms Kamlesh Bahl who took over commission's chair in June.
