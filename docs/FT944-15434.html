941018 FT 18 OCT 94 / World Stock Markets: European equities Last week's enthusiasm from European equities, led by Finland and Germnay, stands in stark contrast to an overview from Merrill Lynch which heralds 'the coming collapse in market multiples'. The US broker's European strategy team believes that price/earnings ratios in major European equity markets could halve as they approach peak earnings. The rise in earnings would make most of the difference in a fall from p/e estimates, 'in the high teens to high 20s on average', to 10 or less; but Merrill also thinks that markets could trend
