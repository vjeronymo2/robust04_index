930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa GOLD shares lost nearly 6 per cent as the correction in the bullion price from recent highs continued. The golds index retreated 98 to 1,584, its lowest level since May 12, with Vaal Reefs down R18 to R274. The overall index shed 36 to 3,890, while industrials gained 3 at 4,560.
