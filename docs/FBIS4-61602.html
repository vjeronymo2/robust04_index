BFN [Text] Moscow, 8 May (ITAR-TASS) -- Azerbaijani President Geydar Aliyev received today Vladimir Kazimirov, a plenipotentiary of the Russian president. ITAR-TASS has learned at the Russian Foreign Ministry that they discussed a Karabakh settlement. Rasul Guliyev, the chairman of the National Assembly, and other Azerbaijani leaders took part in the conversation.
