Language: German Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] Bonn (DPA) -- German Chancellor Helmut Kohl today spoke on the telephone with Russian President Boris Yeltsin to gather first-hand information on the formation of the government in Moscow, government spokesman Dieter Vogel said in Bonn today. [passage omitted] Vogel said Yeltsin ensured Kohl that he will keep to the path of reform in Russia. He is also confident that both houses of parliament will work together in continuing the process of reform. Vogel said developments in Russia and the CIS would form the focus of Kohl's talks with U.S. President Bill Clinton when he visits Washington from 30 January to 1 February.
