Language: Slovak Article Type:BFN [Interview with Finance Minister Rudolf Filkus by "is" in the "Only Question" column; place and date not given: "Budget Deficit for First Three Months Stands at 7.5 Billion"] [Text] [SME] Late last year, the National Council of the Slovak Republic approved a law on the 1994 state budget, which foresees a budget deficit of 14 billion Slovak korunas [Sk]. What is the size of the deficit today? [Filkus] As of today, the deficit stands at Sk7.5 billion. This demonstrates that the assumption of a deficit at the level of Sk14 billion by the end of the year, which was viewed [by the previous government] as a realistic macroeconomic indicator, was not a realistic macroeconomic assumption.
