920911 FT 11 SEP 92 / International Company News: Earnings up 27% at Hysan HYSAN Development, one of Hong Kong's largest property investment companies, achieved net profits of HKDollars 514m (USDollars 66.5m) in the six months to June, 27 per cent higher than the HKDollars 404m reported in the same period in 1991. Earnings were buoyed by the sale of its 50 per cent stake in the Pacific Centre, San Francisco, and by the HKDollars 124m exceptional profit from the sale of Royal Garden, Hong Kong. Analysts said rental income increased by about 13 per cent from last year. The company is to raise the interim dividend to 28 cents from 23 cents.
