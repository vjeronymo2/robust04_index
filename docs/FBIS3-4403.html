Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] A six-member group from the Asia Development Bank arrived in Harbin on the evening of 4 March on their mission of carrying out a six-day on-the-spot examination of the project of building the highway section between Harbin and Jiamusi on the designed highway between Tongjiang and (Shanya), which will be built with the loan offered by the Asia Development Bank. The section of Tongjiang-(Shanya) highway in the province is 641 km long. The highway section between Harbin and Jiamusi, which was designed in the first-phase project, is 332 km long. The total investment made in the highway project is 2.2 billion yuan renminbi. The loan offered by the Asia Development Bank is $140 million. The State Council has formally approved the loan for the highway project.
