May 18, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

Safeway to Pay Fine: Safeway Stores has agreed to pay Santa Clara and Monterey counties a total of $66,500 in civil penalties for selling items that weighed less than labels indicated, prosecutors reported. Santa Clara County Deputy District Attorney Eugene Baldwin said the huge chain overcharged customers at many of its central coast and San Francisco Bay Area markets for meat, bakery and delicatessen items over a period of two years.
