BFN [Text] Sanaa, 29 May (MENA)--Military sources in Sanaa have stated that Former Defense Minister Brigadier General Haytham Qasim Tahir, who was recently relieved of his post by President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, was killed last week. These sources note that the ousted minister was killed during an offensive by the northern forces on the al-Rahah area in the Lahij Governorate. They said that another number of southern military men were also killed in the same offensive.
