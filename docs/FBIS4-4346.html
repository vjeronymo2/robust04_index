BFN [Text] On 28 March, the National Army of Democratic Kampuchea [NADK], in cooperation with the people, launched a commando attack on the Sala Krau position, killing seven enemy soldiers and seizing seven AK assault rifles. The remaining enemy troops were forced to retreat into the jungle. On 29 March, the NADK attacked the 12th Division, which was temporarily stationed at Sala Krau. The fighting lasted five and a half hours. While the NADK was attacking the unit, four T-54 tanks and 200 ground troops were sent to help their companions. However, we killed 24 of them and wounded several others. We also burned two T-54 tanks, captured two others, and destroyed two military trucks. We also recaptured and reoccupied the summit of Srang Hill. We will continue to attack and destroy the remaining enemy troops at Sala Krau.
