Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Azerbaijan's army has been fiercely resisting an advancing Armenian force in northern Karabakh for the second day running. An Azerbaijani defence ministry spokesman reported to Interfax on Saturday that the enemy had been mounting attacks along the Kelbajar, Geranboi, and Terter lines. The Armenian losses in two days of fighting are estimated at 40 men, 2 tanks, and 1 armored vehicle. On the southern front (down the Goradiz line), according to the spokesman, there have been sporadic battles. Azerbaijani army positions and villages were repeatedly shelled throughout Friday. Casualties are being specified.
