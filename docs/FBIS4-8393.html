BFN [Text] Sarajevo, 13 Apr (TANJUG)--Vitaliy Churkin, special Russian envoy for former Yugoslavia, left Pale for the Muslim part of Sarajevo this morning. It is expected that during his talks with UNPROFOR [UN Protection Forces] officials, Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Churkin will try to renew conditions for a resumption of dialogue between the Serb side and representatives of the UN peacekeepers following NATO's military intervention, which strained the relations between the Srpska republic and UNPROFOR. Churkin has been visiting Pale for two days now, and he is expected to return from the Muslim part of Sarajevo early this afternoon.
