930407 FT 07 APR 93 / UK Company News: 16% advance at United Friendly United Friendly Group, the life assurance company, announced a 16.4 per cent increase to Pounds 23.4m in pre-tax profits for 1992, writes John Authers. Earnings per share were 23p, against 20.2p. The total dividend goes up 17.9 per cent to 14.5p, with a proposed final of 9.6p. Profits from life business were up at Pounds 11.2m (Pounds 10.7m), with costs incurred by the entry to the unit-linked market reduced to Pounds 1.3m (Pounds 4.9m). New life business showed increases for both regular premiums of Pounds 123.5m (Pounds 117.1m) and single premiums of Pounds 77.2m (Pounds 48.7m). Analysts were impressed by the company's prediction that its unit-linked business will make a profit for the first time this year.
