940603 FT 03 JUN 94 / UK Company News: RIT Capital net asset value improves 22% Net asset value of RIT Capital Partners, the investment trust, grew by 22.4 per cent to 221.6p per share over the year to March 31. Total net assets were Pounds 437m, up from Pounds 352m. After tax of Pounds 2.7m (Pounds 1.9m) profits increased from Pounds 1.4m to Pounds 5.46m. Earnings per share rose from 0.81p to 3.03p, or 2.82p diluted. A dividend of 1.51p (1.15p) is proposed. At the year end, quoted equities made up 49 per cent of the portfolio (Pounds 248m), government and corporate bonds 11 per cent (Pounds 56m), specialist funds 18 per cent (Pounds 94m), and unquoted companies 17 per cent (Pounds 89m). During the year RITCP sold most of its holding in Newmont Mining, realising Pounds 114m. Its largest unquoted holding is in Amerpharm, half of which is due to be sold to E Merck.
