BFN [Text] On the morning of 29 March, the high-level delegation of the House of Representatives of Malaysia [HRM] headed by His Excellency Tan Sri Datuk Mohamed Zahir bin Haji Ismail, speaker of the HRM, visited Xieng Khouang Province by a special plane. On hand at the Xieng Khouang airfield to warmly welcome the delegation was Phoumi Thipphavon, governor of Xieng Khouang Province; members of the provincial administrative committee; and a large number of cadres and Xieng Khouang residents. During its one-day tour of the proviunce, the high-level HRM delegation visited the Plain of Jars, Phonsavan town, and [words indistinct]. On the morning of 30 March, the high-level HRM delegation led by its speaker and accompanied by Chaleun Yiapaoheu, member of the Standing Committee of the National Assembly, visited the Lao-Thai bridge construction site. The bridge is a gift from the Australian Government to the governments and peoples of Laos and Thailand. Its construction has been smooth and will be completed on schedule. The official opening of the bridge will be held in the near future. Later, the delegation visited a (?handicraft center) and a textile factory operated by the Federation of Lao Women's Unions.
