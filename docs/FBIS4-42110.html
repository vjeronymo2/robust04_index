BFN [Text] Government spokesman Ioannis Kasoulidhis confirmed that the Cyprus Government expects the UN secretary general's report to be issued tomorrow. He added that the government believes that it will be an objective report that will record the facts that attribute blame to the Turkish side. He also noted that the confidence-building measures [CBM's] issue has ended. Mr. Kasoulidhis further said that the government does not see any reason for President Glavkos Kliridhis, who is visiting New York, to discuss the CBM's with the UN secretary general. The spokesman added: The Cyprus Government believes that the Security Council should find other ways to participate in the process of promoting a Cyprus solution. He also made it clear that the government desires to participate in any of those alternative plans of action, which should be decided by the Security Council on the basis of Resolution 889.
