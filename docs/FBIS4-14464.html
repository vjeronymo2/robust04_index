BFN [Text] Kiev -- On 30 March, the Kuchma-Grinev Interregional Bloc of Reforms' Information and Analysis Center told UNIAN that Leonid Kuchma, the bloc's leader, former Ukrainian prime minister and Ukrainian Union of Industrialists and Entrepreneurs' chairman, intends to run in the presidential elections set by Ukraine's Supreme Council to take place on 26 June. According to information from the same source, Leonid Kuchma hopes he will be able to secure the support of a majority in the new parliament as, having gained 91 percent of the electorate vote on 27 March, he won the parliamentary election in his constituency.
