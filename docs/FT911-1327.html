910507 FT 07 MAY 91 / International Company News: PWS rises to Pounds 3.11m PWS Holdings, the Lloyd's reinsurance broking group, lifted pre-tax profits from Pounds 2.73m to Pounds 3.11m in the six months to March 31. Brokerage crept up from Pounds 8.54m to Pounds 8.77m. Lord Pearson of Rannoch, chairman, said that the company had decided to change its accounting policy on goodwill. This had the effect of increasing profits by Pounds 198,000 in the half. Interest payable fell to Pounds 163,000 (Pounds 670,000) and this time there was no exceptional charge (Pounds 400,000). Earnings worked through at 10.1p (10.7p) per share and the interim dividend is raised to 1.5p (1.3p).
