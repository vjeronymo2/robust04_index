BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] President Suleyman Demirel replied to reporters' questions on various issues in Afyon today. In answer to a question on whether he had sent a letter to the Iranian President 'Ali Akbar Hashemi-Rafsanjani, Demirel said: Yes, I sent him a letter on the terrorism issue. Pointing out that terrorist organizations are being squeezed as a result of security forces' operations, Demirel reiterated his call to those involved in terrorism to surrender. Demirel stated that according to Turkey's observations, the terrorists mostly escaped to Iran. Explaining that Iranian officials show a lot of goodwill regarding this issue, Demirel recalled that the territory is very rugged. Demirel said the following regarding his letter to Rafsanjani: [Begin Demirel recording] I know that Iran will not give protection to those murderers but, nevertheless, the information we have is that they are being given shelter in certain areas and I noted their names in my letter. The Iranian president has always greatly respected me and carefully considered what I say. I have informed Rafsanjani that Turkey is disturbed regarding this issue. [end recording] [passage omitted]
