BFN [Text] Lebanon's Hezbollah has praised the martyrdom-seeking operations carried out on Wednesday [6 April] by a Palestinian in the town of 'Afula in northern Palestine, and expresses its full support for the continuation of such operations. Issuing a statement in Beirut today, Lebanon's Hezbollah announced: The anti-Zionist struggle is the legitimate right of the Palestinian people toward the liberation of the holy lands of Palestine from the clutches of the Zionist regime. By carrying out their martyrdom-seeking operations, the Mojahedin are, in reality, defending the honor and dignity of the Islamic ummah. The statement stresses the futility of the path of compromise with the Zionist regime and states: The people of Palestine will never succumb to the bullying of occupiers and compromisers.
