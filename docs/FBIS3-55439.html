Language: Kyrgyz Article Type:BFN [Text] Boris Yeltsin is expected to visit Kyrgyzstan. Janysh Rustenbekov, the head of the presidential apparatus, confirmed that Boris Yeltsin and Askar Akayev discussed a possibility of an official visit to Kyrgyzstan during the Ashgabat summit. Its goal is to discuss the situation in the republic's economy and forms of support to be rendered to our industry by Russia. A preliminary date of the visit is set on 20 January. The Kyrgyzstan side is now preparing documents for intergovernment agreements.
