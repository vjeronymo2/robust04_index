BFN [Text] Cairo, 26 Jun (MENA)--Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa has described as a very good and positive step Sanaa's decision to observe a cease-fire as of midnight last night. It is important to respect the cease-fire in a strict way because it represents the right step toward resolving the crisis, Musa said. Commenting on the postponement of the Damascus Declaration meeting, originally scheduled for Tuesday [28 June], Musa said that the postponement occurred due to certain obligations of some foreign ministers of the Damascus Declaration's signatory countries.
