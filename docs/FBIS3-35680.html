Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Riga, Jan 29, BNS -- A Latvian state delegation, led by Foreign Minister Georgs Andrejevs, left Saturday for Washington to meet with U.S. security-related officials. The group also includes Saeima (parliament) faction heads, Foreign Ministry's Parliamentary Secretary Martins Virsis and State Secretary Maris Riekstins. Latvian politicians are scheduled to meet with State Secretary Warren Christopher, his deputy Strobe Talbott, Nick Burn and Anthony Lake, of the National Security Council, and William Perry, the Pentagon under secretary.
