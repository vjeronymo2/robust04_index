Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today Alija Izetbegovic, president of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Presidency, received Mr. Charles Redman, special envoy of Bill Clinton, and General (Galvin), special envoy for military affairs. The meeting was also attended by Bosnia-Herzegovina Army Commander General Delic. They discussed the possibilities for the implementation of the agreement between the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army and the Croatian Defense Council, on which General (Galvin) and his team will offer their assistance.
