BFN [Text] President Akayev gave a news conference yesterday devoted to the results of his visits to Belgium and France. Speaking about his visit to Brussels, Akayev said that Kyrgyzstan is the 20th state overall and the third Central Asian republic to join the Partnership for Peace program. He confirmed Kyrgyzstan's adherence to freedom and democracy. Other aspects of the visit were also highlighted. Akayev went into detail concerning his visit to France, stressing the importance for the republic of the concluded agreements. Other members of the delegation also answered journalists's questions.
