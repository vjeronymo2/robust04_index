Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The United States is demanding that Israel detain scores of settlers from the hardline and extremist groups and hold them in administrative detention, our correspondent in Washington Arye Golan reports. The United States also thinks that Israel must disarm the settlers and supply them with light weapons for self-defense only. These issues were scheduled to be raised in the preparatory talks of Nabil Sha'th with Dennis Ross, U.S. peace talks coordinator, and Martin Indyk, a member of the National Security Council, before Sha'th's meeting with U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher this afternoon.
