Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Andov Receives Serb's Representatives"] [Text] Macedonian Parliament President Stojan Andov, as President of the Council on ethnic relations in Macedonia, received representatives of the Democratic Party of Serbs in Macedonia. They discussed enforcement of the rights of the Serbian minority, from aspects of the degree of inclusion in the Constitution of issues like the right to education and culture, and the necessity of greater Serbian presence in the mass-media in Macedonia.
