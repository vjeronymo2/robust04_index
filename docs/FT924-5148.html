921128 FT 28 NOV 92 / County Hall sale campaign launched THE London Residuary Body, which is disposing of the assets of the former Greater London Council, is launching an international marketing campaign for parts of the County Hall complex, the GLC's former headquarters. The main County Hall building is being sold to Shirayama Corporation of Japan, which intends to convert it to a hotel. The remainder of the site has planning permission for a 1.3m sq ft office and retail development and a separate 450,000 sq ft office building.
