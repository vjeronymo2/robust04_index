930222 FT 22 FEB 93 / Observer: Over-monitored Meanwhile, some good news on the EC job-creation front. The Commission has decided to monitor the EC Monitor programme which monitors EC research expenditures. In other words the Community's research boffins are being asked to monitor how well Monitor is monitoring them. It's a true Community-wide initiative. The questionnaire comes from Brussels, replies go to Spain, and the Danish ministry of industry is providing the chairman of the review panel.
