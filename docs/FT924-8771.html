921110 FT 10 NOV 92 / World News in Brief: French refinery blasts kill five Five people died and another was seriously hurt when explosions, believed caused by a gas leak, ripped through the Le Mede oil refinery (left) near Marseilles in southern France. More than 250 firefighters took three hours to put out the blaze which followed. The refinery is the second largest owned by French oil company Total.
