920801 FT 01 AUG 92 / Ambitious telecom project THE Far Eastern part of an ambitious project to link the former Soviet Union with a sophisticated international telecommunications network will begin in October with tenders to carry out the work. Mr Oleg Belov, for the Russian end of the project, said yesterday contracts for providing and laying fibre optic cables between the Russian city of Khavarovsk, Japan and South Korea would be put out through competitive bidding. A memorandum of understanding to put the Dollars 120m Far Eastern link into operation by 1995 was signed yesterday by Russia's Inter Telekom, Japan's Kusai Denshin Denwa (KDD) Korea Telecom and two Danish telecommunications companies involved in linking the western and eastern ends of the former Soviet Union via the Baltic Sea.
