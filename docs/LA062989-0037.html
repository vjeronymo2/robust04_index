June 29, 1989, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: SAN DIEGO

A San Diego man who shot and killed an armed intruder inside his apartment Tuesday did so in self-defense and will not be charged, San Diego police said.

Frank D. Cuevas, 44, of 24th Street, shot James Miller, 54, after Miller forced his way into the apartment at gunpoint at 10:40 p.m., according to Sgt. Frank Martinez of the homicide unit. Miller had entered the apartment in pursuit of his ex-wife, who was there at the time, according to Martinez.
