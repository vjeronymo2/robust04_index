Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] This afternoon, Prime Minister Yitzhaq Rabin and Yasir 'Arafat spoke on the phone for 15 minutes. They discussed the possibility of resuming the peace talks. Our correspondent Shlomo Raz reports that Mr. Rabin initiated the conversation. It appears that no Jewish site in Hebron will be evacuated at this time. Minister Moshe Shahal estimated today that the talks between the PLO and Israel will resume before the Passover holidays [starting on 27 March]. Regarding the Waqf decision to ban the entry of non-Muslims to the Temple Mount, Minister Shahal said that Israel will not accept the Waqf decision, but stressed that such disagreements are better solved through silent diplomacy.
