930628 FT 28 JUN 93 / International Company News: Pay boost for Boots' chief SIR JAMES Blyth, chief executive of Boots, the retailing and pharmaceuticals group, was paid a total of Pounds 620,000 last year, an increase of 8.5 per cent, according to the company's annual report. His basic salary rose from Pounds 380,000 to Pounds 415,000, while bonuses, long service payments and other benefits increased from Pounds 191,000 to Pounds 205,000. In addition, Boots contributed Pounds 193,000 to Sir James's pension arrangements, up from Pounds 177,000 the previous year.
