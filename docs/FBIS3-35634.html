Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Armenian Defense Ministry press service has reported that a relative calm is prevailing in the border regions of the Republic. However, the situation continues to remain extremely tense in the Karabakh-Azerbaijani conflict zone. Although the Azerbaijani Army is suffering huge losses in men and material, it is pressing ahead with its desperate attempts to break through the Karabakh forces' defensive lines in various sectors. In the last three days alone the Karabakh forces either destroyed or captured 19 armored cars, including 16 tanks. Nagorno-Karabakh Republic Defense Army headquarters reported that the Azerbaijani attacks in various directions along the combat line, extending from northwest to the southern borders, were completely checked during battles yesterday. The enemy launched its fiercest attacks against Martuni. The Azerbaijanis retreated to their initial positions after losing two tanks and nearly 70 soldiers. The Azerbaijanis also lost a few dozen more troops in the other sectors of the war front. Seven were killed and 18 injured on the Karabakh side. The combat operations resumed on the morning of 28 January in the north of Kelbadzharskiy Rayon and nearby Mardak?rtskiy Rayon.
