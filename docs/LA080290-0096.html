August 2, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Baby Boss: Evan James Springsteen. That's the name of the baby born to Bruce Springsteen and Patti Scialfa.

The birth was announced last week, but the name and other details weren't available until Wednesday. A spokeswoman for the singers said the baby had brown hair and blue eyes and was born at 5:03 a.m., July 25 in a Los Angeles hospital. Both Springsteen and Scialfa have albums due in the coming months. No word on how the baby's birth will affect the completion of the albums or tour plans. GREG BRAXTON
