BFN [Text] Finance Minister Zoran Jasic, speaking in Split today, stated that the first phase of the goverment's stabilization program has achieved its most important goal, that being the reduction of the inflation rate to zero. Nevertheless, reports unconfirmed by the ministry state that the prices of agricultural products have risen around 20 percent. Minister Jasic announced that negotiations with the International Monetary Fund and the World Bank could result in loans of $128 million for reconstruction, although IMF and World Bank sources say the talks are continuing.
