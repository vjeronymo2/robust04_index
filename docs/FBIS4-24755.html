BFN [Text] Beijing, June 2 (XINHUA) -- General Cao Shuangming, commander of the Air Force of the People's Liberation Army (PLA), left here today on an official good-will visit to Russia and Egypt. Cao and his party are invited by Russian Air Force Commander Gen. Deynikin and Egyptian Air Defence Forces Commander Lt. Gen. Ahmad Abdel Rahman Sliman [name as received].
