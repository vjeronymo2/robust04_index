BFN [Text] The collegium of the Belarusian Defense Ministry has published a statement. The leadership of the armed forces expressed concern that the election campaign under way is being accompanied by an intensification of political tension around the Army. The statement recalls that, in accordance with the Constitution, servicemen enjoy civil rights and are not linked to decisions of political parties. According to the law "On the Armed Forces of the Republic of Belarus," the activity of any parties, sociopolitical movements, and other other organizations is prohibited in the Army. The collegium of the Defense Ministry demanded an end to attempts to draw the Army into the political struggle.
