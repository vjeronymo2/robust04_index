940115 FT 15 JAN 94 / World Commodities Prices: Spices The pepper market gradually became more active over the past few days and prices tended firmer in general, reports Man Producten. Muntok white pepper reached a price of about USDollars 2,900 a tonne, cif, for January/March shipment, while new crop positions (Agust shipment onwards) were quoted at about Dollars 2,700 a tonne. The market was very thin and volatile. Black pepper prices recovered as well. Some origins, like Brazil, Vietnam, Malaysia and Indonesia have hardly offered any pepper lately. Only India, with the new but reduced crop around the corner, could be obtained on a daily basis at a level of about Dollars 1,550 a tonne, cif.
