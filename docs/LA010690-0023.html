January 6, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski posted a $110 profit when his $20 win bet on Bojo Ro in the first hit for a $9 for $2 payoff and his $40 win bet on Zoe Akins in the fourth won at $4 for $2. Bob, who started with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $1,207.

For today, Bob's bet is $50 to win on Fleet Form in the ninth.
