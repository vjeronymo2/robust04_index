930820 FT 20 AUG 93 / Italian funding repayment offer Former Italian health minister Francesco De Lorenzo, one of the most reviled figures in the country's huge corruption scandal, was quoted yesterday as offering to repay L4bn (Pounds 1.67m) in illegal funding he received, Reuter reports from Rome. 'The illegal contributions amount to some L4bn. I will give them back, selling what I can,' he told the Corriere della Sera newspaper in an interview.
