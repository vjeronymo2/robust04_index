Language: German Article Type:BFN [DDP/ND report: "More AIDS Cases, But Fewer Deaths"] [Text] Berlin (DDP/ND) -- As compared to the previous year, the number of registered AIDS cases increased in 1993. According to statistical information in the Federal Health Paper 1/94, their number increased from 1,700 to 1,877. On the other hand, fewer deaths were reported. Last year, 203 people died, as compared to 578 in 1992.
