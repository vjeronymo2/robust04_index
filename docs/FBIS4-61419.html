BFN [Unattributed report: "Commission for Talks With Chechnya Set Up"] [Text] Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin has signed a directive on approving the lineup of a delegation to negotiate a draft treaty on the mutual delegation of powers between Russia and the Chechen Republic. The government delegation, to be made up of officials from various ministries and agencies, will be headed by Deputy Prime Minister Sergey Shakhray, minister for the affairs of nationalities and regional policy.
