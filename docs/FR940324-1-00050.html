National Environmental Policy Act The Service has determined that this proposed rule to add designated ports under authority of the Endangered Species Act of 1973 for the importation and exportation of plants is not a major Federal action which would significantly affect the quality of the human environment within the meaning of section 102(2)(C) of the National Environmental Policy Act of 1969. Paperwork Reduction Act This proposed rule contains no new information collection or recordkeeping requirements under the Paperwork Reduction Act of 1980 (44 U.S.C. 3501 et seq. ).
