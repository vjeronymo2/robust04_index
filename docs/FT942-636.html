940628 FT 28 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Higgs and Hill Investors have rejected builder Higgs and Hill's rights issue. The company said yesterday only 31 per cent of the issue, on offer at 105p, had been taken up. Higgs and Hill said sub-underwriters will be required to subscribe , at the subscription price, for the 15,174,422 new ordinary not taken up. Since Higgs launched its issue in May, the FT-SE 100 index has fallen by 250 points, and J Henry Schroder Wagg, adviser to Higgs, blamed this for the poor take-up.
