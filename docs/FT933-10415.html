930804 FT 04 AUG 93 / UK Company News in Brief: Greenalls Group GREENALLS GROUP, the pubs and hotels company, has received an 86 per cent acceptance in respect of its ordinary offer for JA Devenish, the west country pubs group. The offer has now been declared unconditional. Acceptances were received in respect of 47.24m Devenish shares, which included 8.07m shares for the cash alternative (14.7 per cent) which will be satisfied by the sale of 8.22m new Greenalls shares.
