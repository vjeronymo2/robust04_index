Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] An agreement on the utilization of $10 million of the $15 million loan Turkey has extended to Azerbaijan has been signed in Ankara. The agreement was signed by Azerbaijani Ambassador to Ankara Mehmet Nevruz Oglu and Bulent Ozgun, foreign economic relations director general for the Treasury and foreign trade. Ozgun said the loan will be used in the construction of a Foreign Relations Center to be established in Azerbaijan.
