Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tunis, Jan 8, SPA -- Turkish Defense Minister Mehmet Golhan left Tunis on Saturday after a three-day state visit to Tunisia during which he was received by President Zine El Abidine Ben Ali. The Tunisian [as received] minister also held talks with his Tunisian counterpart Abdelaziz Ben Dhia, Foreign Minister Habib Ben Yahya on ways to enhance the bilateral relations between the two countries as well as other matters of mutual interest.
