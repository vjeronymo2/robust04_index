920626 FT 26 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Kenwood offering 1.2 oversubscribed The public offering of shares in Kenwood, the kitchen appliances company which will begin trading on the market next week, was 1.2 subscribed when it closed on Wednesday. Applications up to 1,000 shares will be satisfied in full, as will applications from employees. Applicants who asked for more than 1,000 shares, however, will be scaled down to about 78.4 per cent of the amount they requested.
