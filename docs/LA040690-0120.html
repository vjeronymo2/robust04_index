April 6, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $20 Thursday when Oak Portal, the first half of his parlay, ran out of the money in the first race. Bob, who started the meeting with a mythical $1,000 bankroll, is now at $964.40.

Bob's bet today is a $9 triple from Colroy in the sixth to Chaperral Motel in the seventh to Preston, Dream Of Fame, Polar Boy, River Mike and Brave Capade in the eighth.

Thursday's Bankroll $984.40

Thursday's Loss $20
