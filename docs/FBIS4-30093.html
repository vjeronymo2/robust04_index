BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 22 (KCNA) -- Yang Hyong-sop, chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly [SPA], Tuesday met and conversed in a friendly atmosphere with the delegation of the Upper House of the Kingdom of Thailand headed by speaker of the Upper House Meechai Ruchupan, which paid a courtesy call on him. Present there were Vice-chairman of the SPA Paek In-chun and a minister of Thai Embassy in Pyongyang.
