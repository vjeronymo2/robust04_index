Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "More Federal Assembly Deputies"] [Text] The Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission has confirmed the results of the elections to the Federation Council in Tatarstan. Farit Mukhametshin, chairman of the Tatarstan Supreme Soviet, and Tatarstan President Mintimer Shaymiyev have officially been named deputies of the upper chamber of parliament. The official results of elections to the State Duma in five electoral districts on Tatarstan territory have also been summed up. Gennadiy Yegorov, member of the republic "Unity for Progress" party, has been elected for Almetyevskiy electoral district no. 23; journalist Oleg Morozov has been elected for Moskovskiy electoral district no. 24; Vladimir Altukhov, director of a private enterprise, has been elected for Naberezhno-chelnininskiy electoral district no. 25; Gabdulvakhit Bagautdinov, director of a children's home, has been elected for Nizhnekamskiy electoral district no. 26; and Valentin Mikhaylov, Republic of Tatarstan Supreme Soviet deputy, has been elected for Privolzhskiy electoral district no. 27. Thus only deputies from the Chechen Republic and Chelyabinsk Oblast have not been elected to the Russian Federation Federal Assembly.
