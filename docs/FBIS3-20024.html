Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] The death of [Mexican presidential candidate] Luis Donaldo Colosio was met with consternation, not only in Mexico but also abroad, where the White House was the first to condemn this assassination. In Spain, Foreign Minister Javier Solana said this morning in the Congress of Deputies that he regretted this death. [Begin Solana recording] I am shaken. When I got the news -- at 0500, when the Spanish ambassador in Mexico called me -- I was really dismayed, first of all, because of the loss of a very worthy man whom I had met and appreciated, and then because of what this means for Mexico from a political point of view. Mexico is a brother country, a country with whom we have so many relations. That is why I am shaken. [end recording]
