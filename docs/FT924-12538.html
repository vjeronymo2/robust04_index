921020 FT 20 OCT 92 / Brussels angry at Chernobyl restart The European Commission said yesterday it was deeply worried by Ukraine's decision to restart one of the three power plants at Chernobyl, the site of the world's worst nuclear accident in 1986, Reuter reports from Brussels. Leaks of radioactive gases at a similar plant in Lithuania in the last few days proved the danger of such reactors and the start-up at Chernobyl could only fuel anxiety in those two countries as well as across Europe, EC Commissioner Karel Van Miert said.
