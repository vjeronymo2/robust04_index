Language: English Article Type:BFN [From "News Bulletin" No. 457] [Text] Vilnius, March 15, ELTA -- Seimas [parliament] passed a law on land of the Republic of Lithuania on Tuesday [15 March]. The law was adopted by the left-wing majority in Seimas. The law on land regulates the ownership and exploitation of the land. It is believed that this law will help to carry out the agricultural reform in Lithuania.
