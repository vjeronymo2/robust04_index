Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Important agreements have been signed in Peru between Cuba and Peruvian institutions. These agreements between Cuba and the social security institutes from Lima and Arequipa include the exchange of technology, professionals, and patients. Professor Rodrigo Alvarez Cambras, director of the Cuban Orthopedics Hospital, said that he is visiting Peru at the invitation of the Peruvian Social Security Institute, and that for two weeks he toured several important towns. Accompanied by Dr. (Luis Fleitas), a Peruvian expert, Alvarez Cambras conducted several surgeries and gave seven outstanding lectures on external pin fixation, pseudarthrosis surgery, ligaments, and bone diseases. Alvarez Cambras said that technological and professional exchange will also take place between the two countries, in view of the fact that the two countries expressed great interest in this field.
