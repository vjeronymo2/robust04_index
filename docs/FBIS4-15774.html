BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe 11 April, TASS -- An unknown gunman opened submachine-gun fire on Monday at a multi-storey apartment house in Navoy street in downtown Dushanbe where high-ranking government official of Tajikistan live. One of the bullets hit the balcony of the apartment of Rashid Olimov, the foreign minister. Nobody was wounded. Aliyev is currently on a trip to Moscow. Police refused to say whether the attack was of a political or criminal character.
