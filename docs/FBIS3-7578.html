Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] Mr. Yeo Cheow Tong, the visiting Singapore trade and industry minister, and his delegation called on Lieutenant General Tun Kyi, member of the State Law and Order Restoration Council and minister of trade, at the latter's office at 1130. They held cordial and frank talks on improving investment opportunities, economic and technical cooperation, and bilateral trade. Trade Minister Lt. Gen. Tun Kyi then hosted a luncheon at the People's Park restaurant in honor of Minister Yeo Cheow Tong; Singapore departmental officials; Mr. Alan Yeo, chairman of the Singapore Trade Development Board; and Singapore businessmen.
