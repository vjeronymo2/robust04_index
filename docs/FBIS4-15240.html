BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] Literally a few minutes before the start of our program, the Information Television Agency received a statement issued by Russian President Boris Yeltsin, which expresses regret that the results of the voting in the Federation Council did not make it possible to adopt a final decision on the appointment of a procurator general. This outcome, the document says, does not mean that the former procurator general will return to his post; and thus Aleksey Ilyushenko will remain as acting procurator general. The president also considers that the Federation Council deputies who have been invested with the people's trust will find a suitable way out of the situation that has arisen.
