940908 FT 08 SEP 94 / World Trade News: Rolls-Royce wins order to build six Boeing airliners - Aviation industry contracts ROLLS-ROYCE, the UK aero-engine manufacturer, has won an order worth about Dollars 90m to supply engines for six Boeing 757 airliners for American Trans Air, the 10th largest US carrier, based in Indianapolis, writes Paul Betts. The order represents a switch by the US carrier from competing US manufacturer Pratt & Whitney.
