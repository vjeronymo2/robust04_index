940829 FT 29 AUG 94 / Arts (Openings): Edinburgh The TDK Round Midnight Jazz Festival adds to the frenzy of arts activity in Edinburgh this week. Main attractions include New York state-of-the-art neo-bop from tenorist Gary Crosby's Exile's Gate on Wednesday and from pianist Mulgrew Miller's trio on Saturday, both at the Queen's Hall. Sassy Scots vocalist Carol Kidd opens tonight.
