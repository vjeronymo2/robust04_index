Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, February 24 (KCNA) -- The Korea-Indonesia Friendship Parliamentary Group of the Supreme People's Assembly sent a message of sympathy to the parliamentary group for bilateral cooperation between the House of People's Representatives of the Republic of Indonesia and the Supreme People's Assembly of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea in connection with a heavy loss of lives and properties by an earthquake in Indonesia. The message expressed deep sympathy and consolation to the people in the afflicted area and hoped that the damages from the earthquake would be healed as soon as possible and the people's living be stabilized in Indonesia. The Central Committee of the DPRK Red Cross society sent a message of sympathy to the Red Cross society of Indonesia.
