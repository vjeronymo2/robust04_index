December 30, 1990, Sunday, San Diego County Edition

2 ARRESTED IN DEATH OF ESCONDIDO MAN

Two men were arrested Friday in Los Angeles in connection with the slaying of an Escondido man in September, police said.

Rafael Arreola Castillo, 35, was taken into custody without incident in front of his Los Angeles home on suspicion of murder in the shooting death of Luis Munoz Romero Sept. 3 during an argument at Gilbey's Bar in the 900 block of Valley Parkway.

Antonio Arrello, 36, was also arrested at Castillo's home and was held as an accessory to murder, police said.
