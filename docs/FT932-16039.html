930408 FT 08 APR 93 / Virgin doubles size of aircraft fleet VIRGIN Atlantic Airways, the UK long-haul airline, will double the size of its fleet by leasing four new Airbus A340-300 and four Boeing 747-400 aircraft from International Lease Financing Corp, part of American International Group. Mr Richard Branson (left), Virgin's chairman, is pictured yesterday with Mr Jean Pierson, Airbus Industrie's managing director. Virgin has also taken an option on a new-generation Boeing 777 from ILFC and is negotiating with Boeing to acquire up to four 777s for delivery after 1996. Report, Page 4.
