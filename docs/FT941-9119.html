940216 FT 16 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Bourne End makes pounds 21m purchase Bourne End Properties is buying three investment properties in Cardiff from Rightacres for a total of Pounds 21m. Bourne End said the pro-perties would increase cash flow immediately by Pounds 400,000. One has an income of Pounds 928,000, with income on another ex-pected to rise to Pounds 525,000 during this year. The third is still being built and has a guaranteed rental of Pounds 250,000.
