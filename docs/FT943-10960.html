940802 FT 02 AUG 94 / UK Company News: Pounds 3.66m acquisition for Barr & Wallace Barr & Wallace Arnold Trust, the motor distribution, leisure, holidays and fuel distribution group, is acquiring the freehold property and assets of Normand Bristol, a Mercedes-Benz dealership owned by the Normand Motor Group, for Pounds 3.66m cash on completion. For the year to February 28 Normand Bristol had turnover of Pounds 9.8m and operating profits of Pounds 360,000 after internal rental costs associated with the freehold property of Pounds 150,000. Net assets have a book value of Pounds 2.4m.
