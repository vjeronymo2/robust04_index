BFN [Text] Siham 'Asawati, a 37-year-old resident of Ramallah, was killed this morning by shots fired by IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldiers who were dispersing rioters in the center of the town. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the soldiers were dispersing stone throwers and rioters by shooting rubber bullets and using tear gas. The woman was seriously wounded in the head by a rubber bullet and taken to a Ramallah hospital, where she died of her wounds.
