February 5, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

SHOW OR NO SHOW?

Re the Jan. 29 Pop Eye item about the fire marshals closing the Palomino Club during a Joe Ely performance on Jan. 21:

Ely had played only six songs when the show was stopped. The Palomino refused to give us our money back. "You got a Joe Ely show," they said.

Ely, to his credit, played a few songs for the crowd outside. It took some of the sting out of the debacle, but it took none of the stink out of the Palomino.

RONALD BOYD, Culver City; ELAINE COSSMAN, Los Angeles; BEVERLY BRUINS, Santa Monica; PATRICK CURREN, Santa Monica; ROBERT CARTER, Canoga Park; BARBARA THOMPSON, Culver City
