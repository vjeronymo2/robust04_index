BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 25 (KCNA) -- President Kim Il-song of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea sent a message of greetings on April 24 to President Ali Hassan Mwinyi of the United Republic of Tanzania on the occasion of the 30th founding anniversary of the republic. President Kim Il-song in his message said the founding of the United Republic of Tanzania was an event of great importance in the history of the Tanzanian people and a great progress has been made in Tanzania in the work for national unity and the building of a new society in the past 30 years. He wished the president and people of Tanzania big success in the work for the independent development of the country and expressed the belief that the friendly and cooperative relations between the two countries would continue to develop on good terms.
