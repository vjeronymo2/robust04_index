BFN [Text] Ahvaz, Khuzestan Prov., May 7, IRNA -- United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) is to finance installation of 400 pre-fab residential units in a refugee camp near Shushtar in this southern province. Head of UNHCR office in Tehran Pierre Jean Bur said that pre-fab residential units would begin soon at the Mohsen Bani-Najjar refugee camp near Shushtar. He gave no exact date for the start of the project. He said the project will be implemented in cooperation with the UNHCR office based in Tehran and the foreign national section of the Khuzestan Governorate-General. Some 2,400 Iraqi refugees who have fled to Iran are now residing in the camp.
