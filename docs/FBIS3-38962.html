Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] India has ruled out any outside mediation in the Kashmir issue. Addressing a news conference in Geneva, the finance minister, Dr. Manmohan Singh, said that mechanism available under the Simla agreement for resolving all differences between India and Pakistan has not been exhausted yet. Observing that New Delhi recognizes the domestic compulsions of the Pakistan prime minister for her anti-India tirade, Dr. Manmohan Singh said the problem can be resolved only through negotiations.
