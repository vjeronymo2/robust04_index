941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Edinburgh Japan Net asset value per share at Edinburgh Japan Trust dropped by 12 per cent to 165p at November 30, against 188p six months earlier. The result compared with a 7.6 per cent fall in the Tokyo First Section Index, in sterling terms. Net deficit for the six months was again Pounds 29,000 with losses per share unchanged at 0.29p.
