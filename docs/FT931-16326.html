930107 FT 07 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Taylor Woodrow data graphics sale Microfilm Reprographics has acquired Taywood Data Graphics, part of Taylor Woodrow Construction, for Pounds 1.84m cash. Taywood had turnover of Pounds 2.39m and gross profit of Pounds 310,000 in the 11 months to November 30. It specialises in converting mapping and graphical information into digital form.
