940324 FT 24 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Victaulic makes Pounds 3.4m acquisition Victaulic, the pipeline products maker, has acquired CE Heinke, a wholly-owned subsidiary of Siebe, for Pounds 3.37m. CE Heinke, which makes rubber moulded and extruded products, made profits before interest and tax of Pounds 281,000 on turnover of Pounds 4m in the year to April 3 1993. Included in the price is Pounds 800,000 for freehold properties.
