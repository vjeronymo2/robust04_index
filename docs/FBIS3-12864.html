Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Denis Barbet, second counselor at the French Embassy, has denounced the explosion at the Maronite church. He said it is up to the Lebanese authorities to determine the circumstances and reasons behind the incident. The French official was speaking after a visit to the Bustrus Palace, where he handed the Foreign Ministry's secretary general a message from French Foreign Minister Alain Juppe to Minister Faris Buwayz. He declined to reveal the content of the message, leaving this up to Buwayz.
