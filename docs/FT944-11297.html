941107 FT 07 NOV 94 / Company News (this Week): Sharp recovery for insurance sector British Airports Authority - Other Companies The City forecasts pre-tax profits will rise from Pounds 237m to about Pounds 262m in the first half. The group, which operates seven UK airports, is expected to show strong growth in traffic when it reports on Monday. Analysts will be looking to see if Stansted has moved into profit.
