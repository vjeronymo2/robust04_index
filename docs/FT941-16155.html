940112 FT 12 JAN 94 / Zambia cabinet shake-up President Frederick Chiluba shook up Zambia's cabinet yesterday after two ministers resigned over allegations of drug trafficking, Reuter reports from Lusaka. Mr Remmy Muthoosa was named to replace Foreign Affairs Minister Vernon Mwaanga, who quit last week. At the Ministry of Community Development and Social Services, Princess Nakatindi Wina quit on Saturday, as did her husband, Simota Wina, national assembly deputy speaker. All three denied the allegations. Mr Chitalu Sampa replaced Mr Newstead Zimba as home affairs minister; Mr Dipak Patel became commerce minister.
