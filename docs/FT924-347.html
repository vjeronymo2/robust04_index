921229 FT 29 DEC 92 / Moscow fails to pay farm loans RUSSIA has failed to make Dollars 95.7m (Pounds 61.2m) in payments on US-government backed loans used to buy grain and farm products, the US Agriculture Department said yesterday, AP reports from Washington. Russia missed additional payments totalling Dollars 27.4m on top of Dollars 68.3m already in arrears to six US and foreign banks. Russia has been missing payments since early December, and as a result has been suspended from a government export credit programme.
