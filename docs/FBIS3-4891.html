Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, March 1 (KPL) -- Prime Minister Khamtai Siphandon received here yesterday Ajit Singh, ASEAN general secretary, who is on a four-day official visit to Laos at the invitation of the Lao foreign minister. On behalf of the Lao Government and people, Khamtai Siphandon congratulated and warmly welcomed Ajit Singh, and hoped that his visit to Laos contributed to the promotion of the relations of friendship and cooperation between Laos and member countries of the Association of the Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN). [passage omitted] The Lao prime minister requested the ASEAN general secretary to create favourable conditions for the acceptance of the Lao PDR [People's Democratic Republic] to ASEAN membership at a proper time. Ajit Singh thanked the warm welcome and informed Khamtai Siphandon of the result of the meeting between the ASEAN general secretary and the delegation of the Lao Foreign Ministry. The discussion dealt with matters of interest in the area and in the region. [passage omitted]
