BFN [Text] Individual-run and privately owned enterprises have become important network centers of economic development in Liaoning. The province of Liaoning is noted for its large number of large and medium-sized enterprises and also for its rich actual strength of publicly owned enterprises. In line with the demand of developing the socialist market economy, the province has formulated policies and measures for developing individual-run and privately owned enterprises over the past few years and created a relaxed environment to encourage the rapid development of such enterprises. By the end of 1993, the province's number of individual-run and privately owned enterprises reached 745,000. The industrial output value scored by these enterprises reached 11.8 billion yuan and accounted for 4.22 percent in the province's total industrial output value. The volume of retail sales scored by these enterprises reached 22.2 billion yuan and accounted for (?40.2) percent in the province's total volume of retail sales. They provided 1.62 billion yuan of taxes for the province, which accounted for 6.7 percent in the province's financial and tax revenues.
