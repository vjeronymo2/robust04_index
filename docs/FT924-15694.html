921002 FT 02 OCT 92 / Survey of Software at Work - Autumn 1992 (34): CCN Marketing -Supplier Profile CCN Marketing was founded in the UK in 1989 and is part of the CCN Group. Turnover: Pounds 61.4m in 1990/91 and Pounds 66.8m in 1991/92. The company has about 1,000 employees in the UK. Specialist vertical markets: retailing, financial, media and leisure, but CCN's services can apply to consumer markets in any sector. Software products: include Mosaic Systems and a range of PC and mainframe products for database marketing; these are called Oasis, Relations, and Tactics. Mosaic provides personal computer market analysis software for mapping and reporting on catchment areas, geographical regions, media regions and customer files. They are used for branch development, network evaluation, merchandise planning and planning of promotional activity. Hardware and operating systems: PC to mainframe. Services cover consultancy, system development training and maintenance. User comments: 'Expertise of bureau staff . . . regular data and technology updates.'
