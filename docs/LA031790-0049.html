March 17, 1990, Saturday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY EARNINGS

Tricare Inc.: The Santa Ana occupational health care services company said earnings for its fiscal 1990 third quarter increased 23% to $319,000 from $259,000 for the corresponding period in 1989.

Revenue for the quarter ended Feb. 28 rose 16% to $5 million from $4.3 million.

For the first nine months of fiscal 1990, net income was $1.1 million, 21% higher than the $892,000 posted a year earlier. Revenue rose 17% to $14.9 million from $12.8 million.
