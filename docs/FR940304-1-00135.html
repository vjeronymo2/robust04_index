Economic Justification A. Economic impact on manufacturers and consumers . The per unit increased cost to manufacturers to meet the level 1 efficiency for gas water heaters is zero since it is the same as the baseline; to meet level 2 the manufacturers' cost increase is $5.47, levels 3 and 4 are $18.19, levels 5 and 6 are $28.57, level 7 is $72.14, and level 8 is $358.23. The per-unit increased cost to manufacturers to meet the level 1 efficiency for electric water heaters is $5.32; to meet level 2 the manufacturers' cost increase is $14.33, level 3 is $201.82, levels 4 and 5 are $210.83, and levels 6 through 8 are $585.06.
