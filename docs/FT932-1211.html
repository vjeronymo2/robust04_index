930624 FT 24 JUN 93 / International Company News: French bid by IBM succeeds INTERNATIONAL Business Machines, the world's largest manufacturer of computers, said its tender for CGI Informatique, a French computing services company, had been successful, writes Alan Cane. La Societe des Bourses Francaises said 98.11 per cent of the convertible bonds offered by IBM France had been accepted by CGI shareholders. The bid values the French company at just over Dollars 461m.
