March 31, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

JUST COLUMNING 'EM THE WAY HE SEES 'EM

There should be more Larry Stewart columns and less Allan Malamud in the Times sports section.

Larry's TV-Radio analysis and overall wit far surpass Malamud's overrated Notes on a Scorecard. Look-alikes: Notes on a Scorecard and Dear Abby.

And Tank McNamara's cartoons are about as funny as Keith Olbermann's punch lines.

RANDY LARSCHEID

Westwood
