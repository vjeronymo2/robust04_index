Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Nairobi, 17 March (AFP) -- General Mohamed Farah Aidid and his main rival for power in Somalia, self-styled interim president Ali Mahdi Mohamed, met Thursday [17 March] in the Kenyan capital Nairobi, UN spokesman George Bennet said. Bennet said the meeting was the first since December 1992 between the two men, whose rivalry has caused a serious threat of renewed civil war in Somalia following the current withdrawal of U.S. and other Western troops posted there.
