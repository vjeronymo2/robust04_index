940928 FT 28 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Clayhithe offer Clayhithe, the finance and management provider, has made a recommended offer for the 29.5 per cent of Horstmann not already owned by it, for up to Pounds 4.2m cash. The offer of 600p per share values Horstmann, which makes electronic timers and controls and data acquisition and controls systems, at Pounds 14.1m. There is a share alternative on the basis of 19 new Clayhithe ordinary shares for every two Horstmann held. For the year to March 31 1994 Horstmann's pre-tax profits were Pounds 2.5m on turnover of Pounds 19.9m. Clayhithe announced earlier this year that it intended to become a specialised electronics and engineering company focused on Horstmann.
