BFN [Text] Tokyo, April 1 KYODO -- Two gunmen entered the Tokyo headquarters of the national daily ASAHI SHIMBUN at about 1:30 p.m. Friday [1 April] and took 10 of the company's employees hostage on the building's 15th floor, police said. Police said the gunmen were right-wingers and were also armed with a sword and dynamite. Police said one of the men had fired a shot from a gun.
