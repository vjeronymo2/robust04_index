930623 FT 23 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Endorsement for British carmakers COMMONS LEADER Mr Tony Newton yesterday praised British carmakers after Jaguar's success in the Le Mans 24-hour race at the weekend. Mr Newton also praised other British car manufacturers in recent months who had brought about a large increase in car production. He said: 'With the British car market now growing while Continental markets are plunging into recession, our industry has revitalised itself and is set to win.'
