Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] Silvio Berlusconi has announced his candidacy on air. He has just announced his entry into politics. He will stand for Forza Italia at the forthcoming elections because, he said, he no longer wants to live in a country governed by immature forces, and desires to fight on the side of freedom as an alternative to the left. Berlusconi sent a video recording to public and private sector news bulletins. Here is the passage concerning his candidacy: [Begin Berlusconi recording] I have decided to enter the fray and to concern myself with public affairs because I do not want to live in a country which is not liberal and is governed by immature forces and by men tied twice over to past political and economic failure. In order to live the new life I have chosen, today I tendered my resignation from all company positions in the group that I founded. I am therefore giving up my role as editor and businessman to put my experience and all my efforts at the disposal of a battle in which I believe with the utmost conviction and with the utmost strength. [end recording]
