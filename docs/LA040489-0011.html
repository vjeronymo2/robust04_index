April 4, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"After Officer Dickey had physical control of the situation, he lost emotional control of himself and acted in an unprofessional manner."

-- Dist. Atty. Ira Reiner, announcing the filing of misdemeanor charges against Long Beach Police Officers Mark Dickey and Mark Ramsey, whose videotaped, window-busting confrontation with black activist Don Jackson became a national controversy.
