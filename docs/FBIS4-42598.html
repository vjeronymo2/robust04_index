BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] The United States has submitted to Ankara a draft agreement on the transfer of the defense fund to Turkey. In light of the talks held in Washington, the proposal was conveyed to Ambassador Volkan Vural, chief adviser to the prime minister, by the U.S. Embassy in Ankara. A statement issued by the Press Center of the Prime Minister's Office noted that the proposal is being assessed by the relevant authorities.
