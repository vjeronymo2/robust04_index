Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Abkhazian troops early on Monday thwarted an attempt by Georgian sabotage groups to try to blow up a bridge across the Inguri River separating the two confronting sides, the Abkhazian Defense Ministry said. All of the saboteurs were taken prisoner. It says the attack followed a 30-minute mortar fire on Abkhazian positions close to the village of Tagiloni. There were casualties among Georgian civilians. The ministry says the Abkhazian army has continued operations to wipe out Georgian sabotage groups infiltrating the Gal region.
