920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / EC go-ahead for steel venture EC competition authorities yesterday gave the go-ahead to two joint ventures in the steel and telecommunications sectors, reports Andrew Hill in Brussels. The European Commission has cleared Northern Telecom of Canada to proceed with its FFr700m (pounds73m) investment in Matra Communications. Brussels also cleared Pechiney Electrometallurgie to combine coated wire operations with SKW.
