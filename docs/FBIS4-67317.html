BFN [Text] Bucharest, ROMPRES, 16/5/1994 -- The Bucharest Court heard in the 16 May public session a part of the defendants in the trial of the Dogmanov group standing charges of drug traffic, forgery and use of forgery and traffic of weapons and ammunition. The defendants, two of whom are Colombian citizens, explained how they got acquainted abroad and started the drug traffic connection. They managed as early as 1990 to bring several consignments of drugs in Romania, using, among other things a marble table and 21 pictures. The last consignment, containing about 100 kg cocaine, was concealed in the pedestals of two statutes. The Romanian police managed to trace them and arrested the whole connection, led by Romanian Alexandru Sava Dogmanov. The defendants heard by the court today pleaded guilty of a part of the offenses.
