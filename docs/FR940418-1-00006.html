DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION Federal Aviation Administration 14 CFR Part 71 [Airspace Docket No. 94&hyph;ASO&hyph;3] Proposed Amendment of Offshore Airspace Area, San Juan, PR AGENCY: Federal Aviation Administration (FAA), DOT. ACTION: Notice of proposed rulemaking.

SUMMARY: This notice proposes to amend the Offshore Airspace Area at Puerto Rico. This action would lower the base of the San Juan Offshore Airspace Area from 5500 feet MSL to 2500 feet MSL. The intended effect is to lower the base of the Offshore Airspace Area to provide sufficient controlled airspace for instrument flight rule (IFR) operations, and to create a uniform base of controlled airspace in the area.
