June 18, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

WE'RE NO. 1: Orange County is "the Mecca of mail fraud," says postal inspector Kacy McClelland (D6). "When you've got your scheme perfected, here's the place you put it in motion." . . . Most crooks either rent space in Newport Beach or get a mail drop so they can assume a Newport address. . . . "They are selling a dream, the California promise: 'You can be rich quick and live here like we do in Newport -- the good life,' " he says. James S. Granelli and Tom McQueeney
