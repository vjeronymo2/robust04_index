Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Paris, March 18 (AFP) -- French Prime Minister Edouard Balladur met on Friday morning [18 March] with European Commission president Jacques Delors to discuss "all European questions", Delors said after the talk. He said that during their meeting lasting almost 45 minutes, the two men had discussed "fishing, efforts the community is going to make to help in restructuring and modernizing the French fishing fleet, and the enlargement of the European Union and institutional problems relating to that issue". He added that they had discussed the situation of Air France airline, the national carrier. Both men are potential French presidential candidates in next year's elections.
