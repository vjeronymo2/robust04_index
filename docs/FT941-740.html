940329 FT 29 MAR 94 / Gummer opens extension to Docklands Light Railway Environment secretary John Gummer yesterday opened the five-mile extension to the Docklands Light Railway to Beckton in east London after a delay of 18 months. The Pounds 280m project to extend the railway has been dogged by problems with construction and systems installation. The extension, which connects Beckton with the Isle of Dogs and the City, is funded by the London Docklands Development Corporation. Mr Gummer also launched the West Silvertown Urban Village, where he took the controls of a mechanical digger (above).
