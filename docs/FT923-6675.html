920822 FT 22 AUG 92 / World Commodities Prices: Spices Indonesian cassia prices were firmer this week with spot supplies in Europe very tight, reports Man Producten. Va/ka sticks were quoted at USDollars 2,275 a tonne and kb broken/cleaned at Dollars 1,525 a tonne; shipment prices were respectivcely Dollars 1,970 and Dollars 1,325 a tonne, cif. Seychelles cinnamon was offered at Dollars 1,300 a tonne; Indonesian nutmeg and mace prices were lower - Bwp at Dollars 850 a tonne, shrivels at Dollars 1,300 a tonne and abcd nutmegs at Dollars 1,550 a tonne; and mace at Dollars 1,600 a tonne; all prices on cif terms. Madras fingers were quited at Dollars 1,275 a tonne, cif, and turmeric powder at Dollars 1,390.
