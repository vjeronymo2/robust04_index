Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN ["Presidential Decree No. 17 of the People's Republic of China" -- XINHUA headline] [TEXT] Beijing, 30 Dec (XINHUA) -- The "Decision of the National People's Congress [NPC] Standing Committee on Amending `The Accountancy Law of the People's Republic of China'" was adopted by the fifth meeting of the PRC's Eighth NPC Standing Committee on 29 December 1993 and is hereby promulgated. It will be implemented on the date of its promulgation. [Signed] PRC President Jiang Zemin [Dated] 29 December 1993
