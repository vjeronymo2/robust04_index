921006 FT 06 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Conder sells second division to Miller Miller Group, one of the UK's largest private companies, has purchased the second of the three main divisions of Conder Group, the construction company which went into receivership last month, for an undisclosed sum. Miller, Group, a family-run construction business has acquired Conder Projects, which designs and constructs buildings to order. At the weekend, Conder's receivers sold the company's steel fabricating business for 'a significant seven figure sum' to another private company, Melton Medes, run by Indian-born entrepreneur Mr Nathu Ram Puri. The receivers are believed to be in advanced talks on the disposal of the final of the three divisions, Conder IEI. It was the collapse of talks with Seaboard, the power company, on the sale of this heating and ventilation business that which eventually forced Conder into receivership.
