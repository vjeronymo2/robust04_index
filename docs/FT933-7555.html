930820 FT 20 AUG 93 / UK Company News: County Smaller net assets rise Net asset value per ordinary share of the County Smaller Companies Investment Trust stood at 100.69p at July 1. That compared with the 93.63p standing at June 30 1992 which covered the period from incorporation on April 10 1991. Available revenue for the year to end-June totalled Pounds 556,334 (Pounds 518,941 for period). A second interim dividend of 1.875p, in lieu of a final, makes a 3p (same) total.
