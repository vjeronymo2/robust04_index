Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Andrey Palariya and Vladimir Smelov] [Text] Moscow, 28 Feb -- CIA agent James Morris, who worked in Moscow under the guise of a counsellor at the U.S. embassy, has been declared "persona non grata." An ITAR-TASS correspondent was informed by reliable sources that an official statement about this was made today to U.S. ambassador in Moscow Thomas Pickering. The U.S. spy will leave Moscow within seven days. This was Moscow's reaction to Washington's decision to expel Aleksandr Lysenko, counsellor at the Russian embassy, from the United States.
