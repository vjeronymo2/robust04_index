910430 FT 30 APR 91 / Egypt condemns Israeli policy IN ITS MOST harshly-worded attack since the end of the Gulf war, Egypt yesterday accused Israel of blocking peace efforts. A Foreign Ministry spokesman said building settlements in occupied territories was 'aimed at trying to abort .. efforts to promote a peace settlement', and Mr Boutros Ghali, minister of state for foreign affairs, said Israel was creating obstacles over the make-up of the Palestinian delegation to a peace conference proposed by Mr James Baker, US secretary of state.
