930402 FT 02 APR 93 / Lisbon broker to face charges Portugal's former leading stockbroker, Mr Pedro Caldeira, who fled the country leaving millions of dollars of debts in his wake, was extradited back to Lisbon yesterday from the US, Reuter reports from Lisbon . He faces charges of siphoning off Esc440m (Pounds 2m) from his Lisbon firm of stockbrokers, Pedro Caldeira Sociedade Corretora, and failing to pay millions of dollars owed to a bank and several other creditors.
