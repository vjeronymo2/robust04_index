BFN [Text] We have received a report that elections in all 14 constituencies in Lvov Oblast were valid. The average turnout in the oblast in the second round of elections was 80 percent. Deputy's mandates were won by representatives of Rukh, the Ukrainian National Assembly-Ukrainian Self-Defense, the Congress of Ukrainian Nationalists, the Ukrainian Republican Party, and the Democratic Party. In three constituencies, neither of the candidates won the required majority. Among former people's deputies who were re-elected are Dmytro Chobit and Taras Stetskiv. Larysa Skoryk, Orest Vlokh and Iryna Kalynets lost the election.
