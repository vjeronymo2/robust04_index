February 21, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: GRAMMY WATCH

Bette and Stevie Sing at Grammys: Bette Midler, Stevie Wonder and the Eurythmics' David Stewart were added Tuesday to the roster appearing on the 32nd Annual Grammy Awards show on CBS tonight at 8.

Midler, a nominee for record of the year and best female pop vocal performance, will perform "Wind Beneath My Wings." Wonder, a 17-time Grammy winner, will sing "We Can Work It Out" as part of the special tribute to Paul McCartney. Stewart will be a presenter during the delayed telecast. BETH KLEID
