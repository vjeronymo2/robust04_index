931023 FT 23 OCT 93 / Finance and the Family: Investment trust record - At a Glance Investment trust savings schemes attracted a record Pounds 64.46m during the third quarter of 1993, compared with Pounds 53.89m in the previous three months. Money invested in savings schemes in 1993 has already outstripped the full year figure for 1992. The number of regular savings accounts increased from 62,831 to 64,542, and the average monthly sum invetsed rose from Pounds 75 to Pounds 82.
