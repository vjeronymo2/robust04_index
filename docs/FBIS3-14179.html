Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] It was announced in Nairobi today that the peace negotiations between the government and the two rebel factions under the auspices of the Intergovernmental Authority on Drought and Development [IGADD] states had been adjourned until 16 May. Our reporter in Nairobi informed us that the meeting between the negotiating parties and the council of the IGADD states continued last night and ended this morning. The participants agreed to form a higher committee to discuss the provision of relief to the war afflicted. Our reporter added that agreement was also reached on the general principles to form a basis for solving the southern issue and establishing a mechanism for implementing these principles.
