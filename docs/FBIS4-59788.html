BFN [Text] Talks between Chamber of Deputies speaker, Habib Boulares, and Yemeni Ambassador to Tunisia Ahmad Muhamad Haydar were held today at the Bardo Palace, and focused on the situation in Yemen, the efforts under way to prevent further bloodshed, and means to preserve this brotherly country's unity and security. The Yemeni ambassador praised President Zine Al-Abidine Ben Ali's stance and his call for a discussion of means of common action and efforts to bring about harmony in Yemen and to save Yemen from all that could threaten its unity and security.
