Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, has received a message from his brother His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, president of the fraternal United Arab Emirates [UAE]. The message deals with brotherly relations between the two countries, as well as issues of interest to our Arab nation. The message also includes ways of bolstering brotherly cooperation with our country, unifying the word of the brothers, purifying the Arab atmosphere, and restoring Arab solidarity and cooperation. The message was conveyed by UAE Ambassador in Sanaa Sayf al-Mansuri, who met the brother president today.
