Language: Azeri Article Type:BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Gasan Gasanov, who is on an official visit to Tehran, has said in an interview that the objective of his visit is to hold talks with the Islamic Republic of Iran's leaders and exchange views on the memorandums that were drawn up for [word indistinct] and cooperation between Azerbaijan and Iran when President Hashemi-Rafsanjani visited Baku. Gasan Gasanov held talks with President Hashemi-Rafsanjani and Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati. Special attention was focused on matters related to the dispute and fighting between Azerbaijan and Armenia. It was noted that the establishment of durable peace on the basis of mutual respect for each other's independence and territorial integrity is a decisive factor for the future of all the countries in the region.
