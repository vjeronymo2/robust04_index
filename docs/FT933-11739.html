930728 FT 28 JUL 93 / People: Cohen leaves board of Arlen Paul Cohen has left the board of Arlen after barely a year as chief executive following protracted internal arguments over strategy within the Slough-based electrical accessories group. Cohen opposed his board's moves to bring in Fortress Trust, a private investment company, as a 30 per cent stake-holder. With Cohen's departure, the Fortress Trust deal is set to go ahead, and may be announced as soon as today. Arlen's shares were trading at 14p when the Fortress Trust talks were first revealed. They are now up at 33p.
