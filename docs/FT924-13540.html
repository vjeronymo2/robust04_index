921015 FT 15 OCT 92 / Westland EH101 helicopter available A PRE-PRODUCTION model of Westland's EH101 anti-submarine and troop-carrying helicopter was made available yesterday at the company's Yeovil headquarters for a brief flight by Mr Michael Heseltine, the trade and industry secretary. The aircraft, developed in collaboration with the Italian company Agusta, has already been ordered by the Royal Navy, with the Italian and Canadian governments indicating their intentions to place orders in the near future. Mr Heseltine originally signed the contract authorising the go-ahead of the EH101 programme in 1984 and the future of Westland lay at the heart of the cabinet row that resulted in his resignation from the government in 1986. He said: 'I am keen to see EH101 achieve the further major export success it deserves.'
