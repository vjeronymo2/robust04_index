Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed POSTFACTUM report: "Andrey Kozyrev Again Talks About a Party of War"] [Text] Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev has declared that Russia has no territorial claims on any state, at the same time admitting the presence of such claims to Russia on the part of the Baltics. "It is high time to stop dividing and tearing apart the once single space of the former USSR. The party of war, represented by the nationalist opposition, is using the slogan of vital interests as a pretext for provoking a war. The party of peace, to which I attribute myself, on the other hand, intends to build friendly relations with all states of the former Union," the minister said.
