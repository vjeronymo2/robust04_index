BFN [Text] The Russian Federation Social Security Ministry has confirmed that from May Day, implementation will be continued of President Boris Yeltsin's decree on raising the previously fixed retirement pensions, which started on 1 April. This statement was distributed today in connection with the appearance in some mass media of a so-called denial by Russia's Pension Fund regarding the indexation of pensions on May Day. In short, pensions will go up -- that is the most important thing.
