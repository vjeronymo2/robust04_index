BFN [Text] The cabinet last night approved the draft state budget for the fiscal year 1994-95 in preparation for submission to the People's Assembly. The total budget is 70.541 billion pounds. The cabinet also approved the draft investment plan for 1994-95, which is the third year of the current Five-Year Plan. The total investments will be about 35 billion pounds. During its meeting under Prime Minister Dr. 'Atif Sidqi, which continued until late last night, the cabinet reviewed the results of President Husni Mubarak's visits to Oman, China, the United Arab Emirates, Bahrain, and Saudi Arabia. The cabinet adopted a number of recommendations for implementation of what was agreed upon between President Mubarak and the leaders of these states.
