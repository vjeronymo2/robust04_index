May 22, 1989, Monday, P.M. Final

NATION;

CARGO PLANE CRASHES; 3 KILLED

A sputtering DC-3 cargo plane crashed into a field near De Kalb today, killing all three people aboard.

The plane belonging to Condor Enterprise of Aurora crashed about 10:45 a.m. five miles southwest of the city, said Barbara Dettmer, a spokeswoman for the Federal Aviation Administration.

Three bodies were recovered, but they have not been identified.
