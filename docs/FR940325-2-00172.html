Advisory Committee for Polar Programs; Meeting In accordance with the Federal Advisory Committee Act (Pub. L. 92&hyph;463, as amended), the National Science Foundation announces the following meeting. Name: Advisory Committee for Polar Programs. Date and Time: April 14 and 15, 1994, 8:30 a.m.&hyph;5 p.m. each day. Place: Room 340, National Science Foundation, 4201 Wilson Boulevard, Arlington, VA. Type of Meeting: Open. Contact Person: Dr. Dennis S. Peacock, Science Section Head, Office of Polar Programs, room 755, National Science Foundation, 4201 Wilson Boulevard, Arlington, VA 22230. Telephone: (703) 306&hyph;1033. Minutes: May be obtained from the contact person listed above. Purpose of Meeting: Serves to provide expert advice to the U.S. Antarctic Programs and the Arctic Program, including advice on science programs, polar operations support, budgetary planning, and polar coordination and information. Agenda: The OPP Advisory Committee meets on April 14/15 to discuss the following agenda topics_FY 94/95 Budget, Congressional Update, Organization and Long Range Planning (LRP). The LRP topic will include presentations on Facilities, operations/logistics and discussion of science priorities. Dated: March 21, 1994. M. Rebecca Winkler, Committee Management Officer.
