Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] A NATIONAL NEWS AGENCY correspondent in al-Biqa' has reported that a police force from the antinarcotics bureau of Zahlah District today raided the house of Shakir 'Umran al-Buraydi, 30, who is from 'Irsal but currently resides in Qabb Ilyas. During the sweep, al-Buraydi opened fire at the patrol, killing three policemen on the spot. They are Mustafa Faris, Shawqi 'Awn, and Samir al- Mu'allim. Mu'tazz Abu-Fayd was seriously wounded. An Internal Security force under the command of Colonel Hasan Hallaq, commander of the al-Biqa' military zone, with the support of a Lebanese Army force, arrived immediately after the crime and began sweeps in Qabb Ilyas. Roadblocks were also set up in the area by the Internal Security Forces and the Army.
