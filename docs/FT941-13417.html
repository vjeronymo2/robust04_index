940126 FT 26 JAN 94 / Australian prices edge up Australia's consumer price index rose 0.2 per cent in the last quarter of 1993, for an annual rate of 1.9 per cent, writes Nikki Tait in Sydney. The figure was at the lower end of market expectations and prompted Mr Ralph Willis, the treasurer, to concede that the government's budget forecast of a 3.5 per cent rise in prices in 1993-94 was 'considerably overstated'.
