Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] Reports from the airport say that the crew and passengers were allowed to disembark and are being looked after in the VIP pavilion at the airport. Officials of the airline in Nairobi are reported to have refused to talk to the press. A KTN reporter says that 130 Ethiopians were aboard the plane, an A-300/600 [as heard] Airbus. The Ethiopians were being deported from Jiddah to Addis Ababa. Also on board were nine international passengers. Three hijackers are reported to have commandeered the airbus to Nairobi. One hijacker disembarked with the passengers, while the other two are reported to be still aboard the plane. The hijackers' nationalities were not immediately known.
