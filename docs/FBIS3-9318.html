Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, March 18 (ATA) -- The Supreme Court of Appeal decided to change the decision of the Court of Appeal for 10 former senior officials of the Party of Labour of Albania, the spokesman of the court said. Just like the decision of the Tirana District Court, the new decision too considers guilty the following: Qirjako Mihali, Pali Miska, Besnik Bekteshi, Vangjel Cerrava, Prokop Murra, Llambi Gegprifti, Muho Asllani, Hajredin Celiku, Foto Cami, and Lenka Cuko for having violated the equality of citizens through the creation of privileges for themselves. In a second consideration of the case by the Court of Appeal, these former leaders were accused of the crime of abuse of office. The spokesman explains that the Supreme Court of Appeal changed the decision of the Court of Appeal and sent the case for another consideration by the first level court. According to the decision of the Supreme Court of Appeal, the civil indictment will be dismissed by the obligation that all the defendants will pay back respective amounts of money to the Government Service Department. Sentences for the defendants range from 4-7 years of imprisonment.
