BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin has declared that as far as Turkey is concerned, the confidence-building measures [CBM] package in Cyprus is not dead yet. During a chat with reporters in Istanbul last night, Cetin responded to Greek Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias' statement that the CBM's are dead. Commenting on the negotiating process concerning the CBM's, Cetin declared: The package is not fully alive, but it has not died yet. As far as we are concerned, the process is continuing. In his news conference in Istanbul yesterday, Papoulias blamed the Turkish side for the failure to implement the CBM package and claimed that the package is dead.
