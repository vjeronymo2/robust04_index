Language: Belarusian Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] The Strike Committee at its meeting on 2 February resolved to form worker detachments for maintaining order during the strikes. It was also resolved to request the Belarusian Association of Servicemen to help maintain order. According to BELAPAN, Strike Committee representatives intend to request that the Armed Forces and internal troops not interfere in the upcoming events. A meeting with representatives of a number of political parties and trade unions is also scheduled for 7 February, during which the members of the Council of the Strike Commitees will detail their protest action. Due to organizational reasons, BELAPAN has not been informed of the list of enterprises that agreed to participate in the political strike. However, one can assuredly say that they include the largest enterprises of the country.
