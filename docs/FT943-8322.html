940818 FT 18 AUG 94 / Mideast peace talks rescue Senior Israeli and Palestinian officials met in Egypt yesterday to rescue peace negotiations from collapse amid mounting tension over speeding up Palestinian self-rule to the West Bank and the unstable security situation in the Gaza Strip, Julian Ozanne reports from Jerusalem. Mr Shimon Peres, Israeli foreign minister, said before the meeting in the Mediterranean city of Alexandria that 'the Palestinian side and we are struggling very hard so that this thing will not collapse'. He said attacks on Israelis by Palestinian militant and deteriorating economic conditions of Palestinians in Gaza are 'threatening' the self-rule agreement.
