BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 12 (KCNA) -- The international chuche idea seminar on independence, peace and friendship which had been held in New Delhi in celebration of the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song closed on Sunday. At the closing ceremony "Kimilsongism, Banner of Independence, Peace, Progress and Prosperity", a book published in India on the occasion of the birthday of President Kim Il-song, was introduced to the participants and a congratulatory message of a congratulatory group of the Indian youth read out. Letters to the great leader President Kim Il-song and the dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il were adopted at the closing ceremony. Then followed the adoption of a letter to the Indian president, Shankar Dayal Sharma, and a declaration of the seminar. Shuhachi Inoue, director general of the International Institute of the Chuche Idea, who is the "International Kim Il-song Prize" winner, in his closing address stressed that the current seminar was a historical gathering that vividly proved the validity and invincible vitality of the chuche idea through the successful discussion of the theoretical and practical questions and ways in opposing the imperialist aggression and arbitrariness and building an independent, peaceful new world on the basis of the immortal chuche idea. On the same day, a banquet was arranged in New Delhi in celebration of the birthday of President Kim Il-song.
