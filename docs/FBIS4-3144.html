BFN [By reporters Zhu Youdi (2612 1635 2769) and Yang Zhaobo (2799 0340 3134)] [Text] Beijing, 15 Apr (XINHUA) -- Responding to questions raised by reporters attending a news conference that the State Council Information Office held for Chinese and foreign reporters concerning "China's agenda for the 21st century," Deng Nan, vice minister in charge of the State Science and Technology Commission and Deng Xiaoping's daughter, said: "Deng Xiaoping is very healthy [shen ti hen jian kang 6500 7555 1771 0256 1660] and he is concerned with China's construction. He is also very much concerned with the implementation of all of `China's agenda for the 21st century.' My father still makes it a routine practice each day to finish work. He reads a lot, and he walks. We even played bridge the day before yesterday." During the news conference, many overseas reporters asked about Deng Xiaoping's health. Answering a question raised by a Hong Kong reporter, Deng Nan said: "I understand your concern for my father's health. We are used to all kinds of talk about his health."
