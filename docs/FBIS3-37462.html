Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Moscow, 16 Feb (ITAR-TASS) -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin has had a telephone conversation with FRG Chancellor Helmut Kohl. Some topical questions of bilateral relations and international policy were discussed. The president's press service reports that both leaders spoke in favor of establishing full-scale cooperation between the G-7 countries and Russia, and its involvement in foreign policy discussions already at the present stage. The main theme of the conversation was an exchange of opinions on the Yugoslav problem. The Russian president stressed that it is necessary to settle the crisis in accordance with UN decisions. Boris Yeltsin and Helmut Kohl agreed to continue the dialogue on the situation in Bosnia in the nearest future and to discuss possibilities for new inititiatives toward a peaceful settlement of the conflict.
