930119 FT 19 JAN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Tea There was strong general demand, reports the Tea Broker's Association. Coloury Assams ruled fully firm to often dearer, but plainer types were irregular. Brighter Africans sold well with prices 5 to 10p higher, while mediums remained fully firm. Offshore teas met good competition at generally dearer rates. Quality 210p/kg, good medium 182p/kg, medium 165p/kg, low medium 128p/kg.
