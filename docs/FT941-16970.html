940107 FT 07 JAN 94 / Paris moves to open up savings sector Mr Edmond Alphandery, the French economy minister, yesterday confirmed that he plans to table legislation for a radical reform of the Caisse des Depots et Consignations, the state-controlled savings institution, writes Alice Rawsthorn. The reform of the Caisse des Depots, an idiosyncratic institution which dominates the French savings system and is involved with public sector funding, has been mooted since the conservatives came to office. France's banks have lobbied for it to be dismantled in order to open up the savings market. Mr Alphandery said he feltthat its public sector interests and competitive activities should be separated.
