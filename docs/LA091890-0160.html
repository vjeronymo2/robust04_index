September 18, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

FOR THE RECORD

FarWest S&L -- There were several errors in stories and a chart about FarWest Savings & Loan Assn. that appeared Aug. 18 and 19. The thrift moved to Newport Beach in 1970. The S&L, which held $395 million in junk bonds as of June 30, lost $29.9 million in the first six months of 1990. And David E. Blackford is no longer president of the thrift's parent company, FarWest Financial Corp.
