BFN [Text] On 27 March, the communist Vietnamese puppets and two-headed government gathered their forces, which were ready to be shipped to join their troops in Pailin, at the Kompong Chhnang provincial airfield. On 28 March, the people and National Army of Democratic Kampuchea [NADK] attacked and dispersed the forces, killing nine enemy soldiers and wounding more than 10 others. We also destroyed over 10 assorted armaments, two control posts, three military buildings, and some war materiel. On 28 March, the people and NADK attacked the position of a company, which is part of the invading forces in Pailin. We killed five enemy soldiers, wounded three, and captured another, and seized 15 AK and an AR-15 assault rifles.
