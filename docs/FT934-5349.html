931130 FT 30 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Drayton Blue Chip net assets ahead Drayton Blue Chip Trust reported a net asset value of 88.6p per income share at September 30, up 8 per cent from 81.8p at the March year-end. The increase on the same stage of 1992 was 24 per cent. Net revenue was virtually flat at Pounds 503,000 (Pounds 510,000) for earnings of 3.981p (4.035p) per share. The interim dividend is maintained at 2.905p.
