April 9, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

EMPIRE OF THE OLD

Fine and dandy that you gave AARP a tremendous amount of publicity. However, I do not feel that this organization is living up to its original intent.

Buy their car insurance: It keeps going up and up, and we who are on a fixed income feel that AARP car insurance is in competition with other insurance companies. I called to complain and was told that if I was not satisfied, I should look elsewhere.

M.P. PEYOVICH

Los Angeles
