April 13, 1989, Thursday, Southland Edition

TODAY'S QUOTE

"I still don't get it. Nowadays, they're tearing other places down so they can make them look like this."

-- Richard Tomasian, lamenting the closing of his family's Blvd. Cafe hamburger stand in Duarte, which for decades served travelers on Route 66 but is being bulldozed to make way for a redevelopment project shopping center.
