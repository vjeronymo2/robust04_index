930217 FT 17 FEB 93 / Parliament and Politics: Heseltine defends export cover costs MR MICHAEL Heseltine yesterday hit back at recent criticism of the cost and availability of credit cover for British exporters, highlighting recent cuts in premium rates for sales to the Middle East. The trade and industry secretary said the UK's Export Credits Guarantee Department has cut premium rates by 25 per cent in Abu Dhabi, Dubai, Oman, Saudi Arabia and Bahrain. He also announced the resumption of medium-term cover for Morocco.
