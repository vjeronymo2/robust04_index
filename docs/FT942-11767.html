940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Management buy-out at GB Glass GB Glass, the Doncaster-based glass and lighting components maker jointly owned by Osram and Thorn EMI, has been bought by its management for Pounds 25m with backing from 3i, the venture capital group. For the 1993 year GB Glass returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 3.1m on sales of Pounds 66m, more than half of which were to export markets. 3i is leading a syndicate of investors, including Prudential Venture Managers, to provide Pounds 9m of equity and mezzanine debt. Senior debt and overdraft facilities were provided by Midland Bank.
