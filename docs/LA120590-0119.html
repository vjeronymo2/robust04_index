December 5, 1990, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWS WATCH

GOTCHA! Bob Johnson, retiring after 13 years as El Toro High School's football coach (C1), may be remembered best for making 1987's most spectacular catch. . . . That fall, Johnson sneaked away from football practice and up to an RV parked nearby, swung open the door and found one of his former players videotaping El Toro's plays. . . . It turned out the tapes were going to the coach at archenemy Capistrano Valley High School, and that cost that coach his job. Tom Hamilton and Cathy Curtis
