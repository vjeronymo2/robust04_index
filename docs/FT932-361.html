930629 FT 29 JUN 93 / UK Company News: City Merchants Pounds 6.3m cash call City Merchants High Yield Trust is raising Pounds 6.27m net via a placing and open offer, for the acquisition of investments. New ordinary shares representing 5m offer units have been placed at 130p per unit, fully underwritten by UBS, and can be clawed back by shareholders on a 1-for-3 basis. A second interim dividend of 2p is declared, making 4p to date (4.875p). The directors felt that the UK was more advantageously placed than its north European neighbours to profit from any improvement in the economic growth of the US and Japan.
