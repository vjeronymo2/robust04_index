920114 FT 14 JAN 92 / UK Company News: Newcastle Bldg Soc profits grow 12% Pre-tax profits at Newcastle Building Society rose by 12 per cent to Pounds 12.5m during 1991, up from Pounds 11.2m. Total assets of the society, which ranked 30th by size in 1990, were Pounds 973m, up 14 per cent on 1990, and will reach Pounds 1bn by March 31 1992, the society said. Its free capital ratio rose to 4.57 per cent (4.39 per cent). 'These results show the strength of the medium-sized society and its ability to compete in the hardest markets. Only the strong can survive but with these results behind us we are confident that we can,' said Mr Bill Midgley, group managing director.
