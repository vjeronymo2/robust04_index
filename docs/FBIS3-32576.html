Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Cuban Vice President Carlos Lage Davila [title as heard] is in Colombia, as part of a tour that included Brazil and Venezuela. This visit will contribute to strengthening the bilateral ties between Colombia and Cuba following the resumption, just over three months ago, of diplomatic relations between the two countries. Today Lage will meet with Colombian President Cesar Gaviria and, during his official visit to Colombia, will meet with the ministers of foreign trade, agriculture, economic development, and health. Before returning to our country, Lage, who is also secretary of the executive committee of the Council of Ministers, will meet with the board of directors of the Bank of the Republic, which sets and regulates the nation's financial and fiscal policy. Tomorrow, invited by the powerful National Association of Industrialists, Lage Davila will attend a working breakfast with representatives of that association, of exporters associations, of financial institutions, and of small and medium-sized industries, among others.
