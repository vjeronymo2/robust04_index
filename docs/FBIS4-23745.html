BFN [Text] Kinshasa, June 14 (AFP) -- Zaire's transitional parliament named moderate opposition leader Joseph Kengo Wa Dondo Tuesday [14 June] as prime minister. The vote was 332 in favor and 133 against. He was appointed after two other candidates who also oppose longtime military ruler Mobutu Sese Seko dropped out and threw their support behind Wa Dondo.
