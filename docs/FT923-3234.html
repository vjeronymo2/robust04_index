920914 FT 14 SEP 92 / Construction Contracts: British embassy YEOMANS & PARTNERS, a P&O company, has been awarded a Pounds 450,000 contract to refurbish the British Embassy in Jakarta, Indonesia, for the Foreign & Commonwealth Office. Work has already started to replace electrical installations, suspended ceilings and partitions within the embassy which will remain occupied and fully operational throughout the duration of the project. Yeomans' success in Jakarta follows a series of shopfitting contracts in Czechoslovakia where it has fitted out two carpet and soft furnishing stores.
