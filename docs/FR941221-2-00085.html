Applications may be rejected and returned where the information required is not provided in sufficient detail to permit adequate assessment of the proposal. DOL will select applicants on the basis of merit, giving due regard to geographic diversity, size of project, and the best interest of the government in making awards to conduct demonstrations involving the retraining of at-risk non-managerial workers in the health care industry. The final decision on the award will be based on what is most advantageous to the Federal Government as determined by the ETA Grant Officer. Part V. Reporting Requirements A.
