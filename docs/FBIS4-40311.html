BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vladimir Anufriyev] [Text] Arkhangelsk June 15 TASS -- Russian Vice-Premier Sergey Shakhray arrived in Arkhangelsk on Wednesday to look into the problem of cargo deliveries to areas of Russia's extreme north. Upon arrival, Shakhray said at the airport that he intends to familiarise himself with the situation on the spot and check whether funds allocated for this purposes were efficiently used. During his stop in Arkhangelsk Shakhray will visit an oil base, a sea port and hold a conference with the administration of the Arkhangelsk region.
