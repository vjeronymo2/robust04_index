920229 FT 29 FEB 92 / UK Company News: Sinclair Goldsmith Losses at Sinclair Goldsmith Holdings, the London-based surveyor and estate agency, deepened by Pounds 291,000 to Pounds 589,000 pre-tax for the six months ended November 30. However, the company said it was now experiencing 'more activity than there has been for some time' and added that there was 'every indication that we will make a modest profit in the second half.' Turnover totalled Pounds 1.07m (Pounds 1.92m). Losses amounted to 4.8p (2p) and the interim dividend is halved to 0.5p.
