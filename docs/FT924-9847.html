921104 FT 04 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Mezzanine Capital Mezzanine Capital and Income Trust 2001 had a net asset value per income share of 109.5p (105.7p) and per capital share of 173.5p (133.5p) at March 31. Pre-tax profits for the year to end-March were Pounds 1.17m (Pounds 1.74m) and earnings per income share 5.22p (7.78p). Interim dividend is 5p (7.25p).
