BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 19 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il on April 18 received a letter of thanks from the participants in the 12th April spring friendship art festival which had taken place in Pyongyang. The letter says the festival was a beautiful stage of friendship and unity in which they loudly sang as songs of praise of the times the desire and aspirations of mankind to live happily in a free and peaceful world under the noble idea of independence, peace and friendship. Through a visit to beautiful Korea, it goes on, we have got deep impressions, great confidence and inspiration from the indomitable spirit of the Korean people confidently advancing along the road of independence continuously under the banner of socialism, firmly united behind Your Excellency Kim Chong-il. They said in the letter that during their stay in Korea they have come to know well that under the wise leadership of His Excellency Kim Chong-il the traditions of national culture and art are being preserved and inherited successfully and many literary and art works of eternal vitality and monumental significance have been created to stir up all the people in the Democratic People's Republic of Korea. They stressed that they would actively participate in the future, too, in the April spring friendship art festival under the noble idea of the festival and continue to beautifully decorate it as a great international art festival stage. They wished His Excellency Kim Chong-il good health and long life.
