BFN [Text] The security services reported on 15 May that an armed groups support network was dismantled. A total of 31 people in the support group were active in the areas of Jijel and Mila. The same sources emphasized that this support network--composed basically of militants from the dissolved Islamic Salvation Front-- were involved in the assassination of the head of the executive body of Tassala municipality as well of the arson attacks in Tassala and Rouached. Furthermore, the special judicial council of the capital Algiers issued on 15 May 18 death sentences--four were passed on people present before the court. The sentences were given to members of a group consisting of 47 members active in Tipaza Province, especially in Kolea, Douaouda, and Hadjout.
