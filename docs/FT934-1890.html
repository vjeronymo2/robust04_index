931216 FT 16 DEC 93 / UK Company News: OGC OGC International, the North Sea construction group, has acquired the 50 per cent outstanding balance of United-AOC. The holding was purchased from United Construction Group on the basis of one share for ADollars 2,500 (Pounds 1,100) and the payment of a ADollars 2.52m dividend on a special A share which has been issued by U-AOC to United.
