Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The Cease-Fire Commission meeting in Maputo yesterday decided to begin the second stage of disbanding the paramilitary forces throughout the country on 7 February. In the meeting, it was announced that eight new assembly areas, out of the 29 destined for the government, have been opened. The Mozambique National Resistance [Renamo] will soon report on the opening of four new areas out of 20 destined for its soldiers. As of now, 21 assembly areas are open for the government and 14 for Renamo.
