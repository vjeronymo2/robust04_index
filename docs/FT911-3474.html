910424 FT 24 APR 91 / International Company News: McDonald's advances 10.4% MCDONALD'S, the world's largest fast food chain, yesterday delivered a 10.4 per cent improvement in first-quarter earnings at Dollars 168.5m after tax, write our New York staff. Sales were up 6 per cent at Dollars 4.53bn. The company said its US sales had benefited from special marketing and promotional programmes, such as its 'Grilled Steak Sandwich' and 'Happy Meals'. These had helped to more than offset the effects of a soft economy. Outside the US, sales continued to show strong growth.
