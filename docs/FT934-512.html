931224 FT 24 DEC 93 / UK Company News: IBM exercises Tadpole warrants International Business Machines has exercised its warrants to subscribe for 2.22m ordinary shares in Tadpole Technology. The warrants were exercised by IBM on December 17 at the exercise price of 65p per share. Net proceeds received by Tadpole from the exercise of warrants after payment of expenses are estimated to amount to Pounds 1.44m. Tadpole intends to apply these proceeds for its general working capital requirements.
