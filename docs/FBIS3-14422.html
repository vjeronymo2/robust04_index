Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] The curfew on Hebron has been lifted. According to a decision by Major General Dani Yatom, the commander of the Central Command, the curfew was lifted from the town early this evening, except for the area near Bet Hadassah and the Avraham Avinu courtyard. Only Arab residents who live in this area will be allowed to enter this area. The wholesale market near the Jewish settlement in Hebron will not reopen for the time being. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] reinforced its units in Hebron in order to maintain order and to increase the sense of security for the town's residents.
