930219 FT 19 FEB 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa AFTER recent gains, gold shares lost momentum yesterday as investors took profits. The index retreated 41 to 1,009 while industrials put on 3 at 4,531. The overall index closed a point down at 3,482. De Beers advanced R2.75 to R70.25 while Vaal Reefs shed R6.50 to R178.50.
