Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Alija Izetbegovic, president of the Presidency of Bosnia-Herzegovina, today held a telephone conversation with Suleyman Demirel, president of the Republic of Turkey. This was stated by the Information Service of the Presidency of Bosnia-Herzegovina. Expressing interest in events in Bosnia-Herzegovina, Demirel told President Izetbegovic that Turkey will use its influence in the international community so that the integrity of Bosnia-Herzegovina is preserved. President Demirel will exchange messages on this with U.S. President Clinton, British Prime Minister Major, German Chancellor Kohl, Egyptian President Mubarak, and others. Turkish Prime Minister Tansu Ciller, who will be taking part in the NATO session scheduled for 8 January next year, will support the participation of NATO forces in the implementation of the peace agreement on Bosnia-Herzegovina, if such an agreement is reached.
