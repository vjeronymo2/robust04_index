930216 FT 16 FEB 93 / International Company News: Toshiba to sell Sun Microsystems computer clone Toshiba of Japan plans to sell a new UNIX-based computer produced on an original equipment manufacturing basis by Sun Microsystems of the US, AP-DJ reports from Tokyo. The new model is to be based on Sun's SPARCcenter 2000. Toshiba expects to sell 2,000 units over the next three years.
