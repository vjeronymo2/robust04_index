BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] Defense Minister Pavel Grachev and U.S. Defense Secretary William Perry discussed Russian-American exercises in a telephone conversation and agreed on a meeting in Brussels. Yelena Agapova, aide to the Russian minister, told INTERFAX that the telephone conversation took place on Monday [16 May] night at the request of the United States. Pavel Grachev supported the idea of holding joint Russian-American command and staff exercises at the Totskoye testing range but said that Russian legislators still had to make a final decision on this. During the conversation, Grachev also expressed concern over the situation in the Brcko area in Bosnia.
