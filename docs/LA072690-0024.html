July 26, 1990, Thursday, San Diego County Edition

SAN DIEGO COUNTY DIGEST: ESCONDIDO

The county medical examiner's office said Tuesday that a body found in a ravine near Escondido was that of Maria Lourdes Glass, 30, address unknown.

The body was found Monday off Mountain Meadow Road in a remote area. Sheriff's homicide detectives are investigating.

The caused of death had not been established pending lab results from an autopsy done Tuesday, Deputy Coroner Charles Kelley said.
