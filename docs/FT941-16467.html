940110 FT 10 JAN 94 / Company News (This Week): Tomkins faces bread and butter questions - Other companies On Monday, when Tomkins reports interim results, the market will be looking for reassurance over Ranks Hovis McDougall, which the conglomerate acquired a year ago. The milling and baking company has dragged down its parent's stock amid concern over the impact of last year's bread price wars and the lacklustre food manufacturing sector. Analysts are expecting pre-tax profits of about Pounds 87m (Pounds 47.1m).
