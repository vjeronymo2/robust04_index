BFN [From the "Slavyanka" program of the Russian Defense Ministry] [Text] A delegation of the Russian military space forces led by Colonel General Vladimir Ivanov, the commander of the military space forces, has completed its visit to the United States of America. The Russian delegation paid the Transatlantic visit at the invitation of General Charles Horner, the commander of the air force space command of the U.S. Armed Forces. The press center of the Russian military space forces has reported that prospects for cooperation were discussed during the course of talks with American colleagues, including, among other things, a more efficient use of space navigation systems -- the Russian Glonass and the American NavStar.
