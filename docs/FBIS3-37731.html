Language: Ukrainian Article Type:BFN [Text] Ukraine's deputy minister of defense, Chief of Armaments of the Ukrainian Armed Forces Colonel-General Ivan Oliynyk met military attaches accredited at foreign embassies in Ukraine. Col-Gen Oliynyk stressed that Ukraine is capable of manufacturing up-to-date arms systems to world standards, except for nuclear ones. He stressed that Ukraine's desire to acquire the non- nuclear state status remains unchanged.
