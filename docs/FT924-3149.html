921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Turnround at Campbell & Armstrong CAMPBELL & Armstrong, the shop and office fitting group, swung from losses of Pounds 849,000 to profits of Pounds 30,000 pre-tax for the six months ended September 30. Turnover slipped to Pounds 25.5m (Pounds 31.3m) reflecting a 'more prudent approach to tendering' as overall margins remained under pressure. In view of the difficult prevailing trading environment the interim dividend is omitted - 1p was paid last time. Declaration of a final will depend on second half trading. Earnings of 0.4p compared with previous losses of 4.5p. The shares closed 3p higher at 17p.
