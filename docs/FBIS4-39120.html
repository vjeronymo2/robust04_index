BFN [Report by UKRINFORM correspondent Mikhail Stetsyuk-TASS] [Text] Sevastopol 3 June TASS -- Military experts are completing lists of ships of the Black Sea Fleet to be divided between Russia and Ukraine, journalists learned although no official reports for the media are available. The talks between Russian and Ukrainian military experts which have been going on for three days, were attended today by Ukrainian Deputy Foreign Minister Boris Tarasyuk, Vice-Premier Valeriy Shmarov and Deputy Ukrainian Defence Minister Ivan Bizhan, who arrived in Sevastopol on Friday to take part in the work of groups of military experts. As for the division of naval bases and coastal infrastructure,the sides have been outlining their positions yet. These problems are expected to be high on the agenda of talks scheduled in Kiev on 8-9 June. Yuriy Dubinin, special envoy of the Russian Foreign Ministry, has failed to arrive in Sevastopol today for unknown reasons, although he had been expected since yesterday. However, problems of the location of the fleets' bases are not likely to be discussed in Sevastopol without representatives of the Russian Government.
