931019 FT 19 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Rugby expands Dutch interests Rugby Group has enhanced its Dutch joinery interests via the Pounds 10.3m cash acquisition of Heerlen Windows, Kegro Doors and Van Bruchem Doors. Management accounts showed that the businesses made aggregate pre-tax profits of Pounds 1m on turnover of Pounds 14m for the half year ended June 30. In addition, the group has assumed bank borrowings of Pounds 15.2m.
