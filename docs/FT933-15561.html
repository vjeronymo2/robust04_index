930708 FT 08 JUL 93 / Gas processing plant for Teesside ENRON, the US gas group, is to start construction of a second gas processing plant on Teesside next year. The Pounds 50m investment was confirmed yesterday at the official opening of the company's first Teesside gas processing plant at Seal Sands. The installation and its adjacent sister plant will process North Sea gas fed through the 254-mile central area transmission system pipeline.
