BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondents Svetlana Ivanova and Lev Rumyantsev] [Text] St. Petersburg June 8 TASS -- The session of the council of the CIS interparliamentary assembly, currently under way here, has called for broader interaction among the parliaments of the Commonwealth of Independent States (CIS). The session, presided over by assembly chairman Vladimir Shumeyko, has called on the parliaments of the CIS nations to coordinate the actions of all the constitutional bodies in drawing up national legislations, elaborating model legal acts, and implementing the treaties and agreements adopted within the frameworks of the commonwealth. The council stressed the need of promoting exchanges of information, holding consultations on fundamental problems linked with the development of the CIS. It is planned to discuss during the fifth plenary meeting of the CIS interparliamentary assembly, which will be held from October 28- 29, a plan for the assembly's efforts to harmonise the legislations of CIS nations relating to the economic, social, humanitarian, military and other areas of cooperation within the CIS. The document was drawn up by standing commissions of the interparliamentary assembly.
