940902 FT 02 SEP 94 / International Company News: TWA in accord with pilots Trans World Airlines confirmed it reached a tentative agreement with the Airline Pilots Association on modifying work rules and enhancing participative management, AP-DJ reports from St Louis. The airline said the agreement, reached after 25 days of negotiations, provides for about Dollars 30m to Dollars 35m in cost savings a year. The airline said the agreement covers 2,700 pilots andis subject to a ratification vote. The International Association of Machinists and Aerospace Workers has ratified new agreement that TWA said will save between Dollars 80m and Dollars 85m a year.
