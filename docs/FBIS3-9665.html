Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 28 Mar (TANJUG) -- Yugoslav Foreign Minister Vladislav Jovanovic today received 'Ali 'Abd-al-Salam al-Turyaki, special envoy of the head of state of the Great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriyah. In the course of the meeting, which passed in a friendly atmosphere, the two sides discussed latest developments on the territory of the former Socialist Federal Republic of Yugoslavia, the situation in the former Yugoslav republic of Bosnia-Herzegovina, and perspectives for bilateral Yugoslav-Jamahiriyah cooperation, the Federal Ministry of Foreign Affairs has announced.
