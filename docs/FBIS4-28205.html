BFN [Text] At 0830 on 9 June, the cabinet of the Royal Government of Cambodia [RGC] held a plenary session under the chairmanship of Prince Krompreah Norodom Ranariddh, first prime minister; and Samdech Hun Sen, second prime minister of the Kingdom of Cambodia. The Council of Ministers' plenary session will examine the draft on the status of the Kingdom of Cambodia's civil servants, the draft on mixed enterprises dealing with the export of processed timber, and the clarification of the RGC's political stance concerning the relations with Taiwan and South Korea.
