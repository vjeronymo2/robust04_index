Language: Greek Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Austrian Chancellor Franz Vranitzky this morning began an official visit to Athens. Soon after arriving, the Austrian chancellor had a 30-minute meeting with Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou. Papandreou said afterward that their talks covered issues relating to Austria's accession to the European Union. We are committed, the Greek prime minister and president of the European Union said, to achieving the interstate agreements for the accession of European Free Trade Association countries by March. The Austrian chancellor referred to his friendship with Papandreou and announced that a group of specialists has undertaken to give a new push to the negotiating package on issues of regional, agriculture, and land transit and transport policy. Asked what stance his country will take on the issue of Cyprus' accession to the European Union, the Austrian chancellor said it will be positive. [passage omitted]
