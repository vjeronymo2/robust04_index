941028 FT 28 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Flying Flowers Flying Flowers' pre-tax profits for the third quarter more than tripled from Pounds 83,000 to Pounds 267,000. Turnover showed a similar increase at Pounds 3.03m, up from Pounds 1.02m. In the 39 weeks to end-September, pre-tax profits at the Jersey-based postal flower service more than doubled to Pounds 856,000 (Pounds 395,000) on turnover of Pounds 9.28m (Pounds 4.02m). Cumulative earnings per share came out at 3.47p (1.91p).
