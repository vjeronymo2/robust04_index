Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe February 18 TASS -- Three servicemen of the Tajik National Security Committee were killed and three others wounded as a result of last night's attack on a security unit deployed in the "Pravda" collective farm, located in Pyanj district, near the border with Afghanistan. The raid is believed to have been launched by a Tajik opposition gang, operating on the territory of Tajikistan. Terroristic acts against servicemen of the Tajik national armed forces have lately become increasingly frequent. A short while ago some unidentified terrorists killed in Kurgan-Tyube, the administrative centre of Hatlon region, Colonel Mirzo Saydov, chief of staff of a special-purpose brigade of the Tajik Defence Ministry. Fire was opened somewhat earlier on a car, carrying Colonel Nurali Sharaliyev, deputy defence minister of Tajikistan, who was going from Kurgan-Tyube to Dushanbe.
