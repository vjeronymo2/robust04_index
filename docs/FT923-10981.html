920728 FT 28 JUL 92 / World Trade News: S Africa 'dumping' inquiry THE European Commission is investigating charges that South African manganese steel wearparts are being dumped at unfairly low prices on the EC market, Reuter reports from Brussels. The South African market share was said to have reached 26 per cent in 1991. The share was higher in Britain and France, the EC's two main producers of wearparts - manganese steel castings used in machinery and liable to be worn away by the material processed.
