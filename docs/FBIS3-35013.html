Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] An Arab resident of Misilyah, south of Janin, was shot dead by soldiers. Our correspondent in the territories Golan Yokhpaz reports that the soldiers, who were stationed at a roadblock in the village, detained the man in order to carry out a routine check of his papers. The IDF [Israel Defense Forces] is currently investigating why one of the soldiers shot the local resident. A young Arab was slightly wounded in the leg this morning when shot by soldiers in Halhul after he stoned an IDF patrol. Halhul and Hebron residents rioted following yesterday's killing of a Halhul resident by soldiers. Our correspondent reports that there is a general strike today in most major towns in Judaea and Samaria in solidarity with Palestinian prisoners in Israeli jails.
