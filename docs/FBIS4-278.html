BFN [Text] Durban April 6 SAPA -- An Inkatha Freedom Party [IFP] self protection unit instructor, Mr Philip Powell, says there are 5,000 trained Zulus and that more will be trained despite the emergency regulations, SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reports. Mr Powell told the London DAILY MAIL on Wednesday [6 April] that if the ANC forced the government to invade kwaZulu, the area would be transformed into a powerful symbol and rallying point for the Zulus.
