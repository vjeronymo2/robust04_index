April 15, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

SCHOOL NEWS: INTRODUCTORY COURSE IN TECHNOLOGY OFFERED

Don Bosco Technical Institute in Rosemead will offer local high school students the chance to take an introductory technology course during the summer session July 2 to Aug.3.

Classes will be offered in automotive technology, computer literacy, construction technology, drafting and design, electronics and computer, graphic communications, manufacturing, and materials science.

Each class will be held from 8 to 11 a.m. Tuition is $300.
