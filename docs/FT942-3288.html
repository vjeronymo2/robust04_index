940615 FT 15 JUN 94 / UK Company News: F&C Smaller Foreign & Colonial Smaller Companies achieved an increase in net assets per share of 29 per cent to 175.8p during the year to April 30. Available revenue was Pounds 2.38m (Pounds 1.76m)for earnings per share of 2.56p (1.98p). The dividend is increased from 1.84p to 1.97p, with a proposed final of 1.27p.
