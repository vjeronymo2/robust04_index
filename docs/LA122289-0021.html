December 22, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MUSIC

Toronto Symphony to Tour Pacific Rim: Calling it the most ambitious international tour in its 67-year history, the Toronto Symphony Orchestra has announced a spring tour of the Pacific Rim. Covering 26 days and 11 cities, the tour will also include the first visit to Australia by a major Canadian orchestra. The tour begins in Vancouver on April 30 with a performance at the Orpheum Theatre, then travels to San Francisco for a concert at the Louise M. Davies Symphony Hall. After that, there will be concerts at four cities in Australia, three in Japan and single concerts in Singapore and Taiwan. CLAUDIA PUIG
