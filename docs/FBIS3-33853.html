Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Feb. 1, IRNA -- Leader of the Islamic revolution Ayatollah 'Ali Khamene'i on Tuesday [1 February] attended the shrine of the late founder of the Islamic Republic Imam Khomeyni to mark the beginning of the ten-day dawn (February 1-11) ceremonies. The ceremonies are held on the occasion of the 15th anniversary of the victory of the 1979 Islamic revolution. After attending the shrine of the late Imam Khomeyni, the leader visited the martyrs quarter of Behesht-e Zahra cemetery and paid homage to the martyrs of the revolution and those of the imposed war.
