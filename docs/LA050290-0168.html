May 2, 1990, Wednesday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

NINTENDO PLANS SIMPSONS GAME

Now there's going to be even more Simpsons on the small screen. Acclaim Entertainment Inc. announced today that it has acquired exclusive rights to manufacture Nintendo video games featuring the wildly popular Bart Simpson and his cartoon family.

Robert Holmes, president of Acclaim, said the value of the licensing deal "is amplified by the fact that young adults feel a strong attachment to both 'The Simpsons' and the video gaming world. By joining the two together, we anticipate the result to be an incredibly powerful product."

Based on the family of characters created by cartoonist Matt Groening, the Fox Network series draws high Nielsen ratings from a broad range of age groups including the core Nintendo customers -- 8- to 16-year-olds. A hand-held Simpsons game is expected this fall.
