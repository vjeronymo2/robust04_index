930114 FT 14 JAN 93 / Observer: Loose talk Meanwhile allusions to alcoholic as well as financial liquidity have crept into the row over whether recession is being prolonged by the Bundesbank's monetary policy, which economists in America have dubbed 'super tight'. Vice-president Tietmeyer says he has no qualms about the high interest rates, which have nothing to do with sluggish economic growth. But he does object to being referred to publicly as one of a 'super-tight team'.
