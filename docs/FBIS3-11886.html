Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Buenos Aires LA NACION in Spanish on 8 March publishes on page 9 a 300-word unattributed article on oil production. The article says Government Oil Deposits "increased oil production 24 percent" in January compared with the same month last year. It adds that oil exports totalled 525,004 cubic meters compared with the 73,227 cubic meters exported in January 1993.
