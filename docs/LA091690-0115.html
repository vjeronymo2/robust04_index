September 16, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Michael Andretti took the pole position from Danny Sullivan in a last-lap effort as the lineup was set for today's 200-mile Indy car race at Lexington, Ohio.

Andretti's lap of 115.652 m.p.h. in a backup car came moments before the 30-minute session ended and beat Sullivan's 115.541.
