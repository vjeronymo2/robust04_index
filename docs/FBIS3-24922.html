Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] Yaounde, 3 Feb (AFP) -- The Cameroonian Government has just dismissed 81 new teachers, bringing to 150 the total number of teachers dismissed for striking, it was learned from official sources in Yaounde today. These teachers from high schools in the public sector, who had been on strike since 24 November to back demands for better salaries, were hit by the measure after their supervisors reported their "irregular absence." On 23 January, 73 other high school teachers were dismissed for similar reasons. The previous day, the government had issued a recruitment notice to cope with the possible refusal of teachers to resume work.
