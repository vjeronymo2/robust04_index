BFN [Text] Our province has created a new situation in the work of importing and using foreign capital. By the end of last year, more than 1,000 internal economic cooperation contracts had been signed involving more than 300 million yuan, and the total amount of direct materials and goods coordination was more than 400 million yuan. Eight autonomous prefectures, prefectures, and cities throughout the province have established or developed cooperative relations with 61 areas outside the province based on similar trades and services. Vice Governor Liu Guanghe recently led a Qinghai delegation to visit the United States and Canada to invite investments. It succeeded in signing a total 12 projects. By the end of May, the contracts and regulations of 82 foreign-funded enterprises had been examined and approved and they had received their approval licenses. In addition, more than $35 million in foreign capital had been imported. Of these foreign-funded enterprises, 70 percent are engaged in the exploitation of natural resources and in production involving light industry, textiles, electronics, chemistry, building construction, communications, metallurgy, nonferrous metal, foodstuffs, agricultural and sideline products, and so on.
