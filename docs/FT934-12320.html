931027 FT 27 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Bradford Property at Pounds 17.5m THE SALE of land near Ipswich for a Tesco superstore helped Bradford Property Trust, the UK's largest tenanted residential property company, to lift pre-tax profits by more than 50 per cent, from Pounds 11.6m to Pounds 17.5m, for the six months to October 5. Yesterday the shares closed 8p ahead at 226p. The surplus from property rentals increased by almost 21 per cent from Pounds 5.3m to Pounds 6.4m. Dealing profits rose from Pounds 6.2m to Pounds 11.4m following the Ipswich land sale. Total operating profit was Pounds 17.8m (Pounds 11.5m). Interest payable increased from Pounds 545,000 to Pounds 641,000. The interim dividend, based on the surplus from property rentals after tax and preference dividends, rose from 2.4p to 2.9p. Earnings per share were up from 5.43p to 8.11p. The company's business centres on buying tenanted properties at a discount to reflect the statutory rights of sitting tenants and selling them with vacant possession.
