Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [By Rashid Hilal] [Excerpt] Dr. Haydar 'Abd-al-Shafi has declined an invitation by Nayif Hawatimah, secretary general of the Democratic Front for the Liberation of Palestine, to join the Palestinian opposition to the peace process between Israel and the PLO. In an exclusive statement to AL-QUDS, 'Abd-al-Shafi said that in principle he wants to maintain his independent position, adding: I wish to pursue my national position through this independence. Five days ago, Hawatimah extended an invitation, through the news media, to Dr. 'Abd-al-Shafi to join the Palestinian opposition. [passage omitted]
