October 1, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

PUT ASTROLOGY IN ITS PLACE

Congratulations. You have finally found the right place for the astrological forecasts -- next to the comics. I admit I occasionally read them, for something to laugh at.

Even among the strange people who take them seriously, there are those who find grave faults with them. The few that may some day be taken seriously are those who base their expectations on the astrological arrangements in the sky at the precise moment of birth.

ROBERT HARDING, Northridge
