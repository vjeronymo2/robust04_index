Language: Armenian Article Type:BFN [Text] Tension persists in the southeast of the Karabakh war front. The Nagorno-Karabakh Republic Defense Army Press Service has reported that the Karabakh formations repeatedly halted the enemy's attacks toward Fizuli yesterday. For the past six days the Azerbaijani troops have been trying to break through the NKR's forward defense lines in the sector. Various types of guns were used to resume shelling of Karabakh positions in this sector this morning. Relative calm prevails on the other war fronts. The Armenian Defense Ministry Press Service has just reported that relative calm is also prevailing on Armenia's borders.
