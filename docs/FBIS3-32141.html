Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] In Caracas on 1 February, Cuban Vice President Carlos Lage [title as heard] expressed his confidence that our country's relations with Venezuela will continue to develop under Rafael Caldera's government. Caldera takes office as president of Venezuela today. Lage, who is on his way to Brazil, spent a few hours in Caracas, where he met with current Foreign Minister Fernando Ochoa Antich and with Venezuelan businessmen. Lage told journalists this is a good moment for the ties between the two nations and said he had reason to expect these ties to continue to develop, because the Cuban and Venezuelan peoples are very much alike.
