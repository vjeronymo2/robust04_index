BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] In an effort to end the ongoing clashes in northern Iraq, Northern Iraqi Kurdish leaders Mas'ud Barzani and Jalal Talabani met in Sirnak's Silopi District. The Gendarmerie General Commander General Aydin Ilter attended the meeting. Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin answered reporters' questions on the issue after leaving the Council of Ministers meeting in Ankara. Cetin stated that there was an unstable atmosphere in northern Iraq and added that the clashes between rival Kurdish groups exacerbated the already unstable situation. Cetin said that the aim of the meeting in Silopi was to end this unstable situation and stop the bloodshed. Explaining that the issue of fighting terrorism in northern Iraq will be assessed during the meetings, Cetin added: Our gendarmerie general commander has frequently conducted meetings of this kind. After a lengthy interval, such a meeting is again taking place. In answer to a question, Cetin said that officials from the foreign ministry and the interior ministry are participating in the meetings in addition to the gendarmerie general commander.
