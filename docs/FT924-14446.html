921009 FT 09 OCT 92 / UK Company News: 11% annual growth for Town Centre Town Centre Securities, the property investor and developer, lifted its pre-tax profit by 11 per cent, from Pounds 6.51m to Pounds 7.25m, for the year ended June 30 1992. The dividend is raised from 2.8p to 3.1p, with a proposed final of 2.1p, as earnings per share rose to 5.12p (4.58p). Gross rental and investment income totalled Pounds 16.6m (Pounds 15.6m) and operating surplus came to Pounds 12.8m (Pounds 12.2m). Interest charges were Pounds 5.58m (Pounds 5.64m). At the year-end net asset value had slipped to 121.38p (125.75p) per share.
