Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, Jan. 6, IRNA -- The leader of the Islamic revolution, Ayatollah 'Ali Khamene'i on Thursday [6 January] called for maintenance and consolidation of the friendly relations between Iran and Turkmenistan. Receiving the visiting Turkmen President Saparmurat Niyazov and his companions, Ayatollah Khamene'i said that expansion of cooperation in various fields will be to the benefit of Turkmenistan and Iran. There exist good cultural ties and common religious bonds and customs between the two nations, the supreme leader pointed out. Terming the establishment of an independent government in Turkmenistan as a "divine blessing," the supreme leader expressed hope that the foundations of Turkmenistan's independence will grow stronger in the future. The Turkmen president thanked the leader of the Islamic revolution for favouring promotion of Tehran-Ashgabat relations, and expressed confidence that bilateral relations will be broadened despite the wish of enemies of the two countries. President Niyazov also referred to the construction of hundreds of mosques in his country over the past two years, saying the Turkmen people were reviving their past history and practicing their religion freely.
