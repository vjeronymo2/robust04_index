930120 FT 20 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Practical Inv Trust net assets increase Practical Investment Trust reported an increase in net assets per share to 136.2p as at November 30 1992, against 129.88p a year earlier. Net revenue, however, dropped from Pounds 565,901 to Pounds 526,037 in the six months and earnings per share were 2.63p (2.83p). The interim dividend has been maintained at 1.1p.
