931215 FT 15 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Chemex turns in Pounds 12,961 deficit Despite a turnround from a first-half loss of Pounds 53,679 to a Pounds 81,512 operating profit in the second six months, Chemex International, the chemical analysis company, ended the year to September 30 with a pre-tax loss of Pounds 12,961. The deficit compared with a Pounds 52,083 profit last time, and was struck after an exceptional charge of Pounds 43,131 for capital reduction and relocation costs. Turnover rose by 11 per cent to Pounds 1.33m (Pounds 1.2m); directors said current order books were at record levels. Losses per share were 0.03p (earnings of 0.14p).
