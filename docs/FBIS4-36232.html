BFN [Text] India and Russia have agreed to increase bilateral trade up to the 5,000-crore-rupee mark in the next two years. This view was expressed at a meeting of the cochairmen of the Indo- Russian Joint Commission, Mr. Pranab Mukherjee, and the deputy prime minister of Russia, Mr. Yuriy F. Yarov, in New Delhi today. Mr. Yarov assured India that Moscow will fully implement the agreement signed during President Yeltsin's visit to New Delhi. He said Russia will try to further rationalize import duties to facilitate speedy imports from India. The talks will continue till Tuesday.
