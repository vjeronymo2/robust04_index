BFN [Text] At the present time, all of the prerequisites exist in the Czech Army for the reduction of the Armed Forces to the planned 65,000 in 1995, Defense Minister Antonin Baudys said today. He said that, over the past 18 months, the number of troops was reduced from just below 106,000 to 88,000 men under arms. Key posts on strategic and operational levels were filled with uncompromised and professionally trained generals and officers, Baudys said.
