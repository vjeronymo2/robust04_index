930717 FT 17 JUL 93 / World Commodities Prices: Spices The Indonesian cassia market was firmer on heavy local demand, the arrival of the rainy season and limited cutting, reports Man Producten. In the European market prices increased for va/ka sticks for from Dollars 2,250 to Dollars 2,300 a tonne for spot delivery and for shipment from Dollars 2,070 to Dollars 2,200 a tonne. In the European market Muntok/Sarawak faq white pepper for spot delivery was quited at Dollars 2,100 a tonne, with July/August supplies at Dollars 2,050, August/September at Dollars 2075 and November/December at Dollars 2,150. Sarawak black label black pepper was at Dollars 1,200 for spot and Dollars 1,150 for Jul/August delivery.
