930330 FT 30 MAR 93 / International Company News: Parmalat rises 24% to L52bn PARMALAT, the Italian milk and dairy foods company, raised net group profits by almost 24 per cent to L52bn (Dollars 32.9m) last year, due partly to a 23 per cent rise in sales to L1,636bn. Mr Calisto Tanzi, chairman, said sales should reach about L2,800bn this year, thanks to further acquisitions. Acquisitions this year include a stake in Giglio, an Italian dairy group with sales of about L400bn; Atlanta Dairy, a US dairy products concern with about L100bn in turnover; Lacesa, a dairy products group; and the associated Sodilac company in Brazil, with sales of about Dollars 80m.
