941112 FT 12 NOV 94 / Support urged for Sizewell C plans The government was urged yesterday to support Nuclear Electric's plans for the Sizewell C power station in Suffolk or to risk squandering skills built up in the construction of Sizewell B. The Engineering Employers' Federation, the Engineering Construction Industry Association and the Power Generation Contractors' Association called for 'launch aid' similar to that provided for the aerospace industry.
