July 1, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

HARRY SHEARER

Harry Shearer's article on the information age ("Eyewitness Moods," May 6) was such a delight. I don't always agree with his findings (rare), I don't always understand his musings (rare), but he's a master storyteller (well done) -- we adore him.

RON LAMANTIA

Rancho Palos Verdes
