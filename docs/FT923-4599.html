920905 FT 05 SEP 92 / Markets: At a Glance - Cazenove's US offering Cazenove Unit Trust Managers is offering a protected investment in the US stock market. Investors who place a minimum of Pounds 2,500 in Cazenove's American Portfolio fund will be protected against a fall or more than 10 per cent in the Standard & Poor's 500 Index in the period to August 1, 1993. If the index falls by more than 10 per cent, Cazenove will refund the difference. The management charges on the trust are 5 per cent initial and 1 per cent annual.
