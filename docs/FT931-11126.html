930204 FT 04 FEB 93 / Club Riviera timeshare wound up CLUB RIVIERA, a timeshare company that had its credit licences revoked by the Office of Fair Trading in July, was compulsorily wound up in the High Court yesterday. The company opposed the petition brought by Collective Insurance Agency (Travel), which said it was owed Pounds 147,000. Counsel for the company had sought an adjournment to settle the debts from proceeds of a restructuring.
