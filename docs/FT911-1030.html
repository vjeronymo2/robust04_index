910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / Convoy of Yugoslav tanks and troop carriers move into Listica A convoy of Yugoslav tanks and troop carriers in the village of Listica, south-east of the Croation capital Zagreb, after being halted by Croats protesting against any army intervention in the republic. However, the troops may soon be on the move, following a last night's statement by Ante Markovic, the prime minister, giving the army full constitutional powers to put an end to armed ethnic conflict.
