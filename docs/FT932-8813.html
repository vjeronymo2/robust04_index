930517 FT 17 MAY 93 / UK Company News: Albert Fisher expands in US Albert Fisher Group is paying up to Dollars 4m (Pounds 2.5m) to enter the foodservice distribution market in Dallas, through the acquisition of the customer lists, trademarks and certain assets of American Produce & Vegetable Company. Fisher will use American Produce - which has an annual turnover of about Dollars 50m - as its flagship vehicle in north Texas.
