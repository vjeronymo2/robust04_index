Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Lima, 6 Mar (DPA) -- The government extended for 60 days the state of emergency in four provinces of Apurimac Department, southeast of Lima, today. The measure, published in the official newspaper EL PERUANO, stated certain constitutional guarantees will be suspended and that the Armed Forces will be in charge of internal order. The decree stemmed from the need to facilitate antiterrorist operations in that department.
