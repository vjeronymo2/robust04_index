920205 FT 05 FEB 92 / Jobs to go at tool plant THE 600 GROUP, the machine tool and mechanical handling equipment company, is to close its Colchester plant with the loss of about 280 jobs. The move is part of a reorganisation of its lathe operations. It plans to concentrate manufacturing, design and product development for its Colchester and Harrison ranges at the Harrison factory in West Yorkshire. The Colchester plant has been on short-time working for the past year.
