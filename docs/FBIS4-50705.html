BFN [Text] Beijing, may 20 (XINHUA) -- Today's economic news in brief.

New Air Route
A Boeing 737 airliner concluded the first flight Tuesday [17 May] on a new route from Mudanjiang, in northeast China's Heilongjiang Province, to Shanghai and Guangzhou.
Poverty Relief
East Fujian's Saiqi economic development zone, the country's only poverty-relief project with foreign investment, is proceeding smoothly. By now, 14 foreign-invested enterprises have been established in the zone, with 88 million yuan (about one million U.S. dollars) in total contracted investment.
Radio Paging Network
China's first three-number radio paging network started operation Tuesday in 12 eastern coastal cities in Shandong, Zhejiang and Jiangsu provinces, and Shanghai.
