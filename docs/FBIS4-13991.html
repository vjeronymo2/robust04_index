BFN [Text] In Himachal Pradesh, the situation in the Dharamsala town of Kangra District is peaceful. The town witnessed violent clashes following a stabbing incident on Saturday in which one person was killed and 15 injured. The state home secretary, Mr. S.N. Verma, told our Simla correspondent that the Tibetan youths believed to be involved in the incident have been arrested. Additional police reinforcements have been rushed to Dharamsala. The chief minister, Mr. Vir Bhadra Singh, has appealed to the people to exercise restraint and maintain communal amity.
