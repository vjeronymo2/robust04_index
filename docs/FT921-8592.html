920217 FT 17 FEB 92 / UK Company News: GT Venture net asset value 124.4p Net asset value per share of GT Venture Investment Co stood at 124.4p on December 31 compared with 125p a year earlier and 144p at the June 30 year-end. Pre-tax profits for the six months advanced to Pounds 371,000 (Pounds 346,000) and earnings per share worked through at 1.65p (1.42p) basic and 0.72p (0.7p) fully diluted.
