September 3, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

COMPTON, NORWALK LEGAL AID PROVIDER WILL MEET

The Board of Directors of the Legal Aid Society of Orange County , which provides service in Compton and Norwalk under the name of Community Legal Services, will meet at noon Wednesday at the Legal Aid Society's office, 902 N. Main St., Santa Ana. The public may attend.

Copies of the agenda are available by calling (714) 835-8806 or writing Betty Summers at the above address, ZIP Code 92701
