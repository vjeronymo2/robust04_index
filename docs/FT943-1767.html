940922 FT 22 SEP 94 / UK Company News: Martin Currie Pacific assets rise The six months to August 31 at Martin Currie Pacific Trust ended with net asset value at 169.4p, up 21 per cent from 140.4p 12 months earlier. Income totalled Pounds 637,000 (Pounds 875,000). After interest and management expenses totalling Pounds 541,000 (Pounds 641,000) net revenue was Pounds 46,000 (Pounds 168,000). Earnings per share worked through at 0.12p (0.41p).
