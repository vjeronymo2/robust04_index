921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / Parliament and Politics: Royal separations The break-up of the Waleses' marriage brings into the open rifts in all the marriages of the Queen's children: 1973: Princes Anne married Mark Phillips, the Army officer and Olympic rider, in the first royal wedding watched on colour television. They divorced this year and the princess is to marry Commander Timothy Laurence on Saturday. 1986: The Duke of York married Sarah Ferguson. Early this year it was announced they would separate. 1981: Lady Diana Spencer married Prince Charles. 1987: Rumours of a rift between Prince Charles and Lady Diana begin to emerge. June 1992: Publication of Diana Her True Story, exposing the princess's unhappiness. December 9 1992: Separation of the Prince and Princess of Wales announced.
