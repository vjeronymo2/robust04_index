Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Israeli President Ezer Weizman has arrived in Ankara as the official guest of President Suleyman Demirel. Weizman was received at Esenboga Airport by Foreign Minister Hikmet Cetin. An official reception will be held at the Cankaya Mansion this afternoon. The Israeli president will first visit the Ataturk Mausoleum and then go to the Cankaya Mansion for the reception. After that, the official talks between the two countries will be held.
