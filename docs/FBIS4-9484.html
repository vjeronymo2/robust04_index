BFN [Text] Bratislava April 25 (CTK) -- Some 44 percent of Slovaks agree with Vladimir Meciar's dismissal from the premiership, while 39 percent do not, a poll conducted by the Slovak Statistical Office in early April revealed. Some 17 percent are indifferent to the issue, the poll showed. Meciar's fall (on March 11) was welcomed by ethnic Hungarians (80 percent), entrepreneurs (55 percent) and followers of the "dissidents" from Meciar's Movement for a Democratic Slovakia (88 percent).
