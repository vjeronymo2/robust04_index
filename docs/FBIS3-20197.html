Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Esat Kiratlioglu, deputy leader of the True Path Party [TPP], has declared that his party has emerged as the leading party from the local elections and the biggest credit goes to leader Tansu Ciller. The TPP Chairmanship Council met under Kiratlioglu in Ankara and appraised the election results. In a statement before the meeting, Kiratlioglu said that the TPP is not considering early general elections. Pointing out that the TPP took over an economy ridden with crises, Kiratlioglu noted that the government will try to solve the economic problems by 1966. He also expressed the view that the coalition government will not experience any difficulties. Meanwhile, Prime Minister Tansu Ciller has called a meeting of the TPP assembly group tomorrow morning.
