July 28, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

MURAL CONSERVATION

The July 25 Morning Report incorrectly reported we said the work on the Siqueiros mural has been delayed by the city's uncertainty surrounding future plans for Olvera Street. The plans for building a location for public viewing are necessarily delayed, but not the actual conservation of the mural. We are proceeding with the chromatic restitution in January, 1991.

MIGUEL ANGEL CORZO

Friends of the Arts of Mexico

Los Angeles
