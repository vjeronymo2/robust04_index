Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] At about 1715 today, Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri left for Damascus on a surprise visit, accompanied by Defense Minister Muhsin Dallul and Minister of State Jean 'Ubayd. It has been learned that Prime Minister al-Hariri will hold talks in Damascus within the framework of the continuous consultations between Lebanese and Syrian officials on various issues of interest to the two countries and the Cooperation and Coordination Treaty between them.
