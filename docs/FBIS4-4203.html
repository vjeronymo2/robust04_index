BFN ["Japan's Business Leaders Show Mixed Reactions to New Cabinet" -- XINHUA headline] [Text] Tokyo, April 28 (XINHUA) -- Japan's business leaders showed mixed reaction to the formation of the minority coalition cabinet today of Prime Minister Tsutomu Hata. Some expressed relief at the move that ended 20 days of political unrest following Morihiro Hosokawa's April 8 announcement to resign as prime minister, while others expressed concern over political uncertainties stemming from the weak foundation of the second coalition cabinet, KYODO NEWS SERVICE reported. It quoted Takeshi Nagano, president of the Japan Federation of Employers Associations (Nikkeiron), and Osamu Uno, chairman of the Japan Department Stores Associations, as commenting on the Hata government with different reactions.
