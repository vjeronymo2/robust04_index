November 15, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: ANAHEIM;

DRUGS FOUND IN HOME; 2 ARRESTED

Two Anaheim residents were arrested Wednesday after police searched their home and found more than three pounds of methamphetamines with an estimated street value of $150,000, authorities said.

Vance Souza, 24, and Christal Conkle, 20, were taken to County Jail and booked on suspicion of possession of methamphetamines with intent to sell, police said.

Placentia narcotics agents, who traced the suspects to Anaheim, said they found the drug prepackaged in small bags for street sales. Also seized was an undisclosed amount of cash at the residence.

The arrests capped an investigation by Placentia's Special Enforcement Detail. No other information about the investigation or arrests was available.
