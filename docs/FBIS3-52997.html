Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Khartoum, 10 Jan (SUNA) -- Mr. Awad Khalifah, the commissioner general of the Relief and Rehabilitation Commission, has said that the commission has chartered a Russian aircraft to strengthen its transport fleet. It is expected to arrive in the next few days. The aircraft would be used to transport relief supplies to the areas which cannot be reached by river steamers or railways. [passage omitted]
