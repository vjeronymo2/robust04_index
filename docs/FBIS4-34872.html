BFN [Text] The Foreign Affairs and Cults Ministry has ordered former President Jean-Bertrand Aritide and his employees not to speak on behalf of the Haitian state or to commit the Republic of Haiti to anything with their signatures. This is how it appears in the Ministry press release, which adds: The Haitian Chancery expresses its most formal reservations over statements and actions by members of the overthrown government, statements and actions that the Chancery considers null and void. The Foreign Affairs and Cults Ministry adds that it has asked the OAS secretary general to pass this information on to its various members and regional institutions for whatever it's worth.
