930216 FT 16 FEB 93 / UK Company News: Albert Fisher's bid goes unconditional Albert Fisher, the food processing and distribution group, has declared unconditional its Pounds 29.3m agreed cash offer for Hunter Saphir, the fresh produce, herbs and spice company. J Henry Schroder Wagg, which is advising Albert Fisher, said acceptances had been received by yesterday afternoon in respect of about 50.03 per cent of Hunter Saphir's ordinary shares. Acceptances have also been received in respect of 81.3 per cent of Hunter Saphir's A and B preference shares, as well as all of a class of preference shares owned by Berisford International.
