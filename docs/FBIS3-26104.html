Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN ["Reference News" by Wang Hui (3769 1920): "Reports About Malpractice of Six High-Ranking Officials Made"] [Text] According to the Central Discipline Inspection Commission, NPC vice chairmen Ni Zhifu, Chen Muhua, and Li Ximing were reported by people of their localities and departments. They and their families reportedly were involved in economic problems. There were accusations that their sources of income are subject to suspicion. Metallurgical Industry Minister Liu Qi and Water Resources Minister Niu Maosheng were reported for leading a corrupt life and for improper relations with women. CPPCC Vice Chairman Yang Rudai was reported by the Discipline Inspection Commission of Sichuan for his family's possession of 50,000-yuan in original issue stocks. It is learned that the Central Discipline Inspection Commission has already confirmed the reports about the six high-ranking officials.
