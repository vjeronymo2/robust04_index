Language: Indonesian Article Type:BFN [Text] Indonesia will initiate an international conference on Bosnia-Herzegovina. Speaking to reporters after President Suharto and visiting Bosnian President Alija Izetbegovic held their talks at Jakarta's Merdeka Palace one and half hours ago, Minister-State Secretary Murdiono said both Bosnia and Indonesia, in its capacity as chairman of the Nonaligned Movement, stressed the need to hold an international conference on Bosnia-Herzegovina.
