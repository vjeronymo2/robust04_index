Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Several cases of cease-fire violations were registered in Sarajevo yesterday, UN Protection Forces [UNPROFOR] representative Major Jose (La Bandeira) said today. Three soldiers of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Army were injured by small arms fire from the Serbian aggressor's positions. Major (La Bandeira) confirmed that six howitzers of the Serbian aggressor are positioned 900 meters inside the protected zone around Sarajevo. They are situated near Ilijas. UNPROFOR is negotiating their withdrawal with Karadzic's Serbs even though these howitzers should have long since been removed or placed under the control of the peace troops on the basis of the NATO ultimatum and the agreement reached at Sarajevo airport.
