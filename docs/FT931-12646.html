930128 FT 28 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Murray Split net assets rise Net asset value per capital share of the Murray Split Capital Trust expanded to 141.6p as at November 30 1992, compared with 94.6p three months earlier. Per income share they were unchanged at 51.2p while net assets per zero dividend preference share came out at 115.7p against 112.6p. Managed in Glasgow by Murray Johnstone, the company is paying, as forecast, a first quarter dividend of 2.65p per 10p income share with the intention of maintaining last year's annualised payment of 10.6p. Available revenue for the three months fell from Pounds 1.03m to Pounds 206,184. Earnings per income share dropped to 2.58p (12.83p).
