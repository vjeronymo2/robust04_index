940907 FT 07 SEP 94 / UK Company News: LIG disposes of minilabs London International Group, the condoms and surgical gloves maker, has severed all ties with its ill-fated venture into photo-processing by selling its remaining minilab operations, located in stores of the Boots Group, to Kodak Processing Companies for Pounds 3.8m cash. In May it sold the bulk of its loss-making photo-processing business, ColourCare International.
