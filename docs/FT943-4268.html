940909 FT 09 SEP 94 / UK Company News: PCT Group Continued growth in overseas sales helped boost profits of PCT Group, the Glasgow-based engineering equipment maker, by nearly 10 per cent from Pounds 751,195 to Pounds 822,914 in the first half of 1994. Turnover grew 9 per cent to Pounds 11.39m, against Pounds 10.45m. Earnings per share grew from 6.08p to 6.68p, while the interim dividend has been raised to 2.7p (2.5p).
