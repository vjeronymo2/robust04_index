Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali today received in his office at the Prime Ministry Bob Rae, the premier of the Canadian province of Ontario, and his accompanying delegation. During the meeting, the two sides discussed bilateral relations and the need to reinforce, promote, and develop these relations in all spheres. They also discussed ways to reinforce economic relations as well as the peace process, the stages it reached, and the obstacles it is facing, especially on the Jordanian track, such as the refugee problem, waters, and evacuees. Deputy Prime Minister Dr. Ma'n Abu-Nuwar also attended the meeting.
