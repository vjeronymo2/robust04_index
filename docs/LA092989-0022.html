September 29, 1989, Friday, Valley Edition

2 VALLEY PLAYERS HEAD TENNIS SECTIONAL FIELD

Valley residents Dexter MacBride and Noelle Porter head a strong field in the Southern California Sectional tennis championships, beginning Oct. 6 at the Racquet Centre in Universal City.

MacBride is a teaching professional from Calabasas. Porter, from Chatsworth, was runner-up to Shandra Livingston of Carson in last year's event.
