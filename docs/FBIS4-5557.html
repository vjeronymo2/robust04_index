BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 12 (KCNA) -- A delegation of Korean social scientists led by Hwang Chang-yop, secretary of the C.C., Workers' Party of Korea [WPK] and chairman of the Association of Korean Social Scientists, returned home on Monday after participating in the International Chuche Idea Seminar on Independence, Peace and Friendship held in India. It was met at the airport by Kim Yang-kon, vice-director of a department of the WPK Central Committee.
