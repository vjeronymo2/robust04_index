DEPARTMENT OF COMMERCE Patent and Trademark Office 37 CFR Part 1 Duty of Disclosure CFR Correction In title 37 of the Code of Federal Regulations, revised as of July 1, 1993, on page 63, in §1.175, paragraph (b) was inadvertently removed. As reinstated, the text of paragraph (b) reads as follows: §1.175 Reissue oath or declaration. * * * * * (b) Corroborating affidavits or declarations of others may be filed and the examiner may, in any case, require additional information or affidavits or declarations concerning the application for reissue and its object.
