931027 FT 27 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Lloyd's trust listing HCG Lloyd's Investment Trust yesterday became the first Lloyd's trust to publish its listing particulars following last week's decision by the insurance market to admit corporate capital. The trust is seeking to raise up to Pounds 100m through the issue of 100m shares. The minimum subscription is Pounds 60m and the minimum application per investor is Pounds 20,000. HCG said it had arranged Pounds 119m of underwriting capacity - subject to conditions - with 34 syndicates. Conning & Co and Grimston Advisers will provide insurance advice and JO Hambro will undertake investment management. Meanwhile, Minories Investment Trust has published an investment memorandum. Its adviser is Fenchurch Underwriting Agencies, a Lloyds' Members agent.
