Language: Burmese Article Type:BFN [Text] A Vietnamese delegation led by Mr. Hoang Duc Nghi, minister and chairman of State Commission for Ethnic Minorities and Mountain Region of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam, called on Lieutenant General Maung Thint, member of the State Law and Order Restoration Council and minister of progress of border areas and national races and development affairs, at the latter's office at 0945 this morning. At the meeting, they cordially and frankly exchanged views on matters relating to development activities of ethnic minorities in Myanmar [Burma] and Vietnam, narcotics suppression, crop substitution and income substitution work.
