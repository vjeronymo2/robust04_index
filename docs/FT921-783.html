920327 FT 27 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Futura requests share suspension Directors of Futura Holdings yesterday requested a temporary suspension of dealings in the footwear company's shares pending clarification of the financial position. This followed the recent fall in the share price, which closed at 34p on Wednesday and was suspended at 15p yesterday. The company incurred a pre-tax loss of Pounds 601,000 for 1990 and a further deficit of Pounds 940,000 in the first half of 1991.
