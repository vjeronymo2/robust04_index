January 25, 1989, Wednesday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP;

CLU ENDS SKID WITH 90-89 WIN

Blake Miraglia hit an 18-foot jump shot with 5 seconds left to lift Cal Lutheran to a 90-89 Golden State Athletic Conference victory over Point Loma Nazarene in men's basketball Tuesday night, snapping the Kingsmen's 8-game losing streak.

"This is just a real big win for us," said Steve deLaveaga, who led Cal Lutheran with 32 points and 7 rebounds. "I am very relieved."

DeLaveaga's 3-point basket put the Kingsmen ahead, 86-85, with 2:56 left. Miraglia, who had 18 points, scored Cal Lutheran's last 4 points to complete a 42-37 second-half rally for the Kingsmen (7-12, 1-4 in conference play).

Mike Demeter added 14 points for the Kingsmen, who will play 3 road games before returning home Feb. 7 to play host to Westmont.

The Crusaders dropped to 5-13, 3-2. RALPH NICHOLS
