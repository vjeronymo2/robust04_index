940427 FT 27 APR 94 / UK Company News: Herring Baker Pounds 430,000 in red Severely affected by the losses and closure of the US business, Herring Baker Harris Group, the chartered surveyor and property adviser, suffered pre-tax losses of Pounds 430,000 for the year ended January 31, compared with profits of Pounds 1.36m. However, included in the figures this time were pre-tax profits of Pounds 780,000 from continuing businesses, on turnover of Pounds 15.9m. This was within a group figure of Pounds 17m, compared with last year's Pounds 19.4m. Losses per share were 6.95p, against 3.97p earnings, and a halved final distribution of 1p makes a 1.5p (3.75p) total. Losses and costs from the US side were Pounds 1.2m, while the continuing businesses in the UK and Europe produced operating profits of Pounds 921,000.
