BFN [Text] Three katyusha rockets mounted and ready to be launched at Israel were found in south Lebanon this morning. The rockets were found and detonated at the site by UN soldiers in south Lebanon. Our correspondent Yo'av Aviv reports that several katyusha rockets were fired on the security zone at midday. They fell inside the south Lebanese security zone, near the Israeli border. Qiryat Shemona and confrontation line residents were ordered into shelters. Major General Yitzhaq Mordekhay, the Northern Command commander, said the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] will not permit Hizballah to bring about an escalation in the situation in south Lebanon.
