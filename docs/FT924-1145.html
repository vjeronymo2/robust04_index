921219 FT 19 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Danae net assets little changed Net asset value per income share of the Danae Investment Trust stood to 51.79p at November 30. That was unchanged on the March 31 year-end figure and marginally down on the 51.97p of 12 months earlier. Per capital share the figures were 45.48p, 50.76p and 47.29p respectively. Net revenue for the six months to end-November totalled Pounds 229,188 (Pounds 239,846), equal earnings of 3.24p (3.39p). The interim dividend is a same-again 3.375p.
