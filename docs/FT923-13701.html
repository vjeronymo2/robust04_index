920711 FT 11 JUL 92 / World News in Brief: London equities on downward path The FT-SE index moved above 2,500 briefly in early trading, but the UK stock market ended the two-week trading account firmly on the downward path again. The index closed down 7.1 at 2490.8, moving uneasily in late trading as Wall Street made a slow start to the new trading session. London stock exchange, Page 15
