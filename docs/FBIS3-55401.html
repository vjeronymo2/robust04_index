Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Boris Maynayev] [Text] Bishkek January 6 TASS -- A delegation of the German Ministry for Economic Cooperation arrived in Bishkek, the capital of Kyrgyzstan, on Thursday. The aim of the visit is to expand the cooperation between the two countries. Almabet Matubraimov, the first vice-premier and heads of several ministries and departments met the delegation. The guests brought humanitarian aid worth three million German marks for Kyrgyz children's hospitals. Besides, two agreements were signed -- on establishing Kyrgyz-German foundation for carrying out scientific research, training of specialists and developing of financial cooperation. Today the guests are going to visit southern regions of Kyrgyzstan.
