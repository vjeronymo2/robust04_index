January 30, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: SEAL BEACH;

WINTERFEST OFFERS MUSIC, FOOD-TASTING

Winterfest III, an evening of music and food-tasting to raise funds for the Los Alamitos Unified School District, will be held from 4 to 8 p.m. Sunday at the Bixby Office Park Atrium in Seal Beach.

Food will be provided by area restaurants.

Tax deductible donations of $30 per person will be used for the Teacher Grant Program administered by the Los Alamitos Unified School District Educational Foundation.

For more information, call Barbara Ledterman at (213) 598-6435.
