December 24, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: COUNTYWIDE;

ORANGE COUNTY 1990-1991 RESOLUTIONS

"I resolve to maintain our single-family neighborhoods. We have to learn say no to developers and I have.

We have got to maintain our quality of life - if not, people are not going to want to live here. I also resolve in 1991 to hire more police officers. There is a special initiative in March and I hope and pray that it is successful. As the major I will also work to get my other council members to help settle the Angels' lawsuits and to make sure the sports arena construction goes on time."
