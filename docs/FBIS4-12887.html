BFN [Text] At 1100 today, President Hafiz al-Asad received Turkish Interior Minister Nahit Mentese and his accompanying delegation. The meeting was attended by Interior Minister Dr. Muhammad Harbah and the Turkish ambassador in Damascus. Mentese conveyed to President Hafiz al-Asad greetings from Turkish President Suleyman Demirel and Prime Minister Tansu Ciller. President al-Asad asked him to convey his greetings to Demirel and Ciller. Discussions dealt with bilateral relations and cooperation within the framework of the agreements concluded between the two countries, as well as the good-neighborly relations that the two sides are eager to maintain.
