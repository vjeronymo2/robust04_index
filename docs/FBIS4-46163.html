BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Roman Zadunaiskiy] [Text] Moscow May 26 TASS -- Four unidentified terrorists seized some 30 hostages in a bus near the city of Mineralnye Vody in northern Caucasus on Thursday. According to the Russian Ministry for Emergency Situations, they are armed with three submachine guns and a grenade. The terrorists demand drugs, a helicopter without a crew, 10 million U.S. dollars, four submachine guns, four flack jackets, a grenade launcher and a night sight. The "Ikarus" coach was en route from Vladikavkaz in North Ossetia to Stavropol in southern Russia.
