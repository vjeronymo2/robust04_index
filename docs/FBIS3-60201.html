Language: Urdu Article Type:CSO [News Report: "Abbotabad Heroin Den Raided"] [Text] Abbotabad (News Bureau) -- The police very efficiently raided an old heroin den located in a densely populated part of Abbotabad and arrested a person after recovering 40 kilograms of hashish and a huge amount of heroin. According to the information available, a meeting was held under the chairmanship of Tanvirul Haq, SSP [Senior Superintendent of Police] of Abbotabad who is very interested in eradicating the curse of narcotics, to discuss effective strategies to clean up the traders of death in the area who were involved in narcotics. Roural Abbas Hussein, ASP [Additional Superintendent of Police] of the district, requested cooperation of the residents of Basti Jhuggian. The Cantonment police established a check point on Murree Road and a Suzuki with license plate number APA-1544 tried to speed through the check point. Mohammed Ilias, SHO [Station House Officer] of Cantonment, chased and then searched the Suzuki. He found 42 kilograms of hashish and arrested the driver, Mubarak Khan. The Cantonment police also raided a place in Sheikh Albandi and recovered a huge amount of narcotics, and arrested the leader of the smugglers.
