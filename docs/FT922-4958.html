920603 FT 03 JUN 92 / Opposition to air union merger A CONTROVERSIAL plan for Cabin Crew 89, the airline workers' union, to join the electrical section of the AEEU craft union seems doomed to failure after strong opposition from AEEU engineering leaders. Leaders of the engineering section yesterday told their colleagues who head the electrical section that an amalgamation with Cabin Crew could not happen because of opposition from the TUC.
