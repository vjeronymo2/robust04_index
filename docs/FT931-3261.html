930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / UK Company News: SBC to raise Dollars 50m for fund Swiss Bank Corporation is attempting to raise Dollars 50m (Pounds 35.2m) for a Jersey-based, but London-listed closed-end fund. The Environmental Investment Company will buy shares in companies which are expected to benefit from increased spending on the environment. The minimum investment in the fund is Dollars 10,000 in units of Dollars 50, comprising five shares with a warrant attached. The fund has a 10 year life and the management fee is 1.25 per cent of net assets plus 3.5 per cent of any increase in net asset value each year.
