Seedless grapes at 60-80p a lb (59-90p) continue to be good. English potatoes at 8-12p a lb (8-12p), carrots at 15-20p a lb (10-18p) and cauliflower at 35-55p each (30-59p) are all great value for money. Iceberg lettuce at 45-60p a head (50-65p), round lettuce at 20-25p each (20-25p) and Webbs at 20-35p each (30-40p) are all good quality. Tomatoes at 40-45p a lb (30-49p), celery at 35-49p a head (38-45p). (Note last weeks prices shown in brackets).
