930415 FT 15 APR 93 / 101 brewery jobs to be shed SCOTTISH and Newcastle Breweries yesterday announced 101 job losses over the next six months, affecting management, staff and hourly paid employees. It follows a restructuring of the company's central engineering and building services activities. The job losses will be mainly in Edinburgh, where 66 jobs will go, and Newcastle upon Tyne, where 28 will be lost. Nearly 80 jobs will go when the Chesswood Produce mushroom factory in Horham, Suffolk, closes in June.
