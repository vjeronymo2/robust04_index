June 18, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

SAN CLEMENTE CYCLIST RANKED 3RD IN NATION

Kenny Adams of San Clemente became the third-ranked professional cyclist in the United States Sunday, placing sixth overall in the Core State Pro Championships at Philadelphia.

Adams' teammate, Kurt Stockton of Santa Barbara, placed third overall, becoming the top-ranked American cyclist.

Adams and Stockton compete for the Anaheim-based American Commerce National Pro Cycling team.
