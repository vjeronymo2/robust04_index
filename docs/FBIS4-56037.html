BFN [Text] Warsaw, 25 May -- A three-day visit by a Chinese power industry delegation, headed by Deputy Minister Lu Yanchang, concluded on 25 May with the signing of an agreement on cooperation between the Polish Ministry of Industry and Trade and the Ministry of Power Industry of the People's Republic of China. A working group to coordinate bilateral economic, trade, and technical and scientific cooperation in the power industry was set up. "We are interested in broader cooperation with the Chinese power industry and in trade with China. Investment credits will be granted for this purpose", said Herbert Gabrys, deputy minister of trade.
