BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 Jun (MENA) -- MENA has learned that the meeting of the political committee of the 11th conference of the Non-Aligned Movement [NAM] of foreign ministers, currently being held in Cairo, has witnessed an altercation for the second time between the Iraqi and Kuwaiti foreign ministers. The Iraqi minister requested the conference issue a decision to lift the economic embargo on Iraq, and the Kuwaiti minister objected to that request. MENA has also learned that a number of participants requested that the Iraqi people's suffering be curbed and demanded that the conference issue a recommendation to that effect.
