930901 FT 01 SEP 93 / World Commodities Prices: Jute and Cotton JUTE September/October c and f Dundee BTC Dollars 320, BWC Dollars 330, BTD Dollars 305, BWD Dollars 310; c and f Antwerp BTC Dollars 315, BWC Dollars 315, BTD Dollars 290, BWD Dollars 290. COTTON Liverpool - Spot and shipment sales amounted to 52 tonnes for the week ended August 27, against 194 tonnes in the previous week. Subdued offtake did not bring many operations. Support was forthcoming in certain specialist styles, notably in the Benin and Central Asian range.
