BFN ["Former U.S. President Carter Here" -- KCNA headline] [Text] Pyongyang, June 15 (KCNA) -- Former U.S. President Jimmy Carter and his entourage arrived here today. Kim Yong-nam, vice-premier and minister of foreign affairs, met them. They were met by Song Ho-kyong, vice-minister of foreign affairs, at Panmunjom. At Panmunjom Carter said that he had longed for visiting Korea to meet its leaders from long ago and he was very glad to be invited. He said he hopes to meet President Kim Il-song. He expressed the conviction that Korea's reunification would be realized without fail.
