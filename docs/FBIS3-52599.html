Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Majesty King Husayn received at the Royal Hashemite Court this evening Faruq Qaddumi, head of the PLO Political Department, and the delegation accompanying him. During the meeting, they exchanged views on latest developments in the peace process and the importance of true and serious coordination between the Jordanian and Palestinian sides in light of the bases of His Majesty King Husayn's two recent speeches and in light of objective bases that will serve and secure the interests of the two fraternal peoples. For his part, Faruq Qaddumi stressed the importance of Jordan's constructive role in defending the Palestine question, as well as the services this country extended to the Palestinian people and their just cause. He said that the PLO admits the courageous Jordanian role in defending the rights of the Palestinian people and extending services to them in various areas. He also referred to Jordan's role in absorbing Palestinian immigrants despite its limited material potential. The meeting was attended on the Jordanian side by Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, Royal Hashemite Court Chief His Excellency al-Sharif Zayd Bin-Shakir, Deputy Prime Minister Sa'id al-Tall, and Information Minister Jawad al-'Anani. It was attended on the Palestinian side by Muhammad Zuhdi al-Nashashibi, Yasir 'Amr, and the Palestinian ambassador in Amman.
