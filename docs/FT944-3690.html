941212 FT 12 DEC 94 / Company News (This Week): Greenalls - Other Companies The diverse group with pub, off-licence, hotels and drinks distribution interests is expected tomorrow to report pre-tax profits for the year to September of about Pounds 76m (after some Pounds 9m of reorganisation costs), against Pounds 68m a year earlier. It will be boosted by the first full-year contribution from the Devenish pub estate. A triennial property review is likely to lead to a reduction in net asset value per share from 341p to around 320p. The dividend is likely to increase to around 13p from 12.36p.
