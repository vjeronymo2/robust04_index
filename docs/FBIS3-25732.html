Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The minister of external affairs, Alhaji Babagana Kingibe, will brief the nation early next week on the Nigeria- Cameroon border issue. The minister of information and culture, Professor Jerry Gana, who made this known on Radio Link, said the Federal Government was aware of the dangers of allowing the conflict to escalate. He explained that the government was doing everything possible to maintain peace and to ensure that the situation did not degenerate into war.
