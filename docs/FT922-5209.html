920602 FT 02 JUN 92 / UK bank spells out new rules THE following is a statement from the Bank of England: HM Treasury announced yesterday that, following Resolution 757 of the UN Security Council, Yugoslav (Serbian/Montenegrin) assets held in the UK are being blocked. The Bank of England, on behalf of HM Treasury, announce that bargains entered into in the money, foreign exchange and securities markets, involving Serbian or Montenegrin counter-parties, prior to 0001 hours on June 1 1992 may be completed without reference to the Bank of England. Permission is hereby given for payments to and from the accounts of individuals resident in Serbia or Montenegro, except payments to those territories, to be made without formality. Questions regarding the above should be referred to the Bank of England, telephone numbers: 071-601 4768; 071-601 4607; 071-601 3848; 071-601 3558; Fax: 071-601 4309.
