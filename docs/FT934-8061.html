931117 FT 17 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Ferraris rises to Pounds 681,000 Profits at Ferraris, the medical equipment and specialist engineering group, rose from Pounds 191,400 to Pounds 681,100 pre-tax for the year to August 31. Turnover of continuing operations edged ahead from Pounds 10.4m to Pounds 10.6m - comparative figures have been restated. Earnings rose to 6.1p (1.2p) and a recommended final dividend of 1.25p doubles the total to 2p. The directors said they looked forward to another year of 'positive growth' in 1994. The shares rose 6p to 75p.
