BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 4 May (MENA)--Prime Minister Dr. 'Atif Sidqi and a delegation left Cairo tonight for Amman on a visit of a few days to Jordan during which they will attend the meetings of the Higher Egyptian-Jordanian Committee. The committee will begin its work in Amman tomorrow under the cochairmanship of the Egyptian and Jordanian prime ministers. [passage omitted]
