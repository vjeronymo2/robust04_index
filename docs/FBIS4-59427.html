BFN [Excerpt] IDF [Israel Defense Forces] soldiers in the Gaza Strip apprehended the terrorists who wounded an Israeli truck driver near the settlement of Morag today. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan reports that the terrorists overtook the truck and opened fire. The driver, who was wounded in the chest and leg, was transferred to Soroka Hospital. Following the incident, IDF forces sealed off the area, launched a manhunt, and caught the terrorists. [passage omitted]
