940811 FT 11 AUG 94 / International Capital Markets: ASE to trade 'ratio' contracts The Australian Stock Exchange is to launch 'share ratio' contracts, based on the performance of individual shares relative to the All Ordinaries index, writes Tracy Corrigan. The contracts will be traded on the Stock Exchange Automated Trading System (SEAS) and cleared through its derivatives clearing house. Initial trading will cover ANZ Banking, Broken Hill Proprietary, National Australia Bank, News Corp, WMC and Westpac.
