BFN [Text] Baku, May 11 (SNARK)--Ian Elliasson, a chairman of the CSCE Minsk group, came to Moscow where he took up negotiations with the officials of Russia. The Baku radio reports on the meetings of Ian Elliasson with the USA and Turkey representatives which preceded his visit to Moscow. The Baku radio states the Russian side says "no" to the plan introduced by Mr. Elliasson. Ian Elliasson is of the opinion that only Russia could settle the conflict. Ian Elliasson is expected to visit Transcaucasia.
