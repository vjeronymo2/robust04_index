940702 FT 02 JUL 94 / UK Company News: OMI Intl OMI International, the electro-hydraulics, logistics and electro-optics company, reported a substantial improvement for the year to March 31 with losses falling from Pounds 5.02m to Pounds 100,000. The figure was struck after charges of Pounds 1.24m relating to discontinued activities. Turnover was Pounds 36.6m, including Pounds 7.47m from acquisitions, against Pounds 37.3m, including Pounds 3.75m from discontinued activities. There was a profit before interest of Pounds 450,000 (Pounds 4.85m loss), after taking into account Pounds 999,000 of exceptional costs most of which related to losses on design and build contracts. After a tax credit there were earnings per share of 0.1p (11.6p losses). An unchanged final dividend of 1p maintains the total at 1.75p.
