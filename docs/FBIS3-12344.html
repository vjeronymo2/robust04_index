Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] His Excellency President Ilyas al-Hirawi, Prime Minister Rafiq al-Hariri, and Chamber of Deputies Speaker Nabih Birri have discussed the horrific massacre in the al-Ibrahimi Mosque. They released the following statement: In expression of overwhelming anger and strong condemnation of the al-Ibrahimi Mosque massacre during the dawn prayers, and in affirmation of solidarity with the patient and steadfast Palestinian people, Lebanon--its president, the Council of Ministers, the Chamber of Deputies, and people--hereby declares national mourning and general closure of businesses throughout Lebanon on Thursday, 3 March, 1994, to mark the one week anniversary of the Hebron massacre.
