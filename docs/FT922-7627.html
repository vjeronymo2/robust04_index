920516 FT 16 MAY 92 / Nadir hearing AN APPLICATION from Mr Asil Nadir, the former Polly Peck chairman and chief executive, for the dismissal of some or all of the charges against him is to be heard at Stafford Crown Court on Monday, where Mr Justice Tucker, the judge assigned to Mr Nadir's case, is on circuit. Mr Nadir faces 66 charges of theft and three of false accounting for amounts totalling Pounds 100m.
