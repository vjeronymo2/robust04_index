NEIGHBORHOOD EATERY

MORE EATERIES:

Hogue Barmichael's, 1976 Newport Blvd., Costa Mesa. The kitchen is open Monday through Friday, 11:30 a.m. to 2:30 p.m. and 5 to 9 p.m.; Saturday and Sunday from noon to 9 p.m. (714) 645-3678.

Best known as a premiere meet market, on Friday and Saturday nights this bar/restaurant continues to live up to its reputation. But weeknights, there's plenty of room to sit, order a sizzling steak, a crisp salad or a juicy hamburger and take in the funky decor. JAMES M. GOMEZ
