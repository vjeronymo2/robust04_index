931110 FT 10 NOV 93 / UK Company News: Hartlepools Water rises to Pounds 720,000 Pre-tax profits of Hartlepools Water showed a 7 per cent improvement from Pounds 670,000 to Pounds 720,000 for the six months to September 30. Turnover grew by Pounds 150,000 to Pounds 2.76m. An improved interim dividend of 23p (22p) is declared, payable from earnings per share of 71p (67p).
