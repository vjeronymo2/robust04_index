BFN [Text] Cairo, 3 May (MENA) -- This evening in the Presidential Office, President Husni Mubarak received Dr. Han Wan-sang, personal envoy [name and title as received] of South Korean President Kim Yong-sam, who is visiting Egypt. The Korean envoy said he delivered a message to President Mubarak from the South Korean president. He praised the relations linking Egypt and South Korea, noting that it is high time to develop these relations to the full to face up to the challenges following the Cold War era. Touching on developments in Korea, the envoy said that ties of cooperation between the two Koreas could be developed if North Korea abandons its nuclear activity. In that case, our country could extend assistance to North Korea to help it become a full-fledged member of the international community and we could work together to achieve regional stability, he said.
