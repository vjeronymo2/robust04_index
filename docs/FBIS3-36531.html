Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Irina Shchegoleva] [Text] Sochi February 6 TASS--"Georgian armed groups made a mortar attack on Abkhazian positions on the River Inguri and blew up a railway bridge in Gali district last night", Abkhazian Prime Minister Sokrat Dzhindzholia told ITAR-TASS. "In reply, Abkhazian armed forces began to clear the territory of the militant groups. The operation is proceeding successfully," he said. "There are now 1,500 militants from among Georgian armed groups in Gali district. They fought gun-battles with Abkhazian forces and attacked civilians," Dzhindzholia pointed out.
