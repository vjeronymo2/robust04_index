BFN [Text] The Ministry of Supply and Trade warns all wholesale and retail dealers in the Republic of Yemen about the need to comply fully with the prices for all commodities that prevailed before the emergency was declared in the country. Anyone breaching this warning will be find, have his trade registration confiscated, and his shop closed. He will also be subject to direct imprisonment. The ministry will launch a supply monitoring campaign to control commodity prices. A team from the Ministry of Supply and Trade, the Interior Ministry, and the capital's municipality will take part in the campaign. The ministry's Aden branch and the supply offices in the republic's governorates should also form committees from the offices, the ministry's monitors, and the governorate's security operations for this purpose. We hope everyone will comply and cooperate. It is God who grants success.
