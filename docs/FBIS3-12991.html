Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Russian Deputy Foreign Minister Yegor Ivanov says all sides should return to the Middle East peace talks without preconditions. Ivanov met with Prime Minister Rabin this morning at the Defense Ministry in Tel Aviv. Dani Gur-Arye reports: Prime Minister Rabin and Deputy Foreign Minister Ivanov met for almost an hour in Tel Aviv. Later Ivanov, who recently met with PLO leader Yasir 'Arafat, told reporters most meetings centered on ways to renew the peace talks: [Begin Ivanov recording; Ivanov speaks in Russian with superimposed translation into English] In this new situation none of the sides can set preconditions. The best solution is for all sides to return promptly to the talks. [end recording] Ivanov was asked about criticism Rabin levelled recently at Russia over its attempt to mediate between the sides independently of the United States. [Begin Ivanov recording] Russia and the United States are the co-sponsors of the Middle East process, and there are ground rules for cooperation. With that, each of the co-sponsors is free to take its own initiatives as long as the goal is to further the peace talks. [end recording] Ivanov said he is convinced Russia can help bring the sides back together.
