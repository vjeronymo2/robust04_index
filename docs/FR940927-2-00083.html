Interest Rates The interest rate on Section 7(a) Small Business Administration direct loans (as amended by PL 97&hyph;35) and the SBA share of immediate participation loans is 83/8 percent for the fiscal quarter beginning September 1, 1995. On a quarterly basis, the Small Business Administration also publishes an interest rate called the optional ``peg'' rate (13 CFR 122.8&hyph;4 (d)). This rate is a weighted average cost of money to the government for maturities similar to the average SBA loan. This rate may be used as a base rate for guaranteed fluctuating interest rate SBA loans. For the September&hyph;December quarter of FY 95, this rate will be 73/8 percent. Dated: September 22, 1994.
