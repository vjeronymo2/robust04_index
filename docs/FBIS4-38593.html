BFN ["Ukrainian Presidential Edict on First Deputy Chairman of the Coordination Committee for Combating Corruption and Organized Crime Subordinated to the Ukrainian President" issued in Kiev on 25 May] [Text] 1. To appoint Vasyl Vasylyovych Durdynets as first deputy chairman of the Coordination Committee for Combating Corruption and Organized Crime Subordinated to the Ukrainian president. 2. The edict becomes effective on the day of its signing. Ukraine's President L. Kravchuk Kiev, 25 May 1994
