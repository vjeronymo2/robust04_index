Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The Uzbekistan State TV and Radio Company stopped the relay of morning and evening Novosti news bulletins of the Ostankino TV and Radio Company. INTERFAX reports that the reason for this was that the Ostankino leadership had failed to sign an agreement with Uzbekistan on the relay of its programs for this year. At the same time, it was pointed out the relay of entertainment and other programs of Ostankino continued to the full degree.
