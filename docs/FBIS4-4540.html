BFN [Text] Members of jungle-based armed organizations have been returning to the legal fold along with their arms and ammunitions at various garrisons. This is because they have realized their wrongdoings and have gained correct views. They have also accepted and have faith in the genuine goodwill and activities of the State Law and Order Restoration Council. Mulaki alias Pwint Lin, 35, the son of U Ba Sin of Katokan Village, Tenasserim Township and the platoon leader of the Muslim armed organization in the jungle, returned to the legal fold at Tenasserim garrison on 13 March 1994. He brought in one AK-47 assault rifle, one magazine, and 20 rounds of ammunition.
