Language: Thai Article Type:BFN [Text] Army Spokesman Colonel Phalangkun Klahan said that some newspapers had presented reports accusing the Army of supporting the Khmer Rouge [KR], banning reporters and diplomats from visiting the border, and forcing the repatriation of Cambodian refugees. They also publicized photos showing relations between Thai and Khmer Rouge soldiers. The spokesman said the reports were untrue and were causing damage to the Armed Forces. He said that the Armed Forces upheld humanitarian principles and UN policy in assisting the refugees. It is the duty of the Cambodian Government to look after the refugees since they are Cambodians. He reaffirmed that the Armed Forces did not support the Khmer Rouge.
