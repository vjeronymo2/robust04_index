Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Prime Minister Dr. 'Abd-al-Salam al-Majali, accompanied by Interior Minister Salamah Hammad, today visited the Public Security Directorate. He discussed with Lieutenant General 'Abd-al- Rahman al-'Adwan, director of Public Security, a number of issues dealing with the needs of the public security body and its role in maintaining the security and stability of the Jordanian community. Regarding the objectives of the visit, Lt. Gen. al-'Adwan said that the prime minister's visit was designed to reassure him about the situation in the public security body and how to meet its needs so it can continue its outstanding performance and loyalty in carrying out its noble mission. He said the prime minister was briefed on the public security situation in the Kingdom and the qualitative training activities implemented by the public security body to maintain its abilities, preparedness, and the level of performance of the officers, noncommissioned officers, and soldiers--something highlighted during recent incidents on a level that calls for pride. Al-'Adwan said that the prime minister expressed his confidence in and admiration of the level reached by the public security body. He said he is fully reassured about its ability to fully carry out its tasks.
