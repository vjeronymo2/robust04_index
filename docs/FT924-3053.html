921209 FT 09 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Umeco declines to Pounds 53,000 Profits of Umeco fell from Pounds 68,000 to Pounds 53,000 pre-tax for the 26 weeks ended September 26. Turnover of Pounds 5.56m compared with Pounds 5.42m. Earnings slipped to 0.6p (0.8p) but the interim dividend is maintained at 0.75p 'in view of the strong balance sheet and the underlying strength of the company', the directors said. The USM-quoted company is a maker of aircraft refuellers and a distributor of aerospace components.
