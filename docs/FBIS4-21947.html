BFN [DDP report: "FOCUS Insists: Slip-Ups Occurred in Bad Kleinen"] [Excerpt] Munich -- Despite official denials, slip-ups are believed to have occurred during the antiterrorism operation in Bad Kleinen, which led to the escape of four members of the Red Army Faction [RAF]. This was confirmed yesterday by the news magazine FOCUS. The magazine quotes notes and reports by analysts of the Federal Office of Criminal Investigation [BKA]. In December 1993, the BKA prepared a leaflet showing a man and a woman. Moreover, the magazine quotes the following note: "During a later evaluation of the videotapes of the operation in Bad Kleinen, a specialist of BKA section 21, which is responsible for terrorism, discovered that the man might be one and the same as the man who is believed to have been involved in the attack on former Trust Agency head Rohwedder." An analyst stated that he is "100 percent sure" that the unknown man participated in the attack, the magazine reports. [passage omitted]
