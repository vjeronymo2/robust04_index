October 5, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

CRIME BUSTERS: There is sometimes a trade-off when cities like Anaheim and Santa Ana make major commitments to solve violent crimes like assault, above, and rape (A1). . . . Lesser crimes involving property may be neglected as a result, says Lt. Marc Hedgpeth, commander of the Crimes Against Persons Bureau in Anaheim.

"We are always robbing Peter to pay Paul."

CRIMES SOLVED

Anaheim leads major cities in the state in solving assault cases. Santa Ana ranks fifth.

Santa Ana:

Assaults reported: 1,983

Assaults cleared: 1,125

56.7% convicted

Anaheim:

Assaults reported: 2,550

Assaults cleared: 2,077

81.5% convicted

Source: California Dept. of Justice Steve Emmons and Mark I. Pinsky
