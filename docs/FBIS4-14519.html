BFN ["Ukrainian Presidential Edict on Measures for the Development of Economic Cooperation Between Ukrainian Oblasts and Adjacent Border-Zone Oblasts of the Russian Federation" issued in Kiev on 25 Mar] [Text] In order to expand cooperation between Ukrainian oblasts and adjacent border area oblasts of the Russian Federation, and to promote the development of their economic relations, I resolve as follows: 1. Ukraine's Cabinet of Ministers must: -- Prepare, within a month, a draft agreement between the Government of Ukraine and the Government of the Russian Federation on cooperation between the border area oblasts and start negotiations with the Russian Federation on the conclusion of this agreement; -- Elaborate, within two months, with the participation of Donetsk, Luhansk, Sumy, Kharkiv, and Chernihiv oblast state administrations, and adopt normative acts on the legal regulation of relations between the aforementioned oblasts and border area oblasts of the Russian Federation. 2. To grant the following rights to Donetsk, Luhansk, Sumy, Kharkiv, and Chernihiv oblasts: -- To participate in establishing rules of administration in the border areas with an account of local characteristics; -- To carry out, within the limits of established quotas, export-import operations for the needs of the oblasts at preferential conditions, which are to be determined by Ukraine's Cabinet of Ministers; -- With the participation of corresponding Ukrainian ministries, to coordinate with the leadership of oblasts of the Russian Federation quotas for free training of specialists in educational institutions of the border area oblasts. 3. To recommend that Ukraine's National Bank, with the participation of corresponding state administrations, promote the creation of joint credits and finance institutions with various forms of ownership for the purpose of financing programs for interoblast cooperation, including investments and credits for entrepreneurs. [Signed] L. Kravchuk, President of Ukraine [Dated] Kiev, 25 March 1994
