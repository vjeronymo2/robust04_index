931230 FT 30 DEC 93 / World news in brief: Former RTZ Chairman dies Sir Alistair Frame, former chairman of RTZ, died on Sunday at his home in Surrey. He was 64. A noted nuclear engineer, Sir Alistair joined Rio Tinto-Zinc in 1968 and chaired the company from 1985 to 1991. He went on to serve as chairman of Wellcome and British Steel, but was forced to resign because of illness.
