920710 FT 10 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Dowding & Mills Dowding & Mills, a provider of electrical and mechanical services, has entered the US market via the acquisition of Equipment Maintenance Services for Dollars 11m (Pounds 5.7m). Consideration will be satisfied by the issue of 10.82m new shares to the vendors, conditionally placed with institutions at 53p apiece with a clawback for existing shareholders on the basis of one new share for every 12.38 held. Further consideration to a maximum Dollars 12m is dependent on profits. EMS, based in Wyoming, operates in similar markets to Dowding. D&M's shares slipped 4 1/2 p to 54 1/2 p.
