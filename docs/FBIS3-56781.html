Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Tbilisi, 13 Jan -- An Iberia correspondent learned that the Russian Government has notified the Georgian authorities through unofficial sources that Russia has agreed to accept Georgia in the ruble zone provided Georgia agrees to the following terms: 1. To refuse foreign assets; 2. to hand over to Russia goldmines or pawn Georgia's state property in an exchange for the amount of imported Russian rubles; 3. to refuse carrying out its own credit emissions; 4. the Russian Government is to confirm the state budget [as heard]; 5. to annul all economic laws and introduce laws adopted by Russia on the territory of Georgia; 6. to annul the Georgian customs system and merge it with the Russian customs office; 7. the Russian Government is to appoint one of the vice presidents of the National Bank of Georgia.
