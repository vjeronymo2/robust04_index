941224 FT 24 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Kelsey at Pounds 1.46m A substantial improvement in its soldering products business enabled Kelsey Industries to report profits for the year to September 30 ahead from Pounds 212,000 to Pounds 1.46m. Turnover was little changed at Pounds 50.1m, against Pounds 49.7m. The Hertfordshire-based company, which also has interests in automotive components and industrial roofing, said the result would have been even better but for depressed conditions which affected the UK roofing business. However, with an improved order book, the roofing arm is expected to return to profits. After tax of Pounds 463,000 (Pounds 385,000) and minorities of Pounds 66,000 (Pounds 30,000) earnings per share came out at 24.5p, against losses of 12.2p. A doubled final dividend of 10p raises the total to 13p (8p).
