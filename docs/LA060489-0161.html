June 4, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

THANKS FROM ZIMBABWE

I would like to take this opportunity to thank you for publishing Grahame L. Jones' article on Victoria Falls ("The Smoke That Thunders," March 19). Although our office address and telephone number were not given in the article, we have received a lot of inquiries about our country, Zimbabwe, from your readers.

MARY TENDAI HATENDI
