August 18, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

SPOTLIGHT / COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE: FOCUS: 1 CITY BLVD. WEST BUILDING

Formerly the Bank of America Tower, this 18-story building was completed nearly 20 years ago. The lobby was renovated in 1987 and enlarged. The building is located within The City in Orange, a development which also includes a regional mall, 15 restaurants, 10 movie theaters, a health spa and the Doubletree Hotel. The tower is 78% leased.
