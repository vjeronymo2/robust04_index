941126 FT 26 NOV 94 / UK Company News: McKechnie chief gets 62% rise Mr Michael Ost, chief exec-utive of McKechnie, the plastics and metals components group, received a pay rise of 62 per cent for the year to July 31, taking his earnings to Pounds 275,748. Mr Ost's basic salary rose from Pounds 145,000 to Pounds 185,067, while his performance-related bonus climbed to Pounds 65,519 (Pounds 13,431). Among other benefits, he also received pension contributions of Pounds 13,116. Mr Ost's pay is decided by a committee of non-executive directors. The bonus is set by a formula covering the percentage increase in earnings per share over four years and the achievement of the cashflow budget for that year. McKechnie's earnings in 1993-94 were 27.1p (22p in 1990-91). In 1993-94 its net cash inflow before financing was Pounds 2.05m.
