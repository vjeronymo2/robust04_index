BFN [Text] Interior Minister Nahit Mentese has concluded his official visit to Romania and has returned to Turkey. In a statement at Esenboga Airport, Mentese said that in his talks in Romania, positive developments were achieved on the issues of domestic security and drugs and arms smuggling. He added that the Turkish delegation is continuing the talks. Pointing out that Turkey has candid relations with Romania on the issue of terror, Mentese said that Romania has detained certain PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] members. He noted that terror will be defeated in Romania as well. In reply to a question, Mentese said Greece should stop supporting the PKK, recalling that it is the duty of all world countries to fight terrorism.
