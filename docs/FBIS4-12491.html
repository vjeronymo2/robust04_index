BFN ["Exclusive" report] [Text] Mas'ud Rajavi, leader of the Iranian resistance, has urged the Iranian people to escalate the struggle against the ruling regime in Iran, to pave the way for the liberation army to pounce on the regime. Addressing the sons of Iran during a gathering of thousands of national liberation army fighters at one of the liberation army's camps [in Iraq] on the new Iranian year, Rajavi said: "Escalate your protests against the authority. These protests will pave the way for the liberation army's major assault. The regime is destined to fall." The resistance leader gave an elaborate account of the Iranians' tragic economic and social situation under the ruling regime because of the $50-million foreign debt and the collapse of the rial exchange rate.
