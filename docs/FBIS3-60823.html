Language: English Article Type:BFN [Report by ITIM] [Excerpt] A 60-year-old security guard found murdered in Ashdod Thursday evening was apparently the victim of a nationalistic attack, police said last night. Hamas [Islamic Resistance Movement] claimed responsibility for the murder. Anatoliy Kalsanikov, a guard at a building site in Ashdod's Rova' Tet neighborhood, was found by a night watchman who had come to replace him. Kalsanikov had suffered deep stab wounds in the neck and chest. [passage omitted]
