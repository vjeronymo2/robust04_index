BFN [Excerpt] The Croatian and Macedonian [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM] Chambers of Economics have signed a cooperation agreement. This important document was signed 2 June by the chairmen of the two chambers, Mladen Vedris and Dusan Petrevski. Albino Cehic reports: [Cehic] The agreement has been concluded to improve cooperation and overall economic and trade relations between the two countries and to establish stronger links between enterprises.
