January 23, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: LAGUNA HILLS;

RECYCLING DRIVE AIDS COMMUNITY GROUPS

A recycling drive sponsored by two community groups will be held Saturday in the Target Store parking lot at Alicia Parkway and Charlinda Drive.

From 9 a.m. to noon, volunteers from the Laguna Hills Community Assn. and the Aegean Hills Homeowners Assn. will accept plastic soda bottles, glass containers, newspapers and aluminum.

Businesses are asked to save computer print-out paper. Residents should bundle newspapers in paper bags. Phone books, paperback books and magazines cannot be recycled.

Proceeds will provide scholarships for local high school students and will help finance newsletters for the community groups.
