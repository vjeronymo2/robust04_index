January 17, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

PEOPLE: CALIFORNIA

Fleetwood Enterprises, Riverside, has named Lawrence F. Pittroff senior vice president. Robert W. Graham has been named vice president-administration and human resources, succeeding Pittroff. L. Bruce Boylen succeeds Graham as vice president-travel trailers. Larry L. Mace, previously director of supply-RV group, replaces Boylen as vice president-supply subsidiaries.
