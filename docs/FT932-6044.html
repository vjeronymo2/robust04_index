930601 FT 01 JUN 93 / Demonstrations in Solingen Riot police in Solingen, Germany, yesterday drive back demonstrators two days after the deaths of two Turkish women and three girls in an arson attack blamed on neo-Nazis. Early yesterday, protesters rampaged through the town, near Cologne, looting and causing damage. Police have arrested a 16-year-old rightwinger on suspicion of murder and fire-raising, but inquiries continue. Kinkel appeals for calm, Page 2
