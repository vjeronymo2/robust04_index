Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA Jan. 31 -- Howard Balloch, assistant deputy minister for Asia and Pacific Department of Foreign Affairs and International Trade for Canada, visited Vietnam from Jan. 28-31. While here, Mr. Howard Balloch was received by Mr Le Xuan Trinh, minister and director of the government office and by Mr Nguyen Dinh Loc, minister of justice. He had working sessions with Deputy Foreign Minister Le Mai and Deputy Minister of Trade Mai Van Dau. Mr Howard Balloch also met with senior representatives of Petro Vietnam, the directorate general of Post and Telecommunications and the Vietnam Post and Telecommunications. During their meetings, the two sides exchanged views and reached unanimity on some measures aimed at further strengthening the cooperative relations between Vietnam and Canada.
