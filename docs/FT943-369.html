940929 FT 29 SEP 94 / UK Company News: HCG Lloyd's Trust HCG Lloyd's Investment Trust, which was floated in December last year as a vehicle for limited liability investment at Lloyd's, announced a net asset value of 89p per share as at the June 30 half-year end. Total net assets stood at Pounds 57.9m on June 30 against Pounds 66.1m on December 31. At August 31, the date used by Lloyd's for revaluation, the value of the portfolios had increased to Pounds 58.9m, giving a net asset value per share of 97p. Net revenue was Pounds 658,000, to give earnings per share of 1.01p.
