May 4, 1989, Thursday, P.M. Final

SOUTHLAND;

NO WINNER; LOTTO POT $12 MILLION

No one picked all six winning numbers in the $5.5-million "Lotto 6-49" game Wednesday night, and the jackpot will grow Saturday to an estimated $12 million, lottery officials said today.

Five players picked five numbers plus the bonus to win $298,853 each, while 242 players chose five of six numbers to pick up $3,181 each, and 14,474 players picked four of six numbers to win $48 each. Three of six was worth an automatic $5 each to 272,528 players.
