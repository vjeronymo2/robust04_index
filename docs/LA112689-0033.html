November 26, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

KUSC'S REVISED FORMAT HAS GIVEN A REVISED FORM TO AT LEAST ONE LISTENER

Re "Classical Music Radio: What's Not Too Stuffy and Yet Not Too Hip?" by Claudia Puig, Nov. 19: I wish all those people sending hate mail to Wallace Smith, KUSC-FM general manager, would stop. The new format is absolutely terrific.

Every time a KUSC radio personality starts bumbling through some "imponderable" or a traffic report or programs only a single movement from some work, I simply turn off the radio and go for a walk. It's wonderful! In the last two months I've lost 23 pounds.

Keep up the good work, Dr. Smith. By this time next year I should be ready for a marathon!

STANLEY K. STRAUSS

La Verne
