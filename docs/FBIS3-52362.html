Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] President leader Saddam Husayn has ordered that the allowances given to the dependent minors of pensioners be increased from 200 to 300 dinars. A responsible source at the Ministry of Justice has stated this to an IRAQI NEWS AGENCY correspondent. The source added that the ministry made efforts to improve the department's performance in the care of minors eliminating unnecessary steps in the paperwork. This paperwork is usually monitored by those guardians who oversee the minors. The new procedures are meant to facilitate things for the minors and to assist in processing their application forms as quickly as possible, he noted. The source added that this department implements the task of caring for minors, preserving their rights and funds, and investing their money in a way that achieves the best revenues. He pointed out that the minors' balance totals 1.0003 billion dinars. [passage omitted]
