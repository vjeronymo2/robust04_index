BFN [Text] The State Council chairman, Mr. David Kpomakpor, has been warned not to allow himself to be used as puppet by Amos Sawyer and his bunch of political misfits who have brought disgrace to the West African subregion and untold suffering to the Liberian people. In a statement issued, the executive committee of CAG [expansion unknown] said since the induction of the State Council over one month ago, the council has made no effort to visit other parts of the country to ensure genuine reunification and a process of confidence-building. The statement said Councilor Kpomakpor, as an eminent person on the State Council, must conduct himself in a more credible way to ensure that the contemporary wishes and aspirations of the Liberian people are protected.
