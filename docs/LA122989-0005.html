December 29, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

LEONA HELMSLEY

Leona Helmsley was correct when she said, "Only the little people pay taxes." Her big mistake was in bragging about it!

Her conviction will probably not result in an all-out assault on the many other tycoons who cheat the IRS. But it will remind them not to be so arrogant and outspoken about it!

CLARENCE W. HAYWARD

Los Angeles
