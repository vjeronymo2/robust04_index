BFN [Text] [no dateline as received] According to Arkan [commander of the Serbian Volunteer Guard], there is no room in Kosova either for those who deserted the Yugoslav Army. Arkan hailed "The construction of the refugee camps on the border between Albania and Kosova," adding that his party will soon start an initiative related to the restitution of 7,300 hectares of fertile land which according to Arkan is in the hands of Macedonia [The Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia -- FYROM]. Some days ago, the BUJKU newspaper wrote that paramilitary troops of Arkan and his spokesman Borsiwav Televic [name as received] stayed in Dragash in the border zone with Albania where they provoked some citizens. A report from the Information Centre of Kosova said that Serbian military forces and paramilitary troops have been concentrated on the border zone of Kosova with Albania. A report of the Information Centre of the Democratic League [of Kosova -- LDK] branch in Gjakova on April 30 said that tanks of the Serbian Army directed to Albania and Gjakova have been planted in the vicinity of the border with Albania, in the village Zhup of Gjakova.
