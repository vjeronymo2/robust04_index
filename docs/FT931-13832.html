930121 FT 21 JAN 93 / UK Company News: Wm Ransom keeps up early growth The good start made by William Ransom & Son in the opening four months continued, and for the half year ended September 30 1992 the group lifted sales 7.5 per cent and pre-tax profits 15 per cent. The group manufactures pharmaceuticals, toiletries and cosmetics, and is involved in research for the food and beverage industry. Sales came to Pounds 3.4m (Pounds 3.17m) and profits Pounds 289,000 (Pounds 251,000). Earnings per share were 1.24p (1.16p) and the interim dividend is again 0.525p.
