February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

GRAND CANYON DEFEATS CHAPMAN

Freshman shortstop Doug Kimble hit a three-run double to lead Grand Canyon College to a 7-4 victory over Chapman Monday in a nonconference baseball game in Phoenix.

Kimble's double highlighted a four-run first inning for Grand Canyon (5-0) and teammate Matt Hyde helped with solo home runs in the second and fourth innings.

Steve Halweg (1-1) took the loss, giving up all seven runs in four innings. The Panthers had 13 hits and no errors.

Doug Stenz (2-0) pitched 5 2/3 innings, giving up 10 hits and four runs for the victory.

In a Western Intercollegiate Volleyball Assn. men's game:

Pepperdine 3, UC Irvine 0 -- Randy Bergstedt had 10 kills to lead visiting Pepperdine (6-4, 2-2) to a 15-10, 15-3, 15-5 victory. Steve Florentine had 11 kills and 4 blocks for Irvine (0-4, 0-4).
