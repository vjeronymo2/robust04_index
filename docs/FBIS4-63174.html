BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow May 19 TASS -- Taking into account the acuteness and significance of problems of a social policy, Russian President Boris Yeltsin issued a decree on the establishment of a council on a social policy attached to the president of the Russian Federation. The primary tasks of the council will be to inform the head of state of the processes going on in the social sphere, working out proposals on a strategy of a social policy and its priority directions, the analysis and expertise of drafts of legislative acts on social issues, and the preparation of proposals on the conclusion of inter-state agreements and treaties on social issues. Ella Panfilova was appointed chairman of the council. This was reported by the Russian president's press service.
