BFN [Excerpts] Riyadh, 2 Apr -- The Saudi foreign minister, Prince Sa'ud al-Faysal, denied today that there are differences of opinion between the Gulf Cooperation Council states regarding the stance toward the Iraqi regime. He said: "We have not noticed the existence of differences regarding this matter whether on the level of the summit or the current level," in allusion to the foreign ministers meeting of the states of the council which started its work here today under his chairmanship. Prince Sa'ud spoke to journalists at the end of the second working session of the ministers here tonight. [passage omitted]
