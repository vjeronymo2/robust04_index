Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpts] The Chamber of Deputies held a plenary session today. [passage omitted] Deputy 'Ali 'Ammar assailed the U.S. ambassador's interference in Lebanon's internal affairs. He said: Before the two massacres were committed [Hebron killings and Lebanese church explosion], it was expected that something would happen in Lebanon, as shown by the U.S. ambassador's statements. The ambassador's interference goes beyond all international norms and constitutes intervention in Lebanese internal affairs like between a victim and a murderer. He also provokes certain agents and symbols to foment sedition. Deputy 'Ammar added: It is time that the pertinent authorities stopped responding to the U.S. intentions that seek to cause sedition among the Lebanese people. He asked the Chamber of Deputies to adopt a stand denouncing the U.S. envoy's behavior. [passage omitted] [Beirut Voice of Lebanon in Arabic at 1235 GMT on 8 March adds: "Deputy 'Ali 'Ammar called U.S. Ambassador Mark Hambley the ambassador of filth [safir al-danas]. He described his movements as suspicious and as going beyond diplomatic limits. He said that the ambassador equates victims and criminals."]
