Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] China is not in favor of any form of independence for Jammu and Kashmir. The Chinese feelings over the Kashmir issue were conveyed to Islamabad prior to the India-Pakistan foreign secretary level talks which concluded yesterday. Reports say that China had made it clear to the Pakistan prime minister, Ms. Benazir Bhutto, when she visited Beijing last month, that China would like a negotiated solution of the Kashmir issue and is opposed to any form of independence which some separatist groups desire.
