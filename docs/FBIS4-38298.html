BFN [Report by Shafiq al-Asadi: "Emirates Seize Boat Carrying Iranian Infiltrators"] [Excerpts] Abu Dhabi -- Border and coast guard forces in the United Arab Emirates [UAE] have seized a boat carrying Iranian infiltrators who were trying to anchor off the UAE coast in the emirate of Umm al-Qaywayn, at a time when the UAE is taking decisive measures to foil infiltration operations and tackle the imbalance in the population structure. A spokesman for the border guard forces confirmed that the boat was seized after border guard forces opened fire on it and after a chase in the Gulf in which Interior Ministry helicopters took part. The spokesman said that none of the infiltrators was hurt, the boat has been handed over to border guard forces, and the infiltrators have been referred to the judiciary for legal proceedings to be taken against them. The UAE is currently taking tough measures to foil infiltration incidents conducted by various types of boats which anchor off its coasts along the Gulf and the Gulf of Oman. [passage omitted] The UAE Interior Ministry said in a statement distributed yesterday that border and coast guard forces, supported by police helicopters, foiled an attempt to infiltrate into the UAE by a speedboat which was trying to anchor off the coast of Umm al-Qaywayn. The statement said the boat was fired on but no one was hurt, and the boat was seized and all the people on board were arrested. [passage omitted]
