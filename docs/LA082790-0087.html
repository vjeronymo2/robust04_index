August 27, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

WINGED FLAP: Potential controversy over a winged male nude painted by artist Jim Morphesis for the John Wayne Airport arts program has led airport officials to cancel a printing of several thousand posters for the new airport terminal's Sept. 8 open house. . . . The airport arts commission will decide this week whether to display the original, which resembles a classical torso with an Icarus theme . . . Although less revealing than Michaelangelo's Renaissance works, the painting drew nervous reactions from some county officials. Lanie Jones and Lily Eng
