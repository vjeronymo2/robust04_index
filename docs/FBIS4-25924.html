BFN [Text] Shanghai, June 15 (XINHUA) -- Estonian President Lennart Meri and his party toured the Jinqiao (Golden Bridge) Export Processing Zone here this afternoon. The six-sq-km zone, located in the heart of the Pudong New Area, boasts over 200 projects, each involving an average 13 million U.S. dollars in foreign investment. This afternoon, President Meri also visited township enterprises and farm families in suburban Nanhui County. In the meantime, Estonian entrepreneurs touring with Meri held talks with their local counterparts. President Meri and his party arrived here this morning from Beijing.
