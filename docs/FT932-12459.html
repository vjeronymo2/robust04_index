930428 FT 28 APR 93 / UK Company News: Dakota recovers to IPounds 1.36m Dakota, the Dublin-based packaging and stationery group, reported a recovery in pre-tax profits from IPounds 144,000 to IPounds 1.36m (Pounds 1.33m) in the six months to March 31. Turnover on continuing activities edged ahead from IPounds 8.69m to IPounds 9.41m. The results took in exceptional profits of IPounds 1.11m, reflecting a loss on the disposal of Technodisc in February, outweighed by a gain on fixed assets at Ormond Printing. Earnings per share were 4.8p compared with 0.2p.
