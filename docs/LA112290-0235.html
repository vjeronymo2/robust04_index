November 22, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

DIGEST / LOCAL NEWS IN BRIEF: EAGLE ROCK MAN BEATEN BY GROUP

An Eagle Rock man was beaten and stabbed outside his house late Saturday night by a group of young men, Los Angeles police said.

Henry Raul Espino, 24, told police he was in his house in the 1600 block of Colorado Boulevard when he heard glass breaking about 10:40 p.m. When he went out to investigate, he was assaulted by about 15 men in their late teens to early 20s, said Detective Joe Friend.

Espino was treated at Glendale Adventist Medical Center for cuts and bruises and released.
