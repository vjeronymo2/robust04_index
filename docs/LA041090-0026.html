April 10, 1990, Tuesday, Valley Edition

REGIONAL PEOPLE

Richard M. Drysdale has been elected chairman of Terminal Data Corp., a Moorpark-based producer of document-imaging equipment.

Drysdale, 58, succeeded Stanley R. Schoen, who had been chairman for the past two years.

Schoen, 67, resigned for personal reasons but remains a Terminal Data director, the company said. Drysdale, a Terminal Data director, also is president of Westwood Management Co., a Los Angeles venture capital firm.
