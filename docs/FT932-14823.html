930416 FT 16 APR 93 / UK Company News: Tudor showing improving trend Tudor, the USM-quoted homewares company, made a small profit in the second half of 1992 cutting the year's loss to Pounds 104,000 after exceptional charges of Pounds 74,000. In the previous year there was a profit of Pounds 23,000. Turnover amounted to Pounds 16.7m (Pounds 12.5m). In view of the prospects a single dividend of 0.25p is being paid, against an interim of 0.5p in 1991. Cost of the payment is virtually unchanged following the acquisition of MGB Tiles which represented 48 per cent of the enlarged capital. Losses per share were 1.16p (earnings 0.72p).
