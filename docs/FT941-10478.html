940209 FT 09 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Apollo Metals faces 'short-term pain' Mr Albert Hargreaves, chairman of Apollo Metals, told the annual meeting that the balance sheet remained strong, stocks and debtors were under control, and gearing was relatively low. However, he saw no reason to change the view he expressed in his annual statement that the company may have to experience further short-term pain in order to achieve its longer-term strategic goals.
