930510 FT 10 MAY 93 / NZ 'will debate' republic issue Talk of Australia dumping Queen Elizabeth as head of state and becoming a republic will trigger a similar debate in New Zealand, Prime Minister Jim Bolger said yesterday, Reuter writes from Wellington. 'It is an issue which could have lain dormant for years but now because of Australia's moves, will become a matter of discussion and debate,' Mr Bolger told a conference of his conservative National party in Wellington.
