BFN [Text] Doha, 16 Jun (WAKH) -- Lakhdar Brahimi, envoy of the UN secretary general, left Doha for the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia at noon today following a brief visit of a few hours to Qatar. During his visit to Doha, Brahimi met with Foreign Minister Shaykh Hamad Bin-Jasim Bin-Jabr Al Thani and reviewed with him the Yemeni crisis and the results of his meetings in Sanaa.
