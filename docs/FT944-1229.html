941221 FT 21 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Learmonth slides Shares in Learmonth & Burchett Management Systems fell 12p to 85p as the USM-traded computer services group reported a deficit of Pounds 3.28m. The outcome for the six months to October 31 compared with a pre-tax profit of Pounds 201,000 and a loss of Pounds 746,000 at the year end. It was struck after an exceptional Pounds 2.4m relating mainly to restructuring. Turnover slipped from Pounds 13.1m to Pounds 11m, including Pounds 403,000 (Pounds 1.84m) from discontinued activities. Mr Rainer Burchett, chairman, said the results had been dominated by reorganisation, adding that the company was 'confident of returning to profit' in the second half, but any dividend 'now seems unlikely'. Losses per share came out at 13.7p (earnings of 1p).
