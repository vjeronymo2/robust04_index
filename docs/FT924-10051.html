921103 FT 03 NOV 92 / UK all-party treasury visit Bundesbank Members of the UK all-party treasury select committee visited the Bundesbank yesterday and came away favourably impressed, writes David Waller in Frankfurt. Speaking after a 90-minute question-and-answer session with Mr Helmut Schlesinger, the Bundesbank president, Mr John Watts, chairman of the committee, said he and his colleagues had a much clearer understanding of the Bundesbank's role in events which led to sterling's withdrawal from the ERM.
