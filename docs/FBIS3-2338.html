Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hefei, March 6 (XINHUA) -- Anhui Province in east China has strengthened cooperation with non-Communist Party members and non-party personages in social and economic development. A survey shows that 158 such people hold leading posts in local governments, and a large number of them are serving as heads of industrial enterprises, colleges and towns. Encouraged by the provincial government, they have helped the province to improve work in industry, agriculture, commerce, education, science and technology. They have also helped import more than 500 projects, involving over 600 million yuan. With their initiative, the province has set up 14 colleges and technical schools, a large number of training courses and rural industrial enterprises.
