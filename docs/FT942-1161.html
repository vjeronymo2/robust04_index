940625 FT 25 JUN 94 / UK Company News: Ewart seeks Pounds 4.85m for expansion Ewart, the Belfast-based property company, is seeking a net Pounds 4.85m through a placing and open offer of 9.42m shares at 55p. Four shareholders have undertaken to accept in respect of 34.9 per cent of the issue. The balance has been placed conditionally, subject to a 1-for-2 clawback offer to existing shareholders. The company, which is seeking a listing on the Irish Stock Exchange, is estimating pre-tax profits of not less than Pounds 1.1m (Pounds 156,000 loss) for the year to April 30, giving earnings per share of 5.06p (0.44p).
