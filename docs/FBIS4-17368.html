BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Nikolay Styazhkin] [Text] Pyatigorsk, Stavropol Territory, April 24 TASS -- "We insist that the president, the government and the Federal Assembly of Russia immediately take measures to adjust taxation policy, streamline managerial bureaucracy, resolutely curb crime and ensure strict compliance with laws", said a statement issued here by participants in a visiting session of the Union of Entrepreneurs and Lease-Holders of the Russian Federation. The members of the union drew attention to the urgent necessity to increase efforts to combat crime also because, in their view, a large proportion of the population come to view entrepereneurs -- the main taxpayers -- as a mafia-like corrupt set. "Democracy means a strong authority, not anarchy". The entrepreneurs want to see a realistic transition from intentions to carry on reforms to specific actions, the statement emphasised.
