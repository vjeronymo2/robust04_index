BFN [Excerpt] In today's open session of the Islamic Consultative Majles, the bill on reforming the conditions of choosing judges of the Ministry of Justice was approved. According to our correspondent, the bill provides for women who are eligible for appointment as judges of the Ministry of Justice and who have a judicial background to be employed as advisers at the administrative court of justice, the special civil courts, the public prosecutor's office, the investigations department, the office of legal studies, the legislative office of the Ministry of Justice [as heard], the office for the supervision of minors, the legal office, and other offices with judicial positions.
