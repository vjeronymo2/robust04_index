930717 FT 17 JUL 93 / NEC puts Pounds 20m into Livingston NEC, the Japanese electronics company, is investing a further Pounds 20m on expanding its semiconductor plant at Livingston, West Lothian. The investment is aimed at expanding production of 4MB semiconductors. By the time the project is complete next year the labour force will reach 940 compared with 875 at present. The project will bring NEC's investment at Livingston to Pounds 200m, making it one of the largest Japanese investments in Europe.
