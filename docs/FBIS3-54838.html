Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission decree No. 159 "On the Additional Nomination of Candidate deputies of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly Federation Council in Chelyabinsk No. 74 Two-Seat Electoral District"] [Text] At the end of the registration of candidate deputies of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly Federation Council in Chelyabinsk No. 74 two-seat electoral district for the elections to the Federation Council fewer than three candidates were registered. In connection with the need for the additional nomination of candidate deputies for this district, the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission on the basis of point 7 article 20 of the Statute on the Elections of Russian Federation Federal Assembly Federation Council deputies in 1993 decrees: 1. That the election of Federation Council deputies in Chelyabinsk No. 74 two-seat electoral district be scheduled for 26 March 1994 for the additional nomination of candidates and the implementation of subsequent electoral actions. 2. That the organization and holding of the elections be assigned to the Chelyabinsk No. 74 two-seat electoral district commission for the election of the Russian Federation Federal Assembly Federation Council deputies and to the relevant polling place electoral commissions as previously staffed, at the same polling places and on the basis of the same voters lists with the necessary amendments and additions which have been introduced to them. The announcement of the additional nomination of candidates and the date of the elections should be published in the press. [Signed] N.T. Ryabov, chairman of the Russian federation Central Electoral commission; V.A. Zagulyayev, secretary of the Russian Federation Central Electoral Commission [Dated] Moscow, 25 December 1993
