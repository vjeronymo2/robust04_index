Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] In Jaba Ioseliani's opinion, the Mkhedrioni [horsemen; pro-governmental militia] units must liquidate themselves. Jaba Ioseliani, the leader of the Mkhedrioni, the chairman of the interim committee for emergencies in Georgia, in a conversation with journalists, when asked as to which structures should the Mkhedrioni members be united, answered that it was a personal affair of each member of the organization, though most of them would, probably, enter the rescue corps.
