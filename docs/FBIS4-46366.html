BFN [Text] Egyptian security agencies are trying to gather information about meetings between a delegation of Egypt's al-Jihad Organization and Shaykh Subhi al-Tufayli, former secretary general of Lebanon's Hizballah. These agencies have obtained information that several Egyptian "Afghans" [Egyptians who fought alongside the Afghan Mujahidin against Soviets], who were expelled from Pakistan, have arrived in Lebanon and are now under Hizballah's protection.
