931229 FT 29 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Development Secs open offer take-up Valid applications for the 4-for-5 open offer by Development Securities to raise about Pounds 29.3m have been received in respect of 45.39m new shares, or 48.4 per cent of the issue. The remaining 48.3m shares (51.6 per cent) are to be taken up in accordance with the placing arrangements.
