BFN [Text] On Thursday, between the hours of 0500 and 1800 local time, the water supply of several municipalities in the capital will be affected by breakdowns at the water treatment plant. The Havana City Waterworks Directorate advised that the areas affected will be: Regla, with the exception of the El Modelo neighborhood; Alamar; Camilo Cienfuegos; the Panamerican villas in La Habana del Este; the rum and yeast factories; the El Lucero cream plant; the Rene Ramos technological center in Aroyo Naranjo; the Villa Maria neighborhood, Guanabacoa; the Vista Alegre neighborhood, Diez de Octubre; and the San Miguel del Padron area. The waterworks directorate encourages residents to store water until service is restored.
