BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Vadim Byrkin] [Text] Stepanakert May 25 TASS -- Azeri formations broke relative lull established in the zone of the Karabakh conflict during recent days, ITAR-TASS learned from the press service of the Karabakh defence army. On Tuesday night Azeri troops launched an attack on a strategically important height located in the Gadrutsko-Fizulin direction. The attack was rebuffed, entailing no positional changes. No casualties were reported among Karabakh defence forces. Over the past twenty four hours relative lull was reported in other areas of the front.
