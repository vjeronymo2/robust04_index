Language: Japanese Article Type:BFN [By Moriyasu Okazaki; from NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 21 Mar Morning Edition] [Text] Beijing, 20 Mar -- At a luncheon meeting with Prime Minister Hosokawa on 20 March, Chinese Vice Premier Zhu Rongji touched on Japan-China cooperation in the field of automobiles and urged Prime Minister Hosokawa to promote the Japanese auto industries' access to the Chinese auto parts market, including engines. At the same time, Vice Premier Zhu Rongji stressed that China will guarantee the quality of Chinese-made rice to be imported by Japan. With regard to the auto issue, Vice Premier Zhu said that since Japanese companies were reluctant to establish joint venture projects in the 1970's, China asked for the cooperation of such auto manufacturers as Germany's Volkswagen; that the Big Three of the United States have recently started producing auto parts in China while Korean companies have indicated their intention to produce auto parts; and that Japanese industries now can probably afford to cooperate in the area of auto parts production.
