930715 FT 15 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Telemetrix arm buys in east Germany Zetex, the wholly owned specialist semiconductor subsidiary of Telemetrix, has acquired certain plant and equipment located at Neuhaus, Thuringia in Germany, for the assembly of discrete surface mount semiconductors. A consideration of a nominal DM1 is payable to the Treuhandanstalt. Telemetrix said the open market value of the plant and equipment acquired was about Pounds 1m.
