Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] A former New South Wales Supreme Court judge, Gordon Samuels, has been appointed to carry out the inquiry into Australia's overseas intelligence service, ASIS [Australian Security Intelligence Service]. The inquiry was announced last week by the foreign affairs minister Senator Gareth Evans after allegations of illegal behavior by ASIS agents were made on television by two former members of the organization. Mr. Samuels has been asked to report back to the government by the end of this year. The inquiry will look into the control, accountability, and management of ASIS as well as its methods and the protection of its sources.
