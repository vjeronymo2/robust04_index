930621 FT 21 JUN 93 / New building for Lloyd's Register LLOYD'S REGISTER, the ship classification society, has applied for planning permission for a 35,000 sq m development in Liphook, Hampshire, which will be designed by the Richard Rogers Partnership. The new building will house 1,350 staff from Lloyd's existing offices in Fenchurch Street and Croydon. The company will retain its listed building at 71 Fenchurch Street to give it a presence in the City. The planning application is expected to be considered by the East Hampshire District Council's planning committee in mid July. If it is approved work will start in September 1994, with completion by the end of 1996.
