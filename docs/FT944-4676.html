941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Surrey back in black Surrey Group, the USM-traded licensed bookmaker, returned to the black at the interim stage with pre-tax profits of Pounds 45,000. The figure compares with a Pounds 57,000 loss last time and a Pounds 292,000 deficit at the year-end. Turnover for the half-year to September 30 was 13 per cent ahead at Pounds 23.6m (Pounds 20.9m). Earnings per share were 0.02p (0.04p losses). Mr John McNair, chairman, said the benefits from the Pounds 2.1m rights issue and placing in July would be felt in the second half.
