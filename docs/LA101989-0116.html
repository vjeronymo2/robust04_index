October 19, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Primo Nebiolo, who was ousted as president of the Italian track and field federation amid a business scandal, was reelected president of the Assn. of Summer Olympic Federations.

Several delegates said a key factor in Nebiolo's unopposed reelection was adoption of new rules that will prevent future presidents from serving more than one four-year term and provide for a membership change on the governing council every four years.
