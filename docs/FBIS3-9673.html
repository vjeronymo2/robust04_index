Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] The constituent assembly of Bosnia-Herzegovina, which is supposed to approve the constitution of the new state federation, will be held tomorrow morning. This was stated to HINA [Croatian news agency] by Ivan Bender, president of the Chamber of Deputies of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna. According to him, after the end of tomorrow's constituent assembly of Bosnia-Herzegovina, deputies of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna will leave Sarajevo in the afternoon. Otherwise, the work of the regular assembly of Bosnia-Herzegovina is continuing. The debate is about the draft constitution and the Washington agreement. The resumption of this session was planned for 1400 but up until this broadcast went on air, HINA's correspondent in Sarajevo had not obtained confirmation of this.
