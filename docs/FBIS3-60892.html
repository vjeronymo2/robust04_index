Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Diyarbakir, Dec 27 (AA) -- Security forces have killed 11 terrorists in the Semdinli township of southeastern province Hakkari. A statement by the Regional Governorate of Emergency said today that security forces carrying out operations in the south of the Semdinli township near to Iraqi border, captured 11 terrorists dead. Searches in the region revealed hideouts which contained 30 hand grenades, 12 mines, and foodstuffs. Three militants of a leftist organization were caught in Tunceli and one in Malazgirt. A terrorist surrendered to security forces in Silopi. Security forces caught 24 people who assisted and hid terrorists in the region. The court arrested them. Seventeen hideouts were found in Diyarbakir, Batman, Mardin, Tunceli, and Mus.
