BFN [Excerpts] Algiers, April 6 (AFP) -- A prominent Algerian businessman and opposition party leader, Mehdi Abbas Allalou, was badly injured in an attack in an Algiers suburb Wednesday, security officials said. Allalou, leader of the small Popular Association for Unity and Action (APUA) movement, was rushed to hospital after the attack in the southeastern El Harrach district. Officials gave no details of the circumstances. [background passage omitted] The businessman, known for his close ties to the Gulf countries and Saudi Arabia, took a stance in favour of Kuwait when it was invaded by Iraq in 1991.
