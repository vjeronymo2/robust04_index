DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION Office of the Secretary Fitness Determination of Capitol Air, Inc. AGENCY: Department of Transportation. ACTION: Notice of Commuter Air Carrier Fitness Determination_Order 94&hyph;9&hyph;48, Order to Show Cause.

SUMMARY: The Department of Transportation is proposing to find Capitol Air, Inc., fit, willing, and able to provide commuter air service under 49 U.S.C. 41738 ( see former section 419(e) of the Federal Aviation Act). RESPONSES: All interested persons wishing to respond to the Department of Transportation's tentative fitness determination should file their responses with the Air Carrier Fitness Division, X&hyph;56, Department of Transportation, 400 Seventh Street, S.W., Room 6401, Washington, D.C. 20590, and serve them on all persons listed in Attachment A to the order. Responses shall be filed no later than October 17, 1994. FOR FURTHER INFORMATION CONTACT: Carol Woods, Air Carrier Fitness Division (X&hyph;56, Room 6401), U.S. Department of Transportation, 400 Seventh Street, S.W., Washington, D.C. 20590, (202) 366&hyph;2340. Dated: September 29, 1994. Patrick V. Murphy, Acting Assistant Secretary for Aviation and International Affairs.
