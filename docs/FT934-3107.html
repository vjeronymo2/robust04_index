931210 FT 10 DEC 93 / David Mellor delivers petition to Downing Street against a hospital closure Former minister David Mellor (right) delivers a petition against a hospital closure to Downing Street yesterday. The boxes handed in by Mr Mellor and actor Dinsdale Landen were destined for the Department of Health, protesting against a plan by Mr Mellor's former cabinet colleague Mrs Virginia Bottomley, the health secretary, to close Queen Mary's Hospital, Roehampton, London. There has been a fierce local campaign to save the hospital - which has pioneered plastic surgery and the treatment of burns. Mr Mellor's two children were born at the hospital which is in his Putney constituency.
