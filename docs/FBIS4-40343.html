BFN [Text] Baku (June 15) TURAN -- Yesterday the Milli Mejlis ratified several Azerbaijan's international treaties. Among them are a Friendship and Cooperation Treaty with Turkey and France, an Azerbaijani-Turkish Protocol on Mutual Assistance and Cooperation, an Azerbaijani-Iranian Memorandum of Friendship, Cooperation and Principles of Bilateral Relations, the Law on Mutual Protection of Investments between Azerbaijan and Turkey.
