940212 FT 12 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Updown net asset value ahead 33% Updown Investment saw its net asset value per share advance 33 per cent - from 531.1p to 705.13p - over 1993. Attributable profits at this Cazenove-managed investment trust improved from Pounds 593,000 to Pounds 641,000, including a net contribution of Pounds 70,000 (Pounds 10,000) from the investment dealing subsidiary. Earnings, taking in the offshoot, were 16.03p (14.82p). The single distribution for the year goes up 0.5p to 13p. An incorrect version of this result appeared in some editions yesterday.
