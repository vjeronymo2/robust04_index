Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Flight Control Centre (Moscow Region), March 15 (TASS) -- The crew of the 15th main expedition continues its mission aboard the Mir space station. The cosmonauts refuelled on March 14 the joint power pack of the station with an oxidizer from tanks of the progress M-21 ferry. They will refuel it with the second fuel component on Tuesday. The programme for today also includes measurements of soft gamma radiation in near-earth space as well as technological experiments. Work will continue under the international "Rentgen" programme for astrophysical explorations. Using telescopes of the orbital obvservatory at the "Kvant" module, the crew plans to investigate roentgen sources in the centre of our galaxy. According to the results of medical checkups, Viktor Afanasyev, Yuriy Usachyov and Valeriy Polyakov are healthy and feel well.
