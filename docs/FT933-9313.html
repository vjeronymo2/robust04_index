930811 FT 11 AUG 93 / World Commodities Prices: Cotton and Jute COTTON Spot and shipment sales in Liverpool amounted to 84 tonnes for the week ended 6 August against 172 tonnes in the previous week. Subdued offtake did not bring many operations. Support was forthcoming in certain specialist styles, notably in the Mali range.
