BFN [Text] Cairo, 5 Jun (MENA) -- 'Abdallah al-Asnaj, who was recently appointed deputy prime minister and foreign minister in the Aden government, holds officials in Sanaa responsible for continuing the war and violating Security Council Resolution 934, which stipulates an immediate cease-fire in Yemen. In an exclusive statement to MENA, al-Asnaj called for isolating these officials regionally and internationally. He said: Giving them the feeling that there will be a decision to isolate them will make them accept the cease-fire unconditionally.
