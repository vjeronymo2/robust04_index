BFN [Text] Kiev, 3 May [no dateline as received] -- Ukrainian Foreign Minister Anatoliy Zlenko has received a French delegation in Kiev on an official visit that is headed by Alain Lamassoure, minister-delegate of European Affairs. The sides expressed the certainty that relations between the two states would reach a new level. An agreement on air transport was signed, as well as an agreement on the encouragement and mutual protection of investments. A news conference is planned by Lamassoure will concern, in particular, economic cooperation and stability in Europe, problems of power engineering, and safety of nuclear power stations.
