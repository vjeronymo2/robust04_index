930127 FT 27 JAN 93 / Observer: Terminal As the government, the Labour party and the BMA debate whether - or not - the National Health Service faces a cash crisis and terminal decline, the Institute of Health Service Management at least appears to have made up its mind. The cover article in the current issue of the institute's magazine, Health Services Management, is devoted to detailed discussion about whether Britain's health bosses are better off opting for redundancy or early retirement.
