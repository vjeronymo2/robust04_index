940203 FT 03 FEB 94 / Financial services pay reform urged The Consumers' Association today calls for the financial services sector to move away from payment through commissions to a fee-based system. In the new edition of Which? magazine, the organisation also says there is no consumer representation in the inquiry set up by the Securities and Investments Board, the City's chief watchdog, into the mis-selling of pension transfers, and urges the SIB to give consumers a voice.
