September 20, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

CHAPMAN WOMEN RALLY FOR A TIE IN SOCCER

Tiffini Drobnik scored with a minute left to give Chapman a 2-2 tie with Claremont in a nonconference women's soccer match Tuesday at Chapman.

Claremont had a 2-0 lead in the second half, but Chapman's Shelly Williams scored with 10 minutes remaining to cut the lead to 2-1.

Diane Noonan and Molly Gunn scored for Claremont.
