930407 FT 07 APR 93 / UK Company News: IBC continues its recovery with Pounds 0.8m International Business Communications (Holdings), the publishing, conference and training group, built on its return to the black in the first half to report profits before tax of Pounds 752,000 for the full 12 months to end-December. The turnround from losses of Pounds 4.5m for 1991 reflected the substantial reorganisation completed during the year. Remaining businesses improved their efficiency and profitability, according to Sir Roy Watts, chairman. Turnover totalled Pounds 55.4m (Pounds 50m), including Pounds 2.49m from discontinued operations. Earnings per share of 0.53p compared with losses of 2.9p. IBC repaid Pounds 1.4m of its senior debt during the year; some Pounds 6.5m of the zero coupon loan 1996 was converted into a loan repayable in 2030.
