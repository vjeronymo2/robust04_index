BFN [Text] The Islamic Republic of Iran and Belgium have reached an agreement for the repayment of Iran's debts. This agreement was signed by a delegation of the Central Bank of Iran and officials from the Belgian Government's insurance institute. It includes all Iranian importers' debts which expired by April 1994 as well as all credit documents for the current year. The initial grace period is 26 months for the agreement and the repayment time is four years. In addition to all insured dealings, it was agreed all of the noninsured credits will also be reimbursed in accordance with these arrangements by banks and retailers, as well as the Central Bank of Iran. The sum envisaged under this agreement is approximately $200 million.
