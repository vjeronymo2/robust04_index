920807 FT 07 AUG 92 / Two face oil blackmail charges A MAN and a woman appeared at Bow Street Magistrates Court, London, yesterday accused of blackmailing Conoco and Elf, the oil companies, for a total of Pounds 440,000. Mr Stephen Farley and Ms Emma Mills, both of Haslemere, Surrey, are charged with threatening to cause oil spillages from outlets and tankers in Europe. Mr Farley was remanded in custody until August 13. Ms Mills was bailed until September 3.
