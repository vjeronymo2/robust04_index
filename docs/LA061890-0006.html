June 18, 1990, Monday, Ventura County Edition

3 SOUGHT IN HOLDUP OF MARKET IN FILLMORE

Authorities were searching Sunday for three men who held up a Fillmore market and fled with an undetermined amount of cash.

Ventura County Sheriff's Lt. Wendy Kemp said two men entered Handy Market at 801 A St. about 7:20 p.m. As one of the men walked behind the counter, the other pulled a gun on the cashier. The man behind the counter grabbed money from the cash register, and the two fled in a car driven by a third man who had waited outside.

No one was injured.
