921031 FT 31 OCT 92 / Ecuador to end oil monopoly Ecuador is to end state-owned Petroecuador's monopoly in the country's oil industry, reports Raymond Colitt in Quito. Domestic petrol distribution and the operation of pipelines will be privatised. The Ministry of Mining will grant concessions to risk contracts in the areas of exploration, production and international marketing. The plans are part of an economic reform package initiated by the new government of Mr Sixto Duran Ballen.
