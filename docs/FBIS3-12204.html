Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The People's Assembly met today in a session chaired by Speaker 'Abd-al-Qadir Qaddurah. At the start of the session, Assembly members rose to observe one minute of silence in mourning for the martryrs who fell at the holy Ibrahimi mosque, victims of armed Israeli elements. Afterwards, Qaddurah and several members spoke. They denounced this ugly massacre and stressed that the entire world will realize Israel is a leader of terrorism that is protecting extremism, organizing all kinds of crimes, obstructing the peace process, and placing obstacles in order to foil the peace talks and turn talk about peace and stability in the region into illusions.
