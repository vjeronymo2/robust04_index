940212 FT 12 FEB 94 / College contracts dispute escalates The dispute between further education lecturers and their employers over new contracts escalated yesterday when lecturers at City of Bath College announced that they would strike for two days next week. The National Association of Teachers in Further and Higher Education has already announced a national one-day strike next month. Mr Tim Boswell, further education minister, said this week that he would hold back funding from colleges where lecturers did not sign the new, more flexible contracts.
