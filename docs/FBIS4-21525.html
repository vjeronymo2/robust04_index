BFN [Unattributed article: "Dutch and Ukrainians Reach Preliminary Agreement on the Environment"; from Brussels EUROPE ENVIRONMENT] [Text] The Netherlands and Ukraine signed a "memorandum of understanding" on the subject of environmental protection on February 19 in the East Netherlands town of Amersfoort. This preliminary agreement received the signatures of Dutch Environment Minister Hans Alders and his Ukrainian opposite number MrKostenko. The main points covered in the memorandum of understanding are climate protection, as well as water and nature safeguards. Cooperation in these fields is expected to take the form of scientific and technical information exchange, exchanges of experts and holding conferences.
