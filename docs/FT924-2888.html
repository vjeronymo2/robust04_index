921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Last main Harland Simon offshoot sold Cleveland Machine Controls, a private US company, has bought the European operations of Contraves, part of the collapsed Harland Simon group, for an undisclosed amount from Touche Ross, Harland's receiver. Contraves, which Harland bought last year from Oerlikon Buhrle of Switzerland, employs about 180 people in the UK, Switzerland, Germany and Italy. It is the last main subsidiary of Harland to be sold.
