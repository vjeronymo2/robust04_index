BFN [Text] A ceremony was held in Shanghai on 15 April at which a contract was signed establishing the (Xinminyu) Shanghai Electronic Components Company Limited, a joint Chinese-Russian company in the military equipment conversion sphere. This is the first Chinese-Russian Company of its kind in the (Jinxiao) zone of the Pudong free trade area of Shanghai. The new company will engage in the development of ultra-high frequency equipment -- lasers, ceramic elements, optical electronics, and optical devices.
