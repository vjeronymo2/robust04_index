930719 FT 19 JUL 93 / Egyptian general escapes ambush Moslem militant gunmen avenging 14 comrades hanged in the past five weeks on the orders of military courts ambushed an Egyptian army general's car in Cairo yesterday, Reuter reports from Cairo. Major-General Osman Shahin, commander of the central military region, escaped unhurt. Two attackers, a policeman and a passerby died in the ensuing shoot-out.
