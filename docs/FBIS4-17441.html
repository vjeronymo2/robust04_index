BFN [Text] According to the Defense Ministry of Azerbaijan, last night Armenian troops launched an offensive on the Terter and Geranboy directions in the north of Nagorno-Karabakh, but suffered heavy losses and failed to achieve any significant success. Meanwhile, completely opposite reports are coming from Armenian sources. According to a statement by the press service of the staff of the defense army of Nagorno-Karabakh, this morning Azerbaijani troops went on to the offensive in the Fizulinskiy [Fizuli] and Mardakertskiy [Mardakert] Rayons, but all their attacks were repelled and on several sections the Karabakh detachments managed to seize strategic heights. The Armenian side also reports large losses suffered by the enemy in today's fighting. These were INTERFAX and RIA reports.
