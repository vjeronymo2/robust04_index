BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Prosperity Party [PP] leader Necmettin Erbakan has said that the only winner at the local elections was his party. The people, he said, gave us the message to set up a just order as soon as possible. Addressing a mayors meeting organized by his party in Ankara, Erbakan stressed that the local elections closed one era and opened the era of a just order, of the national view, and of the PP. Turkey's showcase was handed over to us with the mayoral positions we won in the big cities, he said, adding: We will make Turkey a leading country. Erbakan charged that upset by the elections results, certain circles carried out various acts of provocation. He stressed that the acts against women carried out in certain cities were not the doing of the PP. He added that he sent a letter to the Interior Ministry explaining the situation and demanding the culprits be captured. [Begin Erbakan recording] We are coming to save Turkey. We are coming to bring happiness to 60 million people. Our path is one of tolerance, brotherhood, and love. We do not bring bans. We will not interfere with what people eat, drink, or wear. We are coming to bring happiness to 60 million people. Mayoral positions as well as government positions are places of service to the 60 million. We do not discriminate among parties or faiths. We consider every single member of our 60 million strong nation a brother. [end recording]
