930720 FT 20 JUL 93 / Management (The Growing Business): Backing the right characters - In a Nutshell Guinness Mahon Development Capital (GMDC) plans to raise a Pounds 25m fund to back companies with the rights to characters and brands such as Thomas the Tank Engine or Mickey Mouse. The Global Rights Development Fund will finance businesses which have or plan to acquire the publishing, licensing and merchandising rights to characters, brands and other types of intellectual property. It expects to invest sums of between Pounds 250,000 and Pounds 1m. London-based GMDC and the Bank of Yokohama, its ultimate parent company, will provide at least one third of the amount to be raised by the first closing date.
