May 19, 1989, Friday, Orange County Edition

FOUNTAIN VALLEY'S GRAHAM LOSES IN NCAA DOUBLES FINAL

Stanford's Debbie Graham, from Fountain Valley, and Sandra Birch were defeated by Mississippi State's Jackie Holden and Claire Pollard, 6-3, 7-6 (7-5), in the doubles final of the National Collegiate Athletic Assn. women's tennis championships at Gainesville, Fla., Thursday.

Birch won the individual championship by defeating Southern Methodist's Jennifer Santrock, 6-3, 6-3.

Graham and Birch helped top-ranked Stanford win its fourth consecutive NCAA team championship Sunday.
