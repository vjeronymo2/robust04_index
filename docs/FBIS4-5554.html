BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 11 (KCNA) -- "International Kim Il-song prize" was awarded to T.B. Mukherjee, president of the Asian Regional Institute of the Chuche Idea (ARICI) who is vice-director general of the International Institute of the Chuche Idea. An awarding ceremony took place in New Delhi on April 9. Congratulatory speeches were made at the ceremony by former Indian President Giani Zail Singh, the former general secretary of the Indian National Congress Party (I), Director General of the International Institute of the Chuche Idea Shuhachi Inoue, Secretary of the National Council of the Communist Party of India M. Farooqi and Secretary of the Central Committee of the Workers' Party of Korea Hwang Chang-yop who was leading a delegation of Korean social scientists. They said the awarding of the "International Kim Il-song Prize" to T.B. Mukherjee was a common honor and joy for all the members of the chuche idea study organizations and the progressive people who are following the chuche idea, the most excellent of the progressive ideas founded by mankind, and struggling for global independence. T.B. Mukherjee in his speech said he, as a soldier of the great leader His Excellency President Kim Il-song and the dear leader His Excellency Kim Chong-il, would resolutely fight to the end with firm confidence in the cause of socialism, the cause of independence, under whatever circumstances.
