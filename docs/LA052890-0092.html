May 28, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

GOING. . . Orange County people are travelers, much more so than their L.A. Basin neighbors. . . . A 1988 study showed they were 28% more likely to visit foreign parts and a whopping 68% more likely to travel the United States. . . . About 43% of county adults had traveled abroad between 1986 and 1988, contrasted with only about 34% for the greater L.A. area.

FOREIGN VACATIONS

43.4% of O.C. adults took foreign trips, including to Mexico, between 1986 and 1988. Of those:

73.8% took 1 or 2 trips

21.9% took 3 to 6 trips

4.3% took 7 or more trips

Source: Scarborough, 1988 Steve Emmons
