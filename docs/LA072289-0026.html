July 22, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

COLUMN WAS A WELCOME CHANGE IN EMPHASIS

Kudos to Jim Murray and Jim Abbott for making major league sports more than just a game, but a slice of life and reality.

Jim Murray's column on little Erin Bower's unfortunate accident proved that professional athletes of Jim Abbott's caliber are still around, on and off the field.
