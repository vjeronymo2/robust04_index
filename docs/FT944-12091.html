941103 FT 03 NOV 94 / Further 200 Tube jobs to be shed London Underground yesterday announced plans to shed a further 200 jobs prompting claims from the RMT transport union that its real target for redundancies was as high as 1,000. The company sent notices to several thousand staff in management, administrative and secretarial jobs as well as station supervisors and ticket inspectors offering a minimum redundancy payment of Pounds 10,000 compared with the previous Pounds 4,000.
