Language: Georgian Article Type:BFN [Text] Today, the Georgian Supreme Council confirmed Davit Iakobidze as finance minister. The issue of merging the Ministries of Finance and Economy was raised at the session. Should the Supreme Council confirm it, the finance minister will be in charge of the united ministry. As it is important and urgent to confirm the Cabinet of Ministers, the Supreme Council decided to convene on 30 March at 1200 [0800 GMT].
