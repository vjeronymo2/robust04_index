BFN [Text] Tokyo, April 28 KYODO -- New Posts and Telecommunications Minister Katsuyuki Hikasa is one of the main policy experts in Buddhist-backed Komeito [Clean Government Party], an influential party in the coalition government. Hikasa, 48, Komeito's Policy Board chairman, has never failed to peruse the bulk of budget documents every year ever since the party was in the opposition camp until last August. He played an active role in the ruling parties to draft 1994 budget bills and a tax reform plan. Hikasa replaced Takenori Kanzaki, his comrade in Komeito. Hikasa is said to be friendly and is well versed in ancient arts and ceramics. He has been elected a House of Representatives legislator four times from the No. 1 constituency in Okayama Prefecture.
