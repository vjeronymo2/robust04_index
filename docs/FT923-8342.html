920812 FT 12 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Higher interest bill trims Crossroads Oil Operating profits of the Crossroads Oil Group, a USM-traded oil and gas explorer, were static at Pounds 358,222 for the year ended March 31. Available profits fell 15 per cent to Pounds 314,732, mainly as a result of an Pounds 88,193 rise in interest. Oil and gas sales fell 18 per cent to Pounds 735,540.
