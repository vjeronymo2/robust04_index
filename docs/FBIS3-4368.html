Article Type:CSO [Text]The following list includes video reports on the People's Republic of China's second session of the Eighth National Peoples Congress [NPC] extracted from FBIS Video Program Summaries from 10 to 22 March, ordered chronologically. Each entry is prefaced with the FBIS TV Center order number (e.g., BJG 94-148) and the date of broadcast. Tapes may be ordered by calling 1-800-822-5115 for subscribers in Virginia and 1-800-542-8660 for those outside Virginia. Tapes are available for 30 days from 29 March and are for U.S. Government official use only. BJG 94-148 (10 Mar) -- CCTV provides live coverage of the opening of the Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress [NPC]. Jiang Zemin, Li Peng, Li Ruihuan, Zhu Rongji, Liu Huaqing, Hu Jintao, and Rong Yiren appear on the rostrum. Video shows other central leaders in attendance. Qiao Shi opens the NPC session and Premier Li Peng delivers the government work report. (98 minutes) BJG 94-155 (11 Mar) -- 1. Report on Chen Jinhua, minister in charge of the State Planning Commission, delivering a report on 1993-1994 economic and social development plans to the Second Session of the Eighth NPC. Central leaders attend. (20 min) 2. Report on Finance Minister Liu Zhongli delivering state budget report to the Second Session of the Eighth NPC. Central leaders attend. (15 min) BJG 94-156 (12 Mar) -- Report on a news conference given by the press center for the Second Sessions of the Eighth NPC and the Eighth CPPCC National Committee. Agriculture Minister Liu Jiang and Vice Premiers Zhang Yanxi and Wan Baorui answer Chinese and foreign reporters' questions. (15 min) BJG 94-157 (15 Mar) -- Report on the third plenary session of the Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress [NPC] at the Great Hall of the People on 15 March. Finance Minister Liu Zhongli and Xue Ju, chairman of the NPC Law Committee and others read from prepared texts. Central leaders attend. (7.5 min) BJG 94-164 (15 Mar) -- "Special News Feature on Second Session of Eighth National People's Congress [NPC]," featuring 15 March Beijing news conference in a large room at the International Hotel. Qu Geping and other NPC environmental protection committee officials sit at a raised table at the front of the room and answer questions from Chinese and foreign correspondents. (15 min) BJG 94-167 (16 Mar) -- Wang Zheren hosts news conference in Beijing on 16 March. Finance Minister Liu Zhongli and Vice Finance Minister Xiang Huaicheng answer reporters' questions on financial and taxation reforms. Jin Xin, deputy director of the State General Administration of Taxation, also answers questions. (13 min) BJG 94-180 (22 Mar) -- Recorded report on a reception for Chinese and foreign journalists, moderated by Zhou Jue, spokesman for the Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress. Li Peng, Zhu Rongji, Zou Jiahua, Qian Qichen, Li Lanqing, and others attend the reception. Zhou Jue introduces Li Peng, Zhu Rongji, Zou Jiahua, Qian Qichen, and Li Lanqing and then invites Li Peng to speak. After Li Peng concludes his remarks, he answers reporters' questions. Li Lanqing answers a reporter's question on China's reentry into GATT. (20 min)
