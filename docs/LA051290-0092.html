May 12, 1990, Saturday, Southland Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

NEPAL;

KING NAMES PANEL TO DRAFT CONSTITUTION

King Birendra, bowing to demands by Nepal's new reformist government, has appointed a panel to draft a new constitution within three months, Radio Nepal said. A palace communique said the seven-member commission will draft a constitution with recommendations "to strengthen constitutional monarchy and multi-party democracy." The panel was a major goal of Prime Minister Krishna Prasad Bhattarai, whose Nepali Congress Party had vowed to return to an opposition force if the king did not share his power.
