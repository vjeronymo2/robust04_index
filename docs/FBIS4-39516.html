BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Summary] St. Petersburg, 8 Jun--At a news conference following the conclusion of the session of the CIS Interparliamentary Assembly Council here, Council Chairman Vladimir Shumeyko said that it had drafted practical measures for implementing its resolution: "On The Role of the Interparliamentary Assembly of the CIS Member States and Its Tasks in Further Strengthening the CIS." A decision was also made to call on the CIS parliaments to speed up the ratification of multilateral agreements concluded within the CIS and to call on the Council of CIS heads of state to take coordinated action to harmonize legislatures and prepare model laws on such issues as refugees and Afghan war veterans. The session also heard a report by Medetkana Sherimkulov, head of the interparliamentary group on a peace settlement for Nagorno- Karabakh, on the results of the May 1994 Bishkek meeting on settling the conflict in Nagorno-Karabakh. A decision was also made to hold the fifth plenary session of the Interparliamentary Assembly on 28-29 October 1994 in St. Petersburg, as well as an international conference on perfecting criminal law on the territory of the CIS.
