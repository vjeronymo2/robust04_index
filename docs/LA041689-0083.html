April 16, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

RAP

Easy-E and N.W.A. are a disgrace to rap music, their community, and the people in their community. I can't understand why people enjoy listening to such degrading garbage.

Both Eazy-E and N.W.A. claim to rap about he realities of black people in the black community. I'm a black teen-ager who lives in a black community, and I can't relate to such nonsense.

NICOLE BUTLER

Los Angeles
