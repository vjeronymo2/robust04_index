September 24, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

CRITICS' CHOICE: BIG SUGAR: SEASONS IN THE CANEFIELDS OF FLORIDA BY ALEC WILKINSON (ALFRED A. KNOPF: $18.95; 256 PP.)

". . . Wilkinson has written a book that, while superficially modest, is both disturbing and profound. His is the America just out of sight from the highway, a place in which interest and indifference connive to permit the intolerable to persist." David Rieff
