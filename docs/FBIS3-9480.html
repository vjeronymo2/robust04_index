Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] [Announcer] The separation lines between the Croat Defense Council [HVO] and Muslim formations are quiet. Since this morning, however, Serb artillery has been attacking the Muslim-controlled part of Novi Travnik. Zdenko Vranjes reports in more detail. [Vranjes] This morning Serb artillery attacked the Muslim-controlled part of Novi Travnik. Fierce artillery clashes between the Serb and the Muslim forces are still raging. According to this town's people, the separation lines between the HVO and the Muslim army are absolutely quiet. Mostar is also quiet. Last night there were some isolated provocations from infantry weapons in the town center. In Uskoplje [Gornji Vakuf] Muslim and Croat representatives are today discussing the further exchange of POW's in keeping with the all-for-all principle. The exchange of POW's from central Bosnia remains to be carried out. Until the next elections the Croatian Democratic Union [Hrvatska Demokratska Zajednica] of Bosnia-Herzegovina is the only legal representative of Croat interests in Bosnia-Herzegovina. The Croat National Council from Sarajevo or any other minority group of Croat deputies has no right to act on behalf of all Croat deputies and the entire Croat people in Bosnia-Herzegovina, the Bosnia-Herzegovina HDZ [Croatian Democratic Community] said in its statement today.
