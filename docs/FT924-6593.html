921120 FT 20 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Thomas Locker sees modest growth Thomas Locker (Holdings) stayed in profit for the first half and expects the full year to show a modest improvement over the Pounds 306,000 pre-tax achieved in 1991-92. In the six months to September 30 this general engineering group made a profit of Pounds 188,000, compared with losses of Pounds 217,000, from turnover of Pounds 18.7m (Pounds 18.2m). Earnings per share were 0.4p (losses 0.61p) and the interim dividend is again 0.35p.
