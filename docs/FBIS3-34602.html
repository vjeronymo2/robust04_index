Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] The Lower House of Parliament has condemned the terrorist activities that Jordan is witnessing, and expressed their support to any measure the government takes to have a safe and secure Jordan. In a statement issued by the House, the legislators praised the security forces' role in their efforts in guaranteeing the safety and security of the citizens. The condemnation came after the House was briefed by the Minister of Interior Mr. Salamah Hammad on the security situation in the country. The House called on all citizens to stand alert and united against any troublemakers. [passage omitted]
