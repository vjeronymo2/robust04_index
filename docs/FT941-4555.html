940310 FT 10 MAR 94 / International Capital Markets: International equity offer from Sri Lanka John Keells Holdings, a diversified Sri Lankan group with interests in the hotel, property, food and packaging industries, has raised Dollars 33m from foreign investors in the first international equity offer from a Sri Lankan company. Lehman Brothers, which lead-managed the deal, said the company issued 2.25m global depositary receipts (GDRs), with each GDR representing two shares. The issue was priced at Dollars 14.75 per GDR on Monday night, and the receipts were quoted at Dollars 14.75 - Dollars 15.00 on the first day of trading. The company has been listed on the Luxembourg stock exchange.
