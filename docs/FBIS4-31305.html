BFN [Text] The Ministry for Economy has confirmed granting permission to build a storage depot for spent nuclear fuel at the Dukovany nuclear power station. According to the power station's press spokesman, Petr Spilka, today, no appeal against this decision is possible. Thus, following three years of administrative procedures, the Dukovany power station will be able to start building. Preparatory works on the construction will start as early as this week. The storage depot will house 60 containers containing 600 tonnes of spent nuclear fuel, which should be transferred to the projected central in-process store [centralni mezisklad] by the year 2005.
