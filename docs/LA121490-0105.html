December 14, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY NEWSWATCH

SIGN OF VICTORY: But if you do stay away, you might -- repeat, might -- miss a brand-new tradition at the stadium. . . . The Big A -- the original halo-topped scoreboard moved to the parking lot after stadium expansion -- finally is working again. Henceforth whenever the Rams win, a light will slowly crawl up one leg of the scoreboard and down the other in all-night celebration. It could happen Monday; the Rams beat the 49ers three weeks ago up north. . . . P.S.: If the Angels win, the halo will blink overnight. Dallas M. Jackson and Lily Eng
