April 17, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

WORLD IN BRIEF;

COLOMBIA;

STATEMENT INDICATES SPLIT IN DRUG CARTEL

Associates of drug kingpin Pablo Escobar issued a statement condemning their leader for a deadly bomb attack last week, indicating a split in Colombia's powerful Medellin drug cartel. The communique claimed that Escobar did not consult his drug-trafficking allies before the bombing Wednesday of a police convoy that killed 22 people and wounded 85.
