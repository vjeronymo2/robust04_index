920404 FT 04 APR 92 / Finance & The Family Fixed rate mortgage offer - At a glance Newcastle Building Society has launched a two year fixed mortgage at 10.45 per cent (12.1 per cent APR) with a maximum advance of 95 per cent on house purchase and 85 per cent on remortgages. There is an arrangement fee of Pounds 150 and Pounds 250 for remortgages. Customers must take out building and contents insurance and payment protection insurance from Newcastle.
