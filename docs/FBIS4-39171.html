BFN [Report by UKRINFORM for TASS] [Text] Kiev 3 June TASS -- A meeting of Deputy Ukrainian Parliament Speaker Oleg Demin with CSCE High Commissioner for Ethnic Minorities Max van der Stoel was held here today. The commissioner has arrived in Kiev on a working visit at the invitation of the Ukrainian Government. The deputy speaker informed the visitor of the current social, economic and political situation in Ukraine, the work of the new parliament and the situation in the Crimea, where a Ukrainian parliamentary delegation is searching for constitutional and law-governed ways to settle the problems. In his turn, Max van der Stoel said he values high the Ukrainian policy on ethnic relations, including the provision of ethnic minorities rights. In his opinion, this policy is a model one. He counts on the Ukrainian and Crimean governments to start negotiations and find a way out of the situation. In his opinion, there are plenty of ways and one should only wish to use them. Max van der Stoel called for larger and more efficient assistance to Ukraine in order to help it overcome the economic crisis. He stressed he will initiate this and other issues within the limits of his competence and abilities. The meeting was friendly and cordial, and proved high interest of the sides in development of comprehensive cooperation and settlement of all disputable issues in a legal way and through compromises which unite people and bring them peace and good.
