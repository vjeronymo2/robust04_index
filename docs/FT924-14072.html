921012 FT 12 OCT 92 / International Company News: Posco elects Mr Hwang Kyung-ro as chairman THE board of South Korean steelmaker Posco has elected vice-chairman Mr Hwang Kyung-ro as chairman, John Burton reports from Seoul. Mr Hwang has been with the company since it was set up in 1968. He replaces Mr Park Tae-joon, who resigned last week for political reasons.
