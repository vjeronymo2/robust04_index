Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Report by Russian Federation Defense Ministry Information and Press Directorate: "Not Removed from Duties"] [Text] The report carried by certain mass media that Lieutenant General Yuriy Konovalenko, commander of the Russian military unit temporarily stationed on the territory of the former Semipalatinsk test range, has been removed from his post does not accord with the facts. As a Russian citizen and serviceman of the Russian Armed Forces he cannot be removed from his post by decision of another country's leadership. Yu. Konovalenko is currently on leave.
