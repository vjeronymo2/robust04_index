BFN [From 20 Jun RAHVA HAAL, HOMMIKULEHT, p.1; "News Bulletin" No. 118/94] [Text] President Lennart Meri returned from his visits to Kazakhstan and China. Meri saw the visits as fully successful. The Foreign Minister Juri Luik who accompanied the president on his visits, told the journalists that if Estonia wants to be successful in politics, it has to keep close contact with China, who is a permanent member of the UN Security Council. Returning from China via Kazakhstan, Meri and Luik had a short meeting with Kazakh foreign minister in Almaty. The representatives of Estonian business spheres who were the members of Meri's delegation told that they had made useful contacts and see many new possibilities.
