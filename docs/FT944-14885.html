941020 FT 20 OCT 94 / International Company News: Arthur Andersen record Arthur Andersen Worldwide Organisation, the global professional services firm, yesterday announced a 12 per cent rise in revenues to a record Dollars 6.7bn, writes Jim Kelly. Mr Lawrence Weinbach, managing partner and chief executive, said growth had been very strong in the Asia and Pacific region, at 50 per cent, and in the Americas, at 14 per cent.
