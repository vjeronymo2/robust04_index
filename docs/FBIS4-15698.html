BFN [Report by BELINFORM correspondent Leoniid Tratsevskiy for TASS] [Text] Minsk, 8 Apr -- "The campaign to elect our first president, which is now under way, is being accompanied by a stepping up of political and media tension around the Army and its daily activities," according to a statement issued today by the Collegium of the Belarus Defense Ministry. The statement points out that "the Armed Forces of Belarus, which are intended for the armed defense of their country and the protection of its sovereignty and state integrity, must not be an arena for political confrontation by any political parties and sociopolitical movements whatsoever." The statement declares that all attempts to destabilize the political neutrality of the Army and its loyalty to the laws of Belarus will be decisively nipped in the bud. At the same time, the document states, "we will preserve restraint and good sense, we will not allow ourselves to be drawn into political battles and propaganda actions."
