BFN [Excerpt] The working visit of a Tajikistan Government delegation headed by Abdujalil Samadov, chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Republic, is proceeding in Moscow. Abdujalil Samadov held talks yesterday with Sergey Shoygu, chairman of the Committee for Emergencies of the Russian Federation. Questions of rendering assistance to Tajikistan were discussed. The sides agreed that a group of experts from Russia will arrive in Tajikistan in the near future to familiarize themselves with the situation and render assistance in resolving such problems as flooding and taking measures to prevent natural disasters.
