Language: German Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Viennese Cocaine Dealer Arrested At Motorway Service Station"] [Text] Two cocaine dealers were arrested at the Bad Fischau (Wiener Neustadt district) service station, located on the southbound motorway. Gerhard S. (32) and Oswald L. (32), both from Vienna, had offered 120 grams of the drug, worth 100,000 Austrian schillings, to an undercover investigator. Gerhard S. had bought 400 grams of cocaine in Rio de Janeiro for the equivalent of 60,000 schillings and smuggled the drugs to Austria. He sold some 70 grams in Vienna and consumed another portion of the cocaine himself. Another 148 grams were seized during a house search in Vienna's fifth district last year, as a result of which Gerhard S. spent three weeks in prison.
