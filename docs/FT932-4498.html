930609 FT 09 JUN 93 / Parliament and Politics: Crossrail bill ready to proceed THE CROSSRAIL bill clearing the way for a Pounds 2bn project to build two rail tunnels under central London was last night set to receive its long-awaited Commons second reading. Prime minister John Major last month bowed to mounting pressure to underline the government's commitment to the scheme, while emphasising it was determined to ensure it was carried out as cost effectively as possible.
