BFN [Statement by the Union of Georgia's Citizens Press Secretariat: "For Mutually Advantageous Cooperation"] [Text] On 15 June, a protocol was signed in the "Mtatsminda" business club between the International Fund for Reconstruction and Development and the Georgian Citizens' Fund. The main aim of the agreement is to promote wide-scale economic reforms in Georgia and lay the ground for the development of business contacts between entrepreneurs in conditions of mutually advantageous cooperation. Particular attention is devoted in the document to guaranteeing the protection of the rights of the private entrepreneur. The protocol was signed on behalf of the International Fund for Reconstruction and Development by its vice president Sergey Shurygin, and on behalf of the Georgian Citizens Fund by its president Gogi Kavtaradze.
