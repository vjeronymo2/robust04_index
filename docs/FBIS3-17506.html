Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] On Sunday morning, a number of villages in the northwest of Azerbaijan were shelled from neighboring Armenia. The Azerbaijani Defense Ministry told Interfax that the village of Mezem in the Kazakh region had become a target of an artillery and missile attack. Some of the civilians were wounded and several buildings were destroyed. The Azerbaijani Defense Ministry reports that Armenian groups are being concentrated in the Fizuli direction of the southern section of the front. The situation is tranquil in other sections of the front.
