BFN [Text] The IFP [Inkatha Freedom Party] has threatened to embark on mass action if the state of emergency in KwaZulu/Natal is not lifted. The IFP member of Parliament, Mr. Themba Khoza, has questioned the motive for the declaration of the emergency regulations in KwaZulu/Natal only and not in other provinces effected by the violence. He added that the IFP had no problem with the deployment of the army on the province.
