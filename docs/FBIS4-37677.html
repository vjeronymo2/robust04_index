BFN [Report by Sami Sarhan from al-Qalyubiyah] [Text] The security apparatus in al-Qalyubiyah has arrested the first secretary at the Russian Embassy in Cairo, the president of the Russian Language Institute, and an Egyptian woman and her son when they were caught photographing bridges on the Nile River in the city of Banha. Al-Qalyubiyah Security Chief Major General Muhammad 'Abd-al-Fattah 'Umar received a report from Hamdi Mahmud Hasanayn, chief of the security department on the Banha City Council, saying that he spotted the passengers of a car bearing diplomatic plates No. 12401 as they photographed bridges. Major Ahmad al-Dahabi and Captain 'Adil Lutfi went to the site of the incident, where they arrested Kuder Vicev Yuriy, first secretary at the Russian Embassy; Dr. Asingysev, director of the Russian Language Institute [names and titles as published]; Widad Ahmad Wahdan, the widow of a former counselor; and the latter's son Ayman, a student at al-Alsun Higher Institute for Tourism and Hotels. They were all referred to prosecutors for interrogation.
