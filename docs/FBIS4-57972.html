BFN [From the "TVN News" newscast] [Text] This afternoon, the Interoceanic Region Authority [ARI] Board of Directors issued a communique accepting the resignation of General Manager Juan Jose Blau. According to the communique, which consists of three paragraphs, the majority of the Board members decided to accept the resignation, which will become effective on 30 June, a date mentioned in the letter that Blau addressed to the organization. The directors' decision was reached after a meeting held on Wednesday, 25 May. The communique underlines the fact that General Manager Blau has participated in very important ARI activities. The document further states that Blau leaves a trail of honesty and devotion to duty, which merits recognition. The document is signed by ARI President Dr. Carlos A. Mendoza.
