940504 FT 04 MAY 94 / International Company News: North Broken Hill unit plans US buy North Broken Hill Peko's Warman International unit, which makes slurry pumps, is negotiating to buy the assets of Barrett, Haentjens, a US slurry pump company better known as Hazleton Pumps. Warman has annual sales of more than ADollars 200m, and said that the acquisition would bring in further turnover of around USDollars 26m.
