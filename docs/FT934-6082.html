931126 FT 26 NOV 93 / Letters to the Editor: Liquidity Sir, I was most interested to read that Armenia had joined the Hebrides in adopting the dram as its unit of currency ('Armenia introduces own currency', November 22). Does this move herald a pan-European alliance to rival the Ecu, or will it be backed by commodities to form the Whisky Standard? D K D MacKerrell, principal lecturer in accounting, University of Greenwich, Woolwich Campus, Riverside House, Beresford Street, Woolwich, London SE18 6BU
