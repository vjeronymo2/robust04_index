BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA April 15 -- A high-level military delegation led by Senior Lieutenant Gen. Dao Dinh Luyen, member of the party Central Committee, vice minister of defence and chief of the General Staff of the Vietnam People's Army, has been in China for an official friendship visit since April 9. The visit was made at the invitation of the chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army of China. The Vietnamese delegation held talks on April 10 with a Chinese military delegation led by Sr. Lt. Gen Chi Haotian, chief of the General Staff of the People's Liberation Army of China. The two sides informed each other of the economic construction and military activities in their countries and unanimously agreed to continue strengthening the time-honoured friendship between the peoples and armies of Vietnam and China. The delegation was received on the same day by Liu Huaqing, permanent member of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party of China Central Committee and deputy head of its Military Commission. It was also received by Qiao Shi, permanent member of the Communist Party of China's Political Bureau and chairman of the Standing Committee of the National People's Congress of China of the Communist Party of China Central Committee and deputy head of its Military Commission. [sentence as received] It called on the Chairman Mao Memorial Hall, laid a flower wreath to the Monument to the People's Heroes in the Tiananmen Square, and visited the College of National Defence. On April 12, the delegation left Beijing for a tour of various localities in China.
