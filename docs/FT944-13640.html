941027 FT 27 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Murray Split Murray Split Capital Trust reported a net asset value of 136.7p per capital share as at August 31, an advance of 22.7 per cent over the year. Total assets improved by 6.1 per cent to Pounds 25.6m, outperforming a 5.8 per cent gain by the FT-SE-A All-Share Index. The total return on net assets was 10.7 per cent, against 10.1 per cent for the All-Share. Earnings per income share emerged at 11.3p (10.29p). The proposed final dividend is maintained at 2.65p, holding the total at 10.6p.
