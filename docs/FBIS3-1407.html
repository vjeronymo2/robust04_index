Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The South African Defense Force [SADF] and the Ciskeian security forces have agreed at a meeting in Bisho to cooperate to stabilize the situation in the territory. It has also been agreed that the South African security forces will not replace those of Ciskei. The move follows earlier indications that certain officers in the Ciskei Defense Force were opposed to the deployment of the SADF and were in favor of the return of Brigadier Oupa Gqozo.
