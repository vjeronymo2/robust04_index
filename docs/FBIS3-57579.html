Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vilnius, Jan 26, BNS -- Lithuanian President Algirdas Brazauskas left for a one-day visit to the Netherlands Wednesday morning. The president is accompanied by Foreign Minister Povilas Gylys, Defense Minister Linas Linkevicius and other officials. The program of the visit includes meetings with Queen Beatrix, the Dutch premier and the leadership of the Dutch parliament. Minister Gylys intends to sign a Lithuanian-Dutch agreement on investment protection and promotion. On Thursday, the Lithuanian leader will be in Brussels, where he is scheduled to sign the Partnership for Peace framework document. Brazauskas also intends to meet with the leadership of NATO, Secretary General of the European Union Willem Van Eskelen, Chairman of the European Commission Jacques Delors and Belgian Prime Minister Jean-Luc Dehaene.
