September 23, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

NOT A CROSS WORD

Over the past five years, I've looked forward to the Puzzler.

My husband and I have fought over who gets to start first. We've fed our children late dinners; paid Pacific Bell a fortune in exchanging clues, and used our dictionaries, encyclopedias and atlases to the point of knowing now why we forked over all that dough. I won't go into all the other details of what we go through to complete your puzzles each week. We just wanted you to know how much enjoyment the Puzzler gives us.

DENNIS AND PAULA SIMILA

Arroyo Grande
