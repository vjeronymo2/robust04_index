940905 FT 05 SEP 94 / Emerging Markets: News round-up - Pakistan A Karachi Stock Exchange committee has rejected an application by Mineral Grinding Mills to make a 3-for-1 rights issue to pay for the conversion of its grinding mills to cement-making. The committee said the company had not planned the project properly. Another KSE committee last month blocked a 20-for-1 rights issue planned by Tri-Star Power.
