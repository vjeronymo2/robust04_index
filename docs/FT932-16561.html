930405 FT 05 APR 93 / Treuhand to sell off 2,000 enterprises this year The Treuhand agency, responsible for privatising east German industry, is to press ahead this year with selling a remaining 2,000 enterprises. But some of them may have to be shut down. Mrs Birgit Breuel, Treuhand president, said in a radio interview that the sale of 800 of these enterprises would soon be legally binding. She was 'confident' most of the remaining 1,200 could be sold.
