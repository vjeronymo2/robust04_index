BFN [Text] The Ministry of Trade recently issued a circular which announces that effective from 1 July, the procedure of issuing export permits for a single trip will be abolished except in the following cases: -- Goods that are banned from export; -- Goods exported according to special plans; -- Goods of specifically managed industries; -- Special cases: goods for exchanging, semi-export goods, goods intended for labor-intensive produce exhibitions, and goods produced by foreign enterprises in Vietnam. Before the date this circular becomes effective nationwide, the Ministry of Trade and the Customs General Department will select a number of localities and production establishments which will experiment with the new procedures immediately in June. The circular will become effective nationwide on 1 July. The cancellation of the procedure of issuing export permits for a single trip will benefit business establishments in their export activities.
