Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 27 Feb (MENA)--Cairo University students today continued their protests of the massacre in the Hebron Mosque, which left more than 60 Palestinians martyred and several others wounded at the hands of the terrorist Israeli groups. More than 1,000 students organized a peaceful demonstration in which they shouted slogans denouncing the sinful aggression against the unarmed citizens and raised anti-Israeli signs. Meanwhile, Cairo University has decided to cancel two Arabic musical concerts scheduled for today and to postpone until 3 March a folk dance show by Rida Group, in solidarity with the families of the Palestinian martyrs and as an expression of denunciation for this appalling crime. At Al-Azhar University students demonstrated today for half an hour while the opening session of an international competition on the Koran was held on campus. The students shouted slogans hostile to the Israeli practices and denounced the attack on worshipers at the Hebron Mosque early Friday. The students appealed to Arab and Islamic states to adopt a firm stance against the Israeli violations.
