BFN [Text] Warsaw, 11 Apr--President Lech Walesa received at the Belweder today Bulgarian Foreign Minister Stanislav Daskalov, who is paying an official two-day visit to Poland. "For many years, we were in the same political and economic orbit, and certain bonds and cooperation were established. Now, when we are reforming our countries, I think we should return to some issues," the president said. According to the president, Poland and Bulgaria should cooperate more actively in the creation of a vision of a new Europe. Daskalov said that Poland and Bulgaria have common goals: integration into the European Community. Daskalov repeated the invitation by President Zhelyu Zhelev for Lech Walesa to visit Bulgaria.
