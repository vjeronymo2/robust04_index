920327 FT 27 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Burford advances 30% to Pounds 3.45m Burford Holdings, the property and publishing group, lifted pre-tax profits by 30 per cent, from Pounds 2.65m to Pounds 3.45m, in 1991. Mr Nigel Wray, chairman, said the success of the property acquisition programme had resulted in a 5 per cent increase to 52.5p in net asset value per share. The property side contributed Pounds 3.18m (Pounds 2.33m) but publishing profits fell from Pounds 637,000 to Pounds 493,000. Holding company losses were reduced to Pounds 223,000 (Pounds 319,000). Turnover improved to Pounds 9.84m (Pounds 8.99m). Earnings per share rose from 1.5p to 1.8p and the dividend is lifted to 0.95p (0.85p) with a proposed final of 0.5p.
