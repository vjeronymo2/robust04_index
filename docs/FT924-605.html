921223 FT 23 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Batleys profits down by 26% Batleys, the private Huddersfield-based chain of cash and carry wholesalers, increased turnover 7.5 per cent but saw pre-tax profit fall 26 per cent in the half year ended October 31 1992. Turnover came to Pounds 239m (Pounds 222m) while the profit worked through at Pounds 3.1m (Pounds 4.18m). Interest charges were reduced from Pounds 183,000 to Pounds 79,000. Earnings per share were 13.71p (18.81p).
