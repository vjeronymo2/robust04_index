Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Efim Shvartsman] [Text] Kiev February 4 TASS -- Ukrainian President Leonid Kravchuk met on Friday the first popularly elected president of the Crimean semi-autonomous republic Yuriy Meshkov. Meshkov arrived in the Ukrainian capital after his inauguration ceremony in the Crimean administrative centre of Simferopol. Kravchuk congratulated Meshkov upon his inauguration and noted that the two have a lot of common problems to discuss. The Ukrainian president said they should meet more than once in the future, adding that he would like to get first-hand information about Crimea's problems from the president of the republic.
