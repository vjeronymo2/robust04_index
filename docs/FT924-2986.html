921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / World Trade News: Microsoft to open offices in E European countries Microsoft, the world's largest personal computer software supplier, is opening offices soon in Poland, Czechoslovakia, Hungary and Russia as it raises its profile in eastern Europe, writes Alan Cane. The company is committed to developing software distribution channels in eastern Europe similar to those in the west. Microsoft is the developer of MS/DOS, the most popular operating system for PCs. Its Windows programme, which makes PCs easier to operate, is proving successful in western markets.
