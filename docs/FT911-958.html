910509 FT 09 MAY 91 / UK Company News: Brompton falls and passes final pay-out Brompton Holdings, the USM-quoted and Aberdeen-based safety testing and inspection specialist, suffered a fall in pre-tax profits from Pounds 1.31m to Pounds 397,000 in the year to December 31. No final dividend is recommended. Operating profits were down less sharply at Pounds 1.78m (Pounds 2.34m), but interest payable rose to Pounds 967,000 (Pounds 883,000) and there were exceptional charges of Pounds 444,000 (Pounds 142,000) - in respect of tax of Pounds 169,000 relating to South African personnel in respect of earlier periods and a Pounds 275,000 provision against joint venture expenses. Turnover declined to Pounds 37,59m (Pounds 52.28m). Earnings per share tumbled by 7p to 1.2p. The total dividend for the year is halved at 1p (2p).
