DATES: Thursday, June 30, 1994. TIME: 1 p.m.&hyph;5 p.m. NUCLEAR REGULATORY COMMISSION Planning Meeting With NEI on PTS AGENCY: Nuclear Regulatory Commission. ACTION: Notice of meeting.

SUMMARY: The staff of Nuclear Regulatory Commission will meet with NEI (Nuclear Energy Institute) and other interested participants for a public meeting on Thursday, June 30, 1994, to discuss the need for or benefits of additional ``experts'' meetings on pressurized thermal shock (PTS), and a schedule for such meetings if they are warranted. Please note that this is not a technical meeting, and it is only for planning purposes. DATES: Thursday, June 30, 1994. TIME: 1 p.m.&hyph;5 p.m.
