BFN [Excerpt] Riyadh, 2 Apr -- Their excellencies the foreign ministers of the Gulf Cooperation Council [GCC] states arrived in Riyadh today to attend the 50th session of the GCC ministerial council, which will start at the Council's secretariat in Riyadh later today. Their Excellencies Sabah al-Ahmad al-Sabah, first deputy prime minister and minister of foreign affairs from Kuwait; Yusuf Bin- 'Alawi Bin-'Abdallah, minister of state for foreign affairs in Sultanate of Oman; Rashid Bin-'Abdallah al-Na'imi, minister of foreign affairs from the United Arab Emirates; Hamad Bin-Jasim Bin- Jabbur Al Thani, Qatari minister of foreign affairs, and Khalid Bin- 'Abdallah Al Khalifa, Bahraini minister of housing, have arrived.
