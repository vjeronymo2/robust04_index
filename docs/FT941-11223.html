940205 FT 05 FEB 94 / Maxwell: The Musical rehersals continue John Savident (second right) and other actors continued to rehearse Maxwell: The Musical yesterday in spite of Monday's planned court hearing at which Sir Nicholas Lyell QC, attorney-general, will try to have the show stopped. He fears the musical about the life of the tycoon Robert Maxwell will prejudice the criminal trial of those charged with fraud over the collapse of the Maxwell empire
