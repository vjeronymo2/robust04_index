April 30, 1990, Monday, P.M. Final

SHORT TAKES;

SAMMY DAVIS STILL RECUPERATING

Entertainer Sammy Davis Jr., in failing health in a battle with throat cancer, was resting at home seven weeks after his release from a hospital, his publicist said.

"There has been no change. He's resting," Davis spokeswoman Susan Reynolds said today when asked for an update on the song-and-dance man's condition.

Davis, 64, was released from Cedars-Sinai Medical Center on March 13 to recuperate at home from chemotherapy.
