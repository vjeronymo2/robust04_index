931203 FT 03 DEC 93 / UK Company News: UniChem in further expansion moves UniChem, the pharmaceutical distributor and retailer, has announced further expansion in the form of an acquisition and a distributorship. E Moss (Moss Chemists), the retail subsidiary, has acquired Gerald Hughes Chemist, based in Reading, Berkshire, for a maximum Pounds 660,000, to be satisfied by Pounds 380,000 in cash and the issue of 105,889 shares. In addition, UniChem has been appointed sole distributor in Scotland by Numark, the own brand of independent pharmacies.
