BFN [Excerpt] [passage omitted] During a meeting on Thursday evening between the foreign minister of Yemen and Mr. Velayati, our foreign minister, the two parties exchanged views on the historical ties between the two countries and regional and Islamic issues. They also expressed their interest in expanding and strengthening bilateral relations and stressed the need for the unity and solidarity of Islamic countries in order to neutralize the malicious plans of the enemies of Islam. At the meeting, Mr. Velayati expressed our country's support for Yemeni unity and said that a solution for Yemen's problems and the existence of a strong and united Yemen are to the benefit of peace and stability in the region. He added: Those who want to create discord in the united ranks of Muslims have no interest in a united Yemen. Our foreign minister, pointing out that alien powers cannot guarantee the region's security, stressed: The expansion of cooperation among Islamic countries is required for strengthening the world of Islam. The Islamic Republic of Iran believes that if such a cooperation had been prevalent in the Islamic world, the enemies of Islam would not have dared occupy Palestine, which is at the heart of the Islamic countries. Mr. Muhammad Salim Basindwah, the Yemeni foreign minister, described the Islamic Republic of Iran as an important regional power and called for the expansion of bilateral relations.
