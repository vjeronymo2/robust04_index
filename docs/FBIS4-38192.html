BFN [Text] Baghdad, June 29, INA -- Foreign Minister Muhammad Sa'id al-Sahhaf received on Tuesday [28 June] deputy chairman of the Turkish Social Democratic Popular Party and MP Muhammed Gulcegun [title as received]. During the meeting, Mr al-Sahhaf affirmed the significance of promoting bilateral relations to serve the mutual interests. The Turkish politician expressed his party's total support for Iraq with a view to lifting the embargo imposed on its people since August 1990, saying that his visit is also to get acquainted with the inhumane effects left by the embargo on the Iraqi people and to explain this image in the parliament and to the government. He confirmed sympathy of the Turkish people with Iraq who both enjoyed historical, religious and cultural relations and bonds.
