Language: Urdu Article Type:BFN [Text] A high-level meeting in Peshawar was informed today that poppy crops covering 2,090 kanals [approximately 289 acres] of land were destroyed in Bajaur Agency during the current season. Acting Governor Major General, retired, Khurshid Ali Khan, who chaired the meeting, directed that the law enforcement agencies make all-out efforts to eliminate poppy cultivation and heroin laboratories.
