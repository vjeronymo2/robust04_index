Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] During the past 10 months, the country's nonoil exports increased 58 percent by weight and 33 percent by value on the same period last year. According to figures published by the Customs Organization, 4.642 million tonnes of nonoil goods worth 319 billion rials were exported during the past 10 months. Hand-woven wool and silk carpets, pistachio nuts, copper, iron, steel, chemicals, copper and aluminum containers, hides, and dates were among the most important export items during this period.
