Language: Tajik Article Type:BFN [Text] Tohir Ahmadov and Sangmuhammad Zuhurov, leaders of the working group of the Republic of Tajikistan Supreme Soviet's commission on refugees, met with Major General Sufi Manan and Major General Nur Mohammad, representatives of Abdol Rashid Dostam, leader of the National and Islamic Movement of Afghanistan, in Sher Khan Bandar at the request of the Afghan side. During the meeting, the Afghan side informed the Tajik side that in accordance with an agreement signed between the official Kabul government and the Islamic National Movement of Afghanistan it has been decided that Sher Khan Bandar will be transferred to the representatives of Abdol Rashid Dostam. Questions concerning the repatriation of Tajik refugees were also discussed. The Afghan representatives said that in 1994 they will take all the necessary measures to speed up the repatriation of refugees and to guarantee their security. In addition, a transit camp will soon be opened near the Sher Khan Bandar.
