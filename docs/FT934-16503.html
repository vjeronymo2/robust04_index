931006 FT 06 OCT 93 / People: Bank of Scotland - Finance moves Gordon McQueen, general manager, international and treasury, at the BANK OF SCOTLAND, has been appointed general manager UK banking-England, based in London, on the retirement of Bob Wickham. Fraser Campbell, general manager Centrebank, succeeds McQueen and will be succeeded by George Mitchell, divisional general manager and chief executive of treasury services, on March 1, when he will become a member of the management board. Colin Matthew, divisional general manager, UK Banking England, succeeds Mitchell as from the same date when he too will join the management board.
