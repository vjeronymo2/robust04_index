June 29, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

CHILLING WORDS FROM FOLKS WITH COOL NAMES

"In the mornings, I get in my birthday suit and jump in my cold pool."

-- Chili Davis, major league baseball player, California Angels

"I beat the heat by submitting to it.

Heat stimulates creativity in me. It's the growing part of the summer cycle. In this kind of heat, ideas are popping like popcorn and you have to know when to take yourself out of the popper."

-- Mark Frost, co-creator and co-executive producer of "Twin Peaks"

"I take a shower."

-- Arthur (Klondike) Jones, crafts and services worker on "The Arsenio Hall Show" who is often enlisted to perform his unusual style of dance to "Love Train"

"Basically, what I do is kick back. I get a bag of ice. We make these frosty drinks with gin and Super Socko, turn on the a.c. and just chill."

-- Kid Frost, East L.A. rap artist also known as "the chosen one, the frozen one"

"I'm very close with my friends who have pools right now."

-- Carrie Snow, actress and comedian host of PBS' "Comedy Tonight"

"I put my wife, Darlene, in the refrigerator and she rolls all over me."

-- Ice-T, Los Angeles rap star
