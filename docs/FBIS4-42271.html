BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Excerpt] [passage omitted] Addressing a True Path Party meeting today, party leader and Prime Minister Tansu Ciller said on the Cyprus issue that she held a comprehensive exchange of views with President Rauf Denktas yesterday. She stressed that the document of ideas submitted to the two sides by the UN Security Council on 21 March deviated significantly from the confidence- building measures previously approved by the Security Council. Ciller pointed out that progress was made during the Vienna talks held on 11 and 12 May with a view to eliminating the deviations. She added: If this understanding can be translated into written form and confirmed by the United Nations, then future-oriented steps can be taken. Ciller stressed that Turkey will support the views to be posited by Denktas. [passage omitted]
