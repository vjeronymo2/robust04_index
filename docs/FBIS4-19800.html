BFN [Report by Patrick Jarreau on "SOFRES" poll conducted for LE MONDE and RTL] [Excerpts] One year after taking office, the Balladur government is still judged relatively favorably by the French: 32 percent give it a higher rating that its Socialist predecessor, which is favored by only 23 percent. However, one-third of those questioned (and almost one-half of them if the "no opinions" are added) cannot decide or preferred not to speak out in favor of either government.
