CSO Editor's Note: The FBIS Terrorism Desk welcomes suscribers' comments and suggestions at (703) 733-5512. Highlights and major incidents in this edition of the FBIS Terrorism Report include:

Algeria/Italy
Italian police raided what appears to be an Islamic Salvation Front (FIS) safehouse in Perugia, confiscating weapons-training videos and false passports.
Egypt/U.S.
Cairo newspaper AL-AHRAM AL-MASA'I says that Egypt will ask U.S. authorities through Interpol to deport Shaykh 'Umar 'Abd-al-Rahman after his 28 April sentencing in an Egyptian court.
Palestinians/Germany
DER SPIEGEL reports that PFLP-GC member Marwan Khreesat, arrested in Germany in the 1988 "Autumn Leaves" operation, was an "agent" for the German Federal Office of Criminal Investigation (BKA).
Palestinians/Lebanon
"Palestinian sources" told Cairo's MENA news agency that Fatah will withdraw its "military and semi-military presence" from Lebanon.
Turkey/Germany
A German TV program has run footage of explosives training conducted in a northern Iraq PKK camp. See "Selected Video Reports" below. A former PKK official who was planning to leave the party was shot in Germany.
