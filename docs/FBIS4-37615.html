Prime minister's bureau sources believe that a public Rabin-Husayn meeting is indeed to be expected in the coming months. Our political correspondent 'Imanu'el Rosen reports: [Begin Rosen recording] [passage omitted] Although Husayn's remarks should be viewed also in the context of his search for military and economic aid from the U.S., Israeli evaluating elements perceive numerous new positive points in his remarks at the Washington news conference.
