910426 FT 26 APR 91 / 'Green' committee A NEW 'green' committee to be chaired by Mr John Collins, chairman and chief executive of Shell UK, is to be appointed by the government to advise on the role of business and industry in improving the environment. The announcement coincided with a call yesterday by Mr John Banham, director-general of the Confederation of British Industry, for greater co-operation between the government and industry.
