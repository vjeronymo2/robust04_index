Language: Tagalog Article Type:BFN [Text] The Criminal Investigation Service [CIS] has confiscated 26 sets of microcomputers and almost 1,000 diskettes during separate raids in Metro Manila. The raid was conducted by men under CIS Superintendent Raul Medina. The CIS is now preparing to file charges of violating the copyright law against Hyper Corporation of Makati, First Micro Trading Unit of Greenhills, and Maxicorp of Pasig.
