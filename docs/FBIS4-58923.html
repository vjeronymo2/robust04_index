BFN [Text] A Palestinian police force awaiting Israeli permission to deploy in Jericho was delayed for a second day today, pending inspection of the weapons they plan to bring. PLO Ambassador to Jordan al-Tayyib 'Abd-al-Rahim said the police will enter tomorrow or the day after tomorrow. The 270 police who were to be the first contingent were joined early today by another 430 Palestinian soldiers. Meanwhile, a first contingent of the Palestinian police force camped on the Egyptian side of the border and was expected to enter Gaza later today.
