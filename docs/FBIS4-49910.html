BFN [Text] Beijing, May 10 (XINHUA) -- Chen Muhua, vice-chairwoman of the Chinese National People's Congress [NPC] Standing Committee, met here today with Georgeta Adam, visiting president of the Romanian Women's Association of Journalism, Art and Industry, and her party. The Romanian visitors arrived here on Sunday [8 May] at the invitation of the All-China Women's Federation. Chen Muhua is also the president of the host federation.
