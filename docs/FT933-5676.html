930902 FT 02 SEP 93 / Security guards see rise in pay AVERAGE HOURLY pay for security guards has risen by 12p, to Pounds 3.52 an hour, in the 12 months to April 1993, a survey by the British Security Industry Association has found. Average weekly pay has risen to Pounds 194.63, the highest since October 1991 when guards were working a near-60-hour week against just over 55 hours this year. The lowest-paid guards are in the north-east, where average pay for a 55-hour week is just over Pounds 150. Guards in central London earn the most.
