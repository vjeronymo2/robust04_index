January 3, 1989, Tuesday, Home Edition

CS LONG BEACH OPENS BIG WEST PLAY AGAINST NO. 12 NEVADA LAS VEGAS

The Cal State Long Beach men's basketball team will open Big West Conference play against 12th-ranked Nevada Las Vegas at 7:30 tonight in the university gym.

The 49ers (3-6) are led by guard Tyrone Mitchell, a transfer from Arizona State who is averaging 13 points and 5.5 assists in the 2 games he has played since becoming eligible.

Stacey Augmon, a member of the 1988 U.S. Olympic team, leads Las Vegas (7-2 overall, 2-0 in conference play). Augmon averages 7.5 rebounds a game. Guard Greg Anthony is the team's leading scorer with 14.5 points a game. DICK WAGNER
