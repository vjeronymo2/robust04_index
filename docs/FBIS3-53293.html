Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Within the framework of the joint coordination between Jordan and the PLO, the security, land, and borders committee, stemming from the higher joint Jordanian-Palestinian committee, began its work at the Foreign Ministry building today. The Jordanian side was headed by Dr. 'Abdallah Tuqan, and the Palestinian side was headed by Major General Fakhri Shaqqurah. The committee will resume its meetings tomorrow to discuss topics on its agenda.
