BFN [Text] Pyongyang, June 12 (KCNA) -- The council of the South Korean Subway Trade Union on Thursday called a press conference and urged the puppet authorities to lift the wage ceiling, according to a radio report. The trade union warned that it would launch into a general strike that would cause a total suspension of the operation of railways and subways throughout South Korea in case the present "regime" physically put down its demand as the past military regimes had done.
