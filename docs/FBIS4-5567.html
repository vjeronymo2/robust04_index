BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 9 (KCNA) -- A national photo exhibition opened at the People's Palace of Culture in Pyongyang Friday to celebrate the 15th of April. On display there are about 80 pictures showing the deep respect and reverence of the Korean people and the world's progressive people for President Kim Il-song. Among them are the pictures "The Great Leader Comrade Kim Il-song Acknowledging the Cheers of Participants in the Military Parade and One Million Strong Civilian Demonstration Celebrating the 40th Anniversary of the Victory in the Fatherland Liberation War" and "The Great Leader Comrade Kim Il-song on a Foreign Trip Receives a Bouquet Carrying the Best Wishes for His Longevity". Chang-Chol, vice premier of the Administration Council and minister of culture and art, addressed the opening ceremony.
