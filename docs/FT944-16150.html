941014 FT 14 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Servisair offer The Servisair intermediaries share offer has been subscribed 1.2 times, with applications for 7.19m ordinary shares. This represents an aggregate value of about Pounds 9.7m at the offer price of 135p. A total of 6.1m shares were available under the offer, of which 186,015 have been allocated to preferential applicants.
