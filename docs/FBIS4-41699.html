BFN [Text] The unfinished aircraft carrier Varyag, moored in the Nikolayev harbor in the Black Sea, will in all probability have to be scrapped. The scrapping will cost $174.6 million and take 4.5 years to complete. Even though some quarters have expressed interest in buying the vessel, any plans to sell it are hampered by the existing agreement between Russia and Ukraine, under which vessels of this type cannot be sold to a third country.
