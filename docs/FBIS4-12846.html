BFN [Text] Baghdad, 13 Apr (INA)--Oil Minister Dr. Safa' Hadi Jawad has said that Iraq is making preparations to sign new oil deals with several European firms next month. In a statement to the newspaper AL-JUMHURIYAH published here today, Jawad added that the cooperation talks between Iraq and European oil corporations have reached an advanced stage, noting that preparations are currently under way for signing several contracts with these firms after the recent conclusion of cooperation agreements with French oil firms. The minister addressed the issue of oil cooperation between Iraq and Turkey, saying that the Turkish side has demonstrated an urgent desire to reopen the Iraqi oil pipeline across Turkey, particularly since the latter is suffering from an unemployment that is having political and economic effects. The minister asserted that the oil pipeline that runs across Turkish territory is ready for operation once the Iraq sanctions are lifted.
