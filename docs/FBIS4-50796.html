BFN [Text] Beijing, May 23 (XINHUA) -- Chinese top judge Ren Jianxin met here this morning with Austrian General Procurator Dr. Otto F. Muller. Ren, president of the Chinese Supreme People's Court, said that Dr. Muller's current visit to China would help promote cooperation between procuratorates and judicial departments of China and Austria. Ren briefed his guest on China's legal system and the work of courts and procuratorates. Muller said that exchanges of experience and development of friendly cooperation between procuratorates and courts would push forward the cooperation in other fields between the two countries. Muller came to China to give lectures at the invitation of the Chinese Supreme People's Procuratorate.
