May 14, 1989, Sunday, Orange County Edition

JUDGE'S DECISION ON REMOVING FOXES

Judge Robert Kelleher's decision to sanction the "murder" of the foxes at the Seal Beach Naval Weapons Station (May 2) is a small example of the type of narrow-minded idiotic decision made by a man who does not have any understanding of ecology.

Even after animal organizations have offered to trap the foxes, he renders such an unwarranted decision. It's frightening to have such men as Judge Kelleher in positions of power!

JOAN RICHARDSON

San Clemente
