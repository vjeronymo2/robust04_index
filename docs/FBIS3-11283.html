Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] PRENSA LATINA reports that Cuban diplomat Manuel Aguilera de la Paz today presented his credentials as Cuban ambassador to Uruguay to President Luis Alberto Lacalle. Aguilera replaced Abelardo Curbelo, who became ambassador to Uruguay in February 1989 and now represents our country in Mexico. Cuba and Uruguay reestablished diplomatic relations in October of 1985, after 21 years of official isolation decreed by the Uruguayan legislature.
