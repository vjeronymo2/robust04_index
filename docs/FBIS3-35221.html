Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Report by Kassab Samawi] [Text] Before the signing of the document of pledge and accord by the Yemeni political dialogue parties, the Royal Hashemite Court witnessed a series of contacts between His Majesty King Husayn, the Yemeni leaders, and Jordan's guests who were invited to bless this agreement. His Majesty King Husayn received 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Yemeni Presidential Council. The meeting was attended by His Highness Crown Prince Hasan. The meeting, which lasted for some time, was also attended by Yasin Nu'man, head of the secretariat of the Yemeni Socialist Party's Central Committee. His Majesty King Husayn also conferred at Raghadan Palace this afternoon with His Excellency 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Yemeni president; Yasir 'Arafat, president of the state of Palestine; 'Ali Salim al-Bid, vice chairman of the Yemeni Presidential Council; Shaykh 'Abdallah al-Ahmar, head of the Yemeni Reform Grouping; Dr. 'Ismat 'Abd-al-Majid, Arab League's secretary General; and Yusuf Bin-'Alawi, the Sultanate of Oman's minister of state for foreign affairs, before they all headed for the throne hall at Raghadan Palace. During that meeting, maintaining Yemeni national unity and the ability of the Arab nation to overcome its difficulties and resolve its problems within the Arab framework were emphasized. The guests highly appreciated Jordan's role in closing Arab ranks and building solidarity on the basis of confidence, cooperation, and brotherhood. Following the signing of the historic document, His Majesty King Husayn held a fast-breaking banquet at Basman Palace in honor of the Yemeni leaders and the guests. The banquet was attended by His Highness Crown Prince Hasan and top government officials, as well as civil and military officials.
