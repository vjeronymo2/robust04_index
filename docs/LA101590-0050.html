October 15, 1990, Monday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY FOCUS: SANTA ANA;

PEDESTRIAN HIT BY BUS DIES IN HOSPITAL

A 48-year-old pedestrian died Sunday from injuries sustained when he was hit by a bus, police said.

Jorge Alberto Tobar Santano, of Santa Ana stepped off the north curb at 1600 W. 1st St. about 5:30 p.m. Saturday, police said.

Witnesses told police he had stepped off the curb backward, apparently talking to someone and facing away from traffic when he was struck by a westbound Orange County Transit District bus. He died Sunday morning at Western Medical Center.
