930810 FT 10 AUG 93 / Dutch bargemen back at work Striking Dutch bargemen returned to work yesterday after a six-week stoppage, but said they may resume their actions in three months if a dispute over cargo allocation is not settled, writes Ronald van de Krol in Amsterdam. The independent barge owner-operators have been blocking some river traffic and refusing to take cargoes since June to press their demands that 'tourderole', a traditional cargo allocation system for shipments from the Netherlands to Belgium, be written into Dutch law.
