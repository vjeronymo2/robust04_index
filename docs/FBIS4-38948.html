BFN [By UKRINFORM correspondent Mikhail Stetsyuk for TASS] [Text] Sevastopol, 2 Jun (ITAR-TASS) -- Leaders of the Sevastopol state administration and Simon Hemans, the British ambassador in Ukraine, discussed the holding of joint events dedicated to the 140th anniversary of the Crimean war and the organizing of permanent cooperation in tourism. The ambassador visited Sevastopol 1-2 June. The guest was received by Ukrainian Navy Commander Vice Admiral Vladimir Bezkorovaynyy. The press office of the Ukrainian Navy reports that the conversation took place in a warm and friendly atmosphere. The commander briefed the ambassador on the negotiations regarding the forthcoming division of the Black Sea Fleet and the way the building of the Ukrainian Navy is progressing. Simon Hemans confirmed the position of the British side that the Crimean issue is Ukraine's internal affair and that Britain supports Ukraine's policy on the Crimea.
