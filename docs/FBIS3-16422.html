Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "From Position of Mature Partnership"] [Excerpt] Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev stated his intention of conducting his present talks in Vladivostok with U.S. Secretary of State Warren Christopher "from the position of a mature strategic partnership." During the meeting that started in the Vladivostok Airport building straight after the official welcome ceremony for Warren Christopher, the heads of the Russian and U.S. foreign policy departments plan to discuss a brpad range of political, economic, and other issues. In turn U.S Secretary of State Warren Christopher said at a press conference in Beijing before leaving for Vladivostok that consultations and coordination between Russia and the United States are extremely desirable. With regard to the statements that "Russia is unhappy about being regarded as a junior partner," Christopher stressed that "Kozyrev has never said that to me. We are partners in our desire for reforms and support for reformers in Russia. And we will discuss these issues." [passage omitted]
