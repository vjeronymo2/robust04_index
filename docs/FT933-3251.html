930915 FT 15 SEP 93 / Bourassa to stand down Quebec Premier Robert Bourassa yesterday said he would not lead the Quebec Liberals in the 1994 provincial election, writes Robert Gibbens from Montreal. He will continue as premier until the party chooses his successor at a convention in January. Mr Bourassa, 60, was premier from 1970 to 1976, returned to power in 1985, and was re-elected in 1989.
