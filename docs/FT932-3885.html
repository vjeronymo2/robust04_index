930611 FT 11 JUN 93 / International Company News: Executive Life battle goes on THE long-running tussle over the rehabilitation plans for Executive Life, the Californian insurance group, took a further twist yesterday when SunAmerica, a Los Angeles-based financial services company, announced that it was joining the investor group which is seeking to take over the rump of the insurer's business. The investor group is led by France's Mutuelle Assistance Artisanale de France, and its rehabilitation plan for Executive Life won the backing of the Californian insurance authorities in late-1991. However, the proposed reorganisation has since been bogged down by legal actions.
