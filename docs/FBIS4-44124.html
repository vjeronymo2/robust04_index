BFN [Text] President Glavkos Kliridhis described as a big success the decision by the European Union [EU] summit in Corfu to include Cyprus in the Union's next expansion. President Kliridhis expressed Cyprus' thanks for the Greek presidency's action, which resulted in this success, and referred specifically to Prime Minister Andreas Papandreou, Foreign Minister Karolos Papoulias, and Alternate Foreign Minister Theodhoros Pangalos. Referring to a statement by Rauf Denktas on blocking Cyprus' course toward the EU, President Kliridhis said the Turkish Cypriot leader speaks irrationally when he talks about blocking the course and about a struggle in the spirit of the terrorist organization TMT [Turkish Resistance Organization].
