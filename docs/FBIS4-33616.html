BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on the activities of members of the Politburo of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of Cuba monitored from Havana radio and television sources in Spanish between 30 May and 5 June. Division General Cintra Frias has visited the Western Army and pointed out the intense efforts that it is making to become the best command within the Revolutionary Armed Forces of Cuba. (Havana Radio Reloj Network in Spanish 1409 GMT 31 May 94) Maria De Los Angeles Garcia inaugurated the first Molecular Immunology Research Center in Camaguey Province on 31 May. (Havana Tele Rebelde and Cuba Vision Networks in Spanish 0000 GMT 1 Jun 94) Alfredo Jordan has participated in the plenum of the provincial committee in Matanzas, where he stressed the importance of increasing efficiency and improving working conditions for workers. The plenum also analyzed the delays in planting vegetables and tubers caused by the lack of resources and manpower. (Havana Radio Rebelde Network in Spanish 1700 GMT 30 May 94) Jordan has visited Matanzas, where he praised the Basic Cooperative Production Units and stressed that they were called to be the response to Cuba's food production needs. (Havana Radio Rebelde Network in Spanish 0900 GMT 2 Jun 94) Jordan is chairing an agricultural review at a pharmaceutical laboratory in Havana. At the meeting, Jordan pointed out the need to establish a program to supply Havana residents with leafy vegetables and stressed the importance of economic efficiency and cost reductions. (Havana Radio Rebelde Network in Spanish 2300 GMT 3 Jun 94) Yadira Garcia has chaired the provincial plenum of the Communist Party of Cuba in Matanzas. Garcia stressed the need for increased efforts in the harvest production. Alfredo Jordan also attended the plenum. (Havana Cuba Vision Network in Spanish 0000 GMT 31 May 94) Julian Rizo Alvarez stressed the need for the people to participate in the reforestation program during a meeting on the reforestation program's progress in Camaguey Province. (Havana Radio Reloj in Spanish 1403 GMT 30 May 94) Rizo Alvarez met cattle breeders in Camaguey Province and stressed the importance of renewed milk production efforts, as well as the care of resources and animals. Rizo praised the efforts of the Basic Cooperative Production Units in the province. (Havana Radio Reloj in Spanish 1404 GMT 31 May 94) Julian Rizo Alvarez and Maria De Los Angeles Garcia have inaugurated a Molecular Immunology Center at the Carlos J. Finley Medical Institute, in Camaguey. (Havana Radio Reloj Network in Spanish 1932 GMT 30 May 94) Rizo Alvarez and De Los Angeles Garcia have chaired the third provincial plenum of the Communist Party of Cuba in Camaguey Province. The main issue was the science and technology program, which prompted comments by numerous invited researchers. (Havana Tele Rebelde and Cuba Vision Networks in Spanish 0000 GMT 1 Jun 94) Pedro Ross Leal has chaired the economic efficiency assembly of the workers of the Electronic Equipment Production Complex in Havana. Ross stressed that the necessary measures will require in-depth analysis and awareness. (Havana Radio Rebelde Network in Spanish 1700 GMT 3 Jun 94)
