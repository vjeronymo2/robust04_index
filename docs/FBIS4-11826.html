BFN [Text] Cairo, 31 Mar (MENA)--A locally made explosive device planted by an unidentified man under a car parked outside the International Trade Bank near the al-Zamalik Club fence on Jami'at al-Dual al-'Arabiyah Street in the al-Muhandisin neighborhood of al-Jizah went off this morning. The explosion caused no casualties but damaged four cars. The bank's windows were also shattered. Al-Jizah security officials, explosives and other experts, and criminal laboratory experts went to investigate the incident.
