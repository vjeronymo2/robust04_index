Language: Vietnamese Article Type:BFN [Text] Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina Gonzales arrived in Hanoi today for an official friendship visit to Vietnam at the invitation of Vietnamese Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam. On hand to welcome Robaina and his entourage at the Government Guest House were Nguyen Manh Cam; Tran Thi Thanh Thanh, minister in charge of child protection and care and chairman of the Vietnam-Cuba Friendship Association; Le Mai, deputy foreign minister; Do Van Tai, deputy head of the Central Committee Foreign Relations Department; and representatives of various agencies. Cuban Ambassador to Vietnam Tania Delgado Maceira and many other Cuban Embassy officials were also present.
