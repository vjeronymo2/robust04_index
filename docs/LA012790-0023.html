January 27, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

ALBERT'S PERFORMANCE NOT WHAT NHL NEEDED

It was nice to see network TV finally recognize the NHL by televising the All-Star game. However, if the purpose was to help expand awareness of this sport to the American public, NBC made one drastic mistake. By having Marv Albert do the play-by-play, more potential fans were probably scared away due to his ineptness.

MICHAEL I. CORNFIELD, Brea
