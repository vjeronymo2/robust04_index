BFN [Report by BELINFORM correspondent Svetlana Kalinina-TASS] [Text] Minsk, 17 June -- On 16 June the car in which Aleksandr Lukashenko, presidential candidate and well-known campaigner for anticorruption, was shot at after leaving a meeting with the electorate. As a BELINFORM correspondent was told by Likashenko's spokesman, this happened at midnight near the regional center of Liozno in Vitebsk Oblast. Fortunately, the bullet from a passing car of foreign make did not hit Lukashenko. It just went through the car seat. The shooting was confirmed by the Liozno internal affairs directorate. However, no details of the incident have been given. Boris Aldanov, Belarusian deputy minister of internal affairs, reported that an investigation has begun and said he was puzzled why the indicent was reported to the region's militia department eight hours after it happened.
