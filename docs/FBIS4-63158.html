BFN [Text] According to the press center of the Abkhazian Council of Ministers an extended session of the Council of Ministers has been held. An in-depth report on the Moscow agreement of 14 May 1994, on a ceasefire and the separation of forces was made by Zurab Erkvania, a member of the Georgian delegation at the talks and chairman of the Council of Ministers of the Autonomous Republic of Abkhazia. Proceeding from the current realistic situation, the session supported the agreement and drew up the relevant measures. The session discussed and adopted a resolution on the work of the Abkhaz Council of Ministers and immediate measures for stepping up its activity. The leadership of the Council of Ministers of the Autonomous Republic of Abkhazia met with a large group of refugees from Gali rayon. In a sincere and principled conversation, the two sides discussed the Moscow agreement on the settlement of the Georgian-Abkhaz conflict. Suggestions on creating mechanisms for carrying out safely and economically the first stage of the return of refugees were worked out.
