Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Belgrade, 28 Feb (TANJUG) -- Republic of Serbia President Slobodan Milosevic this evening received Gennadiy Shikin, Russian Federation ambassador in Belgrade, who handed him a letter from Russian President Boris Yeltsin. The letter deals with Russia's involvement, initiatives and role, as well as its efforts in solving the crisis in Bosnia-Herzegovina. Russia's involvement and its efforts to solve the crisis in the framework of the peace process is, President Milosevic believes, of great importance and can make a decisive contribution to reaching a lasting and just peace in former Bosnia-Herzegovina, a statement from the office of the president of the Republic of Serbia says.
