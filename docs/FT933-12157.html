930726 FT 26 JUL 93 / Construction Contracts: London offices Four construction management projects worth Pounds 30m have been secured by BUCKNALL AUSTIN. The projects include the fitting out of Coca Cola's new headquarters at Hammersmith (Pounds 8m), project management for fitting out work for the Prudential Corporation in central London (Pounds 17m), the reconstruction of Alderman's House following the IRA bomb in the City (Pounds 2.4m) and fitting out works for a maintenance centre for British Airways in Wales (Pounds 3m).
