October 27, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

SEASIDE RALLIES FOR WIN IN SANTA ANITA FEATURE

Seaside rallied to beat favored Majolique by a head in the $37,000 allowance feature for fillies and mares Thursday at Santa Anita.

Seaside, ridden by Gary Stevens, unleashed a powerful four-wide move around the last turn and held on through the stretch.

The 3-year-old filly covered 1 1/16 miles in 1:43 and paid $20.20, $5.80 and $4.80. Majolique paid $3.60 and $2.60. Black Stockings paid $2.80.
