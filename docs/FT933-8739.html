930813 FT 13 AUG 93 / International Company News: Nabisco issue priced A Dollars 1BN, two-part issue by RJR Nabisco Holdings, the tobacco and food group, was priced yesterday, agencies report. The first tranche, Dollars 500m of notes, due August 15 2005 and with a coupon of 8 3/4 , was priced at 99.705 to yield 8.79 per cent. The non-callable notes were priced at a spread of 305 basis points above Treasuries. The second tranche, Dollars 500m of debentures, due August 15 2013, carried a coupon of 9 1/4 , and were priced at 99.907 to yield 9.26 per cent. Both parts were rated Baa3 by Moody's, and triple-B-minus by Standard & Poor's.
