March 4, 1990, Sunday, Home Edition

COLLEGE NEWS: COUNSELOR AT MT. SAC RECEIVES RECOGNITION

Ed Nettell, a counselor at Mt. San Antonio College in Walnut, is one of five California community college employees recognized by the California Articulation Number System for his efforts in articulation.

Articulation involves matching community college classes to their equivalents in university systems and seeing that student credits at two-year colleges transfer to four-year colleges and universities.
