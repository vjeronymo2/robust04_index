BFN [Text] Zimbabwean President Robert Mugabe, worried that continued world attention on South Africa may marginalize neighboring Zimbabwe, will try to persuade British investors in London on Thursday to bolster his country's economy. Zimbabwean leaders have been touring the world in the past few months on an investment campaign aimed at boosting an economy burdened by rising unemployment. The Zimbabwean president will concentrate in London on his country's track record of peace in contrast to continued violence in South Africa. Announcements on tax relief and amendments to the controversial program are also expected.
