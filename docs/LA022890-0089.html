February 28, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

CS LONG BEACH WOMEN LOSE

After leading by six at halftime, the No. 10-ranked Cal State Long Beach women fell to No. 14 Hawaii, 86-84, Tuesday night in a Big West Conference basketball game at Honolulu.

Judy Moseley had 30 points and Ayesha Brooks added 24 points for Hawaii (24-2, 15-2 in the conference). Long Beach (22-6, 14-3) had a 44-38 advantage at halftime.
