940613 FT 13 JUN 94 / Arts: Schubertiade - Openings The cream of the world's Schubert interpreters gather at Feldkirch in eastern Austria over the next fortnight for a rarified festival of recitals and chamber music evenings. The opening concert tonight is given by the Alban Berg Quartet. Other visitors include Alfred Brendel, Andras Schiff, Peter Schreier and Anne Sofie von Otter.
