January 27, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

The San Francisco Giants signed veteran free-agent relief pitcher Dan Quisenberry to a two-year contract.

Under terms of the contract, Quisenberry, 36, will be paid the major league minimum by the Giants this season, between $80,000 and $90,000. He already is guaranteed $1.1 million in 1990 by the Kansas City Royals.
