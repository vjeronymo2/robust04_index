Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Israeli forces have continued bombardment of the Iqlim al-Tuffah heights and hills since this morning at a rate of one round every 10 minutes. Shells are landing in Jabal al-Rafi', al-Wadi al-Akhdar, the outskirts of 'Arab Salim, Mlita, al-Luwayzah, Yatar, and Jibal al-Butm. Israeli helicopters dropped flares over these areas early this morning. At 1015 today, two shells fell in the area east of Kafr Rumman, accompanied by bursts of machine-gun fire.
