Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By BELINFORM correspondent for TASS] [Text] Grodno, 13 Jan--An official meeting between Belarusian Defense Minister Pavel Kozlovskiy and Polish Defense Minister Reserve Vice Admiral Piotr Kolodziejczyk took place here today. This is the first meeting attended by Kolodziejczyk, who was appointed recently to the post of head of Poland's military department. On the agenda were consultations on matters of military organizational development and cooperation in the area of defense between the two states and also on agreeing upon the forms of interaction between the military ministries in the realization of commitments entered into by the sides during the signing of the corresponding bilateral agreements in April 1993. Questions concerning the preservation of peace and security in Europe were also touched upon.
