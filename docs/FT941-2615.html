940319 FT 19 MAR 94 / World Stock Markets: Brazil Sao Paulo added to Thurday's losses with a fall of 4.4 per cent at the close of a moderate session. A jump in domestic interest rates and continued concern over the country's Pounds 52bn foreign debt pushed stock prices down, brokers said. The Bovespa index fell 620 points to 13,318 on turnover of Dollars 293.9m.
