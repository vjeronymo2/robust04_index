941231 FT 31 DEC 94 / Barclays cash withdrawals rise More than Pounds 304m was paid out through Barclays cash dispensers in the week leading up to Christmas, an increase of almost 6 per cent on 1993, the bank said. During the same period nearly 5m withdrawals were made, an increase of 7 per cent, according to Barclays, which operates the country's biggest dispenser network. On December 23, the busiest day, Pounds 7.8m was withdrawn in the hour after noon. On Christmas day Pounds 2.7m was withdrawn.
