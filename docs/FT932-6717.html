930527 FT 27 MAY 93 / Parliament and Politics: Thorp report sent to Howard THE Pollution Inspectorate has passed its report on the public consultation into British Nuclear Fuels' controversial Thorp reprocessing plant at Sellafield to Mr Michael Howard, the environment secretary. He is expected to take several weeks to consider it. The department has received legal advice that it may need to consider the wider economic and social case for Thorp to guard against legal action by pressure groups.
