June 14, 1989, Wednesday, Orange County Edition

FRIENDSHIP FLIGHT LOG

Tony Aliengena, an 11-year-old from San Juan Capistrano, is piloting a single-engine plane in hopes of becoming the youngest person to ever circumnavigate the globe.

From: Boston, Mass.

Arrival: 4:15 p.m. ADT

Next Leg

From: Moncton, Canada

To: Iqaluit, Baffin Island

900 miles

6 hours (est.)

Departure: uncertain

Arrival: uncertain

Total air time: 20 hours, 10 minutes

Total miles flown: 3,730

Highlight

After a two-day layover, caused partly by equipment malfunction, Tony's entourage finally left the country Tuesday and flew through heavy clouds. The travelers cleared customs in Moncton, Canada, where they planned to spend the night. Today, they will try to make up lost time by flying about six hours through to Iqaluit, on the edge of the Arctic Circle.
