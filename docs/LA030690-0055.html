March 6, 1990, Tuesday, Home Edition

QUOTABLE

"I believe in decentralized theater. I believe there is no reason why any good theater should be bottle-necked in New York and Hollywood. I believe in a dramatic map for America that will include great native playhouses in every town large enough to want one."

-- Margo Jones, 1944, quoted in Helen Sheehy's "Margo: The Life and Theatre of Margo Jones"
