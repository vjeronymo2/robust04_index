November 23, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BAN THIS RECIPE?

I would like you to know that I was really offended by a recipe for "Better Than Sex Cake" (Culinary SOS, Oct. 11).

The cake sounded good, but I won't try it because each time I did I'd have to confront my feeling of distaste. To my mind, giving a cake this name showed a lack of fitness and was out of place. Can't sex be kept out of the kitchen? It's everywhere else.

-- DOROTHY R. CORSENTINO

Los Angeles
