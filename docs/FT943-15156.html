940711 FT 11 JUL 94 / Company News (This Week): HP Bulmer The Hereford-based cider maker, which is withdrawing from the soft drinks market, is expected to report operating profits ahead from Pounds 19.6m to Pounds 21.5m on Wednesday. However, pre-tax profits are likely to be around Pounds 4m following exceptional charges for restructuring, including the exit from soft drinks announced in May and losses from the disposal of its pectin business.
