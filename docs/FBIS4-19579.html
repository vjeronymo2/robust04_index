BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Chief of the General Staff General Dogan Gures received today Lieutenant General (Gheorge Radu), first deputy chief of the Romanian General Staff, who arrived in Ankara on an official visit yesterday. The two commanders conferred for some time. Later, a bilateral military training and cooperation implementation plan for 1994 was signed by General Ahmet Corekci, deputy chief of the general staff; and by Lt. Gen (Radu). The plan involves mutual visits, mutual exchange of information on military issues, and mutual observations of exercises.
