940714 FT 14 JUL 94 / Stoy Hayward in Binder alliance Stoy Hayward, the UK's 10th-largest accountancy firm, yesterday announced an alliance with most of the offices of its rival Binder Hamlyn that are not merging with Arthur Andersen. The link with 13 Binder offices will lift the firm's fee income to about Pounds 95m and move it into ninth place ahead of Pannell Kerr Forster.
