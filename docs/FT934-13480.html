931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / UK Company News: Telemetrix TELEMETRIX's 58 per cent-owned subsidiary, Nasdaq-quoted GTI Corporation, lifted sales in the third quarter of 1993 by 28 per cent to Dollars 32.8m (Pounds 21.7m); net income rose 43 per cent to Dollars 3.56m; and earnings per share advanced 35 per cent to 35 cents.
