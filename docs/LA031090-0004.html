March 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

ROONEY REDUX

Andrew Christie (Letters, March 3) criticizes Howard Rosenberg by asserting the columnist "protests the inevitable reaction to his defense of (Andy) Rooney by saying, gee, if you criticize a black person these days everybody calls you a racist.

That's disingenuous. And arrogant. And dumb."

Christie failed to include the most important point: And true.

LANNY R. MIDDINGS, San Ramon
