Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Johannesburg Feb 15 SAPA -- African National Congress [ANC] President Nelson Mandela says he is not concerned about reported disloyalty among certain elements within the South African Defence Force [SADF], SABC [South African Broadcasting Corporation] radio news reports. Speaking at a news conference on Tuesday after meeting Swedish Foreign Minister Margaretha af Ugglas, Mr Mandela said he was convinced the democratic process had the full support of the majority of SADF members. He was not worried about certain elements who wanted to cling to outdated policies as they would not affect the support the SADF was already giving to the negotiation process.
