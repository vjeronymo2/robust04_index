Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Panama City LA PRENSA in Spanish on 1 March on page 11a reported that Gennadiy Y. Knyazev, Nika Trading International general manager, said his company will participate in Panama's Expocomer '94 commercial fair because Russian investors are interested in expanding their trade relations with Panama and using this country as an import and export center. He added he wants to draw the attention of exporters from Panama and other Latin American countries to the market opportunities Russia offers.
