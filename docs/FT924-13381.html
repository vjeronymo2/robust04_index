921015 FT 15 OCT 92 / UK Company News: Intereurope Tech Difficult trading conditions in defence, civil aviation and construction resulted in a 12 per cent reduction in pre-tax profits at Intereurope Technology Services in the year to June 30. The technical documentation and support group reported a decline from Pounds 1.4m to Pounds 1.23m on sales down 18 per cent at Pounds 10.9m. The final dividend is maintained at 5.4p for an unchanged total of 7.4p. Earnings per share came through at 15.03p (14.74p).
