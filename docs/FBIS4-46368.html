BFN [Text] Mohammad Kazem Khonsari, director general of Middle East and North African Affairs in the Iranian Foreign Ministry, today called on Foreign Minister Faris Buwayz; Shaykh Muhammad Mahdi Shams-al-Din, chairman of the Higher Islamic Shiite Council; and 'Allamah Sayyid Muhammad Husayn Fadlallah. He said that his country supports Lebanon's unity, its people's defense of their legitimate rights, and its stance against the Israeli savage challenges and attacks, noting that Lebanon's brothers will not hesitate to support it in all areas. Al-Khunsari denied that Iran has any knowledge of the fate of Israeli airman Ron Arad and criticized international silence about the kidnapping of Mustafa al-Dirani.
