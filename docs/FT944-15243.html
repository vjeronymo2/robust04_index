941019 FT 19 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Whitchurch listing Whitchurch Group, the meat processing and distribution company, has applied for admission to the Official List. Permission is expected to be granted today with first dealings tomorrow. In February, Shore Capital placed 2.73m shares and raised Pounds 1.28m for the company. The shares currently trade under Rule 4.2 at 54 1/2 p, giving the group a market capitalisation of Pounds 7.28m.
