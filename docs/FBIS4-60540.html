BFN [Text] Minister Moshe Shahal says that Yasir 'Arafat will not be able to visit Jerusalem without an official Israeli invitation, because a person fulfilling a political role cannot visit a foreign country without an invitation. According to Minister Shahal the incidents between Israelis and Palestinian policemen over the last few days stem from a lack of coordination between the Israeli authorities and the Palestinian police. This is reported by our correspondent Dani Gur-Arye.
