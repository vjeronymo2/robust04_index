DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION Federal Aviation Administration [Advisory Circular 25.785&hyph;1A] Flight Attendant Seat and Torso Restraint System Installations AGENCY: Federal Aviation Administration, DOT. ACTION: Notice of issuance of advisory circular.

SUMMARY: This notice announces the issuance of Advisory Circular (AC) 25.785&hyph;1A, Flight Attendant Seat and Torso Restraint System Installations. This AC provides information and guidance regarding an acceptable means, but not the only means, of compliance with the portions of §§25.785 and 121.311 of the FAR which deal with flight attendant seats.
