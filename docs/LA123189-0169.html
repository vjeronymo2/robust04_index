December 31, 1989, Sunday, Valley Edition

BRYAN 3RD IN FIESTA BOWL TENNIS

Bob Bryan, 11, of Camarillo finished third in the boys' 12-and-under division of the Fiesta Bowl tennis tournament in Tempe, Ariz., on Saturday.

Bryan, seeded third, lost to Ramin Pajan of Vancouver, B. C., 6-4, 6-3, in the semifinals but finished third when Rafael Jordan of Puerto Rico defaulted because of a muscle pull.

Bryan's twin, Mike, was eliminated from title contention Friday but finished fifth with two victories Saturday. Mike, seeded seventh, defeated Corey Guy of San Francisco, 7-6, 6-4, and Jake Graiton of Portland, Ore., 6-2, 6-0.
