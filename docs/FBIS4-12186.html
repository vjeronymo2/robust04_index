BFN [Text] His Highness Shaykh Zayid Bin-Sultan Al Nuhayyan, the head of state, received at the Rawdat al-Rif rest house this morning Mr. 'Ali Salim al-Bid, deputy chairman of the Presidential Council in the Republic of Yemen. Al-Bid briefed his highness on the latest developments of the Yemeni situation and efforts to end the political crisis. Views were exchanged on current Arab developments. His highness the head of state emphasized his concern for Yemen's stability and national unity. His highness said: Our care for our brothers and kinsfolk in Yemen is equal to our care for our own people and country. He added: Contributions from any of our brothers toward solving problems in Yemen deserve all appreciation and respect from his brothers because Yemen's stability and security is in the interest of all. For his part, 'Ali Salim al-Bid praised his highness' pan-Arab stands, wise leadership, positive initiative, and continuous efforts for the progress and safety of the Arab nation in general and Yemen in particular. Al-Bid said: We have come to you fully confident of your efforts and endeavors of which we are proud in order to serve Yemen and its progress. The meeting was attended by His Highness Sultan Bin-Zayid Al Nuhayyan, the deputy prime minister; His Highness Lieutenant General Muhammad Bin-Zayid Al Nuhayyan, the chief of staff of the Armed Forces; His Highness Hamdan Bin-Zayid Al Nuhayyan, minister of state for foreign affairs; and Khamis Bayk al-Rumaythi, director of President Zayid's office.
