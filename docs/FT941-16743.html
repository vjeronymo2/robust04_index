940108 FT 08 JAN 94 / Theatre company wins Pounds 3.3m Allied-Lyons, the food and drink company, is to sponsor the Royal Shakespeare Company with Pounds 3.3m over the next three years, Antony Thorncroft writes. The money will be split between the theatre company's core activities and international tours and educational projects. The deal is vital to the future of the RSC. It has a deficit of Pounds 2.4m, and at the end of 1993 Royal Insurance, which had been its principal commercial backer for six years, ended its sponsorship. Mr Adrian Noble, artistic director, said: 'After one of the bleakest years ever for the arts we can celebrate Allied-Lyons's commitment to live theatre, its young audiences and the international esteem in which theatre in this country is held.'
