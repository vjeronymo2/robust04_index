940915 FT 15 SEP 94 / Train given wrong signal, says RMT A passenger train was given the wrong signal yesterday by a Railtrack manager acting as a trainee signaller, the RMT transport union has claimed. The 8.03am Glasgow to Kilmarnock train was given a green light and shown the incorrect route indicator near Busby, the RMT said. The driver followed procedure and challenged the signal. He received an apology from Glasgow Signalling Centre, which admitted: 'The trainee was a bit too fast.'
