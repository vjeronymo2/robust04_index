940715 FT 15 JUL 94 / World News in Brief: Chicago Pizza Pie man Bob Payton dies Bob Payton, who founded the American food chain which includes the Chicago Pizza Pie factory and Henry J Beans, has been killed in a car accident in Hertfordshire. New York-born Mr Payton arrived in Britain in 1973. By the time his My Kinda Town group went public in May this year, he had 30 restaurants in cities including London, Paris and Tel Aviv.
