920425 FT 25 APR 92 / Finance & the Family: Unit trust launch Foster & Braithwaite is launching a new unit trust, called the High Income Investment Trust fund. It will invest in the income shares of split capital trusts and will aim for a yield double that of the FT-A Investment Trust Index (currently about 3.7 per cent). The minimum investment is Pounds 1,000 with a 5.5 per cent initial charge and an 1.25 per cent annual charge.
