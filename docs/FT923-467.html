920929 FT 29 SEP 92 / UK Company News: Soutra Foods changes hands Pauls Food Group, a subsidiary of Harrisons & Crosfield, has sold the business and assets of Soutra Foods to Allied-Lyons. Soutra makes rusks, seasonings and bread crumbs, and is based at Broxburn, Lothian. Its operation is complementary to DCA Industries, Lyons' UK ingredient business. Harrisons said the consideration was not material in relation to its net assets.
