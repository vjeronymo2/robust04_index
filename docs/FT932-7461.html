930524 FT 24 MAY 93 / Iran warns Iraq over rebel raids An Iranian military commander warned Iraq his forces would chase Iranian rebels back to their bases inside its territory if they continue cross-border raids against the Islamic republic, Reuter reports from Tehran. Brigadier General Mohammad Ali Jafari, commander of the Islamic Revolution Guards Corps ground force, made his comments in an interview published yesterday by the Jomhuri Islami newspaper.
