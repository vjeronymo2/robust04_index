920428 FT 28 APR 92 / UK Company News: HC Slingsby tumbles to Pounds 82,000 Pre-tax profits of HC Slingsby, a maker of trucks, ladders and ancillary equipment, dropped from Pounds 495,503 to Pounds 82,500 over 1991 on turnover down from Pounds 11.1m to Pounds 9.95m. Earnings per share tumbled from 33p to 4.4p, but the total dividend is held at 7.5p with an unchanged final of 5.5p.
