920806 FT 06 AUG 92 / BCCI case moved to Old Bailey THE prosecution of the first man to be arrested in Britain in connection with the the collapse of Bank of Credit and Commerce International has been transferred from City of London magistrates court to the Old Bailey. Mr Mohammed Abdul Baqi, a former managing director of Attock Oil, a group based in London, is on Pounds 600,000 bail facing eight charges.
