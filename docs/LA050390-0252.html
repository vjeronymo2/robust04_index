May 3, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

NIXON TO GIVE 90-MINUTE INTERVIEW TO PBS FRIDAY

Following his appearances on the "Today" show all this week, former President Nixon sits for a lengthier PBS interview at 9 p.m. Friday on KDOC and 9:30 p.m. on KDOC.

In "Richard Nixon Reflects," the New Republic's Morton Kondracke interviews Nixon for 90 minutes.
