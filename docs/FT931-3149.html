930317 FT 17 MAR 93 / New killing in Algiers A FORMER minister was shot dead as he left his home in suburban Algiers yesterday, AP reports. The official Algerian news agency APS said Mr Djilali Liabes, 45, was shot at his home in Ben Omar by three gunmen who fled. Ben Omar is in south-east Algiers near Kouba, a stronghold of Moslem fundamentalists, who have faced a tough government crackdown for more than a year. Mr Liabes was recently named head of the 'Group of Experts 2015', to draw up a study on Algeria's future.
