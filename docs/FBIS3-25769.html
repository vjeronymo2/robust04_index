Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Durban Feb 23 SAPA -- The Democratic Party [DP] in Natal announced its election candidates for the provincial legislature and National Assembly this week, many of them leading community members and administrators. Heading the 40-member provincial list for the National Assembly are MPs Mike Ellis, Mahmoud Rajab, and Carol Charlewood. Other candidates include leading Malcolm Wallis, SC [Senior Counsel], farmer and MP Wessel Nel, and environmental campaigner and attorney Oliff d'Oliviera. Leading the 80-member list for the provincial legislature is recently-elected Natal DP Chairman Roger Burrows. Others are Phoenix businessman Omie Singh, Queensburgh Mayor Dave Upfold and retired police station Commander Mike Reddy. A number of the candidates hold positions on both lists.
