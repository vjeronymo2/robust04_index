BFN [Text] Bratislava, April 27 (CTK) -- Forty-five percent of Slovaks believe that early elections (scheduled for September 30-October 1) will be won by Vladimir Meciar's Movement for a Democratic Slovakia (HZDS), who was dismissed [from the post of prime minister] in parliament in March, a poll conducted by the Slovak Statistical Office in April revealed. Fifteen percent of Slovaks believe the victory will be scored by Peter Weiss's Democratic Left Party (SDL). The victory of Premier Jozef Moravcik's Democratic Union of Slovakia (DEUS) was predicted by five percent of those polled. Twenty-two percent of Slovaks would never vote for the HZDS. The same number would not vote for the Christian Democratic Movement (KDH). The poll revealed that the two parties' followers are the strongest opponents of one another.
