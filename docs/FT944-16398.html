941013 FT 13 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Derwent debenture Derwent Valley Holdings, the property investment company, is to issue Pounds 35m of First Mortgage Debenture Stock 2019. The proceeds will be used to refinance the company's other borrowings and extend their maturity profile. The coupon has been set at 10.125 per cent with the issue price at Pounds 99.139 per cent. This gives an effective yield to investors of 165 basis points over the gross redemption yield on 8.75 per cent Treasury Stock 2017.
