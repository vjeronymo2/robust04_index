921124 FT 24 NOV 92 / Losses from accidents detailed ACCIDENTS and ill health at work can cost businesses up to 37 per cent of annual profits, a study by the Health and Safety Executive has found. The study, to be published in the new year, shows company losses through accidents running at between eight and 36 times the insured costs. The executive says nine people are killed and 400 badly injured every week at work.
