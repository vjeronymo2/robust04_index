Article Type:BFN [Editorial Report] Rio de Janeiro JORNAL DO BRASIL in Portuguese on 2 March publishes on page 15 a 300-word unattributed article on the increasing consumption of "skunk," a more potent form of marijuana, in Rio de Janeiro. This article quotes Federal Police operations chief Roberto Frei as saying its consumption has risen in certain areas of the city in the past two months. This new drug is obtained from marijuana but "has a lethargic power 10 times higher than hashish."
