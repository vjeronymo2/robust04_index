930514 FT 14 MAY 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa GOLDS climbed throughout the day as the bullion price held its gains, and threatened to test the Dollars 370 an ounce level. The gold index was 191, or 13.2 per cent, higher at 1,635. Industrials rose 44 to 4,449 as the overall index climbed 123, or 3.3 per cent, to 3,888.
