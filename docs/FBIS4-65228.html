BFN [Excerpt] Lefkosa [Nicosia], May 12 (A.A.) -- The Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus, upon which an embargo is imposed, made an agreement with Israel to boost tourism, a move that would eventually give impetus to the country's economy. Scheduled maritime travel between Gazimagosa [Famagusta] Bay and the Haifa Bay in Israel has been established on a regular basis. Sea buses of the Fergun Navigation Company have been conducting trial cruises since March. [passage omitted]
