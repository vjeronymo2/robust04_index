920928 FT 28 SEP 92 / UK Company News: F&C Pacific net asset value at 190.5p Net assets per share at the Foreign & Colonial Pacific Investment Trust fell from 228.3p to 190.5p basic over the 12 months to July 31. Allowing for the exercise of warrants the drop was from 216.6p to 181.8p. Net revenue for the half year ended July slumped to Pounds 533,000, against Pounds 1.42m, for earnings per share of 0.5p (1.34p). The interim dividend is being maintained at 0.75p.
