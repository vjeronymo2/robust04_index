BFN [Text] Vice President brother 'Ali Salim al-Bid yesterday held a telephone conversation with First Deputy Prime Minister brother Dr. Hasan Muhammad Makki to reassure himself about Makki's health in the aftermath of the sinful attempt on his life yesterday afternoon. He wished him speedy recovery. In a related development, brother Salim Salih Muhammad, member of the Presidential Council and assistant secretary general of the Yemeni Socialist Party, has sent a cable to First Deputy Prime Minister brother Dr. Hasan Muhammad Makki, in which he expressed his condemnation of the sinful incident to which Dr. Hasan Muhammad Makki has been exposed. He wished him recovery.
