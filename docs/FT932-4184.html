930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / UK Company News: UniChem extends network UNICHEM, through its E Moss subsidiary, has acquired a further four pharmacies, and is expected to complete on a fifth shortly, for a total of Pounds 2.3m. One is in Totnes, Devon, through the acquisition of W&JA Rucker, one is in Hull, North Humberside, through the acquisition of Edward Hindle, and two are in Ashton-under-Lyne, Greater Manchester, through the acquisition of Limefast. The fifth is in Burnley, Lancashire, and is being acquired from Earlston. The acquisitions will be financed as to Pounds 1.3m in cash and Pounds 1m by the issue of 476,675 10p shares. The pharmacies, which have an indicated turnover of Pounds 2.9m per annum and net assets at acquisition of Pounds 670,000, will bring the number of pharmacies operated by Moss Chemists to 236.
