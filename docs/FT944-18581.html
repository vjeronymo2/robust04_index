941003 FT 03 OCT 94 / Company News (This Week): Browning-Ferris Industries - Other Companies The US waste services group, is expected to publish its offer document this week detailing its hostile Pounds 364m cash offer for Attwoods. Laidlaw, Attwoods' largest shareholder, has agreed to sell its 29.8 per cent stake at 109p per share, and its 73 per cent preference holding at 85p.
