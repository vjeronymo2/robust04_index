Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Touring the Gaza Strip today, Lieutenant General Ehud Baraq, the chief of staff, said it is the IDF's [Israel Defense Forces] supreme duty to enforce law and order on Arabs and Jews alike. He further said that to foil terrorist attacks, officers and soldiers have to be alert and vigilant. An instant reaction by a soldier who finds himself on the scene of an attempted attack can make all the difference between a successful or a foiled attack. Our correspondent Karmela Menashe reports that the chief of staff inspected the preparations for the implementation of the Gaza-Jericho agreement.
