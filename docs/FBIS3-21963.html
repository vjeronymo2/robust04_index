Language: German Article Type:BFN [Report by Christian Roesner: "Dealers Owning Millions Arrested"] [Excerpts] The turnover that a drug gang achieved with 1,400-1,800 kg of hashish in "Theo's Corner" in Vienna-Fuenfhaus is said to have amounted to 140-180 million schillings; twenty-four offenders, among them four principals, are under arrest. This was reported by Joachim Pirker from the Security Office in the capital on Friday [11 March]. The hashish was smuggled in amounts of 10-30 kg under the back seats of cars from Amsterdam to Austria. [passage omitted] During the arrest on Monday [7 March], savings bank books with deposits of 800,000 schillings, 300,000 schillings in cash, 10 kg of hashish, and some weapons were seized.
