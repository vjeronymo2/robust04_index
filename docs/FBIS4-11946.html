BFN [Text] Fourteen persons, two of whom are at large, accused of attempting to assassinate a police officer, attempting to assassinate a citizen in Oran, and of carrying out numerous robberies, were tried yesterday in Oran. The Public Prosecutor asked for three death penalties. The special court in Algiers yesterday issued 13 death sentences against members of armed groups. Ten of the sentences were in absentia. The charges against them were: Belonging to armed groups, premeditated murder, and violating state security. A statement by the security services reported that the security forces have eliminated six terrorists in the center and east of Algeria in Batna, Boumerdes, Blida, and Jijel.
