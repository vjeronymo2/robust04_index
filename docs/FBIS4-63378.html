BFN [Text] Moscow has not made any statements about recognizing the independent Yemeni Democratic Republic declared by the south Yemen leadership, Viktor Posuvalyuk, director of the Russian Foreign Ministry's Department of Near and Middle East Affairs, said today. He was speaking in connection with accusations by representatives of north Yemen alleging that Russia had recognized the independence of that republic. Meanwhile, MENA reports that Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Salim 'Ali Basindwah, received Igor Ivashenko, Russia's ambassador to Yemen, today. The Russian diplomat said that Moscow supports Yemeni unity without frontiers.
