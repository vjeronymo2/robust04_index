Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 19 (KCNA) -- Yang Hyong-sop, chairman of the Supreme People's Assembly, sent a message of greetings to Charles Bauza Donwahi upon his election as president of the National Assembly of Cote d'Ivoire. Expressing the belief that the friendly relations between the two parliaments will develop in the interests of the two peoples, the message wished him success in his responsible work.
