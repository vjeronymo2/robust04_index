Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 13 (KCNA) -- The U.S. imperialists brought formations of fighter-bombers and pursuit-assault planes to the air above Yongwol, Wonju and the Tokjok Islet, South Korea, from their base in Japan on January 11 to stage an exercise of making surprise air raids on appointed targets with the support of tanker planes and returning nonstop to the base, according to military sources. Synchronizing with the exercise, an Okinawa-based RC-135 strategic reconnaissance plane flew to the air above the Military Demarcation Line area of South Korea and made a flight from east to west for aerial espionage on the whole areas of the northern half of Korea.
