930713 FT 13 JUL 93 / International Company News: Coors to cut 500 jobs ADOLPH Coors, the third biggest US brewer, is to cut 500 jobs, or about 20 per cent of its white collar workforce, in an attempt to reduce costs and improve long-term profitability. Coors hopes to achieve the job reductions through enhanced early retirement packages, incentives for voluntary redundancy and attrition.
