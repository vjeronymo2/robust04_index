BFN [Excerpt] Doboj, 13 Apr (TANJUG)--Muslim units intensified their attacks on Serb positions and residential areas in the Doboj area over the last 24 hours, the information service of the First [Bosnian Serb] Krajina Corps of the Srpska republic's army has reported. Strong Muslim forces carried out several infantry attacks with artillery backing on the Ozren front. Ozren defenders responded efficiently forcing the attackers to withdraw. The Muslims fiercely targeted Serb positions on the western part of the Doboj front. They fired several artillery shells on the town itself, wounding eight civilians, including five children.
