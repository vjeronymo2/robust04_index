BFN [Text] The Defense Institute for Fire Research in New Delhi has developed a fire fighting system that can detect and put out fires in less than one-fourth of a second. The director of the institute, Major General B.S. Kataria, said the automatic fire and explosion suppression system, as it is called, has been fitted on the MBT [Main Battle Tank]-Arjun tank and is to be fitted also on the T-72 tanks. He said this fire fighting system has been integrated with another system to alert the tank crew in the event of a nuclear, biological, and chemical attack. The fire protection system can also be fitted in ships and armored personnel carriers.
