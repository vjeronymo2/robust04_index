Language: Mandarin Article Type:BFN [Text] In the six months ending on 24 December, more than 26,000 Guangxi frontier troops had taken part in mine clearing missions covering a total area of 3.1427 million square meters, exceeding the target by 208 percent and providing a fine environment for trade exchanges and border people. Since the beginning of the mine clearing mission, frontier troops from the Guangxi Military District have fulfilled in a timely manner mine clearing tasks in 12 trade ports, including Dongxing, Dongzhong, Aidian, and Youyiguan; 17 border trade sites, including Zhushan, Canzhan, Lihuo, Banlang, Beishanyouan, and Longyao; as well as along main routes. They have cleared mines from 118 sites and have marked those sites.
