Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] During its meeting this morning under the chairmanship of Dr. Mustafa Kamal Hilmi, the Consultative Council condemned the terrorist attack against Palestinian worshipers at the Ibrahimi Mosque in the West Bank yesterday. Dr. Muhammad Zaki Abu-'Amir, minister of state for People's Assembly and Consultative Council affairs, described the incident as hideous and involved Palestinian brothers praying at the Ibrahimi Mosque. He affirmed that the Egyptian leadership has very strongly condemned this painful incident. Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa has also announced that President Husni Mubarak was extremely disturbed by this incident and followed it up closely. He affirmed that the government condemns this crime and is closely following the situation to adopt what would be consistent with the public interest. He explained the importance of emphasizing that the Palestinians' security is a matter that must be given every consideration. He said all measures must be adopted to prevent a repetition of such incidents and to keep the future of peace from falling into the hands of the extremists and terrorists.
