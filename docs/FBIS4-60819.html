BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Anna Melnikova, Yuriy Sizov] [Text] Moscow, 30 Apr -- Russia once again declares that it condemns the Stalinist repressions against independent Latvia, Boris Yeltsin said in the Kremlin today after the signing of a package of Russian-Latvian documents. Latvian President Guntis Ulmanis thanked Boris Yeltsin for this gesture. For the inhabitants of Latvia these are very generous and fine words, which will help us to come to terms with many psychological contradictions that have been created during these decades, he said.
