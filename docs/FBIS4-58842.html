BFN [Text] Dubai, United Arab Emirates, May 6 (AFP)--The southern Yemen Socialist Party (YSP) called for a ceasefire on Friday to halt its two-day-old civil war against the northern forces of President 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Aden Radio said. The radio, monitored by Gulf news agencies, said the YSP based in the southern port city of Aden "accepts a ceasefire in response to calls from Arab and foreign countries." "This favourable response is dictated by the interests of the Yemeni people, whose lives, belongings, unity, armed forces and economic installations must be preserved," the radio said, quoting a YSP statement.
