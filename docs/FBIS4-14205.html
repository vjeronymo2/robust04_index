BFN [Text] People's Assembly Speaker 'Abd-al-Qadir Qaddurah today received Aleksandr Dzasokhov, member of the State Duma Council, and deputy chairman of the parliamentary group of the Russian Federation. People's Assembly member Hasan Sibahi attended the meeting. The talks dealt with the parliamentary relations between the People's Assembly and the Russian Federation Parliament, and the need to promote these relations on firm and practical bases in the interest of the two countries. Dzasokhov gave a briefing on the Russian Parliament's efforts, particularly those of the parliamentary group, to promote relations with the Arab states.
