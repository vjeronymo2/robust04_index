940616 FT 16 JUN 94 / International Company News: Seat to halve executive posts Seat, the loss-making Spanish unit of Volkswagen, the German carmaker, will abolish half its management posts in an attempt to increase competitiveness, chairman Mr Joan Llorens said yesterday, Reuter reports from Barcelona. Speaking after a meeting of the Seat board, he said the management shake-up would slash the number of executive posts from 201 to 99. Most of the staff would move to other jobs in the company, with about 40 taking early retirement or voluntary redundancy. Earlier, VW said Seat's sales were continuing to recover.
