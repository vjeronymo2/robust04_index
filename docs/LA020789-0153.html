February 7, 1989, Tuesday, Late Final Edition

EVANGELIST SENTENCED FOR MOLESTING BOYS

An evangelist who admitted molesting at least 100 boys along the Southern revival circuit since the 1960s was sentenced today to 2 1/2 years in prison for sodomizing two teen-agers.

"I suppose the thing that troubles me most is somebody of the cloth, who knows the truth, has openly and flagrantly lied," Judge Kenneth Trabue told Mario (Tony) Leyva, a Pentecostal preacher from Columbus, Ga.
