Language: English Article Type:BFN [By E. Oelofse] [Text] Pretoria Mar 13 SAPA -- Law and Order Minister Hernus Kriel has lifted unrest restrictions placed on 52 areas during the violence in Bophuthatswana. In a statement on Sunday [13 March], Mr Kriel said the lifting of restrictions would take place at midnight of 13/14 March. Mr Kriel added that the unrest restrictions had been declared "to contain and combat any possible overflow of unrest from the homeland during the crisis," and not "to inhibit in any manner...(the) expression of free and fair political activity."
