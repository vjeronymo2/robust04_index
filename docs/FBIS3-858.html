Language: Portuguese Article Type:BFN [Text] The Mozambique National Resistance, Renamo, is keeping two arms depots in the former base of Maringue, in northern Zambezia [as heard] Province. A government source in Namarroi told Radio Mozambique that war materiel is being watched by two soldiers. That source also said that a group of 1,000 armed men belonging to the group that used to be led by [former Mozambican National Union leader] Gimo Phiri have been seeing moving in the vicinity of Lugela and Namarroi Districts.
