940513 FT 13 MAY 94 / UK Company News: Schroder Korea net assets improve Net asset value per share of Schroder Korea Fund stood at Dollars 13.13 (Pounds 8.90) at February 28, against Dollars 8.96 at end-December 1992. Fully diluted, the figure was Dollars 12.61 (Dollars 8.96). The net deficit for the 14 months period amounted to Dollars 509,000 (Dollars 119,000 surplus) and losses per share were 10.18 cents (earnings of 2.38 cents).
