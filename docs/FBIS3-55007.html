Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tallinn, January 4 -- "Russian Liberal Democratic Party has its daughter organization in Estonia," Pyotr Rozhok who was active as Vladimir Zhirinovskiy's Estonian representative already before general elections in Russia, told ETA. Rozhok who said he was Zhirinovskiy's advisor, but not head of the Estonian organization, refused to disclose the size of the party's Estonian branch. "Also members of other parties can join our party, even if they are communists," Rozhok said while adding that membership fee was 100 rubles and admission fee ten rubles. Rozhok said one of the party's tasks in Estonia was to provide Zhirinovskiy with materials on Baltics while adding that during election campaign Zhirinovskiy had made abundant use of the information. "When the right time comes, we will tell more about ourselves and our activities," he said.
