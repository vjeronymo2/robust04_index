Language: French Article Type:BFN [Text] A French woman was shot with two bullets in the head this afternoon in Algiers. The woman, aged 45, was married to an Algerian and the mother of three children. She worked with the French Consulate in Algiers. She was the fourth French citizen to be killed in Algeria since last September, and the 25th foreigner killed in the country. The Quai d'Orsay [Foreign Ministry] has condemned what it has called an odious crime. The French ambassador was received this evening by Algerian Prime Minister Redha Malek. He told him about the concern of the French Government. As a sign of mourning, all French services in Algeria will be closed tomorrow, 16 January.
