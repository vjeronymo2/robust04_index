BFN [Mirjana Kuzma report] [Text] This afternoon, president of the Srpska republic Radovan Karadzic met suddenly and unexpectedly with the chief of the UN Protection Forces [UNPROFOR] Civilian Sector (Viktor Andreeyev) in Pale. During the meeting, Karadzic and (Andreeyev) tried to harmonize the agreement reached yesterday at the meeting of the Srpska republic political leadership with UN special envoy to the former Yugoslavia Yasushi Akashi. The organs of the Srpska republic promised to realize the agreement on the withdrawal of the Serbian artillery from the 3 km zone in the sector of Gorazde on the left bank of the Drina River where UNPROFOR forces will subsequently be deployed. The political and military leadership of the Srpska republic will assist in the evacuation of all injured and sick from Gorazde and other settlements, while convoys of all international humanitarian organizations will be guaranteed a safe passage thought the Serbian territories in the company of the Srpska republic police.
