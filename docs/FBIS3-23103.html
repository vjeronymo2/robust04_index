Language: Arabic Article Type:BFN [Text] Cairo, 1 Feb (MENA)--The Interior Ministry issued a statement tonight in which it noted that at around 1800 this evening, an unidentified person placed a plastic bag in a flower bed in the island in the middle of Murad Street in al-Jizah. The bag contained locally made gunpowder and silver-plated paper which caused an explosion. The statement said that a fire brigade arrived at the scene of the incident and succeeded in containing the effects of the explosion, which smashed the glass front of a billboard above the flower bed. The prosecution was notified to launch an investigation into the incident.
