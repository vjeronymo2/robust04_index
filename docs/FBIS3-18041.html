Language: German Article Type:BFN [Text] According to the Friday [25 February] edition of BERLINER ZEITUNG, Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel (Free Democratic Party of Germany) wants to accelerate the prosecution of war criminals from Bosnia who are currently in Germany. In a letter dated 18 February, Kinkel asked the historian Fadila Memisevic to disclose the names of 50 suspects known to her. He wants to pass on the information to the competent authorities, the paper writes. Memisevic works for the center for the registration of war crimes and genocide in Zenica in central Bosnia.
