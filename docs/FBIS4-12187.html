BFN [Excerpts] Brother Lieutenant General 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, chairman of the Presidential Council, returned to Sanaa in God's protection at 1330 today after paying official visits to the Sultanate of Oman and the State of Qatar. He led a high-level delegation to the two countries, and held talks with his brothers His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id of Oman and His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of Qatar. His talks dealt with the fraternal relations between our country and both sisterly countries, the current developments in our country, and the efforts to contain the political crisis and safeguard the national unity march, in addition to the regional, Arab, and international issues and developments of interest to our country, to fraternal Oman and Qatar, to the region, and to our Arab nation. On his arrival at Sanaa International Airport, the brother Presidential Council chairman made a statement. He said: We are satisfied with the outcome of our visits to Oman and Qatar and with the talks we held with my brothers His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id of Oman and His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of Qatar. These talks were positive and fruitful. We sensed a sincere fraternal understanding of the overall issues discussed, whether regarding the ongoing developments in our country, the regional issues, or the Arab developments of interest to our country, to both sisterly countries, and to our Arab nation. He added: The talks particularly dealt with developments in our country and the exertion of efforts to contain the political crisis and its negative repercussions on the national situation as well as its threats posed to stability in our country and the region. Chairman Salih concluded: We were pleased with the understanding and care shown by our brothers in Oman and Qatar who emphasized backing for our people and their unlimited support for Yemeni unity and democracy as a national gain for our people and as a pan-Arab gain for the sons of our Arab nation. A session of official talks between our country and Qatar was held at the Amiri Court in Doha at 0930, Sanaa time, today. The session was led by brothers Lt. Gen. 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman, and His Highness Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani, amir of fraternal Qatar. [passage omitted] Talks dealt with Yemeni developments. The brother chairman briefed his brother His Highness the amir of Qatar on the outcome of the efforts and mediations being made to achieve national accord in our country, contain the political crisis, end all its negative repercussions, and implement the document of pledge and accord. The latest of these efforts was the meeting held between brothers Lt. Gen. 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih, Presidential Council chairman, and his deputy, 'Ali Salim al-Bid, in Salalah in the presence of His Majesty Sultan Qabus Bin-Sa'id of fraternal Oman. Shaykh Khalifah Bin-Hamad Al Thani reaffirmed Qatar's care for Yemen's unity and its support for all efforts and good offices designed to end the crisis between the brothers in Yemen and to spread security and stability throughout Yemen in order to preserve the gains of the fraternal Yemeni people. The amir of Qatar also expressed his great confidence in the wisdom of both the leadership and the various national and political forces and in their ability to circumvent this crisis and respond to the Yemeni people's aspirations. Talks also reviewed the recent developments in the Middle East peace process and regional developments. The two leaders also exchanged views on all that would enhance Arab and Islamic solidarity. Moreover, the talks dealt with ways to consolidate and promote fraternal relations in various fields in a way that will benefit both sisterly countries and peoples.
