BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 20/4/1994 -- The prime minister of the Netherlands, Ruud Lubbers, who is also the leader of the Dutch People's Party (Christian-Democratic,) met Tuesday, 19 April, at the embassy of Holland, during his official visit in Romania, with Emil Constantinescu, president of the Democratic Convention of Romania (CDR), and Corneliu Coposu, president of the National Peasant Christian-Democratic Party (PNTCD). The sides examined the latest political and economic evolutions in the two countries, as well as cooperation relations in the European bodies, the CDR press bureau informs.
