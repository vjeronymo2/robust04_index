921110 FT 10 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Audax Properties dips to Pounds 208,000 Audax Properties produced a pre-tax profit of Pounds 208,000 in the six months to September 30, compared with Pounds 227,000. Net revenue from properties rose from Pounds 1.03m to Pounds 1.18m. The portfolio was fully let with more than 90 per cent of income coming from blue chip tenants.
