Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi VNA March 24 -- General Secretary Do Muoi and his entourage arrived in Kuala Lumpur, this morning beginning his official friendship visit to Malaysia as guest of Prime Minister Mohamed. Mr Do Muoi was welcomed at the airport by Foreign Minister Abdullah Ahmad Badawi and accompanied Minister Daud Taha. After the official welcomed ceremony held in the Parliamentary Square, General Secretary Do Muoi and Prime Minister Mahathir Mohamed reviewed the honour guards of Malaysian armed forces. Mr. Mahathir Mohamed introduced Mr Do Muoi the Malaysian Government's officials. Among those present at the event on the Malaysian side were Deputy Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim, Foreign Minister Abdullah Ahmad Badawi, Cabinet Minister Abang Abu Bakar Mustapha, and Defence Minister Najib Tun Razak. Vietnamese Ambassador to Malaysia Ngo Tat To and members of diplomatic corps attended the ceremony.
