940826 FT 26 AUG 94 / Observer: Retrofit Help. What on earth do you do when you get the following letter from the Bank of Scotland? Dear Sir, we hereby give you notice that by retrocession dated 2/8/94 Bank of Scotland have retrocessed, reponed and restored. . . ' Answers please to Lyndon Jones, managing director, Pointon York, (Fax No 0533 548529), who promises a bottle of champagne for the best English translation. Jones' decision will be final and Bank of Scotland officials are not eligible to enter.
