Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Yaounde, Jan 12 (AFP) -- Cameroon police used water cannon to drive off 100 students marching on the prime minister's office here on Wednesday to protest the death of some students killed in a road accident. The angry undergraduates said 15 of their fellows were killed when a minibus taking them to university was in collision at dawn with a truck on the outskirts of town.
