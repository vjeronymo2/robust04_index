BFN [Text] The security services announced today that the security forces eliminated 14 armed groups members on 18 and 20 April and arrested another 15 members on 17 April in Jijel, El Bayadh, Guelma, and M'sila. The same source has also announced that the mayor of Aouf municipality in Mascara Province has been murdered by armed men. The president of the executive delegation [mayor appointed by government] of Chabat El Ameur in Boumerdes province was also murdered by two armed persons.
