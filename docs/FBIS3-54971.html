Language: English Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Galina Gridneva] [Text] Dushanbe January 5 TASS -- Under arrangements reached by the Governments of Russia and Tajikistan, the introduction of Russian money to replace Soviet begins in Tajikistan as of this Wednesday [5 January]. Chairman of the National Bank Kayum Kavmiddinov announced this on local tv late on Tuesday evening. All banknotes with the denomination of five and ten thousand roubles will be withdrown from circulatuion as of January 5. One thousand, 500 and 100 rouble banknotes will remain valid till January 8. Old banknotes held by the population can be put on special accounts in savings banks, Kavmiddinov said. When all the old banknotes are withdrown, these sums can be taken from the accounts. Sums in excess of 200 thousand roubles will be put on accounts upon the declaration of incomes. The January wages, salaries, pensions and other alowances will be paid in new Russian banknotes in the amount not surpassing 32 thousands which is equal to four minimum wages. The chairman of the national bank announced the ban on taking Russian money out of Tajikistan and a temporary stoppage of the registration of various commercial enterprises.
