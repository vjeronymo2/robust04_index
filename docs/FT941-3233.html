940317 FT 17 MAR 94 / Medical care for hunger strikers Extra medical care has been provided for more than 100 asylum seekers on hunger strike at the Campsfield House Immigration Detention Centre, near Oxford, the Home Office said last night. About 150 people from Asia, Africa and South America have been refusing to eat in protest at being 'treated like prisoners'. They want the authorities to free them while their cases are processed.
