Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The government has decided to extend the ban on the militant outfit, JKLF [Jammu and Kashmir Liberation Front], for another two years. The step has been taken under the Unlawful Activities Prevention Act. The JKLF was declared an unlawful organization by a notification of the Home Ministry two years ago. In the Kashmir Valley, four hard-core militants belonging to an outlawed outfit were killed in an encounter with the Army near Tangmarg in Baramula District yesterday. A spokesman of the Defense Ministry said in Srinagar that one Army jawan [soldier] was also killed in the encounter.
