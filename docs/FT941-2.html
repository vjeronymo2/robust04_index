940331 FT 31 MAR 94 / International Company News: International power side lifts Tractabel Tractabel, the Belgian energy group, said profits rose by 6.3 per cent last year, largely due to the development of its international gas and electricity division. Total consolidated profits at the group, one of Belgium's largest holding companies, were BFr9.9bn (Dollars 288m) in 1992, up from BFr9.3bn the year before. Total turnover in the group rose by 4.4 per cent to BFr263.4bn, up from BFr252.5bn in 1992. The group has proposed a dividend payment of BFr340 per share, up from BFr330 the preious year.
