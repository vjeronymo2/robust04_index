BFN [Text] Beijing, April 4 (XINHUA) -- Chinese Vice-Premier Li Lanqing met with Whitney MacMillan, chairman of the board and chief executive officer of the U.S. Cargill Incorporated, and his party here this afternoon. MacMillan and his party arrived here yesterday as guests of China National Cereals, Oils and Foodstuffs Import and Export Corporation. Cargill Incorporated, founded in 1868, is now one of the largest trans-national companies in the world.
