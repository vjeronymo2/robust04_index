931120 FT 20 NOV 93 / International Company News: Jurys Hotel calls for IPounds 7.6m JURYS HOTEL Group, the Irish-based hotel operator, yesterday announced a 30 per cent increase in interim profits and a IPounds 7.59m (Pounds 7.25m) rights issue to fund expansion plans. Pre-tax profits for the six months to October 31 grew from IPounds 2.16m to IPounds 2.8m (Pounds 2.67m). Turnover rose 21 per cent to IPounds 17.6m and earnings per share came to 10.1p (7.6p). The interim dividend has been held at 2p.
