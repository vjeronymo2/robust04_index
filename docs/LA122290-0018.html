December 22, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

NOT EVERYONE THINKS ROBINSON IS THE PROBLEM

Come on, L.A. sportswriters. If you can't find fault with John Robinson the coach, you surely can comment on his erudition.

When he talked about Cleveland Gary's fumbling problems, he said: "We might have to give him some time to recover his ground."

When commenting on the remainder of the season for the Rams, he said. "This is a team that has to see itself on a larger concept." Now what does all that mean?

If verbiage were a snowflake, Robinson would be a blizzard.

R.A. DUVANICH

Mission Viejo
