February 22, 1990, Thursday, P.M. Final

SOUTHLAND;

4 LOTTO TICKETS PURCHASED IN SOUTHLAND SHARE $67 MILLION

There were four winning tickets purchased in Southern California for the $67-million jackpot in the midweek Lotto 6/49 drawing Wednesday night, lottery officials said today.

The tickets, each worth about $16.7 million, were bought in Los Angeles, Long Beach, Simi Valley and Laguna Niguel.

There were 39 tickets with five numbers plus the bonus number, each worth a $231,448 share of a $9.02-million prize pool.
