940210 FT 10 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Kleinwort Overseas net assets up 30% Kleinwort Overseas Investment Trust raised net asset value by 30 per cent from 224.64p to 292.08p in 1993. The company pointed out that this compared with a capital return of 22.6 per cent on the FT-SE-A World Index. Net profits climbed 8.5 per cent from Pounds 2.84m to Pounds 3.08m, equivalent to earnings of 3.84p (3.54p) per share. The recommended final dividend is 1.9p, making a total of 3.4p (3.3p).
