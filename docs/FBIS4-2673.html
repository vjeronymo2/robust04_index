BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, 11 Apr (XINHUA) -- Mongolian Minister of External Relations Tserenpiliyn Gombosuren said today he has achieved the desired results during his visit to China, for the visit was a complete success. Gombosuren made these remarks while meeting reporters upon his return to Ulaanbaatar after ending a week-long formal visit to China. He said that during his visit, he and Vice Premier and Foreign Minister Qian Qichen exchanged views on bilateral relations, especially economic and trade relations between Mongolia and China, and international issues of common interest. He also delightedly told reporters that Chinese Premier Li Peng will pay a formal visit to Mongolia at the end of this month. He said the major achievements of his visit were that he and China have decided the exact time of Premier Li Peng's visit, and have finalized the text of the political treaty on Mongolian-Chinese relations which will be signed during Premier Li Peng's upcoming visit.
