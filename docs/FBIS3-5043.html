Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, March 3 (KCNA) -- Some 2,000 citizens and students reportedly held a rally and demonstration in Seoul on March 1 to commemorate the 75th anniversary of the March 1st uprising of the Korean people against the colonial rule of the Japanese imperialists. The protesters marched in downtown Seoul, shouting "Yankee Go Home", "Get Out Yankees Who Raise the Danger of War on the Peninsula" and "No `Patriots'". The AP news agency said the protesters demanded an end to the heavy U.S. military presence in South Korea and the cancellation of the plans to deploy "Patriot" missiles there. "Students argue that the heavy U.S. military presence here blocks unification of North and South of Korea", it reported.
