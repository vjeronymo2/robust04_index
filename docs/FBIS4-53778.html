BFN [Text] Phnom Penh AKP May 21 -- Khmer Rouge Leader Khieu Samphan has agreed to participate in the peace negotiations with the Royal Government at King Sihanouk's resident of Chhanghsu-on in Pyongyang to be held on May 27. In a message to King Norodom Sihanouk dated on May 19, Khieu Samphan said he was very happy to participate in the round-table talks for peace and national reconciliation scheduled for May 27 in Pyongyang under the chairmanship of his majesty king. Khmer Rouge's message was an response to King Sihanouk's letter to the faction nominal leader asking for a "clear answer" on the proposed round-table to negotiations. The Chhanghsu-on round-table negotiations will be attended by Chea Sim, president fo the Cambodia People's Party and of National Assembly and acting head of state; Co-prime Ministers Prince Norodom Ranariddh and Hun Sen; and Khmer Rouge Leader Khieu Samphan, King Sihanouk will preside over the talks. King Sihanouk has told the delegations to the talks they will have to pay for their accommodations and flight tickets. The North Korean Government would pay for the costs of hosting the talks in Pyongyang, Sihanouk said.
