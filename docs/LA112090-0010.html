November 20, 1990, Tuesday, Orange County Edition

MATER DEI WILL PLAY AT LEBARD STADIUM

Mater Dei High School will play host to top-seeded Eisenhower in the Division I football playoffs at 7:30 Friday night at Orange Coast College's LeBard Stadium.

In Division VI play, Pacifica will play host to top-seeded Tustin at Bolsa Grande High and Rancho Alamitos will play host to Brea-Olinda at Garden Grove High.

Orange Lutheran has moved its Division IX game against Tehachapi from Brea-Olinda High to Santa Ana Stadium. All games begin at 7:30 p.m. Friday.
