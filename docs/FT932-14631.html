930417 FT 17 APR 93 / World Stock Markets: South Africa INVESTORS were nervous as the country braced itself for possible weekend violence. The overall index gained 18 to 3,563 as industrials lost 5 to 4,367. Golds advanced 4 to 1,203. Vaal Reefs fell R2.50 to R221.00 and De Beers was up 50 cents at R76.
