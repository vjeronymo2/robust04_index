BFN [From NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN 15 June Morning Edition] [Text] On the prospect of UN disciplinary measures regarding the DPRK's (North Korea) announcement to secede from the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA), Foreign Minister Koji Kakizawa said at the 14 June House of Councilors' Budget Committee meeting: "I think it will be economic sanctions." He then said: "Some people have an apprehension that the phased and gradual measures, which we proposed, may be shortened. We hope that North Korea will understand the international community's stance and will not launch hasty countermeasures." He made these remarks in a response to a question by Fujio Saito of the Liberal Democratic Party.
