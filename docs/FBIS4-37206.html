BFN [Text] President 'Arafat, president of the state of Palestine and chairman of the PLO Executive Committee, this morning received ambassadors of the European troika under the chairmanship of the Greek charge d'affairs, the ambassador of Belgium, and the ambassador of the European Commission. President 'Arafat and the ambassadors discussed the need for providing necessary means of support by building the infrastructures of the Palestinian national authority in the Palestinian self-rule areas. He urged them to provide the necessary funds to start the rebuilding needed for these infrastructures.
