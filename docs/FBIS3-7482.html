Language: Lao Article Type:BFN [Text] At noon yesterday, His Excellency Datuk Mohamed Zahir Ismail, speaker of the Malaysian House of Representatives, and a high-level delegation arrived in Vientiane for an official friendship visit to Laos. The visit, scheduled for four days from 27 to 30 March, is in response to an invitation from Saman Vi-gnaket, chairman of the National Assembly.
