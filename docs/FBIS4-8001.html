BFN [Report by Marica Lalovic from Pale] [Text] The talks between the military commanders of the Serbian and the Muslim sides -- General Ratko Mladic and [B-H Army Commander General] Rasim Delic -- which were scheduled to take place at Sarajevo airport yesterday, have not started yet. The mediator in the talks, which should result in the signing of a cease-fire agreement, today held talks with General Mladic and conveyed to him the new demands posed by the Muslim side. Namely, General Rose reported that Rasim Delic was making his arrival to the airport and the talks conditional upon the withdrawal of the Serbian army from all the positions around Gorazde to the lines held prior to the outbreak of the latest clashes. General Mladic warned UN Protection Force Commander General Rose not to accept any conditions for talks and said that only an instantaneous halt of all combat activities on all the separating lines in the former Bosnia-Herzegovina can be discussed.
