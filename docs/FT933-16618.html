930702 FT 02 JUL 93 / UK Company News: Silvermines Losses at Silvermines, the Dublin-based electrical and property group, deepened from IPounds 2.59m to IPounds 3.23m (Pounds 3.1m) in the 1992 year. There were exceptional items of IPounds 2.73m (IPounds 2.69m) and extraordinary charges of IPounds 6.48m (IPounds 3.88m). Turnover amounted to IPounds 36.8m (IPounds 41.7m) with continuing operations accounting for IPounds 31.8m. Losses per share came through at 11.01p (9.71p) and there is no final dividend, leaving the total at 1p (2p).
