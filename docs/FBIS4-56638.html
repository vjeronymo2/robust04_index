BFN [Article by Jose Maria Lopez Barajas] [Excerpt] Vienna -- Chile's admission into the International Center for Genetic Engineering and Biotechnology (ICGEB) represents an important diplomatic achievement for the country. Chile was admitted during the meeting in which the ICGEB was created in Vienna this week. Chile will be a member of the ICGEB Governors' Board. The ICGEB is a UN organization, and its main objective is to study and conduct research in agricultural and livestock genetics to improve production. [passage omitted]
