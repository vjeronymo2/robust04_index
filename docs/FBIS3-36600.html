Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Baku, Feb 7 (A.A) -- Azeri Defense Minister Mehmet Refigi [name and title as received] said Azerbaijan was resolved to rescue its territory from Armenian occupation, it was reported on Monday [7 February]. Refigi told AA reporter that Armenian troops which can not stand against Azeri forces were in panic. Speaking on the recent situation in Azerbaijan Refigi said everything has changed after President Geydar Aliyev has come to power, adding that thirty percent of our territory are under Armenian occupation and more than one million people living here became refugees. Refigi said Armenian attacks were stopped [words indistict] army has been set up. He said administrators, soldiers and the public have a 'belief' in unity, adding that they will reach victory with this unity. Refigi said Armenian soldiers devastated Azeri cities, townships and villages and they set buildings into fire while they fled. He said there would not be any ceasefire unless Armenia withdraws from Azeri territory. Refigi said Turkey has great contributions to Azerbaijan adding that they were thankful for this support.
