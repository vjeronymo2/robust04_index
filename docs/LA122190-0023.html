December 21, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: MOVIES

Hollywood Memorabilia Sells: The Jewish marriage certificate of Marilyn Monroe and Arthur Miller was sold Thursday in London for $13,700, Christie's auctioneers said. The ketubah, a framed marriage certificate with a decorated border in the style of a medieval manuscript, was sold by the executors of Monroe's estate and bought by an unidentified American. Indiana Jones' kangaroo hide bullwhip sold for $24,300 -- more than double its estimate -- to the owner of Paris' City Rock Cafe. CLAUDIA PUIG
