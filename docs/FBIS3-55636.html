Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] The situation on the Tajik-Afghan border is not calm. According to officers of the Russian operational group in Dushanbe, over the past few days the Tajik opposition has continued its armed provocations from the territory of Afghanistan. The 12th and 13th outposts of the Russian border troops and the positions of the servicemen of the Russian 201st Motorized Rifle Division are being subjected to sporadic firing. Fortunately there are no casualties among the servicemen of the Russian Federation. According to the Russian military, the new spiral of tension on the border has been caused by the exacerbation of the military and political situation in Afghanistan, where clashes between various conflicting groups are taking place.
