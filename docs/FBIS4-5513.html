BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 9 (KCNA) -- Celebration functions were held in different countries on the threshold of the birthday of the great leader President Kim Il-song. A joint seminar took place in Harare, Zimbabwe. In his report at the seminar N.T. Mawema, director of a department of the C.C. [Central Committee], the Zimbabwe African National Union-Patriotic Front and chairman of the Zimbabwean National Committee for the Study of the Chuche Idea, said: President Kim Il-song is recognized worldwide as a genuine leader of the people. History has registered many statesmen but such a great leader as President Kim Il-song cannot be found in it. The chuche idea, the great guiding idea of the era of independence, has a strong attraction in leading the struggle of the world people to victory. This shows the exploits of the president as a rare thinker and theoretician. Since President Kim Il-song successfully solved the problem of the succession of leadership for carrying forward the cause of socialism to accomplishment, the centre of the leadership of the cause of global independence and the cause of socialism has been established, firmly guaranteeing its victory. A celebration meeting, a lecture meeting and an opening ceremony of a month of DPRK photo exhibition were held in Lusaka, Zambia, by the housing construction company of the Kim Il-song Institute of Agricultural Science in Guinea and by the Coordinating Council of the Federation of Peace and Friendship Organizations of Mongolia.
