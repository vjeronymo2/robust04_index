BFN [Text] Warsaw, May 10 -- Prime Minister Waldemar Pawlak on Monday received a delegation of the general staff of the Chinese People's Liberation Army led by Chief of the General Staff, General Zhang Wannian. The sides discussed the possibilities of cooperation between the Polish and the Chinese armed forces and prospects for cooperation of the arms industries of the two countries. Earlier in the day the Chinese Chief of the General Staff Zhang Wannian held talks with the Chief of the General Staff of the Polish Armed Forces, General Tadeusz Wilecki. The sides declared their will to develop good cooperation between the armed forces of Poland and China. The Chinese delegation arrived for a three-day official visit to Poland.
