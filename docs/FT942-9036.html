940518 FT 18 MAY 94 / World Trade News: ABB in Dollars 100m German deal Asea Brown Boveri, the Swiss-Swedish heavy engineering group, said it received an order valued at around Dollars 100m (Pounds 66m) to supply and install components for a residual waste incineration plant at Niehl, near Cologne in Germany, AP-DJ reports from Zurich. The order was placed by LC Steinmueller of Gummersbach, the general contractors who are responsible for the turn-key delivery of the plant, the company said. ABB said the plant, which is scheduled to start operating in 1997, would be run by AVG, a waste exploitation and disposal company based in Cologne.
