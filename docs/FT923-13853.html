920710 FT 10 JUL 92 / UK Company News: Microgen Microgen Holdings, the Windsor-based information services group, reported a relatively static performance in its seasonally-favourable first half. On turnover of Pounds 25m (Pounds 24.6m), pre-tax profits for the six months to April 30 amounted to Pounds 4.37m (Pounds 4.33m). Earnings per share held steady at 7.2p and the interim dividend is maintained at 2.2p.
