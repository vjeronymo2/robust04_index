May 10, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: PEOPLE WATCH

Carlin Heads LSD Benefit: Comedian George Carlin will headline a benefit to help promote scientific LSD research.

The May 23 event at Hollywood Live will help fund a permanent site for the Albert Hoffman Foundation, the world's largest archives of LSD research. Hoffman discovered the psychedelic drug in 1938. Joining Carlin will be LSD authority Timothy Leary. GREG BRAXTON
