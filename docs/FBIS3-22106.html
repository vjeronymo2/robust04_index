Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [Text] Last night, on a section of the Iskashimskiy "Pamir" frontier detachment, Russian border guards thwarted an attempt by smugglers to take narcotics from Afghanistan to Tajikistan. The Tajik KHOVAR agency learned from military sources that there was an armed clash between the border guards and the infiltrators. There were no casualties among the servicemen. Two smugglers were detained. Of late, this is not the first instance of contraband deals between Afghans and locals. Recently, Russian border guards detained about 20 smugglers who tried to take a consignment from Afghan to Tajik territory.
