921201 FT 01 DEC 92 / World News in Brief: Sunnie Mann dies Sunnie Mann, wife of former hostage Jackie Mann, died in a Cyprus hospital aged 78 after struggling against lung cancer. Her husband, held captive in Lebanon for more than two years, was with her shortly before she died. Prime minister John Major said: 'Sunnie's courage and fighting spirit throughout Jackie's imprisonment made her a national figure.'
