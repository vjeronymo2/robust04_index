BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 18 (KCNA) -- The inspector-general of the Armed Forces of Libya, Mustafa al Haroubi, who is a special envoy of the leader of the great September First revolution of the Great Socialist People's Libyan Arab Jamahiriya, and his entourage arrived here Sunday. They were met at the airport by Choe Kwang, chief of the General Staff of the Korean People's Army, Chang Song-kil, vice-minister of Foreign Affairs, officials concerned and Ramadan Muhammad El Laubi, acting secretary of the People's Committee of the Libyan People's Bureau in Korea. A ceremony for welcoming the special envoy and his entourage took place at the airport. In the evening, the government of the Democratic People's Republic of Korea gave a party for the special envoy and his entourage at the Mansudae Assembly Hall.
