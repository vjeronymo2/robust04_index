February 11, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

TROJANS FACE IRISH AT SOUTH BEND

George Raveling, USC basketball coach, will change the Trojan lineup for today's game against Notre Dame at South Bend, Ind.

Calvin Banks is expected to start at forward in place of Chris Moore, who missed 7 of 10 shots in USC's 68-65 loss to UCLA Wednesday night.

The Trojans, who have lost 11 straight games, are 7-15. Notre Dame, coming off a 77-58 victory over Fordham Wednesday night at New York, is 12-5. CHRIS BAKER
