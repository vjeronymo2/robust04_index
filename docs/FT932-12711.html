930427 FT 27 APR 93 / UK Company News: Sharp rise at Govett Oriental At March 31 1993, net asset value of Govett Oriental Investment Trust had surged to 262.17p, from 186.3p six months earlier. On March 31 1992 it had stood at 189.9p. Earnings per share came to 1.41p (0.91p) and the final dividend is 0.4p for a total of 0.95p (0.9p).
