921010 FT 10 OCT 92 / McSharry says EC ready for deal but not at any price Mr Ray McSharry, the EC agriculture commissioner, said in Dublin yesterday the EC was 'ready to strike a deal but not at any price' in its talks on the Uruguay Round, Tim Coone writes from Dublin. 'It is absolutely impossible to agree to the (US) request that community production of oilseeds in the community should come down from the present levels of about 13m tonnes to 7m tonnes,' he told a US Chamber of Commerce luncheon in Ireland.
