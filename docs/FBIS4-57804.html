BFN [Text] One day after the Sole Workers Center [CUT] decided in its national congress that it can neither support nor aid the Workers Party [PT], PT presidential candidate Luis Inacio Lula da Silva campaigned on 23 May outside factories in the Sao Paulo ABC region from a loudspeaker-equipped van owned by the CUT-affiliated metalworkers union. On 23 May, Lula, who said earlier he is against CUT support, stated it is up to the workers to decide who should use the van. In the opinion of electoral specialist Antonio Carlos Mendes, Lula violated electoral laws.
