931021 FT 21 OCT 93 / Valleys win Pounds 350m projects THE FIRST projects for a Pounds 350m a year programme to regenerate the south Wales valleys were announced yesterday by Mr John Redwood, Welsh secretary. It is the second five-year programme for the valleys. Expenditure on the first programme totalled Pounds 773m. Mr Redwood said it had drawn in an additional Pounds 700m of private sector investment and shown what could be done for communities when public and private finance worked together.
