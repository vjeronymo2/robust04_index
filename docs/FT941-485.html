940330 FT 30 MAR 94 / BBC Arabic news service is planned More than 200 broadcasting jobs will be created in London following yesterday's announcement that the BBC has signed a 10-year agreement to supply programmes for a new 24-hour Arabic-language television channel. The satellite channel, aimed at the Middle East and north Africa, will be launched from BBC Television Centre in west London in the next few months. The service, from BBC World Service Television, is being financed by the Mawarid investment and industrial group of Saudi Arabia.
