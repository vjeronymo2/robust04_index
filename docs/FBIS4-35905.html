BFN [Text] A Qiryat Arba' resident sustained medium to serious wounds from stones were hurled at him at the Glass junction near Hebron. He was taken to Hadassah-'En Kerem Hospital and is undergoing an operation. Our correspondent Yoram Kohen reports that angry Qiryat Arba' inhabitants responded by stoning Arabs vehicles traveling along the Jerusalem-Hebron road. Nobody was hurt, but several cars were damaged.
