BFN [Unattributed report: "Turks Without Representative in Constitutional Court"] [Text] Referring to the fact that no judge in the Macedonian Constitutional Court is of Turkish origin, the Democratic Party of Turks in Macedonian [FYROM -- Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia] issued a public protest and condemn of the politics of double standards. Although the Turks present a constitutive and constructive element of Macedonia's statehood, this act is a repeated attempt to leave this minority at the margins of the political life in the country, the party announcement says. Such behavior results in distrust for the government bodies and institutions. The announcement concludes this kind of democracy leads nowhere.
