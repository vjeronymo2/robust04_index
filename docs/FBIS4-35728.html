BFN [Excerpt] Cairo, 2 Jun (MENA) -- Ecuadoran Minister of Foreign Affairs Diego Paredes has stated that the Latin American group has chosen Colombia to host the next Nonaligned Movement summit. In a statement to reporters after his meeting with Foreign Minister 'Amr Musa, he said that Columbia is one of the most important countries in Latin America, noting that holding the next summit in Bogota reflects the Latin American group's interest in the Nonaligned Movement.
