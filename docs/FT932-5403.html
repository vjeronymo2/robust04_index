930603 FT 03 JUN 93 / Observer: Sales pitch Carpet king Sir Phil Harris, aiming to resume his throne on the stock market next week, is understandably boasting how he likes to offer his Carpetright customers value. His sales brochures are singing the charms of his own brand carpet Vivaldi - Pounds 7.99 a sq yd in the current sale - by comparison with Kosset's Mozart brand on offer at Pounds 8.99. But never mind the carpet. What would the two composers think of such a measure of their relative merits?
