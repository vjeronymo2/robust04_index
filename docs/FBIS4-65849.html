BFN [Announcer-read report] [Text] Fifteen terrorists belonging to the PKK [Workers Party of Kurdistan] terrorist organization have been killed in clashes with the security forces in Agri's Tendurek Mountains. According to information obtained by a Turkish Radio and Television correspondent, many weapons and large amounts of ammunition have been seized in the area. The operation continues. Four persons were killed when a private car hit a mine laid by terrorists near the Topluca village of Mardin's Savur District.
