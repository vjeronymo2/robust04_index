BFN [Text] Commenting on the continuous provocative statements by Turkish officials, Government Spokesman Evangelos Venizelos stressed that the Turkish Government ought to know that for the Greeks of Istanbul, international law and the Lausanne Treaty remain valid. Regarding the Ecumenical Patriarchate, the spokesman added, tradition and international law remain in effect. The Greek Government deals with these issues calmly and does not fall for the artificial tension that the Turkish side cultivates. The newly elected mayor of the Istanbul region of (Fatih), (Mehmet Ali Sakhilu), recently stated that he would enter the Patriarchate from the gate closed in 1821 following the hanging of Patriarch Grigorios V.
