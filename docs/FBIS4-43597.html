BFN [Text] Defense Minister Konstandinos Iliadhis said that he will discuss the possibility of future arms purchases for the National Guard during his visit to France. However, he made it clear that other arms markets are also being assessed. The defense minister left at noon today for France where, at the invitation of the French Government, he will visit a weapons systems exhibition. He will also meet with Francois Leotard, the French minister of state for defense, and representatives of companies that manufacture weapons systems to discuss questions of technical support to the National Guard. Responding to a question asked before his departure, Iliadhis said that the Defense Ministry police force will have direct contacts with the companies and added that the French Defense Ministry can help in creating good relations with them.
