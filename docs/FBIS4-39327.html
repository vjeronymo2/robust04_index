BFN [Text] The Seimas [word indistinct] has presented the government with a statement of no confidence against the cabinet led by Prime Minister Adolfas Slezevicius. The statement was initiated earlier by the Social Democrats. After receiving an official reply, required within 14 days by the government, the parliament has five more days to open Seimas discussions. More than 50 percent of the parliamentary votes are needed to pass a vote of no confidence against the cabinet.
