May 9, 1990, Wednesday, Ventura County Edition

VENTURA COUNTY NEWS ROUNDUP: MOORPARK;

DISTRICT RATIFIES TEACHERS' CONTRACT

The Moorpark Unified School District on Tuesday ratified a two-year teachers' contract after nearly a year of negotiations.

Under terms of the contract, the district's 200 teachers will receive a 5% pay raise for the current school year, retroactive to September, plus a bonus of 2.3% of their salaries.

Next year, teachers will receive a cost-of-living salary increase and a bonus of 1.75%.

In addition, $225,000 will be available to the seven schools in the district to "address the teacher-pupil ratio situation," Supt. Tom Duffy said.

The teacher-student ratio is 30 to 1, said Yvonne Davis, the district's personnel director. The funds will be used to hire more teachers to meet the growth in enrollment, Duffy said. RUSS CHARVONIA
