November 30, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: SANTA MONICA;

BREAKFAST WITH SANTA CLAUS

The city Recreation Division will hold two separate breakfast sessions with Santa Claus on Dec. 16 at Memorial Park, 14th Street and Olympic Boulevard.

The $2 per person pancake breakfasts will be held from 8 to 10 a.m. and from 10:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. Children under 8 must be accompanied by an adult.

Tickets must be purchased in advance at the city recreation office at Clover Park, 2600 Ocean Park Blvd. Only 150 tickets will be sold for each session.
