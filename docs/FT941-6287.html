940302 FT 02 MAR 94 / UK Company News: Share placing values Cedardata at Pounds 32.2m Shares in Cedardata, the Surrey-based accounting and financial management software group, which is coming to market through a share placing, have been priced at 105p valuing the group at Pounds 32.2m. The company is best known for its modular Cfacs (Commercial and Financial Accounts Systems) accounting software, which was launched in 1987 and now has a strong blue-chip customer base. Since the launch of Cfacs, turnover and profits have grown rapidly. Pre-tax profits before non-recurring items, have risen from Pounds 430,000 in 1991 to a forecast Pounds 2.65m for the year to the end of March. Earnings of 6.1p per share are forecast, up from 4.2p last year. As part of the placing of 13.7m shares, representing 44.75 per cent of the enlarged ordinary share capital, the group is issuing 1.43m new shares to raise funds for the development of Cfacs. The issue is sponsored by Greig Middleton and the shares are due to begin trading on March 7.
