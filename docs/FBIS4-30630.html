BFN [Text] Australian Prime Minister Paul Keating flew into Jakarta yesterday afternoon for a three-day working visit to Indonesia. Foreign Minister ad interim Susilo Sudarman was on hand at the Halim Perdanakusumah International Airport to welcome Australian Prime Minister Paul Keating and his 26-member delegation. According to the schedule, Paul Keating will make a courtesy call on President Suharto at Merdeka Palace today and among other things, will discuss the preparations for the upcoming APEC summit to be held in November 1994. The Australian prime minister will also officially open the Australian trade exhibition, Australia Today 1994, tomorrow in Jakarta.
