Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Shots were fired at a police car this morning near the Kisufim checkpoint at the entrance to the Gaza Strip. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan reports that one of the policemen was slightly wounded by glass splinters and received medical treatment on the spot. Our correspondent reports that a police car on the way to the Kisufim checkpoint spotted a suspicious-looking car driving out of an orchard. When the policemen ordered the driver to stop, he opened fire at them from an automatic weapon. The policemen fired back, shattering the car's back window, but it sped off toward Khan Yunus. Army and police forces are now searching the area.
