BFN [Text] Moscow June 9 ITAR-TASS -- Russian President Boris Yeltsin and President of North Ossetia Akhsarbek Galazov met in the Kremlin today to discuss the situation in this North Caucasian republic within the Russian Federation. The two presidents discussed in general features the possibility of signing a treaty between Russia and North Ossetia ahead of the 220th anniversary of Ossetia's joining Russia. President Boris Yeltsin drew the attention of North Ossetia's leader to the need to implement unswervingly the Russian president's decrees concerning stabilisation of the situation in the North Caucasian republic. President Galazov informed Boris Yeltsin about the process of disbanding unlawful armed formations in the republic.
