BFN [Text] Deputy Foreign Minister Yosi Beilin, who will be departing for Oman this weekend, says that this visit is a big leap forward as far as the Arab boycott is concerned. According to him, Oman is hosting the multilateral talks on water affairs because it wants to solve its water problems, and Israel can offer it know-how and joint research projects. Beilin was interviewed by the Arabic- language weekly PANORAMA, and his remarks were reported by our correspondent Qaysar Kabaha. [Jerusalem Qol Yisra'el in Hebrew at 1505 GMT on 11 April reports that "the four-members Israeli preparatory team returned from Oman this afternoon."]
