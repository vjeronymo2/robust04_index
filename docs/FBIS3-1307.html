Language: Swahili Article Type:BFN [From the press review] [Text] On Burundi, the DAILY NEWS gives prominence to a report in which the Burundi Government has appealed to France to intervene and bring to an end the military campaign to kill loyal Burundi citizens. The appeal was made by Leonard Nyangoma, the minister of internal affairs and security, who spoke to the DAILY NEWS by telephone from his hiding place. The minister, who also urged Tanzania to assist in restoring peace in Burundi, said the killings were continuing and that the Army had moved to the villages. He did not give the number of people who had been killed.
