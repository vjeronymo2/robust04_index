Language: Italian Article Type:BFN [Text] The bandits who kidnapped the two Italian aid workers have made a ransom demand. The terms of the demand have not yet been released. The news was communicated by Marco Benassi, director of the European Agricultural Training Consortium [CEFA], who received the news in a telex sent from CEFA's office in Mogadishu. Benassi said that the kidnapping of Gianfranco Stefani and Sergio Passadore was "a new way of extorting money."
