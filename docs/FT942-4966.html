940607 FT 07 JUN 94 / UK Company News: UK side helps Acal to Pounds 3.65m Pre-tax profits of Acal, the agent for international manufacturers of electronics and industrial controls, expanded from Pounds 3.15m to Pounds 3.65m for the year ended March 31, on turnover of Pounds 78.5m, against Pounds 68.9m. Earnings per share were up at 16p (14.6p) while a final distribution of 4.5p (4.2p) lifts the total dividend to 6.75p (6.3p). The directors said that the rise in profits was primarily because of the improvement in the UK where underlying sales grew more than 25 per cent with profits being doubled.
