920618 FT 18 JUN 92 / Boost seen for polytechnics THE NUMBER of students admitted to degree courses in polytechnics in England and Wales rose by nearly a quarter last year alone, making an increase of more than a third in the past two years, according to official figures published yesterday. The Polytechnics Central Admissions System annual report shows an increase of 22.3 per cent in the number admitted for degree courses, with an 18.3 per cent increase in numbers starting on Higher National Diploma courses.
