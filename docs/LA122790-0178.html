December 27, 1990, Thursday, Orange County Edition

ORANGE COUNTY 1991 RESOLUTIONS;

MSGR. JAIME SOTO

I resolve to celebrate Mass and hear confessions at the central jail once a month during 1991.

I resolve to make a difference in the effort against gangs and drugs in the county. I resolve to help Hispanic families meet the challenges of sharing their faith and culture with their children. I resolve to continue working with voluntary and public leadership to build an Orange County that cares for the least among us.
