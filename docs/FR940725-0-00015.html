(h) If no cracking is detected during any inspection required by paragraphs (g), (h), and (k) of this AD, at any location where a countersunk fastener was found, repeat the inspection thereafter at intervals not to exceed 4,000 landings, in accordance with the procedures described in the Boeing Service Bulletin 747&hyph;53A2312, Revision 2, dated October 8, 1992.
