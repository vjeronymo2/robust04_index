Language: Chinese Article Type:BFN [Text] Hanoi, 26 Feb (XINHUA) -- The Sino-Vietnamese land border joint working group signed a summary of minutes on their first round of talks here today. During the talks, both sides reached an agreement on the contents and procedures of the working group's tasks in a friendly, sincere [tan cheng 0982 6134], and realistic [qiu shi 3061 1395] atmosphere, and decided to hold the second round of talks in Beijing. Vietnamese Vice Foreign Minister (Vo Kuan) met with all members of the Chinese working team and conducted cordial, friendly conversations with them. Chinese Ambassador to Vietnam Zhang Qing participated in the meeting.
