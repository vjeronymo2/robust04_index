BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Nikolay Goncharov] [Text] Orenburg, 30 Apr -- Russian Prime Minister Viktor Chernomyrdin, who is currently in Orenburg oblast, has said that the signing of the Treaty on Social Accord will not be followed by changes in the government economic and cadre policy, although some amendments will be made. He will attend festivities on the occasion of the 250th anniversary of Orenburg Province.
