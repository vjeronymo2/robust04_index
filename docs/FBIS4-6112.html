BFN [Text] Ulaanbaatar, April 15 (OANA-MONTSAME) -- Yesterday the Mongolian Social Democratic Party (MSDP) has officially announced their backing of the actions of political struggle being carried out by the Mongolian Democratic Association (MDA) and the Union of Mongolian Students (UMS) for resignation of the government. The political hunger strike by the members and supporters of the MDA and UMS held at the central square of the Mongolian capital is joined by the deputy chairman of the MSDP with one of his party member. While the MSDP is joining the political struggle the stand of another parliamentary opposition party the Mongolian National Democratic Party (MNDP) was expressed by one of the leadership of the party through the national television saying that the MNDP regards the actions by the MDA and the UMS as a struggle for justice.
