920501 FT 01 MAY 92 / UK Company News: Wellcome sanction WELLCOME Trust yesterday won the sanction of the High Court to reduce its stake in Wellcome, the pharmaceutical company, from 73.6 per cent to 25 per cent. The Trust, a medical charity, plans to sell much of its holding through a global sale this summer, probably in July. The Trust was established under Sir Henry Wellcome's will and needed court approval to cut its stake below 50 per cent. The court said the trust could sell up to 417m shares.
