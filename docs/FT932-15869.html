930408 FT 08 APR 93 / UK Company News: AAH AAH Holdings, the distribution group, has acquired Baylis Holdings for an initial Pounds 4.25m, of which Pounds 2.86m has been satisfied by the issue to the vendors of 544,667 shares, with the balance in cash. A further cash sum, not exceeding Pounds 2.2m is profit-related. Baylis is a specialist in 'shared user' and contract distribution services with customers in the food and consumable household products sectors.
