940212 FT 12 FEB 94 / UK Company News: General Cons beats forecast pay-out The General Consolidated Investment Trust announced a final dividend of 1.81p per income share, bringing the total for 1993 to 8.71p. The figure, higher than the trust's earlier forecast of 8.45p, reflected an 'improved dividend environment' according to Sir Mark Thompson, chairman. The total, however, still falls short of last year's 9.37p. Net asset value per capital share was 290p at the year-end, a rise of 59 per cent on the figure at end-December 1992. Net revenue was Pounds 4.68m (Pounds 4.52m) for earnings of 9.48p (9.56p) per income share.
