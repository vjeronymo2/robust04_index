April 13, 1990, Friday, Home Edition

BRIEFLY

American Medical Merger Completed: A private investor group, which acquired American Medical International last October, said it completed a merger of the Beverly Hills-based hospital management concern with its holding company. AMI was purchased for $1.67 billion by the investor group jointly owned by First Boston Corp., current AMI Chairman Harry J. Gray and Mel Klein & Partners. AMI became a wholly owned subsidiary of American Medical Holdings Inc.
