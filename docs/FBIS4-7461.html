BFN [Text] Jan Kuriata, deputy minister of national defense for armament and military infrastructure, and Robin Beard, NATO assistant secretary general for the Division of Defense Support, have signed a program of practical cooperation between the Ministry of National Defense and NATO for this year. The program includes proposals for meetings of groups of experts on the planning of arms supply, armaments, and military equipment, and also on organization and management of research and development work. Beard's visit to Poland will last two days.
