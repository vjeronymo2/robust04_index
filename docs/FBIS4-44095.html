BFN [Excerpts] Six European countries -- Belgium, the Netherlands, Portugual, Spain, Germany, and the United Kingdom -- today expressed their readiness to offer logistical or humanitarian support for Operation Turquoise. For its part, Italy is ready to send soldiers, but only under certain conditions. More details from Arlette Chabot, our special envoy to the European summit in Corfu: [Chabot] [passage omitted] Italy alone is ready to send soldiers, as many as 5,000, if Mr. Berlusconi can persuade his cabinet.
