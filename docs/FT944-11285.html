941107 FT 07 NOV 94 / UK Company News: London United in Pounds 23m buy-out London United, the west London bus company, has been bought by its staff in a Pounds 22.9m deal, backed by Montagu Private Equity and 3i, the investment capital group. It becomes the ninth London bus company to be sold by London Regional Transport this year and almost completes LRT's privatisation programme which so far has raised more than Pounds 211m. London United, operates 450 buses in south-west London, as well as the Heathrow to central London Airbus. It employs 1,450 staff and had Pounds 36.6m turnover in the year to March 1994.
