BFN [Text] Tripoli, al Maa' [May] 24, JAMAHIRIYAH NEWS AGENCY -- Successive phone calls were underway throughout the 'Aid al Adhah ['Id al-Adha] holiday between the leader of the revolution and the Yemeni leaders 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih and 'Ali Salim al-Bid in the ongoing efforts by the leader to bring an end to the suffering and destruction in Yemen. Over the last 24 hours telephone calls were exchanged between the various parties with the leader emphasizing the stance adopted by the Arab world and its revolutionary forces vis a vis division and those who support division. The leader said that it did not suit the revolutionaries of Aden to be in favour of division. However, 'Ali Salim al-Bid explained to the leader of the revolution the reasons for his decision to break away but also expressed his willingness to search for any way out of the current sitution to restore the unity of Yemen even if at his expense. The contacts made with 'Ali 'Abdallah Salih revolved mainly around a search for a political solution which would guarantee Yemeni unity, which would bring an end to the fighting and prevent further bloodshed.
