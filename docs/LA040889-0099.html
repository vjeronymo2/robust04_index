April 8, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

OPERATIC RICHES ON THE PORCH

I must express my regrets over music critic Martin Bernheimer's recent forays in New York. I'm an opera fan who does not think it is the job of The Times to tell me what's going on in the Big Apple. (The fact that I couldn't care less what Marty thinks also has something to do with it.)

With San Francisco and San Diego running full tilt, there is enough opera in California without having to send out Mr. B. as our good-will ambassador. (Now it all becomes clear why Angelenos have such a bad rep in N.Y.)

I realize "everything is local news," but opera reviews don't travel well. Let's focus on the riches on the back porch, shall we?

PATRICK J. MACK

Santa Monica
