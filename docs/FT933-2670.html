930917 FT 17 SEP 93 / Observer: Card carrying Is your business card collection a monstrous heap spilling on to the floor? Don't worry, it's just the recession. Demand for business cards peaks during a slump, according to a study by Rolodex, the US card company. The theory is that sales staff have to work harder (and give out more cards than usual) and redundant executives are busily 'networking' (and giving out more cards). Life must be very confusing for the sales rep selling business cards.
