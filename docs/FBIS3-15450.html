Language: English Article Type:BFN ["Karabakh's Position Has Found Understanding in the CSCE"-- SNARK headline] [Text] Stepanakert, March 5 (SNARK) -- Masis Mailyan, the chief of the Department of International Organizations of the Foreign Ministry of Karabakh, appreciated the results of the negotiations, that finished in Stepanakert on March 4, as useful. The negotiations were held between the delegation of CSCE Minsk conference, headed by its chairman Yan Eliasson, and the authorities of Karabakh. Particularly, Mailyan told SNARK during the negotiations the Karabakh party turned the attention of the CSCE's delegation to the expediency of the coordination of mediatory initiatives of Russia and CSCE on the peaceful settlement of the conflict. According to Mailyan the official Stepanakert's point of view has found the understanding among the representatives of the CSCE.
