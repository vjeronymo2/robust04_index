Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Josko Krizelj report from Livno] [Excerpt] I have decided to place my mandate as president of the Croat republic of Herceg-Bosna at the disposal of this assembly until further notice. A presidential council will assume the duties of president and will appoint an acting president. Mate Boban said this at the end of his introductory speech at an extraordinary session of the Chamber of Deputies of Herceg-Bosna's Assembly. I will stay with you, Boban said, ending his speech.
