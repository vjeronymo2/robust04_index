Language: English Article Type:BFN [Statement issued by the IEC on 29 March on the SAPA PR Wire Service] [Text] The deadline for the accreditation of international and local organisations for the purpose of observing the elections, expired on Sunday night, March 27. Late applications will still be considered if strongly motivated. The monitoring directorate have accredited a total of 60 international organisations and 22 local organisations. This does not include the inter-governmental organisations, like the United Nations, the OAU and the European Community.
