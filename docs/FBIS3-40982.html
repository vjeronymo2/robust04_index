Article Type:BFN [Editorial report] Tokyo NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN in Japanese 31 January morning edition received via Nikkei Telecom Database, carries a 200-character report noting THE SUNDAY TIMES article on Japan's possession of nuclear weapons cited in second ref. The text of the NIHON KEIZAI SHIMBUN report, filed by London correspondent Yuzo Waki, is as follows: "The 30 January edition of THE SUNDAY TIMES, an influential British Sunday paper, frontpages a report saying `Japan is going nuclear under the arms expansion race in Asia.' The report says: 1) The British Defense Ministry conveyed to the British Prime Minister's Office its concern over `Japan being forced to abandon its non-nuclear stance'; 2) a document submitted in January to the joint Intelligence Committee, the British Government's main security body, notes Japan has all the equipment and technology necessary for an early deployment of nuclear weapons; and 3) an expert who read the document said `Japan could have already acquired its own nuclear bombs, and all they would need to do is to select adequate amounts of plutonium.'"
