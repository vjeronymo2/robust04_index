March 15, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

GLENDALE PUBLIC SERVICE CHIEF TO RETIRE

W.E. (Ed) Cameron, Glendale's director of public service, will retire in April after 20 years as a city employee, Glendale officials said.

The public service department provides water and power to the city's residents. Before assuming the post in 1984, Cameron was Glendale's director of public works.

Earlier, he worked as city engineer of Des Moines, Iowa; city engineer of Bellingham, Wash.; and public works director of Sanger, Calif.
