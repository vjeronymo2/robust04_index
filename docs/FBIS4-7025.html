BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 25 (KCNA) -- A signature campaign is going on in different countries and through international organizations to support "10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation for the reunification of the country" authored by the great leader President Kim Il-song. In India 106,228,100 people had taken part in the signature campaign as of April 13. Members of the Parliament from the National Congress Party (I), the People's Party and the Communist Party, leaders of some 30 organizations including the all-India Trade Union Congress and the All-India Students Federation on behalf of their organizations and many other public figures singed the signature paper. Secretary General of the Pan-African Youth Movement Issebere Hamadoun Ibrahim signed the paper in the name of the 220 million African young people. In Burundi 1,560,000 people of different strata including the minister of institutional reforms and relations of the National Assembly, who is the president of the Liberal Party, and the secretary general of the parliament signed the signature paper from April 1 to 14. In Algeria, as of April 10, the signature paper for support to peace of the Korean peninsula and the Korean people's independent and peaceful reunification had been signed by chairman of the Algeria-Korea Friendship Club Benhamla Abdelhamid and General Secretary of the Movement for Democracy in Algeria Khaled Bensmain on behalf of their organizations, General Secretary of the National Youth Union Boukerzaza Abderachid in the name of 400,000 youths and Chairman of the Students' Union Belaid Abdel Aziz on behalf of 100,000 students. In Tunisia General Secretary of the Tunisian Committee for Solidarity with the Korean Workers Ajmi Ben Khedjza signed the paper in the name of his organization and Professor of the Tunis University Messaoud Chahtour on behalf of teachers of the university on April 11.
