August 23, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH SURELOCK;

THE SILICON SLEUTH

SURELOCK's winning streak came to a screeching halt when he lost all his bets for a total of $160.

SURELOCK's will try to recoup today with a $10 exacta box, Cee's Song-Fille D'Argent-Justoneofthegirls, in the eighth race.

SURELOCK's Wed. Bankroll $2,484

Wednesday's Loss $160

Current Bankroll $2,324

SURELOCK's loss was Janice Goldinger's gain as she turned in a $166 profit when Swifterthanthewind won the fourth race and returned $10.80.

For today, Janice will wager $40 to win on Vault and a $5 exacta box on Vault and Stanford Luck in the sixth race.

Janice's Wed. Bankroll $1,479.75

Wednesday's Profit $166

Current Bankroll $1,645.75

Note: Janice Goldinger, a horse owner, does not bet on races her horses run in. SURELOCK does not own horses.
