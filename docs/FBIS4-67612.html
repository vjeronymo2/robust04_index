BFN [Editorial Report] The following is a compilation of reports on epidemics and diseases monitored through 5 May.

Brazil
Cholera -- The Health Secretariat on 3 May reported that two new cholera cases have been detected, in Rio de Janeiro and Niteroi. A total of 70 cases and six fatalities have been registered in Rio de Janeiro State so far this year. (Rio de Janeiro JORNAL DO BRASIL in Portuguese 4 May 94 p 12) AIDS -- One of every five inmates in the prisons of Sao Paulo is infected with AIDS. Three new AIDS cases are being registered each day and two fatalities every three days among the 52,000 people lodged in the jails. (Rio de Janeiro JORNAL DO BRASIL in Portuguese 4 May 94 p 7)
