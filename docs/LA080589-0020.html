August 5, 1989, Saturday, Valley Edition

AGUA DULCE WOMAN ATTACKED BY BULL

An Agua Dulce woman was gored in the chest and stomped by a bull Friday morning, leaving her with broken ribs and a bruised lung, the Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department reported.

The attack occurred while Marge Seager, 40, was feeding a Holstein bull that she keeps on the grounds of her residence in the 35400 block of Anthony Road, Sgt. Mike Handley said.

The bull apparently became enraged and charged at Seager, he said. It then ran off to another part of the compound, Handley said.

The woman was in serious condition Friday night in the intensive care unit at Antelope Valley Hospital Medical Center, he said.
