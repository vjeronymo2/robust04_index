BFN [By reporter Lin Zhibai (2651 0037 4101)] [Text] Wuhan, 28 Apr (XINHUA) -- With the approval of the Civil Aviation Administration of China, starting from today, Wuhan Airlines will inaugurate a new Wuhan-Hangzhou-Wuhan air route. The airline uses Yun-7 100 Luxury planes to make the round-trip flight on the route on Tuesdays, Thursdays, and Saturdays. This is Wuhan Airlines' 17th air route.
