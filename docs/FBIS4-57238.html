BFN [From the "Evening Information Review" newscast] [Text] Qian Qichen, deputy prime minister and foreign minister of China, and Cuban Government Minister Jose Naranjo expressed their best wishes in Beijing for an expansion of the bilateral and friendly relations of cooperation between the two countries. The Cuban minister hailed the success of the reform and opening process under way in China and the great progress achieved in local economic construction. Jose Naranjo is on his third visit to that Asian country at the invitation of its Foreign Ministry.
