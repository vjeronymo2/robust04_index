940113 FT 13 JAN 94 / UK Company News: Sale of cash and carry side boosts M&W M&W, the Southampton-based convenience store company, reported pre-tax profits ahead by 37 per cent at Pounds 2.77m for the 53 weeks to October 31, against Pounds 2.03m. The result was boosted by exceptional income of Pounds 465,000, mainly resulting from the sale of its cash and carry activities in July, compared with Pounds 35,000 last time. Turnover was Pounds 79.6m (Pounds 72.5m) of which Pounds 8.09m (Pounds 10.4m) related to discontinued activities. Continuing activities showed an increase of 15 per cent. During the period 14 stores were opened or acquired and one sold taking the total to 111. Sales on a like-for-like basis rose 4.7 per cent. Earnings per share came out at 12.52p (8.17p) and the proposed final dividend is 1.9p for a total of 3p (2.5p).
