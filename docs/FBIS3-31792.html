Language: Serbo-Croatian Article Type:BFN [Text] Here is a news item that has just arrived. President of the Bosnia-Herzegovina Presidency Alija Izetbegovic today held a telephone conversation with Turkish President Suleyman Demirel. The Turkish president conveyed to President Izetbegovic his country's readiness to send Turkish troops as a part of the UN Protection Forces [UNPROFOR] to help carry out the peace mission in Bosnia-Herzegovina. In reply to a question about the possible application of the Sarajevo model to other cities, Izetbegovic said that this model was acceptable with certain alterations which envisage the consistent implementation of Resolutions 824 and 836, which oblige the aggressor's side to place heavy weaponry under UNPROFOR control.
