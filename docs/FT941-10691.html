940208 FT 08 FEB 94 / UK Company News: Bradstock buys insurance broker Bradstock, the insurance broker, is to acquire Jensen Dickens, a broker specialising in marine and transit insurance for the freight transport industry. Initial consideration is Pounds 2.43m, of which Pounds 500,000 is in cash and Pounds 1.93m in unsecured loan stock. A deferred amount of up to Pounds 1.75m may be payable in loan stock dependent on Jensen's future results. Turnover of Jensen in 1992 was Pounds 1.18m (Pounds 1m). Pre-tax profits rose from Pounds 52,000 to Pounds 277,000 and further growth is expected for 1993. Net assets at December 31 1992 were Pounds 347,000 (Pounds 179,000).
