940819 FT 19 AUG 94 / Car output rises 3.8% in quarter Car production rose a seasonally adjusted 3.8 per cent in the three months to July compared with the previous three months. Production for export rose 6.5 per cent and home production rose 1.9 per cent, the Central Statistical Office said. Compared with the same three months a year ago total car production also rose a seasonally adjusted 3.8 per cent.
