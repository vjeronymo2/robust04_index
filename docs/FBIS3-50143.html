Language: English Article Type:BFN [Unattributed report: "Division in PDP Grows Deeper"] [Text] Since two days ago, there are two parallel leaderships of the Tetovo branch of the PDP [Party for Democratic Prosperity]. The first is led by Menduh Taci and the second is connected with the legal committee for the preparation of the congress of the party. The dilemma arises as to which leadership will be legal and present the body of the congress. It is interesting to note that the assembly under the leadership of Taci was attended by the representative of the Democratic Party of Albania, Saban Memia. Otherwise, representatives of both factions of the PDP stayed in Tirana, last week, on a "reconciliation" visit. However, the outcome in Tetovo, this weekend, shows that the "axe was not buried."
