920515 FT 15 MAY 92 / International Company News: Molson in CDollars 96m public offering MOLSON, the leading Canadian brewer, is raising CDollars 96m (Dollars 81m) in new equity with a public offer of 3m non-voting A shares underwritten by a syndicate led by RBC Dominion Securities. The offer can be increased by 750,000 shares, bringing total proceeds to CDollars 120m, writes Robert Gibbens in Montreal. Part of the proceeds will be used to refinance commercial paper and support expansion by Molson's hardware retailing subsidiary.
