940328 FT 28 MAR 94 / Business Travel: Algerian unrest France has urged its nationals to return home from Algeria, unless their presence in the north African country is vital. The move follows the murders of two Frenchmen in an Algiers suburb last week. The foreign ministry said in a statement that the stabbing to death of the two men on Tuesday night, blamed by the official news agency APS on Moslem fundamentalists fighting to overthrow the army-backed government, was 'an act of barbarity'.
