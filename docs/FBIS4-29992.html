BFN [Text] Vientiane, June 20 [date as received] (KPL) -- Khamphoui Keoboualapha, deputy prime minister, president of the Committee for Planning and Cooperation on June 18 led a government delegation of the Lao PDR [Lao People's Democratic Republic] to the fifth round table meeting held in Geneva, Switzerland, between June 19-21. The Lao PDR itself hosts the meeting which is under the sponsorship of the UNDP [United Nations Development Program]. The Lao delegation would present to this meeting a development strategy of the country from now till the year 2000. The strategy covers the development of agro-forestry, industry and services, hydro-power construction, education and human resources. The Lao Government delegation will brief donor countries and international organisations about difficulties facing the country and its need in socio-economic development in a hope to attract more international fund and investment. UNDP resident representative to Laos Mrs. Ameerah Haq-perera would also attend this meeting. After this round table meeting, Khamphoui Keoboualapha and his party will pay a good-will visit to France at an invitation from the French Government.
