In the initial approval order, 8 the Commission stated that GSCC should encourage its members to be more accurate in the data they submit in order to reduce, and eventually eliminate, the need for GSCC to substitute or supplement data on behalf of members. The Commission also stated that GSCC should track the identity of the submitting member and the executing firm in order to identify by type of match each trade involving a non-member. The Commission stated that the tracking should provide a basis for GSCC to test the accuracy of member input. While the Commission believes that GSCC should continue to work to improve the comparison rate, the Commission also believes that GSCC has demonstrated sufficient expertise to warrant permanent approval of this rule filing.
