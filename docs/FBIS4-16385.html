BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Ivan Ivanov] [Text] Moscow April 14 TASS -- ITAR-TASS learnt from reliable sources on Thursday that the draft agenda of the summit of the Commonwealth of Independent States will include the following 15 items: 1. Ukraine's accession to the economic union as an associated member. 2. Establishment of a permanent body of the economic union -- interstate economic committee. 3. Creation of a free trade zone. 4. Founding treaty on setting up a Tajikistan aid fund. 5. Measures to ensure cooperation between CIS countries and its charter bodies, on the one hand, and international organisations and forums, on the other. 6. Regulations of the defence ministers' council of CIS member states. 7. Additional measures to stabilise the situation on the Tajik- Afghan border. 8. Cooperation on mobilisation preparations of the economies. 9. Time of stay, composition and tasks of the collective peace- keeping force in the Republic of Tajikistan. 10. Commander of the collective peace-keeping force. 11. Information on progress in the negotiations between the Russian Federation and Central Asian and Transcaucuasian states on cooperation in the protection of their borders with third countries. 12. Convention on ensuring rights of people belonging to national or ethnic, language and religious minorities. 13. Declaration on observing sovereignty, territorial integrity and inviolability of borders of CIS member states. 14. Date and place of holding the next meeting of the Council of CIS Heads of State. 15. Miscellaneous. The above summit draft agenda is preliminary. It can be changed either at a meeting of the CIS coordination-consultative committee or at the meeting of the presidents on Friday.
