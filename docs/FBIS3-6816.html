Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vientiane, March 21 (KPL) -- Mr. Thongsing Thammavong Politburo member of the LPRP CC [Lao People's Revolutionary Party Central Committee], head of the Central Party's Organisational Board, on Marh 19, received the visiting delegation of the Ho Chi Minh National Political Institute of the Communist Party of Vietnam led by its deputy head, Nguyen Van Sao. Mr. Thongsing Thammavong welcomed the visit of the delegation which was here to exchange experiences regarding the training of cadres at the higher party school in Laos. The delegation is scheduled to be here for one week.
