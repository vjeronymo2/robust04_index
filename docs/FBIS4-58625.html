BFN [Text] Last night, Golboddin-Dostam militia fired 250 rockets and mortar shells into the residential areas of Kabul. According to a source of Kabul City Garrison Command, as a result of the rocket attack, three residents of Kabul were martyred and six others were injured. Similarly as a result of the last night's rocket attack, a dwelling was destroyed, and a number of shops were damaged in the city center. The source added that today too, Golboddin-Dostam militia fired rockets into some areas of Kabul. No report about the loss of life and material damage has been received.
