Language: Turkish Article Type:BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] Prosperity Party [PP] leader Necmettin Erbakan has said that his party's mayoral candidates represent brotherhood, unity, and integrity, and that the PP will get all the mayoral positions in southeastern Anatolia after the 27 March local elections. Addressing a party meeting in Ankara, Erbakan said the PP is the only party that is active in southeastern Anatolia, and that the parties imitating the West have been wiped out of this area. He stressed that the problem of terrorism can only be resolved through Muslim brotherhood. [Begin Erbakan recording] It is impossible to end terrorism through the government's violent methods. The real solution to terrorism is the healing power of Muslim brotherhood, and this can be done only by the PP. After terrorism is healed, 60 million people will get real human rights, real freedoms, and real justice. That is when happiness will be restored to our country. [end recording] Erbakan charged that his party will come to power on its own at the end of the first general elections, and that at the end of the coming local elections it will get 1,000 mayoral positions in Turkey, including most of the positions in the greater Istanbul area. Erbakan said: No one believes any more that this government will control the economy or defeat terrorism.
