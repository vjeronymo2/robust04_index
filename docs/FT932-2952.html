930616 FT 16 JUN 93 / World Commodities Prices: Jute and Cotton JUTE C and F Dundee: BTC Dollars 320, BWC n/a, BTD Dollars 295, BWD Dollars 295. C and F Antwerp: BTC Dollars 305, BWC Dollars 305, BTD Dollars 280, BWD Dollars 280. COTTON Liverpool-spot and shipment sales amounted to 22 tonnes for the week ended 11 June against 495 tonnes in the previous week. Subdued offtake did not bring many operations. Support was forthcoming in certain specialist styles notably in the CIS range.
