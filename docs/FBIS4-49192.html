BFN [Text] Beijing, May 4 KYODO -- China on Wednesday [4 May] denied press reports that North Korean President Kim Il-song may visit China this month to discuss with Chinese leaders Pyongyang's suspected nuclear weapons program and its economy. The Foreign Ministry's Information Department denied a South Korean YONHAP news agency report Sunday, which quoted a diplomatic source as saying Chinese Premier Li Peng mentioned Kim's potential visit in his talks in March with visiting South Korean President Kim Yong-sam.
