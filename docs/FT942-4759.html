940608 FT 08 JUN 94 / Observer: Alchemy rules Good old Sir Denys Henderson. Who else but the chairman of ICI dare lecture a group of fat cat bankers that 'only the successful, youthful merchant bankers seem to benefit from the excessive personal rewards which are so hard to justify by the normal standards of industrial remuneration'? He should know - as chairman of Barclays Bank's remuneration committee he presumably gave the thumbs up to the Pounds 1.4m paid to BZW chief executive David Band, 51, Barclays' highest paid director last year.
