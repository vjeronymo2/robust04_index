March 24, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

DEBATE OVER YOSEMITE'S FUTURE

Several commentaries on Yosemite Valley, including David Brower's "Give 'Em Bouquets, Not Bricks" (Editorial Page, March 10), brought back memories of my father's story of his first visit to Yosemite in 1910.

He told of the train trip from Los Angeles to El Portal and the ride into the valley on a stagecoach. "Breathtaking, peaceful beauty," he said.

His next trip to Yosemite Valley was in 1940. His comment after that trip was, "The automobile has ruined my beautiful Yosemite."

HERBERT A. WASSERMAN

Hemet
