BFN [Text] A firebomb was hurled a short time ago at an Israeli vehicle near the entrance to the Jewish settlement of Alfey Menashe. It exploded on the road, and there were no casualties or damage. Seven Nabulus residents were shot and wounded by the IDF [Israel Defense Forces] when riots were dispersed. Our correspondent Moti Amir reports that hundreds of residents demonstrated in solidarity with the Palestinian prisoners on a hunger strike in the Janin jail. The IDF called on them to disperse and when disturbances started, the soldiers used force to disband them.
