Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Ukrainian ambassador to Russia, Volodymyr Kryzhanovshyy, refuted a report that Leonid Kravchuk has refused to take part in the upcoming presidential elections of the republic. In his words, Ukrainian president came out in favor of postponing the election of the head of state. If his opinion is disregarded, he may refuse to run for presidency, said Kryzhanovshyy.
