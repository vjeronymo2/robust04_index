BFN [Report by Vladimir Pasyakin: "Who Will Become Chairman of the Sevastopol City Council?"] [Text] Eleven candidates have registered in Sevastopol for the post of chairman of the City Council. They include Reserve Admiral Aleksey Kalinin, former commander of the Black Sea Fleet, Colonel Ivan Yermakov, former mayor of Sevastopol, Colonel Aleksey Melnikov, deputy head of Black Sea Fleet Air Force communications, and Rear Admiral Aleksandr Penkin, aide to the commander of the Black Sea Fleet for work with personnel. This plethora of military candidates speaks for itself. Sevastopol is the Black Sea Fleet's main base. It is quite possible that a Black Sea Fleet serviceman will become head of the City Council.
