930604 FT 04 JUN 93 / BT cuts price of EC and US calls BRITISH Telecommunications yesterday cut the price of international calls to EC countries, Canada and the US by between five and 10 per cent, depending on the type of call. It also extended discounts for higher volume users. The changes are in line with a 1991 agreement between BT and Oftel, the industry regulator, obliging BT to limit the average price change for its main services in the year to July to 6.25 per cent below inflation.
