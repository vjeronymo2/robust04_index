930916 FT 16 SEP 93 / US Congress in Thorp protest US MEMBERS of Congress have introduced a bill opposing the UK's thermal oxide reprocessing plant (Thorp) and calling on President Clinton to raise the issue with London. Mr Pete Stark, a California Democrat, said: 'Thorp is a direct threat to international security, bringing an additional 59 tons of plutonium into circulation over the next 10 years.'
