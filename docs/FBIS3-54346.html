Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Surt, Ay Annar [January] 25, JAMAHIRIYA NEWS AGENCY -- The general forum of peoples congresses, peoples committees, trades unions, and vocational associations - the General Peoples Congress - resumed their session this morning in the al-Wafa' Hall in Surt in order to deliberate the decisions adopted by the basic peoples congresses at their second ordinary session for the year 1403.
