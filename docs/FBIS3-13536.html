Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tehran, March 15, IRNA -- The Council of Guardians here Tuesday approved the next year budget bill as not being contradictory with the Islamic principles and the Iranian Constitution. The Council's public relations office said that the council had studied the bill in several extra meetings. The Majlis made the amendments to the points which the Council referred to as incorrect twice. The Council of Guardians in its Tuesday morning session announced its final approval with the next year budget bill, the office added.
