Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Beijing, February 9 (XINHUA) -- Vice-Premier Li Lanqing visited Tianqiao Department Store and Hongqiao Agricultural Produce Free Market in Beijing today, the eve of the Spring Festival. At Tianqiao Department Store, Li exchanged festival greetings with customers and sales staff. He was pleased with the store's extensive array of beautifully-lit counters with commodities. Li, also a member of the Political Bureau of the Communist Party's Central Committee, was equally happy to see the plentiful supplies of goods served at the agricultural produce market. He expressed festival greetings to the personnel of the Administration of Industry and Commerce. The Administration of Industry and Commerce is one of the major departments to implement laws and regulations and it plays an important role in ensuring the smooth development of the reforms, he said. Li urged the personnel of the Administration of Industry and Commerce to make a bigger contribution to the development of the national economy.
