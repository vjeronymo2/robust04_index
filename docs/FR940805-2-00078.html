Survey of Community and Migrant Health Centers on Their Use of Case Management for Perinatal Care_New_A Survey of Community and Migrant Health Centers (C/MHCs) participating in the Comprehensive Perinatal Care Program will be conducted to evaluate the use of case management for perinatal care. The information will be used to plan case management and perinatal service delivery strategies in C/MHCs. Respondents: Non-profit institutions.
