940115 FT 15 JAN 94 / Markets: Smaller companies index up - At a glance Saracen Value Trust, specialising in UK smaller companies, is to be launched next month. It is to be managed by Saracen Fund Managers, a new Glasgow-based company run by a former fund manager at Scottish Amicable. It aims to raise Pounds 50m through an offer for subscription of shares at Pounds 1 each, with one warrant to every five shares. Smaller companies are continuing to do well. The Hoare Govett Smaller Companies index (capital gains version) climbed 1.6 per cent to 1744.81 over the week to January 13.
