920814 FT 14 AUG 92 / UK Company News: Net assets lower at Kleinwort Overseas The net asset value of Kleinwort Overseas Investment Trust was 180.4p per share at June 30, down from 194.1p 12 months earlier and 185.4p at the trust's year-end in December. Attributable revenue for the six months to end-June amounted to Pounds 1.64m (Pounds 1.53m) equal to earnings of 2.04p (1.9p). The interim dividend is maintained at 1.5p.
