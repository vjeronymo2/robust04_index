May 28, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

PAT ROBERTSON

Thanks for spoiling my breakfast as I read the article about televangelist Pat Robertson forming a political coalition (Part A, May 15). The American voters resoundingly rejected his right-wing agenda when he ran for president and he's going to attempt to shove it down our throats again.

Perhaps someone should tell Robertson that this is a pluralistic nation and we don't need him trying to dictate what we can read, watch on television or how we conduct our spiritual lives.

His attempts to "see a country governed by Christians" is frightening. I'm quite sure it's from Robertson's point of view. Thanks, but I prefer the vitality and strength that comes with diversity and the contributions of all religions.

JIM SCRUGGS

San Diego
