Language: English Article Type:BFN [Following item transmitted via KYODO] [Text] "A cholera epidemic is spreading in south Kazakhstan, several new cases have been reported within the last few days," Vladimir Stepanov, Director of the Kazakh Anti-Plague Institute, told Interfax. He did not say how many cases had been registered but reliable sources say there are 18 of them. No deaths have been reported. Stepanov said that the steps that had been taken failed to curb the epidemics. Three cases of plague have also been registered in Kazakhstan this summer. A little girl died in the Aktyubinsk Region. The other two patients, a woman in the Kzyl-Orda Region and a man in the Atyrau region, are out of danger.
