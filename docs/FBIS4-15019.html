BFN [Text] The Russian president has issued an edict on the procedure for the publication and the coming into force of federal laws. According to the document, federal laws must be published in the newly launched INFORMATSIONNYY BYULLETEN-SOBRANIYE ZAKONODATELSTVA ROSSIYSKOY FEDERATSII [Information Bulletin-Code of Laws of the Russian Federation]. They are also to be published in ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA and are to be forwarded to the Sistema Legal Information Scientific and Research Center's standard legal information database. Their publication in the Russian Code of Laws and ROSSIYSKAYA GAZETA is to be regarded as official publication. In accordance with the edict, federal laws can also be published in other publications and made public on radio and television. Federal laws come into force on the entire territory of Russia at the same time, ten days following their official publication, if no other date for their coming into force has been specified in them.
