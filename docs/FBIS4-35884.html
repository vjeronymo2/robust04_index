BFN [Text] Foreign Minister Dr. Husayn Sulayman Abu-Salih received the Yemeni envoy to Sudan Mr. Yahya Husayn al-Arashi, minister of civil service and administrative reform today. The Yemeni minister briefed Dr. Abu-Salih on the latest developments in Yemen. He explained that the government is fighting to maintain unity and constitutional legitimacy. He expressed gratitude for the role of Sudan and people towards the crisis. Dr. Abu-Salih reiterated Sudan's declared view on the issue and its rejection of any foreign intervention. The Yemeni minister described the Security Council resolution on the cease-fire as a move for interference in Yemen's internal affairs.
