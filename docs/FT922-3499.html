920611 FT 11 JUN 92 / UK Company News: Comac ends year Pounds 362,424 in black Comac Group, the USM-quoted supplier of specialist staff for the computer industry, reported pre-tax profits of Pounds 362,424 for the year to end-December. This compares with losses of Pounds 34,853 last time and consolidates the turnround to profits of Pounds 187,000 at the interim stage. The result was struck on turnover down slightly from Pounds 10.4m to Pounds 9.97m. At the operating level profits grew from Pounds 62,508 to Pounds 439,641. Income from interests in an associated undertaking fell from Pounds 50,675 to Pounds 7,944. Net interest payable declined from Pounds 148,036 to Pounds 85,161. Earnings per share were 4.05p (0.75p losses).
