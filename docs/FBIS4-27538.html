BFN [Text] Pyongyang, May 31 (KCNA) -- The dear leader Comrade Kim Chong-il sent the 103rd birthday table to Mrs. Om in Kim Chaek city, North Hamgyong Province, the 101st birthday table to Kim San-ok in Wonsan City, Kangwon Province, and the centenary birthday table to Choe Kyong-ok in Pyongwon County, South Pyongan Province. The number of the macrobian persons is growing under the popular health care policy of the state in the DPRK where the people have no worry about food, clothing and housing. The average life expectancy at birth is 74.5 years. The afore-said old women who received benevolent birthday tables said that they were blessed with the sagacious leader and they could not find proper words to express thanks to the great benefit bestowed upon them by the dear leader.
