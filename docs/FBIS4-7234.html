BFN [Text] Speaking at Jakarta's Wisma Negara Guest House this afternoon, Vietnamese Foreign Minister Nguyen Manh Cam reiterated his country's commitment to repatriate Vietnamese refugees still held on Galang Island. Even though he did not give an exact deadline for the repatriation, the Vietnamese minister said his government will do its best to find an early solution to the issue. About 8,000 Vietnamese refugees remain at the Galang island holding center.
