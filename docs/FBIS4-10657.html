BFN [Text] Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina said today in Buenos Aires that Cuba's current foreign policy priority is to strengthen relations with Latin America and the Caribbean. The Cuban minister, who was declared illustrious guest of Buenos Aires, today concluded his official visit to Argentina, where he outlined our country's foreign policy and explained the new trends in our economy. Robaina said to the members of the Foreign Relations Committee of the Argentine Chamber of Deputies that the island has never felt less alone than at present, and, as illustration, pointed out that Cuba already maintains diplomatic relations with 146 countries.
