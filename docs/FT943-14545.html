940714 FT 14 JUL 94 / Civil Service White Paper: Main points The creation of a new senior group within the civil service for about 3,000 top mandarins ranked at grade five and above Top posts to be considered for open advertising, with more outsiders coming in and encouragement for civil servants to seek outside experience Personal contracts for senior civil servants, with more flexible pay reflecting responsibilities and performance Greater devolution of responsibility to Whitehall departments - including for pay and grading Civil service numbers to be cut to below 500,000 by 1998, with de-layering at the top An end to central targets on market-testing - departments to decide on how to live within running costs freeze
