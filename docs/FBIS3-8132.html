Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bucharest ROMPRES, 1/3/1994 -- Four new army commanders have been appointed recently. Their official assumption of duties took place on 28 February and 1 March. The four officers are: Major General Eugen Badalan, Major General Florian Caba, Major General Nicolae Oprea, and Colonel Mihai Palaghia. The release of the army's press bureau informs that the new appointments are in line with the restructuring policies of the Romanian Army, and have been made with the approval of Romanian President Ion Iliescu, commander of the Armed Forces, and on orders of National Defense Minister Lieutenant General Niculae Spiroiu. The new army commanders have passed through the military hierarchy from the stage of platoon commander to that of division commander, the release shows.
