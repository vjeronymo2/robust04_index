January 10, 1990, Wednesday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: POP/ROCK

Most Nods for Soul II Soul The British group Soul II Soul outdistanced dance music divas Paula Abdul and Janet Jackson to grab the most Dance Music Award nominations announced Monday. The award honors the year's most popular and innovative dance tunes and the musicians and record companies behind them.

Nominations and winners are selected by disc jockeys and nightclub operators nationwide in addition to a 50-member advisory board of record label executives. A newcomer to the American Music scene, Soul II Soul was nominated in six of 21 categories including best dance group, best dance artist and best new artist on a major label. Abdul, Jackson and Bobby Brown were runners-up with four nominations apiece. The second annual awards event will be held at the Universal Ampitheater on Feb. 12. SHAUNA SNOW
