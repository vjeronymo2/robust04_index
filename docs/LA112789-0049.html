November 27, 1989, Monday, Home Edition

BETTING WITH BOB

Times handicapper Bob Mieszerski lost $30 Sunday when Earth Angel, the first horse in his parlay from Earth Angel to Patchy Groundfog, ran third in the fourth race. The loss left Mieszerski with a $1,187.30 profit for the meeting. He started with a mythical bankroll of $1,000.

Each racing day Bob will make at least one bet. He could make a straight win bet, he could make a daily double bet, he could make an exacta bet, he could make other exotic bets, or any combination of bets. But, he will make at least one bet.

Beginning Bankroll $1,000

Sunday's Loss $30
