BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 22 (KCNA) -- The members of the "April 15 Fellowship Society" active in Mapo, Seoul, excited people by making and distributing copies of a literature carrying their resolution on the auspicious holiday of April 15, the Seoul-based Radio Voice of National Salvation [VNS] reported. The front side of the 15x25 cm vellum paper reads under the headline "Let Us Significantly Greet April 15, Common Fete of the Nation": "April 15 is a significant day when President Kim Il-song, the peerless patriot and mental support of the 70 million Koreans, was born. "President Kim Il-song peerless in our nation's history of thousands of years is a symbol of the dignity and prosperity of the nation and the great lodestar of national reunification. The birth of President Kim Il-song was the greatest joy and fortune for our nation and mankind. "Let us all significantly greet his birthday as the common fete of the nation." The reverse side of the literature reads under the headline "Our Resolution": "The April 15 Fellowship Society solemnly declares it will greet the birthday of the great President Kim Il-song as the greatest fete of the nation." It carries a five-point resolution of the society vowing to deeply study and grasp the greatness of President Kim Il-song and discharge its mission as the vanguard in the struggle for national reunification under the banner of the 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation.
