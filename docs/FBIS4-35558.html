BFN [Text] Cairo -- [Syrian] Foreign Minister Faruq al-Shar' resumed his meetings yesterday on the sidelines of the 11th Non-Aligned Foreign Ministers' Conference. Al-Shar' met with Yemeni Foreign Minister Muhammad Basindwah. They exchanged views on the latest developments in the situation in Yemen. Al-Shar' also met with Cuban Foreign Minister Roberto Robaina. The talks dealt with relations between the two friendly countries, and the need to reactivate the Non-Aligned Movement's role in light of international changes. Al-Shar' also met with Iranian Foreign Minister 'Ali Akbar Velayati. The talks dealt with the situation in the region, the latest developments in the peace process, and bilateral relations. The foreign minister also met with (Pavel Tratinka), assistant to the Czech foreign minister. During the meeting, the talks dealt with bilateral relations and developments in the Middle East peace process.
