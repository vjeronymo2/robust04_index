BFN [Text] The latest round of Russo-Belarus negotiations on questions of military cooperation has been held in Minsk. As (?Venyamin Popov), the head of the Russian delegation stated, Russia will receive the possibility of using the radio station in Vileyka, even following the conclusion of the withdrawal of strategic forces from Belarus. Apart from that, construction of the missile attack warning radar station near Baranovichi will be completed. These facilities will also remain on Belarus territory even after the year 2000 and will operate in the interests of all member countries of the Treaty on Collective Security.
