October 29, 1990, Monday, Home Edition

MORNING REPORT: ART

Nudes Unveiled: Officials have relented and have allowed the removal of fig leaves covering three nude figurines displayed at Dallas City Hall after the artist accused the city of censorship. Last Monday, park officials ordered the "Getting Better Ain't Always Easy" exhibit by Lubbock artist Future Atkins covered, fearing the 7 1/2-inch pieces would offend visitors. By Thursday, they had changed their minds and removed the makeshift leaves. CLAUDIA PUIG
