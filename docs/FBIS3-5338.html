Language: Korean Article Type:BFN [Text] The U.S. State Department has released a statement regarding a third round of DPRK-U.S. talks. According to foreign media reports, U.S. Secretary of State Christopher on 3 March officially announced that the third round of DPRK-U.S. talks will take place in Geneva on 21 March, as has been agreed between the United States and the DPRK. On the same day, a spokesman for the U.S. State Department issued a statement that Robert Gallucci, assistant secretary of state for political and military affairs, will head the U.S. delegation.
