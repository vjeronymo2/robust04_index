930522 FT 22 MAY 93 / Labour MP for Vauxhall sacked from Citizen's Charter bench MS KATE HOEY, Labour MP for Vauxhall, was yesterday sacked as a front bench spokesman on the Citizen's Charter. She voted with 64 other Labour rebels against the third reading of the Maastricht Bill in the Commons on Thursday night in defiance of whips' orders to abstain.
