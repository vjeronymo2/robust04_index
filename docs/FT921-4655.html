920307 FT 07 MAR 92 / UK Company News: Lightship reduces losses to Pounds 415,000 Lightship, pawnbroking and finance group which changed its name from Harvey & Thompson in December, sharply reduced its pre-tax losses from Pounds 4.76m to Pounds 415,000 in the six months to December 28. The result was struck after exceptional costs of Pounds 835,000, relating to the costs in Philadelphia during the start-up phase of Lightship Financial Group, the US management company. Last time the exceptional charge was Pounds 5.65m. The company was running down the consumer credit, leasing and trade finance books. Lightship, which manages a fund which lends against smaller companies' insured receivables, was 'continuing to make good progress'. Losses per share emerged at 7.49p (106.8p).
