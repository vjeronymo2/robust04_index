November 10, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

QUICK STEP SLEWPY SCORES

Jockey Alex Solis moved Quick Step Slewpy into the lead on the final turn and the pair went on to an easy 3 1/2-length victory in the $32,000 allowance feature Friday at Hollywood Park.

The 3-year-old filly was never threatened after taking the lead and ran the 1 1/8 miles in 1:48 2/5 in defeating favored Casey's Romance. Adorable Vice finished third in the field of 10.

Quick Step Slewpy paid $10.20, $4.60 and $3.00. Casey's Romance returned $3.80 and $3, and Adorable Vice paid $3.80 to show.
