920717 FT 17 JUL 92 / International Company News: US newspaper group rises 4% to Dollars 98.7m GANNETT, the US publisher of 81 daily newspapers, including USA Today, said a recovery in advertising revenues helped it to a 4 per cent rise in net profits for the second quarter of 1992. The Virginia-based company said that its second-quarter net profit was Dollars 98.74m, while earnings per share had improved 13 per cent to 69 cents. Gannett said that its second quarter operating revenues were 2 per cent higher at Dollars 892.1m. Operating income for the quarter was, meanwhile, 9 per cent up at Dollars 176.7m. The company said that its newspaper publishing earnings rose mainly due to higher advertising and circulation revenues, along with significantly lower newsprint costs. At USA Today, the national daily, paid advertising pages increased by 3 per cent during the quarter. Broadcasting revenues were unchanged at Dollars 97.5m as the revenues from Gannett's 10 television stations increased by 2 per cent while revenues from its 15 radio stations declined. Revenues from outdoor advertising division, the largest such company in North America, fell 4 per cent to Dollars 66.8m in the quarter. For the first six months of 1992, earnings per share were 16 per cent higher at Dollars 1.07. Net income was Dollars 154.2m, compared with Dollars 145.1m in the first half of 1991 while operating revenues were 1 per cent better at Dollars 1.7bn.
