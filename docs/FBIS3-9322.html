Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Tirana, March 22 (ATA) -- "All the possibilities exist for more intense relations in all fields between our two countries," President Berisha said in yesterday's meeting with German parliamentarians headed by Mr. Michael Glos. Mr. Berisha said he was grateful to Germany and German parliament for their support to Albania from the very beginning of the democratic processes. President Berisha also called for the Germany's support for Albania's integration in the European institutions. Mr. Berisha said Germany should make its contribution to a fair resolution to the crises in Balkans. "In this context," President Berisha said, "removal or facilitation in the UN embargo on Serbia would escalate the conflict and destabilize the situation in the region." The German parliamentarians thanked President Berisha for the presentation of the situation in Albania. They highly appreciated the achievements of democracy in Albania and promised they will make their contribution to Albania in a concrete and effective assistance of Germany to it. The German parliamentarians were received yesterday by the president of the Democratic Party, Eduard Selami.
