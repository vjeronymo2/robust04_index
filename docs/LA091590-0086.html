September 15, 1990, Saturday, Valley Edition

ROUNDUP

West Bakersfield 21, Antelope Valley 0 -- Tailback Angus Sumlin rushed for 147 yards and two touchdowns for West Bakersfield, which amassed 270 yards rushing at Antelope Valley.

Antelope Valley (0-2) penetrated West territory only once but never got past the Vikings' 20-yard line. MIKE GLAZE
