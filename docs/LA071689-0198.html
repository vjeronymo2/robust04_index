July 16, 1989, Sunday, Home Edition

NEWSWIRE

Emerson Fittipaldi of Brazil again smashed his track record in gaining the pole position for today's Marlboro Grand Prix at 115.541 m.p.h. in East Rutherford, N.J.

Mario Andretti was second at 114.715, followed by Al Unser Jr. at 114.664, Michael Andretti at 114.598 and Rick Mears at 114.520.
