Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Algiers, Dec 30 (AFP)--An Islamic fundamentalist was killed by security forces when three armed men raided a post office in Fouka 25 kilometres (15 miles) from the Algerian capital, the newspaper LIBERTE reported on Thursday. The other two men got away in a waiting car. At Iferhounene, half a dozen armed men attacked the police station and administrative offices but there were no casualties, EL MOUDJAHID reported. At Azazga, in the same region of the Great Kabyle massif, an armed gang on Wednesday got away with 600,000 dinars (26,000 dollars) from a branch of the Local Development Bank.
