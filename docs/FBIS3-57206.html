Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Russian border guards were fired on from guns and rocket installations several times throughout Friday at the Turg height near the 12 and 13 outposts of Moscow's Border Unit on the Tajik-Afghan frontier. The enemy gunners were suppressed by counter fire. No Russian serviceman was hurt. As an army officer in Dushanbe has told Interfax, the height has been continuously shelled for the second day from Tajikistan by small groups of infiltrators from Afghanistan. The spokesman reported that two Tajiks were detained when they tried to cross into Afghanistan in the Ishkashim area of the Gorno-Badakhshan region on Thursday night [20 January]. They were unarmed.
