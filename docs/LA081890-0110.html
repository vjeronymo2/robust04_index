August 18, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

LOS ALAMITOS SATELLITE WAGERING SUNDAY IS FREE

The Los Alamitos Racing Assn. will begin satellite wagering from Del Mar on Sunday continuing through the end of the Del Mar thoroughbred season on Sept. 12.

To celebrate the opening, Los Alamitos will offer free general parking and general admission on Sunday.

In a departure from other tracks conducting satellite wagering, the entire Los Alamitos facility will be open to the public during the Del Mar simulcast including the Turf Terrace dining room, the Cypress Room, and the newly-completed Finish Line Room, a nonsmoking area with table seating for up to 400.

The Del Mar simulcast will continue through the opening weeks of the live harness meet at Los Alamitos, which begins Friday. Patrons attending the Del Mar simulcast during the day may stay for the night harness races at no extra charge.
