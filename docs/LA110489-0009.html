November 4, 1989, Saturday, Home Edition

JUDGE SENTENCES ZSA ZSA GABOR TO 3 DAYS IN JAIL FOR SLAPPING A COP

I certainly hope we've heard the last of Miss Gabor though probably not until she has served every minute of her sentence and paid every dollar of her fine. Only then will the media back off.

As for the rest of us, this whole Zsa Zsa episode is Gaboring.

PATTI GARRITY

Manhattan Beach
