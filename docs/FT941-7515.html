940224 FT 24 FEB 94 / UK Company News: TR High Income TR High Income Trust lifted net asset value per share from 110p to 144.5p over 1993. Fully diluted the figure was ahead from 108.3p to 137.2p. Net revenue increased from Pounds 1.36m to Pounds 1.59m, resulting in earnings per share rising 18 per cent to 6.6p undiluted. The dividend total is held at 6p with a fifth interim of 1.5p.
