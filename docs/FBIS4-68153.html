BFN [Text] Lima Department and Callao Province will remain in a state of emergency until 17 June, according to a decree published in EL PERUANO. Therefore, houses may be searched without a warrant; and restrictions are imposed on the people's right to move from one place to another, to assemble peacefully, and not to be arrested except when an arrest warrant has been issued by a court or by police order in case of flagrante delicto. Police will guarantee public order while the state of exception is in force based on current legal regulations, says the decree published in the official gazette.
