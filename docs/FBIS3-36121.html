Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Liepaja, Latvia, Feb 02, BNS -- The Conference on Security and Cooperation in Europe's mission is visiting the Liepaja city this week to acquaint itself with the problems of ethnic minorities and the Russian troop withdrawal here. Mission's members Jakub Volsky and Johan Olav Seland plan to meet with local authorities, artists and entrepreneurs. The mission will stay in Latvia until September to consult the Latvian government on citizenship, ethnic minorities and troop withdrawal.
