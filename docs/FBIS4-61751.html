BFN [By ITAR-TASS] [Text] Mission Control Centre May 10 TASS -- The crew of the Russian space complex "Mir" is undergoing medical checks today. The commander and the flight engineer's cardio-vascular system is being checked with the help of a "chibis" pneumo-vacuum spacesuit imitating earth's gravitation. Doctor Valeriy Polyakov will carry out the experiment "sport" for determining optimum physical training sessions during prolonged space flights. The cosmonauts photographed Russian central and southern regions this morning, in accordance with the earth's natural resources programme, using the "Priroda-5" photography complex. The space crew conducted geophysical and astrophysical studies and technical experiments on May 7 and 8. The flight of the "Mir" space complex is going on according to schedule.
