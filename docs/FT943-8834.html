940815 FT 15 AUG 94 / Company News (This Week): Britannic Assurance - Other Companies On Wednesday the life insurance company will announce its results for the first half of the year. Like other life companies, it is not required to release interim profits figures, so attention will focus on the interim dividend. Last summer, the interim rose to 3.85p, against 3.433p. Analysts' forecasts for the interim dividend this year are round 4.2p or 4.3p - an increase of 9 or 10 per cent.
