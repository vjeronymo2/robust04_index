Language: English Article Type:BFN [Excerpt] Jakarta, Feb 15 (AFP) -- Visiting minister of state at the German Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Ursula Seiler, Tuesday said she discussed human rights issues with Indonesian President Suharto and stressed the need for a dialogue on the topic. "Despite some differences in views, in regard to human rights, we have a common ground when it comes to the universality of human rights and we must enter into a dialogue in this field and not stick to accusations," Seiler told journalists after paying a courtesy call on Suharto.
