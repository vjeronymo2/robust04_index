941027 FT 27 OCT 94 / UK Company News: Midland & Scottish Midland & Scottish Resources achieved a further reduction in losses in the first half. After reporting a reduction to Pounds 48.3m for 1993, the deficit for the six months to June 30 1994 was cut from Pounds 18.5m to Pounds 7.18m. Turnover fell from Pounds 35.4m to Pounds 24.5m. Administrative expenses of Pounds 6.3m (Pounds 4.89m) included provisions related to an amount due from Mr Martyn Deaner, the former chairman, which the company believes is unlikely to be recovered. Losses per share emerged at 4p (9p).
