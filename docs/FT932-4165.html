930610 FT 10 JUN 93 / UK Company News: Brazilian Inv Trust net assets rise 3.9% The net asset value of Brazilian Investment Trust stood at Dollars 1.003 (64p) per share at March 31, a rise of 3.9 per cent since the trust's launch in May 1992. Net revenue for the period - one of considerable upheaval in Brazil - amounted to Dollars 525,000 for earnings of 0.84 cents per share. A dividend of 0.7 cents is declared.
