BFN [Text] At 1030 today, Vice President 'Abd-al-Halim Khaddam received Dr. Sa'id al-Tall, Jordanian deputy prime minister and minister of higher education, and the delegation accompanying him. Dr. Salihah Sunqur, minister of higher education, and Ahmad al- 'Adayilah, Jordanian ambassador in Damascus, attended the meeting. The discussion dealt with the Arab-Israeli conflict, the Arab state of affairs, and bilateral relations.
