941103 FT 03 NOV 94 / International Company News: CSA granted Kcs586m interest-free loan The Czech cabinet approved a Kcs586m (USDollars 21.5m) interest-free loan to bail out national carrier Ceskoslovenske Aerolinie (CSA), and will wait on seeking another foreign shareholder, said Vaclav Klaus, prime minister, Reuter reports from Prague. 'The government agreed to provide, in 1995, interest-free financial help to the airline in the amount of Kcs586m,' Mr Klaus said. '(This is) to provide the financial means for paying off the leasing payments.' The transport ministry said in September that it would seek a government loan to help relieve CSA from its heavy payment load on its leased aircraft. CSA said its outstanding payment obligations for the year totalled Dollars 42.29m for aircraft leased from Boeing of the US and ATR, a Franco-Italian consortium.
