931211 FT 11 DEC 93 / UK Company News: Albrighton Albrighton, the USM-quoted quarrying concern, yesterday announced a return to the black and is restoring its dividend after a three year absence, with an interim of 0.1p. With turnover up from Pounds 1.16m to Pounds 1.74m, the restructured group turned round from a Pounds 62,000 loss to a pre-tax profit of Pounds 324,000 in the six months to September 30. Mr Humphrey Wood, chairman, said that in the last two months there had been a significant increase in the value of orders received and as a result, a much higher level of sales would be achieved in the second half. Share of associate's losses was Pounds 29,000 (Pounds 4,000 profits). Interest charges fell to Pounds 73,000 (Pounds 186,000). Earnings per share came to 0.6p (1p losses).
