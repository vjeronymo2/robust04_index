931110 FT 10 NOV 93 / Councils launch development body FIVE DISTRICT councils in the north-east of England for which unitary status has been proposed under the present review of local government - yesterday unveiled plans for a joint Pounds 2m development company. Hartlepool, Langbaurgh, Middlesbrough and Stockton in Cleveland, as well as Darlington in County Durham plan to set up the Tees Valley Development Company.
