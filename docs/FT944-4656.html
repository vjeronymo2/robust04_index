941207 FT 07 DEC 94 / UK Company News: Chesterfield Props Chesterfield Properties has acquired the Castle Centre in Stockton-on-Tees, Cleveland, for an undisclosed sum. Built in the early 1970s, the Castle centre contains 275,000 sq ft of retail space, a 125 room hotel let to Swallow Hotels and nearly 1,000 car parking spaces. Castle represents 30 per cent of Stockton's total retail floor space and two thirds of its central area parking.
