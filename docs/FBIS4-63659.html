BFN [Text] The political situation in Crimea was discussed during a meeting of the Crimean Tatar people's mejlis, which took place in Simferopol. A resolution in connection with the danger of an armed conflict arising in Crimea was adopted. It speaks of the unlawfulness of the latest decrees by the Crimean president and Supreme Council which led to the political situation becoming more complicated. In this connection, the resolution says, all bodies in the system of national representation of the Crimean Tatars, should be guided by the norms of international law, the constitution, the laws of Ukraine, and the decisions of the kurultay of the Crimean Tatar people's mejlis. All the necessary measures not to let compatriots be drawn into resistance against the Ukrainian state should be taken, says the resolution of the Crimean Tatar people's mejlis.
