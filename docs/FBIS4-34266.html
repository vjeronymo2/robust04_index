BFN [Text] Buenos Aires, 16 Jun (NA) -- President Carlos Menem denied today that it could be considered a defeat that the chiefs of state participating in the Ibero-American Summit had not issued an opinion against abortion, as "only three or four countries" had opposed his proposal, for which reason the required unanimity was not obtained. Menem also indicated that he "absolutely" was not bothered by the demonstration, called the Federal March to Plaza de Mayo, announced by state unions in the interior of the country, as long as they do not "create any kind of disturbances." The president made these remarks upon arriving at the Metropolitan Airport from Colombia just before 0800.
