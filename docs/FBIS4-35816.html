BFN [Text] Furayh Abu-Middayn, who holds the justice portfolio in the Palestinian Autonomy Authority, says Israel is prepared to release all the Palestinian prisoners in exchange for three soldiers missing since the al-Sultan Ya'qub battle. He asserts that the prisoner release matter was concluded in Oslo, but the MIA issue is hindering the release. Abu-Middayn says Israel is still holding 8,000 Palestinian prisoners.
