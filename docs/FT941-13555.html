940125 FT 25 JAN 94 / UK Company News in Brief: Brown & Tawse Group BROWN & TAWSE Group, the steel and pipes distributor, has sold its Pipe & Tube Group subsidiary to Cobco (114) for Pounds 420,000 in cash and the repayment of Pounds 2.73m of inter-company debt. For the year to March 28 1993 Pipe & Tube returned pre-tax profits of Pounds 190,000 from turnover of Pounds 12.4m. Minority shareholders in Cobco (114) include three current Pipe & Tube directors.
