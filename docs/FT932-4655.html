930608 FT 08 JUN 93 / International Company News: Star Television to become sole supplier to Wharf Cable STAR Television will become the sole supplier to Wharf Cable, the cable television arm of the Wharf group, of international television programmes for Wharf's cable network in Hong Kong, the two companies said yesterday. Wharf, which was recently awarded a pay TV licence, plans to be operating a cable network in October. Initially it will transmit 20 channels on a subscription basis.
