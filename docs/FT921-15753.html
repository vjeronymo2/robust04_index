920108 FT 08 JAN 92 / Mayfair office sold for Pounds 37.5m ARCONA, the Swedish property and construction group, has sold a 40,000 sq ft office building in Mayfair, London, for Pounds 37.5m to undisclosed clients of Citibank of the US. Arcona has given a rental guarantee on the unlet building, and the asking rent has been reduced from Pounds 70 per sq ft to Pounds 58. There will be an additional payment dependent on the income received on first letting. The property in South Audley Street was finished last summer.
