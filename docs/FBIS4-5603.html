BFN [Text] Pyongyang, April 12 (KCNA) -- Vol. 1 of Part 1 "The Anti-Japanese Revolution" of "With the Century", reminiscences of the great leader President Kim Il-song, was published in Bengali by the Dibo publishing house of Bangladesh. The seventh issue of the bulletin published by the Poland-Korea Association carried the full text of "An Echo of Cheers for Independence" from Vol. 1 of Part 1 "The Anti-Japanese Revolution" of the reminiscences and the Russian paper VETERAN detailed summaries of "An Chang-ho Delivers a Political Lecture", "The Path Taken by Cha Kwang-su", and "The Merger of the Three Nationalist Organizations" from Vol. 1. The Russian paper PATRIOT No. 11 edited detailed summaries of "A Raging Whirlwind" and "A Polemic at Dahuangwai" from the Vol. 4 of Part 1 "The Anti-Japanese Revolution" of the reminiscences and the Nepalese paper MAJADUR KISSAN the full text of "An `Ideal Village' Is Transformed Into a Revolutionary Village" from Vol. 2 of Part 1 "The Anti-Japanese Revolution" of the reminiscences.
