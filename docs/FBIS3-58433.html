Language: Spanish Article Type:BFN [Text] Bilbao (Spain), 12 Jan (EFE) -- According to government sources, a bomb blast rocked the Mexican Consulate in Bilbao (northern Spain) without causing any great damage. Government sources in Vizcaya Province reported that approximately 10 minutes before the explosion, an anonymous caller told police that a bomb was going to explode in the building where the consulate is located. No one was inside the consulate at the time of the blast. Spanish police have sent explosives experts to the site to conduct an investigation. Demonstrations have been organized in several parts of Spain in recent days to protest the Mexican Army's repression in the insurrection in Chiapas State, where peasants and Indians are demanding "land and freedom."
