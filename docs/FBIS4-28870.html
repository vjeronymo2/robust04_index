BFN [Text] Comrade Wei Jianxing, member of the Standing Committee of the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee and member of the CPC Central Committee Secretariat and president of All-China Federation of Trade Unions, on 7 June met with a delegation of the Federation of Trade Unions of Korea, which is visiting China, led by Chu Song-il, chairman of the federation. During the meeting, Comrade Wei Jianxing stressed that the blood-tied China-DPRK friendship is a friendship that has overcome the trials of history.
