Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] The ANC [African National Congress] has not been informed about a new initiative concerning the IFP's [Inkatha Freedom Party] participation in the election as mentioned by State President de Klerk. ANC spokesman Cyril Ramaphosa said at a news conference in Johannesburg that he interpreted Mr. de Klerk's remarks about a new initiative as meaning that the government would try to keep the IFP and the kwaZulu government involved in the election process. Referring to the planned meeting between King Goodwill Zwelithini and ANC Leader Nelson Mandela, Mr. Ramaphosa said it was absolutely essential that such a meeting should take place.
