August 17, 1989, Thursday, Orange County Edition

BRIEFCASE: TOURISM/LEISURE TIME

The Anaheim Hilton and Towers has won the Gold Key Award from Meetings & Conventions magazine, a trade publication.

The award, which was given to 75 hotels and resorts, rates properties in food and beverage service, reservation handling, guest services, meeting rooms, technical support equipment and recreational facilities.

The 1,600-room Anaheim Hilton received a Four Diamond Award by the American Automobile Assn. earlier this year.

The hotel has also named Robert Sullivan sales manager. Sullivan previously was convention sales manager at the New York Hilton and Towers.
