921210 FT 10 DEC 92 / UK Company News: Net assets decline at Archimedes Net asset value of the split level Archimedes Investment Trust fell from 576.02p to 469.55p per capital share over the 12 months to October 31. Net revenue amounted to Pounds 318,401 (Pounds 292,094), equivalent to earnings of 25.99p (23.84p) per income share. The second interim dividend goes up to 16p for a total of 25p (23.5p).
