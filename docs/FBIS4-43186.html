BFN [Text] President Glavkos Kliridhis today said he has made it clear that he will resign from his post, if necessary, to give the people the opportunity to decide whether they want further discussion on the confidence-building measures, or whether they prefer to insist on effective alternative procedures before negotiations on the substance of the issue that will open the way for a solution are held. He added: If the UN Security Council follows this procedure, we would be ready to help the secretary general find a just and viable solution to the Cyprus problem. The president said he has made it clear that he remains committed to the 21 March UN document on implementing the confidence-building measures that was prepared by the UN secretary general's representatives, and in his report Butrus-Ghali described it as a fair and balanced document. The president made this statement this morning during an address at a ceremony to receive the credentials of the new Jordanian ambassador to Cyprus.
