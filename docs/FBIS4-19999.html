BFN [Text] Munich -- Foreign Minister Klaus Kinkel (Free Democratic Party of Germany) has called on Russia to make use of its opportunities to exert diplomatic influence to achieve a solution to the conflict in Bosnia. "It will be necessary for the Russians to use and safeguard their influence on the Serbian side," Kinkel stated at the sidelines of an economic meeting in Munich this evening. At a meeting in Bonn on Thursday [28 April], he will discuss the situation in Bosnia with Russian Foreign Minister Andrey Kozyrev.
