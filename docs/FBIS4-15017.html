BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Larisa Kislinskaya] [Text] Moscow April 5 TASS -- Otari Kvantrishvili, 47-year-old Moscow businessman and supposedly a godfather of local mafia was killed today, a reliable source told ITAR-TASS. Kvantrishvili was shot at 20:30 today when he was leaving prestigious Krasnopresnenskiye baths and died while being transorted to hospital. The baths were reportedly included in a zone of his clan's control. Police obtain no data on possible motives of the killing, neither investigators could identify the attacker so far. Kvantrishvili, formerly well-known Georgian athlete and coach, headed the charity Lev Yashin Foundation protecting interests of retired athletes. A year has not passed yet since his elder brother Amiran was shot dead at Moscow shootout between domestic and outside Chechen mafia clans.
