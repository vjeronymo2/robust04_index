Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Shanghai, February 14 (CNS) -- An authoritative source here has revealed that the value of the gross national product (GNP) earned in the city in January this year was RMB [renminbi] 14 billion, 22 percent up over the same period of last year. This amount showed that the city has continued to maintain its rapid economic growth since 1992. According to relevant departments here, the city saw its industrial output last month increasing by 20 percent and the ratio of production to sales reaching 97 percent. There was an even higher growth of turnover of sedans, refrigerators, color TV sets and home air-conditioners. The financial revenue collected by local governments in the city increased by 110 percent over the same period of last year and the value of its exports by 18 percent. In addition, foreign investment in the city has continued to boom with 300 foreign-funded items being approved last month with contracted foreign investment of U.S.$550 million. The city has also seen 26 financial institutions and 59 representative offices set up by foreign financial institutions in 11 foreign countries and regions.
