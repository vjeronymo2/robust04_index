March 9, 1990, Friday, Orange County Edition

COLLEGE ROUNDUP;

UC RIVERSIDE RALLIES IN SEVENTH TO EDGE UCI

UC Riverside scored five runs in the seventh inning to defeat UC Irvine, 7-5, in a nonconference baseball game Thursday at Riverside.

Back-to-back doubles by Pete Weber and Ruben Ayala were the key hits in Riverside's rally.

Jon Damush, Chris Gallego and Jon Skaggs each had two hits for the Anteaters (13-8). Riverside is 17-6.

In nonconference baseball:

Wyoming 5, Southern California College 2 -- Mark Robert hit a two-run home run in the third inning to give visiting Wyoming a 3-0 lead. SCC scored single runs in the eight -- on Doug Rystrom's homer -- and the ninth.

In the Pacific Coast Baptist Bible College baseball tournament at San Dimas:

Liberty Christian 9, Heritage 0 -- Mark Baltodano had nine strikeouts in six innings and had two hits to lead Liberty Christian (3-1). Heritage is 0-2.
