November 29, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: BEVERLY HILLS;

STUDENTS TO RECYCLE SUPPLIES

The Associated Student Body at Beverly Hills High School has put aside $1,000 to purchase 200 boxes to be used in a recycling effort at the school. The boxes, which will be used to collect notebook and Xerox paper as well as other supplies, will be placed in rooms throughout the high school and in school district offices.

A salvage company will pay the student body office a percentage of the selling price for the recycled goods.
