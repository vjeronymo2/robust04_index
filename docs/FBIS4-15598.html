BFN [By GIA-ITAR-TASS] [Text] Tbilisi April 9 TASS -- An explosion took place at the editorial office of the SVOBODNAYA GRUZIA (FREE GEORGIA) newspaper today. According to preliminary information, criminals threw a home-made explosive device from the corridor into the room and ran away. No casualties were reported. Substantial damage was inflicted on the editorial office. The incident is being investigated.
