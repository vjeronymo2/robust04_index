BFN [Text] The Guangzhou Military Region held a meeting yesterday afternoon to relay the spirit of the Second Session of the Eighth National People's Congress [NPC]. Guangzhou Military Region leaders such as, [Commander] Li Xilin, Shi Yuxiao, and others, as well as the military region's headquarters personnel, political department, and logistics department leaders were present at the meeting at which Lieutenant General Liu Xinzeng, Guangzhou Military Region deputy political commissar and Eighth NPC deputy, relayed the spirit of the Second Session of the Eighth NPC. Lieutenant General Shi Yuxiao, Guangzhou Military Region political commissar, delivered a speech on how to implement the NPC session spirit to the letter. In a related development, more than 700 officers and soldiers of the Guangdong Provincial Military District yesterday attended a meeting at the military district headquarters at which Wen Yuzhu, Guangdong Provincial Military District commander and Eighth NPC deputy, relayed the spirit of the Second Session of the Eighth NPC.
