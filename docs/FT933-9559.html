930809 FT 09 AUG 93 / International Company News: SNC Lavalin SNC Lavalin, a Canadian-based international engineering group, continued its turnaround in the first half, with profit of CDollars 8.8m, or 60 cents a share, up from CDollars 700,000, or 5 cents, a year earlier, on revenues of CDollars 357m, up 12 per cent. Most of the gain came in the second quarter, helped by advance payments on international contracts and lower operating costs.
