Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent] [Text] Moscow, 14 Mar -- The press service of the Russian president has circulated the text of a decree by Boris Yeltsin "On certain matters relating to the resignation of the procurator general of the Russian Federation." It states: "Owing to the fact that A.I. Kazannik was appointed to the post of procurator general of the Russian Federation not in accordance with the procedure laid down by Article 102 of the Russian Federation Constitution, and taking into consideration his personal request, I resolve: 1. To regard the resignation of Aleksey Ivanovich Kazannik from the post of procurator general of the Russian Federation as accepted. 2. To deem Point 1 of the decree by the Russian Federation president of 26 February 1994, No.391 "On the procurator general of the Russian Federation" as no longer valid. 3. This decree takes effect on publication.
