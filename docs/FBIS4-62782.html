BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Lyudmila Aleksandrova] [Text] Moscow, 17 May -- Sergey Shakhray announced today that he will resign from the post of deputy chairman of the government of the Russian Federation. At a news conference in Russian Foreign Ministry's press center, he read out a message he has sent to the president of the Russian Federation and to the head of the government. In the document, Sergey Shakhray asked that he be released from his post of deputy chairman of the government "in connection with the appointment of a new minister of nationalities and regional policy even without taking into formal account the opinion of the deputy prime minister in charge of these problems" Sergey Shakhray said his resignation "does not mean he is leaving to join the opposition to the president and the government and does not signify any insult."
