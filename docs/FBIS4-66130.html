BFN [Article signed NI: "Arrival of Polish President Lech Walesa at Zurich-Kloten"] [Text] Zurich, 25 May (ATS) -- The Polish President Lech Walesa and his wife Danuta arrived at Zurich-Kloten airport at 1040 on Wednesday [25 May] on board a special flight from Warsaw. The presidential couple is on a three-day state visit to Switzerland. Mr. and Mrs. Walesa were welcomed at the plane by Confederation President Otto Stich, Federal Council Member Flavio Cotti, and Francois Couchepin, chancellor of the confederation, who were accompanied by their wives. The Polish and Swiss delegations, each consisting of about 20 people, took limousines to the special train for Bern. The Polish president will be received there by the Federal Council, after military honors have been observed at the Federal Palace. The Polish and Swiss delegations will hold official talks this afternoon, after which the Federal Council will host dinner in honor of its guests.
