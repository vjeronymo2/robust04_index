930522 FT 22 MAY 93 / Liquidations rise VOLUNTARY company liquidations in England and Wales rose to 3,454 in the first quarter of the year compared with 3,416 in the same period last year, KMPG Peat Marwick said. A KMPG survey showed nearly 60 per cent of creditors' voluntary liquidations in the quarter were in south-east England, reflecting the way in which the recession had hit the service sector.
