November 17, 1990, Saturday, Home Edition

DEAL FOR STRAWBERRY SEEMS TO MAKE FANS OF BOTH SIDES HAPPY

Hats off to the Dodgers for finally doing the right thing and not letting a great one (Tim Raines, Rickey Henderson, etc.) slip away. Darryl Strawberry will be a great Dodger for many years, but if the Dodgers are really serious about winning a championship, they'll have to dig a little deeper into their pockets and sign or trade for a left-handed relief pitcher, such as Dave Righetti or Ken Dayley.

FRANK PEARLSTEIN

Sherman Oaks
