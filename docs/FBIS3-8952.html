Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Bratislava March 15 (CTK) -- Slovak President Michal Kovac today officially entrusted Jozef Moravcik the task of conducting negotiations on the composition of a new cabinet, Kovac's spokesman Anton Bodis told CTK on Tuesday [15 March]. On Monday [14 March], most of the former opposition parliamentary parties agreed to propose Moravcik, the foreign minister of the former cabinet of Vladimir Meciar, as the new Slovak premier. President Kovac asked the former opposition parties to compose a new government after the Meciar-led Movement for a Democratic Slovakia (HZDS) had not answered his question whether it would propose a person whom he could charge with the composition of the new cabinet. The new cabinet of Premier Designate Moravcik is to be completed on Wednesday [16 March] at the latest, when the current parliamentary session is to resume.
