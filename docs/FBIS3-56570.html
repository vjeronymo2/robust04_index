Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Most members of the Georgian paramilitary group, formerly known as "Mkhedrioni," have surrendered arms as of Friday, Interfax was told by the group's spokesman. Those who are against will be excluded from the group, he said. The decision for disarmament of the group headed by the founder of "Mkhedrioni," Djaba Ioseliani, was made January 10 by the Georgian authorities to help restore order in the republic. The surrendered arms are stored in the group's warehouses.
