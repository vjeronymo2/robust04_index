930309 FT 09 MAR 93 / UK Company News: Brit Biotechnology losses increase British Biotechnology Group, the pharmaceutical company, reported losses of Pounds 3.32m for its third quarter to January 31, against Pounds 1.97m. Turnover rose from Pounds 1.55m to Pounds 1.82m. Losses per share were 9.2p (6.9p). Losses for the nine months were Pounds 8.04m, up from Pounds 7.68m last time, on turnover of Pounds 5.72m (Pounds 3.5m). Losses per share were 23.6p (30.8p).
