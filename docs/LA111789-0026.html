November 17, 1989, Friday, Home Edition

NEARLY BECALMED, CHEETAH WINS RACE

Cheetah drifted across the finish line in almost no wind but nearly 11 hours ahead of the next boat to sweep the line, IOR-A and PHRF-A handicap honors in the 13th biennial race from Long Beach to Cabo San Lucas.

The Peterson 66, co-skippered by Phil Murray and Dick Penningston of Long Beach Yacht Club, sailed past the cape of Baja California at 10:43 p.m. Wednesday.

Mitchell Rouse's Taxi Dancer, also of Long Beach Yacht Club, was next at 9:34 a.m. Thursday. All 14 of the ultra light displacement boats -- or "sleds" -- in those two classes had finished by early afternoon Thursday. Taxi Dancer also was runner-up in IOR-A. Joss, a MacGregor 65 sailed by Dick and Camille Daniels of Long Beach Yacht Club, was runner-up in PHRF-A, more than 13 hours behind Cheetah. RICH ROBERTS
