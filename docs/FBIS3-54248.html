Language: Hebrew Article Type:BFN [Text] Two soldiers were slightly wounded by an explosive charge in Khan Yunus. Our correspondent Nisim Qeynan reports that a homemade explosive charge was thrown at an IDF [Israel Defense Forces] patrol in Khan Yunus this morning. Two soldiers were injured by shrapnel, and four local residents who were at the site of the explosion also suffered injuries. The IDF has launched searches for those who threw the bomb. The two soldiers were taken to the hospital, and a local car rushed the four locals to al-Nasir Hospital.
