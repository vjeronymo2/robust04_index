Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [From the "Novosti" newscast] [Text] We have just received the text of probably the final appeal and the resolution issued by the Central Electoral Commission of the Russian Federation to the people and the leaders of the Chechen Republic. The appeal says: We suggest that you examine and make a positive decision on the question of the Chechen Republic's participation in the elections to the Federal Assembly of the Russian Federation to be held in March 1994. In accordance with the constitution of the Russian Federation, the Chechen Republic was and still remains a component part of the Russian Federation and has every right to be properly represented in the Russian Federation's Federal Assembly. The resolution proposes that elections are to be held on 13 March 1994.
