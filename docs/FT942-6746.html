940528 FT 28 MAY 94 / UK Company News: B Elliott Following the turnround at the midway stage, B Elliott, specialist, electrical and mechanical engineering concern, ended the year to April 1 with a surplus of Pounds 2.81m pre-tax against losses of Pounds 3.06m. Excluding discontinued operations and old contracts, turnover expanded 13.6 per cent from Pounds 72.32m to Pounds 82.13m. Earnings were 6.7p (42.1p losses) and there is a 1p final proposed.
