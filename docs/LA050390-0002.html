May 3, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WORLD REPORT

Congratulations on your first edition of the World Report (April 24)! Well over half of my business is conducted overseas and I have always found The Times to be a little short on international coverage.

A suggestion: Would you include a table of currency exchange rates like you did for the economic indicators? An even better idea would be to have the exchange rates listed daily in the Business section.

GARY G. DAVIS

Irvine
