940409 FT 09 APR 94 / Finance and the Family: Trusts in simple language - At a Glance The Association of Investment Trust Companies has produced a free 20-page guide to investment trusts, which explains all the jargon and technicalities of investment trusts in simple language. The guide is intended to accompany the AITC's monthly information service monitoring investment trust performance, which costs Pounds 28 a year for the monthly version, or Pounds 15 a year for the quarterly. For a copy of the users' guide and a free sample of the monthly information service, call 071-431-5222.
