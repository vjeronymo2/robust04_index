BFN [Announcer-read report over video] [Text] A delegation from Iran has held meetings at the Foreign Ministry on the clashes in northern Iraq. In a statement on the issue, Foreign Ministry Spokesman Ferhat Ataman said Turkey, Iran, and Syria hold continual meetings to discuss the developments in northern Iraq. Stating that the latest situation in the region was assessed with the Iranian delegation, Ataman said a joint military operation is out of the question.
