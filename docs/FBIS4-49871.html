BFN [Text] Beijing, may 12 (XINHUA) -- A Chinese Foreign Ministry spokesman expressed his hope here today that Cambodia will properly settle its domestic issues and realize national reconciliation. Wu Jianmin, the spokesman, made the remarks when asked to comment on the proposal of King Sihanouk of Cambodia to invite the leaders of Khmer Rouge to participate in the new national electioneering. "This is the internal affair of Cambodia," Wu said, adding China hopes that the various parties in Cambodia will, under the leadership of King Sihanouk, properly settle the internal issues of Cambodia and realize national reconciliation.
