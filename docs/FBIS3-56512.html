Language: Russian Article Type:BFN [By ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan, 17 Jan -- Russia is adhering to an impartial stance on the Karabakh settlement and is sincerely committed to stopping the bloodshed in the conflict zone, the Armenian president's press secretary said here today. Speaking at a briefing, Aram Abramyan gave a positive assessment of the recent statement by a Russian Foreign Ministry representative on Moscow continuing its mission of mediation in the region. The representative of the Armenian leadership reported on the continuation of diplomatic efforts to overcome the crisis in the region, in particular, the continuing visit to the region by Vladimir Kazimirov, the Russian president's special envoy. The press secretary reiterated the known fact that Yerevan and Stepanakert have responded positively to the new peace-making initiative by Russia and the CSCE. Now it is up to Azerbaijan, noted the Armenian representative.
