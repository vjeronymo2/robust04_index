May 23, 1990, Wednesday, Valley Edition

CLU DOUBLES TEAM OUSTED IN NATIONALS

Matt Simpson and Tony Cohen, Cal Lutheran's No. 1 doubles team, won a first-round match before being eliminated in the NAIA tennis championships in Kansas City, Mo., on Tuesday.

Simpson and Cohen beat George Newsome and Gareth Hosford of Atlantic Christian (N.C.), 6-1, 6-2, then lost to Billy Scott and Craig Stone of Elon (N.C.), 6-1, 6-4, in the second round.

In singles, Matt Simpson lost to 14th-seeded Zubin Irani of Atlantic Christian, 6-0, 6-2, in the third round.
