920728 FT 28 JUL 92 / International Company News: Krupp-Hoesch merger approved SHAREHOLDERS in Hoesch, the German steel and engineering group, yesterday voted with 99.73 per cent - both in terms of votes and nominal capital - to merge with Fried. Krupp, Reuter reports from Dortmund. Germany's second largest steel group and the fifth steel company in Europe, Krupp-Hoesch will become one of Europe's leading engineers.
