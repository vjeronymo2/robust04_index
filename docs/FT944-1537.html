941220 FT 20 DEC 94 / Polly Peck group hotel rent paid The administrators of Polly Peck International, the failed empire of fugitive businessman Mr Azil Nadir, yesterday moved closer to gaining control of assets in northern Cyprus. A cheque for nearly Dollars 100,000 (Pounds 65,000) was paid by a representative of Voyager Kibris, a PPI subsidiary, to the government of the breakaway republic for unpaid rent on the Jasmine Court Hotel. Mr Chris Barlow, a partner in Coopers & Lybrand and lead administrator, said there were plans to meet the government today to discuss payment of tax debts.
