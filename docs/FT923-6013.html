920827 FT 27 AUG 92 / Medicine hopes for body measurer: British Association for the Advancement of Science A DEVICE to measure a person's size and shape in seconds could have important applications in medicine and the clothing industry. The Loughborough Anthropometric Shadow Scanner, developed at Loughborough University, uses projected strips of light and TV cameras linked to a computer to display body shapes numerically. Professor Peter Jones, head of the research group, said it intended to establish a databank of body shapes.
