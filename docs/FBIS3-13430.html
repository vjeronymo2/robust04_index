Language: Persian Article Type:BFN [Text] With the dispatch of 1,153 air and ground patrol units, the security forces have started the implementation of a special scheme for the period of Nowruz [Persian new year celebrations, starting 21 March]. At a news conference today, Brigadier General Seyfollahi, the security forces commander, also announced: During the Nowruz period, the security forces' patrol units will be dispatched to their designated areas, according to a prearranged plan. He then set out to describe the security forces' activities during 1372 [year ending 20 March 1994] and said: During this year, by closing the country's eastern borders, the security forces succeeded in discovering 90 tonnes of narcotics of various types. Furthermore, in this connection 459 leaders of gangs involved in narcotics distribution were arrested. The security forces commander added: During the past year, security forces personnel were also able to confiscate 10,000 firearms which had been brought into the country illegally. Moreover, during 1372, 51,000 antique objects of various kinds were recovered from several gangs who had intended to take them out of the country. He said: The security forces have been very active in the four border provinces of the country, fighting the import of contraband goods and succeeding in doubling the volume of its confiscations in comparison with last year. The security forces commander also said: Legal proceedings will be taken against individuals who produce and distribute fireworks.
