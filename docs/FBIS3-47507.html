Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Pyongyang, January 12 (KCNA) -- Kim Kwang-chon, vice- chairman of the Central Committee of the South Korean National Democratic Front [SKNDF] (Hanminjon), issued a statement January 6, fully supporting the policy of reunifying the country by the confederacy formula on the principles of independence, peaceful reunification and great national unity reiterated by the great leader President Kim Il-song in his New Year address, Seoul-based radio Voice of National Salvation reported. "No force on earth can break the will of our fellow countrymen to reunify the country in the 1990s no matter how hard the separatist forces at home and abroad may work and how the situation surrounding us may change, so long as the Koreans in the South, North and overseas are guided by the great leader President Kim Il-song whom they all worship and follow and the chuche-based principles and line of national reunification and the 10-point programme of the great unity of the whole nation light the road ahead of the nation," said the vice-chairman. "Hanminjon", he declared, "will join the patriotic people of all walks of life in speeding up the onward movement for the reunification of the country that will unite the 70 million Koreans in one body, under the banner of the 10- point programme of the great unity of the whole nation uo forward by President Kim Il-song and contribute to making this year a year of national harmony and unity."
