September 13, 1990, Thursday, Home Edition

WESTSIDE DIGEST: HOLLYWOOD;

AIDS DISCUSSIONS PLANNED

The public policy division of AIDS Project Los Angeles will hold the first in a series of public forums to address HIV-related political issues from 7 to 9:30 p.m. Tuesday in room 140 of the AIDs Project Los Angeles' Nancy Cole Sawaya Center, 6721 Romaine St.

Topics to be discussed include government policy on HIV, AIDS discrimination, legislation, and funding for AIDS programs and services.
