930701 FT 01 JUL 93 / Parliament and Politics: Patten rejects funding claims MR Chris Patten, the Hong Kong governor, yesterday rejected suggestions that his position was being compromised by large donations to the Tories from wealthy Chinese citizens in the colony. 'Anybody who looks at the history of the last few months and believes that somehow there could be a relationship between party financing and the policies pursued in Hong Kong must have a very . . . exotic idea of what's been happening,' he said.
