Language: English Article Type:BFN [Text] Vladimir Zhirinovskiy, leader of the Liberal Democratic Party, told Interfax that he had appealed Monday [28 February] against the ruling of the Shchyolkovo District Court, Moscow Region. The court canceled the election result in the electoral district where Zhirinovskiy won the December 12 elections. The reason for the ruling is that a Yabloko bloc candidate resigned from the election race in favor of the Russia's Choice candidate but his name remained in the electoral bulletin.
