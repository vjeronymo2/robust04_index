931007 FT 07 OCT 93 / International Company News: DMR confident of revenue growth DMR GROUP, an international information technology group, says strength in its North American operations and better results in Europe will ensure solid revenue growth and sustained improvement in profitability this year, writes Robert Gibbens. In the first quarter to August 31, revenues climbed 13 per cent to CDollars 61m (USDollars 47m) and net profit 10 per cent to CDollars 837,000, or 6 cents a share.
