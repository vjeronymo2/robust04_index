920826 FT 26 AUG 92 / Canary Wharf costs outlined THE COST of maintaining Canary Wharf, the failed London Docklands development which is in administrative receivership, is running at between Pounds 800,000 and Pounds 900,000 a month, creditors were told yesterday. Mr Stephen Adamson, joint administrator and a partner at accountants Ernst & Young, said unsecured creditors, who are owed Pounds 51m, would receive very little if they forced the sale of the development.
