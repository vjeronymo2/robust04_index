March 30, 1989, Thursday, Home Edition

SPORTS GROUPS RECEIVE GRANTS

Three Westside organizations were among recent recipients of grants from the Amateur Athletic Foundation of Los Angeles. The Santa Monica Gymnastic Center received $44,606 for new equipment; the West Los Angeles College Foundation, $41,800 to continue operation of the weight-throwing center at the college, and the Los Angeles School of Gymnastics, $40,000 for equipment and scholarships for rhythmic gymnastics.
