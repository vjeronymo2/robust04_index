BFN [Excerpts] Police say the killing of 28-year-old Yosi Zandani in Bene 'Ayish near Gedera was apparently a terrorist attack. Zandani was found strangled and stabbed to death early yesterday morning. A police spokesman said a letter in Arabic claimed the murder had been carried out to avenge the death of a Gaza Arab. Yitzhaq Rottenberg, who died after being wounded in a terrorist attack on Tuesday, will be laid to rest this afternoon. [passage omitted] A German tourist has been seriously injured in the head in a stone-throwing attack in Qalqilyah. The 43-year-old woman and her daughter, who was slightly hurt, mistakenly drove into the town yesterday, and her car was pelted with rocks. She was taken unconscious to Beilinson Hospital's neurological unit.
