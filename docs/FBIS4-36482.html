BFN [Text] Today, it was revealed that a network of 15 Jewish and Israeli Arab weapons dealers who sold weapons and ammunition to fugitives from the territories has been uncovered. Our correspondent Yafa Glick reports that the Tel Aviv Magistrate's Court today remanded two Netanya shooting gallery owners, Avi Hajaj and Shim'on Farhan, and one of the workers, Yosi Yankelevich. The three are suspected of having sold weapons and ammunition to six Israeli Arabs from al-Tira and Qalansuwa, who in turn sold the weapons to fugitives from Nabulus, Bayt Lahiyah in the Gaza Strip, and Tulkarm.
