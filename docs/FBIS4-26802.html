BFN [Text] Guangzhou, June 24 (XINHUA) -- Philippine Vice-President Joseph Ejercito Estrada ended his China visit and left here today for home via Hong Kong. The Philippine vice-president and Mrs. Estrada arrived here in the capital of south China's Guangdong Province yesterday from Shanghai, China's largest industrial city. Earlier today, Ou Guangyuan, vice-governor of Guangdong, met with Estrada and his party and gave a luncheon in their honor.
