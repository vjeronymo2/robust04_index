BFN [Unattributed report under the "Provincial Chronicle" rubric based on INTERFAX, RIA NOVOSTI, POSTFACTUM materials: "Nazran"] [Text] Kazbek Kostoyev, former presidential adviser on youth affairs, has made an official statement saying that he can no longer stay in parliament, because, in his view, the legislature is "just yet another headache for President Ruslan Aushev." He also stressed that in a republic with a presidential form of government the parliament must coordinate all its actions with the president.
