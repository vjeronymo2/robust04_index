BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondents Viktor Demidenko and Mikhail Melnik] [Text] Kiev 24 May, TASS -- "Talks with the parliamentary delegation from the Republic of Crimea are proceeding in a normal and constructive manner. The Ukrainian side is satisfied with the proceedings," Ukrainian Parliament delegation leader Boris Oleynik told ITAR-TASS. Crimean parliamentarians refused to comment. The meeting continues behind closed doors.
