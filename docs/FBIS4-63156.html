BFN [Report by ITAR-TASS correspondent Tigran Liloyan] [Text] Yerevan, 20 May -- The press service of the Armenian Defense Ministry told this ITAR-TASS correspondent about a violation by the Azerbaijani side of the cease-fire on the border with Armenia. On Thursday [19 May] evening, positions near the border village of Vazashen in Idzhevanskiy rayon in the northeast of Armenia came under fire from personnel carriers. There were no casualties, and the Armenian side did not return fire, the press service noted.
