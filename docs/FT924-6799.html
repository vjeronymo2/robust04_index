921119 FT 19 NOV 92 / UK Company News: Property Partners' shows 9% growth Property Partnerships, the hotels and property group, announced a 9 per cent profits rise from Pounds 925,000 to Pounds 1.01m. The interim dividend is raised by 0.05p to 2.55p. Gross rental income from investment properties improved 19 per cent in the half-year to September 30, from Pounds 934,000 to Pounds 1.12m, and sales of hotel goods and services came to Pounds 2.24m (Pounds 2.23m). Earnings per share worked through at 6.31p (5.76p).
