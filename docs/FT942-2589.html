940618 FT 18 JUN 94 / UK Company News: John Swan Following an increase at midway, John Swan & Sons, the livestock auction and estate agency concern, finished the year to April 30 with pre-tax profits of Pounds 376,898, compared with Pounds 231,346. Turnover, which comprises commission, fees and credit charges, moved ahead from Pounds 1.49m to Pounds 1.52m. Earnings per share expanded from 25.2p to 39.4p while the dividend is increased to 18p (16p).
